Made a low-fat and vegetarian version of this last week and was thrilled with how well it turned out. I often make a breakfast casserole at the beginning of the week and just heat up a serving before work so that I can have a  healthy meal in just a couple of minutes. I used Morningstar Farms soy sausage crumbles in place of the pork sausage, and sauteed it with an onion in a splash of olive oil. Made it VERY low-fat by using Eggbeaters, a blend of fat-free and reduced-fat cheddar, almond milk, and low-fat butter. It was still absolutely delicious, and only had about 4-5 grams of fat and less than 250 calories per serving. My boyfriend says this is his favorite breakfast casserole I have made. Planning to make it again this week.
