This was such a good meal.  The meat mixture is like a mini meatloaf. I followed the recipe for the most part. Here are the changes I made and it turned out delicious.  Instead of using rice, I added 1 1/2 C riced cauliflower.  To make it a little more fiesta I added a can of rinsed black beans and about a 1/2 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese to the filling.  I also added a little bit of cayenne pepper and a some chili powder.  Since I added the beans, I did need to make a few more peppers because I had extra filling. I baked for about 1.25 hours, without covering.  When serving, I sprinkled with more cheese and a dollop of sour cream.  My family devoured it!
