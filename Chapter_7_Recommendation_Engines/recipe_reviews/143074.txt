Thanks for this recipeâ€”the meringue adds a fun twist to the basic baked custard recipe. I'm not a big fan of baked pudding because it's never as smooth as desktop or slow-cooker pudding, but this adds a fun way to use the leftover egg whites as meringue that looks so appealing! 

Here's my variation: I made  slow-cooker rice pudding then added meringue at the end; here's how:  Prepare your favorite slow cooker recipe as described, but refrigerate the egg whites while the rice pudding cooks. During the last 1/2 hour of cooking, follow the meringue preparation for this recipe and remember to preheat the oven. Transfer the pudding to a large baking dish and spoon the  meringue mixture on top, making peaks with the spoon. Bake until meringue is cooked and golden approx. 8-10 minutes.
