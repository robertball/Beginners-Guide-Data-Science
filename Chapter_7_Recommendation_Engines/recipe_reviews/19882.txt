To suit my taste I made a few minor changes.Firstly, I increased the sweetened condensed milk to the full can. Then I used 1/3 cup of Hershey's unsweetened cocoa instead of vanilla. 1/3 cup cocoa gives it a "Mild" chocolate taste. Lastly, I added 2 whole eggs. Ya, I know, I'm gonna Die from the raw eggs. LOL. The eggs keep the ice cream from getting to hard. Mine comes out smooth and velvety. I can scoop it out with a silverware tablespoon. BTW, I've made this recipe now six times and never had to wait 24 hrs. Mine is ready in 12 hrs.plus or minus.

In the preperation, I use my Blender to mix the Evaporated Milk, Sweetened Condensed Milk, and Cocoa. I let it rip at full speed untill I see that the Cocoa is totally blended in. Next, I transfer the mix to a large mixing bowl, and fold in the Cool Whip before using my Hand Mixer with Whisk to fully incorporate all ingredients.

After pouring the mixture into the freezing vessel, (A 2 Litre vessel is perfect). if you see tiny air bubbles give it time to let the air escape. I give it a GENTLE stir. I cover it with Glad Wrap prior to placing the air tight lid on the vessel. DONE.
  
In about 12 hrs. you will have a smooth, scoopable,ice cream treat.
Also, a shallow vessel will shorten the freezing time. ENJOY!
