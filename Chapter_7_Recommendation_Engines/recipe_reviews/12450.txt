THIS IS A GOOD STARTER RECIPE.  YOU NEED CRUSHED PINEAPPLE ; WHEN THE GELELTIN STARTS TO SET UP USE HAND MIXER & START BEATING, GET IT WHIPPED STIFF.   1/2 CAN OF EVAPORATED MILK PUT IN FREEZER 1 HR. W/ BOWL & BEATERS.  BEAT MILK UNTIL LIGHT STIFF. FOLD INTO GELETIN MIXTURE . BEAT EGG WHITES STIFF ADD 4 TBL SUGAR.  SPREAD ON TOP OF PIE PUT IN OVEN 350* F UNTIL BROWNED REFRIGERATE 6 HRS OR MORE
