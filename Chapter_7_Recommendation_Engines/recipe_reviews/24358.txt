I like this recipe because it is so simple and has enough protein to be a vegetarian dish.  It is slightly on the bland side without some modifications, though. I added 2 cloves of garlic based on suggestions by other reviewers.  After tasting it, I sprinkled some caraway seeds on top. Next time, I will add these while sauteeing the onion and cabbage - they add a good depth of flavor. The butter is a bit excessive and the dish was just fine with less (I used a total of 1/2 cup.)  Next time I may try substituting some sour cream for the butter when the noodles are done.  This dish needs a heavy hand with the salt.  I liked it without meat, but my husband thinks it needs some meat.  Overall, it is a simple comfort food dish, and I will definitely make it again with these modifications.
