This pie deserves more than five stars.  My boyfriend and I found the recipe on a coupon or something at the store last summer and immediately bought all the ingredients and made it that night. OH MY GOODNESS!!! This is "IT"!!! This is as close as you can get to Baker's Square Reese's Peanut Butter Cup Pie (thet all went out of business by us!) I did modify the recipe slightly though. I did not save very much of the Hot Fudge Topping for the top of the pie, as I knew it would be farily thin after warming & would run right off the pie, so straight out of the jar (which was 15oz Hershey's brand) I smeared it into the bottom & up the sides 3/4's of it!  I only had 2 - 8oz tubs of coolwhip, so I dumped an entire one into the cream cheese mixture, then scooped in about 2/3's of the 2nd container (maybe a total of 13 - 15ozs) & whipped the mixture well, I poured the remaining 1/3 jar of Hot Fudge Sauce over the pie (but as I thought, it ran!). I did not use the rest of coolwhip on top nor the decorative peanut butter or hotfudge squeezed out of ziplocs, I simply used a can of Redi Whip & as I served each piece I would generously spray the top & then add the perfect touch: 1/2 of a full-size Reese's Peanut Butter Cup sticking straight up out of the redi whip! MY guests LOVED it & it was as good, if not better than Baker's Square's version!
