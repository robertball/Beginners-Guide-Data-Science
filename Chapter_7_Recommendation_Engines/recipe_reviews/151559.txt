I made this tonight but I did change a few things. I doubled the recipe and put it in a 9x13 pan. I used 2 cans of cream of broccoli soup mixed with 1 cup of milk. I used two bags of frozen broccoli. I put each bag in a gallon ziploc bag that I punched a fork into to let air escape while I microwaved for 4 minutes. Then added the broccoli to the soup mixture. I boiled a package of egg noddles and added them to the bottom of the greased pan. Topped the egg noodles with the broccoli and soup mixture. Next I covered that with 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese. For an added texture I doubled the panko breadcrumbs and margarine and baked as directed. It was delicious!! My picky kids even gave it two thumbs up and said they would eat it again! The only thing I might change from my alteration is that I would add another can of soup to the egg noodles before putting them in the bottom of the pan. YUMMY!!
