Well color me astonished! HOWEVER...my DH grew up poor, and actually prefers all things boxed and canned, and although he has "learned" (double hmph!) to love my homemade cooking,and kept asking for a "creamier" Mac n cheese. my kids were horrified at the idea of anything but grams recipe, but after reading through this one I thought it was the best of both worlds and would give it a try, and boy am I glad I did! it really tastes just like my grandma's, only better!
