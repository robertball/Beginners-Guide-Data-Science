Simple recipe and worked perfectly. A good amount for the tin size. My tip is to roll it out on cling film. Sweet crust can be quite delicate so this helps to pick it up and lay it in the tin and then gently peel off. I'm just about to make this again for a pumpkin pie (Libby's) but would be great for any type of pudding pie.
Also, leave the edges having over the tin. I trim them once it's cooked. There's no shrinkage then and you end up with a really neat edge.
