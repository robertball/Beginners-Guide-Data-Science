Phenomenal, I will make it a regular meal at my house!  I once had some Crab Jalapeno Bites at Phillips Seafood that were phenomenal and I've thought of them ever since - these were just like them!
I followed the recipe exactly (even measured) but didn't have whole wheat bread crumbs or chives so I used italian crumbs and red onion.  I baked at 400 for 30-35 min based on other reviews which seemed perfect.  Next time I would chop the shrimp to marble-size chunks (bigger than I did) to add variety to the texture.
I like making this with leftover salmon and frozen cocktail/salad shrimp as a shortcut.  A nice chopper (like Pampered Chef) also makes this very quick and easy.
