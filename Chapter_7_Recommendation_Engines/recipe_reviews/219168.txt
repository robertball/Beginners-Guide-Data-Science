Since I've made some minor adjustments I will only give this recipe 4 stars and comment on the version I make. We've been eating my adapted mild version regularly.

As my husband does not like spicy food, I did not use spicy peppers and adjusted by using green and red bell peppers. I also used two whole chicken legs instead of that many thighs and chorizo for the sausage. I decreased the overall amount of meat and used a little more onion and peppers.

The risotto technique is not necessary to achieve the same flavour results as you can also just toss in more of the broth in one go. I would recommend letting it sit there when almost done for at least 5-10 minutes. We prefer this dish the day after because the flavours have more time to infuse. Also doubles for a nice cold salad.
