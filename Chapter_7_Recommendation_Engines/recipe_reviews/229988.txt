I made this tonight for the first time in many years.  It's the exact same recipe I used to make from a cookbook that  was a wedding gift--40 years ago! Excellent recipe! This is my first time making Swiss Steak. I was, admittedly apprehensive about it. After reading lots of reviews, I decided to brown the meat with vegetable shortening (more shortening is needed than called for in the recipe) after mixing different seasonings (I did not measure) in flour in a ziplock bag. If spicy taste is wanted, add cayenne pepper (we like spicy foods). Shake meat in seasoned flour to coat, add to oil. Add browned meat to 9X13 casserole dish and top with bell pepper, onions and celery. Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350 degrees for approximately 1 1/2 or until meat is tender. I served with white buttered rice and mixed vegetables. DELICIOUS!
