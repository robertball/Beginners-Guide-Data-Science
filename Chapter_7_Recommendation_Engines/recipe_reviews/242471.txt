I recent;y made the conversion to a gluten free, grain free and  reduced dairy diet for health reasons and I've been experimenting with quite a few recipes. This is one of the first one's I've tried that actually satisfied my sweet tooth and craving for "cake/bread" like taste and texture. I made these as mini cupcakes by halving the entire recipe and baking for 12 minutes. It yielded about 24 mini cupcakes that were dense and chocolaty! I'm making this recipe again as regular sized cupcakes and will dust a little powdered sugar over them. I'll also add either a splash of almond milk or 1/4 cup of unsweetened applesauce and see how that goes.  I'm hoping the those changes will produce a mildly less dry and sweeter result. I suggest making it exactly as written once and then adjusting to your specific tastes. Great base for an awesome recipe. I also think a berry preserve type topping would really set this off. :)
