This is my recipe and I just wanted to mention this: Snickerdoodles are traditionally made with shortening and cream of tartar...this gives them a distinct taste and appearance.  You can substitute butter for the shortening and skip the tartar (which can produce a "tinny" taste) but it might also alter the shape of the cookie.  If you like a little more spice, add a little more cinnamon or some pumpkin pie spice.  **I wasn't intending to rate this recipe, just wanted to make this note.**
