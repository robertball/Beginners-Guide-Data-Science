I don't recall having ever had catfish before trying this recipe. My husband is from the South, and he LOOOOOVES catfish. I cut the basil down to about half and I omitted the white pepper. I only used a few grinds of pepper and two pinches of cayenne. He said it needed more cayenne, but I didn't want it too spicy for our daughter. I used three fillets totalling one pound and around two TBS each of butter and vegetable oil. The batter ended up pretty crusty, almost like if it had been deep-fried. He said it was pretty good (high praise coming from that fish-lover!) and my daughter and I enjoyed it too. Thanks for sharing, Traci!
