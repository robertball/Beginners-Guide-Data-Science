I used this as a base recipe to make a wonderful turkey pie to use up some more of our Christmas turkey.  I didn't really bother to measure stuff, but here's how it went: turkey on the bottom with a little gravy to mix in with it (I still had turkey broth left from boiling down the carcass and it made an easy gravy by adding in some cornstarch and salt), a layer of stuffing (I still had some dry stuffing mix in the pantry, so I mixed up 4 servings with about half an onion chopped up in it), a layer of steamed green beans, more gravy, home made mashed potatoes (about 5 medium red potatoes worth - I had to make this fresh as we'd used up the mashed potatoes shortly after Christmas) with some extra minced garlic and rosemary, and topped with parmesan and shredded cheddar.  I baked it at 350 for 40 minutes and it came out wonderfully!  We all loved it and this will definitely go on my list of recipes to make with holiday turkey.
