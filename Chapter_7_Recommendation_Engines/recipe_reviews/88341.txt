Really, really good and really, really easy.  I made the meatballs from scratch.  I used about 1.5 pounds of ground beef, 3/4 of a diced onion, 2 eggs, 1/2 cup bread crumbs, @ 3 teaspoons of diced garlic, 1 teaspoon italian seasoning, salt, pepper, a little onion powder and a little seasoning salt.  I baked them for 25 mins on a broiler pan so the fat would drip off.

Once I added the meatballs to the sauce (I originally made a half batch of sauce since I only made 1.5 lbs of meat) I decided I wanted more sauce so added another can of cream of celery and then added 2 beef bullion cubes and about half a packet of dry onion soup mix since I didn't have any more canned onion soup.  I also added one soup can full of water.  I cooked the whole pot on high for about 3.5 hours on high and it was really delicious.  The only thing we felt it needed was more salt.

We'll most definitely have this again.
