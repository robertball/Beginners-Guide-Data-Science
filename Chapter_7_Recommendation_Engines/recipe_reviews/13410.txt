I had some green tomatoes that I knew wouldn't ripen on the vine, now that it's the end of summer, so I found this recipe and we LOVE it!  I made some modifications based on the other reviews and here's what I came up with... a 'Mexican' version.  I put in three cloves of garlic, 1 1/2 tsp each of ground cumin, dried oregano, sweet Hungarian paprika and ground chipotle; 1 bay leaf, 1/2 cup (packed) each of fresh basil and parsley, pureed in the blender, and .   I added 11/2 cups of canned, diced tomatoes.  I used a 14 oz tin of black beans,  3 cups of frozen corn, 4 cups of chicken bouillon stock and a pinch of sugar.  It was just the right degree of 'hot' for us with the chipotle.  I added a breast of shredded chicken at the last minute (just popped it from the freezer into the pot to cook, then shredded it after).  We topped it with grated Tex-Mex cheese, cilantro and tortilla chips.  Amazing!!  Definitely a keeper! Thanks everyone for your great tips.
