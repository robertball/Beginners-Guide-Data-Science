This was really good.

I changed it up a couple of ways.  Instead of using ranch dressing and diced avacado, I used spicy guacamole. It was really spicy, but really good.

I also tried frying it.  If you wrap it up like folding two ends in, and the nfolding the others over it, you can slide a toothpick (soaked in water) in to hold it together.  Then, coat the bottom of a skillet in olive oil and put it on LOW heat.  If you get it scorching hot in will burn in less than 2 seconds!  Then, just drop in the little bundle and let it brown, flip it and let the other side brown.  When you take it out, you can remove the toothpick and it will stay together.

If you brown it, you can more easily wrap it in plastic and have it for lunch another day. :)
