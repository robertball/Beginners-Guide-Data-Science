Good tasting meatloaf. TO SOLVE THE "FALLING APART" PROBLEM YOU CAN:
1. DECREASE THE RICE BY 1/4 CUP
2. ADD EITHER 1/4 CUP OF BREAD CRUMBS OR TOASTED WHEAT GERM.
3. ADD ONE LARGE BEATEN EGG.
This should give you a firmer loaf suitable for slicing. I always double the recipe and have sliced and lightly fried (in olive oil) meatloaf the third day. My family actually prefers it this way and it is a great time-saving suggestion.
