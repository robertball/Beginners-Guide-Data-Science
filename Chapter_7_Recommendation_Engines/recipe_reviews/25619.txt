Yummy! However, beware, this is NOT a very sweet frosting. It is very fluffy and mild in flavor. Works well in offsetting the richness of a chocolate cake. If you are looking for a sugary sweet frosting, this may not be for you. I boiled my corn syrup in the microwave and it worked like a charm. Make sure you spread it on your cake right away, or it will not spread as smoothly (though it will still taste fantastic)! Also, this makes a lot of frosting. I used it for one 13X9 inch cake, laying it on very thick, and I still would have had plenty left over for another cake.
