I was a bit dismayed when I saw the price for the McCormick's Seasoning packet was $2.00 but I bought it anyway and I'm glad I did. The seasoning and brown sugar creates a "rub" that goes over the pork. Then o.j. is added to the remaining season to create a "sauce" for the potatos and apples. The seasoning's is a bit spicy but is offset by the mellowness of the potatos and sweetness of the apple. P TIP: the directions are a bit confusing. Mix all the seasoning with brown sugar. Then take about 1/4 C and rub on pork. The remaining 3/4 C seasoning/brown sugar is mixed with the O.J.
