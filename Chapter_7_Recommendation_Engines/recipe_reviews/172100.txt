I had some extras that I needed to get rid of so to the ingredients above I substituted 1 BubbaBurger and  I added one egg beaten and some chopped onion.  I put the mixture in a ceramic dish 5"Wide x7"Long x1"deep and put it into a 425 degree toaster oven for about 35 minutes (visually check) .
