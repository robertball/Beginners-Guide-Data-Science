I change things up a bit on this but use the overall idea of it.

I am allergic to corn, so instead of the Corn Starch, I use Tapioca flour.  I generally use the same amount as they say in the recipe.   You can give or take how much depending on the consistency you want.  I have found it to work well, although you will have to mix it in water (1/4-1/2 cup) first before you add it to the strawberries.   Also, Instead of fresh strawberries, I save myself A LOT of time by getting a 1 lb bag of frozen.
