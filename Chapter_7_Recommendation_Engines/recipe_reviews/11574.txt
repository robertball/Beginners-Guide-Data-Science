After baking these successfully last night, I have a few suggestions for everyone making these in order for them to turn out perfectly, as mine did:
-follow the recipe exactly
-use a glass 9x13" pan and grease it with cooking spray, thoroughly
-bake for 20-21 mins, 15 mins is NOT enough!
-bake them at night before bed and let them cool in the glass pan, partially covered, until morning.  Then cut and serve!
This is what I did for this recipe and it turned out great, chewy, sweet, and delicious.  The trick is the glass pan, cooking spray, longer cooking time, and overnight cooling.  Good luck!
