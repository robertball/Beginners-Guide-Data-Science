So far this is my favorite no mayo slaw recipe and I make it regularly. Really good as written, but I prefer less oil and less sugar - I have the oil down to about 1/8 of a cup and the sugar down to 4 Tbsp.  If it doesn't seem like enough liquid, I'll add more vinegar. These changes are only an attempt to make it healthier, not make it taste better. I usually use 1/2 large head of cabbage. Lately I've been using red cabbage, it looks more appealing than green cabbage - which turns a little brown from the soy.  Have also substituted sweet or yellow onion for the green onions.  The sunflower seeds give nice texture, we add them when serving so they don't get soggy and usually skip the almonds. The salad is good without seeds too. Also great as a sandwich topping. Wonder how this would be with some dried cranberries or cherries!
