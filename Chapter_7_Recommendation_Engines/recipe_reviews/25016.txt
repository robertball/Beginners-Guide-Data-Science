A dense and intense chocolate/coconut brownie! Will definitely make this again, with a few changes.  Because the recipe calls for unsweetened coconut, this makes the filling a bit dry.  As some others did, you could use sweetened coconut instead, or do a mixture of 1/2 and 1/2 which I will try next time.  Also, I would cut the amount of the coconut filling in almost half.  Unless you absolutely can't get enough coconut, it seemed a bit too much.  Might also add chocolate chunks, as well.  Decadent!!
