This was a great recipe! Made it 2 times for family dinners over the holiday and everyone loved it. I skipped marinating for 8 hours. The glaze was strong enough to hold its' own. I added enough water to my baking pan to cover the bottom pretty good prior to baking. Before adding the glaze, I baked a 7 lb ham for an 1 hr 15 Min. I then added the glaze and continued baking for another 1 hr 30 Min. Every 30 min I would take a spoon and cover the ham with the glaze until It was cooked. I let stand for about 20 minutes before slicing which I highly recommend. I did not add any of the sliced pineapples as the recipe called for either. This was personal preference because we do not like our meats too sweet. The 8 oz crushed pineapple in the glaze was perfect.
