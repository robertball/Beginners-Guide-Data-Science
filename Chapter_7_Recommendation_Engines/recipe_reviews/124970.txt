This was very tastey & easy.  I wanted to feed a few more people so, I made 1 1/2 times the recipe.  This fit perfectly in a 9x13 baking dish.  The basic recipe is a good 4 star recipe as is but, if you really want to bump it up to a 5 star... Add 1 medium onion chopped, 1 green bell pepper chopped, 1/2 lb cubed ham and some sage. It was taking longer to cook than I expected so after about 25 minutes, I bumped the temp up to 400 for 10 minutes, then to 450 for another 10 minutes and it came out fine.  It might have taken longer to cook because I increased the recipe size. Next time I'll just allow for extra cooking time in my schedule.
