This is a quick, easy and quite tasty guacamole recipe. These are the changes I made: I add the juice of half a lime (use a teaspoon of lime juice if you like), I use garlic seasoning instead of a clove of garlic (makes it easier), about a tablespoon or so of chopped onion, and a little cilantro. I don't go to the trouble of chopping cilantro - I just buy the little tube in the produce section and add about a teaspoon. I also make it without the cilantro and it is still quite tasty. Since I like my guacamole a little chunky, I just use a fork or potato masher to chop up the avocados.
