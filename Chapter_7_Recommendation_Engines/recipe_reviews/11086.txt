This cookie is a favorite of everyone who tries it and can get past the name.  Not overly sweet and the pepper intensifies the lovely cardamom flavor.  The dough is a bit crumbly to cut after it is refrigerated.  Leaving it out on the counter to warm 20 minutes or so does help.  Also, this is a very plain looking cookie.  I have brushed the tops of portions of the batch with milk and sprinkled with coarse, colored sanding sugar to make them fit a broader range of tastes.  Definitely try this recipe.  You will not be sorry.
