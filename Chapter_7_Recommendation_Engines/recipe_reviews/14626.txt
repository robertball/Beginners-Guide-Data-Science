I read the reviews first prior to preparing the Ground Beef Stroganoff and would like to share my minor adjustments.  Ingredients: 1 lb. ground beef 1/2 c. chopped onion 1 tsp. yellow mustard 1 tsp. butter 1/2 tsp. garlic powder 3/4 c. sour cream 1/4 c. mayonnaise  1. Heat skillet on medium-heat and place butter in it to melt. 2. Add ground beef and let it brown (season with salt, pepper and basil). Then add the chopped onions (season again). 3. While browning the ground beef and onions, take a bowl and add the sour cream, mayonnaise, yellow mustard, mix. 4. I served this for dinner on top of rice; it's very filling. My boyfriend thought it looked grotesque but very much enjoyed it and my best friend loved it.

I've been making this dish for many years but I use  1 1/2 to 2lbs. of ground beef, 1 diced med. size onion, I don't use mustard or mayo and I up the sour cream to between 3/4 and 1 cup depending on how many egg noodles and ground beef you use.  I also add garlic powder to taste or minced garlic.  I also use a 16 oz. bag of cooked egg noodles in mine.  Salt and pepper to taste.  I saut the onions and sliced fresh mushrooms (or canned drained mushrooms whichever you have on hand) in with my ground beef.  Drain the ground beef then add in your drained cooked noodles and stir in your sour cream last.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Makes for a fast, filling and delicious meal. I like to mix the noodles in with every thing else.  That way your noodles stay moist and don't clump together under your meat mixture and they don't dry out when storing the leftovers.
