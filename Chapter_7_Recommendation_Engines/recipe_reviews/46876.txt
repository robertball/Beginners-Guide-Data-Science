I'm really not sure how to rate this one. If I rate on taste alone it would get 5 stars. But appearance is easily 50% of the experience and it was UGLY! Maybe 2 stars for appearance.  I will make it again, but I need to work on the appearance. Maybe I won't puree the beans. I did make quite a few changes. Did not use the olive oil as I cooked the veggies in the bacon fat. I will DEFINATELY make this again. My kids hated it so next time I will add the cayenne.
