Absolutely delicious. Made a few changes since there were mixed reviews about the mayo topping and the lack of seasonings. The taste is great and the ingredients are solid.  The technique, however, is not.  The initial 15 minutes of baking at 400 degs overcooks and dries out the flounder.  5-8 minutes is plenty, as flounder is an extremely thin fish and bakes very quickly.  Add to that another 5-6 minutes at 450?  And it is PLENTY done.   A better way to ensure a cooked stuffing without drying out the fish is to pre-bake the stuffing for 10 minutes at 400F in a small bowl just prior to loading it onto the fish.  Your food will be safe, and the fish will remain moist.
