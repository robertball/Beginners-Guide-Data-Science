Very, very good! It doesn't have a prevalent "limey" taste so if you're not a big fan don't be afraid of this recipe. I seasoned my chops (I used boneless) w/ Paula Deen's house seasoning (salt, pepper, garlic powder),  & I seared them over med-high heat in a tbp or so of EVOO. Coated them w/ the glaze (adding the drippings in the pan) & baked them right in the skillet I seared them in (it has a heat-proof handle). I covered it w/ foil for the first 30 minutes & uncovered them for teh last 10. I just bought some lime marmalade...I'm eager to try this recipe again using lime marmalade & orange juice & slices. I served it w/ pinot grigio, a salad w/ vidalia onion vinaigrette & Garlic Asparagus w/ Lime (from thsi site) w/ Creamy Lime Sherbet & a brownie for dessert.
