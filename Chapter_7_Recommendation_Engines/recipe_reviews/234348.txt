These were scrumptious.  Taking the advice of another reviewer,  I marinated the chicken in the BBQ sauce for about eight hours.  When it was time to assemble the kabobs I quartered a large onion, separated the petals and sweated them in EVOO so they'd be tender.  I threaded marinated chicken, fresh pineapple, onion petals and chunks of nectarine on the skewers, brushed them with more BBQ and gave them to my husband to grill.  Did they ever stick to the grate, even after oiling it well!  He cooked the kabobs five minutes per side, just the right length of time for the chicken, but they were a mess to turn.  Next time we will not brush additional sauce on until after they've been turned, maybe even serve more sauce at the table.  But this combo of flavors is exceptionally tasty and we'll definitely make it again!
