After reading the reviews, I decided to give this cake a try. It was yummy and was a hit with the non-nut eaters in my family. The toughest part was getting the cake out of the pan, albeit I was using my sister-in-laws pans rather than mine. Next time I would use higher quality pans and line the bottom with wax paper.
