Here's what I did:  Baked them on 350 degrees for about 15 minutes; used whole wheat flour, no all purpose; added 2 TBSP of sugar; used about 1/2 cup of canola oil AND 1/2 cup of regular applesauce (Motts original, so I expect it has been sweetened); 2 TSP of vanilla. They are a heavier muffin, so don't expect something light and fluffy, but if you are looking for something yummy and substantial, this is a good one.
