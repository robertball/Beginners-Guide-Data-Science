Made this tonight for dinner because I wanted to try something sweet and I wasn't disappointed!  I was skeptical about baking the chicken in a foil-lined pan but it was fine; the chicken didn't stick because of the honey mixture creating a bed of juice at the bottom of the pan.  I took the advice of others and seasoned the chicken as well for the first 30 minute baking session.  I used garlic powder, pepper, and creole seasoning. I was also a 1/4 cup short of honey but I think that was for the better, as the chicken was just sweet enough!  I added the honey mixture as soon as it was done, so about the last 15 minutes of the first 30 minute session.  I probably cooked the chicken a few minutes longer than needed because it was a little overbrowned.  But it was still delicious!   This was quick and easy.  I will definitely make this again!
