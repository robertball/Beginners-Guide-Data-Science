A friend of mine asked for me to find a recipe for pork and bean bread and found this one. I have altered it a little. I use 1/2 cup of unsweetened applesauce and 1/2 cup of oil. I also use a blender for all the wet ingredients and blend into the dry ingredients. It does not look pretty. I also add nutmeg, a pinch in with the other spice.  When it comes out of the oven and has been taken out of the pans to finish cooling, I add a little brown sugar on top to form a crust.  I have made this recipe at least 12 times in the last month and it has been a hit so far. I have seen other reviews on here that they did not like, if you are a true baker, you have to modify the recipes some to make it your own.
