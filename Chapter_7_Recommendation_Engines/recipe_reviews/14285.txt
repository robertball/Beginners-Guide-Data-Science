WOW!!  This salad definitley deserves 5 stars. The dressing is out of this world. I did add 1 tablespoon of sesame oil and 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds to the dressing and decreased the amount of oil to 1/3 cup. I put the dressing in a glass jar and shook it well. I bet rotisserie chicken tossed in would be a great addition too.  Do not add the dressing unitl right before your ready to serve, otherwise it will become soggy. I give this salad my highest recommendation.

I made this salad for a gathering of about 40 people and everyone raved.  It took about an hour to prepare the way I did it.  I thinly sliced 2 large heads of green cabbage.  I used 6 bunches of green onions.  I chopped a 15 oz. container of whole almonds (because I had them in the house) and toasted them in melted butter in a large stainless fry pan.  Put them in a large bowl then melted some more butter in the same pan and stirred in 5 packages of crushed ramen noodles with the chicken flavor packets sprinkled over them, and cooked until toasted. Poured the dressing over everything and tossed it a bit and it was fabulous!
