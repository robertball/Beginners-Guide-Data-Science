Used lean ground turkey instead of beef, 1/4 cup dry quinoa (cooked before adding to mix) instead of breadcrumbs or panko, and only 1/2 cup parm cheese in the mix and 1/2 cup parm cheese on top of the tomato sauce I poured on top.
