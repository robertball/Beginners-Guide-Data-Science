I normally dont change recipes too much, but this recipe was such a great base that I got creative!  I had some leftover shredded zucchini that I needed to use up when I came across this recipe; 1C Zucchini and 1C shredded carrots. In place of the sugar, I used 1C Splenda and 1/4C brown sugar.  I used 1C whole wheat flour and 1- 1/4C all purpose flour.  In place of raisins I used half raisins and half dried cherries.  I also added a dash each of ground cloves and ground nutmeg.  I then glazed them with clear icing and sprinkled toasted coconut.  They turned out moist and tasted fabulous!
