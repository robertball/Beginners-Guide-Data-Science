This is a clever way to enjoy pasta and potatoes.  I made a few simple modifications that made it a bit healthier and added flavor.  

When cooking the onions I used 2T of olive oil and a 1T of butter.  When I made the potatoes I added a 1T of dried spinach mix and used 3/4 of the cream cheese called for. I made sure it was a lower fat cream cheese too.  I did add garlic salt and some Parmesan cheese to add more interest. 

My favorite variation was at the end of baking.  I finished the dish under the broiler to caramelize the onions.  If you think this recipe looks tasty, I highly recommend this last step.
