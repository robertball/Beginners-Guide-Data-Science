After deciding the soup was much too sweet, I decided to change it up. Rather than cook the sweet potato before integrating the savoury ingredients, I peeled and chopped it up into chunks. I then sauted garlic, onion, coriander, and green onions. I added the sweet potato, then the chicken broth and cooked it until it was soft enough to puree. I added the whole thing to a blender..made into a pure texture..took it back to the stove and added the rest of the spices (nutmeg, salt, pepper, and only 2 tablespoons of brown sugar). I let it simmer..added the cream and served.  Much better!!
