If you don't have a grill, this marinade is also good for baking chicken in the oven.  I put the marinade in a ziploc with frozen chicken and let it thaw and marinate overnight. The next day I put it all in a pan, wrapped the chicken with turkey bacon, topped it with cheddar cheese, and cooked it at 375 for about 30 minutes and then broiled for 1 or 2 mins to make the bacon crispy. Served w/baked potatoes & green beans.  Good stuff.
