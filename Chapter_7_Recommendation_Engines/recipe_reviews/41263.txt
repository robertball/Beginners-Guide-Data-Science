I followed everything except the broth and chili seasoning. I used dried Arbol (spicy and wholesome) and pulla (spicy and smoky) in a blended mix. I also used red green and yellow bell pepper for some added veggies.  I then doubled the cumin and garlic powder and added more salt and pepper directly on the beef and veggies since the packet seasoning was absent. Diced tomatoes were added with a swirl of Worcestershire sauce with the chile sauce before I covered the final mixture and slow cooked it for 4 hours.
