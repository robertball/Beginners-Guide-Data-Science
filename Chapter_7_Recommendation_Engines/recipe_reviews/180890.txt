1 can cranberries, whole Ocean Spray
1 jar Spicy Chili Sauce, Heinz
1 cup Ketchup, Heinz
1/2 cup onion, diced
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
2 tablespoons liquid smoke flavoring
2 cups brown sugar, packed
1 1/2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
1 cup whiskey, Sour Mash/Bourbon reduced
