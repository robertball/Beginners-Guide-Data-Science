Okay, here's the deal.  I read most of the reviews, as I usually do, and modified the recipe as follows:   First, I added three tablespoons of sour cream to the cream cheese/ranch blend.  Next, I added a bit more barbecue sauce than the recipe called for.  I like barbecue sauce.  I mean, I wouldn't drink it or anything, but I do like it, and a bit extra didn't hurt.  Third, don't be afraid to add the amount of toppings called for.  It might seem like a lot, but trust me, it's not.  So be generous with the bacon and veggies.  One can never have too much cheese of course.  I added some green onions on top for color and a bit more taste.  Again, be generous.

Now... this is important... the dip needs a sturdy chip, so I used Fritos.  They hold up better than tortilla chips, and you'll appreciate that.  Someone suggested putting a knife or spoon out so people could use this as a spread, which would be easier than dipping the Fritos.  Either way works... spread would be easier if you don't add the sour cream.

Make this recipe.  It's easy.  And... bacon.  And cheese.  I mean, seriously?  Why are you still reading this?  Get off your butt and make it!
