Every now and then when I get bored I head over to my boyfriend's place to make something and I found this recipe and was inspired. They were amazing! We didn't use olives though, because I am not a big fan of them and instead of using a can of chilies we bought 5 or so Anaheim chilies and de-seeded them and cut them up pretty small and mixed them into the cream cheese mixture. My boyfriend is a big meat lover though so we bought a pack of ham and placed them on the tortillas with the mixture and we LOVED them. We only had enough ham to make a few though. One of his room mates ate one of them as a wrap before i sliced them up and said it was heaven. 
I loved these, but did think it was too much without the meat. Will definitely make them again, but probably with the ham again next time. They went quick with 4 boys! We were worried it wouldn't make enough, but this recipe made plenty!
