I've made this 3 times now, the first 2 following the recipe except that I omitted the red bell pepper and used 14.5 oz canned cut green beans.   The 3rd time I made it I realized, too late, that I had forgotten to buy the cream of mushroom soup.   I googled for substitute and replaced the soup by using the following.  

1 1/2 tbs butter
1 tbs flour
1/4 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup milk
4 diced fresh cremini mushrooms (stems removed)
Melt butter, add flour and whisk till nutty smell. 2-3 min over med heat. Whisk in milk & broth and bring to boil, whisking till thick. Add mushrooms and set aside.

I also combined crushed Ritz crackers as well as the French fried onions for the topping.   The recipe as it stands is wonderful, but I thought substituting the above when time to normally add soup gave the dish a deeper flavor.

The above is similar to a bechamel or blond roux depending on how long you cook it before adding the milk & broth, but I'm tempted to try it next by making a darker roux...and YES, the bacon is a MUST!
