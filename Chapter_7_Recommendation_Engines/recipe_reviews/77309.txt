Awesome, decadent, creamy banana pudding!!

To the reviewer who said hers was too "soupy" - did you follow the five-minute chilling step for the pudding?  

The instructions say to mix the pudding mix, condensed sweetened milk, and water - and then to chill the mixture in the fridge for five minutes before proceeding.  This is a critical step!  When I make this pudding, the initial mixture is soupy (even after I beat it with a hand mixer for 2 minutes) - but by the time it comes out of the fridge 5 minutes later, it is much thicker.  That five-minutes of chilling time really does make a difference.  And when I subsequently fold in the whipped cream (which I prepare as the pudding mixture is chilling in the fridge), the pudding becomes even thicker.

I follow this recipe to a "T" and it turns out great every time.  

It's pretty heavy (calorie and fat-gram wise) but so, so delicious and decadent - perfect for a once-in-while special family dessert.
