Made this for Christmas eve for friends who can't eat red meat.  It was a hit!  I used canned pumpkin and added 1 can of kidney beans.  The longer this cooks and sets, the thicker it gets which is how I like my chili.  Could add more chili powder or 1/4 t. of cayenne for a hotter tasting chili.
