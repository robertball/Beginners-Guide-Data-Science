Leaving town in the morning, so of course I cleaned out the fridge. I wasn't planning on cooking, but hubby tells me at the last minute he would like some dinner! Hmmm, half a lemon and white wine in the fridge, some garlic, lots of pasta in the pantry, shrimp in the freezer, and fresh parsley in the garden!  Score! Just a few simple ingredients and I just put a dinner on the table fit for a king!  Couldn't be simpler. Couldn't be quicker.  Couldn't be prettier, and couldn't be more delicious.  Perfect.  I sauteed the shrimp in a mixture of butter and olive oil, used white wine instead of broth, and slowly swirled in a little extra butter at the end which made a silky, beautiful sauce.  Wow--took only as long as it took the pasta to cook and a to-die-for dinner was on the table!  And hubby thought I worked so hard...
