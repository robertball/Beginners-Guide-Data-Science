Amazing recipe! Modified it to be gluten-free by changing the topping and only used 4 cups of zucchini. First, drain the zucchini: peel and chop into 1"-2" pieces.  Put the zucchini pieces in a colander in the sink and sprinkle salt onto it. I added a whole cup of sugar to the zucchini mixture.  I only added 1.5 cups of sugar to the crust, but it's still massively too sweet.  I actually find it too sweet to enjoy much at any one time.
