Due to the other "bland" reviews we did the following:  Run your hand between the skin and meat of the chicken on both sides, loosening the skin from the meat(making like a little pocket).  Rub a little salt and pepper on your hands and rub over the meat to season.  Place FRESH rosemary stems and lemon slices on the meat and then stretch the skin over and secure with a toothpick or two.  I cut tow lemons in half and placed inside the cavity instead of the veggies.  The flavor is very lemony with a nice hint of the rosemary.  The smell is wonderful!
