Made these for easter! I bought neon and regular gel food coloring because it was all I could find and the colors turned out amazing! For people who are into Reiki, this can be a Chakra Cake! I used 3 eight inch round cake pans and a double batch of white cake mix. I weighed and split the batter into 7 medium sized pyrex cups. I used the gel coloring. Because I've had so many requests for this cake, I acquired 7 empty (and thouroughly cleaned) liquid dish detergent bottles (with the pull open, push close caps). I place each of the colored batter mixes in its own bottle, then gently squeeze it into the 3 cake pans ( 3 colors in the 1st pan, then 2 colors in the other 2 pans). To get a good distinction between the colors, you want to add the colors on top of each other gently, as too much pressure will result in the colors 'blobbing' into each other. The detergent bottles can be used over and over again. Please note that the cake pan with the 3 colors will cook longer than the other (2 color) pans.
