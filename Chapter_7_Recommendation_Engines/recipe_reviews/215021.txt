On par with several local restaurants, I recommend using a tiny pinch of ground cloves in place of the 2 whole cloves, as they disappear into the dish and can be quite unsavory when you find them in your mouth. Similar situation with the cardamom pods.
To replicate the rice made by most indian restaurants, use 2 cups basmati rice with 1/2 tsp of whole cumin seeds and 1 tsp of salt and a dash of onion powder. With a pad of butter toast the rice, cumin, salt, and onion powder until some of the rice is a light golden color, add 4 cups water, bring to boil, reduce to a very light simmer until done (15 minutes).
Also goes great with homemade naan bread.
