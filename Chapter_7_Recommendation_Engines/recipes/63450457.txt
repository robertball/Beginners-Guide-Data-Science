Title:
Simple Macaroni And Hamburger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/939787.jpg
Ingredients:
1 & 1/2 cup macaroni
1 lb hamburger
1 - 10.5 oz. can french onion soup
1 - 14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 - 14 oz. can beef stock
1/4 tsp chopped garlic
1/2 tsp Italian seasonings
2 - cups of water
salt and pepper to taste
1 - celery stock chopped (optional)
2 - cubes of beef bouillon
2 - tsp of worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Brown hamburger in olive oil then drain Toss together in a  crock pot Add onion soup, tomatoes, and beef stock. Cook macaroni as per package directions,put in pot about 30 mins before serving. Warm and eat!
