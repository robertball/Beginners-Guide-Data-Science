Title:
Reindeer Poop Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887317.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package white chocolate, coarsely chopped
1 (4 ounce) bar German sweet chocolate, chopped
1 (16 ounce) package semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 (16 ounce) jars dry roasted peanuts

Directions:
Place the white chocolate, German sweet chocolate, chocolate chips, and peanuts into a slow cooker.
Set the cooker to Low, cover, and gently heat the candy for 1 1/2 hours without stirring.
After 1 1/2 hours, stir the mixture, and spoon out by teaspoons onto waxed paper. Allow to cool and set, about 45 minutes.
