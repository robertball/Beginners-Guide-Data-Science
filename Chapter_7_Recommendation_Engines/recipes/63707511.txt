Title:
Sumalee's Happy Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Soba Noodles
2 T Sesame Oil
2 T Brown Sugar
1/4 C Rice Vinegar
1/2 C Soy Sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Lime zest and juice
1 pinch red pepper flakes
1/2 C Cilantro, chopped
3/4 C Carrots, matchbok or shredded
Sesame Seeds (optional)
Bean Sprouts (optional)

Directions:
Mix Oil, Brown Sugar, Vinegar, Soy Sauce, Lime juice Lime Zest, Garlic, and Red Pepper
Cook Soba noodles according to package directions and drain
Note: You can substitute Sesame for Chili Oil.  You can also vary ingredients according to your taste.  I like more vinegar and more red pepper but others may not.
