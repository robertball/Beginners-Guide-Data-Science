Title:
Indonesian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts-bite size pieces
3 Tbsp olive oil
10 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp ginger root, minced
1 small onion, chopped
2 cups green beans, cut in bite size pieces
2 Tbsp lime juice
1 tsp molasses
2 tsp soy sauce
2 tsp brown sugar
2 tsp turmeric
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup water

Directions:
1. Stir fry chicken pieces in 2 Tbsp oil until chicken turns white.  Set aside.
2. Stir fry garlic, ginger root, and onion in 1 Tbsp oil.
3. Boil green beans until tender, but still crisp. Add green beans to onion mixture and mix.
4. Mix together lemon juice, molasses, soy sauce, brown sugar, turmeric and 1/4 cup water.  Add to green bean mixture.  Slowly add remaining water if necessary, but sauce should not be watery.  Add chicken and mix well.  Serve over rice.
