Title:
Jack Rose Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ice
2 fluid ounces applejack (apple brandy)
1 fluid ounce fresh lemon juice
1/2 fluid ounce grenadine

Directions:
Pour ice into a cocktail shaker. Pour in applejack, lemon juice, and grenadine. Shake well, then strain into a cocktail glass.

Submitter: 
Lorem Ipsum
Description: 
Another great cocktail for autumn. Or any time. Applejack and fresh lemon juice with a bit of grenadine.
