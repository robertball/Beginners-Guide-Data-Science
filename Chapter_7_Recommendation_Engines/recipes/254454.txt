Title:
Peanut Butter Yogurt Dip from Yoplait® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3860325.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) container Yoplait® Original French Vanilla yogurt
2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
1 medium apple, cored and sliced

Directions:
Eat a tablespoon or two of yogurt out of the yogurt cup. Stir peanut butter in with a fork until smooth. Serve with apple slices for dipping.

Submitter: 
Yoplait
Description: 
A super easy 3-ingredient dip served right from the yogurt cup that makes a great after school snack or breakfast on-the-go.
