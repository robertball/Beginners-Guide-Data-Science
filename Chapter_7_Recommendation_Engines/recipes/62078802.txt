Title:
Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. pasta
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 6 oz. can tuna, drained
1 15 oz. can sweet peas, drained
1 tsp. accent
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions with salt.  Drain and remove from heat.  Add all remaining ingredients.  Mix well.  Serve immediately.
This dish may also be prepared ahead and baked for 10-15 minutes at 350 degrees until heated through.

Submitter: 
Carla Woodard
Description: 
Tuna, cream of chicken, and sweet peas make this a quick fix for cooks on the go.
