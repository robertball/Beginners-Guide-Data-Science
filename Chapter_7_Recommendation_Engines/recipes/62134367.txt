Title:
Ultimate Mussels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 shallots diced finely
4 cloves garlic pressed
2 tbs butter
3 tbs olive oil
2 pounds mussels (debeared and scrubbed)
~2 cups café zinfandel wine
the juice from 1 lemon
2 roma tomatoes chopped
Kosher salt
Fresh cracked Black pepper
Fresh Parsley
Scallions

Directions:
In a stock pot, sauté the shallots and garlic in the butter/olive oil for a minute.  Add the wine.  Once it comes back up to a boil, add the mussels.  Cover and steam for 3-4 minutes.  Add the tomatoes, lemon juice, salt and pepper, stir.  Cover and simmer another 3-4 minutes or until mussels are opened.  Taste the broth and adjust seasoning, stir.  Ppour into a large serving bowl discarding any mussels that failed ot open, and garnish with parsley and scallions.  The broth goes great with crusty bread!

Submitter: 
Brad
Description: 
This recipe combines the simplicity of steamed mussels, with the acidity of the lemon and wine.  The broth is so good it could be eaten as a soup.
