Title:
Ramen Noodle sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 teaspoon of rice wine vinegar
1 drop of sesame oil
Pinch of salt
1/4 of a lemon (squeezed)
1 garlic clove

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients together and pour over soy sauce. If you are a fan of sesame. Toast some real ones and pour over noodles.

Submitter: 
belle nourriture
Description: 
A delicious substitute for the classic chicken or chili sauce.
