Title:
RITZ PB and Popcorn Blockbuster Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4506127.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups popped popcorn
1 1/2 ounces white baking chocolate, melted
24 RITZ Crackers
2 tablespoons peanut butter
1 ounce semisweet baking chocolate, melted

Directions:
Place popcorn in medium bowl. Drizzle with half the white chocolate; mix lightly. Repeat with remaining white chocolate.
Spread crackers with peanut butter; place on parchment-covered baking sheet. Top with popcorn.
Drizzle with semi-sweet chocolate.

Submitter: 
RITZ Crackers
Description: 
Ritz crackers are topped with popcorn tossed in white chocolate and drizzled with melted semi-sweet chocolate--a sweet treat anytime!
