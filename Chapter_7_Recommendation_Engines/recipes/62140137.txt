Title:
Sweet Onion Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large sweet Vidalia onions, coursley chopped
2 to 3 large garlic cloves, minced
2 Tbsp butter
2 cups shredded extra-old cheddar cheese
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 tsp hot pepper sauce

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute onions and garlic in butter until tender and somewhat carmelized, about 15 minutes. Remove from heat.
Stir in cheese, mayonnaise and hot pepper sauce.
Transfer to an ungreased 1 1/2 quart baking dish.
Bake at 475 degrees for 20 - 25 minutes or unil lightly browned.
Serve warm with crackers and sliced baguette.

Submitter: 
KerriJ
Description: 
Great warm carmelized onion dip for chilly nights in front of the fire!
