Title:
Honey Roasted Pork Loin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. pork loin (boneless)
to taste salt and pepper
1/4 cup honey
2 Tbsp. orange juice
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 teaspoon thyme
1/2 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Season the pork and place in a roasting pan.
In a separate bowl, mix together the honey, juice, oil and thyme.
Pour over the pork.
Add the broth to the pan.
Bake until the internal temperature reaches 150 (45-60 minutes).
Baste frequently.
Strain the pan juices into a saucepan.
Reduce until slightly thickened.
Serve over the sliced pork.
