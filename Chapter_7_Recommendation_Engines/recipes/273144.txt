Title:
Prune Breakfast Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6085170.jpg
Ingredients:
25 pitted prunes
1 cup rolled oats
1 cup unsweetened shredded coconut

Directions:
Combine prunes, oats, and coconut in a food processor. Process until prunes soften and mixture comes together like a dough. Roll into bite-sized pieces.

Submitter: 
Sarabeth Emet
Description: 
Prune and oat breakfast add-on. Keep refrigerated.
