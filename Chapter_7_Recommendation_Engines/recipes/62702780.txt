Title:
Laura's Blackened Chicken Caeser Salad Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/611732.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (6 ounce) boneless/skinless chicken breasts
N'Orleans Cajun Seasoning by Weber (in spice section)
9 large pieces of Romaine lettuce chopped
1/4 cup of diced red onion
1/4 cup Caeser Salad Dressing (I like Newman's)
3 T. Parmesan
4 Medium Flour Tortillas

Directions:
Sprinkle each side of chicken breast with blackened seasoning.  (More or less on your taste preference)
Grill or pan saute chicken breasts.  Slice into thin strips once done.
Mix chopped lettuce, salad dressing, onion, and parmesan in bowl.
Place 1/4 of salad mix in center of each tortilla, top with blackened chicken strips.  Fold up bottom of tortilla and roll in sides.  Secure with toothpick if necessary.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Laura
Description: 
A tasty, light yet filling wrap that is filled with flavor.  Great for lunch or dinner.
