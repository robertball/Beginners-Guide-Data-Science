Title:
My World Famous Pressure Cooker Chinese Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Kg Pork Ribs
1T Garlic Powder
3T Paprika
1/2 t Black Pepper
1/2 t salt
2 T Oil
3 Cups water
3/4 cups ketchup
4 T Brown Sugar
1/4 cup Chinese vinegar
1 t Worcestershire Sauce

Directions:
Put dry ingredients in zipper bag.
Place ribs in bag and shake thoroughly until all ribs are coated.
Place ribs in a oiled wok
Brown ribs.
Add liquid ingredients to pressure cooker.
Add ribs
Cook over high heat until pressure vents
Reduce heat to medium and cook for 15 minutes.
Remove from heat and reduce pressure.
Serve with fried rice and/or noodles
