Title:
Green Tea Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4547050.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vanilla ice cream
3/4 cup brewed green tea
3 tablespoons white sugar
2 drops green food coloring (optional)
12 cubes  ice cubes

Directions:
Combine the ice cream, green tea, sugar, food coloring, and ice cubes in a blender. Blend on high until smooth.
