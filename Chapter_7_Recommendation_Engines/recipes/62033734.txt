Title:
Beef BBQ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb. ground beef
1 qt. celery, put through blender
1 large onion, chopped
salt & pepper to taste
3 C. chili sauce
2 C. catsup
1-1/4 C. brown sugar
1/3 C. vinegar
1/8 C. mustard
1/4 C. worchestershire sauce
4 bay leaves

Directions:
Brown ground beef with onion and celery until meat is browned. Add remaining ingredients. Put into dutch oven or roaster and simmer for one hour. Remove bay leaves before serving.
NOTE: After beef is browned and other ingredients are added, this may be done in a large crock pot or slow cooker for 8 hours on low.
