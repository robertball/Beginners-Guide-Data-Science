Title:
Easy Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Aunt Jessica's Stuffed Peppers
Makes 6 servings
Six peppers -  any color, washed well
One pound ground sirloin
One package microwavable brown rice-two containers per package
1 cup of Ragu Roasted Garlic Spaghetti sauce
2 tsp chopped mint
3 tsp  of Cavendar's No Salt Greek Seasoning
1/2 Vidalia onion finely chopped
1/2 cup finely chopped mushrooms
Open both containers of rice; add to meat, mint, onion and mushroom, mix well.
Pour Spaghetti sauce over mixture and mix well.  Cut the top of the peppers  and save tops - clean pepper seeds and veins out of the peppers.   Place peppers in an oval crock pot  Fill peppers generously with mixture. Replace the tops. Cover crock pot with lid.  Add 1/3 cup of water and set on low for 4 to six hours.
