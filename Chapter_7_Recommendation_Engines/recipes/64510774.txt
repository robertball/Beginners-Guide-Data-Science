Title:
Zucchini Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium zucchini, peeled and cut into chunks
1 small onion, diced
1 10oz can cream of chicken or cream of mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
1 cup grated carrots
1 package Stove Top Stuffing mix, any flavor, unprepared
2 tbsp. butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
Cook zucchini and onion in boiling water, till tender. Drain.
Add soup, sour cream and carrots.
Mix dry stuffing mix with melted butter.
Place 1/2 stuffing mix in bottom of greased casserole dish.
Top with the zucchini mixture.
Put remaining stuffing mixture over zucchini layer.
Bake covered @ 350 x 30 min.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Bread stuffing adds a nice flavor and texture to this prolific garden vegetable.
