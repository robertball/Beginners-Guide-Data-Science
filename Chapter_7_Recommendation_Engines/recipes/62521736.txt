Title:
Toasted Kale Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of washed Kale greens
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1/2 teaspoon sea or kosher salt
pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
.  Thoroughly wash Kale greens, and spread out on a greased baking sheet.
2.  Using a pastry brush or spoon, spread olive oil over each leaf.
3.  Sprinkle salt over leaves
4.  Pepper to taste
5.  Bake for 7-10 minutes, or until leaves are crisp and crunchy at edges
