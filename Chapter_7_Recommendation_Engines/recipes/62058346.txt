Title:
Chunky Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 to 6 pounds chopped, boiled potatoes
6 boiled eggs, chopped
2 celery stalks, chopped
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup Thousand Island dressing
3/4 cup mayonaisse or more to taste
2 tablespoons white vinegar
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 heaping tablespoon honey mustard
2 tablespoon relish
1 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
paprika

Directions:
Place potatoes, chopped eggs, onion and celery in large mixing bowl.  In seperate bowl combine together, Thousand Island dressing, mayonaisse,vinegar, sugar, mustard, relish, seasoned salt, pepper and garlic powder until well blended.  Pour dressing mixture over potato mixture and blend well.  Transfer to container, sprinkle with paprika, cover and refrigerate 4 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Graden
Description: 
I get recipe requests for this potato salad every time I make it.  I came up with this recipe when all the others seemed to lack something.
