Title:
Southern Style Banana Split Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups graham cracker crumbs;
3/4 cup white sugar;
1/4 pound butter, melted;
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese;
1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar;
1 t. vanilla;
4-5 bananas, sliced;
1 (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained (reserve juice);
1 pint strawberries, sliced;
1/4-1/2 c. sugar;
1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed;
1 (16 ounce) jar maraschino cherries, drained;
12 ounces crushed peanuts

Directions:
Combine the graham cracker crumbs, white sugar and melted butter. Mix together and press into a 9x13 inch cake pan; refrigerate until chilled or freeze for a few hours to make spreading cream cheese easier.
Soak bananas in pineapple juice for a bit to prevent browning.
Sprinkle sugar on sliced strawberries and mix.
Beat together the cream cheese, vanilla and confectioners sugar; spread over graham cracker crust.
Layer bananas, strawberries and pineapple over cream cheese mixture; cover fruit with whipped topping.
Top with cherries, chocolate shavings and chopped nuts; refrigerate overnight and serve chilled.
