Title:
Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 fresh chicken breast
4 tablespoons cream cheese
8 tablespoons shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup chopped fresh spinach
4 tablespoons sun-dried tomatoes
2 cloves garlic minced
2 teaspoons onion powder
salt and pepper
4 tablespoons olive oil - for the baking pan

Directions:
slice each  chicken breast in half but keep attached on one seam
place 1 tablespoon of cream cheese in each breast
add the rest of the ingredients dividing among the breasts...season inside the breast as well as on top
fold each breast  over ..Stick 2 tooth picks through to hold the sides shut
in 8x8 pan place oil...and the 4 stuffed breast
cover with tinfoil
Bake 375* for 30-40 min
