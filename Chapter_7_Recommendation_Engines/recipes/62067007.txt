Title:
Alfredo Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 CUPS EVENLY SLICED POTATOES (FOOD PROCESSER WORKS GREAT)
2 CUPS ALFREDO SAUCE ( FRANCESCO RINALDI)
1/2 CUP EVAPORATED MILK
1/2 TSP. GARLIC PEPPER
1/2 TSP. THYME
1/4 TSP. ONION POWDER
1/2 CUP ASIAGO/PARMESAN CHEESE
1/2 CUP CHEDDAR CHEESE

Directions:
SPRAY 8X8 BAKING DISH WITH PAM. BLEND ALFREDO SAUCE,MILK,GARLIC PEPPER,ONION POWDER AND THYME. SPOON A THIN LAYER IN BOTTOM OF PAN. LAYER WITH 1/2 OF POTATOES. COVER WITH 1/3 OF SAUCE AND 1/2 CUP CHEESE. LAYER OF REMAINING POTATOES,SAUCE. BAKE 55 MINUTES. ADD REMAINING CHEESE FOR LAST 15 MINUTES.

Anita Cornett

A  DELICIOUS CHEESY POTATOE SIDE DISH THAT WILL ACCOMPANY ANY MEAL.
