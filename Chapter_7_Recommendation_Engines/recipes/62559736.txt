Title:
Riced Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535567.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower grated-as in on a cheese grater
1/8 cup olive oil
1 cup salsa
2 green onions- sliced
Salt/pepper

Directions:
Put oil in a large saute pan, add cauliflower cook about 10 min. Add in salsa and green onions.
