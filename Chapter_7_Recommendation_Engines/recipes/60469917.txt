Title:
Rudolph's Tipsy Spritzer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups orange juice
2 cups chilled lemon-lime soda
1/2 cup maraschino cherry juice
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1-1/2 cups vodka
garnish with lemon slices and fresh rosemary sprigs

Directions:
Stir in all ingredients; serve over ice. Garnish if desired.
