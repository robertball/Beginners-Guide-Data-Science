Title:
Best Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups tomato juice
4 cup chopped tomatoes
1 1/2 cups chopped cucumber peeled and seeded
1 cup chopped green pepper
1/2 cup chopped yellow pepper
1/2 cup chopped red onion
1 tbsp. basil
1 tbsp. oregano
2 cloves garlic
1-1 1/2 tbsp. of lime juice
1 tsp. worchestershire sauce
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/4 - 1/2 tsp. red pepper sauce

Directions:
In a blender combine 1 1/2-2 cups tomato juice, 2 cups tomatoes, 1/2 cup cucumber, 1/2 cup green pepper, 1/4 cup red onion, basil, oregano, garlic, lime juice, worchestershire, pepper, and hot sauce.  Blend until liquified.  Pour into large bowl and add remaining ingredients.  Mix together and chill for a few hours or overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Carolyn
Description: 
The is a tasty cold soup that is fresh and healthy.  Perfect as and appetizer for those summer night dinners or for a refreshing lunch on a hot day.
