Title:
Soapapillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/797828.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons shortening
3/4 cup water, warm
4 cups canola oil, for frying

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, salt and shortening. Stir in water; mix until dough is smooth. Cover and let stand for 20 minutes.
Roll out on floured board until 1/8" to 1/4" thick. Cut into 3 inch squares. Heat oil in deep-fryer to 375Â°. Fry until golden brown on both sides. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with honey.
