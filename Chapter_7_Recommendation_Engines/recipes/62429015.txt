Title:
Z's Blueberry Peach Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/449735.jpg
Ingredients:
3 C Flour
1 t Cinnamon
1 t Baking Soda
1 t Salt
1 C Applesauce
1/4 C Vegetable Oil
3 Eggs (lightly beaten)
1 t Vanilla
1/2 C Milk
1 C Brown Sugar
1/2 C sugar
2 C  Fresh peaches (chopped)
1 C Blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees and grease or line muffin tin.
Combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda and salt in a large mixing bowl.
In a separate mixing bowl, mix together applesauce, oil, eggs, vanilla, milk, sugar and brown sugar. After combined, add to dry ingredients, then fold in peaches and berries.
Bake in standard size muffin tin 20-25 minutes at 400 degrees
