Title:
Slow Cooker Chili Mac Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground Turkey
2 can chili beans, Undrained
1 green pepper chopped
1 large onion chopped
4 stalks of celery chopped
28 oz can tomato sauce
1T. Chili powder
1 tsp. Cumin
1 tsp. minced garlic
8  oz package of whole wheat macaroni
1 C of cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. In Skillet cook ground turkey with onion until browned.
2. Add cooked meat, beans, vegetables, tomato sauce, and spices to slow cooker.
3. Cook for 6-8 hours on low
4. Cook macaroni in a saucepan add chili before serving.
5. Top with cheese.
Note:  Macaroni can be cooked ahead of time.  Boil until al dente, drain, and then put it in a dish with 1T. olive oil and refrigerate until ready to add to crockpot.
