Title:
Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. ground beef
3/4 c. chopped onions
12 oz. can of tomato sauce
1 T. parsley
2 t. salt (can be either garlic, celery or onion salt if desired)
1 t. garlic powder
1/2 t. pepper
1/2 package lasagna noodles (6 noodles)
1 large container of small curd cottage cheese
2 large packages of mozzarella cheese
Parmesean cheese to taste

Directions:
Brown beef and onion in a pan. Drain.
Add sauce, parsley, salt, garlic and pepper.
Simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes.
Meanwhile, cook the noodles.
Layer as so; meat, noodles, meat, cottage cheese, mozzarella cheese (1 package), parmesean cheese, noodles, meat, mozzarella cheese. Then finish off with more parmesean cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40-50 minutes. Cover for the first 40 minutes, then remove cover for the last 10-15 minutes.
Let stand for 15 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
Mom's lasagna never lasted more than one night, even though she made a 9x13 pan. I'm more than pleased to now be able to share it with others.
