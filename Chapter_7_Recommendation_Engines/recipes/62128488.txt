Title:
Mexican Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cooked cubed chicken
2 cans mexi-corn
2 cans rotel tomatoes
2 cans chili beans
2 cans yellow hominy
1 can diced tomatoes
2 packages dry ranch dressing mix

Directions:
Do not drain off liquid from any of the canned items.
Mix everything together in a large soup pot.
Simmer on medium low heat for about 25-30 minutes or until hot.

Submitter: 
bakeaholic
Description: 
Thick and chunky, this tasty mexican style soup is even better the next day. Everyone asks for the recipe.
