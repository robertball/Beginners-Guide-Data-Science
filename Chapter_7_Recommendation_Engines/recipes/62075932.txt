Title:
Hashbrown Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 LBS Frozen shredded hashbrowns (Break up in the bag to make it easier to mix)
1 Can of Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 Can of Cream of Celery Soup
1 Cup Sour Cream
1 Cup chopped Velveeta
1 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
2-3 TBSP Parsley
2-3 TBSP Dried Onion (optional)
1 tsp. Pepper
2 Soup cans of Milk

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients in a bowl.  Pour mixture into a deep 9 X 13 Pan.  Sprinkle more cheddar cheese on top and cover with aluminum foil.  Bake at 350 on center rack for 2 hours or until bubbly.  It will set well for an hour after baked.

Submitter: 
HAULBUR
Description: 
This is a great side dish handed down from my mother in-law.  Cheesy potato casserole that is great for get togethers or family reunions or just a side dish for at home.
