Title:
Don't knock it,til you try it cheese and crackers. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 buttery round cracker
1 slice of pepperoni,sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 slice of mild cheddar cheese,sliced 1/4 inch thick
and 1 teaspoon of sweet relish

Directions:
assemble your cracker starting with the pepperoni,then cheese,then relish,take a bite and enjoy this different combination,that is surprisingly delicious.you won't want to make just one.

Submitter: 
Karen Steinke Reilly
Description: 
these are a bit different than your ordinary cheese and crackers,with the crisp taste of the buttery round cracker,a nice slice of pepperoni,and a chunk of cheddar cheese,sets your palate up for the sweet relish kick to top it off.enjoy=)
