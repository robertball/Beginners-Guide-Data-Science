Title:
Grilled Firecracker Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs large raw shrimp, peeled & cleaned, tails on
Marinade
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/8 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper *(crushed red pepper flakes)
salt

Directions:
Thaw shrimp in a bowl of cold water, drain.
Add the marinade ingredients to a large zip-lock bag and add shrimp.  Seal and shake to cover all with the marinade then place in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
Skewer the shrimp by going through the fat end first and then the tail end so they make a half loop.  Skewer all and discard remaining marinade.
Season skewered shrimp with salt, grill on medium/high heat,turning until cooked through.
