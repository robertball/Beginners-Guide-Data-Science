Title:
Big Daddy's Flavored Game  Smoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups ice
2 cups grape flavored sports drink
2 scoops vanilla ice cream
2 scoop Mila

Directions:
In a blender, combine ice, Mila flavored sports drink and ice cream. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
