Title:
Beef and Bean Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# ground beef (85/15)
1 small onion chopped
2-3 cloves of garlic, minced (more or less if you like)
1 packet burrito seasoning mix (Olde El Paso or McCormick)
1 16 oz. can refried beans
16 oz. package of grated/shredded mild cheese
1 small can of green chilies (optional)
1 pkg. of 10 in. tortillas (8-10 count)

Directions:
Cook ground beef, add onion, and garlic. drain ground beef. Add can of refried beans and seasoning packet, following directions on packet. Drain green chilies, add to ground beef mixture. Heat on low heat, stirring occasionally till heated thru.
Spoon about two tablespoons of mixture into a warm burrito; add a tablespoon or more of cheese. Fold both sides in, then fold and roll over mixture to help seal up.
Continue to place mixture into tortillas till mixture is used or tortillas run out.
Wrap in waxed paper or place in plastic bags and place in freezer for using in lunch boxes.

Submitter: 
counseljul50
Description: 
This is a hearty beef, bean and chees burrito that is very filling. Good for lunch boxes, right out of the freezer.
