Tangy Classic Daiquiris Recipe

Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
12 1/2 fluid ounces rum
3 cups crushed ice

Directions:
Place juice concentrate, rum, and ice in blender. Pour into pitcher 1/2 filled with ice cubes.
