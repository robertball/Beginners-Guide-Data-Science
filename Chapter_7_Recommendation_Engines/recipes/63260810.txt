Title:
New Red Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds unpeeled red potatoes
3 - 4 hard-boiled eggs, peeled & diced
3/4 cups mayonnaise
3/4 cups plain yogurt
2 tablespoons milk
2 tablespoons pickle juice
1/2 cup sliced green onions
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 cup sliced celery
2 - 3 baby dill pickles, minced
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 - 2 tablespoons fresh dill (or a sprinkle of dried dill)

Directions:
Follow instructions in potato salad article for boiling potatoes, place in a medium bowl.
In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, yogurt, milk, pickle juice, green onions, salt and pepper, minced baby dill pickles, Dijon mustard, and fresh dill. Pour the mixture over the potatoes, and mix together with the eggs and celery. Cover, and chill in the refrigerator approximately 2 hours before serving.
