Title:
Turtles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
72 pecans
1/2 a recipe of Caramels I
150 grams chocolate

Directions:
Arrange pecans into clusters of 3 on a sheet of parchment paper.
Prepare the caramel according to recipe instructions ensuring that it reaches the soft ball stage.
Place a teaspoon drop of caramel on each pecan group.
Melt and temper chocolate.
Pour a half teaspoon drop of chocolate on each turtle to complete.
Let the turtles cool for 1 to 2 hours, and then feel the love.

Submitter: 
sixsnowflakes
Description: 
The best part of Christmas for our family was these turtles made by both my grandmothers and mother. They are delicious and make great gifts if you can keep them for that long! They blow purchased pralines out of the water and will bring you love from whomever you share them with.
