Title:
Gyudon Japanese Beef Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb of rib eye steak or any stir fry beef
1/2 Large White Onion ( It is sweeter )
2 tbsp Soy sauce
1 tbsp brown sugar
1/2 cup water
1 tbsp mirin
1 tbsp sake or rice wine
Green onions (optional)
Sesame Seeds (optional)
Benishoga (japanese ginger optional)
Seaweed strips (optional)

Directions:
First slice the beef thinly into bite sized pieces and set aside.
Cut the onion into thin slices, removing the heart of the onion.
Stir fry the onions for a quick 30 seconds with maybe a dot of oil , not much is needed.
Add the rest of the ingredients but not the beef and let simmer over medium-low heat.
After 3 minutes, add beef and stir / fold. Cover the pan for another 2-3 minutes, checking to see if the beef is cooked.
When done, scoop onto a bowl of short grain white rice and add your toppings.
Enjoy with a pair of chopsticks =]

Submitter: 
LittoBubbo
Description: 
It is basically a common Japanese Fast food where a serving of beef and carmelized onions are on top of Japanese short grain rice. Perfect when hot, cold, room temperature. Good for school lunches on hot days.
