Title:
Chamberlain's White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(1) packet McCormick White Chicken Chili Seasoning Mix, 1.25 oz
(1) Swanson Chicken Stock, 26 oz
(1) Bottle of Spectrum Organic Asian Stir Fry Oil, 16 fl oz
(1) bag Birds Eye Steamfresh Super Sweet Corn, 10.8 oz
(1) container of Taylor Farms Pacific Chopped Green Onions, 4 oz
(2) Marketside Mild or Medium Pico De Gallo, 10 oz containers
(1) Perdue Fresh Ground Chicken, 16 oz
(1) Crystal Farms Pepper Jack Cheese, 16 oz
(1) Crystal Farms Pepper Jack Cheese, 8 oz
(4) Bush's Beans, 15.8 oz (Any mix of white beans Northern,Navy,Butter,Cannellini)I use all four.
All ingredients can be found at Walmart

Directions:
Add 2 tsp. of Stir Fry Oil to your wok or skillet and heat it up on medium to high heat
Add Ground Chicken to heated wok and cook until done
Empty all four cans of beans into a stockpot (DRAIN AND RINSE ALL BEANS)
Cube all Pepper Jack cheese and add to stock pot (It's okay if a couple of cubes don't make it into the pot)Add White Chili seasoning packet,8 to 12 oz of chicken stock, cook until all cheese is melted. Cook on Low to Medium heat.
Add both containers of Pico De Gallo,the container of green onion and the bag of corn to the stock pot.
Add Chicken to stock pot once it's fully cooked (Do Not Drain)
Allow to simmer for 15 minutes for flavors to incorporate. Stir often to avoid scorched cheese.
Remove from heat and enjoy!
