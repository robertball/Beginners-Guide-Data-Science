Title:
Taco Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 flour tortillas
1 pound ground beef
1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
8 ounces shredded Mexican-style cheese blend
1 (14 ounce) bag tortilla chips, crushed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add the taco seasoning and stir together well. Place one tortilla in bottom of pie pan, place meat mixture on top, then layer with sour cream and cheese (can mix meat, sour cream and cheese and add if desired), top with another tortilla and repeat layers 2-3 times ending with meat/cheese mixture on top and then top off with the crushed nacho chips.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 10 minutes, or until cheese has melted.
