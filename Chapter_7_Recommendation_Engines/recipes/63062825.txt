Title:
Pan Seared Duck Breast with Balsamic Onion Marmalade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Duck Breasts
2 Large Red Onions thinly sliced (can substitute Sweet Onions)
3 TBSP Brown Sugar
3/4 cup dry red wine
3 TBSP Balsamic Vinegar
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
To make sauce:
Place brown sugar and onions in sauce pan over medium heat stirring often until onions begin to carmelize - approx 15 mins. (can add touch of olive oil and/or butter)
Stir in wine & vinegar, increase heat to medium-high stirring often until most of the liquid has evaporated - approx 15 mins.
Season to taste with salt & pepper and set aside to cool
Duck Breasts:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and place aluminum cooking sheet inside
Score fat on each duck breast shallowly in crisscross fashion (do NOT slice into meat)
Sprinkle salt and freshly ground pepper over the meat side of the duck
Heat iron skillet over high heat.  When pan i hot, add breasts, fat side down, and cook for 4-5 mins until skin is brown and crispy
Remove pan from heat (save the drippings) and transfer duck to aluminum cooking sheet, fat side up (meat side down) for 6 mins.
Let breasts rest for 5 mins, then slice into thin strips and serve atop rice or quinoa with onion marmalade on top
