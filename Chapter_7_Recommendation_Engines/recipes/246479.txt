Title:
Healthy Hot Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3407036.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
2 teaspoons cacao powder
1/4 teaspoon stevia powder
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Heat milk in a saucepan over medium heat. Add cacao powder, stevia powder, and vanilla extract; stir until hot, 2 to 3 minutes.
