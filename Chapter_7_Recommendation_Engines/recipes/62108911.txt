Title:
Sweet Thai Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 chicken thighs and legs
2/3 cup spicy sweet Thai sauce
2/3 cup honey mustard
2 cloves Chinese garlic, minced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into ziploc bag and let sit in fridge for about 4 hours.
Pre-heat oven to 375, and place the chicken into a baking dish.
Cover with foil and cook 30 minutes, then uncover and continue cooking until done.
Serve over rice with stir fried veggies for a great meal.

Submitter: 
MERRIPAN
Description: 
I tried this recipe when I couldn't think of anything else to do to chicken.  It turned out so wonderful, it's a regular thing for us now.  I hope you enjoy it as well.
