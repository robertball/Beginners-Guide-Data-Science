Title:
Brennan's Irish Soda Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Ts. Softened Butter
2 Cups All Purpose Flour
1 TB. Sugar
1/2 Ts. Baking Soda
1/2 Ts. Salt
1 Cup Buttermilk (1 Cup Milk, 2 TB. Vinegar)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Butter cookie sheet for dough to cook on
Mix Flour, Sugar, salt, and baking soda
Gradually add Milk to the mixture
Knead and form dough
Make a large round loaf, and cut and X onto the top. ( Releases steam and keeps the form)
Cook 45Min. Until golden Brown
