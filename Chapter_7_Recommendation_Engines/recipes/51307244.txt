Title:
Laura's Lemon Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup butter, melted
2 tablespoons lemon juice
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon dried dill weed
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
5 large potatoes, peeled and cubed

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
In a large bowl, stir together the olive oil, melted butter, lemon juice, garlic, dill, salt and pepper. Add potatoes and toss to coat. Spread the potatoes out on a baking sheet and drizzle any remaining liquid from the bowl over them.
Bake in the preheated oven until potatoes are brown and crispy, about 45 minutes.
