Title:
Seafood Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T. butter
4 T. flour
1/2 t. dry mustard
1/4 t. onion powder
1/8 t. garlic powder
1/2 t. white pepper
1 T. lobster base
1 can cream of shrimp soup
1 c. heavy cream
2 c. half & half
1 lb. crab meat
1 lb. shrimp, chopped (cooked, peeled, etc.)
1/4 c. sherry (NOT cooking sherry)
8 oz. clam juice OR chicken broth
1 t. chopped parsley

Directions:
Melt butter.
Whisk in flour, mustard, onion powder, garlic powder, pepper, and lobster base.
Cook on medium (don't scorch) 4-5 minutes.
Add soup, cream, and half & half.  Heat through.
Add crab, shrimp, and sherry.
Thin to desired consistency with clam juice (or chicken broth).
Heat through - don't boil.
