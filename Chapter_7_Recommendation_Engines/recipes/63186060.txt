Title:
PINEAPPLE PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 PIE CRUST (UNBAKED)
1 CAN CRUSHED PINEAPPLE
1/4 CUP OF SUGAR
1 8 OZ CREAM CHEESE
1 CAN SWEETENED CONDENSED MILK
4 EGGS

Directions:
COOK 1 CAN CRUSHED PINEAPPLE WITH SUGAR UNTIL THICK
BEAT CREAM CHEESE AND ADD EGGS 1 AT A TIME AND THEN ADD SWEETENED CONDENSED MILK
POUR CREAM CHEESE MIXTURE INTO PIE PAN AND THEN SPOON PINEAPPLE MIXTURE OVER TOP
BAKE FOR 1 H R AT 350
