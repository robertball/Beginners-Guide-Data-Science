Title:
Zucchini noodles and summer vegetables with sweet pepper chicken sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2512545.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium size zucchini
2 Sweet pepper chicken sausages (sliced)
1 large tomato
1/3 cup chopped White onion
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1 cup fresh spinach (lightly torn)
1/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese
2 TBSP extra virgin olive oil
1 TSP garlic paste or 3 cloves of fresh garlic finely chopped
1 TBSP Spike seasoning
2 TSPS basil
a Pinch of sea salt
Fresh ground pepper

Directions:
First wash and then cut off ends of zucchini. Using a mandolin or pasta maker, create ribbons of spaghetti style noodles. Set aside.
Next, boil a pot of water and cook noodles for about 3 minutes. Drain noodles and set aside.
While noodles are cooking, dice the tomato.
In a saute pan, add 1 TBSP olive oil and heat onion and green pepper for 3 minutes, adding spike and sea salt. Add sliced chicken sausage and cook for 4-5 more minutes until sausage is lightly browned. Add spinach, more spike, and sweet basil and cook till spinach is wilted.
Finally, place the zucchini noodles on a plate or platter, add 1 TBSP olive oil and sea salt and toss lightly. Then, add vegetables and sausage and then tomatoes. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Add salt and pepper to taste.

Description: 
Fresh from the farmer's market! A light and flavorful blend of farm fresh summer vegetables and sweet pepper chicken sausage.
