Title:
California Party Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985533.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 (8 ounce) package Neufchatel cheese, softened
1 cup light mayonnaise
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3/4 cup chopped tomatoes
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
1 teaspoon dried dill weed
Town House® Crackers

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
Combine all ingredients except crackers in a medium bowl. Place in a shallow baking dish.
Bake 35 to 45 minutes or until heated through and lightly browned. Serve with Town House(R) Crackers.

Submitter: 
Keebler® Kitchens
Description: 
Tender artichoke hearts and tangy Parmesan cheese star in this warm, tasty spread that perfectly compliments Town House® Crackers.
