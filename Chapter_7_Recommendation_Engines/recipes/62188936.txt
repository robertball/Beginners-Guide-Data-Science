Title:
Easy Spicy Thai Slow Cooker Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pcs cut up skinless chicken (any pieces will do)
1 bottle Kraft Asian Sesame Dressing and Marinade
1-2 tbsp Thai Chili Paste
1 tbsp garlic ginger
2 tbsp peanut butter

Directions:
Mix dressing, garlic giner, peanut butter and chili paste together until well blended.
Dip chicken into sauce and arrange in crock pot.  Pour sauce over the chicken and cook on low about 4-6 hours.

Submitter: 
jescookin
Description: 
This is an inexpensive, time saving recipe for anyone who loves spicy Thai but can't always afford to get carry out.
