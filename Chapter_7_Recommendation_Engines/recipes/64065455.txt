Title:
Seven Layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ripe avocados
2 TBSP lemon juice
salt and pepper
2 cans bean dip (1 can refried beans, seasoned)
1 cup green onions, chopped
4 oz. cheddar cheese, grated
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
3 chopped tomatoes
2 can black olives, chopped
1 cup Pace or salsa

Directions:
Mash avocados in a bowl with lemon juice, salt and pepper. Combine sour cream and mayonnaise in another bowl. To assemble: spread bean dip in a 9x13 pan; top with avocado mixture, layer with sour cream mixture. Sprinkle with onions, tomatoes and olives and cover with cheese Top with Pace. Serve with chips
