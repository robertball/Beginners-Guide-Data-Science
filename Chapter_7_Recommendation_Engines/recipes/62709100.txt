Title:
Almond and Amaretto Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups softened butter
1 (3oz) pkg of softened cream cheese
2 1/2 cups sugar
3 Tbsp amaretto
1 Tbsp vanilla
2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
6 large eggs
1/3 cup sliced almonds
Amaretto Glaze (recipe below)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 325.  Beat butter and cream cheese at medium speed until creamy.  Gradually add sugar, beating at medium until light and fluffy.  Add amaretto and vanilla and beat until blended.  Gradually add flour to butter mixture beating at low speed just until blended after each addition.
2.  Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating at low speed just until blended after each addition.  Sprinkle almonds over bottom of a greased and floured 12 cup bundt pan; pour batter into pan.
3.  Bake at 325 for 1 hour or until a long wooden pick inserted comes out clean.
4.  during last 10 minutes of baking prepare the Amaretto Glaze.  Remove cake from oven, and gradually spoon hot Glaze over cake in pan.  (continue to add glaze to cake until all of it is used, allowing it to soak into cake after each addition.)  Cool completely in pan on a wire rack.  (about and hour and half)
Amaretto Glaze
bring 3/4 cup sugar, 6 TBPS butter, 1/4 cup Amaretto and 2 TBSP of water to a boil in a small sauce pan.  stir constantly, 3 min. remove from heat and use immediatley.
