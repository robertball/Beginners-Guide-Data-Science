Title:
Grilled Ham and Tomato Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991650.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup CROSSE and BLACKWELL® Apple Curry Chutney
8 thin slices French bread
1 small tomato, sliced
8 thin slices cooked ham
4 thin slices Cheddar cheese

Directions:
SPREAD chutney evenly over slices of bread. Top each of 4 bread slices evenly with tomato, 2 slices ham and 1 slice cheese. Top with remaining bread slices, chutney side down. Generously spray top and bottom bread slices with butter-flavored nonstick cooking spray.
GRILL sandwiches in large nonstick skillet over medium heat for about 3 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Cut in half.

Submitter: 
Crosse & Blackwell®
Description: 
Chutney makes this ham-and-cheese sandwich a cut above the rest.
