Title:
Microwaved Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/825261.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1-2 Tablespoons milk
salt and pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a microwave safe bowl.
Scramble well with a fork.
Microwave on high for 45 seconds.
Stir.
Microwave on high for 30 seconds.
Stir.
Continue microwaving on high for 15-second increments until cooked to your liking.

Submitter: 
Maeraj
Description: 
This is a super easy and mess-free way of making scrambled eggs. Each person can prepare their own eggs in just a couple minutes--adding whatever they'd like to their scramble. The best part is that there is no pan to scrub!
