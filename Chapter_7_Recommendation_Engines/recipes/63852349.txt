Title:
Grilled Chicken Apple Sausage with Angel Hair Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
3/4 cup white wine
1 tblsp chicken bullion granuals
2 tsp corn starch
1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1 small onion (or red onion)
8 oz angel hair pasta
1/4 cup sun dried tomatoes
1 tblsp coarsely chopped fresh basil

Directions:
In a sauce pan, caramelize the onion in butter over low heat, this will take about 30 minutes.
In a small bowl, mix wine, chick bullion, and corn starch.
As onion gets done, saute sausage in small skillet.  Combine caramelized onion and sausage in a bowl, cover and set aside.
Add wine mixture to sauce pan under medium-low heat.  Stir and add pepper, basil and sun dried tomatoes.  Finish sauce with a pat of butter.
As you make the sauce, cook the pasta.
Toss sausage & onion, sauce and pasta together in serving bowl.
