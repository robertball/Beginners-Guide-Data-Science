Title:
Creamy Baked Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz spaghetti, cooked
1 lb sweet or mild Italian sausage
1 medium onion, chopped
1 tablespoon fresh minced parsley
1 tablespoon fresh minced basil
1 clove minced garlic
8 ounces softened cream cheese
28 oz spaghetti sauce
3/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1 cup Mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Cook spaghetti, following directions on package. While spaghetti is cooking, brown sausage over medium high heat and drain. Reserve some fat in pan to cook onions. Cook onions until translucent. Set aside.
Drain spaghetti and return to pot. Add herbs, garlic, and cream cheese. Stir until well-combined.
Spread half of sauce in bottom of 9x13 inch pan. Add spaghetti mixture on top. Layer remaining sauce on top of spaghetti. Top with Parmesan and Mozzarella.
Bake for 30 minutes- until bubbling at the edges and cheese is melted and a little browned.
Allow to sit for 15 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
D. Helgeson
Description: 
Comfort food at its finest. Can be prepared with many on-hand ingredients. Use 1/3 the herbs if using dried. Use spicy sausage if you prefer a little kick.
