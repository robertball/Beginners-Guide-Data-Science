Title:
Fruity, Fizzy Party Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (64 fluid ounce) bottle fruit punch, chilled
1 (64 fluid ounce) bottle unsweetened pineapple juice, chilled
1 (2 liter) bottle ginger ale, chilled

Directions:
Pour fruit punch, pineapple juice, and ginger ale into a punch bowl. Stir to combine.

Submitter: 
Jaay
Description: 
An easy, fruity punch with just the right amount of fizz, perfect for any party.
