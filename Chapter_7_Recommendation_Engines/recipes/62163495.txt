Title:
Gretchen's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs ground beef 85/15
1 medium green bell pepper chopped
1 medium red bell pepper chopped
1 medium onion chopped
1 cup of oatmeal
1 cup of Italian bread crumbs
3 1/2 cups spicy v8 juice
2 eggs
salt to taste
pepper to taste
red pepper flakes to taste
chopped garlic to taste

Directions:
In large bowl mix all ingredients and only 1 and 1/2 cups of the V8 juice. mix well
pour 1 cup of V8 into baking pan and place the meatloaf on the pan.
poke holes on top of meatloaf with fingers and pour the rest of the V8 over the loaf letting the juice go into the holes.
cover with aluminum foil
bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees
take foil off bake an additional 30 minutes or until meat is done.
