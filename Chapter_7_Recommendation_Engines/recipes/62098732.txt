Title:
Chocolate Chip Cream Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese
1 stick butter (not margarine)
1/4 tsp. vanilla
2 tbsp. brown sugar
3/4 cup confectioner's sugar
3/4 cup chocolate chips (semi-sweet)
3/4 cup pecans or walnuts (finely chopped)
1 box graham crackers or graham cracker sticks

Directions:
Cream the butter and cream cheese together.
Add vanilla and brown sugar to the cream cheese mixture, and mix in confectioner's sugar.
Add by hand the mini chocolate chips.
Refrigerate for 2 hours.
Remove from the refrigerator and mold the mixture into a ball and refrigerate for an hour.
Before serving roll into chopped nuts.  Serve with graham crackers or graham cracker sticks.
