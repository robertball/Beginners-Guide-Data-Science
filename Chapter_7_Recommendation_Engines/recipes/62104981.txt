Title:
Rob's Lamb Curry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FILLING:
3 cups cooked, diced lamb
3 cloves garlic
3 T. olive oil
½  t ground cumin
½  t ground turmeric
¼ t ground cinnamon
½ t ground hot red pepper
½ thumb size peeled fresh ginger
1 T red, (yellow) curry paste
½ cup coriander leaves (cilantro)
3 med (red) onions, chopped
1 med eggplant sliced then chopped
¾ cup chopped celery
1 large red bell pepper (paprika)
Salt to taste while cooking.
SAUCE:
1 ½ cup milk
3 T butter
½ cup sweet, simi-sweet white wine
3 T white flour
CRUST:
1 cup white flour
½ t salt
3 t curry powder
6 T pastry shortening
3 T cold water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F (175C)
Food process oil and all spices & leaves
In mix bowl, coat all produce with oil mix
Turn produce into med heated skillet
Sauté 7 min, stirring often.
When tender, add cooked lamb
Remove from heat
Heat milk, low, in pan with butter & wine
When butter melts turn high whisk in flour
Wisk constantly 5 min
Reheat filling, med, & turn in sauce
Salt to taste
Stir 5 min, occasionally, until lamb is hot
Pour filling into greased pie tin or plate
Mix flour, salt and curry power
Cut in shortening
Form into ball and roll 10’’ crust
Seal and flute edges. Slit top.
Bake 35 min. or until brown
Let cool 7 min.

Submitter: 
Rob Hudgins
Description: 
This pie is made from leftover roasted leg or sholder of lamb. In fact, I specifically roast the lamb to make this dish. I think there is no better way to to use lamb leftovers.
