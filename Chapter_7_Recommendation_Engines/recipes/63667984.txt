Title:
apple angel food Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of apple pie filling and 1 box of dry angel food cake mix.....that's it! NOTE: you MUST use the ONE-STEP

Directions:
Mix the 2 ingredients together (the packet of cake mix and the undrained can of fruit/filling) pour in a greased 9x13 pan or dish and bake for about 20 minutes at 350 (or according to baking directions on the box).
People....it can't get ANY easier than this! A dollop or 3 of ice cream finishes it off perfectly :)
