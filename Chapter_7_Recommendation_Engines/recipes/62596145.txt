Title:
Good For You Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/556895.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces semisweet Chocolate chips
2 tbsp. unsalted butter
1 cup sugar
1 large egg
1 egg white
1/4 cup of prune puree (Can find in the baby food section)
2 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 tsp. salt
3/4 cup all purpose flour
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Grease and flour a 8 inch square pan and set aside.
Now to create a double boiler to melt the chocolate, in a small saucepan add about an inch of water and bring to a boil. Then place a heatproof bowl and nestle it on top of the saucepan (Make sure the water does not touch the bowl).
Add chocolate chips and butter in the bowl and stir genly till it's melted and remove from heat. Immmediatly add in the sugar, vanilla, Prune puree and salt and whisk then slowly incorparate one whole egg and one egg white untill smooth.
Incorperate the flour and cocoa little by little with the chocolate mixture.
Spread batter into the pan and bake until top is firm and a toothpick comes out clean. (about 30 Mins)  Let cool completely before you cut them.
