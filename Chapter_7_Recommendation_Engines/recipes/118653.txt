Title:
Tune Up Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (6 ounce) cans Albacore, solid white tuna in spring water, drained
1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing
1/2 cup diced celery
1/4 cup diced green onions
8 slices sandwich bread

Directions:
Mix tuna with Ranch, celery and onions in bowl.  Spread salad evenly on 4 slices of bread; top with remaining slices.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Add flavor excitement to your tuna salad with the addition of creamy Ranch dressing.
