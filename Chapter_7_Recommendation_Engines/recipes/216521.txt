Title:
Sweet Potato Chicken Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992212.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons Mrs. Dash® Caribbean Citrus Blend
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2 sweet potatoes, medium size, cut into 8 wedges each
3 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Heat a cast iron skillet to medium heat. Add 1 1/2 Tbsp. olive oil.
Toss in sweet potatoes and brown on all sides for 1 minute. Remove to a plate.
Add remaining olive oil and brown chicken on both sides. Return sweet potatoes to pan and sprinkle all with Mrs. Dash(R) Caribbean Citrus Blend. Cook 3 minutes, toss and serve.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Dash®
Description: 
Show your chicken some serious love when you brown skinless chicken breasts with sweet potatoes then sprinkle on Mrs. Dash® Caribbean Citrus Blend.
