Title:
Tomato Mozzarella Egg White Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp olive oil
1/4 c sliced grape tomatoes
salt and pepper, to taste
1/3 c egg whites
1/4 c shredded part skim mozzarella cheese
1 tsp grated parmesan cheese
fresh herbs for garnish

Directions:
Heat a small non-stick skillet over medium heat.  When hot, add the oil mushrooms and tomatoes, season with salt and pepper. Cook about 1 minute.
Add egg whites and add mozzarella and parmesan.
Cover and reduce heat to low.  Cook until the eggs are completely set.  Top with fresh herbs if desired.
If using mushrooms, cook them prior to making omelet.
