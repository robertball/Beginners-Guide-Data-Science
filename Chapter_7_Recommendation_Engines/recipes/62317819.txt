Title:
Bacon and Tomato Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices bacon
1 tomato, chopped
1/2 onion, chopped or green onion
3 ounces shredded Swiss cheese
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 (16 ounce) can refrigerated buttermilk biscuit dough or phyllo cups

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). If using buttermilk biscuits, Lightly grease a mini muffin pan.
In a skillet over medium heat, cook bacon until evenly brown. Drain on paper towels. Crumble bacon into a medium mixing bowl, and mix with tomato, onion, Swiss cheese, mayonnaise, garlic powder and basil.
Separate biscuits into halves horizontally. Place each half into cups of the prepared mini muffin pan. Fill each biscuit half with the bacon mixture.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven , or until golden brown.
