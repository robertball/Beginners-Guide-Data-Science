Title:
Wild Rice & Pepper Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/414504.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked long grain and wild rice
1/2 cup chopped red pepper
1/2 cup chopped yellow pepper
1/4 cup chopped green onion
2 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1 tsp. lemon zest
1/4 tsp. fresh ground black pepper
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 c. light mayonnaise

Directions:
Combine first eight ingredients in a medium size bowl and mix well.   Mix in light mayonnaise.
Refrigerate for 1 hour.
Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Jillian
Description: 
A crunchy, cool, refreshing salad with a hint of lemon that goes great with grilled chicken on warm summer nights!
