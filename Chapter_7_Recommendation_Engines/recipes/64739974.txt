Title:
Keto Egg Cups with Fathead Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
less than 1/2 of keto fathead dough recipe (6 balls)
4 eggs
1 c cheddar cheese
salt and pepper

Directions:
Make keto fathead dough; divide into two, then make 8 balls with one half, used only six balls
press into silicon muffin molds
bake crusts for 10 min at 400
mix eggs and cheese, season to taste
add 2 T egg/cheese mixture to each muffin cup
bake another 12 min at 400 or until eggs done

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
easy egg muffins with crust (next time use 5 eggs and 1/2 fathead recipe to make 8 muffins)
