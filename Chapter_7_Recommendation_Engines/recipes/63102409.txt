Title:
Chickpeas with Yassa Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can chickpeas, drained and rinsed.
1/2 head cabbage, chopped
1/4 lb carrots, chopped
4-5 big onions, finely chopped
6 cloves garlic, chopped
2 bay leaves
juice of 3 lemons
1 fresh habanero pepper, chopped
1/4 cup red vinegar
2 tablespoons prepared Dijon mustard
1 envelope Goya vegetable seasoning
.75 tsp black pepper
1 cup drained small green olives without pits

Directions:
Fry the onions, garlic and habanero in a generous amount of oil; add cabbage and cook until softened. Add remaining ingredients and simmer 1 hour or more.  Serve with white rice.
