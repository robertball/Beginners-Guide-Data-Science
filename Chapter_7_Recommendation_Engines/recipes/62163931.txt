Title:
frogeye salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-lb. pkg. acini di pepe: cook in 3 qt. water, 1 T. salt, and 1 T. cooking oil
20-oz. can crushed pineapple, drain and reserve juice
3 (11-oz.) cans mandarin oranges, drained
Small jar marshino cherries, drained
2 bananas
2 apples
20-oz. can pineapple tidbits, drain and reserve juice
2 c. miniature marshmallows
1 c. coconut
4-8oz. Cool Whip (depending on your taste)
Sauce: cook and cool
1  c. sugar
2 T. flour 2 eggs, beaten
1 T. lemon juice
all reserved pineapple juice

Directions:
Cook acini di pepe in water with salt and cooking oil according to directions on package.
After cooked, add pineapple and cooled pineapple sauce. Refrigerate overnight in sealed bowl.
Then add oranges, bananas, apples, marshmallows and coconut. Then add Cool Whip. Will keep for a week in refrigerator but add bananas and apples just before serving.

Submitter: 
tigershoes
Description: 
Had this at a church potluck and loved it. The acini di pepe is small rice like pasta.  It is really delicious.
