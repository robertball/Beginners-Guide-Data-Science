Title:
Baker's Creamy Cream of Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons of bacon fat or butter
1 large white onion diced
1 clove of garlic chopped
4 small potatoes or 2 russle peeled and chopped
8 cups of chicken broth
4 cups of fresh or frozen broccoli chopped
1 cup of half and half
salt and pepper to taste
a dash of nutmeg
1 cup of shreded chedar cheese for garnish

Directions:
Melt down bacon fat or butter over medium high heat in a large stock pot. Add onions and garlic and cook for about 7 minutes; untill tender.
Add potatoes, just to coat.  Pour in chicken broth.
Bring to a boil. Add broccoli.  Bring to a boil again, and cover.
Let it cook for 10 minute or untill potatoes and broccoli are tender.
After they are tender take off the heat and use a boat motor or blender and cream the soup.
Add half and half, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Cook for another 5 minutes on simmer; low heat. Do not let it come back to a boil.
Garnish with shreaded chedar cheese.

Description:

Quick and easy to make. I love to make this to feed my family and to give to someone who needs a hand.  If not makes great leftovers.
