Title:
Overnight Blueberry French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs
1 1/2 cup milk
tsp vanilla
1 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup melted butter
6-8 slices bread
2 cups blueberries

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 pan
Whisk eggs, milk and vanilla in a bowl.
Mix brown sugar, cinnamon, butter in another bowl.
Pour 1/3 of the sugar mixture on the bottom of the 9x13 and add one layer of bread to fill the pan. (3-4 slices and cut to fit if you have to)
Add half the blueberries on top of the bread and another 1/3 of the sugar mixture
Add the final layer of bread, blueberries and last 1/3 of sugar mixture.
Pour egg mixure over and cover with plastic wrap.
Leave in fridge overnight.
Next morning, remove wrap and bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes
