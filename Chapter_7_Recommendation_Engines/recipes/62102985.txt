Title:
Hot Dogs San Fernando Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
INGREDIENTS
8 hot dogs
8 hot dog buns
8 slices of bacon
8 slices of ham
Queso fresco/fresh cheese (crumbled) or your favorite cheese
chopped tomatoes
chopped onion (grilled or raw)
mustard
ketchup
mayo
nacho jalapenos

Directions:
DIRECTIONS
Roll each hot dog in a slice of bacon and secure with a tootpick. Fry the hot dogs in oil until the bacon is crispy.
Open hot dog buns and grill flat. Take the hot dogs and a napkin take off excess oil and the toothpicks. Place the hot dog inside the bun and top it with the dressings (mayo, mustard, ketchup)then add the tomatoes and onions. After adding the dressings and veggies put a slice of ham on top of the ingredients and top it with the cheese. Finally, add jalapenos on the side
Serve and enjoy :)
