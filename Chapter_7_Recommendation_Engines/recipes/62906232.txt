Title:
Cathy's Homemade Navy Bean and Ham Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. dry navy beans  soaked overnight  then rinsed and drained
½ large onion  finely diced
4 stalks celery  finely diced
4 carrots  finely diced
2 cloves garlic  minced
2 lb. smoked boneless cottage ham  cut in ½” chunks
2 T. salt and fresh ground black pepper to taste
46 oz. chicken broth
8 cups water
4 small potatoes  diced, boiled and mashed w/ricer or mixer
½ stick butter

Directions:
Sauté celery, onion, carrots and garlic in ½ stick butter for 10 min.  Add diced ham.  Cook 10 min. longer.  Add salt and pepper.  Add beans.
Add chicken broth and water.  Cook for 30 min.  Add mashed potatoes.  Simmer 3 hours – stirring frequently.

Submitter: 
CATHYRAE
Description: 
I developed this recipe just this past weekend.  My family and friends thought it was delicious.  Just the right mix of beans and smoked cottage ham.  Served with a Salad and Buttery Rolls recipe I found on Allrecipes.
