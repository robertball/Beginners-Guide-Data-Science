Title:
Easiest Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 oz bags) Peanut Butter Chips
1 stick (1/2 Cup) Butter
1 (14 oz.) can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Melt butter in a sauce pan of med heat.
Pour in condensed milk.
Pour in peanut butter chips
Stir until blended.
Pour into a parchment lined 9 X 9 pan.
Let cool, cut into pieces, enjoy.
