Title:
Mojo Seasoning Sauce (Mojo) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp pure Spanish olive oil, heated
2 Tbsp finely chopped onion
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp ground oregano
1/4 tsp ground cumin
Dash ground bay leaves
Dash black pepper
1/8 tsp salt
2 Tbsp sour orange or lime juice
2 Tbsp water
1 Tbsp Sherry wine
1/2 tsp vinegar

Directions:
Mix the onion and garlic, along with the spices, salt, and pepper in a bowl, and then add to a pan with the heated olive oil.  Cook just until onion becomes translucent, and then add juice, water, wine and vinegar and simmer about 5 minutes.  Allow to cool slightly before serving.
