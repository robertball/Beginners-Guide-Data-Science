Title:
Helyn Talkington-Richardson's Raspberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 large pkg raspberry jello
2 c boiling water
2 pkg frozen raspberries
Topping:
3 oz pkg cream cheese at room temperature
1/2 pint whipping cream
1 c crushed pineapple, drained
1 c miniture marshmallows
1/4 c sliced almonds

Directions:
Dissolve jello in boiling water.
Add frozen raspberries. Pour into 9x13" pan and refrigerate until set.
Beat cream cheese with whipping cream until thick.
Fold in drained pineapple, marshmallows and almonds.
