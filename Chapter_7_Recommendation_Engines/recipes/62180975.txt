Title:
Mom's Green bean Vegetable Casserole. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can French style green beans
1 can corn
1 can cream celery soup
1/2 cup sour cream
3/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped onion
pinch of salt
1 stick of butter
2 stacks of ritz crackers

Directions:
drain beans and corn and mix in greased dish
mix next five ingredients and spread over vegetables
mix butter, after melting it with crushed ritz crackers.
spread on top of vegetable mixture
bake at 350 for 45 minutes, uncovered.

Submitter: 
renee mae
Description: 
This is my mom's personal twist to the original green bean casserole. She has made this every thanksgiving because our family demands it!
