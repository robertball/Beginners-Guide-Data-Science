Title:
Garden Veggie Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4328826.jpg
Ingredients:
4 potatoes, cut into thin slices
2 zucchini, cut into thin slices
2 squash, cut into thin slices
4 yellow onions, cut into thin slices
2 carrots, cut into thin slices
4 medium mushrooms, cut into thin slices
1 teaspoon red peppers (optional)
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon garlic power
salt & pepper

Directions:
Combine potatoes, zucchini, onions, carrots, mushroom, squash, garlic powder, red peppers,  salt, pepper, and cumin in a large pot; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook soup at a simmer until the vegetables are completely tender, about 35 minutes.
Leaving pot on stove, switch heating element to off.  Season soup with salt and pepper.
