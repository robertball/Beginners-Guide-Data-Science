Title:
Cajun Chicken Poppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2068554.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large Chicken Breast
1 can Pineapple Chunks
1 lb. Smoked Bacon
Cajun Seasoning
Pinch Salt
Chipotle BBQ Sauce

Directions:
Cut chicken into long thin strips (partially frozen chicken cut's easier).
Season Chicken with Cajun Seasoning and salt.
Roll chicken around 1 pineapple chunk.
Rotate wrapped pineapple and roll again in a half slice of bacon.
spear with toothpick to keep bacon from unraveling.
Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 30 minutes (or grill).
Serve with Spicy Chipotle BBQ Sauce for dipping.

Submitter: 
Karen Dean Covington
Description: 
These are a great little appetizer that used to be served at one of my favorite restaurants. They no longer have it on their menu so I have been making them at home with the same success!
