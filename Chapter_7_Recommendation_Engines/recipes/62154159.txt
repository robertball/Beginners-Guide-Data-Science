Title:
Domingo's Favorite Chipotle Salsa! (La salsa preferida de Domingo!) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Fresh Ripe Tomatoes
4 medium size cloves of Garlic
1/2 White onion
1-4 Red Chilis
2-4 Chipotle Peppers from a can (approx. 4 oz.)
1 bunch of Cilantro
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Place Tomatoes, Garlic, Onion, and red Chilies in a pot coved in water.
Bring water to a boil.
Time for approximately 10 - 15 min (until the tomato's get deep splits or peels start to come off)
Removed all items from the water, Set water aside, and place in a blender or food processor.
Add Chipotle Peppers
Blend Ingredients completely
Add a small handful of cilantro, along with a sprinkle of Salt and Pepper. Blend again.
Salsa should still be red with bits of green (cilantro)
Taste, if you wish add more Chipotle, cilantro, salt and pepper if you wish.
Let salsa cool for One hour and place in a glass jar with a lid.
Salsa will keep in fridge for 2 weeks

Submitter: 
Jerusha Francisco
Description: 
Smokey and Spicy Chipotle Salsa. Just like his Mama used to make.
This recipe is on the spicier side, and  can be altered easily just add or subtract the amount of peppers to create a custom taste that you prefer. What ever you do it will still taste amazing!
