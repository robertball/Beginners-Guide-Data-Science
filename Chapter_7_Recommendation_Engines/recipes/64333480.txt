Title:
Chocolate Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c flour
2 T apple sauce
1/2 c water
1/8 t salt
2/3 c sugar
1 t vanilla
2 T cocoa
2 t baking powder
2/3 c brown sugar
1/4 c cocoa
1 3/4 c hot water

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Mix the first eight ingredients together. Pour into an  8? square baking pan.
Mix the brown sugar and 1/4 c cocoa. Sprinkle over the batter.
Pour the hot water or coffee over the entire top surface.
Bake for about 45 minutes. The topping sinks through the cake to form a pudding layer at the bottom.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
This cake tastes so rich it’s hard to believe there’s no fat or eggs in it. We prefer it chilled, but it can be served at room temperature, or even warm, if you can’t wait.
