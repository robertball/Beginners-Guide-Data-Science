Title:
Slow Cooker Bacon Cheeseburger Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Bacon
1/2 lb. Ground Beef
1 Package (8oz.) Cream Cheese, cubed
1 Package (8oz.) American-Cheddar Cheese blend (2 cups)
1 (10oz.) Diced Tomatoes with Green Chiles, undrained
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
2 Medium bell peppers, cut into bite-size strips
1 package (5.25oz.) mini bagel chips

Directions:
1. Cook bacon over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until crisp; drain n paper towels. Crumble bacon; reserve 2 tbsp bacon for garnish.
2. I'm same skillet, cook beef over medium-high heat 5 to 7 minutes, until thoroughly cooked, drain. Reduce heat to low. Stir in cream cheese, shredded cheese, tomatoes and crumble bacon until chees starts to melt.
3. Pour mixture into 1- to 1 1/2  quart slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 2-3 hours or until hot and bubbly.
4. Stir parsley into dip. Sprinkle with reserved bacon for garnish. Keep on low heat setting to serve.  Serve with bells pepper strips and bagel chips.

Submitter: 
Bryndis Curtin
Description: 
I made this a couple of years ago for a picnic and it was a huge hit.
