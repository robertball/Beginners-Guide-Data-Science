Title:
Weeknight hamburger soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1795691.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground chuck
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cups water
1 28 oz. can diced tomatoes undrained
1 15 oz. can diced potatoes, drained
1 14.5 oz. can mixed vegetables, drained
4 tsp. beef bouillon granules
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/4 tsp. dried oregano

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown ground chuck and drain. Add chopped onions and cook until they are soft. Add the next 8 ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 10-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
S.RalstoninGA
Description: 
This is an easy, hearty soup that can be prepared quickly and tastes great on a cold winter night. Most of the ingredients you already have in your pantry!
