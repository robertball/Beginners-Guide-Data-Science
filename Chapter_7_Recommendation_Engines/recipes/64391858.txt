Title:
Dietrich Ice Box Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
3 1/2 cups plain flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar, add eggs and mix well.
In separate bowl sift together salt, baking soda, flour, and baking powder.  Slowly add mixture to wet ingredients.  Add nuts.  Mix together until a nice dough forms
Shape into rolls and wrap in wax paper.  Refridgerate at least 4 hours.  Cut into thin slices.
Bake on cookie sheet at 350 degrees for 5-10 minutes
