Title:
Shelly Hospitality's Blueberry Turnover Hand Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1113085.jpg
Ingredients:
2 x Pre made pie crust dough
1c Frozen Blueberries
1/2c sugar
1t cinnamon
1 egg beaten
1/4c Turbindo raw sugar/ Sugar In The Raw

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Cut rounds of prepared dough with 5 inch round cutter. In center place: 1T Frozen Blueberries, 1/4t sugar, sprinkle cinnamon. Fold over and seal edges with pie maker or fork tines. Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with Turbindo sugar.  Bake hand pies until brown about 18 - 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Shelly Hiddleson
Description: 
Portion control and flavor without a whole pie! Enjoy.
