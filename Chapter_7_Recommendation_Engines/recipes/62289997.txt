Title:
Ultimate Guinness Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 tablespoons olive oil
3 bay leaves
2 to 2 1/2 lbs of beef stew meat (1 1/2 inch cubes)
2 medium yellow onions quartered
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon dried rosemary leaves
2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour(plus enough to dredge)
1 cup beef stock
1 can Guinness Stout (14.9 oz can)
2 tablespoons parsley, chopped
1/2 lb carrots, peeled and sliced thick
2 large all purpose potatoes 1 to 1/2 inch cubes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat a 6 quart oven proof pot to high heat with olive oil and bay leaves.  Pre-heat oven to 275 degrees.
Lightly dredge meat in flour, shake off excess.  Brown meat on both sides on high heat.
Add onion and cover for 2-3 minutes until onions are clear. Reduce heat to low.
Combine garlic, thyme, flour, rosemary, add to meat.  Stir until smooth.
Add beef stock and stout; simmer, until slightly thickened.
Add parsley, carrots, and potatoes.
Cover and cook in pre-heated 275 degree oven for approximately 3 hours, until potatoes are cooked through.  Stir 2-3 times while cooking.  Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with bread.

Description: 
This is an Irish beef stew made with Guinness.  This stew is very hearty and begs for a good sopping bread.
