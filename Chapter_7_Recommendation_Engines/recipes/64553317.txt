Title:
Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 sticks butter
4 Tablespoon water
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 cup chopped pecans
tempered chocolate

Directions:
Boil to 310 degrees. Pour into 9x13 buttered pan.  put 1 cup chopped pecans on top.  Cool.  Add tempered chocolate.  Break in pieces.  Store in airtight container
