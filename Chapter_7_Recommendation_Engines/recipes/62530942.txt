Title:
Sweet and Tangy Slow Cooker BBQ Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. beef chuck roast
1/2 cup water
1 1/2 tsp. vinegar
2 Tbsp. brown sugar
1 tsp. dry mustard
2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 cups ketchup
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 bottle BBQ sauce

Directions:
Place roast in slow cooker with water. Cover and cook on low for 2-4 hours, until beef can be easily shredded.
Shred beef, remove fat as you go. Remove 1/2 cup broth from slow cooker, and reserve for later. Add remaining ingredients to another bowl and stir. Cover meat in sauce.
Cover and continue to cook on low for 4-6 hours. Add BBQ sauce if needed for taste or texture. Serve on toasted buns. Easy to freeze.
