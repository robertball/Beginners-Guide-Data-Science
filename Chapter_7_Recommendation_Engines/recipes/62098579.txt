Title:
Mayoless Chicken Salad Sandwich Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of cooked chicken
1.5 TBSP potato flakes
1 TBSP olive oil
1 TBSP Dijon Mustard
1 TBSP of milk (more or less to adjust consistency)
dash of pepper
dash of onion powder
1/2 can of green chiles (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a small bowl.  Ensure that the pieces of chicken are broken up and mixed in well.  Put it on some bread and enjoy.

Submitter: 
AUSTAD
Description: 
I despise Mayonaise, and many people are allergic to it.  I developed this recipe for a quick, easy and very tasty Chicken Salad sandwich filling.
