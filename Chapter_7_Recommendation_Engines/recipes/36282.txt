Title:
Lazy Day Citrus Southwest Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (1.25 ounce) package McCormick® Grill Mates® Southwest Marinade
1/3 cup olive oil
1 orange, juiced
1 teaspoon grated orange peel
2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients in self-closing plastic bag.  Add chicken.  Close bag.  Turn bag to coat chicken evenly.  Refrigerate 30 minutes or longer for extra flavor.
Remove chicken from marinade.  Grill or broil 5-7 minutes per side.  Baste once with remaining marinade.  Discard leftover marinade.
Variation:  May substitute chicken parts for boneless chicken breasts.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
The delicious seasonings of southwest cuisine are paired with the sweet citrus flavor of fresh oranges in Lazy-Day Citrus Southwest Chicken.
