Title:
Herby Edamame Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3346249.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
12 ounces shelled edamame, thawed if frozen
2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup cilantro
2 sprigs of mint
salt to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a food processor or blender and blend until smooth.
Remove and devour!

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Herby Edamame Hummus made with a few ingredients in a blender, this simple hummus is layered with creamy Greek yogurt and bouncing with tangy, herby flavors of mint and cilantro. A healthy, gluten free protein packed snack for any day of the week!
