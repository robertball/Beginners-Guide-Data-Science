Title:
Mini Corn Dogs on a Stick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 wooden toothpicks
1 package cocktail wieners
1 can refrigerated flaky biscuit
1 egg, beaten
1 tbs milk
1/2 cup cornmeal
1 tbs sugar

Directions:
heat oven to 400. Grease cookie sheet with cooking spray. Insert a toothpick in narrow end of each wiener. Separate dough into 10 biscuits, and divide again each biscuit into 4 rounds. Wrap each wiener in dough, seal. In pie plate, mix egg and milk. On a plate, mix cornmeal and sugar. Roll each wrapped wiener in egg mixture, then roll in cornmeal mixture.Place seam down on cookie sheet. Bake 10-12 minutes, until golden brown. Remove with spatula.
