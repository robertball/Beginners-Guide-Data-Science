Title:
Vegetarian enchilada wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 corn tortillas
2 garlic cloves, crushed
2 medium zucchini, quartered and then sliced
1/4 c. red bell pepper, diced
8 oz. frozen corn
1 16-oz black beans, drained
1/4 tsp. cumin
1 c. salsa
1 c. red chile sauce
1/4 c. chopped cilantro
1 1/2 c. cheddar, grated

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute garlic in oil about 1 minute.  Add the zucchini and cook till crisp tender.  Stir in red pepper and corn and cook a little longer.  Stir in beans, cumin, salt to taste, and sauces.
Arrange 4 of the tortillas in an overlapping circle across the bottom of a lightly greased 10-inch glass pie pan.  Spoon a third of the bean and zucchini mix over the tortillas and 1/3 of cheese.  Continue with two more layers.  Bake at 350 for 35 to 40 minutes or until top is brown.
Allow to stand for 10 minutes before cutting into wedges.
