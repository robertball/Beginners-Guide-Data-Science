Title:
Picante Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/423193.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
1 Cipollino onion, peeled or minced (regular onion can be used as well)
1/2 red bell pepper, seeded and deveined, cut into slices
1 Cup spinach leaves chopped
3 egg whites + 1 whole egg
1/4 tsp sea salt
1/4 Cup skim milk
Pinch of cumin
1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1/2 cup fresh salsa (optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350F/180C. Make sure oven rack is in center.
2. In a ovenproof skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add veggies to the skillet and cook until soft, maybe 5 minutes.
3. while veggies are cooking combine eggs, milk, salt, cumin and pepper. Whisk well.
4. Pour egg mixture over cooking veggies. Cook until edges are set and center is just beginning to set.
5. Transfer to heated oven and bake another five to eight minutes or until center is firm.
6. Transfer cooked frittata to heated plate and serve with salsa (optional)
