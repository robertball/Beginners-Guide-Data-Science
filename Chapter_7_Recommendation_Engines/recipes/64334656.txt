Title:
Easy Chocolate Mousse Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2062089.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C Heavy Whipping Cream
3/4 Box of Instant Chocolate Pudding
Container of Cool Whip
3/4 C Mini Chocolate Chips
Oreo Pie Crust

Directions:
1.  Beat whipping cream with hand mixer until somewhat thickened.
2.  Pour in pudding mix and beat with mixer until thickened and begging to stick in the mixer.
3.  Spread mixture into Oreo pie crust.
4.  Top with Cool Whip and spread evenly.
5.  Sprinkle chips on top.
6.  Serve!
