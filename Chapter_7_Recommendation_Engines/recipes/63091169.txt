Title:
Warm and Hearty Cauliflower Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large heads of fresh Cauliflower
1/4 tsp. Kosher Salt
1/2 tsp. coarse ground Black Pepper
1/4 tsp. Red Pepper flakes
1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder or 2 tsp crushed fresh Garlic
1 tsp Hungarian Paprika
1/4 Cup REAL  Bacon Bits or crumbled cooked Bacon
1/2 cup shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1 can condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 small can or 1 1/2 cup French Fried onions
1/2 cup Shredded Parmesean Cheese

Directions:
Pre-Heat your oven to 375 degrees
trim and rinse cauliflower breaking apart into bite size flowerets.
place in 9x12 baking dish with 1/4 cup water cover with plastic wrap and microwave on high power for at least 5 minutes. You want the Cauliflower to be about half steamed. A large bowl is fine if the 9x12 will not fit in your microwave.
Remove from microwave pour off excess water but do not drain in a colander.
Sprinkle the Salt, Pepper, Red Pepper Flakes, Garlic, Paprika, Bacon bits and Mozzarella over the Cauliflower and toss to distribute evenly.
Stir in the mushroom soup until the Cauliflower is evenly coated then level out the mixture in the dish.
Top with the French Fried onions then the shredded Parmesean Cheese.
Cook uncovered in middle of oven for 45 minutes
Don't worry about the top burning it will look dark but won't burn if your oven temperature is correct.
Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
Enjoy

Submitter:

Description: 
This twist on Cauliflower Casserole combines the traditional(green bean casserole) style dish with a tastier, spicier flavor. Fresh Cauliflower, Bacon and Italian ingredients make this Casserole just delicious.
