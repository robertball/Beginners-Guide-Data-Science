Title:
Chicken Apple Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (32 ounce) Premium chicken broth
1 Macintosh apple
2 cups of orzo
4 tablespoons of Parmesan cheese
1 bay leaf
1/2 of a store bought precooked rotisserie chicken
1 cup of sliced carrots
1/2 cup of sliced celery
1/2 of an onion

Directions:
Dice up all of your ingredients. Strip the chicken and dice up the meat. Cut the apple into small cubes. Cook the chicken, apple and diced vegetables until soft. Add orzo and cook until soft.

Submitter: 
Tomsyco
Description: 
This is classic chicken noodle soup with a twist. It's easy to make and you use a precooked chicken.
