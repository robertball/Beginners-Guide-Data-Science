Title:
Easy Cannoli Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1075897.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) container part-skim ricotta cheese
1/3 cup confectioners' sugar
1/3 cup miniature chocolate chips
3 tablespoons cream cheese, softened
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 waffle bowls, broken into pieces, or more to taste

Directions:
Mix ricotta cheese, confectioners' sugar, chocolate chips, cream cheese, and vanilla extract together in a bowl. Sprinkle waffle bowl pieces over dip.
