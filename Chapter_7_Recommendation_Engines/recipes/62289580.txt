Title:
Slow Cooker Veggie-Beef Soup with Okra Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian diced tomatoes, drained
1 (16 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables
2 beef bouillion cubes
1 can tomato sauce
Italian seasoning
garlic, 1 or 2 cloves diced
1 cup sliced fresh or frozen okra
2 potatoes, peeled and chopped
1 tablespoon ketchup
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef and onion with garlic until beef is evenly brown and onion and garlic is tender. Drain grease.
In a slow cooker, mix the beef and onion, diced tomatoes, Italian diced tomatoes, vegetables, okra, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, bouillion, potatoes, ketchup, salt, and pepper. Pour in enough water to cover.
Cover slow cooker, and cook 4 hours on Low.
