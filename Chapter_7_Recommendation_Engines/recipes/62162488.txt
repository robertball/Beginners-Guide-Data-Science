Title:
Big Dan's Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 2 9 oz. Jiffy cornbread mixes
2. 1 9 oz. Jiffy golden yellow cake mix
3. 2/3 cup milk
4. 1/2 cup water
5. 3 eggs
6. 1 12 oz. package frozen corn kernels

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Thaw the frozen corn in a small bowl. Combine the two cornbread and the yellow cake mix. Beat 3 eggs with 1/2 cup of water and 2/3 cup of milk. Combine wet and dry. Finally fold in thawed corn kernels.
Separate into muffin tins using paper cupcake liners. Bake for 18-20 minutes. Check for doneness with a toothpick. Let cool and enjoy!
