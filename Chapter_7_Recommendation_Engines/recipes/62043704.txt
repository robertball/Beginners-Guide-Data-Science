Title:
Edible play dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C. peanut butter
3/4 C. dry milk
3/4 C. old fashioned oatmeal
1-2 tbsp. honey

Directions:
In food processor bowl, combine peanut butter, milk powder, and oatmeal; pulse to blend. Add enough honey to form a molding consistancy. Store dough in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Bobby Darapureddi
Description: 
Kids can use dough to form fun shapes, then eat them!
