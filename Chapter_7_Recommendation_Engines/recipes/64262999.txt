Title:
Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large potatoes, boiled & cooled
5 hard boiled eggs, shelled
1/2 cup Miracle Whip
1/2 cup mayonaaise
2 Tbsp milk
1 Tbsp mustard
2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp onion salt
Black pepper to taste
Paprika

Directions:
In a large bowl, cut up potatoes and eggs into small cubes.
In a separate small bowl, mix remaining ingredients except paprika. Add to potatoes and eggs and mix well.  Chill at least 2 hours.
Sprinkle with paprika before serving
