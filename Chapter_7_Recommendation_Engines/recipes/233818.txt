Title:
Zesty Pepper and Onion Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1036025.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (8.75 ounce) jar Dickinson'sÂ® Sweet 'n' Hot Pepper & Onion Relish
Assorted crackers

Directions:
Beat cream cheese in a medium bowl until smooth. Gradually mix in relish. Spoon into decorative bowl. Chill at least 1 hour. Serve with crackers.
For an easier option, unwrap block of cream cheese and place on decorative plate. Pour relish over top. Serve with small serving knife and crackers.

Submitter: 
Dickinson's
Description: 
Try this super-simple tangy pepper and onion dip the next time you need an easy appetizer. All it takes is prepared pepper and onion relish, cream cheese, and some crackers to serve with it.
