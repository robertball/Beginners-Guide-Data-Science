Title:
Baked Guacamole and Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of chicken breasts
1/3 cup of salsa
1/2 cup of shredded cheese
1 tablespoon of guacamole per breast

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350 and grease a baking pan
2. Lay out chicken breasts in baking pan
3. Spread salsa across chicken breasts
4. Sprinkle cheese across chicken breasts
5. Bake for 25-30 minutes
6. Spread one scoop of guacamole on each chicken breast
