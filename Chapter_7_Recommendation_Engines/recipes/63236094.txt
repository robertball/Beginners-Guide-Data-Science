Title:
Cheesy Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups quinoa
3 cups chicken broth
chopped, cooked broccoli
good pinch of salt
a few grinds of seasoning salt
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 large eggs
1 cup milk
1 1/2 cups grated Cheddar cheese, more for sprinkling
Bread crumbs for topping
chopped scallions and tomatoes if desired

Directions:
Cook quinoa with broth at medium boil. (About 20 minutes)
Preheat oven to 350 F. Coat 13x9 inch dish( or 8 individual ramekins) with cooking spray.Whisk together eggs and milk in large bowl. Fold in quinoa mixture and cheese. Stir very well and let some of the cheese melt.  Transfer to prepared baking dish top with Bread crumbs and bake 30-35 mins, until bread crumbs are browned. Top with scallions and tomatoes if desired
