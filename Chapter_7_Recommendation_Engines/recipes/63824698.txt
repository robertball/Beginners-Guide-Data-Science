Title:
Carmel Sticky Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of chopped pecans
18 Rhodes dinner rolls
1 Pkg cook and serve butterscotch pudding
1 stick of butter
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup of brown sugar

Directions:
Spray bundt pan with cooking spray and sprinkle nuts on bottom of pan. Add the frozen dinner roll balls. Sprinkle with pudding powder. Heat margarine, cinnamon and sugar. Pour over rolls. Cover with cloth and let sit overnight. Then bake at 350 degree for 30 minutes. Let pan cool 10 minutes before removing from pan.Turn upside down on plate and serve.
