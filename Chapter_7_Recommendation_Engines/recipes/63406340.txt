Title:
Hot Dog Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz. container of hot dog chili sauce, or make your own.
1 pkg. all beef hotdogs
1 small yellow onion, diced
1 c. sharp cheddar cheese
1 pkg. honey cornbread mix plus ingredients called for on box.
Desired amount of mustard and ketchup.

Directions:
Mix cornbread according to directions on box, set aside.
Line the bottom of an 8x8 glass baking dish with chili sauce.
Top with diced onion.
Cut hotdogs into 1/4" pieces.
Place hotdogs over chili and onion mixture, making sure to cover the entire dish.
Sprinkle cheese over hotdogs.
Pour cornbread batter over the cheese.
Bake 35 minutes or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
Cut into pieces, top with desired amount of mustard and ketchup.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
This is one of our favorites for a busy weeknight.  It's especially good if you make your own chili sauce.  I make a large batch and freeze it in portions to use in this dish.
