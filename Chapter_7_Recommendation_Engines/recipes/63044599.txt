Title:
Asiago Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/921554.jpg
Ingredients:
4 medium yukon gold potatoes, peeled and quartered
1 TBL minced garlic
1/2 cup non-fat milk
3 TBL whipped butter
1/2 cup asiago cheese, shredded
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes for about 20 minutes or until potatoes are fork tender.  Drain and place into large bowl.  Mash the potatoes with a potato masher.  Add in garlic, milk, butter, cheese, oregano, salt and pepper and whisk together.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
These awesome mashed potatoes, with the unique flavor of asiago cheese, garlic and oregano, are always a hit at the holidays or for potlucks.  Using non-fat milk and whipped butter reduces the fat and calories in these mashed potatoes but keeps the flavor.  The asiago, garlic and oregano takes the flavor of these mashed potatoes over the top.  Shhhh! Don't tell anyone these mashed potatoes are lower in fat because it tastes so good they will never know.
