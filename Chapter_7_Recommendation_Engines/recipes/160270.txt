Title:
Rhubourb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup crushed ice
2 fluid ounces bourbon
4 fluid ounces rhubarb soda

Directions:
Fill a highball glass with crushed ice. Pour in bourbon and rhubarb soda; stir and serve.

Submitter: 
c-biskit
Description: 
Rhubarb dry soda and slightly sweet bourbon combine into a delicious cocktail that's not too sweet and not too dry. Serve over crushed ice.
