Title:
Thumbprint Cookies, Original Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
3 tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. almond extract
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. salt
2 cups flour

Directions:
Mix butter and sugar until fluffy.  Add extracts; mix.  Add flour and salt; mix well.  Chill dough 10 minutes.  Roll into 1-inch balls.  Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet.  Press thumb print into center of each cookie.   Bake 10 minutes or until bottom is brown at 375 degrees.
Fill with purchased icing when cookie is completely cool.

Submitter: 
SUSU71
Description: 
This is a very easy recipe.  I like to fill the thumbprint with purchased icing.  Chocolate is my personal favorite, but I like to use colored icing (blue or pink) for a baby shower or yellow for a bridal shower.  Also green and red for Christmas is a  hit.
