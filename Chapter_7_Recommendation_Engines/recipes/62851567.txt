Title:
Chinese Beef and Pepper Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/937706.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup light soy sauce
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon dry sherry
2 teaspoons sugar substitute
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 small clove garlic, minced
1 pound cooked London broil, trimmed of all visible fat and cut into slivers about 1/8" wide.
1 medium red bell pepper, cut into very thin slices
2 green onions, sliced.

Directions:
In a small bowl whisk together the soy sauce, oil, sherry, sugar substitute, ginger, and garlic.  Add the beef and toss to coat.  Cover and refirgerate for up to 1 day, until ready to use.
Drain the beef and reserve the marinade.  Place the beef on a serving plate and toss with the pepper and green onions.  Serve with the reserved marinade on the side.
