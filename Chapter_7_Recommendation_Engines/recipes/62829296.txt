Title:
Blueberry Quinoa with Lemon Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients for Quinoa:6 cups unsweetened apple juice1 TBSP vanilla extract3 cups quinoa1 pint blueberriesIngredients for Lemon Glaze1 TBSP kuzu chunks or corn starch1 cup almond milkJuice from 1 medium lemon1 TBSP maple syrup2 TBSP water

Directions:
Blueberry Quinoa:  Rinse and dry fresh blueberries (could use frozen but need to thaw)Place apple juice, vanilla and quinoa in large pot and bring to a boil.  Cook approximately 15 min.  Gently fold the blueberries into the hot quinoa.  Pour immediately into a 9x13 baking pan.  Pour lemon glaze over the quinoa after the quinoa sets up. Chill at least 2 hours before serving.Lemon Glaze: In small saucepan, dissolve kuzu in the milk and water. Add the lemon juice and maple syrup. Cook over high heat stirring continuously, until thickened (about 2-3 minutes). Very slowly pour the hot glaze over the quinoa, making sure to cover it well.
