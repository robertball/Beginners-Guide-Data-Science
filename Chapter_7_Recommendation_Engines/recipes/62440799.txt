Title:
Snowball Cheese Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 oz.) pkgs. cream cheese, softened
1/3 cup crushed pineapple, drained & minced
1/3 cup minced green bell pepper
1/3 cup minced pecans
1 cup shredded coconut

Directions:
In a bowl, mix all ingredients except the coconut.  It will be sticky.
Divide in half and form 2 balls.
Put coconut on wax paper.  Roll balls in coconut until completely covered.
Carefully wrap each ball in plastic wrap, chill in refrigerator until well set.
Serve with toasted wheat crackers. (Wheat Thins)

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Serve these coconut covered cheese balls with wheat crackers at Christmas time.
