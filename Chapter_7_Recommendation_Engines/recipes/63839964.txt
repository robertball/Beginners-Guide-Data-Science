Title:
Chanterelle Mushroom Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
2 cups chicken broth
1 package chanterelle mushrooms (cleaned)
1 onion (chopped)
1 green pepper (sliced)
1 red pepper (sliced)
3 cups fresh spinach
1 teaspoon garlic
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 small can green chilis

Directions:
Clean mushrooms and cook in chicken broth on stovetop for 20 minutes.
Drain.
Mix all the ingredients in a flying pan for 10 minutes.
Warm tortilla on stovetop. Turn over. Sprinkle with cheese. Spoon mixture on half the tortilla. Fold over. Turn over.
Serve with guacamole.
