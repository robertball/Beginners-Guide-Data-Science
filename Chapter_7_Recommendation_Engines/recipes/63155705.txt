Title:
Habanero Cheddar Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/808894.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package of spreadable cheddar about 8 oz.
1 tsp of habanero powder
2 tsp of cayenne pepper
1 finely diced jalapeno pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients thoroughly, until dip is smoothy and creamy.
Serve with crackers or on a sandwich or with pretzels.
