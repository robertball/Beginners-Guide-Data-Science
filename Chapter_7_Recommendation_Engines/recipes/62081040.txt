Title:
spinach walnut and ham quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4eggs
1C whole milk
2C chopped fresh spinach
1C sliced ham, cut into 1/2 inch squares
1/2 medium onion
3 garlic cloves
1 1/2C shredded Gouda cheese
white pepper
Kosher salt
pinch of nutmeg
1/3 C cream cheese
1/2 C walnuts, chopped finely
Prepared, unbaked 9 inch pie shell

Directions:
1) in a medium bowl, beat eggs, milk and a pinch of white pepper and kosher salt until combined
2) Over medium low heat, saute onion and garlic untill translucent.  add ham, and saute until lightly brown. Season with pepper and salt. Add spinach and cook just for a little bit until starts to wilt. Add 1 cup of the Gouda cheese and cream cheese over low heat. Stirring constatly until all the cheese is melted. Take it off of the heat. Let it cool slightly.
3)In the prepared pie shell, place the spinach mixture on the bottom, and pour in the egg mixture. Stir gently to combine, be careful not to damage the crust. sprinkle the remaining Gouda cheese, and walnuts on top. Bake on 375 degree oven for about 35 to 45 minutes, or until it is set in the middle. Let it cool for about 10 minutes. serve warm.
