Title:
Pan Fried Swai Fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1278113.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Swai fillets
1 cup cornmeal
Salt and pepper, to taste
2 tsp red pepper, crushed
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp old bay seasoning
2 tbsp canola oil
Â¼ cup water

Directions:
Season the fillets with all the spices and cover with the cornmeal.
Wrap the fillets up in cling film and place in the fridge for 30 minutes
Heat the oil in a pan and place the fish fillets. Fry on both sides so that the fish is browned.
Once brown on both sides of all fillets, add the water and simmer on a medium heat. Cover and flip occasionally. Ensure that the fillets are cooked evenly. Simmer for 10 minutes.
Serve warm with a garnish of your choice.
