Title:
Cheese Log Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Velveeta, 16 ounces
1 package cream cheese , 8 ounces*
5 green onions, chopped fine
1 can diced green chili, 4 ounces
3/4 cup pecans, chopped

Directions:
Roll out Velveeta on waxed paper, approximately 1/6 of an inch thick.
Spread cream cheese evenly over the cheese.
Sprinkle with onions, chilis and pecans.
Roll up, cinnamon roll style. Cover and chill for at least an hour.
Garnish with extra onions and pecans.
Serve with crackers.
*I use whipped cream cheese.
