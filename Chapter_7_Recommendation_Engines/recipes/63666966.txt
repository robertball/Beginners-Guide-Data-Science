Title:
Swedish Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup rice
4 cups whole white milk
2 eggs - beaten with additional 1/2 cup milk
1 cup sugar
1 tsp salt
2 tsp cornstarch
1 tsp vanilla  (or 1/2 vanilla 1/2 almond flavoring)
1 1/2 cups milk
Cinnamon

Directions:
Bring rice and milk mistire to boil in double boiler. Cover and reduce heat. Cook slowly one hour+  until rice is soft. Stir occasionally.
Thoroughly mix the eggs and seasonings, and set aside.
Pour milk in heavy pan and add cooked rice.  Just before it comes to a boil gradually add the mixed  ingredients.  Let come slowly to a boil stirring to prevent curdling.
Pour into a bowl and crisscross the top with sprinkled cinnamon.
