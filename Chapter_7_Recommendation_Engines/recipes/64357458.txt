Title:
Italian Beef Stroganof Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2135235.jpg
Ingredients:
200 g Stewing beef, diced
1 large carrot , diced
1 medium celery stalk
250 g fresh mushrooms, sliced
3 mini bell peppers (assorted colors)
1 medium onion, diced (large)
30 ml Extra virgin Olive oil
5 ml Mrs. Dash original
5 ml Mrs. Dash South Western Chilpotle (omit this if sensitive to hot spices)
250 ml PC Original Pasta sauce
125 ml water
225 g Heartland Whole Wheat Rotini (cooked according to package)

Directions:
Saute the carrots celery and peppers in a wok or large skillet, 5 minutes later add the beef and the onions and mushrooms and saute until beef is browned
add the Mrs. dash(es) and stir, then add the pasta sauce and water, simmer uncovered for about 20 Minutes (while cooking the pasta), until water has evaporated and sauce has thickened.
drain pasta then fold into the sauce mixture

Submitter: 
Tony Carriere
Description: 
An Italian version of the classic sour cream beef broth based meal, with a spicy southwestern kick
