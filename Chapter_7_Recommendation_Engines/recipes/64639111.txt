Title:
5 Minute Magic Green Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 avocado
1 cup packed parsley
1 jalepeno, ribs & seeds removed
2 cloves garlic
juice of 1-2 limes
1/c cup water
1/2 cup olive oil
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup pistachios

Directions:
Pulse all ingredients--except pistachios--in food processor until incorporated
Add pistachios & pulse until smooth

Submitter: 
libton
Description: 
Good as a dip or spread or sauce. Add additional water or oil to thin sauce for dressing or marinade
