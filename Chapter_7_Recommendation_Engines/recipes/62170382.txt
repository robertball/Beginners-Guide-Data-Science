Title:
Wonderful hidden greens smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 1shredded zucchini(not over ripe)
1/2 1frozen banana
1/2c orange juice
ice(opt)

Directions:
Blend all the ingredients together.
add sugar/sweetener if you have a sweet tooth but i find the smoothie to be plenty sweet with the banana.
Enjoy
note; using over ripe or dark green zucchini will make this smoothie not so yummy.

Submitter: 
Isabela14
Description: 
Zucchini in a smoothie blended with o.j and banana make a healthy and great tasting breakfast smoothie. And don't worry you can't even taste the zucchini in it.
