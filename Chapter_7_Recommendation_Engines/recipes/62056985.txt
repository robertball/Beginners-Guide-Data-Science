Title:
Apple Cinnamon Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cup chopped dried apples
cup dried cranberries
cup coarsely chopped almonds
cup sunflower seeds
1  cups crisp rice cereal
3 cups granola cereal
cup margarine
cup brown sugar
cup apple juice concentrate
cup corn syrup
2 tbsp flour
1 tsp cinnamon
tsp cloves

Directions:
Spread an 11x15 jelly roll pan with waxed paper.
Combine first six ingredients in a large bowl.
Melt margarine in a pot.  Add the rest of the ingredients.  Bring to a boil and boil 2 minutes.  Pour over the granola mixture and mix well.
Spread in the jelly roll pan and press in firmly.   Allow to cool before cutting into bars.

Submitter: 
IAMJENNIE
Description: 
A no-bake, chewy granola bar made with dried apples, almonds, cranberries and sunflower seeds.
