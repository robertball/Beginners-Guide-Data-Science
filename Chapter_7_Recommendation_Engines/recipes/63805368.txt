Title:
Grandma's Rustic Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-cloves of garlic chopped,
2-onions chopped, 4- carrots chopped thick, 2-red potatoes cut into small cubes, grape tomatoes cut in half, 1-cup of peas,
2-qts chicken stock, 2-bay leaves, 1-teaspoon dry oregano,
1-28 can Italian plum tomatoes or 1-container Mama Mancini marinara.
some kind of smaller pasta...ditilini works well.. You could also add some chicken, beef or sausage (Brown first). Vegetable stock can be used

Directions:
Bring to a boil and simmer on low for 2 hours...add the pasta and cook till andante... covered.

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
I call this a rustic soup because it's hearty but also because you use what's in the fridge. This is how soups were made a long time ago nothing was wasted. You could also add some chicken, beef or sausage.
