Title:
Fruitcake cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup margarine
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/8 tsp salt
1 lb (2 2/3 cups) snipped dates
4 oz (2/3 cup) candied cherries
1/2 cup candied pineapples
1/2 cup coarsely chopped pecans
1/2 cup chopped filberts
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
1. In a large mixing bowl, cream sugar and margarine until light and fluffy. Add egg and vanilla extract, beat well.
2. Stir together flour, soda and salt. Add to creamed mixture, beating until combined. Stir in fruits and nuts by hand. Chill batter.
3. Drop by slightly rounded tablespoons onto greased cookie sheet.
4. Bake in 325Â° oven for 12 minutes. Let cool on wire racks. Store baked cookies in airtight container overnight to soften.
