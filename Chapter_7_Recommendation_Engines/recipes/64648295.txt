Title:
Taco Salad in a Jar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4539439.jpg
Ingredients:
80 grams iceberg lettuce (approximately two cups)
1/4 cup canned black beans, drained
1/4 cup canned pinto beans, drained
6 cherry tomatoes, diced
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese, divided
1/4 cup sliced black olives
3/4 cup cooked chorizo sausage, crumbled
1 cup crushed tortilla chips
1/4 cup sour cream
2 Tbsp salsa verde
a quart glass jar
a 4-ounce glass jar

Directions:
In the smaller glass jar, mix together the sour cream and salsa verde. Put the dressing in the refrigerator.
Tear the lettuce into bite-size pieces. Put half the lettuce into the larger jar. Place half the cheddar cheese on top of the lettuce. Add the pinto beans. Place the remaining lettuce in the jar. Add the black beans. Add the remaining cheddar. Add the diced tomatoes. Add the chorizo. Top with the tortilla chips. Close the lid.
When ready to eat, shake and drizzle the dressing on top. Shake again. Eat. Enjoy.
