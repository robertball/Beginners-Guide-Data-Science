Title:
Swiss Meringue Buttercream Recipe

Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
4 large egg whites
16 tablespoons butter, softened
1 teaspoon clear imitation vanilla extract

Directions:
Whisk egg whites and sugar together in a double boiler until you canâ€™t feel the sugar granules when you rub the mixture between your fingers.
Transfer mixture into mixer and whip until it turns white and about doubles in size.
Add the vanilla.
Add butter gradually and whip to desired texture.
