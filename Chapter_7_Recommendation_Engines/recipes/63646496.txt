Title:
Best Black Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) can black beans, drained & rinsed
3/4 cup chicken broth
1 small onion, chopped
2 clove garlic, chopped
1 tsp. cumin
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon lime juice
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine beans, onion, chicken broth, cayenne, cumin and garlic, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low. Season with lime and salt. Simmer for 15 minutes, and serve.

Submitter: 
Phyllis Trinke Funk
Description: 
This simple black bean side dish works well with Mexican or Cuban meals.Modified from: Best Black Beans
