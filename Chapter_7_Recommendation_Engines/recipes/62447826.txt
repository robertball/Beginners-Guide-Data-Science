Title:
Oriental Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Cole Slaw Mix
1/2 medium Onion, chopped
Sesame Seeds to liking
2 packages Oriental Top Ramen
1/4 cup Margarine
1/3 cup Vinegar
1/2 cup Sugar
2/3 cup oil (as needed)
2 T. Soy Sauce
1 pkg Oriental flavoring
Chopped Crispy Chicken Tenders
1.5 pkg Sliced Almonds

Directions:
Melt margarine in skillet. Add broken top ramen, sesame seeds, and almonds. Saute til brown. Cool.
Heat vinegar, sugar, oil, soy sauce, oriental flavoring until boiling. Boil 1-2 minutes. Cool.
Combine all ingredients when ready to serve. Toss with dressing. Add crispy chicken tenders.
