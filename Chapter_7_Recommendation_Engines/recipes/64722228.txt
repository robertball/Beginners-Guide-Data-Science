Title:
Soda Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1/3 cup sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 (12-ounce) can soda
3 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 5" x 9" loaf pan with cooking spray. Set aside.
Sift flour, sugar, baking powder and salt together in a large bowl. Add soda and mix until combined (Dough will be sticky). Place dough into loaf pan. Pour melted butter over top of loaf.
Bake at 350 degrees, uncovered, for 50-60 minutes, or until top is golden brown and toothpick comes out clean from center.
Serve warm or cold.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Kedney'
Description: 
A homemade version of Pampered Chef's  Beer Bread.  I replaced the beer with soda for our high school Culinary Arts classes.
