Title:
Swedish Cinnamon Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, melted
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 Tbsp corn syrup
2-1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 Tbsp cinnamon
Almond halves, blanched

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix melted butter and sugar.  Add egg and corn syrup and mix well.
Add flour, baking soda and cinnamon and mix well.
Roll in small balls 1 inch in size and top each with a half blanched almond.
Bake at 400 degrees about 10 minutes or until set when touched with finger.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
A butter cookie with a cinnamon twist, topped with an almond.
