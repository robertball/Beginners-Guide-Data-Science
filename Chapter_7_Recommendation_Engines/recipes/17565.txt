Title:
Virgin Pina Colada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6750369.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice
1 1/4 cups pineapple juice
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup heavy cream
2 tablespoons white sugar

Directions:
In an electric blender, blend ice, pineapple juice, milk, cream, and sugar. Blend until smooth.

Description:
