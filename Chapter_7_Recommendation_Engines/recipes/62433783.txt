Title:
Jiffy Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 Tbsp. sugar
4 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1/4 cup cold butter/margarine
1 cup milk
1/3 cup butter/margarine, softened
1 cup brown sugar
3 tsp. cinnamon
1/3 cup raisins
Glaze:
1/2 cup icing sugar (add enough milk to make a thin glaze). Drizzle over cinnamon rolls.

Directions:
In large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Cut in first amount of butter until crumbly. Make a well in the center.
Pour milk into well. Stir to form soft dough  by adding a bit more milk if needed. Turn out on lightly floured surface. Knead 8-10 times. Roll into rectangles about 1/3 inch (1 cm) thick and 12 inches long.
Cream second amount of butter, brown sugar and cinnamon together well. Drop 1 tsp. bits into 12 greased muffin tins. Spread remaining cinnamon mixture over dough rectangle. Sprinkle raisins over top. Roll up like a jelly roll. Cut into 12 slices. Place cut side down in muffin pan.
Bake 400 for 20-25 minutes.
