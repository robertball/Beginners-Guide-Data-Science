Title:
Sausage and Brew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975150.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
2 plum tomatoes - chopped
1 cup beer
3/4 cup water, divided
1 bay leaf
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
13.5 oz.pkg.Johnsonville smoked sausage, sliced thin
1 large onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 lb egg noodles - cooked

Directions:
Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the all the vegetables and saute for 10 minutes, or until tender. Add the beer, 1/2 cup water, bay leaf, thyme, ground black pepper and salt. Simmer and stir for about 10 minutes.
Add the sausage and simmer for another 10 minutes. Remove bay leaf.
In a separate small bowl, combine the flour and remaining 1/4 cup water. Mix well, making sure the flour is completely dissolved. Stir this mixture into the meat mixture. Cook until thickened and bubbly.
Serve over egg noodles or rice.

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
Thin sliced sausage, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, tomatoes, and onion are simmered with beer to make a thick sauce, then served over egg noodles or rice.Modified from: Beef and Brew
