Title:
Fruit Nut Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2027755.jpg
Ingredients:
zest from 1/2 orange (1 tsp or more)
1/4 c orange juice from half a large orange
1/2 c prunes and dates
1/2 c goji berries
3/4 c chopped almonds
1 1/2 cups (5 ounces) quick oats
2 T ground flax
1 T whole flax seed
2 T almond flour
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons (3 ounces) almond butter
1/4 cup coconut oil
1/8 c honey
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
preheat oven to 325
add orange juice to chopped fruit and microwave 1 min or more to soften; let sit
combine zest, almond butter, oil, honey, vanilla
combine dry ingredients
combine fruit, wet ingredients, dry ingredients
press into 9x13 in pan
bake 25 min at 325
