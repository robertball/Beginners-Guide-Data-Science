Title:
Roasted Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/935206.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bulb garlic, unpeeled
2 lbs red potatoes
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup heavy cream
3 tbsp butter
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Wrap garlic tightly in aluminum foil.
2. Roast in a preheated 400 oven for 45 minutes, until soft.
3. Unwrap & let cool.
4. Peel the cloves & squeeze out the pulp.
5. Wash & rinse the potatoes under cold water, halve or quarter potatoes.
6. In a large pot, cook the potatoes at a slow boil for about 20 minutes.
7. Remove from heat & drain.
8. In a large saucepan or bowl, combine the potatoes & roasted garlic with the remaining ingredients and mash with potato masher.

Creamy mashed potatoes with a garlic kick.
