Title:
Chicken Romano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 green bell pepper, diced
6 sun-dried tomatoes
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tbsp granulated garlic
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
2 tsp dried thyme
2 tsp cayenne
1/4 cup breadcrumbs
3/4 cup grated romano cheese
1 tbsp olive oil
6 thin sliced, scallopine style chicken breasts
3 oz. romano cheese cut into thin slices or curls if desired

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 375F
- Mix granulated garlic, salt, pepper, thyme, cayenne, breadcrumbs and cheese in a shallow bowl.
- Coat each chicken breast in cheese mixture, pressing into chicken well. Place chicken into baking dish.
- Bake chicken for 25 minutes, or until slightly golden.
- While chicken is cooking, preheat non-stick skillet with olive oil over medium heat.
- Add green pepper, tomatoes & garlic to skillet. Season w/ salt & pepper is desired. Cook 10 minutes or until peppers are tender.
- Place chicken on plate, top with pepper & tomato mixture.
- Garnish with romano curls if desired.

Description: 
I made this up one night with ingredients I had on hand, and now it is a weekly request in our house!  Everyone always asks for the recipe, and it is so simple. Serve it over a bed of mixed greens for a fancy presentation, or over pasta for a more traditional Italian feel!
