Title:
Grandma Jordan's Herb Roasted Chicken and Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 chicken wings and/or drumsticks
4 medium potatoes, quartered
1 package (8 oz) baby carrots or 2-3 cups carrots chopped to similar size
1 medium onion, cut in eighths
2 Tbsp. olive oil
4 cloves garlic
1 1/2 tsp. dried basil
1 tsp. dried rosemary

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Line a rectangular pan with foil.
Place chicken pieces in pan and brush with 1 Tbsp. olive oil.
In medium bowl, combine basil and rosemary; sprinkle half the mixture on the chicken.
Add remaining olive oil, garlic, potatoes, carrots, and onion to bowl with herb mixture; stir to coat.
Arrange veggies in an even layer around chicken.
Cover dish with foil; fold back one corner of cover for heat circulation.
Bake 45-50 minutes or until veggies are done and meat thermometer reads 170 for white meat and 180 for dark meat.

Submitter:

Description: 
I got this simple and tasty chicken dish from my grandma.  Enjoy!
