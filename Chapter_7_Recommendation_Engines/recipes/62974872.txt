Title:
Cabbage Roll Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb 93% lean ground beef
3 cloves garlic
1 large onion
1 14 oz can diced tomatoes
water (approx 4 cups - depending on how much broth you like)
3 beef bouillon cubes
1 head cabbage, chopped
1 cup dry rice or cooked separately

Directions:
Brown hamburger, garlic and onion.
Add tomatoes, bouillon cubes, and enough water to cover. Bring to boil. Cover and simmer for 30 min.
Add cabbage and more water if necessary. Replace cover and simmer for 30 min, stirring occasionally.
Add uncooked rice and more water if desired. Replace cover and simmer for 20 min or rice is tender, stirring occasionally.
