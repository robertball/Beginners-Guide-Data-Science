Title:
Chocolate Chip Cranberry Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c white whole wheat flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp cinammon
1 cup light butter
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
1/2 cup Splenda
1 tsp vanilla
1 pkg vanilla pudding mix
2 eggs
1/2 cup white chocolate chips
1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
1 cup fresh or frozen cranberries
3 cups old fashioned oats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together flour, soda, & cinnamon.
Cream together butter, sugar, Splenda until fluffy.  Add vanilla, eggs, & pudding mix.  Mix for 30 seconds at high.
Add flour & chocolate chips. Stir in cranberries and oats.
May be made ahead at this point and put in fridge or freezer.
Use a cookie scoop to portion onto a parchment lined baking sheet (1 1/2 inches apart). Bake for 4-5  minutes and flatten cookies. Bake and additional 4-5 minutes until edges are brown. Let cool on a wire rack for at least 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kimberly
Description: 
A good for you soft oatmeal cookie that is bursting with dark chocolate, white chocolate & fresh cranberries. Perfect for holiday cookie season!
