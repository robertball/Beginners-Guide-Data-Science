Title:
Zucchini Pasta Shrimp Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/4 cup yellow onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced (about 3 teaspoons)
1 teaspoon Sriracha Sauce
2 large zucchini
1 cup chicken broth
1 lemon, zest
1 lemon, juiced
1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
1 pound large cooked shrimp
Extra virgin olive oil for drizzle
Fresh parsley, for garnish

Directions:
Using a spiralizer to create your pasta noodles.  Let sit in a colander.
In a large saut√© pan over medium heat add olive oil. Once hot add the onion and garlic until soft.
Add sriracha, chicken broth, lemon zest and juice from half a lemon. Add in the zucchini noodles, salt, and pepper and toss around.  Cook for about 2 minutes.
Add in shrimp and let warm through tossing around.
To serve top with parmesan cheese and fresh parsley then serve family style!
