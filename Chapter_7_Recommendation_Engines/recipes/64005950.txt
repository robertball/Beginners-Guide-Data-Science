Title:
Slow-Cooker Barbecue Ribs-Revised Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds pork baby back ribs
salt and pepper to taste
1/2  cup ketchup
1 cup chili sauce
1 cup barbeque sauce
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 1/2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
3 dashes hot sauce
1 dash liquid smoke
2 Tablespoons finely chopped onion

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Remove silver skin on back of ribs.
Season ribs with salt, pepper, onion powder and garlic powder. Place in a shallow baking pan. Brown in oven 15 minutes. Turn over, and brown another 15 minutes. You may have to use the broiler; drain fat.
In a medium bowl, mix together the ketchup, chili sauce, barbeque sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, oregano, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, liquid smoke, onion and salt and pepper to taste. Place ribs in slow cooker. Pour sauce over ribs, and turn to coat.
Cover, and cook on High 1 hour, and then Low 6 hours, or until ribs are tender.

Submitter: 
Ruth Rae
Description: 
Slow cooker barbecue ribs are an easy and delicious way of making barbecue ribs without the barbecue.Modified from: Slow-Cooker Barbecue Ribs
