Title:
Chili Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans Chili Con Carne
1 (3oz)pkg cream cheese
1 (8oz) corn muffin mix
1 cup cottage cheese

Directions:
Empty cans of chile into 10-inch skillet cover with cream cheese cut into small pieces. Heat chili and cream cheese to boiling in covered skillet over medium heat. Meanwhile blend muffin mix according to package and fold in cottage cheese (Do Not Blend) Pour heated chili mix into 2 1/2 Qt dish and top with muffin mix. Bake at 450 degrees for 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Pamela Euless-Barker
Description: 
For those cold winter nights this will warm you up. 
It's a great meal to serve at a football party.
