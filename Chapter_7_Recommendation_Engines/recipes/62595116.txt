Title:
Miracle Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar spaghetti sauce
1lb browned ground beef (if desired)
6 uncooked lasagna noodles
15 oz ricotta cheese
2c schredded mozzarella
1/4 c parmesan cheese

Directions:
In 2qt dish, spread 1c spaghetti sauce.
Top with 3 noodles, ricotta, 1c mozzarella, parmesan, and 1c sauce.
Add 3 moodles and cover with remaining sauce.
Cover and bake 1 hour at 375.
Top with 1c mozzarella and let stand 5 minutes.
