Title:
All Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen fresh strawberries
1/2 cup frozen fresh blueberries
1 banana
1 1/2 cups orange juice

Directions:
In a blender on high, blend strawberries, blueberries and orange juice.  If too thick, add more orange juice or water to thin.
Once blended, add banana and blend until completely mixed.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Maria Tedjamulia
Description: 
A dairy-free smoothie that's super yummy, nutritious....and EASY!
