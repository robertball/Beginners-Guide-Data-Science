Title:
Bonles Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Hormel 24oz. Boneless Pork Roast (Roast Flavor)
2 Med. Onions(thin slices)
2 Med. Red Potatoes (cut into wedges)
2 Carrots (peal and cut length wise)
6 Garlic Clove (peal and leave whole)
Fresh ground pepper to taste
Salt to tastes
1 14oz Can Beef Broth
Olive oil

Directions:
Let pork sit to room temperature and season with pepper and salt.
Place the onions, potatoes, carrots, garlic, pepper & salt in a Dutch Oven (oval best)
Heat a skillet on high heat and when hot put 1.1/2 turn of olive oil.
Place the pork on the skillet and brown on all sides.
Take the pork out and let it rest for about 3 minutes.
Place the pork inside the Dutch Oven on top of the aromatics.  Add the beef broth. (should cover half of the pork)
Preheat oven to 350 and when ready cover the Dutch Oven (make sure is a tight fit) place on oven for 1 hr. At the end the temperature of the pork should be 155.  Take out and of Dutch oven and let it rest for 10 minutes check the temperature again it should now be 160.
Slices and serve the aromatics and liquid over the pork.
