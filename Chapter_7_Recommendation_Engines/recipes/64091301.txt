Title:
Sicilian Chicken & Grape Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cooked chicken, diced
1/4 cup Sicilian salad dressing
35 gm walnuts, chopped
25 gm red onion, minced
Lettuce
Red grapes
Mango slices

Directions:
Mix chicken, Sicilian salad dressing, walnuts and red onion in a bowl with a rubber spatula.  Serve on top of an individual plate of lettuce.  Garnish with red grapes and mango slices.
