Title:
Marshmallow Strawberry Vivian's Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-10 oz pkg frozen no sugar added strawberries, not drained
1 (16 ounce) package reduced or no fat cottage cheese
lg tub frozen lite whipped topping
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
1 (1 ounce) package sugar free strawberry flavored gelatin mix
1 (3 ounce) package sugar free strawberry flavored gelatin mix
1/2 bag of mini marshmallows (french vanilla are especially good in this, if available)

Directions:
In a bowl, stir together strawberries & gelatin; stir until gelatin appears dissolved; add cottage cheese,and pineapple. Cover, and refrigerate about an hour; when semi set, fold in the whipped topping, and the mini marshmallows.  Refrigerate at least 1 more hour before serving.

Submitter: 
empebi
Description: 
This very easy, light, and refreshing strawberry salad or dessert combines cottage cheese, frozen whipped topping,crushed pineapple,mini marshmallows and sugar free strawberry gelatin.  The whole family loves this any time of the year.Modified from: Vivian's Delight
