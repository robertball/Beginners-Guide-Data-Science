Title:
Mango Salsa with Red Bell Pepper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 mango, diced small
1/2 large red bell pepper, diced small
1/4 cup diced red onion, diced small
2 - 3 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 teaspoon ground cumin (or less if you don't like cumin that much)
1/2 teaspoon green Tabasco sauce or your favorite Hot sauce (more or less to taste)
Pinch sea salt

Directions:
Chop mango, red pepper and red onion into pieces about 3/8 inch square, and combine in bowl.
Mix lime juice, olive oil, cumin, green Tabasco sauce and salt in small bowl, whisk together and mix into salsa.
Add chopped cilantro and stir until combined.
