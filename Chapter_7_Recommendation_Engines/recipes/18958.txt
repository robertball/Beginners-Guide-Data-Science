Title:
Peach Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7525649.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (11.5 ounce) can peach nectar
1 1/2 cups orange juice

Directions:
Fill 2 tall glasses with ice cubes. Pour in equal parts peach nectar and orange juice to fill the glasses. Stir.
