Title:
Giblet gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/924967.jpg
Ingredients:
•	giblets from 1 turkey
•	4 cups cold water
•	4 tablespoons butter
•	4 tablespoons flour
•	2 cups pan drippings or chicken broth
•	1/2 cup milk
•	1/2 teaspoon each salt and pepper
•	2 hard-cooked eggs, chopped

Directions:
Place giblets in saucepan, cover with 4 cups cold water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for about an hour and a half. Drain, allow to cool, chop and set aside. Melt butter in a heavy saucepan and stir in the flour. Cook and stir for 3 to 5 minutes, or until butter barely begins to turn golden. Slowly stir in drippings or chicken broth and milk. Continue cooking and stirring until thickened. Season with salt and pepper. Stir in hard-cooked eggs and chopped giblets and serve.
Makes 3 cups.

Submitter: 
tybright31
Description: 
This is a Southern Style Giblet gravy. My family uses this over our Thanksgiving Dressing.
