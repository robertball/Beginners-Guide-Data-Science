Title:
Our Best Bites Honey Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4481785.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (room temperature)
2/3 cup honey
3/4 cup powdered sugar
Variation: add 1-2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Beat all ingredients with electric mixer until light and fluffy (approximately 30 seconds).
