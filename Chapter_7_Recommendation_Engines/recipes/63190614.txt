Title:
Artichoke Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch green onions, chopped, including some of tops
1/2 cup parsley, chopped
1 lb. sharp cheddar, grated (see note)
14 crumbled saltine crackers
12 oz marinated artichoke hearts, cut up
dash Tabasco
2 cloves garlic (opt)
9 eggs, beaten
salt and pepper

Directions:
Saute onion in 3 tablespoons marinade mixture from artichoke hearts.  Mix well with other ingredients.  Pour into 13 x 9 pan, bake at 325 degrees 40 minutes or until center is puffy and done.  Cool slightly before cutting into squares.  Can be served warm or cool.
