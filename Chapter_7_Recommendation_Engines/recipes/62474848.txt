Title:
Alberta Beef Barley Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb - Stewing Beef
6 cups - Hot Water
2 - Beef Bouillon Cubes
1 pkg - Onion Soup Mix
1/2 lg - Onion
3 lg - Potatoes
2 - Carrots
2 stalks - Celery
2 tbsp - Minced Garlic
1 1/2 cup - Pearl Barley

Directions:
Heat two (2) cups water to boiling in microwave, add beef bouillon cubes and Onion Soup Mix, stir until disolved.  Pour mixture in Slow Cooker - set on High Heat.  Add remainder of water.
Cut raw stewing beef and potatoes into one inch cubes, place into slow cooker.
Dice carrots, onions and celery, place in slow cooker.
Add remainder of ingredients, but NOT the Barley!
Simmer in Slow Cooker for 4 1/2 - 5 hrs stirring occasionally, check potatoes for doneness (should cut easily with edge of fork) but not mushy.
Add Barley at this time, and continue cooking for 30 mins.
Turn slow cooker off and uncover, let cool for 5 mins, will thicken while standing.

Submitter: 
Mike Purll
Description: 
A hearty beef & barley concoction that is sure to warm the heart and soul on the coolest of days.  This dish uses ingredients that you can find in most kitchens... combine them in your slow cooker and in a short while, voila.  A dish your family will be asking for seconds...
