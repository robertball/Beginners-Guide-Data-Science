Title:
"Everything" Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7719250.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp poppy seeds
1 Tbsp sesame seeds
1 Tbsp dried minced garlic
1 Tbsp dried minced onion
1-1/2 tsp kosher salt

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together poppy seeds, sesame seeds, dried minced garlic, dried minced onion, and kosher salt together, then transfer to an airtight jar.

Submitter: 
lutzflcat
Description: 
Save yourself some money, and start mixing up your own all purpose seasoning good on bagels, scrambled eggs, hot popcorn, chaffles, mac and cheese, bloody Marys, cheese balls, bread, buns, etc.
