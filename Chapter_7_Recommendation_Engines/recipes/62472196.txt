Title:
Spicy Lentils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup lentils
14 oz habanero infused, green chile infused, or plain diced tomatoes
1 medium onion, diced
2 Tablespoons tomato paste
1 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Boil the lentils in 3 cups of water for 20 minutes. Drain. Saute onions in frying pan until soft. Add lentils, tomatoes, chicken broth, and tomato paste. Simmer for 20 minutes. Serve with rice, if desired.

Submitter: 
Jessica Weber
Description: 
Lentils, onions, and a can of habanero or green chile infused tomatoes make this dish a quick and inexpensive way to pack flavor and heat all into one meal.
