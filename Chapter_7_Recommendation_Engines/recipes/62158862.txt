Title:
Country Sausage Breakfast Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (contains 2) mini 8" Boboli pizza crusts
1/4 cup sage sausage roll
3 large eggs
1/4 cup half and half
3 tbs shredded swiss or guyere cheese
3 tbs shredded cheddar cheese
2 tbs butter, divided
1 tbs flour
1/8 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Place pizza crusts on a baking sheet and cook for 5 minutes, remove from oven and set aside.
Heat a medium size skillet to medium-high heat and crumble sausage and cook until browned.
Remove sausage with slotted spoon and place on a plate with a paper towel to drain.
Reduce heat on pan to medium-low
Add 1tbs butter and melt
Add flour and stir until melted to make a rue
Add half and half and stir to remove browned bits from bottom of skillet, stir until thick
Add salt and pepper and onion powder and stir
Add drained sausage back to pan and coat the sausage with the gravy
Remove from heat and divide evenly over pizza crusts
Heat a small non-stick pan over low heat, and melt in 1tbs butter then scramble the eggs.
Remove from heat and divide evenly over pizza crusts.
Top with shredded cheeses.
Place back in the heated oven for 3-4 minutes or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Ingalyn
Description: 
The great tastes of biscuits and gravy on a pre-made pizza crust.  Scrambled eggs and cheese make it a filling meal.
