Title:
Garden Vegetable Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8oz) block cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup miracle salad dressing
1 package dry vegetable soup mix

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.  Chill.  It is best if made at least several hour ahead of time.  Serve with raw vegetables or crackers

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
This dip is great with raw vegetables or on crackers.
