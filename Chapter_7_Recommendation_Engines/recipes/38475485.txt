Title:
Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shredded and chopped cabbage
1 grated carrot
1 tbl minced (fine) onion
2 tbl sugar
2 tbl vinegar
a shake of salt and pepper
Miracle Whip

Directions:
Toss together the cabbage, carrot and onion.
Toss together the sugar, vinegar and salt and pepper.  Put this mixture in refrigerator till ready.
When ready to serve the cole slaw, mix in Miracle Whip into the cabbage mixture. Mom didn't write down how much so start with a cup and go from there.
Stir in vinegar mixture right after Miracle Whip.
Serve promptly.
