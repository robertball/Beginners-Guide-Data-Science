Title:
Slow Cooker Turkey Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet Lipton Onion Soup mix
1/2 cup orange juice
1 can cranberry sauce
1 turkey breast

Directions:
mix all together and pour over turkey breast in crockpot.  Salt and pepper the meat.  Cook on low 6-8 hours or until meat reaches 180F.  Let rest 5 min before carving.

Submitter:
