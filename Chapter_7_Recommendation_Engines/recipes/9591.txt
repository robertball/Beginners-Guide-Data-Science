Title:
Everything Cookies I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/973569.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
1 egg, beaten
1 teaspoon cream of tartar
1 cup quick cooking oats
1 cup vegetable oil
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup crisp rice cereal
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
Mix all ingredients.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake until lightly brown.

Submitter: 
J. Clark
Description: 
As the name implies, these cookies have everything.
