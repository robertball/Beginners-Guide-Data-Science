Title:
Skinny Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4547030.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups  new potatoes (I use a small bag)
1/2 cup plain Greek yoghurt
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 tablespoon cooking sherry
2-3 tablespoons  prepared brown mustard
2 chopped green onions
1 stalk chopped green onion
2 tablespoons chopped dill pickle
1 tablespoon pickle juice
Salt and pepper to taste. I start with 1/2 tsp salt.

Directions:
Cover potatoes with salted water and boil 20 minutes or to tender.
Remove from heat. Drain. Cool.
Chop onions celery and pickles.
Mix yoghurt, mayo, pickle juice, mustard and salt and pepper
Toss everything together with the potatoes. Season to taste.

Submitter: 
MairsyDoats
Description: 
Years ago I found a similar recipe in Readers Digest. Delicious potato salad with very little mayonnaise. Greek yoghurt takes it's place.
