Title:
Homemade Chicken (or Seafood) Fettucini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless skinnless chicken breasts,
cut in chunks (or 1 pkg imitation crab chunks & 1 sm bag frozen cooked salad shrimp)
1 can or 8 oz fresh sliced mushrooms
2 T butter or margerine
1 1/2 c heavy whipping cream
1/4 c. shredded parmesan
1 t garlic salt
1/8 t pepper
cooked fetuccini noodles

Directions:
If using chicken, brown chicken & mushrooms in butter until cooked through (if using seafood, omit mushrooms, melt butter, add seafood, and saute at low heat).  Season with garlic salt & pepper.  Add whipping cream and cook until thick (stir constantly).  Once at desired consistency, add parmesan cheese.  Serve over noodles.

Submitter: 
Torrerizor
Description: 
This recipe is really unique as it doesn't use a thickening agent, and is very versatile!
