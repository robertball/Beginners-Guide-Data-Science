Title:
Baked Mini Stuffed Potato Tapas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/685320.jpg
Ingredients:
40 small new or red potatos
4 teaspoon olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
heavy sprinkle of season salt (Lawerys)
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon of chopped fresh rosemary
1 teaspoon of chopped fresh thyme
1 teaspoon of chopped fresh orageno
4 teaspoons butter
2 tablespoons of chopped chives
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 lb of crispy bacon crumbled, reserve 1/4 cup
1 tub of sour cream
8 oz of cream cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Scrub the potatoes, and pierce the skin one time with a fork. Rub the skin with olive oil, then with salt and pepper and sprinkle with fresh herbs.
Place the potato in the preheated oven, and bake for 35 minutes, or until slightly soft, add butter and toss. Let cool to room temp. Slice a deep V on the side of each potato. Mix together shredded cheddar cheese, bacon and sour cream and cream cheese and season salt stuff each potato with this mixture  sprinkle with chives and reserved bacon. Serve room temperature.

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
This baked potato has a lively herb salty skin, and is light and fluffy on the inside. Great for tapas and so easy!Modified from: Perfect Baked mini Potato tapas
