Title:
Laura's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c sugar
1/4 c shortening or butter
1 egg, beatean
3 bananas, mashed
3 Tbsp buttermilk (or 2 Tbsp milk with 1 Tbsp vinegar)
2 c flour
1/2 tsp each baking power and baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
dash fresh nutmeg, optional

Directions:
Blend sugar and butter.
Add beaten egg.
Add mashed bananas.
Mix together milk and vinegar and add.
Sift together dry ingredients and stir in.
Bake at 300 F 1 hour.
