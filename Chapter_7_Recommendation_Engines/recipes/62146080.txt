Title:
dip (Jomama's superbowl dip) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sour cream
1- 8oz package of cream cheese
12oz jar of Pace picante medium sauce
1 can tiny shrimp
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 diced tomato
1 can of quartered black olives
2 chopped green onions
2 boxes of original flavored Wheat Thins

Directions:
* Combine sour cream and cream cheese, mix with electric mixer until mixture is smooth. Spoon mixture into an 8x8 pan, spread across bottom of pan evenly.
*Cover layer of sour cream mixture with 12oz picante sauce.
*Sprinkle shrimp over layer of picante sauce.
*Cover with the 2c. shredded cheddar cheese
*Cover the cheddar cheese layer with the green onion, olives and tomato.
* Refrigerate for 2 hours. Serve and enjoy.
**This dip for some reason is A LOT better when served with the Wheat Thins cracker rather than tortilla chips or any other option.

Submitter: 
Candice Parker
Description: 
This dip has never got bad reviews. In fact I use it to make a good first impression at most party's. It takes the normal sour cream and salsa dip and kicks it into an awesome dip for all to over eat.
