Title:
Maple Almond Blueberry Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups old fashioned oats
1/2 cup wheat germ
1/2 cup oat bran
1/2 cup flax seed meal
1/2 cup slivered almonds
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp. canola oil
1/3 cup maple syrup
1/2 tsp. almond extract
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 cup water
3/4 cup dried blueberries

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together the oats, wheat germ, oat bran, flax seed meal, almonds, cinnamon and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together the oil, maple syrup, extracts and water. Add wet ingredients to the oat mixture, and stir until combined.
Spray large baking sheet with cooking spray. Spread mixture in pan.
Bake for up to 1 hour in the preheated oven, stirring every 20 minutes, or until toasted. Stir in dried blueberries. Let cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
