Title:
The Best Vanilla Pound Cake Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/685673.jpg
Ingredients:
* 2 cups of All Purpose Flour
* 2 cups of Granulated Sugar
* 2 tsp. Vanilla Extract
* 1 tbsp. of Whole Milk
* 5 Eggs
* 2 Sticks of Salted Butter (1/2lb)
* Pinch of Salt

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325F
Grease and flour a bundt cake pan making sure to get all the excess flour out or it will stick to the cake.
In a large bowl cream The butter and sugar. I soften the butter for about 20 sec. in the microwave before creaming with the sugar.
Add the Eggs one at a time mixing well after each one.
Gradually add the flour and salt. Mix for about 2 mins.
Add the Vanilla
Add in the Milk
Mix, but the mix does not need to be smooth because it toughens the cake
pour batter into pan
Bake for about 1 hour, a tooth pick should come out clean when stuck in.
