Title:
Aunt Dolores' Chocolate Pudding Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. flour
1/2 c. butter, softened
1/2 to 1 c. chopped nuts
8 oz. softened cream cheese
1 12 oz. container extra creamy Cool Whip, thawed
1 c. powdered sugar
2 small pkgs. instant chocolate pudding

Directions:
Mix together flour, butter, and chopped nuts together and press into a 9" x 13" pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes.  Cool.  Mix together cream cheese, 1/2 Cool Whip, and powdered sugar.  Spread on cooled crust.  Whip pudding made with 3 cups of milk instead of 4.  Spread over cream cheese layer.  Top with remaining Cool Whip.  Chill and serve by cutting into 3" x 3" squares.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe is handed down to me from a favorite aunt who made it during the holidays.  Many a guest has oo-ed and aw-ed about this simple but tasty dessert.
