Title:
Tomato Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6-ounce can Tomato paste
Water
1 Teaspoon Basil
1 Teaspoon Oregano
1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1 1/2 Teaspoons Minced Dried Onion
Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)

Directions:
Pour tomato paste into a small sauce pan.
Fill the can 1 1/2 times with water and add to the tomato paste along with the remaining ingredients.
Stir over medium heat until mixture comes to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for about 3 minutes.
Serve warm with accompaniments

Submitter: 
Linda
Description: 
An easy, tasty sauce I like to serve with chicken nuggets or fried zucchini.
