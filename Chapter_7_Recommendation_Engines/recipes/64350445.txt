Title:
Herbal Bread Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Water, warm______________2 ½ cups
25/75 oil blend__________½ cup
Bread flour______________6 ½ cups
Yeast, active dry1_______½ Tablespoon
Salt, kosher, coarse_____1 Tablespoon
Sugar, granulated________½ cup
Rosemary, fresh, minced__3/4 cup
Marjoram, dry____________½ teaspoon
Flour, all-purpose_______As needed
Vegetable oil____________As needed

Directions:
Gather all ingredients and equipment.
Add the water and oil to a mechanical mixing bowl; set aside.
Combine the bread flour, yeast, salt, and sugar together in a separate large bowl and mix together; then sift the contents into the water-oil mixture within the mechanical mixing bowl.
Add the rosemary, parsley, and marjoram.
Attach the mechanical mixing bowl to the stand mixer with a dough hook and mix the dough on medium speed for 6-8 minutes.
Dust a clean, level surface with all-purpose flour and transfer the finished dough onto it; knead(fold and press)the dough 6 times to develop the gluten.
Lightly dust the dough with flour and place it, level, into an oiled and floured 9x5" loaf pan; allow the dough’s to proof for 2 hours.
Preheat the oven to 375F and bake until a golden brown crust is achieved; about 30-45 minutes
Allow the bread to cool for an additional 10 minutes before attemting to remove it from the pan.
