Title:
Pineapple Dream Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup melted butter
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cup icing sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 egg yolks
1 can crushed pineapple, drained
1 cup whipping cream
1/2 tsp coconut extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix together first 3 ingredients. (Reserve 1/4 cup of crumbs for topping.) Press into greased 9 X 9 pan. Bake 10 - 15 mins until lightly browned. Let cool while preparing topping.
Cream the second amount of butter and icing sugar together. Add vanilla and egg yolks one at a time, mixing between additions.  Gently spread over crust. Chill about 10 minutes.
Evenly sprinkle crushed pineapple (or strawberries) over bottom two layers.
Whip the whipping cream, adding the coconut extract, until stiff peaks form. Evenly spread over pineapple (or strawberries).
Sprinkle reserved 1/4 cup of crumbs over top.
Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.

Description: 
Great for after a heavy meal. Also very colorful when made with strawberries (in syrup)instead of pineapple. (Prep time includes chill time)
