Title:
Mom's Potato Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups hot mashed potatoes
1 1/2 cups soft stale bread crumbs
1/4 cup finely chopped Salt Pork or Bacon
1 onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 1/2 teaspoon of salt
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 teaspoon Sage or to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°.
1. Add potatoes, bread crumbs, butter, egg, salt, sage and mix them together.
2. Once mixed, add pork and onions, mixing those thoroughly together.
3. Pour into a baking dish and bake in preheated oven 35 - 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Harms
Description: 
This is a great alternative to the usual bread- based stuffing or even an alternative to the usual mashed potatoes. It's been in my family a very long time and don't be stingy with the sage...the more you include, the better it tastes!!
