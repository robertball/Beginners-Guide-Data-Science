Title:
Caesar Dressing for a crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 c. Egg Beaters
1 1/2 oz. crushed garlic
3 1/2 oz parmesan cheese
2 2/3 T red wine vinegar
1 T lemon juice
1 T coarse ground pepper
1 T dry mustard
1 1/2 oz anchovies
1 T Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 c olive oil
2 T chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Blend 20 second on high, then 20 on low
Refrigerate overnight to let flavors marry.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
Combine with romaine lettuce, some shaved Parmesan cheese and your favorite croutons for a delicious caesar salad.
