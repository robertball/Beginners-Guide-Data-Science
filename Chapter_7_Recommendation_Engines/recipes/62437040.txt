Title:
The Elvis Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
butter
white bread
smooth peanut butter
banana
liquid honey
cooked bacon slices

Directions:
Spread the butter on 1 side of each slice of bread.  On the other side of half of the slices, spread peanut butter.  Place banana slices on top of peanut butter.  Drizzle honey over bananas.  Place bacon slices on top of bananas, then place the remaining buttered bread slices on top, butter side out.
Grill sandwiches (much like a grilled cheese) on both sides.  Slice and serve immediately.
