Title:
Seafood Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable oil
1 cup all purpose flour
3 large or 4 small onions, chopped
1 large or 2 small bell peppers, chopped
4 cloves garlic, finely minced
½ cup chopped fresh parsley
2 pounds okra
¼ cup vegetable oil
1½ quarts chicken stock
1½ quarts cold water
½ cup Worcestershire sauce
Tobasco to taste
2 large tomatoes, peeled & chopped
1 cup ketchup --or--
1 can tomato paste
2 can mild Rotel tomatoes, diced
8 slices bacon, chopped --or--
1 cup diced ham
2 lbs smoked sausage
3-4 bay leaves
½ teaspoons thyme
½ teaspoon crushed Rosemary
2 Tablespoons salt
2 cups cooked chicken, diced
1½ - 2 lbs cooked crabmeat
2 - 2½ lbs boiled shrimp
1 pint oysters (optional)
1 lb cooked crawfish
2 teaspoons brown sugar
1 - 2 teaspoons lemon juice
3 - 4 Tablespoons gumbo filé

Directions:
Heat 1 cup oil in heavy pot over medium heat.  Add flour slowly, stirring constantly until roux is medium brown (about 30 - 40 minutes).
Add onion , bell pepper, garlic and parsley; cook over medium heat 45 minutes to 1 hour, stirring constantly. (Mixture may be frozen at this stage).
Fry okra in ¼ cup oil until thick.  Add to gumbo and stir well over medium heat for a few minutes.
Add chicken stock, water, Worcestershire, Tobasco, ketchup or tomato paste, chopped tomato, Rotel, salt, bacon or ham, sausage, bay leaves, thyme and Rosemary.
Simmer 2½ - 3 hours.
About 30 minutes before serving, add chicken, crabmeat, shrimp, and crawfish.
Simmer about 30 minutes.  Add oysters, if desired, during last 10 minutes of simmering period.
Add brown sugar, lemon juice and filé.  Do not boil after addition of filé.
Serve over rice
