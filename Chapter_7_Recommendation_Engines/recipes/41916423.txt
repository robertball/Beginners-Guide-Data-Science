Title:
Yam Pecan Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2# Yams or Sweet Potatoes
4-6 Tabls Butter Melted
2 Eggs Beaten
1/2 Cup Milk
1 C Dark Brown Sugar
4 Tbls. Butter Softened
4 Tbls. Flour
1 Cup Coarsely Chopped Pecans
1/4 teas salt
1/4 teas cinnamon
1/4 teas nutmeg
1/2 teas vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Bake yams until tender when pierced, about 1 - 1 1/2 hrs.  (or microwave).  Remove from oven and let cool.  Increase oven to 375.  Scoop pulp from yams.  Place in large bowl.  Mash pulp until smooth... you should have 4 cups.  Whisk melted butter and eggs into yams.  In a small saucepan heat milk (I microwave).  Stir the hot milk and 1/2 cup sugar into the yams.  Blend thoroughly.  Spread the mixture into a buttered 11 x 7 inch baking dish.  Can be made ahead and refrigerated until ready to bake.  When ready to bake, mix the remaining 1/2 cup sugar, butter and flour until crumbly.  Stir in remaining incredients.  Sprinkle over yam casserole.  Bake until top is crusty, about 30 mins. or longer if it has been refrigerated.  Cool 5 minutes before serving.
