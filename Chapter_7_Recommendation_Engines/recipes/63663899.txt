Title:
couscous with mango, cucumber and cilantro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup water
1 cup couscous
2 Tbs olive olive
2 cups mango chopped
2 cups cucumber chopped
1 cup cilantro
2 Tbs honey
2 Tbs lemon juice
1/2 tsp coarse salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
combine broth and water in a large sauce pan; bring to a boil. Add couscous; cover and cook, stirring occasionally, until liquid is absorbed and coucous is al dente. Remove from heat and place in a mixing bowl. Stir in 1 Tbs olive oil to prevent grains from sticking. Let cool.
After cooled add mango, cucumber and cilantro: toss well
Whisk together honey, lemon juice and remaining tablespoon of olive oil, salt and pepper. Pour over couscous;mix well. serves 8
