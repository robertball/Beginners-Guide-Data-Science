Title:
Thanksgiving Any Day Rollups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/852843.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz reduced-fat cream cheese
4 oz shredded cheddar cheese
4 oz smoked Tofurkey or turkey, chopped
1 celery stick, chopped
2 green onions, chopped
1 tablespoon fresh chives
1/3 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup pecans
salt and pepper, to taste
3 large flour tortillas

Directions:
Combine cream cheese and cheddar cheese in bowl of food processor.  Process until blended.  Add the other ingredients to processor bowl, process until combined.
Spread mixture on tortillas, roll up.
Refrigerate until serving time, or serve immediately.

Submitter: 
FROGERINA
Description: 
With delicious holiday ingredients, you can have an easy, healthy and portable taste of Thanksgiving any day!
