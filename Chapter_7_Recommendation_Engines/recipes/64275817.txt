Title:
Chocolate Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1892368.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
2 cups flour
2 sticks butter or margarine
2 cups sugar
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup cocoa
1/4 cup milk
4 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Lightly spray 9" x 13" pan.
Combine 1/2 cup sugar and 2 cups flour in large bowl.  Cut butter or margarine in until mixture looks like small crumbs.  Reserve 1 cup mixture. spread rest into pan.  Bake for 10 minutes.
While baking, combine 2 cups sugar, 1/4 cup flour, cocoa, eggs, milk and vanilla.  Pour this mixture over the crust, sprinkle with reserved mixture.  Bake another 25 minutes.
Cool completely before cutting. Store covered, in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
Developed using the idea of lemon bars, this is a buttery rich crust topped with a creamy chocolate filling.
