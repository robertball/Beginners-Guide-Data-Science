Title:
Easy Chocolate Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cubed hawaiian sweet bread, crusts cut off and lightly toasted.
1 1/2 cups skim milk
4 tablespoons sugar
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
2 tablespoons Kahlua or other coffee-flavored liquor
1 tablespoon Baileys or other Irish cream
2 teaspoons cinamon
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 large eggs, lightly beaten
6 hershey kisses chopped or other chocolate candy

Directions:
Combine milk through eggs in a medium bowl and stir well with a whisk.  Add bread, tossing gently to coat. Cover and chill 30 minutes to 4 hours.
Pour mixture into 8x8 baking dish or divide evenly into 4 6ounce ramekins. If using ramekins, place in a cake pan filled halfway with boiling water before placing into the oven.
Preheat oven to 325 F.
Top mixture with chocolate candy pieces and bake for 35 minutes or until set.

Description: 
This yummy treat is easy to make and always hits the spot! Serve it warm with a dollop of whipped cream or vanilla ice cream and you've got the perfect chocolate dessert.
