Title:
Bacon Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7617096.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 onion, finely chopped
1 cup dry bread crumbs
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
7 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 egg
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon garlic salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 clove garlic, finely chopped

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix ground beef, onion, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, crumbled bacon, egg, onion powder, garlic salt, pepper, and garlic together in a large mixing bowl until thoroughly combined. Roll into 20 evenly-sized balls and place on a cookie sheet.
Cook meatballs in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 20 minutes.
