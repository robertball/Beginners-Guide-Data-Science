Title:
Tex Mex Barley Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup hulled barley
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups water
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (8.75 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 jalapeno pepper, diced
1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
1 small red onion, chopped
1 jar salsa verde
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon lime juice, or to taste
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Boil salted water in a medium saucepan.  Add barley; reduce heat and cover to simmer barely until cooked, about 40 minutes. Drain and cool.
Combine all ingredients in a bowl, adjusting salt, pepper, and lime juice to taste.  Chill for at least an hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Always Cooking Up Something
Description: 
Zesty Tex-Mex flavors compliment hearty barley, corn, and black beans for a great summer side dish.
