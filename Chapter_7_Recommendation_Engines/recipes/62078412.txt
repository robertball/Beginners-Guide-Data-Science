Title:
Roast Rabbit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp cooking oil
2 tbsp finely minced onion
1/4 cup finely minced celery
2 cups soft bread crumbs
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
rabbit giblets (optional)
1/4 tsp dry ginger
1/3 cup chopped water chestnuts
1/3 cup chicken broth or rabbit broth
1 whole rabbit
1 tbsp butter (softened)
1/2 tsp paprika
2 tbsp jam (flavor of your choice)
2 tbsp soy sauce

Directions:
Heat oil in a small skillet. Add onion, celery, and giblets, cook until soft.
Chop giblets when cooked.
In a large bowl, mix onion, celery, chopped giblets, bread crumbs, salt, pepper, ginger, water chestnuts and broth.
Spoon into rabbit cavity, fasten with skewers.
Blend butter and paprika and brush or rub onto surface of rabbit.
Roast covered at 350 degrees for 1 hr 10 min.
While roasting, prepare glaze by mixing jam with soy sauce.
After 1 hour, 10 min roasting time has passed, remove rabbit from oven and spoon all sauce over rabbit and roast uncovered for an additional 20 minutes.
Be sure to pour the cooked juices into a gravy boat or other serving container for garnishing when served. Enjoy!
