Title:
Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cold water
1 1/2 teaspoon cornstarch
2/4 cup honey
2 tablespoons thinly sliced green onions
1 tablespoon lemon juice
4 teaspoons prepared Dijon-style mustard
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon of hot sauce

Directions:
Place water in a medium saucepan, and mix in cornstarch to dissolve. Stir in honey. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Reduce heat and simmer until sauce thickens, about 15 minutes.
Remove sauce from heat. Stir in green onions, lemon juice, prepared Dijon-style mustard, garlic powder and hot sauce. Serve warm or chill in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Patricia Foreman
Description: 
A tasty honey mustard dipping sauce for chicken fingers, egg rolls, chow mein noodles, ham sandwiches -- there's no limits to this sauce.Modified from: Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce
