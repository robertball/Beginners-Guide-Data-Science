Title:
Jammin' Jerk Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976996.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon McCormick® Caribbean Jerk Seasoning
1 teaspoon McCormick® Thyme Leaves
1 teaspoon McCormick® Onion Powder
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Ground Allspice
2 1/2 teaspoons orange juice
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine Caribbean Jerk Seasoning, thyme, onion powder, allspice and orange juice to make a spice paste.
Rub spice paste on both sides of chicken.
Grill or broil over medium heat 5-7 minutes per side or until chicken is done.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Escape to the Islands with the combination of sweet and spicy flavors in this 20 minute recipe. This easy, 5 ingredient spice paste joins Caribbean Jerk Seasoning with thyme and allspice.
