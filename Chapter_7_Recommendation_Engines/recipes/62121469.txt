Title:
Grandma Burnett's Caramel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 quarts popped corn (1 C unpopped)
2 C brown sugar
1 C margarine or shortening
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 C corn syrup
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp baking soda

Directions:
Boil all ingredients (except corn and soda) for 5 minutes.
Remove from heat and add baking soda.
Pour over popped corn and mix well.
Put in large oven-safe bowl or baking dish and bake for 1 hour at at 250 degrees.
Stir every 15-20 minutes.
Turn out on plat pans and cool.
Store in tightly covered containers.

Submitter: 
Alyson Cerny Howlett
Description: 
This caramel corn is easy and tastes like cracker jacks.
