Title:
Taco Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB. Ground beef
1 LB. Combination Sweet and Hot Turkey Sausage
Â½ C Taco Seasoning
1 C Mild Picante Sauce or Salsa
2 C Mexican Blend Cheese divided
2 C Crushed Tortilla Chips Crushed and Divided
1 C Taco Sauce
Optional Sour Cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix together Meats, Taco seasoning, Picante sauce, and 1 cup crushed chips and 1-cup cheese.
Form into meat loaf pat meat to help stay together and place into baking dish.
Cover and bake for 30 minutes.
Uncover drain grease as well as possible. Then pour Taco sauce over top.
Then top with 1-cup cheese and 1 cup chips.
Bake another 30 minutes.
Serve Sour cream on side

Submitter: 
Judy
Description: 
This one is a little different. When you want to try something new give it a try.  You can change the ingredients to what ever you like on tacos. A little sour cream served on the side adds to the flavor.
