Title:
Chicken Fajitas made in oven Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 2-inch strips
1 tablespoon chopped fresh garlic
1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
2 teaspoons olive oil, divided
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
Vegetable oil cooking spray
1/2 medium onion, thinly sliced
1/2 medium red bell pepper, thinly sliced
1/2 medium green bell pepper, thinly sliced
1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes with green chiles, drained
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
8 flour tortillas (8 inches each)
cooked rice optional

Directions:
Heat oven to 400Â°. In a bowl, toss chicken with garlic, juice, 1 tsp oil, chili powder and cumin; marinate 20 minutes. Coat a rimmed sheet pan with cooking spray; add chicken with marinade, onion, bell peppers, tomatoes with chiles, salt, black pepper and remaining 1 tsp oil; toss to combine. Roast, stirring once, until chicken is cooked through, 20 minutes. Wrap tortillas in moist paper towel and microwave for 15 seconds.  Serve with salsa, grated cheddar cheese and sour cream.
