Title:
Chocolate Pretzel Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag round pretzels
5 bags rolos or caramel kisses
1 bag mini m&ms;

Directions:
Line cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Line pretzel side by side on entire sheet.
Place hershey kiss inside each pretzel.
Bake for 4 min. Immediately press m&m onto each kiss.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
Carmael hershey kisses wrapped around a pretzel for the perfect texture.
