Title:
Rockin's Ridiculous Cornbread Sausage Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound roll Bob Evans sausage (browned, crumbled, sauteed with the onions and celery and drained)
1 onion peeled and diced(sauteed with the sausage)
3 ribs(sticks) of celery diced(sauteed with the sausage)
16 oz seasoned corn bread stuffing mix (pepperidge farm)
2 apples( peeled, cored and diced)
1 8oz can diced water chestnuts drained
1 stick butter(melted in a microwave bowl)
4 cups of chicken broth
1/2 cup dried cranberries(optional but delicious!)
9x13 pan sprayed with pam

Directions:
Prepare the sausage, celery and onions in a medium skillet.
In a large bowl place all the ingredients together including the sausage mixture.
With a large spoon start gently mixing, you'll want everything to be well blended.
Pour the mixture in the 9x13 pan sprayed with pam.
Bake in 350? oven for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This is a delicious version of the traditional sausage stuffing with a couple exciting ingredients thrown in to give it an extra special kick!
