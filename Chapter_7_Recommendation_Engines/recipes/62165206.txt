Title:
Crispy Fried Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289931.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 lbs chicken wings, halved and washed
2 cups buttermilk
1 egg
3 cups all purpose flour
1 cup crushed saltine crackers
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp thyme
1 tbs salt
pinch of garlic powder
4 cups vegetable oil

Directions:
Mix one egg into buttermilk. Place halved chicken wings in a bowl and coat with the buttermilk/egg mixture and allow to sit for at least 30 minutes, refrigerated.
In a separate bowl add all remaining ingredients
In a deep skillet heat the vegetable oil on medium high, and be sure to reduce to medium before cooking.
Dredge the chicken in the flour/cracker mixture and fry in oil for ten minutes, turning often. Remove from the oil allowing residue to drip off before putting on a plate lined with paper towels. Sprinkle additional salt on chicken immedeiately and serve.

Submitter: 
Patrick
Description: 
This traditional southern-style fried chicken is fall-off-the-bone delicious and is simple to make. Throw them in a resealable bag with your favorite Buffalo sauce and shake 'em up for an added delight!!
