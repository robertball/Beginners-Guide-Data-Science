Title:
Miriam's Banana Split Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/756859.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c graham cracker crumbs
1 1/2 stick butter
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese, softened
2 c powdered sugar
3/4 stick butter
2 eggs
5 bananas, sliced
1 large can crushed pineapple, drained
13 oz cool whip
Chocolate syrup, marachino cherries, strawberries, nuts as desired.

Directions:
Melt 1 1/2 stick butter, mix with graham cracker crumbs.  Pat into a 9 X 13 pan.
Beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, 3/4 stick butter and 2 eggs together until creamy.  Pour over crust.
Layer with bananas, crushed pineapple, and cool whip.  Drizzle with chocolate syrup and other toppings.  Cool for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Karyn Watson Woodward
Description: 
no bake recipe, tastes just like you are eating a banana split.  Recipe does contain raw eggs.
