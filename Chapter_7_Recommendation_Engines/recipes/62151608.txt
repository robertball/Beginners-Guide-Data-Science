Title:
Dinner in a Dash Pizza Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Grands butter tastin buscuits
1 1/2 cups of your favorite pizza or spaghetti sauce, divided
2 cups shredded mozzerella cheese, divided
1 1/2 pounds ground beef, cooked with salt, drained, and divided in half.
1/2 cup finely chopped sweet yellow onion

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Cut each biscuit into 8 pieces.
Toss with 1 cup of the sauce and 1 cup of the cheese, and half of the beef.
Spread dough mixture in a sprayed 8-inch square or 2 1/2 quart glass baking dish.
Top with remaining ground beef, onion and then the remaining 1 cup cheese.
Bake for 30 to 40 minutes or until center buscuits are cooked through. (I check with a small fork)
To serve, warm and spread a small amount of the remaining sauce on each serving plate and top with a serving of the pizza.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
Grands buscuit pieces, ground beef and onion are tossed with cheese and pizza sauce making this the easiest and tasiest pizza casserole ever baked. You can add mushrooms, peppers and olives if you like those too!
