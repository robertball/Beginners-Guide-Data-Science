Title:
Stuffed Fig Amuse-Bouche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/466618.jpg
Ingredients:
8 fresh Turkey figs
fresh goat cheese, approx 2 t. per fig
1 pkg Trader Joe's Rosemary Pecans and Cranberries
honey
fresh rosemary for garnish

Directions:
Slice each fig in half.  Place approximately 1 teaspoon of goat cheese in center of fig.  Insert small sprig (2-3 needles) of fresh rosemary, piece of pecan and cranberry.  Drizzle with 3-6 drops of honey.  Serve as appetizer, between courses or as a light dessert. If desired, cheese may be slightly toasted with a hand-held torch prior to garnish.

Submitter: 
Helen Ann Hebert
Description: 
Fresh Turkey figs stuffed with chevre cheese, rosemary pecans and drizzled with honey.
