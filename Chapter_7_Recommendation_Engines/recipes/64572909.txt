Title:
Mostly Vegetable Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3562359.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef, browned and drained
1 small head of cabbage
2 large Vidalia onions, diced
1 lb. white mushrooms, washed and sliced
2 cans dark red kidney beans
2 cans black beans
5 large carrots, sliced
2 large cans crushed tomatoes
1/4 cup Franks Buffalo Red Hot Sauce
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 cup chili powder
2 tbsp. olive oil
2 tbsp. minced garlic
1 cup water

Directions:
Slice or chop all vegetables, set aside.
Brown the ground beef and drain as much fat as you can.  Set aside.
In a large stock pot, get the olive oil nice and hot over med-high heat.
Add onions and garlic.  Cook, stirring regularly until onions are nice and caramelized.
Add ground beef and all sliced vegetables to the mixture.  Pour in crushed tomatoes and 1 cup water.
Let simmer until a pool of liquid forms at the top of the pan, then add all the spices to the soup and stir.
Let simmer over low heat for an hour to allow all of the flavors to mingle.
Serve with shredded cheese and sour cream (optional)

My take on chili and vegetable soup combined.
