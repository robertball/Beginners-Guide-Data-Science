Title:
Prosciutto wrapped melon balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ripe Cantelope
1 package Prosciutto

Directions:
Cut cantelope in half and remove seeds. Using a melon baller, scoop out interior and set aside. Slice Prosciutto lenghtwise into quarter inch slices, then again into two inches pieces, depending on the size of your scooper. Wrap Prosciutto around the melon and secure with a a pretty ruffled toothpick. Serve on a tray lined with a bed of lettuce.

Submitter: 
LINDA MCLEAN
Description: 
The perfect combo; a little sweet and a little salty appetizer that's sure to please!
