Title:
Copper Penny Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1122465.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds carrots, sliced
1 stalk celery, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup distilled white vinegar
2/3 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated

Directions:
Bring a pot of water to boil, add carrots and boil until the carrots are just tender, approximately 10 minutes. Drain well.
In a mixing bowl combine the celery, green pepper, carrots and onion.
Bring tomato soup, oil, vinegar and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener to a boil. Stir well. Remove the mixture from the heat and let cool, 10 to 15 minutes. Pour the mixture over the vegetables, mix until all of the vegetables are coated. Marinate this salad overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
A colorful salad with a fanciful name--lightly cooked carrot coins and crunchy bits of green pepper, onion and celery, tossed together and chilled overnight in a tangy marinade.
