Title:
Simple Strawberry Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 cup white sugar
2 cups quartered strawberries
(No need to strain--just mash)
May add 1 tsp. lemon juice
DELICIOUS!

Directions:
Combine water and sugar in a saucepan over medium-high heat; stir until sugar is dissolved. Mix strawberries into saucepan and bring to a boil; boil for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until strawberries are mushy and sauce is thick, about 10 minutes. Strain liquid into a bottle and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Shirley
Description: 
A simple syrup made with strawberries and sugar is perfect for strawberry milk or a topping for pancakes.Modified from: Simple Strawberry Syrup
