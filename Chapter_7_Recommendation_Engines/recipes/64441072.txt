Title:
Vegetarian Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
1 package taco seasoning
1 red onion, sliced
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 can kidney beans, drained
1 can black beans, drained
2 large tomatoes, chopped
8oz shredded cheese
1/2 bag of tortilla chips
1/3 bottle Catalina salad dressing

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients (except for the tortilla chips and salad dressing) and mix well.
Before serving, crush the tortilla chips and add them to the mixture along with the Catalina. Adjust the amount of dressing to taste, and to ensure the mixture isn't too dry. Mix well and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Adam Venn
Description: 
This is the perfect vegetarian variation of a traditional taco salad. Even meat eaters love this! Its a perfect summertime meal, great for parties and/or potlucks.
