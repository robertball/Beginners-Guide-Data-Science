Title:
Hula's Eggnog Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (4oz) non-instant Vanilla pudding mix
1/4 tsp ground Nutmeg
1 1/2 cups Eggnog
1 tsp rum extract
2 cups Heavy Cream
9" Pie shell - Blind baked
Pinch of Nutmeg

Directions:
In med saucepan combine pudding mix with nutmeg and eggnog and mix well.
Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thick and bubbling.
Remove from heat and stir in extract.
Cover and refrigerate until well chilled.
In separate bowl, whip the cream to soft peaks.
Beat pudding mix till smooth and fold in whipped cream.
Spoon onto pie shell and sprinkle with nutmeg to finish.
Refrigerate 4 hours to set.

Submitter: 
Virginia Keeley
Description: 
Can substitute 2 tsp Rum for 1tsp rum extract
