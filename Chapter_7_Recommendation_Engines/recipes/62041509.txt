Title:
Girl Scout Cookie Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/3 ounce Frangellica
1/3 ounce Creme De Menthe
1/3 ounce Bailey's

Directions:
Add above listed ingredients together and shake well.  Serve in a chilled shot glass.
