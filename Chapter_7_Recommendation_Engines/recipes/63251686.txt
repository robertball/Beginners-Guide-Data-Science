Title:
Candy Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb nuts
1 c sugar
1 T water
1 egg white
1 t salt
1 t cinnamon

Directions:
Beat egg white and water until fluffy. Add nuts and stir. Add remaining ingredients. Mix well. Put in buttered 9x13. Bake 300 30-40 min. Stir every 15 min.
