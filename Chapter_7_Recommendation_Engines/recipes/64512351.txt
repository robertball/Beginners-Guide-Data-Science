Title:
Buttermilk Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 eggs
2 cups buttermilk
1/3 cup oil (canola)

Directions:
Mix flour, baking powder, soda & salt.
In another bowl, beat eggs, add buttermilk and oil.
Stir wet ingredients into dry, just until combined.
Bake in preheated waffle iron till golden brown.
Yield: 16 waffles

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
I like buttermilk in pancakes & waffles, so this should be good. Pretty sure I've tried it at least once..!
