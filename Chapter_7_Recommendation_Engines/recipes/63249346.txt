Title:
White Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 to 1-1/2 lbs lasagne noodles, cooked & drained
1-1/2 to 2 lbs Italian Turkey Sausage, browned & drained
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
Salt & Pepper to taste
1/2 c Shredded Parmesan Cheese
1/2 lb Shredded Jack Cheese
1/2 lb Shredded Fontenelle or Mozzarella Cheese
1-1/2 c Cream or Half & Half
1-1/2 c Milk
6 tbls Butter
6 tbls flour
24 oz Canned Asparagus, drained

Directions:
Prepare Noodles
Mix together browned sausage, mustard and Italian seasoning.
Mix together cheeses.  Reserve 2 cups for topping.
For cream sauce: warm cream and milk in saucepan.  In a separate saucepan melt butter, then add flour an stir until it sizzles.  Add salt & pepper.  Stir in milk mixture slowly and cook until slightly thickened.  Add all but reserved cheese to milk mixture. Stir.
Butter 11x15 baking dish.  Layer noodles, meat, asparagus, sauce, noodles, meat, asparagus, sauce, noodles.  Top with reserved cheese.  Bake at 375Â° for about 30 minutes.
