Title:
Milly's Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
5-8 garlic cloves, minced or pureed
1 med onion, cut up
4-5 roma tomatoes, cut up
8-10 fresh basil leaves minced fine
splash of cooking wine
salt to taste
1 can, 28 oz crushed tomatoes w/garlic
2 cans of chicken stock, 14.5 oz
1 can media crema

Directions:
Place olive oil in heavy pot and turn on stove to med-high heat.
Add the garlic and sautee.
Add the onion and cook until soft.
Add tomatoes, basil leaves, cooking wine and salt and cook, stirring frequently for 3-5 min.
Turn off.
Process mix in blender with the chicken stock and the can of crushed tomatoes, adding the media crema also.
Return to pot, check salt level.
Simmer for a couple of minutes.
Serve over your favorite cheese, and sprinkle fresh basil leaves on top.

Submitter: 
Milly Suazo-Martinez
Description: 
The recipe is how I made it for the first time on 3/21/2007. The type of tomatoes could vary.
