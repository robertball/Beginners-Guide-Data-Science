Title:
Super Simple Skillet Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 can condensed tomato soup
1/2 c Salsa
1/2 c Water
6 tortillas, cut into 3/4" x 1 1/2" strips
1/2 c shredded Cheese

Directions:
Heat cast iron skillet to medium-high heat.
Crumble and brown ground beef, drain fat.
Add soup, salsa, water and tortilla strips.
Stir together and simmer 5 min, stirring occasionally.
Top with shredded cheese.
Serve.
