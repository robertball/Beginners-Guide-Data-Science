Title:
Spaghetti Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz spaghetti
1 Tbl butter
1/3 cup parmesan
2 eggs, beaten
3/4 cup chopped onion/green peppers
1 clove garlic, minced
1 can diced tomatoes (Italian style)
1 can tomato paste (6oz)
1 pkg splenda
1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
1 cup ricotta cheese
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella

Directions:
cook & drain spaghetti. Stirr in margarine, parmesan & eggs while spaghetti is hot. Form this mixture as crust in 10" pie pan
Preheat oven to 350
In skillet cook onion/green peppers & garlic. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, splenda & italian seasoning. Heat through
Spread ricotta cheese over spaghetti crust, then pour tomato mixture over cheese.
Bake for 20min. Sprinkle Mozzarella over top & bake 5 min longer, until cheese melts.
