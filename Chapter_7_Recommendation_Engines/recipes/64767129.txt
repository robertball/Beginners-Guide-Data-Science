Title:
Matrimonial Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crumb layers:
1 1/4 cups flour
1 1/2 cups rolled oats
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup butter or margarine
Filling:
1/2 lb (~ 1 cup) dates, cut up
1/2 cup sugar
2/3 cup water

Directions:
Crumb layers: mix all ingred. in large bowl - cutting in butter until crumbly.
Press almost 2/3 mixture into a greased 9" x 9" pan.
Filling: place all ingred. into a saucepan. Bring to boil & simmer until mushy.
If it becomes too dry before dates are soft, add more water. If you have too much water, just keep simmering until some has boiled away.
Spread over bottom layer of crumbs.
Sprinkle remaining crumb mixture over top. Press down with your hand.
Bake @ 350 x 30 min. until rich golden brown color.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Traditional date squares your gma likely made at least twice/month!
