Title:
Beer Batter Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups flat beer
1 1/3 cups flour
4 T. freshly grated parmesan cheese
1 T. chopped fresh parsley
1 t. salt
Very generous sprinkling of garlic powder
Dash cayenne pepper, to taste, optional
1 T. oil
2 beaten egg yolks
2 beaten egg whites
1 head cauliflower, split into small florets
Light vegetable oil, heated in deep fryer or deep heavy pot to 375 degrees

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
Combine flour, parmesan, parsley, salt, garlic powder and cayenne (optional) in large bowl.
Combine oil, egg yolks and flat beer in small bowl.
Pour oil/egg/beer mixture into dry ingredient bowl.  Stir until combined.
Fold in beaten egg whites.
Toss in cauliflowerettes.  Stir lightly until coated with batter.
Deep fry in small batches until golden brown.  Drain on oven-proof plate lined with paper toweling.  Keep fried batches warm in oven until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
BAJATHECAT
Description: 
There are a lot of beer batter recipes out there but this recipe from my mother-in-law rises above.  She always uses cauliflower as the main ingredient but any favorite vegetable will be a success.  Ever had beer batter jalapenos?  (Rough up the outside so the batter sticks!)
