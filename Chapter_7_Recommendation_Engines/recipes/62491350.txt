Title:
Bulgogi II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/492627.jpg
Ingredients:
•3 Lbs Beef (Sirloin or any cut)
•1 Onion
•½ Carrot
•6 Tbsp Soy Sauce
•3 Tbsp Sugar
•3 Tbsp Asian Pear Juice
•3 Tbsp Cooking Wine
•3 Tbs Green Onion
•1½ Tbsp Garlic
•1 Pinch Ginger Powder
•1 Tbsp Sesame Seeds
•¼ tsp Black Pepper
•1 Tbsp Sesame Oil

Directions:
-Cut 3 Lbs of beef thinly.
-Cut ½ of a carrot, mince 1½ Tbsp garlic, and chop green onions
-In a bowl, combine 6 Tbsp of soy sauce, 3 Tbsp of sugar, 3 Tbsp of Asian pear juice, 3 Tbsp of cooking wine, 3 Tbsp of green onion, 1½ Tbsp of garlic, 1 Pinch of ginger powder, 1 Tbsp of sesame seeds, ¼ tsp of black pepper, and 1 Tbsp of sesame oil. Mix well

Submitter: 
JFKR
Description: 
Bulgogi is one of the most famous Korean foods. Many foreigners visit Korea and try this delicious dish and love it. It not only has good flavor, but the way of eating this food is unique. We usually grill bulgogi on the table while we are eating a meal in a restaurant. Wrap bulgogi with lettuce and add soybean paste, garlic, onion, carrot, or cucumber depending on your taste
