Title:
Layered Dulce de Leche Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
46 Nilla Wafers
1-1/4 cup(s) cold milk
2 package(s) Jell-O (4 serving size) vanilla flavored pudding and pie filling
1 tub(s) 8oz cool whip topping thawed
1/4 cup(s) carmel ice cream topping

Directions:
Chop 4 of the wafers; set aside. Line 9 x 5 inch loaf pan with foil, with ends of foil extending over sides of the pan. Arrange 14 of the remaining wafers in a single layer on bottom of prepared pan; set aside.
Pour milk into a large bowl. Add dry pudding mixes. Beat with a wire whisk 2 min. or until well blended. (Mixture will be thick) Stir in whipped topping. Spoon 1/3 of the pudding mixture over wafers in pan; top with 14 of the remaining wafers and 2 TBS. of the caramel topping. Repeat layers of pudding mixture, wafers and caramel topping. Cover with the remaining pudding mixture. Sprinkle with chopped wafers; press gently into pudding mixture. Cover.
Freeze 4 hours or until firm. Lift dessert from pan, using foil handles; remove foil. Place dessert on serving plate. Let stand 15min. to soften slightly before slicing to serve. Store leftovers in the freezer.
