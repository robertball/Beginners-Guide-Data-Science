Title:
Granny's Pineapple Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
2 T. flour
1 small can crushed pineapple
1/2 stick butter
Confectioner's sugar

Directions:
Soften butter. Mix sugar, flour and pineapple with softened butter over heat. Add confectioner's sugar to what is left to ice outside.
