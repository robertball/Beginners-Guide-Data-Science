Title:
Slow Cooker Adobo Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 cup water
2 bay leaves.
8 cloves garlic, crushed
1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces

Directions:
Place chicken in a slow cooker. In a small bowl mix garlic, soy sauce, and vinegar, and pour over the chicken. Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
An easy slow cooker recipe with soy sauce, garlic, and onion. Do not be put off by the vinegar -- it tenderizes the chicken and loses its potent flavor in the cooking.Modified from: Slow Cooker Adobo Chicken
