Title:
Garlic Infused Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/564211.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil
4 cloves of garlic, crushed
6-7 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
3 tablespoon of butter
4 zucchini, sliced
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 teaspoon fresh parsley, minced (optional)

Directions:
In a large pan, med/high heat, add oil and throw in the crushed garlic to infuse. When browned, throw it away.
Then add the sliced garlic and butter for a quick few stirs then add the zucchini.
Stir and cook until desired bite.
Finish off with parsley.

Submitter: 
moaa
Description: 
For garlic lovers! So simple and delicious side dish. You can mix yellow and green zucchini's together for a brighter presentation. Also, you may use this method for shrimps. Enjoy!
