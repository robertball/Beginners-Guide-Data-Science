Title:
Horseradish Beets (Chrin) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 (15 ounce) cans canned beets
2 (4 ounce) jars prepared horseradish

Directions:
In an electric blender, grind the beets; drain (you can save the juice for soup if you'd like).
Add horseradish to beets and mix well; refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Janis Neill
Description: 
This wonderful beet and horseradish dish makes for an excellent side to any meal. A nice change from borscht!Modified from: Horseradish Beets (Chrin)
