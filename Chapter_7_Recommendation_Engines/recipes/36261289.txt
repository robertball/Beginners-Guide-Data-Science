Title:
Stuffed Dried Figs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 walnut or pecan, half
1/2 dried fig
1 tsp blue cheese or gorgonzola

Directions:
Place the nut half in the center of the dried fig and place on a cookie sheet.
Top the fig and nut with 1 tsp of blue cheese.
Place in the broiler to melt the cheese just a bit. Serve warm, on a platter.
