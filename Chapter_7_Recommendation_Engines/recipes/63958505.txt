Title:
Avacado Dip from Mom Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
31 or 32 oz Refried beans
2 large avocados (make sure they're ripe (soft and dark))
1 T lemon juice
1 t salt
1 T water
1 c mayo
8 oz cream cheese
1 pkg taco seasoning mix
1 c shredded cheddar cheese
tomato
green scallon slices
sliced black olives
tortilla or corn chips

Directions:
1st Layer refried beans
2nd Layer blended avocados, lemon juice, salt and water
3rd Layer mixed mayo, cream cheese, taco seasoning mix
4th Layer shredded cheddar cheese (cover the other layers)
Garnish with dice tomatoes, sliced scallion greens and sliced black olives.
Serve with tortilla or corn chips.

Submitter: 
scollins
Description: 
Got this recipe from my mom. I use it for large gatherings like reunions or football parties.
