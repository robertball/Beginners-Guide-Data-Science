Title:
Papa's American Chop Suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/784183.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound italian sausage
16 oz elbow macaroni
1/2 chopped onion
2 chopped stalks celery
1 chopped green pepper
1 chopped red pepper
2 cloves garlic
2 cans stewed tomatoes

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a separate large skillet over medium heat, saute the sausage, onion, celery and peppers until meat is cooked. Pour the stewed tomato into the skillet and stir well to combine.
When noodles are done, drain thoroughly and return noodles to the pot. Add the sausage mixture from the skillet to the pot.

Submitter: 
Bluestrose
Description: 
American Chop Suey should be a bright, tasty and simple meal. This recipe does not make hamburger helper or spaghetti on macaroni noodles, it is a meal all its own!
