Title:
Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup bran
3/4 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp sea salt
1 egg
3/4 cup raisins/apples/dates
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1/4 cup molasses
3 tbsp. honey
1/3 cup melted butter
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
Paper or re-usable muffin cups

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 425 degrees. Combine bran, flour, salt, baking soda, and raisins in one bowl.  Combine yogurt, egg, honey, and molasses in another bowl.  Stir together, and then stir in walnuts. Bake for 15 minutes in muffin cups.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Hennes
Description: 
These are the recipe my mom has used for years, and are a healthy & tasty breakfast!
