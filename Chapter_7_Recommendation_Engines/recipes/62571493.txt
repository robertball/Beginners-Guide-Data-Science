Title:
Peanut Butter Chews Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup peanut butter
3 cups corn flakes

Directions:
Butter a 8x8x2" pan (glass works best).
Heat corn syrup and sugar to a full rolling boil and boil 1 minutes.
Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter until smooth.
Quickly stir in cereal and pat into pan with dampened spatula.
Cool 1 hour and cut into squares.
