Title:
French Onion Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981260.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
2 cups chopped onion
1 pound ground round
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 large egg white, lightly beaten
1 teaspoon salt
4 (1.5 ounce) French bread rolls or hamburger buns, split and toasted
4 teaspoons fat-free French or blue cheese salad dressing

Directions:
Heat oil in a 12-inch cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion; saute 10 minutes or until tender. Spoon onion into a bowl; let cool.
Add beef, pepper, and egg white to onion, and stir well. Divide beef mixture into 4 equal portions, shaping into 3/4-inch-thick patties.
Sprinkle salt evenly over surface of skillet. Heat salt in skillet over high heat 2 minutes. Add patties; cook 5 minutes on each side or until done. Place patties on bottom halves of rolls; top each with 1 teaspoon French dressing and top halves of buns.

Submitter: 
Cooking Light magazine
Description: 
These juicy burgers get their sweetness from caramelized onions.
