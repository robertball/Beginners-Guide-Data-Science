Title:
Strawberry-Pineapple Smoothie II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/768586.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup apple juice
2 tablespoons pineapple juice
1/2 cup low-fat strawberry yogurt
1/4 cup pineapple chunks, drained
3/4 cup frozen strawberries

Directions:
Pour the apple juice and pineapple into a blender; add the yogurt, pineapple chunks, and frozen strawberries and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
ANDITOO72
Description: 
Delicious and refreshing smoothie full of flavor! Very versatile, you could always substitute banana for pineapple chunks and use more apple juice. Easily doubled!
