Title:
Hot Cocoa Gift Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981946.jpg
Ingredients:
6 1/2 cups non-fat milk powder
1 (5 ounce) package non-instant chocolate pudding mix
1 cup powdered chocolate drink mix
1/2 cup powdered non-dairy creamer
1/2 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine powdered milk, chocolate pudding mix, chocolate drink mix powder, creamer, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, and cocoa. Divide the mixture between two 1-quart jars. Seal, and decorate as desired. These can be stored in a dry area for up to 3 months.
Attach a tag with the following instructions:  Hot Cocoa: Dissolve 1/3 cup cocoa mix in 1 cup boiling water.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Give the gift of chocolate - hot chocolate. This mix makes 24 cups of creamy hot chocolate.
