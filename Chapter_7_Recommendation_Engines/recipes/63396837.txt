Title:
Grilled Sweet Italian Chicken Sausage, Onion, and Pepper Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz pkg al fresco Sweet Italian Chicken Sausage
4 cups Sweet onion (Vidalia), thinly sliced
3 tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
1 jar Roasted Red Peppers, drained and thinly sliced (7 ounces)
1/4 tsp Black pepper
1 Fresh Bread Baguette, halved lengthwise (8 ounce)

Directions:
Heat a large grill pan over medium-high heat.
Coat pan with cooking spray.
Add onion and al fresco chicken sausage.
Cook 1 minute.
Sprinkle with vinegar; cook 14 minutes or until sausage is done, turning occasionally
Add bell peppers, cook 1 minute.
Sprinkle with black pepper.
Arrange sausage mixture evenly over bottom half of bread.
Cut into 5 sandwiches.
