Title:
Griddle Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ¼ cup Evaporated milk,
1 cup corn meal,
1 cup flour,
4 teaspoons baking powder,
¾ teaspoon Kosher Salt,
2 eggs,
¼ cup butter

Directions:
Mix the evaporated Milk and cornmeal and set aside.
Mix the rest of the ingredients together. then add the cornmeal and milk and mix together.
Heat up the griddle, pour desired amount and fry about 3 minutes on each side depending on the size you make them. Cook until not gooey on the inside.

Submitter: 
Shellie Hunt Bragdon
Description: 
I made this by accident was wanting to make cornbread, didn't have milk or unsalted butter so I used regular butter and used evaporated milk. My family loved it.
