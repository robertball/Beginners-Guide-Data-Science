Title:
Chicken Mango Salsa Salad with Chipotle Lime Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/674712.jpg
Ingredients:
Salad:
1-1/2 cups mango salsa, drained (see recipe below)
1/2 cup diced red, green, and yellow bell peppers, combined
1 cup dry broccoli slaw mix (from packaged slaw mix)
2 TBL blue cheese, crumbled
2 TBL dried cranberries
2 TBL pecans, chopped
1 cup cooked chicken, diced or shredded
1 package baby spinach leaves
1/2 cup Chipotle Lime Vinaigrette (see recipe below)
Mango Salsa Recipe:
1 medium mango, diced
2 medium roma tomatoes, chopped
1/2 medium onion, chopped
1 medium jalapeno, seeded and chopped (add more or less to adjust the heat to your taste)
1/4 cup cilantro leaves, chopped
Juice of 1 lime juice
Chipotle Lime Vinaigrette Recipe:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup lime juice
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp chipotle chili powder (add more or less to adjust the heat to your taste)
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
In a medium bowl make the mango salsa recipe by mixing all of its ingredients into a bowl and stir.  Set aside.  Refrigerate if making this in advance.
To make the vinaigrette, mix all ingredients into a small bowl and mix well.  Refrigerate until ready to assemble the salad.
In a large bowl add spinach leaves, broccoli slaw, diced red, green, and yellow bell peppers, diced chicken, cranberries and pecans into a bowl and toss well.  Add mango salsa, sprinkle with blue cheese and drizzle with chipotle lime vinaigrette.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
A delicious colorful, sweet and spicy salad with diced chicken, a mango pico de gallo salsa, blue cheese, and cranberries in a wonderful Chipotle Lime Vinaigrette. It's almost too pretty to eat.....almost.
