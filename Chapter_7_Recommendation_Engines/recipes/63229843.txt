Title:
Mama Legs Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/840204.jpg
Ingredients:
12 hard boiled eggs sliced lengthwise and yolks removed
1/4 cup prepared mustard
1/4 c mayonnaise
1/4 cup minced onion
1/4 cup dill pickle relish
2 drops red pepper sauce
4 pc bacon
1 green onion
paprika

Directions:
1. fry bacon, drain and crumble
2. Mash egg yolks and mix with mustard, mayo, onion, pickle and pepper sauce.
3. Stuff eggs
4. Top with bacon, paprika and onion slice

Submitter: 
Michelle English
Description: 
These are a yummy staple for any celebration. We could never have a get together without them.
