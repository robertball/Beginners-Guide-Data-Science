Title:
Paprika Marinated and Breaded Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
1/4 c Italian Dressing
1 TBS Poultry Seasoning or Emeril's Original Ess.
3-4 TBS of PapriKa
1 c Italian Seasoned Bread Crumbs
1/2 TBS Black Pepper
1/2 TBS Garlic Salt
1 TBS Sesame Seeds
1 TBS of Flour
2 TBS Butter
2 TBS Pure Olive Oil

Directions:
Marinate Chicken overnight  with the italian dressing, poultry seasoning, and paprika.  Work in well and massage.
Preheat the oven to 375F and place large baking dish with the butter and olive oil into the oven.
Mix bread crumbs, pepper, garlic salt, sesame seeds,and flour in a large storage bag.  Coat the breasts in the bread crumb mixture and place into the preheated baking dish.  Bake 30-40 min or until the internal temperature is 165-170F.
