Title:
Easy Pizza Hut Breadsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1070220.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) can  pizza dough (Pillsbury)
1  tablespoon parmesan cheese
1  teaspoon garlic powder
1  teaspoon  garlic salt
1  teaspoon onion powder
1  teaspoon oregano
1/4 cup olive oil (approximately)
marinara sauce, for dipping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450ยบ.
Unroll dough onto cutting board. Cut into 16 even strips.
Place each strip on a lightly greased cookie sheet.
Bake 6-8 minutes until golden brown.
While dough is baking, combine all DRY ingredients.
Remove baked breadsticks from oven and brush with olive oil and quickly sprinkle evenly with dry topping (may have extra topping left over).
Serve with warm marinara sauce.
