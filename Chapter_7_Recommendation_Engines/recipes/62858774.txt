Title:
Lemon Herb Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken
2 cloves garlic
1 lemon
½ onion
½ bunch fresh parsley
3 sprigs fresh rosemary
1 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
1 tsp salt
½ tsp pepper
2 Tbsp all-purpose flour
1 cup chicken stock

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F.
Remove giblets and neck from chicken.
Using side of chef’s knife, smash garlic. Rub inside of chicken cavity with garlic; put garlic in cavity.
Halve lemon, squeeze out juice and set aside. Put lemon halves, onion and parsley into cavity. Tie legs together with kitchen string; tuck wings under back. Place, breast side up, on rack in roasting pan. Tuck rosemary under chicken.
In small bowl, stir together reserved lemon juice, oil, salt and pepper; pour over chicken. Pour ¾ cup water into pan. Roast in oven, pasting occasionally and adding ¾ cup water halfway through, until meat thermometer inserted in thigh registers 185F, 1 1/3 – 1 ½ hours in convection oven, 1 ¾ - 2 hours in conventional oven. Discard contents of cavity; tip juices from cavity into pan drippings. Transfer chicken to platter and tent with foil; let stand for 20 minutes before serving.
Skim fat from pan juices. Sprinkle with flour; cook over med-high heat, stirring for 1 minute. Pour in chicken stock and any juices from platter; boil, stirring, until thickened, about 3 minutes. Strain into warmed gravy boat. Serve with chicken.
