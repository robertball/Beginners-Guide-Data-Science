Title:
Big Ray's Crab-Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3771846.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups penne pasta, uncooked
1 each green, red and yellow pepper, chopped
1/2 cup cherry tomates, halved
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 cup shreddedcheddar cheese
1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette dressing
3/4 lb. flaked crab

Directions:
Cook pasta in large saucepan as directed on package, omitting salt and adding peppers to the boiling water for the last 2 min.; drain. Rinse with cold water; drain well. Place in large bowl.
Add mayo and tomatoes; mix lightly. Stir in shredded cheese and dressing. Refrigerate at least 20 min.

Submitter:

Description: 
Crab, Parmesan and sweet, crunchy peppers combine to create this delicious pasta salad.
