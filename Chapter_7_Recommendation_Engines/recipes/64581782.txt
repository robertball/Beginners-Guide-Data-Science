Title:
Banana pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 egg yolks
4 cups of milk
2 tbl. spoons cornstarch
2/3 cup sugar
1 tbl. spoon vanilla extract
4 tbl. spoon butter
5 bananas
3 tbl. spoon freshly squeezed lemon juice

Directions:
Mix five egg yolks with sugar, vanilla extract and corn starch. (Set aside) Put milk in a medium size pot at medium heat slowly .  While it heats mix in flour one table spoon at time.  Once it slightly  thickens take off heat and add egg mixture to the milk. Keep slowly wisking until thick add butter in to slightly thin out. Set pudding in a large bowl with plastic wrap on top touching the pudding  while cooling. Let it sit at room temperature till it cools . About 35/45 mins.   Once you feel comfortable with the coolness of the pudding you can start on the bananas.  In a medium size bowl smash five bananas then add fresh lemon juice to keep their color. Mix bananas in pudding cover and let chill all night  enjoy the delicious banana flavor.
