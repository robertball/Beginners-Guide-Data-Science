Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces of choice beer (Corona)
3 T. Chili Powder
1 T. Cumin
1 T. Paprika
2 beef boullon
1/2 t. cayenne pepper
2 (15 ounce) Tomato Sauce
2 (15 ounce) Diced Tomato
1 (15 ounce) light red kidney beans,drained & rinsed
1 (15 ounce) chili beans
1 t. sugar
1 lb. of hamburger
1 onion, diced
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 green pepper, diced

Directions:
Combine onion, garlic, green pepper and hamburger in large skillet and cook til browned.  Put in a large pot or crockpot and add remaining ingredients.  Cook for 1 1/2 hours on stove or on low for 6-7 hours.
