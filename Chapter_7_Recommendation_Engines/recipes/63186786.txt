Title:
Pizza crust for high altitude Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages  active dry yeast (1/4 oz. per package)
1 1/2 cups warm water, between 106 and 115 degrees Farenheit (critical!)
1 tbsp. sugar
4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1  1/2 tsp. kosher salt
2 1/2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil

Directions:
Dissolve sugar in water in the bowl of mixer.  Sprinkle yeast on top.
Let sit for 10 min.
Using dough hook, mix in salt and olive oil.
Add flour while mixing and mix for about 2 minutes.
Divide dough ball in half and let each half rise individually in a greased bowl for about 10 minutes.
- at this stage, dough may be refrigerated for a few days or frozen for later use.
Roll out into desired shape (or try your hand at tossing the dough like the pros)
Top as desired
Bake at 450 for 10-12 minutes or until done to your liking

Submitter: 
Sam Martin
Description: 
This crust is a great chewy yet crispy base for pizza awesomeness!
