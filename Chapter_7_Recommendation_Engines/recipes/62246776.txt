Title:
Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/330267.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, very thinly sliced
1 red onion, very thinly sliced
2 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
2 tbsp sour cream
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 tsp sugar
1 tsp fresh chopped dill

Directions:
Mix cucumbers and onions together.
Mix rest of ingredients together in a closed bowl and give it a shake.
Add to cucumbers and chill until very cold then serve.

Submitter: 
Irina
Description: 
This is a REFRESHING, tangy Salad--low in fat. If you want it creamy and not so low in fat, just add 2 tablespoons mayonnaise. Be sure to slice the cucumbers and onions very very thin! Serve as salad or top open faced sandwiches with it!
