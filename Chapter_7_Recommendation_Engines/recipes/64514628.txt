Title:
Comforting Cranberry-Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
3/4 c sugar
2 T flour
16 oz whole-berry cranberry sauce
7 c thinly sliced tart apples
Topping:
1 c old fashioned oats
1/3 c flour
1/3 c packed brown sugar
1 t cinnamon
1/4 c cold butter, cubed

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine sugar and flour, stir in cranberry sauce. Add apples, toss to coat. Transfer to a greased 9x13" baking dish.
In a small bowl, combine oats, flour, brown sugar, and cinnamon; cut in butter until mixture is crumbly. Sprinkle over apply mixture.
Bake at 375 degrees for 35-40 minutes or until filling is bubbly and topping is golden brown. Serve warm.
