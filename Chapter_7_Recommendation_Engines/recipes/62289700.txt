Title:
Southern Pecan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 tbsp. butter
1 cup buttermilk
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 cup all purpose flour
1 cup ground pecans
1 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. paprika
1/8 tsp. pepper
1/4 cup sesame seeds
2 pounds of chicken pieces
1/4 cup pecan halves
1 cup mixed red and green grapes

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees.
Melt butter in a 10X15 baking pan.  In shallow dish, combine buttermilk and egg.  In another dish, combine flour, pecans, salt, paprika, pepper and sesame seeds.  Dip chicken in buttermilk mixture then in flour.  Place skin side down in melted butter.  Turn to coat and leave skin side up.  Sprinkle with pecan halves.  Bake 1 hour, 25 minutes.  Half way through cooking, toss the grapes into the baking pan as well.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a Paula Deen recipe which makes a moist and tender chicken dish.  Very easy!
