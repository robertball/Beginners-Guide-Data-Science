Title:
Chicken Binakol (Filipino Chicken Soup) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 pound fresh ginger root, peeled and sliced
2 onions, sliced
5 stalks lemongrass, sliced
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, chopped
1 cup sliced coconut meat
1 1/2 cups coconut water
1 1/2 cups water
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Saute ginger, onion, and lemongrass in the hot oil until soft, 5 to 7 minutes. Add chicken and saute until tender and no longer pink in the centers, 5 to 7 minutes. Add coconut meat; cook for 3 minutes. Pour in coconut water and bring to a boil; boil for 2 minutes. Add water, salt, and pepper to taste. Serve.
