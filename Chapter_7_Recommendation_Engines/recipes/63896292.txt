Title:
Dill flavored crab dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1081259.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese softened
2 tbsp prepared horseradish cream
2 tbsp fresh dill chopped
2 cans of crab meat
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a mixing bowl combine all the ingredients and beat with a hand mixer on low speed until all is mixed well.
Refregirate for several hours before serving.

Submitter: 
XxlilaxX
Description: 
This delicious dip is perfect for hot summer days or for holidays. Serve on crackers or use for dipping your favorite veggies
