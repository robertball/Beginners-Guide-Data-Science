Title:
Tijuana Taco Soup - Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/995791.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Onion, chopped
1 lb. Ground Beef, browned
4 oz. Green Chilies
1 can Black Bean
1/2 bag or 1 can Corn
1 tsp Cumin
1 can Stewed Tomatoes
1 envelope Taco Seasoning
15 oz. can Tomato Sauce
1 cup Sour Cream
1 bag Cheese Cheddar, shredded, topper
1 bag Fritos Chips

Directions:
Brown meat w/ onion.
Place in crockpot w/ rest of ingredients... minus cheese and sour cream & Fritos.
Cook on LOW for 6 - 8 hours.
Top w/ cheese, sour cream and Fritos.

Submitter: 
Rachael Welch
Description: 
This is good comfort food. Serve with Fritos or mexican corn bread.
