Title:
Cran-Tangerine Smoothis Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 4 oz. cran-tangerine juice drink, chilled
- 1/2 banana, sliced
- 3 medium strawberries, tops removed and cut into halves
- 1/2 c. crushed ice

Directions:
Add all ingredients into a blender and blend well.  Garnish with a strawberry!

Submitter: 
Traveling_Is_Love
Description: 
So summery and so good!  Tangerine, cranberry, banana, and strawberry flavors prove to be a wonderful frozen drink!
