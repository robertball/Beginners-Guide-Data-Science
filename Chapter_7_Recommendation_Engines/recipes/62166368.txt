Title:
Cheeseburger soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/290599.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box macaroni and cheese dinner
1 lb. ground beef
11 oz can corn, drained
3 cups beef broth
1 1/2 cups V8 vegetable juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Prepare macaroni and cheese dinner according to package directions.
Brown ground beef.
In soup pan mix ground beef with macaroni and cheese, corn, beef broth and V8 juice.
Simmer for 5-10 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
Audrey Ansell
Description: 
This quick and easy soup can be on the table in 30 minutes, great for busy school nights.  It is good comfort food and my kids love it!
