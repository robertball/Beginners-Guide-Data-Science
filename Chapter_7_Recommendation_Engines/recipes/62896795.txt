Title:
Jim's Home Made Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/701311.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (3 pound) whole chickens
6 carrots sliced
6 stalks celery sliced
3 large onions, quartered
4- cubes chicken bouillion
1- Tablespoon minced garlic
water to cover
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Put the chicken in a pot with the carrots, celery, onion, chicken bouillion and garlic and simmer until the chicken falls off the bone.Strain the stock and return to the pot. Remove the chicken,tear off the meat and return to the pot. Noodles on the side are an option
