Title:
Spicy White Queso (Cheese) Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 lbs Land o Lakes White American Cheese
1 pint half and half
black pepper to taste
3 T chopped jalapenos (optional)

Directions:
Dice cheese. Melt in 2-quart double boiler on medium-high heat. Gradually add half and half, stirring constantly while adding the liquid. Add black pepper and jalapenos (i fusing). Mixture will be thin at first, but it will thicken up nicely.

Submitter: 
DANIKA1066
Description: 
A delicious, slightly spicy cheese dip that's great with tortilla chips.
