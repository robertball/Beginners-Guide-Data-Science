Title:
Christmas Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 large marshmallows
1 c. boiling water
1 c. pecans, chopped
1 red apple, chopped
1 can crushed pineapples (drained)
1/2 pint heavy whipping cream
1 (3 oz) lime jello

Directions:
Melt marshmallows in boiling water.  Add 3 pz jello.  Stir until jello is completely dissolved.  Let cool.  Once cooled add pecans, apple, pineapples, and last heavy whipping cream.  Do not whip cream.  Stir all together.  Refrigerate until it sets.

Submitter: 
Karen Fraley
Description: 
This is our traditional Christmas salad.  We eat eat every Christmas day after opening presents.  This is part of our family's brunch menu.  I'm 43 and I've had this every year!  Passed down from my grandmother.  I'm passing it on to my adult children.
