Title:
Easy Onion Garlic Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1087819.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
3 tablespoons minced garlic
1 tablespoon granulated garlic, or more to taste
1 tablespoon sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, onion soup mix, garlic, and granulated garlic together in a bowl; add more granulated garlic if desired. Stir sour cream into dip for a thinner consistency. Refrigerate until serving time.

Submitter: 
lkruth
Description: 
Four ingredients, 5 minutes, and you have a delicious appetizer. Spread on your favorite bread (I recommend whole wheat beer bread) or crackers. Great for Thanksgiving, game days, or any time you have guests and don't want too much fuss.
