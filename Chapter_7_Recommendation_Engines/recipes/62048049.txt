Title:
cheese Stuffing for fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1 onion finely chopped
4 garlic cloves finely chopped
1 1/2 cups fresh bread crumbs
salt and pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground rosemary
1/8 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheesee

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic in butter for 5 minutes. stir in remaining ingredients except cheese. Let cool for 5 minutes. stir in cheese and stuff the fish.

Submitter: 
MOLSON7
Description: 
I use this as a stuffing for flounder. This will stuff approximatly 4 pieces.After stuffing I brush fish with some butter and lemon and paprika then bake.
