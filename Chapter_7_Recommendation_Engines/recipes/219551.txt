Title:
Mediterranean Bagel Snacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985140.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons roasted garlic-flavored hummus
2 Pepperidge FarmÂ® Plain Bagels, split and toasted
4 thin slices deli roast beef
2 tablespoons drained jarred roasted red peppers cut into strips
2 red onion slices
1/2 cup packed baby arugula leaves

Directions:
Spread 1 1/2 teaspoons hummus on each bagel half. Divide the beef, pepper strips, onion and arugula between 2 bagel halves. Top with the remaining bagel halves.
Cut each bagel into 8 "bites" and secure with toothpicks.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Bagels are bigger than breakfast! We use them to make a flavorful roast beef sandwich, accented with hummus, roasted peppers, onion and arugula. Now that's a sandwich!
