Title:
Curried chipotle potato, spinach and cheese wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp Olive oil
4 small red Potatoes
Curry paste or seasoning to taste
2 tbsp cream
Tortillas
4 Muenster cheese slices
2 cups Baby Spinach
Chipotle salsa to taste

Directions:
1. Boil red potatoes until tender (about 20 minutes)
2. Dice potatoes.
3. Add Olive oil and potatoes to warm skillet.
4. Slowly stir curry paste and cream into potatoes.
5. Microwave tortilla with 2 slices of cheese in middle for 15-25 seconds. Be careful not to microwave too long or the tortilla will become hard or the cheese will burn.
6. Add some curried potato mixture, handful of spinach and chipotle salsa to the tortilla and heat for another 20 seconds.
7. Wrap and eat.

Submitter: 
Smadera
Description: 
Simple warm spicy wrap. Milk or half and half can be substituted for the cream.
