Title:
Cowboy Caviar Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/792330.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans Mexicorn
1 can hot Rotel
2 cups shredded cheddar
6 green onions (chopped)
1 cup each mayo and sour cream

Directions:
1. Drain the Mexicorn and rotel and pour in bowl.  Add the remaining ingredients mix well and chill for 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Angela Naumann
Description: 
This dip is addictive.  It is a Corn dip that has a Tex-Mex flare.  Quick and easy to make.  Everyone always wants this recipe, so be ready to share.
