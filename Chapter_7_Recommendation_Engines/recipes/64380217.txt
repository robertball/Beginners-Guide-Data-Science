Title:
Ellen's Grape Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz sour cream
8 oz cream cheese
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 lb grapes
2 Tbsp brown sugar
1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Mix sour cream, cream cheese, sugar and vanilla.  Add grapes and mix until grapes are coated.  Pour into large serving bowl.
Sprinkle with brown sugar and walnuts.
Refrigerate overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Creamy, not so sweet dressing makes this fruit salad special.
