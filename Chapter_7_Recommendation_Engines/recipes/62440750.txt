Title:
Orange Raisin Walnut Mini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Muffin ingredients:
1/2 cup Vegetable Shortening (solid)
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs, room temp
1 orange peel, grated
1 teaspoon salt
1 tesspoon baking soda
2 cups flour
1 cup buttermilk
1 cup raisins
1 cup chopped walnuts (or pecans)
Glaze ingredients:
1 cup sugar
juice of 2 oranges

Directions:
Muffin directions:   Cream sugar & shortening together.  Add eggs and orange peel mixing well.
In a seperate large bowl, sift flour, baking soda and salt.
Add flour mixture alternately with buttermilk to batter, stirring after each addition (do not beat).
Stir in raisins and nuts.
Spoon into miniature cup muffin pan that has been sprayed with oil.
Bake in preheated 350 oven for 12-15 minutes.
Glaze directions:   In a small saucepan mix glaze sugar and orange juice; cook over low heat stirring 5 minutes or till sugar melts.
Dip hot/warm muffins quickly in the glaze, then cool on rack.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
These mini muffins are glazed while still warm.
