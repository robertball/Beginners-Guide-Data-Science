Title:
No-Guilt Zesty Ranch Dip Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons bacon bits
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon dried dill weed

Directions:
Combine spices and store in an airtight container. To make into a dip or dressing, mix with your favorite combination of yogurt, mayo, sour cream and buttermilk or milk, totaling 2 cups of wet ingredients. (Do not exceed 1 cup milk/buttermilk) Add a few dashes of hotsauce or a tablespoon of horseradish to make this ZESTY.
Let sit for 2 hrs for flavors to combine.
