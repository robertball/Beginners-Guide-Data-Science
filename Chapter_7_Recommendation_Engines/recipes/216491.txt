Title:
Apple Slaw Hamwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989292.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup shredded tri-color cole slaw blend
1/2 cup chopped sweet-tart, red skinned apple
2 tablespoons chopped green onion, green top only
2 tablespoons bottled, lite slaw dressing
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
4 wedges Weight Watchers Spreadable Wedges, softened (choose your favorite flavor: Original Swiss, Jalapeno Pepper, Garlic & Herb, or Parmesan Peppercorn)
8 slices 45 calorie whole wheat bread
12 slices ultra thin, lower sodium, honey ham slices

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine cole slaw blend, apple, onion, slaw dressing, ginger and pepper to taste; mix to combine.
Spread 1 cheese wedge on each of 4 slices of bread, top with 1/4 cup cole slaw mixture, 3 slices of ham and a slice of bread. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Weight Watchers
Description: 
Color, flavor and crunch--it's all here in these ham sandwiches with cole slaw, chopped apples and ginger on whole wheat spread with soft, creamy cheese.
