Title:
Easy Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992708.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, cubed
1 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup milk
8 ounces fettuccine, cooked, drained

Directions:
Stir cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, butter and milk in large saucepan on low heat until smooth.
Add fettuccine; toss lightly.  Serve with additional Parmesan cheese, if desired. Try serving with Italian bread and a salad tossed with your favorite KRAFT Special Collection Dressing, such as Balsamic Vinaigrette.
