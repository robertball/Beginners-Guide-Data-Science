Title:
Toffee Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 c. microwave popcorn (2 bags)
nuts?
1/2 c. sugar
1/2 c. butter
1/4 c. light corn syrup

Directions:
Keep popcorn and nuts warm in oven at 300.
Melt butter med/low heat.
Add sugar and corn syrup.
Simmer - stirring occasionally.
Candy thermometer to 300 degrees.
Pour over popcorn and nuts.
Stir.
Heat in oven another 5 minutes.
Stir - repeat if necessary.
Let cool on wax paper.
Store covered.
