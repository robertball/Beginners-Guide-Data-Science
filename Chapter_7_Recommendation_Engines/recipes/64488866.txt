Title:
Gluten Free Caprese Cauliflower Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Head of Cauliflower (cored and roughly chopped)
2 Eggs
1/3-1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
2 Tbsp Coconut Flour
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
.
CAPRESE TOPPING
1 heirloom tomato (sliced)
Sliced Fresh Mozzarella
Chopped Fresh Basil
Drizzle Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Drizzle Balsamic Vinegar
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
Line 2 flat baking sheets with parchment paper.
Pulse cauliflower in a food processor until fine.
Steam for about 12-15 mins. Drain well.
When cooled enough to handle, place in a cheese cloth and wring out excess water (paper towels work too).
Combine cauliflower, eggs, cheese, coconut flour, salt and garlic powder.
Divide into 4 parts.
Spread into four circles resembling pizza dough.
Bake 20 mins, until golden crisp edges
Top with Caprese topping.

Submitter: 
Nate Thee
Description: 
Gluten free pizza crust with a Caprese topping. You can use the crust portion of this recipe for any pizza you may want to make.
