Title:
Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/769568.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large avocados (halved, stoned, peeled and mashed)
1 lime (juice)
1 roma/plum tomato (seeded and chopped)
1/4 cup red onion (chopped)
1 jalapeno pepper (seeded and chopped)
1 tablespoon cilantro (chopped)
1/2 teaspoon cumin (toasted and ground)
1/2 teaspoon cayenne
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix everything!

Submitter:

Description: 
It makes a nice foil for hot and spicy dishes. Guacamole is a really simple and versatile dish not to mention being oh so good!
