Title:
Baked Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package of Macaroni (your choice)
2 Tablespoons of butter
2 1/2 Tablespoons of flour
2 1/2 cups of shredded cheese
3 cups of milk
Bread crumbs (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Boil Macaroni in a large pot, stirring occasionally until cooked.
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Mix together with flower until there are no lumps. Slowly add Milk to cheese mixture stirring constantly. Stir in Cheese and let it melt and thicken. Place macaroni in large baking dish and pour sauce over macaroni. Stir.
Sprinkle a little extra cheese and breadcrumbs on top. Place in oven for about 30 minutes.
