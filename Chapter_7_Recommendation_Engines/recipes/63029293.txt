Title:
Speedy Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 avocados
2-3 cloves garlic
squeeze of lemon or lime juice(optional)
1/4 cup onion
1/4 cup tomato
1-2 sprigs fresh cilantro
1/6 jalapeno pepper (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Scoop out meat of avocados, discard pit, and place in Short Cup.
Place remaining ingredients, in order, into the Short Cup.
Twist on the Cross Blade and place cup onto the Power Base.
Give it 3-4 quick Pulses until all the ingredients are chopped and blended.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Tastes best when it's chilled, served with nachos, as a taco or fajita topping, or atop a juicy burger.
