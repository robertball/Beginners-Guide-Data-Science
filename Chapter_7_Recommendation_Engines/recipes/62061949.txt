Title:
Chicken Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cooked chicken breasts, cutinto bite-sized pieces
1 pkg. Taco seasoning
Iceberg lettuce, bite-size
Diced tomatoes
Doritos Nacho chips, crush to bite-size
1 can kidney beans, drained
Shredded Cheddar or Mexican mix cheese
Green Goddess Salad Dressing (secret ingredient)

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with taco seasoning tossing to cover well. Store in refrigerator until ready to use. To assemble: In large salad bowl, mix lettuce, chicken, tomatoes, chips, beans and shredded cheese. Before serving, toss with Green Goddess salad dressing(the secret)--just enough to flavor.

Submitter: 
CAROLINECOOKS
Description: 
Fantastic taste...it's the dressing. Can be prepared ahead and assembled before serving. An office potluck favorite; never had any left-overs.
