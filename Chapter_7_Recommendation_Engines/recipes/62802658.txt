Title:
Chess Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/657010.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
2 eggs
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, melted
2 eggs
4 cups confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Before making the recipe, look on the cake mix for High Altitude instructions.  If there are adjustments given, such as adding water or flour to the recipe, add those additions, but use the remaining directions below:
1.  Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
2.  Grease and flour one 9 x 13 inch pan.
3.  In a large bowl, mix together cake mix, 2 eggs, and 1/2 cup melted butter. Put into prepared pan.
4.  In another bowl, Beat cream cheese until smooth. Blend in 1/2 cup melted butter and 2 eggs. Gradually beat in the confectioner's sugar. Pour over the crust mixture already in the pan.
Bake for 1 hour. Cool.
