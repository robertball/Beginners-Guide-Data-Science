Title:
Malaysian Pisang Goreng Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup self-rising flour
1/4 cup corn flour
1 tbsp rice flour
pinch of salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
3/4 cup of water
1 1/2 tbsp oil
5-6 bananas, halved
oil for deep frying

Directions:
Combine the various flours, salt and baking powder in a mixing bowl.  Pour in water and combine into a batter.  Add in oil and mix until smooth.
Coat banana halves in batter and deep fry in hot oil for three to four minutes until crispy and golden brown.
Remove and drain on paper towels.
Great served with powdered sugar.
