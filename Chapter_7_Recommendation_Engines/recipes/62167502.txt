Title:
STEVES SMOKEY OIL PEPPERS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs hot Italian long peppers
2 heads garlic minced
2 large onions sliced
16 oz. light olive oil
Liquid smoke
Salt and pepper
Hickory/Mesquite smoking chips

Directions:
Heat barbeque grill to hot and get hickory smoking chips ready â€“ soak in water
Wash, stem and cut peppers into 1 inch chunks
Put peppers, onions and garlic in large aluminum turkey roasting pan add oil and sprinkle w/ liquid smoke stir to coat salt and pepper
Put smoking chips in foil pan on hot side of grill and roasting pan on grill
Close lid and turn down heat to med-high
Turn with long tongs after 15 mins blacken a few peppers on grill direct
Roast until peppers are not bright green after turning a few times
When done cool and put in bottles and serve w/ oil

Submitter: 
Steve Fairhurst
Description: 
These oil roasted peppers were inspired by Martinis restraunt in Devon Pa. Long closed but not forgotten - the best Italian food!
