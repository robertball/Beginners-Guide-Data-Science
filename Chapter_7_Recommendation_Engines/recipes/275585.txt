Title:
Cold Seafood Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
10 ounces cooked crabmeat
2 hard-boiled eggs
1 1/2 cups sour cream
1/3 cup picante sauce
1 (.6 ounce) packet zesty Italian salad dressing mix
1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice, or to taste

Directions:
Chop crabmeat and eggs and place in a large bowl. Add sour cream, picante sauce, salad dressing mix, and lemon juice; mix to combine. Place in the refrigerator until chilled, at least 1 hour.
