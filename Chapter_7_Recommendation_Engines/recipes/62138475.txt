Title:
Dutch Breakfast Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of flour
1 tbsp of baking powder
1 cup of milk
1/2 cup of dark brown sugar
1/3 cup of molasses
1 tsp of cloves
1 tsp of cinnamon
1 tsp of ginger
1/2 tsp of nutmeg
1/8 tsp of salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300F
Combine all ingredients and stir until a smooth paste.
Place into a  8x3 buttered loaf pan
Bake for about an hour or insert a toothpick to check if done.
