Title:
Italian Meatball Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound meatballs
1 clove garlic, minced
2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth
1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style stewed tomatoes
1 cup sliced carrots
1 (14.5 ounce) can great Northern beans, undrained
2 cups spinach - packed, rinsed and torn
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a stockpot or Dutch oven, brown meatballs with garlic.  Stir in  broth, tomatoes and carrots, and season with salt and pepper. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes.
Stir in beans with liquid. Cover, and simmer another 15 minutes-20 minutes.
Remove from heat, add spinach.  Replace lid, allowing the heat from the soup to cook the spinach leaves.  Soup is ready to serve after 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Blaire Nicole
Description: 
This soup is easy to put together, and the flavor of the spicy sausage is balanced nicely by Great Northern beans, zucchini, fresh spinach, and carrots.  Makes a delicious winter supper.Modified from: Italian Sausage Soup
