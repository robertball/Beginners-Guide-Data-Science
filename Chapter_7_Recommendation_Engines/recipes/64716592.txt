Title:
Simple instant pot beef chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5767288.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1/2” pieces
6 ounce can tomato paste
14.5 ounce can fire roasted diced tomatoes, undrained
1 cup water
1/4 cup chili powder
1 tsp chipotle chile powder
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp savory spice shop lobo red adobo seasoning
1/2 tsp smoked paprika
1/2 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Add diced tomatoes and beef to instant pot. Set to high pressure and cook for 20 minutes. Allow pressure to release naturally.
Remove lid, switch to normal sauté mode.
Allow liquid to reduce about 5 minutes.
Add in tomato paste and water.
Add in all the seasonings, stir to combine.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
thedailygourmet
Description: 
This chili requires minimal ingredients, and no beans! It packs a full flavor profile and isn’t too spicy. To preserve the integrity of the spices do not add them until the end
