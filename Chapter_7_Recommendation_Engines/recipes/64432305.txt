Title:
CREAMY CAULIFLOWER PASTA WITH FRESH HERBS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2334538.jpg
Ingredients:
1 POUND OF LINGUINE
4 CUPS OF CHOPPED SPINACh
SALT, PEPPER TO Taste
1/2 head of CALIFLOUR CUT INTO LITTLE TREES
1/2 CUP UNSEEETENED PLAIN NONDARIY MILK
2 OR 3 CLOVES OF MINCED GARLIC
1TBL LEMON JUICE
1 TBL. OLIVE OIL
1 TEASPOON MISO PASTE
PARSLEY (OPTIONAL)

Directions:
1.Cook pasta as directed on package,drain and set aside
2.Bring another pot of water to boil with a pinch of salt,and add cauliflower and simmer until the cauliflower is tender, About 5 to7 minutes, drain the toss into a blender or food processor
3.add milk,garlic,lemon juice,olive oil,miso paste,and 1/8 tea.of salt to the blender and mix until creamy. taste and adjust to your taste
4.pour cauliflower puree back on to pasta and mix very well.
5. put into a 9 X 13 pan, cover and put into 200 degree oven to warm for 30 minutes or until it is warmed thru. when ready to serve, top with some parsley, salt and pepper to taste. serve hot.

Description: 
want to enjoy creamy pasta without having to worry about cholesterol? Pureed cauliflower makes this sauce silky and tasty. feel free to add roasted asparagus, or steamed broccoli or roasted peppers to this dish to mix it up.
