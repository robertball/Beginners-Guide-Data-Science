Title:
Zuchinni Noodles, Cannellini Beans, Quinoa Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4539298.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of quinoa (rinsed)
2 cups water
1/2 tsp salt
t tbsp. cooking oil
1 clove fresh garlic, minced
1 cup cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
2 medium zuchinni, spiralized
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1/4-1/2 cup parmesan cheese (shredded)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil water in a medium pot with salt. When boiling, add rinsed quinoa and simmer covered on low till the water is absorbed. 10-15 min.
Place oil in medium saucepan and warm over medium heat. Add garlic, and saute for 30 seconds. Add in cannellini beans and toss with the garlic. Cook for 1 minute then add zucchini noodles. Season with salt and pepper and cook until zucchini is crisp tender. Toss in cherry tomatoes and sprinkle parmesan cheese on top. Portion cooked quinoa into two bowls and serve the zucchini noodle mixture on the quinoa.

Submitter: 
Arissa Farr May
Description: 
Instead of requiring the oven during the hot zucchini and tomato season, here is a way to serve up some of your favorite summer produce, packed with protein and flavor, quickly and right in the frying pan. This recipe serves 2 but can easily be doubled or tripled depending on your garden bounty and dinner guests!
