Title:
Creamy Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups white sugar
1 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
28 jumbo marshmallows
1 (16 ounce) jar peanut butter
1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine sugar, brown sugar, butter and evaporated milk.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, and boil without stirring until mixture reaches softball stage, 234 degrees (about 7 minutes).
Remove from heat and let sit 5 minutes without stirring. Stir in remaining ingredients until well incorporated. Spread into pan and let cool before cutting into squares.
