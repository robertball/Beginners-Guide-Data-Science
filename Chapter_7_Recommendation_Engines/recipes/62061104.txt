Title:
Delicious Chicken Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
8 oz. egg noodle pasta, cooked and drained
1 (10.75 oz) can of cream of celery soup
1 (10.75 oz) can of cream of chicken soup
1 cup of sour cream
1 medium onion, chopped
2 (14.5 oz) cans of mixed vegetables, drained (or your favorite vegetable)
1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
salt and pepper to taste
1 sleeve of buttery round crackers, crushed
1/2 cup of butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Combine all ingredients except crackers and butter; place mixture into a 9 x 13 pan. Combine crackers and butter, top casserole. Bake for 35 minutes. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Butterfly Flutterby
Description: 
This casserole is wonderful comfort food and a complete meal in a pan! This is very yummy and rich and is easily adaptable to your own tastes. Use reduced fat soups and sour cream for a healthier meal. This is great to take to those recuperating or new Moms!
