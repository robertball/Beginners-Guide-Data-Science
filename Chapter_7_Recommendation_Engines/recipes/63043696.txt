Title:
Greg's Famous Spicy baked BBQ Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(8) slices of bacon chopped into small pieces
(1) tbs of the bacon fat reserved
(3) chopped small/medium onions
(4) finely diced cloves of garlic
(1) 14.5 oz can of DelMonte petite cut diced tomatoes with jalapeno
(1) can of Garbanzo beans drained and rinsed
(1) can of Great Northern beans drained and rinsed
(1) can of Navy beans drained and rinsed
(1) can of Kuner's black beans with jalapeno's NOT rinsed
1 cup of KC's Masterpiece BBQ sauce (or similar)
2/3 cup dark brown sugar
1/8 cup + 1 tbs molasses
1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/8 tsp ground cloves
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tbs Poupon Dijon mustard

Directions:
Chop slices of bacon into small pieces and saute (probably could substitute Morningstar in vegetarian version)
After cooking the bacon, use 1 tbs of the bacon fat (add olive oil if necessary) to saute onions & finely diced cloves of garlic.
After saute is complete, add all other ingredients and bring to a simmer.  Pour into baking dish and bake for ~1.5 hours at 275 until thickened.  Cool 10 minutes & serve.
