Title:
Oven-Fried Pork Chops Part Deax Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 thick cut pork chops, trimmedand butterflied
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1 egg, beaten
1 tablespoons milk
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 cup Panko bead crumbs
1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
1 tsp dried thyme

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Pour butter into a 9x13 inch baking pan.
Stir together egg, milk and pepper.
Mix the cheese, panko and thyme together.
Dip pork chops in egg mixture, coat with panko mixture and place in pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 13 minutes. Turn chops and bake for another 13minutes, or until no pink remains in the meat and juices run clear.Cooking times may vary.
