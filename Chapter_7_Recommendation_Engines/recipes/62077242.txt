Title:
Sweet and Savory Ginger Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4  chicken breasts pounded thin
1/3  cup flour
1  egg beaten
2  tablespoons olive oil
1  cup unsweetened pineapple juice
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup soy sauce
2  teaspoon ground ginger
3 cloves garlic minced

Directions:
Coat chicken with egg then flour.
Heat oil in non stick frying pan over medium-high heat.
Cook chicken 3-4 minutes per side, until lightly browned on the outside.
Meanwhile, mix pineapple juice, honey, soy sauce, ginger and garlic.
Pour over the chicken.
Cook, stirring constantly, until chicken juices run clear when pierced and the sauce is slightly thickened.
If the sauce is too thick, add a few tablespoons of water.

Submitter: 
BajaValle
Description: 
This is a tasty and easy chicken recipe. Serve with Basmati rice and cucumber salad.
