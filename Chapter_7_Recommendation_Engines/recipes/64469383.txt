Title:
The Original Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2459139.jpg
Ingredients:
Pancake or Waffle mix | 2 cups
Water | 1 cup
Left-over Spaghetti Sauce (watered down) | 1/3 cup
Coconut Oil (or oil of your choice) | 1/3 cup and 1 tbsp
Kale, Chopped | 1 cup
Garlic Salt | To-taste
Strawberries (optional) | Garnish

Directions:
1.  Using a hand whisk, mix the Pancake or Waffle mix (store brand works the best), water, Spaghetti Sauce, and 1/3 cup of coconut oil.  Continue mixing until small lumps are left. Continue mixing until small lumps are left.
2.  Let mix sit for 4-5 minutes.*
3.  While mix is sitting and raising, place 1-tbsp of Coconut oil in a pan at 350-375 degrees.  Once hot, add in 1 cup of Kale.  Sauté for approximately 3-5 minutes depending on the texture you like.  Add in a pinch or two of Garlic salt (to your taste).
4.  Using a small spoon, put ½ - 1 tsp of mix in the bottom of mini-cupcake pan.  Not to fill over half-way or they will run over.  Then place a bit of the sautéed Kale on top for a delightful treat.
5.  Bake in oven from 10-15 minutes at 375°, (keep an eye on them it all depends on altitude).
6.  Cool in pan for 10 minutes.
7.  If desired, choose a strawberry as a garnishment.
