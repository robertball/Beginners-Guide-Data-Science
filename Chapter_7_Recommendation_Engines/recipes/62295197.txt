Title:
Herbed or Cheesey Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
melted butter or bacon drippings
parmesean cheese
thyme
garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Grease and flour six 6-ounce custard cups.
In a medium bowl beat eggs slightly, Beat in flour, milk, salt, garlic and thyme until just smooth; being careful not to overbeat.Add bacon drippings or melted butter to bottom of HOT tins. Fill muffin cups 1/4 full.Place cube or pinch of Parmesean cheese in to batter and finishing topping with batter until 1/2 full.
Bake at 450 degrees F (230 degrees C) for 15 minutes. Decrease oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake for 10 minutes more. Immediately remove from cups and serve piping hot.

Description: 
The secret to light and airy popovers is a very light touch in mixing and a very hot oven for baking.  These are best served hot from the oven.Modified from: Popovers
