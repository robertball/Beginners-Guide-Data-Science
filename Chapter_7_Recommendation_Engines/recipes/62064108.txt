Title:
Cashew Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breast halves, boiled, cooled, and shredded
dash each of celery salt, garlic powder, and onion powder
2 stalks of celery, finely chopped
1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
1 TBS sweet pickle relish
1/2 cup cashews, coarsely chopped
3/4 cup real mayonnaise
1 TBS spicy brown mustard

Directions:
Boil chicken breast, with a few dashes each of celery salt, garlic powder, and onion powder, for approximately 15 minutes or until no longer pink in the center. Allow chicken to cool. Shred chicken, discarding any gristle or fat. Chop celery, onion, and cashews. (A food chopper works well for this) In a meduim mixing, bowl combine all ingredients adding enough mayonnaise to make very moist. Mix well. Season to taste with celery salt and pepper. Refrigerate several hours or overnight to let flavors develop.

Submitter: 
Stefani Ferguson
Description: 
Cashews add texture to this simple chicken salad. Serve on croissants with a leaf of romaine lettuce for the perfect party sandwiches.
