Title:
South Beach Chopped Salad with Tuna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz can water packed tuna
1/3 chopped cucumber
1/3 c chopped tomatoes
1/3 c chopped avocado
1/3 c chopped celery
1/3 c chopped radishes
1 c chopped romaine lettuce
4 tsp. extra-virgin olive oil
2 tbsp. lime juice
2 cloves of garlic finely chopped
1/2 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Layer ingredients in a decorative bowl.  Mix dressing and drizzle over salad
