Title:
Evan's Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive oil
2 tbsp Low sodium soy sauce
2 tbsp Minced garlic
2 tsp Old Bay seasoning
1 tsp Ginger
1/2 tsp Black pepper

Directions:
Mix all well. Use as a marinade for steaks for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Jodi
Description: 
My 12yo step-son created this marinade. He said he tried it at his grandmother's, and we had just been trying to decide how we wanted to cook the steaks on the grill so we decided to try it - it was terrific!! (and he was thrilled that we all loved it)
