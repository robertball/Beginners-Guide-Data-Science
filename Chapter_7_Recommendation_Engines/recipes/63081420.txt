Title:
Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/778627.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lg. cans white chicken
1 can sliced carrots
1 can cream poto
1 can cream of chicken
dash of milk
salt and pepper to taste
2 pie crust
one egg

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, add to uncooked pie shell. Put other pie shell on top and vent. Brush top of pie shell with egg.
Cook at 350 for 45 min. to 1 hr.
