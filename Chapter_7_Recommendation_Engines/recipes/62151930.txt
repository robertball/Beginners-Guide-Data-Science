Title:
Chili with Corn and Cheddar Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Your favorite recipe for chili
OR this one if you prefer:
1 1/2 lbs lean ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 can (15 oz) whole kernel corn, undrained, divided
1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can (16 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
2 tablespoons chili powder
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 1/3 cups biscuit mix
2/3 cup cornmeal
2/3 cup milk
1/2 cup cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
FOR CHILI:
Cook ground beef, onion and garlic in a large skillet until meat is browned.
Set aside 1/2 cup drained corn.
Stir remaining corn and its liquid along with black beans, tomatoes, tomatoe sauce, chili powder, cumin, black pepper and cayenne pepper into the ground beef mixture.
Bring to a boil and then reduce to simmer for 30 minutes.
OR make your own favorite chili recipe.
FOR DUMPLINGS:
In medimum bowl add biscuit mix, corn meal and milk and mix until well blended.
Stir in reserved corn and cheese.
Drop spoonfulls of dumpling batter on top of chili and cook for 15 minutes.
Cover and cook another 5 more minutes.
