Title:
Grapefruit Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/212317.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen limeade
1 1/2 cups tequila
1 1/2 cups ruby red grapefruit juice
1 1/2 cups ice
4 lime wedges, for garnish

Directions:
Combine the limeade, tequila, grapefruit juice, and ice in a blender; blend until smooth. Serve in glasses garnished with lime wedges.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Jackson Satterfield
Description: 
A mixture of lime and grapefruit make a refreshing margarita. My friends love this one!
