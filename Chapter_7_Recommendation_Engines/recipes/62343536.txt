Title:
Spicy Oven BBQ Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pkg Boneless/Skinless chicken breast fillets.
1tbl veggie oil
1 bottle favorite BBQ sauce
1 bottle favorite beer
3tbl honey
1/2c. chopped dried white peaches
1c. chopped dried chili-spiced mango (or plain dried mango)
1/4c. dried pitted cherries
1 can chipotle chili peppers- chopped- reserve liquid
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 375
Drizzle vegetable oil in 13x9 baking pan
Trim fat and rinse chicken breasts in cold water. Pat dry with paper towel and place in baking pan- topside down.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper- bake for 20 minutes
Place remaining ingredients in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil. Let simmer while the chicken cooks.
Remove chicken from oven and pour all of the sauce over chicken. Flip chicken breasts over and spoon sauce over it until coated.
Place back in oven for another 30 minutes.
Let cool for 10 minutes.
Serve with BBQ sauce left in baking dish.
