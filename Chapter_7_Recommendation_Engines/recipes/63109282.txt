Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
1/2 of a large onion, diced
1 Egg
15 Saltine Crackers, crushed
Dash of Garlic Powder, Salt, & Pepper
1/2 C of Ketchup

Directions:
Pre Heat the oven to 400*.  Place all ingredients except the ketchup into a large bowl.  Mix the ingredients thouroughly with your hands.  Turn the mixture out into a loaf pan and smoosh to evenly spread it in the pan, spread the ketchup over the top.  Cook in the pre-heated oven for 40 - 50 minutes. If there is alot of grease you could drain it off now if you choose.  Let the meat rest a few minutes before serving.
