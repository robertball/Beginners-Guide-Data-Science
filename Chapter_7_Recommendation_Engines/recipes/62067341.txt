Title:
Mexican Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of refigerated crescent roll dough
1 8 oz. bar of cream cheese
2 Tbl. taco seasoning
4 oz. shredded mexican cheese blend
1 can of green chilies
4 Tbls. salsa

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Mix all of the ingredients (except for the crescent dough) in a bowl.
Unroll crescent dough.  Press together the edges of two triangles to form a rectangle.  Spead mixture across the dough and roll longwise (jellyroll style).  Repeat until all dough is rolled up.  Slice each roll into 10 slices (approx. 1/3 inch each).  Place each roll on cookie sheet and bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
DRUMNWRITE
Description: 
This is always a hit at parties.  Make half of the recipe without the green chilis to make it kid friendly or for those who don't like things spicy.
