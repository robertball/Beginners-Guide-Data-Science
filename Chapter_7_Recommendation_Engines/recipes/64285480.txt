Title:
Winter Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oatmeal
1/2 cup baking mix
1/2 cup sugar
Large dash ground cinnamon
1/4 cup melted butter
1 cup milk
Choice of fruit

Directions:
-Fruit in greased baking dish.
-Mix dry ingredients.
-Add wet ingredients to dry.
-Pour over fruit.
-Bake 350 oven. Uncovered.
-Check for doneness after about 30 minutes.
Baking time will vary depending on type, amount, juiciness of fruit.
