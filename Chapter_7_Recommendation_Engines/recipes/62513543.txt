Title:
Italian Sausage & Veggie Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/508326.jpg
Ingredients:
1 med sweet onion, chopped
2 C cauliflower florettes, chopped
1 tsp red pepper flakes
4 Italian chicken sausages, precooked
7 C chicken broth
1 jar roasted red peppers, drained and chopped
3/4 C evaporated fat free milk
1 bag fresh spinach,chopped

Directions:
In a large soup pot, over low/med heat saute onions in cooking spray just until tender(about 10 min). Meanwhile, remove casings from sausage and crumble.
Add broth,cauliflower and crumbled sausage to pot and bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer.
When cauliflower is nearly to your desired tenderness(about 15-20 min) stir in red peppers and milk and continue over low heat just until heated throughout.
Stir in spinach and serve immediately.

Description: 
This is just a simple, healthy dish inspired by tuscan style soups. Easily modified, and I like a little fresh grated parmesan on top when serving
