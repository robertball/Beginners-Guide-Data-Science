Title:
Vicky's southwestern turkey club Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 loaves wheat or sun dried tomato french bread loaves, slice the loaves in half lenth wise.
1 1/2 pound southwestern turkey sliced thin
1/2 pound thin sliced pepper jack cheese
1/2 pound provolone cheese
1 stick butter, (not margerine) melted and 1 1/2 tsp. garlic powder added to it
2 tsp. Italian seasoning
mayo, spinich leaves, black olives, tomato slices

Directions:
split both loaves of french bread legnth wise and spread all 4 halves with butter garlic mixture.  Sprinkle  with Italian seasoning evenly. Top with cheeses and place under broiler for a few minutes till cheese melts.  After cheese melts, layer on southwest turkey and spread on mayonaise to your liking.  (thin or thick)  Top with spinich, tomato slices, and black olives.  Cut into wedges and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Vicky Gunkel
Description: 
This sandwich is a Friday night with a movie regular at my house.  The kids come running in all directions for this hearty sandwich.  Easily a favorite meal in my home.  I have a large family this meal serves but this recipe can easily be halved for a family of 4-6, or bring on the company!!!
