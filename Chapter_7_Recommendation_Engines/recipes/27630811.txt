Title:
Rasberry Fruit Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar Dickinson's seedless rasberry preserves
1/4 cup orage juice
1-2 TBS. water

Directions:
Puree all ingredients in a food processor until smooth.
Add more water for desired consistancy. Makes 1-1/2 cups

Submitter: 
interdesignmsu
Description: 
Great as syrup for pancakes, waffles or crepes.
