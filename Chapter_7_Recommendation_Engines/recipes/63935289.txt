Title:
Slow Cooker Italian Beef Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1090972.jpg
Ingredients:
6 LB Rump Roast
2 TBSP Dried Basil
1 TBSP Dried Oregano
1/8 TBSP Black Pepper
3 Bay Leaves
1/2 TBSP Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
1 TBSP Salt
1 TBSP Garlic Powder
1 TBSP Parsley Flakes
3 C Water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a crock pot.  Cook on low 8-10 hours.  Remove bay leaves, shred meat and return to cooker in juices.

Submitter: 
Maggie Grimley
Description: 
This is my favorite Italian Beef recipe, handed down to me from an old neighbor in Illinois.  I hope you enjoy it as much as my family does.
