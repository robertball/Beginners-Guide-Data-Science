Title:
Kellogg's® Pop-Tarts™ Valentines Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989117.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Kellogg’s® Pop-Tarts® Cherry toaster pastries
White canned frosting

Directions:
Use 2 1/2- to 2 3/4-inch heart-shaped cookie cutter coated with cooking spray to cut out heart from each KELLOGG'S(R) POP-TARTS(R) FROSTED CHERRY toaster pastry.
Use white frosting to write the words 'Be Mine' on each.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S® POP-TARTS® toaster pastries
Description: 
Say ‘Be mine!' with a very cherry, quick-to-fix Valentine featuring POP-TARTS® Frosted Cherry toaster pastries.
