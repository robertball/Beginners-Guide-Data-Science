Title:
potato casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5lbs potatoes,peeled,boiled and mashed
2 pkgs cream cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons butter
2 teaspoons onion salt
bread crumb's(fine)
pepper to taste

Directions:
whip together all ingredients in the potato cooking pot with a electric hand mixer until fluffy.
put into a greased cassrole dish, dot the top with butter and sprinkle with fine bread crumbs.
bake at 350 degrees for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
serve hot.
we use a 9*13 inch glass baking dish

Submitter: 
Kim Truba

nice and simple twist on mashed potato's
