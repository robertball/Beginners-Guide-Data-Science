Title:
FOUR BEAN SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup red onion, minced
1/2 cup bell pepper, minced
1/2 cup celery, minced
2/3 cup red wine vinegar
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
1 (1 lb.) can kidney beans, rinsed
1 (1 lb.) can garbonzo beans, rinsed
1 (1 lb.) can green beans, rinsed
1 (1 lb.) can wax beans, rinsed

Directions:
In a glass container, with a tight fitting lid, combine the onion, bell pepper, celery, vinegar, oil, sugar, salt and pepper. Shake to mix. Set aside.
In a large bowl, gently mix together the rinsed beans and pour the dressing over them; cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate at least 12 hours.

Submitter: 
lgmarge
Description: 
A basic bean salad that is sure to please. A food processor may be used to mince the vegetables.
