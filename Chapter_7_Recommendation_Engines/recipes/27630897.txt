Title:
Butternut Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 cup(s) low-sodium chicken broth
3 tablespoon(s) olive oil
2 cup(s) small butternut squash chunks
1 onion peeled and finely chopped
2 cup(s) arborio rice
3 tablespoon(s) butter
3 tablespoon(s) grated parmesan
1 teaspoon(s) salt
1 pinch(s) black pepper

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, boil broth. Remove and set aside.
In a large saucepan, heat oil over low heat. Add squash and onion; cook for 1 minute. Add rice and cook, stirring, for 2 minutes. Add 2 cups of broth and simmer, stirring, over low heat until absorbed. Keep stirring and adding broth 1 cup at a time until absorbed. The whole process will take about 25 minutes.
In a small bowl, comine butter, parmesan, salt and pepper. Stir mixture into risotto. Add more seasoning if desired.
