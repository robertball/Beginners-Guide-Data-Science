Title:
Brown Rice Pilaf with Onions and Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
1/2 cup fresh or frozen corn (thawed)
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup brown rice
1 1/4 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Heat olive oil in small saucepan over medium heat.
Add corn and onions; saute until lightly browned.
Add rice and stir to coat rice with oil.
Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.  Cover and reduce heat.
Cook until rice is tender, about 45 minutes.
