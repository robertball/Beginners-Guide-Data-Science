Title:
Vanilla Custard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C milk
1/4 C Sugar
4 eggs
1 t pure vanilla

Directions:
In a heavy saucepan heat the milk and sugar almost to a boil.  Break the eggs into a blender.  While the eggs whirl, slowly add the hot milk to cook the eggs.  Add the vanilla.
If the custard is not thick enough to coat the spoon place it back in the sauce pan and cook gently stirring until it thickens.
