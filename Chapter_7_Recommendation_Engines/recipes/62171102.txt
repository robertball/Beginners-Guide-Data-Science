Title:
Jim's famous Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked crab
1/2 C bread crumbs(or Ritz buttery round crackers)
2 T Mayonnaise
1 t dry mustard
1/4 t Old Bay seasoning
2 Eggs beaten
4  green scallions chopped
1 1/4 t lemon juice
1/8 t red pepper flakes
1 t worchester sauce
2 t parsley freshly chopped
1/2 t salt
1/2 t pepper
Butter for frying

Directions:
Mix the eggs, mayonnaise,parsley,worchesteshire, mustard, lemon juice,scallions,salt,pepper and red pepper flakes. Add the crab and bread crumbs. Combine all so that the crab does not break.
Form the crab cakles into the desired size patties and fry them in butter
Approx 4-5 minutes on each side.
