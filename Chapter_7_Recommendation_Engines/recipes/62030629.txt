Title:
Baked Garlic Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh diced tomatoes
4 zucchini/yellow summer squash,sliced
2 large onions, sliced
5 cloves fresh garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups dry white wine
1 bay leaf
1/2 t. paprika
1 1/4 cups colby jack cheese, grated
1 cup romano cheese, grated
1 1/4 cups heavy whipping cream

Directions:
1.  Butter inside of 3 quart baking dish
2.  Combine all ingredients except     cheese and cream and pour into casserole dish.  Canned tomatoes should NOT be used!
3.  Cover and bake for 1 hour at 375 F.
4.  Remove casserole and lower heat to 325 F.  Add cheese and cream.  Bake for 15 minutes longer.
5.  Serve with crusty garlic bread.  Awesome!!

Submitter: 
KelliK
Description: 
You can't beat this soup on a cold winter day!  Easy to make and your house will smell awesome while it cooks!
