Title:
Mini Dessert Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vegetable Oil
Flour Torillas - 1 8 pack of any large size.
Cream Cheese(softened) - 1 8oz bar
Brown Sugar - 1/2 cup
Vanilla - 1 tsp
Cinnamon - 1/2 tsp
Green Grapes - garnish
Strawberries - garnish
Cinnamon & Sugar

Directions:
Begin heating oil in a frying pan.  Oil should be 1-2 inches deep.
Mix cream cheese, brown sugar, vanilla and cinnamon until smooth.  Set aside.
Cut circles in the tortillas about 3-4 inches in diameter.  Using tongs hold the tortilla and dip into hot oil until the "taco shape" holds.  Fry each shell until golden brown and place on papertowel to cool.  Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
Apply a small amount of cream cheese mixture to the "taco shell" as if you were filling with meat.
Top with sliced grapes and minced stawberries.

Submitter: 
JnLAllin
Description: 
I made these on a whim trying to clean out my refridgerator.  Ended up with this gem!  Would be great at a shower or any other get together where you are serving fingerfoods.  Also, would be a great dessert on your family taco night.
