Title:
Simple French Toast in a Cup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp butter or margerine
1/4 c milk
1 egg
1/4 tsp each of cinnamon, sugar & vanilla
2 slices of bread, cubed

Directions:
Put the butter in a coffee mug and microwave on high for 30 seconds to melt. Tilt mug to coat all sides.
Add milk, egg, vanilla, cinnamon & sugar and mix well.
Add breadand press into mixture.
Microwave on high 1 1/2 mins.Add 40 seconds for each additional mug.

Submitter: 
Deb Hughes Cormier
Description: 
A quick & easy breakfast or lunch that you can modify to suite your taste. My 4 kids all love this. Try it with raisin bread too!!
