Title:
Big Ray's Broiled Tilapia Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1017916.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Parmesan Cheese
1/4 Cup Butter, Softened
3 Tbl Mayonnaise
2 Tbl Lemon Juice
1/4 tsp Dried Basil
1/4 tsp Ground Black Pepper
1/4 tsp Onion Powder
1/8 tsp Celery Seed
2 lbs Tilapia Fillets

Directions:
Preheat broiler.  Grease broiler pan or line with foil.
In a small bowl, mix well all but tilapia.
Arrange fillets in a single layer on broiler pan.  Broil a few inches from heat for 3 minutes.  Flip over and broil a few minutes more.  Remove from oven and spread mixture on fillets.  Return to oven and broil for 2 more minutes or until browned and fish flakes easily with fork.
