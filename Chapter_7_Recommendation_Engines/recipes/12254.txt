Title:
Graham Cracker Crust I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3181898.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups finely ground graham cracker crumbs
1/3 cup white sugar
6 tablespoons butter, melted
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)

Directions:
Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, melted butter or margarine, and cinnamon until well blended . Press mixture into an 8 or 9 inch pie plate.
Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 7 minutes.  Cool.  If recipe calls for unbaked pie shell, just chill for about 1 hour.

Description: 
This goes great with many pies.
