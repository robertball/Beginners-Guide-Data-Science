Title:
Joanne's Shrimp with Garlic (Gambas al Ajillo) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of extra large shrimp (about 25-30) shelled and deveined
1 cup water
1 tsp. coarse sea salt
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 tbsp. butter
1 tsp. plus 1 extra tsp. minced/finely chopped garlic
1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper
2 tbsp. white wine vinegar or white balsamic
1/4 cup chopped italian flat leaf parsley
1 tsp. bread crumbs (optional)

Directions:
In a bowl, stir salt into water until dissolved. Add the shrimp and let it soak for 5 minutes. Drain and pat dry.
Heat oil over med-high heat in skillet until hot. Add  shrimp and sprinkle with the 1 tblsp. minced garlic and red pepper. Cook shrimp for 1 minute and turn. Add the addtl teasp of garlic and butter. Cook 1 additional minute or until opaque. Stir in vinegar and parsley then cover. Remove from heat. Do not overcook. Add a sprinkle of breadcrumbs if desired.

Submitter: 
joannemerc
Description: 
A quick garlicy pan sauteed shrimp that is sooo delicious with just enough heat. All you need is a fresh loaf of italian bread to soak up the juices.
