Title:
breakfast casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. olive oil
½ bag thawed hash brown potatoes
½  cup red bell pepper, finely chopped
¼ cup onion, chopped
¼ cup mushroom, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
8 slices bacon, cut into small pieces
½  cup shredded Cheddar cheese
½ cup Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
½ c. spinach, chiffonade
¼ tsp. salt
freshly ground pepper to taste
6 eggs, cracked & whisked in a bowl
1 Tbsp. milk

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. After the oil has is heated up for 5 minutes, Add potatoes, red bell pepper, onions, mushrooms, and garlic. Cook, stirring occasionally until potatoes are tender and slightly browned.
Use shears to cut bacon into small pieces and fry in a small skillet over medium heat until crisp. Blot well on paper towels and set aside.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.  Spray the bottom of a 9” cake pan with cooking spray.  Scoop the potato mixture into the pan so it is evenly distributed.  Whisk together eggs and milk.  Season with salt and pepper. Pour evenly over the potato mixture.  Sprinkle bacon crumbles evenly over the egg. Sprinkle spinach over bacon evenly.  Sprinkle grated cheeses on top.
Bake for 15-20 minutes in the preheated oven, until the egg is set. (convection oven 12-15)
