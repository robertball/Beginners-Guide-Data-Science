Title:
D's Famous Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
1/4 onion, finely diced
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
1/4 lime, juiced
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons canned sliced green chiles, or to taste
1 tablespoon and 1-1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Place the tomatoes, onion, garlic, lime juice, salt, green chiles, and cilantro in a blender or food processor. Blend on low to desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Kim Casey Nugent Roberds
Description: 
This is a Texas recipe I've developed and refined over many years. It's very fast, easy, and I usually have everything on hand. I often give it as gifts to my family back East.Modified from: D's Famous Salsa
