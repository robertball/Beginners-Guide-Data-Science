Title:
Banana Cookies No Flour or sugar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups mashed bananas
1/3 cup applesauce
2 cups oats
1/4 cup almond milk
1/2 cup raisins ( oppitional)
1teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
mix all ingredients and place on cookie sheet sprayed with pam........bake 350 * 15-20 min

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
I make these all the time and I NEVER feel guilty about them! They are egg-less, dairy-less, flour-less, with no added sugar and they are DELICIOUS
