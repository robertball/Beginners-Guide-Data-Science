Title:
zucchini cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 large eggs
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups grated zucchini
2 3/4 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
1 cup walnuts
1 cup chocolate chips
1/2 cup raisens
1 cup flked coconut

Directions:
preheat oven to 350. In a large bowl, cream together butter & sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs & vanilla. Stir in zucchini. In a medium bowl,wisk flour, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. Add to mixture. Stir in everything else. Drop by tbls onto buttered cookie sheets. Bake until lightly browned, 15-20 minutes.
