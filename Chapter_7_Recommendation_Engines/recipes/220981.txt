Title:
Nancy's Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/826542.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cantaloupe - peeled, seeded, and cubed
2 red apples, thinly sliced
1 cup red grapes
1 (12 ounce) can mandarin oranges in juice
1/4 cup sweetened flaked coconut (optional)

Directions:
Layer about half the cantaloupe in the bottom of a large bowl; top with layers of about half the apple slices, half the grapes, and half the mandarin oranges in juice. Repeat the layering.
Top the salad with coconut to serve.

Submitter: 
Nancy
Description: 
This fruit salad is simple, colorful, refreshing, and a little tangy (due to the mandarin oranges), great to bring to potlucks and picnics. I served it at Easter supper this year to counteract all that candy. Shredded coconut can be sprinkled on top for a sweeter salad.
