Title:
Sassy Mini Shrimp Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/561726.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp Celery flakes
4 oz soft Cream Cheese w/chives
10 Shrimp diced
2 Tbsp Mayonnaise
1/2 tsp Worcestershire
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 Cup of Cocktail Sauce(homemade with ketchup and horseradish)
Box of 15 mini Phyllo cups

Directions:
Mix all together except cocktail sauce. Fill cups and add dollop of sauce on the top.
