Title:
Alice's Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bacon
1 onion, sliced thin
1 c brown sugar
1 t garlic powder
1 t dry mustard
1/2 c white vinegar
1 c tomato sauce
1 (15 oz) can butter beans, drained
1 (15 oz) can great northern beans, drained
1 (15 oz) can kidney beans, drained
1 (28 oz) can Bushes Original Baked Beans

Directions:
Brown bacon until crisp, drain and break into bite-sized pieces. Cut onion into thin slices and fry until tender in 3 tsp. of bacon grease. Add brown sugar, garlic powder, dry mustard, vinegar and tomato sauce to skillet. Cook and simmer for 10 minutes. Drain beans and place into large casserole dish. Pour tomato mixture over beans. Add bacon and stir well. Bake covered for 1 hr.@ 350 degrees.
