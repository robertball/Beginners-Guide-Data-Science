Title:
Pork Chop Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 nice size Pork Chops
12 oz. package of frozen hash browns (unthawed)
1 can cream of celery soup
½ Cup sour cream
½ Cup milk
8 oz. package of grated cheddar cheese,(divided)
1 can of French Fried Onion Rings (divided)

Directions:
Combine liquid in sauce pan and pour over hash browns in a casserole pan.
Put 4 oz. of cheddar cheese and ½ can of the Onion rings over all of the mixture.
Fry Pork Chops good and brown on both sides.Lay on top of the potatoes.
Cover casserole with foil and bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
Take out of the oven and put remaining 4 oz of cheese and ½ can onion rings on top of the pork Chops.
Return to oven and bake and additional 5 minutes, uncovered.
