Title:
Cinnamon Applesauce Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine
2 T dark brown sugar
2 large eggs
3/4 c unsweetened applesauce
1 t vanilla extract
1 c all-purpose flour
1 c white whole wheat flower
2/3 c Splenda
1 t baking soda
1 t baking powder
1 T cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (for topping)
2 t sugar (for topping)

Directions:
Preheat to 400
Spray muffin cups
Cream margarine, brown sugar 3-4 minutes. Beat in eggs, applesauce, and vanilla. Stir together dry ingredients and mix with wet ingredients. Spoon in. Combine sugar and 1/2 t cinnamon and spread over muffins. Bake 20 minutes. Cool 5 minutes.
