Title:
Hot Damn!® Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 cups apple cider
2 cups spiced rum
2 cups cinnamon schnapps (such as DeKuyper® Hot Damn!®)

Directions:
Stir apple cider, spiced rum, and cinnamon schnapps together in a saucepan over medium heat until hot, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
XPSYDED
Description: 
A very festive and tasty hot beverage, this was the hit of my Christmas cookie exchange. Serve with a cinnamon stick.
