Title:
Faye's Oatmeal Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 apples peeled, cored and sliced
¾ cup sugar
1 dash salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
¾ cup oatmeal
¾ cup brown sugar
½ cup flour
½ cup margarine

Directions:
Mix sliced apples, sugar, salt and cinnamon together.  Put in the bottom of a baking dish.
Combine oatmeal, brown sugar, flour and margarine together. Drop by spoonfuls onto apple mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees until golden brown and apples are soft.
