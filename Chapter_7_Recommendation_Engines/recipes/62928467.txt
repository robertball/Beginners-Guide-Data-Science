Title:
Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
4 eggs
½ cup flower
1 tablespoon sugar
Dash of salt

Directions:
Butter skillet. Put ¼ cup crepe batter in skillet. Cook for about 1 minute until top slightly bubbles. Flip crepe. Fill crepe with raspberry jam or other desired filling. Roll up crepe. Put in oven at 150 degrees to keep warm until ready to eat.
