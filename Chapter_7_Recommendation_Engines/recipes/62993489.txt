Title:
Baking Release Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c flour
1/2 c shortening
1/2 c oil

Directions:
Mix all three ingredients together until smooth.  Transfer to a sealable container.  Use a pastry brush or a paper towel to apply to pans before batter is added.  Seal and store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
BigShotsMom
Description: 
Homemade pan treatment for the easy release of baked goods.
