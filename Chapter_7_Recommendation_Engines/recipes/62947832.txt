Title:
Turkey Pumpkin Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
13 oz. whole grain pasta
1 lb ground turkey or chicken
1 onion finely chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 large carrot shredded
1 Roma tomato, finely chopped
1 small zucchini, finely chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
15 oz. pumpkin puree
10 oz. jar pasta sauce

Directions:
Cook pasta according to box
Cook turkey in skillet on medium heat for 7-10 minutes, drain any fat
Return to pan, add onion & garlic, saute until onions are translucent.
Add carrot, tomato & zucchini, saute for about 3-5 minutes, until tender
Add red pepper, pumpkin and pasta sauce.
Cover and simmer for 7-10 minutes until zucchini is soft.
Add pasta and mix well
