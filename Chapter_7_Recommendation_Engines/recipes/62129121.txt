Title:
Yogurt and Cherry Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts
3/4 c. Plain Yogurt
1 T. Honey, I use Orange Blossom
1 stalk celery, diced
1/4 c. dried cherries, roughly chopped
1/8 c. freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/2 small onion minced
1/4 c. hazelnuts, chopped
cracked black pepper
sea salt

Directions:
Poach chicken in a mixture of chicken stock with a few Tablespoons of soy sauce.
Cool the chicken and shred it by hand or with two forks.
combine the minced onion, celery, yogurt, dried cherries, hazelnuts, lemon juice, pepper and salt.
Mix shredded chicken with the yogurt mixture.
Spoon onto your bread of choice and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Andrew Benoit
Description: 
Here's my version of a mayo free chicken salad. The yogurt adds a delicious creamy tangy flavor.
