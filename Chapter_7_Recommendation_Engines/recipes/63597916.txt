Title:
Baked Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1000926.jpg
Ingredients:
12 Uncooked Eggs in the shell
Muffin Tin
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 Degrees
Spray muffin tin with cooking spray
Crack eggs one at a time into a measuring cup with a pour spout
Remove any eggshells or debris from raw eggs, then pour into the individual tin opening
Bake for 13-15 minutes depending on if you want the yolks runny or hard.
This is where YOU will need to experiment with YOUR oven and YOUR preferences.

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
We have Brunch at my house alot. This used to mean standing at the stove cooking eggs to-order. But, no more. I bake, put on a plate and serve.
