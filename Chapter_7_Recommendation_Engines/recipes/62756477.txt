Title:
Fruity Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups AP flour
1 cup sugar
4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1/4 cup butter, melted and cooled
1 tsp vanilla
2-3 cups sliced stone fruit or berries*
TOPPING:
1/2 cup AP flour
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 c cold butter

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, milk and melted butter. Stir into dry ingredients, just until moistened. Pour into a greased 13x9" pan. Cover the surface of the batter with choice of fruit(s).
For topping, combine flour and sugars in a bowl. Cut in cold butter until crumbly, then sprinkle over fruit.
Bake at 375F for 35 minutes, or until golden.
