Title:
Strawberry Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint strawberries, washed and destemmed
2/3 cup sugar
1/3 cup water
1 1 oz. envelope of Knox unflavored gelatine
2 egg whites
1 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:
In food processor blend strawberries and sugar.
Place water in microwave safe glass cup and microwave for 1.25 min or until boiling.
Stir in gelatine, until completely dissolved.
Pour dissolved gelatine into strawberry mixture and blend briefly.
In bowl, beat egg whites with hand mixer until soft peaks form.
Pour strawberry mixture into egg whites and blend with hand mixer until just combined.
In separate bowl, with clean beaters, beat whipping cream on high setting until it thickens, about 5 mins.
Gently, fold whipping cream into strawberry mixture until combined.
Pour into goblets or dessert bowls. Refrigerate until set, about 1 hour.
For presentation, top with a strawberry prior to serving.

Submitter: 
oscar'smom
Description: 
A wonderfully refreshing mousse that's really easy to whip up. Pour into beautiful goblets and serve for family or company.
