Hush Puppies Recipe

Ingredients:
1 cup self-rising corn meal
1/2 cup self-rising flour
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon salt
chopped green onions
1 1/2 cup buttermilk

Directions:

Do not stir again
Drop by small spoonfuls into hot grease
