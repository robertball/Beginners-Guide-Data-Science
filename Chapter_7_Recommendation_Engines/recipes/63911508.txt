Title:
Classic Beef Barley Soup (ww) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½	pound round steak, trimmed and cut into ½ inch pieces
½	cup pearl barley
5	cups water
1	onion, chopped
2	carrots, diced
2	celery ribs, diced
1	teaspoon salt
1/8	teaspoon black pepper
1	cup frozen baby lima beans, thawed
2	cups sliced white cremini mushrooms

Directions:
Combine steak, barley, and water in large saucepan and bring to a boil.  With slotted spoon, skim off and discard any foam.  Add onion, carrots, celery, salt, and pepper.  Reduce heat and simmer, covered, 30 minutes.
Stir lima beans and mushrooms into soup; cook, covered, until meat is tender, about 15 minutes.
Healthy Extra: Increase the number of Celery and carrot stalks to 3 each.
