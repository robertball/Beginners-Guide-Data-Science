Title:
Bow Tie Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/790323.jpg
Ingredients:
SALAD:
1 16oz. package bow tie pasta cooked according to directions and cooled.
1 bunch sliced green onions
1 12oz. bag broccoli florets or cut your own
1/2 chopped sweet red pepper
1/2 chopped green pepper
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 carton sliced grape or cherry tomatoes
DRESSING:
1/4 cup sugar
1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
2 cups "Miracle Whip" salad dressing

Directions:
In large bowl, combine pasta with onions, broccoli, peppers, celery and tomtatoes.
In separate medium bowl, mix by hand sugar, cheese, salt, basil and salad dressing. Add this dressing to salad and blend until pasta and other ingredients are coated.
Chill for at least 3 hours (or overnight) before serving.
