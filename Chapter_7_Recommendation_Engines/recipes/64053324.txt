Title:
Chocolate Butter Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3.5 cups/437.5g AP flour
1/2 cup/50g cocoa powder (regular or Dutch processed)
2 teaspoons instant espresso powder (optional)
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups whole or 2% milk
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1.5 cups/3 sticks unsalted butter
2 3/4 cups sugar
6 large eggs
12 ounces bittersweet or semisweet chocolate finely chopped, melted and cooled to tepid or 100F

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Grease 3 9-inch pans or line with parchment.
In medium bowl sift together flour, espresso powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.  Stir together and set aside.
Add extract to milk and set aside.
Beat butter in stand mixer an low speed until soft.
Add sugar at steady stream at side of the bowl.
Increase speed to medium and beat for 2 minutes or until mixture is light yellow in color, aerated and fluffy.  Then scrape bowl down.
With mixer on low, add the eggs one at a time and beat for 20 seconds after each addition.
After adding eggs, increase mixer speed to medium-high and beat mixture about 1-2 minutes.  The mixture will become fluffy and aerated.
Add melted and cooled chocolate by tempering.....tempering= add 1 tablespoon of butter sugar egg mixture to tepid melted chocolate and mix thoroughly.  Then stir in 2 more tablespoons 1 tablespoon at a time.  Then add about 1/4 cup more of butter sugar egg mixture to chocolate mixture before putting it all into the main mixing bowl.
Mix all ingredients on low speed until combined and creamy.
With mixer on low, add flour mixture in 3 equal portions alternating with milk mixture in 2 equal portions beginning and ending with flour.  After last flour addition beat on low for 30 seconds.
Scrape the bowl and fold the batter.
Pour into prepared pans.
Bake 25-40 minutes or until top feels firm and gives slightly..will not spring back.
Cool 10-15 minutes then unmold.
