Title:
Fried Butternut squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Butternut squash
onions
red pepper
oil
chopped tomatoes canned and drained.

Directions:
Cut Squash into large pieces.  Put in boiling water for 2-3 min.  Take out, let cool.. Peel and remove seeds.  cut in to cubes.  Put oil in frying pan add onions, red pepper, tomatoes and squash.  Cook until softened but not too soft.
