Title:
Dad's Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1-1/2 cups bran
3 Tbsp oil
2 Tbsp brown sugar
2 Tbsp molasses
1 egg
1-1/2 cups buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Grease muffin cups.
In large bowl, mix flour, baking soda and salt.  Stir in bran and set aside.
In another bowl, beat oil, sugar, and molasses.  Add egg and buttermilk and mix well.  Mix into dry ingredients.
Fill muffin cups 3/4 full.  Bake for 15-20 minutes.  Can add raisins if desired.
