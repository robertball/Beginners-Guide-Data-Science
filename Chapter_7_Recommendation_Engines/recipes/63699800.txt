Title:
Crunchy Bell Pepper & Cucumber Slaw (Flat Belly Diet - Gluten Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2Tbsp creamy natural peanut butter
2Tbsp rice vinegar
1Tbsp GF mirin
1Tbsp grated peeled ginger
1Tbsp soy sauce
1 red bell pepper, cut into thin strips
1/2 small seeded cucumber, cut into thin strips
1C bean sprouts
4 scallions thinly sliced
1/4C unsalded dry-roasted peanuts chopped

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, vinegar, mirin, ginger and soy sauce until smoothe.
Add the bell pepper, cucumber, bean sprouts, scallions, and peanuts and toss until well coated.
