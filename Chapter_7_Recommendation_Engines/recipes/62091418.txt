Title:
Healthy Whole Grain Chocolate Chip Meusli Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup chocolate chips
1 cup Muesli

Directions:
Mix together the flour, soda and salt and set aside.
In medium bowl, beat butter, brown sugar and applesauce.
Beat in egg.
Beat in flout mixture.
Stir in Muesli and Chocolate Chips.
Drop by teaspoonfuls on baking sheet lightly sprayed with Pam.
Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Mindy Sauve
Description: 
A healthier version made with whole grains instead of refined flour.  There's still some sugar and some fat - but far less than in a traditional cookie.  These are moist and nutty with just a hint of chocolate.  You won't feel nearly as guilty eating these.
