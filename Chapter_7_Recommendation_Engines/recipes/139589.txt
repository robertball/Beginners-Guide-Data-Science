Title:
Russian Tea Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1061009.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup instant tea powder
2 cups instant orange drink mix
1 (3 ounce) package unsweetened lemonade powder
2 cups SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine instant tea powder, orange drink mix, lemonade powder, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, cinnamon and cloves. Mix thoroughly. Store in a sealed jar.
To use, mix 3 to 4 teaspoons of mix with 1 cup hot or cold water. Adjust to taste.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Enjoy this tangy beverage on a cold winter's morning when you want something tasty and hot, but don't want coffee! It makes a great gift from your kitchen, too.
