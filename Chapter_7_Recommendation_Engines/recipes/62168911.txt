Title:
Saucy Sirloin Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. sirloin tips
2 tb. flour
2 tb. butter
1/4 c. chopped green onions
1 c. mushrooms, sliced
1 can condensed beef consomme
1 tsp. Worchestershire sauce

Directions:
Cut meat into thin strips.
Sprinkle meat with flour.
In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium-high heat.
Add the meat and saute until well-browned.
Add the onions and mushrooms and saute for two minutes.
Stir in the consomme and Worchestershire sauce.
Simmer 15 minutes or until sauce thickens slightly and meat is tender.
Serve over rice or buttered noodles.

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick and classy dinner of sirloin tips and mushrooms in a simple gravy, perfect for company! Serve over noodles or rice.
