Title:
Chinese Noodles with Broccoli in Peanut Sauce Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack of any Miracle Noodle Pasta
1 1/2 pounds broccoli florets
1 large red bell pepper, sliced
1/2 cup reduced fat peanut butter
1/2 cup water, divided
1 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp reduced-sodium soy sauce, divided
2 tbsp sesame oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp fresh minced ginger
1/2 cup green onion, chopped

Directions:
In a colander drain and rinse Miracle Noodles very well. Pat dry withat few paper towels.  Set aside.  Whisk peanut butter, 1/4 cup water, brown sugar, 1 tablespoon  soy sauce and vinegar in a medium bowl until smooth, set aside.  Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium heat.  Add broccoli and cook, stirring frequently, until begining to soften and brown in spots, about 6 minutes.
Add the remaining 1/4 cup wdater and 1 tablespoon soy sauce to the pan along with bell pepper, garlic, ginger, and noodles.  Cook, stirring frequently, until the pepper has softened and the liquid has evaporated, 4 to 6 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in the reserved peanut sauce and toss to coat. Optional: Garnish with fresh chopped green onions.
