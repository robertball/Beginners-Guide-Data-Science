Title:
Summer Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1326312.jpg
Ingredients:
3 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers spiced rum
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers banana liqueur
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers grenadine syrup
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers orange juice
3 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers pina colada mix

Directions:
In a blender combine spiced rum, banana liqueur, grenadine orange juice and pina colada mix. Add ice and blend to a frothy consistency.

Submitter: 
SUZY JOHNSON
Description: 
Frothy mixed drink that will cool you off out by the beach or at any summertime activity.
