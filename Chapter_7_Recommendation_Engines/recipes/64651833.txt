Title:
Skinny Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 lb boneless chicken breast, cubed
- Flour
- 2 eggs, beaten
- Panko breadcrumbs
- juice of 1 orange
- 1/3 c soy sauce
- 1/4 c honey
- 1 tbsp corn starch
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp ginger

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400. Grease baking sheet
2. Bread the chicken cubes by dredging in flour, then egg, then panko and place on baking sheet.
3. Bake for 20 minutes
4. During baking, combine juice of orange, soy sauce, honey, garlic, corn starch, and ginger in a saucepan on medium. Whisk until combined. Cook 5 minutes, whisking regularly, until sauce begins to thicken.
5. In a large bowl, coat cooked chicken in orange sauce
6. Serve over rice, top with sesame seeds or orange zest if desired
