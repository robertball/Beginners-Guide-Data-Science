Title:
Stir-fried sausage and peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/457332.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp diced ginger root
1/2 tbsp sambal oelek chili paste
7-10 oz. extra firm tofu, cubed
1 jalapeno, diced
1 yellow onion, sliced
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1 green bell pepper, sliced
1/2 cup baby bella mushrooms, roughly sliced
2 tbsp vegetable oil
2 tbsp low salt soy sauce
1 tbsp rice wine
1/2 tbsp Tabasco sweet and spicy sauce
1 tbsp Silver Sprint Picante Pepper mustard or other similar spicy mustard.
2 Aidells Habanero and Green Chili chicken sausages  or similar
4 hoagie or sub rolls
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Add 2 tbsp vegetable oil to a large skillet on medium-high heat.  Add cubed tofu and fry, tossing every few minutes.  Salt and pepper tofu to taste.  After the tofu has browned sufficiently (about 8-10 min) remove and set aside.  Add last tbsp oil to skillet.
Add diced ginger root and diced jalapenos.  After 1 minute, add sliced onions as well as green and red peppers.  Stir fry until browned (about 6-8 minutes), then add mushrooms and cubed sausage.  Stir fry for an additional 4-5 min.  Lower heat and mix in tofu.  Stir in liquid ingredients including rice wine, soy sauce, tabasco sauce, mustard and chili paste.  Head an additional two minutes.  Serve on toasted hoagie rolls.

Submitter: 
Sachin K.
Description: 
This East meets West recipe is a little odd at first, but that first spicy bite will assuage any fears right away!
This dish can be served on rolls or open-faced hamburger buns!
