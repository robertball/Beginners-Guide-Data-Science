Title:
Blender Hollandaise Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 egg yolks
2 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 tsp. salt
pinch cayenne pepper
1/4 lb. butter

Directions:
In the container of a blender, combine the egg yolks, lemon juice, salt and cayenne pepper. Cover, and blend for about 5 seconds.
Heat butter in sauce pan until bubbling but not yet browning. Turn blender on low and gradually pour in butter. Continue blending a moment until sauce has thickened. Keep in a pan of warm water until served.

Submitter: 
James Speer
Description: 
The easiest way to get perfect Hollandaise is to use a blender. This recipe has all the same ingredients as the classic, but no double boiler and no chance of the sauce separating. I love the lemony flavor on fresh steamed asparagus!Modified from: Blender Hollandaise Sauce
