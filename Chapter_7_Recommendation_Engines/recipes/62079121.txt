Title:
Strawberry Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500 g cleaned strawberries
200 g heavy cream
150 g sour cream
200 g sugar

Directions:
Chop the strawberries into small pieces.
Beat creams and sugar together.
Mix the two.
Refregirate.

Submitter: 
NP
Description: 
You can use theis as a filling for cakes, topping for frensh toast, pancakes or even on its own.
