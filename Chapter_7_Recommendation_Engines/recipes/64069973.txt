Title:
Big Ray's Gyro Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1132311.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package (10 ounces) frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 cup dry bread crumbs
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/4 cup grated Romano cheese
2 tsp dried oregano
1-1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 pounds ground beef, turkey and/or lamb
8 ozs Greek yogurt
1 small cucumber, peeled, seeded and minced
2 crushed garlic cloves
pinch salt
1 1/2 tsp olive oil1 tsp red wine vinegar
1/4 tsp mint

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°. In a large bowl, combine the first eight ingredients. Crumble meat over mixture and mix well. Shape into a loaf and place in a greased 11x7-in. baking dish.
Bake, uncovered, 55-60 minutes or until no pink remains and a thermometer reads 160°. Let stand 15 minutes before slicing.
While meatloaf if cooking, in a medium bowl mix the remaining ingredients for Tzatziki sauce. Serve with pita/hummus and Tzatziki sauce on the side.
