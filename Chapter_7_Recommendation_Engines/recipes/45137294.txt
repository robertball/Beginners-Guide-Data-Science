Title:
Army SOS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 cubes beef bouillon
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 pinch ground black pepper
2 1/4 cups milk
1-1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon onion salt
1 diced onion
2 teaspoons garlic salt
1 heaping tablespoon minced garlic

Directions:
Brown beef in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add minced garlic and diced onion to beef during the last 20 seconds of browning.
Combine flour, bouillon, onion salt, garlic salt & salt and pepper. Stir into skillet and saute all together for about 5 minutes or until flour is absorbed.
Mix milk and Worcestershire sauce. Pour mixture into skillet with beef. Bring all to a simmer, stirring constantly. Cook until thickened, about 5 to 10 minutes. Serve hot!
