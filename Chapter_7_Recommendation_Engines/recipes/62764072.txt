Title:
Audrey's Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef browned and drained
1 lb. bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 large onion, diced
1/2 Cup ketchup
1/2 Cup barbecue sauce
1 tsp. salt
4 T. mustard
4 T. molasses
1 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. black pepper
2 16 oz. cans kidney beans *drained
2 16 oz. cans pork and beans *UNdrained
2 16 oz. cans butter beans *drained

Directions:
Mix together all of the ingredients, except the beans.  When the mixture is well incorporated, gently stir in the bean.  Spray a dutch oven with non-stick spray and pour the bean mixture into the pot.  Bake for 1 hour, uncovered, at 350 degrees.  Stir half-way through cooking.
