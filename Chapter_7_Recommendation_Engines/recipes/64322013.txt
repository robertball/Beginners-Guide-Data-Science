Title:
Broiled Sirloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounce(s) beef, sirloin

Directions:
Place steak on a lined baking sheet. Place in oven 2-4 inches below the burner. Broil until steak reaches desired doneness. Let stand for 5 minutes prior to serving.
