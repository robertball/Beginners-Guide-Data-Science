Title:
Seafood Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871664.jpg
Ingredients:
* 1 lb Cooked Shrimp, peeled and deveined
* 4 oz Cream Cheese
* 2 tsp Minced Garlic
* 2 tsp Cilantro, Fresh
* 2 tsp Lime Juice
* 10 oz Mild Green Chile Enchilada Sauce
* 6 soft taco tortillas

Directions:
Chop Shrimp.
Combine shrimp, cream cheese, garlic, cilantro & lime juice in skillet. Cook over medium heat until blended and heated through.
Spread into tortillas. Place in oven safe pan.
Pour enchilada sauce over tortillas.
Cook @350 for 20 minutes. Until heated through.

Submitter: 
J Scholfield
Description: 
This recipe came about the night I realized I forgot to buy chicken for the Enchilada recipe on the back of the El Paso Green Chile Enchilada Sauce can. Luckily, I had some shrimp in the freezer.
