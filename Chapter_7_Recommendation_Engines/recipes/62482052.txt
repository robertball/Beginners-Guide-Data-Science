Title:
Zesty Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/539650.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 cup frozen whole kernel corn, thawed
1 white onion diced
1 green bell pepper chopped
1/2 red bell pepper chopped
2 jalapeno peppers diced (leave seeds for extra heat)
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
1 cup zesty Italian-style salad dressing
Juice of 1 lime
3 garlic cloves minced
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon mexican oregano (or regular oregano)
1/4 teaspoon cracked pepper
- optional 1 bunch cilantro diced-

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine black beans, corn, onion, garlic, green & red bell pepper, jalapeno peppers, cilantro (optional) and tomatoes. Add zesty Italian-style salad dressing and lime juice.  Season with salt, oregano and cracked pepper; mix well. Cover, and refrigerate overnight to blend flavors.
