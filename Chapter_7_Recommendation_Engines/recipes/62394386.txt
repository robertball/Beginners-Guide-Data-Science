Title:
Orzo Caprese salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# orzo
one of each bell peppers, red, green, yellow, chopped fine
1/2 red onion, chopped fine
cherry tomatoes,cut in half
sun dried tomatoes in oil
bocchonchini of mozzarella-as much as you like
Vinagrette
3 parts grape seed oil
1 part good balsamic vinegar
little garlic
shallots
little fresh lemon juice
little Italian seasoning
salt and pepper
Before serving add a chiffanade of basil to each serving and top with toasted pine nuts.  To toast pine nuts, just put them in a saute pan for a few minutes to release their oils.

Directions:
Cook oro and put into fridge until cool. Wash the orzo with cold water to get rid of the excess starch.
Then put in a large serving bowl. Add peppers, red onion, cherry tomatoes, sun dried tomatatoes and mozzarella.
Mix thoroughly. Then add vinagrette and mix thoroughly again.
Spoon in serving bowls or plates and top with fresh chiffanade of basil and a few toasted pine nuts

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Orzo pasta, peppers, cherry, as well as sun-dried tomatoes, and fresh mozzarella with vinagrette dressing
