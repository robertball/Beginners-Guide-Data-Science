Title:
Mom's Meatballs and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef*
1 Egg
3 cups Rice Krispies
1 cup chopped onions
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 can Cream of Chicken or Cream of Mushroom soup
1 cup milk
2 Cups Jasmine rice
*You can also use 1/2lb ground turkey with 1/2lb ground beef

Directions:
Combine ground beef, egg, Rice Krispies, onions, salt, & pepper in a bowl.  Mix well.  Roll mixture into balls about 1"-1 1/2" diameter.
Cook balls over medium heat until they are cooked through and well browned on all sides.
Remove meatballs from heat and place on a paper towel to drain some of the grease.  Drain grease from pan and add cream of chicken soup and milk.  Heat for a moment, then place meatballs back into the pan in the soup mixture.  Cook covered for about 15 minutes turning meatballs half way through.  Periodically spoon soup mixture over the meatballs.
Serve meatballs and sauce over rice and enjoy!
