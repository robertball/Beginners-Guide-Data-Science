Title:
Super Easy Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken meat
8 cubes  chicken bouillon (small cubes 1cube=1cup)
8 cups water
3 stalks celery, sliced
2 carrots, peeled and sliced
1 bay leaf
3-4 cloves of garlic
salt and pepper to taste
2 cups thin egg noodles

Directions:
Measure water into a large pot, and add chicken, chicken bouillon, celery, carrots, bay leaf, garlic, and salt and pepper.  Boil until chicken is fully cooked.
Remove chicken from the broth.  Chop into small pieces, and return meat to the pot.
Add egg noodles, and cook until tender.

Submitter: 
Jessica
Description: 
Boneless, skinless chicken breast cooked in chicken bouillon is cut into bite-sized pieces and returned to the broth with thin egg noodles in this easy-to-prepare soup.Modified from: Super Easy Chicken Noodle Soup
