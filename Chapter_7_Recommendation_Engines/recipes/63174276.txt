Title:
Mexican shrimp cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups clamato juice
1 tbsp worchestershire sauce
1 tbsp bold and spicy A1 sauce
2 tbsp cholula chili garlic hot sauce
2 lemons juiced
1 avocado diced
1 cucumber diced
3 roma tomatoes diced
1/2 small yellow or red onion diced
1/4 cup diced fresh cilantro
1 lb cooked, frozen shrimp thawed and chopped

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and serve in margarita glasses
