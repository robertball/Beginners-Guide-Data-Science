Title:
Butternut Squash Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 butternut squash
1 lb. rotini pasta
2 T butter
2 T flour
1/2 t mustard
2 cups milk
6 oz. white cheddar cheese
6 oz. cheddar cheese
seasoned dry breadcrumbs

Directions:
Cut butternut squash in half.  Roast at 400 degrees for 40-50 minutes or until soft.  Scoop from shell and set aside.
Cook pasta, drain and set aside.
Melt butter in a medium saucepan.  When the butter stops foaming, whisk in flour and mustard.  Whisk for 1 minute, then gradually add milk.
Whisk for 5-7 minutes until the milk resembles the texture of heavy cream.  Remove from heat and stir in squash and cheese.  When cheese is melted, stir in pasta.
Pour mixture into casserole dish.  Top with breadcrumbs to taste.  Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes.
