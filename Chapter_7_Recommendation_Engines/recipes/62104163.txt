Title:
Tuna and Spinach Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
400 g English spinach
190 g canned tuna in spring water
1/2 tsp salt
3 tbsp thickened cream
1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
3 tbsp finely chopped garlic
1 cube beef stock
2 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
3 medium potatoes
1 tbsp low-fat sour cream
30 g cheddar cheese, sliced thinly.
extra grated cheese/parmesan

Directions:
Preheat oven to 160 c
Boil potatoes until tender, mash without peeling the skin.
Mix mashed potatoes with sour cream, salt, and beef stock.
Boil spinach (leaves only), set aside.
Mix tuna with onion, garlic, salt, pepper, thickened cream.
Prepare a 20 cm diameter casserole/pyrex.
Place half of the mashed potato in the bottom until covering the base of the pyrex completely, thinly.
Place thinly sliced cheddar cheese over the mashed potato.
Place tuna over the cheese, then spinach, then the rest of the mashed potato to cover the tuna completely.
Garnish with grated cheese on top.
Bake for about 20-25 minutes or until the potato and cheese surface looks golden brown.
