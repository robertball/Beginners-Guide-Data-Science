Title:
Big Daddy's Shrimp Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound salad shrimp
2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
1/2 red onion, diced
1/4 cup minced cilantro
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
Stir the salad shrimp, tomatoes, onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, pepper, and garlic together in a large glass bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until the flavors combine, at least 1 hour. Serve cold.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
Salad shrimp, tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and lime juice combine for an tasty version of salsa for dipping your tortilla chips.Modified from: Shrimp Salsa
