Title:
Spinach Mushroom Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pie crust (Pet Ritz always works well)
4 eggs
5 oz fresh (or 1/2 pkg frozen) spinach
8 oz. fresh sliced mushrooms
4 oz. grated swiss, divided evenly
2 oz. feta, crumbled
1/2 medium yellow onion, sliced
3/4 cup milk
1 tbsp. fresh parsley
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp garlic, minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
In a small bowl, whisk eggs, milk and seasonings together.
Toss spinach, mushrooms, onions and feta together in a large bowl (veggies can be layered in dish if you prefer).
Line pie crust with vegetable mixture.
Sprinkle half of the Swiss cheese over the top.
Pour egg mixture over the cheese, swirling bowl to evenly spread seasonings.
Top quiche with the remaining Swiss cheese.
Bake on a cookie sheet for 45-50 minutes.
Cool for 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
EMBRYOCONCEPTS
Description: 
This moist vegetarian quiche is perfect for every meal.  The quiche was originally a Quiche Lorraine, but has since been transformed.  Feel free to add sausage if you choose.  I usually make 2 large pies, or several small ones all at one time, freezing whatever is left over.
