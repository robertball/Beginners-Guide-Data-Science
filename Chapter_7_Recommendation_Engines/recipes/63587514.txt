Title:
Grandma Dalley's Honey Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. Old Fashioned Oats
2 c. coarsely chopped almonds
1/2 c. butter
3/4 c. honey
2 tsp. ground cinnamon
dash salt
1 c, golden raisins

Directions:
Combine oats and nuts. Mix and set aside. Bring butter, honey, cinnamon and salt to boil. Boil 1 minute. Pour honey mixture over the oats and nuts. Toss to blend. Spread on lightly greased cookie sheet. Bake 15 to 20 minutes @ 350Â° (stirring every 5 minutes) until lightly browned. Add raisins the last few minutes. Cool, crumble. Store airtight in a covered container up to 2 weeks.

Submitter: 
Miriam
Description:
