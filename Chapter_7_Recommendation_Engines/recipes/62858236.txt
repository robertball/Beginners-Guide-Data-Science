Title:
Healthier Ez Turkey Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/684724.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 lb Lean Ground Turkey
Chopped Green Salad mix
1/2 packet of taco seasoning mix
shredded cheese
Salsa
Sour cream
8 taco shells

Directions:
Follow the directions on the taco seasoning packet, using turkey instead of beef. While the turkey cooks, warm the taco shells.  Assemble the tacos and enjoy.

Submitter: 
messyjessi, RD
Description: 
Just ground turkey, taco mix, and whatever you like on your tacos.  Lean ground turkey has a better texture than lean ground beef so my family prefers it.
