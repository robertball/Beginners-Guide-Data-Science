Title:
Caulifolwer Au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cauliflower florets
melted butter
flour
salt and pepper
milk
Browned Applewood Smoked Bacon
shredded White Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Layer cauliflower in a buttered casserole.
Combine butter and flour in saucepan for a roux.Add milk and cook till thickened.Add bacon bits. Pour over Cauliflower florets and top with Cheddar cheese. Bake @ 375 for approx 20-30 mins till cauliflower tender and cheese browned. Allow to set 10 mins before serving.
