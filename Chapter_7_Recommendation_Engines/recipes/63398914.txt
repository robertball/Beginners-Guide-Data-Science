Title:
Flour Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 c flour
1/2 c shortening
1 t salt
1 1/2 to 2 c hot water

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in bowl. ; Put out on floured board when a ball is formed.  Knead until pliable.  Pinch off pieces about 2" in diameter and rol out to 6-8 ".  Cook in a dry cast iron skillet or on a griddle.  Cook on each side until bubbles that form are browned.  Push down large bubbles when turning for the last time with a cloth.
