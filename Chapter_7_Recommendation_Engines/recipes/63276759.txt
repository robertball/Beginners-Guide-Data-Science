Title:
** My Apple Rhubarb Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fruit Filling:
4 cups diced rhubarb
3 med. apples; cored, peeled & sliced
1 cup white sugar
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
Topping:
1 cup packed light brown sugar
1 cup old-fashioned oatmeal
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. salt
1/2 cup butter, room temp.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Lightly grease a 9x13" pan, or 1 1/2 qt. shallow baking dish.
In a large mixing bowl combine rhubarb, apple, white sugar, and 3 tablespoons flour. Stir well and spread evenly into baking dish. Set aside.
In a med. mixing bowl combine brown sugar, oats, salt, and 1 1/2 cups flour. Stir well then cut in butter until mixture is crumbly. Sprinkle mixture over fruit layer.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, until bubbly & topping is browned. Serve warm or cold.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Apples give additional flavor to the rhubarb. Serve with vanilla ice cream.
