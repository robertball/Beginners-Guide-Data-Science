Title:
Bacon Home Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. Bacon
4 medium potatoes, diced
1 red or green bell pepper, diced
1 small onion, diced
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook bacon in skillet and set aside.  Pour out all but 2 tbsps. bacon drippings.  Add potatoes, peppers and onions to pan.  Add salt and pepper.  Using back of spatula, press potatoes evenly into bottom of pan.  Cook 15-20 minutes, stirring every 3-4 minutes until potatoes are golden brown and soft.  After each stir, press potatoes into bottom of pan with back of spatula.  Crumble bacon and add to potatoes during last minute of cooking.

Description: 
Diced potatoes, peppers and onions cooked in bacon drippings.
