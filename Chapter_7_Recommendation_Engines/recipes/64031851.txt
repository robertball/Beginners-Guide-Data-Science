Title:
Peanut butter toffee cheesecake brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120457.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box brownie mix & needed ingredients
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
8oz softened cream cheese
1 can of sweetened condensed milk
8oz toffee bits
1 cup chocolate chips (milk or semisweet)
3 tablespoons whipping/heavy cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350, grease a 13x9 pan
mix brownies and spread in pan
Beat cream cheese until fluffy. Add milk and peanut butter and stir until creamy. Stir in 1 cup of toffee bits. Spread evenly over brownie batter.
Bake 30-40 minutes or until cheesecake layer is golden
Cool for 30 minutes
Refrigerate 40 minutes
Microwave chocolate and cream 40-60 seconds and stir until melted and smooth. Spread over cheesecake and sprinkle with remaining toffee bits.
Refrigerate until cold and serve.
