Title:
Seafood Piccata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz packages Trader Joe's Lemon Pepper Papparadelle pasta
6 tablespoons olive oil
2 cups fresh baby bella mushrooms, sliced
3/4 cup minced green onions
2 tablespoons minced garlic
1 pound medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
1 pound bay scallops
1/2 pound crabmeat
2 cups dry white wine
6 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Saute mushrooms, green onions, until tender. Stir in garlic, wine, lemon juice, and butter; cook until heated through.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil, add pasta, and cook for 8 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
Add in shrimp, scallops, and crabmeat to veggie/wine mixture. Cook until shrimp is pink.
Toss pasta with seafood sauce and parsley.
