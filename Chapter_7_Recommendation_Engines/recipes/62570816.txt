Title:
Chunky Monkey Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/547501.jpg
Ingredients:
3 to 4 very ripe bananas, mashed (about 2 cups)
2 cups quick oats
2 cups sugar
1 3/4 cups flour
1/2 cup cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1 1/4 cups melted butter
1 cup chopped walnuts, or toasted slivered almonds
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9 x 13 inch baking pan.
In a large bowl, combine oats, sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
In a separate bowl, stir together mashed banana, eggs and butter.
Pour wet ingredients into dry, add nuts and chocolate chips, and mix until combined.
Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 40 to 50 minutes or until top springs back when gently pressed.
Allow to cool completely before cutting.
