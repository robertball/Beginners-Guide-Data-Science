Title:
Watermelon Salad on a Stick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1328412.jpg
Ingredients:
8 (1 inch) cubes watermelon
8 (1/2 inch) cubes feta cheese
8 fresh mint leaves
8 bamboo toothpicks

Directions:
Thread 1 watermelon cube, 1 feta cheese cube, and 1 mint leaf onto a toothpick. Repeat with remaining toothpicks.
