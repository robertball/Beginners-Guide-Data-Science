Title:
Scrumptious Tex-Mex Enchilada Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
Salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon cumin
2 teaspoons chili powder
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 (4 oz) can diced green chilies
1/2 cup water
1 cup oil for frying
8 corn tortillas
2 cups shredded Cheddar Jack cheese
1 (10 oz) can enchilada sauce
2 tablespoons chopped green onions (optional)

Directions:
1.1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
2.2. In a large skillet, season ground beef with salt and pepper and cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain fat.
3.3. Season meat with cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, and paprika. Add minced garlic, green chilies, and water and simmer 5 minutes.
4.4. In a small skillet, heat oil. Dip corn tortillas one at a time in hot oil until soft (about 3 seconds), making sure the whole tortilla is covered. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
5.5. Arrange 4 corn tortillas on the bottom of a greased 9X9 inch baking pan.
6.6. Scoop one half of the ground beef mixture on top.
7.7. Spoon half of the can of enchilada sauce over the beef.
8.8. Sprinkle cheddar cheese on top.
9.9. Arrange the 4 remaining corn tortillas, meat, and enchilada sauce (reserving 2 tablespoons of sauce)on top of bottom layer.
10.10. Cover with foil and place in preheated oven for 20 minutes
11.11. Remove from oven, moisten edges with remaining enchilada sauce, and sprinkle with remaining cheese and green onions; bake uncovered for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and lightly browned.
12.12. If desired, serve with sour cream, guacamole, and salsa.
