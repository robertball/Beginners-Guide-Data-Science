Title:
French Dip Chibata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. deli sliced roast beef thin
12 cups water
14 bullion cubes
chibata bread or hoagies

Directions:
Bring water and bullion to a boil. Add roast beef in a slice at a time to seperate. When all meat is added give it a stir, turn off heat and serve. Cut bread in half, fill with meat, fill a bowl with broth and dip the sandwich in the juice.

Submitter: 
Wendy Meyer
Description: 
Firm Chibata bread topped with piled of sliced roast beef and dipped in a beef broth. Yum
