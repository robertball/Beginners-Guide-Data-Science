Title:
Ham and Cheese Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/402023.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 ounces sliced ham, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1 egg
1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the first six ingredients; set aside.  Divide crescent dough into 24 portions.  Press into greased miniature muffin cups.
Spoon 1 tablespoon ham mixture into each cup.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 13-15 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Marvin Buffington
Description: 
'These tasty little bites go over great with kids,' writes Mrs. Marvin Buffington of Burlington, Iowa. 'They're also delicious with soups or as party appetizers.'
