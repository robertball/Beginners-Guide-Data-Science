Title:
Red Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1015864.jpg
Ingredients:
- Bag of Organic Red Quinoa
- 1 can red kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 4 garlic cloves, chopped fine
- 8oz Braggs Cider Vinegar
- 1oz olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste
** Add a green bell pepper and red onion for additional veggies.

Directions:
Make quinoa according to bag directions.
Add beans, pepper and garlic cloves to a large bowl.
Add quinoa once cooked and mix.
Add vinegar, olive oil and salt/pepper and mix well.
Put in refrigerator to chill. Tastes best cold.

Description: 
Gluten free, cold quinoa salad packed with antioxidant benefits.
