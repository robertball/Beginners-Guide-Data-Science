Title:
Penne with Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package penne pasta
4 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup chopped red onion
1 tablespoon chopped garlic
1/4 cup white wine
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
1 bunch asparagus
1 Tbsp. chopped basil
1 tsp. red pepper flakes
1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in onion and garlic, and cook until onion is tender. Mix in wine, tomatoes, basil and red pepper and continue cooking 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add asparagus and cook for another 5 minutes, or until al dente.
Mix shrimp into the skillet, and cook 5 minutes, or until opaque. Toss with pasta and top with Parmesan cheese to serve.
