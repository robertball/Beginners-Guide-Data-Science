Title:
Chocolate Snap Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1/2 c. butter
1 egg
2 1/4 c. flour
1/4 c. milk
1 tsp. baking powder
3 tbsp. dark cocoa powder

Directions:
- Cream butter and sugar
- Add well beaten egg, flour and baking powder
- Add milk and cocoa powder
- Mix well.
- Drop by spoonfuls on greased cookie sheet
- Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes
- Cool on rack immediately.

Submitter: 
MrMomz
Description: 
A basic chocolate cookie recipe, easy and adaptable.
These are similar to the Nabisco Chocolate Snaps, now discontinued and a fading memory of American edibles.
