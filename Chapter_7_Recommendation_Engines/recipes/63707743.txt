Title:
Biscuits & Sausage Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bulk Sausage
7 Tbs All-Purpose Flour
7 Tbs Butter
1 Tsp Salt
1/2 Tsp Pepper
3 1/2 cups milk
8 Bisuits

Directions:
In skillet over medium heat , cook sausage until no longer pink, drain if necessary. Stir in flour, butter, salt and pepper; cook and stir until butter is melted. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly. Bring to a boil; boil and stir for 2 minutes, serve over biscuits.
