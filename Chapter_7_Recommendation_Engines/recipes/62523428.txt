Title:
Molten Chocolate Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (1-ounce) squares semi sweet chocolate
1/2 cup butter
1 cup powdered sugar
2 eggs
2 egg yolks
6 Tbs flour
Optional garnishes:
Powdered sugar
Whipped cream or whipped topping
Raspberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.  Butter four 3/4 cup custard cups, place on abaking sheet.
Microwave chocolate and butter in large bowl on high for 1 minute, or until butter is melted.  Stir with wire whisk until chocolate is melted.  Stir in the 1 cup of powdered sugar until well blended.  Blend in eggs and egg yolks.  Stir in flour.  Divide batter among prepared custard cups.
Bake in preheated oven for 13-14 minutes, or until sides are firm but centers are soft.
Let stand 1 minunte, then run a knife around the sides to release.  Invert a dessert plate over the custard cup, then invert the whole thing and remove the custard cup.
If desired, dust lightly with powdered sugar, then garnish with whipped cream or whipped topping and raspberries.

Submitter: 
Keri
Description: 
Little chocolate cakes with liquid centers.  These must be served immediately after baking, so plan accordingly.
