Title:
Cranberry Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/917691.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c. flour
1 Tbs. baking powder
2 c. sugar
1 c. vegetable oil
4 eggs
2/3 c. orange juice
1/2 tsp. salt
2 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 c. cranberries
zest of one orange

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.  Grease a large bundt pan with baking spray.  Using an electric mixer, combine all ingredients except for cranberries and orange zest until thoroughly incorporated.  Stir in berries and zest.  Pour into prepared bundt pan.  Begin checking for doneness after 60 minutes.  Remove from oven, and turn cake out onto a cooling rack.  If cake resists, wait 15 minutes and try again.  Cool for 1 hour.  Dust with powdered sugar if desired.
