Title:
Marge's Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1125756.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
12 fluid ounces tequila
1 cup ice cubes, or as needed
6 fluid ounces triple sec
2 teaspoons lemon juice

Directions:
Blend lemonade, tequila, ice cubes, triple sec, and lemon juice together in a blender until desired consistency is reached.

Submitter: 
goldenCO
Description: 
This is my husband's ex-wife's favorite margarita! Serve in salt rimmed glasses and garnish with lemon or lime slices, as desired.
