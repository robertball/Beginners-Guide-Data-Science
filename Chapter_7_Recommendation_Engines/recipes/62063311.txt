Title:
Squash Side Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small zucchini, italian squash
2 small yellow squash
1 small red bell pepper
3 cloves garlice
2 teaspoons cornstarch
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup vegetable broth
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 teaspoon butter

Directions:
Cut zuchini and squash diagonally 1/4 inch thick.  Cut red bell pepper 1/4 inch strips.  Finely chop garlic.
Mix cornstarch, salt, vegetable broth and set aside.
Heat skillet, add oil, butter and garlic.  Add zuchini and squash, cook for 6 minutes.  Add bell peper and cook for another 2 minutes.
Stir broth mixture to even it out, then slowly pour into skillet stirring constantly.  Cook until sauce boils and thickens.

Submitter: 
MEDIVALU
Description: 
This is perfect as a side dish to compliment a steak of pork chop!
