Title:
Polish Roses Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/303809.jpg
Ingredients:
1 jar of dried chipped beef slices
4oz cream cheese, softened
1-2 Tbsp fresh horseradish, depending on your spice preference
14 green onions

Directions:
Trim and wash green onions.  Mix together softened cream cheese and horseradish.  Spread cheese mixture evenly onto each slice of beef.  Place a green onion onto each slice and roll up.  Serve & enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
This has been handed down through our family for years.  I do not remember ever having a Christmas without these on the table.
