Title:
Peach and Mint Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1126625.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, sliced, or more to taste
4 peaches, halved and pitted, or more to taste
2 fresh mint leaves
1 pinch salt

Directions:
Combine cucumbers, peaches, mint, and salt in a pitcher; stir gently. Refrigerate for 8 hours to overnight.

Submitter: 
KittenandKittyCSCL
Description: 
I found out about this from a friend from the Humane Society where I volunteer, just had to share it! If you don't want to wait all night, try using a juicer.
