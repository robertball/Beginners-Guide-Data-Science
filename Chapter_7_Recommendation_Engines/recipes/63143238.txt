Title:
Macaroni Scramble (Goulash) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. elbow macaroni
2 T. olive oil
1 1/2 lbs lean ground beef
3 cloves garlic, minced (or more to taste)
2 cups chopped onion
1 green pepper, chopped
4 oz. sliced mushrooms
2 8 oz cans tomato sauce
1 15 oz can diced tomatoes
1 T. chili powder
2 tsp. sharp paprika (not sweet)
1 T. Brown sugar
1 T. Worchestershire sauce
1/2 cup dry Sherry
grated sharp cheddar cheese -to cover top of pan
salt & fresh ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook macaroni to slightly aldente. In a large skillet with deep sides, heat olive oil.
Add ground beef and start to brown.  Halfway through, add onions, gr pepper, mushrooms and garlic. Cook until meat is done and vegetables are tender.
Add seasoning, Worchestershire sauce, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, sherry and macaroni.
Mix thoroughly and pour into a 3 quart casserole dish and top with cheddar cheese.
Now you can do one of 2 things: Bake in 350 oven for 30 minutes until cheese melts and casserole is bubbly, or (and this is preferable)leave in refrigerator overnight for the flavors to blend then baked for approximately 1 hr.

Submitter: 
Debi Blair McGinness
Description: 
My Grandma used to make this when I was growing up and I just recently resurrected the recipe.  I forgot how good it is.  My husband takes the leftovers to work.  It a great potluck dish and I'm always asked for the recipe.
