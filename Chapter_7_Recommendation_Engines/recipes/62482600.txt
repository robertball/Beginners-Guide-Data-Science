Title:
Cheese Tortellini Soup and Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/14 pound pork sausage (we prefer cheese/garlic, less fat)
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes
32 oz beef broth
1 diced fresh garlic clove
1 small diced onion, if desired
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp Italian seasonsing (basil, oregano, etc)
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Cook sausage links in frying pan on medium to high heat. To almost completion of browning remove links.
Set aside. Drain sausage fat.  Heat garlic and onions till brown, set apart.  Cut sausage links into quarter to dime size widths.
Return all ingredients to large boiling pot, if needed, continue to cook.  Otherwise, add ingredients to large boiling pot, add tomatoes, spices, and broth.  Allow to boil and reduce to simmmer with covered pot for 1 hour more.  Watch carefully.
When guests or family are to arrive increse heat to boil and drop tortelli in. Allow pasta to cook 3-5 minutes or desired tenderness.  Serve with warm Italian bread and a vegggie of if not included in the dish!

Submitter: 
CATECRUM
Description: 
Yummy! Quick prep soup for fall chilly nights.  You can add easy quick veggies such as kale or spinach for your liking.
