Title:
awesome honey glazed chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1 3lb boiler/fryer chicken cut up
1/2 cup butter divided melted
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 tbsp lime juice
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 1/2 tsp curry powder
1/4 cup bacon bits
paprika (garnish)

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
in large bowl combine flour, salt, and cayenne pepper
add chicken pieces and coat with previous mixture
pour 4 tbsp butter into a 13x9x2" baking pan
place chicken in pan turning pieces to coat
bake uncovered for 30 min
combine brown sugar, honey, lemon and lime juice, soy, curry powder, and remaining butter,
pour over chicken
bake for 45 more min or till chicken is tender basting several times with pan drippings
top with bacon bits and paprika
