Title:
Sticky Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 rolls of refrigerated breadsticks
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 cup whipping cream to which 1 tsp vanilla has been added
sugar/cinnamon

Directions:
Spread the brown sugar in the bottom of a jelly roll pan.
Separate the breadsticks, cut in half, twist and lay in rows on the brown sugar.
Pour the cream over the whole pan.
Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
Bake 20-25 minutes until lightly browned @ 350'
