Title:
Cucumber Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cucumber peeled and diced
3/4 cup chopped green onions including 2" of stems
2 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp flour
4 cups chicken broth
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1/2 cup sour cream
1 to 2 Tbsp fresh dill chopped
Salt to taste

Directions:
Place cucumber, green onions and butter in a sauce pan.
Cover with wax paper cut to fit inside of pan.
Simmer over low heat  for 10 minutes.
Discard wax paper and add flour.
Stir in chicken broth and lemon juice.
Simmer for 10 minutes.
Salt to taste.
Puree soup in a blender, then refrigerate for 4 hours.
Serve cold and garnish with sour cream and dill weed.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Serve this soup cold on a hot summer evening.
