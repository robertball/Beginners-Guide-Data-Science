Title:
BACARDI® Select Egg Nog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986151.jpg
Ingredients:
3 ounces BACARDI® Select Rum
5 ounces half and half
1/2 ounce sugar
1/2 ounce vanilla extract

Directions:
Shake ingredients with crushed ice and strain into a mug. Sprinkle with nutmeg.

Submitter: 
BACARDI®
Description: 
Egg nog is a classic treat to serve at holiday parties.
