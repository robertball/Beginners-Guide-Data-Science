Title:
Spicy Hot Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Mexican-style corn, drained
1 can Rotel diced tomatoes, undrained
1 8oz. package cream cheese

Directions:
Heat all ingredients together on low, until hot.  Use tortilla chips and Fritos Scoops for dipping.

Submitter: 
paynes
Description: 
This recipe is a delicious change from the regular Mexican cheese dips.  Not only does it taste great, but it's so easy to make!
