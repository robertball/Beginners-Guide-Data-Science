Title:
Derby Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick melted butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup white corn syrup
4 eggs beaten
1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 cup whole pecans
1 pie crust

Directions:
Layer pecans and semi sweet chocolate chips in bottom of pie crust
Mix all other ingredients in bowl and then pour into pie crust
Bake at 375 degrees for 50-60 minutes or until knife pulls out of center clean.
** You will need to use a 9 inch deep dish pie pan or you will have to much liquid ingredients.
