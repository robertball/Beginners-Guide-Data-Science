Title:
Mocha Fudge Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5oz Semisweet Chocolate
3oz Unsweetened Chocolate
6tb Butter
1/3c AP Flour
1t Baking Powder
1/4t Salt
2 eggs
2t Vanilla Extract
2t Instant Espresso Powder
3/4c Sugar
3/4c SemiSweet Chips (5oz)

Directions:
Melt 3oz & 5oz of chocolate and butter, let cool.
Sift flour, baking powder and salt, set aside.
In a medium mixing bowl, beat eggs, vanilla and espresso powder until blended, about 10 seconds
Add sugar and blend for 1 minute.
Add melted chocolate mixture, blend 1 minute and scrape the bowl with a spatula.
Add flour on low until just blended.  Finish incorporating by hand.
Fold in chocolate chips
Using a large cookie scoop, make 3tb sized balls and refrigerate covered for at least 4 hours (overnight ideal)
