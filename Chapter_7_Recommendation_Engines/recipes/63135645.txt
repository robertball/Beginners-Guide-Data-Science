Title:
Liquid Dishwashing Soap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup liquid castile soap
3 tsp vegetable glycerin
5 drops tea-tree essential oil
20 drops lemon essential oil
1 1/3 cups water

Directions:
Using a funnel, pour soap, glycerin, oils and water into a bottle.  Shake well to emulsify.
TO USE:  Place soap beside the sink and uses on dishes and your hands.
