Title:
Slow Cooker Keto Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs grass fed ground beef
1-2 T olive oil/ghee/butter
1 yellow onion, diced
1 small red onion, diced
5-10 garlic cloves, minced
1 red bell pepper, diced
2 4oz cans mild hatch green chiles
3 oz or half can tomato paste
14 oz fire roasted tomatoes
1/4 c cabernet
1/4 c pinot noir
3 c beef broth (could use less to make thicker)
4 T cumin
3-4 T chili powder
3 T smoked paprika
2 T cacoa powder
3 T oregano
1-2 tsp chipotle powder
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp cloves
1-2 tsp salt or to taste plus more for onions/beef

Directions:
Combine all dry spices and put in bowl
saute onions, garlic, peppers in olive oil/ghee until softened, add salt to sweat
add 2-3 T spices
put in crock pot
brown ground beef, add salt and several T spices
put in crock pot
add tomatoes, tomato paste and green chiles to crock pot
add wine and beef broth
cook on low for at least 4 hours (I did 6)
