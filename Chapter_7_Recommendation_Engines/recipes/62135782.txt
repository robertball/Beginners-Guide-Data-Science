Title:
Beefy Egg Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef, turkey or pork
1 large onion, finely chopped
2 cloves of garlic, minced
3 carrots, grated
8 oz. shredded cabbage
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 tbsp. sesame oil
1 tbsp. sugar
salt and pepper to taste
20 egg roll wrappers found in produce section, not frozen
Mustard

Directions:
Brown meat and drain.
Add onion, garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, salt and pepper.
Cook for 5 minutes.
Add carrots and cabbage and cook for additional 2 minutes and then remove from heat.
Put 1-2 tbsp. filling in each egg roll wrapper and wrap according to directions on package.
Deep fry until the egg rolls are golden brown.
Dip in mustard and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Miranda Daye Weaver
Description: 
These are egg rolls are easy, loaded with meat, and is big on  flavor.  They are requested by friends and family and a great served with the Chinese Cabbage Salad I found on this web site.
