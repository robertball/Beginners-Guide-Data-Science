Title:
Coconut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup SUGAR
3/4 cup BUTTER
pinch   SALT
1/3 cup FRESH EGGS
3 3/4 cup CAKE FLOUR
2     cup MACAROON COCONUT (DESICATED)
1/4 tsp BAKING SODA
1/2 tsp CREAM of TARTAR
1 TBL COCOA
2/3 cup MILK

Directions:
Cream sugar, butter and salt for 3 minutes.
Add eggs slowly and continue to cream for 2 minutes.
Add flour, coconut, baking soda and cream of tartar
followed by the milk and mix until smooth.
Divide mix in half and add cocoa to half.
With a pastry bag with a 1/2" open star tube,
bag out the white mix 3" long on parchment paper.
Next, bag out the chocolate mix 3" long right
next to the white mix you bagged out.
(The two flavors will bake together)
Bake in a 350* oven until edges are golden brown,
DO NOT OVER BAKE
When cool, using your fingers, ice the top of each
cookie with a warmed fondant icing!
