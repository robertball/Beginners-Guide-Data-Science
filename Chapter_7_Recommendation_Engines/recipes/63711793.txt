Title:
Big Ray's Mexican Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129664.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can  (16.3 oz.) buttermilk biscuits, quartered
2 Tbsp.  butter, melted
1-1/4 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
3/4 cup sliced jalapeño peppers, divided
3/4 tsp parsley flakes, divided
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350ºF.
Spray 9x 5 inch loaf pan with cooking spray. Dip 1/3 of the biscuit pieces in butter; place in prepared pan. Top with 1/2 cup cheddar, 1/4 cup peppers and 1/4 tsp. parsley; repeat layers.
Cover with remaining dipped biscuit pieces, peppers and parsley; top with combined mozzarella and remaining Cheddar.
Bake 40 to 45 min. or until golden brown. Let stand 5 min. Invert bread onto plate; remove pan. Serve bread warm.
