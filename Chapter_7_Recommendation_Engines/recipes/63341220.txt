Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887423.jpg
Ingredients:
8 slices of bacon
1 lb Ground Hamburger
1 cup of Onion, Diced
1 Green pepper, diced
1 14.5 Oz can of tomatoes
1 14.5 Oz of water
3/4 cup uncooked long-grain rice
2 Tablespoons chili powder
2 Teaspoon of salt
1 Teaspoon of pepper
1 Teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- Start Heat Settings -
Very Mild - No Jalapeno Pepper
Mild - 1\2 Jalapeno Pepper, Without Seeds, Diced
Med - 1 Jalapeno Pepper, Without Seeds, Diced
Hot - 1 Jalapeno Pepper, With Seeds, Diced
- End Heat  Settings -
3 Cloves Of Garlic
Sprinkling Of parsley or cilantro.

Directions:
On a baking sheet and rack cook 1lb of bacon, at 350F in oven, Or pan fry.
In a Large fry pan brown hamburger, Remove and set aside.
Pour off all the hamburger grease
Add two tablespoons of bacon grease (Or Olive Oil) to the fry pan then Add rice, and chopped onion. Brown the rice and Onion.
Add Green peppers, Jalapeno Pepper, tomatoes, water, Cooked Hamburger, Chili-Powder, Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Worcestershire sauce.
Cover Simmer for 35 To 40 minutes
Dish, And sprinkle top with bacon, and parsley or cilantro.
