Title:
Turkey Stock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
turkey carcass
one pound of carrots
1 bunch of celery
3 med onions
3 cloves of garlic
3 bay leaves
handful of black peppercorns
4 chicken bouillon
1 green pepper

Directions:
put everything into a soup stock pot. add water. simmer for an hour. take off heat. let it rest for 15 min. the strain through cheese cloth.

Submitter: 
Julia Monroe
Description: 
Make a great stock with the carcass and then creat wonderful soups.
