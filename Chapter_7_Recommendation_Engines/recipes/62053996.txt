Title:
Frosted Banana Bars w/ homeade cream cheese icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
3 eggs
1 1/2 cups mashed ripe bananas
1/8 teaspoon salt
2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups flour
FROSTING
1/2 cup butter/margarine
8 oz. cream cheese softened
4 cups powder sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar together, beat in eggs, bananas and vanilla. In another bowl combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add flour mix to the butter mix, and mix well. Pour mix in a greased 15x10x1 pan and bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 25 min until a toothpick inserted in center of cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
While the cake is baking. Cream butter and cream cheese and vanilla, and very slowly add the powder sugar, mix well. When cake is cool, very carefully frost cake. And cut into bars.

Description: 
Moist and Yummy, almost like a Banana cake with homeade icing. I made these for a family get together and they were gone fast and everyone asked for the recipe.
