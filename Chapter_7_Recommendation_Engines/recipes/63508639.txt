Title:
Low Country Boil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123403.jpg
Ingredients:
16 cups water
1/4 cup Old Bay Seasoning
2 to 3 tsp. ground red pepper (cayenne)
2# smoked sausage, cut into 1 1/2" chunks
2# tiny red potatoes, halved if too large
10 small onions, about 3#
5 ears corn, cut in pieces
2# fresh large shrimp in shell (or remove shell)
1 stick butter, melted
1/4 cup each snipped fresh herbs such as thyme, oregano, basil
Cocktail sauce and bottles hot pepper sauce

Directions:
In Dutch oven combine water, Old Bay and red pepper.  Cover and bring to a boil.  Add sausage, potatoes, onion and corn (can add corn when potatoes are half done).  Return to boiling, cover and simmer 10 minutes.  Add shrimp, cook 2 to 3 minutes until shrimp are opaque.  Remove, let stand 5 minutes.  Drain in large colander.  Combine butter and herbs and drizzle over shrimp and veggies.  Serve with cocktail sauce and hot pepper sauce.
