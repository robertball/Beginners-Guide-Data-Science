Title:
Sweet 'n Sour Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978257.jpg
Ingredients:
8 boneless, skinless chicken breasts or chicken tenders
1 (20 ounce) can DOLEÂ® Pineapple Chunks, undrained
3 teaspoons packed brown sugar
3 tablespoons ketchup
3 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons white or cider vinegar
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Grill or broil chicken 5 minutes on each side or until chicken is no longer pink in center.
Combine pineapple chunks, brown sugar, ketchup, soy sauce, vinegar, cornstarch and ginger in saucepan.  Cook until sauce boils and thickens.
Spoon sauce over broiled or grilled chicken, before serving.

Submitter: 
Dole Food Company
Description: 
Chicken breasts are grilled or broiled, then served in a tangy pineapple sweet and sour sauce.
