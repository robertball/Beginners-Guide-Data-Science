Title:
Dad's Amazing Caramel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8   Quarts Popcorn
2  Cups Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup Light Corn Syrup
1/2 lb. Butter
1/4 Teaspoon Cream of Tartar
1   Teaspoon Salt
1   Teaspoon Baking Soda

Directions:
Put the popcorn in a roasting pan or oven baking pan.
Combine the brown sugar, corn syrup, butter, cream of tartar and salt.
Bring to a boil stirring constantly over medium/high heat.  Using a candy thermometer cook until 260 degrees.
Remove from heat and stir in the baking soda.
Pour the mixture over the popcorn and stir well to coat the popcorn.
Bake at 200 degrees for one hour.
Turn out the caramel corn onto waxed paper and break apart.
Let cool.
Enjoy!

Description: 
This recipe has been a family favorite for years.  It is an easy Caramel corn to make and has an amazing flavor!  This is one you will make again and again.
