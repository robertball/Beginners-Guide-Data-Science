Title:
Easy Peach Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4364844.jpg
Ingredients:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
29 oz can of sliced peaches in syrup
1 box of yellow cake mix
1 stick of butter
1/2 cup of chopped pecans (optional)
1 cup of brown sugar

Directions:
Spread the peaches in the bottom of a 9x9 or 9x13 pan or dish.  Cut peach slices in half.
Dump the dry cake mix on top of the peaches.  Pat just a little.
Either melt the butter and pour over or dot with butter pieces.
Sprinkle with brown sugar and pecans (optional).
Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes.
Serve with whipped cream or ice cream, if desired.
