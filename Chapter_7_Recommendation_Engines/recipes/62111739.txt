Title:
Strawberry Walnut Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head Romaine lettuce
1 head Boston Bibb lettuce
1 pint strawberries, sliced
1/2 cup glazed walnuts, broken into pieces
1 cup shredded colby and monterey jack cheese
1 bottle prepared poppyseed dressing

Directions:
Wash and chop Romaine and Boston Bibb lettuces into bite sized pieces.  Spin or dry thoroughly.
Add sliced strawberries, walnut pieces, and cheese to lettuces in a large bowl.
Just before serving, add 1/4 cup of salad dressing at a time to the salad, tossing well to combine, until salad is lightly coated in dressing.
Garnish with extra strawberries, if desired.

Submitter: 
chef24
Description: 
Green salad with strawberries, glazed walnuts, and cheese, with poppyseed dressing.
