Title:
Creamy Garlic Shrimp & Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon
1/2 lb. cooked shrimp
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms [or jarred sliced mushrooms]
1 package Knorr® Italian Sides™ - Creamy Garlic Shells
1 1/2 cups water
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup frozen green peas

Directions:
1.Cook bacon in large skillet; remove and crumble. Reserve 2 tablespoons drippings.
2.Stir shrimp and mushrooms into reserved drippings and cook 2 minutes or until shrimp are done; remove and keep warm.
3.Stir Knorr® Italian Sides™ - Creamy Garlic Shells, water and milk into same skillet. Bring to the boiling point over high heat. Reduce heat to medium and cook 3 minutes. Stir in peas and cook, stirring occasionally, until pasta is tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in bacon, shrimp and mushrooms; heat through.
