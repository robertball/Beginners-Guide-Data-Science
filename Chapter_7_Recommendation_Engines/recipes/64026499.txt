Title:
Loaded Potato Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium russet potatoes
2 Tbsp Lawry's seasoning salt
1/4 cup Reduced Fat Mild Cheddar Shredded Cheese
3 slices cooked bacon, crispy
2 tbsp. green onions, chopped
Ranch Sauce
1/4 cup reduced fat sour cream
2 Tbsp Reduced Fat Ranch Dressing
2 Tbsp. fat free milk

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 425
2.Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil
3.Spray foil with nonstick spray, I used the olive oil variety
4.Place a cooling rack on the foil to cook the potatoes on
Potatoes
1.Wash each potato and dry with a paper towel
2.Cut potatoes into wedges, whatever size you would like. I got 16 wedges out of each potato
3.Lightly spray potatoes with olive oil spray and then sprinkle season salt over potatoes, toss to coat
4.Place wedges on cooling rack and bake for 40-45 minutes, flipping once, or until potatoes are tender and golden brown
5.Remove potatoes from oven and remove potatoes from the cooling rack and place directly onto the foil
6.Sprinkle cheese & bacon over potatoes
7.Drizzle Ranch sauce over potatoes to your liking
8.Place potatoes back in the oven and let bake for 5 minutes, or until cheese is melted
9.Place on serving platter and sprinkle with green onions
10.ENJOY!!
Ranch Sauce
1.In a medium bowl whisk together sour cream, dressing and milk until smooth, adding in more milk if necessary
