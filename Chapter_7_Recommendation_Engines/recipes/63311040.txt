Title:
Marbled chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/875436.jpg
Ingredients:
Chocolate Chip Cookie dough:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough
1/2 cup butter + 2 tbl butter, softened
1 cup white sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup all-purpose flour
3/8 cup unsweetend cocoa powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup white chocolate chips
1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough
In large mixer bowl, beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
Beat in the egg until combined, and then add vanilla.
Sift together the flour, salt, and baking soda.  Stir into the butter mixture until well blended.
Stir in the chocolate chips.
Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough
In large mixer bowl, beat butter and white sugar until light and fluffy.
Beat in the egg until combined, and then add vanilla.
Sift together the flour, cocoa, salt, and baking soda.  Stir into the butter mixture until well blended.
Stir in the chocolate chips.
To marble the cookies:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Take a half teaspoon of regular cookie dough and roll it into a rope.  Take a half teaspoon of chocolate cookie dough and roll it into a rope.  Twist the two ropes together and then gently roll into a ball.  Place on an ungreased baking sheet.  Continue with the rest of the dough, spacing the cookies about 2" apart.  (See notes for tips on marbling)
Bake for about 8-12 minutes.  Remove from oven, let cool a few minutes on the pan, and then place cookies on a cooling rack to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Carole
Description: 
Using a regular chocolate chip recipe and a chocolate chocolate chip recipe, I came up with marbled chocolate chip cookies.  They are a bit time consuming to make, but they are worth it!
