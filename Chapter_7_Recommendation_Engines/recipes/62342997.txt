Title:
Bacon Chicken with Roasted Red Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 bacon slices
6 chicken thighs
1/2 cup chopped red onion
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 cup salsa
1 7 ounce bottle roasted red peppers, drained & sliced
fresh cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Cook bacon in a large non-stick skillet over medium high heat until crisp.  Remove and drain.  Crumble and set aside.  Reserve drippings.
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper.  Add chicken to bacon drippings.  Saute 2 minutes on each sdie.  Remove chicken from pan.
Add onion and garlic.  Saute 3 minutes.
Stir in salsa and red peppers.
Return chicken to pan.  Cover and cook over medium heat 10-12 minutes or until chicken is done.
Sprinkle with crumbled bacon.  Garnish with cilantro.
Serve with rice.
