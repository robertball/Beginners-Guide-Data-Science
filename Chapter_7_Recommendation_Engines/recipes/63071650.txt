Title:
Amy's Mustard Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup canola oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 tsp taragon vinegar
1 T dijon mustard
1/4 tsp oregano
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
dash white pepper

Directions:
Add all to a container and wisk to combine.  Refridgerate for an hour prior to serving to meld flavors.

Submitter:

Description: 
Tangy and delicious good on on a bed of greens with some parmesan.
