Title:
No-Fail Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
3/4 cup butter
2/3 cup evaporated milk, (or fat-free evaporated milk)
2 cups marshmallow cream, (1 jar)
2 cups chocolate chips, (one 12-oz. bag)
3/4 cup finely chopped nuts
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla, or 3/4 teaspoon of double-strength vanilla

Directions:
Butter 13 x 9 inch pan.
In large saucepan, combine first 3 ingredients.  Heat to boiling over medium-high heat, stirring frequently.
Reduce heat; simmer 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat; add marshmallow creme and chocolate chips; blend well.
Stir in nuts and vanilla.  Pour into prepared pan.
Cover with aluminum foil. Chill until firm.
Cut into squares. Serve.
