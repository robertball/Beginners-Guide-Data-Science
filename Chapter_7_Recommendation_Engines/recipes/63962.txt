Title:
Dulce de Leche from EAGLE BRAND® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/248297.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
Assorted dippers, such as cookies, cake, banana chunks, apple slices and/ or strawberries...

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.  Pour sweetened condensed milk into 9-inch pie plate.  Cover with foil; place in larger shallow baking pan.  Pour hot water into larger pan to depth of 1-inch.
Bake 1 hour or until thick and caramel-colored.  Beat until smooth.  Cool 1 hour.  Refrigerate until serving time.  Serve as dip with assorted dippers.  Store leftovers covered in refrigerator for up to 1 week.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
A fun and foolproof way to make a delicious caramel dip. A perfect complement to apples in the Fall, or berries in Summer. This dip goes great with everything!
