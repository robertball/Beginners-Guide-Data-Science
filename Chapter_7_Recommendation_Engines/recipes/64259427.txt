Title:
Cinnamon Bun Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow cake mix
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup powdered sugar
3/4 cup oil
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1 cup chopped walnuts
2 tablespoons milk
4 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
8 ounces sour cream

Directions:
Hand mix cake mix, oil, eggs and sour cream.
Put half the batter in 9 x 13 pan.
Combine brown sugar, walnuts, cinnamon and spread over entire cake. Spread remaining batter on top. Use a knife to make swirls in the cake.
Bake at 325 degrees for 40 minutes.
Hand mix powdered sugar, milk and vanilla extract and spread on warm cake.
