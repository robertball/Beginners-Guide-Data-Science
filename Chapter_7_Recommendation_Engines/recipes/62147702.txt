Title:
Cranberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cranberries, cut in half
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup sugar
1 cup flour
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 pie crust for 10" pie pan

Directions:
Combine cranberries, brown sugar, and walnuts, spread in the bottom of pie shell.  Beat eggs well and gradually add sugar, continue beating.  Stir in flour and melted butter. Pour batter over cranberries in pie shell.  Cover edges of crust with foil.  Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350, remove foil and bake 35 minutes longer.  Serve with ice cream.

Submitter: 
Stacey
Description: 
This cake-like pie is the perfect combination of sweet and tart.  Very easy, delicious pie!
