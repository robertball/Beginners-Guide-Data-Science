Title:
martha's crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tbs unsalted butter melted
2 eggs
3/4 cup whole milk or add some half and half
1/8 tsp salt
1/2 tbs of of sugar
1/2 cup of flour

Directions:
Melt butter, let cool,add milk,eggs,blend together with blender,whisk ok but harder. Then blend in the rest.
Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour
Melt some real butter in a bowl and brush into non-stick skillet on medium heat
Use 1/4 cup or just a short 1/3 cup to apply batter

Submitter: 
dave
Description: 
I use this to build savory breakfast menus for me and freeze for future also dessert crepes for grandkids and me sometimes ...david
