Title:
Lynchburg Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/680479.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Tennessee whiskey
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger sweet and sour mix
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger triple sec (orange-flavored liqueur)
3/4 cup chilled lemon-lime soda

Directions:
Fill a tall glass with ice cubes and pour in the whiskey, sweet and sour mix, triple sec and lemon-lime soda. Stir with a straw and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Michael Charles
Description: 
Lemon-lime soda is spiked with whiskey, triple sec and a dash of sweet and sour mix. This was given to me by my aunt, it's divine!
