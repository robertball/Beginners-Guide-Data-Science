Title:
Dark Chocolate Marbled banana bread with Greek Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3429423.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup cocoa powder
5 tablespoons hot water
2 large over rip bananas, mashed
1/2 cup melted salted butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla
2 eggs whisked
1/2 cup Greek yogurt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 and grease a loaf pan
In a small bowl whisk together flour, baking soda and salt, set aside.
In a large bowl combine cocoa powder and hot water and stir till smooth, set aside.
In a large bowl combine mashed bananas, melted butter, sugar, vanilla, eggs, and greek yogurt. Mix well until all combined.
Carefully fold in flour mixture to banana mix. Folding until thoroughly combined (try not to overmix)
Take half of the banana mix and combine into bowl with the cocoa mix. Stir carefully but well till all combined.
Spoon both mixtures if banana bread into loaf pan alternating between the two.
Bake for 55 minutes or till done depending on your pan and oven.
Enjoy :)
*tastes wonderful with flavored greek yogurt
*use 3 bananas if your bananas are small

Submitter:

Description: 
I created this recipe using my favourite parts of many different recipes. This bread isn't very sweet, so it's not for those who enjoy a sweeter bread. The chocolate throughout makes this a beautiful bread to entertain with.
