Title:
Apple Strudel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 baking apple - peeled, cored and coarsely shredded
3 baking apples - peeled, cored and sliced
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup golden raisins
1/3 cup coarsely chopped pecans
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
pinch ground cloves
1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed
1 egg
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or nonstick foil.
Place apples in a large bowl. Stir in brown sugar, golden raisins, pecans and seasonings; set aside.
Place puff pastry on baking sheet. Roll lightly with a rolling pin.
Arrange apple filling down the middle of the pastry lengthwise. Fold the pastry lengthwise around the mixture. Seal edges of pastry by using a bit of water on your fingers, and rubbing the pastry edges together.
Slash top of strudel crosswise 4 times.
Whisk egg and milk together, and brush onto top of pastry.  Sprinkle with white sugar.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, or until golden brown.
