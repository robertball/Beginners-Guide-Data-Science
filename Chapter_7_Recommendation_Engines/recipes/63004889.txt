Title:
Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750005.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ham shank or 1 lb kielbasa or 1 lb ham shank
1 cup chopped onion
4 cups potatoes, diced
4 cups boiling water
6 TBS butter
2 tsp salt
1 can creamed corn
2 cans niblets corn
1 can evaporated milk

Directions:
Saute sliced meat and onion in butter
Add water, potatoes, and salt, bring to a boil; simmer until potatoes are tender
Add corns and evaporated milk, let sit 30 minutes and serve
