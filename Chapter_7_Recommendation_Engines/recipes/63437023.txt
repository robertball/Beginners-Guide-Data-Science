Title:
SPINACH SQUARES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10oz. pkg. chopped spinach, defrosted and drained
1/4 lb butter, melted
1 medium onion chopped
1/2 lb. cheddar cheese
1 cup flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking powder
1 c. milk
2 eggs. slightly beaten

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients
Add remaining ingredients
Pour into 13x9 greased floured pan
Bake 350   30 minutes
Cut into squares
Can keep warm on warming tray.
