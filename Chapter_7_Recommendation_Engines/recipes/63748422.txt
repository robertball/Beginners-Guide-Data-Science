Title:
Sesame chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tablespoons dried onion
2 tablespoons ketchup
1 tablespoon oil
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
2 teaspoons corn starch
4 tablespoons sesame seeds

Directions:
Place chicken peaces in crockpot.  Combine remaining ingredients except corn starch and sesame seeds.  Pour over chicken and cook on low at least 4 hours.
Lift chicken from sauce.
Combine corn starch with 3 tablespoons water.  Blend into sauce in crockpot.  Replace crockpot lid and cook on high 10 minutes or until slightly thickened.
Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and add back to sauce.  Continue to simmer on low or serve.  (Serve over a bed of white rice.)
