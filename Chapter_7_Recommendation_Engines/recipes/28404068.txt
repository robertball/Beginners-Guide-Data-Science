Title:
Honey Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 TBS. chunky or smooth peanut butter
3 TBS. honey
2 tsp. reduced-sodium soy sauce
2 TBS. lemon juice
2 TBS. orange juice
(can dip bite size pieces of grilled chicken)

Directions:
Microwave the peanut butter for about 20 seconds or until it begins to melt.  Stir until smooth.
Add the honey, soy sauce, lemon juice, and orange juice to the bowl and mix well.  Serve immediately with chicken.
