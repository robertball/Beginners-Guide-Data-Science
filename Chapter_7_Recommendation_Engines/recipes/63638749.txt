Title:
Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 chicken breast, 1 btl Sweet Baby Ray's sauce, 1/4 c vinegar, 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes, 1/4 c brown sugar, 1 tsp garlic powder.

Directions:
Mix everything but chicken. Place chicken in crockpot (frozen is ok). Pour sauce mixture over chicken. Cook on low 4-6 hours.
