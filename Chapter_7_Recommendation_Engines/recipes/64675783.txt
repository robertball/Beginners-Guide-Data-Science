Title:
Mexican Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chicken
1 quart chicken stock
1 14 oz can tomatoes, crushed
3/4 cup salsa
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2-3 tablespoons chili powder
1 can black beans
2 cups frozen or fresh corn
Shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic in a small amount of olive oil
Add chili powder to onion, mix well
Add chicken
Add stock, tomatoes, salsa, beans and corn; simmer 10-15 minutes
Serve with shredded cheese and tortilla chips
