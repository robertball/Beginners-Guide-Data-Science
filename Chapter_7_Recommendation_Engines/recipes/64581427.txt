Title:
California Bounty Beef and Vegetable Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3651650.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound sirloin steak (or cut of choice)
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 Mexican squash
1/3 large carrot; wide end
2 large radishes
1/2 avocado, diced
1/2 cup grape tomatoes, halved
1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
Sriracha to taste (or use sour cream Sriracha dressing).
Dressing (optional):
2 oz. sour cream
1 tablespoon Sriracha

Directions:
Season steak with garlic salt and black pepper.
Heat olive oil in a pan and cook steak to desired doneness.
Remove steak and let rest; slice into thin strips.
Separately spiralize the Mexican squash and carrot.
Spiralize the radishes and set aside.
Mix sour cream and Sriracha together.
Add 2 TBSPs of oil to pan, and heat over medium heat, add in carrot noodles and cook until slightly caramelized, add the squash and cook for an additional 5 minutes stirring constantly.
Serve immediately topped with beef and garnished with spiralized radish, diced avocado, sliced grape tomatoes and cilantro.
Drizzle Sriracha on top or used sour cream Sriracha dressing.

Description: 
Living in California one can get fresh vegetables year round. Spiralized vegetables are fun and creative for that special main or side spiced up with some California made Sriracha. This recipes is easily adaptable with other vegetables.
