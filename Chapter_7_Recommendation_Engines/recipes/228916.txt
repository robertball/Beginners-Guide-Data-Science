Title:
Cremesicle Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3603366.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup orange juice
1 cup milk
1 cup crushed ice
1 teaspoon white sugar, or more to taste (optional)

Directions:
Blend orange juice, milk, ice, and sugar together in a blender until no large chunks of ice remain.

Submitter: 
Gustavo
Description: 
This tasty treat is quite quick and very simple, yet the result is a delicious snack for people of all ages.
