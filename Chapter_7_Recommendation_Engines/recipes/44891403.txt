Title:
Tahini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sesame seeds, toasted and cooled
Vegetable or olive oil

Directions:
Buy semsame seeds in bulk and toast in oven.  Allow to cool a bit, then process in food processor, drizzling some vegetable or olive oil down the feed tube until you get the consistancy you want.  It won't be as smooth as the bought product but the flavor and price are better.
