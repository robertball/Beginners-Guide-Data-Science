Title:
Pig Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp black pepper
dash of cayenne (if desired)
6 pieces thick sliced bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Into a small bowl, mix sugar, chili powder, black pepper and cayenne.  Set aside.
Spray a rimed bakig sheet wit cooking spray.  Arrange bacon slices onto baking sheet, making sure slices do not overlap.
Bake for 8 minutes.
Carefully flip bacon slices and sprinkle sugar/pepper mixture onto slices.  Return to oven and bake for an additional 8 minutes.
Transfer bacon to a cooling rack and cool completely..
