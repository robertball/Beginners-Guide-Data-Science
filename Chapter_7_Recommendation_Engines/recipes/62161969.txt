Title:
Easiest Steak or Chicken Thigh Marinade for the Grill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Soy Sauce (not low sodium)
1 C water
1 T Oregano (dried)
2 T Garlic (can be any form, fresh, dried, powdered, minced)

Directions:
Add all ingredients into a large ziplock bag and mix.  Add meat and marinate 30 minutes to an hour.  Drain and grill as you normally would.

Submitter: 
Heathercakes
Description: 
Easiest marinade that is so simple, yet everyone keeps asking me to give it to them.  Very mild, but meaty and good.  Even the kiddos will eat it!  The leftover steaks are great with eggs the next morning.
