Title:
Egg Strasa - Dana Roberts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb bread, cubed
1 cup ham, small cubed
1 1/2 cups tater tots, defrosted, crumbled
1 can green chilis, chopped
4 green onions, chopped
1 small can black olives, chopped
1 15 oz can tomatoes,diced, drained
8 oz cream cheese
1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
custard:
8 eggs, beaten
1 cup milk
1/2 t dry mustard
1/2 t salt

Directions:
layer ingredents (except custard and cream cheese) in the order given in a 9x13 pan.
Mix custard ingredents and pour overall.
distributed cream cheese in tiny dollops evenly over casserole.
refrigerate overnight.
before baking, check that custard is visible 3/4 of the way up the pan.  if not, mix two eggs and 1/2 cup milk and pour over casserole.
bake uncovered at 350 for one hour or till eggs are done.`

Submitter:

Description: 
hot breakfast casserole.  assemble the night before and just pop in the oven for breakfast.
