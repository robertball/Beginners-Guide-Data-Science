Title:
Royal Rhubarb Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. fresh or thawed frozen rhubarb
3/4 c. sugar
2 T. quick tapioca
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 package frozen sliced strawberries (or 1 c. fresh)
1 c. uncooked oatmeal
1/4 c. sifted flour
1/3 c. brown sugar
1/4 c. butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine rhubarb, sugar, tapioca, and salt and toss lightly to coat.
Let stand 30 min, stirring occasionally.  Stir in strawberries.
Place fruit in an 8 in. square dish and set aside.
In a separate bowl, combine oats, flour, sugar, and butter.
Sprinkle crumb topping over rhubarb mix in casserole.
bake 45 min at 350.
Best served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
If you love rhubarb/strawberry mixtures, you'll love this sweet rhubarb crisp.
