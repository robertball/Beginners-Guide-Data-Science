Title:
Chocolate Chip Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3161714.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
1 t baking powder
1/4 t baking soda
1 t salt
2/3 c butter, almost melted
2 c packed brown sugar
2 eggs
2 t vanilla extract
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and grease 13x9 pan.
Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda & salt.
In a seperate bowl, mix butter with brown sugar.
Mix in eggs and vanilla extract.
Add to flour mixture and stir just until combined.
Spread the batter in the pan and sprinkle chocolate chips on top.
Bake until toothpick in center comes out clean, about 32-35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Stacy Reardon
Description: 
A delicious, ooey, gooey blondie bar with chocolate chips.  Also good with M & M's and or butterschotch chips.
