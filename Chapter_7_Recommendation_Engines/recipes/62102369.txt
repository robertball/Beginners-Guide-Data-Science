Title:
"Cuban Lunch" Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups peanut butter chips
2 cups butterscotch chips
2 cups chocolate chips
1 1/2 cups crushed ripple chips
1 1/2 cups peanuts (Can be salted or unsalted)

Directions:
Melt peanut butter chips, butterscotch chips, and chocolate chips in saucepan. Stir in crushed chips and peanuts.  Spoon into sprayed mini muffin tin pans (Or you can use just the paper liners)and chill in refrigerator until set up. (Or you can place in freezer to speed up process)

Submitter: 
jowolf2
Description: 
This is named after a chocolate bar sold a long time ago called cuban lunch, it used to come wrapped in a red paper.  Don't be fooled by the ingredients, you'd never know there was potato chips in these.
