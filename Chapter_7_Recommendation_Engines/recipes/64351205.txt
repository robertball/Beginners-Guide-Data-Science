Title:
Green Beans for a Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
6 strips bacon, diced
2 large onions, diced
2 large cans (6 lbs ea) Del Monte green beans, drained
4 cups water
4 beef bouillon cubes
pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Melt 1/2 stick butter.
2. Brown 1 diced onion and 3 strips diced bacon.
3. When bacon is browned, stir in two cups water with 2 bouillon cubes.
4. When cubes are dissolved, add one large can of green beans. Stir well. Cook until beans are hot and broth is simmering.
5. Season with pepper to taste.
Repeat process.
