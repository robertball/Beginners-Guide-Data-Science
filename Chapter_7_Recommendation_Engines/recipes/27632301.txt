Title:
Berry Lemonade Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Country Time Lemonade Flavor drink mix (dry)
1/2 cup water
3 cups ice cubes
1 cup fresh or frozen strawberries

Directions:
Measure drink mix into cap to 1qt. line (1/2 cup). Empty into blender container.
Add remainin ingredients; cover. Bland on high speed 10 sec. Turn off blender. Stir with spoon; cover. Blend an additional 5 sec. or until smooth, using pulsing action.
Serve immediately. Store leftover slush in freezer.
