Title:
Breakfast Sausage Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 lb Pork Butt
1 tbs salt
1 ts black pepper
1 ts sage
1/2 ts ginger
pinch cloves
1/4 ts nutmeg
1 ts thyme
1/4 ts cayenne pepper
1tbs brown sugar
1/2 c crushed ice

Directions:
Chop pork butt into small cubes (3/4" cubes)
Mix all ingredients except ice
coat meat cubes with spice mixture and let set for 2 hrs in fridge
grind seasoned meat with crushed ice with a med fine 1/4" plate
Mix well
Use a 2 oz ice cream scoop and form into patties.
Cook before eating
