Title:
Oh Henry Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
~16 Graham wafer cracker
1 c Graham wafer cracker crumbs
1/2 c Walnuts, chopped
1/2c Coconut
1/2 c margarine/butter
1/2 c milk
1 c brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In 9x12 cake pan, line bottom with Grahman wafer crackers.
Boil together margarin/butter, milk, brown sugar and vanilla for 5 minutes.
Remove from heat and add crumbs, walnuts and coconuts. Stir together and pour over wafers. Place another layer of wafers on top. Let cool.
ICING (make only a small amount):
Reallemon lemon juice
Icing sugar
Spread a thini layer over top layer of wafers.
Ready to serve.

Submitter: 
CLField
Description: 
Kids loved this one, the lemon icing gives some sour with the sweetness of the bar.
