Title:
Betsy's Mandarin Orange Salad Revised Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 Tbsp cider vinegar
2 Tbsp white sugar
1 teaspoons dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 pinch ground black pepper
1/2 cup sliced almonds
2 Tbsp white sugar
1 package lettuce
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 (11 ounce) cans mandarin orange segments, drained

Directions:
In a jar with a tight fitting lid, combine the oil, vinegar, sugar, parsley, salt and pepper.  Cover and shake well.  Refrigerate until use.
In a medium frypan over medium low heat, cook and stir the almonds and sugar until the sugar is melted and the almonds are coated.  Remove from heat, cool, and break apart.  Store at room temperature until ready to serve salad.
Just before serving, in a large bowl, toss together the lettuce, celery, oranges, almonds and dressing until evenly coated.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
This spectacular lettuce salad features is tossed with sugared almonds, celery and mandarin orange segments, and topped with a sweet and tangy vinaigrette.Modified from: Betsy's Mandarin Orange Salad
