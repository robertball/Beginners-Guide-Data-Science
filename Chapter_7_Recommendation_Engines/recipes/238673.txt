Title:
'Cherry' Caprese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1443580.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) basket cherry tomatoes
8 ounces small fresh mozzarella balls (ciliegine)
1 (6 ounce) can large black olives
3 tablespoons torn fresh basil

Directions:
Toss tomatoes, mozzarella balls, black olives, and basil together in a large bowl.

Submitter: 
musia
Description: 
Quick and easy to assemble. Also tasty with chopped scallions.
