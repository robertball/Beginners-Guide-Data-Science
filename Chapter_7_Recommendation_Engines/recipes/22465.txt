Title:
Mother-in-Law's Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/279938.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
1 onion, sliced
2 teaspoons vinegar
1/2 cup ketchup
1 teaspoon yellow mustard
1 teaspoon brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place chops and onion slices in a casserole dish.  In a bowl mix together: vinegar, ketchup, mustard and brown sugar.   Pour mixture over chops. Bake in oven for  1 1/2 to 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Cindy B.
Description: 
The easiest and most tender pork chops.  You can cut them with a fork.
