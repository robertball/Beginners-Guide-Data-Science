Title:
Meat & Three Bloody Mary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 32 oz can campbells Tomato Juice
1 16 oz bottle vodka
1 2 oz bottle of worcestershire sauce
1 lime juiced
2 oz of orange juice
1 T. Horseradish
1 T. Creole Season
1 T. celery salt
1 T. black pepper
Creole Season
Jalapeno stuffed olives
Celery Stalks
Spicy pickled green beans
mini slim jims
A few limes for garnish

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a large pitcher,keep refrigerated.To prepare each drink,dump liberal amount of creole season into a saucer.wipe rim of glass with a slice of lime,and lightly dip rim into creole season.Fill glass with ice,add left over lime slice and pour in drink mix.Garnish with slim jim,celery stalk,green bean,and olive.Meat and three,like the plate lunch.Enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
MrsAudirsch
Description: 
This is the way we do bloody amrys in th south.It's all you need on game day, a snack and drink in one!!!
