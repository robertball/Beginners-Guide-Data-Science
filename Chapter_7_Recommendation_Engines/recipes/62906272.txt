Title:
Sweet Soy Broccoli and Red Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cooking spray or 2 tsp oil
1 head broccoli, broken into florets (about 3 cups)
1 large red bell pepper, cut into strips or chunks
2 cloves garlic
1 Tbsp fresh grated ginger
1 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp brown sugar
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat skillet over medium-high heat; add oil to lightly coat pan.
Combine soy sauce, lemon juice and brown sugar - set aside
Add broccoli and red peppers to hot skillet; stir fry for 2 to 3 minutes.
Add garlic, and ginger, stir fry 1 minute; add butter and cook, stirring, until melted.
Add soy mixture and toss with vegetables until evenly coated.
Season to taste with salt & pepper and serve.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Broccoli and red peppers quickly stir-fried and seasoned with garlic, ginger, soy sauce, lemon juice and a touch of brown sugar, resulting in crisp veggies in a light sweet-tangy sauce.
