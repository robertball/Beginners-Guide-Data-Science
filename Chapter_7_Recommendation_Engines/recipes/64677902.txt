Title:
Billy Goats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups butter, softened
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup cocoa
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
8 oz dried dates, chopped
1 cup rice crispies
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cream butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla together.  In a separate bowl, combine flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.  Mix dry ingredients with the butter mixture.  Gently stir in dates and rice crispies.
Drop by the tablespoonfull onto a greased cookie sheet and bake 13-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
Rich chocolate cookies that are just sweet enough.  An old Holiday tradition.
