Title:
balsamic chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken breast, cut in thin slices
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup olive oil
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon dried basil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the vinegar, olive oil, garlic, dried basil, salt and pepper.
Add chicken and mix together until all of the chicken is coated.  Then cover and place in the refridgerator for 20 minutes at least.
Use a well seasoned cast iron grill pan, and heat before adding the chicken.
Once chicken is is placed on grill cook until each side is golden.
Serve immediately.
It goes well with an arugula salad that is dressed with balsamic vinegar, salt, pepper, olive oil, oregano and parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Felicia Manocchio
Description: 
Chicken breast marinated in mediterranean spices, then grilled on a cast iron grill pan.  It really makes the house smell great.
