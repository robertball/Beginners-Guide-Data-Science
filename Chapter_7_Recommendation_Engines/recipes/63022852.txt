Title:
Peanut butter Chocolate chip cookies Elder Morris Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup Peanut butter
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2 1/2 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
Chocolate chips

Directions:
Stir together first four ingredients.  Beat in eggsa and add dry ingredients.
Bake at 375 for 8-10 min
