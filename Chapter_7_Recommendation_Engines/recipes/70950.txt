Title:
Beef And Broccoli Pepper Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless beef top sirloin steak, cut into thin strips
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 (6.8 ounce) package RICE-A-RONI® Beef Flavor
2 tablespoons margarine
1 small onion, cut into thin wedges
2 cups water
1 cup broccoli flowerets
1 medium red or green bell pepper, cut into strips

Directions:
Toss beef with soy sauce; set aside.  In large skillet over medium heat, saute rice-vermicelli mix, margarine and onion until vermicelli is golden brown, stirring frequently.
Slowly stir in water and seasonings; bring to a boil.  Cover; reduce heat to low.  Simmer 10 minutes.
Stir in beef.  Add broccoli and bell pepper; return to a simmer. Cover; simmer 5 to 10 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender. Stir before serving.

Submitter: 
RICE-A-RONI®
Description: 
A quick, delicious beef and broccoli stir-fry that is easily made in thirty minutes, leaving you with more time to enjoy your dinner.
