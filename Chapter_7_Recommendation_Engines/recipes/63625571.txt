Title:
Roasted Brussel Sprouts - America's Test Kitchen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs fresh brussel sprouts (about 1 1/2 inch in length each)
3 tbsp oilive oil
1 tbsp water
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Wash and drain brussel sprouts.
Trim stem end off.
If small, slice in half;  if larger, cut in quarters.
Place in medium bowl.
Toss with  olive oil, water, salt and pepper.
Place cut-side down on rimmed cookie baking sheet.
Cover with foil.
Place covered brussel sprouts in pre-heated 500 degree F oven for 10 minutes.
Remove foil.
Place uncovered brussel sprouts back into oven for another 10-12 minutes until a little brown on top.
Remove from oven and serve on a platter.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Roasting is a simple and quick way to produce Brussels sprouts that are well-caramelized on the outside and tender on the inside.
