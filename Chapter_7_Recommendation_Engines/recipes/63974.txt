Title:
Chocolate Sheet Cake   Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979497.jpg
Ingredients:
Crisco速 Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
1 1/4 cups butter or margarine, divided
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa, divided
1 cup water
2 cups Pillsbury BEST速 All Purpose Flour
1 1/2 cups firmly packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND速 Sweetened Condensed Milk
2 large eggs
1 cup coarsely chopped nuts
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Coat 15x10-inch jelly roll pan with no-stick cooking spray.
In small saucepan, melt 1 cup butter; stir in 1/4 cup cocoa, and water. Bring to a boil; remove from heat. In large mixing bowl, combine flour, brown sugar baking soda, cinnamon and salt. Add cocoa mixture, beating well.  Stir in 1/3 cup sweetened condensed milk, eggs and vanilla.  Pour into prepared pan.
Bake 15 minutes or until cake springs back when lightly touched.
In small saucepan, melt remaining butter; add remaining cocoa and sweetened condensed milk.  Stir in powdered sugar and nuts.  Spread on warm cake.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND速
Description: 
This is a basic chocolate sheet cake with a hint of cinnamon. A rich chocolate glaze is poured over to take it to a new level of flavor.
