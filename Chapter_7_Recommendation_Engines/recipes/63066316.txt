Title:
Cathy's Mai Tai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 oz light rum
1/2 oz dark rum
1/2 oz Cointreau
1 tsp almond extract
Splash of Cherry juice and 2 cherries

Directions:
In tall glass add handful of ice ( fill half full) and pour alcohol over ice
Add the cherry juice and cherries and stir
Alternatively you can add all ingredients to a shaker, shake and pour over ice in tall glass
