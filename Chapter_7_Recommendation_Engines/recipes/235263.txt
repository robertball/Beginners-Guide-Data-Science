Title:
Trick or Treat Cocktail or Thankstini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
8 fluid ounces marshmallow-flavored vodka (such as Smirnoff®)
4 fluid ounces pumpkin pie liqueur (such as Fulton's Harvest®)
ice, or as desired
2 fluid ounces heavy whipping cream
1 fluid ounce simple syrup
1 pinch ground cinnamon (optional)

Directions:
Blend vodka, pumpkin liqueur, ice, heavy whipping cream, and simple syrup together in a blender until creamy and fluffy. Pour into martini glasses and garnish with a pinch of cinnamon.

Submitter: 
bellabusta
Description: 
Creamy, frothy fall cocktail with marshmallow vodka and pumpkin liqueur. Easy as turning on a blender and so perfect when the leaves start to fall. Garnish with cinnamon or nutmeg.
