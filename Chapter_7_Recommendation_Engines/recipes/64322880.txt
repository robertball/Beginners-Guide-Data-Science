Title:
The Ultimate Cheesy Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2023564.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lbs dried macaroni
1/3 C grated Cheddar
1/3 C grated Swiss
1/3 C grated Jack
1/3 C grated Colby
1/3 C grated Muenster
1/3 C grated Gouda
1/2 C Sour Cream
4 TBLS Butter, cut into pieces
3 Eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 C Milk
Nonstick spray
1/4 cup chopped flat-leaf parsley
1/4 lbs bacon, cut crosswise into thin strips
1 large onion, diced
2 garlic cloves, smashed
1/4 bunch fresh thyme

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F
In a large pot, bring water to boil. Add salt and cook macaroni until al dente, about 10 minutes.
Drain pasta and place in a large bowl and while pasta is still hot add all the cheese. Toss.
In a separate medium bowl, using a whisk, combine the sour cream, butter, eggs, salt, pepper, and milk and add to the macaroni mixture.
Heat a saut√© pan. Add the bacon, render the fat and cook until crispy. Add onion, garlic and thyme and cook for about 5 minutes to soften the onion. Season with salt and pepper. Fold into the macaroni and cheese.
Spray casserole dish with nonstick and pour macaroni mixture into the dish.
Bake for 30 to 45 minutes or until golden brown.
Top with additional cheese, if desired.
