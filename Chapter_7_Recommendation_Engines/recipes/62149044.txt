Title:
Trishie's Baby Munions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Baby Onions (Shallots)
16 Baby Mushrooms
1 Tbs Butter
1/4 tsp Garlic Powder

Directions:
Cut the Baby Onions (shallots) in half.
Put the butter into a pan and melt it over medium heat.
Add shallotts and sautee for 3 minutes.
Add mushrooms and increase the heat.
Sprinkle the Garlic Powder over the Mushrooms & Onion and stir in thoroughly.
Stir the mushrooms and onions frequently for the next 10-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Trishie
Description: 
A mixture of baby mushrooms & baby onions sauteed in butter with a hint of garlic. (MU - from Mushrooms & NIONS - from onions = Munions!!)
