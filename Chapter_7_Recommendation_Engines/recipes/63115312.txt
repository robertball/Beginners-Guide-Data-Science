Title:
Slow Cooker Cheesy Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/792298.jpg
Ingredients:
Yellow Rice (Zatarains New Orleans style yellow rice, makes 4 cups cooked)
1 Can Cream of Chicken
1 Can of Corn
4 Chicken Breasts
Mild Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 onion, chopped

Directions:
1.  Place chicken breasts in your slow cooker.  Top with cream of chicken soup and onion.  Cook on low 6-8 hours depending on your slow cooker.
2.  When the chicken is finished cooking, cook the rice according to package directions.
3.  Add the cooked rice, drained corn, and cheddar cheese to the slow cooker.  Cook on high for 30-45 minutes. Until cheese is melted and everything is heated through.
