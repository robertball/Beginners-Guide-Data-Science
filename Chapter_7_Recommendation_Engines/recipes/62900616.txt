Title:
Tiramisu Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cake:
1 package white cake mix
1 package vanilla pudding
3 eggs
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 1/4 c water
coffee:
1 cup lightly sweetened double shot coffee/espresso of choice
3 T liquor of choice, I prefer amaretto.
filling:
8 oz cream cheese, softened (prefer neufchatel)
3 T coffee or coffee liquor
1 cup powdered sugar
frosting:
1 tub cream cheese frosting
or prepare and beat:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
2 cups powdered sugar
1 t vanilla extract
garnish
24 chocolate covered espresso beans
1/2 cup chocolate shavings

Directions:
line cup cake pan with paper cupcake cups
fill 2/3 full
bake at 350 degrees for 18-23 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean
cool cupcakes and pierce with fork
mix liquor and coffee then brush liberally on each cupcake
Fill pastry/icing bag with  filling & inject about 1 teaspoon into center of each cupcake.
frost each cupcake
garnish with a chocolate covered espresso bean and chocolate shavings

Submitter: 
Michele Weyland
Description: 
I love Tiramisu, it is such an indulgent treat, this is an easy recipe that makes sharing this dessert a treat too.
