Title:
Dad's Ham & Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter;
1/4 cup flour;
1/2 tsp salt;
2 cups milk;
1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce;
4-5 potatoes, peeled, sliced, cooked;
2 cups cooked ham chunks;
1/2 cup shredded cheddar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Cook potatoes. Melt butter in saucepan. Add flour and salt, stir until blended. Slowly add milk. Cook several minutes, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens. Stir in Worcestershire sauce, potatoes and ham. Transfer to casserole dish and sprinkle top with cheese. Bake 30-40 minutes.
