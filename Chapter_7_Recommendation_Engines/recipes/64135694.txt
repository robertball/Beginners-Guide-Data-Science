Title:
Paleo Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup tapioca flour
1 cup coconut flour
1 tbsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp baking soda
2 eggs
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup raw honey
3 medium ripe bananas
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
coconut spread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease bottom and sides of a loaf pan with coconut spread.
In a medium bowl, mash bananas.
Add eggs, water, honey, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
In a large bowl, combine tapioca and coconut flour, cinnamon, and baking soda.
Make a well in the dry ingredients and add wet ingredients. Mix well.
Pour batter into loaf pan and bake for 45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
