Title:
Pistachio Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Base:
50 Ritz cheese crackers, crushed
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
Filling:
1/2 cup milk
4 cups vanilla ice cream, softened
2 pkg. instant pistachio pudding
1/2 tsp. vanilla
Topping:
1 pkg. dessert topping mix, prepared with milk, as per pkg. instructions
or
1 cup whipping cream, whipped with 1 tsp. vanilla & 2 tbsp. sugar

Directions:
Mix base ingredients. Save 1/2 cup for topping.
Press remainder into 9" x 13" pan.
Bake @ 350 x 15 - 20 minutes.
Cool well.
Beat filling ingredients together well and pour onto cooled crust.
Top with prepared whipped topping.
Sprinkle reserved crumbs over top.
Chill until set.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt

A unique taste combo of cheese and pistachio found in this high school home-ec recipe.
