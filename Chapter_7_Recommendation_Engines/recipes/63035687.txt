Title:
Christmas Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/762038.jpg
Ingredients:
1 part apple brandy
1 part apple juice
3 parts sparkling wine
To garnish
apple peel
frozen raspberries

Directions:
Combine the apple brandy and juice in a glass. Top up with sparkling wine and garnish with apple peel and the frozen raspberries.

Submitter: 
TheBritishBaker
Description: 
This recipe looks so festive with the red colour of the raspberries.  This recipe wa taken from Nigel Slater's Christmas show, broadcasted on BBC.
