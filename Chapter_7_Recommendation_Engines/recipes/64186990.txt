Title:
Pumpkin Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic
1 1/2 c. canned pumpkin
1 1/2 c. chickpeas
3 T. tahini
1 clove garlic
1/2 tsp. cayenne (or to taste)
1 tsp. cumin
some green chile (to taste)
2 T. olive oil
2 T. lemon juice
salt and pepper

Directions:
In a food processor, chop garlic, then add pumpkin and chickpeas and process until fairly smooth. add remaining ingredients and process. Season to taste.
