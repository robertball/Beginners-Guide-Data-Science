Title:
Mom's Brown Sugar Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter
½ cup crisco
1 box and 1 cup brown sugar
5 eggs
1 cup milk
3 ½ cups flour
½ tsp baking powder
1 ½ tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.  Grease and flour bundt pan.
Cream butter and shortening until fluffy.
Add sugar 1 cup at a time and cream thoroughly.
Add eggs one at a time and beat thoroughly after each addition.
Mix in flour/baking powder and milk/water alternating, starting with a third of the flour and then half of the liquid and so on finishing with flour, mixing completely between each addition.
Add vanilla.
Continue to mix another minute.
Pour into prepared bundt pan. Note: you can also use 2 loaf pans
Bake at 325 for 1 ½ hours in bundt pan.
