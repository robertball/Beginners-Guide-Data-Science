Title:
No Time to Cook Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of broccoli soup
1/2 cup sour cream
ADD: In the beginning add onion, garlic powder,
ADD: broccoli last 30 min

Directions:
Place chicken breasts in a slow cooker. In a medium bowl, mix the cream of chicken soup and cream of celery soup until smooth Pour over the chicken, making sure it is well coated.
Cover, and cook on Low heat for 7 to 8 hours. Stir in the sour cream about 1/2 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
cookless cook
Description: 
Chicken, creamy soups, and sour cream in a slow cooker - cook all day, ready for you when you get home. Only 4 ingredients! Everyone loves this, a no hassle dinner good enough for company.Modified from: No Time to Cook Chicken
