Title:
Classic Southern Potato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/427545.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 large baking potatoes
3 boiled eggs
5 tablespoons Kraft Real mayonnaise
3 tablespoons yellow mustard
5 tablespoons dill relish or dill chunks
1/4 onion chopped
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel and cut potatoes into cubes.
Boil potatoes and eggs together about 15 minutes until potatoes are somewhat soft and eggs will be hard boiled.
Drain potatoes & eggs.
Rinse, peel and cube the boiled eggs.
Gently mix potatoes, eggs, mayonnaise, mustard, dill relish and onion together until combined.
Add black pepper to taste.

Submitter:

Description: 
This classic southern potato salad is always the first dish emptied at our family get togethers.
