Title:
Cranberry Cornmeal Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter - softened
1 cup flour
1/2 cup fine yellow cornmeal
1 tsp. baking powder
1/8 tsp. salt
1/4 cup orange zest
3/4 cup dried cranberries - chopped
1 1/4 cups sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
4 large egg yolks
2 large eggs
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Butter and flour a 9" round cake pan.
Whisk together dry ingredients and orange zest.
Toss cranberries with 3 tbsp. of the flour mixture.
Beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
Add vanilla and then eggs and yolks, one at a time.
Gradually add flour mixture and mix until just incorporated.
Gently fold in cranberries.
Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth surface with a spatula.
Bake about 40 minutes or until cake is golden brown and tests done in center.
Cool for 20 minutes then remove cake from pan and allow to cool completely.
Dust with powdered sugar before serving.
