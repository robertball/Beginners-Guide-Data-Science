Title:
Suppli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/802459.jpg
Ingredients:
Prepared Risotto
Eggs
Flour
Bread Crumbs
Parmesan Cheese
Tomato Sauce
Oil for Frying

Directions:
Mix together the Risotto, tomato sauce, and flour, the mixture should be sticky.
Take a chunk of mozzerella, and place it in the center of a golf ball size amount of risotto. Roll into a tight ball, making sure that the cheese is completly covered by the rice. The rice should hold its shape.
Mix together equal parts of bread and parmesan cheese. Set aside.
Cover rice ball in flour, then dip in whipped eggs, coating well.
Gently shake off excess egg, and roll the roll in the bread and parmesan mixture.
Fry the ball untill golden brown.
Can be served with a marinara sauce

Suppli is a traditional Roman treat made of risotto and mozzerella.
