Title:
Coriander and Almond Crusted Scallops with Sauteed Spinach and Watercress Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauteed Spinach & Watercress
1 tbs EVOO
2 C fresh watercress
8 C fresh spinach
sea salt
Coriander & Almond Crusted Scallops
6 large scallops
1/2 C white wine
2 pinches of sea salt
1/4 C raw almonds
1 tbs coriander seed
pinch of black pepper
1/2 tbs grape seed oil
2 tsp balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Sauteed Spinach & Watercress
In a large saute pan heat the EVOO on medium heat.
Add the watercress and saute till tender, about 3 minutes.
Remove the pan from the heat and add the spinach to wilt.
Season with salt and serve with Scallops.
Coriander & Almond Crusted Scallops
Preheat oven to 375*.  Remove the tough muscle from the scallops, rinse and pat dry.
Combine the wine and a pinch of salt and place the scallops in this marinade for 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, place the almonds on a baking sheet and bake for 7-8 minutes.
In a food processor coarsely grind the coriander seeds.  Add the toasted almonds, another pinch of salt, and the pepper and grind coarsely.
Remove the scallops from the marinade and coat each side with the almond mixture.
Heat the oil in a skillet on medium - high heat and fry the scallops for 2-3 minutes on each side.
Splash with balsamic vinegar and serve with Sauteed Spinach and Watercress
