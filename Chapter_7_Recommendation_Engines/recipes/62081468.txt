Title:
David's easy beef goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small can tomato paste,1 packet, onion soup mix, 3 lbs. stew meat, 2 cups water

Directions:
Sear the stew meat. Add all of the indgredients and bring to a boil. Cover and reduce heat. The longer you cook the meat the more tender it becomes. Serve over noodles or potatos.
