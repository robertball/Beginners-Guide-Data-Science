Title:
Fluffy Chocolate Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, room temperature
3/4 cup butter, softened
5 to 6 cups confectioner's sugar
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
4 oz. semi-sweet chocolate, melted and cooled slightly
1/8 tsp salt
2 tsp milk (as needed)

Directions:
Cream cream cheese and butter together thoroughly.
Blend in vanilla, cocoa, melted chocolate.
Blend in confectioner's sugar, 1/2 cup at a time, mixing thoroughly after each addition.
Add milk, as needed, to obtain desired texture.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
I ran out of cocoa (only had 1/2 cup) for my regular frosting recipe...and unsweetened chocolate for my second favorite recipe, so I came up with this one - it whipped up light, fluffy and delicious!
