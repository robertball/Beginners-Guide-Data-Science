Title:
Quick Potato Salad from Hidden Valley® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2497418.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds new potatoes or red potatoes, cut into 1-inch cubes
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Bacon Dressing
2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives

Directions:
In a large pot over a medium high heat, add the potatoes, salt, and enough water to cover.
Once boiling, cook the potatoes for 10 to 15 minutes or until tender when pierced with a fork.
Drain and set aside to cool slightly.
In a large bowl, mix the potatoes and Hidden Valley(R)Bacon Ranch Dressing together until well-coated. Chill covered for at least 1 hour. Overnight is best.
Serve garnished with the chives.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley®
Description: 
Hidden Valley® Bacon Ranch Dressing is key to this quick, easy and delicious potato salad.
