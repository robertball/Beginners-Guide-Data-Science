Title:
Vegan Spanish Rice Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3c rice, prepared
1 lg red bell pepper, diced
1/2 lg onion, diced
1 md Asian eggplant, diced
1 md yellow summer squash, diced
3T capers
16oz can Roman or Pinto beans
olive oil
salt
garlic mill
whole mustard seeds
hot pepper flakes
cilantro, dried
cumin

Directions:
Place the bell pepper and onion in a large frying pan.  Add enough olive oil to coat them and the pan but not enough to leave excess.
Cover and place on medium heat.  When the onions begin to become translucent, add the eggplant and squash.  Re-cover.
When the veggies are thoroughly cooked, add the capers and beans.  Season to taste with all seasonings.
When the beans are thoroughly warmed, remove from heat and add the rice.  Stir well, re-seasoning to taste.  Serve.
