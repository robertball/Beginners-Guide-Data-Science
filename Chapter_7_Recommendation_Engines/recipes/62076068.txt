Title:
Fast & Easy Chicken Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. cooked rotini
1 Rotisserie chicken
2 celery stalks, finely chopped
1 can, cream of mushroom soup
2 1/4 cups milk
1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup red bell pepper, finely chopped
1 cup cheddar cheese
2 onions, chopped
2 TBSP. butter

Directions:
Remove meat from chicken and chop up.  In a heavy saucepan, melt butter, then cook onions, celery and pepper for 5 minutes.  Add milk, soup and seasonings.  Cook over medium-low heat until bubbly.  Add chicken and half of cheese, remove from heat. In 13x9x2 pan, spray non-stick cooking spray.  Place rotini in pan.  Pour mixture over pasta.  Sprinkle rest of cheese over it.  Bake uncovered for 25 minutes at 350 degrees.
