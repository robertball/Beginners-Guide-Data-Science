Title:
Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4502432.jpg
Ingredients:
Fettuccine noodles
Boneless, skinless chicken breasts cut into stripps (or pre-cooked, frozen, seasoned chicken).
1 8 oz Heavy whipping cream
Parmesan cheese
salted butter
garlic
parsley

Directions:
Boil a pot of water and add fettuccine noodles. Use a large pan to heat chicken thoroughly. Mince garlic and parsley. Use a medium pot to melt 1 stick of butter; when butter is melted add minced garlic and heat for approx 3-5 min/or until thoroughly heated. Add 8 oz bottle of whipping cream; heat thoroughly. Add whole container of parmesan cheese until slightly thick, then add parsley. Drain noodles, add chicken and sauce and serve.

Submitter: 
Danielle Livingston
Description: 
Creamy Chicken fettuccine with homemade Alfredo sauce. Dive into this delicious dish and enjoy all fresh ingredients.
