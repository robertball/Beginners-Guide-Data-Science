Title:
Glazed Dill Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1039156.jpg
Ingredients:
9 cups peeled and sliced carrots
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/8 cup chopped fresh dill
1 teaspoon salt (optional - salt to taste)
1/2 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper

Directions:
Put carrots in pan, add water to cover. Bring to a boil over medium heat and simmer.  Check for tenderness.
Simmer until water has evaporated and the carrots are tender.  If water nearly gone and carrots not tender, add 1/2 cup water, bring to boil, and continue simmering.
Stir in butter, brown sugar, dill, salt, and pepper.

Description: 
My family loves carrots, and this recipe is simple, easy to make, and delicious!  This is a variation of the Maple Dill Carrots that is a bit more favorable to the health-conscious while maintaining all the flavor.Modified from: Maple Dill Carrots
