Title:
Gingerbread- semi-homemade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box spice cake mix
1/2 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup molasses
1/3 cup vegetable oil
4 large eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees
mix eggs-buttermilk- molasses- oil- eggs - til combined-
add ginger- cinnamon and cake mix- mix 2 minutes-
pour into prepared 9 X 13 pan(i just spray with cooking spray)-
bake 20 minutes- rotate- bake 20 more minutes.
