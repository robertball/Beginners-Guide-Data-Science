Title:
Tilapia with olives and peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs Olive Oil
4 6oz tilapia filets
2 thinly sliced red bell peppers
1 medium onion thinly sliced
3/4 cup green olives
1/2 cup fresh flat leaf parsley, chopped
2 Tbs fresh lime juice
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat 1 Tbs of the oil in a large non stick skillet over medium-high heat. Season both sides of the fish with salt and pepper and cook until opaque throughout. about 5 minutes each side
Meanwhile heat the remaining tablespoon of oil in a second large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the peppers and onion stirring often, until tender, 8-10 minutes. Stir in the olives, parsley, and lime juice along with additional salt and pepper if desired. Serve over the fish.

Submitter: 
DaraLynn Ketcham
Description: 
I came across this awesome recipe in a magazine a few years back and it immediately became a family favorite. THe surprising combination of fish and bell peppers along with green olives and onions make this a flavorful savory main dish
