Title:
Meatloaf Minis Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb extra lean ground beef
1 pkg (6 oz) stuffing mix
1 tsp garlic powder
3/4 cup barbecue sauce
3/4 cup mild cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 375F. Mix meat, stuffing mix, 1 cup water and garlic powder. Press into 12 muffin cups sprayed with cooking spray. Make an indentation in the center of each mini loaf with a spoon; fill with sauce. Bake for 30 minutes or until cooked through (160F). Top with cheese, bake for 5 minutes more or until cheese melts.
