Title:
Floating cucumber, tomato and onion salad. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/450243.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
1 large sweet onion, thinly sliced
1 med to lrg tomatoe, diced with soft meat of tomato removed
1 1/2 cups sugar (or "Splenda" type sweetner)
1 cup white vinegar
1/4 cup Balsamic vinegar
1 1/2 tablespoons salt
1 teaspoon celery seed
Container: 2 quart container
Prep Time: 15 minutes

Directions:
Place prepared cucumbers, onions, and tomatoes in a 2 quart container. Sprinkle with salt. In a seperate small bowl, combine sugar, vinegar, and celery seed. Whisk together and pour over cucumber mixture.
Cover and refrigerate 12 hours before serving for best results.

Submitter: 
Reuben Howell
Description: 
This is a refreshing summer dish, served nice and cold.  Perfect on a hot Summer's day, however my family loves it year round.
