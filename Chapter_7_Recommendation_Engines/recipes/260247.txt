Title:
Easy Honey Herbed Goat Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4557523.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces goat cheese
1 tablespoon shallot, minced
1 1/2 tablespoons Aunt Sue’s® Raw & Unfiltered Honey, plus more to finish
1 tablespoon creme fraiche (optional)
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
2 teaspoons chopped fresh chives, plus optional chive batons to garnish
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Fold all ingredients well to combine.
Serve with toasted baguette and drizzle with Aunt Sue's® Raw & Unfiltered Honey.
Garnish with chive batons if desired.

Submitter: 
Aunt Sue’s® Raw Honey
Description: 
Impress your guests with this easy-to-make, yet delicious appetizer.
