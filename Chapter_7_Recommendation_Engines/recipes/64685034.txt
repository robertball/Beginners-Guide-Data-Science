Title:
Goulash Soup by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5007087.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
Seasoning such as Lowarys, garlic powder, onion powder, celery salt, pepper
1/2 small onion, chopped
3 tsp minced garlic
7 c beef broth or water (usually 4c broth and remainder water)
3 14oz  cans diced Tomatoes
6-8oz tomato sauce
2 TB Worcestershire sauce
2 cups dry macaroni
Cheese (if desired)

Directions:
In a large stock pot, add ground beef, garlic and onion, seasoning â€“ cook until beef is no longer pink. Drain the grease.
Add broth/water, cans of chopped tomatoes, sauce,  Worcestershire. Bring to a boil and simmer for 8-10 minutes
Add the dry macaroni and let cook for 15 minutes.
Top with cheddar cheese.
Experiment with different seasonings and tomatoes such as Italian.

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
Twist on a tradtional comfort food. Turn simple goulash into soul warming soup.
