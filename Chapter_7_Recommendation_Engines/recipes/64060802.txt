Title:
Oriential Dressing for Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons honey
1 1/2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon Grey Poupon Dijon Mustard
1/8 teaspoon sesame oil

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a blender and blend well.
Store in lidded glass jar in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Celeste
Description: 
Simple oriental dressing for any salad.
