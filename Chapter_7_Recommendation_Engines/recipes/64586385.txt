Title:
Spaghetti with Spinach-Avocado Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz uncooked whole-grain spaghetti
1 cup spinach leaves
1/4 cup rinsed, drained unsalted cannellini beans
1/4 cup fresh basil
2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
2 tsp grated lemon rind
1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tsp kosher salt
2 garlic cloves
1 ripe peeled avocado
1/4 cup chopped tomato
2 Tbsp sliced toasted almonds

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions, omitting salt and fat. Reserve 1/2 cup cooking liquid. Drain pasta.
Combine reserved 1/2 cup cooking liquid, spinach, and next 8 ingredients (through avocado) in food processor; process until smooth. Combine pasta and spinach mixture; toss to coat. Sprinkle with tomato and almonds.
