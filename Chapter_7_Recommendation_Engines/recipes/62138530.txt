Title:
Mandarin Sunset Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. mandarin vodka
1 1/2 oz. orange liqueur (Grande Marnier/Triple Sec)
Splash of mandarin orange juice
Splash of cranberry juice
Mandarin orange segments to garnish glass

Directions:
Mix all ingredients over ice in a shaker & shake.  Drop a few mandarin organe segments in martini glass & pour in drink.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Mommy Epstein
Description: 
This mandarin martini is light & fruity, yet luscious & sweet!
