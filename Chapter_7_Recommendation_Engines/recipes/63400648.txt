Title:
Simmered Cabbage with Beef, Shan Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBS Peanut Oil
1 Cup thinly sliced challots
1 TSP Salt
1 TSP Turmeric
1/2 TSP ground red pepper
1/4 LB (or more) ground sirloin
4 Cups shredded Cabbage
1 Cup thin plum or cherry tomato wedges
1/3 Cup roughly chopped unsalted roasted peanuts

Directions:
1. Heat Wok or dutch oven over med heat
add oil, swirl to coat and add shallots, salt, turmeric, and red pepper cook 3 min or until shallots are tender
Add beef, cook 2 minutes or until beef begins to brown
Add cabbabe and tomatos and toss. Reduce heat to med-low cover and cook 10 minutes or until cabbage wilts
Stir in peanuts, cover and cook 10 minutes until cabbage is tender
Serve with rice
