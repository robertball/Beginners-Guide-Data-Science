Title:
Summer Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups macaroni
Vegetables:
2 ripe tomatoes
1 green onion
1/2 green pepper
1/4 cucumber
Sauce:
2/3 cup sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/3 cup ketchup
1/4 cup vinegar
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp paprika

Directions:
Boil macaroni.
While it is cooking, chop up all vegetables and put in a bowl.
Once macaroni is cooked and rinsed, add it to the bowl of veggies. Mix all sauce ingredients together in a separate bowl.
Combine that well and pour over macaroni mixture and stir in.

Submitter: 
tojo5
Description: 
Quick and easy. Tastes best when it sits for a while before serving.
