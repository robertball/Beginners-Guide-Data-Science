Title:
Pumpkin torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/911952.jpg
Ingredients:
5 eggs, separated
1 C. Granulated sugar
1 1/2 C. Pumpkin puree
1 C. All purpose flour
1/2 tsp. Cinnamon
1/2 tsp. Ginger
3/4 tsp. Baking Powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 (163 C)  degrees fahrenheit
Grease and flour a 9 " springform pan and set aside
In a small bowl separate eggs and  put whites aside for later use.
In a large mixing bowl beat egg yolks until light and yellow coloured
Add sugar to the egg yolks and beat until nice and smooth
In a separate medium size bowl beat the egg whites until frothy and light and set them aside until you are ready to add them to the egg mixture
Pour the pumpkin puree into the mixture and beat with an electric mixer on medium speed for 4 to 5 minutes
In a medium size bowl combine flour, spices and Baking powder
Pour the dry ingredients into the egg mixture and beat until light and fluffy
Gently fold in the beaten egg whites and completely incorporate them into the batter
Pour the batter into the greased and floured springform pan and put in oven for 35 minutes
When lightly browned take out and let sit for 5 minutes before removing the ring from the sides.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very light and tasty cake with the goodness of pumpkin and spices that make it a like a fancy pie that is much easier to make.
