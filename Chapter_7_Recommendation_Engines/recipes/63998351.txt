Title:
Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 3/4 cup butter, softened
* 1 cup packed brown sugar
* 1/2 cup granulated sugar
* 1 teaspoon baking powder
* 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
* 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
* 2 eggs
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour (or self rising and omit the baking powder and baking soda)
* 2 cups rolled oats
* 1 cup raisins

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl beat butter with an electric mixer on medium to high speed for 30 seconds. Add brown sugar, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and if desired cinnamon and cloves. Beat until combined, scrapping sides of bowl occasionally. Beat in eggs and vanilla until combined. Beat in as much of the flour as you can with the mixer. Stir in any remaining flour. Stir in rolled oats. Then stir in the 1 cup of raisins (or snipped dried tart cherries).
Drop dough by rounded teaspoons 2 inches apart onto an ungreased  cookie sheet. Bake in a 375 degree f oven for 8 to 10 minutes or until edges are golden. Cool on cookie sheet for 1 minute. Transfer to a wire rack and let cool.
