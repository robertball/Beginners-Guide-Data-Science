Title:
Stove-Top "Roasted" Red Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Large Red Potatoes
3 Tablespoons Butter
2 Teaspoons Salt
1 Teaspoon Pepper
3/4 Teaspoon Dried Parsley
1/2 Teaspoon Dried Basil
1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1/2 Teaspoon Onion Powder

Directions:
1. Wash potatoes and slice into bite-sized pieces.
2. Place cut potatoes into a glass microwave safe bowl and microwave for 8 minutes.
3. Remove from microwave and allow to cool for several minutes.
4. Heat skillet over medium heat.
5.  Add butter. Once melted, add potatoes.
6.  Add seasonings to potatoes, to-taste.
7. Cook for about 6-8 minutes, or until the potatoes are softened and browned.
8. Remove from heat and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Mason Longnecker
Description: 
Flavorful, delicious potatoes. Great for when oven-roasting isn't an option!
