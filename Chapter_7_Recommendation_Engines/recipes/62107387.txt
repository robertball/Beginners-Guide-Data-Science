Title:
Creamy Chicken Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet spaghetti
2 chicken breasts, cut into bite sized
pieces.
8 mushrooms, finely chopped
1 onion, finely chopped
1 red capsicum, finely chopped
1 can diced tomatoes with juice
½ cup light tomato sauce
½ tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp chilli powder
½ tsp garlic powder
½ cup light sour cream

Directions:
Boil spaghetti in a pot.
In a frypan, sauté mushrooms, onion and capsicum.
Add the chicken pieces, cooking for 5-7 minutes until chicken is browned.
Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, cayenne pepper, chilli powder and garlic powder. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat and stir in sour cream.
Serve over cooked spaghetti in bowls.

Submitter: 
Chicken
Description: 
This is a quick weeknight diner guaranteed to warm you up. It is easy on the waistline, but still has a rich and creamy taste.
