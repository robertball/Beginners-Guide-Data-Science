Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2446435.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of baking mix
1/2 cup of cold water
3/4 cup of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1/4 cup of butter
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Combine baking mix, water, and cheese in a bowl.
Use spoon to dish mix onto an ungreased pan.  Unbaked biscuits should be approximately 1 inch in size.
Melt butter and mix in spices.
Brush the biscuits with the butter mix and bake for 8 to 10 minutes.
