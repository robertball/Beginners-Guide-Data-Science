Title:
Chocolate Nutella Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/692035.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 cups crushed vanilla wafers
2 T. dark cocoa
1/4 cup sugar
1/3 cup butter, melted
Filling:
3 8-oz. pkg. neufchatel cheese
1 cup sugar
3 medium eggs
1 13-oz. jar Nutella
Topping:
1 cup sour cream
1 cup chocolate chips, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine wafer crumbs, cocoa, sugar, and butter.  Press in bottom and up the sides of a 9" springform pan. Pake for 10 minutes.
Cream neufchatel cheese and sugar until smooth.  Add eggs, beat until smooth.  Add Nutella, beat until smooth.  Pour into crust.
Bake for 55 minutes.  Remove from oven for 5 minutes.
Combine sour cream and melted chocolate chips, spread over cheesecake.  Return to oven for 5 minutes.
Cool for 10 minutes.  Run knife around sides of pan. Continue cooling for at least 1 hour.
Refrigerate overnight to develop flavors.
To serve, remove sides, slice.  This may be frozen, if desired.

Description: 
With a chocolate crust and a chocolate topping, this baked Nutella cheesecake appeals to chocolate lovers!
