Title:
Healthy and Easy Lemon Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/970251.jpg
Ingredients:
•1 15 oz. can chickpeas (aka garbanzo beans)
•1 teaspoon minced garlic
•3 tablespoons lemon juice (from fresh lemon)
•3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (darker the better)
•1 tablespoon sunflower oil
•1 tablespoon tahini
•1/2 teaspoon lemon zest (from fresh lemon)

Directions:
Drain liquid from can of chickpeas
Combine all ingredients into food processor and blend for 1 minute. Stir with spoon. Continue to blend for 3-5 minutes until smooth.
Pour into serving bowl and serve with baked pita chips or raw veggies.

Submitter: 
Desire For Fit
Description: 
There is nothing more refreshing than the taste of lemon. This lemon hummus will make eating raw veggies or low fat pita chips lots of fun without the guilt!
