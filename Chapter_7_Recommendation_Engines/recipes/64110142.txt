Frozen Blueberry Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients:
1 cup Frozen Blueberries
3/4 cup Greek Yogurt
1 Peeled Kiwi
3/4 cup 3% Milk
Sweetener:2 teaspoons raw sugar (or 1 packet Stevia Sweetener)

Directions:
Sweetener: The best sweetener is Stevia, if you must use real sugar use raw sugar.
Ensure that the berries are frozen, it's frozen in replacement of ice. You can use regular yogurt, but Greek Yogurt contains more protein.
