Title:
July 4th Tasty Cool Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10.25oz.Package Yellow Cake Mix
1 8oz. Package Regular Cool Whip
10 Strawberries
1/2 cup  Blueberries
1/8 cup Rum
2 teaspoons Granulated Sugar

Directions:
Bake cake according to directions on box
Slice strawberries in thirds and put them into a bowl with the sugar and rum, let soak
Let baked cake cool and then slice in half -width wise
Spread the cool whip onto the tops of the cake halves
Arrange fruits onto the cake halves, making one more visually appealing
Lift the cake half with the visually appealing design and place on top of the other half (making a layer of cool whip in between them)
Refrigerate until time to serve.

Submitter: 
dsilveira
Description: 
This cake is a snap to make and tastes great. Great way to use all the fruit you have in your house. Very cool and refreshing!
