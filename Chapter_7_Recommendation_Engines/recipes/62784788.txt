Title:
Asian-Alaska Salmon Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14.5 oz can of salmon, drained and chunked
1 package fresh stir fry vegetables
1 package vegetable or chicken flavored ramen noodles
4 green onions sliced
1 TBL fresh gated ginger or 1 tsp ground ginger
2 cloves garlic
1 TBL soy sauce
1 tsp rice wine vinegar
1/2 tsp sesame oil

Directions:
Bring 5 cups of water to a boil in a large sauce pan. Add stir fry mix cook for 3 minutes. break raen noodles into 4 pieces stir into water. Add onions, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, sesame oil and ramen seasoning simmer for 3 minutes. Stir in salmon heat throughly. Serve immediately.
