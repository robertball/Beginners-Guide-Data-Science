Title:
Singapore Sling Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint Gin
1/2 cup cherry brandy
4 cups water
1/2 cup grenadine
1/2 cup cherry juice
1/2 cup lemon juice
2&1/2 cups pineapple juice
1 can frozen pink lemonade
1/2 cup white sugar

Directions:
Mix all the above ingredients together, in a 4 Litre ice cream container.Stir and place in the freezer. During the first 24 hours, stir the mixture at least twice.
To serve, place 4tbsp. of the slush into a glass and fill the glass with lemon lime pop. Serve with a straw garnish with a cherry. Enjoy!
