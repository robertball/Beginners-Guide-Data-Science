Title:
Cheesy Hashbrown Bake by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2014156.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large bag frozen shredded hashbrowns
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp garlic powder or Alpine Touch seasoning.
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 can cream of potato soup
8 ounce sour cream
1/4 c milk
1-2 cups finely crushed crunchy topping: bugles, corn flakes, cheese crackers, croutons

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Place potatoes in greased or Pammed 9 x13 baking dish
Combine melted butter, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onions, cheese, sour cream and soup in large bowl. Mix well and spread over potatoes evenly. Optional: sprinkle with Lowrys seasoned salt.
Add crushed topping on top of mixture.
Bake uncovered 45 minutes, until bubbly and potatoes are tender.  Let set 5-10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
I based this off a different potato casserole recipe, but made some of my own changes and tweaks and it was fabulous!
