Title:
Ginger Snaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup of butter
2 cups of sugar
2 well beaten eggs
1/2 cup molasses
2 tsp vinegar
3 3/4 cups of flour
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
2-3 tbsp of ginger
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp cloves

Directions:
Mix ingredients until blended
Form dough into 3/4 of an inch sized balls (dough should be sticky)
Place on a greased cookie sheet and bake for 12 minutes.  As the cookies bake, they will develop the characteristic crinkled surface.

Submitter: 
charlotte henry
Description: 
Yummy cruchy ginger snaps. Great with a nice hot cup of milky sweet tea or coffee!
