Title:
Baked Teriyaki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon cold water
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
Cooked tofu pieces, tempeh, or cauliflower wings

Directions:
In a small saucepan over low heat, combine the cornstarch, cold water, sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, ginger and ground black pepper. Let simmer, stirring frequently, until sauce thickens and bubbles.
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Place tofu, tempeh or cauliflower pieces in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Brush with the sauce. Turn pieces over, and brush again.
Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Turn pieces over, and bake for another 15 minutes. Brush with sauce every 10 minutes during cooking.
