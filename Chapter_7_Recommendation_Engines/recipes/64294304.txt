Title:
Gluten-Free Crépes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1940272.jpg
Ingredients:
•1½ cups (7 oz) Gluten-Free Multi-Purpose Flour (such as MixoFlour)
•¼ teaspoon salt
•6 large eggs
•3 cups milk
•2 tablespoons butter, melted and cooled.

Directions:
1.In a blender combine all the ingredients and process until smooth.
2.Heat a 10-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot and brush with oil. Add ¼ cup of the batter to the skillet and tilting the skillet to swirl the batter around until it covers the bottom of the pan. When the top of the crépe is dry, about 2 minutes, flip it and cook the other side for 15 to 20 seconds. Repeat the process, adjusting the heat as needed, until all the batter is used.

Submitter: 
antally
Description: 
Simple, delicious and versatile. You can fill them with fruit for breakfast/desert or fill them with meat and vegetable for dinner. You don't need to oil the pan each time until the pan starts to stick.
