Title:
Best Cabbage Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups chopped cabbage
1 lb browned ground beef
two cans kidney beans (lite or dark)
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can tomato paste
1 tbls worcestershire sauce

Directions:
cover chopped cabbage with water and simmer until cabbage is tender.
when cabbbage is ready, reduce heat to low or simmer - add ground beef, beans, tomatoes and tomato paste.  Let it simmer til you can't wait any longer. serve your self. enjoy.

Submitter: 
chemjo
Description: 
My mom and I used to get this soup at a Big Boy restaurant in Northern KY till it closed.  Them Mama D started making it herself.  Filling, wholesome, yummy - words don't describe!
