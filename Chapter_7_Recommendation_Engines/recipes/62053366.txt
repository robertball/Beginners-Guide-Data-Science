Title:
Grandmama's Tea Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar,
4 cups flour,
1/4 cup milk,
1 dash salt,
3 eggs,
2 tsp baking powder,
2 sticks butter.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix dry ingredients, except sugar.  Blend butter, sugar, eggs, and milk.  Combine with dry ingredients.  Knead mixture like biscuit dough.  Roll our on a floured surface, and cut into whatever shapes you like.  Bake 12-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
Grandmama made this for all the teas and luncheons.
