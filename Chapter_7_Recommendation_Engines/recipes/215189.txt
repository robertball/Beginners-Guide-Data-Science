Title:
Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/441727.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
1/2 cup yogurt
1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
1/4 cup pineapple juice
1 cup strawberries
1 teaspoon orange juice
1 teaspoon milk

Directions:
Blend the banana, yogurt, sugar, pineapple juice, strawberries, orange juice, and milk in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
This recipe is delicious! You may substitute the strawberries for any other berries or fruit.
