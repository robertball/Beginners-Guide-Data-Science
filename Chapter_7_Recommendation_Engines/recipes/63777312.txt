Title:
Chicken Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 package of ground chicken
- 1 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 garlic clove, crushed
- kosher salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tsp. freeze dried basil
- 1 14oz can of whole tomatoes
- 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese
- spaghetti noodles

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over med-high heat
Fry ground chicken and onions until chicken is cooked through and onions are tender.
Add salt, pepper, basil and crushed garlic and cook for 1 minute.
While chicken is cooking use a food processor to blend the tomatoes until smooth.
Add blended tomatoes to chicken in skillet.
Let simmer for 10 minutes then add parmesan cheese and stir.
Serve over al-dente spaghetti noodles
