Title:
Sugared Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1egg white
1tbsp water
1cup white sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/2tsp cinnamon
1pound walnuts (or pecans)

Directions:
In largebowl, whip egg white and water. Coat walnuts. Mix sugar, salt and cinnamon in separate bowl. Add spice mixture to walnuts and coat. Spread evenly on cookie sheet and bake at 250 degrees for 1 hour. Stir every 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
JacksDad
Description: 
This delicious recipe goes well with salads, crepes or is a tasty snack all by themselves!
