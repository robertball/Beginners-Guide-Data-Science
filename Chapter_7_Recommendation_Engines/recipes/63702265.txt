Title:
Macaroni and Ham Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14.5 oz can of Cream of Chicken soup
8 oz diced ham
8 oz of Shell macaroni
1/2 teaspoon of mustard
4 oz frozen corn
4 oz frozen carrots and peas
1 cup of milk
Pinch of pepper
1/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package.
Add cream of chicken and milk to an empty pot on low to medium heat.
Add frozen vegetables, pepper, mustard, and ham to soup and bring to boil.
Drain pasta when done and add to soup.
Stir well and add cheese
