Title:
Crock Pot Pepper Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs beef sirloin, cut in 2 inch strips
1/2 tsp garlic powder
3 tbs vegetable oil
1 beef bullion cube
1/2 cup hot water
1 tbs corn starch
1/2 cup chopped onion
28 oz can of stewed tomatoes, with liquid
3 tbs soya sauce
1 tsp sugar
2 large Red/Green Peppers cut into strips
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Sprinkle beef with garlic powder
In a large skillet heat oil over medium heat and brown the beef strips
Transfer to a slow cooker after browned
In a small bowl, Add the beef bullion cube and hot water, stir until dissolved
Mix in corn starch to the beef bullion and water and dissolve
Pour over meat in the slow cooker
Stir in onion, stewed tomatoes, soya sauce, and sugar
Cook on low for 6 hours or high for 3 to 4 hours
Add Red/Green Pepper strips for the last hour of cooking (so they do not get mushy)
