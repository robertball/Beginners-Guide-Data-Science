Title:
Seafood Tomato Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 medium onion, chopped
1 can sliced mushrooms
1/4 cup cooking sherry or white wine or mixture
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Mushroom
2T minced garlic
1/2 cup half and half
1 cup diced canned tomato, drained slightly
1 pound fresh fish fillet (any fish), cut into 2-inch pieces
4 cups hot cooked linguine or spaghetti

Directions:
Heat the butter in a 10-inch skillet over medium heat. Add the onion  and mushrooms and  minced garlic and cook until it's tender.  Add the cooking sherry during this process.
Stir in the soup, half and half and tomatoes. Heat to a boil. Add the fish to the skillet and reduce the heat to low. Cover and cook for 10 minutes or until the fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.
Serve over linguine.
