Title:
Fruity Energy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6866375.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup pitted whole dates
1/2 cup dried mango
2 scoops Emergen-C® Protein Fuel & Superfoods Pomegranate Mango
1/2 cup cashew butter, or more to taste
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup finely chopped roasted cashews

Directions:
Place dates and mango in a blender or food processor; pulse until very finely chopped. Add Emergen-C(R) Protein Fuel & Super Foods Pomegranate Mango; pulse to combine.
Add cashew butter and salt; pulse until well-combined and almost smooth.
Using your hands, roll 1 tablespoon of mixture into a ball, then roll ball in chopped cashews to coat. Repeat with remaining date mixture and cashews.

Submitter: 
Emergen-C®
Description: 
These energy bites combine Emergen-C® Protein Fuel & Superfoods Pomegranate Mango, dried fruits, and nut butter for a delicious snack that's packed with protein in every bite. Chopped cashews are the finishing touch to add a satisfying crunch.
