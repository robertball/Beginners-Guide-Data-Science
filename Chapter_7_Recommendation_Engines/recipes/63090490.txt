Title:
Deep Fried Mozzarella Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 mozzarella sticks (cut each in half)
1 cup flour
2 eggs
2 T water
1 cup panko

Directions:
In three bowls
Add flour into one bowl
Add eggs and water into another bowl
Add panko into another bowl
Working from left to right
dredge each cheese stick first in flour, then egg wash then panko.  Lay single layer on cookie sheet and freeze for at least an hour.
Deep fry at 350-375 for 2-3 minutes, until golden brown.  Watch carefully as soon as you see any sign of cheese drips, take out immediatly.

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
These come out perfect!  Very beautiful and crispy
