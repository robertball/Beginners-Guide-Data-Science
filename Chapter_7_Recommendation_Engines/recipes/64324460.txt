Title:
Mexican tomato flavored rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2028512.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of long grain rice
1 ½ tablespoons of Knorr tomato bouillon (powder form)
Couple dashes of onion powder
Couple dashes of garlic powder
2 ½ cups of water
2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

Directions:
Fry the rice in a saucepan till it is golden brown
Once the rice is golden brown add the water slowly, then add the tomato bouillion, garlic powder, and onion powder
Stir the mixture around and bring to a boil
Let the mixture boil for about 2 minutes then bring down the heat to low
Cover the saucepan and let it sit till the mixture is almist fully absorbed by the rice and then turn off the heat and let it sit in the saucepan about 5 minutes
Let it cool down and enjoy!
