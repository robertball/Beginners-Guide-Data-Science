Title:
Smoked Salmon-Caper-Dill Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976367.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/3 cup chopped fresh dill
2 teaspoons minced onion
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
12 ounces bow tie pasta, cooked
2 cucumbers, peeled, seeded, and cubed
6 cherry tomatoes, halved
5 ounces smoked salmon, cut into small pieces
1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
1/4 cup capers
Garnish: fresh dill sprigs

Directions:
WHISK together first 7 ingredients in a large bowl.  Toss with cooked pasta and next 5 ingredients.
COVER and chill.  Garnish, if desired.

Southern Living magazine
