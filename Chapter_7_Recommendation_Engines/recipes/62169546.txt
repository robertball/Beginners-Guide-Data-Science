Title:
Stella's Avocado Egg and Onion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/303873.jpg
Ingredients:
1 avocado, mashed
2 hard boiled eggs, mashed
1/4 to 1/2 a white onion, finely chopped
salt to taste
bread, toasted if you want.

Directions:
Mash eggs
add avocado, I use a fork
add finely chopped onion
salt to taste
Mix together well
Rip bread and dip.

Alicia
Description:
