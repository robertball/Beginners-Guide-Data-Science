Title:
Caramelized Onion, Sausage, & Goat Cheese Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/561358.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pie crust
1/2 large vidalia/sweet onion, chopped
3 Tbsp salted butter
1/2 pound spicy breakfast sausage
1/4 cup red, yellow, and green bell peppers, diced
5 eggs
1/2 cup half and half
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup goat cheese
1/4 cup cheddar cheese
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
*Preheat oven to 375*
1. Melt butter over medium low heat. Add onion, season with salt and pepper, and cook very slowly, stirring often, for about 20-25 minutes, until brown and very soft.
2. Cook sausage until done; set aside. Cook bell peppers until soft; set aside.
3. Beat eggs together with half and half, sour cream, salt, and pepper.
4. Line pie pan with crust and fill with onions, sausage, peppers, goat cheese, and 1/8 cup cheddar cheese.
5. Pour egg mixture into pie and top with remaining cheddar cheese.
6. Cook for 25-30 minutes or until fork inserted into middle comes out clean.
