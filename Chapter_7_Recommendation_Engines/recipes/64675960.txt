Title:
Parmesan Aioli/Whole Artichokes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup finely grated Parmesan
1 chopped garlic clove
1 tablespoon water
1/4 teaspoon each salt and pepper

Directions:
Fill large pot with enough water to reach bottom of steamer basket
Add 1 teaspoon of salt and bring to boil
Cut off top third of the artichoke
Peel stem and cut sharp tips off of leaves
Rub cut surfaces with half a lemon
Place artichokes stem side up with 2 lemon halves in basket
Cover and steam; 25 minutes
Make aioli for dipping

Great for dipping cooked whole artichokes
