Title:
Modified Old Fashioned Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2129423.jpg
Ingredients:
375 ml whole wheat flour
325 ml gold Medal self rising flour
25 ml Mrs Dash Southwestern Flavor (use original if sensitive to hot spices)
5 ml black pepper
1 large onion white or red
350- 500 ml water or 1% milk

Directions:
preheat fryer to 375 f. combine all dry ingredients and mix well
add the milk or water, until it forms a batter able to coat the onion rings
Slice the onion rings into 7 mm slices (using a slicer for best results)
add rings to batter tossing gently to coat then dip them by forkful into hot oil, and cook until both sides are golden brown or desired color is reached
Drain in a colander lined with paper towel

Submitter: 
Tony Carriere
Description: 
just like the classic onion rings only made with extra fiber (with the whole wheat flour)and with a southwestern kick
