Title:
The BEST Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/319558.jpg
Ingredients:
24 frozen hash brown triangle patties
4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
2 packages bacon or 1 package Italian sausage
8 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 onion
1 clove garlic
6 mushrooms sliced
1/2 teaspoon salt
pepper to taste
1/2 teaspoon ground mustard
a dash of nutmeg before putting in oven

Directions:
Place hash brown patties in a single layer in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish and bake 15 minutes. Sauté onion/garlic/mushroom in butter. Take hash browns out of oven and sprinkle with bacon or sausage, cheese & onion/garlic/mushroom mixture,.
Optional: Add dehydrated onion & garlic powder to egg mixture instead of sautéing mushrooms, onions and garlic.
In a bowl, beat eggs, milk, salt and mustard. Pour over ham.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees (175C)  for 45 minutes. Uncover; bake 15 minutes longer or until edges are golden brown and a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
