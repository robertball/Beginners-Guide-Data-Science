Title:
French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Six eggs, separate yolks
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp cinnamon
3/4 tsp cream of tartar

Directions:
separate yolks
add vanilla to yolks
add cinnamon to yolks
put cream of tartar in egg whites
whisk whites on high until stuff peaks form
stir yolks together
fold yolks into whites
coat bread and cook.
