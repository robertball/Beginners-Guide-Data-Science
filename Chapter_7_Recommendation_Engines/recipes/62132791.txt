Title:
Mexican Cornbread Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger
1 medium onion
1 package taco seasoning
1 can mexican style tomatoes, drained
1 can cream style corn
1 Cup colby cheese
1 package corn bread mix
eggs according to corn bread mix
milk according to corn bread mix

Directions:
Dice onion
Add hamburger and onion to skillet. Cook on medium high heat until hamburger is completely brown. Drain.
Add taco seasoning. Cook as directed on package.
Drain tomatoes. Add tomato and corn to hamburger. Cook to heat and mix. Pour into casserole dish. Top with cheese
Prepare cornbread as directed by package directions
Pour on top of meat mixture. Bake according to cornbread directions.
Top with sour cream or salsa as desired.
