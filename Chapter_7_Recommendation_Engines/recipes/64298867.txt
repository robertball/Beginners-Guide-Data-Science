Title:
Bread Pudding with Salted Caramel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1950271.jpg
Ingredients:
Bread Pudding:
5C (1/2") cubed french bread (about 8oz)
1C evaporated fat-free miilk
3/4C 1% milk
1/3C granulated sugar
2Tbsp bourbon
1Tbsp vanilla extract
1tsp cinnamon
1/4tsp kosher salt
2 large eggs
Sauce:
3/4C packed light brown sugar
3Tbsp boubon
1Tbsp unsalted butter
6Tbsp half and half, divided
1tsp vanilla extract
1/8tsp kosher salt
Cooking spray

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350
2. To prepare bread pudding, arrange bread in a single layer on a baking sheet.  Bake at 350 for 8 minutes or until lightly toasted.
3. Combine evaporated milk and next 7 ingredients (through eggs) in a large bowl; stir with a whisk.  Add bread cubes.  Let stand 20 minutes, occasionally pressing on bread to soak up milk.
4. To prepare sauce, combine brown sugar, 3 Tbsp bourbon, and butter in a small saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a boil.  Simmer 2 minutes or until sugar dissolves, stirring frequently.  Stir in 5Tbsp half and half; simmer 10 minutes or until reduced to about 1 Cup.  Remove pan from heat.  Stir in remianing 1 Tbsp half and half, 1 tsp vanilla and 1/8tsp salt.  Keep warm
5. Spoon half af bread mixture into a 9X5 inch loaf pan coated with cooking spray.  Drizzle 3 Tbsp sauce over bread mixture.  Spoon remaining half of bread misture over sauce.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until a kniff inserted in the center comes out clean.  Serve warm sauce with bread pudding.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
Succulent Bread Pudding with Salted Caramel Sauce has a perfectly moiste, creamy interior with a crisp outer crust.  The layer of bourbon-splashed caramel sauce in the middle of the bread pudding is the secret to this velvely rich interior.
