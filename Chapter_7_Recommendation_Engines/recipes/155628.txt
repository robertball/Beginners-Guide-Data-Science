Title:
Thai Chicken and Vegetable Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977597.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ounces skinless, boneless chicken breast halves or thighs
1 teaspoon curry powder
2 tablespoons oil
1 (14.5 ounce) can DEL MONTE速 Original Recipe Stewed Tomatoes
1 (14.5 ounce) can DEL MONTE速 Cut Green Beans, drained
1 (14.5 ounce) can DEL MONTE速 Sliced Carrots, drained
1/4 teaspoon red pepper, crushed
2 cups hot cooked rice

Directions:
Cut chicken into 3/4-inch cubes; toss chicken evenly with curry powder.
Heat oil in large skillet; add chicken. Cook and stir over medium-high heat 3 minutes.
Add undrained tomatoes, beans, carrots and crushed red pepper. Bring to boil; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, 5 minutes.
Serve over rice and sprinkle with 1/4 cup peanuts or toasted coconut, if desired.

In just 20 minutes, you can prepare a rich, satisfying chicken and vegetable curry dinner.
