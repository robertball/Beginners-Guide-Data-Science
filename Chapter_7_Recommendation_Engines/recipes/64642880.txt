Title:
VEGAN BANANA PANCAKES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4518699.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup oat1 cup soya milk 1/3 cup of flour1 large banana1 pinch of saltstrawberries and one banana for the toppingmaple syrupoil for cooking

Directions:
Start by cutting the banana in small pieces and melt it with a fork. Take a bowl and add all the ingredients : flour, oat, melted banana, soya milk and the salt. I used soya milk with vanilla flavour as to give my pancakes the vanilla flavor.Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over medium high heat.Start cooking your pancakes. Cook 2 to 3 minutes or until the edges are dry. With a spatula turn them and cook again for 2 to 3 minutes.Serve them hot with lots of maple syrup, strawberries and banana!
