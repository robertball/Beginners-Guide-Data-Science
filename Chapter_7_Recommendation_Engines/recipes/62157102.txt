Title:
Flax Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup whole flax seed
1/4 cup ground flax seed
1 1/2 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup skim milk

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingreadients together, work in butter.
Add milk bit by bit while stirring, til dough forms.
Divide in 4, wrap in plastic wrap and chill a minimum of 10 minutes or as long as overnight.
Roll out very thin and cut into wedges. (1/16" thick)  Bake at 325 for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Phyllis Trinke Funk
Description: 
Crunchy, tasty and simple to make.  These become a favorite wherever I take them.
