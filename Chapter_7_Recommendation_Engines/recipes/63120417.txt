Title:
Pasta Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 oz tomatoes chopped pomi
7 oz tomato paste organic
32 oz chicken stock organic
30 oz white beans
8 oz pecorino & grana pardano (50/50 mix)
5 oz spinach chopped frozen (1/2 box)
2 oz celery (sliced thin
-
1 Tbsp butter unsalted
3 slices bacon organic
4 cloves garlic minced
6 oz onion minced
-
8 oz ditalini pasta cooked
3 cups pasta water

Directions:
Slow cook first 7 ingredients
Cook butter & bacon in pan on med high, remove bacon & mince. Add garlic & onion to pan to sweat on medium. Add to slowcooker.
Cook for several hours on high
Cook pasta and add to slowcooker w/3 cups pasta water.
Cook for 1 hour on low
