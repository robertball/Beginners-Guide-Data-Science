Title:
Shake-n-Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb. (1 tube) Ritz crackers, crushed
1 tsp garlic salt
1 TBSP chili powder
1 tsp onion salt
1 tsp poultry seasoning
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup oregano

Directions:
Mix together in large bowl.  Dip chicken or pork in water or milk and coat well with mix.  Place in baking pan.  Bake at 400 degrees uncovered for about 30 minutes.  Cover and bake 15 to 20 minutes longer.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This is a better coating for chicken and pork than the kind you will find at the grocery store.  I usually double the recipe and store it in a ziploc baggie for later use.
