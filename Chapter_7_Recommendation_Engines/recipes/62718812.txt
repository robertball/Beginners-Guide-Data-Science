Title:
Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups frozen peas, thawed
1/2 cup cubed Cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 stalk celery

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the peas, cheese and onion. Stir in the mayonnaise then cover and refrigerate until serving time.

Submitter: 
Katrina Freed
Description: 
You'll need just five ingredients for this cool salad that's nicely sized for two people. 

Increase the amount of ingredienta and take this as a side dish to your next picnic or potluck.

This was always a hit at family picnics back in the 70's and I have seen it still in some delicatessens across the countryModified from: Pea Salad
