Title:
Magic Potion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Creepy crawler ice ring:
1 cup gummy worms
1 qt lemon lime drink
Drink:
1 cup boiling water
2 pack (4 serving) lime flavored gelatin
3 cups cold water
1-1/2 liters of lemon lime pop chilled
1/2 cup superfine sugar

Directions:
Creepy crawler ice ring:
Arrange gummy worms in bottom of 5 cup ring mold; fill mold with soda. Freeze until solid.
Drink:
Pour boiling water over gelatin in heatproof punch bowl; stir until gelatin dissolves.  Stir in cold water.  Add lemon lime soda and sugar; stir well - mixture will foam for several minutes.
Place gummy ice ring in bowl to serve.
