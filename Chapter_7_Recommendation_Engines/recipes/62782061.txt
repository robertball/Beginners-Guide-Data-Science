Title:
Tahiniless Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans garbanzo beans, drained and liquid saved
6 T olive oil
5 T lemon juice
4 T bean liquid
3 garlic cloves
1 t salt
1 t coriander
1/8 t cumin
1/8 t cayenne pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in the bowl of a food processor and process until smooth.

Submitter: 
The Bunny Chef
Description: 
Tahini is $15 a jar here but we love hummus so I often make it without the tahini.  You can use 2 T more bean liquid in place of 2 T olive oil if desired.
