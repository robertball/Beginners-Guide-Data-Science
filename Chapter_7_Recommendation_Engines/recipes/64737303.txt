Title:
sour cream and onion cucumber chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4 cups thinly sliced cucumbers
2 Tbs olive oil
3 1 Tbs onion powder
4 1 Tbs minced onion
5 2 tsp garlic powder
6  1 tsp dill weed
7  1/2 tsp sea salt

Directions:
! slice and dry cucumbers
2 coat cucumbers with  olive oil
3 whisk the rest of the ingredients in s bowl
4  lay cucumbers on dehydrator  trays
sprinkle dry mix over cucumbers
5 Dehydrate at 125-135 for 3-4 hours
