Title:
Hamburg Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp oil
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 lb. ground beef
1 10 3/4 oz. can condensed tomato soup
1 1/4 c cut green beans, canned, fresh or frozen
Pastry dough for 2-crust pie (your own recipe or store-bought)
4 to five slices American cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Heat oil in large skillet.
Add onions and saute 2 to 3 minutes, or until tender.
Add ground beef and cook until browned.
Add soup and green beans to beef; stir to combine.
Place bottom crust in a 9"pie plate.
Fill pie crust with beef mixture.
Layer cheese slices over top of meat.
Top with remaining pie crust dough, crimping edges together to seal.
Poke two or three holes on top to vent.
Bakes at 350 for 40 to 50 minutes, or until golden brown.
Let sit about 10 minutes before cutting.

Description: 
Surprisingly easy to prepare, this simple but hearty dish is the definition of comfort food.  I also experiment using ground turkey and various vegetables, and it is always delicious!
