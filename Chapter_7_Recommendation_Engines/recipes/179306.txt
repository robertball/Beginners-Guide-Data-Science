Title:
Baileys Cherry Cafe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985985.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Baileys Coffee Irish Cream
1 ounce Captain Morgan Original Spiced Rum
1 dash maraschino cherry juice
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1 tablespoon sugar

Directions:
Rim martini glass with cinnamon and sugar.
Then drizzle cherry juice in the glass to layer.
Shake Baileys and Captain Morgan with ice, strain, enjoy.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Maraschino cherry juice flavors this cocktail featuring Baileys Coffee Irish Cream and spiced rum.
