Title:
5 Minute Chocolate Mug Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 TBS all-purpose flour
4 TBS sugar
2 TBS cocoa powder
1 egg
3 TBS milk
3 TBS vegetable oil
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
3 TBS chocolate chips (optional)
1 large coffee mug

Directions:
Add dry ingredients to mug and mix well.  Then add egg, milk, oil, and vanilla and mix well.  Then add chocolate chips and mix.  Microwave for 3 minutes on high.  Let cool in mug for 5-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
I received this recipe in an email and have played with it until it was just right.  It's a very quick snack or dessert and really good!  We use a cappuccino mug for this.
