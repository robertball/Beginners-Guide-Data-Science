Title:
Italian Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 cup Olive Oil
1 large Vidalia Onion(sliced thinly)
1 tablespoon Garlic(minced)
2 14.5 ounce cans of Italian Stewed Tomato
1/8 cup Tomato Paste
1-1/2 teaspoon Basil
1 tablespoon Italian Seasoning(rubbed between palms)
Salt & Pepper to taste
2 pounds potato(Skin on washed, thinly sliced)
1 cup Kraft Parmesan & Romano Cheese

Directions:
In Dutch oven on med high heat.
Add Olive Oil, Onion & Garlic cook till translucent
about 3-5 minutes, Stirring.
Add Italian Stewed Tomato, Tomato Paste, Basil,& Italian Seasoning.
Bring to boil, Turn down heat to low and Simmer 20 minutes.
Preheat oven 400
In botton of Casserole dish lay down about 1/3 prepared sauce in the bottom.
Put 1/2 of potato on top of sauce.
Then put 1/3 Cup of Parmesan & Romano Cheese on top of potatoes
Repeat Till all Sauce, potato and cheese are used.
