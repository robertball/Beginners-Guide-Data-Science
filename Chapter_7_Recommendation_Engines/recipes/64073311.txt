Title:
Cinnamon Roll Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup dark karo
2 tsp. dark molasses
2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and spread on roll dough. This will make a syrup when baked.

Submitter: 
g'ma
Description: 
easier than sprinkling sugar and cinnamon. Leave out the molasses and karo if you want just plain cinnamon rolls.
