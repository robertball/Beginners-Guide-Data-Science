Title:
Taco Wonton Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332600.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (25 count) package wonton wrappers
1 pound ground beef
1 pkg taco seasonings
3/4 water
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Brown ground beef on medium-high heat, until meat is no longer pink. Remove and drain off excess grease. Return to hamburger meat back to pan and add water and taco seasonings, bring to a complete boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 7 to 8 minutes on low-medium heat.
In the meantime, press one wonton wrapper into each muffin-tin cups. Bake 5 minutes. Remove from the oven & place the wonton cups on a cookie sheet.
Mix hamburger mixture, cheddar cheese, pepper jack cheese until well blended.
Spoon mixture into cups. Bake 5 to 10 minutes or until bubbly. Remove from oven, let cool slightly and serve.
