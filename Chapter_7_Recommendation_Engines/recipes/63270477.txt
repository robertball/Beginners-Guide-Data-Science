Title:
Sloppy Tots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
tater tots
1 can sloppy joe mix
shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
pre-heat oven as directed on tater tot package.  While tots bake, brown ground beef.
When brown, drain and add slopply joe mix.
Simmer for 5-10 minutes
arrange tots on plate top with sloppy joe and add shredded cheese.

Kid favorite, quick and easy for a busy night.
