Title:
Super Easy Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/400430.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box with 2 rolled 9 inch crust pies
2 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cooked
1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease and flour a 2 quart casserole dish.
Rough chop chicken breasts.
In a large bowl, combine chicken, cream of chicken soup, Cheddar cheese, mixed vegetables, poultry seasoning, and salt and pepper. Mix thoroughly.
Lay one sheet of pastry in bottom of greased and floured pan. Pour chicken mixture over pastry. Cover with second sheet of pastry. Seal edges of top and bottom pastries. Cut steam vents in top.
Bake in preheated oven for 50 minutes, until pastry is golden brown.
