Title:
Chicken Diable Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken parts (about 3 lbs.)
4 T. (1/2 stick) of butter or margarine
1/2 c. honey
1/4 c. Dijon mustard
1 t. curry powder
1 t. salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F.
wash chicken, pat dry. (Remove skin if you wish)
Melt butter and mix with honey, mustard, curry powder and salt in a shallow baking pan.
Roll chicken parts in honey mixture and arrange in a single layer in same pan.
Bake for 1 hour until chicken is tender and richly glazed.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
Another family favorite--an oddly delectable blend of honey, mustard and curry powder. Smells relish while cooking!
