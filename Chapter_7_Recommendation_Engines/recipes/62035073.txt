Title:
Shredded Barbecued Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion diced
6 cloves garlic minced
1 tsp black pepper
1 T olive oil
1 tsp chili powder
1 cup chili sauce
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1 T worcestershire sauce
tabasco to taste
3-4 lb pork roast

Directions:
saute onion and garlic in olive oil over medium heat till translucent.  Add chili powder and pepper and stir.  Add chili sauce, vinegar, brown sugar, worcestershire and tabasco.  Simmer 5 minutes.  While it's simmering, trim fat off pork roast and place in crock pot, then cover and cook on high 6 hours or low for 10.  When meat is cooked and very tender, remove and shred it.  Then return meat to sauce.  Serve on warm rolls.
