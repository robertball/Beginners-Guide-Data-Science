Title:
Smoked Salmon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887173.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
3 dashes Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons horseradish
1 teaspoon fresh dill weed, chopped
4 tablespoons green onion, chopped
4 tablespoons ketchup
12 ounces smoked salmon, chopped

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir cream cheese until it is no longer in a hard form.
Add next 5 ingredients and blend well.
Fold in salmon.
Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
The perfect way to stretch pricey smoked salmon: Here, it's crumbled in cream cheese with fresh herbs and seasonings.Modified from: Smoked Salmon Spread
