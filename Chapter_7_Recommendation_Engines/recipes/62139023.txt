Title:
Shrimp Fettuccini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. medium shrimp, raw and peeled
1 cup real butter
1 bell pepper, chopped
1 onion, chopped
12 oz fettuccini noodles
parmesan cheese
3 Tbsp parsley
3 Tbsp flour
1/2 tsp red pepper
1 pint half and half
8 oz velveeta

Directions:
Saute bell pepper and onion in butter, add parsley and flour.
Add velveeta and let it melt all the way, then add 1/2 and 1/2.
Add red pepper, then add the uncooked shrimp.
Cook until shrimp are good and pink, stirring frequently.
While getting the shrimp mixture ready, cook your noodles as you normally would.
Drain and mix in with shrimp mixture.
Spread into 10x13 casserole pan and sprinkle parmesan cheese on top.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
