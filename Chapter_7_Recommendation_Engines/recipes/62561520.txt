Title:
Heather's Orzo with Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 cup uncooked orzo pasta
1/2 tsp. minced garlic (opt)
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil (for garnish - opt)

Directions:
Melt butter in heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Stir in orzo and saute until lightly browned. Stir in garlic and saute another minute or so.
Stir in chicken stock and bring to boil. Cover. Reduce heat and simmer on low until orzo is tender and liquid is absorbed, about 15 minutes.
Mix in basil and crushed red pepper. Season with salt and pepper - serve immediately. If don't serve right away & starts clumping together, may need to add touch of olive oil or chicken stock right before serving
