Title:
5 Minute Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1005009.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 teaspoons salt
1/3 teaspoon celery seed
3 green onions, thinly sliced
2 (8 ounce) packages coleslaw mix

Directions:
Whisk red wine vinegar, sugar, salt, celery seed, and green onions in a salad bowl until sugar and salt have dissolved. Toss coleslaw mix with dressing. Refrigerate 4 to 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Tasha
Description: 
Celery seeds add nice flavor to this tangy oil-free slaw.
This goes well with fried fish.
