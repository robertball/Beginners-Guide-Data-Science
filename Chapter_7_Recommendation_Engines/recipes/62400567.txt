Title:
Mrs. Field's Chocolate Chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup softened butter
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
2 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
2 1/2 cup flour (sifted)
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
18 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In large bowl, cream butter, sugars, eggs and vanilla.
In another bowl, mix together the flour, salt, baking powder & baking soda.
Combine wet and dry ingredients.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Place golf-ball size dough portions 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake for 9 - 10 minutes or until edges are light brown.
