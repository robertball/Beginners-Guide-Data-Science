Title:
Simple Salisbury Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977166.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular, 98% Fat Free or 25% Less Sodium)
1 pound ground beef
1/3 cup dry bread crumbs
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 egg, beaten
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups sliced mushrooms

Directions:
Mix thoroughly 1/4 cup soup, beef, bread crumbs, onion and egg. Shape firmly into 4 patties, 1/2 inch thick.
Heat oil in skillet. Cook patties until browned. Pour off fat.  Add remaining soup and mushrooms. Heat to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 10 minutes or until done.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Using ground beef to make salisbury steak patties ensures a tender salisbury steak with a shortened cooking time. Mushrooms and cream of mushroom soup cook with the patties to make a savory and creamy gravy.
