Title:
Wine Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 cup oil
3/4 cup wine, rum, Irish cream, marsala or sherry (try different liquors and enjoy the flavors)
1 t. nutmeg
1 white or yellow cake mix
1 3.5 oz. pkg. vanilla instant pudding mix
1 T. cinnamon
1 T. cocoa
1/2 cup sugar
Extra oil and sugar for coating pan bottom and sides.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In large mixing bowl combine eggs, oil, wine or sherry, nutmeg, cake mix and pudding mix.  Beat for 5 minutes.
Oil sides and bottom of bundt cake pan. Sprinkle well with granulated sugar.
Mix together cinnamon, cocoa and sugar.
Pour 1/3 cake batter into pan, alternate with 1/3 cinnamon mixture, then batter, then cinnamon mixture, finishing with cinnamon mixture on top.
Needs no frosting.
Bake at 350 F for 45 minutes (or until toothpick inserted in middle of cake comes out clean).
Cool on wire rack.  Can be made 24 hours ahead.  Serve with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.

A delicious and easy bundt cake using cinnamon and cocoa.
