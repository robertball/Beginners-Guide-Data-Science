Title:
Chicken Tetrazini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked cubed boneless chicken breast meat
1 can Cream of Chicken soup
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
1 c chicken broth
1 sm can sliced mushrooms
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 to 2 tsp garlic salt (to taste)
Approximately 2 c cooked spaghetti

Directions:
Combine soups and chicken broth in a large skillet.  Heat and stir til well blended.  Add mushrooms, garlic salt, and half the cheese.  Cover & simmer on low heat, stirring occassionally for 5 minutes.  Pour mixture into greased 2 qt casserole.  Stir in spaghetti, spreading the pasta with a fork so it's evenly distributed.  Top with remaining cheese.   At this point you can either refrigerate, freeze, or bake at 350 for about 30 minutes or until cheese melts and mixture is bubbling.

Submitter:

Description: 
I've never served this to anyone who didn't love it and it's sooooo easy!  On top of that, you can put it all together, stick it in the fridge, clean up and cook it later.  Also freezes wonderfully!
