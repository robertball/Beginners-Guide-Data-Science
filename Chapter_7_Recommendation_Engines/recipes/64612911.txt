Title:
Patsy's Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups of old fashioned oats
2/3 cup of ground flax seed
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 cup of coconut
1/2-3/4 chopped nuts
1/2 cup of honey
1/2 cup of coconut oil
4 tsp vanila

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients
Combine oil, vanilla, honey
Combine wet and dry
Pour onto well greased cookie sheet
?Bake at 300 for 35 min- mixing half way thru
Cool and add dried fruit, raisins, crasins, etc
