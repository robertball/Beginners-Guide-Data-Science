Title:
Crispy Rice Caramel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c popped corn
2 c rice krispies cereal
¾ c packed brown sugar
½ c corn syrup
2 Tbsp butter
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Line a 13x9 in pan with foil, leaving 1 inch overhang, grease foil lightly and set aside.
In a greased bowl, stir together popped corn and cereal, set aside.
In a saucepan over medium heat, cook sugar, corn syrup and butter, stirring with a wooden spoon for about 4 minutes until sugar dissolves.  Increase heat to med and bring to rolling boil, stirring constantly.  Boil without stirring for 4 minutes.
Remove from heat and stir until bubbles stop; stir in vanilla.  Pour over cereal mixture, stir to coat well.  Using spatula, immediately press into prepared pan; let cool.
Using foil as handles, remove from pan and transfer to cutting board.  Break into 20-2 inch pieces.  Caramel corn can be stored in airtight container for up to 1 wk.

Submitter: 
LMLSCA
Description: 
This treat is simple to make and lighter than traditional caramel corn recipes. It's great for kids' parties.
