Title:
Spring fresh fruit salad recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2531015.jpg
Ingredients:
-  1 basket of blueberries
-  1 basket of strawberries, sliced
-  1 orange, peeled and sliced
-  1/4 cup thinly sliced

Directions:
In large mixing bowl with blueberries, strawberries, orange and orange zest.
Gentle toss it together. Let it sit in the fridge 20 minute before serve.

Submitter: 
natty's pantry
Description: 
Strawberries, blueberries , oranges and mint .... This is a perfect light salad for summer.
