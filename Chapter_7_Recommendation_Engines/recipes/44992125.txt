Title:
Cheddar Bay Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups baking mix
1Cup Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 1/3 cups water
1 teaspoon Garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
1/4 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning (in seafood section)
1/2 cup melted butter
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon dried parsley
1/8 Old Bay seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
In a mixing bowl, combine the baking mix, cheese, Garlic powder, onion poweder parsley, Old Bay and water. Mix until dough is firm. Using a small scoop, place dough on the prepared pan.
Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 8 to 10 minutes, or until very very light golden brown.
Combine the melted butter, garlic powder, salt, onion powder, Old Bay and parsley. Brush over baked biscuits and return to oven for an additonal 3 to 5 min.
