Title:
7-Up Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1874985.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups Bisquick or a baking mix like Jiffy
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup 7-up
1/4 cup butter (4 tbsp) margarine or butter, melted (plus extra melted butter to brush on top of cooked biscuits)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Cut the sour cream into the baking mix until mixture resembles course crumbs.
Add the 7-up and mix with a spoon just until combined.
Pour the melted butter onto your baking sheet with 2 inch sides.
Drop biscuits or roll and cut out onto the butter.  Bake in preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes checking for doneness.
Remove from oven and brush with more melted butter.

Submitter: 
Lu
Description: 
Fluffy, golden biscuits. Can replace Bisquick with 2 cups self-rising flour.
