Title:
Moroccan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. oil
1 Onion, finely diced
1 1/2 t. cumin
1 t. tumeric
3/4 t. cinnamon
3/4 t. ginger
1/4 t. cayenne
1 15/5 oz can garbanzo beans
2/3 cup quartered dried apricots
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
2 t. honey
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
2 C cooked chicken chunks
1 pkg Couscous
1/4 C toasted sliced almonds
1/4 cup coarsely chopped mint leaves

Directions:
In a skillet over medium heat, heat oil add onion and saute until golden, about 5 min.
Stir in cumin, tumeric, cinnamon, ginger, and cayenne, and saute 30 seconds.
Add garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed, apricots, broth, honey, salt and pepper to taste.
Cover and simmer 5 minutes.
Add chicken, simmer 3 minutes.
Serve chicken over cooked couscous and garnish with almonds and mint.
