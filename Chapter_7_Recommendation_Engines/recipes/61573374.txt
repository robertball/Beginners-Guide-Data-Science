Title:
Baked Polenta w/Italian Sausage Mushroom Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Polenta
3 1/2 Cups Water
1 Cup Polenta (grits)
3 Tablespoons butter or margarine
1/4 teaspoon each salt & pepper
3 teaspoons garlic finely chopped (3 large cloves)
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
For the Sauce
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
10 ounces sliced white mushrooms
1/4 teaspoon each salt & pepper
3 Tablespoon flour
1/2 cup chicken stock
2 cups cooked Italian sausage, sliced 1/4 inch
1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes with basil, garlic & oregano (such as Del Monte)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
In an 8x8x2-inch baking dish stir in polenta, water, 1/4 tsp each salt & pepper and garlic
Bake for 40 minutes or until most of the water has been absorbed
Stir in Parmesan cheese
Heat olive oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Cook onion 3 minutes, add mushrooms, sprinkle with remaining salt & pepper and cook for 7 minutes
Stir in flour and cook 2 minutes, stirring constantly
Pour in chicken stock, diced tomatoes, and sausage. Simmer for 15 minutes
Spoon over polenta and serve

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
Suprisingly easy method for preparing polenta. No standing over the stove stirring for 30 minutes. And, the best part...it turns out perfect every single time. The Sauce is fresh and compliments the polenta well.
