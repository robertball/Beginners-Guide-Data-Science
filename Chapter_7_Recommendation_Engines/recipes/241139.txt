Title:
Smoked Sausage Black Bean Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2022189.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) package Hillshire Farm® Smoked Sausage, diced
3 (15 ounce) cans black beans, drained
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes and green chiles (such as RO*TEL®)
4 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning
1 cup water
6 (10 inch) flour tortillas
Toppings such as shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, sour cream

Directions:
Cook sausage in a skillet over medium heat until browned. Stir in black beans and diced tomatoes and continue cooking until mixture is hot, about 5 minutes. Stir in cream cheese, and taco seasoning, and water. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer for about 5 minutes.
Spoon mixture into large tortillas, top with your favorite toppings, and roll up tortillas to form burritos.
