Title:
Tex-Mex Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1101483.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast
1 TBSP Fajita mix
2 TBSP EVOO
16 oz jar of salsa
1/2 can corn, drained
1/2 can black beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup cheddar cheese

Directions:
Season chicken on both sides with fajita mix.
Heat EVOO in a skillet over medium-high heat.
Brown chicken on both sides, about two minutes per side depending on thickness of the breasts.
Mix salsa, corn and black beans in a bowl while chicken browns.
Shred cheddar cheese and set aside.
Once chicken is brown on both sides, pour salsa mixture over the chicken and cover cooking for about 20-25 minutes
Place chicken breasts on top of the salsa mixture and sprinkle with cheese cover until melted.
Serve over rice.
