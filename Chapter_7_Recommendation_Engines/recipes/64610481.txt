Title:
Roasted Salted Raw Almonds  from "An Oregon Cottage" by Jami Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 Cups raw whole almonds
1 1/2 TBS Hot water
1 1/2 Tsp Sea Salt
2 TBS EVO
Sea Salt to taste

Directions:
Heat Oven to 375 Degrees
Line a large Baking Sheet with parchment paper
Place almonds in a large non-plastic bowl as you will be putting the hot nuts back into the bowl after baking
Mix 1.5 tsp of Sea Salt with 1.5 TBS of HOT  water and stir till dissolved as much as possible
Pour salted water over nuts and stir till all nuts are completely coated
Transfer to prepared baking sheet spreading nuts to a single layer
Bake for 8 minutes and stir well. and then bake for 7-8 more minutes till nuts are crisp and brown,  Don't  over bake.
When done, let cool slightly on the baking sheet and then pour back into heat resistant bowel and drizzle with EVO and stir till well coat.  Use only the amount of oil to cover nuts.  Don't over oil.

Description: 
Best roasted Almond Recipe ever.  I found this on-line and have made them twice as printed and they are fabulous.  Stir the salt in the water well but don't expect it to dissolve completely   These are the best and certainly less expensive than roasted almond.
