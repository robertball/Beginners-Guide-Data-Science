Title:
Healthy Chocolate Mug Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066873.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 tablespoons cocoa
2 tablespoons honey
1 egg
1/2 tablespoon milk
1 medium banana, mashed, OR 1/4 cup applesauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a medium to large sized mug until blended.
Microwave on high for 2 minutes, or until cake rises and no longer appears uncooked.

Submitter: 
megan
Description: 
Perfect for a college dessert because it can be made in a microwave, this cake is both delicious and healthy!
