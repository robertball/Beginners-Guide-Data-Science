Title:
Famous Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
3/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup quick-cooking oats
1/4 cup chopped quick cooking oats
1 cup dark brown sugar -- packed
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup butter -- softened
2 tablespoons honey
3 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 large eggs
1 1/2 cup raisins
1/4 cup finely chopped walnuts
1/4 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
(not part of the original recipe but 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon is optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300F. Take a fourth of a cup of oat and crumble with hands or blender untill finely chopped. Next mix all chopped and unchopped oats in a  bowl with  flour, baking soda - and salt.  Mix well and set aside. Cream butter and sugar. Add to this wet honey, vanilla, and eggs. Mix at medium speed until light and fluffy. Take half the walnuts and chop finely .... leaved the other half coarsely chopped. Add the flour mixture, raisins, and walnuts to the wet mixture and blend at low speed just until combined. (This can all be done by hand) Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets, 1 1/2" apart. Bake for 20-24 minutes or until cookies are light golden brown. Cookies should be soft and delicate. Let cool for a minute or two untill set...... then carefully and quickly transfer cookies to a cool flat surface. Enjoy!
