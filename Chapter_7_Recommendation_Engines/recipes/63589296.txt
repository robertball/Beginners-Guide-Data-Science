Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken (or Beef) Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
2 15 oz cans diced tomatoes with green chilies
1 15 oz can chili beans
1 15 oz can black beans
1 15 oz can kernel corn, drained
1 pkg taco seasoning mix
3 skinned, boneless chicken breasts (or 1 lb browned ground beef)
1 12 oz can beer (or soup broth...chicken if using chicken, beef if using beef)
Optional toppings:
Shredded cheddar cheese
Sour cream
Tortilla or Frito chips

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except optional toppings in slow cooker, and cook on low for 4-5 hours.
If meat is chicken, remove chicken and allow to cool enough to handle. Shred chicken and return to slow cooker.
Cook another 1-2 serves and serve with (optional) toppings.
