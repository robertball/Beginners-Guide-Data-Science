Title:
Southwest Sausage Skillet Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 pkg Eckrich Smoked Sausage links, sliced
1 medium zucchini, halved lengthwise and sliced
3 cups cooked rice
1 can (14.5 oz)diced tomatoes w/ green chilis
1 cup frozen whole kernel corn, thawed
2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp dried oregano leaves
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
1) Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add sausage and zucchini. Cook ans stir for 8 minutes.
2) Stir in rice, diced tomatoes (with juice), corn, chili powder, oregano, and salt. Simmer over low heat 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat.
