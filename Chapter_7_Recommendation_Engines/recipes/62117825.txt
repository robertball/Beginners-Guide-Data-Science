Title:
Whip Cream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. flour
3/4 c. milk
3/4 c.shortening
2 c powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
pinch of salt

Directions:
Heat the milk and flour slowly over low heat to form a pase and cool.
Bet shortening and powdered sugar at medium speed until whipped.
Add cooled flour paste, vanilla and salt.  Beat at high speed until creamy.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
A whipped cream icing great for any cake or cupcake.
