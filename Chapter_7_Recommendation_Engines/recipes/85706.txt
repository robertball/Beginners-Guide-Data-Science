Ingredients:
1 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI速 Mixers Pina Colada mix
1/2 cup BACARDI速 Rum
5 cups ice
Blue Curacao to taste
Pineapple and cherries for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Pina Colada mix, BACARDI(R) Rum and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
