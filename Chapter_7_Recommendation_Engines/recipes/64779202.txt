Title:
Tuscan Chicken and Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cans Cream of Celery Soup
2 Cops water
1 Can White Kidney Beans (cannellini)
1 Can Diced Tomatoes, undrained
2 Cups Shredded or Diced Chicken
3 slices Bacon, Cooked and Crumbled
3 Cups Fresh Baby Spinach Leaves
Olive Oil
Parmesan

Directions:
Heat soup, water, beans, tomatoes, chicken and bacon in large pot over medium-high heat to boil.
Stir in Spinach. Cook for 5 minutes (until spinach is wilted.
Serve with a drizzle of Olive Oil and Sprinkle with parmesan

Submitter: 
Nita Rockwell Minto
Description: 
Smooth creamy Tuscan Soup in a Jiffy.  Perfect to serve with an Italian Salad for Lunch or Dinner.
This recipe was given to me from a friend that had served this soup at her daughter's wedding, and it had been loved by all!
