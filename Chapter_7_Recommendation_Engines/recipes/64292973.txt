Title:
NY Times Veggie Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can tomatoes with zesty mild chilies, drained
1 garlic clove, minced or pressed, or 1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
2 green onions, chopped
1 cup chopped carrots
1 cup parsley or cilantro
2 cups quick rolled oats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450ยบ. Process the first seven ingredients using an immersion or a regular blender
or food processor. Remove contents into a large bowl and stir in the oats. Form into patties, place
on a sprayed baking sheet, and bake for 8 minutes. Turn oven up to broil and cook for 2 more
minutes, until the tops are nicely
browned.
