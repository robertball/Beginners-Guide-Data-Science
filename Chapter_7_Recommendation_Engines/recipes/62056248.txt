Title:
Gramma's Meatloaf in the crock pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of ground round
1 onion chopped
1/2 bell pepper chopped
6 oz can tomato sauce
fine cracker crumbs
chili powder
salt & pepper

Directions:
Crumble 1/4 of the ground round in a large bowl.  Add 1/4 of the onion, bell pepper, tomato sauce and cracker crumbs. Add about 1 teaspoon of chili powder and 1/4 tsp of salt and pepper.  Repeat this layering process until all ingredients are combined.  Mix all ingredients with your hands and form a ball.  Place in crock pot and cook on low for 8 hours.  This can be made ahead and put in the fridge until ready to cook.

Submitter: 
Donna Leigh
Description: 
Very flavorful meatloaf with onions, bell peppers, chili powder, tomato sauce, cracker crumbs and NO EGG! Easily made in the crock pot.
