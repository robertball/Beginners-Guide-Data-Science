Title:
Philly cheese steak stuffed peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pre-heat oven to 350.
Sauté onions and mushrooms.
Grill or sauté sliced meat.

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350. Sauté onions and mushrooms. Grill or sauté sliced meat. Place into half of a cleaned pepper, top with mozzarella or cheddar cheese - bake for 20 minutes at 350. We love them! (Serve with rice or salad)
