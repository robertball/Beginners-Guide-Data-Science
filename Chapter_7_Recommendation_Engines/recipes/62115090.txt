Title:
Crab Pate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of imitation crab
1 block of softened cream cheese
1/2 c. seafood sauce, not cocktail (a little spicy!)
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 of an onion

Directions:
place onion in food processor and chop very finely
drain juice from onion,add crab and garlic
chop finely
add onion and crab mixture to cream cheese
mix thuroghly
shape mixture into a large ball and place on serving tray press slightly so bottom is flat
take a spoon and make an indentation on top of ball approx. 4 in. around and an inch deep
pour seafood sauce into indentation
serve with crackers and veggies

Submitter: 
Kristy
Description: 
Super easy, Super incredible dip, serve with veggies and crackers, sure to be a hit, with a spicy kick.
