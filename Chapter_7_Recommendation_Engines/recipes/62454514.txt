Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoon cream of tarter
1 cup smashed ripe bananas (3-4)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup shortening
2/3 cup sugar
2 eggs, well beaten (add extra egg in mountains)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine flour, soda, tarter, and salt. Cream shortening well and beat in sugar. Add eggs, mix well. Mash rip bananas. Add flour alternating with bananas.
Put into greased loaf pan. Bake 350 for 1 hour (45 minutes in mountains).
