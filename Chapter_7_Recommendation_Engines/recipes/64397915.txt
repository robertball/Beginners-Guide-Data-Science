Title:
Oat Flour Flat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet active dry yeast or 2 ½ teaspoons
1 ½ cups warm water
1 teaspoon sugar
3 cups organic oat bran flour or blend 3 cups old fashioned oats into oat flour
1 ½ teaspoons sea salt

Directions:
In a small bowl mix yeast, ½ cup warm water and sugar together
Whisk with fork
Let sit for 10 minutes
In a large bowl combine flour, salt, yeast and remaining cup water
Stir until incorporated.  The dough will be sticky
Cover with a towel and let rest for 1 hour
Place rack to lowest rungs in the oven
Preheat oven to 500 degrees (heat baking pan on lowest rack)
Remove dough from bowl
Place on a well floured surface, kneed dough for 1 minute
Cut into 6 pieces
Roll out into circles
Place two circles onto hot the baking pan
Bake four minutes
Turn over and bake for two additional minutes
Repeat
