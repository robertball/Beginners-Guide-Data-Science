Title:
My family macaronni and cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs uncooked long macaroni
4 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups milk
two eggs
dry mustard to taste
salt and pepper to taste
2 lbs cheddar cheeese
bread or cracker crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Prepare the  macaroni until barely el dente.
Meanwhile, melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium high heat. Stir in the flour until a cream colored paste forms. Then pour in the milk and stir constantly until this comes to a boil, then stir for 1 more minute, add salt, pepper and mustard to taste. Remove from heat and set aside.
When the macaroni is cooked, spread 1/2 of it into the bottom of a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking dish. Then layer 1/2 of reserved cheese. Repeat this one more time: macaroni, Beat two eggs into white sauce and pour over top.   cover with remaining cheese and buttered crumbs
Cover and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.
