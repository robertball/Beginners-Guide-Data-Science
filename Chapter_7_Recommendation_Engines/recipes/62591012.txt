Title:
Jess's Juicy Ranch Cheeseburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/553844.jpg
Ingredients:
1 packet (1 ounce) Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
1 pound ground beef lean
1/2 cup of oats
1/2 cup of shredded pineapple
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
4 hamburger buns, lightly toasted
Red onion, tomato, & romaine lettus to top.

Directions:
Preheat the grill or broiler.
In a large bowl, combine seasoning mix with the beef, oats, pinapple & cheese and mix with clean hands until incorporated or in mixer. Shape the meat into four ¼ pound burgers about 1-inch thick. Grill the burgers about 5 minutes on each side for medium doneness.
Serve immediately on the hamburger buns, with red onion, tomato and lettus.
