Title:
Creme Brulee French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123063.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter
2 T. corn syrup
5 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. salt
1 c. packed brown sugar
8- to 9-inch round loaf of bread
1 1/2 c. half & half
1 tsp. Grand Marnier

Directions:
Melt butter, brown sugar and corn syrup in heavy pan.  Pour into 9x 13 inch dish.
Cut six (i-inch)slices of bread.  Arrange in dish in one layer; squeeze together.
Beat eggs, half & half, vanilla, Grand Marnier and salt.  Pour over bread.
Chill 8-24 hours.
Bring bread to room temperature. Bake uncovered, until puffed and golden, about 35-40 minutes at 350 degrees.
Serve immediately.
