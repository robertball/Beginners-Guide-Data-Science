Title:
Microwave Almond Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole blanched almonds
1 tsp. butter
2 1/2 cups white chocolate (found @ bulk food stores, like Bulk Barn or Nutters, etc.)

Directions:
In 9" glass pie plate, place almonds and butter. Cook on high x approx. 3 minutes, or until almonds are toasted, stirring twice during cooking.
Set almonds aside.
Place chocolate in large bowl & cook on high approx. 2 minutes, or till softened.
Stir in almonds.
Pour onto waxed paper-lined baking sheet.
Spread to desired thickness.
Refrigerate till set. Break into pieces.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Quick, easy classic almond bark. Maybe too easy...you may have to hide some for later!
