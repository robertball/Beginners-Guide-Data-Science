Title:
Oyster Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans oysters do NOT drain
50 baby carrots cut in half
4 russet potatoes cut into bite size pieces
1 small can evaporated milk
2 tbs. butter
1 small yellow onion
3 stalks of celery
4 beef boulion cubes
salt and pepper

Directions:
cut up vegetables and put into pan, cover with water, add boulion cubes and boil for 30 min. Add canned oysters and juices and 1 tsp. pepper. Boil for 3 more minutes. Reduce heat to low and stop boiling. Add evaporated milk and 2 tbs. butter. Do not boil cook until butter melted then serve. Salt and Pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Robyn Dye
Description: 
A great stew for a cold wintery night. It will warm you up inside.
