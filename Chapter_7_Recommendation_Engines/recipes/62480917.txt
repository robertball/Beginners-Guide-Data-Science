Title:
Dulce de Leche Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sugar
3 tbsps corn starch
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1-1 1/2 cups Dulce de Leche
5 large baking apples
1 pastry for double crust pie

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees.
Mix the dry ingredients together in a large bowl until well blended.
Add the Dulce de Leche to bowl and stir until the sugar mix has been evenly distributed.
Peel, core, and slice the apples into the bowl, stir until the apples are evenly coated.
Roll out the pastry and line a pie plate with the bottom crust.  Add the filling, heaping it in the middle and top with the top crust.
Slice vents in the top crust and place in preheated oven.
Bake at 450 for 20 minutes and then lower the heat to 350 and continue baking for 25-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lauren Hewitt
Description: 
I made this recipe as an experiment at Thanksgiving Dinner.  I've had requests for it ever since.  Like a caramel pie, but not too sweet, it's wonderful warm or cold.  And excellent for breakfast the next day!
