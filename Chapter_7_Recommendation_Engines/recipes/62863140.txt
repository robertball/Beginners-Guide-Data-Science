Title:
Banana Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large bananas
1 tablespoon of peanut butter (optional)
1 tablespoon of honey (optional)

Directions:
Peel the bananas and cut them into 1-inch chunks.
Place the banana chunks onto a sheet pan and freeze for at least 2 hours.
Put the frozen banana chunks into a food processor or blender and blend until the mixture reaches an ice cream consistency.
Add peanut butter and honey, if desired, and continue to blend until the ingredients are combined.
