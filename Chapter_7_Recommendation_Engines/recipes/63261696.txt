Title:
Mother-in-law's Top Secret Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 C oil
2 C sugar
6 egg yolks
1 lb. grated carrot
3 t cinnamon
2 t baking soda
1 t salt
2.5 C flour
6-7 stiffly beaten egg whites

Directions:
Cream oil and sugar.  Add egg yolks beaten with 2 T warm water.  Add carrots, sift dry ingredients and add to oil mixture.  In a big bowl, beat egg whites and then fold into cake mixture.
Bake in 3 9" round pans. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
DuffMan
Description: 
My mother-in-law always got rave reviews for her carrot cake, but she would never share the recipe.  I found the recipe tucked into one of her cookbooks after she passed away.

Enjoy!
