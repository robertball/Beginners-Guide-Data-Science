Title:
Yummy Baked Chicken Thighs in Tangy Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds skinless chicken thighs
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 cup ketchup
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 cup water
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
1 tablespoon paprika
1 tablespoon sugar
1 tablespoon Worcestershire saue
1/4 cup butter, melted
Dash of A.1. sauce
1 teaspoon mustard

Directions:
Mix all ingredients (except chicken) in medium saucepan. Heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Arrange chicken thighs in baking pan. Pour sauce mixture over chicken. Bake about 2 hours in 300 degree oven.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
SeattleSun
Description: 
Another delicious recipe my mother makes and I finally got the recipe for. Tender chicken thighs and tangy sauce goes perfect served over rice. Adults and kids alike will love this! Enjoy!
