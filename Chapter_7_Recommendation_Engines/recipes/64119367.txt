Title:
Spiced Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 ripe apple, cored, peeled, cut into chunks
3/4 cup water
1 small Bosc pear, cored, peeled, cut into chunks
Dash ground cinnamon
Dash ground cloves
2 cinnamon sticks, for garnish

Directions:
Place apple, water, pear, cinnamon and cloves in Nutri Ninja Cup.
PULSE and hold Single Serve button until smooth.
Remove blades from cup after blending and serve as is or gently heat until warm before serving. Garnish with cinnamon sticks.
