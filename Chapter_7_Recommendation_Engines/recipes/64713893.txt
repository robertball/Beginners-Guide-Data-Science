Title:
Lindas Keto Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5674931.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Almond Flour
1/4 cup coconut flour
1 tbs baking powder (if strict keto, use a keto friendly baking powder)
1/8 tsp salt
1 tbs powdered Swerve
4 large eggs
1 tsp maple extract
1 tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
1 tbs Chai seeds (optional)
3 tbs butter
2 oz cream cheese softened in microwave
1/3 cup milk of your choice (i use half cream, half water)

Directions:
Heat skillet on the stove and add the butter. melt until it turns slightly brown then allow to cool while preparing the rest of the batter.
In a bowl thoroughly whisk the dry ingredients together.
Add the eggs, maple extract, softened cream cheese, milk and browned butter.
Mix until no longer lumpy.
If batter is too thick, add more milk to reach your desired consistency.
Heat skillet on low to medium
Add butter or oil of your choice for cooking ( tip-coconut oil works well)
Drop spoonfuls of batter, making 2 inch sized silver dollar pancakes, on the skillet.
Cook until the pancakes appears set, then flip and continue cooking.
Top with your choice of keto approved syrup, keto whipped cream and or berries.

Submitter: 
Linda Marie
Description: 
While this recipe is slightly more involved, it is full of flavor, Keto friendly and best of all, has the texture a lot like that of a regular pancake.
