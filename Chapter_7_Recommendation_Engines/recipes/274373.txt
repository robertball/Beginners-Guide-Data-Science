Title:
Chimichurri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6606721.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup chopped flat-leaf parsley
10 cloves garlic, finely chopped
2 tablespoons aji molido (Argentine ground red pepper)
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 bay leaves

Directions:
Mix together red wine vinegar, olive oil, parsley, garlic, aji molido, salt, oregano, pepper, and bay leaves in a bowl. Transfer to a glass jar with a tight-fitting lid. Let the flavors blend for 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
PapiBakes
Description:
