Title:
Peppermint Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/760673.jpg
Ingredients:
- 2 pkg (8oz) Cream Cheese
- 1/2c Sugar
- 1 tbsp Vanilla
- 2 Eggs
- 1/2c Peppermint Candy (crushed)
- 1 Honey Gram Pie Dish

Directions:
Mix together the cream cheese, sugar and vanilla with an electric mixer on low speed. Once well mixed add the eggs
Once everything is mixed, with a spoon mix in the crushed peppermint (leave a few extra for topping)
Pour into the pie dish and bake for 40 min or until set.
Remove from oven and refrigerate for several hours or over night

Submitter: 
Mrs Sarah
Description: 
Delicious! If you love cheesecake and love peppermint candy this will be perfect! Great for the Holidays!
