Title:
Mexican Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/607231.jpg
Ingredients:
* 12  oz.  dried mostaccioli or rigatoni pasta (3 cups)
* 1  lb.  bulk pork sausage
* 1  cup  chopped onion
* 1  16-oz. jar medium-hot garlic salsa
* 2  8-oz. pkg.  shredded (Monterey Jack) cheese (4 cups)
*     Tomato wedges, sliced jalapeño peppers, and chopped fresh cilantro
*     Salsa

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease 3-quart rectangular baking dish; set aside. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain; set aside.
Meanwhile, in large skillet cook sausage and onion until meat is browned and onion is tender. Drain fat, Return sausage and onion to skillet. Stir in salsa and cheese.
Bake, uncovered, for 35 minutes or until bubbly. Let stand 10 minutes. Top with tomato, jalapeño, and cilantro. If desired, serve with additional salsa. Makes 12 servings.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
Sausage and salsa add instant spice and flavor to this simple layered casserole.Modified from: Mexican Mac and Cheese
