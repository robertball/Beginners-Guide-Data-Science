Title:
Avon's Apple Pie Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
2/3 cup sugar
1 egg yolk
1/3 cup apple butter
2 1/3 cup flour
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon apple pie spice
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
Add egg yolk, vanilla and apple butter and mix well.
Whisk flour, cinnamon, and apple pie spice together and add. Mix until well blended.
Divide dough in half.
Shape each half into a 6 inch disc on waxed paper
Refrigerate for 30 minutes or longer.
Invert one disc of dough into an ungreased cookie sheet and free from into a cirlce, with lightly floured hands, Deeply score into 8 wedges.
Bake 25-30 minutes @ 300F or until golden brown

Submitter: 
Avon- status quo PRO
Description: 
Looking for a way to use up apple butter, I came across this idea in an old cookbook that has clever ideas for pantry items. I adapted it to what I now believe is perfect.
