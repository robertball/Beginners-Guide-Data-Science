Title:
Grilled Potato Skins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large russet potatoes
4 Tbs butter, melted
salt & pepper
1 cup grated cheddar
6 slices bacon, cooked & crumbled
1 cup sour cream
2 Tbs slice green onions
1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
chopped chives, for garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Clean & bake potatoes in oven for 1 hour. When done , allow to cool slightly & cut in halves.
Preheat grill to medium heat., allow to cool slightly350 F
Scoop out the pulp, leaving a 1/4 inch thick shell for the toppings. cut the potatoes into quarter wedges, brush with butter and season with salt & pepper. Place on grill and cook until crisp, about 10 minutes. During the last few minutes of grilling, remove potatoes and place on a cookie sheet. Top the potatoes with cheese and bacon & place back on top of grill.
Everyone can add their own toppings.
While potatoes are grilling, combine the sour cream & horseradish in a small bowl, mix well.
