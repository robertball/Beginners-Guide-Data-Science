Title:
Crowd Pleasing Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium cabbage ( thinly chopped )
1 onion ( finely chopped )
2-3 grated carrots
1 cup vinegar
3/4 cup sugar
2/3 cup canola oil ( or olive oil blend )
2 tsp salt
1 tsp celery seeds

Directions:
Place chopped cabbage, onion and grated carrot in large bowl.
Bring to a boil, vinegar, sugar, oil, salt and celery seed.
Pour while hot over vegetables. Let stand at least 3 hours before serving. But is best if made the day before.
Keeps 2 weeks in the refrigerator.
