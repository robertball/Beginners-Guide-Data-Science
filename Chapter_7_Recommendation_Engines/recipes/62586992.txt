Title:
Cranberry Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz pkg cream cheese
1 16oz can whole berry cranberry sauce
1/3 c chopped pecans
1 Tbsp orange zest

Directions:
Beat cream cheese till fluffy & then beat in the remaining ingredients. Chill for 1-2 hrs before serving so the flavors can blend.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is a wonderful creamy concoction best served with thin wheat crackers. Perfect for holiday get togethers
