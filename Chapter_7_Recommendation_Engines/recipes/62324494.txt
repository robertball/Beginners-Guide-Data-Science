Title:
Spicy Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/382170.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (28 ounce) cans baked beans (I prefer Bush's)
1/2 cup dark corn syrup
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons yellow mustard
2 tablespoons ketchup
2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
1/2 lb bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Fry bacon until about half way cooked.  Stir all ingredients together in large bowl.  Pour contents of large bowl into a 9x13 baking dish and top with slices of semi-cooked bacon.  Bake for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
amylrowe
Description: 
This recipe comes from my grandmother.  I have modified it a bit to suit my family's spicy taste buds and it is a big time winner!  I NEVER have any leftovers at any family event or party.
