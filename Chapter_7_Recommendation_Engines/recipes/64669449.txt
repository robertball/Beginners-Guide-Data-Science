Title:
Chris' Caesar Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4579546.jpg
Ingredients:
¾ Cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 Tablespoon Apple Cider Vinegar
1 Tablespoon Dijon Mustard
1 Tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
4 Anchovy Filets
5 Garlic Cloves
¼ Cup grated Parmesan
2 Tablespoons Mayonnaise
¼ teaspoon Black Pepper
1 Tablespoon Chopped Fresh Parsley

Directions:
Blend all ingredients together in blender.

Submitter: 
catlady
Description: 
Everyone in our family loves Caesar Salad so I have come up with this healthy and amazingly delicious Caesar dressing recipe.
