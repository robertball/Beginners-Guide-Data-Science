Title:
Southwestern Rice Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
2 10 oz packs of frozen chopped spinach
1 bag Baja Corn Blend
2 bags Mahatama Spanish Rice
1 tsp chili powder
1/4 cup canned jalepnos
1 tBSP taco seasoning
1 tsp garlic
3 tBSP ketchup for rice
3 tBSP dry minced onion
3 and 2/3 cups water

Directions:
Bring water to a boil. Add bags of rice, ketchup and onion and simmer covered till rice is tender, about 20-25 min.
Cook burger until done, season with salt, chili powder, taco seasoning and garlic. Add Jalepenos. Add Spinach, Add corn blend and mix all well.
When rice is done combine rice with burger mixture and serve.
