Title:
Wagon Wheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
2 cups uncooked wagon wheel pasta
1 can stewed tomatoes
1 1/2 cups water
1 box (10 oz.)frozen corn
1/2 cup BBQ sauce
salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
Fry the meat and drain. Stir in all other ingredients. Bring to a boil. Cover and simmer 20 min. or until pasta is tender.
