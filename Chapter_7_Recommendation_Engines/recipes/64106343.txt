Title:
Mama Collie's Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pie shell
2 beaten eggs
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup evaporated milk
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
2 drops pepper sauce/ hot sauce
2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
1 1/2 cups shredded cheese
1 can deviled ham spread

Directions:
Preheat cookie sheet 5 minutes in a 450 degree oven.
Bake crust on that heated cookie sheet for 6 minutes.
Reduce oven heat to 325 degrees.
Mix all ingredients. Blend.
Pour into pie shell.
Bake 40 minutes.
