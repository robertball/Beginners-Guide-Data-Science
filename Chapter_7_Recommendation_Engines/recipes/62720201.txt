Title:
Bernie's Buttercream Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs C&H confectioner's Sugar
1/2  cup warm water (room temperature)
1 tablespoon stay ice
1 3/4 Cup Crisco
2 Teaspoons clear vanilla
1 Tablespoon butter flavor powder
2 Tablespoons olive oil
1/4 Teaspoon salt plain popcorn salt

Directions:
Reserve two tablespoons of water. Mix all liquids, including Crisco, together first.
Add some powdered sugar plus other solids, then add the rest of the powdered sugar. If too stiff add additional water a teaspoon at a time as needed.
Do not over-beat.
