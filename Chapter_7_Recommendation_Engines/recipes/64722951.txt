Title:
Harvest Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5997908.jpg
Ingredients:
Dressing (Yeilds about 1 cup of dressing)
1/3 cup of apple cider vinegar
1/3 cup of honey
1/3 cup of country style Dijon mustard (Grey Poupon recommended)
1/3 cup of mayonnaise
1 teaspoon of salt
1/2 teaspoon of fresh ground pepper
Juice from 1/2 lemon
Pinch of red pepper flakes
Salad
Lettuce, spring mix (1 container)
1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
1 Fuji apple, sliced into very thin wedges
3/4 cup of Gorgonzola, crumbled
2/3 cup of walnuts, roughly chopped
2/3 cup of dried cranberries

Directions:
Whisk all dressing ingredients together in large bowl, chill in fridge for at least two hours before serving
Prepare all salad ingredients (leaving the apple for last to prevent browning), then layer all salad ingredients in serving dish.
Toss with dressing and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
LaurenSyd
Description: 
A dinner party worthy autumn salad with apples, Gorgonzola and a homemade creamy honey cider vinaigrette.
