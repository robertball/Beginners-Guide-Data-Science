Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2446427.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4  cup (4 tablespoons) butter
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 large eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1-2 drops of green food coloring, use less if using gel
Green sprinkles in various sizes
Melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Beat together butter, vegetable oil, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
Stir in baking powder, baking soda, salt, ground nutmeg, and vanilla extract.
Alternating, add the flour and the milk, beginning and ending with flour. Stir until smooth.
Add food coloring for an even green color.
Spoon mixture into a greased muffin tin, filling just halfway.
Bake for 10 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
Allow to cool for 5 minutes, before removing, covering in melted butter, and rolling in sprinkles.
Allow to fully cool and serve.
Beware of roaming Dinomites.
