Title:
Hawaiian Coleslaw, from justapinch.com Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (14 oz) coleslaw mix
1 1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 can (8 oz) crushed pineapple, drained
1 can 6 oz) mandarin oranges, drained
1/4 c chopped maraschino cherries (I think I would like to try this with only cutting the cherries in half)
1/4 teaspoon salt and pepper

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a bowl; toss to combine well.  Refrigerate for 2 hours or more.
Toss again just before serving.
