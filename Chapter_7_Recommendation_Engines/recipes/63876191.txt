Title:
Pecan Crusted Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1076705.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8-oz. Package Cream Cheese, softened
8 oz. Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1/3 cup Miracle Whip
1/3 cup Chopped Onion
1/3 cup Chopped Green Bell Pepper
1/2 small Jalapeno Pepper, with or without seeds (if you like spice, keep the seeds)
1 cup Finely Chopped Pecans

Directions:
In a food processor, blend all ingredients until smooth. Spoon mixture on to plastic wrap and form into a ball. Chill in refrigerator for at least an hour. Remove plastic wrap and roll in chopped nuts.
