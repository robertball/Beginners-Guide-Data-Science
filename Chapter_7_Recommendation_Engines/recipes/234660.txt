Title:
Rum and Raisin Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1124482.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup almond milk
1 frozen banana, cut into chunks
2 tablespoons rum
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon raisins
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Blend almond milk, banana, rum, honey, raisins, and nutmeg in a blender until smooth, about 30 seconds.
