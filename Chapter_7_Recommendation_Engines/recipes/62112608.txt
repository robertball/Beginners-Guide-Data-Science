Title:
Oat & Molasses spice Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 Cup All purpose or cake flour
1 Cup Oats
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp ground cinnimon
3/4 tsp allspice
1/3 Cup Milk
1/4 C Molasses
2 TBL oil
2 Large Eggs
3/4 C. Softned Butter

Directions:
Sift or mix dry ingrediants.  Slowly add wet ingrediants start with the milk, then, molasses, and oil.  Make sure each ingrediant is mixed before adding the next.  Add eggs slowly  Make sure each is mixed before adding next.  Then add Butter.  Bake at 400 degrees for 22-25 minutes.  Eat alone or with a little butter..

Submitter: 
Christal Hudson Ellard
Description: 
These have to be the best Muffins to ever come out of my oven.  As my son said, it made his tongue so happy it was beating his mouth up!
