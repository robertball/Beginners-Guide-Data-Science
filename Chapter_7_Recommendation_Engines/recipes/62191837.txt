Title:
Pumpkin Pie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans pumpkin
4 eggs
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 can evaporated milk
2 t. cinnamon
1 t. ginger
1/2 t. nutmeg
1 yellow cake mix
1 cup margarine, melted
1/2 c. nuts

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except cake mix, margarine, and nuts.  Pour into an ungreased 9x13" pan.  Sprinkle the entire cake mix on top of the pumpkin mixture.  Pour melted margarine over the top.  Sprinkle with nuts.  Bake at 350 degrees for one hour or until set.  DO NOT OVERCOOK!
