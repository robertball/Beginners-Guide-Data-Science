Title:
Paleo Apple Pie Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3985114.jpg
Ingredients:
1 apple, roughly chopped
1/4 cup frozen pineapple chunks
1 tablespoon cashew butter
1 tablespoon unsweetened flaked coconut
1 teaspoon flax seed meal
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, or as desired
1 cup almond milk

Directions:
Layer apple, pineapple, cashew butter, coconut, flax meal, vanilla extract, and cinnamon into a blender; add almond milk. Blend mixture until smooth.

Submitter: 
Alli Shircliff
Description: 
This cinnamon and vanilla-scented smoothie will make you feel like you are biting into an apple-pie-flavored donut and is paleo friendly!
