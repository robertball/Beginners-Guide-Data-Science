Title:
Stuffed Mushroom Caps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 mushrooms, Alouette Garlic and Herbs spreadable cheese, seasoned bread crumbs.

Directions:
Clean 18 mushrooms and remove stems. Place hollow side up on baking shee. Fill with 1 teaspoon Alouette and sprinkle with seasoned bread crumbs. Bake at 375 degrees for 12-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
JDVMD
Description: 
This is a great recipe to serve by itself or as a side dish. The folks at Alouette offer this recipe. I use this quite frequently as a side item to steak or beef tenderloin. Very simple and easy to make.
