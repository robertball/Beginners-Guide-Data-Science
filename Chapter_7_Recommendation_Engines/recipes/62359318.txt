Title:
Old-Fashioned Strawberry Buttermilk Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups buttermilk
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 eggs, beaten
2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/3 cup butter, melted
2 cups fresh strawberries, sliced

Directions:
In a medium mixing bowl combine buttermilk and baking soda, then stir in beaten eggs.
In a seperate bowl, combine remaining dry ingredients.
Gradually stir dry ingredients into buttermilk mixture, then stir in melted butter.
Pour batter onto hot greased griddle in 1/4 cup rounds.
Top each pancake with sliced berries, and let cook until small bubbles form on surface of cake.
Turn and cook on reverse side until lightly browned.
Serve with sliced berries, syrup and butter.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
This is a very old recipe for strawberry pancakes.  We love these during the summer when fresh, sweet berries are available in abundance. These are delicious mounded with more sliced berries, syrup and butter. Enjoy!
