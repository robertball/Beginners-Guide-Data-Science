Title:
Herbed Slow Cook Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed low fat cream of chicken and herbs soup
1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into strips
1 dash Marsala wine
1 T olive oil
Garlic
Rosemary
Black pepper

Directions:
Brown the chicken in a pan with the herbs and olive oil.  Combine soup, chicken, and wine in slow cooker.
Cook on Low setting for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
messyjessi, RD
Description: 
Browning the chicken ahead of time helps keep it from getting dry in the slow cooker.Modified from: Super Easy Slow Cooker Chicken
