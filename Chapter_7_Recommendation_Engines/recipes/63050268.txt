Title:
Nanas Baked Chicken, Sausage & Potatoes & Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 6 chicken thighs (separate leg & thigh)
2. 6 Mild Italian Sausage links ( I cut them in half)
3. 5 small potatoes
4. 2 whole peppers( can use green, red or yellow - color is great)
5. 1 large onion - sliced
6. 1 small can mushrooms ( optional)
7. 1/3 cup white wine
8. 2 teaspoons garlic powder or minced garlic
9. 2 teaspoons basil
10. salt and pepper
11. 1/2 cup olive oil
( may use Italian seasoning as well )

Directions:
In Medium frying pan ...place small amount olive oil and brown 1. and 2. ... turning to brown on all sides
Prepare large baking pan ( or jelly roll pan) by sprinkling olive oil on the bottom
remove meat from fry pan and place browned chicken and sausage evenly on the baking pan
wash 4. and cut generous slices - add to fry pan as well as 5. and 6. cover and cook 5 min- remove and place evenly around meat
sprinkle evenly 7. 8. 9. 10. over the top..a little more olive oil
Bake uncovered 375* for 1 hr ( turn the potatoes chicken etc after 30 min)to evenly brown

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
The standard served for company...once you get it all on the pan and place in the oven to bake your job is done and you can enjoy the company
