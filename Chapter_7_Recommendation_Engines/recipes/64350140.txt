Title:
Turkey Sandwich Gone Nutty! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2113073.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices of sandwich bread
1 slice of american cheese
2 slices of turkey
2 tablespoons of peanut butter

Directions:
Place bread slices into toaster.
Once finished toasting, smother a tablespoon of peanut butter on each slice of bread.
Place a slice of turkey on each side of peanut butter, then add the cheese in between.
Press together firmly.

Submitter:

Description: 
I know this sounds really unappetizing, but trust me, it's delicious!
