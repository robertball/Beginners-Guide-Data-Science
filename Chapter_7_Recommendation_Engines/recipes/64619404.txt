Title:
Funeral sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 roll) packages of sweet Hawaiian rolls
1 1/2 lbs Virginia ham
12 slices swiss cheese
1 stick real butter
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp poppy seeds
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Cut rolls in half and place bottom half of rolls in 9 x 13 pan (you'll need 2 pans).
Layer ham and cheese on each roll. Replace the tops of the rolls to make sandwiches.
In saucepan, heat butter, Worcestershire sauce, poppy seeds, onion powder and garlic powder. When all butter is melted, brush onto tops of sandwiches.
Cover with foil and refrigerate 1 hour up to 24 hours.
Bake sandwiches 15 minutes or until cheese is melted.
