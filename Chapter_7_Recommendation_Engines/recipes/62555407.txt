Title:
Sautéd Carrots and Leeks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1127681.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons of butter
1/3 cup of chicken broth
1 tablespoon of sugar
2 medium leeks
4 medium carrots or 2 cups of baby carrots
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Clean and Cut carrots and leeks. Put everything in a pan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer away liquid for about 15 minutes.  Sauté for a few minutes more.

Submitter: 
mcgerm
Description: 
A side to use when you have lots of carrots and leeks to use up from the garden.
