Title:
Ranch Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pounds lean ground beef
1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix

Directions:
Preheat the grill for high heat.
In a bowl, mix the ground beef and ranch dressing mix. Form into hamburger patties.
Lightly oil the grill grate. Place patties on the grill, and cook 5 minutes per side, or until well done.

Submitter: 
diane
Description: 
The beef burgers stay juicy and delicious on the grill or stove top! Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.Modified from: Ranch Burgers
