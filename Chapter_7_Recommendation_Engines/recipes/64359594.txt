Title:
Rhubarb Custard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Double pie crust
4 cups rhubarb, diced
3 eggs
2 cups sugar
1/4 cup flour
1 tsp vanilla
3 Tbsp milk
1 Tbsp butter, melted

Directions:
Roll out pastry for pie crust and place in pie dish.  Place rhubarb in crust.
In a large bowl, beat the eggs slightly.  Mix in sugar, flour, vanilla, milk and butter.  Pour mixture over rhubarb.
Cover with top crust and seal the edges.  Bake at 400 degrees F for 50-60 minutes.
