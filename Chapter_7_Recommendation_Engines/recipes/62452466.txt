Title:
extra flavorful crockpot short ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-5 lbs beef short ribs
1 tb veg oil
1 lg onion
1 cup water
1 cup ketchup
1/3 c red wine vinegar
1 tlb paprika
1 teaspoon curry (i use more, cuz i like it)
1/2 tea chili powder
1/2 tea dry mustard
2 tea salt
4-5 potatoes (peel and quarter)
4 carrots  (small or cut to small)

Directions:
combine all ingredients, cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours.
