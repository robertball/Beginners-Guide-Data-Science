Title:
Blueberry-Pineapple Salad with Creamy Yogurt Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5548215.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh blueberries
1 20-oz can pineapple chunks in juice, drained and juice reserved
3 tbsps whole milk plain Greek yogurt
1 tbsp of reserved pineapple juice
1 tsp honey
1/4 tsp lime zest

Directions:
1. In a medium bowl, whisk together Greek yogurt, 1 tbsp pineapple juice, honey, and lime zest.
2. Fold in blueberries and pineapple chunks until coated with yogurt mixture.
3. Serve immediately.
Note: Amount of honey may be increased or decreased depending on the sweetness of the fruit.

Submitter: 
Kim
Description: 
Easy and delicious fruit salad! The yogurt dressing also goes great with strawberries, kiwis, and mangoes too!
