Title:
Pina Colada Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pineapple upside down box cake mix
1 small can pineapple tidbits
1 cup or to taste shredded coconut
1 can Thai coconut milk, not the kind in dairy aisle,  think coconut soup kind
2tbsp coconut oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Grease 13x9 pan
Lay down tidbits and about 3tbsp coconut milk,  top with 1/2 c coconut
Dump cake mix on top of that.
Dump  3/4 can of coconut milk on top and drag a spoon through it all. Don't mix completely,  but stir around some so you don't end up with dust nuggets
Top with remaining coconut
Bake for about 1hr

Submitter: 
Colleen Hibbitts
Description: 
Dump cake easy,  but better!  I love pina coladas, so this made perfect sense!
