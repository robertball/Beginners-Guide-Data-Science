Title:
Honey and Brown Sugar Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1097300.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Pork chops
½ teaspoon garlic powder
½ teaspoon spicy paprika
½ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon black pepper
2 Tablespoons olive oil
3 TBSP honey
3 TBSP brown sugar

Directions:
Pat chops dry with a paper towel on both sides.
Mix garlic powder, paprika, salt and pepper together and season chops on both sides patting seasonings into the meat.
Heat electric skillet to high with olive oil, once hot sear meat on both sides about 2 minutes each side.
Reduce heat in electric skillet to medium.
Mix honey and brown sugar together and warm in the microwave to completely mix.
Spoon ½ the honey and brown sugar mixture on one side of all chops, turn and cook on medium for 7-10 minutes depending on thickness of the chops.
Spoon other ½ of the honey and brown sugar mixture on chops, turn and cook on other side for 7 – 10 minutes depending on thickness of the chops.
