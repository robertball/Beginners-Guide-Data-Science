Title:
** Skillet Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. AP flour
3/4 c. cornmeal
2-3 tsp. sugar
2 1/2 tsp. baking powder
3/4 tsp. salt
2 beaten eggs
1 c. milk
1/4 c. + 1 Tbl. vegetable oil

Directions:
Stir together flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, & salt; set aside.
Combine eggs, milk & 1/4 c. oil.
Stir egg mixture into flour mixture until moistened.
Add 1 Tbl. oil to 8-10" cast iron skillet. Place in 400 degree oven to heat, 3-4 min. Remove pan from oven; swirl oil in pan to coat bottom and sides.
Pour batter into hot skillet.  Bake 15-20 min. (usually 20) or until toothpick comes out clean.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Adapted from BHG cookbook.  If you heat skillet with oil in the oven before pouring in the batter, the edges of the corn bread get crisp & crunchy.
