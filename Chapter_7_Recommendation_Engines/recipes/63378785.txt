Title:
Holiday Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon apple cider
64 oz cranberry juice cocktail
1 750 ml bottle Amaretto
4-5 cinnamon sticks

Directions:
Stir all ingredients together in a pot and heat.
Refrigerate after mixing and reheat as needed.
