Title:
Big Daddy's Banana and Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small bananas, broken into chunks
1 cup frozen unsweetened strawberries
2 scoops Mila
1 (8 ounce) container vanilla low-fat yogurt
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
In a blender, combine bananas, frozen strawberries, Mila, yogurt and milk. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
Strawberries and bananas are blended with yogurt and milk in this easy and delicious smoothie.Modified from: Groovie Smoothie
