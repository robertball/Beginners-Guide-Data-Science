Title:
Mood-Improving Strawberry Cocoa Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1569669.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup ice cubes
4 strawberries
2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Blend milk, ice cubes, strawberries, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth.
