Title:
Veggie Nacho Nibblers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bell peppers (red and yellow)
1/2 cup cheese
1/8 tsp crushed red pepper

Directions:
Cut 2 bell peppers into 6 strips each, then cut strips in half. Put on a microwaveable plate; sprinkle with 1/2 cup cheese and 1/8 tsp crushed red pepper. Microwave for 1 minute or until cheese melts. Serve Warm.
