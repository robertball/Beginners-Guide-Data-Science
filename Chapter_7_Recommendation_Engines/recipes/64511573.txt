Title:
Crackerjack Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
2 cups rolled oats
2 cups Rice Krispies cereal
1 1/2 cups flour
1 cup coconut
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingredients together well.
Add remaining ingredients. Mix well.
Shape into balls. Do not flatten.
Bake on cookie sheet @ 375 x 8 - 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Crunchy, with rice krispies cereal, rolled oats and coconut - from my favorite cookbook series.
