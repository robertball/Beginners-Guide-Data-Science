Title:
The Souper Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp Olive Oil
8 oz (227 gm) Turkey Breast, boneless & skinless cut in 1" cubes
3 oz Canadian Bacon, diced
1/2 cup White Onion, chopped
1 tsp garlic, minced
3 cups chicken broth, sodium reduced
2 cups carrots, diced
1 cup potato, peeled and cubed
1 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp rosemary
1/4 tsp black pepper, ground
2 cups broccoli florets
1 can (398 ml) cream-style corn
1 can (398 ml) whole-kernal corn
1/2 cup sour cream (5%)

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large pot over medium-high heat.
Add turkey and cook until browned.
Add bacon, onions and garlic and cook for 2 minutes.
Add broth, carrots, potatoes, thyme, rosemary and pepper.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer for 8 minutes.
Stir in broccoli and simmer for 3 more minutes.
Add creamed corn and corn kernels.
stir in sour cream and serve hot.
