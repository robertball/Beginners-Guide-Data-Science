Title:
Oreo Bon Bon's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16oz) package of Oreo cookies
1 (8oz) package of cream cheese
1 (16oz) package of semi-sweet chocolate chips
*Garnish* White chocolate, almond slivers, colored candy sprinkles

Directions:
Break up oreo's, and crush them in a food proccesor, so they become sand-like, and add softened cream cheese to crushed oreo's, mixxing very well.
Let mixture chill overnight
Melt semi-sweet chocotate chips, and roll oreo mixture into 1 inch balls.
With toothpicks, dip oreo balls into melted chocolate, and place on waxed paper.
When all oreo balls have been dipped into the chocolate, chill them for 4 hours.
*Optional* melt white chocolate, and drizzle over tops of each oreo balls, or decorate with almond slivers, or candies.

Submitter: 
Tiffanie DeMayo
Description: 
A fun, and easy bon bon even the kids can help make. These bon bon's are so heavenly, one might think they came from a fancy candy shop!
