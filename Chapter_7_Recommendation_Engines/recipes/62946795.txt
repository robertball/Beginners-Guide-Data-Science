Title:
Jaime's Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 cups shredded cooked Chicken Breast (about 2-3 skinless boneless breasts)
2(8 oz) packages Cream Cheese (softened)
8 oz Frank's® Red Hot® Buffalo Wings Sauce
1 jar Marie's® Chunky Blue Cheese Dressing
2 cups shredded Cheddar Cheese divided

Directions:
1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Stir the Cream Cheese with a fork or whisk until it's smooth. Stir in the Hot Sauce, Blue Cheese Dressing, shredded Chicken and 1 and a 1/2 cups of the Cheddar Cheese.
3. Bake for 20 minutes.
4. Top with the remaining Cheddar Cheese and return to the oven for 10 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
JAIM71
Description: 
The delicious taste of Buffalo Wings in a zesty, creamy dip. Serve with your favorite Tortilla Chips, Bagel Chips, Crackers or Vegetables. This recipe is always the hit of the party!
