Title:
Potato, Bacon Cheese Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 3 medium Potatoes diced small
6 eggs
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 cup chopped onions
1/2 lb. bacon
2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Boil potatoes in boiling salted water for 5 minutes. Drain and rinse well.
While potatoes are boiling, fry bacon in large frying pan and drain on paper towels.
Remove all but about 2 Tbs bacon grease.
Fry boiled potatoes in remaining bacon grease until brown. Move potatoes aside and add onion. Cook just until clear.
Mix cooked potatoes and onion together and scoop into a greased 2.75 quart baking dish.
In a bowl, beat eggs, milk, butter, 1 cup grated cheese, salt and pepper. Pour over potato/onion mixture.
Sprinkle with crumbled, cooked bacon and 1 cup grated cheese.
Bake, uncovered, at 370 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
