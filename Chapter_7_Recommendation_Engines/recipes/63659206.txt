Title:
Super Easy Blueberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Blueberry Pie Filling
1 stick Butter
½ box Yellow Cake Mix (dry)

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 375 degrees.
Spray a 9x9 baking dish with nonstick spray. I used my round corning baking dish. Pour a can of Blueberry Pie Filling in your baking dish. Pour cake mix evenly over mixture. Drop a stick of butter on top and put in a preheated oven for 20 – 25 minutes.
