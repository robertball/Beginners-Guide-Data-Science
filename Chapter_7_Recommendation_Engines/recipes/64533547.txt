Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup diced sweet onion
1 1/2 lb. skinless, boneless thighs cut into bite size pieces
1 (14.5 ounce) can fire roasted diced tomatoes with liquid
1 small can tomato paste
1 1/2 cups chicken broth, fat free, low sodium (optional vegetable broth or water)
2 tablespoons chili powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (more or less to taste)
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
Kosher or sea salt to taste
1 (15.5 ounce) can Cannellini or Navy beans, drained and rinsed
1 (15.5 ounce) can Kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1/4 cup reduced fat cheddar cheese for garnish

Directions:
Combine all the above ingredients in the slow cooker. Cover and cook on low 8 hours in a 4-6 quart slow cooker. Spoon into bowls and garnish with a little cheddar cheese and diced onion, if desired.
Note: The chicken called for in this recipe is raw when going in the slow cooker. If you already have cooked chicken, just add it about an hour before the end of cooking time. A serving size is 1 cup. 6 Weight Watcher points

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
A delicious chili recipe that cooks itself.  The fire-roasted tomatoes make the difference!
