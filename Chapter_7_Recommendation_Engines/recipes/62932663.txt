Title:
Turkey Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Turkey Carcus
8 Cups Cold Water
1 Whole Onion Peeled
1 Large Carrot
1 Stalk Celery
1/3 Cup Barley
1 Cup Potato
1 Cup Turnip
1/2 Cup Onion
1 Cup Carrots
1 Cup Green Beans
1 Cup Parsnips
1 package of Lipton Chicken Noodle Soup Mix
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring cold water to a boil and add turkey bones, whole onion, carrot and celery.  Turn to simmer for 5 to 6 hours.
Strain away meat from the turkey and save to add to the soup later.
Add 1/3 cup of barley to the broth and cook for 1 hour.
Add the remaining vegtables (cut into bit size pieces) and simmer 1 more hour.  Add 1 package of lipton Chicken Noodle soup mix for the last 15 minutes.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
