Title:
Fruit Juice Spritzer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Perrier water or club soda
1/2 cup fruit juice (naturally sweetened juice like apple cider, orange, grapefruit, pineapple, or grape)

Directions:
Combine juice with Perrier water or soda and ice to make a nutritious, fizzy drink.
