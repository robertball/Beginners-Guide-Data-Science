Title:
Debbie's Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 Cup butter
2 1/2 cup sugar
1 can 29 oz pumpkin
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
4 tsp ground cinnamon
2 tsp ground all spice
3 1/2 cup flour
1 cup raisins or craisins
1 cup nuts

Directions:
Mix dry ingrediants
Cream butter and sugar until fluffy
blend in pumpkin
Add dry mix with spoon
Stir in raisins and nuts
Divide in two and pour into 5 # 9 loaf pans
Bake at 350 for approx 1 hr

Pumpkin bread with raisins and nuts.
