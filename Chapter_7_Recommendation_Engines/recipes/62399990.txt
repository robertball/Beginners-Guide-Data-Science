Title:
Jan's Chocolate Stuffed Raspberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/430584.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 6 ounce package ripe Raspberries.
Approximately 42 berries.
1- 12 ounce package White Chocolate Chips. Approximately 42 chips.

Directions:
Gently push chocolate chip point first into raspberry.
Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description:

Delicious with white chocolate or milk chocolate chips!
