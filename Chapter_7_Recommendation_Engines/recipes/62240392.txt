Title:
Hot Spiced Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon apple cider
2-1/2 cups granulated sugar
8 cinnamon sticks
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teasoon allspice
juice from 1 lemon

Directions:
Pour cider into large pot.  Add sugar, cloves, allspice, and lemon juice; mix well with a whisk.  Add in cinnamon sticks.  Heat over low to low-medium heat for approximately 1 hour, or until piping hot, stirring occassionally.

Submitter: 
Michelle Salois Krantz
Description: 
Not only is this cider delicious, but it makes the house smell wonderful, too!
