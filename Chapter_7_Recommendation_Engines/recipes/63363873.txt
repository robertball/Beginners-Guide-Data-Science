Title:
smoked turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 lb turkey
Brine Mixture:1 cup of coarse salt
1 cup of brown sugar
2 cups of water
1 cup of corn syrup
2 quarts of apple juice

Directions:
wash turkey in cold water(remove inerds package), mix brine mixture in separate bowl, ensure all dry is dissolved. Place turkey in large plastic bag(garbage bag), in a bowl and pour brine in cavity and around turkey. Tighten bag and tie so turkey is completely covered. Brine for 12 hours minimum.
Smoke with applewood chips
