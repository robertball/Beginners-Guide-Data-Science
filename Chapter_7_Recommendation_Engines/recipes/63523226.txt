Title:
Pull Apart Pizza Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/961937.jpg
Ingredients:
Parchment paper for baking
1 TB of cornmeal
1 can of Pillsbury Crescent Seamless dough Sheet
12 Tsp Pizza Sauce
1 pouch of sliced Pepperoni about 12 slices
6 Tsp chopped raw onion
6 large olives sliced in half the long way
1 8oz pkg. 2 Cups Sargento Off the Block Mozzarella Shredded
3 sheets of Phyllo dough
1/2 C water or egg wash for brushing Phyllo dough

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Spread Cornmeal on a baking sheet
Cover baking sheet with parchment paper and sprinkle paper with cornmeal. Spread Seamless dough sheet out on Cornmeal cut into 12 equal squares. Keep all dough squares together and touching, do not separate them. Put 1 Tsp. of Pizza Sauce on each square followed by one slice of pepperoni, then 1/2 Tsp. onion on each then 1/2 sliced olive on each. Then sprinkle 1/2 bag of cheese over the entire dough sheet... brush water around the edges of the dough sheet and then top with three sheets of phyllo dough. Each brushed with water or egg wash press around all edges including insides of the twelve squares as well as you build the 3 layers of phyllo dough trim away the extra phyllo dough down to the size of the original crescent dough sheet. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top and bake for 12 minutes or until cheese is melted and top crust is golden brown. Pizza puffs will easily Slide off the parchment paper  onto a serving platter, the just pull these apart and enjoy.

Description: 
Pull apart pizza puffs, simple, quick and fun. The More cheese the better!
