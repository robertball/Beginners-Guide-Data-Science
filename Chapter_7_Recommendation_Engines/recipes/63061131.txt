Title:
Pan fried Parmesean Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 #Boness Skinless Chicken Breasts
1 Cup Italian Seasoned Bread Crumbs
1/2 Cup Parmesean Chicken
2 Tbsp Oil

Directions:
Mix bread crumbs with parmesean cheese.  Dredge chicken strips in the bread crumb mixature.  Pan fry in oil until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Joyce Lorraine Druyor
Description: 
Strips of  Pan fried Chicken  made with Italian Seasoned Bread Crumbs mixed with Parmesean Cheese tghen fried in a little butter or oil.  Very tasty
