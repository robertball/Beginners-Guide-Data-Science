Title:
Pretzel Cocoa Krispie Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/764764.jpg
Ingredients:
1 16oz bag marshmellos
6 Tbsp real butter (salted)
1 Tbsp real butter to coat 9 x 13 casserole dish
10 cups rice krispie style cereal
1 cup cocoa krispie style cereal
1 cup pretzels crushed

Directions:
Butter up casserole dish.
Meaure out cereals and pretzels and set aside.
Melt butter on low heat in large cooking pot.
Add marshmellos to melted butter and stir till melted and smooth.
Add cereal mixture to melted marshmellos stir gently till completely coated.
Turn out into buttered 9 x 13 casserole dish and pressed down.
Cool, cut into squares and enjoy.
