Title:
Banana Strawberry Oat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030899.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of strawberries; quartered
2 bananas
4 eggs
2 1/2 cups of flour (3/4 all-purpose, 1 1/3 whole wheat)
1/2 cup rolled (not instant) oats
2 teaspoons baking powder
3 tablespoons olive oil
2/3 cup of sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375ºF (190ºC)
grease and flour two 9x5 bread pans and set them aside.
clean strawberries and cut them into quarters, sprinkle with sugar in a bowl and set aside.
mix together flour, oats, baking powder, sugar and cinnamon together in a larger bowl using a baking mixer.
put bananas in with strawberries. Add the eggs and oil.
Before mixing the wet ingredients, mash the bananas a little; mix the wet ingredients with the baking mixer.
pour mixture into the dry ingredients, and mix all together with the baking mixer.
add a little more sugar if necessary, but not too much more.
Pour half of the batter into one bread pan, and the rest into the other.
Bake for 25-35 minutes until golden brown and stick test comes out clean.

Description: 
This was a great way to use up those bananas that have been sitting on the counter and those strawberries that were sitting in the fridge! Super tasty and a healthy snack!
