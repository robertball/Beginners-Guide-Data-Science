Title:
Peanut Butter Banana Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 perfectly ripe bananas - not mushy nor too green.
1/4 cup peanut butter.

Directions:
Cut peeled bananas in 1 inch slices.  Place on a cookie sheet and freeze overnight.  Place frozen bananas in a food processor and blend until all pieces are chopped.  Add peanut butter and process until thick and creamy.  Add anything else you heart desires and mix together.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
This is about as easy and fresh as you can get.  You are only limited by your imagination.  Nuts, chocolate, marshmellows or whatever else you desire in an ice cream can be added.
