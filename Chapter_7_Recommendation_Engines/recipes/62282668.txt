Title:
The Casino Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/352325.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oz gin
1/2 oz Luxardo maraschino liqueur
1/4 oz Fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in an ice-filled shaker.
Shake for 1 minute (not kidding)
Strain into a chilled cocktail glass
Garnish with Maraschino cherry

Submitter: 
Rudy Torrijos III
Description: 
The most complex tasting, oddly simple, and quite ridiculously good.
