Title:
Spiked Peach Iced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 c. water
1/2 c. bourbon
1 tub Crystal Light Peach Iced Tea Mix
1 c. orange juice
1 orange sliced
Ice

Directions:
Mix first four ingredients in a pitcher.
Chill.
Serve over ice and garnish with orange slices.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
This is a refreshing tea I like to serve during the summer.  It's also great for get-togethers like bridal showers and such.
