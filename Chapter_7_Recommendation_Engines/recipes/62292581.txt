Title:
Chickpea curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ # chickpeas, cooked till soft
1 med. potato, diced
1small onion, diced
2 med. tomatoes, diced
½ tsp. mustard seeds
2 bay leaves
½ tsp. pepper
2 tsp. coriander powder
2 med. jalapenos, cut in small pieces
1 small piece ginger
salt to taste
2 T. oil
10 cilantro leaves, cut

Directions:
In a blender, puree tomatoes, pepper, salt, coriander, jalapenos, and ginger.
Heat oil in a heavy pan.  When it starts to smoke, add mustard seeds.  (keep a lid handy for when they pop!)  Add bay leaves and fry for one minute.  Add potatoes and cover; cook for ten minutes, till tender.  Add the chickpeas and the puree.  Stir.  Bring to boil.  Stir and cook for 12-15 minutes until curry becomes a little thick and syrupy.  Remove and add 4-5 drops of lemon juice if desired for a slight tang.  Garnish with cilantro.
