Title:
Scott's Buffalo Wing Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Tablespoons of Butter (or Margarine)
6 Tablespoons of Hot Sauce
6 Tablespoons of Ketchup
2 Tablespoons of Honey

Directions:
Combine ingredients and cook on med heat. Once sauce starts to bubble, simmer for 15 min. Toss cooked chicken wings in sauce, and serve. This should be enough sauce for about 2 dozen wings.

Submitter: 
ScottCim
Description: 
This is a mild sauce, with lots of taste.
