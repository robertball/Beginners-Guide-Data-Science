Title:
Lemon Ice Box (Cloud) Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987215.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 1/2 cups finely crushed vanilla wafer crumbs
1 envelope unflavored gelatin
1 1/2 cups water
6 tablespoons pre-sweetened lemonade flavor drink crystals

Directions:
Melt butter in small saucepan; stir in wafer crumbs. Press firmly on bottom and up side of 9-inch pie plate; chill.
Soften gelatin in 1/4 cup water in small saucepan. Over low heat, heat until dissolved; set aside.
Combine sweetened condensed milk, remaining water and drink crystals in medium bowl; mix well. Stir in gelatin mixture.
Pour into prepared crust. Chill 3 hours or until firm. Garnish as desired.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
This tart lemon pie couldn't be any easier. There is very little mixing, and no baking involved. The hard part is waiting for it to chill.
