Title:
Fresh Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
mine
1/2 C buttermilk
3/4 C mayonaise
1/4 c sour cream
1 tsp garlic pressed
1 tsp minced onion
1 1/2 tsp chives chopped
1 1/2 tsp dill weed chopped
1 1/2 tsp fresh parsley chopped
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Let sit for 30 minutes or overnight.
Note: Garlic can be finely minced or run it through a garlic press.

Submitter: 
Dolly
Description: 
All fresh ingredients make this Ranch Dressing the way it should be. For salads or as a dip.
