Title:
Creamy Mushroom Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 1/2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 small onion, sliced
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular, 98% Fat Free or 25% Less Sodium)
1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons dry sherry
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 or 2 TBS of sour Cream
Instant Mashed Patatos

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoons butter in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned. Remove chicken.
Heat remaining butter. Add mushrooms, onion and garlic and cook until tender.
Add soup, milk, sherry and pepper. Heat to a boil.  Stir in sour cream. Return chicken to pan. Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or until done. Place Chicken over Mashed patatos and cover w/ gravy

Submitter: 
Krista
Description: 
This dish reminded me a chicken dish at a awesome restuarant in my town.  It is so good.  I put it over mashed patatos instead of noodles and served with a side of vegetables and it was a big hit at my house.Modified from: Creamy Chicken and Mushrooms
