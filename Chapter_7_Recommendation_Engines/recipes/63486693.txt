Title:
thekruser's Sweet and Spicy BBQ Pork and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/959282.jpg
Ingredients:
1 4-5 lb boneless pork loin roast
4 potatoes
1 white onion
1 c BBQ sauce (total)
1/3 c water
1/4 c maple syrup
1 T apple cider vinegar
1 t Salt
1 t Pepper
1/2 t Smoked paprika
1/4 t Cayenne pepper

Directions:
Cut potatoes into about 1 1/2 inch cubes. Put in slow cooker.
Cut onion in half then cut each half into 4 pieces. Put on top of potatoes in slow cooker.
Place pork loin roast on top of onions.
Mix 2/3 c BBQ sauce, vinegar, water, syrup, and all dry ingredients in a bowl. Pour over pork.
Place slow cooker bowl (not the electrical part) in refrigerator overnight. (optional if you are preparing the night before)
Cook in slow cooker on low for 8-10 hours.
30-45 minutes before serving, pour 1/3 c BBQ sauce over pork and turn up to high.
Slice pork and serve with the potatoes and onions.

Submitter: 
Adam Thekruser Kruse
Description: 
An amazing pork loin roast recipe you can prepare the night before and cook in the slow cooker while you are at work. Nice sweet and spicy flavor.
