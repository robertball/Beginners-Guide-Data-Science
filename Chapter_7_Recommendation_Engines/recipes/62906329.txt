Title:
Grandmas Best Big, Fat, Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SIFT TOGETHER AND SET ASIDE:
.....2 cups all-purpose flour
.....1/2 teaspoon baking soda
.....1/2 teaspoon salt
**
CREAM TOGETHER UNTIL WELL BLENDED:
.....3/4 cup unsalted MELTED butter
.....1 cup packed brown sugar
.....1/2 cup white sugar
**
BEAT IN UNTIL LIGHT & CREAMY
.....1 Tablespoon vanilla extract
.....1 egg AND 1 yolk
**
MIX IN SIFTED MIXTURE JUST UNTIL BLENDED
**
STIR IN USING A WOODEN SPOON
.....2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
**
DROP DOUGH  by 1/4 CUPS,   3 " APART ONTO GREASED OR PARCHMENT LINED COOKIE SHEETS
**
BAKE 15 - 17 MIN UNTIL EDGES ARE LIGHTLY TOASTED.
**
COOL ON BAKING SHEET FOR A FEW MINUTES THEN TRANSFER TO RACKS TO COOL COMPLETELY
