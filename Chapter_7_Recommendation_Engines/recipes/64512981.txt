Title:
Football Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 square soft white dinner rolls
6 Tab. mustard
8 oz. thin deli ham
8 oz. thin swiss
pepper
4 Tab. butter
2 Tab. finely chopped onion
1 Tab. poppy seeds
2 Tab. worchestershire sauce
1 t. garlic powder

Directions:
350
Slice rolls spread w/ 4 TAb. mustard and arrange bottoms in 9x13 pan. Place ham/cheeze.  Season w/ pepper. Put on top bun. Combine butter, onion, poppy seeds in a bowl. Microwave until butter is melted and onion is tender, about 1 min. Then whisk in worchestershire, remaining mustard, and garlic power. brush/pour over buns. Cover with aluminum foil and let sit for 10 min, or up to overnight. bake for 20 min covered, 10 min. uncovered.
