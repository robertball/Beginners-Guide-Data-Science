Title:
Rich Spinach Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed, well drained
2 cups small curd cottage cheese
1 cup cubed process cheese (Velveeta)
3/4 cup egg substitute
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, cubed
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a 3-qt. slow cooker, combine all ingredients; mix well. Cover and cook on low for 2-1/2 hours or until the cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Vioda  Geyer
Description: 
'I found this recipe in an old slow cooker cookbook,' recalls Vioda Geyer of Uhrichsville, Ohio. 'When I took the side dish to our church sewing circle, it was a big hit.'
