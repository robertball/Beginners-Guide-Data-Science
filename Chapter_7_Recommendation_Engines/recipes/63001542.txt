Title:
Spritz Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c. Butter
2c. Sugar
4 egg Yolks
1t. Vanilla
1/2 t. Almond Extract
5c. Flour

Directions:
Cream butter. Gradually add sugar. Creaming well after each addition until fluffy. Add eggs, vanilla and almond extract. Gradually add flour. Bkae in 400 degree oven for 10 minutes.
