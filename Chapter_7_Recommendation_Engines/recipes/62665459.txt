Title:
A Good Easy Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 tablespoons butter
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
fresh garlic to taste
1/2 teaspoon seasoning salt
1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Melt butter &amp; olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Rub seasoned salt and onion salt on chicken,after it browns a little add chicken broth and let it simmer for 15 minutes.  Make gravy from juice, can add worchestershire sauce or  kitchen bouquet

Submitter: 
Jacque
Description: 
Sprinkle chicken breasts with garlic powder, onion powder and seasoning salt - then sautee and enjoy. Couldn't be easier! Great recipe for quick and easy meal, even for the pickiest eater!Modified from: A Good Easy Garlic Chicken
