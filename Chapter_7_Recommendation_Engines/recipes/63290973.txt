Title:
Inka Mama's Aji Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of iceberg lettuce
3 leaves of romaine lettuce
5 cloves garlic
7 serrano peppers
3/4 cup olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Rinse and dry lettuce and peppers
Cut up lettuce to fit into your blender.
Trim the the peppers, do not take the seeds out.
Combine the lettuce, garlic, peppers and olive oil in the blender.
Blend until smooth, adding more olive oil in very small amounts as needed.
Add salt and pepper to taste

Submitter: 
doch83
Description: 
The spicy green Aji sauce from Inka Mama's. This sauce is commonly served with bread as an appetizer, and goes well with almost any Peruvian dish. It is fantastic over rice.
