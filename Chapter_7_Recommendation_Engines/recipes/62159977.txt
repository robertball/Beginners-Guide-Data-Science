Title:
Elbow Macaroni & Kidney Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup elbow macaroni
1 can dark red kidney beans
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup light mayonnaise
2-4 tbsp red wine vinegar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil macaroni according to package directions, reducing cooking time by 2 minutes (I do 4 minutes); drain and rinse with cold water.
In large bowl, combine all ingredients, mixing well.
Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Rapp Griffis
Description: 
Red wine vinegar adds just the right touch to this delicious and easy dish
