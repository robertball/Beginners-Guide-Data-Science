Title:
Sweet Potato Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c self-rising flour
2 heaping tbsp sugar
pinch salt
3/4 c mashed sweet potatoes
1/2 stick room temp butter
1/4 c milk
extra flour

Directions:
Mix flour, sugar and salt.  Mix butter into sweet potatoes, then cut into flour mixture with fork. Add milk and work into dough.  Spread flour on surface and roll out to desired thickness. Cut with biscuit cutter and place on cookie sheet with edges touching. Brush with melted butter, bake at 450 for 20 minutes.
