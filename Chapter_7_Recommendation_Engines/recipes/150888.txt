Title:
Frozen Mango Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978592.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila
3 ounces Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix
1 cup diced and peeled mangos
1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
4 ounces crushed ice
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Rub rim of a margarita glass with half a lime and dip into sugar.
Place Jose Cuervo Especial(R) Tequila, Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix, diced and peeled mangos, and crushed ice into a blender; mix in blender until smooth.
Pour into the prepared glass and garnish with lime wedge.
