Title:
Harold's Baked Spinach Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 oz) boxes chopped frozen spinach
8 slices bacon
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 pt heavy cream
8 oz Swiss cheese, shredded
2 (4 oz) cans minced clams, drained
3 T flour
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees.
Thaw frozen spinach; drain and squeeze dry.
Cook bacon until crispy.
Remove bacon from pan and set aside.
Brown onion in bacon grease.
Crumble bacon into onion mixture.
Add spinach, Swiss cheese, cream, clams and flour.
Stir.
Cover and simmer on low heat 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Place in greased 2 quart casserole dish.
Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Cover and bake at 250 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Kotz Walls
Description: 
This was my dad's specialty. It was requested for every holiday and birthday...even by people who hated spinach!! It goes especially well with turkey and ham
