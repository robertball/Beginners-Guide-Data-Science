Title:
Cin Chili Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb Velveeta, cubed
1 can Rotel tomatoes and chilis
1 lb Cin Chili meat mixture (Recipe follows)
2 Lbs lean ground beef, such as sirloin
14 ounce can beef broth (suggest Swanson)
8 ounce can tomato sauce (suggest Hunts)
Seasoning batch #1
1 T onion powder
2 T garlic powder
1 t jalapeno salt (or 1/2 t jalapeno powder & 1/2 t salt)
1/4 t black pepper
1 t chicken bouillon granules
1 t beef bouillon granules
Seasoning batch #2
5 T chili powder
1 t garlic powder
1/2 t cayenne pepper
2 t ground cumin
1/4 packed brown sugar
Seasoning batch #3
1/8 t cayenne pepper
1/2 t garlic powder
1/2 t onion powder
2 t chili powder
1 1/2 t ground cumin

Directions:
Brown beef, add beef broth and tomato sauce. Simmer 1 hour, uncovered. Add seasoning batch #1 and blend well. Simmer 35 minutes, uncovered. Add seasoning batch #2 and blend well. Simmer 20 minutes, uncovered. Add seasoning batch #3 and blend well. Simmer 10 minutes, uncovered. Cool and chill to allow flavors to marry. Place cubed Velveeta, Rotel tomatoes and 1/2 the Cin meat mixture (freeze other 1/2 meat mixture for future dip) into a crock pot/slow cooker. Heat on high stirring until cheese is melted and mixture is blended and heated through. Serve hot with tortilla chips for dipping.

Submitter: 
FunwithFood
Description: 
A spicy beef and cheesy dip served hot with tortilla chips. A nice change from the typical Rotel or canned chili and and cheese dips. It takes a bit of cook time, but if your game it's worth the time!
