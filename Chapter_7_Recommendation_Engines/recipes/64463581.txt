Title:
Thai Red Rice and French Green Lentil Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2428789.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked Thai red rice (also called cargo rice)
1 cup uncooked French green lentils (also called Puy lentils)
1 tsp salt, divided
1 bay leaf
1 cup fresh peas, hulled
1/3 cup slivered almonds
1/2 cup light vegetable oil
2 Tbsp fresh chopped parsley
2 Tbsp fresh lime juice, or more to taste

Directions:
Soak the red rice in fresh water for 30 minutes.
In a separate bowl, soak the lentils in fresh water for 30 minutes.
Drain the rice and simmer it in 3 cups of water with 1/2 tsp of salt until the water has been absorbed or cooked al dente, not soft. About 20 minutes.
Drain the lentils. In a separate pot add the drained lentils, add 3 cups of water  mixed with the other 1/2 tsp of salt and the bay leaf. Bring to a boil. After the lentils have boiled for 15 minutes, add the peas. Simmer until the until the water has been absorbed or until the legumes are al dente, not soft.
Drain the rice and the legumes. Discard the bay leaf. Allow to cool slightly then, in a large serving bowl, combine rice, legumes, almonds, vegetable, parsley, and lime juice. Gently fold the ingredients until they are evenly combined.
Allow to rest either at room temperature or in the refrigerator before serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
Thai red rice and French green lentils, both full of nutrition, make for a healthy salad with a crunchy texture and nutty yet delicate flavor. This salad is great on its own but really shines when accompanied with spicy dishes. The rice is full of B vitamins and the lentils are packed full of vitamins and minerals; this salad is as good for you as it is delicious!
