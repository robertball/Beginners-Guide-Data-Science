Title:
Awsome Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 large potatoes
8 oz cream cheese
1/3 c - 1/2 c heavy cream
10 garlic cloves
salt
pepper

Directions:
Dice potatoes and place in water along with garlic cloves, boil until potatoes can easily be pierced with fork
Drain potatoes, do not throw out cloves.
Place potatoes, cream cheese, and heavy cream in blender, blend till smooth, and more or less cream depending on how thick you want your potatoes
Season with salt and pepper
