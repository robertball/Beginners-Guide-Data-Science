Title:
Mother's Day Mug Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991050.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups KELLOGG'S速 RICE KRISPIES速 cereal
Canned frosting or decorating gel

Directions:
In large saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
Add KELLOGG'S(R) RICE KRISPIES(R) cereal. Stir until well coated.
Cool slightly. Using buttered hands shape cereal mixture into mug shapes, reserving small portions of cereal mixture for handles. Attach one handle to each mug, securing with frosting. Decorate with additional frosting. Best if served the same day.

You'll melt more than marshmallows when you make these yummy mugs with the kids on Mother's Day.
