Title:
Air-Fried Taco Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5822040.jpg
Ingredients:
•	2 jumbo hot dogs
•	1 tsp taco seasoning
•	2 hot dog buns
•	1/3c guacamole
•	4 tbsp salsa
•	6 slices pickled jalapenos

Directions:
1.	Preheat an air fryer to 390°F. Make sure the air fryer is at temperature for at least 4 minutes.
2.	Cut 5 slits into each hot dog. Evenly rub one half tsp of taco seasoning over each hot dog. Place them inside the air fryer basket. Cook for 5 minutes. Place the hot dogs inside the buns and put them into the air fryer and cook another 4 minutes. Check the hot dogs. Take them out when the bun is toasted and the hot dogs have crisped.
3.	Take the hot dogs out and top with equal amounts of guacamole, salsa, and jalapenos.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Hot dogs inside their buns cooked in an air fryer then topped with taco fixings redefine Taco Tuesday. The air fryer renders the hot dog crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside.
