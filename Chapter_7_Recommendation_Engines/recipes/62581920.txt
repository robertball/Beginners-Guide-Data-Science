Title:
Buttermilk Chocolate Fudge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz melted chocolate or 4 Tbsp cocoa
3 c sugar
4 eggs
1 c hot water
1 c butter
2 tsp baking soda
3 c flour
1 1/2 c buttermilk
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cream together chocolate, sugar, eggs, hot water & butter. Add soda, flour buttermilk & vanilla. Bake in a 9 by 13" pan at 350 till it bounces back when touched.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is an easy cake that we whip up sometimes when we need to take a dessert somewhere. Top with any icing or use it as a layer cake!
