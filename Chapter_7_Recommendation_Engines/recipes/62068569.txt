Title:
artichoke tomato soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of artichoke hearts
1 small onion
1 clove of garlic
1 can of tomato soup. (you can use another can if you're serving more) This is a dense soup
1 c cubed cheese 1/2 inch cubes - your choice of cheese (pepperjack or feta; anything you'd like)

Directions:
chop and fry up the onion, garlic & artichokes. When they start to turn light brown, add a can of soup with it's can of water (3/4 of the can filled). Once the soup begins to bubble add the cheese until melted. Add salt/pepper as needed.

Submitter: 
Victoria M.H.
Description: 
warm - full -yummy soup -it could be used as a pasta topping aswell very versatile. If you change soups to a cream of mushroom it's just as yummy.
