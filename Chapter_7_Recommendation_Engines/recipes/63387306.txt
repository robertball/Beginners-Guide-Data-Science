Title:
Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c chicken broth
1 onion, diced
1 potato, peeled and cubed
2 carrots, peeled and cubed
3 ribs of celery, cubed
1 c leftover cooked chicken, cubed
2 T Rue (1 T butter + 1 T flour, melted together)
1 t sage
1 t oregano
1/2 t paprika
1/2 t pepper

Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients in a pan and stir over heat.  Put in a baking dish and spoon the crust on top.  Bake 375' for 45 minutes.
Quick Spooned On Crust
1 c flour
1 1/2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
1 c milk
1 t vinegar
1/2 c melted butter
1/2 t pepper
Combine ingredients and spoon over chicken.
