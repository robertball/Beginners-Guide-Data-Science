Title:
Apple Kuchen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6     Medium apples peeled, cored, and sliced
1 1/2C  Flour
3 tspn  Baking Powder
1/2tspn salt
1/2 C   sugar
1       egg
1/2c    milk
1 tspn  vanilla
1/4 c   shortening
TOPPING
1 c     sugar
4 Tbsn  flour
4 tspn  cinnamon
4 Tbsn  melted butter

Directions:
Beat the egg, milk, vanilla and shortening until blended
Add the dry ingredients and beat until dough forms
spread the dough on a large cookie sheet
top with the sliced apples
sprinkle the topping over the apples
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes

Description: 
One of my Mother's favorite recipes. Great with your morning coffee or a cold glass of milk.
