Title:
Nacho Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. au gratin potatoes
1 can corn, drained
1 can rotel tomatoes, undrained
2 c. water
2 c. milk
2 c. Velveeta, cubed
1 dash Tabasco
½ t. cumin
½ t. green chile sauce
? t. dried jalapeno.

Directions:
In 3 qt. saucepan, combine the potatoes and sauce mix, corn, tomatoes and water; mix well.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15-18 minutes or until potatoes are tender.  Add milk, cheese and hot pepper sauce if desired; cook and stir until the cheese is melted.  Garnish with parsley, if desired
