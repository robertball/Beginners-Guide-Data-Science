Title:
Chili-Topped Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Russet potatoes (1 1/4 lb)
1 can (15 oz) chili
1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1/4 sour cream

Directions:
Microwave potatoes for 8 to 12 minutes or until done. Heat chili. Cut potatoes; top with chili, cheese and sour cream.
