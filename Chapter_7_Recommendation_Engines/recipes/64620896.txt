Title:
Low-Fat Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups dry penne pasta
1 small onion, finely chopped (if tolerated)
2 (6 ounce) cans solid white tuna, water packed
2 (10 ounce) cans fat-free cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1/2 cup 0% Greek yogurt, low-fat sour cream, or low-fat mayo
1 cup skim milk or 1% low fat milk
1 tablespoon Mrs. Dash seasoning mix
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp black pepper (or to taste)
1 cup grated reduced-fat cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Grease a 9 x 13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
Boil pasta in a large pot of boiling water until al dente; drain then transfer to a large bowl.
Add in onion and tuna; toss to combine.
In a saucepan, heat soup with milk until smooth. Stir in yogurt/mayo and seasoning/spices until combined.
Pour into the bowl and toss to combine. Transfer to the prepared baking dish and bake for about 30 minutes.
