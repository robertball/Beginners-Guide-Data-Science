Title:
Antipasto sandwiches with goat cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12-oz.) jar roasted red peppers, rinsed and sliced
1 clove garlic minced
1 (6-oz) jar marinated artichoke hearts, drained and chopped (save marinade
2 baguettes, focaccia or ciabatta loaves
8 oz. creamy goat cheese
1/4 c. black olive paste or finely chopped Kalamata oives
2 c. arugula

Directions:
Mix garlic with goat cheese.  Spread bread with cheese and paste; top with peppers, artichokes, and arugula.  Cut each baguette into 4 pieces.  Sprinkle with pepper.
