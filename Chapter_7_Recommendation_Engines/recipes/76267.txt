Title:
Golden Pumpkin Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990013.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup plain fat-free yogurt
1 tablespoon fat-free sour cream
2 cups pumpkin puree
3 eggs
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
4 teaspoons baking powder
1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice
1 cup raisins
1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Spray two 9X5 inch loaf pans with butter-flavored cooking spray.  Set aside.
Blend yogurt, sour cream, pumpkin, eggs and vegetable oil in a large mixing bowl.  Add flour, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, baking powder and pumpkin pie spice.  Stir well.  Add raisins and walnuts.  Stir until just blended.
Spread batter into prepared pans.  Bake 45-50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Cool and cut each loaf into eight thick slices.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
These velvety moist pumpkin loaves are great to make any time. They make a perfect snack, or give them as a gift during the holidays.
