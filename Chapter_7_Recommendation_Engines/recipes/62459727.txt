Title:
Raspberry Cream Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint heavy whipping cream
1/2 box cheesecake or white chocolate pudding mix
1/4-1/2 cup raspberry sauce from White Chocolate Rasberry Cheesecake.

Directions:
Whip cream in KA for a few seconds, add pudding mix.  Beat until incorporated, mousse like consistency.  Add raspberry sauce.
