Title:
Refreshing Celery Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 tsp celery seed
1/8 cup finely chopped celery

Directions:
Mix, chill, and serve with veggies or crackers.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
I put this together in a pinch when I had company coming.  It sounds simple but it's a really great and refreshing dip.
