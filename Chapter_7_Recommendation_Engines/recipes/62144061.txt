Title:
Rachel's Delicious Very Berry Protien Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mixed frozen berries (blue berries, strawberries, etc.)
1/2 cup fat free vanilla yogurt
1/2 cup vanilla whey protien powder
2 cups fresh brewed and cooled unsweetened green tea

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and cover with the lid.
on medium speed select frape and allow to blend for about 3 - 5 minutes.
You may drink as a meal or divide it into four snacks or serve a fruity snack with the family.

Submitter: 
Rachel Dobbs
Description: 
This is a delicious mixed berry protien smoothie that is very healthy, fits in about every diet and helps burn fat and build muscle. Even my kids love this as a fruit smoothie!
