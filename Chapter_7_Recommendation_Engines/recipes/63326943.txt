Title:
Chicken Veggie Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/881657.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons olive oil
3/4 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast meat - cut into bite-size pieces
1 small diced onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup diced carrots
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
4 cups fat free chicken broth
1 packet Hidden Valley Ranch seasoning
3 Tablespoons tomato paste
1 cup chopped fresh spinach
1 cup frozen or fresh corn
1 can northern beans, undrained
fresh or pickled diced jalepenos

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. add the chicken and saute for 5 to 10 minutes, or until browned; set aside.
To the same saucepan add diced vegetables and saute. Add the chicken broth, ranch seasoning, tomato paste and reserved chicken.
Stir together well, bring to a slow boil, reduce heat to low and simmer for 25 minutes. Stir in the spinach, corn and beans.  Add diced jalapenos to individual serving bowls as desired.

Kid-friendly soup with lots of veggies.  Complete the meal with a salad and some pesto bread sticks.Modified from: Chicken Veggie Soup II
