Title:
Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large white potatoes, cubed
6 large cloves of garlic, crushed
1 T. Kosher salt
4 T. butter
4 oz. cream cheese
1/3 c. milk
1 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper

Directions:
In a large stock pot add the potatoes, garlic and salt and cover with water.  Bring to a boil over high heat then reduce heat to medium and boil for 15 minutes, or until potatoes are fork tender.
Strain the potatoes and return to pan while they are still hot.  Add the remaining ingredients to the pot.  Use a potato masher to mash the potatoes to desired consistency.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
These mashed potatoes are always a hit.  They taste like restaurant quality, and my secret is the cream cheese
