Title:
Camille's Buttermilk Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c Crisco
2 c Sugar
4 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
3 c flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
¾ tsp. salt
1 ½ c buttermilk

Directions:
In a large bowl cream Crisco and sugar with electric mixer until fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time beating after each addition, then add the vanilla.
In a separate bowl combine together the baking soda, baking powder, salt and flour.
Add the flour mixture to the Crisco mixture alternating with the buttermilk.
Bake at 350 in a greased tube (Bundt) pan for 1 hour or until done.
Sprinkle with confectionery sugar when cool.
