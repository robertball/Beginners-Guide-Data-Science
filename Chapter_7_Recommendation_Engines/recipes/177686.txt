Title:
Prego® Easy Skillet Chicken Parm Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979671.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 1/2 cups Prego® Traditional Italian Sauce
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Heat oil in 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook 10 minutes or until well browned on both sides.
Stir sauce and  3 tablespoons Parmesan cheese in skillet. Reduce heat to medium. Cover and cook 10 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and remaining Parmesan cheese over chicken. Let stand 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Chicken breasts are simmered in traditional pasta sauce, then topped with mozzarella cheese.
