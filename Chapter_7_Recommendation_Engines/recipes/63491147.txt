Title:
Beef, Mac and Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg.  Mac and Cheese
1 lb. lean ground beef
14 1/2 oz. Italian stewed tomatoes undrained
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 c. Shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Prepare mac and cheese in large saucepan as directed on package omitting butter.  Brown meat in skillet, drain.  Mix together with tomatoes and oregano.  Spoon into a 9"baking dish and sprinkle with cheese.  Place in Oven of 400 degrees for about 10 minutes until cheese melts.
