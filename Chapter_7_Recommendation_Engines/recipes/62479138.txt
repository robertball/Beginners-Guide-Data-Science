Title:
Joe's home made Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 large onion, chopped
5 pounds fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 1/4 cups butter or margarine
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
2.5 teaspoon pepper
1.5 teaspoon salt
10 cup milk
10 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup minced fresh parsley
ground nutmeg
Sour cream

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute onion and mushrooms in butter for 3 minutes or until onion is tender. Stir in flour, pepper and salt; gradually add milk and broth. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Add parsley and nutmeg if desired. Top individual servings with a dollop of sour cream.

Submitter: 
Joseph Brojakowski Jr.
Description: 
This easy recipe turns commonplace ingredients into a wonderfully tasty soup.Modified from: Elegant Mushroom Soup
