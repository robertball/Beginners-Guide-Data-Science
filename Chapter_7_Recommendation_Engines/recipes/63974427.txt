Title:
Cheddar and Swiss Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large potatoes, thinly sliced
2 T. flour
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 1/2 c. shredded sharp cheddar, divided
5 oz. swiss cheese, grated or chopped fine, divided
2 med. onions, finely chopped
1 1/2 c. milk
2 T minced fresh parsley

Directions:
Place 1/3 potatoes in an oiled shallow 3 qt. baking dish.
In small bowl, combine flour, salt, & pepper.  sprinkle 1/2 over potatoes.
Sprinkle with 1/3 c. each of swiss and cheddar cheese and 1/2 the onions.
Repeat layers, then top with remaining 1/3 potatoes.
Pour milk over all.
Cover and bake at 350 for 50 - 60 min or until potatoes are nearly done.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake, uncovered, 10 min. longer or until cheese is melted and potatoes are tender.
Sprinkle with parsley.
