Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C cooked sweet potatoes, mashed
1 stick margarine, softened
2 eggs
1 t vanilla
1 C white sugar
Topping:
1 c brown sugar
1/3 c margaine, melted
1/2 c flour
1 c chopped nuts, pecans or walnuts

Directions:
Mix cooked mashed sweet potatoes with margarine, eggs, vanilla and sugar.  Pour into a lightly greased baking dish or casserole dish.
Combine the topping ingredients until thoroughly mixed and place on top of the potatoes.  Bake for 35 minutes at 350*.

Submitter: 
Adina Ryan
Description: 
This is one of my North Carolina family recipes and a  favorite especially at Thanksgiving.  I never liked sweet potatoes until I tasted this dish.  Sweet like a dessert.
