Title:
Creamy Tomato Basil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1765391.jpg
Ingredients:
2 T butter
2 T olive oil
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 tsp. dried or 2 T fresh parley finely chopped
2 tsp. dried basil or 2 T fresh basil finely chopped
fresh cracked pepper and sea salt to taste
3 14 oz. cans diced tomatoes
1 clove garlic, finely minced
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup heavy cream
2-3 tsp. sugar

Directions:
Heat olive oil and butter in heavy sauce pan. Add onion, parsley, basil, salt and pepper and saut√© until onions are translucent.
Add tomatoes, broth and garlic and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
Using a stick (immersible) blender, blend mixture until smooth. Reduce heat to low and slowly add heavy cream.  Add sugar for desired sweetness.
