Title:
Queso Blanco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup monterey jack cheese
8 ounces green chilies
1/2 cup half-and-half
4 tablespoons onions, chopped fine
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1 serrano pepper, chopped fine (optional)
1 tablespoon fresh cilantro, chopped fine (optional)

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a double broiler and cook until smooth and creamy.  Serve with chips.

Submitter: 
Love2cook32
Description: 
This is a the white cheese dip that is served at popular mexican food restaurants.
