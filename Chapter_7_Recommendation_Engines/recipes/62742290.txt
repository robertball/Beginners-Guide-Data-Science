Title:
Ensalada De Lentejas (Lentil Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. Goya Lentils
1 stalk celery
1 carrot
Fresh thyme (optional)
1 onion, peeled
1 clove garlic, peeled
1 bay leaf
1 whole clove
2 packets Sazón Goya sin Achiote
Salt and pepper to taste
Dressing
1/2 cup Goya Olive Oil
3 tbsp. Goya Red Wine Vinegar
2 tbsp. minced red onion
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 whole Goya Pimiento, minced
1 tsp. Goya Adobo with Pepper

Directions:
1.	Combine all dressing ingredients in a mixing bowl. Set aside.
2.	Rinse and drain lentils (do not soak).
3.	In a medium saucepan or soup pot, cover lentils with water. Add celery, carrot, thyme, peeled onion, peeled garlic, bay leaf, clove, Sazón, salt and pepper.
4.	Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, covered, about 30 minutes, or until lentils are tender but firm.
5.	Drain and transfer lentil mixture into a large bowl.
6.	Discard onion, garlic and bay leaf. Remove carrot and celery, slice, and mix back into lentils.
7.	Add dressing and mix thoroughly.
8.	Serve warm over lettuce, garnished with sliced pimiento.

Submitter: 
Timothy Kelty
Description: 
Lentils, chick peas, beans galore are grown in Old Castile. This lentil salad, very much like a French version, is best served warm.
