Title:
Hummus and Artichoke Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/835215.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons hummus
1 large whole-wheat sandwich wrap
3 marinated artichoke hearts, cut into small pieces
1/4 cup shredded fat-free mozzarella cheese
1 roasted red bell pepper, cut into small pieces
1 cup fresh spinach leaves

Directions:
Spread hummus onto sandwich wrap. Arrange artichoke hearts and red bell pepper pieces atop the hummus. Scatter mozzarella cheese over artichokes and bell pepper; top with spinach. Roll the sandwich wrap around the ingredients to serve.

Submitter: 
WJESSIE77
Description: 
Very easy to make wraps. Can be made individually as a meal for one person or make multiples, cut into sections, and serve as an appetizer.
