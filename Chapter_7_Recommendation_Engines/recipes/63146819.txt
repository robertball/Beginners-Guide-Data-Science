Title:
Allie's Mother-In-Law's Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/808743.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup red wine vinegar (or balsamic vinegar if preferred)
2/3 cup oil (blend several kinds or use one  i.e. oilive, vegetable, canola)
1/2 to 1 tsp course ground Black pepper ( lots of fresh ground black pepper)
1 clove of garlic
1 cup Blue cheese, divided

Directions:
Blend oil, vinegar, pepper, and garlic using an imersion blender until mixed.  Then add half of the blue cheese and blend again using immersion blender.  Then mix in the remaining blue cheese without the blender.  Store in a mason jar in refrigerator.  Shake well before serving.

Submitter: 
AllieGeekPi
Description: 
My MIL always made this for us.  It's the perfect blend of blue cheese, vinegar, garlic and black pepper for a blue cheese dressing that's a change of pace from the typical creamy blue cheese dressing.
