Title:
Salmon -Wasabi Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 ounces sliced smoked salmon
8 oz. package of cream cheese
2 1/2 teaspoons wasabi powder (add/delete depending on how much of a kick you want)
4 teaspoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons sour cream
4 flour tortillas

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, wasabi powder, lemon juice, and sour cream together.  Spread over four flour tortillas and cover with slices of smoked salmon. Roll up like a Swiss roll and trim off ends. Cover with clingfilm and put in fridge for two hours, Slice in 1/2 inche pinwheels and serve.
