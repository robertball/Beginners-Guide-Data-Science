Title:
Glazed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 sm. bag baby carrots
3/4 c. brown sugar
1/2 stick butter
1 C. water
Salt to taste

Directions:
Put carrots, sugar, butter, salt, and water into medium saucepan
Cook until tender. Water should be mostly cooked out.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
These are my favorite carrots. I like to make them with meals involving chicken and alfredo sauce. There is a nice sweet/savory balance.
