Title:
Bacon Dijon Cheddar Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
4 slices of bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
2 Tbsp. finely chopped onion
fresh herb for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, mustard and cheddar cheese in mixer on medium speed until blended.  Add bacon and onion.  Stir gently until mixed in well.  Transfer mixture to serving bowl.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.  The flavors need to blend.  Garnish before serving with chopped fresh parsley, chives or cilantro or finely julienned green onions.  Serve with crackers or crostini.

Submitter: 
Kathy W.
Description: 
These flavors blend so nicely that you can use this for just about any occasion from a football party to a women's tea!
