Title:
East Coast Shrimp Creole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/429656.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb small (shell-on) shrimp (36 - 45 count)
2 1/2 cups water
1 small onion (diced)
2 sticks celery (chopped)
2 bell peppers (chopped)
2 T creole seasoning
2 28oz. cans diced tomatoes
4 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Clean and devein shrimp, saving the shells.  Set shrimp aside.
Boil the shells in water and reduce to 3/4 cup.  (approx. 15 minutes) Pour through a strainer into a large saute pan and discard the shells.
Add onion, celery, and peppers to the saut√© pan and simmer until most of the liquid has been absorbed.  (approx. 15 minutes) Add the diced tomatoes and creole seasoning. Simmer on low 30 minutes to thicken.  Add shrimp and cook until pink.
Serve over cooked rice.

Submitter: 
SHORECOOK
Description: 
I created this dish for friends several years ago while vacationing in Florida.  Recently, when they came for dinner at the Jersey Shore, they requested it again.  Having made this from Florida to Jersey, it gets it's name East Coast Shrimp Creole.
