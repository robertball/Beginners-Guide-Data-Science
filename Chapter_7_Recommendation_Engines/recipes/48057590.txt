Title:
Spinach Salad I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup diced red onion
2 Tbs white sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon celery salt
2 tsp mustard powder
1/3 cup olive oil
1/4 cup white wine vinegar (or rice vinegar)
2 eggs
5 ounces fresh spinach - chopped, washed and dried
1/2 head iceberg lettuce - rinsed, dried, and chopped
1/2 pound bacon - cooked and crumbled
1 cups crushed croutons
2 slices red onion - separated into rings

Directions:
Place eggs in a saucepan and cover completely with cold water. Bring water to a boil. Cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, peel and slice into rings..
In a small saucepan, combine the diced onion, sugar, black pepper, celery salt, mustard, vegetable oil, and vinegar. Whisk over high heat until all ingredients are well mixed and onions are translucent. Remove from heat and cool to room temperature.
In a large bowl, combine the eggs, spinach, lettuce, bacon and croutons. Toss together.
Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat evenly. Top with onion rings.
