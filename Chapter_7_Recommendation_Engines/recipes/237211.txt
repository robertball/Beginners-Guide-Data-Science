Title:
Healthy Cereal Jar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6777245.jpg
Ingredients:
9 cups rolled oats
1 1/2 cups vanilla whey protein powder
1 1/2 cups oat bran
1 1/2 cups wheat germ
1 1/2 cups dried fruit
6 tablespoons chia seeds
6 tablespoons flax seeds
4 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 tablespoon salt

Directions:
Mix oats, protein powder, oat bran, wheat germ, dried fruit, chia seeds, flax seeds, cinnamon, and salt together in a container or jar; cover with a lid.

Submitter: 
Shawn Harquail
Description: 
Great healthy alternative to sugary and fattening breakfasts. When ready to eat, put in a bowl, add boiling water, enjoy!
