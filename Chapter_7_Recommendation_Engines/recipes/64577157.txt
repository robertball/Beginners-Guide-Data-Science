Title:
Jambalaya Recipe

Ingredients:
4 Andouille Sausage
4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
2T Butter
2 Green Bell Peppers Diced
4 Celery Stalks Diced
29oz Diced Italian Tomatoes
2T Cajun Seasoning
2T Onion Powder
3/8t Garlic Powder
1t Red Pepper Flakes
1t Black Pepper
2t Salt
1t Hot Sauce
4t Worcestershire
5c Chicken Broth
2 1/2c Rice

Directions:
Grill Meat let stand then cube up.
Saut√© peppers and celery in the butter until soft.
Add meat, tomatoes, and all other seasonings cooking about 10 minutes.
Finally add chicken broth and rice cooking until most the liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.
