Title:
Curried Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tbs butter
1 large onion, coarsely chopped
12 oz evaporated skim milk
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 1/2 tbs flour
2 tsp curry powder
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp salt
dash of pepper
4 cups cubed cooked fish
1 tsp lemon juice
2 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onions and cook about 5 minutes, remove from heat and set aside. Combine milk and stock in saucepan and bring to boil over medium heat. Remove from heat and set aside.
In a cup mix flour, curry, ginger salt and pepper. Sprinkle over onions and stir to make a paste. Cook for about 3 minutes, do not brown. Add the hot milk mixture to the skillet all at once, stirring with a whisk. Simmer stirring occasionally  for about 10 minutes or until thickened. Add fish and lemon juice to skillet and mix well. Add rice and fold in thoroughly. Heat through.
