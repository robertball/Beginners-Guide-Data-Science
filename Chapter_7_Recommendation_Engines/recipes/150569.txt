Title:
Minute速 Rice Breakfast Rice Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 cup Minute速 White Rice
1 (8 ounce) carton Dutch apple nonfat yogurt
1/4 cup raisins
1/4 cup chopped nuts
1 teaspoon cinnamon
Apple juice (optional)

Directions:
Prepare rice according to package directions.
Combine rice with yogurt, raisins, nuts and cinnamon in medium bowl; mix well.  Chill. When ready to serve, add a small amount of apple juice to moisten, if desired. Garnish, if desired.

Description:
