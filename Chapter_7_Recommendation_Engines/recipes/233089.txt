Title:
Rum-beer Float Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1123949.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle root beer
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger spiced rum
2 scoops vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Pour root beer into a mug; stir in rum. Spoon ice cream into root beer mixture; let ice cream melt slightly into the liquid, about 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
drunkfood
Description: 
This is a quick and easy dessert cocktail for parties, and it adds an adult twist to a classic root beer float.
