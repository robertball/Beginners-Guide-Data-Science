Title:
Mexican Thai Fusion Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb thinly cut chicken strips
1/4 c soy sauce
2 TBSP dark brown sugar
1/4 tsp turmeric
1 large dried Ancho chili pepper, torn
1/4 tsp allspice
1 TBSP lemongrass

Directions:
Mix chicken and seasonings in a plastic bag. Marinate for at least one hour, preferrably more.
Cook in a skillet on medium heat until thoroughly cooked (about 10-15 minutes).
Alternately, you can skewer chicken with bamboo skewers which have been soaked for about 20 minutes and cool them over a small flame.

Submitter: 
ally-gator
Description: 
Tasty marinated chicken gets a kick from smokey Anch Chili peppers. Serve with a mandarin & spring green salad and a good Reisling.
