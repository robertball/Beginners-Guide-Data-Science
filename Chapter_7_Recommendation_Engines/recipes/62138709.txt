Title:
Amazing Oven Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 med Potatoes
2 tbsp olive oil
salt
cracked black pepper
season salt

Directions:
cut potatoes into chunks, wash and dry off real well
place potatoes in a bowl, add olive oil,salt,pepper, and season salt .... about 1 tsp each , use your hand and make sure all the potatoes are well coated then place on a ungreased cookie sheet .
place in a preheated oven @ 400 degrees for 30 minutes, turning occasionally then another 30 minutes or til the inside is tender and the outside is golden brown

Submitter: 
Shannon Skeeters Fecho
Description: 
with these potatoes , you wont miss the loads of oil in regular fried potatoes. Golden on the out side,tender on the inside.
