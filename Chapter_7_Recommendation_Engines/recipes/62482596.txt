Title:
Really Cheesy Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/486814.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Medium potatoes (chunks)
2 Cans of Cheese & Broccoli (near condensed canned soups)
1 Can of condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
5 Carrots (chopped small)
2 Stalks Celery (chopped small)
1/2 Medium Sweet Yellow Onion (Minced)
6 Cups Water
2 1/2 Cups Milk
1/2 Tsp. Garlic Salt
Pepper to taste
2 Tbsp. Butter
16 Oz. Block of Velveeta Cheese (Cubed)

Directions:
Saute minced onions with butter in stock pot until tender. Add water, milk, potatoes, carrots, cellery, all 3 cans of soups, garlic salt, pepper to the onions in the stock pot and boil on high until potatoes are tender. (Stirring every few minutes) Turn down heat to low-med and mix in Velveeta Cheese cubes. Stir well and let the mixture sit on low-med heat to thicken a bit. Soup should be ready about 30 minutes later.
