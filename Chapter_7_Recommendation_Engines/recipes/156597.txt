Title:
Harvest Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980863.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) box Birds Eye® Cooked Winter Squash, thawed
3 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
8 slices day old cinnamon raisin bread, cut or torn into bite-size pieces
1/2 pint whipped cream

Directions:
In medium bowl, whisk together squash, eggs, milk, brown sugar, vanilla and nutmeg until thoroughly combined. In lightly greased 7x11-inch baking dish, place bread pieces in single layer. Pour egg mixture over top. Let stand at least 30 minutes. Bake in preheated 350 degrees F oven for 30 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Cut Into 8 squares. Serve warm with a dollop of whipped cream on top.

Submitter: 
Birds Eye®
Description: 
Don't save this dessert just for entertaining.  This warm dessert is creamy and sweet.  It is so good you will want to have it often.
