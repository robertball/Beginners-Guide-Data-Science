Title:
Bacon Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices of bacon diced
1 shallot, diced
1 small onion, diced
1/4 cup strong coffee
3 T cider vinegar
3 T dark brown sugar
1-1/2 t maple syrup

Directions:
in a frying pan over medium heat, fry bacon until fat has rendered off and strips start to brown
move bacon to a bowl, then remove all but 1 T of bacon fat from frying pan - add diced onion and shallot to pan and cook until soft and translucent
add coffee, cider vinegar, brown sugar and maple syrup - bring mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to medium low
add bacon back to pan and stir to coat - simmer mixture until its thick and syrupy, 5-10 minutes
remove to bowl and allow to cook, cover nad refrigerate

Submitter: 
M. Karen
Description: 
this is best paired with burgers, the original recipe has then stuffed into the burger with a cheese beer sauce
