Title:
Lasagna Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound turkey sausage
2 cups onions, chopped
1 cup carrots, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
6 cups chicken broth
1 (14.5 ounce)can Italian-style stewed tomatoes, chopped
1 (10 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 cup spiral pasta
2 cups fresh chopped spinach
1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/4 tsp. dried basil

Directions:
Brown sausage in large saucepan over medium high heat. Add onions and carrots; saute 3 minutes. Stir in garlic, and saute another 2 minutes.
Add broth, stewed tomatoes, and tomato sauce and bring to a boil.
Drop in pasta and basil and simmer until cooked, about 10 minutes.
Stir in spinach and cook until wilted.
Place 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese into serving bowls and pour soup on top.

Submitter: 
AppleChef
Description: 
This is literally lasagna in a bowl! If you like lasagna, this is this soup for you. Given to me from my aunt in Maine, it's a warm family favorite. We always laugh at how much it really tastes like an Italian lasagna dish!
