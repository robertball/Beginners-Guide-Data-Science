Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Chocolate Butterscotch Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982065.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
4 cups KELLOGG'S* CRISPIX* KRISPIES* cereal
1/2 cup chocolate chips
1/2 cup butterscotch chips
1/3 cup margarine or butter
42 large marshmallows

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 120 degrees C 250 degrees F.
In large microwaveable bowl microwave marshmallows and margarine at HIGH 100% for 2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute. Stir mixture until marshmallows are melted.
Add to cereal and chips mixture, stirring well until coated.
Press mixture into a buttered 3.5 L 13x9 inch baking pan.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes. Cool completely on cooling rack.
Cut into bars, squares or diamond shapes.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* and CRISPIX* KRISPIES* provide double crunch to these chocolate butterscotch bars.
