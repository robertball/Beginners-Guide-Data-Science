Title:
Potato Wedges with Cumin and Cilantro Sour Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-4 medium russet potatoes, sliced lenthwise into 3/4 inch wedges
-2 Tbs olive or vegetable oil
-1/2 tsp ground cumin
-1/2 tsp garlic salt
-1/4 tsp seasoned salt
Cilantro Sour Cream:
-1/2 cup sour cream
-2 Tbs chopped fresh cilantro
-1/4 tsp ground cumin
-dash of garlic salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 500 degrees.  Line a baking sheet with foil.  In a large bowl, toss the potatoes, oil, and next 3 ingredients, then arrange on baking sheet in a single layer.  Roast about 25 min.  While potatoes cook, combine all the ingredients for the Cilantro Sour Cream, mixing well.  Chill until potatoes are tender. Serve in a bowl next to Cumin Potatoes for dipping or dollop on top.

Submitter: 
DARLA
Description: 
Tender, hot oven baked potato wedges served with a cool dipping sauce.Serve as a side dish or an appetizer with a somewhat southwestern flavor.
