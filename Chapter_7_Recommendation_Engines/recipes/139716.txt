Title:
Refreshing Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2028015.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
8 cups water
1 1/2 cups lemon juice

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener and 1 cup water.  Bring to boil and stir to dissolve SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener. Allow to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate until chilled.
Remove seeds from lemon juice, but leave pulp. In pitcher, stir together chilled syrup, lemon juice and remaining 7 cups water.
