Title:
Company Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cans French Style Green Beans
2 TBSP Butter
1 Small Can Pet Milk
2 Cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 Small Jar of Pimento's
1 Cup Bread Crumbs of Crushed Frito's

Directions:
Drain green beans and place in a large microwaveable bowl.  Combine butter, pet milk, cheddar cheese and pimentos in a sauce pan and heat over low heat until cheese is melted.
Pour cheese mixture over green beans and top with bread crumbs. Microwave 5 minutes or until hot.
