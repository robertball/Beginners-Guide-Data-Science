Title:
Breakfast Baked Potato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 baked potato
1/3 cup of cooked crumbled sausage(i use a precooked for a time saver)
1/3 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
1 over easy cooked egg

Directions:
split your baked potato open,butter if desired,salt and pepper to taste,add cooked sausage,shredded cheddar,and top with over easy hot egg to melt the cheese. you could also add salsa,or sour cream,or chives,the toppings are endless use your imagination and taste buds to guide you.

Submitter: 
Karen Steinke Reilly
Description: 
baked potato topped with sausage egg and cheddar cheese.
