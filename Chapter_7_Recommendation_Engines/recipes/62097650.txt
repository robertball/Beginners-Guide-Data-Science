Title:
Beef and veggie soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cubed serlion steak
4 large potatoes (any kind)
2 stalks celery
1 large onion
1 green pepper
6 cloves of garlic
1 28oz can plum tomatoes (sliced up)
4 cups beef broth
2 cups water
oregano, salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot brown beef, season with salt and pepper.
Chop up onion and potatoes and add them to the browned beef.
Add chopped up celery and green pepper.
Mince the garlic and add to pot.
Pour in water and bring to boil.
Add tomatoes(with juice), beef broth, oregano, salt and pepper.
Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
Lower heat to min and cover.
Cook for 3 hours sttirring occasionally or until meat is tender.

Description: 
Delicious on a cold night.  Serve with crackers or crusty bread.  You can substitute any veggies that you like.
