Title:
Dill Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
16 oz. sour cream
1 c. mayonnaise
3 tbsp. minced onion
3 tbsp. dill weed
1 tbsp. seasoned salt
1 round loaf any bread
1 extra loaf for dipping

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except bread.  Blend thoroughly.  Refrigerate several hours.  Cut top off from round bread to use as a serving "dish."  Fill with dip.  Cut top of loaf and extra loaf into small cubes for dipping.
