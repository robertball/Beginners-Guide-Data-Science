Title:
Zipper Bag Omelets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 quart-sized zipper top freezer bags
(don't use the thinner storage bags)
1 dozen large eggs
Omelette ingredients; Chopped ham, crumbled bacon, shredded cheese, onions, peppers, tomatoes, s, olives, salsa, etc.

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil.  Have each guest write their name on a bag with a permanent marker;   2 eggs into each bag and shake.  Add ingredients as desired and shake again.  Zip up the bags, getting all of the air out.  Place the bags into the water for exactly 13 minutes.  Omelettes will roll out of the bags when unzipped.

Submitter: 
Debra
Description: 
These little omelets all cook at the same time in a big pot of water .. fast and easy for family gatherings or camping trips! Everybody makes their own! Wonderful served with fresh fruit and coffee cake.
