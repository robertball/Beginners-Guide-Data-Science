Title:
Asian Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup canola oil
3 T lime juice
3 T Rice Vinegar
1 can anchovies
1 t honey
1 jalapeno, chopped
1 cabbage, shredded
1 carrot, julienned
2 or 3 green onions, sliced.

Directions:
In a large bowl mix cabbage, carrots, and green onions. Mix rest of ingredients in a small food processor.  Pour dressing over cabbage and mix together.

Submitter:

Description: 
Fresh tasting slaw that goes well with fish or grilled chicken
