Title:
Almond Coffee Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy whipping cream
2 cups half and half
1 (14 oz) can of sweetened condenced milk
1 tbsp vanilla extract
1 tbsp almond extract
2 tbsp instant coffee granules

Directions:
Mix all liquid ingredients together. Add instant coffee granules and mix until disolved. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour before churning.
