Title:
Feta bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Extra light virgin olive oil
4-5 Roma tomatoes
1 bunch of green onions
1 pack of traditional feta cheese
Cavenders Greek seasoning

Directions:
Cover the bottom of a shallow dish with olive oil. About a 1 to 1.5 quart dish is good.
Dice tomatoes and spread evenly over top of olive oil.
Chop green onions starting at bulb about half way into dark green part and sprinkle over tomatoes. (make sure you cut off and discard roots).
Smash feta so that it isn't so chunky sprinkle over tomato and onions.
Liberally add seasoning to your liking.
Serve with French bread or tortilla chips. Also really great on pizza.

Submitter: 
JnLAllin
Description: 
Easy, Yummy appetizer. Great for parties or just because. 

Great thing about this recipe is you can make it how you like. Less onion more onion, less seasoning more seasoning.  Anyway you make it, tastes great.
