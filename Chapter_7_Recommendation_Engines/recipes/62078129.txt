Title:
Cook and Serve Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 bananas
vanilla wafers
1 lg box Cook and Serve Vanilla Pudding
2 c. half and half
1 c. milk(may use all half and half)
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Make pudding according to directions on box, substituting 2 cups of half and half for 2 cups of regular milk.  Add vanilla.
Layer cookies, bananas, then pudding, ending with cookies on top.

Submitter: 
Mary Ann Lackey
Description: 
Tweaking the Cook and Serve Pudding makes it very smooth and creamy. We like it as well as made from scratch.
