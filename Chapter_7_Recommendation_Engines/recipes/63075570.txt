Title:
Lentil & Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoons olive oil
1 large onions, cubed
1 1/2 baby carrots, sliced
1 stalk celery, diced
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 can petite diced tomatoes
3 1/2 cups chicken stock
1 cup lentils - soaked, rinsed and drained
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 bay leaves
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon sweet smoked paprika
1 teaspoon sage
1/2 teaspoon ground bay leaves
1/2 cup white wine

Directions:
Saute the onions in oil in a killet until they are glossy.
Stir in celery and carrots, and saute for 10 minutes.
Add the garlic and saute for 2 minutes until garlic is fragrant.
Transfer vegetables to a medium stockpot and stir in tomatoes, chicken stock, lentils and spices.
Stir well, then add the wine and bring the mixture to a boil.
Slowly reduce the heat and cook covered for 1 hour on low to medium heat.  Remove cover and cook on medium high for about 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Vickie Boozer
Description: 
Onions, garlic, carrots, celery, tomatoes, paprika and bay leaves flavor this comforting lentil soup, cooked in an aromatic broth of white wine and chicken stock.Modified from: Lentil Soup
