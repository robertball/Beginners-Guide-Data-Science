Title:
Chicken & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/490419.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 chicken breast
2 cans chicken broth
1 can cream of chicken
2 cups rice
If you like to add:
corn or broccoli
sharp cheddar cheese
cresant rolls

Directions:
bake chicken & cut into small pieces. Put cut up chicken, chicken broth, & cream of chicken in crock pot. Then when you get home from work add 2 cups of rice.  Stir & add water as needed.
