Title:
Easy Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium chopped onion, 3 T. butter, 1 lb. ground chuck, 1 small head coursely shredded cabbage, 1 10&1/2 oz. tomatoe soup, 3/4 t. salt, 1/4 t. pepper

Directions:
Saute chopped onion in butter. Add ground chuck, and seasonings, heating thru but not browning. Mix in cabbage and tomatoe soup. Cover and bake 1 hour at 350 degrees.
