Title:
Smokey Parmesan Sweet Potato Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large sweet potatoes
1/4 cup Shaved Parmesan Cheese
1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp smoked paprika
1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Peel the potato with a vegetable peeler and discard the skin. Continue to use the vegetable peeler to make thin strips of sweet potato. when finished drizzle the potato with olive oil. Toss the potato strips with the remaining spices and place the strips on an oven safe cooling rack or parchment line baking sheet. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until crisp.

Submitter: 
Korben
Description: 
After trying several potato chip recipes this one is a collage of the most successful recipes I have tried. The recipe could easily be modified with additions of other spices to suit any taste or any meal. These are great with burgers and are much healthier than traditional chips!
