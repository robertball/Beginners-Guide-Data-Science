Title:
ATK Blueberry Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T lemon juice
2 cup milk
3 T butter, melted and cooled
1 egg, large
2 cups flour (10 oz)
2 T sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup blueberries, rinsed and drained on paper towels (to remove excess juice that would stain pancakes).
2 T vegetable oil

Directions:
Curdle milk with lemon juice and set aside
Melt butter and allow to cool slightly
Whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and salt.
Whisk butter and egg into milk mixture.
Make a well in center of flour mix, add milk mixture, and lightly whisk only until just incorporated.
Heat griddle for 3 to 5 minutes, then brush with vegetable oil (only as needed).  Ladle batter onto skillet, then sprinkle blueberries onto pancakes.
Cook until large bubbles begin to appear (1 to 1 1/2 minutes), then flip and continue to cook until golden brown (1 to 1 1/2 minutes more).
Serve immediately.
