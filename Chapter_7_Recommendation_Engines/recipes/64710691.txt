Title:
Cold Soba Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 (3 cup capacity) storage containers
5 (2 ounce) dressing containers?
1 (14 ounce) package of Soba noodles?
1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
1 large carrot, shredded?
4 green onions, thinly sliced?
1 cup shelled edamame?
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped?
10 ounces (1 1/4 cups) Thai peanut sauce

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain.
In a medium bowl, toss the noodles with sesame oil. Divide the noodles evenly between the 5 containers.
Top the noodles with carrot, green onions, edamame and cilantro, dividing these evenly between the 5 containers. Place lid on each container. Place 2 ounces of peanut sauce into each dressing container. Refrigerate until ready to eat.
When ready to serve, mix one dressing container with one serving of noodle salad until evenly coated.
Cook's note: Thai peanut sauce can be found in the Asian section of your grocery store. Pre-shelled edamame can be found in the frozen section.?

Submitter: 
B. Tario
Description: 
This cold salad is easy to make and great for prepping healthy options for the weeks lunches or snacks.  I like to add some heat using Sriracha or chili garlic paste mixed in the Thai peanut sauce.
