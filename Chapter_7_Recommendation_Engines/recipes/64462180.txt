Title:
Spinach Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large eggs, beaten
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 tbl cooking fat
1/2 onion, diced (plus a few slices for topping the frittata)
1 bag (9oz) baby spinach, roughly chopped
1/4 lemon, jucied and zested

Directions:
1. Set the oven to broil
2. In a mixing bowl, whisk the eggs with the salt and pepper
3. heat a large oven-safe skillet over med heat. add the cooking fat. Add onion and tomato until softened, 2-3 minutes. Add spinach and let it wilt for 30 secs. Add the eggs and fold them into the vegetables. Cook, without stirring to let the eggs set on the bottom and sides of the pan, 3-4 mins. Lay a few tomato clices on top. Drizzle the  lomon juice and sprinkle the lemon zest over the top..
4. Transfer the pan with the eggs to the oven and broil 4 -6 in from the heat. for 3-5 min. until the top is golden brown. Cut into clices and serve hot out of the pan.
