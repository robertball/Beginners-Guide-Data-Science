Jess' Huli Huli Chicken Marinade Recipe

Ingredients:
1/4 c. worshtershire (sp)?...
1/4c. rice wine...

1/2 c brown sugar...
4 cloves minced garlic...
1/3 c ketchup...
1 pcs 1/2 inch thick ginger root crushed...
1 round onion (med) chopped...

Best Cooked on a rotisarie over hot coals
