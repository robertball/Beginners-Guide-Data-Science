Title:
Potatoes Deluxe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb frozen hash browns (thawed)
1/2 stick butter or margarine
1 cup chopped onion
1 package (8 oz) cheddar cheese
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 tsp garlic salt
1 pint sour cream
1 cup crushed corn flakes (for topping)
1/4 cup butter or margarine (for topping)

Directions:
Thaw potatoes 30 mins.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix potatoes, onion, soup, sour cream, butter, cheese, and seasonings.
For topping, melt 1/4 butter and toss in crushed corn flakes.
Sprinkle corn flakes mixture on top of potatoes.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
