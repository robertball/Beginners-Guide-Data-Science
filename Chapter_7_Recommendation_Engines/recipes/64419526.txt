Title:
Asian Inspired Warm Chicken Salad - HEALTHY! (Slow Cooker) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2298074.jpg
Ingredients:
*Sauce Base
1/2 Cup White Wine Vinegar
1/2 Cup Rice Wine Vinegar
3/4 Cup Peanut Oil
1/4 Cup Sesame Oil
1/4 Cup Sriracha
1/4 Cup lite Soy Sauce
1 Table Spoon Onion Powder
2 Table Spoons Garlic Salt
2 Table Spoons Ginger Powder
1 Table Spoon Black Pepper
*For the crock pot
2 Cups Filtered Water
8 - 10 boneless/skinless chicken strips
1 package frozen stir fry veggies
(or use fresh broccoli, snap peas, red peppers, onions, mushrooms and carrots)
*Serving:
Cooked Rice
Raw Spinach

Directions:
Add all the sauce base items into a bowl and whisk.
Add the water to the base and whisk again.
Place chicken in low heat crock, top with sauce/water combo
Add frozen (or fresh) veggies, give a gentle stir.
Cook on low for 3 hours, drop to keep warm
Make rice
Use bowls to serve, build with spinach first, then rice, use ladle to pour on a copious amount of the sauce base first then top with chicken and veggies.
