Title:
Chicken & Mushroom Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/816090.jpg
Ingredients:
9 uncooked lasagne noodles
1/4 c. margarine or butter
8 oz. (3c.) fresh mushrooms, sliced
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. lemon juice
1/4 c. flour
2 tsp. chicken-flavored instant bouillon
3 c. milk
2 c. cubed, cooked chicken
1/3 c. & 2 Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
15 oz. ricotta cheese
8 oz. (2c.) shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 c. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook lasagne to desired doneness as directed on package. Drain; rinse with hot water.
In large saucepan, melt butter; add mushrooms, garlic, salt, & lemon juice. Saute until mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in flour & chicken bouillon; blend well. Add milk. Cook over med-high heat until mixture thickens and boils, stirring constantly. Stir in chicken and 1/3c. parsley.
Heat oven to 325Â°F. To assemble, spread about 1/2c. sauce in ungreased 13x9-in. baking dish. Layer 1/3 noodles, 1/3 ricotta, 1/3 of mozzarella, 1/3 of sauce, & 1/3 of Parmesan cheese; repeat layers, ending w/Parmesan on top. Sprinkle w/2 Tbsp. of parsley.
Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly. Let stand 10-15 mins. before serving.
