Title:
Mushroom and Sesame Seed Saute' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound white mushrooms, wiped clean and thinly sliced
3 T. sesame' seed oil
2 T. peanut oil (or vegetable oil)
3 green onions, sliced diagonally, white and green included
1 T. water
salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Heat the oils in a medium-sized fry pan over med-high heat.  Add the mushrooms and saute' until golden brown, about 6 minutes.  Add the green onions, water, salt and pepper.  Saute' for another 1 or 2 minutes until water has evaporated.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
BAJATHECAT
Description: 
This is the easiest mushroom side dish.  My family loves these as a simple side dish or as a topper for grilled beef or chicken.  For a quick and light lunch, I like to make these mushrooms and toss them with boiled rice.
