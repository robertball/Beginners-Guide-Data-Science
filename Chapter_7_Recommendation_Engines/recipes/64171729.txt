Title:
Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag Bayou Magic Jambalaya Mix
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 can French Onion Soup
1 pkg. Down Home Sausage
1 lb. Shrimp (if desired)
1/2 lb. cooked Chicken (if desired)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450Â°
In a large bowl, mix together Cream of Chicken soup, French Onion soup, and 1/2 soup can of water.
Add Jambalaya Mix and mix together thoroughly.
Add sausage, shrimp, and chicken. Mix together thoroughly.
Pour mixture evenly into a large pan or casserole dish.  Cover with foil.
Bake for 1 hour and 45 minutes, checking after 1 hour and 30 minutes.
