Title:
Chicken Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/697642.jpg
Ingredients:
8oz Chicken (White Or Dark), 1/2 inch Cubed
1 cup of lettuce thinly sliced
4 oz of sour cream
4 oz of salsa (Fresh Or Goya Salsa Pico de Gallo)
4 corn tortillas
4 Oz re-fried beans
FOR THE RED SAUCE
1/4 Teaspoon Cumin
1/8 Teaspoon of Pepper
2 Cups Tomatoes
1 Jalapeno Pepper
5 Cloves of Garlic
1/4 Teaspoon of Oregano
1/4 Cup of Chicken Stock
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 onion, chopped

Directions:
In A Pan Cook Red Sauce (10-15 min), Cumin, Pepper, Tomatoes, Jalapeno Pepper, Garlic, Oregano, Chicken Stock, salt, onion. Put In Blender And Puree, Or Use An Immersion blender, Strain
as your cooking the sauce brown chicken in a frying pan, drain Excess oil
on high heat add red sauce to chicken, and cook down, stirring till sauce is thickened and cooked into the chicken.
Using A taco Forming tool deep fry corn tortillas for about 30-60 seconds, for crisper tacos fry for 1 1/2 mins. You can also fry the corn tortillas in a shallow fry pan with oil using tongs, holding the taco open as the corn tortillas cook.
heat re-fried beans in a small sauce pan on low or microwave, Remember to stir to prevent burning.
Take corn tortillas fill with 1 oz of re-fried beans each, add chicken, add Lettuce, top with Salsa, cheese, and sour cream.
chicken tacos, Done.
