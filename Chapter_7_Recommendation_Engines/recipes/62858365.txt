Title:
Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (30 oz.) pkg. frozen hash browns
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 (16 oz.) container sour cream
1 (10.75 oz.) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 onion, chopped
1 cup butter
3 cups corn flakes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425F.
Pour the hash browns into lightly greased 9x13-inch baking dish. In a large bowl, combine the cheese, sour cream and soup.
In a large skillet over medium heat, combine the onion with 1 stick butter and saut√© for 5 minutes. Add this to the soup mixture and spread over the potatoes in the dish.
Next arrange the crushed corn flakes over all in the dish. Melt the remaining stick of butter and pour this evenly over the corn flakes.
Bake at 425F for 1 hour.
