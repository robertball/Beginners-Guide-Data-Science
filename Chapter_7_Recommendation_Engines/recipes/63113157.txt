Title:
Pinkys Lemon Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup lemon juice
1 1/2 tsp. Tabasco
1 1/2 tsp. Jalepeno Tabasco
1 tsp. garlic powder or 2 cloves crushed garlic
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tbls. crushed red pepper
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix together in large bowl. Stab meat with a fork liberally. Ocassionally rotate meat in the bowl. Keep covered in refriderater ideally overnight. We found that grilling over charcoal gave it a wonderful flavor.

Submitter: 
Jessica Warden
Description: 
This citrus based marinade packs just the right amount of spice and is great for fajitas or any cut of steak. I came up with this marinade while on vacation when my husband and I had limited ingredients to work with.
