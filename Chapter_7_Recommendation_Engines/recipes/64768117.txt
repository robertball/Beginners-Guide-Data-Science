Title:
Grits Dumpling;  "Griess noekerl" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz of Whole Egg
6 oz of Salted Butter
9 oz of  med-Fine Grits
salt, Muscat (pinch),   Parsley and or Dill.

Directions:
Incorporate the Butter into the grits; mix into a meal. next;  beat the eggs  with salt,
and add them to the meal; . add the Muscat and mix into a homogeneous mass. Press down. and refrigerate for 30 min.   Set 2 quarts of Salted Water to simmer.   when ready,  use a tablespoon or Teaspoon depending on your Choice of size, and form  Large Olive size Dumplings with slightly
tapered ends.  Suspend in Simmering water.  (do not let stick to bottom). Cook for 10-15 min.  Do not Boil !)  Serve with, in Both with Chopped fresh Greens.  ( * for a Change mix the greens into the meal mix.)

Alois

Tender Dumpling of Grits, Butter and Egg.
