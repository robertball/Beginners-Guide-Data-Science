Title:
Applesauce Date Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp allspice
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/4 tsp salt
2 eggs
1 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup soft butter
2 cups hot applesauce
1 cup chopped dates
3/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. Grease and flour a 9"x9"x2" pan.
2. Sift flour with baking soda, spices, salt. Add eggs, sugar, butter and 1 cup of applesauce. At low spead, beat just until ingredients are combined.
3. At medium speed, beat 2 minutes longer, scraping bowl occasionally.
4. Add remaining applesauce, dates and walnuts. Beat an additional minute. Pour batter into prepared pan.
5. Bake 50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center come out clean. Cool 10 minutes. Remove from pan.
6. When entirely cool, frost with Cream Cheese Frosting.
