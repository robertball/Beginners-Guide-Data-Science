Title:
Peanut Butter Chocolate Granola Cereal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472258.jpg
Ingredients:
13 cups old fashioned oats
1 cup wheat germ
1 1/2 cups wheat flour
2 cups coconut, toasted
1 pkg. or 9 lg. graham crackers, lightly crushed
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups peanut butter
1 cup dark brown sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 cup honey
1/2 cup canola oil
1 T vanilla
1 1/2 cups mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingredients in large bowl. Set aside. Melt butter and peanut butter in medium saucepan over medium heat. Add sugar,salt, honey, oil and vanilla. Continue cooking just until sugar is melted (about 5 min). Pour over dry ingredients and stir well. Put into 3-13x9 pans and bake at 350 for 1 to 1-1/2 hour (or until lightly browned), stirring every 15 min. Remove from oven and immediately sprinkle over top of each pan about 1/2 cup chocolate chips and stir lightly. Let cool. Store in covered container (ice cream bucket) and keep in cool dry place.

Submitter: 
Maria Joy Schrock
Description: 
A mouth-watering granola for Chocolate peanut butter lovers. Add cold milk to it in the morning and it will be a great, healthy start to your day. I can't wait to get up in the morning if I have this on hand!
