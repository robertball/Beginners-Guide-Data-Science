Title:
Slow Cooker Apple Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 apples â€“ peeled, cored and chopped into small cubes
3 cups water
3 cups sugar
2 1/2 cups dark brown sugar
4 tbsp lemon juice
5 tsp ground cinnamon
2 tsp ground cloves
2 tsp ground allspice
pinch of salt

Directions:
1) Add cut apples into the crockpot
2) In a large bowl add all other ingredients and mix well.
3) Pour mixture over apples and stir well.
4) Cook on high for 1 hour. Stir.
5) Cook on low for 10 hours making sure to stir often.
6) Cook on high until desired thickness is reached (approximately 1-2 hours)

Submitter: 
Brandi Starr
Description: 
After apple picking with the kids I combined two recipes and it turned out amazing. It tastes great on everything and while it is cooking it makes the house smell yummy.
