Title:
Ranger Cookies (Cookie Mix) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985221.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound 1.5 ounce) pouch Betty Crocker® oatmeal cookie mix
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 tablespoon water
1 egg
3/4 cup crisp rice cereal
1/2 cup flaked coconut

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees F. In large bowl, stir all ingredients until soft dough forms. Drop dough by rounded teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until edges are golden brown. Cool 1 minute; remove from cookie sheet. Cool completely.

Submitter: 
Betty Crocker®
Description: 
Packing cookies in lunches? Here's a great pack-and-munch treat.
