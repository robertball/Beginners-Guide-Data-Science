Title:
Prickly Pear Tonic and Lime Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/4 cup prickly pear juice
1/4 cup diet tonic water
1/4 lime, juiced

Directions:
Mix water, prickly pear juice, diet tonic water, and lime juice together in a glass.

Description: 
Wonderfully refreshing drink after a long run or in the morning to rehydrate.
