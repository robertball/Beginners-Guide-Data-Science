Title:
Rachel's Mother's Spinach Salad with Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad Dressing
1/2 Cup sugar
1 tsp. dry mustard
1 tsp. salt
1 small onion
1/3 Cup white vinegar
1 Cup oil (prefer canola oil)
Toss over:
Assorted greens- Bibb, Iceberg and Spinach
1-2 cans Mandarin Oranges, drained
Chinese Noodles
Cashew nuts

Directions:
In a blender or food processer put in the sugar, dry mustard, salt, small onion and process for 1 minute.  Add half of the white vinegar and continue blending. With the blender or food processer running, slowly add the salad oil and remaing vinegar. Dressing should be creamy in appearance. Store in a jar with lid until ready to serve. Note: You may not need to use all the salad dressing. Store in the refrigerator until ready to use again.
In a large salad bowl, place the assorted greens, Mandarin Oranges and Cashew nuts. Pour the dressing over and toss.  Add the Chinese Noodles and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
This recipe is always requested whenever I bring it to parties. Simple to make, attractive to serve and delicious. The combination of Mandarin oranges with onion  Cashew nuts and a sweet/sour dressing is outstanding. Add grilled chicken breast for a heart healthy menu.
