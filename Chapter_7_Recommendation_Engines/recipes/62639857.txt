Title:
Awesome Eggplant Pasta Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 tsp olive oil
1 sweet onion
10 fresh basil leaves
1 28 oz can whole tomatoes
1 portabella mushroom chopped
1 eggplant peeled and cubed
1 red bell pepper diced
Fresh Mozzarella cubed
1 lb Rigatoni

Directions:
Saute onion in olive oil until translucent.
Add can of whole tomatoes and fresh basil to sauteed onion.
In another large pan saut√© 1 portablello mushroom, eggplant, and red pepper until soft.
Add tomato sauce to eggplant mixture and simmer for 30 minutes.
Cook rigatoni as directed.
Toss rigatoni with fresh mozzarella and tomato sauce.
Garnish with fresh basil and serve immediately.
