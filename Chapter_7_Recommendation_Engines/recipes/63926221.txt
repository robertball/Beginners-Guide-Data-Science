Title:
Kathy's Texas Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons cooking oil
2 pounds beef top sirloin, thinly sliced
1 pounds hot Italian sausage, casings removed
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tablespoons cayenne pepper
6 tablespoons chili powder
3 tablespoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 teaspoons salt
14.5 ounces diced tomatoes
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 can chicken broth
1/4 cup honey
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
Shredded Cheddar cheese
Sour Cream

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat; cook the steak, sausage, onion, and garlic in the pot until the onions are soft, about 5-10 minutes.
In a small bowl combine cayenne, chili powder, oregano, salt and pepper.  Add to the above mixture and cook for 3-5 minutes.
Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, chicken broth, honey, and kidney beans; bring to a boil.
Set slow cooker on LOW and cook for 4 hours or more.
Serve in bowls topped with shredded cheddar cheese and/or sour cream (both optional)
