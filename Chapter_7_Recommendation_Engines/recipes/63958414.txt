Title:
Big Ray's Southwest  Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1098219.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
1 can (14-1/2 oz.) beef broth
1 can (16 oz.) roasted tomatoes and green chiles, undrained
1 can (14 1/2 oz.) corn
1 can (14 1/2 oz.) black beans, rinsed and drained
1 large bell pepper, cut into thin strips
2 cups white rice, cooked
1-1/2 cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Brown meat in large oven-proof skillet, drain grease. Add onions and let soften. Stir in broth and tomatoes; bring to boil.
Add peppers, corn beans and rice; stir. Cover; simmer on low heat 5 min.

Submitter:

Description: 
Want creamy, cheesy Southwest authenticity? It's right here, with savory ground beef, bell peppers and green chiles in one easy 25-minute skillet.
