Title:
Letha's Caramel Frosting for Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/617896.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c. margarine
1 c. brown sugar
1/4 milk, half/n/half, or cream.
1 lb package of powdered sugar

Directions:
Melt margarine in saucepan over medium low heat.  Add brown sugar and cook over medium heat until it bubbles.
Remove from heat.
Let cool slightly then stir in milk.
Heat again until mixture boils.  (Stir constantly).
Remove from heat.
With a mixer beat in about 1 lb of powdered sugar to attain spreading consistency.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Recipe given to me by my mother-in-law for my husband's favorite cake and frosting.  That was in 1968!  Still a family favorite.  Works best on sheet cake as you have to work fast before it 'sets up'.
