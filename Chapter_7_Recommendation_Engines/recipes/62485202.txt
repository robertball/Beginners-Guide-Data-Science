Title:
Mom's Mexican Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. hamburger
1 large onion diced
8 oz (1/2 bag?) small elbow macaroni, cooked and drained
1 can french onion soup
1 8 oz can tomato sauce
8 oz cheddar cheese, grated
1 tsp celery salt
2 tsp pepper
2 Tbl chili powder

Directions:
Brown hamburger, onion, salt, pepper, celery salt, chili powder.  Add soup and tomato sauce. Simmer for about a half an hour. Add cooked macaroni.  Transfer to 9x13 casserole dish.  Top with cheese.  Cook at 350Â° until cheese is melted and warmed through, about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sharon Springer Andrews
Description: 
Forget any notions of Hungarian Goulash with paprika, this is Mom's Goulash and in Texas they like chili powder!  Serve with au gratin potatoes for a warm your tummy winter meal.Modified from: Mom's Goulash
