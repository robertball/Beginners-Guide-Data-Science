Title:
Spiced Cider Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups apples, peeled, cored and cut into chunks (or sliced)
1 cup apple cider
1 Tbsp cinnamon
Dash nutmeg
Dash ground cloves
1/4 cup flour
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup quick oats*
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 Tbsp cinnamon
1/4 cup butter, cut into pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Butter a 2-qt baking dish.
In saucepan, combine cider, 1 Tbsp cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves.  Place over medium-high heat; whisk in flour.  Cook, whisking constantly, until mixture thickens and begins to boil.  Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
Pour cider mixture over apples; toss to coat. Pour into prepared baking dish.
In separate bowl, combine oats, brown sugar and 1 Tbsp cinnamon.  Cut in butter with a fork, two butter knives or pastry blender until throughly mixed. Sprinkle over apples.
Bake for 30 to 40 minutes until golden brown and bubbly. Let sit 10 minutes before serving.
