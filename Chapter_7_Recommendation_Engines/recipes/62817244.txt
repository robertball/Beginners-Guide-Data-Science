Title:
Banana Pudding a la Robert Fillingim Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 very ripe bananas
5 large boxes vanilla pudding
13 1/3 cups whole milk
1 pound bag vanilla wafers
12 oz whipped topping

Directions:
Line the bottom of a plastic food storage box with vanilla wafers and then the bananas slices. pour pudding over the layers.  decorate top edge with wafers.  spoon topping over pudding. chill for a hour.

Submitter: 
Woodbine United Methodist
Description: 
this makes a crowd pleasing, party sized bowl of pudding.
