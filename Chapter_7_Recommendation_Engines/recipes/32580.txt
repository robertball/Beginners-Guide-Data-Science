Title:
Chocolate Mug Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4477837.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups chocolate ice cream
2 cups milk
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a blender, combine ice cream, milk, brown sugar, white sugar and cinnamon. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
LADYSU
Description: 
If you like chocolate and you have a sweet tooth then this is your drink. It tastes like hot chocolate but it's cold!
