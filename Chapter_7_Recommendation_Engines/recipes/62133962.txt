Title:
Cowboy Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ½ cups flour
½ tsp salt
2 cups brown sugar
2/3 cups butter
½ tsp cinnamon
½ tsp nutmeg
1 cup chopped nuts
2 tsp Baking Power
½ tsp baking soda
1 cup sour milk (milk & 1 TBL vinegar)
2 whole eggs

Directions:
Mix first seven ingredients until crumbly.  Reserve 1 cup for topping.
Add remaining ingredients:
Mix well.  Pour into two 8” round pans.  Sprinkle with reserved crumb mixture.  Bake at 375° for 30 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
