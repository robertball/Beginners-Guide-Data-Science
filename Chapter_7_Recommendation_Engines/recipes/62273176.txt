Title:
The Cheesiest Lasagna Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/345972.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 pound Italian sausage
1 yellow onion, chopped
1 handful fresh mushrooms, chopped
1 2.25 ounce cans black olives, chopped
3 Roma tomatoes, chopped
2 cloves fresh garlic, chopped (divided)
2 bay leaves
2 26 ounce jars spaghetti sauce with basil seasoning
1 tbsp Italian seasoning
2 tsp dried oregano
1 tbsp garlic powder (divided)
2 tsp black pepper (divided)
1 tsp salt
1 pound Ricotta cheese
2 eggs, beaten
3 cups Mozzarella cheese, shredded (divided in 3 parts)
1 cup Monterrey Jack cheese, shredded
1 cup Provolone cheese, shredded
1 cup Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese (divided)
1 package lasagna noodles
1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Brown hamburger meat in skillet, adding 1/2 tbsp garlic powder, 1 tsp black pepper, 1/2 tsp salt and 2 tsp dried oregano. Drain and set aside.
Brown Italian sausage in skillet, adding 1/2 tbsp garlic powder, 1 tsp black pepper, 1/2 tsp salt. Drain and set aside.
Over high heat, In Large pot, pour 2 jars spaghetti sauce, the 3 Roma tomatoes, the onion, the mushrooms and the olives. Add the 2 bay leaves, 1/2 the chopped garlic, 1 tbsp Italian seasoning. Add the drained hamburger and sausage meats. When it come to a boil, turn heat between low and medium. Cover, stirring every 15 minutes or so for 1 hour.
While sauce is cooking, in medium mixing bowl combine 2 beaten eggs, the other 1/2 of the fresh garlic, 1 pound Ricotta cheese, 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese and 1 cup Mozzarella. Cover and refrigerate.
In large pot filled 3/4 with water, add 1 tbsp olive oil, bring to boil, cook package of lasagna noodles according to directions, rinse in cold water and strain.
Spray 13x9 pan with cooking spray. Layer bottom with 6 lasagna noodles. Spread 1/2 Ricotta mixture evenly over noodles. Pour 1/2 the sauce on top. Evenly spread Provolone and Mozzarella cheese. Again, layer 6 lasagna noodles, spread the other 1/2 Ricotta mixture over noodles. Pour remaining sauce on top. Sprinkle 1 cup Mozzarella, 1 cup Cheddar and 1 cup Monterrey Jack cheese. Sprinkle remaining Parmesan cheese and then remaining garlic powder on top.
Cover tightly with foil, bake in preheated oven at 375 for one hour. Take foil off of the pan, bake an additional 10 minutes.
Allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving.
