Title:
Crystal's Quick Avocado Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1112320.jpg
Ingredients:
1 avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
1/2 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1/4 cup chopped cilantro, or to taste
1/2 lemon, juiced, or to taste
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix avocado, corn, cilantro, lemon juice, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; let sit 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Suzq777
Description: 
This is yummy dish from a great friend. I hated avocado with a capital 'H,' and this made me love it. It is a great party appetizer or a quick weeknight meal for one. Eat by itself or serve with pita bread or tortilla chips.
