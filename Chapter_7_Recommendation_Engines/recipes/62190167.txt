Title:
Pomegranate-Blueberry Smoothie! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two 6oz containers of vanilla yogurt (active cultures and pro-biotic preferred)
1 cup pomegranate juice
1 cup frozen blueberries
1 naval orange, peeled and sectioned

Directions:
Puree juice, fruit, and yogurt on HIGH 1 min, until smooth.

Submitter: 
Patrick Ashley
Description: 
This smoothie is not only good, but good for you! HUGE antioxidant punch from the pomegranate juice and blueberries, and the pro-biotic cultures in the yogurt aid digestion. What's not to love? Simple and quick to make and guaranteed to please!
