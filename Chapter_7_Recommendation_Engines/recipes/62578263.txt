Title:
Peppermint Butter Cream Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
4 cups powdered sugar
1/4 cup milk
1/8 tsp. peppermint extract

Directions:
Beat butter until creamy, scraping down the bowl as you go. Add 3 cups of sugar, milk and peppermint extract.
Beat until combined, and add more icing sugar if needed (to get a velvety texture).
