Title:
Creamcicle Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982738.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups DANNON® Vanilla Lowfat Yogurt or DANNON® Light 'n Fit™ Vanilla Nonfat Yogurt
1/3 cup orange juice concentrate, defrosted
orange zest, for garnish
shaved crystallized ginger, for garnish
Optional fresh berries, sliced pineapple and green grapes, for dipping

Directions:
Combine orange juice concentrate and Dannon(R) yogurt. Transfer to a small serving dish.
Garnish with orange zest and crystallized ginger. Arrange fresh berries, grapes or pineapple around dip and serve.

Submitter: 
DANNON®
Description: 
This easy orange flavored dip is the perfect compliment to pineapple, grapes, or your favorite seasonal fruits.
