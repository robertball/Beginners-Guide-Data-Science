Title:
Winter Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. pkg cream cheese, softened
1 3/4 cup sugar
4 eggs
2 cups flour
2 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp.salt
1 cup vegetable oil
3 cups chopped apples
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray Bundt pan with Pam.
Combine cream cheese, 1/4 cup sugar, and 1 egg.
Mix well, set aside.
Combine remaining dry ingredients.
Add oil and 3 eggs to dry ingredients.
Mix until just moistened.
Fold in apples and nuts.
Reserve 2 cups batter.
Pour remaining batter into pan.
Pour cream cheese mixture over batter and spread evenly.
Carefully spoon reserved batter over cream cheese mixture, spreading lightly to cover cream cheese mixture.
Bake for approx. 50-55 minutes or until cake tester comes out clean.
Cool 10 minutes and them remove from pan.
Cool completely and then sprinkle with powdered sugar.
