Title:
Rene's Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 C all-purpose flour
1 t salt
1/2 t baking soda
3/4 C unsalted butter, softened
1 C granulated sugar
3/4 C packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 t. vanilla
3 C semisweet chocolate pieces
3/4 C flaked coconut (optional)

Directions:
In a bowl, combine flour, salt and soda, set aside.
In lg. mixing bowl, beat butter with electric mixer 30 seconds. Add sugars. Beat on med. speed about 5 min. or until mixture is light and fluffy, scraping sides of bowl occ. Beat in eggs and vanilla. Beat in as much of the flour mixture as you can with mixer on low speed. Stir in any remaining flour mixture with a wooden spoon until combined. Stir in chocolate and coconut, if you like. You can cover and store in refrigerator 24 hrs or bake immediately.
Drop by 2 T portions or by using a med. cookie scoop 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets. Lightly press down to flatten. Bake 350 degree oven 12 min.(14 min. if chilled) or until edges are light brown. Cool on cookie sheets 1 min. Transfer to wire racks to cool.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Coconut adds chewiness. Sitting overnight mellows flavor and makes softer, browner cookies. Lots of chips.
