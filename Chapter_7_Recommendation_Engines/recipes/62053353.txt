Title:
Mama's Strawberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-8 inch baked pie shells,
2 quarts sliced strawberries,
sugar to sprinkle,
4 TBSP cornstarch,
juice of 1 lemon,
1 TBSP butter,
2 cups sugar,
1/2 pint heavy cream, whipped.

Directions:
bake pie shells, and cool.  Wash berries and drain.  Cover the bottom of pie shells with 1/2 of the berries.  Sugar them lightly.  Crush the rest of the berries, and mix with cornstarch, lemon juice, butter, and 2 cups sugar.  Cook until thick.  Cool.  Pour into pie shells, and cover with whipped cream.  Refrigerate 2 hours, and serve.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
My mother would bake this every Easter Sunday, and it was always a hit.
