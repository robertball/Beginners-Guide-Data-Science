Title:
Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup canned pumpkin
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup applesauce
1 egg
1 cup unbleached bread flour
1 cup white whole wheat flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon milk
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Combine pumpkin, sugar, vegetable oil, applesauce, and egg.  In a separate bowl, stir together flours, baking powder, ground cinnamon, and salt. Dissolve the baking soda with the milk and stir in. Add flour mixture to pumpkin mixture and mix well.
Add vanilla, chocolate chips and nuts.
Drop by spoonful on greased cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for approximately 10 minutes or until lightly brown and firm.
