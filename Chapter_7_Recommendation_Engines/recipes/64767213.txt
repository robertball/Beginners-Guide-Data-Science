Title:
Canape of Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3.5oz. of Ham,  thinly sliced.
1 small onion
2 Tomatoes
1 Boiled Egg.
1 Tbsp. of Vegetable Oil
4 slices of your Choice Bread, or Favorite.
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Slice each piece of Bread in half;  diagonally.
Slice the onion into rings and fry in the oil until Brown.
Butter the Bread and dress;  in order;  with;  Ham, fried Onion, -
chopped Egg, Sliced Tomato,  Salt & Pepper.

Submitter: 
Alois
Description: 
a Tasty ( open face Sandwich);  Canape of Ham , onion, tomato and Egg.
