Title:
Instant pot pan juice gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5736217.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp corn starch
2 Tbsp water
1 cup pan juice (from pressure cooked chicken, pork, beef, turkey, etc.,)
1/2 tsp browning sauce (use only for beef gravy)
1/2 tsp garlic salt

Directions:
Set instant pot on low sauté mode.
Add water and corn starch to instant pot, stir to create a slurry.
Add the pan juices, stirring constantly until thickened.
Add the garlic salt, stir to incorporate.
Add the browning sauce if making beef gravy - omit this step if you’re making chicken, pork, or turkey gravy.

Submitter: 
thedailygourmet
Description: 
This gravy uses the natural liquid from chicken, pork, beef, or turkey after it has cooked in the instant pot.
