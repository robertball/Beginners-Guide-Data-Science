Title:
Gayle's Chicken Salad a la Croissant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4470489.jpg
Ingredients:
Dressing:
2 Tbsp. white sugar
¼ tsp. salt
1 tsp. (or less) dry mustard
2 tsp. flour
1 egg (well beaten prior to mixing)
¼ cup milk
¼ cup white vinegar
1 tsp. butter
¼ cup miracle whip
Salad ingredients:
Chicken/turkey (3 ¾ cups canned chicken or ~4 chicken breasts)
1 can pineapple tidbits
Toasted slivered almonds
Shredded cabbage
Green onions (diced)
Green/purple grapes

Directions:
Mix dressing ingredients over low heat, then slowly add milk and vinegar. Cook sauce until thickened (over medium heat) and stir constantly. Stir in butter and let cool.
Mix in Miracle Whip and mix with salad ingredients.
