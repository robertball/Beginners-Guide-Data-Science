Title:
green bean & feta salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/333622.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb. fresh green beans
1/2 cup Feta Cheese
1/4 cup  chopped red onion
1/3 cup KRAFT FREE Italian Dressing

Directions:
Cook beans in boiling water 7 min. or until crisp-tender. Drain and rinse with cold water. Toss beans with the cheese, onion, and dressing, lightly stir.
Serve immediately. Or, cover and refrigerate several hours or until chilled
