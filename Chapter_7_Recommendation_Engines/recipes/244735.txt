Title:
Garlic Chicken Salad Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2497421.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Roasted Garlic Dressing
2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
2 medium carrots, peeled and diced
1/4 cup frozen peas, thawed
1 celery rib, diced
4 whole grain sandwich rolls, lightly toasted
Lettuce (optional)
Tomato (optional)

Directions:
In a bowl, add the chicken, carrots, celery, peas, and Hidden Valley(R)Original Ranch(R)Roasted Garlic Dressing and toss until well coated. Chill covered for 30 minutes.
Divide the chicken salad between the sandwich rolls and top with lettuce and tomatoes if desired.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley®
Description: 
Shredded chicken tossed with Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Roasted Garlic Dressing and chopped veggies makes a quick and delicious filling for sandwiches.
