Title:
Herb Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1084902.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon white sugar
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
2/3 cup vegetable oil
1 tablespoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Measure the salt, sugar, pepper, mustard, paprika, vinegar, oil, basil and Worcestershire sauce into a medium jar.  Shake well and chill.

Submitter: 
Patty Hern
Description: 
This dressing also tastes wonderful on cold pasta.  It actually is best to use dried basil in this recipe, because fresh basil will turn a funny color.
