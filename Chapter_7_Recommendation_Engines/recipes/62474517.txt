Title:
Crab Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2lb crab
3 T basil
1/3c roasted red pepper
2 green onion
3/4 c parmesan
1/2 c mayo
3 T wasabi Aioli
2 t lemon juice
1/4 t red pepper flakes
12 slices baguette-toasted
1/3 c pine nuts
lemon wedges
WASABI AIOLI
2 garilc
2 T wasabi
1 T rice vinegar
pinch of salt
2 egg yolk
1/4 c EVOO
3/4 c canola oil

Directions:
Make Aioli, creating an immulsion
Mix all but bread together and bake 450* 5 min.
Garnish with pine nuts and lemon.
