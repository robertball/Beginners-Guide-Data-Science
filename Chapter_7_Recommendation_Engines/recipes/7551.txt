Title:
Buttery Milk Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/792835.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a saucepan combine the butter or margarine, evaporated milk, white sugar, and the vanilla extract. Bring to a boil over medium high heat and continue to cook for 5 minutes. Pour over your choice of cake while syrup is still warm.

Submitter: 
Suzanne Stull
Description: 
Creamy tasting syrup that's great when poured over cakes with fruit.
