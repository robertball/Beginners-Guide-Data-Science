Title:
Chinese Napa Cabbage Salad with Grilled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz lean pork
1 cup uncooked rice
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup sesame seeds, toasted
1/2 cup blanched slivered almonds
1 large head napa cabbage, shredded
6 green onions, chopped
1/8 cup olive oil
1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1/8 cup dark sesame oil
4 tablespoon Ideal sugar alternative

Directions:
Cook rice. Heat butter in a pan, add almonds and sesame seeds.  Stir often to prevent burning. grill pork and cut into small portions.  Prepare the dressing by whisking together the vegetable oil, rice vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil and Ideal and bring to a boil in a pan over low heat. Shred Nappa Cabbage and green onions. Toss cabbage, pork, rice and dressing in a large bowl and serve hot! This former salad is now a meal!

Submitter: 
Irene
Description: 
A sweet and sour dressing is the perfect thing for this very Asian cabbage salad. It mates perfectly with the crunchy ramen noodles, almonds and sesame seeds. Makes six servings.Modified from: Chinese Napa Cabbage Salad
