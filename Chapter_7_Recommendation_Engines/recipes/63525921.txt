Title:
Lean and Green Crockpot Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 bone-in chicken breast halves, skin removed
1 teas dried oregano
1/2 teas seasoned salt
1/4 teas pepper
2 Tbsp olive oil
1/4 cup water
3 Tbsp lemon juice
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 teas chicken bouillon granules
2 teas minced fresh parsley

Directions:
Rinse the chicken and pat it dry with paper towels. In a bowl, mix the salt, pepper and oregano in to a rub. Apply the rub to the chicken. Put the oil In a skillet and brown the chicken over medium heat.
Now itâ€™s time to move it all to the crockpot. Put the water, lemon juice, garlic, and bouillon into the skillet and turn up the heat. Bring it a boil. Pour the mixture into the crockpot over the chicken. Cover and cook for 3-4 hours on low. Baste chicken. Add parsley. Cover and cook 30 minutes longer.
