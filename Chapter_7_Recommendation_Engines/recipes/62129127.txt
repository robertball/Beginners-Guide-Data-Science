Title:
Best Brownie Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (1 lb, 2.3 oz) of your favorite brownie mix
1 egg
1/3 cup vegetable oil
2 Tbls. water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Mix all ingredients until combined.
Use cookie scoop to place dough 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet
Bake 8-10 minutes (cookies will be soft)
Let cool completely on baking sheet
Remove from baking sheet and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
Incredibly easy and delicious brownie cookies made in minutes. These are great for family gatherings.
