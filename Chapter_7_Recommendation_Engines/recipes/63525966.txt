Title:
Lean and Green Pork Loin with Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
pork tenderloin (each serving will be 5 - 7 oz)
2 teaspoon rosemary
2 teaspoon thyme
1 clove garlic, minced
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tablespoon olive oil
8-10 medium asparagus spears per person

Directions:
Arrange asparagus on roasting pan. Mix herbs and spices together. Brush pork with oil and rub in spices. Let sit 45 minutes in refrigerator. Top asparagus with pork and roast at 325 for 30-45 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 150Â°F.
