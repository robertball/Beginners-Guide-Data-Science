Title:
Crocked Tater Tot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz Frozen Tater Tots
1 lb Cooked Chopped Ham
1 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
2 Cups Milk
1 10oz Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1 cup Sour Cream
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in Slower cooker - mix well.  Cook High 4 hours or low 5-7 hours.  Stir occasionally.  Add additional milk or water for desired consistency
