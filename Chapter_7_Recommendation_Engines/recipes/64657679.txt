Title:
Creamy Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 corn tortillas
2 cups chicken stock
One 8oz block cream cheese, chunked
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup butter
2 cups diced chicken
3 cups shredded blend cheese
1 cup milk, plus more if needed
1/4 cup onion
4 cloves garlic
hatch chiles, roasted and seeded, to taste
1/2 tsp Penzey's Fajita Seasoning

Directions:
Add butter to sauce pan  over medium heat and saute onions until translucent
Add flour and cook till golden brown
Add chicken stock and allow to thicken, stirring constantly.  Add 1 cup of milk.
Add cream cheese and garlic and allow cream cheese to melt entirely, add chiles to taste.
Remove from burner and add 1 cup shredded cheese, stirring to melt.  If too thick, add additional milk.
Combine chicken and 3/4 cup shredded cheese in bowl and toss to combine
Microwave the tortillas until soft and pliable, 30-60 seconds.
Ladel 1/3 of the sauce into the bottom of a 9x13 pan.  Divide chicken mixture among the 12 tortillas and roll, placing seam side down.  Cover tortilla rolls with remaining cream cheese mixture and top with remaining shredded cheese.  Bake 30 minutes at 350.  Allow to rest 10 minutes before serving.
