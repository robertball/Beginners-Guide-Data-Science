Title:
Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Kelloggs Corn Flakes
12 oz chicken breast, boneless, skinless
1 TBS skim milk
4 TBS fat free margarine
1/4 TBS paprika
1/2 tsp salt
9 tsp parsley
3 tsp chives
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Mix 2 TBS of margarine, milk, chives, salt and garlic powder. Mix crushed cereal, parsley and paprika in a seperate bowl. Dip chicken into milk mixture, then coat lightly with cereal mixture.
Place in a 13x9x2 rectangular pan sprayed with non-stick cooking spray.
Drizzle with 2 TBS of margarine and bake uncovered for 25 minutes.
