Title:
The Monahan Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups ice
2 fluid ounces rye whiskey
1 fluid ounce sweet vermouth
1/8 tablespoon Amer Picon
1 Maraschino cherry, for garnish

Directions:
Put ice in a cocktail shaker. Pour in whiskey, sweet vermouth, and Amer Picon. Shake or stir, and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a maraschino cherry (or a twist of lemon).

Submitter: 
Lorem Ipsum
Description: 
It's like a Manhattan, only with Amer Picon instead of bitters.
