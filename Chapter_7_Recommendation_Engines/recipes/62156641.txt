Title:
Russian Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pork chops
2 table spoons of Vegeta(seasoning)
can get at any Russian Foods store
1 teaspoon of ground pepper
2 beated eggs
2 cups of four
1 cup of vegetable oil

Directions:
beat pork chops with kitchen hammer to make thin, cut in to half or how big you want them to be.
Season the pork chops evenly.
beat the eggs and put in plate to dip in.
put flour in a plate to dip the pork chops in.
put oil in stove at 5, let it heat up.
when oil is heated dip the pork chops in the eggs coating really good then in to flour, coat it good..then place onto stove and cook untill golden brown.
if making for alot of people just double the ingredients.
enjoy!!
