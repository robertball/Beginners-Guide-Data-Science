Title:
Absolute Best Lasagna Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs lean ground beef
0.5 lbs Italian Sausage
1 large onion, chopped
2-3 garlic cloves minced
1.5 - 2 teaspoons salt to taste
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon dried parsley flakes
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon dried basil
2 (14.5 ounce) cans whole tomatoes, undrained and chopped
2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
24 ounces ricotta cheese
2 eggs, beaten
0.5 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons parsley
0.5 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 lb mozzarella cheese, divided
12-15 lasagna noodles

Directions:
Brown ground beef, Italian sausage, onion and garlic
Add salt, pepper, parsley, oregano, basil, chopped tomatoes with juice, and tomato paste; stirring well until mixed
Cover and simmer 1 hour (or longer, but watch for getting too dry)
Cook lasagna noodles
Spray a 13.9 baking pan with cooking spray
Combine ricotta cheese, eggs, pepper, 2 tablespoons parsley, Parmesan cheese, and 1/2 lb of mozzarella cheese.
In a lasagna pan, layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture.  Repeat
Top off with layer of noodles.  Sprinkle remaining mozzarella cheese evenly and cover noodles completely
Bake at 375 F for 40-60 minutes, or until cheese mixture is thoroughly melted.  Cover with foil for 40 minutes and then uncover for 15 to 20 minutes.
Let sit for 15 mins before cutting and serving.
