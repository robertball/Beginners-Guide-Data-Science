Title:
12 Hour Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole head of lettuce, chopped
1 whole cauliflower, chopped
1 red onion, chopped
1 small green pepper, chopped
1 pint or less Hellman's Mayonaise and 2 Tablespoons sugar combined.
Bacon, cooked till crisp and crumbled.

Directions:
First layer the lettuce in bowl, then layer the cauliflower on top of lettuce.
Layer the red onion on top of cauliflower, followed by the green pepper.
Spread the mayo over the top as the next layer.
Sprinkle 1/2 - 3/4 lb.  bacon crumbled over the top of the mayo.
Grate 1/2 - 3/4 lb. mild colby cheese over the top of the bacon layer.
Cover and refrigerate for 12 hours.  The directions then say to toss just before serving but I don't remember Mom tossing it.

Submitter: 
Sue
Description: 
Mom made this for many family gatherings.  Served in a glass clear bowl, its beautiful on the table.
