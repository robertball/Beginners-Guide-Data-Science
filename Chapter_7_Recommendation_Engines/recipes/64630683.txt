Title:
Japanese-Style Pork Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
150 grams pork
30 grams shredded konnyaku (devil's tongue fruit jelly)
2 Tablespoon sake
mizuna
1/4 piece yellow onion
1 piece tomato
2 pieces piiman (Japanese green bell peppers)
3 Tablespoon vinegar
1 Tablespoon honey
1/2 Tablespoon fish sauce
1 piece dried chili pepper
1 1/2 Tablespoon olive oil
1 clove garlic, grated
peanuts, crushed

Directions:
In a microwaveable bowl combine pork, shredded konnyaku and sake. Cover then cook in the microwave for 4 minutes.
Dice the onion, tomato, and piiman. Cut the mizuna into 5-cm widths.
Whisk vinegar, honey, fish sauce, dried chili pepper, olive oil and garlic in a bowl.
In a large bowl toss  the pork, vegetables and sauce. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving. You can put crushed peanuts on top.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
This dish is a little pork with many vegetables. The pork is steamed in a microwave. Best served chilled (if everyone at home can wait).
