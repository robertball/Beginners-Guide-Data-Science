Title:
Grill Basket Beef Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980436.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked rice
2 1/4 cups water
1/2 cup Market Pantry™ soy sauce
2 tablespoons oyster sauce
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 pound Sutton & Dodge™ round steak, cut into 1-inch cubes
1 (8 ounce) package mushrooms, cut in half
1 onion, cut into 1/2-inch wedges
1 (7 ounce) can baby corn, drained

Directions:
Heat grill.
Cook rice according to package directions.
In small bowl, combine soy sauce, oyster sauce and garlic. Set aside.
When grill is heated, place oiled grill basket on grill over medium  heat, add steak, mushrooms, onions and corn. Cook and stir  frequently for 10 to 15 minutes, or until meat is of desired  doneness. During last 5 minutes of cooking, add 1 tablespoon  at a time of sauce while stirring.
Serve stir-fry over cooked rice.

Description: 
Why not enjoy stir-fry and the great outdoors? With this recipe you can do both - and give your stir-fry a great grilled flavor.
