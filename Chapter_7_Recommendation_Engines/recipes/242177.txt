Title:
Watermelon Fizz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2316582.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut water
1/2 cup watermelon puree
1 teaspoon stevia
1/2 cup ice cubes, or as desired

Directions:
Blend coconut water, watermelon, and stevia together in a blender until smooth.
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; add watermelon mixture. Cover shaker and shake until chilled; pour into a poco grande glass.

Submitter: 
Christian Sidiangco
Description: 
Mouth-watering nutritious mocktail with the power of coconut water and watermelon. Garnish with an orange twist, watermelon wedge, and grapes with two blending straws.
