Title:
Prepared Yellow Mustard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp. dry ground mustard (mustard flour)
1/4 c. water
3 Tbsp. white distilled vinegar
1/2 tsp. Wondra (name brand) Flour
1/4 tsp. plus 1/8 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. turmeric
pinch garlic powder
pinch paprika

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan over medium heat.
Whisk until smooth.
When mixture comes to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes, stirring often.
Remove pan from heat.
Leave uncovered for 1 minute, then cover pan with a lid until cool.
Chill and store in a covered container.
Makes 1/4 cup

Submitter: 
JillH
Description: 
Make your own prepared yellow mustard.  It is so simple, and it is much healthier to eat!
