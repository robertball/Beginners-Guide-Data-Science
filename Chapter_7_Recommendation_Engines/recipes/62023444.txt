Title:
Chicken Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked cubed white meat chicken
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 cup chicken broth
1 can sliced mushrooms (or pieces)
1 1/2 tsp garlic salt
1 1/2 c cheddar cheese
8 oz spaghetti

Directions:
In large skillet combine soups, broth, mushrooms, garlic salt, & 1/2 c cheese. Simmer til blended well and stir in chicken.  Continue to simmer on low while cooking spaghetti.  In coated 2 qt casserole, combine soup mixture and spaghetti, making sure to evenly distribute the pasta.  Top with remaining cheese and bake at 350 for 35 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
lonwolf32
Description: 
This is a great make-ahead recipe and it also freezes very well.  It's always been a big hit with my family and a favorite at pot luck dinners.  To add a little texture you can top with canned onion rings during the last 10 minutes of baking!
