Title:
Wasabi - Jalapeno Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup non-fat sour cream
1/2 cup non-fat mayonnaise
1 T. (or to taste) powdered wasabi
1 jalapeno (or to taste), finely minced
1 t. cider vinegar
salt, to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Refrigerate for about 15 minutes to let flavors develop.
If adding additional wasabi, add a little bit at a time and refrigerate 5 minutes before tasting.  Wasabi gets hotter upon sitting.

Submitter: 
BAJATHECAT
Description: 
My husband and I wanted to come up with a zippy sauce to use with a variety of foods - raw vegies, deep-fried calamari, fish cakes, etc., etc.  This is a staple!
