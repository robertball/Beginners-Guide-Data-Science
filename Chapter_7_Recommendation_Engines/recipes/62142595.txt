Title:
Easiest Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 container prepared frosting (chocolate or vanilla)
1 18oz jar creamy or chunky peanut butter

Directions:
In medium bowl, stir ingredients together until well blended. Spread into buttered 8x8 square pan.  Refrigerate 1 hour or freeze 30 minutes.  Cut into squares and serve.

Submitter: 
anmlnrs
Description: 
No cooking, no boiling, just two ingredients* and it never fails.  (*You can add vanilla, marshmallows, etc if you wish.)
