Title:
Zippy Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1060860.jpg
Ingredients:
12 hard-boiled eggs, halved lengthwise with yolks and whites separated
1/2 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon hot yellow pepper sauce

Directions:
Combine egg yolks, mayonnaise, mustard, and hot sauce in a food processor; puree until smooth.
Spoon egg yolk mixture into a pastry bag with a decorative tip; pipe filling into the cavity of the egg white halves.

Submitter: 
simone stevin
Description: 
I concocted this recipe for my longtime friend and colleague Colleen B. for her 60th birthday celebration. Decorate with a sprigs of parsley or dill.
