Title:
Sweet Apple Chicken Sausage with Homemade Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz package al fresco Sweet Apple Chicken Sausage
1 tsp Canola oil
3 oz Sour dough bread, pulsed in a food processor until crumbly
1 tsp Butter, melted
12 oz Elbow macaroni
2 cups Extra Sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1/4 cup Egg substitute
1 12 oz can Evaporate low fat milk

Directions:
Saut&eacute; chicken sausage in oil over medium heat until browned and internal temp reaches 165 degrees.
Remove, slice into pieces and set aside.
Cook pasta according to package directions
Pulse bread in a food processor until crumbly
Place crumbs in a bowl, add melted butter and mix well
Place butter crumb mixture on a cookie sheet and bake at 375 degrees for 12 minutes or until golden
Return macaroni to the pan and place over medium-low heat stirring in cheese, egg substitute and milk.
Cook for 4 minutes or until cheese melts, stirring constantly.
Stir in sausage pieces last and spoon into bowls.
Serve with toasted breadcrumbs on top.
