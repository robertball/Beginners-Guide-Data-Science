Title:
Perfect Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/595625.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. kielbasa, sliced to 1/2" thick rounds
2 T. olive oil
2 cups onions, chopped
1 cup celery, chopped
1 cup red bell pepper, chopped
1 lb. medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
2 t. garlic, minced
2-14 oz. cans diced tomatoes
2 cups cooked white rice
2 t. Tabasco
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown kielbasa in oil over medium heat, about 3 minutes.
Stir in onions, celery, and bell pepper.  Saute 5 minutes.
Add shrimp and garlic; cook for 3 minutes more, stirring constantly.
Stir in tomatoes and rice.  Cook 3-4 minutes to heat through.
Season with tabasco, salt, and pepper.

Description: 
This is the best jambalaya recipe I have made.  It's easy and always a hit when I serve for company.  I hope you enjoy it as much as we do!
