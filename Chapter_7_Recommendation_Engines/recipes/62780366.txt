Title:
Herbs Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon garlic powder (or minced fresh)
1/2 teaspoon fresh thyme
1/2 teaspoon fresh tarragon
1/2 teaspoon of fresh rosemary
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 center cut bone-in pork chops
2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
2 tablespoons butter
1 cup water
2 cubes  beef bouillon (or 1 cup beef broth to substitute the water and buillon)
1 Tablespoon of cornstarch
2 Tablespoons of water

Directions:
Combine the salt,garlic powder, herbs and black pepper in a small bowl and rub on both sides of the chops. Dissolve the cornstarch in the water and set aside. Melt the butter or margarine in a large skillet over medium high heat and saute the chops for 3 minutes per side, or until well browned. Add the mushrooms and brown for another 2 minutes.
Meanwhile,prepare one cup of beef broth/buillon (warmed up). Add this to the chops/mushrooms, reduce heat to low, cover and simmer chops for 40 minutes. Slowly add the cornstarch mixture stirring to thicken the sauce to your like.

Submitter:

Description: 
Pork chops rubbed with a fresh herbs, salt and pepper are pan fried in butter until golden brown then simmered in a simple beef bouillon.Modified from: Sage Pork Chops
