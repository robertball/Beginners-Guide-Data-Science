Title:
Gluten Free Teff Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1254211.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup water
3 tablespoons tapioca flour
1/4 cup teff flour
1/4 cup pepitas (roasted pumpkin seeds)
1 tablespoon flaxseed
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
1 teaspoon black sesame seeds
1 tablespoon sesame seeds
1 tablespoon teff grain
1/4 teaspoon salt
Salt to sprinkle after baking

Directions:
Combined water, teff flour and tapioca and stir till smooth
Combine remaining ingredients and create soupy mixture
Pour into oiled 9 x 12 pan
Tap pan to evenly distribute ingredients
Preheat oven to 375 (190 Celsius)and bake for 40 minutes or until crisp
Sprinkle with salt immediately after removing from oven

Submitter: 
Timbo
Description: 
Super easy recipe that everyone can enjoy including vegetarians, vegans, gluten-free, kids and everyone else!  The tapioca helps bind everything together and makes for a wholesome nutritious snack that tastes great!
