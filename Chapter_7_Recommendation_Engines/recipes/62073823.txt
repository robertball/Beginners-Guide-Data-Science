Title:
Chocolate Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box digestive bu=iscuits
1 cup cocoa
3 tablespoons fine sugar
3 tablespoons liquer
rosewater
chopped almonds or coconut sprinkles

Directions:
Mash the biscuits and add the cocoa, the liquer, the sugar and the rosewater. Mix with your hands well until a dough is formed.  Make small balls and roll in the chopped almond mixture or the coconut sprinkles.  Put in the fridge.

Submitter: 
GATOULA
Description: 
No need to bake chocolate truffles, ideal for childrens' birthday parties.
