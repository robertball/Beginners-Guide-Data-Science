Title:
Best Beef Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 # beef roast
2 carrots minced
2 stalks of celery minced
2 beef boullion cubes
2 chicken boullion cubes
1 cup water

Directions:
Place beef roast in roasting pan, add minced carrots, celery, boullion cubes and water. Cover. Bake in preheated oven @ 500 for 20 min. Reduce heat to 325 cook additional 60-75 min for med rare.

Submitter: 
Livie's Mommy
Description: 
Beef Roast with a touch of celery and carrot giving the broth a boost in flavor that will render the perfect base for a wonderfully flavored gravy if you so choose. The roast is tender and Oh so flavorul.
