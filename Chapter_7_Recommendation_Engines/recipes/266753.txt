Title:
Bake Sale Golden Peanut Butter Bars  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798700.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
1 egg, beaten
1/2 cup cold butter
1 cup finely chopped peanuts
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine flour, brown sugar, and egg in a large bowl. Cut in butter until mixture appears crumbly. Stir in chopped peanuts.
Set 2 cups of the peanut crumble aside. Press remaining mixture into a 9x13-inch baking pan.
Bake crust in the preheated oven until lightly browned, about 15 minutes.
Beat condensed milk, peanut butter, and vanilla extract together in a large bowl. Spread over the prepared crust. Top with reserved peanut crumble.
Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, about 25 minutes. Cool before cutting into bars.

Description: 
If you like peanut butter and need a good recipe to bring to a bake sale, this is what you need. Store covered at room temperature.
