Title:
Honey Mustard Glazed Salmon on Spicy Kale Lentils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/962889.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Lentils
2-1/2 Cup Low Sodium Chicken Broth
1 Tsp Red Pepper Chili Flakes
1 bundle of Kale, spine/stem removed and chopped (bite size)
2 Salmon Fillets
1 Tbsp Honey
2 Tbsp Dijon Mustard
Himalayan Sea Salt
Ground Milled Pepper
1/2 tsp of Olive Oil or Misto with Olive Oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
In a large saucepan, bring chicken broth to a boil
Add lentils and red pepper chili flakes. Stir and reduce the heat to low.
Pack the kale on top of the lentils cover and cook for 25 mins, stirring occasionally.
Remove lid and simmer for 10 minutes more or until lentils and kale are tender
Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
Meanwhile, place salmon fillets in a baking dish. Lightly glaze salmon with olive oil or use Misto Spray.
Season salmon with salt and pepper, to taste.
Bake for 12 minutes.
In a small bowl, stir together honey and mustard. After 12 minutes, remove fillets from oven and coat thoroughly with honey-mustard glaze. Return to oven and bake 10 minutes more or until cooked through.
Divide lentil mixture among plates.
Top each with a salmon fillet and serve immediately.
