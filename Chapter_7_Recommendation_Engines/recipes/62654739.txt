Title:
Easy Rolled Flakey Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/588811.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups flour
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/2 cups shortening
1 egg, beaten
1 Tablespoon vinegar
1/2 cup ice cold water

Directions:
Sift the flour, salt and baking powder together.
Cut in the shortening until mixture resembles fine crumb like texture.
Stir the egg and vinegar into the flour mixture until incorporated.
Add the water 1 Tablespoon at a time, until a ball forms, mixing after each addition.

Submitter: 
thedailygourmet
Description: 
This pie crust really lives up to the name!  It's incredibly flakey, and the dough is EASY to work with!  I've never found another pie crust similar to this one, it's so versatile and I enjoy using it with my chicken pot pie.
