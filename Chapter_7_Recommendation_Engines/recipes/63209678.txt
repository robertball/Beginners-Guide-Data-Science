Title:
Pineapple dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1/2 lb. vanilla wafers, crushed (2 cups crumbs)
5 Tbsp. melted butter
Mix well.  Butter and flour a 12 x 8 inch pan.  Place 3/4 of crumb mixture in pan, pressing firmly.
Filling:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup icing sugar
2 eggs, well beaten
1/4 cup chopped nuts
1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 pint cream, whipped

Directions:
Cream butter, sugar and eggs.  Spread over crust.  Ad layer of chopped nuts, then crushed pineapple.  Spread on whipped cream.  Sprinkle on reserved crumbs.  Refrigerate 24 hours to ripen.  Serves 8 - 10.

Submitter: 
Kath
Description: 
Soooo. Yummy!  Make sure you let it chill for 24 hours before serving.  This allows the flavours to blend.
