Title:
better than SEX cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package devils food cake mix
1/2 can sweet condensed milk
6 ounces carmel ice cream topping
3 bars covered toffe, chopped
1 container frozen whipped topping,thawed

Directions:
1.) bake cake according to package directions for a 9x13-cool for 5 min. make slits across top of cake, making sure not to go trough bottom.2.) In a saucepan over low heat, combine milk +caramel topping
stiring until smooth. pour warm mixture over the top of the cake,letting it sink into slots.sprinkle crushed choc toffe bars liberally across the entire cake,while still warm 3.) let the cake cool completely.Then top with whipped topping. Decorate top of cake with more choc toffe chunks + swirls of caramel topping. refrigerate + serve from pan.
