Title:
No Bake Cookies VII Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1292696.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1 cup marshmallow fluff
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together the peanut butter and marshmallow fluff. Stir in chocolate chips. Roll into 1 inch balls and serve.

Submitter: 
WIFEY811
Description: 
This recipe is made with Chocolate chips, peanut butter and marshmallow fluff! No cooking or baking necessary. What could be easier?
