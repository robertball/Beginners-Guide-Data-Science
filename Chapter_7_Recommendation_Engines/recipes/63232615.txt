Title:
Korean Style Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
2 tbsp sugar
4 tbsp water
3 tbsp rice vinegar
3 tbsp sesame oil
3 tsp red chili powder

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a shaker cup and shake or whisk together in a bowl.  Delicious over spinach salad
