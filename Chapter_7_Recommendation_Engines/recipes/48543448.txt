Title:
Baked Rigatoni with spinach, ricotta and fontina Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound rigatoni
2C ricotta (about 1#)
3T olive po;
1/2t grated nutmeg
10 oz pkg. frozen spinach, thawed
5T grated parmesan cheese
3/4t salt
1/4t pepper
6oz fontina cheese, grated(about 1 1/2C)

Directions:
Heat oven to 450 degrees. Oil 9 x 13 baking pan
In lg. pot of boiling, salted water, cook rigatoni until almost done, about 12 min. Drain. Put pasta in baking pan and toss w/1 T. oil.
Meanwhile, squeeze as much water as possible from spinach. Put the spinach in food processor and puree w/ricotta, 3T of Parmesan,nutmeg, salt and pepper. Stir spinach mixture w/pasta. Top w/remaining fontina and Parmesan. Drizzle w/remaining 2T oil over top. Bake 15-20 min., until golden brown
