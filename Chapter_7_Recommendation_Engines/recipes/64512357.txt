Title:
Carrot Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1 cup wheat bran (I've subbed all bran cereal)
1 2/ cup brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 1/4 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 eggs
3/4 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup canola oil
2 tbsp. molasses
1 1/2 cups grated carrots (about 2 large)
1 cup raisins (yummy if u like them, but optional)

Directions:
Mix first 7 ingredients.
Combine eggs, buttermilk, oil and molasses.
Stir wet ingredients into dry, just until moistened.
Fold in carrots and raisins.
Fill greased muffin tins 3/4 full.
Bake @ 350 x 25 - 30 minutes, or until tests done with toothpick.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt

Moist and delicious - with buttermilk & molasses.
