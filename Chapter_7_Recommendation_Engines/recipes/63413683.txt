Title:
Sweet & Peppery Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
3 tbsp. water
1 tsp. garlic salt
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. ground pepper
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
3 cups pecan halves
Cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
In a large skillet, combine all ingredients except pecans.
Bring to a boil over medium heat.
Boil gently, uncovered, for 2 minutes.
Add pecan halves and stir until well coated.
Line a 15x10 cookie sheet with foil then spray with cooking spray.
Spread nuts onto foil.
Bake for 15-20 minutes or until browned, stirring occasionally.
Cool then break apart large clusters if necessary.
Store in an airtight container.
