Title:
Black-Eyed Pea Gumbo with shrimp and Andouille sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium green bell pepper, chopped
5 stalks celery, chopped
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup brown rice or Farro
4 (15 ounce) cans black-eyed peas with liquid
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes and green chiles
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 lb cooked shrimp
Chicken Andouille sausage, sliced
Cajun Spices

Directions:
Saute Andouille sausage and add the vegetables to the sausage after a few minutes. Cook the onion, pepper, and celery until tender.
Pour in the chicken broth, and mix in rice/Farro, black-eyed peas with liquid, diced tomatoes and green chiles, diced tomatoes, and garlic.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer 45-60 minutes, or until rice/Farro is tender. Add water if soup is too thick.
Add cooked shrimp for the last 10 minutes to heat through.
