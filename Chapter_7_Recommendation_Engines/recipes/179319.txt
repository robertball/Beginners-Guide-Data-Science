Title:
Cuervo Especial Pomegranate Margarita Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986366.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Jose Cuervo Especial
3 ounces Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix
2 ounces pomegranate juice
1 piece lime wheel
1 tablespoon salt (optional)

Directions:
Combine Jose Cuervo Especial, Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix and pomegranate juice into cocktail shaker half full with ice.
Shake well and strain into a chilled martini glass (rim glass with salt optional).
Garnish with lime or lemon wheel.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
This margarita-inspired cocktail, made with Jose Cuervo Especial, Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix and pomegranate juice, has added sophistication when poured into a chilled martini glass rimmed with salt.
