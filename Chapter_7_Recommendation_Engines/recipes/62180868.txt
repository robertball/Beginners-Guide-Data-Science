Title:
SWEET, Sweet Potato Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 Large sweet potatoes
3 tbls spoons butter/margarine
1 tbl spoon cinnamon
1 tbl spoon white sugar
1 tbl spoon brown sugar
1/4 tsp spoon ground ginger
1 tbl spoon salt

Directions:
Peel potatos and cut in to 1/4" fries
Melt butter/Marg, Cinnamon, Brown Sugar, Sugar, Ginger and Salt together in a flat bottom bowl in the Microwave for a minute or so.
Drege fries in melted mixture and place on foiled and cooking sprayed sheet or pan.
Bake @ 375 for 30-35 minutes

Submitter: 
Holleta
Description: 
Super Yummy Sweet Potato fries baked in your oven with a delicous Cinnamon/Brown Sugar/Butter glaze. Stock up on Sweet Potatos because these go F A S T!
