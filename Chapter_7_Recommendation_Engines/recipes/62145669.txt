Title:
Chocolate Mint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick salted butter, softened
3/4 cup packed light or dark brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
2 large eggs
2 2/3 cups sifted all-purpose flour
10 oz package Andes baking chips

Directions:
1- Thoroughly combine the first seven ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
2- Mix in the Andes baking chips.
3- Add the flour a little bit at a time, stirring after each addition. Dough will be very thick.
4- Refrigerate the dough for 45 minutes.
5- While dough is chilling, preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
6- Form dough into balls, about the size of a US fifty cent coin.
7- Slightly flatten the dough balls.
8- Bake for 8 minutes, or until the tops are barely golden and cracked.
9- Cool for a few minutes.

Submitter: 
Jake Andricks
Description: 
Delicious, soft, and just like grandma's cookies!
