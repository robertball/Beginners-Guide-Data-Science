Title:
Fresh Fruit Salad with Arugula and Black Pepper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/853013.jpg
Ingredients:
4 C fresh arugula
1 banana sliced
1 C fresh blueberries
12 strawberries, sliced
1 mango, peeled and sliced into thin wedges
1 C cottage cheese
1/2 C toasted almonds or whole pecans
2 T well-aged balsamic vinegar
3 T good-qualiy olive oil
Freshly cracked black pepper

Directions:
Divide the arugula between two large salad plates.  Evenly divide the fruit on top of the greens.  Mound the cottage cheese in the center of each salad.  Sprinkle the toasted nuts over each plate and then drizzle the balsamic and then the olive oil over both servings.  Season liberally with freshly cracked pepper.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
The peppery arugula and the addition of freshly cracked black pepper add a delicious zest to a summer fruit salad.
