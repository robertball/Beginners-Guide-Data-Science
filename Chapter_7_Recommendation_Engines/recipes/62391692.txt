Title:
Kids Homemade Playdough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 cup salt
4 teaspoons cream of tartar
2 cups water
2 tablespoons salad oil
your choice of color food coloring

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Cook over medium heat until a soft ball forms. Knead until smooth.
