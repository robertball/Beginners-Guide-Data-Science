Title:
Dawn's Kickass  Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/842923.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 jar beef gravy
2 cups frozen mixed veggies
1 tsp chopped onion
3 cups mashed potatoes

Directions:
1. Brown the beef & drain.
2. Add onions, gravy, and veggies
3. Spoon into baking dish
4. Spread mashed potatoes evenly over beef mixture, drizzle with butter. Sprinkle with paprika
5. Bake 30 minutes @ 350

Submitter: 
Erica Maynard Kenney
Description: 
Easy peasy shepherd's pie the way my friend Dawn makes it.
