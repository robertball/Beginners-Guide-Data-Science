Title:
G-G's Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4471805.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. (2 cups) room temperature butter
1 cup icing sugar
1/3 cup cornstarch
3 cups flour

Directions:
Combine sugar, cornstarch, and flour in a bowl.
Using your hands, thoroughly mix butter into dry ingredients.
Using an electric mixer for 2 minutes, cream ingredients until they turn a shade lighter.
Form small dough balls with hands and put on ungreased cookie sheet.
Using flour and a decorative flipper, press dough balls down onto cookie sheet.
Bake at 300 F (275 in my oven) for 30-35 minutes on the middle rack.
