Title:
Southern Fried Catfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984229.jpg
Ingredients:
vegetable oil for frying
4 (4 ounce) fillets catfish
3/4 cup cornmeal
1/4 cup Gold Medal® all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup milk
2 eggs, beaten
1/8 teaspoon red pepper sauce
2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions:
Heat oven to 275 degreesF.  Heat oil (2 to 3 inches) in 4-quart Dutch oven to 375 degreesF.  Rinse fish; pat dry with paper towels.
Mix cornmeal, flour and salt in large bowl.  Stir in milk, eggs, pepper sauce and 2 tablespoons oil until well blended.  Coat fish with cornmeal batter, shaking off any excess.
Fry 2 fish filets at a time 5 to 8 minutes or until golden brown.  Drain on paper towels.  Keep warm on cookie sheet in oven while frying remaining fish.

Submitter: 
Betty Crocker®
Description: 
It's the perfect coating for your perfect catch!  Enjoy catfish fried the traditional southern way in a crispy, well-seasoned cornmeal coating.
