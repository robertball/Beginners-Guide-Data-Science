Title:
Clean Green Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 celery stalks, leaves removed
1 cucumber
5 parsley stalks
2 Granny Smith apples, halved
3 romaine leaves
3 kale leaves
1 tbsp lemon juice, freshly squeezed
Water, as needed for blending

Directions:
Add all ingredients to blender or juicer and blend. Pour into bottle and chill.

Submitter: 
Brittany Curtis
Description: 
Use as part of a juice cleanse or as a clean meal replacement.
