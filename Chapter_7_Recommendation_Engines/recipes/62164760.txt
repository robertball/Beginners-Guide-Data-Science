Title:
Brunswick Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (3-4 lb) Boston Butts, cooked and chunked
4 whole chickens, cooked and chunked
8 (16oz) cans tomatoes, drained and mashed
12 (16oz) cans cream-style corn
6 (16oz) cans white corn
6 (16oz) cans lima beans
4 pounds frozen okra-sliced
1 gallon chicken broth
3 (32oz) bottles catsup
5 pounds potatoes, finely diced
3 large onions, finely diced
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
4 teaspoons turmeric
4 teaspons ground cumin
1 (10oz) bottle Worcestershire sauce
1 cup tomatoe juice
Tabasco to taste
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook and prepare meats, reserving chicken broth.  Drain and mash tomatoes, reserving juice. In a very large cooking pan, combine all ingredients and mix well. Add reserved liquids to stew, as needed, during cooking. Cook over low heat for 12-18 hrs. Cooking time may vary according to taste. Serves 100.
