Title:
The Best White Chocolate Almond Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups of White Chocolate Melting Wafers
3 Tbs Butter
1 tsp Almond Extract
1-1/4 Cup of Whole Unsalted Almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
1.Place almonds on cookie sheet an roast for about 10 minutes until toasted. Stir occasionally.
2. Line another cookie sheet with parchment paper.
3. Microwave wafers and butter in a medium microwave-safe bowl on MEDIUM-HIGH (70 percent) power for 1.5 minutes; stir. Microwave an additional 20-30 seconds more. Add almond extract and stir until smooth. Stir in almonds.
4.Spread mixture evenly onto cookie sheet. Chill. Break into small snack size pieces.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kimberley
Description: 
Toasting the almonds and adding almond extract is what make this recipe stand alone. It is a great treat to give out during the holidays. Substitute broken up candy cane for the almonds for a change.
