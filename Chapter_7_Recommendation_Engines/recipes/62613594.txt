Title:
Marvel's Easiest Lentils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/567828.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of lentils (French or Puy)
1 box no fat, low sodium broth (Chicken or Vegetable)
1 carrot grated
1 medium onion finely diced
3 whole peppercorns
4 whole cloves of garlic
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 sprig of rosemary

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a medium to large sauce pan or bean pot
Fill empty broth box with water and add 3/4 to pot
Bring to boil then cook uncovered on medium high until tender (about one hour total), adding additional water if needed

Submitter: 
Marvel's Kitchen
Description: 
True comfort food without the fat.  Lentils are a breeze to make, good for you and a great side dish.  A great base for tarting up leftovers as well.
