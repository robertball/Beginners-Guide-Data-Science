Title:
Broiled Garlic Chicken LGLS 175 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken breast, halved and skinned
1/2 tsp garlic, peeled and minced
1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tsp Dijon Mustard
1/2 tsp salt
Black pepeer to taste
1 tbsp Bragg liquid soy, or wheat-free shoyu soy sauce
TGSS 10 cloves, 10 shallots, evoo

Directions:
1. Small bowl- combine all ingredients for basting suace
2. Brush over chicken
3. Broil 10-15 minutes each side, 2 inches from heat, brushing frequently with sauce
4. Add Tsp or so of TGSS Page 77 onto to serve.
