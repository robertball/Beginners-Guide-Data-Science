Title:
Caramel Puff Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) bagpuff corn
1  cup butter (no substitutes)
1  cup brown sugar
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1  teaspoon baking soda
Optional peanuts or other nut of choice but is delicious all by itself.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250*
Place Puff Corn (also nuts if using) in a large baking pan, allowing room to stir.
In 2 quart or larger sauce pan bring the butter, sugar & corn syrup a to boil, allow to boil for 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
Add the baking soda,  this will cause the caramel mixture to foam up.
Remove from heat immediately and stir briskly until baking soda is mixed.
Pour caramel mixture over puff corn in baking pan and mix until puff corn is coated all well as possible.
Bake at 250* for 45 minutes, stirring every 10-15 minutes.
Remove from oven, spread out onto wax paper to cool.
