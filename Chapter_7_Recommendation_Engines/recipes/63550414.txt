Title:
Chicken Fried Venison Cubed Steak Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 1/4 pounds venison cubed steak
1 egg
1/4 cup milk
3/4 cup panko bread crumbs
1/4 cup vegetable oil
24-ounce already prepared butter and herb mashed potatoes
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
Salt, pepper & garlic powder (optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a 7x11 inch baking dish with aluminum foil.
2. Season steak with salt, pepper & garlic powder to your liking.
3. Place flour in a shallow dish. Coat steak and set aside.
4. In a medium bowl, combine egg and milk; mix well. 5. Place panko bread crumbs in a second medium bowl.
6. Dip flour coated steak into egg mixture, coating completely, then into bread crumbs. Set aside. Discard any remaining coating mixture.
7. In a large deep skillet, heat oil over medium heat until hot, not smoking. Add steak and cook 6 to 7 minutes, until juices are clear and coating is golden brown. Turn occasionally.
8. Place steak in single layer in baking dish after draining grease on a rack or paper towels.
7. Spread mashed potatoes over steak.
8. Sprinkle cheddar cheese on top.
9. Bake 15-20 minutes or until potatoes are warmed through and cheese is melted.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a Southern favorite that I've tweaking to our family's liking. We always have venison on hand and this is a great way to utilize it. The dish is very filling and makes a great meal paired with veggies and biscuits on a cold night!
