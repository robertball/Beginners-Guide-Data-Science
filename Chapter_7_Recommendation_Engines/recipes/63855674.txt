Title:
Easy Spaghetti Red Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can diced tomatoes (I get the one with basil and oregano)
1 can tomato sauce
1 onion chopped
1 clove garlic diced
olive oil
Italian herbs (seasoning)
spaghetti noodles

Directions:
Put a couple tbsp of olive oil in frying pan
Leave oil in pan.Add chopped onion and garlic. Saute for a few minutes
Pour in 1 can diced tomatoes and sauce
Turn down heat to a simmer
Add Italian herb spices. Cook through until hot.
While sauce is heating, make your noodles according to package direction.
Serve with Parmesan cheese and garlic bread
Add a green salad
