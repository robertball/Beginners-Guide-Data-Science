Title:
Alexander's Creamy & Dreamy Super Meatball Sub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
21 Meatballs
2 1/3 Containers of shredded Mozzarella Cheese
2 2/3 Containers of Parmesan Cheese
7 Cheesesteak Rolls
1 1/2 Containers of tomato sauce
2 pinches of black pepper
2 pinches of salt

Directions:
1. Pour 1 container of tomato sauce into a large bowl & sprinkle with 2 pinches of black pepper.
2. Place 21 meatballs into the tomato sauce & sprinkle with 1 pinch of salt.
3. Heat the large bowl of meatballs & tomato sauce in a microwave for exactly 3 minutes.
4. Prepare the 7 cheesesteak rolls.
5. Take the meatballs out of the bowl.
6. Put exactly 3 meatballs into each of the cheesesteak rolls.
7. Pour an even amount of the leftover sauce on each of the seven super meatball sub sandwiches.
8. Sprinkle an even amount of parmesan cheese & a pinch of salt on top of the meatball subs that have sauce on them.
9. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella onto the sauce and the parmesan cheese.
10. Heat the meatball subs one at a time in the microwave for 2 1/2 minutes or until the cheese has melted.
11. Let cool for 2 1/2 to 3 1/3 minutes.
12. Enjoy!
