Title:
Loaded Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head of cauliflower, cut into bite size pieces
6-8 strips of bacon, cooked and crumbled
6 tsp chopped green onions
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
2 cups Colby Jack Cheese, grated (may use cheddar)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
In a large pot boil water and cook cauliflower for 8-10 minutes, drain and let cool.
In a large bowl combine sour cream, mayonnaise1/2 of the crumbled bacon, 3 tsp green onions, 1 cup of cheese and cauliflower, mix well.
Place in a 9 X 13 baking dish and cover with 1 cup of cheese and the rest of the crumbled bacon.
Bake for 15-20 minutes until cheese is melted.
Top with the remaining green onions and enjoy.
