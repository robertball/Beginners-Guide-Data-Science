Title:
Indian Lentil Cauliflower Soup (Oh She Glows) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2161954.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon of coconut oil
1 large yellow onion, diced
2 garlic cloves, peeled and minced
1 tablespoons  of ginger, peeled and finely grated
2 tablespoons of curry powder
1.5tsp of coriander
1tsp of ground cumin
6 cups of low sodium vegetable broth
1 cup of lentils (uncooked)
1 head of cauliflower cut into medium piece florets
1 large yam, peeled and diced into bite sized pieces
2 Large handfuls baby spinach
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. In large saucepan, heat oil over medium heat.  Add the onion and garlic and saute for 5 to 6 minutes, until translucent.
2. Stir in ginger, curry powder, coriander, and cumin and saute for 2 minutes more.
3. Add the broth and red lentils and stir to combine.  Bring the mixture to a low boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes more.
4. Stir in cauliflower and sweet potato.  Cover and reduce heat to medium-low.  Simmer for 20-25 mins, until the cauliflower and sweet potato are tender.  Season with salt and pepper.  Stir in spinach and cook until wilted.
5. Ladle soup into bowls and top with cilantro if desired
