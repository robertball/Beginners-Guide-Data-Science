Title:
Spaghetti Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. spaghetti, cooked and drained
2 medium tomatoes, sliced or diced
1 small or medium red onion, diced
1/2 bell pepper, diced
1 small can chopped ripe olives
1 cup (or 8 oz. bittle) Italian dressing
1 small pkg. dry Italian dressing mix
1/2 jar "Schilling Salad Supreme"
1 cup sliced mushrooms

Directions:
Combine spaghetti, tomatoes, onion, peppers and olives. Combine both Italian dressings and the Salad Supreme. Mix well. Pour mixture over spaghetti mixture amd toss well. Chill overnight and toss again before serving.
