Title:
Cheesy Bacon Slider Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 18 oz pkg Hawaiian sweet rolls
2 c shredded cheddar cheese, divided
1 lb ground beef
1 small  onion, chopped
1/2 14 oz can diced tomatoes with garlic and onion
1/2 tbsp Dijon mustard
1/2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
12 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
Glaze...
1/2 c butter
1/8 c brown sugar
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
1 tbsp sesame seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°.
Without separating rolls, cut the full dozen in half horizontally. Arrange bottom half in greased 9x13 pan.
Sprinkle with 1 c cheese, and bake 3-5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
In a large skillet, brown ground beef and onion and drain grease. Stir in tomatoes, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper. Cook 1-2 minutes until fully combined.
Spoon beef mixture over rolls, sprinkle with remaining cheese and bacon. Replace top half of buns.
Combine all glaze ingredients - except sesame seeds - in microwaveable bowl, cover and microwave on high until butter is melted.
Stir until mixed and pour over rolls. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
Bake, uncovered 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
