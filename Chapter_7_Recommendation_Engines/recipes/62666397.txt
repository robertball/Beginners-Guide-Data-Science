Title:
Beer-Braised Chicken Thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 bone-in, skin on chicken thighs
salt and pepper
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 link Chorizo sausage, casing removed and chooped or 4 slices bacon, chopped
1 onion, chopped
2 ribs celery, chopped
1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
2 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
1 tbsp all-purpose flour
1 bottle lager beer (12 oz)
1 can dice tomatoes (14 oz)
1 cup chicken stock
2 tbsp hot sauce
scallions, white and green parts, thinly sliced for garnish

Directions:
Pat chicken thighs dry and season with salt and pepper.
Heat 1 tbsp oil in a Dutch oven over med-high heat. Add the chicken and brown on both sides in two batches.
Remove the chicken to a plate, spoon out half the drippings, then add the sausage. Brown for 2 minutes, the add the onion, cellery, bell pepper, garlic and thyme cook to soften, about 10 minutes over medium heat.
Add the flour, stir for 1-2 minutes, then pour in the beer and let the foam subside. Stir in tomatoes, stock and hot sauce. Let the sauce thicken a bit then slide the chicken back into the pot and simmer to cook through. Garnish with scallions
Serve with warm crusty bread.
