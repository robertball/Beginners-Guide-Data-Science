Title:
Susan's Praline Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar, divided
1 tsp vanilla, divided
1 1/2 cups flour
2 8oz pkg cream cheese, softened
2 eggs
1/2 cup Heath Chips
3 tbs caramel ice cream topping

Directions:
Prehat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix butter, 1/2 cup sugar and 1/2 tsp vanilla until light and fluffy.  Gradually add flour until blended.  Press onto bottom of 13X9 pan.  Bake for 20-23 minutes or until lightly browned.
Mix cream cheese, remaining 1/2 cup sugar and 1/2 tsp vanilla until well blended.  Add eggs and mix well.  Stir in chips.  Pour over crust.  Dot top of cream cheese layer with topping.  Swirl through with knife for marble effect.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.  Cool and cut into bars.  Store in fridge.
