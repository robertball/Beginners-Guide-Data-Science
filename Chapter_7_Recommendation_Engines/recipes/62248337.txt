Title:
Hot chocolate w/ Kahlua Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz milk chocolate
1 c heavy cream
6 c milk
1 1/4 c kahlua
Marshmallows if needed.

Directions:
Combine cho and cream till melted, add milk. , but don't boil.
Combine 2 T kahlua with 1/2 c chocolate.
