Title:
Tempura Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable broth
1/3 cup mirin (preferred), or sweet sherry, or honey, or sake (for a stronger flavor)
1/3 cup soy sauce

Directions:
Blend soy sauce, mirin (or sweet sherry/sake/honey) and vegetable broth in a saucepan. Bring to a boil and then remove from heat.

Submitter: 
Michael St. Laurent
Description: 
A Japanese dipping sauce.
