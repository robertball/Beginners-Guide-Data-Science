Title:
Big Ray's Broiled Salmon with Yogurt-Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6623116.jpg
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Tbsp minced shallot
1 Tbsp minced dill
1 tsp Dijon mustard
2 Tbsp lemon juice
3/4 full fat yogurt
1 Tbsp olive oil
salt
pepper
4 6oz boneless, skinless salmon fillets

Directions:
In a small bowl, stir together garlic, shallot, dill, Dijon mustard, lemon juice,  yogurt, olive oil and salt & pepper to taste.
Set sauce aside or refrigerate up to 1 day.  Preheat broiler with rack set 8" below heating element.  Season salmon with salt and pepper to taste.  Broil until browned and center temperature registers 115 degrees to 125 degrees, about 5 minutes.  Serve with sauce.

Description: 
Salmon is a delicious fish that is rich in omega-3, making it good for the heart.  This is a simple recipe to help incorporate it into your family's diet.
