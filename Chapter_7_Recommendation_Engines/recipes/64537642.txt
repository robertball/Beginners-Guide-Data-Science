Title:
Instant Pot Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz bag of split peas
2 Tbsp bacon grease or EVOO
3 medium carrots, sliced 1/4" thick
2 celery stalks, sliced 1/4" thick
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
6 cups of chicken broth
3 cups of cubed ham
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp smoked paprika
1/2 tsp chili powder

Directions:
Place peas in a large bowl and cover with water.
Heat oil in Instant Pot on Saute function.
Add in vegetables and saute until celery softens and onion starts to turn translucent, about 5 to 10 minutes.
Add in broth and scrape off any browned bits from the bottom of the pot.
Add in remaining ingredients.
Drain peas and add into pot.
Cover, set to Soup function and adjust time to 10 minutes.
Naturally release for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
duboo
Description: 
The original calls for a 1/2 of sea salt, but I found it way too salty, so I omitted salt completely.     The original calls for 12 minutes of pressure and the peas were over done, so I adjusted to 10.   Delicious otherwise.
