Title:
Hawaiian Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886685.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Can condensed sweet milk
2 (8oz) Packs cream cheese (softened)
2 Tsp Vanilla extract
1/4 Cup milk
2 Eggs
Dash of salt
1 Cup Splenda
1 Cup Coconut shavings
1 Can Crushed Pineapple (Drained)
1/5 Cup Pecans Crushed
1 (12 Serving) Premade Grahm Cracker crust
1 (regular size) tub of whipped cream (Cool Whip)

Directions:
Mix everything until it's mixed well. Pour into pie crust, bake on 350 for 35-40 minutes or until it's set-up and browned around the edges slightly. Chill overnight. Cover in the drained pineapple. Cover that in whipped cream. Sprinkle whipped cream with some extra coconut shavings & crushed pecans.
