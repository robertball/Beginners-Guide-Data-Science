Title:
Basic Cake Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup butter
1 egg
2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 cup of milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray 9x9 pan with cooking spray.
Melt butter in small bowl.  Add sugar.
Beat egg into mixture and then add vanilla.
In a larger bowl mix flour, baking powder.
Add wet mixture and stir until mixed well.
Add milk and stir until smooth.
Pour into pan.
Bake 35 minutes.  Test with toothpick in center of cake.

Submitter: 
Tammy
Description: 
I use this recipe almost on a daily basis.  Its a basic cake mix that you can change up depending on the flavor you want.  I substitute flavors like cherry and pineapple in the place of the milk for a variety of things or add cinnamon for a nice breakfast cake.
