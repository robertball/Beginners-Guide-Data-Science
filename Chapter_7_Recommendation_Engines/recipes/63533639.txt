Title:
Jan's  Whiskey-N-Spice BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 cup minced onion
3 tbsp minced garlic
1/2 cup Chili Garlic Sauce
1 cup water
1 cup tomato paste
3/4 cup white vinegar
1/2 cup brown sugar
3 tablespoons honey
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
1 teaspoons liquid smoke flavoring
1/2 cup whiskey
1 tbsp onion powder
1 tbsp hot paprika (optional)
1 tbsp smoked paprika
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
3 tablespoons dark molasses
1 tablespoon crushed red pepper (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large sauce pan.  Heat to boiling stirring constantly.  Cover and reduce to a low simmer.  Stir occasionally.  Cook about 2 hours until thick and the sauce has reduced about 1 cup.
