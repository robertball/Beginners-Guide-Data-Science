Title:
The Best Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Large russet potatoes
1 stick of real butter (1/2 cup)
1 pint of Heavy Whipping Cream
1/2 cup milk (I use skim)
3/4 cup chopped onion
1 tsp. pepper
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. thyme
1/8 tsp. oregano
1 tsp. minced onion
1/8 tsp. minced garlic

Directions:
Peel potatoes, slice very finely.
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
In saucepan, heat all other indgrediants until warm but NOT boiling.
Place peeled potatoes in a well greased 9x9 glass pan.
Pour warm saucepan mixture over potatoes.
Place in oven at 325 degrees uncovered for 2-2.5 hours.
Serve Immediately!

Submitter: 
Sarah Simons
Description: 
This is a recipe that was handed down to me from my Grandmother Carol. The best recipe I have ever had - and I am real fussy about scalloped potatoes. Makes great left overs! Enjoy!
