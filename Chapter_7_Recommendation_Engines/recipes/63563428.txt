Title:
Smokey Black Bean Soup with Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices bacon
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 large carrots finely chopped
1 large onion finely chopped
2 stalks celery finely chopped
1/2 large red pepper finely chopped
1 box Rachael Ray Chicken Stock
1 (28 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
1 pkg black beans rinsed soaked overnight according to pkg directions
1-1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 heaping tablespoons chili powder
salt/pepper to taste
sour cream, for garnish (optional)
shredded Cheddar cheese (optional)

Directions:
Slice the bacon into 1/2 inch pieces. Fry the bacon in a bottom of a6-8 qt. cast iron kettle over medium-high heat, until cooked but not crispy, about 10 minutes. Remove the bacon to a bowl and set aside.
Roughly chop the carrots, onion, and celery and transfer to a food processor. Pulse the vegetables until finely chopped.
Add the chopped vegetables to the pot and saute till tender.  Add the garlic at the end and saute for another minute or so.
Add the chicken stock and can of diced tomatoes with their juice.  Add the beans and the bacon and the seasonings.  Bring to a boil then reduce heat and simmer covered for several hours stirring occasionally.
Add water if the consistency is too thick for your liking.
Serve the soup in bowls and top with sour cream, shredded cheese.
