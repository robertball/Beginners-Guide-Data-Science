Title:
Tater Tot Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1054100.jpg
Ingredients:
32 oz package Ore-Ida Tater Tots®
1/2 C sour cream
1 C spicy cheese sauce
2 C prepared taco meat
1/4 C jalapeño slices
1/4 C diced onion
Any additional nacho toppings desired

Directions:
Prepare Tater Tots® according to package directions.
Warm cheese sauce and taco meat.
Place Tater Tots® on a plate.  Top with desired nacho toppings.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
This delicious dish takes classic nachos and replaces the chips with Tater Tots®- they are absolutely addictive!
