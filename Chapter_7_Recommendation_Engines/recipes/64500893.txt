Title:
Homemade Hamburger Helper - Chili Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion, finely diced
4 1/2 cups hot water
1 cup milk
3 cup whole wheat elbow macaroni
1 (28 oz) can diced tomatoes, drained
2 Tablespoons cornstarch
3 Tablespoons chili powder
3 teaspoons onion powder
3 teaspoons garlic powder
2 1/2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons sugar (optional)
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. Brown ground beef with onion in large skillet, drain fat.
2. Add hot water, milk, macaroni, diced tomatoes, corn starch, chili powder, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, sugar, and sugar.
3. Bring to boil then simmer on low for 12 minutes or until macaroni is tender. Stir occasionally.
4. Remove from heat and stir in cheddar cheese.
5. Let the mixture stand, uncovered, for about 5 minutes. It will thicken as it cools.
