Title:
Portland Rickey - Winner of the 2013 Tales of the Cocktail Competition Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 oz Martin Miller's Gin
3/4 oz fresh lemon juice
1/4 oz Green Chartreuse
4 oz saison or biere de garde style beer
squeezed half of lemon (lemon shell), for garnish

Directions:
Combine the Martin Miller's Gin, Green Chartreuse, lemon juice, and beer in an ice-filled highball glass. Drop the squeezed half-lemon into the drink. Gently stir.

Submitter: 
Seth Kolloen
Description: 
Jacob Grier of Metrovino in Portland, OR is the creator of the Portland Rickey, a nod to the classic Rickey. The Portland Rickey won the Tales of the Cocktail 2013 Official Cocktail Competition.
