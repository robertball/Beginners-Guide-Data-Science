Title:
Soy Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup gradulated sugar
1 egg
1 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup soy milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 tablespoon chocolate sundae syrup
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 3/4 cup sifted all-purpose flour

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
2. Leave butter at room for 5 minutes. Beat until soft. Add sugar and blend until creamy.
3. Beat in egg, peanut butter, salt, baking soda, vanilla, milk and syrup.
4. Add flour and knead dough with hands.
5. Roll dough into small balls and bake for 15 minutes on a greased cookie sheet.

Submitter:

Description: 
These cookies are rich, crumbly, and not beany!
