Title:
Cream of Carrot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken stock
2 carrots, peeled and sliced
1 potato, peeled and sliced
2/3 cup milk
dash curry powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook carrots & potatoes in stock until tender.
Drain (reserving stock) and puree in blender with milk.
Return to the pot with stock, season well with salt, pepper, parsley and curry powder.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is one of those recipes I had tucked away and always *meant* to make ... but never did.  Now I'm kicking myself - what a simple fabulous soup! The curry gives just the right amount of kick.
