Title:
Easy Drop Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 T Egg Beaters (1/2 an egg, beaten)
2 T whole wheat flour
1 T chopped onion
salt, to taste
1 1/2 tsp oil

Directions:
Mix the Egg Beaters, whole wheat flour, chopped onion, and salt until the mix thickens. If desired, add other spices.
Heat the oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
Drop the mix in a tablespoon at a time and cook for a few minutes on each side.
