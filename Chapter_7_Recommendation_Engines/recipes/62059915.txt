Title:
Apple Breakfast Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups apples, peeled, cored and chopped

Directions:
Cream together the butter and sugar. Add the eggs and beat well. Sift together the flour,salt, baking soda, cinnamon and cloves. Add to the butter mixture. Stir in the apples. POur mixture into a greased standard loaf pan. Bake in a 350 oven for 1 hour. Let bread stand in pan until cool. Remove from the pan and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
Warm sweet apple bread is perfect for breakfast or for a snack. I have enjoyed this recipe many times.
