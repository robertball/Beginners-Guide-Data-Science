Title:
Big Ray's Onion Beer Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5198716.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp butter
1 medium onion, sliced thin
2 tsp minced garlic
3 cups flour
1 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp baking powder
1 tsp table salt
12 oz beer
2 Tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and place rack in top 1/3 of oven.
In a 9 x 13 dish, place butter, onions and garlic and while oven preheats, let butter melt and onions get soft (watch so butter does not get too brown).
In a bowl mix together the flour, sugar baking powder and salt.  Add beer and stir until mixed.  Dough will be sticky and heavy.
When butter is melted, pour butter , onions and garlic into a bowl, leaving about 1 Tbsp of butter in dish.  Coat dish with remaining butter.  Spoon dough into dish and spread out evenly. Divide onions on top of dough and pour butter and garlic over dough.   Sprinkle rosemary over dough.
Bake in top 1/3 of oven for 20 to 25 minutes until skewer comes out clean.  Cut and serve immediately.
