Title:
Lazy Lady's Cordon Bleu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. boneless/skinless chicken breasts
1/3 lb. sliced Swiss cheese from deli
1/3 lb. sliced smoked ham from deli
1 can (10.75 ounce)Cream of chicken soup
1 cup milk
handful of fresh mushrooms, sliced
6 T. butter, melted
1 cup dried seasoned breadcrumbs

Directions:
1. Grease shallow baking dish or coat with nonstick spray.
2. In a small bowl combine soup and milk.  Pour enough mixture into pan to coat bottom.
3. Place chicken in baking dish.  Season with salt and pepper.
4. Julienne ham and cheese slices and layer over chicken.
5. Sprinkle mushroom slices over top
6. Pour remaining soup mixture over top.
7. Bake uncovered in 350 degree oven for 20 minutes.
9. Meanwhile, mix seasoned breadcrumbw with melted butter.
19.At end of first 20 minutes, top with
buttered breadcrumbs and bake an additional 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
KAET
Description: 
A quick weeknight version of the French classic.  While not exactly authentic, it's still rich and yummy.
