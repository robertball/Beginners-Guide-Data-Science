Title:
Chokecherry Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 cups chokecherries
5 cups water
3 cups sugar
1 box regular or lite Certo

Directions:
Place berries in a saucepan, add water, bring to boiling point and simmer gently until tender, ~ 15 min.
Crush berries through a course sieve.
Mix juice and sugar and bring to hard rolling boil, stirring constantly.
Simmer x 5 min.
Stir Certo into 1/4 cup sugar.
Add to juice and 1 tsp. margarine or butter
Place back over high heat till boiling.
Add rest of sugar. Stir till boiling.
Put into jars
Double batch fits in dutch oven ~ 3 1/2 qts
