Title:
Always Juicy Baked Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8-10lb. smoked ham, bone in
1 bottle good quality beer
1/2 C loose packed brown sugar
2 TBSP dijon mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In med. bowl: whisk together beer, brown sugar and mustard. Place ham, flesh side up, into large roasting pan. Pour beer mixture over top.
Bake, uncovered, for 15 mins. Baste 4 or 5 times.
Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees.
Cover and cook for 1 1/2 hrs, basting frequently.
Remove cover and bake another 15 mins., continuing to baste. Take roaster from oven and let ham sit for 10-15 mins before carving.

Submitter: 
bitsybites
Description: 
A never fail recipe that is a family favorite. Frequent basting during the cooking time is key to a delicious result. We can't help taking several
tester bites while waiting for dinner to be ready.
Makes great left-overs for sandwiches.
