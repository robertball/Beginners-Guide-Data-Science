Title:
Light 'n Fluffy Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1016460.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 Tbsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
6 Tbsp. sugar
3/4 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
1 egg
1 cup greek vanilla yogurt
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 cup reduced fat buttermilk
1 egg
1 Tbsp. water
2 Tbsp. turbinado or regular sugar (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degree.
In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar until thoroughly mixed.
With a pastry knife, cut in butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Stir in egg, yogurt, vanilla and buttermilk until moistened.
Turn dough out onto lightly floured surface gently kneading twice.
Pat dough into a 9-inch round circle.
Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Using a pizza cutter or sharp knife, cut into eight wedges.
In a small bowl, beat egg with water.
Brush egg mixture over top until lightly covered and sprinkle with sugar.
Bake at 425 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Jillian
Description: 
Moist, light, fluffy and delicious - scones are not only simple to make but are a tasty little treat to be enjoyed anytime of day. Best served warm with butter and your favorite jam, they are the perfect accompaniment to a piping hot cup of coffee or tea!
