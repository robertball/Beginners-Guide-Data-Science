Title:
Southern Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium sized green cabbage, shredded
3 medium sized carrots, peeled and shredded
2 T shredded red onio
1 cup light mayo
2T white vinegar
3 T sugar
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper

Directions:
in a small container with lid, combine mayo, vinegar, sugar,salt and pepper, put on lid, shake until well blended, put in fridge
in a very large bowl, combine cabbage, carrots and onion, mix to incorporate very well
Pour mayo mixture over cabbage, toss to coat, cover and chill for at least one hour or until ready to serve

Submitter: 
M. Karen
Description: 
Lighter version of traditional creamy coleslaw
