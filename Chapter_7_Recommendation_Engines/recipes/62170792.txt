Title:
Saucy Sheperds Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 medium sized russet potatoes, peeled and boiled
4 T. butter, unsalted
2 tsp. sea salt, divided
1/2 C. milk
1 lb. lean ground beef
6 green onions, minced
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato sauce
1 T. oregano
1 T. basil
1 T. thyme
1 tsp. black pepper
2 (10.75 ounce) cans green beans, drained

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine boiled potatoes, 1 tsp. sea salt, butter and milk.  Beat with hand mixer until creamy; set aside.
In a skillet, brown ground beef with onions and garlic.  Stir in tomato sauce, seasonings and remaining salt, let simmer on low heat for 30 minutes.
In a sufficient cassarole dish, pour beef and tomato mixture and green beans into bottom, stir until evenly blended.  Spread mashed potatoes evenly over top.
Bake in preheated 450 degree oven for 25-30 minutes or until top is lightly browned and bottom layer is bubbling.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Seasoned tomato sauce gives this traditional English dish a kick of flavor.  One of your favorite comfort foods just became tastier...you'll want to eat this all winter long!
