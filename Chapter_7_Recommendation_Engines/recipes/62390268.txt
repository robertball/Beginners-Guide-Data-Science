Title:
Pesto Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Basil pesto
1/4 cup Herbed soft fresh cheese
10 cherry tomatoes, sliced length-wise
2 thick slices of italian or french bread

Directions:
Spread cheese on slices of bread.
Spread Basil pesto on top of cheese.
Arrange tomatoes seed-side down on pesto
