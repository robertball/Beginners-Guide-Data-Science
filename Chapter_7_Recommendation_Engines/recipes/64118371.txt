Title:
Grandma Bronieth Childress Old Fashioned Gingerbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1/2 cup fresh churned butter
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1 cup molasses
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon
* 1 teaspoon ground cloves
* 1 Tablespoon ground ginger
* 1 Tablespoon baking soda
* 2 1/2 cups flour
* 2 eggs
* 1 1/2 cups buttermilk

Directions:
1)In a bowl cream butter and sugar until well blended. Blend in remaining ingredients. Grease a 9x13 baking pan. Heat pan in 350 Degree F oven until very hot. Remove from oven, pour batter and return to oven. Bake for 40 minutes at 350 Degrees F.
