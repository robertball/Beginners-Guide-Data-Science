Title:
Creamy Coconut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package white cake mix
1 (14 ounce) can cream of coconut
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk (may omit)
1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (10 ounce) package flaked coconut

Directions:
Prepare cake according to package directions.  Bake in a 9x13 inch pan.  Cool completely.
In a small bowl combine cream of coconut and condensed milk.
Poke holes in cake with a straw or fork.  Pour milk mixture over cake (refrigerate overnight) and spread with whipped topping.  Sprinkle coconut over cake.
Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Culicerto
Description: 
This is a coconut cake that you won't have to slave over!  A white cake is filled with a  cream of coconut and condensed milk mixture, topped with whipped topping and finished with flaked coconut.  It's that easy!Modified from: Creamy Coconut Cake
