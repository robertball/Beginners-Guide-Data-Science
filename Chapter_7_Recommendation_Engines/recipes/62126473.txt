Title:
Enchilada Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two pounds of ground beef
One 12 ounce can of enchilada sauce
One 12 ounce can of black beans
3/4 cup of oatmeal
Two tsp garlic salt

Directions:
Mix ground beef, 1/2 can of enchilada sauce, oatmeal and garlic salt.
Layer 1/3 of meat mixture in pan then cover with 1/3 can of black beans.
Continue layer of ground beef and black beans ending with layer of ground beef.
Pour remaining enchilada sauce over the loaf.
Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees.

Easy mexican enchilada meatloaf with black beans and oatmeal.
