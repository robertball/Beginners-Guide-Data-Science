Title:
Abode Italian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2tbsp olive oil
2 cloves of garlic
8 small to med boneless skinless, trimmed chicken thighs
1 large tomato chopped
1/2 cup chopped celery
8 oz small button mushrooms whole
3 spring (green) onions chopped reserving green tops
1 tbsp basil leaves
1/3 cup dry white wine
Freshly ground black pepper and salt to taste.

Directions:
In a large skillet, place the olive oil and brown the chicken with the garlic.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper and add the celery, basil, spring onions, mushrooms and wine. Cover and simmer 15 minutes on a med-low heat.
Add the tomato, basil, onion greens and stir until blended.  Cover and cook an additional 5 min.  Serve on top of orzo or white rice.

Submitter: 
Sneakyteaky
Description: 
This salivating economical chicken recipe is sure to become a favourite in your abode as it has for mine when I first put it together for an unexpected crowd last year.  Button mushrooms, a fresh garden tomato, white wine and homegrown basil makes chicken thighs memorable.
