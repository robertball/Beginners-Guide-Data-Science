Title:
Italiano Meat Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground round
1/3 cup chopped green pepper
3/4 cup water
6 oz. can seasoned tomato paste (Italian Herb or Garlic & Onion)
1/3 cup parmesan cheese
1½ oz package dry spaghetti sauce mix
1 deep dish pie shell
1½ cups mozzarella cheese
1 or 2 cloves chopped garlic (optional)

Directions:
Brown beef; drain. Add peppers and garlic, cook 2 minutes. Stir in water, tomato paste and dry sauce mix. Cover and simmer 10 minutes. Sprinkle 1/2 of parmesan cheese on bottom of pie crust; spread half of the meat mixture over parmesan. Sprinkle 1 cup mozzarella cheese over meat; layer remaining meat and parmesan cheese. Bake on cookie sheet in preheated 350 oven for 15 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella and heat until melted. Serve with fresh green salad for a complete meal.

Submitter: 
Paulina Johnson
Description: 
Easy to make dish when you crave Italian food and want something different.
