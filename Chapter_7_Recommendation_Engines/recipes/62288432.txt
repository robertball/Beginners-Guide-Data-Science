Title:
Red Pepper Cheese Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sweet red peppers, roasted
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 tsp. crushed black peppercorns
3 oz. mozzerella or asiago cheese, cut in 1/2 inch (1 cm) cubes

Directions:
Cut peppers lengthwise into 1/2 inch (1 cm) wide strips. Toss withy oil and pepper.
Wrap 1 pepper strip around each cheese cube; secure with toothpick. (Bites can be covered and refrigerated for up to 8 hours.)
Makes 24.
