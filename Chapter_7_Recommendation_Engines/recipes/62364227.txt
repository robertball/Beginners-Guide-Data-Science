Title:
Sausage and Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Chopped onion
1 16 oz smoked turkey sausage cut into 1/4 in slices
2 garlic cloves
1 14 oz can fat free chicken broth
2 TB brown sugar
2 TB tomato paste
1 t dried thyme
1 t black pepper
3 16 oz cans of white beans (cannelini works)
1/8 t ground red pepper
3 slices white bread
2 TB fresh chopped parsley
cooking spray

Directions:
Heat dutch over over med high heat.  Coat pan with cooking spray. Add onion and turkey sausage- saute 5 min or until browned.  Add garlic and saute.  Stir in chicken broth,  Stir in brown sugar, tomato paste, herbs, red pepper, and beans.  (Bay leaf if desired).  bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat, and simmer 5 min.  Place bread in food processor and pulse 10 times (or use breadcrumbs).  sprinkle evenly over bean mixture in casserole dish.  bake at 375 for 15 min.  Discard bay leaf.  Sprinkle with parsley.
