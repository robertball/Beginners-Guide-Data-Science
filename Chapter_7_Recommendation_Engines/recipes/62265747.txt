Title:
Dreamsicle Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
Box of Orange cake mix
3 oz box Orange jello dry
3 oz instant pudding dry(vanilla/french vanilla)
Frosting:
8 oz soft cream cheese
1 tsp Vanilla
1+ C Powdered sugar
3 oz Orange jello dry
8 oz Cool whip

Directions:
Make and bake cake to directions adding in dry jello and dry pudding. 2 layer cake or 13x9. Mix cream cheese, sugar, vanilla, dry jello and fold in cool whip, frost cake.
