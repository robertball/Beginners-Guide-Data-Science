Title:
Big Ray's Tilapia that Bites Back Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4572455.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large egg
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 tsp Italian seasoning
3 Tbsp bread crumbs
1/2  crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 tsppepper
4 tilapia fillets (6 ounces each)

Directions:
Beat egg  in a shallow bowl. In another shallow bowl, combine the cheese, Italian seasoning, bread crumbs, pepper flakes and pepper. Dip fillets in egg, then cheese mixture.
Place in a 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan coated with cooking spray. Bake at 425Â° for 10-15 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork
