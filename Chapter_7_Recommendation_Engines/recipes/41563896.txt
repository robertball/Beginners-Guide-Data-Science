Title:
Cashew Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless chicken breasts
3 cups cood tricolor spiral pasta
2 celery ribs, chopped
6 green onions, chopped
2 green peppers, chopped
1 1/2 cup mayo
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 tbs cider vinegar
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp lemon juice
2 cups salted cashew halves

Directions:
Grill chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces. Combine with pasta, celery, green onion and green peppers.
In a separate bowl, combine mayo, brown sugar, vinegar, salt and lemon juice. Pour over salad. Add cashews
