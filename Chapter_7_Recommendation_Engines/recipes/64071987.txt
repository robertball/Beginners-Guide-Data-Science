Title:
Gluten Free Chocolate Chip Cookies (Vegan) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1132921.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup almond flour
1/2 cup brown rice flour
1/2 cup cocoa powder
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon almond extract
4 figs chopped
1/4 cup + 1 tablespoon cup agave
1/2 cup chocolate chips
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 cup warm water
2 tablespoons chia seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees.
Stir chia seeds into warm water.
Let sit for 15 minutes
In a mixing bowl, stir together flours, salt, baking powder, cocoa powder, chocolate chips.
Add chopped figs, agave, almond and vanilla extracts to chia seed mixture.
Blend thoroughly in a Blendtec or Vitamix blender.
Stir together wet and dry ingredients.
Spoon out on parchment paper placing parchment paper on the baking rack in the oven.
Press to flatten cookies with a wet fork.
Bake 12-15 minutes.
Cool on cooling rack.
