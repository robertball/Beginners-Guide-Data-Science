Title:
BREAKFAST CREME BRULEE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp low sugar strawberry preserves
6 oz fat-free vanilla Greek yogurt (I used Chobani)
1 tbsp turbinado sugar (sugar in the raw)
1 sliced strawberry to top

Directions:
1. Place fruit preserves at the bottom of a 6 or 7 oz ramekin.
2. Top with yogurt. When ready to serve top with sugar.
3. Using a torch, melt the sugar and form a crispy top. Cool a few minutes then top with fruit.
4. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe makes extremely sweet & tasty breakfast in a snap!
