Title:
Cindy's Turkey Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/302677.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups cooked turkey meat, chopped
1 cup finely chopped celery
3 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, mix the turkey meat, celery, relish, and mayonnaise. Season with salt, and pepper. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Submitter:
