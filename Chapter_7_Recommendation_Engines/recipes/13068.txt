Title:
Deer Soup with Cream of Mushroom and Celery Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/776975.jpg
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
4 carrots, chopped
1 pound venison (deer meat)
1 pound boneless pork loin, cubed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 pint water
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup

Directions:
Add onion, carrots, deer meat, pork, salt, sugar, and water to crock pot. Cook for 30 minutes.
Add cream of mushroom soup and cream of celery soup. Cook 3 1/2 to 4 hours.
