Title:
OHIO ANGEL COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/346859.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cream of tartar
1/4 tsp salt
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
1/2 cup granulated sugar

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat shortening with an electric mixer on med-high for 1 minute.  Add the brown sugar, 1/2 cup sugar, baking soda, cream of tartar and salt.  Beat till mixed.
Beat in the egg and vanilla till the mixture is combined.
Beat in as much of the flour as you can with the mixer.  Stir in any remaining flour with a wooden spoon.
Shape into 1 inch balls.  Roll in the 1/2 cup sugar to coat.  Place 2 inches apart on baking sheet.
Bake in a 375 degree oven for 8-10 minutes or until starting to brown for tender cookies or until golden brown for crispier cookies.  Cool completely on wire rack.

Submitter: 
Karen Packer
Description: 
These cookies are tender and sugary and you can change the depth of flavor by using a dark brown sugar or light brown sugar.
