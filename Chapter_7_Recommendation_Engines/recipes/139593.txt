Title:
Frosty Mochas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/756710.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cold coffee
2 cups milk
1/4 cup chocolate syrup
1/4 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated

Directions:
Pour coffee into ice cube tray. Freeze until solid, or overnight.
In a blender, combine coffee ice cubes, milk, chocolate syrup and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Your friends will rave about these frosty drinks! Freeze your leftover coffee in ice cube trays and keep them on hand to make these drinks anytime. These sugar-free treats are delicious topped with whipped cream.
