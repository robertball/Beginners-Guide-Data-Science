Title:
Cuban bread french toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg whites
2 pieces of fresh Cuban bread
1 tsp water
butter to coat pan

Directions:
separate the egg whites from the yolks.
add the water.
melt the butter in the pan.
coat the bread in the egg whites.
put into the pan and fry for 2 minutes on each side.
enjoy.

Submitter: 
Elle Mcgrath
Description: 
this is one of my favorite breakfast recipes. it is crunchy Cuban bread french toast. you will love it.
