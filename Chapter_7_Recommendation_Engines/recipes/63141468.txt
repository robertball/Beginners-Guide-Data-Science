Title:
TW Fried Catfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs Catfish
Salt--To taste
Pepper- To taste
Tex Joy Season All
1/2 lb. Saltine Crakers
1 Package Sliced Almonds
1 Pt. Milk
2 Eggs
1 tsp Garlic Juice
Peanut Oil

Directions:
Season fish with Salt, Pepper and Tex Joy Season All.  Put in refridge for 1 to 3 hours.  Crumble crakers by hand or food processor and add sliced almonds.  Beat eggs and add milk and Garlic Juice.  Soak fish in egg mixture for 5 to 10 minutes and then batter fish with crakers and almonds.  Fry in peanut oil that has been preheated to 375 degrees.  Fish is done when it floats to the top of the fish fryer.

Submitter: 
Terry
Description: 
One of my favoite Fried Catfish recipes when having a fish fry.  This is not to spicy and is usually a crowd favorite.  Adapted from one of one of my old working buddies in the late 70s.
