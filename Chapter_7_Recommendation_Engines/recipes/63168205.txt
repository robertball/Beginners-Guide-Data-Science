Title:
Cauliflower Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cloves of garlic toasted in olive oil
1 head of cauliflower florets in large pot
Red pepper flakes
Lemon
Salt and pepper
Bread crumbs
Olive oil and parmesan toasted in a fry pan (parsley or thyme added in the end)
Butter

Directions:
Olive oil and parmesan toasted in a fry pan (parsley or thyme added in the end)
Boil cauliflower in just enough water to almost cover it until it is falling apart
Add a little butter (?3T instead of cream)
Blend with immersion blender
Season with salt, pepper and red pepper flakes
Cook and drain pasta
Add juice of half a lemon to pasta while it is still hot
Pour sauce over pasta then sprinkle with parm and toasted bread crumbs.
