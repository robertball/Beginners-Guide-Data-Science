Title:
Pork Tenderloin with Herb Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup red wine vinegar or cider vinegar
1/4 cup butter
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoons seasoned salt
1 1/2 teaspoons dried parsley flakes
1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 boneless pork tenderloin

Directions:
Melt butter in 9 x 13 pan in microwave. Add remaining marinade ing. Add pork, cover and cook at 350 for 30-40 minutes.
