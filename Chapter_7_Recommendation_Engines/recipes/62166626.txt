Title:
Refreshing Summer Cucumber Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups very hot tap water
8 tbsp sugar or Splenda brand sweetener (use more if you like a sweeter drink)
1/2 large cucumber, washed and sliced
1 lemon, squeezed for it's juice.
1/2 cup Real Lemon brand juice concentrate (or add more fresh lemon juice)
3 to 4 cans lemon lime soda or ginger ale.
Ice cubes
Thin slices of lemons and cucumbers cut into halves (use as a garnish)

Directions:
Add sugar and water to picher and stir until sweetener disolves.
Stir in fresh lemon juice from lemon, Lemon juice concentrate and cucumber slices.
Stir well.
Refrigerate 30 minutes.
When ready to serve, stir in the soda and pour over glasses that are full of ice cubes.
I add a half slice of lemon and cucumber to each glass as a garnish! Very pretty!
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
This is the most refreshing summer beverage you've ever tasted. It's unique in the way is infuses the flavors of fresh garden cucumbers, the tangy brightness of fresh lemons, and the lightness of an effervescent lemon-lime soda to create a delicious thirst quenching southern summer ade. Perfect when relaxing on your front porch swing on a warm summer night.
