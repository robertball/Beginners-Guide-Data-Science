Title:
Crusted Baked Salmon & Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (6oz. each) bags of fresh baby spinach
1 salmon fillet (1 Â½ lbs.)
1 teaspoon olive oil
3 tablespoons honey
3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
1/4 cup mayonnaise (Hellmans)
1/4 cup cornflakes
2 tablespoon sliced almonds
2 tablespoon chopped pecans

Directions:
Coat a 9x13 baking dish with non-stick spray.
Place spinach in baking dish, top with salmon.  Drizzle the olive oil over the salmon & spinach.
Combine honey & mustard.  Brush 2 T. of the honey-mustard mixture over the salmon.
Add the mayonnaise to the remaining honey-mustard; refrigerate until serving.
In a food processor combine the cornflake, almonds and pecans process until ground.  Press mixture onto salmon.  Bake uncovered at 450 for 18-22 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.           Drizzle cooked salmon with reserved honey-mustard sauce.
Serve the spinach & salmon on top of couscous or wild rice on each serving plate.
