Title:
Angel Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/4 cup butter
1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
1 cup white wine
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed chicken soup
4 ounces cream cheese
can of drained mushrooms
package frozen veggies
1 pound angel hair pasta

Directions:
Combine in crockpot:
butter, dressing mix,wine, mushrooms and chicken soup. Mix in cream cheese, and stir until smooth.
Cook on low for 5-6 hours.
After about 4 hours, add frozen veggies

Submitter: 
Bobbee Valentine
Description: 
Bake chicken breasts in a delectably rich sauce made of butter, dry Italian salad dressing mix, wine, golden mushroom soup and cream cheese with chives. Serve over angel hair pasta for a dish that is fit for your most elegant dinner parties.Modified from: Angel Chicken Pasta
