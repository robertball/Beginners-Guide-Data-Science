Title:
Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp Butter
3 Tbsp olive oil
1-1/2 lbs of boneless chicken breast, cut into 1" cubes
1 onion chopped
zest of one lemon
2 Tbsp lemon juice
2 tsp paprika
1 tsp granulated garlic
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1 14.5 oz can of diced tomatoes, undrained
1 cup heavy cream
1-1/2 tsp corn starch
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Saute chicken in butter and olive oil until no longer pink
Add onion and continue cooking until onion is translucent
Add lemon zest, lemon juice and all of the spices, continue cooking until spices are well blended
Stir in tomatoes, cover and simmer for 30 minutes
Add salt
Add cream and blend well, continue simmering until cream is bubbling
Add corn starch
Serve over rice

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Spicy chicken in a cream sauce with an East Indian flair.
