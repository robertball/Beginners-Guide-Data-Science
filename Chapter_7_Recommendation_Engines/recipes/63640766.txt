Title:
Spicy Mango Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken wings
1/2 C Corn Flake crumbs
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp hot paprika
1/2 tsp Hungarian paprika
1 Egg, beaten
2-3 TBS melted butter
Sauce
1/4 C store bought spicy mango salad dressing
1 TBS ketjap manis
1/2 tsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp minced fresh ginger
2 TBS palm sugar
a couple TBS water to thin the sauce.

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients for the sauce and let sit while preparing everything else.
Mix the spices together.  Dip the wings in the egg, then in the spice mix.  Place on a greased cookie sheet and drizzle with the melted butter.  Bake at 400 degrees for 25 minutes.  Pour the sauce over the wings and bake an additional 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
CaraMeg
Description: 
Tender, juicy wings in a sweet and spicy sauce, with the perfect crispy skin!
