Title:
Rouladen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 slices 3/8 in  raw beef
meat rub
dijon mustard
20 slices pre cooked bacon
dill pickle
sauerkraut
beef broth
beef gravy

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
pound sliced beef
apply rub, some dijon mustard
sauerkraut, bacon, dill pickle
and hold with toothpick
place in baking dish and add beef broth
make beef gravy
