Title:
ROAST PORK LOIN WITH APPLE TOPPING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp. 	sugar
2 Tbsp.	flour
1-1/2 tsp. 	salt
1 tsp. 		dry mustard
1 tsp. 		caraway seeds
1/4 tsp 		black pepper
1/4 tsp. 	ground sage
4-5 lbs. 	pork loin roast
Topping:
1-1/2 cups 	applesauce
1/2 cup 	brown sugar
1/4 tsp. 	cinnamon
1/4 tsp. 	mace
1/4 tsp. 	salt

Directions:
Mix sugar, flour, salt, mustard, caraway seeds, pepper and sage
together.  Rub mixture over surface of pork.  Place fat side up in
roasting pan and bake at 325 for 1.5 hours.  Mix topping ingredients and
spread over roast.  Bake 1 hour longer.  Add water to bottom of pan to
make excellent gravy.
