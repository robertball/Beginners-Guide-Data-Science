Title:
Barbecue Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
3/4 cup ketchup
3/4 cup honey OR 1/2 cup brown sugar
4 cloves garlic, pressed
1 - 2 t. Tabasco sauce

Directions:
Mix and use!
This is especially good on pork.

Submitter: 
BAJATHECAT
Description: 
When I am not in the mood to make an elaborate barbecue sauce, I like to use this simple but excellent glaze.  It provides plenty of flavor with just a few simple ingredients.
