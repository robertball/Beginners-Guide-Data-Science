Title:
LOADED TURKEY MEATLOAF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/953530.jpg
Ingredients:
20.8 OZ pkg. LEAN GROUND TURKEY (approx. 1-1/2 lbs)
2 tbsp. olive oil
6 oz. pkg fresh mushrooms lightly sliced and chopped
1/4 cup chopped and minced red and/or green peppers
1 medium onion finely chopped
2 cloves garlic minced
2 eggs lightly beaten
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/3 cup ketchup
1/3 cup bread crumbs and 2 slices of torn up white bread
1/4 cup milk
1/2 tsp. thyme
1 tbsp. chopped italian flat parsley
3/4 cup frozen peas
4 oz mozzarella cheese shredded or diced
few shakes of Worcestershire sauce  approx 1 tbsp.
1/4 tsp. salt or to taste
fresh cracked black pepper or to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Soak the white bread in milk. Add breadcrumbs. In a medium fry pan, add oil and saute onion, garlic, peppers and  mushrooms until wilted approx. 4-5 min. remove from heat and add chicken broth and Worcestershire sauce and let cool. Combine all other ingredients except the mozzarella, tbsp grated cheese and peas. Mix meat mixture well. The mixture will be loose but formable. In a large nonstick oblong pan, spoon 3/4 of the mixture lengthwise and form a gully. In that gully add the peas and top with mozzarella and the tbsp grated cheese. Spoon the remainder of the meat mixture on top of the loaf and seal in the peas filling. It may be easier to form with oiled hands. squeeze any ketchup pattern on top of meatloaf and Bake at 350 for 50 min.

Description: 
This lean colorful healthy turkey loaf has all the vegetables you need baked right in. It consists of 4 of your food groups. This make great leftovers your family will love (if there's any left). Try to use a fresh mozzarella for this dish.
