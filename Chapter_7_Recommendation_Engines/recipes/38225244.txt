Title:
Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Olive Oil
Corn Tortillas
1 Pkg Fish
1/2 t Cumin
1 t Chile Powder
1/4 t Cayenne Pepper
2 pinches of salt
1 dash of pepper
Toppings:
Avocado
Lime
Cilantro (use as you would lettuce)
Shredded cheddar cheese
Sour Cream

Directions:
Warm Tortillas:
Warm olive oil on med. in pan
Warm tortillas 20 seconds per side
Place on Towel to drain
Cook Fish:
Mix seasoning in large bowl
drop in fish
gently season each side
Cook fish on lightly oiled pan
let fish cook 3mn per side
Serve:
Place pieced up fish in tortillas
squirt lime juice over fish
add toppings as you wish
