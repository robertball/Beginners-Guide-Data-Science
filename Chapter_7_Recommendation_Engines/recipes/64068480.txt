Title:
Big Ray's Healthy  Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1131752.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ripe medium banana
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup applesauce
2 Tbl ground flaxseed
2 Tbl honey
2 cups old-fashioned oats
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat banana and brown sugar until smooth. Add applesauce; beat well. Stir in flax and honey. In a small bowl, combine the oats, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg and baking soda; stir into creamed mixture just until blended. Gently stir in raisins.
Press into an 11-in. x 7-in. baking dish coated with cooking spray. Bake at 350Â° for 14-18 minutes or until set and edges are lightly browned. Cool on a wire rack.
