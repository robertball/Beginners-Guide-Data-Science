Title:
Alexander's Ultimate Burger Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 beef patties { already cooked to perfection}
1 pound of any type of cheese
6 jars of pickled dills
30 slices of tomato
30 slices of onion
2 Hamburger buns

Directions:
1. Place 1 hamburger bun on a plate.
2. Then place 1 beef patty on the bun.
3. Then place the cheese on the beef patty.
4. Then place 6 pickled dills on top of the cheese.
5. Place 5 tomato slices on top of the pickled dills.
6. Place 5 onion slices on top of the tomato slices
5. Place 1 more beef patty on top of the onion slices.
6. Continue with the ingredients until you have 4 beef patties & 4 slices of cheese, 6 pickled dills & 5 tomato slices & 5 onion slices on each bun.
7. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe makes a perfect, juicy burger that is sure to blow your mind & excite your taste buds to no end!
