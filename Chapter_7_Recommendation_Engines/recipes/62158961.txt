Title:
Baked Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box elbow macaroni (or other shaped pasta)
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 1/4 cup sour cream
1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
2 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1 Tbsp paprika
Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Boil macaroni according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and return to pot. Add butter, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, 2 cups of Cheddar cheese, paprika, and pepper to taste. Mix ingredients together until cheese is mostly melted. Pour into a 9x13 baking dish. Top with remaining Cheddar cheese. Bake for 30 minutes, or until bubbly and cheese is slightly browned. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Tina Bassett
Description: 
This is an adaptation of the homemade macaroni and cheese my mother made as a child. Super easy and delicious!! Note: Does not make a creamy sauce.
