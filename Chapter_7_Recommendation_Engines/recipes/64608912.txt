Title:
Easy Tuscan Style Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Thin Pizza Crusts
2 Small Jars of White Pizza Sauce
Salami (Thinly Sliced, about 10 slices)
Roasted Red Peppers
Baby Spinach
Banana Peppers
Oregano
Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Lightly brush olive oil pizza crust
Layer white sauce, salami, red peppers, banana peppers, spinach, and top with cheese.  Sprinkle oregano on top.
Cook in oven for about 9 minutes, until cheese is melted
Optional: Broil for cheese to get a slightly crispy top

Submitter: 
Chandice
Description: 
Similar taste to Domino's Tuscan Pizza, easy and nice change from regular pepperoni
