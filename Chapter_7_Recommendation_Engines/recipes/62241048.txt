Title:
Linda's Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 strips of bacon cooked and crumbled
4 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 large onion, chopped
1 cup chicken broth
2 cans whole corn
2 cans cream style corn
1 teaspoons salt
ground black pepper to taste
1 cup half-and-half
3 cups milk

Directions:
Put the bacon in a large pot over medium-high heat, and cook until crisp. Drain and crumble, reserving about 2 tablespoons drippings in the pot.
Mix potatoes and onion into the pot with the crumbled bacon and reserved drippings. Cook and stir about 7 minutes. Pour in the chicken broth,milk, and stir in corn and cream style corn. Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer, and cover pot. Simmer 20 minutes, stirring frequently, until potatoes are tender. Add half and half just before serving.
