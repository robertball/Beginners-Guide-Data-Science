Title:
Green Raz-ma-taz Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium banana
1 serving plant-based protein (pea, hemp, etc)
1 cup coconut water
1/2 cup raspberries, with a few for garnish
1 large handful spinach

Directions:
1.) Wash the raspberries and spinach then combine all ingredients in the blender.
2.) Blend until smooth, about 60 seconds.
3.) Serve and enjoy!
