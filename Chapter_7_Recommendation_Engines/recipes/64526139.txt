Title:
Big Ray's Hot Cheesy Bacon Dip Recipe

Ingredients:
3/4 cup Shredded  Cheddar
1 bag real bacon bits
2 green onions, finely chopped, divided
1 pkg. (8 oz.) Neufch√¢tel Cheese, softened
1/3 cup Mayonnaise
1/2 tsp. of smoked paprika.

Directions:
Spray inside of crock pot with cooking spray.
Mix remaining ingredients until blended;pour into crock pot.
Heat on low for 1 hour or until hot and bubbly around edge, turn crock pot to warm.
Serve with breadsticks and/or flatbread.
