Title:
Homemade Pasta Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 c. flour
1 T. olive oil
2 eggs
1 t. salt
1/2 c. water

Directions:
In large bowl, with wooden spoon, stir flour, oil, eggs water and salt to make a stiff dough.  On slightly floured surface, knead dough until smooth (not sticky) about 20 times.  Wrap dough in saran wrap and let rest 30 minutes.
