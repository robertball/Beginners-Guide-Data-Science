Title:
Dutch Baby Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/804076.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbs butter
3 large eggs
3/4 cup milk
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup flour
2 tbs sugar
1/8 tsp salt
dust with powdered sugar
topping of choice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Beat eggs well, then add milk, vanilla, sugar, salt and flour, mix well.
Coat 10" cast iron skillet with 3 tbs butter and pour mixture in.
Cook 15-20 minutes.  (16 seems to work well for my 10 inch skillet)
Dust with powdered sugar and add favorite topping.
