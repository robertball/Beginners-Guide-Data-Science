Title:
Super Soybean Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of soybeans (raw)
2 cans of Cream of chicken soup mix
1 cup of chopped turkey breast
1 16oz bag of your favorite frozen veggies (i used mixed)
8 oz. of Sour Cream (Fat Free can be subsituted)

Directions:
Soak soybeans overnight or prepare in crock pot for approximately 10-14 hours.
After soybeans are completely cooked, mix in the Cream of Chicken, chopped turkey breast, frozen veggies and sourcream.
Let warm for approximately 30 minutes to 1 hour.
Serve with grated cheddar or parmesean cheese on top.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a soybean based recipe that is healthy and delicious with lots of flavors and textures.  My kids even like it.
