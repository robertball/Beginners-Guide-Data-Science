Title:
French Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb beef roast
1 pkg beef gravy mix
1 pkg or can au jus mix
water to almost cover
sliced onion (optional)

Directions:
In crockpot cook until fork tender
High 4-6 hours
low 6-8 hours
shred with fork
place on hard rolls
dip in au jus
use cheese and onion as desired

Submitter: 
Michele Weyland
Description: 
Hearty spin on the original.
