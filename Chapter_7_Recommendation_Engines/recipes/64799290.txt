Title:
Lighter Skillet Mexican Street Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ears of fresh corn, husks and fibers removed
2 tbsp evoo
1 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp ground cumin
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tbsp non fat plain greek yogurt
1/4 cup crumbled cotija cheese, divided
1 lime
1 tbsp chopped cilantro

Directions:
Use a knife to remove the kernels of corn from the cob by running the knife down the cob. Heat the oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the corn kernels. Sprinkle the chili powder, cumin, salt,  pepper, yogurt, and 3 tbsp cotija cheese. Mix well. Stir in juice of 1/2 the lime. Continue to stir until the kernels are cooked through, about 8 minutes total.
Serve topped with the remaining cheese, remaining lime cut into wedges,and chopped cilantro.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Mexican street corn in a skillet version for those times when grilling is not an option. I made this version lighter by using lower fat and calorie ingredients. This recipe is super fast, so it can be whipped up at the last minute if necessary.
