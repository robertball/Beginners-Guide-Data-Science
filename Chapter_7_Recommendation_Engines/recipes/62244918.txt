Title:
No Bake Blueberry Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups graham wafer crumbs
1/2 cup melted butter
4 tbsp sugar
8 oz package cream cheese
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp lemon juice
2 tsp vanilla
1 L cool whip
2 to 3 cups blueberries

Directions:
combine graham wafer crumbs, butter and sugar.
Press in a 9x9 inch pan.
Bake at 250 F for 5 min.  Cool.
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until light and fluffy.
Add sugar and salt.  Continue to combine.
Mix in lemon juice and vanilla.
Fold in cool whip and blueberries.
Refrigerate several hours before serving!
