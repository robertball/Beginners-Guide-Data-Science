Title:
Creamy Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(3) Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
(1) Bag of Wide Yolk Free Noodles
(1/4 c.) Sour Cream
(1) 10 oz. Can of Cream of Celery Soup
(1) 26 oz. Can of Cream of Mushroom Soup
(1) 16 oz. Bag of Frozen Mixed Vegetables
(1) Large Can of Mushrooms (drained)
(1/2) White Onion - diced
(3 c. ) Sharp Cheddar Cheese (less if wanted) - divide

Directions:
Boil Chicken until cooked - then shred / cube
Boil Noodles until al dente
Mix Chicken, Noodles, Soups, 1 1/2c. Cheese and all vegetables in large baking dish.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Bake in oven at 350 for 45 minutes (depending on oven)

A delicious mix of vegetables, chicken, and cheese! A family pleaser.
