Title:
Double Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup shortening
1 1/2 cups light brown sugar
1 tablespoon water
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1/3 cup Hershey's Cocoa or Dutch Processed Cocoa
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups (12 oz. package) Hershey's Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 375 degrees
2. Beat shortening, brown sugar, water, and vanilla in a large bowl on medium speed of mixer until well blended. Add eggs and beat well. Stir together flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to sugar mixture, beating on low speed just until blended. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet.
3. Bake 7-9 minutes or until cookies are set. Cookies will appear soft and moist. Do not over bake. Cool 2 minutes, remove from cookie sheet to wire rack. Cool completely. Makes about 3 dozen cookies.
