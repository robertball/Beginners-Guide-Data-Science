Title:
Cheesiest Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 cup diced onion
1/2 cup carrots
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
2 1/2 cups peeled and diced potatoes
3 cups chicken broth
1 cup heavy cream OR use half & half & sour cream
1 3/4 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1/4 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, melt butter.  Cook onion in butter until softened.  Stir in potatoes, carrots, garlic and broth, bring to a boil, then cover, reduce heat and simmer 15 to 20 minutes, until potatoes are tender.
Puree 1/2 of the potato mixture in a blender or food processor or using an immersion blender; return to pot over medium heat.  Stir in cream, cheese, dill, pepper, salt and cayenne.  Bring to a low boil and cook, stirring, until thickened, 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Colleen Robertson Curtiss
Description: 
Cream and sharp Cheddar are added to pureed potatoes and the soup is seasoned with dill and cayenne for a creamy, thick meal.Modified from: Cheesiest Potato Soup
