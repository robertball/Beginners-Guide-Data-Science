Title:
Ginger Snaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2C     flour
1Tbsn  Ginger
2tspn  Baking Soda
1tspn  Cinnamon
1/2tspn Salt
3/4C   Shortening
1C     Sugar plus enough in a small bowl to roll cookies in
1      Egg
1/4C   Molasses

Directions:
Heat oven to 350
Cream shortening and sugar
Add egg and molasses and beat until smooth
Add dry ingredients and beat until a soft dough forms
Roll dough into balls about 1 tspn at a time and roll them in the sugar.
bake 12 to 15 minutes

This was my grandmothers recipe.  A good plain ginger snap
