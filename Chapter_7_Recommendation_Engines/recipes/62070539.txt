Title:
Rigatoni Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg cooked rigatoni pasta
1/2 pkg baby spinach
1/4 cup chopped green onions
grape tomatoes
1 can black olives
cooked, cubed chicken pieces
1/3 cup cashew halves
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
2 Tbsp Sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp Accent
1/4 cup olive oil
3 Tbsp Rice Vinegar

Directions:
1.  Cook, drain and chill pasta.
2.  Combine first eight ingredients in a large serving bowl and toss.
3.  In a small bowl combine remaining five ingredients. Mix well and let set for 15-20 minutes.
4. Pour dressing over salad and lightly toss.

Submitter: 
Penny Allen
Description: 
Every time I take this somewhere I get requests for the recipe.  It looks pretty and is easy to make. The hint of sweetness in the dressing gives in a unique flavor.
