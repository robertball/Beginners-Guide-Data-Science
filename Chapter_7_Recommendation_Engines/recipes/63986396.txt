Title:
Big Ray's Cauliflower Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1106053.jpg
Ingredients:
4 oz butter crackers (one sleeve)
1/2 cup butter
1 large head cauliflower
1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat your oven 350 degrees.
Crumble butter crackers into a small bowl and mash them with butter until soft. Place cauliflower into a 9x13 inch casserole dish.
In small saucepan, combine milk, sour cream, garlic, onion and mayonnaise with wire whisk. Bring the mixture to a boil; boil for 1 minute. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in cheese until melted. Pour this sauce over the cauliflower. Cover with foil and bake for 35 min.
Remove foil and crumble the butter and cracker mixture over the cheese and cauliflower. Bake for 30 min or until golden brown.
