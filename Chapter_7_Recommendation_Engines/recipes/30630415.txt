Title:
Roasted Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb asparagus
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Holding asparagus by stem end and halfway up the stalk, bend stalk to snap off woody end. Discard woody end. In a 9-inch square cake pan, gently toss asparagus with oil, pepper and salt; spread out evenly in pan. Bake in 350 degree F oven for 25 - 30 minutes.
Sprinkle with parmesan cheese; bake for another 2 to 5 minutes.
