Title:
Eggless Mayonaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tb sugar
1/2 tsp dry mustard
dash cayenne
3 Tbs cider vinegar
1/4 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 cup evaporated milk
1 cup oil

Directions:
Blend dry ingredients with milk in a blender until smooth.  Add vinegar and mix just a moment.  Add oil gradually and blend for about a minute.  Keeps about 1 month in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
bawkbagawk
Description: 
A wonderfully tasty spread for sandwiches, salads, or to substitute for mayonaise in recipies.
