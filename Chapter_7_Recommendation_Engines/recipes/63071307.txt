Title:
Robin's Macadamian and Coconut Caramels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1/4 tsp cream of tartar
3/4 cup sweet condensed milk
1/2 cup flaked coconut
1/2 cup macadamian nuts
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Line dish with foil and butter.
In saucepan, put sugar, syrup, cream of tartar and butter.  Bring to boil, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and add milk slowly.
Over low-medium heat, heat to 244 degrees.
Remove from heat and add coconut, nuts and vanilla.
Pour into pan and refrigerate 2 hours.
Remove foil from caramel and cut into 1 inch squares.  Wrap like taffy in wax paper pieces.
