Title:
Braised Balsamic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
ground black pepper to taste
1 teaspoon garlic salt
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 large onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme

Directions:
Season chicken breasts with ground black pepper and garlic salt. Heat olive oil in a medium skillet, and brown the onion and seasoned chicken breasts.
Mix tomatoes, balsamic vinegar, basil, oregano, rosemary and thyme together.  Pour over chicken. Simmer until chicken is no longer pink and the juices run clear, about 15 minutes.
