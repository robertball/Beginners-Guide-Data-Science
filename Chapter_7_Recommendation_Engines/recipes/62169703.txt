Title:
Three ingredient Peanut Butter fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz jar peanut butter (creamy or chunky, any brand)
1 jar whipped chocolate frosting
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Empty entire contents of the jar of peanut butter into a medium sized bowl.  Stir in entire contents of container of whipped frosting. Add vanilla, stir until blended. Spred into buttered 8x8 square pan. Refrigerate for 3 hours or freeze for 1 hour. Cut into squares. Serve.
*For easier mixing, melt frosting slightly in microwave before mixing with peanut butter. Vanilla or White Chocolate frosting works well, too.
