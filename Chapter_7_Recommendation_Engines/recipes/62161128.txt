Title:
meatloaf cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of medium grade ground beef
1/3 cup of milk
1 cup crushed saltine crackers
1 teaspoon of seasoned salt
1 teaspoon of pepper
2 cloves of garlic chopped
1/2 cup of chopped onions
1/2 cup of chopped green pepper
1 tablespoon of worcershire sauce
1 egg
1/2 cup of ketchup
1/2 cup of brown sugar
4 cups of mashed potatoes
1 cup of shredded cheddar

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
mix all ingredients except ketchup,brown sugar,mashed potatoes and cheese
use a muffin pan with 6 slots
mix your ketchup and brown sugar together and put into the bottom of each slot about a tablespoon of the ketchup mixture
add enough meat to the slots to where you have 1/2 inch space remaining on top
cook in preheated oven for 30minutes or until no longer pink in the center
take the pan out and drain the fat
top each cupcake with your mashed potatoes and a tablespoon of shredded cheddar for each mini meatloaf
put back in the oven for aproxamatly 10 minutes til the cheese melts...

Submitter: 
Karen Steinke Reilly
Description: 
these look like a little cupcake because with the meat inside of a muffin pan and mashed potato icing.my kids eat these up quick.
