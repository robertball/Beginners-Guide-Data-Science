Title:
Chocolate Coca Cola Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups unsifted flour
2 cups sugar
2 sticks margarine
2 Tbsp. cocoa
1 cup Coca Cola
1-1/2 cups miniature marshmallows
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 tsp. soda
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Combine flour and sugar in a bowl.  Heat margarine, cocoa and Coco Cola to boiling in a saucepan.  Pour over flour mixture.  Add remaining ingredients; mix well.  Pour into greased 9"x13" pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
Coca Cola Icing:
1/2 cup margarine
3 Tbsp. Cocoa
6 Tbsp. Coca Cola
1 lb powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup chopped nuts
Bring first 3 ingredients to a boil.  Pour over powdered sugar in a mixing bowl; mix well.  Add vanilla and nuts.  Pour over warm cake.
