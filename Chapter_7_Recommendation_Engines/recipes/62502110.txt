Title:
Curried Butternut Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/500336.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown rice
2 cups frozen, cubed butternut squash, cooked
1 cup frozen peas, cooked
1/2 cup diced onion
1 medium carrot, shredded
2 garlic cloves, pressed
1 (15.5 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
1/4 cup coconut milk
3 tablespoons tamari
1/2 teaspoon ground curry powder, (or turmeric)

Directions:
In a saucepan, bring 1 1/8 cups water to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 40 minutes.
Meanwhile, heat the frozen peas and squash according to package instructions.
Dice and saute the onion.  Shred the carrot and add it and the pressed garlic to the onion, once onion is almost finished cooking.
Mix coconut milk with tamari and curry or turmeric powder.  Combine all ingredients in saute pan, and let cook over low about 10 minutes- until flavors are combined.  Enjoy!
