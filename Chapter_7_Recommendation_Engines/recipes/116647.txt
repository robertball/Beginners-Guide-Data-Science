Title:
Feta Cheese Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993183.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, cubed
4 cloves garlic, peeled
1 (4 ounce) package crumbled feta cheese
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Place the potatoes and garlic in a large saucepan; cover with water. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce heat; cover and cook for 15-20 minutes or until tender. Drain and transfer to a mixing bowl; mash. Add the feta cheese, cream, salt and pepper; beat until fluffy.

Submitter: 
Jan Stone
Description: 
Jan Stone of Cincinnati, Ohio jazzes up her mashed potatoes with feta cheese and garlic. I dont peel the potatoes, because my family likes the texture better. But feel free to peel them if desired, she suggests.
