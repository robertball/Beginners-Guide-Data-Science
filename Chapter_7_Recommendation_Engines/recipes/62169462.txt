Title:
Perfect Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Shortening
1 Cup Butter
8 Cups Powdered Sugar
1/2 teaspoon Salt
2 teaspoons clear imitation vanilla extract
6 fluid ounces heavy cream (milk can be used as substitute)

Directions:
1. Cream shortening and butter until smooth. Add powdered sugar and continue mixing until well blended.
2. Add salt, vanilla, and whipping cream and mix on low speed until moistened. Beat on high speed until frosting is fluffy.

Submitter: 
thechulita
Description: 
After researching several buttercream icing recipes, this seems to be to best combination of ingrediants for me. Keep in mind that this is for medium consistancy icing. More powdered sugar will be needed for stiff consistancy for piping things like roses, etc.
