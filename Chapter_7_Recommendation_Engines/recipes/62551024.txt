Title:
Cinnamon Honey Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1/2 cup honey
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine butter, confectioners' sugar honey and cinnamon. Beat until light and fluffy.

Submitter: 
Dan Rasmussen
Description: 
This is a creamy honey-cinnamon treat, and is absolutely wonderful on toast, bagels, muffins, pancakes, waffles.Modified from: Cinnamon Honey Butter
