Title:
Butter Dipped Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine
2 tsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. worcesterchire sauce
2 cloves chopped garlic (I use the kind in a jar)
3/4 cup buttery crackers, crushed
1/2 cup parmesan cheese, powdery kind
parsley flakes, to taste
1.5 lbs. chicken tenders
salt & pepper, to taste

Directions:
Heat the butter, lemon juice, worcestershire sauce, garlic and salt & pepper, to taste, in the microwave until just blended.  Mix the cracker crumbs, parmesan cheese & parsley together and set aside.  Dip the chicken pieces in the butter mixture, coating well, and then roll the chicken pieces, covering completely, in the crumb mixture.  Place chicken in appropriate size pan for amount of chicken and  cover and bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees; uncover and continue to bake for an additional 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Shults3
Description: 
Chicken tenders dipped in flavored butter and rolled in cracker crumbs and parmesan cheese.  This is great served either with pasta or mashed potatoes.
