Title:
5 Cheese Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. frozen shredded hash browns
1/2 stick butter or margarine melted
1tsp. pepper
1/4 Cup chopped onion
2 cans cream od chicken soup
8 oz soour crream
1 Cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 Cup shredded montery jack cheese
1/2 Cup parmesan cheese
Topping:
1Cup crushed corn flakes
3 Tbsp. butter or margarine melted

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except for the topping in a large bowl. Spread the mixture in a 9x13 pan.
Combine the cornflakes and melted butter for the topping. Spoon the topping mixture over the top of the casserole.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Ellen Hubenett
Description: 
This is a must at all our family dinners. I got the original recipe from my grandma and have tweaked it over the years to suit my family's tastes.
