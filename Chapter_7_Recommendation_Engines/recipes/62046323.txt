Title:
Three cheese Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces egg noodles
4 tablespoons butter divided
1 tablespoon flour
1/2 cup milk
salt and pepper
1/3 cup sheredded cheddar cheese
1/3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup fresh parmesan cheese shredded
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Cook pasta and drain. Toss with 2 tablespoons butter. In saucepan melt remaining butter, whisk in flour till smooth. Stir in milk whisk until thickens. Stir in salt, pepper and cheeses stir till smooth. Add parsley and toss with noodles.
