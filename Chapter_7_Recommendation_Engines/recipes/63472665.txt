Title:
Grilled Split Chicken with Rosemary and Garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole 3 pound chicken
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
2 garlic cloves

Directions:
Cut chicken in half lengthwise along backbone, cutting to, but not through other side.
Combine buttermilk and remaining ingredients pour over chicken. Marinate for 24 hours.
Grill with indirect heat.
