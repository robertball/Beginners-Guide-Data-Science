Title:
Kay's Stimac's Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
bag med or large shrimp raw
green onion
cream cheese
sour cream
garlic powder
onion salt
cayenne pepper
salt as needed
celery small diced

Directions:
Clean & cook Shrimp in tiny bit of olive oil cook till just pink, put in food processor till coarse. Add 1/4 c lemon juice,  mix minced onion, garlic powder onion, cream cheese & sour cream. mix throughly. add red pepper to taste. Refrigerate till ingredients are melded
