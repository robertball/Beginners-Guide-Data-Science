Title:
Breakfast Cake II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup shortening
2 eggs
1 tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
3/4 cup milk
2 cups flour
3 tsp baking powder
Topping: 2-3 Tbsp butter or margarine
1/2 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs and beat until light and fluffy. Add sifted dry ingredients alternately with milk, beating until smooth after each addition. Pour into a 9" square pan and sprinkle with butter and brown sugar mixture on top. Bake at 350 for about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
J
Description: 
A nice cinnamon/brown sugary cake delicious for breakfast. Place a slice into a bowl and pour milk or cream over top.
