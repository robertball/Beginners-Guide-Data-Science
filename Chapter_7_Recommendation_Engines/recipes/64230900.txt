Title:
Joyce's coconut cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042771.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg white cake
2 cups sugar
1cup milk
11/2 tsp coconut flavoring
i container of cool whip
1 cup coconut

Directions:
Bake cake mix as directed in a 9x13 pan
Boil sugar, milk, coconut flavoring.
Punch  holes in cake while hot
Pour hot milk mixture over cake
cool and spread cool whip and sprinkle
with coconut
