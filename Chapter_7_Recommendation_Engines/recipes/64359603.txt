Title:
Tomato basil chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 full or 4 split boneless  chicken breasts
1can tomato soup
1 Tbs Worschestershire sauce
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1 Tbs dried basil or 3-5 fresh basi leaves, snipped
3 Tbs unsalted butter
3 cloves crushed garlic
1 small yellow onion, diced
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saut√©e onion and garlic in butter until carmalized.
Remove onion and set aside.
Add chicken and brown on all sides over medium heat.
Mix tomato soup, vinegar and Worschestershire sauce.
Return onion and garlic to pan, add basil, sauce mix, salt and pepper.
Cover and simmer over low heat at least 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through
and tender.
Remove lid and reduce to desired consistency.
