Title:
French Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984527.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package frozen French-style green beans
1 (4.5 ounce) jar sliced mushrooms, drained
3 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
toasted slivered almonds

Directions:
Cook green beans according to package directions; drain.
Add mushrooms and keep warm. Combine butter, rosemary and basil; drizzle over bean mixture and toss to coat. Sprinkle with almonds.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home
Description: 
Frozen green beans get tasty herb flavor from rosemary and basil while toasted almonds add crunch.
