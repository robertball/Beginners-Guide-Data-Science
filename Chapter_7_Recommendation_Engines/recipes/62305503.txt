Title:
PASSOVER FUDGE PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz choc chips (german sweet choc)
1 cup marg
2 cups sugar
6 Tbl cake flour
1/2 tsp salt
6 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp almond extract
2 cups chopped nuts

Directions:
Melt chocolate & margarine together. Let cool. Add vanilla & almond flavors
Mix dry ingredients together. Add to beaten eggs
fold in chocolate mixture & nuts. Pour into (3) 8" foil pans or (2) 9" glass pie pans
Bake 350 for 45 min. DO NOT OVERBEAT ANY MIXTURE OR OVERBAKE111
