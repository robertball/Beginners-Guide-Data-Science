Title:
Big Ray's Baked Muffin Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7192261.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large eggs
1 1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup applesauce
2 Tbsp butter, melted
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup maple syrup
2 1/2 cup old fashioned oatmeal
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup raisins
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
1/4 cup chia seeds
1/4 cup flax seeds

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray muffin pan with cooking spray. In a large bowl mix all ingredients until combined.  Fill cups 3/4 fill with mixture.  Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.  Remove to wire rack to cool.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
These are nice to have on hand in the fridge or freezer, to grab a quick snack or healthy breakfast. You can change your spice and/or additions to match your tastes, I will sometimes use pumpkin spice for the cinnamon, chopped. pecan or walnuts for the sunflower seeds
