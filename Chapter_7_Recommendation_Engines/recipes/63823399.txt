Title:
Eggplant & Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060681.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small eggplant
2 small, firm tomatoes
2 teaspoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon oregano
Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Peel eggplant and remove ends.
Slice eggplant and tomatoes into 1/2 inch slices (about 12 each).
Sprinkle sugar over tomatoes.
Mix flour, salt, and oregano in small bowl.
Dip slices of eggplant and tomatoes in flour mixture.
In medium frying pan, heat butter to cover bottom.  Fry eggplant slices on both sides until lightly browned (about 2 minutes each side).  Remove to platter, keep warm.
Add more butter if needed.  Fry tomatoes about 1 minutes on each side.  Place one tomato slice on each eggplant slice.
Add parmesan cheese if desired.

Description: 
This is a quick, easy, and healthy eggplant side or small lunch.
