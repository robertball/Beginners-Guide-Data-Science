Title:
Easy Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 6.5 oz cans clams (Chopped, minced or whole, depending on your preference)
1 8 oz. bottle clam juice
4 cups chicken broth
1/2 bag Publix southern style hash browns
1/2 bag Birdseye frozed sweet corn
1/2 bag Publix season blend (chopped onions, celery, green peppers and parsley)
1/3 to 1/2 cup bacon bits
2 cups half and half
1/2/ cup white wine (may be omitted)

Directions:
Add 4 cups of chicken broth to a large kettle
Add the bottle of clam juice
Add the juice from the can of clams and reserve the clams for later
Add white wine
Bring to a boil
Add potatoes, corn, bacon and season blend
Return the soup to a boil
Lower heat to a simmer for 5 10 8 minutes, until the potatoes are done
Add half and half and clams
Add salt and pepper to taste
Bring back to simmer
Serve

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Fast and delicious, a hearty soup perfect for winter evenings.  No chopping, dicing, or prep work, just a can opener!
