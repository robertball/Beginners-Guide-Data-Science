Title:
Roasted Butternut Squash Soup With Brie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lge. or 2 sm butternut squash
1 carrot
1 leek, white part only
1 medium onion
2 tbsp. butter
8 cups chicken OR vegetable stock
1 apple, peeled and chopped
1 bay leaf
salt and pepper
1-8oz. pkg. Brie Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350(F).  Cut squash in half, lengthwise, and remove seeds.  Place cut side down on baking sheet and bake until tender(about 45 minutes).
Chop carrot, onion, and leek into 1 inch pieces; place in a large pot.  Gently saute in butter(do not braown).  Scrape flesh from cooked squash and add to vegetables.  Add stock and bring to a boil.
Add apple and bay leaf to stock mixture; simmer, uncovered for 40 minutes.  Remove bay leaf and puree soup in batches using a blender.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Slice off outer skin of Brie and cut into 1/2 inch pieces.  Place cheese in bottoms of the soup bowls, and fill with hot soup.  Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
This sweet and savoury soup is autumn comfort food at its best.  Every bite has a swirl of melted brie cheese in it for added flavour and interest.  Add a loaf of crusty bread, and a salad, and you have a delicous meal on a cold day!
