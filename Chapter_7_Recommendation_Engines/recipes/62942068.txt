Title:
Syrniki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/722344.jpg
Ingredients:
1kg quark cheese / tvorog / cottage cheese - drained, no added sugar
2 eggs
4 tbs sugar
2/3 cup flour
2 pinches of salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp vanilla essence
40g raisins (optional)
30g flour
Oil for frying

Directions:
1. Grate/push the cheese through a sieve or meat grinder to get an extremely fine almost powdery conisistency
2. Add sifted flour, salt, sugar, vanilla, and eggs (and raisins if you chose to use them). Mix well.
3. Take approximately 2 tbs worth of the mixture, roll it into a ball and dip it into the extra flour
4. Make a semi-circle with your index finger and thumb, and form a patty from the mixture exactly this size. Square off the edges. Continue until the misture is used up.
5. Heat about 20ml oil in a skillet over a medium-low heat. Once oil is heated, begin frying the patties.
6. Once the patties form a crisp, dark brown shell below, turn them over, put the heat on low, cover them and fry another 3-4 minutes or until a shell has formed
7. Repeat process for all patties
8. Serve with jam or sour cream

Russian breakfast/dessert. Fried quark cheese patties. Crisp outside and warm and creamy inside.
