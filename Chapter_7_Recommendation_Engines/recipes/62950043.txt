Title:
Crock pot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs.hamberger
8 potatoes,cubed
2 onions,chopped
1 cup celery,chopped
20 ounces frozen peas
2 tsp.salt
1/2 tsp.pepper
1/2 tsp.garlic powder
20 ounces condensed tomato soup
1 Tbsp.beef bouillon
1 1/2 cup water

Directions:
Fry hamberger,drain well.
Add vegetables and spices.Stir well.
Cook on low for 8-10 hours.
