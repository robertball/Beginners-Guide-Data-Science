Title:
Dirty Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6063425.jpg
Ingredients:
5 pounds potatoes washed with skins removing any bad spots
1 head garlic peeled
1 quart sour cream
Lawry's Seasoning salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
In large cooking pot place sliced potatoes with peels on and peeled garlic cloves
Cover with water and bring to a boil lower temperature and cover cooking for 25 minutes
Test potatoes with fork making sure they are soft
Drain potatoes add Lawry's seasoning salt and pepper and sour cream and mash until smooth and not lumpy
Test to make sure you have it to taste and serve

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
I have never understood why most people peel their potatoes for mashing thats where all the vitamins and minerals are in the potato.  The garlic and sour cream make these mashed potatoes a star!
