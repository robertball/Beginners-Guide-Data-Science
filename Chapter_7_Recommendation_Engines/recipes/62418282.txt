Title:
Carbanara with Saffron Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salt and pepper
1 pd linquine or egg noodles
1 1/2C chicken stock
1/4tsp saffron(2pinches)
3Tbsp evoo
1/3 pd thick cut pancetta(cut in 1/4 inch dice pieces)
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1/2C dry white wine
2tsp turmeric
3 egg yolks
2/3C grated ramano cheese plus more for the table
A generous handful of chopped parsley for garnish

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil, salt it add the pasta and cook until al dente. Drain, reserving a ladleful of pasta cooking water.
In a small saucepan, bring the stock and saffron to a boil over medium-high heat. Lower the heat and simmer until reduced.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat the evoo, over medium heat. Add the pancetta and cook until crisp, about 5min. Stir in the garlic for 1-2min, then stir in the wine and turmeric.
Beat the egg yolks with the reserved pasta cooking water. Add the pasta to the skillet. Pour the saffron stock and toss; season with pepper. Turn off the heat and stir in the egg yolks, cheese and parsley. Pass extra cheese at the table!
