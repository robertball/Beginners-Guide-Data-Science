Title:
Soft Peanut Brittle2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. sugar
1/4 c. water
1 1/2 c. light corn syrup
2 c. salted peanuts
2- 2 1/2 c. peanut butter, warmed
1/2 t. vanilla
1 1/2 t. baking soda

Directions:
Combine sugar and water in heavy saucepan. Bring mixture to full rolling boil over high heat, stirring constantly. Stir in corn syrup. Cook to hard crack stage, 300 degrees.
Meanwhile, mix peanuts, peanut butter, soda and vanilla. Remove syrup from heat; at once add peanut butter mixture and stir. Working quickly, pour onto warmed buttered cookie sheet; spread with fork. Cool; break into pieces.
