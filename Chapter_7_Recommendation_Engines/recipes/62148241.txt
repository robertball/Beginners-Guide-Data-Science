Title:
scalloped sweet corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1/4 cup green pepper, chopped
2 T. butter
2 T. flour
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
1 tsp. salt
1 1/2 c. milk
2 cups corn, fresh or frozen (thawed)
1 slightly beaten egg
3/4 cup shredded sharp cheddar
1/2 cup crushed buttery crackers
1/2 cup bread crumbs mixed with 2 T butter

Directions:
Saute onion and green pepper in 2 T butter until soft. Blend in, and cook until bubbly, the flour, paprika, salt, and mustard. Remove from heat. Add milk gradually with a whisk. Return to heat, and bring to a boil and cook one minute, stirring constantly. Remove from heat, add corn, egg, cheese and crackers. Pour into small baking dish, top with buttered bread crumbs. Bake 375 degrees 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Christina J Dykstra Goetzke
Description: 
I came up with this recipe after trying to find one that uses fresh or frozen corn. All the ones I saw used canned corn, and we freeze our own sweetcorn every year. It is very delicious and buttery.
