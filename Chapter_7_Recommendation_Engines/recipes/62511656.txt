Title:
Chocolate Chip/Walnut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507246.jpg
Ingredients:
•1 cup (2 sticks) butter or margarine , softened
•1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
•1 cup granulated sugar
•2 eggs
•2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
•2 cups all-purpose flour
•1 teaspoon baking powder
•1 teaspoon baking soda
•3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
•1/2 teaspoon salt
•1-1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
•1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ºF.
In large bowl, cream butter and sugars with electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla; mix well. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon and salt; mix well. Stir in chocolate chips and, if desired, nuts. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto cookie sheet.
Bake 9-10 minutes or until edges are golden brown. Cool on pan on wire rack 5 minutes; remove from cookie sheet and cool completely
