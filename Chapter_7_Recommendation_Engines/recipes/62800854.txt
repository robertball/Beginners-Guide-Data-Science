Title:
Spinach & mandarine orange salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of spinach
1 cup dates
1 can mandarine oranges
1/2 red onion thinly sliced(or more to taste)
1/4 c. olive oil
2 tbl. lemon juice

Directions:
Put spinach in bowl. Add mandarine oranges and dates. Mix olive oil and lemon juice. Toss together and top with onions.
