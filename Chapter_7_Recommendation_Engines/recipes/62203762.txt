Title:
Cinnamon Spice Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter, softened at room temperature
2/3 cup confectioners' sugar, sifted
2 large eggs, at room temperature
1/8 teaspoon (Pinch) salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon each, ground cardamom, ground ginger, and ground nutmeg
2 teaspoons cinnamon
4 tablespoons of granulated sugar
2 tablespoons cinnamon

Directions:
In a mixer or with a hand mixer in a large bowl,on medium,beat butter until smooth.
Add confectioners' sugar and extract and beat until smooth and well combined.
Add the eggs and the salt, blend until combined.
Reduce speed to low and blend flour and 2 teaspoons cinnamon and other spices until combined.
with a spatula, mix in any remaining flour.
Form dough into 1 inch to 1 1/4 inch thick logs and wrap with plastic or waxed paper.
Chill dough in the fridge for at least 2 hours
Preheat oven and line two baking sheets with parchment paper. Slice log into 46-50 cookies, place on baking sheets leaving 1/2 inch space between each cookie.
Bake cookies for 12 to 14 minutes until set but not browned. In a bowl, or on a plate combine remaining cinnamon and sugar and roll warm cookies into cinnamon sugar until cookies are coated.
Transfer cookies to cooling racks and cool to room temperature.

Submitter: 
Kimmi
Description: 
These cookies are like Snicker-doodles but with a twist. Spiced cookies are perfect for the winter holidays.
