Title:
Pumpkin Cookies II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups canned pumpkin
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
4 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Cream shortening and sugar, then add   the eggs.  Mix well.  Add vanilla and   pumpkin.  Set aside.
Sift dry ingredients, then blend into the &#34;wet&#34; ingredients.  Fold in the nuts and  raisins.  Mix well.
Drop by heaping teaspoons onto a greased cookie sheet, bake for 10 - 15 minutes.  Cool.  Best eaten if you let them &#34;ripen&#34; for a day.  Store in a sealed  container.
