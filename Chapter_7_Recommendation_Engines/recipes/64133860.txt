Title:
Tomato Soup with Chicken and Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1343021.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
1 onion, diced
2 cups diced cauliflower florets
1 large chicken breast
2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
1 28-ounce can crushed tomatoes
2 cups chicken broth
1-2 cups water
1 Tablespoon sugar
Salt to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  Saute onion and cauliflower until onions are transparent.
Cut chicken breast into 1-inch pieces and add to the pan.  Add Italian seasoning and continue sauteing until chicken is cooked through.
Pour in crushed tomatoes, broth, and water.  Add sugar.  Keep pan on burner until liquids are heated through.  Taste soup and modify water and salt amounts as necessary.

Submitter: 
Mrs.Morgan
Description: 
The added chicken makes this soup a hearty upgrade from regular tomato soup.  The cauliflower doesn't stand out, making it a great way to sneak in some veggies without compromising a good comfort food.  Serve with grilled cheese or cheese quesadillas to make the meal just right!
