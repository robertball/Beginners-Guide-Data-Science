Title:
Mini Apricot Cheesecakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980537.jpg
Ingredients:
24 vanilla wafer cookies
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup apricot preserves

Directions:
Place wafers flat side down in paper-or foil-lined muffin cups; set aside.  In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.  Add the eggs, lemon juice and vanilla; beat well.  Fill muffin cups three-fourths full.  Place on a baking sheet.
Bake at 375 degrees F for 17-20 minutes or until top is set.  Cool on a wire rack for 20 minutes.  Top each cheesecake with 2 teaspoons preserves.  Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Carol  Twardzik
Description: 
In Spy Hill, Saskatchewan, reader Carol Twardzik relies on vanilla wafers to create the no-fuss crusts for these darling individual desserts. For a different look and taste, vary the kind of jam that tops these tempting treats.
