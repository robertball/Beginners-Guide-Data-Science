Title:
Homestyle Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006236.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 lb. steam in bag potatoes such as Simply Spuds™Steamables™Golden Potatoes
4 hard boiled eggs chopped
2/3 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped green onion (one bunch)
Dressing:
1/4 cup mayonnaise such as Hellmann's®Mayonnaise
1/4 cup creamy sald dressing such as Miracle Whip® Salad Dressing
2 Tbsp. sour cream (optional)
2 tsp. dijon mustard
1 tsp. yellow mustard
1/4 tsp. white wine vinegar
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. of celery salt
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Prepare Simply Spuds(TM)Steamables(TM)Golden potatoes according to directions on back of package.
Allow potatoes to cool.
Peel and cut potatoes into cubes.
In a large combine potatoes, hard boiled eggs, celery and green onion.
In a medium sized bowl prepare the dressing by combining Hellmann's Mayonnaise(R), Miracle Whip(R) Salad Dressing, sour cream (optional), dijon mustard, yellow mustard, white wine vinegar, garlic powder, celery salt, salt and pepper.
Gently fold dressing into potato salad until fully incorporated.
Cover and chill for 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Jillian
Description: 
Potato salad is an all time classic picnic food and a great addition to any barbeque. This recipe is simple and easy to prepare making it a staple for all our backyard get togethers!
