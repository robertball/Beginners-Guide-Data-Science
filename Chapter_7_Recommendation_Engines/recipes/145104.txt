Title:
Peanuts & Cracker Jacks Rafts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977949.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head Tanimura & Antle® Iceberg Lettuce
1 cup Cracker Jacks® or caramel-coated popcorn & peanuts
1 (11 ounce) can mandarin orange segments, drained
1/4 cup salted peanuts
1/2 cup poppy seed dressing

Directions:
Core lettuce; rinse and drain well. Cut crosswise into 4 even slices.
Top with Cracker Jacks, orange segments and peanuts. Drizzle with dressing.

Submitter: 
Tanimura & Antle®
Description: 
Iceberg lettuce provides a crisp base to this fun, sweet-and-salty salad.
