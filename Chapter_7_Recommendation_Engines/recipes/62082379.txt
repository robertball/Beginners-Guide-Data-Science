Title:
Moorish Style Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 pounds lean pork filet
1 teaspoon ground cumin
2 tablespoons paprika
5 tablespoons olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut pork into cubes- about 3/4 of an inch square. Season pork cubes with salt and pepper. In large bowl, combine cumin, paprkia, and olive oil. Add meat and mix well. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour. Preheat broiler. Thread 3-4 cubes tightly onto short wooden skewers and broil for 12 minutes, turning and basting with marinade frequently.
