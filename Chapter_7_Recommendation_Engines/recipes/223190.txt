Title:
Mock Caprese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/860789.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fat-free cottage cheese
2 tomatoes, chopped
1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil leaves, or to taste
1/2 teaspoon salt
freshly ground black pepper to taste
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar (optional)

Directions:
Combine cottage cheese, tomatoes, basil, salt, and black pepper in a bowl. Stir in balsamic vinegar.

Submitter: 
Denise Kasz Willmarth
Description: 
Fresh mozzarella is expensive. I love caprese and came up with this. I use fat-free cottage cheese to make it really low calorie.
