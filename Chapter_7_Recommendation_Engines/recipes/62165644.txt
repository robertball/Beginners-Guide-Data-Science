Title:
Cheesiest Cheddar Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Butter, room temperature
2 Cups Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1 1/2 Cups All Purpose Flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 - 2 tablespoons water (if needed)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Using a mixer combine all ingrediants until thoroughly combined.  Mash dough together with hand to see if it comes together.  If it is too dry add water as needed.Remove from mixer and roll into a ball by hand.
On a lightly floured surface roll dough to 1/8" thin. If dough is difficult to roll chill in fridge for 15 minutes.
Using a pizza cutter cut dough into square or rectangles.  Repeat until all dough is used.
Place squares on large cookie sheet covered with either parchment paper or a silicone baking sheet.
Bake for 10 - 12 minutes or until lightly brown around edges.
Transfer to baking sheet and allow to cool.
Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
Forget the little dried up crackers that taste like cardboard in the grocery store.  You can make the cheesiest crackers with ease using a few simple ingrediants.
