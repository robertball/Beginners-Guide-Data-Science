Title:
Country Sausage Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1034662.jpg
Ingredients:
1	16oz. roll Jimmy Dean breakfast sausage (not maple)
4	tablespoons up to Â¼ cup flour
2	teaspoons ground sage (to taste)
3 	cups 2% milk
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1.	In a skillet or Dutch oven  cook and crumble sausage.  When cooked through stir In flour, you are going to want to add  flour until the grease on the bottom of the pan is all soaked up.  It will be a bit pasty.  Cook stirring about 30 seconds.  Add salt pepper and sage.
2.	Add about half of the milk stirring constantly.  Once it starts to thicken quite a bit add the remainder.  Keep stirring.  If it gets too thick add more milk or a little water.  Making sure to stir and cook at least 2 to 3 minutes after each addition to check consistency.  Then taste and adjust seasonings.
