Title:
Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all purpose flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 lbs. beef round steak 1" thick
2 Tbl. sp. shortening
1 8oz. can tomatos
1 minced onion
1/2 green pepper chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Stir together, flour, salt and pepper. Sprinkle one side of the meat with half of the mixture; and pound in, turn over and repeat on the other side.
Cut the meat into 6 serving pieces.
Melt the shortening in a large skillet; brown meat over med. heat, about 15 minutes. Cover tightly; simmer for 1 hour, adding water ( or beef broth) as needed.
Turn meat and add the remaining ingredients and simmer for an additional Â½ hr.
