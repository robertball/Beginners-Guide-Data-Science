Title:
Crock Pot Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 cans cherry, apple, or peach pie filling
1 box yellow cake mix
1 1/2 stick (3/4 c) butter
1/2 c brown sugar
1 c quick oats
1 c walnuts

Directions:
Pour pie filling into crock pot. Mix cake mix, butter, brown sugar, oats, and walnuts. Put cake mix mixture over pie filling. Cover, cook on low 3-4 hours.
