Title:
bob's slow cooker baked beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups great northern beans (drained)
1/2 lb. ground chuck
1 small onion (diced)
2 cups canned tomatoes crushed (1 pint)
1 1/4 cup ketchup (10 oz.)
1/2 cup packed brown sugar

Directions:
in a large pan brown and crumble ground chuck until done and drain.
add the rest of the ingredients and stir to combine.
transfer to a crock pot, cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours.
or you can transfer to a casserole dish and bake covered at 350 degrees for 2 hours. (I prefer the crock pot method)
