Title:
Pizza Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990091.jpg
Ingredients:
1 envelope Lipton® Recipe Secrets® Beefy Onion Soup Mix
2 pounds ground beef
1 1/2 cups fresh bread crumbs
2 eggs
1 small green bell pepper, chopped (optional)
1/4 cup water
1 cup RAGÚ® Old World Style® Traditional Sauce
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Combine all ingredients except 1/2 cup Pasta Sauce and 1/2 cup cheese in large bowl.
Shape into loaf in 13 x 9-inch baking or roasting pan. Top with remaining 1/2 cup Sauce. Bake 50 minutes. Sprinkle top with remaining 1/2 cup cheese. Bake an additional 10 minutes or until done. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Ragu®
Description: 
Enjoy the delicious flavors of pizza in a delicious meatloaf.
