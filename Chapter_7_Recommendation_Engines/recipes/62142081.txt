Title:
Holiday Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 beef brisket(3 to 4 pounds)
1 can (14-1/2 ounces) beef broth
1/3 cup chopped cranberries (optional)
1 can (16 ounces) whole-berry cranberry sauce
1 cup cranberry juice
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed
1 thinly sliced, large white onion,
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
1. In a bowl, combine flour and beef broth until smooth.
2. Stir  cranberry sauce, cranberry juice, garlic and rosemary into the bowl with the broth mixture and mix well.
3. Transfer mixture into a large roasting pan and top with the onion slices.
4.Season brisket with the salt and pepper; place fat side up in the pan. Cover and bake at 350Â° for 3 to 3-1/2 hours or until meat is tender, basting occasionally.
5. Remove brisket to a serving platter and let stand for 15 minutes. Thinly slice meat across the grain; serve with onion and pan juices. Top with warmed chopped cranberrys.

Submitter: 
Christy Lane
Description: 
This brisket recipe dresses the beef brisket up in a whole new way just ready for Thanksgiving and/or Christmas. It is a great alternative to the traditional turkey and ham or an additional meat option for those holiday guests.
