Title:
Orange Rosemary Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup + 2 Tablespoon  orange juice
3 Tablespoon olive oil
3 Tablespoon chopped fresh chives
2 Tablespoons+ 3/4 teaspoon  chopped fresh rosemary
3/4  teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 chicken breasts or 1 pkg
triple sec
cornstartch

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix the orange juice,orange peel olive oil, chives, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Place the chicken in the mixture. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator at least 4 hours.
Note: if using dried rosemary, strain the marinade
Arrange the chicken on grill.    Turn and brush frequently with the remaining marinade mixture, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
Boil the left over marinade, add a splash of tripple sec and 1 teaspoon of cornstarch in a bit of water.
Serve as a sauce if desired

Submitter: 
jeis2990
Description: 
It's fabulous hot or cold. I make a big batch so there are leftovers (which make tasty quesadillas). I move my oven rack to a lower setting to keep the chicken moist, and even then it barely takes 30 minutes.Modified from: Orange Rosemary Chicken
