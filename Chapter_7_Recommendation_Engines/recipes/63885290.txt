Title:
Easy Sausage & Cheese Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077958.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. sausage
4 c cheddar cheese
3 c baking mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
In a med. bowl, combine the sausage, cheese & dry baking mix. Mix together, & shape mixture into walnut-sized balls. Place on a foil-lined cookie sheet.
Bake at 400 for 20 mins.

Submitter:

Description: 
They go great with some grape jelly bbq sauce.
