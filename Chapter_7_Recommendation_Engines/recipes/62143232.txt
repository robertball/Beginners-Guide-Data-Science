Title:
Cornbread Cranberry Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg Jimmy Dean sausage (one hot, one regular)
16 oz. cornbread
2 cups chicken broth heated
2 cups celery
1/2 cup butter, melted
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 chopped onion
1 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
Brown sausage, drain. Crumble with fork. Combine remaining ingredients, mixing well. Pour into a 9" x 13" greased pan and bake covered at 325 for 30 min.
