Title:
BBQ Bacon Cheese Dog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 OSCAR MAYER Beef Frank or Wiener
2 slices OSCAR MAYER Ready To Serve Bacon
1 tablespoon KRAFT Cheddar CLASSIC MELTS Cheddar & American Shredded Cheese
1 hot dog bun
1 tablespoon KRAFT Original or THICK 'N SPICY Spicy Cajun Barbecue Sauce
1 tablespoon chopped onion (optional)

Directions:
Place frank, bacon and cheese in bun; wrap in paper towel.
Microwave on HIGH 30 to 45 seconds.
Top with barbecue sauce and onion.
