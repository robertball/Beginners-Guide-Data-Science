Title:
Best Ever Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can cut green beans
1 small white onion, chopped
1 small can sliced mushrooms
3 strips uncooked bacon, cut up
1/4 cup slivered almonds, toasted
1/4 teaspoon Liquid Smoke (optional)
3 cubes beef bullion

Directions:
Pour undrained beans into large saucepan.
Add bullion, bacon, mushrooms, onions, and smoke flavoring.
Heat to slow boil for two minutes, then simmer for for 30 minutes, stirring occasionaly.
Toast almonds under broiler for 3-5 minutes.
Just before serving, stir the almonds into the beans.

Submitter: 
KerryT
Description: 
I serve these beans year-round and always get compliments for them. Flavorful and so easy!
