Title:
My grandma's southern style meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground sirloin or ground chuck
1 small yellow onion (chopped)
1/2 cup green pepper (chopped)
1 egg
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup ketchup
salt and pepper to taste
Glaze:
1/4 cup honey
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup prepared mustard

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees.
Combine ALL ingredients and mix well (with your hands).
Coat a loaf pan with non stick spray.
Put mixture into loaf pan and put half of the glaze mixture ontop.
Bake for 45 minutes and add the rest of the glaze.
Bake for an additional 15 minutes.
Allow to cool for 10 minutes before enjoying!
