Title:
Prosciutto Wrapped Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound prosciutto, sliced
4 oz. Chive & Onion or garlic & herb soft cream cheese
16 spears fresh asparagus, trimmed
Olive oil

Directions:
Blanch asparagus for 1-2 minutes. (put in boiling water, then plunge into ice bath).
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Spray baking sheet with olive oil.
Spread prosciutto slices with cream cheese. Wrap slices around 4 asparagus spears. Arrange wrapped spears in a single layer on a medium baking sheet.
brush bundles with olive oil.
Bake 8-12 minutes in the preheated oven, until asparagus is tender.

Submitter: 
ANITASHAMMER
Description: 
A few spears of asparagus, some slices of prosciutto, and a bit of flavored cream cheese is all you need to assemble this fancy little side dish.Modified from: Prosciutto Wrapped Asparagus
