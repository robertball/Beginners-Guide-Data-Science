Title:
Broccoli Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh broccoli, chopped
2 cups shredded chicken
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup cheddar cheese
1/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup mayo
1 tsp lemon juice 1 tsp curry powder
1 tsp garlic powder
Pepper to taste
1 cup bread crumbs
2 Tbsp melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Bring large pot of water to boil and cook broccoli 3-4 minutes, drain. Toss bread crumbs in melted butter. Stir together all remaining ingredients. Top with bread crumbs. Pour into 9x13 pan and bake 30-45 minutes.
