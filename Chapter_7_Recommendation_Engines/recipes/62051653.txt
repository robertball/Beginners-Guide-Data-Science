Title:
Nanaimo Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BOTTOM:
1/2 c. butter
2 packets hot cocoa mix
1 tsp. vanilla
1 egg, beaten
2 c. graham cracker crumbs
1 c. unsweetened coconut flakes
1/2 c. chopped walnuts
FILLING:
1/4 c. cream cheese
4 tbsp. milk
2 tbsp. instant vanilla pudding mix (dry powder)
2 c. sifted confectioners' sugar
TOPPING:
4 oz. Milk  Chocolate
1 tbsp. butter

Directions:
Bottom Layer:
Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat. Add hot cocoa packets, vanilla and egg. Stir mixture continously until it thickens. Remove from heat. Fold in coconut, graham cracker crumbs and walnuts. Press into greased nine inch baking pan. Chill for about an hour.
Filling:   Whip cream cheese with beater until light and smooth. Mix in milk, pudding powder and powdered sugar. If mixture is not spreadable, add more teaspoons of milk until it is a spreadable consistency. Spread over bottom layer. Chill until firm.
Top layer: Melt chocolate and butter over very low heat, while stirring.   Spread chocolate mixer on top and allow to sit until cool. Cut into bars before chocolate gets too cold, and then chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
CINDERW
Description: 
This is a popular cookie bar in Canada. This is however only one of many variations on this recipe.
