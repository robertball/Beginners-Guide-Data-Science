Title:
Lamb  marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves minced garlic
1/4 c. grated onion
1 t. salt
1 t. fines herbes
1/2 t.  pepper
1/2 t. thyme
1/2 c. salad oil
1/2 c. white wine or lemon juice

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and marinate lamb for several hours.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
This is a wonderful marinade for a butterflied leg of lamb that I got from my friend Bev.
