Title:
Brown Sugar Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. butter
1 egg
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
1 C. brown sugar
2 C. flour
1 tsp. soda
1 C. milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Mix all ingredients together.  Pour into muffin tins.  If you don't use the paper muffin holders, make sure you grease each muffin tin.
Bake at 375 degrees for 15 min.

Submitter: 
Kelly Norton
Description: 
These are Grandma Bingham's recipes and my favorites.  They are yummy and moist!
