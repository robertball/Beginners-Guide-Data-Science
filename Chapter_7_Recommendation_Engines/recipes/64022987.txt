Title:
Garlic Sweet Potato Mash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs (4 medium) sweet potatoes, peeled & cubed
1 tbsp butter
3 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 cup 1% milk
2 tbsp light sour cream
salt and fresh cracked ground pepper, to taste

Directions:
In a large pot boil sweet potatoes in salted water until tender, drain in a colander.
Meanwhile, melt butter and saut√© garlic until lightly golden. Return potatoes to the pan, add milk and sour cream; mash until smooth and creamy. Adjust salt and pepper to taste.
