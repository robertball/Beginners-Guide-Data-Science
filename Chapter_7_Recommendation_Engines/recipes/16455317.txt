Title:
Bacon Cheese Appetizer Toasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 pkg. party rye or pumpernickle bread
- 1 Cup Veleeta
- 1 Cup sharp cheddar cheese
- 1/4 tsp. dry mustard
- 1&1/2 Tablespoon worcestershire sauce.
- 7 slices bacon cooked and crumbled

Directions:
- Mix all ingredients together
- Butter both sides of bread and toast in a skillet
- spread mixture on toasts
- Broil until hot and bubbly

Submitter: 
Singer6
Description: 
A favorite a parties.  The mixture freezes well so it can be prepared ahead of time.
