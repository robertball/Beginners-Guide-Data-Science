Title:
Ginger Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup light soy sauce
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 teaspon minced fresh ginger root
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 spring onions (green onions, scallions) chopped

Directions:
Combine soy sauce, red wine vinegar and minced ginger. Set aside
In a small pan melt brown sugar on high heat, stirring rapidly, until it is melted, but not burnt.  Add soy sauce mixture. Bring to boil until brown sugar is melted again.
Remove from the heat, pour into serving dish and garnish with spring onions.
