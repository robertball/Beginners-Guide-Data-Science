Title:
Soft & Luscious Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4536574.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 cups of flour
1 cup of powdered sugar
1 cup of mayonnaise
3/4 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon of salt
1/2 cup butter
4 medium-sized overripe bananas

Directions:
1. Move oven rack to one level below the middle.
2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
3. Whisk two eggs, add to large mixing bowl.
4. Add flour, powdered sugar, mayonnaise, sugar, baking soda, salt to same bowl. Stir briefly.
5. In seperate bowl, mash the butter and bananas. Add the mixture to the large mixing bowl.
6. Hand-stir, fold, and mash until it reaches an almost pudding-like texture.
7. Coat a 9x9 square glass pan with a layer of butter. Pour in mixture.
8. Bake in the oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
9. Check the center with a toothpick or fork tip. It should come out clean.

Description: 
It's soft and luscious, super sweet, tear away banana-flavored deliciousness.
