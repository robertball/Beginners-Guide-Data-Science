Title:
White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 chicken breasts, cooked & cut up
5 c. chicken broth
1-2 cans green chilis, chopped
1-2 cloves garlic
1 c. onion
4-5 cans great Northern beans
1 can stewed tomatoes
Sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Combine beans & broth on high. Add rest of ingredients & salt & pepper. Simmer for 45 min. on low.  Serve with dollop of sour cream on top if desired.
