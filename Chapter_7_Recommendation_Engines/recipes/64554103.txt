Title:
Dad's Pinto Bean Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3346539.jpg
Ingredients:
-1 lb. bag of pinto beans(approx. 2 cups worth)
-1 onion
-1 large clove of garlic
-2 cubes of brisket dripping bouillon
-package of ham hocks
-chili powder
-salt (Fiesta brand pinto bean seasoning can be used instead of salt & pepper)
-cayenne pepper
-chicken broth

Directions:
-The night before cooking beans, sort through them to remove any rocks, etc. and then put in pot and cover with water. Make sure there is at least a couple inches of water above beans because they soak up lots of water.
-The next day drain and rerinse beans
-Cover clean beans with chicken broth (about 1" of broth over beans)
-Add diced onion, garlic, bouillon, ham hocks, and season to taste
-Bring to a boil
-Reduce heat, cover, and simmer on lowest setting for 2-2 1/2 hrs occasionally checking on liquid  level and seasoning needs.
