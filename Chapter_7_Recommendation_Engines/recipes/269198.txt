Title:
Chocolate Ice Cream Mug Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6355458.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup vanilla ice cream, melted
1 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1 scoop vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Mix melted ice cream, flour, and baking powder together in a microwave-safe mug. Stir in cocoa powder. Microwave until top of cake looks done, about 90 seconds. Top with vanilla ice cream.

Submitter:

Description: 
What's better than ice cream on your mug cake? Ice cream in a mug cake! Not just that, you can top it with ice cream, too!. Sounds like heaven!
