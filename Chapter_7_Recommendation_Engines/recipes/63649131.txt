Title:
Mississippi Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
mayonaise
mustard
lemon jiuce
corn starch
paprika

Directions:
Mix mayo and mustart to taste (should be a light yellow color), add approx 2 TBSP lemon juice, whisk in cornstarch to thicken, add a few dashes of paprika to taste.

Submitter: 
Marquise Beauchemin
Description: 
We used to buy Mississippi brand salad dressing at the store, I can no longer find it, my Dad made this recipe for it and it's darn close. We also used this as a spread for sandwiches. Enjoy!
