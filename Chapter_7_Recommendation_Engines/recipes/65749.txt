Title:
Mediterranean Orzo Saute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980913.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 small red onion, sliced into half-moons
1/2 pound orzo pasta, cooked as package directs and drained
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
1 (6 ounce) jar Lindsay® Spanish Olives Stuffed with Pimiento, drained and halved
3/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/4 cup toasted pine nuts
1/4 cup lemon juice
3 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
1 tablespoon fresh Italian parsley, chopped
Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion; saute for 3 minutes. Stir in pasta, tomatoes, olives, feta, pine nuts, lemon juice, basil, and parsley. Heat through. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Lindsay® Olives
Description: 
A delicious orzo pasta dish with fresh tomatoes, feta cheese, and pine nuts. Tossed together with Lindsay® Spanish Olives and fresh basil and parsley, it is great served warm or cold.
