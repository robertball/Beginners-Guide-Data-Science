Title:
Salsa Chicken Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 boneless chicken breasts cut into bite-sized cubes
1 15 oz. jar Garlic Alfredo Sauce
1 15 oz. jar Alfredo Sauce
1 8 oz. container of fresh cut medium salsa
1 lb. Mezze Penne pasta
3-5 tspns Chicken Seasoning spice
1 tspn Olive Oil OR Olive Oil cooking spray.

Directions:
Cook Mezze Penne pasta per directions (al dente). Drain, rinse and set aside.
While pasta is cooking heat olive oil (or spray) in a large frying pan until hot. Add chicken cubes and season with Chicken Seasoning spice while stir frying until cooked thoroughly, about 5-7 minutes.
Add both jars of Alfredo sauce and Salsa, stirring until sauces are mixed well.
Add pasta and heat through over low heat, about 30 minutes.
