Title:
Pumpkin Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pumpkin Cake:
* 3 eggs
* 1 cup sugar
* 2/3 cup pumpkin
* 1 teaspoon lemon juice
* 3/4 cup flour
* 2 teaspoons cinnamon
* 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
* 1 cup walnuts (optional)
Ingredients for Filling:
* 1 cup powdered sugar
* 18 oz. cream cheese
* 4 Tablespoons butter
* 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Use a cookie sheet with four sides, grease and flour cookie sheet, preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
For the cake, beat eggs for 5 minutes, then fold in sugar, blend all cake ingredients.
Bake for 15-20 minutes, then dump out on a towel that has been covered in powdered sugar, then sprinkle powdered sugar on top, lay another towel on top of cake then roll up while warm, set in refrigerator for one hour. Then remove from towel and mix filling.
For the filling; mix the filling ingredients together and spread on cake then roll back up, chill for 4 hours, then serve.
