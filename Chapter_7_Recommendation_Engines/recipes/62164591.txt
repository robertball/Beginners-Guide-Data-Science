Title:
Mom's Cucumber Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices of bread (preferably whole wheat)
4-6 Tbs Cream Cheese
1 avacado, peeled, pitted and sliced
1/2 cucumber sliced thinly
Alfalfa sprouts
Tomato slices
2 Tbs sunflower seeds

Directions:
Spread cream cheese on bread slices. Sprinkle sundflower seeds on top of the cream cheese. Top with cucumber, avacado, tomato and sprouts. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jenwee99
Description: 
My mom and I made up this sandwich when I was a little girl. It is very easy and tasty! We sometimes would add raisins and nuts to give it a different flavor.
