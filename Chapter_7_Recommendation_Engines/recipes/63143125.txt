Title:
Pork Chops by SuZanne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 center cut porkchops, seasoned with salt and pepper
3 T oil for frying - I use lite olive oil
1 medium yellow onion, sliced thin
1 can mushroom soup
1/2 soup can of water
balsamic vinegar to taste - I use 2 Tbs.

Directions:
Heat oil in cast iron skillet or other fry pan. Fry pork chops over medium-high heat till nicely browned. Remove chops from pan and set aside. Use same pan with drippings to saute onions. When onions are translucent stir in soup and water to make a sauce. Stir in balsamic vinegar to taste. In baking dish pour enough sauce to cover bottom. Lay pork chops in dish and cover with reamaining sauce. Bake at 350 degrees until porkchops are cooked through.
