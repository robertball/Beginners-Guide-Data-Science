Title:
Rubarb Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 unbaked pie crust
2 eggs
2 heaping tbs flour
dash of salt
1 cup sugar
4 cups rubarb

Directions:
Beat eggs until foamy
add flour, salt and sugar to egg mix.
Chop rubarb into  bit size pieces.
Put rubarb into pie shell and pour the egg mix over the rubard.
Bake 350 for 40  min or until tender.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description:
