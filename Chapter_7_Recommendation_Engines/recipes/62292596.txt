Title:
Snappy Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ c. soy sauce
2. T. white wine
2. T. lemon juice
½ tsp. tobasco sauce
2 green onions, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, crushed
2  ½ - 3 # chicken, cut up (or breasts)

Directions:
Combine first six ingredients.  Pour into large plastic bag with chicken.  Refrig. for six to eight hours.  Drain, and grill.
