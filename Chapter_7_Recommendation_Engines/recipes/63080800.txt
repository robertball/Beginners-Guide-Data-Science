Title:
Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. butter
2/3 cup sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
2 cups (4oz) miniature marshmallows
1 1/2 cup (9 oz) peanut butter chips
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
in medium saucepan, stirring occasionally, combine butter, evap. milk, sugar and salt. Cook over med. heat.
Bring to full boil; cook 4-5 min. - stirring constantly.  Remove from heat.
Stir in marshmallows, peanut butter chips and vanilla.  Stir vigorously 1 min. until marshmallows melt and blend.
Pour into buttered 8 inch or 9 inch square pan.  Cool.
Melt choco chips; spread on fudge
Chill before serving!
