Title:
Meatball Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground sirloin
1/2 small onion finely diced
1/3 c bread crumbs
1 egg
3T Worcestershire sauce
salt & pepper to taste
4T butter, divided
1 lb. mushrooms, sliced
1 can (10.5 oz) beef broth
1t ground mustard
2T all purpose flour
1/3 c sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Combine ground sirloin, onion, bread crumbs, egg, Worcestershire sauce, and salt and pepper in a medium bowl.  Mix well.
Divide meat mixture into golf-ball sized meatballs.  Place on greased cookie sheet.
Bake 15 minutes.  Set aside.
Melt 2T butter in a large pan over medium heat.  Saute mushrooms ~10 minutes until soft.  Push mushrooms to the side of the pan.
Add additional 2T butter and melt.  Whisk in flour.
Add broth and mustard and bring to a boil.
Add meatballs to pan and cook for 10-15 minutes.  Remove from heat.
Add sour cream and stir to combine.
Serve over rice or egg noodles.
