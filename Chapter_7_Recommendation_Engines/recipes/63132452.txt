Title:
Oven Fried Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/799560.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Pork Chops
1 medium egg
3 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon water
Â½  teaspoon garlic powder
1 pinch ginger
1 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Line pan with aluminum foil and spray with non stick spray
Beat egg, soy sauce, water, garlic, and ginger together in a bowl.  Place bread crumbs in separate bowl.
After rinsing chops, dip them in egg mixture, then coat both sides with crumbs
Bake for 50 - 55 minutes
