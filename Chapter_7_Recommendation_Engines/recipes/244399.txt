Title:
Hoisin Honey Chicken Wings from Reynolds Wrap® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2364520.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil
1 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger
Salt and pepper to taste
6 chicken wings, separated
1 (18 x 18-inch) sheet of Reynolds Wrap® Heavy Duty Aluminum Foil
1/2 cup chopped green onions
1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
Mix hoisin sauce, soy sauce, sesame oil, honey, ginger and salt and pepper together in a bowl. Toss chicken wings in sauce mixture to coat.
Place on an 18 x 18-inch sheet of Reynolds Wrap(R) Heavy Duty Aluminum Foil. Form a tightly sealed packet.
Grill on prepared grill, turning once, about 30 minutes.
Carefully open packet and top with green onions and sesame seeds.

Submitter: 
Reynolds Kitchens®
Description: 
Grill up this fresh take on classic chicken wings from Julie Lee of Julie's Kitchen.
