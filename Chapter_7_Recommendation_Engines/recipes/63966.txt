Title:
Homemade Irish Cream Liqueur Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6177167.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups Irish whiskey, brandy, rum, bourbon, and scotch or rye whiskey
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
2 cups whipping cream or coffee cream
2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
2 tablespoons instant coffee
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon almond extract

Directions:
In blender container, combine ingredients; blend until smooth.  Serve over ice.  Store tightly covered in refrigerator.  Stir before serving.

Submitter: 
EAGLEBRAND®
Description: 
Luxurious home made  Irish Cream Liqueur is a delicious winter drink, and it makes an excellent hostess gift for parties.
