Title:
Barbequed Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup water
2 1/2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon white vinegar
1 tablespoon tomato paste
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
Dash of cayenne
24 large or jumbo shrimp
12 bacon slices, cut in halves

Directions:
Melt butter in medium saucepan
Add onion and saute until soft
Stir in ketchup, water, Worc sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, tomato paste, dry mustard, red pepper
Simmer uncovered for 15 minutes
Remove from heat; add stir in shrimp
Let stand for 15 minutes
Preheat broiler
Remove shrimp from the sauce
Wrap each shrimp with a piece of bacon; secure with wooden pick
Place on greased rack on grill or in broiler
Cook 3-4 minutes; turn and cook remaining side until bacon is crisp
Reheat sauce and serve seperately
