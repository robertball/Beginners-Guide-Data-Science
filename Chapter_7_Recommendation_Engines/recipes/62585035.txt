Title:
Sweet and Sour Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon oil
4 pound arm or chuck roast
2 onions, sliced
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup sugar
Juice of 2 lemons
Salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a heavy skillet or large pot with tight fitting lid. Add roast and onions. Brown, turning frequently. Add remaining ingredients, cover, and simmer over low heat for 3 to 3 1/2 hours; turning every 45 minutes and uncovering for the last 15 minutes to reduce juices.
