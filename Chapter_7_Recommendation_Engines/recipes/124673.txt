Title:
Cranberry Macadamia Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978381.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound white confectioners' coating, cut into pieces
1 (3.5 ounce) jar macadamia nuts
1/2 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
In a saucepan, melt candy coating in a saucepan over medium-low heat, stirring until smooth. Add nuts and cranberries; mix well. Spread onto a foil-lined baking sheet. Cool. Break into pieces.

Submitter: 
Pamela  Galiardi
Description: 
'I fill Christmas tins with this special candy to give as gifts,' says Pamela Galiardi of San Jose, California.
