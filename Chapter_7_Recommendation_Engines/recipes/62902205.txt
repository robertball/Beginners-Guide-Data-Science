Title:
Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups fresh basil leaves
3 cloves garlic
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/3 cup olive oil
1/4 cup dry roasted, unsalted sunflower kernels
1 pinch ground black pepper
1 pinch sea salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a food processor or blender. Blend/chop to a paste.

Submitter: 
ThePolo
Description: 
My recipe for pesto. Not sure where it came from, but I've been making it this way since college. I've substituted sunflower kernels for pine nuts, because who really likes pine nuts anyway?
