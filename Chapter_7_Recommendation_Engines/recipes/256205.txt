Title:
Buffalo Mashed Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4148617.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package Green Giant® Mashed Cauliflower - any variety
2 tablespoons cayenne pepper sauce
1 tablespoon butter
2 tablespoons crumbled blue cheese
1 green onion, chopped

Directions:
Heat Green Giant® Mashed Cauliflower according to package directions.
Stir in hot sauce, butter, blue cheese, and green onion. Drizzle with additional cayenne pepper sauce, if desired.

Submitter: 
Green Giant
Description: 
Simply stir a few ingredients into our rich and creamy Mashed Cauliflower and you have a side dish that takes center stage. This couldn't be easier to make and once you taste it, you might want to keep it all for yourself!
