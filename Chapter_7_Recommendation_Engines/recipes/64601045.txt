Title:
Honey Garlic Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c Garlic-hoisin stir fry sauce (V-H)
1/2 c honey
3 -5 tsp minced garlic
2 tbs soya sauce
1/2 - 1 tsp sesame oil
Melted butter (optional)

Directions:
Simmer ingredients for 4 -6 minutes add butter, if desired before serving.
Store leftovers in mason jar in refrigerator 4 - 6 weeks (without butter).
