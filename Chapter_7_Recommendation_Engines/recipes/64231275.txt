Title:
Pear, Gorganzola and Walnut People Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Gorganzola piccante (chilled well)
Walnuts, halved
Dried cranberries
Decana pears, ripe but still firm
Baby spinach leaves
Balsamic vinegar glaze
Skewers

Directions:
Per skewer: one cube cheese, one cube pear, one spinach leaf (for the hair), one cranberry and one walnut just stabbed enough that it will still stand.  A squirt of glaze for each.
