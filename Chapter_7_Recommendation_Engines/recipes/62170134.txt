Title:
Tator Tot Hotdish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds hamburger
1 medium onion
3 stalks of celery
1 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. salt
1 can of corn with most juice
3/4 cup chicken broth
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 bag of tator tots
Colby Jack cheese, if desired

Directions:
Dice onion & celery and put into a fry pan with hamburger. Cook until hamburger is throughly cooked. Mix in both cans of soups, chicken broth, add salt and pepper to taste preference. Put the mixture into a 9x13 pan. Cover the top of mixture with cheese, if desired, and then top with tator tots. Cook in oven on center rack at 375 degrees for 1 hour minutes or until mixture bubbles and tator tots are browned.

Submitter: 
Kasey Lyn Cotter
Description: 
Comfort food that tastes great and is easy! This has been a family tradition for as long as I can remember. It's delicious. I have seen many different variations of this dish- feel free to tweak it the way you like! Happy eating.
