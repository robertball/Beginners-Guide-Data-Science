Title:
Loubia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. vegetable oil
1 medium onion, chopped
2 pounds green beans
4 medium tomatoes peeled and chopped
1/2 tsp. cumin
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium sized pot, saute onion in vegetable oil until soft, but do not brown.
Add remaining ingredients.
Cook in covered pot on stove until beans are tender and tomatoes start to thicken.
