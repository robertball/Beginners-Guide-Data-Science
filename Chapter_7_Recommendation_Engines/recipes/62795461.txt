Title:
Bill's Mango-Blueberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/4 cups white sugar
2 eggs
3/4 cup vegetable oil
2 1/2 cups mangos, peeled, seeded and chopped
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/4 cup blueberries, fresh or dehydrated

Directions:
Combine all of the dry ingredients.  Beat eggs with oil and add to flour mixture.  Add mangoes, lemon and blueberries.
Pour into 2 greased 8 x 4 loaf pans.  Bake at 350 degrees F -175 degrees C  (325 degrees F for glass pans) for about 60 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
I didn't have any raisins so I substututed blueberry's.  An amazing combination of flavors that explode in your mouth.  Slice while still warm from the oven and slather with butter.  Yum!Modified from: Chinky's Mango Bread
