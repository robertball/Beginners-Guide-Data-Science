Title:
Tasty bacon small bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
bacon-use as much or as little as you like.
8 oz onion & chive cream cheese-brought to room temp
3 refrigerated crescent rolls

Directions:
1. Take cream cheese out so it can raise to room temp
2. Cook bacon & crumble
3. Mix together cream cheese & bacon
4. Lay out rolls & cut each in half
5. Place a small ball of the cream cheese/bacon in the middle & roll up the crescent roll.
6. Bake at 350 degrees until brown
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
JenEvison
Description: 
These are a great treat to whip up & serve to a crowd!
