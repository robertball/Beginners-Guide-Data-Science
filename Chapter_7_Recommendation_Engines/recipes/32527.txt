Title:
Indiana Bulldog Recipe

Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce vodka
1 fluid ounce coffee flavored liqueur
1/3 cup milk
1/3 cup cola-flavored carbonated beverage

Directions:
Into a tall glass with ice, pour vodka, coffee liqueur, milk and cola. Stir.
