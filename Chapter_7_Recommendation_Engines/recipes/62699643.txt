Title:
Oven Baked Parmesan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/610236.jpg
Ingredients:
6 chicken breasts
1 cup dry bread crumbs, seasoned
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/3 cup of olive oil or vegetable oil
6 garlic cloves minced or
1/3 cup of garlic powder

Directions:
Marinate chicken in oil and garlic for about 1 hour in the refrigerator.
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Dip chicken breasts in bread crumbs and parmesan cheese.
Coat baking dish with 2 tbs oil
Place chicken in baking dish.
Bake for about 45 minutes until golden brown and thoroughly cooked.

Submitter: 
Mare
Description: 
Simply Delicious ! 

The breadcrumbs I use in this recipe are my homemade ones. Which are already seasoned with herbs and spices (salt and pepper). If you need to add salt and pepper do so when the chicken is marinating.
