Title:
Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 cube steaks
2 large yellow onions
4-5 large green bell peppers
1 bunch celery
1 10lb. can diced tomatoes
seasoned flour, as needed

Directions:
Dice all vegetables into 1 inch dice.  Dredge cube steak in seasoned flour and brown on flat top.  In a 4" hotel pan, make layers of tomatoes, celery, and peppers, then cube steak.  Repeat.  There should be 3 layers of vegetables, bottom, middle, and top.  Cover and cook 1-1 1/2 hours.
