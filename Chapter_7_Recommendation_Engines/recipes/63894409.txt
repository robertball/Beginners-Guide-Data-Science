Title:
Cashew Swiss Salad with Poppy Seed dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
DRESSING (make 24 hours in advance)
24 hour dressing: Make ahead and refrigerate
1 c olive oil
1/3 c vinegar
1/3 c grated onion
3/4 c sugar
1/2 tsp mustard
1 tsp poppy seeds
pinch of salt
SALAD
1 or 2 heads of green or red leaf lettuce
1 cup of chopped cashews
2 cups grated Swiss cheese

Directions:
Make dressing about 24 hours ahead and refrigerate, mixing occasionally.
Pour dressing over chopped lettuce, toss with cashews and swiss cheese. Serve.
