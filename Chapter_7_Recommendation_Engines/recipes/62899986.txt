Title:
Mexican-Style Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/702702.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T. olive oil
1 pound ground chicken
1 cup chopped onion
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 (10 ounce) can enchilada sauce
1 cup fresh or frozen corn
1 1/4 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon salt
2/3 cup uncooked elbow macaroni
2/3 cup shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large nonstick skillet, heat olive oil and cook chicken and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink.Stir in the tomatoes, enchilada sauce, corn, water, chili powder, oregano and salt. Bring to a boil. Add macaroni. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until macaroni is tender. Sprinkle with cheese; cover and cook 2-3 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
