Title:
Independence Day Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/323049.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Pkgs of Jiffy White Cake Mix
1 1/2 Cups of Cold Water
3 Egg Whites
1 Cup Strawberries
1 Pkg Strawberry Jello
2 Tbs Vanilla Extract
1 Cup Blueberries
1 Pkg Berry Blue Jello

Directions:
Prepare cake pans (8 or 9 in.) for baking by preferred method (grease, flour, or Baker's Joy)
Strawberry Layer: Add 1/2 cup cold water and 1 egg white to cake mix, beat according to directions on box. Chop strawberries and puree until smooth.  Mix in pureed strawberries and 1 pkg strawberry jello to batter.
Vanilla Layer: Add 1/2 cup cold water, 1 egg white, and 2 Tbs vanilla extract to cake mix, beat according to directions on box.
Blueberry Layer:  Add 1/2 cup cold water and 1 egg white to cake mix, beat according to directions on box.  Puree blueberries until smooth.  Mix in pureed blueberries and 1 pkg berry blue jello to batter.
Pour batter into cake pans. Baking time will vary for 8 or 9 in. cake pans.  Refer to cake mix package for baking times.  Because of the addition of fruit and jello, cake will take a few more minutes to bake.
Can top with butterceam frosting, cool whip, or a glaze.
