Title:
Kinaja Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/749687.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup orange juice
2 kiwis, peeled and sliced
3 ripe bananas, sliced
3 pineapple rings
1/2 cup unsweetened pineapple juice
2 cups crushed ice

Directions:
In an electric blender, mix together orange juice, kiwi, bananas, pineapple, and pineapple juice until well blended. Pour the mixture into a pitcher.
Place the ice in the blender, then pour the fruit mixture over the ice. Process until the ice is blended in with the fruit juices.

Submitter: 
Andrea
Description: 
Serve this fruit smoothie in tall glasses with straws.
