Title:
Carrot Ginger Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (3 lbs) Baby Carrots
2 Tbsp  Wegmans Basting Oil
2 Tbsp  Vegetable Oil
2 medium  yellow onions, peeled, diced (about 2 cups)
2 Tbsp  chopped fresh ginger root
2 cartons (32 oz each)  Thai Culinary Stock
1 container (32 oz)  Bolthouse Farms 100% Carrot Juice
1 1/2 Tbsp  Garden Gourmet Lemon Grass Herb Blend (Produce Dept)
2 Tbsp  sesame oil
1/4 cup  hoisin sauce
1 Tbsp kosher salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Toss carrots and basting oil in large bowl. Spread in single layer on baking sheet. Roast on center rack of oven 30-45 min, until lightly browned and tender. Set aside.
Add vegetable oil to stockpot on MEDIUM. Add onions; cook, stirring 10 min. Add ginger; cook, stirring, about 2 min. Add roasted carrots and stock. Bring to a boil; simmer 10 min.
Add carrot juice. Carefully puree soup with handheld blender until smooth. Add lemon grass, sesame oil, hoisin sauce, and salt.  Blend well. Simmer 5 min.
