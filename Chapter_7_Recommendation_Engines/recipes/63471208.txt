Title:
Cinnamon Roll and Cream Cheese Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/946090.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package 8 ounce cream cheese
2 cans of refrigerated cinnamon rolls with icing
1/4 cup butter cut into pieces

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease bundt pan with cooking spray.
Cut block of cream cheese into 20 pieces. Set aside the icing from the cinnamon rolls.
Cut each cinnamon roll in half and flatten each piece.
Place 1 chunk of cream cheese on each piece of dough then pinch edges closed.
Place each ball of dough into the bundt pan and place butter pieces on top.
Bake 40 to 45 minutes or until golden brown. Cool for ten minutes then remove from pan.
Drizzle the icing over the top of the bread and serve warm.

Submitter: 
Michelle
Description: 
This monkey bread is a delicious and easy breakfast that can be prepared the night before. Just place it into the oven in the morning and enjoy! My family loves this monkey bread and it is so simple to make.
