Title:
Hamburger Helper Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of ground beef
1 (1oz) packet onion soup/dip mix
1/2 tsp pepper
3/4 tsp gran garlic
1/2 onion, sliced *or* 1 tsp dried
1 tsp parsley
2 tsp cornstarch
3 cups pasta (egg noodles, etc) apx 150 to 170g
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 cup milk
1 1/2 c low sodium beef broth/water/mix
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Brown the ground beef and then drain off fat in a large skillet.  Remove from pan and set aside.
Saut√© onions and mushrooms until golden.  Remove from pan and add to cooked beef.
Add broth, milk, and seasonings to pan (setting aside a little liquid to mix with cornstarch).  Stir in cornstarch and bring to boil - cook until slightly thickened.
Return beef mixture to pan - stir - then add dried noodles.
Mix well and bring to a boil.
Reduce the heat, cover, and cook for 15-20 minutes.
Take off heat and gently stir in the sour cream.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Cloned recipe for boxed mix.  Note:  I cut soup mix down by half first time and used dried ingredients.  Original recipe only called for 2 cups noodles which resulted in watery sauce and too few noodles.  Seasoning wasn't great with the half packet ... not bad, just not killer stuff.
