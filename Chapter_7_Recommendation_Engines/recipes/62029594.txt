Title:
easy black bean soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices thick bacon cut int 1/2 inch pieces
1 large onion chopped
2 celery stalks chopped
2 carrots chopped
2 (15oz) cans black beans drained
4 garlic cloves chopped
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
1/2 teaspooon cayenne pepper
1/4 cup dry sherry
sour cream for garnish
sliced green onion for garnish

Directions:
cook bacon  about 12 minutes until crisp. remove from pan . drain reserve 2 tablespoons of the drippings. set bacon  aside. In drippings cook onion, celery, carrots, garlic until tender about 8 minutes.stir in beans, broth and pepper simmer for 15 minutes. Puree soup in blender. Return to pot. Add sherry and cooked bacon heat through. Garnish each serving with sour cream and onion.
