Title:
Roasted Vegetable Pasta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium eggplant
1 red pepper
1 large tomato
1 large onion
3 cloves garlic
1 stick celery
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
salt and pepper
1/4 cup olive oil
1 can pasta sauce
1/2 package pasta shapes
2 cups shredded cheese

Directions:
Set oven to 400'
Chop up vegetables and garlic, season with salt, pepper and basil. Coat vegetables with olive oil. Place on cookie sheet and roast for 30 mins.
Cook pasta in boiling water until al dente.
Reduce oven to 350'
Combine pasta, vegetables, pasta sauce and 1/2 of the cheese.
Place in a casserole dish, top with remaining cheese.
Bake in oven for 20mins until cheese is bubbling.

Submitter: 
SAJA2003
Description: 
An easy but popular pasta dish, substitute whatever veggies you have on hand. Also good with left over cooked chicken too.
