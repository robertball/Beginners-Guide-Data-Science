Title:
Garlic Tuna Bowties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. bowtie pasta (farfelle)
1 can of solid white albacore tuna in water, drained
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced (divided)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta for 8 minutes; drain.
While pasta is cooking, place drained tuna in medium bowl and flake.  Add olive oil, salt & pepper, and 2 cloves of garlic to the tuna.  Mix.
Pour pasta in the tuna mixture, add more salt & pepper, and mince the last clove of garlic on top of the pasta.  Mix well.
Cover bowl with plastic wrap and place in microwave for one minute.  Add more salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Jeankat
Description: 
A garlicky pasta with tuna fish!  Quick and easy to fix, and absolutely delicious!
