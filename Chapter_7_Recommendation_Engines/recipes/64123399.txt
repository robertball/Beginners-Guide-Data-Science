Title:
Ranch Dressing - Low Sodium, Low Fat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Tofu Mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup skim milk
2 tablespoons cultured buttermilk powder
2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
2 tablespoons minced chives
1 teaspoon minced garlic
3/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons Mrs. Dash Original or similar salt free seasoning blend
1/2 to 1 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
2 teaspoons minced dill weed

Directions:
using a food processor, blender, or hand whisk combine tofu, sour cream and buttermilk until smooth and creamy. Add all remaining ingredients and stir well. Chill well before serving. Use promptly. For each 2 tablespoon serving: 25 calories and 75 milligrams sodium.

Submitter: 
kcryss
Description: 
Delicious low fat low sodium ranch dressing for salads and dipping. Give up the fat and sodium without losing flavor!
