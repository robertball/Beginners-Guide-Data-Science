Title:
Delicious Healthy Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
1lb ground turkey breast
3c tomato sauce
3T Italian seasoning (or 1t each dried basil, parsley, and oregano)
1/4t freshly ground black pepper
1/4t garlic powder
1/2c chopped mushrooms
6c chopped fresh spinach (or chard)
2c fat-free ricotta
1/4t nutmeg
1 package whole-wheat lasagna noodles(about 8 oz, or 9 noodles)
2c (8 oz) shredded part-skim mozzarella

Directions:
Create 2 mixtures while cooking the noodles in a large pot of salted water
Turkey Mixture:
Saute 1/2 cup chopped onion in large pan for 2 minutes.
Add 1lb ground turkey breast cook 5-7 minutes more.
Add 3cups tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, black pepper, garlic powder, 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms then simmer about 2 minutes & season to taste
Ricotta Mixture:
In a large bowl combine the Ricotta, Spinach, & nutmeg
Spray a 9x13 baking pan and layer 3 times in order with 3 noodles, 1/3 ricotta mixture, 1/3 turkey mixture, 1/3 of the cheese.
Bake in a 375 degree preheated oven for 20~25min.  Allow to cool 5min before cutting & serving.
