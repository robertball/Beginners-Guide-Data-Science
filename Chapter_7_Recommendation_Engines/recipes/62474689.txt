Title:
Tator Tot & Cheese Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 cups milk
Salt and pepper, to taste
2 cups grated yellow cheddar cheese
2 cups grated white cheddar cheese
1/2 cup parmesean cheese
1 cup diced ham
2-lb. bag tator tots, thawed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â° F. Grease a 9" x 13" dish. In a large bowl whisk eggs and milk. Add salt and pepper. Add 1 cup each of the cheeses plus the parmesean cheese, ham and tator tots. Mix thoroughly. Pour into dish and sprinkle remaining cheeses on top. Bake for 50 minutes until the cheeses are melted and bubbly.
