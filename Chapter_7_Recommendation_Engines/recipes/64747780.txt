Title:
INSTANT POT Grandma's Hungry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, cut in half
1 stick of butter, melted
3 onions, white or yellow
1/4 cup chicken broth
2 tbsp minced garlic
1 tbsp spice blend
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp smoked paprika
2 tbsp flour sifted
24 oz sour cream

Directions:
SET ASIDE FLOUR AND SOUR CREAM.
roughly chop the onions, large pieces hold up better.
place onions in the bottom of the instant pot.
add melted butter and broth on top of the onions.
mix garlic, spice blend, both paprikas, salt & pepper  add to the onions, STIR.
place chicken on top of the onions.
INSTANT POT- meat button or HIGH pressure set for  9 minutes.
allow to release pressure naturally. do not rush, this will make the chicken rubbery & tough.
to make the next step easier, remove the chickens and some onions to a plate. use the SAUTE button to bring the juice to a bubble.
sift in flour and stir until no lumps and the sauce has thickened a bit.
add sour cream buy the spoon full, stirring to incorporate.
cancel saute feature.
add chicken back to instant pot.
be sure to reset the KEEP WARM function.
serve over rice, potatoes, or pasta.
