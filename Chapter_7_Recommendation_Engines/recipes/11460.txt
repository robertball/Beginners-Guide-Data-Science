Title:
Rum Flavored Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1060674.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter
1/4 cup milk
2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon rum flavored extract
1 tablespoon hot water

Directions:
Heat butter and milk over a low heat. When melted remove from heat.
Stir sugar and rum extract into the butter and milk mixture. Use hot water to thin the icing to your desired consistency.

Submitter: 
millie atchison
Description: 
This frosting is rum-tasty and delicious. Try it out on any cookies calling for icing.
