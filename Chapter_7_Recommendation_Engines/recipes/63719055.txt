Title:
Pinto Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1033241.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Slices of whole grain bread, crumbled finely
1 tsp. Dried ginger
1 tsp. Curry Powder
1 tsp. Celery seed
1/2 tsp. Salt
6 Pickled onions, sliced
1 14 oz can Pinto beans, drained and mashed
3 Tbsp. Tomato paste
2 Tbsp. Canola oil
1 Tbsp. Fancy Molasses
2 Tbsp. Brown sugar

Directions:
Crumble whole grain bread slices in a medium sized bowl
Add spices, salt and pickled onions mixing together until well blended
Cook pinto beans in a saucepan until heated through, drain them and mash into a fine puree
Stir in tomato paste, molasses and brown sugar with the pinto beans in the saucepan
Mix in the bread crumb mixture and stir until mixture forms a smooth paste
Form into patties and saute in frying pan in oil  for 3 to 5 minutes until lightly browned on top.
Remove to a dinner plate to cool completely

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very spicy veggie burger with a smooth texture that is a good alternative for the usual beef burger for dinner.
