Title:
Chili (Tina's) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 white onion, finely chopped
1 sweet green bell pepper, finely chopped.
1 sweet red bell pepper, finely chopped
4 green onions (.85 oz)
1/4 cup chopped cilantro. (.75 oz)
4 lbs ground turkey
1 lb diced pork chop
2 tsp cumin
1 tsp crushed red pepper
1 Tbsp chili powder
2 28oz cans crushed tomatoes
1 14oz can petite diced toms with chiles
beans of choice (*optional)

Directions:
night before: Finely chop white onion, peppers, green onion and cilantro Place in a sealed container and place in the fridge.
Brown ground turkey with salt and pepper and drain while Browning cut pork chops. Store together with ground turkey in fridge.
Day of: Add cut veggies, browned meat, crushed tomatoes, petite diced toms with chiles, spices and any beans you want. Set to low for about 6-8 hours. Can add pasta towards end of cooking and taste to season more.
