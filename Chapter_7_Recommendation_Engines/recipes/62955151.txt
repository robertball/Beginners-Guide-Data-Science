Title:
NO BAKE QUICK AND CHEAP  BANANA CHOCOLATE MOUSE  PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/732938.jpg
Ingredients:
1 BANANA
2 (3.2 OZ.)BOXES OF JELL-O CHOCOLATE MOUSE
2 CUPS MILK
1 (6OZ.) CHOCOLATE READY CRUST(LIKE KEEBLER)
1 (8OZ.) TUB OF KRAFT COOL WHIP(ORIGINAL)WHIPPED TOPPING.

Directions:
MAKE JELLO MIX (FOLLOW DIRECTIONS)POUR INTO PIE CRUST.
SLICE BANANA ON TOP
SPREAD COOL WHIP ON TOP
PUT PLASTIC TOP ON,FROM PIE CRUST, PUT IN FRIDGE FOR 1 HOUR AT LEAST OR OVER NIGHT UP TO YOU.
