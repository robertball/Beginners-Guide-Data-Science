Title:
Best Fruit and Veg Salad with Poppyseed Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Head of Romaine, cut into small pieces
1/2 English cucumber, quartered and sliced
Apple, cut into small pieces
Craisens
Feta cheese
Pecans
Dressing
1/3 c light tasting olive oil
1/4 c sugar
2 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 tbsp lime juice
1/2 tsp mustard
1 tsp finely chopped green onion greens
1 tsp poppy seeds

Directions:
Prepare salad and chill.
Emulsify dressing ingredients and chill.
Dress salad lightly right before serving.

Submitter: 
KKraft
Description: 
Versatile salad, can use a variety of fruits. Vinaigrette can be used for any salad (Bonnie F)
