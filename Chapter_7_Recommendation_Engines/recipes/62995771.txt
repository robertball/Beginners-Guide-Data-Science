Title:
Beef-N-Rice Mexi-Meal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750955.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 red bell pepper, chopped
juice from 1 lime
2 tablespoons chili sauce
8 ounces shredded cheese
1/2 cup onion, chopped
14 ounces can corn, drained
1 cup salsa
1 teaspoon cumin
1 tablespoon oil
sour cream
flour tortillas, optional
steamed rice, amount as needed and optional

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown beef and onion; drain. Remove from skillet and set aside. Wipe down inside of skillet. Add 1 tablespoon oil to skillet and saute red peppers and corn for 3-4 minutes. Stir in beef, lime juice, salsa, chili sauce and cumin; cook for 5-6 minutes. Serve over rice or in flour tortilla. Top with shredded cheese and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Ann Brown
Description: 
Great meal by itself or wrapped up in a tortilla!
