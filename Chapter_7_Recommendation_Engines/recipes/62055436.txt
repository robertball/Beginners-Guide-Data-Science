Title:
Cheesy Onion Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sour Cream,Cheddar Cheese, Onions, Creamed Corn, Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix,Cayenne Pepper

Directions:
1 Box of Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix, follow directions on box
1 Medium Onion, sliced into rings
1 Cup Sour Cream
1 (8oz.)pkg. Of Cheddar
1 (15oz.) can of Creamed Corn
3 tbsp of Margarine or Butter
1/8 tsp of Cayenne Pepper
Make Jiffy Corm Muffin Mix according to package, and set aside. Use a 9x11 in rectangle pan. Preheat oven to 325.
Sautee onions in the 3 tablespoons of margarine or butter in a Medium. On medium heat add onions, cook until tender.
Mix Sour Cream, 4oz of Cheddar, Creamed Corn, Onions, and Cayenne pepper in a bowl, mix well! Spread mixture on top on corn muffin mix, spread evenly, top with leftover cheese. Bake until Corn Bread is golden brown. Glass dish is best to be able to check doneness of cornbread so it doesn't burn Cook for 25 to 30 minutes. Should not be gummy. Remove from oven, let set will firm as it cools. Serve warm. Great Side Dish!
Additions: Recipes is also tasty, is 1 cup of Cooked Broccoli is added.
