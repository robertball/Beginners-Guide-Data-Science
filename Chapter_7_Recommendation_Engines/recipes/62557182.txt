Title:
Tonja's Chicken and Corn Chowder Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  Cups Cooked Chicken
1  Can Cream of Potato Soup
1  Can Cream of Chicken Soup
1  Can Chicken Broth (15 oz.)
1  Can Mexican Corn (11 oz.)
1 oz Green Chiles
1  1/2 Cups Milk

Directions:
Boil the chicken in water until it is cooked and then cut it into small cubes.
Combine the chickent, potato soup, cream of chicken soup, chicken broth, mexican chiles and milk in a medium size pan.
Bring the soup to a boil and then reduce heat to a simmer for 10 minutes.
Enjoy!

Description: 
A delicious soup recipe that we have been making for years.  Everyone who tries this soup wants the recipe.  A great soup for anytime during the year!
