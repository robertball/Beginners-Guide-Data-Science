Title:
Grilled Cajun chicken thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken thighs
1/2 c kikkoman low sodium soy sauce
1 T hot pepper sauce
2 cloves garlic
1 T onion powder
1 T thyme
1/2 t ground red pepper

Directions:
rinse chicken thighs under cold water, pat dry - combine kikkoman, hot sauce, garlic onion powder, thyme and pepper in a plastic bag - add chicken thighs
close the bag and turn to coat - marinate for 30 minutes
preheat grill to medium
make drainage holes in sheets of reynolds wrap non-stick foil with a large grilling fork - place foil sheets with holes on grill rack with non-stick side towards food - immediately place chicken on foil - grill chicken 5 minutes - turn - continue grilling and turning every 5 minutes until chicken is tender - about 30 minutes
