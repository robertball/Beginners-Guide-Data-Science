Title:
Fresh Slow Cooker Sweet Potato's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 fresh sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced
2/3 cup butter, melted
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoon orange juice
4 eggs, beaten
1 cup milk
1 cup oats
2/3 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
4 teaspoons butter, melted
2/3 bag marshmallows

Directions:
Lightly grease a slow cooker.
In crock, stir 2/3 cup butter melted, white sugar and 1/4 cup brown sugar. Beat in orange juice, eggs and milk. Add sliced sweet potato and stir until coated in mixture.
In a small bowl, oats, 2/3 cup brown sugar, flour and 4 tablespoons butter melted. Spread the mixture over the sweet potatoes. Cover the slow cooker and cook on HIGH for 3 to 4 hours. During last 1/2 hour of cooking add marshmallows to top.

Submitter: 
Andrea Luhman
Description: 
Sliced Fresh Sweet potatoes slow-cooked with butter, sugar, orange juice, and milk, with marshmallow and oatmeal topping .Modified from: Slow Cooker Sweet Potato Casserole
