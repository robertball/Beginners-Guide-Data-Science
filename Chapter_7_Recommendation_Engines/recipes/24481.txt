Title:
Hot Scotch Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/748344.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) envelope instant hot chocolate mix
1 cup hot milk
1 fluid ounce butterscotch schnapps

Directions:
In a mug, dissolve instant chocolate mix with hot milk. Stir in butterscotch schnapps.

Submitter: 
SHADYEYEZ
Description: 
This is your basic cup of hot chocolate, but with a kick! Everyone has their own way of making hot chocolate - do that, then just add a bit of butterscotch schnapps. Top with whipped cream if desired.
