Title:
PK Hoagie Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4509872.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup warm water
2 TBL yeast
1 tsp. sugar
1/3 cup oil
3 TBL sugar
2 tsp. salt
2 cups hot water
7 cups flower

Directions:
Mix 1/2 cup warm water, yeast, and 1 tsp. sugar together and set aside to proof.
In large mixer, combine yeast mixture with oil, sugar, salt and hot water, mix well.
Add 3 cups of flour and mix, then add in the rest of the flour to make a nice bread dough.
Knead and put in a greased bowl and let rise for 20 minutes.
Punch down, and let rise again for another 20 minutes.
Punch down and form in the shape of hoagies on a greased cookie sheet, let raise.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
Best served cut in half and toasted on both sides.
Makes approximately 10 large hoagie rolls.

Submitter: 
Matthew Freestone
Description: 
French Bread Hoagie Buns, meant to mimic Papa Kelsey's hoagie rolls.  Got this recipe from http://marshasrecipes.blogspot.com/.
