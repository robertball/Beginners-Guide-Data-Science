Title:
Def Corn 5 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Green Giant mexi-corn
1 10 oz. package yellow rice
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 stick of butter
1 8 oz. package of shredded chedder cheese.

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.
Grease a 9x13 or similar sized baking dish.  Easiest way is to use the butter wrapper by wiping the inside of the wrapper onto the dish.
Prepare the rice, omitting any butter or oil called for.
Mix all the ingredients in the baking dish.
Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the edges of the casserole are bubbly.
