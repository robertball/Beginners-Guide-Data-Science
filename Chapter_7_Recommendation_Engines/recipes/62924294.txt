Title:
** Chicken & Rice AuGratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cut up chicken (or boneless breast halves & thighs)
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 box Uncle Ben's Original Wild Rice Mix
1 1/2 c. water
2 c. shredded cheddar cheese (divided)
1/4 c. chopped onion
Black pepper, to taste
Dash Tabasco sauce (opt.)

Directions:
Place chicken in 8x12 baking dish.
Combine soup, water, rice, 1 1/2 c. cheese, onion & pepper in medium bowl.
Pour over chicken.
Bake 350 degrees for 90 min.
Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 c. shredded cheese & return to oven until melted.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
This is adapted from a dish Mom made when I was a kid back in the 50's-60's.  It's the definition of comfort food for me.
