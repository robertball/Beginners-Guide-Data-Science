Title:
Salsa Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound tomatillos, diced
1/2 cup onion, chopped
2 teaspoons garlic, minced
1 sweet pepper, chopped
cilantro
cumin
coriander
lime juice
2 cups water

Directions:
Boil ingredients. Simmer 10-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
Eat this great sauce with grilled chicken and tortilla chips.
