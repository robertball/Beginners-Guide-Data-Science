Title:
Egg Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c Monterey Jack shredded cheese
2 tbsp flour
2 cans green chilies, drained
12 oz Jimmy Dean sausage, browned and drained
12 eggs
1 c milk

Directions:
Mix M Jack cheese with flour and layer in 9x13 baking dish.
Combine remaining ingredients and pour over M Jack layer.
Let set in fridge overnight.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45-60 minutes.
