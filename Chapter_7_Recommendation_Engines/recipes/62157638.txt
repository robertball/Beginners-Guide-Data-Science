Title:
Mom's Barbecue Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatloaf:
1½ lbs ground beef
1 cup fresh breadcrumbs
1 onion, finely chopped
1 egg, beaten
4 ounces (½ cup) tomato sauce
1½ tsp salt
¼ tsp pepper
Sauce for meatloaf:
12 ounces (1½ cups) tomato sauce
1½ cup water
3 tbsp vinegar
3 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp prepared mustard
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Mix together ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, egg, tomato sauce, salt and pepper.
Form into a loaf and place in a shallow pan, about 7x10 inches.
Combine the sauce ingredients and pour over the loaf.
Bake in a 350° oven for 1 hour 15 minutes.
Baste occasionally.

Submitter: 
rtigger4
Description: 
I grew up on this version of meatloaf and never understood why others looked down on it.  To add a little spice to this recipe, try substituting an 8-ounce can of Ro-Tel tomatoes for one of the cans of tomato sauce.
