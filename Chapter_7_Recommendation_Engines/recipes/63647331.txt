Title:
Zucchini Tots in Parmesan Cheese Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FOR THE CHEESE BOWLS:
1 tablespoon grated parmesan cheese for each cheese cup made
parchment paper
cookie sheet
offset spatula
mini muffin tins
FOR THE ZUCCHINI TOTS:
1 cup grated zucchini
1 beaten egg
1/4 medium onion, diced
1/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1/4 cup finely shredded sharp cheddar
1 tablespoon horseradish
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
For the cups:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Spread 1 tablespoon of grated cheese on the cookie sheet lined w/ parchment paper, leaving a2 inch space between each as they will spread out.
Bake for 5 minutes or until golden brown.
Remove from oven and cool for 20 seconds.
Using the offset spatula, carefully lift the parmesan disk off the paper and drape it over the outside BOTTOM of the muffin tin.
Cool completely and remove from the muffin tin.
Store in the fridge for 5 days in an air-tight container.
For the tots:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Spread grated zucchini on a clean dish towel.  Roll up and squeeze out as much moisture as possible.
Combine all ingredients and mix well.
Spray mini muffin tins with cooking spray
Spoon mixture into tins and bake for 16-18 minutes.
When cool, spoon into parmesan cheese cups for appetizers.
