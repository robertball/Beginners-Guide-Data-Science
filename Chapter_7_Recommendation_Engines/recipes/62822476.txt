Title:
Banana Fudgesicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 very ripe bananas, chunked
1/4 cup plain yogurt
1/2 cup milk
3 T cocoa

Directions:
In a blender, puree all ingredients together until smooth.  Pour into popsicle mold and freeze.

Submitter: 
IAMJENNIE
Description: 
Ripe bananas provide all the sweetness these creamy, rich popsicles need.  No sugar added so you can feel good about giving them to your kids on those hot summer days.
