Title:
Slow Cooker Italian Beef for Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups beef broth
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon onion salt
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 bay leaf
couple pepperoncini
2 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
1 (2.5 - 3 pound) rump roast or chuck roast

Directions:
Combine water with salt, ground black pepper, oregano, basil, onion salt, parsley, garlic powder, bay leaf, and salad dressing mix in a saucepan. Stir well, and bring to a boil.
Place roast in slow cooker, and pour salad dressing mixture over the meat.
Cover, and cook on Low for 10  hours, At about 8 hours,take out beef and shred and put back in pot along with a couple pepperoncini. When done, remove bay leaf. Serve on rolls with sharp provolone cheese
