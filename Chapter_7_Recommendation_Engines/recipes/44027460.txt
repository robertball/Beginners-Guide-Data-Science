Title:
TexMex Venison Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
1 lb ground venison
1 tsp minced garlic
1 small onion, chopped
2 T olive oil
1 package Spanish rice mix
1 can RoTel tomatoes with liquid
1 cankidney beans, rinsed
1 can sweet corn with liquid
2 cups water
1/2 package chili seasoning mix
sour cream
Shredded cheese

Directions:
Heat 2 T olive oil in large skillet. Brown venison. Add garlic and onion, cooking until onion is translucent. Drain and set aside.
Put 2 more T olive oil in the skillet. Stir in Spanish rice mix, cook until golden (about 5 min). Add meat and remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil, cover and reduce heat to med-low. Simmer 15 minutes. Remove cover, check rice. Cook any additional time needed until rice is tender.
Serve with sour cream and shredded cheese if desired.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
Good stew-like comfort food! Like a thick taco soup.
