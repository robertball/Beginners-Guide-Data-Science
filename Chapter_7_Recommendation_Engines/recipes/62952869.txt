Title:
Veggie Korma Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1  onion, diced
2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
6 cloves garlic, minced
3 potatoes, cubed
6 carrots, cubed
1 or 2 fresh jalapeno peppers seeded and sliced
1/4 c. ground unsalted cashews
1/2 to 1 c water
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 T. salt
2 1/2 tablespoons curry powder
1 1-2 cups broccoli, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 can coconut milk
1 bunch fresh cilantro for garnish

Directions:
Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the onion, and cook until tender. Add ginger and garlic, and continue cooking 1 minute.
Add potatoes, carrots, jalapeno, cashews, water and tomato sauce. Season with salt and curry powder. Cover and simmer 15 minutes, or until potatoes are tender, stirring frequently.
Stir broccoli,  green bell pepper, red bell pepper, and cream into the skillet. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 10 minutes. Garnish with cilantro to serve.
