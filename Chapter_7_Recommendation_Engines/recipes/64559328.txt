Title:
basic burger Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 1/2 # lean ground beef
3 T. finely grated onion
2 tsp. worcestershire sauce
3/4 tsp. dry mustard
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients gently. Shape into 4 patties. Grill.
