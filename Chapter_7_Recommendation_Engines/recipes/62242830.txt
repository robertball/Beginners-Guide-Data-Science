Title:
Greek-Style Baked Salmon II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (5 ounce) salmon fillets, with skin
1/8 cup olive oil
1/2 can of diced tomatoes, drained
1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
8 green olives, sliced
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Brush each salmon fillet on all sides with olive oil and arrange into the bottom of a stone baking dish with the skin side facing down. Scatter the tomatoes, feta cheese basil, and olives over the fillets; sprinkle with the lemon juice.
Bake in the preheated oven until the salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 20 minutes.
