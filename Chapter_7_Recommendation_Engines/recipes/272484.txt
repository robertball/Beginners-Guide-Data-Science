Title:
Light Avocado Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6531146.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large avocado, diced
1 mango, diced
1/2 red onion, diced
5 strawberries, diced
1 lime, juiced
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix avocado, mango, red onion, strawberries, and lime juice in bowl. Add salt and pepper to taste.
