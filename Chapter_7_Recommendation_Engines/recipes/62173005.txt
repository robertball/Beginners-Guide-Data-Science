Title:
Marble cheesecake bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pkg super moist marble cake mix
1/3C butter(softened)
1 egg
2pkg (8oz)cream cheese
3/4C sour cream
1/3C water
2/3C sugar
2 eggs

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. Grease a 13x9in pan. Reserve 1C cake mix(dry) Blend the remaining cake mix, butter and one egg on low speed until dough forms. Pat in bottom of pan.Bake until golden and crust begins to pull away from the sides.15-20min. Beat cream cheese and sour cream mixture on low speed until smooth. Blend the reserved cake mix,water,sugar,2eggs into the cream cheese mixture scrapping bowl constanly.30sec. Beat on medium speed 2 min. Reserve 1 1/4C cream cheese mixture; pour remaining mixture over the crust. Blend the cream cheese mixture and marbling mixture in small bowl until well combined. Drop dark batter by gernerous Tbsp. randomly in 6-8 mounds onto yellow batter. Cut through batter with a knife in s shaped curves in one continuous motion. Turn pan and repeat. Bake until set and cake springs back when touched in the center.30-36min.  Refriderate until firm.
