Title:
Air Fryer French Toast Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5641722.jpg
Ingredients:
4 slices thick day old bread
1/3 cup milk
2 large eggs
1 tbs butter, melted
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp cinnamon
powdered sugar

Directions:
Cut each slice of bread into thirds
Mix milk, eggs, butter, vanilla and cinnamon in a bowl
Dip each stick in the milk mixture and place in parchment lined basket of air fryer, making sure they are not touching; cook in batches if necessary
Preheat air fryer to 370 degrees
Cook for 6 minutes, flip and cook for an additional 3 minutes
Sprinkle each stick with powdered sugar

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, these French Toast Sticks make a delicious breakfast!
