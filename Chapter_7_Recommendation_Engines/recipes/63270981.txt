Title:
Slow Cooker Turkey Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 pound) bone-in turkey breast
1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
l Stick butter, melted
Turkey broth

Directions:
Rinse the turkey breast and pat dry. Cut off any excess skin, but leave the skin covering the breast. Rub onion soup mix all over outside of the turkey and under the skin. Place in a slow cooker.Pour l stick butter, melted over turkey.  Add l can turkey broth. Cover, and cook on High for 1 hour, then set to Low, and cook for 7 hours.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
Quick and easy way to cook turkey in the slow cooker. With only two ingredients, the only hard part is waiting.Modified from: Slow Cooker Turkey Breast
