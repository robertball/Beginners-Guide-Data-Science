Title:
Salsa Sweettreats way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large tomatoes, quartered
3 jalapeno's, seeds removed, halved
1 white onion, quartered
4 garlic cloves
4 dried New Mexico chili pods
14.5 ounce diced tomatoes with chili's
2 tablespoons sugar
pinch of cumin
1/4 cup cilantro
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Roast the tomatoes, onions, two jalapeno's and garlic in the oven at 375 for 20 minutes. In a pan add one tablespoon of oil and the New Mexico chili pod's. Toast on each side. Remove from heat and fill pan with water and let sit for 30 minutes. In a blender add roasted vegetables, spices, cilantro, jalapeno(seeded) sugar, diced tomatoes. Remove stems from dried chili's and add to the blender. Blend until smooth. Chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour.

Smokey homemade salsa. One of my families favorites.
