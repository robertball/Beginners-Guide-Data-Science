Title:
Cauliflower Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Small head of cauliflower
3 rashers of bacon chopped
3 spring onions chopped
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup flour
1 cup milk
1 egg
pinch salt
1 tbsn grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cut cauliflower into heads and boil till tender. Drain
Break cauliflower into small pieces and add bacon & spring onions. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
To make batter, sift flour in separate bowl, and beated egg, milk, parmesan & pinch salt. Wisk until smooth. Add to cauliflower mix.
Shallow fry in batches until golden brown on both sides.
