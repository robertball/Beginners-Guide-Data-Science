Title:
Magic Wands for Halloween Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/509535.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) container vanilla frosting
3 drops orange food coloring, or as needed (optional)
1 (12 ounce) package salted pretzel rods
1/2 cup multicolored candy sprinkles, or to taste

Directions:
Separate 1/2 cup frosting between 2 bowls. Add orange food coloring to 1 bowl; mix until blended.
Line a medium glass container with waxed paper.
Spread frosting over the top half of each pretzel rod. Roll in sprinkles until heavily covered. Place rods upright in the prepared container to harden and dry.

Submitter: 
lkmiec
Description: 
Fantastically festive for Halloween, Harry Potter-themed parties, or your daughter's Princess birthday party! Use frosting and sprinkles of any color variety you prefer. Tons of fun to do with the kids and a great presentation!
