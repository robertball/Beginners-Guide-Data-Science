Title:
Creamy Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg frozen egg noodles
6 frozen chicken breasts
4 cubes chicken boullion
1 pkg country style frozen vegetables (corn, carrots, green beans)
1 Tablespoon Italian seasoning
1 cup milk
4 cloves chopped garlic
1/2 onion, chopped
1 Tablespoon olive oil
6 cups of water
2 cans cream of chicken soup

Directions:
Boil chicken in water with boullion. Once chicken is cooked, remove and shred with forks. Skim any fat off top of pot and return chicken to pot. Meanwhile saut√© onion in olive oil with garlic until semi transparent. Add to chicken mixture. Add frozen vegetables, Italian seasoning, cream of chicken soup and milk. Simmer until noodles are tender.

Submitter: 
Shannon Krueger
Description: 
Creamy chicken noodle soup is great alone or over mashed potatoes.
