Title:
Caramelized Apple Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 red delicious apples, sliced
2 tbsp butter.
2 packets splenda
11 oz. baby spinach
1 red onion, sliced
1 c. cup walnuts,pecans, or sliced almonds
1 c. gorgonzola or feta cheese, crumbled

Directions:
Place skillet on medium heat, melt the butter, then add the sliced apple. Cook for about 10 minutes turning the apples. Remove from heat and sprinkle with the splenda and stir. Place baby spinach, red onion, nuts, and crumbled cheese into a bowl. Place the caramelized apple on top and serve with your favorite vinaigrette.
