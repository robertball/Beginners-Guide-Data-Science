Title:
Roasted Garlic Flat Iron Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 garlic clovesGarlic
2 lb Flat Iron Steak
1/2 c Olive Oil
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
1) Arrange garlic cloves on a sheet pan then pour olive oil over galic cloves; bake until golden brown, stiring once to prevent burning.
2) After cloves have cooled place garlic and olive oil into a food processor, process until a paste is formed.
3)Sprinkle Steaks with desired amount of Salt and Pepper, then rub steak on both sides with the Garlic Paste
4) Barbeque or Pan fry steaks
