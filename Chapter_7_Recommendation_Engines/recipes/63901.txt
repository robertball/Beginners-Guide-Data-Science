Title:
Fruit and Nut Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977693.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package cornbread stuffing
1 2/3 cups water
2 teaspoons HERB-OX® Chicken Flavored Bouillon Granules
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup dried cranberries and/or raisins
1/4 cup toasted, chopped pecans

Directions:
In large saucepan, combine seasoning packet from stuffing mix, water, bouillon and butter.  Bring mixture to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.  Stir in stuffing and cranberries or raisins.  Remove from heat and let stand covered for 5 minutes.  Add pecans and fluff stuffing with a fork.

Submitter: 
HERB-OX® Bouillon
Description: 
This easy stuffing recipe is great for a weeknight supper. Cornbread stuffing is enhanced with dried cranberries and toasted pecans.
