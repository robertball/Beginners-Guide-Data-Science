Title:
Creamy Chicken Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) double pie crust
3 Tbs cooking oil
1 small onion, diced
1 C sour cream
1 (10.75 oz) can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 C shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 (16 oz ) package frozen vegetables, for soup (including potatoes)
2 green onion tops, chopped
1 1/2 C chopped cooked chicken or turkey

Directions:
Press bottom crust into 9-inch pie plate.  Do not pake.
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
For filling, heat oil in small saucepan; add onion and saute until tender.  In a large bowl, mix sour cream, soup, cheese, salt and pepper; stir to combine.  Add sauted onions, vegetables, green onions and chicken; mix well.  Spoon filling into unbaked pie crust.  Cover pie with top crust; flute edges.  Cut slits in top crust to allow steam to escape.
Bake for 25-35 minutes or until crust is golden brown.  Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
