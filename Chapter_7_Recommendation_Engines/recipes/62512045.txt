Title:
Spaghetti Taco's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507465.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 jar of your favorite spaghetti sauce
1 box of spaghetti noodles
hard shell tacos
optional:
black olives
mushrooms
shredded cheese
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef; drain then add spaghetti sauce and mushrooms.
Cook spaghetti noodles according to package directions. (To test if they are done, take one noodle and throw against cabinet door. If it sticks, the noodles are ready.) Drain the noodles.
Mix meat sauce mixture and noodles together.
Put spaghetti in a taco shell and top with olives and shredded cheese.
Enjoy.
