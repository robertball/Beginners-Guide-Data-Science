Title:
Steak & Mushroom Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb thinly sliced steak, cut into strips
2 tbsp butter 1 cup chopped yellow onion
1 6 oz package sliced baby portabello mushrooms
1/2 6 oz. package sliced white button mushrooms
3-4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp beef boulion granules
1 cup very hot water
1 cup sour cream
1 tbsp flour
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp pepper
1 package egg noodles, cooked according to package directions

Directions:
Dissolve the boulion in the hot water and set aside.
Stir the flour into the sour cream.  Add the sour cream mixture to the boulion mixture and blend.
In a large, deep saucepan, melt the butter and then add half of the meat and cook just until cooked through.
Push all of the cooked meat to the outer edges of the pan and add the rest of the meat.
Add the mushrooms, garlic and onions on top and cook, stirring ocassionally, until meat is cooked through and onions and mushrooms are tender.
Season with salt and pepper.
Add the sour cream mixture and cook until the sauce is thickened and bubbly.
Serve over egg noodles.

Submitter: 
CHRISTYG
Description: 
I developed this recipe about 10 years ago and it's still one of my family's favorites.  Lean steak, sauteed onions, mushroom and garlic with a light sour cream sauce.
