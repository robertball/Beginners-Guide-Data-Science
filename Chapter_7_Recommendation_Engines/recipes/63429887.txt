Title:
Pork Roast with Sauerkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pork Loin Roast- 1 1/2 to 2 pounds
1 Jar Sauerkraut
4-6 New Potatos
1 Large Onion

Directions:
Place pork into crockpot
Cut up potatoes into 1 1/2-2 inch pieces
Same with Onion and place on top of Pork
Pack whole jar of Sauerkraut on top
Cover and cook on high for 4-5 hours or on low for 8-10 hrs

Submitter: 
Ron Turner
Description: 
Another recipe from my Mom. Simple and quick- you can start it in the morning before work and come home to a whole delicious meal ready to eat. It's another comfort food- great for a cold winter day. 
I like to serve it with a garden salad.
