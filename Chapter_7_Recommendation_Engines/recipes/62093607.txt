Title:
Rosemary Dijon Prime Rib Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 -   5 lb rib roast
3 -   Garlic cloves
4 oz  Dijon mustard
Kosher salt
Rosemary leaves

Directions:
Cut garlic into small slivers
Make small cuts all along to outside edge of the roast.
Place garlic slivers into the cuts.
Heavily salt outside edge of roast.
Thinly coat the outside edge with dijon mustard.
Sprinkle with a generous amount of rosemary.
Place bone side down in a baking dish.
Cook at 450 degrees for 30 minutes.
Turn the oven down to 350 degrees
Place meat thermometer into thickest part of the meat and away from the bone.
Cook to desired doneness
140 degrees for rare
155 degrees for medium
170 degrees for well done

Submitter: 
Sandy
Description: 
This wonderfully easy prime rib will make you think twice about heading out to a restaurant.
