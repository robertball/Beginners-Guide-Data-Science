Title:
Arugula, Fennel, and Orange Salad II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons mandarin orange juice (from can)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 cup olive oil
1 bunch arugula
1 can mandarin oranges
1 bulb fennel bulb, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons sliced Kalamata olives

Directions:
Whisk together the honey, lemon juice, mandarin orange juice, salt, and pepper; slowly add the olive oil while continuing to whisk.
Place the arugula in the bottom of a salad bowl; scatter the orange segments, fennel slices, and olives over the arugula; drizzle the dressing over the salad to serve.
