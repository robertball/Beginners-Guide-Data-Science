Title:
Teriyaki Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984978.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7.6 ounce) package Gorton's® Grilled Fillets (any flavor)
3 cups cooked rice
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 large Spanish onions
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms
3 stalks celery
1 bunch scallions
1 (8 ounce) can bamboo shoots
3 tablespoons water
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup strong hot chicken bouillon
3 cups raw spinach leaves

Directions:
Cook rice according to the directions on the package.
Slice onions, mushrooms, and celery thinly. Cut scallions in 1 1/2 inch lengths. Drain bamboo shoots, wash and dry spinach and discard stems.
Heat oil in wok or an electric skillet. Stir fry all vegetables except spinach for 3 minutes. Add water, soy sauce bouillon and Grilled Fillets. Cover and let simmer for 10 minutes. Add spinach leaves and cook for 3 more minutes.
Cut fillets into bite sized pieces, place vegetables and fish on rice and serve.
