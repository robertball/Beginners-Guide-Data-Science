Title:
Corn & Cheddar Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. sugar
4 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup milk
2 tbsp. melted butter or vegetable oil
1 (approx. 418g) can creamed style corn
3 tbsp. chopped fresh dill or 2 tsp. (or less) dried dill
green onion or chives, chopped - optional
2 cups (or less) grated cheddar cheese
sour cream, to serve

Directions:
In large bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt.
In another bowl, beat eggs, stir in milk & butter or oil.
Pour milk mixture into flour mixture. Add corn & dill (& onions, if using).
Stir just until blended (don't worry about a few lumps).
Pour batter onto med-hot skillet (approx. 1/4 cup for each pancake).
Sprinkle each with a few tbsp. of cheese.
When bubbles pop on surface, flip & cook until cheese is crusty.
Serve hot with sour cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A favorite side dish of my father-in-law's. A unique pancake that compliments a main dish meat. Skip the spuds and try these!
