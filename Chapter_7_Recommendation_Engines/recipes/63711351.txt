Title:
No Sugar Added Peanut Butter Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whipping cream
2 cups skim milk
1 1/2 cups peanut butter
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
3/4 cup Splenda Measure

Directions:
Mix all together in a blender.  Chill for a few hours.  Then process in ice cream maker.
