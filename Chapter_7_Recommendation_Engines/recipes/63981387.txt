Title:
Cheesy Casserole with Assorted Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1104676.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 medium onion, chopped
1 cup mixed peppers (green, red, orange, yellow), chopped
1 tablespoon olive oil
cooking spray
4 slices whole wheat bread cut into 1/2 inch cubes
1 cup shredded low fat Cheddar cheese
1 cup low fat cottage cheese
3/4 cup Egg Beaters (or 3 large eggs)
3/4 cup almond milk
1/4 teaspoon ground mustard
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon ground pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Heat olive oil in a frying pan over medium high heat. Add onions and peppers, stirring and cooking until vegetables start to soften.
Spray a large casserole or baking dish with cooking spray. Spread the bread cubes evenly. Sprinkle cheddar cheese over bread cubes.
In a mixer, beat cottage cheese and Egg Beaters until the mixture is smooth. Blend in almond milk, mustard, nutmeg and pepper. Stir in onion and peppers.
Pour egg mixture over bread cubes. Gently press egg mixture into bread cubes and let stand for 10 minutes.
Bake for 50 to 60 minutes or until center of casserole is set and top is golden brown.

Description: 
Assorted peppers add both flavor and color to this rich-tasting but healthy dish, which can be served for family or part of an elegant buffet for breakfast or as a main dish served with a mixed salad.
