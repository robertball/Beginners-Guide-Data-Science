Title:
Bacon, Apple & Brie Panini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices bread
2 tablespoons butter, room temperature
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard (optional)
3 strips Brie cheese, thinly sliced
1 Fuji apple, thinly sliced
4 strips bacon, cooked

Directions:
Heat grill pan or Panini press to medium-high.
Meanwhile, butter one side of each slice of bread. If including, spread mustard on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread (otherwise, leave it unbuttered).
Place strips of cheese on the unbuttered side of bread. Place thinly sliced apple on top of the cheese. Top with bacon and place buttered slice of bread on top.
Place into into Panini press or grill pan and press down. Grill until lightly browned on both sides.

Submitter: 
Angela
Description: 
I had a panini like this at a winery in the Napa Valley, so this is my best attempt to recreate it! These make an amazing lunch and would also be great for bridal showers, baby showers, etc...all you have to do is cut them up into bite-size pieces and insert a toothpick!
