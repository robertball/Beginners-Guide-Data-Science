Title:
Thrive - Chicken Coconut Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken breast, chunked or cut into strips
1 cup coconut milk
2 cups broccoli, chopped
1 cup mushrooms, washed, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 Tbs curry powder
1 large clove garlic, chopped
1 tsp fresh ginger, minced
Dash of cayenne pepper

Directions:
Wash chicken well.
In a large skillet, add coconut milk, chicken, broccoli, mushrooms and onion.  Cook on medium high heat for 15 minutes.
Add in curry powder, garlic, ginger and cayenne pepper to taste.  Turn heat down to medium low and cook for another 15 minutes.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Cyndi Martin McAlpine
Description: 
This is a nice, spicy chix dish.  Serve over Basmati, or long grain white rice is best.
