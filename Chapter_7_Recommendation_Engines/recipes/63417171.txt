Title:
Corn Bread Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/922747.jpg
Ingredients:
5 (8.5 ounce) packages 'Jiffy' corn bread mix
1 1/2 cups chopped onion
3 cups celery (w/leaves)
1/2 cups butter
1 (32 ounce) box chicken stock
1 tablespoon dried sage
1 tablespoon dried thyme leaves
1 tablespoon pepper

Directions:
Prepare corn bread mix according to package directions in cast iron casserole (can be made night before.) Crumble cornbread when ready to make dressing.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter and saute the celery and onion until soft.
In a large bowl, combine the celery, onions, crumbled corn bread, chicken stock, sage, thyme & pepper; mix well.
Place into dish and bake at 375 degrees until hot & bubbly about 45 minutes.
