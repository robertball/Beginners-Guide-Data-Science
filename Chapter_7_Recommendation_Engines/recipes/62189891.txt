Title:
mexican wedding cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/310474.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c butter
1/2 c powdered sugar
2 c All purpose flour
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
cream butter and sugar together.  Mix in salt and vanilla.  Add flour.  Roll into balls and bake at 375 for 13-14 minutes.
When cookies are almost cooled roll into powder sugar.
