Title:
Fresh Corn and Zucchini Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/877023.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups zucchini, shreaded & squeezed dry
1 cup fresh corn kernels (approx 1 large ear corn)
1/4 cup finely diced onion
3/4 cup fresh shredded Parmesan cheese
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon each of season salt, lemon pepper, & garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 cup milk
2 eggs, beaten
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions:
Combine zucchini, corn, onion, and Parmesan cheese in a large bowl. Whisk flour, baking powder, milk, eggs, and melted butter in a separate bowl until moistened; pour batter over zucchini mixture. Stir until mixed.
Heat vegetable oil in a griddle or skillet over medium-high heat. Drop 1/3 cup portions of the zucchini batter onto the griddle; pan-fry until browned, about 5 minutes on each side.Garnish with sliced green onions and more fresh parmesan cheese,if desired

Submitter: 
Dee Stillwell
Description: 
Corn, zucchini, onion, and Parmesan cheese are pan-fried in these delectable pancakes that are perfect for a light meal or side dish.Modified from: Fresh Corn and Zucchini Pancakes
