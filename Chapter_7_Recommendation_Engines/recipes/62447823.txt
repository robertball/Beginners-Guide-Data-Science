Title:
Slow Cooker Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb Sausage or Beef
3/4 cup Corn Meal
1.5 cups Milk
1 egg
1 can 14.5oz Diced Tomatoes
1 can Corn
1 pkg Taco Seasoning
1 cup Mexican Cheese Blend

Directions:
Use slow cooker liner or spray slow cooker with non-stick spray. Cook sausage or beef and place in slow cooker.
In another bowl combine corn meal, milk, and egg. Stir into slow cooker. Add tomatoes, corn and taco seasoning. Stir well.
Cover and cook low for 4-6 hours. Top with cheese 5 minutes before serving.
