Title:
Sweet Potato Chard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T.  Butter (unsalted)
2 T.  EVOO
4 medium  Sweet Potatos, sliced
1 T.  Cinnamon Sugar
1/2 Red Onion, chopped (divide)
1/4 Cup  Brown Sugar
1 bunch  Organic Swiss Chard, chopped
1/3 Cup  Parmesian Cheese, shredded (optional)

Directions:
Melt 2 Tbls. Butter with 2 Tbls. EVO in large saucepan on Medium heat then swirl to coat pan.
Add sweet potatos & brown (about 3 mins.) then turn over to brown other side, sprinkle Cinnamon sugar, add red onion, then brown sugar & add 1 Tbls. butter(cook additional 3 minutes).
Add remaining red onion, and swiss chard, butter, stiring frequently (saute approx 2 to 3 minutes).
Remove from heat, mix in parmesian, immediately transfer to serving dish & serve.
