Title:
Laurie's Special Chunky Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/891569.jpg
Ingredients:
4 apples - peeled, cored and cut into bite sized chunks
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 cup water
4 tablespoons brown sugar

Directions:
Place apples in a medium saucepan over medium low heat. Sprinkle with cinnamon, then add water and cook until the apple bits become soft and mushy to your liking.
Stir in brown sugar and mix well; if desired, top with ice cream and serve.
