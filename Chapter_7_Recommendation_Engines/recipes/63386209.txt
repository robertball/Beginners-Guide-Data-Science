Title:
Spiced Almonds I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c whole unsalted almonds
1 t ground cumin
1/4 t garlic powder
1/4 t cayenne pepper
1/2 t salt
2 t olive oil
1/4 t hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Toast the almonds in a dry skillet over a medium heat stirring frequently, until fragrant, about 3 minutes.  Transfer the almonds to a bowl.
In a small bowl, stir together the cumin, garlic, cayenne pepper and salt.  Heat the oil in the skillet over medium heat.  Stire the spices into the oil and cook, stirringuntil warm, about 30 minutes.  Add the almonds and cook, stirring frequently, until the nuts are warm and the spices are evenly distributed.  Add the hot pepper sauce and stir to distribute.  remove the almonds from the pan and allow them to cool before serving.
