Title:
Easy Crustless Custard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
3 tbsp. flour
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
pinch of salt
1 tbsp. butter, softened
2 cups milk

Directions:
Mix together in bowl in order given or put everything in a blender & pulse till evenly mixed.
Pour into a greased pie plate (no pastry needed).
Bake @ 350 x 50 minutes, or more, till set and not jiggly or tests done when inserting a knife comes out clean.
Can be served warm or cold, with or without toppings of choice (ie. fresh or canned fruit, compote, etc.). We usually serve it without toppings.
Store in fridge.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A simple way to make custard.
Can be prepared in a blender to make it even quicker.
