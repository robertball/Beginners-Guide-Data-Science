Title:
RICE PUDDING IN A CROCKPOT Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of sticky rice
2 cans of evaporated milk
1/2 cup of sugar
1 tsp of vanilla
1-2 cinnamon sticks

Directions:
Fill the slow cooker with all the ingredients. Make sure that the rice is not cooked. Turn the slow cooker on for 2.5 hours on low heat.
Stir occasionally.
Add a cup of raisins or chopped apples for a nice variation.

Submitter: 
LISAMARIE
Description: 
A wonderful creamy rice pudding done in a crockpot for those long nights by the fireplace or as a special holiday treat on Christmas Eve.
