Title:
Tejano Style Shrimp Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked medium shrimp, chilled
1/2 large cucumber, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
or diced celery
1/2 large tomato, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
8 green onions, thinly sliced
1 ounce fresh cilantro, finely chopped
1 serrano pepper, thinly sliced
1 diced avocado (opt.)
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1 lime

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine shrimp, cucumber, tomato, green onion, cilantro, and serrano pepper. Stir in tomato sauce, and vinegar. Squeeze lime juice over mixture.

Submitter: 
Cindy Lee Thurman
Description: 
For those of you unable to make it to south Texas, A shrimp cocktail made Tex Mex style to include cilantro and chile serrano for that authentic flavor that will blow your tastebuds away, without leaving your home! Serve with saltine crackers.Modified from: Tejano Style Shrimp Cocktail
