Title:
Peach Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042644.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb of egg noddles
3/4 cup of brown sugar
1&1/2 cup peach preserves
1&1/2 stick of butter
4-6 eggs

Directions:
Boil noodles-drain
mix all the ingredients and bake 1hr. in 350 oven in a 9x13 greased baking dish. let sit and cool some before cutting.
