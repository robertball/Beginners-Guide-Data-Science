Title:
Matt's Garlic Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp. garlic powder
1 small head of cabbage, chopped
1 small green pepper, chopped
1 med. carrot, shaved
1 med. cucumber, thinly sliced
2 small tomatoes, cut in wedges (optional)
2 tsp salt
1/4 c. vinegar
1/2 c. canola oil

Directions:
Put garlic powder in small bowl.
Add just enough water to make a runny paste.
Put all vegetables in a bowl and pour garlic mixture over.
Stir and let stand 10 minutes.
Mix salt, vinegar, and oil together and pour over vegetables.
Mix thoroughly and refrigerate overnight.

Submitter: 
Robin
Description: 
Fresh salad with healthy ingredients for garlic lovers.  Similar to the one served at a local restaurant that was destroyed in a fire, hence the name Matt's as a tribute to the Restaurant.
