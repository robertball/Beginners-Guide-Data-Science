Title:
Mo's Spicy Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 pounds beef stew meat, cubed
1 package Beef Stew Seasoning Mix
3 cups water
2 large potatoes, cubed
1 small onion, chopped
1 can (10 oz) Ro*tel Diced Tomatoes and Green Chilies
1 tsp. Franks Red Hot Sauce
1 cup frozen corn
Flour
Garlic Salt (to taste)
Black Pepper (to taste)
1 tbsp. Olive Oil

Directions:
Combine flour, garlic salt and pepper.  Dredge stew meat in flour mixture.  Heat about 1 tbsp. olive oil in pan.  Add batches of stew meat to the oil and brown.  Add more olive oil to pan if needed for additional batches.  In a crock pot, mix stew seasoning with water.  Add beef, corn,  potatoes, diced tomatoes and Franks Red Hot Sauce.  Cook on High 4-5 hours.

Submitter: 
MOCOOKS2
Description: 
Wanting to do something different with the standard beef stew, I added a spicy twist to this family's comfort food.  It's quick and easy and sure to please the hungry!
