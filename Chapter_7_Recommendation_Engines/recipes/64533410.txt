Title:
Low Calorie Italian Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3073343.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package ground, lean Jennie O turkey breast
1 1/2 cups marinara sauce
1 cup cooked brown rice
2 medium zucchini, sliced into thing long strips
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp oregano
1 tbsp minced garlic
1/2 cup low calorie mozzarella
1 tbsp parmesan for sprinkling

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Prepare a square 9x9 baking dish with cooking spray
In a skillet, brown turkey with the seasonings
When the turkey is half way through browning, add marinara sauce to the pan
Remove from heat once browned completed
Then to the baking dish add a small amount of sauce to the bottom of the dish
Layer zucchini slices covering the bottle of the dish
Add rice, then turkey mixture, then some cheese
Repeat layers until everything is used (mine had 3 layers)
Sprinkle top with parmesan if you like
Cover with foil & bake in the oven 30 minutes
Remove foil then bake another 15 minutes
Allow casserole to sit 10 minutes before serving or portioning for the freezer
