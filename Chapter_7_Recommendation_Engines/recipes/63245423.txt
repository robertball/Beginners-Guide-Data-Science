Title:
You Drive Me Bananas Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/847291.jpg
Ingredients:
Vanilla Yogurt
1 Banana
4-5 Strawberries
2 tbs Coconut water (Pineapple-flavored)
1 scoop Vanilla-flavored Whey Protein
1 tsp Lemon Juice
4 tbs honey*
ice*

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in blender, using the "Blender" setting.
Can place in freezer for few mins to chill.

Submitter: 
Leslie Ivery
Description: 
A very healthy smoothie that is SO great, you won't even know it's healthy for you :)
Very kid-friendly...my toddler brother LOVES it!
