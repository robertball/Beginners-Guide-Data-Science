Title:
Honey Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup Balsamic Vinegar
1 small onion
1 T. soy sauce
3 T honey
1 T sugar (or Splenda)
2 cloves minced garlic
½ t. red pepper flakes
2/3 c. EVOO, or Canola or Vegetable Oil

Directions:
In a blender put in all ingredients except oil.  Process on high speed gradually infusing the oil.  Process for 2 minutes.
Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
CATHYRAE
Description: 
I kept trying to duplicate the delicious Honey Balsamic Vinaigrette from TGI Fridays.  This is very very close.
