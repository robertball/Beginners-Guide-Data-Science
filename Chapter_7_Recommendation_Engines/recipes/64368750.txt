Title:
SonShine Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup unsweetened apple sauce
1 1/2 cup peanut butter
2/3 cups packed brown sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 eggs
1/3 cup water
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
2 cups quick cooking oats
1/2 cup wheat germ
1/4 cup ground flax seed
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease two baking sheets or line them with parchment paper.
In a large bowl, using an electric mixer, beat together applesauce, peanut butter, brown sugar, and vanilla until creamy. Beat in eggs and water.
In a separate bowl sift together flours,salt, cinnamon and baking soda. Combine Oats,wheat germ and flax seed.  Mix dry ingredients into peanut butter mixture. Stir in raisins or dates, if desired.  Drop by 1 1/2 tsp. dough scoop onto prepared baking sheets.
Bake in preheated oven for 18 to 20 minutes. Cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to cooling racks. Store in an airtight container.
