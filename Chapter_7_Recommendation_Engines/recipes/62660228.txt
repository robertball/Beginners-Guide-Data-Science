Title:
Creamy Chicken, Bacon and Tomato Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups whole wheat Bowtie pasta, uncooked
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
3 slices Bacon, cooked, crumbled
1/2 Purple Onion, chopped
4 gloves of Garlic, minced
1 (19 ounce) can Italian-style diced tomatoes, undrained
1/2 (250 g) package Philadelphia Brick Cream Cheese, cubed
1/2 cup water
1/4 teaspoon pepper
3 tablespoons Kraft 100% Parmesan Grated Cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed on package.
Meanwhile, cook chicken in large skillet in a little bit of bacon grease on medium heat 5 to 6 min. or until chicken is done, stirring occasionally. Add onion and garlic before the chicken is completely browned. Add next 7 ingredients; mix well. Cook 3 min. or until cream cheese is completely melted and mixture is well blended, stirring frequently.
Drain pasta; place in large bowl. Add sauce; mix lightly. Sprinkle with Parmesan.

Submitter: 
The Native Cowgirl
Description: 
Chunks of tender chicken are simmered in a creamy tomato sauce with bacon, purple onions, and garlic then tossed with farfalle pasta, and sprinkled with Parmesan cheese for a quick and tasty weeknight dinner.Modified from: Creamy Chicken, Bacon and Tomato Pasta
