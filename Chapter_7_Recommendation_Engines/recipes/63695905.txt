Title:
Big Ray's Hearty Hamburger Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130194.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 pound ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
4 cups diced cabbage
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1-1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp paprika
2 cups milk
Hot mashed potatoes
Additional paprika, optional

Directions:
In a large saucepan, cook the beef and onion over medium heat until the meat is no longer pink and onion is tender; drain. Add cabbage; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Sprinkle with flour, salt and paprika; mix well.
Gradually add milk. Bring to boil; boil and stir for 2 minutes. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10-12 minutes or until the cabbage is tender. Serve over potatoes. Sprinkle with paprika if desired.
