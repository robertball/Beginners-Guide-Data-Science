Title:
Easy Thai Chicken Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/495870.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Prepared Thin Pizza Crust (Boboli)
1/2 Sliced Red Bell Pepper
1/4-1/2 Cup Duck Sauce
2 Cups Shredded Provolone Cheese
1 10 oz Package, Sliced Cooked Chicken
1/2 Cup Thai Peanut Sauce
Palmfull of Chopped, Fresh Cilantro
1 Tablespoon Crushed Red Pepper

Directions:
Pre-heat Oven to 450
Spread duck sauce over pizza crust.
Toss crushed red pepper over crust.
Evenly place sliced pepper.
Toss cooked chicken with peanut sauce.
Top pizza with shredded provolone.
Sprinkle Salt and Pepper to taste.
Bake 8-10 mins. until cheese is melted.
Before serving, spread chopped Cilantro.
