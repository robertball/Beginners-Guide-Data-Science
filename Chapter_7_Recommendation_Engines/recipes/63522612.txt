Title:
Slow Cooker Spinach Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
1 Yellow Onion
1 Jar Spaghetti Sauce
1 Box Lasagna Noodles
10 oz Goat Cheese
1/2 cup Mozzarella or Pecorino Cheese
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
1/2 cup Milk
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Brown ground beef in skillet over medium heat. Thinly slice onions and add to skillet. Cook until soft & caramelized. Add spaghetti sauce.
Combine cheeses, salt & pepper. Add a few tablespoons of milk until the consistency is spreadable.
Spread a thin layer of spaghetti sauce mixture on bottom of crockpot. Place a double layer of uncooked lasagna noodles on top, breaking to fit. Dollop cheese mixture on top of noodles and spread evenly. Put several handfuls of spinach on top of cheese. Repeat until all ingredients have been used. Finish with sauce.
Cook on low for 4 hours.

Submitter: 
AFulton
Description: 
You'll never know this was made in a crockpot! Just as good as traditional lasagna with a fraction of the prep work and the added nutrition of spinach.
