Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/497116.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Packet of taco seasoning - 1.25 oz (can substitute 3T of chili powder, 1T salt)
1 packet of Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing (not dip)
1 Cup of water
2 lb browned and drained ground beef
1 can of Golden Hominy
1 can of Corn
1 can of Pinto Beans
1 can of kidney beans
1 can of Rotel
2 cans of diced tomatoes

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients with a whisk in soup pot so that there are no clumps.
Add above into soup pot with the 2 packets of dissolved seasoning mixes. Simmer. Serve with shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream and plain fritoes.

Submitter: 
pmackercher
Description: 
This is an amazing soup that is quick and easy to make. Everyone will love it.
