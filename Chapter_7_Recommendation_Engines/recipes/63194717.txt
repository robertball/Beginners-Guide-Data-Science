Title:
Stuffed Shells III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package jumbo pasta shells
1 eggs, beaten
1 (32 ounce) container ricotta cheese
1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
8 ounces grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1 tablespoon dried parsley
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (28 ounce) jar pasta sauce
1 lb ground turkey
1/2 box frozen chopped spinach

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.ingredients but the turkey and sauce until well combined.  Stuff cooked shells with ricotta mixture and place in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Cook turkey and add sauce.  Pour over stuffed shells tucking meat into shells as much as possible.  Sprinkle with mozz cheese.
Bake in preheated oven 45 to 60 minutes, until edges are bubbly and shells are slightly set.
