Title:
Rockin' Apple Turnovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
5 c. flour
2 c. plus 2 T shortning
1 t salt
1 pck. yeast
1 c. milk
2 beaten eggs
3/4 c. evaporated milk
Filling:
18 medium apples
1 1/2 c. sugar
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/4 cup uncooked tapioca
Cinnamon to taste
Nutmeg to taste

Directions:
Mix flour, shortening, and salt together as you would a pie crust, let rest 10 min.
Add 1 pck. yeast dissolved in 1 c. luke warm milk, the beaten eggs and evaporated milk
Mix thoroughly and refridgerate overnight
Dice apples and add to large pot
Add sugars, cinnamon, and nutmeg
Add tapioca
Cook until apples are tender over low heat, mixing thoroughly
Chill the filling until you are ready to use
To assemble:
Flour work area thoroughly
Roll out about 1/4 of the dough to about 1/8 inch thickness
Cut with an empty 2 lb. coffee can
Spoon scant 1/8 cup filling onto each round of dough
Fold over in half and seal the edges by brushing with water and pressing together
You may freeze them at this point by placing them on waxed paper on the freezer, when frozen through, place in gallon zip lock bags and bake later
To bake:
Bake at 375 for 15 min.
from frozen, bake at 375 for about 20 min.
Watch closely and remove when lightly browned

Submitter: 
Lisa Schmidt
Description: 
This is a recipe I received from a friend and always get rave reviews and requests to make.  You can also use any thick pie filling like strawberry-rhubarb or cherry.
