Title:
Orange Glorious Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066128.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 cup ice water
1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
12 cubes  ice
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/8 cup sugar

Directions:
In a blender combine milk, water, orange juice concentrate, ice cubes, vanilla and sugar.
Blend until smooth.
Pour into three 12 oz glasses and enjoy with a straw.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Milk, o.j., vanilla, sugar, water and ice...make an Orange Glorious combination.Modified from: Orange Glorious I
