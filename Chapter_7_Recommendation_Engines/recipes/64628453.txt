Title:
Ann Romney's Buttermilk Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4471237.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. light corn syrup
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine butter, buttermilk, sugar, and corn syrup in a saucepan and bring mixture to a boil, stirring until butter is melted and sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and add baking soda and vanilla. Serve warm.
