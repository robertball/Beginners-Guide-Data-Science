Title:
Pina Colada Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413358.jpg
Ingredients:
6 strawberries, sliced
1 peach, sliced
1 banana, sliced
1 slice of watermelon, cut into chunks
1/2 c. blueberries
1/2 c. fresh pineapple, cut into chunks
1/4 c. pina colada drink mix

Directions:
Combine fruit in a mixing bowl.
Drizzle drink mix over fruit and stir until well blended.

Submitter:

Description: 
A nice summery fruit salad that will have you reminiscing of time spent on the beach.
