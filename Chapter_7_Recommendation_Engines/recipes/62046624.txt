Title:
Grandma's Chocolate No Fail Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. sugar
3/4 c. butter
5 oz. can (2/3 c.) evaporated milk
7 oz. (2 c.) jar marshmellow creme
12 oz (2 c.) semi sweet chocolate chips
3/4 c. chopped nuts (optional)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Butter 13X9 pan.
In large saucepan, combine first 3 ingredients.  Heat to boiling stirring constantly.  Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes while continuing to stir constantly.  Remove from heat and add marshmallow cr√®me and chocolate chips; blend well.  Stir in nuts and vanilla.  Pour in pan and chill until firm.  Cut into squares.

Description: 
Growing up, I remember my grandmother making this recipe every Christmas.  It's easy, delicious and like the name says, No Fail.
