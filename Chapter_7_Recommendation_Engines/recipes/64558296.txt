Title:
Meat loaf, al Italiano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3416188.jpg
Ingredients:
11 oz lean ground pork
18 oz lean ground beef
1 lg egg
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
3 oz chopped mushrooms, about 1 cup
1/2 cup chopped red pepper
1 large clove garlic, finely chopped or 1 tsp micro planed
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1/2 cup Italian flavoured bread crumbs, with Romano cheese
3-4 sprigs Italian parsley, finely chopped
Oil, as needed for the skillet
Glaze, optional
1/4 cup tomato sauce
1 tsp Dijon mustard, I like Maille
1 tsp dark brown sugar
1 tsp vinegar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ยบ
Add a small amount of oil to a skillet , warm and add the mushrooms, pepper and onion.
Cook on med. heat  for 3-4 mins or until just soft.
Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds
Set aside and let cool.
In a large bowl mix the egg, bread crumbs and parsley, mix well.
Add the cooled veggies, mix.
Add the ground meats and mix, gently.
Place the mixture into a loaf pan and form into a loaf shape.
Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes or until the temp is 160 at the thickest part.
Drain excess oil occasionally.
Brush on the glaze, if using, when the loaf has been in the oven for 45 minutes.
Brush again every 10-15 minutes.
Let rest, loosely covered with foil, for 10 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
The original recipe was from the old country, Italy, that I've adapted to what's available to me. The mushrooms add an earthy flavour and keeps the loaf very moist.  MIL used white mushroom but I prefer cremini's.  I love leftovers in a sandwich but there's seldom any left. Enjoy!
