Title:
Butter Rum Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon butter flavored extract
1 tablespoon rum flavored extract

Directions:
Bring the water, white sugar, and brown sugar to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, and stir in the butter and run extracts; simmer 3 minutes longer.

Submitter: 
JILLENA
Description: 
A great flavor twist for waffle or pancake syrup.
