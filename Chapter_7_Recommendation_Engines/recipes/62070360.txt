Title:
Sweet and Sour Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. thick bacon slices, fried crisp and crumbled
1 - 2 large onions, sliced
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. salt
1/2 c. cider vinegar
2  15 oz. cans dried lima Butter Beans - drained
1  1 lb. can green lima beans - drained
1  1 lb. can kidney beans - drained
1  1lb. 11 oz. can New England Style Baked Beans - undrained
(I also add a can of drained blackeyed peas when I'm in the mood)

Directions:
Place onions in skillet that has fried bacon.  Do not drain the bacon fat.  Add sugar, mustard, garlic powder, salt and vinegar.  Cook 20 minutes, covered.  Add onion mixture to beans.  Add crumbled bacon.  Either bake at 350*  for 1 hour or place in crockpot on low for 4 - 6 hours.  Enjoy!!!
