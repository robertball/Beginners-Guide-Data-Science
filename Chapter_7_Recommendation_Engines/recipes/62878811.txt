Title:
Grilled Lentil Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 15 oz cans lentils, rinsed
1/2 cup fresh cilantro
1/2 cup fresh parsley
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp roasted cumin
1 cup Italian seasoned Panko bread crumbs
1 egg
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Combine 1 can of lentils, cilantro, parsley, garlic, cumin, salt and pepper in a food processor and puree.  Mix in the other can of lentils,salt, pepper, egg and bread crumbs.  Form into 2 inch patties.
Spray george foreman grill with cooking spray and grill patties until well browned.

Submitter: 
ltlmsmfft
Description: 
This is a great vegetarian dish that can be served several different ways.  Over couscous, in pita's or even over rice.
