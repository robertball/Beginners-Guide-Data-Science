Title:
Magical Mustard Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 boneless chicken breasts
1 c. yellow mustard
1 c. seasoned breadcrumbs
1 c. parmesean cheese
2 T. butter
2 T. vegetable oil

Directions:
Coat chicken with a generous amount of mustard.
Roll in bread crumb/parmesean cheese mixture. Refrigerate for 20 minutes.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Melt margarine in a greased baking dish. Add oil and chicken.
Bake for 30-35 minutes, turning chicken once.
