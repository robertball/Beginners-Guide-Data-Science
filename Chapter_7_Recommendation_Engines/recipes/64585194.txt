Title:
Dave's Creamy Chicken and Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth
2 cups water
2 cooked boneless chicken breast halves, shredded or cut in small pieces
1 medium onion, chopped and sauteed
1 package quick cooking long grain and wild rice with seasoning packet
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
3/4 cup flour
1/2 cup butter
2 cups heavy cream or milk

Directions:
In large pot over medium heat, combine broth, water, chicken, and onion.
Bring just to boiling, stir in rice, reserving seasoning packet.
Cover and remove from heat.
In small bowl, combine salt, pepper, and flour.
In medium saucepan over medium heat, melt butter. Stir in contents of seasoning packet until mixture is bubbly.
Reduce heat to low, then stir in flour mixture by tablespoons to form a roux.
Cook until thickened, 5 minutes.
Stir cream mixture into broth and rice. Cook over medium heat until heated through.
