Title:
Limoncello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 lemon
1 liter vodka or Everclear
2 cups white sugar
4 cups water
1.  Directions: Peel Zest from lemons and place into a large glass bottle.  Pour in alcohol.  Cover and let infuse for 2 weeks at room temperature.
2.  After two weeks, combine sugar and water in a medium saucepan.  Bring to a boil, DO NOT STIR.  Boil for 15 minutes.  Allow syrup to cool to room temperature
3.  Stir alcohol mixture into syrup.  Strain into glass bottles and seal with corks.  Add a lemon strip for decoration.  Let mixture age for 2 weeks at room temperature.
4.  After 2 weeks store in freezer

Directions:
Use small empty 8 oz wine bottles from the .99 store.
