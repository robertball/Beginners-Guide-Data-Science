Title:
Piadine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 each Piadine
250 g Gouda Cheese
150 g Rucola
1 each Fresh Mozzarella
100 g Cherry Tomatoes

Directions:
Melt gouda cheese in piadine
Warm some piadine and add fresh rucola, mozzarella slices and tomato slices
