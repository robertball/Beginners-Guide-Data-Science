Title:
Stuffed Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup + 1 tbsp. balsamic vinegar - divided
4 plum tomatoes
1 cup Italian croutons - crushed
4 tbsp. shredded Italian cheese blend - divided
1 tbsp. olive oil

Directions:
Preheat broiler.
In small pot, bring 1/2 cup balsamic to a boil and cook 3 min. or until reduced by half.
Halve 4 plum tomatoes and remove seed and pulp, reserving 1/4 cup of pulp.
Place tomatoes on baking sheet.
Combine croutons, tomato pulp, 1 tbsp. balsamic and 2 tbsp. cheese.
Divide mixture among tomatoes.
Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with 2 tbsp. cheese.
Broil for 2 min. and drizzle with remaining balsamic before serving.
