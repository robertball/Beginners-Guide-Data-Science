Title:
Pork Chop Marinade - Pineapple Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
2 cloves garlic
1 whole yellow onion, diced
1 cup pineapple juice
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 Tbs brown sugar
pepper, salt to taste

Directions:
mix marinade and pour over pork chops. Marinate overnight or for 3+ hours.

Submitter: 
Starsha
Description: 
Sweet/tangy marinade for pork chops. Great for summer grilling!
