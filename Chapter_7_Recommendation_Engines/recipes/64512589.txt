Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Teriyaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 skinless chicken thighs
1/2 cup soy sauce
2 tbsp. brown sugar
2 tbsp. ground ginger
2 garlic cloves, minced

Directions:
Wash and dry chicken.  Arrange in slow cooker.
Combine remaining ingredients.   Pour over chicken.
Cover.  Cook on high 1 hour.  Reduce heat to low and cook 5-6 hours, or until chicken is fork-tender.
