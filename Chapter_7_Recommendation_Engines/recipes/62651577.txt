Ingredients:
2 cups chicken stock
2 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons light cream
Coarse Kosher salt to taste
white or black pepper to taste
Serving Size: 1/4 Cup
Servings: 8

Directions:
Make a roux out of the butter only cook until blonde stage has been reached. Pour in the stock while whisking the sauce.  Cook until reduced by 1/4. Add the cream slowing before service. Season to taste.

ChefMonty
