Title:
Apricot Glazed Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
one (1) 8-10 lb. pre-cooked ham (bone in)
1/2 cup Apricot Preserves
3 Tbs. honey mustard
whole cloves if desired

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit.
Prepare Ham:
Score the "fat-side" up portion of the ham.
If desired, insert cloves at X's.
place in shallow baking dish.
Add about 1/2 cup of water to bottom of baking dish.
Cover ham loosely and bake about 90 minutes (or 10-15 minutes per pound).
Prepare Glaze (begin to prepare about 10 minutes before Ham is finished cooking):
In a small cooking pot and on low heat, slowly warn Apricot Preserves until it reaches a smooth warm consistency.
Remove from heat.
Add in honey mustard and mix well.
Remove Ham from oven and uncover.
Generously spread on the Apricot/Honey Mustard glaze mixture and return to oven.
cook ham uncovered another 15-20 minutes or until glaze just begins brown.
Remove from oven and let ham stand about 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Submitter: 
KINCATT
Description: 
Apricot preserves and a hint of honey mustard make a wonderful glaze for your traditional Easter Ham (or any other special occassion).
