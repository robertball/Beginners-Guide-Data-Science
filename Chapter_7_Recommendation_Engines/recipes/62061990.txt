Title:
Favorite Crustless Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. grated cheese
6 or 7 ham or bacon slices
mushrooms
sauteed onions
4 eggs
1/2 c. flour
2 Tbsp. margarine
1/2 tsp. salt
pepper to taste
1 1/2 c. milk

Directions:
Butter a 10-inch pie and or a 7 x 11 inch baking pan.  Sprinkle cheese in bottom.  Add ham/bacon, mushrooms and sauteed onions to pan.  In a blender, combine eggs, flour, margarine, salt, pepper, and milk.  Blend well.  Pour mixture into pan and bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Traveling_Is_Love
Description: 
A fantastic quiche with ham or bacon slices, mushrooms, sauteed onions and it only takes about 45 minutes total!
