Title:
Brazilian Kids Parties - Brigadeiro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 tablespoon butter
4 tablespoons powdered chocolate
granulated chocolate to roll the brigadeiros

Directions:
Stir together condensed milk, chocolate and butter over medium heat until you can see the bottom of the saucepan. It should look like a thick paste.
Let cool just long enough for you to be able to handle it.
Shape into walnut size balls, with buttered hands, and roll them in the granulated chocolate.

Submitter: 
Cacau
Description: 
This is Brazilian number one candy at kids parties. Its chocolate flavor drives everybody crazy. It is a complete success!
