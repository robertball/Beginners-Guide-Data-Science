Title:
Taco Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lb. ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic
1 envelope taco seasoning
1/2 cup water
1 can (11 oz) Mexi-corn, drained
1 can (10.5 oz) condensed Fiesta Nacho Cheese soup
1 can (10.5 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 package (32 oz) frozen Tater Tots
1 cup shredded Mexican four cheese blend

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Spray 9 x 13 glass baking dish with cooking spray
Brown ground beef with onion and garlic in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Drain fat.
Stir in taco seasoning and water to cooked ground beef.  Bring to boil.
Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add corn and soups to seasoned beef, mixing thoroughly.
Spread beef mixture into sprayed 9 x 13 baking dish.
Sprinkle cheese on top.
Arrange tater tots in a single layer, covering entire dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes or until tater tots are golden brown and crispy.
Place under broiler last 5 minutes for additional crisping of the tater tots, if desired.

Submitter: 
Daisy K
Description: 
This is a Mexican inspired variation of the original casserole.  It is a big hit with my family and requested often!
