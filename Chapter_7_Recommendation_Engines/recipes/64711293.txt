Title:
Hamburger with Garlic and Onion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
½ onion, chopped
2 teaspoons season salt
1 ½ tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 ½ tablespoons garlic, minced
4 hamburger buns
4 sliced of cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine ground beef, onion, season salt, worcestershire sauce, garlic, salt and pepper.
Form the mixture into four patties.
Place burgers on grill or in pan, cook until browned all the way through.
Place cheese on the burgers, and place burgers on buns to serve.
