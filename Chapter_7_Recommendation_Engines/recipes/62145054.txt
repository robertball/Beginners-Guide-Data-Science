Title:
Mama's Chalupas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BASE
12 Corn Tortillas
Olive Oil
FILLING
1 lb Ground Pork
2 tsp Cumin
1 tsp Chili Powder
1 tsp crushed garlic
1 can Re-fried Beans
TOPPINGS
2     c Shredded Cheddar, Pepper Jack, or Monterey Jack Cheese
1/2 c Chopped Onions
1/2 c Chopped Tomatoes
3/4 c Shredded Lettuce
1/4 c Sliced Jalapenos
1/4 c Fresh Cilantro
Salsa

Directions:
Brown ground pork and garlic.
Drain and add cumin and chili powder
Combine seasoned burger and re-fried beans, mixing well.
Cover mixing dish and set aside
Heat 1/2 inch oil in skillet
Place tortillas (one at a time) into hot oil
Turn after 2 to 3 seconds
Using tongs, hold each side of tortilla up for 3 to 5 seconds to fry into a "boat" shape.
(Flat version accomplished by frying tortillas 6-8 seconds per side)
Drain well on paper towels.
Top each tortilla with 1/12 of the meat mixture and serve.
Present toppings with serving utensils for each person to add to their own taste.

Submitter: 
Carrie Bell
Description: 
I put recipe together when I had no time to go to the store and my family was tired of the same ol' taco dinner.  I've been making it for them every since!
