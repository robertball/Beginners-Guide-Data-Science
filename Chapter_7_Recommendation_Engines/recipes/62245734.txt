Title:
Oven Roasted Red Potatoes and Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large red potatoes, cut into chunks
extra virgin olive oil to coat
2 cloves garlic
pinch teaspoons dried rosemary
pinch teaspoons dried thyme
kosher salt to taste
1 bunch fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1 inch pieces
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Toss the red potatoes with olive oil, garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt and pepper.
Bake, covered, 30-35 minutes in the preheated oven.
Toss asparagus with olive oil and salt.
Add asparagus to potatoes, increase heat to 450, cook covered 2-5 min, then uncovered another 5 min.

Submitter:

Description: 
This garlicky red potato and asparagus dish is easy and delicious served either hot or cold.  Rosemary and thyme give it a sophisticated flavor. Try adding a little chopped red pepper, too...yum!Modified from: Oven Roasted Red Potatoes and Asparagus
