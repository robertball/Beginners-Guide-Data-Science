Title:
Orange Cider Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2 cinnamon sticks
1 tsp. whole nutmeg
2 cups apple cider or juice
6 cups orange juice
fresh orange thinly sliced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in slow cooker except orange slices.
Cover.  Cook on low 4-10 hours or on high for 2-3 hours until heated through.
Float thin slices of orange in cooker before serving.
