Title:
Pesto Chicken Florentine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped (or 2 tsp from jar)
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into strips or bite sized pieces
1- 10oz fresh spinach leaves
1 (4.5 ounce) package KNORR Alfredo sauce mix
2 tablespoons pesto
1 (8 ounce) package dry penne pasta
1 tablespoon grated Romano cheese
1 box sliced mushrooms
4 oz cream cheese (softened) with chives
2 diced Roma tomatoes

Directions:
In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente. Rinse under cold water and drain.
While pasta is oooking, heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Sprinkle chicken with Old Bay Garlic & Herb spices and Italian Seasoning (or seasonings of choice).  Add chicken and cook for 7 to 8 minutes on each side. When chicken is close to being cooked through (no longer pink inside), add garlic and saute for a minute or two.  Add spinach and saute all together for 3 to 4 minutes.
Meanwhile, prepare Alfredo sauce according to package directions. Add cream cheese/chives and stir until thickened.  When finished, stir in 2 tablespoons pesto; set aside.
Add chicken/spinach mixture to pasta, then stir in pesto/Alfredo sauce. Stir in tomotoes (just to warm a bit) Mix well, top with cheese  and serve.
