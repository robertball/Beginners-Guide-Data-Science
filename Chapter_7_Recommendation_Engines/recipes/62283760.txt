Title:
Fluffy Fudgy Decorator Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
1-1/2 teaspoon vanilla
5 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tablespoons Hershey's chocolate syrup
2 (1 pound) boxes confectioners' sugar
Dash salt
3/4 cup warm water

Directions:
Whip all ingredients together in a large bowl for 10 minutes. Refrigerate frosting for a thicker consistency, especially if piping decorations using a pastry bag.

Submitter: 
SUZZANNA
Description: 
A delicious fluffy frosting that spreads easily and, if slighly chilled, holds its shape well for piping decorations.
