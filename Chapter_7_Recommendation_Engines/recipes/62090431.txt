Title:
Kiki's Poblano Vegetable Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
for the roux:
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons flour
For the soup:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 large yellow onion, diced
1 large yellow bell pepper, diced
1 large red bell pepper, diced
1 cup diced celery
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
3 poblano chilies, roasted, peeled, seeded, chopped
4 cups vegetable stock
3 cups sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped
1 cup baby carrots
1 cup yellow squash, cubed
1 cup zucchini, cubed
1 T paprika
1 T cumin
1 T oregano
1 T thyme
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1 cup frozen italian green beans
1 cup frozen lima beans
2 cups whole kernel corn
3 cups heavy cream

Directions:
In a large dutch oven saute onions, bell peppers, garlic, celery, and chiles in oil for 7-10 minutes, until soft. Add stock, potatoes, carrots, squash, zucchini, and spices and simmer for 15 minutes. Add green beans, lima beans, corn and cream. Return to low simmer for10 minutes. Make the roux, in small sauce pan melt butter, sprinkle with the flour and cook while constantly stirring until it turns a medium brown color, then whisk it into soup. Simmer until thickened, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
KIKI810
Description: 
This smokey vegetable chowder is creamy with a hint of heat from the poblano chilies.  It's a great vegetarian dish, yet hearty enough for meat lovers.  Serve with crust garlic bread.  The flavors only get better with time and this soup freezes very well.
