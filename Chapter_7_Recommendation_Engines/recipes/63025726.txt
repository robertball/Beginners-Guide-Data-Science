Title:
Garbanzo Bean Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz of farfalle pasta
1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 (8 oz) can olives
1/2 jar pasta sauce
1 cup sliced celery
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 small onion, diced
3 tablespoons lemon
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed on box. Saute celery, onion and garlic in olive oil and lemon. Add remaining ingredients heat through. Toss with pasta.

Submitter: 
Raechel Valerius
Description: 
This recipe is a great way to use up a can of garbanzo beans or those last few spoonfuls of tomato sauce! It's also very versatile so you can add different spices and vegetables as the mood strikes!Modified from: Garbanzo Bean Salad II
