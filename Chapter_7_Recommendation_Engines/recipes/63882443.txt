Title:
Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup sugar
1/2 cup Crisco
2 eggs
3 tbsp buttermilk
1 cup mashed, ripe bananas (2-3 bananas)
2 cups sifted self-rising flour
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350F(175C)
Thoroughly mix sugar, shortening and eggs.
Stir in buttermilk, mashed bananas and flour.
Stir in soda and fold in nuts.
Bake in a bread loaf pan, 50-60 minutes.

Submitter: 
CBerrier86
Description: 
A great old recipe for Banana Nut Bread. This can also be used to make muffins by simply pouring into muffin cups.
