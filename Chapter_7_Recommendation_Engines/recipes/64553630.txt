Title:
Grilled Chorizo & Cheese Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chorizo
Cheddar Cheese
Avocado
White Onion
Creamy Sriracha Sauce
Unsalted Butter
Bread

Directions:
1. Form chorizo into two patties and cook over medium heat in frying pan. Add onions when Chorizo is close to finished and cook an additional 3-4 minutes, until onions are soft.
2.  Preheat a second frying pan at medium heat.
3. When chorizo is finished, create sandwich by laying out slice of bread, cheese, chorizo, creamy Sriracha sauce, onion, avocado, another layer of cheese, then other slice of bread.
4. Add one tablespoon of unsalted butter to the preheated frying pan.
5. Add sandwich when butter is melted, flip sandwich immediately to soak up butter on both sides.
6. Cook sandwich over medium heat until bread is brown and toasted evenly.

Pan grilled Chorizo sandwich with cheese, avocado, grilled onion,  and creamy Sriracha sauce.
