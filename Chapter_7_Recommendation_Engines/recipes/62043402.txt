Title:
The Ultimate Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cup flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
3 Tbs sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
3 eggs (separate the whites and put into another bowl)
7 Tbs vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups milk

Directions:
Preheat the waffle iron
sift the dry ingredients into a bowl
mix the wet ingredients, including the milk, yolks, oil, and vanilla. (except the egg whites) and put into the dry mix.
mix until there are no lumps.
beat the 3 egg whites until stiff then gently fold them into the batter using a spatula or wooden spoon. Just until mixed, DON'T OVER DO IT!!!
grease your waffle iron then pour approx. 1/2 cup batter in your waffle iron.
