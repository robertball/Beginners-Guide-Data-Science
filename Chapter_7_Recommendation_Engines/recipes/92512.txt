Title:
Paint the Town Red Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976460.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head red leaf lettuce, rinsed, patted dry
1 cup radicchio lettuce OR finely shredded red cabbage
1 cup raspberries in light syrup, drained
1/2 small red onion, cut into thin rings
Raspberry Vinaigrette:
3 tablespoons juice from raspberries
2 teaspoons canola oil
2 teaspoons raspberry vinegar
1 teaspoon granulated sugar

Directions:
To prepare dressing: combine raspberry juice, canola oil, raspberry vinegar and sugar in a container with a tightly fitting lid. Shake until sugar dissolves.
In a large bowl, tear leaf and radicchio lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Toss in raspberries and onion. Drizzle vinaigrette over salad and toss. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
The Canola Info Virtual Kitchen
Description: 
Celebrate with this festive salad. Canola oil gives salad dressings a smooth yet light mouth feel.
