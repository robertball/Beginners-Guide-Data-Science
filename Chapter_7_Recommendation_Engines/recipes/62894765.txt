Title:
Robin's Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can pumpkin puree (796 ml)
6 eggs
1 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup apple sauce
1 cup water
2 tsp vanilla
4 cups white sugar
3 cups whole wheat flour
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons salt
3 tsp pumpkin pie spice-cinnamon,nutmeg,cloves,ginger
1 1/2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 2 muffin pans.
In a large bowl, mix together pumpkin puree, eggs, oil, water, vanilla and sugar until well blended.
In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and spices.
Stir the dry ingredients into the pumpkin mixture until just blended. Fold in chocolate chips.
Pour into the prepared muffin pans.
Bake for about 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Muffins are done when toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
