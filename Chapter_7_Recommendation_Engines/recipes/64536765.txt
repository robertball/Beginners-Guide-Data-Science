Title:
Flourless Double Chocolate Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3134082.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup NuttZo Chocolate Seven Nut & Seed Butter
1 very ripe mashed banana
1 egg white
1/4 cup light honey
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Prepare a muffin pan by spraying it with cooking spray
Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until batter is smooth
Pour batter into muffin pan filling muffin cavities 3/4 of the way full
Bake 12 minutes until a toothpick can be inserted in the middle & removes clean
Allow muffins to cool in pan a few minutes before removing & cooling completely on a wire rack
Keep in the refrigerator up to a week to enjoy
