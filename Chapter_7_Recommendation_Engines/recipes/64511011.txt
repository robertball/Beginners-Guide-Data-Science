Title:
Qick & Easy Spinach Rice(Palak Rice) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cup Spinach leaves
1 big carrot
1 small onion
1 spoon ginger-garlic paste
4 green chillies
1 spoon roasted coriander powder
2 cupswhite  rice (rice cooker cups)
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
3 spoons oil
Salt - Adjust to taste

Directions:
Preparation:
Chop onion & carrot into small pieces
Cooking:
Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
Add Carrot, onion & green chillies  and fry them for 5 min.
Add Ginger garlic paste and fry for about 5 min.
Meanwhile, grind spinach leaves to get paste.
Add spinach paste to the pan and fry it until there is no water in spinach.
Add salt,coriander powder and rice .
Shift them to an electric rice cooker bowl and add 41/2 cups of water (You can add more water if needed).
Switch on the rice cooker.
