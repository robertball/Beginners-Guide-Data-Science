Title:
Honey-Gingerbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/2 c all purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp salt
4 tsp ground ginger
4 tsp ground cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp ground cloves
1 tsp ground nutmeg
2 stck butter, at room temp
1 c sugar
2 eggs
1 c honey
1/2 c molasses

Directions:
Whisk together flour, baking soda, salt and spices. Set aside.
Beat butter and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well. Beat in honey and molasses. Gradually add flour mixture, and beat until just combined. Divide dough into 3 portions. Wrap and refrigerate for 1 hr.
Preaheat oven to 350 F. On floured parchment, roll dough to 1/4" thick. Brush off excess flour. Slide dough on parchment paper to a baking sheet and freeze for 15 mins.
Cut out shapes. Transfer to parchment lined baking sheets, and freeze 15 mins.
Bake cookies untill lightly golden brown on bottom, about 12 mins. Cool on racks. Decorate with royal icing, and desired sprinkles. Store in an airtight container up to 1 week.

Description: 
This is a very tasty Christmas dessert. This recipe is very nice for Christmas parties as it makes 2 1/2 dozen 2 1/2 inch cookies.Modified from: Gingerbread Cookies
