Title:
Mango Salsa Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 7oz pkg. Del Monte Mango Chunks
1/2 Red Bell Pepper
2 Tbls. Onion, minced
1 Tbl. Fresh Cilantro,chopped
1/2 Jalapeno Pepper,seeded and minced
1 Lime,juice of
1/2 Lemon, juice of
Salt and Pepper to taste
3 Tbls. Olive Oil
up to 12oz Tilapia

Directions:
Combine mango, red pepper, onion, cilantro, jalapeno, lime and lemon. Season with salt and pepper. Let sit in fridge for an hour or so. Drain juices from salsa into a plastic bag or container reserving the salsa for later. Add olive oil to drained liquid and then add fish and let sit for 30min. to 1 hour. Place fish on baking sheet and season with salt, pepper and and a little garlic(fresh or granulated). Bake in a 425F. oven for 12 min. Top with remaining salsa and  serve.

Submitter: 
Mad Butcher
Description: 
This is a variation of a recipe that I found on Allrecipes.com. I streamlined it by using prepared mango and using the salsa liquid as a marinade for the fish. And I also bake the fish instead of grilling to simplify the recipe.
