Title:
Flakey Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans refrigerated crescent rolls
1 stick butter, melted
3/4 cup sugar and cinnamon
1/2 to 1 cup pecans or other nuts according to taste

Directions:
Dip triangle of dough in butter, dip in the sugar mixture, sprinkle with nuts. roll as for creacent rolls and break off pieces about the size of walnuts. Place in 8" cake or pie pan. Continue with remaining dough. These are better served after they cool, and are even good the next day.

Alice Mayo Edelhauser

Flakey tender monkey bread
