Title:
Brownies for Ice Cream Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz semi sweet chocolate
2 oz unsweetened chocolate
2 eggs
1/2 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 gallon Ice cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9-inch rectangular pan.
Melt chocolate in a double boiler or microwave. Allow to cool and beat in eggs.
In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, chocolate eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes. Do not overcook.
Allow brownies to cool. Cut into 24 brownies in the pan.
Using a knife or spoon, put ice cream between two brownies, wrap individually and freeze.

Submitter: 
Lily Peterson
Description: 
A delicious ice cream sandwich that I make in bulk and freeze for those hot summer days. The brownies by themselves are also a delicious treat and the real chocolate in them makes them rich and tasty.
