Title:
Totally Easy Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/662656.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium ripe avocado, peeled and cubed
1 tablespoon salsa
1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
lime juice
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and moosh up with a fork.  Serve immediately.  Doubles or triples easily.
