Title:
Cheesy Sweet Potato and Baked Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 sweet potatoes or yams
3 green onions, diced
2 cans vegetarian baked beans
7 strips vegetarian (soy-based) bacon, diced
4 cloves garlic
1/4 c BBQ sauce
2 T honey
1/4 t cayenne
1/4 t mustard powder
1/4 t garlic powder
1 t olive oil
8 oz + 4 oz sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
pinch salt
pinch black pepper

Directions:
*Preheat oven to 350.  Oil a 9"x13" casserole.
*Wrap garlic cloves in tin foil and place on top rack of oven for 5-7 minutes until soft and juicy.
*Scrub sweet potatoes, dry and generously poke holes in surface with a fork.  Microwave for 11 minutes, rotating periodically.  Place in freezer until cool throughout.
*In a large mixing bowl, combine beans, onions, vegetarian bacon, cayenne, mustard powder, garlic powder, BBQ sauce, honey and 4 oz shredded cheddar cheese.
*When garlic is finished roasting, remove cloves from peel and smash with back of spoon.  Add to bean mixture.
*Peel potatoes and slice into 1/2" slices.  Add 1 t olive oil to hot skillet.  Pan fry potato slices approximately 2 minutes on each side, until browned.  Sprinkle with dash salt and black pepper.
*Arrange potatoes on bottom of oiled casserole.  Pour bean mixture over potatoes.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on top.  Bake in 350 degree oven for 20 minutes.  Serve with homemade cornbread.
