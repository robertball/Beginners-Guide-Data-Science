Title:
Oatmeal Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups regular oatmeal
2 1/2 cups boiling water
1 cup butter
2 cups brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
2 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup flour

Directions:
Pour boiling water over oatmeal and butter. Let stand 20 minutes. Add remaining ingredients and stir together and bake in large pan at 350 F for 30 minutes.
