Title:
Pasta con broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1 med. onion, chopped
1 1/2 pounds cheese, any variety, chunked
1 c milk
1 stick margarine, cut into small pieces
16 oz box large shells, cooked
2 large bags frozen broccoli,cooked

Directions:
Add the frozen broccoli to the boiling pasta during the last 5 minutes. Drain and set aside. Mix all the other ingredients in a large casserole dish. Put the dish in preheated 350 degree oven and heat until the cheese has melted. Add the shells and broccoli. Bake for 1 hr.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
This makes a lot but all the more for leftovers. This is requested often by my family.
