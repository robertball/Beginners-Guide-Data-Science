Title:
BBQ Sauce That Can't Be Beat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Ketchup
1 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 c brown sugar
1/4 c molasses
1 1/2 t liquid smoke
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1/4 t garlic powder
1/4 t onion powder

Directions:
In a 2 quart saucepan combine all ingredients, whisk together well and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 40 min. Remove and serve or refrigerate for later use.

Submitter: 
Livie's Mommy
Description: 
This is a sweet BBQ Sauce that we just love on Pulled Pork, Chicken and Hamburgs . Everyone who tries it says its the best yet. I usually double or triple the batch as it keeps well in the refrigerator and is a favorite of ours.
