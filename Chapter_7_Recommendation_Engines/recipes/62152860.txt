Title:
Tex-Mex Tater Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1  can of tomatoes
1  can of corn
1  can of black beans
1 package of taco seasoning mix
1/4 cup water
1 bag of tater tots
2 cups of grated cheese (plus or minus depending on your taste)

Directions:
In large skillet brown hamburger then drain off fat and return to skillet, add tomatoes, corn, black beans, taco seasoning and water. Mix well. In 9x13 pan empty contents of skillet and top with tater tots. Bake according to tater tot directions or at 425 degrees for about 20 minutes. Remove from oven top with cheese and bake until cheese has melted. Remove and top with your choice of toppings such as sour cream and salsa.

Submitter: 
Crystal Fuller Nute
Description: 
This is a great casserole that will warm you up on a cold day!
