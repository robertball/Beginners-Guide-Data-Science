Title:
Authentic Spanish Paella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into strips
1 cup finely chopped onion
1 red pepper, julienned
1 green pepper, julienned
1 cup frozen peas
1 1/2 cups cooked shrimp
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 10 oz can chicken broth
1 1/2 cups water
2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp paprika
dash salt, pepper, cayenne and ground turmeric

Directions:
In a large pan, heat one tbsp of the olive oil over medium heat and add the chickebn. Cook until lightly browned.
Remove and then heat 1 tbsp of olive oil and cook the onions, red and green pepper and garlic until the onions are soft and lightly browned, about 5 minutes. Add the broth, water, rice, tomatoes and all the spices.Bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes until rice is tender.
After, stir in the cooked chicken, shrimp and peas, uncovered for 2-3 minutes. Garnish with a pinch oregano over each serving. Enjoy!
