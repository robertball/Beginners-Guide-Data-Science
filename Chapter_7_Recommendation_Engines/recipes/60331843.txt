Title:
Carrot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups sliced carrots
3 tablespoons butter
1 onion, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 lb Velveeta, shredded
1/2 cup seasoned croutons
French fried onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a 2 quart casserole dish.
Bring a pot of water to a boil.  Add carrots and cook until tender but still firm, about 8 minutes; drain.
Melt 3 tablespoons butter in a medium saucepan.  Saute onions.  Layer carrots, onion and cheese.
Top with bread crumbs and french fried onions.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until heated through.
