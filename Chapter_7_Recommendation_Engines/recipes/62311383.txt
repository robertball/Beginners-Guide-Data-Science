Title:
Wendi's Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can garbanzo beans, drained (save the juice)
3 Tablespoon roasted sesame tahini
2 Tablespoon lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
2 cloves garlic, pressed
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 teaspoon honey
1 pinch paprika
1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley
olive oil for drizzling

Directions:
Place the garbanzo beans, tahini, lemon juice, salt, garlic, olive oil, and honey in a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth adding bean juice by the tablespoon if necessary to make a thick consistency.
Taste.  Add more salt if needed.
Transfer mixture to a serving bowl.  Refrigerate or serve immediately.
Drizzle with additional olive oil over the garbanzo bean mixture. Sprinkle with paprika and parsley.
