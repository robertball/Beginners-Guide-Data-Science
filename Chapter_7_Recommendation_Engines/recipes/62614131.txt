Title:
Roasted Eggplant Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant
1 15oz. can garbanzo beans
4 tbsp olive oil
3 garlic cloves
1/4 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 400 degrees.
Finely mince garlic
Remove skin from eggplant and chop into one inch cubes.
Place eggplant cubes in a large bowl and toss with olive oil, minced garlic, salt, pepper, basil and thyme.
Spread eggplant mixture on a baking sheet lined with parchment and sprayed with non-stick spray.
Roast for 10 minutes then flip the eggplant pieces over and roast for another 10-15 minutes. Watch for over browning.
Remove roasted eggplant from the oven and place in a food processor or blender. Drain garbanzo beans and place in food processor or blender. Add lemon juice. Combine ingredients until smooth (add extra olive oil if still chunky or dry, one tbsp at a time). Scoop Eggplant Hummus into a medium size bowl and ENJOY!

Submitter: 
CaseyMarieG7
Description: 
This smooth eggplant dip goes wonderfully with vegetables or pita bread. It is versatile and delicious! Enjoy!
