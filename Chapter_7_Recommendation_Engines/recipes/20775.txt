Title:
Sao Paulo Lime and Vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/625412.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce vodka
2 cubes ice, crushed
1 lime, juiced
1 tablespoon white sugar

Directions:
Put crushed ice in a tall glass. Pour in the vodka. Add lime juice and sugar to taste. If it is too strong, simply dilute with water.

Submitter: 
Tara
Description: 
This drink is for an acquired taste, but is VERY popular in Brazil and quite tasty if you like tart things.
