Title:
Coconut Rice with Edamame Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz frozen shelled edamame
1 Tbsp sesame oil
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 Tbsp sesame seed
1 tsp chile powder
1 tsp garam masala or other curry powder
salt and pepper
2 1/2 C shrimp bouillon
juice of 2-3 small limes
2 C jasmine rice
3/4 C shredded coconut, toasted
1/2 C coconut milk
salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.
Combine frozen edamame with sesame seed, sesame oil, olive oil, garam masala and chile powder. Add extra drizzles of olive oil as necessary.
Roast 20 minutes, tossing after the first 10, then again after 5.  Remove from oven and set aside.
Meanwhile, bring shrimp bouillon to a boil, then add lime juice, then rice.
Bring to a simmer then turn heat to low.  Let rice cook 15-20 minutes until done and can be fluffed easily with a fork.
Remove from heat and stir in coconut milk.  Toss in edamame and toasted coconut. Salt and pepper to taste.
