Title:
Chia Seed Refrigerator Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cup strawberries
2/3 cup rhubarb
2 T honey
2 T chia seeds

Directions:
Processess all ingredients in a blender and refrigerate over night. The chia seeds will gel, thickening the fruit puree.
