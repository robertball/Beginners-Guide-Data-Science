Title:
Grossmutter Fritz's Zucker Keks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C Butter (1/2 Margarine, if you must!)
2 Eggs, Unbeaten
1/4 tsp Salt
3 TBLS Milk
1 C Sugar
3 1/2 C Flour - Sifted
1/2 tsp Vanilla
3 tsp Baking Powder
(Optional: A Dash of Nutmeg or Cinnamon.)

Directions:
Cream Butter, Sugar and Eggs.
Add Milk and remaining ingredients - Adding the flour LAST and just 1 C at a time.
Beat until just Mixed and Smooth.
Cover and CHILL the Dough for Several Hours.
Roll-out on a Floured board and Cut with a Cookie Cutter. Sprinkle with Sugar.
Bake in a PRE-Heated 375 F Oven for 7-10 Mins.
