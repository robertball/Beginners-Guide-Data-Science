Title:
Cheddar Biscuit Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup self-rising flour*
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1/2 tsp. garlic flakes
1/2 tsp. basil

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the flour and cheese.  Stir in milk and mayonnaise just until moistened.  Fill five greased muffin cups two-thirds full.  Fill empty muffin cups halfway with water.  Bake at 425 degrees F for 17-20 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Lisa Garnier
Description: 
It takes only four ingredients to stir up these moist and tender cheddar biscuits. 'You can double the recipe have leftovers for breakfast the next day. Just split open, toast and butter,' suggests Sara Dukes of Bartow, Georgia.Modified from: Cheddar Biscuit Cups
