Title:
Mama Butzine's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. hamburger
2 can (reg size) Kidney beans
2 can (reg size) Chili beans
1 lg. can tomatoes
1 med - lg. can tomato sauce
1 can tomato paste
1 onion
Salsa, if desired
Salt & Pepper
Chili Powder
2 Tbsp brown sugar, if desired

Directions:
Brown meat with onions then drain.
Add salt, pepper, and chili powder.
Drain kidney beans before adding.
Add chili beans (do not drain).
Add brown sugar, if desired
