Title:
Joe's Delicious Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2233730.jpg
Ingredients:
Bunch of Cilantro
1 Lime
1/2 purple Medium onion
2 Cans of Mexican Stewed tomatoes
Teaspoon of Salt
Teaspoon of Pepper
Tablespoon of Garlic powder
1 Jalapeno

Directions:
Blend lime juice, 1/2 purple onion, 2 Cans of Mexican stewed tomatoes (un-drained), salt, pepper, garlic powder, jalapeno.
