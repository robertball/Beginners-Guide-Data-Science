Title:
Alfredo Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990702.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon I Can't Believe It's Not Butter!® Spread
1/2 cup walnuts, chopped
1 large clove garlic, finely minced
1 (15 ounce) jar Bertolli® Creamy Alfredo Sauce
8 ounces linguine, cooked and drained

Directions:
Melt spread in 2-quart saucepan over medium heat and cook walnuts 3 minutes or until golden. Stir in garlic and cook 30 seconds. Stir in sauce. Reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, 3 minutes or until heated through.
To serve, toss sauce with hot linguine. Sprinkle with additional chopped walnuts and garnish, if desired, with chopped fresh parsley.

Submitter: 
Bertolli
Description: 
Walnuts and garlic are cooked until golden, then simmered with a creamy Alfredo sauce, and served over linguine for a tasty dinner that's on the table in 30 minutes.
