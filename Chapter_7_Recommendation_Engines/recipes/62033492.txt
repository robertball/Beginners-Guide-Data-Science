Title:
Mr. Shires Beer Burgers and Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Beer Burgers
1 egg
1/2 cup beer
1/2 cup crushed crackers
1/4 cup finely chopped onions
6 tbsp creamy french dressing
2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp salt
1 1/2 pound hamburger
6 hamburger buns

Directions:
Combine egg, 1/4 cup beer, cracker crumbs, onion, 2 tbsp dressing, cheese, and salt.  Add meat, mix well.  Form into 6 patties, 3/4 inches thick.
Barbeque Sauce
Mix remaining beer and dressing.  Grill meat over medium coals 10 minutes.  Turn once, baste occasionally with sauce.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a good burger for grilling outside.  The taste is mouth watering.  Just imagine the 2 american favorites(beer and burgers) in one recipe!
