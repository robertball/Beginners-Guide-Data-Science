Title:
Vidalia Onion Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups Vidalia onions, chopped bite sized
1 stick margarine, melted
1 1/4 cups cornbread dressing (Stove Top is good)
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup sour cream
salt & pepper (to taste)

Directions:
Mix together onions, margarine, 1 cup of the cornbread dressing, soup, and sour cream.  Place mixture into an ungreased 8 x 11 pyrex baking dish.  Sprinkle 1/4 cup of dressing over the top.  Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Georgia Julie
Description: 
A delicious side dish, this goes especially good with grilled or roasted chicken.
