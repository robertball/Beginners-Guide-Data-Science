Title:
Mexican Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1099835.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 can fire roasted tomatoes(14.5 ounce)
1 medium onion,diced
1 small bell pepper, chopped fine
1 small zuchinni, shredded
1 cup frozen corn, thawed and drained
1 large carrot, shredded
1 jalepeno, seeded and minced
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp seasoned salt
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1 1/2 lbs ground beef (or ground turkey)
1 cup crushed flavored tortilla chips
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese, divided
salsa, optional

Directions:
Heat oven to 400ºF.
Mix ingredients with 1 cup of the cheese.
Press into 24 muffin cups sprayed with cooking spray. Use back of spoon to make indentation in center of each. Place muffin pan on foil-covered baking sheet.
Bake 20 to 25 min. or until meatloaves are done (160°F). Top with remaining cheese (and salsa if desired); bake 3 min. or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
A twist on a classic.  I often use a jumbo muffin pan (two of 6 each)instead of the regular size,(big appetites).  Adding the vegggies is a sneaky way of getting them into your kids diets and streches the meal a bit farther.
