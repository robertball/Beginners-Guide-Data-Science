Title:
Pam's Orange-Pineapple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/175579.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix (Duncan Hines Butter Golden)
4 eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 (11 ounce) can mandarin orange segments with liquid
1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 large package instant vanilla pudding mix
2 (8 ounce) cans crushed pineapple with juice

Directions:
Combine cake mix, eggs, vegetable oil, and mandarin oranges and beat well for about 3 minutes.
Bake in 2- 9 inch greased and floured round cake pans for 25-30 minutes in a pre-heated 350 degree F (175 degrees C) oven.
To Make Frosting:   Mix dry pudding mix with whipped topping and pineapple (include juice). Refrigerate for 15 minutes.
Split each cake layer in two horizontally.
Frost cake as you would any layer cake, stacking all four layers.  Chill at least 2 hours (preferably overnight) before serving. Keep refrigerated.
