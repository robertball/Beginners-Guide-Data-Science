Title:
FESTIVE SWEET POTATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 CUPS COOKED, MASHED SWEET POTATOES
1/2 CUP SUGAR
1/4 CUP MILK
1/3 BUTTER-MELTED
1 TSP VANILLA
2 EGGS, BEATEN
1 CUP FLAKED COCONUT
1 CUP BROWN SUGAR
1/3 CUP FLOUR
1/3 CUP BUTTER
1 CUP CHOPPED PECANS

Directions:
COMBINE 1ST SIX INGREDIENTS AND MIX WELL. SPOON INTO A LIGHTLY GREASED 8 INCH SQUARE PAN
COMBINE REMAINING INGREDIENTS; SPRINKLE OVER TOP OF SWEET POTATOES.
BAKE AT 350 FOR 25 MINUTES OR UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN
