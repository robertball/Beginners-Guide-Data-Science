Title:
Wildtree Chipotle Lime Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken or steak cut into strips
2 cups bell peppers (approximately 2-3 peppers, cut into strips
1 white onion, cut into slices
2 tbsp Wildtree Fajita Seasoning Blend
2 tbsp Chipotle Lime Seasoning Blend (or omit Wildtree Fajita Seasoning Blend and use 3-4 tbsp Wildtree Chipotle Lime Seasoning Blend)
2 tbsp Wildtree Roasted Garlic Grapeseed Oil

Directions:
Freezer Bag Preparation Put all ingredients in a gallon ziplock bag, mix together, expel air, close, label, double bag, and freeze.
Defrost bag in refrigerator.
Heat skillet and cook meat until done.
Serve with tortillas and rice and condiments
