Title:
Mom's Christmas Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2997366.jpg
Ingredients:
4 1/2 c. sugar
3/4 c. margarine
1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
2 1/2 lbs. Hershey bars (5 1/2  7oz. bars)
12 oz. package semi-sweet chocolate chips
7 oz. jar marshmallow cream
2 tsp. vanilla
pinch of salt

Directions:
Put the Hershey bars, chocolate chips, mallow, vanilla and salt in a large bowl.
Mix together the sugar, margarine and evaporated milk in a sauce pan. Boil for 5 minutes, stirring most of the time.
Pour the sugar mixture over the chocolate and stir until mixed.
Pour into a greased jelly-roll pan. Let set before cutting.

Submitter: 
Lisa Eagen
Description: 
Mom's fudge melts in your mouth! Add crushed pecans or chopped Andes mints to add some more flavor.
