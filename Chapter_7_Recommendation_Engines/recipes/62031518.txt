Title:
Chili Releno Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Monterey Jack cheese, grated
3 or 4 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup flour
salt and pepper to taste
1 large can Whole chilies

Directions:
Beat together milk, flour, eggs, salt and pepper. Layer in a greased 9x13 casserole dish, some chilis, then pour some of the milk/flour/egg mixture over the chilis, then some cheese. Repeat layers till all ingredients are used. Bake at 350 for 50 minutes.

Submitter: 
Michele O'Sullivan
Description: 
Chilis oozing with melted cheese in an egg mixture.
