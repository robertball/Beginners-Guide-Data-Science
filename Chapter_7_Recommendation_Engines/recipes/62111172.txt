Title:
Cherry, Chocolate, and Buttermilk Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Scones
_______________________
1 Cup all-purpose flour
1 Cup cake flour
1/4 Cup sugar
2 Teaspoons baking powder
1/2 Teaspoon baking soda
1/4 Teaspoon salt
1 Teaspoon orange zest (yummy!)
8 Tablespoons (1 stick) unsalted butter, cold
3/4 Cup dried cherries, sweet or sour (maybe a little of both!)
1/2 Cup miniture chocolate chips
2/3 Cup buttermilk
Topping
________________
3 Tablespoons sugar
1/4 Teaspoon cinnimon

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Lightly flour a work surface.
In large bowl mix the flours, sugar, baking powder and baking soda, salt, orange zest with wire whisk.
Cut butter into 1/2 inch cubes and add to flour mixture. Using the paddle of a stand  mixer or your finger or a pastry cutter, cut the butter into flour mixture untill it is a fine texture and looks a little like cornmeal with evenly dispersed chunks of butter
Stir in cherries, chocolate chips, and buttermilk, stirring just until the moment of dough becomes wet andd sticky.
Turn the dough out onto the floured work surface and pat it into 1-inch thick circle. Cut the circle into 8 wedges. Mix the cinnimon and sugar together on a small plate. Press the top of each wedge heavily into sugar mixture and place the scones evenly spaced on the baking sheet with the sugared side on top.
BAKE UNTIL FIRM TO THE TPUCH ON TOP AND LIGHTLY GOLDEN BROWN, 15 TO 20 MINUTES. SERVE WARM AND WITH MILK.
