Title:
Easy Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans Baked Beans with brown sugar
2 oz spicy ground pork sausage
Tabasco sauce

Directions:
Brown sausage in skillet. Add beans and sausage to casserole dish. Sprinkle with tabasco sauce, if desired. Heat in oven 325F for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Gina
Description: 
Simple and fast, with spicy pork sausage to jazz it up.
