Title:
Shrimp Wrapped with Prosciutto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4540484.jpg
Ingredients:
18 raw extra large shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 lemon, zested and juiced
Olive oil
1/4 pound imported prosciutto, thinly sliced
Fresh basil for garnis

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees
Place shrimp in shallow dish and add lemon zest and juice.
Wrap each with a piece of prosciutto and place on a baking sheet (lined with parchment paper).  Drizzle with a little olive oil.
Bake for 8 minutes.
Drizzle extra olive oil and  chiffonade fresh basil for garnish!
