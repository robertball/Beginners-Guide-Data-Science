Title:
Fudgy Pecan Pie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST:
1 -19 oz. box of your favorite fudge brownie mix (bake as directed on box for a 13"x9" pan)
FILLING:
3 large eggs
3/4 cup corn syrup
3/4 cup sugar
2 T. butter, melted
1 t. vanilla extract
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
1  1/2 coarsely chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a 13x9" baking pan.  Prepare brownie mix according to package directions.  Spread evenly in pan.  Bake for 10 minutes.  For the filling:  Beat eggs, corn syrup, sugar, butter and vanilla in a medium bowl.  Stir in chips and nuts.  Pour evenly over partially baked crust.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until set. Cool in pan.

Submitter: 
Amy Brolsma
Description: 
A cross between a brownie and pecan pie, who could ask for anything more!
