Title:
Karyn's Awesome HOT Crockpot  Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground turkey or beef
1 28 oz can pureed tomatoes
1 6 oz can tomato paste
1 green pepper diced
2 jalapeno peppers diced
1 onion diced
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 packet chili seasoning mix
2 tablespoons red pepper
2 15 oz cans red kidney beans
1 tablespoon steak sauce
salt to taste

Directions:
In large skillet, brown ground turkey or beef. Drain fat. In crockpot add meat and remaining ingredients. Cook on low for 6-8 hours.Yum!!

Submitter: 
KARYN821
Description: 
This chili recipe is really a mixture of recipes I have encountered through time. This is a thick, hearty chili.  I have gotten many many compliments on it. Enjoy!!
