Title:
Spicy Mexican Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - 5 lb. chuck roast
1 24 oz. jar salsa
1 medium onion chopped
2 tsp. chili powder
1 1/2 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. oregano
2 cloves garlic minced
1 7 oz. can green chilis mild

Directions:
Cut chuck roast into chunks trimming off fatty areas. Put in crock pot with all other ingredients. Cook for 10-12 hours on low. Remove lid, and cook for 1 more hour on high. Remove roast with slotted spoon, shred with fork. Add liquid from crock pot, a tablespoon at a time until desired consistency.

Submitter: 
KMFLOWERS
Description: 
Great for burritos or tacos. A favorite for my family on Cinco de Mayo. Great when served with an avocado salsa that has avocado, tomatoes, onions, cilantro and lime juice.
