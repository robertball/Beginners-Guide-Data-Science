Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds beef tips, cut into small to medium size pieces
2 cans of cream of mushroom
1 small onion cut up
1/2 cup of butter
1 package of Mushrooms (optional)
1 pckage of wide egg noodles

Directions:
Mix 1/2 cup of butter, chopped onions, sliced mushrooms, 2 cans of soup together, place meat in the crock pot and cook for 8 hours on low.
Boil noodles till al dente, drain and serve with meat mixture on top.
We enjoy it with butter bread.

Submitter: 
Starr
Description: 
Was in a hurry one day and threw this all together in the crockpot, When I got home I was so suprised because it tastes like Beef strogranoff made the hard way.. So much simpler
