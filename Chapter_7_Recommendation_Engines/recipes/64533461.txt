Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11 green apples, peeled, cored & sliced 1/4" thick (no smaller)
1 cup white sugar
t tbsp all purpose flour
1 tsp ground cinnamon
2 containers raspberries
TOPPING
2 cups quick cooking oats
2 cup all purpose flour
2 cups packed brown sugar
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Place the sliced apples in a 9x13 inch pan. Mix the white sugar, 1 tbsp of flour & ground cinnamon together and sprinkle over apples. Stir so all apples are covered in spices.
In a bowl combine the oats, 1 cup flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda & melted butter together. Crumble evenly over the apple mixture. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until apples are fork tender.
