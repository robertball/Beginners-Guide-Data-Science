Title:
M's Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029999.jpg
Ingredients:
1	cup heavy cream
¼	cup butter
¾	cup grated parmesan cheese
1	dash garlic powder
¼	teaspoon black pepper
2	tablespoons shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat butter and cream in saucepan over medium low heat.  Once warm whisk in the parmesan cheese.  Once incorporated add garlic powder and pepper.  Continue to heat until it thickens to the consistency you like.   Stir in the shredded parmesan cheese,  stirring until it is melted and smoothly incorporated into the sauce.
