Title:
Mitchell's Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 9 inch prepared graham cracker crust
2 boxes chocolate pudding or pie filling mix
1 8oz container Cool Whip, optional

Directions:
Prepare pudding or pie filling mix per directions on box.
Pour into crust.
Chill in refrigerator at least 3 hours, preferably overnight.
Prior to serving, spread Cool Whip over top of the pie.
ENJOY!
