Title:
Citrus Marinated Roasted Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup grated orange rind
4 cups fresh orange juice (about 8)
16 cloved garlic crushed
1 tbsp. red pepper flakes
8 sprigs fresh rosemary
8 sprigs fresh thyme
2 tbsp. whole black peppercorns
1/2 Cup EVO
1 12 / 14 lb. Turkey
1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
coursely ground pepper

Directions:
1. Combine all marinade ingredients in disposable aluminum roasting pan, large enough to hold the turkey and to allow the marinade to rise high enough to coat about half the turkey.  Rinse turkey well and pat dry.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Place in marinade.  Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours, or overnight, turning turkey several times to evenly marinade.
2. Preheat barbeque
3. Remove turkey from marinade and strain solids.  Stuff inside turkey and grill until done, according to weber directions.
