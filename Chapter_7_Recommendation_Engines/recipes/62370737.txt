Title:
Almond Raisin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup raisins
1 1/2 cup water
1 heaping Tbsp of butter
2 3/4  cup of flour
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp baking soda
1 egg
1 cup of sugar
1 cup of almond paste, grated
1 cup of walnuts

Directions:
Cook raisins in water for 4 minutes
Add the butter.
Let this mixture coo. Meanwhile,
Beat the egg with the sugar.
Mix together flour, salt and baking soda.
Add to the egg mixture,alternating with the cooled raisins and water.
Finally stir in the almond paste and nuts.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
Makes 2 loaves- Yum!

Submitter: 
Cindy Yonkman
Description: 
From Grandma Yonkman- delicious! She adds do not try to substitute almond extract for almond paste- you need the texture of the almond paste!
