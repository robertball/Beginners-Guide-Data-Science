Title:
Veggie Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg crescent rolls
2-8oz cream cheese, softened
3/4c mayonnaise
1 pkg dry ranch dressing mix
1/2c shredded cheddar
3/4c each cut up vegetables as you like- carrots, broccoli, cucumber, green onion, etc.

Directions:
Spread crescent rolls on the bottom of a greased jelly roll pan.
Smooth seams well and bake @ 350 for 10 minutes.
Mix cream cheese, mayonnaise, and dressing mix.
Spread cheese mix on cooled crust.
Layer with cheese and chopped vegetables.
Cover and regrigerate until ready to serve.
Cut into squares.
