Title:
Cranberry Almond Snack Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3073491.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sliced almonds
1/2 cup almond meal
3/4 cup dried cranberries
3/4 cup dates, pitted
4 tbsp almond butter
1 tsp almond extract

Directions:
Add cranberries & dates to food processor
Combine until a paste forms
Toss in remainder ingredients & combine for just a few seconds
Then wet hands with water to shape into 1" balls

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
A sweet no bake snack treat made with simple ingredients and in just minutes. A healthy lunchbox snack!
