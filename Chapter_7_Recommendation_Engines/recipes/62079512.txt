Title:
Italian Sausage Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
4 Tbls. olive oil
1 28oz. can CRUSHED tomatoes
2 cups water
2 Tbl. dried oregano
2 Tbl. dried basil
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
4-5 links sweet Italian sausage, cut into 2 inch pieces (about 1 1/2 lbs.)
1 large onion, roughly chopped
1 bell pepper, any color you like (yellow is nice)cut into bite sized pieces.
2 cups fresh green beans, cut into bite sized pieces
2 SMALL zucchini, cut into 1 inch rounds
2 med. carrots, cut into 1 inch rounds
3 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into bite sized pieces
1 Tbls. sugar
salt - to taste
ground black pepper- to taste

Directions:
Start by sweating the garlic and onion in the olive oil until translucent and tender.
Add the bell pepper and saute for 5 minutes.
Add Italian sausage pieces cooking just long enough to  brown them.  Add the carrots, the can of crushed tomatoes, water,oregano, basil, red pepper flakes, sugar, 1 tsp. salt, and 1 tsp. freshly ground black pepper.
Simmer, covered, on low heat for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
Now add the zucchini, green beans, and potatoes.
Simmer on low heat for an additional 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
When the potatoes are tender, the stew is done.  Add additional salt and pepper to taste.
Enjoy with good crusty bread.

Submitter: 
Cyndi K.
Description: 
This is a hearty, simple, filling, and delicious meal, especially on a cold and blustery day.  The only thing it needs is a loaf of really good crusty bread and maybe a glass of hearty red wine. Very satisfying.
