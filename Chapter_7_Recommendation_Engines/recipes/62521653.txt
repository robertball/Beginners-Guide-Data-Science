Title:
Beer Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 dark beer
4 strips cooked bacon
2 large cans baked beans
1/4 c catsup
1/4 c brown sugar or 3 T molasses
1/4 c brown mustard
1 diced onion

Directions:
use an oven safe dutch oven
spray with pam
mix all ingredients and pour in pan
bake 1 hour at 350 degrees or until thick and bubbly
