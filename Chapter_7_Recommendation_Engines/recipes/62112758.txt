Title:
Meringue Torte with Cherry or Blueberry Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 egg whites
2 C. sugar
3/4 tsp. cream of tarter
1 tsp. vanilla
2 C. crumbled soda crackers
1 C. chopped walnuts
1 1/2 large containers of Cool Whip
1 large cream cheese
1 can cherry pie, blueberry or your favorite flavor pie filling.

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees.
Beat egg whites until stiff but ont dry.
Add cream of tarter and sugar gradually while beating.
Add vanilla
Fold in crackers and walnuts.
Bake in lightly greased 9x13 pan for 25 minutes.
Cool.
Beat the whipped topping and cream cheese together until well blended.
Spread evenly over the cooled torte.
Spoon on 1 can of cherry pie filling or your favorite flavor.
Serve immediately.
