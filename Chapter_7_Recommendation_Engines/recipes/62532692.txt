Title:
Marla's favorite:  Stuffed mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package mushrooms
1 clove minced garlic
1/4 cup butter
2/3 cup bread crumbs
1/2 shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Rinse and drain mushrooms
Remove stems.  Blend them in food processor.
Season the bread crumbs in a mixing bowl.  Be creative (or use pre-seasoned bread crumbs!)
In saucepan, melt the butter and add the garlic.
Add the cook the mushroom stems to the saucepan.  Mix until it's smooth
Add garlic, cheese, and then seasoned bread crumbs.
Spoon mixture into mushrooms
Bake at 425 for 15 mins.  Stuffing should be lightly browned.  Bake longer if necessary.

Submitter: 
scott_ma
Description: 
Stuffed Mushrooms with a simple stuffing.  Easy to make and doesn't contain seafood!  The kids love them.  Try getting smaller mushrooms... the bigger mushrooms just don't taste as good.
