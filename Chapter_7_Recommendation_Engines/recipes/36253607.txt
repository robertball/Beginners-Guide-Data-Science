Title:
Berried Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups Honey Maid Graham Cracker Crumbs
1/4 cup sugar
6 TBS. butter or margerine, melted
1 8oz package of Philedelphia Cream Cheese, softened
2 TBS. cold milk
1 8oz tub Cool Whip topping, thawed, divided
1 qt. strawberries, hulled, halved
3-1/2 cups cold milk
2 pkg (4-servinf size) Jell-O Vanilla Flavor Instant Pudding and Pie Filling

Directions:
Mix crumbs, sugar and butter in 13x9 pan; press onto bottom of pan.  Refrigerate 15 minutes.
Beat Cream cheese and 2 TBS. milke in a large bowl with wire whisk until well blended.  Gently stir in half of the whipped topping.  Spread over crust; top with strawberries.  Pour 3-1/2 cups of milk into large bowl.  Add dry pudding mixes.  Beat 2 minutes.  Pour over strawberries.
Refrigerate 4 hours or until set.  Spread remaining whip topping just before serving.
