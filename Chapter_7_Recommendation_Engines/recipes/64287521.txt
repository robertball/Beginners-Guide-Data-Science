Title:
Granola, Yogurt, & Fruit Parfait Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 cups vanilla yogurt
2 cups your favorite granola (get a few options)
2 cups mixed fresh berries ( raspberries, blueberries, strawberries (hulled and sliced)

Directions:
Hull and slice berries if needed.
Set out yogurt, granola, and berries with serving spoons.
Have students or staff serve if necessary to control portions.
