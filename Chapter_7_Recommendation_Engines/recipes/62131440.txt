Title:
Sarah's Mushroom Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, minced
¼ cup butter
3 packages fresh mushrooms, cleaned, stems removed, sliced
16 oz. light sour cream
5 cups dry dumpling-style egg noodles, cooked
1 clove fresh garlic, minced (from jar ok)
1 tablespoon flour
1/2-1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 can cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
Sauté onions and garlic in butter until soft.
Add mushrooms, cook until tender.
Stir flour into soup, add soup, salt/pepper to mixture.  Whisk well.  Heat through.
Remove from heat.
Stir in sour cream.
Serve warm over noodles and with toast.

Submitter: 
Sarah Dipity
Description: 
Creamy vegetarian mushroom stroganoff.  Great served over toast or biscuits and with a side salad.
