Title:
Ham, Green Bean, Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ham, in 1 inch cubes
2 cans white potatoes, cut in half
2  cans cut green beans
¼ c. dried onions
½ t. dried garlic
¼ t. black pepper

Directions:
Put ham in crockpot.  Add potatoes and beans with the juice.  Season with  onions, garlic, black pepper and salt if necessary.  Stir.  Let cook in crock pot until warmed through.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
This is a great recipe from a friends that used to go to our church.  We made this on our camping trip to Minnesota with our church family.
