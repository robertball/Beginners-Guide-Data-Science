Title:
Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads broccoli, cut into bit-size pieces
2 pkgs of bacon, cooked, crumbled
1/2 red onion, chopped
raisins
sunflower seeds
1 cup mayo
1/2 cup sugar
2 Tbsp. red wine vinegar

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in a large bowl.  Mix last 3 ingredients in a small bowl until blended.  Pour over salad, stir, chill & enjoy!
