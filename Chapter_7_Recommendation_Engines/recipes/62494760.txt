Title:
Gramma's French Toast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 c bread cubes 2"x2" lightly toast before (cubing)
6-7 eggs
2 c milk
1/4 c white sugar, divided
1/4 c brown sugar, divided
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 tbsp margarine, softened
3 oz creme cheese, softened
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly butter an 9 x 13inch baking pan.
Line bottom of pan with bread cubes. In a large bowl, beat together eggs, milk, 1/2 white and brown  sugar, salt and vanilla and nutmeg. pour egg mixture over bread. Dot with margarine and creme cheese; let stand for 15 minutes.
Combine remaining sugars with 2 teaspoon cinnamon and sprinkle over the top.  Bake in preheated oven about 40 to 50 minutes, until top is golden.
Serve with berries, syrup, whipped creme etc
