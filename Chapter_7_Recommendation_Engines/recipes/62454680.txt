Title:
Val's Lower Sugar Pumpkin Dump cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin
1 (12 fluid ounce) can nonfat evaporated milk
4 eggs
1/2 cup Splenda Baking Blend
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground cloves
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 cup chopped pecans or walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.
In a large bowl, whisk together the pumpkin, Splenda Baking Blend, salt, nutmeg, ginger, cloves and cinnamon.  Stir in the milk, then beat in the eggs one at a time.  Pour pumpkin mixture into the prepared pan.
Sprinkle the yellow cake mix over the pumpkin mixture, then sprinkle on the nuts.  Drizzle melted butter over all.  Bake in the preheated oven for 55 minutes, or until the edges are lightly browned. Allow to cool.

Submitter: 
Valerie Kasper
Description: 
A delicious cake with changes suitable for diabetics or those just desiring a lower sugar content.Modified from: Aunt Janet's Pumpkin Dump Cake
