Title:
Sylvia Wood's Pan-Fried Orange-Ginger Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 egg whites
1/4 cup 1% low-fat milk
1 pound boned and skinned chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon cracked black pepper
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup olive oil
1 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/2 teaspoon Sylvia's Secret Seasoning®*
1 1/2 cups fresh orange juice
1/2 cup water
2 tablespoons sliced fresh ginger
4 teaspoons cornstarch
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Whisk egg whites until frothy; gradually whisk in milk. Set aside.
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper and dip in egg white mixture. Dredge in flour, shaking off excess.
Cook chicken in hot oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally until chicken is lightly browned. Drain chicken on paper towels.
Combine SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, Sylvia's Secret Seasoning(R), orange juice, 1/2 cup water and ginger in a small saucepan. Bring SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener mixture to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring constantly.
Combine cornstarch and 1/4 cup water, stirring until cornstarch dissolves; add to SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener mixture, stirring until mixture returns to a boil. Add chicken and cook 2 to 3 minutes. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Use this no-fuss recipe for a quick weeknight meal. Serve with rice and steamed broccoli for a complete meal.
