Title:
Scottley's Marinated Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 15 oz. cans aparagus spears, drained
1/2 cup prepared Italian salad dressing
1/4 tsp Kosher salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 cup grated parmesean cheese

Directions:
Drain both cans of asparagus being careful not to break the spears.  Wisk together the dressing, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl with a fork.  Gently place aspargus in serving dish and drizzle with marinade.  Marinate for five minutes, garnish with cheese, and serve.

Submitter: 
Scottley
Description: 
You won't believe how good this is, how simple it is to make, and how quickly it comes together.  I like it with a dry-rubbed grilled chuck steak, but it also pairs well with chicken, mild fish, and pork dishes.
