Title:
Raspberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen raspberries
1-2 heaping tablespoons protein powder
8 oz. cran water or filtered water
tablespoon flaxseed oil
1/4 teaspoon Stevia Plus or to taste

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in blender.
