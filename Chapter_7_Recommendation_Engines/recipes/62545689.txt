Title:
Cafe Iced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/526786.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Black Tea Bags
1 Lemon Zinger Tea Bag
1 Mandarin Orange Spice Tea Bag
1 Peppermint Tea Bag
1 Raspberry Zinger Tea Bag
4 cups water

Directions:
Boil 4 cups of water.   Pour into heat proof glass measuring cup and add all tea bags.  Brew for 5 minutes.
Split tea between 2 1 gallon pitchers.
Add 11 cups of cold water to each pitcher (less if you prefer a stronger tea).
Refrigerate for an hour.
Serve over ice and enjoy!
