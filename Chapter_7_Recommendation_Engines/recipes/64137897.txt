Title:
Fiesta Fry Pan Meal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb ground beef
1/2-1 onion, chopped (per taste)
2 cloves garlic, crushed
3 medium carrots, chopped into small pieces
4 ribs celery, chopped
2 cups diced zucchini
1.5 cups water, divided
2 Tbsp 'Better Than Bouillon' beef base
1 Tbsp taco seasoning (your own or bought)
2 tsp dried oregano
2 tsp dried basil
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp pepper, fresh ground
1 cup salsa, medium or mild
1 14oz can diced tomatoes (with seasoning or plain)
1 5oz can tomato paste
1 10oz can corn

Directions:
1. Fry ground beef over med-high heat until no longer pink. Drain if needed.
2. Add garlic and onion & saute until clear. I add a bit of water if getting dry.
3. Add carrots, celery, and spices & saute with some water for approx 6-8 minutes.
4. Add zucchini & continue to saute (adding water as needed) until veggies tender.
5. Add salsa, tomatoes, and tomato paste.  Use a bit of water to rinse the cans and add to pot.  Heat until  bubbling.  Turn heat down to simmer.
6. Add corn.  Heat for 2 minutes & serve or leave on stove for 30 minutes to blend all the spices.
7. Serve with rice or pasta.
