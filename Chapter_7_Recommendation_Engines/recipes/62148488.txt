Title:
Italian Hotdogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 potatoes. peeled and sliced (or cubed)
1 onion, thinly sliced
1 green pepper, thinly sliced
8 hot dogs
1 cup monteray jack with jalapeno cheese,shredded
8 hoagie club rolls
2 tb olive oil

Directions:
In a medium frying pan heat olive oil, add potatoes cook on medium for 10 mins.
add onions and pepper,cook additional 7 mins or until soft.
preheat oven to 350
while veggies are cooking preheat grill
grill hotdogs until done
slice hoagie rolls
place a butterflied hotdog in the roll and top with veggies
top all with cheese
place in oven just until cheese is melted.
