Title:
Sweet Gingered Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 chicken wings
1 c flour
2 tsp salt
2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 c honey
1/4 c frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
1/2 tsp ground ginger

Directions:
Combine flour, salt, paprika, and pepper.
Coat chicken wings, shake off excess, place on large greased baking sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, remove from oven and drain.
Combine honey, orange juice concentrate, and ginger.
Brush generously over wings.
Bake at 325 degrees for 30-40 minutes or until chicken tests done, basting occasionally with more sauce.
