Title:
Potato Soup with Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1648504.jpg
Ingredients:
5 potatoes, diced
1 cup diced fully cooked ham
2/3 cup finely chopped leek
1/3 cup diced celery
1/3 cup diced carrots
1/4 cup butter
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon dill
1 bay leaf
salt to taste
coarsely ground black pepper to taste
48 fluid ounces low-sodium chicken broth

Directions:
Combine potatoes, ham, leek, celery, carrots, butter, garlic powder, dill, and bay leaf in a slow cooker; season with salt and pepper. Pour chicken broth over the mixture.
Cook on Low for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
combatbootmom
Description: 
Easy slow cooker potato soup with ham. Perfect for a wintry day and makes the house smell delicious. Serve with buttered toast and follow with warm apple pie.
