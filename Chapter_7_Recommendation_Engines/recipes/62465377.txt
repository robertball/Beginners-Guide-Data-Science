Title:
Almost No Fat Banana Bread II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup white sugar
1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 t salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 egg whites
1 t vanilla
1 cup banana, mashed
1/4 cup applesauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease an 8x4 inch loaf pan.
In a large bowl, stir together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and cinnamon.  Add egg whites, bananas and applesauce; stir just until combined. Pour batter into prepared pan.  Top with brn sugar mixed with a little water and drizzle on top.
Bake in preheated oven for 50 to 55 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of loaf comes out clean. Turn out onto wire rack and allow to cool before slicing.
