Title:
Creamy  Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1233092.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups fresh strawberries, hulled
1 medium ripe banana, peeled and halved
1/2 cup raw cashews
1 tablespoon natural cane sugar (optional)
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
1 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Place all ingredients in Oster(R) Versa(R) Performance Blender in order listed.
Select programmed SMOOTHIE setting.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Oster® Versa®
Description: 
Fresh strawberries, banana, cashews and coconut milk make a delicious smoothie in just minutes.
