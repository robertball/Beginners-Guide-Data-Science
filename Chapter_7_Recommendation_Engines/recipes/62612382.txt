Title:
Thin crust Chicken Bacon Ranch Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can condensed chicken soup (+ 2tbsp water)
1 Chef Boyardee pizza crust mix
4 raw chicken tenders, Diced
1 1/2 cup Mexican blend shredded cheese
1/2 cup bacon bits
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup ranch dressing (+ 2tsp.milk)
* Sauteed Onions and Mushrooms.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425'. Make Pizza crust according to box instructions. I sprinkled Garlic powder on the dough (according to taste).I added 2 tbsp water to the soup and stirred until creamy.and spoothed evenly to top of crust.I then sprinkled 1/2 cup Parmesan over the soup.(next layer). I used some raw chicken tenders and sauteed them with some onion and mushrooms and added this to the pizza. I then added bacon bits and shredded cheese. Baked at 425' for 10 minutes, I then removed it from the oven, drizzled the ranch dressing(add 2 tsp milk to thin it out a bit)..put back in the oven for 7 min. Let cool 2 minutes and Enjoy!
