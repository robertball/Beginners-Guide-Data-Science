Title:
40 garlic chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lb 4oz chicken
1/2 lemon
40 garlic peeled
2 T evoo
4 sprigs thyme
2 sprigs rosemary
4 sprigs parsley
1 carrot
2 celery
1 onion
1 1/2 c white wine
Crusty bread

Directions:
Rub chicen with oil. Place lemon and 4 cloves inside chicken.
Make a bed with remaining veggies, pour on oil and wine.
Cover tightly, bake 400* 1 1/4 hour.
Squeeze garlic onto bread, make gravy with juices.
