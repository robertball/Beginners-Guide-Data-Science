Title:
Swedish Meatballs IV with Sour Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/649234.jpg
Ingredients:
*** Meatballs ***
1/2 lb ground beef
1/2 lb ground pork
1/2 lb ground veal
1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
1/4 cup finely minced onion
1/4 cup half & half
3 tablespoons snipped parsley
1 teaspoon Jesse's Cajun Seasoning or salt
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
*** Sour Cream Sauce ***
3 tablespoons real butter
3 tablespoons flour
1 1/2 cup beef stock
1/4 teaspoons Jesse's Cajun Seasoning or salt
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Process all Meatball ingredients in a food processor for until it's a fine paste. Shape into 1 1/4 inch balls by placing into a square of cellophane, twisting it tight to condense and shape the meatballs. Place meatballs in Pyrex baking dish and bake for 25 minutes in preheated 375 degree oven.
While meatballs are cooking, heat the butter in a saucepan over low heat. Stir in flour and cook till bubbly. Remove from heat and stir in remaining ingredients except sour cream. Boil and stir one minute till thickened. Remove from heat and stir in sour cream. Serve over meatballs.

Submitter: 
Jesse Cody
Description: 
Classic Swedish meatballs seasoned to perfection with a rich sour cream sauce that will keep your guests coming back for more.
