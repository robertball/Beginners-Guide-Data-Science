Title:
Tin Roof Sundaes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 half gallon vanilla ice cream
1 cup mini marshmallows
1 cup spanish peanuts
2 cups chocolate syurp

Directions:
Scoup 2  rounds of ice cream into each dish.
Divide peanuts and marshmallows equally amongst dishes.
Cover with chocolate syurp and serve.
