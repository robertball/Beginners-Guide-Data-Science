Title:
Double Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 cup butter - softened
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/4 cups flour
1/3 cup cocoa powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
3 cups uncooked quick oats
6 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix sugar and butter in a large bowl until fluffy.
Add egg and vanilla and mix well.
Sift together dry ingredients and stir into creamed mixture.
Add oats and chocolate chips.
Dough will be very stiff - add up to 1/4 cup water if necessary.
Roll into tablespoon sized balls and place on lightly greased cookie sheet and flatten slightly.
Bake 10-12 minutes.
