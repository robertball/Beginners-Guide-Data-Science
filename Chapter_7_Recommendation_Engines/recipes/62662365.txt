Title:
Cucumber and Tuna Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
200g tin Tuna in Olive Oil - drained and mashed
Small handful of fresh Flatleaf Parsley - chopped
Lemon Juice
Â½ small Red Onion - finely chopped
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
1 Cucumber - Cut in small  5 mm to 1 cm thick slices - peeled if you prefer
Additional Extras to  taste - Olives, Capers and Anchovies - finely chopped

Directions:
Mix the tuna with olive oil, parsley, lemon juice and red onion and season with salt and pepper;
Be adventurous and add some finely chopped olives, capers and anchovies;
Place the tuna mixture on top of each cucumber slice and serve

Description: 
A piquant, tasty appetizer fit for any occasion. The beauty is you can choose your own mixture and serve different ones whenever you wish.
