Title:
Paleo Yam Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 yams, thinly sliced
1 tablespoon coconut oil
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil; add yams. Cook until very soft, about 8 minutes. Drain; peel off skins.
Heat coconut oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add yams; cook and stir until browned, 5 to 10 minutes. Sprinkle cinnamon on top.
