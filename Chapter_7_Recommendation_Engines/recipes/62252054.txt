Title:
Texas Crock Pot Carne Guisada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds top beef sirloin, cubed
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium green bell pepper, chopped
1 cup tomatoes, diced
3 fresh jalapenos, chopped, seeded and stems removed 3 teaspoons garlic, minced
2 teaspoons ground cumin
2 teaspoon chili powder
2 teaspoons oregano
2 teaspoons salt
2 cups beef broth
1 tablespoon cornstarch

Directions:
Place all ingredients except corn starch into a crock pot. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch with with 4 tablespoons of beef stock and add to pot. Cook on high for 4 hours or low for 6.

Submitter: 
Armywife
Description: 
Chunks of sirloin, simmered and infused with tomatoes, bell pepper, onion, garlic, and  seasonings which makes the guisada (gravy). Serve as tacos with warm tortillas or over spanish rice.Modified from: South Texas Carne Guisada
