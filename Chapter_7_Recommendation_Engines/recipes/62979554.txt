Title:
Best Ever Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup very cold water

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Cut in shortening and butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in water until mixture forms a ball. Divide dough in half, and shape into balls. Wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
Roll out dough on a floured counter.  Don't over work it.  Use as directed in pie recipe.

Submitter: 
Donna Campisano
Description: 
Some flour, vegetable shortening, water and a pinch of salt is all that 's needed to make this very flaky crust that will make your favorite pie even better than usual.Modified from: Best Ever Pie Crust
