Title:
Fudgy Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T margarine
4 oz unsweetened chocolate
1/3 c skim milk
1/3 c apricot preserves
1 egg yolk
1 t vanilla
1/2 c all purpose flour
1 1/2 c sugar
1/2 t baking powder
1/8 t salt
3 egg whites
1/8 t cream of tartar
1/3 c coarsely chopped walnuts

Directions:
Heat margarine, chocolate, milk and apricot preserves in small saucepan, whisking frequently, until chocolate is almost melted. Remove from heat; whisk in egg yold and vanilla; mix in combied flour, sugar, baking powder and salt until smooth.
Beat egg whites and cream of tartar to stiff peaks in large bowl.  Fold chocolate mixture into egg whites; fold in walnuts if desired.  Pour batter into greased 8 inch square baking pan.
Bake in preheated 350' oven until brownies are firm to touch and toothpick comes out clean.  18 to 20 minutes (do not overbake).  Cool on wire rack.  Serve warm or at room temperature.
