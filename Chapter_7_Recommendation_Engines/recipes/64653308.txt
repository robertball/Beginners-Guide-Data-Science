Title:
Braised Apple Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Red cabbage julienned
Green cabbage julienned
Johnny red apples peeled and sliced
Sugar
Yellow onion julienned
Pepper
Butter Salt
Olive Oil
FUSTINI’S Cinnamon Pear Balsamic Vinegar
FUSTINI’S Red Apple Balsamic Vinegar

Directions:
In sauté pan with a mix of butter and oil, add onions and soften. Add cabbage, apples and the Cinamon Pear and the Apple Balsamic vinegars, just enough to coat the vegetables. Season with salt, pepper and sugar and cook to marry flavors.
