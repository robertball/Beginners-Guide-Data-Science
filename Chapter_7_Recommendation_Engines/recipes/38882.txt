Ingredients:
28 KRAFT Caramels, unwrapped
1 tablespoon water
60 OREO Mini Bite Size Chocolate Sandwich Cookies
4 (1 ounce) squares BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Baking Chocolate, melted

Directions:
Melt caramels with water in small saucepan on medium-low heat, stirring frequently until melted and smooth; set aside.
Arrange chocolate sandwich cookies in 20 clusters of 3 on large lightly greased cookie sheet.
Spoon 1 tsp. caramel mixture over each cluster of cookies; drizzle each with 1 tsp. melted chocolate.  Let stand until set.  Remove from cookie sheet.  Store in airtight container.
