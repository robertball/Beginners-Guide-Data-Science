Title:
Crustless Quiche With Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C roasted vegetables, broccoli, cauliflower, sweet potatoes, onions
Light EVOO , as needed to bake veggies
1 C shredded Swiss, Jarlsberg or sharp cheddar cheese
4 lg. eggs
2/3 C light cream or half and half
dash nutmeg
1-2 T shredded parmesan cheese for the top

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Lightly oil a 9-in. glass pie pan or spray with cooking spray. Arrange veggies in bottom of dish to mix colors.
Sprinkle w/cheese.
In large bowl, mix beaten eggs with cream. Add nutmeg and pour mixture over vegetables. Top with parmesan cheese.
Bake 35 min, until eggs are set and top as browned.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Oven roast or grill assorted veggies, put in a pie pan, bake with cheeses and light cream, then eat. So good.
