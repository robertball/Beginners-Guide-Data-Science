Title:
Marshmallow Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451467.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large can of fruit cocktail
1 can of mandarin oranges
1 20 oz. can of pineapple chunks
3 oz. cream cheese (slightly less than half a package)
8 oz. Cool Whip
1 bag of mini marshmallows
1 box (3 oz.) of strawberry flavored Jell-o
*Add other fruit, canned or fresh, if desired

Directions:
Soften cream cheese in large bowl.
Drain all canned fruit well.
Mix Cool Whip and dry Jell-o flavoring with the softened cream cheese using spoon or mixer.
Make sure all Jell-o is dissolved.
Add fruit and marshmallows to Jell-o mixture.
Coat fruit and marshmallows evenly but be careful not to stir too hard.
Chill for 30 minutes.
