Title:
Flavorful Slow Cooker Swiss Steak Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
salt and pepper to taste
2 pounds boneless round steak (1/2 inch thick) cut into eight pieces
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
4 medium onions, sliced
4 medium potatoes, quarted
1 pound baby carrots
1 (10.75 oz) can of condensed tomato soup, undiluted
1 (14.5 oz) can of diced tomatoes, with liquid
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon minced fresh garlic
1 bay leaf

Directions:
In a resealable bag, combine the flour, salt and pepper. Add steaks in batches and shake to coat.
Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Brown meat on both sides. Remove and place the meat into the slow cooker.
Add the onions, potatoes and carrots into the slow cooker.
In a bowl, combine the tomato soup, diced tomatoes and spices. Pour the mixture over the meat and vegetables.
Cover and cook on low for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Slow cooking the meat with vegetables and seasonings results in a very tender, juicy and flavorful steak, with dinner made in one pot.
