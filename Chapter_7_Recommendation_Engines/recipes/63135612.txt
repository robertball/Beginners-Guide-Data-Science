Title:
Chef John's Cajun Corn and Crab Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon melted butter
1/2 cup diced onion
1/2 cup diced bell pepper, or jalapeno, or combination of any sweet/hot peppers
1/2 cup diced celery
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon Old Bay
1 1/2 tbsp flour
3 1/2 cup water or stock, divided (2 1/2 for the pot, and 1 cup for the blender)
1 pound sweet corn kernels, divided
2 cloves peeled garlic
8 oz fresh crab meat, divided
1/4 cup cream
1 tsp sweet Spanish paprika, or to taste
green onion to garnish

Directions:
Add aromatic vegetables and butter over medium heat until the vegetables are done (from translucent to carmelized). Add flour, and seasonings. When the flour smells done, add 2 ½ C stock/broth/water to the pot, add ½ corn and about 2 oz crab (the rest will be added fresh at the end). Raise heat to medium high and bring to a simmer. While that is heating, in a blender, put the rest of the stock and the other half of corn AND THE PEELED GARLIC to the blender, Puree that mixture and stir it into the pot. Wait until it comes back to a simmer. When reaches simmer, decrease heat to low and let it reduce and meld for 35 to 40 minutes.
Add the cream, paprika and the rest of the crab. Spoon into bowls and garnish with green onions. YUM
