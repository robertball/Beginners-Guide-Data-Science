Title:
Paprika-Quinoa Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 md potatoes (~1 1/2 lbs), peeled & quartered
water
1 lg green bell pepper, diced
1c frozen diced onion
1 c quinoa
2 md chayote squash (~1 1/2 lbs), cored & diced
10oz frozen corn
1 16oz can black beans, drained
1T butter/spread
~2T milk
canola oil
~8oz tortilla chips, crushed
paprika
salt
cilantro
parsley
milled garlic/garlic powder
ginger
chili powder

Directions:
Place the potatoes in a pot and cover with water.  Boil until tender, then drain.
Meanwhile, place the peppers & onions in a large frying pan with just enough canola oil to coat them & the pan.  Place the pan on medium heat.
Once the onions are thawed, add the squash, corn, and beans.  Add paprika, salt, cilantro, parsley, and garlic to taste.  Add ginger and chili powder sparingly.
As soon as you do this, preheat the oven to 400 degrees F and prepare the quinoa with 2c of water, according to package instructions.  It should be finished cooking in 10-15 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine the cooked potatoes, butter, and milk.  Mash.
Add the quinoa, and salt, paprika & garlic to taste.  Add chili powder & ginger sparingly.  Whip together.
Add the vegetables and mix thoroughly.
Coat the bottom of a large casserole pan with canola oil.  Cover with the crushed tortilla chips.
Spoon the potato mixture into the casserole, careful not to disturb the tortilla chips.  Smooth.
Place in the oven for 8-10 minutes.  Serve.
