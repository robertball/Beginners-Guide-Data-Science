Title:
Old Fashion Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2 1/2 C Sugar
1/2 tsp.  salt
2 tsp Vanilla
1 12 oz. MILNOT
1/2 Gal. Milk

Directions:
Beat Eggs
Add Sugar, salt and Vanilla
BEAT thoroughly.
Add MILNOT & Milk.
FREEZE.
