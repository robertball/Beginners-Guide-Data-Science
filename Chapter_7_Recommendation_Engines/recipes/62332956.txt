Title:
He-Man Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For 3 Servings:
3 large chicken breasts
1/2 pound of Applewood Smoked Bacon
3 Cups of your favorie chili (no beans works best)
1 Cup Shredded Cheese (pepperjack or mexican blend)
1 Bottle sweet Barbecue sauce (Sticky fingers works best)
Bamboo skewers and toothpicks soaked in water

Directions:
1: Butterfly the Chicken breastsand lay them flat on a sheet of saran-wrap
2: Cover with another sheet of Saran wrap and flatten with a mallet
3: Lay the bamboo skewers parallel to the direction of your butterfly cut on the chicken
4: Spoon out about 2-3 Tsp of chili per piece of chicken over the skewer.
5: Sprinkle with 1-2 tsp of cheese per chicken
6: Roll the chicken/chili/cheese around the skewer and secure with toothpick.
7: Wrap with the bacon and secure with toothpicks.
8: Glaze with Barbecue sauce and grill with medium direct heat. Be sure to turn often and spray down the grill as the bacon will have a tendency to stick to the grill. Glaze once more just before removing from the grill.
9: Remove skewers/toothpicks and serve!

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a grilled-chicken recipie inspired by a dish found in Del Rio, TX. Truly a man's meal, and not for the faint of arteries, this dish combines three of men's favorite things: Grilling, Bacon, and Chili. Dont love it till you try it!
