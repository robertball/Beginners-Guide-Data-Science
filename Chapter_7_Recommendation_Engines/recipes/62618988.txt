Title:
Jill's Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6744885.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbl olive oil
1 onion, sliced
1 tbl garlic, crushed or I use Spice World, squeeze garlic.
4 Italian sausages, cut in 1/2 inch slices.
1 tsp Italian spices
1 tsp dryed basil
1 14.5 oz can Hunts Dice tomatoes (Basil, Garlic & Oregano)
1 8 oz can Hunts tomato sauce.

Directions:
Saute the onion and the garlic
Add the slices of sausage
Stir fry till sausages are browned
add the dry spices and cans of tomato sauces.
Simmer for 10 minutes.
