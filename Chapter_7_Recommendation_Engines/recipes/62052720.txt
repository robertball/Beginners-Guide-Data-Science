Title:
My Million Dollar PIe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can sweetened condensed milk
5 TBSP lemon juice
1 small container whipped topping
1 20 ounce can crushed pineapple(drained)
1/2 cup chopped, jarred cherries
1 cup nuts, chopped(your choice of kind)
1 graham cracker crust

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except crust.  Place in pie crust, and chill for 3 hours.  slice and serve.  Place a few whole cherries or pineapple slices on top with sprig of mint for a pretty presentation.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
I had a pie somewhat like this before, but didn't get the recipe.  So I fooled around in the kitchen and came up with this.  Enjoy!
