Title:
Oven BBQ Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1/2 can tomato soup
3 Tablespoons sugar
1/2 clove garlic, minced or pureed
dash of Tabasco Sauce

Directions:
Blend all ingredients together in a saucepan; bring to a simmer on medium heat.
While baking a pork roast baste frequently with the sauce.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This is a great tangy bbq sauce cooked on pork roast.  I double the recipe as to have extra to serve on the side.
