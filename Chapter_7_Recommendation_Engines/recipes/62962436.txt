Title:
Choclate Eclaire Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes French Vanilla Pudding
3 1/2 cups milk
12oz cool whip
1 box graham crackers
Milk Choclate frosting

Directions:
Mix the pudding & milk then add cool whip.
Layer Graham crackers, half of the white mixture, another layer of crackers, the rest of white mixture, layer of crackers then choclate frosting (mix a little milk w/ frosting)

Description: 
A tasty but easy pudding dessert.
