Title:
Cocoa Fudge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cake flour or 1 2/3 cups all-purpose flour*
1 1/2 cups sugar
2/3 cup cocoa
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups buttermilk
1/2 cup shortening
2 eggs (1/3 to 1/2 cup)
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Heat oven to 350F(180C)
Grease and flour a 13x9 baking pan or two 8 or 9" round layer pans.
Measure all ingredients into large mixing bowl and blend 30 seconds on low speed, scraping the bowl constantly.
Beat 3 minutes on high speed, scraping bowl occasionally and pour into pan(s)
Bake 13x9 for 35-40 minutes, or the two layers for 30-35 minutes until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool and frost as desired.
