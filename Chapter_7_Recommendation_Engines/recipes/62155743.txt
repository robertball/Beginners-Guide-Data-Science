Title:
Finger Jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/705805.jpg
Ingredients:
5 envelopes unflavored (Knox) jello
2 6oz. packages jello (any flavor)
2 1/2 cups cold water
1 cup sugar
2 cups hot water
1 cup cold water

Directions:
In a medium bowl, dissolve the unflavored jello in 2 1/2 cups cold water.  Set aside.
Combine the flavored jello, sugar and hot water in a large saucepan.  Bring to a boil and then add the unflavored jello mixture, stirring to dissolve.
Remove from heat and add 1 cup cold water.
Pour into a 13x9" pan.
Chill in the refrigerator until firm (about 3 hours).
When set, cut into bite-sized squares or use cookie cutters to make fun shapes!

Submitter: 
Alley
Description: 
This is the best finger jello recipe.  I've been making it for many years.  It's simple to make, and kids love it!  You can be very creative for parties or events by using cookie cutters to cut the jello.  Enjoy!
