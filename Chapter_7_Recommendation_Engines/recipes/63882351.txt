Title:
Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1 1/2 cups sugar
3/4 cup buttermilk
2 eggs, beaten until fluffy
2 cups flour
1/4 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup mashed bananas
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F(175C)
Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Mix shortening, buttermilk, sugar and the two beaten eggs, then add sifted ingredients in and mix.
Add in mashed bananas and vanilla, beat until well blended.
Lightly grease and flour bundt cake pan (or two 9" layer cake pans) and pour mix in. Bake for 35 minutes or until toothpick inserted comes out clean.
Frost as desired (I recommend buttercream)

Submitter: 
CBerrier86
Description: 
This is my grandmother's recipe for a banana cake, and my mom's choice for her birthday cake every year especially with home-made buttercream frosting!
