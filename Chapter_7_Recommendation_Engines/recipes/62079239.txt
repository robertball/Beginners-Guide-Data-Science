Title:
Southern Calico Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 lbs lean ground beef (you can do half sausage and half beef for more flavor)
5-7 slices of peppered bacon
1 onion
1 cup ketchup
2 tbs mustard
1 cup dark brown sugar
1 tbs liquid smoke
2 tbs honey
2 tbs A1 stake sauce
1 tsp pepper
2 cans of pork and beans, drained
1 can of large kidney beans, drained
1 can of large butter beans, drained
1 can of ranch style beans
salt to taste

Directions:
1. In a large pot brown beef/sausage. At the same time cook your bacon and cut it into small pieces. Cut the onion into small pieces and add to beef until it is transparent. Drain all grease once the meat is cooked through.
2. Add all other ingredients to pot and simmer for 15-20 minutes. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Angie
Description: 
These beans can be a meal all by themselves. They have an amazing flavor that everyone loves. People ask me to make this again and again. Try it, you'll love it! If you can cook it outside in an iron pot it will be even better! Really good with corn bread.
