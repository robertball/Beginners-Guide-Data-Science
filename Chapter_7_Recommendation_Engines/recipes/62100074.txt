Title:
Church Windows Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg semi sweet chocolate chips
1 stick margarine (NOT butter)
1 bag colored marshmallows
1 c walnuts (optional)
coconut

Directions:
In a saucepan, melt butter and chocolate.
Add walnuts if desired.
Let cool.
Spread 2 sheets of 20" long wax paper.
Coat with coconut.
Pour marshmallows in to pan and stir until coated.
Divide in half and pour on coconut to form a loaf.
Coat with extra coconut and roll in wax paper.
Put in fridge or freezer for several hours before cutting.
Slice 1/2" slices.
Store in fridge or freezer.

Submitter: 
Tina McKellar Musial
Description: 
This is an old family favorite...and cookie from the north.  No matter where else I've lived, no one has heard of it.
