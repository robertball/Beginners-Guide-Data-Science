Title:
Cinnamon Carmel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. butter
1 C brown sugar
1/4 C corn syrup
1/2 t. baking soda
Cinnamon to taste
1/2 C popcorn, popped

Directions:
Combine butter, sugar, and corn syrup in microwave-safe bowl.
Cook 2-3 min. Stir. Cook 1-2 min. (less time=gooey, more=crunchy)
Add baking soda and cinnamon, stir.
Pour over popped popcorn and mix with spoon
Microwave 30 sec. Stir.
Eat warm or spread on metal sheet or wax paper to cool.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This is a great party snack. Freshly popped popcorn smothered in cinnamon carmel. This is great for making popcorn balls. A great recipe for kids!
