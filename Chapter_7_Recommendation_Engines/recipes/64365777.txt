Title:
Bulgarian Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2169500.jpg
Ingredients:
16oz dried lentils, 10 cups water, 1/2 chopped white onion, 1/2 chopped red bell pepper, 1 carrot peeled and chopped, 1 (14.5oz) can of diced tomatoes, 1 tablespoon salt, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 garlic cloves chopped, 1 tablespoon Bulgarian Oregano, 1 teaspoon mint, 1 tablespoon paprika, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
add lentils and water in a large pot and cook on high heat
after it boils, add all other ingredients and cook until soup thickens and some lentils begin to crack (10-15 minutes)

Submitter: 
no_fun_at_all
Description: 
This is my favorite lentil soup. Its super easy to make, healthy and really hearty.
