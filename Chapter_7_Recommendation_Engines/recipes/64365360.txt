Title:
Mama D's Crab Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2160745.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 Red onion
1 cup Real or imitation crab meat
1 tspn Garlic powder
1 cup Peas
1/2 cup Mayonnaise or to taste
1 pkg English muffins or onion bagel

Directions:
Steam or boil peas
Finely chop red onion (only use 1/4 of onion per 1 cup of crab meat).
Finely chop crab meat
In a medium bowl combine crab meat, peas, red onion, garlic powder and mayonnaise.

Submitter: 
Danielle Livingston
Description: 
This recipe will have your taste buds tingling for more. A delightfully appetizing and low calorie recipe that you will want to share.
