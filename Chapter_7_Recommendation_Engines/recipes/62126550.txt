Title:
Swedish Pancakes III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white flour
1 tsp. salt
1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp. baking soda
1 c. sour cream
1 c. milk
1 c. water
1/4 c. oil
2 eggs

Directions:
In small bowl,stir flour, salt, sugar and baking soda together.
In larger bowl, blend sour cream, milk, water, oil and eggs.
Mix together with dry ingredients.
Cook in a hot buttered skillet. Add flour to thicken if desired.
