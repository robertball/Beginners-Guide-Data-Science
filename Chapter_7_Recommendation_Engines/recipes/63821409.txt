Title:
Halloween Zombie Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1059898.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
2 eggs
1 teaspoon Nature's Seasoning (or to taste)
1 small onion finely chopped
3/4 cup butter wheat crackers
1/2 cup finely shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
2/3 cups milk
1 hard boiled egg (cut in half)
Sauce:
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1/8 cup Stubb's barbecue sauce
Garlic chips for teeth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease medium sized cookie sheet (can cover with foil for easy clean up).
Mix together ground beef, eggs, Nature's Seasoning, onion, crackers, cheese, Worcestershire Sauce, and milk in mixing bowl.  Shape into desired shape on cookie sheet, leaving holes for the eyes and mouth.
Cut the hard boiled egg in half.  May use one or both for eyes.  Press meat mixture around eyes in desired shape.
Mix together brown sugar, ketchup, and barbecue sauce.  Spread half of the sauce mixture over the head, eyes, and mouth.
Bake in preheated oven for 50-60 minutes until cooked through, depending on thickness.
Place garlic chips in mouth opening for teeth.
Brush with remaining sauce or serve sauce over individual portions.
