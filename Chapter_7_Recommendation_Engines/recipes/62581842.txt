Title:
Black Swan Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c oil
4 Tbsp sour cream
5 Tbsp red wine vinegar
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp dry mustard
2 Tbsp sugar
2 Tbsp chopped parsley
2 crushed garlic cloves
pepper

Directions:
Blend sugar, garlic, mustard & sour cream in a food processor or blender. Add vinegar. Blend & slowly add a stream of oil. When all blended fold in parsley & pepper
