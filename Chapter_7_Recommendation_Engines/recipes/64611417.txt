Title:
Creamy Chipotle Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4181454.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cloves garlic minced
1 small onion diced
6 oz portobello mushrooms sliced
2 Chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
1 can green peas
2 T olive oil
.25 cup white wine
3 T butter
3 T flour
1.5 C milk
.5 C shredded sharp cheese
12 oz box pasta (I like shells)
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Prepare pasta per directions on the box. Sautee garlic, onions and mushrooms together in olive oil over medium low heat until translucent. Add chilies and 2 T adobo sauce.  Add wine and butter until butter is melted. Sprinkle flour over the top. Slowly add milk while stirring  continuously. Add cheese stir until melted. Add peas. Toss with cooked pasta.
