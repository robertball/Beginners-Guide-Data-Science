Title:
Graham Cracker Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2270415.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Graham Cracker
2 Tablespoon Peanut Butter, I used crunchy
2 Tablespoon jam or jelly, any flavor

Directions:
Spread 1 graham cracker with peanut butter them with jam. Top with remaining graham cracker.

Submitter: 
tcasa
Description: 
Quick and easy alternative to a peanut butter & jelly sandwich. Great as an after school snack. Growing up my mother use to make these all the time. Back then I was a fussy eater and would not eat anything in my lunch that was soggy.
