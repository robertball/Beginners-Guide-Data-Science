Title:
One Dish Chicken and Rice Bake(bone-in) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can(10 3/4 oz.) cream of mushroom
soup
1 1/2 cups water
3/4 cup uncooked white rice
1/4 tsp. paprika
freshly ground black pepper
4 chicken breast halves(about 2
pounds)

Directions:
In 2-quart shallow baking dish, mix soup, water, rice, paprika, and pepper. Place chicken on rice mixture. Sprinkle with additional paprika and pepper.
Bake at 375 degrees for one hour, or until chicken is no longer pink and rice is done.
