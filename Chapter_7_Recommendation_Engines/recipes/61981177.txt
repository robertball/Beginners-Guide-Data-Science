Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices bacon
1 large green pepper, chopped
1 large onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 cans(28 oz.)diced tomatoes
2 cups water
2 cups raw regular rice
2 Tbsp. chopped parsley
1 tsp. salt
generous amount black pepper

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat, fry bacon until crisp. Drain; crumble and set aside.
In a large saucepan over medium-high heat,in bacon drippings,cook green pepper, onion, and garlic until vegetables are tender-crisp.
Stir in remaining ingredients, except for bacon. Heat to boiling; reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 25 minutes, or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.
When ready to serve, sprinkle with reserved bacon.
