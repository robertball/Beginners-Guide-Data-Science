Title:
Five-Minute Chocolate Cake for One Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/566807.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
2 tbsp coconut flour
6 tablespoons sugar substitute
1 egg white
4 tbsp skim milk (or almond milk)
2 tablespoons plain nonfat yogurt (add more if you like)
2 tbsp non-fat dry milk
3 tablespoons sugar-free chocolate syrup
2 mini bars of sugar-free hershey's chocolate (or 1 tbsp of sugar free chocolate chips, if using bars, break into pieces)
1/4 cup torani/da'vinci sugar-free chocolate flavor syrup

Directions:
Mix ingredients in mug throughly
Top (swirl-like motion) with syrup-Be generous!.
Microwave on high for 5 minutes.
Cool.
Enjoy a fudgy pudding like dessert! Yummy!

Submitter: 
WickedCreations
Description: 
A flourless, chocolately cake, mixed with bits of chocolate surprises and generous helping of chocolate syrup really makes this a warm fudgie cake just enough to share for one in FIVE MINUTES!!  Diabetic friendly and oh, did I tell you-it rocks!! YUM!! Cures all chocolate craves that is GUILT-FREE!!
