Title:
Bacon and Potato Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. thick-sliced bacon, cut into 1 inch pieces
1 large onion, diced
1 lb. button mushrooms (optional)
2 cups chicken broth
1/4 c. flour
2 cups whole milk
2 cans cream-style corn
6 potatoes, peeled, diced, and cooked until soft, then drained
2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Brown bacon in dutch oven.
Remove bacon and allow to drain on paper towel.  Remove all but 3 Tbsp. of bacon drippings.
Add onion to drippings and saute on low heat until transparent.
Add mushrooms (optional) and saute 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
Add flour, whisking constantly and cooking until lightly brown.
Slowly add chicken broth, whisking constantly to maintain smooth texture.
Add cream-style corn, stirring to blend.
Add milk and whisk.  Bring to a simmer, stirring frequently.
Add cooked potatoes.  Simmer on a very low heat for 5 minutes to heat potatoes through.
Serve with a hearty loaf of whole-grain bread.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
This comforting chowder is rich, yet hearty.  Bacon infuses the creamy soup base, which is chock full of potato chunks, sweet corn, mushrooms and onions.  A meal-in-one for the family that could also masquerade as man food!
