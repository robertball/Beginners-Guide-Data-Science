Title:
mediterranean chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs diced boneless skinless chicken
2 tbls extra virgin olive oil
1 bag frozen chopped spinach, thawed & drained
1/4 cup + 2 tbls of feta cheese
1/2 cup diced tomato
1 tbls minced garlic
1/8 cup heavy cream
Salt and Pepper to taste
Angel Hair pasta

Directions:
Season chicken to taste and brown in olive oil until almost, but not completely done. Add veggies including garlic and saute about 5 minutes. Add feta and cream and toss well. Serve over hot pasta.
