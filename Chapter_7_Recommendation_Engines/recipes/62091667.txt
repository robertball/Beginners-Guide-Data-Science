Title:
Granny Chava's Baked Semolina Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
750 cream cheese
4 eggs
4 tbsp semolina
100g butter
1 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla essence
Finely grated peel of 1 lemon
3/4 cup golden raisins

Directions:
Separate eggs and whip whites until stiff.
Mix the egg yolks with the remaining ingredients until well blended.
Fold in egg whites.
Bake at 200 degrees celcius for 15 minutes and then lower to 160 degrees celcius for an additional 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Shelley
Description: 
My grandmother got this recipe as a wedding present from a friend and it became my mother's annual birthday cake. When I got married, my grandmother finally let me in on the secret recipe!
