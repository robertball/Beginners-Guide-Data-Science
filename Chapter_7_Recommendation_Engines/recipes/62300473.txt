Title:
Maple-Butter Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/364395.jpg
Ingredients:
10T UNsalted butter, at room temp.+ more for the pan
1C Walnut pieces
2C All-purpose flour
1t Baking powder
1/2t Baking soda
1t Salt
11/2C Packed light brown sugar
3 Large eggs
2T Vanilla extract
6oz White chocolate, cut into chunks
FOR THE TOPPING:
1/3C Maple syrup
6T UNsalted butter
1/4C Heavy cream
**Vanilla ice-cream for serving & 1/2C walnut pieces for garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Line a 9X13 baking pan with foil;leaving an overhang on two sides; butter the foil or spray with cooking spray. Pulse 1/2C walnuts in processor until almost fine. Do not over process. Whisk ground nuts, flour, baking soda, and salt in med bowl and set aside.
Beat 10T butter and brown sugar with a mixer on Med. speed until fluffy, roughly 2 minutes. Beat in the eggs one at a time, scraping down bowl with a rubber spatula when needed. Beat in vanilla. Gradually, beat in dry ingredient mixture until JUST combined. Fold in white chocolate and remaining 1/2c of walnuts.
Spread the batter into the prepared pan and bake until the Blondies are light brown around the edges and spring back when pressed, about 30 minutes. Cool slightly.
TOPPING: Place maple syrup, and butter in a small saucepan over med. and cook until it bubbles and thickens, swirling the pan, about 6 minutes. Add heavy cream and continue to cook until mixture is the consistancy of caramel.
Use foil to lift blondies out of pan, cut into bars. Serve warm topped with ice-cream, and maple-butter syrup and the walnuts.

Description: 
A combination of walnuts and white chocolate that will have you craving for more. They also taste like the ones that are served at a national restaurant chain.
