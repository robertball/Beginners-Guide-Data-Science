Title:
Herb Crusted Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/575147.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter, melted
1 tablespoon flour
2 tablespoon prepared dijon mustard
1 teaspoon cracked peppercorns
1 teaspoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground rosemary
1 lb pork tenderloin

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In small bowl, mix together first 7 ingredients. Lightly rinse tenderloin and pat dry. Using a small metal spatula, spread paste over top and sides of pork. Place in roasting pan and roast approximately 30 minutes, or until internal temperature reaches 155 degrees Fahrenheit. Remove from oven and let rest 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Alberta Rose
Description: 
This unique rub adds a savory, slightly sweet crusty coating to seal in the juices of a tender pork loin.
