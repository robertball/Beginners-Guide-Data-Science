Title:
Monkey Shine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/636297.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
2 fluid ounces vodka
4 fluid ounces vanilla-flavored cola

Directions:
Fill a tumbler with ice, pour in vodka and cola; stir and serve.
