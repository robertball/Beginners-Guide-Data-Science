Title:
Kale Caesar with Cucumbers and Leeks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/885477.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Tablespoons of Mayonnaise
6 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
1/4 Cup Dijon Mustard
1/4 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
Juice of Half a Lemon
2 Teaspoons of Worcestershire
4 Teaspoons of Minced Garlic
1 Teaspoon of Anchovy Paste
Half-Whole bag of Kale
1 Cucumber, sliced
1/4 Cup of Leeks, chopped
Fresh Cracked Black Pepper

Directions:
Soak and rinse Kale and spin in a lettuce spinner. Pick out all the ribs and stems.
Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Toss Kale in the skillet for 1-2 minutes or until the bunch has reduced in size by 1/4. Remove and store in the refrigerator for one hour.
Mix mayonnaise, olive oil, mustard, parmesan cheese, lemon juice, Worcestershire, garlic and anchovy paste in a mixing cup. Beat together fully and store in refrigerator for one hour.
Plating:
Mix kale, cucumbers, leeks and dressing together in a large salad bowl and serve.
