Title:
My perfect yellow cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of sugar
2 1/2 cups of flour
1 1/2 tbls of baking powder
1 1/2 tbls of baking soda
1/2 tsp of salt
2 large eggs
1 cup of milk
1/2 cup of vegetable oil
2 tsp of vanilla
1 cup of boiling hot water

Directions:
Preheat oven 350Â° degrees
Butter and flour preferred pan
Add all dry ingredients
Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla
Mix well
Add cup of boiling water
The batter will be soupy
Pour batter into buttered flour pan
Place in middle off oven rack
Time cook 35-40 minutes
Time cook also varies on preferred pan use
Poke in the middle of cake with a toothpick to ensure its done.
Once done take from oven and place on cooling rack.

Deirdre Harwood
