Title:
Hummus Layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz. pkg cream cheese
1tbsp. Italian Seasoning or oregano
1tbsp, lemon juice
1 10oz. container of roasted garlic hummus
1/2 cup cucumber thinly chopped (unpeeled)
1 cup chopped tomatoes
1/2 cup pepperocini rings (found by banana peppers in a jar)
4oz. crumbled feta cheese
1/4 cup of chopped green onion
1/2 cup of halved, pitted kalamata olives (optional)

Directions:
In a bowl combine the cream cheese with lemon juice and italian seasoning. Spread mixture in the bottom of a glass pie dish. Spread hummus on top of cream cheese layer. Sprinkle top with next 6 ingredients.  If not serving immediately refrigerate. Serve with pita chips.

Submitter: 
Burken
Description: 
A nice switch from the mexican layer dip. Super easy and very versatile!
