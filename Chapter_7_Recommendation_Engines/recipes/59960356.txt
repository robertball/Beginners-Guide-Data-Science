Title:
Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 pounds crabmeat
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 tablespoon lemon zest
1/8 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning TM
2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
1/4 c onion finely chopped, 1/4 c finely chopped celery, garlic - sauteed
1 cup saltine crackers, crushed (or Ritz or Panko - japanese breadcrumbs)
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 c Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese grated
4 tablespoons canola oil
Sauce:
2/3 c light sour cream
2 T stone-ground mustard
1/4 tsp pepper
1/8 t Old Bay seasoning

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine 1 egg, lemon zest, Old Bay Seasoning, chopped basil, sauteed veggies, crushed crackers and mayonnaise. Mix thoroughly.  Fold in crabmeat.
Form 5 ounce patties out of the crab mixture (should make about 16 patties), and chill until cold before cooking.
In a skillet, heat 4 tablespoons of oil over medium heat. Saute the crab cakes for 4 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
For the sauce, combine ingred with whisk.
