Title:
Low Carb Hot Dog and Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp mayonnaise
1 Tbsp yellow mustard

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise and mustard together thoroughly in small bowl.  Perfect for dipping hot dogs, chicken nuggets or tenders, or putting on sandwiches.

Submitter: 
SUNSHINE6420
Description: 
I love this mixture, it's deceptively simple but has great flavor.  When my Mom and I did low carb we put this on almost everything we ate.  The yellow mustard can be substituted with spicy if you wish.
