Title:
Blueberry Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2614414.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 tsp Butter
1 Eggs
1 tsp Vanilla Extract (No Corn Syrup, Gluten Free)
1/4 cup Olive Oil
3/4 cup Brown Rice Flour
3/4 cup Oat Flour
3/4 tsp Kal Pure Stevia Extract (equivalent to 1 1/2 cups Sugar)
2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Salt
3/4 cup Water
1/2 cup Frozen Blueberries

Directions:
Add Butter to large glass mixing bowl and melt in microwave
Mix in Eggs, Vanilla Extract and Olive Oil
In a separate glass mixing bowl, Mix Brown Rice Flour, Stevia, Baking Powder, and Salt
Once dry ingredients are evenly mixed, mix into liquid
Add Water slowly until pancake batter consistency, you may need more or less than listed
Mix in Blueberries
Fry in olive oil
