Title:
Will's Veggie Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Quantities of seasonings should be adjusted for the amount of vegetables:
3 parts ground cumin
2 parts mild chili powder
1 part salt
1 part white pepper
Julienned vegetables

Directions:
Saute seasonings in olive oil on a medium high heat until cumin browns.
Add veggies and saute until done.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This a savory way to stir fry vegetables.  Serve over rice or as a side dish.
