Title:
Cheeseburger Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/3 cup Ketchup
12 ounces cubes process cheese spread
2 Â¾ cups water
1 cup uncooked elbow macaroni
Salt/Pepper

Directions:
In large skillet, brown beef; drain. Stir in water, ketchup and onion powder. Bring to boil; stir in macaroni. Reduce heat; cover. Simmer 8-10 minutes or until macaroni is cooked. Add cheese; stir until melted. Salt & Pepper to taste.
