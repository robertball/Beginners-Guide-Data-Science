Title:
Mini Fillo Cheesecake Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/549533.jpg
Ingredients:
30 (Two boxes of 15) Athens Mini Fillo Shells
2- 8 ounce packages cream cheese, softened
4 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
1/2 cup thawed frozen whipped topping
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
Sugar to taste - ~ 1-2 tablespoons
1/2 tablespoon Amaretto
1/2 cup Chocolate for drizzling OR 1/2 cup blueberries or fruit of choice.

Directions:
Bake Athens Mini Fillo Shells in a pre-heated 350 degree oven for ~ 4 minutes.
Once baked - completely cool.
Stir cream cheese and sugar in a small bowl with a whisk until smooth.
Fold in whipped topping, vanilla extract and Amaretto.
To cooled Fillo Shells:
Add a layer of chocolate or fruit to the botton of each Fillo Shell then cover with Cream Cheese Mixutre.
Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Top each Fillo Shell with whatever flavor you have on the bottom of the shell. OR if using fruit use a nice variety.
