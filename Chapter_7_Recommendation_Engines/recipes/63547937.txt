Title:
Veggie Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups  vegetable broth
2 cups  Green Beans
4 cups  Spinach (raw)
2 cup   Chopped Carrots
1/4 cup chopped Onions
1 tbsp  salt
1 tbsp  Pepper
2 cups  chopped beets
2 cups  beet greens
1/8 cup minced garlic
1 cup   mushrooms
1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
1 cup  chopped potatoes

Directions:
1. Put the broth in a large pot on the stove, and turn it on low.
2. While the broth is getting warm chop the necessary items.
3. Measure all vegetables except the spinach and put them in the broth.
4. Wait until the soup is hot and the vegetables are soft before adding spinach.
5. Finally add salt and pepper for flavor.

Carissa Kohne

A delicious dish for a cold winter day.
