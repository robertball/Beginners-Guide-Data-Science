Title:
Chocolate-Covered Peanut Crisp Triangles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup firmly packed brown sugar
3 tablespoons light corn syrup
2 tablespoons crunchy peanut butter
2 tablespoons butter
2 cups POST HONEY BUNCHES OF OATS Cereal
Topping:
5 squares BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Baking Chocolate, chopped
1/3 cup crunchy peanut butter
1/4 cup PLANTERS COCKTAIL Peanuts, chopped

Directions:
Line 13x9-inch pan with foil; spray with cooking spray. Set aside. Microwave brown sugar, corn syrup, 2 tablespoons peanut butter and butter in large microwaveable bowl on HIGH 2 minutes or just until peanut butter and butter are melted and mixture is well blended, stirring after each minute. Add cereal; mix well. Press firmly into prepared pan.
Microwave chocolate and 1/3 cup peanut butter in microwaveable bowl on HIGH 1 to 2 minutes or until chocolate is completely melted when stirred, stirring after each minute. Spread over cereal layer in pan; sprinkle with peanuts.
Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm. Cut into 16 bars; cut each bar diagonally in half to make two triangles. Store in airtight container in refrigerator up to 4 days.

Submitter: 
BAKER'S Chocolate
Description: 
These crisp chocolatey peanut treats make a great addition to a holiday cookie tray or wrapped as a gift.
