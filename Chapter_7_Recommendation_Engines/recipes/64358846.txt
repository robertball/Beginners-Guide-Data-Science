Title:
Texas Buttermilk Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2139434.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C sugar
1/4 C flour
1 C buttermilk
3 eggs, beaten
1/2 C butter, melted
1 t vanilla extract
1, 9-inch, deep dish pie crust, unbaked

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
In a small bowl, mix together the sugar and flour.  Set aside.
In a large bowl, mix together by hand the buttermilk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract until well combined.
Add the sugar mixture to the buttermilk mixture.  Stir to combine.
Pour filling into the pie crust.
Bake 60-70 minutes, or until the middle of the pie is barely set.
Cool on wire rack.  Serve when warm or cold.  Refrigerate to store.

Submitter: 
Nancy Anne Morris Martin
Description: 
I had leftover buttermilk and went searching to see if buttermilk pie is a thing.  Is it ever!  After scouring over many recipes I decided to make this.  So glad I did!  Easy and using ingredients readily available in my kitchen, it's now one of my favorite pies.
