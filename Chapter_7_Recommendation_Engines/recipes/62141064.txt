Title:
Quick Lobster Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 package of refrigerated imitation lobster meat
1/2 capful of liquid Old Bay seasoning
1 medium sized can condensed tomato soup
2 tbs cream cheese
1 heaping tbs cocktail sauce
2/3 cup fat free half & half
1/4 tsp black pepper
Water

Directions:
Melt butter in saucepan.
Add in lobster meat and Old Bay and saute until hot.
Stir in remaining ingredients.
Batch soup in blender or food processor until smooth and creamy.
Place soup back into saucepan and heat through, adding water to desired thickness.
Serve with buttered biscuits or crackers.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
Imitation lobster meat and canned tomato soup are combined with Old Bay seasoning and cream making this recipe the easiest and creamiest lobster bisque ever.
