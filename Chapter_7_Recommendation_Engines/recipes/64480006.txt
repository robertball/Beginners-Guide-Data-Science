Title:
Lavender Tom Collins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 fresh lavender flowers
3 cups boiling water
2 ounces gin
6 ounces Collins mix
1 cup ice cubes
1 lavender ice cube
2 maraschino cherries

Directions:
Place the lavender flowers in an empty ice cube tray.
Bring the water to a boil. Then, pour the boiling water into the ice cube tray, over the lavender.
Let cool before placing in the fridge to freeze.
Pour the gin and Collins mix over the regular ice cubes, and give it a stir.
Top with a lavender ice cube.
Garnish with maraschino cherries

Submitter: 
Heather
Description: 
Lavender brings some yummy sophistication to the classic Tom Collins. You can make your own Collins mix with soda and lavender simple syrup, or try this quick work-around. The flavor will slowly melt, giving the perfect hint of lavender!
