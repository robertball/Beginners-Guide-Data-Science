Title:
S'more Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Brownie mix (or choc cake mix)
1 pkg milk chocolate chips
1 pkg miniature marshmallows
Graham crackers (chocolate flavor or regular flavor), separate into sections of two crackers.

Directions:
Bake the brownies or chocolate cake per package instructions.  Turn off oven.  Spread chocolate chips and mini marshmallows over top. Leave pan in oven an additional 5 - 10 minutes until chips are melted and the marshmallows are softened.
Remove pan from oven, cover top of marshmallow mixture evenly with graham cracker sections, pressing firmly.  Let sit to cool. Cut into bars along edge of crackers.
Store covered at room temp.

Submitter: 
anmlnrs
Description: 
Given to me by my mother-in-law, this recipe will spruce up ordinary cake or brownies.  I've made this many times and always find my husband up at 3am eating the rest with a large glass of milk!
