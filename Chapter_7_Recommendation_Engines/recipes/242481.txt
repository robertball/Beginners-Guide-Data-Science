Title:
Chicken Taco Bowls with Pinto Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F2315892.jpg
Ingredients:
6 (8 inch) flour tortillas
1 (18 ounce) package cooked chicken breast strips
1 (8.8 ounce) pouch UNCLE BEN'S(<sup>&reg;</sup>) <sup>&reg;</sup>EADY <sup>&reg;</sup>ICE(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Pinto Beans & <sup>&reg;</sup>ice
2 cups prepared pico de gallo
½ cup shredded Mexican blend cheese
1  avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced
2  jalapeno peppers, thinly sliced
½ cup avocado ranch dressing, or to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Place each tortilla in a non-stick tortilla bowl maker; bake for 12 minutes. Allow to cool and remove from bowl maker.
Prepare chicken strips according to package directions.
Prepare rice according to package directions.
Divide chicken strips, beans and rice, and pico de gallo among the tortilla bowls. Garnish with shredded cheese, avocado slices, and jalapeno slices. Top with avocado ranch dressing.

Submitter: 
Uncle Ben's
Description: 
Get ready for Cinco de Mayo or any other time with these taco shell bowls filled with chicken strips, pico de gallo, and UNCLE BEN'S(<sup>&reg;</sup>) <sup>&reg;</sup>EADY <sup>&reg;</sup>ICE(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Mexican Style Pinto Beans & <sup>&reg;</sup>ice for an easy and fun night dinner.
