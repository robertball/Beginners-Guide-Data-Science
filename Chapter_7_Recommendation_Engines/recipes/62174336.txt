Title:
Banana Sour Cream Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3/4 cup butter
2 1/2 cups white sugar
3 eggs
6 very ripe bananas, mashed
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 teaspoons baking soda
4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Grease four 7x3 inch loaf pans. In a small bowl, stir together 1/4 cup white sugar and 1 teaspoon cinnamon. Dust pans lightly with cinnamon and sugar mixture.
In a large bowl, cream butter and 3 cups sugar. Mix in eggs, mashed bananas, sour cream, vanilla and cinnamon. Mix in salt, baking soda and flour. Stir in nuts.  Divide into prepared pans.
Bake for 1 hour, until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Kiersten Butler Stahl
Description: 
Sour cream gives enough tangy bite to take the edge off this banana bread 's sweetness.  Dusting the baking pans with cinnamon sugar gives these small four loaves a gentle spice undertone.Modified from: Banana Sour Cream Bread
