Title:
Beef Stew a la Francaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 pound beef stew meat
3 cups beef broth
1 cup green tea
1 1/2 cups dry vermouth
2 cups new potatoes
2 large carrots, quartered
4 large shallots
3 teaspoons chopped fresh tarragon
1 tsp. parsley
1 bay leaf
1/2 cup Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Melt butter In a large stockpot over medium-high heat. Dredge the beef in flour and fry for 2 to 3 minutes, or until evenly browned on the outside.
Pour the chicken broth, tea and vermouth into the stock pot. Add the potatoes, carrots, and shallots. Season with tarragon, bay leaf, Worcestershire sauce,  honey, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and cover. Cook 2 1/2 hours to blend the flavors.
Remove cover and continue cooking 20 minutes to thicken. If desired, finish with 1 Tlb. butter. Voila!

Sandra Holding

This rich, hearty beef and vegetable stew features the engaging flavor of fresh tarragon, and is the perfect way to warm up a cool, rainy evening.Modified from: Tarragon Beef Stew with Vermouth
