Title:
Creamy Comfort Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/962194.jpg
Ingredients:
6-8oz egg noodles or penne noodles, cooked as directed
2-3 cooked chicken breasts, cubed
1 cup sour cream
1 can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Mushroom soup
1 can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Chicken soup
4oz canned mushrooms, drained
1/2 white onion, chopped
1/2-3/4 cups milk
salt
pepper
bread crumbs
1-2 cups Sargento Chef Blends Shredded 6 Cheese Italian

Directions:
Mix together sour cream, cream of mushroom soup, cream of chicken soup, canned mushrooms, onion, milk, 3/4 cup Sargento Chef Blends Shredded Cheese Italian, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
Add chicken and noodles; mix; put in 9x13 pan.
Sprinkle the remainder of the cheese on top, then top with bread crumbs.
Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.
