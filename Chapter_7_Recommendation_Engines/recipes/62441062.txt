Title:
Pumpkin Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 Tbsp. baking powder
¾ tsp. pumpkin pie spice
¼ tsp. salt
3 egg yolks
1 ¾ cups milk
½ cup oil
½ cup canned or mashed pumpkin

Directions:
Stir in dry ingredients. Beat 3 egg whites stiff and fold into batter.
