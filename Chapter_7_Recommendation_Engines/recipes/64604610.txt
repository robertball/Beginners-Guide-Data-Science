Title:
Tuscan Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
1 yellow onion, chopped
12 oz. chicken sausage links, any flavor, sliced
4 cloves garlic, minced
28 oz. can crushed tomatoes
4 c. low-sodium chicken broth
1-1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
kosher salt
Freshly ground black pepper
20 oz.. refrigerated cheese tortellini
15 oz. can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
5 oz. baby spinach
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, heat oil. Add onion and cook until tender and lightly golden, 6 minutes. Add chicken sausage and cook until sausage is golden, 4 minutes more. Stir in garlic, crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, and red pepper flakes and season with salt and pepper.
Bring to a rolling simmer and add tortellini. Simmer 20 minutes.
Stir in white beans and spinach and cook until wilted, 1 minute.
Garnish with Parmesan and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a great main dish soup. Serve with garlic bread. I adapted it from a recipe found at Delish.com.  This will be a wonderful simple supper solution at our house!
