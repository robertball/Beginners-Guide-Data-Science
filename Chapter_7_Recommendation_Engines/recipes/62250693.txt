Title:
Pork Tenderloin with  Apple Ginger compote Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2.5 - 3 pound) pork tenderloin
3 whole apples cut into bite sized pieces
1 cup white wine
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup dried cherries or cranberries
1/4 cup of butter
2 tbls of ground ginger
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
1. Place pork tenderloin in sloww cooker and cook on high for 2 hours.
2. Mix compote ingredients in a bowl and add to the slow cooker.
3. Cut apples into bite sized pieces, add to slow cooker.
4. Cover apples in compote and cook until apples are tender, approximately 1.5 - 2 hours.mixture.

Submitter: 
Jeff Wiles
Description: 
A delicious pork tenderloin meal prepared in a slow cooker.  The apple compote adds a unique and delicious flavor.Modified from: Apple Glazed Pork Tenderloin
