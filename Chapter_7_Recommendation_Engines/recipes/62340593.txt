Title:
Caribbean Curried Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked rice
2 cups chopped cooked chicken
6 green onions, chopped (whites and greens)
2 medium mango, peeled and diced
1 small red pepper, diced
6-8 Tbsp plain yogurt
6-8 Tbsp mayo
1 1/2 - 2 tsp curry powder (or to taste)
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Cook rice, chill.
In large bowl, combine chicken, onions, mango and red pepper.
In small bowl, combine yogurt, mayo, curry powder and salt.  Combine and blend well.
Combine salad with chilled rice and dressing.  Top with dry roasted peanuts.
(Depending upon your taste for a salad that is more wet or dry, you might want to increase/decrease amount of the dressing a little bit.)
