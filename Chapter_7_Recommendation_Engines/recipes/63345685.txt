Title:
Holler Peno Watermelon Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/888947.jpg
Ingredients:
1 med cucumber peeled and diced
4 cups watermelon diced same size as cucumbers
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
1/2 large red onion finly slivered or diced
1 large fresh lime
2-3 diced with seeds fresh jalapenos
Kosher Salt to taste
Fresh ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop and drop all.
Squeeze lime over bowl.
Salt Pepper to taste.
Cover refrigerate 2 hrs.
