Title:
Amish Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. butter
1 chicken bouillon cube
32 oz. chicken broth
12 oz egg noodles

Directions:
In a large pan, brown the butter.
Add broth and bouillon cube, bring to a boil.
Add noodles, bring to a second boil, cover and turn off heat (leave pot on the same burner).
Let stand 30 minutes, stirring 2 or 3 times.

Submitter: 
Amy Hague Miller
Description: 
I used to cook these with the turkey drippings but these were just as good!
