Title:
Chix Wings in Honey, Soy Sauce & SeasameOil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chix Wings - Segmented and skinned
Equal Parts: Honey, Soy Sauce & Dark Toasted Seasame Oil
1/2 Part:  Dry Sherry
To Taste: Garlic, Hot Sauce, Fresh Grated Ginger

Directions:
Prep Wings and soak in Heavily SALTED H2O for 2 hr.
Rinse and soak in Clear H2O for 2 hr.
Drain H2O.
Cover with Marinade and refrigerate overnight.
Drain before cooking.  Reserve Marinade.
Bake in 9" x 13" dish @ 350F for 45 min.
-------------
Heat Reserved Marinade to boiling. Reduce to "Soft Ball" stage.
Serve over cooked wings.

Marinade and bake for a delicious Appitizer or Evening Snack
