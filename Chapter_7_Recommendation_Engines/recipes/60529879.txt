Title:
Granny's Angel Spiced Chicken Wangz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons olive oil
6 vinegar or lemon juice
2 Tbsp granulated garlic
4 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 c worcestershire sauce
1/4 c soy sauce
salt and ground black pepper to taste
20 chicken wings

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Combine the olive oil, garlic, chili powder, garlic powder,soy sauce, worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper in a large, resealable bag; seal and shake to combine. Add the chicken wings; reseal and shake to coat. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to an hour. Arrange the chicken wings on a baking sheet.
Cook the wings in the preheated oven 1 hour, or until crisp and cooked through.
