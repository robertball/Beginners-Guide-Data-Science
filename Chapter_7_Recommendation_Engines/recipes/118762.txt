Title:
Green Chili Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/180356.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked rice
2 (4 ounce) cans chopped green chilies
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 pound Monterey Jack cheese, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
2 cups sour cream

Directions:
In a bowl, combine all ingredients. Transfer to a greased 2-qt. baking dish. Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Naomi  Newkirk
Description: 
'This cheesy bake is wonderful for church potlucks because it goes well with any main course,' reports Naomi Newkirk from Sacramento, California. 'I often sprinkle a little paprika over the top for color.'
