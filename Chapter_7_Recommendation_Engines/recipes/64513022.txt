Title:
Lemon Red Snapper Almadine with Herbed Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4476428.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lemon
Cooking spray
4 (6 ounce) fillets of red snapper or other firm white fish.
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
4 tablespoons melted butter
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh herbs ( such as parsley, rosemary, thyme, or basil}
2 oz slivered almonds toasted

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 425
2.  Cut lemon into 8 slices.  Place slices, in pairs.on a rimmed baking sheet coated with cooking spray.
3.  Place 1 fillet on each pair of lemon slices.  Combine salt, paprika, and pepper, sprinkle evenly over fish.
4.  Bake at 425 for ~25 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork or until desired degree of doneness.
5.  While fish bakes, melt the butter and combine with lemon juice and herbs in a small bowl.
6.  Place fish on individual serving plates; top with herbed butter, and toasted almonds.

Submitter: 
N8TE
Description: 
A fragrant herbed butter and roasted lemon slices complement the sweet, nutty flavor of red snapper for a super-fresh dish.  Toasted slivered almonds add to the flavor.  Adapted from Cooking Light.
