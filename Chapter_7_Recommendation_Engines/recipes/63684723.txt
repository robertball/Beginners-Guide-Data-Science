Title:
Sloppy Joes ala Great Gramdma Hasz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 small chopped onion
3.5 oz. catchup
1/2 bottle chili sauce
1/4 chopped green pepper
6 oz. sweet relish

Directions:
Brown ground beef and onion.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer 30 min.
Serve on buns.

Submitter: 
Grammy M B H
Description:
