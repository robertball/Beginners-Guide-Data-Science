Title:
Citrus Crush Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4520587.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 orange, peeled and chopped
1/2 lemon, peeled, seeded and chopped
1/2 cup pineapple chunks
3/4 cup Almond Breeze Unsweetened Original Almondmilk

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Orange, lemon, pineapple, and almondmilk combine for a refreshing snack or on-the-go breakfast.
