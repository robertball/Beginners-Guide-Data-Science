Title:
Sticky Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/889867.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Lbs Potatoes Peeled
4 Garlic Cloves Skinned
Extra Sharp White Cheddar Grated About 1 Lb
1/4 Cup Heavy Cream
1 Cup Bacon Bits
Salt & Fresh Black Pepper

Directions:
Steam the potatoes and the garlic until soft and put it to the food processor. Add the cheese and the heavy cream. Add salt and pepper to flavor.
Puree the mix and put into a backing dish. Sprinkle the top with the bacon pieces and put into the broiler a minute or two until the top browns. Remove and serve.

Submitter: 
Buon Appetito
Description: 
Mmmmmmm It Is almost like creamy grits and the bacon work's as a contrast of texture. For those who do not eat meat just leave the bacon out.
