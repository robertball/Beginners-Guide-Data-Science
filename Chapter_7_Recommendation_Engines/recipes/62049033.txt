Title:
Cranberry-Walnut Cookies Recipe

Recipe ingredients included in narrative...

Directions:
In a bowl, soak 3 cups raisins in 3/4 cup bourbon for 1 hour.  In a large bowl cream together 1 stick butter (softened) & 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar until mixture is light and fluffy; then beat in 2 eggs.  Sift together 1 1/2 cups flour, 1 1/2 tsp. baking soda, 1 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon, 1/2 tsp. EACH ground nutmeg and ground cloves.  Stir flour mixture into sugar mixture with 1 cup chopped dried cranberries, 1 cup chopped walnuts & the raisins with their liquid.
Drop by spoonfuls onto a cookie sheet & bake for 15 minutes in a 325 degree oven.  Cool on wire racks...
