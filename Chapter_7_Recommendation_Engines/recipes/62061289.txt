Title:
Light Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 tablespoons chili sauce
1 cup quick cook oats
1 small onion, chopped
1 tablespoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon coarse black pepper
1 pound ground sirloin
1 pound ground turkey
2 large eggs
2 large egg whites
1 teaspoon jar minced garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Coat glass or stoneware 9 x 13 dish with cooking spray.
Place 3 tablespoons chili sauce and the next 9 ingredients in large bowl and mix well ( with your hand works best).
Shape mixture into a 9 x 5 inch loaf on prepared dish.  Bake for 50 minutes.  Spoon 4 tablespoons chili sauce over loaf and bake 20 minutes more or until meat thermometer registers 160 degrees.  Remove from oven and let rest 10 minutes before slicing.
