Title:
Banana Anna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/620692.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
1 1/2 cups milk
1 tablespoon honey
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions:
In a blender combine banana, milk, honey and nutmeg. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
ACHNACH
Description: 
A sweet, healthy, easy way to make a banana smoothie. You can substitute soy milk if you like.
