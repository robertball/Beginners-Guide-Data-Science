Title:
Awesome Pork Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup soy sauce (Or Low Sodium Soy Sauce)
3 Tbs sesame oil
1 Tbs Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 Tbs garlic powder
1 Tbs minced garlic
2 Tbs "Dales" steak seasoning
1 Tbs meat tenderizer
1/4 cup asian steak sauce (If available)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a square casserole dish.  Add pork and coat thoroughly.  Marinate for 10 - 20 minutes as desired.  Grill or broil until cooked through.  Brush marinade on each side after turning.  (If recipe is too salty for your taste, add 1/3 cup of water.

Submitter: 
EarlyRetirement
Description: 
An awesome oriental twist on pork chops and pork steak.  I cooked this at my house and even my ultra picky husband loved it.
