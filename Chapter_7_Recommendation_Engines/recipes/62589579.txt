Title:
Broccoli Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5c water
1- 6oz pkg long grain and wild rice mix (Uncle Ben's)
1 can cream of chicken soup
1-1/2 c milk
8 oz cream cheese, cubed
1/4 t salt
10 oz. frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
1 large carrot, shredded
1/4 c toasted sliced almonds

Directions:
Combine water, rice mix, and seasoning packet; boil.
Reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.
Add soup, milk, cream cheese, and salt; stir until melted.
Add broccoli and carrot; cook 5-6 minutes.
Garnish with almonds.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener

Very good, can also be made with low fat ingredients
