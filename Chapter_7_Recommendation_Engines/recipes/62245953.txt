Title:
Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/969696.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 t vanilla
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 t baking soda
1 t cinnamon
1/2 t salt
3 cups oats
1 cup rasins

Directions:
Cream butter & sugars.
Beat in eggs 1 at a time.  Add vanilla.
In a separate bowl, sift flour, cinnamon, baking soda and salt.
Stir into butter mixture
Stir in oats, raisins and chocolate chips if desired.
Bake @ 350 for 10-12 minutes.

Description: 
Quaker Oats recipe - our personal favorite.  Also great with chocolate chips thrown in.
