Roasted Garlic Potatoes Recipe

Ingredients:
11 medium potatoes, thinly sliced
1 (14 ounce) can SwansonÂ® Seasoned Chicken Broth with Roasted Garlic

Directions:
Place potatoes in 3 quart shallow baking dish. Pour broth over potatoes. Cover.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 40 minutes. Uncover and bake 15 minutes or until tender.

Description:
