Title:
Tequila Fruit Dip and Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/752493.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons tequila
2 tablespoons sweetened lime juice
1/4 cup sugar

Directions:
Stir together the sour cream, tequila, lime juice, and sugar in a small serving bowl.

Submitter: 
J.P.
Description: 
Use this creamy and tangy dip for dipping fruit or as a fruit salad dressing. It's excellent for strawberries. Most people don't know there's tequila in it. They only know that it is really yummy!
