Title:
Gail's microwave green beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11/2lb green beans washed
2 cups cherry tomatoes
1/2 stick butter
1T sugar
1tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 T chopped basil
1/4 cup feta cheese

Directions:
Boil beans for 3 minutes-drain and lay in a oblong pyrex dish. Cut tomatoes in half and sprinkle over beans.
Heat together butter,sugar,garlic, salt, pepper and basil. Pour over beans and tomatoes.Add the feta. Put in fridge until ready for cooking then heat in Microwave for 4-5 minutes.Serve right away
