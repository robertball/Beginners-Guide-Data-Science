Title:
Beef Tips Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 lbs. stew meat
1 can French Onion soup
2 cans Golden Mushroom soup

Directions:
Brown stew meat.
Put in crockpot with soups and cook 6-8 hours.
Serve over rice or egg noodles.
