Title:
Low Fat Cake Mix Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 box cake mix (your choice, the chocolate cake mix makes them taste like brownies and the lemon makes them like lemon bars, etc.)
-1 (8oz.) container cool whip
-1 egg
-Powdered Sugar

Directions:
Mix the cake mix, cool whip, and egg together in a large bowl.  The mixture might be rather sticky.
Form into balls and roll in powdered sugar.
Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes and allow to cool on the pan before removing.

Submitter: 
Eric
Description: 
All of your family and friends will go crazy over these!  I made them for my weight loss group last year and everyone always wants the recipe!
