Title:
Peanut Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsps. reduced fat peanut butter
1 tbsp. honey
2 tbsps. soy sauce
1 tsp. chili oil
1 tsp. sesame oil
1 tsp. fresh ginger, grated
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 green onion, chopped

Directions:
Melt the peanut butter in a large microwave-safe glass or ceramic bowl, 15-20 seconds.  Whisk the honey, tamari and chili sauce into the peanut butter, then stir in the sesame oil and ginger. Mix in the garlic and green onions.
Chill before serving. Serve with fresh or steamed veggies.
