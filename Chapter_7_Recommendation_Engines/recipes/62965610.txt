Title:
Pickle Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/732331.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oz. of vodka
a splash of dill pickle juice (or to taste)
1 dill pickle spear

Directions:
- fill a mixing tin half full with ice
- add vodka and pickle juice
- shake until well chlled
- strain and pour into martini glass

Submitter: 
CFlott
Description: 
A different twist on the traditional olive martini.  If you're a fan of pickles then give it a try!
