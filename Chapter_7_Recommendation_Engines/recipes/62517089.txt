Title:
Candied Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510367.jpg
Ingredients:
large Can of Sweet Potatoes
Cinnamon and Nutmeg to taste
3 Sticks of Margerine or Butter
Brown and White Sugar
Mini Marshmellows

Directions:
Combine Margerine or butter and sugars in a saucepan
simmer until thick
Place sweet potatoes in large buttered dish
pour saucepan mixture over sweet potatoes
sprinkle with cinnamon and nutmeg
mix and top with marshmellows
Bake for 30 mins
