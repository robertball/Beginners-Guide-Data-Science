Title:
Green Power Oat Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4473975.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Quaker® Oats (Quick or Old Fashioned, uncooked)
3/4 cup unsweetened coconut milk
1 cup baby spinach leaves, tightly packed
1/4 cup parsley leaves
1/2 cup cored and chopped green apple
1 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 cup ice

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Quaker®
Description: 
Start your day with this green power smoothie combining oats, coconut milk, spinach, parsley, apple, honey and lemon.
