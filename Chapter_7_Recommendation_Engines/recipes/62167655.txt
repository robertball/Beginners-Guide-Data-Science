Title:
Sweet & Tangy Broccoli Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups broccoli tips
1 cup cabbage, shredded
1/8 cup red onion, slivered
2 baby carrots, grated
1/8 cup bell pepper, slivered
2 tablespoon sugar
4 tablespoon milk
2 tablespoon lemon juice
2 teaspoon vinegar
1/4 teaspoon salt
dash pepper
1/4 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
Combine broccoli tips, cabbage, red onion, bell pepper, and carrots in serving bowl and set aside.
Using a measuring cup, whisk together sugar, milk, lemon juice, vinegar, salt & pepper.  Add mayonnaise and stir to combine.  Pour over vegetables and toss well.
Cover and refrigerate at least two (2) hours before serving.
Note:  vegetables may easily be increased or decreased according to personal taste.  Substituting part buttermilk makes for a nice change as well.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Crunchy broccoli tips and cabbage combined with a tangy sweet dressing make this a cool treat.  We like to pair with a spicy barbecue chicken and baked beans.
