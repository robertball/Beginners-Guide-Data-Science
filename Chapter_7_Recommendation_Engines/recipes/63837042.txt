Title:
Delicious 15th Birthday Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066181.jpg
Ingredients:
For apple layer:
6 mcintosh apples
1 tablespoon of cinnamon
5 tablespoons of sugar (can be reduced if neccessary)
For cake batter:
2 3/4 cup of sifted flour
1 tablespoon of baking powder
1 teaspoon of salt
1 cup of vegetable oil (or oil of choice)
1 3/4 cup of sugar
1/4 cup of water
2 1/2 teaspoons of vanilla
4 whole eggs

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C)
Peel, core, and chop the apples and toss with sugar and ground cinnamon
In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and salt until well combined
In a seperate bowl, mix the oil, sugar, vanilla and water until combined
Add the wet ingredients to the dry and until it is too dry to mix further
Add in the eggs one at a time, mixing after each addition
Grease a bundt pan VERY WELL with crisco or margarine.
Pour half of the batter into the pan, then spread half of the apples evenly across this level. Pour the remaining batter over the chunks and top with remaining apples.
Place in the preheated oven and let bake for 1 1/2 hours.
Remove from oven and let pan cool completely before flipping over onto serving dish.
