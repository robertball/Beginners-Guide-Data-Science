Title:
Libby's Famous Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 t salt
1/2 t ginger
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 t cloves, ground
2 eggs
15 oz pumpkin puree
12 oz evaporated milk
1 unbaked 9" deep dish pie crust

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix sugar, salt, cinnamon, ginger & cloves.
Beat eggs in a large bowl, then stir in pumpkin and sugar/spice mixture.
Gradually stir in evaporated milk.
Pour into pie shell.
Bake @ 425 for 15 minutes.  Reduce temp to 350 and bake for another 40 to 50 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center of pie comes out clean.
Cool on rack for 2 hours.
