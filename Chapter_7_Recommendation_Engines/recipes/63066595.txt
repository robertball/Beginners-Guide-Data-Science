Title:
Pinche Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/772634.jpg
Ingredients:
Una pinche ham
pineapple slices
maraschino cherries
brown sugar
toothpicks

Directions:
Preheat de oven to like 325F.  Unwrap de pinche ham, and get it all nice & clean, ju know, like nice & clean, ju know?  Now put some seasonings, like the pineapple slices and cherries with tooth picks and sprinkle with brown sugar, so it looks pretty bruto..  Now get a roaster pan, big enough for the pinche ham to fit in, and place into oven.
Now cook the pinche ham for like 20 - 25 minutes per pound... get it all nice & hot, and brown..
Once internal temperature is like 260F, De pinche ham is done! AYY YAY YAYYYYYYYYY! Plenty of pinche ham for like ju and your cousins.
