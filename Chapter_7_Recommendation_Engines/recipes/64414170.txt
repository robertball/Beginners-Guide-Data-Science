Title:
Chocolate Macaroons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5216555.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c coconut sugar
1/4 c maple syrup
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/8 c coconut oil
1/8 c cocoa
1/4 c unsweetened coconut
1/2 c quick-cooking oats
1/4 c (hemp hearts, crushed nuts, seeds, or more oats)

Directions:
Heat all but cocoa, coconut and oats in a small pot on medium until bubbling.
Turn temperature to low and simmer for 5 minutes, watching that the mixture doesnâ€™t burn.
Remove from heat and stir in cocoa, coconut and quick-cooking oats.
Drop on to parchment paper lined baking sheet.
Refrigerate until cold.
