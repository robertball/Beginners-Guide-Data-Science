Title:
Desperate For Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup melted butter (2 sticks)
1 ½ cups white sugar
¾ cups brown sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 beaten eggs
2 ½ cups flour
1 ½ cups chips (any combination)
2 cups chopped nuts (any combination; includes coconut and dried fruits)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Melt butter (microwave or stove top).
In mixing bowl, combine butter, white sugar and brown sugar.
Add vanilla, baking soda and salt; combine well.
Add eggs, combine completely.
Add half the flour (1 ¼ cups); all chips and nuts. Combine.
Add the rest of the flour and mix thoroughly.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheets, 12 to a standard sheet (If the dough is too sticky, chill slightly.)
Bake for 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned.
Let cool on sheet 2 minutes, remove from cookies sheets to wire rack.

Submitter: 
B
Description: 
These are a pantry clean out cookie. You can use a variety of ingredients and mix it up depending on your mood or preferences. The REAL bonus, you don't have to wait for the butter to soften because you use melted butter!Modified from: Desperation Cookies
