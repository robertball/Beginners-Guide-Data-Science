Title:
Healthy Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 scoop Whey (I use Now Sports Brand) (about 25 grams)
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp dried mustard
1/4 tsp basil
1/4 tsp celery seed
1/2 tsp chives
1/2 tsp parsley
1 tsp rice vinegar
water 2 tbs or to desired thickness

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients then the vinegar and slowly add water until you reach the desired thickness.  Will thicken slightly after sitting for about 30 min.

Submitter: 
Jeramyn Feucht
Description: 
Ranch dressing based on soy protein is a healthy addition to any salad.
