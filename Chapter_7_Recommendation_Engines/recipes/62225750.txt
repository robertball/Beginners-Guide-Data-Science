Title:
Cheesiest Bacon Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bacon, cooked and crumbled
3 8 oz packages cream cheese
1 medium onion, chopped
10 oz frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1/2 cup mayonnaise
5 oz shredded parmesan cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients and spoon into a 2 quart baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.  Serve with crackers, veggies or pita.

Submitter: 
sarahlynn
Description: 
A rich dip that is perfect for a cold night!  Warning: this is not a diet recipe!!
