Title:
Caribbean Dream Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 tablespoons minced prepared garlic
1 small onion, chopped
1 lb. ground sirloin
1 lb. ground pork
1 28 oz can whole tomatoes
2 cups low sodium beef broth
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon cinnamin
1/2 teaspoon allspice
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon salt
1 14 oz can black beans, rinsed
1/2 cup golden raisins
1/4 cup green olives, cut in half
1/4 cup slivered almonds

Directions:
Add oil a large pot over medim heat. Add garlic and onions and saute until soft, about two minutes.  Add beef and pork and cook until browned. Remove excess grease.  Return the pot to the stove and add the beef broth and tomatoes with their juice, crushing each by hand. Add spices, beans, raisins and salt.  Bring mixture to a boil, and reduce heat to a simmer.  Cook covered for 30 minutes.  Add olives and almonds and cook an additional 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
The most unusual chili you will make!  The flavors are sweet and spicy, just like the Islands.  Enjoy with tortilla chips and a cold Caribbean beer.
