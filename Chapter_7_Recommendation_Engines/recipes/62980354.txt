Title:
Rubys Jello salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ruby's Jello Salad Mix together: 1 large can crushed pineapple, drained - 1 bag frozen raspberries, thawed and drained - 2 ripe bananas, mashed - Prepare 1 large box raspberry jello, place in fridge to thicken slightly. Add fruit. Return to fridge to let set. Mix together : 1 8 oz brick cream cheese, softened (beat the heck out of the cr cheese before adding the marshmallow cream) and 1 jar marshmallow cream. Spread cream cheese mixture over set jello. return to fridge
