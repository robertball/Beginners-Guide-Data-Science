Title:
Non-dairy corn chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp olive oil or butter
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 bay leaf
1/4 tsp thyme
1 green pepper, chopped
2 c mushrooms, chopped
4 potatoes, peeled and cut into small cubes
4 c chicken broth (low sodium)
3 c water
1/2 tsp white pepper
1 tbsp Spike seasoning
4 c frozen corn

Directions:
1. Saute the onions in the oil or butter along with the bay leaf and thyme in a medium soup pot, and cook until onions are tender.
2. Add the rest of the ingredients to the soup pot, except the frozen corn. Turn up heat and bring to a boil.
3. Simmer for 15 minutes, then add the frozen corn and cook for another 15 minutes
4. Turn off heat. Process 1/2 of the soup in a blender until smooth and return to the soup pot. Mix together.

Submitter: 
GEKKO
Description: 
This is delicious, easy-to-make, dairy-free corn chowder recipe I adapted from a recipe my mom gave me. The creaminess doesn't come from milk, but from blending part of the soup in a blender. Enjoy!
