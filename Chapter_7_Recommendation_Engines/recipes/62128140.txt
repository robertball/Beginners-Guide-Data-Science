Title:
A Simple Rib Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283944.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup veg. oil
2 tbls. ketchup
2 tbls. soy sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together well.
Brush on ribs during last 10 minutes of grilling.
This is enough sauce for about 5 lbs. of ribs
