Title:
Apple Cheddar Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3594215.jpg
Ingredients:
8 eggs
1 c shredded cheddar cheese, divided
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tbsp butter
2 apples, peeled, cored and sliced

Directions:
Melt butter in oven-safe skillet over medium heat.
Combine eggs, 1/2 of shredded cheese, salt, and pepper.
Pour egg mixture into pan.
When eggs start to set, about 1 minute, arrange sliced apples on top, and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Transfer to oven.
Bake at 450 degrees for 20 minutes.
