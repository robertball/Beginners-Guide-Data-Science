Title:
Pumpkin Custard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup pumpkin puree
2 cups milk
3 eggs, beaten
1 T flour
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Pour 1 cup of milk into a two cup measuring cup.  Add pumpkin puree until the milk reaches a level of 2 cups.  Pour this into mixing bowl.  Now pour 1 cup of milk into the measuring cup and then add it to the bowl.  Add rest of ingredients and mix well with electric mixer.  Pour into deep 9" pie pan.  Bake in preheated 375 degree oven for about 50 minutes or until center tests done.
