Title:
Mom's Company Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Pork Tenderloin patties cut about 1 1/2" thick
Salt & Pepper
Garlic Salt
2 Onions, sliced
2 Tomatoes, sliced
6 slices bacon

Directions:
Season meat on both sides with salt, pepper, and garlic salt.
Place one onion and one tomato slice on each tenderloin patty.
Wrap with bacon; secure with wooden tooth picks.
Place in heavy 2 - 3 quart covered casserole.
Bake at 325 degrees for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.  Remove cover during last 1/2 hour of cooking.

Submitter: 
KONWAL
Description: 
This was my Mom's favorite company dinner when I was growing up.  Just found it again, tried it and my husband and I both loved it!  Tastes as good as I remember it tasting.
