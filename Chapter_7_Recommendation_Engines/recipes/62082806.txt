Title:
easy slow cooker mexican chicken soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 chicken thighs
2 (15 oz) cans black beans, drained and rinsed
1 (15 oz) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
2 (15 oz) cans chicken broth
1 (4 oz) can diced green chiles
2 (15 oz) cans diced tomatoes
1 (1.25 oz) package taco seasoning
Shredded cheddar or jack cheese, (optional)
Sour cream (optional)
Crushed tortilla chips (optional)
Cilantro, chopped (optional)

Directions:
1.  Place the chicken thighs in a pot of water, boil until cooked.  Remove from pot and let cool until able to handle.  Shred chicken.
2.  Place beans, corn, tomato sauce, broth, chiles, tomatoes and taco seasoning in a slow cooker.  Add shredded chicken.
3.  Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours.
4.  Serve topped with cheese, sour cream, tortilla chips and cilantro, if desirec.

Submitter: 
michelle o'c
Description: 
I always keep certain canned goods on hand to whip up standards.  You can  cook chicken thighs ahead of time and add to soup, or you can put boneless cuts of chicken in at the beginning of the cooking time.  Slightly spicy, it's a terrific 'comfort' food!
