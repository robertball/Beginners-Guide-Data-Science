Title:
The Candy Bar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089839.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Kahlua Hazelnut
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Kahlua Mocha
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers Irish cream liqueur

Directions:
Combine the Kahlua Hazelnut and Kahlua Mocha. Pour over ice. Top with Irish cream.
Stir and enjoy!
