Title:
School Hot Lunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2741482.jpg
Ingredients:
2-2.5lbs chicken breast
2 packets of dry chicken gravy (McCormick is great)
1 10oz cream of chicken soup
2 cups water
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine cream of chicken soup, gravy packets, water, and pepper in your slow cooker.
Mix well.
Add chicken.
Cook on low setting 8 hrs.

Submitter: 
Casey O'Connor
Description: 
Reminiscent of hot lunch served at school, this dish brings tender chicken and gravy to comfort you at any time. Serve as a open sandwich or over delicious mashed potatoes. So easy a child could make it!
