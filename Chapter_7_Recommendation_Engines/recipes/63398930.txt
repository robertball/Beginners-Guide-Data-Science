Title:
Custardy Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 c unbleached flour
1 c milk
1/2 t salt
4 T melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375'.
1.  Beat the eggs and milk.  Add flour and salt.  Beat with a fork until mixture is uniform.
2.  Preheat the muffin pan in the oven 5 minutes.  Brush cups with melted butter generously.
3.  Fill each muffin hole 2/3 full with batter.  Work quickly so the tin stays hot.  Place in oven.
4.  Bake 35 minutes without opening the oven.  Prick each popover with a fork to let steam escaped (to help them hold shape)  Serve immediately with butter and jam.
