Title:
Saints and Strangers Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2976569.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces vodka
2 fluid ounces freshly squeezed grapefruit juice
1 1/2 fluid ounces freshly squeezed pomegranate juice
1/2 fluid ounce sweet French vermouth
1/2 ounce cream of balsamic (balsamic glaze)
1/4 fluid ounce cold-pressed cranberry juice
1/8 teaspoon freshly ground pink peppercorns
ice cubes
1 pinch freshly grated orange zest

Directions:
Place vodka, grapefruit juice, pomegranate juice, vermouth, cream of balsamic, cranberry juice, and peppercorns in a cocktail shaker. Fill shaker with ice and shake well; strain into a martini glass. Sprinkle with orange zest.

Submitter: 
cheesemite
Description: 
This cocktail is crisp and elegant, with deep, earthy notes and a ruby color.
