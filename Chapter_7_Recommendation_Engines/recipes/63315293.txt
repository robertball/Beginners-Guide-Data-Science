Title:
Rockin's Red Skinned Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds clean, scrubbed new red skinned yukon potatoes(if you can't get red yukon red skinned whites will do.)
6 eggs
1 pound bacon
1 onion, finely chopped
1 teaspoon celery seed
2 cups mayonaise
1 tablespoon mustard
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and set in the refrigerator to cool.
Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and boil for 10min. Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.
Cut bacon in 1/2 inch strips cutting  several slices at a time.
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain and set aside.
Chop the cooled potatoes, leaving skin on.  Add to a large bowl, along with the eggs, bacon, onion and celery seed.
Blend mayonaise and mustard and gently blend into the potato mixture.
Salt and pepper to taste. Top with cheddar cheese.
Chill for an hour or over night before serving.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
In my family, pretty much everthing is better with bacon in it. Top it with cheddar cheese and it's a huge hit! The flavors in this salad blend to make a very satisfying side dish to anything from a friends (Ginny's) going away party to a warm summer family barbeque!!!Modified from: Red Skinned Potato Salad
