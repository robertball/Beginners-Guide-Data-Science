Title:
Apple and Raisin Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/717166.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter
2 Royal Gala, Jonagold or Granny Smith apple - peeled, cored and sliced
2 cup white wine
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 teaspoon ground cinnamon OR Grand Marnier
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2/3 cup golden raisins
1 - 2 pinches of salt

Directions:
Melt butter in a skillet over low heat. Add apples, and increase the heat to medium-high. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in the white wine, brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla, and golden raisins. Cook over medium heat until the liquid has reduced and is thick.

Submitter: 
Milly Suazo-Martinez
Description: 
Use it as a filling, for topping waffles, funnel cakes or ice cream or just eat it alone!Modified from: Apple and Raisin Sauce
