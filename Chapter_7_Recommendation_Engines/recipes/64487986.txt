Title:
Grilled Herb Rotisserie Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2563120.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3-4 pound) whole chicken
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 of onion, cut in quarters
1 garlic clove, smashed
Dry Herb Rub for Chicken:
2 tablespoons Salt
1 Tablespoon Pepper
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1/2 tablespoon onion powder
1 teaspoon thyme
1 teaspoon basil
1 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon poultry season
1/8 teaspoon cumin

Directions:
Make dry rub .
Tie legs and wings on chicken.
Place onion and garlic inside chicken cavity.
Rub chicken with olive oil then coat chicken with  the dry rub all over.  Chill for 6 hours in the refrigerator . To make ahead: chicken can chill for up to one day in the refrigerator.
Remove chicken from refrigerator and let come to room temperature for 30 minutes.
Preheat grill on  medium- low.  (Our grill is a three burner grill, I turned off the middle burner to create indirect heat).
Secure chicken on rotisserie spit. Place disposable aluminum pan under chicken to catch drippings. Grill for 2-3 hours depending on size of chicken  or until an internal temperature of 165 is reached basting chicken every half  hour.
Remove chicken from rotisserie and allow to rest for ten minutes.
