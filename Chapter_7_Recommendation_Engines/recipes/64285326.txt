Title:
Veggie-packed slow cooker black bean soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium carrots, grated
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 red pepper, finely diced
1 cup green puree (or chopped spinach)
2 15 oz cans black beans, drained
15 oz can chopped tomatoes
15 oz can refried beans
1 1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp cumin
1 cup water (or try carrot juice)
Salt and pepper to taste
Optional toppings:
diced avocado
sour cream
shredded cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients apart from your toppings in your slow cooker, and cook for around 8 hours on medium.
If desired, blend before serving and add your chosen toppings.
