Title:
Vegan Chocolate Coconut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2730059.jpg
Ingredients:
WET INGREDIENTS;
2 tbsp ground flax mixed with 5 tbsp water.
1/3 cup Coconut oil
1/4 cup Almond butter/ nut butter
1/2 cup Cane Sugar
1 tsp Vanilla
1/4 cup Almond Milk
DRY INGREDIENTS
1 cup WW Flour
1/2 cup Oats
1/3 cup Shredded Coconut
3 tbsp Cocoa Powder
1/4 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Baking Soda
1/2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 cup Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Note; Add flax and water together, and let sit. These will be added last to wet ingredients
Note; Must heat coconut oil until it becomes completely melted and fairly hot, not to get chunky when cold things are added
-Mix wet ingredients together
-mix dry ingredients together
-Mix all together and add choco chips
-bake at 350 for 11-13 mins
