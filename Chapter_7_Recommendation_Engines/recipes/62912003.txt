Title:
Angel French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/708164.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
splash milk
dash cinnamon
quick grate of nutmeg
2 thin slices of angel food cake (store bought is perfectly fine)
Pat of butter
Maple syrup
Fresh fruit

Directions:
Beat egg with fork until nicely frothy.  Add splash of milk and mix thoroughly.
Add dash of cinnamon (to taste) and quick run of a nutmeg over microplaner.
Get grill or pan warming up and melt a dollop of butter in same.
Briefly soak the angle food cake peices in egg mixture - don't let them sit in there - just quickly toss about until coated (apx 1").
Cook in heated pan just as you would regular french toast - be sure to turn and cook all sides.
Remove from heat, add a pat of butter, some maple syrup, and fresh fruit if desired and enjoy!
