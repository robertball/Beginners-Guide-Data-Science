Title:
Blue Cheese and Pear Tartlets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces gorganzola cheese, crumbled
2 ripe pears - peeled, cored, and chopped
4 oz. cream cheese
ground black pepper to taste
candied walnuts
1 (2.1 ounce) package mini phyllo tart shells

Directions:
Mix together gorganzola cheese, pear, and cream cheese. Season to taste with pepper. Spoon mixture into cooled shells. Place a candied walnut on top.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 15 minutes. Serve warm.
