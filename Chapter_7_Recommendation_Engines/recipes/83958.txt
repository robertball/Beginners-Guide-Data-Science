Title:
Classic Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981756.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) package Lawry's® Taco Spices & Seasonings
1 pound lean ground beef
4 cups shredded lettuce
1 (16 ounce) can red kidney beans, rinsed and drained (or pinto beans)
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
2 medium tomato, cut into wedges
1/2 cup sliced green onions
1 (2.25 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
Tortilla chips or corn chips
Salsa or salad dressing

Directions:
In large skillet, prepare Taco Spices & Seasonings and beef according to package directions.
In large bowl, combine seasoned beef and remaining ingredients, except salsa or salad dressing; toss.  To serve, top with salsa or dressing.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
Taco salads are fun to eat and filled with Mexican flavor. This recipe includes all the key ingredients: beef, bean, cheese, tomatoes, olives, and your choice of salsa or dressing as a topping.
