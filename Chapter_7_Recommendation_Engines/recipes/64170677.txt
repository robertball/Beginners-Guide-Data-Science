Title:
Berry Tifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4-serving size package vanilla instant pudding and pie filling mix
2 cups milk
1  8-ounce package cream cheese, softened
1  6-ounce carton vanilla yogurt
1  10.75 ounce loaf frozen pound cake, thawed and cut into 3/4 inch cubes
6 cups fresh strawberries, quartered
2 cups fresh blueberries and or raspberries
1 receipe Berry sauce

Directions:
1.  Prepare pudding mix according to package directions using the 2 cups milk; set aside.  In a large mixing bowl beat cream cheese and yogurt with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.  Stir in pudding.
2.  To assemble trifle, in a 2 1/2 - 3 quart glass bowl layer one-third of the cake cubes, one third of the strawberries, and one third of the raspberries.  Repeat layers two more times.  Cover and chill for 4 - 24 hours.
3.  To serve, spoon Berry Sauce onto desert plates.  Top with trifle.
Berry Sauce
In a blender or food processor combine 2 cups fresh or thawed frozen strawberries or raspberries; 2 -3 Tbls. sugar; and 1 Tbls. raspberry liqueur, rum or orange juice.  Cover and blend or process until smooth.  If desired, press sauce through a fine-mesh sieve to remove seeds.  Cover and chill until ready to serve ( up to 24 hours).
Makes about 1 cup.
