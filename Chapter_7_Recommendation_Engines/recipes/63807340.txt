Title:
Onion/Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons Butter
4 Medium Onions
1/2# Fresh Mushrooms
2 Tablespoons Beef Base
8 Cups Water

Directions:
Slice the onions through the root and the again cross ways to make strips. Slice the mushrooms. Melt the butter on medium heat and add the onions and mushrooms. Saut√© till onions are translucent and show just a hint of caramelization. While the onions and mushrooms are cooking, heat the water and add the beef base. Once the onion mix is done, add to the water. Do not boil. If desired you can cool over night in the fridge to allow flavors to meld. Serve with Italian or French bread.
