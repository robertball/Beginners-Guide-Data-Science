Title:
The Best Scones Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cup   Flour
2 tsp   Baking soda
4 cup   Oats - Old Fashioned
8 tsp   Baking Powder
16 tbsp Sugar
2 1/4 tsp Coarse Kosher Salt
4       Eggs
1 pound Butter, not melted cut in small cubes
3 cup   Milk

Directions:
Mix all dry ingrediants in a large bowl.
Mix all wet ingrediants in a small bowl except butter. Mix wet and dry ingrediant together then add butter cubes.
Cut scones into 5 oz pieces and cook at 350 for 15 mins or until done.

Submitter: 
mariehipple
Description: 
Unlike most scone these are moist and almost biscuit like. I like putting Jasmine tea and honey in mine though they are very versitile.
