Title:
Tropical Dream Chia Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs chia seeds
1 medium banana
1/2 c fresh diced pineapple
1/4 c water

Directions:
In a blender blend banana and water
Pour blended mixture into a dessert container or bowl.
Add chopped pineapple.
Stir in chia seeds and mix very well.
Pop into the refrigerator to set up.  The longer you let it set up the better it tastes.  (overnight is ideal)
