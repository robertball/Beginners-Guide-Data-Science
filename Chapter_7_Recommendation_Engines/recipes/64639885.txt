Title:
Roasted squash nachos Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 cups butternut squash ( I get froZen bagged)
Olive oil
8 cups yellow tortilla chips
2 cups cheddar cheese grated
1 cup fire roasted corn
1/2 c sliced pickled banana pepper
1 cup halved cherry tomatoes

Directions:
Toss squash with oil place baking sheet 424 till tender 15 -20 min
On deprecate baking sheet- chips cheese bake 5 min top with the squash ... add corn tomatoes and peppers ....
