Title:
Pork Souvlaki with Pasta and Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pork souvlaki skewers
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 mini bell pepper, sliced
1 medium onion, halved and sliced
1 large carrot, chopped
1 bunch broccoli, cut into bite sized pieces
1 tsp minced garlic
3 tbsp Vegetable oil
¼ tsp cayenne pepper
3 tsp black pepper
2 tsp Italian seasoning
2 cups water
1 cup low-sodium tomato juice
2 1/4 cups uncooked Catteli Healthy Harvest Rotini
1 tsp Accent food enhancer
1 tsp chili powder
½ cup water
1 tsp cornstarch

Directions:
1 Remove the souvlaki from the skewers and cut into bite sized pieces, the saute in vegetable oil with garlic.
2 Add the carrots and celery, then broccoli, peppers, and then onions, saute about 10 minutes
3 Add the water and tomato juice and rotini then the spices, cook on medium heat about 15  minutes, then simmer for another 15 minutes or until pasta is al-dente, add cornstarch and water to thicken sauce
