Title:
Old Fashion Egg Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large Egg, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 eggshell of water, 2/3 cup of flour
*Note: recipe can be doubled or tripled ONLY.

Directions:
- Beat egg and water
- Add salt and beat well (use a large bowl)
- Add flour and mix with fork, adding flour if  needed so dough holds together and is no longer sticky
- Knead in bowl until you can pat it turn out onto a well floured surface
- Flour surface well as needed when rolling the dough. Turn ever so often, to ensure the backside doesn't stick
- Continue this process until the desir3ed thickness is reached (usually 1/4 to 1/8 of an inch)
- Using a pizza cutter cut dough across the middle and then cut strips in the desired width of the noodle
- Shake out the noodles put onto a well floured cookie sheet
- Allow the noodles to dry overnight uncovered
TO COOK:
- Shake off excess flour and put noodles into boiling broth
- Stir gently, and cover after the broth comes to a boil again. REDUCE heat
- Cook 30 to 35 minutes, until desired tenderness stirring occasionally.
- Enjoy!
