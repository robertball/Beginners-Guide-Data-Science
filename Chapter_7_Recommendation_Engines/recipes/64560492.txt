Title:
Homemade All-Purpose Cleaner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Orange (or any other citrus) peel
Distilled White Vinegar
An air-tight glass jar/ mason jar
Spray bottle

Directions:
First: Add your citrus peel into a mason jar until it's about 3/4 full
Next: Add the vinegar till the jar is full
Then: Put on the lid and let sit for two weeks before use
Finally: After two weeks strain the liquid into a spray bottle and toss the peels.
Note: When ever you eat oranges or grapefruit or use lime or lemon in a recipe save the peel and make this cleaner. If you have more that one jar at a time make sure you mark when you made each jar.
It is up to you what size of jar you use.

Submitter: 
Hokulani Pomaikai
Description: 
This is a citrus cleaner made from citrus peel and distilled white vinegar. It is great for cutting through greasy and grime in your kitchen. This cleaner is safe for little ones and your pets too.
