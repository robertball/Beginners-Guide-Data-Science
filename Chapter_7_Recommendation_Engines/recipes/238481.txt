Title:
Turkey Gravy from McCormick® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1253742.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 (.88 ounce) package McCormick® Gluten-Free Turkey Gravy Mix

Directions:
Stir water gradually into Gravy Mix with whisk in small saucepan.
Stirring frequently, cook on medium heat until gravy comes to boil. Reduce heat and simmer 3 to 4 minutes. (Gravy will thicken upon standing).

Submitter: 
McCormick Spice
Description: 
Serve savory gluten-free turkey gravy over roasted turkey or mashed potatoes.
