Title:
Grandma's Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/348942.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup shortening
6 cups sifted powdered sugar
4 tsp. meringue powder
1/4 cup water
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. cream of tarter
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, beat together the butter and shortening until light and fluffy. Gradually beat in powdered sugar. In a small bowl, stir together meringue powder and water. Beat into frosting. Add remaining ingredients; beat until light and fluffy. If frosting is too thick, add more milk until desired consistency is achieved.

Submitter: 
larkspur
Description: 
This is my family's recipe for buttercream frosting. It works wonderfully for decorated cakes, and tastes great!
