Title:
Queso (cheese) dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb "Velveeta" type cheese (store brand is good, too)
3 large Roma tomatoes, diced
1 large white onion, diced
2 Poblano peppers (roasted & peeled), diced
2 Anaheim peppers (roasted & peeled), diced
1 jalapeno pepper (roased & peeled), diced
1/4 c. heavy cream (or half & half)
1 tbsp butter
2 tsp cumin

Directions:
Cube cheese and put in slow cooker.
Dice tomatoes and add to cheese.
Dice onions and add to cheese.
Roast peppers under broiler (or on grill), cool,          peel & dice and add to cheese mixture.
Add cumin to cheese mixture.
Add butter to cheese mixture.
Turn on slow cooker to "low".
Allow mixture to begin melting and add cream (a little at a time).
Dip is ready when all cheese has melted and hot throughout.
Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
SHRIMPBAIT
Description: 
This is the BEST queso dip! Being from Texas, I know my queso, and I think this is the one that beats them all! It's full of flavor and all fresh ingredients.
