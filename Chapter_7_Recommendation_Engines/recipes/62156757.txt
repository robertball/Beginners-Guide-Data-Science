Title:
Creamed Tuna and Peas over Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 6oz. cans tuna [drained]
2 10.5 oz cans of Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 can of milk
1 16oz bag of frozen peas
1 14oz. box of Instant Rice
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Prepare your rice according to box directions,for as many servings as you want, set aside.
Empty your two cans of drained tuna in a skillet on medium heat, stir to break up tuna.
Empty your two cans of cream of mushroom soup in skillet with your tuna, stir.
Add one can of milk, stir.
Add your peas, stir till bubble hot.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Pour over rice.

Submitter: 
Trish Beier
Description: 
Very easy and fast. My mom made this for us when I was growing up.There's no hard or fast rules to it.Just go with your taste. I like alot of pepper. My father liked alot of hot sauce on it. Great with some crusty bread.
