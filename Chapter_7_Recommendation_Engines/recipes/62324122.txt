Title:
Baked Indian Pudding (Maple Syrup) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
4 cups hot milk
1/2 cup maple syrup
1/4 cup molasses
2 eggs, slightly beaten
2 Tablespoons butter or margarine melted
1/3 cup brown sugar (packed)
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cinnamon
3/4 tsp ginger (powdered)
1/2 cup cold milk
Vanilla ice-cream or light cream

Directions:
In double boiler, slowly stir cornmeal into hot milk.  Cook over boiling water, stirring occasionally, 20 minutes.
Preheat oven to 300 degrees Farenheit.
Lightly grease 2 quart (8 1/2 inch round) baking dish
In small bowl, combine rest of ingredients, except cold milk;
Stir into cornmeal mixture.  Mix well.  Turn into prepared dish
Pour cold milk on top...do NOT stir
Bake uncovered 2 hours, or just until set but quivery on top.  Do not overbake.
Let stand 30 minutes before serving.  Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or light cream.
Makes 8 servings.

Submitter: 
NB Roy
Description: 
Delicious baked pudding with maple syrup.  Great for that Thanksgiving or Easter Feast.  Served warm, but also great cold.
