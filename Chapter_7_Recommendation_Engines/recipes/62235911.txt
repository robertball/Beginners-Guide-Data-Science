Title:
Ranch Bacon and Parmesan Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of your favorite pasta
1 Cup Ranch dressing
6 strips of bacon cooked and drained of fat then diced
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
1 carrot peeled and diced
1 rib of celery diced
1 red onion diced

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package instuctions.  Mix all ingredients in large bowl except for Ranch dressing.  After pasta is cooled, add to bowl and mix well with Ranch dressing.  Refrigerate at least for 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Chef John Politte
Description: 
A great side dish for the summer picnic.  I made this on accident when I put some soup ingredients into the bowl instead of the stock pot.  I doctored it up and added other ingredients and Viola!
