Title:
Baked Chicken with Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken weighing about 2 pounds, cutting into 4 pieces
1/2 cup butter
1 3/4 cup frozen peas
3 medium carrots, sliced thinly
1 pound potatoes, peeled and sliced thinly
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1 cup dry white wine
2 Tablespoons water
2 large tomatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
2 Tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
1.  Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Season the chicken with salt and pepper
2.  Melt 5 tablespoons of butter in a large Dutch oven or flameproof casserole over medium heat.  Add the peas, carrots, and potatoes and sauté until lightly browned,  8-10 minutes.  Remove and set aside.
3.  Melt the remaining butter in the same pan and sauté the chicken and onion until lightly browned, 8-10 minutes.  Pour in the wine, water, and tomatoes.  Cover and bake for 30 minutes.
4.  Add the sautéed vegetables.  Sprinkle with the parsley and season with salt and white pepper.  Cover and bake until the chicken and vegetables are tender, about 1 hour.
