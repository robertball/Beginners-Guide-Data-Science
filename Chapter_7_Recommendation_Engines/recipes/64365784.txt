Title:
Healthy Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3201263.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large boneless skinless chicken breasts, 10 cups water, 3 oz crushed walnuts, 2 large celery sticks chopped small, 3/4 of a bunch of Italian parsley chopped fine, 3/4 bunch of green onion chopped fine, 10oz Smart Balance or similar low fat mayonnaise, 2 teaspoons salt, 1 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
in a large pot boil chicken in the water until cooked thoroughly (about 25 minutes)
remove chicken and shred (can be done by using 2 forks and tearing it in opposite directions)
in a large salad bowl add all but mayonnaise and mix well
mix in mayonnaise
refrigerate mixture and let cool before serving

Submitter: 
no_fun_at_all
Description: 
This a an easy to make healthy chicken salad that tastes great. I like to pair it with a light and healthy soup such as my other recipe for Spinach noodle soup.
