Title:
Peanut Butter Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/372994.jpg
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons water
2/3 cup powdered sugar
1 tablespoon creamy peanut butter

Directions:
Stir together the water, sugar, and peanut butter until smooth.

Submitter: 
QTCHEF
Description: 
This is a sweet peanut butter glaze, great on top of pastries or brownies and especially Danishes!
