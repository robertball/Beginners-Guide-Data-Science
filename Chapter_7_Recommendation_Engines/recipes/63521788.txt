Title:
Ruby's Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
3/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
3 chopped garlic cloves
3/4 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons prepared mustard
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
4 hamburger buns or 8 slices of bread

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat, combine the turkey, onion, garlic, and green pepper. Cook until turkey is no longer pink, and drain off excess liquid.
Stir in the ketchup and mustard, and season with salt, pepper, and other spices. Simmer for 20 minutes on low.
Serve on hamburger buns or whole wheat bread.
