Title:
Chicken Enchilada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken, cut up
1 lemon
1 lime
1 tbs minced garlic
1 medium onion, quartered
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp chipotle powder
5-6 jalapeno peppers, finely chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 tbs vegetable oil
1 can green enchilada sauce
1 bunch cilantro
2 cups shredded cheese
10 flour tortillas

Directions:
Place chicken in a pot and cover with water.
Add juice of lemon and lime, then just drop them in.
Add the garlic, onion, cumin and chipotle.
Boil for 1 hour.
Remove chicken from the pot and allow to cool enough to handle. Reserve 1/2 cup of broth.
Remove skin and bones and shred the chicken
Over medium heat, saute onion and jalapeno in oil
Add the chicken and a 1/2 cup of broth.
Add 1/2-1 cup of the enchilada sauce to the chicken mixture, increase heat to high and cook until chicken begins to brown.
Pour about a cup of enchilada sauce in a 9X13 dish and dip a tortilla.
Fill tortilla with about a 1/2 cup of chicken.  Top with some cheese and cilantro.
Roll tortilla and place seam side down in the baking dish.
Cover enchiladas in enchilada sauce and top with cheese.
Bake for 20 minutes.
