Title:
Low Carb Coffee Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups heavy cream
3 large eggs
1 vanilla bean
1/4 cup coffee beans
1/2 cup splenda sugar blend

Directions:
Heat the cream in a large heavy sauce pan until it's very hot bot not boiling; then take the cream off of the heat
Split the vanilla bean lengthwise and drop into the hot cream.  Drop the coffee beans into the hot cream also.
Cover the cream and let the vanilla and coffee steep in the cream until it reaches the desired flavor intensity; about 1 hour.
Strain the cream into a bowl and discard the vanilla bean and coffee beans.
Return half the cream to the saucepan, beat the eggs and add to the cream in the saucepan, and add the spenda
Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until thickened (approximately 10 minutes).  Mixture should smoothly coat the spoon.
Cool, then add the remaining cream.  Refrigerate overnight.
Freeze the custard mixture following the directions of your ice cream maker.

Submitter: 
Jim
Description: 
This Low Carb High Fat ice cream has a deep coffee flavor due to the way the coffee is infused into the cream.
