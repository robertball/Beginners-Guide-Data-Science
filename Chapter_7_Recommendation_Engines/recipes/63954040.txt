Title:
French Onion Soup My Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp butter or margarine
1 red onion
1 white onion
1 spanish onion
2 medium yellow onions (the ones which come in a carry bag)
pinch of white sugar
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 cup red wine or white wine or sherry
3 cups beef broth
grated cheese (what ever you have)

Directions:
Skin then slice onions into rings.
In soup pan add oil and butter and heat at medium  high heat.
Add onion sugar and black pepper.
Saut√© until some of the onions are a caramelized brown. (about 5 to 10 minutes)
Add wine scraping the bottom of the pan of the bits.
Add beef broth and simmer for at least 1 hour but if you can go longer it is better.
Service with your favorite grated cheese or no cheese if you wish.

Nadine

My family enjoys this soup.   DS#2 would never touch French Onion Soup until he tried this.  LOL he always orders it when we are out.
