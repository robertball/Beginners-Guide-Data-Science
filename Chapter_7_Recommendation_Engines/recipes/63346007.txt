Title:
Pumpkin Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 tsp cinnamon
2 TBL granulated sugar
1 1/2 cups milk
4 tsp baking powder
1 cup canned pumpkin
1 tsp salt
4 eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Mix together dry ingredients. Combine milk, eggs, butter and pumpkin. Stir into dry ingredients until just blended. Pour about 1/3 cup on to a hot griddle. Cook until top bubbles and turn then repeat for the other side. Top with your choice of syrup
