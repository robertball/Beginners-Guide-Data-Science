Title:
Winner Buttermilk Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 2/3c flour, probably more
2T white sugar
5t baking powder, aluminumfree
1 1/2t salt
1t baking soda
1t cream of tartar, probably optional ;)
1c shortening, chilled preferably
2c buttermilk

Directions:
Basic procedure. 16 15oz tomato sauce size can biscuits, 1/2" dough.
400 15 - 20; 425 may be fine for thin biscuits; 400 for an inch or so, before baking.
When using for Buffalo Chicken Pockets, bake at 400; higher temp the dough overbakes & the inner side isn't done enough.
