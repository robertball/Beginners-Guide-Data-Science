Title:
Crustless Spinach Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tblsp. vegetable oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 (10 oz.) pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well-drained
5 eggs, beaten
3 cups shredded Muenster cheese
1/4 t. salt
1/8 t. pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*.
Lightly grease an 8x8 baking dish.
Heat oil in a large skillet.
Add onions and cook until soft.
Stir in spinach and continue cooking until excess moisture has evaporated.
In a large bowl, combine eggs, cheese, salt and pepper.
Add spinach and stir to blend.
Pour into prepared dish.
Bake until eggs have set, about 30 minutes.
Let cool 10 minutes before serving.
