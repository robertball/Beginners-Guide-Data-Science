Title:
Cheese Popcorn Snack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/856353.jpg
Ingredients:
4 quarts plain popped popcorn
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon onion salt
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Place popcorn in two 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pans. Drizzle with melted butter. Combine garlic salt and onion salt; sprinkle over popcorn. Top with cheese. Bake at 300 degrees F for 5-10 minutes. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Denise  Baumert
Description: 
'These great snack reminds me of my high school days,' comments Denise Baumert of Jameson, Missouri. 'My sisters and girlfriends and I would stay up late, listen to music, talk and munch this crunchy treat.'
