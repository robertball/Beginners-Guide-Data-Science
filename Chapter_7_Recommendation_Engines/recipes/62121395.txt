Title:
Honey Mustard Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup dijon mustard
1/4 Cup olive oil
1/4 Cup lemon juice
1/4 Cup honey
2 cloves minced garlic

Directions:
In screw top jar, combine all of the ingredients.
Cover and shake well.
Serve immediately or cover and store in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
Shake well before serving.
Tip: Dressing has best flavor after being stored in refigerator for 1 day after preparation.

Submitter: 
Hank's Mom
Description: 
This dressing is especially good on Cobb salads!
