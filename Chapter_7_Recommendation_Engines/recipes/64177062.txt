Title:
Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Olive Oil - enough to cover skillet bottom
4 to 6 chicken breasts
Jar of salsa
Package of shredded cheese Tex-Mex flavor

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Pour oil into skillet and brown chicken on both sides. Place  chicken into greased 9 x 13. Place a teaspoon of salsa on each chicken breast and spread it over the meat evenly. Sprinkle cheese over salsa. Place in the oven for 15 to 20 minutes until chicken is cooked through and cheese is melted and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Rosebud
Description: 
This is a tasty chicken dish that can be served over a bed of rice or with a side of roasted potato wedges. Any flavor of shredded cheese will do.
