Title:
Lemon Dijon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6534885.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 chicken breasts, bone-in and skin-on
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup Dijon mustard
juice of 1/2 lemon
1 tbsp olive oil
grated zest of one lemon
1 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
2 tbsp parsley flakes
salt and pepper

Directions:
Pierce the chicken breasts with a fork and rinse well.  Combine mayo, dijon, lemon zest and juice, and the olive oil, and coat chicken liberally.  Marinate 1/2 an hour or up to 6 hours.
Combine bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, parsley and salt and pepper.  Coat marinated breasts and shake off excess.
Place on a rack over a sheet pan, and bake for one hour in a preheated 350 degree oven.   Allow to rest for a few minutes, and enjoy!
