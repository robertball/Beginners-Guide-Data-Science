Title:
SUGAR-FREE CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
5 Tbsp. margarine
1 large egg
1 tsp. vanilla
2/3 cup Splenda No Calorie
Granulated Sweetener
1/2 cup Hershey's Sugar-Free
semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup toasted walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Sift together the flour, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
With an electric mixer, cream the margarine for 1 minute. Add the egg and vanilla; beat on high for another 1 minute. Add the Splenda and beat until well blended. Beat in the flour mixture until smooth. Gently fold in the chips and the nuts. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours.
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Line 2 cookie sheets with parchment paper. Divide the dough into 24 equal balls. Arrange on the prepared baking pans; flatten slightly with the bottom of a glass.
Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes or until browned on the bottoms. Cool on wire racks. Store in an air-tight container.
