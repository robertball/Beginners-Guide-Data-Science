Title:
Spiced Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large russet potatoes
4 large sweet potatoes
1/4 - 1/2 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup honey
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1-1/2 tsp salt, or more to taste
dash cayenne pepper

Directions:
Peel and cut russet potatoes into 2" chunks.  Put into a pot of cold salted water and cook until potatoes are soft.  Drain well.
Meanwhile, peel and cut sweet potatoes into 2" chunks and steam for 20-30 minutes, or until soft.  Combine with cooked russet potatoes.
Mash potatoes with a potato masher or a hand mixer until fluffy.  Add butter and mix well.  Add honey and spices and mix well to blend.

Submitter: 
LINDA W.
Description: 
A wonderful alternative to plain mashed potatoes, this is a great side dish with roasted pork tenderloin or baked chicken.
