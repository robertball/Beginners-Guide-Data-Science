Title:
Chili and Lime Roasted Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp olive oil
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
8 oz baby carrots, trimmed
1/8 tsp kosher salt
2 lime wedges

Directions:
Preheat oven to 500 degrees.
Combine oil, pepper, and carrots on baking sheet. Bake at 500 degrees for 15 minutes, stirring after 10 minutes. Sprinkle with salt.
Serve with lime wedges.
Cals: 63; Fat 2.4g (sat 0.3g); Pro 1g; Fiber 4g; Sugars 5g; Iron 1mg; Sodium 209mg
