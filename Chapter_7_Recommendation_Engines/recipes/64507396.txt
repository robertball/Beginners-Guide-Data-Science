Title:
Smokey Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 T olive oil
1 onion, chopped
10 garlic cloves, chopped/crushed
1 T smokey paprika (or more)
1 T cumin (or more)
salt to taste (use smoked salt flakes if available)
1 c pumpkin puree (I used left over pumpkin with cumin)
15 oz diced tomatoes no salt
8 oz tomato sauce
6 oz tomato paste
2 c or more beef broth
2 T balsamic
handful chopped parsley (consider adding oregano)
water

Directions:
heat oil to medium/high heat
saute onion until soft
saute garlic until soft/fragrant
add herbs until fragrant
add pumpkin
add diced tomatoes, sauce and paste
add beef broth
add balsamic
add parsley
add water as needed
heat on high until bubbly
reduce to low and simmer as long as you can, to desired taste/consistency
serve with spaghetti and goat cheese and parsley
