Title:
Black Bean and Corn Salsa/Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4c balsamic vinegar
1/4c cider vinegar
2T brown sugar
1 1/2t fresh lime juice
1/2t cumin (ground)
1/4t salt
1 garlic clove minced
1c fresh or frozen (thawed) whole-kernel corn
1c chopped red pepper
3/4c chopped onion
1/3c minced fresh cilantro
1(15oz) can black beans, drained and rinsed

Directions:
Bring first 7 ingredients to a boil in a small saucepan.
Reduce heat and simmer 2 minutes till sugar is disolved.
Combine vinegar mixture, corn and remaining
Best if it has a couple hours to chill before serving.

Description: 
This is a yummy salsa to eat with tortilla chips!
