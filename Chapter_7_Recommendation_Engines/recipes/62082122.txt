Title:
Chicken Bacon Ranch Rollup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts, cooked and chopped
8 slices of pre-cooked bacon
1 tomato, diced
1/2 c. ranch dressing
2 c. mozzarella cheese or pizza blend cheese
1 tbsp. garlic salt
1 tube of refrigerated pizza dough

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Roll out pizza dough onto a rectangular cookie sheet or baking stone. Sprinkle garlic salt onto the crust. Cover the crust to the edges with the cheese, chicken, bacon, tomato. Drizzle ranch dressing over the top. Roll the pizza dough lengthwise like a jelly roll and press the ends together to seal. Cook for 25-30 min., until golden brown. Let stand for 5min., then slice and serve.

Submitter: 
Deborah Miller
Description: 
This delicious recipe wraps a combination of chicken, bacon, cheese, tomatoes and ranch dressing in a refrigerated pizza dough. It's super easy and very delicious.
