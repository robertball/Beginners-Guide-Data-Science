Title:
Cheesecake with Sour Cream topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1026340.jpg
Ingredients:
CHEESECAKE
1 graham cracker crust
3 pkg. (8 oz. each) PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
3/4 cup sugar
3 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
TOPPING
1 cup sour cream
1/3 cup confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups whipped topping

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 325 degrees
BEAT cream cheese and sugar in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended. Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing on low speed after each addition just until blended. Add vanilla; mix well. Pour in pie crust.
BAKE 50 to 55 minutes or until center is almost set. Refrigerate 4 hours or overnight.
In a medium bowl, combine sour cream, sugar and vanilla; mix well. Fold in whipped topping. Spread on top of refrigerated cheesecake.
