Title:
Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/875873.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c lemon juice
1/4 c tahini
2 T olive oil (optional)
15-oz can of garbanzo beans (drain, save liquid)
2 cloves of garlic, pressed
1/2 tsp ground coriander
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp paprika

Directions:
In a blender, blend the lemon juice and tahini (and olive oil).
Add garbanzos and enough of the liquid to achieve desired consistency.
Add remaining ingredients; blend thoroughly.
Chill.
Sprinkle with paprika; add olive oil if desired.
