Title:
German Cut out Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb margarine or butter
2 cups brown sugar
2 eggs
3 teaspoons of vanilla extract
4 cups flour
1 tsp. soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Cream together the margarine, brown sugar and eggs.  Add the remainder of ingredients and mix well.  Roll out dough and cut into your favorite shapes. Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes.  If desired, decorate with your favorite icing and sprinkles.

Submitter: 
jill
Description: 
This is a family favorite. The dough is very easy to handle and no need to refrigerate. Great for the holidays!
