Title:
Equal® and Cinnamon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Equal® Spoonful*
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Combine Equal(r) and cinnamon in a shaker jar or bowl. Sprinkling ideas: toast, French toast, cereal, fresh fruit, applesauce, yogurt, cottage cheese, hot cocoa and coffee, warm fruit pies and cobblers.

Submitter: 
EQUAL®
Description: 
Use this sugar-free version of cinnamon sugar on toast, cottage cheese, fresh fruit or applesauce and save 88% of the calories of the traditional mixture.
