Title:
Healthy Honey Cinnamon Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Muffins:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup quick oats
1/2 cup sugar
4 Tbsp. ground flax seed
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup mashed bananas
1/3 cup honey
1/4 cup applesauce
1/4 cup water
2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
1 egg
Topping:
2 Tbsp. sugar
3 Tbsp. packed brown sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Grease 12 muffin cups or use paper liners.
In a large bowl, combine the flour, oats, 1/2 cup sugar, flax seed, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon and salt.
In a medium bowl, combine the bananas, honey, applesauce, water, oil and egg.  Mix well.
Add wet mixture to dry mixture and stir to blend.  Fill prepared muffin cups with batter.
In a small bowl, combine the 2 Tbsp. sugar, 3 Tbsp. brown sugar and 1 tsp. cinnamon.  Sprinkle on top of muffins.
Bake for 25 minutes, until golden brown and a toothpick inserted comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
I was inspired by a delicious banana bread recipe that combined the flavors of honey, cinnamon and banana.  So I came up with my own very healthy version in muffin form!  This muffin also boasts a crunchy cinnamon sugar topping.
