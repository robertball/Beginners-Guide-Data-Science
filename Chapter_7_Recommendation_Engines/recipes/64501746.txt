Title:
Big Ray's Crock Pot Scalloped Spuds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2616400.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package (3 pounds) frozen cubed hash brown potatoes
1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted
1-1/2 cups whole milk
2 cup (8 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup plus 1 Tbsp butter, melted, divided
1 minced onion
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
3/4 cup crushed potato chips

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the hash browns, soup, milk, cheese, 1/2 cup butter, onion, salt and pepper. Pour into a greased 5-qt. slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 3-4 hours or until potatoes are tender.
Just before serving, combine the potato chip crumbs and remaining butter in a pie plate. Bake at 350Â° for 4-6 minutes or until golden brown. Stir the potatoes; sprinkle with crumb topping

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This slow-cooked side tastes great with almost any main dish and is a snap to assemble with convenient frozen hash browns. It's a good way to make potatoes when your oven is busy with other dishes.  This dish is great the first time around but don't like to be reheated.
