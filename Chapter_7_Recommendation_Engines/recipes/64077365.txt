Title:
West Texas Fried Venison (or Beef) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2# Venison (or Beef)
1/4 Cup Flour
Salt
Pepper
3 T. Bacon Grease (from cooking bacon)
1 Stick Celery
3 Onions (sliced)
1 t. Worcestershire Sauce (Lee & Perrins)
2 Cups Diced Tomatoes
1 Pk. Wide Noodles

Directions:
Cut meat into serving sizes. Coat with flour and salt and pepper to taste.
Melt the bacon grease in a pan on med high heat and add the seasoned meat. Brown the meat on both sides.
Add in the onions and celery and cook on med low till celery is just tender.
Add in the Worcestershire Sauce and tomatoes and simmer for about 2 hours or till meat is tender.
Cook the noodles as per directions and drain.
Serve the meat over the noodles.
