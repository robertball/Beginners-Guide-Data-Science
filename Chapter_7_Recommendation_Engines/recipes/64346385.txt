Title:
Cornbread I Vegan-style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup soy milk w 1 tsp lemon juice mixed in
1 cup stone ground cornmeal
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 egg sub - flax or chia
1 tablespoon applesauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (225 degrees C). Heat  a 9 inch round iron skillet in the heating oven.
In a large  bowl, stir the cornmeal, salt and baking soda together. Stir in the egg sub and milk.
Remove skillet from the oven and pour the batter into the skillet.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove from oven when top of cornbread is brown and it feels firm to the touch. Turn out on to a serving plate. Cut into wedges and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
