Title:
Spicy Garlic Lime Chicken - Using Microwave Pressure Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
1-1/2 cups chicken broth
6 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
Juice from 2 limes, then put the squeezed lime INTO the the microwave with all else.

Directions:
Saute chicken until golden brown, about 6 minutes on each side.
In a small bowl, mix all wet and dry ingredients together, add to the chicken broth, heat in microwave.
Pour some of the seasoned liquid into the microwave  pressure cooker, add three of the six chicken breasts, place all four of the lime halves into the pot, add the remaining 3 chicken breasts.  Pour remaining seasoned liquid over the chicken breasts.
If necessary, add water to raise the liquid level to be 1" below the rim of the pressure cooker.
Microwave for 30 minutes.  Let the pressure reduce before opening the pressure cooker.
