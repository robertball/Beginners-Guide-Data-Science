Title:
Moist Caramel Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/787292.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box caramel cake mix
1 large package instant vanilla pudding
4 eggs
1 cup oil
3/4 cup water
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup vodka
caramel syrup

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together and bake in a bundt pan or 9 x 13 baking dish at 350 degrees until toothpick comes out clean. Once cool, drizzle caramel syrup over top and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Alexandra
Description: 
This cake will stay moist and yummy for days after it is made!
