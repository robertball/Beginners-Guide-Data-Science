Title:
Chicago Steakhouse Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/690222.jpg
Ingredients:
2   Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 cup chopped onion
1   Tbsp. brown sugar
1   Tbsp. sweet smoked Spanish paprika
1   Tbsp. sweet Hungarian paprika
1   Tbsp. dry mustard
2   Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1   cup cola
1   cup Spicy Hot V8Â® juice
3/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup bourbon
1/4 cup rice vinegar
1   Tbsp. reduced-sodium soy sauce

Directions:
In a large heavy-bottom saucepan over medium heat sitr together olive oil, onion, and brown sugar. Stir in sweet smoked Spanish paprika, sweet Hungarian paprika, and dry mustard.
When onions are soft and spices are fragant, stir in Worcestershire sauce, cola, and Spicy hot V8 juice, ketchup, bourbon, rice vinegar, and reduced-sodium soy sauce. Brin to boiling; reduce heat to medium-low and simmer 15 minutes or until thick. Makes about 3 cups sauce.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
Steak sauce is a mouthwatering combination of cola, bourbon, soy sauce, and spices. Cayenne pepper adds a touch of heat to her signature six-spice rub.
