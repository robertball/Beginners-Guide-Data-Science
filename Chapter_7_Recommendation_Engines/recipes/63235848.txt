Title:
garlic-thyme marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup canola oil
3 T red wine vinegar
3 T chopped fresh thyme leaves
2 T smoked paprika
3 cloves minced garlic
1/4 t. cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together 1/4 cup canola oil, 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar, 3 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme leaves, 2 tablespoons smoked paprika, 3 cloves minced garlic and 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper. Place marinade and 2 pounds flank steak in a resealable plastic bag. Turn to coat. Refrigerate 6 hours to overnight, turning occasionally. Remove steaks, discard marinade and grill.

Submitter: 
GOLDENS
Description: 
Found this recipe in a newspaper, good for flank steak but I used sirloin and marinated a few hours. I thought it turned out great, not spicy at all. Carmelized the outsided perfectly.
