Title:
Cream of Mushroom and Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2	10 ounce packages of fresh mushrooms
1/2 	cup uncooked barley
1	onion chopped
2 	tablespoons butter
9	Tablespoons all purpose flour
3	(14.5 ounce) cans low sodium chicken broth
1 1/2 	cups half and half cream
2	chicken bouillons
1/2	teaspoon fresh ground pepper
1/4 	teaspoon dried thyme

Directions:
In a medium pan, bring water to a boil and cook barley for approximately 30 minutes.  In a large saucepan, saut√© onion and mushrooms in butter until tender.  Mix flour with broth until smooth stirring into the mushroom mixture.  Bring to a boil and cook until thickened.  Reduce heat and stir in cream, pepper, bouillons, thyme and cooked barley (drained).  Let simmer for an additional 20 minutes, stirring frequently.

Submitter: 
Sandi Miles
Description: 
My Husband and I recently went to lunch and we both ordered the cream of mushroom soup but they also had barley in it.  We both loved it so a few days later I decided to try making it .... voila, my version.  Hope you enjoy it as much as we did.
