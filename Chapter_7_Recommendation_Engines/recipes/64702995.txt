Title:
Easy French Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 lbs Onion, Vidalia  sliced thin
4 Tbsp Butter, Clarified
1.5 qts Beef stock**
1.5 qts Chicken stock**
2 tsp Poultty Seasoning
Salt and pepper, to taste
½ cup Dry White Wine
Croutons
Provolone Cheese Slices, Cut Medium Thickness

Directions:
Sauté the onions in a pot with the butter over low heat until the onions are caramelized but not burnt.
Deglaze the pot with the white wine then add the beef stock, chicken stock and poultry seasoning,
Bring to a simmer and cook on low for 25-30 minutes. Add Salt and Pepper to taste.
(Optional) Serve in croc bowls and top with croutons and cheese. Carefully place oven safe crocs in the oven under the broiler until the cheese is melted if you wish.

Submitter: 
ToriGeib
Description: 
Whenever I was a kid, I would always order French Onion Soup if it was on the menu. Now that I'm older, this has become one of those soups I love to make on a cold day  or with a big cheeseburger.
