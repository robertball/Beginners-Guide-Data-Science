Title:
ABC Rib eye steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 rib eye steaks, 1/4” slices, 1” long
1/3 package of tofu, cubed in 1/3” squares
1 package of enoki mushrooms, base trimmed (3.5 oz)
1 large red onion, sliced into thin rings, then cut in half
8 heads of baby bok choy, base trimmed
2 tbsp olive oil, divided
sauce:
3 tbsp oyster sauce
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp mirin or sweet rice wine

Directions:
Mix together the sauce ingredients and set aide
Heat a large wok on high, and add 1 tbsp of olive oil.  When hot, add the thinly sliced red onion and cook until just glazed.  Remove from pot and set aside.
Add the remaining 1 tbsp of olive oil to the same wok, stir fry the steaks pieces until desired doneness.  Remove and set aside, leaving any remaining meat juices in the pot.
Add the red onion back in with the bok choy, tofu, and enoki mushrooms.  Stir fry until the tofu is heated and the bok choy is wilted.  Add the steaks and the the sauce, toss to coat.
Serve hot

Submitter:

Description: 
Got some good steaks in the fridge, but the weather not cooperating with your grilling plans?  Try stir-frying your steaks in this Chinese inspired dish with mixed Asian ingredients.  Quick and easy, always a hit!
