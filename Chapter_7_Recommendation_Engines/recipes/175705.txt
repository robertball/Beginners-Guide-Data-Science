Title:
Easy Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 firm-ripe to ripe avocados
1/4 cup lime juice
2 tablespoons minced green onion
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 tablespoon minced fresh jalapeno chiles, or more to taste
1 clove garlic, pressed or minced
Salt

Directions:
Peel, pit, and coarsely mash avocados. Add lime juice, onion, cilantro, chilies, garlic, and salt to taste, mixing well. Serve in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Safeway
Description: 
Enjoy this delicious and creamy guacamole, spiced to your taste.
