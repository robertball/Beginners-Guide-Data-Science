Title:
Pasta with Scallops, Zucchini, and Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound dry fiorelli pasta
3 tablespoons of butter
splash of olive oil
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 chipollini onion
1 large zucchini, diced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 cup fresh italian parsley
3 roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped
4 large sea scallops
3/4 cup tomato sauce (plain)
pine nuts (optional)
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large pot with boiling salted water cook pasta until al dente (about 9 mins). Drain.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet heat oil and butter, add garlic and onion and cook until tender. Add the zucchini, salt, red pepper flakes, dried basil and saute for 10 minutes. Get another pan out add about 1-2 tablespoons of butter, make sure pan is good and hot to get a sear on the scallops. Cook scallops for about 3 mins per side. Add chopped tomatoes, tomato sauce and chicken stock to the zucchini mixture and simmer about 5 mins then add fresh parsley. Simmer another 2 mins or so (parsley should still be green).
Add pasta to the sauce pan, mix well. Transfer portions to bowls, top with fresh parm and pine nuts and then place scallops on top of the pasta!

Submitter: 
Shannon O'Keefe
Description: 
Sea scallops, chopped tomatoes and fresh parsley are added to a saute of garlic, onions and zucchini, then poured over fiorelli pasta for an elegant seafood dish. Serve as an entree.Modified from: Pasta with Scallops, Zucchini, and Tomatoes
