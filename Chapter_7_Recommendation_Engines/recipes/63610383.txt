Title:
Steak Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp. salt
4 tsp. paprika
2 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. onion power
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp. ground coriander
1/2 tsp. ground turmeric
1 tsp. ground cumin
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
Mix all together in a small bowl
Sprinkle seasoning over both sides of beef before cooking.

Submitter: 
Donna M. MacLean
Description: 
Easy to make, easy to use.  If you like mix this in larger quantities, and keep in container.
