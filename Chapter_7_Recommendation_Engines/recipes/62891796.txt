Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter
1 1/2 c. sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
2 bananas, smashed
2 egg yolks beaten (save the whites)
1 c. sour milk*
2 c. flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
2 egg whites beaten
1 table vanilla
1 cup crushed walnuts (optional)
*To sour milk add three teaspoons vinegar or lemon juice to milk, let stand for few minutes before using

Directions:
Cream butter, sugar, and salt. Add mashed bananas, egg yolks, and sour milk. Sift into first mixture the flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Fold in egg whites, vanilla and walnuts. Bake at 350 for one hour in a greased 9x12 pan.
