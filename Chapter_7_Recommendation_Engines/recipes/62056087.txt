Title:
Fiesta Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fiesta Stew
INGREDIENTS
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 (1.4-ounce) package taco seasoning
4 skinned and boned chicken breast halves, cubed
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 (15 1/4-ounce) can whole kernel corn drained
1 /4 c red and green peppers
1 (15-ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (14 1/2-ounce) can Mexican-style stewed tomatoes
1 (4.5-ounce) can chopped green chiles
3 cups water
1 cup uncooked  rice
1 (2 1/4-ounce) can sliced ripe olives
1(10 3/4-ounce) can condensed  cheese soup
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 tablespoon lime juice

Directions:
INSTRUCTIONS
Combine flour and taco seasoning in a heavy-duty zip-top plastic bag; add chicken. Seal and shake to coat. Add any remaining flour mixture to pan.
Cook chicken in hot oil in a large Dutch oven over high heat, stirring often, 4 minutes or until browned. Reduce heat to medium-high; add onion and garlic; saute 5 minutes. Stir in corn, next 6 ingredients. Reduce heat to low and simmer for a couple of hours .Remove lid, and stir in cheese soup, chopped cilantro, and lime juice.
