Title:
Chocolate Chip Chocolate Clouds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 pieces choc. chip cookie dough
2 C (12oz) semi-sweet choc. chips,divided
1/4 C milk
8oz non-dairy whipped topping,thawed,divided
1 t vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Place 12- 2 1/2" foil bake cup on baking sheet with sides Put cookie dough pieces in each cup
Bake 12-14 min. or until just baked and light golden brown. Remove to wire rack to cool completely.
Place 1 1/2C choc. chips and milk in large,microwavable-safe bowl. Microwave on high (100%) power for 1 min.;stir. If necess. microwave additional 10-15 sec. stirring until just melted. Cool about 20 min. until reach room temp.
Reserve 1/2 C whipped topping, refrigerate. Combine remaining topping and vanilla in med. bowl. Gently fold half of topping mixture into choc. mix. Add remaining whip topping, mix lightly. Spoon over cookie base in muffin cups. Refrig. 1 hr.
FOR GARNISH AND TO SERVE: Line baking sheet with wax paper. Place remaining chips in heavy duty plastic bag. Microwave on high for 30 sec.;knead. Microwave additional 10-15 sec.;knead until smooth. Cut tiny corner from bag. Drizzle 12 squiggly designs about 2-3" square onto prepared pan. Refrig. until firm, 5-10 min.
Top each mouse cup with small dollop of reserved topping. With tip of knife gently remove drizzles from paper and insert standing up into dollops. Serve immed.
