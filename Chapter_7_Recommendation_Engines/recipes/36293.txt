Title:
Texas Grilled Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds flank steak
1 tablespoon oil
1 tablespoon McCormick® Chili Powder
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Oregano Leaves
2 tablespoons lime juice
Flour tortillas
Salsa
Shredded Cheddar cheese
Sour cream

Directions:
Brush steak with oil.
Combine the next 3 ingredients to make a paste.  Rub paste over steak and refrigerate overnight
Broil or grill and slice thinly to serve.
Wrap steak in warm flour tortillas.  Serve with salsa, Cheddar cheese and sour cream.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Excite your tastebuds with the robust flavor of Texas Grilled Steak, served thinly sliced, in warm tortillas.
