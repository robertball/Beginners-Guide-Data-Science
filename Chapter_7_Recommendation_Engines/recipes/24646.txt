Title:
Guilt-Free Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3781500.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups crispy corn cereal squares
1 cup dried mixed fruit, chopped
1/4 cup yogurt covered raisins
1/3 cup chopped mixed nuts
2/3 cup banana chips

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together crispy corn cereal squares, dried fruit mix, yogurt covered raisins, mixed nuts and banana chips. Store in sealed, airtight containers.

Submitter: 
sal
Description: 
Here's a simple party or trail mixture that doesn't contain chocolate or salty snacks! It's great to munch on under any circumstances. Play with the ingredients until you get the combination you like best.
