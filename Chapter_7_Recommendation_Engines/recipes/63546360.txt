Title:
Berries and Cream Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/968619.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cream
3/4 cup half and half
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup frozen raspberries
1/2 cup frozen blackberries
1 cup frozen blueberries
1 cup frozen strawberries
1/2 cup milk (optional)

Directions:
Add the cream and half and half into the blender and blend on low speed until it begins to thicken.
Add the vanilla and sugar and mix in for about 30 seconds.
Combine with all of the berries and blend until smooth, if necessary use the extra milk to make blending easier and add more liquid.
Pour, garnish with whipped cream and/or berries and enjoy!

Submitter: 
The Smoothies 101
Description: 
If you have ever had a bowl of berries mixed with a little sugar and then topped with some whipping cream, you know that it is heavenly. If you haven't tried it before, maybe you shouldn't so that you can avoid forming an addiction. Whether you've had it or not, here is a great recipe that tastes just like it. It is simply in the on-the-go, drinkable form.
