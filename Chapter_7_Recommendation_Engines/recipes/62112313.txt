Title:
Spinach with Cheddar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pkg. frozen spinach, cooked and drained
16 oz. cottage cheese or ricotta
1/4 C. butter or margarine
4 eggs, beaten
3 tbsp. flour
3/4 C shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cook the spinach according to the directions and drain most of the liquid before putting in a mixing bowl.
Add the remaining ingredients, eggs last and mix well.
Pour into a greased casserole dish and bake 325 for 1 hour.
