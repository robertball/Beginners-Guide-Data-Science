Title:
Lattice Top Fresh Blueberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Pie Crusts
3 Pints fresh Blueberries
3/4 Cup "Brownulated" light brown sugar
2 Tablespoons Lemon Juice
lemon zest of 1/2 lemon
1/4 Cup Tapioca
1 teaspoon 7th Heaven "Sweet Heaven" spice blend or
1/4 teaspoon vanilla powder, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, and 1/4 teaspoon fresh ground nutmeg
1 Tablespoon cold butter

Directions:
Pre-Heat oven to 400 degrees
Line 9 1/2" pie plate with bottom crust
Blend Blueberries, sugar, lemon juice & zest, Tapioca, and Spices. Pour into bottom shell and dot with butter.
Top with lattice pie crust.
Bake on bottom rack 40 minutes with edges foiled, remove foil and bake 10 more minutes or until done.
Pie should bubble up through top. Place pie on foil or silpat mat.

Submitter: 
Vanessa C
Description: 
I wanted to find a recipe for fresh blueberry pie. I had to create this recipe because I couldn't find one I liked. Blueberry pie is my Mother-in-laws' favorite dessert.
