Title:
Stephanie's Savory Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/884860.jpg
Ingredients:
24 medium mushrooms
6 tbsp butter
1 small onion chopped (1/4 cup)
2 cloves minced garlic
1 package cream cheese 3oz
3 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
1 cup pepperidge farm herb seasoned stuffing
3 strips of bacon, cooked and chopped (optional)

Directions:
1. Heat oven 425 degrees F. Remove stems from mushrooms. Chop enough stems to make one cup, set aside. Fry 3 strips of bacon until crispy, set aside.
2. Heat 2 tbsp butter in a 2qt saucepan over medium heat until melted. Brush mushroom caps with melted butter. Place the caps in a 3qt shallow baking dish, put the hollow side up.
3. Heat the remaining 4 tbsp of butter in the saucepan until it is melted. Stir in chopped stems, onion, and garlic. Cook until stems are tender. Stir in cream cheese, parmesan, parsley, and bacon. Add stuffing and mix lightly.
4. Spoon a heaping tbsp into each mushroom cap. Bake 10 minutes or until filing is hot.

These are the best stuffed mushrooms you will ever taste! I don't really know how I got this recipe but it's super easy to make and they are perfect for any occasion! I usually serve it as an appetizer or side dish
