Title:
Summer Sangria Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz Frozen Lemonade
12 oz Water
1 Bottle Red Wine

Directions:
Combine Lemonade, Water and Red Wine.  Put in the freezer overnight. Great for a summer party!

Submitter: 
Razorback
Description: 
Delicious and easy Sangria Slush! The alcohol prevents the mixture from completely freezing.  The result is a slushy drink that is a tangy, sweet treat.
