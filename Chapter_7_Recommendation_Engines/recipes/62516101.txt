Title:
Uncle Dicks Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4lbs of potatoes
8oz cream cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 Tbsp salt
1/4 tsp white pepper
1 clove of crushed garlic
1 Tbsp of melted butter
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 cup of fresh chives

Directions:
Peel,cut and boil until fork tender then drain.Mix in cream cheese,sour cream,salt,white pepper and crushed garlic.Mash potatoes to desired consistency.Then put in a baking dish,cover and refrigerate overnight. Remove baking dish from frig,then cover top of potatoes with melted butter and and bake in oven at 350 degrees (preheat oven)for 30 minutes,remove from oven and garnish with fresh chives.

Submitter: 
Denise
Description: 
This is recipe is loved by everyone who has tried it.My husbands uncle kept this recipe a secret for many yrs and would not share it with anyone,we had to wait until he passed away & then my husbands aunt gave us the recipe.I made these for a thanksgiving dinner we had at church and now every time we have any sort of meal at church I am always asked if I going to make uncle dicks potatoes, even in the summer this is what they want me to make.I hope you enjoy this recipe as much as we do.
