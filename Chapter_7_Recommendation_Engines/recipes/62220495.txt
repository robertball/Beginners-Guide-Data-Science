Title:
Pap Casher's Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/314979.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup of cocoa
3 cups of sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix the first top 4 ingredients, bring to a boil (230 degrees) until it forms a soft ball form. Take from heat & add the last 3 ingredients. Pour into a buttered 8 x 8 pan. * to make peanut butter flavored just omit the cocoa.

Submitter: 
Jeni
Description: 
This was my great grandfathers fudge recipe, I can remember this as a small child. I started making this with my Grandma when I was a teenager. Chocolate or peanut butter fudge made without marshmallow creme.
