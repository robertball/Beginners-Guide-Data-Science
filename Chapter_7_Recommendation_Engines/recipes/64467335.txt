Title:
Pickled Eggs or Polish Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2446912.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups white vinegar
3 cups filtered water
3 Tbl sea salt
1 Tbl pickling spice
2 arms lengths of polish sausage, cut in chunks (or 4 dozen hard boiled eggs)
2 large onions, cut in thin rings
4 large, sterilized mason jars with lids

Directions:
Add first 4 ingredients to a large pot. Boil for 2 minutes.
Stir until all of the salt has dissolved.
Divide your eggs and/or meat and your onions in to 4 equal parts. Add to the 4 jars. Fill jars with boiled liquid. Put on the lids.
Once jars cool, put in fridge for up to 2 weeks. Should sit for at least 2 days for best flavour.

Submitter: 
BusyBeeTami
Description: 
If you use a traditional canning method, you could certainly put these on the shelf. This recipe as is, can not be used for preserving.
