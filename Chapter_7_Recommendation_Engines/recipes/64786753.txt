Title:
Grandma's Homemade Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs (3 whole and 2 yolks)
1/4 cup water
1 tea salt
3 cups flour (maybe more)
4ea 32oz boxes of chicken broth

Directions:
1. Beats eggs, salt and water with egg beaters
2.  Beat in two cups of flour
3.  With a wood spoon, mix in remaining flour (make it as thick as you can stir)
4. Divide into four balls
5.  Roll out on well floured pastry cloth with well floured rolling pin
6.  Put out four sheets of wax paper and heavily flour each one.  Let it sit for 1 1/2 hour.
7.  Turn 1/2 way (hard to flip).  Stack together.
8.  Cut into strips and stack together.  Cut Noodles.
9.  Let dry for several hours.
10.  Cook uncovered in a rich seasoned broth for 15-20 minutes.
11.  Serve
