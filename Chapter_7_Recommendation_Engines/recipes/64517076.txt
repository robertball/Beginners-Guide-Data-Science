Title:
Simply The Best Baked French Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2836123.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
3 lb bag of onions chopped into medium pieces
2-3 minced garlic cloves
sprinkle of flour
1/2 cup of white wine
3 fresh bay leaves torn into pieces
3 32 oz beef broth boxes
1 10.5 oz can of chicken broth
1 tsp worcheshire sauce
2 teaspoon dried thyme or 1 sprig of fresh
salt and pepper to taste
Slices of French bread (toasted)
Slices of provolone cheese
Shredded Swiss cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Melt butter with olive oil in a metal 8 quart stock pot (not nonstick) on medium heat. Add onions and occasionally stir until caramelized. (about a hour)
Stir in garlic, let cook for about a minute then deglaze the pan with the wine. Let cook until almost dry. Stirring occasionally.
Sprinkle with flour lightly covering the onions, let cook about 3 minutes (stirring constantly)
Add beef broth, chicken broth. bay leaves, worcheshire sauce, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper, and simmer for several hours covered.
Toast the bread.
Heat the oven broiler.
Ladle soup into oven safe serving bowls and place a layer of bread on top. Then add a slice of provolone, some shredded Swiss, and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese. Place bowls on cookie sheet and broil in the preheated oven until cheese melts, bubbles, and starts to brown.
