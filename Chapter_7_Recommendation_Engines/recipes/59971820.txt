Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/271304.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
6 slices Swiss cheese, sliced
4 slices cooked ham, sliced
1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Coat a 7x11 inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness. Slice each chicken breast into 4 slices.
Sprinkle each piece of chicken on both sides with salt and pepper.  Cut each slice of cheese and ham into 4 pieces. Place 1 cheese slice and 1 ham slice on top of each breast piece. Roll up each breast piece, and secure with a toothpick.  Place in baking dish, and sprinkle chicken evenly with bread crumbs.
Bake for 25 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink. Remove from oven, and place1/8 cheese slice on top of each breast piece.  Return to oven for 3 minutes, or until cheese has melted. Remove toothpicks, and serve immediately.
