Title:
Holiday Praline Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 c flour
1/3 c brown sugar, firmly packed
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 c butter
2 eggs
1/4 brandy (or bourbon)
2 c powdered sugar
2 c pecans, finely chopped
36 pecan halves

Directions:
Whisk together flour, brown sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in butter until crumbly. Beat together 1 egg and 2 tbsp of the liquor; blend into flour mixture. Pat into a 13x9x2 inch baking pan sprayed with non stick oil.
Combine remaining egg liquor. Blend powdered sugar. Stir in chopped nuts. Spread mixture over dough. Arrange pecan halves atop. Bake in 375 degree oven, 25 mins or till set. While warm, cut into 36 bars. Cool completly. Store in airtight container.
