Title:
Asian Marinated Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. fresh asparagus, trimmed
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup rice vinegar
2 TBS vegetable oil
1 tsp. sesame oil
2 cloves minced garlic
1 TBS grated fresh ginger or 1 tsp powdered ginger
2 TBS agave nectar or honey
pinch cayenne pepper or to taste
1 TBS toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
Cook asparagus in a pot of boiling water 1-2 minutes.  Drain and plunge into bowl of ice water to stop cooking.
Whisk together remaining ingredients except sesame seeds.  Pour over asparagus in baking dish, adding water if necessary to submerge asparagus.  Cover and chill several hours or overnight.
Drain and arrange on platter.  Sprinkle with sesame seeds.

Submitter: 
Robin
Description: 
A delicious balance of sweet, salty, tangy and spicy.
