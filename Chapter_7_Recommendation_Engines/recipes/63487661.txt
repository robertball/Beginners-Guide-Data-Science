Title:
Chicken Salad with Celery and Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked shredded chicken breast meat
1 cup celery chopped
1/2 cup slivered or sliced almonds toasted
2 tbs minced onion
1/2-3/4 cup mayo (may substitute some sour cream)
2 tbs fresh lemon juice (may add the zest)
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
stir almonds in a dry pan over medium heat until toasted.  watch carefully! immediately remove from the skillet to cool spread out on a plate
stir together the mayo, lemon juice, s/p.  combine the chicken, celery, almonds and onion with the dressing.  garnish with paprika, parsley, or hard-boiled eggs
option: bake 350F for 20 mins a casserole topped with grated cheese
