Title:
Cabbage Roll Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/352453.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
1 (29 ounce) can tomatoes, undrained
1 medium cabbage, chopped
1/2 cup carrot, sliced
1/2 cup uncooked white rice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
pinch of nutmeg
1 (14 ounce) can beef broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large skillet, brown beef in oil over medium high heat until redness is gone. Drain off fat.
In a large mixing bowl combine the onion, tomatoes, cabbage, carrot, rice, salt, peppper, Worcestershire sauce and nutmeg. Add meat and mix all together. Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Pour broth over meat mixture and bake in the preheated oven, covered, for 45 minutes. Stir, replace cover and bake for another 30 minutes.

Description: 
This casserole combines chopped cabbage with ground beef, tomato sauce, and rice. Preparation is much simpler than for standard stuffed cabbage rolls.Modified from: Cabbage Roll Casserole
