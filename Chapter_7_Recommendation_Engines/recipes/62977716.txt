Title:
Chocolate Cookie Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg.	 (3.9 oz.) JELL-O Chocolate Instant Pudding
1/2 cup	cold milk
36	 OREO Cookies, finely crushed (about 3 cups)
1-1/2 pkg.	 (8 squares each) BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Chocolate (12 squares), melted
1 square	 BAKER'S White Chocolate, melted

Directions:
BEAT pudding mix and milk in medium bowl with whisk 2 min. Add cookie crumbs; mix well.
2SHAPE into 42 (1-inch) balls. Freeze 10 min. Dip in semi-sweet chocolate; place in single layer in shallow waxed paper-lined pan.
3REFRIGERATE 20 min. or until firm. Drizzle with white chocolate; refrigerate 10 min. or until firm.
