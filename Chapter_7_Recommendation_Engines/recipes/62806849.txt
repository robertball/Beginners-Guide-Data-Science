Title:
Super easy tuna and rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 bag of Uncle Ben's 90 second microwave rice in the bag
-2 small cans of tuna in water
-1 tsp olive oil
-2 tsp lemon juice
-2 tsp minced garlic
-Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat rice in microwave for 90 seconds. Add drained tuna, garlic, olive oil, lemon juice and mix well. Microwave again for 45 seconds, stir again. Salt and pepper to taste and serve.
Makes 2 servings.

Submitter: 
Sarah Meedel
Description: 
I made this one night out of desperation on limited ingredients in the house and was surprised at how good it was and done in under 5 minutes.
