Title:
Creamy Red Italian Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/859394.jpg
Ingredients:
1-8oz can tomato sauce 1/2 cup olive oil 1/4 cup wine vinegar
1 Tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon oregano, crushed
1 clove of garlic minced
1/4 tsp coarse crushed black pepper
1/4 tsp basil, crushed
1/4 to 1/2 cup mayo and/or blend of mayo/sour cream

Directions:
In a screw top jar, combine all ingredient (except mayo or sour cream), cover and shake.
In a bowl, add 3 to 4 heaping tablespoons of mayo/sour cream.  Pour in desired amount of Red Dressing.  For dips, make it thicker - for salad dressings, add more and whisking until desired consistency is reached.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
This is a modification of Red Italian Dressing from Rit11_NMC.  Her original dressing is fantastic - I simply added a little mayo and/or mayo/sour cream to mix to cream it up a bit.
