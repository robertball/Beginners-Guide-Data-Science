Title:
Apple-Oatmeal Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4or5 med. cooking apples
6T firmly packed light brown sugar
6T granulated sugar
1/2 cup flour
3/4 c uncooked oatmeal
1 stick butter melted
1/2 c. water

Directions:
Core, peel and thinly slice apples
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Spread apples in bottom of a 8x8x2 baking dish
In a bowl combine brown sugar, white sugar, flour and oats.
Stir in melted butter.
Spread this mixture over apples
Pour water evenly over top.
Bake uncovered until the apples are tender.  About 30 min.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
Combines basic apple and oatmeal goodness.  Sweet enough  without  the extra sugar
