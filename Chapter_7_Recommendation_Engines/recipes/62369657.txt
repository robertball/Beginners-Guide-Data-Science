Title:
Chicken, Basil, and Feta Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz box of Penne Pasta, cooked according to directions, drain and put into large bowl
4 chicken breasts, seasoned, grilled & sliced into thin peices.
1-6oz. crumbled Feta Cheese
1 small chopped sweet onion
4 - 6 roma tomatoes, chopped
8 - 10 basil leaves sliced thin (or add more to taste)
1/2 cup chopped sun dried tomatoes--with oil (or more to taste)
1 - 16 oz bottle of Caeser Dressing (I like Cardini's Brand--again you may want more of less depending on tastes).

Directions:
With the cooked pasta in a large bowl, add onion, feta cheese, basil, sun dried tomatoes, fresh roma tomatoes, and toss together.  Add sliced chicken toss again.  Add desired amount of dressing (usually whole bottle).  Let marinate in refrig. for a few hours before serving.  Can be served room temp. or cold.

Submitter: 
tizzylizz
Description: 
A seasonal favorite!  This has been made for many, many summers & it is still a hit.  We love to use our fresh garden basil and tomatoes, then grilling the chicken makes it such a summery dish.
