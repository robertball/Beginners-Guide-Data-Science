Title:
Japanese Wasabi Popcorn Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of buttered microwave popcorn
3 tablespoons of Nori Komi Fuikake (prepared sesame seed and seaweed blend found at most Asian Markets)
1/2 cup of Wasabi Green Peas

Directions:
Pop bag of buttered microwave popcorn according to retailers direction
Once popcorn is done, open the top of the bag and pourr in Nori Komi Furikake
Hold the open end of the popcorn bag closed with 1 hand and shake lightly a few times so that the Nori has a chance to spread.
Open the bag and add the Wasabi Peas
Close the bag again and shake lightly 2 or 3 times to blend
Pour into large bowl and serve!

Submitter: 
Kimberley Drake Smith
Description: 
Popcorn blends beautifully with Wasabi Coated Green Peas and a few dashes of Nori Komi Furikake...it is also great if you add some Japanese Rice Crackers!
