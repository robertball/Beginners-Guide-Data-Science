Title:
Walnut Blue Cheese Artichoke Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1074168.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head lettuce, shredded
8 ounces crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
1 (8 ounce) jar marinated artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 cup walnut pieces
1/4 cup pitted and chopped kalamata olives, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Toss lettuce, Gorgonzola cheese, artichoke hearts, walnuts, and olives together in a bowl.
