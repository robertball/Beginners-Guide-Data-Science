Title:
Green Beans & Mushrooms in Ginger Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/519158.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter
1/3 c. chopped green onions
1/3 c. chopped fresh cilantro
1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
4 cloves garlic, minced
12 oz. fresh button mushrooms, sliced (about 3 ½ c.)
2 tbsp soy sauce
¼ c. unsweetened coconut milk
1 tbsp lime juice
2 bags of Steamed green beans

Directions:
In a large skillet heat butter over medium-high heat.  Add green onions, cilantro, ginger and garlic.  Cook and stir for 1 minute.  Stir in mushrooms and soy sauce; cook for minutes.  Stir in coconut milk.  Bring to boiling; reduce heat.  Simmer, uncovered, about 5 minute or until sauce thickens.  Remove from heat.  Stir in lime juice.  Pour over steamed green beans.  Can also be served over hot cooked rice instead of green beans.
