Title:
Panko Crusted Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tilapia Filets
Zest of 1 lime
1 1/2 cup of panko bread crumbs
1 1/2 cup of flour
3 eggs, beaten together
1/4 cup chopped cilantro or parsley
salt
pepper
garlic powder
cayenne pepper
Canola Oil

Directions:
Season filets with salt and pepper.
Put flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs in separate dishes.
Add lime zest, salt, pepper, garlic powder and cayenne pepper (all to your preference) to panko bread crumbs. Mix with fork to blend evenly
Starting with first tilipia filet put in flour, coat both sides and gently shake off excess
Dip tilapia into eggs, turning once
Dredge in breadcrumb mixture to coat the filet
Repeat with remaining filets
Heat 2-3 Tbspns Oil in pan.
Once oil is hot, place filets in pan.  Cook until it is browned and filets lift easily.  Turn and cook on other side.  Fish will be done when the temperature reaches 135 degrees.
Serve with brown rice and mango salsa

Submitter: 
EAMJM
Description: 
Enjoy this easy and delicious dish with brown rice and mango salsa.  Would also be great for fish tacos!
