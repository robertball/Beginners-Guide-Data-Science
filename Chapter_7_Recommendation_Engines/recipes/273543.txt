Title:
Avocado Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6723143.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pods cardamom pods
2 cups cold milk
1 ripe avocado, peeled and chopped
6 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
1 pinch salt (optional)

Directions:
Pop cardamom pods open and crush or grind seeds into a powder.
Combine cardamom, milk, avocado, sugar, and salt in a food processor. Blend until smooth. Divide between 2 glasses.

Submitter: 
Anu
Description: 
Avocado is not common in sweet drinks, but it's delicious. Try this shake and you won't regret it.
