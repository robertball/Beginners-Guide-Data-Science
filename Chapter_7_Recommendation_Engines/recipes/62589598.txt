Title:
Black Rice, Spinach, Salmon and Mango Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups black rice
1 8oz. salmon fillet
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. fresh grated ginger
1 garlic clove, minced
2 1/2 tbsp. canola oil
3 cups diced mango
1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
1/2cup green bell pepper, finely chopped
1/2 cup scallions, thinly sliced
6 oz. fresh baby spinach

Directions:
Rinse rice and drain well.  Cook in boiling water for 35 min or until al dente.  Drain and cool.
Heat skillet sprayed with cooking spray over med-high heat.  Cook salmon about 3 min. on each side or until it flakes easily.
Cool and break into bite sized pieces.
Combine lime juice and next 4 ingredients (through garlic) and stir well with a whisk.Gradually add oil, whisking constantly.
Add rice, mango, pepper, tomatoes, onions, and spinach, tossing gently.  Top with salmon and serve.
