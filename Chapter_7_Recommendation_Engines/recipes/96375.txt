Title:
Barbecue Turkey Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990690.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (20 ounce) package JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE® Extra Lean Boneless Turkey Breast Tenderloins
2 tablespoons barbecue sauce
Prepared 14-inch pizza crust
2 teaspoons olive oil
1/4 cup barbecue sauce
1/2 medium red onion, thinly sliced
1/2 green bell pepper, diced
1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat Monterey Jack cheese
1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 500 degrees F. Slice turkey into 1/4-inch-thick strips. Bring 4 cups water to a boil in large saucepan over high heat. Stir in turkey; cover and remove from heat. Let stand 3 to 4 minutes or until turkey is no longer pink in center. Drain; toss with 2 tablespoons barbecue sauce and set aside. Brush oil evenly over prepared crust. Spread remaining 1/4 cup barbecue sauce over crust, leaving 1-inch border. Arrange onion slices over sauce. Top with turkey, bell pepper and cheese. Bake 10 minutes or until crust is dark golden brown. Sprinkle with cilantro and cut into 8 wedges.

Submitter: 
JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE®
Description: 
Barbecued turkey and Monterey Jack cheese top this terrific pizza along with bell peppers and red onion.
