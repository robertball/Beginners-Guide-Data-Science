Title:
Creamy Apple Blueberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4-1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 egg
1 cup yogurt or sour cream
1 tsp vanilla ext.
3 cups sliced apples
1 cup blueberries
Topping
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup oats
1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix the flour,salt,sugar,nutmeg and cinnamon.  Add the egg, yogurt or sour cream, and vanilla. Mix all ingredients well and set aside.  Toss apples and blueberries and pour into a pie shell.  (I use deep dish)
Pour the wet mixture over the fruit and bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes. Reduce temp. to 350 and bake for about 30 minutes more.
When the pie is almost done mix the dry ingredients for the topping and cut in the butter.  sprinkle on the top and bake for 15 minutes more or until golden browned.
Cool and serve.

Submitter: 
Megan Karlowsky
Description: 
This is a wonderful pie I have created out of a few other recipies.  It is a nice blend of fruit and cream with a crispy top.
