Title:
Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. mayonnaise
1/2 C. buttermilk
1 T. red wine vinegar
1 T. sugar
1 t. garlic powder
1 t. onion powder
1 T. dried parsley (or 2 T. chopped fresh parsley)
1/2 t. dried dill (or 1 t. fresh dill)  optional
1/2 t. ground pepper
1/2 t. salt
dash of sweet paprika

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients well or shake in salad dressing shaker.  Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.

A simple, low cost ranch dressing that you will love!
