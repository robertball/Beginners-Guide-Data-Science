Title:
Fannie Mae,s Apple pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups of Apples
½ cups Sugar
¼ cup Brown Sugar
3 Tbls Flour
1 Tsp ground Cinnamon
½ tsp Ground nutmeg
¼ tsp Ground Ginger
1 Tbls Lemon Juice
¼ cup Butter or Margarine
Pastry for one 9” Pie Crust top and bottom crust

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients and pour over Peeled apple slices in a freezer bag add lemon juice. Toss mixture until slices are well coated. Turn apple mixture into a pastry lined pan, heaping in the center. Dot with butter or margarine.  Add top crust crimp edges to seal top and bottom crust and cut gashes for steam to escape. Bake at 450 degrees F for 10 minutes, then reduce to 350 degrees and bake 40-50 minutes longer.
Serve with a dollop of whip cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream

Submitter:

Description:
