Title:
Fluffy French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup milk
1 pinch salt
4 eggs
3/4-1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 heaping tablespoon white sugar
12 thick slices day old(or couple days old) bread(I use Durham,Challah,or Italian)*best 1st in order
4 pinches of brown sugar(1 pinch/3 pieces)

Directions:
Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt,vanilla extract and white sugar until smooth.
Very slowly, whisk in beaten eggs.
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over lo/medium heat.
Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Sprinkle each side with cinnamon to taste and lightly sprinkle brown sugar over top. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.
optional:top with butter and/or powdered sugar, and/or maple syrup

Submitter: 
S Sconi
Description: 
This French toast recipe is different because it uses flour- and already beaten eggs are slowly added- both help make this fluffy. This version is a somewhat sweet version with the option to easily make it more or less sweet to taste to cater to the tastes of everone in your family.
