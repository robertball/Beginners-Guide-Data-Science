Title:
Raspberry Peach breakfast smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 oz frozen raspberries
2 large skinned diced peach
1 C plain lowfat yoghurt
1/2/ banana
Fresh raspberries for garnish

Directions:
Pour all ingredients (except garnish) in blender, blend until smooth. Pour, garnish, serve.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
A fresh summer breakfast smoothie.
