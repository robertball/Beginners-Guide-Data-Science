Title:
Grilled Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices good bread
6 slices muenster cheese
sliced tomato
butter for grilling

Directions:
Butter outsides of bread. Layer 3 pieces of cheese, the tomatoes, then the remaining cheese. Grill until perfect doneness. I cook it on low to let the toms warm up.
