Title:
Roasted Almond  Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/604348.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
1 c. brown rice
1/2 c. wild rice
1/4 c. chopped sweet onion
3 1/2 c. chicken broth or vegetable broth
1/2 to 3/4 c.  Accents Original Roasted Sliced Almonds
Salt & Pepper to taste
(Finely chopped fresh mushrooms, celery or carrots or other vegetables of choice are optional)

Directions:
Sauté the onion in the oil until the onions start to brown.
Stir in the rice mix and sauté for a few minutes.
Add in the broth and water; bring to a boil.
Cover and lower heat to a simmer. Cook for about 60-70 minutes, or until rice is tender.
Fluff with a fork and stir in the fresh parsley, salt and pepper.
Top with Accents Original Roasted Sliced Almonds.  Serve.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Nutty savory side dish that goes nicely with fresh salmon, beef or grilled chicken main dishes; and served with a fresh vegetable salad.
