Title:
Judi's marinated chicken breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 whole chicken breast with bone in or 1 chicken
cut into 8 pieces
1 can diced tomatoes
1/2 bottle of italian
1/2 tsp oregano
1 can button mushrooms with juice.
2 bay leaves
1 package onion soup mix

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Add about 1/4 cup water to the marinate. Add chicken to a zip lock bag and pour in the marinate. Marinate over night. Pour all the ingredients in a 9x13 pan and bake at 350 uncovered for 25 minutes, then cover and continue to cook til done.
