Title:
Tater Tot Hot Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1/2 tsp salt
Pinch black pepper
1 32 oz bag tater tots, thawed
1 12 oz bag frozen mixed vegetables
1 10.75 oz can cr of mushroom soup
1 10.75 oz can cr of celery soup
1 6 oz can French-fried onion rings

Directions:
Brown ground beef with onion, salt, and pepper. Drain. Spread into bottom 9x13 inch baking dish.
Fold soup into vegetables and tater tots carefully and spread onto ground beef.
Top with onion rings.
Bake at 350 degrees for one hour.
