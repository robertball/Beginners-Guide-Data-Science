Title:
Blueberry Oatmeal Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 c AP flour
3/4 c quick cooking oats
1/3 c sugar
2t baking powder
1/2 t salt
1 egg
1/2 c milk
1/4 c canola oil
1/4 c sour cream
1 c blueberries
1/4 c quick cook oats
3T AP flour
3T brown sugar
2T cold butter

Directions:
Combine flour, oats, sugar, baking powder, salt.
Beat egg, milk, oil, sour cream; stir into dry.
Fold in blueberries; do not overmix.
Pour into 9" sprayed round pan.
Combine topping ingredients, cutting in butter until crumbly.
Sprinkle topping over batter.
Bake @ 400 for 20-25 minutes; cool on rack.
