Title:
Easy Curry in a Hurry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 cup Basmati/Indian Rice
2 cups chicken stock
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 pound chicken breast cut into cubes
1 large yellow onion cut in half and then sliced
1 small clove garlic minced
1 can stewed tomatoes
3 tablespoons cumin
3 tablespoons curry powder
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon paprika
1 can coconut milk

Directions:
Add 1 tbsp olive oil and butter to pan over medium heat.
Once butter is melted, add rice and cook for 3-4 minutes or until fragrant.
Add chicken stock, stir and bring to a boil.
Once boiling turn down to low and cover for 20 minutes.
While rice is cooking, heat a large sauce pan to medium heat and add olive oil.
While rice is cooking slice onion and mince garlic, and cut chicken into cubes.
Add onion and garlic and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
Remove chicken from pan.
Add tomatoes, spices and coconut milk.
Cook for 5 minutes and add chicken back in.
Turn to pan to low and cover, letting ingredients simmer until rice is ready.
Serve rice with curry and sauce on top.

Submitter: 
NICOLE
Description: 
This is a quick and easy curry recipe I use to spice up meal time during the week.  This also makes great leftovers!  By adding a couple of extra items to your shopping list each week you can make this delish meal anytime!  Pork can also be substituted for chicken in this recipe.
