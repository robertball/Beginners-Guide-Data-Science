Title:
Pineapple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CAKE:
1/2 cup of vegetable oil
2 cups of sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp of vanilla
2 tsp baking soda
2 cups all purpose flour
1 (20 oz)can of crushed pineapples
FROSTING:
1 8 OZ Pkg of cream cheese
2 cups powdered sugar
1 stick of butter
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
1. Mix together oil, eggs, and vanilla.
2. Add sugar, flour, baking soda.
3. Combine well and then add crushed pineapples.
4. Pour into a recatangle pan and bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes or until golden.
Let cake cool before adding frosting.
Frosting:
1. Let cream cheese and butter sit out of the fridge until well softened.
2. Beat cream cheese with powdered sugar, butter, and vanilla.
3. When well mixed, spread over cake.
This recipe works well as cupcakes too.

Submitter: 
Felicia Manocchio
Description: 
A very moist and tasty cake.  It tastes good plain or with the frosting.

It goes well as desert after eating 'Perfect Pineapple Orange Chicken'
