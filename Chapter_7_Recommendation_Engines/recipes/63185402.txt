Title:
Sweettreat's Soft Creamy Caramel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup light brown sugar, packed
1/2 cup light corn syrup
7 ounces sweetened condensed milk
1 tablespoon whipping cream
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a saucepan add all of the ingredients expect the salt and vanilla. Using a candy thermometer keep stirring the mixture until it reaches 230 degrees. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the salt and vanilla. Pour into a buttered 8x8 pan and refrigerate until set.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
This is the recipe I use to wrap around my cheesecake strawberries. We just love this soft buttery caramel and it's so easy to make and oh so good.
