Title:
Moroccan Carrots and Garbanzo Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 garlic cloves
1 can garbanzo beans
bunch carrots
handful of cherry or other tomatoes
handful raisins, prunes, and/or dates
olive oil
cumin
paprika
cinnamon
chili powder
turmeric
juice of 1-2 lemons
water or other liquid (stock, wine, etc)

Directions:
combine all and bake at 400-500 degrees until carrots soft or desired consistency (40 min-1 hour)
serve with goat cheese, rice, or bitter greens salad
