Title:
Brown Sugar Meatloaf (Ellen's Version) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sliced of bread
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 small onion, chopped
2 tsp. minced garlic
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 5x9 inch loaf pan and place bread in the bottom (to absorb the grease while cooking).
In a mixing bowl, thoroughly mix everything except sugar, ketcup and worcestershire sauce. Shape into a loaf and place on top of bread in loaf pan.
Mix brown sugar, ketchup and Worcestershire sauce.  Pour over the meat loaf.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until juices are clear.
Cut into slices, remove any visible bread from the bottom and serve.
