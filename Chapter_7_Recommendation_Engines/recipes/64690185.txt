Title:
Hula's Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 very ripe mashed bananas
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1/3 cup melted butter
Sift dry ingredients
1 1/2 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Set oven to 350.
Combine 3 mashed bananas + 3/4 cup sugar + 1 egg + 1/3 cup melted butter.
Fold in sifted dry ingredients 1 1/2 cup flour + 1 tsp baking powder + 1 tsp baking soda + 1/2 tsp salt until smooth.
Scoop into muffin cups
