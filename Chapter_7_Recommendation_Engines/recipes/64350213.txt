Title:
Fruit Cocktail Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CAKE
1-1/2 cups sugar
1-1/2 cups flour
1-1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1-1/2 tsp vanilla
2 eggs, beaten
3 cups fruit cocktail, drained (reserve juice)
1/2 cup brown sugar (or less)
1/2 cup walnuts, chopped
SAUCE
1/2 cup butter
1-1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup reserved fruit cocktail juice
2 tsp vanilla
4 tsp vinegar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
To make the cake, in large bowl combine 1-/2 cups sugar, flour, baking soda and salt.  Add 1-1/2 tsp vanilla, 2 eggs, and fruit cocktail.
Pour into a greased 9" x 13" baking pan.  Sprinkle with brown sugar and walnuts.  Bake for 50-60 minutes.
To make the sauce melt 1/2 cup butter in medium saucepan.  Add 1-1/2 cups sugar, 2 beaten eggs and fruit cocktail juice.  Boil until thick.
Remove from heat and stir in 2 tsp vanilla and vinegar.  Serve by spooning sauce over cake.  Delicious served warm.
