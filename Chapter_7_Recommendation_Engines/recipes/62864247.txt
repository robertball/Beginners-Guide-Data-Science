Title:
Chicken Brine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken
1 gallon water
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup kosher salt
3/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup olive oil
1 teaspoon each dried rosemary, dried thyme, black pepper

Directions:
Boil water in large pot, then add sugar and salt  until dissolved. Add rest of ingredients, stir. Take off burner, let cool completely before adding chicken. (you may add ice cubes if you wish) Once cooled, pour over chicken and put in refridgerator for no more than 10 hours. Once you are done brining, remove chicken, rinse under water for about 30 seconds and pat dry. Prepare chicken as you wish (roasting, grilling, etc)

Submitter: 
b.riddle
Description: 
This is a good brine for chicken that my family really likes. It's easy to adjust to suit your own tastes. Enjoy!
