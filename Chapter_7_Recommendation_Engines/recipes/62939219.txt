Title:
Mexican Potatoe Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Baking Potatoes cut 1/2" thick
3 T Oil
1/2 Pound Hamburger
1 can Black Beans
2 T Taco Seasoning
2 T Water
8 oz Shredded Cheese Cheddar or Pepper Jack
Tomatoes, Lettuce, Sour Cream and Guacamole for garnish

Directions:
Toss potatoes in 2 T oil, season with salt and pepper.  Bake in a single layer at 450' until golden brown about 20 minutes.
Brown hamburger in 1 T oil.  Drain beans and add to hamburger.
Add taco seasoning and 2 T water.
Lower heat and cook for 10 minutes stirring occasionally.
Sprinkle half the cheese on top of the potatoes.
Add the meat mixture and top with the rest of the cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, sour cream and guacamole.
This can be made with different cheeses, salsa, onions, cilantro or hot sauce depending on your taste.
