Title:
Caribbean Calypso Hot Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 onion
2 garlic cloves
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 Tbsp tequila or rum
2 Tbsp lime juice
2 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
2 tsp dry mustard powder
2 tsp salt
1 tsp ground allspice
5 habanero peppers

Directions:
Seed and stem the peppers, wearing gloves.
In a small pan, saute the onion and garlic in the olive oil until transparent.
Add the sugar and cook on low heat until the sugar caramelizes with the onions a little.
Add all ingredients in a blender and puree until smooth.

Submitter: 
Jordan VanDijk
Description: 
Packed with tangy sweet flavour and a tonne of heat! Reduce the number of peppers if you would like it milder.
