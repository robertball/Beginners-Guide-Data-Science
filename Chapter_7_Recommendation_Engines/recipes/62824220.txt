Title:
Oreo Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. cold milk
1 (3.4 oz) box instant vanilla pudding
8 oz. frozen whipped topping, thawed
15 Oreo cookies, broken into chunks
Additional cookies, halved (optional)

Directions:
In a bowl, whisk milk and pudding mix for 2    minutes or until slightly thickened.
Fold in whipped topping and cookies.
Refrigerate at least    4 hours.
To serve, top with cookie halves, if desired.

Submitter: 
maryL
Description: 
This is a great dessert to serve!  It can be made as-is or feel free to substitute ingredients for fat-free and/or sugar-free for a healthier dessert.  Enjoy!!
