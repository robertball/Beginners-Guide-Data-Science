Title:
PIZZA DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
1 JAR READY MADE PIZZA SAUCE (RAGU)
1 PKG. SHREDDED MOZZARELLA CHEESE (2C.)
1 8 OZ. CREAM CHEESE
1/4 LB. DICED PEPPERONI
1 SMALL ONION-DICED
1 SMALL CAN PITTED OLIVES-CHOPPED
Spray a ceramic casserole dish (quiche) with Pam or grease with shortening.  Spread cream cheese on bottom.  Pour pizza sauce over cream cheese and spread.  Sprinkle on top with pepperoni, onion, and olives.  Top with Mozzarella Cheese and bake 400 for 20-25 min. Serve with pita chips, Triscuits, or even baguettes

PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Great dip for parties or for snacks for the family.  Easy to prepare.
