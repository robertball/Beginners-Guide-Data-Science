Title:
Oriental Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag chopped coleslaw
1 pkg chicken flavored oriental noodles(ie. Ramen)
1/4 Cup sugar
1/4 Cup rice wine vinegar
2 Tbs sesame oil
1/4 Cup sunflower seeds
1/8 Cup toasted Sesame seeds
1/2 Cup chopped green onions
1/2 Cup slivered almonds

Directions:
Place coleslaw in large bowl and set aside.
remove seasoning from noodle package and crush into small pieces. Pour noodles over coleslaw. Put seasoning into small bowl and add oil, vinegar & sugar. Mix well and pour over coleslaw mixture; add almonds, green onion, sunflower seeds toss to coat. Top with sesame seeds  Refridgerate at least 2 hours.

Submitter: 
lisascooking
Description: 
This is a very simple recipe I adapted from one similar I had at a potluck dinner. It is salty yet sweet and goes great with picnic food.
