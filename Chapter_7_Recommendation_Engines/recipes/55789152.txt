Title:
Gooseberry Pie Grandma Ethal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
1 1/2 cups white sugar
2 T tapioca
4 cups fresh gooseberries
pinch salt
1/4 t nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F Roll dough out to fit a 9 inch pie plate. Place bottom crust in pie plate. Roll out top crust and set aside. In a small bowl crush 1 cup of berries, add sugar, tapioca, salt and nutmeg. Cook over heat until bubbly, add rest of berries.
Place  in the pastry lined pie pan and dot with butter. . Cover with top crust and seal and flute edge. Cut a few slits in the top to allow steam to escape.
Bake in the preheated oven for 15 then 350* for 25 min. Allow to cool. You may wish to cover the edge of pie with foil to prevent over-browning. If you do so, remove the foil for the final 15 minutes of baking.
