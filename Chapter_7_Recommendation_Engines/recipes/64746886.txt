Title:
Italian Chicken Pizza Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6605113.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken, shredded
2 8oz packages cream cheese
1/2 c no sugar tomato sauce
3/4 c chicken bone broth
5 garlic cloves, minced
2 tsp garlic powder
1/2-1 tsp onion powder
1 T oregano
1-2 tsp fennel seeds
1-2 pinches red pepper flakes
3-4 oz fresh basil
6 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
2-3 T olive oil

Directions:
preheat oven to 400F
soften garlic in olive oil over low heat
combine all sauce ingredients except basil and mozzarella cheese over low-medium heat
blend with hand mixer
add basil to sauce
put chicken in 9x11 pan
pour sauce over chicken
cover with shredded cheese
back 20-30 min at 400F or until cheese browned and bubbling
