Title:
Spinich and eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boxes frozen, chopped spinach
4 eggs, beatened
4 slices of crumbled, crunchy, cooked baon,
2 Tablespoons olive oil or other cooking oil
2 -4 Tablespoons butter
cup chopped onion
1/4 cup minced garlic
1 cup mushrooms, coarsely chopped
1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
seasoned salt
pepper

Directions:
Allow frozen spinach packages to thaw.
In a large skillet, cook bacon strips over medium high heat, turning once,  until crisp.
Remove bacon,  reserving pan drippings.
Drain bacon strips on paper towels.  allow bacon to cool, crumble and set aside.
Place thawed spinach in colander. Press and squeeze out as much water as possible and allow to drain.
Heat the reserved bacon dripping over medium high heat.
Add onions stirring occasionally until soft and translucent.  (approximately 3 minutes)
Add the spinach, mushrooms, and garlic. Stir to evenly distribute and cook approximately 5 minutes.
Stir in beaten eggs, salt and pepper.  Allow to cook until desired consistency.
Fold in cheese and bacon, serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Reuben Howell
Description: 
A simple, easy, and delicious breakfast dish or serve as a dinner side dish. Use more or less eggs and cheese depending on the meal situation!
