Title:
Shrimp Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30 Button mushrooms
1 clove minced garlic
3 tbs butter
2 cups of baby shrimp
3-4 tbs grated Parmesean cheese
5 tbs olive oil
4 tbs chopped parsley
6 tbs breadcrumbs
Juice of 1 lemon
3 tsp shallots
3 tsp cognac. (or More to taste)
2 tbs Dijon mustard
1 tsp ground pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients with shrimp to give a good consistency.  Add grated cheese to taste and refrigerate.
When ready to prepare, saut√© mushroom buttons in garlic and butter over medium low flame until softened
Place mushrooms caps in baking dish and fill with stuffing
Bake 350 for 5 to 10 minutes until cheese is melted and stuffing is heated through

Debe
Description: 
One of my favorite appetizers! I always add a little more cognac!
Never have one left on the tray!
