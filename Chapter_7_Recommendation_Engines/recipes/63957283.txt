Title:
Healthy Banana Nut Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1097894.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon olive oil
2 ripe bananas
2 large eggs
1 tablespoon apple sauce
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/3 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon sugar
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/8 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons finely chopped walnuts

Directions:
Heat oil on a griddle over medium high heat.
Beat bananas, eggs, apple sauce and vanilla extract in a mixer.
Add flour, cinnamon sugar, baking soda and salt. Mix until batter is well blended. Fold in chopped walnuts.
Ladle about 1/4 cup of batter per pancake onto the hot griddle. Cook pancakes until bubbles form and edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes. Flip pancakes and cook until second side is brown, about 1 to 2 minutes.
Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Chopped nuts give these deliciously healthy pancakes the taste of banana nut bread. A nice alternative to ordinary pancakes, these are also quick and easy to make.
