Title:
Nana Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Water
1 Egg
1 Tsp Salt
2 Tbsp Oil
2 Tbsp Sugar
2 Tbsp powdered milk
3 Cups Flour
2 Tsp Yeast (instant)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients together in a large bowl.
Kneed dough until it feels "right" 5-7 min
Let it raise in a greased bowl in a warm place until double in size (45-60 min)
Shape into buns and place on greased cookie sheet.
Let raise in warm space until double in size once again (45 min)
Bake at 350 degrees until golden brown (apron 10-12 minutes).
Makes 12 buns

Description:
