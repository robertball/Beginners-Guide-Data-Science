Title:
Easy Cheesy Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. reduced fat mayonnaise
8 oz. shredded Monterey Jack cheese
8 oz. Neufchatel (or reduced fat) cream cheese, softened
2-3 scallions, minced (including greens)
12-14 Ritz-type crackers, finely crushed
3-5 slices well cooked bacon, crushed

Directions:
Combine softened cream cheese,mayo,and Jack cheese in bowl.
Stir in scallions.
Combine crushed crackers and bacon in food storage bag.            Fill glass pie dish or similar size casserole with cheese mixture, spreading out to edges of dish. Press to flatten  top.
Sprinkle cracker/bacon mixture over cheese, to cover.
Microwave approximately 7 minutes. You will see cheese bubbling under the topping. Serve hot, with dippers.

Submitter: 
smartina
Description: 
This is so easy to make, and is always a huge hit. Simple ingredients, and right into the  microwave! It is thick and gooey and works well served with bagel chips, melba toast, or breadsticks. Totally addictive!
