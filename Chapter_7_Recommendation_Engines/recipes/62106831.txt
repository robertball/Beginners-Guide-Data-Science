Title:
Shrimp Augratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30 shrimp, de headed and de tailed
1 cup of cheddar cheese
2 cups of milk
2 cup of flour
1/2 cup of bread crumbs
Pinch of nutmeg
1/2 cup of parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon of seasoning blend
1 tablespoon of butter
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 shallot or red onion, sliced finely

Directions:
Add oil and butter to a skillet on high heat; add flour to make a thick rout.
Add milk to the rout while stirring.
When thick add more milk and stir.
Add salt and nutmeg to the rout.
Place the shrimp in a casserole dish and coat it with salt and seasoning blend.
Add cheddar cheese and sprinkle on top of the shrimp.
Add shallots, 1 teaspoon of seasoning blen and parsley on top of the casserole dish.
Then add the bread crumbs and 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese on top and bake until crisp.
You can serve the Shrimp Au Gratin on top of some of your favorite grilled vegetables, like portobello mushrooms.

Description: 
A unique way to eating shrimp, its Augratin.  Try it over grilled veggies and its even better.  Its very filling and appetizing.
