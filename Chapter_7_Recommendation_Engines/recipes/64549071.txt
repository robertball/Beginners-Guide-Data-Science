Title:
Thai Tea with Guilt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups boiling hot water
2 Thai black tea bags
1 star anise
1 cardomom seed pod crushed with back of knife
1 whole clove
2 sugar substitute packages
ice
1/2 cup fat free half & half

Directions:
Simmer the tea and spices in the boiling water for  20-30 minutes on very low heat.  Strain and add sugar substitute.  Allow to cool.
In two tall tea glasses, fill with ice and tea.  Top with fat free half & half

Submitter: 
Karen Barris Calabro
Description: 
A healthy version of the restaurant tea to cool the heat after a spicy Thai meal.
