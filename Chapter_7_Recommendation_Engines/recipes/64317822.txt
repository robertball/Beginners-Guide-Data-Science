Title:
veggie baked ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. olive oil
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
1 large zucchine, halved lengthwise and thinly sliced
8 oz. mushrooms, sliced
1 large red pepper, sliced
8 oz pasta, cooked (ziti, penne)
16 oz. marinara sauce
15 oz. ricotta cheese
1 c. grated Parm

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 Grease a 9 x 13 pan. Heat oil, add onion and cook till soft. Add other veggies and cook till soft and liquid has boiled off a bit. Remove from heat. Add pasta, sauce ricotta, salt and pepper.  Stir well. Pour into dish. Top with Parm. Bake 30 minutes.
