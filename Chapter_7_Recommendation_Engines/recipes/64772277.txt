Title:
Kaiser Schmarn  / The Kaisers' Cooks' senseless  mistake. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 oz of Flour
5 eggs
12 oz  Milk
1/2 tsp salt
4 tbsp powdered Sugar
4 oz Raisins
4 tbsp. Butter

Directions:
( Preheat the oven to 200 deg. ) mix flour with 3 tbsp milk into a smooth batter;  add the Eggs and beat into a smooth batter and add the salt and the rest of the milk.  Heat half of the Butter in a non-sticking pan ( Iron Skillet) when it begins browning  pour all the Batter in and adjust medium Heat.  after some time. add the Raisins.  When you see a swell;   Puncture; make a tear and cover with an Lid when you think that side is done;  turn it over. ( the Mess);  cover and put into the oven.  It should rise and when done 6-7 min.  remove and in the Pan;  Tear into small pieces with two forks. add the rest of the Butter and continue frying on the range and sprinkling half the powdered sugar.  Arrange on serving plates and Dust with the rest of the Sugar.
Serve with Apple Sauce, Cranberry Sauce, Berries, fresh apple slices.
*  apple slices may also be added with the Raisins.
