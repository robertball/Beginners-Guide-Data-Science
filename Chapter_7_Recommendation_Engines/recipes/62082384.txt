Title:
Pot  Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pound beef bottom round
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 cups onion, chopped
1 large tomato, chopped
1 bay leaf
6 sprigs fresh thyme
1 1/2 cups dry white wine
1/2 cup water
1 1/2 pounds potatoes
1 pound baby carrots
1 1/2 cups green peas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees. season roast with salt and pepper. Set dutch oven on stove over high with 2 tablespoons oil. When hot sear roast on each side for 3 minutes. Arrange onion, tomato, bay leaf, thyme around meat. pour in wine and water. Bring to boil. Cover and put in oven for 3 hours. Remove and add potatoes and carrots. Cover and bake 1 1/2 hours. Remove and set on stove top over low heat. Add peas to pot and simmer 3 minutes.
