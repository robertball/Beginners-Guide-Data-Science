Title:
Mom's Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups sugar
1 1/2 Cups oil
4 eggs
2 1/4 Cups flour
2 tsp salt
1 small can crushed pineapple
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla
3 Cups grated Raw carrots
1 1/2 Cups raisins, (optional)
1 1/2 Cups chopped nuts, (optional)
Frosting: 1 pkg Cream Cheese, softened
1 lb. powdered sugar
1/2 stick oleo or butter, softened
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Combine sugar, oil and eggs in large bowl. Beat 2 minutes. Add dry ingredients. Add carrots, raisins and pineapple.
Pour into prepared long pan.  Bake 1 hour or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Blend together frosting ingredients.  Frost cake when cool.

Submitter:

Description: 
This was my mom's favorite recipe for an old fashioned carrot cake, moist and delicious!
