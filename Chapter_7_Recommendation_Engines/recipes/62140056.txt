Title:
Dark Chocolate and Caramel Dipped Apples covered with Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium or large sized Granny Smith Apples
30 caramel soft candies
1/2 bag of Hersheys Dark Chocolate Chips
Handful of almonds
Popsicle Sticks
Other ideas for toppings... sprinkles, coconut, candy bars,cookies, you can use your imagination!

Directions:
Line cookin sheet with wax paper cover good with PAM
Unwrap caramels  put in deep bowl with 2 tbsp of water microwave on high for 2-3 minutes stir (they should be almost liquified)
Wash and thouroughly dry apples remove stems insert sticks
Dip Apples into Caramel you may need a spoon to help cover
Put onto cookie sheet and let cool in Fridge for 15 minutes
Meanwhile crush almonds or topping choice
Melt chocolate in deep bowl using directions on bag
Dip Apples in chocolate again you may need to use a spoon to cover
sprinkle with almonds
place on cookie sheet and allow to chill in fridge for 20 minutes or until chocolate is hard
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Tarryn
Description: 
The perfect fall dessert to fufill any craving sweet,sour,chewy, chocolate, and crunchy. Just like the gourmet ones you can buy at the store except Quick, Fresh, and more affordable!
