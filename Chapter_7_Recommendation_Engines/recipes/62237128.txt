Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups sugar
2/3 cup canola oil
4 eggs
1 (16 oz) can of pumpkin
2/3 cup water
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
3 1/3 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon cloves
1/2 cup walnut (optional)

Directions:
Mix sugar, oil and eggs.  Then add pumpkin and water and beat well.
Mix all dry ingredients together.  Add to pumpkin mixture all at once.
Pour into either mini loaf pans or 1 large loaf pan sprayed with cooking spray.  Bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes for large loaf pan or about 30 minutes for mini loaf pans.

Submitter: 
dhalladay
Description: 
This is a very moist pumpkin bread.  Every time I make it, everybody always requests the recipe.  It's a family favorite.
