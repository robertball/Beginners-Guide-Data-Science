Title:
Original Chocolate Chip Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1 1/4 cup all-bran original cereral
1/2 cup skim milk
1/2 cup orange juice
1 egg
3 tbsp vegtable oil
1/2 firmly packed brown sugar
1 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix cereal, milk and juice.  Let stand until cereal softens.(approx. 3 mins).
Meantime, in a separate bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Add egg, oil and sugar to cereal mixture.  Beat well.
Add flour mixture, stirring just until combined.
Fold in chocolate chips
Spoon batter evenly into lightly greased or paper lined muffin pans.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown and tops of muffins spring back.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lucy Mirabella Nino
Description: 
A healthier alternative for those who love the taste of an original chocolate chip muffin.  Kids will love these (and you will too!)
