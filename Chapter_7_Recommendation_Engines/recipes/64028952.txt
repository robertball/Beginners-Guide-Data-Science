Title:
Alba Spicy Beef Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1119545.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Sirloin steaks- thin
4 pieces Queso Fresco cheese
4 fresh poblano peppers-roasted/peeled
4 green onions
1/4 C cilantro sprigs
Monterey steak seasoning blend
2 C. Beef broth
1 small can tomato sauce
2 tbls butter
1 pkg of egg noodles-cooked/ buttered
Flour for dredging
Cotton twine for tying

Directions:
Season the steak
Lay the chilies horizontal across the meat, stuff with cheese, cilantro, green onion.
Roll up and secure with twine.
Dredge in flour and sear off.
Pour in the broth/tomato sauce, simmer for 1/2hr. Add the butter, take the steak out and let rest 10 minutes, slice the steak, serve over buttery noodles along with pan gravy.
I served this with coleslaw and flour tortillas.
