Title:
Nonna's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Cloves Garlic, minced
1 Large Onion (Diced)
1 Green Pepper (Diced)
Olive Oil
2 lbs Sausage (2X sweet to hot)
1 Jar of Tomato Sauce
2 Cans of Red Kidney beans (Drained and rinsed)
2 tsp Salt
2 tsp Black Pepper
2 tsp Chili Powder
Rigatoni
Fresh Parmesan (grated)

Directions:
In a frying pan; warm oil
Add diced garlic and onion, and cook for 5 minutes
Add sausage and cook until brown
In crock pot, add tomoato sauce, beans, salt, pepper, chili powder, green pepper, cooked garlic and onion.
Mix and add sausage
Cook 8-10 hours on low
Stir every few hours
Pour chili over cooked rigatoni
Garnish with parmesan

Submitter: 
KerriJ
Description: 
My mother-in-law's secret Italian-style chili recipe - so easy, cooks all day in the slow cooker and then served over rigatoni with thick slices of freshly grated parmesan!
