Title:
Ooozey Cheeseburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/4 cup breadcrumbs
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 tsp. ground black pepper
3/4 cup shredded cheese
hamburger buns

Directions:
In a large bowl, thoroughly mix ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, half the shredded cheese, ground pepper and Worcestershire sauce.
As the ingredients begin to mix together add the rest of the cheese.
Form into approx. 2 - 4 hamburger patties.
BBQ on grill or on a large skillet over medium heat for approx. 4 minutes or until internal temperature reaches approx. 145 degrees for rare, 160 degrees for medium, or 170 degrees for well done. Place cooked cheeseburger on hamburger bun and dress as desired.

Submitter: 
Anthony Maciel
Description: 
Easy to make hamburgers with cheese that oozes out of the meat for a cool and different look to traditional cheeseburgers!
