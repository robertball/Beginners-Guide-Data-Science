Title:
Peking Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 green onion thinly sliced
3 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp dry sherry
1/3 cup cornstarch
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp white pepper
2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs cut into 1 inch chunks
2 tbsp vegetable oil

Directions:
in a medium bowl, combine scallion, soy sauce, sherry, cornstarch, ginger, sugar and pepper - mix well.  Add chicken chunks and toss to coat completely, marinate for 3 hours
In a large skllet, heat oil over medium heat remove chicken from marinade, discarding marinade, and cook chicken in batches 8 to 10 minutes, or until crispy on all sides and no pink remains

M. Karen
Description: 
This easy version of a classic Chines dish is short on work but long in taste
