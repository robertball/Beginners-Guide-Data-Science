Title:
DEVILIED PORK CHOPS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/738392.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp.butter
1/4 tsp.each of cayenne pepper
black pepper
white pepper
6-6oz.boneless pork chops
1/2 cup chili sauce
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 tsp.each of salt
basil
paprika
chili powder
thyme
oregano
2 Tbsp.worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp.dijon mustard
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Make a smooth paste of the butter and peppers.
Place chops into a casserole dish and spread butter over top.Place under the broiler for 3 minutes,turn the chops over and broil for an additional 3 minutes.
Combine the remaining ingredients together in small bowl.pour over chops and bake for 20-25 minutes.
