Title:
Tarragon Parmesan Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound salmon fillet
2 tablespoons chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 tablespoons chopped red bell pepper
4 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons whole wheat flour
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon
1 teaspoon dried basil
2 tablespoons milk
2/3 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a skillet over medium heat, saute celery, onion and bell pepper in the butter until tender.  Add the flour, salt, pepper, tarragon, basil and milk; blend well.  Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly until thickened.  Add the cheese and stir until melted.
Place fish in a baking dish; spread the sauce evenly over the top of the fish.  Bake, uncovered until fish is cooked through and flakes easily when tested with a fork.
