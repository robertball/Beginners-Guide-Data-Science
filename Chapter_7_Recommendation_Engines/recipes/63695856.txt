Title:
Blackberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1027493.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups fresh or frozen (thawed) blackberries
2 Tb. white sugar
2 Tb. blackberry schnapps
A squeeze of fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Place berries in a small saucepan. Mash gently with a fork.
Over medium heat, stir in sugar, schnapps and lemon juice.
Stir constantly until berries break down and mixture begins to boil.
Cool slightly and serve over homemade ice cream, cake or other desserts.
Garnish with fresh berries.

Submitter: 
Cynthia Ross
Description: 
When blackberries are in season, celebrate them with this quick and delicious dessert topping.
