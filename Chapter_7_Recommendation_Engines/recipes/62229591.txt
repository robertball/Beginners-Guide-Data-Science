Title:
Kentucky Derby Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter, melted and cooled
1/4 c sugar
3/4 c brown sugar
1/2 c flour
3/4 c light corn syrup
4 lg eggs
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
2 tbsp Kentucky bourbon (JB Black Label)
3/4 semi-sweet mini chocolate chips
1 1/4 c toasted pecan pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Spread pecan nuts on a cookie sheet, and toast 1 hr stirring every 15 min. Let cool.
Whisk together flour and sugar. Set aside. In a bowl mix together butter, sugars, syrup, eggs, vanilla and bourbon until smooth.
Stir in pecan pieces and chocolate chips and pour into a prepared deep dish-pie plate.
Bake in a 350 preheated over 50-60 mins or until set.
Can be served warm or cold with whip cream or a scoop of ice cream.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
This is a pie traditionally served at the annual Kentucky Derby for 50 years.
