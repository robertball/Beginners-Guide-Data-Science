Title:
Val's Napa Valley Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Skinless chicken thighs
Seasoned flour
1 medium onion, minced
1 cup ketchup
1/2 cup cooking sherry
1/3 cup water
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon brown sugar

Directions:
Coat chicken with seasoned flour; set aside.  Blend remaining ingredients into a casserole dish large enough to hold sauce along with chicken.  Add chicken, turning to coat with sauce.
Bake at 350F. for 1 to 1 1/4 hours, basting occasionally until tender.  Serve over white rice.
