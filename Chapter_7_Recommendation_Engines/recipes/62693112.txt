Title:
Cherry Tomato Snap Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz sugar snap peas, trimmed
2 tbsp water
2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
1 clove garlic, minced
3/4 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt free Onion and Herb seasoning ex: Ms Dash
1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat for 5 minutes. Add snap peas and 2 tbsp of water to skilet and cover. Cook 3 minutes or until bright green. Remove cover and cook until water has evaporated, about 2 minutes. Add tomatoes, garlic, sugar and salt free seasoning to skillet and cook 3 minutes. Stir in lemon juice and serve.

Submitter: 
raebro
Description: 
A new take on Snap peas. Goes great with Stuffed Cheesey Chicken.
