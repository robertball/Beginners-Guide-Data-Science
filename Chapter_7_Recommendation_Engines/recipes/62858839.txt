Title:
SCD Banana Muffin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c. Almond flour
1/2 c. honey
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/8 tsp salt
4 eggs
2 mashed bananas
1/4 c. melted butter (if needed)

Directions:
mix all ingredients except butter in a food processor. If batter is too thick add butter a little at a time until muffin consistency.
Line cupcake tins with paper liners fill tins about 1/2 full. Bake at 375 for about 15 minutes or until the muffins spring back when pressed.
