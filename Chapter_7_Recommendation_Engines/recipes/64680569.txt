Title:
Pickled Ham Salad Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4881194.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound cooked ham
2 large stalks celery minced
1/4 red onion minced
1/2 cup mayonnaise, or to taste
1/4 cup sour cream (Optional)
1 tablespoon ground mustard
1/3 cup sweet relish
1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Chop the ham and add to a food processor and pulse until finally diced.
In a bowl add all ingredients and mix well.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
Whenever I cook a ham then with leftovers I make a ham spread. Goes great on a sandwich with lettuce and tomato or on a bed of lettuce. Easy to make and stays well in refrigerator for a few days.
