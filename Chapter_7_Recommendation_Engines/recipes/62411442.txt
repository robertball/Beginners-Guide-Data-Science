Title:
Shepherds Pie **Southern Style** Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/437969.jpg
Ingredients:
24 oz Velveeta Shells and Cheese (Family Size)
2 cans Corn
2 cans Peas
6 Medium potatoes (peeled)
2 lbs ground beef (browned)
1/2 Cup Sour Cream
4 Tbsp Butter
1 pkg taco seasoning

Directions:
Pre-Heat Oven 350 Degrees
For best results use a 13x9" glass casserole dish
Brown ground beef until there is no pink. Add 1 pkg taco seasoning stiring thouroughly and set aside.
Peel 6 medium potatoes, cut into cubes and boil until tender. Drain and put into a mixing bowl with 1/2 Cup of Sour Cream and 4 Tbsp of butter. Using an electric mixer mash until smooth on medium speed.
Drain your peas and corn (or vegetable of choice) set aside.
Prepare the Velveeta Shells and Cheese as directed on the package.
You will layer the ingredients in a 13x9" glass casserole dish starting with the mac n cheese on the bottom. Next the browned ground beef. Then your vegetables. Finally, your mashed potatoes being sure that it is 2-3" thick. Sprinkle your cheddar cheese and voila an easy delectable meal!!

This is not your traditional Shepherds pie. Macaroni and cheese gives this a real southern kick!!! 

Simple with everyday pantry ingredients-the ultimate southern comfort meal your family will surely enjoy.
