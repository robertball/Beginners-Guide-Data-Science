Title:
Slow Cooker Maple-Cranberry Pulled Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/675667.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3-lb turkey breast, bone in or boneless*
1 minced jalapeno
1 medium onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp horseradish
1 tsp dijon mustard
1 14-oz can whole berry cranberry sauce (or 1 1/3 cup homemade)
1 tsp hot pepper sauce
2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
Salt & pepper
1/4 cup maple syrup
1/4 cup brown sugar
5 or 6 sage leaves or 2 tsp dried sage
3 sprigs thyme or 1 tsp dried thyme
2 bay leaves
1/2 cup chicken stock

Directions:
Place turkey in crockpot. *I  leave skin on for flavor, but remove it later.
Combine onion, garlic, horseradish, dijon, cranberry sauce, pepper sauce, vingar, salt & pepper, maple syrup and brown sugar, and pour over turkey.  Add herbs and bay leaves.
Cover and cook on high heat 4 to 6 hours, or until done.  Remove turkey from crock, remove and discard skin and shred turkey with fork.  Remove bay leaves, sage and thyme sprigs.  Return shredded turkey to crockpot and heat through.
Serve on rolls.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Turkey breast slow cooked in a sweet and tangy sauce featuring cranberry sauce, maple syrup, apple cider vinegar and jalapenos.   You can also sub turkey thighs and legs if your crockpot can accommodate them!
