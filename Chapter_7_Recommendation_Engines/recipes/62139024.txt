Title:
Crab Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans cream of potato soup
1 small block Velveeta
1 small block Mexican Velveeta
2 pints half and half
1 1/2 pints whipping cream
2 small grated onions
2-3 lbs. lump or crab claw meat
cajun seasoning to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except crab meat, in a large dutch oven.
Stir over medium heat until all cheese is melted and soup is hot.
Add crab the last 10 minutes of cooking.

Submitter: 
Ashley
Description: 
This soup was served at my rehearsal dinner for my wedding, and everyone loved it. This is a quick and easy soup that is absolutley delicious!
