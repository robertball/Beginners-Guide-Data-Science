Title:
Cosmopolitan Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/993566.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
1 teaspoon dry vermouth
1/2 fluid ounce cranberry juice
1 twist lime zest, garnish

Directions:
Fill a shaker with ice and pour in vodka, vermouth and cranberry juice. Shake vigorously for 10 seconds. Strain into a cocktail glass. Garnish with a twist of lime

Submitter: 
KEN WILLIAMS
Description: 
This is a great drink for folks who like the presentation of a martini, but can't stomach the strong alcohol taste. The cranberry juice takes the bite right out of this drink and gives it a smooth taste.
