Title:
Six Week Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. to 20 oz. box Post Raisin Bran (25 oz. will be too much)
3 cups sugar
5 cups flour
5 t. baking soda
1 t. salt
4 beaten eggs
1 cup canola oil
1 quart buttermilk
extra raisins, optional

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients in very large bowl.
Add all remaining ingredients.  Stir to blend.
Store in covered container in refrigerator and use as desired.
Batter will last 6 weeks.
Lightly spray muffin tins with cooking spray.  Bake at 400 for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
Although this recipe makes 5-6 dozen regular size muffins, I prefer to make mini muffins with this recipe.    
It stores in the refrigerator for 6 weeks.  
Bake them when you want to have hot fresh muffins.     
They freeze well after baking.
