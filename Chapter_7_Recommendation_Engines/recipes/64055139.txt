Title:
Epicure's Mexican Layer Dips in Glasses Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1127738.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup prepared Epicure's Pueblo Bean Dip
1/3 cup prepared Epicure's Guacamole
1/3 cup sour cream
1/3 cup salsa
4 Tbsp. grated cheese

Directions:
Layer bean dip, sour cream, guacamole, and salsa. Top with grated cheese (and a little sour cream if you want).

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
A great appetizer to serve with taco chips.
