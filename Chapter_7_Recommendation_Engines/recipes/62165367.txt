Title:
Onion Haystacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large onions
3 cups flour
1 cup milk
2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1 quart oil

Directions:
Slice onions very thinly. They should not be in rings, and you should be able to see the knife through the slice of onion.
Heat oil to 375.
Mix the milk with 1/4 cup flour.
Mix milk and onions together.
Mix flour, salt, baking powder, pepper, and paprika in bowl.
Use tongs to put onions from milk into flour.
Stir onions to coat.
Add a handful of coated onions to hot oil.
Remove when browned.
Drain on paper towels.

Submitter: 
Kitty Johnson
Description: 
Onions are thinly sliced then fried. There isn't a fair within 100 miles of here that don't sell these.
