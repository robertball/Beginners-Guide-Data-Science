Title:
slow cooker bulgogi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade
4 tablespoons soy sauce
2 teaspoons sesame oil
2 tablespoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 clove garlic- chopped
Everything else
1 1/2 pounds beef, thinly sliced
1-2 green onions, cut into 1-inch pieces
1/2 tsp. sesame seeds, toasted

Directions:
Marinate meat for at least a half hour (but better if done overnight!). While meat is marinating, make a second batch of marinade for the slow cooker.
Put marinated meat and extra marinade into crock pot. Cook on low for 6-8 hours.
