Title:
Chicken Satay Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744653.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. 8 oz. Boneless Chicken Thigh
3/4 Small Onion
1 1/2 Garlic Cloves
Sauce:
3 TBS Lemon Juice
1 1/2 TBS Ground Coriander
1 1/2 Tumeric Powder
3/4 tsp Salt
3 TBS Sugar
1 1/2 TBS Cooking Oil
6 TBS Coconut Milk
1 TBS Cooking Oil

Directions:
Cut chicken into small cubes (3/4" pieces)
Finely grate garlic and onion into bowl
Add sauce ingredients to same bowl, stir well
Add chicken to bowl and marinate for 1 hour min.
Heat 1 TBS oil in frying pan over Moderate Heat
Add chicken and all sauce to pan, reduce to low heat
Cook thoroughly, changing between medium and low heat
