Title:
Lemon Crinkles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup shortening
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 egg
1 T grated lemon rind
1 ½ cups flour
½ tsp baking soda
½ tsp. cream of tarter
¼ tsp. salt
¼ tsp. ginger

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Mix shortening, sugar and egg thoroughly, add lemon rind.  Stir in dry ingredients.
Roll into 1” balls, dip tops in granulated sugar.
Bake on ungreased baking sheets for 10-12 minutes.   .

Description: 
An old family favorite, lemon cookies with a hint of ginger.
