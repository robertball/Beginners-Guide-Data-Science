Title:
Grandma's Aebleskevers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup milk
2 egg yolks
1 tablespoon shortening
3/4 cup flour
3/4 teaspoon baking powder
Pinch of salt
1 tablespoon sugar
2 egg whites

Directions:
Beat egg whites until stiff.
Mix all other ingredients together in a separate bowl. Fold in egg whites.
Heat up aebleskever pan. Grease with oil. Add batter, flip when partially cooked.
Serve with powdered sugar.
