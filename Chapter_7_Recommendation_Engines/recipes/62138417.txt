Title:
Cream of Portobello Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Portobello Mushrooms
1 large white onion
2 TBS minced garlic
1 Stick of Butter
1/2 Cup Flour
1 qt. Heavy Whipping Cream
1/2 Gallon Milk
Salt to taste

Directions:
Dice Mushrooms and onions. In a larger double boiler pot Sautee Mushrooms, garlic and onion in the butter until the mushrooms are tender and black. Add flour to make a thick paste, then quickly add the heavy whipping cream and Milk. Simmer on low until the mushrooms are completely cooked and add salt to your desired taste. This usually takes about 45 minutes from start to finish

Submitter: 
Rachel Dobbs
Description: 
My Families favorite homemade cream of mushroom soup made with hearty portobello mushrooms.
