Title:
Indonesian Fried Rice (Vegetarian) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744674.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Cups Cooked Rice
Stir-Fry:
1 Small Onion cut into RINGS
1/2 Cucumber, thinly sliced
Seasoning:
4 1/2 tsps cooking oil
1/2 an Onion, finely chopped
1 Clove of Garlic, grated
1 tsp Crushed Red Pepper
1/2 tsp Black Pepper
1 TBS Lemon Juice
Sauce:
1 tsp Salt
2 TBS Soy Sauce
1 TBS Lemon Juice
1/2 tsp Black Pepper
Chives (as needed)

Directions:
Mix together seasoning ingredients
Mix sauce ingredients
Stir Fry: Heat enough oil for deep-frying in a pan to 325 degrees; add onion rings and reduce to low heat; add cucumber slices; when onions are browned, transfer to dish covered in paper towels
Seasoning: In the same pan, with about half of the remaining oil from the stir-fry (approx 1 1/2 TBS), add garlic and chopped onion, cook until fragrant; add lemon juice, black pepper and chives
Add rice, stir-fry, and sauce to seasoning, mix well
