Title:
Easy No-Cook Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounce can tomato sauce
6 ounce can tomato paste
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs
1 teaspoon basil (optional)
Few shakes of garlic powder
Pinch of red pepper flakes
Salt, to taste

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients.

Submitter: 
Kristen M
Description: 
I like to avoid prepared and processed food, but with two young children, anything I make has to be easy and worth the effort (i.e. tasty!). Note: I usually use Muir Glen tomato products.
