Title:
Slow Cooker Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993454.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound Bob Evans® Original/Regular Recipe Sausage Roll
2 cups frozen peas and carrots
1 (24 ounce) package Bob Evans® Mashed Potatoes
1 (12 ounce) jar beef gravy

Directions:
In medium skillet over medium heat, crumble and cook sausage until brown.  Place in slow cooker. Add peas and carrots. Top with mashed potatoes.  Pour gravy on top of potatoes. Cover and cook on low 4 to 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Bob Evans®
Description: 
All of the elements of a sausage shepherd's pie are layered into a slow cooker for an easy and satisfying meal.
