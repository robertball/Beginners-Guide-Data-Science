Title:
Citrus Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup onion, finely Chopped
1 Cup carrots, finely chopped
1 Cup celery, finely chopped
2 Tbs. tomato paste
1 Cup beef stock
1/4 Cup fresh Italian parsley, chopped
1/2 Cup red wine
1/2 Cup orange juice
3 strips orange zest
1 1/2 lbs. beef chuck, cubed (1-1/2")
1 Cup red potatoes quartered
1 Cup green beans trimmed
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1 bay leaf

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute the onion, carrot, and celery until tender.  Place in the Crock-Pot.  In a small bowl, whisk the tomatoe paste into the beef stock.  Add the vegtables.  Add the remaining ingredients to the crock-pot.  Cover; cook on low 8 to 10 hrs. (or High for 4 to 5 hrs).
