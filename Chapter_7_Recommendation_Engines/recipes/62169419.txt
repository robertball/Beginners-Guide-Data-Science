Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts cut into 2 x 1/2" strips
1 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup dry white wine or white cooking wine
1 onion, finely chopped
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. dried thyme
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
2 TBSP. parsley flakes
6-8 Fresh mushrooms, sliced thin
3 TBSP. cornstarch
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup half & half
8 oz. spaghetti, cooked & drained
1/2 cup greated parmesan cheese

Directions:
In slow cooker combine chicken, broth, wine, onion, salt, thyme, pepper & parsley.
Cover and cook on LOW 4-5 hours.
Then turn to HIGH and add mushrooms.
Dissolve cornstarch into water; Stir into chicken; Cover and cook on HIGH for 20 minutes
Stir in half & half, cooked spaghetti and half of the parmesan cheese.
Re-cover, continue to heat on high for 5-10 minutes
Sprinkle with remainin parmesan (or more to taste) before serving.
