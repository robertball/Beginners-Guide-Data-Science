Title:
Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup applesauce
1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
1/4 cup honey
2 large eggs
1 cup peanut butter
2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
1 teaspoon coconut extract
4 ripe bananas, mashed
1 cup whole wheat flour
3/4 cup all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup chocolate chips
1/2 cup walnuts, chopped
STREUSEL TOPPING
1/2 cup 5-grain oatmeal mix
1/2 cup flour
6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Lightly grease two 5 x 9 loaf pan.
In a mixing bowl mix all bread ingredients together except chocolate chips. Fold in chips after everything else is combined. Pour equally into prepared pans.
Prepare streusel topping by combining dry ingredients and then adding melted butter. Toss to combine. Sprinkle equally over the tops of the two leaves.
Bake at 325 degrees F for about 45 to 50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the loaf comes out clean. Remove to a wire rack to cool.

Submitter: 
Sioranth
Description: 
A moist loaf packed with bananas, peanut butter, and chocolate. A lightly spiced streusel topping adds a sweet crunch to this dense quick bread.
