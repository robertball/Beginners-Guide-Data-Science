Title:
Magic Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (3 cups) Pepperidge Farm seasoned croutons
l box chopped spinach, thawed and drained well
4 eggs
2 cups half and half
8 oz grated cheddar cheese
Nutmeg (optional)

Directions:
Thaw and drain spinach (I actually press it with a spoon)
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Put croutons in 9-inch pie plate
Sprinkle spinach over croutons
Sprinkle cheese over spinach
Combine eggs and half and half by whisking well. Pour over the spinach-cheese-crouton combination.
Sprinkle with nutmeg.
Bake 40 minutes (check at 35)

Submitter: 
food-dancer
Description: 
This quick crustless quiche uses seasoned croutons instead.  You make it the night before to bake in the morning  Spinach and cheddar cheese combine to make a good flavor.  Very easy to make.
