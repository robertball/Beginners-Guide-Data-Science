Title:
6-Ingredient Gluten-Free Paleo Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6815562.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups blanched almond flour
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon Italian seasoning
2 eggs
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine almond flour, salt, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning in the bowl of a food processor; mix well. Add eggs and olive oil; pulse until a soft dough is formed. Place dough between 2 sheets of parchment paper and roll out dough to 1/4-inch thickness.
Remove top parchment sheet and transfer crust to a pizza pan; prick pizza crust with a fork.
Bake in the preheated oven until golden, about 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Fioa
Description: 
A foolproof and delicious gluten-free and Paleo pizza crust made in minutes. It has just 6 basic ingredients, and there's no need for gluten-free flour mixes as it's only made with almond flour.
