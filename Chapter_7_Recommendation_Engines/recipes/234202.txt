Title:
Easy Maple NUTELLA速 Baked Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1044668.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups quick-cooking rolled oats
1/3 cup lightly packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup milk
1 teaspoon maple extract
1 cup assorted fresh berries
1/2 cup unsalted trail mix
1/2 cup NUTELLA速

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Spray an 8-inch (20 cm) square baking pan with vegetable cooking spray.
In a large bowl, combine oats, sugar, cinnamon and baking powder. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs, milk and maple extract; stir into oat mixture just until combined.
Fold in fruit and pour into pan. Sprinkle top with trail mix.
Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until tester inserted into the center comes out clean. Let cool for 10 minutes. Serve with a tablespoon (15 mL) dollop of NUTELLA(R).
