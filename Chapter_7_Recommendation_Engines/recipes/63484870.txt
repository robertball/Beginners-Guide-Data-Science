Title:
Preschool "Grilled" Cheese Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
70 slices bread
1 cup melted butter
35 slices (Cheddar) cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Using a pastry bruch, butter one side of 35 slices of bread, and place butter-side down on a baking sheets. Arrange cheese on each slice of bread. Spread butter on 35 remaining slices of bread, and place them buttered-side up on top of the cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 6 to 8 minutes. Flip the sandwiches, and bake an additional 6 to 8 minutes, or until golden brown.
