Title:
Snacks' Crazy Sweet Corn Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1/4 pound margarine, melted
1/4 Cup milk
1/4 Cup white sugar
1/2 Tablespoon Vanilla extract
1/2 Tablespoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon clove
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
8 oz. low fat sour cream
1 (15 oz.) can of creamed-style corn
2 (15 oz.) cans of sweet corn kernels, drained
1 (8.5 oz) package of corn bread mix

Directions:
Pre-heat your oven to 375 degrees.In a bowl, beat eggs.Add the margarine, milk, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, clove and nutmeg. Blend well.Mix in the sour cream, creamed and sweet corn.Gradually stir in the corn bread mix and blend fully. Grease a 2 quart casserole dish or pan. Add mixture and bake for 45-60 minutes until light brown on top and firm.

Submitter: 
Snacks
Description: 
I've made this delicious side dish for many occasions and I always get compliments. This comes in handy around the holidays but really, is a great way to spice up an otherwise boring meal. It also makes a great late-night snack.
