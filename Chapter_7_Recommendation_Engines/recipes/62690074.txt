Title:
Red Chili Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pd Ground Beef
1 can (5 oz) green chili
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1/2 tablespoon pepper
1/2 tablespoon salt
1 yellow onion, diced
4 large tortillas
Red Chili Sauce:
2 tablespoons Flour
3 tablespoons Lard
1/2 cup NM Red Chili Powder
2 cups water
2 tablespoons garlic powder
1 tablespoons salt
1/2 tablespoon Oregano

Directions:
Meat:
Dice onion. Combine beef and onion and brown in large skillet.  Turn down heat, add seasonings and chili. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
Red Chili:
Brown flour in lard to light caramel color.  Add chili powder and let heat up (the heat activates the chili taste).  Slowly add water, stirring to prevent lumps.  Add seasonings and let simmer for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
TheCattlemansWife
Description: 
Easy, tasty and freezes well for future.  I make my own tortillas with Quaker Harina Preparada (very easy).  We also like with sopapillas.  All seasoning to taste.  I double the red chili batch and freeze in ice cube tray, then bag.
