Title:
Instant Pot® Acorn Squash Stuffed with Italian Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5666061.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 pound Italian pork sausage (mild, hot or sweet)
1/2 small onion, diced
1/4 cup diced bell pepper (red, orange, yellow or green)
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/4 teaspoon oregano
2 tablespoons panko bread crumbs
1/4 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
1/2 marinara sauce, divided use
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella, divided use
1 acorn squash halved and seeds remove
1 cup water

Directions:
Add one tablespoons of olive oil to inner liner and set pressure cooker according to manufactures instruction to saute. When oil becomes hot add sausage, onion and bell pepper. Cook until sausage is browned and no longer pink.
Add the garlic, oregano, bread crumbs, black pepper, 1/4 cup marinara and 1/4 cup of mozzarella. Stir until well combined. Remove mixture and drain of excess grease.
Stuff each squash half with the sausage mixture. Place trivet inside the inner pot and add 1 cup water.  Close, lock the lid and seal the vent. Set cooker on high pressure according to manufactures instructions and set timer for 4 minutes. Allow 10-15 minutes for pressure to build. When time is up allow for natural pressure release for 7 minutes. Vent remaining pressure if any.
Unlock and remove lid. Carefully remove squash halves and plate. Top with remaining marinara and cheese.
