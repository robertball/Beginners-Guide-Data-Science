Title:
Easy Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 31 oz. cans Pork & Beans
1- Medium size onion chopped
1- Green bell pepper chopped
1 cup ketchup
1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
5-6 slices uncooked bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
In a 9x13 cake pan or baking dish mix together pork & beans, onion, bell pepper, ketchup & brown sugar.
Mix all above ingredients well so that brown sugar is completly dissolved.
Place uncooked sliced bacon on top of bean mixture.
Put in oven and cook for 1 hour.
