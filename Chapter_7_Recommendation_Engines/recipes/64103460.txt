Title:
Japanese Hibachi Grill Yum Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1239408.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Best Food brand mayonnaise
1/4 Michiu brand Rice Wine
2 tbs melted butter
1 tsp ketchup or tomato paste
1 tsp white sugar
1/4 tsp garlic (add more to taste)
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp cayenne

Directions:
Stir all of the ingridients together in a bowl. Microwave for 45 sec -1 min to heat enough to help blend flavors together. Gets even better after sitting in the fridge overnight.

Submitter: 
Ashley Dellinger
Description: 
Love going to the local Japanese Hibachi Steakhouse but hate paying $20 for a plate. This is so good over sauteed (in butter) shrimp, carrots, zucchini, onions over fried rice.
