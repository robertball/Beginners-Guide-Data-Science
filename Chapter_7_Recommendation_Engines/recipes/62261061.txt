Title:
Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 small green bell pepper, sliced
1 small onion, sliced
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves (about 1 lb.)
3 Tbsp. sherry or chicken broth
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
1 jar (1 lb. 10 oz.) Ragu® Old World Style® Pasta Sauce

Directions:
Heat olive oil in 12-inch skillet over medium heat and cook green pepper, onion and garlic powder 3 minutes. Add chicken and brown 3 minutes. Stir in sherry and pepper; cook 3 minutes.
Stir in Pasta Sauce and cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, 8 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked. Serve, if desired, over hot cooked rice or pasta.
