Title:
Slow Cooker Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/965605.jpg
Ingredients:
2 - 15 oz cans red kidney beans
2 - 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
1 can tomato paste
1 tbsp olive oil
2 lbs lean ground beef
1 med onion, chopped
2 ribs celery, chopped
1 small can mushroom pieces, drained
1 orange pepper, chopped
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 med can baked pork and beans with molasses
1 tbsp hot sauce (ie. Frank's Red Hot)
2 tbsp molasses
2 - 3 tbsp chili powder
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp cumin
Salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet.  Saut√© onion and garlic till softened.  Add beef and cook until browned.
Add all ingredients to slow cooker and stir well.
Cook on low for 8 - 10 hours or high for 4 - 6 hours.
Serve with favourite toppings (ie. sour cream, grated cheddar and/or crushed tortilla chips) and cornmeal muffins.
