Title:
Brown Sugar Caipirinha Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3816688.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lime, cut into wedges
1 teaspoon brown sugar
crushed ice
2 fluid ounces cachaca (Brazilian rum)

Directions:
Combine lime wedges and brown sugar in a lowball cocktail glass; mash with a cocktail muddler to release lime juice. Top glass with ice. Pour in cachaca and stir.

Submitter: 
Cally
Description: 
The brown sugar picks up on the inherent smokiness of the cachaca. A cocktail to drink all summer long!
