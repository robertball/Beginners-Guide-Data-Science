Title:
Baked Mac and cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb noodles
16 sharp cheddar (shredded)
8oz Parmesan cheese (shredded)
8oz velveeta cheese (cubes)
2 12-oz cans evaporated milk
1tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1tsp tobacco
2 eggs
Panko bread crumbs
4 tsp butter
Paprika (dash)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Combine milk, salt, pepper eggs, tobasco in small bowl. Cook noodles. Mix noodles, cheeses, and milk mixture in huge bowl. Pour into casserole dish. Sautee panko in butter. Pour over Mac and cheese. Sprinkle with paprika. Bake at 400 for 25 minutes. Eat a ton.
