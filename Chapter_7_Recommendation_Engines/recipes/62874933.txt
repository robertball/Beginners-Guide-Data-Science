Title:
Napa Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 heads napa cabbage, shredded
1 bunch minced green onions
2 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles, broken
2 tablespoons sunflower seeds
1 cup slivered almonds, toasted
3/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup sesame oil
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 cup Splenda
1 tablespoons soy sauce

Directions:
Brown the ramen noodles, sunflower seeds and almonds in sesame oil until lightly browned. Mix balsamic vinegar, olive oil, sesame oil soy sauce and Splenda well.  Pour dressing over cabbage, onions, and crunch mixture just before serving.
