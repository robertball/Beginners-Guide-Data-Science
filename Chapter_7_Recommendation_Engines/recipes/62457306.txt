Title:
Red Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups skim milk
1 14.5 oz can low sodium chicken broth
6 medium red potatoes, diced
1/2 medium red onion, diced
1 cup bite-sized veggies
1 cup diced cooked ham
3 large cloves garlic, crushed
1 teaspoon basil
1 teaspoon sea salt or ground kelp
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a large saucepan over high heat.
Bring to a boil and reduce heat to medium-high.
Boil until potatoes are tender, approximately 15-20 minutes, stirring frequently.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
The title of this soup comes from the ingredients, not the color.  It's a delicious way to use up holiday leftover ham and vegetables and makes a perfect hot winter meal.
