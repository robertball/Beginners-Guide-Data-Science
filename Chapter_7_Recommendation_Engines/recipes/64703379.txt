Instant PotÂ® Stew using Frozen Meat Recipe

Ingredients:
1 Large onion, finely diced
3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
2 T Avocado oil
2 1 pound packages of stewing beef
1/4 cup dry red wine
6 medium carrots sliced
3 ribs celery, sliced
5 L potatoes, cut into large cubes
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp parsley
1 tsp oregano
1 bay leaf
2 cups beef broth
1 T tomato paste
1 tsp salt, or to taste
1/2 tsp pepper, or to taste
1 T corn starch
2 T water

Directions:
Saute the onions and garlic until soft, about 5 minutes
Add the wine and continue sauting until reduced by 1/2.
Add the beef broth, herbs, salt and pepper and stir.
Add the tomato paste, stir.
Place the frozen meat into the pot and add the carrots, celery and potatoes.
Place the lid on the pot and close the vent.
Press the Meat/Stew button and adjust time to 45 minutes.
Allow pressure to come down naturally for 10 minutes.
Carefully move the vent to release the remainder of the pressure.
Mix the water and cornstarch and pour slowly into the pot.
Press the saute button and cook until slightly thickened.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
Have you ever planed on making stew for dinner but forgot to take the meat out of the freezer? This happens to me all the time so I developed this recipe.
