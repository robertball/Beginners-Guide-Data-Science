Title:
Peachy Pennsylvania Dutch Baked Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/403323.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans of peaches
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 Cup of sugar
4 tablespoons butter
1 large egg
1/2 C milk
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 Cups rolled oats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Drain peaches and pour them on the bottom of a 9X13
sprinkle with cinnomon and sugar
Cream butter, sugar, eggs and then add milk.
Add baking powder, salt and milk.
Pour mixture over peaches
Bake 30 minutes
Spoon into bowls and serve with honey and heavy cream.  Yum!
