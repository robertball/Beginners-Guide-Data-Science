Title:
Naturally No-Cal Sweet Iced Green Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/433670.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups water
6 green tea bags
juice of 1 lemon (about 3 Tbsp.)
6 packets stevia no-calorie sweetener

Directions:
Bring water to a boil, remove from heat, and steep tea bags for 1 to 1 1/2 minutes.  Cool, then pour into a pitcher.  Add lemon juice and sweetener.  Refrigerate or serve over ice.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
Lightly sweetened with a natural no-calorie sweetener and a splash of lemon juice make this tea a wonderful refreshing drink on a hot summer day.  The antioxidants from the green tea are an added bonus!
