Title:
Best Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
1 egg
1 tbsp. Vanilla
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup vegetable oil
1 1/2 roll oats
1 cup corn flakes crushed
3/4 coconut
2 1/2 flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup macadamia nut or pecan (coarsely chopped)
1 cup chocolate chunks or chips

Directions:
Use ungreasd cookie sheets.  Cream together butter and sugars. Add egg, vanilla and beat well. stir in Vegetable oil. Add oats, corn flakes and coconut. Mix. combine flour and baking soda an stir in nuts and/or chocolate chips. drop tsp of dough onto sheet and flatten with fork (dip in water). bake about 12 min or browned. Allow to cool and store in airtight container.
