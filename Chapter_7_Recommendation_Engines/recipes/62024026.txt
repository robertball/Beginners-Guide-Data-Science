Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
4 slices of deli baked or boiled ham
4 slices of swiss cheese
1/2 stick of butter (melted)
1 cup of Seasoned bread crumbs
1/4 cup of Grated Parmesean Cheese
1/2 teas. Garlic powder
1/4 teas salt
1/4 teas pepper

Directions:
Flatten/pound chicken breast until approx 1/4 inch thick. Place 1 slice of the ham and 1 slice of the swiss cheese on top of the breast. Roll up; start at the smaller end. Secure with a toothpick. Complete remaining breasts. Melt butter and place 2 tablespoons in a glass pie plate for baking. Leave remaining butter in a bowl. Roll the breast in the butter and then the mixture of bread crumbs, parmesean cheese, salt,pepper and garlic. Place the breasts on the pie plate and dot with a small bit of butter. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes; or until golden brown and cheese is melted and oozing.

Description: 
This is a delicious, easy recipe for a family dinner or company; A bread crumb/Parmesean cheese coated Chicken breast filled with ham and swiss cheese and baked till golden brown.
