Title:
Cowboy Rib Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/670473.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Tbls Smoked Paprika
1 Tbls Chipotle Chili Powder
1 Tbls Brown Sugar
2 tsp Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
1 1/2 tsp Garlic Salt
1 tsp Ground Coriander
1/2 tsp Ground Cumin
1/2 tsp Cinnamon
1/2 tsp Ground Black Pepper
1 tsp Kosher Salt (or to taste)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl and you're good to go!

Submitter: 
Knightcraft
Description: 
A smokey, rub with unexpected flavor and just the right touch of background heat. Even your kids will love it!
