Title:
Scrambled Egg Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon
6 eggs
1 tblsp milk
3/4 can diced chiles or jalapenos
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy. In a bowl, beat the eggs and milk. Add  green chiles, salt, pepper . Stir in crumbled bacon and cheese.
Spoon by 1/3 cupfuls into greased muffin cups. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
