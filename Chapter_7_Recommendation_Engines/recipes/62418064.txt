Title:
Italian Anise Pillows Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/443185.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Cups All Purpose Flour
1 Cup Sugar
1 Cup Butter or Margarine, softened
3 eggs
5 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 Tbsp. Anise flavored extract

Directions:
In a large bowl cream sugar and butter.  Add eggs, baking powder, salt, and anise extract. Mix on med. speed until well incorporated.  Add 4 cups of flour.  Mix on low speed until dough forms.
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees F.
Shape 1 heaping tsp. of dough into a 1 1/2 inch oval.
Repeat with remaining dough, placing cookies 1 inch apart on ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake 10 min. or until cookies are lightly browned.
Remove cookies to wire racks.  Cool.
Store in a tightly cover container.

Description:
