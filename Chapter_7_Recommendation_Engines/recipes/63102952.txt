Title:
Roasted Red Pepper Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/787118.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds seeded/skinned roasted red peppers, pureed
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 onion, finely chopped
4 ounces butter
1/3 cup AP flour
32 ounces chicken stock
14.5 ounce can petite diced tomatoes
1 tablespoon fresh basil, chiffonade
1/2 tablespoon fresh oregano, minced
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 Pint Half and Half

Directions:
Melt butter in a large saucepan.  Saute garlic and onion until translucent.  Stir in flour until fragrant and just starting to brown.  Add stock all at once and bring heat to high stirring until smooth.  Add pureed peppers and tomatoes.  Season with basil, oregano, salt, and pepper, adjusting as needed. Bring soup to to just under a boil and add half and half.  Reduce heat to prevent scortching or boiling.  Serve as desired.

Submitter: 
Niland
Description: 
This simple and delicious soup is excellent in a sourdough boule, or with filled pasta, or any sausage.
