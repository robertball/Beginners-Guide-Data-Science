Title:
Fall-Off-The-Bone Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. pork ribs
water (for boiling ribs)
2 garlic cloves (crushed)
salt (to taste)
barbeque sauce (to taste)

Directions:
Fill a deep pot with water, about 2/3 of the way up and boil on high heat.
Once it starts to boil, add garlic, salt and ribs.
Lower heat to medium and allow to boil slowly for about 3 hours.
Pre-heat oven to 250 degrees.
Drain ribs and place them in a 9x13 in. baking pan.
Cover with barbeque sauce and allow to heat through (about 20 minutes).
Serve hot.

Submitter: 
VETTY430
Description: 
These ribs literally fall off the bones after you've boiled them for a few hours.  Boiling ribs may sound weird but they are really good.  Easy and you can modify the flavors to your own taste.
