Title:
African Bownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
4 large eggs
1/4 tsp salt
2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
2 tsp vanilla
1 cup unsweetened cocoa
2 tsp dry instant coffee

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and put the mixture in a greased and dusted (with cocoa) 9x13 pan.
Bake at about 325'F (162'C) for 30-40min (24-30 minutes convection oven) until firm.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Kedney'
Description: 
This is a recipe we made with no measuring tools. We only used wooden spoons, rubber scrappers, a baking dish, prep bowls, large bowls, a mug, and a regular spoon.
