Title:
Chocolate Mousse Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (8 squares) semi-sweet baking chocolate
1/4 c water
8 eggs, separated
1 1/2 t vanilla
2/3 c sugar

Directions:
Heat chocolate with water in saucepan over very low heat, stirring until smooth.  Stir in egg yolks, add vanilla.
Beat egg whites in large mixing bowl until foamy throughout.  Gradually beat in sugar until stiff peaks form, about 3 min.
Stir a small amount of egg whites into chocoate mixture to lighten, fold chocolate mixture into the remaining egg whites.
Pour 4 c chocolate mixture into a buttered 9 in pie pan sprinkled with sugar.  Chill the remaining mixture.
Bake at 350 for 25 minutes, or until just set.
Cool slightly, then chill 1 hour.
Center will fall, forming a shell.
Spoon chilled chocolate mixture into th echilled shell.
Chill 3 hours or overnight.
Garnish with whipped topping and chocolate curls if desired.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
A very rich mousse in a fudgy pie shell.
