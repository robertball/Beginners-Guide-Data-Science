Title:
Easy BEEF Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
2 lbs ground beef
2 cups sour cream
2 (4 ounce) cans chopped green chilies
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon salt
10 (8 inch) whole wheat tortillas, warmed
2 cans Enchillada sauce

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine 2 cups cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, chicken, sour cream, chilies, pepper and salt. Spoon about 1/2 cup off center on each tortilla; roll up. Place seam side down in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 20 minutes. Uncover; sprinkle with remaining cheddar cheese. Bake 5 minutes longer or until cheese is melted. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
