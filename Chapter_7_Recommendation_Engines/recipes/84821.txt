Title:
Fresh Strawberries with Brown Sugar Cinnamon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (16 ounce) packages fresh strawberries
1/2 cup light brown sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Saigon Cinnamon
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Pure Vanilla Extract

Directions:
Wash and gently pat dry strawberries. Place on serving dish.
Combine brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Spoon sour cream into a second small bowl; stir in vanilla. Dip berries in sour cream, then in brown sugar/cinnamon mixture. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Gourmet Collection®
Description: 
Fresh berries are the perfect light dessert - made even more special with this quick, flavorful dip. Serve the berries separately so that each person can dip their own berries in sour cream then the brown sugar cinnamon blend.
