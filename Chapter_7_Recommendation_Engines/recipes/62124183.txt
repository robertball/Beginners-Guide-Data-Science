Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. grated chedder cheese
1 head of lettuce, chopped
1 small can of kidney beans, drained
1 large onion, chopped
4 med. tomatoes, chopped
1 lb. hamburger, browned with 1/2 pkg. of taco seasoning

Directions:
Cook hamburger and seasoning.  Cool.  Combine all ingred.  Just before serving, add one large bag of crushed doritos and sauce.
Sauce:
1 8oz. bottle of 1000 island dressing
1/3 C. sugar
1T. taco seasoning
1T. taco sauce

Submitter: 
Lynne Marvelli Vansteenwyk
Description: 
Handed down to me from my mother-in-law, now it is a favorite on my side of the family too.  This is a good recipe for a family gathering or a potluck.
