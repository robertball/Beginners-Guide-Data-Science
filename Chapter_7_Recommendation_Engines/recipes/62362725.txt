Title:
Bulgur Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798475.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup uncooked bulgur wheat
3/4 cup water or chicken stock
2 Roma (plum) tomatoes, seeded and finely chopped
1/2 cup shredded fresh spinach
1/3 cup finely chopped red onion
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 teaspoons olive oil
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

Directions:
Bring wheat and water to a boil in a small saucepan. Turn down to low heat; cover and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed. Fluff and cool.
In a medium bowl, whisk together lemon juice, oil, and salt.
Add and mix bulgur, tomatoes, spinach, and onion.
Refridgerate for 30 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
A very healthy and addicting Middle Eastern salad. Great way to add fiber in your health.    The ingredients can be adjusted easily for your liking such as mint, parsley, avacados and even feta cheese.
