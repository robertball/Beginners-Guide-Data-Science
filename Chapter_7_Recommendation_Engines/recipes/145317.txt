Ingredients:

Directions:
Heat grill.  Place steaks, salad dressing and peppers in resealable food-safe plastic bag.  Seal and turn bag several times to coat the steaks.  Remove steak from marinade; discard marinade.
Place steaks on grill over medium-high heat.  Cover and cook 10 to 14 minutes, turning once, until desired doneness.
