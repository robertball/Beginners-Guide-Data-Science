Title:
Aunt Lori's Didelles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 eggs, 1/2 pound margarine, 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar, 3 teaspoons vanilla, 7 teaspoons baking powder, 6 cups flour, powdered sugar, water, rainbow sprinkles.

Directions:
In one bowl, combine eggs, margarine, sugar, and vanilla; mix thoroughly.
In separate bowl, mix baking powder and flour.
Combine all ingredients.  Shape small amount of dough between hands to form into a pencil shape and then connect the ends to form a complete circle.
Bake at 400Â° for 10 minutes.  Cool cookies and then dip in icing and sprinkes.
Icing:  Mix powdered sugar & water in a small bowl until well blended.
