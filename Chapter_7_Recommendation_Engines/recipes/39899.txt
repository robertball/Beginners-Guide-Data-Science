Title:
Fricken Yummy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/632621.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger apple schnapps
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger peach schnapps
4 fluid ounces lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Directions:
Fill an 8 ounce glass with ice. Pour in apple and peach schnapps. Fill glass with lemon-lime soda; stir.
