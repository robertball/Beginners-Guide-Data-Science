Title:
Michmeig's Graham Pudding Banana Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box Instant Chocolate Pudding Mix
1 Cup Milk
Graham Crackers (Approx 10 Full Graham Crackers)
2 Large Bananas
1 Tub Whipped Topping
Chocolate Jimmies or Other Desired Crushed Candy Topping (Optional)

Directions:
Prepare Instant Pudding With Only 1 Cup of Milk and Allow to Set for 5 Minutes.
Fold Half of the Whipped Topping Into Prepared Pudding.
Crush Up 5 Graham Crackers and Layer on the Bottom of an 8x8 Pan. (NOTE: Graham Crackers Do Not Have to Be Finely Crushed, Just Broken Into Very Small Pieces).
Slice 1 Banana and Layer Banana Slices on Top of Graham Crackers.
Layer One Half of the Pudding/Whipped Topping Mixture on Top of Banana Layer.
Crush Remaining 5 Graham Crackers and Layer on Top of the Pudding/Whipped Topping.
Slice Second Banana and Layer Banana Slices on Top of Graham Crackers.
Layer Remaining Half of the Pudding/Whipped Topping Mixture on Top of Banana Layer.
OPTIONAL: Sprinkle Chocolate Jimmies or Other Desired Crushed Candy Topping.
Refrigerate for 4 Hours.
Serve Cold.

Submitter: 
MICHMEIG
Description: 
I Came Up With This Recipe When Looking to Make Something That Was Similar to a Layered Pudding Parfait.  I Also Think it Would Work Well With Cherry Pie Filling Instead of Bananas.
