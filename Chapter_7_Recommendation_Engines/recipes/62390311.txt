Title:
Mock Bananas Foster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine
2/3 cup dark brown sugar
3 1/2 tablespoons rum flavored extract
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3 bananas, peeled and sliced lengthwise and crosswise
1/4 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
1 cup of low fat strawberry yogurt

Directions:
In a large, deep skillet over medium heat, melt margarine.  Stir in brown sugar, rum extract, vanilla and cinnamon.  When mixture begins to bubble, place bananas and walnuts in pan.  Cook until bananas are hot, 1 to 2 minutes.  Serve at once with strawberry yogurt.

Submitter: 
Crystal Halm
Description: 
Bananas are cooked in a bubbling pan of dark brown sugar, margarine, rum extract, walnuts and cinnamon and served with strawberry yogurt.Modified from: Bananas Foster II
