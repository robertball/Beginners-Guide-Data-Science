Title:
Savory Gorgonzola Bacon Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
3 tablespoons granulated sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 cup Gorgonzola cheese
4-5 slices of bacon, fried & crumbled
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1 egg, beaten
1 tablespoon fresh chopped parsley
1 cup whole milk
1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
Grease an 8 cup muffin pan and set aside.
In a medium bowl blend flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, cheese, bacon and parsley.
In a separate bowl combine egg, milk, and oil. Stir into dry ingredients.
Blend just until moist.
Fill muffin cups 3/4 full and bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown.
These are best served warm.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
These simple, delicious, flavor packed muffins are prepared in no time! I like to serve these with eggs especially, or apart of a brunch buffet. These are also very tasty along side tomato soup. Enjoy!
