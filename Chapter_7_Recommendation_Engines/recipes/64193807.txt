Title:
Andrea's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds mild Italian sausage
1/2 cup minced onion
1 medium onion, sliced
4 Roma Tomatoes sliced
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
1/2 cup water
1 Jar mariana sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (or 1/2 cup dried)
12 lasagna noodles
16 ounces ricotta cheese
16 ounces cottage cheese
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
6 cups mozzarella cheese shredded
2 cups five cheese Italian blend shredded cheese
1/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
1. Brown sausage, onion, and garlic over medium heat. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and water. Season with salt and pepper, and simmer, while the noodles cook approximately 15 minutes.
2. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
3. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, cottage cheese, the shredded five italian cheese blend, the egg, parsley, basil, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
4. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
5. To assemble, spread a thin layer of olive oil on the bottom of a deep 9x13 inch baking dish.First layer noodles: add meat mixture sprinkled with shredded mozzarella. Second layer noodles: and add marinara sauce with 2 cups shredded mozzarella. Third layer noodles: spread ricotta cheese mixture. Fourth layer noodles: add marinara sauce and 2 cups mozzarella. Top with sliced roman tomatoes and sliced onion and sliced mozzarella cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.

Andrea Luhman
