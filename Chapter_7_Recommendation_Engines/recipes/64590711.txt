Title:
Lemony Iced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3782252.jpg
Ingredients:
15 cups of water
6 black tea bags
2 cups of sugar
1 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice

Directions:
To make the tea part, put two cups of water in a saucepan, add tea bags. Bring the pan up to a boil, remove from heat and let the tea steep for 10 minutes.
Once cooled some, add tea to a gallon pitcher with the two cups of sugar. Mix until sugar is dissolved.
Add in the lemon juice and remaining 13 cups of water. Stir and chill.

Submitter: 
Jessie D.
Description: 
This is a combination of my two favorite summertime drinks. Makes a great mixer for spirits too!
