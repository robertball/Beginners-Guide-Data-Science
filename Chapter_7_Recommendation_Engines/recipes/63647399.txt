Title:
Pie for Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1013831.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Eggs
1 ¾ cup Milk
1 cup Cottage Cheese
1 TBS Butter
4 TBS Flour
½ cup Sugar
1 tsp Vanilla
½ tsp Salt
½ tsp Nutmeg
2 cups Crisped Rice Cereal

Directions:
Beat eggs.  Add other ingredients and mix well.
Pour into greased Glass Pie Plate.
Bake at 325 degrees for 70 minutes.
Pour ¼ cup Pancake Syrup over baked pie.
Refrigerate several hours or overnight.
Serve cold.

Submitter: 
Sloan4
Description: 
“Who wants Pie for Breakfast?”  This recipe, made the night before, is a simple to make, and simple to serve dish enjoyed the next morning.
