Title:
Eggplant Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6963523.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups eggplant, cooked and mashed
1 sleeve of saltine crackers, crushed
1 small onion, chopped
5 tbsp butter
3/4 cup milk
1 1/2 tsp salt
3/4 tsp pepper
2 eggs, separated

Directions:
In a medium skillet, sauté onion in butter until softened.
In a large bowl, mix the cooked and mashed eggplant with the cracker crumbs, cooked onion and butter, milk, salt, pepper and egg yolks.
In a small bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gently fold egg whites into the eggplant mixture.
Turn into a well buttered 2 quart casserole dish and bake at 300 degrees for one hour.
