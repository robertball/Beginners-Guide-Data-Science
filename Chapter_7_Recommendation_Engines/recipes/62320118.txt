Title:
Reva's Sour Cream Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 cups cooked, diced chicken breast
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup onions, minced
1-2 cans chopped green chilies, drained
1/2 cup cooking oil
12 flour tortillas
1 1/2 cups shredded Monterey Jack or cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a saucepan, heat soup, sour cream, onion, and chilies for about 5 minutes. Add chopped chicken pieces to mixture.
Heat oil in a skillet, soften tortillas in oil and drain on paper towels.
Top each softened tortilla with about 2 tablespoons of chicken and soup mixture; roll up. Place in greased 9x13 baking dish, seam side down.
Pour remaing mixture over rolled enchiladas and top with shredded cheese. Bake 30 minutes.
