Title:
Deluxe Pizza Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb. ground beef
1lb. sweet Italian sausage
2 (15 oz.) cans kidney beans, rinsed and drained
2 (15 oz.) cans Italian stewed tomatoes
1 (15 oz.) can tomato sauce
2 (15 oz.) jars of pizza sauce
3 cups of water
1 (7 oz.) stick of pepperoni, cut into 1/8" slices on the bias
1 cup green pepper, chopped
1 cup onion, chopped
1 (4 oz.) can sliced black olives, drained (optional)
1/2 cup sliced mushrooms (optional)
shredded mozzarella (optional)
parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
In an 8 quart stockpot, brown ground beef on medium until no longer pink, drain and set aside.  In the same stockpot, crumble the Italian sausage and cook on medium until lightly browned, drain and add Italian sausage and browned ground beef back into stockpot.  Add drained kidney beans, Italian Stewed tomatoes, tomato sauce, pizza sauce, water, sliced pepperoni, green pepper and onion.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 20 minutes.  After removing from heat add the drained, sliced black olives (optional) and sliced mushrooms, (optional).  Allow chili to sit 5 minutes before serving.  Serve with shredded mozzarella (optional) and parmesan cheese (optional).

Description: 
Your favorite pizza toppings in warm bowl of chili.  Serve with a tossed salad and garlic bread.
