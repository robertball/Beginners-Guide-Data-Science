Title:
Mint Nanaimo Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Base
1 cup mint chocolate chips
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup icing sugar
1 egg
1 1/2 cup Graham cracker crumbs
Filling
1/4 cup butter
2 cups icing sugar
2 T milk
Green food colouring
Topping
1 cup mint chocolate chips
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Base
Melt chips and butter over hot water
add icing sugar, egg and beat well
Stir in wafer crumbs
Press into 8 x 8 and let chill in fridge for about an hour
Filling
Cream butter, icing sugar, milk until smooth and fluffy
Add drops of green food colouring until desired colour is reached
Spread over chilled base
Chill again for a half hour
Topping
Melt chips and butter over hot water
spread evenly over filling and base
