Title:
Maple Candy Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978803.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
2 tablespoons maple extract
1 1/2 cups chopped nuts
1 (2 pound) package confectioners' sugar
24 (1 ounce) squares semi-sweet chocolate, chopped

Directions:
In large bowl, mix together EAGLE BRAND(R), butter, maple extract and nuts. Gradually beat in confectioners' sugar.*
Roll into 1-inch balls. Keep refrigerated until ready to dip.
In heavy saucepan or double broiler, over low heat, melt chocolate. Dip balls into chocolate. Place on wax paper lined baking sheet until set. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Set out these scrumptious nut, maple, and chocolate candies at your next holiday party.
