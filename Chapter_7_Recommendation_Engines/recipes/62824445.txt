Title:
Blueberry Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/844748.jpg
Ingredients:
Pastry for one crust pie
4 cups fresh blueberries
1/4 cup flour
2/3 cups sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 cup sour half and half

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Line pie tin with crust.
Place blueberries in crust.
Mix remaining ingredients and pour over berries.
Bake 35 to 45 minutes until crust is nicely brown and filling is set.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Joy Mahoney
Description: 
An old family favorite!  Best with wild blueberries, but store bought taste great too.
