Title:
New Orleans Corn Bisque with Smoked Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package Hillshire FarmÂ® Smoked Sausage
1 medium onion, chopped
3 cloves minced garlic
1 stalk celery, chopped
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
Cajun seasoning to taste
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 bag frozen corn
4 cups milk
2 cups cubed potatoes, cooked, drained (3 medium potatoes)

Directions:
Cut sausage into 1/2 inch cubes (slice then quarter).
Melt butter in large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, celery, and sausage; cook and stir until tender. Add flour, salt and pepper; cook, stirring constantly for 1 minute.
Add milk. Increase heat to medium-high and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute. Add corn and return to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer 10 minutes.  Stir in potatoes; heat through.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
This creamy corn bisque with smoked sausage is a quick way to warm up on a chilly week night.Modified from: New Orleans Corn Bisque with Smoked Sausage
