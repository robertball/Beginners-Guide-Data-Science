Title:
Christmas Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1060873.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) container powdered non-dairy creamer
1 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup instant decaffeinated coffee granules
1/2 cup granulated sugar substitute (such as SplendaÂ®)
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 pinch salt

Directions:
Mix creamer, brown sugar, decaffeinated coffee, sugar substitute, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl until thoroughly combined. Store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
ggilbert
Description: 
I have used this recipe for years for teachers' gifts, gifts to share with friends, and to have on hand at my own home. It is a favorite of everyone who has ever had it. So easy to make. To enjoy, place 3 to 4 teaspoons of Christmas Coffee in a coffee cup, add hot water, and mix.
