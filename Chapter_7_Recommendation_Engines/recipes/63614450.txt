Title:
Granola-Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1/4  cup succanat sugar
1/4 cup agave
8 figs
3 flax eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup spelt  flour
1/2 cup amaranth flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 3/4 cups granola
1 cup raisins
1/2 cup chopped, unsalted dry-roasted peanuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.
Blend together Olive Oil, Succanat, agave, figs, flax eggs, vanilla.  Combine the flour, salt and baking soda, stir into the creamed mixture. Finally, stir in the granola, raisins and peanuts. Drop dough by heaping teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheets, about 2 inches apart.
Bake 12 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven,  until cookies are lightly browned around edges. Remove from baking sheets to cool on wire racks.
