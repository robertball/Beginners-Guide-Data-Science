Title:
Grecian Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups DannonÂ® Plain Yogurt
1 cup diced plum tomatoes
1/2 cup minced purple onion
1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano
1/4 teaspoon cracked black pepper

Directions:
Drain yogurt 25 to 30 minutes. Add all other ingredients and mix well. Serve as desired.

Submitter: 
DANNON
Description: 
Yogurt mixed with the bold Greek flavors of tomato, Kalamata olives, oregano, and feta cheese make a delicious creamy spread, great for a sandwich or a dip for pita bread.
