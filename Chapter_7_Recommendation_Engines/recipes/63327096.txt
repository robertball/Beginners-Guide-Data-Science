Title:
Plum Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 red or black Plums
1 tbls olive oil
1 large shallot, sliced lengthwise
1/2 C packed light brown sugar.
1/4 C apple cider vinegar
1 tbls chopped garlic
1 tbls mustard seeds
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Halve and pit the plums.
Cut into 1/4 inch wedges.
Heat the oil over medium heat.
Add the shallot and cook until soft. 2 - 3 minutes.
Add the brown sugar and stir to coat all the pieces of shallot.
Add the apple cider vinegar, garlic mustard seeds, bay leaf, pepper and salt, plus 1/4 C of water.
Cook until fragrant, about 3 minutes.
Stir in plums and bring to a simmer, Cover.
Cook 7-8 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Uncover and let cook another 10-15 minutes - until fruit is soft and juices have thickened.
Season to taste with salt and more pepper if needed.
Let cool. Store refrigerated in a glass jar.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
This Plum Chutney is adapted from Bon Appetit Slather a little of this over some turkey or pork in your sandwich, or pair it with roasted pork tenderloin for dinner.
