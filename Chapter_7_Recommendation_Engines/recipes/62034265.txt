Title:
Beef and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs. ground beef
1 tbsp. butter or margarine
1 large onion, chopped
1 cup green pepper, chopped
1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 pkg (10oz) wide noodles
2 cans (10-3/4 oz each)tomato soup
1 can (10-3/4 oz each) cream of mushroom soup
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cook noodles according to pkg directions.  Drain and set aside.  Brown beef in a large skillet.  Drain beef and set aside.  In the same skillet, melt butter over medium high heat.  Saute onion and pepper until tender.  Add beef, Worcester sauce, noodles and soups; mix well.  Spoon into greased 3 quart casserole; top with cheese.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Diane Blanchard Angell
Description: 
ground beef and noodles in a creamy mixture of soups sprinkled with cheese!
