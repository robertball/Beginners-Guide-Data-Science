Title:
Kahlua Orange Vinaigrette Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/844056.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
2 tablespoons orange honey
1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon tarragon, finely crushed
2 cups light salad oil
2/3 cup Kahlua
1 cup fresh orange juice

Directions:
Blend vinegar, lemon juice and seasonings with salad oil.  Add Kahlua and orange juice, and beat well until blended.

Submitter: 
Kahlua
Description: 
This light and tangy dressing goes well with seafood and avocado salad, or mixed green salad bowls.
