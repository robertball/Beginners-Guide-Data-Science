Title:
Quick Chicken Piccata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
Cayenne Pepper, to taste
Salt & Pepper to taste
Flour, for dredging
5 T Olive Oil
3 T Capers, drained
1/2 C Chicken Stock/Broth or White Wine
1/4 C Lemon Juice
6 T Cold Unsalted Butter
1/3 C Fresh Parsley, chopped

Directions:
If the chicken breasts are large, pound them down to about 1/2" thickness between two sheets of plastic wrap.
Season generously with Cayenne Pepper and Salt n Pepper. Dredge in flour.
Use a heavy skillet on medium high heat, heat the oil. Shake off the extra flour of chicken and place in pan. Turn heat down to medium and cook for 4-5 min per side.
Remove chicken from pan and set aside. Leave heat at medium. Add Capers and smash half of them with a fork to release more flavor and cook for about 30 secs then add white wine to deglaze the pan. Boil for ~2 min until it reduces by half. Add lemon juice,splash of water and cold butter cut in chunks.
Turn heat down to low and keep the sauce moving until butter is melted. Add Parsley and chicken just to reheat.
Serve over pasta (Capellini) or serve with a side. Spoon over the Piccata sauce.
