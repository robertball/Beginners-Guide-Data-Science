Title:
lemon yogurt sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c plain, whole milk yogurt
1/2 t lemon zest
1 t fresh lemon juice
1/4 t salt
pinch pepper
1 T fresh chives
1 1/2 t fresh dill, chopped

Directions:
In a medium bowl stir together all ingredients until well combined.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
