Title:
Lynn's pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package ranch dry dressing
1 bottle orville redenbacher popcorn oil
3 bags Snyders pretzels

Directions:
In a very large bowl mix the ranch dressing packet and the whole bottle of oil.
mix in all the pretzels.
Every 10 minutes for the next hour stir or shake the bowl if you have a tight fitting lid.
Then freeze and best if they are stored in the freezer until serving.
