Title:
Jalapeno Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/809266.jpg
Ingredients:
10 Jalapeno Peppers
1 Lime
1 clove of garlic
1 tsp. of Jalapeno powder
1/2 tsp of garlic powder
Fresh cilantro (10 sprigs)
1 Tablespoon of Extra virgin Olive oil.

Directions:
De- Seed Jalpenos (unless you really want it hot leave them in)
Squeeze out all of the lime juice out of the lime.
Combine all ingridients except lime juice and mix in a food processor or blender until completely combined.
Add in all lime juice slowly, while mixing.
Chill and save or serve at room temperature right away!
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
kristinamarie
Description: 
A beautiful green sauce that can be used on tacos or on practically anything if you love peppers like I do!   Spice it up!
