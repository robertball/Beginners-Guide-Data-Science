Title:
Campbell's速 Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  Campbell's速 Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 cup milk
2 teaspoons worchester sauce
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
8 cups cooked cut green beans ( 2 lb frozen)
2 2/3 cups French's速 French Fried Onions
Frank's hot Sauce
creole seasoning

Directions:
Stir soup, milk, worchester sauce, pepper, beans, 1 1/3 cups onions, and a splash of hot sauce in 3-qt. casserole.
Bake at 350 degrees F. for 25 min. or until hot. Stir.
Top with remaining onions and sprinkle with creole seasoning. Bake for 5 min. more.
