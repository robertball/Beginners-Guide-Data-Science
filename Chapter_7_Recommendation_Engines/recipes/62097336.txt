Title:
Donna's Venison Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lb venison meat cubed for stew
1 cup flour
1tsp salt
1tsp black pepper
4 Tbsp oil
two yellow onions cut in chunks
1 bunch celery in chunks (include the tops
1 lb carrots peeled and cut in chunks
5 medium potatoes peeled and chunked
2-3 Tbsp worchestor sauce
1-2 good squirts of yellow hotdog mustard
4-6 bayleaves
2 cubes beef boullion

Directions:
Toss venison with flour and salt pepper mix.  Brown in oil in large stew/stockpot.
Add onions and celery to brown.
Add potatoes and carrots.
Completely cover with water.
Add Worchestor, mustard, and bayleaves, and boullion-stir.
Add additional salt pepper to taste if desired.
Cook on low approx 6 hours stirring occasionlly.   thickness of stew depends on amount of potatoes added.

Submitter: 
rosburn
Description: 
This is my family's favorite venison stew recipee (also works well with beef), given to me by by Mother-in-Law.  Enjoy!
