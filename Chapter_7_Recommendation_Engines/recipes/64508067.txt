Title:
Flounder with a Lemon Hazelnut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup unseasoned breadcrumbs
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 pound sole or flounder fillets
1 egg, beaten
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp butter
1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
2 tbsp roasted hazelnuts, chopped
1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
In a shallow bowl, mix the breadcrumbs, thyme, and garlic powder.
Dip the fish first into the egg, then into the breadcrumb mixture, coating both sides evenly.
In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat and add fish, cooking until both sides are a golden brown.
Remove fish from pan and place on serving dish, keep warm.
Add the butter to the pan and melt.
Add in the lemon juice, red pepper, hazelnuts, and parsley. Cook until hot and spoon over fish, serve.
