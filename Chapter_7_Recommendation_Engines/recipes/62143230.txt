Title:
Sausage Oyster Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg. Jimmy Dean sausage (one hot, one regular)
16 oz. plain croutons
1 pint of oysters, drained reserving liquor
Reserved liquor plus enough chicken broth to make 2 cups, heated
2 cups celery
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 cup chopped onion

Directions:
Brown sausage, drain. Crumble with fork. Chop oysters. Combine with remaining ingredients, mixing well. Pour into a greased 9" x 13" pan and bake covered at 325 for 30 min.
