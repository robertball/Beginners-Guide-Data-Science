Title:
Chicken Pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Stuffing:
1 large can cooked chicken
1/2 cup stuffing mix (dry)
2 oz cream cheese
1 t milk
1/2 T sour cream
salt and pepper to taste
Sauce:
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 can milk
3/4 cup shredded colby cheese

Directions:
Mix together stuffing ing. in a bowl.  Take 1 container pre pakaged crescent roll dough, separate into triangles, press out and fill with 1 scoop of stuffing.  Press to seal, and put into a 9x13 baking dish.  Mix sauce ing together and pour over pockets.  Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes covered, uncover for last 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
TINA B
Description: 
This casserole was brought to me after having my second baby, and it was so tasty!  I had to get the recipe right after we gobbled it up!  Tasty chicken, stuffing, and cheese tucked into crescent roll dough, topped with a sauce and baked, delicious!
