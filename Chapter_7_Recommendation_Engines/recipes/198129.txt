Title:
Katie Lee's Spiced Nuts 'n Chex® Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989544.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sugar
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper (cayenne)
1/4 cup butter or margarine
2 cups Corn Chex® cereal
2 cups Rice Chex® cereal
2 cups Wheat Chex® cereal
1 (11.5 ounce) can mixed nuts

Directions:
In small bowl, mix sugar, chili powder and red pepper; set aside.
In large microwavable bowl, microwave butter uncovered on High about 40 seconds or until melted. Stir in cereal and nuts until evenly coated. Stir in sugar mixture until evenly coated.
Microwave uncovered on High 5 to 6 minutes, stirring after 2 minutes, until mixture just begins to turn brown. Spread on waxed paper to cool. Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
Chex® Cereal
Description: 
Chili powder and red pepper spice up this party-perfect mix--it will disappear quickly!
