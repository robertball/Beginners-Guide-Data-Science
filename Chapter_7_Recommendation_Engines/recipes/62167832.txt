Title:
Apple Of Your Eye Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 cup graham cracker crumbs
3 tablespoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 cup butter, melted
2 tablespoons pecans, finely chopped
Filling:
3 (8 oz.) pkgs. cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup sugar
3 eggs
3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
Topping:
1 1/2 cups apples (granny smith), chopped and peeled
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
6 tablespoons caramel ice cream topping
2 tablespoons chopped pecans or peanuts

Directions:
Crust: Combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, cinnamon, melted butter, and chopped pecans. Press in lightly greased springform pan.
Bake for 10 minutes.
Filling: Mix together, cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract with blender until smooth. Pour into springform pan.
Topping: Mix together, apples, lemon juice, sugar, cinnamon. Pour over filling. Bake at 350 degrees F. for 50 minutes or until set.
Drizzle caramel and chopped nuts over top. Let cool. Place in fridge for at least 4 hours.
