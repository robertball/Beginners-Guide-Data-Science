Title:
Creamed Cornbread with Jiffy Mix - Simple and Delicious!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2192823.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Boxes of Jiffy Mix
2 Large Eggs
1 Can of creamed Corn (about 15 oz)
1 Can of Yellow or white Corn (about 15 oz)
2/3 Cup Sour Cream
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Set oven to 320. Combine all ingredients and mix with a fork until you reach an even consistency (2 mins or less). Try not to over mix. Pour mix into a buttered 9x9 glass dish. Cook at 350 for 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Mandycu2172
Description: 
This cornbread is incredibly simple to make but the results are moist and delicious! I always get tons of compliments on this dish that my grandma has made since I was a kid. Serve hot out of the oven with butter for the best results.
