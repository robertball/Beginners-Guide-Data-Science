Title:
Creamy Chicken & Broccoli Pasta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2215233.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 c rotini pasta
1 small bunch broccoli, chopped (about 3 cups)
1 lb boneless chicken breast, cut into bite size pieces
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 c chicken broth
3-4 oz Neufchâtel cheese, cubed
1 c shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
Seasoning (Alpine Touch, garlic powder, Lawrys seasoning, parsley flakes)

Directions:
Heat oven to 375.
Cook pasta in large pan according to package directions, add broccoli for about the last 4 minutes.
Meanwhile, heat olive oil in large skillet on medium high heat.  Add chicken, cook about 3 minutes until nearly cooked. Season chicken to taste. Add broth, simmer 3 minutes until done. Reduce heat to low.  Add Neufchâtel cheese and 1/2 cup mozzarella,  and cook and stir for 1-2 minutes until melted.
Drain pasta and broccoli, add to chicken mixture and mix well.
Bake, covered, 15 minutes or until heated through. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Bake uncovered 3-5 minutes, or until melted. Let stand 5 minutes.

Healthier and lighter version of a chicken Alfredo style pasta.
