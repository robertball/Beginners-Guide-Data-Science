Title:
Salmon Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 -1 lb Can of Salmon
1 Tablespoon Lemon Juice
1 Tea. Onion Flakes
2 Tea. Horseradish
1/2 Tea. Liquid Smoke
1 dash Hot Pepper
1/2 Tea. Salt
1 8-ounce package Cream Cheese
3/4 Cups - Finely Chopped Pecans

Directions:
Pour juice off salmon remove bones and skin.
Flake up salmon into a large bowl.
Add lemon juice, horseradish, liquid smoke, hot pepper and salt.
Mix throughly and then add cream cheese and mix throught again.
Chill several hours or overnight in fridge. - I usually put mine into a bowl to help keep a nice shape.
The next morning - or several hours later; remove from bowl and roll in the chopped pecans.
Chil again for several hours
I serve with a mild cracker so as not to take away the flavors of the salmon - like Socialbles
Enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
Debbie Cash
Description: 
This is such a great appitizer.  Its simple to make and loved by everyone where ever I take it.  People beg me to bring this to gatherings.  I even made 33 one night for a wedding and shaped into hearts.  They were beautiful.
