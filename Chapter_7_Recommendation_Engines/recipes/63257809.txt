Title:
Meatloaf they come home for Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-3lbs ground beef
-1 med. Yellow onion
-2 Large eggs
-1 Cup italian style bread crumbs
-1 Tsp each, garlic powder, oregano, thyme and parsley
-2 Tbsp each worstershire, steak sauce & ketchup
-1 Tbsp of hickory liquid smoke
SAUCE TOPPING:
-1 Cup Ketchup
-1/4 Cup brown sugar
-1 Tsp coarse ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl combine beef with the rest of the meatloaf ingredients. Do not over mix.
Transfer mixture to a 9 by 13 baking pan & shape meat into a loaf. Using a pastry brush, brush on the sauce. Place in the oven at 350 degrees for about 50 minutes or until your meatloaf reaches 160 degrees internal temperature. Hope you enjoy!
