Title:
Spicy Sausage Egg Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups seasoned croutons
2 Tbsp. butter, melted
1 pound Jimmy Dean ground Hot sausage
2 cups shredded pepperjack cheese
6 eggs
1 3/4 cup milk
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 inch pan with cooking spray.
Spread croutons out on the bottom of the pan and drizzle with melted butter.
Brown gound sausage on stovetop over medium heat. Drain and let cool for a few minutes. Then sprinkle sausage over crouton layer.
Cover sausage layer with shredded pepperjack cheese.
In a medium mixing bowl, beat eggs, milk, mustard, salt and pepper with a whisk. Pour over croutons, sausage and cheese.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.
In the morning, preheat oven to 325 degrees. Bake for 50 minutes. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
HARTMR
Description: 
This is an egg bake with a little spiciness . . . sausage, egg and pepperjack cheese. Prepare the night before and bake it off in the morning.
