Title:
Chocolate Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Large Hershey's Chocolate Bakr
Ghiradelli 72% Cacao Intense Dark
1/2 cup Whipping Cream
2-3 Tbsp butter

Directions:
Melt chocolates in microwave, stirring every 30 seconds. Melt butter with whipping cream in microwave or on stove-top. Mix together.
