Title:
Lemony Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 sm boneless, skinless chicken breasts
salt
pepper
flour
2T butter
3 T olive oil
3/4 C chicken broth
8 lemon slices, thin
1 T lemon juice
2 T soft butter
1/2 lb mushrooms, sliced

Directions:
Season chicken with salt and pepper , then dip in flour and shake off excess. In a large skillet, melt 2 T butter with the 3 T olive oil over moderate heat. Add chicken and saute about 3 minutes on wach side, or until they are golden brown. Transfer to a plate. Add 1/2 cup chicken stalk and boil 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly and scraping and browned bits clinging to the bottom and sides of the pan. Add mushrooms and saute for 1-2 minutes. Return chicken to skillet and simmer over low heat 10-15 minutes, or until chicken is cooked thru.
Add 1/4 cup of remaining chicken stock to the juices in the skillet and boil briskly until the stock is reduced to a syrupy glaze. Add lemon juice and cook, stirring for o1 minutes. Remove the pan fromt he heat, add 2 T butter and pour sauce over chicken.
