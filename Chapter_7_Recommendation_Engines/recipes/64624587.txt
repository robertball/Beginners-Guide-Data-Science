Title:
Smothered green beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4462417.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pounds of fresh green beans (or four cans)
1/2 cup onions, minced
1 teaspoon of garlic salt
2 pounds of fresh bean, trimmed
1/2 teaspoon of crushed red pepper
16 OZ of tomato paste
2 teaspoons of dried cilantro
1 cup of water
2 teaspoons of lemon juice
1/2 of cumin
2 teaspoons of ginger powder
3 teaspoons of coconut oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. In a mixing bowl, mix tomato paste, onions, garlic salt, crushed peppers, dried cilantro, 1 cup of water, lemon juice, cumin, ginger powder,  and coconut oil.  Mix ingredients together until blended.  Place freshly cleaned green beans in baking pan.  Pour the tomato mix over the green beans.  Cover the baking pan with foil, place covered green beans in oven until the green beans are tender.   let cool and serve.
