Title:
Homemade Orange Soda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz can pulp free orange juice concentrate, thawed
1 2 liter bottle lemon/lime soda, chilled
1 orange, thinly sliced

Directions:
Note: this beverage is best when mixed just prior to serving
Combine orange juice concentrate and lemon lime soda.
Pour over ice in individual glasses and garnish with orange slices.
