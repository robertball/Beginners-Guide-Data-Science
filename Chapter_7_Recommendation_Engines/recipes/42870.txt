Title:
Spinach Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained
1/3 cup seasoned bread crumbs
3 tablespoons crushed seasoned stuffing
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream
2 teaspoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first five ingredients; mix well. Combine the mayonnaise, sour cream and lemon juice; add to tuna mixture and mix well. Transfer to a greased 2-cup baking dish. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until heated through.
