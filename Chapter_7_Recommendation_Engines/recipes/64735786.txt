Title:
Dandelion Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6562952.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pound Dandelion Leaves
1/2 C Olive Oil
1/8 C Balsamic Vinegar
1/2 t Garlic Powder
1/2 t salt
1/4 t peper

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit
Line baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside
In a bowl mix together the oil, vinegar, garlic powder and salt.
Toss leaves into this mixture
Lay the leaves in a single layer on the baking sheets
Drizzle the remaining oil mixture over the leaves
Bake for 12-16 minutes, watching closely to ensure that the dandelion chips do not burn
Leaves will harden as they cool.

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Forget Kale Chips!  Dandelion chips are plentiful, crispy and yummy!
