Title:
Brazilian Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
2 Tbsp minced garlic
1 Tsp olive oil or canola oil
2 cups long grain white rice
4 cups water
1 Tbsp tomato paste
2 tsps salt
1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
In medium saucepan, on medium-low heat, cook onion and garlic in oil until soft and golden. Careful not to brown. Stir in rice and cook until golden. Add tomato paste, stir all together. Add water, cover, heat to boiling, and then cook on low for 20 minutes. Do not stir.

Submitter: 
Claudia Harris
Description: 
My Mom used to make this Brazilian rice with ingredients she found in grocery stores here. Delicious with Brazilian black beans. It's also super easy!
