Title:
Almond Butter And Coconut Brown Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soft butter
1/2 cup almond butter
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup coconut brown sugar
1 egg
1 and 1/4 cup all purpose  flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp.baking soda

Directions:
Heat oven to 375F.
Beat butter, almond butter, both sugars and egg until well creamed. Add flour, baking powder and baking soda and stir until blended. Roll into 1 inch balls and put about 3 inches apart on parchment paper, or ungreased foil, shiny side down. Flatten in a criss- cross fashion with a fork that is dipped in flour. Bake for 10-12 minutes. These cookies spread during baking.

Submitter: 
manella
Description: 
Wanted to try using brown coconut sugar and almond butter in a cookie, so I played around in the kitchen and came up with this cookie recipe. A crispy cookie that would go well with hot chocolate. Or any hot drink.
