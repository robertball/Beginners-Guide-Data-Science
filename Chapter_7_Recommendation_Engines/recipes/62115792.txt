Title:
Skillet Sea Scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup dry bread crumbs
½ tsp. salt
1 pound sea scallops
2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon olive oil
¼ cup dry white wine OR reduced sodium chicken broth
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon minced, fresh parlsey
1 garlic clove, minced.

Directions:
In a resealable plastic bag, combine bread crumbs and salt. Add scallops, a few at a time, and shake to coat.
In a large skillet over med. high heat, brown scallops in butter and oil for 1 ½ to 2 minutes on each side or until firm and opaque. Remove and keep warm.
Add the wine or broth, lemon juice, parsley, and garlic to skillet, bring to a boil. Pour over scallops. Serve immediately. 3-4 servings

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
A fast but gourmet meal for dinner guests, these sea scallops are sure to impress! If using regular chicken broth, omit the salt.
