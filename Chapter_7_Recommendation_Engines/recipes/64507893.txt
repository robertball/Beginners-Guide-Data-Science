Title:
Brad's Beef Bourguignon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2690087.jpg
Ingredients:
1 four pound beef Chuck roast trimmed and cut into 1 inch chunks
3/4 pound of bacon cut into 1 inch chunks
1 lb mushrooms (baby portobello)
3 cups carrots coarsely  chopped
1 lb fingerling potatoes chopped into 1 inch chunks
1 jar of pearl onions drained
1 liter of bold red wine (cab, Merlot or burgundy)
2 large tablespoons of Beef paste
16 oz of beef broth/stock
3 large cloves of garlic pressed
1 bunch of fresh parsley
1 bunch of fresh thyme
5 bay leaves
1 tbs garlic salt
Fresh cracked black pepper
1 can tomato paste
1/4 cup flour
3 tbs Olive oil
Large dumpling Egg noodles

Directions:
1.  Brown bacon, and drain and remove from pan.
2.  Using fat from bacon in same pan, brown the beef in the bacon drippings in small batches, remove and drain.
3.  Using fat from bacon, cook carrots, mushrooms and potatoes for 3-4 minutes
4.  add the beef and bacon back to the pot, and add rest of ingredients to pot (except onions)
5.  Tie parsley and thyme into a bouquet garni for easy removal
6.  Bring to a boil and reduce to simmer.  Cook for 1 hour.
7.  Make a roux with olive oil and flour.  Add to stew until desired thickness
8.  Add onions and simmer for an additional 45 minutes
9.  Remove bouquet garni and bay leaves
10.  Serve stew over egg noodles with garlic toast.

Submitter: 
Brad
Description: 
This hearty stew great for football games and cold fall days!  It also freezes well for future meals.
