Title:
black bean flat bread pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bell pepper (red, yellow, or orange)
1 Tbsp yeast
pinch of sugar
1 cup warm water, divided
3 cups whole wheat flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 can black beans
1/2 red onion
1 tablespoon chives
1 can tomatoes
3 chili peppers, (or jalapeno peppers (for spicier dishes) deseeded and stems removed
Parmesean cheese
1/2 cup parsley
1/2 cup cilantro
1/8 cup chives
4 tablespoons olive oil
juice of 1/2 lemon
2 cloves garlic (minced)

Directions:
preheat broiler
cut pepper in half, remove membranes, seeds, and stem.
place on baking sheet skin side up and press down to flatten.
broil for 10-13 minutes or until skins become black.  Remove from broiler and wrap in foil or plastic wrap immediately to cool.
Once cooled, peel and slice into small slivers
Dissolve yeast, sugar, and 1/2 cup water in large bowl, let proof.
Stir in flour, additional water, and salt until stiff dough forms.
Turn out of bowl and knead for 10 minutes or until dough is soft and elastic.
Coat another large bowl with oil or cooking spray
Add dough, cover and let rise for 1 hour.
Grease 11x14 inch baking sheet (sprinkle with corn meal if desired)
Press dough into baking sheet making sure it has reached all 4 edges and is even throughout.
Add 2 cups of black bean mixture, you may have extra coating dough evenly(this can be stored in refridgerator)
Sprinkle Parmesan Cheese evenly over top of mixture
Bake at 450 degrees for 14 to 16 minutes.
Let cool at least 10 minutes.
Combine Parsley, Cilantro, Chive, Oil, Lemon juice and garlic in small bowl.
Add prasley mixture to top of pizza.
Add Cooled bell pepper over pizza.
Slice pizza and serve.

Submitter: 
A.Davala
Description: 
This hearty pizza has an ethnic flare and packs a lot of flavor.  It can be used as an appetizer or the main course and is good hot or cold.
