Title:
Tortilla Crusted Tilapia with Lime and Cilantro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. tilapia
4 Tbs. butter
1 oz. lime juice
1 tsp. cilantro
1 tsp. kosher or sea salt
broken tortilla chips from the bottom of the bag

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees.
Spray a 9x13 pyrex pan with cooking spray.
Melt butter on large plate in micro.
Add lime juice, cilantro, salt.
Gently stir.
Put tortilla chips in gallon ziploc.
Use rolling pin to crush chips, then pour onto second plate.
Dredge each filet in butter mixture,coat with chips, and arrange in pan.
Bake 15 min., then turn.
(Optional: after turning, spray top of fish with butter cooking spray, sprinkle with extra cilantro and salt, and sprinkle with lime juice).
Bake for 15 more minutes.
Test with fork for doneness.
For a really crunchy top, broil for one minute more.

Submitter: 
KimmerG40
Description: 
Crunchy crust with buttery lime flavor.  The kids said it was the best tilapia they'd had in their life.
