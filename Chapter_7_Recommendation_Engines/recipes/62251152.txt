Title:
Cheesy Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion chopped
1/4 c. butter
1/4 c. flour
1/2 t thyme
1 t salt
1/4 julienned carrots
1/4 c. chopped celery
12oz bag frozen broccoli (or more)
32 oz chicken broth
3 1/2 cups milk
8 oz cheese shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c. cheese powder (optional)
1/2 c. cool water

Directions:
Start out pouring the chicken broth in a 4 qt pot and bring to a boil. When it starts to boil , add broccoli and bring to boil again. Boil for 5 min. Do not discard broth.
In an 8 qt pot melt butter in the bottom of the pot. Add onion, and simmer a minute or so. Add carrots , celery and thyme and simmer 5 more minutes. Add quarter cup flour and stir up . Quickly add about a half cup of the milk and continue stirring. Every time it thickens up add another 1/2 c. to cup of milk. Add salt. Once this is all stirred in begin pouring in the broccoli/chicken broth a bit at a time and stir. Once all the broth is poured in, add the broccoli. Once it thickens up again , add the shredded cheese. While this is melting in the soup. Pour cheese powder in a bowl and mix with 1/2 cup cool water . Once smooth , pour into soup and stir until thickened again. Keep on low simmer if you want it thicker

Description: 
A quicker than you think family pleaser as well as heart warmer on a cold winter day!
