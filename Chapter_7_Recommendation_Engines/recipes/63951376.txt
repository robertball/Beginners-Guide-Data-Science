Title:
French Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
1 cup ketchup
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup vinegar
1 small onion, minced
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place oil, ketchup, sugar, vinegar, onion, lemon juice, paprika, and salt into a bowl; wisk until smooth. Store in refrigerator.
