Title:
Crazy Casey's Long Island Spiced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/670771.jpg
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons white sugar
4 tablespoons water
4 teaspoons fresh lime juice
4 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
1 fluid ounce spiced rum
1 fluid ounce tequila
1 fluid ounce vodka
1 fluid ounce gin
1 fluid ounce triple sec (orange-flavored liqueur)
1 cup ice cubes
2 fluid ounces cola-flavored carbonated beverage
1 lemon wedge

Directions:
Stir together the sugar, water, lime juice, lemon juice, rum, tequila, vodka, gin, and triple sec in a glass until the sugar has dissolved. Add the ice, and pour in the cola; garnish with the lemon wedge to serve.
