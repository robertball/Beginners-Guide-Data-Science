Title:
Broccoli and Cauliflower Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup(s) broccoli & cauliflower, frozen

Directions:
Steam the broccoli and cauliflower over boiling water until it's reached the desired texture.
Alternatively, you may steam it by placing it in a covered, microwave-safe dish with about 1 tablespoon of water and heating to desired texture. Be careful of the steam when removing the cover.
