Title:
Chicken and Pasta Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 chicken breasts - cooked and shredded
1 can of cream of chicken soup
1 cup of sour cream
16 oz. package of pasta
1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/3 cup of Italian bread crumbs
1 tbsp. parsley flakes
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350.
Spray a 13x9 casserole pan with cooking spray.
Cook pasta according to directions, drain and return to pot.
Add chicken, cream of chicken soup, sour cream and parsley to pot and mix well.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Spread mixture into casserole dish then sprinkle evenly with bread crumbs and cheese.
Bake for 5-10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
