Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup of granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1 tsp. vanilla extract
2  1/2 cups of flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Cream together butter, peanut butter and sugars.
Add beaten eggs and vanilla extract.
Sift together 2 and 1/2 cups flour, salt and baking soda. Add to mixture.
Use a small cookie scoop. Then shape into a ball. (Optional -roll in granulated sugar)
Place 8 on a large cookie sheet. Flatten by using a fork dipped in water. Make crisscross design on cookies.
Bake for 10 minutes at 375ยบ or until done.
If dough is sticky, chill in the refrigerator for 30 min. or more.
