Title:
Slow cooker french onion roast and swiss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can French onion soup
1 can cream of onion soup
3 cans beef broth
1 beef roast 3-5 pounds your preference
Pepper to taste
1 pkg Swiss cheese
1 pkg hoagie buns

Directions:
Place roast in slow cooker.  Pour French onion soup beef broth cream of onion and pepper in slow cooker. Cook on low setting for 7-9 hours or until roast is tender. Pull apart roast into shreds with fork. Place roast back In slow cooker on warm setting. Toast hoagie buns on broiler setting for 6 minutes or to desired crispness. Place swiss cheese and shredded roast on toasted hoagies. Serve juice on side for dipping or place on hoagies.
