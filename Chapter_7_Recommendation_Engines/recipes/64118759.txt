Title:
Easy Corn Puff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10 oz. packages frozen corn
1 C. Bisquick
1 C. milk
2 eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1 C. shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325. Butter 5 cup souffle dish or 1 1/2 qt. round casserole dish. Cook corn as directed, drain. Beat baking mix, milk, eggs and salt by hand until smooth. Stir in corn and cheese. Pour into souffle dish. Bake until knife inserted halfway between center and edges comes out clean, about 1 hour.
