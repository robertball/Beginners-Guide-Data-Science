Title:
Kate's Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 T. butter
1 red cabbage, shredded or cut into small pieces
4 T. real maple syrup

Directions:
Melt the butter in a large saucepan or pot.
Add the cabbage, stir, and cover.
Cook over low or medium heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent burning.
Stir in the maple syrup and continue heating for an additional 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jen Cardenas
Description: 
This is a slightly sweet cabbage side dish that my sister gave me the recipe for.  Cooked this way, the cabbage has a very subtle flavor that even kids will enjoy.
