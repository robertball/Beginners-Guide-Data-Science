Title:
Easy Spinach Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 butter or margarine
1/2 cup chopped scallions
1/2 cup chopped carrots
1 small garlic clove, finely chopped
6 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup small pasta or rice
One bag (10oz) fresh spinach, chopped

Directions:
In large saucepan melt butter or margarine, add scallions, carrots and garlic, saute until scallions are soft.  Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.  Stir in pasta or rice, simmer 5-10 minutes.  Stir in spinach.  Cook for 2-3 minutes until spinach is done.  Add salt & pepper to taste.  Top with parmesean cheese, if desired.
