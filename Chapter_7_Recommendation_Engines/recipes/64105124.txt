Title:
Orange Dream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz. orange soda
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (8 oz) tub Cool Whip, thawed

Directions:
Place soda, condensed milk, and whipped topping,into an ice cream maker; stir until combined.  Freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions.
Others said not to use the extra sugar. Instead of using an ice cream maker; could just use a blender or food processor.

Submitter: 
Colleen Robertson Curtiss
Description: 
This frozen dessert  - tastes like those orange push ups you remember from childhood.Modified from: Orange Dream
