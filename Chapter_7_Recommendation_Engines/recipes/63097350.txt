Title:
Tony Horton's Mother's Pancake recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of fat free cottage cheese
6 eggs
1/2 cup of whole wheat flour
1/4 cup canola oil
Pinch of salt
Dash of vanilla extract
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Blend or food-process the first six ingredients on high until smooth. Add milk slowly to reach batter consistency. Cook on a hot, nonstick griddle. Number of pancakes varies by size.

Submitter: 
Irene
Description: 
I got this from a P90X news letter.  I love pancakes and am always looking for a healthier and lighter alternative that taste like the real thing.  These are the closest I have found and they are low carb.  I don't put in the milk because I think they make them too runny.
