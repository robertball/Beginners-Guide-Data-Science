Title:
Smokey Red Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 oz) can light red kidney beans
1 teaspoon liquid smoke
1/2 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
5 tablespoon unsalted butter
1 cup day old cooked rice
1/2 cup chicken stock

Directions:
Throw in the first 5 ingredients in a pot, medium heat.
Semi-mash the beans, stir to blend then add the rest. Stir often. Add more broth if needed.
Cook for 20-30 minutes until soupy thick consistency. Add more butter and liquid smoke for your desired taste.
