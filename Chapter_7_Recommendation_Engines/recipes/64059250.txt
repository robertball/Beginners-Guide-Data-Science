Title:
Easy Cheese and Ham Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
1 small onion, chopped
6 T. butter
1/4 C. flour
4 1/2 C. milk
Diced Ham
Frozen Peas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place the potatoes, onion, ham, and peas in 9x13 pan sprayed with Pam.
Made a roux, melting butter, and add flour.  Stir for a minute to take away flour taste.
Add the milk or cream mixture and stir until thick.
Pour over potatoes and ham.
Cover and bake for 1-1 1/2 hours until potatoes are done.
Bake in the preheated oven until the potatoes are tender, about 1 hour.
