Title:
Easy Stuffed Mannicoti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (8 oz) mannicoti, uncooked
1 3/4 cups (425ml) ricotta cheese
3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
1/4 cup grated fresh parmesan cheese
1 egg, beaten
2 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
1 Tbsp oregano
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1 tsp crushed garlic
1 cup water
1 lb ground italian sausage
1 jar (30 oz) spaghetti sauce

Directions:
Brown and drain sausage
Add spaghetti sauce and 1 cup of water, Mix well
Heat to boil, then reduce to a simmer and cover
Mix ricotta, 2 cups mozzarella, and parmesan cheese
Add egg and spices to cheese mixture, mix well.
Fill uncooked manicotti with cheese mixture
Put a small amount of sauce and sausage mixture in bottom of 13x9 inch pan.
Place filled manicotti in pan
Pour remaining sauce over manicotti
Top with remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese
Top with foil
Bake 350 degrees for 1 hour and 15 minutes.  Take off foil for last 15 minutes of cooking.  Let sit for 15 minutes before serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
My husband loves this manicotti receipe. It's easy to make, always tasty, and the best thing... you don't have to cook the noodles before stuffing.
