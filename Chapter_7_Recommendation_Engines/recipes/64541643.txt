Title:
Pumpkin Almond Joy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3174761.jpg
Ingredients:
8 tbsp GF rolled oats
8 tbsp almond flour
4 tbsp almond butter
4 tbsp pumpkin puree
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp almond extract
1 packet truvia sweetener
dash of sea salt

Directions:
Pulse all ingredients in a food processor to combine
Be careful not to over combine, they just need to be loosely combined
Roll the batter into balls & place in the refrigerator to harden

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
A no bake snack bite made with almond butter and oats blended with winter spices. A healthy snack solution that will save you time and money!
