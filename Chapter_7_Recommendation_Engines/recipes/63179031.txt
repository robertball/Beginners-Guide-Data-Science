Title:
Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tube refrigerated chocolate chip cookie dough (16-18 ounces)
1 8-ounce package of cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Grease an 8" x 8" baking dish.
Cut cookie dough in half, press half of cookie dough in prepared baking dish.
Blend cream cheese and sugar, add egg, stir until combined.
Pour cheese mixture over cookie dough.
Crumble remaining half of cookie dough over cheese mixture.
Bake @ 350F for 35-40 minutes until toothpick near center comes out clean (should look mostly set).
Cool on wire rack.
Refrigerate.
