Title:
Perfect Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2  cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. salt
1 Tbsp. sugar
12 Tbsp. cold unsalted butter, but into pieces
1/4 cup chilled shortening
1 Tbsp. white vinegar
1/2 cup very cold water

Directions:
1.  In a food processor pulse together, flour, salt and sugar.  Add butter and shortening.  Pulse 8  - 10 times  or until mixture resembles coarse crumbs, and some of the butter is pea-size.  (Alternately combine in a bowl using a pasty blender of a fork)
2.  Comine vinegar with cold water.  While pulsing While pulsing crumb  mixture in processor, drizzle in just enough water mixture to form dough..  Have dough, wrap  in plastic  wrap and press into disks.  Chill for 1 hour or until ready to use.
