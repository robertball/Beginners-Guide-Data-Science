Title:
Cucumber Tea Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4457779.jpg
Ingredients:
24 Snack Factory® Gluten Free Original Pretzel Crisps® Minis
1 cucumber, sliced
1 (4 ounce) package cream cheese, softened, or more as needed
Dried dill

Directions:
Cut slices of cucumber into thirds or quarters. Place on half of the Gluten Free Original Pretzel Crisps(R) Minis.
Spread or pipe cream cheese on top of cucumbers, then sprinkle with dill. Top with another mini to make itty bitty gluten free tea sandwiches.

Submitter: 
Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
Description: 
Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps® Minis are topped with a cucumber slice, a bit of cream cheese, and a dusting of dill then topped with another mini for bite-size tea sandwiches.
