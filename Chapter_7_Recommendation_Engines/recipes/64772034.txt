Title:
Prince's Toast /  Prinzen Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz Veal or Beef Filet Medallions
1 slice of Ham
1 oz of Green Beans
3 oz of Green Asparagus (from a Jar or can will do ) fresh cooked if you have it.
Butter, salt , Pepper,
2 tbsp Butter
salt, butter
1/2 tsp. mustard
1 tsp, Dry Bread crumbs
1 boiled egg
1/2 tsp. chopped parsley

Directions:
Toast and butter the Bread and set aside;  arranged on a plate.  Tenderize, salt and pepper the Filets and Fry until done in Butter.  arrange on Toast.  in Same Pan; in butter;  Fry the Chopped Ham, Chopped green Beans, Asparagus and mushrooms;  arrange on medallions.  In the Same Pan;  add the butter then Carefully. add the crumbs and mustard when frothy add 1/2 chopped Egg. parsley; .  mix and dress the Vegetables .
Serve:   with  Pan fried Potatoes or French Fries. and or Onion Rings.

Alois
