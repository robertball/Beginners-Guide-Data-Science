Title:
Slow Cooker Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
1 (16 ounce) can chili beans
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans
1 (12 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 cups water, more or less
1 (14.5 ounce) cans peeled and diced tomatoes
1 (10 ounce) can Rotel tomatoes
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1 (1.25 ounce) package ranch dressing mix

Directions:
In a medium skillet, cook the ground beef until browned over medium heat. Drain, and set aside.
Place the ground beef, onion, bell pepper, chili beans, kidney beans, tomato sauce, water, diced tomatoes, ranch and taco seasoning mix in a slow cooker. Mix to blend, and cook on Low setting for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Melissa Gardner
Description: 
Browned ground beef  seasoned with a package of taco seasonings is combined in a slow cooker with canned beans, tomatoes, and green chilies.  Serve with tortilla chips and a dollop of sour cream.Modified from: Slow Cooker Taco Soup
