Title:
Play dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 cup salt
2 tsp cream of tartar
1 Tbs Veg oil
1 cup water
food coloring gel (I put it in the water)

Directions:
mix all ingredients except glitter
heat in sauce pan well stirring the mix constantly until it forms a ball
remove at once
add glitter and knead until smooth and uniform
