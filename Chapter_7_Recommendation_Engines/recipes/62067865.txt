Title:
cuecumber shrimp soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 a cuecumber diced into small pieces
1/4 c of cooked shrimp
2 tbs light caesar salad dressing

Directions:
mix ingredients and place in the microwave for 5 minutes. This can be pureed into a soup and is ideal for those days you want something light and warm. or it can be refrigerated and eaten as a salad.

Submitter: 
SHANNON32
Description: 
This is a quick intersting soup that will have you wondering in the beginning but it offers a unique taste to those who are willing to experiment. The crispness of the cuecumber is refeshing and this recepie can also be converted into a salad
