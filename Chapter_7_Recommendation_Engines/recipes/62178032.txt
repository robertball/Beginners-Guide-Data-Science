Title:
Chocolate Mush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/288943.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chocolate chips (melted)
1/2 cup cocoa powder (optional)
1 egg
1/2 stick butter
3 tbs light maple syrup
1/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C) Grease a 8x8 inch baking pan, then set aside.
In a large bowl, cream together melted chocolate chips, cocoa powder, butter, maple syrup, and brown sugar. While mixing ingredients add egg.
Pour batter into greased pan and cook in the preheated oven for 5-8 minutes. Cool completely.
