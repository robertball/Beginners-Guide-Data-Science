Title:
Mango Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup mango puree
fresh lemon juice (about 1/2 lemon)
teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Add first 3 ingredients into heavy bottom saucepan, Bring to boil and skim...Add lemon juice and boil for 15 minutes. Add vanilla just before serving.
