Title:
Oven baked banana chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2233705.jpg
Ingredients:
As many bananas as you want

Directions:
Preheat oven to 175 degrees
Slice bananas into thin slices
Bake for 2 hours. After 1 hour has passed, flip the banana slices.
