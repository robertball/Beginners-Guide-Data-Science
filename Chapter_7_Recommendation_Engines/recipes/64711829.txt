Title:
Warm Grilled Peach & Kale Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5613814.jpg
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup chopped yellow onions
1 medium red or green pepper chopped
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoon freshly grated giner
1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
Salad:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2lb kale, veins removed
2 medium peaches
1/3 cup goat cheese
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Combine all dressing ingredients together in a jar with a lid. Twist lid onto jar, shake, and refrigerate, 8 hour to overnight.
2. Preheat grill to medium high heat.
3. Cut peaches into eights leaving the skin on set aside. In a bowl toss kale with veins removed and 1 tablespoon of oil. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Place peaches and kale on grill. Continuously toss kale approx. 5 minutes or until softened and lightly charred on edges. Cook peaches about 3 minutes, turn, and cook for another 3 minutes or until grill marks appear.
5.  Remove kale and toss with dressing. Top kale with peaches and goat cheese.  Serve warm.
