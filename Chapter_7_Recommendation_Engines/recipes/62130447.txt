Title:
Sticky Cinnamon Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP butter
1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
1 TBSP corn syrup
2 cups flour
1 TBSP baking powder
1 TBSP brown sugar
pinch salt
3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup veggetable oil
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup raisins and/or chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Combine first amount of butter, brown sugar and corn syrup in a small saucepan and heat until smooth and melted. Pour into well greased 8 X 8 pan.
In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, 1 TBSP sugar and salt. Add the milk and oil and stir by hand until you have a soft dough. Do not overmix.
On a lightly floured surface, pat or roll the dough into a 9 X 14 rectangle. Sprinkle with the 1 cup brown sugar, cinnamon and raisins and/or nuts.Starting from the long side, roll tightly jelly-roll style into a log. Cut into 9 biscuits using a serrated knife and place cut side down in the pan.
Bake for 20 mins, until golden brown and bubbly. Invert onto plate while still warm.
