Title:
Italian Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz) pkg tri-color rotini
1 pkg. Hormel mini-pepperoni
1 cup mozzarella/string cheese sticks - sliced
3/4 cup red onion - diced
1 green bell pepper - chopped
1 tomato - chopped
1 red bell pepper - chopped
1 small can sliced black olives
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 (8oz) bottle Wishbone Robusto Italian Dressing
1 pkg Wishbone Robusto Italian Dressing mix

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions & cool quickly in ice bath. Combine 1/2 bottle of dressing with package of dry seasoning mix. In large bowl, Combine all ingredients except Parmesan cheese.  At this point can add more of remaining dressing to preference, just make sure to combine well.  To finish, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
