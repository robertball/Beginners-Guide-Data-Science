Title:
Black-eyed Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (16 oz) black-eyed peas, drained
1 c finely chopped celery
1/4 c sweet or green onion, chopped
1/2 c sweet green pepper, chopped
2 c lettuce, cut in long, thin strips
1 medium sized tomato, chopped
1 cup commercial coleslaw dressing

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together; toss lightly.  Serve immediately.
