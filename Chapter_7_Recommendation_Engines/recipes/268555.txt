Title:
Vegan Chocolate Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5845007.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sweet potato
5 tablespoons almond milk, or as needed
4 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tablespoons agave nectar
2 tablespoons peanut butter
1 teaspoon almond extract

Directions:
Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer. Bring water to a boil. Add sweet potato, cover, and steam until tender, 25 to 30 minutes. Remove and allow to cool. Peel and cut into chunks.
Combine sweet potato, almond milk, cocoa powder, agave nectar, peanut butter, and almond extract in a blender; blend until smooth. Add more almond milk if mixture is too thick. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Rita
Description: 
The secret ingredient in this chocolate pudding is sweet potato. You need to adjust the amount of almond milk to the size of the sweet potato - more or less, depending on the size of the sweet potato.
