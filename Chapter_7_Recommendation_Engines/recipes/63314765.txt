Title:
Lemon Crinkle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c softened butter
1 c sugar
1 egg
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp vanilla
2 tsp. lemon zest
1 1/2 c flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 c powdered sugar

Directions:
preheat oven to 350.
use non stick spray
Cream butter and sugar. Whip in vanilla, egg, zest, and juice. Stir in dry ingredients except for powdered sugar.
roll dough into balls and roll on powdered sugar.
bake 9-11 min or until cookies look mattr, not melty or shiny.
