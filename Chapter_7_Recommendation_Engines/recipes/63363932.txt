Title:
Seasoned Oven Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Medium potatoes
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 1/2 teaspoons paprika
3/4 teaspoons sea salt
3/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
cut each potatoe into 12 wedges. In a large bowl, combine oil and seasonings. Add potatoes and toss to coat.
Transfer to a 10x10x1in baking pan covered in aluminum foil and greased with shortening. Bake at 400degrees for 35-45 minutes or until tender, turning once.

Submitter: 
Jessica Schultz
Description: 
Wonderful, flavorful recipe from Taste of Home, Ultimate Halloween 2012 Edition. The combination of garlic powder and paprika packs a punch without being too spicy for the kids.
