Title:
Lighter Chocolate Chip Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 c. white whole wheat flour
1/4 c. wheat germ
1 3/4 c. skim milk or almond milk
2 tsp baking powder
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 c. light margarine, melted
3 T. Demarara (large-granule) brown sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/2 c. chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients to form a smooth batter.
Drop spoonsful of batter onto greased skillet, cook about one minute on each side or until golden brown.
Serve with maple syrup or sliced strawberries.

Submitter: 
YouKnowWho18
Description: 
I made these in my dorm on Friday mornings to kick off the weekend- most of them were eaten straight out of the pan by the line of friends drawn by the scent of melting chocolate!
