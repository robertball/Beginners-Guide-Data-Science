Title:
Brown Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/8 tsp. salt

Directions:
Mix margarine and sugar well.
Beat in egg and vanilla.
Stir in dry ingredients. Mix well.
Drop by tablespoonfuls onto cookie sheet.
Bake @ 350 x 8 minutes or more.
