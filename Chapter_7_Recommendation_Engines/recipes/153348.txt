Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups apple juice
1/2 cup sugar
3 tablespoons cornstarch
1 pkg. (4 serving size)  JELL-O Brand Strawberry Flavor Gelatin
1 pint strawberries, halved
1 (6 ounce) HONEY MAID Graham Pie Crust

Directions:
Mix apple juice, sugar and cornstarch in saucepan; cook on medium heat 2 minutes or until thickened, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Add gelatin; stir 2 minutes until completely dissolved.
Place strawberries, cut sides down, in crust.
Pour gelatin mixture over strawberries. Refrigerate 2 hours or until firm. Store leftover pie in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
Apple juice, strawberries, and strawberry gelatin combine for a jewel-like pie that's the perfect finale for a summer meal.
