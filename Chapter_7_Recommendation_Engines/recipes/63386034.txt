Title:
Salmon Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
spinach leaves (fresh)
pecans (lightly roasted)
salmon (broiled)
olive oil
lemon
vinegrette salad dressing
croutons
salt and pepper

Directions:
on each salad plate, place fresh washed spinach leaves, lightly roasted pecans (slightly chopped) broiled salmon (cut up in chunks) in the oven broil salmon at 375' with olive oil and lemon sprinkled on it until translucent and vinegrette salad dressing and homemade croutons (to make croutons, in a fry pan heat olive oil and chopped garlic, cut up slices of bread in cubes and fry them in the olive oil, salt and pepper then after.
