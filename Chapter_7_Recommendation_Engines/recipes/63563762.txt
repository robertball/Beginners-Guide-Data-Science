Title:
Butternut Squash Soup with a Kick II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium butternut squashes, peeled and chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 avocado, chopped
1 quart chicken broth (or vegetable for vegetarian)
1/4 cup Almond milk
2 Tbsp cinnamon
2 Tbsp cayanne pepper
2 Tbsp nutmeg
2 Tbsp coconut oil, melted
1 Tbsp ginger
1 Tbsp honey

Directions:
In a large pot, sauté the onion with the coconut oil until golden brown.
Add butternut squash, broth, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, nutmeg, honey,  and ginger.
Bring to a boil and allow the squash to cook until fork tender
Once tender, use a food processor to purée all ingredients together.
Add the avocado chunks slowly making sure to process after each addition.
***Optional: Garnish with a dash of cinnamon and chopped pecans.
