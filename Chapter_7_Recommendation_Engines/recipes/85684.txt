Title:
Shrimp Purses Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984593.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked and deveined shrimp, minced
1 teaspoon spicy brown mustard
1/4 cup red pepper, diced
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/8 cup white onion, minced
1/2 cup celery chunks
2 hard boiled eggs, diced
1/4 avocado, diced
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
Salt and pepper to taste
1 (4.5 ounce) package pre-made crepes
Chives (optional)
Paprika to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together, except crepes and paprika.
Fold crepes in half and place spoonful of shrimp mixture in middle. Bring up all edges to form a ''purse'' and secure with a toothpick.
Optional: tie a chive around purse.
Sprinkle with paprika to taste and serve immediately.
