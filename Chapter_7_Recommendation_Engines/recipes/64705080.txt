Title:
Bread Pudding Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5460075.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large eggs
1/2 cup butter milk
1 1/2 cup whole milk
1/2 stick butter - melted
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 package Kings Hawaiian Rolls (12) - tear each roll into 6 pieces
4 oz cream cheese
1/4 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
In a large mixing bowl, combine very well, all ingredients except for bread, cheese and raisins.
Add bread and raisins and mix gently to just moisten.
Pinch bits of cheese over bread mixture and fold in.
Bake in a buttered baking dish for 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
Cool for at least 1/2 hour before serving.
DELICIOUS!

Description: 
It's so fun to play with ingredients to make one of a kind bread pudding.  At it again, I created a bread pudding with a creamy bit of a tart twist.  Incorporating buttermilk and cream cheese is surprisingly  dee-licious!   Made with King's Hawaiian rolls makes it especially yummy!
