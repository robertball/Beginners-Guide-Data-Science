Title:
Parmesan Cheese Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. water
1/2 c. butter
1/2 t. salt
1 c. flour
3/4 c. grated parmesan cheese
4 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Heat water and butter in a medium saucepan until it reaches a rolling boil.
Add salt and flour stirring vigorously until mixture forms a ball.
Remove from heat and stir in the parmesan cheese.
Add eggs, one at a time, stirring well after each addition.
Divide dough evenly into a 6-cup popover pan.
Bake until lightly browned, approx. 40 minutes.
*may be baked in a standard muffin pan for approx. 30 min.

Submitter: 
Diana S.
Description: 
These are delicious savory puffs, very similar to cream puffs, but with the addition of parmesan cheese. My 2 sons like them warm from the oven spread with butter. I have also served them split and filled with seafood cream sauce for a rich, elegant dinner.
