Title:
bbq ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound rack back ribs, cut lengthwise across bones into halves
1/2 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons packed brown sugar
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
3 tablespoons dry mustard
3 tablespoons cold water

Directions:
1. trim excess fat. mix ketchup, brown sugar, lemon juice, salt, garlic powder, and ginger
2. coat both sides of pork, cover and refridgerate for at least 2 hours
3. remove pork reserve marinade, place in a foil wrapped rack and bake uncovered in a 400 degree oven brushing occasionally with marinade for 30 mins
4. turn pork and bake, brushing occasionally with marinade until done for about 30 minutes longer
5. mix mustard and water until smooth let stand for 5 minutes. serve with ribs
