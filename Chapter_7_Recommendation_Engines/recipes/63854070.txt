Title:
Pasta Primavera with Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup whipping cream
3/4 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup basil pesto
1/4 cup finely chopped sun-dried tomatoes, soft & drained
8 oz dried linguini pasta
1/4 cup butter
1 1/2 cups assorted raw vegetables, cut in bite sized pieces
2 tsp olive oil
1 1/2 cups chicken breast, cut in bite sized pieces
2 tblsp fresh basil, cut chiffonade
1 tblsp Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat cream and chicken broth in 1-quart sauce pan until hot.
Whisk in cheese, pesto and sun-dried tomatoes. Keep sauce warm.  if necessary, thin sauce with more chicken broth to coat pasta and vegetables evenly.
Cook pasta in large pot of boiling water until tender but still firm to bite, stirring occasionally.  Add butter and toss.
Steam vegetables until crisp tender, about 5 minutes.  Heat oil in large non-stick skillet over medium heat.  Add chicken pieces and saute until chicken is heated through, about 2 minutes.
Add vegetables, chicken and sauce to pasta in pot.  Toss gently to combine.  Spoon pasta onto plates.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and basil.  Serve.
