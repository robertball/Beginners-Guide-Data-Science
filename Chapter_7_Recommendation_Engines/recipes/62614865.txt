Title:
Caramel Chocolate Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (9 oz)devil's food cake mix
2 pkgs (3.9 oz each) instant pudding mix
1 carton (12 oz) frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 jar (12 1/4 oz) caramel ice cream topping
1 pkg (7 1/2 or 8 oz) English toffee bits or almond brickle chips

Directions:
Prapare and bake cake according to package directions for and 8" square baking pan.  Cool on a wire rack.  Prepare pudding according to package directions.
Cut cake into 1 1/2" cubes; place half of the cubes in a 3 qt. trifle bowl or large glass serving bowl; lightly press down to fill in gaps. Top with half to the whipped topping; pudding, caramel topping and toffee bits; repeat layers.  Cover and refrigerate until serving.
