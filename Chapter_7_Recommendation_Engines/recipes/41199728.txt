Title:
Spicy Grilled Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brine:
2 cups hot water
3 cups Kosher salt
4 cups ice water
1 pound (15-20 count) cleaned, shell-on frozen shrimp
1 garlic clove
1 tsp Kosher salt
1/2 tsp cayenne
1 tsp sweet paprika
2 Tbl extra virgin olive oil
2 tsp fresh lemon juice

Directions:
For brine:  Stir salt and hot water in big pot until mostly disolved, add ice water and stir until completely disolved.
Add shrimp to brine and allow to sit and thaw for 20-25 minutes; rinse and pat dry
Mince garlic clove and salt together with sharp knife to make a paste.  Add to medium mixing bowl.
Add cayenne, paprika, olive oil and lemon juice to garlic/salt mixture, stir to combine
Add shrimp, toss to coat.
Grill on medium-high grill heat approximtely two minutes each side--do not overcook or the shrimp will toughen.
