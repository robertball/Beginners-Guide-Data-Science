Title:
Old fashioned Fried Oysters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- pints raw oysters
1- Saltine crackers
Salt and pepper to taste
2-eggs
butter for frying

Directions:
take 1/2 a box of Saltine crackers and crush them into crumbs. Make sure the crumbs are not fine but more coarse. Whisk the eggs.Take the oysters and dip them in the egg batter then coat them.Melt the butter to a frying pan on medium heat then add oysters. Fry the oysters to a golden brown and serve.
