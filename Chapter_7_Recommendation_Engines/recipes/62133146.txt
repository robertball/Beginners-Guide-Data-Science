Title:
Traditional Irish Carrot Soup with a Twist (of lemon) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds pre-sliced carrots
2 cups diced onions
2 tbs olive oil
6 whole cloves
6 diced garlic cloves
4 cups of chicken broth
1 dried chipotle pepper (whole)
1/4 cup fresh Meyer lemon
1 tsp brown sugar
salt & pepper
6 dollops creme fraiche (1 per serving)
Minced fresh parsley for garnish

Directions:
Saute carrots, onion in the oil over medium low heat until the onion is just starting to turn translucent.
Add garlic and saute 1 minute being careful not to allow the onions to turn brown.
Add 3 cups of the broth and chipotle pepper and simmer covered until carrots are very soft - about 20 minutes.
Remove the chipotle and stir in  brown sugar until dissolved.
Blend soup in a food processor until smooth.
Stir in lemon juice.
Season with salt & pepper to taste using remaining stock to thin as desired.
Divide soup into bowls and add a dollop of creme fraiche and top with a pinch of parsley.15 minutes

Submitter: 
Jeff
Description: 
Carrot soup is a staple of great Irish cuisine.  This version of a traditional classic adds some eclectic flavors that create a dish that is sweet, spicy, tangy, and creamy all in one elegant slurp.
