Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 short ribs, on the bone
1-1.5 c. BBQ sauce
2 T. brown sugar or honey
1 T. ginger
1 sliced onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 tsp. liquid smoke

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in slow cooker.
Cook on low for 8-12 hours, or on high for 3-4 hours.
Serve with brown rice and steamed vegetables.

Submitter:

Description: 
Falling off the bone, sweet and savory at the same time- best ribs I've ever made!
