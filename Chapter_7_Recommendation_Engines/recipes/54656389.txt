Title:
Grilled Bacon Jalapeno Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 fresh jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1/2 pkg. ranch dressing
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
12 slices bacon partially cooked in microwave

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
Mix cream cheese, ranch dressing and cheese together.  Spread cream cheese mix to fill jalapeno halves. Wrap with bacon. Secure with a toothpick.
Place on the grill, and cook until bacon is crispy.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
Jalapenos are stuffed with cream cheese, wrapped with bacon, and barbecued on the grill.Modified from: Grilled Bacon Jalapeno Wraps
