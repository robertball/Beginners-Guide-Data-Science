Title:
Everything Good in the world Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3772679.jpg
Ingredients:
8-10 Cups potatoes, diced finely
16 ounces cheddar cheese, cubed or shredded
1 Cup mayonnaise (real)
1 package bacon, cooked and torn into large bits
1/2 Cup green onions, chopped

Directions:
In a 9x13 pan or 3 quart casserole dish, mix potatoes, cheddar cheese, bacon, and green onions.
Stir in mayonnaise.
Bake at 325 for an hour and a half or until potatoes are tender.
Top with additional cheese if desired (but of course you desire!).
