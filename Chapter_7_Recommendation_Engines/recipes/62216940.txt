Title:
Mediterranean Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 sweet onion
4 cloves of garlic, chopped
1 TBS extra virgin olive oil
2 medium tomatoes, diced
2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1-2 summer squash, sliced thin
1-2 zucchini, sliced thin
3.5 oz pkg crumbled feta (basil & tomato)
1 can sliced black olives
2 cups mozzarella cheese
refrigerator pizza dough
Italian seasoning to taste

Directions:
saute onions and garlic in olive oil on medium, after 3 mins add thyme, sugar, salt and pepper and continue to cook for 3 minutes or until onions and garlic are tender.
Prepare pizza dough on baking sheet. Use a large enough cookie/baking sheet so dough is quite thin in order to make pizza wedges.
spread onion garlic mixture on dough
On top of mixture pile squash, zucchini, black olives, feta, and tomatoes
Top with Mozzarella
Sprinkle Italian seasoning to taste
bake in a preheated oven 400 degrees for 15 minutes or until crust is crispy, cut into small square wedges.
