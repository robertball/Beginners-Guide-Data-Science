Title:
Bacon-wrapped Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch asparagus
4-8 strips of bacon

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2. Trim and wash asparagus spears.
3. Wrap bundles of 2-3 asparagus spears in bacon strips. Don't overlap the bacon.
4. Cook for 25 minutes on middle rack. Still on middle rack, broil until bacon crisps.
