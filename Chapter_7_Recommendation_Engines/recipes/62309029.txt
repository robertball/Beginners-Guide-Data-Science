Title:
St Patrick's Day Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/370263.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups cooked corned beef, cut into 3/4" cubes
3 1/2 cups cooked red potatoes, cut into 3/4" cubes
4 cups medium chopped cooked cabbage
1 1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1/3 cup Deli or grain mustard
1/2 cup white wine
1 tsp granulated garlic
2 tsp black pepper
4 sheets phyllo dough, thawed according to pkg directions
butter flavored cooking spray
1 Tbsp melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees.
Spray 9 x 13 pan with non-stick cooking spray.
In a large bowl, combine corned beef, red potatoes, cooked cabbage and Swiss cheese.
In a small bowl, whisk Deli mustard, white wine, granulated garlic and black pepper.
Pour mustard wine sauce over the corned beef/potato/cabbage mixture, and mix evenly.
Spread mixture in the 9x13 pan.
Lay out 4 sheets phyllo dough on clean, dry counter, covering with plastic wrap or damp cloth between handling.
Spray top sheet with butter flavored cooking spray and lay on top of the 9x13 casserole, folding the overhang back onto the casserole. Spray the top dry side of the phyllo sheet.
Repeat with the remaining 3 sheets of phyllo, overlapping on the uncovered portion of the pan.
Using a pastry brush, apply the melted butter to the very top of the last phyllo sheet layer.
Bake 375 degrees for 45-55 minutes, until top is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Jeannine Ross
Description: 
A tasty way to eat corned beef, cabbage and potatoes...perfect for a St. Patrick's Day feast, or simply good eating any other time!
