Title:
Quick-Poached Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989099.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter, melted
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon fresh dill weed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 pounds salmon fillet, bones removed
1/4 cup lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Mix butter, garlic, dill, salt and pepper in a small bowl.
Place salmon on a piece of heavy-duty foil and pour lemon juice over the fish. Rub dill mixture all over. Seal completely in foil and bake until just cooked through, about 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Pam Anderson
Description: 
Sealed in a foil package, fresh salmon cooks perfectly in a fragrant, buttery lemon-dill sauce.
