Title:
The Best Meatloaf Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. hamburger
2 stalks finely chopped celery
2 diced onions
6 scallions finely chopped
6 slices of dried bread, chopped small
1 cup ketchup
2 packets dried onion soup mix
2 tsps dijon mustard
2 tablespoons steak sauce
2 cloves finely minced garlic
1/2 tsp ginger
3/4 tsp fresh ground pepper
1/2 tsp garlic pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 1/2 Tbls parsley
3 Tbl Worcestshire sauce
4 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Beat eggs and set aside. Combine all other ingredients into large bowl. Add eggs and mix well (mixing with your hands works best). Divide in half. Place each half into 5x9 loafpan and shape. Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 60 minutes. Serve with garlic mashed potatoes for the ultimate comfort food meal.

Submitter: 
Love to cook
Description: 
Absolutely the best meatloaf ever! So delicious, your mouth will be watering when you smell it cooking (and the taste is even better). You put everything but the kitchen sink into it but it's well worth the effort.
