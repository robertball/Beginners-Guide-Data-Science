Title:
Cherry Kooga Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. Flour
1 c. Sugar
1 stick Butter, room temp
2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 egg
Milk
2 cans Cherrie Pie Filling
_________________________________
Crumble Topping
1 c. Flour
1 c. Sugar
2 tsp. Cinnamon
1 stick Butter. room temp

Directions:
Put dry ingredients into a bowl and mix.
Crack the egg in a measuring cup, then add milk to the 1 cup line.
Add liquids to dry, except for cherries.
Mix together well and spread into a greased cookie sheet with sides.
Spread Cherrie Pie Filling over the top.
Mix crumble ingredients together and crumble over the top of the Cherries.
Bake at 325` for approx 30 min.
