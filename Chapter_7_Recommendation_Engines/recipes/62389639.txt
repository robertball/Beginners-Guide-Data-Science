Title:
Vanilla Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
2+ Tablespoon flour
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon allspice
1 cup cold water
2 tablespoon butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a saucepan, mix together the sugar, flour, nutmeg, and allspice; blend in cold water.  Bring to a simmer and simmer until clear and thickened.  Blend in butter and vanilla.  Serve over bread pudding or other desserts.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
This is a great sauce to serve over gingerbread or bread pudding!
