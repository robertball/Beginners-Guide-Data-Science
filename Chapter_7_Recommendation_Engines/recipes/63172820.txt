Title:
Mediterranean Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz baby spinach
1 pint carton of grape or cherry tomatoes
olive oil
red wine cooking vinegar
dried basil
salt

Directions:
Cut the tomatoes in half.
Put enough olive oil in a pan to cover its bottom.  Place the spinach in the pan.  Toss it, covering it with olive oil.  Add more oil if needed.
Place the pan on medium heat.  Cover it with its lid until the spinach wilts.
Then, add the tomatoes.  Add the vinegar, basil, and salt to taste.  Saute until the tomatoes are thoroughly cooked but not too runny.

Description: 
A classic recipe of spinach and tomato.  A great side for pasta, and many other dishes too.
