Title:
Chocolate Chip Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990129.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Hain Pure Foods Organic Turbinado Sugar
1 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, melted
2 cups mashed ripe bananas
3 eggs
2 3/4 cups Arrowhead Mills Organic All Purpose Flour, sifted
2 1/2 teaspoons Hain Pure Foods Gluten Free Featherweight Baking Powder
1 teaspoon Hain Pure Foods Iodized Sea Salt
1 cup Sunspire Semi-Sweet Chocolate Baking Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees F; line 24 muffin tins.
Combine the Arrowhead Mills Organic All Purpose Flour, Hain Pure Foods Iodized Sea Salt and Hain Pure Foods Gluten Free Featherweight Baking Powder in a bowl.
In the bowl of a mixer, combine the sugar, butter, bananas and eggs until well mixed.
Add the dry ingredients and mix until evenly combined; stir in chocolate chips.
Spoon batter into prepared muffin liners and bake for 28 minutes. Test with a wooden pick. Muffins are done when wooden pick comes out clean. Let cool in pans for 10 minutes and then turn out onto cooling rack to cool completely

Submitter: 
Hain Celestial
Description: 
Classic moist banana muffins are extra special with the addition of chocolate chips.
