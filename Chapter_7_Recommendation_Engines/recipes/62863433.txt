Title:
IBBZ Date Nut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. all purpose flour
1 tsp. baking powder
3/4-1 c. sugar, your choice
1/4 tsp. salt
1 c. chopped dates
1 c. chopped nuts, your choice
3 eggs, beaten
powdered sugar

Directions:
Combine flour, baking powder, sugar and salt. Add dates, nuts and eggs until thoroughly mixed. Spread batter into a 9" square baking pan that has been coated with cooking spray. Bake at 350 for 35-40 mins. Cool and cut into "logs" and then roll in powdered sugar. Let dry a few minutes before eating.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
A tasty, healthy date nut bar that is quick and easy.
