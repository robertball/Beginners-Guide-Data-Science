Title:
Easy Baked Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1529361.jpg
Ingredients:
stick of butter
one pack of dried Italian seasoning
One bag of fresh shrimp

Directions:
Melt a stick of butter in the pan. Slice one lemon and layer it on top of the butter. Put down fresh shrimp, then sprinkle one pack of dried Italian seasoning. Put in the oven and bake at 350 for 15 min, nice and easy
