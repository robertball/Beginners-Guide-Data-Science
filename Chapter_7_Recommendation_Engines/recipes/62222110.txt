Title:
Three day coconut cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1178036.jpg
Ingredients:
1(16.5 ounce) box of yellow cake mix
2 (14 ounce) packages coconut (not frozen)
1 (16  ounce) container sour cream
2  cups white sugar
1 (12 ounce) container cool whip

Directions:
Follow the directions on back of cake mix box and bake in two 9 inch round cake pans.
When done, let cool completely and take dental floss or fishing line and split the layers in half.
Take 1 package of  coconut and set to the side. (will not need it at this point)
In a large bowl, Stir 1 pkg of the coconut the sour cream and sugar.
Mix well.
Set aside 1/2 cup of this mixture.
Spread the rest between the layers.
Mix 1/2 cup reserved mixture and the cool whip and spread all over the cake.
Take the other package of coconut and sprinkle all over the cake.
Put the cake in air tight container for 3 days. After 3 days enjoy.
