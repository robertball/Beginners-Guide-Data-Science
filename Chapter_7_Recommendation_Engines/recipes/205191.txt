Title:
Cocoa Yogurt Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991154.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) container low-fat raspberry, lemon, orange or vanilla yogurt
1/4 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 cup frozen reduced-fat non-dairy whipped topping, thawed
1/3 cup Kellogg's® Cocoa Krispies® cereal
1 tablespoon flaked coconut, toasted
1 cup fresh raspberries, sliced strawberries or cut-up mango

Directions:
In small bowl stir together yogurt and vanilla. Fold in whipped topping. In another small bowl stir together KELLOGG'S COCOA KRISPIES cereal and coconut. Set aside.
In two parfait glasses or dessert dishes alternate layers of yogurt mixture, raspberries and cereal mixture.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
Description: 
Fresh raspberries layered with yogurt, whipped topping and Kellogg's® Cocoa Krispies® cereal make outrageous sundaes for big and little kids.
