Title:
Fruit Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451479.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can pineapple chunks
1 pint strawberries
2 bananas
3 peaches
3 plums
*Other fruit if desired
1/2 cup honey
2 tbs. lime juice
1/8 cup chopped fresh mint leaves
Shish Kabob Skewers

Directions:
Soak shish kabob skewers in water before use so they don't scorch.
Prepare grill with medium heat.
Cut banana chunks, plums and peaches.
Hull strawberries and drain pineapple chunks.
Place fruit onto skewers.
Grill fruit for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally.
Prepare fruit dressing with honey, lime juice and mint by combining in small bowl.
Brush fruit kabobs with fruit dressing 4 mintues before done grilling.
