Title:
Fresh Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tomatoes, diced
1 onion, chopped
1 cucumber, sliced
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
1/2 cup chopped parsley
2 tablespoons crushed garlic
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
2 tbs. olive oil
crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the tomato, onion, cucumber, bell pepper, basil, parsley, garlic and vinegar and olive oil. Toss and add salt and pepper to taste. Chill and top each serving with feta cheese when serving.
