Title:
Beef Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978351.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 1/3 cups water
1 (1.5 ounce) package ORTEGA® Burrito Seasoning Mix
8 (6 inch) flour tortillas
Additional ingredient suggestions: SARGENTO® ChefStyle Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese, shredded lettuce and chopped tomatoes

Directions:
BROWN beef; drain. Stir in water and seasoning mix. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low; cook for 3 to 4 minutes or until mixture thickens.
SPREAD 1/2 cup beef mixture on each tortilla. Top with cheese, lettuce and tomatoes; fold into burritos.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
Additional Serving Suggestions: Serve beef mixture inside ORTEGA ® Taco Shells or over ORTEGA ® Tostada Shells; top as desired. Or, spoon over tortilla chips, sprinkle with shredded cheese and heat in microwave until cheese is melted. Top with sliced ripe olives, guacamole and ORTEGA ® Thick & Chunky Salsa.
