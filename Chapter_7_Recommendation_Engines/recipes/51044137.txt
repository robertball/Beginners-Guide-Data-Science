Title:
Easy Biscuit Mixture Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup baking powder
1/4 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons salt
2 cups shortening

Directions:
In a large bowl mix together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt. Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Store in an airtight container for up to 6 weeks.
To make biscuits: Mix 2 1/4 cup biscuit mix with 2/3 cup milk. Make into dough ball place on floured surface kneed 10 times, then roll out to 1/2 inch thick. Cut with biscuit cutter or large dia. glass. Bake in preheated 450 degree oven for 10 to 12 minutes.
