Title:
Dumpling Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
2 cups water
1 cup brown sugar
1 tbsp. butter
1 tsp. vanilla
Dumplings:
1 cup flour
2 tbsp. butter
2 tbsp. white sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup milk (or water, but I prefer milk)

Directions:
Put water, brown sugar and butter in dutch oven over med/high heat.
Boil x 5 minutes. Reduce heat to low/simmer.
Add vanilla.
Mix dumplings in a bowl - rub together flour, butter and sugar till crumbly. Add rest of ingredients. Mix till moistened, being careful not to overmix. Drop evenly (by heaping tbsp.) into hot sauce.
Cover and boil slowly on low - low/medium x 20 min.
Serve hot or warm with ice cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Stovetop Dumplings cooked in a butterscotch sauce, served warm over ice cream.
I like to serve this dessert with a lighter meal.
Serves at least 6.
