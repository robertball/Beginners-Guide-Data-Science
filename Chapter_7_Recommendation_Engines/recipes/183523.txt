Title:
Honey Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/392638.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup honey
1 tablespoon dried dill weed

Directions:
Whisk together the mayonnaise, honey, and dill in a small bowl.

Submitter: 
Alex
Description: 
This simple sauce goes great with chicken fingers. It's so easy and so good.
