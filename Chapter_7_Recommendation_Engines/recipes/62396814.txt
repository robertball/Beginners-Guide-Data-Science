Title:
Mom's Chocolate Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Standard Pastry Pie Crust: 8 or 9 inch Two-Crust
2/3 C. plus 2T. shortening
2 C. all-purpose flour
1 t. salt
4 to 5 T. cold water
Filling: for 9 inch pie
1 ½ C. sugar
1/3 C. cornstarch
½ t. salt
3 C. milk
2 oz. unsweetened chocolate
4 egg yolks, slightly beaten
1 T. plus 1 t. vanilla

Directions:
PIE CRUST: Cut shortening into flour & salt until particles are the size of peas. Sprinkle in water 1 T. at a time. Tossing till all the flour is moist. Knead 2 or 3 times, shape into a ball, divide in half, ½ half a bit larger than the other. Shape the Lg. ½ into flattened ball on lightly floured counter or table. Roll crust 2 inches larger than inverted pie pan. Fold in ½ ease into pie pan, unfold & lightly press into pan. Trim top edge 1 inch larger than pie pan roll under & flute.
*NOTE FOR CHOCOLATE CREAM PIE CRUST:  prick the pie crust a lot all over bottom & sides bake @ 475 until light brown, 8 to 10 minutes, cool.
FILLING: Mix sugar, cornstarch, & salt in 1 ½ qt. saucepan. Slowly stir in the milk. Cook over med. heat, STIRRING CONSTANTLY, until mixture thickens & boils. Boil & stir 1 minute.  Stir at least ½ of the hot mixture GRADUALLY into beaten egg yolks. Blend into hot mixture in saucepan. Boil & stir 1 minute. Remove from heat, stir in vanilla. Pour into baked pie shell, press plastic wrap onto filling. Refrigerate at least 2 hours, but no longer than 48 hours, Remove plastic wrap top with whipped cream.
