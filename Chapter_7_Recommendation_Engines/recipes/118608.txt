Title:
Celebration Crab Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984664.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (6 ounce) can lump crab, drained
1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
3 tablespoons chopped green onions
1 teaspoon hot sauce
Toasted baguette slices
Fresh thyme, if desired

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except toast slices; mix well. Refrigerate, covered, at least 1 hour to allow flavors to blend. Serve with toasted baguette slices. Garnish with fresh thyme.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Crab, Ranch dressing, and cheese are blended together for a creamy dip that can be served warm or cold.
