Title:
Garlic Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tablespoon Olive Oil Divided in Half
1 1/2 cups finely chopped carrots
10 garlic cloves smashed
4 cups chicken stock
1 Tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
1 Bay Leaf
1/4 cup dry sherry
3 egg yolks
1/2 cup grated parmigiano cheese
2 Tablespoons chopped Italian Parsley
salt and pepper to taste
4 slices sourdough bread

Directions:
Heat 2 Tablespoons oil and saute' garlic and carrots 3-5 minutes until tender. Add broth, thyme, bay leaf, and sherry bring to a boil then cover and simmer 30 minutes. In small batches puree' in blender and return to pan. Whisk together egg yolks, 2 Tablespoons oil, and parmigiano. Add to soup and cook 10 minutes more stirring constantly, very important DO NOT BOIL!!
To serve place sourdough slices into 4 bowls and ladle soup over bread.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is the Italian version of chicken soup. Wonderful anytime but great for fighting off colds and the flu!
