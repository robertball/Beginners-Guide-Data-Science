Title:
Roasted Potatos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 spuds
3 TBL olive oil
1t garlic powder
2t onion powder
1t Allepo pepper
1T Italian seasoning
1t black pepper
1t sea salt

Directions:
cut the spuds into 1/2" pieces
boil spuds for 10 minutes or until tender (check with fork) before the skin starts peeling off
drain spuds, leave in pan
Add all the ingredients and mix well
spread the spuds on a couple of cookie sheets
bake for 20 minutes then turn them
bake for another 10 minutes or until they are to desired crispiness
