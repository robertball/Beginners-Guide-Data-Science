Title:
Gang Up Apple Pear Breakfast Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507086.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C Flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/6 C butter
5 tsp ice water
1 macintosh apple
1 Bosc pear
1/2 tsp cinnamon (+ more to taste)
1/2 Tbsp shaved butter
splash of water

Directions:
Make the dough: mix dry ingredients together (flour & Salt) cut in butter, til mixed and crumbly. Toss with 1 tsp of water at a time until the dough holds together. Roll dough out and use a glass to cut into 12 rounds. Tuck the rounds into a mini-muffin pan making 12 little pie crusts. Prebake the crust for 10 minutes in a preheated 350 degree oven.
While the crust is cooking, peel, core and dice the apple and pear. Stew the fruit with a splash of water and cinnamon until done - about 10-15 minutes.
Fill the mini pie cups with fruit, top with a thin shave of butter and bake for 15 minutes.
remove from oven and cool, sprinkle with extra cinnamon if desired.
