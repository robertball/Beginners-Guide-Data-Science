Title:
hashbrown Casserole III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pieces of white bread
half a package of frozen diced hashbrowns
one package of ground sausage browned
8 eggs.
One can of chedder cheese
1/2 a cup of milk
4 slices of american processed cheese
3 cups of chedder.

Directions:
8x10 pan buttered
break up bread and mix with hashbrowns put in bottom of pan and press down gently.
salt and pepper before next layer.
add browned sausage
layer the processed cheese on top of the sausage.
layer half of the chedder on top of the processed cheese.
mix cheese soup, milk and eggs.
poor over top
Let sit overnight or cook immediatley.
bake 1 hour at 350.
add rest of chedder cheese last 10 minutes of baking.
of course always with an egg dish check for doneness.
