Title:
Orange Slice Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1258547.jpg
Ingredients:
18 orange slices
1 c. brown sugar
3/4 c. white sugar
1 c. shortening (or 1/2 margarine + 1/2 shortening)
3 large eggs
3 1/2 c. sifted flour
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. cream of tartar
1 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Refrigerate orange slices for 30 minutes.  Slice into 1/8" pieces and sprinkle with flour.
Mix all ingredients, including orange slices, together and chill for 30 minutes or more.  Shape cookies into round balls.  Place on greased cookies sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 11-15 minutes.
