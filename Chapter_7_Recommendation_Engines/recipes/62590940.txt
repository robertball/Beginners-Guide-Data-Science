Title:
Cordon Bleu Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices whole wheat bread
6 chicken cutlets
1-8oz. pkg. cream cheese - thinly sliced
1/2 lb. ham - sliced
1 1/2 cups shredded Swiss cheese - divided
1 lb. fresh broccoli florets or 2-10oz. pkgs. frozen broccoli - thawed
2 cans (10 3/4oz.) cream of chicken soup
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Place bread in bottom of greased 13x9 baking dish.
Layer chicken, cream cheese and ham over bread.
Sprinkle with 1 cup of Swiss and top with broccoli.
Combine soup and pepper and spoon over broccoli.
Sprinkle with remaining Swiss.
Bake, uncovered for 50-55 min. or until bubbly.
