Title:
Granpa's Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Six hard boiled eggs
1 tablespoon softened butter or margarine
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
1 tablespoon salad dressing [Miracle Whip prefered]
3/4 teaspoon mustard
1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish [optional]
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
apx. 1/8 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Boil eggs at least 20 minutes
Remove from heat and let cool
Peel, half "longways", and remove yokes.
Mix yokes with all ingredients
Place apx.1 teaspoon mixture into each egg "half"
Sprinkle with paprika
Garnish with pickles and or olives[optional]
Refrigerate until ready to serve
