Title:
Mocha Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979254.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups creme-filled chocolate sandwich cookie crumbs
3 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
3 large eggs
3 (1 ounce) squares semi-sweet chocolate, melted
2 tablespoons Kava® Coffee, dissolved in 1 tablespoon warm water
1 tablespoon warm water
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup mini-chocolate chips
Whipped topping

Directions:
Heat oven to 300 degrees F. Combine cookie crumbs and butter; press firmly on bottom of 9-inch springform pan.
In large bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Gradually beat in sweetened condensed milk until smooth. Add eggs, chocolate, dissolved coffee and vanilla. Mix well. Stir in 3/4 cup chips.
Pour into prepared pan. Bake 1 hour and 5 minutes or until center is set. Cool. Chill. Just before serving, remove side of springform pan. Garnish with whipped topping and remaining 1/4 cup chips. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
This creamy cheesecake is a mocha-lover's delight, rich with the flavors of chocolate and coffee then crowned with whipped topping and mini chocolate chips.
