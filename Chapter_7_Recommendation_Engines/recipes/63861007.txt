Title:
Cocoa Crunch Cookies (Gluten free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1071536.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. Corn Flakes cereal, crushed
1 C. Brown rice flour
1 C. Gluten free blend flour
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1 tsp. Cinnamon
3 Tbsp. Dry premium cocoa
1 tsp. Xanthan gum
1/2 C. Butter
1 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs
3 Tbsp. Yogurt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
Blend flour, gum, cinnamon, cocoa, b. powder and b. soda together in a small bowl. Set aside
Mix butter and sugar together in a large bowl forming a smooth paste
Add eggs to the butter/sugar mixture and beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth and creamy
Crush corn flakes with a rolling pin and add to butter/sugar mixture and beat well to make a dough that holds together well
Stir in yogurt and blend with all ingredients and combine well
Add dry ingredients to wet mixture and meld all ingredients together to form dough
Take a small amount of dough and roll into a ball placing 2 inches apart on cookie sheet
Flatten with a fork to make nice rounds and bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes until lightly browned
Remove from oven and place on wire rack to cool completely before storing in a canister

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very crunchy cookie that is gluten free with corn flakes and good cocoa flavour to make a nice alternative to the regular cookie fare.
