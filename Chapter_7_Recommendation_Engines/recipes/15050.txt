Title:
Quick and Easy Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/73593.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoons frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed

Directions:
In a medium size serving bowl, mix together marshmallow creme, cream cheese, and orange juice. Refrigerate and serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Nicole
Description: 
I got this recipe years ago and it's always a great hit! It's wonderful served with grapes & strawberries.
