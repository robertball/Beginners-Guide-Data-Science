Title:
A Way Better Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521805.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cans (14.5 oz) Green Beans
2 Tbls Butter
2 Tbls Flour
1 C Milk
1/2 tsp Garlic Powder (Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herb)
1/4 tsp Marjoram
1/4 tsp Dill Weed
3/4 C. Grated Cheddar (Sharp)
1 Can (4oz.) Mushrooms
1 Can (14 oz) French's Fried Onions

Directions:
Melt Butter and add Flour.  Cook about 2-3 mins, cool.  Whisk in Milk to make a Bechamel.  Add all herbs and 1/2 C of Cheese. Simmer over low heat until cheese is melted. Stir in Mushrooms, Green Beans and add 1/2 can of French's Onions.  Pour into casserole. Bake 350F until heated through (20-30 mins) Top with remaining cheese and French's onions and heat until Cheese is melted and onions are browned.
