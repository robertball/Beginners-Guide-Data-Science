Title:
Caramel Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/247284.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cold fat free milk
1 (1 ounce) package instant sugar-free butterscotch pudding mix
2 cups reduced-fat whipped topping, divided
3 1/2 cups chopped unpeeled apples
1/2 cup chopped salted peanuts

Directions:
In a bowl, whisk milk and pudding mix for 1 minute (mixture will be thick). Whisk in 1 cup whipped topping. Fold in apples. Refrigerate until serving. Sprinkle with peanuts.

Submitter: 
Dawn Harvey
Description: 
Kids of all ages will love the caramel apple taste of this easy-to-prepare dish. 'This salad could arguably be used as a dessert,' says Dawn Harvey of Danville, Pennsylvania. 'It's very pretty with red apples or a combination of red and green apples.'
