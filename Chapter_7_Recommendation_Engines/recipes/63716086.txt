Title:
Chicken a la King I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (4.5 ounce) can mushrooms, drained, liquid reserved
1/2 cup small cube carrots
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup green peas
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 teaspoons chicken bouillon powder
1 1/2 cups milk or evaporated milk
1 1/4 cups hot chicken broth
4 cooked, boneless chicken breast halves, chopped

Directions:
Cook and stir drained mushrooms, carrtos and onions in butter or margarine over medium heat for 5 minutes.  Remove from heat.
Blend in flour, salt, and pepper. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is bubbly.  Remove from heat.
Stir in green peas (frozen is fine), instant bouillon, milk, water, and reserved mushroom liquid.  Heat to boiling, stirring constantly.  Boil and stir for 1 minute.
Stir in chicken.  Heat through.

Submitter: 
Kilyena
Description: 
Easy and elegant chicken dish.  It's a great way to use leftover chicken or turkey.    Serve over cooked rice, toast, or noodles or puff pastry shells (found in the frozen section).  Can be made ahead and reheated.Modified from: Chicken a la King I
