Title:
Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 large fresh mushrooms
1/4 Cup sliced green onions
2 cloves minced garlic
1/4 Cup butter
2/3 Cup dry bread crumbs
1/2 Cup crumbles blue cheese
3 strips crumbled crisp cooked bacon

Directions:
Rinse and drain mushrooms.
Remove stems and reserve caps.
Chop enough stems to make 1 cup.
In a medium saucepan cook the chopped stems, green onions, and garlic in butter until tender.
Stir in bread crumbs, cheese, and bacon.
Spoon mixture into mushroom caps.
Arrange stuffed mushrooms in a 15x10x1 inch baking dish.
Bake at 425 degrees for 8- 10 minutes or until heated through.

Easy dish that looks complicated.  Impress all your friends with this delicious appetizer!
