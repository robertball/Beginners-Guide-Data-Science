Title:
Pumpkin Cake Easy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/581517.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups pumpkin puree
2 cups white sugar
1 cup oil
4 eggs
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1/2 tsp nutmeg
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp powdered ginger

Directions:
Blend everything together with mixer then mix in flour till incorporated pour into a 9x13 inch, greased or lined pan and bake in preheated 350 oven for 30 to 40 min or until a toothpick comes out clean.  Remove from oven and cover with towels, foil, or plastic wrap to keep the cake moist till it cools.  Frost and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Pamcancook
Description: 
Have made this cake over and over and never fails so tasty.  My family is very picky and ask for this cake all the time.  Awesome with canned cream cheese frosting or add chocolate chips and or walnuts for a lunch snack cake very adaptable recipe.
