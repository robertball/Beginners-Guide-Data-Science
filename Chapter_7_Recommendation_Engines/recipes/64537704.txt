Title:
"OOO-MAMI" Sweet & Spicy Dip & Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 Tbsp salsa verde
2 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp Sriracha
1 Tbsp fresh ginger, minced
2 Tbsp cilantro, finely chopped
2 Tbsp lime juice
1 Tbsp rice vinegar
2 Tbsp water

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a container with a tight fitting lid.
Mix until sugar is dissolved.
Best if  refrigerated for at least 1 hour before serving.
Store in tightly sealed container for up to 2 weeks.
Serve as a dipping sauce for pot stickers, eggrolls, or fried chicken. Use as a marinade for grilled meats and seafood - fantastic with chicken, pork, and shrimp.

Submitter: 
Jose Napoleon Cuasay Artiaga I
Description: 
A top secret ingredient (salsa verde) gives this fusion sauce a different element that will have you saying, OOO-MAMI!
