Title:
Taco Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
jar white cheese dip
1 pound ground beef
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix/use taco mix from this site.
1 can ranch style beans or pintos
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
8 ounces shredded Mexican-style cheese blend
1 (14.5 ounce) package crushed nacho doritos

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lay crescent dough flat on the bottom of a square cake pan and bake according to package directions.
Meanwhile, saute onion and garlic slowly in pan.  add the ground beef over medium high heat. Add the taco seasoning and stir together well. When dough is done, remove from oven and place white cheese dip, meat mixture,ranch beans, sour cream, mexican cheese and then top off with the crushed nacho chips.
Return to oven and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 10 minutes, or until cheese has melted.
watch that the chips don't burn, may need to use foil to protect them...

Submitter: 
Bossier
Description: 
A pre-baked crust of refrigerated crescent-roll dough is filled with a mixture of browned ground beef and taco seasoning layered with sour cream, shredded cheese, and crushed nacho chips. It is returned to the oven for about 10 minutes.Modified from: Taco Pie
