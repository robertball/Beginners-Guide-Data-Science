Title:
Banana Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 bananas, mashed
1 can frozen orange juice concentrate
1 can frozen lemonade concentrate
1 quart pineapple juice
4 cups sugar
6 cups water

Directions:
Mix bananas, juice concentrates, and pineapple juice.  Boil sugar and water together and add to  juice mixture.  Freeze until mushy.  When serving use 1 part frozen slush to 1 part 7-Up.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
This is my husband's FAVORITE!!!  Never had any left over any time I have made it!
