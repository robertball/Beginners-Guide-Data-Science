Title:
Philadelphia Creamy Chicken Florentine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
1/2 cup halved red pepper strips
1 tub (10 oz) Philadelphia Savory Garlic Cooking Creme
1 pkg (6 oz) baby spinach leaves
2 cups hot cooked penne pasta
2 TBSP toasted Italian pine nuts

Directions:
Cook chicken and peppers in latge non-stick skillet on medium heat 5 to 6 minutes or until chicken is done.
Add cooking creme; cook and stir 3 minutes.
Stir in spinach and pasta; top with nuts.
