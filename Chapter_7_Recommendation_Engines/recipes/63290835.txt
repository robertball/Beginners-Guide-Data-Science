Title:
Doggie Frozen Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/867308.jpg
Ingredients:
1 32 ounce size container plain yogurt
2-3 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
1 ripe mashed banana
2-3 tablespoons honey

Directions:
Mash banana with a fork in large bowl.  Add the yogurt, peanut butter and honey. Stir to mix thoroughly. Spoon mixture into cooking oil sprayed mini or regular size muffin tins or paper lined muffin cups. Freeze uncovered until set (about 24 hours). Unmold and seal frozen treats in ziplock bags. Keep frozen until served.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe was given to me by friends who have just become new dog owners. They are crazy in love with this dog and created this treat to save money and give their pet something wholesome. These can be made in mini or regular muffin cups depending on your dogs size.
