Title:
Jan's Cranberry Orange Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup white sugar
1 large egg, beaten
1/2 cup orange juice
grated rind of 1 orange
2 tablespoons melted butter
2 tablespoons hot water
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup raw cranberries well chopped, washed, stems removed
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Heat oven to 325*
Combing flour, baking powder, soda and salt in a large mixing bowl and set aside.
Mix beaten egg with the orange juice, rind, butter, water and vanilla.  Fold into flour mixture until blended.  Do not over mix.
fold in cranberries and pecans.
Grease and flour a 9x5 loaf pan.
Spoon batter into prepared pan.
Bake at 325 for approximately 60 minutes.  Bake until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.  Cool before slicing.
Wrap leftovers well and use within one week or freeze.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
Sweet and tangy bread rich with pecans.  Moist and delicious. Makes a nice presentation when sliced.
