Title:
Whole Wheat Rye, Cardamom & Walnut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup rye flour
1 cup water
2 tbsp honey
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp lemon juice
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp sugar
3 fresh cardamom pods
2 1/2 tsp dried / bread machine yeast

Directions:
Mix the yeast with the sugar and 1/4 cup of the water. Leave for 10 minutes until frothy.
Remove the seeds from the cardamom pods and grind into a powder.
Add all the ingredients into the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
Bake on whole wheat bread cycle.

Submitter: 
Shelley
Description: 
A wonderfully rich, delicious and aromatic bread. A favorite sliced loaf or great when baked as dinner rolls.
