Title:
Olive Garden Chicken Gnnochi Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup potato buds
3/4 tsp and 1/8 tsp salt
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 cup boiling water
1 egg yolk
2/3 cup flour
2 tbs veg oil
1/2 cup shredded carrot
1/4 cup diced onion
1/4 cup chopped celery
2 tbs cornstarch
4 cups chicken broth
4 cups water
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1 1/2 cups half and half
1 roasted chicken with meat removed and chopped
2 cups chopped fresh spinach

Directions:
1. Gnocchi:   mix potato buds with salt and nutmeg.  Stir in boiling water and continue mixing until no dry flakes remain.  stir in the egg yolk until the mashed potato mixture has an even pale yellow color throughout.
2. Stir in flour and knead for about a minute with your hands.
3.  Use 1/2 teaspoon to measure out small portions of the dough.  using floured hands roll the small dough portions around between the palms of your hands, making a ball, then press each ball down onto floured wax paper, making a small disk.  When all gnocchi is made you can make the soup.
4.  Heat up oil in large pot over medium heat.  add the carrots onion celery and garlic and saute for 3 minutes.  whisk the corstarch into the chicken broth and add to the vegetables along with the water.  when it begins to boil add the gnocchi.  Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes, then add 1/2 and 1/2 and simmer 10 to 15 min more or until soup begins to thicken.  add the chicken and spinach and simmer for 10 more minutes or until the soup has thickened and spinach is tender.
