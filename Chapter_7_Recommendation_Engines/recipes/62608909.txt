Title:
Provencal Chicken and Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/802677.jpg
Ingredients:
6 oz. bacon, diced 1 inch
1 (14.5 oz) can Italian diced tomatoes
1/2-3/4 cup of pearl onions
1 (15 oz.) Great northern beans, rinsed and drained
1 lb boneless thighs
salt and pepper
1/2 tablespoon kosher salt

Directions:
Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add bacon; cook about 5 mintes or until bacon begins to brown. Add onions; cook for about 3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until onions begin to turn light golden brown.
Add chicken thighs; cook about 6 minutes or until chicken is lightly browned on both sides.
Stir in remaining ingredients, scraping up any brown bits from bottom of skillet; heat to boiling. Reduce heat to low; cover and cook 10 minutes.
Uncover; cook 5-10 minutes longer until chicken is no longer pink in center and sauce is slightly thickened.

Description: 
Easy to prep and simple in taste. You may use chicken breast if you prefer. This can be baked as well.
