Title:
Brenda's Delicious Amazingly Easy Fruit Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 - 10  large peaches, peeled and sliced
2 baskets of blackberries  (can substitute blueberries)
1/2-3/4 c. of sugar (depending on how sweet the fruit is)
3 large serving size heaping spoonfuls of flour
a shake or 2 of cinnamon (not a lot)
Topping:
Melt 1 cube of margarine or butter
Add:  1 cup each of flour, brown sugar, old-fashioned oats
a sprinkle of cinnamon

Directions:
Turn on oven to 350 degrees
1.  Cut up fruit
2.  Place fruit in large (9x12) casserole dish
3.  Mix flour, sugar, cinnamon in a small bowl, then pour over fruit
4.  Gently stir fruit in pan to get mixture evenly throughout
5.  Melt 1 cube butter
6.  Stir oats, flour, sugar and butter together
7.  Crumble over the top of the fruit
8.  Bake for 45 min-one hour

Submitter: 
Julie Hill Alvarez
Description: 
Super easy, tasty way to cook up in season (or frozen) fruit in to a dessert that every one will enjoy.  Crust can be made ahead in a ziplock bag, then just add melted butter, smoosh around and crumble over top of fruit.
