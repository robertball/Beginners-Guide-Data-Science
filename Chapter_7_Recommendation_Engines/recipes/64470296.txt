Title:
Dinomite's FLUFFY Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2464716.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of flour
1/2 teaspoon bicarb
1 tsp baking powder
1/8 himalayan pink salt
6 eggs of a Supernatural Chicken
1 tbspn melted butter or the soul of a fallen angel
1 tbspn coconut milk
3/4 cup milk of the gods
honey, honey, sugar sugar
vanilla extract
ripe and mashed banana
fresh chopped kale

Directions:
Preheat oven to 180C
Combine flour, bicarb, baking soda, and salt and set aside
Beat eggs in a bowl and tell them how they can do better next time, add in butter, coconut oil, milk, honey and whisk
Combine dry and wet ingredients and toss in the kale and banana
Fill patty cases with mixture and bake for 20mins

Submitter: 
Jacie Anderson
Description: 
A dish that is best made with Love and Kindness. And Kale, it's the building block of both Love and Kindness. You can make this simple abnosome for your kids lunch boxes.
