Title:
Insalata Caprese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tomatoes
1 clove garlic
1/4 cup extravirgin olive oil
salt
fresh cracked pepper
3 buffalo mozzarella
1 handful chopped fresh basil

Directions:
Chop tomatoes and mozzarella balls into small pieces.
Mix in crushed garlic, olive oil, salt to taste, and fresh basil chopped.
Serve and enjoy!
