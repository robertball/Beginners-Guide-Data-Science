Title:
Low Fat Lemon Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6" cheesecake- 1 package of 1/3 less fat cream cheese (softened)
6oz greek yogurt
1 egg
juice and zest from 1 lemon
1tbsp homemade vanilla
1/3 cup sugar
crust pretzels, ground flax seed and enough melted butter to bind together- press into pie pan and bake 10 min before filling it

Directions:
combine ingredients and pour into crust. I baked mine at 350 for about 40 min. you could also make 12 mini cheesecakes with this recipe using a muffin tin. my crust was pretzels, ground flax seed and enough melted butter to bind. I baked it for about 10 min before filling it
