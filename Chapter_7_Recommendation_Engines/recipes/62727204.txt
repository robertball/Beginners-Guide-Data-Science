Title:
Chicken and Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp butter
1 tsp olive oil
1 pound of fresh green beans
2 cups of cubed chicken
2 slices of bacon
1/4 cup pistachios, shelled
1 clove garlic
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 Tbl parmesan cheese
1 Tbl fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Thoroughly clean the beans. Cut off the ends. Add beans to a large pot and cover with hot water. Once boiling, cook for 7 minutes, or until just cooked. Drain, set aside.
Melt butter and olive oil in a large skillet on MED-HI heat. Add chicken and cook through, about 10 minutes. Set aside.
Add bacon, cook until crisp. Crumble and set aside. Add pistachios, garlic, salt and pepper, beans, bacon, chicken, parmesan cheese. Cook until heated through, about 2 minutes.
Pour in lemon juice, stir and serve.
