Title:
Ham and Sweet Potato soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/969027.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups peeled and diced sweet potatoes
1 Tablespoon chopped garlic
1/3 cup diced celery
1 teaspoon each basil
& parsley flakes
1/3 cup finely chopped onion
Â¼ cup each corn and chopped carrots
3/4 cup diced cooked ham
1/2 cup chopped green or red peppers
5 cups chicken stock
1/2 teaspoon salt & 1 tsp pepper to taste
5 tablespoons butter
6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups milk
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Combine the sweet potatoes, celery, onion, ham and  chicken stock in a stockpot. Stir in the other vegetables and garlic and herbs. Bring to a boil, then cook over medium heat until sweet potatoes are tender, about 10 to 15 minutes. Stir in the salt and pepper. Stir in the other vegetables , garlic and herbs
In a separate saucepan, melt butter over medium-low heat. Whisk in flour with a fork, and cook, stirring constantly until thick, about 1 minute. Slowly stir in milk as not to allow lumps to form until all of the milk has been added. Continue stirring over medium-low heat until thick, 4 to 5 minutes. Stir in shredded cheddar cheese
Stir the milk mixture into the stockpot, and cook soup until heated through. Serve immediately.
