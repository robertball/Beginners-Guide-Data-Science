Title:
German Pancake Easy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large eggs
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup milk
2 TBSP sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp salt
2 TBSP butter
Powdered sugar
Jam or fresh fruit, for serving

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, combine eggs, flour, milk, sugar, vanilla and salt; blend until smooth. Let batter rest while oven preheats to 425 degrees. Place butter in a heavy, ovenproof skillet, place in hot oven for 1 -2 minutes to melt (do not burn). Carefully swirl pan to coat with butter; pour in batter. Bake for 18 - 20 minutes until pancake is puffy and deeply golden. Cool 5 minutes, then liberally dust with powdered sugar; cut into 4 wedges and serve with jam or fruit. Refrigerate any leftovers.
