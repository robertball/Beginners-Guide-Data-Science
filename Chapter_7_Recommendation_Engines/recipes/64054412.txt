Title:
chipotle tostados Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can black beans, rinsed and drained or 2 c. cooked salted black beans
green chiles to taste, chopped
1 c. roasted corn
1 onion, diced
2 canned chipotle peppers with adobo sauce (or to taste)
goat cheese or other
milk
6 corn tortillas

Directions:
Saute onions till tender. Stir in roasted corn and green chiles.
Heat black beans till hot.
Make chipotle cream sauce: mix goat cheese and milk to a creamy sauce consistency. Add salt. Stir in chipotles and some adobo. Heat to warm. Taste and adjust salt; add more chipotle if desired.  Alternately, make a small amount of white sauce and add pepper jack cheese and chipotles.
Brush oil on corn tortillas (both sides) and cook in a skillet till mostly firm.
To serve: spoon beans on tortilla, top with creamy sauce and veggies.

Submitter: 
Susie
Description: 
The chipotles give a nice, smoky flavor to these tostados.
