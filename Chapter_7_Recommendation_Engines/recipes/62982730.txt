Title:
Italian Garlic Knots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3to 4 cloves garlic, chopped
2tablespoons butter
2tablespoons olive oil
1teaspoon chopped fresh basil leaves
1teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
1can (11 oz) Pillsbury® refrigerated original breadsticks
Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
1 Heat oven to 375°F. In small microwavable bowl, microwave garlic, butter and oil on High 30 seconds or until butter is melted. Stir in basil and parsley; set aside. 2 Unroll dough; separate into 12 breadsticks. Twist and tie each breadstick into a “knot-like” ball; place in ungreased 9-inch round cake pan. 3 Spoon butter-herb mixture evenly over top of each knot. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. 4 Bake 13 to 15 minutes or until golden brown
