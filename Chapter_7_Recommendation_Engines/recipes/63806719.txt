Title:
Easy Low Fat Ranch Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1055709.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup low fat or fat free plain Greek yogurt
1 .4oz packet powdered buttermilk ranch dressing mix

Directions:
Mix the contents of the packet into the yogurt, stirring thoroughly to ensure even distribution of flavor and smooth consistency. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving for best flavor.

Submitter: 
Lee Crowell
Description: 
A super quick and easy low fat or fat free dip - tailor it to your needs by choosing your favorite plain yogurt and dressing mix.
