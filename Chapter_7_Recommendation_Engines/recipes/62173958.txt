Title:
Spicy Deep Fried Pumpkin Seeds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh pumpkin seeds
3 tb garlic salt
3 tb oldbay seasoning
1 tb crushed red pepper
1 qt peanut oil

Directions:
1.Preheat the peanut oil in deep fryer to 375
2.Pour pumpkin seeds into deep fryer. I used the baket to hold them down
3.Deep fry 10 mins
4.Use a slotted spoon to get the seeds out
5.Drain on paper towls or brown paper bag
6.Season with spices
7.Let cool and enjoy
