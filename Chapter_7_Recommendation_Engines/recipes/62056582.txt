Title:
GARLIC PARMESAN CHICKEN WINGS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6534847.jpg
Ingredients:
4LB CHICKEN WINGS
6 cloves minced garlic
1/2 CUP SHREDDED PARMESAN CHEESE
1/2 CUP SALTED BUTTER
SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE
2 QUARTS VEGTABLE OIL

Directions:
HEAT THE 2 QTS OF OIL AND DEEP FRY THE WINGS FOR APPROX 20 MINS.STIRRING THE WINGS OCCASIONALLY. WHILE THE WINGS ARE COOKING MELT THE BUTTER IN A SMALL SAUCEPAN. SAUTE THE GARLIC IN THE BUTTER ABOUT 6 MINUTES.PREHEAT OVEN TO 350. WHEN WINGS ARE DONE COAT WITH MELTED BUTTER PLACE IN FOIL LINED PAN AND SPREAD PARMASAN CHEESE OVER WINGS. BAKE AN ADDITIONAL 20 - 25 MINS AT 350. IF YOU DONT LIKE PARMASAN YOU CAN USE ANY OTHER SHREDDED CHEESE OF CHOICE! ENJOY!
