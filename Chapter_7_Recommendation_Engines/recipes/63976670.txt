Title:
Whole Wheat Sweet Potato Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c whole wheat flour
1/4 c coconut oil
3/4 cup applesauce or grated apple
1/2 c sugar
1 egg
2 c mashed sweet potatoes
2 t cinnamon
1/2 t nutmeg
1/2 t ground ginger
2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
1/2 t baking soda
Optional topping:
1/2 c chopped walnuts or pecans
2 T butter
2 T brown sugar
1/2 t cinnamon

Directions:
1. In a large bowl combine flour, spices, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
2. In another bowl whisk together egg, sweet potatoes and oil.  Add sugar.
3. Stir egg mixture into dry ingredients until just combined.
4. Pour mixture into greased or lined muffin tin.
5. If using, mix topping ingredients until they form a course crumb texture.  Spoon evenly over muffins.
6. Bake muffins in preheated oven at 400ยบF (approx. 200ยบ C) for 16-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
