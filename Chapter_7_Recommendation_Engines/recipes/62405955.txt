Title:
Red Kidney Bean curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 740 ml can of red kidney beans (or 2 cups of dried beans left to soak overnight)
1 medium tomato diced
1/2 onion
4 cloves of garlic diced
2 tsp ginger sliced finely in 1 inch strips
1 handful coriander or cilantro, chopped for garnish
2 tbls oil, vegetable, sunflower or canola
1/2 tsp chilli powder or cayenne pepper
1 tsp turmeric
2 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Heat  oil in a saucepan on medium heat.
Add cumin seeds, till they start to bubble
Add onion and garlic. Wait till softened
Add tomato.
When the mixture softens, add dry spices and stir. Wait for 5 minutes, allowing the spices to release their oils and flavour.
Add kidney beans and stir till evenly mixed with the above.
If using canned beans add 1 cup of water, if not, 2 cups.
Place lid on the pan, with a little gap for venting heat
Cook on low-medium for 15 minutes for canned beans, or 25minutes for soaked beans
Once the consistency of the liquid is that of broth, and the beans are soft yet firm, add ginger strips and coriander for garnish.
