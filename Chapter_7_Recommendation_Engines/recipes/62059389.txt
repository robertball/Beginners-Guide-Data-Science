Title:
hersheys deepdark chocolate cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1-3/4 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup hershey's cocoa
1-1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
2eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup boiling water

Directions:
1.Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour 2 round 9inch pans.
2.In a large mixer bowl combine dry ingredients. Add eggs,milk,oil and vanilla extract,beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.Remove from mixer,stir in bioling water(batter will be thin).Pour into prepared pans.Bake 30 to 40 minutes or until wooden pick inserted comes out clean. Cool cake for 10 minutes in pans, then remove from pans and cool cakes completely. best if frosted with one-bowl buttercream frosting.
