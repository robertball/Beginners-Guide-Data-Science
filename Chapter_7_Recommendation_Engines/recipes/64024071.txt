Title:
Beet-iful Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
for cake:
2 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
2 tbs unsweetened cocoa powder
3/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 cup coconut sugar
3/4 cup butter
3 eggs
2 cups beet puree
1 cup milk
1 tsp apple cider vinegar
1 tsp honey
for topping:
1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
3 tbs confectioners' sugar

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
line 2 24 cup mini muffin pans with paper liners
combine flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, salt in medium bowl.
in large mixing bowl beat the sugar and butter until light and fluffy. beat in eggs.
combine beet puree, milk, vinegar, and honey. whisk. slowly add to butter mixture, stir.
slowly add flour mixture, stir until just incorporated.
scoop batter into prepared pans. bake 20-25 minutes
while cupcakes baking, whip cream and sugar until soft peaks.
cool cupcakes and top with whipped cream
