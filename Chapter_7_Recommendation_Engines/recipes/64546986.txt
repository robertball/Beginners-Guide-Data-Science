Title:
Knorr Steak Fajita rice sides chili (vegetarian) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (14.5oz) cans beans. I used dark red kidney and pinto. i drained and rinsed the beans
2 (14.5oz) cans diced tomato (too much, but it didn't look like chili.
2 1/4 cup water (microwave directions for just the rice)
1 onion, chopped and cooked to almost brown
3 TB margarine (tub)
dash of cayenne pepper

Directions:
microwave the rice as the package says.
at the same time, cook the chopped onion in LOTS of butter/Margarine until just brown.
add in the beans and tomatoes
mix everything together and bring to a boil.
add the finished rice and mix everything together
