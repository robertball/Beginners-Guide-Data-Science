Title:
Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tomatoes, chopped into large pieces
1 cucumber, peeled, chopped into large pieces
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped into large pieces
1/2 red onion, chopped into large pieces
1 Tbsp cilantro, roughly chopped
1 Tbsp parsley, roughly chopped
1 small garlic clove, roughly chopped
1 cup tomato juice
1Tbsp olive oil
1Tbsp red wine vinegar
1/8 tsp paprika
1 Tbsp of Worcestershire sauce
Salt & pepper

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients in a blender or food processor, process until smooth
Season with salt & pepper to taste
Chill till ready to serve
Garnish with a shrimp or avocado if desired.

Submitter: 
BOGINIA2
Description: 
Great chilled vegetable soup (no bread) that I fell in love with on a trip to Spain.
