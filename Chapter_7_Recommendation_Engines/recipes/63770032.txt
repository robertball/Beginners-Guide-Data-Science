Title:
Spicy Chickpea Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 lb boneless chuck strips (marinated steaks for grilling)cut into small pieces
2 spicy Italian sausages cut into small pieces
1 sweet onion chopped
4 garlic cloves minced
6 mini peppers yellow, orange, and red chopped
2 Serrano peppers or jalapeno peppers chopped
2 tbls tomato paste
2 (14 oz) cans of chopped tomatoes
1 can chickpeas
3 tbls chili powder
1 tbls paprika
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tbls oregano
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Brown ground beef, steak and sausage in a large skillet over medium high heat until cooked through. Drain grease. Meats can be cooked separately.
Add meat to slow cooker along with, garlic, onion, peppers, tomato paste, tomatoes, chickpeas, chili powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, oregano and salt.
Cook for 1 1/2 to 2 hours on high or 3 to 4 hours on low until vegetables are tender.

Submitter: 
Liz Porteus
Description: 
Add this tasty, colorful chili to your menu during the winter months. It is relatively quick and easy to make. A variety of meats or vegetables can be added, like corn or potatoes. This chili is different and packs a fiery punch.
