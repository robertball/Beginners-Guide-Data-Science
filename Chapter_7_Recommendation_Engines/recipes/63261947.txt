Title:
Mom's Peach Stuff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c flour
1/2 c sugar
2t baking powder
2T butter
1/2c milk
3c peaches
1/2c sugar (or so)

Directions:
Mix the first 4 ingredients and place in a 9x13 greased pan.
Top with peaches mixed with sugar.
Bake 350* for 30 min.
Could add cinnamon, vanilla, nutmeg to the peaches
