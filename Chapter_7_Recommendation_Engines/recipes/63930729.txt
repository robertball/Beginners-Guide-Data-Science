Title:
Lower Sodium Option - Sea Salt! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sea Salt (rocks)
Blender
Salt Shaker

Directions:
Pour sea salt into electric blender and grind to a fine powder.  Pour desired amount into any salt shaker.  Use for anything you might use standard table salt.

Submitter: 
Mara C Manzer
Description: 
Have your salt and eat it too! Sea salt is VERY inexpensive and less sodium than standard table salt.
