Title:
Braised Chicken with Grapes in Mushroom-Sherry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp kosher salt
1 cup flour
1 tsp poultry seasoning
1 tsp smoked paprika (or regular paprika)
1 tsp  black pepper
1 tsp rosemary
1 tsp Italian seasoning
8 chicken thighs, bone-in, skin-on
1/4 cup cooking oil
6 tablespoons butter
8 ounces mushrooms, white button or cremini, wiped clean and quartered
I onion chopped
2 stalks celery
2 tablespoons flour
1 cup chicken stock
Â½ cup  sherry
1 cup seedless grapes, red or white halved

Directions:
1. Combine salt, pepper, paprika, poultry seasoning, and flour on a large plan and dredge  chicken thighs evenly all over with mixture.
2. Cook chicken in oil in dutch oven on both sides until brown, and them remove to plate.
3. Remove oil from dutch oven and wipe out any burned bits.
4. Melt butter in dutch oven, add celery and onion and cook until soft, add garlic and mushrooms and cook 1-2 minutes.
5. Add rosemary, Italian seasoning, chicken stock and sherry and bring to a boil, then reduce to simmer and add chicken and grapes to liquid
6. Place lid on dutch oven and cook on low heat for 30 minutes or more  until done.
Serve over pasta, rice, or cauliflower rice.

Description: 
Simple and elegant braised chicken dish, with grapes and mushrooms in a sauce made with chicken stock and sherry. Equally impressive for entertaining or on the table for the family.
