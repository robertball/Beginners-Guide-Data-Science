Title:
Sirloin Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Asian Toasted Sesame
1/2 lb sirloin, thinly sliced
3 cups frozen oriental stir-fry vegetables
2 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Heat dressing in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add meat and vegetables, stir-fry for 5 minutes or until meat is cooked through. Serve over rice.
