Title:
Chicken Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices of bacon, diced
1 pound chicken breast, bitesized
1 onion, chopped
9 oz mushroom, canned, drained
1 1/2 cup chicken broth
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 t salt
1/8 t paprika
2 T flour
8 oz sour cream
Hot cooked noodles

Directions:
Saute bacon pieces until scrips.  Drain (reserve 2 T of drippings), and set aside.
In drippings, saute chicken, onion, and mushrooms until done.
Add broth, garlic, salt, paprika, dash of salt & pepper, and the bacon.  Cover and simmer for 10".
Combine flour and sour cream mixture and mix until smooth.   Add to the skillet.
Bring to boil, cook and stir for 2" or until thickened.
Lay hot buttered noodles in baking dish, and pour chicken mixture over top.  Sprinkle with additional paprika then serve

Description: 
Diced chicken in a creamy mushroom sour cream sauce, poured over hot buttered noodles.
