Title:
pinto beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag-10 oz pinto beans
4 cups beef broth
2 cups water
1 can 10 oz diced tomatoes with creen chilies, with juice
1 onion
3 colves garlic, minced
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp cumin

Directions:
Soak beans over night in water (see directions on bag)
In the morning drain beans and add to crock pot. Add remaining ingredients. Stir till mixed well.
Cook on high 8 hours or until beans are tender.
Serve in a bowl topped with onions, cheese and sour cream!
