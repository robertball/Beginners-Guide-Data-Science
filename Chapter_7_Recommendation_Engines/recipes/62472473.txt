Title:
pumpkin risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup pumpkin or squash (cooked and mashed, or grated)
6 cups FRESH chicken stock
2 carrots diced
1 stalk celery diced
1/2 onion chopped
3 cloves garlic
2 cups torn fresh spinach
2 cups arborio rice
1 cup parmesan- grated
.5 pound italian sausage, browned and crumbled

Directions:
Heat the broth in a separate pan.
In a large heavy bottom pot, saute lightly the carrots, celery, onion and garlic.
Add the cooked sausage, spinach, pumpkin and rice. Stir.
Add the hot broth about a half cup at a time, stirring constantly.
Continue to add broth and stir until all broth is absorbed and rice is tender- about 20-25 minutes.
Add cheese, stir.
