Title:
Big Ray's Sweet Potato - Apple Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045629.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp olive oil
1 large onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped into 2" pieces
3/4 lb potatoes, peeled and chopped into 2" pieces
2 apples, peeled, cord and cut into 2" pieces
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup water
1/4 cup male syrup
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
nutmeg, for garnish if desired
apple chips, for garnish if desired

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large lidded pot over medium heat.  Ad onion;cook for 4 minutes.  Add garlic; cook for 1 minute.  Stir in both type potatoes, apples, chicken broth and water. Cover and bring to a simmer.  Let simmer 20 minutes.
Blend soup with immersion blender or in batches until smooth.  Return to heat.  Stir in maple syrup, salt and pepper.  Serve with nutmeg and / or apple chips if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
For a perfect slightly sweet soup to help usher in the change of seasons to autumn, this soup is nice and quick.
