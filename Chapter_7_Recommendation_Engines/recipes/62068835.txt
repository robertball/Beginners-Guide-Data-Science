Title:
Sweet Saucy Ham Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ham steaks
1/3 c. margarine or butter
3/4 c. brown sugar
2 Tbsp. milk

Directions:
Place margarine and brown sugar in large skillet over medium-high heat.  Stir occasionally,incorporating brown sugar into the butter.  Place ham steaks on top of mixture.  Cook 10-12 minutes, flipping steaks occasionally, until sauce gets very bubbly.  Add 2 Tbsp. milk (push steaks around to incorporate milk), cook 3-5 minutes longer.  Place steaks on plate and pour sauce on top.

Submitter: 
Lori G.
Description: 
Ham steaks cooked on the stove with butter and brown sugar which eventually turns into a sweet maple syrup sauce (great for dipping!).
