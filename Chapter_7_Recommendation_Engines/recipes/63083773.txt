Title:
SAUSAGE AND BEAN SOUP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 @ 14 oz pkgs	cheddar brats, cut in 1/4” slices
1 @ 14 oz pkg	Little Smokies, halved
1 @ 14 oz pkg	Chicken Sausage, cut in 1/4” slices
2	         green bell peppers, seeded and  deveined, sliced vertically and then cut in 1/2” pieces
3 cans	beans of choice, drained, liquid reserved
4 cans	beans of choice with liquid
1 can	diced tomatoes with green chilies, drained, liquid reserved
3 cans	diced tomatoes with green chilies with liquid
1 can	mild green chilies with liquid
1 can	chili con carne with beans
16-oz jar	red spaghetti sauce
1+1/2 tsp	garlic powder
1+1/2 tsp	salt
1+1/2 tsp	black pepper

Directions:
Preheat a 12-quart Nesco oven or your stove’s oven to 400º.
Place the sliced sausage in the Nesco pan or a large, deep roasting pan in your stove’s oven.
Cover the  pan, turn heat down to 350º and cook for 30 minutes, stirring once or twice.
When sausage is sizzling and starting to brown on the edges, stir in the bell pepper pieces.
Cook for 30 more minutes, stirring once or twice, or until the peppers start to soften.
Stir in remaining ingredients, cover pan and cook until heated through; turn heat down to 200 and cook for a couple of hours, stirring 3 or 4 times; turn off heat, uncover and let cool.
Place cooled pan in fridge and leave overnight.
The next day, place pan in oven, cover and heat on 200º for a couple of hours, stirring 2 or 3 times.
If mixture seems too dry, add some water or broth.

Submitter: 
Fishwrap
Description: 
I needed a large amount of soup, and I concocted this hearty, meaty recipe that used up ingredients I happened to have on hand.  It is perfect for cold winter nights, and it is a very forgiving recipe.
