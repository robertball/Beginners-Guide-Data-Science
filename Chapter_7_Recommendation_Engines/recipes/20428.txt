Title:
Cool Off Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/84966.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups strawberries, hulled
1 cup orange juice
1 (8 ounce) container strawberry yogurt
2 cups ice
1 banana

Directions:
In a blender combine strawberries, orange juice, yogurt, ice and banana. Blend until smooth.
