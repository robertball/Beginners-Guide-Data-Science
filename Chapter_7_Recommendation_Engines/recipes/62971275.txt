Title:
Stuffing bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Small box of Stove Top stuffing-any flavor.
Can of Chunky vegetable stew
1 lb of hamburg

Directions:
Cook hamburg and drain. Mix with vegetable stew and pour into a 9x9 pan. Prepare stuffing as directed and spread over beef and stew mixture. Bake at 350 for 20min.
