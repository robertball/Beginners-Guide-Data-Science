Title:
Mocha Truffle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
2 tsp instant coffee granules
2 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/3 cup cocoa
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
In microwave melt butter and 1/2 cup chips. Stir until smooth. Stir in coffee granules; cool for 5 minutes.
In mixer combine eggs, sugars, and vanilla. Add the chocolate mixture. Combine flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt; fold into sugar and chocolate mixture. Stir in remaining 1 cup chips.
Drop by round tspfuls 2 inches apart onto baking sheet. Bake in convection oven for 10 minutes or until tops appear slightly dry and cracked. Cool for 1 minute before removing to wire rack to cool completely.
