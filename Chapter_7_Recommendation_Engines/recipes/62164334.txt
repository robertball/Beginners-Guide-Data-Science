Title:
fettucini pasta with shrimp,broccoi,and lemon butter sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. of fettucini pasta cooked to package directions
1 tablespoon of olive oil
1/4 cup of finely chopped onion
2 teaspoons of finely chopped garlic
2 cups of frozen broccoli florrets
1 Lb.of uncooked shrimp peeled and deveined
1 stick of melted salted butter
1/2 cup of ricotta cheese
1/2 cup of parmesean cheese,divided
1 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon of salt...or to taste
1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice

Directions:
in a large skillet add the olive oil and heat up on medium heat
add the garlic and onions and sauteuntil transparent
add the broccoli florrets and cover for 6 minutes
add the shrimp to the skillet and saute until pink
add the butter,ricotta,parmesean,crushed red pepper,salt,and lemon
continue cooking until it starts to bubble then add your pasta to the skillet, toss it around a bit and serve on a platter..

Submitter: 
Karen Steinke Reilly
Description: 
this is really easy and taste awesome with a ricotta and parmesean cheese mixture along with a mild lemon and butter taste
