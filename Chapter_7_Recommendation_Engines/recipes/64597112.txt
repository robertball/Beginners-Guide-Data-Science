Title:
Mocha Frappuccino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup chilled cold brew coffee concentrate
1/3 cup whole milk or half-n-half
1 tablespoon granulated sugar
1 cup ice cubes, large
2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
1. Add to a blender jar 1 cup of ice cubes, ? cup of cold brew coffee concentrate and ? cup of whole milk or half-n-half, 1 tablespoon of granulated sugar and 2 tablespoons of chocolate syrup.
2. Put the lid on the jar and blend for about 30 seconds or until smooth.
3. (optional) To serve top with whipped cream and drizzle with chocolate syrup.
