Title:
Mango-Banana - Pear Fiber Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 banana peeled and sliced
1 mango peeled, pitted and cubed
1/4 to 1/2 pear sliced/cubed
1 heaping tbsp of cup greek yogurt
2 tbsp of flaxseed powder
1/2 tsp of stevia
Desired ice cubes for desired consistency

Directions:
Combine the banana, mango, pear, yogurt, ice, and powder in a blender; blend until smooth.
