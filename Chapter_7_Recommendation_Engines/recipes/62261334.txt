Title:
Buko's Wing Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbl spoons Butter/Margarine
2 tbl spoons Franks Red Hot
1 tbl spoon Huy Fong Schircha sauce (for more heat)
3 tbl spoons Ketchup
1 tbl spoon Sugar/Honey
2 tbl spoons Parmesean cheese

Directions:
Mix all ingridents together and Microwave for 1 minute. Taste and adjust heat and sweetness to your liking/intended audience.

Submitter: 
Holleta
Description: 
The Perfect Wing Sauce! Heat and sweetness can be adjusted to your liking.
