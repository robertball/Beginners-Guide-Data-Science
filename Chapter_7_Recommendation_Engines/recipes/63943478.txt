Title:
Megan's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
----Meatloaf----
2 lbs ground chuck
2 eggs
1 pkg Lipton dry French Onion Soup mix
3/4 cup quick oats
3/4 cup water
1/3 cup ketchup
----Sauce----
1/3 cup ketchup
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 tsp mustard

Directions:
Mix all meatloaf ingredients together and shape into a loaf form in a 9x13" casserole dish
Spread sauce on top
Bake at 350 for 1.5hrs

Submitter: 
Emily Bailey
Description: 
One of the best consistencies of any meatloaf recipe I've ever tried. The oats keeps it moist!
