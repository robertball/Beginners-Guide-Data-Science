Title:
Kisses Acorn Treats Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Kisses Acorn Treats:
Hershey Kisses
Mini Vanilla Wafer Cookies
Peanut Butter or Butterscotch Chips
Royal Icing or Decorator's Frosting

Directions:
unwrap the kisses
use the icing to "glue" the peanutbutter or butterscotch chip to the top center of one small vanilla wafer and the icing to "glue" a Hershey Kiss to the otherside of the wafer in the center...ediable acorns

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
make acorns out of peanutbutter chips and vanilla wafers and kisses so easy and cute
