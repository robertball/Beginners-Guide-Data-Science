Title:
Melba's Ham Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. ground ham
1/2 lb. ground pork
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup bread crumbs
1 cup milk
2 tsp. dry mustard
dash pepper
1/2 cup vinegar
1 1/2 cup brown sugar
1 TBSP mustard
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Combine the ground ham and pork thoroughly.  Add eggs, bread crumbs, milk, dry mustard and pepper.  Shape in a loaf.  Place in a 9x13 inch pan.  Combine vinegar, brown sugar, water and mustard in a saucepan.  Bring to boil.  Bake at 325 degrees for 1 1/2 hours.  Baste frequently.
