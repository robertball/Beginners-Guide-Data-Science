Title:
Amazingly Simple Pulled Pork Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 lbs) Boston Butt Pork Roast
1 (12 oz) can of cola
1 1/2 cup Apple Cider Vinegar
Crushed black peppercorn (optional)
1 1/2 cups Hickory and Brown sugar flavor BBQ Sauce
10 Deli Style Buns

Directions:
Pour Can of cola into Slow Cooker, Place Pork Roast in slow cooker, Pour Apple Cider Vinegar over the roast., Sprinkle pepper onto roast
Cook Roast on Low for 8 to 9 hours.
Remove roast from slow cooker and discard liquid. Put the roast back in the slow cooker, remove the string net. Separate meat using 2 forks. Once meat is shredded, add BBQ sauce 1/2 cup at a time untill desired consitency.
Cover and cook on low for an additional 30 to 45 minites.  Stir meat and serve on buns.

Submitter: 
IRISHGINA74
Description: 
This is BBQ Pulled Pork done Ohio Style! Just six ingredients and a slow cooker, you probably already have in the pantry. This is so delicious and so easy to make, it is perfect for large families, or small as it freezes wonderfuly.
