Title:
Sugar Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. Sugar
1/3 c. Flour
Pinch of Salt
1 c. Whipping Cream
Cinnamon
Pie Crust

Directions:
Heat oven to 350*.  Combine sugar, flour and salt and let sit to combine.  Mix in whipping cream and let sit until well blended (when stirred, mixture should not look grainy).  Pour into crust and bake for 45-60 minutes (pie is done when it bubbles on top).  Sprinkle cinnamon on top.
