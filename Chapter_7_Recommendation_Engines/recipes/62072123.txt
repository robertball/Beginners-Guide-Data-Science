Title:
Mamma Mia's  Italian Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 -16 oz Pkg Dry Pasta of Choice
1 – 7 oz Jar Roasted Red Peppers, drained and chopped
1 – Cup Pitted Kalamata Olives, drained and sliced
1 – 14 oz Cans Artichoke Hearts (water packed), drained and roughly chopped
1 Bunch Green Onions, diced
8 oz Fresh Mozzarella Cheese, cubed
1 - Pint Grape or Cherry Tomatoes (if using cherry, cut in half)
4 oz Sliced Hard Salami, cut into strips
4 oz Pepperoni, Cubed
8 oz (1/2 of a 16 oz Bottle) – Italian Dressing
8 oz (1/2 of a 16 oz Bottle) – Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing
1 Cup Shredded Parmesan
Ground Black Pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions on package.  Drain and rinse with cold water.
Transfer pasta into very large mixing bowl.  Add chopped red peppers, Kalamata Olives, Artichoke Hearts, Green Onion, Mozzarella, Tomatoes, Salami, Pepperoni and Garlic.  Toss to mix.
Add 8 oz of Italian Dressing and 8 oz of Balsamic Vinaigrette.  Toss to coat.
Sprinkle in Parmesan/Asiago blend and toss to incorporate.
Chill at least 3 hours before serving, tossing occasionally.
