Title:
Cauliflower Leek Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
3 leeks, cut into 1 inch pieces ( I used 1 leek and 1 yellow onion to save $)
1 large head cauliflower, chopped
3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
5 cups vegetable broth
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
1 can white beans drained and rinsed
1/4 cup light sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400*
Chop cauliflower, spread on baking sheet and spray with "Pam" type spray. roast in oven about 40 minutes stiring every 10 minutes until evenly browned but not burnt.
Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat, and saute the leeks, and onion,for about 10 minutes add garlic and continue cooking 3 minutes. Stir in the vegetable broth, beans and cauliflower and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 25 minutes.
Remove the soup from heat. Blend the soup with an immersion blender or hand mixer. Season with salt and pepper. pour in bowl and top with a spoonful of light sour cream.
