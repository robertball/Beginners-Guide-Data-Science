Title:
Raspberry Granola Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/293143.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 tbsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 cup milk
1 tbsp applesauce
1 egg
1/2 cup uncooked granola
1 cup whole raspberries
3 tbsp yogurt (optional)

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients and set aside.
Beat egg, milk, and applesauce.
Slowly add dry ingredients and beat well.
Pour on griddle one at a time, adding drops of uncooked granola and whole raspberries right to the uncooked pancake.
Optional: Mix yogurt with desired amount of raspberries and use as spread instead of butter or syrup.
