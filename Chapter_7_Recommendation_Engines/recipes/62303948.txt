Title:
Asian Coleslaw-redone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
1 tablespoons vegetable oil
2.5 tablespoons natural (no sugar added)creamy peanut butter
1.5 tablespoons soy sauce
1.5 tablespoons brown sugar Splenda
1 tablespoons minced fresh ginger root
1 tablespoons minced garlic
2.5 cups thinly sliced green cabbage
1 cups thinly sliced red cabbage
1 carrot, julienned
3 green onions, chopped
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk together the rice vinegar, oil, peanut butter, soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, and garlic.
In a large bowl, mix the green cabbage, red cabbage, carrots, green onions, and cilantro. Toss with the peanut butter mixture just before serving.

Submitter: 
Robin
Description: 
This was a great recipe but I needed to make it smaller and lighter.  I reduced the amount of oil, used splenda brown sugar blend, and used natural peanut butter. A big hit with everyone!Modified from: Asian Coleslaw
