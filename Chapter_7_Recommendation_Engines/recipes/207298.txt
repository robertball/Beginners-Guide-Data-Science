Title:
Kahlua Eggnog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990823.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces Kahlua
1 ounce vodka
Prepared eggnog

Directions:
Pour ingredients over ice in a rocks glass. Garnish with a cinnamon stick and/or mint sprig .

Submitter: 
Kahlua
Description: 
Kahlua and vodka make this eggnog a special treat for fall and winter gatherings.
