Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. ground beef, browned and drained
1 small onion, chopped
1 (1 oz.) package taco seasoning
1 (1 oz.) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
water to taste (optional)
1 (16 oz.) frozen corn
1 (15 oz.) can kidney beanes, undrained
1 (15 oz.) can tomato sauce
1 (15 oz.) can tomatoes with green chilies, undrained
Optional: corn chips, shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream

Directions:
In a soup pot, simmer all ingredients together for 20 to 30 minutes.
Serve with corn chips, shredded cheddar cheese and sour cream, as desired.
