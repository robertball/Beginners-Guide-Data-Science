Title:
Georgian Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs fresh green beans, trimmed
3 Tbl unsalted butter
1 large red onion in quarters, thinly sliced
1 1/2 tsp red wine vinegar
3 Tbl canned chicken broth
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 Tbl fresh cilantro, finely chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1  Drop beans into large pot of salted boiling water and blanch for 3 minutes.  Drain.  Run under cold water, drain thoroughly, and pat dry.
2  Melt 1 Tbl butter in skillet over medium heat.  Add onion and saute, about 3 min.  Drain.
3  Melt 2 Tbl butter in large skillet, add onions and green beans and saute about 5 min.  Stir in vinegar, broth, salt and pepper.  Cook for 10 min.  Add cilantro.
4  Simmer covered to desired tenderness, and serve.

Submitter: 
DOCTOR KITTEN
Description: 
Scrumptious and tangy greenbeans from the country of Georgia.  A great side dish with any meat.
