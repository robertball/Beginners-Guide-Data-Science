Title:
Buckeye Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. butter, melted
2 c. peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla
1 c. fine graham cracker crumbs
5 c. confectioners' sugar
3 c. chocolate chips
1/4 c. butter

Directions:
Mix together melted butter, peanut butter and vanilla.
Mix in graham cracker crumbs and then confectioners' sugar.
Press into foil-lined 9x13 pan.
Melt together chocolate chips and 1/4 c. butter.  Spread over peanut butter mixture.
Put in refridgerator to set chocolate.

Submitter: 
cicada77
Description: 
When you're craving Buckeyes but don't want to take the time to dip them.  These are a combo of peanut butter bars and buckeyes.
