Title:
Sixteen Year Old Banana Bread =] Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
A little less than 2 cups of flour
1/2 cup of light brown sugar
1/2 cup of granulated white sugar
4 bananas mashed
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon baking soda
3 eggs
1/3 cup chopped and toasted walnuts
1 stick of  butter
1 teaspoon salt
2 dashes of cinnnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven tO 350ÂºF and line bottom of three mini loafs or one 9x5 loaf pan with parchment paper. Grease the sides a bit.
Cut up butter until they resemeble crumbs and add in sugar all at once. Beat with a whisk until mixed considerably well.
Beat in mashed bananas, eggs, and then stir in vanilla. Mix well.
Sift flour, baking soda, and salt in a seperate bowl. Add to wet ingredients one third at a time.
Ladle batter into the pans and don't fill all the way to the top. Bake 50-60 for mini loaves, and 60-70 for 9x5 pans on middle rack.
Cover tops with foil until half way done, then let exposed but make sure the tops dont get burnt.
Cool in oven for ten minutes and remove from pans. Peel parchment paper off and cool off entirely.
Outside should be crispy and inside soft.
