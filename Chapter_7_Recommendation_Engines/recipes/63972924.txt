Title:
Mom's Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. beef, any cheap cut or stew meat
1 packet onion soup mix
2 c. water
1 small can mushrooms, drained
1/4 c. red wine
garlic powder
1/3 c. flour
sour cream
cooked egg noodles

Directions:
Trim and cube meat, cut into bite sized pieces.
Brown meat in a large skillet.
Add mushrooms and saute until warmed through.
Add water, wine, soup mix, garlic powder, salt 7 pepper to beef in saute pan.
Simmer, covered, until meat is tender.
Make a paste of flour and cold water.  Stir into liquid in pan slowly to thicken.
Add sour cream to taste.
Serve over cooked egg noodles.
