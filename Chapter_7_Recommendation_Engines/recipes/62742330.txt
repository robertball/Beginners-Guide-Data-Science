Title:
Pumpkin Spice Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box dry spice cake mix
3/4 pkg (12 oz.) of cinnamon chips
1 small can (15 oz)pumpkin
1 cup chopped pecans (optional)

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients thoroughly until you see no more dry cake mix.Stir in nuts, then spoon muffin cups 2/3 full. Bake at 375 degrees for about  25-30 minutes.  Yield 18 muffins.

Submitter: 
C. Davidson
Description: 
Rich, moist & spicy flavored muffin. Great for breakfast, brunch or dessert!
