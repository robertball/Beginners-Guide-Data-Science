Title:
BBQ Chicken Snacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984431.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup cooked, shredded chicken
2 tablespoons barbecue sauce
2 teaspoons thinly sliced red onion
2 Quaker® Lightly Salted or Salt Free Rice Cakes
1 tablespoon low fat shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Combine chicken, barbecue sauce and red onion in small microwave-safe bowl; mix well. Microwave on HIGH (100% power) for 30 seconds, or until heated through.
Divide mixture evenly between Lightly Salted Rice Cakes.
Sprinkle with cheese and cilantro.
