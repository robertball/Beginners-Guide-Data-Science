Title:
Pepperoni Pizza Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans tomato soup
3 cups water
2 cloves of garlic
1+1/2 cups elbow macaroni (or any other pasta)
2 green onions, diced
2 tsp dried oregano
1/3 cup diced pepperoni
1 cup shredded mozzarella
1 cup shredded cheddar

Directions:
Boil up the tomato soup, water, and garlic.
Stir in macaroni/pasta, green onions, oregano, and pepperoni.
Cook until the pasta is just about done.
Pour soup into oven-proof bowls (or a casserole dish).
Top with cheeses and oregano.
Stick it in your broiler for about 5 minutes.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
SINGLEGUYWHOCOOKS
Description: 
A buddy of mine LOVES all things pepperoni.  So why not dump a bunch into a tomato-based pasta soup and see what happens?  Broiled cheese on the top sets it off.  Be sure to serve with breadsticks!!!
