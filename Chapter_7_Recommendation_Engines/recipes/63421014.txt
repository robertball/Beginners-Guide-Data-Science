Title:
Annies Turkey Brine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Quart of apple cider
2 oranges, sliced
1 1/2 gal water
1 1/2 cup salt
2 cups of brown sugar
6 cloves of garlic, crushed and minced
6 bay leaves
3 T of rosemary
3/4 c of celrey leaves and stalks from the inside of a bunch
1 T of thyme

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and bring to boil, boil 5 mins, remove from heat. Let cool.
After Brine is cooled, place turkey in bag, add brine, then allow turkey to soak in the brine fo at least 24 hours.
Remove turkey from brine and pat dry before roasting.
