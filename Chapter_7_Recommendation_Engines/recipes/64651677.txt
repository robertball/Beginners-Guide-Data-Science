Title:
Smoked Salmon Platter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound sliced smoked salmon
1/4 cup sour cream
2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill
1 tablespoon capers, rinsed and drained

Directions:
Arrange salmon slices on large platter.
Mix sour cream and dill in small bowl to blend. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Spoon sour cream mixture in center of salmon or serve it in a bowl to the side.
Sprinkle capers over salmon and season with black pepper. Serve!

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
My easy smoked salmon platter is perfect for entertaining or Sunday brunch! Either way it will be a hit. This also follows the paleo diet!
