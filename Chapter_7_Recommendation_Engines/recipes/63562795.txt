Title:
Tuna Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
100g/3½oz tinned Tuna - drained
75g/2½oz Cherry Tomatoes - chopped
Handful fresh Basil - chopped
30g/1oz Jalapeno Peppers
Chunk Baguette - cut in half lengthways
60g/2oz Cheddar Cheese - grated
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.
Place the tuna, tomatoes, basil and peppers into a bowl and mix well;
Spoon the mixture onto the baguette, sprinkle over with cheese and season to taste;
Place onto a baking sheet then into the oven to bake for some 5 to 6 minutes or so, or until the cheese has melted;
Serve.
