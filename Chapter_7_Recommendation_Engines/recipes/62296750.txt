Title:
Basic Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa
2 cups water
1/2 onion, diced
1 bay leaf
2-3 cloves garlic, diced
1 tbsp Olive oil
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
sautee the onion and garlic in the olive oil until soft.
Add in the quinoa, water, and bay leaf and bring to a boil.  Cover and reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes, or until all the water is absorbed.

Submitter: 
Jessica
Description: 
This recipe makes an excellent base for dishes that normally go with rice.  If you want to eat it alone, add more veggies  and spices to taste.
