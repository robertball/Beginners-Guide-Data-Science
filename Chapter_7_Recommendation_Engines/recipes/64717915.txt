Title:
African peanut soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
butter and olive oil
chicken thighs
onion
garlic
ginger
nutmeg
1 t cinnamon
2 t curry
2t smoked paprika
2 t cumin
1 t garlic powder
1 t onion powder
sweet potato
carrots
collard greens
diced tomatoes
chicken broth
1 cup peanut butter
chopped peanuts
cilantro
hot sauce
corn chips

Directions:
Cook the onions in butter, add the garlic and ginger ( you can remove from pot) add chicken thighs and brown.
add carrots and sweet potatoes, everything but collards, peanuts and cilantro - corn chips of course.
Cook until the carrots are soft, add collards, cook for about 5 min. Add peanuts and cilantro - serve with chips.
