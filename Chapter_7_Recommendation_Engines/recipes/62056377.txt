Title:
Cream Cheese Muffin Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 tube (10 oz) refrigerated biscuits
1 3 oz. pkg. cream cheese, cut into 10 cubes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. In small bowl, combine sugar and cinnamon. In another bowl, combine butter and vanilla. Separate biscuit dough into 10 biscuits. Press each into a 3-inch circle. Dip cream cheese cubes into butter and then in cinnamon-sugar. Place one in center of each biscuit. Fold dough over cube; seal and shape into balls. Dip balls in butter and then in cinnamon-sugar. Place seam-side down in greased muffin cups. Bake at 375 for 14-18 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Submitter:

Description: 
Easy to make and delicious! Great for breakfast or even dessert. Especially good served with vanilla ice cream!
