Title:
Pumpkin, celery root and carrot salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1001607.jpg
Ingredients:
Celeriac or celery root raw -  1 cup shredded
Carrots raw- 1 -2 mid sized carrots
Pumpkin raw - 1 cup
Olive oil - 2 tablespoons.
Salt/pepper per your taste and wish.

Directions:
Nutrition:

Pumpkin , another main ingredient is also very useful: it is rich in antioxidants, such as vitamin-A, vitamin-C and vitamin-E Pumpkin is also an excellent source of many natural poly-phenolic flavonoid compounds such as ?, ÃŸ carotenes, cryptoxanthin, lutein and zea-xanthin. Preparation:
Preparation of this dish is very very simple. Shred all the ingredients (peeled and washed of course)
When shredded, mix the ingredients well, then add some salt/pepper if you wish. Add olive oil and your breakfast is ready. You can add some sesame seeds on top for a special taste.
Enjoy your salad!
