Title:
Calchones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2070186.jpg
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 16 oz can chickpeas,heat until hot,strain but save liquid,the mash chickpeas.
1 cup Hershey cocoa
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 ¼ cups sugar
Dough:
3 cups of flour
¾ cup sugar
3 eggs
¾ cup butter
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 tablespoons Crisco
3 tablespoons of water

Directions:
Mix filling ingredients all together using bean liquid to make a thick paste. Put in refrigerator to cool before stuffing calchones. Be very careful using bean liquid, you do not want the paste runny.
Mix all dough ingredients together. Add the tablespoons on water one at a time.Dough should be consistency of pie crust.
To cook,roll out dough and cut out 3 inch circles, a cup can be used. Add 1 teaspoon of filling to each circle. Fold dough over in half and seal with fork tongs and a little water.
Fry in ¼ inch oil. Drain on paper towels. Before serving sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Ursula
Description: 
This is a recipe handed down from my stepfather from Italy. They were only made around Christmas time but I love them so much I cheat and make them other special occasions too! So worth the work!
