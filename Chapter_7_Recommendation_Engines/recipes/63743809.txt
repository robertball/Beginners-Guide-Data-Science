Title:
Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 med onions cut into rings
1 1/2 cups milk
2 beaten eggs
1 1/2 crushed soda crackers (well seasoned)
Dash paprika

Directions:
Place onion rings in milk and let stand 15 min
drain
Dip in beaten eggs and coat with cracker mixture
Season with paprika
Cook in hot in oil

Submitter: 
Margaret Byrne
Description: 
Great side dish for steaks or burgers
