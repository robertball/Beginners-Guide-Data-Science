Title:
Chuck's Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
1 can refried beans
1 12 oz. bag Tortilla chips
1 onion
8 oz. Shredded cheddar cheese
Sour cream
Guacamole
Jalapeno slices
1 package (1.25 oz.) Taco Seasoning
1 cup water

Directions:
1. Dice onion and place in a small serving bowl.
2. Brown hamburger meat.
3. Add water and taco seasoning. Stir. Let simmer for about 3 min.
4. Heat refried beans in a sauce pan
5. On a plate, place a 3 oz. layer of tortilla chips, add about a quarter of a pound of the taco-seasoned hamburger, refried beans, and cover with about 2 oz. of the shredded cheese. Place in an oven preheated to 350 degrees for about 7 min. until cheese is melted. Alternately, you can place in a 1200 watt micro wave oven on high for about 1 min.
5. Top plate with diced onions, a dollop of sour cream, a dollop  of guacamole, and sprinkle with jalapeno slices. Serve.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Livermore
Description: 
This is a quick and tasty treat from south of the border. Serves 4.
