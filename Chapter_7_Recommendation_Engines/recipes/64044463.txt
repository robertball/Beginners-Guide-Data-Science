Title:
Banana/Peanut Butter Smooth Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen banana
2 tablespoons peanut butter powder
1 cup almond milk/sugar free
1 tablespoon raw cocoa powder
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
blend all together until smooth with ice cubes till desired texture

Submitter: 
Barbara
Description: 
Love this and the powdered Peanut Butter only has 45 calories in it as opposed to using regular peanut butter which has over 200 calories per serving
