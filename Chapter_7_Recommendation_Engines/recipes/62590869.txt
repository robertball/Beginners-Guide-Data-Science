Title:
Nana's Shrimp and Grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups water
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup quick-cooking grits
1 tablespoon butter
2 teaspoons olive oil
1 1/2 pounds fresh shrimp, shelled and deveined without tails
1 can of Campbell's Cream of Shrimp soup
1/2 cup water (may add more to taste) no less
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a saucepan, bring the water and salt to a boil. Stir in the quick grits and reduce the heat. Cook slowly for 5 minutes. Simmer on low to keep warm. In a skillet, heat the butter and oil over medium heat. Saute the shrimp until pink. Stir in soup and one half cup water cook until soup is heated through. Remove from heat and salt and pepper to taste.
Place grits in a bowl and top with shrimp mixture!
