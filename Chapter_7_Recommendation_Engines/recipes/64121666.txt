Title:
Hot Peanut Butter Fudge Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can evaporated milk
3/4 c. peanut butter
2 Tbs brown sugar
1/4 c. honey

Directions:
1. Mix all ingredients together in a medium sized pot.
2. Cook on medium-high until thickened, stirring frequently while paying attention to sides and bottom of pot.
3. Remove from heat. Serve warm poured over ice cream or with a fresh banana on toast.

Submitter: 
tiffany.renee
Description: 
Tastes like peanut butter fudge. An easy, quick sauce or topping. I just whipped this up one night on a whim and I liked how it turned out. I used evaporated milk because it's what I had on hand, but it turned out to be a good thing!
