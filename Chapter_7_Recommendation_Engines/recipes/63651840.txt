Title:
the best banana pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package low fat cream cheese
1 (cup) sour cream
2 small packages instant fat free sugar free banana pudding mix
3 cups cold skimmilk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
4 bananas, sliced
1 (12 ounce) package vanilla wafers

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy.  Beat in sour cream, pudding mix, and cold milk until smooth. Reserve 15 whole wafers for top of pudding.
Line the bottom of a 9x13 inch dish with vanilla wafers. spread approx 1/3 of mixture on top; line sides of dish with wafers. Arrange sliced bananas evenly over pudding. blend whipped topping with remaining pudding mixture and put on top. place reserved wafers on top (crumble any remaining wafers and sprinkle on top. Chill overnight.
