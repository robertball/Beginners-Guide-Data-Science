Title:
Black Bean Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15oz. can of black beans
2 tsp of lemon juice
1 tbs. of dried basil leaves
1 tsp of garlic powder

Directions:
Open black beans and drain well. There should be no black bean "juice". Pour black beans into food processer. Add lemon juice, basil, and garlic powder. Blend until a thick paste forms. Scoop out into a bowl and enjoy with some chips! This recipe creates about 1 and 1/2 cups of hummus.

Submitter: 
thepurplebaker
Description: 
This is a delicious and easy hummus to make. It's great to replace the meat in sandwiches with this hummus--it gives the sandwich great flavor.
