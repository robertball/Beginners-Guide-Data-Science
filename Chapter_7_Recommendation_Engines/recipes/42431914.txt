Title:
Spinach Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-10oz pkgs. frozen spinach, thawed and drained
1 stick butter
3 eggs, beaten
1 t salt
1 t ground thyme
1 C biscuit mix
garlic powder to taste
1 # shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Spread into greased 9 x 13in pan. Bake 350 x 30 min. Cut into squares.
