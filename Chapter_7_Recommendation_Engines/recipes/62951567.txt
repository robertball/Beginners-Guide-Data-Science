Title:
Sweet N Sour Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/726371.jpg
Ingredients:
14 Turkey bacon strips, diced
2 large onions, halved and thinly sliced
1 cup thinly chopped carrots
3/4 cup packed Splenda brown sugar
3/4 cup cider vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
2 tablespoon butter
2 teaspoon lemon pepper
1 teaspoon ground turmeric
1 1/2 teaspoon ground mustard
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 (28 ounce) can baked beans, undrained
2 (16 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1 (15.5 ounce) can pinto beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can lima beans, drained
2 (15.5 ounce) can black beans, drained
1 (15.5 ounce) can white beans, drained
1 (14.5 ounce) chicken brot
1 (4 ounce) mild diced ortega chiles, undrained
optional - I had onion cheese from costco & added it to the crock pot (approx 3/4 cup) to crockpot

Directions:
In a large deep skillet, cook bacon over medium heat until crisp. Remove with slotted spoon to paper towels. Wipe pan clean. Saute onions, carrots in with extra virgin olive oil & butter until tender. Add brown sugar, vinegar, salt, mustard, garlic powder, lemon pepper, salt & turmeric . Bring to a boil.
In a 6-qt. slow cooker, combine beans, ortega chiles and chicken broth. Add onion, carrots mixture and bacon; mix well. Cover and cook on low for 5 hours or until heated through.
