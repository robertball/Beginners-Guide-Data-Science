Title:
Crash Hot Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 small round potatoes
Virgin Olive Oil
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp cumin or caraway seeds
1 tbs Chopped fresh thyme or rosemary
Cracked Pepper

Directions:
Pre heat oven 250 degrees
Boil potatoes in salted water
Simmer 15 minutes.  Just cook, don't soften.
Drain and transfer to lightly greased baking tray.
Squash potaotes flat.
Brush tops with oil, sprinkle with salt, pepper & thyme
Bake on top shelf 20 minutes until crisp.

Submitter: 
CZARDAS
Description: 
This is a great alternative to roast potatoes.  So simple but impressive. You can use a potatoe masher to squash them down or any heavy implement. Just give them a good hit once.
