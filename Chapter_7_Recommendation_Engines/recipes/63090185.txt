Title:
Chinese Lo Mein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/782143.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. Spaghetti (pre-cook)
1 Chicken Breast (slice into thin strips)
1 Tbsp. Cooking Wine (White)
1 tsp. Corn Starch
1 Tbsp. Sugar
1 tsp. Fish Sauce
2 Tbsp. Hoisin Sauce
1 Tbsp. Oyster Sauce
1 tsp. Soy Sauce
1 tsp. Garlic
1 tsp. Ginger
Broccoli Florets
Onions (White diced)
Green Onions (entire green onion)
Carrots
Cabbage (optional we do use it)
Bean Sprouts (optional)
Toasted Sesame Seeds (optional)
Chilli Sauce (optional)

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan bring water to boil add spaghetti and cook till done.  Remove from heat, drain and place in cool water & set to the side.
In skillet heat ginger & garlic.  Add Chicken and cook till 75% done.  Add broccoli, white and green onions, carrots and cabbage.
Add Chilli sauce to taste if you choose to use it, we do not.
Add bean sprouts, and garnish with toasted sesame seeds and serve
