Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons butter
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
2 cups chicken stock
1 onion, finely diced
2 (10 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough, torn into pieces

Directions:
Place the chicken, butter, soup, and onion in a slow cooker, and pour chicken broth over mixture.
Cover, and cook for 5 to 6 hours on low. About 90 minutes before serving, place the torn biscuit dough in the slow cooker and be sure and push down into the liquid. Cook until the dough is no longer raw in the center.

Easy, creamy chicken with delicate dumplings made from refrigerator biscuits, slow cooked to comfort food perfection.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings
