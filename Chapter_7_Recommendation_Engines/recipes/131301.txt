Title:
Lift Your Mood Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon tomato paste
1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
2 teaspoons sunflower oil
1 cup baby spinach, rinsed and dried
1 cup romaine lettuce, chopped
1/3 cup garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
2 tablespoons pine nuts
2 tablespoons sliced roasted red peppers
1/3 cup grape tomatoes, halved
1 strip pre-cooked microwaveable bacon

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine tomato paste, vinegar, and oil.  Set aside.
In a large bowl, toss the remaining ingredients together.  Drizzle with dressing.  Serve immediately.
