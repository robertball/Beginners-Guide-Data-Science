Title:
Crunchy Broccoli Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 Cup mayonnaise
1/8 Cup sugar
2 T red wine vinegar
1 T white vinegar
1/8 cup smoked almonds, chopped
1/4 cup raisins
1 10oz. bag California Slaw or Broccoli Slaw

Directions:
Add mayonnaise, sugar and vinegars together in a large bowl.  Stir with fork until well blended.  Add remaining ingredients and gently toss.  Cover and refrigerate until use.

Submitter: 
DuffMan
Description: 
A crisp and crunchy slaw, easily made with packaged vegetable slaw.
