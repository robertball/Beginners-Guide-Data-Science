Title:
Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/995899.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Stick Butter
1 Stick Oleo
1 C. Sugar
3 Tbsp. Water
3-4 Hershey Bars
1 C. Chopped Pecans

Directions:
Boil and stir continually over very high heat until tan.  It will appear burned on bottom of pan.  Pour out quickly onto cookie sheet and spread broken chocolate over top.  Top with chopped pecans if desired.
Refrigerate
