Title:
Oriental Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8-oz can crushed pineapple in natural juice
4 oz sliced water chestnuts
1 c cooked, cubed chicken
2 T slice green onion
Oriental Dressing:
1 T mayonnaise
1 tsp honey
1/2 tsp soy sauce

Directions:
Drain pineapple.  Sliver the water chestnuts.  Mix all ingredients except the dressing.  Refrigerate.
Dressing:  Mix ingredients; pour over chicken salad and stire to moisten.
