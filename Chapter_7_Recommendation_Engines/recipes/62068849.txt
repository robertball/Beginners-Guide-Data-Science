Title:
Ranch Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 saltine crackers
1 cup dry parsley
1/2 cup dry onions
2 Tablespoons thyme
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon onion salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup buttermilk and
1 Tablespoon Ranch Seasoning

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients in a blender until finely chopped.  This is the seasoning.  To make a Ranch dressing, mix 1 Tbls. of seasoning with buttermilk and mayonnaise.

Submitter: 
BEARNESTA
Description: 
A dry mix you can use to make ranch dressing or seasoning for meat and potatoes.
