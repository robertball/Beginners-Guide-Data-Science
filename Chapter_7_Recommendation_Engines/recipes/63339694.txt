Title:
Grandma's Wonderful Picnic Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887745.jpg
Ingredients:
cake:
1 c sugar
2 c flour
1/2 t salt
1 t soda
2 t baking powder
2 t cinnamon
1/4 t cloves
2 eggs
1 c salad oil
1/2 c nuts
1/2 raisns
2 c old fruit drained blended (or apple sauce) (or cottage cheese)
topping:
1/2 c brown sugar
1 c choc chips
1/c nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease 13x9 inch pan.  Combine sugar, flour salt, soda baking powder and spices and set aside. Beat eggs and salad oil until creamy.  Add fruit and dry ingredients and beat until smooth. Add nuts and raisins. Pour into a 13 x 9 inch pan.  Cover with topping mix and bake for 30 to 35 min or until knife stuck in the center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Cheryl King
Description: 
Grandma was about the best baker I've ever known.  This is the cake she brought to every picnic or family reunion.  It can't be beat.
