Title:
Baked Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz box of Elbow Macaroni
2 Cups of Mild Cheddar Cheese
1/2 Cup of Parmessean Cheese
1 can of Evaporated Milk
1/2 Cup Butter
1/8 Cup of Sugar

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350 degrees
Prepare Macaroni according to Directions (It's better to prepare al dente or slightly undercooked)
Prepare a Casserole Dish (Butter, non-stick cooking spray, etc..)
Once Macroni has finished Cooking Drain and return to pot
Add all ingredients to the Macaroni and Mix well.
Pour Macaroni Mixture into Casserole Dish
Cover and bake for 30-45 minutes when cheese is melted and is bubbling
Uncover and bake for 10-15 (this is where you can add a layer of cheese to the top if desired)
