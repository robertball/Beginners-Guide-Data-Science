Title:
Ed's Basic Deli Chicken Breast Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4oz Chicken Breast Deli Sliced
2 Pieces of Potato Wheat Bread
1 Slice Cheddar Cheese
1 Leaf of Lettuce
2 Slices of Tomato
1 slice of onion
1tsp Spicy Brown Mustard
2 slices avocado

Directions:
1. Toast Slices of bread
2. Spread mustard on bread halves
3. Stack all ingredients on bread slice in half
