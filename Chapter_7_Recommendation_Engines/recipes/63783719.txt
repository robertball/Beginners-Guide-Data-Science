Title:
Chicken & Wild Rice Soup Fall 2013 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. wild rice
4 c. fat-free, low-sodium chicken broth (to cook the rice)
1/4 c. (heaping) finely chopped sweet onion
2 large garlic cloves, minced
3 c fat-free, low-sodium chicken broth
1 1/2 -2c cubed baking potato (1 large baker)
3 c low-fat milk
1/3 c. all purpose flour
10 oz light processed cheese, cubed (such as Velveeta Light)
2 -4c  chopped roasted skinless, boneless chicken (Costco Roasted chicken works great when in a time crunch)
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper (or to taste)
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 c chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Directions:
Bring rice and 4 c. chicken broth to boil; turn down to simmer and cook x 50 minutes until rice splits open.
Heat a large dutch oven over medium-high heat.
Coat with small amount of olive oil.
Add onion; sweat on low 2-3 minutes.
Add celery; sweat on low 3-4 minutes.
Add minced garlic.  Saute' 3 minutes.
Add 3 c. chicken broth and potato.
Bring to boil over med-high heat.
Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 5 minutes or until potato is tender.
Combine milk and flour, stirring well with a whisk.
Add the milk mixture to potato mixture.
Cook 5 minutes or until slightly thick, stirring constnatly.
Remove from heat; add cheese, stirring until cheese melts.
Stir in rice (and any broth left with the rice), chicken, pepper and salt.
Garnish with parsley, if desired.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Using little fat and low calorie ingredients this becomes a hardy satisfying soup to serve with freshly baked bread and a crisp green salad.
