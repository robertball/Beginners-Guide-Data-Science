Title:
Spanish Rice in the Pressure Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 CUPS LONG GRAIN RICE (SUCH AS UNCLE BEN'S)
1 3/4-2 CUPS BROTH, WATER OR V8
1/4 CUP SALSA
15 OZ ROTEL W/ JALAPENO PEPPERS
1 LARGE ONION CHOPPED
1 SMALL GREEN PEPPER CHOPPED
1 1/2 TSP GARLIC POWDER
1/2 TSP CHILI POWDER OR TO TASTE
SALT & PEPPER

Directions:
Place all ingredients in pressure cooker. Use rice setting (may be 10 min depending on the pressure cooker).
Allow pressure to cool down naturally.
If rice is still to dry add some more broth or V8
