Title:
Black Forest Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9" x 13" pan of baked & cooled brownies
1 250g cream cheese
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/3 cup whipping cream
1 can cherry pie filling
2 squares semisweet baking chocolate

Directions:
Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla till smooth.
Beat cream till stiff; fold into cream cheese mixture. Spread over brownies.
Top with pie filling.
Melt chocolate in micro. on med. until smooth.
Drizzle over brownies.
Refrigerate for at least 10 minutes, till chocolate is set.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Our favorite brownies, topped with classic black forest additions.
