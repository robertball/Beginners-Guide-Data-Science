Title:
CHEAPIST CREAM CHEESE BROWNIE PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/727950.jpg
Ingredients:
1 PACKAGE (8OZ.)CREAM CHEESE,SOFTENED
3 TBSP. SUGAR
1 TSP. VANILLA
1 EGG
1 (15.8) BOX OF BROWNIE MIX
1/4 CUP VEGETABLE OIL
1 TBSP. WATER
2 EGGS
TOPPED W/COOL WHIP & OR NUTS (OPTIONAL)

Directions:
HEAT OVEN TO 350*F
CREAM CHEESE LAYER-in a medium bowl,beat cream cheese,sugar,vanilla and 1 egg with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth(set aside)
BROWNIE LAYER-in a large bowl place brownie mix,oil,water and 2 eggs,beat 50 strokes with spoon.
SPRAY A 9-INCH GLASS PIE PLATE,WITH NON-STICK SPRAY WITH FLOUR.
SPREAD 1/2 OF BROWNIE MIX ON BOTTOM OF GLASS PIE,THEN SPREAD CAREFULLY 1/2 OF CREAM CHEESE MIXTURE ON TOP OF BROWNIE LAYER,THEN REPEAT,REST OF BROWNIE MIXTURE&THEN THE REST OF CREAM CHEESE MIXTURE.
