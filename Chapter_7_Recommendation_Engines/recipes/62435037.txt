Title:
Saffron CousCous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two pinches (about 5 - 7 threads) of saffron
2 tablespoons warm water
1 cup cous cous
1 cup vegetable, chicken, or beef stock
Scant 1/4 cup of currants
1 celery stalk, diced
1/2 teaspoon of cumin
2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
Harissa to taste OR ground red pepper OR red pepper flakes
1 tablespoon lemon juice
Sea salt to taste

Directions:
Combine the saffron and the warm water.  Set aside.
Combine the stock and the couscous in a medium sauce pan.  Bring the mixture to a boil, remove from heat, cover and set aside for five minutes.  Uncover and fluff with a fork.  Transfer to a serving bowl.
Add the saffron mixture, the currants, the celery, the cumin the olive oil, the harissa (or whatever hot red spice you are using) and the lemon juice.  Add sea salt to taste.  Toss to combine.  Chill until serving time.

Submitter: 
toastyfrenchy
Description: 
A light side dish, or a great lunch box salad.  The addition of saffron makes it just interesting enough!
