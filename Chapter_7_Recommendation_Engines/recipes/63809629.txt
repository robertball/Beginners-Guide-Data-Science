Title:
Delish angel hair pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1056468.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded chicken breast, cooked, and seasoned with seasoned salt.  1 cup diced mushrooms, 2 cups chopped spinach, 1 cup cherry tomatoes,- halved, 1 yellow onion, chopped, 2 cloves of garlic, chopped, 1tbsp salt, 2tsp black pepper, 1 cup chicken broth, dash of balsamic vinegar, 1/4 cup zesty Italian dressing, 8 oz chives and onion cream cheese, 1tbsp olive oil, 1 pkg angel hair pasta, boiled, 3 tbsp capers, chopped, shaved fresh Parmesan cheese.

Directions:
Boil noodles as directed. Drain and rinse under cold water and set aside. In a large and deep sauce pan, add olive oil on medium high heat. Saut√© onions, garlic, and mushrooms til softened. Add balsamic vinegar, and chicken broth. Let simmer on medium-low heat until thickened. Add salt and pepper.  Add chicken. Mix well. Add dressing and cream cheese. Stir until melted into sauce. Once sauce is bubbly, add chopped spinach, tomatoes, and capers. Stir well. Let cook for about 20 minutes and then take off the heat. Put pasta into a large bowl and pour sauce over pasta. Mix well until pasta is well coated. Serve into bowls and top with Parmesan cheese. This pasta is delish and comforting. My family loves it!
