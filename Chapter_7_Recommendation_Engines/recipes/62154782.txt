Title:
Easy Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups Bisquick baking mix
3/4 cup 7-Up
8 ounces sour cream
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 425
In a large bowl mix Bisquick, sour cream and 7-Up together.
Melt butter in a pan.
Roll out the biscuits and dip them in the melted butter. Place biscuits in a pan and pour the remaining butter over them.
Bake for 15 minutes.
Serve Warm

Submitter: 
Great Sommelier
Description: 
Easy biscuits for you to whip up for the family.  These are always a hit at my house.
