Title:
Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 lbs ripe tomatoes, peeled and chopped
2 med. onions, chopped
1/4 cup vinegar
1 tsp. dried crushed chilies (crushed red pepper)
1 1/2 tsp. table salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 x 114 ml canned chopped green chilies
1 tsp. sugar
1 1/2 tsp. paprika

Directions:
Combine all ingred. in large pot. Bring to boil on med.-high heat, stirring often. Boil gently until thickened. This will take about 1 1/2 hrs. Pour into hot sterile jars to within 1/4" of top. Seal.
This may also be frozen in containers.
Makes 3 pints
