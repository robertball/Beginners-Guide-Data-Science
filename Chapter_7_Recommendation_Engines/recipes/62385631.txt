Title:
Healthy French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices whole or multigrain bread
2 eggs
4 packets of splenda
3/4 tsp Cinnamon
1/2 tsp Nutmeg
1/2 cup Almond milk

Directions:
Whisk together all ingredients except bread. Pour mixture into a flat bottomed pan. Lay bread slices in mixture and allow to soak for 2-3 minutes. Flip bread and allow to soak for another 2-3 minutes. Fry bread over medium heat, flipping frequently, until golden brown on both sides, usually about 5-7 minutes. Serve with your choice of topping.

Submitter: 
Cheri Roman
Description: 
A healthier take on French toast with a nutty flavor and a fast finish.
