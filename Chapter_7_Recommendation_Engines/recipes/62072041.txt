Title:
Fantasy coffee cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 Yellow cake mix
4 eggs
2/3c. oil
8oz. sour cream
1C. brown sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
Icing:
2C. powdered sugar
4 Tbsp. milk
1 Tbsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325o
Spray 9x13in. baking dish, set aside.
Mix together brown sugar and cinnamon and set aside.
In large bowl mix Cake mix, eggs, oil, and sour cream until well combined.
Pour 1/2 of the batter into prepared baking pan, sprinkle with all but 1/4c. of sugar/cinnamon mixture.pour remaining batter over top of the sugar. sprinkle remaining 1/4c. of sugar mixture over the top. Take a butter knife and run through cake batter in swirl pattern.
Bake in oven for aprox: 40 min.
While cake is baking prepare icing combine all ingredients well. Pour over cake while still hot.  Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
ReneeW.
Description: 
This cake smells so good baking that it's hard not to start eating it the second it comes out of the oven. Pair with a good cup of coffee or a glass of milk and you cannot go wrong!!
