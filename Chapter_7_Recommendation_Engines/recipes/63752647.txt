Title:
Icing Alternative Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Instant Pudding Packet (Any Flavour)
1/2 the amount of milk called for on the pudding mix
1 - 8 oz cool whip

Directions:
Mix instant pudding packet with milk.
Whisk until it begins to thicken.
Then fold in 1 8 oz container of Cool Whip.

Submitter: 
Jeff Harrell
Description: 
A great frosting spread on cakes and piped onto cupcakes, a tasty filling in crepes or on waffles along with some fruit. much less sugar too!
