Title:
Reindeer munch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of Rice Chex cereal
2 cups of thin pretzel sticks
1 cup of cocktail peanuts
1 cup of M&M's
12 oz. vanilla flavored candy melts

Directions:
1.  In a large bowl combine the cereal, pretzels, peanuts and M&M's.
2. Melt white chocolate and drizzle over mixture.
3. Spread mixture onto large piece of foil and let stand for 30 minutes. Break into small bite sized pieces.
Makes 12 cups

Submitter: 
Melinda Johnson
Description: 
Easy and fun to make this snack with the kids. Ideal replacement to the cookies for Santa!
