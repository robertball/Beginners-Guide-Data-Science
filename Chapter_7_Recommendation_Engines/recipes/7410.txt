Title:
Wedding Cake Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/185860.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup shortening
1/2 cup milk
2 teaspoons clear imitation vanilla extract
8 cups confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Cream the butter and shortening together until smooth. Gradually add the sugar, milk, and vanilla extract.  Mix until smooth.
