Title:
Orange/Fruit Julius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 cup water
1/2 can of frozen juice concentrate
2 Tbsp. sugar
2 tsp. vanilla
12 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in blender, add ice cubes last and blend until smooth.
