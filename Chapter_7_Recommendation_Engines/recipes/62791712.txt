Title:
Trishie's "Pirates of the Caribbean" Grilled Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/662147.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fresh Pineapple
1 fl oz Captain Morgans Rum
1 fl oz Malibu Rum
1/2 tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice
2 Tbs granulated white sugar

Directions:
Trim and peel pineapple.
Cut into quarters from top to bottom.
Slice out the core from each quarter.
Cut each quarter slice in half lengthwise
Now cut each piece in half crosswise. (you should now have 16 wedges of pineapple about 3-inch by 1-inch each).
In a small glass mix the Captain Morgans & Malibu
Place the Pineapple wedges into a bowl and pour the liquid mixture over the wedges rolling them in the liquid a few times.
Sprinkle the Pumpkin Pie Spice gently over the pineapple wedges.
Sprinkle the sugar gently over the pineapple wedges.
Leave to marinate for at least 1 hour.
Preheat barbecue grill & either oil or spray the grill rack with non-stick cooking spray.
Place pineapple wedges on the grill and cook approximately 3-4 minutes on each side (turning so that grill marks are on all sides)
Remove from the grill and brush with the "Marinade" one more time.
Serve and enjoy!
