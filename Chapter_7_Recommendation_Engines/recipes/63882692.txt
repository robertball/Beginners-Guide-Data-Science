Title:
Chicken and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1 cup chicken broth
1 1/2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups cooked white, brown, or wild rice
4 cooked, cut chicken breasts
3 ounces sliced mushrooms
1/3 cup chopped green bell pepper
2 tbsp chopped pimiento(optional)
1/4 cup slivered almonds(optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350F(180C)
In a large saucepan, heat butter over low heat. Blend in flour, salt and pepper and cook, stirring until smooth and bubbly. Remove from heat.
Stir in broth and milk, heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute and stir in remaining ingredients.
Pour into ungreased 10x6 baking dish or a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish.
Bake, uncovered, for 40 to 45 minutes. Sprinkle with parsley if desired.
