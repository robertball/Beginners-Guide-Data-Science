Title:
Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 --- 20oz. can crushed pineapple  (undrained)
2/3 cup sugar
1 -- 3oz. pkg. lemon jello
1 -- 8oz. cream cheese. (softened)
1 cup diced unpeeled apples
1/2 to 1 cup chopped nuts
1 cup chopped celery
1--8oz. carton cool whip.

Directions:
In sauce pan (med.) combine pineapple and sugar. Boil 3 minutes. Add Jello. Stir until jello is dissolved. Add cream cheese, and stir until mixture is thoroughly combined. Cool. Fold in apples, nuts, celery and whipped topping. Pour into a 9 inch square pan. Chill until firm.

Submitter: 
Marjorie Taylor
Description: 
A cool and refreshing salad to be used as a side dish of dessert.
