Title:
Savory Lemony Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups quinoa(cooked)
1 tablespoon Olive Oil
1 teaspoon mustard seeds
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
10-12 curry leaves
1/2 cup onion chopped finely
2 tablespoons peanuts
3-4 finely chopped Green Chilies
1 teaspoon turmeric powder
2 teaspoons Salt
1 table spoon lemon juice

Directions:
Medium heat a saute pan, add olive oil, mustard and Cumin seeds.  Let them splutter.
Add the curry leaves and onions.  Saute them till translucent.
Add all the other ingredients except lemon juice and quinoa.  Stir fry for about 1 minute.
Add the Quinoa and Lemon Juice.
Mix well
