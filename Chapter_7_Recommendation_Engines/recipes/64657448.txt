Title:
Balsalmic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds boneless chicken breast
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 tsp garlic salt
2 T olive oil
1 onion, thinly sliced
14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
1/2 balsalmic vinegar
1 t dried basil
1 t dried oregano
1 t dried rosemary
1/2 t dried thyme

Directions:
Season chicken with pepper and garlic salt. Heat oil in a large skillet. Add chicken and onions. COok until chicken is browned. Pour in tomatoes, vinegar, and herbs. Simmer until chicken is done, about 15 to 20 minute. Serve over rice or noodles or beside a salad.

Submitter: 
Angie Landsettle
Description: 
Healthy and simple chicken option. 198 cal, 8 g fat, 2 g fiber, and 27 g protien per serving.
