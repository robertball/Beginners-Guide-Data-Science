Title:
Andrea's Pomegranate Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2781480.jpg
Ingredients:
3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and diced
1/2 cucumber, peeled and diced
1/2 bunch green onions, sliced
1 pomegranate, peeled and seeds separated
1 lime, juiced
2 tablespoons cilantro paste
salt to taste

Directions:
Stir avocado, cucumber, green onions, pomegranate seeds, lime juice, and cilantro paste together in a bowl. Season with salt.

Submitter: 
Andrea MacEachern
Description: 
This guac is always a huge hit at parties and potlucks! I've gotten many requests for the recipe. It is so easy and delicious! You can use fresh cilantro instead of a big sploosh of cilantro paste, but the paste is easier and tastier.
