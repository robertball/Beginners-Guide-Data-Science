Title:
Lemon Blueberry Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1312816.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Olive Oil
1/2 cup White Vinegar
1/4 cup whole fresh Blueberries
1 tsp Sesame Seeds
Lemon Juice (1 Lemon)

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, combine Olive Oil, White Vinegar, Lemon Juice, and Blueberries, and blend until smooth.
Transfer to a serving container and add Sesame Seeds.
Mix well and serve.

Submitter: 
EvilJekyll
Description: 
A quick and easy home made salad dressing that's great for the summer.
