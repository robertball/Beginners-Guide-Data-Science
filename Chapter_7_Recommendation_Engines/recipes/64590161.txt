Title:
Soothing Hot Ginger Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3773457.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (12 oz) Canada Dry Ginger Ale (regular or diet)
1 Lipton Tea Bag (regular or decaffeinated)

Directions:
Pour Ginger Ale in microwaveable mug and microwave for 1-2 minutes.
Add teabag to mug and let it seep for 3-5 minutes or until desired strength. Enjoy.
Note: For an extra treat try at winnings Honey Vanilla Tea instead.

Submitter: 
tcasa
Description: 
Delightful twist on hot tea. I was giving this after having my third baby in the hospital. This tea helps with upset stomachs, nausea and is just tasty.
