Title:
Mom's Evil Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of celery soup
1/2 c milk
1/2 c sour cream
1/4 t ground pepper
1 bag (24 oz) thawed hash browns
1 c shredded cheddar cheese
1 c fried onions

Directions:
Combine everything except 1/2 c of cheese and onions.  Bake at 350 for 35-45 minutes, covered with foil.  Uncover and top with remaining cheese and onions.  Bake additional 5 minutes.
