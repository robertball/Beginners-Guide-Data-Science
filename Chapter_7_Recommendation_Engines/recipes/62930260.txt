Title:
Healthy Enchalidas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB 99% fat free ground turkey or chicken breast
1 LB ground bison or organic lean beef
1 medium onion
2 TBLS Mrs. Dash Southwest Chipotle seasoning
1 can green chilis - more if desired
2 jalapanos
1 can low sodium tomato sauce
1 can refried beans
1 small can enchidada sauce
8 oz package reduced fat taco cheese
2 pkg La Tortilla Factory, low carb tortillas

Directions:
Brown meat, onion and jalapanos then add Mrs. Dash, chillis and tomato sauce and let that cook a few minutes.
Spread refried beans on each wrap then add meat and fold, lay seam side down in a 9x13 pan.
Spoon enchilada sauce over each wrap until covered, sprinkle with a little cheese and bake 20-30 minutes in a 350 degree oven. Cover the pan with foil until the last 5 minutes
