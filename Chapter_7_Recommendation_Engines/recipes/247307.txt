Title:
Cheeseburger Rice Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3684600.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 tablespoon butter
2 zucchini, sliced 1/2-inch thick
1 tablespoon dried minced onion
2 cups water
1 (5.5 ounce) package Knorr® Rice Sides™ - Beef flavor
2 tomatoes, diced
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 cup grated Cheddar cheese
Salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown ground beef in skillet over medium to medium-high heat, stirring to crumble. Transfer browned beef to a bowl.
Melt butter the same skillet; add zucchini and saute until just slightly softened, about 5 minutes.
Return ground beef to skillet along with dried onions, water, Knorr(R) Rice SidesTM- Beef, chopped tomatoes, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low; cover and cook for 7 minutes. Top with cheese. Let sit 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Karen Dean Covington
Description: 
Ground beef, zucchini, tomatoes and rice are sautéed to perfection.
