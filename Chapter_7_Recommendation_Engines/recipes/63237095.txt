Title:
Wina And Rod's Chili Noodle Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/843461.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds of medium ground beef
2 Green Bell Peppers chopped fine
2 Honey Sweet Onions chopped fine
1 bunch of fresh green onions chopped fine
1 32oz. can of diced tomatoes
1 32 oz. can of whole tomatoes crushed
1 24 oz can of tomatoe sauce
1 6oz. can of tomatoe paste
2 teaspoons minced garlic
6 teasponns chilli powder
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (add more for more heat)
1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
2 teaspoons roasted garlic and peeper spice mix like clubhouse
4 dried whole red chili peppers
2 tablespoons of crushed red pepper flakes
2 teaspoons of sea salt
3 cups of elbow macaroini
Half a cup of parmasean cheese
1 cup of shredded old cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef combined with the onions in a large pan until meat is browned and onions are transparent. Drain meat and onions but leave about 1/4 cup of the fat.
Place beef and onions and the rest of the ingredients except the macaroni and cheeses in a slow cooker mix well and slow cook for 8 hours on low.
Boil macaroini for about 7 minutes until firm but not fully cooked. Drain macaroni and place noodles in a casserole dish. Spoon chili mixture in with the noodles adding a generous amout of the chili to fully engulf the noodles. Top with parmasean cheese and then shredded cheddar. Bake in the oven for a half hour at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
DARKSACRIFICE
Description: 
No Bean Chili slow cooked for 8 hours then baked in the oven infused with elbow macaroni. This is a favorite in our household, inexpensive, extremely tasty and filling.
