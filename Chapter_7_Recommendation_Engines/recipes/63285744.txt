Title:
Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 medium onion, diced
1 tsp minced garlic
Salt and pepper to taste
1 small can of evaporated milk
1 pound of moterey jack cheese, grated
1 sleeve of saltine crackers, crushed
4 tbsp butter, melted
4-6 bell peppers

Directions:
Par boil peppers 4-5 minutes, drain well and set aside. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with the onion, garlic, salt and pepper. Drain well. Add the canned milk and melted butter. Mix in the crushed crackers and half of the cheese. Stuff peppers, then sprinkle with additional cheese. Place in a large dish and bake 30-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Julie Rehagen
Description: 
A good stuffed pepper with no sauce.
