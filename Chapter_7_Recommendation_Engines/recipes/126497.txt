Title:
White Chocolate Mousse Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983049.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package premium white chocolate baking squares
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
2 cups whipping cream, stiffly whipped
1 (9 inch) baked pie crust
Fresh raspberries or strawberries (optional)

Directions:
In saucepan, over low heat, melt chocolate with sweetened condensed milk. Stir in extract.  Cool to room temperature.  Fold in whipped cream.
Spoon into pie crust.  Chill 4 hours or until set.  Serve with fresh raspberries or strawberries (optional).

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
This rich and velvety white chocolate mousse pie only looks like it takes hours to make.  Garnished with raspberries or strawberries, this pie will impress your guests.
