Title:
Teriyaki Flank Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. vegetable oil
1/2 c. soy sauce
1/2 c. orange juice
1 t. ginger
1 clove garlic
2 T. sugar

Directions:
Mix ingredients and soak flank steak all day. Grill to medium rare or medium, slice thin on the diagonal to serve.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
This marinade, used with flank steak was a major favorite in my house. We always grilled it over our little charcoal hibatchi grill on the back patio. Dessert - toasted marshmallows!!
