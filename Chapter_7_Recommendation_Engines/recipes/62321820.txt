Title:
Strawberry Yogurt Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small carton strawberry yogurt
1 3 oz. package vanilla instant pudding
1 20 oz. can crushed pineapple (undrained)
1 8 oz cool whip
1 pint fresh strawberries
fresh blueberries
banana slices, optional

Directions:
Mix the yogurt, pudding, and crushed pineapple with juice.  Add the 8 oz of cool whip and the berries.  Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
MN Nice
Description: 
This is an extremely refreshing fruit salad.  A friend shared the recipe with me and I've been making it nonstop since.  It is only recommended to use fresh strawberries and blueberries.  It's well worth it!
