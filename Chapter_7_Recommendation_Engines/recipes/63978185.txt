Title:
Tuna Noodle Casserole III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package medium egg noodles
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons EVO
1/3-1/2 cup diced onion
1/3-1/2 cup diced celery
1/2-3/4 lb chopped crimini mushrooms
2 cloves diced garlic
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/3-1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons capers
1 1/4 cup frozen peas
Salt pepper and ground red pepper to taste
1.5 cup shredded mozzarella
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese and 1/4 cup bread crumbs mixed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter a 2.5-3 quart baking dish
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 10-12 minutes or until al dente; drain.
While pasta is cooking sauté in a large deep pan onions, celery and mushrooms in butter and EVO till mushrooms have sweated out and wilted.
Add garlic and sauté 30 sec
Add mushroom soup, milk, sour cream, seasonings, peas and capers
Mix well and turn heat to low
Add drained noodles, tuna and cheese to soup/vegetable mixture and fold together.
Pour into baking dish and cover top with breadcrumb mixture
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30-45 minutes.
Let rest for 10 min
