Title:
Light Chocolate - Buttermilk Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1051256.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/4 c. all-purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1/2 tsp.baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
3/4 cup hot strong coffee
2 cups sugar
1/3 cup vegetable oil
2 lg egg whites
1 lg egg
2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup butterrmilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease the 10 inch Bundt pan.
In medium bowl, with wire whisk, mix flour, basking soda, baking powder, and salt.  In 2 cup measuring cup, with fork, mix cocoa hot coffee until well blended.
In large bowl, with mixer at low speed, beat sugar, oil, egg whites and egg until blended.  Increase speed to high; beat until creamy, about 2 minutes.  Reduce speed to low and beat in cocoa mixture. Add flour mixture alternatingwith buttermilk,   beginning and ending with the flour.  Beat just until blended.
Scrape batter ino prepared pan: bake 45 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center of cake coes out clean.
Cool cake ten minutes in pan on wire rack. invert onto rack to cool completely,
