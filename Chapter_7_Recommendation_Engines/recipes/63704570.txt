Title:
RAW Date, Ginger & Nut Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029561.jpg
Ingredients:
- 1/2 lb dates, remove pits
- 1 cup walnuts
- 1 cup almonds
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon fresh grated ginger

Directions:
Add dates, walnuts, almonds and spices into your blender or food processor (whichever can blend smooth or has a dough setting).
Blend until smooth (some chunks of nuts may remain)
Serve with sliced apples, pears, carrots or celery -- or any fruits/vegetables of your choice!

Submitter: 
Desire For Fit
Description: 
RAW RAW RAW! This raw butter is not only healthy, but makes eating raw fruits and vegetables more fun.
