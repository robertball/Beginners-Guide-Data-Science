Title:
Apple Dumplings I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) can refrigerated flaky biscuit dough
4 apples - peeled, cored and cubed
1/2 cup white sugar & 1/2 cup brown sugar
1 T or so water
Pecans or walnuts & raisens
1/2 cup butter, melted
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Butter a 7x11 inch baking pan.  Separate biscuit dough into 8 pieces.  Flatten each piece of dough into a circle.  Wrap one biscuit around each apple half and place, seam side down, in pan.
In small bowl, combine sugar, water, melted butter and vanilla.  Pour mixture over dumplings in pan.  Sprinkle cinnamon on top.  Bake 20 min and turn bake another 20 min. Add sauce last 10 minutes

Submitter: 
Jacque
Description: 
Sweet apple halves are each wrapped in biscuit dough and poured over with a buttery sauce that's laced with vanilla and cinnamon. They're baked until golden and served hot.Modified from: Apple Dumplings I
