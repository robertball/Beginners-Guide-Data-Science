Title:
Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked, drained, and mashed squash
1 Tbsp cornstarch
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup milk
2 Tbsp minced dried onion or 1/4 c chopped fresh
1/4 cup (1 stick) melted margarine

Directions:
Mix and put in greased dish and bake at 350F for about 40 minutes until lightly browned.
