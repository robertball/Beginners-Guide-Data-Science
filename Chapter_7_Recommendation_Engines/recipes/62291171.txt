Title:
Janet's Tortellini-Spinach Soup (customized by Amy) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. olive oil
1 c. thinly sliced carrots (used shredded carrots)
1/2 c. chopped onion
1 garlic clove, minced
3 14oz cans chicken broth (used 1 lg 46 oz can)
1 9 oz pkg refrigerated cheese-filled tortellini
1/2 t. oregano
10 oz box frozen cut leaf spinach, drained
1/3 c. shredded mozarella cheese (used parmesan)
2 c. chopped cooked chicken, optional

Directions:
Heat oil in large suacepan over medium heat until hot.  Add carrots, onion, and garlic; cook and stir 3-6 minutes or until onion is tender.
Add broth; bring to a boil.  Add tortellini and oregano; return to a boil.  Reduce heat; boil gently 5 minutes.
Add spinach; cook 2 to 4 minutes or until spinach and tortellini are tender, stirring occasionally.  Spinkle each serving with cheese
