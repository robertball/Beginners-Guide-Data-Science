Title:
Danish Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cucumbers
1 T salt
1/2 cup vinegar
1/2 cup sugar (oops I lied)
1-1/2 cups water (cancels out the sugar!)
1/4 t pepper
Parsley for garnish

Directions:
Wash cucumbers
Slice thinly (with skin on)
Layer slices in a bowl with salt sprinkled on each layer
Let sit for about 2 hours to draw out bitterness
Rinse and drain thoroughly
Mix vinegar, sugar, water and pepper together until sugar is dissolved
Add sliced cucumbers
Chill
Garnish with snipped parsley
