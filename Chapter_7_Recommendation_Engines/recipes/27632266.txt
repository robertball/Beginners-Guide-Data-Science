Title:
Pomegranate Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz. citrus vodka
1/2 oz. fresh lemon juice
1/4 oz. pomegranate juice
1 oz. simple syrup

Directions:
Shake all ingredients well with ice and stain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with flamed orange peel and serve.
