Title:
Sandra's Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2996114.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 cup ketchup
1 10 oz. can tomato soup
2 Tbsp. brown sugar
2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 Tbsp. vinegar
2 Tbsp. mustard

Directions:
Saute hamburger and onion (drain grease), then add remaining ingredients.
Simmer for one hour and serve on hamburger buns (or you may cook in slow cooker).
Enjoy :)
