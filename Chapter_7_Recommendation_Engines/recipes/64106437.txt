Title:
Chicken Pineapple Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz pineapple chunks
10.5 oz chicken broth
2 tbsp cornstarch
2 tbsp soy sauce
1/4 tsp ginger
2 cups broccoli florets
1/2 cup diagonally sliced carrot
2 cups cubed cooked chicken
Rice

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving juice. In small bowl, combine reserved juice, broth, cornstarch, soy sauce and ginger; set aside.
Stir fry broccoli and carrot until tender crisp.
Stir in chicken and broth mixture. Reduce heat to medium high. Cook until mixture boils and thickens. stirring often. Add pineapple; heat through.
