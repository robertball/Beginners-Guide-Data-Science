Title:
Chicken Pasta Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon butter
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken
1 can chicken broth (15 oz aprox)
1 1/2 cups prepared spaghetti sauce
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp dried oregano
3 cups rotini pasta
3/4 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
In large skillet, over medium heat, melt the butter. Cut chicken up into 1 in. pieces. Cook until no longer pink, stirring occasionally.
Add broth, spaghetti sauce and spices. Heat to boiling.
Add pasta. Stir until well coated. Cover and reduce heat to low.
Simmer 15 minutes until pasta is tender, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat and add parmesan. Stir.

Submitter: 
MSABBYK
Description: 
This is a one dish meal that is very simple and fast and still has the homemade taste we love. It combines pasta with chicken in a spaghetti sauce that uses chicken broth, basil and oregano. Finish is off by mixing in grated parmesan cheese and it is sure to be a keeper. Quick enough to prepare on weeknights.
