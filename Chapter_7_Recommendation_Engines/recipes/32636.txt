Title:
Party Punch II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/428709.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen sliced strawberries in syrup
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
8 cups water
2 cups brewed black tea
2 cups white sugar
3 cups light rum
2 trays ice cubes
2 (2 liter) bottles lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage, chilled

Directions:
In a punch bowl, combine strawberries, orange juice concentrate, lemonade concentrate, water and tea. Stir in sugar and rum. Add Ice, and pour in lemon soda.

Submitter: 
Dana
Description: 
This yummy punch is easily doubled for big parties!
