Title:
Big Ray's Crock Pot Cilantro Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4496881.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken thighs (about 8)
Juice from 2 limes
1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
1 (16 oz) bag frozen corn
2 minced garlic cloves
1/2 onion, chopped
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 tsp cumin
salt and pepper to taste
Cook on LOW for 4-6 hours or on HIGH for 2-3 hours. Mine only took 4 hours on low and even was a little dry. (Note: each crock pot is a little different so just keep an eye on your chicken and make sure it doesn't dry out).
tortillas, sour cream, guacamole, salsa, and cheese, optional
-

Directions:
Cook on LOW for 4-6 hours or on HIGH for 2-3 hours.
Serve with tortillas and toppings such as with sour cream, guacamole, salsa, and cheese (optional).
Freezer Meal Instructions:
To Freeze:
Place all ingredients in a resealable gallon-sized freezer bag. Mix together and zip bag closed. Store in freezer for up to 3 months.
To Prepare:
When ready to eat, remove from freezer and thaw in fridge for 24 hours.
