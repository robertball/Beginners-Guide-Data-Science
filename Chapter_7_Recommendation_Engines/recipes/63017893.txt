Title:
Yeasty Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
3 tbsp sugar
1tsp salt
1/4 cup dry milk
1 cup warm water
2 tbsp butter
2 1/4 tsp. yeast

Directions:
Combine in bread machine in order suggested by manufacturer. Use dough setting- completely. Take out when done. shape into balls about 2 inches in diameter. Let rise 30m minutes. Bake for 15-20 minutes in a 350 degree oven.

Submitter: 
Pamela Mills
Description:
