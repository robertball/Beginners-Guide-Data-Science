Title:
Two-ingredient cake AKA healthy, flourless version of apple pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1231063.jpg
Ingredients:
1kg of millet (about 2,2 lbs)
2kg of apples (4,5 lbs)
*cinnamon

Directions:
Grate apples (without skin), mix them together with millet (uncooked) and pour into baking tray (that's been already buttered)
Bake in the oven at 390ºF (200ºC) for 90 minutes. ? Cool for 15 minutes and take out of tray.
Top with greek yoghurt and cinnamon!
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
mariarita
Description: 
This is my Granny's recipe as an alternative to an old-fashioned apple pie. My whole family loves it!
