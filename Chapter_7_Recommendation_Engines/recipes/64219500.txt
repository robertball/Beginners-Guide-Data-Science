Title:
Waffle Iron Churros Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1645965.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup of Water
4 Tablespoons of Unsalted Butter
1/4 Teaspoon of Salt
1 Tablespoon of Sugar
1/4 Teaspoon of Cinnamon
1/2 Cup of Flour
2 Large Eggs

Directions:
In a small saucepan, bring water, butter, salt, sugar, and cinnamon to a simmer over high heat.
When the butter has melted, lower heat to medium, add flour, and stir rapidly.  Continue to cook over medium heat, stirring, for 1 minute.
Remove saucepan from heat and allow mixture to stand for 5 minutes.  Add eggs one at a time, stirring until each is incorporated.
Cook 1/4 cup of batter in waffle iron for 5-10 minutes.
Bursh with melted butter and dip in cinnamon sugar (1/2 Cup of Sugar, 1 Teaspoon Cinnamon).
Serve with melted nutella.
