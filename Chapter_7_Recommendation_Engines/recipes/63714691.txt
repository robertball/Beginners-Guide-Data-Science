Title:
Paleo-Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 banana
1 tsp arrow root

Directions:
Squash banana.
blend all ingredients (very well) either in mixing bowl, blender or food processor.
Add fruit to taste.
Pour over hot skillet.
Flip when you see bubbles.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Nichoel Casey
Description: 
This is a Paleo version of traditional pancakes. Serve with butter.
