Title:
Portobella Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp Butter
2 Tbsp Flour
1 C Half and Half
3/4 C Italian Cheese Mix shredded
10 Portabella Mushrooms (baby-bellas)
4 Cloves garlic
1/2 Onion
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a chopper, add mushrooms, onion, and garlic cloves - process to a paste.  Melt 2 tbsp butter in saucepan, and sautee paste.  In separate saucepan, melt 2 tbsp butter, and slowly add 2 tbsp flour.  Cook stirring constantly 4-5 minutes, creating a roux.  Slowly add 1 cup half and half, and stir constantly until thickened.  When sauce is thick, add cheese, and salt & pepper to taste.  Stir until cheese is melted and sauce is smooth.  Add sautee mixture and cook 2-3 minutes.  Serve over pasta.
