Title:
IceCream Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/754404.jpg
Ingredients:
2 - 12 count boxes of icecream sandwiches
1 small jar crunchy peanut butter
1 large tub Cool Whip
1 box dry chocolate pudding mix
2 - 8 count packs of individually wrapped Reese Peanut Butter Cups
1 jar hot fudge sauce
1 jar caramel sauce

Directions:
Layer 1 box of icecream sandwiches in a 9"x13" pan.
Melt 1 jar of crunchy peanut butter in microwave and spread 1/2 over icecream sandwiches.
Mix 1/2 packet of dry chocolate pudding mix in with 1/2 tub of Cool Whip and spread over peanut butter.
Unwrap and crumble 1 pack of Reese Cups over Cool Whip.
Heat hot fudge and caramel sauce and drizzle half of each jar over peanut butter cups.
Reapeat each step, beginning with icecream sandwiches.
Freeze 1-2 hours...and then hide it...from yourself!

Submitter: 
Frauline
Description: 
This yummy, chocolatey, peanut buttery, frozen layered icecream treat is sure to be a hit with all!  This is an often requested dessert in my house.  Warning: It's VERY addictive!
