Title:
Mixed Olive Tapenade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz Black olives, pitted
8 oz Green olives, pitted
8 oz Kalamata olives, pitted
8 oz Mixed (Greek or Mediterranean) olives, pitted
6-8 cl Roasted Garlic
1/4 C Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Directions:
Use a food processor to rough chop the olives and garlic. Batch size will depend on the capacity of your machine.
Mix all well and add olive oil.
Store in refrigerator. Let it sit for 2-3 days before using to give the flavors a chance to blend.

Submitter: 
Joan
Description: 
I have had a hard time finding a commercial tapenade that I really like - so I made my own.
