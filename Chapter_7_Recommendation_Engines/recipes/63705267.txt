Title:
Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029751.jpg
Ingredients:
3 3/4c (1 lb box) confectioners sugar
1/2 c butter softened
3 to 4 TBS milk
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
1. In a mixer at low speed, add sugar, butter, milk and vanilla.  Beat at medium speed 1 to 2 minutes until creamy. Add more milk if needed until it is the desired consistency.
