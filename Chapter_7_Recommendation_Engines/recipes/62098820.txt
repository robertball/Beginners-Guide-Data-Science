Title:
Broccoli Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box jiffy cornbread mix
2 eggs
one chopped onion (optional)
1 cup mozzarella
1 small box chopped broccoli
1/2 stick melted butter
8oz. small curd cottage cheese

Directions:
Mix together the cornbread mix, eggs, onion(if desired), broccoli, butter, and cottage cheese.
Fold in the mozzarella cheese.
Pour into 9 in square baking dish
Bake for 35-40 minutes or until toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean.
