Title:
Puffed Pear Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
3/4 cup milk
2/4 c flour
1 tsp almond or vanilla extract
1/4 tsp salt
1 ripe pear, thinly sliced
2 TBS sugar
3/4 tsp cinnamon
2 TBS butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Place a 12 inch cast iron skillet in the oven to warm it.  Whisk together eggs, milk, flour, extract and salt until smooth. In a separate bowl toss the pear with cinnamon and sugar.
Remove pre-heated skillet from oven, add butter and swirl until melted. Arrange the pears in a single layer and pour in batter. Return to oven and bake 25 minutes or until puffed and golden.
