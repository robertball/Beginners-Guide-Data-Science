Title:
Strawberry (or Banana) Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts strawberries or bananas
box of vanilla wafers
1 3/4 - 2 cups sugar
2 tablespoons flour
sprinkle of salt
2 eggs
2 cups milk
2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions:
Combine sugar, flour and salt
Stir in eggs and milk
Cook until thickened. (see note)
Add vanilla.
Arrange a layer of vanilla wafers on bottom of bowl
Cover with a layer of fruit
Cover with a layer of sauce mixture.
Continue layers ending with a sauce layer.
