Title:
Impossible Custard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
½ cup shredded coconut (optional)
¾ cup sugar
1-teaspoon vanilla
½ cup Bisquick
½ stick margarine
2 cups milk

Directions:
Put all ingredients in blender.
Mix about 2 minutes on mix cycle.
Pour into greased 9” glass pie plate.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 50 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jeff Harrell
Description: 
Custard Pie that forms its own crust.
