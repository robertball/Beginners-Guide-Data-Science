Title:
Chicken Nacho Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993372.jpg
Ingredients:
4 small boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup chunky salsa
1/4 cup sour cream
1/2 cup crushed tortilla chips
1/2 cup KRAFT Tex Mex Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F.
Place chicken on foil-covered baking sheet. Slice 3 cuts on top of each chicken breast with sharp knife.
Top each chicken breast evenly with salsa, sour cream and tortilla chips.
Bake 20 minutes. Remove from oven; sprinkle with cheese. Bake 10 minutes or until chicken is done (170 degrees F).

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese
Description: 
Nachos, everyone's favourite appetizer, gets turned upside down into a quick and easy chicken bake.
