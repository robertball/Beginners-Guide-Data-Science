Title:
Easy Tortellini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283943.jpg
Ingredients:
1-8oz. pkg. spinach tortellini(dry type not frozen)Ronzoni or Barilla brand
1-8oz. pkg. 3 cheese tortellini(dry type not frozen)Ronzoni or Barilla brand                      1-6oz. jar marinated artichoke hearts-cut into small pieces
1 small pkg. grape tomatoes-sliced in half
6oz.can black pitted olives-drained and sliced
1/2 of a red onion-sliced thin
1/4 cup grated carrots
12 oz.of any oil and vinegar dressing _ Check out my Lite Italian Salad Dressing recipe published on this site!

Directions:
Cook tortellini according to package directions,al dente.
Drain and cool completely.
Mix together with above ingredients and chill a few hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
A blend of spinach and three cheese tortellini and marinated artichoke hearts. A perfect summer side dish or main dish! Try my Lite Italian Salad Dressing recipe with this salad!
