Title:
Hash Brown Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onions
1 10.5 oz. can cream of celery soup
1 3 oz. package of cream cheese
4 cups frozen shredded potatoes (O'Brien is good).

Directions:
Cook onion in a little water until tender.  Drain.
Mix soup and cream cheese in saucepan, heat and stir until smooth.
Mix potatoes and onion in 2 quart casserole.  Stir in hot potato mix.  Cover.
Bake 1 hour 15 minutes at 350 degrees. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese (1 cup??) and return to oven to melt.
