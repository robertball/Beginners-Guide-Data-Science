Title:
Banana Pina Colada Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2001808.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large banana
1 cup coconut juice blend, or more to taste
2 pineapple spears
3 cubes ice
1 tablespoon agave nectar

Directions:
Blend banana, coconut juice blend, pineapple, ice, and agave nectar together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
awakenedone
Description: 
This wonderful smoothie is like island heaven, rich and delicious and highly nutritious, sweetened only by raw agave nectar.
