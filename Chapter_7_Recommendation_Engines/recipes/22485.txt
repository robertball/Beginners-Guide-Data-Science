Title:
Holiday Mini Kisses Treasure Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 1/3 cups MOUNDS® Sweetened Coconut Flakes
1 cup HERSHEY®'S Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
1 1/3 cups HERSHEY®'S Holiday Candy Coated Bits

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 F.  Stir together graham cracker crumbs, flour and baking powder in small bowl; set aside.
Beat sweetened condensed milk and butter until smooth; add reserved  crumb mixture, mixing well.  Stir in coconut, baking pieces and holiday bits.  Drop by rounded tablespoons  onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned.  Cool 1 minute; remove from cookie sheet to wire rack.  Cool completely.  About 3 dozen cookies.
Variation:  Use 1-3/4 cups (10-oz. pkg.) Hershey's Mini Kisses Milk Chocolate or Semi-Sweet Baking Pieces and 1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts.  Omit Holiday Bits.  Proceed as directed above.

Submitter: 
HersheysKitchens.com
Description: 
Chewy holiday cookies with coconut, chocolate chips and holiday colored baking pieces in them.
