Title:
curried lentils and rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup jasmine rice
1 T canola oil
1 T curry powder
1 cup onion
1 cup celery
4 cups water
1 cup lentils
1 T honey
1 T salt
1/2 cup sour cream
cilantro to taste

Directions:
cook rice, set aside
heat oil curry powder in large pan over med heat
add onions and celery cook for 5 mins
add water and lentils bring to a boil
simmer 22 mins
add honey and vinegar salt and sour cream.
ladle over rice

great with fish, or pork
