Title:
Good, Bad and Yummy Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/3 cup white sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon (optional)
2 egg, beaten or whisked
2 cups milk
1 TBL vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat skillet or griddle.
Add approximately 1 - 2 TBL oil.
Use wire wisk or fork to blend dry ingredients together.
Create a well in the center of powder mixture.
Measure out milk and add vanilla.
Pour liquid into powder and add whipped eggs.
Blend entire mixture together into smooth.
Let sit for a few minutes.
Pour desired amount onto heated skillet and cook until bubbles appear.
Flip and cook second side until golden brown.

Submitter: 
LeJoy Nichols
Description: 
I found a recipe here that I ended up changing so much it hardly resembled what I started out with and my family LOVED them.  I thought it would be a nice addition.  These pancakes are a blend of good and not so good for you ingredients, hence the name.
