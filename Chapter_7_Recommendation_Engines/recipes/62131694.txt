Title:
Simple Char Siu Pork Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper

Directions:
Mix together soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic powder and crushed red pepper in a bowl.
For best results, put pork in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over the pork. Marinate overnight.

Submitter: 
eviltwin
Description: 
I made this one night when I only had a few ingredients on hand. These four simple ingredients give the pork a wonderful flavor.
