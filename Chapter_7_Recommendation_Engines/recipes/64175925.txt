Title:
Tofu Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 package silken tofu
3 frozen strawberries
5 frozen peach slices
1 cup orange juice or milk (soy, almond)

Directions:
In a blender, combine tofu, strawberries, peach slices and orange juice or milk. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
You can use either fresh or frozen fruit and use any variation of fruit and juice.Modified from: Un-Tofu Tofu Smoothie
