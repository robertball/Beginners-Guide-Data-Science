Title:
Fabulous Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/570038.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch fresh spinach - torn, washed and dried
10 fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 onion, thinly sliced
4 eggs
4 tomatoes, chopped
sea salt to taste
1/3 cup olive oil
1/8 cup rice wine vinegar

Directions:
Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil. Cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.
Combine the spinach, mushrooms, onion, eggs and tomatoes; toss together and sprinkle with salt.
Whisk together the oil and vinegar. Pour over salad and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Uleta Weibel
Description: 
This salad is hearty enough to be used as a main entree, or it makes a great side dish.
