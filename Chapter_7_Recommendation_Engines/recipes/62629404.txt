Title:
Chicken and Gnocchi Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups chicken broth
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 tablespoons unsalted butter
3/4 teaspoon sugar
1 carrot sliced thin
1 stalk celery sliced thin
1 cup dry potato gnocchi
4 oz frozen peas
4 oz frozen corn
1 cup shredded chicken
2 oz baby spinach
Add To Taste
salt
freshly ground black pepper
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Combine chicken broth, garlic, butter and sugar in a large saucepan over medium-low heat.
Bring to a simmer, and cook uncovered about 2 minutes or  until the garlic is tender.
Add the carrots and celery and continue to simmer another 2 minutes.  Carrots and celery should be ALMOST tender.
Bring to a full boil, stir in gnocchi, cooking 2 to 3 minutes until the gnocchi are al dente and rise to the top.
Stir in frozen peas and cornâ€¦simmer for about 30 seconds.
Stir in chicken and spinach.  Turn off the heat.  Season with salt and pepper. Ladle the soup into bowls and add a generous amount of freshly grated cheese.

Description: 
A warm and cozy soup for chilly fall and winter nights.  This recipe is quick and easy but with all the comfort of homemade.
