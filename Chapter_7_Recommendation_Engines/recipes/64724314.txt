Title:
Homemade Garlic Mayonnaise Aioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg at room temperature
1 clove garlic
1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon lemon (or lime) juice
3/4 cup olive (or avocado) oil
Optional I -  1/4 tsp black pepper, or
Optional II -  1/8 tsp cayenne pepper with tbsp paprika

Directions:
In measuring cup combine all ingredients except oil and whisk with fork until combined.
With a hand immersion blender, mix these ingredients and
slowly add oil in a small stream until creamy.

Rudy Torrijos III

For everything you can think of especially hamburgers and chicken salad.
