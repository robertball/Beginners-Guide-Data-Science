Title:
Chicken, Broccoli  Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ziti
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon butter
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut in bite size pieces
1 cup chicken broth
1 teaspoon chicken boullion
1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1 clove of garlic, minced
9 ounces thawed cut broccoli
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1/4 cup heavy cream
2 tablespoons minced onion
1/4 cup white wine
2 teaspoons cornstarch
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions, or until al dente.
Heat oil and butter in pan over medium heat.  Add garlic and sautee for 1 minute, or until tender.  Salt and pepper chicken to taste, then place in pan and cook 4-5 minutes or until cooked through.  Add red pepper flakes and minced onion, along with broth, boullion and wine.  Simmer 5 minutes.  Add thawed broccoli and cook for an additional 5 minutes.  Mix cornstarch with water, then add to mixture in pan, along with heavy cream and parmesan cheese.  Heat through, but do not boil.  Continue simmer until desired thickness.  Add drained pasta to pan and gently toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Irlandes
Description: 
This is a really easy and quick meal for a crowd.  Recipe can easily be doubled for more, or cut in half for fewer.
