Title:
Pastina and eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/784519.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. (2oz.) pastina
2 eggs, slightly beaten, (or egg substitute)
2 tbsp. butter
3 tbsp. grated Romano cheese
Salt & Pepper, to taste
Milk (or cream), as needed

Directions:
Boil water and cook pastina according to directions. Drain most of water and add butter. Beat eggs slightly and pour in. Slowly cook over low heat to cook egg. Add cheese, salt and pepper to taste. Add milk to desired consistency.  Stir andt serve warm.

Submitter: 
AllieGeekPi
Description: 
Simple dish of tiny pasta, eggs and milk.  Great for anyone that's under the weather or little ones learning to eat solids.  Kids love this, as do adults.  Italian comfort food.
