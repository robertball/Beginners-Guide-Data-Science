Title:
Decadent Green Tea Slushie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 tablespoon jasmine green tea
1/4 cup white sugar
1/3 cup yogurt

Directions:
Boil water and prepare tea in a pot. Allow to steep for at least half an hour.
Pour tea into an ice cube tray, and freeze until hard, about four hours, or overnight.
Put six ice cubes into a blender and pulse until mostly crushed.
Add sugar and yogurt, and blend until mixed.

Submitter: 
hondafifi
Description: 
I devised this recipe while trying to find a cheaper substitute for Thai bubble tea. It's best with jasmine green tea, but regular green tea works fine too.
