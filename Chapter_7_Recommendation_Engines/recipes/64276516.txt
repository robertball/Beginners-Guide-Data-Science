Title:
Steak Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 onion, thinly sliced
1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
1 pound thin-sliced flank steak
1 packet fajita seasoning
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 package small flour tortillas
1 tomato, diced
1 cup guacamole
1 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded cheese
1 can sliced olives

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over med-high heat.
Add onions to skillet and cook for about 3 minutes.   Add peppers to skillet and cook about 3 more minutes until vegetables are softened.  Remove from pan and cover to keep warm.
Add steak to pan and cook until browned on all sides.  Add fajita seasoning, following directions on packet.
Serve with warm tortillas, diced tomato, sour cream, cheese, guacamole and olives.
