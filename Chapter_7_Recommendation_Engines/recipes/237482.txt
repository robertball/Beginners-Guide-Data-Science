Title:
Quick Avocado Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1127253.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups almond milk
1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced
1 small banana, sliced
1/4 cup ice, or as needed
1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Directions:
Blend almond milk, avocado, banana, ice, and honey in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Mona Redjaie
Description: 
Great Middle Eastern-style smoothie. You can substitute any milk for the almond milk.
