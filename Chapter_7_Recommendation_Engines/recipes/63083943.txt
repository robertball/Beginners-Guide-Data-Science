Title:
Seasoned Pumpkin Seeds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb pepitas
1 TBSP Olive Oil
1 TBSP Chili Powder
2 Tsp Garlic Powder
1 TBSP Tamari
Salt

Directions:
Using a gallon zip lock baggie, mix all ingredients together except the salt.
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.  Place coated pepitas evenly on baking sheet.  Bake for 15 min, then turn the seeds.  Bake another 15 minutes.  Remove and lightly salt.  Cool before eating.  Keep sealed in container and store in refrigerator.

Submitter:

Not the old roast and salted pumpkin seeds.
