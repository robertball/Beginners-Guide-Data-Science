Title:
TUNA CASSAROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/764207.jpg
Ingredients:
2 CANS OF TUNA
1 BAD OF EGG NOODLES
1/2 CUP OF SOUR CREAM
1/2 CUP OF MILK
1 CAN OF MUSHROOM (OPTIONAL TO YOUR TASTE)
BREAD CRUMB TOPPING
1/2 CUP OF BREAD CRUMBS
1/4 CUP OF PARM
1/4-1/2 MELTED MARGARINE

Directions:
PRE-HEAT OVEN @ 350 ..COOK EGG NOODLES, DRAIN ...PUT INTO CASSRLOE DISH ADD ALL OTHER INGREDIENTS MIX TOGETHER. THEN MELT MARGARINE AND MIX IT W/ THE BREADCRUMBS AND AND PARM AND SPRINKLE ON TOP OF CASSAROLE. bAKE @ 350 FOR 30 MIN
