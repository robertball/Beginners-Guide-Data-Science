Title:
Charro Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans pinto beans
2 cans white beans
3 tomatoes, diced
1/2 large sweet onion, chopped
3 serrano peppers, seeded and chopped
1 lb bacon
1 1/2- 2 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
Brown bacon, remove from pan and chop.  Saute onion, peppers and tomatoes in bacon drippings.
Rinse beans.
Put everything in pot, cover with water and simmer for a few hours.  You can also put in a crock pot and let cook all day on low.

Submitter: 
kelsimad
Description: 
Tasty Tex-Mex beans in broth.  Spiciness can be adjusted by adding more peppers or including some of the seeds.
