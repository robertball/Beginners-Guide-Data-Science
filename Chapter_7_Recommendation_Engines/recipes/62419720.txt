Title:
Tuna Casserole - Healthy Style Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 (16 0z) package uncooked pasta shells
2 (6 oz) cans tuna drained
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup
1 cup low fat ricotta cheese
1 cup thawed frozen green peas
1 (16 oz) package frozen broccoli thawed
1 lb mushrooms washed sliced and drained
1 med onion chopped
salt
pepper
parmesean cheese

Directions:
1.  boil pasta according to package directions
2.  sautee onion and mushrooms
3.  defrost peas and broccoli in microwave
4.  mix drained hot pasta with all ingredients
5.  eat
