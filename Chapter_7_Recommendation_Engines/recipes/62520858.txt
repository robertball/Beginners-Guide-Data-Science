Title:
Biscuits & Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Biscuits:
2 cups flour (a little less)
1 Tbsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup shortening
3/4 cup milk
1 egg
Gravy:
1 lb. sausage
1/2 cup flour
3-4 cups milk
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
For biscuits, mix dry ingredients and crumble in shortening. Mix eggs and milk together, then add to shortening mixture.
Spoon biscuits onto cookie sheet and bake at 400 F for 12 minutes.
For Gravy, cook sausage on medium heat and drain extra grease.
Add flour and stir into meat until it becomes brown.
Add milk and stir to thicken (do not boil). Add salt and pepper to taste.
Pour over biscuits and enjoy!
