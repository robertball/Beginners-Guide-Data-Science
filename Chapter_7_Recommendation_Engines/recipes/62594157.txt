Title:
COP cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
2 1/2 cups rolled oats
1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
In a large mixing bowl, beat the PB and butter with a mixer for 30 seconds.
Add the brown sugar, granulated sugar, baking powder, and baking soda and beat on med. speed until mixture is fluffy.
Add the eggs and vanilla and beat well.
At low speed, beat in flour.
Stir in rolled oats and chocolate chips.
Drop dough onto cookie sheet using a cookie scoop.
Bake cookies for 10-12 minutes.
*For "Biggies" you can drop dough using a 1/2 cup scoop.  Baking time remains the same.

Submitter: 
Jessica Kathleen
Description: 
Chocolate chips, oats, and peanut butter work together to make a delicious cookie!
