Title:
Karena's Deep Dish Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb shredded cheese
1 lb cottage cheese
10 eggs
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1/2c flour
spices of choice
fillers of choice (ie meat, veggies, etc)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F
beat 10 eggs mercilessly!
blend dry ingredients in separate bowl, then whisk into egg
add cheeses [reserve small amount of shredded & parsley to garnish top]
add filler options
butter/grease oblong cake pan or two pie plates
bake at 400 F for 10 min then reduce heat to 350 for remaining 45 min
cool 15 min before slicing
