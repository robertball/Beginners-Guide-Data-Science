Title:
floyd eudy's hungry chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts (halved)
1/4 lb butter
3 medium onions (chopped)
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp paprika
4 tbsp flour
16 oz sour cream

Directions:
saute onions in butter & transfer to 13x9 casserole dish.
mix flour with salt, pepper & paprika.
sprinkle flour mixture onto chicken breasts & arrange in casserole dish.
cover tightly with foil and cook at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours.
add sour cream, cover & cook for 30 more minutes.
