Title:
Cream Cheese Chocolate Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Box chocolate cake
8 oz cream cheese
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
1 cup chocolate chips
Sliced almonds

Directions:
Make chocolate cake mix as explained on the box.
Fill cupcakes 1/2 way.
Mix cream cheese, sugar, egg, and chocolate chips.
Top cupcakes with cream cheese topping.
Sprinkle sliced almonds on top.
Bake according to cake mix directions.
