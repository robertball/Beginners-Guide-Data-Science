Title:
Bacon Spinach Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup rinsed Quinoa
1 1/2 cup Chicken Broth
1 pound Bacon, cut into bite sized pieces
1 onion chopped
1 bag baby spinach
1 TBS Cilantro

Directions:
In a small sauce pan add quinoa and chicken broth.  Bring to a boil over medium heat.  When it starts to bubble cover and turn to low. Simmer for 15 minutes.
In a saucepan add chopped onion and bacon.  Cook until done.  Remove onto a paper towel covered plate to drain. In the same saucepan in the leftover bacon grease, add spinach and cook until wilted.  Add the quinoa and onion bacon mixture.  Stir and serve.
