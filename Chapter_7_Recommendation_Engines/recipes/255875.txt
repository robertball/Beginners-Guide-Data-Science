Title:
Creamy Pineapple and Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4056214.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen pineapple chunks, thawed
1 cup milk
1/2 banana
1 teaspoon honey
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine pineapple chunks, milk, banana, honey, and vanilla extract in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Khaleah Onyx Norris
Description: 
This smoothie is great to have for breakfast or just any time you need something sweet and fruity!
