Title:
A Different Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups AP flour
1 1/3 Cups shortening
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
1 egg
1 tsp white vinegar
5 to 6 tsp ice water

Directions:
Mix the flour, shortening, salt and baking powder then add the egg, vinegar and water. Add just enough water that the dough comes together. Flatten into a disk and cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate. Divide and roll out for two crusts.

Submitter: 
Jim K
Description: 
Pie crust that is different than the usual. I don't know where it came from, but I copied it on a 3 X 5 card some time ago and it was labeled, Perfect Pie Crust.
