Title:
Cilantro, Bean and Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1225234.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup dried Pinto Beans
1 lb. ground beef
1 tsp. Spike seasoning
1 T. Olive Oil
1 onion, chopped
1 T. minced garlic
2 tsp. dried Mexican Oregano
1 T. ground cumin
1 T. dried cilantro
4 cups chicken broth (2 cans)
1 cup water
2 T. tomato paste
1 can (14.5oz) petit diced tomatoes
1/2 cup diced green onions
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro (or more to taste)
2 T fresh squeezed lime

Directions:
Soak beans overnight according to package directions, drain, add fresh water to cover.  Simmer until beans are soft (approx. 40-45 min.) Drain.
While beans are cooking, brown ground beef with Spike seasoning.  When browned, remove from heat and set aside.
Heat olive oil in skillet on medium heat, add onion, and saute for 5 minutes, or until onion is soft and translucent and starts to brown.   Add garlic and saute 2 minutes more.  Next add Mexican oregano, cumin, and dried cilantro and saute 1 more minute.
Combine drained beans, browned meat, onion mixture, chicken broth, water, tomato paste and petit diced tomatoes in heavy stock pot.  Simmer over low heat for 45 minutes, adding a more water to achieve desired consistency for the stew.
When stew is ready, add green onion, chopped cilantro and a squeeze of lime.  Serve hot with corn bread.

Submitter: 
Angela Naumann
Description: 
This savory pinto bean and beef stew is perfect for cilantro lovers.  The combination of Mexican spices makes this a quick family favorite.
