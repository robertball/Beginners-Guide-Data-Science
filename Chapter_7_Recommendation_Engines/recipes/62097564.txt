Title:
Green Goddess Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 garlic clove minced
1 1/4 C mayonnaise (Helmans)
1/2 C. sour cream
3 Tbsp. minced parsley
1 Tbsp. tarragon vinegar ( don't substitute)
4 oz. can anchovies, drained and mashed
salt & pepper
Dill to taste

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients and serve with a fresh, crispy vegables. Good with fresh scallions, carrot sticks, mushroom caps, cherry tomatoes, cheddar cheese cubes, etc.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
A different vegetable dip with a delicious flavor for the vegetable tray. Everyone raved about it at our last cocktail party.
