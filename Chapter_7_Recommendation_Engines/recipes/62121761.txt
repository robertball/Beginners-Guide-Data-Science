Title:
Grilled Chicken Salad with veggies and fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Package of Mescaline Greens
1 Green Apples
1 cup of grapes
1 cup walnuts
1 Cucumbers chopped finely
3 or 4 sliced Strawberries (optional)
2 Chicken Breasts (olive oil, kosher salt, pepper, garlic powder)
Champagne Vinaigrette
bleu cheese

Directions:
Cook Grill chicken as you would like. or use this recipe:
Pound Chicken to keep moiste rub with olive oil, then kosher salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Grill on Medium for about 6 minutes each side
Salad:
Put Entire bag of greens in bowl
Chop apple into squares
cucumbers into finely chopped peices
Slice Strawberries
Blue Cheese into squares
Add walnuts and grapes
Mix with Dressing to taste
Cut chicken place on top

Submitter: 
Tarryn
Description: 
Simply a fabulous twist on the typical grilled chicken salad. Perfect for a hot summer day and certain to please everyone with its variety.
