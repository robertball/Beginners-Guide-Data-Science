Title:
Gourmet Chicken Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 prepared pizza crust like Boboli
1 1/2 c shredded rotisserie chicken
1/2 cup Ranch-style salad dressing
1 bunch asparagus, chopped
1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
1 c grape tomatoes, halved
1/4 c green opinion, chopped
1 1/2 c shredded mozzarella or Monterey Jack cheese
6 slices cooked crumbled bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Place chopped asparagus and olive oil in a bowl; toss to coat.
Spread ranch dressing on crust.
Layer all ingredients on pizza crust.
Bake for 15-20 minutes, until cheese is melted and bubbly.
