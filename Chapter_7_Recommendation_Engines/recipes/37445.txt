Title:
Kraft's Burritos Grande Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (1.25 ounce) package TACO BELL HOME ORIGINALS Taco Seasoning Mix
1 (16 ounce) container BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1 (16 ounce) can TACO BELL HOME ORIGINALS Refried Beans
6 (10 inch) flour tortillas
1 (8 ounce) package KRAFT Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Brown meat; drain.  Add seasoning mix; prepare as directed on package.  Stir in 1 cup of the sour cream.
Spread beans down center of each tortilla.  Top with meat mixture and 1 cup of the cheese.  Fold bottom and top of each tortilla about 1 inch over filling.  Fold each side about two-thirds over filling to completely enclose filling.  Place, seam-side down, in greased 13x9-inch baking dish; cover.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 25 minutes.  Uncover; sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Bake an additional 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Serve with remaining sour cream.
