Title:
Spinach Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
1 diced red bell pepper
1 diced yellow onion
2 t minced garlic
3-4 c washed & dried spinach leaves, chopped
1/4 - 1/2 t garlic powder
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp salt, or to taste
4 oz neufchatel cheese, cubed
3 wedges Laughing Cow french onion cheese
6 eggs
1-3 c 2% milk
1 c cheddar/jack blend shredded cheese, italian blend shredded cheese, or combination of both, divided
1 large tomato, sliced thinly

Directions:
In large skillet, saute red pepper and onion in olive oil until soft and lightly brown.  Add garlic; sautee 1 min.  Add spinach and garlic powder and saute until wilted.  Reduce heat to very low and add neufchatel and laughing cow cheeses. Stir until melted.
In small bowl, beat eggs and milk well. Stir into sauteed vegetables in skillet; add all but 1/4 c of the shredded cheeses.  Stir gently until evenly distributed.  Pour into greased 8x8 baking pan.  Top with tomato slices; cover with reserved cheeses.  Bake at 350 30-40 min, or until middle is firm and cooked through.
Serve warm
