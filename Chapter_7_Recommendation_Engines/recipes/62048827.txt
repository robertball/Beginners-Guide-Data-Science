Title:
Lemon Cheese Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Yellow cake mix with pudding
1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/3 c. sugar
1/3 c. oil
2 eggs
1 T. lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix cake mix, 1 egg, and 1/3 c. oil until crumbly.  Reserve 1 cup on mixture for topping.  Pat remaining mixture lightly into an ungreased 9X13 inch pan.  Bake 15 minutes.
Beat cream cheese, sugar, lemon juice, and 1 egg until light and fluffy.  Spread over baked layer.  Sprinkle with reserved crumb mixture.  Bake 15 minutes longer.  Cool.  Cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Kahmann Harvey
Description: 
A very easy lemon bar recipe.  You could substitute a lemon cake mix for the yellow to make it more lemony, or a chocolate cake mix and omit the lemon, and add vanilla extract and chocolate chips to the cheese mixture.
