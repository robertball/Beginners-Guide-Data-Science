Title:
Perogy Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 large or 40 small frozen perogys
2 chopped onions
1/2 cup milk
2 Tbsp butter
1 cup cooked ham sliced or cubed
2 green peppers
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 10oz cans condensed mushroom soup

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet and saute onions till soft. Add green peppers cook 5 more mins.
In a medium size casserole dish combine perogies, onions, green peppers and ham.
Combine milk and soup and pour over casserole. Top with cheddar and bake 35 mins at 350
optional: add mushrooms when cooking green pepper
