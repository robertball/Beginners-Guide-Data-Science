Title:
Zesty Italian Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984479.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, cut into half slices
1 pound Italian sausage
2 (14.5 ounce) cans RED GOLD® DICED TOMATOES with BASIL, GARLIC & OREGANO or REDPACK® DICED TOMATOES with BASIL, GARLIC & OREGANO
1 (8 ounce) can RED GOLD® TOMATO SAUCE or REDPACK® TOMATO SAUCE
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 (16 ounce) package ditalini or ziti pasta, cooked and drained

Directions:
In a large skillet cook sausage and onion over medium high heat until meat is no longer pink; drain well.
Add RED GOLD(R) or REDPACK(R) DICED TOMATOES WITH BASIL, GARLIC & OREGANO and RED GOLD(R) or REDPACK(R) TOMATO SAUCE and heat through.  Add pasta and cheese and blend together.

Submitter: 
Red Gold®, Inc.
Description: 
When you have very little time to prepare dinner, this recipe will become a family favorite.  Italian sausage and the already Italian flavored Red Gold tomatoes enhance the great flavor of this quick and easy meal.
