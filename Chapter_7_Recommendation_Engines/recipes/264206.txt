Title:
Keto Avocado-Spinach Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6839734.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup coconut milk
1/2 cup water, or more as needed
ice cubes
1/2 medium avocado, pitted and scooped from shell
1/2 cup fresh spinach
2 tablespoons erythritol
1 tablespoon medium-chain triglyceride (MCT) oil
1/2 teaspoon vanilla powder

Directions:
Combine coconut milk, water, ice cubes, avocado, spinach, erythritol, MCT oil, and vanilla powder in a blender. Blend until smooth.
