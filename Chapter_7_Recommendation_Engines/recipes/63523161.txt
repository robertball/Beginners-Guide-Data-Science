Title:
Creamy Lime Chicken with Dill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 T lime juice, divided
3 T butter, divided
1 15 oz. can chicken broth
2 T. flour
1 c. milk
½ tsp. dill weed
Cracked black pepper and sea salt

Directions:
Filet each chicken breasts horizontally to make thin cutlets and place in quart size zipper bag.  Pour 1 T of the lime juice in bag and seal. Flip bag to coat.  Set aside to marinate for about 10-15 minutes.
Melt 1 T butter in skillet over medium-high heat.  Remove chicken from bag, reserving lime juice.  Sprinkle both sides of chicken with salt and pepper.
Cook chicken until lightly browned.  Turn and repeat.  Add chicken broth and reserved lime juice and reduce heat to medium.
In small saucepan, melt 2 T. butter over medium heat. Add 2 T. flour.  Stir and cook until thick and bubbly.  Add milk and continue stirring and simmering until thickened.
When chicken is cooked to 180 degrees, remove from pan and keep warm.  Add thickened cream sauce to skillet.  Stir briskly with a whisk to combine.  Add 1 T. lime juice, ½ tsp. dill, and cracked black pepper to taste.  Simmer until desired thickness.
Return chicken to pan and cook a minute or 2 more.
