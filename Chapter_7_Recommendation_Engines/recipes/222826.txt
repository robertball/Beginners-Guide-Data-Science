Title:
Perfect Garlic and Parmesan Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988997.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sheet (12x36 inches) Reynolds Wrap® Non-Stick Aluminum Foil
4 medium red potatoes, cut into bite-size pieces
2 tablespoons olive oil or vegetable oil
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
Salt and pepper
1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F or grill to medium-high.
Center potatoes evenly on sheet of Reynolds Wrap(R) Non-Stick Aluminum Foil with non-stick (dull) side toward food. Drizzle with oil. Sprinkle with garlic, rosemary, salt and pepper.
Bring up foil sides. Double fold top and ends to seal making one large foil packet, leaving room for heat circulation inside.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes on cookie sheet in oven.
OR grill 15 to 20 minutes in covered grill. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese before serving.
Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese before serving.

Submitter: 
Reynolds Kitchens®
Description: 
Red potatoes seasoned with rosemary and garlic are cooked in a sealed foil packet and served with grated Parmesan cheese for an easy and delicious side dish.
