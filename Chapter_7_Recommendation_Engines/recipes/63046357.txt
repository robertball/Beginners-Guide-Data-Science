Title:
Steak Diane - Grilled Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb filet mignon steaks
1 tsp. fresh ground pepper
1/2 cup butter
2 tsp. dry mustard
8+ oz. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 1/2 cup sliced green onions
1 Tbsp. lemon juice
1 Tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 cup fresh chopped parsley

Directions:
Trim off excess fat from steak.
Sprinkle pepper over both sides, rub in well.
Grill steak to preferred doneness.
While steak grillin -  melt butter in frying pan. Stir in mustard, mushrooms, and onions. Saute 10 minutes or until onions are tender. Mix in lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, salt and parsley. Remove from heat.
Spoon sauce over steak and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Jaymie Hayes Kerstein
Description: 
Steak Diane is traditionally pan fried, but grilling it is pretty delish too!
