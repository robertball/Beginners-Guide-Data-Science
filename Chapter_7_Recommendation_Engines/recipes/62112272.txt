Title:
Slow Cooker Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 - 2.0 pounds of boneless/skinless chicken breast
24oz jar Salsa (your favorite brand)

Directions:
Place chicken in bottom of slow cooker, cover with jar of salsa.  Cook on low for 6-8 hours.
Serve over rice.
Leftover chicken can be shreaded with a fork and used to make chicken/cheese quesadillas.
