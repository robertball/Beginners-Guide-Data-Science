Title:
quick and easy  PB&J;  on a stick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
peanutbutter,(whatever kind you prefer)jelly,(whatever kind)toothpicks, and grapes(if desired)bread

Directions:
take your bread and cut it into squares about 1x1 in size.Next,spread the peatnutbutter on 4 squares and jelly on the other 4 you should have 1 square left over.Take 4 toothpicks and slide 1 peanutbutter and 1 jelly on each toothpick the put a grape on each side.You should have 4 servings. ENJOY!:)
