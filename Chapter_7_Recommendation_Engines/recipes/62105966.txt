Title:
Tuna Casserole Chinese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 family size can (26 oz) Cream of Mushroom soup
1/3 C mayo (not salad dressing!)
3-4 Tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp sesame oil
4 Tbsp freeze dried sliced onion
1 can sliced water chestnuts
4 stalks celery (tops too), sliced
1 standard can of albacore, water packed
6 oz crunchy Chinese noodles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Mix soup, mayo, soy sauce and sesame oil in a good sized bowl. Add water chestnuts, celery, onion and mix well.
Add drained tuna and mix well. Fold in about 2 cups of the Chinese noodles reserving 1/2 cup. Turn into casserole that has been sprayed with a non-stick spray. Top with remaining 1/2 cup noodles. Bake uncovered in oven for about 50 minutes til hot and bubbly.
