Title:
The Easiest and Best Ranch Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground turkey
1 egg
3tbsp Ranch mix *recipe below
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
in a large bowl mix ground turkey, egg, ranch mix and salt and pepper together til blended. divide mixture into four parts and pat each part lightly into a patty. Refrigerate for later use. Freeze for much later use, or pan fry / grill right away. 5 mins per side,or until juices run clear.
*Ranch Mix
1/3 c of dry buttermilk powder
2 tbsp dried parsley
11/2 tsp dried dill
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp onion powder
2 tsp dried onion flakes
1 tsp. gr. black pepper
1 tsp dried chives
1 tsp salt (optional)
3 tbsp. of this mix equals one store bought packet.

Submitter: 
debbie eckstein
Description: 
Turkey burgers made with homemade ranch seasoning mix.  You know EXACTLY what's in your burger. no chemicals, no preservatives, just great flavours.  Can be pan fried or grilled, either way, you come away with very tasty good for you burger! win win.
