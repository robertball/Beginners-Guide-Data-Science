Title:
No Pasta Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large zucchini
salt
1 pound frozen spinach, prepared according to package (or fresh)
1 pound ground turkey
1 sweet onion, chopped
2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon onion power
2 teaspoons garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste
2 jars spaghetti sauce (or make your own)
32 ounces part skim ricotta
16 ounces mozzarella, shredded
8 ounces Parmesan, shredded
2 eggs
3 tablespoons fresh parsley or basil
salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Peel the zucchini.
Slice the zucchini lengthwise.
Place the zucchini on top of paper towels in a single layer and sprinkle with salt.
Cover with another layer of paper towels and cover with a sheet pan with weight on top to help remove excess moisture.
Prepare spinach and remove all moisture using paper towels or cheese cloth.
Brown ground turkey with onion, oregano, onion powder, garlic powder, and salt/pepper.
Drain and return to pot.
Add spaghetti sauce and heat through.
Reserve some mozzarella and/or Parmesan to sprinkle on top.
In a large bowl, mix ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan, eggs, parsley/basil, and salt.
In a 9x13 pan, ladle meat sauce to cover the bottom, layer of zucchini, layer of cheese mixture, layer of spinach, layer of cheese mixture, layer of zucchini, and cover with remaining sauce.
Cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes.
Uncover, sprinkle reserved cheese, and return to oven for 10-15 minutes, until brown and bubbly.
Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Linda Caprice Jordan Diaz
Description: 
This lasagna recipe substitutes zucchini for pasta and is a tasty treat for carb-watchers.
