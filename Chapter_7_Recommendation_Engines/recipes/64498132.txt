Title:
Chicken shish kebab with Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Boneless chicken breast
Zucchini
Tomatoes
Onions
Mushrooms
Marinade
50ml soya sauce
30ml brown sugar
15 ml olive oil
5ml dry mustard
2ml fresh ginger

Directions:
Marinate  chicken 2 to 4 hours.
Put on shish kebab stick or barbecue foil plate
Broil or cook on barbecue until chicken is ready
Heat the Marinade and boil for 10  minutes
