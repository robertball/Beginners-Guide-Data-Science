Title:
Baby Beet Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans whole baby beets- drained and rinsed
1-2 cans mandarin oranges- drained
1 large purple onion
2 cups apple cider vinegar
2 cups orange juice

Directions:
Cut beets into quarters. Add mandarin oranges. Cut purple onion into chunks and add. Add OJ and vinegar.

Submitter: 
acook
Description: 
Cold and refreshing. I don't eat the onions but their flavor infuses into the dish. Adjust OJ and vinegar if the OJ is too sweet or not sweet enough. It tastes even better the next day!
