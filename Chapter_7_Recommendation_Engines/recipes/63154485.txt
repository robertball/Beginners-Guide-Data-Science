Title:
Date Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped, dates
1 tsp soda
1 cup boiling water
1 cup sugar
1 cup flour
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
Nuts, of choice optional

Directions:
Combine the dates, soda and water, mix until soda is dissolved and cool.  Add the sugar, flour, egg, vanilla and nuts.  Spread in a 9 x 9 baking dish that has been sprayed with Pam.  Bake at 350' 25-30 minutes.
