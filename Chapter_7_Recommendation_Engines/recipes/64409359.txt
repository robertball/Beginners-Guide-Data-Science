Title:
effortless ice tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
300 ml boiling water
5 black tea tea bags
10 ml lemon juice
1 can frozen juice concentrate 300 ml apx.
1.2 L cold water
optional  ice

Directions:
1) in a liquid measure add teabags to boiling water,  wait 15-60 min add lemon juice and remove teabags
2) in a 2 L jug add frozen juice concentrate then add the lemon tea mixture to jug,mix,add cold water , and mix again
