Title:
Dad's Day Rib Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2349088.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups ketchup
1 cup water
1/2 cup apple cider
1/2 cup light brown sugar
1 TBL honey
1 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1/2 TBL onion powder
1/2 TBL ground mustard
1/2 fresh squeezed lemon
1 TBL Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
In a medium saucepan combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer. Cover, stirring frequently, for approximately one hour. Remove from heat, let sit for approximately 15 minutes, serve.
