Title:
Buffalo Chicken & Rice Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/3153282.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon I Can't Believe It's Not Butter!(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Spread
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
1 (5.6 ounce) package Knorr(<sup>&reg;</sup>) <sup>&reg;</sup>ice Sides(<sup>&trade;</sup>) - Chicken flavor
1 cup chopped carrots
1 cup chopped celery
2 tablespoons cayenne hot sauce, or more to taste
2 tablespoons crumbled blue cheese

Directions:
Melt Spread in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat and cook chicken, stirring occasionally, until chicken is thoroughly cooked, about 5 minutes. Remove chicken and set aside.
Add 2 cups water, Knorr(R) Rice Sides(TM) - Chicken flavor, carrots and celery to same skillet and prepare according to package directions.
Stir in chicken and hot sauce. Serve sprinkled with blue cheese and, if desired, additional chopped celery.
