Title:
Jim's Fresh Spinach Pasta Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups unbleached all purpose flour
1/4 cup seminola
3 eggs
1 cup fresh spinach leaves

Directions:
Puree spinich and eggs together in a food processor.
add the dry ingredients
remove dough and knead for 1-2 minutes until it's smooth.
Wrap dough tightly in plastic wrap and allow it to rest 20 mins.
Roll out in a pasta maker
To serve plunge into a pot of rapidly boiling salted water until tender approx 2 minutes.
remember this is fresh pasta and does not need to be reconstituted so the boling time is much less.
Remove and serve with your favorite sauce
