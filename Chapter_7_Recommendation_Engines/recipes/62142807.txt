Title:
Chicken Wontons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken breast
1 egg
1 stalk scallion
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 tablespoon sesame oil
50 wonton wrappers

Directions:
Mince chicken. Combine with beaten egg, minced scallion, salt, soy sauce, oil, and sesame oil. Mix well.
Place one teaspoon of filling in center of wrapper. Moisten two adjacent edges and fold into a triangle. Moisten sharp corners and press them together to make a hat shape.
In large pot, heat water to a boil. Add wontons, stirring to prevent sticking. Do not cover. When water reboils, add half a cup cold water. Wait for another boil and add another half cup water. When water boils again, wontons are done.
Serve in soup of heated chicken broth with splash of soy sauce and sesame oil.

Submitter: 
Jaline
Description: 
These boiled chicken wontons are delicately flavored and would go great in a chicken-based soup.
