Title:
Passover Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. salt
2 tablespoons sugar
1 1/2 cups boiling water
2 cups matza meal
1/2 cup melted shortening
4 eggs

Directions:
Dissolve the salt and sugar in the boiling water. Pour over the matza meal in a bowl and mix well. Let stand for 5 min. Add the shortening and the eggs and mix throughly. Form into patties about 3/4 inch thick and 2 1/2 inches wide. Grease the muffin cups with additional shortening and place formed patties in muffin cups. Bake at 450 to 475 degrees for 20-25 minutes.

Description: 
A traditional muffin served for Passover.
