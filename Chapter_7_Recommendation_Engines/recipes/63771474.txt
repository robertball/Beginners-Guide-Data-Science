Title:
Wheat Sandwich Bread-Single Recipe, Long Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup honey (150 grams)
2 1/4 cups warm water
1 1/4 Tablespoon yeast
1/3 cup coconut oil or butter, melted (65 grams oil)
1 cups oats
5.5 cups of whole wheat flour (3 2/3 cup hard wheat berries, 700 grams)
2 Teaspoons salt
1 Tablespoon wheat gluten

Directions:
Proof yeast in liquid. Add remaining ingredients, and allow bread machine to knead/rise. Put in loaf pan, rise until doubled. Bake at 350 degrees, 25-30 minutes.
