Title:
** Chocolate Chip/Graham Dream Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 6-oz. pkg. semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 c. crushed graham crackers
1 c. coconut
Frosting (opt.)
1 6-oz. pkg. chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees.
Mix condensed milk, 1 6-oz. pkg. chocolate chips, graham crackers & coconut together in lg. bowl.
spread in buttered 7x11" baking pan.
Bake 25 min.
Remove from oven & cool before serving.
Optional frosting: sprinkle remaining  6-oz. chocolate chips over warm bars. Let stand until chips soften; spread evenly.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
This recipe goes back to the 1970's-1980's. Yummy, decadent!
