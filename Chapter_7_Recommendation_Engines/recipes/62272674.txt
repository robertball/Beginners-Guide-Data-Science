Title:
Easy Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 box chicken broth or stock
2 cooked chicken breasts
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, drained
1/2 packet taco seasoning
1 tsp.cumin
1 tsp. garlic powder or garlic salt
pinch cayenne

Directions:
Add all to a pot and bring to boil; simmer for 30 minutes to blend flavors.  Best second day.

Submitter: 
Cupcake
Description: 
Super easy recipe -- super delicious. Garnish with grated cheese and a dollop of sour cream.Modified from: Six Can Chicken Tortilla Soup
