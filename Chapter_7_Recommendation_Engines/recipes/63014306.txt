Title:
Christmas Danish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1/2 cup warm water
2. 1 pkg yeast
3. 2 1/2 cups biscuit mix (Buisquick)
4. 1 beaten egg
5. 1 tablespoon sugar
6. 1 16oz pkg cream cheese softened
7. 1/4 cup sugar
8. 1 tablespoon lemon juice
9. 1/4 cup jam ( strawberry a family favorite)

Directions:
night before baking
In mixing bowl combine 1. 2. till yeast dissolves...add 3. 4. 5. mix well..turn unto dusted flour surface and knead dough gently 20 times...roll out on flour surface 14" x 9" (to fit on baking cookie sheet)...mix in small bowl 6. 7. 8. and spread down the center of the dough...with butter knife cut slits 3" long 1" apart fold strips over one another to "braid) the bread...cover and chill overnight
in AM bake 350* oven 20 min remove from oven and spoon 9. down the center bake 5 more min cool 10 min before slicing

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
A traditional Christmas morning danish....prepare the night before..rises all night ready to bake in the am...while you unwrap presents the danish bakes ready to serve for breakfast
