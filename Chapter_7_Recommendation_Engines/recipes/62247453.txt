Title:
Cranberry Center Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/330621.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
1 box turkey flavored stuffing mix (6 oz)
1 can turkey gravy (14 1/2 oz)
1 can whole berry cranberry sauce (14 oz), divided
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
In a large bowl, combine ground turkey, stuffing mix, gravy, salt and egg.
Divide mixture.  Pat half of mixture into an ungreased meatloaf pan.
Place half of cranberry sauce over mixture in meatloaf pan.
Pat the remaining mixture into meatloaf pan.
Top with remaining cranberry sauce.
Bake in preheated oven for approximately 45 minutes.  Check the center of the loaf with a knife.

Description: 
A quick-to-make, wonderful, warm, weekday meal!  Meatloaf recipes constantly evolve in my kitchen, but this variation is not only a personal favorite but frequently requested by my boyfriend.  Any traditional Thanksgiving side dish compliments perfectly.  Yum!
