Title:
Award Winning Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless chicken breasts
1 yellow onion diced
4-6 cloves garlic
24 oz of chicken broth - use "Better than bullion" chicken bullion to make the broth instead of using canned broth.   DOUBLE the bullion needed for 24 oz, to enhance flavor
30 oz. Northern Beans
8 oz. Green Chilies, Mild or Hot, or 4 oz. of each
15 oz. corn, froze or canned.  If using canned, be sure to drain well
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
1 tsp cumin
3/4 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
Small handful cilantro - chopped
8 oz cream cheese
1/4 c. half and half

Directions:
Add chicken to bottom of slow cooker, top with salt , pepper, cumin, oregano, chili powder, and cayenne.
Top with diced onion, minced garlic, northern beans, chilies, corn, broth, and cilantro.
Cook on low for 8 hours
Remove chicken and shred
Add cream cheese and half and half, sir, then cook on high 15 mins or until thickened.
Top with avocado, sour cream, tortilla strips,  jalapenos, or Monterrey cheddar shredded cheese.
