Title:
Oil-free Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon shallot, minced
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Whisk the balsamic vinegar and Dijon mustard together in a small bowl.
Whisk in the remaining ingredients, whisking constantly until fully incorporated.
Let the dressing sit for 10 minutes to allow the flavor to develop.
