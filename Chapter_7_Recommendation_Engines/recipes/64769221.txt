Title:
Jerry's Venison Slow Cooker Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound pork sausage
2 pounds ground venison or lean beef
2 cans of chili beans
1 jar salsa of choice - about 26 oz
1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 can of other type bean for variation - pinto, black, white.... drained and rinsed
1 can diced tomatoes with chilis
1 can mushroom soup
1 can tomato sauce, 15.5-16 ounces
8oz or more of velveeta - cubed
1 packet of chili seasoning - (1.25 oz McCormick)

Directions:
Brown the meats in skillet. Add to large slow cooker, add everything else, give it a stir, and heat on low until heated through.  Forgiving recipe! Use what you have.

Submitter: 
Dawn Forson
Description: 
So easy to put together! Makes a large slow cooker full of thick meaty chili that does not taste like game meat.
