Title:
Heidi's Whoopie Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Devils Food Cake Mix
3/4 C brewed coffee
1/2 C oil
3 eggs
1/4 C flour
3/4 C shortening
3 C powered sugar
pinch salt
1 t vanilla
2 egg whites

Directions:
Mix cake mix, coffee, eggs, oil and flour until well blended. Scoop on to cookie sheet with small cookie scoop and bake at 325 degrees for 8-9 minutes. Meanwhile blend the rest of the ingredients togther in mixer with wisk attachment at high speed until fluffy.
Pipe onto cookie and cover with another cookie to make a sandwich.
