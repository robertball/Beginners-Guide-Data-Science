Title:
Quick marinated pork roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/951342.jpg
Ingredients:
2 - 3 lb pork rib roast or tenderloin
1/4 cup rice vinegar
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/8 cup Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp ginger paste (or 1" fresh ginger, finely chopped/grated)

Directions:
Mix marinade ingredients in a large ziplock bag and then add roast.  You may want to poke the roast with a fork to help marinade penetrate it
Marinate on counter for 30 min - 1 hr, or in fridge overnight
Turn occasionally to make sure it stays coated
Remove from marinade and place roast in a roasting pan or shallow baking dish
discard marinade
Bake in 325 degree oven for 25 - 30 mins per pound or until meat thermometer reaches 155 degrees Fahrenheit
Let stand for 15 mins before carving
