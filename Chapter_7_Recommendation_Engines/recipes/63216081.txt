Title:
Mexican Chilli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp olive oil
1 lb rump roast, cut into small cubes
2 onions, chopped
4 garlic cloves, crushed
4 oz can diced green chilies
2 Tbsp chili powder
1 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp ground cumin
2 bay leaves
3 Â½ cups beef stock
3 oz tomato paste
5 oz enchilada sauce (1/2 can)
2-15 oz cans pinto beans, drained
Cooked white rice (optional)
Optional toppings:
Sour cream
2 cups Monterey Jack cheese, grated
Tortilla chips

Directions:
1) Heat oil in a large pan over high heat and brown meat.
2) Add onions and garlic and cook until softened, 4-5 minutes.
3) Add all spices and cook for 2 minutes.  Stir in chilies, tomatoes paste, enchilada sauce and beef stock.  Bring to boil.
4) Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 50 minutes or until meat is tender.
5) Add beans, mash beans to thicken if desired, simmer for 5 minutes.
Serve in bowls over rice and top with favorite toppings.

Submitter: 
Tina Menard
Description: 
Beef chilli soup, packed with flavor, delicious over rice and topped with cheese or sour cream.  Great for company, camping and freezing.
