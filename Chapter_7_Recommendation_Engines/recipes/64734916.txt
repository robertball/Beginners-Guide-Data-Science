Title:
Low Carb, Gluten Free Sausage Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6239316.jpg
Ingredients:
100g Almond Flour (~1 Cup)
15g Coconut Flour (~2 Tbsp)
15g Gelatin (~2 Packets)
½ tsp Baking Powder
1 egg
1 lb Bulk Sausage (Hot)
1 lb Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325°F.
Combine all ingredients by hand, by stand mixer, or by food processor.
Make the dough into uniform balls (~1-1½ inch in diameter) on lined jelly roll pan (Parchment, Foil, or Silicone, never bake wax paper)
Bake for 25±3min.
Let cool on jelly roll pan until oil is reabsorbed.
May be eaten immediately, refrigerated, or frozen.

Submitter: 
Mary Hillegass
Description: 
As good as bisquick sausage balls, but without the carbs and gluten. Keto Friendly!
