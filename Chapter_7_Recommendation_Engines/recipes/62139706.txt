Title:
Hot Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices of bacon
1 vidalia onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
1 small head of cabbage, shredded
2 Tablespoons of chili powder
1 can diced tomatoes with green chiles

Directions:
Fry bacon until crisp and drain on a paper towel.  In bacon fat, saute onion and  green pepper for five minutes.  Add cabbage to onion/pepper mixture and cook for five more minutes.  Add chili powder and diced tomatoes/green chiles and cook two more minutes.  Mixture should be slightly crisp.  Crumble reserved bacon and mix into cabbage and serve warm.

My family loves this version of slaw.  It is very different and low carb.
