Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2449426.jpg
Ingredients:
12 eggs
1 cup baby kale or spinach
6-8 slices bacon
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 cup  shredded mozzarella
pinch of salt & pepper (per quiche)
pinch of nutmeg (per quiche, optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook bacon until crispy, then crumble. Set aside.
Chop baby kale or spinach finely. Set aside.
Spray muffin tin generously with non-stick cooking spray.
Crack one (1) egg into each section. Scramble gently with small whisk or fork.
Add bacon crumbles, chopped kale, cheeses, and seasoning to each quiche as desired. Stir ingredients gently into scrambled egg, then top with a sprinkle of grated parmesan.
Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until eggs reach desired doneness.
Cool for 5 minutes before removing from muffin tin.
Serve immediately, or store in airtight container in fridge for up to 3 days.

Submitter: 
ESKIMOPIEO
Description: 
Fluffy, savory, single-serving mini frittata/quiche, filled with all the goodies a growing Dinomite could want. Easy to customize for picky eaters!
