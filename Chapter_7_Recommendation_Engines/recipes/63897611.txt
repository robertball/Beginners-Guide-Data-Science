Title:
Croisants with ham and swiss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 packages of crescent drop biscuits
2 lbs honey ham
2 lbs swiss cheese

Directions:
Roll out rolls. Place a thin slice of ham and a thin slice of swiss on each roll. Roll up and cook according to roll package instructions.
