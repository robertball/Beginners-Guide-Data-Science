Title:
Cappacola Eggplant  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices eggplant
8 slices cappacola ( deli style ham can be substituted }
salt and pepper to taste
parmesan cheese
shredded lettuce , sliced tomatoes and sliced onions { vegetables optional }

Directions:
Preheat oven to 500 degree F .
Slice eggplants lengthwise into 8 slices , approximately 1/2 inch in thickness .
Season eggplant with salt and pepper to taste .
Place eggplant slices on a baking sheet , lightly coated with olive oil .
Bake egg plant until soft and brown .
Remove baking sheet from the oven and sprinkle the 8 eggplant slices generously with parmesan cheese .
Place one slice of cappacola or deli style ham on each of the 8 slices of eggplant .
Lower oven temperature to 350 degrees and return eggplant to the oven .
When parmesan cheese melts , remove from the oven .
Garnish four eggplant slices with the shredded lettuce , sliced tomatoes and sliced onions { vegetables optional }.
Cover each of the four eggplant slices with the remaining four slices .
