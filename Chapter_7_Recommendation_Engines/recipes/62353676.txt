Title:
Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small bags peas , thawed
1 chopped sweet onion
1 small bag shredded cheddar cheese
1 lbs cooked and chopped bacon
1 cup golden rasins
1/2 cup sunflower seeds
Dressing
1 cup mayo
2 T wine vinegar
sugar to taste

Directions:
Mix everything together and chill for 1 hour .
