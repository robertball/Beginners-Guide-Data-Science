Title:
Ginger cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One cup white sugar
1 beaten egg
2 cups of flour don't sift
1 teaspoon ground ginger
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 cup of butter or margarine
4 tablespoon of molasses
1 tablespoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon of cinnamon
Pinch of salt

Directions:
Mis together the above.  Chill overnight form into small balls about the size of half a walnut
Roll into granulated sugar
Put on undressed cookie sheet
Bake about 2 inches apart
Bake at 350 for 14 minutes or less
