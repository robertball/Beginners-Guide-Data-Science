Title:
Almond Butter and Blueberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1254736.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 ice cubes
1 cup almond milk (or coconut milk)
1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
1 heaping tablespoon almond butter
1 heaping tablespoon chia/flax seeds
1 scoop vanilla protein

Directions:
Place all ingredients in your blender and combine then enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
My Almond Butter and Jelly Smoothie is a spin off “peanut butter and jelly” and it is my perfect mid-afternoon snack! Sometimes even my lunch!
