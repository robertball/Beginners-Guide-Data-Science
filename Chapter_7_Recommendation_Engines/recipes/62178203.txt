Title:
Sagetti sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef
1 onion (chopped)
1 can mushrooms
1 green pepper(chopped)
oregano
salt
pepper
garlic
salt
2Cans of tomatoes
4 small cans of tomato sauce
parmesean cheese to taste

Directions:
Brown the beef, onions, mushrooms and green peppers. Drain the fat. Add the spices to taste; then add the tomatoes and sauce and cheese.
