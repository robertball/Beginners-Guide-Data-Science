Title:
Chicken and corn chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken breast, cooked and shredded
6 cups chicken broth
1/3 c flour
2 1/2 c corn (frozen or fresh)
3 cloves garlic
1 red bell pepper, diced
3 Russet potatoes, peeled and diced
1 large yellow onion, diced
2 bay leaves
1/4 c butter
1 1/2 c half and half

Directions:
1. Saute onions and peppers in butter. Add garlic.
2. Stir in flour and cook about 2 minutes. Slowly stir in broth.
3. Add potatoes, bay leaves and salt and pepper. Bring to boil and then simmer until potatoes are tender, about 10 minutes.
4. Add in chicken, corn and half and half and simmer another 10-15 minutes.
