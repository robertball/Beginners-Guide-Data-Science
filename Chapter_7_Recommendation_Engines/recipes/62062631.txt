Title:
Chris' Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
28 oz can whole tomatoes, drained
28 oz can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 sweet onion, quartered
1 bunch green onions, using bottom half only
1 bunch cilantro tops only
1/4-1/2 pound jalapenos
1 lemon, juiced
1 lime, juiced
1 orange, juiced
salt, pepper, garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Put whole tomatoes, jalapenos, and spices in a food processor or blender. Whirl until finely chopped. Add onions, cilantro and more spices to taste,fruit juices and whirl again until finely chopped. Pour in a bowl and add diced tomatoes. Mix by hand until combined. Refridgerate until cold. Enjoy! Play with the ammount of peppers and spices to get the combination you can enjoy. Seeding the jalapenos makes them MUCH more mild.
