Title:
Cajun Grilled Tuna Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large lemon, juice and grated rind
1 large lime, juice and grated rind
2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
6 tuna steaks (6 oz each)
2 tablespoons coconut oil

Directions:
Preheat grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
Combine lemon and lime rind and juice, seasoning, garlic powder, salt and pepper in a small bowl.
Rub mixture all over tune steaks.
Using the coconut oil, rub the grill rack or pan to prevent sticking. Place tuna on rack or pan.
Grill covered (with lid or grill cover closed), 2 minutes preside or to desired degree of doneness.
