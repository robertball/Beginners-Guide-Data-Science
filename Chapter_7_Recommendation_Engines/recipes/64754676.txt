Title:
Pressure Cooker Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans green beans, drained
4 slices of bacon
1 onion, chopped
1/4 c water
garlic salt
pepper

Directions:
Turn your pressure cooker to high heat & cook the bacon until crisp. Remove from pot onto plate lined with a paper towel and let cool.
Add chopped onion to bacon drippings in the pressure cooker and cook until translucent.
Crumble the bacon (or slice into bits) and add back to the pot.
Add drained cans of green beans and water to bacon mixture. Season to taste with garlic salt (1+ tsp) and pepper (1/2+ tsp) and stir.
Secure lid & cook on high for 10 minutes. Release pressure either way (quick or natural).

Submitter: 
Alyssa Long
Description: 
Quick, easy green beans. Start these while you're cooking your main dish & forget about them till you're ready to eat. These are not crisp green beans but soft to the bite.
