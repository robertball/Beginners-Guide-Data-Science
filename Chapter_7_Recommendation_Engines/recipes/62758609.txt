Title:
Mung dal (Pesarapappu) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c mung dal
1/4 tsp turmeric
3 1/2 c water
1/2 tsp salt
4 T oil
1/2 T cumin seed
1 T ginger paste
2 - 4 hot green chilies, chopped
1 T lemon juice
4 T cilantro, chopped
1 tsp cayenne or hot chili pepper (optional)

Directions:
Place the turmeric, salt, dal, and water in a saucepan.  Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low and simmer the beans for 30 to 40 mins, til the beans are soft.  Stir occasionally to make sure they do not stick or burn.
Turn off the heat.  Stir or beat with a whisk to break the dal up.  Return to low heat.
Measure all remaining ingredients out and place them close to hand.
heat the oil in a small fry pan over med-high heat.  Add the cumin seed - when they start to brown (about 10 secs) add the ginger and chilies.  Continue frying for a couple of minutes, then pour into the dal and stir thoroughly.
Add the lemon juice and about half the cilantro and stir.
Serve with rice, garnished with some of the remaining cilantro.
