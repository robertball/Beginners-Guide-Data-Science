Title:
Branston daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
ice cubes
1.5 fluid ounces rum
1.5 fluid ounces triple sec
1.5 fluid ouces branston pickle
40g brie, or other soft cheese
Worcestershire sauce
Wedge of stilton

Directions:
Pour rum, triple sec, branston pickle & soft cheese into a blender and pulse once or twice to combine. Add ice cubes and blend until drink is slushy and no ice chunks remain, 1 to 2 minutes.
Serve in a martini glass, add a splash of Worcestershire sauce & add the wedge of stilton into the edge of the glass.

Submitter: 
Neil Griffiths
Description: 
This deliciously sweet & tangy cocktail is perfect for brunch and those wanting an alternative to a bloody mary.
