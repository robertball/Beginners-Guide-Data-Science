Title:
Beef, tomato and green pepper Chop Suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb round steak(sliced 1/2in thick slices
2Tbsp soy sauce
2Tbsp salad oil
1Tbsp cooking wine
4tsp cornstarch
1/2tsp sugar
1/4tsp ground ginger
2 green pepper (cut in lengthwise stips
2 onions-quatered
1tsp salt
2 tomatoes cut into wedges
3C hot cooked rice

Directions:
About 2 hours before serving:Cut steak lenthwise slices. In large bowl combine the steak, soy sauce, oil,wine, cornstarch, sugar and ginger. Set aside.
In large skillet or wok, over medium high heat in 2Tbsp oil cook pepper, onion and salt. Stir quickley and frequently as they cook until the vegies are tneder. Remove to another bowl. In the same skillet or wok cook the steak in 3Tbsp oil. Cook the steak until the desired doneness.Add the vegetables. Put tomatoes ontop and heat through-don't cook the tomatoes. Serve over hot rice.
