Title:
Lamb Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. lamb stew meat
1 garlic cloves
4 med carrots, sliced
6 pearl onions
3 small potatoes
1/2 tsp. basil
10 oz. bag of frozen peas
2 Tbsp. parsley
salt;pepper

Directions:
Dredge meat in flour and brown in hot shortening in dutch oven.
Add 3 cups water, garlic, 2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper
Cover, simmer 1 hour or til meat almost tender
Add diced veggies and basil
Cook uncovered 20 min.
Add peas, parsley and dumplings, cover again and cook for 18 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Kim Snyder
Description: 
My favorite stew that my mom always made. It takes me back home everytime I taste it.
