Title:
1-2-3 Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990504.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 pounds chicken legs and thighs
1 (26 ounce) jar Ragu® Chunky Pasta Sauce

Directions:
Heat oil in 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat and brown chicken. Stir in pasta sauce. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer covered 45 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked.

Submitter: 
Ragu®
Description: 
For this tasty and easy cacciatore, chicken is browned and then simmered in flavorful pasta sauce.
