Title:
Crab omelette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small potato, peeled and diced
2 tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion. chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
120g crab meat
Salt and pepper to taste
1 small tomato, diced
1 box (42.5 g) raisins
60g peas
1 red pepper (capsicum), chopped
3 eggs, beaten

Directions:
Fry potatoes in olive oil and set aside. Using the same pan, add more olive oil if necessary and saute onion and garlic.
Add crab meat and season with salt and pepper. Stir to mix and cover. Cook 2 minutes.
Add tomatoes, cook 2 minutes, then add raisins, peas and red pepper. Mix and cook another 2 minutes.
Pour eggs over the mixture and let eggs cook until set. Turn the omelette over once. Cook another minute.
Transfer mixture to a serving platter. Serve hot with bread or rice.

Submitter: 
lola
Description: 
Crab and egg go very well together in this delightful dish. Fresh crab meat is best but if you can't find it, use canned.
