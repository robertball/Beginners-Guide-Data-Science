Title:
cherry pie delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz. package of cream cheese
1 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
1/8 cup of lemon juice
2 cans of cherry pie filling
2 graham cracker pie crusts

Directions:
in a bowl beat the first 3 ingredients with a blender until smooth
pour half of the mixture into each graham cracker crust and set in the fridge until firm or 6 hours.
add each can of cherry pie filling to each pie and set in fridge until cold and firm. ready to eat!
