Title:
Mom's Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Basic sweet roll dough
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/2 cup sugar
2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3/4 cup raisins (optional)
1 cup powdered sugar, sifted
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 tablespoon milk

Directions:
1. Roll each half of Basic Sweet Roll dough into a 12x8‚Äù rectangle. Brush each with melted butter.
2. Combine sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle over dough. Sprinkle with raisins, if desired.
3. Roll each piece, starting with long side; seal seams. Slice each into 12 rolls.
4. Place rolls, cut side down in two greased 9 x 1 1/2 inch round baking pans.
5. Cover and let rise until double (about 35 minutes.)
6. Bake at 375 degrees for 18 to 20 minutes.
7. Combine powdered sugar, vanilla, and enough milk to create an icing to drizzling consistency. Drizzle icing over warm rolls.
