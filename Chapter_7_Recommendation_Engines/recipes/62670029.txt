Title:
Fiesta Fry Pan Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound Ground Turkey or Beef
1 Package Taco Seasoning
2 Cups Water
1 Can RoTel
1 Can Black Olives
1 Cup Frozen Corn or 1 Can Corn
1 1/2 Cups Uncooked Instant Rice
Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
In a skillet, cook turkey until browned.
Stir in taco seasoning, water, olives, RoTel and corn; bring to a boil. Add rice. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.
Sprinkle with cheese; cover and let stand until the cheese is melted.
