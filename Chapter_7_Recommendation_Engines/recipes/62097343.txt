Title:
Best  Double CrustPeach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/8114915.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
3 C. plain flour
1/2 t. salt
2 T. sugar
1 C. butter flavored shortening
1/4 C. cold water
Filling:
2 can peach pie filling
1/2 t. apple pie spice
1/4 t. grated citrus peel (lemon or orange)
1/4 C. brown sugar
1 stick of butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Crust:
Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
Add shortening and mix well, using pastry blender.
Add water and form into a ball.
Roll out half the dough for the bottom crust and the other half for the top crust.
Press bottom crust into baking dish and prick in a few places - bake for 10 min., remove from oven to cool.
Filling:
Mix pie filling, cinnamon, and citrus peel in a bowl.
Pour into baking dish over bottom crust.
Sprinkle brown sugar over the top of filling mixture.
Slice sick of butter into slabs and dot over the mixture.
Place top crust over mixture, and cut slits to vent.
Bake for about 40-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
3D's Mom
Description: 
This cobbler has a  top and bottom crust. It has always been a hit with my husband and kids. It can be served warm, alone or with a scoop of ice cream.
