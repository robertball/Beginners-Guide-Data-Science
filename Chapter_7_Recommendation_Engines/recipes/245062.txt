Title:
Banana Oat Energy Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3341620.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups rolled oats
2 bananas, mashed
2 carrots, grated
1 apple, grated
1 cup unsweetened applesauce
1/2 cup chopped peanuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
Mix oats, bananas, carrots, apple, applesauce, and peanuts together in a bowl; spread into the prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes.
