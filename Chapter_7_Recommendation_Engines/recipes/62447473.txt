Title:
Very Versatile Curried Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
1 medium unpeeled granny smith, chopped
1 stalk celery, thinly sliced
1 scallion, thinly sliced
3/4 cup light mayonnaise
2 teaspoons fresh-squeezed lime juice
3/4 teaspoon curry powder

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first four ingredients. Combine mayonnaise, lime juice and curry powder; add to chicken mixture and stir to coat. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve. Serve 1 tsp of salad on each cracker.  If desired, top with chopped walnuts and/or fresh parsley or cilantro.

Submitter: 
Dawn Eberly Wampler
Description: 
This is a versatile recipe that can be adjusted for many tastes.  Here, my version lightens up the fat content and was served with crackers.Modified from: Curried Chicken Tea Sandwiches
