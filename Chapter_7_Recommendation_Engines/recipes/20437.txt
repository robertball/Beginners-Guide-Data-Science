Title:
Kiwi-Lime Refresher Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger kiwifruit flavored syrup
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger lime flavored syrup
6 fluid ounces carbonated water
4 fluid ounces orange juice

Directions:
Fill a 20 ounce glass to the top with ice. Pour in kiwi and lime syrup, carbonated water,  then top with orange juice. Stir to blend before enjoying this refreshing beverage.

Submitter: 
JoLee
Description: 
Kiwi and lime flavor this carbonated orange juice drink.
