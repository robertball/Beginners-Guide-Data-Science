Title:
Sauerbraten Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 beef bouillon cube
3 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp raisins
2 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
1/4 cup coarse gingersnap crumbs
12 pre-cooked meatballs
Hot cooked rice

Directions:
Bring water and bouillon cube to boiling. Add rest of ingredients except meatballs and rice. Cook and stir till smooth. Add thawed or frozen meatballs. Cover and simmer about 15 minutes, stirring now and then. Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Barbara
Description: 
This is a very good (though probably not authentic) German Sauerbraten Sauce. Easy and quite tasty.
