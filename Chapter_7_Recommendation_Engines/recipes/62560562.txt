Title:
Winter Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
3 cloves garlic, chopped
2 onions, chopped
1 cup chopped celery
5 carrots, sliced
4 cups chicken broth
2 cups water
2 cups V8 juice
1/2 cup red wine (optional)
1 15-oz. can kidney beans, drained & rinsed
1 15-oz. can cannelini beans, drained & rinsed
2 cups baby spinach, rinsed OR
1 box frozen chopped spinach, thawed & squeezed dry
3 med, potatoes, washed and diced, unpeeled
1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup small pasta (ditalini, shells)
2 tablespoons grated Pecorino Romano cheese for topping
1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions:
In a large stock pot, over medium-low heat,  heat olive oil and saute garlic for 2 to 3 minutes.  Add onion and saute for 4 to 5 minutes.  Add celery and carrots, saute for 1 to 2 minutes.
Add chicken broth, 1 cup water and V8, bring to boil, stirring frequently. If desired add red wine at this point.  Reduce heat to low and add kidney and cannelini beans, spinach leaves, potatoes, oregano, basil, salt and pepper.  Simmer for 30 to 40 minutes, the longer the better.
Fill a medium saucepan with water and bring to a boil.  Add macaroni and cook until tender.  Drain water and set aside.
Once pasta is cooked and soup is heated through place 2 tablespoons cooked pasta into individual serving bowls.  Ladle soup on top of pasta and sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.  Spray with olive oil and serve.
