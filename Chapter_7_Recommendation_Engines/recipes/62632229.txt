Title:
Pecan Pie Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. light brown sugar
2 eggs (beaten)
2/3 c. softened butter
1/2 c. all-purpose flour
1 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Greaes muffin pan (minnitarure cup pa works best). Mix together the brown sugar, eggs and butter. Then add flour and nuts. Mix by hand, and then pour mixture into muffin pan until each muffin cup is 3/4 full. Bake 20-25 minutes for regular sized muffins, less time for miniatures.
