Title:
Grandma Jean's Chocolate Puffs (Viva Puffs) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6260540.jpg
Ingredients:
Cookie base:
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1 cup butter
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. baking soda
2 1/2 cups flour
2 Tbsp. milk
Marshmallow topping:
2 cups sugar
1 cup water
2 pkg. gelatin (dissolved in 1/2 cup cold water)
2 egg whites
Jam
8 oz. milk or semi-sweet chocolate
2 tsp. butter

Directions:
Combine cookie ingredients, chill, roll into balls, flatten, and bake.
Boil sugar and water for 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
Add gelatin (dissolved in cold water).
Add stiffly beaten egg whites. Beat until light and feathery.
Melt chocolate and butter together.
Spread jam on cookie, then marshmallow. Lastly, cover with melted chocolate.
Once chocolate hardens, put in freezer. Put wax paper between the layers.
