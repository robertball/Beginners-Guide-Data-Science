Title:
Chocolate Mousse Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz semi-sweet baking chocolate
1/2 C water
8 eggs, separated
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
2/3 C sugar

Directions:
Butter 9‚Äù pie plate. Sprinkle with sugar.
Melt chocolate with water in sauce pan on low. Stir till smooth.
Stir in eggs yolks, add vanilla.
Beat egg whites. Gradually add sugar to form stiff peaks. Stir small amount into chocolate mixture, then fold chocolate into remaining meringue mixture.
Pour 3 C into pie plate. Chill remaining mix. Bake at 350 for 25 min or until set. Cool slightly - chill 1 hour.
Spoon chilled mix into shell. Chill 3 hours.
