Title:
Cheddar, Cauliflower & Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/783872.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups cauliflower,trimmed and chopped
1 cup potato,peeled and diced
2-10oz.cans condensed chicken broth,undiluted
3 Tbsp.butter
2/3 cup onion,chopped
1/2 tsp.dried thyme
3 Tbsp.flour
3 cups whole milk
2 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium saucepan,cook cauliflower and potato in broth untill tender,about 12-15 min.Add a little water to cover vegetables if necessary.Meanwhile in large pot,melt butter over medium heat and saute onion and thyme untill onion is tender,about 5 min.Stir in flour and cook stirring for 1 min.Gradually add milk,whisking constantly and cook untill slightly thickened.Stir in cauliflower,potatoes and broth.When mixture is heated through,remove from heat and add cheese,a little at a time,stirring untill cheese melts.
