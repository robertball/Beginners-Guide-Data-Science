Title:
Chicken Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F7305024.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (<sup>&reg;</sup>egular or 98% Fat Free)
½ cup milk
4  skinless, boneless chicken breasts
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 ½ cups Pepperidge Farm(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Herb Seasoned Stuffing, finely crushed
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
Mix 1/3 cup soup and 1/4 cup milk in shallow dish.  Coat chicken with flour.  Dip into soup mixture.  Coat with stuffing.
Place chicken on baking sheet.  Drizzle with butter.  Bake at 400 degrees F for 20 minutes or until done.
Mix remaining soup and milk in saucepan.  Heat through.  Serve with chicken.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Oven-fried chicken is crunchy with a coating of herb-seasoned stuffing.
