Title:
Fish Fillets Italiano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion, thinly sliced
1 head  garlic, minced
2 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1/2 cup black olives, pitted and sliced
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup dry white zifandel wine
5 large fillets cod/swai
2 tablespoons fresh chopped basil
salt and pepper to taste
pinch of rosemary
pinch of tyme

Directions:
In a large frying pan, heat oil over medium heat.  Saute onions and garlic in olive oil until softened.
Stir in tomatoes, olives, parsley, and wine.  Simmer for 5 minutes.
Place fillets in sauce.  Simmer for about 5 more minutes, or until fish turns white.

Submitter: 
Jose Olmeda
Description: 
Cod and haddock fillets work well for this braised dish.  An extra easy and quick way to fix a superb tasting fish dish!  Serve over rice.Modified from: Fish Fillets Italiano
