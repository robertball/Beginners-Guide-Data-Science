Title:
Mom's Red Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb pinto beans
ham bone or hocks
2 onions, chopped
2 green bell peppers, chopped
4 stalks celery, chopped
1 C parsley
1/8 C butter
1/4 C Worchesshire sauce
tabasco to taste

Directions:
Soak beans overnight. Simmer beans, ham, onion, pepper and parsley for 2 hours to until tender. Add butter, worcheschire and tobasco and simmer for 30 minutes more.
