Title:
Pork Tenderloin with Creamy Dijon Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 - 3 lbs pork tenderloin
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 pckg sliced mushrooms
2 large spoonfuls of sour cream
2 tbsp dijon mustard
1 medium onion
1/4 cup of white wine
1-2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
salt, pepper, garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Slightly brown pork tenderloin in olive oil on all sides over medium heat in a skillet. While browning the meat, season each side with salt, pepper and garlic.
Pour wine into bottom of slow cooker then place pork on top. In the same skillet, cook onion and mushrooms until slightly tender, then add to slow cooker.
In a bowl, combine soup, mustard and sour cream then spread over meat. Cook on low 7-8 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
This pork tenderloin cooks all day in the slow cooker with a creamy dijon and sour cream sauce. It melts in your mouth. I serve it with mashed potatoes and roasted green beans.
