Title:
Spice Cake Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white sugar
1 tbl corn starch
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 cup milk
1/4 cup margarine
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Combine sugar, corn starch, nutmeg, milk, and margarine, heat over medium heat.
Cook until desired thickness, remove from stove and add vanilla.
