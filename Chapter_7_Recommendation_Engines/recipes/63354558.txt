Title:
Fire Roasted-Ranch Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/892308.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ( 10 oz.) can refrigerated pizza crust
2 al fresco all natural Sweet Italian Chicken Sausages, diced
1/3 of a red pepper, cut in strips
1/3 of a yellow pepper, cut in strips
1/4 of an onion, cut in strips
salt and pepper, to taste
1/4 cup Progresso Recipe Starters fire roasted tomato cooking sauce, or tomato sauce
1/8 tsp. oregano
1/4 cup Ranch dressing
Several shakes of Frank's Hot Sauce, to taste
30 fresh baby spinach leaves
1 cup mozzarella cheese
1 cup fiesta blend cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Roll out the pizza dough onto a jelly roll pan and cook for 10 minutes.
While pizza dough is cooking, brown the diced sausages in a skillet sprayed with cooking spray. Remove from skillet.
Add strips of peppers and onions and cook until done.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Add the oregano to the fire roasted tomatoes (or tomato sauce) and spread over half the pizza crust.
Spread the Ranch dressing over the other half of the pizza crust.
Shake Frank's Hot Sauce several times over the Ranch dressing side.
Top with sausage, peppers, and onions.
Add the spinach leaves, more or less to cover the top.
Add the mozzarella cheese to the fire roasted tomato side.
Add the fiesta blend cheese to the Ranch side.
Bake for 8 minutes.

Description: 
I wanted to fix a pizza one night, but I couldn't decide what kind I wanted. So I fixed two in one.
