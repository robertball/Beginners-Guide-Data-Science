Title:
Mashed Potatoes with Ham & Herb Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1556307.jpg
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch chunks
1/4 C. olive oil
4 scallions, thinly sliced
4 mushroom caps, thinly sliced
1/4 chicken broth
1/4 lb. ham, diced
2 T. minced fresh parsley leaves
1 tsp. thyme
1/2 tsp. rosemary
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1 T. white wine vinegar
1/2 C. sour cream
1/8 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
In large saucepan, cook potatoes in boiling water for 20 minutes or until tender. Meanwhile, in 10 inch skillet heat oil over medium heat. Add scallions;saute 1 minute. Add mushrooms, saute 2 minutes. Remove pan from heat.; add chicken broth, ham, parsley,thyme, rosemary, salt, red pepper flakes and vinegar. Mix until well blended. Drain potatoes; place in large bowl. Add sour cream and mash until smooth. Add salt & pepper to taste. Arrange potatoes on a long platter, make a trench in potatoes and fill with ham mixture.

Ursula
Description: 
Very easy recipe! Left over mashed potatoes work just as well.
