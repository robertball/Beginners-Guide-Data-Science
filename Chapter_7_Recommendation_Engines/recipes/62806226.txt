Title:
Dinner  Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/658792.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup warm almond milk (70 to 80 degrees F)
1/2 cup warm buttermilk
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
3/4 teaspoons salt
2 1/2 cups  flour
1/2 cup cornmeal
3/4 cup oatmeal
1/4 cup barley flour
2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
In bread machine pan, place all ingredients in order suggested by manufacturer.  Select dough setting (check dough after 5 minutes of mixing; add 1 to 2 tablespoons of water or flour if needed).
When cycle is completed, turn dough onto a lightly floured surface.This can be made without a bread machine, put warm milk and sugar and butter in a bowl, let sit till puffy(just a few minutes) add in the eggs, salt, and the flour slowly, when it pulls from the sides of the bowl, turn out and knead until smooth.  Divide into 24 portions; shape into balls.  Place in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan.  Cover and let rise in a warm place for untill double.  Bake at 375 degrees F for 13-16 minutes or until golden brown.
