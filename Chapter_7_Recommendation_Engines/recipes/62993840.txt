Title:
The Best & Easiest Chicken Parmesan Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T Olive Oil
2 cloves of crushed Garlic
couple of shakes of Red Pepper Flakes (Optional)
5-6 boneless chicken breasts
2 C Tomato Sauce
1/4 C Chopped fresh Basil leaves
8 oz grated Mozzarella Cheese, halved
4 oz grated Pamesan Cheese, halved
5 oz pkg of Garlic Croutons (Secret Ingredient)

Directions:
In a standard 9x13" casserole dish, add the oil, garlic and red pepper to the bottom of the dish. Layer the breasts on top. Pour the tomato sauce over the breasts.
Sprinkle the basil, half of mozzarella & parmesan cheeses and garlic croutons.
Now layer on the remaining cheese.
Bake in a preheated 350 degrees oven for 35 minutes.
