Title:
Blueberry Buckle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup of sugar
1/4 cup butter
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
2 cup flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup blueberries
Topping:
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup flour
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup soft butter

Directions:
Mix sugar, butter & egg together.
Stir in milk.
Sift in flour, baking powder, and salt.
Blend in berries.
Spread in 8x8 greased pan.
Sprinkle with topping.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.

Cindy Yonkman
