Title:
Cottage Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/4 pounds of top sirloin beef, cut into 1/2" chunks
salt
pepper
1 T vegetable oil
1 small onion
1 package frozen peas and carrots, thawed
2 - 12 oz jars of prepared gravy
1/2 tsp thyme
3 cups prepared mashed potatoes
paprika

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 - coat a 3 qt casserole dish with cooking spray
sprinkle beef with salt and pepper
in a large skillet heat oil over medium heat, brown beef and onions 6-8 minutes, drain off excess liquid of needed
add peas and carrots, gravy and thyme, mix well and pour into casserole dish.  Place mashed potatoes over top
bake for 45 minutes or until heated
