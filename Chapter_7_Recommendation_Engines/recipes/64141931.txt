Title:
Dad's Golden Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup whole wheat pastry flour (Bob's Red Mill)
1/2 tsp salt
4 tsp baking powder
1 Tbsp brown sugar
1 egg
1 cup milk
1/4 cup oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.  In a bowl mix dry ingredients together.  Add egg, milk and oil.  Beat for 1 minute or until smooth.
Bake in a greased 8 inch square pan for 20-25 minutes at 425 degrees F.
