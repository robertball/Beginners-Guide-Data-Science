Title:
Old Fashioned Gingerbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/308739.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white sugar (360 calories)
1/2 cup margarine (840 calories)
1 egg, beaten (70 calories)
3/4 cup brown sugar + 1/4 cup hot water (297 calories)
2 1/2 cups flour (1100 calories)
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda (12 calories)
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon (6 calories)
1 teaspoon ground ginger (6 calories)
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves (3 calories)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup hot water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â° F.
Grease a glass loaf pan with shortening.
In a large bowl, cream together the sugar and margarine.
Beat in the egg and then the dissolved brown sugar water.
Add the baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves until combined.
Add the flour,  stirring in the hot water to help with blending.
Pour into prepared pan.
Bake 1 hour until a knife in center comes out clean.
COVER WITH PLASTIC WRAP to avoid drying out.
