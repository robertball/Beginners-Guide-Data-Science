Title:
Microwave Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c cooked white rice (minute rice, short grain)
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 c sugar
1/2 c raisins
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 c milk or half-n-half

Directions:
Mix rice, eggs, sugar, raisins, vanilla and cinnamon in 2 quart glass casserole. Set aside.
Microwave 2 c milk 3 - 4 minutes until hot but not quite boiling.  Pour into rice mixture.  Cover with tight fitting lid or plastic wrap.  Microwave on low (30% -- 300 watt) for 20 - 25 minutes. Remove cover immediately, allow to stand on solid heat-proof surface until center is set.

Submitter: 
anmlnrs
Description: 
Super easy rice pudding recipe for the microwave.  Recipe was made using 1000 watt microwave. Adjust accordingly.
