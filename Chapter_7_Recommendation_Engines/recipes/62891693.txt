Title:
Creamy Roasted Hatch Pepper Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-7 roasted hatch green chilies, seeds and skin removed
1 jalapeno pepper seeded
1 1/2 cup ranch dressing
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 Cup Cilantro Leaves
Juice of 1 lime
1/4tsp salt

Directions:
In a blender, combine all ingredients.  Let rest for 1 hour or more for best flavor.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
CHRISPENTINA
Description: 
This zippy dip will have your mouth singing for more! Roasted Hatch Green Chilies, Jalapenos and creamy ranch dressing combine to make the perfect dressing for salad and a mouthwatering dip to die for!  Let rest for a mouth watering melding of heat and creaminess!
