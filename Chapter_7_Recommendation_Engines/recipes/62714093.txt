Title:
Cream Cheese Cherry Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint ripe cherry tomatoes (note: Real/true cherry tomatoes these days are hard to find.  You have to have the right size tomato to have it a nice bite size.   But if you can find them, this is a great appetizer.)
3 oz. cream cheese, room temperature
1/4 cup mayonaise
1/4 t. minced garlic
1 t. dry basil
1/8 t. salt
1/8 t. black pepper
fresh parsley chopped or dried parsley

Directions:
Wash tomatoes.  Slice off the tops of the tomatoes with a sharp knife.
Carefully scoop out seeds and core.
Drain upside down on paper towels while preparing the filling.
In a small bowl beat the cream cheese until smooth.  Add mayonaise, garlic, basil, salt and pepper.
Fill tomatoes with the filling.
Set filled tomatoes on serving platter and sprinkle tops with parsley.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
Not only are they yummy, this recipe makes a pretty platter at a holiday gathering.  Set the stuffed tomatoes on top of a lettuce leaf lined platter.
