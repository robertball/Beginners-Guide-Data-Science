Title:
French Fries (oven baked) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/656666.jpg
Ingredients:
12 large baking potato
3/4 cup olive oil (or vegetable oil)
2 tablespoons paprika
2 tablespoons garlic powder
2 tablespoons chili powder
2 tablespoons onion powder
Salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F .
Wash and cut potatos into wedges. Mix  oil, paprika, garlic powder, chili powder and onion powder together. Coat potatoes with oil/spice mixture and place on a baking sheet.  Turn the potatoes half way through baking them.
Bake for about 30 minutes or until donein preheated

Submitter: 
Philmont
Description: 
For traditional french fries,  spray the cut potato wedges with cooking spray (pam) or lightly coat with oil and sprinkle with salt.
