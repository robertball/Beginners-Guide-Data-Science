Title:
Spicy Tuna Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained
2 tablespoons Lite Miracle Whip
1 1/2 teaspoons cream-style horseradish sauce
1 tablespoon chopped dill pickles
2 leaves lettuce
4 slices bread
2 slices tomato
2 thin slices  red onion

Directions:
Combine the drained tuna, lite miracle whip, horseradish sauce, and pickles in a small bowl until evenly mixed. take 2 slices of bread, and top with a leaf of lettuce. Spread the tuna mixture onto the lettuce leaves. Top with the tomato and red onion slices, and finish with the remaining slices of bread.

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
This is a great way to serve tuna from a can. The more horseradish, the spicier. Delicious quick lunch or dinner!Modified from: Spicy Tuna Sandwich
