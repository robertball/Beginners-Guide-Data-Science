Title:
Keto Breakfast Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4970791.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
2 tablespoons virgin coconut oil
1 tablespoon butter
2 cups hot coffee
1 pinch ground cinnamon

Directions:
Place egg, coconut oil, and butter in a blender. Add hot coffee; blend on low until combined, about 1 minute. Pour into a coffee cup and sprinkle cinnamon on top of the froth.
