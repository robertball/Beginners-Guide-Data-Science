Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/945068.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Chicken breast (OK if frozen)
1 box of stuffing
1 cup of chicken broth
1 can of cream of chicken
1/2 c water

Directions:
Layer bottom of crock pot with chicken.
Poor chicken broth over chicken.
Mix stuffing with can of soup and 1/2 c water. Layer mixture on top of chicken.
Cook 6-8 hours on low.

Submitter: 
Sidnie Anderson
Description: 
A set it-and-forget it recipe for Chicken and stuffing lovers.
