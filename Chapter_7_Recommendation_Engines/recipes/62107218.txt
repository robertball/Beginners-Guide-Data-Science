Title:
Chocolate Marshmallow Melt Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter or margarine
2 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1/4 cup peanut butter
3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips
3/4 cup miniature marshmallows
1 cup quick or old-fashioned oats, uncooked
1/2 cup flaked coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Grease cookie sheets or line with foil.
Combine the butter or margarine, flour, baking soda, and baking powder in a large bowl and mix until well-blended.  Beat in granulated sugar, brown sugar, eggs, and peanut butter.  Add chocolate chips, marshmallows, and oats and mix with a spoon or bare hands.
Shape dough in 1-inch balls.  Flatten slightly and press into coconut on one side.  Place 2 inches apart on baking sheets.
Bake about 10 minutes, or until light golden brown.  Remove to cool.

Submitter: 
Jenn G.
Description: 
Beautiful, big and puffy cookies with peanut butter, chocolate chips, marshmallows, and oatmeal, dusted with coconut.
