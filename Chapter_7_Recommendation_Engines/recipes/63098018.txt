Title:
Cranberry Pork Chops (SC) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops (1.5 lbs)
1/2  back fresh cranberries or 1 can whole cranberries
1/2 cup apple juice
1/4 cup sugar
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cloves
2 large sweet potatoes

Directions:
Place pork chops in slow cooker with 1/2 cup water.
In a bowl, mix cranberries, both sugars, apple juice and cloves. Pour over pork chops. Cook on low for 8 hours.
Bake sweet potatoes in oven and mash pulp. Serve pork chops over mashed sweet potatoes.
