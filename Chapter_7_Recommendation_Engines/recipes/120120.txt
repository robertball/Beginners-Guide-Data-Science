Title:
Fried Chicken Coating Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990029.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup paprika
2 tablespoons salt
2 tablespoons pepper
1 tablespoon ground mustard
1 tablespoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
ADDITIONAL INGREDIENTS:
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1 (3 1/2) pound broiler-fryer chicken, cut up
vegetable oil

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first eight ingredients; mix well. Store in an airtight container in a cool dry place for up to 1 year.

Submitter: 
Eileen  Frank
Description: 
Eileen Frank relies on this made-in-minutes mixture to cook up a down-home chicken dinner. 'We think this flavorful coating tastes a lot like the one at a well-known chicken restaurant chain,' she writes form Castor, Alberta.
