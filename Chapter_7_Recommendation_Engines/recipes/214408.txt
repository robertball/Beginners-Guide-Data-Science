Title:
Smirnoff Moscow Mule Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991379.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka
3 fluid ounces ginger beer
1 teaspoon sugar syrup
1/4 fluid ounce lime juice
1 sprig mint
1 slice lime

Directions:
In a glass with ice, add Smirnoff No.21 Vodka, sugar syrup, and lime juice.
Top with ginger beer and stir.
Garnish with mint sprig and lime slice.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
This famous cocktail has a mule kick of spicy ginger beer, combined with vodka and lime juice. Serve with a slice of lime and a mint sprig.
