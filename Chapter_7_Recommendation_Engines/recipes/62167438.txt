Title:
Baked Apicots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 – 16 oz. cans apricot halves - drained
1-1/2 tubes Ritz crackers – crushed
¾ c. brown sugar
1-1/2 sticks butter – melted

Directions:
In a 2 quart baking dish, layer ½ cup crushed Ritz crackers, 1 can apricots, 1/3 cup brown sugar; ½ cup crushed Ritz crackers, repeat and repeat.  Bake at 325 for 30 to 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
CATHYRAE
Description: 
Worked with a friend on this recipe.  Super simple, rich and delicious.  Great side for Thanksgiving and all of your Holiday Entertaining.
