Title:
Lentil Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1092635.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C. Dried green lentils, rinsed and washed
2 C. Water
1 tsp. Salt
1 C. Celery, finely chopped
1 Red onion, finely chopped
2 Tbsp. Canola oil
1 tsp. Dry mustard
1 tsp. Dry ginger
1 Tbsp. Fancy Molasses

Directions:
Rinse lentils under cold running water and place in a large saucepan with 2 cups of water over medium heat
Bring water to a boil and allow to simmer without boiling over and cook until all the water has been absorbed and lentils are nicely cooked
Mash lentils with a potato masher until pureed. Set aside
In a frying pan saute celery and red onion in oil until soft and tender
Drain off excess oil and put celery and onions in a separate bowl
Mix in molasses, mustard and ginger together and stir vegetable mixture into cooked lentils and form into patties and fry lightly in oil.
Serve with your favourite vegetables along with rice or other grain accompaniment.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A tasty vegetarian burger that is different from the usual hamburger with chewy green lentils that would go well with other vegetables and whole grains.
