Title:
Jenn's Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad Ingredients
1 Cup of Uncooked Brown Rice or 2 Pkts of Microwave Brown Rice
6 Spring Onions sliced
1 Diced Red Capsicum
1 Diced Green Capsicum
1/3 Cup Currents
60 gm Cashews & Peanuts
2 Tablespoons of Sunflower seeds
3 Tablespoons of chopped parsley
1 small can of sweet corn kernels
Dressing
3/4 cup of sunflower oil
4 tablespoons of Soya Sauce
32 Tablespoons of lemon juice
1 Clove of Garlic crushed
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Cook Brown rice
Mix all of the salad ingredients in a medium bowl.
To make dressing, add ingredients to a jar, shake to mix.
Pour dressing over salad ingredient wile rice is still warm.

Submitter: 
CZARDAS
Description: 
This is a different rice salad with a tang of soya sauce and you'll love the surprise of cashews & peanuts
