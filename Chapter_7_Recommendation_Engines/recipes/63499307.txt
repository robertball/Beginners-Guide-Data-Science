Title:
Dill Veggie Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/955362.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C Mayonnaise, preferably made with Olive Oil
1 C Sour Cream, preferably light
1 Tbsp Dried Onions
1 Tbsp Dried Parsley
1 tsp Dried Dill

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in medium-sized bowl. Let stand in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes to rehydrate the onions before serving.

Submitter: 
Alicia
Description: 
This is the amazing go-to dip in our house. You can use it for anything from veggies, to a sauce for wraps and even a chicken nugget dip. We love it!
