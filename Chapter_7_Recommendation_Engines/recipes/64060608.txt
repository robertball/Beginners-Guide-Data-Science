Title:
Pumpkin Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129297.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c oats
¼ c unsweetened coconut
½ c rice puffs
2 T quinoa uncooked
1 T flax seeds
1-2 tsp cinnamon
½ tsp pumpkin spcie
½ tsp ground ginger
1 egg white, beat/frothy
3 medjool dates (softened in approx. 3T water; use water also)
1/3 c pumpkin puree
½ tsp vanilla
2 T coconut oil
salt to taste

Directions:
mix dry ingredients
beat egg white to frothy
add remaining wet ingredients to egg and blend (in blender)
combine wet and dry
bake at 325 for 40 min on parchment lined cookie sheet; stir every  10-15 min

gluten free, no added sugar pumpkin granola
