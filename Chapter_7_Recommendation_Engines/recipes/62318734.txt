Title:
Nameless Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1000215.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 c shortening
1-1/2 c sugar
3 eggs
1-3/4 c flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
3/4 tsp nutmeg
2 Tbsp cocoa
3/4 c sour milk*
1 tsp vanilla
1 c finely chopped walnuts, divided

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs.
Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, spices and cocoa.
Add sour milk alternately. Add vanilla and nuts.
Pour batter into greased and floured cake pans. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
Cool completely. Frost with Dixie Dream Frosting.

Submitter: 
Nancy
Description: 
This recipe was named by my grandmother. My mother entered the cake in the county fair and brought home first place for three years in a row.
