Title:
Triple Pork Stuffed Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Thick cut Pork Chops
4 Strips of Bacon
1/2 lb Pork Sausage
4 Cups white bread (cubed)
1 medium onion
1 Celery stalk (with leaves)
1 Garlic clove
1/4 Cup butter (unsalted)
1/4 Cup Chicken Broth (Low sodium)
1 egg
1 tsp Poultry Seasoning
1 tsp Dried Oregano
1/2 tsp Dried Sage
1/2 tsp Thyme

Directions:
Cut bread into half inch cubes and place in a large mixing bowl.
Dice onion and garlic and chop celery.
Melt 1/4 cup of butter in frying pan and add onions, garlic and celery. Cook until tender.
In seperate pan cook pork sausage and drain.
Combine in mixing bowl vegetables with butter, sausage, chicken broth, egg, and all spices to the bread. Mix well.
With pearing knife cut a slit on a side of the pork chops. You will want to make this pocket as large as possible without making holes and slits in the other sides.
Stuff the pork chops as full as you can. With a strip of bacon wrap the entire side of each pork chop. secure the bacon with a tooth pick. Place any extra stuffing in the dish around the pork chops. Cover with aluminum foil and place in oven heated to 350 degrees for 40 minutes. Remove foil and cook for another 15 minutes or until juices run clear.

Description:
