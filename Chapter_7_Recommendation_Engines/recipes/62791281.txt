Title:
BLT Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. bacon
2-3 large tomatoes
1 head romaine lettuce
1 loaf white bread
Mayo to taste
Black pepper to taste

Directions:
Fry bacon and set aside on a paper towel topped plate to cool slightly.  While frying bacon, toast 12 pieces of bread, but do not butter.  Slice tomatoes and tear lettuce into large pieces, then spread mayo on cooled toast, topped by bacon, tomatoes, and lettuce.  End with desired amount of black pepper on the other side.  Combine and eat immediately.

Submitter: 
Realmom
Description: 
Crispy bacon, fresh garden tomatoes and lettuce join black pepper and mayo atop toasted white bread.  One of the best things about summer!
