Title:
Polish Reuben Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/376245.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2 lbs Polish sausage, cut into 1/2" pieces(I use 2-14oz. Hillshire Lite Kielbasa)
1 1/3 cups milk
2 cups shredded Swiss Cheese or Monterey Jack
1/2 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup whole wheat bread crumbs(I use Panko)
1 Tblsp. prepared mustard
2 Tblsps. melted butter
2 cans(16 oz. each)sauerkraut, rinsed & drained
1 pkg. UNCOOKED med-width noodles(I use Ronzoni wheat noodles)

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees
Grease a 13x9 pan or spray with Pam
Combine soup, milk, onion & mustard in a med. bowl, blend well.
Spread sauerkraut in pan. Top with UNCOOKED noodles. Spoon soup mixture evenly over the top.
Top with sausage, then cheese.
Combine crumbs & butter in small bowl, then sprinkle over top.
Cover tightly with foil.
Bake for 1 hour or until noodles are tender.
