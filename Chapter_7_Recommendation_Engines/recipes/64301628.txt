Title:
Tastes like Summer Slowcooker Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1957915.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast, halved
2 cups canned corn or frozen
1 796 ml can diced tomatoes(with the juice)
1 red onion,diced
3 cloves of garlic,diced
1 Red bell pepper, Sliced
1 Bunch of Cilantro
1 Lime, juice and zest
2 tablespoons of oregano
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients into slow-cooker except for the corn and red pepper
Cut stems off of cilantro bunch and tie together with cooking twine, toss into slow cooker
Cook on high for 4 hours or low for 6 hours
When there is 2 hour left in cooking time add in the corn and red pepper.
before serving remove cilantro stems, and add fresh chopped cilantro leaves on top.

Submitter: 
Chef V
Description: 
I love to make this anytime of the year. Its easy to put together and the entire dish can be used for a base to many other recipes. Its very versatile, and reminds me of summer.
