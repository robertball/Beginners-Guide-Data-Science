Title:
Scot's Chicken and Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground chicken (about 2 breasts if grinding own)
2 Tbs garlic, minced
1 medium onion, diced
2 cans canneloni beans, rinsed and drained
1 can diced tomatoes w/ green chilis
1 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp chili powder
1 can whole kernel corn, drained
1 can fat free cream of chicken soup
1 can water

Directions:
Brown ground chicken in skillet - if not Nonstick, you might need to add a tablespoon of olive oil
Add onion and garlic half through cooking chicken
Add remainder of ingredients, cover and cook on low for about 15-20 minutes, until onions tender

Submitter: 
Scot Jones
Description: 
This is a fun recipe for chili that is light on the calories and fat!
