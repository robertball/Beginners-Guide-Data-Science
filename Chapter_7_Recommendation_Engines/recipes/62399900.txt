Title:
Desert Pear Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/430520.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 oz premium Silver 100% Blue Agave Tequila
1/2 oz Cointreau
1 oz Desert Pear Monin
6 oz. Sweet and Sour mix
(You can make your own sweet and sour mix by combining 1/2 part lime, 1/2 part lemon, and 1 part simple syrup)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in shaker filled with ice; strain into chilled margarita glass. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
2RedAZ
Description: 
A cool and refreshing twist on the original Southwest favourite. A Longhorn Steakhouse original.
