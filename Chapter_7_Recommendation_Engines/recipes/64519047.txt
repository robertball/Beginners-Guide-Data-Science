Title:
20 minute Mac & Cheese by Southern Living Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart milk
6 TBSP butter
6 TBSP flour
8 oz shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
8 oz shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1/2 TSP black pepper
1 TSP hot sauce
1 LB elbow macaroni
1 1/2 cup Panko breadcrumbs
2 TSP olive oil

Directions:
Preheat broiler with oven rack 8-9 minutes.
Bring 4 qt of water to boil (add salt to taste)
Meanwhile, microwave milk in microwave safe glass measuring cup covered in plastic wrap for 3 minutes .
While milk is heating melt butter in a 12 inch cast iron skillet over medium heat
Reduce heat to medium low; add flour and cook; whisking contantly, about 2 minutes
Gradually whisk in hot milk
Increase heat to medium high and bring to a low boil; whisking often
Add pasta to boiling water and cook for 8 minutes
Meanwhile continue to cook sauce; whisking often for 6 minutes
Remove sauce from heat, whisk in cheese, pepper and hot sauce
Stir in olive oil to panko breadcrumbs
Drain pasta and fold into cheese sauce
Sprinkle with panko mixture
Broil 1-2 minutes and serve immediately
