Title:
shannon's super bowl homemade meatball sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz Jar of Heinz chili sauce
1 1/4 Cups Grape Jelly
teaspoon of salt
teaspoon of ground black pepper

Directions:
Mix ingredients in sauce pan; heat through until desired thickness and the jelly is melted through. Add to meatballs in a crock pot and let cook on low heat for 2 hours or until you can't wait any longer. ENJOY!

Submitter: 
mommyto3babyboys
Description: 
This recipe may sound strange for this sauce, but it tastes like a sweet BBQ sauce. GREAT FOR MEATBALLS AND LITTLE WEENIES! My husband loves this for his football games.
