Title:
Chicken Salad from Liz Rose Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole chicken breasts split
2 stalks celery
1 large onion
1 carrot
For Salad
Cooked chicken breast bones and cubed
1 Cup celery chopped
1/2- 3/4 Cup stuffed olives "your choice" skuced
1 Cup Walnuts or Pecans, toasted and chopped,but not
fine
Dressing:
Sour Cream
Miracle Whip salad dressing
Cream

Directions:
Boil chicken breasts as for broth, with celery, onion and carrots.  Reserve broth for later use.
Remove chicken and cool.
Bone and cube chicken and place in large bowl.
Add 1 cup chopped celery, 1/2 -3/4 cup sliced stuffed olives. Toast 1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans. (prefer pecans)[to toast nuts: preheat oven 325 degrees,  place nuts on baking sheet with 1/4 cup melted butter and salt to taste and toast until golden brown 8-10 minutes stirring two or three times.]
Mix sour cream and salad dressing with a little cream and pour over chicken and vegetables.  Add the nuts just before serving. Garnish with fruits such as: grapes,slices peaches, mangos,cherries,or fresh pineapple slices. Serve on a bed of Bib  Lettuce and garnish with fruits or vegetables.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Recipe was given to me in the late 60's early 70's and was a favorite Anne Petrella, the mother of my good friend, Sheryl. Liz was Anne's good friends for many years and this recipe was quite a popular salad for wedding showers, ladies luncheons and garden parties menus.
