Title:
Big Ray's All Day Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1031448.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbs frozen orange juice concentrate
2 cups chicken broth
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 cup tomato paste
2 Tbs soy sauce
2 Tbs brown sugar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 dash allspice
4 lb chicken pieces
1/4 lb mushrooms, sliced
2 Tbs butter
11 ounces mandarin orange sections, drained
1/2 bell pepper, sliced
1/4 tsp ground ginger
3 Tbs cornstarch
1/4 cup cold milk
1/4 cup cold water

Directions:
Combine marinade ingredients with chicken pieces.
Marinade overnight in refrigerator. Combine chicken and marinade in crockpot. Cover; cook on low for 6-8 hours.
Increase heat to high. Saute mushrooms in butter. Add remaining ingredients except cornstarch, milk, and water to crockpot. Combine cornstarch and liquids until smooth. Stir into crockpot. Cover; let cook for 30 minutes.
