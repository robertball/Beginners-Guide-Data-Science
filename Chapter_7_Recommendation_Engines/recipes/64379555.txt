Title:
Slow Cooker Sauerkraut with Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tart apples, peeled and sliced
1 large onion, sliced
water to cover
1 quart sauerkraut
1/2 teaspoon fennel seed, or to taste

Directions:
Arrange apples and onion in the bottom of a slow cooker. Pour in enough water to cover bottom of the slow cooker crock.  Add sauerkraut and fennel seed.
Cook on High for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Scott Kallen
Description:
