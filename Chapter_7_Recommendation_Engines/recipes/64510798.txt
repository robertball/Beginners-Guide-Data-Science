Title:
Angelfood Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 angelfood cake, cubed
1 4 serving size caramel or butterscotch instant pudding mix
2 cups milk
500 ml whipping cream
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
chocolate syrup
maple flavored pancake syrup

Directions:
Prepare whipped cream, using sugar & vanilla.
In large bowl, layer 1/3 cake.
Drizzle with both syrups.
Prepare pudding with milk.
Layer 1/3 pudding over cake.
Layer 1/3 whipped cream over pudding.
Repeat twice for total of 3 layers.
Drizzle chocolate syrup over top layer of whipped cream.
Chill.
