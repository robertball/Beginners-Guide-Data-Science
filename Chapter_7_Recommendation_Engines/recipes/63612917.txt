Title:
Enchilada Suizas Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1005184.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
3 ounces light cream cheese, softened
1 cup light sour cream
1 cup jar Herdez Tomatillo Verde Mexican Cooking Sauce
1 (4-ounce) can chopped jalapeños (optional)
2 cup reduced fat monterrey jack cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In a medium, oven safe dish, mix chicken, cream cheese, sour cream, tomatillo verde sauce, jalapeños and half of the monterrey jack cheese.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on top.
Bake in the oven for about 15 - 20 minutes or until cheese melts and dip is heated through.
Top with sliced onions, cilantro, or your other favorite toppings and serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This is a different twist on chicken enchilada dip.  With sour cream and Herdez Tomatillo Verde Mexican Cooking Sauce, it has been transformed into Chicken Suizas Enchilada Dip.
