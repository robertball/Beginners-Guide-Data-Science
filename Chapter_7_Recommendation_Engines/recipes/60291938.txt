Title:
Chocolate Eclair Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cold milk
2 (3.4 ounce) packages JELL-O Vanilla Flavor Instant Pudding
1 (8 ounce) tub COOL WHIP Whipped Topping, thawed
64 Amretti cookies
2 squares BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Chocolate

Directions:
Beat milk and pudding mixes with whisk 2 min. Stir in COOL WHIP.
Arrange 16 cookies on bottom of 8-inch square dish; cover with 1/3 of pudding mixture. Repeat layers twice. Top with remaining cookies. Refrigerate 4 hours.
Microwave chocolate in microwaveable bowl on HIGH 1 min. or until melted when stirred. Drizzle over dessert.

Submitter: 
Carol A
Description: 
Alternating layers of vanilla wafers and a creamy vanilla pudding mixture are topped with more wafers and drizzled with melted chocolate.Modified from: Chocolate Eclair Squares
