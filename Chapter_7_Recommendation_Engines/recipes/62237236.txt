Title:
canadian caesar salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/324898.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 tsp. salt
2-4 garlic cloves
2 tsp anchovy paste
1 tbs cappers
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 cup olive oil
1 1/2 tsp. worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. vinegar
1/2 tsp. hot mustard i prefer keens
4-6 dashes of tobassco sauce
Juice of 1/2 lemon
2 egg yolks
Real Bacon Bits
Crutons (desired anount)
Parmesan Cheese (desired amount)
1-2 heads Romaine Lettuce (torn up)

Directions:
Directions:
In a wooden ceasar salad bowl, put in the salt, then add the chopped garlic cloves, and crush with fork until a creamy consistency. Add Capers and crush in with the galric mixture, Add anchovy paste, and mash with garlic mixture, until a creamy consistency.
Add pepper and olive oil, and stir with fork until well blended. Add worcestershire sauce, vinegar, mustard and tobassco sauce and stir well with fork. Add 2 egg yolks, blend well, then add juice of lemon, blend well. Then add romaine, 1 head if you like your salad very saucy, 2 heads if you would like less saucy.
Then add your bacon bits, crotons, then fresh Parmesan Cheese. Bon Apetit!
