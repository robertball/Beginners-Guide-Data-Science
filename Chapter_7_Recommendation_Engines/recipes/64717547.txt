Title:
Chocolate Chip Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
?½ cup vegetable oil
¾ cup sugar
?½ cup plain yogurt
1 teaspoon baking powder?
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon baking soda
?1 large ripe banana, mashed
½ teaspoon salt?
¾ cup semisweet chocolate chips
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
In another bowl, combine egg, oil, yogurt, and vanilla. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Using a rubber scraper, fold in bananas and chocolate chips.
Spray a muffin tin and fill 12 muffin cups evenly with batter. Bake at 350 degrees for 22-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks.
