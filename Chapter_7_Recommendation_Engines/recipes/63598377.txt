Title:
Lactation Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1001036.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
4 tbsp water
2 tbsp flax seed meal
3 egg yolks
1 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
2 cups all purpose flour
3 heaping tbsp brewers yeast
1 tbsp wheat germ
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cream of tartar
2 1/2 cups old fashioned oats
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix flaxseed with water in small bowl and let sit for 5 minutes.
In mixing bowl, cream together butter and sugars.
Mix in egg yolks and vanilla.
Stir in flaxseed/water mixture.
In separate bowl, combine flour, brewers yeast, wheat germ, baking soda, salt, and cream of tartar.
Stir dry ingredients into mixing bowl until combined.
Stir in oats and chocolate chips.
Roll dough into walnut sized balls and place 2 inches apart on cookie sheet.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes.

Submitter: 
kfwarden
Description: 
These cookies are great for breastfeeding mothers! They are moist and super yummy and they help boost and/or maintain milk supply. And yes, husbands can eat them too! I have to hide these from my hubby when I make them!
