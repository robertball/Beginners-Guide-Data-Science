Title:
Spinach Lasagna (no-cook noodles) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 cloves garlic, chopped;
1/2 cup onion, chopped;
10 oz frozen spinach, thawed and drained;
2 eggs;
1 pint ricotta cheese;
1/2 tsp salt;
1/2 cup grated parmesan;
1 large jar pasta sauce (Barilla is great);
2 cups shredded mozzarella;
8 lasagna noodles, uncooked

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Lightly grease 13&quot;x9&quot; baking dish. In a small pan, cook garlic, onion and spinach until hot. In a medium bowl, beat eggs and combine with ricotta, salt, spinach mixture and half the parmesan. Spread 1/2 cup pasta sauce on bottom of baking dish. Place half the noodles over the sauce, spread with half the spinach mixture, half the mozzarella and half the remaining pasta sauce. Repeat using remaining ingredients. Top with parmesan and additional mozzarella (if desired). Cover securely with foil and bake for 1 hour. Lert stand 10 min before sserving.

Submitter: 
charlotte221
Description: 
This delicious vegetarian lasagna is quick and easy because you don't have to precook the noodles. You could easily add meat to beef it up.
