Title:
Alice's Sweet Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup brown sugar
1 Jar Traditional Prego Sauce
Angel Hair Pasta
Butter
Oil
Salt
Browned Hambuger(1/2 lb)

Directions:
Cook angel hair noodles as directed on package with some salt in the water.  Strain and then add a little bit of butter and a little bit of any oil you prefer.  Adding both gives it a new flavor.
Mix cooked hamburger Prego, and brown sugar.  Add more brown sugar to taste if needed.
Mix sauce and pasta together.  Eat fresh or place in fridge and eat the next day or freeze for later.

Submitter: 
Krystallynn
Description: 
My Aunt Alice always made this for me and my cousins when we were growing up.  Its really sweet, but its a family favorite. Either add a lot or just a bit, do not go for the middle grounds.
