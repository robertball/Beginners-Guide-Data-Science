Title:
Fiesta Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can black beans or black eyed peas w/jalapenos
1 can pinto beans w/jalapenos
1 can white shoepeg corn
1 green, red & yellow pepper, diced
1 red onion, diced
2 tsp. cumin
16 oz. Fat Free Italian dressing
Fritos scoops

Directions:
drain all beans, do not have to use all dressing, use according to taste. Mix. Marinate overnight for best results.
