Title:
Pina Colada Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/293481.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vanilla yogurt
1 banana, frozen
1 cup pineapple chunks
1/4 cup orange juice
1 Tbsp. flaked coconut

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender.  Cover and blend until smooth.  More or less orange juice may be added to achieve desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
This is my non-alcoholic healthy smoothie version of the classic cocktail.  Very nutritious and refreshing!
