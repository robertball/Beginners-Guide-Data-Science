Title:
Devilishly Delicious Ranch Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985414.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 1/4 pound) loaf hearty round bread, unsliced
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup shredded Mozzarella or pizza blend cheese
1 packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
1/2 cup chopped orange bell pepper
1 (2.25 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained, reserving 2 slices
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
Assorted fresh vegetables

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
Hollow loaf, leaving bottom 1-inch think. Cut 1-inch cubes from the hollowed out inside bread; set aside. Use leftover top of the bread for a stem.
Place loaf on a baking sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly toasted. Remove from oven. Cool on wire rack until ready to use.
For dip, combine cream cheese, mayonnaise, shredded cheese and Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing & Seasoning Mix in medium microwave safe bowl. Heat in microwave on high 1 to 1 1/2 minutes, stirring halfway through. Remove and stir until cheese is completely melted. Stir in remaining ingredients. Heat 1 minute or until heated through.
Spoon dip into bread. Place 2 reserved olive slices near top for 'eyes' and triangle orange peppers for the nose and mouth.  Serve with reserved bread cubes and fresh vegetables for dipping.

Hidden Valley® Ranch
