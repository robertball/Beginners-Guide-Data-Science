Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/558790.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef, cooked and drained well
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 can butter beans (lima), drained and rinsed
1 large envelope Lipton Onion Soup mix
4 cups water
1 small can tomato paste
2 TB fresh minced garlic (more or less to taste)
2 Tb Italian seasoning (more or less to taste)

Directions:
Spray crock pot with cooking spray.  Mix all ingredients in crock pot.  Cover with lid.  Cook on low, 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Carrie Caywood Nehmer
Description: 
On cold days, I love to serve this soup with homemade dinner rolls.  It's very easy and very flavorful, and my whole family loves it!
