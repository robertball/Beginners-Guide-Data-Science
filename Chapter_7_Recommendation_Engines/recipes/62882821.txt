Title:
Chicken Parmesan via MAM Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices of bread
1/4 bunch of parsley
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon salt
1/8th teaspoon pepper
1 cup butter, melted
4 chicken breast or 8 leg/thighs

Directions:
heat oven 350 degrees
tear bread with parsley and crumb in mixture
mix crumbs, parsley, cheese garlic, powder, salt and pepper.
dip chicken in melted butter and then crumb.
place in shallow baking pan and pour remaining butter over chicken.
bake for one hour (do not turn meat over)
