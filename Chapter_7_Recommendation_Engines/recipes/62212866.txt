Title:
Tettrazini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooked chicken or turkey
1/2c butter
1/2c chopped onion
1 clove garlic
1 lb sliced mushrooms
1 1/2 t salt
1/2 t pepper
1/4 c flour
2 1/2 c of 1/2&1/2
1 can chicken broth
1 cup shredded mozzarella
some parmesan cheese
12 oz cooked linguine
TOPPING
1/4 c bread crumbs
1 TBL melted butter

Directions:
melt butter in Dutch Oven.
sautee onion & garlic til tender.
Add mushrooms and saute 5 min Add S&P
whisk in four and stir 1 min
Add 1/2&1/2 and broth
Remove from heat, stir in cheeses (1/2 c parmesan)
stir in chicken and linguine
spoon into casserole dish
combine last two ingredients and sprinkle in bands
Bake 350 for 20 min
