Title:
Short Cut --Strawberry Short Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf pound cake
2 cups Fresh Strawberries or
1 cup frozen strawberries thawed.
1 1/2 cups whipped topping
1/3 cup confectioners sugar
1/2 (2-3 tbs) lemon juice

Directions:
Slice the pound cake into 1/2 inch slices or thinner. Set to the side. Slice the strawberries and toss with the confectioners sugar and the lemon Juice to taste.
On a plate lay one slice of cakes spoon over with Strawberry mixture and a dollop of whipped topping and top with another slice of cake and spoon over with strawberry mixture .
enjoy.

Submitter: 
Kysha Camille Rodriguez-Chomat
Description: 
I love to cook but hate things that take too long especially deserts so I came up with this great desert Idea. Fresh strawberries and a buttery pound cake really make this pop.
