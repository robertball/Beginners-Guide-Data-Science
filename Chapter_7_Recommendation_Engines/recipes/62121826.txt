Title:
April's Lima Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10.75 oz. can of cream of celery soup
1 and 1/4 cup of milk
2 10 oz. frozen packages of lima beans thawed and drained
1 medium diced onion
1 8 oz package of sliced cheddar cheese
20 crushed butter crackers
salt and pepper to season

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F degrees (175 degrees C).
Mix the soup and milk together in a baking dish.
Add drained and thawed lima beans and minced onion.
Season with salt and pepper and stir.
Top with slices of cheese.
Sprinkle crushed crackers of the cheese.
Bake covered with foil for 1 hour (60 minutes).
Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 - 15 minutes uncovered until cheese is brown and bubbly.
