Title:
V-Day treat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of Marshmallows
1/2 to 3/4 box of rice krispies
4 cups Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
V-day Sprinkles
1/4 cup butter
(ur gonna need - popsicle sticks and Heart cookie cutter)

Directions:
In a large Pot, put Marshmallow and butter in..Put on low.Meanwhile, melt chocolate chips in a bowl..When Marshmallows are fully melted take out and mix in with rice krispies, put in a dish. Wait till firm. Then cut out into Heart shapes (w/ cookie cutter)Stick popsicle sticks in the bottom of the Heart, dip in the Chocolate..and sprinkle the Sprinkles on top! :)
