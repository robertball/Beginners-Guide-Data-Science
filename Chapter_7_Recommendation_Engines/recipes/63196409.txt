Title:
Chocolate Cinnamon Pull Aparts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can biscuits
2 T Hershey's Cocoa
sprinkle salt
2 T Butter
2 T Splenda Brown Sugar
1/8 t cinnamon
1 T mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cut biscuits into quarters.  In meauring cup combine butter, cocoa, salt, brown sugar and cinnamon.  Microwave 30 seconds.  Mix well.  Add vanilla.  Sprinkle biscuits into pie plate and pour chocolate mixture over top evenly.  Bake for about 15 - 20 minutes until edges brown.  Sprinkle with mini chocolate chips.  Let sit for a couple minutes then serve.
