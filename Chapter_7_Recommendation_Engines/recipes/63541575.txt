Title:
The Magnificent Butter Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 Cup soft Butter
1/2 Cup (runny)to 3/4 Cup (firmly set) Brown Sugar
3/4 Cup (firmly set) to 1 Cup(runny) corn syrup
1/8 Tsp Salt
2 slightly beaten Eggs
1 Tsp Vanilla
1-2 Tsp Lemon Juice
2-3 Heaping Tablespoons sour cream
Dash of Cinnamon or Nutmeg (optional)
3/4 Cup steamed seedless Raisins (optional)
3/4 Cup finely diced mixed nuts (optional)
Pastry for tarts or frozen mini or regular tart
shells.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F
Cream the butter, sugar, corn syrup and salt together.
Stir in the beaten eggs, vanilla, lemon juice and spices.
Stir in Sour Cream.
If using raisins, pour boiling water over raisins and let stand 10 minutes; then drain and dry.  Sprinkle nuts or raisins in tart shells.
Pour filling into shells.
Bake at 400F for 10 minutes; reduce oven temp to 350F and bake 5-10 minutes longer until pastry is golden.

Submitter: 
LOBSTER
Description: 
A true Eastern Canadian Butter Tart.  Runny or Firmly set, Nuts or Raisins, you choose!  Every Canadian has a preference!
