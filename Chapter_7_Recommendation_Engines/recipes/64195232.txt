Title:
Pigs-In-Blankets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. Bisquick
1/2 C. cold water
8 hotdogs or small cocktail wieners

Directions:
Heat oven to 450. Mix baking mix and water until soft dough forms. Roll out dough. Cut out circles and then circles into wedges, size according to hot dogs used. Place frankfurter on wedge and roll up starting at wide side. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet until golden brown, about 15 minutes. Serve with mustard.
