Title:
Panera Broccoli Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4830713.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups broccoli florets
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup sharp cheddar cheese
1 heaping cup of gouda cheese
1 cup carrots, chopped
2 small white onions
2 large cloves of garlic, minced
1 cup half and half
3 tablespoons flour
3 tablespoons butter
1 bay leaf
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon dried basil
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/8 teaspoon salt
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Dice onions,mince garlic and shred carrots
Chop the broccoli into small pieces
Small pot place chicken broth, broccoli, onion, garlic, carrots and bay leaf; cook 8-10 minutes
Roux in large soup pot:
Melt butter, whisking constantly
Add flour, continue whisking
Off heat, pour in the vegetable mixture and stir in 1 cup of half and half (room temp)
Place back on heat, uncovered
Remove bay leaf, season with spices
Add cheddar and gouda cheese
Serve immediately
