Title:
Dream Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon pulverized baker's ammonia
2 cups all-purpose flour

Directions:
Cream the butter.
Add sugar and cream again.
Sift together the flour and baker's ammonia then stir into the butter and sugar. Mix well.
Shape the dough into four 12-inch rolls.
Cut into slices and roll into balls.
Put the balls onto ungreased cookie sheets and press lightly.
Bake for 12-15 minutes at 375 degrees.

Submitter:

Description: 
Crisp, light little cookies. Andrew Olson gave the recipe to me (it's the one his mother uses).
