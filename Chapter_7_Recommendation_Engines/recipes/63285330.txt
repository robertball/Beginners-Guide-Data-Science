Title:
Rachael's Chicken Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 - 1 cup cooked wild rice with seasoning (Uncle Ben's long grain is best)
1 lb cut up or shredded chicken
1 can 14 oz chicken broth
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 cups milk
2-3 cups of cheese
1/3 a 2/3 cup vegetables
1 pkg Ranch dressing
Green onion to garnish

Directions:
Throw it all together and simmer from 1/2 to 4 hours. Add the cheese after soup is hot.

Submitter: 
MARCIL
Description: 
A great, hearty soup and comfort food! I always double this recipe.
