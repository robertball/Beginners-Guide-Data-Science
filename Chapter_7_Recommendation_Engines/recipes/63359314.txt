Title:
Spicy Chicken Sausage Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894521.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz dried navy or white beans
14  oz package of Alfresco Buffalo Chicken Sausage
4 tbs butter
1 onion,chopped fine
3 stalks of celery,chopped to bitesize
2-3 carrots,chopped to bitesize
1/4 tsp garlic powder
4 cups of chicken stock or broth

Directions:
Rinse beans well, remove any floating or discolored bean and stones.
Boil beans in 6 quarts of water for 10 minutes,turn off heat, cover and let pot cool on stove for one hour.
After an hour,rinse and drain beans in colander.
In the pot you boiled the beans in, melt butter, and saute onions, carrots, and celery til soft.Peel casing from sausage and saute the chicken sausage into crumbles until lightly browned.
Add chicken broth, garlic powder, salt and pepper (to taste) and beans, bring to a boil, then cover and reduce heat to low for 3 hours. You may need to add more water during cooking as bean swell.
