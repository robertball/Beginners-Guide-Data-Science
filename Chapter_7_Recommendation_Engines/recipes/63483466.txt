Spicy Slow Cooker Black Bean Soup Recipe

Ingredients:
1 pound dry black beans (vs 2 cups), soaked overnight

6 cups vegetable broth vs water

2 small carrots
1 bay leaf
2 celery stalks
1/2 tsp garlic powder vs 4 cloves minced garlic
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
3/4 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp salt
2 cups ham before serving--optional

Directions:
Place the dried beans into a colander and rinse thoroughly. Place the beans into the slow cooker and add the six cups of water. Add the vegetables to the beans and water. Add the spices, stir, and cover. Turn the slow cooker setting to low and allow to cook for ten hours without removing the lid. Remove the bay leaf prior to serving.
Alt Cook on High for 4 hours. Reduce heat to low and continue cooking for 2 hours, or until you are ready to eat.
