Title:
Dirt Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages chocolate flavor instant pudding
1 qt. (4 cups) cold milk
1 cup whipped cream
20 chocolate OREO cookies or chocolate graham crackers,finely crushed
16 gummy worms or gummy bugs
8 clear plastic cups

Directions:
Prepare both pudding mixes as directed on the box with cold milk. Let stand 5 minutes or until set up. Gently mix in 1/2 cup of whipped cream into pudding.
Layer the bottom of the cups with 1tbs. of crushed cookie. Next layer each cup with 1/4 cup chocolate pudding, followed by 1tbs. more crushed cookie. Repeat until the cups are full, at the top of the cups add the remaining whipped cream.
Refrigerate before serving at least 1 hour or until needed.
Before serving: add 2 gummy worms on top
