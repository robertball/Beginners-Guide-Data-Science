Title:
Lemon Lime Tilapia with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon butter
1 tilapia fillet
salt to taste
Ground black pepper to taste
1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 1/2 tablespoons lime juice
3 green onions, chopped
5 Button Mushrooms chopped

Directions:
Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a medium skillet over medium heat. Place tilapia in the skillet, and season with kosher salt and pepper. Pour half the lemon and lime juice over the tilapia, and continue cooking 3 minutes.
Flip the tilapia, and season with salt and pepper.  Cover with remaining lemon and lime juice. Stir in green onions and mushrooms into the skillet. Continue cooking 5 minutes, or until fish is easily flaked with a fork.
