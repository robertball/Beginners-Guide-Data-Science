Title:
Crustless Spinach Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten
3/4 C flour
1 C milk
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
1 tsp baking powder
1 11 oz. box frozen chopped spinach
1/2 of an onion, finely chopped
1 lb shredded Cheddar cheese
Variation; use 1/2 cup cheddar, 1/2 jack cheese.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease 9x13 inch pan
Whisk together eggs and milk.
Gradually add flour, baking powder and seasonings to the egg and milk mixture. Add cheese and thawed spinach; mix well.
Pour into well greased 13x9 inch pan, Spread mixture evenly in pan.
Bake 30-35 minutes at 350 degrees.
Quiche will be a light golden brown.
Allow the quiche to cool for 1/2 hour.
Cut into squares and serve.
This recipe may also be baked in two 9 inch pie plates.

Submitter: 
Dana Hall
Description: 
My grandmother makes this for us every time we come to visit. It's deliciously simple and kids and adults alike gobble it right up! This is great as an appetizer or as paired with a salad for a main course. Try substituting broccoli for the spinach as an alternative.
