Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2464167.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C. Vanilla wafers cookie crumbs
1 C. Finely chopped nuts, Walnuts or Pecans
1 C. Powdered sugar
2 Tbs. Cocoa Powder
1/4 C. Rum
1 Tbs. White corn syrup.

Directions:
Finely chop nuts to crumb size. Crush wafer cookies with rolling pin until small crumbs. Mix all dry ingredients in bowl. Then blend in rum and corn syrup. Roll into balls, amount depends on size of balls. Roll balls ins sifted powder sugar or cocoa powder. This recipe can be easily doubled.

Submitter: 
GishWheS15
Description: 
Go back in time with this prehistoric treat! No bake Dinomite bites for last minute parties with Fograt, Wooster and Elopus.
