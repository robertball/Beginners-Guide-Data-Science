Title:
God's Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce/Marinade mix
1 Bottle Bulls-Eye Bold Barbecue Sauce
1 Bottle Sticky Finger's Sweet Memphis Barbecue Sauce
1 Bottle Chipotle Tabasco Sauce
1/4 Cup Extra Virgin Olive oil
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1Tsp Italian seasoning
Frozen bag of Chicken Wings
Cayenne pepper

Directions:
Combine all of the marinade/sauce ingredients in a blender and blend throughly. Make as much sauce as you need,
Thaw and marinate the wings for approx. 12 hours. (best to do this overnight)
Grill over direct medium heat until done. Dust with cayenne pepper and glaze with extra sauce.

Submitter: 
Brad Huebner
Description: 
The best Chicken wings known to man. If God made chicken wings, these would be better. Simple, easy, always a crowd pleaser
