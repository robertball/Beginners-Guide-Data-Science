Title:
White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  48 oz jars of great northern white bean
1  28oz can hunts crushed tomatoes
3  4oz cans of diced green chiles
2lbs of chicken diced or 2 cans of shredded chicken (optional: cook with diced onion)
1  32oz can of chicken both (more or less for soupiness)
1 pkg taco seasoning
-  tsp of cheyenne pepper
-  tsp of ground cumin
-  tsp Mexican ground oregano
tsp ground marjoran
2 cloves of garlic or 2 tbsp of garlic juice

Directions:
Diced green chile's keep it MILD, they are not hot.
Throw it all in a slow cooker until it is done.  I like to have mine with sour cream.
