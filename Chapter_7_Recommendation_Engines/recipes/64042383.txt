Title:
Mama's Marvelous Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1123623.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lb ground beef
1 lb pork sausage
2/3 C bread crumbs
2 eggs
1/2 C saut√©ed onion
1/2 C parmesan cheese
2 Tbsp Italian seasoning
1 Tbsp black pepper
1 Tbsp garlic powder
1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
2 tsp onion powder
1 tsp rosemary
1/2 tsp cayenne

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Combine all ingredients well using stand mixer with paddle attachment.  Form meatballs into 2 Tbsp balls.  Place on baking sheet and cook until meatballs reach internal temp of 165 degrees F (about 20 minutes), flipping halfway through cooking.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
These tasty meatballs will make you wonder why you ever bought them pre made!  Easy to make, and great for making ahead and freezing for future use.  I love them with spaghetti and marinara!
