Title:
Breakfast Roll-Ups with NUTELLA® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1041670.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon NUTELLA®
1 (6 inch) whole grain tortilla
1 banana

Directions:
Spread NUTELLA(R) evenly on tortilla wrap. Place peeled banana on one edge of the tortilla. Wrap the tortilla around the banana by rolling it from one side to the other. Cut into 1-inch thick sections to make several rolls.

Submitter: 
NUTELLA®
Description: 
A perfect breakfast for families on-the-go! Only three ingredients needed!
