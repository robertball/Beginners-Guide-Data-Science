Title:
San Francisco Club Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
smoked turkey
smoked ham
1/2 teaspoon hot sauce
3 slices bacon
2 tablespoons mayonnaise or salad dressing
3 slices sourdough bread
herb-garlic or swiss cheese, sliced
romaine lettuce

Directions:
In a large skillet, over medium heat, cook bacon, drain and reserve.
In small bowl, combine mayonnaise and hot sauce. Spread on one side of each slice of toast. Top one slice toast with Hillshire Farm Deli Select turkey and cheese. Top with second toast slice. Add Hillshire Farm Deli Select ham, bacon and Romaine. Finish with toast. Anchor with toothpicks. Cut in half.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee
Description: 
A blend of mayonnaise and just a hint of hot sauce adds just the right amount of spice to this club sandwich made with sourdough bread and smoked meats.
