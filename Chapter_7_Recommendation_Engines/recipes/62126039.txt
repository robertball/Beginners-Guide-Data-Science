Title:
Prell's famous grilled chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 boneless, skinless chicken breast
1/2 tsp curry powder
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp adobe seasoning
1 package goya sazon seasoning
1 med yellow onion, sliced
4 - 6 cloves garlic, crushed

Directions:
Split chicken breast in half so they are not to thick
In a medium bowl combine all ingredients
Add chicken and cover each piece with seasoning
place in a resealable bag and refrigerate for 2 or more hours.
Grill on med hot grill for 5 to 10 minutes per side until chicken is cooked
The onions will fall off and that is ok.
Make sure to wear gloves as the goya and curry tend to stain hands.

Submitter: 
prell2k4
Description: 
This is a recipe I created with some of my favorite ingredients.  Very moist and perfect for the grill.  Requested often by my picky son.
