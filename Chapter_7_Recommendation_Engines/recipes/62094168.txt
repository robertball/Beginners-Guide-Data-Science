Title:
Almond Cloud Dream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ c water
2 eggs
1 package unflavored gelatin
1 ½ c whipping cream
½ c sugar
½ tsp. almond extract

Directions:
Pour  water into saucepan and sprinkle gelatin over top. Wait 1 minute, then dissolve gelatin over low heat.
Meanwhile, place remaining ingredients in a blender and mix for two minutes. Pour gelatin mixture into blender and process until blended. Pour into dessert glasses. Chill for at least 2 hrs or until gelatin "sets" and garnish with fresh fruit, whipped cream, etc.

Description: 
A delicious almond flavored creamy desert that tastes like it took a long time in the kitchen. My sister-in-law requests that I make this every year for her birthday.
