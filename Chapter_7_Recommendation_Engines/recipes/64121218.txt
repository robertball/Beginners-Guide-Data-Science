Title:
Potatoes O'Brien Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1303950.jpg
Ingredients:
1 LB Red potatoes, boiled and cubed
1/2 cup bell pepper
1/2 cup white onion
1/8 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp kosher salt
1/8 cup olive oil

Directions:
Heat skillet over medium-high heat (cast iron works best)
Add olive oil (can also use veg oil)
Spread potatoes in the bottom of the skillet
Add onion and bell peppers on top of potatoes
Allow to cook for 5 minutes and then flip over for another 5 minutes
Cook another 5 minutes until browned to your preference
