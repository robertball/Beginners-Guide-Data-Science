Title:
Stuffed Salmon Fillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup Olive Oil
2 Cloves Garlic
1 Tbsp Salt
1 Tsp Ground Black Pepper
2 Tbsp Butter
1 Cup Steamed Chopped Spinach
1 Cup Crumbled Feta Cheese
1 Egg
1 Cup Chopped fully cooked Crab or Lobster
4 Fresh Salmon Fillets (Cut Thick)
1 lemon

Directions:
Step 1
In a Medium Sauce Pan put the butter on low heat. Chop the garlic cloves and add to butter in the pan. Simmer for about 5 minutes, stirring frequently so it doesn't burn. Add the spinach, salt and pepper and cook for an additional 10 minutes stirring frequently. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
Step 2
Take a 9 x 11 baking pan and pour about 1/8 cup of the olive oil on the bottom. Make sure the bottom of the pan is well coated. Slice the salmon fillets in half leaving about 1/2" not cut on the bottom of the fillet.
Step 3
In a medium Mixing bowl, combine lobster or crab meat, egg, feta cheese and the spinach mixture. Mix well.
Step 4
Stuff the  salmon fillets with the stuffing, place them in the pan and, drizzle the rest of the olive oil on top.
Step 5
Place in the oven for 20 minutes at 400 degrees.
(Due to everyone's ovens being different, you will have to check the fillets and alter the cooking time if needed)
Enjoy! Serve with lemon wedges.
