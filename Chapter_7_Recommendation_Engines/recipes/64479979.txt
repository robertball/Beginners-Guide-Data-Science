Title:
German Potato Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 potatoes (unpeeled, washed)
1 egg
1/4 cup onion
1/2 tsp salt
2 tbsp flour
butter or oil

Directions:
Cut up potatoes and put in blender
Add egg, onion, salt and flour
Blend, stopping frequently to scrape down the sides.
Heat nough butter, oil or mixture of butter and oil to coat bottom of (wrought iron) frying pan. Keep on high heat.
Drop spoonfuls of batter into pan, pressing down lightly with back of spoon.
Fry until brown, about 3-4 minutes.
Remove and keep warm in oven.
Continue with next batch, adding more butter (or oil) as needed.
Serve with apple sauce, sprinkle with sugar or serve plain.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
Original recipe using grated potatoes, squeezing out the liquid before adding all the ingredients as stated in this recipe.
