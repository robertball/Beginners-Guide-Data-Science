Title:
Ruby Tuesday Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Rotini Pasta
3 cups diced cooked chicken
1 cup mayo
10 oz bag frozen broccoli florets, thawed
2 cups diced or shredded deli ham
1/2 cup ranch dressing
1 cup sour cream
2 t garlic salt
1 t pepper
chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

Directions:
Cook the pasta. Cool under running water and drain. Place pasta in a large bowl. Add remaining and combine well.
