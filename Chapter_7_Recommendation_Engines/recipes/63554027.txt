Title:
Grandma Jones' Lemon Fluff Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz. can evaporated milk--chilled at least 4 hours
1 small package lemon Jello
1 3/4 c. very hot water
1 cup sugar
1/4 c. lemon juice
2 1/2 cups crushed vanilla wafers (crushed graham crackers work, too)

Directions:
Dissolve Jello in very hot water.
Add sugar and lemon juice to Jello and chill until partially set.
Whip partially set Jello mix until fluffy.
Whip chilled evaporated milk until fluffy.
Fold fluffy Jello mix carefully into whipped milk.
Cover bottom of 13 x 9 pan with a thin layer of cookie crumbs.
Pour Jello mixture on top.
Sprinkle with remaining cookie crumbs and chill 2-3 hrs. until firm

Sudie B
Description: 
An old family favorite. Light and refreshing for summer.
