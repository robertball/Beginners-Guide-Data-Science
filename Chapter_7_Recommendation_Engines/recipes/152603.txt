Title:
Chocolate Chunk-Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976570.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
2 teaspoons CALUMET Baking Powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1 cup mashed ripe bananas
1/3 cup oil
1/4 cup milk
6 (1 ounce) squares BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Baking Chocolate, coarsely chopped
1/2 cup chopped PLANTERS Walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in large bowl; set aside. Stir eggs, bananas, oil and milk in large bowl until well blended. Add dry ingredients; stir just until moistened. Stir in chopped chocolate and walnuts.
Pour into greased 9x5-inch loaf pan.
Bake 55 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes. Remove from pan; cool completely on wire rack. Cut into 18 (1/2-inch-thick) slices to serve.

Submitter: 
BAKER'S Chocolate
Description: 
Peppered with chunks of chocolate and walnuts, this banana bread makes a rich and satisfying snack any time of day.
