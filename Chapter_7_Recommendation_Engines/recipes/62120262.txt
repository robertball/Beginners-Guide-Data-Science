Title:
Rhubarb Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
shortbread crust:
1/2 cup butter(softened)
1 cup flour
5Tbsp powdered sugar
filling:
2 eggs
1/4th cup flour
3/4 tsp salt
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 cups cut rhubarb (sliced peices about 1/4th of an inch thick)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheight.
For crust:
Mix butter, flour and powdered sugar together well and mash it firmly into the bottom of a 9" square pan.
Bake the cruat for 15 minutes, until crust is hardened slightly and very fragrant, not browned at all.
For filling:
Mix the eggs, flour,salt, brown sugar and white sugar with a whisk until totally smooth.  Whisk or stir in the rhubarb until it is well coated.
Put the filling mix on top on the short bread crust and bake it for 40 minutes.  Cool at least 10 minutes before cutting into bars and serving.

Submitter: 
Kitchen Witch
Description: 
A shortbread like, very sweet yet tart tasting dessert bar that is great alone or with vanilla icecream.
