Title:
Tomato and Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vinegar, red wine
1/2 tablespoon mustard
1 tablespoon cilantro, fresh
1 teaspoon oil, olive
1/4 teaspoon peppercorns, black
1/3 cup tomato(es), chopped
1 medium cucumber(s)
1/2 medium onion(s), red
1 large egg(s), hard boiled
only use egg white
2 leaves lettuce, red leaf

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk the vinegar, mustard, cilantro or parsley, oil, and pepper. Add the tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, and egg white. Toss gently to coat.
Arrange the lettuce leaves on a platter. Top with the salad and drizzle with any remaining dressing. Serve immediately.
