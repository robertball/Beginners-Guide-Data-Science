Title:
Peach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 cup white sugar
* 1/3 cup butter
* 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
* 1 egg
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 3 cups fresh peaches, pitted, skinned and sliced
* 1 recipe pastry for a 9-inch single crust pie

Directions:
1) Linea 9-inch pie pan with crust. Brush crust with egg white. Place peaches in the unbaked pie shell.
2) Cream together the sugar and butter. Add the egg, flour, and vanilla; mix together completely. Spread this mixture over the top of the peaches.
3) Bake at 300 degrees f (150 degrees C) for 1 hour.
