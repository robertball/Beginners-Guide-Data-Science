Title:
Baked Oatmeal II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups rolled oats
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup Grade B Maple Syrup
2 teaspoons McCormicks Roasted Saigon Cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
2 eggs
1/2 cup melted butter
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt. Beat in milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Stir in dried cranberries. Spread into a 8x8 inch baking dish.
Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes.
