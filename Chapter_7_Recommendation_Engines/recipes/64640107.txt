Title:
Kid-Friendly Shredded Steak Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4535233.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds flank steak
2/3 cups low sodium beef stock
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
3 tablespoons minced garlic
2 teaspoons chili lime powder
2 teaspoons minced onion
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon pepper
4 tablespoons olive oil (2 for searing, 2 for marinade)
1-2 8 ounce packages shredded cheddar-jack cheese
10 burrito sized tortillas
Oil spray
Guacamole
Sour Cream
Salsa

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275 degrees.
Combine stock, lime juice, apple cider vinegar, garlic, onions, spices and 2 tablespoons olive oil.
Whisk ingredients together until completely blended.
In a dutch oven heat 2 tbsp olive oil on stove top.
Pat flank steaks dry and season with salt and pepper.
Brown steaks in the dutch oven on each side on medium/high heat.
Once browned, combine steaks and marinade in the dutch oven.
Cover and bake at 275 degrees for 2 hours or until meat is slightly pink in center and tender.
Drain liquid from pot and shred beef with forks.
Heat skillet on stovetop
Spray oil in skillet
Lay a tortilla in pan
Add desired amount of shredded beef and cheese to top of tortilla and cover with another tortilla.
Heat each side of the tortilla in pan until golden brown.
Cut quesadilla into 8ths and serve with sour cream, guacamole, salsa
Kids may like their beef cut into tiny pieces
