Title:
Big Ray's Lebanese Lentils & Red Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3771853.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup dry lentils
3/4 cup chopped red peppers
1/3 cup chopped red onions
6 Tbsp olive oil
6 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 pkg. (4 oz.)  crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Place lentils in medium saucepan. Add enough water to pan to cover lentils by 2 inches. Cook on medium heat 30 min. or until lentils are tender; drain. Add peppers, onions; mix lightly. Cover. Refrigerate 3 hours or overnight.
Combine oil, vinegar and garlic. Pour over lentil mixture; mix lightly.
Add cheese; toss lightly.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Cooked lentils are tossed with onions, mint, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, garlic and feta cheese for a unique salad. For added flair, serve in red pepper halves, if desired.
