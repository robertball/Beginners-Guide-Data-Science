Title:
Cheesy Salvadoran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter
2 eggs
2/3 c sugar
2 c flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 c milk
2 c grated cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Put paper liners in tins. Cream butter & sugar w/ a fork till smooth. Add eggs & stir till well blended. Put flour & baking powder in another bowl. Stir so that baking powder is mixed in well.  Stir a little milk into the butter & sugar the stir in a little flour. Keep alternating till all mixed. Put cheese into batter & mix well. Fill muffin cups & bake for 20-25 min.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
We sometimes use these muffins in place of cornbread muffins, they are wonderful!
