Title:
How to make a Quick Gravey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Equil amounts of soft BUTTER and flour,
seasoned Broth or pan drippings,
About a foot of wax paper,

Directions:
Soften 1 stick of Butter and 8 Tbs flour, you can melt Butter be careful not to let get to to the stage where your butter will seperate, add 8 to 10 Tbs flour mix well, then put mixture on wax paper, roll up so it is about the size of a stick of butter, Twist ends then put in fridge. Now you are ready next time you want to thicken a sauce or gravey, just unroll the log and cut off what you need add to pan drippings or broth makes a great gravey. GOOD EATS

Submitter: 
Rick Falsetta
Description: 
Who said you cant learn anything watching TV. Easy way to make gravey or thicken a sauce, You just need to be prepaired by making your thickening agent ahead of time and keep it in the fridge.
