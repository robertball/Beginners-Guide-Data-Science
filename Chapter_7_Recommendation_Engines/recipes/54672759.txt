Title:
Company Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
16 oz. tomato sauce
1 c. cottage cheese
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
¼ c. sour cream
½ c. green pepper, diced
½ c. green onions, sliced
8 oz. noodles

Directions:
Brown hamburger; add tomato paste and cook down.
Mix together cottage cheese, cream cheese, sour cream, green pepper and green onions.
Cook noodles.
Spray 9" x 13" pan with PAM.  Spread ½ of noodles into pan.  Cover with cheese.
Cover with remaining noodles.  Spread hamburger over the top layer of noodles.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
