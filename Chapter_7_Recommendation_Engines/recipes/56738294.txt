Title:
Costco Freezer Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 ounces elbow macaroni
32 ounces shredded sharp Cheddar cheese (1 costco block)
48 ounces small curd cottage cheese (1 costco tub)
32 ounces sour cream (2/3 costco container)
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 teaspoons salt and pepper to taste
2 cup dry bread crumbs
1 cup butter, melted
1 cup onion

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil, add pasta, and cook until done; drain.
In 9x13 inch baking dish, stir together macaroni, shredded Cheddar cheese, cottage cheese, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper. In a small bowl, mix together bread crumbs and melted butter. Sprinkle topping over macaroni mixture.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes, or until top is golden.
For my house, this makes 8 pans, 8x8;
