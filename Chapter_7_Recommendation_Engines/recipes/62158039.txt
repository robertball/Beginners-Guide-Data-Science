Title:
Beer Battered Fish Tacos with Spicy Chipotle Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup beer
3/4 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon dry mustard
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 lb firm white fish, sliced into strips
8 Tbs vegetable oil
6 Flour Tortillas
1/2 cup nonfat yogurt
2 Tbs mayo
2 diced chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
1 Tbs of Adobo sauce
Shredded lettuce
cilantro
corn

Directions:
Mix beer, flour, salt, oregano, mustard, cayenne, and pepper in a food processor.
Transfer the batter to a casserole dish.
Add fish and coat with batter.
Heat 8 Tbs oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
While waiting for the oil to heat up, mix yogurt, mayo, chopped cilantro, adobo, and chipotles to make chipotle cream.
Transfer one-third of the battered fish to the pan, shaking excess batter off first.
Cook until golden brown.
Drain fish of oil on a paper towel covered plate.
Cook the remaining fish in shifts adding oil as necessary.
Add corn, shredded lettuce, cream, and cilantro with fish in a flour tortilla.

Submitter: 
Lindsey
Description: 
This cream is made from nonfat yogurt to cut down on the extra calories.  I also use cabbage instead of lettuce if that is what I have around.
