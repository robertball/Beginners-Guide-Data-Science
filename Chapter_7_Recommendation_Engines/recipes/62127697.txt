Title:
Asiago Merlot Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 flank steak, pounded thin
10 pieces bacon
8 garlic cloves
1 package cooked bacon
1 c asiago
1 bottle merlot

Directions:
Pound steak thin, gently score meat
Cook bacon till just crunchy, add bacon and greese to food processor, mince, set aside
Mince Garlic, set aside
Spread bacon over meat, then garlic
Add asiago to spinach then spread mix over garlic
Roll meat tightly, (widest part of meat to widest part)secure with tooth picks, cover and let sit over night
Following day: remove tooth picks, cut met into 3/4 in rings, and secure with picks again
Brown meat in skillet, then place in deep pan, add enough Merlot to cover bottom and about 1/2 of the meat. Cover with foil and bake at 300 for 1 hr
Once meat is done, reserve juices and boil down and thicken, pour over meat and garnish with asiago
