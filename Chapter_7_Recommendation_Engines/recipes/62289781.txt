Title:
Lenas Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 tbsp sugar
1/2 cup oil
2 cups milk
3 eggs separated

Directions:
Separate eggs and beat the whites in a small bowl and set aside.
In a large bowl combine dry ingredients and stir.  Then add your liquids and stir until smooth.  Lastly, gently fold in your egg whites.

Submitter: 
STARLENA1
Description: 
My family really loves this basic waffle recipe...always comes out crispy and light in texture and easy to make.  It is not a dense heavy pancake batter like in some of the other recipes I tried.  Easy to add other flavourings such as cinnamon, vanilla or fruit.
