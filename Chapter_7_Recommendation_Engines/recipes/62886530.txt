Title:
Mac & Cheese Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 OZ's Elbow Macaroni
1/2 + 1/8 Cup Butter
3/8 (1/4 + 1/8) Cup Flour
48 OZ's Milk
1 1/2 Pounds Sharp Cheddar Shredded
3/4 Cup Bread Crumbs

Directions:
Cook Macaroni according to package directions until almost tender.  Meanwhile in a larger pot melt 1/2 Cup butter, stir in flour until smooth. gradually stir in milk and bring to a boil.  Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.  Reduce head and add cheese, stirring until melted.  Drain macaroni and stir into sauce.
Transfer to a greased 13X9 inch baking dish.  Toss remaining butter and toss with bread crumbs.  Sprinkle over bread crumbs.
Bake uncovered, at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes until golden brown.
