Title:
Classic Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds boiled, cubed potatoes (roughly 6 medium potatoes)
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup chopped celery
2-3 hard-boiled eggs, cut up.

Directions:
Combined cubed potatoes in a bowl with the onion and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Add mayonnaise, toss until potatoes are coated, and stir in celery and eggs

Submitter: 
CBerrier86
Description: 
A classic potato salad is a must on any cookout or picnic, and this is my favorite variation.
