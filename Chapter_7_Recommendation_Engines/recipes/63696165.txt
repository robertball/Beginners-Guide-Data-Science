Title:
Apricot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T vegetable oil
6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup apricot preserves
2 T siracha or other chili sauce
2 tsp Dijon mustard
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. In a large frying pan, heat oil over medium heat. Season chicken with salt and pepper to taste and cook until browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Add broth to pan and cover. Simmer 20 minutes, until the chicken is done. Remove chicken to a platter and cover with foil to keep warm.
3. Combine preserves, chili sauce, and mustard in the pan and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Allow to thicken slightly. Pour over chicken and serve.

Description: 
This Apricot Chicken is delicious served over rice with a side of steamed veggies.
