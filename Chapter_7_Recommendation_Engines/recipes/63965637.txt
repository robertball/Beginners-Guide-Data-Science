Title:
April's Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups hot tap water
2/3 cup oil
2/3 cup honey, or a little more
2 tablespoons salt
3 tablespoons instant yeast
whole wheat flour, 5-8 cups

Directions:
Pour hot water into bowl adding oil, honey, and salt.  Mix slightly to even temperature of water.  Put yeast on top of mixture then add flour until sides and bottom of bowl are free of dough.  Place lid on bowl and knead 5 minutes on high speed.
Put into loaves.  Turn oven to 350 degrees and bake until lightly browned.  Approximately 30-35 minutes.  (Baking time varies according to size of loaves)

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
Got this from my sister-in-law and it was our staple for years!
