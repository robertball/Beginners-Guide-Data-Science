Title:
Spicy Molasses Glazed Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5874931.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup molasses
1 1/2 tablespoon hot pepper relish
2 cloves garlic
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/3 cup cilantro
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 pound salmon with skin

Directions:
1. Combine molasses, relish, garlic, oil, cilantro, onion power, and cayenne pepper in a blender. Blend until smooth.
2.  Place salmon in a shallow glass baking dish, and coat with the molasses marinade mixture. Cover the dish, and marinate salmon in the refrigerator 30 minutes, turning once.
3.  Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
4. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake salmon skin side down uncovered 20 minutes, or until internal temperature reaches 145 degrees.

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
Baked Salmon made with a quick blended marinade with the deep rich flavor of molasses and the undertones of some spice.
