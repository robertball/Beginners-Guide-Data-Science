Title:
Healthy Turkey, Bean and Squash Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground turkey (93/7 is best)
1/2 cup texturized vegetable protein (optional)
1 can petite cut diced tomatoes
2 cans red beans, drained and rinsed
32 oz. box reduced sodium chicken broth
1 medium onion, diced
1 zucchini or yellow squash, grated
3 T. chili powder
1 T. cumin
1 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. red pepper
1 clove garlic, pressed or diced
1 bay leaf
2 T. pickled jalapeno juice
Salt to taste

Directions:
In a large stockpot, cook ground turkey until no longer pink, drain off fat if needed.
While meat is cooking, mix texturized vegetable protein with 1/2 cup chicken broth, set aside.
When meat is done, add all ingredients to pot and bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Use potato masher to crush beans and break up turkey until you reach the desired consistency.  Continue cooking another 10 minutes.
Remove bay leaf and serve with favorite chili toppings.

Submitter: 
Ashley O.
Description: 
This chili recipe is a staple at our house.  Using a potato masher makes all the difference.
