Title:
Cream Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 2 tubes crescent dinner rolls
- 2 8oz packages of cream cheese
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 egg yolk

Directions:
Press one tube of crescent rolls into 9x13 dish. Cream sugar and cream cheese together. Add vanilla and egg yolk. Mix well and spread over crescent roll "crust". Press second tub of rolls onto wax paper to form a top "crust" Place on top of cream cheese mixture. Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes until brown. Sprinkle with powdered sugar. Watch it disappear!

Submitter: 
Cassondra Farinholt Liles
Description: 
Easy as pie to make and there's never a drop left! My family thinks it's even better cold the next day!
