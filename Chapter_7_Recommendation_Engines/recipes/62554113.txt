Title:
Polly's Pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag stick pretzels
1 cup vegetable oil
1 beer delivered to significant other in other room watching TV
1 package dry Ranch Original dressing mix
1 tablespoon Cayenne Pepper
1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning

Directions:
Mix oil and dry seasoning mix in a small bowl.
Add pepper and Cajun seasoning and stir well.
Pour pretzels into a large pan and coat with mixture.
Stir before putting into the oven.
Bake for 1 hour @ 225.
Stir every 15 minutes
