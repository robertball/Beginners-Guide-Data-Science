Title:
Tim's Best Dry Rub Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup Sukrin Gold brown sugar alternative
1 tbsp salt
2 tbsp onion powder
2 tbsp smoked paprika
3 tbsp McCormicks Grill Mates Applewood Rub
1tbsp garlic powder
1tbsp black pepper

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a bowl and mix. Add  rub to the bottom of a breader bowl, add wings or ribs to the the top of the breader bowl and shake till coated. We cook our wings in an air fryer for 20 to 30 minutes. We normally grill the ribs, but plan to give them a try in the air fryer soon.

Submitter: 
Irene
Description: 
Great low carb dry rub for wings, ribs or pork butt.
