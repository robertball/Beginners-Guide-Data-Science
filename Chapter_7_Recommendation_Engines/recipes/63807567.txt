Title:
Cranberry Beets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups sliced peeled fresh beets
14 oz whole-berry cranberry sauce
2 tablespoons orange juice
1 teaspoon grated orange peel
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place beets in saucepan and cover with water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until tender.
In another saucepan, heat cranberry sauce over medium heat until melted. Add orange juice, orange peel and salt. Drain beets; gently stir into cranberry mixture. Heat through.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
When I'm looking for a different way to put cranberries on the menu, this unique side comes immediately to mind.  Paired with beets, the berries are tasty and tangy...and their ruby-red color really brightens up my holiday dinner table.
