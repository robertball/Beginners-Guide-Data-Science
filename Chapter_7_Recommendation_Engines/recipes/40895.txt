Title:
Sandies® Lemon Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975946.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Keebler® Sandies® Simply Shortbread Cookies, crushed
1/4 cup melted butter
3 eggs
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 teaspoon lemon zest, finely chopped
1/3 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Combine Sandies(R) and melted butter in a mixing bowl.  Press into a 9 x 13 inch baking pan.  Bake for five minutes, remove from oven and proceed with recipe.
Whisk together the remaining ingredients except the powdered sugar in a mixing bowl.  Pour over cookie crust and bake for 20-25 minutes or until lightly browned.
Let cool and dust with powdered sugar.  Cut into 2 inch squares.

Submitter: 
Keebler® Kitchens
Description: 
Buttery Sandies® Simply Shortbread Cookies make a meltingly delicious crust for this classic lemony confection. Whip up a batch for the perfect housewarming or holiday gift.
