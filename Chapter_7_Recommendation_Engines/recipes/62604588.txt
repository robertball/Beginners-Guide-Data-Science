Title:
Clone of King's Hawiian Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pineapple juice
1/2 cup water
2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
1 egg
1/2 stick I Can't Believe It's Not Butter®, melted
6 tablespoons sugar (or 1/2 cup Splenda®/Sugar Blend)
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 teaspoon ginger
3 cups bread flour

Directions:
Heat water & pineapple juice and put in large bowl. Sprinkle with yeast and let rest for about 5 minutes. Add egg, I Can't Believe It's Not Butter, sugar, vanilla & ginger and mix well. Stir in flour 1/2 cup at a time until fully incorporated. Cover and let rise, about 1 hour. Punch down, put in a greased pie pan and cover. Set aside for a second rise, about 1 hour.
Bake in an oven preheated to 350 degrees, for 25 to 30 minutes.
