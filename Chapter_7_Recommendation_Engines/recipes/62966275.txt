Title:
Whole Wheat Peanut Butter Oatmeal Dark Chocolate Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/732605.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat pastry flour
1 t baking soda
1 t cinnamon (I used a good, strong Vietnamese)
1 stick (1/2 cup) salted butter - if you use unsalted add 1/4 t of salt - (don't add salt if you use salted butter)
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/2 t vanilla
1 egg
1/2 cup old fashioned oats
1 cup dark chocolate morsels

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Line baking sheet with parchment or silpat.
Whisk the ww pastry flour, baking soda and salt (if using). Set aside.
Cream the butter, peanut butter, sugar, brown sugar and vanilla until well incorporated. Add the egg and beat to combine. Slowly add the dry mix just until combined, stir in the oats and dark chocolate morsels.
Using a cookie scoop drop onto baking sheets about 2 inches apart. Bake for approximately 10 minutes. Cool on the baking sheet and then store in airtight container.
