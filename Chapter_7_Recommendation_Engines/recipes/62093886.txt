Title:
Triple Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup soft butter
2/3 cup butter flavored Crisco
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar (light or dark depends on your prefrence)
1 small box cheesecake pudding mix
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
3 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
1/2cup milk chocolate chips
1/2 cup white chocolate chips
Nuts if desired.  (macadamias are good)

Directions:
Cream together butter, Crisco and sugars.
Add in pudding, eggs and vanilla and beat until light and fluffy.
Combine flour, salt and soda.
Add gradually to creamed mixture until well incorporated.
Stir in chocolate (and nuts if desired)
Drop by rounded tablespoons full onto baking stone.
Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 10 minutes or until lightly golden.
Remove to wire wrack to cool completely.
Store any that make it longer than the first 2 hours in an air tight container.
These are best eaten warm from the oven, so try freezing the dough in preformed balls to bake as needed.

Submitter: 
Melissa
Description: 
The best chocolate chip cookies you'll ever eat.  Made with semi-sweet, milk and white chocolate chips and real butter these are definately a treat.
