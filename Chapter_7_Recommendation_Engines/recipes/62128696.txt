Title:
Cucumbers with Sour Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium cucumbers, peeled & sliced
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
6 T. vegetable oil
2 T. vinegar
3/4 cup REAL mayonnaise
3 T. cream or milk
3 T. fine chopped fresh onion OR 1 1/2 T. dried
1/2 tsp. dried Basil
1 tsp. lemon juice
2 T. sour cream

Directions:
Mix together with a whisk the salt, pepper, oil and vinegar.  Add sliced cucumbers to this and refrigerate for two (2) hours; then drain (do NOT rinse).  Mix together the mayonnaise, cream or milk, onion, basil and lemon juice; add to drained cucumbers.  Salt and pepper to taste and add sour cream.  Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Shults3
Description: 
A marinated cucumber salad that is best made with fresh garden cucumbers when available.  Also good as a dip if you chop the cucumbers instead of slicing them.
