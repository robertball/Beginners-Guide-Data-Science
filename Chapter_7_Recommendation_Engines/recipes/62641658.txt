Title:
What the ELLE...HUMMUS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbls minced garlic
2 teaspoons coarse salt
1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
3 tablespoons sesame-seeds
3 tablespoons lemon/lime juice
1 tablespoon honey
2 tablespoons water, or as needed

Directions:
Mash garlic with the salt in a small bowl. Place into a blender or food processor along with the garbanzo beans, sesame-seeds, lemon/lime juice, honey, and enough water to cover the beans. Process until smooth.
