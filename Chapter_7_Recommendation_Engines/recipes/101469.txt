Title:
Fried Chicken Tenders Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977717.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons Lawry's® Seasoned Salt
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4-inch strips
1 cup oil
4 cups salad greens
1/3 cup Wish-Bone® Ranch Dressing
1 cup cooked black-eyed peas or black beans
1 cup fresh or drained canned corn
1 small papaya, peeled, seeded and sliced

Directions:
In shallow dish, add milk. In large resealable plastic bag, combine flour and Lawry's(R) Seasoned Salt. Dip chicken in milk, then place in bag and shake to coat with flour mixture.
In 12-inch skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat and cook chicken, a few pieces at a time, until thoroughly cooked and golden; drain on paper towels.
In large bowl, toss greens with Dressing. Add peas and corn. On 4 serving plates, evenly divide greens, then top with papaya and chicken. Serve, if desired, with warm corn bread.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
Breaded, seasoned chicken strips and slices of papaya top a colorful salad of mixed greens, corn and black-eyed peas.
