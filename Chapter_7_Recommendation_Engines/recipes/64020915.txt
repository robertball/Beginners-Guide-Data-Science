Title:
ID Sparks Speedy 30 - Second Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1116875.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups Fresh (washed and bagged) Baby Spinach
1/3 cup Salted Cashews (or your favorite nuts - Pecans are delicious too!)
1/3 cup Dried Berries (Dried Strawberries are yummy!)
1/3 cup Sparkling Cranberry Dressing

Directions:
Place spinach in medium bowl, then add nuts and berries.  Top with dressing and lightly toss.  That's it!!! There's no cooking or chopping, just tossing and enjoying!

Submitter: 
ID Sparks
Description: 
This is my family's favorite salad! I love it because it is so quick and versatile! My grandkids all love to make this salad. Well, except the babies, but that's only a matter of time. ;-)
