Title:
Kalee's Peach Apple Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 liter) bottle Riesling
2 cups apple juice
1 cup peach schnapps
1 (8 ounce) can pineapple chunks in juice
2 oranges, peeled and sectioned
2 apples, cored and cut into wedges
1 liter ginger ale

Directions:
Mix Riesling, apple juice, and peach schnapps together in a large pitcher. Stir in pineapple chunks and their juice, orange sections, and apple wedges. Refrigerate until flavors blend, 1 hour to overnight. Add ginger ale just before serving.

Submitter: 
Kalee
Description: 
A light, fruity white wine sangria perfect for a hot summer day! Serve over ice.
