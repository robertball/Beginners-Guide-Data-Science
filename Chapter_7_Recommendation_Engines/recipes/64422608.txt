Title:
White Bean Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary
1 garlic clove
1 15oz can Connellini beans
4 oz crumbled feta cheese
2 tablespoons olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons chopped Kalamata olives

Directions:
Place garlic and rosemary in a food processor and process until finely chopped.  Add beans and half the cheese, and process until smooth.
While still running, drizzle in oil and add salt and pepper.  Transfer to a bowl and stir in remaining cheese and olives.
