Title:
Doro Wat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Bermuda Onions, chopped finely
3 oz butter or olive oil
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon paprika
1/2 black pepper
1/4 teaspoon ginger
3 cup water
3 lb chicken, cut in 1-inch pieces, bones and neck and gizzards included.
1/4 cup lemon juice
8 peeled hard-boiled eggs

Directions:
In a 4- to 6-quart Dutch oven or heavy stew pot. Brown Bermuda Onions chopped finely, without fat, until quite dark, stirring constantly.
Add butter or olive oil, cayenne pepper, paprika, black pepper, ginger.Blend the seasonings into the onions. Add 1 cups of water.
Soak chicken in 2 cups WATER to which lemon juice has been added, for 10 minutes.
Drain the water from each piece of chicken. Add chicken to onion mixture, stirring it through. Cover. Simmer over low heat until chicken is tender.
Add more water, if necessary, to bring to stew texture (or if Wat is watery, thicken with 2 tablespoons of flour dissolved in 2 tablespoons of water).
Add eggs a few minutes before serving.
