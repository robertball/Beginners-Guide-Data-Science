Title:
Strapping John  / "Strammer Max" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Slices of Brown Rye Bread
1 oz of Butter
2 oz of sliced Gruyere or Swiss Cheese
2 oz of sliced Ham
1 Egg.
Parsley, salt & Pepper.

Directions:
Butter the Bread and place on the Large serving Plate.  Lightly fry the Ham Turning and covering with the Cheese.  Set aside while frying the Egg. ( Not to well; Yolk should be runny).  when done
Cover the bread with the Ham and Cheese and Top with the Egg.  Sprinkle with Parsley; Salt and Pepper.

Submitter: 
Alois
Description: 
Hearty Open Face Sandwich with Ham, Cheese and Egg.
