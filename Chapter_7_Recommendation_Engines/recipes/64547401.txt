Title:
Big Ray's Healthy Coffee Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3250207.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup nonfat plain Greek yogurt
1 cup cold brewed coffee
1 1/2 bananas, cut into chunks
1 Tbsp ground flax seed
1/2 tsp chia seed
2 tsp honey
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp  nutmeg powder
6 ice cubes

Directions:
Place all of the ingredients in a heavy-duty blender (one that can crush ice).
Blend until smooth. Serve.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Great way to start the day! When my bananas start to get a bit soft, I peel them and cut into pieces and store in the freezer.  This recipe is a great way to use them up and you can cut down on the ice cubes.
