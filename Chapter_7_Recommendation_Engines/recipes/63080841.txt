Title:
Beautiful White Chocolate Butter Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/778368.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup butter, softened
3 Tablespoons milk
2 1/4 Cups powdered sugar
8 oz. white chocolate, melted and cooled

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat butter, milk, powdered sugar, and white chocolate at low speed until blended.
Increase speed to high and beat until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
ajo263
Description: 
This is a delicious frosting that gives an extra depth of flavor to an otherwise plain buttercream.  It is smooth enough to beautifully frost a cake, and stable enough to use for piping.
