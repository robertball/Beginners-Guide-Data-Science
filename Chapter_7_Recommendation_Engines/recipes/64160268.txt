Title:
Zucchini Tots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1428650.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C grated zucchini
1/4 C minced onion
1/2 C bread crumbs
1/2 C shredded cheddar cheese
2 eggs
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp basil
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp rosemary
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp sage

Directions:
Place zucchini in colander in sink or over bowl.  Lightly salt zucchini and allow extra liquid to drain for 1 hour.  Lightly press with paper towel to remove remaining excess liquid.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Spray mini muffin tin with cooking spray.  Combine all ingredients in large mixing bowl.  Evenly distribute zucchini mixture between muffin cups, flattening to make them even with top of muffin tin
Bake tots for 15-20 minutes or until browned.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
These delicious little tots are just as yummy as their potato cousins.  Easy to make, delicious to dip, and a great way to use up extra zucchini from the garden!
