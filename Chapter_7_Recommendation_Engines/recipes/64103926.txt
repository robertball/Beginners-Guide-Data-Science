Title:
Paleo Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1254681.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups blanched almond flour
3/4 teaspoon sea salt
1teaspoon baking soda
Â¼ cup coconut oil, melted and cooled
2 large eggs, room temperature
1 tablespoon  raw organic honey

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
In a medium bowl, combine almond flour, salt and baking soda with a fork.
Add in coconut oil and combine into the mixture
In a small bowl whisk eggs then combine into the mixture.
Mix until a dough ball forms.
Using a large ice cream scoop to ensure all biscuits are the same size.
Scoop batter and add to parchment paper then lightly press down to create a biscuit.
Bake for 15 minutes.
