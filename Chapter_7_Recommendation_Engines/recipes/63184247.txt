Title:
Summer Radish Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups sliced radishes
1 Cup finely sliced red onion
1 Cup sliced and seeded cucumber
1/2 tsp salt
Dressing:
1 clove of garlic minced
1/2 Cup Extra virgin olive oil
1 Tsp. fresh chopped dill
2 Tbs white wine vinegar
1/2 tsp sugar

Directions:
Wash and slice each of the red radishes. Salt and set aside. Chop remaining vegetables and add to large bowl.
Wash, dry and chop dill weed.
Peel and chop garlic add both to a small bowl add vinegar, oil and sugar mix well.
Drain liquid (if any) from radishes and add to bowl with veggies. Pour dressing over and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
*Serve with a sprinkle of bacon bits

Description:
