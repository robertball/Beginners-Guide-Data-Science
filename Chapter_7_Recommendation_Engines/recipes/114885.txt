Title:
Ciao Bella Ciabatta Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991761.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ciabatta sandwich rolls, sliced horizontally
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 (16 ounce) sirloin steak, cooked, thin-sliced
1 (7 ounce) package Borden® Shred Medleys™ Tuscan Herb Shredded Cheese, seasoned
4 romaine lettuce leaves
4 tomato slices
4 red onion slices

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350 degrees F.
SPREAD mayonnaise equally over both cut sides of each roll; layer sliced steak on bottom half of each roll.
SPRINKLE seasoned cheese equally over top and bottom half of each roll.
ARRANGE in baking dish; cover loosely with foil and heat for 12 to 15 minutes or until cheese is melted.
LAYER lettuce, tomato and onion.
PLACE top halves of rolls on each sandwich to serve.

Submitter: 
Borden® Shred Medleys™
Description: 
Cooked steak, melted Tuscan Herb cheese, and fresh vegetables on ciabatta rolls make this a tasty hot sandwich.
