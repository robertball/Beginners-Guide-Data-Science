Title:
Garden Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983415.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups shredded cabbage
1 cup shredded carrots
1/3 cup chopped green pepper
1/3 cup chopped onion
1 cup MIRACLE WHIP Salad Dressing
salt and pepper

Directions:
Mix vegetables and salad dressing in large bowl. Season to taste with salt and black pepper. Refrigerate several hours or until chilled. Serve in bowl lined with green and purple kale, if desired.
