Title:
Bacardi Rum Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 box yellow cake mix
1 pkg.vanilla instant pudding
4 eggs
1/2 cup Bacardi gold rum
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup cold water
Glaze:
1/2 cup marg.
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup Bacardi rum

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.Grease and flour 4L bundt pan or 3L tube pan.Sprinkle nuts on bottom of pan.In mixing bowl combine cake mix,pudding,eggs rum,oil and water.Beat on low speed until moistened.Beat at high speed for 2 min.Pour batter over nuts.Bake for 1 hr. til tooth pick comes out clean.Cool 15 min.then turn onto serving plate.
Glaze:
Melt marg.in sauce pan.Carefully stir in sugar,water and rum.Boil 5 min.stirring constantly.Remove from heat.Prick top of cake with fork.Spoon and brush glaze evenly over top and sides.Allow cake to absorb glaze.
