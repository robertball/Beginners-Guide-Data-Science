Title:
Yummy Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/469384.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb of lean ground beef, turkey or chicken
1 package of taco seasoning
3 romaine hearts
2-3 diced avocados
1 cup of cherry tomatoes
1 cup of halfed snap peas
1/2 cup grated or chopped carrots
1/2 cup of chopped cucumber
1 cup of drained and rinsed black beans (optional)
1/2 cup of drained green or black sliced olives
1/2-1 cup shredded cheddar of monteray jack cheese (extra old is best)
1 cup crushed zesty cheese dorritos
1/4 cup of fat free italian dressing, or to taste

Directions:
Cook and drain meat then season acccording to package and set aside.
Wash and chop romaine hearts and veggies while meat is browning.
Toss lettuce, veggies, beans and olives in a large bowl.
Add cooked and seasoned meat while still warm.
Add shredded cheese.
Toss ingredients with salad dressing and top with crushed nacho chips.
Serve and enjoy!
TIP: My husband likes his salad slightly heated in the microwave (about 30 seconds as it brings out the flavours).

Submitter: 
jenyoung2005
Description: 
We use our leftover taco meat (beef or turkey) for this flavorful dinner salad.  Loaded with romaine hearts, avocado, olives and zesty cheese nacho chips for any interesting and delicious alternative to croutons!

This yummy salad works great with just about any veggies you have available and the kids love it.
