Title:
Easy Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  4-5 lb beef roast(any cut will work but the less fat the better)
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1  16 ounce jar pepperonci peppers or 1 jar hot garden mix pickled vegetables

Directions:
Place roast in crockpot, sprinkle with garlic powder, add peppers with their juice, cover and cook on low 8-10 hours or until beef is tender.  Serve on hoagie rolls with mozzerella cheese.  Also makes a great main dish when topped with a simple marinara sauce

Submitter: 
Alane Owens Gross
Description: 
Easy Italian beef with a kick.  Great for tailgate parties
