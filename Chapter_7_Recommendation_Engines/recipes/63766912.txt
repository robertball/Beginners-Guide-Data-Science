Title:
Curried Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 T curry powder
1 t salt
2 t fresh ground pepper
1/4 c olive oil
1 T red wine vinegar
2 T non-pareil capers
1/4 med sweet onion, diced
4-4 oz cans albacore tuna

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Refrigerate for at least one hour before serving.
Add to any preferred bread or crackers.

Submitter: 
Tyra Newhouse Farris
Description: 
The capers and curry make a delightful combination of tart and spicy!
