Title:
Aunt Libby's Southern Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1042510.jpg
Ingredients:
LOAF:
2 lb hamburger
2 eggs
1 medium onion
1 cup ketchup
1 Cup Italian breadcrumbs
1 1/2 tps salt
1/4 tsp pepper
SAUCE: HEAT
3 Tbsp white wine vinegar
3 Tbsp brown sugar
2 Tbsp dijon mustard
1 1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup water
2 tsp worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
For loaf mix:
hamburger
eggs
onion chopped
breadcrumbs
salt
pepper
1 cup of ketchup
Combine loaf ingredients together and mold into lightly buttered standard meatloaf pan.
For Sauce:
In a saucepan on medium heat, mix together vinegar,
brown sugar, mustard, 1 1/2 cups ketchup, water, and Worcestershire sauce until warm (about 10 minutes).
Pour 1/3 of sauce over meatloaf and bake in preheated oven for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, basting the meatloaf with the remaining sauce every 25 minutes or so.
Take out of oven and let cool at least 10 minutes before serving.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Potter
Description: 
This my 86 year old Aunt Libby's home-made meatloaf recipe.  I finally had her write it down for me last year.  You can be sure no-one will tell you this meatloaf is dry!!!
