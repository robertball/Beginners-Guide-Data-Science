Title:
Falafel Bites a must! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4704960.jpg
Ingredients:
1 - 16 oz can garbanzo beans drained
1/2 cup Panko bread crumbs divided
1/4 cup chopped white onion
1 egg
2 tablespoon dried parsley
1 tablespoon curry powder
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper
1 cup olive oil approximately for frying
Serving Sauce:

Directions:
Place in food processor  (or blender) garbanzo beans, 1/4 cup of Panko bread crumbs, onion, egg, parsley, curry, garlic, black powder, lemon juice and 1/4 cup olive oil
Puree till coarse. You don't want too moist, so add more Panko slowly as needed.
Take tablespoon of mixture and roll into balls and coat with Panko crumbs. Place on plate. Repeat until mixture gone.
Heat approximately one inch canola/olive oil in skillet over medium heat
Pan fry dollips until crispy golden brown each side approximately 2-3 mins each side.
Remove and drain on paper towels (reduce excess oil)
Serve with a  dipping Tzatziki Cucumber sauce or even a spicy Sriracha sauce

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
Easy and super quick Falafel recipe and I had everything in my pantry already.  I love as snack with a Tzatziki Cucumber sauce.
