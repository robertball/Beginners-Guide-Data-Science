Title:
Crock Pot Italian Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs beef stew meat
1/2 bag baby carrots, quartered
1/2 yellow onion, sliced & quartered
4 red potatoes, quartered
2 stalks celery
2 cloves garlic, chopped
3/4 cup red wine
1/4 cup beef or chicken broth
4 tbsp tomato paste
1 tsp dried rosemary
seasoned flour (s&p;, onion powder, garlic powder)
vegetable oil
salt & pepper

Directions:
* Toss meat in seasoned flour, shake off excess
* Coat saute pan in about 1 tbsp vegetable oil, brown meat on all sides
* Add meat to crock pot
* Cover with vegetables
* Mix wine, broth, tomato paste, rosemary in a small bowl to combine
* Add to crockpot
* Cook on low 4-6 hours or until vegetables are tender

Submitter: 
Christine Foley Wanner
