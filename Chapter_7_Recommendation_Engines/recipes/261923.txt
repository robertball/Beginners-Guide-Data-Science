Title:
Sugar-Free and Keto Fat Bombs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups unsweetened coconut flakes
1/2 cup melted coconut oil
1/2 cup melted cocoa butter
1/4 cup hemp seed hearts

Directions:
Blend coconut flakes, coconut oil, and cocoa butter in a food processor until creamy, about 2 minutes. Shape mixture into 1-inch balls; roll in hemp hearts. Chill in the refrigerator until set, about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Fioa
Description: 
Coconut oil, cocoa butter, and hemp seed hearts combine to create these amazing sugar-free and keto fat bombs, ready to boost up your workout.
