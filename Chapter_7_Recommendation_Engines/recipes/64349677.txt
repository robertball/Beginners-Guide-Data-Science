Title:
Authentic Gluehwein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2111715.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup orange juice
1/2 cup white sugar
3 cinnamon stick
1 orange
10 whole cloves
1 (750 milliliter) bottle red wine

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine the OJ, sugar, and cinnamon sticks. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer
Slice the orange and add the slices to the simmering water as well as the cloves. Continue simmering for 30 minutes, until syrupy
Pour in the wine, and heat until steaming but not simmering. Remove the cloves and orange slices. Serve hot in mugs or glasses that have been preheated in warm water (cold glasses will break.)

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
When my husband and I returned from the Christmas Markets in Munich I scoured the internet trying to find a Gluehwein recipe that we so fell in love with.  Many came close but none were perfect, so after some tweaking and perfecting.....this is it!
