Title:
Grilled Salmon with Rosemary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb Salmon
2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
2 tsp fresh lemon juice
1/4 tsp salt
pinch freshly ground black pepper
2 cloves garlic minced
2 tsp fresh rosemary leaves chopped
Olive Oil Cooking Spray

Directions:
Cut fish into 4 equal size portions.  Combine olive-oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, garlic and rosemary in a bowl.  Brush mixture on to fish.
Spray rack of broiler pan with olive oil cooking spray. Arrange Fish.  Broil 4" from heat for 4-6 minutes per 1/2 inch thickness.  If fish is more than 1" thick turn half way through cooking.
