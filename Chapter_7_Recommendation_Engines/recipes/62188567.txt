Title:
Loaded Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium red potatoes boiled till tender and cut into chunks leaving some of the skin on potatoes
5 boiled eggs, shells removed and cut into chunks
1 lb bacon
1 Table spoon of bacon grease reserved
4 green onions chopped
1/4 cup red onions
8 oz. sharp cheddar cheese
1 cup Hellman's Real Mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 tsp Kosher salt
1/2 tsp cracked black pepper

Directions:
Boil potatoes and eggs till done. Fry 1 lb bacon and reserve 1 bacon grease. chop both green and red onions.
In a large bowl add all the ingredients and mix well. If the salad is to thick add more sour cream or mayonnaise.
Refrigerate for at least 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Kathi Richards Smith
Description: 
This is a potato salad is changed up by adding cheddar cheese and bacon, it was created created after I had such great reviews on my loaded baked potato soup. It is the same concept but turned into a salad.
