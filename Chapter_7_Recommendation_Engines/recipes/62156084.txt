Title:
Grandma's Cheese Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz bag of Egg Noodles
1 8oz package of Cream Cheese, softened
2 cloves Garlic, minced
1 medium Onion, chopped
1 cup Ricotta Cheese or Cottage Cheese
2 TBL Dill Weed
4 TBL Butter
Salt to taste

Directions:
Cook egg noodles in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente, drain.
Meanwhile, melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.  Add onions and garlic, cook until onions just start to carmelize.  Stir in dill, cream cheese and ricotta; cook until heated through and warm.
Add noodles back to the large pot and pour the cheese mixture over.  Stir to mix well.  Salt to taste.

Submitter:

Description: 
Sunday's growing up were always spent on Grandma's farm.  She always prepared a big lunch so we were full when it came time to get the work done.  This was one of my favorite noodle dishes she made and one of the few things I have been able to recreate.
