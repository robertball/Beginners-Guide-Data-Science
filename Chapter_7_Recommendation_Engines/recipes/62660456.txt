Title:
Poached Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of frozen salmon filets;thawed
3 tbsp lemon juice
1 cup water
1/2 tsp salt
8 cloves of garlic, chopped
1 sliced yellow onion
8 tbsp butter or margarine
1/2 tbsp fresh or dried dill

Directions:
Place salmon in a greased slow cooker.
In a saucepan, bring all ingredients except for salmon to a boil.
Pour over salmon.
Cover and cook 4 hours on low.
Serve with rice
