Title:
Meat Ball Hoagie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/713902.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Hoagie Roll
10 Small Or 5 Large Meat Balls (Or So)
1/4 cup Spaghetti Sauce
3 Slices Of Provolone Cheese
2 Oz Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
Preheat Oven To 375F
If the sauce Is not as thick as you like it simply place the sauce in a fry pan and turn up the heat stirring as it boils and reduces.
Take Hoagie Roll And Place It In The Oven On A Pan With Rack, For 5 Mins To Soften Up The Roll.
Slice Open Roll, Add Slices Of Provolone Cheese
Add Meat Balls, And Top With Sauce.
Sprinkle Mozzarella Cheese Over The Meat Balls
Put Back In Oven And Melt The Cheese
Take Out Of Oven After 10 Mins, And Serve.

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
The first meal my wife and I had after we got married (about 35 years ago) at a restaurant\deli in Wildwood, NJ. called A&LP (It is Still there) it was the best meat ball hoagie I have ever had. So meat ball hoagies have always had a special place in my heart.
