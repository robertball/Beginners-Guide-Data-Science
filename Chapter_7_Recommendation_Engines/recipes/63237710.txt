Title:
Fire Roasted Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Roma tomatoes, halved lengthwise
1 small white onion, quartered
1 clove garlic
1 jalapeno, roughly chopped
1/4 cup cilantro
1 dried New Mexico, chipotle, or guajillo chili pepper, rehydrated
~juice of 1 lime

Directions:
Preheat oven broiler
Place the tomatoes on a foil-lines sheet pan, and broil to char the skins 13-15 minutes, then flip and char the other side for an additional 5 minutes.
Combine the tomatoes with all the other ingredients in a food processor until well blended.
