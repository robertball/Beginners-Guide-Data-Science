Title:
Debbie's Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. sweet sausage - browned
1/2 C. chopped celery
1/2 C. chopped onion
3 C. water
1 tsp. chicken bouillon
1 16 oz. can crushed tomatoes
1 8 oz. can kidney beans
1 10 oz. box broccoli or any vegetable

Directions:
Mix all together and cook until heated through.

Submitter: 
Ursula
Description: 
This soup is so easy, fast and hearty! Makes a great quickie dinner.
