Title:
Al Fresco Italian Chicken Sausage Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894417.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Al Fresco Italian Chicken Sausage Links
1/2 stick of butter
4 tbs. flour
2 cups milk
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown sausage in skillet over medium heat.
Remove sausage from pan.
Melt butter in pan.
Add flour and whisk for 1-2 minutes, until mix thickens.
Add milk while whisking constantly.
Add sausage back into gravy if desired.
Salt and pepper to taste - stir until thickened.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jennifer C. Martin
Description: 
This is a spicy little sausage gravy - tastes great over rice, Cajun food, or even biscuits!  Enjoy!
