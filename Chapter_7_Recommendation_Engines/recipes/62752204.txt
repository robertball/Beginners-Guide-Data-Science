Title:
Balsamic chicken salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/634655.jpg
Ingredients:
2 heads of Romaine lettuce
3 chicken breasts
1 jar of balsamic vinegarette
1 sliced avocado
1 can of whole kernel corn, drained
1 can of black beans, drained
ranch dressing
balsamic glaze
bacon pieces

Directions:
marinate the chicken in the balsamic vinegarette for at least a 30 minutes
bake the chicken in the oven on 400 degrees until cooked through
slice the chicken fajita style
cut up the lettuce and lay in a shallow pasta bowl
lay the chicken on top
add the avocado
add just enough corn and black beans to cover the top or to your liking
add cooked bacon pieces
drizzle the top with ranch dressing
drizzle the top with balsamic glaze

A great salad to have for lunch or dinner!
