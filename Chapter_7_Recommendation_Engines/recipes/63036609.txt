Title:
Sweet Pepper Pasta Toss with Baby Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package uncooked farfalle (bow tie) pasta
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 medium red bell pepper, chopped
1 medium yellow bell pepper, chopped
2 cups baby spinach
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 pinch dried basil
1 small chopped jalape√±o pepper
1/8 cup balsamic vinegar
salt and ground black pepper to taste
4 ounces Parmesan, shaved

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in red pepper, yellow pepper, spinach and garlic, add balsamic. Season with basil, salt and black pepper. Cook until vegetables are tender.
In a large bowl, toss cooked pasta with skillet mixture. Sprinkle with parmesan to serve.
