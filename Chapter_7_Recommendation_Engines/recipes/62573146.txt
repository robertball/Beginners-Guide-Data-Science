Title:
Sweeter Toads in the Hole I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/543874.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package pork sausage links
2 eggs
1 cup Bisquick
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup Grade A maple syrup
1/2 cup milk
Capful vanilla
a few shakes of cinnamon (sorry I didn't measure)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Brown sausage in a large skillet; drain and place sausage in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a small bowl beat eggs; add bisquick, maple, water and milk and beat all together. Let sit 5 minutes.
Pour mixture over sausage and bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until top is browned and crusty.
