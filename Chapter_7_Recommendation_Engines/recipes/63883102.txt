Title:
Aluminum Foil Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077247.jpg
Ingredients:
7 potatoes
2 large onions
2 t oil or butter
Beef strips, hamburger patties or chicken breasts

Directions:
Place foil in cooking pan. Add sliced potatoes, onions, salt & pepper to taste; add oil or butter.
Cover potatoes w/ patties. Pull foil over ingredients.
Bake 1 1/2 hours in 300 oven. Don't open foil until food is ready!

Description: 
Healthy meal that can go in the slow cooker, oven or on the grill.
