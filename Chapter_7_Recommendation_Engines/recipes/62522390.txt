Title:
Green Chili Onion Olive Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954649.jpg
Ingredients:
4 oz. chopped green chilis
8 oz. chopped black olives
1 can petite cut diced tomatoes
8 green onions chopped
1 t. Garlic salt
1 t. Garlic powder
2 T. Vinegar
4 T. Olive Oil
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and refrigerate overnight.
