Title:
Green Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/814230.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 small can evaporated milk
1/2 lb. soft processed cheese (like Velveeta)
1 sm. can green chilies ,chopped
1/2 lb.longhorn cheese, grated
1 cup onions, chopped (optional)
1 lb. ground beef (with a little Lawry's seasoning)
1 dozen tortillas

Directions:
Heat soup, milk, and processed cheese until melted.  Add chopped green chilies.
Cook meat and onions.  Set aside.
Fill each tortilla with meat mixture and longhorn cheese.  Roll tightly and place in baking pan.
Pour cheese sauce over tortillas and cover with foil.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
