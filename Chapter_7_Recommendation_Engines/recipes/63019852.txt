Title:
Hamballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 lb. ground pork
1 egg, beaten
1 can tomato soup
1 bottle Sweet Baby Ray's bbq sauce (original)
1/2 C milk
1 tsp. vinegar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In large bowl, mix together ground pork, ground beef and egg.  Form into small meatballs.
In medium size bowl, combine tomato soup, bbq sauce, milk and vinegar.  Mix well.
Place meatballs in a single layer in a greased 13x9 baking dish.  Bake in oven for 30 minutes or until juices run clear.  Drain off fat.
Pour sauce mixture over meatballs and bake for 15 minutes more and serve!

Submitter: 
RavenQ
Description: 
This super simple,delicious recipe has been in my family for generations.  It can be made as an appetizer or main dish and everyone always loves it! It is also great for potluck style get togethers!
