Title:
Refreshing Orange Scrub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 of an Orange
4 T Cornmeal

Directions:
Squeeze orange juice and pulp into a bowl and add the cornmeal. Mix into a paste.
To Use:  Apply onto freshly washed face and body. Scrub gently for 2-3 minutes. Rinse, tone, moisturize.
