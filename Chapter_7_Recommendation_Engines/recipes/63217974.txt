Title:
Jammed Packed Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/868342.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 cup ea. chopped onions, celery, green peppers
1 tsp. minced garlic
8 oz (227g) turkey kielbassa, casing removed, cut into bite size pcs.
2.5 cups reduced sodium chicken broth
1 1/3 cup salsa
1 1/4 cup uncooked rice
1 bay leaf
1 tsp cajun seasoning
1/2 tsp ea. dried thyme and oregano
12 oz (340g) uncooked shrimp
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley

Directions:
Heat oil in large stock pot over med-high heat.  Add onions, green pepper, celery and garlic. cook and stir until veggies begin to soften.
Add keilbassa and cook for another 2-3 minutes. add all remaining ingredients except shrimp and parsley.  Bring to a boil. reduce heat and simmer, covered for 15 minutes or until rice is tender. Exact cooking time will depend on brand of rice. stir occasionally to prevent rice from sticking to bottom of pot.
stir in shrimp and parsley.  Cook for 5 more minutes, until shrimp are cooked through. let stand covered for 5 minutes before serving.
