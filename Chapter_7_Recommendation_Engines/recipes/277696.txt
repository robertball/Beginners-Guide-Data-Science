Title:
Vanilla Walnut Berry Trail Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7471791.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 cups California walnut halves and pieces
1 cup yogurt-covered raisins
3/4 cup raw pumpkin seeds
2 cups freeze-dried strawberries

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium skillet over medium heat. Swirl in vanilla and sugar, then add walnuts. Cook, stirring frequently, for 5 minutes, or until toasted. Let cool completely.
Add raisins and pumpkin seeds to walnuts and toss well to mix. Add strawberries and toss gently. Store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
California Walnuts
Description: 
Lightly sweetened vanilla-flavored walnuts are tossed with yogurt-covered treats and dried fruits.
