Title:
Roasted Rosemary Parmesan Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/667311.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbsp. Olive Oil
2 cloves Garlic Minced
1 tbsp. Dried Rosemary
3 tbsp. sliced Scallions
1/4 c. parmesan cheese
5 medium sized Red Potatoes cut into bite size chunks
Salt and Pepper as desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400* F. Place olive oile, rosemary, garlic, salt, pepper, and potato chunks in a large ziploc bag.  Toss until all potatoes are coated with the mixture.  Place in oven for 40 minutes turning them once halfway through the cooking time. When there is only 5 minutes remaining sprinkle parmesan and scallion slices on the potatoes and continue cooking.
