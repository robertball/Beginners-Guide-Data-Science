Title:
cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Graham cracker crust ( or make your own with 1 pkg. grahams, crushed, combined with 6 T. melted butter)
FILLING
3 eggs
12 oz. cream cheese
½ c. sugar
1 tsp. lemon juice
¼ c. sour cream
TOPPING
½ c. sour cream
2 T. sugar
½ tsp. vanilla
Combine, and pour on cake.  Bake 5 minutes at 425.  Chill for eight hours.

Directions:
Mix filling ingredients well.  Pour into crust.  Bake 25 minutes at 375 or until fairly firm.  Dust with cinnamon. Cool.
Combine topping ingredients and pour on cooled pie.  Bake 5 minutes at 425.
Chill pie for 8 hours
