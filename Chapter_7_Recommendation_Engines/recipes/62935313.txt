Title:
Pizza Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/719164.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1 oz of peperoni, sliced
4 oz ham, chopped
8 oz mozzarella cheese
4 oz mild cheddar cheese, shredded
1/4 tsp dried oregano leaves
1/4 tsp dried Basel leaves
1/4 tsp dried rosemary leaves
1/4 tsp dried thyme leaves
1/8 tsp garlic powder
8 oz tomato sauce
4 Kaiser rolls, or large loaf of French bread

Directions:
Brown ground beef in skillet over moderate heat, stirring until pinkness disappears. drain off all fat.
Stir in cheese, ham, peperoni, herbs and spices Add tomato sauce.
heat mixture until bubbly. Spoon onto split rolls or on length of French bread which has been split horizontally Top with the cheese.
Place under broiler. Broil until cheese melts, about 3 min. Serve immediately

Very quick, and easy to make Italian Style Sandwich full of flavor.
