Title:
Chelsey's Strawberry Salad with Poppy Seed Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3876100.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head green leaf lettuce - rinsed, dried, and chopped
1 red onion, sliced into rings
1 pint strawberries, halved
1/2 cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle WhipÂ®)
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup milk
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Directions:
Place lettuce, red onion, and strawberries in a large bowl. Place creamy salad dressing, sugar, milk, vinegar, and poppy seeds in a jar with a tight-fitting lid; cover jar and shake until smooth. Pour dressing over salad; toss to combine.

Submitter: 
Chelsey Carr
Description: 
A delightful, light salad that's perfect for the summertime. It's a huge family favourite!
