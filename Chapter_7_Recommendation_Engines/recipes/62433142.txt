Title:
Key Lime Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/452536.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces limoncello
3/4 fluid ounce vodka
3/4 fluid ounce triple sec
2 tsp. key lime juice
1 slice key lime

Directions:
Pour the limoncello, vodka, triple sec and key lime juice into a cocktail shaker over ice. Cover, and shake. Strain into a chilled martini glass and garnish with the key lime slice.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
A tart and refreshing martini, straight up.
