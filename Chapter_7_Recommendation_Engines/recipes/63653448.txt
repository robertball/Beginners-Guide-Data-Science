Title:
Chicken Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1015353.jpg
Ingredients:
2- 2 1/2 lb of boneless skinless chicken thighs
3/4 White Onion Sliced
2 Cloves of Garlic (Smashed)
Tsp. Salt
1 Package of Thin Spaghetti
1/2 Block- 32 oz- Velveeta Cheese
1 Can- 10 3/4 oz- Rotel (Mild or Original)
1/2 Can- 10 3/4 oz- Cream of Mushroom
1/2 Can-14.5 oz- Chicken Broth (Store brand is fine)
1/2 Green Bell Pepper (Chopped)
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Put 3/4 chopped onion, 2 smashed garlic cloves and chicken breasts in pot. Put enough water in the pot to cover chicken breasts. Bring to a boil, cover and reduce heat to medium- medium high and cook for 10-15 minutes or until chicken is cooked all the way through. When finished shred chicken with forks.
While the chicken is cooking, begin cooking the noodles.
When noodles are finished add cream of mushroom, can of rotel with juice, chopped green bell pepper and a little chicken broth in pot on low heat. Mix.
Add small pieces of velveeta at a time and mix. Slowly add a bit more chicken broth. When the cheese melts, add more pieces of velveeta and chicken broth. Repeat until velveeta and chicken broth are gone.
Add chicken. Salt and pepper to taste. Mix and enjoy!
