Title:
Ice Cream Sandwich Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
17  mini ice cream sandwiches
1 large tub Cool Whip, thawed
1/4 cup chocolate syrup
1/4 cup caramel syrup
1 Skor bar, chopped

Directions:
Arrange 14 ice cream sandwiches in an ungreased 13 x 9 pan. Cut remaining sandwiches in half lengthwise; fill in the spaces in the dish.
Spread with caramel and whipped topping.
Drizzle with chocolate syrup and sprinkle with chopped Skor bar.
Cover and freeze for at least 45 minutes. Cut into squares.
