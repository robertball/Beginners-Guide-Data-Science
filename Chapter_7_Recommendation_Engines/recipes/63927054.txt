Title:
Oven-Roasted Cauliflower with Garlic, Olive Oil and Lemon Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 to 6 cups cauliflower floret, about 1 1/2 inches in diameter (from 1 medium cauliflower)
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1 Tablespoon sliced garlic
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
2 Tablespoon grated Parmesan
Chopped chives, for garnish

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 450 to 500 degrees
Place the cauliflower floret in  large sauté pan or a roasting pan.  Drizzle the olive oil over he cauliflower, and season with the garlic, lemon juice, salt and pepper.  Place the sauté/roasting pan in the oven and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally to ensure even roasting.
Remove from the oven and sprinkle with the Parmesan.  Garnish with chopped chives and serve immediately while still warm.
