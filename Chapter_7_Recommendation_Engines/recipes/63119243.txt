Title:
Old-Fashioned Molasses Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C butter/margarine, shortening
3/4 c molasses
2 1/4 c GF flours
1 c sour cream/yogurt/milk/half and half (buttermilk)
1 t baking soda
1/4 t salt
2 t cinnamon
1/4 t ground cloves
2 t ginger

Directions:
1. Beat butter/shortening with molasses
2. Beat in everything else.
3. Bake 20-22 min at 350 degrees in paper muffin cups.
