Title:
Italian Style Asparagus with Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
250g/8oz Asparagus - trimmed with bottom ends peeled
45g/1½oz Butter
100g/3½oz Onion - finely chopped
120g/4oz Prosciutto/Serrano Ham - cut into thin strips
250ml/8fl oz Crème Fraiche or Double (Heavy) Cream
60g/2oz Parmesan Reggiano Cheese - grated
Salt - for pasta
400gr/14oz Fettuccine or Tagliatelle

Directions:
Boil water in a large pan, add the asparagus whole and cook for some 5 minutes or so or until tender but not mushy;
Set aside to cool, reserving 120ml/4fl oz/8tbsp of the cooking liquid in a glass;
When cool enough to handle, cut asparagus into 2cm/¾in lengths;
Pour large quantity of water for the pasta in a large pan and place over a high heat;
Melt the butter in a large sauté pan, add the onion and cook over a medium heat, stirring occasionally, until softened and a rich golden colour;
Stir in the prosciutto and sauté until it has lost its’ raw look and colour;
Add the asparagus, raise heat to medium high and cook, stirring, until lightly coloured;
Pour in the reserved liquid and cook until evaporated;
Stir in the cream and cook until reduced by half;
Remove from the heat and set aside;
When pasta water is boiling and sauce is off the heat, add salt and drop of olive oil and add pasta, stirring constantly at first to ensure all the strands are separated;
When cooked to “al dente”, drain well and toss with the pasta and the cheese;
Serve immediately.

An Italian style flavour feast. Asparagus and Prosciutto in a rich creamy sauce creates a dish fit for any table or occasion
