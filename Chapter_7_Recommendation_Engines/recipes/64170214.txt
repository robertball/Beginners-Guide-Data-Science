Title:
Cauliflower Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups cauliflower florets
1/2-1 cup yellow banana peppers
1/2 cup onion or red onions
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
3 extra large eggs
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian salad dressing mix
(or use ranch)  Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup olive oil for frying, or as needed

Directions:
Process cauliflower, yellow peppers and onions in a food processor finely minced; transfer to a large bowl.
Stir flour, eggs, baking powder, and Italian dressing mix and Parmesan cheese into cauliflower.
Heat enough olive oil to cover the bottom of a frying pan over medium heat.
Drop heaping tablespoons of cauliflower mixture into the hot oil; fry until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.

Submitter: 
Colleen Robertson Curtiss
Description: 
These cauliflower fritters are easy to prepare and a different way to eat cauliflower. They are a great appetizer to serve your family and friends.Modified from: Cauliflower Fritters
