Title:
Deconstructed Screwdriver (The Raw Egg) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2183736.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup orange juice, or as desired
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers vodka, or to taste

Directions:
Fill a food-safe silicon-based round ice mold will orange juice; freeze until solid, 2 to 3 hours.
Carefully open the ice mold and place the orange juice ball into a martini glass; add vodka.

Submitter: 
Sabri Sansoy
Description: 
Frozen ball of orange juice bathed in vodka. I get a lot of comments that it looks like a raw egg!
