Title:
Pineapple Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/960353.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) block of cream cheese, softened
1 (8 1/4 oz.) can crushed pineapple, in heavy syrup, drained, with syrup reserved
1 tsp. sugar (optional, or to taste)
1/4 cup Sargento's Off the Block Mild Shredded Natural Cheddar Cheese, Fine Cut

Directions:
Using mixer, cream the softened cream cheese until fluffy.
Add drained crushed pineapple and sugar, if desired, and mix.
Add a little reserved syrup, if needed, to achieve spreading consistency.
Stir in shredded cheddar cheese.
Refrigerate 2 hours to meld flavors.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
I wanted to create a recipe that reminded me of my childhood when my mother would serve pineapple rings with grated cheese on top. This recipe is best served with a plain cracker like Club crackers, so that the flavor of the spread shines and not the flavor of the cracker.
