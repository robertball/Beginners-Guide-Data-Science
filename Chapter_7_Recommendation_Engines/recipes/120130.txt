Title:
Pesto Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/587745.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces uncooked angel hair pasta
6 tablespoons olive oil
2 (3 ounce) packages cream cheese, cubed
2 garlic cloves, minced
16 fresh basil leaves
2 plum tomatoes, chopped
3 tablespoons shredded mozzarella cheese
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, for pesto, combine the oil, cream cheese, garlic and basil in a blender or food processor; cover and process until smooth.
Drain the pasta and place in a serving bowl. Top with tomatoes and pesto; sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.

Submitter: 
Irene  Smoliga
Description: 
Add crusty bread and a salad to this easy pasta dish to make a complete meal in mere minutes. 'The creamy sauce with fresh tomatoes and basil is a pleasant change from traditional tomato-based sauces,' writes Irene Smoliga of Harrisburg, Pennsylvania.
