Title:
Crystal Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 oz. package lemon gelatin
1/2 cup pineapple juice (drained from can of crushed pineapple)
1/2 cup salad dressing (like Miracle Whip)
1/2 cup diced red apple with peel
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 cup crushed pineapple, drained
1 1/2 cup hot water (bring to boil)
1/2 cup whipped dessert topping (like Cool Whip)
1/2 cup mini-marshmallows

Directions:
In a large bowl, dissolve gelatin in hot water.  Add pineapple juice.  Refridgerate until slightly congealed.  Fold in remaining ingredients.  Transfer mixture to a mold or serving bowl. Chill until set, preferably over night.

Submitter: 
DONNA2002
Description: 
A delicious gelatin salad that goes with any Holiday meal.  Would even travel well if kept in a cooler with ice.
