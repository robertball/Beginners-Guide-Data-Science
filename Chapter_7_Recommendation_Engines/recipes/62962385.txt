Title:
Peanut Butter cookies w/ choclate star Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/746609.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup butter = 1 stick (softened)
1/2 Cup Peanut Butter
1/2 Cup Cane Sugar
1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
1 egg (room temp)
1 1/4 Cup all purpose flour
3 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
Choclate stars or Hershey bar squares

Directions:
In a medium bowl: mix butter, p-nut butter, sugar & eggs.
In a seperate bowl mix: flour, powder & soda. Then add to wet mixture.
***cover and chill 30 minutes to an hour
Shape into 1 inch balls, roll the ball in sugar and then bake @ 375 degrees for 5 to 8 minutes then place choclate star --- let cool completly.
