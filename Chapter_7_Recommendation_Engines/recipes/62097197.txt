Title:
Chicken and Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 or 3 nice size bunches of Broccoli
2 large packages of split Chicken Breasts with skin and bones
¾ C. milk
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 C. Hellmann’s Mayo
1 tsp. mild curry powder
1/8 tsp fresh black pepper
¼ tsp salt
1 (8oz) block of mild cheddar cheese, grated
6 Tbs. of butter or margarine (can be increased to one stick)
1 small package of Pepperidge Farm Herb Dressing

Directions:
Chop broccoli into bite size pieces, steam and set aside.
Cook chicken in a large pot until done. (About 35-45 minutes) Cool and cube into 1-inch pieces.
With a wire whip, combine soup, mayo, milk and spices and set aside.
Melt butter.
Add stuffing mix to melted butter and toss until covered.
ASSEMBLE IN ORDER IN A LARGE BAKING DISH
Broccoli
Chicken
Soup mixture
Cheddar cheese
Stuffing mix
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
Be careful not to burn dressing mix on top. It can be added later if you wish.
