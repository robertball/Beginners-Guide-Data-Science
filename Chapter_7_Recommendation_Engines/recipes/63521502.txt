Title:
Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/961538.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken, cooked and cubed
1 onion, chopped med/fine
2 Tbsp canola oil
1 clove	garlic, minced
2 cans White Beans, rinsed and drained
1 cans chicken broth
2 cans green chilies, chopped
1 tsp salt
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp	ground white pepper
1/4 tsp	chipotle seasoning
1 c sour cream
1/2 c whole milk

Directions:
In a saute pan, cook onions and garlic in oil until translucent, about 3 or 4 minutes.
In large stock pot, add cooked chicken, beans, chicken broth, green chilies, salt, oregano, cumin, white pepper, and chipotle seasoning.
Add onions and garlic to stock pot and heat on medium low heat for 10 minutes.
Turn to low and add sour cream and milk. mix well.
Serve warm wish crackers or homemade biscuits.
