Title:
Portabello Mushroom Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Portabello Mushrooms, big enough to fit a bun
4 slices of turkey bacon
1 small onion, thinly sliced
2 leaves of Romaine lettuce
2 tomato slices
2 T. horseradish sauce
2 Buns

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400^.  Remove stems from mushrooms, and wipe clean with a damp cloth.
2. Wrap 2 slices of turkey bacon around each mushroom cap and place on a baking sheet.  Bake for 15 minutes.
3. Meanwhile, heat a small non-stick pan to medium heat.  Add the onions and a touch of water and saute until onions are brown.  About 10 minutes.
4.  Take mushrooms out of the oven and drain on paper towel.  Spread horseradish sauce over each bun.  Place a tomato slice, a lettuce leaf, 1/2 the onions and the mushroom burger on the bun and top it off with the mushroom.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
Thick portabello mushrooms wrapped in turkey bacon and baked, and then topped off with carmelized onions, lettuce, tomato and horseradish sauce.  It is for sure a winner and a healthy alternative to fatty beef burgers.
