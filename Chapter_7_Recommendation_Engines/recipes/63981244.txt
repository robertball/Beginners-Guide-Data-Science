Title:
Fried Rice with Cilantro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 tablespoon sesame oil
5 cloves garlic, finely chopped
2 green chilies, diced
2 cups cooked jasmine rice, chilled
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 tablespoon fish sauce
1 tablespoon soy sauce (or amino acids)
2 teaspoons chopped green onion
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil leaves
5 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
2 cups cubed skinless, boneless chicken breast meat or shrimp(optional)

Directions:
Heat the oil in a wok or large skillet, over medium-high heat. Fry the garlic until golden, then add the chili pepper and chicken meat (if adding), and stir-fry until cooked through.
When the chicken is cooked, add the rice, sugar, fish sauce, and soy sauce. Cook over medium heat, stirring gently. When the mixture is well blended, stir in the green onions, basil, and cilantro. Cook for 1 more minute, then serve hot.

Submitter: 
Angela Olesen Seggie
Description: 
A delicious fried rice. You can use either chicken, pork, bacon or seafood - great with calamari and prawns.Modified from: Fried Rice with Cilantro
