Title:
Grandma's Pastry Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sifted all purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
2/3 cup shortening
5-6 Tablespoons cold water

Directions:
combine all dry ingredients. Add shortening and slowly add water. Knead dough until desired consistency. Divide dough into two equal pieces. On a floured surface roll dough with a rolling pan until large enough to fit the pie pan. Add desired filling and place the second crust on top. Poke holes on top and bake according to pie instructions.

Submitter: 
Danielle Kroll
Description: 
This is the best pie crust for any kind of pie. It comes out perfect every time.
