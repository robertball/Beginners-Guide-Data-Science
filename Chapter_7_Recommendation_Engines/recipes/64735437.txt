Title:
Eye of Round Beef Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pound beef eye of round roast
Salt/pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 500 degrees
Season meat and put in roasting pan
Place roast in preheated over; immediately reduce temp to 475 degrees
Roast for 20 minutes  (7 minutes per pound)
Turn off oven and let the roast sit for 2 1/2 hours
Do not open door
Remove from oven; intenal temperature of meat should be 145 degrees
