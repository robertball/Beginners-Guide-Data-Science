Title:
Pumpkin Pie Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2350757.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1 tsp. Dry ginger
1/2 tsp. Cinnamon
1 C. Evaporated Milk
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs, lightly beaten
1 1/2 C. Pumpkin puree
1 C. Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 12 cup muffin pan. set aside
In a small bowl blend flour, b.powder, b,soda, ginger and cinnamon until well combined
Mix together pumpkin puree, lightly beaten eggs, sugar and chocolate chips until light and creamy
Add dry ingredients slowly to wet mixture and stir until just combined
Pour small amounts of batter into cups filling 2/3 full  and bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes  until lightly browned
Remove from oven and  allow  to  cool for 5 minutes before removing from muffin cups
Place on a serving plate to cool completely before storing away in a container

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
Pumpkin pie in a muffin with chocolatey flavour  that makes them very delicious for dessert or a snack
