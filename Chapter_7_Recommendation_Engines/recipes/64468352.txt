Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Ala Stormtroopers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2452837.jpg
Ingredients:
Pretzel sticks, big & small marshmallows, white chocolate, Star Wars Marshmallows

Directions:
Melt white chocolate in the microwave at 30 second increments until melted.
Put marshmallows on the ends of your pretzel sicks (some small, some large)
Coat your "dinosaur bone" in white chocolate, place on waxed paper to cool.
