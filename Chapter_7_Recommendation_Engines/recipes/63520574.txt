Title:
Easy Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes, peeled and cut up
4 T milk
1 T butter
1/2 tsp seasoning salt
2 tsp cooking oil
1 - 1/2 lbs lean ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
1 T flour
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/3 milk
1 cup cooked peas
1 cup cooked sliced carrot
2 T ketchup
1 T worcestershire sauce
1 tsp prepared horseradish
and paprika to sprinkle on the top.

Directions:
Cook potatoes in boiling salted water, in a large saucepan until tender. Drain.
Add milk, butter, and seasoning salt, mash, and set aside.
Heat the cooking oil in a large frying pan on medium. Add beef and onion. Scramble-fry to about 10 minutes until beef is no loner pink. Drain.
Add salt, pepper, and second amount of milk.
Heat and stir for about 2 minutes until boiling and thickened.
Add the rest of the ingredients (minus the potatoes) to the beef mixture. Spread into a small baking dish and then spread the potatoes onto the beef mixture.
Top with paprika and bake in the oven at 350F for 30 minutes.
