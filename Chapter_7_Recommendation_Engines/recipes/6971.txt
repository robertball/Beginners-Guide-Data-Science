Title:
Honey Of An Oatmeal Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3934874.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/4 cup honey
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup rolled oats
2 1/3 cups bread flour
1 teaspoon active dry yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in bread machine pan in the order suggested by the manufacturer.
Select Light Crust or Basic setting, and press Start.

Submitter: 
Merrilee
Description: 
Our family goes through a loaf of this every day. Slightly sweet, and light. Great with dinner, and a favorite for sandwiches.
