Title:
Hamburger Ratatouille Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 # lean ground beef
2 med. onions, chopped
32 oz jar spaghetti sauce w/mushrooms (4C)
14 oz can tomatoes, cut up
2-15 oz cans red kidney and/or barbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
4 C peeled and cubed eggplant or cubed zucchini
4 oz can diced green chili peppers, drained
2-3 T chili powder
1 t ground cumin
6 slices American cheese, cut into triangles (opt.)
Chopped green onion (opt)

Directions:
In Dutch oven, cook beef and onion until beef is brown and onion is tender but not brown. Drain off fat. Stir in spaghetti sauce, undrained tomatoes, beans, chili powder and cumin. Bring to boil; reduce heat. Cover; and simmer 20 min. or until eggplant and/or zucchini is tender. Season w/salt and pepper.
ladle into bowls and top w/cheese and onion

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Can be made ahead and frozen. Made with eggplant or zucchini. Spaghetti sauce gives this chunky vegetable chili a head start.
