Title:
Vegan Ginger Pecan Cranberry  Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Spelt Flour
1/2 Cup Oat Flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup quick oats
1 Chia Seed Egg
1/2 cup agave
1/4 molasses
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1 Tablespoon Fresh Ginger
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 F
Combine Ginger, Chia Seed Egg, Agave, Molasses, Olive Oil Blend thoroughly
Combine all dry ingredients in bowl and mix together.
Add wet ingredients to dry.
Spoon out onto parchment paper or oiled cookie sheet.
Bake for 12-15 minutes

Submitter: 
ChrissyintheKitchen
Description: 
Fresh Ginger in these cookies makes the taste pop. Sweetened with agave and molasses, these cookies are a healthy treat.
