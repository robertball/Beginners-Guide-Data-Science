Title:
Pop Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  2.65 oz.package natural(no butter) microwave popcorn, popped
1/2 pound (lowfat) bacon fried and crumbled
1 small can sliced water chestnuts
1/2 cup chopped green top onions
8 oz. lowfat cheddar cheese
1/2 cup miracle whip (more or less if desired)

Directions:
In a large bowl combine all the ingredients together except the miracle whip.  Mix in the miracle whip just before serving.

Amy Brolsma

I love serving  this salad as a main summertime dish
