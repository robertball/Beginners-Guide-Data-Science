Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Lbs. Sweet Potatoes(cooked and mashed)
8 oz.can Crushed Pinapple
1 stick Butter(melted)
1 Cup Sugar
1/2 Cup Raisins
1 Tbl.Vanilla
Topping:
1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup Butter
1/2 Cup Flour
1/2 Cup Chopped Nuts

Directions:
Mix together first 6 ingedients and put into greased casserole dish.
Mix together Topping ingredients and put on top of sweet potato mixture.
Bake for 30min. at 350F.

Submitter: 
Mad Butcher
Description: 
This is a basic sweet potato recipe that we have been using for xmas dinner for many years.
