Title:
1- 2 Turkey Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices turkey breast
4 medium Boston lettuce leaves
Cilantro Mayonnaise
4 scallions
4 red bell pepper strips

Directions:
place one slice turkey on a lettuce leaf.  Spread with cilantro mayonnaise.  Add 1 scallion and 1 pepper strip.  fold into a tight cigar-like roll.
