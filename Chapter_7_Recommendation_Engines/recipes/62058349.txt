Title:
Puree of Pumpkin Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
2 large onions, chopped
2 leek, white part only, chopped
2 lbs canned pumpkin
8 cups chicken broth
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon curry powder
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
2 bay leaves
2 cups half-and-half.

Directions:
In large soup pot, melt butter.
Saute onion and leek until soft.  Stir in pumpkin, chicken broth, salt, spices, and bay leaves.  Bring to a boil.
Lower heat and simmer, uncovered, for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove bay leaves.
Puree the mixture in batches in food processor or blender for a smooth texture.
Return to soup pot.  Add half-and-half and cook over moderate heat, stirring occasionally, until heated through.
