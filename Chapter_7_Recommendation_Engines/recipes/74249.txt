Title:
After School Veggie Pita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986050.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Mann's Broccoli Cole Slaw
2/3 cup hummus (Middle Eastern chickpea dip), purchased or homemade
4 teaspoons jalapenos, fresh and finely chopped
2 pita breads (6 inches in diameter)
4 tomato slices, large

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together Mann's Broccoli Cole Slaw, hummus, and jalapeno peppers. Using kitchen scissors or a sharp knife, cut pita breads in half. Fill each pita half with 1 or 2 tomato slices and 1/2 cup of the broccoli slaw-hummus mixture.

Submitter: 
Mann's Fresh Vegetables
Description: 
Quick and easy to prepare, these hummus and broccoli-filled pita pockets make for a great lunch, or even a light dinner, any day of the week.
