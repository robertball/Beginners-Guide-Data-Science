Title:
Substitute for Whipping Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6776608.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter, melted
3/4 cup whole milk, slightly warmed.

Directions:
Stir melted butter and milk together until combined. The warmth of the milk helps the butter to re-unite with the fat solids in the milk.
