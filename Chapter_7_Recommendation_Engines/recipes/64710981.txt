Title:
Ham and Gruyere Stuffed Breakfast Croissants Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5588286.jpg
Ingredients:
12oz Package Grands Crescent Rolls
8 tbsp Shredded Gruyere Cheese
8oz Ham Steak (Chopped )

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Chop ham into 1/2" pieces.
Unroll crescent dough and separate individual rolls at perforations.
Place 1 oz of ham and 1 tbsp of Gruyere in center of dough.
Fold the two longer sides of dough triangles overlapping each other to seal ham and cheese.
Arrange on a baking sheet and space 1" apart.
Bake in preheated oven for 12 to 14 minutes until golden brown.
Let cool completely on cooling rack.
Wrap individually in wax paper then place into large resealable bag and freeze.
To reheat, take out night before and place in refrigerator to thaw.
Reheat in microwave for 45 seconds at 40% power or until desired temperature.

Submitter: 
B. Tario
Description: 
A quick grab and go breakfast time saver and a great use of leftover ham.  This recipe can make a weeks worth of meals that can be frozen and reheated on medium to low power (40% power) for approximately 45 seconds.
