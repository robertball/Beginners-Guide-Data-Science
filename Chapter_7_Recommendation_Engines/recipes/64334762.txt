Title:
Banana Bunt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2062978.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/3 cup liquid egg whites (or 2 large eggs)
3 large bananas, mashed
1/4 cup skim milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup flour
2-3 tablespoons of chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Grease & flour a 9 by 13-inch metal baking pan
Cream butter & sugar in a large bowl. Mix in eggs & banana.
Add milk & vanilla, blending well.
Sift flour & baking soda. Add to banana mixture.
Pour into prepared pan. Sprinkle chocolate chips on top.
Bake 50 to 55 minutes
