Title:
Chicken Noodle Kale Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1508331.jpg
Ingredients:
4-5 Chicken thighs (skin on)
2 tablespoons minced garlic
1/2 small onion, diced
5-6 carrots, sliced
2 cans Campbells Cream of Chicken Soup
2 cups Milk
5 tablespoons Cornstarch dissolved in hot water
6 chicken bouillion cubes
7-8 Cups hot water
2-3 bunches of Fresh Kale, ripped into bite sized pieces (discard spine)
Chicken seasoning
Egg Noodles
Margarine or Butter

Directions:
1. Boil 4-5 chicken thighs in a pot of hot water with chicken bouillon cubes.
2. Sautee fresh garlic, onion and kale in a skillet with margarine or real butter.
3. Take chicken out of the pot when its done cooking, and shred the chicken.
4. Take garlic, onion and kale mix and add to chicken broth in pot along with carrots and shredded chicken.
5. Add 2 family size cans of Cream of Chicken soup and about 1/4th of a container of chicken seasoning.
6. In a glass, mix about 5 tablespoons of cornstarch with hot water and add to pot. Add 2 cups Milk to make soup creamy.
7. Cook an hour and serve over noodles.
8. In a separate pot, boil egg noodles for 11 min, drain, add to chicken soup.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
The most amazing homemade chicken noodle soup.  I kinda just started throwing things together one day and it turned out amazing.  My children ate all of this within a week!!  It freezes well also!
