Title:
Gretchen's Cinnamon Chocolate Chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1   cup brown sugar
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup shortening
4   Tbl butter
1/4 cup cinnamon applesauce
2   eggs
1   tsp vanilla
2 1/2 cups flour
2   tsp cinnamon
3/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven at 370 degrees.  Combine sugars, butter, shortening, and applesauce in a large bowl.  Beat on high until combined thoroughly.  Add eggs and vanilla.  In a seperate bowl, combine cinnamon, baking soda, salt, and flour.  Add as much of that combination in the beater as you can.  If you can't add any more, use a wooden spoon.  Stir in mini choc. chips.  Use a teaspoon to form balls on a cookie sheet.  Bake at 370 degrees for 10 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.  Let cool.

Description: 
This is my 6th grade daughter's own creative invention.  They turned out great! A soft choc. chip cookie with a touch of cinnamon.
