Title:
Julian's Cheese Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped onions
1 cup monterey jack cheese, grated
1 cup colby cheese, grated
1 cup cheddar cheese, grated
salt
1 dozen corn tortillas
2 TBSP + 1 cup oil
1 small can tomato sauce
3 TBSP chili powder

Directions:
Combine cheese, onions, and salt.  Set aside.  Mix 1/2 of the tomato sauce, 2 TBSP of the chili powder, and 2 TBSP oil.  Simmer in shallow skillet.*  Heat remaining oil in in another skillet.
Dip corn tortillas (one at a time) in hot oil and immediately dip in tomato sauce mixture, turning once to coat both sides; remove to plate.  Put some of cheese mixture on tortilla and roll. Place in baking pan (spray with cooking spray first) and repeat until all torilllas are rolled.
Top with any remaining cheese and tomato sauce mixture.  Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes.
*When sauce mixtures begins to run out, add remaining tomato sauce and chili powder.  Also add a little water if necessary.
