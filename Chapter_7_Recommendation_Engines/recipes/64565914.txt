Title:
Mexi-Chili-Mac (gluten free option) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound organic ground turkey (not extra lean) may use beef
1 medium onion chopped
4 cloves garlic fine minced, about 1 tablespoon
2 teaspoons chili powder
pepper, cayenne, cumin to taste (go easy unless you like it hot)
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon dried cilantro (can use fresh, add last to dish before serving)
1 15 ounce can tomato sauce
1 15 ounce can of pinto, kidney or black beans, drained & rinsed
1 15 ounce jar mild chunky salsa or picante of choice (spicy hot salsa if desired)
2 cups low sodium beef or chicken broth
2 cups uncooked gluten free macaroni of choice (corn, brown rice, quinoa) may use regular macaroni as desired
toppings optional: shredded cheddar, pepper jack, sour cream, green onions fresh cilantro

Directions:
Brown meat with onions and garlic.  Add tomato sauce, salsa, seasonings, add Worcestershire sauce, broth, and pasta.  Cook on medium low (simmering) with lid on.  Stirring frequently until much of liquid becomes absorbed.

Submitter: 
gem bee
Description: 
Simply done, fast, perfect with immediate satisfaction for the pasta hankering.  Make this dish as HOT or mild as you like.
