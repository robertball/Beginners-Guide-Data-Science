Title:
Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Sour cream
1.5 cups Mayonnaise
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp Garlic powder
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Black pepper
4 oz Blue cheese; crumbled

Directions:
Whisk together all ingredients. Put in a covered container and refrigerate - give it several hours to let the flavors mingle. Canning jars make excellent containers.

Submitter: 
Nesto
Description: 
Make you own blue cheese dressing! It's simple and so much better than store bought.
