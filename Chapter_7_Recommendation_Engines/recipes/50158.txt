Title:
Raspberry Tea Sparkler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986861.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Apple Flavor LIBBY'S® JUICY JUICE® 100% Juice
2 cups PERRIER® Sparkling Water
1/2 cup Raspberry Flavor NESTEA® Sweetened Iced Tea Concentrate
3 cups ice cubes
Fresh mint leaves (optional)

Directions:
PLACE Juicy Juice, sparkling water and Nestea in large pitcher. Stir until combined. Serve over ice. Garnish with mint.

Submitter: 
Nestea®
Description: 
This sparkling tea with raspberry and apple flavors is sure to be a hit at your next outdoor party.
