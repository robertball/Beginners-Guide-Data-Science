Title:
Nenni's Italian Pork Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/285216.jpg
Ingredients:
9 lbs pork shoulder
3 tablespoons garlic powder
4 tablespoons fennel seed
2 tablespoons crushed red pepper
4 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons black pepper
2 tablespoons parsley
1/2 cup white wine
pork casings

Directions:
Cut up and grind pork shoulder. Add all seasonings and mix well by hand. Stuff pork casings with sausage and put in quart freezer bags. Keep in refrigerator overnight and then transfer to freezer. Fry in skillet as needed.

Submitter: 
Paul Nenni
Description: 
This is an old family recipe passed down from my grandmother to my father in the 1950's. It has been made countless times by my family.
