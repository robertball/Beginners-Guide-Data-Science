Title:
Pineapple Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Flouor
1 Cup Confectioners Sugar
1 Cup Melted Butter
1 Can (20 ounces) Crushed Pineapple and Juice
1 Cup Sugar
3 Tablespoon Cornstarch
1/2 Cup Waater
1 teaspoon Almond Extract
1/4 Cup Melted Butter
1/2 Cup Brown Sugar (packed)
1 Cup Flour
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/4 teaspoon Baking Soda
3/4 Cup Rolled Oats

Directions:
Place pineapple, 1 cup white sugar, cornstarch, water and almond extract in a sauce pan.  Mix thoroughly.  Bring to a boil over medium heat; stiring constantly.  When thickened, remove from heat and cool.
Using a medium sized bowl; mix 2 Cups flour, confectioners sugar, and 1 cup melted butter.  When dough forms, press into a 13 x 9 inch prepared baking pan.  Press dough so that ir comes up the sides of the pan a bit.  Bake in a 350 oven for 15 minutes.  Remove; spread pineapple filling over crust.
In the same medium bowl used for the crust, mix 1/4 cup melted butter, brown sugar, 1 cup flour, salt, baking soda and rolled oats.  Mix by hand until it forms a fine crumb mixture.  Evenly sprinkle over the top of pineapple filling, patting down lightly.  Return to 350 oven and bake an additional 25 minutes.  Cool completely before cutting into bars.
