Title:
Cajun Paella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large boneless chicken breast, cut into large pieces
2 Andouille sausage links, sliced
4 large grilling prawns, peeled and deveined, and cut in roughly quarters
1 lobster tail, cut into thumb-sized pieces
3 T olive oil
1 large shallot
4 garlic cloves
1/3 of a bunch of flat-leaf parsley, major stems removed
1 15 oz can fire-roasted whole tomatoes, drained and crushed
1/2 red pepper, diced
2 c rice
4 c chicken stock
generous pinch of saffron
1 t hot sauce
1 T Worcestershire sauce
2 T Creole seasoning
2 bay leaves
1/2 c frozen peas, thawed
SPICE MIX FOR CHICKEN
2 t sweet paprika
1 t dried oregano
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Rub spice mix over chicken pieces and let sit in refrigerator for 1 hour.
Heat oil in large frying pan.
Add chicken pieces and brown on all sides.
Remove from pan and reserve.
In the same pan, sautee shallot, garlic and parsley for 2-3 minutes on medium heat.
Add tomatoes and red pepper and let carmelize a bit.
Fold in the rice and stir to coat the grains.
Pour in chicken stock, Worcestershire, hot sauce, bay leaves and creole seasoning and bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
Reduce heat to medium, cover and cook 5 minutes.
Add 1 c water, sausage, chicken and saffron, cover and cook 5 minutes.
Add a generous shake of salt and pepper, cover and cook three minutes.
Nestle shrimp into mixture, cover and cook four minutes.
Nestle lobster into mixture and sprinkle in peas, cover and cook 4 minutes or until lobster is opaque all the way through.
