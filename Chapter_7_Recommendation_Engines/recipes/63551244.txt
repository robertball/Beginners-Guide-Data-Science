Title:
Cajeta (Dulce de Leche) Arroz con Leche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cinnamon sticks
1 tablespoon vanilla extract or a vanilla pod sliced in half
1 can (14 oz) condensed milk
1 cup cajeta (goat milk spread, caramel)
1 1/2 cup whole milk
1/2 cup raisins
3 cups cooked white rice
4 oz peanut brittle

Directions:
Add cinnamon sticks, vanilla, condensed milk, cajeta, milk, and raisins into a slow cooker and set on low. Mix and allow warming for 30 minutes.
Then add in cooked rice. Mix it well and continue to warm on low for an additional 2 hours. Stir every 30 minutes or so.
