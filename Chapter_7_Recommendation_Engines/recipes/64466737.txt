Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2443540.jpg
Ingredients:
Ingredients
2 egg whites
5 cups sugar
2 cups fresh kale
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cream of tartar
dash of meat tenderizer
Skin of locally sourced dinosaur smaller than yourself for garnish (if extinct, substitute with hot dog)

Directions:
Beat 2 egg whites with whisk until firm. (Note: Those with bug-like limbs may need to use blender for mixing.)
Boil water, sugar, salt, kale and cream of tartar. Hold 2 tbsp kale as garnish.
Stir into whites and beat until stiff peaks form
Sprinkle with meat tenderizer and remaining kale.
Let sit for 5 minutes.
Cut spirals of dinosaur and place on top of peaks.
