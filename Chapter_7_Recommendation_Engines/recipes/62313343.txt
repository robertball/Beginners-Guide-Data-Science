Title:
Agua Fresca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/373636.jpg
Ingredients:
3 or 4 slices of Cantelope (you can use ANY fruit, melons are the best)
1 1/2 Cups water
6 Tablespoons of granulated sugar

Directions:
In a blender add cantelope (or whichever fruit you decide), water, and sugar. Blend until smooth consistency or until most of the fruit is pureed. Serve over ice and enjoy this fruity drink!

Submitter: 
lkeene
Description: 
A nice fruity drink served over ice for a delicious cool down on a nice day.
