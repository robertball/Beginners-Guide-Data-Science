Title:
Harvela's Tender Chunky Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T oil
2 lbs beef stew meat, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
2 cups chopped white onion
3 garlic cloves, crushed
1 28 oz can diced tomaotes, undrained
1 15.5 oz can red beans, rinsed drained
1 15.5 oz can dark kidney beans,                         rinsed/drained
3 6oz cans tomato paste
2 cups water
1/2 cup dry red wine (water is fine if you don't want alcohol)
4 Tbs chili powder
2T cumin
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (1/2 tsp if you dare!)
2 bay leaves

Directions:
Heat oil in stock/soup pan.  Add beef, onions, and garlic
Cook 5-8 minutes or until beef is lightly browned.
Stir in remaining chili ingredients.
Cover and cook 3 hours, or until beef is tender.  STIR FREQUENTLY.
Remove bay leaves.
Serve.
Some great toppings are cheese, green onions, sour cream, frito chips, etc.

Submitter: 
chowsito
Description: 
This is a melt in your mouth chili, made with beef stew meat.  Its great alone, or served over pasta, a potato or rice!  Absolutely incredible!  Thanks to my Dad, Harvela for passing it along!
