Title:
Simple Beef stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs beef cubes for stew
1/3 cup flour
garlic salt
1/2 cup red wine
1 can v-8
1 bay leaf
1 tablespoon worcsteshire sauce
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 onion chopped
3 cloves garlic chopped

Directions:
Mix flour and garlic salt in large ziploc bag. Add meat and shake to coat with flour. Heat olive oil in saut√© pan and add meat, browning for a couple minutes. Will probably have to do in batches to prevent overcrowding.
After meat is done, deglaze with wine.
Add meat, deglazed wine and remaining ingredients to crockpot.
Cook on high for 4-6 hours or low 8-10 hours.
