Title:
Pasta and Vegetable Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984119.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package BUITONI® Refrigerated Three Cheese Tortellini, prepared according to package directions
1 (15 ounce) container BUITONI® Refrigerated Marinara Sauce
1 3/4 cups water
1 teaspoon MAGGI Reduced Sodium Chicken Flavor Instant Bouillon
1 (16 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables

Directions:
Combine sauce, water and bouillon in large saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Add vegetables; return to a boil. Reduce heat to low; cover.
Cook for 10 to 12 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Stir in prepared pasta; heat through.

Submitter: 
Buitoni, courtesy of meals.com
Description: 
Cheese tortellini and mixed vegetables in a chicken and tomato-based broth make a quick and hearty stew on busy nights. Sprinkle each serving with Parmesan cheese for extra calcium.
