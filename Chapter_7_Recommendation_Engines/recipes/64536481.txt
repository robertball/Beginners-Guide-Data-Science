Title:
Pumpkin Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 16 ounces room temperature cream cheese
* 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
* 3 Tablespoons minced onion
* 2 Tablespoons salsa
* 2 teaspoons ground cumin
* 1 teaspoon minced jalapeno
* Crushed nacho flavored tortilla chips
* Bell pepper stem for the top of the pumpkin shaped cheese ball

Directions:
1) Mix the cream cheese, cheddar cheese, minced onion, salsa, ground cumin, and minced jalapeno together.
2) Scoop the mixture onto plastic wrap and use the wrap to form the mixture into a 5-inch pumpkin-shaped ball; chill at least 2 hours.
3) To serve, unwrap, roll in crushed nacho flavored tortilla chips and press a bell pepper stem into the top.
