Title:
Tamale Pie with Cheese Cornmeal Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 Tb olive oil
1/4 tsp. dark sesame oil
1 large sweet onion, chopped
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 4-oz can chopped chilies
1 14-oz can whole kernel corn, drained
2 14-oz cans stewed tomatoes, chopped in blender
1/2 tsp salt
1 Tb chili powder
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
1 cup water
Topping:
1 1/2 cup water
1/2 cup powdered skim milk
1 tsp. salt
2 Tb butter
1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
1 cup shredded mexi-cheese (cheddar will do)
2 eggs, beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  In large skillet, brown onion in oils, then add beef & brown.  Pour off excess grease.  Add vegetables & seasonings & simmer for 5 minutes.  Mix the 1/2 cup cornmeal and 1 cup water, stir into skillet, cover & cook 10 minutes over low heat.  Pour into greased 2 1/2 quart casserole dish or 9 x 13" baking pan.
Topping: Heat water, powdered milk, salt & butter. Gradually stir in the 1/2 cup cornmeal & cook, stirring, until thickened.  Remove from heat; stir in chees & beaten eggs.  Pour around the outside edge of casserole. Bake in preheated 375 degree oven 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Yomama
Description: 
This is the best Tamale Pie recipe I've ever tasted, and is a favorite main dish for potlucks! Two things make it special - the cornmeal in the sauce makes it a little thicker than average, and the cheese in the topping gives it an amazing texture.
