Title:
Fluffy Marshmallow Cream Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp hot water
1/4 tsp salt
1 jar (7 oz) marshmallow cream
1/2 c shortening
1/3 c confectioners' sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine water and salt until salt is dissolved.  Cool.
In a separate bowl, beat the marshmallow creme, shortening, confectioners' sugar and vanilla until light and fluffy.
Beat in the salt mixture.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Nolan
Description: 
Light and fluffy in texture, this recipe yields enough filling for 2 dozen cupcakes.
