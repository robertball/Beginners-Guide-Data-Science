Title:
Awesome Zesty Steamed Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs large shrimp
1cup of water
12oz beer
4oz whiskey
2 jalapenos minced
3tb Old Bay Seasoning

Directions:
Combine water, beer and whiskey in a large pot and bring to boiled
place shrimp and jalapenos in steamer basket and top with old bay then cover
steam 10 mins or until pink
