Title:
Carrot Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter
2 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp maple flavoring
2 1/4 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
½ tsp baking soda
½ tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup finely chopped walnuts
½ cup milk
1 cup finely grated carrots

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar.  Beat in eggs one at a time.  Mix in vanilla and maple flavoring.  In a separate bowl mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, salt and walnuts.  Add 1/3 of the flour mixture into the butter mixture until combined (about 1 minute).  Then add half the milk and mix until combined.  Then add 1/2 the remaining flour mixture and mix until combined.  Then add the rest of the milk and mix until combined.  Then add the rest of the flour mixture until combined.   Fold in carrots.  Scoop into the cupcake liners and bake at 145 C for 15-17 minutes.  Cool and top with favorite cream cheese frosting

Submitter: 
Bobbie Susan
Description: 
My family doesn't really like carrot cake but I love it.  So, I experimented with my own recipe and found by grinding the walnuts up really fine made all the difference.  Now, we all can enjoy them.
