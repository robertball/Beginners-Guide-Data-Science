Title:
Linguine with Red Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981723.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package frozen littleneck clams
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano leaves, crushed
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 (24 ounce) jar PregoÂ® Veggie Smart Smooth & Simple Italian Sauce
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1/2 (16 ounce) package linguine, cooked and drained

Directions:
Heat the clams, garlic, oregano, red pepper, vinegar and sauce in a 4-quart saucepan over medium-high heat to a boil.  Reduce the heat to low.  Cook for 5 minutes or until the clams are hot.  Stir in the parsley.  Add the linguine and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Why go out when it's so easy to make this restaurant favorite right in your own kitchen? Frozen clams and a good jarred sauce make it simple and tasty.
