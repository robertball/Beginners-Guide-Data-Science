Title:
Baked Ranch Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breast
1 cup Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing (original)
2  cups bread crumbs
2 tablespoons cooking oil

Directions:
1. put the Hidden Valley original Ranch Dressing and the Bread crumbs into separate med sized bowels .
2. spread the cooking oil on a cookie sheet to coat and set aside.
3.take the boneless chicken ( i place mine in a grocery bag and beat with a meat cleaver) roll in the ranch dressing and then the bread crumbs. place on the prepared cookie sheet.
4.place in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.
5. let stand for 5 minutes.
