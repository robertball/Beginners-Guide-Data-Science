Title:
Rotini Ranch Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 ounce package of tri-color rotini
1 package of Hidden Vally Ranch dry buttermilk dressing
1 carrot, sliced
1 celery, sliced
1/4 c. broccoli broken or cut into small pieces
1/4 c. cauliflower broken or cut into small pieces
1/3 c. olive or grapeseed oil

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions, drain and set aside.
Prepare vegetables.
Add ranch mix to the oil.
In a large bowl mix the pasta with the oil and veggies.
Stir well to coat
Refrigerate 4 hours before seving to let flavors meld.

Submitter: 
Sandy Schoon
Description: 
This pasta salad is always a hit.  It is colorful and easy too!
