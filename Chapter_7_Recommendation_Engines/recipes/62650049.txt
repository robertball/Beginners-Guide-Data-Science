Title:
Marble Mocha Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Packages of Cream Cheese
1 Can of Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 tsp Vanilla Extract
2 Eggs
1 heaping tbls Instant Coffee
1/4 cup Melted Butter
1 tbls Cocoa
1 cup Graham Cracker Crumbs
2 tbls Brown Sugar

Directions:
Mix melted butter, cocoa, and sugar. Slowly incorporate graham cracker crumbs. Press the mixture into a spring form pan or into paper cup lined muffin tins. place in the refrigerator to cool.
With an electric mixer, blend softened cream cheese, sweet milk, vanilla and eggs until smooth and creamy. Mix coffee with about 1/4 cup of water or desired amount into a separate cup. Pour the coffee around the cheesecake mix. Only stir it a little bit, so as to get the marble effect.
Scoop the cheesecake batter into the muffin tins or pour directly into the spring pan. Bake for about 20-30 minutes, depending on the pan. Use a knife to test when it is done. If the knife comes out clean, it is ready.
Place in the refrigerator for about an hour or until cooled all the way.
