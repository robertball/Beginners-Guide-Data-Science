Title:
Pot Roast & Vegetables 2013 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
flour
garlic powder
Salt & Pepper
4 lbs boneless chuck roast
4 cellery stalks - diced
1 onion - large - chopped
2 potatoes - large - quartered
1 parsnip - quaratered
4 carrots - halved
2 Tbsp horseradish
4 garlic cloves - minced
8 oz Merlot
8 oz cream

Directions:
Cover roast in mixture of flour, garlic powder, salt & pepper.
Heat a little oil in an iron skillet on an outside grill burner on high (dredging smokes a lot).
Sear each side 3-5 minutes until crust is brown.
While roast is searing, add celery & onion to crock pot.
After roast is browned add to pot.
Deglaze pan w/ Merlot until slightly thick, add cream, garlic & horseradish & add to pot.
Add rest of vegies (potato, carrot, parsnip. Optional - roast vegies first
Cover & cook on high 2 hrs, then low 6 hrs.
Optional - mix cornstarch and cold water together and slowly mix into gravy to thicken.
