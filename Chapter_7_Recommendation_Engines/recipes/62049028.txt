Title:
Sara K's Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients:
Ingredients are included in the directions....

Directions:
Cream together 1 stick butter (softened) with 1 cup sugar.  Add 4 eggs, 1 cup flour, 1 tsp. baking powder, a pinch of salt, 1 can (16 oz.) Hershey's syrup & 1 tsp. vanilla; mix well.  Pour into a 13x9" pan & bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.
FROSTING
Boil 1/2 stick butter with 1/3 cup evaporated milk (no substitute!) and 1 cup sugar for 2 minutes over medium-low heat.  Add 1/2 cup EACH chocolate chips & coconut.  Continue stirring until chocolate chips are completely melted.
Pour frosting over cake as soon as it comes out of the oven; spread frosting evenly to all sides.
Let cake cool completely before cutting.
