Title:
Chinese Noodle Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  Pkg. (12 0z.) Butterscotch chips
1  3 oz. can Chinese Noodles
1  Cup Salted Peanuts

Directions:
Melt butterscotch chips in double boiler.  Stir in noodles and peanuts.  Drop by heaping teaspoon onto wax paper.  Let cool completely until hardened.  You can store these in plastic containers or freezer bags in the freezer.

Submitter: 
CAROL46
Description: 
Chinese Noodle Candy is a butterscotch treat you will love.  No one can eat just one. I always make them at Christmas time and keep them in the freezer.  They are a great dessert when unexpected guests drop by.
