Title:
Special Chocolate Fudge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T. Cocoa
1 stick margarine
1 C. water
2. C flour
2 C. Sugar
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 eggs
1/2 C. buttermilk
1 tsp. vanilla
Icing:
6 T. Cocoa
1 stick margarine
6 T. buttermilk
1 lb. powdered sugar
1 T. vanilla

Directions:
Bring to a boil cocoa, margarine, and water.  Mix flour, sugar, soda and salt.  Add cooled cocoa mixture to dry ingredients.  One mixed add eggs, buttermilk, and vanilla.  Pour into greased pad.  Bake at 375 for 20 minutes.
Icing:
Bring to a boil cocoa, margarine, and buttermilk.  Add powdered sugar and vanilla.  Pour over hot cake.
