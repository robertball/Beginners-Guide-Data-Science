Title:
Turkey Club Melt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices bread
16 slices american cheese
8 slices tomato(seeded drain on paper towels)
8 slices turkey breast
thousand island dressing
8 slices bacon cooked drain on paper towels
4 tablespoons butter softened

Directions:
Lay out 4 slices of bread. Spread a little thousand island dressing on bread slices. Place 2 slices cheese, 2 slices tomato,2 slices of turkey, 2slices bacon, 2 slices of cheese. Spread a little more dressing on remaining bread slices.Cover each sanwich.Lightly butter top of sandwich with butter. Place butter side down in heated nonstick skillet. Top with plate let cook till golden brown about 5 minutes. Butter other side of sandwich flip over and again top with plate cook 5 minutes till browned

Submitter: 
MOLSON7
Description: 
Nice change from a simple grilled cheese. Serve with soup or salad.
