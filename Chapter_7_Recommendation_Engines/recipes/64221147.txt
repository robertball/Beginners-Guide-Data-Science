Title:
Cherry Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese
1/3 c. lemon juice
14 oz. sweetened conensed milk
1 tp. vanilla
1 graham cracker pie crust (large)
20 oz. cherry pie filling

Directions:
1.  Bring cream cheese to room temperature.
2.  Combine ingredients and blend until smooth.
3.  Pour into graham cracker crust.
4.  Refrigerate until set.
5.  Top wth 20 oz. can of cherry pie filling.
