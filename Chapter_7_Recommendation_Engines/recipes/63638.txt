Title:
Polenta with Bacon and Sage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup coarsely chopped bacon
1 tablespoon chopped fresh sage
2 tablespoons shallots, minced
1 (10 ounce) package frozen corn kernels, thawed
5 1/2 cups water
1 cup polenta
1/2 teaspoon Savory Marketâ„¢ Crispy Bacon Flavor
1/2 cup half-and-half
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook bacon in heavy large skillet over medium heat until crisp. Using slotted spoon, transfer bacon to paper towels to drain. Pour off all but 1 1/2 teaspoons fat from skillet. Add sage, corn and shallots and saute. Add the water and increase the heat to medium-high and bring to boil. Gradually whisk in polenta. Reduce heat to low; cook until polenta begins to thicken, stirring frequently, about 20 minutes. Stir in the half & half, cheese, and bacon. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
