Title:
Coconut Macaroons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 egg whites
1 c sugar
1 tbsp cornstarch
1 pinch cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt
2 c flaked coconut
2 drops bottled almond flavoring
small oblaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Line cookie sheet with parchment paper.
In a bowl whisk together cornstarch, cinnamon, salt; set aside.
Beat egg whites until stiff; then whisk in gradually the cornstarch mixture, and almond flavoring.
Gently fold in the coconut (do not beat or stir).
Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper. Place teaspoonfuls on the oblaten on the cookie sheets.
Bake 18-20 minutes, or until lightly browned. Cool on wire rack. Can be kept for a week in a container closed tightly.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
Coconut Macaroons have a wonderfully sweet coconut flavor with a crisp exterior and a soft and chewy texture. This is a recipe my mother would use from her german cookbook. Its a great way to use up leftover egg whites.
