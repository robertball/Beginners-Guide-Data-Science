Title:
Georges Restaurant Rotisserie Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chickens
1 cup of Bone dust meat rub
maybe 2 litres of buttermilk, made from powder
1 cup of Woo hoo bbq sauce

Directions:
wash the chickens and let dry
on a large tray, rub the meat rub over and inside the chickens
let sit for maybe a half hour
put chickens in plastic bag and pour butter milk in and seal
put in fridge for 24 hours or 2 hours or 4 hours or whatever you want
remove chickens from buttermilk and let sit
preheat BBQ, 2 outside burners at 350, and the rotisserie burner at 375
take BBQ rotisserie assembly and spray with oil
tie chicken legs and wings to hold close to body
assemble both on rack and place in the Q
put pie plates under each to gather juices
cook for about 75 minutes, bushing juices on each
when temp is about 160, remove from rack and tent with aluminum foil

Submitter: 
George. P. Cooper
Description: 
Rosie doesn't want the boneless skinless breasts anymore. The Rotisserie chicken gives you a meal, then maybe chicken sandwiches, and, for sure, chicken soup
