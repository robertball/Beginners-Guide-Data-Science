Title:
Strawberry-Rhubarb Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups sliced rhubarb (about 1 1/2 pounds)
2/3 cup granulated sugar
1 pkg. (3oz) strawberry flavored gelatin
1 pkg. (9oz) yellow cake mix (such as Jiffy)
3/4 cup water
1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
2 tsp confectioners sugar, for garnish
Whipped topping, optional

Directions:
1) Heat oven to 350 degrees. Coat an 11x7x2 inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
2) Place rhubarb in a prepared baking dish and sprinkle with granulated sugar. Then sprinkle with gelatin. Sprinkle the cake mix over the gelatin and then drizzle 3/4 cup of water. Drizzle the melted butter on top.
3) Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes. Remove from oven and cool on a wire rack for 15 minutes.
4) Serve warm or at room temperature. Dust with confectioners sugar and serve with whipped topping, if desired.
