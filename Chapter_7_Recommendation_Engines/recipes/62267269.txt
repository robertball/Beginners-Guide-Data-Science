Title:
Slow Cooker Dumplin and Go Cheesy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (11 ounce) can cream of Cheddar cheese soup
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
2 Tbsp dried parsley
1/2 tsp Mrs. Dash
1/2 tsp pepper or to taste
Dumplings:
1 cup flour
1 tsp. baking soda
dash of salt
dash of sugar
enough water to make sticky.

Directions:
Spray slow cooker with cooking spray. Place chicken breasts inside. In a medium bowl mix together soup and milk, and pour mixture over chicken. Season with pepper to taste and garlic powder, italian seasoning, parsley and Mrs Dash.
Cook on High for about 4 hours then turn to low for remaining two hours. Note: Do not lift lid while cooking!
45 minutes before done drop dumplings into mixture.

Submitter: 
Julie Hansen Ostler
Description: 
Cheddar soup and chicken--dump and go, but do not be late for dinner.Modified from: Slow Cooker Dump and Go Cheesy Chicken
