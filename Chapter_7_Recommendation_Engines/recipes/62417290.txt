Title:
Easy, Delicious, Eggplant and Rigatoni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/442347.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggplants (about 6" long and 4" around)
1 large onion chopped
2 cloves garlic chopped
2 1/2 cups fresh tomatoes chopped
1 yellow squash about (6" long and 3" around)
1 tsp salt
2 Tbl Light Olive Oil
1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
1/2 pound Rigatoni Pasta

Directions:
Peel Eggplant, and cut into cubes about 1/2 inch big. Place in large skillet with oilve oil, onion, salt and garlic. Cook uncovered about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add the chopped tomatoes and the unpeeled squash cut into cubes like the eggplant, to the skillet. Add the basil and simmer covered for about 10-15 minutes.
Layer 1/2 of the cooked rigatoni in bowl and add 1/2 the eggplant mixture, repeat with second half of pasta and eggplant mixture. Sprinkle top with Romano or Parmesan Cheese.
