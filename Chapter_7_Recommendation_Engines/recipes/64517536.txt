Title:
Homemade - Cream of Cauliflower and Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of cauliflower, chopped
1.5 lb  potatoes, cubed
1 medium onion
1.5 garlic clove, chopped
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
6 cups tap water
1.5 tsp thyme
1.5 tsp sage
1 tsp oregano
2 bay leaves
3 tsp black pepper
1 tsp sea salt
10 tsp non dairy coffee whitener
1.5 cups 1% milk
3 tbsp cornstarch

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic in large soup pot about 2 minutes, then add potatoes and cauliflower then saute another 5-7 minutes, stirring constantly
Add water and spices and bring to boil, boil about 20 minutes then turn to simmer about 45-60 minutes.
add coffee whitener, stir cornstarch into cold milk then add to soup then allow to thicken some with a hand blender puree the soup leaving a few lumps, simmer another 20 to 30 minutes stirring

Submitter: 
Tony Carriere
Description: 
a Low fat cream soup, using low fat coffee whitener to give the creamy taste without fat you may add 1.5 cups of cream if you wish
