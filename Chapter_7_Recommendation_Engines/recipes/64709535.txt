Title:
Spicy Cucumber Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5548357.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cucumbers, peeled, seeded, and finely diced
2 cans Original Rotels
1 pkg Fiesta Ranch Dressing Mix
1/4 c. Cider Vinegar
3 Tbsp. Fresh Cilantro, chopped (or to taste)
Tortilla Scoops

Directions:
Mix cucumbers, Rotels, Ranch dressing, vinegar, and cilantro together.  Refrigerate at least one hour.  Serve with Tortilla Scoops.
