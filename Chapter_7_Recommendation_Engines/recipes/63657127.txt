Title:
Canned Biscuit Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 pk canned biscuits
2 oz melted butter or margarine (1/2 stick)
2 tbsp brown sugar
Filling:
1/2 cup sugar
2 tbsp cinnamon
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp vanilla
Glaze:
1/2 cup confectioners sugar
2 tsp milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Butter bottom of 8" round or square pan
Sprinkle in 2 tbsp of brown sugar into pan
Prepare filling, adding all ingredients and mixing well.
Prepare glaze, mixing ingredients well.
Rolling or hand punching each biscuit flat, coat with melted butter and sprinkle filling mix to coat the dough. Roll up the dough then cut in 1/2 placing cut side down in pan. Continue until all biscuits are made yielding 20 pieces.
Cook 15 minutes in 350 preheated oven. Rolls are done when biscuit portion on top begins to turn brown.
Apply glazing to your liking.
