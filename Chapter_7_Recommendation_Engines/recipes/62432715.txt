Title:
Flax Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. yeast
3/4 cup ground flax
4 cups flour
2 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. salt
3 cups luke warm water
4 (or 4 1/2) cups flour

Directions:
In a separate bowl, mix 2 Tbsp. yeast, 3/4 cup ground flax and 4 cups flour.
In a mixing bowl, beat 2 eggs, 1/2 cup sugar, 1 tsp. salt and 3 cups luke warm water.
Add approximately 4 cups flour, then add yeast/flour/flax mixture. May need another 1/2 cup flour.
Rise 15 minutes, punch down and let rise 15 minutes again and make into loaves (let rise until double--1 hour).
Bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes.
