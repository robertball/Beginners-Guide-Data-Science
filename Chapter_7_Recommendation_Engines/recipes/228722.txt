Title:
Lushy Slushy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (46 fluid ounce) can pineapple juice
1 1/2 cups vodka
3/4 cup white sugar
1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
1 (6 ounce) can water
1/2 cup amaretto liqueur
3 (12 fluid ounce) cans or bottles cherry-flavored lemon-lime soda (such as Cherry 7UP®)

Directions:
Mix pineapple juice, vodka, sugar, orange juice concentrate, water, and amaretto liqueur in a large container; freeze overnight. To serve, fill a large glass about half full of the slushy mixture and fill the rest of the glass with cherry-flavored lemon-lime soda.

Submitter: 
madrileña
Description: 
A frozen drink that is perfect on hot summer nights.
