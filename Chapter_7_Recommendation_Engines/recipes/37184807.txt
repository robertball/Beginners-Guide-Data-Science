Title:
Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies (No Bake) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1/2 c. milk
1 c. sugar
2-3 T. cocoa
2 c. instant or minute oatmeal
1/2 c. peanut butter
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
In med. sauce pan melt butter and milk. in small mixing bowl mix sugar and cocoa together then add to milk & butter. bring to a boil then let boil ONE FULL MINUTE ,stir constantly. Take off heat, add peanut butter & vanilla. Stir till peanut butter is melted. Then add oatmeal, mix well. Drop on buttered wax paper (I use a table spoon). Let cool, then EAT!!
