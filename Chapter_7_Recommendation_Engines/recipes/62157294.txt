Title:
Rick's Barbequed Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tbs. molasses
2 tsp. brown sugar
1 tbs. Dijon mustard
2 tsp. Cajun spice
1 tbs. lemon juice
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 tbs. dried parsley
3 - 4 dashes tabasco sauce
Ground black pepper to taste
2 lbs. large, peeled, raw shrimp - tails on
12 wooden barbeque skewers

Directions:
In large, non-pourous bowl, thoroughly mix together first 10 ingredients.  Add shrimp and gently stir to coat shrimp.  Cover and refrigerate 6 hours or overnight, stirring occasionally.
Thread shrimp onto wooden skewers.  Discard remaining marinade.
Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Place skewered shrimp on grill, and barbecue for approximately 10 minutes or until shrimp is no longer opaque, turning once.

Submitter: 
Jackie
Description: 
This tangy, tasty shrimp marinade, given to me by a friend, is sure to please all the barbeque lovers out there.  The leftover shrimp is also delicious cold from the fridge.
