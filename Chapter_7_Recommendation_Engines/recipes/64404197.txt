Title:
Holy Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 Roma Tomatoes
4 Hass Avocados, Ripe (soft to thumb)
1 small white onion
Lime juice (fresh, to taste)
Fresh Cilantro, chopped
Kosher salt, to taste
Serrano Pepper (optional for spicy)

Directions:
Dice tomatoes, removing seeds, place in colander to drain
Dice white onion and set aside
Scoop avocados with a spoon out of outer skin.  Save pit.
Mash avocados - some chunks are okay
Mix in tomatoes, cilantro, onion, lime, and salt.
Place in airtight container with pits (they prevent browning).  Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Scot Jones
Description: 
This might possibly be the best guacamole I've ever had, especially when served fresh.
