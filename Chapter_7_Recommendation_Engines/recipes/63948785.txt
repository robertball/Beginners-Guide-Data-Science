Title:
Manhattan (cocktail) Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
4 (6 ounce) boneless skinless chicken breast halves
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1/4 orange marmalade
1/4 cup cherry spreadable fruit (Polaner All Fruit)
1 teaspoon low sodium soy sauce
1 teaspoon butter
1 tablespoon bourbon

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper.
Heat a large heavy skillet over medium-high heat add oil to pan and swirl to coat.  Add chicken to pan; cook 5 minutes on each side or until done.
Remove chicken from pan, keep warm.
Meanwhile, place marmalade, cherry spreadable fruit and soy sauce in small saucepan; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 3 minutes or until very thick, stirring frequently.  Add butter stirring to combine.  Remove from heat; stir in bourbon.  Spoon glaze over chicken.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This dish mimics a Manhattan cocktail.  

Serving ideas; serve  this with a nice rice dish, wild rice, spinach salad and/or glazed carrots (glazed carrot recipe below in notes) .
