Title:
Pork Casserole with Lemon and Rosemary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Pork Cubes
2 Tbsp. oil
1 Cup boiling vegetable, beef or chicken stock
3 Tbsp. Lemon Juice
1 Clove garlic, minced or pressed
1 Tsp. Dried Rosemary
salt and pepper to taste
1 (10 oz) Package of frozen Peas
1 (10 oz) Package of frozen Broccoli Spears
(Or you may use any combination of vegetables you wish)

Directions:
In a heavy skillet, brown pork in heated oil
Transfer pork to 2 qt. casserole dish
Add boiling stock, lemon juice, garlic, rosemary, salt and pepper
Cover and bake at 350' for 1 1/4 hours or until pork is almost tender
Add peas and broccoli, Cover and bake 15 more minutes or until vegetable are heated through.

Description: 
A Full Flavored Pork Casserole slowly baked to provide a welcome change to your dinner table.
