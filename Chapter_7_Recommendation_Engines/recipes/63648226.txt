Title:
Cheeseburger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
2 onion, chopped
4 cloves of garlic, crushed
6 oz of green chili
2 teaspoon dried basil
4 tablespoons dried parsley
8 tablespoons butter
6 cups chicken broth
2 lbs hash brown potatoes
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
8 ounces Queso Dip
1 1/2 cups milk
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup sour cream

Directions:
Brown beef, drain and set aside. Saute onion, green chili, garlic, basil and parsley in 1 tablespoon butter until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.  Brown hashbrown just until slightly crisp.
To 5qt soup pan, add broth, vegtables, potatoes and beef and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover  and simmer for 5 - 10 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Meanwhile, in a small skillet melt remaining butter. Add flour and cook and stir for 3-5 minutes or until bubbly. Add to soup. Cook and stir soup for 2 minutes. Reduce heat to low.
Add cheese, milk, salt and pepper and cook and stir until cheese melts. Remove from the heat. Add sour cream.
