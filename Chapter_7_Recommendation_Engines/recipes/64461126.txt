Title:
Rosemary Chicken and Brie En Croute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz can crescent rolls
2 tbs minced green onions
6 oz. brie, rind removed, cubed
1 1/2 cups chopped cook chicken breast
1 egg, beaten
1 tsp rosemary
1 tbs Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. Separate dough in 4 rectangles, firmly press perforations to seal. Spoon minced onion into center of each triangle, top with 1/4 cheese cubes, then top with chicken. fold short ends over chicken, overlapping slightly. Fold open ends over about 1/2 inch to form rectangle. Press all edges to seal. Place seam side down on baking cookie sheet. Cut 3 slashes on top of each roll. Brush with egg, sprinkle with rosemary and Parmesan cheese. Bake 15 minutes or until golden brown.
