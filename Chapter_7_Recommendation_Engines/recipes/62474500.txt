Title:
Chacala Vong sweet & sour soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 garalic
1" ginger, grated
2 t turmeric
1 T fish sauce
1/2 T sugar
1/2 red chili pepper chopped
1lb 10oz white fish, cut into small chunks
4 T oil
2 T water
dill
NUOC NAM
2 garlic
1/2 red chili
3 T sugar
4 T fish sauce
1 T rice wine vinager
1 T water
TO SERVE
1lb 2 oz cooked rice noodles
14oz spinach
2 bunches basil
1 3/4 c peanuts, chopped

Directions:
Mix first 6 together, maranate fish over night. Cook in oil, turn off heat and add dill to wilt
Mix Nuoc Nam to dissolve sugar. Heat in microwave.
Serve with remaining ingredients.
