Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2550856.jpg
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless frozen chicken breast halves
3 tablespoons butter
2 cans condensed cream of chicken soup
1/2 onion, finely diced
Salt and pepper to taste
1 large can refrigerated biscuit dough-roll out, season with salt and pepper and cut into dumplings.

Directions:
Place the chicken, butter, soup, and onion in a slow cooker, and fill with enough water to cover.
Cover, and cook for 6 hours on High. About 90 minutes before serving, add the "dumplings" stirring gently so dough is covered with liquid.

Submitter: 
Angie Brindley
Description: 
Easy chicken and dumplings using refrigerated biscuit dough. Great served over mashed potatoes or optionally made with added vegetables of your choice.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings
