Title:
Roast Chicken with Sweet Potatoes and Chickpeas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds bone-in, skin-on chicken pieces (about 8 pieces)
2 medium sweet potatoes (about 2 pounds total; cut into wedges)
1 large red onion (cut into wedges)
1 15-oz can chickpeas (rinsed)
6 cloves peeled, smashed garlic
3 T olive oil
2 tsp smoked paprika
1 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees.
Toss together chicken, sweet potatoes, onion, chickpeas, garlic, oil, paprika, cumin seeds, salt, and pepper.
Roast on rimmed baking sheet, placing chicken, skin-side up, atop the vegetables, until vegetables are tender and chicken is cooked through (165 degrees), 50-70 minutes.
Serve with pesto.
