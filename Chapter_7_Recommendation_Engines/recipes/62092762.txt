Title:
Everyday Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white rice
1 cup 2 tablespoons water
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1/4 cup frozen corn kernels
2 tablespoons margarine or butter
salt

Directions:
Combine everything in a rice cooker or 1.5 quart pot and boil.
Serve with good food!

Submitter: 
Apple Jacks
Description: 
We make this simple dish in our little 1-quart rice cooker almost every day and the yummy smell tells us that dinner is on it's way!
