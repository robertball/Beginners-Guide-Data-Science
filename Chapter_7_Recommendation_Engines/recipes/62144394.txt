Title:
Veggie and Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
1 cup carrots
1 cup green beans
1 cup mushrooms
1 cup water chestnuts
1 Tbs hot chilli paste
3 Tbs toasted sesame seeds
1 Tbs garlic
1 Tbs ginger
1/2 cup water
2 tsp cornstarch
3 Tbs soy sauce
1 Tbs sesame oil
1 Tbs & 2 Tbs peanut oil
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper and slice into strips
Cook chicken in 1 Tbs of peanut oil until browned
Remove chicken once cooked
Add 2 Tbs oil to pan at medium high heat
Add ginger, chilli paste, and garlic and let cook for 15-30 seconds
Add carrots and green beans
Cook for 5 minutes
Add mushrooms
Cook for 2 minutes
Add water chestnuts and chicken back to pan
Mix together soy sauce, water, cornstarch, and sesame oil in separate bowl and then add to pan
Let thicken
Serve over white rice and sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds
