Title:
Beef Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. Round Steak
½ sliced Onion
1 package Mushroom Onion Soup mix
2 cups Beef Stock
2 cups dry Red Wine
1.5 cups sliced mushrooms
Salt and Pepper to taste
¼ cup Water
1 Tbsp Cornstarch
Cooked egg noodles

Directions:
Cut the steak into 1” chunks.
Put steak and next 6 ingredients (through salt and pepper) in crock pot. Cook on high 4 hours.
Mix water and cornstarch. Add to crock pot.
Cook another ½ hour or until desired thickness.
Serve over egg noodles.

Submitter: 
Kristin C
Description: 
Tender beef in a red wine sauce with mushrooms and onions. Serve over noodles or mashed potatoes.
