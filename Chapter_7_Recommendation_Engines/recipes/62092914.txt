Title:
Easy Smothered Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 center cut pork chops
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
Salt
Pepper
Onion Powder
Garlic Powder
Tony Chachere's or Season-All
Medium sliced or diced onion
1 or 2 small cans Cream of Mushroom Soup (depending on amount of gravy desired)
Equal number soup cans of water

Directions:
Heat oil on medium to medium high heat
Season pork chops to desired taste with all dry seasonings on front and back of each chop.
Sear both sides of chops on each side and set aside.
Saute onion until tender
Add soup and cook until slightly browned.
Add water to soup mix - stir thoroughly
Add pork chops and simmer for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
BAYOU GAL
Description: 
This is one of my family's favorite meals!Easy Smothered Pork Chops with a wonderful mushroom and onion gravy that goes great over rice, potatoes, or noodles. Works great in slow cooker also.  They always come back for seconds!
