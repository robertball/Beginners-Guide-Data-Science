Title:
Thanksgiving stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes stuffing
2 grannysmith or macintosh apples
1/2 c slivered almonds
1/2 c chopped walnuts
1 c dried cranberries
2 c celery chopped
1 c onion diced
1 egg
2 cans broth
water as needed
1 T butter

Directions:
carmelize onions and celery in butter
mix egg and broth
mix all ingredients together
place in oven safe caserole dish
cover and bake 1-2 hours at 350 degrees until internal temp over 160 degrees
uncover and on low broil lightly brown
