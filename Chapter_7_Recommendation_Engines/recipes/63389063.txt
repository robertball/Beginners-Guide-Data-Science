Title:
Slow Cooker Cocoa Cinnamon Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/909265.jpg
Ingredients:
Cocoa Cinnamon Marinade Ingredients
•	1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa
•	1 tablespoon cumin
•	2 teaspoons cinnamon
•	2 teaspoons sea salt
•	1 teaspoon allspice
Slow Cooker Ingredients
•	2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup (or chicken)
•	Cocoa Cinnamon Rub
•	1 ¼ cups water
•	5 ½ pounds pot roast

Directions:
In a slow cooker, mix cream of mushroom (or chicken) soup, cocoa cinnamon marinade spices, and water.  Place pot roast in slow cooker and coat with soup mixture.
Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.

Submitter: 
Marie Blau McCoy
Description: 
This delicious melt in your mouth pot roast is so wonderful, and left overs make great sandwiches.
