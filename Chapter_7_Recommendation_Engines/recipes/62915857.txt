Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2/3 C broth
1/4 t black pepper
4 C spinach
2 T green onion, thinly sliced
1 can green chile (4oz)
6 tortillas, 7 or 8in.
8 oz lt. sour cream
1/4 C plain low fat yogurt
2 T flour
1/4 t cumin
1/4 t salt
1/2 C fat-free milk
1/2 C cheddar cheese-sprinkle over baked dish
tomato, chopped or salsa for garnish

Directions:
In lg. skillet, combine chicken, broth and pepper. Bring to boil. Cover and simmer 12-14 min. Drain well and shred. Cook spinach, steam 3-5 min for fresh. Combine chicken, spinach and green onion; set aside.
For sauce, combine sour cream, yogurt, flour, cumin and salt. Stir in milk and chile peppers. Divide sauce in half. Store one portion in covered container.
Combine remaining sauce and chicken mixture. Put in tortillas. Place seam side down in baking dish. Cover with foil and chill 4-24 hrs.
Preheat oven 350. Spoon remaining sauce over tortillas. Bake covered for 20 min, uncover and bake 20 min. more

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
This dish needs to be made ahead so as to sit for 4-24 hrs., longer is better.  Low fat, low cal version
