Title:
Chicken and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon, chopped
2 1/2 to 3-pound chicken, cut into serving pieces
10 oz. pkg mushrooms, quartered
1 clove garlic, finely chopped (or more to taste)
1 envelope Onion Soup Mix
1 1/4 C water
1/2 C red wine or water
1/2 t dried oregano, crushed

Directions:
In Dutch oven or 6-quart saucepan, cook bacon until crisp. Remove and set aside; reserve drippings. Add chicken to drippings and brown; remove and set aside. In same Dutch oven, add mushrooms and garlic and cook over medium heat, stirring, 4 minutes. Return chicken and bacon to Dutch oven, add Onion Soup Mix blended with water, wine and oregano. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer covered 40 minutes or until chicken is done.

Submitter: 
Heidi
Description: 
This is one of my all-time favorite dishes. It has tons of flavor with bacon and mushrooms and it's also pretty simple to fix. I usually cook a package of chicken thighs rather than a whole chicken cut up. We actually fight over the leftovers at my house!
