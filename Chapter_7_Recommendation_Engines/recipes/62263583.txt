Title:
Dijon Salmon Salad with Lemon and Capers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 or 5 oz. cooked salmon (about 1 1/2 cups, flaked), chilled
1 1/2 tsp. capers, drained and chopped
1 1/2 tsp. fresh dill, chopped
1 scallion, chopped
1/4 cup non-fat yogurt
1 tbsp. mayonnaise
1 tsp. dijon mustard
1 tsp. fresh squeezed lemon juice
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together yogurt, mayonnaise, dijon mustard and lemon juice.
Stir in capers, dill and scallion.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Fold in flaked salmon.
Serve on top of salad greens, in a sandwich, on a toasted English muffin, as a spread for cracker, etc....

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Great use for leftover poached or baked salmon.  Can be served on salad greens, in a sandwich or even as a spread for crackers.  The dressing itself makes a very flavorful tartar sauce.
