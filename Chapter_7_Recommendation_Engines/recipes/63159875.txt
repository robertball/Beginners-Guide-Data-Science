Title:
Chef John's Chocolate Mint Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brownie Batter:
1/2 cup of butter, melted
1 1/8 cups sugar
3/4 cup cocoa
1/4 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Vanilla
2 large eggs
2/3 cup flour
Icing:
1 cup powdered sugar
1/4 tsp. Mint extract
2 tbsp. Milk

Directions:
In a small saucepan, melt butter. Meanwhile, in a heat safe bowl, whisk together sugar and cocoa. Add hot butter to sugar and cocoa in bowl. Whisk to combine. Add salt, vanilla and one egg. Whisk well. Add second egg and continue to whisk. At this point, change from whisk to spatula. Incorporate flour and blend until just combined. Spread into a lightly greased 8x8 inch glass baking dish. Bake at 325 degrees for about 35 minutes, or until pick comes out nearly clean. Allow to cool completely, then frost.  Icing: Combine powdered sugar, extract and milk. Blend well and spread/pour over cooled brownies. Allow to cool completely and set before cutting.
