Title:
Easy German Red Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound Bag of fresh Sauerkraut (drained)
16 oz jar of Pickled Beets, reserve liquid
2 medium Bay Leaves
2 tbsp butter
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain kraut. Combine all ingredients in 2 qrt pot and simmer for about 15 minutes until heated through and serve.

Submitter: 
camille
Description: 
Easy and inexpensive side dish that is excellent with Round Steak Sauerbraten from this site.
