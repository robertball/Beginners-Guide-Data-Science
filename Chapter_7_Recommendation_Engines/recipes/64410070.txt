Title:
Oatmeal Carrot Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups All Purpose Flour
1 Cup Oatmeal
1 Tsp baking Powder
1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
1/2 Tsp Cinnamon
1 Cup Dark Brown Sugar
1 Cup Light Brown Sugar
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
1 Tbs Maple Syrup
1 Stick Softened Butter
1/2 Cup Corn Oil
2 Eggs
3 Cups Grated Carrots
1 Cup Craisons
1/2 Tsp Salt
Spray Shortening

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 DEGF.
Spray cookie sheet
Mix dry ingredients.
Mix in carrots and crasions.
Mix in remaining ingredients.
Spoon 1 tablespoon cookies in to sheet. Leave room for them to double in size.
Bake 12 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Vinnie Marotta
Description: 
A good recipe for leftover carrots from juicing.
