Title:
Pasta with Chick Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chick peas
300 g Pasta - short type like "tubetti" or shells
50 g Pancetta - finely chopped
1 onion - finely chopped
1 clove garlic - finely chopped
1/4 cup olive oil
6 fresh tomatoes - finely chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
50 g Romano cheese - grated
3-4 leaves fresh basil
chili flakes to taste

Directions:
Cook chick peas until tender.  If you use dry chick peas, soak overnight before cooking and always start the cooking process in cold water.  If you use canned chick peas, heat in its own juice.
Saute onion and garlic for 1-2 minutes.  Add Pancetta and cook for 2-3 minutes.  Add tomatoes, basil, salt and pepper.  Cook on medium heat for 10-15 minutes, then add to chick peas.  cook pasta "al dente", drain and add to the chick peas.  Mix well.  Top with grated Romano cheese and serve.
