Title:
breakfast bubble pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans biscuits
sausage gravy
1 pound bacon
8 eggs
Shredded cheese

Directions:
preheat oven to 425
cut biscuits in 1/4s and line a sprayed 9x13 pan bake 12 min. remove from oven pour warmed gravy over top of biscuits and sprinkle crumbled bacon over gravy. scramble eggs and cook. spread eggs over bacon, top with shredded cheese - return to oven for 5 to 10 min. till cheese melts.
