Title:
3 Ingredient Berry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536208.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12 oz bottle of fizzy water, I picked Perrier
1 lb bag of unsweetened berries.
1 package of your low fat favorite cake mix

Directions:
Start off by coating an 8Ã—8 pan with nonstick spray and adding your bag of frozen berries.
In a separate bowl, combine your fizzy water and cake mix. Mix just until combined.
Pour your cake mixture over your berries and bake according to the packaged directions.
Turn off oven and let sit in the oven for 45-50 minutes.
Allow to cool, and Serve as is or topped with whipped cream or ice cream.
