Title:
Roasted Green Beans with Dill Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/308738.jpg
Ingredients:
2# geen beans trimmed
1 T. olive oil
1/2 tsp coarse salt
Vinaigrette
1 T. olive oil
2 T. red wine vinegar
1 1/2 tsp dijon mustard
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp dry dill (or 1 tsp fresh)
1/2 tsp coarse ground pepper

Directions:
Bake 450 degree oven for 20 minutes.
Mix vinaigrette and pour over beans.
