Title:
Bleu Cheese Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package frozen green peas
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup slivered almonds
1/2 cup chopped green onions (about 1 bunch)
6 thick slices of bacon
1/2 cup bleu cheese crumble
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Thaw peas on a paper towel.
Place thawed peas in a large bowl.
In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream and vinegar; stir into peas.
Slice the bacon into matchsticks; saute until crisp, draining a couple of times as you go.
Toast almonds in a skillet over medium heat; add to peas along with remaining ingredients.
Cover and refrigerate for several hours or overnight.
