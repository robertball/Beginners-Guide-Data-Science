Title:
Crab Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10-12 English Muffins, halved
1 jar Old English Cheese Spread
1 can Crab, minced (6 oz)
1/4 lb. Oleo
1 1/2 tsp. Mayonnaise
1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp. Seasoned Salt

Directions:
Heat all ingredients except English Muffins together until melted.
Spread on Muffins
Broil until bubbly then cut into quarters
