Title:
Cider Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. beef stew meat, 1" cubes
3 T. flour
2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1/4 t. thyme
3 T. vegetable oil
2 c. apple cider OR apple juice
1-2 T. vinegar
3 potatoes, peeled and diced
4 carrots, peeled and sliced
2 onions, sliced
1 stalk celery, sliced

Directions:
Coat meat with the mix of flour, salt, pepper, and tyme.
Brown meat, half at a time, in the hot oil. Drain off fat.
Return all meat to pan. Stir in apple cider, vinegar, and 1/2 c. water. Cook and stir til mixture boils.
Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for about an hour, until meat is tender.
Stir in vegetables and cook 30 minutes more, or until vegetables are done.
