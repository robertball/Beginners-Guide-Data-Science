Title:
Shrimp with Feta and Tomato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon Olive Oil
4 Scallions sliced
2 Garlic Cloves, minced
3 tomatoes chopped
1/4 c. dry white wine (sherry)
1 tbs fresh lemon juice
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 pound large shrimp peeled and deveined
1/3 feta cheese
1/3 cup minced flat leaf parsley

Directions:
Heat oil in large nonstick skillet. Add scallions and garlic and cook stirring as needed until softened (2-3 min)
Add the tomatoes, wine, leomon juice, oregano and pepper: cook stirring as needed until the tmoatoes are just soft (about 2 min)
Add the shrimp; reduce heat and simmer covered until the shrimp turn pink (3-5 min)
Remove from heat and stir in the cheese and parsley. Serve immediately
