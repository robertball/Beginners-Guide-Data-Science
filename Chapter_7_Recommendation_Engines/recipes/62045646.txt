Title:
Polish Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2- 3 lbs flour
8 eggs
2 c. milk (scalded)
1 1/4 stick margarine or butter (melted)
3 pkg yeast (dissolved in 1 c. warm water)
1 cup sugar
1 tbsp salt

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together.  The dough will be sticky.  Knead for 10 min.  Rise in bowl in warm area until doubled.  Punch down, knead and shaped into 3 loaves.  Rise again until over the pan.  Bake at 350Â° for 1/2 hour.

Description: 
During the holidays is only when our Polish food would come out.  But we all looked forward to the Polish Bread with every meal.  If you like King's Hawaiian Bread, this is denser hearty version.
