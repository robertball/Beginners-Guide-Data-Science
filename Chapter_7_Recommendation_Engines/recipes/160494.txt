Title:
Potato Taco Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 1/2 cups chunky salsa
1 (10 ounce) package frozen spinach, thawed, squeezed and drained
3/4 cup water
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning
1 (20 ounce) package Simply Potatoes® Homestyle Slices
1 1/2 cups sour cream
3 cups Crystal Farms® Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 cup corn chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.  In 10-inch skillet cook ground beef until no longer pink.  Drain.
Stir in salsa, spinach, water and taco seasoning; set aside.
Spray 13x9-inch baking dish with non-stick spray.  Place half of the Simply Potatoes(R) on bottom of pan. Top with half of the meat mixture, 3/4 cup sour cream and 1 1/2 cups cheese.  Repeat layer ending with the cheese.
Bake, covered, 45 to 50 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Uncover; top with corn chips. Let stand 10 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Simply Potatoes®
Description: 
It's lasagna with a Mexican twist! This bake-ahead meal is great for nights when there's no time to cook.
