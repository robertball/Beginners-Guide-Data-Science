Title:
Won Ton Wrapper Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of ground chorizo hot sausage
crushed garlic
one onion
two green peppers, or jalape√±o peppers
bunch of scallions
1 tub of cream cheese
1 bag of shredded cheddar
40 wonton wrappers

Directions:
1. place wonton wrappers into mini-muffin tins, and toast until a little crispy, remove from oven
2. brown sausage, diced onions, peppers, garlic, and scallions until cooked
3. remove from heat, add cream cheese and cheddar, mix well
4. drop spoonfuls into toasted wonton wrappers and cook for 15 minutes at 350
5. serve hot

Submitter: 
Christine
Description: 
Great for a party, can be as spicy as you want to make them.
