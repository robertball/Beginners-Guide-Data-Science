Title:
Slow-Cooker Hearty Beef Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986268.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds extra-lean ground beef
2 (19 ounce) cans mixed beans, rinsed
2 cups salsa
1 (14 ounce) can no-salt-added tomato sauce
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 onion, chopped
1 cup frozen corn, thawed, drained
1 cup KRAFT Tex Mex Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Brown meat; drain. Add to slow cooker with remaining ingredients except cheese; stir. Cover with lid.
Cook on LOW 5 to 6 hours (or on HIGH 3 to 4 hours).
Stir just before serving. Serve topped with the cheese.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese
Description: 
Try this simple slow-simmering beef chili. The salsa adds a nice little kick and saves on prep time.
