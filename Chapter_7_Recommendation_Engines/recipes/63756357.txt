Title:
Vegan Cajun Hoppin' John Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 md onion, diced
1 md green bell pepper, diced
10oz tomato paste
3c cooked brown rice
16oz can red kidney beans, drained
10oz frozen blackeyed peas
15 drops Tabasco sauce
canola oil
dill weed
smoked salt
mustard powder
garlic mill
savory

Directions:
Combine onion and pepper in a large frying pan with just enough oil to coat them and the pan but not enough to leave excess.
Cover and place on high heat until thoroughly cooked, about 15min.
Add remaining ingredients, seasoning to taste.  Remove from heat when all ingredients are well-heated.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
This dish is just spicy enough to enhance, rather than take away from, its great depth of flavor.  Goes well with cornbread.
