Title:
Easy Honey Mustard Mozzarella Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
3/4 cup honey
1/2 cup prepared mustard/dijon
corn starch
lemon pepper to taste
bacon bits
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
mix a little corn starch with honey and mustard
Place the chicken breast halves in a baking dish, and drizzle evenly with honey and mustard. Sprinkle with lemon pepper.
Bake chicken 25 minutes in the preheated oven. Top each breast half with bacon bits, and sprinkle evenly with cheese. Continue baking 10 minutes, or until chicken juices run clear, bacon is crisp, and cheese is bubbly.

Submitter: 
Diane Manzella
Description: 
Chicken is glazed with mustard and honey, then baked with bacon and cheese. A favorite!Modified from: Easy Honey Mustard Mozzarella Chicken
