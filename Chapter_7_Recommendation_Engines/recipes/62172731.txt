Title:
Cherry glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One pd sour pitted cherries
1/4C sugar
1Tbsp cornstarch
2 drops of red food coloring

Directions:
Drain the cherries, reserving juice. In a small saucepan combine sugar with the cornstarch and juice. Stir over low heat constantly until thickend or clear. Mix food coloring and cherries. cool
