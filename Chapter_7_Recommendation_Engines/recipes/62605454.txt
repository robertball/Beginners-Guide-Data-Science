Title:
Chicken and Rice Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
1-2 tablespoons olive oil
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
8 ounce package sliced fresh mushrooms
1-1/2 cups water
1 box Near East Rice Pilaf
1 head of broccoli
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
In heavy nonstick skillet, saute onion in olive oil until translucent. Add chicken and cook over medium heat until browned on one side, about 4 minutes. Turn chicken and add mushrooms, and cook until chicken is browned on second side. Remove chicken from pan.
Add water, rice, and seasoning packet to skillet with mushrooms. Place chicken around edge of skillet. Add broccoli to center.
Cook over medium heat until mixture boils. Then reduce heat to low, cover, and cook 10 minutes. Stir to mix broccoli with rice mixture. Add sour cream and heat another 1-2 minutes.
