Title:
Jim's Homemade Slow Cooker Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422007.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds ground beef
40 oz light red kidney beans
1- lg onion diced into a larger dice
1- teaspooon cayene pepper
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon soy sauce
1 teaspoons minced garlic
4 can ( 10.5 oz) Rotelo diced tomato and pepper
1 12 oz bottle mr & mrs t bloody mary mix
2 teaspoons chili powder
2  12oz cans v8 juice
1 6 oz can tomato paste

Directions:
brown the ground beef and combine all in a 6 qt slow cooker for 4 hours.

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
This is my homemade chili like no other. It has a little kick but it won't have your mouth on fire. Just enough to know youre eating some good chili!
