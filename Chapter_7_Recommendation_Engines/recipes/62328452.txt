Title:
Turkey Salad With Tomato, Avocado, and Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/385056.jpg
Ingredients:
*  1 large head green leaf lettuce, torn into pieces (about 6 cups)
* 1/2 pound thinly sliced deli turkey
* 2 large tomatoes, cut into wedges
* 1 avocado, cut into bite-size pieces
* 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
* 1/2 cup shaved Parmesan (2 ounces)
* 4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
* 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
* kosher salt and pepper

Directions:
1.  Divide the lettuce, turkey, tomatoes, avocado, onion, and Parmesan among bowls.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the oil, vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Drizzle over the salad.
