Title:
Budget Apple Crisp II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/270002.jpg
Ingredients:
8 apples, peeled, cored and sliced
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1 tablespoon butter, softened
2 packages brown sugar cinnamon instant oatmeal
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup butter, softened

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
In baking dish toss apple slices with cornstarch, pumpkin pie spice, 1/4 cup brown sugar.  Dot 1 tablespoon butter over the top.
In a medium bowl place the packets of instant oatmeal, all-purpose flour, 1/4 cup brown sugar, and softened butter.  Using your fingers mix together these ingredients until a nice crumbly topping forms.  Cover the apples with this mixture.
Place into preheated oven and bake for about 45 minutes, or until bubbly and nicely browned on top.

Submitter:

Description: 
Use the best apples the season has to offer, they are usually the least expensive as well.  I used packets of instant oatmeal that have cinnamon and brown sugar in them, you can substitute quick cooking oats if you don't have any packets around.Modified from: Apple Crisp II
