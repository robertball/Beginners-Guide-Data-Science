Title:
Weatherford Fudge Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
3 T. cocoa powder
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1 tsp. vanilla
greased 9" pie pan
vanilla ice cream or whipped cream, optional

Directions:
Mix flour, sugar, salt, and cocoa powder in a small bowl, running a whisk through to break up any lumps.
Melt butter in small saucepan over low heat (or in microwave in oven-able bowl).
Mix flour mixture with butter.
Stir in eggs and vanilla.
Pour into well greased 9" pie pan.
Bake 20 minutes at 350 F.
Cool slightly.
Serve in pie wedges topped with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream...also good plain!
