Title:
Sunset Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2tsp finely shredded lime peel
1 cup lime juice
3/4 cup sugar
ice
1 bottle of Pinot Noir or Syrah, chilled
1 nectarine, pitted and sliced

Directions:
In a one quart glass measuring cup combine lime peel, lime juice and sugar; add water to equal 4 cups. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours.
To serve, place ice in six 16oz glasses. Add 2/3 cup of the lime juice mixture into each glass. Pouring alongside of glass, slowly add 1/2 cup of wine. Thread nectarine slices on skewers and add to glasses.
