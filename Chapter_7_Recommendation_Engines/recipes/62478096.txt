Title:
Grape Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. sour cream
8 oz. cream cheese (Softened)
1/2 cup sugar
4 lbs. grapes (white or black seedless)
1/2 to 1 cup pecans (chopped)
1 tsp. vanilla flavoring

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and chill.  Can be made a couple of days in advance.

Submitter: 
Exhibits
Description: 
This is a great salad to take to a gathering,perhaps also good for events like Thanksgiving.  Easy to make and can make ahead.  I got this recipe from a fellow golfer.  At Christmas time I use red and green grapes.
