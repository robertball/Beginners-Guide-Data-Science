Title:
Cranberry Peach Dessert Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1933057.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 C. Light cream
3 Eggs, beaten
1 C. Granulated sugar
1/2 C. Whole berry cranberry sauce
1 C. Peaches (chopped into 1/2" pieces)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 9" by 5" by 3" loaf pan. Set aside
Make cranberry sauce according to package directions and then use in recipe when cooled and desired consistency is reached
In a small bowl blend flour and b. powder together. Set aside
Beat eggs in a small bowl until light and frothy.
Combine light cream, sugar and beaten eggs in a large bowl and mix on medium speed with an electric mixer until mixture is light and creamy
In a separate bowl mix peach wedges and cranberry sauce together.
Add fruit to cream/sugar mixture and beat on medium speed until fruit is nicely blended into mixture
Continue to beat with electric mixer on medium speed adding dry ingredients to wet mixture until all the flour is well combined and batter is smooth
Pour batter into prepared loaf pan and bake for 40 to 50 minutes until loaf tests done in the middle
Remove from the oven and allow to cool before removing from loaf pan
To remove loaf firmly tap loaf against cutting board and loosen with a knife around the edges

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very moist and delicious loaf with cranberries and peaches for a tasty dessert
