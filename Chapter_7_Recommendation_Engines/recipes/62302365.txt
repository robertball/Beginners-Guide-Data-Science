Title:
Pear Apple Kuchen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c. drk brown sugar
1/3 c. butter (or marg.) softened
2 eggs
2 c. bisquit mix (I used Bisquick)
2/3 c. yogert (I used homemade)
1 egg
2 red pears, peeled, cored and chopped
2 apples peeled, cored and chopped
1/3 c. syrup (I used Pecan Praline, could use corn syrup or honey)
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/4 c. sugar and 1 tsp. cinnamon (mixed)
1/2 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease bottom and sides of 11 x 7 deep casserole dish with shortening. Mix sugar, butter and eggs until smooth. Stir in bisquit mix. Spread in prepared pan. Mix yogert, honey, vanilla and egg, stir in apple/pears. Spoon over batter in pan. Sprinkle top with cinnamon, sugar and pecans. Bake 45 - 50 mins. or until center is set. Cool 10 mins.
