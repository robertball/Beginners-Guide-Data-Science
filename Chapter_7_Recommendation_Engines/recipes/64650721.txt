Title:
Pat's Special K Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Eggs
6 Cups Special K Cereal
1/2 Walnuts, chopped
1 Envelope (Liptons) dry Onion Soup mix
4 Cups Cottage Cheese, small curd
1/4 Cup Melted Butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Combine all ingredients.  Spray a 2 or 3 quart dish with non-stick cooking spray.  Place mixture in evenly in dish.  Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 1 hour.  Then remove aluminum foil and bake for 15 minutes longer.
Remove from oven, cool and serve.
