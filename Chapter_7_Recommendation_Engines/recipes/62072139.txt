Title:
Tuscan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
boneless skinless Chicken breast
Sundried tomatoes diced
fresh garlic minced
oil
1 cup parmesan cheese thick grated

Directions:
tenderize chicken breasts with a mallot to about a 1/2 inch width.
In a saucepan mix oil, garlic and sundried tomatoes  heat add chicken.
cook until browned slightly
place chicken on a baking sheet with sides
pour parmesan cheese over do not use all
of the parmesan cheese you will need some for the other side.
place in oven on 375 for about 15 mins turn over the chicken breasts use the rest of the parmesan cheese on this side cook for another 15 to 20 until chicken is done.
serve with my alfredo sauce over fettucini and you will think your at the olive garden.
It is Delicious
Hint: works best with fresh parmesan cheese.
