Title:
Vegetarian Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1104889.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust
¤ 2 & 1/2 cups of flour
¤ 1/2 cup of refrigerated water
¤ 1/2 cup of corn oil
¤ 1 pinch of salt
Filling
¤ 5 cups of mixed vegetables
¤ 1 small onion, chopped
¤ 1/2 cup of milk
¤ 4 large eggs
¤ 3/4 cup of feta cheese
¤ 1/4 cup of mozzarella cheese
¤ 2 tbps of lemon juice
¤ Salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375°C. Finely chop and fry the onion until golden brown. Add the vegetables, lemon juice and salt. Set aside. Mix the eggs, milk, cheese, salt and pepper in a separate bowl.
Prepare the crust by mixing the oil with the water. Pour it gradualy to the flour and salt and keep mixing until obtaining a thick dough. Place the dough on 2 round 9" diameter baking pans.
Place the vegetables on the crust and pour the egg mixture on it. Bake for 20 minutes or until it's done.

Description: 
Very healthy quiche full of vitamins! If you're craving vegetables for brunch, this light recipe would definitely satisfy your appetite. Enjoy :)
