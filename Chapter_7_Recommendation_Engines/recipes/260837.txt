Title:
Coconut Milk Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4571736.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup light coconut milk
1 (1 ounce) square dark chocolate, chopped
1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
1/8 teaspoon curry powder (optional)

Directions:
Heat coconut milk in a saucepan over medium-low heat until warm, 3 to 5 minutes. Add chocolate and sugar; whisk until incorporated and chocolate is melted. Pour mixture into a blender and add curry powder. Cover and hold lid down with a potholder; blend until smooth, about 15 seconds.

Submitter: 
Crema
Description: 
This delicious vegan hot chocolate is made with coconut milk and dark chocolate and a pinch of curry. Plus sugar of course, but you can use other sweeteners as well.
