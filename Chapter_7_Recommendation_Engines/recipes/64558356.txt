Title:
Sprouts Dal Khichadi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3417191.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Large onion (Chopped)
1 pound fresh green peas
1 tomato (chopped)
1 1/2 cups basmati rice
1 1/2 cups of whole moong (sprouted)
1 teaspoon garlic paste
1 teaspoon ginger paste
2 tablespoons oil
1 pinch of asafoetida ( hing)
1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
1/2 tablespoon cloves powder
2 teaspoon chili powder
4 to 6 curry leaves
2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon turmeric powder (Haldi)
2 cup water
chopped coriander for garnishing
1 cup curd(Optional)

Directions:
Heat the oil in a pan and add the mustard seeds to it. When they crackle, add cumin seeds and hing and curry leaves.
Add the onions and fry for some time till they turn translucent.
Add the ginger paste, garlic paste, turmeric powder, chili powder and cloves powder and saut√© for 1 to 2 minutes.
Add whole sprouted moong and basmati rice and green peas, 2 cup water and cook for 15-20 minutes till completely cooked.
After cooking add chopped tomato and salt and again cook for 5 minutes.
Serve hot khichadi in bowl and garnish it with coriander.
Serve with curd.

Submitter: 
Harshada Dhande-Sarode
Description: 
This is indian style rice dish flavored with sprouted moong and fried onions. This is very healthy and low calorie recipe  can serve with or without curd.
