Title:
Beer Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs self raising flour
salt
1 bottle of beer.

Directions:
place the flour in a bowl.
make a well in the centre and add the salt.
pour the beer, mix and knead (adding more flour if necessary) until the dough doesn't stick to your hands.
leave to prove in a warm place for about 1 hour. Then bake in a moderate oven for 1 hour.
You can also make it into little rolls.

Submitter: 
jane Gibraltar
Description: 
U.K. Standard. My mother taught me this recipe. A very simple and easy way to make bread.
