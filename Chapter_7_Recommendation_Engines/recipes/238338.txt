Title:
Cantaloupe Sipper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2437575.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups cubed cantaloupe
1/2 cup chilled lemon juice
1/4 cup dark rum
1 tablespoon honey
ground ginger

Directions:
Blend cantaloupe, lemon juice, rum, honey, and ginger together in a blender until smooth.
