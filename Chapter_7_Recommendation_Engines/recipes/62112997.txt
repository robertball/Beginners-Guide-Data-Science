Title:
Hot pepper sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. ketchup
1/2 C. vegetable oil
1/2 c. white vinegar
1/2 C. sugar (use white or brown or a combination)
2 quarts chopped hot peppers (habenaro, serrano, jalapeno)
small onion chopped (1/4 C.)
cumin, curry, salt, pepper optional

Directions:
Chop onion and peppers.  Combine all ingredients.
Simmer until heated through.  Jar and process in canner for 15 minutes or refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Janet Larsen
Description: 
This is a hot pepper sauce that is great served on crackers, bruschetta, or mixed with soft cream cheese to make a spread.
