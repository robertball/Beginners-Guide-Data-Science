Title:
Peanut butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mix thoroughly: 1/2 cup soft shortening (or butter)
1/2 cup peanutbutter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar (packed)
1 egg
Sift together and stir in: 1 1/4 cups sifted flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp soda
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Chill dough. Roll into balls the size of a lg walnut.
Place 3" apart on a lightly greased baking sheet.
Flatten with a fork dipped in flour-criss cross.
Bake in a quick moderate oven (375 degrees) for 10-15 min, or until set, but not hard.
