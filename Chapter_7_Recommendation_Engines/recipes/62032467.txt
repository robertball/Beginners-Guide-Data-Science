Title:
Garlic Parmesan Vineagrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Olive Oil
1/2 cup Canola oil
2 cloves of garlic - pressed or chopped fine
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
1/3 cup parmesan cheese grated
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
In a bowl or container mix all above ingredients.  Mix or shake well.  Taste for salt & pepper.  Pour over a salad of your choice.  If the dressing been in the refrigerator and the oil is solid bring to room temperature before serving.

Submitter: 
Tina Andre' Fox
Description: 
This vineagrette is so easy to make and very delicious.  Whenever I serve this on a salad, I get asked for the recipe.  I love it on leafy greens, keeps in the refrigerator for at least 4 weeks if not more.
