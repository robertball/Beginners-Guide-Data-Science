Title:
Grammy Clark's Sweet Brown Bread (AKA Molasses Bread) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Molasses
1 Tbsp Shortening
1 Cup Rolled Oats
1 tsp Salt
2 Cups boiling water
1 Cup warm water
1 tsp sugar
2 pkgs of yeast (regular, not quick yeast)
6 Cups of flour
Butter for brushing

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together molasses, shortening, rolled oats, salt and boiling water (first 5 ingredients in the list).  Stir
In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar and yeast (next 3 ingredients in list).  Do NOT stir.  Let sit/rise for 10 minutes.
Pour yeast into molasses mixture.
Add 6 Cups of flour (1 cup at a time), stir.  Knead once dough becomes too tough to stir.
Add enough flour to take stickiness out of dough.
Wash out bowl and grease with shortening.
Put dough in bowl and grease top of it.  Cover with towel and place in oven (with light on) until it doubles in size.
Grease bread pans, place rolled dough in pans and let sit to rise again (1hr or so).
Bake at 350 for 40 mins.
Butter tops when done.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
Delicious and sweet brown oat bread...I still remember the smell wafting through the house every time we visited grammy Clark.  She would serve with it with molasses beans & wieners.
