Title:
Chicken & Asparagus in white wine with Garlic Mashers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 6-oz chicken breasts
3/4 t salt
1/2 t black pepper
2 TB butter
1/2 c flour
1/2 c dry white wine
1/2 c fat free, sodium free chicken broth
3 garlic cloves
1 lb asparagus, trimmed
2 TB parsley
1 TB lemon juice
1 1/2 of potatoes of choice
4 garlic cloves
kosher salt

Directions:
Peel potatoes and start boiling, while butter melts in pan, pound chicken then salt and pepper, dredge chicken in flour, cook chicken 3 min on each side, remove from pan and keep warm.  Add wine, broth, and garlic to the pan, scraping loose brown bits, cook for 2 min.  Add asparagus, cover and cook three minutes until crisp-tender.  Remove from heat and stir in parsley and juice.  Mash potatoes and add garlic and kosher salt.

Submitter: 
Laura Leh
Description: 
Works well with any green veggie. Nice dinner on a cold winter night.
