Title:
Chipotle Cheddar Beer Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ½ cup flour
½ cup sugar
2 ½ teaspoons baking powder
½ teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 ½ cup shredded cheese
3 diced chipotles in adobo (vary in size, adjust to taste)
12 oz. beer of choice

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 375.
2) Grease a loaf pan.
3) Mix all ingredients except for beer with a large whisk.
4) Mix in beer until just combined. Pour into loaf pan.
5) Bake for 50-60 minutes until golden brown and fork piercing the center comes out clean.
6) Wait 10 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
Leah Joy
Description: 
I love this hearty dense bread served along side some taco chili or tortilla soup. Adjust chipotle amount to your desired heat level! And don't forget to top your slice with a little butter!
