Title:
Jalapeno Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2883539.jpg
Ingredients:
6 summer yellow squash, sliced
2  medium yellow onions, sliced
8 oz. can pickled sliced jalapeno
2 c. shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided
3 eggs
2 c. heavy cream
salt and pepper

Directions:
Grease a 2 quart casserole dish.
In a mixing bowl, combine eggs, heavy cream and salt and pepper.
Layer the squash, 1 /2 c. cheese, onion, egg mixture and jalapenos in the prepared casserole dish like you would with a potato gratin.
Top with remaining cheese and wrap with foil.
Bake for 45 minutes.
Take off foil and bake another 20 minutes or until the top is golden and bubbly.
