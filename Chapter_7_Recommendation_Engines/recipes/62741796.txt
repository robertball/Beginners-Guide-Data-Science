Title:
Chelsea's cheesecake bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tubes crescent rolls
1 egg
1 cup sugar
2 8 oz cream cheese
1 T sugar
1 T cinnamon

Directions:
press 1 tube rolls into greased 13x9 pan
beat egg, sugar, and cheese together and add to pan
lay another rolls ontop
sprinkle cinnamon and sugar on top
Bake 375 30 min
