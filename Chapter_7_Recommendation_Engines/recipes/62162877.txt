Title:
Sausage Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bread - 8 slices
Mozzarella Cheese - 2 cups
Sausage - 750 gm
Eggs - 8
Dry Mustard - 3/4 tsp
Milk - 3.5 cups
Cream of Mushroom Soup - 1 can

Directions:
Brown the sausage in pan
Grate cheese
Tear bread into small cubes
Mix eggs, mustard and 2.5 cups milk and beat it together
Take a baking tray.
Layer bread on bottom, then cheese and sausage and finish with egg mixture on top
Refrigerate overnight
Before baking, dilute mushroom soup with 1 cup milk, pour on top and bake at 300F for about 90 minutes
