Title:
Blueberry Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/688747.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 cup milk
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
1 cup flour
1 tablespoon sugar
3 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup blueberries

Directions:
Add dry ingredients to liquid ingredients
Blend until flour is moistened
Add blueberries and mix until thoroughly mixed
Grease frying pan lightly with oil
Pour batter from tip of a large spoon to make pools of batter slightly apart
Turn pancakes as soon as they are puffed and full of bubbles
Serve immediately with syrup

Description: 
A great way to start your morning! These pancakes are delicious! Good for breakfast lunch or supper!
