Title:
Crispy French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992098.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup egg substitute
1/2 cup fat-free milk
1/4 cup orange juice
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 dash ground nutmeg
12 (3/4 inch thick) slices day-old French bread
1 1/2 cups crushed cornflakes

Directions:
In a shallow dish, combine the egg substitute, milk, orange juice, vanilla and nutmeg.  Add bread; soak for 5 minutes, turning once.  Coat both sides of each slice with cornflake crumbs.  Place in a 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan coated with nonstick cooking spray.  Bake at 425 degrees F for 10 minutes; turn.  Bake 5-8 minutes longer or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Flo  Burtnett
Description: 
Field editor Flo Burtnett, Gage, Oklahoma, lightens up her golden French toast with egg substitute and skim milk, then flavors it with orange juice, vanilla and a dash of nutmeg. The cornflake coating adds a fun crunch.
