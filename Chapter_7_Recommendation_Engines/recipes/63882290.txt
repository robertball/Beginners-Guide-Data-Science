Title:
No-Bake Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2 tablespoons cocoa
1 stick butter or margarine
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
3 cups uncooked Quaker old-fashioned or Quick(1 minute) oats

Directions:
Lay out sheets of wax paper on a table or similar surface.
Combine sugar, cocoa, butter and milk in a 3 or 4 qt pot. Cook this mixture one minute or until butter is melted and mixture is mostly smooth.
Add in peanut butter, vanilla and oats, stirring well until oats are coated and peanut butter is combined.
Immediately remove from heat and spoon mixture onto wax paper. Let stand 2-3 hours to fully set.

Description: 
Sometimes called hobo cookies, these quick and easy cookies are delicious and a tradition in my family!
