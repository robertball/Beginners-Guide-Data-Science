Title:
Barbecue sour cream pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 loin pork chops
1/2 cup water
2 TB brown sugar
2 TB chopped onion
1 beef bouillon cube or 1 tsp. instant beef bouillon
2 TB catsup or chili sauce
1 garlic clove, minced
2 TB flour or cornstarch
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
In a large skillet with a little oil or butter, brown chops.  Add 1/2 cup water, brown sugar, onion, catsup, garlic and bouillon and stir.  Cover and simmer for approximately 40 minutes or until tender.  Remove chops.  In a small bowl, combine flour with 1/4 cup water and make paste.  Slowly add to the broth, stirring constantly until thickened.  On a low heat, stir in sour cream but do not boil.  Serve over chops.  Great with rice!

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
I had some sour cream leftover so I threw it in with the tomato sauce and I think these turned out great!  Not too barbecuey but still has a great barbecue flavor!!!
