Title:
Italian Beef Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4-pound boneless sirloin or rolled rump roast, trimmed and cut in 2 to 3-inch pieces
1/2 C water
1 0.7-ounce package Italian salad dressing mix
2 t Italian seasoning, crushed
1/2 to 1 t. crushed red pepper
1/2 t garlic powder
10 to 12 Kaiser rolls or other sandwich rolls, split or 1 loaf French bread, split
Purchased roasted red pepper strips (optional)

Directions:
Put beef in an electric crockery cooker. Stir together water, dressing mix, Italian seasoning, crushed red pepper, and garlic powder; pour over beef. Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 10 to 12 hrs. (Or cover and cook on high setting for 5 to 6 hrs).
Remove meat with a slotted spoon. Using 2 forks, shred beef. Serve beef on rolls. Top w/roasted red pepper strips, if desired. Drizzle w/ some of the juices to moisten.
