Title:
Mama's Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4528643.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Gevalia® Cold Brew Concentrate - House Blend
1 cup cookies and cream ice cream
1 tablespoon half-and-half
1/4 teaspoon almond extract
1 chocolate wafer cookie, crumbled (optional)
Whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
Combine cold brew concentrate, ice cream, half-and-half, and almond extract in blender.
Blend on high for 30 to 60 seconds until it reaches desired consistency.
Top with crushed cookies and whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Bari Oglesbee
Description: 
This milkshake made with Gevalia® Cold Brew Concentrate and cookies and cream ice cream is the perfect way to cool off on a warm summer afternoon.
