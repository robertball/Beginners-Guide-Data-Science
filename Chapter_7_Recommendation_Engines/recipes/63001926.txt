Title:
Italian Cupboard Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/755921.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz great northern beans (dry)
1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
10 oz fresh curly spinach leaves
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp garlic powder
5 cups water
1 small ham hock
Salt to taste

Directions:
Cover white beans with at least an inch of water and let soak overnight (about 12 hours) in room temperature water.
After soaking, rinse beans in colander.
Add ham hock, beans, and tomatoes, basil, garlic powder, oregano, and water to crockpot.
Cook in crockpot on high for 3 hours or low for 6 hours.
Wash, tear into small pieces, and wilt spinach in a pan, then add cooked spinach to soup.
