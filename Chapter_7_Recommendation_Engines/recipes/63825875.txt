Title:
Vegan Gluten Free Carrot Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Rye Flour
1/2 cup Brown Rice Flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon clove
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon allspice
1/4 cup sucanat
1 cup carrots
1/2 cup walnuts
3 tablespoons chia seeds
1 cup warm water
1/4 cup agave
1/2 cup almond milk

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees.
In large bowl combine flours, spices, sucanat, baking powder and salt.
Mix thoroughly.
In a blender, soak chia seeds in water for about 5 minutes.
Add carrots and agave.
Blend completely.
Add chia seed mixture to flour.
Mix thoroughly
Add walnuts
Transfer to bread pan
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes

Description: 
Vegan Gluten Free Carrot Bread. Made with agave and sucanat, this bread is fat-free and a low-glycemic alternative.
