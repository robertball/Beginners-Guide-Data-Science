Title:
Avocado Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/30013.jpg
Ingredients:
1 baguette, cut into 1/4 inch slices
Olive oil cooking spray
1 large Roma tomato, chopped into 1/4 inch cubes
4 fresh basil leaves, thinly sliced
1 ripe Hass avocado peeled, pitted and chopped into 1/4 inch cubes
1 garlic clove, finely chopped
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon white balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Place baguette slices on cookie sheets and spray with olive oil cooking spray. Toast in 350 degrees F oven for about 8 minutes or until lightly browned. Remove, cool and set aside.
In bowl gently mix tomatoes, basil, avocado, garlic, oil and balsamic vinegar.
Spoon avocado mixture onto toasted baguette slices.

Submitter: 
Fresh Chilean Hass Avocados
Description: 
Toasted baguette slices are topped with tomato, basil, and creamy avocado.
