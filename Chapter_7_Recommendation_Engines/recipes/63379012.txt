Title:
Penny's Best Chicken in Wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/922362.jpg
Ingredients:
12 Saltine Crackers Crumbled
1/2 Cup Butter
4 lbs Boneless Chicken Breasts cut into 1 inch pieces
12 Fresh Mushrooms sliced
1 Medium Onion Chopped
1/2 Cup Chicken Broth
1/2 Cup White Wine
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 Dash of Tarragon (Optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Dip Chicken in cracker crumbs and let stand for 10 minutes.
Melt 1/2 Cup of Butter in frying pan and brown chicken pieces, reserve.
Remove chicken to cassarole dish.
Add chopped onion and sliced mushrooms to frying pan and then saute until onions are translucent.
Add 1/2 cup of chicken broth and 1/2 cup of white wine to the frying pan with Salt, pepper and tarragon and then let come to a boil.
Pour mixture over the chicken, cover and bake for 45 minutes at 375 degrees.

Submitter: 
Howard
Description: 
A wonderful family recipe that we have turned to often over the years.  This simple dish is delicious and best served for its comforting warmth during the cooler months.
