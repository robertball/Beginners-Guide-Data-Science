Title:
Chicken and Black Bean Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T vegetable oil
1/2 C onion, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 1/2 C cooked chicken breast, cubed
1 (15.5) oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 1/2 C colby jack cheese, shredded
1/2 T lime juice
1 t chili powder
1 t cumin
1/8 t garlic powder
Salt and Pepper to taste
15 sheets (9" x 14") fillo dough, thawed
6 T butter, melted

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet.
Add onions and peppers and saute for 5-7 minutes.
In a large mixing bowl combine sauteed vegetables, cooked chicken, black beans, cheese, lime juice, chili powder, cumin and garlic powder; mix lightly.  Salt and pepper to taste.
Layer 5 sheets of fillo, brushing each with melted butter.  Repeat 2 more times.
Cut width of dough in half.
Place about 1 cup of filling evenly down long side of fillo, about 1" from bottom edge and 1/2" in from both sides.
Start rolling from edge containing filling.
Once filling is enclosed, fold over exposed edges.
Repeat for remaining rolls.
Score top to vent.
Brush outside with butter.
Bake rolls seam side down, atleast 1" apart, on ungreased cookie sheet in preheated 350 degree oven about 30-35 minutes.

Submitter: 
FoodFan
Description: 
A flavorful burrito made with fillo dough.  Serve with sour cream and salsa.
