Title:
Creamy Cavatappi Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound dry Cavatappi Pasta
1 Tbs Olive Oil
1 Tbs Butter
2 cloves garlic
1 tsp dried basil
1 Cup Heavy Cream
6 Campari tomatoes, quartered
1/4 Cup grated Fontinella or (Romano or Parmesean) cheese
1/4 cup fresh flat leaf parsley, chopped
Salt and Pepper to taste
2 Tbs of lemon juice

Directions:
Cook Cavatappi according to directions on box
Heat oil and butter over low temp
add garlic and sweat 1 minute
add basil and cream, cook 6 minutes until it thickens a little
add tomatoes, cheese, parsley, salt, pepper and lemon juice to the hot cooked  pasta
pour the garlic cream sauce over the pasta and stir gently
ENJOY!

Submitter: 
TERRIS911
Description: 
This is such a wonderful side dish to chicken or shrimp, but I have even made it as a meal all by itself, be sure your tomatoes are ripe, and if it's winter, the little salad tomatoes work well too.
