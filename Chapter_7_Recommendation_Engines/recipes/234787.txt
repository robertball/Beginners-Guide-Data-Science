Title:
Idahoan® Picnic Dog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1052979.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Idahoan® Buttery Homestyle Mashed Potato Cup
4 Kosher-style hot dogs
3 tablespoons stone ground mustard
4 potato flour buns
Celery, chopped
Radishes, finely sliced
Kosher pickle spears
Yellow mustard
Red onion, finely diced

Directions:
Grill hot dogs until done. Prepare Idahoan Buttery Homestyle Mashed Potato Cup according to package directions and stir in stone ground mustard. Place not dog on bun and spread potato mixture on top. Add toppings to your taste. No ketchup needed!

Submitter: 
Idahoan
Description: 
Grilled hot dogs on potato bread buns are topped with mashed potatoes with mustard and your choice of toppings.
