Title:
Easy Elegant Baked Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb basa fish fillets
1/2 stick melted butter
1 lemon sliced thinly
1 TBS capers
Garlic powder
Garlic pepper seasoning
Salt to taste

Directions:
Put fish fillets in baking pan.  Drizzle with melted butter.  Season with garlic powder, garlic pepper seasoning, salt to taste.  Top with sliced lemons and capers.
Bake 15-20 min until fish is white and flakes easily with a fork

Submitter: 
Mair M
Description: 
I use basa fish fillets.  They are mild, sweet, firm-textured and can be dressed up with just about anything.  There aren't many recipes for basa out there, so I created my own, using my favorite flavors.
