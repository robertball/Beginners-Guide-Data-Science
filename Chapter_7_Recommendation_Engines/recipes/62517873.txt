Title:
Chicken Tallerinni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken breasts
1 can diced green chili's (4ounces)
1/4 cup butter
1 onion
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 jalapeno chopped (optional)
2 10.75 ounce cans cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup milk
1 pound Velvetta cheese
3 Tablespoons butter, melted
1/2 sleeve Town House Original crackers
1 pound angel hair spaghetti noodles

Directions:
Fill a large pot halfway with water. Add the chicken and cook until done about 45 minutes. Remove the chicken and set aside. In the water you cooked the chicken add the spaghetti noodles and cook for 10 minutes drain and set aside. In a large pot saute the onions, jalapeno and bell pepper with 1/4 cup butter. Add the diced chili's, cream of mushroom soup, milk and cheese. Cook until the cheese has melted. Chop the chicken into bite size pieces. Add the spaghetti and chicken and mix completely. Place in a 9x13 inch pan. In a small bowl mix the crushed crackers and 3 tablespoon of melted butter. Sprinkle over the top of the casserole and bake at 350 for 15 to 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
Creamy chicken casserole even the kids will love. One of my sons favorites he requests it often.
