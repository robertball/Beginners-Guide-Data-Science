Title:
Super Bowl Cheesy Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4468768.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Salsa or Salsa Verde
2 inch slice of Velveeta (approx 8 Tablespoons)
2 Tablespoons Cream Cheese (I used Garlic Herb)
1/2 can of refried beans (typical 16 oz can so 8 oz)
Salt/Pepper to taste
Tortilla or Nacho Chips.

Directions:
Melt the Salsa, Cream Cheese and Velveeta cheese in microwave for 45 seconds on High.
Stir ingredients and microwave an additional 45 seconds on High.
Add refried beans and microwave an additional 45 seconds on High.
Add salt/pepper to taste and serve with tortilla or nacho chips.

Submitter: 
Holleta
Description: 
A last minute dip I threw together with ingredients on hand 20 minutes before the Super Bowl that turned out to be the real winner of the Super Bowl! Refried Beans, Cheese, Salsa and Cream Cheese...That's it!
