Title:
Nisha's Cream Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1070234.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/2 cup butter
1 cup flour
4 eggs

Directions:
Bring water and butter to a boil. Remove from heat and add flour. Stir until mixture forms a ball.
Beat in eggs one at a time, mixing well. Drop mixture by the spoonful on to cookie sheet (with parchment paper).
Bake at 400 F for 35-40 minutes.
cool and add desired filling (cool whip, instant puddings, toppings, chocolate, fruit, etc...sprinkled with powdered sugar).
