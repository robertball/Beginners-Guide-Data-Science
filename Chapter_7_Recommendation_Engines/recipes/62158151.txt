Title:
Spicy Thai Steak and Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/290598.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 lb. top loin steak
1/2 of a carrot cut in thin matchsticks
1 celery stalk, sliced thin
3 green onions, sliced thin
4 mushrooms, sliced thin
1/4c red pepper, cut in thin strips
2c shredded cabbage
1c bean sprouts
2 packages of beef Ramen noodles
1 1/2c water
1/2c beef broth
1T soy sauce
2-3T Thai chili peanut stir fry sauce
1/8t ground ginger
2T olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook noodles in 1 1/2 c water and ONE seasoning packet per package directions.
While noodles are cooking, slice steak into 1/4 inch strips.
Get a large frying pan or wok hot and add 1T olive oil. Stir fry steak until med. rare.
Remove from pan and set aside.
In same pan add 1T olive oil, carrot, celery, green onions, and red pepper.  Saute 2 min.
Add soy sauce, ginger, and chili peanut sauce to 1/2c beef broth and whisk together.
Add mushrooms, bean sprouts, and cabbage to pan and pour sauce over all.  Cover and cook additional 3 min.
Toss cooked noodles and steak in pan with vegetables.
Add salt and fresh ground pepper to taste and serve in bowls.

Submitter: 
Audrey Ansell
Description: 
Ramen noodles and colorful vegetables make steak and cabbage a simple stir fry that is on the table in no time. Serve it with chop sticks and they'll think you ordered take out!
