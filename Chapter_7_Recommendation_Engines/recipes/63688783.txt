Title:
Chorizo soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Chorizo sausage cooked and drained
2 lg onions sliced thinly
2 c. Long Grain wild rice
6 cups water
1/2 c. Burgundy wine ( any dark red wine will do but I prefer burgundy for this.
Salt and Pepper
beef base

Directions:
Cook Chorizo in med skillet at med hi heat till done. Boil rice, set both aside. saute onions in butter, add Burgundy reduce by half. Heat water to a boil add beef base stir till dissolved add onions, chorizo and rice, salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Ken Spaulding
Description: 
I created this one for cold days when I was in Montana it is pretty spicy and will take the chill out of ya
