Title:
Clare's Gluten Free Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup gluten free flour (almond and sweet rice flour combo)
1 cup sugar (brown or white)
5 tablespoons cocoa
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup shortening (butter, or GF margarine) softened
2 eggs
1 teaspoon bourbon vanilla extract

Directions:
To make it easy, Clare melts the butter in a glass bowl in a microwave, then adds the rest of the ingredients.
Add 1/2 cup of nuts if desired.
Spread in an 8x8-inch baking pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes. Cool, Cut, wrap and freeze.
Note: to make these brownies minty, add 2 teaspoons peppermint extract to the batter.
