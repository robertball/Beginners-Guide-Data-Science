Title:
Caprese Sausage Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/884509.jpg
Ingredients:
1 - 12 OZ. package fully cooked al fresco Sundried Tomato Chicken Sausage or fully cooked al freso Sweet Italian Chicken Sausage
1 - 8 OZ. carton Ciliegine (small fresh Mozzarella balls)
1 - 8 OZ. jar Sun-Dried tomato halves, packed in olive oil
24 fresh basil leaves

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400.
Trim the tips off each sausage link and cut each link into six equal sized slices.
Put the sausages in a large non stick skillet, cut side down.
Cook on each side for 3 minutes over medium heat until lightly browned on each cut side.
While cooking sausage, cut twelve small mozzarella balls in half.
Cut 12 sun-dried tomatoes in half lengthwise.
Remove sausage from skillet to shallow baking pan.
Put half a mozzarella ball on top of each sausage slice.
Top each sausage/cheese stack with a slice of sun-dried tomato.
Bake for 5 to 8 minutes or until heated through.
Place a basil leaf on top of each sausage/cheese stack and serve.

Description: 
The freshness of Caprese with a savory bite of sausage is the perfect marriage. If you have four simple ingredients, you can dazzle your drop in guests in minutes! The bonus is that this little appetizer is low fat.
