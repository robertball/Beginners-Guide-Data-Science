Title:
Homemade Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2496465.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cherry tomatoes (basket of, chopped.)
2 mango (a little small cubed.)
1 shallots (choped.)
2 lime juice
1/2 tsp salt
1 cilantro (branch, finely chop.)
1 jalapeno chilies (a little small cubed)

Directions:
Mixed all ingredients in the medium mixing bowl until it well combine. Serve with your favorite tortilla chips or chips.

Submitter: 
natty's pantry
Description: 
This well balance homemade mango salsa is super refreshing-Balance taste from mango and cherry tomato. Go well with seafood dishes also
