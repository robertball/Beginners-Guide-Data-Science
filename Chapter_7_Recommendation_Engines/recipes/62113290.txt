Title:
Hawaiian/Chinese Haystacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can pineapple tidbits
1 green pepper
2 Tbsp oil
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 C cooked and cut up chicken pieces
2 Tbsp soy sauce
3 C cooked rice
*veggies
*chow mein crunchy noodles

Directions:
Brown chicken pieces in skillet w/oil. Once browned, removed any extra oil and add some pineapple juice to chicken. Sear for just a minute, add soy sauce.
Steam rice, veggies, green pepper, etc.
Heat up cream of chicken soup.
Layering Order Bottom to Top: Rice, Veggies, Chicken, Pineapple Tidbits, Cream of Chicken Soup (drizzled), Chow Mein Noodles

Submitter: 
LeviCooks
Description: 
I've heard these called Hawaiian Haystacks and Chinese Haystacks so whichever they are, they're great!
