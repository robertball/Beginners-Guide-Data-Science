Title:
Butternut Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium butternut squash, baked and cooled
1 whole onion, chopped
1 tablespoon ginger, grated
6 cups chicken broth
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup sour cream
sliced green apple for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Saute onion adn giner in 2 tablespoons oil over medium heat until softened.
Scrape squash out of shell into stock pot and add chicken broth.  Bring to a boil.
Pour soup into a blender and blend until smooth.  (May have to be done in stages, depending on capacity of bledner or food processor).
Wipe stockpot clean with a paper towel to remove any remaining bits and pour blanded soup back into pot.  Add salt and pepper adn bring to a boil once more.
Ladle into bowls, garnish with apple slices and/or sour cream, if desired.  Serve with sour cream.
