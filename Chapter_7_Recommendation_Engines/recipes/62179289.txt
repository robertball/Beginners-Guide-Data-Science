Title:
Award Winning Ham Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ham Balls:
1 1/4 # ground smoked ham
1 # lean ground pork
1/2 # ground beef
2 eggs
1 1/2 C. crushed graham crackers
1 C. milk
Sauce:
1 can tomato soup
3 T. vinegar
1 1/2 C. brown sugar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients for ham balls in a large bowl.
Form into small balls and place side-by-side in a 9"x13" baking dish (you may need more than one).
Mix ingredients for sauce and pour over balls.
Bake at a preheated 325 degree F oven for 45-55 minutes.

Submitter: 
Whitney
Description: 
Delicious; the other meats make it not too hammy, and the sauce is the perfect sweetness to compliment the meat.
