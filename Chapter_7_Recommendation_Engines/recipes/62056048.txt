Title:
Spicy Cinnamon Peanuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of GOOD honey-roasted peanuts
1-2 tsp. Cinnamon

Directions:
Stir the cinnamon into the peanuts, pour into serving dish and enjoy!  This is a recipe that sounds simple but is so so good.  The spicy cinnamon adds a taste and texture that really makes this average snack food pop.  It looks really nice filling brandy snifters at a party!
