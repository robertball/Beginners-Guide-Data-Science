Title:
Jambalaya with Chicken Breast and Chorizo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 chicken breasts, thin cut, thinly sliced
1 package hot, smoked, dried chorizo
1 medium-sized onion, diced
1 large green bell pepper
1 green chili pepper
1/2 14.5oz can diced tomato, drained
1Â½ cups rice
1-2 tbsp. cajun seasoning
1-2 tsp. Paprika
1 tbsp. Garlic powder
1/4 tsp. Salt
1/4 tsp Ground pepper
1 packet sugar
1-2 tbsp. vegetable oil
Water

Directions:
Using a large frying pan, heat vegetable oil, then cook onion and peppers on medium heat for about 5 minutes uncovered.
In a separate pot, begin cooking rice.
Add chicken, chorizo, and spices to pan and cover on medium-low heat.
When chicken is about 75% done, add diced tomatoes and sugar and cover.
Keep covered until rice is done, then add rice to pan, stir, and simmer for 5 minutes.
