Title:
Chocolate Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/369284.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup ground walnuts (optional)
6 tablespoons butter of margarine, softened
1/3 cup baking cocoa

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Beat flour, powdered sugar, nuts, butter and cocoa in large mixer bowl until soft dough forms.
Press dough onto bottom and up side of ungreased 9 or 9 1/2 inch fluted tart pan with removable bottom or 9-inch pie plate.
Bake for 12 to 14 minutes or until puffed.
Cool completely in pan on wire rack.

Submitter:

Description: 
A simple chocolate crust that can be used for all your favorite pies.
