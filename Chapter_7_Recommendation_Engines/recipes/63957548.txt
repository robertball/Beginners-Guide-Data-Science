Title:
White corn guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sweet white corn
1/4 cup black beans
1/4 cup jicama, finely diced
1/2 cup onion, finely diced
1/2 red bell pepper, finely diced
1/2 serrano pepper, finely diced (I omitted this)
cilantro, to your liking
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
2 avocados, finely diced
1 lime, juiced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, squeezing lime over the avocado pieces to delay browning.
