Title:
Chicken Tortolini and Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1687724.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs Boneless Skonless Chicken Breast raw and cubed
2 Cups Dried Tricolor Tortolini
2 Cups of Fresh Green-beans
1 Can of Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Can of Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 Cup of Sour Cream
2 Cups of Milk
1 Tablespoon of Dried Dill Weed
1Teaspoon of Garlic Powder
1Teaspoon of Onoin Powder
1/2 teaspoon of Ground Black Pepper
1-1/2 Cups of Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1/2 cup of Graded Parmisan Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cook green beans in boiling water for 7-10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
Cook Tortolini according to package instructions (in boiling water for about 15 minutes)  drain and set aside.
Meanwhile, mix cream of chicken soup, cream of mushroom soup, sour cream, milk, cheddar cheese, grated Partisan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, dried dill weed, and black pepper in a bowl. Stir cubed chicken into mixture.
Lightly spray a 9x13 dish with cooking spray. Spread 1/2 of the chicken/ soup mixture into pan. Add green beans, then add cooked Tortolini. Spread remaining chicken/ soup mixture over the top. Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour.
