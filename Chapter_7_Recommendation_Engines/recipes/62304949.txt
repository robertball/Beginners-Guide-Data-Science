Title:
Benji's Parmesean Roux Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Stick of Butter
2/3 Cup of Flour
1/2 Cup of Milk
3/4 Cup Grated Parmesean Cheese

Directions:
In small saucepan, melt butter.
Once butter is fully melted, add flour.
Whisk together until a stiff consistency forms.
Add the Milk.
Whisk.
Add the Parmesean Cheese, until stiff.
Remove from heat and cover until ready to serve.
