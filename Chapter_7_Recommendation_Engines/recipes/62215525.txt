Title:
White Fruit Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*   2/3 cup butter
* 1-1/3 cups confectioners' sugar
* 2-3/4 eggs, separated
* 1/2 ounce candied citron peel
* 1/2 ounce candied orange peel
* 1/3 cup dried currants
* 1/2 cup candied pineapple chunks
* 1 cup golden raisins
* 1/2 cup candied cherries
* 1-1/3 cups pecans, chopped
* 1 cup sifted all-purpose flour
* 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons orange juice

Directions:
Chop pineapple, raisins, and cherries.  Combine chopped fruit with currants, peel, and citron; soak in orange juice overnight.
Preheat oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C). Place a small pan of water in the oven.  Line one 5 x 9 inch loaf pan with parchment or doubled waxed paper.
In a large bowl, cream butter and confectioner's sugar.  Stir in beaten egg yolks.  Stir in fruit, juice, and nuts.  Mix in sifted flour.
In a clean bowl, beat the egg whites to peaks.  Fold into batter.  Fill pans 2/3 full.
Bake for 1 to 1 1/2 hours until golden brown, or until toothpick comes out clean when inserted.
