Title:
13 Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb mixed beans
1 medium Onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 tbs. chili powder
1 lb veggie protein sausage
1 16 oz canned tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. cumin
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Wash beans thoroughly; cover with water and soak overnight.  Drain beans.  Add all the above ingredients except sausage to beans.  Cover with water and bring to boil; reduce heat and simmer 1-2 hours, until beans are tender.  Add browned pan sausage to beans and cook until sausage flavor is well blended into the beans.
