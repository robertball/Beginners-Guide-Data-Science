Title:
Lamb shanks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs  Lamb shank
3 Gr salt
2 gr red pepper
1.5 gr Nutmeg
1 lb onion
1 lb carrots
0.25 kg celery
0.5 gr thyme
Lamb gravy

Directions:
Cut the lamb shank at the bone side in 2 and take out the parts of the meat from the middle (left and right)
These parts are the tender ones.  The other meat you free of from all of the white meat.
Spice the tender ones and prepare the oven pan with all of the vegetables.
Brown the tender ones on all sides then put them in the oven at 167F for 3 hours.
For the ragout, cut the rest of the meat into small pieces, brown them with some carrots, celery and onion.  Cover with gravy, and simmer for 2.5 hours.
