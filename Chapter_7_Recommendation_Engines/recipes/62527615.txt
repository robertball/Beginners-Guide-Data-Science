Title:
Savory Roasted Root Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup diced, raw beet
4 carrots, diced
1 onion, diced
1/2 lb brussel sprouts, quartered
2 cups diced yams
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 parsnip, diced
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon dried thyme leaves(or part sage)
salt and pepper to taste
1/3 cup dry white wine ( can us some Balsamic v.)
1/4 lb bacon, diced
1 small apple, cored, diced

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Place the beet, carrot, onion, potatoes, garlic, yams, sprouts into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Drizzle with the olive oil, then season with thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
Bake, uncovered, in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, stirring once midway through baking.
Cook bacon untl crisp, then add apple and cook until softens, about 2 minutes. Remove the baking dish from the oven, and stir in the wine, bacon and apples. Return to the oven, and bake until the wine has mostly evaporated and the vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes more. Season to taste with salt and pepper before serving.
