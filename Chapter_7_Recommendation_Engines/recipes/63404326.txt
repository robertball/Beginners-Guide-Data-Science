Title:
Greek Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. shelled, uncooked shrimp
1/2 C. olive oil
salt and pepper
4 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
1/2 C. finely chopped parsley
1 tomato, sliced very thin
1/4 lb. feta cheese, crumbled

Directions:
Wash and dry shrimp.  Saute shrimp in hot oil for 5 minutes.  Remove to baking dish.  Season with salt and pepper.  Add chopped garlic and parsley to the oil remaining in the pan and cook for 1 minute.  Pour this sauce over the shrimp.  Arrange slices of tomato over shrimp and sprinkle feta cheese over all.  When ready to serve, heat in 350 degree oven until hot.  Broil briefly to brown, if desired.  Serve with rice.
