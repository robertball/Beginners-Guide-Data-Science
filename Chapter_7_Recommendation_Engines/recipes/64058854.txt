Title:
Lemon-Basil  Quinoa  Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa (rinsed very well)
2 cups chicken broth(low sodium)
1 large lemon, zested and juiced
1/2 cup drained and diced roasted red peppers
1/4 cup dried cranberries(low sugar)
2 tablespoons minced red onion
2 tablespoons minced fresh basil.

Directions:
In a pot bring quinoa and broth to a boil. Reduce heat and let simmer 15-20 minutes. When cooked put  cooked quinoa in a bowl, toss with  1 tablespoon lemon juice, and the lemon zest, add remaining ingredients and toss.
