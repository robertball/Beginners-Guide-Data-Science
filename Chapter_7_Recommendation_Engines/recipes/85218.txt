Title:
Orange and Radish Salad with Cinnamon Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
Vinaigrette:
1/3 cup olive oil
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 tablespoons orange juice
2 1/2 teaspoons sugar
1 teaspoon McCormick速 Ground Cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon McCormick速 Ground Cumin
1/8 teaspoon McCormick速 Ground Red Pepper
1/8 teaspoon salt
Salad:
3 seedless oranges, peeled and thinly sliced
1 bunch fresh radishes, thinly sliced
1 (6 ounce) package baby spinach
1/4 cup sliced almonds, lightly toasted

Directions:
In a small bowl, blend vinaigrette ingredients. Set aside.
In a large bowl, combine all salad ingredients except almonds.
Just before serving, toss half of vinaigrette with salad mixture. Sprinkle with almonds. Serve remaining vinaigrette at the table.

Submitter: 
McCormick速 & Company
Description: 
Try this memorable salad that includes juicy oranges, fresh radishes, baby spinach and almonds tossed with a light vinaigrette featuring cinnamon, lemon and orange juices. It's a perfect salad to top with leftover turkey strips for a light main dish, too.
