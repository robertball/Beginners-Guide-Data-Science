Title:
Pico Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10oz pkg chopped frozen spinach, thawed and drained
1 14oz can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 tbsp chopped garlic
1/2 cup light mayonnaise
1 8oz light cream cheese, softened
1 3oz light cream cheese, softened
juice from 1/2 a lemon
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup grated romano cheese
1 12oz container of fresh pico de gallo (tomatoes,cilantro,onion,jalepeno)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Spray 8x8 baking dish with non-stick cooking spray
Mix cream cheese and mayonnaise until smooth. Then mix in remaining ingredients.  Spread into prepared baking dish.
Bake covered until heated through (approximately 20 minutes).

Submitter: 
Aimee
Description: 
Excellent hot dip. Serve with tortilla chips or pita chips.
