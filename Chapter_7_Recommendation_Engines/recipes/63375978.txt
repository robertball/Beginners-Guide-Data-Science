Title:
Sweet Apple Chicken Sausage Waldorf Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 12 oz packages Sweet Apple Chicken Sausage
3-4 apples
1/4 cup golden raisins
1/4 cup walnuts
2 tbsps balsamic or plain vinaigrette
1 bunch Arugula
wraps or ciabatta rolls

Directions:
Rinse arugula and place in bowl.
Add apples, raisins, walnuts and toss with vinaigrette.
Grill chicken sausage in a skillet and cook over medium high heat until sausages are browned.
Remove from heat and cut into Â¼ inch slices.
Add sausage pieces to salad and toss. Serve.
IMPORTANT! Vinaigrettes may contain gluten, please read nutritionals carefully and skip the wrap and/or ciabatta rolls. Look for "Gluten Free" dressings at your local grocer.
