Title:
The Lemon Family's Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 tomatoes, red and green, cut into chunks
6 onions, large, chunked
6 bell peppers
12 anaheim chiles
10 garlic cloves
3/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup vinegar
1 Tbsp. black pepper
2 Tbsp. salt

Directions:
Puree tomatoes, onions, pepper, chiles and garlic.  Add to a large stock pot along with the rest of the ingredients.  Simmer and stir 3 hours or to desired thickness.  Put into pints and hot water bath for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
drjwall
Description: 
My son loves this salsa the most, so it's often what I bottle with the garden tomatoes!
