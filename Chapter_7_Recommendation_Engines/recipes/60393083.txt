Title:
Roasted Potato Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sweet potatoes
4 YukonGold potatoes
8 New potatoes
1/4 cup plus 2 TBS olive oil
1 tsp. dried tarragon
1/8 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.  Peel and cube the sweet potatoes and Yukon Gold potatoes.  Scrub the New potatoes and cut into cubes.
Place the potatoes in a large saucepan.  Add enough lightly salted water to cover the potatoes.  Bring to a boil and cook potatoes for 3 min.  Drain thoroughly.
Spread the potatoes in a single layer on a large nonstick baking sheet.
Drizzle the potatoes with olive oil and sprinkle with tarragon, salt and pepper.  Roast the potatoes until browned and crisp, about 25min.  Serve immediately.
