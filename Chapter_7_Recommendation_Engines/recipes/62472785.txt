Title:
Oven Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups low fat buttermilk
1 Tbsp. Cajun seasoning, divided
1 chicken (4 lbs.), cut in 8 pieces, skinned
2 Tbsp. fresh thyme leaves
4 cups cornflakes, crushed
Cooking spray

Directions:
Combine buttermilk and 1 1/2 tsp. seasoning.
Soak chicken in buttermilk mixture 1/2 hour.
Heat oven to 425 degrees.
Combine remaining ingredients; coat chicken with cornflake mixture, then cooking spray.
Bake on greased baking sheet 45 minutes or until crisp.
