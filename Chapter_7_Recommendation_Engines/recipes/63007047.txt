Title:
Easy Caramel Custard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750910.jpg
Ingredients:
Caramel Sauce
1-2 TBSP hot water
4 TBSP sugar
1 TBSP regular water
Custard mix
2 eggs
1 cup milk
5 TBSP sugar

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees. For Caramel, boil water and sugar DO NOT STIR while boiling,  only shake pan while boiling. Once it turns golden brown, add 1 TBSP hot water. For custard mix, beat 2 eggs, heat milk and sugar, make sure to stir the mix, do not let milk boil, just desolve the sugar. Once desolved, add milk to egg mixture. Pour caramel mix into a pan, and then pour in the egg mixture. in a bigger pan than the one you are using, pour enough hot water to cover the bottom half of the custard pan. Place in oven. Take out with oven mitts, and allow to cool for 5-10 minutes. Once cooled place in refrigerator.
