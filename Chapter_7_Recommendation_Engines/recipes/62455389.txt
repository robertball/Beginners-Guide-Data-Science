Title:
Roasted Red Pepper Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med. Red Pepper, stem & seeds removed
1 can Garbanzo Beans, drained, juice reserved
1/3 c. Tahini
1/4 c. Lemon Juice (or less if you don't like tart)
1/2 t. Salt
1-2 cloves Garlic, halved
1 T. Olive Oil
Paprika (dash)

Directions:
Roast pepper in 400 degree oven for 45-55 min (until skin is charred), turning once.  See note.
Using a fork, carefully peel skin off of pepper and discard.
Combine pepper, garbanzo beans, tahini, lemon juice, salt and garlic in food processor.  Blend until smooth.  Add juice from beans, if necessary, 1 tablespoon at a time.
Top with olive oil and sprinkle with Paprika to serve.

Submitter: 
Molly-Blue
Description: 
It's a family favorite!  We even made several batches and served it at my wedding.

You can use low-sodium beans for a healthier recipe.
