Title:
Plassis (African Beef & Peanut Dish) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound lean ground beef (or ground chicken)
1/3 cup chunky or smooth peanut butter
4-6 cups fresh spinach leaves (or one package frozen)
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 clove garlic (optional)

Directions:
1. Scramble-fry the ground beef over medium-high in a large frying pan until no longer pink.
2. Stir in peanut butter until well mixed through.
3. Add cayenne pepper and garlic.
4. Stir in spinach leaves until wilted.
5. Add tomatoes and simmer on low heat for 5-10 minutes or until ready to eat.  Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
MONICA DEREGT
Description: 
This unique recipe is so easy to whip up!  The ingredients provide a wonderful combination of flavours and the spinach and tomatoes create a healthy dinner choice. Measurements can be adjusted according to taste.
