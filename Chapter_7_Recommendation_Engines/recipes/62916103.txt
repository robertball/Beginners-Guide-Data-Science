Title:
Apple Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 C apples, cored, peeled and chopped
1 C sugar or 1/4 t stevia concentrated powder plus unsweetened applesauce
1 C raisins
1/2 C pecans, chopped
1/4 C veg. oil
2 t vanilla
1 egg, beaten
2 1/2 C flour, sifted
1 1/2 t baking soda
2 t cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven 350. Lightly oil 9x13 pan.
In lg. bowl, combine apples, sugar, raisins and pecans; mix well. Let sit for 30 min.
Stir in oil, vanilla and egg.
Sift together flour, soda and cinnamon; stir into apple mixture about 1/3 at a time just enough to moisten dry ingred. Turn batter into pan.
Bake 35-40 min

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Raisins and pecans are added to cake and baked in 9x13-in. pan.  Can use a sugar substitute and applesauce to decease calories
