Title:
Kate's Sour Cream, Cheddar & Scallion Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
1 Tablespoon sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
3 Tablespoons chilled butter, cut in small pieces
3/4 cup shredded sharp cheddar
1/4 cup finely chopped green onions
1 cup buttermilk
1/3 cup sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.
Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup & level with a knife.
Combine flour and next 4 ingredients in large bowl, stirring with a whisk.
Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse meal.
Add cheese and onions and toss well.
Add buttermilk and sour cream.  Stir just until moist.
Drop dough by 1/4 cup full measure onto a baking sheet coated with spray.
Bake at 450 for 15 minutes.
