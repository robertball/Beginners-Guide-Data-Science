Title:
Turkey Chorizo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
2 teaspoons chili powder
2 teaspoons smoked paprika
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon coriander
1 teaspoon dried oregano
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a medium bowl.
Mix well using your hands.
For best flavor, refrigerate 8 hours or overnight.
To cook, brown in skillet with olive oil, breaking up with a wooden spoon.

Submitter: 
Andrew Rocco
Description: 
Healthy, turkey-based chorizo sausage for use in tacos, burritos, enchiladas, eggs, etc.
