Title:
Bobby's Blueberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•1/2 cup butter, at room temperature
•3 eggs
•1-1/2 cups sugar
•3 cups all-purpose flour, sifted
•1 cup sour cream
•4 teaspoons baking powder
•1 teaspoon salt
•2 cups fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Combine all ingredients except the berries and mix well. Fold in berries. Pour into a greased and floured tube/bundt pan. Sprinkle a layer of sugar on top. Bake at 450 degrees F for 10 minutes, then reduce heat to 350 degrees F and bake for another 40 to 50 minutes. Instead of blueberries, try substituting 2 cups fresh cranberries and 1/2 cup walnuts.
