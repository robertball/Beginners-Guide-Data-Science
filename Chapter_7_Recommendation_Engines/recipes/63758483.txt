Title:
Grandad's Smoked Bacon and Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1oz butter
150g/5oz smoked streaky Bacon - chopped
1 Onion - chopped
1 Carrot - finely sliced
1 Leek - finely sliced
¼ head small Cabbage - shredded
150g/5oz Red Lentils
450ml/1pint Chicken Stock
Freshly Ground Black Pepper
Garnish: Flat Leaf Parsley - roughly chopped

Directions:
Melt butter, add bacon and fry gently for some 2 to 3 minutes or so until golden but not crisp;
Add onion, carrot, leek and fry gently for some 8 minutes or so to soften a bit;
Add lentils, cabbage and stock, bring to boil and simmer, stirring occasionally, for some 15 to 20 minutes or so or until everything is tender but the lentils are still whole, not mushy;
Then you can choose: Serve whole with veg intact or Remove from heat and “Blitz” with blender;
Return to heat, season with pepper and serve;
Garnish with parsley.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A satisfying, full flavoured comfort blanket for the Autumn and Winter months.
