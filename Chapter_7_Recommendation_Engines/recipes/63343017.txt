Title:
Scotch Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/888062.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound bulk pork sausage
Salt and pepper to taste (Optional)
7 hard-boiled eggs
3 eggs, beaten
1 cup flour
2 cup of panko bread crumbs

Directions:
Divide the sausage into six portions; flatten and sprinkle with salt and pepper (If desired). Shape each portion around a peeled hard-cooked egg. Roll in flour, then roll in beaten egg, then in the panko bread crumbs. Place One Breaded Egg in a deep fryer at 350Â° for 6-7 Min's minutes or until crust is browned. The meat should no longer pink.

Mark Farmer
Description: 
A crispy coating and pork sausage gives a different treatment to these hard-boiled eggs.
