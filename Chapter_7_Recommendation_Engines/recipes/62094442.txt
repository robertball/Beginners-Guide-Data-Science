Title:
Strawberry Chiffon Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint strawberries
3/4 cup sugar
1 Tbsp. unflavored gelatin
1/4 cup cold water
1/4 cup boiling water
1 Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup heavy cream, whipped
2 egg whites, stiffly beaten
1 graham cracker crumb pie shell

Directions:
Reserve 1/2 cup berries as garnish. Crush remainder and mix with sugar; let sit for 30 minutes. Soften gelatin in cold water for five minutes; dissolve in boiling water and mix with crushed berries, lemon juice, and salt. Cool mixture; when it begins to thicken, fold in whipped cream and beaten egg whites. Pour into pie shell and chill until firm; garnish with sweetened, flavored whipped cream and whole berries.

Submitter: 
Akatsukami
Description: 
This is a totally non-bake recipe; you don't need to turn the oven on at all.  Perfect for those hot summer days when you don't WANT to turn on the oven.
