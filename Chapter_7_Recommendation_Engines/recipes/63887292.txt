Title:
Peach and Mint Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 3 cucumbers, cut
4 to 5 peaches (preferably white), halved
a couple mint leaves
a sprinkle of salt

Directions:
Cut (and skin if desired) the cucumbers, halve the peaches, put in a pitcher of some sort. Add in mint leaves and the sprinkle of salt (to draw out the juices), swirl around gently a couple times, then leave in the fridge overnight.
