Title:
Darden gingerbread waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup molasses
1 cup sour milk
1-1/2 cup flour
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon clove
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/3 cup melted butter

Directions:
Beat eggs until fluffy and light. Add sugar, molasses and milk.
Mix dry ingredients into egg mixture and stir until smooth.
Stir in melted butter.
Cook in a waffle iron, according to manufactures instructions. Be sure to grease the waffle maker to prevent sticking.

Submitter: 
CarolynCooks
Description: 
These waffles are wonderful with fresh peaches and berries. Make this gluten free by adding 1/2 cup of each; rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca starch, and a pinch of xanthan gum.
