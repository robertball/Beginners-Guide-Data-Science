Title:
Spicy Pickled Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
2 cups vinegar
4 Tbsp pickling salt
1 Tbsp sugar
baby carrots
cloves of garlic
sliced jalapeno

Directions:
Boil water, vinegar, pickling salt and sugar.
Place 2 cloves of garlic and sliced jalapeno in the bottom of each jar.
Add baby carrots to each jar.
Top with boiled brine mixture.

Submitter: 
CLField
Description: 
A easy way to pickle carrots with no dill!
