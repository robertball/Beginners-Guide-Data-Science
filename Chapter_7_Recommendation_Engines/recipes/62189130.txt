Title:
Crockpot Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/3 cup cooking oil
3 cups water
12 oz smoked sausage, sliced and browned
1 1/2 cups cooked chicken, chopped
1 pkg frozen sliced okra
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1/2 cup chopped celery
4 garlic cloves, minced
2 bay leaves
1 tsp cajun seasoning
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp ground red pepper
1 (14oz) can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 (12oz) package frozen, shelled, devained shrimp
3 cups hot cooked rice

Directions:
1.For the roux, in a heavy 2-quart saucepan stir together the flour and oil until smooth.
2.Cook over medium-high heat for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
3.Reduce heat to medium.
4.Cook and stir constantly about 15 minutes more or until a dark, reddish-brown roux forms.
5.Cool.
6.In a 5-quart crockery cooker place water.
7.Stir in roux.
8.Add sausage, chicken, okra, onion, sweet pepper, celery, garlic, bay leaves, cajun seasoning, salt, pepper, can tomatoes, and ground red pepper.
9.Cover; cook on low-heat setting for 6 to 7 hours then add shrimp for 20 minutes.
10.Skim off fat.
11.Serve over the hot cooked rice.

Submitter:

Description: 
Traditional Gumbo, with okra, chicken, sausage and shrimp, made easier by a slow cooker.
