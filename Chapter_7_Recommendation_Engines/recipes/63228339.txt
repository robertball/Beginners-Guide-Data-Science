Title:
Parmesan-Panko Asparagus Spears Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1659645.jpg
Ingredients:
20 each medium Asparagus spears (about 1 pound or a little more)
2 egg whites
1-1/2 Tablespoons Mayonnaise
Salt and Ground black pepper to taste
2 cups Panko breadcrumbs
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
1/8 teaspoon grated Lemon peel
2 to 3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1/2 of a fresh Lemon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 Deg. F.
Snap off the woody stems from asparagus.
Mix eggs whites, mayonnaise, and salt and pepper together to blend.
On a plate or sheet of waxed paper, mix together Panko breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasonins, and grated lemon peel.
Dip each asparagus spear into the egg white-mayo mixture to cover well.  Then drop onto the Panko-Parmesan mixture to coat evenly.
Place a rack on top of a baking sheet.  Place coated asparagus spears on top of the rack.
Drizzle the spears with the olive oil.
Bake for approximately 15-17 minutes at 425 deg. F. until golden and crispy.
Remove from oven, squeeze with a little fresh lemon juice, and serve immediately.

Description: 
Asparagus spears are dipped in an egg white-mayonnaise mixture, then coated in parmesan and panko breadcrumbs, before a trip to the oven to bake in to a crunchy, delicious side dish.
