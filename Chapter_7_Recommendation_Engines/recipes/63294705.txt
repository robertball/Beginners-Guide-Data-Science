Title:
My Maple Walnut Pound Cake Recipe

Ingredients:
1     cup   butter or margarine, softened
1 1/2 cups  granulated suger
1/2   cup   packed brown suger
5 eggs,at room temperature
21/4cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp backing powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 cup milk
4 tsp maple flavavouring
1 1/2 cups  chopped walnuts
( same walnut for garnish)

Directions:
inlarge bowl thorounghly cream better and sugers, with electric mixer, ubtil fluffy, Beat in eggs one at a time. In another bowl .combine flour, baking powder and salt. In measuring cup , combine milk and maple flavouring ; beast into butter mixture alteratively with flour mixture. Mix in 1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts . Turn into greased 9 or 10 inch tube pan or bundit pan. Place pan on lower rack of oven .Bake at 300 oven for 1 hour 15 minutes.Cool in pan for 15 minutes. Drizzle with Maple Glaze and garnish with walnuts.
Malpe Glaze
In small bowl,mix 1 cup sifted suger with 1 to 1/2 tbsp milk to make a glaze of thick pouring consistency, stir in 1/2 tsp maple flavouring.
