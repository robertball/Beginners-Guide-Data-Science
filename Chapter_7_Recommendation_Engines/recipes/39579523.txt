Title:
Good For Ya Choc. Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C butter
1 C white sugar
1 C brown sugar
2 eggs
1 t vanilla
2 C flour
2 1/2 C blended oatmeal
1/2 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
1-2 C choc. chips, to your liking

Directions:
Cream butter and sugars. Add eggs and vanilla and mix some more. Stir in flour, oatmeal, baking powder and soda, and choc. chips. Mix well. Bake 6-7 min at 375

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Use a blender or coffee grinder to pulverize oatmeal. Adjust coarseness to your liking
