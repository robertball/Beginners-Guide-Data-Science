Title:
Blueberry Zucchini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup of white sugar, 1/4 cup of brown sugar, 1/4 cup of milk, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1 cup shredded zucchini, 1 egg, 1/2 cup of fresh blueberries, 1/2 cup of chopped pecans, 1/2 cup of olive oil, 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 12 muffin cups, or line with paper muffin liners.
2.Combine flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt in a bowl. Mix egg, oil, milk, and vanilla extract in another bowl; stir into dry ingredients until just moistened. Fold in zucchini, blueberries, and pecans. Fill prepared muffin cups 2/3 full.
Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
