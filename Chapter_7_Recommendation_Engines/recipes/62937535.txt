Title:
Ragu Pizza Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8oz) Package Low-fat cream cheese, softened
1 cup Ragu Old World Style Traditional Pasta Sauce
1/4 cup shredded low-fat Mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup chopped turkey pepperoni

Directions:
In a 9-inch microwave-safe pie plate or bowl, layer cream cheese, pasta sauce, cheese and pepperoni. Microwave on high 1 Minute or until heated through. Serve with sliced veggies or bread sticks.

Submitter: 
Kristen Boesenberg
Description: 
Fabulous is the only way to describe this super easy dip. Eat it with veggie slices, bread sticks, garlic bread, or french bread.
