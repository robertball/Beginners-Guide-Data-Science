Title:
Yummy Lemon Herb Tilapia Filets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless & skinless Tilapia filets
3/4 cup flour
4 Tsp Lemon Herb seasoning (I use McCormick's)
1 Tsp Paprika
Butter

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients together, for more zest add more Lemon Herb seasoning in a large ziploc bag.
Toss one filet at a time in the bag of dry seasonings until entirely covered.
Fry in butter in a skillet over medium heat until nice and golden on both sides.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Island Girl
Description: 
Delicious quick recipe for Tilapia filets. Great with jasmine rice and green beans.  A family favorite!
