Title:
Spiced Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/670841.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups canned pumpkin ( not pumpkin pie filling )
1 cup melted butter
1 1/2 cups granulated white sugar
1 cup light brown sugar
2 eggs
1 Tbsp vanilla
4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 Tbsp baking soda
1 Tbsp baking powder
2 Tbsp ground cinnamon
1/2 Tbsp cloves
1 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
In a large bowl, add melted butter, white sugar and brown sugar. Mix well. Add eggs and vanilla, stir to combine. Add canned pumpkin and stir to combine all ingredients thoroughly.
In a separate bowl, add flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg. Mix well.
Add flour mixture to the pumpkin mixture and stir until well combined.
Divide the batter into 3 ungreased loaf pans, being sure to smooth batter evenly in pan. Bake in preheated oven for 45 - 50 minutes or until toothpick inserted into middle of loaf comes out clean.

Submitter: 
KellieTheBaker
Description: 
A pumpkin loaf spiced with cinnamon, ground cloves and nutmeg. I came up with this recipe after many failed attempts at pumpkin loaf. I adapted a favourite banana loaf recipe and this was the result. Yum! This recipe yields 3 loaves. Stock your freezer!
