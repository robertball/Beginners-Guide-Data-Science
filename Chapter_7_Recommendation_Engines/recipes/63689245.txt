Title:
Tomato Basil Garlic Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1025754.jpg
Ingredients:
tablespoon olive oil
6 slices of bacon
large onion (Sweet)
3 cloves of garlic
3/4 tsp crushed fennel
1/4 tsp red pepper
1/4 cup chicken broth
3 cans 14.5 oz stewed tomatoes undrained
3 tsp dried basil or 1/3 cup fresh basil
parmesan cheese to taste

Directions:
tablespoon of oil in pan
add onion, garlic, fennel, red pepper and bacon, sautee. cook about 15-20 minutes stirring.
add chicken broth , cook until liquid reduced.
add tomatoes and basil bring to a boil. cook until
onions turn clear in color.
simmer
top with parmesan cheese, I add some in the center also.
use angel hair dreamfields pasta
