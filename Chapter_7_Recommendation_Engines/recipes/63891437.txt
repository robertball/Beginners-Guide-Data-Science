Title:
Creamy Crockpot Hot Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2. 1/2 cups half and half
114oz can sweetened condensed milk
5 cups milk
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a crock pot. Cover and cook on low for 2 hours stirring occasionally, until mixture is hot and chocolate chips are melted completely. Stir before serving. Garnish as desired.

Submitter: 
Ellen Hubenett
Description: 
This is a super yummy homemade hot cocoa that was shared with me by my son's teacher.
