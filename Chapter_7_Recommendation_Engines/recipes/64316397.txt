Title:
Slow Cooker Garlic and Brown Sugar Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2002014.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 cup brown sugar
2/3 cup vinegar
1/3 cup lemon-lime soda
2 tablespoons minced garlic
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon water

Directions:
Put chicken in slow cooker.
In a mixing bowl, whisk together brown sugar, vinegar, lemon-lime soda, garlic, soy sauce, and pepper. Pour over the chicken in the slow cooker.
Cook on low for 6 hours or high 3-4 hours, until the chicken is practically falling apart.
Remove chicken and shred.
Pour the liquid into a saucepan.
In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and water until no lumps remain. Pour into saucepan and whisk to combine. Bring to a simmer and let it go for about 3 minutes or until it thickens slightly. Stir in chicken.
Serve hot.
