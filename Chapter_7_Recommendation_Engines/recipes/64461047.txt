Title:
Denver Potato Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
3 cups frozen hash brown potatoes
4 oz./ 1 cup Swiss cheese
1/2 cup diced ham
1/2 cup green peppers

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. Grease 9 inch pie plate. In medium bowl combine eggs, onion powder, thyme, salt and pepper. Beat well. Stir in potatoes, cheese, ham and green peppers. Pour into pie plate. Bake for 40-45 minutes, until set.
