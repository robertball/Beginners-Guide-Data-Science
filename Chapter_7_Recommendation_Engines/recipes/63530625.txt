Title:
Gluten Free Walnut Carob Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup succanat
1/2 cup agave
3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon gluten-free vanilla extract
3/4 cup carob powder
3 Chia Seed Eggs
3/4 cup brown rice flour
1/2 cup almond flour (almond meal)
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees.
In blender or food processor combine: Olive Oil, agave, succinct, vanilla, chia eggs.
In a large bowl mix together brown rice flour, almond flour, baking powder, salt, walnuts, carob powder.
Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients, mix well.
Spread in a greased baking dish, wet hands and pat down so that the brownies are even and flat.
Bake for 40-45 minutes.
