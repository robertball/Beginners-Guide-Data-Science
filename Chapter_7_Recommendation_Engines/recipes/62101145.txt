Title:
Spinach and Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (10 oz) frozen leaf spinach
1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts, drained, chopped
2 (8 oz each) packages cream cheese (softened)
3/4 cup sour cream
2-4 cloves garlic, pressed or chopped
2 cups shredded mozarella cheese
1/2 cup shaved parmesan cheese, divided
1 tsp. fresh cracked pepper
1 tsp. salt (to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Thaw and drain spinach.
Drain and chop artichokes.
In Large bowl, mix together Spinach, Artichokes, pressed garlic, salt and pepper.
Add cream cheese, sour cream, abour 1 1/2 cups of the mozzerella cheese, and 1/4 cup of the parmesan cheese.
Mix together really good.  Place in a lightly greased 8x8 or 9x9 square baking dish or stone.
Top with remaining mozzerella and parmesan cheeses.
Bake for about 30 minutes or until cheeses are melted.
Serve with tostitos, triscuts, fresh vegetables, or crackers.
ENJOY!
