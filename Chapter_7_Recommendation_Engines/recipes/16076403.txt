Title:
Blonde Apple Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Sticks Butter,salted (softened)
1 Cup White Sugar
1 Cup Light Brown sugar (lightly packed)
2 Eggs
2 Cups Flour
1 tsp. Baking Powder
1 tsp. Cinnamon
6 medium (McIntosh) apples, peeled, cored and diced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Lightly grease 9x13 baking dish. In a large bowl cream together first 4 ingredients. Add flour, baking powder and cinnamon. Mix well after each addition. Add diced apples and stir by hand. Spread into pan and bake for 45 minutes or until toothpick inserted into the middle comes out clean. (Toothpick will have moist crumbs on it)

Submitter: 
herbalady
Description: 
Moist, cake-like brownie that is perfect for fall. Everyone asks me for this recipe once they've tasted them. Wonderful served warm with vanilla ice cream. Enjoy!
