Title:
Chipotle Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. can garbanzo beans
1/4 cup leftover garbanzo liquid from can
1 1/2 tbsp tahini
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
3 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
2 cloves crushed garlic
2 chipotle peppers (canned in adobo sauce)

Directions:
Add ingredients to blender or food processor and blend until smooth.  Optional: sprinkle top with cayenne pepper.

Submitter: 
Rachel
Description: 
Mildly spicy hummus with chipotle pepper.
