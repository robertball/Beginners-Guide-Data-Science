Title:
chicken fruit salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Hellman's mayonnaise
2 Tablespoons honey
1/8 teaspoon ground ginger (optional)
2 cups chopped, cooked chicken
11 ounce can mandarin orange segments, drained
1 cup chopped apple
1 cup red seedless grape halves
8 ounce can pineapple chunks,drained
1/2 cup toasted pecan halves

Directions:
Combine mayonnaise, honey and ginger.  Mix well.  Add remaining ingredients.  Mix lightly. Chill

Description: 
This is not your ordinary chicken salad.  It's light, fruity taste makes it perfect for an afternoon luncheon. Serve with croissants and your meal is complete!
