Title:
Beef Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/888866.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Lb. beef brisket
1/3 c. Natural Mosquite Original
2/3 c. Worcestershire Sauce
1 tablespoon Adolph's Unseasoned Meat Tenderizer
1 tablespoon Lawry's Seasoned Salt
2 teaspoons black pepper
2 large cloves of minced garlic
1 small bottle Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ Sauce
Reynold's Wrap No Stick Foil

Directions:
Marinate brisket with seasonings, smoke and mosquite overnight in turkey bag. Next day, drain liquid. Line cookie sheet with foil. Pour barbeque sauce over brisket and cover with foil, making sure to tightly crimp to create a seal. Bake 4 hours on 350°. Add more barbeque sauce. Lower oven temp to 250° and bake 2 more hours.
