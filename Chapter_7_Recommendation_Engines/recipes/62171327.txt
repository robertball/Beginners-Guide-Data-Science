Title:
Cheeseburger Hotdish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 slices of bread
1 lb. ground beef
1 large can of cream of mushroom soup
Velveeta® cheese loaf
Butter or margarine to spread
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°.
Brown beef.
Add soup and season with salt and pepper to you preference.
Stir mixture over medium heat until just heated through.
Butter one side of 6 of the slices of bread and put butter side down in a 9x13 pan.
Pour soup mixture over the top of the bread and top with one layer of Velveeta®.
Butter one side of the remaining 6 slices of bread and put on top with the butter side up.
Bake in the oven for 30 minutes or until top and bottom are golden brown.

Submitter: 
Sandy Schoon
