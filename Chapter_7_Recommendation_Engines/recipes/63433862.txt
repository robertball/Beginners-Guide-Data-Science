Title:
Slow Cooker Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb boneless, skinless Chicken Thighs
1 package of Taco Seasoning
1.5 cups Salsa
2 cans of Mushroom Soup
Sour Cream

Directions:
Add the chicken into the slow cooker and sprinkle the chicken with the package of taco seasoing.
In a bowl mix the salsa and the two cans of mushroom soup.  Pour over the chicken in the slow cooker.
Cook on low for 6 hours.
Stir in 1/2 cup of the sour cream just before serving or set aside and add a bit on top of each serving.

Submitter: 
Tannis
Description: 
One of my favorite quick and easy go-to dishes that never fails to satisfy!  A blend of salsa and chicken makes a very zippy savory flavor when done and creates a lot of sauce which is best served over rice.
