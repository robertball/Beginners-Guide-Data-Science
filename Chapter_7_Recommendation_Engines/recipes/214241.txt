Title:
Heavenly Cream Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989778.jpg
Ingredients:
40 NILLA Wafers, finely crushed
1/3 cup margarine or butter, melted
1 pkg. (4 serving size)  JELL-O Vanilla Flavor Cook & Serve Pudding
1 3/4 cups milk
1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, cut up
1/2 cup chopped PLANTERS Pecans
1/4 teaspoon rum extract

Directions:
Mix wafer crumbs and margarine. Remove 1/4 cup of the crumb mixture; reserve for later use. Press remaining crumb mixture firmly onto bottom of 9-inch square baking pan.
Prepare pudding mix as directed on package for pudding, using the 1-3/4 cups milk. Add cream cheese, pecans and extract; cook on low heat until cream cheese is completely melted and mixture is well blended, stirring frequently. Pour over crust; sprinkle with the reserved crumb mixture.
Refrigerate several hours or overnight until firm. Cut into 9 squares to serve. Store leftover dessert in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Nilla
Description: 
Vanilla pudding is mixed with pecans and rum extract and chilled until firm on a cookie crust for these delicious cream squares.
