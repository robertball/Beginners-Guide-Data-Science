Title:
Jim's Balsamic Scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs or sea scallops
8 T all purpose flour
2 t salt
4 t pepper
1 t cayenne pepper
2 t marjoram
2 t paprika
4 t garlic powder
4 T olive oil
1/2 C Balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Pat dry the scallops with a paper towel and lay them out to dry. Fill a zip lock baggie with the flour, salt, pepper, marjoram,cayenne pepper, paprika and garlic powder. shake well then add the scallops coating them.
Heat 4 T of olive oil on medium heat in a saute pan big enough to fit all the scallops. Sear the scallops for 3-4 minutes on each side.
Drizzle on the balsamic vinegar. ENJOY

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
The most tasty scallops you will ever try. I like my food with a little zip! You can leave out the cayenne pepper if you don't like it spicy.
