Title:
Mary's Seafood Salad Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Usually this is what I make for gatherings so, you know to cut back if just for a few people.
Albacore tuna (in water)
red onion (to your taste)
celery or celery seeds (to your taste)
shrimp (a bag of frozen salad ones, plus  about a lb. of steamed ones)
crab meat
garlic powder (a little bit)
red pepper or black pepper . . . whichever one you prefer (I'm allergic to black so I use red)  you might even experiment with lemon pepper. . .
mayonaise (i use the one with olive oil, but again which one you use is your choice)
Old Bay seasoning  (LOTS OF IT!!!  YUMMY)
Dill relish (but I think you used pickle juice)
Can Also add Pasta
