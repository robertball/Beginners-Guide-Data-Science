Title:
Independence Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 (8 ounce0 can crushed pineapple, drained
1/3 cup dry bread crumbs
7 1/2 tsp chopped fresh parsley
4 1/2 tsp minced and seeded green chiles
2 tsp crushed dried oregano
1/8 tsp ground black pepper
6 hamburger buns
1 tomato, sliced
1/4 cup prepared mustard
1/4 cup ketchup

Directions:
Preheat grill.  Combine ground beef, cheddar cheese, crushed pineapple, dry bread crumbs, parsley, green chiles, garlic salt, oregano, and pepper in a large mixing bowl.  Mix gently until throughly combined.  Shape into patties.  Grill 4 inches from hot coals, for about 4 minutes on each side.  Serve with hamburger buns, tomato, prepared mustard, and ketchup.

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
This recipe was a favorite of ours when we were in Germany.  We would invite some German friends over for a barbeque.  They loved these burgers too!  My best friend Darin Asfeld, could eat about 2 and was full!
