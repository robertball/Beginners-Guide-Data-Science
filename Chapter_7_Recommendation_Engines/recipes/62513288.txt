Title:
Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/2 pounds pot roast
2 Tblspns. olive oil
2 Tblspns. flour
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
1 can beef consumme
3-4 carrots peeled and chopped into chunks
1 onion, quartered

Directions:
Rinse and pat dry the roast ~ dredge in the flour
Heat olive oil in a large skillet.  Brown the roast on all sides.
While the roast is browning ~ in a slow cooker, mix cream of mushroom soup, dry onion soup mix and consumme.  Add in carrots and onions.
Place pot roast in slow cooker and coat with soup mixture. Add water to cover if necessary.
Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.
