Title:
Tuna Caesar Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can solid white albacore tuna, drained and flaked
1/2 large or 2 small (persian) cucumbers, diced
2 roma tomatoes, diced
3 celery stalks, diced
1 cup canned garbanzo beans, drained
1 cup hearts of palm, sliced
1/2 - 3/4 cup creamy caesar dressing
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and chill overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Brklynn225
Description: 
A creamy, crunchy and delicious salad perfect for a quick lunch.  Best when made with creamy caesar dressing, not vinagrette.  I use Cardini's Classic Caesar (THE BEST!)
