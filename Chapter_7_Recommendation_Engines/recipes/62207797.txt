Title:
Crazy Yummy Cranberry Pecan Cookies w/ Orange Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients for Cookies:
2  ¼ c. flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 c. Imperial Margarine or Butter
¾ c. sugar
¾ packed brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
1 c. chopped pecans or walnuts
1 c. oatmeal or coconut
1 cup Craisins
Ingredients for Orange Glaze:
½ cup orange juice fresh squeezed (Organic Odwalla OJ works also if you don’t have time to fres squeeze)
1 1/2 c. confectioners sugar
Zest of one orange finely grated
1 tbsp. butter

Directions:
SIFT TOGETHER THE FOLLOWING AND SET ASIDE:
2  ¼ c. flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
CREAM TOGETHER THE FOLLOWING IN A LARGE BOWL:
1 c. Imperial Margarine or Butter (I use organic butter)
¾ c. sugar
¾ packed brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
ADD TO CREAMED MIXTURE: (but do not over mix)  2 eggs
ADD:  Flour mixture slowly mixing as you add it in
ADD: 1 c. chopped pecans or walnuts,  1 c. oatmeal or coconut, 1 cup Craisins
DROP:  Drop spoonfuls onto a an ungreased baking sheet and bake at 375 degrees for 10-12 minutes
Cool them on a cooling rack placed on wax paper. Top with orange glaze as they cool.
ORANGE GLAZE:
Mix the orange juice and confectioners sugar together in a microwave safe bowl.  Stir until the sugar dissolves.  Add the zest and butter and microwave for 30 second intervals until butter is melted and the mix is smooth upon stirring.  Drizzle glaze over cookies (or scones)
