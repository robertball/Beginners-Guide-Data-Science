Title:
Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Spaghetti noodles
1 jar Spaghetti sauce
1 lb. hamburger
1 small chopped onion
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brown hamburger with onion; drain grease. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti noodles according to package directions. Drain noodles but do not rinse. Add spaghetti sauce to hamburger  and heat until warm. Serve over warm noodles. Top with parmesan cheese if desired.
