Title:
New Potatoes in Brown Butter Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1033911.jpg
Ingredients:
4 lbs of new red potatoes
2 cups low sodium beef broth
3 cups water
1/2 cup butter
fresh parsley

Directions:
Cut potatoes in half so they are about the same size and add to pot with beef broth and water.
Boil until fork tender.
In skillet make brown butter sauce while potatoes are cooking.
Add potatoes to crock pot (unless serving right away) and pour brown butter sauce over potatoes.
Add fresh parsley and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description: 
This recipe reminds me of home and is one of my favorite comfort foods.
