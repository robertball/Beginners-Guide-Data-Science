Title:
Key Lime Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 egg whites
3 juiced limes
1 cup sugar
5 drops green food coloring

Directions:
Put the egg whites over a double boiler and whisk quickly, until stiff peaks form. (an electric hand mixer works very well for this)
Take off heat and set aside
Mix lime juice, sugar and food coloring until the sugar has dissolved.
Slowly fold in the lime mixture to the egg whites
Put the mousse in glasses and chill

Submitter: 
Julia L
Description: 
This is a fresh play on light fluffy mousse
