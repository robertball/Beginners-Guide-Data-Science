Title:
French Toast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976327.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) loaf Pepperidge FarmÂ® Cinnamon Swirl Bread, cut into cubes
6 eggs, beaten
3 cups milk
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Place bread cubes in greased 3-quart shallow baking dish.  Mix eggs, milk and vanilla.  Pour over bread.  Cover and refrigerate 1 hour or overnight. Uncover.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 50 minutes or until golden.  Sprinkle with confectioners' sugar.  Serve with maple syrup, if desired.

Description: 
Tempting cinnamon bread is soaked in a custard overnight, ready to be baked for a perfect brunch dish.
