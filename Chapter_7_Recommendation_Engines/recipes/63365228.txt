Title:
Pork Chops with Tomato Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Bone in Pork Chops
1/2 cup Heavy Cream
4 tbsp Butter
1 Small onion finely chopped
1 small bell pepper finely chopped
1 Jalapeno finely chopped
3 cloves of Garlic finely chopped
2 tbsp Olive oil
1 can diced tomatoes w/chiles
1/2 cup chicken stock

Directions:
1. In a skillet add butter and vegetables and cook till soft.
2 Meanwhile grill pork chops till done
3. Stir in tomatoes and chicken stock into vegetables. When the mixture is hot stir in cream and simmer for 10 min to thicken.
