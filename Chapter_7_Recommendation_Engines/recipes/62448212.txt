Title:
Spaghetti alla Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz. dried spaghetti
1 large onion, thinly sliced
2 garlic cloves, chopped
6 slices bacon
2 Tbsp. butter
6 oz. mushrooms, sliced
1 ¼ cups heavy cream
3 eggs, beaten (optional)
1 cup grated parmesan
Salt and pepper to taste
Sage to garnish

Directions:
Cook pasta
Heat 2 Tbsp. oil and sauté onion until transparent. Add garlic and bacon—fry until bacon is crisp. Transfer to a plate.
Melt butter in skillet and sauté mushrooms for 3-4 minutes, then add bacon mixture.
Mix together the cream, eggs and cheese in a large bowl and season to taste.
Working quickly, pour spaghetti into bacon mixture and pour in egg mixture.
Toss spaghetti mixture quickly and serve immediately. Serve with extra grated parmesan.
