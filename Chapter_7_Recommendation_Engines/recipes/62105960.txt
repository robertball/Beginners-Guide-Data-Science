Title:
Italian Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs-try cage free, no hormones..they taste better!
1/2 cup fresh mushroom -minced
2 gloves garlic-minced
1/4-1/2 cup red onion -minced
1/2 tbl olive oil
salt and pepper

Directions:
In bowl, scramble eggs. In frying pan, put the 1/2 tablespoon oil and heat on medium.  Throw all veggies in and saute until the mushrooms and onions are soft.  Throw in eggs and stir. Scramble like  normal  scrambled eggs. Serve by themselves or with jalapano jelly.
