Title:
Bara Brith Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4c Dried Currants
3/4c Golden Raisins
1c Hot Tea
3/4c Demerara Sugar
1 1/4 c Self-Raising Flour
1 egg (beaten)

Directions:
Pour hot tea over dried fruit and let soak overnight.
Add sugar to fruit and remaining liquid and stir thoroughly.  Add flour and egg and mix until completely combined.
Line 2lb loaf pan with greased parchment paper.  Spread batter evenly in pan and bake in 350 degree oven for 40 minutes.  Let cool in pan 5 minutes before transferring to cooling rack.

Submitter: 
Femmie London
Description: 
A sweet, yeastless Welsh bread studded with dried fruit.  I generally soak my raisins in Irish Breakfast tea, but feel free to experiment with flavors.
