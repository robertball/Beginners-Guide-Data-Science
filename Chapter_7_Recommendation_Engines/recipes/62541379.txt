Title:
Simply Pumpkin Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup Flour
2 tsp. Baking Power
1/2 tsp. Cinnamon
1/4 tsp. Nutmeg
1/2 tsp Salt
1/4 cup sugar (white or brown)
1 Egg beaten
1 cup Milk
1 Tbs. Oil
1/2 cup Pumpkin

Directions:
Sift and stir dry ingredients together in a bowl.
Whisk Milk, Oil, Egg, and Pumpkin in a separate bowl.
Pour Milk mixture into flour mixture and stir to moisten. Batter will be slightly lumpy.
Heat griddle to medium (about 350). Ladle or scoop batter onto a hot griddle. Wait until edges are dry and bubbles form on top but do not fill when they pop. Turn. Cook another minute then remove from griddle and serve warm with butter and syrup.

Description: 
Quick, easy, delicious pumpkin pancakes from scratch. Developed for my pumpkin loving grandson who will eat almost anything containing pumpkin.
