Title:
Low Carb Maple Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7769256.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup water
3/4 cup Swerve Brown sweetener
1/4 cup Allulose sweetener
3/4 teaspoon Maple Extract

Directions:
Bring water and both sweeteners to boil over medium high heat.
Reduce heat to medium low, add Maple Extract and simmer for 3 minutes.
