Title:
Mango Shrimp Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb.cooked shrimp
1 cup diced mango
2 tbsp. fresh mint, chopped
1 cup diced cucumber
1 tbsp. lime juice
2 tsps. lime zest
1/4 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. hot chili peppers
rice vinegar to taste

Directions:
Combine lime juice, lime zest, sugar and chili(if desired) in a bowl and mix well.
Combine shrimp, mango and mint.  Add dressing and toss.
Taste and if desired add a splash or two of rice vinegar.
