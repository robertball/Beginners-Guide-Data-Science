Title:
Dill to Sweet Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart of whole Kosher or Polish dill pickles
2 1/2 cups white sugar
2/3 cup vinegar
1/4 cup water
1 tsp celery seed
1 tbsp poppy seed

Directions:
Rinse pickles well in cold water. Wash pickle jar thoroughly. Slice pickles and place back into the clean jar. In a medium saucepan, mix the sugar, vinegar, water and seeds. Bring to a boil and cook 2 minutes. Pour liquid over the sliced pickles and seal jar immediately. Let stand in the refrigerator for one week. Sweet and crisp!!

Submitter: 
Julie Rehagen
Description: 
Prepare a week ahead of time. May want to drain some of the seeds off when serving.
