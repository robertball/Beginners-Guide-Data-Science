Title:
Fabulous Wet Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 (4.5 ounce) can diced green chile peppers
1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
1 (15 ounce) can chili without beans
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
1 cup salsa
6 (12 inch) flour tortillas, warmed
2 cups shredded lettuce
1 cup chopped tomatoes
1/2 cup black olives, chopped
2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese
1/2 cup chopped green onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Crumble ground beef into a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until evenly browned. Add onion, and cook until translucent. Drain grease, and season with garlic, cumin, salt and pepper. Stir in the green chilies and refried beans until well blended. Turn off heat, but keep warm.
In a saucepan, combine the chili without beans, tomato soup and salsa. Mix well, and cook over medium heat until heated through. Turn off heat and keep warm.
Dip tortilla in sauce; fill generously with meat mixture.  Spoon a generous amount of the sauce over the top, and sprinkle with cheese and green onions. Bake for 10 - 15 minutes, until cheese is melted.
Top with lettuce, tomato, black olives.
