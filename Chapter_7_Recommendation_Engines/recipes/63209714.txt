Title:
Philly Cream Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz packages of cream cheese
1/2c. Miracle Whip
1/3c. Parmesan Cheese
10 slices of cooked bacon
3/4c. Chopped pecans or sliced almonds

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, miracle whip and parmesan cheese-use mixer. Crumble bacon and stir into cheese mixture. Let set in fridge for a few hours or over night. Shape into ball and roll in chopped nuts of choice.
