Title:
Oriental Overnight OMG Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- Dark Soy Sauce (Kikkoman is the best) 100 ml (0.4 Cups)
- Fresh Orange, 1, zest and juice
- Fresh Lime, 1, zest and juice
- Strong mustard, 1 Tbsp
- Lemongrass, 1 stalk, finely sliced
- Fresh ginger, 1 walnut-sized chunk, finely sliced
- Fresh garlic, 2 cloves, finely sliced
- Fresh peppercorns, crushed, 2 Tbsp
- Coriander powder, 2 Tbsp
- Paprika, 2 Tbsp
- Cumin powder, 2 Tbsp
- Fennel seeds, crushed, 1 tsp
- Honey, 2 tsp
- Tomato pur√©e, 2 Tbsp (a.k.a. Tomato paste)

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together.
Paste over the meat and let marinate overnight in a closed dish in the fridge.
Grill on a lightly oiled rack.

Submitter: 
Swiss Phil
Description: 
An unusual marinade that seems to work really well on beef, buffalo, horse, veal, venison, lamb, chicken, turkey, duck, and pork.
