Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2461463.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter or margarine
7.5 ounces of marshmellow fluff
6 cups Rice Crispy/orKrispies cereal
12 wooden ice cream sticks
1 package cinnamon candies

Directions:
1. In large saucepan melt butter over low heat.
Add marshmallow fluff and stir until completely mixed. (Gives it that extra fluffy-ness)
You can also use a 10 oz. packages of marshmallows or 4 cups of mini marshmallows.
Remove from heat.
2. Add RICE CRISPY/KRISPIES cereal.
Stir until well coated.
3. Using 1/2-cup measuring cup coated with cooking spray
divide warm cereal mixture into portions.
Using buttered hands shape each portion into ball.
Roll ball over cinnamon candies, press into treat if needed.
Cool or Chill, however you like to relax.
Insert wooden stick.
Best if served the same day.
