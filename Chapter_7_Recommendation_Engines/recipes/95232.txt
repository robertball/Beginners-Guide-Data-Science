Title:
Spinach Artichoke Feta Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/552343.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 (10 ounce) box frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 (4 ounce) container feta cheese, crumbled
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, chopped with hard leaf tips removed
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 (1 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the cream cheese, spinach, feta cheese, artichokes, garlic and Ranch dressing mix. Mix together with clean hands until well blended. Form into a ball or log and set on a serving plate. Done!

Submitter: 
LEOGIRL74
Description: 
If you like hot spinach artichoke dip, this is for you. It's yummy and quick. Serve with crackers or garlic toast.
