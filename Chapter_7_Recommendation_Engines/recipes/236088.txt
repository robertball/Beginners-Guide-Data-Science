Title:
Pumpkin Pie in a Glass Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089861.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces Kahlua Pumpkin Spice
1 fluid ounce hazelnut liqueur
2 1/2 fluid ounces heavy cream
1 ounce pumpkin puree
1/4 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Combine Kahlua, hazelnut liqueur, cream, pumpkin puree, and pumpkin pie spice in a martini shaker filled with ice. Shake to combine. Pour into glass.
