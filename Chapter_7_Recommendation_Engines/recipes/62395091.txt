Title:
Laura's Favorite Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1 lb. lean ground beef
1/4 lb. ground pork
1 Tb. minced onion, dried
1 tsp. minced garlic
28 oz. can crushed tomatoes with basil
6 oz. Italian-style tomato paste
1/2 cup water
2 beef bouillon cubes
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1 bay leaf
1 tsp. basil
1 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. thyme
1 tsp. sugar

Directions:
In a stock pot or large skillet, crumble and cook ground beef and ground pork in olive oil over medium heat, until browned.
Meanwhile, in a small bowl, dissolve bouillon cubes in boiling water.
Add minced onions, garlic, beef broth, and remaining ingredients to the pot.
Simmer, covered, over low heat for 1 hour.  Serve over pasta.

Submitter: 
cheri
Description: 
This is an easy Italian meat sauce that can be served over pasta or used in baked dishes.  It freezes well too!
