Title:
Black Bean Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can black beans
7 dates (1/4 c softened)
1/4 to 1/3 c coconut oil melted
1/4 c plus more (to taste) agave
1/3 to 1/2 c cocoa
1 T vanilla
1/4 tsp salt or adjust
2 eggs

Directions:
preheat oven 350
blend all ingredients except eggs in blender/Vitamix until smooth
gently whisk eggs and add to blender just to blend (do not over-mix)
bake at 350 in 9 in round or 8x8 in square pan 30 min
