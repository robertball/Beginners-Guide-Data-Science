Title:
Fried chicken with pineapple and tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. chicken pieces. legs and thighs.
3/4 c. all purpose flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. paprka
8 tbsp. butter
1 (13 1/2 oz.) can pineapple chunks with juice (not crushed)
3 medium tomatoes

Directions:
Place flour, salt, pepper and paprika in plastic ziplock bag. Shake to mix well. Place chicken (2 or 3 at a time) in flour bag and shake well to coat chicken. Melt butter in large skillet and arrange chicken so it will brown evenly. Cook over moderate heat till chicken is rich golden brown. turn grequently to assure even browning. Add pineapple chunks and juice to chicken. Remove stem from tomatoes; cut into wedges and arrange in pan with chicken. Cover tightly and continue to cook over moderate heat for 35 mins. Remove cover and cook 10 mins longer to reduce liquid and to crisp chicken. Place chicken on heated platter and pour any remaining liquid over chicken. Serve over rice
