Title:
Mocha Shakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981207.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup warm water
2 tablespoons HERSHEYÂ®'S Cocoa Powder
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
1/2 cup milk
2 cups vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Place water, cocoa, sugar and instant coffee in blender container.  Cover; blend briefly on low speed.  Add milk.  Cover; blend on high speed until thoroughly blended.  Add ice cream.  Cover; blend until smooth.  Serve immediately.
