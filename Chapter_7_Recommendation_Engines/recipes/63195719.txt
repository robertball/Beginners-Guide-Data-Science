Title:
Chocolate Chip, Oatmeal, Almond, Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c butter
1 c brown sugar
1 egg
1 tspn vanilla extract
1 1/2 c all-purpose flour
1 tspn baking soda
1/2 tspn salt
1 tspn cinnamon
3 c rolled oats
1/2 c raisins
1/2 c chopped almonds
1/2 c semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325F
Cream sugar and butter until light and fluffy.
Add egg and vanilla extract and mix well.
In seperate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon and mix.
Add flour mix to wet ingredients until combined.
Mix in oats until wetted.
Stir in raisins, nuts, and chocolate chips.
Roll dough into 1" balls and flatten slightly, then arrange on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper.
Bake for ~12 minutes. Let cool slightly on cookie sheets before removing to your serving plate.

Submitter:

Description: 
A delicious combination of pantry staples make for delightfully tender and chewy cookies. I made this as a late-night snack when I couldn't decide on a recipe...and now they're a favorite!
