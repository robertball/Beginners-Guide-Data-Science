Title:
Black Eyed Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
2 tbsp sugar
1 (to 3) cloves garlic, pressed
2 can black eyed peas, rinsed
2 plum tomatoes, diced
1 red bell pepper (small), diced
jalapeno (deseeded & diced)
1/2 cup parsley, chopped

Directions:
Mix olive oil, balsamic vinegar, sugar, and garlic well to make dressing.
Set aside dressing.
Mix peas, tomatoes, bell pepper, jalapeno, and parsley.
Pour dressing over.
Refrigerate.
