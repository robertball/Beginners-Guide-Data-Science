Title:
Cream Cheese Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1009062.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ¼ cup flour
1 ½ tsp baking powder
½ tsp baking soda
1 package (8 oz. each) Cream Cheese, softened
¼ cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
3 fully ripe banana, mashed
2 egg

Directions:
HEAT oven to 350°F.
MIX flour, baking powder and baking soda. Beat cream cheese, butter and sugar in large bowl with mixer until blended. Add bananas and eggs; mix well. Gradually add flour mixture, mixing well after each addition. Stir in nuts.
POUR into greased and floured 9x5-inch loaf pan.
BAKE 1 hour 10 min. or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 5 min.; remove from pan to wire rack. Cool completely.

Submitter: 
Mark Kowitz
Description: 
Thanks to the addition of cream cheese, this classic bread is unbelievably moist. And while it won't be easy, you should wait until it is completely cooled before slicing.
