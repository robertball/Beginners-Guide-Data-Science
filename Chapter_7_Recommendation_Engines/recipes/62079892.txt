Title:
Crab and Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream (do not use fat-free)
1 cup mayonnaise
2/3 cup Parmesan cheese
1 lb. lump crab meat
15 oz. can artichoke hearts, drained, chopped
1 tsp. Old Bay seasoning
2-3 drops hot sauce

Directions:
Combine sour cream, mayonnaise and parmesan cheese in a mixing bowl, mix well. Gently fold in crab meat, artichokes and seasonings. Spread into a greased serving dish or pie plate and bake at 350 F for 35-40 minutes, or until bubbly and brown around the edges. Serve with crackers or sliced baguette.

Submitter: 
Anne Z.
Description: 
This mild, creamy dip really lets the flavor of the crab meat shine through.
