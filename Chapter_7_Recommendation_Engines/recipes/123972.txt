Title:
Marzetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979527.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
1/2 green pepper, chopped
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup, undiluted
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/3 cup water
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
8 ounces wide egg noodles, cooked and drained
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef, onion and green pepper; drain off fat. Stir in next nine ingredients. Spoon into a greased 3-qt. casserole; top with cheese. Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes.
