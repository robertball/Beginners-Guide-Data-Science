Title:
Bread Machine Pumpernickel Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4424449.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/8 cups warm water
1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/3 cup molasses
3 tablespoons cocoa
1 tablespoon caraway seed (optional)
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 1/2 cups bread flour
1 cup rye flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 1/2 tablespoons vital wheat gluten (optional)
2 1/2 teaspoons bread machine yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select  Basic cycle; press Start.

Submitter: 
Ann Davis
Description: 
This is a hearty, good-tasting bread. Just put all the ingredients in the bread machine and walk away.
