Title:
Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5771206.jpg
Ingredients:
1 to 1.5 lb pork tenderloin
1 tsp olive oil (or ghee)
2-3 T Mexican seasoning, as desired (cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, chipotle powder, salt)
1/4 to 1/2 lemon, juiced
1/4 to 1/2 lime, juiced
2-3 lime wedges
2-3 lemon wedges
1-2 orange wedges
2-3 garlic cloves, smashed
2 T olive oil

Directions:
preheat oven to 400
pat dry tenderloin
spread rub over all sides of tenderloin, let rest 15-60 min
heat cast iron pan over medium high heat
add 1 tsp olive oil (or ghee)
add tenderloin to sear on all sides, for 8-10 min total (get a good sear before turning to create crust)
remove from heat, let cool for a few seconds
carefully place lemon, lime, orange wedges and garlic in pan
squeeze lemon/lime juice in pan
drizzle olive oil on bottom of pan
put cast iron pan with tenderloin into preheated oven, bake for 15 min
remove from oven to check for doneness (140-145F degrees at thickest part)
cover with foil and let rest 7-10 min
served with Mexican crema and pan drippings, and salad with avocado, cilantro and Mexican Crema dressing
