Title:
Baked Scallops & Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter, melted
3 cloves garlic, minced
3/4 pound bay scallops, rinsed and drained
3/4 pound shrimp, shelled and deveined
1/2 cup seasoned dry Panko bread crumbs
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Pour melted butter and minced garlic into a 2 quart oval casserole dish. Toss the scallops and shrimp with the garlic and butter, then distribute butter mix and seafood evenly inside the dish.
Combine the bread crumbs, onion powder, garlic powder, paprika, parsley, minced garlic and Parmesan cheese. Sprinkle this mixture over the scallops.
Bake in pre-heated oven until scallops are firm, about 20 minutes.
Serve over pasta.

Submitter: 
Valerie Kasper
Description: 
Delicious baked scallops and shrimp with butter and garlic. Bay scallops are the little ones and they tend to be sweeter tasting than sea scallops.Modified from: Baked Scallops
