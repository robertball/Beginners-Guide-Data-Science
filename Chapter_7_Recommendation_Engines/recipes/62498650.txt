Title:
Frozen Fruit dairy-free smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Original Almond Breeze
1 cup Frozen mango cubes
1/2 cup frozen strawberry slices
1/4 cup blueberry (optional)
1/5th of a medium avocado (about an 8th of a cup or less)
2 teaspoons sugar (optional)

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a blender, blend until smooth (add more milk if you want a thinner smoothie)

Submitter: 
kosher tx cook
Description: 
This delicious smoothie, made of frozen mangoes, strawberries, and avocado (and blueberries if you like), tastes great even without he sugar! (Oh and don't get turned away by the avocado, you can't taste it)
