Title:
Chicken & Rice Easy Slow Cooker Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boxes of Uncle Ben's Original Wild Rice Blend
2 Cans Sliced Water Chestnuts
2 Lg Cans of Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Rotisserie Chicken OR 4 Cans of Chicken
1 Cup Water

Directions:
If using a rotisserie chicken, remove the meat and cut it up into bite size pieces.
Mix in the crock pot the chicken, rice, water chestnuts, cream of chicken soup & water until well blended.
Cook on high 4-6 hours.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
A very easy recipe that is tasty and extremely easy dish to make! It is also very easy to modify to personal liking. What else could you ask for?!
