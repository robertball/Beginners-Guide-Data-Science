Title:
divine pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 loin pork chops
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 soup can of dry white wine
1/3 container of crumbled goat cheese

Directions:
brown pork chops in small amount of olive oil. Mix soup and wine and pour over chops. Simmer covered for 20 minutes. Remove chops. Stir in cheese until smooth and pour over chops.
