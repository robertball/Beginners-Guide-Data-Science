Title:
Hot Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.8 l cranberry juice
1 l pineapple juice (apple juice can be subbed)
6 whole cloves
1 cinnamon stick, broken up

Directions:
Put juices in perk, saucepan or slow cooker.
Place spices in coffee tray of perk (or tie in double layer of cheesecloth or use a tea ball for saucepan/crock pot).
Run a perk cycle or simmer in saucepan/crock pot till heated & spices have infused the juice. This actually improves if it sits heating.
Serve hot.
This can be left on a very low temp. for hours.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Perk these ingredients together in a small coffee perk or simmer in saucepan or slow cooker.
