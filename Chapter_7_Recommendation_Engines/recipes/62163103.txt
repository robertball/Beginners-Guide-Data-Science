Title:
Yamilka's Flan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Caramel Sauce:
1 cup sugar
Custard:
2 14 ounce cans sweetened condensed milk
1 12 ounce can evaporated milk
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
pinch salt
8 eggs
1 cup water
Other:
Water (enough to fill half a large pot)
1/2 lemon

Directions:
Caramel Sauce:
Melt suar in the bottom of a flan pan, stirring constantly until sugar melts and is light brown in color.  Set aside.
Custard:
In a large bowl, combine sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, sugar, vanilla, salt, eggs, and water.  Mix with an electric mixer.  Pour in flan pan, ontop of sugar mixture.
Other:
Boil water in a large pot with lemon.  When boiling, put flan pan in pot of boiling water.
Bake in oven at 350F for about 1 hour and 16 minutes.
When finished wiggle flan out of flan pan onto a platter with high sides.
Let cool in the refrigerator until cold.
