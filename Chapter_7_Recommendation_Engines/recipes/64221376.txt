Title:
Slow Cooker Caramel Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 tart apples, peeled and sliced
2 Tbsp sugar
2 Tbsp brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 Tbsp melted butter
1/3 c. caramel topping
1 c. oatmeal
1 box yellow cake mix
3/4 c. butter
Vanilla ice cream
Caramel topping

Directions:
Mix the first four ingredients in a slow cooker. Drizzle with 2 Tbsp melted butter and 1/3 c. caramel topping.
In a separate bowl, mix oatmeal and yellow cake mix. Cut in butter until it resembles coarse crumbs. Place on top of apples in the slow cooker.
Cook on high for 2.5 to 3 hours. Serve with ice cream and additional caramel topping.
