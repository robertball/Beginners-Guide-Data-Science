Title:
Creamy Carolina Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds red potatoes
3 hard-boiled eggs
2 ounces diced pimientos
2 scallions, chopped
2 slices crispy bacon, chopped
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons yellow mustard
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon celery seed
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Cook whole potatoes in a saucepan (with enough water to cover) for approximately 20-25 minutes; drain; cool.
Cut potatoes into 1" cubes (peeling is optional).
Place potatoes in large bowl with chopped eggs.
Combine pimientos and next 11 ingredients (this mixture can be prepared in advance and left in fridge overnight).
Pour mixture over potatoes and egg.
Toss gently to cover potatoes.
Sprinkle paprika over top to garnish.
