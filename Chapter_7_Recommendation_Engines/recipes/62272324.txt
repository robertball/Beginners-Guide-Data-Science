Title:
APPLE CARAMEL MUFFIN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Preheat oven to 350
grease muffin tins
2 C. all-purpose flour
3/4 C. granulated sugar
2 1/2 t. cinnamon
2 t. baking powder <#>
1/2 t. salt
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 C. milk
1/2 stick unsalted butter
1 1/2 t. vanilla extract
1/2 C. apples, peeled and finely diced
3/4 C. diced caramel candy squares (about 12)

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon and salt <#>.
In another bowl, combine egg and milk. Stir in butter and vanilla
extract. Add flour mixture and stir just to blend. Stir in apples and
caramels. Divide batter evenly among prepared cups. Bake for 25 minutes
or until tops spring back when lightly pressed. Serve warm.
