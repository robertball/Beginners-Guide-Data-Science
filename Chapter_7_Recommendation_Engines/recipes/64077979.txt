Title:
McDonalds Special Sauce/Big Mac Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1169340.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons French dressing
2 tbsp sweet pickle relish
1.5 tablespoon finely minced white onion
1.5 teaspoon white vinegar
1.5 teaspoon sugar
Pinch of salt

Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients in a small bowl. Stir well.
Place sauce in a covered container and refrigerate for several hours or overnight to blend flavors.
Stir the sauce a couple of times as it chills.
