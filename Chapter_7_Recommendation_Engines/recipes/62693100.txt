Title:
Crunchy No-Fry Chicken with Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups corn flakes
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 teaspoon rosemary
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1 cup brown jasmine rice
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 and 1/4 cup low sodium chicken stock

Directions:
In a heavy sauce pan bring brown rice and 2 cup chicken stock to boil.
Reduce rice to a simmer and start timer for 40 minutes.
While rice is cooking combine the corn flakes, garlic powder, black pepper, rosemary and Italian seasoning in a food processor.
Run food processor until mix reaches desired consistency.
Transfer corn flake mix into shallow bowl.
Dip the chicken into the remaining 1/4 cup stock. Coat with the corn flake mixture. Place the chicken onto a baking sheet.
Bake chicken at 400 degrees F for 20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
Fluff rice with fork, plate and serve.
