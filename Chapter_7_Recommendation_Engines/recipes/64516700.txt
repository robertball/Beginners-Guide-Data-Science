Title:
Pumpkin Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2828678.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T. extra virgin olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
½ medium apple, peeled and diced
1 large clove garlic, minced
1 t. fresh ginger, minced
½ t. fresh thyme, minced
½ t. ground cinnamon
1/8 t. ground cloves
1 t. salt
½ t. freshly ground black pepper
2 c. fresh pumpkin puree
3 c. Swanson unsalted chicken broth
½ c. heavy cream
Garnish:
4 strips bacon cooked and crumbled
2 green onions, chopped
½ c. sour cream
½ c. seasoned croutons

Directions:
1. Heat large stock pot over medium-high heat. Add olive oil and onion and sauté until onion is translucent, about 3 minutes. Add the apple, garlic, ginger, thyme and sauté until garlic and ginger is fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add the cinnamon, cloves, salt, pepper, pumpkin and chicken broth and bring mixture to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
2. Turn off the heat and using an immersion blender puree the soup until soup is smooth. Turn heat back on and bring soup back to a boil and reduce heat to simmer for an additional 30 minutes.
3. Turn off the heat and stir in cream. Serve soup with whatever garnish you desire.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
This creamy pumpkin soup is made with fresh pumpkin and is so easy to make! Ultimate comfort food for those cold winter nights!
