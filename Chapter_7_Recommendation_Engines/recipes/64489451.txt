Title:
Clam chowder a la Nancy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
1 large onion, chopped
8 red potatoes, chopped
2-3 bay leaves
1 lb bulk pork sausage (Bob Evans works well), browned and drained
1 quart of half and half
2  or 3 cans of evaporated milk
3 cans of baby clams, juice drained and reserved
Salt and pepper to taste
Add more or less of these herbs, to taste:
2-3 t. thyme
1-2  t marjoram
1-2 t. paprika
1-3 T sugar (optional) reduces "clamminess" and enhances flavors
2-3 drops of tobacco sauce (or more)

Directions:
In a large soup pot, saut√© onions in butter until translucent.
Add herbs and seasonings.
Add potatoes, clam juice and enough water to cover potatoes. Boil potatoes 20 minutes or until tender.
Add browned sausage.
Add half and half and evaporated milk and heat, but do not boil.
Add clams and heat through.
Add the sugar (if desired) and tabasco sauce.
Enjoy!
