Title:
Garlic Chicken with Orzo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups uncooked orzo pasta
3-1/2 cups chicken broth
1 tablespoon olive oil
2-4 cloves garlic, minced
1/4-1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
salt & pepper to taste
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
3 cups fresh spinach leaves torn into bite size pieces
2-3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese plus more for topping

Directions:
Bring chicken broth to a boil with 1 clove of minced garlic.
Add orzo pasta, cook for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occaisonally, until al dente.
Heat the oil in a skillet over medium-high heat, and stir in the remaining garlic and red pepper about 30 seconds.
Stir in chicken, season with salt & pepper, and cook 5-6 minutes, until lightly browned and juices run clear.
Reduce heat to medium, and mix in the parsley, spinach, cooked orzo with remaining broth. and parmesan. Continue cooking 2-3 minutes, stirring until combined and spinach is wilted.
Serve topped with Parmesan cheese.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a simple recipe with a spicy kick for garlic lovers.  Orzo cooked in broth doesn't need draining, it gets absorbed and starch thickens it into it's own sauce.Modified from: Garlic Chicken with Orzo
