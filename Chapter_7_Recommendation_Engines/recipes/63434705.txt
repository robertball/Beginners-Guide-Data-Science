Title:
Asian Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cook Time:  20 min
Prep Time:  20 min
Ingredients
( Serves 4 )
3/4 pound ground chicken
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
2 tablespoon soy sauce
2 tablespoon fresh lime juice
2 large garlic cloves, finely minced
1 large jalapeno chili, chopped fine
1 teaspoon garlic chili sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil
2 tablespoon vegetable Oil
Boston lettuce leaves
chopped green onions
roasted peanuts, chopped

Directions:
Cook Time:  20 min
Prep Time:  20 min
Ingredients
( Serves 4 )
Directions
1.Combine first seven ingredients in a bowl and stir to blend well.
2.Heat both oils in a heavy medium sized skillet till hot but not smoking.
3.Add chicken mixture and saut√© until chicken is just cooked through (about 5 minutes).
4.Transfer chicken mixture to a platter and arrange lettuce leaves around edges.
5.Serve, passing the chopped green onions and/or chopped roasted peanuts if you are using these.
