Title:
5- Minute Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups semisweet chocolate morsels
1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
1/4 cup butter
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional) or
1 cup dried fruit (optional)

Directions:
Melt butter in large microwave bowl.
Add morsels and milk into bowl and microwave on medium high in 60 second increments, stirring between increments, until chocolate is melted, about 3-5 minutes.
Stir in nuts or fruit.
Pour into well-greased 8x8" glass baking dish.
Refrigerate until set.
