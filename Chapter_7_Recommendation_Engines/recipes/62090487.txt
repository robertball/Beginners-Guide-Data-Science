Title:
Kiki's Lava Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped Jalapeno peppers, seeds, veins and stems removed
1/2 cup chopped Serrano peppers, seeds, veins and stems removed
ONE ( NOT one cup) Single Habanero pepper, seeds, veins and stems removed
1/2 cup Garlic, chopped
2 tablespoons tarragon vinegar
Salt to Taste

Directions:
In a food processor, blend all ingredients, until it becomes a paste.
Be Careful and enjoy!!

Submitter: 
KIKI810
Description: 
The name for this chili paste came from a very good friend who loves very hot food.  This packs a lot of heat so use it sparingly in soups, salsas, dips or as a condiment.  It's great in marinades and added to mayonnaise to spread on sandwiches or burgers.
