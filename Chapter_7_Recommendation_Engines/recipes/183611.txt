Title:
Newman's Own Honey of a Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991442.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (5 ounce) bags prepared salad greens
2 thinly sliced red onions
1 cup red grape tomatoes, cut in half
2 cups cooked chicken, diced
1/2 cup Newman's Own® Lighten Up® Honey Mustard Dressing

Directions:
Mix two lettuces together. Arrange on plates. Divide onions and tomatoes evenly among the plates. Top with chicken. Dress salads with Newman's Own Lighten Up(TM) Honey Mustard Salad Dressing. Serves 4 as a main course.

Submitter: 
Newman's Own®
Description: 
Whip up this simple, savory and sweet salad for any easy and light meal anytime.
