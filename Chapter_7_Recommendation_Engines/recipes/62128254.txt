Title:
Unleavened Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C. Flour
2 T. Oil or Butter
3 Large Eggs
1/2 C. Water or Milk
1 tsp. Salt

Directions:
In a large bowl combine flour and salt.
Make a well in the center of the flour mixture.
In a separate bowl melt butter and mix in the egg.
Pour the egg mixture into the well of the flour. Mix together with a fork until crumbly.
Slowly add milk, using hands to mix the dough.
When dough holds together in a ball, roll out and place on a greased cookie sheet.
Score with a knife into the desired size squares. Prick with a fork.
Bake at 375 for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sara
Description: 
This is an easy recipe that is the consistency of a heavy bread.
