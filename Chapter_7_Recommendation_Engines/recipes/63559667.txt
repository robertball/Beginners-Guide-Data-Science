Title:
Crispy Juicy Oven-Fried Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2 cups buttermilk
2 cups crushed herb-seasoned dry stuffing
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 egg
1 tablespoon water
2 tablespoons peanut oil

Directions:
Place chicken breasts and buttermilk in re-sealable plastic bag and refrigerate at least one hour.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In a 9 x 13 pan, cover bottom with peanut oil.
In a flat dish, mix together flour, salt and pepper.
In a bowl, slightly beat egg with water.
In another flat dish, place the crushed stuffing.
Remove chicken from buttermilk, dredge each breast in flour, then in egg, then in stuffing, coating to completely cover.
Place in baking dish and bake for 25 minutes.  Turn each piece over and continue baking for another 25 minutes or until chicken is done and coating is very crispy.

Submitter: 
Judy in Delaware
Description: 
This is a very crispy, moist and tender chicken dish without the messy frying.  The oven does a great job making that crispy coating we all love.
