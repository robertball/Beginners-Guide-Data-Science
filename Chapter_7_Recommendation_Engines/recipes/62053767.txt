Title:
outback ceaser salad dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup egg substitute
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons water
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon anchovy paste
2 cloves garlic, pressed
2 teaspoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon coarse ground pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon dried parsley flakes, crushed fine

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl. Use an electric mixer to beat ingredients for about 1 minute.
2. Cover bowl and chill for several hours so that flavors can develop.

Submitter: 
DEBMCE4
Description: 
This tastes much like the recipe of a famous steakhouse chain.  It uses an egg substitute rather than real egg yolks, therefore it will keep fresh for quite a while in the fridge.
