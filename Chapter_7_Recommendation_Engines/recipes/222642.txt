Title:
Ranch Ham Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/874859.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
3/4 avocado - peeled, pitted, and mashed
1 (1 ounce) package ranch salad dressing mix
3 green onions, chopped
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
11 (8 inch) flour tortillas
22 slices thinly sliced ham

Directions:
Beat cream cheese, avocado, and salad dressing mix in a small bowl; stir in onions and bell peppers. Spread 3 tablespoons cream cheese mixture over each tortilla; top each tortilla with two slices ham.
Roll tortillas up tightly and wrap in plastic wrap; refrigerate until firm, about 30 minutes. Unwrap and cut into 3/4-inch slices.

Submitter: 
olebuck
Description: 
Pretty pinwheel appetizers are layered with ham, avocado, and green pepper. Use a jalapeno pepper if you like a little spice.
