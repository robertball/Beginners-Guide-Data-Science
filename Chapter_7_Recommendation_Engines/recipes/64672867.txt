Title:
Greek Tortellini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4588528.jpg
Ingredients:
1 20 oz pkg refrigerated cheese tortellini
1 1/2 c grape tomatoes, cut in half
1 large cucumber, chopped
1 c kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
1/2 red onion, chopped
3/4 c crumbled feta cheese
Dressing....
1/4 c EVOO
3 tbsp red wine vinegar
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp dried oregano
Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Cook tortellini according to pkg directions, drain, rinse, and cool.
Place tortellini in large bowl, add rest of salad ingredients.
In a small bowl, combine all dressing ingredients and whisk well.
Pour dressing over salad and stir until well coated. Serve immediately or chill.
