Title:
Grilled Potato Salad with Crazy Steve's Cajun Cukes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/794533.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lbs small red skin potatoes
1/2 lb bacon diced
1 medium red onion, diced
2 TBS Kosher Salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp rosemary
1 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp celery seed
5 TBS Mayonnaise
2 TBS Dijon mustard
1/2 cup Crazy Steve's Cajun Cukes brine
1 jar Crazy Steve's Cajun Cukes, diced

Directions:
1. Saute bacon and diced onion together. When bacon is slightly crisp, drain bacon and onion from pan.
2. Mix spices into bacon drippings.
3. Wash & dry potatoes. With skins on, roll potatoes in bacon and spice mixture.
4. Heat coals on grill to medium heat. Place potatoes on hot grill. Turn once golden brown. Remove from heat when fork goes into potato easily.
5. Let potatoes cool slightly, then chop into bite size chunks
6. Mix Mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, 1/2 cup Crazy Steve's Cajun Cukes brine & 1 jar Crazy Steve's Cajun Cukes, diced, together with bacon and onion. Mix chopped potatoes into mixture.
Can be served warm or cold

Submitter: 
crazy Steve
Description: 
This potato salad is for anyone who loves bacon & onions, a little sour & spicy. Can be served hot or cold.
