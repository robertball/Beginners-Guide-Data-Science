Title:
Light Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•1 can whole tomatoes (15 ounces)
•1 tbsp Extra-virgin olive oil
•salt (to taste)
•1 clove Fresh garlic
•6 basil leafs

Directions:
Strain a can of whole tomatoes and pull the tomatoes apart by hand. Add the olive oil, salt, garlic, and fresh basil and mix together.
