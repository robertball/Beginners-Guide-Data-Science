Title:
Grilled Chicken Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive or vegetable oil
1/4 cup lemon juice
4 teaspoons minced garlic
2 teaspoons honey
1 1/2 teaspoons dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon Kosher salt
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
1 red bell pepper, cut into large chunks
1 (10 ounce) package fresh cremini mushrooms, sliced in half
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks
MARINATE OVERNIGHT

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine oil, lemon juice, garlic, honey, thyme, red pepper flakes, ground black pepper and Kosher salt.
Cut chicken into 1-inch wide strips.
Place chicken and half the marinade in a large resealable bag, seal, toss to coat; marinate in the refrigerator overnight.
Reserve the other half of the marinade for basting in a covered bowl and place in refrigerator.
Place chicken on skewers on place on the grill over medium heat.
Grill, turning and basting with reserved marinade, for 12 minutes or until juices run clear.
Place red bell pepper, mushroom and pineapple on skewers and grill for 6-8 minutes or until veggies are crisp tender.
Serve over cooked brown or white rice.
