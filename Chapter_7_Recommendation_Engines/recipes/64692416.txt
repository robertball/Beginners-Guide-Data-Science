Title:
Heritage Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5163620.jpg
Ingredients:
15 ounces Tomatoe Sauce or Diced Tomatoe
28 ounces Tomatoe Crushed
2 bouillon cubes or teaspoon base (chicken or beef)
1/2 cup red wine (optional)
6 ounces Tomatoe Paste
1 onion chopped
1/2 cup celery chopped
1 cup orzo
20 ounces frozen mixed vegetables
2 cups baby spinach, rinced (optional)
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese for topping
2 tablespoons basil (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook the orzo as directed on the package and drain.
In a stock pot add the orzo, onion, celery, tomatoes, sauce, paste, bouillon or paste, and red wine.
Bring to light boil, then zimmer at medium heat for 10 minutes, and stir ocassionally.
Add the basil and frozen vegetables and zimmer for another 5 minutes.
Add the baby spinach and stir, then serve in a bowl, salt ad pepper to taste, and top with Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
This was a recipe in a family cookbook from my wife's side. It is simple recipe that skips some of the use of fresh vegetables by substituting frozen. If you like tomato soup then you will like this. I suggest you add more water to your liking when you add the spinach.
