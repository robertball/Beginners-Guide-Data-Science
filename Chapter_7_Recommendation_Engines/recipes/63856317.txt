Title:
Juicy Steak, Red Onion and Blue Cheese Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
STEAK
2 x 225g/8oz Sirloin/Rib Eye Steaks
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
4 slices Ciabatta - for toasting
ONION SLICES
1 Red Onion - finely sliced
3tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
BLUE CHEESE SAUCE
120ml/4fl oz/10tbsp White Wine
120g/4oz Strong Blue Cheese
45ml/3tbsp Double Cream or Cr√®me
Fraiche

Directions:
ONION SLICES
Mix the onion with oil in a bowl and season with salt and pepper;
Oil and heat a griddle pan, add the onion and cook for some 5 minutes or so or until soft and golden;
Remove and keep warm;
THE CHEESE SAUCE
In a small saucepan, pour in the wine and, on high heat, reduce by half;
Reduce heat to low, add the cheese, stirring continuously until melted;
Add the cream and, still on low heat and stirring continuously, cook until sauce thickens, but not boils, to your taste.
THE SANDWICHES
Oil and season the steaks;
Oil and heat a griddle pan to sizzling, add the steaks and cook each some minutes on each side to desired taste - rare/well done etc;
Remove and keep warm;
Lightly oil the Ciabatta, add to the pan and, still on high heat, toast to desired crispiness;
Remove and lay the steaks onto a slice each, cover with onions, drizzle over the sauce and top with other slices.
