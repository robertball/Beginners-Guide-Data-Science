Title:
Delicious Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs
1-1/4 cups oil
1 (15 oz) can pumpkin
2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
2 (3 oz) packages cook-and-serve vanilla pudding mix
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Beat eggs; add oil and pumpkin.  Beat until smooth.  Combine remaining ingredients and beat in.  Pour into two greased or sprayed 8 x 4" loaf pans, and bake at 325 degrees for 75-80 minutes; or five 5 x 2-1/2" mini loaf pans for 50-55 minutes.  (Note:  Do not bake at a higher temp or the outside will be burned and the inside gooey).
