Title:
Italian Spaghetti Sauce with Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS
1 pound lean ground beef
1 pound Italian Sausage
2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1 tablespoon Fresh parsley
2 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tsp dried basil
3 egg, beaten
SAUCE
1 1/2 cup chopped onion
10 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup olive oil
4 (28 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes
4 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoon white sugar
2 bay leaf
2 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tsp Italian seasoning

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, parsley, Parmesan, black pepper, basil, garlic powder and beaten eggs.  Mix well and form into 12 balls.  Store, covered, in refrigerator until needed. Brown meatballs in ooven???
In a large saucepan over medium heat, saute onion and garlic in olive oil until onion is translucent.  Stir in tomatoes, salt, sugar and bay leaf.  Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer 90 minutes.  Stir in tomato paste, Italian Seasoning, basil, 1/2 teaspoon pepper and meatballs and simmer 30 minutes more.  Serve.
