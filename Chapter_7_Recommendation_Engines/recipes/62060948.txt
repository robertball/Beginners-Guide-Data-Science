Title:
Chewy Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
4 eggs
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup sifted all-purpose flour

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Melt margarine and chocolate in a double boiler. Let cool.
3. Beat eggs and salt untill light and foamy. Add sugar and vanilla and continue beating untill well creamed.
4.With a few swift strokes, combine the chocolate mixture and the egg mixture
5. Bake for 25 minutes in a greased 9x13 inch pan. Cut when cool.
