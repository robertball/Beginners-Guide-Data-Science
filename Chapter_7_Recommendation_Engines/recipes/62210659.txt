Title:
Vanillekipferl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound Flour
180 grams Butter
100 grams ground Almonds
90 grams Sugar
Vanilla sugar to coat

Directions:
Mix ingredients together.
Knead into a soft dough.
Roll marble size amounts into little slugs, and lay them on a parchement paper lined baking sheet in cresent-shapes.
Bake at 180ÂºC for about 15 minutes.
While still warm, roll in vanilla sugar to coat.
