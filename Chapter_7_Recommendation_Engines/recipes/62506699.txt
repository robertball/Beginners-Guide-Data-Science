Title:
No-Bake Cranberry Sauce Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 tablespoon butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup Splenda Sugar Blend (for baking)
1 (9 inch) prepared shortbread cookie crust
1 (3 ounce) package raspberry-flavored gelatin mix
1 cup boiling water
1 (14.5 ounce) can whole berry cranberry sauce
1/3 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Beat the cream cheese, butter and vanilla extract together in a mixing bowl until smooth and fluffy.
Sift in the Splenda and mix until thoroughly blended. Spread evenly over the bottom of the pie crust.
Whisk the gelatin and boiling water together in a small bowl until dissolved.
Stir in the cranberry sauce and walnuts.
Pour over the cream cheese layer in the pie crust.
Refrigerate until set, 3 to 4 hours.
Garnish with toasted wlnuts.
Serve chilled.
