Title:
Japanese 7 Spice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4554824.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons dried orange peel
4 teaspoons garlic powder
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons toasted sesame seeds
2 teaspoons black sesame seeds
2 teaspoons ground black pepper
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Directions:
Combine orange peel, garlic powder, salt, toasted sesame seeds, black sesame seeds, black pepper, and red pepper flakes in a jar. Seal jar.

Submitter: 
kimmi
Description: 
Spice mix. Store in a jar for up to 6 months.
