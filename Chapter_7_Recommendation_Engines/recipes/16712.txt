Title:
Dessert Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5686895.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18 ounce) package refrigerated sugar cookie dough
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1/2 cup sliced banana
1/2 cup sliced fresh strawberries
1/2 cup crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 cup seedless grapes, halved

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Press cookie dough evenly into a 12 inch pizza pan. Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, until golden brown. Cool in pan on wire rack.
Spread whipped topping over cooled crust. Arrange fruit in a decorative pattern. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Michele O'Sullivan
Description: 
Refreshing sliced fruit and creamy whipped topping over a tender cookie crust -  delicious, impressive, and easy. Enjoy! Kiwi and peaches may also be added to pizza if desired.
