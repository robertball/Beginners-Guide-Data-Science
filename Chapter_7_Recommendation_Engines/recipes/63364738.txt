Title:
Beef Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 can refried beans
1 10-12 ounce bag tortilla chips, crushed
1 jar mild salsa
1 small onion, chopped
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
1 package taco seasoning
1/2 cup water
3 Tbsp. butter
3 Tbsp. flour
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup sour cream or plain nonfat yogurt
1 (4 oz) can diced green chillies

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  grease a 9x11 baking dish.
Brown ground beef with chopped onion.  Add refried beans, taco seasoning, water, and salsa.  in a separate pan melt butter and stir in flour.  bring to boil and cook 1 minute.  stir in chicken broth and simmer until thickened.  stir in sour cream and diced green chilis.
Put half of crushed tortilla chips in bottom of baking dish.  Spread half of ground beef mixture, half of shredded cheese, then repeat this layer.  Pour sour cream sauce over top and bake uncovered for 20 minutes.
