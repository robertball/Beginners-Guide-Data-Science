Title:
Paty Pie's Double Layer Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1st layer
4 oz Cream Cheese
1 Tbls Half and Half
1 Tbls Sugar
1 1/2 Cups of cool whip topping
1 Grahm Cracker Crust (6 oz )
2nd layer
1 cup half & Half
2 pkgs vanilla instant pudding
1 can pumpkin
1 tsp cinnamon
1 3/4 Tsp pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
1st layer:
Mix Cream Cheese, 1 Tbs. milk and sugar in large bowl with wire whisk until smooth. Gently stir in whipped topping. Spread evenly into pie crust.
2nd layer:
Pour 1 cup milk in to bowl. Add pudding mixes. Beat with wire whisk 1 minute. (mixture will be thick.) Stir in Pumpkin and Spices with wire whisk until well blended. Spread over cream cheese mixture
Refrigerate 4 hours or until set. Garnish with rest of whipped topping if desired.

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
Going through my moms hand written recipe book... I remembered this one but thought it was long gone... many times she would use canned pie fillings for the top (2nd Layer) Easy and so good!
