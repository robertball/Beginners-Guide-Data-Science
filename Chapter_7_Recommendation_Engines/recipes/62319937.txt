Title:
Bev's Orange Chicken--Modified Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup orange juice concentrate
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
4 boneless chicken breast cutlets

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small bowl, stir together the orange juice concentrate, soy sauce, onion soup mix and garlic powder; set aside. Rinse chicken, and pat dry. Place chicken cutlets into a 8x8 inch glass baking dish. Pour the orange juice mixture over.
Bake, uncovered, for 1 hour in the preheated oven.  Baste every 20 minutes if chicken isn't covered by sauce.
