Title:
Amazing Buffalo Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1981800.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups diced cooked chicken
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup blue cheese salad dressing
1/2 cup hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHotÂ®)
1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
1/4 cup ranch dressing, or to taste

Directions:
Stir chicken, cream cheese, blue cheese dressing, hot pepper sauce, crumbled blue cheese, and ranch dressing together in a slow cooker.
Cook in the slow cooker on High until heated through, about 20 minutes.
