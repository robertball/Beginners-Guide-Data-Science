Title:
Curry Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Quarts Cooked chicken or Turkey
2 Cans water chestnuts
1 LB seedless grapes
2 Cups Toasted Sliced Almonds
2 Cups diced celery
2 1/2 Cups Mayonnaise
1 Tbsp Curry Powder
2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
1 Large Can Pineapple Chunks Drained

Directions:
Dice chestnuts and chicken
Slice Grapes in half
Dice Celery and mix with 1 1/2 cups almonds
SAUCE; Mix Mayonnaise, curry, soy, and lemon juice
Combine with chicken mixture
Pour in Lettuce Cups and garnish with Almonds and pineapple chunks
