Title:
Spaghetti Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces spaghetti
2 tbsp olive oil
2 1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs
1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
4 tbsp butter, softened
1 cup ricotta cheese
1 lb lean ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1/4 cup green bell pepper
6 ounces tomato paste
1 tsb sugar
1 tsb dried oregano
1/4 tsb garlic powder
2 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Boil spaghetti with 2 teaspoons salt and 1 tablespoon oil until tender
Drain
Add eggs, parmesan cheese, and butter.
Mix and shape into a crust in greesed 10-inch pie plate.
Spread Ricotta cheese over crust.
Preheat over to 350 degrees
Heat 1 tablespoon oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.
Brown hamburger meat with onion and green pepper.
Drain.
Mix in tomato paste, sugar, oregano, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and garlic powder.
Spread mixture over Ricotta cheese.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
Sprinkle with Mozzarella cheese and bake 5-10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Aubrie Maegan Puffenbarger
Description: 
“Cooked noodles coated with eggs, butter and Parmesan, are pressed into the bottom of a pie pan and topped with a savory fry of beef, onion, green pepper, tomatoes, oregano and garlic powder, then mozzarella is baked over the top.”
