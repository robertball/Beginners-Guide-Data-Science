Title:
Easiest fruit Crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  14oz cans of sliced peaches drained
2  14oz cans of sliced pears drained
1/2 cup of all purpose flour
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/3 cup cold butter of margerine

Directions:
place fruit in a 9x9 inch baking dish
Combine dry ingredients, cut in butter or margerine until mixture is crumbly and moist.  Sprinkle over fruit evenly.
Bake at 375 F until topping is crisp and golden.  Can be served warm or cold with vanilla ice cream or wipped cream.
