Title:
chocolate terrine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz coarsely chopped bittersweet chocolate (or chocolate chips)
1/2 cup of butter, softened
4 egg yolks
2 1/2 oz of confectioners sugar
1/2 tsp of ground cinnamon
2 cups of heavy cream, chilled
2-3 drops of vanilla extract

Directions:
Melt the chocolate in a double boiler or in the microwave till completely melted. Add the butter and stir till smooth.
In a large bowl, using an electric mixer on medium to high speed beat the confectioners sugar with the egg yolks and cinnamon until foamy. Beat in the chocolate mixture, set aside to cool
In a mixing bowl, whip the cream, add the vanilla and continue to whip until the cream forms soft peaks. Fold into the chocolate mixture
Line an 11" cake mold with waxed paper and pour in the batter. cover and refridgerate or 12-24 hours
serve sliced
