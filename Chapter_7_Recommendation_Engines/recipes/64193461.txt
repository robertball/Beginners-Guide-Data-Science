Title:
three cheese butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup softened unsalted butter
1/3 cup goat cheese
1/4 grated old cheddar
1/4 c grated parmesan
1 tbsp chopped parsley
1/2 tsp cayenne
pinch of salt

Directions:
mix all together in food processor or mini chopper
scape butter mixture onto wax paper and roll and pinch ends, refrigerate and slice for use
