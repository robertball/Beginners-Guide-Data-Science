Title:
Mushroom Brie Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One bunch green onions, white part only – chopped
1 medium Shallot – minced
6 cups sliced mushrooms (more if you like) - (I use a variety – Shitake, Crimini, Baby Bellas)
1 stick butter – divided
1/2 c. dry Sherry
8 cups beef broth
4 cups Half-n-Half (1 qt.)
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
6 oz. (or so) Brie cheese - cubed – RIND REMOVED (all of it) *
5 T. cornstarch
5 T. water to mix w/cornstarch
1/2 t. Cayenne Pepper
1/4 t. nutmeg
Pita Bread

Directions:
Saute onion, shallots and mushrooms in 1/2 stick butter.  Add Sherry and beef broth.  Simmer for 10 minutes.  Add Half-n-Half, and Brie.
Simmer soup whisking until the cheese melts and is smooth.  Add Cream of Mushroom soup and the rest of the butter.  Mix cornstarch w/water and whisk into the soup to thicken.  Bring to a soft rolling boil and remove from the heat.  Add Cayenne, Nutmeg and Salt (if needed) at this time.
Serve with Pita Bread
