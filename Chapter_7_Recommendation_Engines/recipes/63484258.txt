Title:
amateur coffee and vanilla icecream drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T coffee beans
24 oz water
2 1/2 oz condensed sweet milk
2 T Hershey syrup
1/2 cup whole milk
1 1/2tray ice cubes
vanilla ice cream

Directions:
make coffee with beans and water
use food processor to crush 1 tray of ice cubes and then put crushed iced cubes into coffee; make sure to unplug coffee pot to keep cool
add condensed sweet milk, Hershey syrup, whole milk
then pour into a glass full of vanilla ice cream
let sit for two minutes, so ice cream blends in the coffee
add crushed ice cubes to glass
