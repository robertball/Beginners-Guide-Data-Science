Title:
Macaroni and Cheese W/bacon and onion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/426003.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Cups elbow macaroni
18 Oz Velveeta cheese (cut into cubes)
2 8 oz bags shredded cheddar cheese
1 large onion
1 lb package of bacon
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup butter
1/2 cup flour
3 1/2 cups milk

Directions:
Boil noodles as directed on box. Set aside and let cool. Cut bacon into bite size pieces and fry in pan until golden brown and crisp, drain and set aside. Cut one Large onion into small to med chunks or to your liking. In a med pan add 1/2 cup butter let melt and then add onion. Saut√© until transparent set aside.
In large pot add 1/2 cup butter let melt then add in 1/2 cup flour to make a rue. Mix well then slowly add in milk stirring until well mixed, add in your salt and pepper. When mixture is warmed through add in Velveeta stir until melted then add 1 package cheddar cheese stir until melted then add onion(with the butter) and bacon and mix well. Then add your noodles to cheese sauce and transfer to a 9x13 baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees uncovered for 40 minutes or until bubbling. Then add 1/2 to 3/4 bag cheddar cheese on top and bake for 10 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Amy Maynard
Description: 
This is what I call comfort food. I get nothing but compliments on this one. Even my in-laws loved it. A rich and creamy cheese sauce incorporated with bacon and onion.
