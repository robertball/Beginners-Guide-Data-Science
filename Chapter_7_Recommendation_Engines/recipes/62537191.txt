Title:
Jennifer's Spicy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521334.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb of lean hamburger meat
2 Cans of Heinz Baby Diced Tomatoes
1 Can of Heinz Tomato Sauce
2 Cans of mild kidney beans
1 Can of hot kidney beans
1/4 Cup Diced Onion
1 Mild Chili Seasoning Packet
1 Hot Chili Seasoning Packet
2 Cups of Water (add as much or as little needed for preferred consistency)

Directions:
Brown the meat by boiling in skillet, meat should be crumbly. Drain grease and water from meat and add meat and all other ingredients into large pot.
Heat until hot. Top with cheese, ketchup, crackers, jalapenos, anything really. Enjoy!
