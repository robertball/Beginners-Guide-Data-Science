Title:
Orange Poppy Seed Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oil
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup sour cream
1/3 cup poppy seeds
1/4 cup orange juice
1 Tbsp grated orange rind
1 tsp vanilla
1-1/4 cups flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
Pinch of salt
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Cream sugar and oil.  Beat in eggs.  Add sour cream, poppy seeds, orange juice, orange rind, and vanilla and mix well.
Add flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  Mix well.  Bake in greased bundt pan at 350 degrees F for 40 minutes.
Let stand 5 minutes, invert on rack and cool completely.  Dust with powdered sugar.
