Title:
Cranberry-Brie Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup cranberry sauce
1 puff pastry sheet
1 Brie triangle

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 375
Thaw the pastry sheet flat and cut into 3-inch squares.
Press each square into each hole of a mini muffin tin with a wine cork.
Place a 1-inch slice of brie in the center of each puff and top with 1 tablespoon of cranberry sauce.
Bake for 10 min or until the edges are toasted a light brown.
Let cool for at least 5 min before serving.
