Title:
Pretend You're in the Tropics Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2387122.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana, sliced
1 mango - peeled, seeded, and diced
1 (6 ounce) container coconut yogurt
6 fluid ounces 1% milk
1 tablespoon ground flax seed

Directions:
Blend banana, mango, coconut yogurt, milk, and ground flax seed in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
BigShotsMom
Description: 
Banish the blahs with this slightly sweet, rich, healthy smoothie.
