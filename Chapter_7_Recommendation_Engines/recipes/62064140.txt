Title:
The Best Flourless Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup creamy peanut butter
2 eggs
1 cup maple syrup
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350 deegrees F.
2. Mix eggs, peanut butter and syrup in a medium sized bowl.
3. Stir in nutmeg, cinnamon, and pumpkin pie spice.
4. Bake for 10 minute. Let cool.
