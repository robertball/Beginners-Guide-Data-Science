Title:
Cream Cheese Macaroons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
3 oz. cream cheese
1 egg yolk
1 tsp. oranje juice
1 tsp. almond extract
1 1/4 cups flour
2 tsp. baking powder
3 3/4 cups sweetened coconut, divided

Directions:
Beat butter, cream cheese, and sugar in a large bowl until well-blended.  Add egg yolk, orange juice, & almond extract, mix well.  In a small bowl, stir together flour and baking powder.  Gradually add to butter mixture, beating until well blended.  Stir in 3 cups coconut.  Cover and refrigerate until firm enough to handle, about 1 hour.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Shape dough into  1 inch balls and roll in reamining 3/4 cup coconut.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake 10-12 minutes or until lightly browned.  Cool a few minutes on cookie sheet, transfer cookie to cooling rack to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Ali
Description: 
This is my mom's recipe.  It makes a very creamy cookie, and they aren't as sweet as a traditional macaroon.
