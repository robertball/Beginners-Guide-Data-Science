Title:
Blueberry Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package lemon cake mix
4 cups fresh blueberries
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup butter,very cold cut in small pieces
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix berries, sugar, and cinnamon in the bottom of a 9 x 13 inch pan. Mix the cold butter and cake mix in a food processor until well blended and Cover berries with  cake mix. Do not stir.
Bake for 30 minutes, or until light brown.  Serve warm or cold. Top with ice cream if desired.
