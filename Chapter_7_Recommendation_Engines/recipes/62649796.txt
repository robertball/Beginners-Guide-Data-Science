Title:
Turkey Burger and Bean Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground turkey breast
1/2 onion, chopped
1 can pinto beans (drained)
1 can tomato sauce
1 can diced tomatoes (drained
sloppy joe "spice packet" of your choice

Directions:
Brown turkey and onion.
Add beans, sauce, diced tomatoes, and packet. Stir.
Bring to boil then simmer 10-20 minutes.
Serve open-faced on bread or roll.
Add low-fat cheese if you wish.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
This is a healthy twist on an old favorite.
