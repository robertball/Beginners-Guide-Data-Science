Title:
Sour Cream Banana Coffee Cake revised Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
1-1/4 cup sugar, divided
3 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 mashed ripe bananas
3/4 cup sour cream
1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, cream butter and white sugar. Beat in eggs and vanilla. Stir in bananas and sour cream. Combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture. Combine the walnuts, cinnamon and brown sugar.
Spoon half of batter into a greased 9x13 pan. Sprinkle with nut mixture; top with remaining batter. Bake at 350 degrees F for 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.
