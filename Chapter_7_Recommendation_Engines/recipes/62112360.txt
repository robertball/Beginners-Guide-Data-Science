Title:
Kathleen's Lima Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 oz.) frozen baby lima beans
1/2 C. sour cream
1 Tbsp. milk
1 Tbsp. vinegar
1 Tbsp. salad oil
1/2 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp. sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
dash paprika
1/2 C. thinly sliced celery
4 lettuce cups.

Directions:
Cook lima beans according to package directions.
Drain and cool.
Blend sour cream, milk, vinegar, salad oil, garlic, sugar, salt, pepper and paprika.
Combine with the lima beans and celery.
Cover and chill.
At serving time, spoon mixture into lettuce cups and sprinkle with additional paprika for color or add small pieces of red pepper or pimiento.
