Title:
Chipa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6479584.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups tapioca flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
2 cups Mexican mixed cheese
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 cup oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
Mix all the dried ingredients with the cheese
Make a well in the middle of the dry ingredients
Add the eggs, milk and oil
Mix well
Let it sit for 15 minutes
Form into balls and place on cookie sheet
Bake at 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 15 minutes
Makes 10 chips
