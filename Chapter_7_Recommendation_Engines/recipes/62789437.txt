Title:
Super Easy Baby Back Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 racks of top-loin ribs 2 lbs each (commonly called baby back ribs)
1 Tbsp kosher salt - to season ribs
1 1/2 tsp ground black pepper - to season ribs
Ingredients for sauce
1/2 cup Dijon Mustard
1/2 dark-brown sugar, packed
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1/4 cup honey
4 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
Turn on one side of a gas grill to medium-high heat.  Cover and preheat until the thermometer registers 300 degrees.  Sprinkle the ribs evenly on both sides with salt and pepper.  Place bone side down as far from the flame as possible (you can use a second tier rack too.  Cover and cook until the meat pulls back from the bones (1 1/4 to 1 1/2 hours). Combine ingredients for sauce in a bowl and mix well.  When ribs are cooked, brush one side of a rib rack with the sauce, flip onto the flame, sauce side down, and cook until glazed (2 to 3 minutes.  Repeat with other side.  Remove from the grill and repeat glazing with with two other rib racks.  Cut between the bones and serve.
