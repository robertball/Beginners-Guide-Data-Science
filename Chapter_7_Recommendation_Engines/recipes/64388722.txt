Title:
Easy Spicy Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of frozen spinach
4 tbsp of salsa

Directions:
Follow the heating directions for cooking the frozen spinach.
After it's finished warming up, place spinach in a bowl and spoon in 4 tbsp of salsa.
Mix well.

Submitter: 
Chef Lizzie
Description: 
So spicy and delicious! It's a tasty way to eat your green veggies!
