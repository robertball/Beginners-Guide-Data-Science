Title:
Hummus, Pita Chips and Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small pita, 100% whole-wheat (4-inch)
1/2 cup(s) pepper(s), red, bell sliced
2 stalk(s) celery
1/2 cup(s) cucumber(s) sliced
4 tablespoon hummus

Directions:
Slice pita into triangles and slice vegetables. Serve with hummus and enjoy!
