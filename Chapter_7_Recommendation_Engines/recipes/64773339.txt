Title:
Keto  Spinach Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz package of cream cheese
2 cups shredded  mozzarella cheese
1 tsp onion powder
1 tbsp baking powder
3/4 C coconut flour
2 eggs
1/4 tsp salt
1 C shredded cheddar
1 clove  garlic, minced
2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
1/2 tsp garlic salt

Directions:
Divide cream cheese into 2 portions, 3 oz and 5 oz.
place 3 oz cream cheese and mozzarella in to a microwave safe bowl and cook in 1 minute intervals until melted; combine together.
To the cheese mixture add, coconut flour, onion powder, eggs, baking powder and salt. Mix until well combined.  Place in refrigerator.
In another bowl mix remaining cream cheese, cheddar, spinach, garlic and garlic salt until well combined.
Place dough between 2 pieces of parchment and roll out to a 11x14 in rectangle. As close to 1/4 inches thick.
Spread spinach mixture  onto the dough leaving one inch, across the long side uncovered.
Using the parchment gently roll up jelly roll style. Ending with the uncovered strip.
Let rest in the fridge for 10 minutes.
Preheat the oven to 350. Grease a 9 inch springform pan.
Remove roll from refrigerator and cut inch discs. You may have to discard the ends for the best presentation.
Arrange in the pan and bake in the oven for 25-35 minutes until golden.
