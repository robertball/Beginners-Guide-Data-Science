Title:
Fried Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
1 egg, beaten
1/2 c. water
2 tbsp. corn meal
1 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 c. milk
1 tbsp. sugar

Directions:
Mix flour, baking powder, sugar and corn meal.  Mix in egg, salt, water and milk.  Beat well until smooth.  Heat oil to 370 degrees, drop mixture into oil by teaspoon.  Fry until puffed and golden brown.  Drain on paper towels and sprinkle with X sugar.
