Title:
Eggplant Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 choped eggplant
6 stalks celery
2 lg onions
1 green pepper
3 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Soak chopped eggplant in salted cold water for 2 hours
Fry celery, onions, green pepper in oil until soft.
Rinse egglplant.
Add to pot.
Cover and cook 45 mins, stirring occasionally.
Add 2 cups cooked rice.
