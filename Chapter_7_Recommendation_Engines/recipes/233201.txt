Title:
HERDEZ® Hot Chipotle Mocha Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1025438.jpg
Ingredients:
8 fluid ounces hot, strong brewed coffee
2 tablespoons miniature chocolate chips
2 tablespoons vanilla-flavored coffee creamer
1/2 teaspoon HERDEZ® Traditional Chipotle Mexican Cooking Sauce, or to taste
Sugar to taste (optional)

Directions:
Combine coffee and chocolate chips together in large mug and mix until chocolate has melted. Stir in creamer. Add cooking sauce and sugar to taste; stir.

Submitter: 
Arizona Desert Flower
Description: 
Spicy kick to get your day off right! Experiment with different flavored creamers to mix up your coffee drink!
