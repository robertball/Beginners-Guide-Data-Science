Title:
Surprise Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/490433.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup unsalted butter
2 eggs
4 ripe bananas, mashed
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon baking powder
dash ea.
cinnamon
nutmeg
allspice
cloves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).  Grease and flour one 9 x 13 inch cake pan.
Cream together the butter or margarine and the sugar.
Add eggs, bananas, flour, soda, powder salt, nuts, spices and vanilla.  Mix thoroughly and pour batter into the  prepared pan.
Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 30 minutes.  Cool and frost cake.
Frost with a light white frosting.
Variations:
add a bit of lemon extract and zest for a zestier,, summery flavor.
add some cinnamon for a more fall/wintery flavor
or try some black walnut, rum or bourbon extract, for a heavier taste.
