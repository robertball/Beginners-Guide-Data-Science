Title:
Simple Savory Chicken Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds chicken breast cut into chunks
1 white onion quartered
1 12 oz bag of mini red, yellow and orange bell peppers (about 14) sliced in half lengthwise
2 tsp oregano
4 oz crumbled  feta cheese
1/2 C Italian dressing

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a resealable plastic bag. Mix well and marinate for 2-4 hours.
Soak 6 wooden skewers for 30 minutes.
Preheat outdoor grill on the low setting.
Thread chicken, peppers and onion on skewers. Taking care to keep as much cheese on the skewer as possible.
Place skewers on the lightly oiled grate. Close lid.
After 8 minutes, gently flip the skewers. Grill an additional 6 minutes. Remove from heat and let rest for 5 minutes.

Description: 
These grilled chicken skewers are easy.  Grill them on low heat for juicy chicken without a lot of flair ups. You can put the chicken down and forget it. Make sure to use the mini peppers because they trap some of the cheese.
