Title:
Salmon Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/475756.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Eggs
1 pkg Saltine Crackers
1 t Seasoned Salt
2 t Old Bay
4 cans Salmon (undrained)
========
GARNISH
========
Lemon Juice
Lemon Pepper Spice

Directions:
MIX first four ingredients to a large mixing bowl.
OPEN cans and add to bowl.
BLEND with hand mixer until completely mixed.
HEAT electric skillet to 350-375Â°.
SPRAY skillet with cooking spray.
FORM mixture into patties by packing into a greased 1/2 cup measuring cup, put on griddle, then smash with a greased spatula.
COOK patties on both sides until golden brown.
SERVE with lemon juice and lemon pepper seasoning.
