Title:
Cinnamon Coffee Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/687749.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
TOPPING:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar; beat in egg and milk. Combine flour, baking powder, salt and nutmeg; stir into creamed mixture (batter will be stiff). Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full. Bake at 350 degrees F for 15-20 minutes or until muffins test done. Meanwhile, melt butter and combine sugar and cinnamon. Remove muffins from oven; immediately dip tops in butter, then in cinnamon-sugar.

Sharon  Mensing
