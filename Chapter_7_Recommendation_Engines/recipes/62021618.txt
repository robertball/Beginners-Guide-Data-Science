Title:
Blueberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1/4 cup sour cream
2 mashed bananas
1 1/3 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups blueberries

Directions:
Cream the sugar and butter.  Beat in eggs and sour cream.  Add bananas.  Combine dry ingredients and stir in just until combined.  Fold in berries.  Bake in sprayed loaf pan and bake about 1 hr at 350 degrees.
