Title:
Mandarin Orange Rice and Shrimp Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 11 oz can mandarin oranges, drained, juice reserved
water
1 Tbsp vegetable oil, divided
1 tsp salt
1 c U.S. jasmine or long grain white rice
2 Tbsp bottled Thai sweet chili sauce plus additional for topping
1 lb med. shrimp, peeled and deveined
12 whole large leaves, bibb or iceberg lettuce
1 c matchstick cut or shredded carrots

Directions:
1. Combine mandarin orange juice with enough water to equal 2 c. liquid. Bring juice mixture, 1 tsp. oil and salt to boil in saucepan over med.-high heat. Stir in rice; cover and reduce heat to simmer. Cook 15 min. or until liquid is absorbed.
2. Stir mandarin oranges and sweet chili sauce into cooked rice. Meanwhile, heat remaining 2 tsp. oil in skillet over med. heat. Add shrimp and saut√© just until cooked through, about 4-5 min.
3. Arrange lettuce leaves on platter. Spoon rice into center of each lettuce leaf. Top with carrots and shrimp. Drizzle sauce on top.
