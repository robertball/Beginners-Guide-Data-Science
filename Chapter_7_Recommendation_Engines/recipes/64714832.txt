Title:
Nicole's White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5705534.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 pound chicken breast, chopped into bite size pieces
1 medium onion, chopped
2 large jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
1 tablespoon flour
3 15 ounce cans navy beans
1 10 ounce can green enchilada sauce
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon Mexican oregano
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 cup chicken broth

Directions:
1. Heat oil in a large soup pot over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook for 4 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.
2. Add onion and jalapeno. Cook for another 4 minutes or until onions are translucent.
3. Add flour and stir until incorporated.
4. Stir in remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
Change things up this winter by making chili with chicken instead of beef. For a milder version you can substitute a green bell pepper for the jalapenos. Garnish with cheese and cilantro if desired.
