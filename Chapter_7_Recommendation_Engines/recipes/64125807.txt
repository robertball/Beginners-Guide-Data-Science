Title:
Hot Mexican Bean Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1317631.jpg
Ingredients:
Crepes
1 C. All purpose flour
1/2 C. Milk
1/2 C. Water
2 Eggs
2 Tbsp. Melted butter
Filling
1 14 oz can Pinto beans, drained
1 C. Whole kernel corn, drained
1 C. Que Pasa Medium salsa
1 C. Whole tomatoes
2 tsp. Dry ginger
1/2 tsp. Chili powder
1/2 C. Monterey Jack Cheese, grated

Directions:
Combine milk, eggs, flour and melted butter together. Beat with a wire whisk until mixture is free of lumps
Pour 1/4 cup of  batter into saute pan over medium heat and cook crepe on one side for 2 to 3 minutes until crepe develops bubbles on the surface. Turn over and wait for a minute and then flip onto a serving plate
To prepare filling heat pinto beans in a small saucepan and bring to a boil. Remove from heat and allow to cool. Mash beans with a potato masher to a fine puree and set aside
In a large bowl combine salsa, corn, tomatoes and spices together. Add  bean puree and mix all ingredients together well.
Spread mixture over the middle of one crepe and roll up from one end tucking the end in neatly. Sprinkle with grated cheese on top of crepes. Assemble crepes on a parchment lined cookie sheet and bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees Fahrenheit until cheese is melted.
