Title:
Chocolate peanutbutter cornflake cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup chocolate chips
2 Tbsp peanut butter
3 Cups cornflakes

Directions:
On low heat, melt chocolate chips and peanut butter.
Stir in cornflakes til completed covered with mixture.
Spoon onto wax paper and place in fridge to cool.

Submitter: 
linda
Description: 
Sweet and salty, these cookies are quick and easy to make, requiring only three ingredients.
