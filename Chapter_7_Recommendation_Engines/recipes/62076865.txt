Title:
Skewered Grilled Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs new red potatoes, quartered
1/2 C water
1/2 C light mayo
1/4 C dry white wine
2 t dried, crushed romemary
1 t garlic powder

Directions:
Cook potatoes on high in microwave with water for 15 minutes, stirring half way through. Drain.
Mix the rest of the ingredients,pour over potatoes, and toss to coat. Let marinate for up to an hour.
Skerwer poatoes, saving left over marinade. Grill, covered on high 6-8 minutes brushing occasionally with reserved marinade and turning halfway through.

Submitter: 
kimberlyj
Description: 
New potatoes coated in a romemary and garlic marinade then skewered and grilled.
