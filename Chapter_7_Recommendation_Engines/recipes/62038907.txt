Title:
First Prize Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
2 cups brown sugar
1/4 lb. butter
1 tsp. corn syrup
1 ( 6oz. ) can carnation milk
2 cups icing sugar
Walnuts,chopped

Directions:
Put 1st. 5 ingredients in a pot on stove and boil  strong for 5 min. stirring continously. (Turn stove down a bit after the mixture starts to boil strong. Down to Med.) Remove and add 2 cups of icing sugar. Place in greased pan and cut when cool.Add walnuts. (optional)
