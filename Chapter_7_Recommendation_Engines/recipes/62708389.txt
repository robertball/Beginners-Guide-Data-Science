Title:
Fruit Filled Meringues Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614295.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large peaches, peeled, halved and pitted
1 cup fresh blueberries or frozen blueberries thawed
2 tbs orange juice
2 egg whites
Â¼ granulated sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F
Pat peaches dry with paper towels and place hollow side up on a baking sheet.
Divide 1 cup of blueberries among the hollows of the peach halves.
Drizzle with orange juice . Set aside
In a bowl using a electric mixer beat the egg whites to soft peaks.
Gradually add the granulated sugar and continue to beat until stiff peaks form
Using a thin metal spatula gently spread meringue mix over top of each peach.
If you have a pastry bag pipe mergingue stars over each peach half.
Bake until meringue is golden brown about 10 minutes.
Dust desserts with confectioners and sugar.
