Title:
Baked Beans Old Fashion Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef, browned and drained
2 large cans baked beans or pork and beans
1 C. ketchup
2 tbsp. dry mustard
2 tbsp. vinegar
1/4 C. brown sugar
1 pkg. dry onion soup mix (Lipton)

Directions:
Brown the ground beef in a skillet and drain the fat.
In a large casserole dish combine the ingredients and bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Baked Beans with ground meat adds flavor and texture. Something different from the usual baked bean recipes.
