Title:
Green Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30 oz. canned green beans, drained
1 red onion, minced
1 - 16oz. bottle Italian salad dressing

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Let chill before serving

Submitter:

Description: 
Italian dressing and fresh onion give new life to these canned green beans.
