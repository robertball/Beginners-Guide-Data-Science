Title:
Stacey's Apple Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 cup milk
1 teaspoon melted butter
1 egg, beaten
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup finely diced granny smith apples
oil
powdered sugar

Directions:
Combine all purpose flour, baking powder, brown sugar, cinnamon and salt using a whisk until combined.
In a seperate bowl combine milk, melted butter, beaten egg, vanilla and apples until combined.
Add flour mixture to wet mixture until just combined.  Do not over mix.
Heat 1/4 inch of oil in large skillet to 350 degrees.  Drop rounded teaspoons of batter and fry on both sides until golden brown.
Drain on paper towels.  Using a sifter sprinkle powdered sugar on top of fritters.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
After trying to find a recipe for apple fritters that would tantalize my taste buds I decided to create my own!  My first try I was able to create a recipe that delivers on every level the perfect apple fritter!  Crisp on the outside and perfectly tender on the inside!
