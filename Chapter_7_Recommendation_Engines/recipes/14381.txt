Title:
Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1010375.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 clove crushed garlic
2 teaspoons white sugar
2 teaspoons salt

Directions:
In a jar with a tight fitting lid, combine vinegar, oil, garlic, sugar, and salt. Shake well.

Submitter: 
Karen Castle
Description: 
This is simple to make and delicious, especially if you like garlic.
