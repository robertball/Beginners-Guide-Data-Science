Title:
MONGOLIAN BARBECUE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One lbs Eye of round, cut thin, apx 1/4"
3 tbsp soysauce, 1 stp Accent, 2 tbsp wine, 1/4 tsp hot pepper, 2 tbsp sesame oil, 2 tbsp salad oil, 2 tbsp choped green onions, 2 tsp ginger, mashed, 2 cloves garlic, mashed, 2 tsp sugar and a dash of pepper.

Directions:
Mix and marinade. Soak 15 minn. BBQ rare, medium or well done as desired. For authintic flavor this dish must be cooked over charcoal fire.
