Title:
Dilly Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, juices reserved
1/4 cup diced celery
1/4 cup chopped fresh dill weed
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
2 tablespoons thinly sliced green onion
2 tablespoons  mayonnaise
2 tablespoons plain low-fat yogurt or sour cream
1/2 teaspoon prepared Dijon-style mustard

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix tuns, celery, dill, parsley, chives, mayonnaise, yogurt, and mustard.  Add tuna juice as needed for proper consistency.

Submitter: 
Melissa Gardner
Description: 
Fresh dill, chives and a dab of mustard give this tuna salad a wonderful flavor. The combo of mayonnaise and yogurt for the dressing is light and refreshing. Makes four generous servings.Modified from: Tuna Salad With Fresh Dill
