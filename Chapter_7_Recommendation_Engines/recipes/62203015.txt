Title:
Easy Pumpkin Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg yellow cake mix (reserve 1c for topping)
1/2 c. melted butter
3 eggs
3 cups Libbys Pumpkin Pie mix(Dark brown can,with the spices in it)
2/3 c milk
1/4 c sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 c butter

Directions:
Grease bottom only of a 13x9 pan.
RESERVE, 1 Cup of dry cake mix.
Crust - Combine remaining cake mix, 1/2 c melted butter and 1 egg. Press into pan.
Filling - Combine Libby's Pumpkin pie mix, 2 eggs, and 2/3 c milk until smooth.
Pour over crust.
Topping - Combine 1 c of reserved cake mix, 1/4 c sugar, 1 tsp. cinnamon, and 1/4 butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over filling.
Bake in preheated 350 oven for 45-50 minutes until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
Cool and store covered in frig.
Cut into squares and serve topped with RediWhip or Cool Whip
