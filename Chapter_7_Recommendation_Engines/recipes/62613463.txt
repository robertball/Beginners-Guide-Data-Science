Title:
Coconut Custard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Single pie crust
1c flaked coconut
3 eggs, beaten
2-2/3c 2% milk
2/3c sugar
3T AP flour
1t vanilla
1/2t salt
1/4t ground nutmeg

Directions:
Line a 9" deep dish pie plate with pastry; trim and flute edges.
Line unpricked pastry with double layer of foil.
Bake at 450 8 minutes.
Remove foil and bake 5 more minutes.
Sprinkle coconut over crust and set aside.
In a large bowl, combine eggs, milk, sugar, flour, vanilla, and salt.
Pour over coconut and sprinkle with nutmeg.
Bake uncovered at 350 for 45-50 minutes.
Knife in center will come out clean.
Cool on a wire rack.
Refrigerate leftovers.
