Title:
Crockpot Mac & Cheez Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz. box macaroni elbows
4 cups sharp cheese (shredded)
8 cups mild cheese (shredded)
4 cups shredded velveeta cheese
1 can evaporated milk
2 eggs
1/4 stick butter
dash of salt and pepper
paprika

Directions:
boil elbows until tender.  allow them to cool.  add sharp cheese and 1 1/2 of the mild cheese and 1/2 the velveeta cheese with the eggs, milk, salt and pepper, and butter.  Grease crockpot well.  Add mixed ingredients.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on top then sprinkle with paprika.  Allow to cook on high for 4 hours or until the top is brown.
