Title:
MIssion Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2/3 cup Brown Sugar
1 2/3 cup White Sugar
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
3 cups flour
1 tsp soda
1 tsp salt
2 cups Chocolate Chips
2 cups pecans - chopped (optional)

Directions:
Cream butter, sugar, vanilla and eggs.
Add flour, soda and salt.
Add chocolate chips and chopped pecans.
Bake at 350 degrees for 12 minutes
