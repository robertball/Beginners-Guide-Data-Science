Title:
Light But Yummy Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup all purpose flour
2/3 cup whole wheat flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
1 large egg
2 medium egg whites
3/4 cup honey
1/4 cup oil
1 tbsp. vegetable oil
1 tbsp. lemon juice
3 cups grated carrot
3 oz. light cream cheese(softened)
3 tbsp. confectioner's sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
Heat oven to 350(F).  Lightly coat the bottom of an 8 inch round cake pan with non-staick cooking spray.  Line with waxed paper, and coat paper with cooking spray.
In a small bowl, combine flours, baking soda, cinnamon and salt.  In a large bowl, using an electric mixer, beat eggs, honey, oil, and lemon juice until blended.  Stir flour mixture and carrots into egg mixture until just blended.
Spread batter into prepared pan, and bake until a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean, about 30-35 minutes.  Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then remove from pan and cool completley.
For frosting, cream together softened cream cheese, confectioner's sugar, and vanilla.  Spread over cooled cake and serve.
