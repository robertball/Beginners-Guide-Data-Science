Title:
Balsamic Viniagrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 prunes
1/2 c balsamic vinegar
3 medjool dates
little apple puree
1 tsp dijon mustard to taste
3 garlic cloves
juice from prunes/apple or water
salt

Directions:
boil prunes in 2 cups water
put into blender
add balsamic, garlic, dates, apple puree, water/juice, salt, pepper, mustard
adjust water to taste
puree until well blended
