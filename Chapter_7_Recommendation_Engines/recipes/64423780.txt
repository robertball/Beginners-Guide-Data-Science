Title:
Egg Salad with Chopped Gherkins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoon relish, or more to taste
1 teaspoon  mustard
1/4 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
chop eggs
Stir chopped eggs, mayonnaise,relish, mustard,paprika together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Jody Comerford
Description: 
This classic egg salad with chopped gherkins works well on sandwiches, crackers, in a lettuce cup, or in a tomato half.Modified from: Egg Salad with Chopped Gherkins
