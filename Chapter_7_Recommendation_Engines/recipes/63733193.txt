Title:
popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup all purpose flour
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1. heat oven to 450 and generously grease 6 6 oz custard cups.
2. beat eggs slightly then add in flour milk and salt until smooth
3. fill custard cups 1/2 way amd bale for 20 mins
4. decrease oven temp to 350 and bake for 20 mins
