Title:
Apple Raisin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspooon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup butter
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups grated apple
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease two 8x4 inch loaf pans.
In a medium bowl, sift together flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. In a separate large bowl, cream together butter, sugar, and vanilla. Beat eggs in one at a time. Stir apple and raisins into creamed mixture, then stir in flour mixture until moistened. Pour batter into prepared cake pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center of loaf comes out clean. Allow to cool slightly before turning out onto wire rack.

Submitter:

Description: 
This bread is moist, sweet and spicy. It is great served warm.
