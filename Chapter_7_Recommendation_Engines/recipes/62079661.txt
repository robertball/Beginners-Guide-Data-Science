Title:
Rosemary Sweet Sweet Potatoes with Ginger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced 1/4th inch thick
1/2 cup Honey
2 Tbls of Milk
4 Tbls Fresh grated Ginger
3 Tbls Grapeseed oil
1/2 Tbls Cardamom
1 tsp ground Cloves
butter for baking dish
1 tsp ground Black Pepper
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
1. Peel and slice Sweet potatoes; grate ginger - set aside.
2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
3. Stir together honey and milk to even consistency
4. In large bowl, toss potatoes with honey/milk mix, soak for 10 minutes, drain half of remaining mix
5. Toss in remaining ingredients and coat potatoes evenly
6. Place potatoes in single layer baking dish - lightly buttered - bake for 20 minutes.
7. Turn potatoes over and cook for about 20 more minutes or until fork tender and caramelized in color.
5.
