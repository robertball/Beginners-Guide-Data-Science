Title:
"Nacho Mama's" Nachos Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/8 C. (or to taste) inst. (dried) chopped onion
1 std. pkg. taco seasoning mix (mild, med. or hot - to taste)
1 (#303) can sliced new potatoes, drained and coarsely chopped
1 (#303) can refried beans (reg. or w/jalapeno - to taste)
corn tortilla chips (or bake your own)
8-12 oz. pkg. shredded Mexican blend (or your favorite) shredded cheese
Optional Toppings (to taste):
pickled nacho jalapeno slices
guacamole
sour cream
chunky salsa
thinly sliced green onions

Directions:
Brown meat, onion & taco seasoning in large, heavy skillet; drain meat in strainer over skillet; reserve about 1-2 Tbsp. of drippings.
Add potatoes to skillet & warm slightly; stir in refried beans and meat mixture. Heat mixture over med-low heat, stirring often, until mixture is thoroughly blended and heated through. Remove from heat.
Depending on method, either place chips on microwave-safe platter OR on large baking sheet lined with alum. foil and lightly sprayed with non-stick cooking spray.
Evenly spread a 1 tsp. to 1 Tbsp. of mixture (depending on chip size) on chips; top with cheese & jalapeno slices. Microwave on high for 1-2 min., rotating once (or until cheese melts) OR bake in 350 deg. oven until cheese melts. Allow to cool for 3-5 min.
Top with any or all of the optional toppings and/or salt & pepper to taste.

Description: 
I came up with this in self-defense of the slopped-together, processed cheese nachos available in restaurants these days. These are sometimes the main course in my home!
