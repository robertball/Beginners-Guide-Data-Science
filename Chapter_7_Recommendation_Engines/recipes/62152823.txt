Title:
Soy Marinated Skirt Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB Skirt Steak, Cleaned
1 Cup Mirin
1/2 Cup soy sauce (LOW SODIUM)
1 1/2 Cups sweet Soy Sauce
1 cup sake
1 cup Pineapple Juice
1 TB. Sugar
1 TB. Ginger Minced
1 TB. Garlic Minced
1 TB. Scallions, chopped
1/4 bunch of citantro
1 TB chili paste (OPTIONAL)

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, add all ingredients and marinate for 3 hours. Next remove steak from marinate. Pour marinate into pot over medium/ high heat and reduce marinate by half (about 2 cups) Strain. Reserve. Brush grill with vegatable oil and grill steak to your wellness.

This is a really great recipe that can be served over rice.
