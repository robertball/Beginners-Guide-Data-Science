Title:
Creole Chcken Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Your favorite Spanish Rice Mix
1 cup water
1-15oz. can diced tomatoes with chilies.
1-8oz. can tomato sauce
4-6 skinless chicken thighs
Creole Seasoning
2 T. Butter

Directions:
In medium skillet brown chicken thighs that have been seasoned with Creole Seasoning.  After the chicken has been browned remove and set aside.  Add to skillet the first four ingredients, stir and then return the chicken thighs to pan.  Bring to a boil then lower to a simmer, cover and simmer for 40 minutes.  Check for chicken doneness.  When chicken is done serve on a platter the rice with chicken on top.
