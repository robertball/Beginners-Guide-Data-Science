Title:
Creamy Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8-oz pkg cream cheese, cubed
3/4 cup (3 ounces) grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup milk
8 ounces fettuccine noodles, cooked and drained
1 lb. chopped or shredded cooked chicken (optional)

Directions:
In large saucepan, combine cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, butter and milk.  Stir over low heat until smooth.  (Or heat in microwave, stirring often, until melted and combined.)  Add fettuccine and chicken; toss lightly.
