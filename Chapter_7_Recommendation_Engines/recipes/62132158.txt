Title:
Szechuan Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound long green beans
1 tsp szechuan peppercorns
1 Tbsp peanut oil
1 tsp minced garlic
1/8 tsp dried ginger
3 Tbsp soy sauce
4 Tbsp red wine vinegar
2 tsp hoisin sauce
1/2 tsp sesame oil
1 green onion, chopped
1 Tbsp sesame seeds

Directions:
Heat the peppercorns in a dry pan on medium until they are nice and aromatic, then grind them up with a mortar and pestle.
Heat the oil in a small pan on medium heat.
Add garlic and cook for 1 minute or until it starts to turn colour, add the ginger and peppercorns, and stir into the garlic.
Add the remaining sauce ingredients and heat.
Steam the beans for 5 minutes. Toss them in the sauce and sprinkle with sesame seeds

Submitter: 
Jordan VanDijk
Description: 
My take on the long green beans you find in some of the asian fusion restaurants. If you don't have the szechuan peppercorns this dish will still taste great, though you may want to add some chili flakes or other spice to give it some pep instead of the peppercorns.
