Title:
Hummingbird Cake Cottage Cheese Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6401953.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup low fat (1%) cottage cheese
1 tbsp maple syrup (or to taste)
2 tbsps pineapple juice
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
pinch ground nutmeg
pinch ground allspice
Pineapple chunks
1 banana, sliced
3 tbsps toasted pecans, chopped
1/4 cup toasted coconut (optional)

Directions:
1. Stir cottage cheese, maple syrup, pineapple juice, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, and allspice together until thoroughly combined. Divide into two bowls.
2. Top each bowl with pineapple chunks, banana slices, pecans, and coconut (if using). Serve immediately.

Submitter:

Description: 
All your favorite Hummingbird Cake flavor in a slightly-better-for-you version!
