Title:
Cheese Danish Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages cream cheese (8 oz each)
2 cans crescent rolls
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 egg
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1 tablespoon milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray a 9x13 pan with nonstick spray.
Spread 1 can of crescent rolls along the bottom of the pan.
Mix together cream cheese, sugar, vanilla, and egg.
Spread the cream cheese mixture on top of the crescent rolls in the pan.
Top the cream cheese mixture with the second can of crescent rolls.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until lightly browned.
Once cooled, mix together powdered sugar and milk to make a glaze.
Pour the glaze on top of the danish squares.
Best kept in fridge and served cold.
