Title:
Burrito Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Round Steak or Pork, trimmed and cut in bite sized pieces, about 1 lb.
flour
salt
pepper
chopped onion
1 can chopped green chilis, undrained

Directions:
Coat meat pieces in flour seasoned with salt and pepper.
Cook in skillet with oil until browned.
Add chopped onion to skillet and brown with meat.
Add green chilis and liquid.
Add water and simmer until mixture creates a gravy.
Serve in flour tortillas with burrito toppings.
