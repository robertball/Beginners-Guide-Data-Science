Title:
Penne with vodka sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBS. olive oil
6 garlic cloves, minced
pinch of red pepper flakes
28oz. whole peeled tomatoes with juice
3 TBS. tomato paste
1 tsp. sugar
1/3 cup vodka
coarse salt
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 lb. penne
finely grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, heat oil over medium-high heat.  Add garlic and red pepper flakes; cook until fragrant.  Add tomatoes and their juic, breaking tomatoes up with wooden spoon.
Stir in tomato paste, sugar, vodka and 3/4 cup water; season with salt.  Simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionaly, until sauce has thickened, 20-25 minutes.  Stir in cream; cook just until heated through.
Meanwhile cook pasta according to package directions.  Drain.
Add sauce to pasta, and toss to combine.  Serve immediately; sprinkle with parmesan.
