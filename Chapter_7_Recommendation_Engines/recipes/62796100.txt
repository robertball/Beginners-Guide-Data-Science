Title:
Shanghai Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb thick Shanghai Noodles (or Buccichinni noodles)
1/4 cup ketchup
3 1/2 Tbsps sesame oil
3 1/2 Tbsps soya sauce
2 Tbsps of brown sugar
2 tsp of kosher salt
1/2 Tbsp lime juice
1/4 cup of chopped green onions
2 carrots, cleaned and shredded
1 small zuchinni cut into match sticks
1 red pepper cut into match sticks
1 Tbsp of toasted sesame seeds
1 tsp of chili flakes

Directions:
cook pasta according to package directions.
In a small bowl,  wisk ketchup, oil, soya sauce, sugar, salt,and lime juice.
In a very large bowl, add the cooked and cooled noodles, veggies, sesame seeds, and chili flakes.
Pour on the wisked ingrediants, combine, put into a storage container, and chill for at least 2 hours before  serving

Submitter: 
Tina Kauffman
Description: 
Tangy, addictive, Asian  pasta salad.    Great  for lunch boxes!
