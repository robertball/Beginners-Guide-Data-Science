Title:
Sun-Dried Tomato Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon paprika
1 1/2 tablespoons tahini paste
1/8 cup fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup olive oil include oil from sun-dried tomatoes
1/4 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained
1 (15.5 ounce) cans garbanzo beans, drained
1/8 cup finely shredded fresh basil (optional)

Directions:
Place garlic, salt, paprika , tahini, lemon juice, olive oils and sun-dried tomatoes  into a food processor; process until smooth.  Add the garbanzo beans and process until smooth again, scraping the sides of the bowl occasionally. Finally, add the basil, and pulse a few times until mixed in. Spread the hummus into a shallow serving dish. Refrigerate 1 hour.
