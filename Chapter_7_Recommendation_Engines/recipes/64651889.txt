Title:
Easy Italian Chickpea Pasta Salad Bento Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4540987.jpg
Ingredients:
Easy Italian Chickpea Pasta Salad
4 ounces rotini pasta
1/4 cup green pepper, diced
1/2 of a 15.5 ounce can of garbanzo beans (rinsed and drained)
1/2 of a medium tomato, chopped
1/2 cup zucchini, sliced
2 tablespoons red onion, diced
4 tablespoons light Italian dressing, or to taste
Kalamata olives (optional)
Ricotta and Strawberries
1/2 cup ricotta cheese
2 teaspoons honey
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
8-10 strawberries, sliced

Directions:
Easy Italian Chickpea Pasta Salad
1. Cook 4 ounces of rotini pasta according to package directions. Rinse with cold water, drain and place in medium mixing bowl.
2. Add green pepper, garbanzo beans, tomato, zucchini, onion and salad dressing, and stir until well combined. Divide into 2 bento boxes.  Top each salad with some Kalamata olives.
Ricotta and Strawberries
1. Mix ricotta cheese, honey and vanilla extract until fully combined and smooth.
2. Divide the ricotta mixture into 2 bento boxes. Place 4-5 sliced strawberries next to each serving of ricotta. You may mix the strawberries into the ricotta before eating, or just enjoy them alongside the ricotta.

Submitter: 
Tammy Lynn
Description: 
A chickpea pasta salad is made quick and easy using your favorite bottled Italian dressing, and tastes great paired alongside creamy ricotta sweetened with honey and strawberries. This protein-packed lunch tastes light but is filling.
