Title:
Zach's Buffalo Wing Goodness Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4436497.jpg
Ingredients:
12 chicken wings, 1st & second sections
½ cup all-purpose white flour
1/8 teaspoon Cayenne pepper (red pepper)
¼ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon paprika
1/3 cup Defcon 2 sauce (order it from Defcon Sauces for the most flavorful experience) or substitute for your favorite sauce
Blue cheese or ranch dressing, for dipping

Directions:
If frozen, thaw chicken wings in refrigerator overnight or for 4-6 hours, covered with plastic wrap in a large bowl.
Preheat oven to 375°F.
Line cookie sheets with foil and spray foil with cooking oil spray.
In a large bowl, combine flour, cayenne pepper, salt and paprika. Mix well.
Pat Dry wings with a paper towel then dip thawed chicken wings into flour mixture to coat thoroughly.
Place floured wings on foil covered cookie sheets and lightly spray wings with cooking oil spray.
Bake wings in 375° oven for 48 minutes, do not turn.
Set oven to broil, and broil for approximately 5 minutes or until crisp and browned.
In a large bowl combine chicken wings and Defcon 2 sauce and toss until wings are evenly
Serve with sliced carrots and Celery for cool crunching and blue cheese or ranch dressing for those who need it.

Submitter: 
Howard
Description: 
Father/Son teamwork over the summer of 2012 produced our favorite wing recipe after many fun weeks.  The wings are baked to a crispy perfection with just the right amount heat and maintain a robust flavor that will make this a recipe a favorite.
