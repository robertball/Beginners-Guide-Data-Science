Title:
Mexican Style HotDogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/294008.jpg
Ingredients:
8 hot dogs
1 package bacon
8 hot dog buns
8 tablespoons sour cream
8 tablespoons ketchup
4 tablespoons mustard
1 cup chopped roma tomatoes
1 cup chopped white onion
1/2 cup chopped jalapenos

Directions:
Heat fry pan to medium heat.  Wrap 1-2 slices of bacon around each hot dog in a spiral motion.  Once hot dogs are wrapped place them in the pan and fry until all sides of bacon are golden brown.
While the hotdogs fry, open the 8 hot dog buns and apply 1 tablespoon sour cream, ketchup and 1/2 tablespoon of mustard to each bun.
Once hot dogs are cooked place one on each bun and top with tomato, onion and jalapenos.
Enjoy!!
