Title:
Walnut Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. coconut
1 c. gram cracker crumbs
1 stick margarine
12 oz. package chocolate chips
1 can Eagle Brand Milk

Directions:
Mix together 1st 3 ingredients & press into bottom of 9 x 13 baking dish.
Sprinkle chocolate chips & walnuts on top of mixture
Pour Eagle Brand over and bake in a pre-heated oven at 325 degrees for 20-25 minutes
