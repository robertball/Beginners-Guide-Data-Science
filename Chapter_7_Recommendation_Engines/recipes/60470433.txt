Title:
Summer Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lb yellow squash
1 lb zucchini
1 small sweet onion, chopped
2-1/2tsp. salt divided
1 cup grated carrots
1 (10-3/4oz) can cream of chicken soup
1 (8oz) container sour cream
1 (8oz) can water chestnuts, drained and chopped
1 (8oz) package herb-seasoned stuffing
1/2 cup butter melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Cut squash and zucchini into 1/4" thick slices; place in a dutch oven. Add chopped onion, 2tsp. salt, and water to cover. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, and cook 5 minutes; drain well.
Stir together 1 cup grated carrots, next 3 ingredients, and remaining 1/2tsp salt in a large bowl; fold in squash mixture.  Stir together stuffing and 1/2 cup melted butter, and spoon half of stuffin mixture in bottom of a lightly greased 13x9 baking dish. Spoon squash mixture over stuffing mixture, and top with remaining stuffing mixture.
Bake at 350 for 30-35 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown,shielding with aluminum foil after 20-25 minutes to prevent excessive brownining if necessary. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
