Title:
Judy's Sheppards Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 pd ground sirloin
1 small chopped onion
2 clove fresh garlic chopped fine
4 oz.can drained mushrooms
10 oz can creme mushroom soup
2 2/3 cup chicken broth
4 tbsp. butter
1 cup milk
3 cups instant mashed potatoes
8 oz shredded mild cheeder cheese
1/3 cup real bacon bits

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
brown sirloin, garlic, onion, mushrooms
turn off heat , drain meat in collander
place meat back in pot
add 10 oz creme mushroom soup and stir well
seperate pot:
2 2/3 cup chicken broth
4 tbsp. butter
bring to a boil
add 1 cup milk
3 cups instant mashed potatoes
remove from heat and stir well
spread meat in greased 7x11 pan
cover meat with mashed potatoes
cover with aluminun foil
bake for 45 minutes
take out of oven and remove aluminun foil
cover with 8 oz shredded cheese and 1/3 cup bacon bits
do not cover and place back in oven for 10 minutes
or until cheese is melted
let stand for 30-45 minutes
and serve
even better next day
this casserole can be made a day ahead and kept in refrigerator till ready to cook.
