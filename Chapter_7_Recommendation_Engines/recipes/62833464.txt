Title:
Linguine with Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch spinch cleaned and shredded.  I include the stems, but feel free to exclude them.
1 16oz box of quality whole wheat linguine
Salt for pasta water
Olive oil
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Bring a pot of well salted water to a boil.  Add the linguine and cook until its just al dente, but not yet ready.
Add the spinach to the already cooking pasta.
Cook until the pasta is to your liking.
Reserve 2 cups of the pasta water.
Drain the pasta and spinach.  Place in a serving bowl.
Add the reserved pasta water and mix with 1-2 tbsp of fruity olive oil.
To serve: serve in bowls.  Be sure to include some of the juice in each bowl.  Add a touch of olive oil and sprinkle with the Parmesan cheese.
