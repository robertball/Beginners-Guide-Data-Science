Title:
Basic Kahlua Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
3/4 cups instant espresso
2 1/4 cups vodka
4 cups sugar
1 vanilla bean, split

Directions:
1. Mix water, sugar and coffee in a large pan.
2. Heat and stir until dissolved.
3. Cool to room temperature.
4. Add the vodka and vanilla bean; stir to combine well.
5. Store in a large jar in a cool, dry place for 2-3 weeks, swirling once or twice a week.
6. Strain the kahlua and bottle as desired.

Submitter: 
Karen Semmelrock
Description: 
Basic Kahlua recipe--very good, like store bought.
