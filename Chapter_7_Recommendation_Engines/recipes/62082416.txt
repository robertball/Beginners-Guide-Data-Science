Title:
Jan's Binga's Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz instant potatoes
1/4 c milk
1 T dried onion flakes
12 oz small curd cottage cheese
1 c sour cream
3 eggs
1 t salt
1 t garlic powder
1 - 2 T butter

Directions:
Grease or spray with cooking spray a 9 x 13 casserole.
Bring 2 1/2 c water and 1/4 c milk to boil.  Take off heat and add potato flakes.  Let sit for five minutes.  Uncover and let cool.
Soften 1 T dried onions in 1/4 c water.
Separate the 3 egg yolks from the whites.  In a large bowl, beat the yolks.  Add the the potatoes, cottage cheese, sour cream, salt and garlic powder.  Drain the water off the onions and add to the mixture.
In another bowl, beat the egg whites to stiffen.  Fold the egg whites into the potato mixture.
Transfer to the greased casserole and dot with butter.
Bake at 350 for one hour.
