Title:
tasty garlic spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/441041.jpg
Ingredients:
*8 oz. garden vegetable cream cheese (softened)
*1/2 cup mayonnaise
*3/4 tsp. Italian seasoning
*1 garlic clove crushed and chopped in small pieces

Directions:
combine all ingredients in small bowl. whisk until smooth
spread on top of crackers or bread.
