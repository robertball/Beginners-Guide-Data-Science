Title:
Refrigerator Pickle Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1028881.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups thinly sliced, unpeeled cucumbers
3/4 cup sliced 1 -inch long green onion pieces
1/2 cup diagonally sliced celery
1/2 cup thinly sliced carrot
1 small red bell pepper, cut into strips
1 garlic clove, crushed
1 teaspoon sale
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon mustard seed
1 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
1/2 cup white vinegar

Directions:
Microwave directions: In a large bowl, combine cucumbers, onions, celery, carrot, bell pepper, garlic and salt, mix thoroughly.
In clean a pint or quart canning jars, pack the vegetable mixture into the jars, leaving room just below the neck of the jar for liquid to be added.
In 4 cup microwave-safe measuring cup or bowl combine all remaining ingredients.  Microwave on HIGH for 2 to 3 minutes or until mixture boils.  Stir to dissolve sugar. Pour hot vinegar mixture over vegetables; cover with tight-fitting lid.  Invert jar; then turn upright to distribute spices evenly. Let cool and then refrigerate at least 24 hours to blend flavors before using.  Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Amanda Elaine
Description: 
Packed with great flavor and lasts for several months stored in the refrigerator.
