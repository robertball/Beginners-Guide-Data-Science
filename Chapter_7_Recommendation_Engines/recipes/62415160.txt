Title:
Baked Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (20 ounce) cans unsweetened pineapple chunks
6 tablespoons Splenda
12 tablespoons butter, melted
6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
16 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
50 buttery round crackers, crumbled

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and spray a 3-quart baking dish with Pam for Baking.
Drain pineapple, reserving 6 tablespoons juice.
Combine pineapple, reserved juice, sugar, butter, flour, and cheese.  Mix well.
Spoon mixture into the buttered dish.
Top with cracker crumbs.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes, or until bubbly.
