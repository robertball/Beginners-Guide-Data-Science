Title:
Slow Cooker Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (32 oz.) package frozen Southern style bash browns
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (12 oz.) can evaporated milk
8 oz. sour cream
1 (2.8 oz) can french fried onions, divided

Directions:
1. Grease the slow cooker with cooking spray or shortening.
2. In a large bowl, stir together the first 5 ingredients with half the french fried onions.
3. Pour mixture into slow cooker.  Cover and cook on high for 4 hours (or low for 8).
4. Top with remaining onions immediately before serving.

Submitter: 
scarlett
Description: 
An easy cheesy potato recipe that goes great with ham.  I double this recipe to fit in my 5 quart slow cooker for parties.
