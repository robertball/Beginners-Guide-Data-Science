Title:
Shipwreck Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small yellow onion sliced
3 large potatoes sliced thin (skin off)
1 can of baked beans
1 can of tomato soup
1 lb. ground beef

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 Degrees F., while heating brown ground beef.
In a casserole dish spray bottom of the pan with cooking spray, add a layer of potatoes, onions, baked beans, ground beef, and the top will be the tomato soup.
Cook for about 50 min. or until potatoes are fully cooked.

Submitter: 
ArtistAngie
Description: 
This was one of my favorite things to make while growing up! I love the ground beef on top where it gets a little crunch to it from being one of the top layers.

It's hearty meal and reminds me of summer.
