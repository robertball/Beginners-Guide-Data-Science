Title:
Chocolate Christmas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4454104.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons milk
1/4 cup chocolate syrup
1 cup ice
1 banana, cut into chunks
3 candy cane chocolate candy kisses, unwrapped

Directions:
Blend milk and chocolate syrup together in a blender. Add ice, banana, and candy kisses; blend until smooth.

Submitter:

Description:
