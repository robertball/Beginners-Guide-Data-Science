Title:
Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 eggs
1/4 cup mayonnaise
3 tbsp sour cream
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
Garnish:
1/4 tsp paprika

Directions:
Arrange eggs in single layer in large deep saucepan; pour in enough cold water to come at least 1 inch (2.5 cm) above eggs.
Cover and bring to boil over high heat. Immediately remove from heat; let stand, covered, for 20 minutes.
Drain and run cold water over eggs until cooled.
(Make-ahead: Refrigerate in airtight container for up to 2 days.)
Serves 24
