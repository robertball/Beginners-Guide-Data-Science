Title:
HAM BALL Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CANNED HAM
OR (2) TINS OF HAM FLAKES
8 OZ. CREAM CHEESE
1 TBSP CHOPPED GREEN ONION
PARSLEY CHOPPED
WALNUTS OR PECANS CHOPPED

Directions:
BEAT CREAM CHEESE UNTIL SMOOTH THEN ADD HAM AND CHOPPED ONION, INCORPORATING WELL TOGETHER.  PLACE IN FRIDGE UNTIL FIRM.  REMOVE FROM FRIDGE AND FORM INTO A BALL.  ROLL THE BALL IN FINELY CHOPPED WALNUTS AND PARSLEY THAT HAVE BEEN MIXED TOGETHER FOR A MORE EYE APPEALING PRESENTATION.

BETTYCOOK
