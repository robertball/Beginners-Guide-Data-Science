Title:
Red Velvet Cake III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix with pudding
4 eggs
1 ounce red food coloring
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 cup water
1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
3 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1 cup cold milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease and flour one 9 x 13 inch pan.
Mix together cake mix, eggs, oil, food coloring, water, vinegar, and cocoa. Pour into prepared pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 to 40 minutes.  Cool.
