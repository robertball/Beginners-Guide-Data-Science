Title:
Almond Flour Pie Crust #1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/949981.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups blanched almond flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 cup olive oil
1 Tablespoon water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl, combine almond flour, salt, and baking soda.
Stir the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until thoroughly combined.
Press the dough into a 9 1/2" pie pan.
Bake for 15 minutes until golden brown.
Remove from the oven and let cool completely before filling.
