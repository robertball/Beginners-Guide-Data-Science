Title:
Kenny's Mint Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box White cake mix
1 cup water
Creme de mint (divided)
Chocolate Frosting
Cool Whip

Directions:
Mix white cake mix, 1 cup water and 1/4 cup creme de mint along with any other ingredients the cake mix calls for.  Bake cake as instructed.  Cool.
Spread chocolate frosting on cooled cake.
Mix creme de mint to cool whip to taste and add to top of chocolate frosting.

Submitter: 
GevingCook
Description: 
This cake was served by the church ladies at Kenny's funeral and a few Gevings really enjoyed it.  Rana tracked down the baker to a friend of Greta's and shared it with us. Thank you Rana, Greta and friend!  ~ Gina
