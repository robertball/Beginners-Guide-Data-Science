Title:
Chicken Appetizer Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
French Bread or Bagette Loaf
Ken's Sweet Vidalia Dressing
Chicken Breast (canned)
Chopped Celery
Walnuts
Craisins
Mayonnaise

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a small mixing bowl
(except mayonnaise and bread).
Add as much salad dressing as desired to make recipe moist and adhere the ingredients.
Slice French Bread or Bagettes into size desired (I make mine 1-1/2in thick)  (these are finger sandwiches). Spread small amount mayonnaise onto each slice and fill with chicken spread.
Enjoy on a hot summer day!
