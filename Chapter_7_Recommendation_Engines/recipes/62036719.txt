Title:
Sweet yet Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen meatballs
1 12 0z. jar of Grape Jelly
1 large bottle of any BBQ sauce

Directions:
Toss in the meatballs, jelly and BBQ sauce in your crockpot.  Cook on low for 30 min. and serve.  Very simple.

Submitter: 
DAWNCIL
Description: 
Everyone begs me to bring these to all of the holiday gatherings and Superbowl.  They are easy to make and taste very good.
