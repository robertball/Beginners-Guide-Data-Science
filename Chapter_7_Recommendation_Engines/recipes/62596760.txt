Title:
Flourless Peanut Butter Cookies Plus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup peanut butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
Combine ingredients and drop by teaspoonfuls on cookie sheet. flatten with tines of a fork. Bake for 8 minutes.  Cookies will be crumbly when hot, so allow them to cool on the cookie sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Jennie Strong
Description: 
Heavy, yummy, and very peanut-buttery.  You'll need lots of milk to eat these!!
