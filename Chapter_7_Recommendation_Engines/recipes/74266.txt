Title:
Asian Style Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981753.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded 1/2-inch thick
1 1/2 pounds chicken breasts, boneless, cooked and cut or torn into strips
4 cups Mann's Stringless Sugar SnapÂ® Peas
4 cups Mann's Broccoli Cole Slaw
6 scallions, finely sliced
1 bunch cilantro, coarsely chopped
1 1/2 cups chow mein noodles
1 cup cashew pieces, roasted, salted
3 ounces ginger, fresh
1 1/2 cups light corn syrup
1 cup Aji-Mirin sweet cooking rice wine, see note
1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon sesame oil

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine lettuce, chicken, Mann's Stringless Sugar Snap Peas, Mann's Broccoli Cole Slaw, scallions and cilantro.
To make the dressing, puree ingredients from ginger to end together in a food processor.
Just before serving, gently toss salad with chow mein noodles and dressing to taste. Garnish with cashews and serve.

Submitter: 
Mann's Fresh Vegetables
Description: 
Crispy iceberg lettuce, sugar snap peas, and broccoli coleslaw tossed together with chicken and cashews and finished with a sweet and tangy dressing with a hint of sesame.
