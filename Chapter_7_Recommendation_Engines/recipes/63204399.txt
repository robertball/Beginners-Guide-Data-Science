Title:
Coffee mug Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 Tablespoon butter, softened
2 Tablespoons sugar
1/2 an egg, beaten
2 tablespoons sour cream
2-3 drops vanilla extract
1/4 cup flour (do not use self rising)
1/8 teaspoon baking powder
Crumb topping:
1 tablespoon butter
2 tablespoons flour
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
In a coffee mug, mix together butter and sugar until fluffy. stir in egg, sour cream and vanilla. Add flour and baking powder. Stir until smooth.
In a separate bowl, combine all ingredients for crumb topping and cut in butter until a crumbly consistency. Sprinkle over cake batter in mug.
Cook in microwave. Start with 1 minute cook time, then at 10 second intervals until toothpick inserted comes out clean.

Description: 
A quick, single serving of cake that can be made using any of your favorite flavors. Great, easy recipe for kids to make for themselves.
