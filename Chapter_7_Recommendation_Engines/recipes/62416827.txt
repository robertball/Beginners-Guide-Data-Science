Title:
Hawaiian Haystacks - Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 to 12 chicken tenders (cut into chunks)
1 cup chicken broth
2 cans (10 3/4 oz each) Cream of Chicken soup, condensed
1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
6 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Place chicken in greased 3 1/2 to 5-quart slow cooker.
Combine all remaining ingredients except rice, and pour over chicken,
Cover and cook on high heat 3 - 4 hours or on low heat 6 - 8 hours.
Serve over hot rice with a variety of cold toppings that could include grated cheese, chow mein noodles (dry crispy type),diced tomatoes, diced green peppers, sliced green onions, peas, pineapple tidbits, shredded coconut, raisins, and other family favorites.
