Title:
Edamame Fresca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-12 oz. pkgs. edamame (shelled, fresh or thawed frozen)
2-14 oz. cans black beans (drained and rinsed)
1-14 oz. can garbanzo beans (drained and rinsed)
1 each Red and Yellow Pepper, chopped
1 sm. Red Onion, chopped (soak in water for 5 min. after chopping)
3 T. Italian Parsley, chopped
2 cups Seasoned Rice Vinegar
3 T. Fresh Ginger, crushed
2 T. EVOO
Salt & Pepper to Taste

Directions:
In a large glass bowl, mix all ingredients together (carefully folding to avoid smashing the beans).  Refrigerate overnight.

Submitter: 
VH
Description: 
Refreshing edamame salad that's light, refreshing and healthy!  Great with BBQ'd Chicken.
