Title:
Dulce De Leche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/4 pints whole milk
1 lb 5 oz sugar
3 Tbs white wine vinegar

Directions:
Bring milk and sugar to a boil in saucepan. Reuduce to a simmer and add vinegar. This will cause the milk and sugar to separate.
Simmer for 3 hrs or until liquid evaporates, leaving an almost solid toffee. Cool completely and serve.
