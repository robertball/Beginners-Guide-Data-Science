Title:
Peach Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup marg
1 cup brown sugar
1 box yellow cake mix
3 cups slice peaches, reserve juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Place marg & brown sugar in 9x13 pan & place in oven while it's heating. when marg is melted, stir to combine.
mix cake as directed, using juice from peaches in place of water. Arrange peaches on top of brown sugar. spread batter over peaches.
Bake 45-50 min until cake is set. Let stand for 5 min. Turn upside down on platter.
