Title:
TIRAMISU CHEESECAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
TIRAMISU CHEESECAKE
40 NILLA wafers, finely crushed (about 1 1/3 cups)
3 Tbs. butter, melted
4 pkg. (98 oz. each) Philadelphia Cream Cheese, softened, divided
3/4 cup sugar
6 Tbs. brewed strong coffee, cooled, divided
3 eggs
1/3 cup milk
1 pks. (3.4 oz.) Jell-O Vanilla Flavor Instant Pudding
2 cups thawed Cool Whip Whipped Topping, divided
1/2 square Baker's Semi-Sweet chocolate, coarsely grated
16 fresh raspberries 9 (about 1/2 cup)

Directions:
325 DEGREES   28 MINUTES OR UNTIL CENTER IS ALMOST SET
MIX WAFER CRUMBS AND BUTTER; PRESS INTO BOTTOM OF A 13X9 INCH PAN
BEAT 3 PACKAGES CREAM CHEESE, SUGAR AND 1 TBS. COFFEE IN LARGE BOWL WITH MIXER UNTIL
WELL BLENDED
ADD EGGS, 1 AT A TIME MIXING ON LOW SPEED AFTER EACH JUST UNTIL BLENDED.
POUR OVER CRUST
BAKE 28 MINUTES.  COOL COMPLETELY
BEAT REMAINING CREAM CHEESE IN MED. BOWL WITH MIXER UNTIL CREAMY
ADD MILK AND REMAINING COFFEE,  MIX WELL
ADD DRY PUDDING MIX; BEAT 2 MINUTES.
STIR IN 1 1/2 CUPS COOL WHIP.
SPREAD OVER CHEESECAKE; SPRINKLE WITH CHOCOLATE.
REFRIGERATE 4 HOURS BEFORE SERVING.
GARNISH WITH REMAINING COOL WHIP AND RASPBERRIES JUST BEFORE SERVING.
