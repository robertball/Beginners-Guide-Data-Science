Title:
Whipped Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup icing sugar
1/4 cup cornstarch
1/2 lb. butter (1 cup)
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
chopped candied cherries (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and whip them together until light and fluffy (like icing).
Place on cookie sheet with cherry in center (optional).
Bake at 325 until bottoms are very light brown (7 minutes).
