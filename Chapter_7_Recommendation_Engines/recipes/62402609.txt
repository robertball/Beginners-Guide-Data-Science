Title:
Haystack Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 lg. can refried beans mixed with a packet of taco seasoning
2. Dice 3 avocados/blend in food processor. Add 3 tblsp lemon juice and 1 c. sour cream
3. 1 can sliced olives
4. 1 sm. can green chilies, diced
5. 1/2 cup green onion, chopped
6. 1 cup jack cheese, shredded
7. 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
8. 3 tomatoes, chopped
9. alfalfa sprouts
tortilla chips to serve alongside.

Directions:
Layer ingredients in order 1-9. Refrigerate. Serve with tortilla chips as a dip.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
Easy to make and transport. Great for a light lunch.
