Title:
Kamikaze Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1031024.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 fluid ounce lime juice
1/2 fluid ounce triple sec
1/2 fluid ounce vodka
1 cup ice

Directions:
Combine lime juice, triple sec, and vodka in a cocktail shaker. Add ice, cover and shake until chilled. Strain into chilled short glass or cocktail glass.

Submitter: 
Allrecipes
Description: 
It doesn't get much simpler--or easier to remember--than the recipe for the kamikaze cocktail. Equal parts lime juice, triple sec, and vodka are shaken over ice and served straight up.
