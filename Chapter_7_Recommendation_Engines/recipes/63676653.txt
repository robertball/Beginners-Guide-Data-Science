Title:
Afterburner Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lg Bermuda onions	Chopped
3 Lg red tomatoes	Chopped
3 Lg bell peppers (red, orange, yellow)	Chopped
2 New Mexico chili peppers (or 2 Red Fresno + 2 ‘Long’ (~4-in green)	Chopped
12 oz Hot chopped peppers (e.g., B&G; or Goya)
16 oz Mild (or medium or hot) picante sauce
16 oz Hot taco sauce
¼ - 1 tsp Chili powder
¼ - 1 tsp Cumin

Directions:
Mix all ingredients carefully but thoroughly. Ideally, refrigerate 2 – 3 days to develop flavors.

Submitter: 
eaglerock777
Description: 
I like to prepare this 'salsa with a kick' for office parties. It's a lot of prep work, but well worth the effort! The guys descend on this treat and don't come up 'til the bowl is empty.
