Title:
Ranchy Roasted Potato Fans- Made on the Grill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/649665.jpg
Ingredients:
4 potatoes
1 medium onion
4 tbs olive oil
2 tbs powdered ranch dressing mix

Directions:
Preheat outdoor grill on low heat.
In a small bowl mix olive oil and dressing mix.  Set aside.
Scrub potatoes clean and place wooden spoons on either side of each potato.  Make cuts across the potato about 1/4 apart, down to the wooden spoon. Place in a pyrex or high temperature safe dish.
Coarsely chop onion- place in dish.
Spoon oil and ranch mixture evenly over potatoes.
Cover tightly with foil and place on grill.
Check every 10 minutes flipping the potatoes over each time.
