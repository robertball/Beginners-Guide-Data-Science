Title:
Bandeng Presto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/693578.jpg
Ingredients:
-500-600 Gr bandeng(milk fish, dry it (500-600 gr bandeng)
-3 Stalk lemongrass (3 btg serai)
-2 bay leaves (2 lbr daun salam)
-Water sufficiently (air secukupnya)
Spices:
-10 Shallots (10 btr bawang merah)
-4 garlic (4 sng bawang putih)
-8 pieces kemiri (8 btr kemiri)
-2 Cm ginger (2 cm jahe)
-1 Cm galangal (1 cm lengkuas)
-1 Teaspoon salt (1 sdt garam)
-1 / 2 spoon pepper (1/2 sdt merica)
-Bumbu Kunyit (Tumeric)

Directions:
-Prepare the cooker (Siapkan panci presto).
-Marinate the fish with spices, then wrapped with a leaf or aluminum foil (Lumuri ikan dengan bumbu halus, kemudian bungkus dgn daun atau aluminium foil).
-Then put in the cooker, add bay leaves and lemon grass (Kemudian masukkan ke dalam panci presto, tambahkan daun salam dan serai )
-Pour in enough water until the fish is submerged (Tuang air secukupnya sampai ikan cukup terendam)
-Cook kurleb 1 hour (depending on size of fish) over medium heat (Masak kurleb 1 jam (tergantung besarnya ikan) dengan api sedang) (Setelah proses pematangan dengan panci presto selesai).
-After the maturation process is complete with cooker (masak kembali dalam keadaan panci terbuka hingga air terserap habis)
Cook again in a state of open pan until the water is absorbed out.
-And then fried until golden (Kemudian goreng hingga kecoklatan).
-Or dip the fish first kedlm beaten egg, then fried until turn yellow Atau celupkan ikan terlebih dahulu kedlm kocokan telur, kemudian goreng hingga kuning).
