Title:
Buttery Garlic Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh green beans
3 cloves fresh minced garlic
3 tablespoons butter or margarine
few dashes of lemon pepper
salt to taste

Directions:
Trim and snap green beans in 1/2.
In large pan, add green beans and cover with water.
Boil for about 5 mins, or until desired tenderness is reached.
Drain water.
Add butter to pan and cook until melted, tossing beans.
Add garlic directly to pan and cook in butter until tender. Be careful not to over cook garlic, as it will cause it to become hard and bitter in taste.
Sprinkle beans with lemon-pepper and salt!

Submitter: 
LookWhatsCooking
Description: 
Only fresh green beans and garlic will do for this easy, healthy and flavorful side dish.
