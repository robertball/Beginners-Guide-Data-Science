Title:
vegetarian chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. chopped onion
1 red bell pepper
1 green pepper
1 zucchini
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 T. chili powder
1 tsp. seasoning salt
1 can each, Great Northern, kidney, and black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (28 oz) diced tomatoes
1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
green chiles to taste
Water for desired consistency

Directions:
Chop peppers and zucchini in bite-sized pieces.  Saute peppers and onion till tender, about 10 minutes.  Add garlic and zucchini along with the chili powder and salt.  Saute briefly.  Add remaining ingredients.
Cover and simmer about 20 minutes.
