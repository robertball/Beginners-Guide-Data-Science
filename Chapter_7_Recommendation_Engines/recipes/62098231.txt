Title:
Mom's Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box of Elbow Macaroni
Mayo (To Taste)
1 Can of Baby Shrimp
1 Heaping Tablespoon of Relish
1 Small White Onion Minced in Food Processer
Salt & Pepper (To Taste)
1 Can of Sliced Black Olives (Optional)

Directions:
Cook, drain, and completely cool pasta.
Combine cooled pasta with mayo until desired creaminess is achieved.
Mix in all other ingredients and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Dominique Lynne
Description: 
A simple, but delicious macaroni salad my mom makes for summer cook-outs to go along with hamburgers and hotdogs! Everyone loves it, so she always makes a ton! Plus, it's always good the next day! Mmmm...
