Title:
Heath toffee cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289862.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Butter ; (2 sticks)  softened
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup light brown sugar ; packed
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 large eggs
3 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons cream of tartar
1 bag heath toffee bits

Directions:
Heat oven to 350Â°F. Lightly grease cookie sheet.
Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, vanilla and salt in large bowl until blended. Add eggs; beat well. Stir together flour, baking soda and cream of tartar; gradually add to butter mixture, beating until blended. Stir in toffee. Drop by heaping teaspoons onto prepared cookie sheet.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool slightly. About 6 dozen cookies.
