Title:
Easy Green Beans with Cream Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5755857.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (14.5 ounce) cans green beans (such as Del Monte® Fresh Cut®), drained
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons cream cheese
salt and cracked black pepper to taste

Directions:
Pour drained green beans into a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer 10 minutes.
Add butter and cream cheese to the saucepan with the green beans. Stir until melted and well incorporated, 3 to 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
With only 3 ingredients, these easy-to-make green beans will have even the pickiest of kids eating them. Adults love them too!
