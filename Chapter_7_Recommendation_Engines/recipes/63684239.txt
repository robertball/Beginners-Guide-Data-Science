Title:
Indian Summer Raspberry Peach Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1024710.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bottle of red wine
1/2 cup peach schnapps
1/2 cup pomegranate juice
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
2 peaches, sliced
1 orange, sliced
2 lemons, sliced
1/2 pint of raspberries
24 oz raspberry flavored soda water

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large pitcher or punch bowl, add ice and enjoy!
