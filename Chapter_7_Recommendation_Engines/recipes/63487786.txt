Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123298.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 baked 9-inch pie crust
1 1/2 oz. unsweetened chocolate
1 Tbsp. butter
2 cups milk, scalded
1 cup sugar
3 egg yolks
2 Tbsp. cornstarch, dissolved in 1/3 cup milk
1/2 tsp. vanilla
whipped cream

Directions:
Using pastry blender, mix powdered sugar, peanut butter and melted butter until crumbly. Cover bottom of crust with mixture, reserving remainder for garnish.
Melt chocolate and butter in double boiler over gently simmering water.  Add milk and blend until smooth.  Whisk in sugar and stir until dissolved.  Whisk in yolks.  Whisk in cornstarch  mixture and stir until thickened, about 20 minutes.  Remove from over water.  Blend in vanilla.  Cool to room temperature, stirring frequently.  Pour into crust.  Top with whipped cream.  Sprinkle with garnish.
