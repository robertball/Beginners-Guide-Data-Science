Title:
Touchdown Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
1 package McCormick Chili Seasoning
1 can (15 oz.) kidney beans, drained
1 can (14 1/2 oz.) diced tomatoes
1 can (8 oz.) tomato sauce

Directions:
COOK ground beef and onion in large skillet over medium-high heat 5 minutes or until cooked through, stirring frequently. Drain, if needed.
STIR in McCormick Chili Seasoning and remaining ingredients. Bring to boil; cover. Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve with shredded cheese, sour cream and chopped onion, if desired.

This chili is so easy to make, takes no time at all to have it ready to be devoured.
