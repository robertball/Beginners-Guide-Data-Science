Title:
Pretzel Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/425604.jpg
Ingredients:
Dough
1 cup warm water
1 3/4 tsp rapid-rise yeast
1/2 tsp salt
3 tbsp sugar
1 tsp malt powder
3 tbsp vegetable oil
2 1/2 to 3 cups bread flour
Kettle water:
about 6 quarts of water
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp sugar
Topping:
1 egg, beaten
1 tsp sugar
2 tbsp sea salt

Directions:
Wisk water and yeast together and let stand 2-3 mins to dissolve yeast.
Briskly whisk in salt, sugar, malt, oil and most of flour.
Using dough hook, knead on lowest speed for 8-10 mins. Adding more flour if necessary to make a stiff dough.
Remove dough hook, and spray dough with cooking spray, or drizzle a little bit of oil on top and let rise 40 minutes.
Roll the dough into 1" size balls, and place on parchment lined baking sheet.Allow to rise 20-25 mins.
Preheat oven to 425.
In a large pot bring water to a boil and stir in baking soda, salt and sugar. Drop in pretzels, a few at a time, for about 30 seconds. Using a slotted spoon, transfer them to a baking sheet lined with parchment.
Combine beaten egg and sugar, and brush tops of pretzel bites with the mixture. Then sprinkle each bite with sea salt.
Bake for about 15 minutes, until golden brown.
