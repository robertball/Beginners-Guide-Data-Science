Title:
Easy peach cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 cups baking mix
7 cups milk
3.5 cups butter melted
5 large cans sliced peaches drained
6 cups of white sugar
6 Tbsp cinnamon
3 Tbsp nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix milk and baking mix in large deep buffet insert.
Add peaches evenly
Add sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg..stir
Add melted butter
Bake for 1 hour, or until Brown on top, and semi firm.
Let cool for 10 minutes
Serve with vanilla ice cream (optional)

Submitter: 
Scott
Description: 
A easy and very tasty peach cobbler. This recipe is a favorite with my guys.
