Title:
Pineapple-Green Onion Salsa with Cook's Ham Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 - 1 pound Ham Steak
1 cup pineapple tidbits
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, with pulp
1 green onion, minced
1/4 red bell pepper, minced
1/2 teaspoon lemon zest, finely minced
1 dash Tabasco sauce
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons brown sugar
1/8 teaspoon dry mustard
1 pinch ground cloves

Directions:
Place ham steaks on panini grill over medium-high heat. Grill ham steaks 3 to 5 minutes per side, turning once.
Mix together all ingredients for salsa.
Serve Ham Steak with a side of Pineapple-Green Onion Salsa.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
This easy recipe is courtesy of Chef Scott Newman, Oba, Portland, Oregon. A zesty pineapple salsa is the perfect colorful complement to a grilled ham steak.Modified from: Pineapple-Green Onion Salsa with Cook's Ham Steak
