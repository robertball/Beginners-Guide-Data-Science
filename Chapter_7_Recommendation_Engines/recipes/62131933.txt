Title:
Stir-fry Udon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
½ c. hoisin sauce
½ c. water
1 T cornstarch
1 tsp. sugar
½ tsp. fresh, grated ginger
¼ tsp. red pepper flakes
Kosher salt to taste
Fresh ground pepper to taste
Stir fry:
2 tsp. peanut oil
½ onion, sliced
3 cloves garlic, pressed
2 carrots, peeled & sliced diagonally
1 small bunch asparagus cut diagonally into 1” pieces
1 c. thinly sliced, cooked chicken (leftover rotisserie chicken is great)
1 c. sliced mushrooms
2 c. sliced napa cabbage
2 pkgs. ramen noodles, prepared without seasoning

Directions:
Sauce:  Whisk sauce ingredients together in a small bowl and set aside.
Stir fry: Heat peanut oil in a nonstick skillet. On medium-high heat, saute onion, garlic, carrots and asparagus until slightly tender, three to five minutes. Add chicken to the stir-fry skillet and toss to warm.  Add mushrooms, cabbage and sauce to the skillet. Toss all to coat with sauce, place a lid over the pan, and reduce heat to low. Cook for another three to five minutes.
Serve stir fry over prepared ramen noodles.

Submitter: 
Shelley Lima
Description: 
This is a great way to couple vegetables that must be used with ramen noodles. A quick-and-easy after work meal bursting with flavor.
