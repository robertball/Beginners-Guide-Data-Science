Title:
Black bean chipotle soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups or 3 cans black beans (rinsed and divided)
4 c. chicken stock
1 chipotle chile (in adobo sauce) plus 1 T. adobo sauce
2 T. oil
1 onion, chopped
1 green pepper, diced
1/2 red bell pepper, diced
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp. cumin
2 T. lime juice
1 small tomato, finely chopped
1/4 c. chopped cilantro

Directions:
In a blender, combine 1 can (2 c.) black beans, 2 c. chicken broth, and the Chipotle chile and sauce. Set aside.
In a stock pot, heat oil and sauce the peppers and onion till soft, about 10 minutes. Then add the garlic and cumin and cook just till fragrant, about a minute.
Add the reserved bean mix and remaining beans and simmer for 15 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the lime juice, tomato, and cilantro. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
