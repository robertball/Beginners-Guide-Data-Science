Title:
Buckwheat Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg dry yeast
1/2 cup lukewarm water
2 cups cold water
1 cup flour
2 cups buckwheat flour
1 tsp salt
1 Tbsp molasses
1/4 cup oil
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup hot water

Directions:
In large bowl, dissolve yeast in lukewarm water.  Add 2 cups cold water.
In another bowl, mix flour, buckwheat flour and salt and add to yeast mixture.  Beat vigorously until smooth.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.
In the morning mix molasses, oil and baking soda mixed in 1/2 cup hot water.  Add to pancake batter.  Let stand at room temperature for 30 minutes.
Bake in greased skillet.
