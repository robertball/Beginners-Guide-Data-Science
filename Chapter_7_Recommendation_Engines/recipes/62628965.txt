Title:
Grandma's Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb bag of potatoes
1 container of Daisy Sour Cream
½ jar of Hellman’s Mayonnaise
Salt
Pepper
1 Jar of Bread and Butter Pickles
Pickle Juice
Vinegar
6 Eggs
1 tbl spoon of Mustard

Directions:
Boil potatoes whole and leave the skins on. Boil until tender with fork but hard enough to cut without them falling apart. While the potatoes are boiling mix the sour cream (leave ¼ in container), Mayonnaise, salt, pepper, pickles, eggs and mustard. Add a dash of pickle juice and vinegar to make it creamier. When potatoes are finished cut into cubes and add the sauce mixture. Salt and pepper to taste.
