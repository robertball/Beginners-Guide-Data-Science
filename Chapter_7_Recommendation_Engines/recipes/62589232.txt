Title:
Crock Pot French Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 beef roast (pot roast, whatever is on sale)
1 can french onion soup
1 can beef consomme
1 can beef broth
beef bouillon
Provolone cheese
Hoagy rolls

Directions:
Place all ingredients in crock pot and cook all day on high.
Shred meat and serve on rolls.
