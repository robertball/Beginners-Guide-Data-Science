Title:
Cucumber sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8-ounce block of cream cheese
1/2 package of dry Italian dressing
6-8 slices of pita bread
2 cucumbers
lemmon pepper seasoning

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and Italian seasoning until smooth.  Spread over the top of the pita bread.
Peel and slice cucumbers and place on pita bread, covering as much of the bread as you can.
Sprinkle generously with lemmon pepper seasoning.
Using a pizza cutter, slice into pieces.  Best if chilled for 1-2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Shawn Hoffman Zimmerman
Description: 
Quick and easy appetizer that makes a great finger food for picnics, football games and family reunions.
