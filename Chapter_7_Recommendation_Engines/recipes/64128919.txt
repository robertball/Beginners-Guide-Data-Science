Title:
Vegan Banana Plus Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 FROZEN bananas
1/4 cup coconut oil
2 Tbsp. maple syrup, honey or other sweetener
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 Tbsp. almond butter
Pinch of salt

Directions:
Add frozen bananas to food processor; pulse until smooth.
Add remaining ingredients; pulse for 30 seconds to a minute.
Chop peanut butter cups (SEE RECIPE) and mix them into ice cream. Or stir in your choice of cocoa powder &/or peanut butter, fruit or nuts or?
Freeze ice cream for 30 minutes to an hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Nice to be able to enjoy a truly healthy frozen snack or dessert, without the chemicals you get in store bought.
