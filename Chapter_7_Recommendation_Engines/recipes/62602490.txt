Title:
Marvel's No Knead  Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet of yeast
1 cup lukewarm water
2 teaspoons sugar
3 1/2 cups of flour
2 teaspoons salt
1 tablespoon oil (olive or vegetable)

Directions:
Mix one cup lukewarm water with 1 packet of yeast and 2 tsp sugar (stir and let sit for a bit)
In a food processor, put 3 1/2 cups flour (if you have semolina add one cup to 2 1/2 cups of flour), and salt and oil
Mix briefly and add yeast mixture
Mix until a ball forms, cover and let rise about an hour
Punch down and roll out...

Description: 
A no fuss, no kneading pizza dough
