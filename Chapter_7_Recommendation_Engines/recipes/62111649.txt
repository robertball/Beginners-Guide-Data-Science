Title:
Chocolate Covered Cherry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (0.13 ounce) package unsweetened black cherry flavored drink mix
1 tub whipped topping, thawed
1 cup chocolate chips or chunks
1 9" pre-made chocolate graham cracker crust

Directions:
Mix first 4 ingredients together and pour into pie crust.
Refrigerate or freeze until set.

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick and delicious pie that tastes like you're eating a chocolate covered cherry.
