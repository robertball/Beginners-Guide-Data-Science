Title:
Chocalate Filled Cresent Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pk. of Nestle chocalate tresure (truffles) with choice of filling
1pk. pillsbury Cresent Rolls
Cinnamon Sugar

Directions:
Preheat open according to package
Open dough and seperate into factory perferated triangles
Sprinkle entire top of cresent Roll with cinnamon sugar and place one unwrapped nestle treasure of the large side of the cresent
Roll Cresent into cresent roll around nestle treaure and pinch edges to avoid choclate oozing out
Bake according to package and Serve while hot

Soft Flaky Pastery with a melting chocalty center
