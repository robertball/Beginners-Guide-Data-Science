Title:
Baked Slow Cooker Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 to 3 pound) whole chicken
salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon paprika
1/2 stick of butter

Directions:
Wad three pieces of aluminum foil into 3 to 4 inch balls, and place them in the bottom of the slow cooker.
Rinse the chicken, inside and out, under cold running water. Pat dry with paper towels.
Rub the chicken with butter.  Season the chicken with the salt, pepper and paprika.  Add celery and  onions inside chicken.  Place in the slow cooker on top of the crumbled aluminum foil.
Set the slow cooker to High for 1 hour, then turn down to Low for about 8 to 10 hours, or until the chicken is no longer pink and the juices run clear.
