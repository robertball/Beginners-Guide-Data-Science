Title:
Swamp Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/690335.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger apricot brandy
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger lime flavored vodka
2 cups prepared lemonade
1 cup ice

Directions:
In a tall glass over ice, combine apricot brandy, lime vodka and lemonade. Stir.

Submitter: 
C19651
Description: 
This refreshing beverage has the appearance of swamp water, but the aroma and flavor are not unpleasant!
