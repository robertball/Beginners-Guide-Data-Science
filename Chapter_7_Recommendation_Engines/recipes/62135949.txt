Title:
Quick Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter, melted
1 large can sliced peaches
1 teaspoon almond extract
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 cup self-rising flour
1 cup sugar
1 cup milk

Directions:
Melt butter in a 9x9 baking pan. While butter is melting, mix together self-rising flour, sugar, and milk. Mix well and set aside. Open peaches and add the almond extract to the peaches. Pour the peaches into melted butter, then puor the batter over the peaches. Do not mix together. Sprinkle top with cinnamon. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes to 1 hour or until golden. Serve warm with ice cream.

Submitter: 
Jane1947
Description: 
This is a quick dessert that is pretty good. It has been around for a while, but I added a new twist.
