Title:
Wine Slushies (With Ice Cream Maker) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2384809.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle sweet wine (such as Riesling)
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 lime, juiced

Directions:
Combine wine, water, sugar, and lime juice together in a bowl until sugar is dissolved; transfer to an ice cream maker. Follow manufacturers' instructions for ice cream.
