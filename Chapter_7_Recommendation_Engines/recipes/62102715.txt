Title:
ZIPLOC OMELET Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(2) Eggs - large or extra large
Cheese
Diced Ham
Chopped Onion
Green Pepper
Diced Tomato
Hash Browns
etc.

Directions:
Crack 2 eggs into the bag (not more than 2)
Shake to combine.
Each guest adds prepared ingredients of choice to their bag and shake.
Make sure to get the air out of the bag and zip it up.
Place the bags into rolling, boiling water for exactly 13 minutes.
You can usually cook 6-8 omelets in a large pot.
For more, make another pot of boiling water.
Open the bags and the omelet will roll out easily.

Submitter: 
BETTYCOOK
Description: 
This recipe was given to me from a friend. This work's great!!  Good for when all your family is together.  The best part is that no one has to wait for their special omelet!!!).  Have guests write their name on a quart-size Ziploc freezer bag with permanent marker and get ready to prepare their own special omelet.
