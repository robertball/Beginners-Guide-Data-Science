Title:
Ultra Clean Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 Medium Baked sweet potato
10 cups roasted cauliflower
2.4 lbs ground chicken breast
3 egg whites
1 cup quick oats
2 med green bell peppers
1 red onion
2 tbs paprika
1 tbs garlic, chopped
1 tbs dried basil
1 tbs oregano
2 tbs fish sauce

Directions:
bake sweet potatoes
roast cauliflower
mix all chicken ingredients.
Spray a muffin tin. Mush into 20 balls.
Cook at 375F for 35min
