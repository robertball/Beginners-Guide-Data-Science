Title:
Easy Strawberry Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
4 1/2 cups water
1 (10 ounce) package frozen strawberries, pureed

Directions:
In a large pitcher, mix together lemonade concentrate and water. Stir in strawberries.

Submitter: 
Dawn Norby
Description:
