Title:
(Arrachera) Skirt Steak Taco Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lbs Skirt Steak
1 Small White Onion-Sliced thin
5 Garlic Cloves-halved and mashed
1 Orange-thinly sliced (with peeling)
1-1/2 Teaspoons Cumin
1/2 bottle light-colored beer (lager style)
1/2 Cup Soy Sauce
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
In a large baking dish place a layer onion, orange, garlic, then place a layer of meat on top of it. Sprinkle about 1/2 cumin, and salt and pepper.  Pour half of beer and soy sauce over it.  Then repeat step until meat is gone.  Cover and let marinade at room temp for 30-60 minutes.  Grill about 6-7 minutes per side.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is a quick and simple marinade for skirt steak.  Make sure to ask the butcher to cut the meat thin and run it through the tenderizer.
