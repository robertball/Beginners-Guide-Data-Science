Title:
Charley's Slow Cooker Mexican Style Meat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (4 pound) chuck roast
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/4 cups diced green chile pepper
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1 (5 ounce) bottle hot pepper sauce
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 cans stewed tomatos
2 cans tomato sauce
2 cups burgandy wine
2 T soy sauce
2 T worchester
2 T garlic powder
3 Cans corn
3 Cans peas

Directions:
Trim the roast of any excess fat, and season with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place meat in hot skillet, and brown meat quickly on all sides.
Transfer the roast to a slow cooker, and sprinkle onion over meat. Season with chile peppers, chili powder, cayenne pepper, hot pepper sauce, and garlic powder.  Add enough water to cover the roast 1/3 of the way.
Cover, and cook on High for 6 hours, checking to make sure there is always at least a small amount of liquid in the bottom. Reduce heat to Low, and add stewed tomatoes, tomato sauce,. continue cooking for 2 to 4 hours, or until meat is totally tender and falls apart.  Add peas and corn the last hour
