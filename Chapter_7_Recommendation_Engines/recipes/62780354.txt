Title:
Cherry-Wheat Germ Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
3/4 cup Craisins
1/2 cup toasted wheat germ
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon allspice
1 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup canola oil
1 large egg, lightly beaten
Cooking spray

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees.
Combine flour through allspice in a large bowl & stir with a whisk.  Make a well in center.  Combine buttermilk, oil & egg, stirring well with whisk.  Add liquid mixture to dry ingredients just until moistened. Spray muffin pan with Pam, fill & bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.
