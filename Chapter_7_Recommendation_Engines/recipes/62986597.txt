Title:
Potato Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 medium potatoes (red OK)
1 large onion
3 eggs
1.5 tsp salt
.25 tsp baking powder
.25 tsp black pepper
1/4 cup better or margarine, melted
1/3 cup flour

Directions:
Put onion and potato thru food processor grater 2X.
Mix in remaining ingredients.  Bake in greased glass loaf pan at 335 for 1.5 hours until golden brown.
