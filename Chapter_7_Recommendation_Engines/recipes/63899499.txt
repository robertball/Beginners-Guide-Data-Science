Title:
Red Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1081892.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ketchup
1/2 cup chilli sauce
1/2 oil
5 garlic cloves crushed
1/4 cup white vinegar
1 tbsp white sugar
2 tbsp mustard
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients in a airtight container. Add the cubes of chicken or beef. ( If doing kabobs )
Mix well all together and refrigerate for at least 24 hours.
