Title:
Linguine with White Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
large pinch of crushed red pepper
1 cup dry white wine
1 can baby clams in juice
1 tablespoons cold unsalted butter
salt and fresh ground black pepper
1 pound linguine, cooked al dente
1/4 cup chopped flat leaf parsley
1 tablespoon chopped oregano

Directions:
Heat oil in med stockpot over high heat. Add garlic and cook for a minute. Add crushed red pepper and cook for a few seconds. Add white wine and clam juice and cook until the mixture reduces by half, about 3 minutes.  Add the butter and cook until melted, season with salt and pepper. Add the clams and heat through. Stir in the pasta, parsley and oregano until combined.
