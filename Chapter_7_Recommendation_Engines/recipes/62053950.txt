Title:
ice cream-cookie cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 half gal. carton plain vanilla ice          cream, softened
1 package oreo cookies
1 12 oz.container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 jar chocolate fudge dessert topping

Directions:
Coarsely crush oreos (place in plastic bag and crush with rolling pin) and place half in bottom of glass 9x13 baking dish. Evenly spread softened ice cream on top of cookies. Drizzle half of the fudge sauce ontop of ice cream and then layer the whipped topping over that.Place the remaining crushed cookies on the whipped topping and finish off by drizzling the rest of the fudge sauce on top. Heating the fudge sauce slightly makes it easier to evenly drizzle. Cover with either a lid or plastic wrap and freeze for at least 2 hours. Slice into serving pieces and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Rebecca
Description: 
This is a simple yet delicious ice cream dessert that takes just minutes to assemble. Once frozen, it produces a rich and wonderful combination of flavors that every ones loves!
