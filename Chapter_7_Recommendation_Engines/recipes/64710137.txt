Title:
Instant Pot Cowboy Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bacon, 5 slices diced
Onion, 1 roughly diced
Garlic, 3 cloves
Black Beans, 2 15oz cans drained and rinsed
Diced Tomatoes, 14.5oz can
Salt, 1.5 teaspoons
Jalapeño, seeds removed and quartered

Directions:
Set the instant pot to sauté and cook the diced bacon until the fat begins to render, at which time add the onions and continue to cook until bacon is browned.
Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute, then add the drained/rinsed black beans, diced tomatoes including the juice, salt, and jalapeño.
Install lid and pressure cook for 20 minutes.  After the beans have pressure cooked for 20 minutes, manually release pressure, and remove lid.  Set the pressure cooker to sauté and and stir the beans for approximately 5 mins, breaking some of the beans up in the process, while evaporating some of the liquid.

Description: 
These instant pot cowboy beans are rich and creamy, and extremely easy to make.
