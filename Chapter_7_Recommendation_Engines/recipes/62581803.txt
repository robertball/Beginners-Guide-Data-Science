Title:
Emily's Mom's Angel Hair Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts
2 Tbsp butter
1/2 of a pkg of dry Italian salad dressing mix
1/4 c white wine
1/2 can Golden Mushroom soup
2 oz cream cheese w/ chives
8oz angel hair pasta

Directions:
In a large saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add dressing mix, wine & soup. Blend in cream cheese & stir till smooth. Arrange chicken in a baking dish & pour the sauce over. Bake at 325 for 1 hr. When chicken is nearly done cook the pasta & drain. Serve warm chicken over the pasta. Can garnish w/ sauteed bell peppers.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is a recipe given to me by my dear friend Emily's mother, a recipe that's great for entertaining.
