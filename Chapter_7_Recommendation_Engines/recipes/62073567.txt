Title:
Low-fat chocolate banana muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium ripe bananas, mashed
1 egg
1/3 c light sour cream
2 T applesauce
2 T skim milk
1 t vanilla
1/4 c cocoa powder
1/2 t salt
1 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
2 t Splenda
1 T brown sugar
1 c all purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Beat first 6 ingredients (banana through vanilla) in a medium bowl. In a separate bowl, mix remaining ingredients. Add flour mixture to banana mixture and stir until combined. Spoon into muffin tin coated with cooking spray. Bake for about 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center of muffin comes out clean.
