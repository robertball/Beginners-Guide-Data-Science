Title:
Leah's black bean and chicken quesadiller Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/875847.jpg
Ingredients:
6 flour tortillas
-Stuffing-
1 can black beans (rinsed)
1 lb chicken
1 minced jalapeno (or other pepper)
1 4oz can of chopped green chilies
2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese
-Marinade for chicken-
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup lime juice
1 tbs minced garlic
salt and pepper to taste
-Dips-
avocado
sour cream
salsa

Directions:
Mix the olive oil, lime juice, garlic, half the can of green chilies and the salt and pepper in a zip lock bag. Add the chicken and marinade for 30 min. While your chicken is in the marinade, drain and rinse black beans in a colander. Then rid the jalapeno of its seeds and either dice fresh or grilled. Once the chicken is done, place on grill or skillet and cook each side for 6 min. Once the chicken is done, dice into small cubes. Then mix beans, chicken, other half of green chilies, jalapeno and cheese together until blended. Brush one side of a tortilla with butter and place on flat griddle of pan. Scoop mixture onto tortilla and place another butter covered tortilla on top. place on medium heat and cook until side is brown and flip (making sure to not spill filling) remain cooking until both sides are brown and enjoy with avocado, sour cream and salsa. YUMMY!
