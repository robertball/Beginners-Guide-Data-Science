Title:
Andy's Sticky Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
3 tbsp honey
juice of 1 orange
1/3 tsp baking soda
1 chicken stock cube

Directions:
Cut chicken into strips or cubes
Mix honey, vinegar, orange juice and baking soda in a bowl large enough for the chicken
Marinate chicken for at least 10 minutes
Stir fry the chicken (no oil required) with all the marinade for 5 mins
Sprinkle in the chicken stock cube and mix the chicken
Cook until sticky (around another 5-10 minutes)

Submitter: 
Perversia
Description: 
This is a recipe created by my partner once when we were trying to make something else and realised we didn't have half the ingredients.  It works well served with rice or noodles.
