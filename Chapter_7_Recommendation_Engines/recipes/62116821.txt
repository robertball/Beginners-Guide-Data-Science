Title:
Spicy Shrimp Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 small jalapeno, diced (may be de-seeded)
1 small onion, julienned
1 clove of garlic, minced
2 large red bell peppers, julienned
3 stalks of celery, sliced
1/2 tsp salt
2 1/2 tablespoons Oyster Sauce
1 tsp soy sauce
2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions:
In a wok or large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium high heat.
Sautee onions until slightly browned and softened, add garlic and saute another minute.
Add red bell peppers, celery, jalapeno, and salt.Continue to saute for 5-9 minutes or until vegetables are slightly softened.
Toss in shrimp and saute for 3-4 minutes.
When shrimp are nearly cooked, drizzle oyster sauce and soy sauce over the mixture and cook for another 2 minutes or until shrimp are opaque.

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
I only had a few ingredients in the fridge and I was hungry; so I threw them all together and it was YUMMY! The red bell peppers gives this dish a subtle sweetness and the jalapeno gives it a kick! Best served with a side of jasmine rice.
