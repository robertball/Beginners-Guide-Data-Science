Title:
Arby-Q-Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/999524.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C water
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup white vinegar
scant 1 t. onion powder
scant 1 t. garlic powder
1/2 t smoked paprika
3 T W sauce
1/8 t. salt
1/8 t. cayenne
Thickener
1 T cornstarch
2 T water

Directions:
Combine first 9  ingredients and simmer on med low for 20 minutes.
Mix 1T starch and 2 T water and add to sauce.
Simmer additional 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Cool. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
PAMELA D. aPROpos of nothing
Description: 
Not a dead ringer but not a bad substitute either. This recipe is out on the web but I removed the sugar and used smoked paprika for our taste.
