Title:
Chicken Breasts with black pepper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Chicken Breats (skinless & bonesless)
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tbsp ground black pepper
2 tbsp butter
1 cup of water

Directions:
In a large skillet bring 1 cup of water to a boil
place your two whole chicken breast in for approximately 5 mins on either side.
Drain water, and remove chicken from pan. On a cutting board cut each breast legnthwise in half so you now have 4 chicken peices. They will most likely still be pink in the middle.
Melt butter in skillet, place in your chicken and sprinkely with salt and lots of pepper. Cook for 3-4 mins on each side.
You'll end up with a cripsy exterior and juicey interior.
Serve with Mashed potatoes.
