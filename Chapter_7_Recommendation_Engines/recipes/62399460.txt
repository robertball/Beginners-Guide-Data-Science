Title:
Luscious Lime Coconut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
3/4 cup butter, cold
FILLING:
4 Eggs
1 1/4 cups sugar
3/4 cup lime juice
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 drop green food coloring (optional)
3/4 cup shredded coconut
1/3 cup confectioners' sugar for dusting (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
In a large bowl, combine the flour and sugar. Cut the butter into small slices and add to mixture; either cut in with pastry cutter or use your hands to squeeze/crumble the butter into the mixture.
Press into the bottom of a lightly greased 13x9 inch baking pan. Bake crust in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a medium bowl, beat the eggs, sugar, lime juice, baking powder, flour and food coloring until well mixed. Pour over hot crust, sprinkle coconut evenly over top.
Return to preheated oven and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until light golden brown. Cool on a wire rack. Dust with confectioners' sugar (optional). Cut into squares.
