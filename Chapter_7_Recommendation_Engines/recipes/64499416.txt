Title:
Big Ray's Cheesy Zucchini Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2613436.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups thinly sliced unpeeled zucchini (4 small)
1 cup baking mix
1/2 cup onion,  finely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/2 tsp dried marjoram or oregano leaves
1/8 tsp pepper
1/2 cup vegetable oil
4 eggs, slightly beaten

Directions:
Heat oven to 350ÂºF. Grease bottom and sides of rectangular pan, 13' x 9".
Stir together all ingredients. Spread in pan.
Bake about 25 minutes or until golden brown. Cut into 2-inch squares; cut squares diagonally in half into triangles.
