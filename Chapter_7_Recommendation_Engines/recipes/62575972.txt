Title:
Pesto Penne Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/603750.jpg
Ingredients:
Pesto
1 cup fresh basil
2 or 3 garlic cloves
1/4 cup pine nuts
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
4 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons lemon juice
Other ingredients
4 cups penne ''plume'' ( the small penne )
1/2 cup sliced black olives (Kalamata)
1/2 cup diced roasted red pepper
1/2 cup of chopped sun dried tomatoes
1/4 cup roasted pine nuts
1 cup chopped asparagus
1/2 cup sliced zucchini

Directions:
Put all ingredients for pesto in a food processor and whiz until you get a pesto texture.
Cook penne pasta
Heat large deep pan ,add a little olive oil and roast pine nuts until lightly brown then set aside .Saute asparagus, zucchini then add remaining ingredients and pine nuts.
When pasta is cooked , drain water then put pasta into bowl and add 1 tablespoon olive oil and toss, then add pasta to veggie mixture in pan and add 1/4 cup of the pesto and toss well.
Serve in bowls and add grated parmesan cheese...Oh yeah !

Submitter: 
Mikey's Kitchen
Description: 
This is a recipe you can serve hot or cold. A great side-dish or can be a full meal. My kids love this and it's always a hit.
