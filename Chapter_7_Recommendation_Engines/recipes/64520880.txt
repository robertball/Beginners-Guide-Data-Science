Title:
The Devils Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 egg hard boiled
1/4 Cup Mayo
2 TBS Dijon mustard
1 TBS chopped jarred jalapeno
dash of garlic powder
S/P to taste
Dusting of cayenne

Directions:
Cut each egg in half.  Scoop out the hard yolk into a mixing bowl.  With a fork mash/ separate the yolk.  Add the Mayo, Mustard, Jalapeno, garlic powder and salt and pepper and mix.  Pipe the filling into each egg half and sprinkle with cayenne pepper.  Refrigerate until ready to eat.

Submitter: 
Love2cook32
Description: 
Traditional deviled eggs with a devilish twist.  Jalapenos replace the pickle relish and cayenne replaces the paprika.  Very tasty and not too hot.  You can add more jalapeno for more heat.
