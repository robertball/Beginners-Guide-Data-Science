Title:
Macaroni Meatball Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/766487.jpg
Ingredients:
20-25 frozen Italian meatballs, thawed in microwave
1 green pepper
1 medium onion
2 garlic cloves, minced
olive oil
5 cups of water
1 quart crushed tomatoes
1 can tomato paste
1T brown sugar
2t Italian seasoning
1t salt
1/4t pepper
2C uncooked pasta noodles

Directions:
Chunk and cook onion and green pepper using olive oil in a Dutch oven or soup kettle.  When onions are translucent add all remaining ingredients except for pasta.  Bring to a boil then add pasta, cook until tender.  Serve with croutons and parmesan, if desired.
