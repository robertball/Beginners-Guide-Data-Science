Title:
Melanie Sandwhich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large white eggs
2 slices of american cheese
2 slices of salami
2 slices of white bread
a little bit of milk (not much needed)
butter(for the pan)
ketchup to your likings

Directions:
First, mix the eggs in a bowl, with a little bit of milk.
Second, turn on the stove, and butter the pan.
Third, add the eggs to the pan and add the cheese.
After that, scramble the eggs.
Then, pour the eggs on a slice of bread.
Next, spread ketchup on the other slice of bread.
Add the salami to the sandwhich, and put on the other slice of bread.
Finally cut the sandwhich in half and eat eat eat!

Submitter: 
Melanie
Description: 
When I was little, my dad would make me a delicious Melanie Sandwhich.  It was a perfect serving...and to this day i still make an occasional Melanie Sandwhich.
