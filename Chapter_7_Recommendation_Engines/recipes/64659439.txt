Title:
Crack Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 regular cans of green beans, drained (note: you can substitute a similar amount of frozen green beans, about 4 (12-ounce) bags, thawed, or you can use fresh green beans, too)
12 slices bacon
2/3 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup butter, melted
7 teaspoons soy sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder

Directions:
Put the drained beans in a 9x13 pan. Cook the bacon, chop and add  the cooked bacon pieces over the beans.
Mix the remaining ingredients ( the crack sauce). Pour over the beans and bake 40 minutes at 350.
Toss and serve
