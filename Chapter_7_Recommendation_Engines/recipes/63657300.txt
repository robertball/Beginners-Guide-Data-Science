Title:
Big Ray's Dry Rub Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1016408.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbl brown sugar
1 1/2 Tbl paprika
1 1/2 Tbl season salt
1 1/2 Tbl ground black pepper
1 Tbl garlic powder
1 Tbl onion powder
1 tsp red pepper flakes
1 tsp cumin
1/2 cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients. Store in an air tight container in a dark and dry place until ready to use.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
After tweeking the spices for several years, I finally found that perfect blend that everybody loves....or else. Great on beef, pork or chicken to grill or bake.
