Title:
Bleu Cheese Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 baking potatoes
1 white onion
8 oz. bleu cheese
1 cup parmesan cheese
7 slices bacon
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup flour
1 1/2 cup milk
3/4 cup half and half
1 tablespoon pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon thyme

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Peel potatoes and slice into 1/4 inch slices.
Cook bacon until crispy and crumble into small pieces.
In a medium saucepan on medium heat melt butter. Add flour and whisk the two for 1 minute.
Turn heat down to low. Slowly add the milk and half and half. Add the sour cream. Whisk for approximately 5 minutes or until sauce is thickened.
Add bleu cheese, parmesan, pepper, salt, garlic, and thyme. Stir until sauce is smooth.
In a greased 9x11 baking dish, layer potatoes, cheese sauce, onions, and bacon crumbles. Sprinkle a teaspoon of pepper over top.
Bake uncovered for 1 hour and 20 minutes or until nicely browned on top.

Description: 
This potato casserole dish is for all those bleu cheese lovers out there! Its a unique spin on a classic dish. Perfect for holiday meals and for special dinners.
