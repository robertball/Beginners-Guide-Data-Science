Title:
Toffee Crunch Grahams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 whole Graham Crackers
1 1/2 cups unsalted butter
1 cup packed, brown sugar
2 Tbsp Maple Syrup
2 cups sliced almonds

Directions:
Line a 15" x 10" x 1" baking pan with heavy duty aluminum foil.  Place graham crackers in pan.  In a saucepan combine butter, brown sugar, and maple syrup.  Bring to a boil and boil for 3 minutes (~300 °F on a candy thermometer).  Carefully pour over graham crackers and then sprinkle with almonds.
Bake at 400°F for 4-5 minutes until it starts to bubble.  Cool in pan for 4 minutes.
Pull up foil and separate from hardened cookies.  Break cookies into 2" x 3" pieces and transfer to wire rack to cool completely
