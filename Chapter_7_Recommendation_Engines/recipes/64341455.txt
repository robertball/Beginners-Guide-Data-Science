Title:
Homemade Garlic Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
400 g Multigrain French baguette
75 ml Chefmaster 52% oil margarine
25 ml minced garlic
25 ml dried parsley
15 ml black pepper *or to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F, slice baguettes into 1/2" slices then cut each slice into 2, melt margarine in large wok or skillet adding the garlic, pepper and parsley, once melted let cook about 2 minutes to bring mingle flavors of the spices, then remove from heat and stri baguette into butter mixture tossing to coat, pour croutons onto a cookie sheet, bake in oven until browned. let cool and store in conainer until needed

Tony Carriere
