Title:
Drink for insomnia (Goldenmilk) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c water
1/4 c turmeric
2 c coconut milk
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. coconut oil
1 tsp. chopped ginger
4-6 black peppercorns
stevia

Directions:
Add turmeric to water in small sauce pan.  Stir over gentle heat for 7-10 minutes.  If too thick, add a little more water.  Store paste in small glass jar in fridge for up to 2 weeks.
Put coconut milk into small saucepan.  Add cinnamon, coconut oil,  1 tsp - 1 tbsp of the turmeric paste,  ginger,  peppercorns, one pinch of stevia,
Stir; simmer covered for 10 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally.
Strain through fine mesh strainer to get rid of ginger and peppercorns.  Pour into a mug (makes about 2 cups).  Sprinkle with cinnamon
