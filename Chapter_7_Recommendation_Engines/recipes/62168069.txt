Title:
Cauliflower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups raw cauliflower, broken into small pieces (small head)
3 boiled eggs, diced
1/2 small carrot, make small shavings with a potato pealer
3 small green onions
1 Tbs. mustard
5 Tbs. sweet pickle relish
5 Tbs, light mayonaise
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook cauliflower in salted water until tender, drain well; cool.
Add all ingredients and mix.

Rosalie

1/2 cup servings has 75 calories and 8 carbs, appro.
