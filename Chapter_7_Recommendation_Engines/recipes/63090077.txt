Title:
Chicken Cacciatore Weight Watchers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 to 3 pound chicken, skinned and cut up.
4 ounces sliced onion
2 garlic cloves
2 medium tomatoes (fresh or canned diced)*
3/4 cup tomato puree
1 teaspooon salt
1/2 teaspoon crushed oregano
1/4 teaspoon pepper
+ If using canned diced tomatoes, increase the tomato puree

Directions:
Brown chicken on all sides in a large preheated nonstick skillet.  Remove from skillet and brown onion slices and garlic.  Return browned chicken with remaining ingredients to skillet; cover and simmer for 45 minutes (DO NOT BOIL)) until chicken is tender and sauce is thick.  To further reduce the sauce, remove the shicken and cook liquid at high heat until it evaporates and thickens.
