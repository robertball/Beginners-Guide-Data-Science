Title:
Peanut Butter Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts cut into chunks or strips
1tbsp ground coriander
2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
4tbsp vegetable oil
2 tbsp clear honey
1tbsp vegetable oil
2 tbsp mild curry paste
2tbsp brown sugar
2 generous tbsp of peanut butter - chunky or smooth, it is up to you
200ml coconut milk

Directions:
- in a large bowl, mix the chicken strips in with the ground coriander, garlic, 4 tbsp veg oil and honey.
- cover with cling film and leave the chicken to marinade for a minimum of 10 minutes.
- for the sauce, heat the 1 tbsp veg oil in a large pan. Add the curry paste, brown sugar, and peanut butter and fry for about a minute.
- add the coconut milk and bring the mixture to a boil, constantly stirring. Then, reduce heat and allow to simmer for 5 mins.
- meanwhile, heat a separate frying pan and stirfry the chicken mixture until the meat is cooked.
- serve with basmati or jasmine rice.
