Title:
Taco Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1032635.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
1 taco seasoning packet
1/2 cup salsa
1/4 cup water
1 cup cottage cheese
2 cups cheddar cheese, divided
21 jumbo shells
12 oz enchilada sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Prepare jumbo pasta shells according to package.  Drain and set aside.
In a large skillet, brown meat. Drain fat.  Return to skillet and add taco seasoning, salsa and water.  Simmer 5 minutes.
In a large bowl, combine cottage cheese, 1 cup of cheddar cheese and taco meat.
Stuff each shell with the taco meat and place in 9x13-inch pan.  Top with enchilada sauce and remaining cheese.
Bake for 30 minutes, until bubbly.
