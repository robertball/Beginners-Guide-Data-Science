Title:
Trishie's Black Cap Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb blackcurrants, topped and tailed
2 tsp lemon juice
1 oz. sugar
4 oz. flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
4 oz fresh white breadcrumbs
1 oz sugar
1 tsp grated lemon rind
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 pint milk

Directions:
1. Well butter a 2 pint pudding bowl.
2. Rinse and lightly drain the blackcurrants and place in a dampened saucepan with the lemon juice and sugar.
3. Cook very getntly for 5 to 10 minutes, then spoon into the pudding basin.
4. Sift the flour and baking powder together in a bowl, then mix in the breadcrumbs, sugar and lemon rind.
5. Stir in the eggs, then add the milk and stir until combined well.
6. Leave to stand in a cool place for 15 minutes, then pour over the blackcurrants.
7. Cover with a piece of well buttered greaseproof paper and seal with kitchen foil.
8. Place in a steamer and steam over a saucepan of boiling water for 2 to 2-1/2 hours, topping up the water as necessary.
9. Turn out onto a plate and serve.

Submitter: 
Trishie
Description: 
This is a recipe for a Traditional Irish light Sponge pudding. The black cap is provided by the blackcurrants!
