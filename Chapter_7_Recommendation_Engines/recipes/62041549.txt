Title:
Grannies Famous Eggnog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, separated
3/4 cups sugar
1 pint heavy whipping cream
1 pint milk (may need more for thinner nog if not using alcohol)
1 1/2 cups rum
5 cups of whiskey
(if desired a non-alcoholic nog replace the whiskey and rum with 1 1/2 tsp rum extract)

Directions:
Beat egg whites until stiff.  Add 1/4 cup sugar.  In seperate bowl, beat egg yolks, add remaining sugar while beating.  Mix yolks and white together.  Add milk and 1/2 pint cream, rum and whiskey.  Stir throughly.  Fold in 1/2 pint of cream whipped.  This is good but strong, dilute with more milk if you think necessary.

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
This eggnog recipe has been in our family for generations.  My grannie will make gallons of it for special holidays.  I tell ya, a few gallons isn't enough!
