Title:
Giblet Gravy III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups chicken broth
Neck (cut in half) & Giblets
2 large white onions, sliced
1 cup sliced carrots
1 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup celery leaves
6 cloves garlic (peeled)
1 bay leaf
6 tbsp butter, softened
3/4 cup all purpose flour
turkey drippings
salt to taste (optional)
pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
Place broth, neck, all giblets except the liver, onions, carrots, wine, celery, garlic, and bay leaf and bring to a boil.  Simmer for 1.5 hours.
Add the liver and simmer for another 30 minutes.
While the broth is simmering, combine the butter and flour until a thick paste.
Remove and discard neck and giblets.  Strain broth, pressing vegetables to extract broth, and discard vegetables.
Add turkey drippings to broth.  Add flour mixture gradually and stir until smooth.  Boil until desired consistency.  Add salt and pepper to taste (optional).

Submitter: 
Tikeren
Description: 
My mother has made this old fashioned gravy every Thanksgiving for as long as I can remember!  It is delicious with Turkey and mashed potatoes.  Start making this while the turkey is cooking since it requires about two hours of simmering.
