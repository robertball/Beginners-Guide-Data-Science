Title:
Low Carb Coconut Flax Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp Unsweetened Coconut Flakes
1/2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Stevia
1 lg Egg
1 tsp Silk Unsweetened Coconut Milk
1 tsp Coconut Oil
4 Tbsp Golden Ground Flax Meal
1 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
Transfer your batter into a microwave safe mug.
Cook your batter in your mug in the microwave on high for 1 minute.

Submitter: 
Amy Waterman Swithers
Description: 
A perfect, quick, low carb muffin!
