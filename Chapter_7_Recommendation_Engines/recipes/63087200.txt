Title:
Hearty Turkey Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean turkey
1 med onion
2 small zucchini quartered and sliced
1 large carrot
3 14 oz cans low sodium beef broth
1 jar 26 oz garden style pasta sauce
1 16 oz can kidney beans drained
1 15 oz can great northern beans
1 14 oz can Italian diced tomatoes
1 tblsp dried parsley
2 tblsp hot pepper sauce
2 tsp oregano
1 tsp pepper
1 cup uncooked  pasta

Directions:
Brown turkey and onion until no longer pink. Add zucchini and carrots cook and stir 1 minute. Stir in broth, pasta sauce, beans, tomatoes, and spices. Bring to boil, reduce heat cover and simmer 45 minutes.  Cook pasta according to package. Just before serving Soup add pasta.
