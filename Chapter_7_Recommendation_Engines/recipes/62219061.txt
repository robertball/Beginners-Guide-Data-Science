Title:
Champagne Chicken and Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20-30 precooked shrimp
2 green onions
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp pepper
1 c water
1/4 c flour
1 cube chicken bouillon
3 tbsp butter
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
3/4 pound mushrooms, thinly sliced
2 c heavy cream
1 c champagne

Directions:
Thaw precooked shrimp.  In medium bowl combine shrimp, green onions ( cut diagonally in pieces 1 in long), lemon juice, salt and pepper; set aside. In small bowl combine water, flour and bouillon, set aside.
Add butter to large skillet and over medium heat, cook chicken breasts until brown and tender (approx. 10-12 minutes). Add 1/3 cup champagne to the frying  pan and simmer the chicken breasts for another 3 to 4 minutes. While chicken is simmering cook mushroom in another frying pan, until tender. Remove mushrooms with a slotted spoon. Put shrimp in pan which you cooked the mushroom and cook until shrimp turns pink about 2 - 3 minutes. Remove chicken to a platter and keep warm. Stir flour mixture (set aside earlier) into the chicken skillet until blended. Gradually add cream and champagne. Cook, stirring constantly until mixture thickens and boils; stir in mushrooms; heat throughly. Place a few shrimp on each chicken breast and pour sauce over chicken and shrimp.
Enjoy
