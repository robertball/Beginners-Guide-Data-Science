Title:
Caramel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 bags of microwave popcorn
1 stick of butter
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup corn syrup

Directions:
Pop bags of popcorn, shake out loose kernels and place in large container/bowl.
Mix sugar and syrup and bring to a rolling boil.
Add in butter, milk and vanilla and stir.
Pour over popcorn and toss well till all is coated.

Submitter: 
Julie Moon
Description: 
Caramel corn that is buttery smooth and stays soft!
