Title:
Peanut Butter Snack Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/822587.jpg
Ingredients:
12 vanilla wafer cookies
1 (8 ounce) carton frozen whipped topping, thawed, divided
1 cup cold milk
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix

Directions:
Place wafers in paper- or foil-lined muffin cups. Top each with 1 tablespoon whipped topping. In a mixing bowl, combine milk and peanut butter. Add pudding mix; beat on low speed for 2 minutes. Fold in remaining whipped topping. Spoon into prepared cups. Cover and freeze. Remove from the freezer 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Nancy Clark
Description: 
'When our kids were little, they loved this cool and creamy summertime treat,' relates Nancy Clark of Cochranton, Pennsylvania. 'We'd keep several batches in the freezer so there were plenty when their neighborhood friends came over to play.'
