Title:
nannys german potato soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb roll sausage ( reg or hot and spicy)
3 lbs potatoes
1/2 c chopped onion
1 package frozen egg noodles
4 c milk
1/4 c margerine
salt and pepper

Directions:
1.brown sausage drain and set aside.
2. peel and chop potatoes to bite size chunks. cover with water and boil. season.
3. when potatoes are soft add sausage and egg noodles boil till egg noodle soft and warm.
4. turn down and simmer add milk and margerine. stir. season.
5. serve

Submitter: 
kimmy schmidt
Description: 
I grew up on my nannys german potato soup.sausage, noodles and potatos whats not to like.
