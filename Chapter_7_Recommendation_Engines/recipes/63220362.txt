Title:
Strawberry Mint Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups strawberries, hulled
1 tbsp fresh mint leaves (or more, to taste)
6-8 ice cubes
1 tbsp vanilla syrup or honey
1/2 cup kefir or yogurt (optional)
Splash of orange juice

Directions:
Put ice cubes in blender, followed by remaining ingredients.
Blend until smooth.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
BakingBabe
Description: 
This healthy, creamy smoothie is so refreshing on a hot day!  Make it an adult beverage by adding rum.  It's like a healthy version of a strawberry daiquiri!
