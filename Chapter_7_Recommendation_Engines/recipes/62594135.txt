Title:
Chip Salsa Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can Rotel Tomatoes
3 Tbsp. Chopped Cilantro
1 Can Black Beans (drained & rinsed)
1 Can Sheepeg White Corn (drained)
1 4 oz. Can Chili's (jalepeno's)
3 Tbsp. Green Onion chopped
3 Tbsp. Light Olive Oil
1/8 tsp. Lemon Pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well. Allow to sit in fridge for 4 hours. Serve cold with Frito Lay Scoops!

Description:
