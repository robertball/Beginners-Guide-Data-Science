Title:
Cheesy Salsa Mac Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs 80% lean ground beef
2 c fresh salsa
1/8 tsp ground pepper
1 tsp taco seasoning
1.5 c elbow pasta
1.5 c hot water
1 c milk
8 ounces velveeta, cubed
4 ounces shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef in a large, deep skillet. Drain.
Add pepper and taco seasoning, stirring to combine.
Mix in fresh salsa and simmer for 5 minutes.
Add water, milk, and pasta. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 10 min or until pasta is done.
Stir in cheese and heat through.

Submitter: 
shackbart
Description: 
Cheesy skillet pasta with ground beef. Use hot salsa for a nice kick.
