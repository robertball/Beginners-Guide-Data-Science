Title:
Savory 3-Cheese Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 refrigerated pie crust
1 cup grated Swiss
1/2 cup marbled jack cheese
3 tablespoons grated Romano
1/2 cup chopped medium onion
1 cup fresh broccoli florets
1 1/2 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
1/4 teaspoon dried marjoram
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
3 eggs
1 cup whole milk
1/2 cup half and half
7-8 cherry tomatoes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Sprinkle the cheeses over the bottom of the pie shell
Evenly distribute the onion, broccoli and mushrooms over the cheese layer
Mix the eggs and spices together in a bowl
Add milk, half and half and mix well
Pour over the vegetables and cheeses
Cut cherry tomatoes in halve and place, cut side up in a ring on the top
Sprinkle an additional 1 tablespoon of Romano over top
Bake for 15 minutes
Turn down to 300 degrees and bake an additional 45-55 minutes
