Title:
Tortellini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large bag tri-colored tortellini
1 c. diced Capicola (Italian ham)
1 c. diced (FRESH) mozzarella
1/2 c. cold cooked green peas
1/2 c. grated Parmesan or Romano cheese
1 small bottle chilled zesty Italian dressing

Directions:
Cook, then chill the tortellini for about 2 hours. In a large bowl, add tortellini, Capicola, Mozzarella, peas, and italian salad dressing and toss gently, but well. Sprinkle grated cheese as desired and serve cold.
