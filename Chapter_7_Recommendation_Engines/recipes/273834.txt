Title:
Lemon-Poppy Seed Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6639125.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
3 tablespoons frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
2 teaspoons poppy seeds
1 pinch salt
1 slice lemon

Directions:
Combine sour cream, lemonade concentrate, poppy seeds, and salt in a bowl; mix to combine. Garnish with lemon slice.

Submitter: 
Bibi
Description: 
This easy, creamy, fresh-tasting dip will give your fresh fruit tray some extra zip. Try it with any combination of fresh fruit. I especially like it with mangos and bananas.
