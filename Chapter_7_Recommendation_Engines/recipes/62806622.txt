Title:
Feta Cheese Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/2 cup green Spanish olives, sliced
1/3 cup red onion, chopped
1/3 cup mushrooms, chopped
1/4 cup chicken broth
2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
2 teaspoons dried oregano
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat the grill for medium high heat.
In a large bowl, combine turkey, feta cheese, onions, mushrooms, garlic, broth, olives, oregano, and pepper. Mix together, and form into patties.
Lightly oil the grate. Place patties on the grill.  Cook for 10 to 12 minutes, turning halfway through.

These delicious turkey burgers are hearty and moist. Lean turkey mixed with green olives mushrooms, onion, fresh garlic and feta cheese. Grill and serve on your favorite whole wheat bun!Modified from: Feta Cheese Turkey Burgers
