Title:
easy and elegant chocolate dipped  treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 sleeve buttery crackers (like Ritz)
1 cup creamy peanut butter
2 cups chocolate melting wafers

Directions:
Spread peanut butter on half of crackers, topping with other half to make sandwiches. Melt chocolate wafers in microwave or double boiler until smooth and dip sandwiched crackers in. Dip them in all the way or half way, which ever you prefer. Set dipped crackers on waxed paper lined tray and chill until chocolate is set. Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Rebecca
Description: 
These are so simple, yet sooooo good! Who would have thought that just three simple ingredients would produce something fit for company!
