Title:
Cow Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb stir fry beef
1 onion chopped or scallions
pepper, fish sauce, soy sauce
3 cups rice, cooked with beef bouillion

Directions:
cook rice with bouillion.  saute onion in oil.  push onion aside and cook eggs. remove onions and eggs.  cook beef on high seasoning with pepper.  add cooked rice, onion, eggs with fish sauce and soy sauce
