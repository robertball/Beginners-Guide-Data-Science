Title:
Cheese Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Mozzarella cheese
1 egg
1/4 cup Almond flour + 1/4 cup for coating
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mix Mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg and Almond flour.
Roll into 4 to 6 balls and flatten into patties.
Mix 1/4 cup Almond flour with extra Parmesan and dip each patty into mixture.
Place in freezer for 10 minutes .
Bake  at 425 degrees for 3-5 minutes then flip patties and cook another 3-5 minutes until top begins to brown and crisp.
