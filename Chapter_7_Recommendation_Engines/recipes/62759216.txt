Title:
CLASSIC LASAGNA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 lasagna noodles
2 Tbsp.olive oil
1 onion,chopped
2 garlic cloves,minced
1 cup chopped mushrooms
1 1/2 lb.hamberger
700 ml.pasta sauce
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/3 cup water
1 tsp.oregano
1/2 tsp.Italian spice
1/2 tsp.chilis if desired
500g.Ricotta cheese
1 pkg.frozen spinach,remove all liquid
1 tsp.oregano
1/2 cup shredded mozzerella

Directions:
Cook lasagna.
In sauce pan heat oil.cook onion,garlic and mushrooms till tender.Add beef,cook until browned,drain fat.
Stir in sauce, water, parsley,and spices.Simmer 10-15 min.
In bowl combine ricotta with spinach and oregano.
In 9x13 pan spread 1/4 of sauce on bottom,add 4 lasagna noodles.Add another 1/4 of sauce mixture and top with 4 lasagna noodles.Spread cheese mixture on top,add last 4 noodles.Cover with remaining sauce.Sprinkle cheese on top.Cover and bake at 350 for 30-35 mins.Let stand 10 min.before cutting.
