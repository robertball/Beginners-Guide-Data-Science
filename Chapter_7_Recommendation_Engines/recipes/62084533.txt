Title:
Mandarin Orange Jello Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Jello-
2 (15oz) Cans of Mandarin Oranges drainded
4 (.3oz) Orange Jello Mixes made by instructions on the box DO NOT PUT INTO FRIDGE WITHOUT PUTTING ORANGES IN!!
Topping
1 (3.9oz) Instant Jello Vanilla pudding made by instructions on the box
1 container cool whip

Directions:
Make the jello according to manufacters instructions, take the drained cans of oranges and pour into the jello. Pour into the bowl you will be serving in. Place in the frig for about 2-3 hours covered with plastic wrap. Then make the Jello pudding and place into the fridge.. When the mold is done take it out as well as the pudding and cool whip, mix the cool whip and pudding together and serve!

Submitter: 
Love2c0ok
Description: 
I got this recipe from my aunt. It is so yummy with or without the topping! You can even switch it up and put some crushed or cubed pinapples and cherries! Use any flavor jello! There's so many possibilities!!!
