Title:
Rice and Sausage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C uncooked minute rice
1-1/2 lbs Sausage
1 Large Onion Diced
1 Green Pepper Diced
1 C Diced Celery
2 Pkg Lipton Instant Chicken Noodle Soup
4-1/2 C Boiling Water
1/4 C Slivered Almonds
1 Small Can Sliced Mushrooms

Directions:
Brown sausage and set aside.
Pour off grease, saving a small amount to saute onions, peppers and celery.
Saute vegetables until just tender.
Add sausage back to sauteed vegetables, add uncooked rice and set aside.
Boil water and add packages of noodle soup, do not salt water.
Cook 7 minutes.
in a 9x13 inch greased pan add rice, sausage and vegetable mix with noodle soup and mix to distribute ingredients.
Add mushrooms and almonds and mix.
Bake the casserole at 350* for 45 minutes.
