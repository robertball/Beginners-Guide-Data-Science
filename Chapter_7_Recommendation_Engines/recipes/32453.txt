Title:
Lemonade Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/840771.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can frozen pink lemonade concentrate
1 (10 ounce) jar maraschino cherries
2 (6 ounce) cans frozen pink lemonade concentrate
1 (2 liter) bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
1 (750 milliliter) bottle red wine
1 orange, sliced
1 lemon, sliced

Directions:
In a pitcher, mix together 1 can lemonade with 1 can of water. Pour mixture into an ice cube tray, and place a cherry in each cube. Freeze until solid.
In a punch bowl, combine 2 cans lemonade concentrate, lemon-lime soda and red wine. Stir in lemonade ice cubes, and garnish with maraschino cherries, orange slices and lemon slices.

Submitter: 
Baggy
Description: 
Great and simple punch for any occasion, using pink lemonade and wine.  Not too sweet.
