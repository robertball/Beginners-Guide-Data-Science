Title:
Buttermilk Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups all-purpose flour
2 tbsp brown sugar
1.5 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1.5 cups buttermilk
2 eggs
0.25 cup better

Directions:
Combine all dry ingredients
Fold in wet ingreduents, do not overmix
Pour batter onto pan
Flip after bubbles begin to form
