Title:
Cream of Something Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tsp minced garlic
2 small onions, diced
2 cups main ingredient (mushrooms, celery. chicken, etc.), chopped
1 cup butter
1 cup flour
4 cups milk
3 cups vegetable or chicken broth
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute garlic, onion and main ingredient, set aside. Melt butter over medium heat; whisk in flour. Cook 2 minutes. Add milk and broth, stir. Add garlic, onion and main ingredient. Bring to boil; reduce heat and simmer 10-15 minutes or until desired consistency is reached. Stir often. Season with salt and pepper. Let cool completely; pour by 1 1/2 cups into zip-loc bags and freeze.
