Title:
Fireweed Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1½ c. tightly packed, clean fireweed blossoms
2¼ c. water
2 tbsp. lemon juice
3½ c. sugar
3 oz. liquid pectin

Directions:
Boil blossoms until gray in color.  Strain until liquid is clear.  Boil 1¾ cups of liquid, lemon juice, and sugar until it reaches a rolling boil.  Add the pectin and boil for another minute.  Pour into hot jelly jars and seal.

Submitter: 
Peggy McCormack
Description: 
Make your own jelly from fireweed wildflowers!
