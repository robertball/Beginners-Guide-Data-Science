Title:
Anne-chiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
package of 12 frozen beef burritos
1 large can enchilada sauce
1/2 finely diced onion
2 cups shredded cheese
2 green onions chopped

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Put 1/2 cup enchilada sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 pan.
Lay out all the burritos in the pan.
Sprinkle finely diced onion on the burritos.
Pour the enchilada sauce on top of the burritos.
Cover with foil.  Bake for 45 minutes.
Remove from oven.  Add the cheese and bake 15 more minutes uncovered.. or until cheese is melted.
When ready to serve, sprinkle green onion over pan of Anne-chiladas.

Submitter: 
Anne Carsten Christensen
Description: 
This is my 'cheater' recipe for enchiladas.  My son calls them Anne-chiladas.  His friends come over when they know I am making them.  It is a big hit with my family and their friends.  A quick go-to dish during the high school football season.
