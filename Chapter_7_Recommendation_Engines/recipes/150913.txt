Title:
Red Snapper (Crown Snapper) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980264.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Crown Royal® blended Canadian whisky
1/4 ounce amaretto liqueur
6 ounces cranberry juice
1 orange peel

Directions:
Pour Crown Royal(R) blended Canadian whisky, amaretto liqueur, and cranberry juice into ice-filled highball glass and stir; garnish with orange peel to serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Classic cocktails need the authentic taste of Crown Royal® blended Canadian whisky, with a delicately smooth, creamy taste and long-lasting finish.
