Title:
Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons butter
1 Tablespoon Olive oil
1 Cup Carrots, coarsely chopped
1 Cup Celery, chopped
2 Cups Onions, coarsely chopped
1 Head Garlic, minced
2 Cups Green Cabbage, shredded finely
10 Cups Water
2 Cups split green peas
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried tyme
1 teaspoon dried marjoram
1/2 teaspoon dried summer savory
1/4 teaspoon tried sage
1/8 teaspoon dried tarragon
1/2 teaspoon celery seed
1/4 teaspoon coriander
1 teaspoon sea salt
Ground Black pepper to taste
1 Vegetable Bullion cube
5 Tablespoons fresh chopped parsley

Directions:
In large soup pot melt butter, add oil, and saute onions until translucent.
Add carrots, celery, garlic and cabbage and saute, frequently stir for several minutes,
Add Water, split green peas, seasonings, and bullion cube.
Cover and simmer for 90 minutes, stirring frequently.
Stir in Parsley and adjust seasonings to taste.
Very hardy filling soup great with a fresh loaf of bread.

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Very healthy hardy soup great on a cold night.  Freezes well in air tight container.
