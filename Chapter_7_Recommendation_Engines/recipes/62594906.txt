Title:
Lightened Peanut Butter Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/556187.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups mashed ripe banana (3 medium)
1/3 cup plain yogurt
1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
3 tablespoons melted butter
2 large eggs
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1/4 cup ground flaxseed
3/4 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/8 tsp. ground allspice
Cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Combine first five ingredients in a large bowl, beat with mixer on medium speed until combined. Add granualted and brown sugar, beat until well blended.
In a small bowl whisk together, flour, flaxseed, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and allspice.
Add flour mixture to banana mixture and beat on medium just until blended.
Pour batter into a 9 X 5 inch loaf pan that has been coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 350F. for 65 min. or until wooden pick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Cool in pan for 10 min.  Remove bread from pan and cool completely on a wire rack.

Submitter: 
Denise
Description: 
Many of us are searching for a lighter version of recipes that we love.  For all of the peanut butter and banana bread lovers, this recipe combines both peanut butter and bananas into a very moist bread, that is a bit lighter in fat and calories.
