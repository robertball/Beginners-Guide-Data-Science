Title:
Basil Bunt with Balsamic Strawberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box white cake mix
1 package instant vanilla pudding
1/2 cup vegie oil
4 eggs
1 cup sour cream
2 tbsp fresh basil, finely chopped
1 pint fresh strawberries, quartered
2/3 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a bunt pan generously. In a large bowl combine the oil, eggs and sour cream until blended.  Add the cake mix, basil and instant pudding, stir to combine. pour batter into bunt pan and bake 40-45 minutes. Remove from oven and let cool in bunt pan for 20 minutes before inverting onto rack to cool completely.
In a small sauce pan combine the balsamic vinegar and sugar. Bring to a boil and let simmer for 5-6 minutes.  Remove from heat and let cool to room temp.
Toss the fresh strawberries wth the balsamic syrup just before serving.  Spoon over the bunt and garnish with mint.
