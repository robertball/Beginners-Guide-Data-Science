Title:
Texas Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can black-eyed peas or pinto beans, rinsed
1 can black beans, rinsed
1 can shoepeg corn, drained
1/2 c. chopped red onion
1/2 c. yellow, red, or green peppers, diced
1 can diced tomatoes
dressing:  1/2 c. cider vinegar; 1/2 c. sugar; 1/4 c. olive oil

Directions:
Mix first 6 ingredients.
Mix dressing ingredients and heat til sugar is dissolved.
Add tabasco sauce to taste.
Allow to cool.
Pour over veggies and marinate several hours in frig.
Pour some dressing off before serving.
Dip with scoops.
