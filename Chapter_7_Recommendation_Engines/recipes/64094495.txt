Title:
Chicken with Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast halves, boneless & skinless
2 Tbsp oil
1 cup walnut halves
1 red pepper, 1/2 inch cubes
1 green pepper, 1/2 inch cubes
2 Tbsp bean sauce
2 Tbsp sugar
1/4 cup chicken stock
1 Tbsp sherry

Directions:
Cut chicken into strips.  Stir fry chicken in oil at 400 degrees F and remove.
Stir fry walnuts in oil at 324 degrees F and set aside.
Stir fry red and green pepper cubes in oil at 350 degrees F for 1 minute and remove.
Reheat 2 Tbsp oil in wok to 300 degrees F.  Add bean sauce.  Stir 3 minutes.  Add sugar.  Stir 1 minute, then add stock and sherry.  Stir until brown.
Turn up heat to 425 degrees F.  Add chicken and peppers.  Stir fry quickly for one minute and remove to platter.  Sprinkle walnuts over chicken and serve.
