Title:
Eggless Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups of flour
2 cups of sugar
2 t baking soda
1 t salt
1/2 cup cocoa
3/4 cup oil
1 t vanilla
2 t vinegar
2 cups water

Directions:
Mix the dry ingredients and tehn add the wet ones, and mix until smooth.  With a mixer, beat for about a minute.  Prepare pan by greasing and flouring lightly.  Pour into 24 cupcake pans, two 9-inch pans, or one 9X11 -inch pan.  Bake at 350 degree for approxiamately 20 - 25 minutes (cupcakes) and 30 minutes for cake.
