Title:
Barley and Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 scallions
4 cloves chopped garlic
1 T finely chopped ginger
2 T canola oil
2 T tomato paste
1 1/2 T curry powder
1 13.5-oz. can coconut milk
1 cup dried red lentils
1 cup pearl barley
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 cup plain yogurt

Directions:
Cook sliced scallions (white parts only; reserve green parts), garlic, and ginger in oil in a large pot over medium heat, stirring frequently, until soft, 3-4 minutes.
Add tomato paste and curry powder.
Cook, stirring, until tomato paste darkens, 1-2 minutes.
Add coconut milk, lentils, and barley, 4 cups water, and salt.
Simmer, stirring occasionally, until lentils and barley are tender, 35-45 minutes. (Add water if needed to loosen.)
Top with reserved scallion greens and yogurt.
