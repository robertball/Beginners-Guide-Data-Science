Title:
Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 load French Bread
1/2 C. Butter (1/2 C. reconstituted Butter Buds may be used for heart healthly alternative.)
1 tsp. Margarine
1 crushed garlic clove (may substitute 1/4 tsp. minced garlic)
1/4 tsp. pepper
3 T. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°. Cream butter, add garlic and rest of ingredients. Spread generously between slices of bread. Sprinkle top with a few drops of water. Bake in foil with top open for 10-15 minutes.
