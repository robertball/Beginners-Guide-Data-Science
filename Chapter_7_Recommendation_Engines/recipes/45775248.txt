Title:
London Broil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/303259.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb. london broil
2 Tbs. olive oil
1/2 cup zesty italian dressing
1/4 cup basalmic vinigar
1/4 cup wishtershire sauce
1 Tbs minced garlic
1 tsp onion powder
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a Ziploc baggie mix all ingredients, close baggie and shake well. Add London Broil and marinade for 2 days in the refrigerator. Remove London Broil from baggie and discard. Grill London Broil for 6 minutes on each side. Let rest for 5 minutes before cutting. Cut against the grain in very thin strip, less than 1/8 in thick if possible. Steak will be medium rare.
