Title:
Pizza Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can refrigerated crescent roll dough
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
24 slices pepperoni
Italina seasoning to taste
1 (14 ounce) can pizza sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
Soak toothpicks in cold water for 15 minutes
On a large baking sheet, pinch the 8 crescent roll dough triangles into 4 rectangles. Chop pepperoni in a food processor, pulse the cheese and a little sauce.Layer each rectangle with 6 slices of pepperoni and even amounts of mozzarella cheese. Roll tightly lengthwise , secure log with toothpicks and place in the freexer for 20 minutes. Then slice each into 4 or more pieces with a very sharp knife. Brush the rolls with an eggyolk wash.
Bake in the preheated oven on their side on a sheet cover with cooking spray until golden brown, about 12 minutes. Serve with pizza sauce for dipping.
