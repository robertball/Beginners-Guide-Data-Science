Title:
Homemade Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 apples (or cores and peelings if you dehydrated or made applesauce)
water to cover apples
1/4 cup sugar
1 T cinnamon
1 T allspice

Directions:
1. Put into stock pot quartered apples (no need to peel or core). Or use the cores and peelings from dehydrating or the scraps of making applesauce.
2. cover apples with water then add a little bit more
3. add sugar, cinnamon and allspice and stir
4. boil 1 hour uncovered
5. cover simmer for 2 hours
6. strain 2 times, the second time with a cheese cloth
7. let cool and then refrigerateor freeze for later use
