Title:
Peppered Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) pork loin roast*
4 tablespoons pepper blend seasoning
3-4 garlic cloves
3 tsp onion powder
3 tsp sea salt

Directions:
Rub all surfaces of roast(s) with seasoned pepper, garlic, onion powder and sea salt. Place pork in shallow roasting pan and roast in 350 degrees F. oven for 1 to 2 hours, until internal temperature (measured with a meat thermometer) reads 150 to 155 degrees F. Remove from oven and allow roast to rest for 10 minutes. Slice and serve.

Submitter: 
Hank Blakely
