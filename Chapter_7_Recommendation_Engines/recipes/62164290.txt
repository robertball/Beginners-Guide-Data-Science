Title:
Honey Ginger Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1 - 1 1/2 pounds pork tenderloins
1/2 cup duck sauce
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup honey
2 tbsps. grated fresh ginger
4 cloves minced garlic
1/4 spanish onion, chopped

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients in a bowl except for the pork.  Place tenderloins in a large plastic sealable bag, pour contents of bowl over the pork into the bag.  Seal and marinate overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Take tenderloins out of bag and place in a baking dish and pour the marinade over the pork.  Cook 45 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 155 degrees.

Submitter: 
MARIACOZ
Description: 
An easy marinade makes this pork tenderloin simple and delicious.
