Title:
Aunt Lee's Italian spaghetti sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground beef
2-3 Garlic cloves finely chopped
6 Stalks of celery chopped
1 large onion chopped
1 large green pepper chopped
1 can of tomato soup
2 cans of tomato sauce (1 large can)
1 cup fresh mushrooms (you can substitute canned if you like
1 Tblspn Worcestershire sauce
1 Tblspn Brown sugar
1 Tsp paprika
3 Tsp Chilli powder

Directions:
Start browning the hamburger, simmer with the onion, mushroom, worcestershire sauce,lgreen pepper, garlic and brown sugar and spices.  When vegetables are starting to get tender, about 15-20 minutes, add the tomato soup and tomato sauce and simmer 2-3 hours until the vegetables are tender.
Serve over long spaghetti noodles.
