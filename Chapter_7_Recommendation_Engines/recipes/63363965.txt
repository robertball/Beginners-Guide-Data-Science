Title:
Fancy Schmancy Smoked Salmon and Bagels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Bagels
12 Oz Cream Cheese
12 Oz Smoked Salmon
1 Small Red Onion, finely sliced
1/4 cup Capers
Dijion Mustard

Directions:
Bagels can be toasted or not.  Spread cream cheese on bagel, top with smoked salmon, then a slice or two of red onion, a few capers and a dollop of dijion mustard.
For a party, arrange bagels and toppings on a nice serving tray and use nice bowls to serve the cream cheese and dijion mustard.

Submitter: 
ltlmsmfft
Description: 
This is not your ordainary bagel with cream cheese.  This is a great no cook dish to bring to parties or to serve for a holiday brunch.
