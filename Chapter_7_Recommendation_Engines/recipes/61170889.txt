Title:
Garlic Shrimp Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound uncooked linguine
1/3 tablespoon butter
1/2 teaspoon EVOO
3 tablespoons white wine or chicken stock
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 shallot minced
1/2 teaspoon corn starch
1 tablespoon tomato sauce
salt and pepper to taste
2 teaspoons grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a medium saucepan, melt butter &EVOO over medium low heat; add wine, cheese, garlic, shallot, parsley and salt and pepper to taste. Simmer over low heat for 3 to 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
Dry out shrimp.  Grill/pan fry/whatever.
Increase heat to medium high and add cornstarch
Add shrimp to skillet; cook for about 3 to 4 minutes or until shrimp begins to turn pink. Do not overcook.
Divide pasta into portions and spoon sauce on top; garnish with Parmesan cheese and fresh parsley, if desired.
