Title:
Spaghetti in Italian Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Italian sweet sausages
2 onions, chopped
4 cloves garlic, crushed or minced
1/8 cup olive oil
14 medium tomatoes, peeled and quartered
1/2 cup green onions, minced
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon oregano
1/4 teaspoon rosemary
1/2 cup dry red wine
1 pound spaghetti, cooked and drained

Directions:
In large skillet, brown sausages with onions and garlic in oil until tender. Add tomatoes and all remaining ingredients except spaghetti. Bring to boil; reduce to simmer and cook 1 hour. Remove sausages and allow sauce to cool slightly. Slice sausages and set aside. In blender or food processor, puree sauce. Return to pan with sausages. Bring back to boil and simmer 5 minutes. Serve over spaghetti.
