Title:
Sam's German Chocolate Caramel bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg caramels
1/3 Cup evaporated milk
1 Pkg German chocolate cake mix
3/4 C soft butter
1/3 C evaporated milk
1 C chopped nuts
1 C chocolate chips

Directions:
Separate 1 cup of dry ingredients aside.
Melt the caramels into 1/3 cup evaporated milk and let stand.
Mix cake mix, butter, evaporated milk and chopped nuts and spread into greased and floured 9x13 pan.
Bake for 6 minutes @ 350.
remove and sprinkle chocolate chips over the top, then spread caramel mixture on top.  Sprinkle remaining dry mixture over the top and bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
Remove and cool, serve.
