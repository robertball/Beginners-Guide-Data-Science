Title:
Cassava Cake #2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags grated cassava (found in the frozen section of Asian market)
1 (16oz) bottle or macapuno strings (optional)
1 (14oz) can coconut milk
1 (14oz) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (12oz) can evaporated milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 pinch fresh lemon rind
2 eggs

Directions:
Mix coconut, evaporated and sweetened condensed milk throughtly in a large bowl. Set aside 1 cup for topping.
Mix in cassava. macapuno, vanilla and lemon rind.
Pour mixture into 9 x 13 inch baking pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes (until top is firm)
Mix remaining 1 cup of liquid mixture with 2 eggs for topping.
Pour topping over cake after the top of the cake is firm. Cook an additional 20 minutes.
Let cake cool throughly before cutting, and serving.
