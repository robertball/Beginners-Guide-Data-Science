Title:
Hickory Flavored Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean 93% ground turkey
1 dash salt
1 tsp liquid hickory flavored sauce
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp parsley
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp cumin powder
4 tbsp liquid egg substitute

Directions:
Thaw turkey, mix in egg substitute, add spices and hickory seasoning.
Form into patties, and grill over hot coals, or indoor grill.

Submitter: 
Irene Padilla Lopez
Description: 
Great recipe--very tasty! I added grated carrots for a little extra; could also use grated zucchini. I'm going to try this with low-sodium soy sauce, barbeque sauce, pizza sauce, taco sauce or nutmeg and feta instead of the hickory flavor, too. My family loves a variety of cuisines.
