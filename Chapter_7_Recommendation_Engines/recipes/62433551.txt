Title:
Johnny Cake (cornbread) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cornmeal
2 cups flour
4 tsp. baking powder
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup margarine
1/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk

Directions:
Blend dry ingredients, cut margarine in and add wet ingredients. Stir.
Bake at 425 for 25 minutes or until lightly brown.
