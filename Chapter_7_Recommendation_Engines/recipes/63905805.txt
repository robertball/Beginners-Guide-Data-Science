Title:
Lemon bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pgk lemon jello
2/3 C hot water
2 lemons (juice & zest the rind)
1 C sugar
1 can evaporated milk (put in freezer 2 hrs ahead
1 1/2 C graham cracker crumbs
1/4 C sugar
1/2 C melted butter

Directions:
1.  Mix water & jello in bowl
2.  Refrigerate first as it starats to set up
Remover from Refrigerator
3.  Add lemon juice & rind and evaporated milk
Whip until stiff
4. Add sugar & beat
Mix & press in dish - save a little for top
Bake @ 375 for 8 min
