Title:
Hot Dog Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 head fine grated cabbage
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1 tbls sugar
1 capful of apple cider vinegar
1 heaping tbls of mayonnaise

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and cover. Place in fridge for 1-2 hrs minimum to blend flavors. Make ahead and take to picnics or eat it for lunch by itself!

Submitter: 
KR
Description: 
This slaw is sweeter than most and I prefer the cabbage to be grated very fine. Let it set in the fridge to blend flavors (about 2 hrs).
