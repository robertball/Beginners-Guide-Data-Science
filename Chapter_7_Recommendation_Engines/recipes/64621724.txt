Title:
Chocolate Fudge Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Duncan Hines Butter Fudge Cake Mix
2 eggs
1/2 cup Canola oil
1 cup milk chocolate chips
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Do not preheat yet.
Mix cake mix, eggs, and vegetable oil together in a bowl until well mixed.  Let rest in refrigerator one hour.  Roll cookie dough into 1 inch balls and arrange on a baking sheet. (about 28-36)
Bake in the preheated oven at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).until lightly browned around the edges, 7 to 9 minutes. Cool on the baking sheet 2 to 3 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool.
For filling, put chocolate chips in a double boiler over simmering water. Stir in milk and vanilla, mix well.  Let set up till stiff, but still spreadable.  Spread between 2 cookies.  Store in sealed container.
