Title:
Homemade Pear Pie from Scratch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1091360.jpg
Ingredients:
Pie Fillings:
- 5 Bosc Pears (Sliced- I leave skin on).
- 1/2 cup of Sugar.
- 1/2 cup of Brown Sugar.
- 1/4- 1/2 cup of Unbleached Flour.
- 1/2 tsp of grated Lemon peel or half Lemon squeezed.
- 1/2 tsp of Cinnamon.
- 1/8 tsp of Salt.
Pie Crust:
- 1 1/2 cup of Unbleached Flour.
- 1/2 tsp Salt.
- 1/2 cup or 1 stick of Unsalted Butter chilled and diced into cubes (best to chill in freezer for 15 minutes after dicing).
- 1/4 cup of Vegetable Shortening chilled.
- 3-4 tbs of Ice Water.
Egg Wash:
- 1 large Egg
- 1 tbs of cold Water.

Directions:
1 prep the dough:
- Recommend dough mixer (your hands will get very tired). If no processor will need to kneed for 15 minutes, until dough forms into a firm ball.
- Place dough in fridge, covered with a moist cloth.
- In a large bowl combine, Flour, Salt, and mix together.
- Add chilled Butter and 1 tbs of Chilled Water at a time (until dough becomes thick and sticky).
- Once dough is done being mixed, you need to use a processor for 15 minutes or kneed dough for 30 minutes.
- Place in refrigerator with a wet towel covering it for at least 30 minutes.
For Pie Fillings:
- In a large bowl combine Pears, Sugar, Brown Sugar, Lemon, Cinnamon, and Salt.
- Mix all ingredients together for 10 minutes by hand, for another 10 minutes add 3 tbs of flour at a time until it becomes thick and glossy.
Making the Pie:
- Preheat oven to 420, lightly spray pie dish with cooking spray.
- Take dough and kneed only folding about 4 times, dip hands in flour to prevent it from sticking, separate into 2 even pieces.
- Flatten one piece to cover the pie dish and place on bottom, with dough hanging off pan.(Keep other piece in fridge with moist towel).
- Add a small layer of flour and spread evenly in pie dish.
- Add the fillings until it is just below the brim of dish.
- Take 2nd piece of dough and roll it out, then cute into thick strips.
- Evenly lay one set of strip going one direction, then a second set going opposite direction.
- Fold over hanging dough to cover the edges from the strips, to make crust
- Press edges of dough down with fingers first, then use back of a fork and press along crust.
- Place in oven on medium rack.
- 15 minutes into baking add the Egg Wash on top of Pie and then finish baking until Golden brown.
- Set pie on cooling rack for 15-20 m
