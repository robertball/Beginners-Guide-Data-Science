Title:
Trishie's Pork Escalopes in a Rosemary infused Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666695.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Pork Tenderloin (5oz)
1 clove Garlic crushed
1/2 TBS Olive Oil
Pinch of dried Rosemary.
2 Cups Heavy Cream
2 tsp of dried Rosemary

Directions:
Cut the Pork into 1/2" slices and pound thin into Escalopes.
Generously brush Pork Escalopes with Olive Oil and rub in crushed Garlic then sprinkle the pinch of Rosemary on top.
Refrigerate for 1 hour
Heat a heavy based frying pan to hot and fry the Pork Escalopes till nicely browned on each side making sure they are cooked through then remove from pan.
Pour the Cream into the same pan stirring to loosen the meat juices from pan and then add the 2 tsp Rosemary and gently simmer for 10 minutes.
Strain the cream sauce to remove the Rosemary and return to pan.
Put the Pork Escalopes into the cream sauce and simmer gently for 5 minutes.
