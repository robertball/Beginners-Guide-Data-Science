Title:
Sloppy Joe Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package 32 ounces hash brown potatoes, thawed
1 can (10 3/4 oz) condensed cheddar cheese soup, undiluted.
1 Egg
2 Pounds Ground Beef
2 TBS Finely Chopped Onion
1 can (15 1/2 oz) Sloppy Joe Mix

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, soup, egg.  Spread into a lightly greased 5 quart slow cooker  Cook onion and meat until no longer pink.  Drain and add Sloppy Joe Mix.  Spoon over potato mixture.  Cover and cook on low for 4-4 1/2 hours or until heated through.
