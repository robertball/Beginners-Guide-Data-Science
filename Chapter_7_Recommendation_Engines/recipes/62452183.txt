Title:
Spanish Patatas Bravas Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/465527.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Teaspoons Smoked Paprika
2 Teaspoons Cumin
1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1 Teaspoon Onion Powder
1 Teaspoon Black Pepper
1 Teaspoon Salt (or to taste)
1/2 Teaspoon Cayenne Pepper (Optional or to taste)

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients together, shake onto french fries or chips.

Submitter: 
Sarah Garland
Description: 
Replicating the taste of patatas bravas from a Spanish Tapas bar; my friend concluded that the key ingredient is the Smoked Paprika. Sure enough, that did it!
