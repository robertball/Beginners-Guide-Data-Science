Title:
Pasta with Smoked Kielbasa Sausage), Peppers and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. smoked kielbasa sausage, cut in 1/2 in. slices
1 – 16 oz package Frozen “Pepper and Onion Stirfry” Mix (Or 1 red bell pepper, seeded and sliced.  1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced and 1 large sweet yellow, Spanish or Vidalia Onion)
1 lb. dried pasta of your choice
1 – Bunch Green Onions, diced
1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup parmesan cheese from a can
1/2 stick butter
1/2 cup olive oil
2 tsp minced garlic
½ tsp. dried red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
1.  Slice and brown sausage in large skillet or sauce pan over medium heat until lightly browned, about 15 minutes.  Remove from skillet and set aside.  Wipe skillet clean with paper towel and return to stovetop.
2. Mean while, cook pasta according to package. Drain in colander and return to pot.
3. While pasta is cooking, add oil and butter to large skillet or sauce pan. Heat over medium heat until hot. Stir in garlic, peppers, onions, salt, black pepper, red pepper flakes and cayenne.  Sautee for 10 - 12 minutes, until onions are translucent and slightly browned.  Add keilbasa, toss and cook for 3-4 minutes and remove from heat.
4. Pour over pasta, add both Parmesan cheeses and toss to coat well
Serves 4
