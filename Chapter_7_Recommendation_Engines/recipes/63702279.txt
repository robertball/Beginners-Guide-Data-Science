Title:
Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
8 oz diced ham
1 tspn garlic powder
1 tspn onion powder
2 tbsp canola oil
1 cup uncooked rice
1/2 cup frozen peas and carrots

Directions:
Cook rice ahead of time and let it cool somewhat.
Beat eggs in bowl.
Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in pan and pour eggs in pan.
Cook eggs and set aside when done.
Heat another tablespoon of oil at medium heat and turn to low once heated.
Fry rice, ham, and frozen vegetables together.
Add garlic powder and onion powder and mix.
Cook at medium heat for about 3 minutes.
Add cooked eggs and mix.
Serve
