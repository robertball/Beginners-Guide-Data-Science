Title:
Ooey Gooey Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/692719.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg cooked pasta noodles ( I use Elbow - big)
3 tbs butter
1 clove fresh minced garlic
2 teaspn flour
1 teasp dried mustard
1 teasp garlic powder
1 teasp worsterchester sauce
salt and pepper to taste
3 cups half and half
1/2 log of velvetta Cheese
1/2 cup any cheese you want
1/2 cup any cheese you want
(I use whatever I have in the fridge)
But normally parm and cheddar

Directions:
Melt butter in sauce pan, add garlic and saute, then add all spices.  Whisk all together and then add half and half and left start to thicken and then add cheeses.  Stir until all melted on med to low heat. Once all melted add to pot of pasta, stir and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Reesa's Fab Kitchen online
Description: 
Yummy Mac and Cheese.  So ooey and gooey my kids go wild over this dish.  It is great with anything, chicken, beef or pork.
