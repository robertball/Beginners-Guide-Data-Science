Title:
Easy Baked Barbeque Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds chicken (whatever pieces you like)-skin removed if you prefer
1 bottle favorite barbeque sauce

Directions:
Put the chicken in a large covered casserole.  Pour the entire bottle of sauce over the chicken.  Turn the chicken to coat completely. Cover and bake in a 350 (F) degree oven for 60 minutes.  Uncover and bake for another 30 minutes.

Description: 
Very easy and delicious.  This dish is elegant enough to serve to company and simple enough to have on a busy day!
