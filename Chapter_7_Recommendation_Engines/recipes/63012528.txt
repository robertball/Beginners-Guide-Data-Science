Title:
Cream Cheese Tuna Balls with Hot Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. Mashed potato flakes
1 tbsp. instant minced onion
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. garlic salt
2 (6 1/2 oz.) cans tuna, drained and flaked
2 (3 oz.) pkg. cream cheese, softened
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 c. finely crushed corn flakes
HOT MUSTARD SAUCE:
1/4 c. dry mustard
2 tbsp. sugar
1/6 tsp. salt
1/4 c. apple cider vinegar
1 egg
1/2 c. mayonnaise

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In large bowl, combine 1 cup potato flakes, onion, salt, and garlic salt.
Add tuna, cream cheese and eggs. Mix in remaining 1/4 cup potato flakes.
Shape tuna mixture into forty two 3/4 inch balls. Roll in cornflake mixture. Place tuna balls on ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until lightly golden brown. Insert toothpicks for serving.
For Hot Mustard Sauce:
In small saucepan, combine all sauce ingredients except mayonnaise. Simmer over low heat, stirring constantly until thickened, about 2 minutes.
Remove from heat and chill for 10 to 15 minutes.
Remove from refrigerator. Stir in mayonnaise.
