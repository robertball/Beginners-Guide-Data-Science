Title:
Gluten-free, Vegan Blueberry muffins with optional lemon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6072778.jpg
Ingredients:
¼ cup roasted quinoa flour
1/8 cup tapioca starch
1/8 tsp baking powder
¼ tsp baking soda
1 tsp lemon zest (optional)
1 tsp vinegar or 1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp extra virgin coconut oil
1/8 cup non-dairy milk (I used coconut, see notes)
1/8 cup maple syrup or honey (see notes)
Frozen blueberries (see directions for more info)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Line a muffin tin with muffin liners/muffin cups.
Add roasted quinoa flour, tapioca flour, baking powder, baking soda and lemon zest to a bowl.
In a separate bowl, mix extra virgin coconut oil, non-dairy milk, maple syrup or honey and vinegar or lemon juice.
Transfer dry ingredients (quinoa flour, baking powder, etc) to bowl with wet ingredients and mix until well incorporated.
Fill each muffin cup 2/3 to 3/4 full with muffin batter and place frozen blueberries on top.
Bake for about 12-17 minutes
Take out of oven and serve!

Submitter: 
Gracker's Kitchen
Description: 
Dark or light colored Blueberry muffins with an optional addition of lemon zest and lemon juice for extra flavor. Personal portioned. Triple or quadruple the recipe portions if you would like to make this for a party or potluck. 

*For light colored muffins use ¼ cup + 1/8 cup gluten-free oat flour and 1/8 cup rice flour instead of roasted quinoa flour and tapioca starch.
