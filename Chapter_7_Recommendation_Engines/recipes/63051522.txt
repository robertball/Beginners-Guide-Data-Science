Title:
Sunday Dinner Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg Yeast
1 tsp Sugar
1/4 C Warm Water
1 C Hot Water
1/2 C Sugar
1/2 C shortening
2 tsp Salt
3 Eggs
4 1/2 C Flour

Directions:
Combine yeast, 1 tsp sugar and warm water.
Allow to dissolve and begin to bubble.
Add beaten eggs to yeast mixture.
Combine 1 c hot water, 1/2 c sugar, shortening and salt.
Add to yeast mixture.
Begin to add flour one cup at a time until dough is slightly sticky and moist.
Mix well and beat in mixer until smooth.
Let rise for about 30 minutes.
Beat down and cover.
Refrigerate overnight.
Roll out and brush with melted butter.
Sprinkle with a very small about of sugar.
Roll up and cut a 1/2 inch thick.
Place on a greased cookie sheet or in muffin tins.
Let rise for three hours.
Bake at 400* for 10 minutes.
