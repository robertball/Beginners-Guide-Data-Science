Title:
Steam Cooked Dumblings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*About 2 1/2 cups flour
*1 egg
*1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
*1 cup water

Directions:
Put your flour into a mound in a mixing bowl
Add your egg and baking powder
Mix a little
Then add your water
Knead until you have a soft dough ( add flour and water as or if needed)
Roll your your dough into 3 or 4 6-8 inch long loafs
Place in a steamer until fully cooked
Cut loafs into 1" pieces
Place in pie dish and cover until served

Submitter: 
Tim Drexler
Description:
