Title:
Tomatoe soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1(29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed chicken broth, undiluted
2 tbsp margarine
2 tbsp white sugar
1 tbsp chopped onion
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups heavy whipping cream

Directions:
In a large stock pot mix tomatoes, chicken broth, butter, sugar, onion and baking soda for 1 hour
In a double boiler heat cream until hot.  You can also do this in a regular saucepan over low heat, but watch it carefully so that the cream does not scorch.
Once heated through add to tomato soup and serve
