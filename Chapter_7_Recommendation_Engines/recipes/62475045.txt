Title:
Dor's Old Fashioned Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
4 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
3 cups milk
3 eggs
1 T. vanilla
1 stick butter, melted

Directions:
Have eggs and milk at room temperature.
In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, sugar and cinnamon. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk and butter. Mix lightly. add egg. Mix until smooth.
Let stand for l0 minutes to start rising..
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
