Title:
Best Braised Balsamic Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/836962.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 3 1/2 pound chuck roast
1- 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
1- large onion sliced
2 - teaspoons thyme leaves
2 - Tablespoons red wine vinegar
2- Tablespoons balsamic vinegar
2- teaspoons hot sauce or to taste
salt and pepper to taste
3- tablespoons olive oil for browning
1- C all purpose flour for dredging

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350. Heat 3 Tablespoons olive oil on medium high on the stove in a cast iron braising pot. Take the roast and season with salt and pepper, the dredge in all purpose flour, then put it in the pot to brown it about 5 minutes each side.
Slice the onion and scatter over the top of the meat. Then take the red wine vinegar and pour it around the sides of the meat. Pour 1/2 of the balsamic vinegar over the top of the meat and the other 1/2 around the sides. take the crushed tomatoes and pour 1/2 around the sides and the other 1/2 over the meat. Sprinkle the Thyme leaves over the top of the meat then the hot sauce. Put the cast iron lid on and cook at 350Â° for 3 1/2 hours until the meat comes off with a fork.
