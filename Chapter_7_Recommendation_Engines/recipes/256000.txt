Title:
Cinnameg Chocolate Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4113051.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups chocolate ice cream
1/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon Dutch-processed cocoa powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Combine chocolate ice cream, milk, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, nutmeg, and cinnamon in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
A thick milkshake that blends fall spices and chocolate together. Top with whipped cream and an extra sprinkle of cinnamon on top.
