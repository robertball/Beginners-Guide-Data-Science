Title:
Ham, Potato & Carrot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs sliced ham
1 onion diced
1 cup peeled sliced carrots
3 cups sliced potatoes
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 1/4 tsp flour
1 cup milk
sprinkle of salt
sprinkle of pepper

Directions:
Brown ham and onion in a greased frying pan.
Remove from pan, add flour, soup and milk, heating until it boils.
Arrange carrots, potatoes, ham, onion, salt & pepper in a 9x13 pan.
Pour sauce over top.
Cover and bake 350' for 1 hour.
Uncover for 15 minuets more to brown potatoes.

Submitter: 
Rachelle Shockey
Description: 
An easy homecooked casserole with a few simple ingredients.
