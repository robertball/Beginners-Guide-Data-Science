Title:
Baked Veggie Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole zucchini
1 whole yellow squash
1 whole red bell pepper
1 whole egg, beaten
2 tbsp. flour
1 tsp. ranch dressing mix, or to taste
canola oil spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a baking tray.
Cut green and yellow squash in half and scoop out seeds.  Cut all vegetables into julienne strips.
Combine flour and ranch seasoning in a bag or bowl.
Dip vegetable sticks in beaten egg and then toss in flour mixture.  Place on a baking sheet and spray generously with oil.
