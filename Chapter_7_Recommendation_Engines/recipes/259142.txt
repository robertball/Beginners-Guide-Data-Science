Title:
Absolutely Fabulous Shrimp Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4993743.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup reduced-calorie mayonnaise
1 cup chopped cooked shrimp meat
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon garlic salt
2 (12 ounce) packages sourdough English muffins, split

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
Combine mayonnaise, shrimp, Cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, and garlic salt in a large bowl. Spread mixture over 1 side of each English muffin. Arrange muffins into a 1 layer on the baking sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven until brown and bubbly, about 20 minutes.
