Title:
Easy Cake Mix Lemon Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg yellow cake mix (2 layer size)
1 pkg (3 oz) instant lemon pudding mix
1/2 c oil
1 c milk
4 eggs
Dash mace
Confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients but sugar and beat with an electric mixer 2 minutes. Bake in a greased 10-inch tube pan 1 hour at 350F. When coool sprinkle with confectioners' sugar.
