Title:
Lemon Squares Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1/2 cup butter
2. 1 1/3 cups flour
3. 1/4 cup sugar
4. 2 eggs
5. 1/2 cup sugar
6. 2 tablespoons flour
7. 1/4 tea baking powder
8. 3 tablespoons lemon juice
9. powdered sugar to sprinkle on top of bars

Directions:
Crust : mix 1. 2. 3. together and pat down in 8"x8" pan ( ungreased)...bake 350* 15-20 min...while baking make the filling : mix in small bowl 4. 5. 6. 7. 8.
When crust is done pour the filling on top and spread evenly - bake 18-20 min  cool and then sprinkle powder sugar on top cut into small squares

a nice tart lemon flavor...nice to double this recipe
