Title:
Jennifer's Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5454065.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (11 ounce) can shoepeg corn, drained
1 (8 ounce) package crumbled feta cheese
4 green onions, chopped
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup cider vinegar
2 tablespoons white sugar

Directions:
Mix black beans, corn, feta cheese, and green onions together in a bowl.
Whisk olive oil, vinegar, and sugar together in a separate bowl until smooth; drizzle over corn mixture and toss to coat.
Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until flavors blend, at least 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Nancy Watkins King
Description: 
I had this on the fourth of July for the first time. It's my cousin's recipe. I almost ate the whole bowl. So light and fresh. Serve with chips. Tostitos® Scoops!® work great!
