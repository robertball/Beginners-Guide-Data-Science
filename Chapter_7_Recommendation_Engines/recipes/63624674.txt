Title:
Honeydew & Pineapple Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Cubed Pineapple
2 Cups Cubed Honeydew Melon
1 Tablespoon Honey
1 Tablespoon Lemon Juice
Pinch of Salt

Directions:
Using a blender or food processor pulse the fruit until smooth. Mix in the honey, lemon juice, and salt. Pour into ice cube trays or popsicle molds and freeze.

Submitter: 
Angel_1988
Description: 
The pineapple & honeydew pair perfect! The zing of the pineapple plus the kick from the lemon & salt take these melon pops to a whole new level. There were a big hit at my girls night & my toddler can't get enough either!
