Title:
Healthy French toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1125840.jpg
Ingredients:
For French toast 1 egg ,2 teaspoons cinnamon and 1 tablespoon heavy cream also your choice of healthy bread you will need non stick spray. Also 2 tablespoons of organic maple syrup and strawberries to top with .

Directions:
Mix the egg in a small bowl then add the cinnamon whisk until combined then finally add the heavy cream . Next you are going to take your pan and spray with non stick spray place on low heat and dip your bread in the egg mixture then place on your pan cook for 2 min on each side when done top with maple syrup and strawberries enjoy
