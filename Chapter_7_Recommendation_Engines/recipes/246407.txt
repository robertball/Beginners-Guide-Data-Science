Title:
Chipotle Aioli (Spicy) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4507582.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons lime juice
1 1/2 tablespoons chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
1 large clove garlic, minced
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise, lime juice, chipotle peppers, garlic, and salt together in a bowl. Place in refrigerator until chilled, at least 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Miller
Description: 
Great on chicken or tuna sandwiches, or as a dip.
