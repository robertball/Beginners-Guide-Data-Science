Title:
Seven-Up® Cake II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7371719.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups butter
3 cups white sugar
5 eggs
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons lemon extract
3/4 cup lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Directions:
Cream together the butter and sugar for 20 minutes.  Add eggs, one at a time.  Add flour, lemon extract and fold in the 7UP (TM)  soft drink.
Pour into a well-greased 12 cup Bundt pan.  Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 60 to 75 minutes.

This Bundt® cake made with 7-UP® is features only a few ingredients and steps for an easy dessert.
