Title:
Vegam Dreamy Creamy Fudgsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can FULL FAT* coconut milk
1/4 cup +  1 Tb dutch cocoa powder (or normal)
1/4 tsp almond extract (plus 1/4 tsp coconut extract for mound-like fudgesicles)
scant 1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup rasberries**

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in blender on high for 2 minutes (depending on the strength of your blender) or until the mixture has reached the consistency of pudding/mousse
Smooth mixture into disposable cups (or if you want to be fancy, popsicle molds)and place a popsicle stick, plastic spoon or fork inside the center of the cup
Freeze for at least 3 hours
Run HOT water over the cups until the fudgesicle slides out easily- enjoy!

Submitter: 
Myrna Sobernheim
Description: 
The creamiest, dreamiest homemade fudgesicles you have ever tasted- and vegan!  And this recipe isn't JUST for fudgesicles- pour it in an ice cream maker for smooth chocolatey ice cream, whip up with a beater for a velvety mousse, or drizzle it on ice cream or other desserts for a decadent sauce, it
s even a delicious frosting! The possibilities are endless!
