Title:
Bob's Honey Mustard Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup yellow mustard
1/4 cup honey
3 Tablespoons Sweet brown sugar Hictory Sauce
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 teaspoon steak sauce
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves about 1 lb.
1/2 can beer
2 teasoons of salt

Directions:
Preheat the grill for medium heat.
Soak the Chicken in the beer, pat dry, Salt
In a shallow bowl, mix the mustard, honey, mayonnaise, and steak sauce. Sweet brown sugar Hictory BBQ Sauce
Set aside a small amount of the honey mustard sauce for basting, and dip the chicken into the remaining sauce to coat.
Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill chicken over indirect heat for 15 to 20 minutes, turning occasionally, or until juices run clear. Baste occasionally with the reserved sauce during the last 10 minutes. Watch carefully to prevent burning!
