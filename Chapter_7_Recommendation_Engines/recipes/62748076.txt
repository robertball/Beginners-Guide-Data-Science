Title:
White Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/632889.jpg
Ingredients:
2-15 ounce cans of cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
3/4 teaspoon of kosher salt, or to taste
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
A pinch of thyme
3 Tablespoons olive oil
2 Tablespoons lemon juice
1 large clove of garlic, crushed

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a food processor until very smooth, adding a tablespoon or two of hot water if the beans seems disinclined to become creamy.
