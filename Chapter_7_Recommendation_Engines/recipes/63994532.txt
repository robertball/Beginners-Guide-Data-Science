Title:
Sandy's Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 strips bacon
3 Tbs. butter
2 cans diced potatoes, drained
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cups chicken broth
2 cans cream-style corn
2 teaspoons salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper, or to taste
2 cups milk
1/2 cup cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. Fry bacon in a large pot over medium-high heat until crisp, reserving the bacon drippings. Remove bacon to cool.
2. Add butter to skillet, followed by potatoes and onion. Cook until onions are clear, about 5 minutes. Add chicken broth and corn. Crumble bacon back into the pot. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Add milk and cheese to the pot and stir to heat thoroughly.

Description: 
I tweaked a couple of other recipes and came up with this one on a cold, rainy Georgia night. My husband really liked it! It's quick, easy and tasty!
