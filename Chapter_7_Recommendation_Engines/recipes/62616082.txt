Title:
Caramel Peanut Butter Apple Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C peanut butter
4T milk
1/4C caramel ice cream topping

Directions:
Combine all in small saucepan. Heat over low heat, stirring constantly until melted and warm
Microwave directions: In microwavable dish, at med (50%) power, heat all ingred. for 1 min, stir well. Microwave additional min. until melted and smooth

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Can be made in microwave or stovetop. Simple and quick with only 3 ingredients.
