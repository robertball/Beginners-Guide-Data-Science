Title:
Simple Chicken Cordon Bleu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup garlic lemon marinade or italian dressing
2 chicken breast filets
1/2 cup italian seasoned bread crumbs
2 slices of precooked deli ham
1/3 cup grated swiss cheese

Directions:
Marinate chicken for about half an hour, or longer if you like.
Preheat oven to 400.
Remove chicken and discard marinade.
Press chicken into bread crumbs be sure to coat evenly.
Bake chicken in a sprayed glass bake dish for 20 minutes.
Top each with a slice of ham and half the cheese.
Bake and additional 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
*~Lissa~*
Description: 
Easy to make and taste yummy.  I used to fuss over the rolled kind, but the cheese always melted out.  These were no trouble at all!
