Title:
Leftover Chopped Turkey Salad  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7412662.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked turkey meat, chopped
2 stalks celery with leaves, chopped
1/2 small red onion, chopped
1/2 avocado, peeled, pitted, and chopped
1/3 cup mayonnaise, or more to taste
1/8 cup reduced-fat balsamic vinaigrette dressing
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix turkey, celery, red onion, avocado, mayonnaise, balsamic dressing, garlic salt, and pepper together in a medium-sized bowl. Adjust to taste. Cover with plastic wrap and chill in a refrigerator before serving.

Submitter: 
Ann Philbeck
Description: 
A great way to use leftover turkey in salad! Makes a wonderful, wholesome salad for sandwiches or just served over a bed of lettuce!
