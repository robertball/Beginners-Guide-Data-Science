Title:
Low-Fat Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749298.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1/2 cup granulated sugar (or you could do 1/4 c. brown 1/4 c. granulated)
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 cup canned pumpkin pie mix
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 egg, 2 egg whites
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp cloves
1/4 tsp ginger
1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Mix dry ingredients in a small bowl.
In a larger bowl, mix wet ingredients and spices.
Whisk dry ingredients into wet ingredients until combined. Fold in chocolate chips.
Pour into a prepared 9 x 5 bread pan and bake 45-55 minutes

Description: 
A healthier version of a holiday favorite without missing any flavor! Moist and delicious.
