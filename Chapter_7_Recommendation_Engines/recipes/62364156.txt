Title:
Laura's Turkey Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound extra-lean ground turkey breast
1 taco seasoning packet
1/2 bag baby spinach
1/2 head of romaine lettuce
1 onion chopped
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 cup sliced black olives
1/4 cup lowfat ranch dressing
1/4 cup taco sauce
1 (14.5 ounce) package low-fat baked tortilla chips
1 box of cherry tomatoes
1 avocado

Directions:
Place turkey in a large deep skillet and follow taco seasoning packet. Crumble and set aside to cool.
Mix together the veggie ingredients in large salad bowl. Pour ranch/taco sauce mixture and mix in 2 cups broken tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Laura Leh
Description: 
Variation on a turkey taco salad. Taco seasoning added to the meat for flavor.  Baby spinach and romaine lettuce.  For a bit of crunch, tortilla chips are folded into the salad at the last minute.Modified from: Turkey Taco Salad
