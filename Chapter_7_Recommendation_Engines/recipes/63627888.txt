Title:
Asian Shrimp with Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz fettuccini or mafalda pasta
6 cups assorted fresh vegetables, such as broccoli, red peppers, scallions and baby spinach
1 bottle (13.75 oz) teriyaki sauce
1/4 cup peanut butter
1 TBL peeled grated fresh ginger
1 1/2 lbs large shrimp, peeled, deveined

Directions:
1. Cook pasta according to package, adding broccoli during the last 3 minutes of cooking time. Drain, reserving 3/4 cup cooking water.
2. Whisk teriyaki sauce, peanut butter, ginger and 1/2 cup reserved water until smooth. Coat shrimp in 1/4 cup of sauce mixture.
3. Coat large nonstick skillet with cooking spray, heat over medium-high heat. Add peppers and scallions; cook, stirring, until browned, 3 minutes. Add shrimp, cook, stirring, 8 minutes. Add pasta, broccoli, spinach and remaining sauce; cook, stirring, until spinach is wilted, adding remaining water if needed
