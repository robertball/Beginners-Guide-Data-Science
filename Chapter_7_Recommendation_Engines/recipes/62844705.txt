Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# ground beef
Taco seasoning packet
1 can pinto beans
Regular bottle thousand island dressing
2 tomatoes, diced
2 green onions, chopped
1 head lettuce, chopped
1/2# cheddar cheese, shredded
Black olives
1 bag Doritos nacho cheese chips

Directions:
Brown ground beef, season according to directions, let cool.
In large bowl, mix all ingredients except chips.
Serve salad over chips.
