Title:
Gussie's "Wash Day" Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1(7.25 oz)package macaroni and cheese
4 slices bacon, diced
1/2 green pepper, diced
1 medium onion diced
1 can condensed tomato soup
1/4 C katchup
ground pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium pot, boil macaroni according to package directions, about 7 minutes.  Set powdered cheese aside.  Meanwhile, fry bacon over medium heat in a heavy fry pan until crispy. Drain off about 1 tablesppon of fat and saute green pepper and onion in bacon grease with the bacon.  Once tender, turn heat to low and mix in condensed tomato soup and katchup.  Sprinkle in packaged powdered cheese and stir well.  Allow mixture to simmer for 1 minute, peppering as desired.  Mix with cooked, drained macaroni and serve.

Submitter: 
MRSPARKERII
Description: 
This recipe was my great-grandmothers creation for her laundry days when she didnt have time to prepare a complicated meal.  This depression-era recipe isn't fancy, but it's inexpensive, tasty, and satisfying.
