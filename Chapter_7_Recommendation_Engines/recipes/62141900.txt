Title:
Creamy Mussels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds mussels, cleaned, scrubed and debearded
3 shallots, chopped
5 cloves garlic, minced
3 TBS extra virgin olive oil
5 TBS butter, unsalted
1 cup heavy cream, more if desired
1 1/2 cup dry white wine
5 Roma tomatoes, seeded and chopped
1/2 cup basil, chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In large pot, heat the olive oil, add shallots and garlic. Cook for about two minutes. Make sure not to burn.
Add butter, wine and cream. Bring to boil.
Add mussels, cook for 5-10 minutes, or until the mussels all open.
Add tomatoes and basil, and more cream at this stage if desired. Season with salt and pepper. Cook an additional 3 minutes.
Serve with crusty bread and the rest of the wine

Submitter: 
Jackie Fernandez-Gonzalez
Description: 
Very delicious mussels in a creamy wine sauce. Served with crusty bread and the rest of the wine makes this a great meal. *can use chicken stock in place of wine*
