Title:
Steak n' Shake Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. ground beef
1/2 tsp. salt (lowery's season salt)
10 oz. can French onion soup
1 tbsp. chili powder
2 tsp. cumin powder
1/2 tsp. pepper
21 oz. can kidney beans
6 oz. can tomato paste
8 oz. can tomato sauce
2 tsp. Hershey's cocoa
10 oz. Coca Cola

Directions:
Brown and crumble ground beef.  Dust with salt. Add Beef to slow cooker.  Put onion soup through blender for 1 minute; add to beef. Add the rest of the ingredients to slow cooker and stir. Cook on low 6 hr or high for 2 hours.
