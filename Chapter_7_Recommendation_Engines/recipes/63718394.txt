Title:
Big Ray's Brew Pub Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1033093.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup beer
1 cup water
2 Tbsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. seasoned salt
Dash Cayenne pepper
Dash chili powder
I broiler chicken, cut into 6-8 pieces (2.5-3 lbs)
1/4 cup Mayonnaise
1 Tbsp. Dijon Mustard
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. chopped fresh rosemary

Directions:
Mix first 6 ingredients until sugar is dissolved; pour over chicken in sealable bag. Turn pieces over to evenly coat both sides of each piece. Refrigerate 1 hour to over night to marinate.
Meanwhile, mix remaining ingredients.
Heat grill to medium-high heat. Remove chicken from marinade; discard marinade. Braise 5 min each side over direct hear to sear then grill over indirect heat 15 min. on each side or until done (160ÂºF), brushing occasionally with mayo mixture.
