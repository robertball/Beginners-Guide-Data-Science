Title:
Devils Food Cream Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 Devils Food Twinkies
3/4 cup strong hot coffee, slightly sweetened
1/3 cup Kahlua
1/3 cup Hershey's Chocolate Syrup
2 (3 oz) pkg vanilla chocolate pudding
2 cups cold milk
12 oz. Cool Whip, thawed
1 square German's Sweet Chocolate

Directions:
Lay twinkies in the bottom of a 9x13 pan. In a bowl, mix hot coffee, Kahlua, and Hershey's Syrup. Slowly drizzle over twinkies. Mix pudding and milk ... mixture will be thick. Carefully spread pudding over twinkies. Spread Cool Whip over the top. Grate some of the German's Sweet Chocolate over the top. Cover and refrigerate for at least 5 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Sue S.
Description: 
Yet another Twinkie dessert, this time with Devils Food Twinkies, coffee, kahlua, pudding, and whipped cream. Fabulously chocolately and satisfying
