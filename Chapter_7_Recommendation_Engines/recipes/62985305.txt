Title:
Beef Daube Provencal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp. olive oil
12 garlic cloves, crushed
2 lbs. chuck, cut into 2 inch cubes
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 cup red wine
2 cups chopped carrot
1 1/2 cups chopped onion
1/2 cup beef broth
1 tbsp. tomato paste
1 tsp. fresh rosemary, chopped
1 tsp. fresh thyme, chopped
Dash of ground cloves
1 can of diced tomatoes (14.5 oz.)
1 bay leaf
3 cups cooked egg noodles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300.
Heat oil in  Dutch oven over low heat.
Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove garlic and set aside.  Increase heat to medium high and add beef.
Sprinkle with 1/2 tsp. salt and 1/4 tsp. pepper.
Brown well on all sides.
Remove beef from pan and add wine, scraping all brown bits from bottom of pan.
Put beef and garlic back in pan and add remaining ingredients except noodles.
Cover and bake for about 2 1/2 hours.
To prepare in slow cooker, cook for 5 hours on low.
Serve over noodles.
