Title:
Mom H. Pumpkin Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/759662.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C. sugar
2/3 C. pumpkin
1 tsp. lemon juice
3/4 C. flour
2 TBSP cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. salt
3 eggs, beaten 5 minutes
1 C. nuts
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. ginger
Frosting:
1/2 box powdered sugar
4 Tbsp. margarine
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and spread on a jelly roll pan. Bake at 375 for 15 minutes. Let cool in pan 5 minutes. Run knife around edges to make for easier transfer. Turn out on a white towel that has been sprinkled with powdered sugar. Roll up towel and let set while cools. To make frosting, beat all ingredients until smooth. Unroll pumpkin roll while it is still a little warm. Spread frosting on and roll up again. Wrap in foil and place in refrigerator.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a recipe from my mom.  I enjoy this pumpkin roll very much; and I don't especially like pumpkin.  This has won many guests over; and I am often getting requests for the recipe.
