Title:
Aunt Jewel's Chicken Dressing Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast meat
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  milk
1 8 oz. can mushrooms
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
1 (6 ounce) package seasoned cornbread stuffing mix
1/4 C. chopped onion
1/2 C. chopped celery
2 cloves minced garlic

Directions:
Place chicken in a large skillet saute with onion, garlic, celery, and pepper, until juices run clear.
Remove chicken from pan, Cut chicken into bite size pieces Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl mix together cream of chicken soup and cream of celery soup. put part of stuffing mix inbottom of greased casserole dish,tope with chicken,  Fill one empty soup can with milk, and mix milk with soups & mushrooms. Pour mixture over chicken. top with remaing stuffing mix. In a small bowl combine stuffing, and broth; mix together and spoon mixture over casserole.
Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes.
