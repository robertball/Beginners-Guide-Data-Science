Title:
Lawry's California Center Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980746.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 medium onion, chopped
3 1/2 cups cooked, shredded chicken OR turkey
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes in juice
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
1 (1 ounce) package Lawry's速 Taco Spices & Seasonings
1/2 teaspoon Lawry's速 Seasoned Salt
1/4 teaspoon Lawry's速 Garlic Powder With Parsley
12 corn tortillas
2 (2.25 ounce) cans sliced black olives, drained
5 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
In large skillet, heat oil.  Add onion and cook over medium-high heat until tender.  Add chicken, tomatoes, tomato sauce, chiles, Taco Spices & Seasonings, Seasoned Salt and Garlic Powder With Parsley; mix well.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat to low, cover and cook for 15 to 20 minutes.
In greased 13x9x2-inch baking dish, place 4 corn tortillas.  Pour 1/3 of chicken mixture over tortillas, spreading evenly.  Layer with 1/3 of olives and 1/3 of cheese.  Repeat layers 2 times ending with cheese.  Bake, uncovered, in 350 degrees F oven for 30 to 40 minutes.
