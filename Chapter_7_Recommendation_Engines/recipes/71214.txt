Title:
JIF® Peanut Butter Bunny Crisp Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992141.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 (10 ounce) packages marshmallows
2 cups Jif® Creamy Peanut Butter
10 cups crisp rice cereal
CRISCO® No-Stick Cooking Spray
1/2 cup Smucker's® Jelly Beans, or as desired
4 pieces licorice, or as desired
20 gum drops, or as desired
20 decorative candy pieces, or as desired
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
2 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 1/2 cups sifted powdered sugar

Directions:
In a large saucepan, melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows. Stir until completely melted and remove from heat.
Stir in JIF(R) and mix well to incorporate.
Add rice crisp cereal and stir until well coated.
Press into two 9-inch pans, well coated with CRISCO(R) No-Stick Cooking Spray, and allow to cool.
Cut ears and bow tie from one cake. This is done by cutting two football-shaped ears from the top and bottom of one cake layer, leaving a bow-tie-shaped piece in the middle.
Position two ears at top of whole round cake and place bow tie under face.
Apply optional glaze, if desired. Decorate with SMUCKER'S(R) Jelly Beans, marshmallows, licorice and gum drops or any of your favorite cake decorations.

Submitter: 
JIF®
Description: 
Your children will enjoy decorating this bunny cake made with crisp rice cereal, marshmallows, and Jif® Creamy Peanut butter.
