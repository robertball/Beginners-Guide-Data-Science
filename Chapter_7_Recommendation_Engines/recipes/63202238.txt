Title:
Lg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head Iceberg Lettuce
Spinach
2 carrots
1 cucumber
1 bell pepper, any color
deli meat or pepperoni
shredded cheese
1 tomato
Italian & Ranch salad dressing

Directions:
Shred lettuce and spinach into a large bowl (or separate small bowls to serve), add diced or chunked vegetables. Add in meat. Keep the dressings and cheeses separate to be added individually.
