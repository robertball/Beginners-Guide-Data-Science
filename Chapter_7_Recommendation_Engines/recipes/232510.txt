Title:
Easy Feta Chicken Bake from ATHENOS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1012277.jpg
Ingredients:
6 (4 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons lemon juice, divided
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 1/2 cups ATHENOS Traditional Crumbled Feta Cheese
1/4 cup finely chopped red pepper
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Arrange chicken in 13x9-inch baking dish.
Drizzle with 1 Tbsp. of the lemon juice. Season with salt and black pepper. Top with feta cheese; drizzle with remaining 1 Tbsp. lemon juice.
Bake 35 to 40 min. or until chicken is cooked through. Sprinkle with red pepper and parsley.

Submitter: 
ATHENOS
Description: 
Chicken breasts are baked with feta cheese and lemon juice, then sprinkled with fresh parsley and chopped red bell pepper before serving.
