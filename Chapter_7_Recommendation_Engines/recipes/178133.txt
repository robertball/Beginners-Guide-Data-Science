Title:
Green Slime Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/618067.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups spinach
2 cups frozen strawberries
1 banana
2 tablespoons honey
1/2 cup ice

Directions:
Place the spinach in the freezer until frozen, at least 1 hour.
Combine the spinach, strawberries, banana, honey, and ice in a blender. Blend until smooth. Serve immediately.
