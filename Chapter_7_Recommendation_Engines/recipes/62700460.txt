Title:
OREO Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag OREOS
2 Small boxes instant pudding
1 medium size Cool Whip (12oz)
1 Cream Cheese bar

Directions:
Mix cream cheese with pudding.
In same bowl, mix in cool whip.
With casserole dish, add 1/2 bag oreos & layer bottom of dish. Then add bowl of pudding mix, and top off with Oreos.
