Title:
Chops Cooked in Hard Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, sliced
2 pork chops butterfly cut
1 (12 fluid ounce) bottle of hard cider (Woodchuck or Angry Orchard)
2 cubes  vegetable bouillon

Directions:
Arrange onion slices on bottom of slow-cooker. Cut butterfly chops in half and place on top of onions. Pour in hard cider and add bouillon cubes. Cover and cook and low 6 to 8 hours.

Submitter: 
cookbookangie
Description: 
Simple and tasty!  Tender chops slow-cooked in vegetable bouillon, onion, and hard cider sauce.Modified from: Beer Chops I
