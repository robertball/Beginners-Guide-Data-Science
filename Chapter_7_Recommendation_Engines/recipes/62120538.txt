Title:
Bacon and Creamed Cheese Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup bacon bits (the soft kind) or chopped bacon
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 pound whole button mushrooms with stems and insides removed

Directions:
Remove stems and inside of mushrooms with paring knife.
Stir cream cheese in small mixing bowl until smooth.
Stir in bacon bits and garlic powder until well mixed.
Spoon cream cheese mixture into mushroom caps.
Place mushrooms in 9" by 13" baking dish.
Broil 5 inches from heating element for 4 to 6 minutes.
Serve warm.
