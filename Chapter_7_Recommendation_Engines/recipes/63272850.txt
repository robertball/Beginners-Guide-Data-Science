Title:
Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. butter or margarine
1 1/2 c. white sugay
1 c. brown sugar
2 eggs
2 t. vanilla
1 1/2 c. flour
1/2 c. cocoa
1 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt
3 c. oats (quick or regular)
1/2 c. chopped nuts

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat butter and sugars until light and fluffy; blend in eggs and vanilla.
In a separate bowl, stir together flour, cocoa, soda and salt; gradually ad to butter mixture, mixing well.
Stir in oats and nuts. Drop by tablespoons onto a cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees for 11-12 minutes (cookies will be moist in the center)
