Title:
Turkey Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/855113.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbsp dry onion soup mix
1 c water
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp garlic
1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 egg
1 c Italian bread crumbs
1 1/2 lb ground turkey or beef
1 can mushroom soup, condenced
1 can of water
3 tbsp brown gravy mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400'. In a large bowl, mix together dry onion soup with water, pepper, garlic & Worcestershire. Strain & save both liquid & bits. Put back in bowl 1/2 cup broth & bits with egg, ground turkey & bread crumbs, combine thoroghly. Using a 1/2 cup measure, scoop out 8 equal portions of meat mixture, shape into 8 oval patties & place on a cookie sheet. Bake 20 minutes.
In a saucepan, blend remaining broth with mushroom soup, 1 can of water that you use to clean out the soup can & brown gravy mix. Blend until smooth. Heat to boil, stirring constantly. Turn off heat. Turn patties over & pour gravy over meat in pan, cook for 10 more minutes.

Reminds me of the old scool days lunch. YUM! Ground turkey is mixed & then formed into patties which are baked and sevrered with a mushroom gravy.Modified from: Salisbury Steak
