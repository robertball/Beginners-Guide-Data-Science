Title:
Spicy Braised Short Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Spare ribs, Heinz Chili Sauce, Red Wine, Beef Broth, Red Wine Vinegar, Worchestershire, 1 large onion (chopped), Tomato Paste, 5-6 cloves of Garlic (minced), Sriracha, Red Pepper Flakes, Flour, EVOO, Salt, Pepper and Garlic Powder

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
Season the meat with salt and pepper
Pour flour into a ziplock bag with some garlic powder, put meat into bag and shake to coat
Heat EVOO on the dutch oven til very hot and brown the meat on all sides (3 minutes on each side)
Set meat aside and cook the onions for 5-7 minutes, add garlic with some butter and tomato paste
Deglaze the pan with the wine and add the chili sauce, sriracha, and red wine vinegar and bring to a boil
Add the meat and transfer to oven
Braise for about 3 hours and serve over rice or mashed potatoes
