Title:
Pork and Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound pork, cubed
1 onion, chopped
2 bell peppers, chopped
1 can drained tomatoes (or chopped fresh)
1 teaspoon basil (or chopped fresh)
1 teaspoon parsley
water
noodles of choice

Directions:
Combine ingredients in crock pot. Cook on high 4 hours, then low 2 hours. Add noodles during last 2 hours.
