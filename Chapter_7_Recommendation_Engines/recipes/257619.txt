Title:
Frozen Mango Bellini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4516042.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces mango juice
3 fluid ounces Prosecco (such as Caposaldo DOC)
1/2 fluid ounce orange-flavored liqueur (such as CointreauÂ®)
10 ounces ice cubes
1 sprig fresh mint

Directions:
Pour mango juice, Prosecco, and orange liqueur into a blender in the order listed; add ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into a serving glass. Garnish with mint.

Submitter: 
Cocktail Jim
Description: 
A crazy refreshing bomb of mango flavor and Prosecco! Garnish with fresh mango if preferred.
