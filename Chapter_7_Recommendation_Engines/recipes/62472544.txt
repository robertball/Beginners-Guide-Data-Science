Title:
Sweet and Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bouillon cubes (1 chicken, 1 beef)
1 1/3 cups liquid (juices off of canned pinapple and mandarin oranges; top the rest off with water if needed)
1/4 cup ketchup
3/4 cup sugar
1/3 cup cider vinegar
2 Tbsp. cornstarch
1 Tbsp. soy sauce

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan.
Heat over medium heat on the stove, whisking constantly, until sauce is thickened and bubbly.

Submitter: 
DarcieA
Description: 
My mom serves this sauce with what we call Hawaiian Sundaes.  Rice, cooked chicken, pinapple, green onion, coconut, etc.  People can make their own sundae with toppings of their choice- great for even the pickiest of eaters!
