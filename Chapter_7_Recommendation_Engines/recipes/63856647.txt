Title:
Pizza Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  lb. ground meat
1  16oz. cooked spaghetti noodles, drained
1/2 tsp. ea. oregano, garlic powder
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
2 oz. sliced pepperoni
26 oz. jar pasta sauce
16 oz. can diced italian tomatoes
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
8 oz. shredded italian cheese blend
(any other pizza toppings you might like)

Directions:
Place noodles in casserole dish.   In a separate bowl whisk milk & egg,   add jar of sauce, tomatoes, garlic powder & oregano.  Mix well with noodles.  Layer ground meat, add layer of pepperoni, sprinkle Parmesan cheese, and Italian cheese.  Layer pepperoni on top.  Bake in oven at 350Â° for 30 minutes.
