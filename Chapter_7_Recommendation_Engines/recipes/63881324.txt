Title:
Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 can tomatoes
1 can water
4 qt milk
1/2 tsp soda
1/2 stick margarine
Salt & Pepper
Goldfish & Grilled Cheese Sandwiches

Directions:
Blend together the tomatoes, milk & water. Add 10-12 crackers; crumbled up.
Then soda, butter, salt & pepper to taste. Bring to a boil.
