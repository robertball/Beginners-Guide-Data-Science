Title:
Double Almond Chocolate Chip Cookies (Gluten-free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988497.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups almond flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup grapeseed oil
1/2 cup agave nectar
1 tablespoon vanilla
1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
1/2 cup sliced almonds

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine almond flour, salt and baking soda. In a medium bowl combine grapeseed oil, agave and vanilla. Stir wet ingredients into the almond flour mixture until thoroughly combined. Fold in chocolate chips and almond slices.
Spoon dough 1 heaping tablespoon at a time onto a parchment lined baking sheet, pressing down with palm of your hand to flatten. Bake in a 350 degree F oven for 7 to 10 minutes, until lightly golden. Cool cookies on the baking sheets for 20 minutes, then serve.

Submitter: 
Almond Board of California
Description: 
Almond flour, sliced almonds, and dark chocolate chips enhance these delicious nutty cookies, which are sweetened with agave nectar.
