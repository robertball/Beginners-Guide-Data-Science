Title:
Curried Ranch Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless chicken breasts (sliced)
1 tbsp oil
1/2 cup ranch dressing
2 tbsp curry powder
2 cups of your favourite vegetables (I've used peppers, asparagus, mushrooms, broccoli, onions, celery, tomatoes, snow peas)

Directions:
Heat oil in wok.
Add chicken and cook on high heat, stirring frequently until there's no pink. (About 10 minutes).
Add dressing and curry powder. Stir well.
Add vegetables and cook til tender-crisp (about 5 minutes).
Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
linda
Description: 
Quick and easy to make, this dish adds a nice kick to the veggies you have in your fridge. It's great served with rice.
