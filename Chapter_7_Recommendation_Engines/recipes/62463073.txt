Title:
Moravian Sugarcake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs. crescent rolls
1-8oz. pkg. cream cheese
1 c. confectioner's sugar
1 Tbsp. vanilla
2 c. brown sugar, packed
1 stick butter, melted
1/4 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9x13" baking dish. Spread 1 roll of crescent rolls on bottom of baking dish, pinching seams together. Combine cream cheese, confectioner's sugar and vanilla with a mixer. Spread cream cheese mixture over crescent rolls.  Take the other pkg of crescent rolls and spread over cream cheese mixture.  Poke holes in top of crescent rolls with end of wooden spoon. Sprinkle liberally with brown sugar and cinnamon. Pour melted butter over top and bake for 30 min or until golden brown. Let cool.
