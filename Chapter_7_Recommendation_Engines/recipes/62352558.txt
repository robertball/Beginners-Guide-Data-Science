Title:
Best Ever Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts
1 onion, chopped
3 ribs celery, chopped
2 cups small broccoli florets
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup low fat Greek yogurt
1 cup panko crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large pot (season the water), simmer the chicken until tender. Cut or tear the breasts into cubes or small pieces.
In a large bowl, combine the water chestnuts, onion, celery, broccoli, soup and yogurt. Mix well, then stir in the chicken.
Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Sprinkle the panko crumbs on top of the mixture. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.
