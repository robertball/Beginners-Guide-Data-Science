Title:
Russian Apple Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Stale Bread Cubes, French, Italian or Challah bread
2 large Eggs
2 cups Milk
1 tsp Vanilla
1/3 cup Sugar
3 cups Apples (2 or 3 apples), thinly sliced
2 tsp Lemon Zest
1/2 cup Currant or Cherry Preserves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Butter an 8- x 8-inch baking dish, or one of similar capacity.
Place Bread Cubes in a large bowl.
In a mixing bowl, beat together Eggs, Milk, Vanilla and Sugar.
Pour the custard mixture over the Bread and allow to soak while you peel, core and slice Apples.
Gently stir the Zest and Apples into the soaked Bread.
Pour mixture into the baking dish and dot the top with spoonfuls of Preserves.
Bake 1 hour or until Custard is set and top is slightly crusty.
Serve warm or cold.
