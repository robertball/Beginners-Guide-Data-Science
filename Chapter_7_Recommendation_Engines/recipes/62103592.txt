Title:
ZUCCHINI HASH BROWN CASSEROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Four zucchini, approximately 8" length ea.
1 large onion
1 Tbsp olive oil
salt to taste
pepper to taste
1-1/2 lb. ground beef
16 oz. bag frozen hash browns
1 8-oz. can cream of potato soup
2 8-0z. cans cream of chicken soup
1 lg. can Durkee French Fried Onions

Directions:
Brown ground beef.  Drain grease and set aside.
Remove ends from zucchinis.  Cube into bite-sized pieces.
Dice onion.
In medium skillet, heat olive oil.
Add zucchini and onion.
Saute until soft and onions are transparent.
Remove from heat.
Spray 8 x 12 casserole pan with nonstick cooking spray.
Layer frozen hash browns in the bottom of the casserole.
Layer zucchini/onion mixture on top of hash browns.
Layer ground beef on top of zucchini mixture.
Salt and pepper lightly.
Spread canned soup over all using back of spoon.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 20 minutes.
Top with Durkee Fried Onions.
Return to oven and bake additional 10 minutes.
NOTE:  For those who dislike zucchini, other vegetables, such as broccoli, carrots, green beans, may be substituted.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
A hearty, filling casserole that is all-inclusive!  Meat, potatoes, and veggies round out this versatile family dish!
