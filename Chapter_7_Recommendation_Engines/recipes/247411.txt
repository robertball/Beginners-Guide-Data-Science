Title:
Cool and Creamy Cucumber Spread Bites Recipe

Ingredients:
1 English cucumber, minced
4 ounces goat cheese, softened
2 tablespoons minced red onion
1 1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1 (7.2 ounce) package Original Snack Factory速 Pretzel Crisps速

Directions:
Combine cucumbers, cheese, red onion, dill, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a bowl. Stir well.
