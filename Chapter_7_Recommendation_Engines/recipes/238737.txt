Title:
Frozen Melon Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2258083.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups ice cubes
1 (12 ounce) can frozen limeade concentrate
2/3 cup tequila
1/2 cup melon-flavored schnapps

Directions:
Blend ice, limeade, tequila, and melon-flavored schnapps together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Reeves
Description: 
Quick and easy. The perfect blend of tart and sweet in a good frozen margarita.
