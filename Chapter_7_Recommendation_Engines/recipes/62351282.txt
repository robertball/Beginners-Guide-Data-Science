Title:
Popeye Deviled eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/397862.jpg
Ingredients:
8  eggs
1/2 cup mayo ( 1-2 teaspoons more if needed)
1/2 teaspoon mustard
1 10oz box frozen chopped spinach. defrosted
3 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
place eggs in a pot and cover with water bring to a boil, boil 10 mins. remove from heat and run under cold water. peel eggs, cut eggs in half lengthwise and place yolks in a small bowl, whites on a plate.
mash yolks with a fork until a fine crumb. Add mayo and mustard mix well. Add more mayo if mixture isnt smooth. Remove spinach from box and place in a clean dish towel. wrap spinach in taowel over sink and squeeze until all the liqiud is removed. Add to egg mixture and mix well. Add salt and pepper to taste. Scoop mixture by the spoonful and place in halved egg whites. Spinkle with cheese and chill.
