Title:
Rockin' Robin's Romanoff Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 mostly medium to large russet potatoes
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup cream cheese
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup half and half or milk
3/4 cup  grated cheddar cheese
6 chopped green onions, mostly white parts
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
3 chopped green onions

Directions:
Peel, chop and cook potatoes in lightly salted water until tender.   Drain well and happily mash with butter, cream cheese, milk, cheese, sour cream and green onions. Add salt and pepper as desired.   Put in greased casserole and bake @ 375 for 50 minutes while covered.  Uncover and add remaining cheese and onions and cook for 10 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Phoebe
Description: 
No processed cheese here folks, just good ol' cheddar and dairy products.
