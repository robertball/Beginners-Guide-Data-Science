Title:
Piggy-back Lamb Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Lamb blade chops
1 large package fresh sliced mushrooms
8 slices bacon
Cracked black pepper
Seasoning salt of choice
2 tbs olive oil

Directions:
Saute mushrooms in pan with olive oil.
Season bacon slices with cracked pepper and grill (best cooked soft over open flame). Set aside.
Season lamb with seasoning salt (and pepper if desired).
Grill until cooked medium-rare to medium, or according to preference.
Remove from grill and put on plate. Add two bacon slices on each chop and top with mushrooms.

Submitter: 
BERKLAN
Description: 
Quick and easy lamb chops topped with cracked-peppered bacon and sauteed mushrooms. Simple, low-carb and very filling.
