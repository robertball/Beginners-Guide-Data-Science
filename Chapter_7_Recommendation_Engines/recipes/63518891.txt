Title:
Kyra's Krispie Fudge Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/960897.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup of Icing Sugar
2 TBS Butter
1/2 Cup of Peanut Butter
1 Cup of Rice Krispies
Chocolate Icing

Directions:
1.  Cream sugar, butter, peanut butter, till smooth.
2.  Add Rice Krispies, mix together.
3.  Roll into 1 inch balls, and put toothpicks through them all.
4.  Put them in freezer, for 45 mins.
5.  Heat up icing so it's easier to dip.
6.  Take balls and dip into icing.
7.  Place balls in fridge for one hour.
8.  Serve!!

Submitter:

Description:
