Title:
Melt-In-Your-Mouth Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
3/4 cup milk
2/3 cup seasoned bread crumbs
2 teaspoons dried minced onion
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon rubbed sage
1/2 teaspoon thyme
1 1/2 pounds ground burger
1/4 cup ketchup
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground mustard
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the first seven ingredients. Crumble beef over mixture and mix well (mixture will be moist.) Shape into a loaf pan; Bake at 325 for 90 minutes, or 350 for 60 minutes.
In a small bowl, whisk the ketchup, brown sugar, mustard and Worcestershire sauce. Spoon over the meat loaf. Cook 15 minutes longer or until heated through. Let stand for 10-15 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Diane
