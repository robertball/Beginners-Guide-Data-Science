Title:
Smirnoff No.21 Pomegranate Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988156.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka
1/2 fluid ounce Grand Marnier
1 splash pomegranate juice
1 splash tonic water

Directions:
Combine ingredients in shaker with ice.
Shake, strain and serve in a martini glass.
Garnish with lemon twist.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
A pretty pink cocktail, sister to the cosmopolitan, has orange flavored liqueur, vodka, and a splash of pomegranate juice.
