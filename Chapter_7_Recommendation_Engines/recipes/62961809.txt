Title:
Mom's Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups mashed sweet potatoes(approximately 3-4 large potatoes)
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
½ cup milk
½ cup butter
Topping:
1 cup dark brown sugar
1/3 cup all purpose flour
1/3 cup butter
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Boil sweet potatoes and scrape skins off with fork.
Put in mixer and add butter and sugar.
After it has cooled some add eggs one a time.
Add milk a little at a time.
Add vanilla last.
Mix well.
Pour into greased 2 quart baking dish.
Topping:Mix together and pat over potatoes.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
withanh
Description: 
This is my mom's sweet potato casserole recipe that we look forward to every Thanksgiving.  It's everyone's favorite.
