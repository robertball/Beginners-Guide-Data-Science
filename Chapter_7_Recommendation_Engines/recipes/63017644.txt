Title:
tuscan fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb tilapia/flounder
4 roma tomatoes, diced
1 shallot, chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
1 clove garlic, chopped
6-7 kalamata olives, chopped
1/4 cup white wine
1/4 tsp herbes de provence
salt and pepper
parmesan to garnish

Directions:
Heat olive oil in pan -medium heat
Saute shallot for 2-3 minutes
add garlic - up to a minute
add tomatoes, herbes de provence and olives. and cook down
once it starts to look dry, pour the wine in. then cover. (lower heat)
Once it looks like crushed tomatoes, put the fish in, and move the sauce on top of the fish
Cook 3-4 min on medium heat
Flip fish - when it flakes, it's done.
Plate and garnish with parmesan.
