Title:
Big Ray's Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129889.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 cup rolled oats
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 cup butter, softened
6 cups chopped peeled apples
1/2 cup sugar
2 Tbsp cornstarch
1 cup water
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°. In a large bowl, combine first four ingredients. Cut in butter until crumbly. Press half into a greased 9x13 in pan. Cover with apples.
In a small saucepan, combine the sugar, cornstarch, water and vanilla. Bring to a boil; cook and stir 2 minutes or until thick and clear. Pour over apples. Sprinkle with remaining crumb mixture.
Bake 60-65 minutes or until apples are tender. Let let sit 10-15 minute, serve warm.
