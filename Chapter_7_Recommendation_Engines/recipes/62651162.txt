Title:
Broccoli Cheddar Soup X Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/587047.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbs. butter
1 small onion chopped
1/4 c. all purpose flour
2 c. half and half
3 chicken bouillon cubes
3 c. water
2 bay leaves
1/4 ground nutmeg
salt to taste
pepper to taste
1 head of broccoli
1 c. chopped carrots
2 1/2 c. shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Dissolve chicken bouillon in water.
Melt butter in large sauce pan.
Add chopped onion and carrots, and saute until tender.
Slowly add flour stirring until liquid is absorbed.
Slowly add half and half and whisk until cream is smooth (veggies should be only chunks).
Add broccoli.
Bring to boil and continue to stir.
Add salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
Continue to stir until sauce thickens.
Slowly add chicken bouillon broth.
Bring to boil and reduce heat to simmer.
Add bay leaves.
Continue to simmer for about 20 minutes.
Remove bay leaves.
Slowly add cheddar cheese and stir until melted.
