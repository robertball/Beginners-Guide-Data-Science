Title:
Candied Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag carrots-peel and cut
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup orange juice
half a stick of butter

Directions:
Put carrots into a pan and boil or steam.
Drain and put back in pan.
Add brown sugar, orange juice and butter and stir until covered.
Serve hot.
