Title:
Laura's Crock Pot Chicken and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5817548.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts
4 c. chicken broth
1/2 stick butter
1 can cream of chicken soup
10 oz. wide egg noodles

Directions:
Combine chicken, broth, butter, and soup in crock pot.  Cook on low 8 - 10 hours or on high 4 hours.  Remove chicken.  Turn cooker on high and add egg noodles.  While noodles are cooking, cube cooked chicken.  When noodles are tender, return chicken to crock and stir to combine.  Add more chicken broth or water if needed to thin to the desired consistency.
