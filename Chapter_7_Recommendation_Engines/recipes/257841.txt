Title:
RITZ Hotdog Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4517148.jpg
Ingredients:
12 cocktail franks, cut lengthwise in half
24 RITZ Crackers
1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
1 1/2 teaspoons prepared yellow mustard

Directions:
Arrange franks, cut side down, in a single layer on a microwaveable plate.
Microwave on HIGH 20 seconds or until heated through.
Top crackers with relish, frank pieces and mustard.

Submitter: 
RITZ Crackers
Description: 
Ritz crackers are topped with slices of cocktail franks and sweet pickle relish with a dab of mustard for a quick, party or gameday snack.
