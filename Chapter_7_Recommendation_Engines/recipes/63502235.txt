Title:
Roasted Coconut Ginger Peanut Butter Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c Peanut Butter (chunky or smooth)
1/3 c Coconut Oil
2 t fresh grated Ginger
1/2 t Cinnamon
Cumin (I use @ 2 t, but use to your taste...I love cumin)
4-5 carrots (or a bag of baby pealed carrots, if your lazy, or tired...like me :P
Salt, Pepper to taste

Directions:
combine all ingredients except carrots
slice carrots length wise
add carrots and mixture, stir to coat
cook 400-425 for 25-30 min

Submitter: 
MattyHam
Description: 
This is a take off of a vegetarian dish that my friend makes.  This has been revamped, since she isn't a great cook...done right, WOW! You can use more than just carrots too. Goes great with Thai food...but just as good with a roasted chicken
