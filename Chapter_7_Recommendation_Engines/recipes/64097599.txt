Title:
McCormick Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
McCormick Beef Stroganoff Sauce Mix
2 T Oil
1 1/2 lbs stroganoff meat
1 c water
1 c sour cream
noodles

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet on medium high heat.  Add beef; cook and stir 2 minutes or until no longer pink.
Stir in Sauce Mix and water
Bring to boil; cover. Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat, sir in sour cream.  Serve over noodles.
