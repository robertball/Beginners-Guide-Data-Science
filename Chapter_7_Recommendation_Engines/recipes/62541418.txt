Title:
Spinach and rice Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/524071.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box of puff pastry
1 Package(8 ounces) cream cheese room temp
1 cup shredded cheddar or good cheese
1 boil n a bag Uncle Bens white rice(or brown)
2 eggs and 1 egg yolk
Spinach(Depends on how much you want in it)
1 tomato sliced
Any other vegetable(if asparagus or broccoli, id suggest steaming/sauteeing them a bit before putting them into this so it's not so crunchy)
salt and pep
garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Butter your pie dish.
Take one sleeve of puff pastry and roll it out on a floured surface so the lines on the pastry dough somewhat disappears.
Lay inside of a pie dish.cut the edges that are poking out.
Boil your bag of rice as directed on back of box.
While that is boiling,Take the cream cheese,all the eggs and the shredded cheese into a bowl and mix with spoon until well combined.
Once the rice is done and cooled, put into bowl with the cheese egg mixture.
Add the spinach and any other vegetable you may want. Add some garlic,salt and pep to taste.
Pour the mixture into the puff pastry pie dish.
Slice the tomato and lay on top of it.
Roll out the last puff pastry sheet until lines somewhat disappear again and lay on top of the quiche. Cut the edges that are poking out.
Put in oven and bake for about 30-40 minutes until the puff pastry crust on top is nice and brown and well cooked through.

This is perfect for a well needed vegetarian night but yet is very filling at the same time which will please the family.
