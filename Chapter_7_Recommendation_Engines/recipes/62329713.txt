Title:
Quick Avacado Dip w/Toasted Wheat Tortillas and Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/383961.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 whole avacado
2 tbsp salsa
2 12" whole grain, high fiber, flour tortillas
2 oz pre-cooked (grilled or baked), boneless skinless chicken breast
olive oil spray
garlic powder

Directions:
Toasted tortillas:
Turn on oven broiler.  Spray surface of each tortilla lightly with olive oil, then sprinkle with garlic powder.  Slide into wedges and spread, oil side up, on a non-stick banking sheet.  Broil for approx 2-3 minutes, till nicely toasted.  Check frequently.
Quick avacado dip:
Cut in half, remove pit, scoop contents of one half into bowl.  Spritz other half with lemon juice (to prevent discoloration) and store in refridgerator.
Chop and mash avacado in bowl.  Sprinkle with lemon juice.  Add 2 tbsp of salsa.  Mix.
Warm up 2oz chicken breasts and chop or tear into small pieces, approx 1/4" to 1/2" pieces.
Put small dollop of avacado dip and a piece of chicken on each tortilla wedge.  Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Elaine
Description: 
Very fast, easy and nutritious.  The combination of crispy, toasted, high-fiber tortillas and fresh avacado dip makes for a very healthy snack, high in Omega-3's, fiber, and proteind, but low in just about everything else.  See footnotes for WeightWatchers points and other nutritional info.
