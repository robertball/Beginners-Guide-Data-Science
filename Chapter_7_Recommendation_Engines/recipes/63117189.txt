Title:
Favorite Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/792863.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups allpurpose flour
3/4 cup sugar
2 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup vegetable oil or shortening
3/4 cup milk
1 egg
topping:
1/2 cup brown sugar (packed)
1/4 cup all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
3 tbs. firm butter
Mix until crumbly.

Directions:
Heat oven to 375  degrees F. Grease round layer pan, 9 x 1 1/2 inches, or square pan, 8 x 8 x 2.  Blend all ingredients except Topping: beat vigorously 1/2 minute.  Spread in pan.
Sprinkle Topping over batter.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.  Serve warm.
