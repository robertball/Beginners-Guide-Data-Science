Title:
Chocolate Bars - Hard - Sugar Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2106056.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Organic Coconut Oil, room temperature
1 cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
16 scoops Stevia (equivalent to 1 cup sugar)
1 tsp Vanilla Extract (No Corn Syrup, Gluten Free)
1 pinch Sea Salt
3 drops SweetLeaf Sweet Drops Coconut

Directions:
Put coconut oil into a glass pouring measuring cup
Slowly melt in microwave on low defrost setting until clear.
Once melted, stir in the rest of the ingredients until smooth.
Pour into chocolate molds and refrigerate at least 1 hour.
Pop out of molds and store in refrigerator.
Warning - this chocolate will become liquid at 75 degrees or warmer!
