Title:
Barbeque Chicken Club Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Tbls unsalted butter
1 onion, diced
4 carrots, chopped
4 ribs celery, chopped
5 medium red potatoes, peeled and diced
2 Tbls minced garlic
3/4 C flour
5 C chicken stock
14.5 oz can fire roasted tomatoes, undrained
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground pepper
1 tsp granulated garlic
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp red pepper flakes
3/4 C milk
1 C heavy whipping cream
3/4 C shredded cheddar
2 C chopped, cooked chicken breasts
1/2 C barbeque sauce
4 slices bacon, cooked & chopped

Directions:
In large pot, melt butter. Saute onion, carrots, celery, potatoes and minced garlic about 8 minutes.
Add flour. Stir until mixed and cook about 30 seconds, stirring. Add chicken stock, fire roasted tomatoes and seasonings.
Bring to boil and simmer a couple of minutes. Add milk and heavy whipping cream. Return to simmer and cook until vegetables are tender, 12-15 minutes.
Stir in cheddar cheese until it melts. Add chicken, barbeque sauce and bacon.
Stir to combine and serve

Submitter: 
cara1997
Description: 
This is a wonderful soup to make on a cool fall day. It is a more of a Stoup (more like a stew) than a soup.  My family just raves about it and I never seem to make it enough for them.
