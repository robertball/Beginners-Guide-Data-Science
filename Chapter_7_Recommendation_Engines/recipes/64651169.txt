Title:
Big Ray's Smoked Tilapia Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4539511.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tilapia fillets (1/2 lb.)
1/2 tsp. smoked paprika
2  dill pickle spears, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
2 Tbsp mayonnaise
2 tsp. chopped fresh dill
2 submarine rolls, split
2 slices cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat broiler.
Place fish on rack of broiling pan sprayed with cooking spray; sprinkle with paprika.
Broil, 4 inches from heat, 5 min. or until fish flakes easily with fork. Meanwhile, combine next 4 ingredients.
Place fish on bottom halves of rolls; cover with cheese, pickle mixture and tops of rolls.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Tender tilapia fillets topped with chopped fresh dill and garlic make a delicious filling for this sandwich-for-two.
