Title:
Mediterranean Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz) pkg al fresco Sweet Apple Chicken Sausage, fully cooked, cut into 1/2" slices
1 tbsp Colavita Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/3 cup fresh scallions, chopped
3 apricots, dried, finely chopped
2 cups white rice,long grain, cooked (jasmine or basmati)
1/2 cup chicken broth, fat free, reduced sodium
1/4 tsp sage, ground
1 tbsp parsley,fresh, chopped
1 cup yogurt, plain, nonfat Greek style
1/3 cup basil, fresh, chopped
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper, ground
1/4 cup almonds, slivered
2/3 cup grapes, red European type varieties, fresh (seedless), quartered

Directions:
In medium saut&eacute; pan, add oil and saut&eacute; scallions for 3 - 5 minutes. Add the sausage slices and apricots, saut&eacute; for 5 minutes. Add rice, broth, simmer 1 minute, then add the sage, and parsley. Cook for another 2 - 3 minutes.
Add yogurt and basil, and heat thoroughly 1 - 2 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper. Fold in grapes. Place in large bowl or serving dish and top with almonds. Serve either warm or cold.
