Title:
Apple and Spice Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (4 pound) boneless pork roast
1 1/2 cup ( Cinnamon Apples) applesauce
( 1/2-1 C Crushed Pineapple)
2/3 cup packed ( dark) brown sugar
4 tsp (apple cider) vinegar
2 tsp (hickory honey mustard) yellow mustard
1/4 tsp ground cloves
2 TBS flour
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
( couple handfuls baby carrots)
( 6 quartered red potatoes)
( 2 C Apple Juice)

Directions:
Plug in slow cooker, set on high and spray w/ nonstick or add a crock pot liner. Stir together apples, brown sugar, vinegar, mustard and cloves in small bowl; refrigerate half of the applesauce mixture and set aside remaining applesauce mixture. Combine flour, salt, sugar, garlic powder and pepper in another small bowl.
Rub flour mixture evenly over entire surface of pork, sprinkle remaining flour in crock pot, brown roast in cast iron pan, remove and put in CP.  Deglaze pan with 1/2 C apple juice, then pour over roast. Spoon 1 C apple mixture over roast, place carrots and potatoes on sides and pour remaining apple juice over all and cover.
Cook on High 1 hour, reduce temperature to low and continue cooking 5-6 hours or until done.
* Removing lid drastically reduces cooking time! Only check near end!!
Heat chilled applesauce mixture in small saucepan until boiling; boil for 1 minute. Spoon heated applesauce mixture over pork slices.
