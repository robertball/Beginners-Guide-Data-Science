Title:
Russian Tea Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6098467.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1/2 cup sifted confectioners Sugar
1 tsp Vanilla
2 1/4 cups flour
1/4 tsp salt
3/4 Cup finely chopped nuts

Directions:
Mix butter, sugar, & vanilla thoroughly
Stir Flour & Salt together
blend into butter mixture
mix in nuts
Chill Dough
Preheat oven to 400
Roll Dough into 1 inch balls
Bake on ungreased baking sheet 10-12 min (til set but not brown)
While still warm roll in powdered sugar.
Cool
Roll in sugar again.
