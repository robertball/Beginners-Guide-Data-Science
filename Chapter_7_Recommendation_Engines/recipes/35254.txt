Title:
Vanilla Milkshake V Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4957008.jpg
Ingredients:
3 scoops vanilla ice cream
1 1/2 cups milk
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
2 teaspoons white sugar

Directions:
In a blender, combine ice cream, milk, vanilla and sugar. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
POLGARA
Description: 
A thick and creamy milkshake that is so good it will make your taste buds go crazy! For a thinner milkshake, add more milk. For a thicker milkshake, use less milk.
