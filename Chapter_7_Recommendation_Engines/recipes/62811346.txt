Title:
Focaccia bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c lukewarm water
1 1/2 T yeast
2 T sugar
2 T olive oil
4 c flour
2 tsp salt

Directions:
Brush a 9x13 pan with olive oil.
Preheat oven to 375F
Mix together the yeast, sugar and flour.  Let sit for 10 mins, until it begins to foam.
Add to the bowl of your stand mixer along with the olive oil, flour, and salt.  Mix at a low speed for a couple of mins, then increase to med for another 2 or 3 mins.  Mix until smooth.
Pour the wet sloppy dough into the oiled pan, cover, and let rise for 20 mins.
Mix olive oil (about 1 T)with 2 T chopped fresh herbs such as thyme, oregano, parsley, or rosemary.
Use your fingers to make dimples all over the bread.  Drizzle the oil and herb mixture on the top of the bread.
Sprinkle with grated parmesan, and/or carmelized onion.
Set aside for another 15 mins.
Sprinkle with sea salt or other coarse salt.
Bake for 40 mins until the top is firm and golden in color.
Remove from the baking pan to cool on a wire rack.
Cut and serve with a dipping oil.

Very wet, soupy dough, short rise time.  From the Boston Globe.
