Title:
Vegan Mushroom Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb fresh mushrooms, thickly sliced
2 Tbsp olive oil
3 cups vegetable stock or broth
1/4 cup flour
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil in heavy pan.  Add mushrooms and saute until brown and the juice have evaporated, about 18-20 minutes.  Sprinkle in flour, stirring constantly, and cook for about 2 minutes.  Whisk in broth.  Bring to a boil and continue to boil about 8 minutes.  Season to taste.

Submitter: 
Jacqueline Bedsaul Johnson
Description: 
Rich and creamy gravy, perfect for vegans and vegetarians
