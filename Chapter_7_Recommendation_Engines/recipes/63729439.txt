Title:
Pineapple Pulled Pork Slider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup teriyaki sauce
1/2 cup Candied pineapple, roughly chopped
1/3 cup chopped red bell pepper
1/3 cup chopped yellow bell pepper
3 tbsp chopped green bell pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 1/2lb ground pork

Directions:
In a med bowl thoroughly combine teriyaki, pineapple, bell peppers, salt and pepper. Add pork and bring all ingredients together with hands; do not over work the meat. Form pork mixture into 4 patties of even thickness, or 6 smaller patties if desired. Transfer patties to a lined baking sheet, cover loosely with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
Cook each patties on the grill for 6 minutes per side, or until just cooked through.
