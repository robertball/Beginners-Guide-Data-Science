Title:
iced citrus green tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 green tea bags
3 pkg artificial sweetner
1 orange stick pack water flavoring
1 lemonlime stick pack water flavoring
32 oz water

Directions:
boil 8oz water. remove from heat. add tea bags cover and let steep 15 minutes.
pour 24oz water in quart container and add both the packets of water flavoring. add the packets of artificial sweetner.
then pour in the tea and stir!!

Submitter: 
rrekcut
Description: 
like the idea of drinking green tea for the antioxidants, but just cant quite get a taste for it?
this recipe i have worked on since i was at a resturant and they had flavored green tea.
i found the little straw packets of water flavoring ..and I DID IT!!
