Title:
Baked Hash Browns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 frozen hash brown patties
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 cup whipping cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Place patties in a greased 9 X 13 inch pan. Sprinkle with salt and garlic powder. Pour cream over patties. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 50 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake 5 to 10 minutes longer, or until potatoes are tender and cheese is melted.
