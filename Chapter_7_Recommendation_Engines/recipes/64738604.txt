Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz.  cream cheese
8 oz. of your favorite blue cheese dressing
12 oz. Frank's Red Hot Sauce
3 boneless chicken breasts - cooked
3-4 oz. shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook chicken breasts either by boiling or roasting or you can even use store bought cooked chicken.
Shred the chicken and put aside.
Put the cream cheese, hot sauce and blue cheese dressing in a large bowl and mix to combine.
Add chicken to the hot sauce mixture and then put it all in a greased oven safe casserole dish.
Bake at 350 for about 20 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
I serve it with Triscuits and celery.
