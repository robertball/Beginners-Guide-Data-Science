Title:
Raspberry Chia Almond Coconut Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3246840.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups frozen raspberries
2 ripe frozen bananas
1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
4 tbsp unsweetened shredded coconut
1 tbsp chia seeds

Directions:
Add ingredients to a blender & combine
Add to a mason jar & enjoy!

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Spring into summer with this dreamy almond coconut smoothie! Made with Almond Breeze, raspberries and bananas and topped with coconut for pizzaz, this vibrant smoothie will be on blender repeat for months to come.
