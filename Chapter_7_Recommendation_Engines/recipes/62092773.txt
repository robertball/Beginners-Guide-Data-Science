Title:
Venison Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tbsp. brown sugar
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp ginger
1/8 tsp hot pepper flakes
2 pounds venison strips
2 tbsp oil

Directions:
Combine first eight ingredients to form marinade.
Cut venison into very thinly sliced strips.
Place strips in zip lock bag.
Add marinade.
Place in refrigerator for 4-5 hours.
Turn occasionally to coat well.
Remove from bag with tongs.
Heat oil in pan or wok.
Add meat and turn till browned.
Goes great over a bed of rice.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Stir fry sweet soy version of deer strips. Goes great with rice or pasta.
