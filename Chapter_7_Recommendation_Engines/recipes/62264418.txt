Title:
Leonardo's Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger, or other ground meat
2 cups mozzarella
1 32oz can Spaghetti sauce
1 small can sliced olives, drained
1 container sliced fresh mushrooms
1 package spaghetti

Directions:
1. Brown meat and mushrooms
2. Add in sauce and olives, stir. Add in cheese stir. Reduce heat low, increase heat slightly if cheese does not melt. Cover
3. Meanwhile, cook noodles according to package directions.
4. Serve hot with parmesan cheese sprinkled over top if desired with a crisp caesar caesar

Submitter: 
Devin
Description: 
An awesome thick, sauce, filled with mozzarella, olives, mushrooms, and meat over a hot pile of noodles. Delicious
