Title:
Panera Clone Broccoli Cheddar Soup with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs butter
1 Tbs olive oil
1 large chicken breast 1/2 inch dice
1/2 cup finally chopped onions
2 tablespoons flour
1 cup half and half
16 ounces Velveeta
~16 ounces trimmed broccoli  flowerettes, See note
32 ounces Chicken broth
1 cup chopped carrots
8 ounces cheddar cheese shredded by hand
Salt and pepper to taste
Note:  Frozen broccoli like fresh will need to be trimmed of stems so buy more than you need

Directions:
In a 4 quart or larger pot,  heat oil and butter till sizzling and add chicken
When chicken starts to brown add onions cook another 3 minutes.
Add flour and stir for around 3 minutes to form roux
Add half and half in 1/3rd cup increments stirring constantly  till incorporated
Add Velveeta and stir till fully melted
Add broccoli and stir to incorporate
Add chicken broth a cup at a time and stir to incorporate.
Bring soup to a simmer and add carrots and simmer slowly for 10 minutes covered.
Add cheddar slowly allowing it to fully melt, stirring constantly.
Add salt and pepper to taste and simmer another 5 minutes
