Title:
Tender Beef Marinaid Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp Water
1 Tbsp Soya Sauce
1/2 tsp each oil, salt
1/2 tsp each soda, baking powder
2/4 Tbsp cornstarch
1/2 Tbsp Vinegar

Directions:
Mix all together pour over steak or steak cubed (beef or pork) Let marinate at least 2 hours. Over night is too long.. Do not double---too salty

Description: 
Quick, easy and makes meat very tender
