Title:
Turkey Tacos/Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground turkey
1 can black beans (rinsed & drained)
1 15 oz. jar salsa
1 large onion, chopped
8 oz. low fat shredded Mexican Cheese
Taco seasoning (to taste)

Directions:
Cook ground turkey and onion on stovetop using cooking spray.  When cooked, drain and add black beans, salsa and taco seasoning.  Stir to mix.  When heated through add cheese to pan and mix.  Serve either with tacos or nacho chips.

Submitter: 
Carol Kinser Fox
Description: 
I came up with this recipe one evening while craving tacos.  Turned out great and hubby loved it!
