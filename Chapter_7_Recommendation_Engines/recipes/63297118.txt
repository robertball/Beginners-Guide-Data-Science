Title:
Best 100 Calorie Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon cinnamon
3/4 cup white sugar
1/3 cup apple sauce
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/3 cup skim milk
2 cups fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Spray muffin cups with nonstick spray.
Sift together flour, baking powder and cinnamon. Set aside.
In a seperate bowl, mix sugar, apple sauce, egg, milk, and vanilla.
Add flour mixture and mix by hand until just combined.
Stir in blueberries.
Fill muffin cups 2/3 full.
Bake 20 minutes or until golden.

Submitter: 
Shannon Felgner
Description: 
With all the focus on carbs, fat, diet shakes and the like...I think we have all forgotten:  Calories still count!  These muffins are moist, delicious yet not too sweet.  They are muffins, not cupcakes afterall.  Enjoy.
