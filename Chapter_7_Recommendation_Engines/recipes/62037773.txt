Title:
Glorified rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can crushed pineapple
2/3 C. precooked rice
2 tsp. lemon juice
1 1/2 C. miniature marshmallows
1 ripe banana, sliced
1 small container frozen whipped topping, thawed
2 tbsp. chopped maraschino cherries

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving juice. In saucepan combine uncooked rice, 2/3 C. water, pineapple juice, and 1/2 tsp. salt. Stir to moisten rice. Bring quickly to a boil; cover; simmer 5 minutes.
Remove from heat, let stand 5 minutes. Add drained pineapple and lemon juice; cool. Stir in marshmallows and banana. Fold in whipped topping and cherries.
Chill.

Bobby Darapureddi
Description: 
Light, refreshing, a wonderful summertime treat!
