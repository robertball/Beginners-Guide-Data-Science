Title:
Peppermint patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/781630.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter no substitutes,softened
1/3 cup light corn syrup
4 cups confectioners sugar
1 teaspoon peppermint extract
melted chocolate

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl combine butter and corn syrup.Add 2cups of confectioners sugar and extract mix well.Stir in 1cup of confectiners sugar.Turn unto a work surface sprinkled with remaining sugar knead and work with until mixture is smooth.Shape into 1 inch balls flatten and dip in melted chocolate.chill until ready
