Title:
CHEESE AND CRABMEAT BITES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
8 English muffins
1 can crabmeat (pick crab to remove hard catilage)
1 block cheddar cheese (10 oz.)

Directions:
Grate COLD butter and cheese
Mix with drained crabmeat.
Add dash of salt
Spread mixture on halfed English Muffins
Cut into quarters
Freeze
(If desired can add color with paprika before baking)
Bake 425 for 10-15 minutes (Don't bake too long, will get too crispy)

Submitter: 
Mara
Description: 
Nice to have on hand in freezer to use anytime company drops in.
