Title:
Gordy's Baby Back Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1097625.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds baby back ribs
salt and ground black pepper, to taste
2 tsp. smoked paprika
1/2 tsp. sage
1 1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes
1/2 cup water
1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce

Directions:
Spice mix: Mix together smoked paprika, sage, cayenne pepper and red pepper flakes. Set aside.
Cut ribs to fit into slow cooker.
Season top and bottom of ribs with salt and pepper.
Rub spice mix all over ribs.
Pour water into slow cooker. Layer the ribs into the slow cooker.
Cook on High for 3 hours.
Add barbeque sauce to cover all ribs.
Cook an additional 3 hours on high.

Submitter: 
DebbyJean
Description: 
These are my husbands favorite ribs. Mine too since they are so easy to make. I love that I can make ribs even in the winter with this recipe.
