Title:
Green Onion Cilantro Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
10 bunches fresh cilantro
1 bunch green onions, trimmed
1 cup almonds
1/2 cup olive oil
3 ounces grated Parmesan cheese
3/4 cup chicken stock

Directions:
Place cilantro, green onions, almonds, olive oil, and Parmesan cheese in a food processor; puree until smooth. Gradually pour in stock until the sauce has the consistency of a thin gravy.

Submitter: 
MMENIGMA
Description: 
This is a great sauce to serve over chicken or fish, as well as a wonderful sauce to make tamales with (mix with cooked chicken, pork, or tofu).
