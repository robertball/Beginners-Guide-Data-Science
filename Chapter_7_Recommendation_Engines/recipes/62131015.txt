Title:
Mags' Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound dry chickpeas, soaked and cooked, OR 2 (15 oz) cans of chickpeas
2 Tbsp toasted sesame seeds
4 cloves garlic
2 ounces fresh lime juice
4 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
2 Tbsp toasted sesame oil
2 tsp Cavender's Greek Seasoning or lemon pepper
2 tsp table salt
1/2 tsp freshly-ground black peppercorns

Directions:
Drain chickpeas and reserve liquid. Combine chickpeas with remaining ingredients in blender or food processor and pulse until smooth, adding reserved chickpea liquid as needed. Serve as a dip with pita or tortilla chips, raw veggies, crackers or toast points, or use as a vegetarian sandwich spread.

Submitter: 
Eula Thompson
Description: 
Simple, hearty hummus with a good flavor.  Product of about five years of trial and error.
