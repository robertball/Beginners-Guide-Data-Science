Title:
Frozen Yogert Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1162390.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Graham Cracker Crust,1 (8oz)container of COOL WHIP,2 small Yoplait yogurt of your choice

Directions:
Mix the COOL WHIP and the yogurt together.After they are combined,put the mixture in your pie crust and put in the fridge for about 15 minutes(so it will be solid and will be easier to cut)Take out of the fridge and serve with fruit on top.
