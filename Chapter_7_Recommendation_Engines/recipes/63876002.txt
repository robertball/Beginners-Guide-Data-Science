Title:
Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked instant rice
1 can cream of celery
1 can cream of chicken
1 can cream of mushroom
1 lb chicken breast, cut in 2-3 inch pieces

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Lightly coat inside of 9x13 pan with nonstick spray
3. Spread chicken in an even layer on the bottom of the pan
4. Mix rice, cream of celery, cream of chicken, and cream of mushroom in medium bowl
5. Pour rice mixture over chicken and spread evenly
6. Cover pan with aluminum foil
7. Bake for 50 minutes  to 1 hour, until chicken is cooked through
8. Remove from oven and remove foil, let sit for few minutes, serve, and enjoy.
