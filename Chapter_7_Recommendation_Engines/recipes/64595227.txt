Title:
Deviled Pears Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3852530.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans pear halves
Paprika
Fresh cracked black pepper
Real Mayonnaise (or Mexican Lime Mayo)
Hernandez Guacamole Salsa
Zest of 1 lime
Cumin seed

Directions:
Drain pears and place on large plate OR in you deviled egg dish.
Sprinkle with paprika.
Grind black pepper over pears.
Place 2-3 large spoons of mayo and place in corner of Baggie with cut corner off to drizzle mayo all over pears.
Spoon drizzle Hernandez Guacamole Salsa on top.
Zest lime over dish.
Sprinkle Cumin seeds over dish .
Refrigerate before serving at least 15 minutes so all ingredients can develop together.
Serve as a side dish like you would with Deviled Eggs!!

Submitter:

Description: 
A GREAT mexican costal take on canned pears as a side dish to any spicy meal.
