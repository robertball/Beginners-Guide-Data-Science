Title:
Bacon and Egg Cheeseburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground chuck
4 eggs, fried (I prefer soft-fried)
8 slices bacon, cooked crisp
4 slices american cheese, or cheese of choice
1 medium onion, sliced and sauteed in olive oil, optional
4 tablespoons mayonnaise, optional
4 hamburger buns, split and toasted, if desired

Directions:
Shape beef into 4 equal patties and season with salt and pepper on both sides.
Preheat grill or pan and cook burgers 4-5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
Top with cheese and allow to melt.
Toast buns, if desired.
To assemble burger, place burger on bottom half of bun and top with a fried egg and two strips of bacon.
Top with onions, optional, mayonnaise, optional and top of bun.
Serve and enjoy!
