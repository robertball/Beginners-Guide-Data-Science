Title:
Easy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of ground sirloin
1 can black beans, 540 ml, drained
1 can crushed tomatoes, 398 ml
1 large onion, diced
Club House Chili packet

Directions:
Brown ground meat, add salt, pepper to taste. Add onion and stir. Stir in beans and tomatoes and seasoning packet. Stir. Bring to a boil. Reduce to a simmer. Cover and heat through for approximately 10 minutes.
