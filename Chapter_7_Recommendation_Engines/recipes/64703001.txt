Title:
Easy Dark Chocolate Ganache Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Chocolate, Dark
6 oz. Heavy cream
2 oz. Butter, unsalted

Directions:
Heat and stir all ingredients with clean, dry utensil in a double boiler completely smooth.
Chill to thicken before use, or use as a pour over for items such as cream puffs or eclairs.

Submitter: 
ToriGeib
Description: 
Use while warm as a pour over or let cool and use to ice your favorite cakes.
