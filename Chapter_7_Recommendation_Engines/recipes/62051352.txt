Title:
Cheddar-Bacon Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes (white or red work well)
3/4 C sour cream
1/2 C shredded cheddar cheese
3 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
1/4 C chopped chives

Directions:
1. Boil potatoes for 20-25 minutes until tender. Cool potatoes, then peel (if desired) and cube.
2. Mix potatoes, sour cream, cheese, bacon, and chives together.
3. Can be served warm or cold. Season with salt and pepper if desired.

Submitter: 
LLIGETT
Description: 
Tastes like a baked potato with the works--but in a salad form.
