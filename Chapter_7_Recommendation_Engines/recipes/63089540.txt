Title:
Frozen Whole Kernal Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c of frozen whole kernel corn
3 tbsp water
2 tbsp butter
1 tsp Mrs. Dash seasoning

Directions:
Place frozen corn in a microwave safe bowl.
Add water
Place butter on top
Sprinkle 1 tsp of Mrs. Dash seasoning on butter
Microwave on high for approximately 3 minutes. Allow to stand for 1 - 2 minutes in microwave.
Check the corn.
Stir corn until ingredients are well mixed.
Add salt to taste or omit salt for low sodium diets.

Submitter:

Description: 
Frozen corn microwaved with a bit of butter, mixed herbs and water.
