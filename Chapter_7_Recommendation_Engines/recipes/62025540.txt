Title:
A.B.C. Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar, packed
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 c. coarsely chopped almonds
1/2 c. butterscotch chips
1/2 c. chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven at 375 degrees. Line square cake pan with foil and grease lightly.
Soften butter.  Cream in sugar.  Stir in room temperature eggs.  Add vanilla.
In a another bowl, combine flour and baking powder.  Stir into sugar and egg mixture.  Pour batter into prepared pan.  Sprinkle with almonds and chips.  Press lightly into dough.
Bake for 20 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
jmacsaunders
Description: 
A yummy blondie with almonds, butterscotch and chocolate. ABC all on top! A favorite combination. Quick and easy too!
