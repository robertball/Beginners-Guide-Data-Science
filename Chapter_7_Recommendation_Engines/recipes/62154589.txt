Title:
Roasted Red Pepper and Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. olive oil
1 medium yellow onion, diced
3 gloves garlic, thinly sliced
28oz can diced tomatoes
2 roasted red peppers with skins removed and chopped (substitute 1 12oz jar roasted red peppers)
1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
shredded romano or asiago cheese

Directions:
In a 1 1/2 qt. sauce pan, over medium heat, sautee onion in olive oil for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, add garlic and sautee until soft, being careful not to burn.
Stir in tomatoes and red peppers.
Using an immersion blender, blend mixture until smooth consistency is reached.
Reduce heat to low.
Simmer for 30-45 minutes.
Remove from heat, add basil, salt and pepper.
Serve with optional shredded romano or asiago cheese

Submitter: 
LAURENMU
Description: 
A quick and healthy soup that perfectly complements a mixed green salad or a grilled cheese.  It's also a budget-friendly substitute to the store-bought, boxed versions.
