Title:
Quick & Easy Flat Iron Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Flat Iron steaks, 8-oz.
1 bottle Fat Free Italian Dressing
2 tablespoons chopped garlic
2 tablespoons parsley, chopped
2 tablespoons scallions, chopped
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix together in a bowl, dressing, garlic, parsley, scallions and pepper. Using a fork, poke holes into both sides of each steak and add to marinade. Coat steaks with marinade, cover bowl and place in refrigerator for at least two hours. Take out of fridge and grill on high heat. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sarah Johnson
Description: 
An extremely easy marinade that tastes great on any steak.
