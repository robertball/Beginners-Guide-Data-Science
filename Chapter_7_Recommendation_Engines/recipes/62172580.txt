Title:
Devils food cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C brown sugar
1C white sugar
2 eggs
1/2C shortening or 3/4C butter
1/2C choc, or cocoa
2C sifted flour
1/2C sour cream
1/2C hot water
1tsp baking soda
1tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cream the shortening and sugars together. Add the eggs, Beat until well mixed. Add the cream, flour and the chocolate, Last add the soda, hot water and vanilla. Bake at 325 for 45min
