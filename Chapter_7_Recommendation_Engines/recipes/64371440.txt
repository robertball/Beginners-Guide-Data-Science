Title:
Cathedral Windows II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz chocolate chips
1 stick butter
1 pkg colored marshmallows
coconut

Directions:
Melt together the butter and chocolate chips in the top of a double boiler or in the microwave. Stir to blend colored marshmallows
Take 2 pieces of parchment paper.  Sprinkle coconut all over the surface.  Divide marshmallow mixture in half and place each half on top of the coconut.  shape into a log and roll in coconut.  Refrigerate until firm and then slice.
Remove chilled dough from loaf pan, remove the foil, and slice into 1/4 inch slices.
