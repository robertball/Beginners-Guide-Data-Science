Title:
Taste of Home's Horseradish Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982121.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoons picante sauce
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat - drained, flaked and cartilage removed
celery sticks

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, picante sauce and horseradish; mix well. Stir in the crab. Serve with celery.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home
Description: 
This mildly seasoned crab dip is a terrific time-saver when accompanied by celery sticks or your favorite raw veggie.
