Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2446557.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups toasted rice cereal
2 cups Cocoa Pebbles (or other chocolate cereal)
4 cups mini marshmallows
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
4 tbs unsalted butter
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Add mini marshmallows and 3 tbs of the butter to a large pot and cook on low heat until it is melted together and smooth, stirring occasionally with a rubber spatula
Mix toasted rice cereal and cocoa cereal in a large bowl and set aside
While the marshmallow mixture is cooking, put chocolate chips and the remaining tbs of butter into a microwave safe bowl and microwave until chocolate is melted, stirring every 30 seconds
Add melted chocolate to cereal mixture and stir to combine
Once the marshmallow mixture is melted and smooth, add 1/4 tsp of cayenne pepper and stir to combine
Add the cereal and chocolate mixture to the marshmallow mixture and fold with a rubber spatula until marshmallow is fully distributed
Pour heated mixture into a buttered or sprayed 9 x 13 x 2 pan and press evenly with the spatula
Let cool completely before cutting into bite sized squares

Submitter: 
Val Pizzo
Description: 
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites are best described as spicy, chocolatey rice crispie treats
