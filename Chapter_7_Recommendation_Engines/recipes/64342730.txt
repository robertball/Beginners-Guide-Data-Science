Title:
Cheddar Potato Slice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can mushroom soup
1/2 teaspoon paprika
4 baking potatoes (1 1/4 lbs.) sliced thickly
1 cup shredded cheese, any flavour desired

Directions:
Make soup and pepper. In 2 quart greased casserole, arrange potato slices overlapping. Pour soup and pepper overtop and finish with shredded cheese. Cover,  bake at 400Â° for  40 to 45 minutes or until potatoes are tender, cheese has melted and is slightly crispy on top.
