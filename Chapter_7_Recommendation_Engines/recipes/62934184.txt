Title:
Balken Brei II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Qt. Lard Cracklings
3 Qts. Water
3 Tbs. Salt
Pepper to taste
1 tsp. Allspice
1/4 tsp. Ground Cloves
1/4 tsp. Nutmeg
2 lbs. Buckwheat Flour

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except buckwheat.
Bring to a boil.
Slowly stir in the buckwheat flour until flour is all dissolved.
Serve warm with syrup.
