Title:
Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 6oz. can - Tomato Paste
2- 6oz. cans - Water
1/3 C - Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/4 t - Garlic Powder
1/16 - Salt
1/16 - Ground Black Pepper
1/2 T - Dried Oregano
1/2 T - Dried Basil
1/2 t - Crushed Dried Rosemary

Directions:
In a sealable bowl mix all ingredients together, stir, and let sit to marinade flavors together.

Description: 
Thick and tangy pizza sauce.
