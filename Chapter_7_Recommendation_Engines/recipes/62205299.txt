Title:
Chicken Broccoli and Spinach Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (250g) brick cream cheese, softened
300 grams linguini noodles
1 lb. (about 4) boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
2 1/2 c. broccoli florets, chopped
4 chicken bouillion packages dissolved in 1/4 c. boling water
1 c. white mushrooms
2 handfulls fresh baby spinach
10 pieces of bacon
3/4 c. milk
6 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 c. grated fresh parmasean cheese
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp parsely

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to boil, salt and add linguini, cook to time on package. Add broccoli 2 minutes before cooking time finishes. When done strain and set aside.
Meanwhile, bring a skillet to med-high heat cook bacon remove and drain fat. return to stove add mixed chicken broth to pan, bring to slight boil and add garlic, 1 min. add chicken and brown, approx. 5 min. add cream cheese, milk, parmesean, basil and parsley to pan, adjust milk levels to thin or thicken sauce. wilt spinach in microwave in abowl half full of water for 2 minutes, addto pasta, pour  sauce over linguini noodles sprinkle with parmesean cheese and serve with a loaf of crusty bread! enjoy.
