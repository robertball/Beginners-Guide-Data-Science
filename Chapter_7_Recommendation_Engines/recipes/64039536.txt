Title:
Thai Green Curry Spinach Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp oil
1 medium onion, sliced
2 cloves minced garlic
1 small red chili, deseeded and finely chopped
or 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
2 Tbsp green Thai curry paste
12 oz fresh spinach, roughly chopped
10 oz canned coconut milk
10 oz vegetable stock
Black pepper
15 oz can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
Juice of half a lime
2 tbsp. fresh coriander, chopped

Directions:
Heat oil and add onion, garlic and fresh red chili.  (If using dry, add when liquids are added.) Cook for 5 minutes.
Add the green curry paste, cook for 1 minute and combine.
Add the spinach and stir until slightly wilted.
Add coconut milk, vegetable stock, pepper, and chickpeas. (Add dried red pepper if using.)  Bring to a simmer and reduce to desired consistency.
Remove from heat and stir in lime juice and fresh coriander.
