Title:
Ham and Asparagus Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large eggs
3/4 c half-and-half
Kosher salt and black pepper
1/4 lb baked or deli ham, chopped (1 cup)
1 bunch asparagus (14 oz), chopped
4 oz Gruyere, grated (1 cup)
1 store-bought 9-inch piecrust

Directions:
Pre-bake piecrust per package directions.
Heat oven to 325 F.
In a medium bowl, beat the eggs, half-and-half, 3/4 t salt, and 1/4 t pepper until combined.
Mix in the ham, asparagus, and Gruyere.
Pour into the prebaked pie crust and bake until set, 50 to 60 minutes.
Serve with side salad of 6 c mixed greens and 1 c quartered cherry tomatoes, tossed with 2 T olive oil, 1 T white wine vinegar, 1/2 t salt, and 1/4 t pepper.
