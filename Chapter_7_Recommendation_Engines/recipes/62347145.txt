Title:
My Super Moist Apple Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup applesauce
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
3 eggs, beaten
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 teaspoon lemon juice or lemon zest
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2 1/2 cups apples - peeled, cored and diced
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 package cream cheese,softened
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 cup confectioners sugar
1/4 cup finely chopped walnuts
** 1/2 cup fresh rasberries,optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease one bundt pan.
Beat together the oil, applesauce, eggs, vanilla and sugars.
Sift together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg. Add to egg mixture; stir in apples and nuts.
Pour into prepared pan and bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 1 hr or until a cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean. While cake cools, mix cream cheese, sugar and vanilla. Drizzle over partially cooled cake. Sprinkle top with 1/4 cup fine chopped nuts.Sprinkle fresh rasberries around base of cake on plate. Serve warm or room temp.

Submitter:

Description: 
An apple bundt cake,made extra moist with applesauce along with fresh apples. Spiced with cinnamon and nutmeg and drizzled with cream cheese icing. Top with finely chopped nuts and scatter fresh rasberries around cake for a great presentation!Modified from: Fresh Apple Cake
