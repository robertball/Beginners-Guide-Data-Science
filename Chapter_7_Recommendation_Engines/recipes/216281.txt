Title:
Baileys Spiced Nog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988003.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces Baileys® Original Irish Cream
1 dash St. Elizabeth® Allspice Dram or cinnamon
1 whole egg (optional)
1/2 ounce Captain Morgan® Original Spiced Rum
1 dash Angostura bitters

Directions:
Mix Baileys Original Irish Cream, Captain Morgan Original Spiced Rum, St. Elizabeth Allspice Dram, bitters and egg in a cocktail shaker.
Pour into a snifter glass.
Garnish with a sprinkle of grated nutmeg.

Submitter: 
Baileys®
Description: 
Baileys® and spice are a new twist on a holiday classic.
