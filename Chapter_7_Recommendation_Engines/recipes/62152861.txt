Title:
Reuben Crescent Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb thin sliced corned beef
8 slices swiss cheese
small can sauerkraut (6-7oz)
1/3 cup thousand island dressing
1 tube crescent rolls (Pillsbury recommended)
white of one egg, lightly beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Lightly grease an 8x8 loaf pan. Drain kraut then squeeze liquid out completely. Set aside. Just before layering into pan when directed, add thousand island dressing and blend well.
Unroll tube of crescent rolls, divide in two.
Lay first half out on a lightly floured surface.
Pinch seams together and roll out lightly to match diameter of pan.
Press lightly into pan and pre-bake in oven until lightly browned, 8-10 minutes.
Remove from oven.
Place 4 slices cheese on pre-browned surface.
Layer corned beef on cheese.
Spread kraut/ thousand island mixture on beef.
Top with remaining cheese.
Take second half of crescent roll, repeat seam seal and rolling out and then lay on top of cheese, pressing to edges of pan.
Brush with egg white.
Bake for 15-20 minutes until deep golden brown.
Let stand for a few minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
Patti Barrett
Description: 
Corned beef, swiss cheese, kraut and thousand island dressing baked between crescent rolls. Easy and tasty. Kids will love these. Serves 4-6
