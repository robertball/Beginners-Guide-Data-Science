Title:
** Homemade Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. half & half  (1 pint)
1 can (14 oz.) sweetened condensed milk
1 c. heavy whipping cream  (1/2 pint)
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Mix ingredients.
Churn in ice cream freezer per machine directions until soft-serve consistency.
Transfer to covered container & firm up in freezer for at least 2 hrs.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Quick & easy. No cook/no eggs. Ice cream will remain creamy without developing ice crystals if covered air-tight in the freezer. Our go-to recipe.
