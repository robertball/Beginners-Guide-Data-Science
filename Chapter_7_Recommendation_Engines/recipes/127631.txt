Title:
Skillet Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 strips Archer Farmsâ„¢ bacon, any variety, chopped
3 tablespoons butter
8 medium Braeburn or Granny Smith apples
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Cook bacon in large skillet over medium heat until crisp.  Remove bacon and reserve 1 tablespoon of drippings.
Meanwhile, peel, core and slice apples into rings.
Add butter to same skillet, reduce heat to medium-low.  Stir in reserved bacon drippings and apples.  Cook, stirring occasionally, 10 minutes or until apples are crisp tender.
Stir in brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg.  Cook, stirring gently, 5 to 8 minutes longer, or until apples are tender and a syrupy glaze has formed.
