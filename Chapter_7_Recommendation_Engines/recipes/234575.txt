Title:
Grands!® Pepperoni Pizza Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1050686.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16.3 ounce) can Pillsbury® Grands!® Homestyle refrigerated original biscuits
1 (8 ounce) can pizza sauce
2 slices shredded mozzarella cheese
16 slices pepperoni

Directions:
Cut each biscuit into 8 pieces; toss with pizza sauce and 1 cup of the cheese.
Spread in sprayed 13x9-inch (3-quart) glass baking dish. Top with pepperoni and remaining 1 cup cheese.
Bake at 375 degrees F 20 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly.
