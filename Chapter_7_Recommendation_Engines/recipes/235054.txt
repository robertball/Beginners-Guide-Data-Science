Title:
Skinny Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1060407.jpg
Ingredients:
15 ice cubes
1/3 banana
3/4 cup almond milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder, or more to taste

Directions:
Blend ice cubes, banana, almond milk, vanilla extract, and cocoa powder in a blender until smooth.

Debbie Crawford
Description: 
Great shake to keep you going!
