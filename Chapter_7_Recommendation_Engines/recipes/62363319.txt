Title:
Raw Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Raw paté:
2 cup raw pecans
1 cup raw pumpkin seeds
3 teaspoons cumin
1 teaspoon coriander
4 teaspoons Nama Shoyu
3 cloves garlic
3 key limes
1 teaspoon sea salt
1-2 teaspoons jalapeno spice
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
3 tablespoons water
Serve with:
1 to 2 heads romaine lettuce
sliced red onion
sliced tomatoes
diced avocado (1 per person)
sprouts "zesty mix"
1/2 lime per person

Directions:
Directions:
1. Blend all paté ingredients in a blender or food processor until chopped
2. Lay a piece of lettuce on a plate. Place red onion on lettuce leaf.   Dollop on the raw paté and cover with tomatoes, avocado, sprouts, and a squeeze of lime, and a sprinkle of herb-a-mare sea salt.

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Great filling vegan alternative for tacos.  Everyone I have made them for up til now has loved them.
