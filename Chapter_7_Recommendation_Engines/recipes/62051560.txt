Title:
Meat Loaf Hamburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
3/4 cup sour cream
1 cup crushed cornflakes
1 tbsp instant minced onion
1/4 to 3/4 tsp salt
pepper to taste
Hamburger buns

Directions:
Mix hamburger ingredients together; shape into patties.  Grill, broil, or pan fry until meat is no longer pink.  Serve on hamburger buns.

Submitter: 
Diane Blanchard Angell
Description: 
Delicious burgers made with sour cream, cornflakes, and onion.  I also like to butter and grill the inside of my hamburger buns.  A great burger!
