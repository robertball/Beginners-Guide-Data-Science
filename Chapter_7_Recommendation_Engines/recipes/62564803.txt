Title:
Jan's Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/541851.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box "Jiffy" Cornbread mix
1 box "Jiffy" White or Yellow Cake mix

Directions:
Dump both boxes in a bowl together. Follow the ingredient directions on each box.
For example: Each box says to use 1 egg. So you will use 2.
Add all ingredients listed on boxes and mix well.
Pour into a 9 inch of 11 x 9 in pan. Make sure to spray the pan with PAM Spray.
Bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for 30 min. or until done.

Submitter: 
Jannabofanna
Description: 
This recipe is sweeter and less gritty than normal cornbread.
