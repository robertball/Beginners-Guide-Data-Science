Title:
Juicy Crock Pot Chicken Breast For Any Diet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 can petite diced tomatoes
herbs, garlic cloves, onions (optional)

Directions:
Put chicken in crock pot and pour diced tomatoes over chicken. Add herbs if you have them. Cook on low for 6-8 hours.

Submitter: 
Melissa Franz
Description: 
Super easy low fat, low calorie chicken breast. Great to set and forget on a busy day.
