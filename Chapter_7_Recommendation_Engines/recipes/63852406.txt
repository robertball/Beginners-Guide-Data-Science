Title:
Slow Cooker Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-1/2 pounds sweet potatoes, cooked, peeled and mashed
1/3 cup butter, melted
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon orange juice
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1-1/2 cups mini marshmallows

Directions:
Line a slow cooker with a slow cooker liner.
In a large bowl, blend sweet potatoes, butter, white sugar and brown sugar. Beat in orange juice, eggs, milk, cinnamon and nutmeg. Transfer this mixture to the lined slow cooker.
Cover the slow cooker and cook on HIGH for 3 to 4 hours.
During the last 20 minutes of cooking, pour out the mini marshmallows from the bag and spread evenly on the top of the sweet potatoes.  Continue cooking until the marshmallows are melted.
