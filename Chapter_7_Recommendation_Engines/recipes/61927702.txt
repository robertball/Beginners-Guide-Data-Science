Title:
Easy Salsa Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground round or chuck
1/2 cup well-drained bottled salsa
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
2 tablespoons finely chopped green pepper
1/2 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs (or cracker crumbs & Italian seasoning)
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 egg, lightly beaten
3 cloves garlic, minced
dried parsley to taste
other seasonings as desired
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix the meat, salsa, onion, bread (or cracker) crumbs, cheese, egg, and garlic. Season with parsley, salt, and pepper. Transfer to a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or to a minimum internal temperature of 160 degrees F (72 degrees C).
