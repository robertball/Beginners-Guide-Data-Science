Title:
Homemade Tangy Sweet  Mustard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. sugar
1 c. flour
6 tbsp. dry mustard
2 tbsp. turmeric
4 c. vinegar

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients together well in the top of a double boiler. Add vinegar and cook over hot water until thick. While hot, pour mustard into pint canning jars. Seal tightly and process 5 minutes in a boiling water bath.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
An easy to make mustard that can be used in any recipe calling for mustard.
