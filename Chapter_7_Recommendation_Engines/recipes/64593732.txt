Title:
Udon Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3857997.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Cups water
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 1/2  teaspoons asian chicken bullion (we used Lee Kum Lee)
1 1/2 teaspoons soy sauce
1 teaspoon sesame oil
scant 1/4 teaspoon minced fresh ginger
1/2 lb fresh or pre-boiled udon noodles
1/2 lb protein of your choice (BBQ pork, chicken, tofu, shrimp, etc.)
*optional:  bean sprouts, bok choi, green onion, etc.

Directions:
In a 4 quart soup pot add water, salt, chicken base, soy sauce, oil, and ginger. Stir to combine well and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium.
Add fresh protein and cook until done. Add noodles and heat through.
Top with optional ingredients.
Serve and enjoy!
