Title:
Chicken, Bacon & Cream Cheese Fettucini Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slices thick bacon, diced
2 boneless skinless chicken breast cut in chunks & sprinkled with salt, red pepper flakes & garlic powder
1 large clove elephant garlic or 3 cloves regular garlic, finely chopped
2 tbsp butter
5 sliced mushrooms
1/4 chopped fresh flat leaf Italian parsley
4 oz cream cheese
1/4 c milk
1 (16 ounce) package fettuccini pasta

Directions:
Cook pasta in boiling salted water until al dente.  Meanwhile saute diced bacon over medium high heat until translucent, then add garlic and chicken chunks.  When chicken is done add mushrooms, parsley & butter.  Cook 1-2 more minutes, then add milk & cream cheese.  By then, pasta should be ready.  Drain & toss with chicken/cheese mixture.  Serve with homemade bread & green salad.

Submitter: 
Susan Bray Garrison
Description: 
My daughter's new favorite pasta dish!  Bacon, chicken, mushrooms and garlic, cooked in a cream cheese sauce with fresh parsley, and tossed with fettucini.  Dreamy!Modified from: Fettuccini Carbonara
