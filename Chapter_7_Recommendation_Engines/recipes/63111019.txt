Title:
Shrimp Asparagus in a Garlic Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Shrimp
1 bunch fresh Asparagus
1 Pint Heavy Cream
1 pint Light Cream or Half/Half
1/2 Stick Butter/Marginrine combo
2 cups Chicken Broth
Salt/Pepper to taste
2 Tbl Garlic Powder
1 Tbl Old Bay
1 Tbl Italian Seasoning
2 Tbl Flour
Frozen Red and Green Pepper and Onion
1/2 lb pasta or Orzo

Directions:
Break asparagus and saute in butter 4 Min
Remove Asparagus, add flour, more butter and Garlic powder-make Roux
Add Shrimp, cook few minutes
Add Chicken Bullion and cook Shrimp through.
Add Heavy/Light Cream/Half-Half and peppers onions
Cook till thick, add more garlic if needed.
Serve over Pasta/Orzo
