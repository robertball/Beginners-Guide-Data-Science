Title:
Big Ray's Golden Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3388433.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (14-3/4 ounces) cream-style corn
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup finely chopped celery
1/2 tsp paprika
1-1/2 cups crushed corn bread stuffing
4 boneless pork loin chops (6 ounces each)
1 Tbsp brown sugar
1 Tbsp spicy brown mustard

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the corn, onion, celery and paprika. Stir in stuffing. Transfer to a greased 11' x 7" baking dish.
Arrange pork chops over stuffing. Combine brown sugar and mustard; spread over chops. Bake, uncovered, at 400Â° for 35-40 minutes or until meat juices run clear.
