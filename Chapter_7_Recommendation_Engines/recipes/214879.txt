Title:
Man Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/567837.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
2 (14.5 ounce) cans chili
1 (16 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Spread the softened cream cheese evenly into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Pour the chili over the cream cheese and sprinkle with shredded Cheddar cheese.
Bake in the preheated oven until hot and the Cheddar cheese is bubbly, about 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
GLHood
Description: 
This is called man dip because I make it every time we get together with another family that has three sons, plus my brother and the two Fathers us women never get a bite! The men LOVE it!
