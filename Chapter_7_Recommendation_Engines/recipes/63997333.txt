Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons butter
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 onion, finely diced
1 (10 ounce) package refrigerated biscuit dough, torn into pieces
Garlic (minced)
Pepper and poultry seasoning to taste
Chicken broth to cover

Directions:
Place the chicken, butter, soup, and onion in a slow cooker, and cover with chicken broth.
Cover, and cook for 7 hours on low, At the 5 hour mark place the torn biscuit dough in the slow cooker(make each biscuit into 1/4's and drop in the cooker). Cook until the dough is no longer raw in the center.
