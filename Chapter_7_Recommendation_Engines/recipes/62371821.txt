Title:
Raspberry Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 9" unbaked pie crust
4 cups fresh raspberries
2/3 cup sugar
4 tbls flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 c heavy (whipping) cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Place berries in prepared pie shell. Combine dry ingredients then add vanilla and whipping cream and mix well. Pour over berries. Bake at 400 degrees F, 40-50 minutes until filling is set.

Submitter: 
Alberta Bound
Description: 
Our absolute favourite Raspberry Pie. Originally from the Farm Wife News magazine (now Farm and Ranch Living), it's been a family fave for years.
