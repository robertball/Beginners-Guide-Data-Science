Title:
Peppermint Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535482.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips (sub all or part with milk chocolate for sweeter fudge)
1 (14 ounce) can Sweetened Condensed Milk (I used EAGLE BRAND Fat Free)
Bit of salt
1/4 teaspoon peppermint extract
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup crushed hard peppermint candy

Directions:
Combine chocolate chips, sweetened condensed milk and salt in pan over low heat until all chips are melted.
Move away from heat and stir in the extracts.
Pour into wax-paper lined 8x8 or 9x9 dish.
Chill for 2 hours in the fridge.
Remove from dish, peel away wax-paper, and slice.
