Title:
Vodka Cream Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tblsp extra virgin olive oil
1 tblsp butter
2 cloves minced garlic
2 shallots
1 cup vodka
1 cup chicken stock
1 can crushed tomato (32 oz)
coarse salt and pepper
16 oz pasta (penne/rigatoni)
1/2 cup heavy cream
20 leaves basil
bacon -- crumble for topping

Directions:
heat large skillet over med heat. add oil/butter/garlic/shallots. saut√© 3-5min. add vodka, 3 turns around the pan in a steady stream with equal about 1 cup. reduce vodka by half (2-3 min). add chicken stock, tomatoes. bring to bubble and simmer.
cook pasta. Stir in cream and toss hot pasta with sauce and basil (torn). top with real bacon crumble.
