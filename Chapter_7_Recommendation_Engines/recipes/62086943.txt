Title:
Onion Soup for Two on a Budget Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 an onion, sliced thin
small pinch thyme
small pinch Rosemary
small pinch parsley
2 cups broth
1 1/2 tsp sherry
1 tsp Worcestershire
1 tblsp butter
2 tblsp Parmesan cheese, grated
2 rolls

Directions:
In a 2 Qt sauce pan, saute onions in butter until clear and soft.
Add in the rest of the ingredients and simmer for about an hour.
Pour in mugs and sprinkle with cheese.
Cut rolls in half and broil.
Serve rolls on the side.
On A Budget: use a leftover packet of ramen flavoring to make the broth and use parmesan packets left over from a pizza order.  Use any wine instead of Sherry.

Submitter: 
webmonkey
Description: 
While paying for college and still trying to eat, I came up with this recipe. Extremely cheap way to a tasty soup if you use the on a budget hints.
