Title:
Cobb Turkey Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992322.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package ground turkey
4 slices Danish blue cheese
1 tomato, sliced
4 slices Archer Farmsâ„¢ thick-sliced bacon
4 hamburger buns, sliced

Directions:
Heat grill.
Place patties on grill over medium heat.  Cover and cook 8 to 10 minutes, turning once, until no longer pink.
Meanwhile,  cook bacon in large skillet until crisp.  Drain on paper towel; set aside.
Place burgers on buns; top with cheese, tomato and bacon.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
Smoky bacon and blue cheese enhance grilled ground turkey. It's a little like a Cobb salad on a bun...but without the hard-boiled egg.
