Title:
Spinach and Feta Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz Frozen Spinach
1 pkg (4oz) Feta Cheese
8 oz Low Moisture Mozzerella Shredded
4 Whole Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
Course Salt
Ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375. Thaw spinach in a bowl of cool water.  Set aside. Place thawed chicken breasts in a small greased or oiled baking dish. Sprinkle each, on one side only, course salt and ground black pepper.  Drain spinach and squeeze all excess water out. Evenly distribute Spinach on chicken then follow with the Feta Cheese and lastly the Mozzerella Cheese.  Place in oven and bake for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Anne
Description: 
Spinach, Feta, Mozzerella and Chicken go great served with Sour Cream and Chive Baked Potatoes.
