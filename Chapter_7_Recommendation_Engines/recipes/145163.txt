Title:
Refreshing Summer Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) frozen pie crust, thawed
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/3 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
1 cup fresh strawberries, halved
4 kiwi, peeled and sliced
1 cup fresh raspberries
1 cup fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Roll pastry into an 11 inch circle. Lay flat on a baking sheet or pizza pan. Prick several times with a fork. Bake in preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes, until light brown. Remove and allow to cool completely.
In a small mixing bowl, beat together cream cheese and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener until mixture is creamy and smooth. Spread evenly onto cooled pastry. Arrange fruit in a decorative pattern over cream cheese layer. Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
A simple, sweetened cream cheese filling is spread over a freshly-baked pie crust and topped with mounds of sliced fruit: raspberries, blueberries, mandarin oranges, strawberries and kiwi.
