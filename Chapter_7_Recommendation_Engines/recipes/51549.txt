Title:
Svelte Blue Cheese Dressing and Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981854.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup non-fat yogurt
1/4 cup NESTLE速 CARNATION速 Nonfat Dry Milk
1/2 cup chopped green onion
2 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
1 small clove garlic, peeled and minced
1/4 teaspoon crushed dried basil
1/4 teaspoon crushed dried rosemary
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
To thicken yogurt, stretch a piece of cheesecloth over a bowl and place yogurt on top of the cheesecloth. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate. Let yogurt drain for several hours or overnight.
In a small bowl, stir together drained yogurt and milk. Mix in green onions, blue cheese, garlic, basil, rosemary and salt. Cover and refrigerate 30 minutes before serving.
