Title:
The BEST White Chocolate Cheesecake EVER!!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 Cup graham cracker crumbs
2 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
1/2 stick of butter (melted)
Cheesecake:
3 (8 oz) pkgs. cream cheese (softened)
1 (14 oz) can Sweetened Condensed Milk
4 Large Eggs
1 (approximately 12 oz) bag of white chocolate chips
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
Mix the crumbs, brown sugar and melted butter together and press firmly on the bottom of a 9 inch springform pan.
Beat cream cheese in large bowl with mixer at medium speed until fluffy. Gradually beat in sweetened condensed milk until smooth. Add the vanilla and then the melted white chocolate and mix quickly until well blended.
Lastly, add the eggs and mix until blended. Pour into prepared pan.
Bake 1 hour at 300 degrees
Turn oven off but leave cheesecake in oven to cool for at least one hour.
Take cheesecake out of oven and allow cheesecake to cool completely at room temperature.
Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Cleary
Description: 
This is a recipe my mom made for my wedding...I'm not fond of traditional cake so she made all of my guests cheesecake.  After many trials, this was the one that topped the cake...absolutely the best cheesecake ever!
