Title:
Super Bowl Grape Jelly Barbeque Ham  Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. chipped ham
1 doz. sandwich buns
3/4 C. white vinegar
6 Tbsp. grape jelly
1 1/2 tsp. paprika
1 1/2 tsp. dry mustard
6 Tbsp. brown sugar
1 1/2 bottles Chili sauce (Heinz brand)
6 Tbsp. water

Directions:
In a 2 quart saucepan, combine the vinegar, jelly, paprika, dry mustard, brown sugar, Chili sauce and water.
Simmer for 5 minutes.
Add the ham and marinate in the refrigerate overnight.
Before serving, heat over very low temperature for 1 hour and watch carefully stirring occasionally.
Make sandwiches and serve immediately.

Description: 
Delicious make ahead and serve the next day recipe for a party. No one will ever guess the secret ingredient..grape jelly.
