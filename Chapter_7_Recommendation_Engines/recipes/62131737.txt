Title:
Boxty Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound potatoes cooked in their skins.
1/2 pound grated raw potatoes
1 1/2 cups buttermilk
1 3/4 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
pepper and salt to taste
butter for frying

Directions:
Remove skins from cooked potatoes. Mash with buttermilk. Add grated raw potatoes, flour, soda, pepper, & salt.
Scoop onto hot, buttered griddle to make 3 inch cakes. Fry until golden and crispy.
Serve with butter or honey.
