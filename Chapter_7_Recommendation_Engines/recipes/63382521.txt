Title:
Terri's White Bean Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1028065.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T. coconut (or vegetable) oil
2 onions, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 (14.5 ounce) cans low sodium chicken broth
1 jar roasted salsa verde
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (7 ounce) can diced green chiles
1 T. chili powder
1 t. cumin
1 can salt free corn
1 pound diced, cooked chicken meat OR 1 pound ground chicken or turkey
2 (15 ounce) cans white, black or pinto beans
1 pinch salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil, and cook onion until it separates.
Add garlic and ground chicken or turkey, if using the raw. Stir and crumble meat as it begins to cook.
Continue until meat is cooked through.
Stir in broth, salsa verde, tomatoes, chilies, and spices. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 minutes.
Add corn, chicken (if using cooked), and beans; simmer 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
This chili rivals any chili made with beef.  It is so delicious and so much lower in fat and calories.
