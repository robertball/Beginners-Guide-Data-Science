Title:
Candy Coated Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg whites
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 dashes vanilla extract
4 cups pecans
2 T cinnamon
1/3 C white sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (135 degrees C). Line a cookie sheet with wax paper.  Spray the wax paper with cooking spray.
Beat egg white until stiff. Add brown sugar and vanilla.  Stir until smooth. Mix in pecans and stir until coated. Pour the nuts onto the prepared cookie sheet.
Bake until browned, approximately 15 minutes. Meanwhile, mix together the cinnamon and white sugar. Remove from oven, stir, and sprinkle liberally with cinnamon and sugar mixture, and stir again. Place back in the oven, and continue to bake until no longer shiny, stirring occasionally.
