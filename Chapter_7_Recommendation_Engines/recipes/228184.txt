Title:
Extremely Easy Italian Buffet Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984347.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds boneless beef chuck roast
2 medium onions, cut into 1-inch chunks
1 large red sweet pepper, cut into 3/4-inch pieces
14 ounces Classico® Tomato and Basil pasta sauce
1 1/2 pounds zucchini, cut into 3/4-inch chunks
Salt
Pepper
Hot cooked spaghetti (optional)

Directions:
Trim fat from roast. Cut roast into 1-inch cubes. In a 3-1/2- to 4-quart electric crockery cooker layer beef, onions, and red sweet pepper. Add Classico(R) Tomato and Basil pasta sauce sauce. Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 9 to 10 hours or on cook high-heat setting for 5 hours.
If cooking on low, turn cooker to high. Add zucchini; cook 1 hour more or until meat is tender. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve over hot cooked spaghetti, if desired.

Submitter: 
Classico
Description: 
Slow cooker beef roast with vegetables and Classico® Tomato and Basil pasta sauce is an excellent topping for pasta.
