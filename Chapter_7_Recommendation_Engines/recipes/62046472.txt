Title:
Celery Cheese Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 teaspoon granulated garlic
2 cups mayonaise
4 stalks celery, washed, ends trimmed and cut in half.

Directions:
Celery can be washed and cut and placed in storage bags and stored in your refrigerator's crisper drawer the night before.
Adding a little at a time, combine first three ingriedents in a large bowl. Stir until mixture is well coated and creamy. Add more garlic or mayonaise if desired.
Stuff each celery stick with the cheese mixture.

Submitter: 
Wineaux
Description: 
You can customize this by adding more or less garlic suitable to your taste.  I make these as a Thanksgiving appetizer and it's always for a crowed of 20 or more. But for a small group these can be whipped up in no time, just by halving the recipe.
