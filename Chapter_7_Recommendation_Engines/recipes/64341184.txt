Title:
How To Cook Prime Rib Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Prime Rib Roast

Directions:
Let roast sit out 2-4 hours. Want raw roast to be at room temperature.
Cook bone side down, Fat side up.
Season with garlic powder and cracked pepper.
Salt pulls moisture away from meat so don't use.
Figure about 15 minutes per pound with an internal temperature of 140 (medium rare).
Preheat oven to 425 and cook for 15 minutes.
Lower oven to 325 and continue to cook till 140 internal temperature is reached.
Remove from oven and let sit, covered loosely with foil, for 1/2 hour.
