Title:
Miniature Lemon Cakes with Cream Cheese Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1 (3.4 ounce) package instant lemon pudding mix
3 eggs
1 cup water
1/2 cup sour cream or plain yogurt
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1 8-ounce container cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 cup powdered sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Grease mini muffin cups. In mixing bowl, combine cake mix and pudding mix. Add eggs, water, sour cream, and lemon juice. Beat for 5 minutes. Spoon batter into muffin cups, filling each half full. Bake 15 minutes. Let cool 10 minutes in pan, then remove cupcakes to a wire rack.
In mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar and vanilla. Beat slowly at first, then increasing speed, blend until smooth. Frost cupcakes.

Description: 
Petits Fours are a traditional afternoon tea dessert, but this time-saving version is quicker and just as dainty because you bake them in mini-muffin tins.
