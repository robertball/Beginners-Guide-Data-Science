Title:
Greek Yogurt Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/360412.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Rolled Oats
1 Cup Water
1/2 Medium Banana sliced
1 Pinch of Salt
1/2 tsp Cinnamon (optional)
1/4 Cup Greek Yogurt
2 TBS Strawberry Jam
1 TBS Peanut Butter

Directions:
Add oats, banana, salt and cinnamon in a microwaveable bowl. Add water and microwave for 2-3 mins on high.
Once cooked, mix it to distribute the cinnamon more.
Leave to stand for a few minutes to cool down and thicken.
Add the Greek yogurt, top with jam or sweetener of your choice and peanut butter. Sprinkle with some more cinnamon if you desire.
Mix it all up and enjoy!

Submitter: 
TableTennis
Description: 
Oatmeal is a great way to start a day! I have it almost every morning so to not get bored I add different toppings and vary the ingredients I put in, always healthy and always delicious! Greek yogurt goes fantastically with hot oats and makes it creamier, adds protein and most important of all makes a standard bowl of oatmeal phenomenal!
