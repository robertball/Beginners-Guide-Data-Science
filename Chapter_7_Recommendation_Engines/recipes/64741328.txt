Title:
Johnny Carino inspired AngelHair and Artichoke by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6417538.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ounces Angel Hair Pasta
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 small or 1 large chicken breast, sliced thin
2 cloves fresh minced garlic
1 can (13.75ounces) quartered marinated artichoke hearts + juice
2 diced roma tomatoes
1 small can sliced black olives
1 jar (2.4 ounces) capers + juice
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 cup freshly chopped basil (4 tbsp frozen)
1/2-1 c shredded  Parmesan-Asiago
Optional 1tsp red pepper flakes

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package instructions.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet warm oil over medium heat, brown chicken with garlic powder until barely pink.
Stir in artichokes and  juice, tomatoes, olives, capers and caper juice and pepper.
Continue sauteeing until vegetables are warmed through (about 3-5 minutes).
Add garlic and sauté 1-2 minutes longer.
Add basil and mix well.
Divide pasta onto four large plates, top with vegetable mixture and a little of the juice sauce, garnish with Parmesan.
Serve warm.
Or add pasta, mix well  and serve FAMILY style!!

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
OMG! I fell in love with this dish at Johnny Carinos, unfortunately the closest Carinos to me is 200 miles away! I found a  copycat recipe and have tweaked it, including adding chicken. Mmm it’s on my regular rotation.
