Title:
Lemon Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 Cups Heavey Cream
1 Cup Milk
3/4 Cup Sugar
2 Tbsps Lemon Zest, Finely Grated
6 Large Egg Yolks
1/2 Cup Lemon Juice
1 tsp of Vanilla Extract

Directions:
Bring cream, milk, sugar, and zest just to a boil in a 2 quart heavey sauce pan, stirring occasionally.
Lightly beat yolks in a large bowl, then add hot sweetened cream mixture in a slow stream, whisking.
Pour cream and egg custard mixture into the saucepan and cook over a moderately low heat, stirring constantly.
Continue cooking until custard mixture reaches a temperature of 170* or the custard mixture coats the back of wooden spoon.
Pour custard mixture through a sieve into a clean bowl and then stir in lemon juice and vanilla extract.
Cool custard, stirring occasionally, then chill until cold.
Freeze custard mixture in an ice cream maker.  Transfer to an air tight container and place in freezer until hardened.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Egg custard based lemon ice cream with lemon zest.
