Title:
Simple, Delicious Salmon Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of Salmon per person. Go for Wild Caught Salmon if you can find it.
Drizzle of Olive Oil
Pinch of Salt
1 tsp. Black Pepper
1 lemon

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven's broiler and set the oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source. Line a jelly roll pan with aluminum foil, and spray with nonstick spray.
2. Drizzle olive oil on the cooking pan and place the salmon fillet skin down in the pan.
3. Place the pan in the oven and broil for about 10-15 minutes or until the fish flakes with a fork.
4. Remove, sprinkle on pepper, salt and a few grinds or 1 tsp of black pepper.
Cut the lemon in wedges and serve on each plate for individual dressing.
