Title:
Yummy Vegetarian Pasta / Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/357119.jpg
Ingredients:
Sauce:
2 Tbs Olive oil
1 Pkg of Yves Veggie Ground Round
1 Onion chopped
4 Cloves of Garlic diced
1/2 Pepper large chopped(Red, Green, Yellow or Orange)
1/2 Cup of Carrots chopped
1 Cup Broccoli cut into small crowns
1/2 Cup Celery chopped
1 Cup Zuchini chopped
1 Cup Mushrooms sliced
1 Jar Tomato Sauce - Your flavour choice
1 Can Tomato Paste
1 Tbs Oregano (optional)
Salt and Pepper
Pasta:
1 Box of Spaghetti Noodles
1 Tbs Oil
Optional:
Fresh Parmesan Cheese
Garlic Bread

Directions:
Heat oil in large pan over medium heat and cook veggie ground round until hot.
While veggie ground round is heating up, chop/dice the vegetables.
Add onion, garlic, pepper, carrots, broccoli, celery, zuchini and mushrooms to veggie ground round.  Stir frequently.
Once vegetables are soft (about 10min of cooking)add tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, salt and pepper and simmer on low-med heat for about 15min stirring frequently.
While simmering sauce heat your water and oil for spaghetti noodles and cook according to package directions.
Drain noodles and serve with sauce on top and sprinkle with fresh parmesan cheese.
Serve with garlic bread.
