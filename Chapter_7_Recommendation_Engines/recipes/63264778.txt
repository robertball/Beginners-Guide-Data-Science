Title:
Creamy Greek Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cucumber, chopped
1/2 small block feta cheese (traditional style packaged in brine)
1/4 cup mayonaise
1/4 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons olive oil
3 tablespoons milk
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium size bowl or food processor. Blend ingredients using a hand processor or regular food processor. Adjust seasonings to taste.

Submitter: 
Lea Davis
Description: 
This is a dressing I made for an american style greek salad (one with a hearty lettuce not typically found in a traditional greek salad).
