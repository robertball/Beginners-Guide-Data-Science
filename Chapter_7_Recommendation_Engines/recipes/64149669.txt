Title:
Grilled Sirloin Steak with Mushroom sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 sirloin steaks
salt
pepper
vegetable oil
Mushroom sauce

Directions:
1.  Season the steaks with salt and pepper and dip in oil.  Allow any excess to drain away before placing on rods.
2.  Place the steaks presentation side down on the grill.  Grill undisturbed for about two minutes.  Turn 90 degrees during grilling.
3.  Turn steaks over and complete cooking to desired doneness, about two to four minutes more.
4.  Top each steak with mushroom sauce.  Serve at once.
