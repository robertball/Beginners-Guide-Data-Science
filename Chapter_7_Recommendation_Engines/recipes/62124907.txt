Title:
Crock Pot Mac N Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten
1 stick melted butter
4 cups shredded cheese (sharp or mild cheddar, american also makes it good)
1 large can evaporated milk
3 cups cooked elbow macaroni
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Spray crock pot with cooking spray
Mix all ingredients, except 1 cup of cheese, in crock pot
Sprinkle 1 cup of reserved cheese on top
Cover and cook on low for 3 hours
DO NOT OPEN THE LID UNTIL THE 3 HOURS HAS ELAPSED!

Submitter: 
ChefJoe
Description: 
This is great for taking to work luncheons, it's easy to make and tastes like oven baked.  Try different cheeses, like pepper jack and monterey as well as different additions like chunks of ham, green chilis, etc.
