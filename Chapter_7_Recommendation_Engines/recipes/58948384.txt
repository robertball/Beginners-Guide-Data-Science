Title:
Budget Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/265240.jpg
Ingredients:
3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
1/4 cup (small bunch) chopped cilantro
3 cloves, peeled finely chopped garlic
1 medium	jalapeno pepper, seeded, chopped finely
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1 lime juiced

Directions:
Mash together the salt and avocados in a small bowl. Squeeze the juice of 1 lime into the bowl.  Stir in the chopped jalape√±o, garlic and cilantro. Can be chilled for up to an hour but is best served immediately.

Submitter: 
CookingOnABudget
Description: 
I like to keep my guacamole simple and let the creaminess of the avocado be the star.  I usually wait until my local grocer has a sale on avocados, and by leaving out the pricey tomatoes and added onion, this traditional Mexican dish can be very affordable.Modified from: Guacamole
