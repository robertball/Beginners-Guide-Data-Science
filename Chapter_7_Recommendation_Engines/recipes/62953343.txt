Title:
Asiago Parmesan Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 oz Asiago Cheese, grated (1-1/4 cups)
5 oz Parmesan cheese, grated (1-1/4 cups)
1 Cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1 bunch green onions, sliced (1 cup)
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 tablespoon garlic powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and place in a shallow oven-proof dish.
Bake in a 350F degrees, uncovered for 15-20 minutes until melted and bubbly. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Barb Ross
Description: 
Wonderful when served on flat bread or crusty bread coins.
