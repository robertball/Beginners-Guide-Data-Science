Title:
Greek-Style Tuna Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
1 (5 ounce) can chunk light tuna in water, drained
1 tablespoon chopped red onion
2 teaspoons prepared Greek vinaigrette salad dressing (such as KraftÂ®)
3 pitted Kalamata olives, chopped

Directions:
Mix the tuna, onion, salad dressing, olives, and feta cheese together in a bowl.
