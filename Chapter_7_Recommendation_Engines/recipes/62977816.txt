Title:
Fluffy Strawberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Store bought Graham Cracker Crust
1 box Strawberry-flavored gelatin
1/4 cup Sugar
1/2 cup boiling water
1 Container (8oz) Frozen Whipped cream ( Thawed)
2 1/2 Cups sliced fresh strawberries
8 Fresh Strawberry halves (optional)

Directions:
Wash and slice strawberries,Heat 1/2 cup water to boiling, in a medium bowl, mix gelatin, sugar, and boiling water until gelatin is completely dissolved. Refrigerate mixture until it just begins to get syrupy and thickens about 10 minutes.
Stir in Whipped topping until well blended, Fold in the sliced strawberries, and spoon into graham cracker crust. Place sliced strawberry halves around the outer edge of the pie. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours for it to settle. Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Starr
Description: 
My grandmother made this pie every year for Thanksgiving. She would make it with a homemade pie crust, but I love it with a graham Cracker crust. Either way is great! It's perfect for a very sweet and light dessert. It just melts in your mouth! Enjoy!
