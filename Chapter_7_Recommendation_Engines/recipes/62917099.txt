Title:
Hot Hawaiian Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Stick butter
1 T poppy seeds
4 T sugar
2 T onion flakes
2-3 T dijon mustard
2 pkgs Hawaiian sweet rolls (12 in a package)
1/2 pound ham, sliced thin
1/2 pound turkey, sliced thin
1/2 pound provolone, sliced thin
1/2 pound mozarella or swiss cheese, sliced thin

Directions:
1.  Combine butter, mustard, onion flakes, and poppy seeds in pan and melt over low heat.
2.  Cut rolls in half lengthwise and place bottoms in a 9 X 13 pan.
3.  Cover cut roll bottoms with meats and cheeses
4.  Place roll tops on top of meat and cheeses.
5.  Slice into individual sandwiches
6.  Pour butter sauce over sandwiches
7.  Bake, uncovered, in a 350 degree over for 15-20 minutes on center rack of oven.
