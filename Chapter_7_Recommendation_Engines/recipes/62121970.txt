Title:
Amazingly Frothy Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup strawberries, sliced (Fresh is best)
1/2 cup milk (nonfat or lowfat okay)
1/2 cup water
1/8 cup sugar
2/3 teaspoon vanilla (extract or flavouring to preference)

Directions:
Cut up strawberries in blender, add other ingredients, blend well, and enjoy!!

Submitter: 
SADS
Description: 
Similar to the Strawberry Julius - if it settles, just mix it up again. The vanilla gives it a nice kick!
