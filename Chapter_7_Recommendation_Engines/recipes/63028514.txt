Title:
Outback's Victoria's Filet with Lobster Mushroom Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 filet steaks
Sauce: 2 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
2 tablespoons chives, chopped
6 ounces of lobster claw meat
1 1/2 cups of heavy whipping cream
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Season steaks completely with your preferred seasoning.
Oil grill surface.
Adjust heat to desired temperature depending on how you like you steak cooked.
Melt butter in pan and add the mushrooms and chives, then cook for 1 minute on high heat.
Add lobster meat and saut√© for an additional 45 seconds.
Add heavy whipping cream and bring to a boil, cook for an additional 1 minute.
Pour over the top of your favorite steak!  Also goes very well with fish and chicken.
