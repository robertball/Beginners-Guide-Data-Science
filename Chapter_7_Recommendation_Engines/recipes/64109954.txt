Title:
Tagliatelle with heirloom tomatoes red onion olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1263652.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 T olive oil (used 1 T olive oil and 1 T butter)
1 red onion, chopped
6-8 garlic cloves, chopped finely
Little balsamic
3/4 to 1 c olives, chopped
2-3 heirloom tomatoes, chopped
Handful fresh basil, chopped
Salt
9 oz tagliatelle gluten free pasta (used jovial)
Little pasta water

Directions:
Make pasta
Heat oil on low-medium in large pan
Saut√© onion in oil/butter until starts to soften
Add garlic
Add salt
Cook low until soft
Add olives
Add balsamic
Add tomato
Add basil
Season to taste
Add pasta
Add little pasta water if needed
serve with basil, and Parmesan if desired
