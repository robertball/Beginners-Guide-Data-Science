Title:
Artic Peach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen sliced peaches
2 oz champagne
1 oz peach schnapps
1 oz vodka
4 oz kerns peach nectar
2 or 3 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in blender. Blend on high speed for approx 30 seconds or until ice is completely crushed and drink is smooth. Pour into a 12oz glass and serve with a straw.

Submitter: 
Sherrie Rodgers
Description: 
Very similar to a popular drink and a particular chain restaurant.
