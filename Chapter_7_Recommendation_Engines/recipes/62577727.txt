Title:
Egg Nog Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
COOKIES
1 cup Unsalted Butter, softened
1 cup Sugar
1 cup Eggnog
1 large Egg, room temperature
3 ½ cups Flour
1 tsp Baking Powder
1 tsp Baking Soda
½ tsp Salt
TOPPING
Eggnog
1 ½ cups Powdered Sugar

Directions:
COOKIES
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cover baking sheets with parchment paper.
In a large mixing bowl, cream the Butter and Sugar.
Add Eggnog and Egg and beat well.
In a separate bowl, whisk together the Flour, Baking Powder, Baking Soda and Salt.
Add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix well.
Drop by teaspoonfuls on the parchment paper.
Bake about 7 minutes or until edges are slightly brown.
Remove to wire racks and cool completely.
TOPPING.
Combine Eggnog with the Powdered Sugar until it reaches drizzling or frosting consistency - as you like.
Drizzle over cookies or frost and allow to set.
