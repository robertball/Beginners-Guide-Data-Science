Title:
Mango, Tomato and  Avacado Salsa II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2-2 mango's - peeled, seeded and diced
1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
4 medium tomatoes, diced and slightly drained
2 serrano chiles, seeded and finely minced
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
1/4 cup chopped red onion
1 tablespoons olive oil (optional)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine the mango, avocado, tomatoes, serrano chiles, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in the salt, lime juice, red onion, and olive oil. To blend the flavors, refrigerate for about 30 minutes before serving.
