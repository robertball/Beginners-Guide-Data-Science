Title:
toffee apple ckes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz/ 4 tbsp butter, plus extra for greasing
2 apples
1 tbsp lemon juice
9 oz/ 1 3/4 c all purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
2 1/2 oz/ 1/3 c light brown sugar
3 1/2 fl oz/ generous 1/3 c milk
3 1/2 fl oz/ generous 1/3 c apple juice
1 egg
topping:
2 tbsp light cream
1 1/2 oz/ 1/4 c light brown sugar
1/2 oz/ 1 tbsp butter

Directions:
grease 12 c muffin pan. core and coarsely grate one of the apples and set aside. slice the remaining apple into 1/4 in think wedges and toss in the lemon juice. sift together the flour, baking powder and cinnamon, then stir in the sugar and grated apple.
place the butter in a saucepan and heat gently until melted, then mix with the milk, apple juice, and egg. stir the liquid mixture into the dry ingredients, mixing lightly until just combined
spoon the batter into the muffin pan and arrange 2 apple slices on top of each. bake in a preheated oven 400 for 20-25 mins or until risen firm and golden brown. run a knife around the edge of each cake to loosen, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
for the topping,placing all the ingredients in a small pan and heat, stirring, until the sugar is dissolved. increase the heat and boil for 2 mins, or until syrupy. cool slightly, then drizzle over the cakes and let set
