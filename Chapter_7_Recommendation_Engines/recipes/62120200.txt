Title:
Ultimate Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 C sugar
1 large orange
1/2 t grated fresh ginger
4 C cranberries (or one bag)
1/2 C (2 oz.) toasted pecans

Directions:
Grate the orange peel and ginger and add to a pot with the sugar.
Add the juice from the orange into the pot and begin to simmer over medium heat until the sugar is dissolved.
Add cranberries and cook until they pop - about 5 minutes.
Add pecans and cool sauce
Can be made a few days in advance of serving.
