Title:
Kale Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch of kale
1/2 cup olive oil
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
1 1/2 tsp. cumin or paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Remove stems from kale.
Wash and dry leaves.
Make sure leaves are completely dry.
Place leaves on a large bowl.
Drizzle olive oil on leaves
Sprinkle with spices and toss well to coat.
Bake for about 15 minutes or until kale is crispy.
Remove from oven and turn the leaves over.
Turn oven off and return kale to oven for about 5 more minutes.
Kale burns easily so watch closely during cooking process.
