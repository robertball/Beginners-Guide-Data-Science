Title:
Cucumber-Mango Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/846613.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb mangoes
1 lb cucumber
1/2 t rice vinegar
2 t sesame seeds
1 t agave nectar

Directions:
Cut and clean the mangoes into 1" pieces.
Peel two strips of skin off of the cucumber from opposite sides.  Slice the cucumber into very thin (1/4" or smaller) pieces.
Toss the mangoes and cucumbers together in a bowl with the vinegar, sesame seeds, and agave.

Description: 
A sweet and tangy Caribbean summer fruit salad.
