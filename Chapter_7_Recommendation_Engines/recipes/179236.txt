Title:
Festive Fruit Wreaths Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982604.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
1 teaspoon vanilla
5 cups KELLOGG'S® RICE KRISPIES® Cereal
1/2 cup chopped dates
1/2 cup chopped red candied cherries
1/2 cup chopped green candied cherries
1/2 cup broken walnuts
Multi-colored candies (optional)
Powdered sugar (optional)

Directions:
In large saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat. Stir in vanilla.
Add KELLOGG'S RICE KRISPIES cereal, dates, red cherries, green cherries and nuts. Stir until well coated.
Transfer cereal mixture to wax paper. Using buttered hands shape mixture into wreath approximately 8 inches in diameter. Decorate with candy and sprinkle with powdered sugar, if desired. Best if served the same day.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
Description: 
Festive holiday wreaths are made with Rice Krispies®, candied cherries, and walnuts.
