Title:
Roasted Cornish Game Hens With Meyer Lemons,Rosemary Easy AirFryer Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4803122.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cornish hen (approximately 2 pounds)
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1 tsp. chopped thyme
1 tsp. chopped rosemary
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. sugar
zest of 1 meyer lemons

Directions:
1.Set the Cornish hen upright on a cutting board and with the back of the hen facing you, use a chef's knife to cut from the top of the back bone to the bottom of the back bone, making 2 cuts. Remove the back bone. Split the hen lengthwise, cutting through the breastplate. Take the two halves of the hen and set them aside.
2.In a mixing bowl combine all ingredients for the marinade, then add the hens. Refrigerate for 1 hour up to 24 hours.
3.Preheat Air Fryer to 390°F. Remove the hens from the marinade, draining any additional liquid with a strainer and add hens Air Fryer and cook for 14-16 minutes or until the leg is at an internal temperate of 165°F.
4.Serve and enjoy.
