Title:
Mushroom Topping for Any Meat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz mushrooms, sliced
1.5 T. butter
1 T. balsamic vinegar
2 T. heavy cream

Directions:
Saute mushrooms slowly in butter, keeping mushroom slices separate from each other.
Lower heat, add balsamic vinegar.
Stir for half a minute, then add cream.
Remove from heat, stir for half a minute.
Serve over any meat, or as a side dish.
