Title:
Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/476451.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg, beaten
½ cup sugar
1/3 cup white vinegar
1 ½ T dry mustard

Directions:
Beat together egg and sugar in saucepan over medium heat. Mix vinegar and mustard and add gradually to egg mixture.  Stir constantly until thick.

Submitter:

Description: 
We serve this with Ham on Easter Sunday. Enjoy!
