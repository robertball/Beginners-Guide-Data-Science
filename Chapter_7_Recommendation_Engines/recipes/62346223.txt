Title:
Sensational Strawberry Kiwi Zing Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/394528.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups frozen strawberries
2 kiwi peeled and sliced
1 cup vanilla yogurt
1 cup orange juice

Directions:
After peeling and slicing the kiwi, measure each ingredient and pour into blender. Blend for 30-45 seconds until smooth. Top with whipped cream or garnish with fresh fruit.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
Orange juice gives it an extra zing, but you may substitute it for apple juice if you prefer. This is a smooth smoothie, however, you can add ice if you prefer that as well. Make this organic by using all organic ingredients!
