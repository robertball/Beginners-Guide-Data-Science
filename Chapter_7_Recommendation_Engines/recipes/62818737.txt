Title:
Lemon Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
boneless chicken breast, about one per person.(4)
3-5 stalks of celery
vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups of lemon juice
2 cups of uncooked rice
about 2 1/2 cups of water

Directions:
cut chicken and celery into small pieces.
saute' chicken and half of celery in oil until chicken starts to brown.
add one cup of lemon juice to chicken and simmer.
In another pot bring remaining lemon juice and water  to a boil.
Add rice and remaining celery to water and cook until rice is done.
Stir in chicken mixture and serve.
