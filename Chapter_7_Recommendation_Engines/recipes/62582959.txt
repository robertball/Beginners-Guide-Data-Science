Title:
Easy Microwave Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/549594.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups sugar
1 cup butter, softened
1 Tbsp corn syrup
2 Tbsp water
1 tsp vanilla extract
3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips
2/3 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
In a large microwave-safe bowl, mix sugar, butter, water, and corn syrup. Microwave on high for 4 minutes, stirring every 1-2 minutes. Heat 6 minutes longer or until thickened and amber in color. Add vanilla extract and stir well. Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch pan. Spread chocolate chips over the hot toffee. Sprinkle the top with chopped nuts.
Allow to cool completely and break into pieces.

Description: 
This is super easy to make in the microwave and turns out yummy every time.
