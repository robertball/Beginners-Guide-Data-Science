Title:
Magic Custard Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs, separated and at room temperature
1 tsp vanilla
3/4 cup sugar
1 stick butter
3/4 cup flour
2 cups milk, at room temperature
Powdered sugar for dusting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Grease and line 8x8-inch baking dish with parchment paper.
Separate eggs. Beat whites til stiff and set aside.
In another bowl, beat  yolks and sugar until light.
Add butter and vanilla, beat 2 minutes more.
Add flour and mix until fully incorporated.
Slowly add milk and beat until everything is well blended.
Gently fold in egg whites, 1/3 at a time.
Pour into baking dish and bake for 60 minutes or until lightly golden.
Cool and dust heavily with powdered sugar.
