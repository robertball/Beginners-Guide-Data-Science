Title:
Neiman Marcus Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups butter
4 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 cups granulated sugar
2 cups brown sugar, packed
1 teaspoon salt
4 eggs
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
5 cups oatmeal, powdered*
1 8oz Hershey bar
24oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
3 cups chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
In an electric mixer, cream butter and sugars.
Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
Add vanilla.
In a separate bowl, mix together dry ingredients, reserving 1/4 cup of flour.
Add dry ingredients, a little at a time to wet, incorporating well.
Toss chocolate chips, nuts and candy bar with reserved flour.
Add to cookie mixture, folding in.
Roll into golf ball size and place 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 10-12 minutes, let cool on sheet for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
