Title:
Tofu with Spinach, Indian-Spiced Rice, and Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of 16 oz extra-firm tofu
1 package of 10 min instant rice
1 cup baby spinach
1 tbs olive oil
2 scallions, chopped
2 stalks of celery, chopped
1 lime
2 tbs garam masala
1/2 tbs cumin
1/2 tbs coriander
1/2 tbs ground pepper
1/2 cup tahini
1/2 cup plain yogurt

Directions:
heat water for rice and cook until soft
add 1 tbs garam masala
heat 1 tbs olive oil in pan over medium heat
pat-dry tofu with paper towels until most of the moisture is gone
cut tofu into 1/2 inch cubes and put into pan
add corriander, ground pepper, and 1/2 lime
cook until crispy, stirring occasionally, about 20 min
in a food processor, combine spinach, scallions, celery, 1 tbs garam masala, 1/2 lime, tahini, and yogurt until smooth
mix with tofu in pan
cook for another 5 min
serve on top of rice

Submitter: 
KOOLBRICK
Description: 
A great meal with grilled tofu, spinach, and indian-spiced rice. All ingredients compliment each other to make an excellent indian inspired dinner.
