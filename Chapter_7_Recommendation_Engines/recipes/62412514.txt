Title:
Lemon Poppy Seed Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 cup flour
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup sugar
2 t lemon peel
1 egg yolk
Filling:
5 packages cream cheese
1 3/4 sugar
1/4 cup poppy seed
3 T flour
3 T lemon peel
1/4 t salt
5 eggs
2 egg yolks
1/4 cup whipping cream
1 cup lemon curd

Directions:
1.  Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Lightly grease 13/9 inch pan.  Mix all ingredients until dough forms.  Press evenly on bottom of pan.  Bake 15 minutes or until golden.
2.  Increase temperature to 475.  Beat cream cheese, sugar, poppy seed, flour, lemon peel and salt in large bowl with electric mixer for about 1 minute or until smooth.  Beat in eggs, yolks and whipping cream on low speed until well blended.  Pour over baked crust.
3.  Bake 15 minutes.  Reduce oven temperature to 200.  Bake about 45 minutes longer or until center is set.  Turn off oven and leave cheesecake in oven for 15 minutes.  Cool in pan on wire rack 15 minutes.  Cover and refrigerate at least 12 hours until chilled.
3.  Spread lemon curd over top of cheesecake.  Store covered in refrigerator.
