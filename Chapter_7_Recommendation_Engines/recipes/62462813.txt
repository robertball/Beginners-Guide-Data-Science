Title:
Thymed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 bone-in chicken breast halves
1 c. vegetable stock
1/4 c. orange juice
1 pkg. dry Italian dressing mix
1 tsp. dried thyme

Directions:
Place chicken pieces in crockpot
Mix stock, juice and dressing mix and pour over the chicken.
Sprinkle thyme over the chicken.
Put lid on and cook on high for 3-4 hours.

Submitter: 
Dawn Eberly Wampler
Description: 
This is a crockpot adaptation that I created due to not having half of the ingredients for the original recipe.  I also made changes to lower the fat content and make it a bit healthier. It turned out OK so I'm posting it here for posterity... and future reference.
