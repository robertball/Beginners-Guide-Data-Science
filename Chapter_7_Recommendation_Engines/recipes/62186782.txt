Title:
Mocha Tiramisu Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate cake mix with pudding
1 cup water
3/4 cup Sweetened Condensed Milk
2 large eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup Kahlua
2 tablespoons Kahlua
1 (14 ounce) can Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 cups whipping cream, whipped
1 tablespoon instant espresso coffee
1/4 cup boiling water
1/4 cup Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/4 cup butter
2 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped
1/4 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease six (8-inch) round cake pans.
In large bowl, beat 3/4 cup sweetened condensed milk, cake mix, water, eggs and oil on low, until blended. Pour batter equally into each prepared pan. Bake at 350 for 14 minutes. Cool in pans on wire racks 10 minutes. Remove from pans, cool.
Brush each layer evenly with liqueur.
In large bowl, beat cream cheese, Kahlua, and sweetened condensed milk until well blended. Fold in whipped cream. Chill Creamy Coffee Filling.
In small saucepan combine water and espresso; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and stir in sweetened condensed milk. Over medium heat, cook and stir until mixture comes to a boil. Remove from heat; stir in butter. Cool Espresso Sauce.
Place 1 layer in a 4-quart trifle dish or bowl; drizzle with 1/5 of the Espresso Sauce top with 1/5 of the Creamy Coffee Filling. Repeat procedure with remaining cake layers, ending with cake layer.
Melt chocolate and cream in microwave for 30 seconds.
Garnish cake with chocolate ganache. Chill. Store covered in refrigerator.
