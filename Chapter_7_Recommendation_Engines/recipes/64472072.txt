Title:
Dinomites's Fluffy Bites! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2477296.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans of Grands Flakey Biscuits
1/2 cup salted butter, melted
Cinnamon
Sugar

Directions:
Mix equal parts cinnamon and sugar in a bowl. Cut each biscuit into fours then roll into balls. Dip balls into melted butter then roll in cinnamon and sugar mixture. Loosely pack a greased bundt cake pan. Cook for 30 minutes at 350. After removing from oven, immediately flip bundt cake pan onto a plate. Enjoy!
