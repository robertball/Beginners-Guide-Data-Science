Title:
Aunt Grace's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/405205.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C Flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 C Crisco
1 1/2 C white sugar
2 eggs
2 bananas
3/4 C sour milk  (3/4 C milk, 1 tsp lemon juice)
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients. With Mixer, cream Crisco, sugar.  Add eggs, banana's, sour milk and vanilla.
Bake 350 40-55 minutes. Makes 2 loaves!

Submitter: 
Sweetness14445
Description: 
This is my great aunt's 'secret' recipe for a moist banana bread that gets rave reviews. Whats even better is, you only need 2 bananas!
