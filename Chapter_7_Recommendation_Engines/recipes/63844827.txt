Title:
Big Ray's Sun-Dried Tomato Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068030.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package (8 ounces) Cream Cheese, softened
3 Tbs sour cream
3 Tbs mayonnaise
1/2 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and patted dry
1 clove garlic
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
2 green onions, sliced

Directions:
Place the first eight ingredients in a food processor; cover and process until blended. Add green onions; cover and pulse until finely chopped.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This dip is great for tailgate parties or as an appetizer for any occasion. It's so quick and easy to pull together and just full of flavor. Serve with crackers, pretzels and/or vegetables.
