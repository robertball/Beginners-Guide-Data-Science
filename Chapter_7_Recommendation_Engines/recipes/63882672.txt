Title:
Hamburger Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped (about 1/2 cup)
1/4 cup butter or margarine
2 tbsp flour
1 tsp salt
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp pepper
1 8oz can of mushroom pieces, drained
1 can condenced cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
2 cups egg or other noodles.

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef and onion in butter until onion is tender and beef is lightly browned. Drain majority of fat.
Stir in flour, salt, garlic, pepper and mushrooms, cook 5 minutes, stirring constantly, and remove from heat.
Stir in soup and simmer uncovered for 10 minutes.
Stir in sour cream and noodles and simmer, covered, until noodles are tender.
