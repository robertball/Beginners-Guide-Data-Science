Title:
CHICKEN BREASTS IN PHYLLO Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. mayonnaise or cream cz
1/3 c. lemon juice
2 tsp. dry tarragon
24 sheets phyllo
1 1/3 c. butter, melted
1/3 c. grated Parmesan cheese
1 c. chopped green onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
12 chicken breast halves, skinned and boned
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine mayonnaise, onion, lemon juice, garlic, and tarragon to make a sauce. Place 1 sheet phyllo on working surface. Brush with approximately 2 teaspoons butter. Spread 1 1/2 tablespoons sauce on each side of chicken breast. Spread melted butter on 2nd phyllo sheet (place on top of first sheet). Place chicken in one corner of buttered phyllo sheets. Fold corner over breast, then fold sides over and roll breast up in the 2 sheets to form a package. Place in ungreased baking dish. Repeat with remaining breasts and phyllo sheets. Brush packets with rest of butter and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Wrap each breast in plastic wrap and place in freezer bag. Thaw before baking. 375 in ungreased baking dish for 30 min. or until golden brown.
