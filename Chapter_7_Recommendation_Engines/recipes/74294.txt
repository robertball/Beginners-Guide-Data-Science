Title:
Sweet n Spicy Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978670.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch Mann's BroccoliniÂ®, cut in half
1/2 cup red onion, diced
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
3/4 pound chicken breasts, boneless, skinless and cut into cubes
1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained
2/3 cup sweet & spicy stir fry seasonings
1 (8 ounce) can pineapple, drained

Directions:
In large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken and stir fry until no longer pink, about 5 minutes. Add Mann's Broccolini, red onion and water chestnuts; stir fry for 5 minutes. Add sweet & spicy stir fry seasonings and pineapple chunks; heat through and serve.

Submitter: 
Mann's Fresh Vegetables
Description: 
Stir-frying is a fun, easy way to enjoy Asian-style cooking at home. Broccolini and chicken are the highlights of this delicious dish that's exotic enough to excite and simple enough to please everyone.
