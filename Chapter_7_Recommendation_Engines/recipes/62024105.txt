Title:
Ritz Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 chicken breasts, cooked & cooled
1 pkg. low-fat cream cheese
2 cans cream of chicken soup
2 rolls Ritz crackers
1 stick butter, melted (or butter-flavored spray)

Directions:
Break up chicken and put in the bottom of a casserole dish. Mix cheese and soup over medium heat until well blended. Pour over chicken. Top with crumbled crackers. Coat crackers with butter. Bake at 350 until bubbly.

Submitter: 
Heather K.
Description: 
This a delicious recipe with chicken, cream cheese, Ritz crackers, cream of chicken soup, and butter.  With only 5 ingredients, this is as easy as it gets.
