Title:
Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups ground nuts
1/4 cup homemade yogurt or small amount of fruit juice (add last and adjust amount depending on consistency of batter)
1/2 cup honey (more or less as desired)
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/8 teaspoon salt (optional)
4 eggs (or pureed fruit)
2 mashed extra-ripe bananas

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
Grind the nuts in the blender and empty into a bowl.
Place eggs and honey into blender and mix thoroughly.
Add egg mixture and mashed bananas to nuts and blend by hand or with electric beater.
Add yogurt as needed to bring to bread batter consistency.
Blend in baking soda and salt.
Spoon batter into a greased 1 quart baking dish and bake for 15-20 minutes or until the bread springs back when pressed.
