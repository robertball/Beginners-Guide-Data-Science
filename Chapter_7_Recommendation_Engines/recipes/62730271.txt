Title:
Gingerbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup molasses
1 egg
2 cups flour
1/2tsp.salt
1/2 tsp.baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. ground cloves
1 tsp. ginger
1 1/2 tsp.cinnamon
1/2 tsp.nutmeg

Directions:
Combine shortening with sugar,add the egg and molasses.Add flour and remaining ingredients.Mix till smooth.Chill in fridge for one hour.Roll about 1/4 inch thick.Bake at 350 for 8-10 min.Makes 10-12 men.
