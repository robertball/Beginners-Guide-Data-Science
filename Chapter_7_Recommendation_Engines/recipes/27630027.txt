Title:
Carribean Pumpkin Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Olive oil
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped red pepper
1 TBS. minced garlic
1 tsp. ground cumin
2-1/2 cups veggie broth
15oz can 100% pumpkin puree
15oz can black beans
14-1/2oz can diced tomatoes
salt and pepper to taste
cilantro
sour cream

Directions:
Swirl a little oil in the bottom of a 2 quart saucepan over medium heat. Saute onions and peppersabout 5 minutes or until tender.
Stir in garlic and cumin. Cook one minute, then stir in broth, pumpkin, black beans (rinsed), diced tomatoes (drained), and salt and pepper.
Bring to a boil; reduce heat, cover and simmer 5 minutes to blend flavors. Serve, if you wish with cilantro and sour cream.
