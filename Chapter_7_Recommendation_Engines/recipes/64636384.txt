Title:
Whole Wheat Banana Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. Whole wheat flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. kosher salt
1/2 tsp. baking powder
2 eggs, room temp
1/4 c. coconut oil, melted
1/3 c. pure maple syrup
2 ripe bananas, mashed
1/4 c. unsweetened applesauce
1 c. frozen or fresh blueberries
1/2 c. chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 425 deg. Prepare 12 cup muffin tin with baking spray.
1. In a medium bowl add flour, baking soda, salt and baking powder and stir together til blended and make a well in the center.
2. Add eggs, coconut oil and maple syrup to the well and bring all ingredients together until just combined, do not over mix.
3. Add mashed bananas, applesauce and blueberries, and walnuts, if using and stir til just combined.
4. Divide batter into the twelve muffin tins.
5. Bake at 425 deg. for 6-7 mins. Reduce heat to 350 deg. and bake for another 15 mins.
Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack to cool completely.
