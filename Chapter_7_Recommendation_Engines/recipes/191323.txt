Title:
Tangy Green Herb Dressing Recipe

Ingredients:
1/2 cup DannonÂ® All Natural Plain Yogurt
1 tablespoon (packed) chopped fresh basil
1 tablespoon (packed) chopped fresh chives
1 tablespoon (packed) chopped fresh dill
1 tablespoon (packed) chopped fresh mint
1 teaspoon red wine vinegar
1/8 teaspoon sea salt
1/8 teaspoon freshly cracked pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender and puree until smooth and uniform in color. Season with salt and pepper.

Description: 
A lively dressing made with yogurt brings out the taste of fresh basil, chives, dill, and mint.
