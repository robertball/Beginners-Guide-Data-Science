Title:
Hot Alaska Salmon Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (15 oz) salmon
1/3 c. mayo
2 T. each minced parsley and onion
2 T. drained, chopped pimento
2 tsp. lemon juice
dash pepper
3/4 c. shredded Cheddar, divided
8 slices French bread or split English muffins, etc.

Directions:
Drain salmon; break into large chunks.  Add mayo, parsley, onion, pimento, lemon juice and pepper; toss gently to mix.  Fold in 1/2 c. cheese. Spread about 1/4 c. mixture on each slice of bread.  Sprinkle remaining cheese over salmon mix.
Broil about 6 inches from heat 3 to 4 minutes or until cheese melts and tops are golden.
