Title:
Dried Beef Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1005207.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
3 ounces dried beef
2 green onions, chopped

Directions:
In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, dried beef & green onions. Form the mixture into a ball and refrigerate before serving.

Submitter: 
Faith N
Description: 
This recipe is our family's holiday favorite. Grandma usually doubles the recipe otherwise it won't last long! The ball can be rolled in ground nuts, bacon bits, or left plain -- it's up to you!Modified from: Dried Beef Cream Cheese Ball
