Title:
Crushed Cracker Tenderloins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound pork tenderloin
1 egg, beaten
1 tablespoon steak sauce
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 cup saltines, finely crushed
vegetable oil, for frying (approx 2 tbsp)
garlic powder, to taste (1/2 tsp or more)

Directions:
Combine egg, steak sauce and garlic powder in a shallow dish.   Place crushed crackers in another shallow dish.
Cut tenderloins crosswise into 1/2 inch thick slices.   Pound or flatten slices to 1/4 inch thickness.
Dip each slice into egg mixture turning to coat.   Then into crackers turning to coat.
Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot.   Add pork slices and cook 4 to 5 minutes per side or until browned.
