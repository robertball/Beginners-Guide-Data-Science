Title:
Old Fashioned Flaky Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Flour
3/4 Cup of Lard (Shortening)
pinch salt
Pinch sugar
1/2 cup water
1 tsp vinegar

Directions:
Combine Flour, shortening, salt, and sugar. Knead by hand until mixture is crumbly.
Add vinegar to water and slowly add to the flour mixture. Dough should not be sticky!
pat dough with flour and roll out.
*If you over mix the dough after the water has been added- dough will become tough!

Submitter: 
CJR
Description: 
This recipe has been passed down in my family for many generations... This is the ONLY pie crust recipe I ever use, and anyone who tastes it just has to have the recipe!
