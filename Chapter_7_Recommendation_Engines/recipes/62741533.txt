Title:
Buttermilk Coconut Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup of Butter(1 stick)
1 Cup Sugar
1/2 Cup Buttermilk
1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1/2 Teaspoon Coconut Extract

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine sugar, buttermilk, butter, and baking soda; boil for 7 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in coconut.
