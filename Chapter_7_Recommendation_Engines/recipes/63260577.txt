Title:
TC's Pasta Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces uncooked pasta
1 cup julienned sweet red pepper
1 tablespoon canola oil
3/4 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cubed
4 green onions, sliced
1/2 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1 inch pieces
3/4 cup chicken broth
1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon grated lemon peel
2/3 cup frozen peas, thawed
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon water
2 tablespoons reduced fat sour cream
1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook fettuccine according to package directions. Drain, set aside; keep warm.
Meanwhile, in a 12-in. nonstick skillet, saute chicken in oil for 1 minute. Stir in the red pepper; saute another minute.  Stir in asparagus, broth, lemon juice, salt, thyme and lemon peel. Cook for 1 minute or until asparagus is crisp-tender.
Stir in peas and green onions; saute for 1 minute or until heated through.
Combine cornstarch and water until smooth; stir into chicken mixture.
Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 1-2 minutes or until thickened and chicken is no longer pink.
Remove from the heat; stir in sour cream.
Transfer to a large bowl. Add pasta to chicken mixture. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Thisni Caza
Description: 
TC's version of a mild lemon sauce seasoned with thyme, lightly coating this attractive medley of tender chicken, pasta, asparagus, red pepper and peas. Original from Marietta Howell of Okmulgee, Oklahoma.Modified from: Fettuccine Primavera
