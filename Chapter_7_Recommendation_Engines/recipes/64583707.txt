Title:
Frozen Amaretto Sour Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3684481.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C Sweet and sour mix
4 C Lemon lime soda
1 1/3 C Amaretto
10 oz jar Maraschino cherries, drained

Directions:
Pour sweet and sour mix into empty ice cube trays and freeze until solid.  Pour lemon lime soda into empty ice cube trays and freeze until solid.
Place frozen sweet and sour mix, frozen lemon lime soda, amaretto, and maraschino cherries into blender and blend on high until smooth.

The classic smooth, sweet, and tangy drink gets a makeover for summer!
