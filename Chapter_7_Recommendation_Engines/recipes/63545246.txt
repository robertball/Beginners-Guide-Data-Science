Title:
Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/968368.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Sq unweetened chocolate
1 stick butter
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup self rising flour
1 tsp vanilla
2 eggs

Directions:
Melt butter and sugar together. Add in eggs, one at a time. Add chocolate, stir until melted. Add remaining ingrediants. Bake in pre-heated oven at 325 for 25 minutes.

Brownie type pie. Easy and quick to make.
