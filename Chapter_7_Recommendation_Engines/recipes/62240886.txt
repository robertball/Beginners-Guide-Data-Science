Title:
Protein Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/329972.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup rolled oats
4 egg whites
Optional add ons:
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp Splenda
1 tbs soy protein
1/4 cup blueberries
1 tbs agave nectar

Directions:
Separate egg whites into a small bowl and blend in your choice of cinnamon, vanilla, Splenda, or soy protein. Fold in rolled oats.
In a non stick pan add a small amount of olive oil over medium heat. Shape mixture into a circular pancake and add fresh blueberries (or frozen blueberries microwaved for 30 seconds) on top.
After about 2 minutes flip and let cook for another 2 minutes or until done.
Drizzle with agave nectar

Submitter: 
Brittany
Description: 
A delicious and healthy way to start your day! Egg whites mixed with rolled oats and your choice of toppings will leave you feeling full and satisfied
