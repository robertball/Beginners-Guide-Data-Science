Title:
ORANGE STREUSEL LOAVES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 PKG(18 OZ)WHITE CAKE MIX
1 PKG (30Z) ORANGE GELATIN
2 TBSP BROWN SUGAR
1 TSP CINNAMON
1 TBSP BUTTER, SOFTENED
1 PKG (4 SERVING SIZE) VANILLA INSTANT PUDDING MIX
4 EGGS
1 CUP SOUR CREAM
1/3 CUP VEGATABLE OIL
GLAZE:
1 CUP POWDERED SUGAR
1-2 TBSP MILK OR ORANGE JUICE

Directions:
COMBINE CAKE MIX AND ORANGE GELATIN.  PLACE 2 TBSP OF CAKE/GELATIN MIX, BROWN SUGAR AND BUTTER IN A MEDIUM BOWL.  MIX WELL WITH FORK UNTIL CRUMBLY.  SET ASIDE.
COMBINE REMAINING CAKE/GELATIN MIX, PUDDING MIX, EGGS, SOUR CREAM,AND OIL N LARGE BOWL.  BEAT AT MEDIUM SPEAD FOR ABOUT THREE MINUTES.  POUR BATTER INTO 2 GREASED AND FLOURED 9x5 INCH LOAF PANS.  SPRINKLE WITH STREUSEL MIXTURE.  BAKE AT 350 FOR 50-60 MINUTES OR UNTIL TOOTHPICK COMES OUT CLEAN.  COOL IN PANS FOR 15 MINUTES.  LOOSEN LOAVES FROM PANS AND INVERT ONTO COOLING RACK.  COOL COMPLETELY.
FOR GLAZE, COMBINE POWDERED SUGAR ANDMILK OR ORANGE JUICE UNTIL SMOOTH.  DRIZZLE OVER COOLED LOAVES.

Submitter: 
Diane Blanchard Angell

THIS IS A WONDERFUL, MOIST BREAD FOR ANY HOLIDAY! SINCE IT MAKES TWO LOAVES, THERE'S ONE FOR MY FAMILY AND ONE TO SHARE - IF YOU CAN PART WITH IT!
