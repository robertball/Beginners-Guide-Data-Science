Title:
Apple Cinnamon Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter softened
1/4c firmly packed light brown sugar
1c flour
1/4 c quick cooking oats
1/4 finely chopped walnuts
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 pkgs cream cheese
1 can Eagle brand
3 eggs
1/2c frozen apple juice concentrate, thawed
2 medium apple cored

Directions:
Beat 1/2 c butter and sugar til fluffy.
Add flour, oats, nuts, cinnamon; mix well. Press firmly on bottom and halfway up side of 9" springform pan. Bake 300 for 10 minutes
Beat cream cheese until fluffy. Gradually beat Eagle brand until smooth. Add eggs and juice mix well. Pour into prepared pan. Bake 45 min or until springs back when touched lightly. Cool
In large skiller Cook apples in remaining 1 tbsp butter til tender. Arrange on top of cheesecake srizzle With glaze. * Chill.
Glaze: In small saucepan, combine 1/4 c frozen apple juice concentrate, thawed, t tsp cornstarch and 1/4 c cinnamon; mix well. Over low heat, cook and stir until thickened. Makes about 1/4 c.
