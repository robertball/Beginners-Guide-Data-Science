Title:
My Mom's Chupe (Venezuelan) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large onion (diced)
3 Russet potatoes (peeled and diced small)
4 tbsp of butter
3 chicken breasts (diced)
8 cups of water
2 cups of corn (I prefer frozen)
milk
4 mozzarella sticks (chopped coarse)
salt
pepper

Directions:
Melt butter in a large pot
add onions and potatoes
cook on medium heat, stirring constantly, until tender but not brown
turn heat to med-high and add chicken
cook until chicken is white
add water and bring to a simmer
add corn and bring to a simmer again
season to taste
Serve hot to boil, add milk and chunks of mozarella

Submitter: 
Sara Perry
Description: 
The Venezuelan version of chicken noodle soup, I still make it every time I have a cold... and my foster kids beg me to make more!
