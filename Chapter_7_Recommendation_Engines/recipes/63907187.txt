Title:
Best Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
1/4 C Onion, chopped
1/4 C Green Pepper,chopped
1/2 12 oz btl Chili Sauce
1   C  Ketchup
1/4 Brown Sugar
1/2 t. prepared mustard (dry)
Hamburger Buns

Directions:
Brown Ground Beef in 2qt saucepan on med., drain fat.  Add onion, and gr pepper.  Stir in Chili Sauce (Heinz), and ketchup (I like Heinz), and mustard.  Simmer for 15 min., serve on hamburger buns.  Enjoy!
