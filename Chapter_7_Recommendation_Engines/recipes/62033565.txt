Title:
Coke Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can coca cola
1 pkg dry onion soup mix
4 carrots, cut and cleaned
3 med potatoes, cut and peeled
1 med onion, cut into 8-10 wedges
1 3-5 lb. roast

Directions:
Add vegetables to crock pot, place roast on top of veggies. Add onion soup mix, then pour coke on top. Cover. Turn crock pot on low for 8-10 hours. Done

Submitter: 
ALISA
Description: 
Very easy and delicious recipe...worth the wait. Very tender.
