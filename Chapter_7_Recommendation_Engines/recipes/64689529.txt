Title:
Heritage Scalloped Potatoes and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5102299.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds potatoes, thinly sliced
3 onions slice thinly
7 tablespoon butter
1/4 cup flour
1 can 14.5 ounce chicken broth less sodium
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/8 teaspoon ground pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
paprika

Directions:
In a Aluminum Half Size Deep, 9x13 add a layer of potatoes. Salt and pepper layer to taste and dust with flour. Slice 2 tablesppon of butter and spread out over layer and finally layer onion on top.
Repeat process of potato, salt and pepper, flour, butter, and onion.
Add another layer of potato, salt and pepper to taste, and layer onion.
In a medium saucepan, melt buter and stir in flour. Cook until frothy.
Gradually stir in broth, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper. Stir frequently until sauce bubbles and thickens. Pour over potatoes.
Sprinkle paprika over the top, and bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hour.
