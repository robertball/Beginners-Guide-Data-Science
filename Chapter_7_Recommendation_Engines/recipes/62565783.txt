Title:
Monster Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 lb. brown sugar
2 c. granulated sugar
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 tsp. clear syrup
4 tsp. baking soda
1/2 lb. butter (not margarine)
1 1/2 lb. peanut butter
9 c. regular oatmeal (lg. container)
6 oz. pkg. chocolate chips
1/2 lb. M&Ms or Reeses Pieces

Directions:
Mix ingredients in order given.
Grease cookie sheets.
Drop cookies 6-9 per sheet (1/2 ice cream scoop ea.; flatten slightly with bottom of glass.)
Bake 350 degrees 8-12 min., until slightly brown on edges. Cookies will be soft. Do not overcook!
Let cookies cool slightly on cookie sheet to firm up before moving to cooling rack/newspapers to cool.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Recipe from a friend in Lincoln, NE; 1980's. 
These were the most requested cookies by the kids when growing up.  
Full batch makes a LOT of cookies, but dough keeps well in refrigerator for up to one week for later baking.
