Title:
Jello Magic Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups boiling water
1 pkg jello
1 tub cool whip

Directions:
Stir boiling water into dry Jello mix in medium bowl at least 2 minutes until completely dissolved.
Add whipped topping; stir with wire whisk until cool whip is completely melted and mixture is well blended.
Refrigerate 8 hours or until firm. If using a mould, unmould dessert to serving plate just before serving.
