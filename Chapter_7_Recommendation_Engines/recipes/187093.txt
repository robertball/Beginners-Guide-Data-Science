Title:
Snack S'mores for One Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990800.jpg
Ingredients:
4 RITZ Crackers
8 JET-PUFFED Miniature Marshmallows
1 teaspoon miniature semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Place 2 of the crackers on microwaveable plate; top evenly with marshmallows and chocolate chips.
Microwave on HIGH 10 to 15 seconds or until marshmallows are puffed.
Top with remaining crackers. Press top crackers lightly into marshmallows to secure.

Submitter: 
RITZ
Description: 
Satisfy that craving for S'mores with RITZ crackers, miniature marshmallows and chocolate chips.
