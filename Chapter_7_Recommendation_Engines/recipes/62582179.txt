Title:
Original beef burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs lean ground sirloin
1 medium onion - grated
2 cloves of garlic - grated
1 egg
1 tsp seasoning salt (or to taste)
2 tsp hamburger spice mix
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp allspice

Directions:
Mix all spices together.
add all ingredients together and mix well.
refrigerate for 30 min
shape into patties as per desired thickness.
broil in a 400 degree preheated oven till cooked through.
