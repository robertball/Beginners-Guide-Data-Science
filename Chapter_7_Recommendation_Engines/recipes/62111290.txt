Title:
EASY ASIAN DIPPING SAUSE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 CUP SOY SAUSE
1/2 CUP RICE WINE VINEGAR
2 TABLESPOONS HONEY
3 CLOVES GARLIC MINCED
2 TABLESPOONS FRESH GINGER MINCED
2 TEASPOONS SESAME SEEDS
2 TEASPOONS SESAME OIL

Directions:
MIX ALL INGREDANTS & SERVE IN PERSONNAL DIPPING DISHES. BE SURE TO SCOOP GARLIC & GINGER INTO EACH BOWL.
