Title:
Broccoli Harvest Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cups chopped fresh broccoli
1/4 cup raisins
1 cup unpeeled, cored and chopped Red Delicious apples
1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
1/4 cup bacon bits
1/3 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tsp. sugar

Directions:
1) In a large bowl, combine broccoli, raisins, apples, and onion. Stir in bacon bits and cheese.
2) In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, vinegar and sugar. Add mayonnaise mix to broccoli mix. Mix well to combine.
3) Cover and refrigerate for at least 20 minutes. Gently stir again just before serving.
