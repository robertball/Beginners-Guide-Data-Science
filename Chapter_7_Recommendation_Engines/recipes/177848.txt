Title:
Chicken and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981803.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1/2 cup milk
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 cups cubed cooked chicken or turkey
3 cups cooked medium egg noodles or bow tie pasta
Chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Mix soup, milk, black pepper, cheese, chicken and noodles in saucepan.  Heat through.  Garnish with parsley.
