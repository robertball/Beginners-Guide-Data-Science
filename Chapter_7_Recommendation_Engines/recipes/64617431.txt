Title:
English Muffin Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/2 cups GP Flour
1/3 cup Non-Fat Dry Milk
1/4 tsp Baking Powder
2 tsp Kosher Salt
2 tbsp Instant Dry Yeast
2 1/2 cups Hot water (115° - 120°)
1 tbsp Honey
2 7

Directions:
Combine Flour, Yeast, Non-Fat Dry and Baking Powder
Combine water and honey
Combine water with dry mixture and begin mixing.
Add salt
Mix until a batter-like dough forms.
Divide mixture in two and spoon into two greased and cornmeal dusted bread pans.
Preheat oven to 425°
Allow loaves to double in size then CAREFULLY place in oven and cook for 15 min or until reach an internal temp of 180° - 200°.
Remove loaves and allow to cool in pans for a few minute. Remove loaves from pans and allow to finish cooling on racks.
Wait until loaves are cooled before slicing.

Submitter: 
Dave Niezabitowski Sr.
Description: 
Enjoy the nooks and crannies of the familiar English Muffin in a quick and easy to make loaf.
