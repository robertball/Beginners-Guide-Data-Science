Title:
Southern Style Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
# 1 large head green cabbage, cored, and very finely shredded
# 3 Kirby (pickling) cucumbers, peeled, seeded and sliced paper thin
# 2 tbs kosher salt
# For Dressing:
# 1/2 c white vinegar
# 1/2 c granulated sugar
# 1/2 tsp salt
# 1 tbs dijon-style mustard
# 1/4 c vegetable oil
# 1/4 c heavy cream
# 2 tbs sour cream
# salt and fresh ground pepper to tast

Directions:
* Mix together the shredded cabbage and sliced cucumber in a large colander
* Toss well with the salt and leave 20 minutes o
* Squeeze the slaw firmly by handfuls to extract as much liquid as possible, then use your fingers to toss and loosen the squeezed slaw
* Toss it into a large bowl
* Dressing:
* Bring the vinegar, sugar, and salt to a boil in a small saucepan over medium heat, stirring just until the sugar is dissolved.
* Boil for three minutes and whisk in the mustard and oil
* Allow to cool slightly before stirring in the heavy cream and sour cream.
* Taste and adjust the salt and pepper
In a pan, throw in large cup of of cabbage mixture, and drizzle sauce over it.  Cook about 2 -3 minutes.  Serve immediately.
Sauce can be refrigerated for a couple of days.

Description:
