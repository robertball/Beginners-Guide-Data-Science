Title:
Fake Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can crescent rolls
1/2 stick softened butter
Sugar
Cinnamon

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 (or whatever the crescent roll directions call for).
2. Open the crescent rolls and spread a generous amount of butter on them.
3. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top of the butter and roll them up.
4. Place the rolled up rolls on a greased cookie sheet.
5. Brush with melted butter and sprinkle a little cinnamon and sugar on top.
6. Bake for 13-15 minutes.
