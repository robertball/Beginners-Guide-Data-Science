Title:
White Cake Frosting I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3781158.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup shortening
1/4 cup water
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon clear imitation vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon powdered butter flavoring
4 cups confectioners' sugar

Directions:
With an electric mixer blend all ingredients until mixed.

Submitter: 
APARSONS
Description: 
White wedding cake frosting. This is a very good frosting for decorated cakes.
