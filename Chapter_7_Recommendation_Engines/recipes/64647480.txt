Title:
LTF DTox: Mediterranean Chicken Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 clove garlic, diced
1 cup uncooked quinoa
2 large chicken breasts, cooked and cut into bite size pieces
1 large red onion, diced
1 large green bell pepper, diced
1/2 cup chopped Kalamata olives
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
1 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup olive oil
baby spinach (optional)

Directions:
1. Add garlic to water and bring to a boil.
2. Stir in quinoa; reduce heat to medium-low; cover and simmer until quinoa is tender and the water has been absorbed (15-20 min).
3. Scrape the quinoa into a large bowl.
4. Stir in chicken, onion, bell pepper, olive, parsley, chives and salt.
5. Mix lemon juice, balsamic vinegar and olive oil.
6. Drizzle mixture over the salad and stir until evenly mixed.
7. Serve warm or cold over a bed of baby spinach.
