Title:
Okra Tamarind  Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp oil, separated
1/4 tsp black mustard seed
3-4 curry leaves (optional)
1 small-medium onion, sliced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups of frozen cut okra, thawed
1 can diced tomatoes
2 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (or to taste)
1 tsp ground dried turmeric
2 tsp salt (or to taste)
2.5 cups water
1/2 tbsp tamarind concentrate
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
1. Fry Okra in about 1tbs or oil. Cook until the "stickiness" of the okra is no longer visible. About 10 minutes.
2. Heat 1 tbsp of oil on medium heat with mustard seeds until the mustard seeds pops (about 5 minutes). Then add in curry leaves (30 seconds).
3. Add onions and garlic, fry 5 minutes.
4. Add tomatoes and cooked okra, cook 3-5 minutes.
5. Add all spices (cumin, coriander, black pepper,  turmeric, salt). Mix well.
6. Add water and bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer. Simmer for 3-5 minutes.
7. Turn off heat and stir in tamarind concentrate and chopped cilantro. Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Sarah Garland
Description: 
Cheap and delicious, this soupy vegetarian curry goes wonderful with cooked rice. Anti-veggie eaters love this enough to go for seconds, and are surprised that okra tastes good!.
