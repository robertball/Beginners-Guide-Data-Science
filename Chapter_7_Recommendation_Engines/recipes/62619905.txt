Title:
Southwest Chipotle Chicken & Black Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (28 oz) Hunt's diced tomatoes
2 cans Goya black beans (not strained)
1-2 boneless, skinless chicken breast
1 package McCormick spicy chili seasoning
4 Tbsp McCormick Grill Mates Cinnamon Chipotle Rub (to taste)
1 TBsp McCormick Chili Powder
Ground cayenne red pepper to taste
Sliced pickled jalapenos + juice to taste (or fresh, seeded jalapenos)

Directions:
Mix tomatoes, black beans, McCormick chili seasoning, McCormick Grill Mates Cinnamon Chipotle Rub, McCormick Chili Powder in large cooking pot on medium high heat.
In another pot, boil chicken breast until thoroughly cooked. Shred chicken by hand and place in chili mix.
Add red pepper, jalapenos, extra chipotle mix (and any other peppers or favorite ingredients to taste).
Bring to boil. Set on low heat and cover, or transfer to crock pot/slow cooker and allow to slowly cook until desired thickness is achieved.

Submitter: 
Mark Bender
Description: 
The favorite among favorite chili's.  Chipotle spices and unique flavors give this a unique taste all its own.
