Title:
Yummy chicken soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Homemade chicken broth (made with carrots and onions)
Chicken stock from Costco
Shredded cooked chicken meat
Sweet potatoes, peeled, cut into bit sized pieces
Carrots, ditto
Zucchini and/or summer squash, sliced
Rice or other grain
Can of black beans, rinsed, drained
Can of crushed tomatoes
Seasonings such as Italian blend, salt

Directions:
Bring broth to a simmer with the potatoes and carrots.  Also add the grain.  When half cooked, add squash.  When almost done, add remaining ingredients.  Simmer.

Submitter: 
None
Description: 
The sweet potatoes and black beans are key.
