Title:
Meat Loaves  Italiano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup oats
2 8oz cans tomato sauce
1 2 1/2oz. can sliced black olives
1 egg
1 small onion minced
1/2 teaspoon italian seasoning
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 pound ground beef
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 cup shredded mozzerella cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees. In bowl combine oats, 1/4 cup tomato sauce, 1/4 cup olives, egg, onion, italian seasoning and salt and ground beef.Mix well. Shape into 4 4 by 2 inch loaves; place in baking dish. Bake for 25- 30min drain fat. Meanwhile combine remaing tomato sauce, olives and garlic cook over low heat until hot. Spoon sauce over loaves sprinkle with cheese and bake 2 minutes until cheese melts.
