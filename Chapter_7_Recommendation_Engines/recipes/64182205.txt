Title:
My Spicy Chicken Thai Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tube prepared lemongrass
2 T pre-minced garlic
3 T pre-minced ginger
6 cups chicken broth
1 tablespoon coconut oil
2 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut in half
12 ounces fresh white mushrooms, quartered
2 teaspoons red curry paste
3 tablespoons fish sauce
1 lime, juiced
1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
1 red onion, sliced
1/2 bunch cilantro, roughly chopped
1 lime, cut into wedges, for serving
1 fresh jalapeno pepper, sliced into rings

Directions:
Heat coconut oil in a large soup pot over medium heat; Stir in chicken; cook and stir until chicken starts browning.
Stir in mushrooms and cook for 5 more minutes.
Stir in chicken broth. Stir in lemongrass, ginger, garlic, red curry paste, fish sauce, and lime juice until combined. Add coconut milk; return to a simmer and cook on low for 15 to 20 minutes.
Skim off any excess oil and fat that rises to the top and discard.
Stir red onion into the chicken mixture; cook and stir until onion softens, about 5 minutes.
Remove from heat and add about 1/2 the cilantro.
Serve with plates of cilantro, lime wedges, and fresh sliced jalapenos.

Description: 
This Thai-inspired soup is full of chunks of tender chicken and mushrooms, and is rich and spicy with the addition of coconut milk, red curry paste, ginger, and garlic.Modified from: Spicy Chicken Thai Soup
