Title:
Mom's Salmon, Pasta and Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of bowtie pasta
1 cup of frozen peas
1/2 pond of salmon
1/2 cup of parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook the pasta according to directions on the box.  Defrost the peas in the microwave. Set aside.  Briefly cook salmon in the microwave for about 1 minute.  Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and put it in a pan.  Turn on the heat and add the cooked salmon, peas and cheese. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Julia L
Description: 
My mom has made this so many times and her recipe never fails.  I hope you enjoy!
