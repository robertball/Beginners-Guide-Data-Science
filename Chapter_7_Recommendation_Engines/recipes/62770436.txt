Title:
Company Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 boned chicken breasts, skinned
4-6 bacon slices
1/4 lb. chipped dried beef
2 cans Cream of Mushroom or Cream of Chicken soup
1 12 oz. sour cream

Directions:
Pound breast to flatten, then rolland wrap with bacon and secure with toothpick.
Butter a casserole dish and lay slices of chipped beef on the bottom.
Place chicken rolls on top.
Mix soups and sour cream in a bowl, then pour over chicken.
Cover with foil or lid and bake 3 hours at 300 degrees F.
Quick method
Boil chicken breasts for 25 min.
Remove skin and bones.
Place chicken on dried beef in a greased casserole.
Place backon slices on top
Broil until bacon is partially cooked.
Mix soups and sour cream together then pour over top.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 30-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
There are two methods to prepare this dish.(long and short.)Both are equally delicious. This chicken recipe is very nice to bring to someone home from the hospital and not really feeling well enough to cook for themselves or their family, or it's a great dish to prepare for special guests.
