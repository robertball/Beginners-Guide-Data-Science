Title:
Mexican Hot Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tablespoon instant coffee
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 3/4 cup flour
2/3 cup cocoa powder
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1-2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup shredded sweet coconut
2 cup milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a large bowl, cream butter, sugar, eggs, instant coffee and vanilla extract until fluffy. Combine dry ingredients in a seperate bowl. Slowly add dry to wet until blended. Fold in coconuts and chocolate chips.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until set. Cool slightly on the cookie sheet before transferring to cooling rack. Yield: About 5 dozen.

Submitter: 
mkecupcakequeen
Description: 
A double chocolate cookie inspired by the flavors of mexico. Great for dipping in coffee or milk, leaves a warm taste on the tongue. Spice to taste, coconut is optional, but makes for a great texture.
