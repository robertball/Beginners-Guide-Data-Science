Title:
Chicken Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 grilled chicken breast, diced into bite size pieces
24 oz bowtie pasta
1pt whipping cream
1/2c diced cooked bacon
1/4c scallions, thinly coined
1/4c chopped parsley
1c parmesan, grated
salt, pepper, & butter to taste

Directions:
Boil pasta unti soft.  Saute bacon, scallions, and parsley for 5 minutes in butter. Add cream and heat to 80*. Add cheese, salt, pepper until sauce begins to thicken. Add chicken, simmer for 3 minutes. Pour over  pasta & toss before serving.
