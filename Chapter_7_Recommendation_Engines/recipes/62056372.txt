Title:
Yellow Cake Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
4 eggs
1 stick margarine
3 cups powdered sugar
1 (8 oz) pkg. cream cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Mix cake mix, ONE egg and stick of margarine. Spread in a 9 x 13 pan.
Beat powdered sugar, three eggs and cream cheese until smooth. Pour over cake mix layer. Bake 30-40 minutes at 350.

Submitter: 
MRSMTAYLOR
Description: 
Makes an easy, last-minute dessert! Looks and tastes impressive even though it's so simple to make. The cream cheese and yellow cake is a tasty combination.
