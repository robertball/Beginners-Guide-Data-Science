Title:
MOCHA FUDGE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP. BUTTER
2/3 CUP EVAPORATED MILK
1-2/3 CUPS SUGAR
1/2 TSP SALT
2 CUPS MINI MARSHMALLOWS
1 CUP CHOCOLATE CHIPS
1/2 CUP BUTTERSCOTCH CHIPS
1 TBSP INSTANT COFFEE GRANULES
1 TSP VANILLA

Directions:
IN A HEAVY SAUCEPAN, COMBINE BUTTER, EVAPORATED MILK, SUGAR, AND SALT EVER MEDIUM HEAT.  BRING TO A BOIL AND COOK FOR A MINUTE OR SO, STIRRING CONSTANTLY.  REMOVE FROM HEAT, STIR IN MARSHMALLOWS, CHOCOLATE CHIPS, BUTTERSCOTCH CHIPS, INSTANT COFFEE, AND VANILLA.  STIR BRISKLY UNTIL MARSHMALLOWS ARE MELTED.  POUR INTO A BUTTERED 8-INCH SUARE PAN.  COOL WELL.

Submitter: 
Diane Blanchard Angell
Description: 
A FUDGE FOR COFFEE LOVERS MADE WITH CHOCOLATE AND BUTTERSCOTCH CHIPS
