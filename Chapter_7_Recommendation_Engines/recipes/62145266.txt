Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (2 sticks) butter, softened
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1 package (4 Servings) instant french vanilla pudding mix
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 1/2 cups flour
2 cups milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Mix butter, sugars, pudding mix (the dry mix - do not prepare), and vanilla until smooth.
Add the eggs.
Stir together the baking soda and flour.
Add the flour mixture to the butter/sugar mixture.
Stir in the chocolate chips.
Drop dough by the tablespoon on to a prepared cookie sheet.
Bake 375 degrees for 10 minutes.  Cookies will be soft but be sure not to overbake.  The cookies should be a golden brown color.
