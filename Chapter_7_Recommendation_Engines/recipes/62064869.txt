Title:
Easy Shrimp Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 12oz. bag frozen, cooked, tail-off, baby shrimp
flour tortillas
1 TBS. minced garlic
1 TBS. Olive oil
1 TBS butter
paprika
salt
Toppings:
cheddar cheese, shredded
tomato,diced
lettuce, shredded
sour cream
salsa

Directions:
Thaw shrimp in a colander under cool running water. Meanwhile, dice tomatoes, shred lettuce and cheese.
Heat oil and butter in skillet. Add shrimp and garlic and saute for 3 minutes maximum. While cooking shrimp, heat tortillas.
Layer shrimp,then cheese and desired toppings into tortilla.
Fold bottom up 1 inch and proceed to roll.

Submitter: 
Wineaux
Description: 
So fast, so easy, with just a few ingredients you probably already have. This is my 7 year old's most requested dinner!
