Title:
Chilled Indian Style Carrot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz can sliced carrots
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
2 green onions/scallions, sliced thinly
1/4 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp garam masala
pinch each of salt and cumin
2 8 oz cans sweet potato baby food, or mashed sweet potatoes
3 c vegetable or chicken broth
Plain yogurt (optional)

Directions:
In a dry sauce pan over low to medium heat, gently stir spices for 2-3 minutes, or until they become very fragrant. Be careful not to burn them!
Add olive oil, garlic, and onions. Gently saut√© until tender.
Strain and add carrots and half of the broth. Simmer for 5 or so minutes.
In a blender, puree the hot mixture.
Return puree to the sauce pan and add the sweet potatoes and the rest of the broth. Stir on medium heat and add a pinch more of the spices if desired.
Store in the refrigerator until chilled and serve with a dollop of plain yogurt.
