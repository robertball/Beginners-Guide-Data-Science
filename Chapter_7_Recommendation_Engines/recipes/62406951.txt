Title:
Crunchy Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs thinly sliced potato
16 oz sour cream
1/2 C butter, melted
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 C grated cheddar cheese
1 C diced onions
1/2 lb lean bacon, cooked and crumbled
salt and pepper to taste
2 C corn flakes
1/4 cup melted butter

Directions:
Precook potato in boiling water on stove until slightly crisp, about 5 minutes. Remove from stove and drain well. Mix all other ingredients together except corn flakes and 1/4 C butter. Start with the potatoesand cover with 1/2 the saucein a buttered casserole. The another laver of potatoes and the other 1/4 cup sauce. Combine corn flakes and butter and sprinkle over the top. Bake at 350 for 1 hour or until potatoes are done and sauce is bubbly.
