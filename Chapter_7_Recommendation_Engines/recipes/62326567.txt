Title:
Pepper Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups flour
2 tbsp. sugar
2 tsp. course ground pepper
1 tsp. salt
2 pkgs. Rapid Rise Yeast
1 1/4 cups water
3 tbsp. soft margarine
1 egg
1 egg yolk
1 tbsp. water

Directions:
Combine 3 cups flour, sugar, pepper, salt and yeast.
Heat 1 1/4 cups water & margarine 130 degrees. Add flour mixture, 1 egg and last of flour. Knead 4 min.
Divide 12 pieces and arrange in bundt pan. Set pan in hot water for 15 min. Brush top of dough with 1 egg yolk and 1 tbsp. water. Bake 375 degrees 25 min.

Submitter: 
jbertsch
Description: 
This recipe was passed on to me by my aunt and we used to beg for her to make this as children. The pepper is slight in comparison to the overall taste. The brushed egg yolk mixture gives the bread a crunchier outside and soft inside.
