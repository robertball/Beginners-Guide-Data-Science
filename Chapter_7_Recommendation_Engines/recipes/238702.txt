Ingredients:
2 tablespoons salt
2 teaspoons white sugar
3/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon cornstarch

Directions:
Whisk salt, sugar, paprika, turmeric, onion powder, garlic powder, and cornstarch together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
hobbyzu
Description: 
Easy enough! Mix up a batch of this and store it in an airtight container for future use.

This is a great substitute. I increased the paprika a bit for more color.
