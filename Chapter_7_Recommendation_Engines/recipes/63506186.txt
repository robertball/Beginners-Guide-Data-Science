Title:
Pico de gallo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/957865.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large vine ripened tomatoes, diced
1 medium sized white onion, diced
1/4 bunch of cilantro chopped, (use more or less depending on your taste)
2 jalapenos, diced (use more or less depending on your taste)
1/2 teaspoon of minced garlic
juice of half a lemon
1 tsp of salt
pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop and dice all ingredients and add to a bowl.
Squeeze lemon into bowl and mix ingredients until well distributed.
Add salt and pepper, combine and taste.
Add more pepper if needed.
Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description: 
Nothing is better with homemade Mexican food than a fresh beautiful bowl of Pico de gallo.
