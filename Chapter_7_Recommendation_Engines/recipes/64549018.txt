Title:
Kicked up Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked rice (2 cups uncooked)
1 cup sour cream
1 small can of chopped green chilies
8 oz of shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cook the rice according to package directions.
While still hot, stir in remaining ingredients until well blended.
Put in a pyrex baking dish and bake at 350 degrees (F) until heated through. (About 15-20 minutes)

Submitter: 
Rollyne Klem
Description: 
My mother used to make this at the holidays. One of the family favorites. Cheesy rice with a green chili kick, great with beef or ham!
