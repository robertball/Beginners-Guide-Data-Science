Title:
Maple-Balsamic Vinaigrette with a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small shallot, chopped
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup maple syrup
1 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1 tsp prepared horseradish (not cream-style)
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup extra-virgin olive oil

Directions:
Place shallot, vinegar, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, horseradish, salt, and pepper into a blender. Pulse to combine, then add the olive oil in a steady stream with the motor running.
