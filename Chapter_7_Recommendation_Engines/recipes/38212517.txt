Title:
White Chocolate Raspberry Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
2 bricks of chocolate grahams, crushed
2 sticks butter, melted
1 tablespoon sugar
Filling
3 8oz packages cream cheese, softened
3 large eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
3/4 cup sugar
Mousse
2 cups heavy whipping cream, cold
2 tablespoons sugar
1 10oz jar seedless raspberry jam
3 teaspoons Knox gelatin (unflavored)
3 tablespoons cold water
1/2 cup white chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a springform pan, mix grahams, sugar and melted butter until crumbly.
Press mixture into bottom and part way up the sides.
Bake crust for 8 minutes and remove from oven, cool for 10 minutes, then place in refrigerator.
For filling, place cream cheese and sugar into mixer, mix completely.  Add eggs, one at a time, until incorporated.
Add vanilla.
Lower oven temperature to 325.
Pour mixture into crust, bake for 50-60 minutes.
Watch carefully at the last 10 minutes.
Remove from oven and allow to cool at room temperature for 1 hour.
Place into fridge for a minimum of 2 hours.
For the mousse, sprinkle gelatin over water, stir and let sit one minute.
Microwave on high for 30 second.
Combine gelatin with jam, place in fridge for 10 minutes.
Whip heavy cream until soft peaks, add sugar and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
Reserve 1/4 cup of whipped cream, fold the remaining cream with the jam mixture.
Spread mousse onto cooled cheesecake.
Place reserved whipped cream into a piping bag (or ziptop bag) and dollop cream around the edge and in the center.
Sprinkle the chips around the cake, over the whipped cream.
Refrigerate 4 hours to overnite.
