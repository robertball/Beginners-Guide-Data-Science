Title:
Italian Sweet Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 tbsp butter
1/2 tsp salt
3 tbsp lukewarm water
1/4 pound (250grams) ricotta cheese
2 1/8 cups flour
1 egg white
2 egg yolks
1/4 tsp cinnamon
2 tbsp confectioners sugar
1/2 tsp almond extract
vegetable oil or canola oil for frying.

Directions:
1. Combine 2 eggs, butter , flour,salt and water, and knead 1 minute until dough is smooth and stiff(can us an electric mixture for this part).Let stand 5 minutes.
2.Beat ricotta cheese, egg white, egg yolks,cinnamon and sugar together until creamy.
3. Divide dough into 2 parts. Roll into paper thin sheets and cut 2 rounds(using a small biscuit cutter) for each ravioli.
4.Place 1tsp of the riccota filling in the center of 1 round, cover with the second round, and press edges together firmly with a fork.
5. Fry in oil in large saute pan or pot .Oil should be deep enough to cover ravioli .Heat oil to about 370F until golden brown, about 3 minutes.
6.Remove from oil with slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
6.Sprinkle with additional confectioners sugar or roll in fine sugar.Serve immediately
