Title:
Crowd Pleaser Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion
1 16 oz. sour cream
7-10 med to large yellow squash
8 tbsp. butter or marg.
2 packs ritz crackers

Directions:
Cut ends off of squash and cut into 1/4 in. sliceses.
Place squash in large pot and boil.
Chop onion and place in pan with 4 oz. butter and sautee.
When squash is nice and soft place in strainer and stir with large fork to drain water and also break apart the squash to desire.
In baking dish place squash, onion, sour cream and cheese and combine by stirring.
Top with ritz crackers pouring remaining 4 oz. melted butter on top. Bake at 375 for 30-45 min.

Submitter: 
Heather Thompson Szeder
Description: 
Simple and mouth watering squash casserole. A few simple ingredients makes this easy to please everyone!
