Title:
Ed's Ice Box Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of graham cracker crumbs
16 oz. small marshmallows
2 (10 oz) jars of torn cherries (undrained)
Pecans
1 tsp. of Pet

Directions:
Mix ingredients and form into log. Wrap in waxed paper, then tin foil. Freeze.
