Title:
Biko (Sweet Rice) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups sticky rice
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup coconut milk

Directions:
Cook rice in 1-3/4 cup water until done.
Cook sugar and coconut milk in small saucepan until syrupy consistency.
Add syrup to rice and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
