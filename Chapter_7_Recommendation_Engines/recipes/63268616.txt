Title:
Carol's Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. potatoes
9 eggs
1/2 c. miracle whip
1/2 c. mayonnaise
2 1/2 T. mustard
1/2 t. salt
1 1/2 T. sugar

Directions:
Wash, cube, and boil potatoes. Let cool.
Boil, cool, peel, and dice eggs.
In a bowl, mix together potatoes, eggs, and all other ingredients.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
Delicious, sweet, and a great picnic salad.
