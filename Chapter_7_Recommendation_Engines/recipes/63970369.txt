Title:
Vegetable Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500 grams beef filet
2 bay leaves
1 red onion
2 cloves
2 Tablespoons vegetable broth powder
1 walnut size piece of ginger
500 grams potatoes
1 red pepper
1 kohlrabi
1 carrot
1 parsley root
250 grams broccoli
nutmeg

Directions:
Cut everything into into bite size pieces. Brown beef in a pan with cloves and bay leaves. Add everything with 1.5 L water and cook for 35 minutes.
