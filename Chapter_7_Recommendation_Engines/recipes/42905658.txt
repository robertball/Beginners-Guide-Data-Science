Title:
Fresh Vegetable Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 green onions, chopped
2-3 medium tomatoes, diced
1 sm can chopped ripe olives
1 sm can diced green chilis
1 tsp olive oil
1 tsp vinegar
garlic salt
tobasco sauce

Directions:
Mix all together in small bowl, Garlic salt and tobasco to taste
