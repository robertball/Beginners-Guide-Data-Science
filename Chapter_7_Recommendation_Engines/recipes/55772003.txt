Title:
Key Lime Cheesecake II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/245796.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups finely ground graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup coconut, toated
1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
24 ounces cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup white sugar
1 cup sour cream
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
4 eggs
1/2 cup key lime juice
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1/4 cup miringue powder
1/4 cup key lime juice
1/2 cup sugar

Directions:
To make Crust: Tightly wrap outside of 9&#34;; springform pan in 2 layers of aluminum foil.  In a bowl stir together the graham cracker crumbs and coconut, stir in the butter well.  Pat the mixture into the bottom and 1/2 inch up the side of pan and chill the crust in the refrigerator for 10 minutes.
In a large bowl with an electric mixer, beat cream cheese until smooth.  Add 1 cup sugar and continue to beat scraping down sides.  Beat in the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in the sour cream, flour, lime juice and vanilla. Add 1-2 drops electric green food coloring.  Beat the mixture until it is smooth.
Pour the filling over the crust.  Pour boiling water into roasting pan then place cheesecake into pan.  Water should come about halfway up sides of pan.  Bake the cheesecake in the preheated oven for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes, at 325 degrees F or until center is barely set. Remove cheesecake from roasting pan and peel off aluminum foil.  Let the cheesecake cool on a rack for 1 hour, then chill it, loosely covered, overnight.  Remove the cheesecake from the pan and transfer it to a cake stand.
In clean bowl combine 1 cup heavy cream, 1/4 cup key lime juice and 1/4 cup miringue powder and beat to until thick using whisk attachment.  Add sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks form.  Add 2 drops electric green food coloring.  Chill.  Put mousse mixture in pastry bag fitted with large star tip and pipe on top of cake.
