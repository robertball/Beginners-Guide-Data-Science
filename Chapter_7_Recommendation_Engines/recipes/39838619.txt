Title:
Salad Dressing for Fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T. cornstarch
1/2 c. sugar
1/8 t. salt
1 c. boiling water
1 T. lemon juice
Rind of 1 whole orange
Whipped cream

Directions:
Mix cornstarch, sugar, salt and boiling water, cook until dissolved.  Cool, add lemon juice and orange rind.  Fold into whipped cream (amount not specified, probably 1 pint)

Submitter: 
PacNWCook
Description: 
A citrus and creamy dressing to cover your diced apples, bananas, raisins and other less moist fruits you may want to add to a fruit salad.
