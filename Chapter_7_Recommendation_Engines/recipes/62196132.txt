Title:
Cranberry, Orange and Apple Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz bag fresh cranberries
1 cup chopped pecans
1 orange, peeled, seeded and cut in chunks
1 apple, cored and cut in chunks
1/2 an orange zest
1/2 cup Splenda
1/2 cup white sugar

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a food processor.  Pulse until you have small bits.  Add Splenda and sugar, and refrigerate for 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
Make this refreshing cranberry salad/relish while the cranberries are in the stores.  It's delicious!
