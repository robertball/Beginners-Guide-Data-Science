Title:
Joyce's Baked Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 strips bacon
½ cup onion
4 t. chicken bouillon
4 cups water
¼ t. white pepper
¼ t. black pepper
½-1 t. seasoned salt
¼ c. margarine
¼ c. flour
1 c. milk
1 pint whipping cream
1 cup cheddar cheese
3 green onion, sliced
8 potatoes, baked

Directions:
Bake the potatoes until done; set aside to cool.  When cooled, cut into cubes; set aside.  (Do NOT peel)  In a soup kettle, cook the diced bacon.  Saute the onion in the bacon drippings until translucent, about 5 minutes.  Add the chicken bouillon and water.  Let simmer about 15 minutes.  In a saucepan, melt the butter; stir in flour and whisk to a smooth consistency.  Slowly stir in milk making sure to avoid lumps. Reduce heat to low; cook for five minutes, stirring frequently.  Stir into chicken stock mixture.  Whisk until smooth.  Stir in potatoes and warm thoroughly.  Add the heavy cream and the cheddar cheese.  Sprinkle green onions in soup mugs.  Pour soup over the top and serve immediately.
