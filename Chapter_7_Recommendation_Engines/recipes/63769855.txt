Title:
Homemade Turkey Sausage Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1044885.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey or chicken
3/4 cup ground Rosemary Olive Oil Triscuits
1 egg
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp dried sage
1 tsp salt
butter

Directions:
Preheat griddle to medium high.
Mix all ingredients except butter.
Place mixture on cutting board/counter covered with wax paper.
Roll/flatten to 1/2 inch thickness.
Cut with 3" round biscuit cutter.
Gather unused mixture, flatten and cut until all mixture is used up.
Place patties on hot griddle.
Flip when sides of patties start turning grey.
Place a pat of butter on top of each patty allowing to melt. Spread if necessary.
Patties will be done when golden brown on both sides.

Submitter: 
Shantelle
Description: 
Easy and delicious!
Created these one morning when I realized I didn't have bacon or sausages for breakfast. My husband who's from Ireland says they remind him of the 'white pudding' they have back home.
