Title:
Mexican Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut bite-size
1 1/2 T. flour
2 t. olive oil
1 lb. sweet potatoes, peeled, cut bite-size
1 yellow pepper, cut bite-size
1 c. onion, chopped
2 c. water
1 (28 oz) can diced tomatoes
2 1/2 T. chili powder
1 T. minced garlic
1 t. dried oregano
1 c. frozen corn
1 (16 oz) can kidney beans, rinsed
1/2 c. chopped cilantro

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with flour; toss to coat.  Heat 1 1/2 t. oil in a nonstick Dutch oven.  Add chicken; saute' 5 min until cooked through.  Remove.
Heat remaining 1/2 t. oil.  Add sweet potatoes, pepper and onion, cover and cook 7 min. stirring often, until lightly colored.
Add water, tomatoes, chili powder, garlic and oregano.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer 10 min until vegetables are almost tender.
Add corn and beans; simmer 2 min until vegetables are tender.  Remove from heat, stir in cilantro.

Submitter: 
Tiffany Donnelly
Description: 
Chunky stew, with great chili and oregano overtones.
