Title:
Almost stuffed pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork loin chops, about 1" thick
2 T lime juice
3-2/3 c coarsely crumbled cracker crumbs
about 1C finely chopped mushrooms
3 scallions chopped
1 T dijon style mustard
1 T dried parley flakes

Directions:
preheat oven to 350, coat 9 x 13 baking dish with cooking spray
brush both sides of pork chops with lime juice and lay them in prepared baking dish
in a medium bowl, combine remaining ingredients, mix well and spoon over chops
bake 40 - 45 minutes
