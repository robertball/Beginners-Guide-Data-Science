Title:
Sausage Cheese Bread Log Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/840822.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tubes Pillsbury French Bread Loaf
1 lb. breakfast sausage
8 to 10 oz. shredded cheese (mix of cheddar & swiss)
2 eggs
Butter for brushing
Cooking spray

Directions:
Brown sausage and drain.  Take both loaves of bread out of tubes.  Combine both loaves into one.  Roll out with rolling pin to 15x12 rectangle.
In bowl, combine sausage, cheese and eggs.  Mix well and spread onto bread.  Roll up like jelly roll.  Place on greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.  Remove from oven and brush butter over bread.  Place back in oven and bake for an additional 5 minutes.
Cut into desired size of pieces and serve.
