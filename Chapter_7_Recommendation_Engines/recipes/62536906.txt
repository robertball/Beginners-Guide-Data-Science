Title:
Barbecued Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521147.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Pounds Brisket, Fresh
1 1/4 Cups Water, Divided
1 Whole Onion, Chopped
3 Cloves Garlic, Minced
1 Tablespoon Vegatable Oil
1 Cup Ketchup
3 Tablespoons Red Wine Vinegar
2 Tablespoons Lemon Juice
2 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
1 Tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
2 Teaspoons Corn Starch
1 Teaspoon Paprika
1 Teaspoon Chili Powder
1/4 Teaspoon Salt
1/4 Teaspoon Black Pepper
1/4 Teaspoon Liquid Barbecue Smoke

Directions:
Place brisket in a large Dutch oven. Add 1/2 cup water. Cover and bake at 275Â° for 2 hours.
Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan, saute onion and garlic in oil until tender. Add ketchup, vinegar, lemon juice, brown sugar, Worcestershire Sauce, cornstarch, paprika, chili powder, salt, pepper, and remaining water. Simmer, uncovered, for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
Add liquid smoke; mix well. Drain drippings from Dutch over. Pour sauce over
meat.
Cover and bake 1-2 hours longer or until meat is tender.

James Noble
Description: 
This brisket will come out melt-in-your-mouth tender.
