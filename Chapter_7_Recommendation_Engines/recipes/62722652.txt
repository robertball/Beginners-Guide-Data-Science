Title:
Perogie Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/620754.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package frozen perogies
2 cups cooked and drained ground meat of choice
2 - 3 398ml cans of Pasta Sauce
1 cup frozen vegetables (peas, carrots, beans)
1 cup cheese
Seasoning recommendations: Cajun, oregano, basil, garlic, pepper

Directions:
Note: All the ingredients are flexible to taste. ie: If you like more sauce, accommodate yourself!
Preheat oven to 350'F
Start frying raw meat in a large frying pan. When cooked thoroughly add in canned sauce, frozen vegetables, and seasonings. Turn heat to low and stir until warm. Turn heat off and set to side.
lightly oil or spray bottom of 9x13 casserole pan.
spoon in a small amount of sauce mixture and coat bottom of pan.
Individually arrange frozen perogies on pan and evenly cover with sauce mixture. Top with cheese.
Cover with tinfoil and bake aprox 20-30 mins until heated through out.
Take tinfoil off when near done if you prefer browned cheese.

Submitter: 
Kelly Dawn Fischer
Description: 
This very flexible dish can be served on the side or as the main course! A Cheesy tomato'y perogie bake that is quick and easy!
