Title:
Chocolate Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup whole wheat flour
1 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 tsp. of salt
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup applesauce
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup lite sour cream
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
4 over-ripe bananas, mashed
1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1/3 cup walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease muffin pans.
In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and cocoa powder, Create a hole in the middle.
In another bowl, cream together butter, applesauce & sugar for 5 minutes. Add eggs. Blend in sour cream and vanilla. Add bananas.
Stir in chocolate chips. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
