Title:
Remoulade Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4  cup mayonnaise
1/2  cup celery, finely chopped
1/2  cup green onions, chopped
1/4  cup Italian parsley, chopped
1/4  cup horseradish
1/8 of a lemon (a slice), with rind, deseeded
1  bay leaf, crumbled
2  tablespoons creole mustard
2  tablespoons catsup
2  tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1  tablespoon Dijon mustard
1  tablespoon distilled white vinegar
1  tablespoon Tabasco sauce
1  tablespoon garlic, minced
2  teaspoons paprika
1  teaspoon salt

Directions:
With motor running in food processor or blender, add ingredients one at a time until well mixed and lemon rind is finely chopped.
Chill in refrigerator at least 2 hours or overnight.
If you like it extra spicy add a little more horseradish

Submitter: 
BEARNESTA
Description: 
A spicy, creamy sauce best used for dipping seafood.  Also excellent with cheese fries, chicken tenders, or on top of a steak!
