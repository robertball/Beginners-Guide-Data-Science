Title:
Peppermint Twist Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. chocolate Teddy Grahams
1/4 c. sugar
1/2 c. (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
2 lb. cream cheese, room temperature
1 c. sugar
4 large eggs
1/4 c. half and half
1 t. vanilla extract
1/2 t. peppermint oil (or 2 t peppermint extract)
2/3 c. finely crushed peppermint candies

Directions:
Wrap 9" spring form pan in 18 x 18" piece of heavy duty aluminum foil.
Add Teddy Grahams to food processor, pulse until you have a fine powder.  Add sugar and melted butter.  Mix again.  Press into bottom of a 9 inch springform pan.  Bake at 325 for 15 minutes.  Let cool on cooling rack while making filling.
Bring 4 quarts of water to a boil.  (To be used later)
Add cream cheese to stand mixer, mix on med-low speed for 2 min.  Add 1/3 of sugar, mix 1 min.  Scrape bowl.  Add another 1/3 of sugar, mix 1 min.  Scrape bowl.  Add remaining sugar, mix 1 min.  Scrape bowl.
Add eggs, one at a time, mixing for 1 minute after each.  Add half & half.  Mix 1 minute more.
Add vanilla, peppermint oil and peppermint candies.  You can also add a few drops of red food coloring for swirl.
Pour into springform pan.  Place springform pan inside of a large roasting pan on the middle rack in preheated oven.  Fill roasting pan with boiling water until water comes half way up the side of the springform pan.  Bake at 350 for 1 hour and 15 min, or until thermometer inserted into center reads 140-150 degrees.
Turn off oven and let cheesecake sit for at least 3 hours (in the oven).  Remove from oven and cool completely at room temperature, then cover and refrigerate.

A festive cheesecake for the Christmas season!
