Title:
Elegant and Easy Cherry Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package cherry gelatin
1c. boiling water
2 loaves pound cake cut into 1 inch cubes
1 can cherry pie filling
1 carton whipped topping

Directions:
1. Dissolve gelatin in water; refrigerate for 15 minutes.
2. Place half of the cake cubes in a 3 quart trifle or serving bowl; top with half the gelatin and pie filling. Repeat layers. Top with whipped topping. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Description: 
This is an elegant, yet easy dessert that's perfect for everyday or entertaining.
