Title:
Rich n Creamy Brownie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brownie:
1 pkg brownie mix
2 eggs
1/3 cup water
1/4 cup oil
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)
Topping:
8 oz cream cheese
2 eggs
1 lb confectioners sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease bottom of 9x13 pan
Mix brownie ingredients and spread in pan.
Beat topping ingredients and spread over brownie mixture
Bake for 45-50 minutes - do not overbake.
