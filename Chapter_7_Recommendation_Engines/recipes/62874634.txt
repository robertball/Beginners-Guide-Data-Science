Title:
Black Forest Sundaes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint vanilla ice cream
Canned pitted cherries
4 chocolate wafer cookies, crushed
Whipped cream

Directions:
Put a couple of scoops of ice cream in each sundae glass. Top with cherries, crushed cookies and whipped cream.
