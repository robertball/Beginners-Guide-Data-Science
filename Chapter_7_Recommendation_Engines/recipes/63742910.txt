Title:
Caesar Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons mayonnaise
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
3 large garlic cloves, chopped
2 teaspoons anchovie paste
1/2 cup lemon juice (3 lemons, or bottled juice ok); could substitute red wine vinegar
2 teaspoons sea salt
1/2 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
1 1/2 cups good mild olive oil (or Mediterranean Oil Blend)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and blend.

Description: 
Easy and delicious. Safe, since it uses mayo instead of raw egg.
