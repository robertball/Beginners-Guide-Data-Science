Title:
Tara 's Quick and Easy Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/927533.jpg
Ingredients:
For the Potatoes:
4 potatoes(yukon gold) potatoes, sliced into 3/8-inch thick rounds
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste
5 or so ( I just eye ball it) tablespoons unsalted butter
For the Egg's:
8 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese+ 1/4 cup for topping
5 slices Swiss cheese
1/2 cup crumbled bacon+ 2 tablspoons for topping
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1/4 cup chopped minced onion
2 tsp minced garlic (2 garlic cloves)
1 tablespoon parsely

Directions:
For the Potatoes:Combine potatoes, salt, ground black pepper, cayenne pepper, in a large non stick skillet.Stir to evenly coat.Cook potatoes on medium heat flipping occasionly just untill they are almost done.Place in the bottom of a   greased (i use pam) casserole dish.For the Egg's:In a large bowl, beat the eggs,milk,minced onion , salt,garlic and pepper. Stir in cheddar cheese, bacon and green pepper. Pour over potatoes.Place sliced Swiss cheese on top,Sprinkle with bacon crumbles,1/4 cheddar cheese & parlsey. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
