Title:
Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077593.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c water
1 c lemon juice (about 4 lemons)
1/2 c sugar
lemon or orange slices, if desired
fresh mint leaves, if desired

Directions:
In large pitcher, stir water, lemon juice & sugar until sugar is dissolved.
Serve lemonade over ice. Garnish with lemon slices & mint.
