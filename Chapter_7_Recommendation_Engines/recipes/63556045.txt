Title:
Mom's Famous Raspberry Apple Gelatin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small packages raspberry jello
2 cups boiling water
1 10 oz. package frozen raspberries
1 15 oz. jar applesauce
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mini marshmallows
1 TBSP. marachino cherry juice

Directions:
Mix the jello, water, raspberries and applesauce.  Pour into a jello mold or pretty bowl. Chill until set.  Dressing:  Mix together sour cream, mini marshmallows, and cherry juice and let stand for one hour at room temperature.  Mix again and serve on the side with the jello mold.

Submitter: 
iammykidzmom
Description: 
My mom always had this at Thanksgiving or other holidays to take the place of cranberry sauce.  My kids now look forward to having it for all the holidays!
