Title:
Chocolate-Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Peanut Butter
1 Stick & 2 Tbls Butter
2 Tbls Powdered Sugar
2 Cups Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
1/2 of a 7oz Jar Marshmallow Cream
2 tsp Vanilla
2 Cups Sugar
5oz Can Evaporated Milk

Directions:
Mix Peanut Butter & 2 Tbls Butter in a small glass bowl. Microwave on high for 30-60 seconds
Stir Powdered Sugar into Peanut Butter mixture until blended, set aside.
In a large bowl, add Chocolate Chips, remaining butter, marshmallow cream, and vanilla.
In a large sauce pan, combine sugar & condensed milk. Turn heat to low and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Boil mixture for 6 minutes, stirring constantly.
Pour chocolate chip mixture into the sauce pan until chocolate & butter melt.
Pour melted chocolate mixture into a 9x13" pan.  Swirl in Peanut Butter mixture & refrigerate until set.
