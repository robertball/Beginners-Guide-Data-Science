Title:
Paul & Phil Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic, minced
3/4 cup cream or half & half
1 tblsp olive oil
Fresh basil & oregano or 1 tsp Italian seasoning
1 1/2 tblsp fresh lemon juice
1/2 tblsp Dijon mustard
1 chicken breast, sliced & seasoned with salt & pepper
3 strips bacon

Directions:
Fry the bacon, drain and break into small pieces.  Set aside.
Brown chicken in bacon drippings and set aside.
Drain skillet of bacon grease.  Saute garlic in olive oil.  Add lemon juice and reduce by half.
Stir in cream and dijon mustard. Bring to boil, reduce heat to low simmer.  Add basil & oregano or Italian seasoning.  Simmer 5-10 minutes.
Add back chicken & reheat to desired temp.
Serve over tri-color cheese tortellini.
