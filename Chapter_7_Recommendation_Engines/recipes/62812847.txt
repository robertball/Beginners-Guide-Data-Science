Title:
Zuppa Toscana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/748680.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound Italian or chicken asiago sausage
2 large russet baking potatoes sliced in 1/4 slices
1 large chopped onion
1/2 package bacon bits (may substitute Canadian bacon)
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups chopped kale or Swiss chard
2 14 1/2 oz cans chicken broth
1 quart water
1 cup heavy whipping cream (or half and half)

Directions:
Cook sausage in a 300 degree oven for 30 minutes: drain on paper towels and cut into slices or mince.
Place onions, potatoes, chicken broth, water, and garlic in a pot and cook on medium heat until potatoes are done. Add sausage, bacon, salt, and pepper to taste and simmer for another 10 minutes. Turn to low heat; add kale and cream. Heat thoroughly and serve.
