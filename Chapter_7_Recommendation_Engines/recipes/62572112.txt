Title:
The Good Eggs Benedict Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/543157.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 Slices of Hillshire Farm Baked Ham Hearty Slice
2 English muffins, split
2 tablespoons butter, softened
Hollandaise Sauce Recipe

Directions:
Prep Hollandaise Sauce Recipe.
Poach eggs. Warm Ham. Toast and butter english muffins. Half way through eggs poaching, pour hot butter in double boiler 1 tbsp at a time with other Hollandaise ingredients. Whisk. Keep warm in double boiler. Pour over poached eggs, ham, and english muffin. Enjoy!
