Title:
Aunt Debbi's Cheese  Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 PKGS OF PHILADELPHIA CREAM CHEESE (LITE IS OKAY TO USE)
3 OR 4 STALKS OF GREEN ONION (CHOPPED FINE)
2 PKGS OF DELI SLICED HAM DICED FINE & DIVIDED
1 TSP OF ASCENT
1 1/2 TSP OF WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE
**OPTIONAL YOU CAN ADD OTHER MEATS,  LIKE CRISP
BACON CRUMBLED

Directions:
MIX TOGETHER CREAM CHEESE, GREEN ONION, WORCHESTER SAUCE, ASCENT & 1 PKG OF HAM CHOPPED FINE.  WHEN COMPLETELY MIXED TOGETHER ROLL CHEESE BALL IN THE OTHER PKG OF CHOPPED UP HAM AND CHILL AT LEAST 2 HOURS TO LET THE FAVORS BLEND THEN EAT WITH VEGGIES, CRACKERS, BREAD, CHIPS, ETC.

Submitter: 
Karyn Osborne
Description: 
Recipe from my sister, soft Cheese ball rolled in chopped ham instead of nuts ... my family loves it.
