Title:
Country Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb extra lean ground beef
2 cups of herbed stuffing mix
1 1/2 cup water
3/4 cup chopped onions
1 cup fresh mushrooms
1/2 cup of barbecue sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Mix meat, stuffing mix, 1 1/4 cup of water and onions until well blended
Shape into six 1/2 inch thick oval patties
Place on baking sheet
Bake 25 or until cooked until 160f
Spray large nonstick skillet with cooking spray
Add mushrooms and cook on medium-high heat for 5 minutes or until lightly browned, stiring occasionally
Add barbecue sauce and remaining 1/4 cup water
Reduce heat to low
Cook 1 to 2 minutes or until is heated throughly
Serve over patties
