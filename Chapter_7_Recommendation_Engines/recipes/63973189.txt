Title:
Aunt April's Chuckwagon Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. bacon, cooked and crumbled
1/2 lb. hamburger
1 small onion, chopped
1 can kidney beans
1 can butter beans
1 can pork & beans
1/4 c. brown sugar
1/4 c. white sugar
1/2 c. ketchup

Directions:
Brown meat and onion, drain.
Drain kidney and butter beans.
In casserole dish, combine all ingredients and stir well to combine.
Bake at 325 for 45 min.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
I'm not sure if this is April's recipe or Grandpa's, but it is certainly a Pierce family recipe.
