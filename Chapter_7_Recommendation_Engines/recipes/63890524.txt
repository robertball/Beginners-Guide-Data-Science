Title:
Mama June's Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter softened
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup molasses
1 egg
2 1/4 cup flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp cloves
1/2 cup crystallized ginger
large crystal sugar for rolling cookies

Directions:
1. Cream together butter & sugar. Stir in molasses & egg until well blended. Combine flour, baking soda, salt, & spices. Add to molasses mixture. Stir in crystallized ginger.
2. Use a cookie scoop to make 1 inch balls. Roll in large crystal sugar.
3. Bake at 350 degrees on ungreased cookie sheet (or sheet lined with parchment paper) for 10-12 minutes.
