Title:
Cherry Lime Beergarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3172559.jpg
Ingredients:
ice
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen limeade concentrate
1 cup cherry-flavored rum (such as Bacardi Torched Cherry®)
5 (12 fluid ounce) cans or bottles beer (such as Budweiser®), or more if needed

Directions:
Fill a large pitcher a quarter-full of ice. Add limeade and rum; stir with a spoon to combine. Pour in enough beer to fill the pitcher. Mix to combine.

Submitter: 
Brynn Finnimore-Ladd
Description: 
This is an absolutely delicious drink that is perfect on a hot day!
