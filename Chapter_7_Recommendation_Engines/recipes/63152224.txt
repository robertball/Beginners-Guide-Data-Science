Title:
Stir-fried Peppers and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093079.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large red peppers
2 large green peppers
2 large orange
2 medium onions
1 teaspoon sesame oil
sea salt, to taste
ground pepper, to taste
olive oil, for frying

Directions:
Seed peppers and cut into slices, lengthwise. Cut the slices in half if they are too long.
Cut onions into slices, lengthwise.
Heat oil in a large frying pan or wok over medium-high heat. Stir-fry the vegetables just until they start to soften.
Season with sesame oil, salt and pepper. Serve.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Stir-frying captures the natural flavor and color of the vegetables. Sesame oil and sea salt bring out the flavors of the vegetables in this quick and healthy dish.
