Title:
Angela's Lime-Cilantro Pork Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
black pepper to taste, optional
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
2 cloves minced garlic
4 Tablespoons fresh lime juice
3 Tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro (or to taste)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well.
Pour into plastic bag and add meat.
Marinate at least 6 hours.
Grill.

Submitter: 
Dutchgirl
Description: 
My friend, Angela, gave me this recipe.  It works beautifully on pork chops and pork tenderloin alike.
