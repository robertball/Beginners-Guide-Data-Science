Title:
CHOCOLATE PEANUT BUTTER CRISP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c creamy peanut butter
1 3/4 c white chocolate chips
1 c rice crisp cereal
1/2 c mini marshmallows
1/2 c mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix combine peanut butter and white chocolate chips in large bowl and microwave 1-2 minutes, until melted. Stir until well blended and fold in cereal. Let cool at room temp. While cooling, line a 8"x 8" baking dish (size is very flexible, the smaller the pan, the thicker the candy.)with plastic wrap, leaving extra length to press candy once poured. Once cool, fold in marshmallows and chocolate chips. Spread mixture into pan and cover candy with excess wrap, press to even out. Refrigerate 30 minutes to set.

Description: 
A yummy combination of white chocolate, peanut butter, rice krisp cereal and other treats combine to make a delicious dessert.
