Title:
Ono Chicken Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken thighs boiled and deboned
3 boxes broccoli, thawed and drained
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 cup mayonaise
1/2 tsp curry powder
1 TBL + 1 tsp lemon juice
1 can mushrooms

Directions:
Mix together everything except chicken. Spread 1/2 of mixture in 9x13 pan, top with chicken and remaining sauce. Bake at 350 until heated through.

Submitter: 
lovetoeat
Description: 
My mother in-law gave me this recipe almost 30 years ago when I first got married and couldn't cook very well and we couldn't afford to buy much more than chicken thighs. It's still a family favorite and always makes me think of her when I make it.
