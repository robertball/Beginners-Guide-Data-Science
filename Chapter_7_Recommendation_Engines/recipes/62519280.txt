Title:
Kraft Easy Broccoli Cottage Bake (crustless quiche) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 container (500g) 1% cottage cheese
300g frozen chopped broccoli (or asparagus), thawed
3/4 Cup (60g) light shredded chedder
21 grams sundried tomatoes, rehydrated and chopped
1/4 Cup roasted red pepper, well drained, chopped
4 Svgs (200g) egg beaters
3 Tbsp lite parmesean
3 Tbsp bread crumbs

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in bowl.  Put in 9" greased glass pie plate.  Bake 45mins on 350F.  Let stand 10mins before serving

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
A crustless quiche recipe from the Kraft magazine.  Can be done with a variety of veggies (not just broccoli)
