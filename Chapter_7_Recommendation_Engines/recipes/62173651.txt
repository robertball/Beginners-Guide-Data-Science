Title:
Oven Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs stew meat
1C chopped celery
1 1/2C sliced carrots
1 1/2C sliced potatoes (cubed)
1/2C chopped onion
3Tbsp tapioca(uncooked)
1 boullion cube
1Tbsp sugar
salt and pepper to taste
1 bay leaf
worcestshire sauce to taste
1 big can of tomato juice

Directions:
In large casserole dish add all of the ingrdents except the tomato juice. Once all other ingredents are in the dish then cover with tomato juice, just to cover. Bake covered in oven for 5hours at 250.
Add the potatoes in the last 1 1/2hours of cooking.
