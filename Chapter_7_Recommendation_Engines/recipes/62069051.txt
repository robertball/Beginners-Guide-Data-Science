Title:
Best Barbequed Halibut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 large pieces of fresh halibut
1 tsp. lemon pepper
1 tsp. Lawry's seasoned salt
1 large onion sliced
1 cube butter
lemon juice

Directions:
Place Halibut on Heavyduty foil. Sprinkle lemon pepper and seasoned salt over all.
Put butter pats on all of Halibut
Place seperated onion slices on top of halibut and sprinkle lemon juice over all.
Seal foil tightly and place on another piece of foil upside down and seal.
Barbecue until Halibut is done.  About
20 minutes.
Unwrap foil and serve.

Description: 
Melt in your mouth barbecued Halibut smothered in onions.
