Title:
Really Yummy Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15oz can black beans, drained & rinsed
1 8oz can whole kernel corn, drained
4 green onions, sliced
1 cup red bell pepper, chopped
2 avocados, coarse chopped
Fresh squeezed juice of 3 limes
3 T fresh cilantro, chopped
2 T ground cumin
1 T sea salt

Directions:
In a small bowl, lightly toss the chopped avocado with the lime juice being careful not to turn the avocado to mush.
Mix together all the remaining ingredients and then add the avocado & lime juice mixture.
The salsa is delicious when served immediately or refrigerate for 1 - 3 hours to allow the flavors to blend.
