Title:
Quick Brown Rice and Mushroom Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 small onion, chopped
1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
4 ounces chicken broth
1/2 cup Minute® Brown Rice, uncooked
1 t parsley,

Directions:
Heat oil in medium saucepan on medium heat. Add onions; cook 3 minutes or until crisp-tender, stirring occasionally.
Add mushrooms; cook 3 minutes or until mushrooms are tender, stirring occasionally. Add broth; stir. Bring to boil.
Stir in rice; cover. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 5 minutes. Remove from heat; let stand 5 minutes. Add parsley; mix lightly.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
This flavorful rice pilaf made with Minute® Brown Rice, mushrooms, onions and  chicken broth, is wonderful paired with chicken or pork.Modified from: Quick Brown Rice and Mushroom Pilaf
