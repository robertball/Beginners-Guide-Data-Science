Title:
chicken rice burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sliced green onions
1 large garlic clove, minced
2 Tbsp butter or margarine
7 cups shredded or chopped cooked chicken
1 Tbsp. chilli powder
2 1/2 cups chicken broth, divided
1 16 oz jar salsa or picante sauce, divided
1 c uncooked long-grain rice
1/2 c sliced black olives
3 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
12 10-inch flour tortillas, warmed
Additional picante sauce and cheddar cheese.

Directions:
In a skillet, saute onions and garlic in butter. Add 1/4 cup chicken broth and 3/4 cup salsa sauce.  Heat through and set aside. In a medium saucepan, bring rice and remaining broth to a boil and reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until nearly done.  Stir in remaining salsa and cover. Simmer fo 5-10 minutes until rice is tender.  Stir in chicken, and onion/garlic mixture, then add olives and 2 cups cheese.  Mix well and spoon 1 cup of filling on each tortilla. Fold sides and ends over and arrange burritos into 2 ungreased 9x13 glass baking dishes.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake at 375 for 15 minutes or until heated through. Serve with remaining picante sauce, sour cream and guacamole, if desired.
