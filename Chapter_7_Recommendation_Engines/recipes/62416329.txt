Title:
Best Calico Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. diced bacon
1/2 lb. lean ground beef
1 large onion
2 Tbsp. granulated sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar packed
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup barbecue sauce (any kind)
2 Tbsp. prepared mustard
2 Tbsp. molasses
1/2 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 16 oz can red beans or kidney beans
1 16 oz can butterbeans
1 28 oz. pork and beans in tomato sauce or Bush's Original Baked Beans

Directions:
Brown ground beef and put in large casserole dish or dutch oven.
Fry bacon until crisp. Add to ground beef.
Slice a large onion and saute in bacon grease.  Add to meat mixture.
In with the meat and onions, mix sugars, ketchup, barbecue sauce, mustard, molasses, chili powder, salt and pepper.  Stir.
Add undrained beans and stir.
Bake with cover at 350 for 1 hour and 15 minutes.  If mixture is too juicy, remove cover the last 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Joan Gerland Crabtree
Description: 
These calico baked beans are full of flavor and are a step up from the normal calico bean dishes.  Whenever I make them, I get rave reviews and requests for the recipe and when brought to a potluck, they are the first to go!  I never leave with leftovers!
