Title:
Very Banana-y Whole Wheat Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs olive oil
1/2 cup Brown Sugar (not packed down)
1 tsp vanilla extract
3 eggs
1/3 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 3/4 cup mashed bananas
1 3/4 cups whole wheat flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). For Convection, preheat to 275 to 300 degrees F
In a large bowl, beat oil and Brown Sugar together. Add eggs, vanilla extract and applesauce and mix well. Stir in bananas. Stir in flour. Blend in chopped nuts. Cover the bottom of a 9x5 inch non-stick loaf pan with parchment paper, and pour batter.
Bake 60 minutes or until a toothpick/skewer comes out clean. Cover with a towel and cool on wire rack for 1/2 hour before slicing.
