Title:
Pasta Fagioli with cannellini beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons of canola oil
1/4 cup chopped onion
3 cloves chopped garlic
1 container  Pomi chopped tomatoes (26oz)
3 cups unsalted chicken broth
15 oz cannellini beans
1/2 teaspoon parsley
1/4 teaspoon red pepper
1/4 teaspoon sage
1/4 oregano
1/4 teaspoon salt and pepper
1 cup of dried Gluten Free shell or macaroni pasta
1 teaspoon basil

Directions:
Heat oil in pot over low-medium heat until hot.
Add onion till soft and add garlic
Add undrained tomatoes bring to boil
Add undrained cannellini beans, chicken broth, parsley, basil and pepper to pot and bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
Let boil for approximately 5 minutes and then let simmer for a few minutes covered.
Add pasta to pot and boil approximately 15-20 minutes or until pasta is tender.
