Title:
Red, White and Blue Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984310.jpg
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cups boiling water
1 (3 ounce) package JELL-O Brand Strawberry Flavor Gelatin, or any red flavor
1 (3 ounce) package JELL-O Brand Berry Blue Flavor Gelatin
1 cup cold water
1 1/2 cups sliced strawberries
1 (3 ounce) package JELL-O Brand Lemon Flavor Gelatin
1 pint vanilla ice cream, softened
1 1/2 cups blueberries

Directions:
Stir 1 cup of the boiling water into each of the red and blue gelatins in separate medium bowls at least 2 minutes until completely dissolved.  Stir 1/2 cup of the cold water into each bowl.
Place bowl of red gelatin in larger bowl of ice and water.  Stir until thickened, about 8 minutes.  Stir in strawberries.  Pour into 9x5-inch loaf pan.  Refrigerate 7 minutes.
Meanwhile, stir remaining 3/4 cup boiling water into lemon gelatin in medium bowl at least 2 minutes until completely dissolved.  Stir in ice cream until melted and smooth.  Spoon over red gelatin in pan.  Refrigerate 7 minutes.
Meanwhile, place bowl of blue gelatin in larger bowl of ice and water.  Stir until thickened, about 7 minutes.  Stir in blueberries.  Spoon over lemon gelatin in pan.
Refrigerate 4 hours or overnight until firm.  Unmold.  Garnish as desired.  Store leftover gelatin mold in refrigerator.
