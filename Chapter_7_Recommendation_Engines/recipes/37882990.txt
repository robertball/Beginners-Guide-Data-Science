Title:
Birdseed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups boiling water
2/3 cup rolled oats
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. salt
2 tsp. yeast
1 egg
1/4 cups millet seeds
1/2 cups flax seeds
1/4 cups pumpkin seeds
2 T poppy seeds
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup white flour

Directions:
Pour boiling water over rolled oats and millet seeds.
Add margarine, brown sugar, and salt.
Let set about 30 minutes, until lukewarm.
Add yeast, egg and additional seeds and whole wheat flour; mix thoroughly.
Add additional flour, a cup at a time, mixing between each addition.
Turn out and knead until smooth, adding flour if needed.
Put back in bowl and let rise, covered with a damp cloth.
Form into one loaf and put in greased bread pan.
Let rise again.
Bake at 350 degrees F for about 30 minutes or until browned top and bottom.
While the bread is still hot, rub a stick of butter over the top of the loaf for a shiny finish and great flavor.

Submitter: 
LAURISSIMA
Description: 
My mom came up with this recipe years ago after trying similar bread at Whole Foods. I have substituted flax seeds for the millet when I didn't have them, and it was just as good. OK to sub one seed for another. They add a crunchy texture, good flavor and excellent nutrition.
