Title:
Fran's Italian Margaritas - On the Rocks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 part tequila (30-30 is the best anejo tequila for the $)
1 part amaretto (Disaronno)
1 part sweet & sour mix
1/2 part orange liqueur (triple sec)
1/2 lime, fresh squeezed

Directions:
Slightly moisten rims of 4 12-ounce glasses and dip in confectioners' sugar to rim the glasses;fill each with crushed ice.
Combine the tequila, amaretto, sweet & sour and orange liqueur in a martini shaker with a small amount of ice - shake well.  Pour mixture into glasses.  Garnish drinks with lime or orange slices if you wish.
