Title:
Best Bircher Meusli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups rolled Quaker oats
3 tablespoons raisins
3 tablespoons honey
1 1/2 cups plain yogurt
1 1/4 cup soy milk
Optional ingredients to add before serving:
slivered almonds
walnuts
grapes
blueberries
drained pineapple
dried fruit
sliced apple
sliced peach
sliced strawberries
sliced banana

Directions:
In a glass bowl, mix together the oats, raisins, honey, plain yogurt, soy milk. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Serve in the morning with the optional add-in ingredients.

Submitter: 
Denice Breen
Description: 
Easy, healthy and delicious. Bircher Meusli is a cold European breakfast cereal that is made the day before and placed in the refrigerator overnight. A variety of fruit can be stirred in for more flavor.
