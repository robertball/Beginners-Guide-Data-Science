Title:
Chicken Fried Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3Cup oil
6 cube steaks
2C flour
2 eggs
1/3-1/2C milk
pepper
paprika
garlic powder
Home made Gray:
1Tbsp corn starch
1C milk
2Tbsp butter
1/4tsp pepper

Directions:
In a frying pan put oil to cook steaks in. In one bowl put flour and seasonings mix well. In other bowl scramble eggs and add milk, mix and add pepper and garlic powder.
Dip steaks in egg mixture than the flour mixture, put in frying pan- cook on med heat 10-15minutes each side until golden brown.
For gravy:
Combine cornstarch and milk in a small saucepan,stir until smooth. Add butter, pepper and extra if you want. Stir Constantly, Simmer over medium heat for one minute until it thickens.
