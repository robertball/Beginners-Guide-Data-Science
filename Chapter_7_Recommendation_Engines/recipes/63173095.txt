Title:
Authentic Sicilian Veal Scaloppine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb veal cutlets
1/4 cup flour
salt and pepper
4 tbsp olive oil
1 cup marsala wine
1/2 cup of water
1 tbsp fresh minced parsley
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms

Directions:
Combine flour, salt and pepper.
Dredge veal cutlets lightly in flour mixture.
Brown cutlets in hot oil over medium heat about 2 min per side. Remove from pan and set aside.
Saute mushrooms in 1 Tbsp oilve oil til slightly browned.
Return veal cutlets to pan.
Blend wine and water and pour over browned cutlets.
Turn down heat to low, cover pan and cook for 2 or 3 minutes.Do not allow to boil.
Serve immediately.

Submitter:

Description: 
Another one of Mom's recipes made with marsala wine and fresh mushrooms. One of my husbands favorites. Can also be used with chicken cutlets insteal of veal.
