Title:
Lasagna Spinach Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4oz (1/2 of 8-oz pkg) Cream Cheese, softened
3oz (1/2 of 6-oz pkg) Fresh chopped spinach
1-1/4 Cups  Shredded Low-Moisture Whole Milk Mozzarella Cheese, divided
1/4 Cup  Grated Parmesan Cheese
6 Lasagna noodles, cooked
2 Cups  Spaghetti sauce

Directions:
HEAT oven to 375ÂºF.
MIX cream cheese, spinach, 1 cup mozzarella and Parmesan until well blended; spread onto noodles. Roll up tightly.
PLACE, seam-sides down, in 9-inch square baking dish; top with sauce and remaining mozzarella.
BAKE 30 min. or until heated through.
