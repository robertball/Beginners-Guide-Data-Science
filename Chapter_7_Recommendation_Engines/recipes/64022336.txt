Title:
Simple Plum Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. fresh plum tomatoes
1 stick of butter
1 medium yellow onion - halved
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. sugar

Directions:
Cut tomatoes lengthwise, place in large pot, cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
Remove and put through food mill to remove skins and seeds.
Put back in pot, add remaining ingredients and simmer uncovered for 45 minutes.
Remove onion before serving.
Sprinkle with Parmesan if desired.
