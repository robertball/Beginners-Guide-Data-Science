Title:
The Bistro's mac "n" cheese with grilled chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MACARONI BASE
1- 1 pound box Celientani (corkscrew) pasta
1/4 cup minced shallot (2 large)
2 T olive oil
1 quart heavy cream
8 oz. Boursin Garlic and Fine Herb Gournay Cheese, crumbled.
CHICKEN
4 - 6oz boneless skinless chicken breast
2 T olive oil
salt and pepper
2 t chopped fresh parsley
2 t chopped fresh rosemary.
ASSEMBLY
1/4 cup diced pancetta
2 T oil
1/4 cup bread crumbs
4 t chopped parsley
4 t white truffle oil

Directions:
MACARONI BASE - Cook pasta according to directions, drain.  Saute the shallots in the olive oil until lightly colored, add heavy cream.  Reduce the heat to a gentle simmer and reduce by half, about 8 - 10 minutes.  Stir in cheese, and pasta.
CHICKEN - drizzle the chicken with the olive oil and season each side with salt and pepper.  Sprinkle with chopped parsley and rosemary.  Grill the chicken until juices run clear.
ASSEMBLY - Cook the pancetta until crisp, strain the pancetta leaving any fat in the pan.  Sprinkle bread crumbs in pan.  Saute until dark and toasted about 1 minute. Remove and drain on paper towel.  Cut chicken into strips and stir into the macaroni base. Stir in the pancetta, parsley and white truffle oil.  Divide among 4 serving bowls and top with bread crumbs.  Serve immediately
