Title:
Almond and Date Smoothie with Maca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4575410.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dates, pitted and roughly chopped
1/2 cup rolled oats, quick or slow cooking
1 teaspoon gelatinized maca powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
3 tablespoons almond butter
1/2 cup Almond Breeze Original OR any Almond Breeze Almond-Cashew Blend
1/2 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
1 whole ripe banana
1 cup ice

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a powerful blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Spice up your morning with a dairy-free smoothie with a sprinkle of super food. For extra creaminess use any Almond Breeze Almond-Cashew Blend instead of Almond Breeze Original.
