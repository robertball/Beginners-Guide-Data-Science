Title:
Roasted Chicken & Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts
4-5 medium or large potatoes
Asparagus, small bunch
4 Large carrots
Olive oil to coat chicken and veggies
Salt
Garlic Salt
Pepper
Rosemary (optional)

Directions:
Cut Chicken Breasts into halves or thirds.
Cut all vegetables into cubes, rounds, or smaller chunks.
Coat Veggies and Chicken with a small amount of olive oil.
Lay Veggies and Chicken chunks out on a large cookie sheet that is covered in foil.
Sprinkle spices over all of it.
Bake for 30-45 minutes at 375.
