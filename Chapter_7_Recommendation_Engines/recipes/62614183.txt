Title:
Kale & Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. chopped kale leaves
1 onion chopped
1 minced garlic clove (opt.)
2 T. butter
6 eggs
pinch of Salt

Directions:
Melt butter in pan and add kale, onion and garlic.  Cook until tender.  Add raw eggs and mix into vegetables to make scrambled eggs.  Salt to taste.

Submitter: 
Miriam
Description: 
This is a great way to use vegetables with breakfast and a good way to cook kale.
