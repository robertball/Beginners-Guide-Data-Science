Title:
Asian Ginger Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbs minced fresh ginger root
1 medium sized carrot
2 tsp sesame oil
2 Tb rice vinegar
1 tsp Bragg's Amino Acids (or 2 tsp soy sauce)
1 Tb brown sugar
1 tsp lemon juice
1/4 cup green onions (1-2)
Water to cover

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a food processor.  Store covered in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Cindi Fortmann
Description: 
Terrific salad dressing for all salads - especially great for use on cabbage salads.  Tastes just like the Japanese restaurant version.Modified from: Asian Ginger Dressing
