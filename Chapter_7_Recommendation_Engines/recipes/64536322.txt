Title:
Cool quinoa & chickpea salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3107729.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked white quinoa
1 can garbanzo beans
2 finely cubed persian cucumbers (better with skin on, 1 regular cuke also fine)
2 tablespoons finely chopped red onion
2 stalks finely chopped celery
4 oz Super lemon garlic dressing http://allrecipes.com/personal-recipe/64536316/super-lemon-garlic-dressing/
Additional seasoning to taste, for example: garlic parsley granules, pepper, salt, Vegesal

Directions:
Combine everything and let it sit in the fridge, preferably at least an hour.

Submitter: 
Ruby
Description: 
Healthy and delicious. Great way to use leftover quinoa.
