Title:
Greek Shrimp with Tomatoes, Oregano and Feta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds large shrimp (cleaned and deveined)
Sea Salt & Ground Black Pepper to taste
5 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1 sweet onion, chopped
5 cloves of garlic, crushed
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
2 Tablespoons of dried oregano
1 1/2 c diced canned tomatoes OR Tomato Sauce Or 4 Large Fresh Tomatoes, seeded and diced.
1/2 teaspoon sugar
8 oz. Feta Cheese
1/4 cup fresh parsley chopped

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 450 degrees.
Sprinkle the shrimp with the salt and black pepper.
Over medium-high heat in a saute pan, heat 2 T of the oil. Add the shrimp and stir until they turn pink, about 3 minutes.
Transfer the shrimp to a casserole dish.
In the same pan over medium heat, warm the remaining 2 T of oil, add the chopped onion and saute until tender, about 7 minutes.
Add the garlic, crushed red pepper flakes, and oregano and saute for 2 minutes.
Add the tomatoes and simmer until sauce has thickened slightly, about 5 minutes.
Add the sugar and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Pour the sauce over the shrimp then sprinkle with feta cheese and bake for 5-8 minutes until it melts.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
This dish is very simple to make, but it's very robust  with flavor. I love Mediterranean flavors, so this is a favorite for sure! I usually serve this with a Greek side salad, rice pilaf and warm, crusty bread for dipping up the extra sauce.
