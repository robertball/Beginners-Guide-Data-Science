Title:
Microwave Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 onion, diced
1/2 bell pepper, diced (optional)
6 saltine crackers, crushed
pinch of salt
pepper
8 oz tomato sauce
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup vinegar
1 T prepared mustard

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together the tomato sauce, brown sugar, vinegar and mustard, set aside.
In a Separate bowl mix together raw ground beef, onion, crackers, egg, salt, and pepper and half of the tomato mixture.
Pour into a microwave safe dish and shape into a loaf form.
Spread the remaining tomato sauce mixture on top of the loaf.
Place in microwave and cook for 20 minutes, turning every 5 minutes unless you have a turn table in the microwave.
Serve and enjoy.
Note: The classic green bean cassarole goes great with the meatloaf.
