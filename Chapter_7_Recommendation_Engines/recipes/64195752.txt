Title:
Ham and Cheese Pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1555501.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 count) can refrigerated biscuits
cubed ham
shredded cheese (cheddar recommended)

Directions:
Separate the biscuits and flatten them. They should be fairly thin.
Add ham and cheese.
Close and seal with fork tines.
Bake 350F degrees about 7-10 minutes- until brown.
