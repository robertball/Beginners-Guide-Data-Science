Title:
Apple-Honey Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup all purpose flour
2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon apple pie spice
1/8 teaspoon baking soda
1 egg beaten
3/4 cup apple juice
2 tablespooomns honey
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
extra butter/ oil for cooking

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients in a bowl.
Mix together egg, honey, apple juice and oil and add to dry ingredients.
Heat butter or oil in a griddle or fry pan. Place spoonfuls of mixture and cook over medium heat until bubbles burst on surface. Flip over and cook until golden brown.
Serve with maple syrup.
