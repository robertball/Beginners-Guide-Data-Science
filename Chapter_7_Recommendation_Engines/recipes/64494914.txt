Title:
Leftovers Inspired Veggie, Pasta and Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of small cut pasta, (elbow, ditali etc.)
1 tsp. salt (for boiling pasta)
2 tbs. olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium garlic clove, minced
2 tbs. tomato paste
1 medium carrot, chopped
1 stalk of celery, chopped
1 qt. of chicken stock
1 heaping cup of shredded cabbage
1 medium potato, peeled and diced
1 cup of beans, any kind, canned or fresh cooked
dash of salt, optional
fresh black cracked pepper
grated parmesan

Directions:
In a small sauce pot boil some water, add salt and pasta and cook pasta to pkg directions or very al dente.  Drain and set aside.
In a large pot, heat olive oil and add onion.  Cook on medium low heat until soft and translucent.  Add minced garlic and cook for 1-2 mins.  Add tomato paste and cook for another 1-2 mins.  Add chicken stock, carrots, celery, cabbage and potatoes and beans.  Simmer for 20-30 mins.  Add cooked pasta and simmer until just heated through, about a minute.
Serve with fresh cracked black pepper, and grated parmesan.

Submitter: 
debbie eckstein
Description: 
Everything that went into this soup, was leftover!  you don't have to use leftovers, but it just makes it so much more easier than it already is.  Use this recipe as a base and go with whatever leftovers you have and it will turn out beautifully. Serve with rolls or crusty bread for a satisfying meal.
