Title:
'Faux' Stuffed French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/483566.jpg
Ingredients:
French Toast:
Stale french bread, about 6 inches
4 eggs
1 c milk
2 TBS granulated sugar
1 Tsp vanilla
Filling:
1 package reduced-fat (neufchatel) cream cheese
1 c confectioner's sugar
1 T Vanilla

Directions:
Slice the french bread diagonally in slices 1/2 inch thick
Preheat electric griddle to 300 degrees
In a shallow dish, mix the eggs, milk, granulated sugar, and 1 Tsp vanilla
Lay slices in Egg mixture and let soak 2-3 minutes
Turn slices in mixture and let soak another 2 minutes
Place slices on lightly greased, hot griddle
Cook until golden, turning once, about 5 minutes each side
Filling:
Beat cream cheese, confectioner's sugar, and 1 Tbs vanilla until smooth and creamy.
Layer cream cheese filling between two slices of french toast.
Sprinkle with confectioner's sugar (optional)
Serve with fresh fruit, fruit syrup, or maple syrup

Submitter: 
KY Piano Teacher
Description: 
French toast layered with soft vanilla cream cheese and fruit syrup - so delicious and easy.  This version helps you avoid soggy french toast from the filling.
