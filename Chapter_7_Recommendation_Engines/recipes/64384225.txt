Title:
Ranch Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb pork tenderloin
2 T. vegetable oil
2 - 3 T Ranch dressing powder

Directions:
Trin Tenderloin of fat.
Depending on time for cooking, either leave whole or cut into thirds.
Dry tenderloin with a paper towel and rub with vegetable oil.
Sprinkle over Ranch dressing powder, coating all surfaces thoroughly.
Grill on a hot grill pan until brown, then transfer to oven until 160 degrees OR grill on outdoor grill.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Super fast super easy last minute preparation for meat.  No marinating required.
