Title:
Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 c. + 1 tbsp. maple syrup
1/4 c. + 1 tbsp. honey
2 tbsp. all-natural peanut butter
1 egg white
1 tbsp. milk
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ground allspice
2 cups old-fashioned oats
1 1/2 c. whole-wheat rice crispies cereal
1/2 c. dark chocolate chips
1/4 c. dried cranberries
1/4 c. chopped walnuts
1 tbsp. wheat germ (can add more if desired)
1 tbsp. ground flax seed (can add more if desired)

Directions:
Combine first seven ingredients in one bowl. Combine next four ingredients and add to first bowl.  Mix well.  Add all other ingredients and mix well.  Mixture will be sticky and dry.  Press into 13X9 baking dish that has been coated with cooking spray.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes.  When warm (not hot), cut into bars and cool on wire rack. If they bars are not sweet enough for your taste, add a bit more maple syrup and honey in the beginning of recipe.
