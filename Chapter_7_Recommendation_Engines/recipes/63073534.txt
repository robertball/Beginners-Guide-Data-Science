Title:
Cool Whipped Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887257.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine pudding mix with milk and vanilla.
Mix until smooth.
Gently fold in whipped topping until no streaks remain.
Spread evenly over cake, be sure to refrigerate cake after it has been frosted.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
A light and cool topping vaguely resembling Bavarian cream.Modified from: Cool Whipped Frosting
