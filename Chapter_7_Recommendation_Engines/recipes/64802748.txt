Title:
European Chocolate Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/8432227.jpg
Ingredients:
1 TBL butter
4 oz. semi-sweet chocolate
6 TBL heavy cream
1 ½ cup powdered sugar
1 ½ vanilla
¼ tsp salt

Directions:
Slowly melt butter and chocolate over a double boiler.
Mix well, add heavy cream, and vanilla.
Add powdered sugar.
Mix until smooth.
This will keep several weeks in the fridge.
We actually keep some in a mason jar in our freezer.

Submitter: 
Stephanie Wentzlaff
Description: 
This is the topping for the Cream Puffs and French Custard Filling.
