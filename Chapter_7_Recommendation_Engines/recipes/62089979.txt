Title:
lemon balm pesto spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups lemon balm
1/2 cup olive oil
4 cloves garlic
one 16 oz jar whole tomatoes (or 6 fresh)
5 (or more) dried shitake mushrooms diced
3 onions chopped
1/2 t arrowroot
1/2 t salt & to taste
spaghetti pasta

Directions:
food process the first three ingrediants until the lemon balm is still chunky (makes it prettier).  set aside.
get a pot of water boiling for the spaghetti.
in a small saucepan, combine tomatoes, onion, and mushrooms (still dry).  simmer until much of the water is evaporated (15 minutes).  break up the tomatoes as you stir.
add arrowroot to thicken the remaining water.
add the lemonbalm pesto from the first step.  simmer another 5 minutes
add salt
serve over spaghetti pasta and with garlic bread.
