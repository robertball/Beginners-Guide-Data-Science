Title:
Creamy French Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Shredded Carrot
2 Tbsp Sliced Green Onion
2 tsp. olive oil
4 oz tub light cream cheese
2 Tbsp Milk
1 Can (14 1/2 oz) French Style Green Beans (No Salt added)

Directions:
Cook and stir shredded carrot and onion in hot oil in medium saucepan 1 to 2 minutes or until tender.   Stir in the cream cheese and milk; cook over low heat until smooth.  Add beans: heat through.  Optional - Garnish with fine carrot curls.

Submitter: 
Donna Ruark
Description: 
This easy is a different and delicious way to serve green beans.  Goes especially well with chicken or Turkey.  Actually from the back of a delmonte can.  Have been using it for years.
