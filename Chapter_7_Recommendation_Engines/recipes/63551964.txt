Title:
Roast Chicken with rosemary and lemon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken
1 tablespoon dried rosemary
salt
pepper
4 strips of lemon zest
1 small onion, quartered
1 T olive oil
1 T + 1/4 tsp. lemon juice
1/2 cup water
1 T flour

Directions:
Heat oven to 425.
Rub cavity of chicken with rosemary, 1/4 tsp. salt and 1/8 tsp. pepper and stuff with lemon zest and onion.
Twist legs behind back of chicken and tie together. Put chicken, breast-side up in a roasting pan.
Coat with oil and sprinkle with 1/4 tsp. salt, 1/8 tsp. pepper and 1 T lemon juice.
Roast up to 1.5 hours.
For gravy: pour fat from pan, into saucepan. Bring to boil and whisk in heaping T of flour + 1/2 cup cold water. Return to boil. Add a < 1 tsp. salt and pepper.
