Title:
Whole-Wheat Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4476354.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine, room temperature
3/4 cup Splenda Sugar Blend
1 cup Smuckers Natural Peanut Butter
2 large eggs
1 tsp Baking Soda
1 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Salt
1 tsp Vanilla
2 cups Whole Wheat Flour
1/2 cup Chocolate Chips (we used 1/4 cup white and 1/4 cup chocolate)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°; line cookie sheet(s) with parchment paper and non-stick spray.
In a mixer with paddle attachment, cream margarine and sugar until fluffy.
Add peanut butter and whip until combined.
Add eggs and vanilla, whip until combined.
Add baking soda, baking powder, salt, and flour. Mix until well combined.
Gently stir in chocolate chips.
Drop by tablespoon onto greased cookie sheet lined with parchment paper. Flatten with a fork in a criss-cross pattern.
Bake in a 350° oven for 12 - 15 minutes. Cool on cooling rack for 10 minutes before eating.
