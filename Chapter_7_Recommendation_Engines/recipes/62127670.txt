Title:
Amazing Roasted Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound asparagus (root ends snapped off or trimmed)
1 tsp olive oil
1 tsp Kosher salt

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
2. Line cookie sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Lay asparagus spears side by side on the cookie sheet and drizzle with oil.  Using you hands, coat the asparagus spears with the oil.
4. Sprinkle salt evenly over the asparagus spears.
5. Bake for 8 minutes.

Submitter: 
LouiseCol
Description: 
I live in asparagus country and we gladly eat our fill of it each Spring.  This is our go to recipe with easy preparation and clean up and clean flavors.  This quick, simple asparagus recipe will keep your family coming back for more!
