Title:
Wholesome Pumpkin Pie Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 cup whole wheat pastry flour
1/2 cup quick cook oats
1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
1/2 cup butter, softened
Filling:
1 (15oz) can pumpkin
1 (14oz) can fat free sweetened condensed milk
2 eggs (or use egg substitute equivalent)
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp ground allspice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
FOR CRUST:
Combine flour, oats, brown sugar and butter with a pastry cutter until crumbly. Press onto bottom of a 13 x 9" baking dish.  Bake for 15 minutes.
FOR FILLING:
Beat pumpkin, condensed milk, eggs, and spices with a mixer until smooth. Pour onto crust. Bake for an additional 35 minutes or until a knife insterted in the middle comes out clean.
Refrigerate and cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Trina
Description: 
A delicious lower-fat pumpkin pie filling baked onto a wholesome crust that stars oatmeal and whole wheat.  Good to eat, good for you, and easy to make!
