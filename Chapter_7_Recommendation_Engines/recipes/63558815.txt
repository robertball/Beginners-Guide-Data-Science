Title:
Spanish Wonder Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rice, raw
1/4 cup cooking oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1/4 medium green pepper, chopped
1 clove garlic
1 cup tomato salsa
2 cups chicken broth
1 cube chicken bouillon (or 2 tsp bouillon)
1/2  tsp salt
1 T chopped parsley

Directions:
In deep uncovered cast iron skillet, fry raw rice in oil over medium heat until golden brown, stirring occasionally. Be careful not to burn rice.
Lower heat to med-low.
Add chopped onions, pepper, and garlic and saute until tender.
Add salsa, chicken broth, bouillon, and salt and bring to a full boil.
Cover and turn heat to low and cook for 20 minutes stirring only once or twice.
Remove from heat and stir in parsley.
Serve or keep warm for later.
