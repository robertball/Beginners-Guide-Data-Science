Directions:
in milk to dissolve the yeast, add the sugar and set aside for 15-20 minutes, the yeast alive.
Mix the milk, yogurt, sugar, salt, egg,  vegetable oil. Mix well, add  yeast, sifted flour and knead a soft dough.
Cover the bowl with foil and put the dough in a warm  place for about 1 hour. Then the dough and divide obmyat into two equal parts. One part of Step 3 to remove again in a bowl and cover with a kitchen towel.
of the second piece of dough roll out the layer thickness of  3-4 mm (table podpylit flour if necessary).
Squeeze the dough glass mugs (I have a diameter of 7.5 cm). Remains of the test collect, obmyat again roll and squeeze mugs. Then cover the blank film, so as not to dry off.
On the roundels distribute evenly for 1 hour. l. mince (full), a pinch of cheese.  Fold it in half.  Then again in half. Tips slightly below  prischipnut.
Place the workpiece close to each other in shape  (diameter 28 cm).
Form greased with vegetable oil first,  then put on the bottom of the baking paper and it also  lubricate oil (so it will be easy  to choose pie forms).
When all the roundels are using, go to  the second part of the test in the same manner and to act as a  first.
To midway take 3 halves of roundels  spread stuffing. Impose a half overlaps each  other. Close the roll, starting from the bottom and put  in the middle of a pie. Shape to cover the film and give  time for proofing, about 20-30 minutes.
Lubricate the top lightly whipped mixture: 1 egg yolk +  1 tbsp. l. milk.
Bake in preheated oven at 180 t-° C,  about 35-40 minutes in the middle of the oven (15 minutes  before the end of baking to slow down the temperature to 175 ° C).
Ready to grease the cake with melted butter  oil (30 g ) and cover Towelie for 15 minutes.
