Title:
Amazing Lentils and Kale Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbls olive oil
1 cup shredded carrots
1 cup chopped onions
5 cloves minced garlic
1 bag lentils
6 cups vegetable or chicken broth
cayenne, salt, and pepper to taste
1 24oz jar chunky salsa, mild or medium
1 bundle kale, chopped

Directions:
Saute carrots, onions, and garlic in oil for about 8 minutes until softened in a heavy pot.  Add lentils, broth, and salsa.  Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer until lentils are softened, about 45 minutes.  Check for seasoning, add kale, cover and cook just until wilted.
