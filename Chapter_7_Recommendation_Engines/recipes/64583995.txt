Title:
Garlic Scapegoat Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-10 fresh garlic scapes, minced
8 ounces goat cheese, room temp
4 ounces cream cheese, room temp
1 clove garlic, minced
½ lemon, juiced and zested
1 teaspoon salt
½ cup toasted pecans, chopped

Directions:
Combine garlic scapes, goat cheese, cream cheese, garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest and salt with fork until blended. Portion into a serving bowl, garnish with garlic scapes and top with toasted pecans. Serve with fresh vegetables or pita chips. If serving later, refrigerate, and pull out of fridge 1 hour before serving.
Cook's note: Garlic scapes are peppery and garlicky, so select the amount to add based on your preferences.

Matt Wencl
Description: 
Don't resist the garlicky goodness and spring-fresh flavor of this zippy dip!
