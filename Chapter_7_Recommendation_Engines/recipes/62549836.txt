Title:
Cranberry Orange Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cranberries - cut in halves (frozen)
1/4 cup sugar
1 3/4 cups flour
3/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 tbsp. butter
3/4 cup orange juice
1 egg
grated rind of 1 orange

Directions:
Grease a loaf pan. or 4 small loaf pans
Preheat oven to 350
Combine cranberries and 1/4 cup sugar.  Measure flour, sugar, baking powder, soda and salt into bowl.
Stir to blend.  Cut in butter.  Make a hollow and add orange rind, orange juice and egg together, then stir into dry ingredients mixing just enough to blend.
Fold in cranberries.
Pour into greased pan.
Bake 60-65 minutes (Small loaves 35 minutes).  While still warm spread with glaze.  Mix 1 cup icing sugar and about 2 tbsp. milk to make glaze.
Cool before slicing.
