Title:
Di Sicilia Sausage Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (1-pound) package Rotini, cooked as package directs & drained
1 package Italian Sausage links, cut into 1-inch pieces
2 (26-ounce) jars mushrooms & ripe olives pasta sauce
1 (15-ounce) container ricotta cheese
3 cups (1 pound) shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
2 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F. In large skillet, over medium heat, brown sausage; pour off fat. Add pasta sauce; heat. In medium bowl, combine ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella, Parmesan and eggs. On bottom of 13x9-inch baking dish, spread 1 cup pasta sauce mixture. Top with half each of the Rotini, ricotta mixture and pasta sauce mixture. Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella. Repeat layering, ending with pasta sauce mixture. Bake 45 minutes or until hot and bubbly. Let stand 15 minutes.
