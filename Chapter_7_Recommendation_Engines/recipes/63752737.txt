Title:
Apple Cider Float Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 scoops vanilla ice cream
1/2 cup Apple Cider
1/4 cup Ginger Ale
garnish: ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a 16 oz. frosted mug, add in 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream. Then pour in apple cider and ginger ale. Garnish with a dash of ground cinnamon.
Add a fun straw and spoon. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Jeff Harrell
Description: 
Use these for a Back to School treat or for a Fall Fest Dessert. With a tiny apple slice, these were actually pretty fun to make miniaturized as a quick snack (or sweet tooth craving!)
