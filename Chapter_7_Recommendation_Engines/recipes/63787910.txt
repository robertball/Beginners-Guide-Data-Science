Title:
Brown Sugar Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup milk
2 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 small onion, chopped
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
3/4 cup finely crushed saltine cracker crumbs
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
In a mixing bowl, mix thoroughly first 7 ingredients and shape into a loaf.
Mix together, in a separate bowl, the brown sugar and ketchup. Then spread over the top of the meatloaf.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until juices are clear.

Submitter: 
Ashley Rauschnot
Description: 
This brown sugar meatloaf is glazed with brown sugar and ketchup for a moist and flavorful weeknight dinner.Modified from: Brown Sugar Meatloaf
