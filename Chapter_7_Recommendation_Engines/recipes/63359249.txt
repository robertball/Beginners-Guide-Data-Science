Title:
Mom's jello-ed yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large box jello, strawberry or strawberry banana
2 Cups boiling water
2 cups plain all natural yogurt
1 sliced banana or sliced or diced kiwis or pears
if using banana , dip in 7up or lemon juice to keep from browning

Directions:
put the powdered jello into a 8x8 Pyrex baking dish or other serving dish
add the 2 cups boiling water.  stir until dissolved.
let cool off enough to be warm not hot. (approx 100 degrees F)
gently stir in the yogurt. a whip is handy to get all the lumps out,
add the sliced bananas or other fruit.
chill until solid enough to cut with a pancake turner.1-3 hours
You can add whipped cream if desired, but not needed...
