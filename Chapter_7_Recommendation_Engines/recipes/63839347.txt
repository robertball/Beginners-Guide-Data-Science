Title:
Cloud Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all purpose flour
1 cup milk
1 egg
2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
butter or vegetable oil for frying
maple syrup for topping

Directions:
Separate the egg placing the yolk in a medium size bowl and the white in a small mixing bowl.
Add the milk, melted butter, sugar
and salt to the yolk mixture and combine.
In a separate bowl sift the flour and baking powder then combine with the wet mixture.
Using a whisk, beat the egg white until stiff and white.
Gently fold the egg white into the batter.
Melt a tablespoon of butter or vegetable oil over medium heat.
Spoon approximately 1/4 cup of batter and allow to cook for approximately 2-3 minutes.
Once bubbles form on the top of the cakes flip and continue to cook for an additional minute.
Serve immediately with warmed maple syrup.
