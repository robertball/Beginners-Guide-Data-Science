Title:
Pleasing Potato and Chicken Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. red potatos, cleaned and diced in 1/2" chunks
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breast, cubed
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 c parsley, rough chop
1/2 medium onion, minced
32 oz. chicken broth
4 T olive oil
2 T butter
Salt to taste
Freshly ground pepper to taste

Directions:
In large skillet, heat oil and butter over medium heat.
Saute onion and garlic in oil until tender.
Sprinkle cubed chicken with salt and pepper.
Add cubed chicken to skillet and saute until no longer pink and has a nice golden brown color.
Stir in broth and 3/4 c parsley.
Bring to a boil.
Add potatoes.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender, stirring occasionally.
Sprinkle with salt, pepper and remaining parsley.

Submitter: 
Brandon Willard Gossman
Description: 
This is a very hearty potato and chicken meal that my family raves about.  Very easy and quick, this meal combines everything in one pan.  This feeds my family of five with leftovers.  Leftovers are even better the second time around with this dish.
