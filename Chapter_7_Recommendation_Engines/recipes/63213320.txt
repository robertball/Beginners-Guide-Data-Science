Title:
Artichokes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 artichokes
button
garlic

Directions:
Peel off outer stems.
Cut top and bottom off artichoke.
Snip tops of artichoke stems if sharp.
Add tiny pieces of garlic and button between stems.
Steam for 45 minutes.
