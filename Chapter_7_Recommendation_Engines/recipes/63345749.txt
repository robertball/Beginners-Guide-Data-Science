Title:
Potato Casserole (Party Potatoes) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2 pounds frozen Hash Brown , thawed  Shredded or cubed  (I like shredded)
-½ cup buttermelted
-1 pint sour cream2 cups or 16 oz container
-¼ tsp pepper
-1 tsp salt
-½ cup chopped onions
-2 cups cheddar cheese
-2 cans cream of chicken soup
Topping
-2 cups cornflakes crushed
-¼ cup buttermelted

Directions:
Mix all ingredients thoroughly. Sprinkle topping on casserole. Bake at 325 degrees for 45 min.
