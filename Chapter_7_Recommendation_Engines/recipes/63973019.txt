Title:
Turkey Chow Mein Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. chopped cooked chicken or turkey
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1/4 c. chopped onion
1/4 c. chopped green bell pepper
1 c. chopped celery
1 5 oz. can chow mein noodles

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except chow mein noodles in sauce pan.
Heat and stir just to mix.
Pour into 13x9 casserole.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Preheat oven to 350.
Reserving some for garnish, sprinkle chow mein noodles over dish.
Bake for 45 min. or until bubbly.
Server over hot rice and top servings with reserved noodles.
