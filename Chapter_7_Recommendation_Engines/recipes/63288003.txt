Title:
Pizza Margherita on the grill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 minced garlic clove
2 whole wheat pizza crust
1 1/2 C shredded part skim mozzarella cheese
1 lb of fresh sliced tomatoes, nice to add in yellow or orange.
1/2 C fresh  basil leaves
1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Prepare lightly oiled grill for medium-high heat.
Mix oil and garlic in a small bowl. Brush one side of pizza crust with the oil.
Place oiled side down on grill and cook until slightly browned, about 2 minutes.
Flip crust and and oil the other side, then top with cheese, scatter tomatoes, grill another two minutes or longer until bottoms are golden brown.
Remove pizza to a cutting board and top with basil and drizzle with vinegar. Season with black pepper to taste.
Cut each pizza into 4 slices.

Submitter: 
*Sherri*
Description: 
This is a healthy grilled pizza to use some of those fresh garden tomatoes and basil.
