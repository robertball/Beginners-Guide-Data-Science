Title:
Rick's Yellow Cake Made with Honey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white unbleached flour
1  cup honey
1/2 cup shortening
3/4 cup milk
3  teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspooon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9" inch pans. Mix together the flour, baking powder and salt; set aside.
In a large bowl, cream honey and shortening. It will not be fluffy as with sugar. Add eggs and vanilla beating thoroughly. Add flour mixture alternately with milk, beat for two minutes. Pour batter into the prepared pans.
Bake at 325 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the cake comes out clean.
