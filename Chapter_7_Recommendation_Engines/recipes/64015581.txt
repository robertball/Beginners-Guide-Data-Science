Title:
Championship ribs - green egg Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slabs of ribs with membrane removed
1 c. dry rub
1 c. honey
1 1/2 c, apple juice
2 c. honey BBQ sauce

Directions:
Set the egg at 350 with plate sitter.
1 Cover the ribs with rub, two-thirds on meaty side and one-third on boney side. Let sit 30 min.
2. Cook for 1 1/2 hours using a rib rack.  Remove ribs to cookie sheet brush with the honey.
3. Put ribs in aluminum pan with about 1 inch of apple juice.  Cover with foil and continue cooking for about 1 hour.  keep adding apple juice to pan if needed.
4. Can than be removed from pan and brushed with BBQ sauce. Heat for a few mis, than serve.
