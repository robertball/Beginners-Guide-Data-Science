Title:
Moroccan Pumpkin and Lentils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbl. olive oil
1 large onion, diced (1.5 cups)
1 14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes, or 4 tomatoes, chopped
1 xcup green lentils, rinsed
1 Tbl. paprika
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 tsp. ground cumin
1 2 pound pumpkin, peeled, seeded, and cubed
1/2 cup tomato puree
1/4 cup chopped cilantro, for garnish

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large saucepan over medium high heat.  Add onion and jalapeno and saute 10 minutes, or until onion is translucent and golden.  Add tomatoes, lentils, and spices, and cook 2 to 3 minutes more.  Stir in pumpkin, tomato puree, and 2.5 cups water.  Season with salt and pepper.
Reduce heat to medium low, and cook 40 minutes or until pumpkin and lentils are tender, stirring occasionally and adding more water if needed.  Season with salt and pepper.  Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.
