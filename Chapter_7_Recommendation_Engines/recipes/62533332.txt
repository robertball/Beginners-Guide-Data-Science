Title:
Mom's Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags herb seasoned stuffing
2 cups chopped celery
2 cups chopped onion
2-3 granny smith apples, chopped
1 pkg. link sausage, fried & chopped
2-3 Tbs. sage
1-2 tsp. poultry seasoning
2 sticks margarine
5 cups chicken broth

Directions:
Combine celery, onion, apple and sausage
Boil margarine & broth.
Add to celery mixture, mixing thoroughly
Stuff turkey, put remainder in greased casserole dish
Bake turkey as directed. Cover & bake casserole stuffing at 350 for 30 minutes.

Description: 
My mom's traditional stuffing recipe.  It wouldn't be Thanksgiving dinner without it.
