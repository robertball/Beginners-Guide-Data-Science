Title:
CAJUN SHRIMP SKILLET SUPPER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 oz (284 g) shrimp ring
2/3 cup (156 ml) brown rice
1/4 cups (60 ml) spaghetti, broken up into 1/2" (2 cm) lengths
1 cup (250 ml) onion, chopped
1 cup (250 ml) celery, chopped
1 6 oz (170 g) package Mann's Snow Peas
1 tsp (5 ml) low sodium chicken bouillon
1 tbsp (15 ml) Mrs Dash
1 tsp (5 ml) cayenne (if using extra spicy Mrs Dash Omit this)
2 tbsp (30 ml) extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Saute rice and spaghetti  with onion, celery, snow peas and shrimp until golden brown, stir in 2 1/3 cups (550 ml) water, Mrs. Dash and cayenne (if used)
Bring to a boil then cover and  simmer 45 minutes or until brown rice is cooked
