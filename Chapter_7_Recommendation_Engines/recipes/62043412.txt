Title:
Shoyu Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs. boneless, skinless chicken thighs
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1 tsp. sea salt (can substitute kosher salt)
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp. fresh ginger, minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*. Place chicken in a baking dish so that it is in one layer. Combine sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, salt, garlic, and ginger and pour over chicken. Cover with foil and bake.
