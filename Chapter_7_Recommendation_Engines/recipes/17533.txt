Title:
Shrimp Tostadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983055.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked bay shrimp
1 (14.5 ounce) can recipe ready diced tomatoes, drained
1 cup chopped white onion
1 (4 ounce) can ORTEGA速 Diced Green Chiles
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
3 tablespoons lime juice
1 (16 ounce) can ORTEGA速 Refried Beans, warmed
1 package (10) ORTEGA速 Tostada Shells, warmed
2 1/2 cups shredded romaine lettuce

Directions:
COMBINE shrimp, tomatoes, onion, chiles, cilantro, vegetable oil and lime juice in medium bowl; cover. (May be prepared ahead and refrigerated.)
SPREAD about 2 tablespoons beans over each tostada shell. Top with 1/4 cup lettuce and 1/2 cup shrimp mixture.

Submitter: 
Ortega速
Description: 
You can substitute cooked lobster or firm-fleshed fish for the shrimp in this recipe if you wish. Seafood is a very popular main ingredient in Mexican cooking, especially in the coastal towns and villages.
