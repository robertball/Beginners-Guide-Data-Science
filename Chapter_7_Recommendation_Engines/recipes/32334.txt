Title:
Groovie Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6936966.jpg
Ingredients:
2 small bananas, broken into chunks
1 cup frozen unsweetened strawberries
1 (8 ounce) container vanilla low-fat yogurt
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
In a blender, combine bananas, frozen strawberries, yogurt and milk. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
Clara Simons
Description: 
Strawberries and bananas are blended with yogurt and milk in this easy and delicious smoothie.
