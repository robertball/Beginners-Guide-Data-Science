Title:
Simply Divine Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup peanut butter(creamy)
1 (8 ounce) package of cream cheese (softened)
16 ounce cool whip
1 tablespoon milk
gram cracker pie crust ( any kind- I use the oreo pie crust)

Directions:
using the mixer combine the peanut butter and cream cheese. Beat until well blended.
Next using spoon stir in gently the cool whip and the milk. Put into the pie shell. Chill in the frig for a least a few hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
SFLETCH
Description: 
This is a wonderfully light tasting pie thats perfect anytime. It does need to chill for a least a few hours. I usually let chill it overnight.
