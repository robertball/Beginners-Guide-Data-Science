Title:
Amy's Antipasto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
canned button mushrooms, drained
cherry tomatoes
black olives, drained
cucumber, cubed
pepperonci, drained
mozarella or provolone, cubed
salami, cubed
pepperoni, sliced
Caesar salad dressing (oil & vinegar based), 1-2 bottles depending on amount of meat and vegetables used

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and marinate over night.  Serve with toothpicks.
