Title:
Beef and Guinness Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean stewing beef
4 Tablespoons oil
2 Tablespoons flour
Salt
Freshly ground pepper
Pinch of cayenne
2 onions, coarsely chopped
1 large clove garlic, crushed (optional)
1 5 ounce can tomato puree, dissolved in 4 tablespoons water
1+1/4 cups Guinness stout beer
4 cups beef stock
2 cups carrots; cut into chunks
1 cup mushrooms; cut in halves
Tied together
1 bay leaf
2 sprigs fresh parsley
1 sprig fresh rosemary
1 sprig fresh thyme
1 sprig fresh savory

Directions:
Trim the beef of any fat or gristle, cut into cubes of 2 inches
Toss them in a bowl with 1 tablespoon oil.
Season the flour with salt, freshly ground pepper and a pinch or two of cayenne. Toss the meat in the mixture.
Heat the remaining oil in a wide frying pan
Over a medium high heat, brown the meat on all sides.
Remove meat and set aside.
Add the onions, carrots, mushrooms, crushed garlic, cover and cook gently for about 5 minutes.
Place saut√©ed veggies and beef in stewpot.
Add tomato paste to pan stir for 2 minutes
Pour the Guinness beer into the frying pan to deglaze the pan.
Add Guinness and tomato paste mixture into stewpot
Add beef stock
Add bouquet garnet
Cover with the lid of the casserole and simmer very gently until the meat is tender 2 to 3 hours.
Remove bouquet garnet before serving
The stew may be cooked on top of the stove or in a low oven at 300 F
Taste and correct the seasoning. Scatter with lots of chopped parsley.

Description: 
Rich and delicious stew, serve with mashed potatoes. Will make a Guinness lover out of everyone.
