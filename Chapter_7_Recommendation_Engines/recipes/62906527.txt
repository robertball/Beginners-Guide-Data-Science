Title:
CHEDDAR LOONIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/705920.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp.sea salt
1/8 tsp.cayenne pepper
1 cup shredded canadian sharp cheddar cheese
2 tsp.finely chopped fresh thyme

Directions:
Sift together flour,salt and cayenne in a medium-size bowl.Cream the butter for 2 minutes.Mix in the cheese and thyme.Add the dry ingredients to the mixture.Mix on low speed untill the dough comes together.
Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead gently.Form the dough into a disc,then divide in half.Roll each half into a cylinder slightly larger than 1 inch in diameter.[Loonies are slightly more than 1 inch in diameter.]Wrap each cylinder in parchment paper.Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 350.Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.Slice the dough into 1/4 inch loonies.Bake for 20 minutes or untill golden brown.
Cool loonies on a baking rack.
