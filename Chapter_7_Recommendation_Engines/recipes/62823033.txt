Title:
Tuna fish wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small cans drained tuna
½ large carrot, shredded
¼ cup chopped red onion
¼ cup chopped red bell pepper
¼ cup finely chopped cilantro
1/3 cup chopped golden raisins
1/3 cup chopped peanuts or almonds
Chopped jalapenos to taste
Dressing:
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup cream cheese
Juice of ½ lime
Curry powder to taste - ¼ tsp approximate
Jalapeno juice to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl combine all ingredients.  Add dressing and mix.   Spread evenly on large flower tortilla (this will make two rolls)
Roll up and chill for 1 hour before slicing.
