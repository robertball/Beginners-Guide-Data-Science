Title:
Crab Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion finely chopped
1 celery stalk finely chopped
2 tblsp. butter
2 tblsp. bacon grease
1 1/2 cup chicken stock
1 quart heavy cream
1 tblsp. fine chopped parsley
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon seasoning salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
Dash red pepper
1/8 teaspoon chili powder
1/8 teaspoon dill seasoning
Dash sugar
1 pound crab meat
2 tblsp. flour
* 1/4 cup sherry optional

Directions:
Sautee onions, celery, butter, bacon grease, and parsley untill browned in skillet.  In large boiler add chicken stock and onion mixture over medium heat.  Slowly stir in cream and seasonings.  Add crab and simmer for 15 minutes.
Make a thin roux and stir into chowder to thicken slightly.  *Just before serving add sherry. Optional

Submitter: 
LANAN
Description: 
This is a creamy Crab Chowder that is made with sauteed onions, celery, garlic, and parsley.  One of my family favorites.
