Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
1 onion, finely diced
2 cups rolled oats
2 eggs, beaten
2 tablespoons steak sauce
1/2 cup ketchup
3 tablespoons prepared mustard

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine ground meat, soup, chopped onion, rolled oats, eggs, steak sauce, ketchup and mustard. Mix well.
Shape into a loaf, and pat Into a loaf pan.
Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven for 1 hour or until done.

Submitter: 
jandjwhite
Description: 
Mustard, steak sauce, and condensed tomato soup flavor this meatloaf made with ground round, chopped onions, and rolled oats. Makes 9 generous servings.Modified from: Meatloaf
