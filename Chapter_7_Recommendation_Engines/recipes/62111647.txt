Title:
Family Favorite Breakfast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups Crispy Rice Cereal
1 Pound Sage Sausage
1/2 Cup Chopped Onion
1 Cup Cooked Rice
2 Cups Extra Sharp Cheddar Cheese, Grated
5 Eggs, beaten
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Cook sausage and onion together in skillet until browned.
Lightly spray bottom of 9" x 13" pan with cooking spray.
Layer ingredients in the pan as follows: 2 cups Rice Cereal, cooked sausage and onions, cooked rice, & cheese.
Mix eggs, soup, and milk together in medium bowl.
Pour mixture over layers.
Sprinkle with 1/2 cup cereal.
Refrigerate overnight.
Bake uncovered for approx. 30 minutes on 350 degrees, or until lightly browned.

Description: 
This breakfast casserole a favorite in our house. We don't just have it for breakfast. For dinner, I'll make it with Ham and Swiss cheese. Try a meatless version with black beans, chiles, and pepper jack cheese. The possibilities are endless!
