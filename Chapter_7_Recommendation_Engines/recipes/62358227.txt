Title:
Amazing Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
5 Strips Bacon
2T Minced Onion (Dried)
Sliced Cheese Of Your Choice
2 Splashes Worcestershire Sauce
A Splash of Liquid Smoke
1T McCormick Steakhouse Seasoning Grinder

Directions:
Cook the bacon untill it is very crispy
Chop up the bacon to small peices.
Next take the raw beef and add seasoning, onion, and bacon, mix well (I use my hands lol)
add worcestershire and liquid smoke, mix again
Seperate into 4 pattys
Fry or try grilling them!
