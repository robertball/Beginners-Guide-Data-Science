Title:
French Canadian dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter
1 1/2 cups sliced onion
3 cloves of crushed garlic
2 L beef stock
1 cup soy sauce
2 tbs Worcester sauce
500g thinly sliced "Montreal Smoked Beef" or if not available roast beef (sandwich meat)
150g thinly sliced back bacon (Canadian bacon)
2 bay leafs
10 sandwich baguettes

Directions:
- heat butter in large pot on meddium heat
- add onions and garlic to pot and suate untill onions are translucent in color
- add beef stock, soy sauce, Worcester sauce to pot and stir
- add roast beef and back bacon to pot one slice at a time
- add bay leafs to pot and bring to a boil
- simmer on low for at least 20 min
- use tongs to remove meat from the "au jus" and evenly distribute it inside the sandwich baguettes
- take out the bay leafs from the pot ladel whats left (the "au jus") to bowls for dipping
- serve hot
