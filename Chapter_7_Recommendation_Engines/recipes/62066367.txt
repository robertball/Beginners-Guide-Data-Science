Title:
Butter Pecan Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1/3 C butter, melted
1/3 C sugar
1 ½ C graham cracker crumbs
½ chopped pecans
Cake:
3 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
½ C sugar
3 eggs
2 C sour cream
1 teaspoon Vanilla
1 C chopped pecans

Directions:
To make crust, mix ingredients in food processor or by hand.  Press into bottom and part way up sides of 9 inch springform pan.  Preheat oven to 475 degrees.
To make cake, beat together cream cheese and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each.  Add sour cream and vanilla.  Stir in pecans.
Pour into prepared pan and bake for 10 minutes.  REDUCE TEMPERATURE to 300 and bake for 50 more minutes.  Then TURN OFF OVEN, open the door slightly and let the cake sit in the oven for ONE HOUR more.  Remove.  Freezes well.  Thaw in refrigerator before serving.
Makes 12- 14 servings. Very rich so slice small portions.

Submitter: 
PGS
Description: 
I have made this cheesecake for years and it is always a big hit. Note: you will need a springform pan.
