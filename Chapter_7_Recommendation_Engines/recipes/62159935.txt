Title:
Cilantro Lime Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 peeled cloves of garlic
1 large bunch of fresh cilantro
1/4cup extra virgin olive oil
2-3 fresh limes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Clean and chop 1 inch of the stems off the cilantro.
Add cilantro to food processor and pulse until chopped.
Cut ends off garlic cloves and add to processor and pulse quickly.
now quarter 2 limes and cut peels off roughly, a little rind is ok
Add limes to the processor with olive oil and pulsate.
Now add salt and pepper to taste.
Pour mixture over steak, chicken, fish or veggies and refridgerate at least 2 hours.
Grill with remaining lime on grill and Savor!

Submitter: 
REBECCADK
Description: 
A flavorful tangy marinade perfect for summer grilling. Fresh and light, it is also quite healthy!
