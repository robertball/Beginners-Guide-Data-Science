Title:
Tracy Ann's Favorite Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/668796.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 cup water
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 1/2 teaspoons Italian Herb
1 1/2 teaspoons crushed red pepper

Directions:
Mix together the tomato paste, water, and olive oil. Mix well. Add garlic, salt, pepper, Italian herb & crushed red pepper. Mix well and chill over night in fridge to let flavors blend. No cooking necessary, just spread on pizza crust, add toppings, cheese, and cook your favorite pizza.
