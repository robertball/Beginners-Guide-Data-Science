Title:
5 Minute Mug Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large coffee mug
4 T. flour
4 T. sugar
2 T. powdered cocoa
3 T. Milk
3 T. chocolate chips (optional)
3 T. Vegetable oil
1 egg
Splash of vanilla extract

Directions:
Stir dry ingredients into a mug.  Add in the egg.  Mix in the milk and vegetable oil.  Stir in chips and vanilla until well incorporated.  Microwave 3 minutes (may vary depending on the type of microwave) Cool.  Remove from cup.  Enjoy
