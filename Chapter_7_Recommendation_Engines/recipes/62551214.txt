Title:
Cream Cheese Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/530281.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth
4 cups peeled cubed potatoes
1/4 cup minced onion
1/2 stick of butter
1/2 tsp seasoning salt
1/4 tsp white pepper
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/4 cup bacon, chopped
1 8 0z block cream cheese
Shredded cheddar cheese
Diced japalenos

Directions:
Saute onion in butter till tender....do not allow to brown
Add broth, potatoes and seasonings.
Cook till potatoes are tender
With a hand held blender mix the soup (caution it is hot).  Either puree till smooth or leave some potato chunks
Add cream cheese allow to melt and combine with hand blender
Add bacon allow to simmer gently (do NOT boil) so flavors blend
Ladle into bowls and garnish with shredded cheese and jalapenos

Submitter: 
Lori Krol Rothfuss
Description: 
Tastes like a baked potato....and very warming on a winter night
