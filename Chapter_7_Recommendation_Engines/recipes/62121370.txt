Title:
Deviled Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 hard boiled eggs, shell removed and sliced
3 heads Romaine Lettuce, chopped
1 cup sugar
3 tablespoons yellow mustard
1 cup mayonaise
Optional:
Paprika sprinkled

Directions:
Chop Romaine lettuce and place in a large bowl. Mix the sugar, mustard, and mayo. Add water if too thick, 1 teaspoon at a time. Refrigerate until mealtime. At mealtime, toss the dressing with the lettuce. Place the sliced eggs around the salad and sprinkle on paprika if desired.

Submitter: 
Seahorsie
Description: 
It's a salad that tastes like Deviled Eggs. My family loves it. This is a recipe for a large family as I have 5 kids. Another experiment that really turned out great.
