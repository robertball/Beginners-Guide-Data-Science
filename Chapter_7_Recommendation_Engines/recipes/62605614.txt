Title:
My Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. ground meat
1 large onion, chopped
4 oz. can green chilies, chopped
1 pkg. mild taco seasoning mix
1 pkg. Fiesta Ranch dressing mix
1/2 -1 teaspoon chipotle seasoning
1/2 teaspoon jalapeno seasoning
10 shakes green Tabasco
10 shakes chipotle Tabasco
1 can yellow corn
1 can white hominy
1 can chili beans
1 can black beans
2 cans diced tomatoes
2 cans stewed tomatoes

Directions:
Brown the ground meat with the onion.
Drain and rinse the meat/onion mixture
Put all ingredients in an 8-9 quart pot.
Add all other ingredients; simmer for at least 2 hours.
Serve in bowls sprinkled with cheese.
