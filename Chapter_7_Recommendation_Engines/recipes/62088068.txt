Title:
Easy Box Cheese Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (8.5 oz) corn muffin mix, prepared as directed
1 tsp minced onion
1/2 c shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Add onions and cheese to prepared muffin mix. Bake as directed in a 8" square greased pan.
