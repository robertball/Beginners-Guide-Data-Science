Title:
Oatmeal Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quick cooking oatmeal
1 cup buttermilk (1cup milk/less 2tbls.  PLUS 1 Tble vinegar = buttermilk)
1 egg beaten
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
*Soak Oatmeal in buttermilk for 15 minutes:
**After oatmeal is done soaking, add egg, sugar and oil. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a separate bowl.
*** Then combined the dry ingredients with the oatmeal mixture.  JUST UNTIL moistened.
**** Fill greased or paper lined muffin cups 3/4ths full.
***** Bake at 400* for 16-18 minutes or until muffins test done.
****** Cool in pans for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack.
MAKES 8 LARGE muffins or 12 average size muffins.

Submitter: 
Delores Cromwell
Description: 
My kids LOVE this.  I serve it with dinners.... and left overs... (IF THERE IS ANY) is eaten up quickly at breakfast.
