Title:
Peaches and Cream Cake by EAGLE BRAND速 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979090.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package Pillsbury速 Angel Food Cake or prepared loaf angel food cake, frozen (for easy slicing)
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND速 Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 cup cold water
1 teaspoon almond extract
1 (4-serving size) package instant vanilla flavor pudding mix
2 cups whipping cream, whipped
4 cups peeled, sliced fresh peaches

Directions:
Cut cake into 1/4 inch slices; arrange half the slices on bottom of 13x9-inch baking dish.
In large bowl, combine sweetened condensed milk, water and almond extract.  Add pudding mix; beat on low, well.  Chill 5 minutes.  Fold in whipped cream.
Spread half the pudding mixture over cake slices; arrange half the peach slices on the top.  Top with remaining cake slices, cream filling and peach slices. Chill 4 hours or until set. Cut into squares to serve. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND速
Description: 
Peaches and fluffy vanilla cream are layered with thinly sliced angel food cake to make a simply unforgettable dessert.
