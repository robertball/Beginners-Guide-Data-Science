Title:
Cheesy Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) packages frozen cubed hash brown potatoes, thawed
1 (10.5 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
1 (16 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup melted butter
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a glass 9x13-inch baking pan.
Combine potatoes, cream of chicken soup, Cheddar cheese, 1/2 cup melted butter, onion, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; spread into the prepared baking pan.
Bake in the preheated oven until bubbling and browned on top, 60 to 90 minutes.
