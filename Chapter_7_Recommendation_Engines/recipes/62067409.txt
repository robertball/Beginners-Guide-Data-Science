Title:
World's Best Vegan Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups self-raising flour
2 cups soy milk
1 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp custard powder
Optional ingredients: blueberries, raisins,vanilla essence etc

Directions:
Directions:
1.Mix all ingredients in a large bowl and whisk until batter is consistent.
2.Cook on a greased pan until bubbles are formed then flip over and cook till brown.
3.Serve with Maple syrup.
World's Best Vegan Pancakes!

Submitter: 
Timothy Tang
Description: 
These are the best vegan pancakes ever created. The secret is to use custard powder which makes the pancakes taste and look like regular non-vegan pancakes.
