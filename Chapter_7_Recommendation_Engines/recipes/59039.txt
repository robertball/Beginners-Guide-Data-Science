Title:
Fruity Banger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/398474.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers vodka
1 ounce peach schnapps
1 ounce triple sec
3 fluid ounces orange juice

Directions:
Fill a tumbler with ice, and pour in the vodka, schnapps, triple sec, and orange juice. Stir and serve.

Description: 
If you're trying to find the right drink to get the job done and like things on the fruity side, then the 'Fruity Banger' is for you! It's all fruit and happiness.
