Title:
Savory Sandwich Ring Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 11 oz. package refrigerated french bread dough
3 cloves garlic, pressed
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano, ****
1/2 teaspoon dried basil, ****
8 ounces deli meats, (thinly sliced )such as hard salami, bologna, or ham
4 ounces cheese, (thinly sliced) such as Muenster, Swiss, or American
2 cups chopped lettuce
6 tablespoons Italian salad dressing, divided
1 onion
1 green or red bell pepper
1 tomato
1/2 cup pitted black olives

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.  Place dough seam side down on 15" Baking Stone, pinching ends together to form circle.  Cut six to eight diagonal slashes (1/2" deep) on top of dough with Serrated Bread Knife; spray with vegetable oil using Kitchen Spritzer.  Press garlic over top of bread dough circle with Garlic Press, spreading evenly with Skinny Scraper.  Sprinkle with oregano and basil.  Bake 26-30 minutes or until deep golden brown.  Immediately remove from Stone to Non-Stick Cooling Rack.  Cool completely.
Meanwhile, chop lettuce into thin strands using Garnisher.  Using Vario-Slicer, thinly slice onion, green pepper, and tomato.  Slice olives using Egg Slicer Plus.  To assemble sandwich, cut bread in half horizontally using Serrated Bread Knife.  Cover bottom half of bread ring with overlapping meat slices.  Top with cheese slices.  Spoon 2 tablespoons salad dressing over circle of lettuce.  Layer onion, green pepper, and tomato slices over lettuce.  Top with olives.  Spread bread with 1/4 cup salad dressing over cut side of bread top using Pastry Brush; place over sandwich.  Cut into wedges and serve.
