Title:
Margarita Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) salmon fillets
3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
3 tablespoons honey
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
fresh ground pepper & light dusting of cayenne pepper
lime slices for garnish

Directions:
Place salmon fillets in individual foil packets in a 9x13 inch baking dish. In a small bowl, stir together the lime juice, honey, and olive oil. Pour over the salmon and reserve at least 2 TBSP. Sprinkle with fresh ground black pepper and a light dusting of cayenne. Seal the foil packets and let sit for 30 minutes to marinate.
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (220 degrees C). Bake for 20 minutes in the center of the preheated oven, or until fish flakes easily with a fork. Serve immediately over bed of herb rice & organic steamed broccoli. Pour reserved marinade over salmon & garnish with lime slices.
