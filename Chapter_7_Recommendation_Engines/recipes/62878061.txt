Title:
Sun-Dried Tomato Basil Orzo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups uncooked orzo pasta
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil leaves
1/4 cup chopped oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes
2 tablespoons butter
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 14oz. can chicken broth
1 clove garlic

Directions:
1. Melt butter in heavy skillet over meadium-high heat.  Stir in orzo and saute until lightly brown. 2. Stir in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.  Cover.  Reduce heat abnd simmer until orzo is tender and liquid is absorbed 15-20 min.
3. Put sun dried tomatoes, basil, parmesan cheese, and garlic in food processor. Pulse until blended. May need some of the oil from the tomatoes.
4. In a bowl toss together the orzo and the tomato mix, salt and pepper.  Serve warm or chilled

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
Basil and sun-dried tomato add flavor and zing to this simple yet delicious pasta salad recipe. It's perfect for picnics, and great served warm or cold.Modified from: Sun-Dried Tomato Basil Orzo
