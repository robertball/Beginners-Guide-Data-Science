Title:
Brown Rice and Corn Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 15.25 cans of whole kernel sweet corn, drained
2 cup cooked brown rice, cooled
1/2 cup skim milk
2 eggs, beaten
2 tablespoons fresh chopped chives
2/3 cup whole wheat flour
2 teaspoon baking powder
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
Salt and pepper, to taste
Olive oil

Directions:
In a large bowl combine corn, rice, milk, eggs, and chives.
In a separate bowl combine flour, baking powder, nutmeg, salt and pepper.
Mix dry ingredients into the corn and rice mixture. Combine well.
Fry 1/4 cup portions of the mixture on a lightly oiled griddle over medium heat. Cook until golden brown on both sides.
Serve while hot.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
A delicious and different side dish that goes with any meat. It works equally well for a family supper or a special dinner.
