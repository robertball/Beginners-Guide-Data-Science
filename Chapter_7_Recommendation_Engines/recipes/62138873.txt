Title:
Easy Company-grade Pepperoni Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 medium onion, diced
3/4 package Hormel sliced TURKEY Pepperoni, diced
2 teaspoons minced or chopped garlic
3/4 package fresh sliced mushrooms
26 oz can ready-made "garlic and herb" spaghetti sauce
3/4 package of your favorite pasta...I use thin spaghetti.

Directions:
Heat olive oil in family sized skillet on medium heat
Add diced onion and saute until translucent.
Add pepperoni and saute for 1 minute.
Add garlic and sliced mushrooms - saute until slightly soft.
Add spaghetti sauce and continue to cook over medium heat till bubbly, turn heat to low, cover, and allow to simmer 10 minutes or until mushrooms are soft.
Serve over your favorite pasta, with shredded parmesan and a green salad.

Submitter:

Description: 
This terrific recipe is easy enough to make on a busy weeknight, but delicious enough to serve for company.  My husband raves about this meal and it is so easy!  With the right ingredients, this is a healthful, and flavorful choice.
