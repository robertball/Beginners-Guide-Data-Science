Title:
Squash and Red Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large squash, pre-cooked & cubed
1 large onion, chopped
1 tbsp bacon fat or oil
2 cups red split lentils
water to cover
2 tsp apple cider vinegar
2 bay leaves
salt to taste
Garnish:
sour cream - dollup for each bowl
cilantro - chopped
1 pkg bacon

Directions:
Cook bacon in oven at 385F for 20 minutes or until crispy.
Saute onions in bacon fat for 2 minutes, add lentils and saute for another 2 minutes. Add bay leaves and water to cover and cook until lentils or soft.  Add squash and cover with water.  Add salt and vinegar. Bring to a boil and then reduce to simmer until squash is soft.
Remove bay leaves. Use immersion blender to blend soup until smooth.
Serve soup with dollup of sour cream, pinch of cilantro and some broken up crispy bacon.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
If you have some squash that you've pre-cooked, this soup is so quick and easy.
