Title:
Sweet Potato Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the enchiladas
•3 sweet potatoes, sliced thin lengthwise with a mandoline
•1lb grass fed ground beef
•½ yellow onion, diced
•2 garlic cloves, minced
•1 (4oz) can diced green chiles
•½ teaspoon cumin
•¼ chili powder
•¼ sea salt
•¼ black pepper
•3-4 tablespoons Gold Label Virgin Coconut Oil
For the sauce
•1 (14oz) can tomato sauce
•¼ yellow onion, minced
•? cup vegetable broth
•1 tablespoon Gold Label Virgin Coconut Oil
•½ teaspoon garlic powder
•½ teaspoon oregano
•½ teaspoon chili powder
•salt and black pepper, to taste

Directions:
1.	Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2.	Use a mandoline  or knife to thinly slice your sweet potatoes lengthwise
3.	 Heat up a large skillet or griddle and place 2 tablespoons of coconut oil in it. Once hot, Cook your sliced sweet potatoes for 3-5 minutes per side or until sweet potatoes are soft (NOT crispy).  Place sweet potato slices on a paper towel to remove excess oil. Continue until all potatoes are cooked.
4.	Add in a bit of oil, minced garlic cloves, and onion to skillet - When the onion becomes translucent, add in your ground beef, green chiles and spices. Cook Completely.
5.	Add all sauce ingredients to meat and heat to boil.
6.	Pull out an 8×8 or 9×9 glass baking dish, add a spoonful of meat sauce mixture on the bottom and layer sweet potatoes and meat sauce.
7.	Add vegan cheese to top (optional)
8.	Place in oven and bake for 15 minutes
9.	Let cool for 5 minutes
