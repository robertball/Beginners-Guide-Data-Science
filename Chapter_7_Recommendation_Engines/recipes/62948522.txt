Title:
ROASTED CHICKEN & POTATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/725023.jpg
Ingredients:
6 BONE-IN CHICKEN THIGHS,SKIN REMOVED(ABOUT 2LBS.)
1 1/2LBS. SMALL RED POTATOES,CUT INTO 1-INCH CUBES
2 TBSP. OLIVE OIL
2 TSP. PAPRIKA
1 TSP. MINCED GARLIC
1 TSP. SALT
1/2 TSP. COARSE GROUND BLACK PEPPER
1 1/2 TSP. CRUSHED ROSEMARY LEAVES

Directions:
MIX OIL,ALL SPICES AND SALT IN A LARGE BOWL.
ADD CHICKEN AND POTATOES,TOSS AND COAT WELL.
ARRANGE CHICKEN AND POTATOES IN SINGLE LAYER
ON A FOIL-LINED BAKING PAN 15X10X1-INCH SPRAY
WITH NO-STICK COOKING SPRAY.
ROAST,UNCOVERED,PREHEATED OVEN 425*F
FOR 30 MINS. OR TILL CHICKEN IS COOKED THROUGH
AND POTATOES ARE TENDER,TURNING POTATOES OCCASIONALLY
