Title:
Our Backyard Grilled Peel and Eat Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound medium sized shell on fresh shrimp (approx 26)
2 tbs Olive oil
Fresh Italian parsley
Seasoned salt, like Lawrey's
1/2 cup salted butter
3 cloves garlic minced

Directions:
Cut the parsley where the leaves start, set stems aside.
Rough chop leaves.
Wash shrimp and pat dry- Place in a ziplock bag with  olive oil and stems that were set aside.  Refrigerate for a few hours.
Preheat grill to medium high.
Soak wooden skewers for 30 minutes.
Thread shrimp onto skewers. Season generously with seasoned salt.
Grill for 3 minutes a side until pink and firm.
At the same time, In a grill safe pan, place butter, garlic and chopped parsely. Melt and simmer until just golden brown. Remove from heat.
When shrimp is cooked, remove from skewers and drop into butter mixture. Toss to coat.  Serve immediately.
Get a napkin- you'll need it.
