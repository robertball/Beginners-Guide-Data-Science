Title:
Yummy Blintz Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2 or 3 packages of different blintzes defrosted
-.75 stciks melted margarine
-1 large container sour cream
-1 cup of sugar
-4 eggs beaten
-1 teaspoon vanilla extract
-Cinnamon (as much as you want)

Directions:
-Pour the melted margarine into bottom of large baking dish.
-Place blintzes on top of melted margarine.
-Beat together sour cream, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
-Pour that on top of blintes
-Sprinkle cinnamon over top
-Bake in a 350 degree oven for about 50 minutes.

Submitter:

Description:
