Title:
Grapefruit Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
22 oz filtered or sparkling water
4 grapefruit slices
2 rosemary sprigs
3 small jalapeno slices, seeded

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a 32-oz mason jar or pitcher.
Stir gently, cover, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (longer for more flavor).
Store in refrigerator up to 3 days.
