Title:
very easy chicken stuffed manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb. skinless boneless chicken breasts
1 pkg.uncooked large manicotti noodles
1 jar of perfered spaghetti sauce
1 bag mozzarella cheese
about a cup of water

Directions:
preheat oven to 350degrees. slice chicken into about 1 inch slices. not too thick that they won't fit into each noodle. after slicing chicken rub with garlic salt and stuff each noodle with chicken. place about a half a cup of sauce in the bottom of the pan and begin arranging stuffed noodles on top. combine remaining sauce and water in jar and pour over noodles. cover with cheese. cover with tin foil and bake for 30-45minutes.
