Title:
Cheesy Creamed Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 dash ground black pepper
Pinch of red pepper flakes
1/2 t garlic powder
1 medium head cabbage, chopped
1 medium onion, sliced
1 t olive oil
1 cup grated hard cheese (Asiago, Parmesan, Romano or combination)
Panko bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large frypan, saute chopped cabbage and onions in olive oil until slightly softened.
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt butter, add flour, salt and pepper, red pepper flakes and garlic powder. After flour is combined and browned slightly, gradually add milk, stirring constantly.  Stir constantly until a thick white sauce has formed.
In a 2 qt baking dish, alternate layers of sauce, cabbage and cheese.  Cover top with bread crumbs.
Bake approximately 30 minutes, until sauce and cheese are bubbly.
