Title:
Sweet and Savory Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. fresh green beans, trimmed and cut
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
1/2 medium red onion, sliced
1 teaspoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons brown sugar

Directions:
Melt butter in saute pan.
Add red onions to the butter. Cook until translucent, about 2-3 minutes.
Add brown sugar and cook about 1 minute more.
Add washed green beans and lemon juice.
Cook for about 10-15 minutes on med-high heat, or until green beans are tender.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
AppleChef
Description: 
A great side dish using fresh green beans. Light and delicious!
