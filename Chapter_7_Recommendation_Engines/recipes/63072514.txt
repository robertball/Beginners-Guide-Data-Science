Title:
Angel Food based  Cake in a Mug from Pininterest /AR's  What's for dinner mom Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mix together in a gallon bag:
1 1-step Angelfood cake mix
1 cake mix of your choice (lemon, chocolate, spice, etc)
Water (2-3T, depending on method used)
Toppings of choice

Directions:
Place contents of one 1-step Angelfood cake mix,  one cake mix of your choice (lemon, chocolate, spice, etc) in gallon zip-lock style bag.  Knead the bag gently to mix the dry cake mixes.  When you're ready for cake: In a large (12-oz or more) greased mug, mix 1/3 cup of the cake mix with 3T water and stir well. Cook in microwave on high for 1 minute. Or, in a cereal bowl, mix 1/4 cup of cake mix with 2T water. Cook in microwave on high for 1 minute. Top with toppings of your choice: chocolate syrup, fruit, nuts, whipped cream, etc.

Description: 
A simple cake-in-a-Mug recipe mixing a one-step angel food mix with a flavored cake mix.
