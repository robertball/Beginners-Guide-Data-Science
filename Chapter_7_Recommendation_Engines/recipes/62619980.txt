Title:
Nutty Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bag Spinach
1/2 Head Cabbage, finely shredded
3 - 4 Carrots, shredded
1/2 Cup Dry Roasted Peanuts
DRESSING:
1/3 Cup Sugar
1 Clove Garlic, Minced
1/2 Cup Oil
1 Tea. Salt
1/4 Cup Tarragon Vinegar; (or regular/your favorite)
1 Tea Paprike
Juice from 1/2 Lemon
1/3 Cup Catsup

Directions:
Mix Salad ingredients together.
Mix Dressing.
Add  dressing to Salad just before serving. (Pour little at a time till it gets coated as you want.)
Dressing can be saved for about 2 weeks if you do not use it all

Submitter: 
Debbie Cash
Description: 
Nice refreshing salad.  I use purple cabbage to make it really colorful and pretty.  I also use Honey Roasted Peanuts.
