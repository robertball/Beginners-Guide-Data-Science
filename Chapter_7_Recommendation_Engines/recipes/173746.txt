Title:
Pawpaw (Papaya) And Mango Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/239405.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sliced mango
1 cup diced, peeled papaya
1 cup orange juice
1/4 cup lime juice
1/4 cup white sugar, or to taste
1 teaspoon grated orange zest
4 cups water

Directions:
Place the mango and papaya into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth; add the orange juice, lime juice, sugar, orange zest, and water. Blend well. Serve over crushed ice.
