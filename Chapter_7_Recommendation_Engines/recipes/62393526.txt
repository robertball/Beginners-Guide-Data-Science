Title:
CHINESE SESAME CHICKEN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs of chicken parts skinned.
1/2 cup plus 1 tbsp water divided.
1/3 cup unsweetened apple juice
2 tbsp each, low sodium soy sauce and chopped green onion or scallion.
1 tbsp each, ketchup and brown sugar.
2 garlic cloves
dash of  of ground red pepper to taste
1 tsp cornstarch
2 tsp sesame seed toasted

Directions:
cook chicken in in heavy pan in a little olive oil until both sides are brown about 6-7 minutes.remove from pan and put aside.
in small bowl, combine all remaining ingredients except for 1 tbspn water and cornstarch
put in sauce pan and cook over low heat for about 2 minutes. add chicken and cook over low heat for 20 minutes.
remove chicken to plate and and combine the remaining tbsp of water and corn starch together in a small bowl, add to mixture in pan and cook until a little thick. pour over chicken in desired casserole dish and top with sesame seeds or your favorite topping.

Submitter: 
Diane Dizacomo
Description: 
this a great recipe from an old weight watchers cookbook from the early 80s, and not too difficult at all.Be careful not to overcook the chicken.I actually misread the amount of chicken for 1 lb and it came out just as good.
