Title:
Basic Chocolate Buttercream Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter or margarine, room temperature
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
1 box (16 oz.) confectioner's sugar, shifted
1/3 cup milk
3 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
In a large bowl, with an electric mixer on low, beat butter and 1/4 cup of cocoa until light and fluffy.
Gradually add milk, remaining cocoa, and vanilla. Add confectioner's sugar in small amounts at a time until of icing consistency.

Submitter: 
Kaley
Description: 
This is a basic recipe that allows you to make the choice of how sweet it is. Top cupcakes and muffins and especially chocolate zucchini cakes with this icing.
