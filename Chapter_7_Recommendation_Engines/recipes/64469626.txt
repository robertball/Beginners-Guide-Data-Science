Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
50 Chocolate Donut Holes
1 Almond chocolate baking bark bar
powdered sugar
1 bag of sprinkles

Directions:
If you wish to make your own chocolate donut holes make these first.
1. Grab a large plate or pan and cover in aluminum foil.
2. Take a bag of sprinkles (rainbow or chocolate are fine) and pour them into a small bowl. Set aside for later.
3. Take the almond chocolate baking bark bar and place it in a pan. Place the oven on low-medium heat to melt the bar. Stir the bar until copletely melted.
4. Take a donut hole and place a tooth pick in it. Hold the donut hole by a tooth pick and cover the donut hole in the melted chocolate.
5. Once the donut is covered in the chocolate place it in the bowl of sprinkles. Roll the donut around until it is covered in sprinkles.
6. Next place the donut on the plate or pan.
Continue steps 4-6 with the rest of the donut holes. Once this is done place the plate into the fridge. Refrigderate for 30 mins.
Sprinkle powdered sugar unto your dinomite fluffy bites and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Danielle Currier
Description: 
This is a fun easy recipe for any GiShwHES lovers who want to make a yummy dessert to honor our 2015 mascot Dinomite. Very easy and very tasty and fluffy!
