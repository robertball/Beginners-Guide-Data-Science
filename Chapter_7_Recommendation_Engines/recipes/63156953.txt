Title:
Famous Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1-3/4 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup gourmet Cocoa
1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1-1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup boiling water
Frosting
1/2 cup butter, melted
2/3 cup gourmet Cocoa
3 cups powdered sugar
1/3 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Heat oven to 350Â°F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
Stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt in large bowl. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla; beat on medium speed of mixer 2 minutes. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.
While Cake is baking in a medium bowl whisk butter and cocoa together. Gradually add Powdered sugar and Milk alernating.
Allow cake to Cool for 10 minutes; remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely.
Frost once cooled.
