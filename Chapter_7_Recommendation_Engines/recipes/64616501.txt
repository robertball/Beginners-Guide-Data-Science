Title:
Chicken Broth for Rice or Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6778509.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups low sodium chicken broth.
2 spring onions, cut in half.
4 large garlic cloves, broken.
1 sprig thyme
1 bunch cilantro, stems only
3 bay leaves
2 slices thick bacon lardons, cut in thirds.
1/4 cup corn oil

Directions:
In heavy saucepan, heat oil to medium.
Add all but bacon, and saut√© 10 minutes.
Add broth, simmer again 10 minutes.
Add bacon lardons, and simmer additional 40 minutes.
Strain for rice, and add fresh vegetables, and shredded chicken for soups.

Description: 
Can be reduced and reconstituted to save freezer space.
