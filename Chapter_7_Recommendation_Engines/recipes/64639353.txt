Title:
Smoked Stuffed Pork Loin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4510063.jpg
Ingredients:
-1 pork loin with silver skin and fat trimmed off
-1/2lb spicy breakfast sausage
-2 jalapeños diced
-cream cheese
-pork rub
-cooking twine

Directions:
-cut into loin while "unrolling" it into a flat piece of meat
-season both sides with pork rub
-spread cream cheese over meat then spread out diced jalapeños
-after cooking breakfast sausage, put an even layer of it on meat as well
-carefully roll up loin back into a fairly tight log
-use cooking twine to secure
-smoke at 220 degrees until internal temp reaches 140-145 degrees
-remove from smoker, wrap in foil, and let rest at least 20 minutes
