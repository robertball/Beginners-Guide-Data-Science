Title:
Italian Lemon Ice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2272392.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups water
2/3 cups sugar
2 teaspoons lemon zest
3/4 cup fresh squeezed lemon juice
1 oz Chambord (optional, for adults)
Mint leaves & lemon wedge for garnish

Directions:
In medium sauce pan whisk together water, sugar and lemon zest then bring to a boil over high heat. Continue to whisk and boil for 5 minutes.
Let mixture cool for 10 minutes. Whisk in lemon juice.
Transfer mixture to a 8x8 pan and freeze for 1.5 hours. Remove pan from freezer and break up ice with fork while stirring. Freeze again for 3 hours. Using fork scrape & mix ice into shavings.
Serve in chilled glasses garnished with mint & lemon wedges.
For a little something extra for adults top with 1 oz of Chambord.
