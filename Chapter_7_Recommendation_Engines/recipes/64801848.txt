Title:
Greek Pita Pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 flat pita breads
1/2 cup roasted pine nut flavored prepared hummus
4 slices provolone, cut in half
1 cup Monterrey Jack cheese, shredded
4 teaspoons dried oregano
4 Tablespoons Parmesan cheese, grated
12 Kalamata olives, sliced in half
8 pepperoncini peppers, chopped
1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
1 cup tomato, seeded and chopped
1 cup cucumber, seeded and chopped
1 cup prepared tzatziki sauce, for dipping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Lay the pita breads out on 2 16"x11" sheet pans.
Spread each pita with 2 Tablespoons hummus, starting from center and leaving small border around the edges.
Top each pita with 1 cut slice provolone, evenly spacing the halves onto the pita.
Next, evenly top each pita with 1/4 cup shredded cheese, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, 1 Tablespoon Parmesan, 6 olive slices, equal portions of pepperoncini, and 1/8 cup red onion.
Bake for 10-13 minutes until cheese is melted and pita is lightly browned.
Top each pizza with 1/4 cup tomato and cucumber, lightly pushing into cheese.
Cut pizzas into 4 slices and serve with tzatziki as a dipping sauce.

Submitter: 
BrewCrew
Description: 
Hummus in place of traditional tomato or cream sauce?  Yes, please.  Using prepared hummus and tzatziki sauce makes recipe is a superb time-saver.  Try as a quick weeknight dinner or surprisingly fresh brunch course.
