Title:
Cranberry Cheese Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
1 1/2 c. oats
3/4 c. + 1 tbsp. packed brown sugar
1 c. butter, softened
1 8-oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 14-oz. can sweetened condensed milk
1/4 c. lemon juice
2 tbsp. cornstarch
1 16-oz. can whole-berry cranberry sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In large mixing bowl, combine flour, oats, 3/4 c. brown sugar, and butter.  Mix until crumbly.  Reserve 1 1/2 cups crub mixture and press the rest firmly into bottom of 13"x9" baking pan.  Bake 15 minutes.
While crust is baking, beat cream cheese in small bowl until fluffy.  Gradually add sweetened condensed milk.  Mix until smooth.  Stir in lemon juice.  Spread evenly over prepared crust.
In small bowl, break up cranberry sauce.  Add cornstarch and remaining brown sugar, mix well.  Spoon evenly over cheese layer.  Top with reserved crumb mixture.  Bake 40 minutes or until golden.  Store covered in refrigerator.
