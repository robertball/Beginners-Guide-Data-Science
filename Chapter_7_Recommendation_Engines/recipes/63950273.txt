Title:
Baked Ziti I - Dre - easier version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb dry rigatoni pasta (2 boxes)
2 (32 oz) jars pasta sauce - 1 sausage, 1 meat (no more, will be too wet)
16 oz sour cream
48 oz italian cheeses, shredded (6x8oz bags)
Leaves of entire fresh basil plant, minced (or 2 packs of fresh refrigerated basil)

Directions:
Boil ziti to al dente, about 8 minutes; drain and cold rinse.
Preheat the oven to 400. Oil (2) 9x13x3 baking pans.
Mix everything together in large pot except half of shredded cheese.
Spread onto pans.
Top with remaining cheese.
Cover with foil and bake each pan for 45 minutes until ingredients are bubbling.
Remove foil and bake 10 or 15 minutes until top cheese is browned.
If storing for later, allow to cool in fridge before cutting
