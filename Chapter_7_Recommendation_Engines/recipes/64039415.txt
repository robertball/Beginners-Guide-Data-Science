Title:
Bistro Chicken Salad Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Breasts from Rotisserie Chicken
20 Grapes
Hand full of Slivered Almonds
Ranch Dressing (to taste)
Mayo (to taste)
6 leaves Romaine Lettuce
1 Tomato
Italian Bread Crumbs (desired consistency)
1/2tsp Sage
1/2tsp Thyme
1/4tsp pepper
Wrap (Any Flavor) or Flat Bread etc.
Drizzle of Olive Oil & Balsamic Vinegar(optional)

Directions:
Rough Chop Chicken Breast from Rotisserie Chicken.
Slice Grapes in halves or quarters. Thin Slice Tomatoes and then in half.
Mix with Mayo,Ranch,Slivered Almonds,Italian Bread Crumbs,Grapes,Thyme, Sage,and Pepper.
Rough Chop Romaine Lettuce.
Assemble Bistro Wrap.
Lay wrap flat and layer Romaine Lettuce and Tomato. Add chicken salad mixture.
Add Olive Oil and Vinegar if desired.
Fold Wrap and Enjoy.
