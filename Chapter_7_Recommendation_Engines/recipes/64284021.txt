Title:
Oven Cleaner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz. Dawn Dishwashing liquid
4 oz. (bottled) Lemon Juice
8 oz. White Vinegar
10 oz. Water

Directions:
Put everything into a spray bottle. Spray into a  cold oven, avoiding elements. Leave overnight and wipe clean in the morning.

Chemical free cleaning solution.
