Title:
Chicago Style Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 2.5 lb rump roasts
2 - 10.25 oz cans of beef broth
2 packages of Good Seasonings Italian salad dressing mix
1/2  jar of pepperoncini peppers
1/2 jar or giardiniera peppers
1 long loaf of French bread, Italian hoagie rolls or bread of choice
1 bag of shredded mozzarella

Directions:
Cook roasts, dressing mix, broth and peppers in crock pot on low for 5-7 hours.
Turn roast.
Continue cooking for another 5-7 hours or until tender enough to shred.
Toast and cut bread into desired servings .
Pile bread with meat and jus.
Top with shredded mozzarella.
Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Jessica Pisano
Description: 
Spicy and delicious Chicago inspired Italian beef. Add peppers if a spicier dish is desired. Either way, make sure to top with or serve a side of jus!
