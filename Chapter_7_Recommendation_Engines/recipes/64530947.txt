Title:
Crock Pot Balsamic Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb boneless pork shoulder roast (sirloin roast)
kosher salt, to taste
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1/3 cup chicken or vege broth
1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
1 tbs Worcestershire sauce
1 tbs honey

Directions:
Season the pork with salt, garlic powder and red pepper flakes and place it into the slow cooker.
Mix together the broth & vinegar and pour it over the pork.
Pour honey over pork.
Set timer for 4 hours on High or 6-8 hours on Low.
