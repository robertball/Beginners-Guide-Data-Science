Title:
Corn Bread Sausage Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3808890.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8-count can corn bread twists
32 smoked beef cocktail sausages
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup mustard
1 tablespoon honey

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F. Unroll cornbread twists and separate into 16 strips. Grease a large baking sheet.
Cut each dough strip into halves. Wrap one dough strip around each sausage. Place on prepared baking sheet. Bake until golden brown, about 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine mustard and mayonnaise in small bowl. Mix well. Stir in honey.
Place a small serving bowl in center of serving platter. Fill with sauce. Arrange cocktail sausages around bowl.
