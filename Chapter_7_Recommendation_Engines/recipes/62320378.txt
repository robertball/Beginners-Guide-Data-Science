Title:
Pan-Fried Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon coarse salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed

Directions:
Melt butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Stir in the olive oil, salt, and pepper. Add asparagus, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes, turning asparagus to ensure even cooking. Add garlic last few minutes and keep turning asparagus.
