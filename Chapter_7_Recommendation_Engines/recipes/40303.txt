Title:
Ro's Rum Runner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1513172.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 fluid ounces rum
1/4 fluid ounce coconut flavored rum
1/2 fluid ounce banana liqueur
1/2 fluid ounce blackberry brandy
2 fluid ounces sweet and sour mix
1 1/2 fluid ounces orange juice
1 dash grenadine syrup
1 wedge orange, garnish
1 wedge lime, garnish

Directions:
In a tall glass full of ice, pour rum, coconut rum, banana liqueur and blackberry brandy. Fill glass with sour mix and orange juice, then top with a dash of grenadine. Garnish with wedges of orange and lime.

Submitter: 
Arianne
Description: 
This is a fruity version of a Rum Runner that always brings people back for more!
