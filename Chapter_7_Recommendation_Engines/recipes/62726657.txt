Title:
Baked Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. brown rice
2 C bouillon (any flavor) I used beef broth
1 C water
1 C dried tomatoes
1/4 C onion
1/4 C bell pepper (juliene strips)
1/2 C sliced ripe olives

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In casserole dish mix all ingredients together. Cover and bake for about an hour-until the rice is cooked

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Take a little more cooking time than on stovetop but the added ingredients in this recipe make it so much better.
