Title:
Little Traveler's Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3301141.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
1 (10 ounce) package baby spinach leaves
1 small red onion, thinly sliced
2 cups cooked macaroni
12 ounces cooked chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 (12 ounce) jar roasted red peppers, thinly sliced
8 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
1/4 cup poppy seed dressing, or to taste
1/4 cup French-fried onions, or to taste

Directions:
Mix romaine lettuce, spinach, and red onion together in a bowl; portion lettuce mixture onto serving plates. Top each serving with macaroni, chicken, roasted red peppers, and bacon. Serve with poppy seed dressing and French-fried onions on the side.

Submitter: 
Rachel B.
Description: 
My mother-in-law served this for the 4th of July. It was a great meal for a warm summer evening when you don't want anything too warm.
