Title:
Crock Pot Venison Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030262.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground venison
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium yellow bell pepper, seeded and chopped
32 oz kidney beans
28 oz tomato puree
14 1/2 oz diced tomatoes, chili style
3 tbsp chili powder
1 tsp salt
1 large bay leaf, broken in half
1/4 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
4 tbsp ground cumin
1 tbsp cumin seed
1/8 tsp cinnamon
1 pinch ground black pepper
1/2 cups water

Directions:
Brown the venison in a stock pot (I use non-stick).
Add onion and pepper, saut√© until slightly soft, about 10 minutes.  When done, dump the mixture into a crock pot.
Add remaining ingredients and cook on low for at least 8 hours.
