Title:
Thirst-Quencher's Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 slices lemon
2 teaspoons lemon juice
Fresh mint leaves, optional
3 packets SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener
1/2 cup chilled soda water or water
3/4 cup ice

Directions:
Mash lemon slices, juice, mint leaves, and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener with a spoon in a tall glass until lemon and mint leaves are bruised.
Stir in soda water.  Add ice.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Fresh lemon slices and mint leaves release an abundance of flavor in this slightly fizzy lemonade.
