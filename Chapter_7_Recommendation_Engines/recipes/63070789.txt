Title:
Oven Roasted Red Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886985.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon garlic, minced
1 teaspoon basil
1 teaspoon rosemary
1 teaspoon parsley
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
4 large potatoes, cubed

Directions:
In a large plastic bag, combine everything.
Close bag, and shake until potatoes are fully covered.
Pour potatoes on a lightly greased baking sheet.
Bake at 400ยบ for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
