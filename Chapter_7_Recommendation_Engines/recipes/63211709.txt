Title:
Toasty Sweet Coconutty Graham Chex Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/832518.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (6oz) packages of slivered almonds
1 (14oz.) Bag of sweetened shredded coconut
1 Box of Rice or Corn Chex or half of both
1 Box of Golden Grahams
1 Â½ cup butter
2 cups sugar
2 cups corn syrup

Directions:
Toast shredded coconut and slivered almonds in separate jelly roll pans at the same time at 350 degrees. After the first 15 minutes remove from oven and stir every 4 to 5 minutes until coconut and almonds are golden brown.
In a large bowl, combine coconut, slivered almonds, Rice Chex, Corn Chex and Golden Grahams.
In a sauce pan, cook butter, corn syrup, and sugar to soft call stage (230-240 degrees). Then pour over Chex mixture in the large bowl. Stir until well coated.
Pour mixture onto 2 cookie sheets to cool. Stir occasionally as mixture is cooling to prevent clumping. Store in an air tight container.
