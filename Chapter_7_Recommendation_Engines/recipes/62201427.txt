Title:
The Ultimate Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/303840.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oz unsweetened chocolate
3 oz semisweet chocolate
9 oz self-rising flour
1 tsp baking soda
8 oz unsalted butter
2 cups firmly packed dark brown sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract
3 Egg Beaters egg substitute
0.5 cup reduced fat buttermilk
1 cup boiling water

Directions:
1. Melt the chocolate in a double boiler.
2. Sift flour and baking soda together in large bowl.
3. Put butter and sugar in a separate large bowl and beat until fluffy.
4. Beat in vanilla extract and Egg Beaters one at a time, beating well after each addition.
5. Fold in the melted chocolate and mix until well blended.
6. Fold in flour and baking soda mixture.
7. Stir in buttermilk and boiling water.
8. Divide between 2 lightly greased 9 inch shallow cake pans.
9. Bake for 375 degrees for 24 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cara
Description: 
The richest, fudgiest chocolate cake that tastes even better after being refrigerated. Top and layer with vanilla buttercream frosting for an unbelievable treat.
