Title:
Pumpkin Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust;
1 1/2 cups flour
2 tablespoons white sugar
1/2 cup butter, softened
Cream cheese layer;
8oz cream cheese
2 eggs
1/2 cup white sugar
Pumpkin layer;
2 cups pumpkin
3 eggs, separated
1/2 cup white sugar
1 envelope plain gelatin, dissolved in 1/ cup cold water
1/2 cup half & half
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tablespoon cinnamon
12oz whipped topping, divided

Directions:
Mix together flour, sugar, and butter with pastry blender, press into bottom of a 9x13 pan. Bake @ 400* for 10 minutes until lightly browned.
Meanwhile, cream sugar and cream cheese, add eggs and beat well. Pour over baked crust.
Reduce oven to 350*. Bake cream cheese mixture 20 minutes until set. Cool.
Mix egg yolks & sugar together, add half and half slowly. Over low heat, combine and cook pumpkin, egg mixture, salt, & cinnamon.
Stir constantly until mixture thickens slightly.
Remove from heat and add gelatin. Cool.
Beat egg whites until stiff, fold into pumpkin mixture, fold in half of whip topping.
Pour over cooled cream cheese layer, top with remaining whipped topping.

Submitter: 
Billie Miller
Description: 
This deliciously different pumpkin dessert features a cream cheese layer under a cooked pumpkin filling and a baked crust. Your guests will be wanting seconds!
