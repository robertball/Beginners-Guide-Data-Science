Title:
Keith's Shiny Sugar Cooking Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749902.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tsp milk
2 tsp corn syrup
1 cup sifted powdered sugar
food paste coloring
flavoring if desired

Directions:
Mix together the milk, corn syrup, and coloring and flavoring.
Gradually add in powdered sugar until it reaches desired consistency.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
This is the recipe used for icing his cookies for his Cookie Business. It is stackable when dried.
