Title:
Mexican rice #2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked white rice rinsed thoroughly
3 tbs olive oil
2 cups chicken broth
2-3 tbs dried onion
1 cup salsa (my homemade)

Directions:
rinse rice until water runs clear.  heat olive oil on high.  add rice and dried onion.  stir fry until slightly brown bout 5 minutes adjusting heat as necessary so not to burn.  add 2 cups chicken broth and salsa.  cook until tender and juice is gone.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a quick and easy recipe for authentic Mexican / Spanish rice.
