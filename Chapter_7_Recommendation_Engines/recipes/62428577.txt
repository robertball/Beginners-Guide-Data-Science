Title:
Gluten-free & So Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/449419.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C all-purpose gluten-free flour
1/4 tsp xanthan gum
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/3 C salted butter, melted
1/2 C packed brown sugar
1/4 C white sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 egg
1 C semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 325 degrees. Grease or line your cookie sheet with parchment paper
In a small bowl mix flour, xanthan gum and baking soda. Set aside.
In a medium bowl cream butter and sugars until well blended. Beat in vanilla and egg until light and creamy. Mix in dry ingredients until just mixed. Stir in chocolate chips with a wooden spoon. Drop dough by Tbl on to your cookie sheet.
Bake 10-12 minutes, or until edges are lightly toasted. Cool on the cookie sheets.
