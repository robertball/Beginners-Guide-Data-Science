Title:
Dead Man Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb lean ground beef
1 tbsp butter or veggie oil
1 med onion, chopped
1 cup celery diced
1 tbs garlic or garlic powder
2 eggs
1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1 tbsp cajun seasoning
1 cup ketchup

Directions:
Saute onion, celery and garlic in butter or oil in a skillet until soft.  In a large bowl mix ground beef and cooked veggies together.
Form into stick man on a lightly greased sheet or jelly roll pan. Bake 35 minutes in a 350 degreee oven. Add sliced olives for eyes and about 1 cup of ketchup to abdomen and bake about 10 minutes more. Serve with a plastic knife impailed in the abdomen and a side of macaroni and cheese.

Submitter: 
MissB
Description: 
Traditional meatloaf made creepy. Kids love this. Be sure to feed them before they go trick or treating.
