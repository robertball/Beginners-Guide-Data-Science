Title:
Peachy Peach Custard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
crust: 3 cups of flour
1 1/2 to 2 sticks butter softened
1 tsp of salt ( optional)
1/2 tsp of cinnamon
1/2 tsp sugar
Custard: 2 large cans of sliced peaches drained well
(reserve 1 cup of peach juice for custard)
1 cup of sugar w/ 2 tsp of cinnamon mixed in (you can use less or more depending on taste)
2 12 oz. cans of evaporated milk
3 eggs

Directions:
To make the crust cut flour,butter and salt,cinnamon and sugar together till crumbly.
Put it In a 9x13 pan and start with sides first and work your way around the pan to the bottom.Be sure to not make your corners to thick with the flour mixture.
Layer peaches in bottom of the pan and then  mix cinnamon and sugar together in seperate bowl and sprinkle the mixture on top of peaches .
put in a 350 degree oven for 20 minutes.(for higher altitudes adjust oven to 400 degrees.)
While peaches are cooking:beat together in a large bowl the reserved peach juice,milk and eggs and mix well .
After the 20 minutes in the oven take pan out and pour the custard over peaches and bake 40 to 50 minutes or till center sets.

Submitter: 
Sonya
Description: 
A creamy custard peach pie that is easy to make and tastes great. My mom made this for us kids growing up on holidays or special occasions.....it was so popular that it turned out to be my brother's birthday cake he loved it so much.
