Title:
Angel Lush With Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (20oz) crushed pineapple, undrained
1 pkg (3.4oz) vanilla flavor instant pudding (can use sugar-free)
1C thawed whipped topping
10 oz round angel food cake, cut into 3 layers
Seasonal berries

Directions:
Mix pineapple and dry pudding mix in med. bowl. Gently stir in whipped topping
Stack cake layers on plate, spreading pudding mixture between layers and on top of cake
Refrigerate 1 hr or until ready to serve. Garnish with favorite seasonal berries

Judy Bodnar
