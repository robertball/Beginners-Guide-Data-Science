Title:
party pineapple casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 200z. pineapple chunks
1/4 cup sugar
3 tbs flour
1 stick butter melted
1 cup shred sharp ceddar
3/4- 1 cup crushed buttery crackers

Directions:
drain pineapples, saving 3 tbs of juice
combine flour, sugar, melted butter & cheese
add pineapple juice mix well
gently fold in chunks.
pour into greased casserole dish.
bake @ 350 for 20 min
remove from oven & sprinkle crackers over the top- dot with butter
return to oven & cook 10 more minutes
