Title:
Chicken Kiev Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c melted butter
4 chicken breasts, skinned
Mixture #1
1/2 c bread crumbs
1/4 c Parmesan cheese
2 tsp Italian Seasoning
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp each salt, pepper, Greek seasoning, & seasoned salt
Mixture #2
1/4 c white cooking wine
1/4 c chopped green onions
1/4 c parsley flakes

Directions:
Dip chicken breasts in melted butter then dip into mixture #1.  Put in baking dish and bake uncovered 45 min at 375 degrees.  Save remaining melted butter.  Add it to mixture #2.  When chicken is golden brown pour this mixture over chicken.  Continue baking 10 more min.  Serve drippings as sauce.

Submitter: 
Nicole
Description: 
Our favorite version of Chicken Kiev. We acquired this recipe from a family friend years ago, one of the few things my brother would offer to cook growing up! Great served with noodles.
