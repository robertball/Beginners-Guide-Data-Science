Title:
Leftover Ham Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped cooked ham
1 cup elbow macaroni or penne
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup milk
1 can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 teasp. black pepper
1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
1/2 teasp. cayenne pepper
1/4 teasp. red pepper flakes
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).Cook pasta until eldente
In a large bowl combine the ham/leftover meat, macaroni, cheese, milk, soup, vegetables, pepper, cayenne pepper and red pepper flakes and mix well. Spread mixture into a lightly greased top with bread crumbs cook in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes, or until hot and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Patricia Foreman
Description: 
This is a great way to use leftover ham. A creamy soup mixture of ham, pasta, cheese and veggies provides a great next day casserole.Modified from: Leftover Casserole
