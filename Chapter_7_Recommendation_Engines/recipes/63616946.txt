Title:
Parker's Favorite Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1005981.jpg
Ingredients:
260g self rising flour (or 2 cups)
40g plain flour (or 1/3 cup)
1 tsp ground cinnamon
140g brown sugar (or 2/3 cup)
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup + 1 TBSP milk
2 eggs, whisked
4 TBSP butter, melted and cooled
3 very ripe bananas, mashed

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a loaf pan with cooking spray.
Combine the flours, cinnamon and sugar in a medium bowl. Set aside.
In a large bowl, mix the milk, eggs, vanilla, melted butter and mashed bananas.
Next add the flour mixture into the banana mixture and stir just until combined. Do not over mix.
Spoon everything into the loaf pan. Bake for 45-55 minutes or until a knife comes out clean. Do not over bake.
Remove loaf from pan and cool on a cooling rack.

Submitter: 
Kristin McNeil
Description: 
I have tried many different banana bread recipes. My son and I think this recipe is the best! It's also super easy!
