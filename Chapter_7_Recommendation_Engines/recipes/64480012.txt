Title:
Lavender Ice Cubes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2613209.jpg
Ingredients:
12 fresh lavender flowers
3 cups boiling water

Directions:
Place the lavender flowers in an empty ice cube tray.
Bring the water to a boil. Then, pour the boiling water into the ice cube tray, over the lavender.
Let cool before placing in the fridge to freeze.

Submitter: 
Heather
Description: 
Lavender ice cubes are perfect to have on hand whether you're adding it to a mild cocktail, punch or simply adding flavor to your water.
