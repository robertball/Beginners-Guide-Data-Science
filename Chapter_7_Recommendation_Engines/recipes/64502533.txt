Title:
Caramel Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup caramel topping
1tsp cinnamon
! cup flour
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1tsp cinnamon

Directions:
1. Peal and slice apples.
2. Mix cinnamon and caramel topping and toss apples with mix. Put mix in pan.
3.  Mix flour, sugars, cinnamon and butter and sprinkle over apples.
4. Bake in 375 oven
