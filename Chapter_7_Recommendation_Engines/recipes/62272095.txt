Title:
White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless/Skinless Chicken Breast
2 Med. Onions Chopped
48oz. Northern Beans (Do not drain)
2 cans Chicken Broth
14oz. Salsa
2 tsp. Chili Powder

Directions:
Place chicken in bottom of crock pot.
Add onions,beans, broth, salsa, chili powder.
Cook on HIGH for 1 hour
Reduce temp. to LOW for 6 hours
Shredd chicken half way through cooking time.

Submitter: 
Richard Godfrey
Description: 
A family favorite on a cold day.  It can be spiced up or down to your tastes.
