Title:
Perogie Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pizza dough ingredients:
3cups all purpose flour
2 1/4tsp yeast
2 tsp sugar
1cups warm water
1stp salt
1/4 cup olive oil
toppings ingredients:
6 potatoes thinly sliced
250 ml sour cream
2tbsp garlic powder
1 brick marble cheese (shredded)
1 bundle greeen onions (chopped)
bacon bits to taste

Directions:
Add sugar and yeast to warm water and let sit for a few minutes until yeast bubbles
In seperate bowl blend flour and salt, once yeast is ready add to flour mixture along with olive oil mix well until all ingredients are well incorporated and a stick dough is made.Let rise for an hour.
While dough is rising take thinly sliced potatoes and in deep frying pan or pot add enough water to cover potatoes and cook until potatoes are soft but still crispy.
Once potatoes are ready remove from water add a thin layer of oil to bottom of pan and fry potatoes lightly.
Add galic powder to sour cream this will be your sauce.
Spread dough out onto  pizza stone.
Spread a layer of the sauce on pizza dough.
Lay potatoes out on top of sauce covering dough entirely. Cover with shredded cheese sprinkle bacon bits and chopped geen onions on top.
Bake at 400F for 15 to 20 min or until crust looks golden brown.
Slice and serve with plain sour cream to dip in.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth Herrera
Description: 
Tastes like perogies on a pizza. Two of my favourite things blended together. Tastey potatoes on a pizza crust covered in all the traditional perogy fixings, bacon, cheese and green onions.
