Title:
HAZELNUT CREAM CREPES WITH RASPBERRY SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crepes
4 		eggs
2-2/3 cups 	milk
2-1/2 cups 	Robin Hood Cranberry Orange Pancake Mix
Filling
1 pkg. (250g) 	cream cheese, softened
1/4  cup 	icing sugar, sifted
1 envelope 	Dr. Oetker Vanilla Sugar
1/2  cup 	ground hazelnuts
1 cup 		whipping (35%) cream, whipped stiff
Sauce
1 pkg (15oz/425g) frozen whole raspberries in syrup
Orange juice
1/3 cup 	granulated sugar
3 Tbsp.	Cornstarch
1 Tbsp. 	Lemon juice

Directions:
Crepes:  Combine all ingredients, whisking until smooth.  Heat lightly greased 6‚Äù crepe pan or frying pan.  Remove from heat.  Spoon in about 3 tbsp. batter.  Lift and tilt pan to cover bottom with batter.  Return to heat. Brown lightly; turn and brown other side.  Repeat with remaining batter.
Filling:  Beat cream cheese, icing sugar and vanilla sugar until smooth.  Fold in hazelnuts and whipped cream.
Sauce:  Drain raspberries reserving the juice.  Add orange juice to raspberry juice to make 2 cups.  Mix sugar, cornstarch and raspberry juice in saucepan.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a boil.  Remove from heat.  Stir in lemon juice and reserved raspberries.  Keep warm or cool to room temperature.
Assembly:  Fill crepes.  Roll up.  Top with sauce.
