Title:
Chicken and Egg Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups egg noodles
3 stalks chopped celery
4 Tbsp. butter
4 chicken bouillon cubes
1/4 cup flour
2 cups water
1 Tbsp. thyme
1 to 2 cups of milk
2 chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Boil 6 cups egg noodles as directed on package.  Drain.  Pour in casserole dish and set aside.
While egg noodles are cooking, in large saucepan cook celery in butter with bouillon cubes until soft.  Mix flour with water and add to pan with thyme.  Boil for 5 minutes or until very thick. Slowly add milk and simmer until medium thickness. Add chicken pieces.
Pour celery sauce over egg noodles and sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top. Serve immediately or bake in microwave or 350 degree oven until hot and bubbly.

Submitter:

Description: 
A homemade celery sauce and cubed chicken is served over cooked egg noodles and sprinkled with Parmesan cheese.
