Title:
My Mum's Potatoe Latkes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4619558.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large Russet potatoes, peeled and grated
1 small yellow onion, grated
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
2-3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2-3/4 cup oil for frying
To top off:
Applesauce, Sour Cream and sliced smoked salmon/lox

Directions:
Peel and grate potatoes and onion and mix together in large mixing bowl.
Let stand approx 10 minutes so liquid will rise to top.
Remove excess liquid and stir in eggs, pepper, salt, baking powder  and all-purpose flour to make mixture hold.
Heat oil in  375 degree electric skillet. Drop spoonfuls into hot oil and fry till bottoms are golden brown and crisp approx 5 to 7 minutes. Flip and fry other side till golden brown.
Repeat spoonfuls till all Potatoe Latkes mixure is used.
Line baking sheet pan with brown paper bags or paper towels and place latkes to drain excess oil.
Serve hot with apple sauce or sour cream and top off with some sliced smoked salmon/lox.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
A traditional Hanukkah favorite. Simple and easy to make. Fantastic anytime of the year.
