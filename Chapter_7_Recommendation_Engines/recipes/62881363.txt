Title:
spaghetti and meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce
1 large onion, 2 garlic cloves (minced), 2 tbsp veg or olive oil, 3 (10.75 oz) cans tomato puree, 1.5 c water, .25 c grated parmesan, 1tbsp dried oregano, 1 tbsp salt, 1 tbsp sugar.
Meatballs
1 lbs ground beef, .25 lbs italian sausage, .75 c crushed saltines, 4 eggs (beaten), 2 cloves garlic (minced), 3 tbsp greated parmesan, 1 tsp dried oregano.
Hot cooked Pasta

Directions:
In dutch oven, saute onion and garlic in oil until tender. Add the next seven ingreients; mix well. Simmer, uncovered, for 1.5 hrs. Meanwhile, combine beef,pork, and cracker crumbs in a large bowl. Add the eggs, garlic, Paresan, and oregano; mix well. Shape into 1.5 in balls; brown in a skillet, turning once. Add to sauce; simmer, uncovered, 1.5 hrs longer. Serve over pasta.
