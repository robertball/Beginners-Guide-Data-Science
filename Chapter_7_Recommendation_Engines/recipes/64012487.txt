Title:
Mama Collie's Real Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
2 tablespoons flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 cups milk
3 eggs, separated
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 box vanilla wafers
2 - 3 bananas
6 tablespoons  sugar

Directions:
-Slightly beat egg yolks
-Add milk
-Combine the first three ingredients
-Mix together in top of double boiler
-Cook on low, stirring constantly, until the consistency of custard
-Add vanilla
-Line a baking dish with vanilla wafers
-Slice bananas over the wafers
-Pour half of the custard mix over wafers/ bananas
-Repeat with another layer of wafers, then bananas, then custard
-Whip the egg whites with the 6 tablespoons of sugar
-Beat until stiff
-Put this meringue on top of pudding
-Bake 425 degrees about five minutes, or until brown

Submitter: 
aunt star
Description: 
My grandparents ran a restaurant before I was born, but she could always cook for a crowd. This is a true Southern banana pudding.
