Title:
Caesar Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head romaine lettuce
1 cup croutons
1/2 tub parmesan cheese
1/4-1/3 bottle of caesar dressing

Directions:
Tear lettuce into bite size pieces and place in large bowl.
Add croutons, parmesan cheese, and salad dressing. Toss salad and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
SST
Description: 
You may substitute packaged caesar salad mix if budget allows. Prep time is based on recipe size and may take longer depending on how much lettuce you have to tear.
