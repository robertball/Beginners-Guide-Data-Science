Title:
Butterscotch Dessert Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup light brown sugar
1 cup light corn syrup
1/4 tsp. salt
2 cups light cream, divided
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Melt butter over low heat in large saucepan. Add sugars, syrup, salt and 1 cup cream. Cook over medium heat, stirring, until mixture reaches soft ball stage (234 degrees), about 15 minutes. Add remaining 1 cup cream, bring mixture back to a boil, stirring, and gently boil for 2 minutes. Remove from heat, add vanilla. Serve warm or at room temperature. Delicious on ice cream, pound cake, or sliced fruit.
