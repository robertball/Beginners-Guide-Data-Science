Title:
The Olive Aioli  for Mini Lamb Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg yolk
1 Tbsp grainy Dijon mustard
1/2 lemon, juice only
4-6 cloves of garlic, pureed
1-1 1/2 c extra virgin olive oil
2-3 green onions or a bunch of chives, finely chopped
2 Tbsp minced parsley
1 tsp finely minced fresh rosemary
1/2 c finely minced Kalamata olives (i omitted these)
hot chili flakes to taste
salt to taste
1/2 Tbsp red wine vinegar (optional)

Directions:
In a non-reactive bowl whisk together the egg, mustard and lemon juice, then slowly drizzle in the oil, whisking thoroughly as you do to form an emulsion. Add the remaining ingredients, balancing the flavors at the end. Cover and chill.

Submitter: 
Here's To Good Friends
Description: 
Use this anywhere a mayo with gusto is needed - in pasta salad, with roasted flank steak, on sandwiches, as garnish for crudites, even as a dip.
