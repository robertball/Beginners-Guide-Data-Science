Title:
My Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/716623.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups of all purpose flour
1 1/2 tablespoons of salt
1 tablespoon of Onion powder
1 tablespoon of Garlic powder
1 tablespoon of cyan pepper powder
1 tablespoon of black pepper

Directions:
Take chicken and coat with flour mixture, put in refrigerator for 20 mins on a dish keeping the chicken separated.
Take two eggs, and milk and mix in a bowl
dip chicken in egg mixture, and then in flour mixture put in refrigerator for 20 mins on a dish keeping the chicken separated.
Preheat oven to 300F
Preheat deep fryer to 375F once temperature is reached, Reduce deep fryer to 350F, and put chicken in the deep fryer 15 mins, do not overload the fryer, Maybe 3 or 4 pieces
Place in Oven Too Keep Warm, And to finish cooking.
If you make another batch, return temperature to 375F, once the temperature is reached reduce heat to 350F, and put more chicken in.
Place in oven for 15 mins Too Keep Warm, And to finish cooking, As you make french fries.
