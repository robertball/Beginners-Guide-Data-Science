Title:
Spinach and Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen spinach
2 cups fresh pumpkin, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 can tuna, drained
4 slices anchovies
2 tablespoons cream cheese
1/2 cup cheddar cheese
nutmeg
oregano
8oz puff pastry
vegetable oil for frying
2 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 380 degrees F
Place spinach and pumpkin in a microwave steamer and microwave until tender, about 5 minutes
In a medium skillet, heat oil and cook onion until translucent. Add spinach and pumpkin and cook for a further 2 minutes
Remove frying pan from heat and add tuna, anchovies, cream cheese, cheddar cheese, nutmeg, and oregano. Stir with a fork, mashing the pumpkin slightly
Roll the dough into a circular shape and place on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper
Place the filling in the centre of the dough and fold like a wrap. Cut 3 vertical strips on the surface of the pie
Brush the dough with milk and bake in the pre-heated oven for 20 minutes, or until dough is golden and crispy

Submitter: 
A. Apolskis
Description: 
A delicious pie my family came up with when we had left-over pumpkin from Halloween.
