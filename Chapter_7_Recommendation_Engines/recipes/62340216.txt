Title:
Pina Colada Shrimp and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. large shrimp
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 c. pineapple juice
1 20 oz. can pineapple chunks, drained
2 c. minute rice
1 c. coconut milk
1/4 c. coconut toasted
1 green onion sliced

Directions:
Stir shrimp in oil about 4 minutes until heated through.
Add pineapple juice and chunks, bring to a boil.
Stir in rice and coconut milk, cover.  Remove from heat and let stand 5 minutes.  Make sure all of liquid is absorbed and cook until that happens.  Fluff with fork and spoon on to platter.  Sprinkle with coconut and onion.
Toast coconut for 8-10 minutes on 350 in the oven prior to dish completion.
