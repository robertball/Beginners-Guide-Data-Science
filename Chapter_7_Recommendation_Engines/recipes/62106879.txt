Title:
Dani's Pasta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound penne pasta
1 lb seasoned ground turkey sausage
8 oz softened marzcapone cheese or cream cheese
28 oz jar pasta sauce
1 small sliced zucchini

Directions:
Cook entire package of pasta according to directions on package.
while pasta is cooking, cook sausage until done. Add the sliced zucchini to sausage and saute for just a minute or two so the zucchini cooks and softens a little. Drain and set aside.
Drain pasta and pour cooked pasta into a 9x13 baking dish.
add cheese to pasta and stir until cheese is melted and well mixed in the pasta.
Mix in sausage,zucchini and pasta sauce
bake in 350 degree oven until zucchini is done and everything is hot.
serve with hot garlic bread.

Submitter: 
Danielle Harbold
Description: 
This is a very quick and easy meal!  It only takes a few minutes to put it all together with items you probably already have in your kitchen.  I use turkey sausage that I get from the meat market, you could substitute regular ground sausage if you like.
