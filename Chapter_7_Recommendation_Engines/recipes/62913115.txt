Title:
Sauteed Swiss Chard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch swiss chard, thoroughly washed
2 Tbl oil
2 cloves, crushed garlic
1 shallot, sliced
pinch salt
1/4 C dry, white wine
1 Tbl butter

Directions:
Separate stems from leaves of swiss chard.
Heat oil in pan. Saute stems of swiss chard until tender. Boil a pot of salted water. Add leaves of swiss chard and boil until wilted.
Add leaves to saute pan. Add shallots and saute for an additional min or so, add garlic, saute another min. Add white wine and simmer until dissolved. Add butter and salt, serve.
