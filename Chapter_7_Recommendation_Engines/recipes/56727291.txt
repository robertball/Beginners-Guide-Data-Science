Title:
Kahlua Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ready to use Grahm cracker pie crust Choc.
12oz bag Semi-Sweet choc. chips
1/4 cup Kahlua
5 egg yolks
1-1/2 cups whipping cream
Whip cream (optional)

Directions:
Put first 3 ingredients in a blender and blend for a minute just to mix up.
Heat whipping cream in he microwave aprox 3 min. till bubbles around edges.
Put cream in to blender and blend.
Pour in to pie shell(s) and refridgerate till harden.
Slice, add whip cream, serve.
