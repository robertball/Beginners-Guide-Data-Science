Title:
SALSA TEXAS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 MED. CAN OF WHOLE TOMATOES
1 CLOVE OF GARLIC  (OPTIONAL)
3 JALAPENOS  (ADJUST TO TASTE)
1/4 INCH SLICE OF YELLOW ONION
1 BUNCH OF CILANTRO (TRIM OFF BOTTOM STALK)
SALT (TO TASTE)

Directions:
POUR TOMATO JUICE FROM THE CAN INTO BLENDER
ADD THE ONION AND PULSE BLEND FOR A FEW SECONDS (LESS FOR CHUNKY, ETC)
FOLLOW WITH THE REST OF INGREDIENTS USING THE PULSE FEATURE. i LIKE MINE A BIT ON THE CHUNKY SIDE.
SALT TO TASTE AND WATCH YOUR FRIENDS ENJOY!
