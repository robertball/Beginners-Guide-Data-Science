Title:
Limeade Vodka Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Six pack Corona (or other light beer)
1 can frozen limeade
1-2 cans vodka or rum

Directions:
Mix the frozen limeade in a bowl with 1-2 cans of liquor. Add the limeade mixture to the beer and stir. Serve cold and over ice.

Submitter: 
Emmy
Description: 
A refreshing and fizzy cocktail perfect for lounging by the pool or beach!
