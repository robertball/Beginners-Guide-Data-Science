Title:
Enchilada Spaghetti Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium spaghetti squash
vegetable oil cooking spray
salt & pepper
1 lb ground turkey
16 oz jarred or canned enchilada sauce
6 oz Queso Fresco cheese crumbled (optional)
16 oz shredded Mexican cheese

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees
split squash lengthwise using your preferred method
scoop seeds from squash and discard
spray cut face of squash liberally with oil
season liberally with salt and pepper
place squash face down on foil lined cookie sheet and place in oven for 1 hour
while squash is heating, brown ground turkey
remove squash from oven and turn face up and allow to cool until manageable to the touch
using fork, scrape strings of squash from shells and place evenly in 13 x 9 baking dish
mix 8 oz of enchilada sauce into browned turkey and layer evenly on top of squash
crumble 6 oz Queso Fresco cheese on top of sauce layer
evenly spread Mexican cheese on top of sauce layer
add final 8 oz of enchilada sauce  on top of cheese and bake 15 minutes until melted
enjoy!

Description: 
A delicious twist from the standard Italian variation.  Adjust the heat to your preference, quick and easy!
