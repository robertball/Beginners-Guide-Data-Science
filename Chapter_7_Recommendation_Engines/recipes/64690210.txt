Title:
Hula's Monster Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 1/4 cup light brown sugar or dark if that's all we have
1 cup sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp vanilla
12 ounces creamy peanut butter
1 stick soft butter
1/2 cup multi colored chocolate candies, like M&Ms and stuff
1/2 cup chocolate chips
1/4 cup raisins if you want
2 tsp baking soda
4 1/2 cups quick oats

Directions:
Set oven to 350 Watch Cookies closely as do NOT want to over bake or ur fired!
Combine 3 eggs + 1 1/4 cup light brown sugar + 1 cup sugar and mix well
Add 1/2 tsp salt + 1/2 tsp vanilla + 12 oz creamy peanut butter + 1 stick butter mix well
Stir in 1/2 cup multi-colored candies + 1/2 cup chocolate chips + 2 tsp baking soda + 4 1/2 cups quick oats and 1/4 cup raisins if you like them
Weigh out 108gm (3.8oz) cookie ball, then squish them a bit into cookie form
