Title:
Chicken In The Yellow Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/608651.jpg
Ingredients:
8 pieces cut up chicken. (Light, dark or both)
1/2 C. Flour
1/2 C. Oil for frying
1 can Campbell's French Onion soup
2 cans Campbell's Cream soup of your choice:
Cream of Chicken, Mushroom or Celery etc.

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350* F.
Coat clean chicken pieces in flour and fry in hot oil until brown on all sides.
Place in oven proof bowl that has been coated with non-stick oil spray.
Add 3 cans of soup.
Cover bowl with foil and bake for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Buffy Ehlers Whitney
Description: 
Super easy chicken entree' using only 4 ingredients baked in one dish, the Yellow Bowl (Large oven safe mixing bowl made by Pyrex).
Of course you can use any large oven proof mixing bowl but the recipe was passed to me with that name attached for that reason.
