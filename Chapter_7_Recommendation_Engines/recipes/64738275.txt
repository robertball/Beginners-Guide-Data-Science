Title:
Oded's Savory Lunchbox Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6327564.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sliced olives (or peppers, or pepperoni, or pretty much anything else that isn't too watery)
2 large eggs
4 tbsp canola oil
1 cup cottage cheese
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
3/4 cup self rising flour
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and pour into greased muffin pan (about 1/4 cup per muffin cup).

Submitter: 
Michele Avissar-Whiting
Description: 
These savory muffins are super easy to make, simple to customize, and perfect for jazzing up your kids' lunchbox.
