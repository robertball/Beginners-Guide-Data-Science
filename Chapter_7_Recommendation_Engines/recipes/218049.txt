Title:
Chopped Nutritious Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989630.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (5.3 ounce) package Dannon Oikos® Yogurt 0% Plain
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon low fat mayonnaise
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons chopped parsley
2 romaine hearts shredded
1 cup halved grape tomatoes
1 cup diced seedless cucumber
1/2 cup diced red pepper
1/2 cup minced purple onion
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine yogurt, vinegar, mayonnaise, parmesan, and parsley; mix well.
Add remaining ingredients, and toss to coat. Serve immediately with croutons if desired.

Submitter: 
Dannon Oikos®
Description: 
Colorful, crisp veggies are tossed with a light, creamy dressing in this quick, fresh salad.
