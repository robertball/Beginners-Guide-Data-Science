Title:
KFC Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 8 Cups shredded cabbage
* 1/4 Cup carrot, shredded
* 1/3 Cup sugar
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1/8 teaspoon pepper
* 1/4 Cup milk
* 1/2 Cup mayonnaise
* 1/4 Cup buttermilk

Directions:
Combine cabbage and carrots in a large salad bowl. Mix remaining dry ingredients together and mix well. Pour liquid ingredients over the dry ingredients and stir well. Pour liquid ingredients over the cabbage and carrot mixture and stir. Refrigerate at least 2 hours or overnight is better, it is necessary to make sure it is refrigerated for at least the 2 hours because it really blends the flavors and taste so much better. Stir well before serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
This cole slaw taste just like KFC's and is great as a side dish.
