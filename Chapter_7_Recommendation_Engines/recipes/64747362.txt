Title:
Big Ray's Good Morning Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6622763.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup canola oil
1/3 cup raisins
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup shredded coconut
1 cup whole wheat flour
3/4 cup regular flour
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1 8oz can crushed pineapple w/ juice
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 large eggs
2 medium carrots, shredded (about 1 cup)
1/2 peeled, shredded green/tart apple
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease regular muffin tins with oil.
Place raisins in small bowl and cover with hot water and set aside.  Roast walnuts and coconut on baking sheet for about 4 minutes, until slightly brown and fragrant.  Let cool.
Whisk together flours, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, salt and ginger in medium bowl.  Whisk eggs, oil and brown sugar in separate bowl until creamy and well combined.  Whisk in pineapple and it's juice.
Drain raisins, discarding liquid.  Stir into pineapple mixture, carrots, apple, vanilla, coconut  and walnuts.  Add flour mixture, stirring until it is incorporated to form batter.
Fill muffin tins 3/4 full and bake on middle rack for 20 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Transfer to wire cooling rack and cool 5 minutes, then remove muffins placing them directly on rack and allow to cool completely.

Description: 
Make your morning glorious with these easy muffins.  They deliver that full good morning flavor with their complex mixture of taste and texture.  They also freeze well, so you can bake them once and enjoy the payoff for months to come.
