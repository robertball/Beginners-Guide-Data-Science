Title:
Creamy Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/555498.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRANDÂ® Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon salt
3 1/2 cups hot water
3 cups milk
Mini marshmallows (optional)

Directions:
In large saucepan over medium heat, combine sweetened condensed milk, cocoa, vanilla and salt; mix well. Slowly stir in water and milk.  Heat through, stirring occasionally.  Do not boil.  Top with marshmallows, (optional).  Store covered in refrigerator.
