Title:
Island Spice Cold Brew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4528647.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces GevaliaÂ® Cold Brew Concentrate - Vanilla, divided
2 ounces sweetened condensed milk
2 ounces half-and-half
1/8 teaspoon ground cardamom
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
ice cubes

Directions:
Pour sweetened condensed milk and half-and-half into small measuring cup or pitcher. Add cardamom and cinnamon. Whisk until smooth and blended.
Place ice cubes in mugs. Pour 4 ounces cold brew concentrate into each mug. Pour half of spice mixture into each glass slowly over the back of a spoon.

Submitter: 
Doug Matthews
Description: 
You can taste the spices in the cool breezes of the South Seas in every sip.
