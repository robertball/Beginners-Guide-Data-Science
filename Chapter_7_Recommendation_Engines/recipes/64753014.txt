Title:
Delicious Vanilla Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6832354.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ¼ cups of All Purpose Flour
2 ¼ tsp of baking powder
¾ tsp salt
¾ cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
1 ½ cups of granulated sugar
3 large eggs (room temperature)
1 ½ tsp of vanilla
1 cup of milk (butter or whole)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Grease and flour your cake pans.
In a medium bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt until well combined. Set aside.
In a new bowl, cream butter and sugar on med-high until pale and fluffy (approx 3mins). Reduce speed and add eggs one at a time fully incorporating after each addition. Add vanilla.
Alternate adding flour mixture and buttermilk, beginning and ending with flour (3 additions of flour and 2 of milk). Fully incorporating after each addition.
Bake for 30-35 mins or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Place cakes on wire rack to cool for 10 mins then turn out onto wire rack

Submitter: 
Laurel
Description: 
This is an easy to use and simple recipe for a traditional, mouth-watering, vanilla cake. It's great for any occasion where deliciousness is required, and is out of the oven in less than an hour..
