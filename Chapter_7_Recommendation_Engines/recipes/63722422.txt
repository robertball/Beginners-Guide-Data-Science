Title:
Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1039539.jpg
Ingredients:
1 avocado, chopped
1 tsp lime juice
1/2 fresh jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
1/4 cup diced tomatoes
1 Tbl minced onion
1/2 tsp minced garlic
1/8 tsp salt, to taste

Directions:
Mix together in a small bowl and chill until serving.
