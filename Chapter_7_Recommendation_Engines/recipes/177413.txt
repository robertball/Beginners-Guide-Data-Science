Title:
Baileys Espresso Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985612.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces Baileys Irish Cream
1/4 ounce Smirnoff No. 21 vodka
1 shot cold espresso

Directions:
Add Baileys Irish Cream, Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka, and shot of cold espresso.
Shake and pour into martini glass.
Garnish with cherry.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Baileys Irish Cream, Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka, and cold espresso are blended and served in a martini glass, topped with a cherry.
