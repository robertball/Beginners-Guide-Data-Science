Title:
Big Ray's Pierogi Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3105029.jpg
Ingredients:
9 lasagna noodles, uncooked
4 cups hot mashed potatoes
milk, as needed
8 oz cottage cheese
1 can (14.5 oz) sour kraut, rinsed and squeezed dried
6 green onions, thinly sliced
8 sliced bacon
2 cups  Cheddar Cheese , divided

Directions:
Heat oven to 375Â°F.
Cook bacon until crisp.  Crumble and set aside.
Cook noodles as directed on package, omitting salt. Meanwhile, combine potatoes, cottage cheese, onions, 1/2 of the bacon, sour kraut and 1 cup cheddar, if mixture seems dry add a little milk
Place 3 noodles in 13" x 9" baking dish sprayed with cooking spray; cover with 1/3 of the potato mixture. Repeat layers twice. Top with remaining bacon and cheddar; cover.
Bake 33 to 35 min. or until heated through, uncovering for addition 10 min. Let stand 10 min. before cutting to serve.
Can be  prepared and assembled  the night before and kept in fridge.  Take out 20 minutes before baking and allow to reach room temperature.
