Title:
Crispy Mashed Potato Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cold mashed potatoes (prepared with milk and butter)
2 egg, lightly beaten
1/4 cup finely minced green pepper
1/4 cup finely minced onion
2 Tbs flour
2 Tbs Parmesan cheese, grated
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon olive or vegetable oil
(Additional)
2 Tbs All-purpose flour
2 Tbs Parmesan cheese
* Ormore is required

Directions:
Combine well all the ingredients except oil; mix well. In a small skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Make a patty, then roll in the flour/Parmesan cheese mixture; press with a spatula to flatten evenly. Cover and cook for 8 minutes or until bottom is crispy, flip an cook other side. Invert onto a serving plate.
