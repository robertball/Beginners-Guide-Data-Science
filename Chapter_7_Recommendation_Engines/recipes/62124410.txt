Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds boneless beef round steak (1/2 inch thick), cut into serving-size pieces
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
2 cans French Onion soup
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 onion sliced
1 can sliced mushrooms
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Trim round steak and cut into serving-size pieces and place in the slow cooker.
Mix 2 cans French onion soup, 1 can Cream of mushroom soup, and Worcestershire sauce in a bowl, then pour over the round steak.
Add in sliced onion, can of mushrooms, and salt and pepper to taste.
Cook on high for at least 4 hours.
Serve over rice, noodles, or mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
HollyG
Description: 
For those that do not like tomatoes, this is a quick and easy recipe for swiss steak in the slow cooker.  Feel free to add more spices, but my family is picky so I don't add too much, and they love it.
