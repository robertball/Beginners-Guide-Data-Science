Title:
Ranch Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger
2 TBSP of Hidden Valley dressing mix, dry

Directions:
Mix hamburger and mix together.  Divide into 4 patties. Preheat counter top grill to high. Cook for 2 minutes.
Serve with your choice of toppings: lettuce, tomatoes, onion, mayonnaise, mustard and ketchup.
