Title:
Cream Cheese Mold with Blackberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (0.25 ounce each) envelopes unflavored gelatin powder
1 cup water
1 (8 ounce)packages cream cheese
1 cup half and half cream
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 1/2 cups water
2 cups frozen black berries
2 Tbs white sugar

Directions:
Sprincke the unflavored gelatin powder in 1 cup of water. Let stand for 5 minutes. In a small sauce pan, heat the softened gelatin stirring constantly until it disolves completly.
In a blender, mix the cream cheese, half and half, condensed milk and 1 1/2 cups of water perfectly.
Add the disolved gelatin and stir well.
Pour the mixture in a gelatin ring mold and refrigerate until set.
Meanwhile, defrost the black berries and blend them with the sugar. Pass the mixture thru a colander.
Serve the gelatin with the sauce.

Submitter: 
Maria Padin
Description: 
This is a delicious easy creamy dessert. You can change the blackberries for any fruit you like.
