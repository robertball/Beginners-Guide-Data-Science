Title:
Lentils and Bacon Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/301364.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup lentils
3 cups chicken broth
4 cup cold water
1/2 pound of bacon cut into thin strips
1/2 cup small diced red onion
3 cloves of garlic minced
2 cups fresh spinach stems removed
1/4 cup small diced carrot
1/4 cup small diced celery
1 tablespoon dried italian herbs
1 bay leaf
2 Tablespoons white vinegar
2 tablespoons tomato paste
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large stock pot on medium heat, render down the bacon until half cooked, add onions, carrots, celery and tomato paste cook until translucent. Deglaze the pot with the chicken broth. Add the water dried lentils, bay leaf and garlic. Simmer for about 45 minutes, or until lentils are soft. Turn heat down to medium low. Add vinegar, herbs, spinach, salt and pepper to taste. Cook for another 10 minutes then serve and enjoy.

Description: 
This a very flavorful soup for lentils. The layers of flavor really bring out the best in this hearty soup. Great for a rainy day!
