Title:
Taco Pizza from Pillsbury® Artisan Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984345.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (11 ounce) can Pillsbury® refrigerated thin pizza crust
1 pound (at least 80%) lean ground beef
1 (1 ounce) package Old El Paso® Taco Seasoning Mix
1/4 cup water
1 (4 ounce) can Old El Paso® chopped green chiles, drained
1/4 cup sliced ripe olives, drained
2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
1 1/2 cups shredded lettuce
1 cup seeded, chopped tomato
Crushed corn chips, if desired
Old El Paso® Salsa, if desired

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F. Spray or grease 15x10-inch or larger dark or nonstick cookie sheet. Unroll dough on cookie sheet; starting at center, press dough into 15x10-inch rectangle.
In 10-inch skillet, cook beef, taco seasoning mix and water as directed on package. Spoon evenly on dough. Top with chives, olives and cheese.
Bake 13 to 16 minutes or until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted. Top with remaining ingredients.

Submitter: 
Pillsbury Artisan Pizza Crust
Description: 
If your family enjoys tacos, then why not top a pizza with the same delicious combination? Easy and so delicious!
