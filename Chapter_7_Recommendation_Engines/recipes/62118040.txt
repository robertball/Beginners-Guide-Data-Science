Title:
Quick Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup flour
salt and pepper to taste
1 tsp. Italian seasoning
2 tablespoon butter, separated
1 lb. skinless, boneless chicken tenders
1 onion, sliced
1 8 oz. package fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed golden mushroom soup
1 1/4 cups Marsala wine

Directions:
Pound chicken tenders until thin.  In a bowl, flour, salt and pepper, and Italian seasoning.  Dredge chicken through flour mixture to coat.
In a large skillet, sauté the chicken in 1 TSP butter. Once chicken is lightly browned on all sides, remove from pan and cover.
In same pan, add remaining butter and sliced onion and sauté till translucent.  Add mushrooms and brown slightly.  Add chicken back to pan.
In a separate bowl, whisk together soup and wine.  Add mixture to the pan. Cover and simmer until chicken is no longer pink inside, about 20 minutes.
Serve over angel hair pasta and French bread.

Description: 
Canned Golden Mushroom soup is the trick to this quick and tasty spin on Chicken Marsala.
