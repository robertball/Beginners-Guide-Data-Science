Title:
Wallaby-Darned Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/233877.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger peach schnapps
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 cup champagne
1/2 (16 ounce) package frozen sliced peaches
1/2 cup fuzzy navel mix
1 cup ice

Directions:
Place the vodka, schnapps, sugar, champagne, peaches, fuzzy navel mix, and ice into a blender. Blend until smooth and serve.

Submitter: 
PRELUDELJS
Description: 
A close replica of my favorite steakhouse drink. Peachy and frozen, made from peach schnapps, vodka, champagne, and fuzzy navel mixer.
