Title:
Skillet Caramel Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c sugar
1/4 tsp salt
2 c milk
1 tsp vanilla
5 Tbsp flour
5 eggs, seperated
3 Tbsp butter
9" baked pie shell

Directions:
Melt 1 c sugar over low. Mix 1 c sugar, flour & salt. Mix egg yolks & milk & add to the flour beating lightly. Pour into melted sugar. Cook until smooth & thick. Add butter & vanilla. Pour into pie shell & top w/ meringue. Bake @ 350 for 5-10 min to brown.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is my mother in law's caramel pie recipe that we have every holiday.
