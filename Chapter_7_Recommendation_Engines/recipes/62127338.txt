Title:
Spicy Salmon Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Salmon fillets
2 cups cooked brown rice
10 cups of chicken boullion flavored water or Broth
1 bag broccoli
1 bag mixed veggies
2 cups frozen country style cut hashbrown potatoes or fresh cut
3 cloves garlic diced
1 medium yellow or white onion chopped
1 Tbsp onion powder
1 Tbsp garlic powder
1 tsp season salt
1 Tbsp paprika
1 Tbsp pepper
2 Tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions.
Boil water and add chicken boullion cubes about 8 or enough to taste like a broth.
Add onions.
Sprinkle salmon with a little of the spices.
Heat a frying pan over medium high heat with olive oil.
Add spiced salmon and diced garlic to hot frying pan.
Cook salmon on both sides.
Break salmon apart into small chunks when it is done.
Add salmon and garlic to boiling broth.
Add rice, broccoli and mixed vegetables.
Add remaining spices and stir.
Cook another 15 min. or until vegetables are slightly tender.
Then Add potatoes.
Simmer mixture for another 15 min. stirring occasionally adding more spices as needed.

Submitter: 
deven
Description: 
A spicy hearty stew full of salmon, rice and vegetables.  We make this meal at least three times a month.  It has been a family favorite since we left Toronto.
