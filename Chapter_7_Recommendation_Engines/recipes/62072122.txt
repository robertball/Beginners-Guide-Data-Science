Title:
Raspberry Mint Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Oz Cream Cheese, softnened
2 C. Marshmallow Creme
2 - 6oz Containers Lemon flavored yogurt
4 tsp chopped fresh mint leaves
6 Oz Fresh Raspberries

Directions:
With and electric mixer, beat cream cheese until fluffy.  Add marshmallow cream and yogurt.  Beat until smooth.
Add Mint and raspberries and beat on low, to mix.  Transfer to bowl and chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
Serve with assorted cut up fruit

Submitter: 
SAVVYHOSTESS
Description: 
Easy and light, this dip is great for showers and potlucks.  Holds well for a day or two.
