Title:
Grandma's Doughnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 TBLS oil
3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup white vinegar
1 heaping teaspoon baking soda
2 cups flour
1 tsp nutmeg
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Heat about 1 quart of oil to 375 degrees
put sugar, eggs and oil in mixing bowl
mix
in a liquid measuring cup put 3/4 cup milk, 1/4 cup vinegar and heaping teaspoon of baking soda--milk will become foamy
add milk mixture to bowl
mix
add remaining ingredients and mix
drop by teaspoonful in grease for about 1 minute each side
drain on paper towels

Submitter: 
Angie D.
Description: 
Best cake doughnuts!!  Easy to make.  Just mix and drop in hot grease!!
