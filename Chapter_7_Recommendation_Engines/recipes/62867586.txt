Title:
Scrambled Eggs with Cheddar Cheese and Onion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large eggs
A little less than 1/4 cup milk
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
1 tbsp salted or unsalted butter
1/2 medium onion, diced
1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk and season with salt and pepper.
Melt the butter in non-stick skillet over medium heat.
Add the onion and cook, stirring, until golden, about 3-5 minutes.
Add egg mixture and cook, stirring, until loosely set, about 3 minutes.
Sprinkle cheese over the eggs and stir to combine well.
Continue cooking, stirring for 2 to 4 minutes depending on the firmness you like your eggs.
Serve immediately...with some toast!
