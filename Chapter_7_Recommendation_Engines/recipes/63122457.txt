Title:
Beef and Vegetable Soup with Barley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/795194.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c vegetable oil
2 lbs beef stew meat, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
2 c finely chopped onion (in processor)
2 garlic gloves, minced (in processor)
28 oz canned diced tomatoes
2 1/2 lbs boiling potatoes, cut into 1/3-inch cubes
10 oz celery, chopped in processor
13 oz carrots, chopped in processor
2 c (14.4 oz) pearl barley
2 T salt
4 oz kale
4 bay leaves
2 t dried basil
12 c beef broth
4 c water
16 oz frozen corn
20 oz frozen peas

Directions:
Heat oil in large stockpot over medium-high heat. Add beef and cook until brown, stirring occasionally, about 8 minutes.
Add onion and garlic and saut√© until onion is tender, about 5 minutes.
Stir in tomatoes with juices and all remaining ingredients except
frozen vegetables.
Cover and simmer until meat is tender, about 90 minutes.
Stir in frozen vegetables and bring back to a simmer.  Add salt and pepper to taste, and water if soup is too thick.

Sarah Masi
Description: 
This recipe feeds a lot of hungry folks. You might want to cut it in half.
