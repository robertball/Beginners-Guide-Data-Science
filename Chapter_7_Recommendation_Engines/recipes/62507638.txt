Title:
Poor Man's Chicken Fricassee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (26 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
1 (26 ounce) can of milk
2 1/4 cups Bisquick or similar pre-mixed baking product
1 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix soup and one can of milk together in a 9 x 13 baking dish.
Cook soup mixture for 15 minutes to heat it up.
While the soup mixture is heating up mix the Bisquick and 1 cup milk to make dumplings.
Pull soup mixture out of oven and drop dumplings all over the soup mixture.
Cover with tin foil and bake for 15 minutes.
Uncover and finish baking 10-20 minutes until a toothpick poked in the dumplings comes out clean.

Description: 
This is a good recipe when you are short on groceries or have picky eaters. My husband and kids eat this at least 2-3 times a month.
