Title:
Dadar Gulung ( Indonesian pandan pancake) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/805090.jpg
Ingredients:
150 gr all purpose flour (plain flour)
1 egg
250 ml coconut milk (i add 50 ml more)
1 teaspoon pandan paste
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
Filling : 2 Cups of coconut,1 cup of brown sugar, pandan leave- cook with a little bit of water.

Directions:
Mix flour, egg, coconut milk, salt, oil,pandan paste together, whisk until smooth.
Meanwhile mix the filling ingredients together in a small pot, and simmer for 15 minutes, or until dry (or water absorbed)
Heat a small pan and add a little oil (greased), make a pancake until the batter finish.
Place the ready pancake with the coconut filling, fold in the ends and roll up.
Serve.
