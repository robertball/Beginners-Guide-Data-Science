Title:
Kale Chips in the Microwave Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2024472.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch flat-leaf kale - ribs trimmed away and discarded and leaves cut into 2-inch pieces
1 tablespoon olive oil, or to taste
sea salt to taste

Directions:
Arrange kale pieces on microwave-safe plates so they do not overlap. Drizzle olive oil over the kale pieces; season with sea salt.
Cook each plateful of kale in microwave oven until crispy, 2 to 2 1/2 minutes per batch.
