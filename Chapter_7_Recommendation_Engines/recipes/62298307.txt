Title:
Southfork Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Southfork Chili
2# lean ground beef, or use soy "meat" or tempeh, and extra beans
2 c. chopped onion
1 chopped green pepper
3 cloves chopped garlic
1 to 2 T. crushed hot red peppers
3 T. chili powder
2 tsp. cumin
½ tsp. dried oregano
2 tsp. salt
2 cans (1#) red kidney beans, undrained
2 cans (1#) whole tomatoes, undrained
12 oz. frozen corn
1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
tortillas

Directions:
Sauté ground beef; pour off fat.  Add onion through salt.  Cook, stirring until onion and gr. pepper are tender.  Ad rest and stir.  Cover and simmer 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Serve on tortillas.
