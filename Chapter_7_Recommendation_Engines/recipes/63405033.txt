Title:
Wendy's Chili Knockoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
One 29 oz can tomato sauce
One 29 oz can kidney beans with liquid
One 29 oz can pinto beans with liquid
1 cup diced onion
1/2 c diced green chili (2 chilies)
1/4 c diced celery (1 stalk)
3 medium tomatoes, chopped
2 t cumin powder
3 T chili powder
1 1/2 t black pepper
2 t salt
2 c water

Directions:
Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat; drain off fat.
Using fork, crumble the ground beef into pea sized pieces.
In a large pot, combine the beef plus all the remaining ingredients, and bring to a simmer over low heat.  Cook, stirring occasionally for 2-3 hours.  Or add beef and remaining ingredients to slow cooker and simmer on low 8-10 hours.
