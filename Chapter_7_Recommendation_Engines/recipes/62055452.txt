Title:
Almond Roca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
2 sticks butter
1/4 cup water
1 TBS white Karo syrup
1/2 lb blanched almonds, chopped fine
8 oz chocolate chips, melted

Directions:
Put first four ingrediants in a saucepan and cook to hard crack stage (300)over medium to medium-low heat, stirring constantly.  Pour into buttered pie tin or 10 inch cake pan (round or square).  Cool, turn out and break into pieces.  Coat with melted chocolate chips and roll pieces in chopped almonds.
