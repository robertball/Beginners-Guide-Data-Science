Title:
Eggnog Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 (10oz) pkg white chocolate morsels
1 1/4 c. flour
3/4 c. sugar
1 t vanilla
1/4 tea. salt
1/4 tea. nutmeg
3 eggs
1 1/2 c. powdered sugar
3 table. butter, softened
1/2 tea. vanilla
1-3 table. eggnog (or heavy whipping cream)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease and lightly flour bottom of 9x13.  Heat butter and chips in a heavy saucepan over low heat until just melted and only slightly lumpy- stir frequently.  Stir in four, sugar, vanilla, salt, nutmeg and eggs.  Spread into pan and bake 30-35 min.  For frosting, combine powdered sugar, butter, vanilla and eggnog til a speadable consistency.

Submitter: 
JUNEBUFF1
Description: 
This is a sweet white chocolate blonde-brownie with an eggnog frosting.  Sprinkle the top with nutmeg and it will taste like the holidays!!
