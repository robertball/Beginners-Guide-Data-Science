Title:
Billionaires Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979865.jpg
Ingredients:
5 ounces crushed ice
1 ounce Jose Cuervo Reserva De La Familia® Tequila
1/2 ounce Grand Marnier Cuvee du Centenaire liqueur
2 ounces freshly squeezed lime juice
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Fill a shaker with ice.
Add Jose Cuervo Reserva De La Familia(R) Tequila, Grand Marnier Cuvee du Centenaire liqueur, and lime juice.
Shake well.
Pour into a glass with ice.
Garnish with lime wedge.

Submitter: 
Cuervo®
Description: 
Drink carefully; this Margarita made with Jose Cuervo Reserva De La Familia® tequila is too precious to spill.
