Title:
Creamy Cucumber Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) bottle zesty Italian-style salad dressing
1 (5 ounce) package melba toast rounds
2 small cucumbers, sliced
1 sprig fresh dill, or to taste

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and dressing together in a bowl. Spread mixture over melba toasts. Top with cucumber slices and sprinkle with dill.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
Refreshing and delicious appetizer. I've had friends make this with bottled zesty Italian dressing or the dry packaged dressing. People love it either way.
