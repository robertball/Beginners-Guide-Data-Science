Title:
Goat Cheese & Olive Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/315459.jpg
Ingredients:
1 300g pkg of goat cheese - sliced
3 tablespoons of fresh chopped basil
1/2 teaspoon of dried rosemary leaves
1/2 teaspoon of chopped freshparsley
1/2 teaspoon of ground thyme
jar of sliced sundried tomatoes
jar of pitted sliced kalamata olives
4 cloves of garlic
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
sliced baguette for spreading/dipping

Directions:
Layer all above 7 ingredients in order in a small serving dish.  Place a garlic clove in each corner, then drizzle olive oil on top.  Cover and refridgerate overnight.  Take out 2 hours before serving and make sure to toss the garlic cloves before serving.

Submitter: 
Lucy Mirabella Nino
Description: 
This will not last at a party!  Yummy goat cheese, sundried tomatoes & kalamata olive combination will make anyone wanting more.  Savoury and delish! Enjoy!
