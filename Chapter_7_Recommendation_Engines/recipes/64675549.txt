Title:
Ice Box Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soft shortening
1 cup brown sugar, packed
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 3/4 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup chopped nuts (fine)

Directions:
Mix shortening, sugar, egg and vanilla thoroughly
Blend flour, soda, salt and nuts into shortening mixture
Form into a roll (2") and wrap in waxed paper
Chill until firm
Slice and place on ungreased cookie sheet
Bake  at 400 degrees for 8-10 minutes
