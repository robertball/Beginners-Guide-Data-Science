Title:
Karyn's Fried Cheddar Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 potatoes sliced
4 slices cooked bacon
1 onion diced
2 tbsp butter
1 tbsp parsley flakes
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
add butter to bacon grease from bacon that was cooked, add diced onions. Add potato slices..sprinkle with parsley flakes. Add cooked bacon. Cook until tender. add salt and pepper and sprinkle shredded cheddar  over all potatoes, stir into potatoes to melt.Enjoy!

Submitter: 
KARYN821
Description: 
Delicious bacon flavored fried potatoes made more delicious with cheddar cheese! Delicious with bacon and eggs or any breakfast!
