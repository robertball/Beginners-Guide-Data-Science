Title:
Blackberry/Red Raspberry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1 cup seasoned apple cider vinegar
1 (10 ounce) jar seedless blackberry or raspberry jam

Directions:
Combine the olive oil,  vinegar and  jam in the container of a blender. Process until smooth. Store in a jar in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
A very easy, flavor-filled dressing that goes with any kind of salad. Only three ingredients! I like to use it with romaine, feta, toasted pecans, bacon, and red onion.Modified from: Red Raspberry Vinaigrette
