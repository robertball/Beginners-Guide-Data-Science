Title:
cold asparagus w/curry dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup mayonaise
1/4 cup sour cream
1-2 tblsp curry powder

Directions:
Mix well and serve with cold asparagus. Adjust the type and amount of curry powder for stronger or weaker curry flavor and heat

Submitter: 
Jani  Whitsett
Description: 
A delicious dip for cold or hot asparagus or artichokes. It is also excellent on potatoes and other veggies
