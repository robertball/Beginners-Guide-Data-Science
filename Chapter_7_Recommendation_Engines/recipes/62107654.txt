Title:
Bread Pudding, Original and simple! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf French bread, stale
4C. scalded whole milk
1 ¼ C. sugar
5 eggs
2 tsp. Vanilla
½ tsp. Cinnamon
½ tsp. Nutmeg, fresh ground if you can
1C. rough chop pecans
1C. raisins are optional
1/2 stick of butter sliced

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 350 degrees
Scald the milk and cube the stale french bread.
In a large mixing bowl, pour the hot milk over the bread, get it all soaked and set aside.
In seperate bowl, mix the eggs, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon and nutmeg together well
Pour this mixture over the bread mixture and mix well trying not to break up the bread to much
Mix in the pecan and raisins
Slice the cold butter and fold in.
Put the pudding into a buttered 9" x 13" baking pan
Bake for an hour to an hour and a 1/2 at 350.
Serve with vanilla ice cream!
