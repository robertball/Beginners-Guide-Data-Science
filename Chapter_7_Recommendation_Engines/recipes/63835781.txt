Title:
Turkey Brine for 12-22 pound turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Gallons Cold Water
2 Cups Kosher or Sea Salt
1 Cup Granulated Sugar
1 T crushed garlic or 1 t dry garlic
Fresh Rosemary and Thyme, (3-4 twigs of each)
1 t cracked Allspice Berries, (or 1/2 t of dry spice)
1 t black pepper

Directions:
Take 2 quarts of water and heat to a boil, add sugar and salt to dissolve.
Add remaining spices and balance of water.
Submerge turkey and weight down.
Brine for 12-36 hours.
Roast as desired.

Submitter: 
Ed Price
Description: 
I love a brined bird.  When you think about cooking any poultry for more than 2 hours you definitely want a bird that was packed in a saline solution or brined yourself.
