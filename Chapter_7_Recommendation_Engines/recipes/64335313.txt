Title:
Crispy Chocolate Covered Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup natural peanut butter
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 tablespoons ground flax seed
1 tablespoon honey
1/3 cup crispy rice cereal
1/3 cup dark chocolate

Directions:
Combine peanut butter, flax seed, ground cinnamon,  and honey until thoroughly combined.
Chill thoroughly, then shape the mixture into 1 inch balls.
Roll the balls in crispy cereal, and freeze for 15-30 minutes.
Melt the dark chocolate in the microwave at 30 second intervals, then roll the balls in the chocolate.  Place on a cookie sheet lined with wax paper, then freeze or chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
An attempt at a more diabetic-friendly peanut butter ball for my mom's sweet tooth.
