Title:
Smokey Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Salmon
2 Tbs Maple Syrup
1 Tbs Butter
1 tsp Cajun Creole Seasoning
Sprinkle of  salt & pepper

Directions:
Heat grill to Med-High, place fish on foil sprayed w/ non stick spray, Mix maple syrup and spices in a small bowl. Brush fish with glaze and seal foil (Or use a smoker bag)
Heat for 15-20 minutes, or until flaky.

Submitter: 
wendi
Description: 
Salmon grilled with a light seasoning of spices, with the sweetness of maple syrup!
