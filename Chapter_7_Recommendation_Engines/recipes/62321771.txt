Title:
New England Hearty Kale Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/379197.jpg
Ingredients:
1pkg of stew meat
1pkg of Chorizo or Kielbasa
1pkg of Linguica(Portuguese Sausage)
1 can of kidney beans 8oz
5lb of potatoes
1 pkg of frozen Kale
1 pack of carrots
2 onions
1 bag of Portuguese rolls

Directions:
Lightly coat the stew meat w/flour and fry in butter till browned remove and dry off grease
Next fry all the Linguica and kielbasa dry off grease
Chop onions in 1/2 add the 4 1/2's and cut carrots.
Place all the meat, onions, and the carrots in the pan and add water (or chicken stock) to just above the meat.
Let it simmer for 4-5 hours adding water to keep at same level throughout the time.
After simmering all that time add water to fill pan almost to the top.
Keep cooking till stew meat easily breaks apart then add 6-7 potatoes peeled and chopped(whatever size you like) also add kidney beans and frzn Kale into the soup and cook until potatoes are soft.
Next serve with Portuguese rolls or any choice of rolls
