Title:
Italian Shredded Beef Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound chuck roast, cubed
2 cups beef broth
1 cup water
1 T italian seasoning
1 T oregano
1/3 cup onion, diced
2 clove garlic, crushed
1 t salt
1 t pepper
French rolls

Directions:
1. Cube the beef into two-inch cubes
2. Add all of the ingredients to the pot and simmer covered for three to four hours until the beef falls apart.
3. Stir from time to time and add more water if necessary.
4. Uncover the pot and watch carefully for the last 15 minutes to evaporate moisture.
5. Keep the beef warm and serve with horseradish sauce and small, bite-sized rolls as a hors douvres; or mound the beef onto a hard roll and have it for lunch, also with horseradish sauce

Slow cooked, shredded chuck roast simmered in italian spices and served on french rolls.
