Title:
Wendy's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground beef
29 oz. can of tomato sauce
29 oz. can of kidney beans - undrained
29 oz. can of pinto beans - undrained
1 cup diced onion
1/2 cup diced green chiles
1/4 cup diced celery
3 medium tomatoes - chopped
2 tsp. cumin
3 tbsp. chili powder
1 1/2 tsp. pepper
2 tsp. salt
2 cups water

Directions:
Brown beef in large pot and drain off fat.
Combine remaining ingredients and cook about 2-3 hours over low heat, stirring occasionally.
