Title:
Seitan Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb seitan strips
1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
1 green bell pepper, cut into strips
1 yellow bell pepper, cut into strips
1/2 red onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, mined
1 Tsp chili powder
1 Tsp paprika
1 Tsp cumin
2 Tbsp low sodium soy sauce
3 Tbsp olive oil
1 pkg (10) whole grain flour tortillas

Directions:
Saute onions & garlic in oil until tender - 3 to 5 mins.
Add remaining ingredients and cook until seitan is cooked through, about 7 - 10 mins.
Wrap filling in tortillas and serve with taco sauce  and Tofutti Better than Sour Cream and Guacamole.
