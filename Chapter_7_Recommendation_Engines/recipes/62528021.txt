Title:
Moroccan Tagine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1lb chicken or turkey sausage
1/2 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 small butternut squash, peeled and chopped
1 (15.5 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
2 or 3 carrot, peeled and chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chilis
1 (14 ounce) chicken broth
1 tablespoon sugar
large bunch spinach, kale or chard
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground coriander
1 dash cayenne pepper

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat, and cook the sausage, onion, and garlic about 15 minutes, until browned.
Mix the squash, garbanzo beans, carrot, tomatoes with juice,greens, broth, and sugar into the skillet. Season with salt, coriander, and cayenne pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, and continue cooking 30 minutes, until vegetables are tender.
