Title:
Bacon Wrapped Water Chestnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bacon
2 cans whole water chestnuts
1 c ketchup
1 c sugar

Directions:
In a bowl, mix ketchup and sugar.
Cut bacon strips in half.
Wrap bacon around water chestnuts, using a toothpick to secure.
Dip each into ketchup mixture, and place on large baking sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes, until bacon is done.
Can be made the day before and warmed in a crock pot.
