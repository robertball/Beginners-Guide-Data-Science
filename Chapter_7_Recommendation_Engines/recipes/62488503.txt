Title:
Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6905995.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. shortening
2 eggs
2 heaping T. cocoa
1/2 c. boiling water
2 c. flour
1/4 t. salt
2 T. vinegar
1 c. milk
2 t. soda
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Cream together white sugar, brown sugar & shortening.
Add eggs & cream until fluffy.
Dissolve cocoa into boiling water and add to above.
Add flour, salt and soda to above.
Mix milk and soda together then add vinegar.
Add to rest of mixture, mixing well.
Add vanilla.
Pour into greased and floured round cake pans.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
Frost with Grandma's Caramel Icing!
