Title:
Chicken Bombay Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. marg.
1/2 c. liquid honey
1/4 tsp, chili
14 tsp. curry
3 lbs. chicken
1/4 c. mustard
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. onion powder
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Mix first 9 ingred. in saucepan, heat and liquify. Roll chicken in sauce and bake uncovered 375 F for one hour. spoon on rest of sauce half way through.
