Title:
Barbarella's German Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Eggs
2 Level Tablespoons Sugar
2 Heaping Tablespoons Flour
3/4 Cup Milk

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a blender and blend on low speed.
Warm skillet or griddle on medium heat.  Pour batter to desired size.  Heat until golden brown. Turn and brown on other side. Serve with fresh berries and whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Deborah
Description: 
This great pancake similar to a blintz is simple, easy and delicious to make.
