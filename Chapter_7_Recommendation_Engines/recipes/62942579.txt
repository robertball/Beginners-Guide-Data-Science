Title:
Reuben Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil OR olive oil flavored spray
1 cup Pumpernickle Rye bread crumbs*
1 cup cooked ground corned beef
1 cup Bavarian sauerkraut, drained, squeezed and chopped
1 cup Swiss cheese, finely shredded
1/2 cup finely minced onion
2 T. cider vinegar
2 T. mayo
1 T. crushed garlic
1 T. Dijon mustard
1 T. sugar
1 egg, beaten
Sauces for dipping:
Russian dressing, honey mustard, horseradish sauce, etc.

Directions:
Brush surface of large baking tray with o.oil or spray
Combine remaining ingredients and mix well by hand.
Form into 1 inch balls and place about 2 inches apart on baking tray.
Bake in preheated 400° oven for 15-20 minutes.
Cool slightly, carefully remove from tray with spatula and place in crockpot.
Refrigerate until ready to heat for party.
* use four slices of Pepperidge Farm Deli-Swirl Rye and Pumpernickel bread, dried in a 200°oven for about 1 hour.  Process in blender to make fine crumbs.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Combine the best of all the ingredients in a Reuben for a delicious, easy to eat appetizer.
