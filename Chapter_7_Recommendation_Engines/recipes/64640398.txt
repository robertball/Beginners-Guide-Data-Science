Title:
Salsa Fiesta Cod Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4512711.jpg
Ingredients:
2 individually frozen cod fillets, 4 oz each
14.5 oz can Fire Roasted Salsa Style Diced Tomatoes
1 cup tightly packed leafy greens of your choice, chopped
3 fresh green onion, chopped
Shredded Fiesta Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Open can of diced tomatoes and pour into baking dish. Stir in the chopped leafy greens, mix through the salsa. Chop only the greens of the onion and add to the salsa mixture. Place thawed cod fillets in the baking dish, atop the salsa. Chop the remaining onion  and disperse over the cod fillets. Sprinkle shredded fiesta cheese over the fillets. Bake in oven for  20-30 minutes or until fish is cooked through. Serve as is or over cooked brown rice. Enjoy!

Description: 
Spicy, quick and easy! Cod fillets  cooked on a bed of greens and salsa, topped with fiesta cheese.
