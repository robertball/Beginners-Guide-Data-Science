Title:
Amy's Delicious Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1/2 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
1 egg
1/4 cup regular breadcrumbs

Directions:
Preheat a grill for high heat.
In a large bowl, mix together the ground turkey, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, minced onion, egg and crackers using your hands. Form into four fat patties.
Place patties on the grill, and cook for about 5 minutes per side, until well done.

Submitter: 
Virginia Mott
Description: 
So delicious and low in fat! Melt on some pepperjack cheese, slice on some roasted jalepenos, and a dollop of chipotle mayo. Serve with sweet potato fries.Modified from: Amy's Delicious Turkey Burgers
