Title:
Freezer Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
2 (8 oz.) packages cream cheese, softened
16 oz. cheddar or jack cheese, shredded
2 (4 oz.) cans Ortega diced chilis
6 (10 oz.) cans green enchilada sauce
32 fajita sized flour tortillas

Directions:
mix the cream cheese with the green chilis and 1 can of enchilada sauce. pour cream sauce over shredded chicken in a bowl and stir to combine.
in the bottom of 4 foil baking pans pour 1 can of green enchilada sauce and spread evenly to cover bottom of pan. take your tortillas and fill generously with the creamy chicken mixture and roll up. place rolled enchiladas in pan seam side down and cover each pan of enchiladas with sauce. sprinkle top of enchiladas with cheese. cover each pan with a double layer of aluminum foil.to bake – preheat oven to 350° and bake unfrozen for 45 minutes or 70-90 minutes if frozen. frozen enchiladas will hold in freezer for 4-6 month.
