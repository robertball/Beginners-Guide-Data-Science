Title:
Roasted Sweet Potatoes With Balsamic Drizzle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 # sweet potatoes, about 3 med., peeled and cut into 1/2-in. thick wedges
1 T EVOO
1/4 T salt, or to taste
black pepper to taste
1 C balsamic vinegar
2 T honey
1 t butter

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees.  Line rimmed sheet pan with foil
Spread potatoes on pan and sprinkle with EVOO, toss well
In single layer, roast potatoes until tender, 25-30 min.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper
Meanwhile combine balsamic vinegar with honey in saucepan
Bring to boil over med-high heat and cook until syrupy and reduced to 1/3 cup, 12-15 min. Watch that it doesn't burn during last minutes.
Swirl in butter and remove from heat.
Drizzle sauce over potatoes and serve

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
This recipe is from a diabetic website.  Balsamic vinegar sweetened with honey and cooked to syrupy consistency is drizzled over roasted sweet potatoes.
