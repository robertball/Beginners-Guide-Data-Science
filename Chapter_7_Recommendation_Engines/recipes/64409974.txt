Title:
Apple CranberryTurkey Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2276719.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups shreded turkey
1 cup Apple Chunks
2 Celery Stalks diced
1 cup dried cranberry
1/2 cup fresh parsley
3/4 cup mayonaise
1 salt/pepper
3/4 cup pecans
3/4 cup plain yogurt
pineapple tid bits to taste (Without juice)

Directions:
combine all ingredient and stir. Refridgerate for 2 hours and servce in king hawaiian rolls
makes about six cups of turkey salad
