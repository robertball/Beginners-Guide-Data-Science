Title:
Debdoozie's Blue Ribbon Chili- Veggie Style and cleaned up Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 T minced garlic
2 1/2 cups tomato sauce
1 (8 ounce) jar pace salsa
2 T Chili powder
1 T Cumin
1 t basil
1 T Oregano
1 t paprika
1 t black pepper
1/2 t cayenne pepper
2 (15 ounce) can light red kidney beans
2 (15 ounce) can dark red kidney beans
2 cups corn
1 red bell pepper chopped

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat saute the onion for 10 minutes, or until onion is tender
Add everything to a crock pot mix well, cook on low for 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Lindsay Thorpe
Description: 
Throw it all in the crock pot for 6 hours for an easier meal. Serve with corn bread on the side.Modified from: Debdoozie's Blue Ribbon Chili
