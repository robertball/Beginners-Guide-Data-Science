Title:
Vegetable & Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cloves garlic
2 medium onion
4 medium potatoes
1 lb. (6-8) carrots
1 celery stalk
2 (20 oz.) cans black beans
1 (16 oz.) red lentils
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp oregano
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
2 (15 oz.) can diced tomatoes
8 cups (2 boxes) vegetable broth
1 cup water
Freshly ground black pepper to taste
2 tsp salt (or to taste)

Directions:
Mince the garlic and dice the onion. Cook both in a large pot with olive oil over medium heat until tender. Meanwhile, slice the celery and peel and slice the carrots. Add the carrots and potatoes to the pot and continue to saut√© for about 5 more minutes.
Add the vegetable broth and water. Add the celery. Increase heat to medium-high and let boil for 10 minutes.
Drain the can of black beans and add it to the pot along with the dry lentils, cumin, oregano, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper. Finally, add the diced tomatoes (with juices)and stir to combine.
Let the pot come to a boil again. Once it reaches a boil, turn the heat down to medium, and let it slow boil for 15 to 20 minutes or until potatoes are cooked and lentils are tender.
Taste the soup and add salt and pepper as needed.
