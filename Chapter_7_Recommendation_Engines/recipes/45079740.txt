Title:
Blackberry Pecan Brie Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes frozen puff pastry tarts, thawed
2 (8 ounce) round Brie cheese
1/3 cup blackberry preserves
3 tablespoons chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and lightly grease with cooking spray.
Lay out patsry tarts and place a few cubes of Brie in each tart.  Bake until soft (about 12 minutes).  Spread the jam evenly over the top of the Brie. Sprinkle the pecans atop the jam and bake for about 8 more minutes.
Bake in preheated oven until the pastry is golden brown, about 20 minutes.
