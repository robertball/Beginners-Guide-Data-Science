Title:
Creamy Chardonnay Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large chicken breasts
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1/2 c Chardonnay
1 yellow onion
1 T grill seasoning
1 t salt
1 t basil
1 T minced garlic

Directions:
Saute chicken breasts & onion in 1 T EVOO with the grill seasoning. Combine the cream soups, wine & other seasonings & pour over chicken after it's browned. Simmer about 10 min to make a nice sauce. Serve with rice or pasta & a salad.
