Title:
Peach banana smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 can of peaches (15 1/4 oz)
* 1 cup of plain or vanilla yougurt ( your choice)
* 1/4 cup of orange juice
* 2 semi-ripe bananas
* 2 or more cubes of ice
* about 1/4 cup of sugar (more or less your choice)

Directions:
Combine all ingrediants into blender
put blender on highest setting to crush ice
blend until you like the consstancy
pour and enjoy
