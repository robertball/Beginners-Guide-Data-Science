Title:
Moms Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb Pork Roast
1/2 C Sherry
1/2 C Low Sodium Soy
2 T finely chopped garlic
1 T Dry Mustard
1 T Dry Ground Ginger
1 t Dry Thyme

Directions:
Put all ingredients in large plastic bag.
Marinate in frig for up to 24 hours.
Place all ingredients in roasting pan @ 325 degrees for 2.5-3 hours.
