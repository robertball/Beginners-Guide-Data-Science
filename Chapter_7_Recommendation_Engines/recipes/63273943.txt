Title:
Sunday Night Bouillabaisse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tsp EVOO
1 small onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, chopped
2 medium tomatoes, diced
2 tbs fresh parsley, chopped
1 tbs fresh thyme, chopped
1 tbs fresh rosemary, chopped
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp chili flakes
1 lb white fish, cubed (haddock or cod)
1/2 lb shrimp, peeled & deveined
1 C white wine
2 C fish or vegetable stock
1/2 tsp sea salt
pepper to taste
1/2 lb mussels, washed & de-bearded
1/2 lb small clams
1 lemon, cut into wedges
fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Directions:
In a large pot, heat the olive on medium heat.
Add the onions and garlic and saute a few minutes until the onions sweat.
Add the tomatoes, herbs and chili flakes and saute another few minutes.
Lay the fish, then the shrimp on top of the veggies.
Add the white wine, stock, salt & pepper and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 4 minutes.
Add the clams, vover and simmer until they start to open, about 5 minutes.
Add the mussels and simmer another 3 minutes until the mussels and clams open fully and the fish is firm.
Ladle into bowls and top with lemon wedges, parsley and pepper.
