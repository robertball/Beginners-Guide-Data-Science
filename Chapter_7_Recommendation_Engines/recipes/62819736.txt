Title:
Grilled Littleneck Clams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/665437.jpg
Ingredients:
3 dozen littleneck clams - rinsed
juice from a fresh lemon - optional
cocktail sauce - optional
melted butter - optional

Directions:
Heat grill to medium heat.
Rinse any sand off of the clams.
Add clams to upper shelf of grill
Cook about 5 mins until the shells start to pop open and the clam juice sizzles inside.
Take off grill top with optional lemon juice, melted butter or  cocktail sauce.
Enjoy!

Description: 
Littleneck clams are grilled to perfection in this quick and easy appetizer.
