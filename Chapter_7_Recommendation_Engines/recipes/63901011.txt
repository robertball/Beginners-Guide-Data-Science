Title:
Chicken, Mushroom & Broccoli Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen hash browns
1 500ml tub low fat sour cream
2 cans condensed cream soup (broccoli, mushroom, chicken,whatever you have on hand)DO NOT ADD LIQUID!
2 cups low fat shredded cheese (again, whatever you have is fine, cheddar, tex-mex)
1 head of broccoli, cut into bite sized pieces
1 8oz package of mushrooms, diced
1 package (500g) cooked chicken breast, diced
1/4 cup onion, diced (optional)
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1/4 cup melted margarine
1/3 cup shredded or grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray a large (9X13) baking dish with cooking spray. Set aside.
You may choose to blanch your broccoli for a few minutes depending on your preference.
Saute mushrooms and onions for a few minutes over medium heat until softened. Remove remove from heat.
combine all other ingredients except parmesan cheese in a large bowl. mix well to combine.
Transfer to baking dish and top with parmesan cheese.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for 35 - 45 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Ellen Violet-Muir
Description: 
The basic hash brown casserole recipe can be turned into a main dish with the addition of meat and vegetables. Try different combinations like diced ham and asparagus or cauliflower and mini meatballs! Just be sure that you meat is pre-cooked.
