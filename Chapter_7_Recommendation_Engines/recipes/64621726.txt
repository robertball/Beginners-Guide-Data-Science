Title:
Buddda Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large sweet potato, dubbed in 1 inch squares
1 Large red onion, diced
4 tbsp olive oil, divided
kosher salt
fresh ground pepper
2 cup baby spinach
1 pound  boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 tbsp ginger root, minced
3 cloves garlic, minced divided
1 tbsp soya sauce
2 tbsp smooth peanut butter
1 tbsp honey
1/4 cup lime juice
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp cilantro, chopped
1 tsp sesame seeds, toasted
1 avocado thinly sliced
1.5 cups Quinoa
3 cups Chicken Broth

Directions:
Place 1.5 cups quinoa and 3 cups of chicken broth on the stove and bring to a boil, turn to simmer and allow to simmer until all the chicken broth is absorbed.
Preheat oven to 425F.
Spread sweet potatoes and red onion on a cookie sheet and drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper and toss.
Bake for 20-25 minutes or until sweet potatoes are tender
Heat 1 tbsp of olive oil on a skillet, place ginger root and garlic in hot oil and saut√© for 1 minute, add chicken breasts and cook for about 6 minutes per side or until cooked through and juices are clear. Let rest for 10 minutes, then cut into 1 inch pieces.
Make dressing by whisking together 1 garlic clove, soy sauce, peanut butter, honey and lime juice until evenly combined.  Whisk in sesame oil and 1 tbsp of olive oil until smooth.
Divide quinoa between bowls. Top with chicken, sweet patties, baby spinach and avocado.  Spinkle cilantro and toasted sesame seeds and drizzle the dressing on top.
