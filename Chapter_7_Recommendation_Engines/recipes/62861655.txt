Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup shortening
2 eggs
1 cup peanut butter
2 ½ cups flour
2 tsp. baking soda
½ tsp. salt

Directions:
Preheat oven at 375F.
Mix all ingredients and drop by rounded spoonfuls on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake for 12 minutes.
