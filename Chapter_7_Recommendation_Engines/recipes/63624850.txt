Title:
Easy Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooking spray
8 corn tortillas, cut into 1/2" strips
1 (49.5 oz.) can chicken broth
1 (10.75 oz.) can cream of chicken soup
1 (15 oz.) can black beans, rinsed & drained
1 (15 oz.) can corn, drained
1 (15.5 oz.) can white hominy, drained
1 (10 oz.) can diced tomatoes & green chilies
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup celery, chopped
1 (12.5 oz.) can chicken breast
2 Tbsp. lime juice
1 Tbsp. garlic salt
1 Tbsp. cayenne pepper

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Spray a foil lined baking sheet with nonstick cooking spray. Arrange the tortilla strips on the baking sheet in a single layer; spray with cooking spray. Bake until lightly toasted and crisp, about 5-10 minutes.
Heat oil in a skillet, add celery and onion and saute until tender, about 5 minutes. Add canned chicken, lime juice, garlic salt and cayenne pepper. Set aside.
In a large pot, over medium heat, combine the chicken broth, soup, black beans, corn, hominy and tomatoes. Add the chicken mixture. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer about 30 minutes.
Ladle soup into bowls and top with tortilla strips.
