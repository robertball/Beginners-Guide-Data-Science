Title:
Ramen Noodle Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. pkg. slaw mix or 8 cups chopped green cabbage & 2 chopped carrot
1 bunch green onion (I use the green tops up to the tough part)
5 TBSP sesame' seeds, toasted
3/4 c almonds, sliced
2 pkg. chicken flavored ramen noodle (uncoooked)
Dressing:
1/3 cup EVOO
5 TBSP white vinegar (white-wine or rice ok but not necc.)
3 TBSP sugar
2 of the seasoning packets from the Ramen noodle pkgs.

Directions:
Toast sesame seeds & almonds in a dry pan on stove top or low oven. DO NOT BURN!
Mix dressing ingredients and pour over slaw mix or chopped cabbage & carrot.
Variations; add pulled/shredded chicken if desired, or for an light dinner entree'. Serve chilled or at room temperature (not if adding chicken!, of course)
