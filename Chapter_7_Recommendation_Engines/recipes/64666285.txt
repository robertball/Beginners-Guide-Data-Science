Title:
Stuffed Zucchini Boats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 med. zucchini
1 lb ground Italian Pork Sausage
1 bell pepper - yellow, orange, or red, diced
1 bell pepper - green, diced
1 lg. sweet onion, diced
3-4 carrots, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. basil
2 cloves garlic, minced
15 oz can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 c. mozzarella cheese, shredded

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
2. Cut zucchini in 1/2 lengthwise and use spoon hollow out, leaving 1/4 of the flesh. Reserve pulp.
3. Brown the sausage in a skillet over med. heat.  Remove the sausage from the pan with a slotted spoon and set aside leaving the dripping in the pan.
4. Reduce the heat to med. and add the peppers, carrots, celery, onions, and spices to the skillet; cook until the vegetables are soft, about 7 min. Add the tomatoes and sausage to the pan and simmer uncovered for about 10 min, stirring frequently.
5. Fill the zucchini with the mean mixture and top with the shredded cheese.  Bake in the preheated over for 30 minutes.
