Title:
Crunchy Dessert Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pint vanilla no-sugar-added, fat-free ice cream, softened
1 cup reduced-fat whipped topping
1/2 cup reduced fat peanut butter
1 (1 ounce) package instant sugar-free butterscotch pudding mix
1 cup Grape-Nuts cereal

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the first four ingredients; beat until smooth. Stir in cereal. Transfer to a foil-lined 8-in. square pan. Cover and freeze for 3-4 hours or until firm. Use foil to lift out of pan; discard foil. Cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Shirley  Reed
Description: 
'My son-in-law is diabetic and loves these five-ingredient frozen dessert bars,' shares Shirley Reed of San Angelo, Texas. 'With their nutty crunch from Grape Nuts cereal, we think they taste like the inside of a Snickers candy bar.'
