Title:
Ham, Swiss & Red Onion  Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 slices SARA LEE® Soft & Smooth™ Whole Grain White Bread
2 ounces honey cured or smoked ham, sliced
1/8 red onion, thinly sliced
4 slices Swiss cheese
Sweet mustard

Directions:
Pre-heat broiler. Toast the bread slices and spread sweet mustard on one side of each slice.
Top each slice with ham, 1 slice red onion, and 2 slices of cheese, in that order. Place on a cookie sheet.
Broil until cheese is melted (about 4-5 minutes). Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee Soft & Smooth™
Description: 
These delicious broiled open faced sandwiches are made with ham, cheese and red onion. A little sweet mustard adds a nice finishing touch.
