Title:
Golden Bread Shrimp Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
10 medium size shrimps (shelled except tail)
5 white slices of bread (cut in half and roll it flat)
1/2 onion  (sliced)
2 tsp curry powder
salt and pepper to taste
1 egg for brushing (well beaten)
oil for deep frying
Marinate the shrimps with onion, curry powder , salt and pepper in a bowl for half an hour.  Then  take a flatten white bread and scoop one marinated shrimp and some onion onto the bread and roll it, keep the tail out.  Closed the edges with egg .   Continue for each shrimp.
Heat the pan with enough oil to cover the bread shrimp roll (medium heat).  When Chopstick inserted  is bubble, it is hot enough.  Put in a few at a time and fry it until the bread turned golden in color.  Drain on paper towel.
Serve on a plate of lettuce.

Submitter: 
JANELIM
Description: 
Great presentation for a special occation.  Serve with rice and tomato sauce as dip.   Easy and delicious!
