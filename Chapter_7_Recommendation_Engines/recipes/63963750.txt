Title:
The Best Vegetarian Chili in the World Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 teaspoons olive oil
1/4 medium onion, chopped
1 bay leaf
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1 tablespoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon salt
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 jalapeno peppers, chopped (if you like heat)
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 (4 ounce) cans chopped green chile peppers, drained
1 (12 ounce) packages vegetarian burger crumbles
1 (28 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes, crushed
2 tbsp cup chili powder
1 tsp ground black pepper
1/2 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1/2 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
1/2 (15 ounce) can black beans
1/2 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Stir in the onion, and season with bay leaves, cumin, oregano, and salt. Cook and stir until onion is tender, then mix in the celery, green bell peppers, jalapeno peppers, garlic, and green chile peppers. When vegetables are heated through, mix in the vegetarian burger crumbles. Reduce heat to low, cover pot, and simmer 5 minutes.
Mix the tomatoes into the pot. Season chili with chili powder and pepper. Stir in the kidney beans, garbanzo beans, and black beans. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer 45 minutes. Stir in the corn, and continue cooking 5 minutes before serving.
