Title:
Two Step Salad Dressing for One Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp mustard
2 tsp lime juice
1/2 tsp olive oil
garlic salt to taste

Directions:
Whisk ingredients together in a small bowl.
Pour over salad and devour.

Submitter: 
Joyce
Description: 
Quick and easy salad dressing for those stay-at-home nights. Made with mustard and lime juice, this dressing is refreshing and summery.
