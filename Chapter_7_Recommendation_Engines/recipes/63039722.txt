Title:
Impossible Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs beaten
1 and 1/2 cups tasty grated cheese
1 small onion, chopped
6 rashers rindless bacon, chopped
1/2 cup self raising flour
1 and 1/2 cups milk
1/4 cup chopped parsley
salt & fresley ground black pepper
2 cups of your choice of - chopped mushrooms, peppers, cherry tomatoes, corn, peas, beans, grated pumpkin etc

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees C.
Spray pie-plate (or muffin tins) with non-stick baking spray.
Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
Pour into the prepared pans.
Bake for 40-45 minutes until set and golden brown.
Can be eaten hot or cold, cut into thick slices.

Submitter: 
Caroline Ashwood
Description: 
Wanted to make little personal quiche things, so I looked for a crustless pie.
