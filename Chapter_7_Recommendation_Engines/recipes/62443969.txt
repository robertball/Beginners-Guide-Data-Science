Title:
Easy Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/580940.jpg
Ingredients:
5 large potatoes (or as many as you see fit)
1 Tsp Salt
1 Tsp Pepper
2 TBSP Italian Seasoning
1 1/2 TBSP Minced Garlic
3-4 TBSP ofOlive Oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degerees
Pierce potatoes with fork, wrap with paper towel and cook for 5 minutes.
If you can pierce easily with fork remove and let cool for a few moments.
Cut into chunks when ready, may be a little hot still.
Place tin foil on baking sheet then drizzle with olive oil.
Spread 1 TBSP of minced garlic and sprinkle 1 TBSP of Italian Seasoning on baking sheet.
Spread potatoes over sheet evenly.
Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with remaining garlic and seasonings.
Bake for 15 minutes, sprinkle with cheese, bake for another 5 minutes until melted and bubbly.
ENJOY!
