Title:
Baked Pita Triangles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/223492.jpg
Ingredients:
6 pitas (6-inch rounds), split, then quartered

Directions:
Adjust oven rack to upper-middle position and heat oven to 325 degrees.
Place pita triangles directly on rack. Bake until golden brown and completely crisp, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Pam Anderson
Description: 
Pita wedges are great for an array of dips and spreads, and can be baked and stored in an airtight container for two days.
