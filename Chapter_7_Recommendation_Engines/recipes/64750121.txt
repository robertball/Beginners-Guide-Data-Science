Title:
Moist Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chocolate cake mix
1 can condensed milk or DIY (1/2 cup boiling water, 1 cup nonfat dry milk, 2/3 cup sugar, 3 tablespoons melted butter, a few drops of vanilla extract ...Pour the boiling water into a blender. Add the nonfat dry milk, the sugar, melted butter, vanilla, blend for about 1 minute )
fudge sauce...1 Tbsp butter, 2 oz chocolate , 1/2 cup white sugar, pinch of salt, 1/4tsp vanilla, 1/4 to 1/3 cup creamo...melt butter in saucepan, add chocolate stir until melted then add sugar and vanilla and pinch of salt, stir and add creamo until thickened. let cool

Directions:
Bake chocolate cake according to directions in a 9x12 pan. Let cool
Make holes with a wooden spoon all over top of cooled cake. Pour condensed milk over top and let sit at least 1 hour.
Pour fudge sauce over top. Let sit until ready to use. Then spread with whipped cream before serving.
Spread whipped cream over top.
Store leftovers in refrigerator.
