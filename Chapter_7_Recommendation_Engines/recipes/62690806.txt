Title:
Spanish Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/605607.jpg
Ingredients:
4 green bell peppers (whole)
5 mushrooms (diced)
1/2 medium onion (diced)
1 clove garlic
1 tbsp olive oil
chicken broth
1 box spanish or mexican style rice
1/2 lb ground beef
1 cup V8 tomato juice
cilatro (for garnish)
1 1/2 cups Mozzarella cheese
salt and pepper

Directions:
sautee onion in olive oil until translucent
add garlic and mushroom and sautee until tender
add rice from box and sautee with above mixture until coated then follow the instructions on the box for cooking the rice (using chicken broth instead of water)
In another skillet cook ground hamburger just until it's no longer pink
once the rice is done, add the cooked ground beef, and tomato juice. This will be the stuffing!
Clean the bell peppers, cut the top off just below the stems. Stuff the rice and meat mixture into each one.
Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
Remove the peppers and sprinkle cheese on the top of each one then stick it under the broiler just until the cheese bubbles and starts to turn brown.
Garnish with cilantro and enjoy
