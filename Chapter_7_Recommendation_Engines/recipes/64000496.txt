Title:
Brownie Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa
3/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 cup butter - softened
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Line cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Combine flour, cocoa, salt and baking powder in a bowl.
Beat butter with sugar until fluffy.
Add eggs, one at a time.
Mix in vanilla then gradually add dry ingredients.
Scoop dough out, wrap in plastic and refrigerate for 1 hour.
On floured surface, roll dough to 1/4 inch thickness then cut out desired shape and place on cookie sheet.
Bake 8-11 minutes, until edges are firm an centers are slightly soft and puffed.

Description: 
Makes 5 1/2 dozen cookies
