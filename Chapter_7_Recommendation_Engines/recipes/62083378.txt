Title:
Low Carb Sweet & Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 cup Splenda
1/2 cup sugar free pineapple syrup (such as DaVinci's)
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup low carb ketchup
1/2 tsp soy sauce

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and bring to a boil.  Continue boiling until reduced by half (approximately 15-20 minutes).  If necessary, thicken further with your favorite low carb thickening agent, such as xanthum, to the desired consistency.  Serve with chicken or pork and desired veggies.
