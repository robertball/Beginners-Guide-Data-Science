Title:
Mrs. Sigg's Peanut Butter Cookies Revised Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289485.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Cream the butter and sugars. Add eggs and blend. Add peanut butter and vanilla. Add dry ingredients and stir until well blended.
Dough is soft.  Cool in refrigerator overnight before baking.
Measure out tablespoonfuls of dough and roll into balls.  Roll in sugar and place 3 inches apart on cookie sheets. Make criss-cross pattern with a sugared fork.
Bake in a pre-heated oven at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) 8 minutes exactly. Do not overbake. Leave on sheets for 2 minutes before removing. Cool, and store in covered container.

Submitter: 
Jacqueline Allison
Description: 
I adjusted some of the instructions so the dough was easier to use, used only butter and lowered the oven temperature.Modified from: Mrs. Sigg's Peanut Butter Cookies
