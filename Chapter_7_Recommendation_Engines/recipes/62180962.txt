Title:
Garlic Mashed Potatoes. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Qt. Water
4 garlic cloves, minced.
Salt to taste
8 Red potatoes
1 pkg. cream cheese
optional: butter, mayo.

Directions:
Chop potatoes and mince garlic cloves, while bringing water to a boil. When water is boiling add garlic and chopped potatoes.
When Potatoes are tender, drain and mix cream cheese with blender into potatoes till creamy, add salt and butter to taste!
I always add a little  mayo!
enjoy!

Submitter: 
renee mae
Description: 
We love garlic in our family, and this is the perfect combination of a good garlic infused mashed potato side.
