Title:
Carole's Hungarian Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound stew beef, cubed
1 large onion, sliced
1/2 medium rutabaga, cubed
1 can mushrooms
1/2 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoon brown sugar
2 teaspoons paprika
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/8 tsp chipotle pepper
1 cups water
2-4 tbsp red wine (Merlot)
2 tbsp cornstarch

Directions:
Place beef in crock-pot, and cover with onion, rutabaga and mushrooms.
2. In a medium bowl, stir together ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, pepper, paprika, mustard, wine and 1 cup water.
3. Add to crock-pot and mix.
4.  Cover, and cook on Low for 8 to 9 hours, or until meat is tender.
5.  Mix cornstarch with a bit of water to form a paste, and stir into goulash. Stir continuously until thickened.
6.  Serve over cooked egg noodles.
