Title:
Tuna & Cottage Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I can of tuna fish, in water
1 1/2 Tbs Mayonaise
1/3 cup cottage cheese
1/2 tomatoe, diced
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Open and drain the tuna fish.
Mix tuna with mayonaise.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Add cottage cheese on top of tuna in bowl.
Add the diced tomatoes on top of the tuna and cottage cheese.
Mix all together or leave each ingredient unmixed, it is good either way.
It is best when you gat a bit of the fish and cheese in each bite.
Also try on a bed of baby spinach.

Submitter: 
StephanieLyn
Description: 
It sounds odd. If you can get past the title, you'll be glad you tried it. My mother ate this when she was pregnant with me, and it has been a family favorite ever since. It is a great protien-rich lunch or light dinner.
