Title:
Southwestern Egg Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
3 skinless, boneless chicken breast half
small onion minced
small bell pepper chopped
1 can of corn
1 can of black beans rinsed
5oz. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
4oz. can of green chilis
1T ground cumin
1T chili powder
2 teaspoon salt
2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
10(6 inch) flour tortillas

Directions:
Rub 1 tablespoon vegetable oil over chicken breast. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, cook chicken approximately 5 minutes per side, until meat is no longer pink and juices run clear. Remove from heat and set aside.
Heat remaining 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Stir in green onion and bell pepper. Cook and stir 5 minutes, until tender.
Dice chicken and mix into the pan with onion and bell pepper. Mix in corn, black beans, spinach, green chilis, cumin, chili powder, salt. Cook and stir 5 minutes, until well blended and tender. Remove from heat and stir in Monterey Jack cheese so that it melts.
Wrap tortillas with a clean, lightly moist cloth. Microwave on high approximately 1 minute, or until hot and pliable.
Spoon even amounts of the mixture into each tortilla. Fold ends of tortillas, then roll tightly around mixture.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or freeze.  If baking from frozen, bake at 350 for 20 minutes flip and bake for another 10.
