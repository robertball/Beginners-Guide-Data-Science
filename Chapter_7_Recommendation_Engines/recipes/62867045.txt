Title:
Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
Â¼ cup butter
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Place chocolate chips, condensed milk and butter in large microwaveable bowl. Heat on medium until chips are melted, about 3-5 minutes, stirring once or twice during cooking. Stir in nuts.
Pour into well-greased 8x8-inch glass baking dish. Refrigerate until set.
