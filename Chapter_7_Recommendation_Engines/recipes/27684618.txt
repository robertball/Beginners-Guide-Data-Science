Title:
Red-Pepper Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15oz can of chickpeas, drained
12oz jar roasted red peppers, drained
2 TBS. cream cheese
2 TBS. garlic infused oil
1 TBS. fresh lime juice
1 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
In a food processor, puree chickpeas, peppers, cream cheese, oil, lime juice, paprika, and salt until smooth.  Store in fridge up to 2 days.
