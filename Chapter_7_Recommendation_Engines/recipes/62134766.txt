Title:
Oven Roasted Tri Tip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1        Tri tip (preferably untrimmed)
1/4 cup  Oil
1/4 cup  Soy Sauce
1 med    Wedged Onion
1 tsp    Pepper
2 tsp    Cumin
1 tsp    Crushed Garlic

Directions:
Marinade tri tip 2-24 hours in fridge.  Preheat oven to 425 with tri tip on counter.  Place tri tip on shallow roasting pan (preferably on rack) with onions surrounding it. Remove roast when internal temp. reaches 135°F for medium rare; 150°F for medium (30-45 minutes). Allow to rest at least 15 minutes before slicing against the grain.  Enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
ROBYNSHERE
Description: 
We all know tri tips are made for grilling, but weather and life don't always permit outdoor cooking.  So here's a plan B that gets an A in our house.
