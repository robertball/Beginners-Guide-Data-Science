Title:
Frozen Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups flour
2/3 cup baking powder
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon cream of tartar
2 teaspoon salt
1 cup shortening
2 1/4 cups milk
2 large eggs

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Dough will be stiff. knead a few minutes. Roll and cut out as regular biscuits. Place on cookie sheet and freeze. When frozen, place in plastic bags. Ready any time.
Keeps for months.
Bake at 450 for 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
rlt11_NMC
Description: 
These biscuits are very nice to have made up and to have on hand when you are in a hurry. So much better then can stuff.
