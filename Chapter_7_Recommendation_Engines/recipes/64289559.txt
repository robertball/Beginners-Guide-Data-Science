Title:
Ampa's Slow Cooker Pork Loin with Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon dried sage
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
1 teaspoon dried marjoram
salt and pepper to taste
1 medium onion peeled and sliced
2 apples sliced
1 stalk of celery roughly chopped
1 5-6 lb boneless pork loin
2 cups apple cider or apple juice.

Directions:
1.In a small bowl, combine the sage, thyme, rosemary, marjoram, salt and pepper. Rub over roast. Cover, and refrigerate roast for 6 to 8 hours, or overnight.
2. Place sliced onion, celery and apples in bottom of slow cooker.
3. Place pork loin in slow cooker.
4.  Add apple cider/juice.
5.  Cover and cook on Low until very tender, 6 to 8 hours.
