Title:
scrambled eggs with smoked salmon asparagus and goat cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp butter
8 stalks asparagus, woody bottoms removed, chopped into 1 in pieces
salt and black pepper to taste
8 eggs
2 tbsp fat free milk
1/4 c crumbled goat cheese
4 oz chopped smoked salmon

Directions:
heat the butter in a large nonstick skillet or saute pan over med heat. when the butter begins to foam, add the asparagus and cook until just tender, season with salt and pepper
crack the eggs into a large bowl and whisk the milk.season with a few pinches of salt and pepper and add to the pan with the asparagus, turn the heat down to low and use a wooden spoon to constantly stir and scrape the eggs until they begin to form soft curds. a minute before theyre done, stir in the goat cheese.
remove from heat when the eggs are still creamy and soft (remember that like meat eggs will continue to cook even after you cut the heat)and fold in the smoked salmon
