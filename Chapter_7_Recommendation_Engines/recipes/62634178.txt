Title:
Dirty Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bacon grease (approx. 2 tbsp)
1 cup onions, chopped
1 lb chicken livers, washed and thoroughly drained
1 lb smoked turkey sausage, cut to bite sized pieces
2 tsp parsley
1 clove minced garlic
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp basil
1-10 3/4oz can chicken broth (1 1/4 cups) SAVE THE CAN
2 cans water (2 1/2 cups)
3 cans instant rice (3 3/4 cups)
salt and pepper to taste
Hot sauce to taste
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper (optional)

Directions:
Saute onions in bacon grease, in a dutch oven size pot, til browned. Add chicken livers to onions and cook, chopping into small pieces as they cook. Add smoked sausage and heat thru. Add rest of ingredients and bring to a boil. Trun off heat and let stand til rice is cooked, about 5-7 minutes. Stir to fluff and serve. Makes 6-8 servings

Submitter: 
Beckie Peach
Description: 
I like quick and easy. This recipe can be put together in 30 min. or less, and makes a good one dish meal. The combination of smoked turkey sausage, chicken livers and herbs, along with instant rice makes this dish a simple, yet flavorful comfort food.
