Title:
white pizza dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 envelope Lipton herb with garlic soup mix
8 0z sr cream
8 oz ricotta cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup chopped pepperoni
1 loaf Italian Bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In 1 qt casserole combine all ingredients,save 1/4 c mozzarella and sprinkle on top. Bake uncovered 30 mins. Dip with Bread.Enjoy.
