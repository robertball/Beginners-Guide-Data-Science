Title:
Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp Coconut Oil
1lb stewing lamb
1/2 lb shaved steak
3/4 lb lean ground pork
3/4 lb lean ground beef
1 onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp rosemary
1 tsp paprika
1 tbsp cumin
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/8 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tbsp beef bouillon
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 can tomato sauce
1tbsp worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup water (extra if needed to balance flavors)
1 cup frozen peas
1 cup diced carrots
2 1/2 pounds  potatoes, peeled and halved
2 tbsp butter
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
2tbsp mayonnaise
1/3 cup milk
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Sear lamb in coconut oil. Add to crock pot, retaining fat in skillet.
2. Brown shaved steak in skillet. Discard fat. Add to crock pot.
3. Deglaze skillet with ground pork. Brown. Add to crock pot.
4. Deglaze skillet with ground beef. Brown. Add onion, garlic, rosemary, paprika, cumin, cayenne, cinnamon, bouillon, salt and pepper. Add to crock pot.
5. Deglaze skillet with water, tomato sauce and worcestershire sauce. Heat and add to crock pot.
6. Add carrots and frozen peas to crock pot. Stir to combine everything and cook on high for 4 hours.
7. Boil potatoes. Drain. Mash with butter, cayenne, mayonnaise, milk, 1/3 cup parmesan, salt and pepper.
8. Spread meat mix in 9x13 casserole dish. Top with potato. Fluff with fork. Sprinkle remaining parmesan on top.
9 Bake at 375F for 45min until golden brown on top.

Description: 
Tons of flavor! Could you skip using the slow cooker? Sure, but it helps to develop the flavors dramatically. You won't be sorry! Make sure to use LEAN meats, though discarding the fat as necessary should result in plenty of flavor without greasiness!
