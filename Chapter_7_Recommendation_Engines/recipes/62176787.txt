Title:
Chocolate chip bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 c butter
2 1/2 c brown sugar
2 1/2 c flour
1/2 t salt
2 t vanilla
2 1/2 t baking powder
1 12 oz chocolate chips
1 c nuts (roasted)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cream butter and sugar, then add dry ingredients.
Pour into a shallow large baking pan.
Bake for 20 minutes, but watch.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
Kind of like chocolate chip cookie, but all in one pan!
