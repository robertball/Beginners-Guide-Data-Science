Title:
Spicy Black Bean Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cloves garlic
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon tahini
1 whole jalepeno, stem cut off
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil, plus extra for drizzling
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
Dash of Paprika

Directions:
1. Place the garlic in a food processor and mince. Add the lemon juice, tahini, jalepeno, black beans, extra virgin olive oil, cumin, salt, and crushed red pepper. Pulse until pureed completely. Place in a serving dish and drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with paprika.

Submitter: 
AshleyTex
Description: 
This spicy hummus goes great with homeade whole wheat pita pockets or pita chips. Sprinkle with EVOO and paprika and serve along side a yummy Lamb Gyro!! Delicious!
