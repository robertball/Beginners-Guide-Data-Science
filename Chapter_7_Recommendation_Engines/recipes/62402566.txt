Title:
Rum Balls 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz cream cheese
5C powdered sugar
4 squares unsweetened chocolate
1/4C Rum
1C chopped nuts

Directions:
Mix the cream cheese until creamy. Add powdered sugar,rum and unsweetened chocolate(melted). Roll in to small balls and then into chopped nuts. Refrigerate. Makes about 90 balls. Note: follow exact, if more rum is added, you will need to add alot more powdered sugar.
