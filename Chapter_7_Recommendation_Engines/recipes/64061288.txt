Title:
Freezing Corn from the Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7-8 dozen corn (about 60-65 cups)
22-24 quart size freezer bags
5 cups of Half & Half
1 lb + 1 stick of butter (5 sticks)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place up to 48 cups of corn in large roasting pan, remainder in small roasting pan.
Bake uncovered, on hour on bottom rack.  Stir occasionally.
Remove from oven to cool.
Place 2 - 3 cups of corn, with liquid, in quart size freezer bags.
(If using Foodsaver - pre-freeze 6 - 8 hours before sealing.)
To reheat - cook in micorowave from frozen about 7 minutes or if in Foodsaver bags cook on stove top in boiling water.
