Title:
Spice Islands® Creme Brulee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978397.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy cream
4 egg yolks
2 tablespoons sugar
2 tablespoons Spice Islands® Pure Vanilla Extract
3 tablespoons brown sugar
plus
1 teaspoon brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
Scald cream using a double boiler, if available.
Whisk eggs, white sugar and vanilla in a separate bowl. Temper the eggs by mixing 2 tablespoons of hot cream into the yolk mixture. Stir the tempered yolks into the cream. Strain cream and evenly divide the mixture between 5 porcelain ramekins. Place ramekins in a 1-inch water bath.
Bake for 45 to 50 minutes. Remove ramekins from water bath and cool on wire rack 10 minutes. Refrigerate a minimum of one hour.
Just before serving, sprinkle 2 teaspoons brown sugar evenly over each ramekin. Broil ramekins for 2 to 5 minutes until sugar is melted. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Spice Islands®
Description: 
Rich and creamy with a crunchy caramelized topping!
