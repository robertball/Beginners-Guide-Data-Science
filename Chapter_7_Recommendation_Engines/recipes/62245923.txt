Title:
Mommy's Monster Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 1/4c. brown sugar
1 c. sugar
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. vanilla
1 12oz. jar creamy peanut butter
1 stick butter, softened
1/2 c. M&Ms
1/2 c. chocolate chips
1/4 c. raisins
2 t. baking soda
4 1/2 c. quick cooking oatmeal

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Line cookie sheets with parchment paper.
In a very large mixing bowl, combine the eggs and sugars.  Mix well.  Add the salt, vanilla, peanut butter, and butter.  Mix well.
Stir in the M&Ms, chocolate chips, and raisins.  Fold in baking soda and oatmeal.
Drop by tablespoons 2 inches apart onto the prepared cookie sheet.  Dot with additional M&Ms (about 5).
Bake 8-10 minutes.  Do not overbake.  Let stand about 3 minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool.
When cool, store in large ziplocks.
