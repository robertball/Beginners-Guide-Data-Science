Title:
Chocolate butter cream frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-Tablespoons butter or margarine softened
2.5-3 cups powdered sugar
1/2 cup nestles quick
1/3 cup milk
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and beat on medium speed till smooth and creamy. Add more milk if too thick.
