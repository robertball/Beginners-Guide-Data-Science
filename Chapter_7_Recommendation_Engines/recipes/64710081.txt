Title:
Summer Crock pot Vegetable Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
3-6 ears sweet corn (cut off the cob) I used left over cooked
1 1/2 cup zuchinni (chopped)
2-3 c sliced kale
4-6 cups vegetable or chicken broth
1 c cream cheese (I used 1/2 c homemade compound cream cheese with bacon, onion and parsley) plus 1/4 c plain cream cheese
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 t garlic
crispy cooked bacon for garnish

Directions:
Instructions
Carefully remove corn from cobs
Add raw vegetables to slow cooker add broth
Add garlic, thyme, salt and pepper
stir Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours or high for 2-3
Stir in cream cheese and cooked vegetables (if using) and return cover until heated through
Stir well until cream cheese is fully combined
Top with chives and bacon or as desired
thicken if desired with a slurry made with flour and stock

Momofone
Description: 
Uses up summer produce and fridge left overs. Used a 6 quart crockpot
