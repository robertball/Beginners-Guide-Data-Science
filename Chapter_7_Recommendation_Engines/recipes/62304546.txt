Title:
thai red chicken curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/385108.jpg
Ingredients:
chicken cutlets
salt
canola oil
2 bell peppers
water
2 tablespoons red curry paste
15 ounces coconut mild
rice
basil
lime

Directions:
season the chicken with salt. in batches, cook in canola oil over medium-high heat until golden; remoove and slice. add 2 sliced bell peppers and 1/4 cup water to pan; cook until tender. add 2 tablespoons red curry paste and 15 ounces coconut milk; simmer 4 minutes. add the chicken. serve with ride, basil, and lime.
