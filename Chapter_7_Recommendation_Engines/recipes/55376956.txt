Title:
Yeastless "Crazy Crust" Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
Oregano
Basil
Garlic powder
Onion powder
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1 egg, lightly beaten
2/3 cup milk
1 Tbs. water
1 Tbs. olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a rimmed pizza pan or baking sheet.
In a large bowl, stir together first eight ingredients. Mix together eggs, water, oil and milk; add to dry ingredients; stir well. Place batter/dough onto prepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, until crust is set. Remove and cool slightly.
Add toppings and bake until cheese is melted, about 10 more minutes.
Set under broiler, if needed to complete.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
A quick and easy way to make your own pizza crust. Simply add the meat and vegetable toppings of your choice.Modified from: Crazy Crust Pizza Dough
