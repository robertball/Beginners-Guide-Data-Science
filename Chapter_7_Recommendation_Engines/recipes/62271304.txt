Title:
Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/344826.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter
1 cup brown sugar
½ cup sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1¼ cups all-purpose flour
½ cup non-alkalized cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon salt
2 cups old- fashioned oatmeal
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ºF. Grease a cookie sheet (or line with parchment paper). Cream butter and sugars together in a large bowl.
Add eggs and vanilla extract to butter mixture until incorporated.
Mix flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl. Add to butter mixture.
Add oatmeal and chocolate chips.
Roll into tablespoon-sized balls and put on prepared cookie sheet.
Bake for 12-15 minutes. Cool on a rack.
