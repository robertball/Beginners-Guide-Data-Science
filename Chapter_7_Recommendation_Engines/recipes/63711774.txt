Touch Lemon Juice - to taste
Salt and freshly ground Black pepper
1 to 2 slice(s) toast - buttered
A large dram (or more) Vodka

Directions:
In a small bowl roughly mash the sardines with the ketchup, Tabasco, Worcestershire, lemon juice;
Season to taste with salt and pepper and spread mixture on toast;
Add huge sprinkling of vodka and serve
