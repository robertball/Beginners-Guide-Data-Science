Title:
Instant Pot Mulled Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7689621.jpg
Ingredients:
One gallon apple cider
Peels or zest from two navel oranges, reserve oranges
1 cinnamon stick
6 whole cloves
1/4 tsp allspice

Directions:
Add all ingredients to an Instant Pot, give a quick stir.
Close and lock lid, set for high pressure, 6 minutes.
Allow a natural release. Use a slotted spoon to strain out solids. Discard.
Serve warm garnished with orange slices.

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
A fun, fall treat that is great with both apple cider or wine.
