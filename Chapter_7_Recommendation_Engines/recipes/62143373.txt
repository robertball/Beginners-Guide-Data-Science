Title:
Lushy Slushy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. amaretto
1 1/2 c. vodka
46 oz. pineapple juice
3/4 c. sugar
6 oz. orange juice concentrate
6 oz. water
Cherry 7-Up

Directions:
Combine alcohols, juice, sugar, concentrate, and water and freeze overnight.
To serve: Fill a glass 3/4 full with frozen mixture and fill the rest of the glass with Cherry 7-Up.
