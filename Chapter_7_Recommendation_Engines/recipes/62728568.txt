Title:
Red Wine Sauce with Mustard - Vegetarian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium shallots (or one large onion), chopped
1/2 cup dry red wine
1/2 cup homemade stock (veggie stock of your choosing)
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
2 tablespoons of coconut oil (or butter)
2 tablespoons of flour or cornstarch
1 teaspoon minced fresh thyme leaves
Salt and ground black pepper, to taste

Directions:
Saut√©ing the shallots or onion in the coconut oil or butter.
Turn off heat and let mixture cool.
Fold in 2T of flour or cornstarch, making sure that there are no lumps.
Slowly add the stock and wine, then heat liquid  until thickened.
Reduce heat to medium, add the balsamic vinegar and Dijon mustard.
Add the fresh thyme.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve immediately over your main course (noodles, potatoes, rice, etc.).
