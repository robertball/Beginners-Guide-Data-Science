Title:
8 Hour Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb chuck roast
1/2 cup flour
salt and pepper to taste
1 packet onion soup mix
1 cup beef broth
1 cup water
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup red wine
2 gloves of garlic, minced
1 onion, chopped
1/2 lb baby carrots
15-20 baby red potatoes, cut in half

Directions:
Mix together the flour, salt, and pepper. Dredge the chuck roast in flour mixture until just coated.
Sear all sides of chuck roast in pan on medium high heat.
Pour broth, water, soup, wine, onion, garlic, and onion soup mix into crock pot and mix until well combined.
Place chuck roast in crock pot and cook on LOW for 7 hours.
Add carrots and potatoes, then cook for an additional hour.
