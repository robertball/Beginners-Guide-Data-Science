Title:
Home Made Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/436757.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Hand fulls of Tomatoes (red or yellow)
1 Onions
4 Green onions
1-2 Jalapeno pepper (Half for mild. Up to two if you must have it HOTT!)
1 Handful of Cilantro
1/2 Lime
1/2 tsp- Garlic salt

Directions:
Wash Tomatoes and peppers. Trim blemishes and cores out.
Chop all fresh ingredients and put into a large bowl.
Add seasonings.
Get chips and you're ready to go!
