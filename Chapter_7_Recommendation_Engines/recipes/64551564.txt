Title:
Hearty Sausage and Broccoli Rabe 'Casserole' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4530676.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb hot  italian sausage remove casing
15 oz can of cannellini beans wash off all liquid
1 pac baby bella mushrooms wash and cut in slices
1/2 of a medium yellow onion coursely chopped
4 garlic clovers finely chopped
1 cup chicken broth
1 store bunch of broccoli rabe rinsed and coursely chopped so pieces can be enjoyed on a fork.
seasoning salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
On stove top in a large dutch oven cook sausage crumbling pieces as they cook
Leave oil and juices in pot remove and reserve sausage.
Add onion to pot and cook until nicely soft
Add garlic and combine with the onion cook 3 minutes
Add the rinsed beans and mix.
Add mushrooms and 1 cup chicken broth
Raise heat stir and let it come to a boil immediately remove from heat
Dump all the broccoli rabe on top
Cover pot and place in oven for 30 minutes
After 15 minutes the rabe has lost alot of volume .
Remove the pot and mix all ingredients season to taste with salt and pepper and return to the oven for the last 15 minutes.
Serve on its own or plate over a bed of pasta such as bowties or fusilli

Submitter: 
Jeffrey MacNair
Description: 
Plate of the 'casserole' over pasta. Don't forget to fold in the reserved sausage for the last 15 minutes.
