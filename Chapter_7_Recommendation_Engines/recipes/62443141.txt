Title:
Famous Longaniza Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/459271.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks of Longaniza (located next to the chorizo in the grocery store)
5 Eggs
1/4 cup milk
2 cups of frozen potatoes cooked.
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Remove longaniza from its casing and add to large skillet. Heat stove to med high and break longaniza up with back of spoon as it is cooking, you want the size of ground beef. While longaniza is cooking beat the eggs and add in the milk to the eggs, and salt and pepper to taste. After meat is cooked,about 10 minutes add in the eggs. Stir the mixture until eggs are set. Once eggs are set add in your cooked potatoes. Serve with warm tortillas or make into burritos.

Submitter: 
Brooke'smom
Description: 
These burrito's are a family favorite! Great flavor simple to make. We Love to make these on a Sunday while watching football! These burrito's taste just like chorizo but without all of the oil.
