Title:
Quick and Easy Sticky Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 clove minced garlic(fresh or jarred)
1/8 tsp ground ginger
1/8 tsp pepper
2 TBSP flour
1 TBSP vegetable oil
1-2 lb chicken cut into 1 inch(bite size) pieces
2 bags "boil in bag" rice(optional)

Directions:
If serving over rice, put water in a pot on to boil. When boiling, add rice for 10 min.
While water's warming, combine all the other ingredients(except chicken)in a large bowl and wisk until sugar is dissolved. Add chicken.
Add oil to wok and heat to med/hi. Add chicken mix and cook(stirring often) until cooked through- approximately 12-15 min.
ENJOY! Serve the chicken over rice.

Submitter:

Description: 
Chicken drenched in a sweet & tangy sticky sauce, and served over rice-all done in under 30 minutes! A recipe the whole family will love!
