Title:
Laura's Fancy BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup molasses
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1 tsp. steak sauce
2 tbsp. Southern Comfort
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/8 tsp. allspice

Directions:
Combine ingredients in saucepan and cook on stove at low to medium heat until thickened and reduced by about half, about 20-30 minutes.  Stir mixture constantly, and turn to low if it bubbles too much.

Submitter: 
CLOUDSSUNRAIN
Description: 
Barbeque sauce made with red wine vinegar and Southern Comfort for a little extra something.  Great for basting chicken or other meats.
