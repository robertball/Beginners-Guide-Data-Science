Title:
upside down pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 2 lb ground meat
* 1 med onion,diced
* 16 oz sour cream
* 2 cups shredded cheese
* 1 packet spaghetti seasoning
* 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
* 1 can crescent rolls
(low fat sour cream, cheese, and crescent rolls may be used)
(sour cream can be omitted)

Directions:
brown ground meat and onion.  drain
preheat oven to 350
add seasoning packet and tomato sauce. let simmer for 10 minutes
pour in the bottom of pan
spread sour cream over meat
add cheese
cover with crescent rolls
bake for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
melissa
Description: 
hamburger casserole that is layered like a pizza with the crust on top. fun for kids to eat!!
