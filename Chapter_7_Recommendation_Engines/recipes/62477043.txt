Title:
Sazon - dry seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp salt
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp ground Cumin
1 tsp ground Coriander
1 tsp Annatto Powder

Directions:
Blend all ingredients together and store in a cool dry place.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
Before GOYA packaged Sazon seasoning Mexican cooks would make up their own blend of spices.
