Title:
Spicy Chipotle Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 romaine lettuce leaves
1 ½ lbs. ground beef
2 Tbsp. EVOO
2 small onions, chopped fine
2 green bell peppers, chopped fine
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 -- 15 oz cans kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 -- 15 oz cans black beans, drained and rinsed
For the Sauce:
2 -- 15 oz cans tomato sauce
1 cup water
½ cup canned chiles in adobo sauce
3 Tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp. sea salt
1 tsp. dried oregano

Directions:
In a blender, blend tomato sauce, water, canned chiles, chili powder, salt, and oregano. Set aside.
In a large dutch oven over medium heat, saute the onions and peppers, with a pinch of salt, in EVOO until softened.
Add ground beef and increase heat to medium high.  Cook until browned and drain excess grease.
Add minced garlic and blended sauce to the ground beef and cook for 5 minutes.
Add black and kidney beans to ground beef and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve.

Submitter: 
halfnotes
Description: 
I had leftover canned chiles in adobo sauce, and thought I would try it in a lettuce wrap recipe.  It turned out great and my family loved it!  It is pretty spicy, but that's how we like it.
