Title:
Slow Cooker Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 sweet potatoes, baked and mashed
1/3 cup light butter, melted
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 cup Egg Beaters
1/4 cup fat free half and half
1/4 cup skim milk
1 cup mini marshmallows (optional)

Directions:
Lightly grease a slow cooker or recommend using slow cooker liner for easy clean up.
In a large bowl, blend sweet potatoes, 1/3 cup light butter, white sugar and brown sugar. Beat in pineapple, eggbeaters, half and half and milk. Transfer this mixture to the prepared slow cooker.
Cover the slow cooker and cook on LOW for 3 to 4 hours.
Add marshmallows the last 15 minutes of cooking.
