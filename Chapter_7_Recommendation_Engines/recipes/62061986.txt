Title:
Beef Serendipity Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. beef, cubed
1/4 c. butter
2 cans condensed golden mushroom soup
1 Tbsp. finely chopped onion
fresh mushrooms

Directions:
Brown thawed ground beef in butter and drain out most of the liquid.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and simmer over low heat for about 2 hours.  Serve over noodles or rice.

Submitter: 
Traveling_Is_Love
Description: 
A wonderful comfort food that's very simple to make.  This beef and mushroom sauce with a hint of onion is great over pasta or rice!
