Title:
Hot Fudge Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C Sugar
1/4 C Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 C water
1 C Heavy Cream
1 C Light Corn Syrup
1/2 C semi sweet chocolate chips
1/4 C Butter
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix first three ingredients in a heavy saucepan, whisk in water until well blended. Bring to a simmer over medium high heat, remove from heat and whisk in cream, corn syrup, and 1/2 the chocolate chips. Return to med high heat and boil. Whisk frequently until the bubbles become small and the syrup is thick and sticky (about 225 deg. on a candy thermometer). 5-8 minutes. Remove from heat and add the other half of the chocolate chips, butter, and vanilla. Whisk until smooth.

Submitter: 
Cathy Egan
Description: 
Great Hot Fudge for Ice Cream topping or fondue.
