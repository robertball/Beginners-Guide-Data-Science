Title:
Chinese Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of cabbage, shredded
6 whole green onions, chopped
1 2-1/4 oz sliced almonds
1/2 c. sesame seeds
1 pkg. chicken flavor top ramen, smashed - flavor packet removed
1/4 c margarine
Optional:  Cooked diced chicken
DRESSING
1/2 c. vegetable oil
1 tbsp. soy sauce
1/4 c. red wine vinegar
1/3 c. sugar
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter in large frying pan & add smashed top ramen noodles with sesame seeds & almonds - brown.
Add chicken flavor packet from top ramen to above mixture, mix well & cool.
Mix cabbage & onions in large bowl, then mix with above ingredients.
Mix oil, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, salt & peper for dressing.
Toss with salad and serve.

A quick and easy salad.  Popular at pot-lucks.
