Title:
Hungry Hubby Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C grated carrots
2 C pureed banana
1 C pureed blackberries
2 large eggs
3/4 C 1% milk
3/4 C brown sugar
1 1/2 C whole wheat flour
1 tsp baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 & spray a 9X12 cake pan with non- stick spray.
In a large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients & mix well.
Pour into prepared pan & bake 45-60 minutes, until top is browned & sides have pulled away from the pan edges. Middle will still be slightly moist.
Cool completely before serving.

Submitter: 
Jaree
Description: 
A filling, low-cal, not-to-sweet, nutrient packed cake bar that works for breakfast, snacks or even dessert if you top with a bit of whipped topping.
