Title:
Corn Casserole III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 (15 ounce) can creamed corn
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn
1 (8.5 ounce) package corn bread mix
1 - 1-1/2 cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a mixing bowl, stir together the sour cream, butter, creamed corn, corn niblets and corn muffin mix.
Pour into a 1 quart casserole dish and bake for 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Laura Minott Shattuck
Description: 
This mixture of creamed corn, corn niblets, sour cream, butter and muffin mix makes a suitable side dish for meat or poultry.Modified from: Corn Casserole III
