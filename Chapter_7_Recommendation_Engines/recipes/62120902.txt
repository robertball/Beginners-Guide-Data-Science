Title:
Roasted Chicken and Sausage with Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 boneless chicken thighs
8 sweet Italian sausage links
1 red pepper cut into 2" pieces
1 yellow pepper cut into 2" pieces
1 red or white onion cut into 6 wedges
4 large cloves garlic sliced thinly
2 cups broccoli florets
4 medium potatoes cut into 2" pieces
1 can artichoke hearts packed in water, drained
1 small zucchini cut into 2" pieces
1 small yellow squash cut into 2" pieces
8 baby portabella mushrooms cut in half
1 cup grape tomatoes
1 cup kalamata olives
12 basil leaves roughly shredded
spring of fresh rosemary minced
1 tsp dried oregano
1 pinch of dried red pepper flakes (optional)
salt and pepper to taste
3 T olive oil
2 T butter

Directions:
Heat a large skillet over high heat and add 3 T olive oil and 2 T butter.
Place chicken thighs in pan and cover with lid and saute until browned.  Remove chicken to large roasting pan.
Add sausage to skillet and saute until browned.  Remove sausage and add to roasting pan.
Saute the vegetables and garlic (excluding the tomatoes and olives) for 2-3 minutes.
Pour vegetables and pan drippings into roaster pan.  Add tomatoes, olives, chopped rosemary, shredded basil leaves, oregano, pepper flakes and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Mix together and arrange chicken, sausage and vegetables into single layer.
Roast in pre-heated 425 degree oven for 45 minutes or until chicken and sausage are fully cooked.

Description: 
A hearty meal to be shared with friends and family. Your kitchen will be filled with a wonderful aroma created from this roasted Italian dinner.  Have plenty of red wine and crusty bread to share with your guests.  Enjoy!!
