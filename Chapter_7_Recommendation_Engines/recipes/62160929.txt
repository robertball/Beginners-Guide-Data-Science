Title:
MINI  Gorgonzola pizza appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pizza dough either purchased or homemade. Roll out dough and cut circles about 3 inch and pre bake for 5 min. oven temperature 425.
l lb. fresh spinach, briefly sauteed in olive oil,cooled. Remove most of the liquid by squeezing. Chop spinach if leaves are large.
1 small jar of pesto
1/2 lbs. Gorgonzola cheese, crumbled
1 small sweet onion, thinly sliced
1 Cup Kalamata Olives, pitted, and  sliced

Directions:
Lightly cover MINI pizzas with pesto leaving a small plain border.
Top each with prepared spinach, onion slices, Gorgonzola Cheese and olives. Optional: hot pepper seeds
Bake 5 min. at 425 F. or until cheese melts.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
One of my favorite pizza recipes for appetizers or you may add a toss salad and you're set for a gourmet lunch with wine.
If you make your own pizza dough, add  1 cup of whole wheat flour to replace 1 cup of all purpose flour in the recipe. Makes a healthy and tasty pizza dough.
