Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2/3 cup flour
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup butter
2 tsp cinnamon
apples

Directions:
Peel and slice apples into a 8x8 baking dish.  Combine the rest of the ingredients with a pie blender until crumbly and spread over the apples.  Bake at 350' for 30 minutes
