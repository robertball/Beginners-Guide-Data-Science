Title:
Easy Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar ready made chocolate frosting (or vanilla frosting works too if you prefer)
1 16oz jar creamy or chunky peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla, optional

Directions:
Heat frosting in microwave for a minute just to aid in mixing. Pour into medium sized bowl. Add entire jar of peanut butter and tsp vanilla. Mix until blended. (Can add 1 - 2 TBS milk, if needed for mixing). Pour into buttered or well greased 8x8 square pan. Refridgerate for one hour or freeze for 30 - 45 minutes until firm. Cut into squares. Store in refridgerator or freezer.
