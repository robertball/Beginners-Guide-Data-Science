Title:
Bacon Wrapped Crab Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/406519.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12oz pkg Leg Style Imitation Crab Meat, removed from package and remove all plastic wrapping.
12 slices Smoked flavored Bacon, slice in half
toothpicks

Directions:
Peheat oven to 425 degrees.
On a clean cutting board, lay out 3 slices of bacon horizonally with out over lapping. Place 1 crab leg style meat vertically at one end of sliced bacon, and roll all 3 slices of bacon around the crab meat.
With a knife slice in between each bacon slice and cut into 3 little portions. Secure each portion with 2 toothpicks.
Place appies on a jelly roll(preferred) or cookie sheet pan. Lay out appies in a single layer and bake for 15-20 minutes, then with thongs turn appies over and bake an additional 10-15 minutes or until appies are a nice crispy golden brown.
Remove from oven and cool 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
My daughter's favorite thing to eat at Oriental resturants on the buffets are Crab meat wrapped with Bacon. So it's a nice treat for her when I make them at home.
