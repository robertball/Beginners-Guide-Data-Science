Title:
Kitty's Potatoes & Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp	cooking oil
3 cups	cooked potatoes skins on, cubed
6 Bacon strips, cooked and cut into small pieces
1/4 cup	green pepper, diced
1/2 cup	red onions, thinly sliced
6 eggs
2 Tbsp	milk
1/4 cup	shredded cheese (your choice)
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in large non stick skillet
Add potatoes and cook on medium-high until golden
brown
While potatoes are frying microwave bacon and cut into small pieces
Add peppers, onions, and bacon to potatoes and cook until vegetables are slightly tender
Meanwhile whisk together eggs, milk, and cheese
Pour egg mixture over potatoes, peppers, onions and bacon and allow a few seconds for it to congeal slightly
Stir everything in the pan with a large spoon until eggs are light and fluffy

Submitter: 
Katrina Freed
Description: 
This is one of my favorite brunch recipes.  If I happen to have any leftover Boardwalk Skillet Meal it takes about five minutes... a bit longer from scratch but always well worth the effort!
