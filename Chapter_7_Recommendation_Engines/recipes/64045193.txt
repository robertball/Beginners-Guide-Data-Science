Title:
Blue Ribbon Cheesecake by Anita Wisniewski Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 16 oz cream cheese
2.16 oz creamed cottage cheese
3. 1 1/2 c sugar
4. 4 eggs lightly beaten
5. 2 talb lemon juice
6. 1 tea vanilla
7. 3 talb flour
8. 3 talb cornstartch
9. 1/2 lb butter ( 1 cup) softened
10.16 oz sour cream

Directions:
preheat oven 325 degrees
Beat 1. & 2.Blend...Gradually beat in 3. & 4. beat till smooth. Stir in 5. 6. 7. & 8. beat in 9. till smooth...Blend in 10. pour into greased 9" springform pan ( greased with butter).Bake for 1 hr ..turn oven off and LEAVE CHEESECAKE ALONE FOR 2 HRS ....DO NOT OPEN THE OVEN DOOR AT ALL...remove and chill serve
