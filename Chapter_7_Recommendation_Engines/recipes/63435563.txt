Title:
Tender Tenderloin Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/931339.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lbs Tenderloin tips
1 can Mushroom soup
1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
1 medium onion chopped
1/4 cup water
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Combine ingredients in crock pot. Cook 8 hours. Serve over egg noodles, rice or mashed potatoes.
