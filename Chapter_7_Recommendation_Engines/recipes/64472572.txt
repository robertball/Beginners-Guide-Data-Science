Title:
Slow Cooker Cranberry-Apple Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7-10 apples, cored and diced
12 0z (1 bag) whole cranberries, cleaned and sorted
1 cup of fluid-water, cranberry juice, orange juice, apple juice or cider
1/2 cup sugar or to taste; will vary with sweetness of apples and type of fluid used
1/2 tsp cinnamon (opt)
1/8 tsp cloves (opt)
1 tsp orange zest (opt)
Diced nuts may be added to taste if desired.

Directions:
Place fluid, diced apples and cranberries in slow cooker on "high".  Cook for approx 5 hours, or until apples and cranberries are soft.  Add sugar to taste, zest, and spices as desired.  Cool and refrigerate.  If desired, this mixture can be processed in a sieve or a food processor until smooth, then sugar, zest and spices may be added as desired. Heat briefly to meld flavors, cool and store in refrigerator.  If nuts are desired, add while cooling prior to refrigerating.

Submitter: 
Elle
Description: 
A tart sauce using apples and cranberries.  Great over pancakes or waffles.
