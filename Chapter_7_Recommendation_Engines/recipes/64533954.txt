Title:
Mint Banana Chocolate Chip Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3078920.jpg
Ingredients:
1 very ripe banana
1/2 cup GF rolled oats
3/4 cup Gluten Free oat flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 tbsp 1 tbsp flax meal + 2 tbsp water (can substitute 1 egg white if not vegan)
1 cup almond milk
1 tbsp coconut oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 tbsp mint chocolate chips

Directions:
Make flax egg by combining in flax meal in a small bowl with 2 tbsp water
Allow flax egg to sit 5 minute in the refrigerator until mixture becomes an egg-like texture
All all ingredients except chocolate chips to a food processor and process until combined well
Add chocolate chips & mix into batter by hand
Heat a skillet over medium heat and spray with cooking spray
Add 2-3 spoonfuls of batter to skillet (a 4 inch size pancake)
Cook until small bubbles appear, about 5 minutes
Then carefully flip & cook another 4 minutes until both sides appear brown
Serve immediately

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Made with banana, almond milk, oats & flour, these light, silky & sweet Mint Banana Chocolate Chip Pancakes are vegan & gluten free pancakes!
