Title:
Simple Hush Puppies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup pancake/biscuit baking mix
1 cup cornmeal
2 Tbsp finely chopped onion
1 tsp + a pinch of salt
1 egg
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together egg, milk, & onion. Add the biscuit mix, cornmeal, & salt. Stir until blended.
Using a small ice cream scoop, drop batter into hot oil and fry until golden brown.
Drain, serve, enjoy.
