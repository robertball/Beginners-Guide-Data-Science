Title:
Cottage Cheese Dumplings II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 pint dry curd cottage cheese
1 tsp salt
1 egg yolk
1/4 tsp pepper
Dumplings:
3 eggs
1 tsp salt
1/2 c milk
flour to make stiff dough
3 Tbs oil, for frying
Sauce:
4 - 6 shoulder or picnic ham slices
1/4 c chopped green onion
3 Tbs butter, margarine, or oil
2 pints sour cream with chives
1 pint regular sour cream
1/4 c half-n-half

Directions:
Mix cottage cheese, salt, egg yolk, and pepper until well blended. Set aside.
Set large pot of water on stove to boil.
In large bowl, combine 3 eggs, salt, milk and flour until stiff dough forms. Roll out on floured surface to 1/4" thick. Cut dough into squares.
Place small amount of filling mixture onto sqare, fold into small triangle. Sealing edges.  Drop into boiling water for 7 - 10 minutes.  Place in strainer to drain.
Heat 3 tbs oil in skillet.  Fry dumplings until lightly browned on both sides.
In separate skillet, brown ham slices and green onion in butter or oil.  Stir in sour cream and half and half. Heat through.
Pour over dumplings and serve.

Description: 
Cottage cheese filled dumplings served with ham or sausage in a sour cream sauce. A bit of work, but well worth the effort!
