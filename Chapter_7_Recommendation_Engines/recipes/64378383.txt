Title:
Creamy Parmesan Polenta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2194887.jpg
Ingredients:
1 18oz tube polenta
1 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup grated parmesan
1 tsp ground pepper
dash paprika

Directions:
Dice polenta into 1/2 inch cubes. Place chicken broth and diced polenta in saucepan over medium heat. Heat for 5 minutes, stirring often with whisk. Remove from heat and add parmesan, pepper and paprika to taste. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Keith Haney
Description: 
Easy and delicious creamy polenta. Makes a great side, appetizer, or base for a hearty stew.
