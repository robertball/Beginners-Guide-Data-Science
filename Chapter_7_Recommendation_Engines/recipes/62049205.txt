Title:
Ginger Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons ginger
1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon cloves
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1/2 cup sour milk
1/2 cup molasses
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup raisins

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees(F). Grease and flour an 8" loaf tin.
In a large bowl sift together flour, baking soda and spices.
In blender mix all other ingredients until smooth.
Add liquid ingredients to dry ingredients, including raisins and mix well.
Pour into prepared loaf tin and bake for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Chris T
Description: 
Delicious gingery, spicey cake for desert. Goes gread with a huge scoop of vanilla icecream and whipped cream.
