Title:
Watermelon Pink Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4555766.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
2 tablespoons lemon juice
4 teaspoons white sugar
1 small slice (0.5 oz) watermelon

Directions:
Combine water, lemon juice, sugar, and watermelon in a blender. Blend until smooth.
