Title:
Chicken Tikka Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992686.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup plain yogurt
1 1/2 tablespoons tomato sauce
1 1/2 tablespoons Spice Islands® Curry Powder
1 teaspoon Spice Islands® Paprika
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 pound chicken tenders
4 lemon wedges

Directions:
Combine yogurt, tomato puree, curry powder, paprika, lemon juice and salt in a medium bowl. Add chicken tenders; stirring to coat.
Thread chicken onto skewers. Grill 8 to 10 minutes on medium heat or until cooked through, turning frequently. Just before serving, squeeze lemon wedges over chicken.
