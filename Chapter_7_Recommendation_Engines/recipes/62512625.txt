Title:
Over the top blueberry bread pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
4 cups heavy whipping cream
2 cups sugar
3 tsp vanilla
2 cups fresh blueberries or frozen
1 pkg (10 to 12 oz)white baking chips
1 loaf (1 lb.)French bread,cut into 1-in cubes
Sauce
1 pkg white baking chips
1 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:
In a large bowl,combine the eggs,cream,sugar and vanilla.Stir in blueberries and baking chips.Stir in bread cubes,let stand 15 minutes or until bread is softened.
Transfer to a greased 13-in.x9-in baking dish.Bake,uncovered at 350 for 50-55 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.Let stand for 5 mintues before serving
For sauce,place baking chips in a small bowl.In a small saucepan,bring cream just to a boil.Pour over baking chips,whisk until smooth.Serve with bread pudding
