Title:
Basic Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Roma tomatoes
1/2 bunch cilantro
1/2 white or red onion, peeled
~juice of 1 lime
1 jalapeno pepper
1 habanero pepper (optional)
1 clove garlic
~fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Take all ingredients and mix in a food processor. You do not need to chop anything. Just pulse a few times and you are done.
Hand chop tomatoes, cilantro, onion, peppers, garlic and combine in a bowl with the lime juice.
