Title:
Spanakorizo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
1 cup long grained rice
2 cups water (or stock)
1 bunch spinach
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 small onion (chopped)
1 lemon (juice and zest)
1/4 cup dill (chopped)
1/4 cup feta (crumbled)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Directions:
1. Cook the rice as in the water as directed.
2. Heat the oil in a pan.
3. Add the onion and saute until tender, about 5 minutes.
4. Add the rice and spinach and cook until the spinach wilts.
5. Add the lemon juice, dill and feta and remove from the heat.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste

Submitter: 
Julie Barracato
Description: 
A fabulous Greek rice dish with dill,spinach and lemon.Accompanies Lamb deliciously as well as a side of a cool refreshing cucumber sauce...Enjoy
