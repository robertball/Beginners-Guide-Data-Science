Title:
Cranberry Chicken & Potato Slow Cooker Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 onion, chopped
3 medium potatoes, chopped bite size
12 boneless skinless chicken thighs
1 1/2 cups whole cranberry sauce
1 tbsp chicken bouillon powder
1 tsp cider vinegar
1 tsp prepared mustard
1 1/2 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt
3 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
Place onion and potato in the bottom of the slow cooker. Arrange chicken on top.
In a small bowl, combine remaining ingredients and mix well. Spoon over chicken. Make sure you cover each breast.
Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours or on high for 3 to 4 hours.

Submitter: 
PolkaDot
Description: 
This is super easy to make, just mix together the sauce, throw everything in the slow cooker, and go about your day! Serve with steamed veggies on the side for a complete meal.
