Title:
Crash Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 whole new potatoes (any small round potatoes)
3T olive oil
kosher salt to taste
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil a pot of salted water.
Add potatoes and cook until fork tender.
On a sheet pan, generously drizzle olive oil.
Place potatoes on the sheet pan, leaving plenty of room between.
With a masher, gently press down each potato until it slightly mashes, then rotate masher and do again.
Brush tops of potatoes generously with more olive oil.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper (other herbs if desired).
Bake @ 450 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
