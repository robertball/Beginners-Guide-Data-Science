Title:
Baking Powder Substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 t. baking soda
1/4 t. corn starch
1/2 t. cream on tartar

Directions:
Combine thouroughly to make one teaspoon of baking powder.  This will become active as soon as it is mixed with any liquid ingredients.  So, be sure to cook the batter right away.

Submitter: 
Rachael Varblow
Description: 
Have you ever been halfway through a recipe, only to realize that you don't have the baking powder you need to complete it?  This simple substitution for baking powder works every bit as well as the real thing.
