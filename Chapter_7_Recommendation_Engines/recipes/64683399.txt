Title:
Chimichurri Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh flat-leaf parsley (about 1 bunch of parsley)
1 cup fresh cilantro (about 1 bunch of cilantro)
1/4 c fresh basil or oregano if desired
1/2 c red onion
6 cloves of garlic
3 T fresh lemon or lime juice
2 T red wine vinegar tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp freshly-ground black pepper
1/8 tsp red pepper flakes, or more, to taste
1/2 c olive oil

Directions:
combine all ingredients in blender
salt to taste
