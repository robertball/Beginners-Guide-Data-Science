Title:
Paradise Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups ice
1 1/2 fluid ounces gin
1 fluid ounce apricot brandy
2 fluid ounces fresh orange juice
1 lemon twist for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Pour in gin, apricot brandy, and fresh orange juice. Shake well, then strain into a chilled glass. Garnish with a twist of lemon if you like.
