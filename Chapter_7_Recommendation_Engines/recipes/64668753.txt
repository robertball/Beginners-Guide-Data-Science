Title:
Quick Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
Half of a sweet onion
1 tbsp flour
1 Ham steak - cut into 1/2" pieces
24-32 oz chicken stock
1 cup potatoes diced small
1/2 cup milk
2 cans creamed corn
1/2 cup frozen corn kernels
1/2 cup shredded cheddar
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Saute onions in oil until soft
Add ham and cook 3 minutes
Sprinkle in flour - cook 1 min
Add cans of corn, chicken stock (more for thinner soup, less for thick) and potatoes
Simmer for 10 minutes
Add frozen corn, milk and cheese - stir to combine and warm through
Salt and pepper to taste
