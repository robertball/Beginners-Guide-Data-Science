Title:
Barb's Famous Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg Cream cheese with onions
1 container Imperial Cheddar
2-3 Tbsp Ranch dressing
2-3 drops tobasco
2-3 drops onion juice

Directions:
When cheeses are at room temperature blend all well and shape into ball.  chill overnight
Roll in chopped walnuts.
