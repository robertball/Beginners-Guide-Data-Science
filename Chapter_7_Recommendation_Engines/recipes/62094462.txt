Title:
Amazing ground beef tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
2 T oil
1 small onion, diced
1 small can tomato sauce
1/2 can of corn
1 T garlic powder
1 t salt
1 t pepper
1 t ground cumin
1/4 t cayenne
1 t paprika
1/2 c water
Taco shells
Toppings such as cheese, sour cream, lettuce

Directions:
Heat the oil in saute pan.
Add onion and ground beef.
Cook until ground beef is cooked through and crumbly.  Drain fat.
Add in corn and cook for 1 minute.
Add in tomato sauce, garlic powder, salt, pepper, cayenne, cumin and water.
Simmer for about 10 minutes or until liquid is gone and meat is thickened into your sauce.  Serve.
Put into shells or tortillas and put in your favorite toppings.

Submitter: 
naedwards
Description: 
I love tacos and came up with this recipe one day when I didn't have taco seasoning.  These are very good and are great topped with cheese, salsa, sour cream and avocado.  Yum!
