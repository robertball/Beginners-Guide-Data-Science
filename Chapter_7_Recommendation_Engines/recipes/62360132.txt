Title:
Pretzel Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/403873.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup plus 2 Tablespoons of melted butter
3 tablespoons sugar
2 1/2 cups grounded pretzels

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, mix together butter, sugar and pretzels. Press the mixture into a 9x13 inch baking pan or pie pans. Bake in oven for 10 minutes; fill with desired pie filling.

Submitter:

Description: 
Adds a salty and crunchy element to any pie. Makes a 9x13 inch pie or 2 9 pies.Modified from: Pretzel Crust
