Title:
Apple Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1708673.jpg
Ingredients:
2 small apples (coarsely chopped), 2 cups All Purpose Flour, 1 cup granulated sugar, 2 eggs, 2 tsp Baking Powder, 2 tbs Margarine (may substitute any type of veg. oil.  I.E. coconut oil, or peanut oil), 1/4 cup milk, 1/2 tsp of the following ground ingredients:  Ginger, Nutmeg, Cloves, and Cinnamon.

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375 deg F.  Combine ALL ingredients in a mixing bowl (in any order) and blend them together thoroughly with either a whisk or a fork. Pour batter into 9" cake pan.  Bake on middle rack for 20 min (turn cake half way).

Submitter: 
Mara C Manzer
Description: 
This is a recipe I invented on the fly because I wanted some comfort food that would be easy to make, inexpensive, not TOO sweet, but just sweet enough.  Enjoy!
