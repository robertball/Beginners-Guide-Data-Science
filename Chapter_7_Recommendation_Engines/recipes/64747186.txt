Title:
Root Beer Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
2 cups heavy cream
1 cup 2% milk
4 tsp root beer extract
1 1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients together then place in ice cream maker and process according to manufacturer's directions.
Place churned ice cream into a freezer container and freeze overnight, or a minimum of 4 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
Ice cream that is infused with root beer flavor. It is fabulous!
