Title:
Lemon Tahini Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 Cup Tahini
3 Tablespoons fresh Lemon Juice
2 Cloves Garlic
Water

Directions:
Place Tahini, Lemon Juice, and Garlic in a small bowl.  Using a whisk, gently blend together.  Add water, a tablespoon at a time, and whisk gently until the dressing reaches the desired thickness.  Typically, this is 4 to 5 tablespoons of water.

Submitter: 
LonestarNR
Description: 
I love how versatile this dressing is.  I use it in a lot of different recipes as a tasteful binder.
