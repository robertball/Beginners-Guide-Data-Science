Title:
ATK Blueberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1056847.jpg
Ingredients:
1 recipe Foolproof Pie Dough
6 cups blueberries (about 30 ounces)
1 Granny Smith apple, peeled and grated on large holes of box grater, then wrung dry in towel.
2 teaspoons grated zest and 2 teaspoons juice from 1 lemon
3/4 cup sugar (5 1/4 ounces)
2 tablespoons quick-cooking tapioca, ground (see note)
Pinch table salt
2 tablespoons  unsalted butter, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
1 large  egg, lightly beaten with 1 teaspoon water

Directions:
DOUGH: roll 1 disc out on floured (up to 1/4 cup) counter to 12" circle, apz 1/8" thick. Roll dough loosely around rolling pin & unroll into pie plate, leaving at least 1-inch overhang on each side. Gently press bottom down. Leave dough that overhangs plate in place; refrigerate until dough is firm, apx 30"
FILLING: Rack to lowest position, baking sheet on oven rack, & heat to 400 degrees. Place 3 cups berries in med saucepan & set over medium heat. Mash berries several times to release juices. Continue to cook, stirring freq & mashing occas, until about half of berries have broken down & mixture is thickened and reduced to 1 1/2 cups, apx 8". Let cool slightly.
Transfer apple to large bowl. Add cooked berries, uncooked berries, zest, juice, sugar, tapioca, and salt; toss to combine. Transfer mixture to dough-lined pie plate & scatter butter pieces over filling
Roll out second disk of dough to 11" circle, 1/8" thick. Using 1 1/4-inch round biscuit cutter, cut hole in center of dough. Cut 6 more holes from dough, 1 1/2 inches from edge of center hole & equally spaced around center hole. Roll dough around rolling pin & unroll over pie, leaving at least 1/2" overhang on each side.
Using scissors, trim bottom of overhanging dough, leaving 1/2-inch overhang. Fold dough under itself so that edge of fold is flush with outer rim of pie plate. Flute edges using thumb and forefinger or press with tines of fork to seal. Brush top and edges of pie with egg mixture. If dough is very soft, chill in freezer for 10 minutes
Place on sheet & bake 30 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees and continue to bake until juices bubble and crust is deep golden brown, 30 to 40 minutes longer. Transfer pie to wire rack; cool to room temperature, at least 4 hours.
