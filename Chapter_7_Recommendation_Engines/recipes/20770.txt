Title:
Quite a Peachy Pour Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 cups ice
1 cup peach schnapps
2 (6 ounce) cans frozen peach juice concentrate
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a blender combine ice, peach schnapps, peach juice concentrate and vanilla. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
COSTANZA
Description: 
A drink that is smooth and sweet, and yet can also pack a punch!
