Title:
Tortellini Chowder II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1569220.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large onion chopped
1 large red bell pepper
2 Tablespoon minced garlic
1 4 ounce can diced green chile's
2 Tablespoon butter
6 cups chicken broth
4 cups peeled chopped potatoes
1/4 cup chopped bacon
2 Teaspoons cumin
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
4 cups cheese tortellini
4-5 Tablespoons flour
1 15 ounce can green beans chopped
2 cups milk
2 cups cream (or 2 cups half and half)
1 pack Knorr (dry) leak soup mix
2-3 drops hot sauce(optional)

Directions:
In a large pot saute the butter, potatoes, onions, 1/4 cup chopped bacon, garlic and bell pepper for three to five minutes. Add the chicken broth, milk, salt, pepper, cumin and cook on medium heat for 15-20 minutes or until potatoes are soft. Add the Leak soup mix, green beans and cheese tortellini. In a small bowl mix the cream with 3 Tablespoons of flour until smooth. Add to the chowder cook for 10 to 15 minutes until chowder thickens. Add hot sauce if desired.
