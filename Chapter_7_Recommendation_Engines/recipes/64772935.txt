Title:
Rhubarb Mimosa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 stalks rhubarb, cut into 1/2" pieces
1 cup sugar
1 sprig mint leaves
1 bottle prosecco, chilled

Directions:
1. Combine rhubarb, sugar, and 2 cups water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil and stir until the rhubarb is slightly broken down. 10 minutes.
2. Remove from heat and add mint. Let sit 1 minute and then strain. The mixture should be syrupy. If its too watery, return to stove and cook down more.
3. Divide the syrup amoung 6 champagne glasses andd top with the prosecco.
