Title:
Healthier Oatmeal cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup raisins
1 1/4 cups water
2/3 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 cup rolled oats
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/4 cup wheat germ
1/4 cup ground flax seed
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions:
Boil raisins for 2 to 3 minutes, reserving raisin water.
Cream margarine and sugar.  Add eggs, oatmeal and 9 tablespoons raisin water.  In a separate bowl, sift together flours,wheat germ, flax seed, baking soda and spices  Stir in raisins.
Drop by a rounded teaspoonful onto cookie sheet.  Bake 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
sparks55
Description: 
Very moist oatmeal cookie that is low sugar, high fiber. The raisin water adds plenty of sweetness.Modified from: Oatmeal Raisin Cookies III
