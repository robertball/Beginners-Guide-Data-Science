Title:
Taco Ring Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 crescent roll packages
2 lbs. hamburger meat
1 packet taco seasoning
1 small yellow onion
1 cup cheese (more if desired)
Toppings
Lettuce
Sour Cream
Tomato
Salsa

Directions:
Brown meat with small onion
Drain meat
Place meat back in cooking pan or seperate bowl
Add taco seasoning and cheese and stir until cheese is melted
On a large baking sheet place the crescent rolls in circular shape
You want the points facing away from the center
Press the crescent rolls together on the pan
Spoon meat and cheese mixture over the crescent rolls
Fold the tip of the crescent roll over the meat
Bake at 375 degrees for 15 minutes
Place desired toppings on top of taco ring and enjoy
