Title:
*EASY BEET SALAD* Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/396508.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium beets
1 carrot
1 clove of garlic, crushed
4 tablespoons of mayo
1 handful of *LA CHOY* dried asian noodles
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, shred the beets and carrots. Add the garlic, mayo, and seasoning. Mix all together and cool in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving. Sprinkle with asian noodles and serve.

Submitter: 
alyonamade
Description: 
This is a Ukrainian classic. This quick recipe is not only intense in flavor but also very easy to make with ingredients you have in hand!
