Title:
Apple Pie Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tubes of cinnamon rolls
4 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1 Tblsp cinnamon
1 tsp. vanilla extract
2 Tblsp butter
1 cup brown sugar
2 Granny Smith apples, chopped with skin
Icing from rolls

Directions:
Chop the rolls into cubes
Whisk together eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla
Heat butter in skillet, add chopped apples and brown sugar; cook  until soft
Put cut up rolls in 8 x 8 pan
Pour over egg mixture
Top with chopped apples and icing
Bake in 375 degree oven for 25-30 minutes.
