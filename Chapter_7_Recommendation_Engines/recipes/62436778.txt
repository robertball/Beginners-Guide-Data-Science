Title:
Baked Oatmeal II for Two Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups rolled oats
1/4 cup Splenda brown sugar blend
1 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
1/4 cup melted butter
1-1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 cup dried raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt. Beat in milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Stir in raisins. Spread into a 8x8 inch baking dish.
Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes.
