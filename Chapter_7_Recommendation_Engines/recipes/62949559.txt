Title:
Maw-Maw's 2 2 2 5  Cake... Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/725545.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2 sticks butter
2 cups flour
2 tsp vanilla
5 eggs
1 cup pecans (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
in lg mixing bowl combine sugar and butter (cream together)
mix in eggs (one at a time)
Stir in flour
add vanilla and pecans and mix
Bake at 350 degrees in well greesed bundt pan about 45 minutes

Submitter: 
Nauntie
Description: 
Yummy! Great for brunch, holidays, or most anything. Need to bring a dish, this one travels well. Can be put on a paper plate and wrapped with plastic wrap, and you have no dishes to worry with. Simple ingredients, and looks good right out of the bundt pan! 

 Recipe source: Laura Moody (Maw-Maw)who got it from one of the ladies from Globemaster Tools (Doris Watson) (1967 or 1968)
