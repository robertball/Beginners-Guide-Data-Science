Title:
Old Bay速 Shrimp Salad Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 pound shrimp, cooked
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup chopped celery
2 teaspoons Old Bay速 Seasoning
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Peel and devein shrimp; cut in half and place in bowl.  Set aside.
Combine dressing ingredients and mix well.  Pour dressing over shrimp and toss gently.  Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour.
Toss salad just before serving and serve on bed of lettuce or sandwich roll.
