Title:
PB Kiss Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of peanut butter
3 Tbsp. crushed walnuts (optional)
1 egg (whole)
1/2 cup of white sugar
Hershey kisses (peeled)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
In a bowl mix the first 4 ingredients together (Usually easier with an electrical beater).
Make 1 in. balls of the cookie mix
Place on a cookie sheet with parchment paper
Bake for about 15-20 min. or until a toothpick comes out clean
Place on a wire rack
While still warm place a Hershey's kiss on each cookie (Don't push too hard on the kiss or it will become mush).

Submitter: 
Chef Kiki
Description: 
This is a gluten-free recipe that has quickly become a fave! Very rich and very addicting (most people can't have just 1).
