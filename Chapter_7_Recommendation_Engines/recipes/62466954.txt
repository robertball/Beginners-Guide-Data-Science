Title:
Slow Cooker Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups lentils
7 1/2 cups water
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 onion, diced
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1 celery stalk, diced
Salt and pepper
1 1/2 tsp. cumin

Directions:
Rinse lentils with hot water and place in slow cooker.
Add water, broth and ham. Stir.
In a saucepan, brown onion, garlic and celery. Add to slow cooker.
Season with salt, pepper and cumin.
Cook on low for 8 hours.
Puree entire soup before serving.
