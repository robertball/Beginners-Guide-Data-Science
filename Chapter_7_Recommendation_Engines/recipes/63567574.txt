Title:
Cheesecake Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (19.8 ounce) package brownie mix
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 egg
1/3 cup white sugar
1 t vanilla
3 T butter
1 T flour

Directions:
Prepare the brownie mix as directed by manufacturer. Preheat oven to temperature indicated on box. Grease a 9x13 inch pan or line with parchment paper.
Spread 1/2 the brownie batter evenly into the prepared pan. Using an electric mixer, beat together the cream cheese, egg and sugar, vanilla butter and flour until smooth. Dollop the cream cheese mixture on top of the brownie batter.  Spread rest of brownie batter on top.  Swirl together using a knife or skewer.
Bake according to manufacturer's instructions. Brownies will be done when a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Cool in the pan, then cut into bars and serve.
