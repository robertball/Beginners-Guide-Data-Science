Title:
Baked Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. extra lean ground beef
1/2cup each chopped onions and green peppers
24 oz. spaghetti sauce
10 ounces Philadelphia Italian Cheese and herb cooking crème, divided
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
3 cups cooked penne pasta

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Brown meat and vegetables in large nonstick skillet. Stir in spaghetti sauce, 3/4 cup cooking crème and 1/2 cup mozzarella; cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes until cheese is melted. Add pasta; mix lightly.
Spoon into 2 qt. casserole; top with remaining cooking crème and mozzarella. Cover
Bake 20 minutes or until heated through, uncovering after 15 minutes.
