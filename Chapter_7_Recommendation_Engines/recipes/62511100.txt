Title:
Granny Annie's Cranberry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 boneless chicken breasts, skinned
1 8oz. bottle Russian dressing or Dorothy Lynch salad dressing
1 pkg. dry onion soup mix
1 large can cranberry sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Place chicken in 9x13 inch pan.  Bake for 20 minutes.
Pour off juice.
Mix salad dressing, soup mix and cranberry sauce.  Pour over chicken.
Bake uncovered for 45 min.
