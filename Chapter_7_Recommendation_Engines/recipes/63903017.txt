Title:
Spinach Bread Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1082881.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Knorr vegetable soup mix
1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
1 1/2 cups sour cream
5 ounces spinach (frozen, chopped, thawed, rinsed, and squeezed out)
Hawaiian bread

Directions:
Thaw, rinse, and drain spinach.
2
Mix ingredients together and serve with Hawaiian or twist bread.
3
Refrigerate before serving.
