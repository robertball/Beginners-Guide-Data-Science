Title:
Balsamic Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 5 oz. lean pork
* 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
* 1/4 tsp. black pepper
* 2 1/2 tsp. balsamic vinegar
* 1/3 tbsp. olive oil

Directions:
Rinse, trim of visible fat, and pat dry pork. Cut pork into thin slices. Season with garlic powder, black pepper and balsamic vinegar and turn to coat evenly. Heat olive oil in non stick skillet to medium high and saut√© pork 2 to 3 minutes, until no longer pink. Remove and serve with brown rice.
