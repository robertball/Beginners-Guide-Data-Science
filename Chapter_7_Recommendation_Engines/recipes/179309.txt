Title:
The Ciroc Royal Grape Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983330.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Ciroc Vodka
1 ounce white grape juice
1 splash champagne
1 piece seedless grapes

Directions:
Shake ingredients over ice and strain into a chilled glass.
Top with a splash of champagne and garnish with a frozen grape.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
A grape-flavored Ciroc Vodka cocktail is enhanced with a frozen grape garnish.
