Title:
Quick Fix Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984575.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (11 ounce) package Sister Schubert's Parker House Rolls, defrosted
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/4 teaspoon Italian herb blend
1/3 cup pizza sauce
1/2 cup shredded Mozzarella
16 pepperoni slices
1/4 cup diced green bell pepper
2 thin slices white onion, separated into rings
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and coat with non-stick vegetable oil spray.
Carefully remove Sister Schubert Parker House Rolls from pan and place on prepared pan. Using a rolling pin, compress the rolls into a 9- inch round. In a small bowl, combine olive oil and herb blend and brush top of crust. Spread pizza sauce over top of crust. Top with remaining ingredients. Bake 18 to 20 minutes. Loosen pizza with a large spatula if necessary. Cool slightly and slice into desired pieces. Serve.
