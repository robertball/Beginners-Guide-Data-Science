Title:
Oriental Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag spinach , 1 bag carrots , 1 small red cabbage , 1 large sweet onion.  2 packages Ramon noodles. 2 or 3 TBS Sesame seeds , 1/2 cup Slivered Almonds (toasted)
Dressing: 3/4 cup Olive oil , 1/4 cup Rive vinegar , 1/8 Cup Sugar , 2 TBS Salt , 1 tsp Sugar , 1 small can Mandarin Oranges (optional)

Directions:
Shred spinach , carrots , cabbage. Sliver onions.
Place all ingredients in big bowl with crushed noodles, add dressing mix well

Applewood
Description: 
A neat salad with a bit of sweet and sour
