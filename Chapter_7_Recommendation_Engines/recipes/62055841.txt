Title:
Dorsey Vegetable Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch scallions, minced, white parts and some green
4 hard boiled eggs
1 cup Hellman's or Kraft mayonnaise
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
2 tbs. extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
Heavy dash of Tabasco sauce
1/2 tsp. salt
ground peppper to taste
1 tbs, fresh parsley leaves, minced

Directions:
Place eggs in cold water, bring to a boil, and cook for ten minutes. Rinse the eggs in cold water. Meanwhile, blend the other ingredients together in a bowl, except for the parsley. Shell the eggs and coarsely chop them. Top your vegetables with the sauce, some of the eggs, and parsley.

Submitter: 
COOKINCOWGIRLS
Description: 
I make this sauce when I want an easy, delicious topping for green beans, asparagus, broccoli, cailiflower, or even veggie burgers.
