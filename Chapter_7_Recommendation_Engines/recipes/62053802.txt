Title:
Low Fat  potato soup Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
6 cups diced potatoes (I prefer larger chunks)
1 carrot diced
1 med. onion diced
1 large can chicken broth (reserve 1 cup for gravy)
2- Â½ inch slices of deli ham diced
MCKORMICKS COUNTRY STYLE GRAVY MIX

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a large pot and bring to a boil for about 5 minutes, turn to simmer.  In a separate container whisk in packet of country gravy mix while broth is cold.  Add broth and gravy to pot and simmer for about one hour.
