Title:
Chipotle Barbecue Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/722755.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken legs or 2 Chicken Legs with thighs
1 15 oz can of tomato Sauce
3 tbsp of onions, minced, or 1 tbsp of onion powder
3 tbsp of garlic, minced, or 1 tbsp of garlic powder
--- Optional Smoke, Your Selection ---
Mild: 1 teaspoon of liquid smoke
Medium: 2 teaspoons of liquid smoke
Very: 3 teaspoons of liquid smoke
--- Optional Tangy, Your Selection ---
Mild: 2 tablespoons of apple cyder vinegar
Medium: 4 tablespoons of apple cyder vinegar
Very: 6 tablespoons of apple cyder vinegar
--- Optional Heat, Your Selection ---
Mild: 2 tablespoons of chipotle hot sauce
Medium spicy: 4 tablespoons of chipotle hot sauce
Spicy: 6 tablespoons of chipotle hot sauce
--- End Of Options ---

Directions:
place 1 cup of hickory wood chips into a 2 cup measuring cup, fill with water and allow it to set until you are ready to cook.
Put the chips on coals, or if your using a gas grill in a wood chip box and set it on the lower rack.
Put chicken in the grill on the top rack, at a low burner setting for 45 mins.
After one hour coat chicken with sauce and turn after 5 mins.
Coat the chicken once more and again, turn after 5 mins.
the chicken is now ready to eat with what ever side dishes you wish to have, like corn on the cob, beans, grilled potatoes etc....
