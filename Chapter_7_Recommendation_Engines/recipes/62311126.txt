Title:
Baked Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 med to large idaho bakers
1 small chopped red onion
two stalks of celery chopped small
Red pepper diced
6-8 hard boiled eggs - diced
Dressing
Mayo - about 3/4 cup
White wine vineger about
1-2 TBLSpoons of sugar (sweeten to taste)
A couple of good squirts of Dijon mustard
salt to taste
1 Tea of tarragon

Directions:
Bake potatoes, cool and dice them up
Mix with veggies and eggs
Cover and coat with dressing
Chill for a few hours before serving
