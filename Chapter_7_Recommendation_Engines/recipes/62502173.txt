Title:
Hazelnut Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine (softened)
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar, packed
2 large eggs
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 1/4 cup flour
1/3 cup finely chopped hazelnuts (toasted and skins removed)
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
2 3/4 cup quick-cooking rolled oats (not instant)
1 1/3 cups chocolate hazelnut spread (Nutella)

Directions:
Cream margarine and sugars in a bowl. Add eggs 1 at a time, beating well after each addition. Add vanilla and beat until smooth.
Combine next 5 ingredients in a small bowl. Add to margarine mixture in 2 additions, mixing well after each addition until no dry flour remains.
Add rolled oats, mix well and let stand 30 min. Roll dough into balls, using 1 1/2 tsp. for each. Arrange 2 inches apart.
Bake at 350 F for 8 minutes.
Let cool, then spread 1 tsp. Nutella on bottom of cookie and make cookie sandwiches.
