Title:
Sparkling Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. frozen orange juice concentrate
12 frozen strawberries, sliced in sugar
2 bananas, peeled and cubed
1 c. club soda, cold
1/2 tray of ice cubes
1 c. milk
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Blend banana, strawberries, and partially frozen orange juice.
Add vanilla, milk, cream, and club soda and blend.
Slowly add ice and blend until all ingredients are mixed.

Submitter: 
MELLYGIRL23
Description: 
This is a good cold drink on a warm summer day.  It is easy to make and my kids really enjoy it.  I often omit the sugar to make it slightly healthier for my boys and use powdered vanilla sometimes as well.
