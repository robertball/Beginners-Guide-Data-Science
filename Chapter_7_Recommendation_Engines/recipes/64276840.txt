Title:
Apricot Chicken I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves or 12 thighs
1 1/2 (1 ounce) packages dry onion soup mix
1 (10 fluid ounce) bottle Catalina salad dressing
1 cup sugar free apricot preserves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place the chicken pieces in a 4 quart casserole dish. Mix the soup mix, dressing and jam together, and pour over the chicken.
Cover dish and bake for 1 hour in preheated oven or 4-6 hours on low in a crock pot.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Crystal S
Description: 
Quickly prepared with jam, soup mix and bottled salad dressing, this chicken will surprise you with its intricate melding of flavors.Modified from: Apricot Chicken I
