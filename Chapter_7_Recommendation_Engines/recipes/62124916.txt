Title:
Best Chili Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb Ground beef
1 lg can tomato sauce
1 lg can kidney beans
1 lg can pinto beans
1 lg can diced green chilis
2 lg cans diced tomatoes
1 medium onion, diced
2 ribs celery, diced
2 tsp cumin powder
3 T chili powder
1 1/2 tsp black pepper
2 tsp salt
2 cups water

Directions:
Brown ground beef, drain fat.
Add all remaining ingredients
Simmer for 2 hours

Submitter: 
ChefJoe
Description: 
My family loves this chili recipe.  My sister and brother-in-law ask me to make it for them all the time.  Serve with grated cheese and a dollop of sour cream for extra goodness.
