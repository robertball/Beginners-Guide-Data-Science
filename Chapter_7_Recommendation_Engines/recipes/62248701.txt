Title:
Corned Beef Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (5 1/2 pound) corned beef brisket with spice packet
7 small potatoes, peeled and diced
4 carrots, peeled and diced
3 cloves garlic, chopped
1 head of cabbage

Directions:
Put brisket into a Dutch Oven and cover with water about 2" over the brisket.  Put spices from packet into a tea steeper and add to water.
