Title:
Cauliflower "Potatoes" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head of cauliflower, cut into pieces
1/4 cup homemade yogurt
salt, pepper to taste
parsley and paprika to garnish

Directions:
Cook cauliflower until just tender. Drain.
Puree cauliflower in blender or food processor and then add yogurt, salt, and pepper and blend thoroughly.
Reheat and serve garnished with parsley and paprika. The pureed cauliflower may be placed in a baking dish, sprinkled with grated cheddar cheese and heated in the oven until the cheese melts.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This recipe from Breaking the Vicious Cycle by Elaine Gottschall is a delicious mashed potato substitute.
