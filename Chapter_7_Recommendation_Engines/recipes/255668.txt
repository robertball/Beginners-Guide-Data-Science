Title:
Cranberry Orange Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4061115.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups Unsweetened Vanilla Almond Breeze Almondmilk
1 cup frozen cranberries
1/2 cup fresh or frozen raspberries
1 orange, peeled and seeded
2 tablespoons chia seeds
2 scoops protein powder

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Cranberries, raspberries, and oranges are blended with vanilla almondmilk, chia seeds, and protein powder for a nutritious on-the-go breakfast or snack.
