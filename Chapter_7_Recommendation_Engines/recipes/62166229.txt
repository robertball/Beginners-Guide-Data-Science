Title:
Quick Crock Pot Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef
1 med. green pepper diced
1 can kidney beans - rinsed and drained
1 can diced tomatoes
2 cans Mexican stewed tomatoes
1 sm. can diced green chilis
1 sm. can whole kernel corn (I prefer no salt added)
2 cloves garlic
2 Tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp. sea salt
pepper to taste

Directions:
In a skillet brown ground beef and drain.
Add all ingredients except corn into a crock pot with ground beef, stirring gently to combine.
Set on low heat for 6-8hrs.
30 minutes before serving add corn and stir until well mixed.
I serve mine with sour cream, shredded cheese and onions. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Quita143
Description: 
This is a great chili recipe that can be made in a crock pot for those busy days!
