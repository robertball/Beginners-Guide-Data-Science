Title:
Welsh Rarebit from Dolores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 1" thick slices of French bread
5 1/2 cups grated sharp cheddar cheese
2 eggs
1/3 cup heavy cream
1 Tbsp. dry English mustard
3 teas. Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp. Guinness

Directions:
Toast the breat and set aside.
Combine remaining ingredients in a large food processor and blend together to make a smooth paste.
Spread cheese mixture on the toasted bread and refrigerate for about 1 hour to chill.
(Or place in freezer for 15 minutes.)
Preheat the broiler.  Transfer toast to broiler tray and broil until the cheese is golden brown, watching carefully.
Cut into triangles to serve.
