Title:
Choco Banana Marshmallow Sandwich Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bread
20 grams butter, softened
2 tbsp chocolate cream
1 pc banana
16 pcs marshmallows

Directions:
Preheat the sandwich maker.
Spread butter on one side of each bread.
On the non-buttered side of 2 bread, spread the choco cream.
Slice the banana thinly then arrange it on top of the bread with the choco cream.
Arrange the marshmallows on top of the banana slices then top with another slice of bread with the buttered side up.
Put the sandwich inside the sandwich maker then toast for 5 minutes or until the bread are crispy.
Slice then serve.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
Sweet sandwich toasts which your little ones (and maybe the big ones too) will surely love. This sandwich toast is hot, crispy, and sweet - a great choice for snack.
