Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/421633.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup butter
2 to 2-1/2 Cups Flour
2 Cups brown Sugar
2 Eggs
1 Teaspoon vanilla
1 Teaspoon baking soda
1 12oz package chocolate chips

Directions:
Cream butter and brown sugar together.  Then add the 2 eggs and vanilla; mix again.  Then add the flour, baking soda and mix through. Add flour 1/2 cup at a time until you reach correct cookie consitency. Add the chocolate chip and mix them in.  Then take about 1 tablespoon and place the dough on pan until filled and cook about 10-15 minutes.
