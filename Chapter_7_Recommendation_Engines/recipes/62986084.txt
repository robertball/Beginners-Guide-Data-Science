Title:
Awesome Sausage, Apple and Cranberry Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/4 cups cubed white bread
1 pound Jimmy Deans Sage Sausage
1 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped celery
1 1/2 teaspoons dried rosemary
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
Applesauce with Cinnamon
3/4 cup dried cranberries
1/3 cup minced fresh parsley
1 cooked turkey liver, finely chopped
1 1/2  cup turkey stock
4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
Poultry Seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degree F (175 degree C). Spread the white bread cubes in a single layer on a large baking sheet. Bake for 5 to 7 minutes in the preheated oven, or until evenly toasted. Transfer toasted bread cubes to a large bowl.
In a large skillet, cook the sausage and onions over medium heat, stirring and breaking up the lumps until evenly browned. Add the celery, rosemary, and thyme; cook, stirring, for 2 minutes to blend flavors.  Sprinkle with poultry seasoning
Pour sausage mixture over bread in bowl. Mix in chopped apples, dried cranberries, parsley, and liver. Drizzle with turkey stock and melted butter, and mix lightly.  Spoon into turkey to loosely fill.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
Freshly toasted bread cubes,  sausage, chopped apples and dried cranberry combine to create a tasty fruited stuffing for a 10-pound turkey.Modified from: Awesome Sausage, Apple and Cranberry Stuffing
