Title:
Philly Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 bell pepper
1 onion
13 oz deli roast beef Oscar Mayer - Slow Roasted Roast Beef Cured 96% Fat Free (13 slices)
8 Laughing Cow Light Swiss wedges
1T Johnny's French Dip Concentrated Au Jus Sauce
2T Water
48 wonton wrappers
2C shredded mozzarella cheese - part skim

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 375.
In a heatproof bowl, combine cheese wedges, water and Au Jus sauce. Sit in warm place to rest for now.
Saute mushrooms - put on top of cheese wedges. Stir a little, and allow heat of vegetables to melt cheese to make the sauce.
Chop onion & pepper. Saute together until golden. Add to mixing bowl - again stirring to use the heat to melt the sauce more.
Chop beef. Stir into mixing bowl.
Split mixture into two equal parts.
Push a wonton wrapper into the bottom of each of the cups in the muffin tins (24 of them).
Spoon the roast beef mixture evenly amongst the wonton wrappers.
Press another wonton wrapper on top.
Top with remaining beef mixture
Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly over the top of each cup.
Bake for 18 minutes until golden brown.
Let cool 5 minutes before removing from muffin tin.

Another Main dish Muffin - easy to make ahead for busy night suppers or take for lunch.
