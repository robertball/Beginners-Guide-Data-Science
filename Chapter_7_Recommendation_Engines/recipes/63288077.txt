Title:
Pomegranate Lemonade Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2oz Bacardi 151 rum
splash of Grenadine
Minute Maid lemonade
Rose's Cocktail Infusions Pomegranate Twist Mix
margarita glass
notched lemon wheel

Directions:
Fill glass to the top with ice and pour ice into shaker.
Add rum. Fill to the top of the ice (before ice begins to float) with half pomegranate mixer and half lemonade.
Shake briefly and pour into margarita glass.
Add a splash of grenadine and place lemon wheel on the rim.
Serve with a smile!

Submitter: 
PastaGeek7
Description: 
This delicious combination of lemonade and pomegranate mixer has quite the kick. Great with a pasta dish and some good friends!
