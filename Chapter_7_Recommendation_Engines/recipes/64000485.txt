Title:
Chicken, Ricotta and Spinach Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups part skim ricotta cheese
1/2 cup thawed spinach squeezed dry
1 cup cooked chicken breast - shredded
3/4 cup Parmesan or Asiago cheese
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 cup fresh chopped parsley
1 egg beaten
20 jumbo pasta shells
24 oz. spaghetti sauce
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cook shells for 3 minutes less than instructions, drain and cool.
Pour half of sauce into a 13x9 baking dish.
Mix ricotta, Parmesan, spinach, garlic powder, chicken and half of the parsley.
Season with salt and pepper.
Add egg and stir to combine.
Fill a quart sized plastic bag with the mixture, then cut off one corner to create a piping bag.
Fill each shell with about 2 tbsp. of filling.
Place stuffed shells seam side down in baking dis.
Top shells with remaining sauce and then sprinkle with mozzarella and remaining parsley.
Cover with foil and bake for 20-30 minutes or until shells are bubbling and cheese is melted.
