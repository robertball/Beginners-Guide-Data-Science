Title:
Toasted Walnut Apple Pie Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3272780.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium Gala apple, cored & chopped
1/4 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
1/2 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
3 tbsp Gluten free rolled oats
1 tbsp walnuts
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
pinch of nutmeg & all spice
1 packet stevia sweetener
4 ice cubes

Directions:
Heat a small pan to medium low heat, add walnuts & toast ~5 minutes stirring occasionally
You will know they are don't when you smell them
To a food processor or blender, add all ingredients
Combine well, add smoothie to a glass, top with more walnuts if desired & enjoy

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
With warm flavors of fall and coolness of summer, this Toasted Walnut Apple Pie Smoothie is the perfect autumn dessert drink! Tastes like your favorite pie with half the calories.
