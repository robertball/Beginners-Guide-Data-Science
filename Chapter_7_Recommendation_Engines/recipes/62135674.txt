Title:
Balsamic Flat Iron Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
! Flat Iron steak  Usually packaged in 1-2lbs
1/4 c good balsamic vinegar
3/4 c Extra virgin olive oil
2 cloves garlic minced
1 tsp dijon mustard
Fresh cracked pepper
Fresh ground sea salt

Directions:
In a bowl whisk together the balsamic vinegar, garlic and olive oil.  Add the mustard and salt and pepper. Whisk all together and marinade and steak in a ziplock bag overnight,if possible, but if you only have a few hours, this is still delicious. Grill or broil till med rare.  Let set for 10 min, it will continue to cook and the juices will set up...this is a must. Carve against the grain in thin slices.  Serve with sun dried tomato mashed potatoes and a hearty salad. YUM!
