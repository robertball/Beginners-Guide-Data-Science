Title:
Buttercream Candies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling
1/2 cup Butter. softened
1(3-ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
4 cups powdered sugar
Coating Ingredients
1 (12oz) package(2 cups)real semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 tablespoons vegetable shortening

Directions:
1. Combine butter, and cream cheese in a large bowl.  Beat at medium speed scraping the bowl often, until smooth(about 1-2 mins).  Blend in vanilla, and reduce mixing speed to low, and gradually add the powdered sugar until well mixed(4-5 mins).  If mixture is too soft to mold into balls. cover and refrigerate for 1 hr.
2.  Shape rounded teaspoons of mixture into 1 inch balls.  Place on waxed paper lined baking sheets.  Cover loosesly and refrigerate until firm (2 hrs), or overnight.
3.  Melt Chocolate chips and shortnening in 1 qt saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly until smooth (3-4 minutes).  Using a fork dip buttercreams into chocolate, and let excess chocolate drip off.  Place on waxed paper baking sheet, and refrigerate at least 30 minutes to set chocolate.
You may alos subsititue different flavors for the vanilla.  Substitute 11/2 teaspoons of almond, orange, or rum extract.

Submitter: 
CARAMELDIVA1922
Description: 
I have been making these candies for a couple of years now, and adding variations to give out for valentines.  They are true to the original taste of vanilla buttercreams.  Makes 5 dozen pieces
