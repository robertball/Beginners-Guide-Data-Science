Title:
Strawberry Chocolate Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Strawberries
Whipped cream
Chocolate pudding
Optional toppings:
Sprinkles
Mint leaves
Marshmallows
Crushed peppermint candies
Chocolate/white chocolate chips

Directions:
Wash, pare, and slice strawberries lengthwise. Place a layer on the bottom of a parfait glass, pour in 2 tbsp. pudding, then 2 tbsp. whipped cream. Repeat until glass is filled; fill four other glasses. Add extra toppings or serve just as. Chill until cold.

Submitter: 
The Writing Baker
Description: 
This is super quick and easy to make! It's best with strawberries freshly picked. You can put the trifle into a single dish, too. I didn't measure, as usual, so you will need an undefined amount of ingredients.
