Title:
Honey BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Xanthan Gum______¼ teaspoon
Salt, Kosher, Coarse______1 ¼ teaspoons
Black Pepper_______¼ teaspoon
Dark Brown Sugar___¼ Cup
Tomato Paste_______¼ Cup+2 ½ Tablespoons
Water______________¼ Cup
White Vinegar, Distilled____1/4 Cup+2 ½ Tablespoons
Worshershire Sauce___1/2 Tablespoon
Honey, Pure Clover___1/4 Cup+2 Tablespoons

Directions:
Gather all ingredients and equipment.
Blend the Xanthan Gum, salt, black pepper, and Brown sugar together.
Add the tomato paste to a sauce pan and thoroughly mix it with the aforementioned ingredients.
Add the remaining ingredients and bring the sauce to a boil, then reduce to a simmer; continue to simmer for an additional 5 minutes while stirring the sauce gradually.
