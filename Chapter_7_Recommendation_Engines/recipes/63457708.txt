Title:
Ghettalian Garlic Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Texas Toast
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 cup colby jack cheese
1/2 cup garlic, minced
1/2 tsp tabasco sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix mayo, butter, garlic, tabasco sauce and cheese together in a large bowl.
Spread on top of Texas toast.   It should be almost an inch high on top of the toast.
Bake until cheese is melted into the toast and slightly browned on top.
