Title:
Lime-Flavored Beer Battered Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/703130.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of flour
2 tablespoons of onion powder
1/2 tablespoon of garlic salt
1 tablespoon of fresh parsley
2 cups of lime flavored lite beer
4 large chicken breasts
salt and pepper

Directions:
Soak chicken in 1 cup of beer for 30 mins.
In large bowl, mix flour, onion powder, garlic salt, and fresh parsley.  Whisk in cup of beer and let sit for 30 mins.
Heat large skillet with 2 inches of vegetable oil.
Cover chicken and pound chicken to 1 inch thick.  Salt and pepper both sides.
Dip chicken in batter and let fry until a deep golden brown.  Use meat thermometer to ensure chicken is thoroughly cooked at 165 degrees.
Enjoy!
