Title:
Cherry Double-Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 c. unsifted all-purpose flour
1/2 c. unsweetened cocoa powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 c. butter or margarine (softened)
1/4 c. granulated sugar
3/4 c. packed light brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 (4-oz.) package instant pudding, chocolate flavor
2 large eggs
1 (12-oz.) package chocolate chips
1 c. dried cherries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°.
Reconstitute the dried cherries by placing into a bowl and covering with boiling water.
Let sit for five minutes, drain cherries well and chop into small bits.
Sift flour and cocoa with baking soda and salt into a bowl and set aside.
In a large bowl, combine the butter and sugars, beat until smooth and creamy.
Beat in the eggs, vanilla and pudding mix; let the batter rest for 5 minutes, then beat again.
Gradually add the flour/cocoa mixture.
Stir in the chocolate chips and cherries.
Scoop into balls using a small or large ice cream scoop and place onto an ungreased cookie sheet two inches apart.
Flatten the cookies slightly on top.
Bake for 9-10 minutes for small cookies, 13-14 minutes for large cookies.
Let cool completely on a rack.
