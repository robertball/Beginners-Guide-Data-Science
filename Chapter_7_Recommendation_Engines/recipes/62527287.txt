Title:
Golden Lamb Celery Seed Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ C Sugar
1/3 C White Vinegar
1 C Salad Oil
1 1/2 tsp Dry Mustard
1 tsp Salt
½ tsp Grated Onion
1 TBLS Celery Seed

Directions:
Put all dry ingredients in blender. Add onion. Add small amount of oil and mix well. Add vinegar and oil alternately, ending with oil. Mixture should be "creamy".
Add Celery Seed AFTER blending is finished.
Pour into bottle.  Refrigerate.
Make 4-5 days in advance for fullest flavor.
