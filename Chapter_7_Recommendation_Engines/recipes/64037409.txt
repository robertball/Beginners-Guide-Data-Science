Title:
Lemony New Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lemons
2-1/2 lbs new potatoes
6 cloves of garlic - halved
Salt 3 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp fresh parsley
Salt
Fresh ground pepper

Directions:
1. Finely grate zest from 3 lemons into a bowl; press juice from all 4 lemons into a separate bowl
2. Put potatoes in a pot with lemon juice, garlic and salt. Cover with water by one inch.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium low. Cook until just tender. Drain, discard garlic.
3. Gently toss with olive oil, grated lemon zest and parsley.  Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Diana B. Jeck
Description: 
Excellent with roast lamb or beef.
