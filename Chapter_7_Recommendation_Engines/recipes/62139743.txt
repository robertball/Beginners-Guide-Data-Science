Title:
Eggs Newport Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 - 12 Hard boiled eggs, peeled and chopped
1/2 Cup milk
1/2 Cup mayonnaise
1 Can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 lb. Bacon, cooked and crumbled
2 T Chives, chopped
English Muffins, (1 package)
Butter
Paprika or cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
Mix milk, mayonnaise and soup in a large bowl until smooth; stir in chopped eggs.  Pour in a baking dish. Top with crumbled bacon and chives.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes or until hot and bubbling.
Spoon on top of toasted and lightly buttered English muffins.  For a nicer presentation, keep the bacon and chives upright.  Sprinkle lightly with paprika or cayenne pepper for desired color and taste.

Submitter: 
Traci-in-Cali
Description: 
Delicious creamed eggs with bacon and chives spooned on top of toasted English muffins.  This is also a great recipe to use up all those left over Easter eggs!
