Title:
Classic Escargots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/929121.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 can of escargots (24 in one can)
1 stick of unsalted butter
1 shallot, minced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons parsley
salt and pepper to season

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees (f.)
Drain and rinse escargots.
Place butter, shallot, garlic and parsley in food processor. Season with salt and pepper. Pulse until blended.
Put 1/2 teaspoon of butter mixture in each well of an escargot dish.
Put one escargot in each well and top with more butter mixture.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes and serve with French bread.

Submitter: 
Cynthia Ross
Description: 
A French Chef shared this simple recipe with me. It's very easy and so elegant.
