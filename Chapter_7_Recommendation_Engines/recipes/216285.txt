Title:
Baileys Minty Mistletoe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992210.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces Baileys® Original Irish Cream
1/4 ounce Rumple Minze® Peppermint Schnapps

Directions:
Add Baileys Original Irish Cream and Rumple Minze Peppermint Schnapps.
Shake with ice and strain into martini glass.

Submitter: 
Baileys®
Description: 
Baileys® Original Irish Cream and minty Rumple Minze® Peppermint Schnapps add a festive note to any holiday celebration.
