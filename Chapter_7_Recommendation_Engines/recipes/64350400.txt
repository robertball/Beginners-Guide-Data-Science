Title:
Autumn Spice Cake-Gluten Free/Vegan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Gluten Free/Rice Flour Pastry Flour_2 Cups
Egg Replacer, Dry, Gluten Free______2 Teaspoons
Baking Powder_______________________2 Teaspoons
Baking Soda_________________________1 Teaspoon
Cinnamon_________________________¾ Teaspoon
Organic Brown Sugar_________________¾ Cups
Soy Milk, Unflavored________________1 ¼ Cup
Pumpkin Puree_______________________2 Tablespoons
Pumpkin Seed Oil, Toasted___________3 Tablespoons
Water, Warm_________________________¾ Cup
Vanilla Extract, Pure_______________1 Teaspoon
Vegetable Oil_______________________As Needed
Gluten Free A.P. Flour______________As Needed

Directions:
Gather all ingredients and equipment.
Preheat the oven to 350oF.
Blend the flour, egg replacer, baking powder, and baking soda together in a large stainless steel bowl; sift the flour blend into a mechanical mixing bowl and mount the bowl onto the mixer.
Equip the paddle attachment to the stand mixer and add the organic brown sugar; mix the soy milk, pumpkin puree, pumpkin seed oil, water, and vanilla extract together separately.
Start the mixer on medium low speed, allowing the brown sugar to incorporate into the flour; slowly pour in the liquid then increase the speed as needed. Blend until all of the ingredients are fully combined into a smooth batter.
Grease a 9x13” loaf pan with Oil then dust the walls with the extra gluten free/rice free flour.
Pour the cake batter into the pan making sure to fully scrape the bowl clean; level the surface of the cake batter, then transfer to the oven.
Bake for 45 min to 1 Hour or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Remove the cake from the oven and allow it to rest for 10 minutes away from the heat.
turn the pan upside-down onto a clean surface to remove; Frost as desired.

The Culinist
Description: 
A gluten free alternative to your conventional spice cake!
