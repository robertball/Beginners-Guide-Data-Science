Title:
Gluten Free Vegetarian Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/809691.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Tbs olive oil
1 medium onion diced
5 oz. chopped mushrooms
3 cloves garlic minced
1 medium eggplant diced
1 small yellow squash diced
1 small zucchini diced
handful of fresh spinach leaves chopped
1 tsp salt
2 jars of pasta sauce
2 boxes of no boil gluten free rice lasagna noodles
8 oz ricotta cheese
1 lb shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Heat olive oil to medium high.
Add onions and mushrooms, cook 5 minutes.
Next add garlic, eggplant, yellow squash, zucchini, spinach, & salt. Cook 10 min.
Pour both jars of pasta sauce into veggie mix.
Line a large baking dish (13x9 approx) with noodles. Top with 1/3 of the veggie mix, 1/3 ricotta cheese, 1/3 mozzarella.
Layer noodles, ricotta, & mozzarella 2 more times.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
Let rest 10-15 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
meyer
Description: 
A gluten free vegetarian alternative to traditional lasagna. This no boil version cuts down on preparation time. Easy to prepare and so yummy! ENJOY!
