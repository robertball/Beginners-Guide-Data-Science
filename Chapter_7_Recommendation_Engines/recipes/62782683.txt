Title:
Strawberries and Cream Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups coarsely crushed pretzels
1 and 1 quarter stick melted butter or margarine
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
8 oz. whipped topping
2 cups pineapple juice
1 6oz. box strawberry jello
1 pint frozen strawberries

Directions:
Mix pretzels and butter.  Press into 13x9 pan and bake at 400 degrees for 10 minutes.  Cool completely.
Mix cream cheese and sugar thoroughly.  Fold in whipped topping.  Spread over pretzel mixture.
Bring pineapple juice to a boil.  Remove from heat and stir in jello.  When jello is dissolved, immediately add strawberries and stir.  When soupy, carefully pour over pretzel and cream cheese layers. Refrigerate at least 4 hours or until set.
