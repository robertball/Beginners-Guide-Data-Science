Title:
Curried Tofu Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkt of extra firm tofu, drained and cubed in 3/4" cubes
1 cucumber, peeled and finely chopped
1 cup, peeled and shredded carrot
1 clove garlic, grated
1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
1/2 cup white raisins (optional)
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup, seeded and finely diced red pepper
3 tablespoons honey mustard
1/2 teaspoon sugar
2 teaspoons curry powder

Directions:
Combine ingredients and allow to sit for 20 minutes before refrigerating.
May be made a day ahead.
Allow to sit 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Liz and Sylvan
Description: 
This is loved by vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike.  If you're eating vegan, substitute vegan mayonnaise for regular mayo.
