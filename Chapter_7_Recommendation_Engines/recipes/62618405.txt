Title:
Chicken and Noodle, similar to Bob Evans' Restaurant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Water to cover chicken
carrots, chopped
celery, chopped
onion, chopped (optioinal)
3 lg cubes chicken bouillon (or soup base)
3/4 cup butter, melted
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1.3 lb cooked boneless skinless chicken thighs, torn
into large pieces
(16 ounce) bag Reame's frozen noodles

Directions:
# In a large stock pot, mix water, carrots, celery, onions, chicken bouillon, melted butter, pepper and chicken pieces.
# 2 Bring to a boil, turn to low heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
# 3 Add noodles and simmer on medium-low heat for an additional 10 minutes.
