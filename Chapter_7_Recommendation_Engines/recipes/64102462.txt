Title:
Healthy Salmon Salad Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1235451.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can pink salmon, drained; 1/2 cup greek yogurt or mayonnaise; 1 tablespoon of dijon mustard; 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice; paprika to taste; 1/2 teaspoon celery seed or 1/4 chopped celery; 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper; 1/8 teaspoon salt; 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder; 1/2 grilled onion; avocado; baby spinach; gluten free bread, toasted

Directions:
In medium bowl combine and mix salmon, greek yogurt (or mayo), dijon mustard, lemon juice, paprika, celery seed, black pepper, salt, and garlic powder. Toast bread and layer with salmon salad mixture. Top with baby spinach, grilled onions, and avocado. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Dee Sweets
Description: 
Tuna salad sandwich with a healthy twist! Substitute to your liking to make it even more healthy and flavorful.
