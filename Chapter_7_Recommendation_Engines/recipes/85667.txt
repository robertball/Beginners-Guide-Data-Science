Title:
Homemade Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984804.jpg
Ingredients:
3 avocados, peeled and diced
1/2 tomato, finely diced
1/2 onion, finely diced
1/2 habanero or jalapeno pepper, finely diced
1 clove garlic, minced
Dash of lime juice
Salt to taste

Directions:
In a bowl, mush avocados until slightly chunky.
Add tomato, onion, peppers, garlic, lime and salt. Mix together.
Serve immediately with tortilla chips, vegetables or pita bread.
