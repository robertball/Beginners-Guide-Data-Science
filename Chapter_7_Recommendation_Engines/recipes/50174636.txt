Title:
Cabbage Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 pound smoked sausage, cut into 1/4 inch slices
1 onion, chopped
3 stalks celery
3 cloves chopped garlic
1 medium head cabbage, chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
1 (14.5 ounce) can Rotel tomatoes
1 can El Pato spicy tomato sauce (1/2 can for less spicy)
1 can (14.5 ounce) beef broth
1 cup beef broth (if needed)
1 cup uncooked rice
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
1/4 tsp. Old Bay Seasoning
Pinch of red pepper flakes
Dash of Worcestershire sauce
Salt to taste

Directions:
In a large stock pot over medium high heat, combine ground beef, smoked sausage, onion, celery and garlic. Cook until beef is evenly brown. Stir in cabbage, tomatoes, broth, tomato sauce, seasonings and rice. Season with garlic salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and cook for 35 to 40 minutes, or until rice is done.
