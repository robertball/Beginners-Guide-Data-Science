Title:
Homemade Gluten Free Buckwheat Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4048933.jpg
Ingredients:
20 g buckwheat  flour
100g gluten free pasta flour
1 egg
1 egg white

Directions:
Mix flours. Make a well add egg and yolk. Mix with hands. Roll out until about 1/4 inch thick. Let rest to dry a little. Boil without stirring too much or add to fagioli/zuppa. About 2-4 minutes depending on size.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Gluten free egg tagliatelle made with buckwheat flour. In Italian, this pasta is called Pizzoccheri. Hailing from Northern Italy, these short noodles are served best with a cream based sauce and/or cabbage pr added to stews or soups. This recipe makes enough for two servings.
