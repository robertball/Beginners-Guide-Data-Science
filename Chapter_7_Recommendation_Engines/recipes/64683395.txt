Title:
Grilled Marinated Skirt Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c olive oil
1/2 c lime/lemon juice (used 3 limes and 1 lemon)
1/2 c coconut aminos
1 T fish sauce (or could sub soy/tamari for coconut aminos and fish sauce)
1 T tamari
2 T worcestershire
2 T red wine vinegar
2 T apple cider vinegar
few drops of hot sauce
1/2 to 1 tsp pepper (or whole pepper corns)
5-7 garlic cloves minced
2 lbs skirt steak
1 tsp or more salt

Directions:
Blend all marinade ingredients with immersion blender
Salt steak on both sides
Pour over steak in glass baking pan.
Cover with plastic wrap to marinate for several hours in fridge
When ready to grill, remove steak from marinade, salt if desired
Grill 3-5 minutes each side until slightly charred and to desired doneness

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
Easy delicious skirt steak marinade; also good on tri tip, chicken.  Makes enough for 2lbs steak.
