Title:
Zesty Pasta with Peppers and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb (4 servings) uncooked thin spaghetti (or any other pasta)
2-3 T. olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 sweet vidalia onion, cut in 6 sections
1 green pepper, thinly sliced
8 oz. mushrooms, chopped
1/4 t. oregano
1/8 t. red pepper flakes (or more to taste)
1/2 c. chicken broth
salt and pepper
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Put pasta in boiling water and cook according to directions.
While pasta is cooking, heat oil in large skillet.
Add garlic, onion, peppers and mushrooms and saute over medium-high heat for 7 minutes or until lightly browned.
Add chicken broth, red pepper flakes, oregano, salt and pepper and simmer over medium heat for around 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
Add cooked pasta to pan and mix well.
Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and serve hot.

Submitter: 
Jen Cardenas
Description: 
This is a very quick, easy, and pretty healthy pasta dish.  It can be easily made vegetarian by using vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.
