Title:
Mom's Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-6 Hard Cooked Eggs
2-6 lbs Potatoes
2-6 Tablespoons Mayonnaise
1 (or so) Teaspoons Prepared Mustard
Dilled Pickles, chopped (to taste)
Black Olives, sliced (to taste)
Girard's Original Salad Dressing

Directions:
Hard-cook eggs (see notes below)
Meanwhile:  Peel potatoes; halve lenthwise.  Pressure cook or cover with cold water and cook until barely tender.
Meanwhile:  Shell eggs and mince (I use pastry blender) in a large bowl.
Add:  Mayonnaise, Mustard, Pickles, Olives and Salt (to taste)
Slowly add 1/4-3/4 bottle of Salad Dressing, blending well.
Drain boiled potatoes; cut them into small pieces while hot.  Add them to the dressing.  Mix well.  Place in frig and chill well, stirring occasionally.
Add more mayonnaise (and salt) to taste.

Submitter:

Description: 
I just love - love - love my mom's potato salad.  The recipe card she bequeathed to me is torn and faded, and I realized it was time to put it all online.
