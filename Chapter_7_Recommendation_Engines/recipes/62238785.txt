Title:
Chocolate Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/4 tsp salt
2/3 cup flour
1/2 cup cocoa
4 eggs
5 cups milk
1 stick margarine
3 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients. Add eggs and mix well. Add milk. Mix well. Use medium heat and a heavy saucepan. Stir constantly. Cook until thick. Add vanilla and margarine. Cool and refrigerate.
Goes great with Cool Whip and crushed Oreos.
