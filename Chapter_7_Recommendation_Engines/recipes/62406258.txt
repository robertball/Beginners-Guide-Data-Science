Title:
Dana's Cream Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz packages softened cream cheese
1 T balsamic vinegar
1/3 cup toasted pine nuts
1 cup basil
2 - 3 garlic cloves

Directions:
Mix together cream cheese and vinegar.
In a food processor, coarsely chop nuts, basil and garlic.
Combine all ingredients.
Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
wendy
Description: 
Easy spread you can make ahead of time - great flavor!
