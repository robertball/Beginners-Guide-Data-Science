Title:
Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1-1/2 cups buttermilk
4-1/2 Tbsp oil
3/4 cup whole wheat flour
3/4 cup white flour
1-1/2 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp soda
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Heat waffle iron.  Beat eggs; then add buttermilk and oil and mix well.  Add remaining ingredients and stir until smooth.
Pour batter onto waffle iron and bake according to directions (about 4-5 minutes).

Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Whole wheat flour adds a nice flavor.
