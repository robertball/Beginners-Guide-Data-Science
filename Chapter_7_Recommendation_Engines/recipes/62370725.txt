Title:
Baked Cream Cheese and Onion dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup mayonnaise
5 green onions OR
2 ounces canned diced jalapeno peppers, drained
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Stir together cream cheese and mayonnaise in a large bowl until smooth.
Stir in green chiles and jalapeno peppers / or green onions.
Hollow out the center of a round loaf of artisan bread and fill with cheesy mixture.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
or
Pour mixture into a microwave safe serving dish, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Microwave on High until hot, about 3 minutes.
