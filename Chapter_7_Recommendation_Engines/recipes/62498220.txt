Title:
Sausage Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123081.jpg
Ingredients:
1# sausage (bulk)
1 t. dry mustard
8 eggs
2 c. milk
1 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
8 slices bread, cubed
1 c. grated cheese

Directions:
Brown sausage & mustard.
Drain.
Beat eggs.
Add milk.
Add all other ingredients.
Put in 9x13 dish.
Refrigerate overnight.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
