Title:
Kiss the Cook ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 slab Baby-back ribs.
2-3 cups of rib rub.
2 bottles of your favorite BBQ sauce.
2 teaspoons of chili pepper (optional-- if you like spice!)
2 teaspoons of cayenne pepper (optiona-- if you like spice!)
1 yellow onion
1 green pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250-300 degrees.
Put slab of ribs in large baking pan (a disposable one works great).
Rub ribs with rib rub...using more or less rub to suit you.
Cut up green pepper and onion into long chunks.
Add BBQ sauce to top of ribs, then top with chopped onion and green pepper.
Cover with foil, stick in oven for 5-6 hours, basting every 45 minutes or so.
Voila!
