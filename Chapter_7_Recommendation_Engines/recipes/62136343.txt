Title:
Apple Craisin Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 granny smith apples, diced.
1/2 c. craisins
1/2 t. cinnamon
3/4 t. curry powder
1 c. water
1 chicken bullion cube
1 c. cranberry juice (or apple)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large saucepan.  Simmer over medium-low heat for 20-25 minutes or until liquid reduces by two-thirds, stirring occasionally.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Jess & Ral
Description: 
A chunky, warm apple and craisin sauce to serve over pork or chicken.
