Title:
Stove Top Easy Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Stove Top Stuffing (6 oz)
1 1/2 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
1 can (10.75 oz) condensed cream of chicken soup
1/3 cup sour cream
1 pkg (16 oz) frozen mixed vegetables, thawed and drained

Directions:
Heat oven in 400F. Make stuffing mix. Mix chicken soup, sour cream and vegetables in a 13x9" baking dish; top with stuffing. Bake for 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
