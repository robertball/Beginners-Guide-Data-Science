Title:
Collards and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 chopped and destemmed fresh collard leaves
1 cup Rice
2.5 cups water
1 tsp salt
1 sauce package from chicken flavored Ramen noodles

Directions:
Put everything in pot with tight fitting lid in order listed.  Stir a little.  Cook about 20 minutes or until rice is done.
