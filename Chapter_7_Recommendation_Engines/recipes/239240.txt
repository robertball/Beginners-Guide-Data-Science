Title:
Creamy Green Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1936806.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cold water
1 cup baby spinach
1/2 cup fresh parsley
1/2 avocado, peeled and pitted
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon safflower oil

Directions:
Blend water, spinach, parsley, avocado, lemon juice, and safflower oil together in a blender until smooth.
