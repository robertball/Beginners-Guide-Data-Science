Title:
SPICY GROUND BEEF AND RICE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 Lb. extra lean ground beef
1 C. chopped sweet onion
1 C. uncooked Instant Brown Rice
1 3/4 c. water
1 packet Herb OX sodium free Beef Bouillon
2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. pepper flakes
2 T. Cajun Sunshine Pepper Sauce(our preference)
1 can diced fire roasted tomatoes with juice
1/2 bag baby spinach, chopped or torn
salt to taste

Directions:
Spray large skillet with cooking spray. Over medium high heat cook meat and onions until meat is no longer pink. Drain meat and add rice and stir into meat onion mixture then add rest of ingredients to the pan. Stir until well mixed.
Simmer for 25 minutes or until liquids are absorbed by the rice.

Submitter: 
Mary Stephenson
Description: 
My husband says it's like his Mom's Spanish Rice if she had added spices. It's quick, fairly cheap and filling. Plus you can make it for your spicy/heat comfort zone.
