Title:
Cranberry Almond Snack Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3073491.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup dried cranberries
3/4 cup pitted dates
1 cup sliced almonds
1/2 cup almond meal
1/4 cup almond butter
1 teaspoon almond extract

Directions:
Place cranberries and dates in a food processor; pulse until a paste forms. Add almonds, almond meal, almond butter, and almond extract and pulse just until mixture comes together.
Wet hands with water and shape mixture into 1-inch balls.

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
A sweet no-bake snack treat made with simple ingredients and in just minutes. A healthy lunch box snack! Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
