Title:
Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Dungeness crab meat
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon butter
3 sliced green onions
2 cloves minced garlic
3 tablespoon cream
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 large egg
Cayenne pepper (to taste)
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a sauce pan, sautee onion and garlic, in butter and olive oil.
Combine in medium bowl:
Cream, Dijon mustard, egg, Cayenne pepper (to taste) bread crumbs and grated parmesan cheese. Gently fold in the crabmeat, keeping large pieces together, do not break up.  Form into patties, roll in bread crumbs.  Fry in olive oil over medium heat until golden brown
Serve with a dill mustard sauce or ranch dressing.
