Title:
Potato Flake Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup potato flakes ( any flavour )
3 Tbsp grated parmesan
10 full chicken wings
Melted butter for dipping wings in

Directions:
Mix together potato flakes and parmesan
Dip chicken wings in melted butter then in potato cheese and put on greased pan
Bake 40 mins (turning 1/2 way through time)
