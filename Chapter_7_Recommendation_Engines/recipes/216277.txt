Title:
All-American Summer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988653.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) container BREAKSTONE'S Sour Cream
1 envelope GOOD SEASONS Parmesan Italian or Italian Dressing Mix

Directions:
Mix ingredients until well blended; cover.
Refrigerate several hours to blend flavors.
Serve with red pepper strips, jicama strips, radishes, cherry tomatoes and blue corn chips.

Submitter: 
BREAKSTONE'S
Description: 
Chilling blends the flavors of this easy dip, but it takes just minutes to prepare. Mix BREAKSTONE'S Sour Cream with zesty Parmesan Italian dressing mix, chill, and serve with raw veggies and blue corn chips.
