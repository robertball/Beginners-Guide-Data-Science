Title:
POTATO SALAD PAR EXCELLENCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 potatoes, peeled (about 4 cups)
1 tsp. salt
1/2 cup green onions, chopped
1 cup of celery, sliced
2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
Salt & pepper to taste
Paprika for garnish
Optional ingredients:
1/4 cup green or red pepper, diced
1/2 cup cucumber, sliced
1/4 cup radishes, thinly sliced
1 medium tomato, cut into wedges
Dressing:
1 can of Eagle brand milk
1/2 cup of oil
Â½ cup white vinegar
1 tsp of salt
1 egg
2 1/2 tsp. dry mustard
Dash of cayenne

Directions:
Cook whole potatoes for about 20 minutes, Drain and cool. Cut potatoes into cubes or slices. Add green onions, celery and eggs, tossing lightly. Add any optional ingredients. Cover and refrigerate.
Just before serving, spoon dressing over salad and toss gently. Garnish with paprika. Serve immediately.
