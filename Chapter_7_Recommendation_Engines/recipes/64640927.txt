Title:
texas trash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs butter
1 cup bacon drippings
garlic powder (to taste)
lowry's season salt (to taste)
small bottle of tabasco (to taste)
worchester sauce (to taste)
2 boxes of rice chex
1 box corn chex
1 bag pretzels
2 cans mixed nuts
2 lbs pecans

Directions:
mix first 6 ingredients & simmer for 10-15 minutes
mix all of dry ingredients in largest cake pan. large enough to stir ingredients with out spilling
drizzle the liquid on top and stir together well.
bake at 250 for 45 minutes, stir every 15 minutes.
