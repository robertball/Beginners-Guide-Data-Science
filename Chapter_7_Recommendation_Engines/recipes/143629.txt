Title:
Easy Champagne Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1911077.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (10 ounce) package frozen strawberries, thawed
3 bananas, diced
3/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream together the cream cheese and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener.  Fold in the pineapple, whipped topping and strawberries and mix well.  Stir in bananas and walnuts.
Freeze for 4 to 6 hours or overnight.  Remove from freezer 1 to 2 hours before serving.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Like a frozen fruit mousse, this elegant dessert is a nice substitute for ice cream.  It will keep, frozen, for several weeks.
