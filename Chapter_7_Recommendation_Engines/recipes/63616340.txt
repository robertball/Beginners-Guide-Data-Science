Title:
South of the Border Shrimp Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 roma tomatoes, diced
1 white onion, finely diced
1 jalapeno, deseeded and finely diced
1 hothouse cucumber, peeled, seeded and diced
1 bunch cilantro, chopped
4 stalks celery, diced
Pinch of kosher salt
1 lime, juiced
1 (12 oz) jar Herdez Red Guajillo Chile Cooking Sauce Â®
1 quart (32 oz) bottle tomato cocktail with clam juice such as Clamato
2 pounds cooked shrimp, cleaned and tails removed
2 avocados, diced

Directions:
In a large bowl combine the diced tomatoes, onion, jalapeno, cucumber, cilantro, celery, pinch of salt and lime juice.  Toss to combine.
Add the Herdez Red Guajillo Chile Cooking Sauce (r), tomato cocktail with clam juice and shrimp.  Stir to combine and gently fold in the diced avocados.
Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
This sassy recipe takes your taste buds south of the border with authentic, tangy and fruity fresh flavors.  Mild, yet flavorful, this easy recipe tastes like a bite of summer.  Serve as an appetizer or with warm tortillas for a cool summertime meal.
