Title:
- Cheesy Burger Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of lean ground beef- make sure to use lean so you don't have to worry about draining the grease.
2Tbsp worcestershire sauce
2Tbsp mustard
1/2 cup ketchup
1 cup dry macaroni noodles
2 cups water
6 slices of american cheese

Directions:
In a pan with deep sides brown ground beef on medium heat.(Make sure to use either lean meat or ground turkey or chicken to prevent having to drain the fat)
After meat if fully cooked add worcestershire sauce, mustard and ketchup.  Mix well.  Add macaroni noodles and 2 cups of water, cover and let simmer for 12 mins or until the noodles are tender.  After the noodles are cooked turn heat off, unwrap cheese slices and place on top of noodles. Cover for about 3 mins until the cheese is melted, stir and serve.

Cheesy, creamy one dish meal that kids can make for the whole family.
