Title:
Chicketti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound uncooked spaghetti
3/4 pound cooked chicken, cut up
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup green bell pepper, chopped
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/2 cup chicken stock

Directions:
Cook pasta, according to the package instructions; drain. Add all ingredients, except 1/2 cup cheese, in baking dish. Top with remaining cheese and bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
