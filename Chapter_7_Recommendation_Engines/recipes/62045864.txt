Title:
Butter pecan crisps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup confectioners sugar
2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup all purpose flour
1 1/2 cups chopped pecans
confectioners sugar for rolling

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
In a large bowl cream the butter,sugar, and vanilla until smooth.  Add the flour until combined.  Stir in the pecans.  Roll dough into 1 inch balls and place on ungreased cookie sheet, leaving plenty of room between them for spreading.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until lightly browned.
Cool cookies on a wire rack and then dip in confectioners sugar.

Submitter: 
cookin'mama
Description: 
This recipe was born of a doubling mix up.  Everyone enjoys these more than the cookie I was attempting to make.  I had to try to recreate them to fulfill requests for the recipe.  These can be stored in an airtight container or frozen.
