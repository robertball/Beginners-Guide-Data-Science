Title:
Red, White & Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups coarsely chopped fresh tomatoes
1 cup cubed (1/2") mozzarella cheese (approx. 4 oz)
2 cups thinly sliced romaine lettuce
Dressing:
2 TBL olive oil
1 TBL white wine vinegar
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp sweet basil
1/2 tsp crushed marjoram

Directions:
In large bowl, combine dressing ingredients; whisk until blended. Add tomatoes and cheese; toss to coat. Cover and marinate in refrigerator 30 minutes.
To service, add lettuce to bowl; toos lightly.
