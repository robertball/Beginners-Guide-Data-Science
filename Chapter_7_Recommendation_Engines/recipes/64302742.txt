Title:
Crispy Dijon Mustard Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crispy Dijon Mustard Chicken
Sunflower Oil - for frying
4 Chicken Escalopes - tenderised to ¼in thick
Plain Flour - seasoned with Salt and Pepper - to lightly dust the escalopes
Dijon Mustard - to coat the escalopes
1 Egg - lightly beaten in a bowl large enough in which to dunk the escalopes
100g/3½oz or so Toasted Breadcrumbs on a flat plate - to coat the escalopes

Directions:
Place the escalopes on a flat dish and lightly dust them on both sides with the seasoned flour;
Coat them all over with the mustard, ensuring well covered;
Dunk them in the egg mixture then coat them in the breadcrumbs, again ensuring they are well covered on all sides;
Shake to remove any excess crumbs;
Pour sufficient oil to fry the escalopes into a large frying pan;
On a medium high heat, add the escalopes and fry for 1 to 2 minutes on each side until lightly golden;
Serve.
