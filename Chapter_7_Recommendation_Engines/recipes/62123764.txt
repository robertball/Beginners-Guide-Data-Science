Title:
Spinach, Mushroom, and Blue Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients for the salad
1 bag spinach
1 container slides mushrooms
1 container crumbled blue cheese
Ingredients for dressing (makes for multiple salads)
1 tsp savory
1 tsp red pepper flakes
¼ c cider vinegar
1/8 c water
½ c extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Add salad ingredients to a large bowl
In a separate container, mix the dressing ingredients together
Toss salad with the desired amount of dressing

Submitter: 
tristin
Description: 
Spinach, mushrooms, and blue cheese are tossed with a light flavorful cider vinegar and olive oil dressing.
