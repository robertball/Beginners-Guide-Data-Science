Title:
Mini Pumpkin Cheesecakes (No-Bake) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
12 McVites Digestives Cookies (crushed)
5 tsp. Unsalted Butter (melted)
Filling:
8oz (1 cup) Mascarpone
7oz (1/2 can) Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/3 cup White Sugar
1/2 can Pumpkin Puree (stirred smooth prior to mixing in)
1/2 tsp. Cinnamon
1/4 tsp. Nutmeg
1/4 tsp. Cloves
Whole Pecans for decoration (optional)

Directions:
For the Crust:
Pour the melted butter over the crumbs and mix until combined (Mixture should be able to be pressed down firmly)
For the Filling:
Combine the Mascarpone, Condensed Milk, Sugar, Pumpkin, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, and Cloves in a large bowl.
Beat with an electric mixer until smooth and airy
Take two mini-muffin pans (24 count each) and line with muffin cups
Press 1 tsp. of the crust into the bottom of each well
Spoon 1 tsp. of filling over each crust and smooth the tops
Place a pecan on top of each (optional)
Clill in the refrigerator for 2 hours
Enjoy!

Description: 
This is a recipe I created from several other recipes online... and some imagination! Great for around the holidays, and for taking to parties and get togethers! It has a nice pumpkin flavor, and it's nice and airy too! Not too rich :)
