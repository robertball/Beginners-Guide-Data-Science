Title:
Peggy's tuna noodle casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz carton mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
1/2 onion, chopped finely
2 tsp butter
1 tsp oil
2 tbsp flour
12 oz can fat free evaporated milk
Salt and pepper
Smoked paprika, sprinkle
3 oz Parmesan cheese, grated
1/2 cup frozen peas
Can of tuna, drained
4 oz soba noodles, or any other noodles, cooked and drained
10 ritz crackers, or equivilant amount bread crumbs or panko
2 oz cheese, Colby, cheddar or co-jack

Directions:
Saute mushrooms and onions in the butter and oil in a skillet
Shake the flour and some of the milk in a jar until well- blended.  Add the milk/flour mixture to the mushrooms and onions and stir and cook, until slightly thickened, then slowly add and stir the rest of the milk.  Add seasonings.  Simmer on very low.
Meanwhile, cook and drain noodles.  Butter a casserole dish.
Mix together the mushroom mixture, the tuna and the peas.  Gently add to the drained noodles and mix.
Spread in the casserole.  Bake at 350 about 20 minutes.
Then top with grated cheese and cracker or bread crumbs, mixed together.
Bake about ten minutes more until cheese is melted and starting to brown and casserole is bubbly hot.
