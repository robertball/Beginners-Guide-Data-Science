Title:
Chicken Scaloppine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon, cooked (I use ready cooked bacon bits)
4 oz. pasta, cooked
2 oz. lemon juice
1 oz. white wine or water
2 oz. cream
1/4 lb. butter
salt and pepper
2 Chicken breasts
1//4 cup flour, seasoned
6 oz. mushrooms, sliced
6 oz. artichoke heart, sliced
1 tsp. capers

Directions:
Cook bacon until crisp.
While pasta is cooking make the sauce; Heat lemon juice and white wine in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a boil and reduce by 1/3. Add cream and simmer until mixture thickens (3 to 4 minutes_). Slowly add butter until completely incorporated. Season with salt ad pepper. Remove from hat and keep warm.
Cook pasta and drain.
Heat a small amount of oil and 2 TBSP of butter in a large skillet. Dredge chicken in four and saute in pan, turning one, until brown and cooked through. Remove chicken from pan and add to pan remaining ingredients. Heat until mushrooms soften and are cooked; add chicken back to pan.
Add sauce to chicken mixture and serve over pasta, garnish with parsley.
