Title:
Cream of Seven Herb Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
3/4 cup chopped shallot
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 cups chicken stock
1/2 cup half and half
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/2 pound fresh spinach, roughly chopped
1/4 cup EACH chopped fresh flat leaf parsley, chervil, tarragon, sorrel, loveage, thyme and marjoram
2/3 cup sour cream
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 teaspoon grey salt

Directions:
Heat olive oil over medium heat in a large saucepan. Add shallot and cook for 2-3 minutes until shallot is tender but not browned. Add garlic and continue cooking 5 minutes. Add stock, bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer for about 5 minutes. Add half & half, heavy cream and spinach. Heat thoroughly until spinach is wilted taking care not to let the mixture come to a boil. Add all the herbs and let sit on the heat for 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat and cool slightly. Puree mixture in a blender. Return to saucepan and add sour cream, salt and pepper. Chill thoroughly.

Description: 
A good way to use up those herbs that you bought because you thought they sounded cool and now you have no idea what to do with them!
