Title:
Ten Minute Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 tablespoons flour
3 tablespoons chili powder
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce or diced tomatoes
1 cup chicken broth or water
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder or 1 clove garlic
1/4 teaspoon onion salt or tablespoon chopped onion
salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Stir in flour and chili powder, reduce heat to medium, and cook until lightly brown, stirring constantly to prevent burning flour.
Gradually stir in tomato sauce, water, cumin, garlic (powder), and onion (salt) into the flour and chili powder until smooth, and continue cooking over medium heat approximately 10 minutes, or until thickened slightly. Puree if needed. Season to taste with salt.
