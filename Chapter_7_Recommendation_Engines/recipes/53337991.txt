Title:
Sweet Dried Daikon for Sushi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Dried Daikon Radish, rinsed
1 cup water
1 1/2 tablespoons shoyu soy sauce
2 tablespoons maple syrup (or brown sugar)
1 tablespoon rice vinegar(opt)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a sauce pan, cover and simmer 10 mins.  Drain and press daikon to remove excess liquid. Ready to use in sushi.
