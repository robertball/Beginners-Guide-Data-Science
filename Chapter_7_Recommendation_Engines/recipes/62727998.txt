Title:
Filetto di pollo con salvia e Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
600 grams Chicken (1.3 lbs)
50 grams white flour (.4 cups)
1 glass Marsala Wine
50 grams Butter (3.5 Tablespoons)
50 grams Pecorino Cheese (Sheep-Parmigiano)
Olive Oil
Fresh Sage
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Cut chicken into slices and sprinkle with flour.
Fry in pan with olive oil
Season to taste with salt & pepper
Chop sage and add butter and sage to Marsala wine
Add wine sauce, simmer 5 mins
