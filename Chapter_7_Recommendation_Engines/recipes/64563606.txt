Title:
Warm Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups packed fresh spinach
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 cup Catalina dressing
4 slices cooked bacon, chopped

Directions:
Toss spinach with mushrooms in a large bowl. Microwave dressing and bacon in a small microwavable bowl for 15 to 30 seconds; stir. Pour over spinach salad; toss to coat.
