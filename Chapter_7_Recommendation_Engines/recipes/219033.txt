Title:
TABASCO速 Grilled Peppers and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/848875.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bell peppers, seeded and cut into 1/4-inch strips
4 onions, cut into 1/4-inch rings
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1 teaspoon Original TABASCO速 brand Pepper Sauce
3/4 teaspoon salt, divided
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon sugar

Directions:
Place peppers and onions in a large disposable foil pan. Combine oil, butter, TABASCO(R) Sauce, 1/2 teaspoon of the salt, and pepper and pour over peppers and onions; toss well.
Place pan on grill over medium-low heat and cook about 40 minutes, stirring occasionally until vegetables soften and brown slightly. Stir in remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt and sugar. Serve with grilled sausages and crusty bread.

Submitter: 
TABASCO速 Brand Pepper Sauces
Description: 
Toss pepper strips and onion rings with oil, melted butter, and TABASCO速 Sauce in a foil pan, then grill until browned--a perfect side dish with grilled sausages and crusty bread.
