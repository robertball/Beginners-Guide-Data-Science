Title:
Healthier Best Spinach Dip Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/281785.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup light mayonnaise
1 (16 ounce) container light sour cream
1 medium leek, washed and finely chopped (white and green parts)
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 (4 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and chopped
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1 (1 pound) loaf round whole wheat bread

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise, sour cream, leeks, water chestnuts, salt and pepper, and chopped spinach. Chill in the refrigerator 6 hours, or overnight.
Remove top and interior of whole wheat bread. Fill with mayonnaise mixture. Tear removed bread chunks into pieces for dipping.

Submitter: 
MakeItHealthy
Description: 
It's amazing what a healthy spin we were able to put on this great spinach dip by using fresh leek, more spinach, whole wheat bread, and lower fat cheese and mayo. No need to feel guility as you reach for a second helping!Modified from: Best Spinach Dip Ever
