Title:
Macaron - chocolate filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup heavy cream
6 oz. bittersweet or semisweet chocolate, finely chopped, or chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Heat heavy cream in saucepan.  Swirl once or twice.  You want the edges to bubble but you don't want the entire cream to boil.
2. Pour cream into mixing bowl containing chocolate.  Whisk until smooth.
3. Let cool to room temp (30mins)

Description: 
Chocolate ganache filling for Macaron cookies.
