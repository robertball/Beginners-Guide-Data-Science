Title:
Sloppy Joes II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 cup diced celery
1 diced green bell pepper
2 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
1 1/2 cup ketchup
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 teaspoon white vinegar
1 teaspoon salt
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef, onion, and green pepper and celery; drain off liquids.
Stir in the  mustard, ketchup, brown sugar, vinegar and salt.Mix thoroughly. Reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
Ground beef, onion, green pepper, and ketchup are seasoned with garlic powder and sweetened with brown sugar to make this hearty meat filling. Serve on hamburger buns.Modified from: Sloppy Joes II
