Title:
Basic Sweet Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups all-purpose flour
2 (.25 ounce) packages active dry yeast
1/2 cup white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup milk
1 cups warm water (110 degrees F)
2 eggs (room temperature)

Directions:
Heat in saucepan sugar, milk and salt 'till dissolved. Let cool.
Dissolve yeast in warm water.
Combine with lukewarm mixture.
Add 4 cups flour. Beat with dough hook in mixer at medium speed for 2 minutes.
Add eggs, butter and 1 more cup of flour.
Beat until thick. Stir in the remaining flour until the dough comes together.
Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8 minutes.
Let rise in warm place for 1 hour or doubled in size. Punch down, shape as desired for rolls or coffee cakes.
OR: Cover dough with a damp towel and let rest for 20 minutes. Punch down dough and form into desired shape. Cover and refrigerate dough for up to 48 hours.
Let dough come to room temperature before baking in a 375 degree oven.
