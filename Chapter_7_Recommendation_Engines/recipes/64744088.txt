Title:
KETO CHICKEN THIGHS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Pounds,  6-8 pieces
2 Tbl Olive Oil
2 Tsp Salt
2 Tsp Garlic Powder
2 Tsp Onion Powder
2 Tsp Italian Seasoning
1 Teaspoon Black Pepper
1 Teaspoon Paprika

Directions:
Remove the chicken from their packaging and pat them dry with paper towels.
Arrange the chicken on prepared baking sheet and drizzle with the olive oil rubbing the oil into the chicken to coat.
In a small bowl mix together the salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, and paprika.
Sprinkle the spices over the chicken evenly.
Set Instant Pot for 15 minutes
