Title:
Rachel's Favorite ABC Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef or turkey
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 package frozen mixed vegetables
1 cup alphabet macaroni
1 46oz. can beef broth
1 10oz can tomato sauce
1 tsp. chili powder
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. lawry's seasoning salt
1/2 tsp. oregano leaves
1/2 tsp. basil leaves
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In 4 quart saucepan, cook ground beef 10 min. or until done.
Drain if necessary.
Add beef broth, bring to a boil.
Add pasta, frozen veggies and onion, simmer 15 minutes until pasta & veggies are tender. Stir occasionally.
Add tomato sauce and seasonings, stir and simmer 5- 15 minutes to combine flavors

Submitter: 
Billie Miller
Description: 
This is my 6 year old's favorite soup. An easy way to sneak in extra veggies!
