Title:
Reese's Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup melted butter
1 cup brown sugar, packed
2 cups powdered sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 tsp. salt
2 1/2 tbsp. butter

Directions:
Mix peanut butter, melted butter, vanilla, salt, and brown sugar.
Add powdered sugar and mix well.
Press into 9 x 13 pan.
Melt chocolate chips and buter together.
Spread over peanut butter mixture.
Let set in the refrigertor.
Cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Carol Potts
Description: 
These are supposed to taste like Peanut Butter Cups.  I don't think they do but they are still good.
