Title:
Mom's Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds bottom round roast
Montreal Seasoning
1 packet dry mushroom-onion soup mix
1 can mushroom beef soup-Progresso
10 cloves garlic
8 oz. Portobello mushrooms, sliced
1 onion sliced

Directions:
Mix dry and canned soups together. Add onion and mushrooms, stir. Slice off slab off fat on roast. Poke holes in roast and stuff in cloves of garlic. Make well in soup mix and place roast in well. Season the roast on top with Montreal Seasoning (not too much) Cover and cook on Low setting for 8 to 10 hours.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Kimbrell
Description: 
Browned chuck roast is slow cooked for 8 to 10 hours along with potatoes, carrots, onion, celery, and dehydrated onion soup mix.Modified from: Marie's Easy Slow Cooker Pot Roast
