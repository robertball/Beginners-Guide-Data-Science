Title:
Salmon Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1101028.jpg
Ingredients:
15 oz Pink salmon(bones, skin, and juice)
1 Egg
4-6 Saltine crackers crushed
1 Tbsp Pepper sauce
2 Tbsp Flour
2 tsp Baking Powder

Directions:
Mix all, add more flour if too thin. Fry in hot skillet till browned.

Salmon patties, eat in biscuit with jam.
