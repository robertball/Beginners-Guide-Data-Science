Title:
Grilled Salmon with Cilantro Mint Chutney and Grilled Summer Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
GRILLED SUMMER VEGETABLES
1 bunch asparagus, trimmed down
1 zucchini
1 summer squash
1 onion
1 red bell pepper
1/3 C EVOO
pinch of sea salt
pinch of pepper
GRILLED SALMON
1 1/2 lbs wild salmon
1 tbs EVOO
pinch of sea salt
pinch of black pepper
CHUTNEY
1 small bunch fresh cilantro, including stems, rinsed
2 tbs fresh mint, chopped
3 tbs EVOO
1 1/2 tbs garlic, minced
pinch of sea salt
1 tbs fresh lemon or lime juice
pinch of chili pepper flakes

Directions:
GRILLED SUMMER VEGETABLES
Wash the veggies and cut into the preferred sizes and shapes.
Place all in a bowl and toss with oil, salt & pepper.
Allow to sit for at least 1 hour.
Heat grill pan on medium high heat and place the marinated veggies in, turning occasionally until desired tenderness.
GRILLED SALMON
Season the salmon with the oil, salt and pepper.  Set aside for 10 minutes.
Heat a grill pan on medium high heat and place the salmon skin side down.  Allow the salmon to cook until the skin is charred and the fish is almost cooked through, about 15 minutes.
Turn the salmon over and cook a few more minutes until its done.
Remove to a plate skin up and pull the skin off and flip back over to serve. Spread with the chutney and serve with lemon or lime wedges and Grilled Summer Vegetables.
CHUTNEY
Combine all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
