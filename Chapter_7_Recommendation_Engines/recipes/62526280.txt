Title:
Paratha Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c whole wheat flour plus 1c cake flour OR
2c chappati
1/2 t salt
9T veg oil

Directions:
Add flour and salt in bowl.  Add 2 T oil and rub together.  Slowly add 3/4c and 1T water.  Gather into ball and knead 10 mins and make ball.  Put in bolw and cover for 1/2 hr.  Knead and divide into 6 Keep covered w damp cloth.  Roll into 6 inch rounds.  Pleat edges.  Gather pleats and give small twist and dust w flour.   roll out in 7" diameter.  Heat cast iron griddle over med-low  Add 1t oil and add paratha on heated surface.  Cook 2 mins turn over  Wrap in tinfoil bundle.  Can be reheated @350 for 15 mins.
