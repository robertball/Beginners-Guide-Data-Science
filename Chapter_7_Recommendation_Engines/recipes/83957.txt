Title:
Lawry's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977623.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (3 ounce) package Lawry's® Economy Original Style Spaghetti Sauce Spices & Seasonings
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice
15 ounces ricotta cheese
1 1/2 teaspoons Lawry's® Garlic Powder With Parsley
3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
9 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained

Directions:
Brown ground beef; drain fat. Stir in Spaghetti Sauce Spices & Seasonings, tomato sauce and diced tomatoes. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, for 10 minutes.
Combine ricotta cheese and Garlic Powder With Parsley. In separate bowl, combine 2 cups mozzarella cheese with Parmesan cheese.
In 13x9x2-inch baking dish layer 1/4 of meat sauce, 3 noodles, 1/3 of ricotta mixture and 1/3 of mozzarella-Parmesan mixture; repeat layers 2 times ending with meat sauce.
Bake, uncovered, in preheated 350 degrees F oven for 30 minutes; top with remaining 1 cup of mozzarella cheese and bake until cheese melts, about 1 to 2 additional minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

Don't be surprised if this beefy lasagna becomes your new favorite recipe for the layered Italian classic.
