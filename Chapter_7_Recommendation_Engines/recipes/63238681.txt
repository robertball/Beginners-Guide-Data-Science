Title:
Sonoran Hotdogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/844191.jpg
Ingredients:
Hotdog Weenies
Bacon
Pinto Beans
Potato Buns
Mayonnaise
Diced Tomato
Diced Onion
Pickled Jalape√±os (Blended into a runny paste)
Avocado (Optional)
Chiles Gueritos (Yellow peppers)

Directions:
Wrap the weenies in bacon and cook on a griddle until bacon is crispy
Warm potato bun for 15 seconds in microwave (to soften it up)
Spread mayo inside the bun
Insert Weenie
Top with pinto beans
Add another layer of mayo (this will help keep the following ingredients from falling and add extra flavor)
Add diced onions and tomatoes
Drizzle jalapeno sauce
Dice avocado spread on top of your hot dog
Roast the yellow pepper and enjoy with your hotdog

Submitter: 
Delia
Description: 
Once you've had a Sonoran hotdog you won't want any other kind.  The key to eating the best Sonoran Hotdog is the bread.  There is no substitute for the sweet and fluffy Mexican hotdog buns but I have found a close comparison - Potato buns.
