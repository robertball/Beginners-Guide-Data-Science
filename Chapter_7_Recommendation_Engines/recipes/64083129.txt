Title:
Peas and Mushroom Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
peas
1/4 cup butter
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1/4 teaspoon white pepper

Directions:
Place peas in 1 inch of boiling water. Cover, and cook until tender but still firm. Drain.
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute onions and mushrooms until almost tender. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 3 minutes. Stir in peas salt, seasoned salt, garlic salt, and white pepper. Cover, and cook for 5 minutes over medium heat.
