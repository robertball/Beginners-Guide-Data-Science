Title:
Knud's Scrumptious Sandies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/942682.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (Earth Balance if vegan)
1/3 cup sugar
2 tsp. water
2 tsp. vanilla
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Cream butter and sugar then add water and vanilla and mix well. Blend in the flour and chopped nuts. Put in a large bowl and chill covered for 4 hours. Shape into small (size of a large a large marble)balls and place on ungreaed cookie sheet.  Bake for 20 minutes. Let cool for 10 minutes then roll in powdered sugar to coat.  Yum!
