Title:
Pork Steak & Candied Yam Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 pork steaks/chops, 1/2" to 3/4" thick
salt & pepper (to taste)
veg. oil for browning (2-3 Tbsp.)
2 15-oz cans yams, drained (liquid reserved)
+/- 6 heaping (loose, not packed) Tbsp. brown sugar
+/- 2/3 C. butter/margarine
+/- 1 C. apricot preserves
+/- 3/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
+/- 3/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
+/- 1/4 tsp. ground ginger
+/- 1-1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
+/- 1/4 C. chopped pecans

Directions:
Assemble all ingredients. Preheat oven to 375 deg. F.
Rub steaks/chops with salt & pepper (to taste). Brown steaks in oil on both sides; drain on paper towels; set aside.
Drain yams, reserving liquid (for thinning syrup, if needed); set aside.
In med. pan, combine remaining ingredients & heat over med.-low heat, stirring frequently, until dissolved & slightly bubbly & thickened to desired consistency. Adjust seasonings to taste (adding yam liquid to get desired consistentcy, if needed). Gently stir in yams until well-coated.
Line 13x9 pan (lasagna pan works best) with foil & lightly spray with non-stick cooking spray. Layer steaks in pan; spoon yam mixture over top.
Cover pan with foil and bake at 375 deg. F for 30-45 min. Let stand 10 min. before serving.
