Title:
Twisty Taco Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef or turkey
1 package spiral noodles
1 package taco seasoning
1 (16 ounce) can of tomato sauce
1 bag pre-shredded fiesta blend cheese
1/2 green bellpepper, diced
1 cup sour cream
2 cups salsa

Directions:
In a skillet, brown meat. Meanwhile, bring noodles to a boil and cook as directed on package.
Add tomatoe sauce, chopped pepper, and taco seasoning mix to the meat, let simmer for 5 minutes.
Drain noodles. In a 9 x 13 greased baking dish, combine noodles, sour cream and salsa until well blended.
Top with meat mixture. Bake for 20 minutes. Remove, add cheese to top and bake an additional 5 minutes. Serve with tortilla chips.
