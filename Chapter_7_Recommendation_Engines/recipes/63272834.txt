Title:
Hershey's Triple Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter or margarine
3/4 c. sugar
3/4 c. brown sugar
1 t. vanilla extract
2 eggs
1 T. milk
2 1/4 c. flour
1/3 c. cocoa
1/2 t. salt
1 t. baking soda
1 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 - 9oz bag HERSHEY's kisses with almonds

Directions:
In a mixer bowl, beat butter, sugars, and vanilla. Add eggs and milk; beat well.
Stir together dry ingredients and add to first mixture.
Stir in chocolate chips. Shape dough into balls and bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
Upon removal from oven, press a kiss into the center of each.
