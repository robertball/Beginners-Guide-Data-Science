Title:
Big Ray's Berbere Spice Mix (Ethiopian) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4114362.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp cayenne pepper
5 tsp sweet paprika
1 Tbsp salt
1 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp ground ginger
3/8 tsp ground cardamom
3/8 tsp ground fenugreek
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground allspice
1/8 tsp ground cloves

Directions:
Combine and whisk together all ingredients.
Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Here is a version of the distinctive Ethiopian spice mix that flavors many of their dishes. Use it to season grilled fish, poultry or meat during cooking, or sprinkle it on meat or vegetable dishes at the table.
