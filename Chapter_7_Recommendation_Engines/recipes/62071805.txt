Title:
Cookies and Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box chocolate chip or other favourite cookie  (approx. 20-24 cookies)
2 cups milk warmed
4-6 heaping tablespoons instant flavoured coffee ( use you favorite flavour)
4-6 cups frozen non-dairy whipped topping thawed.

Directions:
Mix the flavoured coffee and warm milk together in a measuring cup.
Place 1/2 cup topping in bottom of an 8x8 inch square baking dish.
Dunk cookie into the coffee making sure cookie is completely immersed.  Make a layer of cookies on top of the cream.  Once the layer is complete top with another  1/2 cup of whipped topping.  Continue dunking cookies and making layers being sure to end with the whipped topping.  Cover and refrigerate at least 12 hours but preferrably 24.  You may sprinkle with cookie crumbs or chocolate shavings before serving if desired.

Submitter: 
BEC
Description: 
A super simple yet elegant dessert.  Needs to be made one day in advance...but only takes minutes to prepare.
