Title:
Easy Cheesy Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz. package of frozen broccoli pieces (thawed)
1 stick of butter
2 cups crushed Ritz crackers
1/2 pound Velveeta cheese

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place thawed broccoli in a greased casserole dish. Cut 1/2 stick of butter into small squares and arrange evenly over the broccoli. Slice 1/2 pound of Velveeta cheese and place over the broccoli and butter. Bake at 250 for 20 minutes.
In a seperate bowl, mix 2 cups of crushed Ritz crackers and 1/2 stick of melted butter.
After the casserole has baked for 20 minutes, pour the Ritz mixture over it and bake another 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
CUPYCAKESMURF
Description: 
A buttery cracker crumb topping sets this quick and easy Broccoli and cheese casserole apart for the rest! Even picky eaters like this one!
