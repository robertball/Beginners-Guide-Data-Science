Title:
Thai noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup stir fry sauce
1 cup soy sauce
1 tablespoon paprika
1 tablespoon ground pepper
olive oil
bowl of warm water
package of rice thai noodles
pinch of parsley
4 boneless chicken thighs cut into bite sized pieces

Directions:
Open the package of noodles and put into warm water for 20 min.
Put a pan with olive oil covering the bottom on the stovetop; heat to high.
Add in the chicken and half of the sauces and spices. Cook chicken thoroughly.
Drain the noodles before adding them with the rest of the spice and sauce. Stir. Serve hot
