Title:
Tomato, Asparagus, Cucumber Salad with Feta Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh asparagus lightly steamed, trimmed and cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
1 container cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
1 cucumber, peeled, seeded and sliced into fourths
1/2 cup chopped red onion
1/2 container (or more) of feta cheese
1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette added 15 minutes prior to serving.

Directions:
Place asparagus in a steamer basket; place in a saucepan over 1 in. of water. Bring to a boil; cover and steam for 5 minutes or until crisp-tender. Drain and immediately place asparagus in ice water. Drain and pat dry.
In a large bowl, combine the asparagus, tomatoes, cucumbers, onion and cheese. Drizzle with vinaigrette and gently toss to coat.
Serve with a slotted spoon.
