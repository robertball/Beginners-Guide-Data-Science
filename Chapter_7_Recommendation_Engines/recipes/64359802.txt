Title:
Teriyaki Sauce Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup cooking sherry
3 tablespoons Splenda
1 tablespoon fresh ginger
1 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Combine ingredients and blend in magic bullet

Submitter: 
Miranda Oswald
Description: 
I use this as a sauce over hot veggies or meat but it works as an Asian salad dressing too. Completely guilt free.
