Title:
Green Chile, Cheese & Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/4 c. all purpose flour
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 c. Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
1 c. cottage cheese
1 c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
14 oz. green chiles, chopped (such as Ortega)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a mixing bowl, whisk eggs with flour, mustard and baking powder. Mix very well.
Stir in the cheeses and chiles.
Pour into a souffle or 9x9 inch baking dish.
Bake for approximately 40 minutes or until set.
Serve hot with toritillas and good salsa.

Submitter: 
Jacki_In_Simi
Description: 
This is the egg dish that I make every year for my holiday brunch and everyone loves it! Of course, I have to make 4 times the amount, but it's very easy to adjust.
