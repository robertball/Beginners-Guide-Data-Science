Title:
Sweet and Sour Meatballs Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(1) 2 lb bag of frozen Rosina meatballs
(1) 11.5 oz. jar of Kikkoman sweet & sour sauce

Directions:
Empty frozen meatballs into an 8x8 pan.
Pour Kikkoman sweet & sour sauce over the top of the meatballs.
Stir the meatballs and sauce so that the meatballs are coated.
Put into oven at 350deg for 25 minutes.
