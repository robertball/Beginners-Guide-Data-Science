Title:
Perfect Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/847245.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter or margarine, melted
2/3 cup unsweetened Cocoa
3 cups powdered sugar
1/3 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
In a medium bowl stir butter with cocoa, add vanilla when well mixed. Alternately add powdered sugar and milk, beating to spreading consistency. Add small amount additional milk, if needed. Frost on cooled cake or cupcakes as desired.

Submitter: 
ANGELSTAR
Description: 
Like the store bought frosting, but you pick how rich you want it to be with how much cocoa you want and what kind you use. I prefer all chocolate to be ghiradelli Much richer flaver, but hershey's works too.
