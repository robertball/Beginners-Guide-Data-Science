Title:
Tempeh with Charred Peppers and Kale Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup canola oil, divided
1 (14-oz) package tempeh, cut into (1/3-inch-thick) slices
2 Tbsp lower-sodium soy sauce
1 cup vertically sliced onion
1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
4 cups thinly sliced Lacinato kale (about 1 bunch)
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 Tbsp cider vinegar

Directions:
Heat large cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add 2 tablespoons oil to pan. Add tempeh; cook 4 minutes, turning to brown evenly.
Drizzle soy sauce into pan. Immediately remove tempeh from pan; keep warm. Increase heat to high.
Add remaining 2 tablespoons oil to pan. Add onion and bell pepper. Cook 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add kale, salt, and pepper to pan; cook 1 minute, tossing to coat. Divide mixture evenly among 4 plates; top with tempeh.
Cals: 384; Fat 25.4g (sat 3.3g); Pro 22g; Fiber 3g; Sugars 3g; Iron 4 mg; Sodium 569mg
