Title:
Chocolate Covered Strawberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2170971.jpg
Ingredients:
3 oz. Semi-sweet Chocolate
3 Tbsp. Butter
1 Large extra serving graham pie crust
16 oz. Cream Cheese, room temperature
1 Cup Sour Cream
1/4 Cup Sugar
1 tsp. Vanilla Bean paste, or vanilla
1 pound Strawberries, sliced
1/4 Strawberry Jam

Directions:
Melt 2 oz. & 2 Tbsp. butter in microwave 2-1/2 minutes at 30% power, stirring occasionally. Spread on bottom and sides of crust. Place crust in freezer. Mix cream cheese in a mixer until smooth. Add sour cream, vanilla paste, & sugar. Pour into crust. In a separate bowl, combine strawberries & jam. Spread on top of cream mixture. Melt 1 oz. chocolate & 1 Tbsp. butter. Drizzle over berries with a spoon or in a Ziploc bag with the end cut. Refrigerate and serve.

Submitter: 
Erica Brown
Description: 
This is one of my favorite pies! It would be a great addition to a holiday meal or a summer picnic.
