Title:
GG's Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp sugar
1 cup warm water
2 pk (4 1/2 tsp) active dry yeast
2 cups milk
4 tbsp. sugar
3 tsp salt
4 tbsp. melted butter
1 cup warm water
9-10 cups flour

Directions:
Dissolve 2tsp sugar in 1 cup warm water and add yeast.  Set aside for about 10 min.
Warm milk and melt butter, then combine.
Add in sugar, salt and 1 cup warm water.
Mix with yeast mixture.
Blend in flour slowly and mix well with mixer until it gets too thick, then knead by hand.
Make into a smooth ball and cover with a cloth.
Put in a warm place to rise.
Let rise till doubled, punch down and let rise again.
Divide into three loaves and place in loaf pans to let rise again and bake.
