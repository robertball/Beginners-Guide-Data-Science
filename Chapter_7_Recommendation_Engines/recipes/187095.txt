Ingredients:
2 RITZ Crackers
2 teaspoons PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
6 small green grapes, halved
2 raspberries
6 strips green apple peel

Directions:
Spread crackers evenly with cream cheese spread.
Top each with 6 grape halves to resemble flower petals and a raspberry for the center of the flower. Add green apple strips for the 'stems' and 'leaves.'

Submitter: 
RITZ
Description: 
Grape halves, raspberries and green apple peel are arranged on cream-cheese spread crackers to resemble a spring flower.
