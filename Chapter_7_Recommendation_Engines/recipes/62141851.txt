Title:
Pink Cloud Congealed Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-  cups buttermilk
1-  6 oz pkg strawberry jello
1-  9 oz pkg Cool Whip (low fat or fat free)
1-  large can crushed pineapple
1-  small jar cherries, drained (optional)

Directions:
Cook juice of pineapple & jello until jello has dissolved
Let cool.  Beat in 2 cups of buttermilk and cool whip. Fold in pineapple and cherries. Let set until firm in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Cathie
Description: 
This is a lovely pink congealed salad. Children and grown ups love this recipe. It is been a long standing dish from my wonderful mother-in-law's table.
