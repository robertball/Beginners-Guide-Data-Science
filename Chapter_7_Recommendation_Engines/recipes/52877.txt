Title:
Dirt Cake from Nestle® Nesquik™ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985766.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (5.9 ounce) package chocolate instant pudding and pie filling mix, prepared according to package directions
2 cups chocolate wafer cookies, crushed
2/3 cup Chocolate Flavor NESTLE® NESQUIK™ Syrup
1 (8 ounce) container frozen non-dairy whipped topping, thawed
1 (3 ounce) package gummy worms

Directions:
COMBINE cookie crumbs and Nesquik(tm) in medium bowl; reserve 1/4 cup.
LAYER half of remaining crumb mixture, half of pudding and half of whipped topping in a large bowl or 8 small bowls. Repeat layers. Sprinkle with reserved crumb mixture; top with gummy worms.

Submitter: 
Nestle® Nesquik™
Description: 
This chocolaty kid favorite, topped with gummy worms and chocolate crushed wafers, will keep your gang content.
