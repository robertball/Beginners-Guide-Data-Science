Title:
Applesauce Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 spice cake mix
3 eggs
1/3 cup oil
1/3 cup water
1 1/2 cups Fisher & Wieser Harvest Apple and Brandy pie filling
1 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Grease and flour a 9 x 13 pan
Combine all ingredients and mix well
Bake for 40-45 minutes
Serve while warm

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Start with a cake mix, add some special pie filling and you've got a quick and easy, moist and tasty dessert.  Serve warm with whipped cream or ice cream.
