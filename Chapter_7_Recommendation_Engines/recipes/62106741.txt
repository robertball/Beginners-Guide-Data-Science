Title:
Jim's Freestyle Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb ground beef
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
6 button mushrooms, chopped
1 egg, beaten
2 t dry mustard
1 t hot sauce
2 t worstershire sauce
1 t black pepper
1/3 C milk
3/4 C crackers, crushed
2 T fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Place in two loaf pans.
Bake in 350 degree oven for one hour.

Submitter:

Description: 
A great throw-together meatloaf recipe perfect for a comforting week-night dinner.
