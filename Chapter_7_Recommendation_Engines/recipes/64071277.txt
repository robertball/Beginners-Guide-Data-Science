Title:
Chocolate Refrigerator Dessert #2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
First layer:
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 cup flour
1/2 cup chopped nuts
Second layer:
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 cup powdered sugar
1 cup cool whip
Third layer:
1 small package of instant chocolate pudding
1 small package of instant vanilla pudding
2 3/4 cups milk
1 Hershey bar
additional chopped nuts

Directions:
First layer:
Mix all ingredients together well, spread in a 9x13 pan and bake 20 minutes. Cool.
Second layer:
Mix all ingredients together and spread on crust.
Third layer:
Mix all ingredients together and spread on cream cheese layer.
Frost with remaining cool whip and sprinkle with grated Hershey bar or chopped nuts.
