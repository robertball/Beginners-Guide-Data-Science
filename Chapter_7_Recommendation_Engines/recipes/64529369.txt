Title:
Classic Extraordinary Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 8 oz Cream Cheese
1/2 cup Grated Cheddar Cheese
1/2 cup grated Mozzarella Cheese
1 cup Mayonnaise
1 tbsp Epicure seasoning for Cheese,Chives, & Bacon Dip
1 tbsp Epicure Lemon Dilly Dip
1 tbsp Epicure 3 Onion Dip Mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Combine all ingredients and mix well and spoon into oven safe dish
Bake for 20 minutes or until heated through and bubbling
