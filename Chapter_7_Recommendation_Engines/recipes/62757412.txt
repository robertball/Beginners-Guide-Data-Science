Title:
Baked Chicken Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp Caesar dressing
4 boneless chicken breasts
1 1/3 cups pasta sauce
Â¼ cup mozzarella shredded cheese.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Line baking sheet with foil and spray with cooking spray.
Toss chicken in dressing and marinate 30 minutes.
Add chicken to baking sheets and bake 20 minutes. Spread 1/3 cup pasta sauce over chicken and sprinkle with cheese on top. Bake 10 minutes.
Warm remaining sauce, serve on the side.
