Title:
Vegetarian Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Medium yellow onion chopped
1 yellow squash chopped into large chunks (2 if small)
1 zucchini chopped into large chunks (2 if small)
1 bell pepper, 1 red pepper
4 sweet peppers
2 Bay leafs
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/2 tablespoon paprika
1/2 tablespoon cummin
3 gloves garlice minced
1 tablespoon oregano
1 tablespoon parcley
1 large can chrushed tomatos
1 can kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
1 can Chili beans
1 can diced tomatos
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
In a large pot heat oil on Med-High heat. Add onion garlic and saute for 2-3 mins unitl onions start to sweat. Add seasonings, herbs, vegetables, and peppers to pot. mix together until everything is mixed evenly. Stir occationally and cook for 5 mins. Add tomatos and beans and reduce heat to slow simmer. Stri occationally and cook for 30-45 mins. The longer it cooks the better it is.
