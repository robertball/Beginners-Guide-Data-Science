Title:
Beef Burrito Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030787.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
1 oz taco seasoning
1 (10oz) can Rotel, undrained
1 (15 oz) can low-sodium black beans, drained and rinsed
2 cups Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 (10.75 oz) can Fiesta Cheese Soup
4 oz plain Greek yogurt
6 large wheat tortillas

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees.
Cook and drain meat.
Stir in taco seasoning, Rotel, and black beans. Heat for 5 minutes.
In small bowl, combine soup and yogurt.
Grease 9x9 pan.
Tear up 3 tortillas and place in bottom of pan.
Spread half of soup mixture over tortillas.
Top with half of meat mixture.
Top with half of cheese.
Repeat layers.
Bake 25-30 minutes.
Top with additional yogurt and salsa, if desired.
