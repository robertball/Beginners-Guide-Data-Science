Title:
Slow Cooker Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 10 3/4oz. cans of cream of potato soup
10 3/4 oz. can cream of celery soup
2- 6 1/2 oz. cans minced clams, drained
3 slices of bacon, diced and fried
1 soup can of water
1 small onion, minced
1 tbsp. fresh parsley
dash of dried marjoram
1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
pepper to taste
2 soup cans of milk

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except 2 cans of milk in slow cooker.
Cover.  Cook on low for 2-3 hours.
Twenty minutes before end of cooking time, stir in milk.  Continue cooking until heated through.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner

A simpler way to make clam chowder!
