Title:
Cousin Cosmo's Greek Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoons all-purpose flour, divided
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 oz. feta cheese, crumbled
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1 clove crushed garlic
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
3 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
1 tablespoons olive oil
1 c chicken broth
1/4 c white wine
2 cups loosely packed torn fresh spinach leaves
1/4 pint cherry tomatoes, quartered

Directions:
On large plate, combine 1 tablespoon flour, 1/4 t salt, 1/8 t pepper. Set aside.
In a small bowl, combine cheese, 1/2 T lemon juice, oregano, 1/4 t salt, 1/8 t pepper, and garlic.  Set aside.
Cut a pocket into each chicken breast.  Stuff cheese mixture into each chicken breast and secure each with toothpick.  Coat chicken breasts with flour mixture.
In large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Cook chicken breasts for 1 to 2 minutes on each side, until golden.
In a small bowl, mix chicken broth, 1/2 T lemon juice, white wine and remaining flour; pour over chicken breasts in pan.  Add tomatoes to skillet, and bring to boil.
Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 8 to 10 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink inside.  Add spinach in during the last 2 minutes of cooking.  Discard toothpicks before serving.
