Title:
South of the Border Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# hamburger
1 pkg. taco seasoning
8 oz. can tomatoes (drained)
1# Velveeta mild style cheese
2 T. margarine

Directions:
Cook beef until done.
Add taco seasoning according to package.
Put tomatoes in blender and chop then add to meat and seasoning.
Melt cheese and margarine slowly and add to meat mixture.
