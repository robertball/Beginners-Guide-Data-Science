Ingredients:
3/4 c milk (scalded and cooled until warm)
2 egg yolks
1 1/4 tsp yeast
2 c flour
4 T butter
1/4 c white sugar
1 tsp salt
2/3 c raisins
Egg Wash
1 egg
1/2 tsp sugar
Topping
1/4 c sliced almonds

Directions:
Mix dough ingredients (except raisins) and knead 9-15 minutes. Add more flour as needed.  Add raisins. Turn out into greased bowl and allow to rise 1 - 2 hours.  Create braid and allow to rise 1 - 1 1/2 hours.
Cook 30-40 minutes at 350.
