Title:
Turkey Boats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 turkey breasts pounded thin
2 ounces sharp cheddar cheese, divided
1/2 c. bread crumbs
1 egg
1/4 c. flour
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 c. dry white wine, divided
1/2 c. chicken broth
1 garlic clove, minced
1 medium shallot, cut into strips
pepper to taste
2 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Place 1 ounce of cheddar cheese on each turkey breast, roll up, and tuck ends under. Roll in flour, dip in egg, and coat with bread crumbs. Refrigerate for 1 hour or overnight.
Heat olive oil over medium high heat in skillet or dutch oven. Place turkey breasts in oil and brown 2 minutes on each side.
Combine chicken broth, 1/2 c white wine and pepper.
Remove turkey from pan and pour in 1/2 c white wine to de-glaze pan scraping bits from bottom of pan. Reduce heat to medium. Add garlic and shallots, sauteing for 90 seconds. Add chicken broth mixture. Once it comes to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low and put the turkey breasts back into the pan and cover. Simmer for 20 minutes, adding more liquid if needed.
Remove turkey breasts from pan. Stir sauce and add parsley, stir occasionally for 2 minutes. Pour sauce over turkey. Serve hot.
