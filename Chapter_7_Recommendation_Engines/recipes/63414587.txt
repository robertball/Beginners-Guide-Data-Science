Title:
Beef-Cheese Crescent Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/921647.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb extra lean ground beef
1/3 cup diced white onion
1/4 cup chopped bell pepper (green or red)
8 oz can tomato sauce
15 oz can cut green beans, drained
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1 can crescent rolls
1 egg, slightly beaten
8 oz Monterrey-Jack cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Add meat to a heavy skillet and brown.
Add in onions, peppers, and green beans. Cook until onions are tender; Drain well and return to skillet.
Add in tomato sauce and garlic powder, mix until well combined. Set aside.
Press the crescent roll dough into the bottom and sides of an 8" pie plate to form an even crust.
Combine the egg and cheese.
Press half of cheese mixture into bottom of crust. Top with all of beef mixture. Finish with remaining cheese mix.
Bake for 20-25 minutes. Serve hot.
