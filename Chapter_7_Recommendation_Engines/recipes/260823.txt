Title:
Maple Syrup Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4576335.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fat-free milk
2 teaspoons maple syrup
1 1/2 teaspoons cocoa powder

Directions:
Bring milk to a boil in a small saucepan. Add maple syrup and cocoa powder, stirring occasionally, until completely dissolved.
