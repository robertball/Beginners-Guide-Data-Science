Title:
Nacho Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1 TB chili powder
1/4tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp paprika
1-1/2 tsp cumin
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 cup diced onion
1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
1/2 cup taco sauce
1 (10 oz) can diced tomatoes with chiliâ€™s (ie. rotel)
1 (15.5 oz) can kidney beans - drained and rinsed
2 cups shredded cheese
4 cups crushed tortilla chips

Directions:
Brown ground beef and drain off fat.
Return beef to skillet and add taco sauce, tomato sauce, tomatoes,  onion, kidney beans, and all the spices.  Cook over medium-high heat stirring frequently for approximately 15 minutes to blend flavors.
Put half of beef mixture in 3 quart casserole dish, half the chips, followed by half the cheese.  Repeat layers.
Place in a preheated 350 degree oven for 10-12 minutes or until cheese is melted.
Top with all your favorite toppings (olives, lettuce, salsa, sour cream, etc)

Description: 
This is something I came up with because we wanted mexican for dinner, but didn't have any enchilada sauce or tortilla shells.  It is a very quick, easy dish which pleased everyone.
