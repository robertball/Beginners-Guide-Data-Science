Title:
Cheese Cart Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound orecchiette pasta (or small shells)
2 TB olive oil
2 TB flour
1 3/4 cups milk at room temperature
1/4 pound Asiago cheese, shredded
1/4 pound fontina cheese, shredded
1/4 pound Gouda cheese, shredded
1/2 tsp white pepper
salt
Crunchy Garlic Bread Topping
1/4 cup butter
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 TB freshly chopped italian parsely
2 cups toasted bread crumbs (or coarsely grind 1/4 loaf of italian bread in food processor and toast in oven)
1/4 cup grated parmesan

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, cook pasta until done.
While pasta is cooking, in a saucepan over medium heat add 2 TB of olive oil. Whisk in flour and cook stirring constantly for 2 minutes. Slowly add the milk and whisk well to get rid of all the lumps. Bring to a low simmer until it starts to thicken. Stir in cheeses and season with white pepper and salt to taste. Drain pasta and return to pot. Pour cheese sauce over pasta and stir.
Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook for about 2 minutes. Add bread crumbs and parmesan cheese. Stir together and then mix in parsely. Stir until crumbs are completely coated with butter mixture.
Serve mac & cheese in small bowls topped with bread crumbs, and a piece of parsley to garnish.
