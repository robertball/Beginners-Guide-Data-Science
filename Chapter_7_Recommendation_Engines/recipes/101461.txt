Title:
Tailgater Rib Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979601.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons firmly packed brown sugar
2 teaspoons Lawry's® Seasoned Salt
2 pounds baby back ribs
Your favorite barbecue sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
In small bowl, combine sugar and Lawry's (r) Seasoned Salt.  Rub seasoning mixture on ribs. In foil-lined shallow roasting pan, arrange ribs meat-side-up; cover tightly with aluminum foil. Bake ribs 2 hours or until ribs are thoroughly cooked. Remove foil and generously brush ribs with barbecue sauce. Bake uncovered an additional 15 minutes to glaze*. Serve, if desired, with additional barbecue sauce.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
Seasoned salt, brown sugar, and your favorite barbeque sauce are all you need to make succulent ribs in the oven.
