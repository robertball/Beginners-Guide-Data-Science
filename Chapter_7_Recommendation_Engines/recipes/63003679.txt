Title:
3 Cheese Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Italian bread crumbs
1 can condensed tomato soup (divided)
2 tsp instant minced onions
1 tsp dried mustard
1 egg
Kosher salt, pepper, garlic powder, worcestershire sauce to taste
1 1/2 lbs ground chuck
1 cup each shredded cheddar, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese
bacon

Directions:
combine bread crumbs, 1/2 tomato soup, onions, mustard, seasoning in a bowl
add beef, mix well
On a large sheet of foil or wax paper, roll out beef mixture, sprinkle cheddar and mozzerella cheese iwithin 1-2 inches of edge.  Roll up jelly roll style.
Seal seam and ends.
Transfer to loaf pan
Top with remaining tomato soup and bacon strips
Bake @350 for 1 hour
Top with parmesan chees
Bake for 15 additional minutes or until internal temperature is 150.
Remove from oven, et stand for 10 minures, slice and serve
