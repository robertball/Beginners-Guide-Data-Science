Title:
Artemis'  steak marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Olive Oil
6 Cloves Garlic, finely chopped
1/4 Cup Fresh Oregano, roughly chopped
2 Pounds steak or Lamb Chops
1/4 Teaspoon Pepper, freshly ground or to taste
1 Lemon, cut in 1/4's

Directions:
Combine olive oil, garlic, oregano, and pepper in a shallow glass dish. 'I use gallon ziplok bags.'
Apply marinade to both sides of steaks and allow to sit 30 minutes to 2 hours.
Grill meat to your desired doneness.
Serve with a squeeze of fresh lemon.

Description: 
I love this marinade on grilled lamb and beef.
