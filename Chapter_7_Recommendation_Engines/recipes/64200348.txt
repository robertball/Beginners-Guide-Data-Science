Title:
Bacon-Wrapped Fingerling Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fingerling potatoes
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp minced fresh thyme
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp paprika
1/2 pound bacon
1/4 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Combine oil and spices in a medium bowl.  Toss potatoes in oil mixture.
Cut each bacon strip in half, lengthwise.  Wrap half a bacon slice around each potato.
Heat a large skillet over medium heat.  Add potatoes and reduce to medium low.  Cook until lightly browned and bacon has tightened around the potatoes.
Place potatoes in a slow cooker.  Add broth and cook on high for 3 hours.
