Title:
Cajun Seasoning Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ts garlic salt (California style)
1 ts bacon salt
1 ts oregano
1 ts thyme
1 ts onion powder
1 ts oregano
1 ts cumin
1 ts paprika
1 ts pepper
1/16 ts cayenne (to taste)

Directions:
Mix all together in small food processor.
Blend/chop thoroughly.
Store in sealed container.
