Title:
Rigatoni Cinque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/616985.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. Rigatoni
6 slices bacon, diced
1 med. sweet onion diced small
3.25 oz frozen peas
2 tbls butter
1 C milk
1 C half n half
1/2 C Tomato/Basil pasta sauce
handful of Parmigiano Reggiano, plus extra for Serving (optional)
Handful of Fresh Basil,torn (optional)

Directions:
Cook Rigatoni to al dente (or to taste) according to package directions.  In a skillet cook bacon, remove and reserve bacon.  To the bacon drippings add the onion and cook until translucent, about 4 minutes.  Add butter, milk and half n half, stir to combine.  Add peas and Parmigiano Regiano and cook on medium heat until sauce thickens.  Add Tomato/Basil sauce, stir to combine. Add cooked bacon and Rigatoni to sauce and stir to combine.  Serve immediately and top with more Parmigiano Reggiano and fresh basil.

Submitter: 
~Gina~
Description: 
Easy and Delicious Rigatoni combines with bacon, peas and onion in a creamy pink sauce.  This recipe is my interpretation of NYC's Sotto Cinque's special pasta with pink sauce.
