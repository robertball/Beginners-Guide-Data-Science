Title:
Shrimp Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/594865.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cup Chicken Broth;
1 Cup Quinoa;
1 Onion, Diced;
1 Red Bell Pepper;
8 Stalks Asparagus, Cut into 1in Pieces;
1 Cup Mushrooms, Sliced;
1/4 Cup Raisins;
3 Cloves Garlic, Minced;
1 Tablespoon Ginger, Minced;
1lb Shrimp, Shelled and De-veined;
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil;
1 Tablespoon Lemon Juice;
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Bring chicken broth to a boil, then add the quinoa. Cover the pot and lower the temperature. Simmer for 15 minutes, then turn off heat. Let the remaining liquid absorb into the quinoa.
Meanwhile, Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Saut√© the garlic, onion and bell pepper until they soften; Add in the asparagus, mushrooms, raisins and ginger. Cook until the asparagus is tender.
Add in the shrimp to the vegetables and cook until the shrimp turn opaque, about 5 minutes
Mix the lemon juice into the quinoa, then toss the quinoa into the vegetables and shrimp. Adjust seasonings
Enjoy!
