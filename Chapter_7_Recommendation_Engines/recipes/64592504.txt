Title:
Oven Roasted Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3807464.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Chicken Breast
Salt and Pepper
Non-Fat Butter Spray

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
Place chicken breast between 2 pieces of parchment paper. With a mallet, pound chicken to make thin even pieces.
Spray baking sheet with non-stick cooking spray.
Season each chicken breast with salt and pepper on each side.
Place chicken on baking sheet.
Spray the top of each chicken with 3 sprays of non-fat butter spray.
Bake for 30 minutes.
