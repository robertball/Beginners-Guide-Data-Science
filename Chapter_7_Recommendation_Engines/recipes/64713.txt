Title:
Fast Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon oil
1 (1 cup) pouch frozen BOCA Meatless Ground Burger
6 green onions, with tops, chopped
1 egg, lightly beaten
2 cups cooked rice
1 (6 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
2 tablespoons reduced-sodium soy sauce

Directions:
HEAT oil in large nonstick skillet on medium-high heat.
ADD ground burger and onions; cook and stir 3 minutes. Spoon burger mixture to one side of skillet. Add egg to other side of skillet; cook until set, stirring constantly.
ADD remaining ingredients; mix all ingredients in skillet until well blended. Cook 5 minutes or until heated through, stirring frequently.
