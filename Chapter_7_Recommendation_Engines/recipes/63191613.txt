Title:
Turkey & Cranberry Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
whole wheat wraps
herbed cream cheese
dried cranberries
sunflower seeds
roast turkey slices
swiss cheese
red onion
alfalfa sprouts

Directions:
Spread cream cheese on wraps; sprinkle with cranberries and sunflower seeds.
Place turkey, swiss cheese and onion on wrap. Add a generous amount of sprouts.
Roll/fold together, slice in half and serve.
