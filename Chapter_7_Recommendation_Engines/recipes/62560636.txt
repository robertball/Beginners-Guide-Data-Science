Title:
Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536160.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Package Ore Ida Potatoes O'Brien
1 Package McCormick Country Gravy Mix
2/3 cup Instant Potatoes
8 Chicken Bullion Cubes
9 Cups of Water

Directions:
Brown potatoes in frying pan. While browning potatoes, take 7 cups of water
and 8 bullion cubes and place in a pot. Boil water and bullion cubes. Make gravy
according to package. After gravy is done and water is boiling whisk in instant
potatoes. After potatoes are brown, pour them into the pot and simmer for 15
minutes.
