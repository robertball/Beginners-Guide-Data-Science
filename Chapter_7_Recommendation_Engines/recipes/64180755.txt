Title:
Angry BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) bottle ketchup
1 (16 fluid ounce)bottle of Angry Orchard
1 small onion, diced
1 1/2 cups dark brown sugar
1/2 cup prepared mustard
3 tablespoons barbeque seasoning
2 tablespoons distilled apple cider vinegar
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 tablespoons liquid smoke
1/2 of habanero diced

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine all ingredients. Simmer on low setting for 12 hours or overnight.

Submitter:

Description: 
A thick, sticky sauce to slather onto anything edible. It cooks all night in the slow cooker.Modified from: Old Style BBQ Sauce
