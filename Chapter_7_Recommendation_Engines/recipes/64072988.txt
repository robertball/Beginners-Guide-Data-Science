Title:
Darlene's Orange Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rolls:
2 cups warm water
1 TBSP dry yeast
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup melted shortening or butter
6 eggs
2 tsp. salt
8 cups flour
Icing:
1/4 cup orange juice
2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Rolls
Sprinkle yeast and sugar on warm water. Add 2 cups flour and mix. Add shortening, eggs, salt and remaining flour. Cover and let rise. Knead lightly and divide in half. Roll out each half in a rectangle. Brush with cinnamon-sugar mixture. Fold in half, roll and repeat the process. Fold again and roll out.
Repeat a total of three times for each half.
Slice in 1x3 inch long pieces with a sharp knife, twist into knots, let rise until double in bulk, bake.
Icing:
Mix ingredients together to the consistency of pancake batter and spoon onto hot rolls.
Cinnamon-sugar mixture:
1 1/2 cup sugar
4 tsp. cinnamon
3/4 cup butter, softened
