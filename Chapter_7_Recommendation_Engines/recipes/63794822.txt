Title:
Soup Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small bag of pinto beans
1 stick butter
1 pack Lil' Smokey's
Enough water to cover yer beans

Directions:
In yer crock pot add yer beans and lil' wieners, salt and pepper and put enough water in to cover the beans. Y'all can add more water If'n ya like yer beans soupy.
Cover and let'em cook on high for 4 hrs.
Turn em down to low and let em cook 2 more hours. Keep an eye on em to make sure they always have plenty of water. When ya get em cooked add yer stick of butter and let it melt real good.
Make ye up some good old cornbread to go with em and y'all got a meal!
