Title:
What the ELLE...Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup+ apple sauce
1 tbls dried white onion
1/4 cup wwflour
2 C chicken broth
1 tbls dried celery
1 tablespoon minced garlic
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup milk
3 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and cube
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
real bacon bits to taste

Directions:
put apple saucein a large saucepan over medium heat. Cook onion and celery in applesauce.  Stir in wwflour until smooth, then gradually stir in 2 cups of chicken broth, garlic, salt, and pepper. Heat through, then stir in milk. Dissolve chicken base in 1 cup warm water, and pour into vegetable mixture.
Place potatoes in slow cooker, and pour heated vegetable mixture into potatoes.
Cover, and cook 5 hours on High, or 8 hours on Low.
Puree about 4 cups of the soup in a blender or food processor, and then stir pureed soup into contents of slow cooker.  Add cheese and bacon to serving.
