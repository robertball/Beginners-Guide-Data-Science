Title:
Mac and Cheese with Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp unsalted butter
3 tbsp flour
1 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups nonfat milk
3/4 cup nonfat sour cream
1/2 cup lowfat sour cream
1 egg, lightly beaten
2 cups reduced fat cheddar cheese
4 cups cooked whole wheat elbow macaroni
1 cup frozen peas
1 cup diced carrots
1 cup small broccoli florets, lightly steamed
1/2 small diced onion
1/4 cup crushed cornflakes or dried breadcrumbs

Directions:
Coat a casserole dish or 11x9 in pan with nonstick spray. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Melt butter in medium saucepan. Stir in the flour, mustard, paprika and salt. Slowly pour in the milk, whisking all the while until there are no lumps in the flour. Continue to stir the milk with the whisk, raise the heat and cook until the milk bubbles and thickens. Remove from the stovetop and stir in the sour cream, egg and cheese.
Toss the macaroni with the cheese mixture. Stir in the veggies and place into prepared baking dish. Top with breadcrumbs or cornflakes.
Bake 30-35 minutes or until bubbly and browning on the top.
Enjoy!
