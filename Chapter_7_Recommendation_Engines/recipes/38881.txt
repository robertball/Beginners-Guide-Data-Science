Title:
Orange-Glazed Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980015.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup A.1. Steak Sauce
3 tablespoons orange marmalade
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1 (1 1/2 pound) beef top round steak

Directions:
Mix steak sauce, marmalade, soy sauce and ginger.
Grill steak for 7 to 8 minutes on each side or until cooked through, brushing with 1/4 cup of the steak sauce mixture during last 2 minutes of grilling time. Let stand 5 minutes before slicing to serve.
Heat remaining steak sauce mixture. Serve over steak.
