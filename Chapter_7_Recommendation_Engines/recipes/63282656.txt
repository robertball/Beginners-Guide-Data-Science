Title:
Pakistani Chapati Bread (like Indian Naan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/864162.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 C All-Purpose Flour
1/2 C Whole Wheat Flour
1 TBSP Clarified or Melted Butter
1/2 TSP Salt
3/4 C Lukewarm Water
Flour to dust and water to mix, if necessary

Directions:
Pour 3/4 C lukewarm water into small bowl and stir in salt. Slowly add the water to the flour and mix well.
Transfer to a flat surface and knead for 10 minutes until dough is firm and consistent.
Let rest for 20 minutes in a bowl covered with a dampened paper towel.
Divide the dough into 8 balls and roll out to circles or oblong shape. Flatter is better.
Cook in a non stick pan until bubbles form then turn over, about a minute on each side.
Brush with butter and cover.
