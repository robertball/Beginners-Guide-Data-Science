Title:
Mandy's Loaded Spagetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Jars Spaghetti Sauce
1 lb ground meat
1 lb mushrooms
1 onion, chopped
1/2 cup sliced olives
1/2 cup red wine
1 1/2 lbs cooked spaghetti
1/2 cup chopped parsley
Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Brown mean until golden.  Remove from pan.  Pour out all but 1 T fat.  Sautee onion and mushrooms until golden.  Add mean, sauce, olives, and wine.  Simmer while spaghetti is cooking.
Toss hot pasta with olive oil and parsley.
Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.

Store bought sauce fortified with meat and veggies.
