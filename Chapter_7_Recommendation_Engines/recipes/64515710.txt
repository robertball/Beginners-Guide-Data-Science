Title:
Cook'n Corona-Barb Cappuccino Maple Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup maple syrup
1/4 cup butter
1 (1/4 cup) envelope instant cappuccino mix
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1 (7 1/2-10oz) can of refridgerated biscuits

Directions:
in small saucepan, combine syrup and butter.
bring mixture just to a boil over medium heat
whisk in cappuccino mix
reduce heat to low
cook and stir for 1 minute
pour mixture into greased 10" dutch oven
sprinkle with pecans
cut biscuits in half and arrange in dutch oven
cover and bake 400 F for 15 minutes until biscuits are just barely golden
spoon some o the topping over the buns and serve
