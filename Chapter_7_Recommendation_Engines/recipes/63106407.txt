Title:
Ground Beef Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 to 2 pounds ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
4 ribs celery, chopped
4 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
4 carrots, cut into 1-inch pieces or diced
1 can tomato sauce
1 can whole tomatoes, crush
1 can peas, do-not drain
1 can corn, rinse and drain
1 can green beans, rinse and drain
black pepper, to taste
1/8 teaspoon thyme
1 or 2 bay leafs
water; as needed

Directions:
In a large Dutch oven/soup pot brown ground beef, drain if necessary.  Add onion, carrots & celery to ground beef and cook stirring until semi-soft.
Add tomato sauce, tomatoes, undrained can of peas, drained corn, drained green beans, and potatoes.  Stir well.
Add black pepper, thyme and bay leaf.  Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.  Continue simmering, stirring as needed, add water if needed 1/4 cup at a time to get the consistency you like.  Don't add too much water at one time, let it cook to thicken then add another 1/4 cup or so of water as desired.
Let simmer with lid on for 1 hour or until vegetables are tender.
Remove bay leaf before serving.

This is a satisfying - very easy & delicious vegetable soup to serve anytime of the year.
