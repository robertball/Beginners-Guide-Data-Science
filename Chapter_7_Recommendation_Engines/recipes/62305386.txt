Title:
Moist Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/367678.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 c. shortening
1/2 c. sugar
2 eggs
1 3/4 c. flour
1 t. baking powder
1/2 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt
1 1/4 c. mashed ripened bananas
1 c. walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Oven to 350 degrees.  Cream shortening and sugar.  Blend in eggs until light and creamy. Add bananas.  In small bowl mix dry ingredients.  Add to banana mixture, blend well.  Add nuts until thoroughly blended.  Pour batter into greased and floured bread pans.  Bake on cookie sheet lined with parchment to keep crust from becoming too dark. Bake 45-50 minutes or until golden and toothpick comes out clean.  Let stand in pan 5 minutes before turning onto cooling rack.  Cool completely before storing in plastic bread bag or ziploc.
