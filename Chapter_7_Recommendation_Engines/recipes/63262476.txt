Title:
Garden Pork Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 boneless Chicken, cut into 1/2-inch julienned strips
2 bags stir- fry vegetables
1/2 bag fresh broccoli
Stir-Fry Glaze
1/4 cup bacon fat
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 cup apple juice
1 cup honey
3/4 cup water
1 small onion, chopped
2 tbls. minced garlic
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 cup corn starch

Directions:
Season chicken with pepper,cover with foil and bake at 325 degrees for one hour. let cool
Cut pork into half inch slices then cut slices into half inch strips making sure that you keep cutting against the grain.
In a small pot,saute chopped onion in bacon fat until soft. Add soy sauce, apple juice, honey, garlic powder and ginger; whip until smooth.
Bring to a boil.
Combine corn starch with water and add to mixture. Cook and stir for 1-2 minutes or until thickened and bubbly.
For service, stir-fry pork on a medium high grill until there is no more pink, add vegetables and saute an additional 4-5 minutes or until hot.
Transfer to a large mixing bowl and toss with marinade.
serve with rice
