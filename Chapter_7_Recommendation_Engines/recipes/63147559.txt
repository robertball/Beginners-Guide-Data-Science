Title:
Old-fashioned Shortcake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c Flour
1 c Sugar
4 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Salt
1/4 - 1/2 tsp Nutmeg to taste
1 stick butter or margarine
1 egg
1/3 c milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 and grease an 8" pan.  Sift flour with sugar, baking powder, salt and nutmeg.  Cut in one stick of butter or margarine.  In a separate bowl, beat an egg and mix in the milk.  Add to the dry ingredients and mix just until blended, being careful not to over-mix.  Bake for 15 minutes and cool on wire rack.  Serve with sweetened sliced strawberries and whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Kuchnicki Merzlicker
Description: 
This is a fabulous old fashioned recipe based on one given to me by a friend.  It was her mother's recipe and it is the best shortcake.  It sops up all the juice and the touch of nutmeg sends it over the top.  My very favorite recipe for strawberry shortcake!
