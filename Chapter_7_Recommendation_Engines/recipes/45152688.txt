Title:
Easy Vegetable Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
4 (15 ounce) cans mixed vegetables
4 (16 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
1 onion, chopped fine
1 potato diced
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large soup pot, cook ground meat over medium heat until browned. Drain grease from the pot.
Add chopped onion, mixed vegetables, and tomatoes. Give it a stir.  Reduce heat, and simmer for about 3 to 4 hours.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Michele
Description: 
Simple!  Brown the meat, and throw it all together into one pot. Serve soup with a cake of corn bread or grilled cheese sandwiches on a cold winter night. You will feel warm all over.Modified from: Easy Vegetable Beef Soup with Ground Beef
