Title:
Avocado Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2140127.jpg
Ingredients:
1 avocado, peeled and pitted
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons lemon juice
2 cloves garlic
1 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Blend avocado, yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, sea salt, hot sauce, and black pepper together in a blender until smooth.

Description:
