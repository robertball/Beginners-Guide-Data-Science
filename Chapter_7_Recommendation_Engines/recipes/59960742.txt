Title:
Amy's Mexican Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1 cup mayonnaise
2 (11 ounce) cans Mexican-style corn
4 green onions, chopped
1/2 jar chopped jalapenos
1 (7 ounce) can diced green chiles
12 oz (1 1/2 c) shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together sour cream, mayonnaise, corn, green onions, jalapeno chile peppers, green chile peppers and cheese.  Ready to serve!

Submitter: 
FrogsMom
Description: 
Serve this spicy dip with tortilla chips, fresh vegetables, or both!  This recipe makes enough for a large party.Modified from: Spicy Corn Dip
