Title:
Lime Salmon Poorboy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Salmon Fillet/steak
2 Limes juiced and zested
2 Tbs. Olive Oil
Salt and Pepper
4 Hoagie Rolls
Slaw

Directions:
Thaw salmon, if frozen. Rinse salmon; pat dry with paper towels.
In a small bowl, combine lime zest, lime juice, salt, pepper and olive oil. Marinate the salmon fillets for approx 1/2 hour.
Make a tin foil pouch for grilling salmon. Grill for 15 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork. Or you can use a pan on the stove top.
Serve salmon on hoagie rolls topped with Slaw. Makes 4 sandwiches.
Slaw: In a medium bowl, stir together juice of one lime, 2 tablespoon olive oil or salad oil, 1/4 c. red wine vinegar, salt and pepper to taste.  Add 1 cup shredded/finely slivered green apple, 2 cup shredded green cabbage, 1/4 onion diced, 1/4 c. chopped fresh cilantro, 1 jalapeno seeded and diced, 1-1 1/2 tsp. freshly grated ginger. Toss mixture to combine. Cover and chill for up to 1 hour, stirring once. Makes about 4 cups
