Title:
Fresh Tomato Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Fresh Firm Tomatoes
1 Small Yellow Onion
1 Bunch of Cilantro
3 Cloves of Garlic
1 Fresh Jalepeno
Juice of 2 Limes
heavy salt
1/2C canned rinsed black beans (optional)
1/2 C thawed frozen corn (optional)

Directions:
Cut tops of cilantro off stems
Slice jalepeno in half lengthwise and remove seeds and veins
Core and cut tomatoes in half
Process onion, cilantro tops, garlic, jalepeno and lime juice in food processor until desired texture
Add tomatoes and salt and pulse a few times until tomato pieces are diced to desired size.
If adding beans and corn, stir alltogether and serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
jalexandropoulos
Description: 
Fresh tomatoes, onion, jalepeno, garlic, cilantro and lime are a few of the fresh ingredients in this awesome salsa.  You will NEVER have jarred salsa again!
