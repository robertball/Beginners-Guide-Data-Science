Title:
Crispy-Crusted Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (2.8 oz) French-fried onions, coursely crushed
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tsp spicy brown mustard
4 thinly sliced top round steaks

Directions:
Place the broilers rack 8-10 inches from the heat source and preheat the broiler.
Place the onions in a shallow dish.
In another shallow dish mix the oil and mustard.
Dip the steakd into the oil mixture and then the onions, coating completely with each.
Place the steaks on a rimmed baking sheet and broil for 3-4 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

Submitter: 
Beth Mower Dodge
Description: 
A diabetic-friendly steak that truly satisfies. Can also be cooked on a grill.
