Title:
Basil Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/421209.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh basil leaves
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup pine nuts, toasted
3 TBS minced garlic (can substitute fresh)
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Heat 1/2 cup pine nuts over medium heat until lightly browned, remove from heat and set aside.
Combine all ingredients (including pine nuts) in a food processor and process until reaching desired consistency (about 3-4 minutes).
Remove pesto from the food processor and use in your favorite recipe that calls for it!

Submitter: 
SavedByGrace
Description: 
An easy to prepare, absolutely delicious pesto sauce made with fresh basil, toasted pine nuts, garlic, parmesan cheese, and olive oil. This recipe is the reason I grow tons of basil every summer!
