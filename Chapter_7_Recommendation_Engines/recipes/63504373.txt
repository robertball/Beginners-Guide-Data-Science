Title:
Italian Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12c. Water
8 cubes Chicken Bulion
1/2t. Garlic Powder
1T. Onion Powder
1 lb. Mild Italian Sausage
1 lb. Hot Italian Sausage
2 lb. Bacon
7 Russet Potatoes
1 bunch Kale
1c. Heavy Cream

Directions:
Heat water, Bulion, garlic, and onion powder to a boil. Brown sausage, dice and brown bacon. Drain all grease, and add to broth. Cut potatoes in half long ways, cut into silver dollar thick slices, and add to broth. Peal kale from stems, and add to broth letting it reduce. Make sure potatoes are soft. Add cream, and heat until warm stirring the entire time to blend.
