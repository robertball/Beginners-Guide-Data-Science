Title:
Refreshing Coconut Lime Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/674017.jpg
Ingredients:
15 oz coconut cream
3/4 cup coconut water
1/2 cup lime juice
1 tbsp lime zest
1/2 tsp coconut extract (optional)

Directions:
Stir together all ingredients in a bowl. Chill in refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
Pour mixture into an ice cream maker and prepare according to manufactures instructions. Freeze sorbet until firm. If you don't have an ice cream maker, you can freeze sorbet in a shallow bowl or dish overnight.
