Title:
Grandma's Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
one 3-4 pound brisket
1 cup ketchup
1 package dry onion soup mix
12 ounces gingerale
carrots and sliced onions (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Combine ketchup, soup mix and gingerale. Pour over brisket.  Add carrots and onions if wanted.  Cover, cook 2 hours.  Uncover and cook 1 hour more.  Slice and serve.

Submitter: 
JodyVTPT
Description: 
My grandmother used to make this every year for get-togethers.  Who would've known that it was this easy?
