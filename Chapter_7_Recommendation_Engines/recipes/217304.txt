Title:
Lemon Blueberry Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F6852911.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chicken breast, cooked and cubed
¼ cup sliced green onion
⅓ cup diced celery
¼ cup red bell pepper, diced
1/2 cup Low-fat Lemon Blueberry Dressing
1 (12 ounce) bag DOLE(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Greener Selection

Directions:
Gently blend all ingredients together and serve over greens.

Submitter: 
Dole
Description: 
Crunchy celery and sweet blueberry dressing flavors this chicken salad set atop mixed salad greens.
