Title:
Honey-Peanut Butter Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c coconut oil
2-4 T raw honey
3/4 c powdered peanut butter
3/4 c almond milk
1/4-1/2 tsp vanilla
salt to taste

Directions:
blend/whip all ingredients in blender
refrigerate to firm

Submitter:

whipped peanut butter honey frosting
