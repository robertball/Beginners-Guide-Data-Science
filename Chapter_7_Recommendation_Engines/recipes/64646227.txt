Title:
LFT DTox: Greek Spaghetti Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 spaghetti squash
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1.5 cups chopped tomatoes
2 tbsp. fresh basil, chopped
2 tbsp. black olives, sliced
Walnuts (optional)

Directions:
1.  Place squash in a very large pan of boiling water, cook for 20-30 min.
2. Remove squash from water when it pierces easily with a fork.
3. While squash is boiling, heat olive oil over medium heat in a skillet.
4. Add onion to the skillet and sauté until soft.
5. Add garlic to the skillet and sauté another 3 minutes.
6. Stir tomatoes into the onion and garlic and cook for a few minutes until heated through.
7. Using oven mitts, cut the squash lengthwise and scoop out the seeds.
8. Using a long fork to pull the strands free and place them in a bowl; toss with sautéed veggies, basil and olives (add Walnuts if desired).
