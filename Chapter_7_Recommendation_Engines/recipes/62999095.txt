Title:
Entenmann's Crumb Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 3/4 cup flour
1 3/4 cup sugar
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 lb butter (1 stick), softened
2 eggs
2/3 cup milk
********
2 reserved cups of flour mixture
1/4 lb butter (1 stick), cold
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tsp cinnamon
1 pinch salt
confectioner's sugar (for dusting)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together in a large bowl the dry ingredients, set aside 2 cups for topping.
Add butter, eggs, milk to flour mixture in large bowl and mix well.
Grease and flour 9x13 inch baking pan (Pam spray with flour).  Spread batter in prepared pan.
For Topping:  Take the 2 cups reserved flour mixture and add to the topping ingredients.  Use pastry blender to make crumb topping.  Cover entire cake with crumb mixture.
Bake 30 minutes.
Cool on wire rack, dust with powdered sugar.
