Title:
Grandma bradley's Blue ribbion rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Butter
1/3 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp. salt
2 Tbsp. Instant potato flakes
1 Cup hot water
2 pkg's Dry yeast
1/2 cup lukewarm water
4 1/2 Cups sifted flour

Directions:
Combine butter, sugar,salt, potaoto flakes and hot water in a bowl.  Cool till it is lukewarm. Sprinkle yeast on lukewarm water, stir to dissolve. Add yeast and 1Cup flour to potaoto mixture. Beat with electric mixer until combined. Add enough dough to make a soft dough. Put in a greased bowl. Let rise 1 1/4 hour. Turn dough onto lightly floured surface. Divide dough into 24 equal pieces. Shape into ball, place in  baking pans. let rise until doubled. About 45 minutes. Bake for 20 minutes.
