Title:
Parmesan Stuffed Potato Skins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Baked potatos - halved lengthwise
3 tbsps extra virgin olive oil
1/2 cup milk
3 tbsps butter
3/4 cup parmesan cheese
5 oz sliced salami
1/4 cup of breadcrumbs
3/4 cups of sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Line baking sheet with parchment paper
Place potatos cut side down on sheet
Brush skins with 1 tbsp oil
Bake until tender (30 minutes)
Cut in half lengthwise - let cool slightly
Scoop out potato into medium pot
Leave 1/4 inch thick shells
Place shells cut side up
Brush with 2 tbsp olive oil
Add milk, butter to pot of potato innards and mash until smooth
Stir in 1/2 of the parmesan and 1/4 of salami
In a small bowl, toss remaing parmesan, salami, bread crumbs
Press mixture into potato skins
Bake until crisp and golden (20-25 minutes)
Reheat mash potato mixture
Mound onto each potato skin
Top with sour cream
Serve warm
