Title:
Fruit Popsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg can of crushed pineapple
3 very ripe bananas
1 can 7 up or diet 7 up
1 6oz can of oj

Directions:
Place all in a blender and blend till mostly smooth and place inside of molds or small dixie cups and freeze. wait 5 hrs then enjoy .

Submitter: 
jessmomof3
Description: 
I got this from my mother in law when I was running a daycare from home and this is one of the biggest hits i ever had . real fruit and a little tang to your popsicles.
