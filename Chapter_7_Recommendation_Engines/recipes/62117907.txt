Title:
Easy Tomato Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 and 1/2 cups uncooked sea shell pasta
2 teaspoons salt
1 8 ounce can plain tomato sauce
3/4 cup fat free sour cream
1 6 ounce can tuna, drained
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh basil (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and lightly oil an 8 by 8 inch casserole dish.
Boil pasta with the 2 teaspoons salt.
Drain pasta and return to pan it was cooked in.
Stir in tomato sauce, fat free sour cream, tuna and garlic powder.
Spoon into casserole dish and cover with foil.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes or until hot.
Remove foil cover and sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and fresh basil.
Return to oven for about 5 minutes or until the cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
The Bunny Chef
Description: 
This is a slightly different tuna noodle casserole that I came up with. It's a winner in my book because it's inexpensive, healthy, easy and yummy! If you don't have fresh basil, just leave it out.
