Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 cup regular no sugar added apple sauce
1 1/2 cups white sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
3/4 tsp nutmeg
8 oz can crushed pineapple
2 cups grated zucchini
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
raisins (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease loaf pan or muffin tins.
Beat eggs, apple sauce, sugar and vanilla until thick.
Stir in flour, salt, baking powder, soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, pineapple and zucchini (and nuts or raisins) together in a bowl until well combined.  Pour batter into prepared pans.
Bake for 40 to 60 minutes, or until tester inserted in the center comes out clean.  Cool in pan on rack for 20 minutes.  Remove bread from pan, and completely cool.

Submitter: 
Jeannine Kuck
Description: 
Really, really good and moist- my kids eat it as quickly as I can make it.  Bread will freeze well, and keep in refrigerator for weeks.Modified from: Mom's Zucchini Bread
