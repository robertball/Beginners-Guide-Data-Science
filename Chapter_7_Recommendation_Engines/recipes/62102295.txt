Title:
Elegant Open-faced  Smoked Salmon Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bread (preferably a scali bread)
2 tablespoons butter, softened
1 tsp finely chopped scallion
1 tsp finely chopped parsely
1/2 tsp bottled minced garlic
8 slices smoked salmon
1/2 cucumber, thinly sliced
1/2 lemon

Directions:
Toast the bread until lightly golden and crispy
In a small bowl combine softened butter, scallion, parsely, and garlic.
Spread each piece of bread with 1/2 tablespoon of the herbed butter. Layer with 2 slices of smoked salmon, followed by the cucumber.
Top the sandwhiches with a squeeze of lemon juice. Cut on an angle and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Hugatunity
Description: 
These sandwiches are simple enough for even the most inexperienced cook, yet delicious and elegant enough to be served to a special someone. Serve with a good wine and a salad for a light dinner that leaves plenty of room for a decadent dessert!
