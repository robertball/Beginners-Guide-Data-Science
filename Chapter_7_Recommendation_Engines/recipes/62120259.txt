Title:
ZEB Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C butter, oil, OR fat
1/2 C flour
3 T bacon drip
4 chopped onions
2 lbs pork chops, cubed
salt
season all
garlic salt
1 small head of cabbage, sliced
1 small head of lettuce, sliced
2 bunches of green onion, chopped
2 bunches of celery tops, chopped
1 can spinach
1 package frozen mustard greens
1 package turnip greens

Directions:
Season pork with salt, garlic salt and season all.
Set aside.
Make a roux with melted butter and flour.
Add onions cooked in drippings.
Now add pork.
Add cabbage, lettuce, green onions.
Cook until tender.
Reserve spinach liquid.
Add spinach, mustard greens, and turnip greens.
Cook slowly, using liquid to keep it from sticking.
Enjoy!
