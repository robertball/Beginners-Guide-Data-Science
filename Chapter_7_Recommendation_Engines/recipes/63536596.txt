Title:
Sausage, Potato and Kale Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/965953.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Kale or swiss chard or spinach
3 cups Potatos, cubed
1 medium Onion, Chopped
1 pound Italian Sausage
1/2 teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes (more To Taste)
2 cups Low Sodium Chicken Broth
2 cups Whole Milk
4 cups Half-and-half
1/2 tsp Dried Oregano
1/2 Black Pepper

Directions:
Picked Over, Cleaned, and tar kale into bit sized pieces and set aside.
In a large pot, crumble and brown the Italian sausage. Drain as much as the fat as you can. Stir in the red pepper flakes, oregano, chicken broth, milk,  half-and-half and potatoes. Simmer for 30 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Give it a taste and adjust seasonings as needed. Stir in the kale and simmer an additional 10-15 minutes, then serve.
