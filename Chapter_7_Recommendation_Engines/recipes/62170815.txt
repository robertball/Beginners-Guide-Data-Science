Title:
Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 15 oz can whole corn drained
2 tomatoes chopped small
2 avocados chopped small
1/2 onion chopped small
3 tbsp lime juice
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
1 tbsp salt

Directions:
Combine all the vegetables, add remaining ingredients and pour over the top. Store in the fridge in an airtight container.
When I want more spice I add Smoky Hungarian Paprika and chili lime powder. Or Substitute garilc oil for the olive oil.

Submitter: 
Kathryn Weidenheimer
Description: 
Simple and delicious, eat it alone or mix it with something. Family loves it
