Title:
Teddy Bear Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup 100% Apple Juice
1/2 cup Hot Water
1 tsp fresh lemon juice
1 tsp honey

Directions:
Combine ingredients and shake or stir. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Kathleen
Description: 
A drink to help soothe sick little ones...only add honey for children over 1 year old. My daughter calls this Teddy Bear juice because of our honey bear. Great for a sore throat...and people who don't enjoy hot tea.
