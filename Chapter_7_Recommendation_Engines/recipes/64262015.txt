Title:
Zucchini noodle casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 average length green zuccuni
1 jar of pasta sauce
1 7 oz container of 2% greek yogurt or equivilent of sour cream
1/4 cup minced garlic
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
italian seasoning

Directions:
preheat oven to 425 degrees
using a spiralizer, shred zucchini into noodles
in a frying pan, sautee 1 tablespoon of olive oil and a teaspoon of garlic and add as much zucchini to fill the pan.  Cook until zucchini is soft and slightly browned.  Continue each batch of zucchini noodles with oil and garlic until it has all been cooked and put into a 9x13 pan. place the marinara into the pan and whisk the yogurt or sour cream on low heat along with salt, pepper, and italian seasoning to taste.  Once the sauce has come together, add to the zucchini noodles and top with mozzarella cheese and more italian seasoning.  Bake until the cheese is bubbling.
