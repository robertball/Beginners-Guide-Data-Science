Title:
Zucchini Parmesan Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup Barilla Plus elbow pasta
2 medium zucchinis with seeds scraped out - chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1 can (approx. 14.5 oz) diced organic tomatoes
1 tsp salt
1.5 tsp dried basil
1 tsp dried oregano
1 cup fresh Parmesan shredded

Directions:
Sautee (on medium for about five minutes) onion and zucchini in grape seed oil until starting to get soft but not brown.  Add can of drained diced tomatoes.  Add salt and dried basil and oregano.  Add cooked pasta with about 1/4 cup of pasta water to onion mixture.  Let that cook on medium for about five minutes.  Spoon into bowls and add about 1/4 cup shredded parmesan to each serving.

Submitter: 
Jill Stevens
Description: 
Yummy, light, fresh flavorful vegetarian pasta dish.  The fresh parmesan adds and extra special touch.
