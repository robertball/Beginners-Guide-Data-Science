Title:
Becky's Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter
1/2 large onion, chopped
3 celery stalks, chopped
1 cup peeled carrots, chopped
bay leaf
2 tbsp flour
10 cups water
11 chicken bouillon cubes
1 tbsp poultry seasoning
1 tbsp dried parsley
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 lb small whole wheat pasta
1/2 lb cooked chicken, shredded

Directions:
Over medium heat in a large pot, melt the butter. Add the onion, celery, and carrots cooking until soft. Add the flour and cook off for about a minute. Add the bay leaf to the pot. Slowly add water then the chicken cubes and then the poultry seasoning, parsley, and pepper. Stir. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally to dissolve the cubes. Add 1/2 lb pasta to the pot cooking until pasta al dente. Remove the soup from the heat, add the chicken and stir.

Submitter: 
BECKY M
Description: 
A nice hot soup is perfect for those chilly autumn/winter days or to alleviate some of those symptoms from a nasty cold. This is a good, healthy, comfort soup!
