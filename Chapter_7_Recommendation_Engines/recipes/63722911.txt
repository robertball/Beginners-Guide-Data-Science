Title:
Basic Chipotle Chicken Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds boneless skinless chicken breast
1 can chipotles in adobo sauce
1 bottle dark beer
1 tbsp olive oil
corn tortillas

Directions:
prep
Place chicken in sandwich bag or bowl pour chipotles and 1/2 beer over chicken, let marinate at least 1 hour(the longer the better).
cook
heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat
pour chicken and marinade in skillet
cook 20-30 min or until internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165 degrees adding remaining beer as liquid evaporates.
shred chicken
serve
on corn tortillas

Submitter: 
Omi Longmire
Description: 
This is VERY basic. My husband isn't a fan of veggies in things so if you add onions and peppers even better! It can get verry spicy so this isnt a dish for bland food lovers(even the mild ways to cook it have a bite)
