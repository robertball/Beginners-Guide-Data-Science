Title:
Mom's Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz thin spaghetti noodles
1 T olive oil
1 t salt
Italian tomato sauce
1/8 cup red wine
1/8 cup Parmesan Cheese
1 t minced garlic

Directions:
Bring 2 quarts water, olive oil, and salt to a boil in large pan.  Boil spaghetti noodles as directed on package.  When done cooking, drain noodles.
While spaghetti boils, combine Tomato sauce, wine, garlic, and Parmesan cheese in sauce pan.  Warm on the stove until bubbles, then simmer until noodles done.
Serve noodles with sauce.

Submitter: 
Marianne Bishop Turner
Description: 
Easy spaghetti for those nights you don't want to cook!
