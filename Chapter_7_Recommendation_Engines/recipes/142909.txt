Title:
Strawberry Orange Coconut Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/849400.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups hulled strawberries
1 orange, peeled
1/2 cup coconut milk
4 Ice cubes (optional)

Directions:
Place the strawberries, orange, coconut milk, and ice cubes in a blender. Cover and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Niki
Description: 
This drink is great for a morning shake, but refreshing anytime.
