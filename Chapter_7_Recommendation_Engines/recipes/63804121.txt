Title:
White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans (15 oz) great northern beans, drained
2 lbs cubed cooked chicken breast
1 jar (15 oz) alfredo sauce
1 can (14 oz) chicken broth
2 cans (4 oz)chopped green chilies
2 cans (15 oz) gold & white corn
1 c (4 oz) shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 c (4 oz) shredded pepper jack cheese
1 c sour cream
1 tsp minced garlic
1 tbsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp white pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Put in crockpot. Cover and cook on low 3-4 hours, stirring occasionally.
