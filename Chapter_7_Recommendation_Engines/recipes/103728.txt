Title:
Chilie Pork Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982498.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground pork
1 large onion, sliced
3 tablespoons chili powder
3 large cloves garlic, minced
1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, undrained
1/2 cup NAKANO® Seasoned Rice Vinegar - Original
1/4 cup water
8 (7 inch) corn tortillas, warmed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
Optional garnishes: diced tomatoes, chopped cilantro, shredded cheese, salsa verde

Directions:
Heat salt in a large non-stick skillet over high heat.
Add ground pork, onion, chili powder, garlic, and pepper. Cook and stir 8 minutes.
Add undrained pinto beans, Nakano(R) Seasoned Rice Vinegar - Original, and 1/4 cup water. Cook and stir 10 minutes.
Wrap ground pork in tortillas. Serve with garnishes, as desired.

Submitter: 
NAKANO®
Description: 
Ground pork is browned with Mexican spices and pinto beans before being served in corn tortillas. Top with your favorite toppings like lettuce, tomato, cheese, cilantro and salsa!
