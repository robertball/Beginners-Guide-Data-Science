Title:
Rosy Grape Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Cranberry Juice
3/4 Cup Grape Juice- from frozen or premixed juice.
1 pkg. powdered fruit pectin
3 1/4 Cups Sugar
5-6  half pint jars, serilized with lids
Parafin for sealing jars

Directions:
In a large heavy pot, combine the cranberry juice, grape juice and powdered pectin until smooth and blended thoroughly.
Bring to a boil over high heat, constantly stirring.
Continue to boil and stir for 1 minute.
Remove from the heat and skim off foam quickly.
Pour jelly quickly into hot stirilized jars.  Wipe the rims of the jars with a clean damp towel.
Let the jars stand for 1 hour then cover with melted wax to seal.
Let stand for wax to harden then cover with lids. You may add a holiday fabric or paper cover and tie with ribbon.
Refrigerate or freeze.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Use pretty glass jars for this vibrant colored full flavored jellie all will enjoy on their toast or muffins. Makes a wonderfully delicious and easy hostess gift for the holidays or for any fall/winter occasion
