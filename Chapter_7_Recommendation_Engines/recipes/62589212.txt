Title:
Crock Pot Italian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken breasts
8 oz Italian dressing
Black pepper
1 can chicken broth
1 can cream of chicken soup
8 oz cream cheese

Directions:
Combine chicken and dressing in crock pot and cook all day on low.
Shred chicken and drain dressing.
Add soup, broth, pepper, and cream cheese last hour before serving.  Turn heat up to high if needed to melt cream cheese.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
Serve over egg noodles or mashed potatoes.
