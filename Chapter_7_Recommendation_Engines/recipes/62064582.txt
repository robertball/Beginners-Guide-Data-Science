Title:
Thick & Creamy Pumpkin Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. pkgs softened cream cheese
1 15 oz. can pumpkin puree
2 cups confectioners sugar
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1 tsp. ground ginger

Directions:
1. Blend cream cheese and confectioners sugar together in a large mixing bowl until smooth.
2. Blend in cinnamon and ginger until color is even.
3. Blend in pumpkin until thick and creamy.
May be eaten immediately or chilled before serving.

Submitter: 
SOMEONESWT
Description: 
Great pumpkin dip for the holidays and everyday.  Can be served with ginger snaps, graham crackers, apples, pears and more!
