Title:
Charles' Homestyle Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Chili Grind Ground Chuck
1 lb Ground Pork
5 Garlic Cloves, minced
1 Onion large, diced
1 tsp Salt
1T Cumin
4T Chili Powder
2C Water
1 can (20 oz)Crushed Tomatoes
2 Jalepenos, seeded/chopped (optional for hot)
Toppings if desired (cheese, onions, fritos, salsa, sour cream, guacamole, etc.)

Directions:
In large stock pot, brown meat over medium-high heat. Add jalepenos (optional, and diced onion, cook until onions are clear.  Add spices, water, and tomatoes. Bring to boil, lower heat to simmer. Cook uncovered for one hour, stirring occassionally.

Submitter: 
Cindy
Description: 
After many years of trying one wild chili recipe after the other, my husband finally developed this oh so easy, oh so delicious chili recipe.  You can make it as hot or mild as you like and it still is great.
