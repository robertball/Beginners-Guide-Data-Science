Title:
Easy Banana Bread Snack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup instant oatmeal
1/3 banana, mashed
Splenda
nutmeg
1 egg white
water, as much as needed

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a bowl.
Microwave until it reaches desired consistency, stirring at least every 30 seconds.
