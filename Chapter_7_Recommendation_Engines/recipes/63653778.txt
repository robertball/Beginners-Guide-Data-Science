Title:
Green bean & Green Lentil Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb raw green (pole / string) beans, ends trimmed
3c cooked brown rice
3c cooked green lentils
1/3 lb sweet green Italian frying peppers
curry powder
wasabi powder
tamari (low-sodium soy) sauce
hot pepper flakes

Directions:
Gather the green beans into bunches and shave of pieces 1/8" long or shorter.
Similarly, slice the peppers into pieces about 1 to 2 inches long and about 1/8" wide.
Combine the green beans and peppers in a large microwavable bowl.  Heat in the microwave for 5 minutes.
Add in rice and lentils.  Add all seasonings to taste.  Stir well.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
This Thai-inspired dish makes use of the crisp flavor of mostly-raw green beans.
