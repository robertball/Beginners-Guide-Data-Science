Title:
Scottish Salmon, Tattie and Pasta Broth Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1tbsp Olive Oil
600g/21oz fillets Salmon - deboned & roughly cut
350g/12oz Tatties (Potatoes) - cut into wee cubes
150g/5oz Baby Spinach
1.3 litres Vegetable Stock
1 Chilli - finely chopped
1 Onion - chopped
2 cloves garlic - finely chopped
200g/7oz thin Macaroni

Directions:
In a large pan heat the oil and add the onion and cook for 1-2 minutes. Add the garlic, tatties and the chilli. Stir and add the vegetable stock.
Simmer until the tatties are almost soft.
In a separate pan fully cook the macaroni in boiling water then drain the water.
Add the spinach and cooked macaroni to the macaroni pan and mix well. Add this mixture to the tatties and vegetables, then stir in the fish.
Simmer for about 5 minutes or until the fish is cooked.

A Scottish comfort broth with a difference. Will warm the cockles of your heart on every occasion Delicious with warmed rolls and butter.
