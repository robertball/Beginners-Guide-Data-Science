Title:
Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
Marinade
1pint - Buttermilk
1/2t - Hot Sauce
1/8t - Ground Black Pepper
1/8t - Salt
Breading
2c - All-Purpose Non-Bleached Flour
1 1/2T - Ground Black Pepper
1T - Salt
1/2T - Paprika
1/4t - Ground Sage
1/4t - Ground Cayenne Red Pepper
1/8t - Ground Cumin
Oil
Peanut Oil
2cloves - Garlic
1/2t - Dried Thyme
1/2t - Rose Mary

Directions:
Wash and trim then pat chicken dry. Soak chick for at least 1/2 hour (the longer the better) in the buttermilk marinade. Pull from marinade and let excess drip off, then cover in flour mixture (repeat for extra crispy). Let sit in flour until dried on (usually about 5min.) while oil heats up to about 350 degrees.

Delicious fried chicken cooked best in a deep fryer.
