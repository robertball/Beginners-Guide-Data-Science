Title:
Katrina's Favorite Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3594203.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c rolled oats
1 c chopped pecans
1 c chopped almonds
1/3 c coconut oil, melted
1/2 c honey
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 c Craisens
1/4 c dried cherries
1/4 c dried blueberries

Directions:
Melt coconut oil, add honey and cinnamon and set aside.
Mix together rolled oats and chopped nuts. Pour honey mixture on dry ingredients and toss to coat.
Spread on large cookie sheet covered with parchment paper.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Stir and bake another 10-15 minutes.
When cooled, add in dried fruit. Store in an airtight container,

Submitter: 
KKraft
Description: 
Feel free to change up types of nuts and dried fruit for variety. Eat as cereal or snack.
