Title:
Chicken Taco Chili - slow cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts (1 lb)
1 8oz can green enchilada sauce
1 8oz can rotelli diced tomatoes
1 diced onion
1 envelope taco seasonings
1 15oz can great northern beans (or any white beans)
2 Tbsp corn starch or tapioca slurry

Directions:
Add all except last 2 ingredients to crock pot
Cook for 3 hours on high
Add last 2 ingredients & cook on high another hour
Serve in bowl with shredded cheese and toasted wraps
