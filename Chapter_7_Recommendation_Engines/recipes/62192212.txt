Title:
Veggie Chicken Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
3-4 cooked chicken breasts, cut into pieces
1 (16 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
2 cups leftover cooked rice
2/3 cup milk
1 cup crushed buttery round crackers
shredded cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl combine the soup, chicken, cooked vegetables, cooked rice and water. Mix well and spread mixture in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Sprinkle crushed cracker crumbs on top and bake in the preheated oven for about 15 to 20 minutes. Top with cheese at the last 5 min of baking.  Let cool 10 minutes and serve.
