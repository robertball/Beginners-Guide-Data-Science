Title:
Buckeye Bash Buffalo Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz rotelle pasta
1 cup ranch dressing*
1/2 cup buffalo wing sauce*
1/2 small red onion, diced fine
1 green pepper, diced
1 red pepper, diced
1 cup carrots, diced
1 cup celery, diced
1 cup grape tomatoes, quartered
1 tablespoon celery leaf
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon white pepper
2/3 cup crumbled blue cheese

Directions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add pasta and cook until al dente.
2. Meanwhile, mix the ranch dressing and wing sauce in a small bowl. Set aside.
3. Once the pasta is cooked, drain and rinse with cold water.
4. Place the pasta, vegetables, seasonings in a serving bowl.  Add dressing to coat.
5. Add the crubled blue cheese and mix well.
6.  Refrigerate until serving time.
