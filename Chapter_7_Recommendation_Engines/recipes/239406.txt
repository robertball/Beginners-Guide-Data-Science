Title:
Garlicky Salt and Pepper Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1473994.jpg
Ingredients:
4 medium Yukon Gold potatoes, cut into 1/2- to 1/4-inch-thick wedges
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon olive oil, or more to taste
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon Diamond CrystalÂ® Fine Sea Salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Heat butter, olive oil, and garlic in a saucepan over low heat until butter is melted; stir in Diamond Crystal(R) Fine Sea Salt and pepper.  Toss potatoes in butter mixture until evenly coated. Roast to desired doneness, 30 to 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Becky
Description: 
Buttery, salty fries that complement just about any main course.  You can add chopped herbs to the butter/oil mixture for another flavor variation.
