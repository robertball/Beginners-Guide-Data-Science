Title:
Spicy Watermelon Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups of cubed watermelon
2 Serrano peppers
1/2 cup prepared salsa verde , like Herdez
1 tsp taco powder
Salt

Directions:
Place 1 whole Serrano pepper and 3 cups of watermelon in a blender on the liquefy setting. Blend until liquified. Set aside.
Finely chop the other Serrano pepper and toss with remaining chopped watermelon. Sprinkle with taco powder. And add salsa Verde. Mix well.
Pour watermelon sauce over mixture until coated. Add to desired consistency. Asd salt to taste. Refridgerate until ready to serve.
