Title:
Curried Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 Lrg russet or sweet potato, peeled,chopped
1 Lrg onion, chopped
1 Lrg carrot, peeled, chopped
2 Tbsp curry powder
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
6 Cups low sodium chicken stock
1 28oz can diced tomatoes
2 Cups lentils, rinsed, drained

Directions:
Heat oil in heavy large pot over med-high heat; Add onion, potato & carrot and saute until veggies begin to soften (about 5 mins); Mix in curry powder and cayenne until fragrant (about 30 seconds); Add broth, tomatoes and lentils and bring to boil; Reduce heat, cover and simmer until lentils are tender (about 45 mins).  Season with salt & pepper and serve.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
This super healthy, delicious hearty soup is great on a cold day as a meal with a piece of garlic toast! Yum!!
Note - this soup is easy to freeze and gets better after each day it sits in the fridge.
