Title:
Yogurt Cereal Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups corn flakes
3/4 flour
1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2cup butter or margarine
1 cup flavoured yogurt (banana/strawberry etc..)
1 egg slightly beaten
2 tablespoons flour
Preheat oven to 350F

Directions:
1) Combine cereal, 3/4 cup flour, sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl.
2) Cut in butter or margarine until coarse crumbs form.
3) Press 1/2 the mixture firmly into the bottom of a greased 8 inch square pan.
4) Mix yogurt, egg and 2 tablespoons flour in another small bowl.
5) Spread over cereal mixture in pan and then sprinkle the remaining cereal mixture on top
6) Bake for 30 minutes at 350 F or until golden brown; cut into bars when cooled.

Submitter:

Description: 
Soft yogurt layer surrounded by crunchy cornflake layers. Makes a great snack or breakfast on the go! Courtesy of Stonyfield Farms Yogurt.
