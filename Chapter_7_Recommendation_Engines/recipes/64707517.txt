Title:
30 Minute Homemade Sauerkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cabbage
1 cup white vinegar
1/4 cup salt
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Coarsely cut one head of cabbage
Place in cooker
Add vinegar
Add salt
Add water
Stir and cover
Lock the lid and close vent.
Set cooker to Chicken/Meat setting (15 minutes)
Allow pressure to come down on its own.
Voila!!  Sauerkraut!  Now, you can eat it hot, can it, or just put in canning jars with plastic lids and place in refrigerator.
Who said you had to wait weeks for it in a crock?!

Submitter: 
Sally Schuyler Wieringa
Description: 
Put your Power Pressure Cooker XL or Instant Pot to work and make an awesome kraut in no time!
