Title:
Special K Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup White Sugar
1 cup Light Karo Syrup
1 1/2 cups Creamy Peanut Butter
6 cups Special K cereal

Directions:
Cook sugar and Karo syrup over medium heat until dissolved (not long). Add peanut butter.
Grease a 9x13 pan and put in Special K cereal.
Pour peanut butter mixture over Special K ceral making sure it is all covered with mixture, stirring it together if necessary.
Let cool and cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Jasmine Gonzalez
Description: 
Peanut Buttery goodness mixed with Special K. Be forwarned this is not for those on a diet!
