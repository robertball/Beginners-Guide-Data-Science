Title:
The BEST Black Bean Salad with Feta Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1 lemon, juiced
1 lime, juiced
2 teaspoons Spicy Mustard
1 tablespoon ground Cumin
3 Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
3/4 cup oil (half EVOO, half Canola
Salad:
2 15 ounce cans of black beans, rinsed and drained
1 15 ounce can of corn, drained
5 green onions, chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro
Garnish:
Crumbled Feta Cheese

Directions:
Whisk all dressing ingredients in small bowl.  Set aside.  Lightly mix all salad ingredients in a large bowl.  Pour dressing over salad to taste, you may choose to not use all of the dressing.  Refridgerate at least 3 hours.  Garnish with feta cheese right before serving.

Submitter: 
Rachel Love
Description: 
Colorful salad with a zip!  The feta and cilantro make this recipe!  This has become one of my favorite side dishes.
