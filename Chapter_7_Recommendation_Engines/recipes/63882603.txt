Title:
Blueberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1 tbsp corn starch
4 cups fresh blueberries (or thawed frozen)
1 tsp lemon juice
1 cup all purpose flour
1 tbsp sugar
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
3 tbsp crisco/shortening
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 400F(200C)
Blend 1/2 cup sugar and cornstarch in medium saucepan. Stir in blueberries and lemon juice. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils. Boil and stir 1 minute.
Pour into ungreased 2 quart casserole dish. Keep fruit mixture hot in oven while preparing topping.
Measure flour, 1 tbsp sugar, the baking powder and salt into a bowl. Add shortening and milk. Cut through shortening six times and mix until dough forms a ball.
Drop dough by six spoonfuls or so onto hot fruit mix.
Bake 25-30 minutes until topping is golden. Serve warm and with cream if desired.
