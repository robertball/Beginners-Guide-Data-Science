Title:
Easy Chicken and Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1508210.jpg
Ingredients:
6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/4 cup teriyaki
1/2 cup soy sauce
cornstarch (to thicken)
water
1 head broccoli
1 head cauliflower
4 carrots
1 packet Swanson Chicken Flavor Boost
2 cups chicken broth
3 cups minute rice
2 tsp. olive oil
Ground Ginger to taste

Directions:
1. Cut chicken, broccoli, cauliflower and carrots into bite-sized pieces.
2. In large saucepan over med-high heat saut√© the chicken with the olive oil until browned, then pour in the teriyaki, soy sauce, chicken broth and flavor boost packet.  Add ground ginger to the mix to taste. Let simmer for about 10-15 minutes.
3. In a separate pot, steam the vegetables.
4. Cook minute rice according to package directions.
5. Combine a small amount of cornstarch with water, slowly pour into chicken mixture and stir until thickened to desired consistency.
6.  Serve veggies over rice, then top with chicken/sauce mixture or combine veggies with chicken and sauce if you prefer your veggies to be saucier and then serve over rice.

A quick to put together chicken and broccoli dish that will have your family coming back for more.
