Title:
Cookie Dough Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/785647.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 8-ounce block of cream cheese, softened
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 cup powdered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3/4 cup mini chocolate chips
Reeses pieces - to sprinkle

Directions:
Cream the cream cheese and butter together until smooth.
Mix in powdered sugar, brown sugar and vanilla with the mixer on low.
Stir in the mini chocolate chips.
Garnish with Reeses Pieces. Serve with nilla wafers or graham crackers.
