Title:
Cream of Pimiento Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/769887.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (4 oz.) jars diced pimiento, undrained
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
2 (14.5 oz.) cans chicken broth
3 cups half-and-half
1 T. grated onion
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. hot sauce
Garnish: chopped fresh chives

Directions:
Process pimiento in a blender or food processor until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides; set aside.
Melt 1/4 cup butter in heavy saucepan over low heat.
Add 1/3 cup flour, and stir until mixture is smooth.
Cook, stirring constantly, 1 minute.
Add chicken broth and half-and-half to flour mixture; cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is slightly thickened and bubbly.
Stir in reserved pimiento, onion, salt, and hot sauce; cook over low heat, stirring constantly until thoroughly heated.
Cover and chill 8 hours.
Cook soup over medium heat 8-10 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
Garnish, if desired.
