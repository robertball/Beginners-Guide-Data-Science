Title:
Super Easy Slow-Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/340835.jpg
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
4 cups chicken broth
1 8oz can tomatoes and green pepper
1 cup corn
1 pack taco seasoning (1.5oz)
2 cups tortilla strips (check the salad aisle)
1/2 cup shredded cheese ("Mexican" variety)

Directions:
Pour chicken broth, taco seasoning, corn, and tomatoes into slow cooker and stir.
Place chicken breasts in broth.
Cook on "low" setting for about 8 hours.
Shred chicken inside slow cooker and stir.
Pour soup into serving bowls, then sprinkle in cheese and tortilla strips.
