Title:
BANANA PUDDING DESSERT Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 PKGS PEPPERRIDGE FARM CHESSMAN COOKIES
6-8 BANANA'S SLICED
2 CUPS MILK
1 (8 OZ) PKG CREAM CHEESE (SOFT)
1 (14 OZ) CAN SWEETENED CONDENSED MILK
1 (12 OZ) CONTAINER OF COOL WHIP

Directions:
LINE THE BOTTOM OF A 9 X 13 PAN WITH 1 BAG OF THE COOKIES & LAYERS THE BANANA'S ON TOP OF COOKIES
COMBINE MILK & PUDDING & BLEND WELL
IN ANOTHER BOWL CREAM CHEESE & CONSENSENED MILK UNTIL SMOOTH
FOLD THE WHIPPED TOPPING INTO THE CREAM CHEESE MIX AND THEN ADD PUDDING MIXTURE & STIR TILL WELL BLENDED.
POUR THE MIXTURE OVER THE BANANA'S & COVER WITH REMAINING COOKIES & REFRIGERATE AT LEAST 3 HRS
