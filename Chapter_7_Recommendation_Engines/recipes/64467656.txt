Ingredients:
1 package refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
2 cups chopped kale
2 cups vegetable oil
garlic powder
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Heat vegetable oil on stove in sauce pan ( you can also use a deep fryer if you have one)
2. use a soda lid to cut circles out of individual raw biscuits.
3. Take 2 cut circles place a liberal amount of chopped kale between the pieces. squish and pinch ends. Some kale should be sticking out.
4. add to fully heated oil.
5. turn once making each side golden brown.
6. remove from oil and roll in Parmesan cheese while still warm.
7. Sprinkle with garlic powder.
8. GIshWEs ON!!!
