Title:
Mandi's Toasted Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf Italian bread
5 tablespoons butter, softened
2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
3 cloves garlic, crushed
1 teaspoon dried oregano
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat the broiler.
Cut the bread in half lengthwise, like splitting a roll .
In a small bowl, mix softened butter, olive oil, oregano, salt and pepper.
Mix in crushed garlic.
Spread the mixture evenly on the bread slices.
Place loaves in a deep baking pan, so the oils do not drip out.
Broil 5 minutes or until slightly brown. --Be sure to keep oven door cracked or at least Check frequently so they do not burn.
Remove from broiler.
Top with cheese and return to broiler 2 to 3 minutes, until cheese is slightly brown and melted.
