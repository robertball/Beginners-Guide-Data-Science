Title:
Chicken Honey Mustard Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce
3tbs  Mayonaise
3tbs  Honey
1tbs  Corn Syrup
2tbs  Seeded Mustard
1tbs  Dried Onion Flakes
---------------------
1     Chicken Fillet Skin removed
1 cup Sliced Button Mushrooms
3     Rashes of Bacon - cut into large chunky pieces
3-4tbs Ranch Dressing
1 cup Grated or sliced Mozzarella
1tbs  Chopped Fresh Parsley
1 Large Pizza Base

Directions:
Cut Chicken fillet into easy to cook strips- not too small.  Pre cook chicken in frypan until just cooked - do not overcook.
Cool chicken and slice into smaller pieces suitable for a pizza.
Prepare Honey Mustard Sauce - Mix together in a bowl the 5 sauce ingredients.  Place chicken into the bowl of Honey Mustard Sauce, make sure the chicken is well coated in sauce. Refrigerate for approx 1 hour.
Take pizza base and cover with Ranch Dressing, place chicken, mushrooms, bacon & cheese onto base. Drizzle with desired amount of sauce.  Place pizza in oven and remove when cooked to your liking.  Sprinkle with parsley and serve.

Description: 
Australian Measurements
I adapted this recipe into a pizza, after enjoying the Aussie Chicken from a well known Australiana restaurant.  This is now a well requested pizza by friends when they come over for a pizza night. ***** Please note I only use a wood fired oven so cook time is only 3 minutes for me, it's probably about 20 minutes normally*****
