Title:
Dino Mite's Fluffy Bites! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2467108.jpg
Ingredients:
2 egg whites
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp cream of tartar
1 fresh kale leaf
1 small sprig fresh basil
1 small sprig fresh oregano
1 small sprig rosemary

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit. While oven is heating, put the fresh kale leaf (spine removed)and  the sprigs of basil, oregano, and rosemary (all trimmed) on a cookie sheet to dry.
Beat the egg whites, cream of tartar, and salt together on high for 2-3 minutes until stiff peaks form. Remove the drying greens from the oven and crumble over top the meringue mixture.  Fold in dried greens.  Spoon onto parchment-topped cookie sheet. Salt and pepper tops to taste.
Bake for 25 minutes until lightly brown.  Yields 2 dozen.
