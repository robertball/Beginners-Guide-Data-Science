Title:
Strawberry Cake with Homemade Cream Cheese Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/347464.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package (2-layer size) white cake mix
1 pkg. (4 serving size)  JELL-O Brand Strawberry Flavor Gelatin
2/3 cup BREAKSTONE'S Sour Cream
Cream Cheese Icing
2 8oz philly cream cheese
1/2 softened cup butter
3 cups confectioners sugar
1 1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 8- or 9-inch round cake pans; set aside. Prepare cake batter as directed on package then add sour cream. Pour 1 cup of the batter into medium bowl. Add dry gelatin mix; stir until well blended. Pour white batter into pans then spoon pink batter into white batter and swirl.  Bake 30 min. Cool 30 min. in pans. Remove to wire racks; cool completely.
beat cream cheese and butter add vanilla = beat in sugar - add more to taste.
Frost cooled cake = YUMMY
