Title:
Double Layer Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz cream cheese, softened (full fat)
1 T. milk
1 T. sugar
12 oz. Cool Whip
1 chocolate or graham cracker pie crust
1 1/2 c. milk
2 - 4 serving packages chocolate pie filling

Directions:
Cream layer:
Mix cream cheese, 1 T. milk, and sugar in large bowl until smooth.  Fold in 1 1/2 c. whipped topping.  Spread in bottom of crust.  Chill while preparing chocolate layer.
Chocolate layer:
Add pudding mix to 1 1/2 c. milk and beat with wire wisk 1 minute.  Stir in 2c. whipped topping.  Spread over cream layer.  Refrigerate 4 hours or until set.
