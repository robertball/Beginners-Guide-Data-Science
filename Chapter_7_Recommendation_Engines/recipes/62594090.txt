Title:
Etouffe' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/557947.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb fried bacon crushed
reserve 4 Tbls. bacon/sausage grease
4 Tbls flour
1/2 lb. ground sausage
2 Cups chicken broth
2 Cans stewed tomatoes
2/3 stalk of celery chopped
1 Bunch of green onions chopped
1 yellow onion chopped
2 lbs popcorn shrimp
4 Tbls. Cajun spices (Tony's Chachere's or anyother brand you choose)
2 tsp. Gumbo Fille(This isn't easy to find so it can be ommited)
1/4 red, yellow, orange and green pepper (1/4 of each or one whole pepper)
2 lbs. raw shrimp
3 Tbls. Cilantra
1 Tbls. minced garlic
3 cups rice cooked and set aside

Directions:
In a large stock pot fry bacon and sausage. Remove meat and lay aside. With 4 Tble spoons of the reserved grease from the sausage and bacon, add flour and make a dark roux. Return meat to stock pot and add chicken broth. Stir well to even out mixture. Add stewed tomatos with juice, diced celery, onoins, popcorn shrimp, cajun seasoning, peppers, garlic and cook slowly for an hour. Add raw shrimp/crawfish, cilantra and continue to cook for an additional hour. Meanwhile, cook rice as directed. Paired well with warm french bread.

Submitter: 
Kathi Richards Smith
Description: 
A cajun soup with a bite. This Lousiana meal is made with bacon, sausage and shrimp and or crwfish and other ingredience make this the perfect soup to warm up those cold afternoons and evenings. Served over rice and paired with warm french bread, makes this the perfect meal.
