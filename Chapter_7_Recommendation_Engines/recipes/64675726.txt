Title:
Cream Cheese Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces Bakers German Sweet Chocolate
3 tablespoons butter
Cheese layer:
3 ounces cream cheese
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tablespoon flour
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
Chocolate layer:
2 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup sifted flour
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 teaspoon almond extract
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Melt chocolate and butter.  Set aside to cool.
Cheese layer:
Cream cheese and butter until fluffy
Blend in egg, four and vanilla
Chocolate layer:
Beat eggs until light colored
Slowly add sugar, beat until thickened
Add baking powder, salt and flour
Blend in cooled chocolate mixture with vanilla, almond and nuts
To bake:
Spread half of the chocolate mixture in greased 8-9" square pan
Top with cheese mixture
Spoon on rest of chocolate mixture
Zigzag through batter with knife
Bake 35-40 minutes.
