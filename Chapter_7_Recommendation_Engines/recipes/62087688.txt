Title:
Buttermilk Dill Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium white potatoes, peeled
3 Tbsp margarine or butter
2 tsp dried dill (fresh is better)
2 Tbsp chicken boullion powder
3/4 cup buttermilk
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil, drain and mash potatoes.
Add remaining ingredients and mash until creamy.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
J
Description: 
Extra creamy mashed potatoes bursting with flavor. No need to add gravy or butter!
