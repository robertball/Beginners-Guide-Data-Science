Title:
Curried Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 -1/2 cups vegetable broth
1/2 cup golden raisins
1-1/2 teaspoons yellow curry powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup uncooked couscous
2 tablespoons lemon juice
pinch of sugar
1/2 cup slivered almonds, toasted
2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

Directions:
In a large saucepan, combine broth, raisins, curry powder and salt. Bring to a boil.
Remove from heat and stir in couscous. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
Fluff with fork. Toss lemon juice, sugar, almonds and parsley with couscous.
