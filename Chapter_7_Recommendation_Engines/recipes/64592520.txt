Title:
Lemon Feta Pesto Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3807530.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Garlic Cloves
? bunch of Flat Leaf Parsley
1/3 Cup Olive Oil
2 Tablespoons Walnuts
? Cup Reduced Fat Feta Cheese
½ Tablespoons Parmesan Cheese
1 Tablespoon Pepper
½ Tablespoon Salt
1 Teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes
Half Squeezed Lemon Juice
1 Pound Asparagus

Directions:
For Pesto:
Combine all ingredients into a food processor and pulse until blended well. Add water to thin out.
Store in fridge for 30 minutes before use.
For Asparagus:
Cut the rough ends off. Place on microwave safe dish, cover with 2 tablespoons water, olive oil, salt and pepper.
Cook in microwave for 2 minutes.
Drizzle each serving with 1 tablespoon of the feta lemon pesto and fresh shaved Parmesan
Notes:
Pesto can be stored for 5-7 days and can also be frozen for future use!
