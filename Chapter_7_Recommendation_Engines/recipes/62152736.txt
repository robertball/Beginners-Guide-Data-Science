Title:
Spring Break Strip Down Green Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. pork loin McCormick Grill Mates Cinnamon Chipotle Rub 5 cloves of garlic, finely minced
2 small diced onions
2 pasilla peppers
3 anaheim peppers
3 jalapeno peppers
1lb. tomatillos, diced
1 c. Red Stripe beer
1 tbsp. lime juice
32 oz. chicken stock
1 tbsp. cumin
2 tsp. dried oregano
2 tsp. dried chopped bay leaf
1 tsp. rosemary
2 tbs. chicken base
1 jar Herdez salsa verde
2 splashes Cuervo tequila
dash of cinnamon
pinch of sugar
sea salt and pepper, to taste.

Directions:
Rub pork loin with cinnamon chipotle seasoning.
Bbrown on all sides in stock pot.
Remove from pot.
Chop pork into small pieces and put back into pot.
Sautee garlic and onions with pork until transparent.
Grill peppers until skins are blistered, place in a brown paper sack for about an hour.
Remove skins and finely dice.
In a separate pot mix tomatillos, peppers, beer, lime juice and chicken stock. Heat thoroughly.
Add to garlic and onion mixture in stock pot.
Add cumin, oregano, bay leaf, rosemary, sea salt and pepper, salsa verde, chicken base, tequila, cinnamon and sugar.
Simmer for about an hour.
Serve with lime flavored tortilla chips and shredded queso fresco.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
This is a recipe I came up with for an upcoming St. Patrick's Day green chili cook-off.  It's comforting and a different take on traditional chili.  It may not look appetizing but one bite and you won't be able to stop eating it.
