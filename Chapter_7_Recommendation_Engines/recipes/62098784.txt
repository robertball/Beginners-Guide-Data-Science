Title:
Crock Pot Beef Strogonaff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package beef cubes
4  cans cream of mushroom soup
garlic powder
onion powder
4 oz sour cream
1 box bow tie noodles

Directions:
In a crock pot add beef
sprinkle garlic powder
onion powder
salt on the beef
Turn crock pot on low
Pour 2 cans of soup on beef.
Cover let cook 2 hours.
After 2 hours stir
Continue to cook on low.
I hour before ready to eat
Add another soup
Add sour cream
Mix well.
Begin to boil water for bow ties.
Follow box directions.
Drain bow ties
Put bow ties in a dish
Ready to serve.
Put bow ties on plate
Spoon stroganoff over bow ties.
Sprinkle salt and pepper on meal.
Serve with italian bread.
Easy Meal for busy moms.
Enjoy
