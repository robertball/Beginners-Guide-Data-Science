Title:
Snickerdoodles (cookies) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shortenting
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs
2 3/4 cup flour
2 teaspoons cream of tartar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Mix shortening, sugar and eggs
Blend dry ingredients
Mix together and form dough
Roll into balls; roll balls into mixture of 2 Tblsp sugar and 2 tsp. cinnamon
Place on ungreased baking sheet
Bake 8-10 minutes
