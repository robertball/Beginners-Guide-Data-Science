Title:
Roasted Parmesan Rosemary Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/349971.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds new potatoes, cut into 1 inch pieces
3 TB olive oil
2 TB grated parmesan
1 TB fresh chopped rosemary
salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 425
Mix potatoes, olive oil, parmesan, and rosemary in a medium bowl.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Transfer to a foil line baking sheet and cook, stirring once, until golden brown, about 40 minutes.
