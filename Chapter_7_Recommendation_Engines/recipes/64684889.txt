Title:
Easy Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1lb ground turkey
15oz can tomato sauce
15oz can pinto beans
1 cup water
1 tbsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Add 1 tbsp olive oil to a medium stock pot and heat on medium-high.
Add ground turkey to the heated pot and cook until browned.
Add the tomato sauce, water, and spices.
Bring to a boil, cover, and let simmer for 15 minutes.
Add the beans, bring to boil, cover, and let simmer for 5 minutes.
Serve with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkling of cheddar cheese.

Submitter: 
Andrew Rocco
Description: 
This fast turkey chili is a great weeknight meal that is still healthy for the whole family.
