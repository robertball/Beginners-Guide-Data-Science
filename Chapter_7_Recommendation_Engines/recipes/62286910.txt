Title:
Slow Cooker Bison Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground bison
1 can fire roasted tomatoes
1 can chili ready diced tomatoes
1 can chili beans
1 cup onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper chopped
1 tbsp cumin
2 tsp chili seasoning
2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp garlic powder
8 oz macaroni, cooked

Directions:
Brown beef in a skillet on medium. Combine all ingredients in a slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours. Add macaroni in last 20 minutes of cooking

Submitter: 
Becca Miller
Description: 
Rich bison adds flavor to this easy chili.
