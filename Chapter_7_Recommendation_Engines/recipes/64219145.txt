Title:
Roast Beef Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb roast beef from deli (sliced thin)
1/2 red onion, diced
1/4 c. bbq sauce
1/4 lb cheddar cheese
Whole wheat tortillas

Directions:
Sautee onion until translucent. Add in roast beef and bbq sauce until heated through. Spoon mixture evenly between 4 tortillas. Top with a slice of cheddar cheese. Fold in half and place on oven rack. Broil until golden brown.
