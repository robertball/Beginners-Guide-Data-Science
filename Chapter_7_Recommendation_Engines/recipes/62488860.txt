Title:
Oven Fried Parmesan Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes
1 TBS canola oil
1 TBS grated parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/8 tsp. pepper

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
2) Scrub potatoes but don't peel. Cut potatoes into cubes. Place potatoes in a plastic bag with oil and shake until well coated.
3) Mix seasonings and add to bag of potatoes and shake until well coated.
4) Arrange potatoes on a single layer on a baking sheet that's been sprayed with cooking spray.
5) Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown.
