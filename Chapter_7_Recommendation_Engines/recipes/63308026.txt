Title:
Red Cabbage & Apple Salad with Walnuts & Cranberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head red cabbage, shredded
1 Granny Smith apple, thinly sliced
1 Tbsp. walnuts
1 Tbsp. dried cranberries
red wine vinegar or balsamic vinegar
salt & pepper

Directions:
Place first four ingredients in bowl.
Toss with vinegar, plus a dash each of salt & pepper.
