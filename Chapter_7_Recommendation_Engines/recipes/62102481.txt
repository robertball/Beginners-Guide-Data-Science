Title:
Swedish meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 lb ground pork
2 cups cubed bread
1 cup milk
2 eggs slightly beaten
2 T. butter or oil
half an onion minced
1 tsp cardamom
1 tsp allspice
1 tsp nutmeg
salt and pepper to taste
Sauce:
2 cups beef or chicken broth
3 T flour
reserved drippings
1 12 oz. can of evaporated milk
salt and pepper to taste
lingonberry jam (optional but highly recommended)

Directions:
preheat oven to 375
Add milk to bread cubes in a bowl for 5 minutes. With pan on medium heat add butter and onion stir in seasonings. cook till translucent set aside.
in bowl with ground meats add onion mixture, and eggs.
wringe bread of the milk(messy and cold) add bread to meats and mix with hands till combined.
make balls and place on greased baking sheets into oven for 15-20 min or till temp reaches 140.
sauce:
in a 2 quart pan over med heat scrape drippings into pan and add flour.
mix and cook for about 3 min.
add broth and canned milk season with salt and pepper.
bring to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes stirring occasionally.  It thickens while it cools down.
serve with noodles or potatoes and definately with lingonberry jam.

Submitter:

Description:
