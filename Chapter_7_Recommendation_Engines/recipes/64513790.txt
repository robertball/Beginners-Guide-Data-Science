Title:
Crockpot Zucchini Chicken Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken breasts cut into cubes
1 small onion diced
1 large zucchini diced
1 green pepper diced
1 tsp garlic, mined
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 T of Italian seasoning
1.5 cups of pasta sauce
1 cup of shredded mozarella cheese

Directions:
1. Spray slow cooker with non stick cooking spray. Combine all ingredients except cheese and stir to combine.
2. Cover and cook on high for 3/4 hours or low 6-8 hours.  In the last 30 min of cooking add cheese and cook until it is melted.
3. Serve over pasta or quinoa and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
