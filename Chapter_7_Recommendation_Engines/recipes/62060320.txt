Title:
Easy Hot Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups cubed red potatoes
6 slices crumpled bacon
3 diced garlic dill pickles
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup ranch style dressing or to taste

Directions:
Scrub red potatoes and boil. after 15 - 20 minutes drain the potatoes and cube, add the remaining ingredients and serve while still warm.

Submitter: 
PATRICIA53
Description: 
A delicious potato salad that can be served warm or cold.  And for those weight watchers calorie wise ranch dressing can be used instead of regular.
