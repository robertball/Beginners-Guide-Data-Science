Title:
Grilled Chicken Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992370.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Sister Schubert's Dinner Yeast Rolls, split
4 (6 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, halved
2 cups Sister Schubert's Barbecue Sauce (see note)
1 red onion, sliced
1/2 head lettuce, torn into pieces
Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bake rolls according to directions on package.
Prepare grill to medium heat. Marinate the chicken breasts in barbecue sauce for 30 minutes. Cook chicken breasts 7 to 8 minutes on both sides or until chicken is thoroughly cooked. Immediately place chicken on rolls with slice of onion and some lettuce.
