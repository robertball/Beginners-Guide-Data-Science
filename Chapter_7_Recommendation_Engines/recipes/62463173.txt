Title:
Artichokes French Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507649.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts
1/2 - 3/4 cup flour
1 egg, beaten
1/4 cup cooking oil
4 tablespoons butter
Juice of 1 medium lemon
3 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup dry sherry or white wine
Salt and pepper
Fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
Drain artichoke hearts and cut in half. Dredge in flour and dip in beaten egg. Saute in hot oil until brown. Transfer to holding dish.
Pour oil out of frying pan, wipe pan and return it to the stovetop. Melt butter. Add lemon juice. Return artichokes to pan, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and add sherry (or wine). Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir.
Remove the artichokes to a serving dish. Reduce the sauce in volume by boiling for 2 to 3 minutes over high heat. Pour sauce over artichokes. Garnish with fresh parsley.

Regional favorite from greater Rochester, NY area. Great appetizer or as a main dish served over pasta.
