Title:
Violette's Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 lasagne noodles
1 lb ground beef
1 tsp italian seasoning
28 oz jar spaghetti sauce
4 oz can mushrooms
15 oz ricotta cheese
2 cups shredded mozzarella

Directions:
break noodles put half in the bottom of a greased slow cooker. Brown ground beef in bottom of a sauce pan drain stir in italian seasoning spread half over noodles in slow cooker layer half of sauce, water, mushrooms,ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese over beef and repeat layers. cover cook on low for 5 hrs.
