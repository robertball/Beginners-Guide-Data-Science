Title:
Cheesy potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 medium red potatoes
2 cups cheddar cheese
1 can cream of chicken
1/2 c. parm cheese
3 Tbsp. butter
1.5 cloves garlic
2.5 cups milk
1 small onion
3 Tbsp flour
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Melt butter in saucepan. Cook onions in butter for 2 min. Add flour, salt, pepper, soup, milk, garlic. Grease casserole dish. Heat to 350.  Place sliced potatoes in dish, cover with sauce. cut up 1 Tbsp butter on top of potatoes and add cheddar. cook 30 min. covered. Then uncover and cook 1 hour. set for 10 minutes to thicken.
