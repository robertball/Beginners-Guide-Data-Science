Title:
WONDERFUL SHORT RIBS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
28 oz. tomato sauce
3 Tbl lemon juice
4 tsp worcestershire sauce
2 Tbl dried parsley
1 tsp dried thyme
2 bay leaves
minced garlic
1 tsp.beef boullion powder
2 tsp salt
1 tsp crushed red pepper
1 onion, cut into rings & browned
3 lbs beef short ribs

Directions:
Place ribs bone side down in a pan, season w/s&p & roast at 450 for 45 minutes. Drain off fat. Saute onions in large sauce pan, then add all other ingredients except meat. Pour sauce over meat & bake covered at 325 for at least 3 hours.
