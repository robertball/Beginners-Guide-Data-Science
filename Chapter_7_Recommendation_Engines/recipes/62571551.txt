Title:
Beef Kushiyaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. beef steak (slice paper thin)
1/6 cup Mirin
1 clove garlic minced
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/8 tsp. Aji
1/3 cup sugar
3 green onions (cut into 2" pieces)

Directions:
Mix everything but green onions and beef.
Marinate beef with mixture for 30 minutes.
Roll slice of beef around green onion and pierce rolled beef with two bamboo skewers - put 3 rolls of meat on every 2 skewers.
Broil 6 to 7 minutes until brown on both sides.
