Title:
Sweetened Condensed Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup powdered milk
1/3 cup boiling water
2/3 cup granulated sugar
3 TBSP butter (NOT margarine)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well in blender until all sugar is dissolved.  If mixture is too thick, you can add a few drops of water until it is the consistency you like.  Use as is or store in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This is a great substitute when you are out of the canned version or if you want to save some money.
