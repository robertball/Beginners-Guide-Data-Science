Title:
Cran-Dandy Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4482589.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cranberry juice
1 cup pineapple juice
1 cup orange juice
1/2 (14 oz) jar maraschino cherries (but use all the juice)
2 Tbsp. lemon juice
2 cups 7-up (more if desired)
1 orange, sliced in rounds

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a gallon pitcher and garnish with orange slices.
