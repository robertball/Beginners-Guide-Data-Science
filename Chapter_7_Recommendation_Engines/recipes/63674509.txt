Title:
Easiest Brown Gravy Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.25 ounce) can beef gravy
1 cup beef broth
1/4 cup Marsala wine

Directions:
Whisk together the mushroom soup, beef gravy, beef broth and Marsala wine in a saucepan. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 10 minutes, whisking frequently. Season to taste

Submitter: 
Dorothy
Description: 
If your pantry is stocked with canned cream of mushroom soup, beef gravy and Marsala wine, tasty gravy is always just minutes away.Modified from: Easiest Brown Gravy Ever
