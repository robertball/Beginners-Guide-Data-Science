Title:
Deep-dish taco squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Deep-dish taco squares
½ pound lean gr. beef
½ c. sour cream
1/3 c. mayo
½ c. shredded Cheddar
1 T. chopped onion
1 c. Bisquick
¼ c. cold water
1 to 2 tomatoes, thinly sliced
½ c. chopped gr. pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 375.  Grease a square baking dish (8x8).  Cook and stir ground beef until brown; drain.  Mix sour cream, mayo, cheese and onion;  reserve.  Mix Bisquick and water until soft dough forms.  Pat in pan, pressing dough ½ inch up sides.  Layer beef, tomatoes, and green  pepper in pan; spoon sour cream mixture over top.  Bake till edges of dough are light brown, about 25-30 minutes
