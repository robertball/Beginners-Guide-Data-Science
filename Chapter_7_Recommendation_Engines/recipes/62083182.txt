Title:
Spicy Peanut Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter (no salt or sugar preferred, but not required)
- 1 lime, juice of
- 1 clove minced garlic
- 1 tblsp hot sauce, any kind
- 2 tblsp coconut milk
- 1 tblsp soy sauce
- 1/4 cup hot water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and stir well with whisk.  Add more water as needed to reach desired consistency, always mixing well.

Submitter: 
Sean Gough
Description: 
Always a favourite, this peanut sauce is light and easy to prepare and great for dipping spring rolls or chicken.  It can be kept in the fridge for a few days.
