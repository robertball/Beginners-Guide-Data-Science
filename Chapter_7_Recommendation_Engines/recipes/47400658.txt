Title:
Crab Classic Salad with Lemon & Parsley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Crab Classic (surimi)
2 Tbl. light sour cream
2 Tbl. light mayonnaise
2 tsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. parsley, chopped
4 Tbl. red onion, small dice
4 Tbl. celery, small dice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, Mix well.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve over mixed greens.

Submitter: 
daf
Description: 
Made in Feb 2009 with Aunt Janice and friend Kathy.  Served on lettuce cups as an appetizer.  Use for salad or sandwich spread also.
