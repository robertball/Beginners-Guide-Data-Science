Title:
Oven Fried Parmesan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Instant potato flakes for 4 servings
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
1 tbsp. seasoned salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 egg
1/2 cup half and half
1 tbsp Paprika
3 lbs. chicken drumsticks or thighs
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In shallow dish or pie plate, mix potato flakes with cheese, seasoned salt, paprika and pepper.
In another shallow dish, with fork, beat egg with 1/2 cup half and half just until mixed.
Roll chicken in egg mixture, then coat with potato flake mixture;
In 13 x 9 pan pan, melt butter in oven.
With pot holders, remove pan from oven and tilt pan to spread butter evenly over pan.
Arrange chicken in pan.
Bake chicken 40 to 50 minutes until fork tender.
