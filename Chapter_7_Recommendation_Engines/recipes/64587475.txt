Title:
Lemon Cream Soda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts water
3/4 cup fresh lemon juice, or more to taste
3/4 cup light brown sugar, or more to taste
1 teaspoon vanilla
Club soda * optional
Crushed ice

Directions:
In a large pitcher combine the water, lemon juice, sugar and vanilla.
Stir to dissolve the sugar.
Serve over crushed ice and top with a splash of club soda.
