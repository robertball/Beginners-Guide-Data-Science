Title:
Thelma's Mason Jar  Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can tomato soup
3/4 C. cider vinegar or white distilled vinegar
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 clove garlic or
1 tsp. onion juice
1 tbsp. Worschestershire sauce
1 tbsp. mustard
1 1/2 C. oil
1/2 C. sugar

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a mason jar with lid and shake until blended. Serve over your favorite salad greens etc.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Forgotten salad dressing. Keeps in the refrigerator, easy to make and is a recipe that has been in the family for years. This is an old recipe from the 30's and the lady who gave it to me has passed away. She will be remembered when ever her recipe is prepared.
