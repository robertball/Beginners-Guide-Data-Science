Title:
Zucchini Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups shredded zucchini
1 cup vegetable oil
3 tsp vanilla
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
3 cups flour
3 eggs
1/4 tsp baking powder
2 cups sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1 16 oz container whipped topping
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese
5 tbs powdered sugar
2 tsp vanilla
3/4 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Mix the first 10 ingredients with a hand mixer.
Prepare 2 cookie sheets with greased waxed paper.  Pour batter spreading evenly.  Bake 12-15 minutes at 350.
Turn out on a cotton kitchen towel sprinkled with powdered sugar.  Remove waxed paper gently.  Sprinkle again with powdered sugar.  Roll as jelly roll.  Cool for 15-20 minutes.
Mix the next 5 ingredients. Unroll and spread with cream mixture.  Chill 2 or more hours.

Submitter: 
Lyn
Description: 
This is similar to a Pumpkin Roll.  Easy way to use up some of your summer crop.
