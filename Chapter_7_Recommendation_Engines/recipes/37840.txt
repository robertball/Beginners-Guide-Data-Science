Title:
Cool Yogurt Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981208.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container BREYERS Lowfat Yogurt, any variety
2 1/2 cups COOL WHIP FREE Whipped Topping , thawed or frozen
2 cups fresh or frozen strawberries or any other seasonal fruit, chopped
2 cups ice cubes

Directions:
Place yogurt, 1-1/2 cups whipped topping, fruit and ice in blender container; cover.  Blend until smooth.  Top each serving with 1/4 cup remaining whipped topping.  Serve immediately.
