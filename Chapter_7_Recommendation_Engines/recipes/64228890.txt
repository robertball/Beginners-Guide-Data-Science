Title:
Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup crumbs (graham, cookie)
1/3 cup sugar
1/3 cup cocoa (optional)
1/3 cup melted butter
3 8 ounce cream cheese/ neufchatel cheese
14 ounce sweetened condensed milk
2 teaspoons vanilla (or 1 vanilla & 1 any flavor)
3 eggs
1 cup chocolate chips (optical)
dash flour (to dust the chips)

Directions:
Mix crumbs, sugar, cocoa and melted butter.
Press into the bottom of springform pan.
Meanwhile, let cheese sit at room temp until it is very soft. Beat well.
Slowly add sweetened condensed milk. Beat well after each addition.
Add flavoring and eggs. Beat well.
Toss chips in flour to coat.
Stir into dough.
Bake 300 for one hour.
Do not open door, but turn oven off.
Let cake sit in oven for additional hour.
Remove and cool.
