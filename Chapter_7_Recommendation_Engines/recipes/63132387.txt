Title:
Hamburger Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/799536.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
2 cans corn
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
2 cans cream of broccoli
1 onion
8 oz. sour cream
1 Package extra wide noodles
1 Package Shredded cheese
Assorted spices

Directions:
1.  Pregeat oven to 350 degrees
2.  Brown hambruger with chopped onion
3.  Drain hamburger and corn
4.  Combine soups, sour cream, and corn
5.  Boil noodles til soft
6.  Combine hamburger with mixed ingredients and cooked noodles into a large glass dish
7.  Cover with shredded cheese
8.  Bake for 25-30 minutes
