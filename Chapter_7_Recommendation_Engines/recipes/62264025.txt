Title:
Indian Butter Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 pint plain yogurt
1 1/2 tsp. chili powder
2 bay leaves
1/4 tsp. cloves
1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
1 tsp. garam masala
6 green cardamom pods
1 tsp. ginger pulp
1 tsp. garlic pulp
1- 14 ounce can tomatoes
1 1/4 tsp salt
2 pounds chicken, skinless boneless cubed
3 ounces butter
1 Tbsp. vegetable oil
1 small onion, sliced
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
4 Tbsp. cream

Directions:
Crush cardamom pods and remove black seeds, place seeds in saucepan. In dry saucepan toast cardamom and cloves over medium heat. Place in spice grinder with bay leaves and grind well.
Place yogurt, all the dry spices, ginger, garlic, tomatoes and salt in mixing bowl and blend thoroughly.
Put chicken in bowl with yogurt mixture and toss to coat. Set aside to marinate.
In large pan, heat oil and butter, add onions and fry for about three minutes. Add chicken mixture and cook ten minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
Stir in cilantro and cream, mix well. Serve over rice.
