Title:
Slow Cooker Chili Lime Chicken Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts (frozen)
1 1/2 Tbsp. chili powder
4 Tbsp. lime juice
16 oz. jar salsa
Flour tortillas
Shredded cheese (optional)
Sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Place frozen chicken breasts in slow cooker. Sprinkle with chili powder and then pour lime juice on top.
Let cook 6-8 hours on Low.
Shred chicken with two forks in slow cooker. Add salsa and mix together.
Let simmer for 30-60 minutes.
Serve with flour tortillas. Top with shredded cheese and sour cream if desired.
