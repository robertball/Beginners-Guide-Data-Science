Title:
Warm Raw Herbal Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups warm (not boiled) herbal tea
3 Tbs miso
3 Tbs dulse
1 1/2 Tbs extra virgin olive oil
1 1/2 Tbs coconut oil
1 Tbs hemp seeds
1 lime, juiced
1 tsp rosemary
1 tsp sage
1/2 tsp maca
1/4 tsp thyme
1/8 tsp cayenne
1 pinch sea salt

Directions:
1. Mix all ingredients in a blender and blend for 1 to 3 minutes.

Submitter: 
Raw_Angel
Description: 
Try this comforting herbal soup recipe. No cooking involved.
