Title:
Perfect Lowfat Strawberry Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup vanilla frozen yogurt
4 large strawberries
1 cup 1% milk

Directions:
Using a hand blender (or any type of food processor), combine all the ingredients together and process until creamy. *If it is not thin enough for you just add a little bit more milk*
Pour into glass and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Froggy+Popovers=
Description: 
This milkshake is an ideal drink for the summer. Not too sweet and full of rich fruity flavor.
