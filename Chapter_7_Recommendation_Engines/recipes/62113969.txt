Title:
Tex-Mex Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs yellow squash - sliced
1 med white or yellow onion - sliced
1 (4 oz) can chopped green chilis (not jalepenos)
1 1/2 cup cottage cheese (small curd)
1 large egg
1/2 cup grated parmesan
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1-2 Tblsp  flour
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
2 Tblsp butter, margarine, or oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Spray or grease a 9x13" pan
In a large skillet, melt the butter (or margarine).
Add sliced squash & onion. Stirring frequently, cook until onions are translucent.
Add black pepper and green chilis, and stir.
Stir in Flour(to absorb the excess liquid). Remove skillet from heat.
In a separate bowl, mix cottage cheese, egg, and parmesan, until completely combined.
Pour squash mixture from skillet into baking pan.
Spread cottage cheese mixture over the top (it doesn't have to evenly cover).
Top with cheddar cheese.
Bake uncovered for 30-40 min, until cheese starts to brown.

Submitter: 
FOKLMEMBER
Description: 
This is a really great recipe to use up all that summer garden squash. Has that green chili flavor, without the heat.  Even the kids like this.

This is also a great make-ahead dish.  I often prepare it the night before and just bake it before dinner.  Leftovers are a hit.
