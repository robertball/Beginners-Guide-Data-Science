Title:
Spinach Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
2 cups finely crushed herb-seasoned dry bread stuffing mix
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/2 cup melted butter
3 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup feta cheese
1/2 onion, chopped and sauted
1 clove onion, sauted
dash cayenne pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl combine spinach, stuffing mix, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, black pepper, Italian seasoning, melted butter and eggs.  Shape into walnut-sized balls and place on a baking sheet.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until heated through and browned.
Serve with Sauce made of: 12 c sour cream
1 tsp mustard
dash worcestershire sauce
1 tbsp soy sauce

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
Spinach is combined with stuffing mix, Parmesan cheese, butter, eggs and savory spices. The mixture is formed into balls and baked.Modified from: Spinach Balls
