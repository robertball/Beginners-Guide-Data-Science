Title:
Buttermilk Cornmeal Cheese Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup pancake mix
1 1/4 cups buttermilk (add 1/4 cup more if needed)
1 6-oz. packet cornbread mix
1/4 cup sharp, shredded cheddar cheese
1 egg
1/8 cup vegetable or canola oil

Directions:
Mix buttermilk, cornbread packet, and pancake mix together, add the egg and oil. Fold in the cheese. Pour by 1/4 cup fulls into a heated, greased waffle iron. Follow manufacture;s instructions for using your waffle iron.
Note: you can also use a buttermilk pancake mix, and then just use reg. milk.

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
A savory waffle with a bite of tang from the buttermilk and cheese! The cornmeal adds a bit of texture. I developed this recipe because I wanted a savory waffle instead of sweet. Great with lots of real butter!
