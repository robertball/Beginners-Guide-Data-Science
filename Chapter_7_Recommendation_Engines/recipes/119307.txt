Title:
Raisin Bran Muffin Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/846174.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/4 cups sugar
1 cup nonfat dry milk powder
6 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup shortening
1 1/2 cups raisin bran cereal
1 cup chopped almonds
ADDITIONAL INGREDIENTS (for each batch):
1 egg
1 cup water

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the first six ingredients. Cut in shortening until crumbly. Stir in cereal and nuts. Store in an airtight container in a cool dry place or in the freezer for up to 2 months.

Submitter: 
Darlene Brenden
Description: 
'My husband likes to take muffins to work,' says Salem, Oregon's Darlene Markel. 'And with this mix in my pantry, I can have muffins ready in minutes.'
