Title:
Chili-Toasted Pecans with Dried Cherries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon vegetabel oil
1/2 teaspoon of bottled hot pepper sauce
2 cups pecan halves
1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon of dried oregano,crushed
3/4 cup dried cherries or a combination of raisins and dried cherries

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325.
In a large bowl combine oil and hot pepper sauce.
Add pecans; toss to coat evenly. Set aside.
In a small bowl combine chili powder,pepper,salt,cumin and oregano. Add to pecans; toss well to coat. Place pecans in a single layer in a jelly roll pan.
Bake for 12-15 minutes or until toasted,stirring every 5 minutes. Stir in dried cherries. Serve warm or cooled to room temperature.
