Title:
Big Ole' Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound stewing meat
5 slices bacon, chopped
3 1/2 cups beef broth
2 cans peeled diced potatoes
2 cups of chopped babay carrots
2 stalks of celery, chopped
2 cups frozen peas
1 bay leaf
Teaspoon thyme
Tablespoon salt
Tablespoon pepper
1/4 cup gravy thickener
2 tablespoons flour
2 tablespoon canola oil

Directions:
In a medium pan, heat 1 tablespoon oil. Once hot, add beef. Cook till no pink is visible.
Chop bacon. dd to the same pan and fry till slightly crisp. Set beef and bacon aside. In a large pot, add
oil, carrots, potatoes, and celery. Heat for 2 minutes. Coat beef in flour. Add beef and bacon. Heat for an additional 2 minutes. Add broth. Heat for 5 minutes, then add bay leaf, thyme, salt, pepper, and flour. Cook fr 45 minutes.
