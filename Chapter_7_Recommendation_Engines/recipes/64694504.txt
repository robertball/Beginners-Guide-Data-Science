Title:
Seafood Crab French Melt Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5221599.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. imitation crabmeat, chopped
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions
1/4 cup diced celery
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 loaf (approx 1lb) 1/2 sliced french bread length wise halved.

Directions:
In large mixing bowl, combine crab, mayonnaise, green onions and celery. stir in the cheese and sal and pepper.
Spread over bottom of the bread; replace top. Wrap in aluminum foil and place on baking sheet.
Bake at 400 degrees for approximately 20 minutes or until heated through.
Cut into slices.

Easy sandwich that fills the tummy always! Crab salad sandwich...what not to like. Great Sunday brunch idea.
