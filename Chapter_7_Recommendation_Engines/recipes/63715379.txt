Title:
Creamy Polenta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable broth
1 cup whole milk
1 leaf of bay leaf
1/2 cup uncooked cornmeal
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3/4 tsp salt

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan, combine broth, milk and bay leaf.  Bring to a slow boil over medium-high heat.  When liquid starts to boil, slowly pour in cornmeal.  Stir continuously.  Reduce heat to low and stir until polenta is smooth and well-combined (about 5-10 minutes).  Remove from heat and stir in salt and cheese until thorougly combined.

Submitter: 
Shelly Culp
Description: 
Creamy polenta.  Taste great with roasted veggies!
