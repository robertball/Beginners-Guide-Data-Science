Title:
Left Over Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked rice
1 lb pre-cooked steak cubed
1/2 small yellow onion diced
1 garlic clove diced
2 eggs scrabbled
1 tbl soy sauce
1 tsp sesame oil
2 tsp olive oil
pinch of cayenne pepper

Directions:
1. Heat up 1 tsp of olive oil in a frying pan and scramble 2 eggs. Once cooked, put in a separate bowl.
2. Add 1 tsp of olive oil to the frying pan as well as garlic and onions. Saute for approx 2 minutes
3. Add rice, steak, chopped egg, soy sauce, sesame oil and cayenne pepper. Cook approx 5 minutes until warm.
