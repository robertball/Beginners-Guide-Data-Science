Title:
Pineapple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of all purpose flour
2 cups of sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons of baking soda
1 (20 ounce) can of crushed pineapple, undrained
Frosting:
1 (8 ounce) package of cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 cups of confectioners sugar
1/2 stick of margarine
1 teaspoon of vanilla
1 cup of chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix all cake ingredients together by hand. Pour into greased and floured 13x9x2 inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 mins.
Mix all frosting ingredients, except pecans, together by hand. Frost cooled cake. Sprinkle with pecans.
