Title:
Cookie Butter Chocolate Chip Goodness Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Cookie Butter
1 Heaping Tbsp Peanut Butter (Creamy Honey works well)
1 Cup Butter
1 Cup Sugar
1 Cup Packed Brown Sugar (golden brown)
2 Eggs
1 Tsp Vanilla
1 Package Milk Chocolate Chips
2 1/2 Cups Flour
1 Tsp Baking Powder
1 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
1/2 Tsp salt

Directions:
1. Cream together Cookie Butter, Butter, Peanut Butter and Sugars, when smooth, add in eggs one at a time, mix until smooth again,add in vanilla. Set aside.
2. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in separate bowl.
3. Add flour mixture slowly to wet ingredients and then add chocolate chips.
4. Let cool in fridge for 1hr.
5. Spoon onto cookie sheet 1inch apart and flatten with dull side of butter knife or fork (like peanut butter cookies) and place in preheated 350deg oven for 8-12min.
