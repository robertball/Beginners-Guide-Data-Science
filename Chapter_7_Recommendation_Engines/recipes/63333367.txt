Title:
Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can chick peas,drained(garbanzo beans)
2 tbs tahini
pinch salt
1/2 lemon, juiced
1 clove garlic, minced or crushed

Directions:
In a blender, combine all ingredients, and puree to desired texture.
Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with a pich of paprika

Submitter: 
Cocoanut
Description: 
Served with a drizzle of olive oil and a dash of paprika, this middle-eastern dish is irresistable!
