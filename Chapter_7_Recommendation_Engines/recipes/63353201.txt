Title:
Sweet Italian Sausage and Polenta casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/891790.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups water
1 cup milk
9.2 oz pkg instant polenta
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
2 TB butter
1 TB olive oil
2 links sweet italian sausage, casings removed
1 cup mushrooms, sliced
14-1/2 oz. diced tomatoes
3 garlic cloves, diced
1/2 cup onions, chopped
1-1/2 cups dry white wine
6 oz. tomato paste
1 tsp. Italian seasoning
1/2 tsp. oregano
1 TB sugar
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Prepare polenta by combining water and milk in a large saucepan along with polenta.
Bring to a boil and add Parmesan cheese and butter.
Stir constanty until thickened approximately 3 minutes
In a large round casserole dish, add the polenta to about a inch depth
Meanwhile, add olive oil to a medium non-stick skillet
Add sausage and cook for about 10 minutes.
Add onions, mushrooms and garlic in skillet and cook until sausage is brown
Add wine, tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, Italian seasoning and oregano and cook over medium heat for about 30 minutes
Add tomato sauce mixture on top of polenta
Put the mozzarella cheese on top of sauce
Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
Rich tomato based casserole made with sweet italian sausage baked in a polenta crust topped with mozarella cheese is a great substitution for your typical pasta dish.  Serve with a green salad and a loaf of Italian bread doesn't get much better than this!
