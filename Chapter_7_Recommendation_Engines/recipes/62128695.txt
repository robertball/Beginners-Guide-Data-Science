Title:
Upside Down Pizza II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground chuck
1 cup chopped onion
minced garlic, to taste
1 (15 oz.) can tomato sauce
1 (1 1/4 oz.) envelope spaghetti sauce mix
1 (4 oz.) can sliced mushrooms, optional
1 (16 oz.) jar Alfredo sauce
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 (8 oz.) can crescent rolls

Directions:
Brown the beef, onion and garlic in a large skillet over medium-high heat; drain and return to skillet.  Stir in tomato sauce, spaghetti sauce mix and mushrooms; cook over low heat for 10 minutes; stirring often.  Spoon the beef mixture into a lightly greased 13x9 baking dish; top with alfredo sauce and sprinkle with cheese.  Unroll crescent rolls and place over cheese.  Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees for 20 - 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Shults3
Description: 
Pizza turned upside down with simple, usually on hand, ingredients.  Kids seem to really like this recipe.  If you like pepperoni - just add a layer of it over the beef mixture.
