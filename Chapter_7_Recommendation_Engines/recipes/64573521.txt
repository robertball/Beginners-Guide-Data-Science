Title:
Corn and zucchini soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  1/2 cup white or yellow corn
1 zucchini
1 teaspoon garlic
1 C. Half n Half
2 cups chicken broth ( or more if preferred )
Seasonings ( Salt and Pepper, Tony Chachere's )

Directions:
Cook corn ( You can use yellow but I prefer white )
Slice zucchini then quarter it then put in skillet .
Put garlic in pan and cook quickly ( garlic burn easily )
Add half n half then chicken broth.
Put in seasonings, salt and pepper.
You can also put in Tony Chachere's if desired.

Submitter: 
Stacie Bonner
Description: 
A nice healthy soup that you can easily make. Made from two favorite veggies, corn and zucchini.
