Title:
Bacon Feta Bruschetta Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695682.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz) package of whole wheat penne pasta
3 slices center cut bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 cup balsamic bruschetta recipe from this site
1/2 cup Feta, crumbled

Directions:
Bring a large saucepan full of water to a boil.  Once boiling, add the pasta and let it cook for about 8 minutes or until it is al dente.
Meanwhile, in a skillet sprayed with cooking spray, add the bacon and cook per package directions.
Once the pasta is done, drain it well.
Add the drained pasta to a large bowl.  Add the bacon, red and green bell pepper and balsamic bruschetta mixture.  Toss until everything is thoroughly distributed.  Top with crumbled feta cheese.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
A delicious and healthy pasta dish with center cut bacon, feta, red and green bell peppers, and a balsamic tomato bruschetta mixture.  It is excellent served either hot or cold and can be served as a main dish, pasta salad, or side dish.Modified from: Bacon Feta Pico Pasta Toss
