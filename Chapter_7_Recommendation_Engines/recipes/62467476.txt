Title:
Cider-Glazed Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Glaze: 1/2 C white or cider vinegar
1/3 C light brown sugar
1/3 C apple cider or apple juice
2 T Dijon mustard
1 T soy sauce
Pinch cayenne pepper
Chops: 4 boneless center-cut pork loin chops
1 T veg. oil

Directions:
Combine all glaze ingredients in med. bowl; mix thoroughly and set aside. Pat chops dry w/paper towels; season w/salt and pepper
Heat oil in skillet over med-high heat. Add chops and cook until well browned, 4-6 min.
Turn chops and cook 1 min longer, transfer to plate and pour off any oil in skillet. Return chops to skillet, browned side up, and all glaze; cook over med. heat until center of chops registers 140 degrees, 5-8 min. Remove chops and tent with foil and let rest 5 min.
Add juices from rested chops to skillet, simmer glaze whisking constantly until thick and color of dark caramel, 2-6 min. Return chops to skillet; simmer glaze, whisking constantly until thick and color of dark caramel, 2-6 min. Return chops to skillet; turn to coat both sides w/glaze

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Cooking time depends upon thickness of chops. If temperature reaches 140 degrees after 1st side is done, don't cook on the other side. Remove chops and continue with directions.
