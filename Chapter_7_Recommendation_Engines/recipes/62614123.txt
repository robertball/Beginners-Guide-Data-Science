Title:
Italian Pasta and Bean Soup with Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
31oz canned white beans, drained and rinsed
1t olive oil
3-4 fresh sage leaves, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2lb cooked sausage, italian style, cut into bite-sized pieces
28oz canned crushed tomatoes
1T canned tomato paste
2-1/2 cups water
3c chicken broth
1T salt
1/4t red pepper flakes
3oz uncooked spaghetti, broken into small pieces

Directions:
Puree half of beans in a blender and set aside.
Spray soup pot with cooking spray.
Add oil and cook sage and garlic 1 minute.
Add sausage and cook until browned, 2-3 minutes.
Add pureed beans, whole beans, tomatoes, tomato paste, water, broth, salt, and pepper to pot.
Bring to boil and stir in spaghetti.
Cook for length of time indicated for spaghetti.
Season with salt and red pepper.
