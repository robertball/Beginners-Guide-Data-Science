Title:
Batida de Coco Rapida (Brazilian Coconut Cocktail) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4444973.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
14 fluid ounces vodka
1 1/2 cups ice cubes, or as desired
1/2 cup coconut milk
7 teaspoons shredded coconut, or as desired (optional)

Directions:
Combine condensed milk, vodka, ice, and coconut milk in a blender; blend until smooth. Pour cocktail into glasses and sprinkle coconut over each.
