Title:
Gudeg Jogja Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472082.jpg
Ingredients:
1. 2 cans young Jack Fruit
2. 4 Hard Boil Eggs
Bumbu Halus
1. 8 Shallots
2  8 Candle Nut (Kemiri)
3. 3 Garlic (bawang putih)
4. 1 Tbsp Coriander (Ketumbar)
5. 1 Inch Galangal (Lengkuas/Laos
6. Air 1200 cc
7. Coconut Milk - half can 165 ML)
8. Garam secukupnya
9. Gula Jawa secukupny (2 Sendok Makan)
10. Asam Jawa
11. 5 lembar daun Salam

Directions:
1. Put all ingredients in the pot.
2. Add water to cover, add teh for color or sweet soy sauce in case you don't like the taste of tea in gudeg.
2. Paste all ingredient with little bit of water
3. Add coconut Milk
4. Cook in small flame for 1 hour or until the soup
is thicken

Submitter: 
JFKR
Description: 
Resepnya pake daun jati biar merah, berhubung daun jati nggak ada di Amerika, jadinya diganti 2 bags teh celup. Hasilnya gudeg cantik dan enak. Bisa juga pake Ayam dan Tahu
