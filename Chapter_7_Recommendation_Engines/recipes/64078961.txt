Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 1/2 cups Sifted all purpose flour
2 1/2 Tbls Baking Soda
2 1/2 tsp Salt
5 Cups Butter
5 Cups Peanut Butter
5 Cups White Sugar
5 Cups Packed Brown Sugar
10 Eggs
2 Tbls Vanilla Extract

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350'F
2. Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside
3.  Cream together butter, peanut butter, white sugar, brown sugar until smooth.  Beat in Egg, then Vanilla.  Gradually blend in the sifted ingredients.
4.  Bake 8-9 minutes (No fan)
