Title:
Chinese Bubble Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. white sugar
1/3 cup pearl tapioca
1 cup dark brewed black tea
2 Tbsp. milk
4 tsp. white sugar
1 cup ice cubes

Directions:
In a small saucepan, bring 2 cups water to a boil. Stir in the 1 teaspoon of sugar until it dissolves. Add the pearl tapioca.  Cook for about 20  minutes.  Rinse, drain, and refrigerate until chilled.
Pour tea, milk, and 4 teaspoons sugar into a cocktail shaker. Shake until the sugar has dissolved and the milk is well mixed in.  Add the ice cubes, and shake again so the whole drink can get cold. Pour into a glass, and add tapioca and stir.
