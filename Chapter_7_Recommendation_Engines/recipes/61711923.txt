Title:
Homemade Flour Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c flour
1 t salt
2 t baking powder
2 T butter
1 1/2 c water

Directions:
Whisk flour, salt, and baking powder in large bowl. Cut in butter until it resembles cornmeal. Add water and mix until dough comes together; place on a lightly floured surface and knead a few minutes until smooth and elastic. Roll into 16 balls. Roll out as thin as desired, using floured rolling pin. Makes 16.

Submitter: 
Kell S-h
Description: 
Traditional flour tortillas - homemade and much better than store bought. Do not substitute vegetable oil or shortening for the lard.Modified from: Homemade Flour Tortillas
