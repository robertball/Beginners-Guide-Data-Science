Title:
Chicken Curry from Betty's Kitchen® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977420.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7.75 ounce) package Tuna Helper® creamy pasta
3 1/4 cups boiling water
1/4 cup milk
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 cups cut-up cooked chicken or turkey
1 teaspoon curry powder, or to taste
Chopped peanuts, flaked coconut or raisins, if desired

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Mix Pasta, Sauce Mix and remaining ingredients except peanuts in ungreased 2-quart casserole or rectangular pan, 10x6x1 1/2 inches.
Cover and bake 30 minutes or until pasta is tender; stir.
Let stand uncovered 5 minutes or until sauce is as thick as you'd like. Sprinkle with peanuts if desired.

Submitter: 
Betty Crocker®
Description: 
We really like chicken with this creamy pasta, but the curry flavor also blends well with tuna.
