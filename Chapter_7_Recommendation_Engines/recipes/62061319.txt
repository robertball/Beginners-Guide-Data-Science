Title:
Mexican Chicken Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooked rotisserie chicken from store
1 Cup sour cream
1 Cup mayo
1 cup salsa (or to taste)
tortilla wraps
shredded lettuce
shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
pull the chicken apart so taht you have lots of chunks of chicken. Mix the sour cream, mayo and salsa together. Mix in the chicken chunks. put about 1/4 cup of the mixture onto a wrap(more if you like it thick and full). put shredded lettuce and cheese onto the mixture that is on wrap. roll up the wrap and enjoy!
