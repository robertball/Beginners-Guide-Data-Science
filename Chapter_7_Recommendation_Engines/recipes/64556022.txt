Title:
corned beef casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. sauerkraut
1 1/4 c. sour cream
1 medium onion, chopped
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
10 oz. cooked corned beef, diced (can use deli or roast your own)
2 1/2 c. shredded swiss cheese
9 slices rye bread
2 T. butter, melted

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients. Spoon into a lightly greased square baking dish. Spread corned beef over mixture; sprinkle with cheese.
Cut bread into triangles and arrange over top so it covers completely.  Spread melted butter over bread. Refrigerate for 8 hours.
To bake: let stand for 30 minutes at room temp. Bake at 350 for 35 minutes or till bread is lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Susie
Description: 
From Sandy Norman.  Easy Reuben substitute. Must be refrigerated 8 hours before baking.
