Title:
Best Ever Cowboy Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup sugar
1 can (14 ounces) pinto beans, rinsed
1 can (14 ounces) black-eyed peas, rinsed
1 can (11 ounces) white shoepeg corn, drained
2 ribs celery, chopped
1 medium red onion, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 bag tortilla chips
1/2 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
In saucepan, bring olive oil, vegetable oil, cider vinegar and sugar to boil, then cool.
Place pinto beans, black-eyed peas, corn, celery, red onion, red pepper and cilantro in large bowl.
Pour oil mixture over vegetables and marinate in refrigerator 24 hours. Stir occasionally during marinating. Drain and serve with tortilla chips.
