Title:
Mike Deatline's Pepper Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/554189.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 tablespoons olive oil
1 (2 lb) round steak
1 large green bell pepper, seeded
1 sweet onion
1 packet McCormick速 Brown Gravy
1 packet McCormick速 Au Jus
4 cups water (WITH McCormick速 packets ADDED)
2 (14.5 oz) cans stewed tomatoes with juice
1 small can (4 oz) mushroom pieces, drained
6 tablespoons La Choy速 Soy Sauce

Directions:
Cut excess fat off steak & cut into bite-sized strips. Cook the meat in a large (12") skillet with olive oil, until browned. Cut bell pepper & onion into similar bite-sized strips and add to skillet. Mix brown gravy & au jus in water to make 4 cups of gravy. Add gravy, tomatoes, mushrooms & soy sauce to the skillet mixture. Bring to a boil, then lower to simmer and cook uncovered for 2 hours. Serve over rice.

This is my husband's recipe, and one of our favorite meals. It's a great way to cook a round steak and it comes out tender everytime.
