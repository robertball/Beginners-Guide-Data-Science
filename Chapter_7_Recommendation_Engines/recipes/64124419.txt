Title:
Cauliflower Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
2 large eggs
1 cup almond flour
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
cold pressed EVOO
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut cauliflower into florets and boil until tender (about 15 minutes).
Drain and mash the cauliflower while still warm.
Add eggs, flour, cayenne, salt, and pepper.
Mix all the ingredients together and form into patties.
Add a small amount of olive oil to the bottom of a skillet over medium heat.
Cook until golden brown on each side (about 5 minutes per side).
