Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 flour tortillas 8" size
1/2 cup onion chopped
1 cup salsa
24 oz chicken breast, boneless, skinless
1/4 cup red bell pepper
1 tsp cumin
1/4 cup green pepper
1/4 cup flour
1 garlic clove
1 tsp chili powder
1 cup fat free sour cream
1 cup water

Directions:
preheat the oven to 350.
Spray skillet with non-stick spray. Saute onion, peppers and garlic for 3 minutes.
mix flour, chili powder, cumin, water and salsa. Add to onions. Cook until thick. Remove from heat and add sour cream.
Combine 2 cups of sauce and chicken. Spoon about 1-1/2 cup of mixture on each torilla, roll up.
Spray 13x9 baking dish. Place trotillas on dish. Top with remaining sauce. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
