Title:
Creamy White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs. boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 medium onion, chopped
1-1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 tbsp. olive oil
2 cans (15-1/2 ounces each) great northern beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (14-1/2 ounces) chicken broth
2 cans (4 ounces each) chopped green chilis
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. ground cumin
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 cup (8 ounces) sour cream
1/2 cup whipping cream

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute chicken, onion, and garlic powder in olive oil until the chicken is no longer pink. Add the beans, broth, chilies, and seasonings. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in sour cream and cream. Serve immediately.
