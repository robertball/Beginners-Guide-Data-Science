Title:
BUSH'S® Sweet Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980592.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) can BUSH'S® Original Baked Beans
1 (16 ounce) can BUSH'S® Kidney Beans, rinsed and drained
1 (16 ounce) can BUSH'S® Garbanzo Beans, rinsed and drained
1 (15.2 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1/2 cup pineapple juice
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1 pinch cinnamon

Directions:
Mix ingredients in large saucepan. Cook until warm, stirring occasionally.
Serve as a side with your favorite grilled dish, warm or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
A new twist on Three Bean Salad - a perfect cookout side dish that only takes 10 minutes to make.
