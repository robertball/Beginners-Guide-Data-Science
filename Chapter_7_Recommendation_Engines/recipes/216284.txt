Title:
Baileys Alpine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990275.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Baileys® Original Irish Cream
1/2 ounce Rumple Minze® Peppermint Schnapps
1 cup coffee
A dollop of whipped cream

Directions:
Add Baileys Original Irish Cream and Rumple Minze.
Add coffee and top with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Baileys®
Description: 
Baileys® Original Irish Cream and minty Rumple Minze® Peppermint Schnapps in coffee with whipped cream make a perfect fireside cocktail.
