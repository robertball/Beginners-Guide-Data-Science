Title:
Mary's Marvelous Slow Cooker Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 Ounce Can Cream of Chicken
10 Ounce Can Cream of Potato
14.5 Ounce Can Fiesta Corn
3 Celery Stalks Chopped
29 Ounce Can Peas
29 Ounce Can Carrots
14.5 Ounce Can Diced Potatoes
1.25 Cup Milk
10 Ounce Uncooked Biscuits

Directions:
Dump all ingredients except biscuits into the crock pot and cook on low for 3-3.5 hours. Separate biscuits into pieces quarter size or slightly bigger. Place biscuits into crock pot and turn on high to cook for 45 minutes, stirring about every 10 minutes. Turn crock pot to low and cook for another 15 minutes. Enjoy!
