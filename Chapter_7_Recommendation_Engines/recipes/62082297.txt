Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup shortening
2 2/3 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 16oz. can pumpkin
2/3 cup of water
3 1/3 cups of flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground cloves
2/3 cup pecans or walnuts
2/3 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Grease two 9x5x3 loaf pans
In large bowl cream shortening and sugar, until fluffy. Stir in eggs, water and pumpkin. Then stir in flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder, cinnamon and cloves. Stir in nuts and raisins.
Pour equal amounts into two loaf pans. Bake for 70 mins or until done

Submitter: 
L. Graham
Description: 
This recipe has been handed down through four generations of my family. Over time the recipe has changed a bit to accomodate more modern ingredients. It is the the best pumpkin bread recipe I have come acrossed yet.
