Title:
Scot's Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 parts basil
1 part parsley
1 part roasted pine nuts (approx.)
Garlic to taste
Olive oil
Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender.
Add enough olive oil to blend.
Add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Basic fresh homemade pesto that is absolutely fantastic when mixed in with nice freshly made/cooked pasta noodles.  Um- good!
