Title:
Homemade Fabric Softener by Maridele Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups hot water
1 cup cheap hair conditioner
1 1/2 cups white vinegar

Directions:
Mix water and conditioner and stir until dissolved.
Add white vinegar.
Use 2 Tablespoons per load.
