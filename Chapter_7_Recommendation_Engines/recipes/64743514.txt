Title:
Baked Chicken Thighs with Orange Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6477627.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken thighs
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1/4 cup bourbon
1/4 cup yellow mustard
1/2 cup orange marmalade
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup onion, diced
salt and pepper

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper on both sides. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Brown chicken on both sides, approx. 2 minutes per side. Transfer chicken thighs to a 8x8 baking dish.
3. In a small bowl combine bourbon, mustard, marmalade, garlic, and onions.
4. Pour sauce into skillet and whisk for 2 minutes, cooking off some of the bourbon. Pour sauce mixture over chicken in baking dish.
5. Bake chicken uncovered in the preheated oven until no longer pink and the juices run clear, about 25 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted  should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
6. Serve chicken and sauce over rice and enjoy!
