Title:
Janie's Chinese Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head Napa cabbage, thinly sliced
5 green onions, sliced
2 packages Ramen noodles, broken
1 cup roasted sunflower seeds
3 chicken breasts, cooked and cut into pieces
for dressing:
4 T sugar or Splenda
1 cup less 2 T salad oil
2 T sesame oil
1 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
3 T lemon juice
6 T rice vinegar

Directions:
Blend dressing ingredients.  Put first five ingredients in a large bowl, then pour on dressing.  Toss and serve.

Submitter: 
Jo Bice
