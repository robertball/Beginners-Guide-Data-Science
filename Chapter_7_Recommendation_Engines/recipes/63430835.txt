Title:
Cheesy Italian Tortellini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (16 ounce) jar marinara sauce
1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style diced tomatoes, undrained
Garlic
Onions
1 (9 ounce) package refrigerated or fresh cheese tortellini
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup Parmesan over the top

Directions:
Crumble the ground beef into a large skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until browned. Drain.
Combine the ground meats, marinara sauce, mushrooms, and tomatoes in a slow cooker. Cover, and cook on LOW heat for 7 to 8 hours.
Stir in the tortellini, and sprinkle the mozzarella and cheddar cheese over the top. Cover and cook for 15 more minutes on LOW, or until the tortellini is tender.

Submitter: 
SouthwestHiAltCook
Description: 
A delicious marinara sauce is cooked all day in the slow cooker. When it's time for diner, just throw in some tortellini, and sprinkle with cheese. When the noodles are cooked, pass around the bread, and enjoy!Modified from: Cheesy Italian Tortellini
