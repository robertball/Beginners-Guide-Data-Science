Title:
Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
"Fish Sauce Mix":
4 TBLS fish sauce
1/2 tsp dried chili powder
2 TBLS rice vinegar
2 TBLS sugar
In Wok:
2 TBLS oil (peanut or canola)
3-6 green onions (sliced)
1/4 cup peanuts (crushed)
3 cloves garlic
3 tsp Sriracha sauce (to taste)
1/4 cup crushed peanuts for topping.
Cooked diced Shrimp or chicken (optional)

Directions:
In small bowl pre-mix the "fish sauce mixture" using the fish sauce, rice vinegar, chili powder and sugar.  In large sauce pan or wok, saute peanuts, green onion and garlic in oil over med high heat, careful not to burn the peanuts for a minute or two.  Add soaked and drained rice noodles and the "fish sauce mix".  Add sriracha, push to one side and scramble egg into mixture.  (Optional add cooked and diced shrimp, chicken or tofu as desired.) Combine and sever with crumbled peanuts on top.

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
Soak rice noodles for 15 minutes in warm water while preparing the rest of the ingredients, drain well before adding to wok.
