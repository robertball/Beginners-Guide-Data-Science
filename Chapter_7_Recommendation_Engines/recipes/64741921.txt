Title:
Keto BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c apple cider vinegar
1/4 c white vinegar
1/4 c coconut aminos
1/2 c water
4 oz butter or more, separated
1 T tomato paste
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp smoked paprika or other keto BBQ seasoning
1/2 tsp smoked salt
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp chipotle powder

Directions:
combine vinegars, coconut aminos, water in saucepan over medium heat
add seasonings
bring to boil, whisk and reduce
whisk in butter, continue to reduce/whisk to desired flavor/consistency
add more BBQ seasoning or butter as desired
