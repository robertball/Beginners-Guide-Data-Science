Title:
Smoked Gouda Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups of Macoroni, Cooked
2.5 cups of heavy cream
1 cup shredded smoked gouda cheese
1/2 cup shredded parmesan Cheese
1 cup panko bread crumbs

Directions:
Place cooked macoroni in baking dish
In a sauce pan
heat cream
add gouda cheese and half of parmesan
Wisk vigorously over low heat (no scortching)
Pour melted mix over macoroni
Sprinkly panko crumbs and remaining parmesan cheese on top
Bake 350 for 10 min or golden brown
