Title:
Moroccan Beef Kebabs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Tzaziki Dip - 2 cups plain yogurt
3tbsps of Victorian Epicure Dip Mix
1 cup of grated cucumber drained
Sea salt to taste
Moroccan Kebabs - 3/4 cup Olive oil
2/3 cup fresh lemon juice
6 large garlic cloves minced
1tbsp lemon pepper
1tbsp black pepper
2tsps of ground coriander
1tsps of ground cumin
4pounds of well trimmed beef 2" cubes
wooden skewers
32 whole dryed appricots soaked in boiling water for 5 minutes
1 red onion sectioned
pita bread cut for serving

Directions:
Tzaziki - mix all ingredients, let stand for 1/2 hour before serving
Kebabs - whisk first seven ingredients in medium bowl
Add beef to marinate, toss to coat
Marinate for a minumum of two hours or overnight in refrigerator
Thread apricot, onion and chunks of beef on skewers
BBQ at medium heat
