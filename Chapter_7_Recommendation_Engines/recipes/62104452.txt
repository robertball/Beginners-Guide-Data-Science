Title:
Simple Tomato & Avocado Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lg. avocados
1 lg. tomato
1/4 small red onion
raspberry and walnut dressing

Directions:
Peel and dice avocados into bite size pieces. Place into medium bowl.
Dice tomato and add to avocado.
Slice red onion very thinly and add to bowl.
Drizzle raspberry and walnut dressing and mix salad gently.

Description: 
A simple salad with a suprising flavor!
