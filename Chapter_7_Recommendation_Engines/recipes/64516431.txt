Title:
Apple Brownies with Caramel Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter or margarine
2 cups brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup chopped, peeled apples
Icing:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup milk
2 cups icing sugar

Directions:
Brownies:
Cream butter and sugar together till smooth.
Add eggs and vanilla. Mix well.
Add remaining brownie ingredients. Mix to combine thoroughly.
Bake in well greased 9" x 13" pan @ 350 x 30 - 35 minutes. Watch closely last 10 minutes, so not overdone.
Let cool completely before icing.
Icing:
Melt butter in saucepan.
Add brown sugar & milk.
Bring to boil.
Remove from heat, let cool.
Stir in icing sugar.
Spread on cooled brownies.
