Title:
Grilled S.O.P.P. Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of  rope sausage like hilshire farm
1 pound of new potatoes
3 bell peppers assorted colors
3 medium onions
Olive oil
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Place potatoes into a microwaveable bowl with 1 TBS of water, cover and  cook on high until potatoes are done about 8 minutes.
Cut each rope of sausage into 5 pieces.
Chop each pepper and onion into 10 chunks.
Cut potatoes in half.
Separate onion chunks in half.
Make skewers by placing onion, potato half, sausage, 3 peppers, sausage, potato half and onion. Repeat making 10 skewers.
Add extra potatoes to each skewer.
Place on a plate and drizzle generously with olive oil to coat and salt and pepper.
Preheat a grill on medium high heat.
Place skewers on the grill with the lid open for 3 minutes. Turn heat to low and close lid for another 3 minutes.
Flip kabobs and cook another 6 minute with the lid closed.
Remove to serving platter. Drizzle with more  olive oil, salt and pepper.

Description: 
We love SOPP.  Sausage-Onions-Potatoes and Peppers; all fried up in one pan. It’s delicious. This a a summer time take on this one dish staple. Instead of pan frying it, you skewer it and grill. We use smoked turkey sausage but any kind of rope sausage  would work.
