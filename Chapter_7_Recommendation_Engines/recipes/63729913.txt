Title:
Re:Bar Re:Vive Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1035758.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Yoplait Origional Vanilla Yogurt
1 Yoplait Container filled with Orange Juice
6 Frozen Sliced Peaches
1/2 Yoplait Container Slivered Almonds
1/2 Yoplait Container Granola

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Alexa Goldman
Description: 
Lived in Israel for a few years and lived on Re:Bar's shakes, now that I'm back in America I can't get them. This tastes almost exactly the same as their Re:Vive shake does. It's my favorite one!
