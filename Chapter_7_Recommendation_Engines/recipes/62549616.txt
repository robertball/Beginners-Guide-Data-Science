Title:
Dorothy-Christine's Amazing Fish Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of mackeral (or other firm fleshed fish), coarsely ground
1 cup of white or yellow onion, finely minced
2 cloves of garlic, finely minced
2 tsp of dried thyme
1 egg beaten
1/2 cup of fresh parsley, finely minced
Salt & pepper to taste
Cayenne pepper to taste (optional)
3 large limes, juiced
1/4 - 1/2 cup seasoned instant mashed potatos
Panko breading
Butter and olive oil

Directions:
Saute the onion, garlic and thyme in butter until onion is translucent.  Set aside to cool.
Mix ground fish with balance of ingredients as well as the sauteed onion mixture.
Form into 3" patties and dip into Panko breading
Place into skillet with butter and olive oil and fry until golden brown and done inside, about 2-1/2 minutes per side.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
I watched Dorothy Faith and Christine Yerbic whip up these breaded and herbed fish cakes at Dorothy's home in Mazatlan. They make a delicious and easy dinner.  Serve with a tomato salsa and fresh corn tortillas, and baked yams.
