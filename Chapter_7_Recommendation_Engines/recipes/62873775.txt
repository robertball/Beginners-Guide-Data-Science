Title:
Dad's Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/690791.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
2 eggs
1 c. self-rising flour
1/4 c. milled flax seed
1 c. sugar
2 over-ripe bananas
1/2 c. nuts (optional)

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar,
add eggs,
then bananas,
flour, flax meal and nuts.
Bake loaves at 325 degrees for 60 minutes.
Bake muffins with cupcake liners at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
Makes 1 large or 2 small loaves or 16 muffins.

Submitter: 
tjmallick
Description: 
My Dad made this bread for years to the delight to our family. Pair it with a dish of fruit and a cup of coffee for breakfast. Yummm! The flax seed meal makes it better for you without altering the flavor.
