Title:
Whole Wheat Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-1/4 cup Whole Wheat Flour
1 tsp. Baking Soda
1 tsp. Salt
1 cup (2 sticks) butter, softened
1-1/2 cup Dark Brown Sugar
1 tsp. Vanilla Extract
2 Large Eggs
2 cups (12 oz. package) Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
1/8 cup Kahlua (coffee liqueur)
2 tbsp. Dark Chocolate Cocoa Powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Combine everything EXCEPT chocolate chips (it's okay if it's a little bit lumpy).
Fold in chocolate chips with a spatula.
Drop by heaping tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake for 12-14 minutes or until golden brown.
Cool in pan on wire rack.

Submitter: 
Mandi
Description: 
I don't cook much, but when I use a recipe, I can't help but change it. I started with the Toll House recipe on my bag of chocolate chips and made some changes, yielding a rich, chewy, chocolatey pile of goodness!
