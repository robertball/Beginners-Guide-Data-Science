Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Rotisserie chicken, deboned and shredded
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c. milk
1 28oz can enchilada sauce
1-2 Tbsp diced green chilies
12 corn tortillas
4 c. shredded cheese (Monterey jack, cheddar- any combo)
1 can sliced pitted olives (optional)
oil for frying

Directions:
Saute the onions and garlic in 1 Tbsp oil.
Add soup, enchilada sauce and chilies. Simmer for 15 minutes. Add chicken to soup mixture for the last 5 minutes.
Soften tortillas in hot oil and drain on paper towels.
Place a handful of cheese, 2-3 olives (optional), and about 1/4 cup of chicken mixture in a tortilla and roll up placing seam down in a 9x13 casserole dish.
Do the same for remaining tortillas and top with meat mixture, remaining cheese and olives.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
