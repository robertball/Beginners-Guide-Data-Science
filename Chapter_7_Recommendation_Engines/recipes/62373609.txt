Title:
Tzatziki Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless, skinless chicken brests
1 cup Prepared Tzatziki sauce
1/2 cup chopped red onion
1 tablespoon cumin
salt and pepper

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a large dish to marinate.
Marinate at least 30 minutes at room temp or refridge for longer.
Bake in preheated oven 375 for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
souplover Sue
Description: 
Transplant yourself to the Ocean breeze coastline of Greece if only for a meal.  This is a moist, quick and easy dish. Pare it with a Greek salad.
