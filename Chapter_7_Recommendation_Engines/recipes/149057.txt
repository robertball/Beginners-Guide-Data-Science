Title:
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies Treats® Christmas Stars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979683.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal
Prepared frosting
Red cinnamon candies

Directions:
Melt margarine in large saucepan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
Add Kellogg's(R) Rice Krispies(R) cereal. Stir until well coated.
Using buttered spatula or waxed paper, press mixture evenly into 15 x 10 x 1-inch pan coated with cooking spray. Allow to cool slightly. Using 4-inch star-shaped cookie cutter, cut into star shapes. Decorate as desired using frosting. Use a dot of prepared frosting to attach red cinnamon candies. Best if served the same day.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
Description: 
During the holidays, kids will love pitching in to help make these star-shaped Kellogg's® Rice Krispies Treats®.
