Title:
Vegetarian French Bread Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/485922.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Inch loaf of french bread cut and open faced.
2 tbs Olive Oil (eyeball it to lightly cover bread)
1 tbs chopped garlic
1/2-1 tsp Italian Seasoning
1/2-1/ tsp Garlic Salt
1/4 c fresh basil
1 medium tomato cut into slices
1/4 c spaghetti sauce (tomato and basil)
2 med baby portobello mushrooms (stems removed and sliced)
1/2-1 C Mozzarella Cheese shredded
OPT 1 tsp crushed red pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees.
Start by cutting your french bread lengthwise and placing on a pizza pan (can use a cookie sheet as well or place on foil directly on rack)
Add the olive oil and spread to cover the bread, add the garlic salt, red pepper, and Italian seasonings evenly.
Spoon the spaghetti sauce onto bread.
Place sliced tomatoes on pizza, add half of the mozzarella.
Add mushrooms, basil, and the rest of the mozzarella.
Place into pre-heated oven and cook for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Laurie Hoeltzel Weathers
Description: 
Was in the mood for some pizza, so looked what I had around the kitchen and came up with this yummy pizza.  I always use ranch on all pizza, except for this one, did not need it one bit full of flavor and super cheesy! You can substitute vegetables or even add meat if you so desire.
