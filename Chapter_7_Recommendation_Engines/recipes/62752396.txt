Title:
Stevia Keylime Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups walnuts
1 package Stevia
1/2 cup Can't Believe It's Not Butter
Directions:
MIX butter, walnuts and stevia in a small mixing bowl.
PLACE on bottom of a greased 9 by 12 baking casserole pan.
CHEESECAKE
Ingredients:
22 packages Stevia
1 cup egg beaters
6 - 8 oz packages Fat Free Cream Cheese
1 cup egg whites
Cheese
2 cups Fat Free Sour Cream
1 cup Key Lime Juice
3 tablespoons corn starch
Directions:
BLEND with a mixer in a large mixing bowl the cream cheese until smooth.
ADD sour cream, key lime juice, stevia, egg beaters, egg whites and corn starch.
MIX until smooth.
PLACE on top of crust.
COOK at 300 degrees F for 45 minutes.
COOL before adding topping.
TOPPING
Ingredients:
2 - 8 oz. packages Fat Free Cream Cheese
3 packages Stevia
1/2 cup Key Lime Juice
Directions:
MIX with a mixer in medium mixing bowl until smooth.
PUT aside until basic pie is cooked and cooled.
SPREAD on top of cooled cheesecake.
REFRIGERATE for 2 hours before serving.

Directions:
All directions are above with ingredients!  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lauren Bright
Description: 
A wonderful tasting, sugarfree, fat-free cheesecake!  The crust is optional.  I do not make mine with the crust.  Enjoy!
