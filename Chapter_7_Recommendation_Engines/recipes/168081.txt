Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Mini Footballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983196.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
6 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
1/2 cup peanut butter

Directions:
In a large microwave safe bowl, heat margarine and marshmallows at HIGH for 3 minutes, stirring after 2 minutes.
Mix peanut butter and vanilla into marshmallow mixture.
Sir in vanilla and KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal until well coated.
Shape mixture into 13 (10 cm) balls.
Use prepared chocolate frosting to pipe on markings and stitching. Best if served the same day.
