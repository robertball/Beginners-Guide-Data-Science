Title:
Roasted Brussel sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Brussel sprouts
1 tsp EVOO
1/2 tsp each of
garlic powder
cayenne pepper
sea salt
1 medium red onion, diced
6 slices of bacon, cut in 1/2-inch pieces
1/4 cup pine nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Rinse, trim and dry thoroughly  Brussel sprouts.
Mix all ingredients together (except pine nuts) and spread evenly on a greased baking sheet.
Roast in the oven for 40 minutes (or until bacon reaches 145 degrees). Stir after 20 minutes.
Toss with pine nuts and serve.
