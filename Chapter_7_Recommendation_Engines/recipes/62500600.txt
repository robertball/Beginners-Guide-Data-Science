Title:
Chocolate Haystacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups (500ml) sugar
1/2 Cup (125ml) unsalted butter
1/2 Cup (125ml) milk
1/3 Cup (75ml) unsweetened cocoa powder
3 1/2 Cups (875ml) Quick Oats
1 Cup (250ml) Sweetened Shredded Coconut
1/2 tsp (2ml) Vanilla
1/2 tsp (1ml) salt

Directions:
1.  In large saucepan, combine sugar, butter, milk and cocoa.  Over high heat, bring to hard boil.
2.  Remove from heat; Stir in oats, coconut, vanilla and salt.
3.  Drop rounded tablespoons onto parchment paper-lined baking sheet.  Let cool completely.
