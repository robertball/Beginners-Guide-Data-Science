Title:
Pressure Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T vegetable oil
3 LB Beef Chuck Roast
1 Large Onion
pepper
season salt
onion powder
1 14.5 oz can beef broth
1-2 Tbsp worcesteshire sauce
4 carrots
4 large potatoes

Directions:
Add vegetable oil in pressure cooker and bring to heat over med-high.  Add roast and brown on all sides.  After browning, add pepper, season salt and onion powder to taste.  Quarter the large onion and add to roast, along with the beef broth and worcesteshire sauce.  Add lid to pressure cooker and bring to pressure.  Once it reaches pressure, reduce heat and cook 30 minutes.  In the meantime, peel and cut potatoes and carrots into bitesize pieces.  Using quick method, reduce pressure, and add potatoes and carrots and bring to pressure again, then cook under pressure for 15 minutes.  Use quick method again to release pressure and place in serving dish.

Description: 
VERY tender and delicious.  Using the pressure cooker saves SO much time, that this recipe can be used on a weekday and still you can manage to eat dinner at a decent hour.
