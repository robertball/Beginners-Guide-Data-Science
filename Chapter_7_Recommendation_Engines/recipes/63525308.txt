Title:
Sunday Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups warm water
3 cups plain or bread flour
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tablespoon salt
1/2 cup shortening
4 teaspoons yeast

Directions:
Set bread machine to dough cycle.
Combine ingredients in the order the manufacturer recommends.
Remove dough from bread maker when the cycle is finished.
Cut the dough and let it rise for 1 hour.
Bake at 425 degrees until browned.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
From Pat Baugh.  This recipe was my favorite growing up every time I ate at the Craig family's cafeteria or restaurant in my home town of Morton, MS.
