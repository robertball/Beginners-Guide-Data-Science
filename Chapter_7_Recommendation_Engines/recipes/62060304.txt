Title:
Baked Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lg. boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/4 cup water
2 lemons
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 tbsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. paprika
1 tbsp. parsley flakes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Lay chicken breasts in a glass baking dish.  Pour in water. Pour olive oil and lemon juice over chicken.  Sprinkle the bread crumbs, garlic powder, paprika, parsley flakes and salt and pepper over the chicken.  Cover pan with foil and poke a few slits in it.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.  Goes great with mashed potatoes or rice.

Submitter: 
SANDI149
Description: 
This is a wonderful and very easy chicken dish.  Everything is in one pan so it makes for easy cleanup.
