Title:
Mary's Texas Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can corn
1 can black beans
2 cans Black Eyed Peas
1 lg container fat free Italian dressing
2 tbs minced garlic
1/4 bunch of parsley OR cilantro
1 onion chopped
1 green, red, yellow or orange pepper chopped
2 lg tomato's seeded and chopped
Juice from 1 lime
Jalapenos and about tbs brine from jar(optional)
Chips or Chicken breast and Mexican cheese

Directions:
1.  Dice tomato's, pepper and onion
2.  Chop parsley or cilantro
3.  Mix all ingredients in a large bowl except for jalapenos
4.  If you like add diced jalapenos and brine from jar to taste.  (remember this gets hotter as it sits)
5.  Put in refrigerator overnight, there are a-lot of flavors that need to settle.
6.  Keep chilled until you serve it.
7.  Serve with chips or heat in a pan and top with
grilled chicken and mexican cheese.
*Leftovers are GREAT served over yellow or Spanish rice topped with grilled chicken, shredded cheese, sour cream and Frito's.
