Title:
Easy Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 loaves frozen bread dough
1 stick oleo
1 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup white sugar
3 T. milk
1 t. cinnamon
white frosting

Directions:
Thaw bread dough.  Break 1 loaf of bread dough onto bottom of a greased 9x13 pan.  Melt the rest of the ingredients(except frosting) and spoon over the broken dough.  Break second loaf over this.  Let rise until double in size, about 2 hours.  Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown at 375ยบ.

Submitter: 
dutchkidzx2
Description: 
This recipe is an easy and yummy alternative to cinnamon rolls.  This recipe is always requested at family get-togethers and brunches and makes a great presentation.  Homemade bread dough can also be used.
