Title:
Costa del Sol Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
400ml/12fl oz Tomato Ketchup
200g/7oz Brown Sugar
100ml/3½ fl oz Red Wine Vinegar
4tbsp Honey
60g/2oz Butter
½ or to taste small Red Chilli - finely chopped
½ Red Onion - finely chopped
3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped
½ to ¾ tsp Cayenne Pepper
½ to ¾ tsp Paprika
½ tsp Salt
½ tsp freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Place all the ingredients in a saucepan and, stirring continuously, heat on high to bring to boil;
Reduce heat to low and simmer gently, stirring occasionally, for some 20 minutes or so until mixture is cooked and sauce has thickened slightly;
Blitz with a hand blender and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
Direct from the land of sun, sea and sand. This will light up any BBQ.
