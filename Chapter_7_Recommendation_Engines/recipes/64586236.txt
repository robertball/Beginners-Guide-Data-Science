Title:
Black Bean and Corn Salsa Fresca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3720489.jpg
Ingredients:
6 ears corn on the cob, fire roasted or grilled and stripped from cob
1 large red onion, diced
2 large Beefsteak tomatoes, seeded and diced. (May substitute 4-6 Roma tomatoes.)
2 (15.5 oz) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup cilantro, minced
2 jalapenos, diced (may be seeded for less spicy taste)
2 limes, zested and juiced
1 Tablespoon garlic powder, or to taste
1 Tablespoon salt, or to taste
1/2 Tablespoon  ground black pepper, or to taste

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, add all ingredients.
Toss mixture thoroughly and transfer to an airtight container.
Chill in refrigerator for at least 4 hours.
Serve with tortilla chips, as a side dish or on top of grilled meats.

Submitter: 
Mark Adams
Description: 
A fresh Pico de Gallo with grilled corn and black beans.  Adjust the heat by seeding all, some or NONE of the jalapenos!
