Title:
Yeast Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. Shortening
½ cup sugar
1 Tbsp. salt
1 cup boiling water
1 cup milk
1 pkg. dry yeast
½ cup warm water
1 tsp. sugar
1 egg, beaten
3½ cups flour, divided

Directions:
Mix together yeast, warm water, and 1 tsp. sugar according to package directions to activate yeast.
Place shortening, ½ cup sugar, and salt in a large bowl.  Add boiling water.  Stir until shortening is melted and sugar dissolved.
Add milk and egg.  Stir to mix.
Add half of flour to liquid mixture.  Stir in yeast.
Add enough remaining flour to form a stiff dough.
Cover and let rise until doubled in bulk.
Punch down, make rolls and let rise again.  Bake at 425 until golden brown.
