Title:
German Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
1 can chicken & rice soup
Flour
Yellow Prepared Mustard - not Djon style
Oil for frying

Directions:
Spread pork chops with yellow mustard the dredge in flour.  Brown in oil.  Remove chops from skillet and drain oil.  Put chops back in skillet, pour chicken and rice soup over.  Add 1/4 to 1/2 can of water.  Cover and simmer for 30 to 35 minutes or until done; basting with pan liquid during the cook time.

Submitter: 
MLT
Description: 
A long time family favorite taught to me by a German friend, hence the name!
This recipe has spread from my immediate family to all branches of the family tree across the country.  Worth trying!
