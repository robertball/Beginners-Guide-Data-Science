Title:
Easy Pesto Bread Rounds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10-oz (approx.) loaf French or Italian bread, cut into 18-20 or so 3/4" slices
1/2 cup Mayonnaise (can  use Lite)
1/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan Cheese
2 T. grated Asiago cheese
(or use 1/2 cup all grated Parmesan)
2 T. commercial or homemade Basil Pesto
1/2 tsp minced Garlic
1/8 tsp. Black Pepper
1/4 tsp. Salt
Preheat broiler.  Put sliced bread on cookie sheet(s) in single layer.  Broil 1-2 minutes until golden brown and toasted (watch so that it does not burn!)   Remove from oven.   Mix rest of ingredients in bowl.  Turn bread slices over to untoasted side.  Spread equal amounts of Mayo mixture onto each bread slice.  Return to oven and broil for 4-5 minutes, until golden brown and bubbling.
Serve hot, or at room temp.   Optional:  Garnish with black olive slices, sun dried tomatoes, roasted red peppers, sprigs of fresh Cilantro
