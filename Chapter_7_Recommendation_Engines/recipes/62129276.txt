Title:
Amazing, Flaky Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Shortening
2 C Flour
1/2 C Ice Cold Water
1/3 C Flour
1 t Salt

Directions:
Cut together the shortening and the 1 cup of flour into pea size balls.
Mix the following together until smooth:  Ice Cold Water, 1/3 C Flour and Salt.
Pour over prepared dough and fold until miquid is incorporated.
Divide into 2 equal portions.
Makes 2 bottom crusts or 1 top and 1 bottom.
Fill and nake as desired.

Submitter: 
amom2boys
Description: 
This is a simple (but not extremely healthy) pie crust recipe that is always flaky and delicious.  All things in moderation!
