Title:
No Crust Egg Custard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
4 eggs beaten
1/4 cup melted butter
3 T. flour
1 lg. can Pet evporated milk
1 tsp. vanilla
nutmeg sprinkled ontop.

Directions:
Butter and flour a pie plate.  Mix and pour all ingredients into pie plate, sprinkle with nutmeg and bake approximately 30 minutes at 350 degrees.  Remove from oven while still shaky in the middle.

Submitter:

Description: 
Very old recipe. Good for a not too sweet dessert with just what you probably have on hand.
