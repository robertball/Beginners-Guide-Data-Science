Title:
Awesome Annie's Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
2 4oz cans of mushrooms
1 bag of pearl onions
3-4 large red potatoes quartered
1/2 bag baby carrots
2-3 ribs of celery sliced
1 1/4 cups beef broth
5 1/2 pounds pot roast

Directions:
Dust the roast with flour and brown it in a little olive oil in a hot pan.  Remove roast and add beef broth and deglaze pan. In a slow cooker, mix cream of mushroom soup, dry onion soup mix, canned mushrooms, vegetables, and beef broth from deglazed pan. Place pot roast in slow cooker and coat with soup mixture.
Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.
