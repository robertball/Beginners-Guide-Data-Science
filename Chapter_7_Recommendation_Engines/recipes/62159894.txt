Title:
Italian Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz pkg tri-colored rotini pasta or penne pasta
3/4 lb Italian Dry Salami
1/2 green bell pepper
1/2 red bell pepper
1/2 med red onion
3 pkgs Zesty Italian dressing mix
1 cup zesty Italian prepared dressing
1 small can chopped olives
1/2 cup fresh pearl sized mozzarella balls (optional)
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to pkg. directions. Drain and rinse with cold water until pasta is cool.
Cut Salami into small pieces.
Thinly half and slice peppers and onion.
Combine all ingredients except for the parmesan-slowly adding both dressings until desired flavor/saltiness is reached.
Mix together well.
Toss with parmesan cheese.
