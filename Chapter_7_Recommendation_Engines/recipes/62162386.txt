Title:
Mama Mia Pizza Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. ground chuck
1 lb. ground hot Italian sausage
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 tsp. dried basil
1/4 tsp. dried rosemary
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. onion powder
Salt and Pepper to taste
1/2 lb. block mozzarella cheese, cut into 2 inch long, 1/4 inch wide chunks.

Directions:
Mix sausage, ground chuck, and seasonings until well blended.  Shape meat into a patty around the chunk of cheese.  Grill on medium high heat until desired doneness is reached.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
I cooked these up this 4th of July and they were a hit, not one left over.  They're delicious and versatile.  You can top with just about anything you'd find on a pizza.
