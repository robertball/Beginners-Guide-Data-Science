Title:
Turkey Burgers with Grilled Red Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/960246.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
3 tablespoons scallions, chopped
2 tablespoons soy sauce, low sodium
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon parsley
1/4 teaspoon seasoned pepper
1 medium red onion
2 teaspoons olive oil
hamburger buns

Directions:
Cut onion into grillable slices. In bowl, combine turkey, scallions, soy sauce, garlic powder, parsley and seasoned pepper. Shape into 4 patties. Brush burgers and onions with olive oil.  Grill burgers about 5 minutes on each side, or until thoroughly cooked.  Grill onions until desired doneness. Place burgers in buns and add grilled onion.
