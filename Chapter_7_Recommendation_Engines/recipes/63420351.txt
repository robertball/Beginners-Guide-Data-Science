Title:
Aunt Margaret's Chinese Ramen Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup salad oil
1 cup sugar
2 T. soy sauce
1/2 cup vinegar
2 pkg. oriental ramen noodles
5 green onions
1 bag cole slaw mix
5 oz slivered almonds

Directions:
Mix together the salad oil, sugar, soy sauce and vinegar and refrigerate overnight.
Discard the ramen noodle seasoning packs and crush the noodles.
Slice the green onions and mix with the cole slaw mix and ramen noodles.
Add dressing to taste.

Submitter: 
Tina Carstens
