Title:
Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups (675g) all purpose flour
2 tbsp (25g) baking powder
3/4 tsp (5g) salt
1/4 cup (50g) sugar
2/3 cup (150g) butter
2 cups (500mL) buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400C/200F.
Sift flour, baking powder, salt and sugar together.
Cut in butter with fingertips until crumbly.
Add buttermilk to flour being careful not to over mix dough.
Turn onto floured countertop/cuttingboard.
Pat dough flat with hands into 1" thick disc. Do not use rolling pin.
Cut dough with 2" biscuit cutter dipped in flour.
Place on floured cookie sheet and into oven on top rack.
Bake for 8 - 10 minutes or until golden/hollow sounding when tapped.
Remove from cookie sheet and cool on wire rack.
Enjoy!
