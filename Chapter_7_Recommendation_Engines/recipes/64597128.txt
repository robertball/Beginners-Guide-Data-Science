Title:
Ashlee's buffalo chicken dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cream cheese
2 large cans of white meat chicken
a half cup of Frank's red hot sauce
sour cream
grated cheddar or Mexican blend cheeses

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Spread cream cheese on bottom of pan used (usually 8x8 but can be doubled in a longer pan)
Mix chicken (2 large cans of white meat chicken) and a half cup of Frank's red hot sauce (I also add a bit of sour cream to this)
Pour this mixture over cream cheese
Sprinkle grated cheddar or Mexican blend cheeses over it all
Put in oven for approx 10-14 minutes until browned slightly on edges
