Title:
Mango Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4570773.jpg
Ingredients:
165ml (2/3 cup) white wine vinegar
(or alternatively 100ml of vinegar and 65ml of lemon juice)
100g (1/2 cup) brown sugar
1 onion, finely diced
4 cloves garlic, peeled
1 tbs fresh ginger
4 mangoes, peeled and slice
5 cardamom pods bruised (optional)
3 cloves (optional)
1 cinnamon stick (optional)
Chilli to taste (optional)

Directions:
vinegar, lemon juice and ginger, garlic and onions in pan. Boil and
Add cardamon, cloves and cinnamon and chili, brown sugar. after 30 min = reduce to 50%
add mango for 5 minutes.
