Title:
Personal Size Fruit Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons butter
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup fruit (any kind)
1 teaspoon sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Put butter in a 2-3 cup baking dish and place in oven until melted. Make a batter of the 4 ingredients. Pour over the butter, then sprinkle the fruit on top of the batter. DON"T STIR. Sprinkle on the remaining teaspoon of suger and bake 15-20 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
I downsized this from a recipe that made a 9x13 pan.  An old recipe, it was popular in the late '60's and '70s (that would be 1960-1970)
