Title:
Four Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can green beans
1 can lima beans
1 can kidney beans
1 can chick peas
3/4 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup finely chopped green pepper
1/2 cup chopped onion (red or sweet)
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup cider vinegar (must be cider)
1/2 cup oil (light, such as canola)
freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Drain beans, rinse and drain again.
In large bowl, mix beans with celery, pepper and onion.
Mix sugar/vinegar/oil and pour over vegetables.
Add freshly ground black pepper and stir gently until all sugar is dissolved.
Refrigerate until chilled. Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Gwen Pomeroy
Description: 
I didn't like bean salads until I tasted this one. Now I'm a big fan. Try it,you'll like it. Compliments of Kim.
