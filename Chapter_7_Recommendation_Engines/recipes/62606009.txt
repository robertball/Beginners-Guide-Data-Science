Title:
Corn Bread Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package corn bread mix
1 envelope ranch salad dressing mix
1 cup milk
6 boneless skinless chicken breast
2 tbls vegetable oil

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine corn bread mix and salad dressing mix.  Pour milk into a shallow bowl.  Dip chicken in milk, then place in bag and shake to coat.
In a large skillet over medium heat; brown chicken in oil on both sides.  Cook, uncovered, over medium heat for 6-7 minuteson each side or until juices run clear.
