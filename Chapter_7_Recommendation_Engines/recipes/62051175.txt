Title:
Khalua Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4C Khalua
1C milk
1 large pack chocolate chip cookies
1 tub whipped topping
1 large chocolate bar, chopped

Directions:
Mix together milk and Khalua.
Dip cookies in milk one at a time, drain and line the base of a spring form pan, filling in the gaps with broken cookies.
Cover with whipped toppping.
Repeat cookie layer.
Top with chopped chocolate.
Cover with foil and freeze overnight.
Remove from freezer for 10 mins before serving.
