Title:
Simple Sausage Cheese Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups boxed biscuit mix
1 pound hot sausage
1 pound sharp cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F
Mix all ingredients
Form into balls
Bake 18 minutes
