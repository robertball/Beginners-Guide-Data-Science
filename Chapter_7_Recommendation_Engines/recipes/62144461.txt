Title:
Korean Beef Shortribs Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp chopped garlic (about 3 cloves)
3/8 cup soy sauce
2 Tbsp sugar
1 whole Asian pear, peeled, cored, and then pureed or crushed
1 Tbsp honey
1 Tbsp Japanese rice wine (mirin)*
1 Tbsp sesame oil

Directions:
1. Mix marinade together until sugar and honey are dissolved/distributed.
2. Can be stored in refrigerator or freezer for later use.
(Serves 4)
*If you don't have access to the rice wine, a splash of dry white wine will also work here.

Submitter: 
Cooking Mama
Description: 
This marinade leaves the meat amazingly tender and gives a sweet soy flavor. It is delicious served with bap (rice).
