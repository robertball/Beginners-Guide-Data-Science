Title:
Mom's Fruit Cocktail Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsifted all purpose flour
1/4 cup wheat germ
1 cup sugar
1 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp pure vanilla extract
1 can (8 3/4 oz) fruit cocktail, undrained
1/2 cup chopped walnuts, almonds or pecans
Frosting:
2 TBS butter, softened
1 pkg (3 oz) cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup Confectioners' sugar
2 TBS honey
dash salt

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Mix flour, wheat germ, sugar, soda, vanilla, fruit cocktail and nuts together in an 8x8x2-inch ungreased cake pan
2. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.  Frost immediately.
3.  To make frosting: In a small bowl with electric mixer @ medium speed, blend butter, cream cheese, Confectioners' sugar, honey and salt smoothly.

Submitter: 
SBFRIAR
Description: 
My mother-in-law gave me this recipe and it has become a favorite at Easter time with our family.  Light and delicious!  Best when chilled and better the second day.
