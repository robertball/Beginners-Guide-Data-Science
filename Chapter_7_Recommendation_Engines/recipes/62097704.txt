Title:
Simplest Crab Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 1lb packages frozen immitation crab meat
1 bunch green onion
1 cup miracle whip salad dressing
1 1/2 t. garlic powder

Directions:
Finely chop both the crab meat and onion, using as much of the green ends of the onions as possible
Mix all ingredients in a bowl
Chill for 2 hours or overnight
Serve on crackers of your choice
Reduce or add miracle whip as desired.

Submitter: 
Angie Bowers
Description: 
This is the cheapest, simplest variation of a crab dip that I've ever seen.  It goes over well with young and old alike!  This recipe is requested of me at every family gathering and until now the recipe has remained a secret!  Enjoy!
