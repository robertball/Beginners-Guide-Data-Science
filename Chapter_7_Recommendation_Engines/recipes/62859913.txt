Title:
Southwest "Chicken" Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup lime juice
2 tablespoons olive oil
4 medium garlic cloves, minced
8 tablespoons chopped cilantro
1 teaspoons chile powder
1 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
nonstick cooking spray
4 medium seitan chicken breast
One 15oz can black beans, drained
4 medium-large roma tomatoes, diced
8 tablespoons chopped scallions
4 cups organic romaine lettuce

Directions:
Place the lime juice, olive oil, garlic, cilantro, chile powder, cumin, and salt in a closed jar.  Shake until well mixed.
Lightly coat a grill with nonstick cooking spray, heat and grill the "chicken" until done.  Slice into 1-inch strips.
Mix together the chicken, beans, tomatoes, scallions, lettuce and a jar mixture and serve.
