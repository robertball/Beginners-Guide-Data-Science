Title:
Basil Chickpea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans chickpeas, drained
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup vegan tofu mayonnaise (to taste)
1 cup chopped fresh basil
2 cloves crushed garlic
3 stalks celery, chopped

Directions:
In a food processor, puree the mayonnaise, basil, garlic, 1 can chickpeas and celery. Leave somewhat chunky. Add second can of chickpeas. Refrigerate for up to a day to marry flavors, and serve on bread or buns.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
