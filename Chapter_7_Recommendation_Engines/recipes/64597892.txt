Title:
Basic white rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of rice
2 cups of boiling water
1tblspn of canola oil
Salt to taste
1/4 tspn of powdered garlic (optional)

Directions:
Put oil in the pot and warm up
Put in the rice , salt and garlic, then add the boiling water, gradially until the rice is covered, about 1/2" -1" .
Bring to a boil uncovered
As soon as it boils, cover it and lower the temp to low, check back in about 20 minutes , if dry, turn the rice and cover for another 15 min or until tender!

It's simple and easy, yet tasty! Me being Puertorrican, I love rice!
