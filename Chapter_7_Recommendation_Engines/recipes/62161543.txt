Title:
Cynful Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter flavored shortening
1/2 c butter
2 1/2 c flour
1 c dark brown sugar
1/2 c sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 c semi-sweet morsels
1 c milk choclate morsels
1 c chopped walnuts, pecans or hazelnuts (optional)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees
In a large mixing bowl cream shortening and butter at high speed until softened.
Add half the flour to the butter mixture.
Then add brown sugar, sugar, eggs, vanilla and baking soda, beat thoroughly until all is combined.
Stir in remaining flour.
Stir in chocolate morsels and nuts if using them.
Drop heaping teaspoons of dough on an ungreased cookie sheet 2 inches apart.
Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until edges are lightly browned.
Remove to cooling racks.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
These are my standard chocolate chip cookies I have been making for years. The addition of nuts is optional, but I do love these with added pecans. These cookies don't spread out super thin, they have a nice height to them. They are loved by all!
