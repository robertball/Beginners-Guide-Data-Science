Title:
Turkey Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/639564.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
2 large bell peppers
1 small onion, chopped
3 gloves garlic, chopped
1 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
1 egg
1/2 cup breadcrumbs
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp basil
1/4 cup grated locatelli cheese
1 (46 ounce) bottle tomato juice

Directions:
Wash bell peppers, slice down the center in half, remove seeds & stems, set aside.
In large bowl combine all ingredients, except tomato juice. Shape ground turkey mixture into 4 evenly shaped balls and press into each pepper. Place prepared peppers in slow cooker and cover with tomato juice.
Cook on high for 2 hours, then reduce to low heat for 4-5 more hours. Baste occasionally.
Serve over hot cooked rice, noodles or alone.

Submitter:

Description: 
A healthy & yummy version of stuffed peppers. So easy to make. Perfect served over rice, noodles or alone with some good bread.
