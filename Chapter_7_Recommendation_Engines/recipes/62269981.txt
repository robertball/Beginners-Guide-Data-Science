Title:
Yummy Apple Cinnamon Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small apples, red delicious or Gala, thinly sliced
Cinnamon
Sugar

Directions:
Core apples, then slice apples paper-thin.
Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
Place apple slices on baking sheet.
Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar on top of apples.
Bake at 225 degrees for about 2 hours (1 hour each side)
Once apples are lightly browned, turn over, and sprinkling the other side with more cinnamon and sugar.
Let cool on wire rack.
Store in an air tight container.

Submitter: 
Trish McQuhae
Description: 
Sliced and seasoned baked apple chips.
