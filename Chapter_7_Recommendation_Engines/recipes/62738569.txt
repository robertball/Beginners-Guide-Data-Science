Title:
Warm & Cheesy Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz soft cream cheese
2 c. sour cream
1/4 jar bacon bits
2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
1 c. chopped green onion
1 can diced green chilis

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.  Combine all ingredients, bake 25-30 minutes until hot.

Submitter: 
chefmom
Description: 
Baked dip goes great with tortilla chips
