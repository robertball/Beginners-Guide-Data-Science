Title:
Vanilla Wafer Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/245801.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups vanilla wafer crumbs
1/3 cup butter

Directions:
Combine wafer crumbs and butter or margarine together thoroughly.  Press into 9 inch pie plate.  Chill until set.

Submitter: 
Carol
Description: 
This is an unbaked crumb crust. Very easy.
