Title:
Lemon Grass Ginger Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (.18 ounce) jars McCormick® Gourmet Collection™ Lemon Grass
1 cup sugar
4 cups water
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Ground Ginger
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes.
Refrigerate 4 hours.
Strain and place in an ice cream maker.  Freeze thoroughly.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Gourmet Collection®
Description: 
Lemon grass and ginger supply the perfect flavor for this refreshing sorbet. This recipe was developed by the McCormick Corporate Chefs for a luncheon given during the 45th National Chicken Cooking Contest held in Baltimore on May 30, 2003.
