Title:
"Mock" Crookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1034698.jpg
Ingredients:
1   package  Puff Pastry
30  Oreos
5-6 Tablespoons cream cheese
confectioners' sugar for dusting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Spray cookie sheet lightly with spray oil.  Cut each pastry sheet into 15 pieces for a total of 30.  Spread out with your fingers.  Put 1/2 teaspoon cream cheese on one side of the Oreo.  Fold the pastry over cookie and seal the edges.  12-15 on the cookie sheet.  Bake for 18-22 minutes, until golden brown.  Let cool and dust with the confectioners'.  Seal any leftovers.

Submitter:

Description: 
After seeing on TV that Crookies were the latest sensation in Toronto and New York, I thought I would see what I could do.  These are so easy and a huge favorite with the kids at parties.
