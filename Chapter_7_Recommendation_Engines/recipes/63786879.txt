Title:
Hot Sausage Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground hot or mild pork sausage
1 (10-ounce) can diced tomato with green chiles (use either hot or mild)
1 (8-ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 C sour cream
1-2 tsp chipolte chili powder.
3/4 cup (3 ounces) shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
Corn chips $

Directions:
Cook sausage in a large skillet, stirring until meat crumbles and is no longer pink; drain well.
Drain diced tomato and green chiles, reserving 1/4 cup liquid.  (Add more or less liquid depending on how thick you would like the dip to be.)
Stir together sausage, diced tomato with green chiles, cream cheese, sour cream, chile powder and reserved liquid.  Spoon into a lightly greased 1-quart baking dish; sprinkle with Cheddar cheese. Bake at 350Â° for 20 to 25 minutes or until bubbly. Or cook in crock pot for 2 hours on high.  Serve with corn chips.
