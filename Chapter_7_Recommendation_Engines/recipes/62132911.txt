Title:
Grandma's Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/355921.jpg
Ingredients:
2 8-oz packages cream cheese, softened
7 c plus 2 T sifted powdered sugar (1 1/2 boxes, sifting optional)
1 c butter, softened, divided use
1 T vanilla
4 oz semisweet chocolate, melted
3 eggs
2 1/4 c flour
1 1/2 t soda
1 1/2 t salt
3/4 c milk

Directions:
Blend cream cheese with sugar and 3/4 cup of the butter.
Stir in vanilla.
Add melted chocolate and mix thoroughly; remove 3 cups of mixture and set aside for icing the cake.
To remaining cheese mixture add remaining 1/4 cup butter and eggs and beat until smooth.
Sift together flour, soda, and salt.
Add dry ingredients to batter alternately with milk, starting and ending with flour.
Turn batter into two round 9-inch greased and floured cake pans.
Bake at 350 40-55 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
Cool cake in pans 10 minutes and then remove onto racks to cool thoroughly.
Ice cake with reserved mixture, and refrigerate until half an hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Marianne
Description: 
This rich chocolate cake starts with the frosting and ends up with a very beautiful result!  This has been a birthday cake favorite for years!
