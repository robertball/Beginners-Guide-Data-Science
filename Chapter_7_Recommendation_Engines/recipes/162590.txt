Title:
Fiesta Chopped Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983944.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) bag salad greens
1 (6 ounce) package OSCAR MAYER Southwestern Seasoned Chicken Breast Strips
1 cup KRAFT Natural Three Cheese Crumbles
1 cup coarsely broken tortilla chips
1 avocado, peeled, pitted and chopped
1/4 cup chopped red onions
1/2 cup KRAFT Ranch Dressing

Directions:
Toss greens with all remaining ingredients except dressing.
Add dressing; mix lightly.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Salad Dressing
Description: 
Toss together crisp salad greens, chicken strips, crumbled cheese, tortilla chips and avocado with KRAFT ranch dressing for a festive lunch or dinner.
