Title:
Amish Breakfast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (12) oz pks Jimmy Dean Low Fat Sausage
1 cup sweet onion, chopped
1 cup zucchini, chopped
1 cup bell pepper, any color,chopped
1 lg aneheim chili, roasted and chopped
18 eggs, lightly beaten
1 pkg frozen shredded southwestern hash brown potatoes, thawed
3 cups low fat shredded Cheddar cheese
2 cups low fat, small curd cottage cheese
1 1/2 cup low fat, shredded Swiss cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook sausage and set aside. Add Onion, zucchini, bell pepper and chili and sautee in remaining grease from sausage until just tender, about 4 minutes. In a bowl, combine the remaining ingredients; stir in sausage and vegetables. Transfer to a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 70 minutes or until set and bubbly. Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting.
