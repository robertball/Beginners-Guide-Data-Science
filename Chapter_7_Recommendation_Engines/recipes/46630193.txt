Title:
Crispy Herb Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup dry potato flakes...try flavored
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon garlic salt and other seasonings
1 (3 pound) chicken, skin removed, cut into pieces
1/3 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F (190 degrees C).   Grease or line with foil a baking sheet or a 13 x 9 inch baking pan.
In a medium bowl, combine potato flakes, Parmesan cheese, and garlic salt.  Stir until well mixed.
Dip chicken pieces into melted butter or margarine, and roll in potato flake mixture to coat.  Place in prepared pan.
Bake for 45 to 50 minutes, or until chicken is tender and golden brown.
