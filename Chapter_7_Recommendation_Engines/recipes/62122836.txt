Title:
Baby Wipes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon baby lotion
1/2 teaspoon baby wash
1/2 teaspoon baby shampoo
2 cups water
1 roll paper towels

Directions:
Boil water.
Add lotion, wash and shampoo.  Stir to mix.
Let cool.
Put into a large plastic tub with a tight fitting lid.
Cut a roll of paper towels in half.  Remove cardboard center.
Set paper towels in liquid until fully absorbed.
To use, pull towels from the center of the roll.  Cover tightly when not in use.
