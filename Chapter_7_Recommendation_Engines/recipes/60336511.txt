Title:
Easy Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 cups cooked macaroni
2 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 c frozen peas (opt)
1/2 c Miracle Whip
Garlic salt and dill, optional
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups French fried onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix soups and miracle whip with spices.  In 13x9-inch baking dish, combine the macaroni, tuna, and soup. Mix well, and then top with cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 25 minutes, or until bubbly. Sprinkle with fried onions, and bake for another 5 minutes. Serve hot.

Submitter:

Description: 
Tuna, macaroni, creamy soup, cheese and fried onions are all you need to make this super easy tuna casserole that I learned from my roommate. It's great as leftovers, too.Modified from: Easy Tuna Casserole
