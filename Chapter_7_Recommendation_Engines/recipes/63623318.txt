Title:
Pesto Chicken Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Olive oil spray
1/2 cup frozen chopped or diced onion, defrosted
1/4 cup prepared pesto sauce
1/4 cup plain bread crumbs
3/4 pound ground white meat - chicken or turkey
1 egg white
4 Tbs fresh or dried chives
Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Line a baking sheet with foil and spray with olive oil spray.  Mix onion, pesto sauce, bread crumbs, ground chicken or turkey and egg white together.  Add chive, salt and pepper. Divide the mixture into two pieces, place on baking tray and shape into 2 lovaves about 6 inches by 3 inches.
Bake 15 minutes.  A thermometer should read 165 degrees.
