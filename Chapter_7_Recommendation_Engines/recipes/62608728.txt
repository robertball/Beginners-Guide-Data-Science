Title:
PF Chang's Taste-Alike Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground chicken breast (turkey breast works too)
1 large onion, finely chopped
1 bunch green onions, finely chopped
1/2 cup pine nuts, finely chopped
1 8-ounce can water chestnuts, finely chopped
2 T fresh garlic, minced
2 t fresh ginger, minced
1-1/2 T soy sauce
2 t Asian sesame oil
2 t Asian chili pepper sauce
1/4 cup hoisin sauce
2 T rice or red wine vinegar
Butter Lettuce Leaves - rinsed and drained (you can also use iceberg in a pinch)

Directions:
Brown ground chicken over medium heat. Add onion and garlic. Crumb meat into tiny pieces. Add remaining ingredients except lettuce.
If you're having a party, put the meat mixture in a crockpot on WARM.
Spoon meat mixture onto piece of lettuce and eat like a taco.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
I combined a bunch of recipes for the ultimate taste-alike. They're almost the same as PF Chang's.
