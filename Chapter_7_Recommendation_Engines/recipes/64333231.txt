Title:
Strawberry Oatmeal Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup almond milk
1/2 cup rolled oats
1 banana, broken into chunks
2 cups frozen strawberries
1/2 cup frozen cherries

Directions:
In a blender, combine almond milk, oats, banana, cherries and strawberries. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
Falen Teferri
Description: 
This is a fast and filling vegan smoothie with a deep pink color and a rich, creamy texture.Modified from: Strawberry Oatmeal Breakfast Smoothie
