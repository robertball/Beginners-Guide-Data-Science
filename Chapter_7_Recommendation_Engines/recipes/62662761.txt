Title:
Pizza Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/601101.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked Rotini
1 pound ground sausage
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup sliced pepperoni
2 4 oz cans Mushrooms
1 can black olives
20 ounces pizza sauce
4 tablespoons milk
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 Cup shredded cheddar chese (or any kind)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cook noodles according to package directions.
In a medium skillet over medium-high heat, brown the sausage and garlic. Stir in the noodles, pepperoni, pizza sauce and milk, and mix well. Pour this mixture into a 2-quart gresed casserole dish.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes, top with the cheese, then bake for 5 to 10 more minutes.
