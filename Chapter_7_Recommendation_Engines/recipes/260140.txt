Title:
Lobster Boil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
12 cups water
1 cup dry white wine
1/4 cup kosher salt
1 tablespoon whole white peppercorns, coarsely ground
1 tablespoon paprika
2 teaspoons lemon zest
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 bay leaves, crushed
2 (1 pound) whole lobsters

Directions:
Bring water to a boil in a tall stockpot. Add white wine, salt, peppercorns, paprika, lemon zest, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, and bay leaves. Stir and return to a boil.
Lower lobsters carefully into the stockpot. Cover and cook over medium heat until lobsters are bright red and claws are easily pulled off, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
JULESMICHY
Description: 
Throw it into the pot with your shellfish of choice (live, frozen, tails, etc.) to add delicious oomph to your bugs! Simmer an additional 3 minutes for every additional pounds.
