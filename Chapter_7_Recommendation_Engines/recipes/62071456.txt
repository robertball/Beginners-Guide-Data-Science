Title:
Italian Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
2 large eggs
1 1/2 c plain bread crumbs
1 c parmesan or parm/romano blend cheese
1 tbsp Worchestire sauce
1 clove garlic, chopped
Italian Seasoning to taste
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in bowl well, adding more sauce, garlic, and spice to taste.  Place evenly into meat loaf pan and bake for 30 minutes at 400 degrees.  Perfect for a couple as the leftovers will make a wonderful sandwich or Shepherds Pie.

Submitter: 
RACHRION
Description: 
A simple, Italian spiced meatloaf that even those tired of the regular meatloaf will love!
