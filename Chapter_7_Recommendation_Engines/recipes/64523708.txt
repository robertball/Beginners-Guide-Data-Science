Title:
Holiday Hot Buttered Rum Batter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cube or half cup of organic salted butter, room temperature
3 tablespoons pure organic maple syrup
2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon pure rum extract

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in small mixing bowl and blend well by hand or beater until thoroughly combined and smooth.  Keep in refrigerator until use.  Scoop what want to use into coffee cups and warm in microwave when ready to use.  Stores up to one month.   Use according to desired taste, 1 tsp. on up to 1 tablespoon.  Can be mixed into hot coffee, hot cocoa, warmed milk or non dairy milk, can also use with a generous splash of rum, or can warm up with rum in demitasse cups.  Top with whip cream, and sprinkle with nutmeg.
