Title:
Nana Whisnant's Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
3/4 c. vegetable oil
2 1/2 c. milk
2 1/2 c. all purpose flour
1 1/2 Tbsp sugar
4 tsp. baking powder
3/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Combine eggs, oil, and milk in a large bowl and beat well (I use a stand mixer and Mom uses a hand blender).
In a seperate bowl combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a seperate bowl.
Add dry ingredients to whe wet ingredients and beat on medium speed until batter is nearly smooth.
Spray waffle iron with non stick cooking spray and bake in a hot waffle iron, about 3 minutes depending on the size of the iron.
