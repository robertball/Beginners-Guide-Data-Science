Title:
Grilled Cheese Chickweed Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6484509.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Pieces of whole grain bread
2 Slices sharp cheddar cheese
2 Slices mozzarella cheese
Sliced roma tomato
Sliced mushrooms
Fresh chickweed
Butter

Directions:
Turn the stove to low heat.
Butter one side of each bread piece and place one piece on a skillet.
Cover the slice of bread with one piece of sharp cheddar and one piece of mozzarella cheese
Layer sliced mushrooms and tomatoes then place a layer of chickweed on top
On top of that place one piece of sharp cheddar and one piece of mozzarella cheese
Finally place the other slice of bread on top with the butter side facing up
Grill on low until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown and cheese is melting over the edges of the bread
Flip the sandwich, turn to low heat, and cover
Itâ€™s done when both sides are golden brown and the cheese is been melted
