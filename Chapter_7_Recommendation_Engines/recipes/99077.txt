Title:
Smoked Sausage Reuben with Honey Mustard Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup honey mustard
2 teaspoons snipped dill
4 slices dark rye bread
1/3 head cabbage, finely shredded
1 pound smoked sausage, sliced lengthwise
8 ounces Swiss cheese, sliced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  In a medium bowl, combine the mayonnaise, honey mustard and dill to make the honey mustard dill sauce.
Place the bread slices side by side in a baking dish. Spread each slice with approximately 1 tbsp. sauce. Layer each slice with 1/4 of the cheese, cabbage and sausage.
Bake for 10 minutes or until warmed through and the cheese has melted. Serve open-face style.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee
