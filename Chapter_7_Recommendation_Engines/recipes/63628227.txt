Title:
Chocolate Chip Walnut cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1/4 cup rolled oats
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/8 tsp cinnamon
6 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
6 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp granulated sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1/4 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
1/4 cup dried cherries

Directions:
Heat oven to 375F.
In a bowl, combine flour, oats, baking soda and cinnamon. In another bowl, cream butter and sugars until fluffy, 3-5 mins. Blend flour mixture into butter mixture.
Stir in choc chips, walnuts and cherries.
Drop dough balls (1tbsp each) onto ungreased cookie sheets, 2 inches apart.
Bake until golden brown, about 9 mins.  Remove from oven; let cool on sheets for 3 mins then transfer to foil to cool fully.
