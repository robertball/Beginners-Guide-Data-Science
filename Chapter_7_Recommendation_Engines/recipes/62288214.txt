Title:
Peanut Butter Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
PEANUT BUTTER FILLING:
2 cups peanut butter (plain or chunky)
2 1/2 cups confectioners sugar
2/3 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup butter, softened
CHOCOLATE TOPPING:
6 oz pkg. semi-sweet or milk chocolate pieces
1/2 cup butter

Directions:
Mix Peanut Butter filling together well (this is the hardest part) and press into 9 x 13 inch pan.
Melt chocolate and butter together and stir till combined. Pour chocolate topping over peanut butter filling.  Cool overnight or at least 8 hours.
