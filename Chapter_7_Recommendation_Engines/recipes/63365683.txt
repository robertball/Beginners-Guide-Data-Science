Title:
Mohnschnecken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500 grams flour
yeast packet
2 tsp sugar
water
250 mL milk
60 grams sugar
80 grams butter
egg pinch salt
filling: Mohnfix, raisins/currants, chopped almonds

Directions:
Put flour in a bowl and make a hole in the middle. Put the yeast, sugar, and a little bit of warm water in the middle. Let it sit for 5 minutes. Then knead the dough, let rise, knead, rise, knead. Separate the dough into 2 balls. Rolls it out into a rectangle about the size of a cookie sheet. Spread Mohnfix filling, raisins, and chopped almonds on the dough. Roll it up, and chop of 1-inch rolls. Brush with egg yolk and bake 20-30 minutes at 200degC.
