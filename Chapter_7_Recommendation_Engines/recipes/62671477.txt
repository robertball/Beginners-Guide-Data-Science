Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
1 can of corn
2 cans red beans (washed & drained)
1 can olives
1 can rotel
3 tbs ranch seasoning mix
1/2 c taco seasoning
1 onion
2 cans of water

Directions:
In lg pot, brown ground beef, onion, & taco seasoning.  Add in remaining ingrediants.  Cook on med until boiling, reduce heat & simmer 20 minutes
