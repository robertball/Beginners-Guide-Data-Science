Title:
Vegetarian Paella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 medium onion chopped
1 medium bell pepper, seeded, ribbed, and chopped
2 cloves of garlic minced
1 1/2 cips uncooked uncoverted rice
2 cans (14.5 oz each) vegatable broth
1/2 cup white wine
1/2 teaspoon saffron threads,smoked paprika or ground tumeric
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 (15 oz) can garbonzo beans, rinsed and drained
1 package (11 oz) frozen artichoke hearts
1/2 cup frozen peas thawed

Directions:
heat oil in a medium skillet. Add onion, bell pepper and garlic. Cook stirring occasionally, until onion softens. Transfer to the slow cooker. Add rice, broth, wine, saffron, red pepper flakes and salt. Stir to level rice. cover and cook on low for 3 hours.
Add garbonzo beans, artichoke hearts and peas. Cover and cook on low until rice is tender and all the liquid is absorbed. Stir well and serve hot.
