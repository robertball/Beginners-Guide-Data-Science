Title:
Banana Colada Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pineapple tidbits 100% juice
1 medium banana
1 cup pineapple juice
1/2 cup coconut, sweetened

Directions:
Put the panana, pineapple tidbits and pineapple juice into a blender and mix until well blended.  Add coconut flakes and mix just enough to be sure flakes are mixed through.  Pour the mixture into your popsicle makers.  Freeze.
