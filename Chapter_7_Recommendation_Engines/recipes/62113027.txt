Title:
Grilled Apple & Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 med. peaches
3 med. apples (I used Gala)
1/4 cup light brown sugar
1 T ground cinnamon
1/2 stick butter, melted...2T reserved
2 packages crunchy cinnamon granola bars (4 bars)
2 packages crunchy apple crisp granola bars (4 bars)
Vanilla Bean ice cream (optional)

Directions:
Slice peaches in half, remove pit
Peel, core and slice apples in half
Grill apples and peaches on med-high grill for 3 min. each side
Remove from grill and cut into bite-size pieces
Place in 9" (or similar) oven-safe casserole dish
Add light brown sugar, cinnamon and butter, (minus 2 T)
and mix well.
Place granola bars in large plastic storage bag and smash until well broken
Spread granola on top of apple-peach mixture
Drizzle remaining 2 T melted butter on top of granola
Place oven-safe casserole dish in grill for 10-12 minutes
Remove and allow to cool slightly (5-10 minutes)
Serve with scoop of vanilla bean ice cream (Optional)

Jim Rockford

Nice summertime dessert on the grill.
