Title:
Garlic Red Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 pounds red potatoes, quartered
1 cup canola oil
3 teaspoons minced garlic
2 teaspoon salt
2 lemons, juiced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place potatoes in a large baking dish.
In a small bowl combine oil, garlic, salt and lemon juice; pour over potatoes and stir to coat.
Bake, covered, in preheated oven for 30 minutes.  Uncover and bake an additional 10 minutes, or until golden brown.
