Title:
Crunches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/415761.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups butterscotch chips
1/4 cup peanut butter
3 1/2 cups cornflakes cereal

Directions:
Melt the butterscotch chips over low heat and add peanut butter.
Stir in cornflakes.
Drop on waxed paper and let cool.
