Title:
Creamy Cran-Raspberry Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 oz.) package raspberry Jell-O
2 cups boiling water
2/3 cup (1/2 can) cranberry sauce, whole OR jellied
1 pint sour cream (regular or light)

Directions:
Pour boiling water over gelatin in a metal or plastic bowl. Refrigerate about 1 hour until very thick (consistency of egg whites). With mixer or whisk, beat in cranberry sauce and sour cream. Pour into pretty 5-cup serving bowl or gelatin mold. Chill till firm.

Submitter: 
RCKim
Description: 
Sweet-tart, cool, and creamy, this refreshing combination of raspberry gelatin, cranberry sauce, and sour cream has been a favorite side dish at our holiday table for over fifteen years.
