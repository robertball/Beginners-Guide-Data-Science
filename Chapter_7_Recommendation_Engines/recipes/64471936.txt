Title:
Easy Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2476513.jpg
Ingredients:
12 marshmallows
8 oz. dipping chocolate, melted
1 cup shredded coconut
1 sheet wax paper or parchment

Directions:
1. Dip half of a marshmallow in melted chocolate.
2. Roll the marshmallow in coconut
3. Place on wax paper until the chocolate cools
4. Serve!

Submitter: 
StephAnn Knotts
Description: 
This special g.i.s.h.w.h.e.s. recipe was passed down from Dinomite's great-great-aunt's brother's dog's grandmother. It's quick and delicious!
