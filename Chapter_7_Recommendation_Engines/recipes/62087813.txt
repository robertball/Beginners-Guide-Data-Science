Title:
Kirsten's dark chocolate chip cookie bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dark brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup butter
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
2 cups flour
2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
1. pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
2. mix both sugars, then add melted butter; mix well.
3. add vanilla and eggs, mix well.
4. add flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and mix very well. then add chocolate chips.
5. put into greased 9x13 pan.  cook for 40 mins.
