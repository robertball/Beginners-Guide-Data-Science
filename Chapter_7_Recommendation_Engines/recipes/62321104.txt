Title:
Skillet Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. sliced fresh mushrooms
1 medium onion, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp. butter
1/2 cup hot water
1 Tbsp. prepared horseradish
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1/4 cup flour
1 beef flank steak (1 1/4 lb), cut into 2 inch strips
8 oz. sour cream
1 can (10 oz.) condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
Hot cooked egg noodles

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute mushrooms, onion and garlic in butter until tender.  With a slotted spoon, transfer from the skillet to a medium bowl.
Add water, horseradish, salt, pepper, soup and milk to bowl and stir.  Set aside.
Place flour in a large resealable plastic bag.  Add beef, a few pieces at a time and shake to coat.
In the same skillet, cook the beef until brown.
Top the beef with the mushroom mixture.  Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 hours or until beef is tender, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat and stir in sour cream.
Serve with noodles.
