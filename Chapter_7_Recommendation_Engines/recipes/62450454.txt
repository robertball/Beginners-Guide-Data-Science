Title:
Apple Pie - Laurie Cutler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 apples
3/4 cup sugar
1-2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. salt
3-4 tbsp. flour
2-3 tsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 tsp. almond extract
butter
egg, sugar, cream for top crust

Directions:
Peel, core and slice apples.  Set in large bowl.
Mix in sugar, spices, flour and lemon juice.
place ina pastry lined 9 inch pie plate.
sprinkle with vanilla and almond flavorings.  Dot with butter.
Top with Crust. Crimp edges.  Cut steam vents.  Brush either beaten egg or egg white.  Sprinkle with sugar or brush with cream or milk and sprinkle with sugar.  Bake at 35 for about 50 min.  May need to cover with foil for half of baking time.
