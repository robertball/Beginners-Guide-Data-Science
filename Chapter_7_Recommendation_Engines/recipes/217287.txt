Title:
Passover Apples and Honey (Charoset) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/971854.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tablespoons honey
1/4 cup ground cinnamon, or to taste
5 cups apples - peeled, cored and chopped
2 cups grape juice
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a bowl, mix the honey, cinnamon, apples, grape juice, and walnuts together until thoroughly combined.

Submitter: 
nadina
Description: 
Simple, quick and tasty. These ingredients go together very well. Chop the apples and walnuts depending on how coarse or fine you like the texture.
