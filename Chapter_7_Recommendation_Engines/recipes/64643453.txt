Title:
Pioneer Woman's Perfect Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5330091.jpg
Ingredients:
3  1/3 c flour
1 1/2 c crisco
1 egg, beaten
5 tbsp cold water
1 tbsp white vinegar
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Cut Crisco into flour with pastry cutter - 3-4 minutes.
To flour / crisco mixture, add in egg, then water, then water, then vinegar, then salt.
Gently stir together until all ingredients are incorporated.
Separate into three balls.
Each makes a single 9" pie crust.
Flatten slightly and place each in baggie to freeze.
Thaw @ 15 minutes before rolling out on floured surface.
