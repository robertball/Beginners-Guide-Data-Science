Title:
Warm Reuben Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4oz. reduced fat cream cheese, softened
1/2C. Thousand Island dressing
1/4# sliced corned beef, chopped (about 1C)
3/4C. well-drained sauerkraut
1pkg. (8ozs.)Swiss cheese slices, chopped
Triscuit rye crackers

Directions:
Heat oven 350 degrees.
Mix cream cheese and dressing in med. bowl; stir in all remaining ingred., except crackers.
Spread onto 9-in pie plate or shallow dish.
Bake 20 min. or until heated through or microwave on high 2-3 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Barb puts her dish in a small crockpot. I don't usually have Thousand Island dressing, so will mix mayo (2T + 2 t), ketchup (1T + 1 t), pickle relish (2T + 2t), and pinch of salt and black pepper (1/8t) to make dressing.
