Title:
Gingersnaps, Extra Crispy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup salted butter, softened
1 cups granulated white sugar
2 large egg whites, beaten slightly
1/2 cup molasses
2 teaspoons white vinegar
3 3/4 cups 100% whole wheat flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
4" fresh ginger or 3 teaspoons ground ginger
zest from 3 lemons
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Add in egg whites, molasses and vinegar.
In a separate bowl, sift flour, baking soda and spices. Add to the butter mixture and mix until well blended.
Form into 1 oz balls. Arrange 2-inches apart on greased cookie sheets. Bake 15 minutes.
Cool on cookie sheet 4-5 minutes, then move to wax paper and let cool completely.

Submitter:

Description: 
A crispy version of soft batch. Great to mash up for lemon meringue pie crust.
