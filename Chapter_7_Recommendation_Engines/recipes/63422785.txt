Title:
Grape Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. green seedless grapes
2 lbs. red seedless grapes
2 - 8 oz. cream cheese, room temperature
3/4 c. sugar
1 T. vanilla
4 Butterfingers Bars, crushed
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 c. crushed pecans, optional

Directions:
Wash and dry grapes.  Combine cream cheese, sugar and vanilla.  Mix with grapes carefully. Spread into 9x13 pan. Mix together brown sugar, candy bars and pecans.  Sprinkle on top.  Refrigerate and serve.
