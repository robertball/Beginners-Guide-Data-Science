Title:
Japanese Sticky Chicken Thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken thighs, cut in half
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
3/4 cup chicken broth
1 teaspoon sesame oil
1/3 soy sauce
2 tablespoons honey
2 tablespoons sugar
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 peice of ginger, minced
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
2 green onions, chopped
1 tablespoon sesame seeds, toasted

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, mix vinegar, broth, sesame oil, soy sauce, honey, sugar, garlic, ginger and red pepper flakes.
When mixture starts to sizzle, add chicken and bring to a boil. Turn down heat and simmer 20 minutes. Remove chicken and place on serving platter.
Turn heat to high and cook until sauce becomes a thick glaze. Remove from heat and pour over chicken. Sprinkle with green onions and sesame seeds.
