Title:
WW friendly Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean sirloin, trimmed and cubed
1 small onion, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 TBSP all purpose flour
2 TBSP olive oil
1/2 cup canned mushrooms
2 cups fat-free reduced sodium chicken broth
1 TBSP light sour cream

Directions:
heat olive oil in non-stick pan, brown meat.  Remove meat from skillett, add onions and garlic.  Cook until translucent.  Add mushrooms.  Add cooked meat.  Sprinkle with flour, stir in.  Add chicken broth.  Cook until thickened.  Stir in sour cream before serving over 1/2 cup brown rice, with a side of steamed broccoli
