Title:
Zucchini, Tomato and Onion Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium Zucchini, thinly sliced
1 large Tomato, thinly sliced
1 medium Onion, thinly sliced
1/2 tsp Dry Mustard
1 tsp Garlic Salt
1/2 cup unsalted Butter
1/4 cup Red Wine Vinegar
1 tsp dry Basil
2 cups Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Layer greased 2 quart baking dish with zuchinni,onion, and tomato.
Repeat layers.
Melt butter on stovetop and add vinegar, dry mustard, garlic salt, and basil.
Pour over vegetables.
Cover with Parmesan Cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Andrea F
Description: 
This yummy side dish is a wonderful way to use those homegrown vegetables in the summer.
This is great for a cookout served along side grilled steak, chicken, hamburgers, etc.
