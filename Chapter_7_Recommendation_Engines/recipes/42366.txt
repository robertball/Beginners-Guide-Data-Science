Title:
Ham and Cheese Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/319840.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2.5 ounce) package sliced fully cooked ham, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1 egg
1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first six ingredients; set aside.
Divide crescent dough into 24 portions. Press into greased miniature muffin cups. Spoon 1 tablespoon ham mixture into each cup.
Bake at 350 degrees for 13-15 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home's Fast Family Favorites
Description: 
These tasty little bites go over great with kids. They have fun making and eating them. These puffs are delicious with soups or as party appetizers.
