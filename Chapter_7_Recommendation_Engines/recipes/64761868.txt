Title:
Habanero Spicy Hot Buffalo Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7155870.jpg
Ingredients:
2 - 2/12lbs Chicken Wings (trimmed in pieces and tips discarded)
Note: if choose not to take the time to trim fresh wings, you can purchase frozen wing pieces and just thaw before cooking.
1/3 cup melted butter (or margarine)
3/4 cup Frank's Red Hot Wing Sauce (or your choice of wing sauce)
1 small habanero seeded and finely diced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
In large mixing bowl toss chicken wings, melted butter, Frank's Red Hot Wing sauce and diced Habanero and toss till well coated.
Bake on large foil lined baking sheet lowest rack for 30 minutes skin side down, then turning after 15 minutes.
Place baking pan on top rack and broil approximately 5 minutes turn and repeat till desired brown crispness.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
Who doesn't love Buffalo Wings on any game day! I know everyone has their own secret sauce but this is truly easy and if you like added heat...Habanero will do the trick. I am from Texas and LOVE hot and heat. I have always said if I don't sweat its NOT hot enough!
