Title:
Baked Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless, skinless chicken breast
1 cup red onion, chopped fine
1 cup celery, chopped fine
2 boiled eggs, chopped
1 cup of mayonaise
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Rub chicken breast lightly with your favorite seasoning rub.
Place chicken breast in a casserole dish.
Cover tightly and bake for one hour, until flakey. Remove foil during last 15 minutes of baking.
In the meantime hard boil two eggs, and finely chop the celery and onions.
When chicken is done, allow to cool and then shred the meat with a fork.
In a large bowl, mix eggs, celery, onions, chicken, mayonaise and salt & pepper.
Allow to chill in the refrigerator for one hour.
