Title:
Tortellini with Pumpkin Sauce and Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of your favorite pasta (I used Buitoni fresh 3-cheese tortelli)
2 Tbsp. butter
1 Tbsp. minced garlic
1 15. oz can pumpkin puree
1 cup evaporated milk
1 cup chicken broth
3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
black pepper
12 oz. bag frozen sweet peas

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions.
In a medium sauce pan, melt butter. Add garlic and cook for 3 min. Add pumpkin, evaporated milk and broth; bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occassionally. Add parmesan cheese and black pepper and stir to melt.
Prepare peas according to microwave directions. Add to sauce. Top pasta with sauce, springking with additional parmesan cheese if desired.

Submitter: 
mrs. walter
Description: 
A rich, easy pumpkin-based sauce marries perfectly with your favorite tortellini. Peas add color and extra nutrition.
