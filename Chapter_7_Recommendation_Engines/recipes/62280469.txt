Title:
No Fuss Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 1/4 cups of 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
1 cup chopped walnuts, optional
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Combine chocolate chips and sweetened condensed milk in medium-size heavy saucepan. Warm over lowest possible heat, stirring until smooth. Remove from heat; stir in walnuts (if desired) and vanilla. Spread mixture evenly into foil lined 8 or 9 inch square pan. Chill for 2 hours or until firm. Lift from pan; turn over to remove foil. Cut into squares.
