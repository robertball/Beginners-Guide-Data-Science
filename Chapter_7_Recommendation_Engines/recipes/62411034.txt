Title:
Blueberry Maple Walnut Snack Cake with Crunchy Streusel Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/438432.jpg
Ingredients:
CAKE:
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/4 maple syrup
1 cup blueberries
1 tbsp flour
1/2 cup walnuts
TOPPING:
2 tbsp flour
2 tbsp brown sugar
3/4 cup oats
1 tbsp maple syrup
3 tbsp butter, cut into cubes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease and flour an 8" x 11" or 9" x 9" baking pan.
Combine 1 1/2 cup flour, 1/2 cup oats, baking powder and salt; set aside.
Cream together 1/2 cup butter and 3/4 cup sugar until light and fluffy.
Beat in eggs, one at a time, then 1/4 cup maple syrup.
Add half the flour mixture and mix until blended; add remaining flour mixture and blend until just combined.
Dust blueberries with 1 tbsp flour and gently stir into the batter; stir in  walnuts.
Scrape batter into prepared pan.
In a separate bowl combine 2 tbsp flour, 2 tbsp brown sugar, 3/4 cup oats and 1 tbsp maple syrup.  Cut in 3 tbsp of butter until combined.  Sprinkle mixture over batter.
Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until topp is golden brown and toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
