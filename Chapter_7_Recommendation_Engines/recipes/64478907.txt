Title:
Tuna Avocado Pasta Salad - with olive oil dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2514149.jpg
Ingredients:
4.5 cups any cooked pasta (i used Rotini)
2.87 oz tin Clover Leaf Tuna with olive oil (provides enough olive oil for recipe about 2 tbsp)
1 medium carrot, diced
1/2 cup celery with leaves, chopped
1 medium onion, diced
2 tbsp Irresistibles Chef Salad Seasoning

Directions:
Boil pasta according to package directions, drain and run under cold water
cut avocado in half and remove pit, scoop out pulp and mash in small bowl
combine all ingredients in larger bowl and stir till thoroughly mixed, chill the serve

Tony Carriere

A very Tasty and Healthy salad very low fat carbs sodium and calories per serving
