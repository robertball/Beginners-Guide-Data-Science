Title:
Strawberry Blast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4520598.jpg
Ingredients:
1 frozen bananas, cut into small chunks
1/2 cup strawberries, hulled and chopped
3/4 cup Almond Breeze Almondmilk Unsweetened Vanilla

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.
