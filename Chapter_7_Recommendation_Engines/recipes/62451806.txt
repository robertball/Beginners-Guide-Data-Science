Title:
First Place Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups self-rising flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cream of tartar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup shortening
2/3 cup sugar
2 eggs, well beaten
1 cup banana pulp (3-4 ripe bananas)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mash ripe bananas with a silver fork.  Cream shortening and beat in sugar.  Add eggs, mixing well.  Mix and sift flour, baking soda, cream of tartar and salt.  Add flour alternately with bananas, mixing well after each addition.  Turn into greased loaf pan and bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour or until a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean and the top of the bread is brown.  Let cool in plan for 5 minutes before turning out on cooling rack.

Submitter: 
Judi Harris
Description: 
My mom used to make this recipe often.  I've changed it a bit by using self-rising flour.  I've found that makes for a higher-rising loaf.
