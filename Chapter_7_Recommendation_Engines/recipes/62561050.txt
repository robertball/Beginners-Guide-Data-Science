Title:
Broccoli and Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Casserole
1 pkg frozen chopped broccoli cooked
1 can cream style corn
1 tbsp chopped onion
4 tbsp melted butter
1 egg beaten
salt and pepper
Topping
3 or 4 strips of chopped bacon
1 c Pepperidge Farm herb stuffing
4 tbsp. melted butter

Directions:
Mix casserole ingredients together and put in a greased casserole. Cover with topping.
Topping, Toss together and spread on top of vegetables.
Bake at 325 uncovered until firm
