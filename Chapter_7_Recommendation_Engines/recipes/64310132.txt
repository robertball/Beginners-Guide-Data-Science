Title:
Kevin's Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon flour
1/2 teaspoon paprika
3/4 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce (Taco Bell Hot)
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 tablespoon ketchup
a dash of salt
1 cups milk
1/2 pound Kraft American cheese (the real kind), cut into cubes

Directions:
Melt butter in a saucepan. Add flour and stir around until flour loses its a caramel color..
Add the remaining ingredients, including the milk, and whisk.
Once it thickens, add the cheese.
Stir until cheese is melted.
Serve with tortilla chips.
