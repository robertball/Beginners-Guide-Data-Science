Title:
Cream Cheese Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chocolate cake mix (your fav kind)
Filling:
1 egg
1/2 c. sugar
8 oz. cream cheese
1 package chocolate chips

Directions:
Make cake mix according to directions.  Then cream together the egg, sugar and cream cheese, add in chocolate chips.  Fill cupcake paper halfway, then add a spoonful of filling into each cupcake.  Cool and frost.
