Title:
Dill Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-12oz. cans of tuna, drained
1 celery stalk, diced
1 sm. onion, diced
1/2 c. fresh dill, chopped
1 c. mayonnaise
1/4 t. lemon pepper
1/4 t. dry mustard
1/2 t. celery salt
1/2 t. kosher salt
1/4 t. groung pepper

Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients well in a medium size bowl.
Cover and refrigerate for atleast an hour (the longer you have to let it sit, the better it is).

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
This recipe is so easy and is never around for too long.  It has a lot of dill in it but that's what makes it unique.  Definitely let the flavors meld together by letting it chill for awhile.  You won't be disappointed!
