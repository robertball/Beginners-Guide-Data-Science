Title:
Outback Steakhouse Bloomin' Onion Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 teaspoons ketchup
2 tablespoons cream style horseradish
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon dried oregano
Dash ground black pepper
Dash cayenne pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a bowl and mix well.  Refridgerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
HOLLY D
Description: 
Tastes just like the dipping sauce for the bloomin onion at Outback Steakhouse.
