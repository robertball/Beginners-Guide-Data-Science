Title:
Canlis Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads romaine lettuce
2 tomatoes
1/2 c.grated romano cheese
1 lb chpt bacon
1 c. croutons
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/4 c,chpt green onions
2 T. olive oil
3 cloves garlic
1 lemon
1 egg

Directions:
Pour 2 T oil into salad bowl. Sprinkle generously with salt, and holding 3 garlic cloves together press and rub bowl interior completly.  Add quartered tomatos and romaine. Sprinkle with bacon, cheese, onion & pepper.  Add dressing and croutons
Dressing
mix 3oz oil, juice of 1 lemon and coddled egg.  Whip and pour over salad.
