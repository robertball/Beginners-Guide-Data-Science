Title:
Firehouse Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (21 ounce) cans pie filling, such as cherry, apple or peach
*May substitute one or both cans pie filling with canned fruit
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1/2 cup butter
Vanilla Ice Cream

Directions:
In a 9x13 inch pan, pour in the pie or fruit filling.  Sprinkle dry cake mix over the cherry filling until the cake mixture is evenly spread. Evenly place little squares of butter over the top of the cake mix.
Bake in a 350 degree F (175 degree C) oven for 35 or 40 minutes or until golden brown. Serve with vanilla ice cream.

Submitter: 
Leo
Description: 
I like this cake with cherry or apple pie filling. The cherry pie filling can also be mixed with crushed pineapple.Modified from: Dump Cake I
