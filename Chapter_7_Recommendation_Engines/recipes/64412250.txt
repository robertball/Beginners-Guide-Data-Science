Title:
Southern Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med sized head green cabbage, shredded
2 med sized carrots, peeled and shredded
1/2 red bell pepper, copped
2 T diced red onion
1 cup light mayo
2 T white vinegar
3 T sugar
1/2 t salt
1/4 t white pepper

Directions:
in a large bowl, combine cabbage, carrots, bell pepper and onion, mix well
In a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients, mix well,  Pour over the cabbage mixture, toss to coat well, cover and chill for at least one hour or until ready to serve

Submitter: 
M. Karen
Description: 
Light version of traditional coleslaw
