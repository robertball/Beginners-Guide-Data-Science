Title:
Easy Quiche Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Eggs
1 Lb. of Cooked Sausage OR Cooked Bacon
2 Cups Shredded Sharp Cheese
1 Can Evaparated Milk
2 Pie Shells

Directions:
Mix ingredients together.
Pour into Pie Shells.
Bake at 350 for 25-30 Minutes
I have made this at night and cooked in the morning to save time.  OR I have Cooked the meat the night before and then just slightly warm the meat and then mix up everything else.
I have also made one of each and just mix ip the other ingredients and pour into shells and then add bacon to one; and sausage to the other.

Submitter: 
Debbie Cash
Description: 
Really Good; and Really Easy Quiche Pie.  Everyone loves it.
