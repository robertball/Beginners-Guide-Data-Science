Title:
Pomegranate Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-½ cups powdered sugar
¾ cup pomegranate juice
¼ cup lime juice
2 cups heavy cream

Directions:
In large bowl, combine all ingredients in order listed above. Blend.
Transfer to stand mixer and whisk until forms soft peaks.
Pour into a loaf pan and cover with plastic wrap (keeping the wrap pressed to the ice cream, to avoid ice crystal formation). Freeze 6 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
MAYAESR
Description: 
No need for an ice cream machine - this comes out perfectly creamy without it somehow! And as light as a sorbet. Yum!
