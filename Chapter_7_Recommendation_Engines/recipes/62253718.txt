Title:
Slow Cooker Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pound ground beef
5 cups onion, chopped
80oz. black beans, with liquid
80oz. kidney beans with liquid
75oz. whole kernel corn, with liquid
40oz. can tomato sauce
10 cups water
55oz. rotel diced tomatoes
5 cups celery
5 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix

Directions:
In a medium skillet, cook the ground beef until browned over medium heat. Drain, and set aside.
Place the ground beef, onion, black beans, kidney beans, tomato sauce, water, diced tomatoes, taco seasoning mix in a slow cooker. After cooking for 1 hour add corn. Mix to blend, and cook on Low setting for 8 hours. May use more corn, rotel, and kidney beans if needed.Add 1/4 cup cilantro.

Submitter: 
Sarah Templet
Description: 
Browned ground beef  seasoned with a package of taco seasonings is combined in a slow cooker with canned beans, tomatoes, and green chilies.  Serve with tortilla chips and a dollop of sour cream.Modified from: Slow Cooker Taco Soup
