Title:
Girls Night Out Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup light mayonnaise
1 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 12-16 oz. can artichoke hearts (not marinated), drained
1 8 oz. box frozen, chopped spinach, thawed & drained
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350
2. Drain and quarter artichoke hearts
3. Mix cheeses, and garlic powder together, add spinach and artichoke hearts, mix until combined
4. Spread mixture into 8x8 baking dish
5. Bake for 30 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and completely melted
6. Serve with various dippers, such as tortilla chips, french bread, celery, crackers

Submitter:

Description: 
The perfect dip for girl's night out or family game night...easy and delicious
