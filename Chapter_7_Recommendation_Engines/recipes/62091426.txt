Title:
Spinach Carrot Braed Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
3 eggs
2/3 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup apple sauce
1 3/4  cups white sugar
3 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup frozen spinach (thawed)
OR
3 cups shredded fresh spinach
1 cup shredded carrots
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Grease two 8 x 4 inch bread pans. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
Sift dry ingredients together in large bowl to aviod clumping.
Beat eggs, oil, vanilla, and sugar together in a medium bowl.
Add sifted ingredients to the creamed mixture, and beat well.
Stir in spinach, carrots and nuts until well mixed.
Pour batter into prepared pans.
Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until tester inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool in pan on rack for 10 minutes. Remove bread from pan, and completely cool.
