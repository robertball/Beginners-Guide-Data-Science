Title:
Grilled Bacon Pepper Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 fresh jalapeno peppers or 6 green cayenne peppers, split lengthwise and seeded
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
6 ounces bleu cheese
4 ounces shredded cheddar cheese
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 pound bacon

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
If using green cayenne peppers it is important that all seeds and membranes are removed in the pepper before using as these yield a much higher potency (heat) than jalapeno peppers.
Mix cheeses and garlic powder until combined.
Stuff cheese mixture to fill pepper. Wrap with bacon (using two pieces of necessary. Secure with a toothpicks.
Place on the grill, and cook until bacon is crispy.
Finished peppers off the grill can be sliced into portions after allowing to cool slightly for easier eating and fun "popper-bites."

Submitter: 
Philip Shores
Description: 
Jalapeno or green cayenne peppers are stuffed with three cheeses seasoned with garlic, wrapped with bacon, and barbequed on the grill.Modified from: Grilled Bacon Jalapeno Wraps
