Title:
Loco Moco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 beef patties
1 packet Lipton onion mix
1 can Campbell's cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
Preheat over at 375F.
Mix onion mix with cream of mushroom soup.
Lay beef patties in a baking pan.
Pour onion/mushroom soup mixture over beef patties.
Bake in preheated oven for 30-45 minutes.
Serve over rice, with sunny-side-up egg on top.

Submitter:

Description: 
Hawaii favorite - beef burger patties, baked in mushroom soup, served over rice and sunny-side-up eggs
