Title:
Bestest Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can Cream of Mushroom OR Cream of  Chicken Soup
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1/2 cup butter or margarine
4-8 ounces cream cheese
1 (32 ounce) package frozen hash-brown potatoes
Garlic pepper to taste
1 cup crushed frosted flakes
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Mix soup, sour cream, 1/2 cup butter, cream cheese and garlic pepper in large microwaveable bowl. Microwave on medium heat for 3-5 minutes, just until ingredients are smooth when stirred. Mix in hasbrowns. Spread into greased 13x9 cake pan.  Sprinkle frosted flakes over potato mixture. Pour melted butter over the top.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes or until hot.
