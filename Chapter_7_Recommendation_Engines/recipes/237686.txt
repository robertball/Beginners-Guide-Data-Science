Title:
Sweet Fire Rib Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4460887.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup smoked paprika
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 tablespoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon salt

Directions:
Mix brown sugar, white sugar, paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, chili powder, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and salt together in a bowl. Store in a sealable container in a cool, dry place.

Submitter: 
SmokinMeats
Description: 
Also goes very good on some chicken, tri-tip steaks, or pretty much anything BBQ. 
I do a lot of smoked foods, and it's perfect.
