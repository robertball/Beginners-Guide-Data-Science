Title:
Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/882127.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 pounds ground turkey
1 small onion, shredded
2 carrots, shredded
1/2 red bell pepper, shredded
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup panko crumbs
1 egg and 1 egg white
1/4 cup chicken broth
2 Tablespoons worcestershire sauce
3 Tablespoons tomato paste
ketchup or your favorite topping

Directions:
Saute the veggie and garlic to soften them.  Add panko crumbs then add to the turkey in a mixing bowl.  Mix well with remaining ingredients except ketchup. Cover a jellyroll pan with parchment paper.  Mold turkey mixture into a 2 inch high loaf and place onto prepared pan. Spread ketchup over the meatloaf. Bake at 350 degrees until no longer pink in the center, about 45 minutes.
