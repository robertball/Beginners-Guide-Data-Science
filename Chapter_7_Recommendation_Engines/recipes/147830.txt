Title:
Double Yogurt Orange Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup granulated sugar
2 teaspoons grated orange peel or lemon peel
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups Kellogg's® All-Bran® Yogurt Bites
2 (6 ounce) containers low-fat orange or lemon yogurt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 cup Breakfast Morningstar Farms® Scramblers® egg substitute (thawed)
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons orange juice or lemon juice

Directions:
Stir together flour, granulated sugar, orange peel, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
In large bowl combine Kellogg's(R) All-Bran(R) Yogurt Bites cereal, yogurt and vanilla. Let stand about 2 minutes or until cereal softens. Add Morningstar Farms(R) Scramblers(R) egg substitute and oil. Beat well. Add flour mixture, stirring until just combined. Portion evenly into twelve 2 1/2-inch muffin-pan cups coated with cooking spray or lined with foil bake cups.
Bake at 400 degrees F about 15 minutes or until golden brown. Cool in muffin pan for 5 minutes. Remove from pan. Cool completely.
Stir together powdered sugar and enough orange juice to make of drizzling consistency. Drizzle over muffins.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® All-Bran®
Description: 
These moist, zesty muffins are a perfect treat for citrus lovers.
