Title:
Roasted Rosemary Chicken Legs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. finely chopped onion
2 Tbsp. margarine or butter, melted
1 Tbsp. chopped fresh rosemary or 1 tsp. dried rosemary
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 chicken legs (about 1 1/2 pounds
1/4 c. white wine or chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F.
Combine onion, margarine, rosemary, salt, pepper and garlic in small bowl; set aside.
Run finger under skin to loosen. Rub onion mixture under and over skin.
Place chicken skin-side up in small shallow roasting pan.  Pour wine over chicken.
Roast chicken 50 to 60 minutes or until chicken is browned and juices run clear, basting often with pan juices.
Carbohydrate: 2 g.; Calories: 263; Fat: 17 g.;  Protein: 22 g.
