Title:
Hawaiian Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes
5 cloves minced garlic
1 T oil
1/2 cup red wine
1 onion, cut into chunks
5 stalks of celery, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
4 carrots, cut into 1 inch pieces
2 T salt
2 T sugar
1 t black pepper
3 bay leaves
1 6oz can tomato paste
10 cups water
4 T cornstarch mixed with 4 T water

Directions:
Heat oil in a pot and braise stew meat on medium-high heat until brown. Add garlic and cook for a few minutes. Add wine and cook for about 5 minutes or until alcohol is cooked off.
Add onions and celery and cook for a few minutes.
Add 10 cups of water to the pot. Add bay leaves, salt, sugar, black pepper, and tomato paste and bring to boil. Lower heat to medium-low and cover and cook for approximately 1 hour, or until meat is tender.
Add carrots and cook for approximately 10 minutes. Add potatoes and continue to cook until vegetables are tender.
Thicken with cornstarch mixture and simmer for a few more minutes.

Submitter: 
Laurie Sueko Lau
Description: 
This delicious beef stew is a traditional beef stew common in Hawaii. It has a delicious tomato flavor along with tender beef and vegetables.
