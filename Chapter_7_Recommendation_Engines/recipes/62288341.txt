Title:
Rainbow Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One 12-0z pkg rainbow rotini, cooked
1/2 c red onion, chopped
1/2 c celery, chopped
1/2 c carrots, sliced extremely thin
1/2 c sliced black olives (one 4-oz can)
1/2 c red pepper, chopped
1/2 c green pepper, chopped
1 c Cheddar cheese, cubed
1 c Ranch dressing or dressing of choice

Directions:
Toss everything in large serving bowl.  May add black beans or kidney beans if desired.  Chill overnight.
