Title:
Colarado Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large, whole chicken breasts, poached
1 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Tblsp. butter
1 16oz. can tomatoes, chopped
1 8oz. can tomato sauce
1/4 cup mild green chiles, chopped
1 tsp. brown sugar
1 teaspoon grond cumin
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. basil
12 corn tortillas
2 cups grated Monterey Jack cheeese (8oz.)
1 1/2 cups sour cream (12oz.)

Directions:
Cut chicken meat into 12 strips and salt to taste. Set aside.
In a saucepan, saute onion and garlic in butter until soft.
Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, chiles, brown sugar, cumin, salt oregano, and basil to garlic mixture. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes. Remove from heat.
Dip each tortilla in tomato mixture to soften.
Place one piece of chicken and 2 tablespoons of cheese into each tortilla.
Roll up and place seam side down ina an ungreased 9x13 inch pan.
Blend sour cream into remaining sauce mixture and pour over enchiladas.
Sprinkle top with remaining cheese.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 40 minutes, or until heated through.
