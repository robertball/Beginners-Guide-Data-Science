Title:
Taste of Home's Creamy Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978517.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package coleslaw mix
3/4 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/3 cup sour cream
1/4 cup sugar
3/4 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/2 teaspoon ground mustard
1/4 teaspoon celery salt

Directions:
Place coleslaw mix in a large bowl.
In a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients; stir until blended. Pour over coleslaw mix and toss to coat. Refrigerate until serving.
