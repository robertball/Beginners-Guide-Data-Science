Title:
Mexican Chicken Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Boneless, skinless chicken breast
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Black Pepper
1 lb. Linguine pasta
1 stick Butter
1 med. Onion, chopped
1 Bell Pepper, red or green, chopped
1 can Rotel tomatoes, mild
1 can Cream of Chicken soup
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
1 lb. Velveeta cheese, cut into medium-sized cubes
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil chicken breasts in a large pot, adding 1/2 tsp. salt and 1/2 tsp black pepper to the water.
Remove chicken when completely done, 10 - 12 minutes.  Cut into bite-sized pieces.
Boil linguine in broth, according to package directions.  Drain pasta & discard water.  DO NOT RINSE.  Set pasta aside.
Melt butter in the same empty pot.  Saute onions and bell pepper until tender.
Add Rotel, soups, cooked chicken, and pasta, one at a time.  Gently mix together.
Add Velveeta and stir together.  Add salt & pepper to taste.
Heat until Velveeta is melted.  Stir to keep from scorching.
