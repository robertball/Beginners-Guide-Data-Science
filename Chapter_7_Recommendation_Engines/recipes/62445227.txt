Title:
Rajah's Jewels Curry Dip I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz.package cream cheese
1 medium navel orange, quartered
1 package golden raisins (Sultanas)
Curry powder to taste

Directions:
1.  Soften cream cheese in food processor for a few seconds.
2.  Add orange sections and blend until the rind is in small sections
3.  Add Sultanas, and when the mixture is soft and well-blended, add curry to taste - maybe up to a tablespoon.
Particularly nice with wheat thins.

Submitter: 
Sunshine
Description: 
This is an extremely simple dip that is utterly addicting.  Ii developed it in two versions;  this one requires a food processor, the other one doesn't.Modified from: Rajah's Jewels Dip I
