Title:
Cranberry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (16 ounce) can whole cranberry sauce
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
1 cup French or Catalina dressing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place chicken in 9x13 inch baking dish. Mix cranberry sauce, soup mix and salad dressing and pour mixture over chicken. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Lisa Nicholas
Description: 
The sweet-tart fruit sauce makes the chicken very moist and very delicious.Modified from: Cranberry Chicken II
