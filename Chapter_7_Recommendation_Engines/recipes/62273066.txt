Title:
Picnic Sausage Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 13.8 oz Pilsbury Classic Pizza Dough
1 - Jimmy Dean Roll Italian Sausage or original (round Bulk)
2 - Cups Mozzarella Cheese
Olive Oil

Directions:
Cook bulk sausage in pan until cooked and drain off fat. Spread the pizza dough evenly out on a 13x9 cookie sheet using your hands. Sprinkle with Sausage and top with Cheese. Roll the dough as you would a cinimon roll crimp the roll closed and place seam side down on the cookie sheet. Brush with olive oil and bake for approximately 15-20 minutes at 350 degrees until golden brown and toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Veronica
Description: 
This quick and easy recipe is great for outdoor picnic's, ball games or when you are out fishing!
