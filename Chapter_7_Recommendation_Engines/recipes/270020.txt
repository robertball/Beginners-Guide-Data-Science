Title:
Quick and Easy Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F6306116.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups water
3 tablespoons chicken soup base
1 pound egg noodles
1 ½ pounds boneless chicken breast cut into 1-inch cubes
1 ½ cups frozen peas
1 cup chopped carrot
1 tablespoon ginger
1 teaspoon ground mustard
1 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Combine water and soup base in a 2-quart pan and bring to a boil, about 5 minutes. Add noodles. Add chicken, peas, and carrots. Add ginger, mustard, and salt. Boil soup until noodles are tender, about 10 minutes more.
