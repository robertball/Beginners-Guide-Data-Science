Title:
Watermelon Surprize Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2096538.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups cubed seeded watermelon
1/2 red onion, cut into thin half-moon slices
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint, or more to taste
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Toss watermelon, red onion, apple cider vinegar, mint, and black pepper together in a large mixing bowl. Refrigerate until chilled completely, about 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Karen Hall
Description: 
This summery watermelon mint salad is dressed simply with apple cider vinegar and black pepper.
