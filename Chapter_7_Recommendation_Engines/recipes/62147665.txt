Title:
Mexican Corn Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups pancake mix
1 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. chili powder
1 egg, beaten
1 1/2 cups milk
1 cup canned cream style corn
2 Tbsp. vegetable oil

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the pancake mix, cumin and chili powder.
In a small bowl, mix egg and milk together.
Add egg mixture to dry ingredients and beat until smooth.
Add the corn and stir until blended.
Heat oil in a large skillet.
When hot, drop by approximately 1/4 cup fulls into the pan.
When fluffy and browned on the bottom, flip.
Cook until golden.
Remove and drain on paper towels.
Top with a dollop of sour cream to serve.

Submitter: 
smitty
Description: 
This is a good side dish when having a Mexican breakfast/brunch/lunch meal. I have been making these for about 14 years and still enjoy them.
