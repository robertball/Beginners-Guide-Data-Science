Title:
Shreddies Quiche Lorraine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991835.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
2 1/2 cups Post Shreddies Cereal, crushed into fine crumbs
1/4 cup butter, melted
Filling:
8 slices bacon, cooked crisp, drained, and coarsely crumbled
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
4 eggs, lightly beaten
1 1/3 cups half and half (10%) cream
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
Pinch of nutmeg

Directions:
Crust: Combine Shreddies with butter. Transfer to 9-inch (23 cm) pie plate; press firmly into bottom and up sides.
Bake at 350 degrees F (180 degrees C) for 10 min. Cool.
Filling: Sprinkle bacon and cheese over crust.
Whisk eggs with cream, black pepper, and nutmeg; gently pour into crust.
Bake 35 min. or until knife inserted in centre comes out clean. Cool 5 min. before cutting.

Submitter: 
Post Foods Canada Corp.
Description: 
Instead of traditional pastry, try this Shreddies crumb crust which provides more nutrition, texture and flavour.
