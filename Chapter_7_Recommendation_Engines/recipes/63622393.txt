Title:
Healthy Cereal Trail Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of cheerios
1 cup of dried pineapple pieces
1 cup of dried cranberries
1 cup of sesame sticks
1 cup of nuts, peanuts cashews, walnuts (etc.) (optional)
1 cup of chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Put all of the ingredients in a ziploc bag and shake.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Julia L
Description: 
This is delicious to snack on, and even to have with milk in a bowl of cereal!
