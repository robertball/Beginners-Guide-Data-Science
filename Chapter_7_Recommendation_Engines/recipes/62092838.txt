Title:
Summer Squash Saute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 pound bacon
1 medium red onion
2 large (about 8 inches each) italian squash (zucchini)
2 large crook neck squash
2 large gold rush squash (about 8 inches each)

Directions:
Dice the bacon and onion, set aside
Quarter the squashes lengthwize and cut into 1/2 inch chunks
In a hot skillet cook the bacon and onion until the onion is tender. Add the squash and cook until tender, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
White Raven
Description: 
Bacon and onions are sauteed with crookneck, golden rush, and italian squashes to lend wonderful flavor to an overall healthy dish that will have even the little ones begging for more!
