Title:
Mediterranean Salad With Chickpea Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535399.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15 1/2 ounce can chickpeas, rinsed
1/2 cup fresh flat leaf parsley
1 clove garlic chopped
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon salt and pepper
2 tablespoons all propose flour
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 cup fat free greek yogurt
3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
8 cups mixed greens
1 cup grape tomatoes
1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced

Directions:
Heat oil in a non-stick skillet over medium- high heat. Cook the patties, turning carefully, untill golden brown, 2-3 minutes per side.
In a small bowl, whisk the yogurt, lemon juice, and 1/4 teaspoon each salt and pepper. Divide the greens, tomatoes, onion, and chickpea patties among plates. Drizzle with the dressing.

Submitter:

Description: 
Chickpea patties, seasoned and browned to a lightly crispy crust. Served with greens and a greek yogurt dressing.
