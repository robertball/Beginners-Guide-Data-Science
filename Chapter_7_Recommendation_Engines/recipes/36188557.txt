Title:
Pina Colada Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup (4 oz.) plain yogurt
4 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 (4-serving size) pkg. instant vanilla pudding mix
1/4 cup crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 tsp. coconut extract
1/2 tsp. imitation rum extract
1/2 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a medium bowl, blend together  yogurt, cream cheese and pudding mix. Stir in pineapple and extracts; fold in whipped topping. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour or until ready to serve.
