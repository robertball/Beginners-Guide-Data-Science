Title:
Kentucky Style Green Beans (aka Greazy Beans) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460836.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups water (or enough to cover beans)
1 ham hock or small chunk of salt pork
2 tablespoons lard or other cooking fat
1 teaspoon seasoning salt
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
2 pounds fresh Kentucky Wonder or Half-runner green beans - rinsed, trimmed and snapped into bite size pieces
1 medium onion, quartered
8 small new potatoes (halved if large)

Directions:
Throw everything except potatoes into a large pot and cook, covered for as long as you can (at least 2 hours). 40 minutes before serving, add potatoes.

Submitter: 
Carol Hammond
Description: 
These beans are intentionally cooked to death.  If you want vitamins, serve a salad. Just good ol' Southern style green beans like my granny used to make!!Modified from: Down-South Style Green Beans
