Title:
Virgin Strawberry Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/888876.jpg
Ingredients:
9 ounces Frozen Sliced Strawberries
4 scoop Kal Pure Stevia Extract (equivalent to 4 tbsp Sugar)
3 ounce Lemon Juice
6 ounces Crushed Ice / Water

Directions:
Add strawberries, stevia, and lemon juice to blender
Blend until evenly mixed
Add ice / water and blend until smooth

Submitter: 
Zan
Description: 
I like to make up a large batch of this and drink the first one, then freeze the rest in individual sized containers for later.
