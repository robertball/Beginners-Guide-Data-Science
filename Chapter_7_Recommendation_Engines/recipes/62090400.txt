Title:
Baked Paprika Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breats
1 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1/4 c. and 2 Tbsp. butter or margarine, divided
1 yellow or sweet onion, sliced into rings

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Spray a 9 x 13-inch baking dish with butter flavored Pam.
Mix paprika, garlic salt, and pepper.
Melt 1/4 c. of butter in microwave.
Brush bottom side of breasts with butter and sprinkle with spice mixture.
Place breasts in prepared pan, bottom side down.
Brush tops of breasts with melted butter.  Sprinkle with remaining spice mixture.
Slice onion into desired thickness.  Seperate into rings.
Melt remaining 2 Tbsp. of butter.
Place onion rings in melted butter. Coat completely.
Arrange onion rings over chicken.
Cover baking dish with aluminum foil.
Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes or until juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Carla Woodard
Description: 
Boneless, skinless chicken breasts are basted with butter, spinkled with a paprika spice mixture, and baked with buttered onions.  This is a quick and easy family favorite!
