Title:
Montana Cowboy Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, diced
3 stalks celery, diced
1 lb. hamburger
1 can red kidney beans, drained
1 can black beans
1 can pork-n-beans
1 can Nalley's lima beans and ham
1 can garbonzo beans, drained (optional)
1 can sliced black olives, drained
1 can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 jar Heinz Chili Sauce
1 can diced tomatoes
jalapenos (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute celery and onion, add hamburger, brown and drain.  Place in large crock pot.  Add remaining ingredients.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Heat on low in crock pot 4 - 6 hours so flavors blend.  *Can also be heated on the stove on medium heat, stirring frequently.
This recipe can be doubled and leftovers work well frozen.

Submitter: 
anmlnrs
Description: 
Slow cooking is best, but not required for this recipe.  Quick and easy and oh, so filling!  Best served with cornbread.
