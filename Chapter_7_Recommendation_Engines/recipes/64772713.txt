Title:
Perfect AND Easy Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7541038.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans butter-flavored Pillsbury Cresent Rolls
1 lb. chicken breast, diced
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 C. Diced onion
2 tbsp. Flour
10 oz. 1/2 & 1/2  (just fill the soup can)
1 can cream of chicken soup
1-2 C.  Frozen mixed vegetables, diced  (qty. determined by room in pie container or casserole dish
Salt and pepper to taste
(Optional) 1/2 tsp. Cracked red pepper
(Optional) 1/2 tsp. Sage

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly spray pie dish (or casserole dish for larger servings).
Cook the diced chicken until no longer pink.  Set aside.
Cook onion until translucent suing In the same pan.
Using a whisk, Mix flour with milk. and add to the pan.  Add the soup and cook until bubbly and thickened. Add all remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.
Lay  one can of the crescent triangles in the bottom ( points meet in the middle) of the dish, molding them into one crust.   Add the vegetable mix until the dish is very full.  Layer the second can  of biscuits in the same way as the crust. Make small slits in the top crust to allow venting.
Cook until the filling bubbles onto the crust. Approx. 30-45 min.
