Title:
Sombrero Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Lbs Hamburger
1 Clove of garlic minced
1 Medium onion minced
2 Cans or Bottles of taco sauce (Whicherver brand you prefer)
1 Package of Old El Paso Enchilada seasoning or taco if you cant find other
1 Can Spicy refried beans
1 Tsp of salt
8 oz of shredded Monteray Jack Cheese
8 Oz of shredded Cheddar

Directions:
Brown hamburger until crumly
Drain and add onions and garlic and cook about 5 minutes.
Add rest of ingredients and cook until mixture everything is melted and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Laura Dobbins Corbett
Description: 
A very hearty mexican dip which can be served with chips or also used as a filler for burritos, enchilads, tacos, etc.  For a dip it should be served in a chafing dish or crockpot to keep warm
