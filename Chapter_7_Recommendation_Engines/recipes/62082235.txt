Title:
Ham Tostadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices of thinly sliced cooked ham.
4 tbs sour cream
1 cup shredded lettuce
1 thinly sliced avocado
4 store bought tostada shells
Hot sauce (tapatio brand) to taste

Directions:
Spread 1 tbs of sour cream on each tostada shell. Top each shell with 2 slices of ham.  Divide shredded lettuce evenly among the four tostadas.  Top with sliced avocado and splash with tapatio brand hot sauce.  Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
GABESGIRL
Description: 
Refreshing change to the traditinal ham and cheese sandwich.  Makes a nice lunch.
