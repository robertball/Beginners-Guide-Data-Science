Title:
Venison Roast & Potato's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 to 3 lb venison roast
1 bottle inject-able creole butter marinade
1 tbsp. Lawry's seasoning salt
1 tbsp. Black pepper
4 medium potatoes
1 medium onion

Directions:
Inject creole butter marinade into roast.
Sprinkle roast, onion slices and potatoes with season salt and pepper.
Place in cooking bag.
Arrange potatoes and onion around roast.
Add 1/2 cup water and tie bag.
Cook for 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
A creole injected roast surrounded by a bed of potatoes and onions. Baked in a cooking bag to keep moist and tender.
