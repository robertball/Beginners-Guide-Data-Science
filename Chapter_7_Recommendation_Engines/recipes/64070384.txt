Title:
Microwave Caramel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 cups of popped corn
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup Karo
1/2 cup butter
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Combine sugar, karo, butter and salt  a large micro-wave safe bowl. Cook 2 minutes on high, stir.
Cook 3 minutes, stir after each minute.
Add soda, vanilla, stir.
Add to popcorn, stir well, cook 1 minute on high, stir. Cook 1 minute, stir. Cook 30 seconds more and pour on counter top to cool.
When cool, break into pieces and serve
