Title:
Boiled Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/408950.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c sugar
1 stick butter/margarine
1/4 c cocoa(optional)
1/2 c milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 c peanut butter
2 1/2 c oatmeal
1 c nuts(optional)

Directions:
Combine and bring to a boil over medium-high heat sugar, butter/margarine, cocoa & milk for 1 minute. Remove from heat, add vanilla, peanut butter, oatmeal & nuts. Mix well. Spoon by spoonfuls onto wax paper.
*Work quickly as mixture will start to thicken in a hurry.

Submitter: 
Jaay
Description: 
These cookies are an age old Christmas tradition for my family, but are wonderful anytime of year.  They're unique - half cookie, half candy.  The ingredients are boiled NOT baked and they contain NO flour.
