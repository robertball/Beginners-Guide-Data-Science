Title:
Hamburger Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pounds ground beef
1 c. diced potatoes
1. c. shredded cabbage
2- 1 lb cans tomatoes
3 c. water/broth
1/4 tsp basil
1/4 tsp thyme
1 cup chopped onion
1. c. diced carrots
1 c. chopped celery
1/4 c. barley
4 tsp. salt
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Cook hamb. and onion until browned. Add remaining ingredients and bring to boil- then simmer for one hour. (Can pressure cook for 5 minutes)

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
One of my favorite soups growing up.Modified from: Melanie's Beef Barley Soup
