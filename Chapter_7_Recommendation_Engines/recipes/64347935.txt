Title:
Mexican Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large tomatoes, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped (optional)
4 Jalapeno peppers, finely chopped (remove seeds optional)
Salt to taste
Black pepper to taste
2-4 Tbsp cilantro (also called Chinese parsely, fresh coriander)
2 Tbsp oil (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Great with tortilla chips.
