Title:
Sourdough Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1054153.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 1/2 cups of ripened, mashed bananas
1 cup of sourdough starter
1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
2 cups of all purpose flour
1 teaspoon of baking powder
1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon of nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon of salt
3/4 cup of chopped pecans
1/2 cup of cranberry/almond slice mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease a 9x5 inch bread pan.
In large mixing bowl beat the first three ingredients together until creamy.
Add the next three and mix well.
In a separate bowl, mix remaining dry ingredients.
Stir the dry ingredients into the batter until blended.  Do not over beat.
Pour batter into bread pan, let it rest for approximately 10 minutes.
Bake it for 60-65 minutes.
Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan or slicing.
