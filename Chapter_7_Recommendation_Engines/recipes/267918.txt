Title:
Apple Almond Butter Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5724323.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup almond butter
1/2 cup Almond Breeze Vanilla almondmilk
1/3 cup peeled, chopped apple
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
4 large pitted Medjool dates

Directions:
Puree all ingredients in a small food processor until smooth. Transfer to a covered container and refrigerate until ready to serve. Serve with apple slices, grapes, banana slices, or strawberries.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
A fruit and nut butter dip that gets a delicious caramel flavor from sweet dates.
