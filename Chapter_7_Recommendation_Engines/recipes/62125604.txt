Title:
Gramma Sue's Dirty Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/893818.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium-size ripe bananas
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/2 c. sugar
2 eggs
1/2 c. sour milk or buttermilk
2 Tbs. sour cream
2 C. flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. cinnamon if desired

Directions:
Using electric mixer on low, mix first 6 wet ingredients until well-blended.  Add the dry ingredients, and mix on medium until all are well-blended but don't overmix.  Stir in 1/2 cup chopped nuts if using.  Pour into greased loaf pan, bake at 350 for 45-50 min.  Test with long toothpick or cake tester for doneness.  Cool in pan for 10-15 minutes then turn out on rack to cool. Yummy cold or toasted!

Submitter: 
SHOGIRL
Description: 
When my grandson was a toddler, he loved bananas, but wouldn't touch the riper, brown-spotted ones, which he called dirty.  These are the best kind to use in this moist, firm, tasty, not-too-sweet loaf.  Add 1/2 cup chopped nuts if desired.  I sour my milk with 1/2 tsp. lemon juice.
