Title:
Pork Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5313840.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c. Paprika
1/2 c. Brown Sugar
1/2 tsp. Cayenne Pepper
2 tsp. Mustard Powder
1 TBLE. Salt
1 TBLE. Black Pepper
1 TBLE. Onion Powder
1 TBLE. Garlic Powder
1 TBLE. Chili Powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together (I use a fork to stir as it breaks up any clumps), and store it in an air-tight container.

Submitter: 
Julie Gilbert
Description: 
This rub will work on any pork, whether you are smoking or grilling the meat.
