Title:
Potato Dry Fry (Curry) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large potatoes, boiled and cubed
1 onion, chopped
3 T oil
1/2 tsp black mustard
1/2 tsp urad
3 dried chilies
a dozen curry leaves
1/2 tsp turmeric
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Either cube potatoes and boil, or boil in the jacket and break apart into chunks.
Prepare the popu - heat oil, add urad dal, when it begins to sizzle, add the black mustard seeds.  When the mustard seeds begin to pop, immediately lower heat a notch and add the curry leaves and crumbled dried peppers.
Immediately add onions and fry until onions are nearly done.
add potatoes, turmeric, and salt.  Stir to coat well.
If mixture is too dry, add a small amount of water.
cover and cook over med heat for about 5 minutes,.  Stir to keep it from sticking.
Serve with rice or nan/chapatti/puri.
