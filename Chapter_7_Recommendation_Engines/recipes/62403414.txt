Title:
Banana Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4C butter
3/4tsp cinnamon
1C mashed banana's
1 egg
1/2tsp Baking powder
1 1/4tsp salt
1C flour
1/4tsp Baking soda
3/4C sugar
2Tbsp milk
1C chocolate chips
1C nuts
powdered sugar

Directions:
Cream butter, add banana's and eggs. Add the dry ingredents; then fold in choclolate chips and nuts. Bake for 25minutes at 350. Dust with powdered sugar once cool
