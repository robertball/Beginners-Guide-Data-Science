Title:
Gelatin Lime and Pears Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 oz package lime gelatin
1 large can of pears, juice reserved
1/2 pint (one cup) whipping cream

Directions:
Pour gelatin into bowl
Drain pears, reserving juice
Prepare gelatin using reserved pear juice and enough water to make 1-1/2 cups total
Chill gelatin until firm enough to whip
Mash pears
Whip cream
Mix all together and chill.

Submitter: 
Geroniho
Description: 
For the longest time, I didn't have a name for this dessert, so my kids ended up calling it GLAP (rhymes with HOP).  It's simple, easy and tasty.
