Title:
G's Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/951227.jpg
Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts diced .
1/2 cup Country Crock churn style or equivalent .
1 green pepper diced
1 red pepper diced
1 yellow pepper diced
1/2 medium onion diced
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1   can  Del Monte® Diced Tomatoes with Basil, Garlic & Oregano or equivalent .
1/2 pound rotini pasta .

Directions:
In a large saucepan cook chicken in 1/4 cup Country Crock churn style on med heat .
In a medium saucepan add peppers , onion , italian seasoning and remaining Country Crock churn style .Cook covered until tender on med heat .
Boil 2 quarts water and add pasta.
Add Del Monte® Diced Tomatoes to cooked chicken . Add peppers and onion . Simmer for 5 minutes.
Serve over pasta.
Enjoy :)
