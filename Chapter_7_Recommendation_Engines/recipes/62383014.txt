Title:
Big Daddy's Mila Berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 cup frozen unsweetened strawberries
1 cup frozen unsweetened raspberries
1 tablespoons sugar
1 scoop of Mila
1 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Place the milk, berries, Mila and sugar in a blender; cover and process until smooth. Add ice cubes; cover and process until smooth.
