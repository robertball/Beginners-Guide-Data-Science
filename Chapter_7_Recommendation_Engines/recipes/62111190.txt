Title:
Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium eggplant
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup margarine
1 pound ground chuck
2 cups homemade or 1 (16 oz.) jar spaghetti sauce
8 oz. grated Mozarella cheese
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven 350.
Peel and thinly slice eggplant.
Place salt and flour in a plastic bag.  Add eggplant and shake well to coat.
Melt margarine in a large skillet, and brown eggplant on both sides. Remove from pan.
Add ground chuck and brown. Drain excess fat.
Place eggplant in a 9" X 13" pan.
Layer with meat, spaghetti sauce, Mozarella and Parmesan.
Bake at 350 uncovered for 30 minutes.
