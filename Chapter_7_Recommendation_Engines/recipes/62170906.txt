Title:
My Fresh Cranberries with Orange, Lemon, and Rum Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh cranberries
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup pineapple juice
1 teaspoon lemon zest
zest of one small orange
1/3 cup white rum

Directions:
Combine all ingrdeients, except rum, in a saucepan and heat over medium high until hot and bubbly and cranberries are split.  Allow to cool slightly and carefully tranfere to a blender.  Puree until smooth or chunky, your preference.  For an even chunkier sauce, puree only half, leaving some whole berries.  Stir in rum.  Transfere to a serving bowl and refrigerate overnight.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This fresh cranberry sauce is a great accompanyment to turkey or ham.  I've had some tea totalers come back for seconds!
