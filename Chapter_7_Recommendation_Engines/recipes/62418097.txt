Title:
Fresh and Simple Peach Salsa with Cinnamon Sugar Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/442886.jpg
Ingredients:
8 peeled and diced fresh peaches
1/4 red onion
1/8 c diced cilantro
1/2 cup quartered yellow cherry tomatoes
1/2 diced jalapeno, seeds and membranes removed
1/4 tsp cardamon
1 TB fresh lemon juice
Juice of 1 lime
10 small flour tortillas (taco size)
1/4 cup melted butter
1/8 cup cinnamon-sugar mixture

Directions:
Combine the peaches, red onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and jalapenos in a bowl. Sprinkle with the cardamon and lime and lemon juice. Stir to combine.
To make the tortillas, dice each into 8 wedges. Brush with melted butter and sprinkle generously with cinnamon-sugar mixture. Bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Emmy
Description: 
After a trip to the farmer's market, I created this salsa! It's a more sweet/less savory salsa and it pairs nicely with sugar cinnamon chips
