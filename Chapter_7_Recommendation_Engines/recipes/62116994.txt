Title:
Venison Gyro's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One 3-4 venison leg roast partialy thawed
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 1/2 tbs ground cummin
1 tbs garlic
2 tsp marjoram
2 tsp rosemary
1 tbs oregano
1 tbs red wine vinegar
salt and pepper
1 package pita bread

Directions:
thinly slice roast into jerky size strips(roughly 1/4 inch)Place strips into bowl or bag after seasoning with  salt and pepper,add in spices,oil,vinegar
Place meat in fridge to marinade atleast 2 hours
Pan fry meat in a skillet and serve on pita bread
