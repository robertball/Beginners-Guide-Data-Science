Title:
Chicken & Green Chili Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tb olive oil
1/2 onion, chopped
2 chicken breasts, cooked & shredded
1/2 (4-oz) can diced green chilies
1/2 C green enchilada sauce
1/4 tsp cumin
salt & pepper to taste
6 oz Mexican blend shredded cheese
5 (12-inch) flour tortillas

Directions:
Heat 1T oil in pan and saut√© onions until translucent.  Add the shredded chicken, chopped green chilies, cumin, salt, pepper, and enchilada sauce.  Mix well and cook for 1-2 min.
Heat tortilla in a large skillet. Sprinkle cheese onto half of the tortilla.  Add the chicken mixture, then sprinkle with more cheese.  Fold tortilla in half over filling.  Heat on both sides until cheese is melted.  Repeat with remaining tortillas.  Cut quesadillas into fours and serve with sour cream and salsa.

Submitter: 
Trina
Description: 
Mild green chilis and chicken blend together with cheese in these tasty quesadillas.
