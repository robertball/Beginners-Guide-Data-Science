Title:
Mach's Delicious Soup! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Celery,  Onion, Carrot, Butter, Beef broth, Chicken, Italian seasoning, Ramen noodles and parsley

Directions:
First, thaw some chicken.
Second, cut 1/2 onion 2 carrots 2 celery.
Third,  saute the onions  in 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat.
Forth, add the vegetables  to the pot.
Fifth, put 2 boxes of beef broth into the pot.
Sixth, cut the thawed chicken and put in the pot.
Seventh, but the heat to hi and boil then, simmer for 45 minutes.
Eighth, put the heat back to medium heat.
Ninth, add 2 teaspoons  of  Italian seasoning  to the soup and mix.
Tenth, put the heat to hi and boil the soup.
Eleventh, put the heat back to medium.
Twelth, put 2 packages of Ramen noodles into the soup and mix.
Thirteenth,  add some parsley to the soup mix.
Enjoy :D
