Title:
Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/949609.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. Butter
1 tsp minced garlic
1 medium onion
2 Tbsp all purpose flour
3 14 oz. cans chicken broth
4 cups half and half
1 10.75 oz can cream of chicken soup
1 c salsa
4 boneless chicken breast drained and shredded
1 15 oz can black beans drained
1 15 oz can kidney beans drained
1 15 oz can whole kernal corn drained
2 tsp ground cumin
1 packet of fajita seasoning
1 16 oz bag tortilla chips
8 oz montrey jack cheese grated
8 oz sharp cheddar grated
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
melt butter in large pot over medium heat. addgarlic and onion and saute until softened, 5 minutes, add flour and stir well cooking about 1 minute.
Add broth and half & half. stir cream of chicken soup, salsa, chicken, beans, corn, cumin and fajita seasoning.
continue to simmer 15 minutes or until ready to serve.
crumble chips add soup and top with cheese and sour cream.
