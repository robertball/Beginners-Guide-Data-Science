Title:
Orange-Carrot Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1832177.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds carrots, scrubbed and trimmed
12 oranges, peeled

Directions:
Run carrots and oranges through a juicer. Strain juice and transfer to a 1-gallon pitcher. Refrigerate.
