Title:
Perfect Gingerbread Men Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3C Flour
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
2 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp Cinnamon
1/2 tsp freshly ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp cloves
3/4C brown sugar (dark or light)
1/2C Molasses
1 egg

Directions:
Cream butter, sugar.  Add eggs and molasses.  Beat until fluffy.  Sift all dry ingredients and slowly add until well combined.  Make a disk and wrap in wax paper.  Refridgerate at least two hours.  Large cookies bake 325 to 350 for 8-10 minutes.  Small cookies 10 to 12 at 350

Submitter: 
SHANNONSPIVEY
Description: 
This is a recipe that I got from my Great Aunt and I have perfected to a point where it is old fashioned spicy but not too sweet.  I get rave reviews and get the most money at Christmas bake sales!
