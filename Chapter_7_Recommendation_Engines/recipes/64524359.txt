Title:
Steve's Famous Pico De Gallo/Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Red onion diced
1 29oz can of petite diced tomatoes- drained (or 3 cups of chopped fresh tomatoes)
3 cloves of minced garlic
1/2 bunch of cilantro chopped
1 jalapeno (other hot pepper) finely chopped
1 tsp salt
1/2 lime

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl place diced tomatoes
Add onion, garlic, jalape√±o, cilantro, salt
Squeeze 1/2 lime into mixture
Stir and refrigerate for 3-4 hours
To make into a salsa, place above ingredients into a food processor until it reaches proper consistency.
Recipe can be customized with different peppers, tomatillos, black beans, corn, fresh mango etc.

Submitter: 
Steven Dallwig
Description: 
This easy and customizable recipe that I have created over the years is a party favorite, loved and requested time and time again.  The recipe can be made as either a pico (with fresh or canned diced tomatoes) or a salsa (run through a food processor)
