Title:
Sausage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups seasoned herb croutons
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 lb. Jimmy Dean sausage
4 eggs
2 1/4 cups milk
3/4 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 tsp. salt
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Put croutons in bottom of 9x13 greased pan. Sprinkle on 1 1/2 c. cheese, then browned sausage.
Beat eggs with 2 1/4 c. milk and seasoning. Pour over and refrigerate overnight.
Mix soup and milk (1/2 c.). Pour over before baking and add cheese.
Bake at 325 for 1 hr. Let set for 10-15 min. before serving.
