Title:
Cheesy Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb of lean ground beef
3 bell peppers, finely diced (1/8")
1 large onion, fineliy diced (1/8")
3 cups cheddar cheese (shredded)
2 cups Panko (japanese bread crumbs)
1/2 cup worshtershire sauce
3 tablespoons A1 steak sauce
3 tablespoons ketchup
2 tablespoons salt
1 1/2 tablespoons pepper
1 1/2 tablespoons garlic powder

Directions:
1.)  Preheat oven to 350
2.)  Saute the onions and green peppers in a little bit of olive oil or butter until tender.
3.)  Mix theh meat and eggs together.
4.)  Mix the remaining ingredients together
5.)  Pull a small piece off and form into a pattie (about the size of a quater) and fry it until it is done.  Taste it and adjust seasonings as desired.
6.)  Form into patties and brown off both sides in a skillet (be sure not to burn them).  Then place it into preheated oven and bake aprox. 10-20 min or until internal temp reaches 155. (you can use your pan drippings from when you baked it or browned it to make gravy)

Submitter: 
Nikki Anderson
Description: 
This a very flavorful and exciting dish!  The onions and bell peppers are sauted off before they are added to the beef and cheddar cheese is added to make it a special dish!  Beef gravy goes good over top of the steaks!
