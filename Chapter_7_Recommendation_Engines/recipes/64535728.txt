Title:
Easy Baked Talapia Italiano' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 talapia pieces
1 cup favorite bottled Italian dressing, or homemade
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
pepper to taste
sliced lemon if desired

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray a large cake or casserole pan with no stick spray.  Depending on how many fish pieces used, will fit accordingly into pan used.
Take dried fish and dredge or sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and pepper, then
place into bottom of dish.  Pour dressing over all fillets to cover well, cover pan to bake about 15-20 minutes depending on thickness of fish.  Fork tender is usually done.
