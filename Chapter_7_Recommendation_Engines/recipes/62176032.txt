Title:
best Key lime pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 9inch baked pastry shell
3 eggs seperated
1 14oz can of sweetened cond. milk
1/2C lime juice concentrate
green food coloring
1/2tsp cream of tartar
1/3C sugar

Directions:
In bowl beat egg yolks, stir in the sweet. cond. milk, lime juice and food coloring. Pour into pastry shell. In mixer, beat egg whites with cream of tartar until stff peaks form; gradually add the  sugar.  Beating until stiff. Spread over the pie  to the edges. Bake at 350 for 12min. cool and then chil.
