Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. cloves
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cup white sugar
1 1/2 cup brown sugar
1 cup oil
3 eggs
16 oz. pumpkin
1 cup nuts or chocolate chips

Directions:
Grease 2 loaf pans.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix sugar, oil, and eggs until well blended.
Stir in pumpkin.
Gradually add the rest of the ingredients; do not overmix.
Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
DarcieA
Description: 
I always look forward to fall because the pumpkin recipes are wonderful.  Bake this bread when the leaves start to change color and your house will smell like all of the seasonal fall scents that we delight in!
