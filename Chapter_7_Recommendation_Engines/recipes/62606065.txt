Title:
Best Roast Chicken + Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon white pepper
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
. . . .
1 onion, quartered
. . . .
1 (4 pound) whole chicken
4 Yukon Gold potatoes
2 carrots
2 parsnips
1 onion, halved
. . . .
14-oz can chicken broth
1 tablespoon cornstarch

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together salt, paprika, onion powder, thyme, white pepper, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and garlic powder
Remove and refrigerate giblets and heart from chicken
Rinse chicken cavity, and pat dry with paper towel.
Rub chicken inside and out with spice mixture.
Place onion into the cavity of chicken.
Place chicken in a resealable bag or double wrap with plastic wrap.
Refrigerate overnight, or at least 4 to 6 hours.
The next day . . . . . .
Preheat oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
Place chicken and veggies in a roasting pan.
Bake uncovered for 5 hours, to a minimum internal temperature of 180 degrees F (85 degrees C).
Remove chicken & veggies from roasting pan and tent with foil to keep warm.
Mix the cornstarch with a bit of chicken broth to dissolve.
Pour remaining broth into the roaster and heat over medium heat, scraping up browned bits.
If desired, chop up retained giblets and neck and add to roaster.
Stir in the cornstarch mixture and cook, stirring constantly, until broth is reduced and thickened.
Let the chicken stand for 10 minutes before carving.
Serve hot with gravy on the side.
