Title:
Overnight Maple French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. cream cheese
1/4 c. icing sugar
2 Tbsp. milk
12 slices French bread
5 eggs
2 1/2 c. milk
1 T. vanilla
1/2 c. maple syrup
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
butter

Directions:
Generously butter a shallow baking dish.
Cream cream cheese, sugar and milk.
Spread evenly over all 12 bread slices.
Arrange bread slices in pan with cheese side up.
Mix eggs, milk, vanilla, syrup and salt until well blended.
Pour over bread.
Sprinkle with cinnamon.
Cover and rerigerate overnight.
Bake uncovered in preheated 350F oven for 1 hour.
Let sit for 15 minutes before serving.
