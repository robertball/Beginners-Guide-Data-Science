Title:
Mama Nancy's Salmon Patties... Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/853289.jpg
Ingredients:
15oz can of pink salmon
4 salteen crackers
1 egg
1 cup seasoned fish fry

Directions:
Drain excess water off of salmon.
Put salmon in a small mixing bowl.
Add crumbled crackers and egg.
Mix well.
Make about 5 small patties.
Coat patties in fish fry.
Place patties onto baking sheet.
Bake in peheated oven at 400 degrees for 15 minites.
Flip patties over and bake an addtional 15 mins.

Submitter: 
Nauntie
Description: 
My husband request that I learned how to make salmon patties like his mama made them. I had never made salmon patties before. This is a great simple quick recipe.
