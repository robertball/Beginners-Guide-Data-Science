Title:
Chocolate Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Eagle Brand Condensed Milk
1 stick margarine
1 can Hershey's Chocolate

Directions:
Mix all together.  On medium heat, in a heavy sauce pan, cook until margarine is melted and blended well. Stir constantly.
