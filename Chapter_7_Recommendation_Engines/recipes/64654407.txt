Title:
Katie's Fresh Corn Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Ears Fresh Corn (cut from cob)
1 Can creamed corn
3 eggs
1/2 cup cooked and crumbled bacon(optional)
2 cups shredded cheddar
1/4 cup finely shredded parmesan
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup Half and Half or light cream
1/2 cup bread crumbs or Panko
1/2 cup pancake mix
1/4-1/2 cup minced peppers (bells for color or jalapenos for heat...mix to your taste!)
4 whole green onions  chopped
1T powdered mustard
1/2 t salt
1/2 t pepper
1/2 t garlic powder
1/4 t Paprika

Directions:
Preheat Oven 350
In a large bowl cut corn from cob and add cheddar cheese, minced peppers, green onions and crumbled bacon (optional)... mix well and set aside
In a smaller bowl combine all wet ingredients( egg, sour cream, creamed corn, and Half&Half) and seasonings except Paprika. Mix well with whisk and  set aside
In a separate bowl add bread crumbs and pancake and combine well
Sprinkle dry ingredients over the corn and cheese mixture and combine well by tossing with fork
Prepare a 10X15X2 with butter, cooking spray or parchment paper and fill with corn/cheese/ breadcrumb mixture. gently level and press mixture into casserole dish.
Mix wet ingredients one more time and pour evenly over the contents of the casserole dish.
Top with shredded parmesan and paprika
Bake for 40 -45 minutes until firm and top is golden brown
Let rest for 15 minutes or longer before serving

Submitter: 
Katie
Description: 
A great way to use seasonal fresh corn! Lovely as a side,  or as a light main dish with a  salad. Keeps well and is delicious reheated... Enjoy!!
