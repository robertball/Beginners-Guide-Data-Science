Title:
Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. cottage cheese
1 egg
2 Tbsp shredded parmesan cheese
8 ounces pasta
1 - 16 ounce jar, Tuscan Merlot pasta Sauce (Vino de Milo)
1/4 c. cream
1/4 c. water
1.5 cups mozzarella
1/4 c. shredded parmesan cheese
8 ounces cooked chicken or sausage

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Cook pasta following directions on package.
While pasta cooks, heat pasta sauce, heavy cream, and water over medium heat until boiling. Reduce heat and simmer for 8 minutes. Add cooked chicken or sausage and continue simmering for an additional 3 mintues.
Meanwhile, stir together cottage cheese, egg, 3/4 c. mozzarella cheese and 2 Tbsp parmesan cheese.
After pasta is drained, add cottage cheese mixture and half of the pasta / meat sauce to pasta and mix well.
Place mixture in a greased, 2.5 quart casserole dish. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese on top. Cover. Bake for 30 min. Remove cover and bake for an additional 10 mintues until bubbly. Let sit for 5 min before serving.
