Title:
Wine Slushie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 a bottle of Red Cat wine
12 oz of frozen fruit punch
1/4 cup of confection sugar
Ice

Directions:
Add ice in a blender. Then add wine, sugar, and fruit punch. Mix together to make a slushie. Add more ice for desired thickness.

Submitter: 
Carey2103
Description: 
Another great way to enjoy a glass of red wine. I had this once at a state fair and was hooked. So yummy on a hot summer day!!
