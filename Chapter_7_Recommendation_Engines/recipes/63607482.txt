Title:
Easy Tomatillo Verde Egg Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1003816.jpg
Ingredients:
Cooking spray
6 slices of bread
1 tsp. of Herdez Tomatillo Verde Mexican Cooking Sauce for each egg cup
6 pieces of bacon, partially cooked
6 eggs
4 green onions chopped
1 T. of grated cheddar cheese for each egg cup
Toppings: shredded cheese, green onions and 1 cup of Tomatillo Verde Sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Spray muffin tin with cooking spray.
Cut circles out of bread 3 inches in diameter.
Partially cook bacon in the microwave.(2-3 minutes)
Wrap bacon around the edge of the bread. Press in the muffin tin.
Add 1 T. of grated chedddar cheese.
Add 1 tsp. of Herdez Tomatillo Verde Mexican Cooking Sauce to each egg cup.
Crack egg in a separate bowl, one at a time. Pour into each muffin tin.
Bake for 15 minutes or until desired doneness.
Meanwhile, warm up 1 cup of Herdez Tomatillo Verde Sauce in a saucepan.
Chop green onions, set aside.
Let egg cups set for 1-2 minutes. Run a knife around the eggs to remove.
Top egg cups with Herdez Tomatillo Verde Sauce, shredded cheese and green onions.

Description: 
Zesty Tomatillo Verde egg cups wrapped in crispy bacon and baked in the oven.
