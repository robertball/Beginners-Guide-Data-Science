Title:
Sean's Mock Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988426.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (15 ounce) cans BUSH'SÂ® Garbanzo Beans, drained, rinsed, and slightly mashed
4 stalks celery, chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 teaspoons lemon juice
2 teaspoons lime juice
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 1/2 tablespoons wasabi paste
1 1/2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup dry bread crumbs, or as needed

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix the beans, celery, onion, and garlic. In a small bowl, blend the lemon juice, lime juice, mayonnaise, and wasabi paste. Season with salt and pepper. Gradually mix in bread crumbs as needed to attain desired consistency.
