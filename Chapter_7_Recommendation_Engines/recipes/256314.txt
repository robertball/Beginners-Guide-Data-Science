Title:
Homemade Crushed Ice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4453233.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups crushed ice
1 1/2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract, or more to taste

Directions:
Combine crushed ice and confectioners' sugar in a small bowl. Stir in vanilla extract. Freeze for 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lexi Lee Foster
