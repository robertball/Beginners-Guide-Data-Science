Title:
Banana Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2/3 cup melted butter
2 large eggs
1 tsp vanilla
2-3 medium ripe bananas, mashed
1-2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.  Grease cookie sheets.  Sift together dry ingredients and set aside.  Cream together butter and sugars until fluffy.  Beat in eggs and vanilla.  Mix in mashed bananas.  Add flour mixture and stir just until combined.  Stir in chocolate chips.
Drop by spoonfuls onto prepared cookie sheets.  Bake in preheated oven 12-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Karen Zastrow
Description: 
A nice alternative to use those too ripe bananas.
