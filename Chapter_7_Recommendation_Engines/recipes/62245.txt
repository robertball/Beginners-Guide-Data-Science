Title:
Easy Livin' Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750297.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups vodka
2 cups club soda
2 cups cranberry juice
1 cup tangerine juice
1 cup lime juice
1 lime, sliced
3 quarts crushed ice

Directions:
Stir together the vodka, club soda, cranberry juice, tangerine juice, lime juice, and sliced lime in a large punch bowl. Add crushed ice to serve.

Submitter: 
KATRINKLE
Description: 
This has got to be the best summer drink I've ever tasted. I made this drink up and it's always a huge hit. It's got tang, and is sweet, but not too sweet.
