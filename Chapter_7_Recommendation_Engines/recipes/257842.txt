Title:
RITZ Strawberry Hearts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4517146.jpg
Ingredients:
4 fresh strawberries
12 RITZ Crackers
2 ounces white baking chocolate, melted
1/2 teaspoon red sprinkles

Directions:
Remove and discard green tops from strawberries.
Cut strawberries lengthwise in thirds, then cut a "V" in top of each strawberry half to resemble heart as shown in photo. Discard removed pieces.
Spread crackers with chocolate, adding about 1/2 tsp. chocolate to each cracker.
Place strawberry slices on chocolate; top with sprinkles.
Refrigerate 5 minutes or until chocolate is firm.

Submitter: 
RITZ Crackers
Description: 
Ritz crackers are spread with melted chocolate and topped with heart-shaped slices of strawberry and red sprinkles.
