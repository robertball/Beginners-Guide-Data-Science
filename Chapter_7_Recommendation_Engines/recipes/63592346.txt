Title:
Tzatziki Sauce or Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 C Greek yogurt
2 large english cucumber or 3 medium cucumber
1T + 1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 black pepper
2 T EVVO
1 tsp white wine vinegar
2 oz fresh lemon juice
2 clove garlic minced
2 tsp fresh dill weed (do not use stalks)

Directions:
Peel cukes, and scrape seeds out with spoon. Finely dice cukes, toss with 1 tbsp kosher salt, place in strainer over bowl. Refrigerate 1-2 hours.
Put yogurt in large bowl. Squeeze liquid from cucumbers and pat dry. Add in all ingredients except greek yogurt. After mixing THEN add yogurt and stir. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.

Great dip with veggies or pita chips!
