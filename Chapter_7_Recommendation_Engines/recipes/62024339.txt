Title:
Reduced calorie Poppy Seed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless, skinless Chicken Breasts
1-10 3/4 oz. can 98% fat free Cream of chicken soup
1 Cup Reduced fat Sour cream
1/3 pound Reduced fat Club Crackers
1 1/2 Tbls. Poppy seeds
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Cut chicken breasts into chunks and cook in saute pan with non-stick cooking spray until cooked through. Salt and pepper chicken to taste.
2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
3. Mix soup and sour cream.
4. Spray 8x8 pan with non-stick cooking spray and add cooked chicken.
5. Spread soup and sour cream mixture over top of chicken.
6. Crush crackers and mix with poppy seeds, salt and pepper.
7. Spread cracker mixture over soup mixture.
8. Bake 40 min covered in preheated 350 degree oven.
9. Uncover and bake 5 min.
10. Serve and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Catherine McCann Shelton
Description: 
This is lower calorie version of the Poppy Seed Chicken recipie. The calories may be lower, but with all the taste of the original!
