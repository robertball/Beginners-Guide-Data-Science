Title:
Steak House Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cups of Wheat Chex
4 Cups of Rice Chex
2 Cups of Cheese Crackers
1 Cup of miniature pretzels
6 Tbsp of butter, cut up
3 Tbsp of steak sauce
1 Tbsp of worschesteshire sauce
1 tsp of cayene pepper
1 Cup of french-fried onions
1 Cup of peanuts
3 tsp onion powder
2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
In large microwave safe bowl, combine cereals, crackers, and pretzels.
In small microwave safe bowl, melt butter.
Stir in the steak sauce, worchesteshire sauce, and cayene
Pour over cereal mixture, and toss to coat.
Microwave, uncovered, for 4 minutes, stirring every 2 minutes.
Add fried onions, peanuts,onion powder, garlic power, toss to coat.
Spread onto waxed paper to cool.
Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
Cindy Yonkman
Description: 
A spicey variation on an old favorite!
