Title:
Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dry
1 3/4c - All Purpose Flour
1T - Baking Powder
1/2t - Salt
2T - Sugar
Wet
2 - Egg Yolks (beaten)
1 3/4c - Milk
1/2c - Cooking Oil
2 - Egg Whites (whipped)

Directions:
Combine all dry ingredients. In separate bowl combine all wet ingredients minus the egg whites. Combine the 2 separate mixtures until blended. Whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form, then fold into batter.
