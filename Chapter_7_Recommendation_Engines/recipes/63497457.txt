Title:
Pickled beets and eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954604.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans sliced beets
12 hard boiled eggs, peeled
1 cup sugar
1 cup water
1 cup cider vinegar

Directions:
Drain beets reserving 1 cup juice.  Layer beets and eggs in 2 2-qt glass canning jars.  Bring other ingredients to a boil.  Pour over eggs and beets.  Cool slightly, cover tightly and refrigerate for at least 24 hours.
For those of you who don't know how to hard boil eggs:  add cold water to cover the eggs in a pan on top of the stove.  Bring to a boil over medium heat.  Reduce heat so water is just below simmering.  Cover.  Cook 15 minutes.
