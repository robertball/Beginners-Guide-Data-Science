Title:
Asian Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (6.2 ounce) package RICE-A-RONI® Fried Rice
1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1/3 cup frozen peas
1/3 cup sliced green onions
2 cups chopped cooked pork, chicken or shrimp
3/4 cup coarsely shredded carrot
Chow mein noodles (optional)

Directions:
Prepare RICE-A-RONI mix as package directs; simmer 10 minutes.
Stir in water chestnuts, peas and green onions; return to a simmer.  Cover; simmer 5 minutes.
Stir in pork and carrot; continue cooking until heated through.  Sprinkle with chow mein noodles, if desired.

Submitter: 
RICE-A-RONI®
Description: 
This easy fried rice dish can be made with any type of meat you have on hand. It's especially good for using leftovers. Try this fun main dish for your next meal.
