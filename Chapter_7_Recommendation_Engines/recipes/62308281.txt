Title:
Chicken Piccata Altered Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 egg
4 T lemon juice
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 t paprika
2 T butter
2 T olive oil
1 cup chicken broth
1 cup white wine
2 t corn starch mixed with 3T cold H2O
1 T capers (optional)

Directions:
In a small bowl, beat the egg together with 1 tablespoon of the lemon juice. Set aside.
In a shallow bowl or dish mix together the flour, garlic powder and paprika. Dip the chicken lightly in the flour, then in the egg/lemon mixture, then back again in the seasoned flour. Refrigerate 20-30 minutes if possible.
In a large skillet, melt butter & olive oil and brown the chicken. Remove and keep warm.
Add broth, wine, and remaining lemon juice to skillet. Stir in corn starch mixture. Cover and let simmer for 20 minutes. Add chicken pieces after 10 minutes, and cook until chicken is tender. Garnish with capers if desired and serve with pasta or rice.
