Title:
Leftover Chicken and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 oz.) box Uncle Ben's Long Grain & Wildrice Original Recipe
2 cups cooked chopped chicken
1 (14.5 oz.) can Cut Green Beans (or French Style)
1 (10.75 oz.) can Cream of Celery Soup
1/2 cup mayonnaise
Salt & Pepper to taste
1 small can sliced water chestnuts, drained(optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Prepare rice according to package directions. Combine cooked rice with remaining ingredients and place in a baking dish. Cover and bake about 30 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Chickentarian
Description: 
Sometimes I grill extra chicken just so I can make this chicken and rice casserole.
