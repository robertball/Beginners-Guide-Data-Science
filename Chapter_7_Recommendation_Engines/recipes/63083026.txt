Title:
Hearty Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices cooked bacon, drained & chopped
2 stalks celery, diced
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
8 potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 fresh red pepper, chopped
olive oil
1 cup chicken stock
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
3 cups milk
3 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley (Substitute cilantro for a different taste)
Add ground pepper and lightly salt to taste
Optional ingredients
1/2 cup cheese
1/2  teaspoon dried tarragon
1 cup ham, cubed
1 cup fresh mushrooms (portobello or button), diced

Directions:
In a skillet, stir fry in light coating of olive oil the chopped celery, onion, and garlic; add the diced potatoes and pepper. Mix in parsley. (Include optional ingredients as preferred: ham, mushrooms) Cook at medium heat, reduce to low as you prepare soup stock.
In a soup pot (medium heat) or slow cooker, whisk the flour into heated chicken stock until smoothly blended. Add three cups of milk. Add sauteed ingredients (Add in cheese if preferred; stir until cheese is melted). Add bacon. Season to taste. Simmer until potatoes are done; stir soup occasionally.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
This is a great way to spice up traditional potato soup.
