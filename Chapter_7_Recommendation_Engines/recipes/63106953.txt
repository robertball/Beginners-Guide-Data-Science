Title:
Fiber?  I'll Drink To That... Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/788661.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 C Edy's Rocky Road Ice Cream (one of the single serving containers)
1.5 oz Vodka
1.5 oz Bailey's
1.5 oz milk
1/3 C Fiber One ® cereal

Directions:
Pour all ingredients into a blender.
Blend.
Pour into a frosty mug and garnish with some Fiber One (R) cereal.

Submitter: 
lovestohost
Description: 
When I received the assignment to create a recipe using Fiber One ® cereal as an ingredient, I knew I was going to be thinking a bit outside the proverbial box.  This is what I came up with.  Cheers!
