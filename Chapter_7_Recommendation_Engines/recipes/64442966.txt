Title:
Curried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 pieces chicken cooked and boned, without skin
2 tablespoon butter
1 clove garlic, (chop with onion)
1 onion, chopped
8 oz.sliced mushrooms
1 apple, cored and chopped
1 chopped green pepper
1 tablespoon curry powder, or more to taste
1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of mushroom soup
1 cup half-and-half cream (can also use sour cream mixed with a little milk)
1 cup currents
1 cup frozen peas

Directions:
Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add the apple and onion to the melted butter, season with the curry powder, and cook and stir until the apple and onion are tender, 7 to 10 minutes. Stir the mushroom soup and half-and-half into the mixture until completely combined; add chicken. Simmer on low about 15 minutes. Add currents and frozen peas. Continue to simmer 30 minutes more.
