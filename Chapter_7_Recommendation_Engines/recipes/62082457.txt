Title:
Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 strips uncooked bacon (diced)
1 small onion chopped
1 cup diced potatoes
1 can diced tomato
1 can clams (separate the broth and clams)
salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 cups half and half

Directions:
Fry bacon lightly and drain grease. Brown onion slightly, add potatoes, tomatoes, salt, pepper and broth from clams.  Simmer over low heat for 1 hour. Add clams and half and half.  Heat but do not boil.  Serve at once with string cheese and crackers.

Submitter: 
STOLZS
Description: 
This has been a family tradition on Christmas Eve for many generations.  Serve with string cheese (yummy to melt in the chowder), and a cheese and salami tray.
