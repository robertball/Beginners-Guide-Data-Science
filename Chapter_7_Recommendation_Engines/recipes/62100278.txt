Title:
World's Best Antipasto Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. shell macaroni cooked and well drained.
½ lb. hard salami cut into bite-sized chunks
½ lb. sandwich pepperoni sliced in half
½ lb. mozzarella cut into bite-sized chunks
½ lb. provolone cut into bite-sized chunks
2 green peppers cut into bite-sized chunks
1 large sweet onion cut into bite-sized chunks
1 jar small green olives
1 can small or medium pitted black olives
Pepperocini (Optional)
DRESSING
¾ cup vegetable oil
½ cup cider vinegar
1 T. dried oregano
1 T. salt (sounds like a lot, but you need it)
½ t. pepper
Blend in blender for two minutes

Directions:
Pour dressing over salad and mix.  Mix salad periodically for 1 day.  Is best to make a day ahead.
MAKES A LOT

Submitter: 
CATHYRAE
Description: 
Hence the name, the best antipasto salad you'll ever eat.  Could make a meal out of it.
