Title:
Summer Squash Herb Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked long grain white rice
2 cups water
1 tbsp olive oil
1 1/2 cups sliced zucchini (green trimmed)
1 cup sliced yellow squash
1/4 cup green onion
1/2 cup red onion
1 cup diced tomatoes
2 tbsp minced garlic
1 tbsp basil
1 1/2 tbsp thyme
1 tbsp paprika
1/2 tbsp dried oregano
1 tbsp salt
Pepper to desire

Directions:
Combine the rice and water plus half of your herbs in saucepan and bring to a boil. Remove from heat and allow rice to absorb water, about 10 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 8x8 casserole dish with non-stick cooking spray.
Heat deep skillet over medium heat, add olive oil when skillet is hot. Saute zucchini, squash, tomatoes, red onion, and garlic together until tender. Add green onion and remaining herbs and saute for about a minute.
Add rice to skillet and incorporate vegetables and rice together well. Transfer to prepared casserole dish.
Bake uncovered about 5 - 10 minutes until casserole is evenly heated throughout, or lightly browned on top.

Submitter: 
Malina
Description: 
This recipe is the perfect dish to use up a lot of those herbs you have hanging around from summer and the abundance of summer squash in your garden. It has everything, low calorie, quick to prepare, shortcuts are easy to come by, heavy enough to fill you up, light enough to pass for a summer meal. You may add other vegetables to your liking.
