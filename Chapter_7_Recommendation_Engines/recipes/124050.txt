Title:
Peanut Butter 'N' Jelly Breakfast Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/78008.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
1 ripe banana, sliced
2 tablespoons peanut butter
2 tablespoons jam, jelly or preserves (any flavor)
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Place all of the ingredients in a blender; blend for 3 minutes or until smooth. Serve cold.

Submitter: 
Loretta Levitz
Description: 
My husband is a big 'kid' who'll often whip up this tasty shake for breakfast on the go. Your kids will love the flavor of a peanut butter and jelly sandwich in it.
