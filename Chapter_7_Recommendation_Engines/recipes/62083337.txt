Title:
Not Stuffed Cabbage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14 ounce can sauerkraut
1 teaspoon caraway seeds
1 pound ground beef or pork
1 cup cooked white rice
1 teaspoon dried onion
½ teaspoon seasoned salt
1 14.5 ounce can stewed tomatoes
1 head cabbage, cored
½ teaspoon garlic salt
2 slices of fried bacon, crumbled
mayonnaise, 1  2 tablespoons per serving (optional)

Directions:
Boil cabbage for 15 minutes. Drain, cool, and chop.
Mix ground meat with cooked rice, dried onion, and seasoned salt.
In 2-quart casserole dish, layer sauerkraut, caraway seeds, meat mixture, tomatoes, and chopped cabbage. Sprinkle with garlic salt.
Bake, covered for 1 hour at 375.
Remove from oven and top with crumbled bacon.
Serve with mayonnaise on the side.
