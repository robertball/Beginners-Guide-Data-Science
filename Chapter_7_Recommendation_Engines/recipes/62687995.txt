Title:
Easy, Favorite Tartar Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
1 tablespoon minced onion
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, sweet pickle relish, Old Bay seasoning, and minced onion. Stir in lemon juice. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
Description: 
Simple tartar sauce.  It is better if prepared several hours before you will be using it, but it can be used immediately.  Store for up to 3 days in the refrigerator.Modified from: Tartar Sauce I
