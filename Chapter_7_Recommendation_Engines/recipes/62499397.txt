Title:
Mimosa's Tex-Mex Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 med. to large onion, chopped
1 (15 ounce) can ranch-style beans or chili beans
1 (10 ounce) can Ro*Tel tomatoes and green chiles
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (12 ounce) package corn tortillas, quartered
4 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Spray a 2-3 quart casserole dish with non-stick cooking spray.
In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef and onions.  Drain excess grease and return meat/onion mixture to skillet.
Add beans and mix throughly, set aside.
In a medium bowl, combine Ro*Tel tomatoes and cream of chicken soup.
Line the bottom of the casserole with 1/3 of the tortilla quarters.  Top with 1/2 the beef, onion, and bean mixture, 1/3 the cheese, and 1/3 the soup/tomato mixture.
Repeat with another layer of 1/3 tortillas, remaining beef, onion, bean mixture, 1/3 cheese, and 1/3 soup/tomato mixture.
Finish by placing remaining tortillas on top, followed by the remaining soup/tomato mixture and remaining cheese on top.
Bake in preheated oven 40 minutes or until browning and bubbly.
