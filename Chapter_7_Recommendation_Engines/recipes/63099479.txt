Title:
encebollado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large tomatoes, cubed
1 med. onion, sliced and cut in half
1/2 c. lime juice
salt and pepper

Directions:
1.  Slice onion and place in 1/4 cup of lime juice with 1 tsp salt.  Let it sit until onions become soft.  Then drain and rinse.
2.  Cube tomatoes and mix with sliced onions.
3.  Pour 1/4 c lime juice over salad and mix, add salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
7woolfords
Description: 
A fresh lime and tomato salad that goes great with rice and fish or chicken
