Title:
Tuna and noodle casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of no yolks extra broad egg noodles
3 5 oz cans of tuna
3 tablespoons corn starch
3 cups milk Preferable 2% or whole milk
6 tablespoons butter
3/4 teaspoon salt
3/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Boil 3 quarts of water add egg noodles boil for 10-12 mins drain.
Mix 3 cups of milk,3/4 tsp salt,3/8 tsp pepper together and 3 tbsp corn starch till smooth and there are no lumps or nearly no lumps from corn starch
Melt the 6 tbsp of butter then add milk mix into saucepan
Constantly stir milk and butter mix at about medium low heat till it thickens to  a nice creamy thick sauce and then remove from stovetop.
Mix the white sauce,egg noodles and tuna together in a baking dish make sure so butter the sides to minimize sticking
Bake at 375 for 50 minutes with a cover then for another 10 or 15 minutes till browned to liking

Submitter: 
Hilaire Watkins
Description: 
Simple homemade tuna and noodle casserole. No soup, no veggies. Just simple white sauce ,tuna and noodles
