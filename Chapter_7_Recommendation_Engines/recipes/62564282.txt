Title:
Shrimp and Mushroom Linguini with Creamy Cheese Herb Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package linguini pasta
2 tablespoons butter
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/2 cup butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 (6 ounce) package cream cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1-1/2 teaspoon dried basil
2/3 cup boiling water
2 pounds shrimp, shelled & deveined
2 tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
salt to taste
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add linguini, and cook until tender, about 7 minutes. Drain.
Meanwhile, heat 2 tablespoons butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic; cook and stir until translucent.
In the same pan, melt 1/2 cup butter with the sliced mushrooms. Saute for 1 minute. Stir in the cream cheese, breaking it up with a spoon as it melts. Stir in the parsley and basil. Simmer for 5 minutes. Mix in boiling water until sauce is smooth.
Saute shrimp in olive oil, garlic, red pepper flakes and italian season until pink. Salt to taste.
Stir cooked shrimp into the cream sauce and heat through.
Toss linguini with shrimp sauce, and serve.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Cooked shrimp and sliced fresh mushrooms are bathed in an opulent sauce of sauteed garlic, rich cream cheese, fresh parsley and a dash of basil. Toss with hot linguini and serve as an elegant appetizer or main dish.Modified from: Shrimp and Mushroom Linguini with Creamy Cheese Herb Sauce
