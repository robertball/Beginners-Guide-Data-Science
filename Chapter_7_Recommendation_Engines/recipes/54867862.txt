Title:
Mom's Baked Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package macaroni
2 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed Cheddar cheese soup
1 cup milk aND A LITTLE MORE TOO.. JUDGE IT YOURSELF
1 pound shredded Colby cheese
heat the soup and the chedder and milk and pour onto the cooked noodles then bake..
you can make bread crumbs for the top as well.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Place macaroni in a 2 quart casserole dish.  Stir in cheese soup and milk until well combined.  Sprinkle with shredded Colby.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes, or until cheese is brown and bubbly.
