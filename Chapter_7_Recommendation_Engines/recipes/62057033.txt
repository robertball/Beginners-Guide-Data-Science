Title:
Coffee Crunch Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart coffee ice cream, slightly softened
1 (2-1/4 ounce) pkg slivered almonds, chopped and toasted
2 (7/8 ounce) English toffee candy bars, crushed
1/2 cup chocolate syrup
1/2 (8-oz) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
8 maraschino cherries with stems

Directions:
Spoon 1/4 cup ice cream into each of eight 4-oz chilled parfait glasses; freeze 30 minutes.  Layer 1/2 tablespoon each of almonds, crushed toffee, and chocolate syrup.  Repeat layers of ice cream, almonds, toffee, and chocolate.  Cover and freeze until firm.
Top each parfait with whipped topping and a cherry.

Submitter: 
ACPHIFER
Description: 
This is an elegant dessert using coffee ice cream and English toffee.
