Title:
Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 lb beef round steak, 1 inch thick
2 tbsp shortening
1 8oz can of tomatoes
1/2 cup minced onion
1/4 cup minced green bell pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
Stir together flour, salt and pepper.
Sprinkle one side of meat with half the flour mixture and
pound in. Turn meat and pound in remaining mix and cut into 6 serving pieces.
Melt shortening in large skillet. Brown meat over medium heat about; about 15 minutes.
Cover tightly and simmer for 1 hour, add small amount of water if necessary.
Mix remaining ingredients and pour over meat. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes or until tender.

Description: 
This round steak is zesty without being overly seasoned or complex in flavor.
