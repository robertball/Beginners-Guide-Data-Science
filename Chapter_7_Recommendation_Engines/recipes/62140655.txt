Title:
choclate peanut butter cotton mouth cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup choclate chips
1/4 cup corn syrup
1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
2 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups quick oats
1 cup honey roasted peanuts

Directions:
cover cookie sheet with wax paper
heat choc chips,corn syrup peanut butter,milk and vanilla in saucepan till melted.
remove from heat stir in oats ,peanuts till well coated
drop by heaping spoonfulls onto wax paper,refridgerate 1 hour
