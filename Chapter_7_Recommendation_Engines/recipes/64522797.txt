Title:
Nut Filling for Kringles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup softened butter
2 cups packed brown sugar
3 cups finely chopped nuts, I used pecans and almonds
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Mix ingredients together in a bowl.
Chill for 15 minutes.
Divide among 4 kringles.
Seal and bake on parchment paper, 375 degrees for 45 minutes, rotating pans halfway through.
When cool, glaze with 1 cup icing sugar, dash of vanilla and water to make pourable, let dry.
