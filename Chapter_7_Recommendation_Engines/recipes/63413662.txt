Title:
Broccoli (or spinach) & corn casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boxes chopped broccoli or spinach
2 cans cream style corn
2 eggs
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Defrost broccoli, drain well, mix with other ingredients. Put in casserole dish. Top with bread crumbs or matzo meal. Dot with marg.
Bake 350 for 1hr until firm
