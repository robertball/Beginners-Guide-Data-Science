Title:
Auntie Pat's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp Olive Oil
1 onion chopped
2 cloves garlic chopped
1 lb ground beef
1 lb ground sausage
2 28 oz. cans tomato puree
2 6oz can tomato paste
3 tbsp. dried oregano
3 tbsp. dried basil
4 tbsp. sugar
1 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onions and sautee until soft. Add garlic and cook for another 3-5 minutes.
Add the ground beef and sausage and cook until browned.
Add the remaining ingredients and stir to combine.
Bring to a boil and simmer for 1 1/2 hours over medium low heat.  Be sure to check sauce while cooking so it doesn't burn on the bottom.

Submitter: 
mam62505
Description: 
A simple sauce that is great in lasagna or baked ziti or just served over pasta.  The sauce is on the sweeter side with the addition of sugar.  You can easily make this meatless by omitting the ground beef and sausage.
