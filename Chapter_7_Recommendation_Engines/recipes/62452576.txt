Title:
My mothers chicken drumsticks(because I miss her and she would let me share) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs drumsticks or thighs
sauce:
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 tbls sugar
salt and pepper
heavy squirt of ketchup (about 2 tbsp)
1 tbsp garlic powder

Directions:
Wash drumsticks (or thighs) if you want to get aggravated, remove the skin. but it is not necessary, it falls off anyway when cooked.
Saute the chicken in a little oil,
after browning, add sauce
if cooking in pot..
cook on medium/low heat for about 2 and 1/2 hours
in crockpot
cook on low for 5 hours

Submitter: 
sharron
Description: 
My mother used to make this dish, chicken drumsticks that taste out of this world. in a large pot or in a crock pot. There were NINE of us to feed, and this fed us all.  of course it was probably double this recipe, but it was great.
