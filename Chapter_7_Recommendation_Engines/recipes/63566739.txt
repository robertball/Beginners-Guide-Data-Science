Title:
Roll-Out Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup(2 Sticks) Unsalted butter, softened
1 1/2 Cups Granulated Sugar
1 Egg
1 1/2 Teaspoons of Clear Vanilla Extract (Imitation or Pure)
1 1/2 Teaspoons of Clear Almond Extract (Imitation or Pure)
2 3/4 Cups All Purpose Flour
1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
1 Teaspoon Salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F
In Mixing Bowl, beat butter with sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in egg and Almond & Vanilla Extracts.
Combine flour, salt and baking powder, add to butter, sugar mixture one cup at a time, mixing after each cup of flour. Do Not Chill Dough. Divide Dough into balls.
On a floured surface, roll each ball into a circle approximately 12 inches wide and 1/8 of a inch thick.
Dip Cookie cutters in flour if needed, before each use. Bake cookies on a un-greased cookie sheet 8-11 minutes or until lightly browned. Makes about 3 Dozen Cookies. Recipe can be Doubled.
