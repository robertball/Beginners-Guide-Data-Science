Title:
Pico de Gallo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Roma tomatoes, seeded and diced
1 yellow onion, diced
1 fresh jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
1/2 bunch fresh cilantro, finely chopped
2 cloves fresh garlic, pressed
Juice from 1/2 lime
salt (if needed)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except salt in a medium bowl, stir and refrigerate for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
Drain liquid and add salt to taste. If serving with tortilla chips, taste with a chip before adding salt to avoid over-salting.
