Title:
Limoncello Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/844072.jpg
Ingredients:
Pudding:
4 large croissants (optionally lightly toasted), torn into pieces
7 large eggs, lightly beaten
½ cup sugar
¼ cup whipping cream
¾ cup milk
¼ cup limoncello
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Limoncello Simple Syrup:
½ cup limoncello or fresh lemon juice
½ cup sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Arrange croissants pieces in a lightly greased 9-inch square baking pan.
Whisk together eggs, sugar, whipping cream, milk, limoncello, and vanilla.
Pour egg mixture over croissants, cover with foil and let stand for 20 minutes.
Bake covered for 30 minutes; uncover and bake an additional 20 minutes or until set and golden brown.
Server warm or chilled. Top with ice cream; drizzle with Limoncello Simple Syrup and garnish if desired.
Limoncello Simple Sauce: Cook limoncello and sugar in small saucepan over medium heat until sugar dissolves and mixture comes to a boil; let boil 1 minute. Cool completely.
