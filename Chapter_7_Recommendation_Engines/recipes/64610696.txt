Title:
Robins homemade cranberry sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups of cranberries, 1 cup of sugar, 3/4 cup of water

Directions:
Boil cranberries and water until the cranberries have mostly split. Add sugar and continue to boil until sauce has thickened, 5 to 10 minutes and voila, there you go.

Submitter: 
Rusty & Robin
Description: 
An excellent whole berry sauce that's easy to make and tastes great.
