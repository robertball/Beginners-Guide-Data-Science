Title:
potato soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large potatoes peeled and cubed
1 small onion chopped
2 stalks celery cut up
2 carrots cut up
1 lb Italian Sausage (ground)
1 pk. unprepared butter mashed potatoes
1 large can chicken broth
2 cups water
1 cup sour cream(optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
place cubed potatoes in a large pot with chicken stock, water,and vegetables. cook until tender. Add unprepared potato mix, Italian sausage and seasonings. Remove 1 cup soup and mix with 1 cup sour ceam is desired and add back to pot.

Submitter: 
MISTIDAWN
Description: 
this soup is spicy, filling, and creamy! Mixing in a package of unprepared instant mashed potatoes gives it some creaminess. Add some sour cream tempered to it and you've got a crowd pleaser and an empty pot!
