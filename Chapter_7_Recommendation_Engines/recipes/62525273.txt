Title:
Beef in Red Wine Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/514871.jpg
Ingredients:
* 1-1/2  pounds  beef stew meat, cut into 1-inch cubes
* 2  medium  onions, cut up
* 2    beef bouillon cubes or 1 envelope (1/2 of 2-ounce package) onion soup mix
3  tablespoons  cornstarch
Salt
Ground black pepper
1-1/2  cups  dry red wine
Hot cooked noodles (optional)

Directions:
In a 3-1/2- or 4-quart slow cooker, combine beef stew meat and onions. Add bouillon cubes or dry onion soup mix. Sprinkle cornstarch, salt, and pepper over beef and onions. Pour red wine over beef mixture in cooker.
Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 10 to 12 hours or on high-heat setting for 5 to 6 hours.
If desired, serve over hot cooked noodles.

Submitter: 
Chef Ristine
Description: 
A Slow Cooker dish that is  dinner party-worthy. Team this tempting wine-simmered beef dish with a crisp tossed salad and crusty bread. (similar to stew meat supper)
