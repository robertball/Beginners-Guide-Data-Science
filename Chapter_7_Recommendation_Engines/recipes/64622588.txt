Title:
Roast in Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3.5 lbs cross rib roast
1/2 yellow onion, chopped
8 garlic cloves, chopped
1 T ginger minced
4 c low sodium beef broth
1/4 c tamari
2 T fish sauce
6 T tomato paste
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp dried thyme
2 T butter
salt
pepper
1/2 tsp guar gum (if using to thicken gravy)

Directions:
put all ingredients except guar gum in crock pot
cook on low for 11-12 hours
cool and skim fat
return to crock pot
add 1-2 sprigs rosemary
season as desired
cook on high for 3-4 hours
serve with cauliflower mash
