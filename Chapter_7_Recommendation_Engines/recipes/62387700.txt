Title:
Chicken Nuggets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. Corn Starch
1 C. Flour
2 eggs, beaten
2 t. Seasoned Salt
2 t. Garlic
1/3 C. Milk
5-6 Chicken breast halves, cut into bite-sized pieces
Cooking Oil (I like canola oil)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except milk in a large mixing bowl.
Add milk slowly until the desired consistency. (It will be fairly thin but not too runny.)
Heat oil in a large skillet med-heat. (Should be about 1"-1 1/2" deep)
Dip chicken in batter and transfer to hot oil.
Cook chicken 7-10 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This is a simple chicken nugget recipe. I have actually substituted pork and made great sweet and sour pork.
