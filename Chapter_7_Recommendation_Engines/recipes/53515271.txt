Title:
Grilled Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 baking potatoes, scrubbed
1 T oil
1 t salt
1/2 C sour cream
1/2 C shredded cheddar
2 green onions, sliced
1/4 C bacon bits

Directions:
Heat grill to med-high. Pierce potatoies and rub w/oil and sprinkle w/salt.
Microwave on high 8 min. or until tender.
Place on greased grate of grill. Grill 12-15 min. or until crisp and lightly browned, turning occas. Split potatoes. Serve topped w/remaining ingredients
