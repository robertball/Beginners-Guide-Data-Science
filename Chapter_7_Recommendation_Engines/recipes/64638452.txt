Title:
Coconut fat bombs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups unsweetened coconut
1/2 coconut oil
2 T honey
1/3 tsp vanilla
1 T special dark chocolate chips

Directions:
Warm coconut oil, add to coconut, honey and vanilla and blend.
Form into balls and freeze for 10 minutes.
melt dark chocolate and drizzle over coconut fat bombs and chill.
