Title:
Vegetarian 15 bean soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/611327.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 oz) bag of 15 bean soup.  throw away seasoning package.
2-3 tbsp olive oil
1 large onion- diced
3 large cloves of garlic- minced
12-15 carrots- peeled and chopped
7-9 stalks of celery- chopped
4 stalks of Red Chard- chopped
6 stalks of Dinosaur kale- chopped
15-20 crimini mushrooms- sliced
12 cups vegetable broth or bullion equivalent
2 bay leaves
1 tsp rosemary
1 tsp dried basil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1.  Soak beans overnight in cold water.  rinse.  Set aside.
2.  Saute onions in olive oil.  When almost to the point of caramelization, add garlic, carrots, celery, chard, kale, and mushrooms.
3.  Add vegetable broth.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to a simmer.
4.  Add bay leaves, rosemary, basil, salt, and pepper.
5. If preparing more than 3 hours ahead of time, wait to add beans until about 1.5-2 hours before eating.  Otherwise, add beans at this point.
6. simmer for 1.5-2 hours or until beans are done.  They should be soft, but not mushy.

Submitter: 
tamara
Description: 
A hearty, vegetarian soup perfect for warming the belly on a rainy day.  My first foray into making a completely vegetarian soup and I can say I didn't miss the meat component at all!  Goes great with a crusty bread.  Good for a large gathering of people or for plenty of leftovers!
