Title:
Gram's Spag. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of Condensed Tomato Soup
2 cups shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
8 oz Spaghetti Noodles

Directions:
Prepare spaghetti noodles according to package.
Drain and set aside.
In medium sauce pan heat tomato soup (undiluted - do not add water or milk to soup) until starts to bubble.
Add cheddar cheese and stir until fully melted and blended into soup.
Take off heat.
Serve noodles on plate and pour a portion of the cheese-soup mixture onto noodles. And enjoy!

Submitter: 
David S. Novak
Description: 
A true comfort food! And easy to make too! I was introduced to this recipe through my wife's family and we've made it a staple in our home when there isn't much time to make a big dinner. I submit this recipe in memory of Annie Wruck.
