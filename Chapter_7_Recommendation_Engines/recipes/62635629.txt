Title:
Stove-Top Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/579448.jpg
Ingredients:
4 c. Potatoes , peeled and diced
1/2 c. Onion , diced
2 tsp. Salt 2 teaspoons salt
1 1/2 c. Milk (2% low-fat)
1/2 c. Cheddar cheese , shredded
1 dash Cayenne
1/4 c. Bread or Cracker Crumbs

Directions:
Combine potatoes with onion, salt and milk in heavy frypan.
Simmer covered for 30 to 40 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Sprinkle cheese, cayenne and crumbs over top.
Continue cooking uncovered for 15 to 20 minutes.
