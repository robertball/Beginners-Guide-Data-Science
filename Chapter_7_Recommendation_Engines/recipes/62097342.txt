Title:
Cream of Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup wild rice
2 1/2 cup water
1 tsp salt
2 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp finely chopped onion
4 cups chicken broth
1/4 cup flour
1/2 cup grated carrots
1/3 cup finely chopped celery
1 pint half and half
slivered almonds for garnish

Directions:
Light boil the wild rice, water, and tsp salt for 45-60 minutes- drain and set aside
Saute the onion in the butter
blend together the chicken broth and flour and heat to near boil then add onion, carrots, celery, and drained rice. Heat completley.  Add half and half and heat completely.  season with black pepper if desired.  serve and top with slivered almonds.

Submitter: 
rosburn
Description: 
This is an amazingly rich and creamy soup!  I only wish it got colder in California to justify making it more often!  But it is perfect for those cold winter states!
