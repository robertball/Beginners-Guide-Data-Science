Title:
Carly's Corn Souffle Casserole Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 can whole kernel corn (undrained)
1 can cream style corn
1 cup Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix
1 cup Sour Cream
1 stick butter or margarine (melted)
2 Eggs (slightly beaten)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in bowl and put into a greased 9Ã—13 pan.
Bake covered for 50 minutes and uncovered for 10 minutes (1 hour total).
