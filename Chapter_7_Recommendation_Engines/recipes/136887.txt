Title:
Creme Brulee  French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985440.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 tablespoons corn syrup
1 loaf Archer Farms™ whole grain white bread, cut into 1-inch chunks
3 cups half and half
2 teaspoons Archer Farms™ vanilla
1/4 teaspoon salt
15 eggs

Directions:
Heat brown sugar, butter and corn syrup in microwavable dish in microwave until butter is melted. Pour into 13x9-inch (3 quart) glass baking dish.
Sprinkle bread chunks over brown sugar mixture in baking dish. Whisk remaining ingredients together and pour over bread. Cover and refrigerate 2 hours and up to a day.
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Uncover baking dish, bake 60 to 70 minutes or until puffed and center is set.
Serve with Archer Farms(TM) berry syrup and a spoonful of fresh berries to make it special.
