Title:
Kelly's Favorite Glazed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Baby carrots
1 large	Green bell pepper, seeded and     sliced into thin strips
1 large	Vidalia onion, sliced into thin strips
2 cups	Light brown sugar
4 tbsps	Butter
½  cup	water
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter and sauté onions and peppers til soft, about 4 minutes.
Add carrots, brown sugar, salt & pepper, and water.  Cover and simmer 30-40 minutes stirring occasionally.
When carrots are soft, turn up heat to medium and cook 10 minutes more.
If a thicker glaze is desired, add a little more brown sugar and butter until desired consistency is achieved.
Recipe as written serves 4, but it can be doubled or halved.

Submitter: 
Linda Glenn
Description: 
This is my friend and neighbor Kelly's favorite dish. Whenever I invite her to dinner, she'll only agree to come if I promise to make this dish.  My Nana used to make a similar dish using honey instead of brown sugar, and added slivered almonds.  It was delicious that way as well!
