Title:
Chicken Pasta II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package pasta bowtie or your prefrence
1/4 pound bacon
2 cloves garlic minced
1/2 cup chopped onion
8 ounces fresh mushrooms chopped
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-sized pieces
3/4 cup white wine or chicken broth
2 cans diced tomatoes that are already seasoned
many varieties pick two you like
1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain& rinse.
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Set bacon aside to drain on paper towels. Drain all but 3 tablespoons bacon grease, then saute onion & mushrooms about 2 minutes, add chicken cook until no longer pink, and juices run clear. Break bacon into pieces, and return to skillet, addd minced garlic , Stir in wine and tomatoes. Sprinkle with Italian seasoning, salt & pepper to taste. Simmer until tomatoes are cooked, about 20 minutes, stir in cooked pasta serve.
