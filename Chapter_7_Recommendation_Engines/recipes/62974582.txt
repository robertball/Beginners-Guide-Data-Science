Title:
Oatmeal Apple Crisp To Die For! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/736175.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Brown Sugar
1 Cup Rolled Oats
1 Cup All-Purpose Flour
3/4 Cup Melted butter
4 Medium Delisious Red Apples
3/4 Cup White Sugar
3 Tbs Ground Cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees F (175 Degrees C)
Spray an 8x8 Pan with Pam.
In a large bowl combine brown sugar, rolled oats, flour & butter (butter should be soft or melted).
Place half of crumble mixture in bottom of pan and press down firmly.
Remove peel and core from apples, slice into thin slices and place on top of bottom layer of crumble.
I a small bowl mix white sugar & cinnamon together thoroughly then sprinkle over top of apples.
Top off with remaining crumble mixture (do not press down, leave lose on top).
Bake in a preheated oven for 40-45 minutes or until golden brown.
