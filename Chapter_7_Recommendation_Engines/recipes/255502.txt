Title:
Healthy Halloween Roasted Red Pepper Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4508637.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained with liquid reserved
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon tahini
1 roasted red pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
Combine garbanzo beans, lemon juice, olive oil, tahini, red pepper, salt, and garlic in a food processor; blend until smooth. Stir in reserved liquid to desired texture.

Submitter: 
Kim Lehman
Description: 
The red pepper gives this hummus a lovely orange color, perfect for Halloween. Serve with black corn chips.
