Title:
Broccoli Apple Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2070209.jpg
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons agave nectar
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
fresh ground black pepper
sea salt
Salad:
6 cups broccoli florets, chopped
1 apple - peeled, cored, and chopped
1/2 cup slivered almonds
1/2 cup grated carrots
1/4 cup diced red onion
1/4 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise, lemon juice, agave nectar, garlic, apple cider vinegar, black pepper, and sea salt together in a bowl.
Combine broccoli, apple, almonds, carrots, red onion, and cranberries in a separate bowl.
Pour dressing into the salad; toss until well-coated. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
The Kilted Q
Description: 
A light broccoli apple salad, good as a side dish or as a meal of its own.

 A great mix of flavors and textures that please with each bite.
