Title:
Old Fashion Caramel Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick margarine
1 1/2 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in double boiler. Bring to a boil and let boil for 2 minutes.
Cool, and beat, beat, beat.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Old fashioned, made from scatch caramel icing. Handed down to me from my mother.
