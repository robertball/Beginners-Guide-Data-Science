Title:
Truly Soft Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 Cup Butter
3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
1/4 Cup White Sugar
1 Egg
2 Teaspoons Vanilla
2 Teaspoons Cornstarch
1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
1 Teaspoon Salt
2 Cups Flour
1 1/2 Cups Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine butter and sugars with a mixer until light and fluffy.
Add in egg and vanilla, beat until combined.
Add in cornstarch, baking powder, salt, and flour.  Finishing mixing by hand if necessary.
Add chocolate chips by hand.
Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheets and bake for about 10 to 12 minutes.
These do not spread much, so they don't need a lot of space on the cookie sheet.

Submitter: 
superfox12082
Description: 
These are a truly soft cookie due to the cornstach in them.  Even a few days later they do not harden up.  Enjoy!
