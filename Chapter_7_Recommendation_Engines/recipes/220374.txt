Title:
Mixed Berry Smoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993203.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup V8 V-Fusion® Acai Mixed Berry Juice
1 cup vanilla low-fat yogurt
1 cup frozen blueberries

Directions:
Place the juice, yogurt and blueberries into a blender. Cover and blend until the mixture is smooth. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Frozen fruit, low-fat vanilla yogurt and V8 V-Fusion® Açai Mixed Berry juice are blended together to create a nutritious and delicious smoothie. Great any time of day, each serving of this smoothie provides 1/2 serving of vegetables and 1 1/2 servings of fruit and is an excellent source of calcium and vitamin C.*
