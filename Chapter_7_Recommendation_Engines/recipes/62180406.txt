Title:
Frosty and Dusty the Snowman Marshmellow Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags of marshmellows (72 pieces)
24 candy sticks
1 pckg baking premium white chocolate
1 pckg baking semi-sweet chocolate
small tubes of decorating jell in black and orange

Directions:
Thread 3 marshmellows on each stick. Divide into 2 portions of 12.
Melt chocolate in microwavable bowl for 2 minutes or untill chocolate is almost melted. Stir chocolate until completly melted and smooth.
Dip 12 sticks in white chocolate for Frosty, and 12 sticks stick in semi-sweet for Dusty. Let the excess drip in the bowl before placing on waxed paper for the chocolate to become firm.
FOR FROSTY:
Use the black jell to make dots for eyes, and coal mouth, and the orange for the carrot nose.
FOR DUSTY
Use white chocolate to make dots for the eyes and coal mouth, and orange jell for the carrot nose.
After they have become completly dry/firm can be put into idividual platic bags tied with little christmas ribbons.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
Very easy, but festive fun candy made with marshmellow dipped in chocolate.
