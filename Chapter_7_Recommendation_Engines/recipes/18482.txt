Title:
Christmas Creamy Eggnog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5911494.jpg
Ingredients:
4 egg yolks
1 (5 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 1/2 cups milk
4 egg whites
1 fluid ounce rum
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, beat egg yolks until they are thickened and light.  Gradually stir in condensed milk,  sugar, vanilla and milk.  Beat the egg whites until stiff, then add them to the milk mixture. Stir rum into the mixture (to taste).  Garnish with nutmeg.

Submitter: 
sal
Description: 
This rich and creamy drink was especially designed for cold Christmas nights. This recipe contains raw eggs. We recommend that pregnant women, young children, the elderly and the infirm do not consume raw eggs.
