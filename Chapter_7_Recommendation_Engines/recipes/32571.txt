Title:
Chocolate Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/423574.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
1 tablespoon chocolate syrup
1 cup milk
1 cup crushed ice

Directions:
In a blender, combine banana, chocolate syrup, milk and crushed ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
