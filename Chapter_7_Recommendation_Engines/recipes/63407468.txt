Title:
My First Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 of a medium yellow onion, chopped
2 carrots, diced
1/2 lb. fresh green beans, ends trimmed and cut into 1 inch pieces
1 stalk of celery, diced
1 can of condensed cream of mushroom soup
6 potatoes, peeled and cubed
3 tablespoons butter
1/3 cup of milk
1/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
2. Place potato cubes in a large pot filled with lightly salted water. Bring potatoes to a boil and cook until softened. Drain the potatoes and transfer back to cooking pot. Add 3 tablespoons on butter, 1/3 cup of milk, 1/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese, and salt and pepper to taste. Blend well with potato masher or electric hand mixer. Set aside.
3. In a large skillet over medium high heat, brown 1 lb. of ground beef. Drain fat.
4. Add to your skillet the can of condensed soup, celery, carrots, onion, and green beens. Mix well.
5. Transfer beef and vegetable mixture into a 2 quart baking dish and spread evenly. Top your mixture with mashed potatoes.
6. Place your dish into the oven for 30 minutes, or until it's baked warm all of the way through and your mashers are nice and golden. Enjoy!

Description:
