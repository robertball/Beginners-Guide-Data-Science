Title:
PANETTONE (ITALIAN EASTER BREAD) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup warm water(105*-115*)
2 packages (1 and /2tbsp.active dry yeast
4and 1/2cups all purpose flour
1/2cup butter
1/2cup white sugar
2-3 large eggs
1tsp.salt
2tsps. grated lemon zest
melted butter
1/4cup golden raisins
1/2cup slivered almonds
1/4 cup sugar.
FOR THE LEMON GLAZE
1and 1/4cups icing sugar, sifted
1/4cup fresh lemon juice
1tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease well 2,9inch tube pans
In a medium bowl combine the warm water and yeast, let stand for 5 minutes. Stir in 1 cup of the flour.
Cover this mixture and let rise in a warm place about 30 minutes.
Meanwhile beat in a large bowl until soft 1/2 cup butter. Gradually add and blend until light and creamy 1/2 cup sugar. Beat in one at a time 2-3 large eggs, add 1tsp.salt,2tsps. grated lemon zest.
Beat in the yeast mixture that was resting, the 1/4cup golden raisins. Then gradually beat in 3 and 1/2 cups flour. Beat the dough for 5 more minutes. Cover the bowl with a clean cloth and let rise about 2 hours or until almost doubled in bulk, in a warm place. When ready punch down the dough and divide it in half. Place in the 2 well greased tube pans, and let rise until puffy, about 30 minutes. Lightly brush the top of the cakes with melted butter and combine the 1/2cup almonds and 1/4cup sugar and sprinkle over cakes.  Bake until golden, about 30 minutes LEMON GLAZE. *See notes*
The glaze should be spread on while the cakes are warm. Beat together until smooth the icing sugar, lemon juice and vanilla, and spread on cakes.
