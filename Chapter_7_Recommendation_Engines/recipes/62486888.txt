Title:
Kent's birthday cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chocolate Cake:
3 cups flour
2 cups sugar
2 tsp. soda
2 tsp. salt
1/3 cup cocoa
2 tsp. vanilla
2 Tbsp. cider vinegar
3/4 cup oil
2 cups water
Peanut Butter Frosting:
3 cups powdered sugar
1/3 cup peanut butter
1-1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/4 to 1/3 cup milk

Directions:
Chocolate Cake:
Mix dry ingredients together.  Add liquid ingredients.  Mix by hand or small mixer.  Pour into ungreased 9x13 pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 - 40 minutes
Peanut Butter Frosting:
Mix powdered sugar and peanut butter.  Stir in vanilla and milk.  Beat until frosting is smooth and spreadable.
