Title:
Frozen Chocolate Covered Cherries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of ice
1 1/2 cups store bought mudslide mix
3/4 cup vodka
6 maraschino cherries
1/3 cup(1/4 of jar) of maraschino cherry juice

Directions:
1. Fill blender 3/4 full with ice.  Pour in remaining ingredients.  Mix on high for two minutes or until it is a smooth consistancy.
2. Pour into 4 glasses.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jennifer K
Description: 
We have this every summer to cool off.  Mudslide mix and cherry juice really disguise the alcohol taste.
