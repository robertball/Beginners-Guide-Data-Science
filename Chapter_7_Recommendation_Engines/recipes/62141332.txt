Title:
Cheesy Mixed Vegetable Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 stick butter
2 cans mixed vegetables (drained)
1 can whole kernel corn (drained)
1/4 cup onion (chopped)
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup Velveeta cheese (cubed)
salt and pepper to taste
cracker crumbs or bread crumbs for topping

Directions:
Melt butter and pour into bottom of baking dish.  Grease the sides as well to prevent sticking.  To dish add the mixed vegetables and corn that have been drained of their juices.  Mix in chopped onion, mayo, and cheese.  Season to taste with salt and pepper. Stir well.  Top with crushed cracker crumbs or bread crumbs.  Bake 350 till hot and bubbly about 20-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cindy Buckley Wuerdeman
Description: 
This is so easy and absolutely delicious.  Anytime I serve this I am asked for the recipe.  It taste like it was made by a gourmet cook, but a novice can make it in less than 10 minutes.
