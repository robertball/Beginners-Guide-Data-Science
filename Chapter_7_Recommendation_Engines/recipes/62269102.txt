Title:
Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can Each, Drained & Rinsed:
White Shoepeg Corn
Black-Eyed Peas
Black Beans
1 Jar Pimiento's
2 T. Chopped Jalapeno
1/2 Sweet Spanish Onion, Chopped
4 Celery Stalks, Chopped
1/2 Cup Olive Oil
1/2 Cup Apple Cider Vinegar
1/2 Cup Sugar

Directions:
Mix all drained and chopped ingredients together.  In small saucepan over low heat, cook oil, vinegar & sugar til sugar dissolves.  Pour over beans, cover and refrigerate overnight.  Serve with tortilla's.

Submitter: 
Brenda
Description: 
An amazingly tasty summer salsa served with corn chips - the perfect dish to pass at a friendly gathering with a bag of tortilla chips!
