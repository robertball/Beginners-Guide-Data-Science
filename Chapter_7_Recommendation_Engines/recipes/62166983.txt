Title:
Cranberry Chicken & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Boneless, skinless chicken strips (2 lbs.)or so
1 Pkg. Lipton onion soup mix
1 Small bottle Catalina Dressing
1 Can Whole Cranberries in Sauce
Cracked or coursely ground pepper to taste
4 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Lightly brown chicken strips in 2 T oil.  Place in 13x9 glass baking dish.
Combine soup mix, dressing and cranberry sauce and spread over chicken. Grind pepper over the chicken & sauce and bake uncovered for 25 min. in a 375 degree oven.
Serve over cooked rice.  With cooked freah green beans and a salad, it is wonderful and pretty enough for company.

Description:
