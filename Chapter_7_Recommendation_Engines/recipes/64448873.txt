Title:
Indian Vegetable Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Coconut oil
8-10 cloves garlic minced
1-2 inch ginger minced
1 onion chopped
3 T curry powder
2 T cumin
1 T paprika
1/2-1 T coriander
1/2 T cinnamon
5-10 cardamom pods crushed/chopped
Cloves? Crushed/chopped
Pepper corn crushed/chopped
Mustard seed crushed
Spice/ginger/cayenne to taste
2 carrots chopped
1 small cauliflower chopped
2 c green beans
1/2 can tomato paste
1 14.5 oz can diced tomato
1-2 fresh tomato chopped
1 can light coconut milk
lemon juice
1-2 c chicken stock (aprox)
salt
serve with: lemon, yogurt, green onion, cilantro/parsley, fresh chopped tomato, rice, naan

Directions:
heat oil in pan
add garlic, ginger, onion to soften
add spices
add carrots, cauliflower, green beans and coat with spices
add tomato paste
add diced tomatoes
add stock as needed
simmer
add coconut milk
season with salt and lemon juice
adjust seasonings
