Title:
Turnip bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c milk
3/4 c half and half
pinch of thyme
pinch minced garlic
1 bay leaf
2 turnips, peeled and finely sliced
1 c spinach or kale
2 oz Gruyere cheese
2 T melted butter
1/4 c Italian or plain bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat over to 400F.  In saucepan add milk, half and half, thyme, garlic and bay leaf and heat for about 3 minutes. Add turnip slices amd cook over medium heat until tender. Meanwhile steam spinach or kale till wilted. Remove bay leaf from turnip mix. Spray a medium casserole dish with non-sticking cooking spray. Using a slotted spoon remove turnips from milk mixture and add to casserole dish. Add wilted greans to  casserole dish. Pour about 3/4 c of milk mixture on top and then top with Gruyere cheese. Mix melted butter with breadcrumbs and top the cheese. Bake for approximately 20-30 minutes until top is bubbly and moisture is mostly absorbed from dish.

Turnips cooked till tender and topped with cheese and bread crumble mix. Yum!
