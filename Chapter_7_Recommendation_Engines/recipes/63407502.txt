Title:
The Bartender's Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/918924.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tablespoon dipping mustard, such as Herlocker's
2 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon minced prepared garlic
1/2 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
1 teaspoon sweet paprika
1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl.  Place steaks in a zip-lock bag and pour marinade over.  Seal bag and massage marinade into steaks.  Marinate 8 hour or overnight, turning bag every so often.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
My husband and I were sitting at the bar for happy hour, and I mentioned I was grilling steak that night to the bartender.  He grabbed a cocktail napkin and jotted down his signature steak marinade recipe for me!
