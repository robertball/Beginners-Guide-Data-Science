Title:
Sweet Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. of Ground Turkey (you can use a full lb. as well)
1 small sweet onion, diced
2 can's Kidney Beans, drained
1 can Pork and Beans, DO NOT DRAIN
1 can Maple or Sugar Beans, drained
1 can white beans (Garbonzo, Great Northern etc.)
1 can black or pinto beans, drained
1 can tomato sauce
2 to 3 Tbl. Chili Powder
1 to 1-1/2 diced Chipolte peppers

Directions:
Cook onion along with ground turkey, drain. Add beans, tomato sauce, peppers and chili powder. Heat through. If you still need more sweetness add suger (brown or white) until you get desired taste.

Submitter: 
Debbie
Description: 
This is a great sweet chili that still has some heat to it. The turkey instead of ground beef also lowers the fat in the meal. It is always a hit.
