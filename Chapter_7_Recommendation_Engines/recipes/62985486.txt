Title:
Pumpkin Bread (Dad's Recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Topping:
1 tbsp. flour
5 tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tbsp. butter - softened
For bread:
3 cups flour
3/4 tsp. salt
2 tsp. baking soda
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. ground cloves
1/4 tsp. allspice
16 oz. canned plain pumpkin
3/4 cup vegetable oil
2 1/4 cups sugar
4 eggs - lightly beaten
2 cups apple - chopped
2 9x5x3 loaf pans - greased

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix together flour, salt, baking soda and spices.
In another bowl, whisk together oil, pumpkin, sugar and eggs.
Add flour mixture to pumpkin mixture and mix well.
Fold in apples and split batter in prepared pans.
Combine topping ingredients until crumb like and sprinkle over top of batter.
Bake for 50-60 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
