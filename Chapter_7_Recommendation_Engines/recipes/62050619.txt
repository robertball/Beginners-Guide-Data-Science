Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. chopped pecans
1/4 c. chopped onion
2 Tbsp. butter
1 4 oz. can chopped green chilis, drained
1 3 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 Tbsp. milk
1/4 tsp. ground cumin
2 C. cooked chicken, chopped
12 7in. flour tortillas
1 10-3/4 can cream of chicken soup
1 8 oz. sour cream
1 C. milk
3/4 C. monterey jack/cheddar cheese
2 Tbsp. pecans, chopped

Directions:
In skillet, saute 1/4 C. pecans and onion in butter over medium heat until pecan toast and onion in translucent.
Remove from heat and stir in 1 Tbsp. chili peppers.
In medium mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, 1 Tbsp. milk, and cumin.  Add nut mixture & chicken. Stir.
Spoon 3 Tbsp. mixture on edge of tortilla and roll placing seam side down.
Place in greased 3 qt. baking dish.
Mix soup, sour cream, 1 C. milk, and remaining chilis.  Pour over tortillas.
Cover & Bake at 350 for 35 minutes.
Remove foil-sprinkle w/ cheese & 2 Tbsp. pecans.  Bake 5 minutes or until cheese melts.

Submitter: 
Nicole73
Description: 
My mom makes these creamy chicken enchiladas and they are the perfect comfort food.  The pecans make them so good and different from regular enchiladas.
