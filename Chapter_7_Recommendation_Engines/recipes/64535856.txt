Title:
Bean with Ham Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3101453.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 chopped onion
1 cup diced celery
1 cup diced carrot
1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
6 garlic cloves, chopped
3 cups diced ham
1 pound dried Great Northern beans
8 cups ham stock (chicken broth if you don't have it)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees.
Heat a dutch oven with oil over medium low heat.  Add onion, celery, carrot, thyme and garlic; cook 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Add beans, and ham stock. Cover and place in oven for 8 hours. Return dutch oven to stove top on medium low heat. Remove 1-1/2 cups of soup and place in blender. Blend until smooth, slowly adding water if needed.  Return blended soup into dutch oven and stir.
Add diced ham and salt and pepper to taste. Cook 5-10 minutes to allow flavors to meld.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
Creamy, rich and oh so delicious! This robust soup will keep you full long after the bowl is empty. 
Perfect recipe for those holiday ham left overs. Boil your bone-in ham to make your stock and freeze until ready to make your soup! This recipe can be made in a slow cooker as well.
