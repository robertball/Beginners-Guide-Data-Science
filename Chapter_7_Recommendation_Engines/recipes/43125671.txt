Title:
Yucatan Chicken & Lime Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1008645.jpg
Ingredients:
3 quarts low sodium, fat free chicken broth
1 large onion, thinly sliced
1 large green pepper, thinly sliced
1 large red pepper, thinly sliced
1 large yellow pepper, thinly sliced
1 large carrot, thinly sliced
1 tomato, blended
1 lime, juice of
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
2 lbs. boneless chicken breast, boiled and shredded

Directions:
Saute garlic in olive oil until lightly browned.
Add onion, peppers, and carrots.
Saute for about 2 minutes.
Pour in chicken broth with the blended tomato.
Add chicken and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low, simmer on low heat for 2 hours.
Remove from heat, stir in lime juice.
