Title:
Penne di Parma Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter
2 cloves minced garlic
1 1/2 cups thinly sliced mushrooms (about 4 oz)
2 oz prosciutto (or thin sliced smoked ham), sliced into thin strips (about 3/4 cup)
1 cup milk
1/2 cup cream
8 oz penne rigate, uncooked
1/4 Cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1/4 Cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
In large skillet over medium heat, melt butter; add garlic and mushrooms.
Cook 3 minutes or until mushrooms are tender, stirring frequently.
Add prosciutto, milk and cream heat to boiling.
Reduce heat to medium; simmer, uncovered, until mixture is reduced by half, about 6 minutes. Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions.
Remove sauce from heat; add 3 tablespoons Parmesan cheese and parsley.
Toss hot pasta and sauce sprinkle with remaining cheese.
