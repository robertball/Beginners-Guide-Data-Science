Title:
Delicious Microwave Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. diced potatoes
2 T. butter, melted
1 t. seasoned salt
1 T. parsley
1 t. garlic powder
1 t. onion powder
salt and pepper to taste
optional: 1/2 C. milk

Directions:
1. Put potatoes in 1-gallon plastic zipper bag. Add seasoned salt, parsley, garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper. Shake until all potato pieces are coated.
2. Empty potatoes into a large microwave-safe bowl. Pour melted butter over potatoes and toss until coated.
3. Cover and microwave on high 5 min. or until soft, stirring every 90 seconds or so.
4. (Optional) Mash cooked potatoes and mix in milk.
