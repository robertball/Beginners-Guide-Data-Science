Title:
Vegetable Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package mixed frozen peppers
1 Package frozen sugar snap peas
1 Can chick peas
1 Can italian style stewed chopped tomatoes
1 Chopped onion
1/4 cup matchstick carrots
2 Tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 tablespoon grated parmesan cheese.

Directions:
Pour olive oil in a large frying pan. Add sugar snap peas and cook until thawed.
Add onion, tomatoes, and chick peas.
Cook about 2 minutes.
Add peppers, basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
Cook 3 to 5 minutes.
Add carrots and cook about 1-2 more minutes.
Serve topped with cheese.
