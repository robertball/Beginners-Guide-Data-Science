Title:
Garlic Scrambled Eggs I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs, beaten
5 cloves garlic, slivered
1 tablespoon olive oil
salt
pepper

Directions:
Heat oil and garlic in skillet over medium high heat until garlic is golden brown.
Remove garlic with slotted spoon and set aside.
Add eggs to remaining oil and cook to desired consistency.
Season eggs with browned garlic, salt, and pepper.
Eat them.
