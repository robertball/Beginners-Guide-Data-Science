Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 pound of hamburger meat
2 strips of green bell pepper
1/2 stalk of celery
2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tbsp brown sugar
1 1/2 tbsp vinegar
1-2  tbsp ketchup
1 teaspoon mustard
1 pack of buns

Directions:
Brown meat. Add onions, veggies and cook till veggies are tender. Add everything else and simmer for about 10-15 mins on low. If dry add 1 tbsp of water.
