Title:
Southwest Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Use any of the following fuit:
Melon - any kind
peaches
nectarines
mango
plums
papaya
seedless grapes
fresh mint leaves
juice of one lime
kosher salt
chili powder
ground red chipotle
honey

Directions:
Cut fruit into bite-sized pieces.  I use at a minimum 3 of whatever fruit I can find fresh at the time.  Sprinkle with 1/4 to 1/2 tsp salt, 1 T fresh mint leaves chopped, 1/8 to 1/4 tsp chili powder (to taste), a pinch of ground chipotle, 1 or 2 T honey and the juice of one lime.  Stir it all together and enjoy.
