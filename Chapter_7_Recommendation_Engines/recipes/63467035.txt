Title:
Wilton's Whoopie Pie Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 cup brown sugar, packed
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups all purpose flour
1/3 cup cocoa powder
1 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
1 cup buttermilk
Whoopie Pie Filling

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Lightly spray whoopie pan with cooking spray
Beat butter and sugar with mixer until light and fluffy, apx 3".
Add eggs & vanilla.  Beat until well combined
Sift flour, cocoa powder, baking & soda powders, and salt in a seperate bowl.
Add half dry into butter mixer on low speed.  When almost incorporated,stop mixer and add buttermilk.  Continue to beat on low until all ingredients are almost incorporated.
Stop mixer, and add remaining dry ingredients.  Mix on low until just combined.
Spoon 2 tablespoons of batter into each cavity;  spread to edge (cavity should be about 2/3rd full).
Bake for 8-10 minutes or until top springs back when touched.
Allow to cool in pan 8 minutes, then remove and cool completely before filling.
To assemble, spread filling on one cake and sandwich with another.
