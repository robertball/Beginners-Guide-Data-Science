Title:
Thai Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless boneless chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
3 carrots, julienned
4 TB olive oil, divided
1/3 cup orange juice
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup water
2 TB catsup
1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp ginger
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 cup peanuts
2 TB cornstarch

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat 2 TB of the olive oil.  Saute carrots with ginger and garlic for 5 minutes.  Remove and set aside.  Add remaining olive oil and saute chicken for approximately 10 minutes or until almost done.  Add carrots and the remaining ingredients and cover and simmer for 5 minutes.  Remove 1/4 cup of broth and add cornstarch to it and stir thoroughly until there are no lumps.  Bring broth to a boil and slowly pour in cornstarch mixture and cook until slightly thickened.  Serve over rice or chinese noodles.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
Slightly sweet, a little spicy and a hint of orange gives this chicken dish a unique flavor that is sure to wake up your taste buds.  Serve over chinese noodles or rice along with a green salad for a delicious, quick meal.
