Title:
Portuguese Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz smoked bacon, diced
8 oz chorizo or kielbasa, 1/4 in coins, or any flavorful sausage
1 lg yellow onion, diced
4 garlic cloves, thinkly sliced
1/4 c tomato paste  (I used ketchup)
2 med Yukon gold potatoes (I used 3 russet potatoes)
2  15 oz can Great Northern Beans (I used white beans-sauce and all)
1  28 oz can diced tomatoes
2 Tbs honey (or sugar) (or brown sugar)
1 Tbs paprika
2 tsp salt (I used 1 1/2, & thought it was plenty)
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Cook bacon in a 6 qt pot until golden and crisp.  Remove and set aside.
Discard all but 1 tbs bacon fat, add sausage, cook until browned.
Remove sausage, reserve.
Discard all but 1 tbs fat &amp; add onion, garlic, and cook until golden, about 4 min
Stir in tomato paste.  Add potatoes, beans, 4 c. water, tomatoes, honey, paprika, salt, bay leaf and reserved sausage.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for about 30 min.
Remove bay leaf and serve with bacon on top.
