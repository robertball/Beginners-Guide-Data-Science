Title:
Inside Out Ravioli II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds medium seashell pasta
5 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach
6 pound lean ground beef, salt & pepper
chopped garlic and dried onion
10 (26 ounce) jars pasta sauce, divided
3 lbs shredded Cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a skillet over medium heat, cook ground beef and dried onion until brown, add garlic near end.  In a large bowl, stir together cooked beef, spinach, 2/3s of pasta sauce and all but 2 cups of the Cheddar cheese.  Reserve 1/3 sauce mixture.  Stir the pasta shells into the sauce mixture.  Pour shell mixture into a 18x13 inch baking dish and top with reserved sauce and sprinkle with reserved cheese.
Bake in preheated oven 1 hour, until browned and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
Ground beef, Cheddar, spinach and pasta sauce baked with seashell pasta -- quick to assemble and quicker to eat.Modified from: Inside Out Ravioli II
