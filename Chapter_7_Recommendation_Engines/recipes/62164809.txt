Title:
Julia's Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/365522.jpg
Ingredients:
2.5 lbs front cut prime brisket
5 potatoes
2 cubes beef bouillon
1/4 cup warm water
1 1/2 cup beef broth
1/4 cup bloody mary mix
3/4 cup baby carrots
1 teaspoon minced onion
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 bay leaf
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon season salt
1/4 teaspoon basil
1/4 teaspoon celery salt
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut up potatoes into fairly large chunks and place in slow cooker.
Brown roast in skillet over medium high heat. Then, place roast on top of potatoes in slow cooker.
Place the 2 bouillon cubes randomly on top of roast and poor in water, beef broth, and bloody mary mix.
Season with minced onion and garlic, bay leaf, onion powder, season salt, basil, celery salt, and salt & pepper.
Cook on high 6 hours or until meat is juicy and tender.
Note: You may use some of the juice to make a thicker sauce with some corn starch and cold water.
