Title:
Leftover meat curry casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion chopped
pinch sugar
1/4 tsp. black pepper
several mushrooms chopped
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 tbsp.. curry powder
1 1/2 - 2 cups leftover meat (any type) chopped
1 cup leftover veggies (optional and any kind will work) chopped
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 - 3/4 can milk
1/2 package egg noodles (even no-egg noodles work)

Directions:
in large sauce pan or dutch oven heat oil on medium high heat.
Add onions, sugar and black pepper.
Fry for about 1 minutes.
Add mushrooms and fry for about 1 minutes.
Add curry powder and allow the mixture to fry for about 1 minutes
Add meat and veggies (is using) cook for about 1 minute
Add cream of mushroom soup and milk
Turn heat down to low and simmer
in a separate pot cook noodles per package directions
Once done drain add to the mushroom mixture.
Allow mixture to either simmer on the stove or pop into the oven with a temp of 350 degrees. for about 1 hour
After about 15 to 20 minutes give a stir and check for flavour.  If need be for your taste add more curry, salt and pepper.  If you find it to thick always add some additional milk until you get the consistency you like.

Description: 
This leftover casserole is great with any kind of meat for sure.
