Title:
Soap Crayons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c pure soap powder ( Ivory Snow)
food coloring
1/2 c water
small shaped containers, molds or ice trays

Directions:
Mix water & soap together, add food coloring & pour into molds. Let harden & pop out. Enjoy

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
These are home made crayons, such a fun activity for a rainy afternoon.
