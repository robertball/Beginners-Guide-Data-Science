Title:
Party Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-12 oz. can Orange Juice (unprepared)
2 cups Grenadine
2 cups lemon juice (or concentrate)
3 cups water
2-2 liter 7-Up or diet 7-Up

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except 7-Up and chill overnight.  Mix half the mixture with one cold bottle of 7-Up.  Throw in some ice cubes to keep it cold.

Submitter: 
cmccreight
Description: 
This punch recipe was given to me by a friend and is always a crowd favorite.  Sometimes, I cut the recipe in half and make it for us at home.  It's yummy!
