Title:
Restaurants' pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. of Jiffy Baking mix or Bisquick
3 extra large eggs
2 1/2 c club soda
1/4 c. melted vegetable shortening, at room temp
vegetable oil

Directions:
1.  Preheat griddle or skillet over medium high heat.
2. Mix all ingredients together there will be some lumps.
3. Let batter rest while you place 1 T. of oil in skillet.  Use 1/2 c batter per pancake.  Flip once after bubbles have popped and edges are cooked on each cake.

Nice fluffy pancakes made the restaurant way.
