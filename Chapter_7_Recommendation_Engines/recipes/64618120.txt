Title:
English Muffin Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123042.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package (12 ounce each)  Johnsonville Sausage Links Original.  I used roll of Bob Evans Sausage
6 English muffins, cut into 1-in. cubes (Lauren said she even used the whole wheat at one time)
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 cup (4 ounce) Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 cup (4 ounce) mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup red pepper, chopped
12 eggs
2 cups milk
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1/4 cup bacon bits

Directions:
1.Cook sausage according to package directions. Cool slightly; cut into ¼-inch slices.
2.In a greased 13-inch x 9-inch baking dish, layer half the English muffin cubes and half the sausage. Repeat layers.
3.Drizzle with butter. Sprinkle with the cheese, onion and red pepper.
4.In a large bowl, combine the eggs, milk, salt and pepper. Pour over casserole. Top with bacon.
5.Cover and refrigerate overnight.
6.Remove from the refrigerator 30 minutes before baking. Uncover and bake at 350°F for 45 to 50 minutes, or until a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
7.Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
