Title:
Lentil and Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound sausage
1 large onion, chopped
3 stalks celery, finely chopped
1 tablespoon chopped garlic
1 (16 ounce) package dry lentils, rinsed
1 cup sliced carrots
11 cups chicken broth
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1/2 teaspoon dried cumin
1/2 teaspoon dried Rosemary and Garlic seasoning

Directions:
Place sausage in a large pot. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain excess grease. Add onion, celery and chopped garlic, and saute until tender and translucent. Stir in lentils, carrot, water, chicken broth and tomatoes. Add remaining seasoning. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat. Cover, and simmer for 1 1/2 to 2 hours, or until lentils are tender.
