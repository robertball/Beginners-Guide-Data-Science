Title:
Overnight Muesli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tblspn Quick oats
1 Tblspn Oat bran
1 Tblspn ground flax seed
1 Tblspn cooked brown rice
12-14 raisins
1/4 tspn cinnamon
1/2- 3/4 cup soy milk (or almond milk, or regular milk)

Directions:
Put first 6 ingredients into 1 cup or larger container, with a lid
Add soy milk, and stir to make sure all dry ingredients are suspended in the milk
Cover container, put in refrigerator, and chill for at least 6 hours
Take out, remove cover and stir contents completely
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
David K
Description: 
Make it the night before, and it is cold, creamy, delicious and healthy for your breakfast the next morning. Like the Kashi bowls, but much cheaper and simpler.
