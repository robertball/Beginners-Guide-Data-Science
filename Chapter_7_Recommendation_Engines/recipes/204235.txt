Title:
All-American Peach Pie Wonder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990515.jpg
Ingredients:
5 slices Wonder® Classic White Sandwich Bread, crusts removed
6 fresh peaches, peeled, pitted, and sliced
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Butter an 8-inch square baking dish.
Cut each Wonder Bread slice into 4 or 5 strips. Spread the fruit in the baking pan and cover with a layer of bread strips.
In a separate mixing bowl, beat together the butter, sugar, flour and egg. Gently spread over the fruit and bread.
Bake 40 to 50 minutes, or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Wonder® bread
Description: 
Savor the sweetness of summer with this wonderful take on an all-American dish. With the calcium of eight ounces of milk in just two slices of Wonder Classic White Sandwich Bread, this pie is not only good, it's good for you too.
