Title:
Milly's Oatmeal Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of butter, room temperature
1 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
2 cups dry oatmeal
Your choice of chips, raisins and/or nuts (optional)

Directions:
Grease and flour a 9 x 13 pan and set aside; preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream together the butter and the sugars until fluffy.
Add eggs one at a time and mix well after each addition.
Add vanilla.
Add flour, oats, salt, baking soda and baking powder and mix well.
Fold in your choice of chips/raisins/nuts.
Spread evenly in pan, place in oven.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 20-25 minutes or until done. Should have a nice golden color.
Let cool for about 5 min, cut into 12 squares.

Submitter: 
Milly Suazo-Martinez
Description: 
These are wonderful, chewy brownies except that they are made with oatmeal instead of chocolate. Add your favorites (nuts, chocolate chips,  chocolate candies, toffee chips) and create a truly delicious snack.
