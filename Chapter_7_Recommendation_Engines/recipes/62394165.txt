Title:
Greta's Fabulous Crock Pot Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 bags of frozen corn
1/2 stick of butter
8 oz of cream cheese
1/4 cup sugar

Directions:
Mix all together, that is it.  4 hours on low in a crock pot or less if you crank it up some.
