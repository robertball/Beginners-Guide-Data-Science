Title:
Dijon Crusted Halibut OR TILAPIA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup reduced fat mayonnaise
1 teaspoon prepared Dijon-style mustard
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup dry bread crumbs   Panko are the best for crispness
11/2  tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
4 (4 ounce) fillets halibut
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/4 cup Panko bread crumbs
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Lightly grease a baking sheet.
In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, mustard, horseradish, and lemon juice. Stir in 1/4 cup bread crumbs and 11/2 tablespoon Parmesan cheese. Arrange fish fillets on the prepared baking sheet. Spread bread crumb mixture evenly over fish.
In a small bowl, mix together melted margarine, 1/4 cup bread crumbs, and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese. Sprinkle over the coated halibut.
Bake for 15 MIN and then turn broiler on and cook an additional 2-3 min. TILL CRISPY ON TOP.
WATCH FISH CLOSELY WHILE UNDER BROILER SO THAT IT DOES NOT BURN!
