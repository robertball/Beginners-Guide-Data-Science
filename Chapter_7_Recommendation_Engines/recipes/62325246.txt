Title:
Tex Mex Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (16 oz.) refried beans
1/4 cup picante sauce
1-1/2 cups prepared guacamole
1/2 cup each sour cream and mayonnaise
4-1/2 tsp. taco seasoning
1 cup (4 oz.) shredded cheddar cheese
Chopped green onions, shredded lettuce and chopped tomatoes
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Combine beans and picante sauce.  Spread onto a serving platter.  Spread with guacamole.  Combine sour cream, mayonnaise and taco seasoning; spread over guacamole.  Sprinkle with cheese,onions, lettuce and tomatoes.  Refrigerate until served with tortilla chips.
