Title:
Ginger Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ginger Minced - 2 tablespoons
Lemon Juice - 1/4th cup
Sugar - 1 cup
Ice cubes as required
Lemon sliced thinly - 1

Directions:
1.In a small pan stir together two cups of water with the sugar and ginger.Bring the mixture to a boil,stirring until the sugar is dissolved.Simmer it for three minutes.Strain the syrup through a fine sieve set over a bowl and let it cool.
2. In a jug, combine the cooled syrup, three and a half cups of water and the lemon juice. Stir the lemonade well.Divide the lemonade among tall glasses filled with ice cubes and garnish each glass with a lemon slice.
