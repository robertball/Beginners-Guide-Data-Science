Title:
Coney Island Chili Dog Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground chuck
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 (6-oz.) tomato sauce
1 cup of water
1 T, white sugar
1 T. yellow mustard
1 T. onion flakes
2 tsp. chili powder (less if the powder is hot)
1 tsp. of Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp.celery seeds
3/4 tsp. ground cumin

Directions:
Saute the ground chuck. Drain grease if necessary.
Add the rest of the sauce ingredients.
Bring to a boil. Turn down heat and simmer until it thickens for 30 min.
Garnishes
chopped onion
shredded Cheddar cheese
