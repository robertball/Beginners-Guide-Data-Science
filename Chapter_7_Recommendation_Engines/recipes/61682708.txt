Title:
Southern Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium (ripe) tomato, diced
1 T. olive oil
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1 (16 ounce) package shredded coleslaw mix
1/4 cup lime juice

Directions:
Place the tomato in a large bowl, and season with salt and pepper. Stir in the coleslaw mix and lime juice. Refrigerate for 10 minutes before serving.
You can add celery and/or onion to this recipe without adding calories.

Submitter: 
ttkc
Description: 
This is a quick and original coleslaw for people who don't like mayonnaise that is made with tomato and lime juice.Modified from: Southern Coleslaw - No Mayo
