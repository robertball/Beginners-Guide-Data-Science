Title:
Wilda's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 pound ground breakfast sausage
1 cup crushed saltine crackers
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup finely chopped green bell pepper
2 eggs
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup ketchup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, sausage, crackers, onion, green pepper, salt, pepper and egg. Mix together well and place in a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 40 minutes.  Top with ketchup and bake 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Peg
Description: 
Ground beef and sausage are mixed with onion, and green pepper. This can also be made with ground turkey.Modified from: Grandma's Meatloaf
