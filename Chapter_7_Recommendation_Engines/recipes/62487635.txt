Title:
Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bunches Broccoli heads, cut into small bite sizes
10-12 slices bacon
1 med red onion, diced
1/2 cup craisins
1/4 cp. sunflower seeds
1/2 cp. sugar
2 tsp. vinegar
1 cp. mayo

Directions:
cut up raw bacon into bits, fry until crispy.
cut up broccoli, wash.
cut up onion.
mix sugar, vinegar, and mayo in large bowl.
Mix in everything.
