Title:
Lavender Icecream w/ macerated berries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz full fat milk
1 good tsp lavender petals
4 egg yolks
3.5 oz caster sugar
10 oz double cream lightly whipped
For the Berries
1/4 tsp lemon zest
2 Tbs sugar
1/2 C black berries
1/2 C strawberries quartered
1/4 C raspberries

Directions:
1. Pour the milk into a small pan, add lavender petals. Heat until bubbles start to appear around the edges, but don't allow to boil. Remove from heat and set aside 30min.
2.Strain the milk through sieve and pour back into the clean pan. Combine egg yolks and sugar in a bowl and whisk until mixed well.
3. Warm milk again until bubbles form around edge, then slowly add to egg mixture while stirring. Then place mixture back in pan and simmer over low heat for 15-20 min, or until custard thickens. Do not allow to boil. Set aside to cool.
4. Stir the cream into the custard,then pour into an icecream maker, once it's reached soft serve transfer to the freezer to set.
For Berries
Mix all ingredients in a bowl coating berries well with sugar. Set aside for about 10 min. Use fruit and rendered juices to top ice-cream.
