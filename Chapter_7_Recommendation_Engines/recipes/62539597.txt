Title:
Sage Cream Sauce for Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Tbl Butter
1/4 cup fine chopped onion
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 Tbl dried sage
1 Tbl flour
1 1/3 cup half & half
1 Tbl Bourbon
Salt & Pepper to taste
Fresh Grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Melt butter in heavy skillet over medium heat.
Add onion and garlic and saute until onion starts to turn transluscent, add sage and stir until well mixed, about 1 minute.
Add salt and pepper (I used about 1/4 tsp salt).
Whisk flour into half and half, add to skillet along with bourbon.
Continue to stir until it starts to boil.
Reduce heat and let simmer until thickened.
Serve over prepared butternut squash ravioli topped with some fresh grated parmesan.

Submitter: 
Debi Blair McGinness
Description: 
I needed a good flavorful sauce for some Butternut Squash Ravioli that I bought and came up with my own version after perusing several sites. It turned out to be the perfect compliment to the butternut squash.
