Title:
Cheesy, Bacon Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/837525.jpg
Ingredients:
8-10 Red Potatoes
2 Cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1/2 Cup Chopped Green Onion
1 Small Bag Oscar Meyer Bacon Pieces
2 Tablespoons Garlic Powder
1 Tablespoon Garlic Salt
1 Tablespoon Onion Powder
1 Tablespoon Italian Seasoning

Directions:
Wash potatoes and cut into small pieces.
In a large skillet, combine potatoes and spices and cook until brown.
Add half of the potatoes to a deep pie or casserole dish and sprinkle with half each of the cheese, green onions, and bacon pieces.
Repeat step 3 for remaining ingredients.
Bake at 325 degrees for 45 minutes, covered.  Remove cover and bake an additional 10-15 minutes to brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
I kind of just winged this recipe and threw it together for a BBQ I hosted for my soccer team and their families.  It was better than expected...and was a real hit!  And it looked ooooooh so pretty, almost didn't want to dig in!
