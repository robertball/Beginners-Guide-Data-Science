Title:
S'mores Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Box mix of brownies ,,,mix following instructions
4 1/2 Graham Crackers
30 Marshmallows

Directions:
8 x 8 pan well greased
mix brownies as directed on the package
place graham crackers on the bottom of pan
pour over the crackers the brownie batter
line up the marshmallows on top to cover the top
Bake 350^ for 25 min
