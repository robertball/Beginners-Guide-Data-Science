Title:
Pineapple Pork Chops and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Minute rice (white, brown, or wild)
2 Medium, thick cut pork chops
1-8oz. can crushed pineapple

Directions:
Thaw chops and pierce with in several places with a fork. Place chops in a shallow dish and cover with juice from the pineapple. Cover the dish and refrigerate for 1-2 hours.
Heat about 2 tablespoons of pineapple juice in a large skillet; add chops. Cook for approximately 3 minutes per side or until juices run clear.
Pour remainder of juice in a glass measuring cup, adding water as needed to make 1 cup liquid.
Prepare rice according to package directions using pineapple juice mixture instead of plain water.
Add crushed pineapple to rice immediately after removing from stove; let sit 5-10 minutes, fluffing occasionally.
Serve pork chops on bed of pineapple rice.

Submitter: 
Lisa Garnier
Description: 
This is a quick and easy recipe that I like to make when I'm short on time. My husband, a somewhat picky eater, loves this recipe! You can substitute chicken, shrimp, or any other meat you prefer as well.
