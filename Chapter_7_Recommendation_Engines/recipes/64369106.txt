Title:
Stout Corned Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds corned beef brisket
4 cloves garlic, lightly crushed
2 cups stout
2 cups broth
2 Tbsp maple syrup
2 sprigs rosemary, rough chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
4 small potatoes (baby reds)

Directions:
Wash and cut potatoes in half then place them in the bottom of the crock pot. next lay the brisket over the potatoes and pour remaining ingredients over brisket, stir, cover with lid and cook on a low heat for 6-8 hours.

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
Do your St. Patrick's day right with Irish Stout beer infused, Irish corned beef.
