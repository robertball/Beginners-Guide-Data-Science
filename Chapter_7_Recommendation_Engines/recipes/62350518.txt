Title:
Easy Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole egg
1 teaspoon prepared Mustard
2 teaspoons vinegar
1 cup vegetable oil
1/2-1 teaspoon sugar
pinch of salt

Directions:
Place the egg, mustard, and vinegar into a blender. blender on low speed  until combined. Then, slowly drizzle in the oil with the blender running (this should take about a minute) adjust blender speed as mixture thickens. Once the oil has been incorporated, scrape the mayonnaise into a bowl, add salt and stir.  Store in the refrigerator up to 3 days.

Submitter: 
BrianAmanda Stroh
Description:
