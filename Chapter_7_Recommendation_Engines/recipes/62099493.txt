Title:
Really Moist Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4541837.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups plain Unsifted Flour
2 tsp. Baking soda
2 tsp. Ground cinnamon
½ tsp. Ground ginger
½ tsp. Salt
3 eggs
1 ½ cups Sugar
¾ cups Mayonnaise
8 oz. Can crushed pineapple in own juice, undrained (save 2 Tbs. for frosting)
2 cups coarsely grated carrots (about 3 large carrots)
¾ cups coarsely chopped nuts
¼ to ½ cups raisins
FROSTING:
3 oz. Cream cheese
4 TBS unsalted butter
2 TBS reserved pineapple juice
2 ½ cups confectioners’ sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Grease and flour two 9 inch round pans.
In medium bowl stir together first five (dry) ingredients. Set aside.
In large bowl with mixer at medium speed beat eggs, sugar, mayonnaise and pineapple until well blended.
With spoon stir in carrots, nuts and raisins.
Pour batter into prepared pans.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until cake tester in middle comes out clean.
Cool in pan 10 minutes, remove from pans, cool completely.
Frost with Cream Cheese Frosting.
FROSTING:
Beat cream cheese, butter and pineapple juice until light and fluffy.
Blend confectioner's sugar slowly into the mixture.
