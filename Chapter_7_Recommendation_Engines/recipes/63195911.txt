Title:
Basic Caeser Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
one head romaine lettuce
fine shredded mozzarella cheese
caeser croutons
caeser salad

Directions:
Cut the romaine lettuce into bite sized pieces. Add the mozzarella cheese and croutons. Mix together. Add the caeser salad dressing to taste. Chill for 10-20 minutes and enjoy!!!!

Submitter: 
Becky Camarillo
Description: 
This is a very easy salad to make. Only four ingredients.
