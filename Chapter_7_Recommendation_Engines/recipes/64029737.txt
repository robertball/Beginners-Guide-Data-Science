Title:
Perfect Iced Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1119845.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh ground coffee
8 quarts cold water
Half & half
2-3 T Sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Pour water over fresh coffee grounds and let sit in covered container at room temp for 8 hours.
Line a fine mesh strainer with cheesecloth and strain liquid, pressing down on grounds as needed to extract all flavor.
Refrigerate after straining.
To make coffee, pack a glass full of ice cubes.  Fill glass 2/3rds full of coffee concentrate.  Add a splash of milk or half and half.  Top off with 2 or 3 T of sweetened condensed milk (or plain sugar if preferred).  Stir and serve.
