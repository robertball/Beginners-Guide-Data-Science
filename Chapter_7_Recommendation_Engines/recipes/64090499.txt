Title:
Skillet fried swai fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 swai fillets
1/3 cup panko
2 tsp seasoning mix (see note)
1 beaten egg
1 tbsp milk
Oil for skillet

Directions:
Preheat oil in skillet.  I use about a tablespoon full.
Mix panko and seasonings.  Mix egg and milk.  Dip fish in egg and milk, then coat with the panko.
Fry until golden and thoroughly cooked.
