Title:
Mexican chocolate porridge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/453490.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1/2 cup water
1 tbsp cocoa powder
2 tbsp sweetener such as honey, sugar or splenda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp cayenne or to taste
a pinch of nutmeg
3/4 cup quick cooking oatmeal
4 tbsp chopped toasted pecans

Directions:
Whisk together milk, water, cocoa powder, sweetener, cinnamon, cayenne, and nutmeg in a pot and bring to a boil.
Stir in oatmeal, reduce heat to low and simmer until porrige reaches desired consistency, stirring frequently (about 5 minutes).
Serve hot sprinkled with pecans.
