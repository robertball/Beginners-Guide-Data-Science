Title:
Cajun Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp EVOO
1 bell pepper (cut into strips)
1/2 cup scallions
2 whole garlic cloves
3 chicken breast halves
1 1/2 cups mushrooms sliced
1 cup crushed tomatoes
1/2 tsp dried thyme

Directions:
1. Chop the green bell pepper and scallions; mince garlic and slice mushrooms. Cut the chicken into strips 1/2 inch wide.
2. In a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, warm the oil until hot, but not smoking. Add the bell pepper, scallions, and garlic. Stir-fry until the bell pepper is tender, about 4 minutes.
3. Add the chicken and mushrooms, and cook, stirring constantly until the chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
4. Stir in the tomatoes, thyme, and half the cayenne pepper. reduce the heat to medium and simmer for 6 minutes.
5. Add the peas, increase the heat to medium-high and cook until heated through, about 2 minutes. If this isn't spicy enough, add a little more of the cayenne pepper to taste.
