Title:
Garlic Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds small red or white potatoes
1/4 cup good olive oil
1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp fresh ground black pepper
2 TBSP garlic, minced (ab. 6 cloves)
2 TBSP fresh parsley, minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Cut potatoes into halves or quarters (bite-sized pieces). In large bowl mix together olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic. Stir well. Add potatoes and toss to cover.
Spread potatoes on sheet pan in single layer. Bake in oven for 45 minutes to one hour, or until browned. Flip twice for even browning.
Remove potatoes from oven and toss with parsley, season to taste. Serve hot.
