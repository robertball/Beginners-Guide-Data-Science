Title:
Jim's Spicy Mexican Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/621945.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless checken breasts
2 - 10 oz cans rotolo diced tomatoes w/ green chiles
1- 10.5 oz can red enchilada sauce
1 - 10 oz bottle Goya pico de gallo sauce
1 -Tablespoon minced garlic

Directions:
Put all the ingredients in the slow cooker then set  the slow cooker on low for 4 hours. Couldn't be easier! Serve over rice or just by itself!

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
This is my own recipe for slow cooker spicy chicken. Very simple to make, very tasty and healthy!!
