Title:
Cajun Dried Butterbeans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/862748.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T canola oil
2 T onions
1/4 cup chopped bell pepper*
1/2 cup dried butterbeans (lima beans)
2" cube tasso* or smoked sausage for flavor (optional)
1 T Tonys Chachere's*
1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
Cooked Cajun (medium grain) white rice

Directions:
In a two cup measuring cup, cover beans with water
For best results, soak a few hours or overnight
You can still cook them if not soaked
Heat oil in a heavy pot over medium low heat
Add onions and slowly sautee until onions are translucent
Add meat
Add beans
Cover with water an inch from top of pot
Cook over medium high heat for 20 minutes
Check the water level
Add water to an inch from the top of pot as needed checking every 20 minutes or as water evaporates
Cook until creamy - about 1.5 hours
Serve over rice

Submitter: 
Wonder Becky
Description: 
This very traditional Cajun recipe is creamy and hearty.  You can make it with traditional smoked meats or vegetarian style.  It goes well with my vinegar coleslaw recipe.
