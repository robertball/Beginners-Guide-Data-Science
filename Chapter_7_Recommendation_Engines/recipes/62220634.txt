Title:
Mom's Tomato Aspic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups tomato juice
3 tsp. chopped onion
1 bay leaf
1/4 tap. paprika
1 package lemon jello
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup finely chopped celery

Directions:
Heat one cup of tomato juice with onion, bay leaf and paprika on high in microwave for 1 minute.
Continue cooking on med for 4 min.
Remove bay leaf.
Add lemon jello, remaining cup of tomato juice, and salt stirring until jello is dissolved.
Refrigerate until partially set
Add celery.
Return to the fridge until fully set.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
The jellied salad that was a must at Thanksgiving and Christmas meals.  I enjoy it so much that I find myself making as a treat even when it's not the holidays.Modified from: 1Mom's Tomato Aspic
