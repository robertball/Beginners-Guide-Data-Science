Title:
Family Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c cake flour
3 t baking powder
1/4 t salt
1 t vanilla
1/3 c milk
1/2 c butter
8 oz cream cheese
1 1/2 c sugar
3 eggs

Directions:
Pre heat oven tp 350
Sift dry ingredients
Cream butter, cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla
Beat in egg one at a time
Stir in dry ingredients in 4 additions, just until smooth
Grease 2 9" pans
Bake for about 70 min

Submitter: 
violet
Description: 
This is the cake my mom and me and my sister grew up with for birthdays. We always color each layer diffrent, pink yellow and green.
