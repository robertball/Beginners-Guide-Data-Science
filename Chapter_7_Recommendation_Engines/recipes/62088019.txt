Title:
Egg and Sausage Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3c herbed croutons
2 1/2c shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2c sausage
--browned and drained about 1lb
4 eggs
2 1/2c milk
3/4 tsp. dry mustard
1 can golden mushroom soup
1/2 soup can milk

Directions:
1. Place croutons in a 9x13pan.
Sprinkle cheese over croutons. Put browned and drained sausage on top of cheese.
2. Beat eggs and milk. Pour over all ingredients in pan.
3. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
4. In the morning dilute soup with 1/2 soup can of milk and pour over the top.
5. Bake uncovered @ 300 for 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Jenna
Description: 
This is a great Egg and Sausage Souffle that my Mom always makes.  My family always serves it with salsa for an extra kick!
