Title:
Tangy Oriental Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 8 oz bottle of either Russian dressing or Creamy French dressing
- 12 oz apricot preserves
- 1 pkg. onion soup mix
- 2 or 3 lbs boneless chicken (depending on how saucy you would like it to be)

Directions:
Mix ingredients in a bowl and thoroughly coat the thawed chicken in it.  Place chicken in a baking dish and spoon remaining sauce on top.  Bake at 350 until juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Traveling_Is_Love
Description: 
With only three ingredients, you can't go wrong!  This tender chicken is coated in a sweet and tangy mixture of dressing, apricot preserves and onion soup.  This is a wonderful recipe if you're just starting out or have minimal prep time (ie: kids running around).
