Title:
Sausage Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Italian Sausage
1 small onion
1 can (28 oz) tomato sauce
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp garlic salt
2 garlic cloves
1 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1 lb spaghetti, cooked and drained
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook sausage and chopped onion and garlic in a pan.
Add tomato sauce sauce, sugar, garlic salt, and Italian seasoning.
Let simmer for 20-30 minutes.
Pour sausage sauce over spaghetti and top with Parmesan.
