Title:
Light and Lusious Ice Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
80 Ritz Crackers, crushed
1 1/2 sticks margarine or butter, melted
2 pkg. instant coconut cream pudding and pie filling
1 1/2 C. milk
1 1/2 qt. vanilla ice cream
1 lg. frozen whipped topping (Cool Whip)
Garnish: toasted coconut or large flaked coconut found at the Fresh Market in Naples,Florida.

Directions:
Place crackers in a zip lock plastic bag and use a rolling pin to crush.
Mix the crushed crackers with melted margarine or butter and line a 9 x13 pan with the crumb mixture. Press with the back of a large off set spatula to flatten.
In a large mixing bowl, beat the pudding and milk together.
Add the ice cream and blend well.
Pour into the pan, top with whipped topping and refrigerate for 24 hours. Top with large flaked coconut and serve
