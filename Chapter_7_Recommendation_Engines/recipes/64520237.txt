Title:
Squash and pear soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  3 LB. Butternut squash, Roasted
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 Tbs. minced ginger
6 Tbs. butter
1 Tbs. curry powder
1/2 C white wine
1 Qt. chicken stock
3 ripe Bartlet pears, peeled, cored and chopped
1/2 C cream or 1/2 & 1/2
Salt & pepper, to taste

Directions:
Roast butternut squash in 375 degree oven for about 1 hour, than peel and mash.
Saute onion, garlic, ginger in butter for 5-6 minutes. Add curry powder and  wine, and cook for 5 minutes.
Add chicken stock, squash, and pears and simmer for 30 min. or until soft. Blend with submersion blender. Add cream and salt and pepper.
