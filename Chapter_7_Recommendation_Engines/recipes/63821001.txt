Title:
Easy Veggie Tuna Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. box seashell pasta
8 oz. raw spinach, torn into small pieces
1/2 sweet yellow onion, diced
1 cup frozen corn
1 can low sodium kidney beans, drained
5 cherry tomatoes, halved
1/4 tsp. pepper
3 small cans tuna fish in water, drained
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 tsp red wine vinegar

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed. Drain. Rinse in cold water and set aside.
Cook and drain frozen corn.
Mix all ingredients and chill for one hour in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Missouri Farm Wife
Description: 
This is an easy to fix salad for a quick lunch. I have substituted canned corn for frozen, garbanzo beans for the kidney beans, and macaroni for shells, depending on the state of my cupboards.
