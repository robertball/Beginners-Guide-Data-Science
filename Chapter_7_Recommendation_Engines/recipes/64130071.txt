Title:
Herb 'n Honey Cucumber Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1331315.jpg
Ingredients:
3 medium cucumbers, partially *peeled and sliced
2 large ripe tomatoes or 1 dozen cherry tomatoes
2 green onions, sliced thin, including greens
8 leaves fresh basil, chopped coarsely
1/2 c. crumbled feta cheese
1/2 c. seasoned rice wine vinegar
1/2 c. honey

Directions:
Layer cucumbers, tomatoes, green onions, basil, then top with feta.
Drizzle seasoned rice wine vinegar over all.
Drizzle honey over all.
May serve immediately or cover and refrigerate up to four hours before serving for enhanced flavor.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
Crisp and sweetly delicious, this salad is a wonderful addition to any summer picnic.
