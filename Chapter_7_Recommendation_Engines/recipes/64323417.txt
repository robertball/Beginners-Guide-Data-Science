Title:
Quick Red Curry Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2027483.jpg
Ingredients:
2 TB Olive Oil
1/4 cup Red Curry Paste
3 cups Coconut Milk
3 cups Chicken Stock
2 Limes - juiced
Zest of 1 Lime
1 TB Chopped Fresh Cilantro or 2 tsp dried cilantro
2 cups Cherry Tomatoes
1 can (14oz) Bean Sprouts
1 lb Uncooked Shrimp (tails on)
1 Cup Cooked Pork/Chicken - chopped
salt & pepper

Directions:
Sweat the Red Curry Paste with the Olive Oil for 5 minutes to release the flavors & aroma.
Add Coconut Milk, Chicken Stock, Lime Juice & Lime Zest - simmer for 10 to 15 minutes
Add Cherry Tomatoes & Cilantro - simmer for 10-15 minutes
Add Uncooked Shrimp, Pork/Chicken and Bean Sprouts.
Simmer until Shrimp is pink/cooked
Salt & Pepper to taste
Serve
