Title:
SCD Shepards Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/593860.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 onion -- chopped
1 small bell pepper -- chopped
2-3 cloves garlic -- minced
24 oz. tomato juice (no added sugars)
salt and pepper to taste
chopped fresh or frozen green beans or, frozen peas
1 head cauliflower
1/2 stick butter
12 oz. sharp shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef, onion, bell pepper, and garlic.  Add tomato juice and string beans (or peas or a mixture of the two) and cook on top of stove till it thickens. Add salt and pepper to taste.
While the meat sauce is cooking,cook the cauliflower till tender. Drain cauliflower and then beat or blend in food processor. Add butter and salt and pepper and about 1/2 of the cheese.
Put meat in a casserole dish. Top with cauliflower potatos and then top with cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes.
