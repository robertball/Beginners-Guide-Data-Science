Title:
Pickled Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz water
8 oz white wine vinegar
4 garlic cloves, cleaned and crushed
1 jalapeno, cleaned, julline
3 sprigs of thyme
2 tbl old bay seasoning
2 bay leaves
4 tbl brown sugar
1 tbl kosher salt
6 black pepper corns, whole

Directions:
-Clean asparagus by cutting off the bottom fibrous parts, about the thickest inch or two.
-combine all ingredients, except the asparagus and bring to a boil.
-place asparagus in a glass or nonreactive jar standing upright and pour the pickling liquid over, and allow to cool to room temperature before putting in the rifigerator.
-keeps in the cooler for weeks.

Submitter: 
JOHNNYDEEZ
Description: 
Pickled asparagus, great for bloody marys! Spicy and refreshing, really enhances the bloody mary!
