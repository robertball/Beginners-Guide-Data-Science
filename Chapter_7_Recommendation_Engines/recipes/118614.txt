Title:
Crunchy Cheddar Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978679.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup cornflake crumbs
1/4 cup shredded white Cheddar cheese
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cracked black pepper
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine crumbs, cheese, salt and pepper in pie plate. Roll chicken breasts in Ranch to coat both sides. Dip top side only in crumb mixture; place on baking sheet. Bake for 35 minutes, or until golden brown and crunchy.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Cornflake crumbs add a crispy crunch to this Ranch-flavored chicken dish.
