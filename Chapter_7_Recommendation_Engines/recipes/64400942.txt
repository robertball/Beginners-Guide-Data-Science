Title:
Vegetable Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Hannafords Multi-grain Pizza Dough
Pizza Sauce
Mushrooms
Onions
Peppers

Directions:
Roll out the dough. Place a layer of sauce. Add some cheese. Put the sliced mushrooms, onions and peppers around the pizza. Cover with cheese. Bake for 15 minutes in a preheated oven.
