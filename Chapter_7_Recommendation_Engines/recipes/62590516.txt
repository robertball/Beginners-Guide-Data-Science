Title:
Tasting is Believing Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
8oz creamy French dressing
1 package dry onion soup mix
1 can whole cranberry sauce

Directions:
Brown chicken in skillet with salt and pepper.
Place in baking dish with sauce.
Bake 1 hour at 325.
Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description:
