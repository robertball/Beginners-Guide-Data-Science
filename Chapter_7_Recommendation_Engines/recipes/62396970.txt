Title:
Sausage, Squash, and Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*1/3 cup bottled Italian salad dressing
* 2 teaspoons minced garlic
* 12 oz. cooked smoked sausage, sliced
* 2 small yellow summer squash thickly sliced
* 2 small zucchini thickly sliced
* 1/4 cup sliced green onions
* 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. In small bowl combine dressing and garlic. In extra-large skillet cook sausage and squash in 4 tablespoons of the dressing mixture for 8 to 10 minutes or until sausage is heated through and squash is almost tender, stirring occasionally
Drizzle remaining dressing; sprinkle green onions and Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Dawn Foster
Description: 
This quick and easy recipe is ready in 30 minutes or less. Serve with garlic bread.
