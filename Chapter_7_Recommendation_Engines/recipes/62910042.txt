Title:
Marty's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For sauce:
1 2-1/2 sized can (28 oz) of tomatoes
1 small (8 oz) can of tomato sauce
1 small can of tomato paste
1 t. sugar
1 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1/4 t oregano
1 c. minced onions
2 cloves minced garlic
1 lb ground chuck
1/4 c. olive oil
The rest of the ingredients:
1/2 lb. lasagna noodles
16 oz ricotta cheese
1/3 c grated parmesan cheese
1 lb of sliced mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Combine the first 7 ingredients.
Heat oil in a skillet and cook onion and garlic until they begin to color.
Add meat and mash with a fork.
Brown slightly.
Add to tomato mixture and simmer for 2 1/2 hours (You can do this ahead and chill it.)
Cook the lasagna noodles following package instructions, adding 2 T of oil to prevent sticking,
Preheat over to 350 degrees.
Into an 8 x 12 x 2 pan pour a thin layer of the sauce.
Over it arrange a layer of noodles, a layer of ricotta, a layer of sliced mozzarella, sprinkle with parmesan cheese and cover with sauce.  Repeat until all ingredients have been used. (cross noodles the other way in the 2nd layer) Top with mozzerella chees slices.
Bake at 350 deg. for 1 hour.
