Title:
Sweet Butternut Squash Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 butternut squash
1 cup white sugar
1 1/2 cups milk
1 tsp vanilla
pinch of salt
2 tbs all-purpose flour
3 eggs
1/4 margarine
16 oz vanilla wafers, crushed
1/2 cup margarine, melted
1 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Put butternut squash in the microwave and cook on high, until soft, 2-3 minutes. Cut in half, scoop out seeds and cube. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add squash and cook until tender, about 15 minutes. Drain and mash. In a 9x13 casserole dish combine 3 cups mashed squash, white sugar, milk, vanilla, salt, flour, eggs ans 1/4 cup melted margarine. Bake in preheated 425 oven for 45 minutes or until set. In medium bowl combine crushed vanilla wafers, 1/2 cup melted margarine and brown sugar. Crumble over top off cooked casserole and return to oven to brown.
