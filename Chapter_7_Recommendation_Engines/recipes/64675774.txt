Title:
Delicious Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
2 cups sugar
3 eggs, beaten
3 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 tablespoon lemon juice
3 cups sliced apples (Jonathan or McIntosh)
1 cup nuts
Icing:
1 cup powdered sugar
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat to 325, bake for 1 hour and 20 minutes
9x13 pan, greased
Mix oil and sugar, add eggs
Mix flour, soda, salt and cinnamon thoroughly
Blend in with wet ingredients
Add vanilla and lemon
Add apples and nuts
Icing:
Pour over warm cake
