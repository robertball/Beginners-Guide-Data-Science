Title:
Suddenly Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10-12 large mushrooms
1/4 cup ricotta cheese
1/2 cup fresh spinach
1 oz. Parmesan cheese
1 clove garlic
1/4 cup onion
splash of chicken or vegetable broth
1 cup flaked imitation crabmeat (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Pop the stems off the mushrooms, making an area for the stuffing.
Load all the ingredients, except the mushrooms, in the Short Cup and twist on the Cross Blade.
Pulse until the consistency is textured, but not smooth.
Spoon the mixture into the mushroom tops and bake on a lightly greased baking dish for 15-20 minutes.
