Title:
Hatch Chili Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28oz can diced tomatoes
1 14.5oz. can diced roasted garlic tomatoes
5 roasted green hatch chilies
1/2 medium onion
1/4 cup cilantro
garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a food processor or blender.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
You'll think you're in Mexico when you eat this easy, quick, no cook salsa.  Great on tortilla chips!
