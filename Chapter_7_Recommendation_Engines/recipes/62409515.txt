Title:
Herb-a-licious Tomato White Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/436579.jpg
Ingredients:
1 TBS Olive Oil
1 clove of Garlic (grated or finely minced)
1 tsp fresh Oregano
1 tsp fresh Thyme
1 tsp fresh Rosemary
1 â€“ 15 oz can of Great Northern White Beans
1 TBS Tomato Paste
1/3 cup Chicken Broth
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, heat 1 TBS of Olive Oil over medium heat.
Grate garlic into oil.
Chop herbs and add to oil and cook 2 minutes.
Stir in 1 can of drained and rinsed White Beans and 1 TBS of Tomato Paste.
Cook an additional 1 minute and then add 1/3 cup Chicken Broth.
Turn heat to medium-low and simmer for about 5 minutes- until sauce thickens.
Season with Salt and Pepper to taste.
