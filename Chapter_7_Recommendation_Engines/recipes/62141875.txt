Title:
Comforting Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1 oz. pouch of dry onion soup mix
1 14 oz. can chicken broth
1 10 oz. can of cream of mushroom soup
1 10 oz. can of cream of celery soup
3/4 c.  white rice
4  boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Sprinkle onion soup on the bottom of a 9x13 dish.
Sprinkle rice on top of soup.
Mix cream soups and chicken broth in a large bowl.  Set aside.
Place chicken breasts over rice.
Pour soup/broth mixture over chicken and rice.
Cover with foil.
Bake at 350 degrees for about an hour or until chicken juices run clear.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
This is a delicious casserole that's super-easy to throw together in a pinch.  The best part is that it uses most things you probably already have in the pantry.
