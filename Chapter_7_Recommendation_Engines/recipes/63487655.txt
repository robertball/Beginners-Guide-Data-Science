Title:
Chinese Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head Savoy** or Napa Cabbage, cored and shredded
1 bunch green onions, finely sliced (both white and green tops)
1 oz (about 1/4 cup or 1/2 a jar) sesame seeds
1 (6oz) package slivered or sliced almonds
1/2 cup vegatable oil (carola)
1/4 cup grnaulated sugar
3 tbs whte vinegar
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
options: add 1 (3oz) pkg ramen noodles crumbled and toasted
add shredded chicken
add shredded carrots, daikon radish or bell pepper

Directions:
toast the sesame seeds in a dry skillet over medium heat, stirring constantly for about 5 mins (caution they can quickly burn).  remove the sesame seeds to a large bowl.  repeat as above to toast the almonds.  add the cabbage and onions to the bow.  place the sugar, vinegar, s/p in a medium bowl and slowly whisk in the oil.  pour the dressing over the cabbage mixture and toss to combine **just before serving
