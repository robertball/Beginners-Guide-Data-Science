Title:
Alpen Magronen, My Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - Baking potatoes, peeled, 1/2-inch dices
10-12 oz. dry pasta such as elbow or rotini
1 medium onion, sliced thin
4 oz each of:
Emmantellar
Gruyer
Jarlsburg
Shredded
1 Cup of cream
1 1/2 Teas. Dry Mustard
1 1/2 Teas. Smoked Paprika
1 Tables & 2 Teas. kosher salt
1 Teas. White Pepper
3 Tabls. Butter
1 Tables. Olive Oil

Directions:
Bring to boil a 2 qt. sauce pan of water
Cook the diced potato until just tender, about 5 minutes, drain, cool & set aside.
Bring the pot back to boil and cook the pasta per package instructions,drain, cool and set aside.
+++
In a saute pan, melt the butter with olive oil, and carmelize the onions until soft & brown
+++
In a large bowl combine the potato, pasta, cheeses, pepper, salt, dry mustard, and smoked paprika
+++
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray or butter a 2.5 quart casserole
Take the contents of the bowl and place in casserole, pour in the heavy cream, and spread the carmelized onions over the top
+++
Place the casserole into the overn and bake for 60-75 minutes, uncovered
Remove from oven and set aside to set for 5-6 minutes.

Description: 
My take on a traditional Swiss Macaroni & Cheese Recipe. A household favorite of ours. Alpine comfort food!
Meatless for Lenten or vegetarian.
