Title:
Amazing Almond Flour Meatloaf (Paleo and Gluten Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatloaf:
2 tbsp olive oil
2 onion, chopped and sauteed
2 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
2 tsp italian seasoning
3 pounds ground chuck
2 egg
1/2 cup of coconut milk (or half/half for primal)
1 cup almond flour
Topping:
3 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoons yellow mustard
1/3 cup ketchup heinz 100% natural
Check All Add to Shopping List

Directions:
Directions
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F.
Heat the olive oil in a medium saute pan. Add the onions, italian seasoning, salt, and pepper and cook over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, for 8 to 10 minutes, until the onions are translucent.
In a large bowl, combine the ground chuck, onion mixture, almond flour, and eggs. Shape the mixture into a a 1" thick rectangular loaf on a sheet pan covered with aluminum foil. Spread the topping evenly on top. Bake for 1 to 1 1/4 hours, until the internal temperature is 160 degrees F and the meat loaf is cooked through.  Serve hot.
