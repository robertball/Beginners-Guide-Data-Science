Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2442506.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Fresh Goose Down
1/2 Teaspoon Cinnamon
2 pinches of fireplace ash (Oak wood ash preferred)
10 mini marshmallows, must be purple
4 kale leaves
1 gallon buttermilk

Directions:
Take 1 lb of goose down and stuff 3 pillows. Don pajamas. Have massive pillow fight, covering the kitchen with feathers.
Preheat oven to 500Â°F
Add all dry ingredients into a bowl and use a potato masher to mush it all up. Add buttermilk. Pour into a floured/greased baking dish. Bake for 4 hours. Remove from oven and let cool for 30 seconds. Add ice cream and enjoy!
