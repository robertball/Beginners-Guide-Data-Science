Title:
Homemade Butterfingers By Daydream Kitchen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
16 oz candy corn
16 oz peanut butter
16 oz chocolate
1 oz butter

Directions:
Directions:
In a pot, over hot water (double pot), melt candy corn and peanut butter while stirring constantly.
Once the mix is ready, pour into a baking pan and let it set.
Cut to desire length.
In a pot, over hot water (double pot), melt chocolate and butter while stirring constantly.
Dip the bars in the chocolate and let it cool.
