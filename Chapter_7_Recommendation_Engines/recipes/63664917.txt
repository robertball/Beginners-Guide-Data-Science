Title:
Bread Machine Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  cup  minus 1 T warm water (110 degrees F)
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon warm milk
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic salt
1 1/2 tablespoons dried parsley
2 teaspoons garlic powder
3 cups bread flour
2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select 1.5lb loaf, Light crust, Basic Bread cycle; press Start.

Submitter: 
Dawnby
Description: 
Garlic, along with a little parsley, is baked right into this delicious bread. Slather it with butter or enjoy it plain.Modified from: Bread Machine Garlic Bread
