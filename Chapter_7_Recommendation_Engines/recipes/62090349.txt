Title:
Chicken in Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large skinless, boneless chicken breasts
4 large slices of swiss cheese
10.75 oz can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup (or more if desired) dry or semi-dry white wine
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup herb flavored suffing mix, crushed
4 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place chicken breasts in shallow buttered casserole dish. Layer cheese on top of each breast. In a seperate bowl, mix soup, wine, and salt & pepper. Pour mixture over cheese. Sprinkle stuffing on top and drizzle with melted butter. Bake for 45 minutes (or until chicken breasts are done).

Submitter: 
lara
Description: 
Easy and impressive chicken dinner in a snap! Can even freeze and bake at a later date.
