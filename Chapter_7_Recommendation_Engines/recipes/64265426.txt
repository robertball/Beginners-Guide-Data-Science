Title:
Spinach and Pomegrante Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 300 g frozen spinach thawed
1 Pomegranate
3 cups Sunrype Frullo Tropical Twist juice (or any unsweetened fruit juice)
1 large ruby red grapefruit
3 scoops Vega Choc-A-Lot Protein smoothie mix (or what ever protein source you want)
1 cup water (added to Pomegranate seeds to make juice
6 sugar twin type sweeteners or to taste

Directions:
Seed the Pomegranate and peel the grapefruit (as you would an orange)
Then run the Pomegranate seeds in a small blender with the 1 cup water, until smooth then run the mash through a sieve into a bowl keeping the juice discarding the pulp and seeds the return juice to a larger 12-cup blender, along with the Sunrype juice and put on blender
Start blending then add the spinach and then the  grapefruit by sections one at a time
Then add the Vega then blend until smooth
use the sweeteners to sweeten it to taste

Submitter: 
Tony Carriere
Description: 
A delicious smoothie made with the goodness of spinach pomegranate and grapefruit with added protein packed with antioxidants from the pomegranate and spinach and grapefruit
