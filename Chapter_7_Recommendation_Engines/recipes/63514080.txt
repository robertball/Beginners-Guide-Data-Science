Title:
Irish Soda Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups flour
3 tablespoons sugar
4 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup raisins
1 tablespoon caraway seeds
2 cups buttermilk
(2 tablespoons of butter and some sugar to go on top of the bread after baking)

Directions:
Grease and flour baking sheet.
Mix flour, sugar, caraway seeds, baking soda and baking powder. Stir in raisins, buttermilk and form into a soft dough. Knead the dough for four minutes. Separate dough into 3 even balls and 1 small stem. Shape into a shamrock and Bake at 350 for 45-50 minutes. When done melt extra butter and baste on top of bread then sprinkle sugar on top.
