Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2471499.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Dry Oatmeal
1/2 Cup Miniature Chocolate Chips
3/4 Cup Peanut Butter
2 TBS Ground Flax or Chia Seeds
1/3 Cup Honey
1 TBS Vanilla

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in large bowl until completely mixed. Form into individual bites. Place in sealed container and chill until ready to eat.

Submitter: 
Kate Crockett
Description: 
Dinomite's energy is waning as the GISHWHES Hunt is winding down...it's been a LONG week and he needs some energy. So DINOMITE'S FLUFFY BITES to the rescue rife with natural sources of energy! ENJOY GISHWHESers.
