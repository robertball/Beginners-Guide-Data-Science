Title:
Bubba's Rice Side Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1043190.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups long grain white rice
4 tbs Olive Oil
2 cups chicken broth
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
2 package of fresh baby portabella mushrooms (sliced)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Put the rice in a medium casserole dish. Pour in the beef broth and French onion soup. Stir until well mixed.
Add sliced mushrooms to the bowl. Add the olive oil without stirring.
Cover the casserole dish. Bake for 1 hour. Remove the bowl from the oven and stir. Let stand for a few minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
BigPapaBear
Description: 
This is an easy rice recipe that anyone can make. There is no measuring and no mixing.  Simply throw all the ingredients together, cook, and enjoy.Modified from: Rice-so-Nice
