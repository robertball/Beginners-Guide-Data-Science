Title:
Brazilian Arroz de Festa (Confeti Rice) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 2 cups basmati or other long grain white rice
* 1/4 cup minced onion
* 3 large cloves garlic, minced
* 2 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 4 cups hot water
* 2 Carrots, peeled and chopped
* 1 Green Bell pepper, chopped
* 1 small red Bell Pepper, chopped
* 1/4 Cup golden raisins
* 1/2 Cup raisins
* 1/2 Cup dried cranberries
* 1 (1.25 oz) whole kernel corn, drained
* 2 tbs. butter, optional

Directions:
DIRECTIONS
1. Place the rice in a colander and rinse thoroughly with cold water; set aside.
2. Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook the onion in the oil for one minute. Stir in the garlic and cook until the garlic is golden brown. Add the rice and salt and cook and stir until the rice begins to brown. Pour hot water over rice mixture and stir. Reduce heat to low, cover the saucepan, and allow to simmer until the water has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
3. While rice is cooking, place the chopped carrots in saucepan with enough water to cover and cook over medium-high heat, after about 5 minutes add the chopped bell peppers. Cook until just tender. Drain and set aside.
4. Microwave the corn for about 2 minutes with 1 tbs. of butter.
5. Soak the raisins and dried cranberries in a small bowl with hot water for about 10 minutes, after they puff up, drain and set aside.
6. Once the rice is done, remove from heat and allow it to sit for about 5 minutes, Transfer to a large pot and add the cooked carrots, bell peppers, corn, raisins and cranberries, toss to distribute and incorporate and add remaining butter if you wish. Reheat before serving.

Submitter: 
Nandabear
Description: 
This is a traditional dish in my family at Christmas and other special occasions. It's nothing more than just dressing up our very simple but tasty Brazilian White Rice and giving it a savory and sweet spin. Enjoy!
