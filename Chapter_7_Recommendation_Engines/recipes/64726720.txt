Title:
Instant Pot® Beef Olé Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6125310.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 pound lean ground beef
1 medium onion, diced
1 1.25 oz. package of taco seasoning
1 6 oz. can of tomato paste
1 1/2 cups water
4 bell peppers (color of choice), tops removed and seeded
Pico de gallo for garnish

Directions:
Add one tablespoons of olive oil to inner liner and set pressure cooker according to manufacturer's instruction to saute. When oil becomes hot add ground beef and onions. Cook until ground beef is browned and no longer pink. Remove from inner liner and drain.
Continuing on the saute mode, return ground beef and onions to the inner liner, stir in taco seasoning, tomato paste and water. Bring mixture to a simmer and cook until mixture thickens. Remove mixture from inner liner and stuff each pepper.
Place 1 cup of water and trivet in the inner liner with the stuffed bell peppers placed on top of the trivet. Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 8 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build. When time has elapsed release the pressure carefully using the quick-release method according to manufacturer's instructions. Unlock and remove the lid.
Serve with you favorite toppings such as pico de gallo.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Since today's electric pressure cookers are so versatile you can also make versatile recipes in them such as these stuffed bell peppers. Top your bell peppers with your favorite garnish such as pico de gallo. The stuffing can also be used for taco salads as well as tacos or burritos.
