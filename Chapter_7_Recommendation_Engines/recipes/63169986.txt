Title:
My Mom's Sicilian Stromboli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 recipe pizza dough or you can use store bought.
3 spanish onions sliced
2 large red peppers sliced
3 cups sliced mushrooms
1 bunch fresh spinach
1 cup black olives
1 cup tomato sauce
salt and pepper.
2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp dried basil
1 egg beaten
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425F
saute sliced onions in a little olive oil, on medium heat til caremelized about 20 min. Remove from pan to cool.
Add more oilve oil to pan and saute red peppers and mushrooms til tender about 10 min .Remove to cool.
Quickly saute spinach til wilted about 3 minutes.  Remove to cool,
Roll out dough  into two pieces onto floured worksurface to about 1/4 inch thickness.
Brush olive oil on dough,
Scatter onions,mushrooms, peppers, and olivesover dough.
Drop tomatoe sauce  by tablespoons over dough.
Season with herbs and salt and pepper.
Roll dough from long ends to seal. Tuck ends under to prevent leaking.
Transfer to baking sheets sprayed with cooking spray.
Mix beaten egg with milk and brush tops of stromboli with egg wash. This will give the strombolis a nice golden crust.
Bake in preheated oven for about 30 min or until tops are golden and botoms are golden brown.
Cool on baking sheets 15 min. Slice into pieces and serve.

Submitter: 
BramptonMommyof2
Description: 
This  is a comfort food my mom used to make. You can vary the vegetables and add ground beef or diced cooked chicken. This one has carmelied onions, sauted mushroom and red peppers and spinach and black olives.
