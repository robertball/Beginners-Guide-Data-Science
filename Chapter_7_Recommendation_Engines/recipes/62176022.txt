Title:
Chocolate chip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C shorening
3/4C sugar(half brown sugar& half white)
1 egg
1tsp vanilla
1 1/8C sifted flour
1/4tsp soda
1/2tsp salt
6oz of chocolate chips
1/2C nuts

Directions:
Cream together the shortening and sugars, then add the egg and vanilla. Sift together the dry ingrdients then add to the creamed mixture. Add the chips and nuts. Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls about 2in apart on lightly greased baking sheet. Bake at 375 for 8-10min until lightly browned. Should be soft. Cool slightly.
