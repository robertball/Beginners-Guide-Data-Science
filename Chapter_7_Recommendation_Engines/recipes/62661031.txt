Title:
Lemon Leek Roasted Cornish Hens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Rock Cornish hens
2 lemons, halved
2 sliced leeks, white part only
2 tablespoons poultry seasoning
olive oil
garlic powder
lemon rub from left over squeezed lemons mentioned above

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Place hens in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Stuff each hen with 1 lemon half and 1/4 of the sliced leeks, then sprinkle with seasoning. Rub the outside with a little olive oil, lemon, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 1 hour, or until hens are cooked through and juices run clear. Let stand 10 minutes, then remove lemon and leeks.
