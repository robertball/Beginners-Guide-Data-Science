Title:
Spicy Cheesy Biscuit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 frozen or refrigerated biscuits
1 stick softened butter
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp dried parsley
1 tsp black pepper
2 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Make the compound butter by mixing butter, garlic powder, dried parsley, black pepper, crushed red pepper, and parmesan cheese together.
2. Place the biscuits on a sheet pan covered with parchment paper.
3. Spread about 1 tsp of the compound butter on top of each biscuit.
4. Bake according to the biscuit manufacturer's instructions. For frozen biscuits, usually it is 20 minutes at 375 F.
5. The biscuits are best served warm.

Submitter: 
Lilia
Description: 
The compound butter gives the plain old ready-to-bake biscuits a nice twist.
