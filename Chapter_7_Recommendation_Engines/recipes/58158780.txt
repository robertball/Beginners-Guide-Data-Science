Title:
BBQ Dry Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups white sugar
1 1/4 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup salt
1/4 cup freshly ground black pepper
1/4 cup paprika
2 tbsp garlic powder
2 tbsp onion powder
1 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together white and brown sugars, salt, pepper, and paprika. Rub onto pork 10 minutes prior to grilling. Store any leftover rub in a sealed container.

Submitter: 
Mike
Description: 
My family has been making this dry rub for years. It is awesome on country-style ribs and pork steaks!Modified from: BBQ Dry Rub
