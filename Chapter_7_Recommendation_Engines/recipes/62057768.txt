Title:
Slow cooker spicey meatball sandwichs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp each dry basil, marjoram, oregano and thyme.
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
3 cloves garlic
1/4 cup finely ground onion
1 lb ground beef
1/2 lb ground veal or turkey
1/2 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
1/3 cup parmesan cheese
1 large can of spaghetti sauce
1/4 cup of dry red wine
6-8 sandwich rolls
optional ingredients are roasted strips of pepper, sliced red onion, sliced olives, provolone or mozzarella cheese.

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat egg, salt, herbs, pepper and garlic.  Add onion, beef, veal or turkey and parmesan cheese. Mix well. Shape into 2" balls. Place in slow cooker. In same bowl mix sauce and wine, pour over meatballs.  Cover and cook for 5.5-6 hours (until no longer pink).  Skim fat if necessary.  Toast rolls if desired, fill with meatballs sauce and optional ingredients.

Submitter: 
DEBMCE4
Description: 
It's the addition of ground turkey or veal that makes these meatballs so tender.  Spice them up even more with extra cayenne pepper.
