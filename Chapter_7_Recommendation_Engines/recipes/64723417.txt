Title:
Amy’s Mr. Johnson Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6020456.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 ounce package of softened cream cheese
1 egg yolk
1/2 Cup of Sugar
1 loaf of plain white sandwich bread
Topping:
1 stick of melted butter
1 Cup of Sugar
1 T of cinnamon

Directions:
In a mixer combine cream cheese, egg yolk, sugar. Mix well.
Cut the crusts of the bread.
Melt the butter.
Mix the Cinnamon & sugar.
With a rolling pin, roll each piece of bread until very thin.
Spread cream cheese mixture on each slice of bread.
Roll each from corner to corner.
Roll in melted butter.
Roll in cinnamon & sugar mixture.
Place on a cookie sheet lined with a silpat.
Bake at 350* for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Amy Standaert
Description: 
I’m not a baker, therefore these are perfect for me to make when I have time. I used to make these for PTA teacher appreciation parties. One of the teacher’s, Mr. Johnson, especially loved these, hence the name they’ve become known as.
