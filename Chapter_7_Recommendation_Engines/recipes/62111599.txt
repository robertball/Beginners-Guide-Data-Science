Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
.5 Cup Sugar
.5 Cup Brwn Sugar
.75 Cup Flour
.25 Cup Butter
4 Cup Sliced Apples
.25 Cup Water
1 Teaspoon Cinnamon

Directions:
Combine sugars, flour, and butter together until crumbly.  Place apples into greased, shallow pan.  Pour water over apples and generously sprinkle with cinnamon.  Spread crumb mixture over apples and bake uncovered 50 minutes at 350.
