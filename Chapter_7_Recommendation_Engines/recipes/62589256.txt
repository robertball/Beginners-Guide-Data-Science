Title:
Unbeatable Sinfully Delicious Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/553900.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Duncan Hines Lemon Delux Cake Mix
3 Eggs
1/3 cup Vegtable oil
1/2 cup Sugar
1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/4 tsp. salt
2 tsp. grated lemon peel
1/4 cup Fresh Lemon Juice ( about two large lemons)
Powdered Sugar

Directions:
Combine 1 egg, oil and cake mix until crumbly
Set aside 1 cup for topping
Pat remaining mixture into an ungreased 9x13 pan
Bake at 350 degrees for 12-15 minuets
While that's baking combine 2 eggs, sugar and the rest of the ingredients and beat with electric mixer until light and fluffy
Add the liquid mixture on top of the baked mixture then sprinkle the top with the 1 cup of dry mixture you set aside
Bake for about 12- 15 minuets at 350 degrees but keep your eye on it you do not want to over bake!
Sprinkle powdered sugar over the top and enjoy!
