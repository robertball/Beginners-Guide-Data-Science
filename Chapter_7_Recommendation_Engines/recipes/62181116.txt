Title:
Scottish Shortbread II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/3 cup white sugar
3/4 cup butter
1 Tablespoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
Blend all ingredients well.  Dough will be stiff.
Press into a 9 x 9 inch buttered dish.  Prick top with a fork.
Bake until pale golden brown on the edges (about 20 minutes but watch closely). Cool and cut into squares.

Submitter: 
JulieT
Description: 
Light, very buttery.  Does not make a large batch, but are simple enough to make many batches.  Recipe does not double well.Modified from: Scottish Shortbread II
