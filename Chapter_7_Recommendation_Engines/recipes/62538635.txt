Title:
Grandma's Brussel Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds brussel sprouts ( cut in half )
1/4 cup sweet butter
1/4 cup olive oil
12 large cloves of garlic, minced
1 can chicken broth (1 1/2 cups)
salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat butter and oil in a large sauce pan. Add garlic and cook over low heat for about 2 minutes. Add broth, brussel sprouts, salt and pepper. Cover and simmer for 8-10 minutes or until sprouts are tender. Top with grated cheese and turn the heat off and cover until cheese has melted.
Enjoy!

This is the brussel sprouts recipe my grandmas makes at every Thanksgiving. They are delish and cheesy, definitely a must make!
