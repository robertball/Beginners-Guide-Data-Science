Title:
Spiced Pumpkin Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Oat Bran 1 cup
Pumpkin puree 1/2 cup
Coconut milk 1/3 cup
Sugar 1/3 cup (add more if you like sweeter)
Ginger powder 1 teaspoon
Cinnamon powder 1/2 teaspoon
Pinch of salt
Fruits or nuts to garnish.

Directions:
Boil 2 cups water in the sauce pan.
Add oats bran and salt.
Keep stirring and cook till oat bran is cooked.
Add the pumpkin puree ,ginger and cinnamon powder and cook for 2 more mins.
Add sugar and coconut milk and check for desired consistency.
Stop cooking when little thinner than desired consistency and let it cool down for sometime.
Serve warm garnished with nuts or berries or just like that!
