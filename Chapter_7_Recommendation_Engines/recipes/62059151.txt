Title:
Citru Sun Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 celestial Seasonings Red Zinger tea bags
4 cups water
2 1/2 cups orange juice
1 lime
1 lemon
1 orange, sliced
1/4 cup homemade syrup
homemade syrup:
1 1/3 cups sugar
1 1/4 cups water

Directions:
Fill ice cube tray with orange juice and freeze for about 4 hours.
In a pitcher, add tea bags and water. Place outside in the sun and let stand about 4 hours.
Remove tea bags a chill tea in fridge about 30 min.
Cut half the lemon and lime into slice and add to tea. Squeeze remaining halves into tea. Stir in homemade syrup. Serve over orange juice ice cubes.
Homemade Syrup:
Bring sugar and water to a boil in saucepan until sugar is dissolved. let cool, then chill, covered.
makes about 2 cups

Description: 
Refreshing iced tea with a kick of citrus. Great for a hot summer's day.
