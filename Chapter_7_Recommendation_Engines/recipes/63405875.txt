Title:
Seasoned Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/917370.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 egg white
1 tsp Parsley (dried)
1 tsp Thyme (dried)
1 tsp Chili powder
3 Vienna Sausages (diced)
1/2 teaspoon Sazon
1/2 teaspoon Adobo
1 small garlic clove (peeled&minced;)
1 tsp Garlic Powder (optional)
2 tbsp Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1/4 cup of milk (optional)

Directions:
In a bowl, beat the eggs with a fork until the yolk and white are combined.
Add all herbs,spices & seasoning into the bowl. Next add the diced sausages & mozzarella to the bowl and mix all of the ingredients together.
Place a skillet over medium heat & pour in enough olive oil to lightly coat the entire surface.
Pour the mixture from the bowl into the skillet ; cook and stir the egg mixture until firmed and no longer runny .(about 5 minutes)
