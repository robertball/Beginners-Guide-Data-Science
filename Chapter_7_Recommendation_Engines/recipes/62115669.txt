Title:
potato leek and bacon soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 larg leeks
5 medium red potatos
5 slices smoked bakon choped
1.5 liters chicken stock (more or less for concistancy)
3 tblsp butter
2 pinch of salt
1.3 tsp corse grond black pepper
1.5 tsp fresh grond coriander to taste
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
in a large pot. cook the bakon to render the fat. add butter and the choped leeks and cook sturing acasionaly untill very soft 20 minutes or so.
dice the potatos small with skin on and add to pot with the chicken stock just over the solids.
bring to boil.  cook till potatos are soft.
add pinch of salt pepper and coriander.
add heavy cream and mix well with a potato masher or an electric mixer.
if its to thick add more chiken stock.  serve and enjoy
