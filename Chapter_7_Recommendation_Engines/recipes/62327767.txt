Title:
Marinated Chicken Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 tablespoons sesame seeds
2 teaspoons minced garlic
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
1 (8 ounce) package fresh chopped mushrooms
2 onions, quartered
1 red bell pepper, cut into large chunks
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks
MARINATE OVERNIGHT

Directions:
In a medium bowl, blend vegetable oil, soy sauce, light corn syrup, honey, lemon juice, sesame seeds, and garlic.
Place chicken  and marinade in large resealable plastic bag, seal, and marinate in the refrigerator overnight.
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat, and lightly oil grate.
Thread chicken onto skewers; thread mushroom, onion, bell pepper and pineapple onto seperate skewers.
Pour marinade into a saucepan, and bring to a boil.
Cook for 5 to 10 minutes.
Place skewers on the prepared grill.
Cook chicken 15 to 20 minutes, turning frequently, until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.
Baste with the boiled marinade frequently during the last 10 minutes.
Cook vegetable skewers 5-7 minutes or until tender crisp.
