Title:
Hunters Schnitzel  " Jaeger Schnitzel" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 oz of Thin sliced Pork Sirloin.
Oil for frying/Flour for Flouring.
1/2 Sliced Onion
4 oz of Mushrooms.  Halved and Diced
2 tablespoons of Flour
3 Tblsp.  Butter
1 tsp. Sweet Mustard
8 oz of Cream or Sour-Cream
salt & Pepper
Chopped Parsley.
Ground;  Coriander and Rye

Directions:
Tenderize; Salt and Pepper the Meat Slices.  Flour and Fry in Hot Oil until done. (Keep Warm).
Fry;  till Brown the Onion and "Champignons"; in Butter. stir in the Mustard.  Sprinkle over the Flour. The Herbs;   Stir.  Add the Sour Cream; Salt & Pepper.  stir and Simmer.  Stir Until Thickened.  (  * Place the meat in a medium"platrusse" and Cover with all the Sauce and Bake in a Hot oven 7 min.) * Serve with the Baking Dish.
Keeping the Tender Slices Warm, Center them on a serving Plate and Ladle over them a Portion of the Sauce.  Serve the rest of the Sauce in a "Sauciere"  Garnish: with Parsley.  Serve:  with;  Fried Potatoes or Bread dumpling or  Macaroni Noodles.   along;  Buttered  Brown Bread.

Submitter: 
Alois
Description: 
Fried Schnitzel in Sour-cream and Mushroom Sauce.
