Title:
Toffee Crunch Apple Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 Granny Smith Apples
pineapple juice
2 8oz blocks cream cheese
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 cups brown sugar (not packed)
2 tsp. of vanilla
1 bag of Heath "Bits of Brickle"

Directions:
Core and slice apples and soak in pineapple juice until ready to serve.
In a bowl, mix together cream cheese, sugars and vanilla until smooth and creamy.
Stir in 1 bag of Heath Bits of Brickle
Serve with apples

Submitter: 
CSUSANNE
Description: 
This dip has become a staple at my parties b/c everyone request it.  Cream cheese, sugar, vanilla and Heath bits of brickle make you think you're biting right into a candy apple.
