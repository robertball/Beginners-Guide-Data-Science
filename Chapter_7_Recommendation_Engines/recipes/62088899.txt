Title:
Spaghetti Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One lb. thin spaghetti--break in half, cook and drain
one diced cucumber
one small diced onion
3 tomatoes, diced. (add this the day you serve to prevent soggy tomatoes)
one green bell pepper, diced
one large bottle WIshbone Italian dressing
Salad supreme seasoning (bottle)
Mix all ingredients the day before to marinade the flavors. Add tomatoes, baby shrimp, and/or flaked tuna, if desired, to make a cold spaghetti salad.

Directions:
Cook spaghetti and drain thoroughly and cool.  Add diced ingredients and toss. Add Wishbone Italian Dressing and Salad Supreme , marinade overnight and chill in a bowl. Add diced tomatoes before serving. Toss in a few baby shrimp and flaked tuna if desired. Serve cold.
