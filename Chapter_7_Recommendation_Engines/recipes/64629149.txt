Title:
Chicken and Sundried Tomatos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4473581.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz, Boneless Skinless Thin Cut Chicken Breast
1 cup (diced), fresh Yellow Squash
1 Cup (diced), fresh Zucchini
1 t minced garlic
3 tbsp(s), Butter - Unsalted
1 bag/box of fresh spinach
1/2 Jar, Tomatoes, sun-dried with oil
2 fl oz, Wine - Table, white
Whole Wheat Bowtie Pasta

Directions:
If you have whole boneless breast slice into cutlets. Season and cook with olive oil. While the chicken is cooking dice 1 of each yellow squash and 1 small zucchini up. Then remove chicken from the pan after it's finished cooking then pour white wine into the hot pan to deglaze. After the pan has come back up in temp add the diced vegetables and garlic. While that is cooking cut up the sundried tomatoes with oil and add to the pan, then add the spinach and butter. Cook noodles of choice, when the noodles are finished drain and add olive oil and some garlic salt.
Plate with noodles on the bottom, chicken then veggies, there will be some liquid pour over the top.
