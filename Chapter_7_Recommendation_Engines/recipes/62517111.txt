Title:
Lorna's Spinich Salad w/Strawberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh spinich, washed, dried and torn into pcs.
1 pint fresh strawberries, sliced
1/2 Cup slivered almonds
Dressing:
2 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp sesame seeds
1 1/2 tsp chopped onions
1/8 tsp worcestershire sauce
1/8tsp paprika
1/4Cup oil
3 Tbsp Wine vinegar
2 Tbsp water

Directions:
1.  Mix Salad ingredients in large bowl
2.  Mix Dressing ingredients in small bowl
3.  Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat
