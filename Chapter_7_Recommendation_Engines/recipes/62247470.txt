Title:
Aunt Fanny's Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-40 oz can quality unseasoned pork and beans
6 TB (packed) brown sugar
1/2 Cup catsup or chili sauce
1 TB "Bold and Spicy" steak sauce
1/4 Cup prepared yellow mustard
2 TB Worcestershire sauce
1 small onion, diced
1/2 lb ground breakfast sausage (not sage-seasoned)
4-6 drops of liquid smoke (optional)
6 slices real bacon (not "ready" type)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°.
Prepare a 2 qt. casserole with cooking spray.
In a medium skillet, cook crumbled sausage and onion until sausage is no longer pink and onion is clear.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine all ingredients, except bacon.  Pour into prepared casserole and top with bacon.
Bake at 350° for 1 hour or until crust begins to form around edges.

Submitter:
