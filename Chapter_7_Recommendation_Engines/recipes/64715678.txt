Title:
Queso Blanco for the instant pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5735144.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces whipping cream
8 ounces Oaxaca cheese, shredded
4 ounces Neufchâtel cheese (low fat cream cheese)
4 ounces chopped green chiles
1 Tbsp pickled jalapeño- chopped finely.
1 1/2 tsp pickled jalapeño juice

Directions:
Set instant pot on the slow cooker setting, select high.
Place all ingredients except green chiles, and jalapeño into the instant pot.
Stir until melted -about 3-5 minutes.
Switch to low on the slow cooker setting.
Allow queso to cook an additional 5-7 minutes. Pour in the jalapeño and green chiles. Mix well.
Serve with chips, or pour over nachos.

Submitter: 
thedailygourmet
Description: 
My own rendition of a smooth Queso blanco made in the instant pot. Feel free to adjust heat to suit your preferences.
