Title:
Healthy Popcorn Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup popcorn, unpopped
1/2 cup honey or corn syrup
1/2 cup organic sugar or white sugar
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/4 cup freshly ground flax seeds
6-9 sandwich bags

Directions:
Pop popcorn, remove old maids, set aside.
In a small sauce pan mix honey and sugar.  Bring to a boil and boil for 1 min.
Remove from heat and add peanut butter.  Mix well.  Now add ground flax seed.
Pour mixture over popcorn and mix well.
Turn a sandwich bag inside out and put over your hand.  Use the sandwich bag to grab a handful of popcorn.  Carefully turn the bag righ-side out around the handful of popcorn.  Use the bag to form the popcorn into a ball.
Decorate bag with yarn or ribbon.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
This tasty halloween treat has been remade to be healthy with peanut butter and flax seeds.  The kids still love them, and mom can feel good about making them.
