Title:
Scallops with Snow Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound snow peas
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup sweet pepper, diced
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1 tablespoon olive oil
3/4 pound scallops
1/2 teaspoon salt, optional

Directions:
Trim the peas by cutting off the stem end.
Place them in a large skillet that has a tightly fitting cover and add the water and cook, covered, for 2 minutes or so.
Take the cover off and cook for a minute or so more.
Add the sweet pepper, butter and oil.
Stir in the scallops and saute over very high heat for 2 to 4 minutes. Serve at once.
