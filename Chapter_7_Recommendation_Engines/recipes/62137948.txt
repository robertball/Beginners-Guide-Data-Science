Title:
Mahi Mahi y mango salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Mahi Mahi Steaks
4 tbsp Blackening Season
4 tbsp Olive Oil
1/4 Cup diced onion
3 tbsp brown sugar
1/2 Can Pineapple
1 Mango Diced
4 tbsp Red Wine
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
In large pan heat 2 tbsp olive oil and add onion, simmer for 3 mins. Add brown sugar and allow to caramelize on onions(1min). Next add mango, pineapple, and wine. Simmer for 3 to 5 mins. Empty pan into bowl.
Season Mahi Mahi with salt and pepper and lightly coat with Blackening Seasoning. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil and add Mahi Mahi. Cook on each side until cooked. (about 3-4 mins each side)
Remove Mahi Mahi from heat and top with Salsa.
