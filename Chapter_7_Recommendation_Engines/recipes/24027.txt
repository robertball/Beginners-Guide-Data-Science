Title:
Butter-Nut Chocolate Topped Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979295.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/8 teaspoon salt
30 HERSHEY®'S KISSES® Milk Chocolates
1/2 cup ground almonds

Directions:
Beat butter, sugar, egg and vanilla in medium bowl until well blended.  Stir together flour, baking soda and salt; add to butter mixture, beating well.  If necessary, refrigerate dough until firm enough to handle.
2.  Remove wrappers from chocolate pieces.  Heat oven to 350 degrees F.  Shape dough into 1-inch balls; roll in ground nuts.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until almost no imprint remains when touched lightly in center.  Remove from oven; immediately press chocolate piece into center of each cookie.  Carefully remove from cookie  sheet to wire rack.  Cool completely.  Chocolate should be set before storing.
