Title:
Brushetta With Goat cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  italian Baguette Sliced 1/4 inch thick on a angle
1  pint grape Tomatoes.Sliced
1  tsp italian seasoning
1  tsp basil minced
1  tsp Garlic powder
1  tsp parsley dried.
1/4 cup of balsamic vinegrette
1/4 cup Goat Cheese crumbled
2  Tbsp butter

Directions:
Butter a large Baking Sheet place the Cut Baguette Pieces on top. In a small Bowl Combine sliced Tomatoe, Italian seasoning, Basil, Parsley, Garlic powder. Stir . Put the tomatoe mixture on top of the bread. Top with Crumbled Goat Cheese. Sprinkle each piece with Balsamic vinegrette then place in the oven until toasty Brown about 5 min on 400 degrees
