Title:
Wienerschnitzel for Octobereformania Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. pork loin
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
2 eggs
1 teaspoon minced parsley
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 pinch ground nutmeg
2 tablespoons milk
1 cup dry bread crumbs
4 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Place each cutlet between two pieces of plastic wrap, and pound with the flat side of a meat mallet until about 1/4 inch thick.
Dip in flour to coat.
In a medium bowl, stir together the Parmesan cheese, eggs, parsley, salt, pepper, nutmeg and milk.
Place bread crumbs on a plate.
Dip each cutlet into the egg mixture, then press in the bread crumbs to coat.
Place coated cutlets on a plate in freezer for 30 minutes.
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook the breaded cutlets until browned on each side, about 3 minutes per side.
Remove to a serving platter.

This easy German recipe was a real hit!  We used pork loin sliced to about 3/4 inch thick, pounded and cut into four pieces for appetizers.Modified from: Wienerschnitzel
