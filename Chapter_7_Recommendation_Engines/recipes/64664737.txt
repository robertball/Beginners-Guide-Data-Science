Title:
Pan Fried Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4567531.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups corn meal
1/2 cup plain flour
2 teaspoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/2 cups water ( add 1 cup water to the mix and stir, add more as needed, the mix should and flatten out in your pan)
1/3 to 1/2 cup cooking oil (for frying)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together , does not have to be smooth but with as few lumps as possible. Dip out mix into your pan to form "pancakes", let set until bottom side is golden brown, then flip and brown the other side... You can make them as big or small as you like..!! Enjoy..!

Submitter: 
Craig T.
Description: 
Back home on the N.C. coast, I grew up eating this fried corn bread and it goes with so many different dishes, from seafood to soups and stews. Easy to make and comes out like pancakes but with crispy edges and golden brown goodness.... even good cold or by itself...!
