Title:
Heidelberg Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
4 Russet Potatoes, scrubbed clean
2 cups frozen sweet corn
1 onion finely chopped
1 can Red Kidney Beans, drained
3 cans Fat free Evaported Skim Milk
1-8 oz block of Velvetta Cheese, cubed

Directions:
Microwave Potates on high for about 2 minutes or until partially tender. Set aside and allow to cool; Meanwhile,in a large pot saute onions and ground beef in a pat of butter over med-low heat. Add corn and kidney beans. Then add cubed velvetta and evaporated milk. Allow mixture to cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until cheese melts. Cut potatoes into cubes and add to the pot. Season lighly with Seasoned Salt and a little black pepper. Reduce heat to med-low and allow to simmer 5 minutes. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Karen D.
Description: 
A cheeseburger soup with chunks of ground beef, potatoes, corn, and kidney beans. Perfect for a really cold winters day.
