Title:
Pasta with vegetable marinara sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box pasta
1 can pasta sauce
8 oz mushrooms
1 can black olives
1 can artichokes
1 tsp olive oil

Directions:
cook pasta according to package.
add olive oil to a large pan
add mushrooms and cook over high heat until soft
add past sauce, drained black olives and artichokes.
simmer for 10 minutes.
Serve sauce over pasta
