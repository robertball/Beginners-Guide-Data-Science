Title:
Light, Fluffy,  Eggless Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/3 C White Flour
1/3 C White Sugar
1 tsp. Vanilla
1/4 C Sour Cream (optional)
1 C Milk (or 1 C Water plus 1/4 C Powdered Milk)
3/4 tsp. Baking Powder
3/4 tsp. Baking Soda
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Ground Cinnamon
Options:  1 Banana (mashed), 1/2 C Applesauce, or 1/2 C canned Pumpkin.

Directions:
Add all dry ingredients to bowl.  All wet ingredients.  Mix well.
Pour by the 1/4 cupfuls onto a pre-heated 325 griddle that has been sprayed with a non-stick cooking spray.
Makes 12 - 16 4" pancakes.

Submitter: 
amom2boys
Description: 
I love these light, fluffy, pancakes.  The best part is that they do not require an egg, and you can use a few different types of fruit in the batter.
