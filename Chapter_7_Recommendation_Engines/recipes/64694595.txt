Title:
Big Ray's Sausage & Kraut Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5223945.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans (14-1/2 ounces each) diced tomatoes, drained
2 cans (14 ounces each) sauerkraut, rinsed and drained
1/2 pound sliced fresh mushrooms
1 large bell pepper, thinly sliced
1 large onion, halved and thinly sliced
2 Tbsp brown sugar
1/2 tsp pepper
2 lbs Smoked Sausage, sliced
12 pretzel sausage buns, warmed and split partway

Directions:
In a 5- or 6-qt. slow cooker, combine first seven ingredients. In a large skillet, saute sausage over medium-high heat until lightly browned. Stir into tomato mixture.
Cook, covered, on low until vegetables are tender, 4-5 hours. Serve in buns.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
A great dish to bring to your next football party. For a fun dinner spin, try these sausages and kraut over mashed potatoes.
