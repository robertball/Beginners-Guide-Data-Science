Title:
Back on Track Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. olive oil
2-4 cloves garlic, minced
2 bay leaves
2 15 oz. cans pinto OR kidney beans, rinsed and drained
2 lbs. potatoes, cut into 1/2" pieces
8 cups vegetable broth
1 box frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine everything except spinach, salt, and pepper in slow cooker.
Cover.  Cook on High 3 hours or on Low 5 hours.
Fifteen minutes before serving, stir in spinach.
Cover cooker and allow to stand 15 minutes.  Taste, then add salt and pepper.
If you wish, use a potato masher to partially mash and thicken the soup.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
This is a great soup for getting back to healthy eating after holiday feasting!  No punishment here!!
