Title:
Hot Crab Dip Recipe

Ingredients:
3/4 Cup nonfat sour cream
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1 tablespoon grated fresh onion
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
3/4 teaspoon dry mustard
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 (8-ounce) tub reduced-fat cream cheese, softened
2 ounces shredded cheese

Directions:
Combine the first 7 ingredients in a bowl.
Stir well with a wire whisk.
Stir in cheddar cheese and crabmeat.
Spoon the crabmeat mixture into a l l/2-quart casserole coated with cooking spray, and sprinkle with paprika.
