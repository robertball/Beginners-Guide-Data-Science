Title:
Mommie's Mushroom Gravy 2.0 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, minced
3 cloves garlic, minced
12 oz mushrooms, sliced and cut into smaller pieces
2 c vegetable broth
2 tsp miso
2 T flour
2 T soy sauce
2 T sherry opt
pepper

Directions:
In a saucepan, cook onions over medium high heat, stirring and adding a splash of water if they start to burn. Add the garlic and mushrooms and continue cooking until mushrooms soften. Add a cup of broth. To the remaining cup of broth add the remaining ingredients, add to pan and continue cooking and stirring until gravy thickens. Season with pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Delicious gravy to serve over mashed potatoes or as you please.
