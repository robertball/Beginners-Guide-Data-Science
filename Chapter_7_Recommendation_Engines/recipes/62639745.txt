Title:
Chuck Wagon Tortilla Stack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
2 to 3 cloves minced garlic
16 ounces baked beans
14 1/2 ounces stewed tomatoes with juice
1 1/2 cups frozen corn
4 ounces green chilies, minced
1/4 cup barbaque sauce
4 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
4-10" flour tortillas
1 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown turkey and garlic until meat is no longer pink.  Add beans, corn, chilies, BBQ sauce, chili powder and cumin.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 10 to 12 minutes or until liquid is reduced.  Coat a large deep skillet with oil.  Place one tortilla in skillet; spread with 1 1/2 cups meat mix.  Then sprinkle with 1/3 cup cheese.  Repeat layers 3 times, cover and cook on low 15 minutes.  Cut into wedges and serve with salsa and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Diana Ackaret
Description: 
An easy one dish Mexican casserole.  Great flavor that is enhanced with the addition of salsa and sour cream.
