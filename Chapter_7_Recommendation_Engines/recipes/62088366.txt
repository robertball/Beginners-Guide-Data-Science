Title:
No Peak Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs stew meat cubed & trimmed
2 Cans Cream of Chicken Soup
2 Cans Sliced Mushrooms, drained (Optional)
2 Envelopes Lipton Onion Soup Mix
1 C Ginger Ale

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.  Mix all ingredients together and pour into a large casserole dish (mixture will be lumpy).  Cover and bake 3 hours.  Serve over noodles or rice.

Submitter: 
LynnG
Description: 
No prebrowning of stew meat!  This makes a great gravy to serve over the noodles
