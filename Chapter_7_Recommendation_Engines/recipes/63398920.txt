Title:
Homemade Grape Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 c sifted whole wheat flour
1 t baking soda
1 c brown sugar
2 c milk
2 T vinegar
1 t salt

Directions:
Mix the flour, soda, sugar, and salt.  Add sour milk and beat until smooth.  Spread dough 1/4 inch thick on a greased cookie sheet.    Bake in a 375' oven about 15 minutes.  When cool, grind in a food chopper.  Do not grind completely or you will get flour!Let it finish drying after grinding.  Before serving, mix in anything else you would like, such as nuts or fruit.  You can bag and tie with wire in family-sized portions handy for an instant breakfast.
