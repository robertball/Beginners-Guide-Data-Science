Title:
Steak Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound sirloin steak (well-trimmed)
kosher salt to taste
black pepper to taste
2T butter

Directions:
Cut meat into bite sized pieces.
Sprinkle generously with salt and ground pepper and coat thoroughly.
Heat skillet over medium high heat and add 2T butter to the skillet.
Allow butter to melt and brown before adding meat.
Place meat in pan in single layer.
Leave meat 30-45 seconds before turning and cook 30-45 seconds longer.
Meat should be seared but not overcooked in center.
Repeat batch adding more butter as necessary.
Pour remaining browned butter over meat before serving.
