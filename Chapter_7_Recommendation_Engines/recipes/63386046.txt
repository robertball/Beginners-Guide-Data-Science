Title:
Garden Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans chicken broth - undiluted
2 (12 oz) can tomato juice or tomato sauce
1-2 cans of chicken (it comes in cans like tuna)
1 can whole kernel corn - drained
1 can french style green beans (drained)
2 small potatoes, peeled and chopped
1 medium onion, peeled and chopped
1/2 c celery chopped
1/2 c carrots chopped
1 1/2 T worcestershire sauce
1 bay leaf
3/4 t garlic salt
1/2 t pepper

Directions:
Combine all and cook in large pot for about 45 minutes to 1 hour.  Stirring occasionally.
