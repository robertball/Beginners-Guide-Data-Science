Cheddary Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe

4 cups hot IDAHOAN速 prepared mashed potatoes
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's速 Cream of Chicken Soup
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1 1/3 cups FRENCH'S速 Original or Cheddar French Fried Onions

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Heat mashed potatoes, soup, 1 cup cheese and garlic powder in saucepan over medium heat. Stir until cheese melts.
Spoon potato mixture into 2-quart baking dish. Top with 1/2 cup cheese and French Fried Onions.
Bake 5 minutes at 350 degrees F or until hot and until onions are golden.
