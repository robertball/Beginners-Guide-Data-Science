Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2474458.jpg
Ingredients:
2 T unflavored gelatin
1 1/4 c cold water, divided
1 1/2 c granulated sugar
1 c light corn syrup
1/4 t sea salt
1 t vanilla extract
Â½ T espresso powder
10 chocolate-covered coffee beans, ground
10 chocolate sandwich cookies, crushed
Nonstick spray
1/4 cup confectioners' sugar

Directions:
1. Spray a 8"x8" square pan with nonstick spray, dust liberally with sifted powdered sugar, covering all sides and bottom, set aside
2. Measure unflavored gelatin into mixing bowl of a stand mixer, gently cover with 3/4 cup cold water, and leave to bloom.
3. Combine remaining 1/2 cup cold water, sugar, corn syrup, and salt in a small saucepan, cover and place over medium high heat for 4 minutes.
4. Uncover and clip a candy thermometer to the side of the pan, continue cooking about 6-8 minutes more, until mixture reaches 235-245 degrees F, then remove from heat.
5. Using the whisk attachment, turn mixer on low and begin mixing the gelatin. Very carefully pour the hot sugar mixture slowly down the inside of the bowl mixing it into the gelatin. once all of the syrup has been added, turn the mixer to high.
6. Beat on high until very thick, about 13 minutes, stop the mixer, add vanilla extract and espresso powder, and mix in on medium speed. Stop, add the ground chocolate covered coffee beans and mix again, melting the chocolate into the batter.
7. Using a buttered spatula, spread the mixture into your prepared pan. Evenly cover marshmallows with 1/4 of the crushed chocolate sandwich cookies and refrigerate a minimum 6 hours, or overnight.
9. Using a sharp knife, cut into 1" x 1" squares and coat each piece on all sides with remaining crushed cookies.
10. These remarkable bites of fluffy goodness will keep up to 1 week in an airtight container.
