Title:
Waldorf Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb Cut up Broccoli
1 lb Cooked Bacon, crumbled
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup Pecans
1/4 Cup Chopped Onions, optional
Dressing:
2 Tablespoons white vinegar
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup Mayo

Directions:
Mix fressing and refrigerate at least one hour or overnight.
In seperate bowl, combine all the other ingredients.
Add dressing a little at a time, and stir well till all is well coated. - Do not use all dressing at this time.
Save remainer of dressing
Refridgerate again till ready to serve
Add more dressing if needed.

Submitter: 
Debbie Cash
Description: 
Wonderful Broccoli Salad, if you like a Waldorf Salad you will love this.  Easy and refreshing.
