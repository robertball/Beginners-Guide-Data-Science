Title:
Minister's Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/780070.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (21 ounce) cans blackberry pie filling (or your favorite)
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
3/4 cup butter, melted
3/4 cup quick cooking oats
1/3 cup chopped walnuts (if desired)

Directions:
Place pie filling in a 1-1/2 qt. slow cooker. Combine dry cake mix and butter (mixture will be crumbly); sprinkle half of the topping over filling. Sprinkle with walnuts if desired. Cover and cook on low for 2-3 hours. Sprinkle the other half of the topping and finish in the oven at 350 until crisp. Serve in bowls.

Submitter: 
Milly Suazo-Martinez
Description: 
You'll need just four common ingredients to put together this comforting dessert from Mary Ann Potter of Blue Springs, Missouri. 'A friend gave me this recipe several years ago,' Mary Ann notes. 'She said a local minister's wife fixed it every Sunday, so she named it accordingly.'Modified from: Minister's Delight
