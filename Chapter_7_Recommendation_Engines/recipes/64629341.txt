Title:
Grilled Cheese Sandwich Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bread
20 grams butter
15 g processed cheese slices
15 g cheddar cheese

Directions:
You can start by preheating the sandwich maker.
Remove the bread crusts from the four sides of bread then spread butter on one side of each bread.
On the non-buttered side of bread, add the two slices of cheese then top it with the other slice of bread with the buttered side up.
Put the sandwich in the sandwich maker then toast for 5 minutes or until the sandwich are crispy.
Cut the sandwiches then serve and enjoy your grilled cheese.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
What's better than a grilled cheese sandwich is a grilled cheese sandwich cooked in a sandwich maker. It's crispy, thin and so much easier to bite. Use two of your favorite cheese and you'll enjoy your grilled cheese sandwich toast so much more.
