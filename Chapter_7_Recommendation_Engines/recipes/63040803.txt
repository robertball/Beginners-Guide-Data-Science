Title:
Benihana Style Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1125195.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white long grain rice, cooked.
2 eggs
1/2 onion, chopped
1 med stalk of celery, chopped
1 small carrot, chopped
1 small bunch green onions, chopped
2 cloves minced garlic
3 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons oil (use for cooking veggies)
2 teaspoons sesame oil
2 tablespoons soy sauce (or more to taste)
6 pinches pepper
1 teaspoon sesame seed

Directions:
Cook the rice per package directions, DO NOT use instant or parboiled rice.  After done, spread out on a sheet pan and let cool, wrap up and place in fridge until ready to use.. within 2-3 days.
Chop onion, carrot and celery in a processor to get tiny bits of veggies.
Scramble eggs in the microwave and chop after cooking.
Saute onion, celery,carrots, green onion, and garlic in the oils and butter until no longer crunchy.
Separate the cold rice into individual grains and mix with vegetables, heat through
Add scrambled eggs.
Add sesame seed.
Add soy sauce into the mixture, stirring well until completely heated through.
