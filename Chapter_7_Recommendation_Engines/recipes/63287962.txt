Title:
LAS PALMAS 1-2-3-ENCHILADAS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
3 cups (12oz.)shredded Monterey Jack cheese divided usage.
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 can (19oz.)Las Palmas Enchilada Sauce
8 corn torttillas
3/4 cup dairy sour cream
1 can (4.5oz.) chopped green chiles.

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine chicken, 2 cups cheese, green chiles and onion.
In a small skillet, bring enchilada sauce to boil: remove from heat. Dip each tortilla into heated sauce to soften.
Spoon 1//3 cup chicken mixture and 2 Tlbs. sour cream down center of each tortilla. Roll: place tortillas seam-side down in 12x8 inch baking dish. Pour remaininng heated enchilada sauce over top: sprinkle with remaining 1 cup cheese. Bake 20 minutes @  350*.
Makes 4 servings.
