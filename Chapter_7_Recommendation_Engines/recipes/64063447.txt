Title:
Turkey... again? Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups turkey
1 3/4 cups chicken broth
4 T marg
2 T flour
1/2 t cajun spice
1/2 t dried parsley
2 green onions, white part only, cut into 1/2 inch pieces

Directions:
Melt marg in sauce pan
add flour and cook for 2 minutes
slowly add chicken broth and whisk until smooth
add cajun spice and parsley
place turkey in a oven proof dish
poor gravy over turkey
add green onions
turn to coat
bake for 25 minutes

A simple dish for left over turkey
