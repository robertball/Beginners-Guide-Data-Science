Title:
Broccoli Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 onion, chopped (or 1 Tbsp. dried minced onion)
1 cup chopped mushrooms
1 (16 ounce) package frozen broccoli florets
3 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
24 ozs. (1.5 pounds) loaf processed cheese food, cubed
2 cups milk
1 tablespoon garlic powder
2/3 cup cornstarch
1 cup water

Directions:
In a stockpot, melt butter over medium heat. Cook onion and mushrooms in butter until softened. Stir in broccoli, and cover with chicken broth. Simmer until broccoli is tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
Reduce heat, and stir in cheese cubes until melted. Mix in milk and garlic powder.
In a small bowl, stir cornstarch into water until dissolved. Stir into soup; cook, stirring frequently, until thick.
