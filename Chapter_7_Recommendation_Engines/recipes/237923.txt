Title:
Lime Habanero Slushie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2078314.jpg
Ingredients:
10 ice cubes
5 tablespoons lime juice
1/4 cup simple syrup
1 very small slice habanero pepper
1 drop blue food coloring
1 drop yellow food coloring

Directions:
Blend ice cubes, lime juice, simple syrup, habanero pepper, blue food coloring, and yellow food coloring together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
mrmanuke
Description: 
A sweet and spicy slushie drink. This is my best approximation of the recipe which I first had at a restaurant.
