Title:
Grilled Corn and Edamame Succotash Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 stripped ears of corn
1 red bell pepper stemmed and seeded
1/2 yellow onion peeled
1 jalapeno pepper
2 tbl spoon olive oil
Salt & pepper to taste
1 cup frozen shelled Edamame
1 tbl spoon olive oil
1 tbl spoon white whine vinegar
Fresh basil sliced

Directions:
Prepare grill to 400 - 450 degrees.
Place corn, peppers and onion on cookie sheet and drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Rub and coat veggies.
Transfer veggies to grill and cook 10 - 15 minutes, turning several times until dark mark appear.
Boil 1 cup of Edamame for 10 - 15 minutes. Let cool
Mix 1 tbl spoon of olive oil and 1 tbl spoon vinegar.
Dice peppers and onion. Cut corn off cob. Place veggies and edamame in serving dish. Toss with oil and vinegar.
Season with basil, salt and pepper
