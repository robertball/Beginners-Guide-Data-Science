Title:
Rice & Lentil Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup lentils
1/2 cup rice
1 2/3 cup chicken broth
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium green pepper, chopped
4 Italian sausage patties/ or links

Directions:
In a large covered saucepan, saute the italian sausage. Cut into bite size pieces.  In the same pan that you cooked the sausage, add the onion & green peper and saute for about 3-4 minutes.  Throw in the rest of the ingredients and cover.  Slow cook for about 30-60 minutes.  Great with french bread and a salad.

Submitter: 
jill
Description: 
This is an easy to make casserole that uses just 1 covered pan. Very tasty and a family favorite. I hope you enjoy it!
