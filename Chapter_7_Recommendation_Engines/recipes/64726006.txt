Title:
Spinach Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-10oz packages of frozen spinach, defrosted and squeezed dry
4T butter
4T flour
1/2t salt
pinch pepper
2 3/4C milk
4oz white cheese
4 slices bacon

Directions:
In saucepan, melt butter; add flour.  Cook for a few minutes and slowly add milt.  Add s&p; bring to low boil and heat until thickened.  Add cheese and continue cooking until melted. Add spinach to sauce; transfer to a shallow baking dish.  Sprinkle with bread crumbs.  Cut bacon into small pieces and sprinkle this over casserole. Bake 350 degrees until bacon is crisp.
