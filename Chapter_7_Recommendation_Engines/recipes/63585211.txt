Title:
Nacho Mamas Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Sharp Cheddar Cheese rough diced
4 Green onions chopped in 4" pieces
4 oz.  1/3 Less Fat Cream Cheese ( I use Philadelphia)
15.5 oz. Can Pinto Beans with Jalape√±os and bacon (I use Trappey's Brand)
15 oz. Can Ranch Style Beans  (I use Ranch Style Beans Brand)
2/3 cup Fresh Cilantro (loosely packed)
Bags Scoopes Fritos

Directions:
Add cheese and onion to food processor and pulse until cheese is broken down and no chunks remain.
Add cream cheese and pulse until blended
Add Ranch Style Beans (not drained) and pulse until blended
Add Pinto Beans with Jalapenos and Bacon (Drained) and pulse until blended
Add Cilantro and pulse until blennded
Serve with Scoopes Fritos.  Makes about  4 cups.

This is a great way to get your kids to eat beans and you will love it as well!
