Title:
Roasted Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6040700.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
i large onion
4 sprigs of thyme
4 unpeeled garlic cloves
3 tbs olive oil
Kosher salt to taste
ground pepper
1/2 cup grated parmesan

Directions:
Preheat oven at 425
Cut 1 head cauliflower into florets, loss on a large rimmed baking sheet
with 1 sliced large onion, 4 sprigs thyme, 4 unpleeled garlic cloves, and 3 tbs
olive oil, season with kosher salt and ground pepper. Roast, tossing occasionally,
until almost tender, 35-40 mins. Sprinkle with 1/2 cup grated Parmesan, toss to combine and roast until cauliflower is tender and brown.10-12 minutes longer.
