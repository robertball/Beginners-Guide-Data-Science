Title:
Hawaiian bread Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/2 cup mashed banana
1/2 cup crushed pineapple with juice
1 egg
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup soft butter
1 teaspoon coconut extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 cup instant mashed potato flakes
3 cups bread flour
1 1/2 teaspoons active yeast

Directions:
add all ingredients in order. Set bread machine to light white
