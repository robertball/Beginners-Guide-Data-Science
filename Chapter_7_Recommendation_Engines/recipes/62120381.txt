Title:
Biscuit n Gravy Breakfast Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans grand buttermilk biscuits
1 pkg country gravy mix prepared
1 lb of cooked bacon
12 eggs
1/4 c milk
salt and pepper to taste
16oz colby jack cheese- grated

Directions:
Press 1 can of grand biscuits onto pizza baking pan to form crust. Spread 1/2 of prepared gravy, then bacon, and 8oz of cheese onto biscuit crust. Repeat for 2nd pizza.  Bake at 350 for 15-17 min.  For variations, change out bacon for cooked sausage, ham, or canadian baco; or add them all!  Add your favorite omlett additions and some salsa to spice it up.
