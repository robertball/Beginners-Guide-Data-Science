Title:
Kufta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. of ground beef
1 bunch of parsley, chopped finely
2 large onions, sliced
1 large onion chopped finely
2 large potatoes, sliced in rounds
3 large tomatoes, sliced in rounds
2 tbl. ground allspice
1 tbl. of salt
1/2 tsp. of pepper.
1 tbl. of extra virgin olive oil
1 16 oz. can of tomato paste
vegetable oil to fry potatoes
pita bread
1 cup of rice
1 1/2 cups of water
1 tsp. of turmeric

Directions:
1.  spice ground beef with salt, pepper, and allspice.  Add olive oil and mix well.
2.  Mix in 1 onion and parsley chopped finely.
3.  Mold beef into football shaped balls and place in a casserole dish.
4.  Place the meat at 350 in oven for 15-20 min. for the meat to tender.
5.  In the meantime fry the potatoes very lightly, they do not have to be cooked they will cook in the oven.  This is done to expedite the cooking process, to make it less fattening and healthier do not fry the potatoes but this means it will take much longer for the dish to cook.
6.  In a pot add 2 tbl. of olive oil and heat.  After oil is hot add the cup of rice and mix for 2 min.  Add 1 1/2 cups of water and turmeric and cook on high heat.  Bring water to a boil to allow the rice and water to level.  Reduce the heat to Low and cover.  It will take 15 min. to cook.
7.  Remove the meat dish and layer the potatoes on top then tomatoes and onions. Return dish for another 10 min. for the vegetables to cook.
8.  in the meantime, in a pot add the tomato paste with a can of water place on high heat for 5 min.  throw in a pinch of allspice and black pepper.
9.  Add the tomato mixture on top of the meat dish and cook for 20 min. so the meat can absorb the sauce.
10. you are all set. Eat it with rice or use pita bread to eat the meat dish.

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
This is a Ground beef dish cooked in a spiced tomato sauce with sliced potatoes, onions, and rounded tomatoes.
