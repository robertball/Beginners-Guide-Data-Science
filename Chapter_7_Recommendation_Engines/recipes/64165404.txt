Title:
EZ zassy green pesto sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1447438.jpg
Ingredients:
80 g fresh Parmesan cheese (no pre-grated)
60 g fresh basil leaves
1-2 cloves of garlic
150 ml olive oil
1/2 lemon juice (or lime)
60 g pine nut
salt
ground pepper

Directions:
grate Parmesan cheese
put everything but lemon juice in blender and blend until smooth paste. season to liking with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
add lemon juice and stir

Submitter: 
wims recipes
Description: 
easily made green pesto sauce ready in less than 5 minutes to mix in with pasta. delicious with chicken. garnish with slices of Parmesan or Pecorino cheese
