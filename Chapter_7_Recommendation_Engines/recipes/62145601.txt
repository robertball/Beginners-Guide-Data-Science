Title:
Spicy Beef Stuffed Onion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb. Ground Beef
1 egg with a little water
1/4 cup hot sauce
Salt and pepper
1/2 cup cornmeal
1 big peeled yellow onion

Directions:
1. Cut a hole in the top of the onion, not all the way through.
2. Dip the onion in the hot sauce, then cornmeal, then the egg wash then the cornmeal again.
3. Season the beef with salt and pepper, then season the onion. Stuff the onion with the beef and wrap in foil.
4. Get some coals hot and place the onion on them. Cook 1 hour, or until golden brown and crisp.
