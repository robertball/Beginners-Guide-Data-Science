Title:
Banana Roll French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1117909.jpg
Ingredients:
8 slices of large wheat toast
2 eggs
1 small package of cream cheese
2 bananas
1/8 cup of sugar
1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon

Directions:
Place a skillet on medium heat with little oil. Take the bread slices and flatten with a rolling pin (or tall glass if you don't have a pin). Soften cream cheese and place in medium bowl. Slice the bananas. then mush with fork until they are a little chunky, than add them to the cream cheese bowl. Add in the sugar and cinnamon to this mixture too. In separate wide bowl, mix the two eggs (this will be your dipping batter). Place 2.5 tablespoons of the cream cheese mixture near one end of a flattened bread slice, then roll. Roll in the egg batter and place in skillet. Flip the roll over after a couple of minutes so that all sides of the roll is cooked. Continue till you have 8 rolls. Serve with whipped cream, syrup or alone. Enjoy!
