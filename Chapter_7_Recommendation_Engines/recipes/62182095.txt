Title:
Peanut Butter Fudge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CUP BUTTER OR MARGARINE
¼ CUP COCOA
1 CUP WATER
½ CUP BUTTERMILK OR SOUR MILK
2 WELL BEATEN EGGS
2 CUPS SUGAR
2CUPS FLOUR
1 TSP. BAKING SODA
1 TSP. VANILLA
1 ½ CUP CREAMY PEANUT BUTTER
1TBSP OIL (IF PEANUT BETTER APPEARS OILY, DO NOT ADD THIS)
½ CUP MARGARINE
¼ CUP COCOA
3-5 TBSP BUTTERMILK OR SOUR MILK
1 TSP VANILLA

Directions:
IN A SAUCEPAN COMBIME BUTTER, COCOA, BUTTERMILK, WATER AND EGGS. STIR CONSTANTLY OVER LOW HEAT UNTIL MIXTURE BUBBLES. IN LARGE BOWL, MIX SUGAR,   FLOUR, AND BAKING SODA. STIR HOT MIXTURE INTO DRY INGREDIENTS. BEAT UNTIL SMOOTH.  STIR IN VANILLA. POUR MIXTURE EVENLY INTO A 9X13 GREASED AND FLOURED BAKING PAN. BAKE IN PREHEATED 350 DEGREE OVEN 25 MINUTES.
COOL CAKE IN PAN THOUROUGHLY
MIX PEANUT BUTTER AND ADD OIL TOGETHER- SPREAD EVENLY OVER COOLED CAKE.
IN SAUCEPAN, HEAT BUTTER, COCOA AND BUTTER MILK UNTIL BUBBLY. PLACE 1 PKG CONFECTIONER SUGAR IN BOWL AND POUR HOT MIXTURE OVER THIS. BEAT UNTIL SMOOTH. ADD VANILLA. SPREAD MIXTURE EVENLY OVER PEANUT BUTTER TOPPING.
