Title:
Calavasitas (Mexican Squash & Cheese) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tblsp olive oil
1 medium yellow onion, diced
1 clove of garlic, minced
3 medium zucchini, sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 tsp. hot sauce (or to taste)
1 14.5 oz. can sweet corn, drained
salt & pepper to taste
1 1/2 cups shredded monterey jack cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet add onion and garlic to oil and saute until onions begin to become translucent.  Add zucchini and turn them as they cook.  When zucchini begin to soften, add tomatoes, hot sauce, corn and salt & pepper.  Continue cooking until juices begin to evaporte and zucchini is cooked through.  Sprinkle cheese over entire dish, turn off heat and cover for about 10 minutes or until cheese is melted. ENJOY!
