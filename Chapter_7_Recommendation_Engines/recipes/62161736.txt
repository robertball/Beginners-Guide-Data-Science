Title:
Daddy's Famous Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ripe Hass avocados (Large)
Lawry's seasoned salt
1/4- 1/2 lime

Directions:
Cut avocado in 4 pieces remove skin & seed.
Place avocado in medium sized bowl and use knife to slice it up then take a fork and mash the avocado (or use a potato masher if making for large party).
Add seasoned salt to taste (a few shakes) and mix in.
Squeeze 1/4 to 1/2 lime depending on taste and mix in.
Place the avocado seed back in to keep it from discoloring quickly and serve with your favorite tortilla chips. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Islandwatergirl
Description: 
This is a very simple recipe for really good guacamole. Even people who say they don't like guacamole will eat this and ask for more!
