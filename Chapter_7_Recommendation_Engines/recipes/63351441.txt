Title:
Tortellini with Mushroom Pesto Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. button mushrooms
4 oz. shitake mushrooms
4 cloves garlic
1/2 cup walnuts
1/2 cup light olive oil
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup Romano cheese
1 bunch fresh flat leaf parsley
1 lb. tortellini
1 cup cream
1/2 cup white wine
2 tsp. corn starch
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine mushrooms, garlic, nuts and parsley in a food processor until coarsely chopped.
Continue to process and gradually add oil until smooth.
Pour into a bowl and add cheeses and salt and pepper to taste.
Cook tortellini to al dente and add sauce.
Garnish with parsley to serve.
