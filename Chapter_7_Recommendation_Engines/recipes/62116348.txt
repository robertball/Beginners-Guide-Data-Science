Title:
african turkey stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Smoked Turkey Legs cut into quarters 1 inch thick
4 Red bell peppers
6 Tomatoes
1 yellow onion
4 Chicken Bullin Cubes
4 Habanera Peppers or Scotch Bonnet (careful both very HOT)
1 Tablespoon minced Garlic or Garlic Powder
2 Tablespoons of Tomato Paste
1 cup of vegetable or oil of your choice
Salt to taste
3 cups cooked Jasmine rice

Directions:
Boil Turkey with 2 bullion cubes until water is almost gone
Blend pepers,tomatos, onion, in blender until smooth
Heat oil in a sauce pan pour blended ingredents
bring to a boil then let simmer for 30-45 mins
add turkey simmer for 15-20min
serve over cooked rice

Submitter: 
Fatimah Soumanou
Description: 
A spicy dish that will fill your stomach and touch every taste bud made of very simple ingredients and easy to make.
It will become a favorite.
