Title:
Puffed Peach Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/571663.jpg
Ingredients:
3 TBL dark brown sugar
¼ tsp. cinnamon
1 TBL fresh lemon juice
2 fresh peaches, sliced
2 TBL butter
2 large eggs, beaten
6 TBL all-purpose flour
Pinch of salt
½ C. milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 425º.  In a medium bowl stir together 1 tablespoon of brown sugar, cinnamon, lemon juice and sliced peaches.  Toss to cover.  Divide the butter between 2 shallow 1 cup ovenproof dishes; heat in oven until butter melts.
Combine eggs, flour, salt and milk.  Mix well.  Divide the batter between the dishes.  Divide the peaches between the dishes, arranging in a pleasing manner, and sprinkle the a tablespoon of brown sugar on the top of each dish.  Bake for 18 to 20 minutes in a 425º oven.
