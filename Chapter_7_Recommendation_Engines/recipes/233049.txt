Title:
Peanut Butter Yogurt Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1034110.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup frozen whipped topping (such as Cool WhipÂ®), thawed
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Beat yogurt, peanut butter, whipped topping, and cinnamon together by hand in a bowl until well-blended, about 2 minutes. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and chill until cold, about 15 minutes.

This is an easy recipe to make and is so good to eat. My family loves it with slices of apples.
