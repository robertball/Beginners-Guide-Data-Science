Title:
Black Bean and Chicken Chili with Lime Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
• 1 onion, chopped
• 3 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 red bell pepper, chopped
• 1 green bell pepper, chopped
• 1 Tbsp chili powder
• 1 15-oz. can tomatoes, with juice
• 4 cups cooked black beans (or 2 15-oz. cans, drained and rinsed)
• 1 cup water
• 1 10-oz. can chicken breast, drained and chopped
• 1/4 cup lime juice
• 1/4 cup chopped cilantro leaves
• salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oil over medium heat. Saute onion and peppers until softened. Add garlic and chili powder and cook about a minute longer. Add chicken, tomatoes, beans, and water. Simmer until flavors combine and vegetables are soft, about 15-20 minutes.
Stir in lime juice and cilantro. Salt as desired.
