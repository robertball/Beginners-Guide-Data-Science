Title:
Mexican Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound ground chuck,
1/2 pound breakfast sausage,
4 eggs,
2/3 cup oil,
1 pkg cornbread mix,
2 cans cream style corn,
1 bell pepper chopped,
1 onion, chopped,
1 cup grated cheddar cheese, or your favorite.

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together, except cheese, and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes, or until meat is done.  Sprinkle with cheese, and brown slightly.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
The sausage in this gives it just a little different taste than all the other recipes.  Hope you like it.
