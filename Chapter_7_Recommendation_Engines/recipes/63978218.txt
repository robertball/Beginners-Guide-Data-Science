Title:
Whole wheat vegie lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box whole wheat lasagna noodles
1 yellow onion, chopped
1 carton portobello mushrooms, chopped to desired size
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 large bunch of spinach, stems removed
12 oz. seasoned, quartered artichoke hearts
1 large can tomato sauce
1 tsp sugar
2 tsp basil
1 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp thyme
1 TB fresh rosemary
2 cups shredded Mozzarella cheese
1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Saute onions over medium heat 15 minutes until translucent
meanwhile, boil and drain noodles according to directions
add mushrooms to saute, cook 3 minutes
add garlic to saute, then add spinach after garlic cooks about 30 seconds
cover saute to wilt the spinach, about 3 minutes
stir together tomato sauce and remaining seasonings.
put first layer of noodles in caserole dish, spread 1/3 of the tomato sauce over noodles.  Add 1/2 the artichokes, and 1/2 the saute, and 1/3 of the cheeses.
Repeat.
The top layer should be noodles, remaining tomato sauce, parmesan cheese, and then remaining mozarella.
Cover with foil and refrigerate a few hours, up to 24 hours.
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes covered, 15 minutes uncovered, and serve after 15 minutes resting.

Submitter: 
tpf2005
Description: 
Here is a healthier version of Lasagna.  It is not traditional, but my family enjoys it.  This is a great recipe to double, and then reheat for another meal the next day.
