Title:
Minestrone soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. mixed dried beans
1/4 c. olive oil
3/4 c. minced onions
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 c. chopped celery
1 (28 oz) can chopped tomatoes
2 c. shredded cabbage
2 c. sliced zucchini
1/4 c. chopped parsley
4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Soak beans by boiling in 3 qts. water for 2 minutes, then let stand, covered, for an hour.  Bring to boil again (or rinse and start with fresh water) and simmer until tender, 2 or more hours, till beans are tender (or use pressure cooker:  after soaking, bring up to pressure then turn off; let pressure release on its own, about 20 minutes.)
Saute onion, garlic, and celery in oil about 10 minutes.  Add to beans along with tomatoes and salt.  Cover and simmer 45 minutes.
Add cabbage, zucchini, parsley, pepper and a little more water.  Simmer 20 minutes or until veggies are tender.
