Title:
Ginny's BBQ sloppy joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4lbs-Hamburger
1cup-Onion chopped
1cup-green pepper chopped
2cans-tomato soup(10-3/4 0z)
3-8oz tomato sauce
3/4cup-brown sugar
1/4cup-ketchup
3Tbls-worcestershire sauce
1Tbls-prepared mustard
1Tbls-ground mustard
1teas-chili powder
1teas-garlic salt

Directions:
In large dutch oven brown beef and onion. add green pepper.
Cook and stir 5 mins and drain. Add next 10 ingredients, bring to a boil and reduce heat. simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
serve on buns

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
Ginny brought these to work. They were the nicest flavor. Couldn't eat just one!
