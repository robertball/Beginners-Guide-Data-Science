Title:
Michelle's Vegetable Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups sliced zucchini
1 1/2 cups sliced mushrooms
3/4 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
9 eggs (can use Egg Beaters)
1/4 cup milk or half-and-half
1 (8 oz.) pkg cream cheese, diced
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Spray a 9x13 pan with Pam.
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium high hat.  Add zucchini, mushrooms, onion, red bell pepper and garlic.  Saute until tender.  Remove from heat and strain fluid out.
In a blender, mix the eggs, milk, cream cheese, cheddar cheese and pepper.  Once it's completely blended, put it into the prepared baking dish.  Add the vegetables and stir to mix.
Bake in preheated oven for one hour, or until center is set.  Serve hot or cold.

Submitter: 
michelle o'c
Description: 
This Frittata can be eaten hot or cold.  This is my go-to recipe when my son needs to grab a quick breakfast before work.  I make it one day, and he can enjoy it every morning thereafter.
