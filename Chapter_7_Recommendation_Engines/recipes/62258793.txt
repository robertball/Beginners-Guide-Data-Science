Title:
Parmesan Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayo
1/4 cup sugar
1/8 cup parmesan cheese
1 pkg coleslaw cabbage mix

Directions:
Mix first three ingredients together. Pour over coleslaw mix, mix together, and chill one hour.

Submitter: 
Nia
Description: 
When my mom asks says what to bring to family dinners she refers to this as THE coleslaw.
