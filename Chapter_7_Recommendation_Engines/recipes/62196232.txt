Title:
Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lb. ground turkey
1 cup pureed white beans
1/4 cup tomato sauce
3/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup plain bread crumbs
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 large egg
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°F. In a mixing bowl, combine by hand all ingredients. Shape mixture on rimmed baking sheet into a loaf. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove from oven and spread 1/4 cup tomato sauce over top. Bake until thermometer inserted in center reads 165°F, about 15 to 20 minutes longer. Remove from oven and let rest, loosely covered with foil, for 10 minutes before serving.
