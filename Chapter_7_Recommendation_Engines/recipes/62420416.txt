Title:
Chocolate Cinnamon Bread pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 each large eggs
1 cup Sugar
2 cups Milk
5 cups Cinnamon Swirl Bread, cubed
1 cup chocolate-covered Raisins (such as Raisinettes)
Buttery flavor Cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 deg. F.
Combine eggs and sugar in large bowl.  Stir together with a whisk, then stir in milk.  Add bread cubes, and stir to moisten evenly.
Add in chocolate-covered Raisins.  Stir to blend in evenly.
Spray an 8" square oven baking pan with cooking spray.
Spoon in the mixture and let it stand for 10 minutes in the baking dish to allow flavors to blend.
Bake for 55 to 60 minutes at 350 Deg. F., until set.
Serve in mugs or custard cups.
Optional:  Add a dollop of whipped cream to each.
