Title:
Make Ahead Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
64 oz reduced salt chicken stock
4 celeery ribs, cut into 2 inch pieces
4 carrots, cut into 2 inch pieces
2 medium onions, quartered
10 whole peppercorns
2 small bay leaves
1 c dehydrated porcini mushrooms, rehydrated for thirty minutes in 1 cup hot water and 1 c sherry, then drained and diced, reserving sherry mixture for stock.
10 T butter
10 T flour
1 t browning and seasoning liquid
1 t salt
2 chicken bouillion cubes or equivalent
Ground pepper to taste
Soy sauce to taste.

Directions:
Combine broth, celery, carrots, onions, peppercorns, bay leaves, mushrooms and sherry mixture in a medium saucepan or pot.
Bring to a boil, then cook, uncovered for twenty minutes.
Strain broth, discarding vegetables and seasonings.
Melt butter in a medium skillet.
Blend in flour.
Cook until mixture is slightly browned, stirring occasionally as mixture foams.
Gradually stir in seasoned broth, browning and seasoning sauce, bouillion and salt.
Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly to mix in flour, until mixture comes to a boil. About 20 minutes.
Boil 1-2 minutes, then remove from heat and refrigerate until next day.
Reheat and taste, adding soy sauce, sherry, butter, salt and pepper to deepen flavor as required.
