Title:
Vanilla Banana Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bananas, peeled and sliced
3 cups cold milk
2 teaspoons vanilla
3 tablespoons sugar
4 cups crushed ice

Directions:
Blend all ingredients until frothy.

Submitter: 
Karen Flores-Perez
Description:
