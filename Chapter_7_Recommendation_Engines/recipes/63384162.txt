Title:
Pumpkin and Bourbon Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white sugar
6 tablespoons bourbon
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
8 egg yolks
8 egg whites
1 cup canned pumpkin
orange zest

Directions:
Bring 2" water to a boil in a 4 quart saucepan over medium high heat.  Place a medium metal bowl over the pan.  Add sugar, bourbon, spices and egg yolks, whisk together, and cook, , whisking constantly, until thickened and pale, about 6 minutes. Remove from heat and set bourbon mixture aside.
In another bowl, whisk 8 egg whites until stiff peaks form, add 1 cup pumpkin, and fold until almost combined.  Add mixture to warm bourbon mixture and fold together until smooth.  Divide amoung serving glasses and sprinkle with orange zest.
