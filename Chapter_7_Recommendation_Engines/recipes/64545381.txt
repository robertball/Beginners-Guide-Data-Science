Title:
Classic Semi-Spicy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb Ground Beef or Turkey
2 tbsp Minced Garlic
1 Large Onion  (Diced)
1-2 Packs Mild chili spice (How spicy do you want it?)
2 16oz Cans Chili Beans
2 15.5oz Cans Dark Red Kidney Beans
1 27oz Can Pinto Beans
1 10 3/4oz Can Tomatoe Soup
1 14.5oz Can Stewed Tomatoes
Red Pepper Flakes(Optional)
2 Tbsp Worchestershier Sauce
2 Cups Shredded Cheese
1/3 Cup Sugar
Sour Cream (Optional)
Saltine Crackers

Directions:
Brown Meat, then add diced onion. Heat on medium until onions are transparent.
Drain
Add chili spice pack(s), stir until meat is well coated.
Add all beans, tomato soup, stewed tomatoes, red pepper flakes (if desired), Worchestershier sauce and sugar.
Heat on high until bubbling.
Reduce heat and let simmer for 10 minutes.
Remove from heat and serve immediately.
Top each serving with a generous amount of cheese, and a dallop of sour cream.
Serve with buttered saltines crackers.
Enjoy!
If using in a crock pot:
Brown meat and onions.
Then add all ingredients to crock pot.
Set to high, let cook for 4 hours.
Top with cheese, and serve.

Submitter: 
Aaron Cooper Jenkins
Description: 
This is my mother's best chili recipe that she has been using since I was a child. It has a great assortment of flavorful beans and mixture of spices with a hint of sweetness for that perfect, home cooked chili everyone loves.
