Title:
Spring Greens, Fresh Pea and Ham Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 1/2 cups coarsely chopped yellow onion
4 cloves garlic, minced
6 cups chicken broth
1 lb Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2 dice
1/2 lb kale, ribs removed and coarsely chopped
2 cups diced canned tomatoes
1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
3 bay leaves
1 lb  ham, cut into 1/2 inch chunks
1 cup cooked or canned Great Northern beans
2 cups fresh or frozen peas
2 tbsp lemon juice
3 tbsp finely chopped flat leaf parsley
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1.  Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium high heat and add the onions, garlic and sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Cook until transluscent and beginning to brown.
2.  Add the stock, potatoes and greens.  Bring to a boil over high heat, then decrease to a simmer and cook until the potatoes are just tender (10 mins).
3.  Add the rosemary and bay leaves along with the tomatoes, ham and beans.
4.  Cook at a low simmer for 15 minutes, then add the peas and cook 5 minutes more.
5.  Season with S&P, lemon juice and parsley.

Submitter: 
CHRISTYG
Description: 
A bright, lemony soup, perfect for using up leftover Easter ham!
