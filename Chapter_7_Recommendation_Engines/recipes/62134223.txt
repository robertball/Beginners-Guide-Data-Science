Title:
Toffee Ice Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 container vanilla ice cream, lightly softened
1 container butterscotch chocolate ice cream, lightly softened
1 package toffee bits
1 package milk chocolate chips
1 small container caramel sauce (can be home-made)
1 banana, thinly sliced (optional)

Directions:
Line the bottom of a springform pan with waxed paper or tin foil. Spread some of the vanilla ice cream on the bottom and sides of the pan. Add a layer of chocolate in the centre and spread out until it meets with vanilla ice cream sides. Cover with plastic wrap and freeze until set.
Once frozen, add sliced bananas over chocolate ice cream. Spread caramel sauce over. Sprinkle with chocolate chips and toffee bits. Freeze until set.
Once frozen, add vanilla ice cream over filling, to fill in until it meets the vanilla ice cream sides. Freeze until set.

Submitter: 
JULICHICKY
Description: 
A classic ice cream cake with toffee bits, choco chips, caramel sauce, vanilla and butterscotch chocolate ice cream! You can use any kind of ice cream flavour and mix up the fillings to include any of your favorite fruit or candies.
