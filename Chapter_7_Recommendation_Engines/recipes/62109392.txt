Title:
Cauliflower on the BBQ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head Cauliflower
4 tbsp. butter
2 tbsp. loose chicken base or more if you want it tastier

Directions:
Tear a large peice of tin foil lay it on the counter
Cut the cauliflower into even sized pieces
Place pieces on foil
Place dabs of butter on top of the cauliflower
Sprinkle the chicken base over the whole thing
Tear another large piece of foil and cover the cauliflower and fold the edges to form a packet
Place on top rack in BBQ for 15 minutes, turn once

Submitter: 
MISSCANADA
Description: 
Cauliflower on the BBQ! Great summer BBQ side dish for any BBQ meal. Foil packet filled with cauliflower and butter with chicken power base. Even the kids will love this one.
