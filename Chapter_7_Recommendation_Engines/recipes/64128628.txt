Title:
Sausage and Pepper Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1327125.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pizza crust, prepared
3/4 C. pizza sauce
1 lb. pork sausage
2 C. fresh bell peppers (mixture of red, yellow, and green)
1 8 oz. pkg FRESH mozarella, shredded
2 tsp. pizza topping seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 with baking stone inside.
Brown pork sausage and drain.
Once baking stone has heated, remove from oven and arrange pizza crust on top. Spread pizza sauce evenly. Distribute pork sausage and bell peppers evenly on top of sauce. Top evenly with mozarella and sprinkle with pizza seasoning.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes or until cheese has melted and crust has started to brown.
