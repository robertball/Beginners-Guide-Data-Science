Title:
Marinade for Venison Steaks or London Broil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c white vinegar
1/2 c ketchup
1/4 c oil
1/4 c worcestershire sauce
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 tsp ground mustard
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and pour over venison steaks or london broil in a bag.  Seal and shake to coat.  Refrigerate overnight or all day (8 hours).

Submitter: 
ESTEPHAN
Description: 
Flavorful marinade with simple ingredients.  Let it marinade all day and have a great london broil for dinner
