Title:
Diane Slow Cooker Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds beef stew meat
salt and pepper to taste
3 T butter
2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed beef consomme
1/2 c cognac or brandy
1 cup water
2 cups mushrooms, halved
4 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup finely minced shallots
2 T Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon ground mustard seed
2 T tomato paste
1/3 cup heavy cream
1/2 c fresh parsley leaves, chopped

Directions:
Season the beef with salt and pepper, and place in a skillet over medium heat. Cook in butter until evenly brown, and drain.  Cook Shallots and garlic in same pan and deglaze with cognac.
In a slow cooker, mix the beef broth, condensed beef consomme, water, mustards, tomato paste, Worcestershire. Place beef mixture into the liquid.
Cover, and cook 6 hours on Low or 4 hours on High.
Stir in heavy cream 30 minutes before the end of the cooking time. Stir occasionally until thickened.
