Title:
MySweetCreations Peanut Butter Cookie Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472338.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
18 Oreo cookies
1 1/2 Tablespoons butter
1/2 Teaspoon cinnamon
Filling:
1 8oz. package cream cheese, softened
1 Cup powdered sugar
1 Teaspoon vanilla
1 Cup creamy peanut butter
3 Cups whipped cream topping
Toppings:
3/4 Cup whipped cream topping
4 full size Reese's Cup, chopped
Smuckers Sundae Chocolate Syrup
Smucker Sundae Caramel Syrup

Directions:
1.) Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Crush Oreos and mix with melted butter and cinnamon. Press mixture firmly into the bottom of a springform pan. Bake for 8 minutes and remove from oven and allow to cool.
2.) Mix softened cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla. Add peanut butter until well blended. Add whipped cream and continue to beat until light and fluffy. Transfer mixture onto the cooled cookie crust in springform pan and spread evenly.
3.) To decorate the top, put 3/4 cup of whipped cream into a pastry bag with a tip and pipe onto the top of the pie. Next, drizzle chocolate and caramel syrup over the pie and add chopped Reese's cups.
4.) Once decorated cover and place in the freezer for 4 hours or until firm and ready to serve.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
Smooth peanut butter mixture on a  baked chocolate cookie and cinnamon crust in a springform pan. Serve with Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, whipped cream, chocolate, and caramel syrup topping.
