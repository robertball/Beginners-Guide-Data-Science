Title:
Turmeric Coffee Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4433936.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut milk (or any nut milk)
1/2 tsp turmeric
1/4 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla
2-4 pkts Truvia (or any sweetner)
1 shot espresso

Directions:
1. Warm all ingredients in saucepan except espresso
2. Once hot, froth (I used an immersion blender)
3. Put espresso in mug
4. Add frothed milk mixture
