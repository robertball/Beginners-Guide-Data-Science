Title:
Cream of Leek Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6nmed leeks
6 tbsp butter
3 tbsp flour
2 tsp chopped chives
1/2 tsp salt
6 cuos milk or light cream
6 cups chicken boullion

Directions:
Thinly slice leeks, discarding top green portion
In med saucepan, cook leeks in butter 8-10 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally
Stir in flours, chives and salt
Add milk and bullion
Cook over med heat until boiling, stirring occasionally
Boil 1-2 minutes
Reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes
