Title:
Quick Tomato-Basil Pasta with Fresh Mozzarella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T EVOO
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 t red pepper flakes
28 oz can crushed tomatoes
14 1/2 oz can diced tomatoes
5 T roughly chopped fresh basil
1/4 t sugar
Table salt
1 # campanelle or fusilli pasta
8 oz fresh mini mozzarella balls, drained and cut in half
1/3 C grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Bring 4 qts. water to boil in large pot.
Meanwhile, heat 2T oil, garlic and pepper flakes in med. saucepan over med. heat until fragrant but not brow, about 2 min. Stir in crushed and diced tomatoes, bring to simmer, and cook until slightly thickened, 15-20 min. Stir in 3 T basil, sugar, and 1/2 t salt.
Add 1 T salt and pasta to boiling water. Cook until al dente. Reserve 1/4 C cooking water, drain pasta, and transfer back to pot. Stir in sauce and remaining T oil, and cook over med. heat until heated through (adding reserved water if pasta seems dry), about 1 min. Stir in mozzarella and season with salt. Serve, sprinkling with remaining 2 T basil and Parmesan.
