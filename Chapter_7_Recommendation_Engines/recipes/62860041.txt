Title:
Anytime Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup ground flaxseed
1/2 cup water
1 cup unbleached organic flour
1/2 cup whole wheat pastry or whole wheat flour
1/2 cup old fashioned oats or oat flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 cup canola oil
1/4 cup non-dairy milk
1/4 cup unrefined organic brown sugar
1/4 cup unrefined organic sugar
1/4 cup roughly chopped dried apricots
1/4 cup raisins
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup chopped almonds and/or dark chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In a small bowl, combine ground flaxseed with water and set aside.  "Flax egg"
In a large bowl, combine flours, oats, baking soda, spices, chopped dried fruits and chocoate chips or nuts.
To flaxseed/water mixture, add sugar, oil, milk and vanilla.  Mix well.
Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients, stirring until just combined.
Drop 12 spoonfuls of cookie dough onto cookie sheet sprayed lightly with non stick spray
Bake at 350 for 12 minutes
Cool for one minute on pan and then transfer to cooling rack.
