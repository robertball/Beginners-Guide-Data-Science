Title:
Super Crunch Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. green cabbage, chopped
1 medium Roma tomato, chopped
1/4 medium cucumber, chopped
4 T. raisins
4 T. dried apricot, chopped
3 T. sunflower seeds (shelled)
dash onion powder
1 T. teriyaki sauce
2 T. sesame oil
1 T. cider vinegar
2 T. soy milk, if desired
sprinkle fresh Parmesan cheese, if desired

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients in medium bowl.
Combine remaining ingredients EXCEPT Parmesan cheese in small bowl and mix well, then pour over salad ingredients. Toss with fork to coat, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if desired, and serve.
