Title:
Laura's Scrumptious Banana Pudding! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags chessmen cookies
7 bananas sliced
2 cups milk
1 5 oz box vanilla pudding
1 8 oz box cream cheese softened
1 14 oz can sweet condensed milk
12 oz whipped cream sweetened.

Directions:
Line your 13 by 9 by 2 inch deep dish with 1 bag of chess cookies and then layer bananas on top of that. In a bowl, combine the milkd and pudding and blend well using a mixer. Using another bowl, combine the cream cheese and condensed milk together and mix until smooth. Fold in the whpped cream into the cream cheese mix, and then add the cream cheese mix into the pudding mix and stir until well blended. Pour mix over the cookies and bananas. Layer the other bag of chess cookies over the whole mixture. Cover the pan and place in refridgerater until ready to serve. And make sure to Enjoy! :-)
