Title:
Steak with  Cuban-Style Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c OJ
1/2 fresh lime (2 T)
2T Vegetable Oil
1 T Montreal Steak Seasoning
1 1/2 tsp. Oregano Leaves
1/2 tsp. Cumin
1 1/2 lb. Boneless Ribeye Steak

Directions:
1.  Mix first 6 ingredients
2.  Place steak in large resealable  plastic bag
3.  Refrigerate 30 min. or more
4.  Remove steak and discard marinade.
5.  Grill steak to your desired doness.
6.  Let rest and then slice.
