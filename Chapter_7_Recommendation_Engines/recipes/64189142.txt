Title:
Lazy Day Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
half onion, chopped
minced garlic
bell pepper, diced
shredded chicken breast (optional)
four cans beans - black, chickpea, kidney, white kidney
28 oz crushed tomatoes
16 oz tomato paste
salt, pepper, Italian seasonings
Lentils and small pasta(orzo, Mexican stars, etc)

Directions:
Saute onions, garlic and pepper.
Open cans and pour in undrained - beans and tomato n paste
Add chicken.
Season and simmer on low all day.
Add chicken broth or water as needed.

Submitter: 
aunt star
Description: 
Easy and forgiving. Grab cans outta panty, make cornbread and eat.
