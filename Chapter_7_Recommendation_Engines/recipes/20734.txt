Title:
Super Hot Cereal Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/59726.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups instant oatmeal
4 tablespoons brown sugar
1 cup raisins
3 tablespoons dry milk powder

Directions:
In a large bowl combine oatmeal, brown sugar, dry fruit, and dry milk. Transfer ingredients to an airtight container or jar to store until ready to use.
To serve: Place 1 cup of mixture and 1/2 to 3/4 cup boiling water (depending how thick you like your oatmeal) into a cereal bowl. Let stand until thickened.

Submitter: 
Joann
Description: 
Make your own instant oatmeal mix. This is a great gift! Add boiling water, let sit until thick, and eat! You may substitute your favorite dried fruit for the raisins if you wish.
