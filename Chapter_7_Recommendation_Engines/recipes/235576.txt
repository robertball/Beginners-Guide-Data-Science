Title:
Kahlua Arroz con Leche Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1070514.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces Kahlua Cinnamon Spice
1 fluid ounce Absolut Vodka
1 fluid ounce sweetened condensed milk
1 fluid ounce freshly brewed coffee

Directions:
Fill a shaker with ice, add Kahlua, Absolut Vodka, condensed milk and coffee. Shake vigorously, strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with cinnamon and cinnamon stick
