Title:
Peanut Butter Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/361015.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup skim milk

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, peanut butter, and brown sugar until well blended. Mix in milk until texture is creamy.

Submitter: 
Jami
Description: 
This sweet, creamy peanut butter dip is great with 4-5 large apples and/or celery!
