Title:
Easy "Charro" Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
80 ounces canned pinto beans - mash and reserve 1 c for thickener
1/2 c bacon grease
1 large white onion - finely chopped
1/2 pound bacon - reserve drippings
1/2 pound ham - diced
1/4 pound chorizo sausage - crumbled
1 chipotle pepper - whole
2 cloves garlic - pricked several times with a fork
Top with any of the following:
lime, green onion, fried pork rinds, salsa, cheese, sour cream

Directions:
Fry bacon, reserving 1/2 C drippings, and set bacon aside.  Transfer drippings to a large pot.  Fry onion in drippings until light brown.  Add ham and chorizo.  Add beans, chipotle, garlic and pureed beans.  Simmer 30 minutes until thick and soupy, adding water if necessary.  Remove chipotle and garlic.  Garnish and serve.
