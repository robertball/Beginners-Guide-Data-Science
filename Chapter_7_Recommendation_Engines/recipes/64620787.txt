Title:
Quart Jar Taco seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chili powder
3 tbsp garlic powder
1 tbsp onion powder
1 tbsp onion flakes
1/2 cup onion soup or beef soup mix bouillon
1/4 to 1/2 cup smoked paprika
2 tbsp  dried oregano
1/2 cup cumin
2 1/2 tbsp salt ( or to taste)
4 tbsp ground black pepper
3 tbsp red pepper flakes ( or to taste)
1/2 cup flour

Directions:
for each pound of browned ground meat add 3 tbsp or more of the seasoning and 1/2 to 1 cup water. simmer down to combine flavors.

Submitter: 
Vanessa Peterson Sehn
Description: 
a Spicy  mix for a wonderfully addictive taco seasoning you can make for pennies instead of purchasing packets
