Title:
Healthier Hot Artichoke and Spinach Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283065.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package neufchatel, or Fat free cream cheesesoftened
1/4 cup light or Fat Free mayonnaise
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup grated Romano cheese
1 clove garlic, peeled and minced optional Roasted garlic
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1 garlic clove, minced
salt and pepper to taste
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 box frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a small baking dish.
In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, Romano cheese, garlic, basil, garlic salt, salt and pepper. Gently stir in artichoke hearts and spinach.
Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish. Top with mozzarella cheese. Bake in the preheated oven 25 minutes, until bubbly and lightly browned.
