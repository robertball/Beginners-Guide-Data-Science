Title:
Gelatin Rainbow Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups popped popcorn
1/4 c. butter (1/2 stick)
3 T. Light Corn Syrup
1/2 c. Sugar
1 pkg. Flavored Gelatin

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 300.
2.  Line 15x10x1 baking pan with foil.
3.  Put butter and syrup in small saucepan.
4.  Cook on low heat until butter is melted.
5.  Add sugar and gelatin.  Stir until dissolved.
6.  Bring to boil over medium heat.
7.  Reduce heat to low; simmer 5 minutes.
8.  Pour immediately over popcorn mixture; tossing to coat well.
9.  Spread into prepared pan, using 2 forks to spread
evenly.
10.  Bake 10 minutes.  Cool.  Remove from pan.  Break
into small pieces.
