Title:
ActiFried Chicken and Wheat Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F5841485.jpg
Ingredients:
1  onion
1 clove garlic
200 grams skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
3  tomatoes, chopped
400 ml water
1  chicken stock cube
1 ActiFry spoon curry powder
130 grams wheat berries
1 ActiFry spoon vegetable oil

Directions:
Thinly slice the onion and finely chop the garlic.
Chop the chicken into 3- to 4-cm cubes and chop the tomatoes into 1- to 2-cm cubes.
Place the water, chicken stock, curry powder, and wheat berries in a saucepan. Bring to a rolling boil. Set aside.
Pour oil into the ActiFry bowl. Close the lid. Heat for 5 minutes.
Pour the contents of the saucepan into the ActiFry and add the remaining ingredients. Close the lid. Cook for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
T-fal Canada
Description: 
This ActiFried stir-fry with chicken breast and wheat berries is flavored with tomatoes, chicken stock, and curry powder.
