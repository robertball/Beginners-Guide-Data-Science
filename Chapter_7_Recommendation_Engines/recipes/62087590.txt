Title:
Tangy Pineapple Sweet & Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Pineapple Juice
2 T Cornstarch
1 C Light Brown Sugar
1/4 C Rice Wine or Cider Vinegar
1/4 t Soy Sauce

Directions:
Stir cornstarch into pineapple juice in pan on unheated stove burner until completely dissolved.
Add all other ingredients and stir.
Turn on burner to medium and bring to a boil.
Stir constantly to avoid burning.

Description: 
A tangy sweet & sour sauce without tomato.
