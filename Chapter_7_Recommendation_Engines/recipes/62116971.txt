Title:
Chicken Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 Chicken Breast
1 lb Sausage (linked)
3 T olive oil
2 C Choped onion
1/2 C each of celery and green pepper
2 Bay leaves
4 cloves garlic
2 T Worcestershire sauce
6 oz Tomatoe paste
16 oz Diced canned tomatoes
1/2 t Cayenne pepper
2 bay leaved
1 c Uncooked white rice
2 1/2 c chicken broth

Directions:
Boil Chicken and sausage in water until cook through, about 30 min.
Cut meat into bite size pieces.
Heat oil and saute onion, garlic and green pepper about 5 min, don't over cook.
Add tomatoe paste, canned tomatoes with juice, spices, worcestershire, and cut meat. Cook for 30 min over low heat.
Add uncooked rice and chicken broth and cook covered for 30 min.
