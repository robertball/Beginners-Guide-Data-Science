Title:
Banana Berry Smoothie II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/808835.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 banana
3 large strawberries
1 tablespoon vanilla yogurt
1 teaspoon honey

Directions:
In a blender, combine milk, banana, strawberries, yogurt and honey. Blend until smooth. Pour into glass and serve.

Submitter: 
Stefanie Forster
Description: 
A delicious and healthy smoothie that will satisfy a sweet tooth.
