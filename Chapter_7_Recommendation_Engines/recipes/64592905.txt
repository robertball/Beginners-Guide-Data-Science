Title:
Gin refresher Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3812103.jpg
Ingredients:
3 dashes orange bitters
4 thin slices cucumber
1 sprig fresh mint
1/2 tsp sugar
Juice from 1/2 a lime
1/2 cup frozen tropical fruit medley
2oz London dry gin
1/2oz red wine
Soda water

Directions:
In a hiball glass muddle bitters, cucumber, mint, and sugar. Fill glass with frozen fruit. Layer lime juice, gin, and wine. Top with soda and give a bartenders stir. Garnish with cucumber lime and mint.

Submitter: 
Devan MT
Description: 
The gin lover's sangria. A deconstructed Pimm's if you will. Subtly sweet and fruity, and oh so refreshing!
