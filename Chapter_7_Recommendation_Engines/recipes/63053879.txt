Title:
Beef and Portabella Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb.lean ground beef
2 cups sliced baby portabellas
1 small onion, diced
1 tbsp. minced garlic
1/4 cup flour
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 pkg. wide egg noodles
1 can beef broth
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
Cook onion and garlic in melted butter until soft.  Add beef, mushrooms and salt and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes until beef is no longer pink.  Stir flour into beef mixture. cook noodles as per directions. Stir broth in to beef mixture and then add sour cream. Simmer for 5 minutes. Drain noodles well and serve topped with beef mixture
