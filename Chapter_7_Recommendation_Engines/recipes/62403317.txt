Title:
Pasta Fagiole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28oz Can crushed tomatoes
2 14oz cans chicken broth
1 lb Ditalini (small shells)
1 15oz can white kidney beans
1 clove garlic
1tbsp olive oil
1 1/2 lb ground beef
1 lb fresh spinach
1/2 tsp oregano
1 tsp basil
1 tsp salt/pepper

Directions:
Saute garlic and olive oil, add tomatoes- stir- simmer 5 minutes and broth and beans(drained) add seasoning and spinich. At rolling boil add pasta. Cover, bring back to boil. Turn off heat. Let sit 15-20minutes covered or until desired constancy.
Enjoy!
