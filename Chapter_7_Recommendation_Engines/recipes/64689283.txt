Title:
Big Ray's Asian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5167248.jpg
Ingredients:
6 bone-in chicken thighs (about 1-3/4 pounds), skin removed
2/3 cup water
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
4 Tbsp orange juice
1/4 cup soy sauce
4 Tbsp ketchup
2 Tbsp white vinegar
6 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 tsp Chinese five-spice powder
4 tsp cornstarch
4 Tbsp cold water
1 large onion, chopped
1 large bell pepper, chopped
1 cup froze peas
sesame seeds, optional
chopped green onions, optional
Hot cooked rice / ramen noodles

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add chicken; cook 8-10 minutes on each side or until golden brown. In a small bowl, whisk water, brown sugar, orange juice, soy sauce, ketchup, vinegar, garlic, pepper flakes and five-spice powder. Place chicken in crock pot, pour mixture over chicken. Cook on low for 5 hours or until chicken is tender. With 2 forks, shred chicken into thin strands.  Add onions, bell pepper and peas to crock pot and cook 1 hour more.
In a small bowl, mix cornstarch and cold water until smooth; stir into crock pot. Cook and stir until sauce is thickened. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and or green onions. If desired, serve with rice or ramen noodles.
NOTE: Can also be made in skillet on stove top if you have no time.
