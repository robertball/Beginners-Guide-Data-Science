Title:
Dairy Free Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups rice milk (coconut, soy, or almond milk will work also)
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the rice milk, vanilla extract, and chocolate chips with a whisk.
2. Stir until chocolate is melted and the mixture is hot. Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
MCakes
Description: 
Dairy allergies? No worries. You can still have a very tasty cup of hot chocolate! This recipe will work great with any kind of dairy free milk. Enjoy!
