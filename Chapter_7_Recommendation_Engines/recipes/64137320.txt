Title:
Simple Cauliflower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1355059.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small can LeSueur young small sweet peas
1 head cauliflower
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 cup finely shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup mayonnaise
1 pkg Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Break cauliflower into small pieces and place in bowl.  Add remaining ingredients.  Mix, cover and let set in refrigerator for at least an hour, best if left overnight.

Submitter: 
Dorothy
Description: 
This is a fast and easy salad.  Friends serve it as a side dish.  I like to eat it all on it's own!
