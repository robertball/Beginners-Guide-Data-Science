Title:
Filet Mignon and Balsamic Strawberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups strawberries, sliced
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 balsamic vinegar
4 fillet mignon steaks, about 6 oz each
salt and pepper to taste
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 Tbsp butter, chilled
fresh ground pepper, to taste

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine strawberries, sugar and vinegar, and let sit for 1 to 3 hours, stirring occasionally.
Sprinkle steaks with salt and pepper and place in oiled skillet over high heat.
Sear each side, and transfer to 400F oven and cook 10 minutes for medium rare.
Transfer to plate, reserving meat juices; tent with foil and let sit for 10 minutes.
Strain strawberries and add to meat juices in small saucepan and heat through, over medium heat.
Add cold butter and roll it around in the pan, without stirring, until the butter is melted.
To serve, arrange filet over dollop of mashed potatoes and top with 1/2 cup of strawberries and freshly ground pepper.
