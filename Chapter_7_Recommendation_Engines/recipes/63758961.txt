Title:
Maple Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1042579.jpg
Ingredients:
• 2 medium acorn squash (about 1-1/2 pounds each)
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 3/4 cup pure maple syrup
• 2 tablespoons unsalted organic butter, melted

Directions:
Cut squash in half lengthwise; remove and discard seeds and membrane. Cut each half widthwise into 1/2-in. slices; discard ends.
Place slices in a large baking dish single layer. Season with salt. Combine syrup and butter; pour over squash.

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
