Title:
Marketing Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Blue Curacao
1 fluid ounce coconut flavored rum
1 fluid ounce vodka
1 dash grenadine syrup
3 fluid ounces champagne

Directions:
In a cocktail mixer, shake together the Blue Curacao, coconut rum, vodka and grenadine. Pour into a champagne flute, and fill glass with champagne.

Submitter: 
Boris
Description: 
A noble drink concocted of Blue Curacao, coconut rum, vodka and champagne. Kinda sweet.
