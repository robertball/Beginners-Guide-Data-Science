Title:
Chicken in Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
1T fresh thyme
1 lemon, juiced
salt/pepper
2T olive oil
3/4c chicken broth
3T Dijon mustard
3T sour cream

Directions:
Season chicken with lemon, salt/pepper, and thyme.
Cook chicken in olive oil.
Remove chicken when fully cooked and cover with foil.
Add chicken broth and mustard; boil, reduce, and simmer 3 minutes.
Whisk in sour cream and cook 2 more minutes.
Serve sauce over chicken and noodles.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description:
