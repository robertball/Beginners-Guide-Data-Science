Title:
Easiest-ever-microwave-omelette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large eggs
1 sausage
1 onion
2 small potatoes(cooked)
2 tomatoes
1 small green pepper
3 mushrooms
3 tbsp milk
1/3 cup grated cheese
3 tbsp butter
1/2 tbsp chives
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
mix eggs, milk, cheese, chives, salt and pepper in a bowl and add finely chopped sausage, onion, potato, tomato, pepper and mushrooms.
put mixture into a greased pie dish and cover with cling wrap. prick.
cook on hich 1 1/2 mins & stir & cover
cook on high 1 3/4 mins & stir
cook (uncovered)for 1 min & serve

Submitter: 
sasa
Description: 
Im not a morning person so i hate to have to work hard before lunch but i love to have a huge breakfast so what i usually make is this easy omlette that anyone can do.
