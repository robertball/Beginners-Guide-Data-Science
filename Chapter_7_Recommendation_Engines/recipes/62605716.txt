Title:
Classic Chicken Scallopini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken breast per couple (split breasts)
Flour, for dredging
1 egg, for egg wash
Salt and Pepper
1 lemon
Large splash of white wine
1/2 cup of chicken broth
Capers, 2 tablespoons
Butter, 1/2 stick
Garlic, 3 or 4 cloves, minced
Can of mushrooms
EVOO
Italian flat leaf parsley, chopped
Shredded parm to top the dish

Directions:
Bring chicken to room temperature
Arrange a plate of flour and a bowl of egg wash for dredging
Butterfly the chicken breasts, separate the halves and in between two pieces of plastic wrap or wax paper andpound to a 1/8 inch thickness all the way through
Salt and pepper the chicken and dip into eggs and coat in flour, set aside to come to room temperature while you make the garlic butter
Mince garlic cloves and smash into softened butter
Melt garlic butter on medium heat in a large skillet  until the butter stops foaming
Take your floured chicken breasts and fry until golden brown  in the garlic butter, set aside and keep warm in the microwave or oven
Boil large pot of water for pasta
Take the chicken drippings and heat back up
Add mushrooms, capers, and lemon and deglaze the pot with white wine and then chicken broth
While you simmer the sauce, boil your pasta
While pasta cooks, continue to simmer the sauce adding EVOO, chicken broth, and/or red wine vinegar to thicken (or flour) throw in the parsley to brighten the color of the sauce
Drain pasta and plate the dish; pasta, chicken and pour sauce over
Top with parmesan and fresh parsley
Hit with one last squeeze of lemon to brighten up the taste
Serve with green salad and crusy sour dough

Description: 
Great lemony italian dish served on linguini or fettucini with a green salad and crusty sourdough.
