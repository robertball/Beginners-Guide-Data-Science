Title:
Pizza Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1 lb country style sausage
3 tbsp dried minced onion
1 tsp garlic powder
1 15 oz can petite diced tomatoes (well drained)
1 14 oz jar pizza sauce (divided in half
2 large eggs
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Set aside the mozzarella cheese and half of the jar of pizza sauce. Combine all of the other ingredients in a large bowl and mix well. Press mixture into a loaf pan and turn out into a shallow baking pan. Bake for approx. an hour and 15 minutes or until cooked through. Remove from heat and top with remaining pizza sauce and mozzarella cheese. Return to oven until cheese is melted and beginning to brown (about 10 minutes)
Remove from oven and allow to sit at room temperature for about 5 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Lindsay L.
Description: 
This meatloaf is a big hit with my family. It's really easy to assemble and makes quite a lot, so our big family can all have seconds and even have some leftover for meatloaf sandwiches.
