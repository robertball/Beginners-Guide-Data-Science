Title:
Asian Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken tenders, cut up in chunks
1 - 2 tbs oil
2 cloves minced garlic
1 cup green onions, chopped
2 cups fresh brocolli (pkgd, already cut)
1 pkg shredded cabbage
2 pkgs Ramen noodles
1 seasoning packet from noodles
1 1/2 cups water
1/2 to 1 tbs hot chile oil
1-2 tbs soy sauce
Thai Vinegar Dressing
1 cup Seasoned rice vinegar
3 cloves garlic (minced and fried in oil)
1 tbs soy sauce
2 dried red chile peppers, cut up

Directions:
Stir fry chicken and onions in oil. Add brocolli and cook till semi tender.
Add cabbage and fry another couple of minutes.  Pour into bowl.  Bring 1 1/2 cups water to boil, add noodes and 1 packet of seasoning only and cook couple of minutes till water is absorbed.  Add mixture in bowl to skillet noodles and add your chili oil and soy sauce.  Heat together about a minute.
Plate your serving and sprinkle with Thai vinegar sauce.
(Note: you could change meat and type of vegetables, but this is my favorite combo)
Thai Vinegar Dressing
Fry garlic in hot oil, remove, and discard oil.
Combine all ingredients and store in refrigerator.  Can be kept for a long time if refrigerated.

Quick and easy stir fry with taste that says you spent hours in the kitchen. Sweet, sour, hot, vinegar sauce is the secret of the entire recipe.
