Title:
Scarlet's Mini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2037395.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Bananas mashed
1/4 cup unsweetened apple sauce
1/4 cup plain (or flavored) Greek yogurt
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
2 teaspoon vanilla
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 and grease mini muffin pan
In a large bowl mash bananas
Whisk into bananas the applesauce, greek yogurt, brown sugar, egg and vanilla.
In a small bowl combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and nutmeg, and mix until combined well.
fold dry mixture into wet, being careful to NOT overmix.
scoop batter 3/4 full into greased mini muffin tin
bake for 10-15 mins (start checking at 10)
remove from oven and remove muffins onto cooling rack promptly so they do not over bake.

Submitter: 
Chef V
Description: 
I created this recipe for my Daughter Scarlet. They are the perfect size for a toddlers hands! These are so versatile you can add any flavor to them to make them different every time. My Daughter is in love with these, and so am i which is why they are Toddler and Mommy approved! Enjoy!
