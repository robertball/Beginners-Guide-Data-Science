Title:
Cranberry Sauce Chicken II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breasts
1 (16 ounce) can cranberry sauce
1 (8 ounce) bottle Russian-style salad dressing
1 packet dry onion soup mix
1 red pepper
1 bunch broccoli
Serve over rice

Directions:
In crockpot, combine chicken, cranberry sauce, Russian dressing, onion soup mix, and red pepper. Cook on low for 6-8 hours, or high for approximately 4, until chicken is tender. 5 minutes prior to serving, steam the broccoli just until tender. Add broccoli to crockpot, and stir. Break the chicken into chunks or bite sized pieces. Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
