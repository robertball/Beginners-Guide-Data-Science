Title:
South African Bobotie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pounds ground meat
2 T butter
1 large onion, chopped
1 thick bread slice, torn into pieces
1 cup 2% milk
1 T apricot jam
4 T lemon juice
3 oz raisins
10 dried apricots, chopped
2 T curry powder
12 almonds, quartered
1 t salt
1/2 t freshly ground black pepper
6 bay leaves
2 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Butter an 8 to 10 cup capacity casserole.
Soak bread in 1/2 of the milk.
Saute the onions in the butter.
Add curry powder, salt and pepper to onions, saute for another minute or so.
Reserve the eggs, remainder of the milk and bay leaves.
Combine remaining ingredients.
Spoon into the prepared casserole and press the bay leaves decoratively on top.
Beat the milk and eggs together, then pour over top (taking care to cover the whole surface).
Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees F.

Submitter: 
squawk93
Description: 
This version of the South African National Dish, Bobotie, is baked in a casserole and served with rice, chutney, shredded coconut and chopped nuts.
