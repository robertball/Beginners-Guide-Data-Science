Title:
tortellini, tomato, and asparagus salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 9 oz packages of cheese tortellini
1 1/2 c of 1.5 in. long slices asparagus
3 TB red wine vinegar
1 TB balsamic vinegar
1 TB olive oil
1/2 t black pepper
4 c arugula
1.5 c tomatoes of choice (cherry/grape nice)
3/4 c grated parmesan
1/2 thin sliced red onion
1/3 c thin sliced fresh basil
1 14 oz can artichoke hearts

Directions:
Cook pasta without salt.  Add asparagus to pasta for the last 2 minutes of cook time.  Drain.
While pasta cooking, whisk vinegars, oil, and pepper, add pasta mixture, arugula, and remaining ingredients and toss to coat.
