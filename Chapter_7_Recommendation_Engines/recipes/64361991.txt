Title:
Easy Blueberry Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2149379.jpg
Ingredients:
250 ml Aunt Jemima Complete pancake and waffle mix
125 ml fresh or frozen thawed blueberries
125 ml low fat 1% milk (added a little at a time)
30 ml Vegetable oil

Directions:
Preheat griddle to 350
in a blender beaker add your blueberries and oil and pancake mix, start blending, then add milk until desired thickness is attained
spray PAM on entire griddle surface or frying pan(on medium low heat), pour pancake batter onto griddle until about 5-6 cm then do same for others
Turn over when bubbles form on top or after about 5-7 minutes

Submitter: 
Tony Carriere
Description: 
while a little high in calories per serving, these pancakes have the antioxidant properties from the blueberries
