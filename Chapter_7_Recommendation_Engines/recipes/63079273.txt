Title:
CRISPY BAKED CHICKEN WINGS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Buffalo Sauce:
1 Tbsp.unsalted butter
1/4 tsp.cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp.pepper
1/4 tsp.kosher salt
1/4 cup Franks hot sauce
Ginger-Soy Glaze:
1/4 cup honey
2 Tbsp.soy sauce
3 large garlic cloves,crushed
1 2x1" piece ginger,peeled,sliced
Wings:
5 lb.drumettes
1 Tbsp.oil
1 Tbsp.kosher salt
1/2 tsp.freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Buffalo sauce:Mix first 4 ingredients in a medium bowl;let stand for 5 minutes.Whisk in  hot sauce;keep warm.
Ginger-Soy Sauce:Bring all ingredients and 1/4 cup water to a bowl in a small saucepan,stirring to dissolve honey.Reduce heat to low,simmer,stirring occasionally,until reduced to 1/4 cup,7-8 minutes.Strain into a medium bowl.Let sit for 15 minutes to thicken slightly;keep warm.
Wings:preheat oven to 400.Set wire rack inside two large baking sheets.Place all ingredients in a large bowl;coat well with oil,salt and pepper.Divide wings between prepared racks and spread out in a single layer.Bake wings until cooked through and skin is crispy,45-50 minutes.
Line another baking sheet with foil;top with wire rack.Add half of wings to ginger-soy glaze and toss to coat.Place wings in a single layer and bake until glaze is glossy and lightly caramelized,8-10 minutes.Toss remaining half of wings in buffalo sauce,no need to bake.Serve immediately.

Description: 
Easy baked wings with buffalo sauce and a ginger-soy glaze.
