Title:
Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can( 15oz) Black Beans, drained and rinsed
1 can (10oz) Rotel Original Diced Tomatos,drained
1/2 cup whole kernal corn ( canned or frozen)
1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
1 jalapeno Pepper, finely diced
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped
1 Tbsp lime juice
1/4 Tsp ground cumin

Directions:
MIx all ingrediaents in a medium bowl. Cover and refrigerate at least 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
Here's To Good Friends
Description: 
A fresh blend of vegetables and beans with the perfect blend of citrus and cumin.
