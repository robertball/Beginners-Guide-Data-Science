Title:
Strawberry Preserves (6-4-58) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart washed berries
2 c sugar

Directions:
combine over low flame and when starts to boil, about 4 minutes, add 2 more cups of sugar and boil another 4 minutes. Put in large bowl and let stand for 3 days. each day at the same time, zigzag the berries with a wooden spoon. on the 3rd day, put in jars cold and cover with foil.
