Title:
Stovetop Chicken Barbecue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 chicken breasts, skinned
1 medium onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 T. margarine
1 c. ketchup
1/2 c. packed brown sugar
3 T. Worcestershire sauce
2 T. prepared mustard
2 T. cider vinegar
1 tsp. Salt

Directions:
In large saucepan, saute onions & garlic in margarine until transparent.  Stir in remainder of ingredients (except chicken).  Add chicken; baste with sauce.  Cook over low heat in covered saucepan for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.  Uncover & reduce sauce.  Shred chicken & toss with sauce.

Submitter: 
Virginia Mom
Description: 
An easy, sweet chicken barbecue for sandwiches.
