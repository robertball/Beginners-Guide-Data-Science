Title:
Stephanie's Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 boneless porkchops
2 Tbsp Salt
2 Tbsp Pepper
1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
1/4 Cup Light Brown Sugar
1 Granny Smith Apple
(peeled/cored and sliced into rings)
8 Strips of bacon
1/4 Cup of Honey

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 400 degrees
Combine salt pepper and garlic powder into a small bowl and season chops front and back.  Then sprinkle with the sugar front and back.
Place in skillet with an apple ring on top of each chop.
Cook just 5min each side until no longer pink on the outside.
Wrap each chop with a strip of bacon and Transfer to a baking dish, then add the honey to the drippings left in the skillet and heat the honey the pour over pork chops, cover and cook for 1 hour!!

Submitter: 
Stephanie
Description: 
These pork chops are loaded with flavor and served over mashed potatoes it will wow any dinner guest!!
