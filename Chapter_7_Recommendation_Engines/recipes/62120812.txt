Title:
Barbeque Ham Sandwiches for a Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. chip chopped ham
Sauce:
3/4 C Vinegar (white or cider)
6 Tbsp. grape jelly
1 1/2 tsp. paprika
1 1/2 tsp. dry mustard
6 Tbsp. brown sugar
6 Tbsp. water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients for the sauce in a pan and simmer 5 minutes.
Allow to cool then add the ham and refrigerate over night.
Next day heat 1 hour over heat and stir. Watch carefully.
Serve on sandwich buns.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
An odd combination of ingredients for a barbeque sauce. Have the deli department in your grocery store chip the ham for you.
Prepare the barbeque and marinate the night  before to allow flavors to blend.
Great idea for the Super Bowl Party and easy to prepare.
