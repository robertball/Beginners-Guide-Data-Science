Title:
Silly Putty Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C all-purpose glue (must contain PVA)
1/2 C liquid laundry starch
food color

Directions:
Pour the glue into a small mixing bowl.
Add food color to the glue.
Slowly add the starch to the glue using your hand to mix the 2 liquids.
Mix for 10-15 minutes to reach the consistency of silly putty.

Submitter: 
PlaygroundHunt
Description: 
Make your own Silly Putty from glue and starch.
