Title:
Sour Cream Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. baking soda
1 cup sour cream
1 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
Topping:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 cup finely chopped nuts, optional

Directions:
Cream butter, sugar and eggs.
Add soda to sour cream. Stir into creamed mixture.
Mix flour, baking powder and salt together. Add to batter.
Stir to mix.
Put HALF batter in greased 9" x 9" pan.
Mix topping ingredients.
Sprinkle 1/2 over batter in pan.
Spoon second half of batter here and there over top. Spread a bit with fingers and back of spoon.
Sprinkle rest of topping over top.
Bake @ 350 x 45 min. or until tests done with toothpick.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
One of our favorites. Layers of moist cake, brown sugar and cinnamon. MMmmm :) This freezes very well too.
