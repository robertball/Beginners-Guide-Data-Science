Title:
Mo's Homemade Chocolate Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp cocoa
1 Tbsp plus 2 tsp water
1 Tbsp oil
1 Tbsp corn syrup
1 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
heat first 4 incredients in saucepan on stove, stir til smooth.  Remove from heat. Blend in 1 cup of powdered sugar. Pour over 8" chocolate cake.  Double recipe for a 9x 13 pan.
