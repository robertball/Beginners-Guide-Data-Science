Title:
Black Bean & Cheese Taco Rollups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/512358.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. can black beans, drained
1/2 cup salsa
1/2 cup-ish sharp cheddar or Mexican blend shredded cheese
4 burrito-size whole-wheat tortillas

Directions:
Mash the black beans in a mixing bowl, then add the salsa. Combine until smooth-ish.
Spread evenly onto the tortillas and top with shredded cheese.
Microwave for 1-2 minutes, or until cheese has melted to your liking.
Serve. Beat the crank-y!
