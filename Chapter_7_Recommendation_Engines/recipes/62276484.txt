Title:
Pink Crush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/348060.jpg
Ingredients:
10 oz. Coconut Rum
16 oz. Strawberry Crush Soda
6 oz. Pineapple Juice

Directions:
Add all of the ingredients into a mixing cup/shaker. Don't shake (it makes the soda fizzy), but slowly tilt the concoction back and forth to mix.
Pour and serve!

A delicious girly drink that everyone will enjoy.  Great for parties.
