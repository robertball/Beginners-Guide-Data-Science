Title:
Cuervo Especial Perfect Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984381.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila
3 ounces Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix
1/2 cup crushed ice (or more)
Salt
Lime wedge

Directions:
Mix all ingredients (except lime and salt) in a blender.
Rub the rim of a glass with lime and then dip into salt to frost.
Pour into glass and garnish with lime wedge.

Submitter: 
Cuervo®
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with the gold standard in tequila, Jose Cuervo Especial® tequila.
