Title:
Authentic, Classic Italian Carpaccio Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2432275.jpg
Ingredients:
500 grams beef fillet, sliced razor thin
1 lemon
100 grams chopped arugula
50 grams shaved Parmigiano Reggiano
3Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil, or more to taste
1/2 Tbsp fresh ground black pepper, or more to taste

Directions:
Squueze some of the lemon on a serving platter and top with a bit of the oil.
Arrange the beef slices in a fan formation on the platter overlapping each slice no more than half of each slice.
Squeeze the rest of the lemon evenly over the meat. Top with with the remainder of the oil. Add the chopped arugula over the meat. Evenly distribute the cheese shavings over the plate. Top everything with fresh ground black pepper.
Allow to rest  in the fridge, covered with plastic wrap for approximately 10 minutes. Serve cool.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Raw, lean beef is sliced thin, topped with lemon, oil, arugula, Parmigiano Reggiano cheese shavings, and black pepper to create the classic, authentic Carpaccio served in Italy. It is used as an appetizer or, especially during the summer, a refreshing light meal.
