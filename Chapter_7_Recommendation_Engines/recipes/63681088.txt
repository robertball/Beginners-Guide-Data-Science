Title:
Aunt Lillian's Squash Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups chopped yellow squash
2 cups chopped onion
4 red bell peppers, seeded & chopped
2 cups white vinegar
2 cups sugar
2 tsp turmeric
2 tsp celery seeds
2 tsp dry mustard

Directions:
Mix all chopped vegetables with the salt. Let stand 2 hours then drain well.
Combine remaining ingredients in a large pot & bring to a boil. Add vegetables & boil 10-15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
Divide between 6 sterile jars & wipe rims. Seal.
