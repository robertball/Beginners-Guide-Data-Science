Title:
Creamsicle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 oz. Grand Mariner
1 oz. Galliano
2 splashs of cream
1 oz. orange juice
dash of grenadine

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients with ice in a shaker. Shake well and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with a wedge of orange.

Submitter: 
JeanieMomof3
Description: 
Tastes a lot like the creamsicle popsicle!
