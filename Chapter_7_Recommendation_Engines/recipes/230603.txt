Title:
Coconut and Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/964373.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut milk
3 scoops vanilla ice cream
2 ripe bananas
2 teaspoons honey (optional)

Directions:
Blend coconut milk, ice cream, bananas, and honey in a blender or food processor until smooth.
