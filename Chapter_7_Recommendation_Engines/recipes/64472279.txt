Title:
Potatoes & More Breakfast Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2510239.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 bag frozen shredded hash browns
4 TBS vegetable oil
1/4 cup diced onion
1/2 cup diced ham
1 cup eggbeaters
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup chopped green onion
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook hash browns on stovetop as directed on bag using vegetable oil in large skillet. Season with salt and pepper to taste.  Once hash browns are fully cooked, push them to one side of the skillet.
On the other side of the skillet, add diced onions and ham and let cook for 2-3 minutes.  Add eggbeaters over onions and ham and scramble over medium low heat, salt and pepper to taste.
Once egg mixture is cooked, stir in with the hash browns and add sour cream, shredded cheddar cheese, and green onion.  Mix until cheese is melted.  Dish out and enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
My family has put together many breakfast skillets over the years, but this recipe is one of my favorites because it combines the flavors of cheesy potatoes with eggs and ham.  My husband has approved this recipe in our household, so we have been making it every weekend!
