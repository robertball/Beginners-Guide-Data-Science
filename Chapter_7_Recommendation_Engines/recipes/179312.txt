Title:
Cuervo Especial Oasis Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986365.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Jose Cuervo Especial
1/2 ounce triple sec
2 ounces cranberry juice
1 ounce orange juice
1 slice lemon

Directions:
Mix ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice.
Shake well and strain into a chilled martini glass.
Garnish with a lemon slice.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
This cocktail is made with Jose Cuervo Especial tequila, triple sec, and orange juice, and adds the fruity tang of cranberry.
