Ingredients:
Leftover Spaghetti
A little leftover sauce
Olive oil
Red pepper flakes
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat olive oil in pan. I like using my cast iron pan for this.
Add spaghetti.
Let spaghetti get crispy.
Add a little sauce to top of spaghetti.
Add red pepper flakes to top of spaghetti.
Add parmesan to top of spaghetti.
Fold spaghetti over to heat up sauce and cheese.
Toss and continue to fry until hot, crispy and cheesy.

Submitter: 
Amy Standaert
Description: 
So much better eating leftover spaghetti this way as opposed to heating it in the microwave!
