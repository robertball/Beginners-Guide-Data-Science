Title:
Baked French Swirl Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf (16 oz.) Pepperidge Farm Cinnamon Swirl Bread; cut into cubes
3/4 cup dried cranberries or raisins
6 eggs, beaten
3 cups Half & Half or milk
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Cinnamon sugar or powdered sugar
Whipped butter
Maple Syrup

Directions:
Place bread cubes and Craisins in greased 3 quart shallow baking dish.
Mix eggs, half & half and vanilla; pour over all
Cover and refrigerate overnight or at least one hour.
Uncover and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until golden brown & set in the center
Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar or powdered sugar
Serve with butter and syrup
Enjoy!!
