Title:
Tuna Tartare Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound very fresh tuna
4 T olive oil
Grated zest of 1 organic lime
3 T fresh lime juice
1/2 t wasabi powder
1 1/4 t soy sauce
6 dashes Tabasco sauce
1 t fresh ground black pepper
salt to taste
1/4 cup minced scallions, white and green parts (2 scallions)
1 minced jalapenos
1 ripe Haas avocado, diced
1 T toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
Dice the tuna to 1/4 inch and place in bowl.
In a small bowl, combine olive oil, lime zest, lime juice, wasabi, soy sauce, Tabasco, salt and pepper.  Pour over Tuna.
Add the scallions and jalapeno, and mix well.
Carefully mix the avocado into mixture.
Add sesame seeds.
Allow the mixture to sit in the fridge for at least an hour for the flavors to blend.  Serve with crackers, blue corn chips or just a fork!

Submitter: 
CookBrooke
Description: 
This is meant to be an appetizer, but I have found this to be my dinner many nights. I downsize by using 4 oz of tuna and cut back on the olive oil.
