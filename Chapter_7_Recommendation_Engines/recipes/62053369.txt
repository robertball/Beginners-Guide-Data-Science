Title:
Old Fashioned Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter,
1 cup sugar,
2 eggs,
1 cup plus 2 tsp flour,
1 TBSP vanilla,
1 cup raisins,
3 TBSP milk more or less,
3 cups quick cook oats.

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar thoroughly.  Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.  Combine dry ingredients, add to creamed mixture, alternating with milk.  Mix well after each addition.  Add other ingredients, and mix.  Drop by teaspoons onto greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees 12-15 minutes.
