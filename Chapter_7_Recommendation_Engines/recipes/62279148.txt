Title:
Burgess Corn Bread (Corn Cake) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup marg
1 egg
1 cup milk
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix everything together pour in a 13x9 pan and cook at 350 for 45 min.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
Serve with chili on a cold day, or as a sweet snack. It has definitely been used as a birthday cake in the Burgess home before!
