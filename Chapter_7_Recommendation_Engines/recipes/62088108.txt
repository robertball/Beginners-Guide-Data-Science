Title:
chicken salad with mango chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
1 T fresh rosemary
2 T red onion
1/4 cup celery
1/2 apple
5 T mayonnaise
2 T Major Grey mango chutney
1 t hot curry powder
juice of 1/2 lime
Salt and fresh pepper to taste.

Directions:
Poach thighs in barely simmering water to which 1 T freshly chopped rosemary has been added, about 5 minutes. Remove thighs and allow to cool. Reserve broth for another use. Chop thighs in chunks; chop vegetables and apples.
Mix all ingredients and allow flavors to blend for a couple of hours.  Serve cold or at room temperature. Dried cranberries, raisins or pineapple chunks can be added. Serves 2-4.

Submitter: 
simone stevin
Description: 
This chicken salad is full of the flavors of India without it being too spicy. So easy and delicious.
