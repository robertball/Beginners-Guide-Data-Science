Title:
small, quick, and easy salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of corn
a head of lettuce
1 cup carrots, sliced
ranch dressing (optional)

Directions:
1.get a medium-sized bowl.
2.chop up the lettuce.
3.toss the lettuce, corn, and the carrots into the bowl. Spread the ranch dressing on it and it's ready to be eaten!!
