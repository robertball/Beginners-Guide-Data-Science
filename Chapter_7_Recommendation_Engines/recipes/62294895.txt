Title:
Cantonese Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (20oz) can pineapple chucks in syrup
3 Tbs. brown sugar, packed
5 Tbs. teriyaki sauce, divided
1 Tbs. vinegar
1 Tbs. tomato ketchup
1 lb. lean ground beef
2 Tbs. minced onion
2 Tbs. cornstarch
1/2 c. water

Directions:
Drain pineapple; reserve syrup. Combine syrup, brown sugar, 3 Tbs. teriyaki, vinegar and ketchup; set aside. Mix beef with remaining 2 Tbs. teriyaki and onion. Shape meat into 20 meatballs. Brown meatballs in skillet and drain. Pour syrup mixture over meatballs; simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Dissolve conrstarch in water; stir into skillet with pineapple. Cook and stir into skillet until sauce thickens and pineapple is heated through.
