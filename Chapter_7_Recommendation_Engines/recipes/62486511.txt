Title:
Caramel Apple Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/489305.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package (18-1/4 ounces) spice cake mix
2 cups chopped peeled tart apples
20 caramels
3 tablespoons milk
1 cup finely chopped pecans, toasted
Popsicle sticks

Directions:
Prepare cake batter according to package directions; fold in apples
Fill greased or lined cupcake tins 3/4 full. Bake at 350* for 18 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan to wire racks to cool completely
In a saucepan, cook the caramels and milk over low heat until smooth. Spread over cupcakes. Sprinkle with toasted chopped pecans. Insert a wooden popsicle stick into the center of each cupcake.
