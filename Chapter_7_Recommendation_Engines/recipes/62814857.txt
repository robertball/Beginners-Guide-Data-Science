Title:
Chicken Broccoli Penne Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons butter
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound fresh broccoli florets
1 1/2 cup vegetable broth
3 tablespoons dried basil
1 pound penne pasta
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
3 tablespoons garlic butter
1/2 pound grilled chicken, cut into bite size pieces

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain.
In large skillet heat oil and butter. Gently brown garlic, add broccoli and saute gently for 2 to 3 minutes. Add broth; cover and simmer until broccoli is tender.
Toss the broccoli mixture, grilled chicken, and the garlic butter with the basil and cooked pasta. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese on top.

Submitter:

Description: 
Broccoli florets are sauteed with garlic, butter and olive oil, simmered in broth, then tossed with fresh basil and hot penne. A sprinkle of Parmesan cheese is the perfect finish. Was a great main dish served with a side salad and garlic toast!Modified from: Broccoli with Rigatoni
