Title:
Black Bean & Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16-oz bag frozen corn
1 C black beans
1 large tomato, chopped
1/2 red onion, finely chopped
1 T fresh cilantro
juice from 1 lime
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Defrost corn.  Toss all items together in a medium sized bowl, and add salt and pepper as needed.

Tricia Lott Williford

Great and easy.  Gluten-free and Weight Watchers approved.
