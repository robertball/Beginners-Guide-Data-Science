Title:
Easy Mini-Pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984432.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 ounce) package Lawry's® Original Spaghetti Sauce Spices & Seasonings
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
1/4 teaspoon Lawry's® Garlic Powder With Parsley
4 English muffins, halved
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar or mozzarella cheese
Your favorite pizza toppings

Directions:
In 2-quart saucepan, combine Lawry's(R) Original Spaghetti Sauce Spices & Seasonings, tomatoes and Lawry's(R) Garlic Powder With Parsley. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer covered, stirring occasionally, 20 minutes.
Broil muffin halves until lightly browned. Evenly top each muffin half with sauce, then sprinkle with cheese and toppings. Broil until cheese is melted.

Cheesy little English muffin pizzas are fun to make and even more fun to eat.
