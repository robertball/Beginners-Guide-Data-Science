Title:
Lemon Dainty Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/786668.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 C. flour
1 C. sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1-1/2 tsp. grated lemon rind
1/4 C. lemon juice
2 egg yolks
1 C. milk (I use skim)
2 egg whites

Directions:
Mix in bowl flour, sugar and salt. Stir in lemon rind, lemon juice, egg yolks well beaten and milk. Fold in 2 egg whites stiffly beaten. Pour in 1-qt. baking dish or six custard cups or ramekins. Set in pan of hot water (1 " deep). Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes. Serve warm or cold.

Submitter: 
syl
Description: 
A light, lemon dessert! Delicious!  I usually double the recipe and use ramekins to bake it in. My mother used to make this, and it has been a favorite with our family or anyone we serve it to.
