Title:
Holiday Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1076207.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 (2.5 ounce) package smoked sliced beef, chopped
3 green onions, finely chopped
1 dash hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, beef, onions and pepper sauce with hands or an electric mixer.  Shape into a ball.  Chill at least 1 hour before serving with crackers.

Submitter: 
Vikki Sorensen
Description: 
This is a cheese ball that is great for an appetizer at a party.  You can dress is up with nuts on the outside or a little extra hot pepper sauce for that extra zing!!
