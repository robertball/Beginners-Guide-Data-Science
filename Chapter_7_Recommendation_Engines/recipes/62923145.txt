Title:
country fried chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
three boneless chicken breasts butterflied
3 cups milk
3 teaspoons of onion powder
3 teaspoons of salt
3 teaspoons of pepper
3 teaspoons of cajun seasoning
2 tablespoons of hot sauce
2 cups of flour
1/2 cup of liquid smoke type steak marinade (dales)

Directions:
take the split breast and place flat in a large freezer bag and lightly pound them down to tenderize not to much so they don't fall apart
add 2 cups milk, 1 teaspoon of each dry seasoning,1 tablespoon of the hot sauce, and 1/4 cup of the marinade into the bag with the chicken, close and shake well to blend.Let marinade for 2 hours
Add 1/2 of the left over dry seasonings to the flour
the remaining milk dry seasonings and liquid smoke marinade get mixed together to batter the chicken with.
Take the chicken from the bag put into the seasoned flour coat well then dip into the milk mix and back into the flour again coat well then slowly place into the fryer for about 10 min flip one time to brown each side evenly take out let rest for a few min then serve.
