Title:
Creamed Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup peas
1 1/C. water
1/4 tsp salt
1 TBSP flour
Milk just below cooked peas
1 tbsp butter

Directions:
Boil peas in water and salt til just done
Drain
add flour and stir into peas in pan
Add milk & butter and cook till thickened.

Submitter: 
joyce o
Description: 
This is a quick and easy way to make this side dish for 2 people without all the fuss.
