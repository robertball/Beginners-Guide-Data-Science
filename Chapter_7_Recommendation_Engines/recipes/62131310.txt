Title:
4 Cornish Game Hens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cornish hens
1 yellow onion, peeled, cut and cut into quarters
1 cup honey mustard salad dressing

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
Clean the Cornish hens and slightly dry them.  Put 1/4 of the onion inside.  Then bake them on a foil covered cookie sheet.
Put them in a preheated oven and baste them quite often with the honey mustard salad dressing.
They will be done in 1 or 1 i/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Linda Grein
Description: 
These are good tasting and very fast to prepare.  You should serve them with a side dish of wild rice.
