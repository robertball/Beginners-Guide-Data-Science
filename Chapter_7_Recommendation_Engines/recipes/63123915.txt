Title:
Mexican Chicken Soup 17DD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1025380.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T. olive oil
1 medium onion, diced
3-4 sticks of celery, diced, including leaves
4 cups chicken stock
2 cups water
1 14 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 1/2 cups shredded, cooked chicken
*1/2 cup frozen peas
*1/2 cup frozen corn
1 T. taco seasoning
1 t. cumin
1/4 t. black pepper
a little salt, if your stock doesn't have any

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute onion and celery in olive oil until tender.
Add all other ingredients.
Bring to a boil.
Lower temperature, cover and simmer for 20 - 30 minutes.
For cycle 1, omit peas and corn.
