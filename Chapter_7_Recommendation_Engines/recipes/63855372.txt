Title:
Grandma's Sour Cream Belgian Cottage Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg yolk
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup milk
3 tbsp butter, melted
1 cup flour
2 tsp sugar
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1 egg white, stiffly beaten

Directions:
Preheat waffle iron.
Put all ingredients except egg white into a large mixer bowl. Beat on low until moist. Beat on medium until smooth. Fold in beaten egg white.
Pour 1/2 of batter onto iron. Bake for 3-5 minutes.
Serve with 100% pure maple syrup and seasonal berries or fruit.
