Title:
Gina's Ceviche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 	Head of garlic (diced as fine as possible)
1/2 	Tsp salt
4 - 6 	Raw scallops (large)
8	Raw shrimp (medium size)
3	Green onions (use onion, not whole green tops)
3	Roma tomatoes (de-seeded)
1/2 	Cucumber (de-seeded)
2	Jalepeno pepper (remove seeds to achieve preferred “heat”)
Lemon pepper and fresh ground pepper to taste
Optional:  Avacado  ¼ - diced small
Cilantro  stripped and minced

Directions:
In medium container (with cover) mix garlic and 1/2 t salt; Mix together and mash to paste
Dice into very small pieces the raw scallops
and shrimp; stir in with garlic/salt mixture.
Squeeze enough fresh lime juice to just cover seafood and refrigerate for approximately 4-6 hrs
Also dice (in same small pieces as seafood) green onions (use onion, not whole green tops), 3 roma tomatoes (de-seeded), 1/2 Cucumber (de-seeded), 2 jalepeno pepper (remove seeds to achieve preferred “heat”)
Lemon pepper and fresh ground pepper to taste
Add Avacado – 1/4 diced (optional)
Add vegetables to seafood mixture after about 3-4 hours (the shrimp should be getting “pink”,)
Add Cilantro – stripped and minced – right before serving.
Serve with tortilla chips or freshly fried corn tortillas and hot sauce.
