Title:
Strawberry Pecan Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (11.5 oz.) frozen pecan coffee cake OR make one yourself using this recipe:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/8 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 cup butter
3/4 cup sour cream
1/2 cup white flour
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
TOPPING:
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1 teaspoons cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1 tablespoon melted butter
FILLING:
8 oz frozen light nondairy whipped topping (or make your own)
1 pint (2 1/2 cups) strawberries, thinly sliced
1/4 cup liquid French vanilla coffee creamer

Directions:
COFFEE CAKE:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Coat 9x round cake pan with cooking spray. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt together. Cream butter and beat in sour cream and sugar. Stir in eggs, then add vanilla. Mix. Fold in flour mixture until just combined. Pour into prepared pan.
TOPPING:
Stir together cinnamon, nutmeg, brown sugar, and chopped pecans. Mix in melted butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over cake batter. Bake 20-25 minutes until toothpick comes out clean. Let cool for 10 minutes, then put in freezer until frozen. Then make the following:
Place whipped topping in bowl to soften. Remove cake from freezer and cut into 1 inch cubes. Slice berries.
Whisk creamer into topping until smooth.
Layer in dessert bowl, one half EACH of the cake cubes, berries, and topping; repeat. Chill until ready to serve.
