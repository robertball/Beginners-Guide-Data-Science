Title:
Sausage Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Butternut squash
1 tbsp. Sugar
1 Onion
1 Pkg. Jimmy Dean Sage Sausage
1 Doz. Baby Potatoes cubed or 2 reg. potatoes
2 Sweet Potatoes cubed
4 Stalks Celery chopped
1/2 C. Cream
3 C. Milk
1 tbsp. Butter
2 tsp. Rosemary
2 tsp. Sage
2 tsp. Thyme
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Peel butternut squash and cube.  Boil squash with sugar for 15-20 minutes until squash is tender.  Place squash and 1/2 of cooking liquids in blender or food processor and blend until smooth, set aside.
In a skillet, brown sausage, once brown add onion, celery and herbs.  Salt and peper to taste. Add squash to sausage mixture.
Meanwhile, boil baby potatoes and sweet potatoes with peel. Add potatoes to sausage and squash mixture.
Add cream and butter, mix.  Add 3 cups of milk to make more of a soup consistency. Again salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
TaraV1976
Description: 
This soup is a fall dish with squash, sweet potatoes, fresh herbs and sausage.  I altered a recipe I had for squash soup that was blended.  I wanted the soup to be chunky and hardy.
