Title:
Easy Slow Cooker French Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds roast (sirloin tip, chuck, rump)
1 (10.5 ounce) can beef broth or beef consomme
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
1/2 can or bottle (12 fluid ounce) heavy beer
2 Cloves minced garlic
1/2 tsp. Garlic powder
1/2 tsp. Onion powder
Pepper
1 Tbl. Worchestershire sauce
1 Jar sliced mushrooms
Salt, to taste
6 French rolls (or kaiser rolls, focaccia, etc.)
2 tablespoons butter (optional)
Provolone cheese (optional)

Directions:
Trim excess fat from the roast, and place in a slow cooker. Add the beef broth, onion soup, garlic, garlic powder, onion powder, mushrooms, and beer. Cook on Low setting for 7 hours.
About an hour before serving, remove beef from slow cooker and slice on the diagonal.  Add salt to taste to liquid and stir.  Put meat back into slow cooker and cook for another 45 minutes to an hour.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Split French rolls or slice other bread.  Spread bread with your choice of: (1) rub with cut side of garlic clove, (2) spread with butter, (3) cover with provolone cheese or whatever you like.  Bake 10 minutes, or until heated through.
Place sliced meat on bread. Serve the sauce for dipping.
