Title:
Stained Glass Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c graham wafer crumbs
1/2 c melted butter
1/2 c w sugar
1- 3 oz pkg orange jello
1-3 oz pkg lime jello
1- 3 oz pkg raspberry jello
1 pkg unflavored gelatin (1 tbsp)
1/4 c cold water
1 cup hot pineapple juice
2 c whipping cream
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 c sugar

Directions:
Combine well and press 2/3 mixture onto bottom of 10 inch springform pan. Chill...set aside remaining crumbs to sprinkle over top at end of recipe.
Dissolve each package of jello seperately in 1 cup of boiling water, pour each one into a sprayed pie pan and chill until firm which is about 2 hours.  Cut into one inch cubes.
Soften the gelatin in 1/4 c cold water, then add hot pineapple juice, stir until gelatin is dissolved. Chill until partially set...about 1/2 hour.
Whip the cream, gradually adding the sugar and vanilla until soft peaks appear. Fold  pineapple gelatin mixture into the whipped cream. Then gently fold in jello cubes. Pour onto prepared graham crust in spring form pan. Sprinkle with remaing crumbs....
Chill at least 6 hours, when firm, slice and serve.....amazing!!!
