Title:
Chicken Crescent Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of low fat refrigerator crescents
1 can of healthy low sodium cream of chicken soup(26 oz)
1 cup fat free cheddar cheese
2 boneless skinless chicken breast

Directions:
roll out each individual crescent
place about a Tbsp of shredded chicken in center and roll it up.
Bake on 350 for about 5 minutes until just starting to turn golden.
Pour Cream of chicken over the top bake an additional 10 min
top with cheddar cheese and bake for 10 more minutes
