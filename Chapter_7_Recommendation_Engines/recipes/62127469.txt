Title:
Vegetarian Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can unflavored tomato sauce or puree (28 oz)
1 cup fat free half and half
1 tbsp turmeric, ground
1 tbsp cumin, ground
1 tbsp coriander, ground
1 tbsp paprika
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1 small bunch scallions, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 shallots, minced
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp butter
1 package vegetarian chicken tenders
1 small bunch fresh cilanto, julienned

Directions:
Heat hald of the oil and butter over medium heat.
Add half of garlic & shallots, sautee until golden (5min).
Add half of all spices except cilantro.  Mix well.
Heat while stirring for 2 min.
Add fake chicken tenders and toss to coat.
Heat while stirring for about 7 min or until cooked through.
Remove fake chicken from pan and set aside.
To same pan add the rest of the oil and butter.
Add the rest of the garlic and shallots and sautee 5 min.
Add can of tomato sauce and mix well.
Add rest of herbs and mix well.
Simmer over low heat for 10 min.
Add cooked fake chicken, and simmer for about 10 min.  Alternately, you can stop here, and then put everything in a crock pot on low for several hours.
Substitutions:
1 shallot = 1 very small onion
1 bag fake chicken = 1 lb chicken tenders
1 bunch fresh cilantro = 1 tbsp dried
Try also with blanched cauliflower florets or another substantial root vegetable, or even leftover roasted potatoes.

Submitter: 
KitchenWitch
Description: 
This is a nice belly-warmer made with typical Indian style curry ingredients.  Great for crock pots.  I use faux chicken (like Quorn) but have had great success with paneer, fried tofu, even cauliflower.  Serve over white rice of some sort.
