Title:
Cucumber Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two cucumbers, cut thin
1 onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup white distilled vinegar (may use cider or rice vinegar if desired)
1-3 tablespoons sugar (to taste)
1 tsp salt
Freshly ground pepper to taste
One tsp celery seed, dill weed or mustard seed, if desired.

Directions:
Soak sliced cucumber in salted ice water.  Chill and drain.
Mix  remaining ingredients well and combine with cucumbers.  Serve.
