Title:
Almond Joy Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3356995.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Gluten Free oats
1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
1/2 tbsp cocoa powder
2 tbsp Silk Almond Vanilla Creamer
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
optional toppings: cacao nibs, coconut flakes, fresh berries

Directions:
Add all ingredients together in an air tight container. Place in the refrigerator overnight or 1-2 hours prior to blending to thicken.
Once the ingredients have thickened, add them to a blender and blend on high until creamy.
Pour blended smoothie in a glass, top with optional toppings as desired and devour!

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Start your day with a thick and creamy Chocolate Almond Joy Smoothie more like desire than breakfast. This gluten free, low calorie smoothie is what dreams are made of!
