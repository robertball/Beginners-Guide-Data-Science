Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 firm apples, peeled, and sliced,
1 tsp cinnamon,
1/4 tsp nutmeg,
1 tsp lemon juice,
1 cup sugar,
1/4 cup flour,
1/4 cup butter.

Directions:
Place sliced apples into a 1 quart baking dish.   Sprinkle with spices and lemon juice.  Mix sugar, flour, and butter until crumbly.  Spread over the apples and bake uncovered 45 minutes at 1175 degrees until apples are tender.  If mixture becomes dry while cooking, add a small amount of water.  Serve with whipped cream.
