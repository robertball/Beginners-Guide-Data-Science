Title:
Beef Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ pound ground beef
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
½ teaspoon onion powder
½ teaspoon paprika
½ teaspoon ground cumin
Fixings:
Tortilla chips
Chopped tomatoes
Chopped peppers
Corn
Cheese
Guacamole
Salsa
Sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Brown the beef with chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika and cumin.
Drain fat.
On a baking sheet, layer Tortilla chips, ground beef, tomatoes, peppers,corn and sprinkle on cheese.
Bake for 5 minutes until cheese is melted.
Layer another layer and bake until cheese is melted.
Serve with guacamole, salsa and sour cream.
