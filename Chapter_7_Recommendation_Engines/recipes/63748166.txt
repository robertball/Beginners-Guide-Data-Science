Title:
Taco Rice and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1040206.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups long grain brown rice, rinsed
4 cups water
1 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp butter
1 small onion, chopped
2 green bell peppers, chopped
5 cloves garlic, minced
*1 packet taco seasoning mix
1 can black beans, rinsed
1 can pinto beans, rinsed
1 cup frozen corn
salt and pepper to taste
cheese (optional)

Directions:
Saute onion and green bell pepper in 1 Tbsp. of olive oil over medium heat.
Once onions are translucent, add brown rice, garlic and butter, and lightly toast for 3-5 minutes.
Add taco seasoning and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
Add water and bring to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat and simmer 45 minutes, or until water is absorbed.
Add black beans, pinto beans and the corn, and cook until heated through.
Top with cheese if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick and easy meal that the whole family can enjoy!  My kids love this and look forward to leftovers.  This can be eaten alone, or thrown in a tortilla with a little cheese and sour cream.
