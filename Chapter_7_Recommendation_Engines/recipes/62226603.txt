Title:
Peanut Butter Ball Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/319202.jpg
Ingredients:
1 jar (16-18oz.)good brand peanut butter(Jiff, Skippy)
1 stick softened butter
1 pound box of confectionery sugar
24 oz. Nestle Chocolate morsels

Directions:
Mix peanut butter, butter, & confectionery sugar together, (easier if you use your clean hands). Form into bite sized balls.
In double boiler, melt chocolate morsels, stirring constantly. Dip balls in chocolate, rolling to cover. Use a slotted spoon to remove to waxed paper. Refrigerate to harden.
