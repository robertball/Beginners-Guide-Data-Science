Title:
Belgium Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pd. butter
6 eggs
32ozs. Soft silk cake floour
3c. Sugar
2c. Brown sugar
1/2t. Salt
1T. Vanilla
1 1/2 shots whiskey

Directions:
1. Melt butter mix with sugars whisk in salt and vanilla. Stir in 1 egg at a time, then flour. Refrigerate overnight.
2. Roll into snakes the diameter of a nickel that will fit on the iron. then regrig. Place dough on heated and greased iron. Cook for aprox. A minute and a half. Hint:recite the Hail Mary prayer. (Kills 2 birds with one stone!!!)cookies made and the rosary prayed!!!
