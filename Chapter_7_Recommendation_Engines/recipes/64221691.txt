Title:
Bread Machine Jalapeno Cheddar Bread (1.5 lb Loaf) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1654572.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup of warm water (115° to 125°)
1 Tablespoon softened butter
¾ cup of diced jalapenos
1 cup Shredded sharp cheddar cheese
¼ cup dry milk powder
1 Tsp Salt
2 Tablespoons white sugar
3 cups bread machine flour
1 ¼ Tsp bread machine yeast

Directions:
1.Add all ingredients to the bread pan (following the order listed above or as your bread machine manufacturer recommends). Observe the dough ball during the second kneading cycle. If it is too wet or sticky add more flour a tablespoon at a time until the ball is smooth.

Submitter: 
Kaitlyn Jewels
Description: 
I adapted this recipe from an old cheddar bread recipe. I love Jalapeno pretzels so I wanted to make a Jalapeno Cheddar bread and it turned out great. It makes an awesome roast beef sandwich!
