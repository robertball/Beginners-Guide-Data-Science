Title:
Dried Beef Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sour cream
2 cups mayonnaise
2 packages sliced dried beef, finely chopped (2.5 oz each)
1/2 teaspoon dried dill weed
2 teaspoons prepared horseradish
Rye crackers, bagel chips, bread bowl, or bite-size fresh veggies

Directions:
Stir together sour cream, mayo, horseradish, and dill weed in medium bowl. Stir in beef.
Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour, but no longer than 48 hours.
Serve dip with cracker, bread or veggies.
Shrimp Dip: Substitute 2 cans (4 oz each) tiny shrimp, rinsed and drained, for the beef.

Submitter: 
Michele Mathis Lavell
Description: 
This is a yummy beef dip that we make for every party. Serve it with pieces of pumpernickel bread, or in a bread bowl made from a round pumpernickel loaf.  Very easy and tasty!Modified from: Dried Beef Dip
