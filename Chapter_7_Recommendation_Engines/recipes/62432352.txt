Title:
Best Banana Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451870.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
3 bananas, mashed
2 eggs
1 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup walnuts (optional)
FROSTING:
6 tbs butter,softened
3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar. Stir in mashed banana. Beat in eggs. Add flour and baking soda. Blend. Spread into a 9 / 13 baking pan and bake for 20 minutes or until a wooden toothpick comes out clean. Cool completely before frosting.
Mix butter and powdered sugar together. Add milk and vanilla.

Submitter: 
Christa
Description: 
These banana bars are quick and easy to make with only 6 ingredients. They are gooey and oh-so-good. Perfect for brightening someone's day!
