Title:
Lemon Bars for Dumbies... Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lemon Cake Mix
3 eggs
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 20 oz. can crushed pineapple with juice
1/4 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°
Mix cake mix, eggs and oil until smooth.  Fold in pineapple (juice included).
Bake in large jelly roll pan for 30 minutes, or until middle bounces back, or toothpick comes out clean.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
