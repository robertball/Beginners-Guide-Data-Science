Title:
Bowl of Oatmeal Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg beaten
2/3 cup milk
1 cup + 3 Tbsp milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 Tbsp brown sugar
1 dab of butter
salt to taste
handful of raisins (optional)

Directions:
In a microwave safe bowl, beat one egg.  Then add the rest of the ingredients and mix well.  Microwave on high 1 minute.  Stir.  Microwave on high 1 more minute.  Stir.  Microwave on high 1 more minute.  Done!

Submitter: 
foodiewendy
Description: 
A spruced up version of a bowl of oatmeal that tastes more like an oatmeal cookie!  I make mine in the microwave for a quick and easy breakfast.
