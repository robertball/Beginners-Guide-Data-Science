Title:
The Best Crockpot Baked Beans (Dad's Recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1110901.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Cups Water, more if needed
1 Pound Dried Navy Beans(soaked overnight) and simmered for 1 hour
1 Medium Onion,sliced
1/4 pound Salt Pork,thinly sliced
2 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup Molasses
1 Teaspoon Salt
3/4 Teaspoon Dried Mustard
1/8 Teaspoon Pepper

Directions:
Soak Beans overnight.
Heat beans in water; cover and simmer 1 hour. or until slightly tender.
Combine brown sugar, molasses, salt, dry mustard pepper with water. Stir to combine.
Add beans, onion and salt pork to crockpot.
Add the rest of the ingredients and enough water to cover beans, stir well to combine.
cover and cook on low for 8 to 10 hours.

Submitter: 
Sandy Lafleur
Description: 
This recipe was handed down from my dad. I adjusted it to go into the slow cooker. It's a lot easier. just set it and forget it.
