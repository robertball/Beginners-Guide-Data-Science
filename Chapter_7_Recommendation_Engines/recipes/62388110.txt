Title:
Chicken with Lemon Cumin and Mint Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/577106.jpg
Ingredients:
zest of 1 lemon
1/3 cup lemon juice
4 tbsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp chopped fresh mint (2 tsp dry)
1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp salt
8 chicken thighs

Directions:
In a roasting pan combine lemon zest, juice, oil, garlic, mint, cumin, coriander, salt and pepper; add chicken and turn to coat well.  Cover and refrigerate for 4 hours.
Bake at 375 F, basting twice with pan juices for about 40 minutes until juices run clear when chicken is pierced.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
This bold flavourful chicken is great on it's own or shredded in a pita with lettuce and hummus.
