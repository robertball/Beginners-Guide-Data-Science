Title:
Easy Coleslaw Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1967016.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon vinegar
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.

Submitter: 
GarlicQueen
Description: 
A creamy coleslaw dressing that can be made with ingredients you already have! You can pour it immediately over coleslaw or refrigerate until needed.
