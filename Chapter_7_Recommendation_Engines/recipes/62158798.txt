Title:
Creamy Homemade Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
2 heaping cups sugar
1/2 gallon milk
12 marshmallows
1 lg can of Pet milk
1 tbsp. vanilla

Directions:
Beat together eggs & sugar in bowl.
Add 1/2 gallon milk.
Put in large pan with 12 marshmallows & cook until marshmallows melt.
Remove from heat.
Add 1 large can Pet milk & vanilla.
Let cool & then pour into ice cream maker.
