Title:
Sugar Cookie for one Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/868128.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons of butter (Softened)
3 Tablespoons of white sugar
1 egg's egg whites
1/8 Teaspoon Vanilla
1/3 Cup Flour

Directions:
In a small microwavable dish mix the butter and sugar
Then add egg whites and vanilla
Finally stir the flour in.
Microwave for 50-55 seconds on high.
Let cool until you feel comfortable eating it.

Submitter: 
ST. GEORGE
Description: 
I found a single serve microwaved chocolate chip cookie, and I figured out how to make a sugar cookie the same way.
