Title:
Baked Chicken Schnitzel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2116367.jpg
Ingredients:
6 chicken breast, cut in half length wise(butterflied)
3/4 cup flour
2 eggs (whisked)
2 Cups seasoned breadcrumbs
zest of 1 large lemon
1 tablespoon paprika
olive oil for drizzling.
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Line a large baking sheet with tinfoil(for easy clean up), and drizzle with oil(a thin layer), preheat oven to 425 and place tray in oven.
Flatten out chicken breast halves so they are all the same width about 1/4 inch
season both sides of chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
mix flour with paprika on a large plate
season eggs with salt and pepper and whisk together in a shallow bowl
place bread crumbs on a large plate, and mix in the zest of 1 lemon.
dredge each piece of chicken in flour, egg, and then breadcrumbs, and set aside on a clean plate.
continue for all pieces of chicken.
When oven is preheated, carefully remove pan from oven and place chicken on in one single layer.
drizzle tops of chicken with oil, and return to oven
cook for 5-6 minutes, flip, and return to oven for another 5-6 minutes.
Enjoy with fresh squeezed lemon juice.

Submitter: 
Chef V
Description: 
Growing up chicken Schnitzel was a classic. I decided to make this dish oven friendly using less oil, and an easier cleanup. This dish tastes great with potato salad, or mashed potatoes and a nice crisp salad. Tastes great the next day cold too! Its a family Favorite!
