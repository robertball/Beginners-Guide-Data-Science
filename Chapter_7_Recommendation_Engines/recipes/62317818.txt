Title:
Homemade Sour Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Heavy Cream
1/4 Cup Milk
1/4 TBS Vinegar

Directions:
Mix milk and vinegar in the cup and let stand for 10 minutes.
Pour heavy cream in the jar, and add the milk mixture. Combine well and let stand at room temperature for 24 hours.
Chill the homemade sour cream before use.

Submitter: 
Celestial
Description: 
A lot of cake and cookie recipes call for sour cream which might not be readily available at home. This quick fix tastes exactly like the standard store-bought sour cream and is easy to make.
