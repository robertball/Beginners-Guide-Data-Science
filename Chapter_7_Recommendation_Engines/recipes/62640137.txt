Title:
Crock Pot Cranberry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cranberry juice
1 bottle spiced apple wine
1/2 cup sugar
one orange
whole cloves
3 cinnamon sticks
1 apple

Directions:
In a crock pot heat 2 cups cranberry juice with 1 bottle spiced apple wine.  Add 1/2 cup sugar.  Float a clove-studded orange and cinnamon sticks, top with fresh apple slices.  Serve warm.

A warm alcoholic beverage, great for holidy open-houses or any group gathering.
