Title:
Steel-Cut Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 cups water
1 dash salt
2 cups steel-cut oats
1 cup raisins
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2/3 cup honey

Directions:
Bring water and salt to a boil in a saucepan. Stir oats into boiling water.
Cook oats until thick and soft, 20 to 30 minutes.
Stir raisins, vanilla extract, and cinnamon into cooked oats; continue cooking 10 minutes more, stirring frequently.
Pour honey over the oats to serve.
