Title:
Pina Colada Pie- Lightened Up! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 gingersnap cookies
1 TBL sugar
1 can (8 oz) pineapple slices in juice
1 TBL cornstarch
1/4 tsp grated lemon zest
1 TBL butter, melted
3 pkgs (8 oz each) 1/3 less fat cream cheese
1 1/2 tsp coconut extract
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 pkgs (1 oz each) instant sugar-free, fat-free white chocolate pudding mix
2 cups cold fat-free milk

Directions:
1. In food processor, process cookies and sugar until fine crumbs form; transfer to bowl. Reserve. Wipe processor clean; add pineapple slices and juice. Puree; add cornstarch and 3 TBL water. Pulse until smooth; transfer to microwave safe bowl. Add zest. Microwave on High in 1-minute intervals, stirring until thickened, 6 minutes. Cool 15 nminutes, stirring often. Refrigerate 20 minutes.
2. Coat 9" springform pan with cooking spray. Stir butter and 1 TBL water into reserved crumb mixture; press into bottom of pan.
3. On medium speed, beat cream cheese and extracts until fluffy, 2-3 minutes.
4. In bowl, whisk together pudding mix and milk; let stand 5 minutes until thickened. Fold pudding into cream cheese mixture; spread half in pan. Dollop 2/3 of pineapple mixture on top. Gently spread remaining batter over; dollop on remaining pineapple mixture. Using knife, lightly swirl pineapple mixture into batter. Cover. Refrigerate overnight.
