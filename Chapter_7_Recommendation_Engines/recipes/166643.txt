Title:
Cuervo Perfect Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983189.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila
3 ounces Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix
1 cup ice (or more)
Salt
Lime wedge

Directions:
Rub the rim of a chilled margarita glass with the lime and dip it into the salt to coat it.
In a cocktail shaker, combine Jose Cuervo Especial, Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix and ice.
Shake vigorously and strain drink into the garnished glass filled with ice.

Submitter: 
Cuervo®
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with the gold standard in tequila, Jose Cuervo Especial® tequila.
