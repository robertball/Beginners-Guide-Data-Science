Title:
Patty's Spicey Pixley Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/640843.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound sliced bacon
3 pounds boneless skinless chicken breast halves, cut into 2-inch chunks
60 wooden toothpicks
1/2 cup brown sugar
4 or 5 raw jalapeno's (seeds removed) sliced into 1/4's
1 (1 ounce) envelope ranch dressing mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
Mix together the sugar and ranch dressing mix in a shallow bowl.
Cut the bacon slices in half the short way,lay a chunk of chicken and a slice of jalapeno pepper on each piece of bacon and secure it with a toothpick. Roll the bacon-wrapped chicken pieces in the sugar-ranch dressing mixture.
Place the bacon wraps on the prepared baking sheets, and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through, the bacon is sizzling, and the crust is browned.

Submitter: 
Patty Cakes
Description: 
Bacon is wrapped around chunks of chicken breast and dredged in ranch flavor for a sweet, savory, simple appetizer. I added sliced peppers to heat them up:)Modified from: Spicey Pixley Sticks
