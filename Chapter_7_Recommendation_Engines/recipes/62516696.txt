Title:
Walking Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Beef
1 can tomato sauce
1/8 tsp. ground cumin
1/8 tsp. garlic salt
Or your favorite taco seasonings...
Shredded Cheddar Jack Cheese
Shredded lettuce
Diced Olives
Salsa
Sour Cream
Single serve bags of Doritos or Fritos Chips. (Foil Bags)

Directions:
Prepare Taco Meat: Brown Ground beef and add tomato sauce and seasonings.  Simmer til thickened.
Serve:  Before opening bag of chips crush lightly with hands.  Open bag and Add all of your favorite taco ingredients...taco meat, cheese, lettuce, etc.
Grab a plastic fork and dinner is on the run.

Submitter: 
J. Lynn
Description: 
These are a great, kid friendly, on the go meal.
