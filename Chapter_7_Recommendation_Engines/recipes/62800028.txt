Title:
Feta- Stuffed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 teaspoon finely shredded lemon peel
1 Tablespoon olive oil

Directions:
1.  Using a sharp knife, cut a pocket in each chicken breast by cutting horizontally through the thickest portion to, but not through, the opposite side.  In a small bowl combine 1/4 cup of the feta cheese and the lemon peel.  Spoon cheese mixture into each pocket.  Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper
2.  In a large skillet heat oil over medium heat.  Add chicken. Cook for 18 to 20 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink, turning once.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
Excellent chicken dish.  Flavors mix to give a tangy taste.  Serve with or over White Bean and Olive Salad.
