Title:
Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (4 ounce) boneless skinless chicken breast halves
3 tablespoons cornstarch
4 tablespoons soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger-optional
2 garlic cloves - minced
3 tablespoons cooking oil, divided
2 cups broccoli florets
1 cup cauliflower
1 cup thinly sliced carrots
1 onion, cut into wedges
2 cups chicken stock
2 tblsp. brown sugar

Directions:
Cut chicken into 1/2-in. strips; Combine soy sauce, ginger and chicken stock and cornstarch, garlic and brown sugar.  Mix well.  In a large skillet or wok, heat 2 tablespoons of oil; stir-fry chicken until no longer pink, about 3-5 minutes. Remove and keep warm.
Add remaining oil; stir- fry broccoli, cauliflower, carrots and onion for 4-5 minutes or until crisp-tender. Return chicken to pan.  Add chicken stock and cornstarch mixture.  Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly.
