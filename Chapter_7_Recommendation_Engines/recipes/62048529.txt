Title:
Chicken and Rice Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Creative Chef Cream Mushroom Garlic Soup
1 cup Water
3/4 cup Uncooked regular white rice
1/4 tsp. Paprika
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In a 2 quart shallow baking dish mix soup, water, rice and paprika. Place chicken on rice mixture. Sprinkle with additional paprika. Cover. Bake 45 minutes or until chicken and rice are done.

Submitter: 
Paula Bellingar Gonzalez
Description: 
This is a one pot meal, no muss, no fuss. Real tasty too!
