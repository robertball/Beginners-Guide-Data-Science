Title:
Jim's Beer Mac & Cheese Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/738823.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound Penne pasta
3- Tablespoons butter
3- Tablespoons all purpose flour
1/8 - teaspoon nutmeg
1 1/2 cups whole milk
2- Cups Goose Island Honkers Ale
1/4 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon fresh ground pepper
1/2 pound sharp cheddar grated
1/2 pound Gruyere grated
1/2 cup Panko bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350Â°. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the Penne until al dente ( about 7 Minutes. melt the butter in a pan and whisk the flour to make a Roux whisking constantly for 2 mins. Add the nutmeg and cook another 1 min. Gradually whisk in the milk and beer and bring to a boil then reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and take 2/3 of your cheese mixture and stir in until all the cheese is melted. Add all the cauce to the pasta and mix well. Spoon the mixture into a buttered baking dish. Top the Penne with the rest of the cheese and Panko bread crumbs. Bake about 40 minutes
