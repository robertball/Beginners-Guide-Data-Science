Title:
Easy Chicken Frankfurters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Oil - 1tbsp
Frankfurters - 1Pkt(sliced into small rounds)
Onion - 1, chopped
Red Chilli Powder - 1tsp
Garam Masala - 1/4tsp
Tomato Ketchuo - 1tbsp (optional)

Directions:
Heat oil in a pan.
Add Onion, cook till its slightly pink. and add red chilli powder and garam masala, mix well.
Add Franfurter rounds, stir well and cook for 2 minutes.
Pour ketchup and mix well.
