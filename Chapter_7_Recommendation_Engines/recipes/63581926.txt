Title:
Chourico Butternut Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/996138.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Large Butternut Squash, peeled, seeded, and chopped
1 Large Sweet Onion, chopped
4 Stalks Celery Hearts, chopped
2 cloves Garlic, chopped
6 oz. Chourico Sausage, sliced and chopped
1 28oz. can Whole Tomatoes
1 32oz. container Lower Sodium Chicken Broth
1 15 oz. can Lower Sodium Chicken Broth
1 15.5 oz. can Cannellini Beans
1-2 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat Olive Oil in a large pot. Saute onion, celery, and garlic for 1 minute. Add Chourico and saute until lightly browned. Break up tomatoes and add to pot. Add Squash and Chicken Stock. Bring to a boil, and let simmer for 5 minutes. Drain and rinse beans, add to the pot. Season to taste with the salt and pepper. Continue to cook until Squash is cooked, but not mushy.

Submitter: 
Vanessa C
Description: 
Most Butternut Squash soups are pureed, not chunky. Chourico is a spicy Portuguese sausage. Use whatever meat is available. I also use leftover ham, turkey sausage, or regular sausage.
