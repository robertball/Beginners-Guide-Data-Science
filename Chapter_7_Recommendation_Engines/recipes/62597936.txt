Title:
Blueberry Sour Cream Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
-----
1 stick butter, softened
1 1/2 c sugar
4 eggs
1/2 c low-fat milk
1/2 c light sour cream
1 t vanilla extract
2 1/2 c flour
1 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
2 c frozen blueberries
Extra sugar to coat pan
------
Glaze:
------
2 c sifted powdered sugar
2 T softened butter
2 - 6 T low-fat milk
1/2 t vanilla
1/2 t almond extract
1/2 t lemon extract

Directions:
Spray bundt pan well with cooking spray, and coat  with sugar. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs, milk, sour cream, and vanilla and mix until well blended.
Sift 2 c flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt into batter; stir until well combined. Stir together remaining 1/2 c flour and blueberries until berries are coated, and fold gently into batter.
Bake at 350 45-55 min or until cake tests done.  Cool 10 min before removing from pan.  Cool completely before glazing.
To make glaze: starting with 2 T milk, mix glaze ingredients with electric mixer until smooth, adding additional milk as needed for desired consistency. Place glaze in ziplock baggie and cut tip from one corner. Drizzle glaze over top and sides of cake.
