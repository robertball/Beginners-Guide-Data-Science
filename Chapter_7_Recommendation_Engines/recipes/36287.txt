Title:
Old Bay® Party Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982117.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint sour cream
1 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons sliced green onions
1 teaspoon Old Bay® Seasoning
Cut-up fresh vegetables and potato chips

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine ingredients. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour or more.
Serve with cut-up fresh vegetables and potato chips.

Submitter: 
Old Bay®
Description: 
Here is a spectacular party dip that is easy to prepare and seasoned just right.  For those counting calories, the dip is equally delicious made with low fat sour cream or mayonnaise.
