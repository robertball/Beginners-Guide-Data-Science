Title:
Buttermilk Peppercorn Ranch Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/777128.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup mayonnaise
1/2 Cup sour cream
2/3 Cup buttermilk
1/2 Teaspoon dried chives
1/2 Teaspoon dried parsley
1/2 Teaspoon dried dill
1/4 Teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 Teaspoon onion powder
1/8 Teaspoon salt
1 Tablespoon ground peppercorns

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together all of the ingredients.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours - overnight is best.

Submitter: 
Alley
Description: 
Simple and delicious.  Came up with this recipe when trying to duplicate a local pizza restaurant's peppercorn ranch dressing that we love.  This dressing goes great with Romaine lettuce, sliced cherry tomatoes, red onion and fresh bacon crumbled on top.
