Title:
Leek, Potato And Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tablespoons olive oil
3 large leeks, white, pale green parts only, sliced
1 3/4  pounds russet potatoes, peeled, diced
1 large yellow onion, chopped
1 3/4  teaspoons vegetable broth
salt and pepper to taste
1/2  cup chopped green onions

Directions:
Heat oil in heavy large pot over medium-low heat.
Add leeks, potatoes, and onion to heated pot.
Saute until onion is just translucent.
Add broth and bring soup to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until all vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes.  Working in batches, puree about four or five cups of the soup in a blender.
Return puree to the pot.
Season with salt and pepper.
Heat to serving temperature.
Garnish with chopped green onions.

Description:
