Title:
Gluten Free Smoked Bacon Sausage Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 slices bacon, chopped
12oz Smoked Sausage, Sliced
3/4 cup carrots, thinly sliced
1 rib celery, thinly sliced
1 shallot, chopped
2 cloves garlic, pressed or minced
4 cups chicken broth
2 cups 2% milk, reserve Â¼ cut to use to thicken
3 Tablespoons gluten-free cornstarch or flour
1-1/2 cups diced baby yukon gold potatoes (I use 1 bag of pre-cut Simply Potatoes and onions)
1 cup sweet corn, fresh or frozen
seasoned salt and pepper
chopped green onions, for garnish

Directions:
Cook Bacon and Sausage in a Dutch Oven.  Remove meat and set aside. Drain all but 2 tbsp of bacon fat.
Add Carrots Celery Shallot to bacon grease and saute for 8 to 10 minutes, add garlic saute 1 additional minute.
Add chicken broth and potatoes bring to a boil and simmer 10 minutes or until potatoes are fork tender.
Add milk, corn bacon, sausage and bring to a simmer.
Wisk reserved milk and cornstarch/GF flour together removing any lumps and add to soup, simmer until soup is thickened.
Add salt and pepper to taste
Garnish with green onions

Easy and kids friendly! Perfect with a salad on a cold evening.
