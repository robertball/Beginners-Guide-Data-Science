Title:
Mandi's High Temperature Eye-of-Round Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2-3 pound) eye of round roast
Montreal Steak Seasoning
Kosher salt
pepper
garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 500 degrees for 20 minutes.
With pastry brush, paint roast with olive oil and season with Montreal Steak Seasoning, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
Place roast on a roasting rack in shallow roasting pan- FAT SIDE UP -  Do not cover or add water.
Reduce the oven temp to 475 degrees & put roast in oven.
Cook for 7 minutes per pound.
Turn oven OFF & leave roast in oven for 90 minutes.  Do Not Peek!!!
*Heat at 300 degrees for 15 minutes to warm.*
Remove roast from oven, tent with foil, and let rest for 20 minutes.
Slice roast  into very thin slices against the grain to serve.
