Title:
bean soup with okra tomato and ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2cups cooked great northern beans
2cups water
2cups ham chopped
3     medium tomatoes chopped
1cup  celery chopped
1     large onion chopped
1cup  v8 juice
1tsp  table salt
1/2tsp black pepper
1tsp  garlic powder
1tsp  onion powder
1TBL  beef flavored soup base
2cups sliced okra

Directions:
put all ingredients except okra in pan.
bring to boil.
reduce heat and simmer 1hour.
add okra.
simmer another 30min.
