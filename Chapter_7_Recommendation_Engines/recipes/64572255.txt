Title:
Big Ray's Impossibly Easy Bacon Cheeseburger Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3594337.jpg
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon
1 lb lean (at least 80%) ground beef
1/4 tsp pepper
1 large onion, chopped (1 cup)
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup ketchup
1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese (4 oz)
1/2 cup baking mix
1 cup milk
1 eggs
1 tsp sesame seeds

Directions:
Heat oven to 400Â°F.  In 10-inch cast iron skillet (or any oven safe skillet), cook bacon over medium-high heat 5 to 7 minutes, turning once, until crisp; drain on paper towels. Crumble bacon; set aside.
In same skillet, cook beef, pepper,onion and garlic over medium-high heat 5 to 7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until beef is thoroughly cooked; drain. Stir in ketchup and Worcestershire sauce. Sprinkle with cheese and bacon.
In medium bowl, stir remaining ingredients with wire whisk or fork until blended. Pour into skillet. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Serve with additional ketchup if desired.
