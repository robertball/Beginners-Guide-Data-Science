Title:
Magic French Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pkgs. (6 oz. each) semi-sweet choco. chips
1 can Eagle Brand sweet condensed milk
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
pinch of salt
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Melt chocolate over hot water  (double boiler).  Remove from heat.
Add condensed milk, vanilla, salt and nuts.
Still until smooth.
Turn into wax paper lined 8 in. square pan.
Chill in airtight container.
