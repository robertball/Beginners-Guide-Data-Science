Title:
Best of the Best Blueberry Muffins - converted to Blueberry Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1 1/4 cups white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
2 cups all-purpose flour, divided, reserve 1/4 c.
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 cup buttermilk (or 1T vinegar + milk)
*2* pints fresh blueberries - rinsed, drained and patted dry, divided
*omit* (2 tablespoons white sugar)
Add as a topping:
1/2 stick butter, cut up
3/4 c. sugar
5 T. flour

Directions:
Position rack in the middle of oven. Preheat oven to *350* degrees F. Butter and flour a 9 x 13 baking pan.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, 1 1/4 cups sugar and salt until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time. Mix together 1 3/4 cup of the flour and baking powder. Mix in the flour mixture alternately with the buttermilk, mixing just until incorporated. Crush 1/4 pint of the blueberries, and stir into the batter. Reserve 1/2 pint blueberries and mix the rest of the berries with the remaining 1/4 cup of the flour, and fold into the batter. Spread into prepared baking pan.  Sprinkle reserved blueberries evenly over top.
Mix topping ingredients in small bowl until well mixed and crumbly.  Sprinkle with fingers evenly over blueberries.  Bake in preheated oven for 30 - 40 minutes, or until golden brown, and toothpick comes out clean.
