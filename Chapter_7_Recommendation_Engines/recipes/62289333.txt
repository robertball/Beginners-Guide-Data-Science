Title:
Tofu Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14-oz pkg. firm tofu, well -drained
1/4 c. chopped fresh basil
3 T. chopped fresh Italian parsley
3 T. c. pine nuts, toasted
2 cloves garlic, peeled
1 T. lemon juice
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes
2 med. zucchini, cut into 1/2-inch slices (about 1 1/2 c.)
20 oz. marinara
8 no-cook lasagna noodles

Directions:
Combine tofu through red pepper in food processor; blend until similar to ricotta in texture. (Alternately, combine tofu, lemon juice, red pepper, and 4 oz. basil pesto in food processor.)
Saute zucchini in oil over medium-high heat for 3 to 5 minutes or until just tender.
Spread 2 T. marinara sauce over bottom of 8 x 8-inch baking dish that has been coated with cooing spray.  Cover with layer of noodles, half of tofu mix and half of zucchini.  top with noodles, remaining tofu and zucchini, and 1/2 c. sauce.  Finish with another layer of noodles and remaining sauce.
Cover with foil, bake one hour at 350.
