Title:
Sparkling Cranberry Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 ounce) can whole berry cranberry sauce
1/3 cup club soda
1/4 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Whisk cranberry sauce, club soda, sugar, and cinnamon together in a bowl until smooth.

Submitter: 
ID Sparks
Description: 
Made with club soda, cranberry sauce, sugar, and cinnamon, this quick and easy sparkling dressing is perfect for a spinach salad.Modified from: Sparkling Cranberry Dressing
