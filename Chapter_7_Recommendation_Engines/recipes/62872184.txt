Title:
Jalepeno Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 Jalapeno peppers
2 Bell peppers
1 1/2 Cups distilled vinegar
6 Cups sugar
1/3 Cup lemon juice
4 ounces liquid pectin
10 drops green food coloring (optional)

Directions:
Blend Jalapenos and Bell Peppers in a blender until finely chopped.
Combine peppers and vinegar in a small saucepan and bring to a boil over high heat. Boil for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove saucepan from heat and stir in the sugar and lemon juice.
Return the saucepan to the heat. Add pectin and food coloring and bring to a boil again. Stir constantly for 1 minute.
Remove from heat. Skim off the foam and bottle in sterilized jars.

My dad makes this every year. Is great with crackers and cream cheese.
