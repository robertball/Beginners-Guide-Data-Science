Title:
Seasoned Butter Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup crushed buttery round cracker crumbs
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
ground black pepper to taste
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 stick butter, cut into pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
Place eggs and cracker crumbs in two separate shallow bowls. Mix cracker crumbs with garlic salt and italian seasoning. Dip chicken in the eggs, then dredge in the crumb mixture to coat.
Arrange coated chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Place pieces of butter around the chicken.
Bake in the preheated oven for 40 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Bobbee Valentine
Description: 
Chicken breasts are dipped in beaten eggs, seasoning, and cracker crumbs, then baked with butter. Moist with the perfect flavoring.Modified from: Famous Butter Chicken
