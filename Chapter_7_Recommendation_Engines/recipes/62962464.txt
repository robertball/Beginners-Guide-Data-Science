Title:
Poppy Seed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds boneless chicken breasts, pounded, cut in half
2 cans cream of chicken soup
8 ounces sour cream
1 tube buttery crackers
2 tablespoons poppy seeds
1 stick butter, melted

Directions:
boil chicken in lightly salted water until tender.
drain, cool, and chop.
placed cooked chicken in greased 9x13 casserole dish.
combine cream of chicken soup and sour cream. spoon over chicken. sprinkle crushed crackers and poppy seed over top. drizzle butter over casserole.
bake at 350 about 30 minutes, until hot and bubbly.
