Title:
Daddy's Dang Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/629442.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C. butter room temperature
1 1/2 C. white sugar
2 eggs
2 1/4 C. flour
1/2 t.baking powder
3/4 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt
1/2 C. sour milk
1 C. mashed ripe bananas
1 t. vanilla
1 C. chopped walnuts
Your favorite recipe for brown sugar frosting!

Directions:
Prepare sour milk by measuring 1/2 c. milk into a glass container then add 1 teaspoon of white vinegar and let curdle. Cream together butter, sugar then add eggs. Blend well. Sift in dry ingredients alternating with sour milk.Add bananas, vanilla then nuts. Bake in a greased loaf pan at 350 degrees for between 45-60 minutes. Toothpick should come out clean. Cool then frost with your favorite Brown Sugar frosting recipe and enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Dylan's Nana
Description: 
My mom tried dozens of recipes before she got the OK from my Dad for this Banana Cake recipe. The difference might be the sour milk but all I know is that Dad said it was worth all other hit and miss recipes! Hence the name Daddy's Dang Banana Cake!
