Title:
Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slices bacon, chopped
3 1/2 lbs. chicken pieces - skin removed
1 onion - chopped
1 head of garlic, cloves peeled
1 tbsp. tomato paste
1/2 lb. cremini mushrooms, sliced
1 cup frozen pearl onions
14.5 oz. can chicken broth
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/2 lb. baby red potatoes, quartered
4 carrots cut into 2" pieces
2 tbsp. flour
2 tbsp. water
2 tbsp. fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
In a large pot, cook bacon until crisp and remove from pot.
Add chicken to pan and cook until lightly brown on both sides.
Remove from pot.
Reduce heat to medium and add onion, garlic and celery, and cook until lightly browned.
Stir in tomato paste, then add mushrooms and frozen onions.
Cook, stirring constantly about 1 minute.
Add broth, wine, potatoes and carrots and bring to a boil, scraping up brown bits.
Return chicken and bacon to pot.
Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for about 25 minutes or until chicken is done.
Whisk together flour and water in a small bowl then add about 1/4 cup of hot stew liquid and stir until blended.
Add liquid to pot and cook, stirring occasionally, until liquid bubbles and thickens - about 3 minutes.
Serve sprinkled with parsley.
