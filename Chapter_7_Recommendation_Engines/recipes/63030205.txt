Title:
Cinnamon Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/926435.jpg
Ingredients:
24 slices firm white sandwich bread, crusts trimmed
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
yolk from 1 large egg
1 1/4 cups sugar
1 T. cinnamon
1 stick unsalted butter, melted

Directions:
Roll bread slices flat with rolling pin.
Mix cream cheese, egg yolk, and 1/4 cup sugar.
Spread a scant tablespoon on each slice.
Roll up tightly.
Mix remaining sugar and cinnamon in a shallow bowl.
Brush roll-ups (including ends) with butter.
Roll in sugar mixture to coat.
Place on a cookie sheet.
Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
Bake roll-ups in a preheated 400 degree oven for 9-12 minutes until bottoms are glazed and golden brown.
Remove immediately to a wire rack to cool.
Cut each diagonally in 3 pieces.
Eat warm or cold.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
These are the easiest thing in the world to make!  And no one ever guesses they are made from white sandwich bread.  They can be assembled in advance and then frozen, making your holiday or special event no trouble at all.
