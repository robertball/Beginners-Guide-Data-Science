Title:
Mexicn style beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-15 oz. cans of pre-cooked pinto beans
1/2 cup of Pace picante sauce
8 oz. of shredded Pepper Jack cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pour both cans beans into bowl and mash up halfway.
Put mashed beans into skillet and turn on low heat.
Mix in 1/2 cup Pace picante sauce in with the beans.
Let simmer while stirring constantly for 3 minutes.
Mix in 4 oz. of cheese while stirring until melted.
Stir in salt and pepper to taste.
Take beans off of hot burner.
Last sprinkle remaining cheese on top.
Let sit until melted then ready to serve and eat.

Submitter: 
1ProudMomof3
Description: 
A cheap easy way to make a side dish to go with any Mexican style meal.
