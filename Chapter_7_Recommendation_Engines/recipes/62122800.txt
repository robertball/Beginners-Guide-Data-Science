Title:
Homemade Lowerfat Butter Toffee Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup half and half cream
2 cups 2% milk
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
5 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon imitation butter extract flavoring
1/2 teaspoon imitation rum extract flavoring
pinch salt
2/3 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon 1/2 cup water
1 cup butter toffee bits (Heath Brand)

Directions:
Combine first seven ingredients in a plastic 2 quart pitcher.
In small saucepan combine sugar, cinnamon, and water heating until just before boil stage.
Gradually combine sugar mixture with milk mixture stirring constantly until well mixed.
Pour mixture into ice cream maker and operate according to manufacturers directions.
When ice cream is ready for freezer, transfer mixture to covered, freezer-safe dish, and freeze for 30 minutes.
Take out of freezer and stir in butter toffee bits.
Cover and place back into freezer until frozen.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
This is an easy, delicious, lower fat butter toffee ice cream. It tastes like a creamier version of ice milk. Wonderful.
