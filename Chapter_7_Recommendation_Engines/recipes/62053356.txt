Title:
DanDan's Strawberry Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces softened cream cheese,
1/2 tsp vanilla extract,
1 cup strawberry slices,
1 cup whipping cream,
1/4 cup sugar,
1/4 cup powdered sugar,
1 graham cracker pie crust.

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, granulated sugar, and vanilla, mixing until well blended.  Mas 3/4 cup strawberry slices; stir into cream cheese mixture.  Whip cream with powdered sugar, until stiff peaks form; fold into cream cheese mixture.  Fold remaining strawberries into mixture, spoon into crust.  Chill at leat 3 hours before serving.  Garnish with additional strawberries and fresh mint.

Rhonda Brock Fuller
