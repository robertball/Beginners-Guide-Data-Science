Title:
Veggies and Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Kraft Ranch Dressing
6 cups assorted fresh veggies, such as carrot sticks, celery sticks, red and green pepper wedges and sugar snap peas

Directions:
Pour 2 TBS. dressing into each of 8 plastic or glass cups.
Place 3/4 cup veggies in each cup.
