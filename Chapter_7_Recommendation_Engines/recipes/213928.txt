Title:
Chicken, Avocado and Provolone Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992040.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded or chopped cooked chicken
1/2 cup chopped tomato
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1 ripe avocado, peeled, pitted, diced
4 slices SARGENTO® Sliced Reduced Sodium Provolone Cheese
4 (8 inch) low sodium flour tortillas
4 leaves red leaf or boston lettuce

Directions:
Combine chicken, tomato, cilantro and hot sauce in a medium bowl; toss well. Add avocado; toss again.
Arrange cheese over tortillas; top with lettuce leaves and chicken mixture. Roll up; cut diagonally in half.

Submitter: 
Sargento®
Description: 
Tasty ingredients like chicken, avocado, Sargento Sliced Reduced Sodium Provolone cheese and tomato make this wrap an easy winner.
