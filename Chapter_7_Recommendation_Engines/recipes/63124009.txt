Title:
Thai Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/795936.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
¼ cup soy sauce
2 teaspoons minced garlic
½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
2 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon fresh lime juice

Directions:
Prepare grill for direct cooking.  Place chicken in shallow baking dish.  Combine soy sauce, garlic and pepper flakes in measuring cup.  Pour over chicken, turning to coat.  Let stand 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine honey and lime juice in small bowl until blended and set aside.
Place chicken on grid over medium coals, brush with marinade.  Discard remaining marinade.  Grill covered, 5 minutes.  Brush both sides of chicken with honey mixture.  Grill 5 minutes more or until chicken is no longer pink in center.
