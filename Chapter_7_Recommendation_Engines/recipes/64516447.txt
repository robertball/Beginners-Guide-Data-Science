Title:
Pizza Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups shredded marble cheese
1/3 - 1/2 cup green onions, thinly sliced
2 green peppers, diced
1 tsp. butter
2 cans tomato soup
2 cans sliced mushrooms, drained
1 large stick pepperoni or other sausage
1/2 tsp. oregano powder
dinner or hamburger buns
additional butter
garlic powder

Directions:
Saute onions and peppers in butter.
Add to cheese, soup and mushrooms; mix well.
Spread buns with butter and a sprinkle of garlic powder.
Cover with a thick layer of the pizza mixture.
Bake under broiler till bubbly and toasted.
Makes about 6 - 1lb containers. This can be frozen. Thaw prior to using.
1 lb mixture covers about 12 buns.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Pizza on a bun - a great hot snack we often enjoyed as kids. These taste great with a bowl of soup too. The large mix can be frozen in small containers. Thaw in fridge before using.
