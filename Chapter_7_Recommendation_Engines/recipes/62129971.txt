Title:
Rainy Day Nacho Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (5 1/4oz) au gratin potato mix (used dry),
1 can (16oz) whole kernel corn (drained),
1 can (12oz) tomatoes and green chilies (undrained),
1 lb. ground beef, browned and drained,
2 c. water,
2 c. milk,
2 c. Velveeta processed cheese

Directions:
Combine browned ground beef, dry potato mix, corn, tomatoes and water in crockpot.
Cook on HIGH for 3 1/2 hours or on LOW for 6 or 7.
Add milk and cheese. Put on LOW 1/2 hour more or until cheese is melted.
Serve with crushed nacho chips and a dollop of sour cream.

Submitter: 
NEgirl
Description: 
This soup is perfect for a cold rainy day at home.  It is so quick and easy and better yet, the kids love it!
