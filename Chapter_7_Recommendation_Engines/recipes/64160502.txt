Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - Chicken breasts (diced)
3 - 4.5oz cans of diced green chilies
4oz - Sour Cream (about 2 spoonfuls)
1- 28oz can of Las Palmas Enchilada sauce
1-16oz package of cheese
2- tablespoons of Natures seasoning
2- tablespoons of red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions:
1. Boil whole chicken breasts for 30 min.
2. Dice chicken breasts into 1 inch squares
3. Pre-Heat over for 375 degrees
4. Add chicken to a large bowl
5. Add green chili
6. Add sour cream
7. Add half of the can of enchilada sauce
8. Add half of the bag of cheese
9. add natures seasoning and (red pepper flakes)
Mix all together until a paste is formed
