Title:
Spinach Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs.fr. spinach(frozen)
2 cups stuffing mix (sug. Pepper Ridge Farm).
2 med. onions chopped
6 eggs slighty beaten
6 tbsp melted butter
1/2 parmesan cheese grated
1 tbsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp. thyme

Directions:
Cook spinach - Dry well
Mix all ingredients together.  Form into walnut size balls.
Freeze uncovered on cookie sheet.  Place in plastic bags.
Before serving bake on greased cookie sheet.
350 deg. for 25 minutes.
