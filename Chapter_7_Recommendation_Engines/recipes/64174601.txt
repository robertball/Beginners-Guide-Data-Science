Title:
Grandma's Breakfast Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1479651.jpg
Ingredients:
Dry Ingredients:
1 1/2 C flour
1/2 C sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
Wet Ingredients:
1/4 C oil
1/2 C milk
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/2 tsp almond extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray muffin tray with cooking spray and set aside.  Mix all dry ingredients together well.  Whisk together wet ingredients in separate bowl.  Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix until just blended.
Fill muffin pan with batter (filling each cup to about 1/2 full).  Bake for 15-20 minutes or until lightly browned.  Makes 10-12 muffins.
