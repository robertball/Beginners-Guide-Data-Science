Title:
Single Serving Deep Dish Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. butter, melted
1 tbsp. white sugar
1 tbsp. brown sugar
3 drops of vanilla
pinch of salt
1 egg yolk
1/4 c. flour
2 tbsp. chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a monkey dish or small bowl.
Microwave 40-60 seconds

Submitter: 
Jeff Harrell

Single serving deep dish chocolate chip cookie!
