Title:
Bill's French Dressing - Salt Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Sugar
2 Cups Vegetable Oil
2-3 Tablespoons of Onion Powder
2/3 Cup salt-free ketchup
2/3 Cup white vinegar
1 Teaspoon freeze dried red onion
1/2 Teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1/2 Teaspoon Garlic powder (I like roasted)

Directions:
Mix sugar, oil, onion powder, ketchup, pepper and garalic, beat for 20 minutes with an electric mixer.
Add the vinegar and red onion, blend well. Season to taste. (Onion and garlic make the biggest diference).
Keep refrigerated.

Submitter: 
Bill Falk
Description: 
Incredible NO SALT replacement for those like me who can not eat salt, and, need a good French dressing on my salad.
