Title:
Amaretto Fudge Cappuccino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984994.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup boiling water
1 tablespoon TASTER'S CHOICE® 100% Pure Instant Coffee
2 tablespoons Amaretto Flavor NESTLE® CARNATION® COFFEE-MATE® Liquid or Powdered Coffee Creamer
1 tablespoon Chocolate Flavor NESTLE® NESQUIK™ Syrup
Whipped cream (optional)
Chocolate shavings (optional)

Directions:
COMBINE water and Taster's Choice(R) in large mug; stir until coffee is dissolved. Stir in Coffee-mate(R) and Nestle(R) Nesquik(TM). Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings.

Submitter: 
Nestle® Carnation® Coffee-mate®
Description: 
Start your morning with a flavorful coffee drink.
