Title:
Planet Organic Edamame & Arame Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/964043.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3C Dry Arame
250grams Edamame
1T rice vinegar
1T Braggs or tamari
2T sesame seeds
1/2t agave syrup
1 clove garlic minced
2T olive oil
1T sesame oil

Directions:
1) Pour boiling water over dry arame and soak for 15 minutes.  Drain
2) Cook edamame according to pkg directions
3) Mix rest of ingredients and pour over edamame and arame.
4) Toss to coat.
