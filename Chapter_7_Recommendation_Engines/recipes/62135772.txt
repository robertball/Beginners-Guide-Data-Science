Title:
Strawberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag Baby Spinach
1 quart Fresh Strawberries
4 oz. crumbled Feta Cheese
1 small Vidallia Onion
1 2oz. pkg of real Bacon Bits
1/2 bottle Kraft Light Raspberry Vinaigrette

Directions:
1. Place spinach in large bowl.
2. Slice strawberries over spinach.
3. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the spinach.
4. Slice the vidallia onion over spinach.
5. Sprinkle the bacon bits over spinach.
6. Pour vinaigrette over salad and toss. Enjoy!
