Title:
Dreamy, Creamy Cauliflower Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4544961.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower, finely grated
2 eggs
salt
1 pinch ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Place cauliflower in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave at maximum power until tender, about 4 minutes. Place on a kitchen towel and squeeze out all liquid; transfer to a bowl.
Whisk eggs, salt, and black pepper in a bowl. Add to the cauliflower in batches; stir well. Shape cauliflower mixture into 3-inch tortillas on the prepared baking sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven until set, about 20 minutes. Let cool slightly.
Heat a skillet over medium heat. Cook tortillas until golden brown, about 1 minute. Flip and cook for 1 minute more.
