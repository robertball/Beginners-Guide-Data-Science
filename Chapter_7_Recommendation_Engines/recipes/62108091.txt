Title:
Vegetarian Worcestershire Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T. water
6 T. brown rice syrup
1/4 cup tamari
2 T. apple cider vinegar
1 tsp. barley malt syrup
1 tsp. ground ginger
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
Pinch ground cloves
Pinch onion powder

Directions:
Place all the ingredients in a blender, bowl, or jar with a lid.  Blend, whisk, or shake until the mixture is smooth and the powders are well incorporated.  Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Marian
Description: 
Add flavor to any recipe with a vegetarian version of an old favorite.
