Title:
Sweet Apple-Cinnamon Baked Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989569.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (21 ounce) can LUCKY LEAFÂ® Premium Apple Fruit Filling
3 cups old-fashioned oats
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon salt
4 eggs, beaten
3 cups milk
1/4 cup canola oil
1 tablespoon vanilla
Milk or half-and-half

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a large bowl combine oats, brown sugar, cinnamon and salt. Stir in LUCKY LEAF Premium Apple Pie Filling.
In a second large bowl stir together eggs, 3 cups milk, oil and vanilla. Stir egg mixture into oat mixture until combined.
Pour mixture into a greased 3-quart rectangular baking pan. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 55 minutes or until oatmeal mixture is set.
Serve warm in bowls with milk or half-and-half.

Submitter: 
Lucky Leaf Fruit Filling
Description: 
Rolled oats, apple pie filling, and cinnamon are blended with an egg-milk mixture, then baked for an easy breakfast or brunch dish.
