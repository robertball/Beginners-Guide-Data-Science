Title:
Chocolate & Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/326056.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C peanut butter chips (separated)(12 oz pkg).
1/4 margarine melted.
1/2 C cocoa powder.
1 tsp vanilla.
4 1/2 C sugar.
1 jar marshmallow creme (7 oz).
1 1/2 C evaporated milk (12 oz can).
1/4 C margarine.

Directions:
Line 13x9 pan with foil. Put 1 C PB chips in medium bowl and set aside. In another medium bowl mix 1/4 C melted margaine, cocoa, and vanilla till smooth. Add 1 C PB chips. In 4 qt pot, mix sugar, marshmallow creme, evaporated milk and 1/4 C margarine. Cook over medium heat stirring constantly until mixture comes to rolling boil. Boil and stir for 5 minutes. Remove pot from heat, immediately add half of hot mix to bowl with PB chips that you set aside. Pour rest into cocoa mix and blend. Beat PB mix until chips are melted, spread in prepared pan. Beat cocoa mix until chips melt and mix thickens. Spread over top of PB layer. Cool. Remove from pan, take off foil, cut into squares. Store in airtight container in cool dry place.
