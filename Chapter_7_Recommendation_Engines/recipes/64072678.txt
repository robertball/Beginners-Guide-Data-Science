Title:
Lemon Aioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Mayonnaise
1 t Lemon Zest
1-2 T Lemon Juice
1 t Dijon Mustard
1 Clove Garlic (pasteed)
S&P; to taste

Directions:
Whip everything together and place in fridge for 1 hour.
