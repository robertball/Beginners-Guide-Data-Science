Title:
Hidden Valley Oyster Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1085667.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 (16 ounce) box oyster crackers
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
1/2 teaspoon dill weed
1/4 teaspoon lemon pepper or garlic powder (optional)

Directions:
Place crackers in a gallon-size Glad-Lock(R) Zipper (TM) Storage Bag. Pour oil over crackers; seal bag and toss to coat.
Add dressing mix and spice; seal bag and toss again until coated.
Bake on ungreased baking sheet at 250 degrees F for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley®
Description: 
Oyster crackers are baked with a dusting of Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Seasoning Mix, dill, and other optional spices until golden brown for an easy, crispy snack.
