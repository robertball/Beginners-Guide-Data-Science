Title:
Thai Beef Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c brown sugar
2 T cornstarch
2 c beef broth
1/3 c soy sauce
1 t onion powder
1 t garlic powder
1 t ground ginger
Hot sauce to taste.
2 lbs boneless steak cut in strips
6 T olive oil, divided
4 c fresh broccoli florettes
2 cups fresh cauliflowerettes
1 1/2 c julienned carrots
2 c sliced mushrooms
1/4 c smooth peanut butter
1/2 c peanuts, chopped
Cooked Wide Noodles

Directions:
Combine firs 8 ingredients and whisk until smooth. Set aside and stir fry beef in 3 T oil until no longer pink. Set aside. Srir Fry Cauliflower and carrots 5 minues, add broccoli and stir fry 7 minutes. Add mushrooms and stir fry a few minutes more (6-8 minutes)
Whisk broth mixture and add to pan. Saute until thickened and reduce heat, add beef and peanut butter till blended. Serve over noodles and sprinkle with peanuts
