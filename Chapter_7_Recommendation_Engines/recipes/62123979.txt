Title:
MEL'S EASY WONTON SOUP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground pork sausage
4 green onions chopped
1 tbsp. Terrakyi Sauce
1 tsp. Ground Ginger
2 32 oz Chicken Broth
1 Package wonton wrappers

Directions:
Mix sausage,  2 chopped green onions, terrakyi and ginger.  Set aside for 30 minutes.
Layout wrappers fill with 1 tsp of mixture.
Put mixture in middle of wrapper, fold diagonly, wet edges with water to seal, fold bottoms together.
You can preboil these in water if you want then add to broth.
Pour Broth into pan, bring to boil, but becareful not to boil to hard or your wontons will open.
Leave wontons in broth from about 3 to 5 minutes
Put about 4 wontons per bowl, garnish with chopped onions.
These can been made and frozen also.

Submitter: 
Cheesecakemama
Description: 
This is easy to make and you can make wontons ahead and freeze for another time.
