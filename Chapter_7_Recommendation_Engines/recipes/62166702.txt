Title:
Chicago style roast duck Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Whole duck
1 tbls garlic pwdr
1 tbls onion pwdr
2 tbsp caraway seed
salt and pepper to tast

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 425
Wash and pat dry duck. Score breast and legs skin with sharp knife being careful not to slice into meat.
Season with garlic,onion ,salt,and pepper, inside and out.
Massage into duck. Sprinkle on caraway seeds being sure they get into the scored fat.Place duck in a roasting pan on a rack breast side up.Roast for 15 min,turn over and roast for 15 min more. Reduce oven temp to 350,turn duck breast side up for 20 min,turn over again for 20 min and turn one more time for 20 min.
Remove duck and let rest.
Quarter bird with poultry scissors and serve with applesuace,bread dumplings and sauerkraut.
