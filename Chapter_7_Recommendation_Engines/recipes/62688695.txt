Title:
Gloria's Maple Walnut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sifted cake flour
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup butter or margerine, softened
1 cup sugar
3 eggs
2/3 cup of whole milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
FROSTING
1/4 cup of butter
1 tablespoon of milk
2 1/2 cups of sifted confectioners sugar
2 teaspoons of maple flavoring
TOPPING
1/3 cup finely chopped walnuts

Directions:
Sift flour with baking powder and salt, set aside.
In a separate bowl, cream butter with sugar until light and fluffy
Add eggs one at a time beating thoroughly after each addition of egg
Mix in, alternately, flour mixture and milk
Add vanilla extract
Add chopped nuts
Bake in a greased 10 inch tube pan for 40 minutes at 350 degrees
Let cool completely. Frost cake and top with 1/3 cup finely chopped walnuts
Frosting
Cream 1/4 cup of butter with 1 tablespoon of milk -
Add 2 1/2 cups of sifted powdered sugar

Submitter: 
TPADULA
Description: 
This is an old family favorite that is very unique!
My mother Gloria created this recipe and it was always served at family gatherings.
