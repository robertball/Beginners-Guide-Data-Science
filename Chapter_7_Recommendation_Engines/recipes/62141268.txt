Title:
Real Mexican Style Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28oz. can diced tomatoes
1/2 medium onion chopped
1 tsp. garlic powder
20 pickled jalepeno rings (make sure you get them in the jar)
1/2 c. cilantro

Directions:
In food processor combine tomatoes, onion, garlic powder, and jalepenos.
Process until restaraunt style consistency (pretty runny).  Add cilantro and give a couple more pulse just to chop it up.
Refridgerate over night to let the flavors meld.
This makes a medium heat salsa, add more or less jalepeno rings to taste.

Submitter: 
Kelly Hagelman
Description: 
Nothing is better than real salsa in it's saucy form!  Forget chunky, this is a pureed treat!
