Title:
Roasted Brussel Sprouts with Butternut Squash & Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 LB. Fresh Brussel Sprouts, Trimmed, & Halved or Quartered
1 LB Fresh Butternut Squash, Skinned, Cut & Cubed (1")
2 sprigs Fresh Thyme
1/2 cup RAW Pecans (Original recipe used chestnuts)
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Prep the veggies.
Spread them out on a cookie sheet.
Drizzle olive oil over veggies.
Add nuts.
Add thyme, salt & pepper and mix.
Roast at 425 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes, until caramelized.
