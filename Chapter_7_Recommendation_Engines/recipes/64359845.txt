Title:
Guilt Free Barbeque sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1 onion, peeled and chopped in Magic Bullet
1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
1/2 cup water
5 tablespoons Splenda
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 cup sugar free applesauce
1/2 cup tomato sauce
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons liquid smoke flavoring

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, mix lemon juice, onion, distilled white vinegar, water, prepared mustard, ground black pepper, salt and cayenne pepper. Bring to a boil, then simmer uncovered 20 minutes.
Stir tomato sauce, apple sauce, Splenda, Worcestershire sauce and liquid smoke flavoring into the mixture. Return to boil, then remove from heat. Refrigerate until ready for use.

Submitter: 
Miranda Oswald
Description: 
I use this for a sauce over hot veggies or meat or on certain salads.  Completely guilt free
