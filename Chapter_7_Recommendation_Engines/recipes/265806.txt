Title:
Sainte Marthe Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6248823.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Granny Smith apples, diced
1 medium zucchini, diced
1/4 cup almond slivers
1/2 lemon, juiced
2 tablespoons olive oil, or more to taste
2 sprigs fresh mint, chopped

Directions:
Combine apples, zucchini, almond slivers, mint, lemon juice, and olive oil in a bowl; toss to combine.
Serve in clear glass bowls or cocktail glasses, which is the presentation used at the Parisian cafe where I first enjoyed this salad.

Submitter: 
Madame Jarrin
Description: 
Named for the quarter in Paris where I first tasted this crisp, fresh salad in a little bohemian cafe. Cut the zucchini and the apples into equal-sized cubes.
