Title:
Jalapeno cream cheese poppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/395770.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can whole jalapeno peppers
4 oz cream cheese
3 green onions chopped
1 cup panko crumbs
1 tbsp garlic salt
1/2 cup flour
1 egg
2 tbsp milk
2 cups vegetable or canola oil

Directions:
Heat oil in a pot on medium/high
Slice tops off jalapenos and cut lengthwise
Mix cream cheese with sliced onions
Fill jalapenos with cream cheese mixture (Put in ziploc bag and snip the corner to make this easier)
Place panko crumbs, flour, and egg with milk in seperate bowls
Mix garlic salt with panko crumbs
Dip filled jalapenos in flour then egg then crumb mixture
Drop jalapenos in hot oil until golden brown and place on paper towels when removed
Sprinkle on chopped green onions if desired
