Title:
Strawberry-Chocolate Freezer Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chocolate graham cracker crust
3 cups low fat strawberry ice cream or frozen yogurt, softened
light whipped cream
1 cup sliced strawberries

Directions:
Spread ice cream evenly over crust.  Dip a large spoon in hot water and run it over ice cream to smooth top.  Place pie in freezer until solid, 2hrs or up to 3 days.
Before serving, spray whipped cream around edge of pie.  Mound strawberries in center and slice and serve.
