Title:
Lighter Creamy Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz fettuccine
1 Tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1 Tablespoons butter
1 clove minced garlic
3/4 cup fat free 1/2 & 1/2
1/4 cup heavy cream
2 oz Lite Cream Cheese (Neufchatel), cubed
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
Sea salt and fresh ground black pepper
3 Tablespoons fresh parsley, minced
additional freshly grated Parmesan for serving

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to boil, add fettuccine and cook until al dente .  Drain then return to large pot and if sauce is not complete, toss with 2 teaspoons of olive oil to prevent pasta sticking together then cover to keep warm.
While pasta is boiling, over med heat, melt butter in a large sauce pan, add olive oil and garlic then saute for 1 min.  Slowly add the fat free 1/2 & 1/2 and heavy cream, stir to combine.  As liquids are warming drop in cubes of lite cream cheese, stir to melt.   Watch heat and do not allow to boil.  Slowly sprinkle in Parmesan until completely combined.  Add salt & pepper to taste and let cook for 1 min.  Pour sauce over fettuccine, sprinkle with fresh parsley and toss to combine.   Serve immediately with an additional sprinkle of fresh parsley and a bit of fresh grated Parmesan.
