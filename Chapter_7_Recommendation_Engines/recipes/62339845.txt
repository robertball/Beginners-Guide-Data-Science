Title:
Breaded Tilapia with Dijon Mustard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/390777.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tilapia fillet
1 teaspoon Dijon Mustard
1 teaspoon Mayo
1/4 cup bread crumbs
1/2 teaspoon parsley
1/2 paprika
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Mix together Mayo and Mustard and set aside.  Combine bread crumbs, parsley, paprika, pepper on a cutting board or shallow dish.  Coat one side of the fish in the mayo mixture and place mayo side down in the bread crumbs.  Coat other side and flip over in the bread crumbs.  Place in greased baking dish and bake for 15-18 minutes.  Fish should flake easily with a fork.
