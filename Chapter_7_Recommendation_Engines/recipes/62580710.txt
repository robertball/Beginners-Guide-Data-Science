Title:
Holly's Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/548177.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese (room temperature)
1/2 cup butter (room temperature)
3/4 cup powdered sugar
2 Tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup chocolate chips (milk chocolate or semi-sweet)
serve with graham crackers or vanilla wafers

Directions:
Beat all ingredients together and then stir in chocolate chips.  Refrigerate until ready to serve with graham crackers or vanilla wafers.

Submitter: 
emilywise
Description: 
This is a quick, easy and fun twist on the great taste everyone loves, chocolate chip cookie dough!  Serve this 5 minute dip with graham crackers or vanilla wafers for a treat young and old will enjoy.
