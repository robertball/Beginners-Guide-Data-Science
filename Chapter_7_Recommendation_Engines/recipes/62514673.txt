Title:
Russian Monks Vegan Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/509416.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 tbsp ground flax, optional
3 tbsp vegetable oil
salt, sugar or honey to taste
1 1/2 cups high mineral content mineral water (like "Apollinaris" or "Gerolsteiner")

Directions:
Place dry ingredients into a bowl. Add oil.
Pour mineral water and mix thoroughly with a wire whisk.
Fry thin pancakes on a small preheated non-stick pan on medium-high until browned on both sides. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
tantuc
Description: 
My mother heard of the ingredients for these lent time blini on TV. After several experiments she came up with this recipe. The flour in the recipe can be replaced with whole wheat, buckwheat, rice, rye or oat for those on wheat or gluten-free diet.
