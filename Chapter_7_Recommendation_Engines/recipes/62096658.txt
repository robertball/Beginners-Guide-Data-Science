Title:
English Muffin Pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 English Muffins, split
1 Jar of Pizza Sauce
1 Package (8 ounces) shredded mozzarella cheese
Any of your favorite pizza toppings!

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Lay english muffins on a baking sheet, split side up, and top with sauce, cheese, and any toppings you prefer.
Bake in preheated oven 5-10 minutes. Enjoy!
Note: To make these in the microwave, lay them out on a paper plate, add toppings, and cook in microwave for 5 minutes on medium high power. (You can put them in the toaster before topping them if you prefer a more crispy crust.)

Submitter: 
Dorese
Description: 
This easy pizza recipe can be made either in the microwave or in a conventional oven. Kids love them for snacks, or serve them for a fun lunch!
