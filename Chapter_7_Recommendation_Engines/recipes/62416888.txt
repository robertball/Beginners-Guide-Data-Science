Title:
Smashed Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. red potatoes
1 brick (8 oz) light cream cheese
1/4 cup light sour cream
2 cups light shredded cheddar cheese
2 Tbs butter
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped onion
4 bacon strips chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes until tender.
Mix potatoes, cream cheese, sour cream and butter. Add chopped celery, onion and bacon. Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
JPsMommie
Description: 
I came upon this recipe when I attempted to make potato salad, but mashed the potatoes too much. The ingredients were perfect together--it's great served cold as well as hot!
