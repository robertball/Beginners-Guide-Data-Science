Title:
Poppy Seed Glazed Ham and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 potato dinner rolls (1 package)
1 lb chipped ham
1 lb sliced swiss cheese
1/2 cup butter
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons mustard
2 tablespoons poppy seeds
2 teaspoons onion flakes (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
2 Without separating into individual rolls, cut tops off.
3 Line 9x13 pan with roll bottoms and place ham and cheese onto bottoms.
4 Put tops of rolls back on.
5 In a saucepan, mix remaining ingredients and bring to a boil for 2 minutes.
6 Pour sauce evenly over sandwiches.
7 Bake uncovered for 20 minutes.
