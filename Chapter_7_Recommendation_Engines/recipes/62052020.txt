Title:
Health Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1/2 cup  wheat germ
1/2 cup  nonfat dry milk
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 cup  butter
1 cup  peanut butter
1 1/2 cups honey
2      eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
2 cups raisins
2 cups dried apricots or dates
1 1/2 cups oats
1 cup  nuts
2/3 cup  unsalted sunflower nuts
3/4 cup  coconut

Directions:
Sift first 6 ingredients together. Beat butter, peanut butter, and honey together. Then add eggs and vanilla and beat.  Add flour mixture and mix well. Then stir in all other ingredients.
Bake at 350 degrees F. for around 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
ARKBUCKS
Description: 
These great tasting cookies are healthier than most cookies. The whole wheat flour, oats, nuts, and dried fruits make it hearty and tasty.  Don't be frightened away by the fact that they are healthy, because they are delicious!!
