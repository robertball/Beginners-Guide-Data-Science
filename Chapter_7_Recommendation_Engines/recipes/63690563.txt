Title:
Green Pepper, Onion And Garlic Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 tsp seasoned salt (Adobe)
1 TBSP Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp ground pepper
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped green pepper, finely
1 lb lean ground beef

Directions:
Combine all ingredients well, divide into 4 equal patties.
Grill or fry in pan till done.
Serve on Kaiser rolls with your favorite toppings.
