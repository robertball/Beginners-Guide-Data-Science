Title:
Super Easy Creamy Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
60z butterscotch chips
14oz can sweetened condensed milk
1tsp vanilla
1/2 cup nuts (optional)

Directions:
Line a 9x9 baking pan with waxed paper extending it over the edge of pan.
In a double boiler or in a microwave safe medium size bowl combine all the ingredients.
Melt until smooth...stirring often.  Pour into pan.  Shake the pan a bit to spread the fudge to the corners.  Refridgerate approximately 1 hour to set.
Cut into bite size pieces and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Jenn Sanchez Ebert
Description: 
I remember my mom making this fudge when I was younger.  Family, friends and even the kid's teachers look forward to it every holiday.
