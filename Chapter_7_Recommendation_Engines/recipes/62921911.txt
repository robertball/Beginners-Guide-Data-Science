Title:
Easy Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs lean ground beef
1 egg
1 small onion, chopped
1 cup 1% milk
1 cup bread crumbs, Italian
3 tbsps Worcestershire Sauce
1 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp sage
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a medium sized bowl
Pat into a small loaf pan
Bake at 350 for 1 1/2 hour

Submitter: 
Dede
Description: 
Easy Meatloaf. Just put in oven to bake and can add your potatoes for baking at the same time. Voila, dinner is served!
