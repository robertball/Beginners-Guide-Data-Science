Title:
Frosted Banana Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Earth Balance Margarine, or sub applesauce
1 1/2 cups organic sugar
Substitute for 2 eggs
1 cup vegan sour cream, or pureed tofu
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup mashed ripe bananas
1 (16 ounce) vegan cream cheese frosting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 10x15 inch jellyroll pan.
In food processor combine 1T flax seeds for each egg; add 3T water for each egg, blending until smooth.  Stir in the sour cream and vanilla.  Mix in the mashed banana.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.   Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; stir into the batter. . Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow bars to cool completely before frosting with the cream cheese frosting.
