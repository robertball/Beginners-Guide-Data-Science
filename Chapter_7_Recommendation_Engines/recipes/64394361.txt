Title:
Green Apple Goodness Shake Recipe
Image:

1/2 Green Apple, chopped
1/2 - 2 tsp Almond Butter
1 tsp Cinnamon
1/2 cup ice
8-10 oz. Almond Milk
2 Scoops Vanilla Protein
1 tablespoon ground flax seed

Directions:
Mix all ingredients into a blender
