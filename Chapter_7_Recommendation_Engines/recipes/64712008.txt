Title:
Air Fryer Southwestern Egg Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5616389.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Jalapeno Jack cheese, shredded
1 cup sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
13.5 oz can spinach, drained
15 oz can black beans, drained and rinsed
2 cups frozen corn, thawed
4 oz can diced green chiles, drained
4 green onions, sliced
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
16 oz pkg egg roll wrappers
canola oil mister

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except egg roll wrappers  and oil in a large bowl.
Place wrapper at an angle and moisten all 4 edges with fingertips dipped in water
Place 1/4 cup of filling in center of wrapper
Fold one corner over filling, fold sides in and tuck and roll
Repeat with remaining wrappers
Mist each egg roll with oil
Place in basket, making sure they are not touching (cook in batches if necessary)
Air fry 8 minutes at 390 degrees, flip and cook an additional 4 minutes

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
Avoid frying by cooking egg rolls in an air fryer and achieving the same crispiness!
