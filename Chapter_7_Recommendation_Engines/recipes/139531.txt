Title:
Instant Cappuccino Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/876849.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup powdered non-dairy creamer
1 cup instant chocolate drink mix
3/4 cup instant coffee granules
1/2 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions:
In a medium sized glass jar, combine the non-dairy creamer, chocolate drink mix, instant coffee, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, cinnamon and nutmeg; mix well.
To prepare, place 2 tablespoons mix in a mug, pour in 3/4 cup boiling water, and stir until dissolved. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Here is a homemade dry cappuccino mix in a jar which uses SPLENDA® in place of sugar. Make it for yourself, or give it as a gift.
