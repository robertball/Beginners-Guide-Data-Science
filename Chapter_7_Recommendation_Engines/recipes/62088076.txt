Title:
Italian Pine Nut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11 tbsp unsalted butter, room temperature
1 c sugar
3 large eggs
2 1/2 c all purpose flour
1 c pine nuts, toasted
4 c cornflakes cereal, crushed
powder sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven 350F. Line 2 large baking sheets with parchment. Using electric mixer, beat butter and sugar in large bowl until blended. Add eggs 1 at a time, beating well after each addition. Whisk flour and baking powder in medium bowl. Add to butter mixture, beating until blended. Stir in pine nuts.
Place crushed cornflakes in shallow baking dish. Using 1 heaping tbsp dough for each cookie, form into balls. Roll each ball in cornflakes, pressing gently to adhere. Transfer balls to baking sheets, spacing 1" apart. Bake until deep golden, 23 min. Cool. Dust cookies with powdered sugar, if desired, and serve.
