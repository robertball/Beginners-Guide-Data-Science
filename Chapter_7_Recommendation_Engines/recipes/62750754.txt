Title:
Marinated Potatoe Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs red potatoes
2 bottles reduced fat spicy italian dressing
6 boiled eggs
two large onions
three to four stalks celery
dill pickle relish
low fat mayonaise
low fat sour cream
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Lightly Boil potatoes with skin on, potatoes should still be slightly firm.
While still hot remove skin, chop into 1" squares and place in large bowl with italian dressing.
Marinate overnight
Drain dressing from potatoes
In Large bowl
Chop boiled eggs
chop onions into small pieces
slice celery into small pieces
If you couldn't find dill relish, slice dill pickles into relish size pieces
combine drained potatoes, eggs, onions, celery, pickles, equal amounts of mayo and sour cream to taste
