Title:
bean and veggie topped sweet potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sour cream
2 tsp. dried basil leaves
1 c. water
2 c. coarsely chopped broccoli
1 c. thinly sliced carrots
2 tsp. butter
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. Italian herbs
1 small onion, chopped
1 small red pepper, in 1/2- inch pieces
1 c. cooked black beans
4 medium hot baked sweet potatoes, cut in half lengthwise
2 ounces cheddar or mozza, shredded

Directions:
In small bowl stir together sour cream and basil.  Set aside.
In 10-inch skillet bring water to full boil; add broccoli and carrots.  Cook over high heat until crispy tender (2 to 3 minutes); drain.  In same skillet, melt butter till sizzling; stir in garlic powder and Italian seasoning.  Add broccoli, carrots, onion and red pepper.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until vegetables are crisply tender (2 to 3 minutes).  Stir in black beans.  Keep warm.
Heat broiler.  Place baked potatoes on broiler pan; spread sour cream mixture evenly over potato halves.  Divide veggie mix evenly among potato halves; top with 1 T. shredded cheese.  Broil 3 to 5 inches from heat till cheese is melted.
