Title:
THE ULTIMATE CHILI!!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/712185.jpg
Ingredients:
Serv 6-8
1 lb beef chuck
1/2 lb italian sausage
15oz chili beans drained
15oz chili beans in spicy sauce
28oz diced tomatoes
6oz tomato paste
1/2 large onion chopped
11/2 stalks celery chopped
1/2 grn pepper chopped
1/2 red pepper chopped
1 grn chile pepper minced
5 cloves of garlic minced
11/2tsp bacon bits or 4 slices
2 cubes beef bouillon
2 beers 1 at a time
2tbsp chili powder
11/2tsp worcestershire sauce
11/2tsp dried oregano
1tsp cumin
1tsp hot sauce
1/2tsp basil
1/2tsp salt
1/2tsp black pepper
1/2tsp cayenne pepper
1/2tsp paprika
1/2tsp white sugar
Shredded cheddar
salsa
sour cream

Directions:
1. Heat a large stock pot over med-high heat. Fry bacon til crispy, remove bacon add chopped celery, onions, and peppers. Saute til tender then add garlic and saute a couple minz. Add chuck and sausage, cook and drain grease.
2. Pour in chili beans, spicy beans, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, bacon, bouillon, and beer. Season with chili powder, worcershire, oregano, cumin,  hot sauce, basil, salt pepper, cayenne, paprika, and sugar. Stir to blend, cover and simmer over low heat for about 2 hrs. Stirring occasionally
3. After 2 hrs, taste, and adjust salt an pepper, and chili powder if neccessary. The longer it cooks the better it taste. Remove from heat and serve. Top with sharp cheddar cheese, salsa, and sour cream!
