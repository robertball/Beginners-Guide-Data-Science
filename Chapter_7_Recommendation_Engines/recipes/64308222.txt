Title:
Go Berry Breakfast Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium ripe banana
1 cup frozen blueberries, thawed
1/4 cup raisins
1/8 cup pomegranate juice
2 tablespoons honey or agave nectar.
2 tablespoon finely chopped dates
1 tablespoon Goji berries (optional)
1 cup old fashioned oats
1 tablespoon chopped walnuts
1 tablespoon raw sunflower seeds
2 tablespoons ground flax seeds
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)
1/4 cup rolled oats (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a food processor or blender, process banana, blueberries, raisins, pomegranate juice and honey until smooth.  Add dates and Goji berries, if using, and process again until smooth.
In a clean food processor or blender, grind oats into flour. Add walnuts, sunflower seeds, ground flax and cinnamon and process until smooth.
In a bowl, mix wet and dry ingredients. Add chocolate chips and additional oats for texture, if desired.
Spray a cookie sheet with non-stick spray. Drop cookie mixture by spoonfuls onto baking sheet, shaping and flattening as needed.
Bake for 25 minutes.
