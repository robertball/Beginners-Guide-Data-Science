Title:
Jon Matsushita's Chicken Tortilla Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
12 tortillas
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 can water and chicken juice
1 onion chopped
3/4 lb grated cheese (cheddar and mexican)
1 can diced green chile peppers
McCormick's Mexican seasoning
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Bake chicken wrapped in foil @350 for 1 hour. Cut into small pieces
saute onions with McCormick's Mexican seasoning
Cut tortillas into 1 inch squares
Mix soups, water, juices and onions together
In a greased 12 x 16 baking dish put a layer of 1/4 of soup mixture on bottom, then 1/2 of the tortillas, 1/2 the chicken, 1/2 the chile peppers 1/4 of soup, 1/2 shredded cheese. Repeat ending with cheese
Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour @ 375. Makes 10-12 servings.
