Title:
Raspberry Corona Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of fresh or frozen raspberries
4 bottles of cold Corona beer 12 oz.
1 12 oz. container frozen pink lemonade, limeade concentrate, thawed
1 cup quality vodka
sliced lemons or limes as garnish

Directions:
To make ahead, stir together lemonade concentrate and vodka in a large container. Stir in raspberries and cold beer just before serving. Serve over ice & garnish with sliced limes or raspberries.

Trisha Frary Haynes

This is a refreshing summertime beverage!
