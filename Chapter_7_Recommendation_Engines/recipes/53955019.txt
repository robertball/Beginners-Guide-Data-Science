Title:
Easy Spicy Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup uncooked white rice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl combine the tomatoes/chiles, soup, rice, salt and pepper. Mix well. If necessary, add a little water.
Place chicken breasts in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Pour sauce mixture over chicken. Cover and bake for about 1 hour 15 minutes, until rice is tender.
