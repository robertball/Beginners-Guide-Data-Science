Title:
Chocolate, Strawberry, and Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1251992.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chocolate soy milk
1 cup plain Greek-style yogurt
1 cup frozen banana chunks
1/2 cup strawberries
1 tablespoon ground flax seed
1 teaspoon chia seeds

Directions:
Blend soy milk, yogurt, bananas, strawberries, flax seed, and chia seeds in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Delores
Description: 
This is a very healthy smoothie that works great for breakfast. My teenagers love it! Also makes for a great lunch or snack. The strawberries can be either fresh or frozen. Ice or water may be added to achieve desired consistency.
