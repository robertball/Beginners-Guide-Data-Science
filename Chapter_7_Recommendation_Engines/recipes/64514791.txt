Title:
Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Miracle Whip Light
12 Large Eggs
Paprika

Directions:
Hard Boil Eggs
Remove Eggs from Shell
Slice in Half
Remove Yolks into a Mixing Bowl
Place Whites in Serving Dish
Mix Miracle Whip and Yolks until Creamy
Fill Whites with Yolk Mixture
Garnish with Parika
Chill
