Title:
Clean Eating Avocado Sandwich Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large Ripe Avocadoes
1/2 cup Plain Greek Yogurt
1/2 tsp of sea salt
1/4 tsp of pepper
1/4 tsp of garlic powder

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a food processor and pulse to a smooth consistency

Submitter: 
Melissa Lindsey Gibson
Description: 
This is a healthier substitute for the fattier sandwich spreads. Its mild enough to be used on a variety of sandwiches or burgers and a good base that can be changed by adding different spices.
