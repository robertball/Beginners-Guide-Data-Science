Title:
Shrimp and Sugar Snap Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. Raw shrimp, peeled and deveined, tails removed
1lb Sugar snap peas
2 Large cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbsp Olive oil
1 tsp Chili oil, or to taste
1 1/2 C Dry white wine
1/4 C Pasta cooking water
Juice from 1/2 lemon
1Tbsp unsalted butter
1/3 C Chopped fresh basil
1 lb Dried linguini

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil and cook pasta per package directions.
While pasta is cooking, using a wok or large sautee pan, heat olive oil and chili oil over medium high heat. Add shrimp, pea pods and garlic and stirfry quickly about 2 minutes, shrimp will still be under cooked. Remove with slotted spoon to a bowl and set aside. Remove wok from flame and add wine. Return to flame and bring to a boil. Boil to reduce 1/3, about 2 minutes. Add shrimp and pea pods back to wok. Add 1/4 C pasta water, bring back to a boil and cook for 30 seconds. Turn off heat and add butter, lemon juice and basil. Stir to combine. Toss with the hot pasta. Serve with good Italian bread.

Submitter: 
EAKE
Description: 
Quick and easy pasta dish with a little bite that will make anyone look like a gourmet chef. Adjust the chili oil to your own spice level.
