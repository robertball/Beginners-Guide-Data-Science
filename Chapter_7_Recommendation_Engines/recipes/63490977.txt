Title:
Orange Hot-Mocha (coffee) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 orange
5 cups strong brewed coffee
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 cup whipping cream (Half & Half)
Extra whipped cream for topping (if desired)

Directions:
With a potato peeler remove the peel in strips from the orange.
In a large saucepan combne the peel strips and hot coffee.  Let stand over medium-low heat for 5 minutes.  Remove and discard orange peel.
In a small bowl shisk together the cocoa powder, brown sugar and cinnnamon.  Whisk cocoa mixture into hot coffee until well combined.  Stir cream into coffee.
Top with whipped cream if desired.  Sprinkle lightly with cinnamon.
