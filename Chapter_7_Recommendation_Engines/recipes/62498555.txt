Title:
Roast Beef and Provolone Cheese - That's a wrap! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16-24 thin slices of deli roast beef
12 slices of provolone cheese
Horsey Sauce
8 (10") flour tortillas

Directions:
Spread horsey sauce on tortilla.
Layer 2-3 slices of beef and 1 1/2 slices of cheese in the middle of each tortilla.
Repeat for each tortilla.
Roll and wrap firmly in waxed paper.  Secure with toothpick.
Refrigerate overnight.
When ready to serve, remove toothpick, leave wrapped and slice in half.
Arrange on serving tray and place bowl of extra horsey sauce in the center of the platter.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Easy to make and easy to serve, these wraps are a hit for any type of get-together.
