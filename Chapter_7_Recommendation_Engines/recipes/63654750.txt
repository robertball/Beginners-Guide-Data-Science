Title:
T.V Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tbsp butter
1/2 c flour
1/2 c sugar
1 tsp baking powder
pinch of salt
1/2 c milk
1 can pie filling (your choice)

Directions:
Preheat over to 400
Place Butter in 8x8 baking dish
Microwave until butter is melted
In a medium mixing bowl, combine all dry ingredients and whisk until mixed well.
Add milk, continue whisking until there are no lumps
Pour mixture evenly into the baking dish.  Do not stir.
Spoon in the pie filling evenly, again do not stir.
Bake for 30 min or until golden brown
