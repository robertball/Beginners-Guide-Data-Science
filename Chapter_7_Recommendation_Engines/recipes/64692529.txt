Title:
Onion Soup Mix/ Dip Recipe (Homemade, dairy free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5165486.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp organic dehydrated onion flakes (or try this tutorial on how to make your own dried onions in the dehydrator)
1 tbsp organic dried parsley or 3 tbsp of organic fresh parsley
1 tsp organic garlic powder or 1/2 tsp fresh organic garlic, minced
1/2 tsp Celtic sea salt or Himalayan salt (or to taste)
Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
Easy Onion Dip Recipe
All of the above
16 oz organic or raw sour cream (full fat)

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, add all dry ingredients.
Store in an air-tight container and use as needed.
Easy Onion Dip Recipe
Prep time includes soaking cashews or almonds for sour cream.
Add sour cream to dry mix ingredients until mixed well.
Wait 5-10 minutes for dip to settle, and then serve.
To make cashew sour cream: Soak 2 cups raw cashews in water for 6-8 hours. Drain liquid. Puree cashews with 2 1/2 tbsp apple cider vinegar and 1/2+ tsp salt. Add 1 1/4 cup water (adding 1/4 at a time) until you reach desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
I love this recipe. It's a piece of cake to make. The basic spices are just 4 ingredients + sour cream = a fresh, easy homemade French Onion Dip anyone can make. You can even pre-make the 4 spices and store in small containers to use as needed.
