Title:
Sweet and Spicy barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup ketchup
1/2 cup honey
1 tblsp onion powder
1 tblsp garlic powder
3 tblsp mustard
3 tblsp apple cider vinegar
1/2 tsp dry mustart
1 tsp chili powder
1 - 2 tsp cajun seasoning or to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a small saucepan and cook over low heat for 20 - 30 minutes.  Great on Ribs, chicken and burgers, always add sauce at the end of your grilling so that the sugars do not burn

Submitter: 
Missie Wallace Petersen
Description: 
A sweet and spicy barbeque sauce that you control the heat.
