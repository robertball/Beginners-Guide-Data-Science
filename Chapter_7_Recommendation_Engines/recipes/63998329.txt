Title:
Cinnabon Cinnamon Roll Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 3 cups flour
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 1 cup sugar
* 4 teaspoons baking powder(or none if using self rising flour)
* 1 1/2 cups milk
* 2 eggs
* 2 teaspoons vanilla
* 4 Tablespoons butter, melted
* 2 sticks (1 cup) butter, softened
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 2 Tablespoons flour
* 1 Tablespoon cinnamon
* 2/3 cup nuts chopped (preferably walnut and is optional)
Glaze:
* 2 cups powdered sugar
* 5 Tablespoons milk
* 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
With an electric mixer or stand -up mixer, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, milk,eggs and vanilla. Once combined well, slowly stir in 4 Tablespoons melted butter. Pour into a greased 9x13-inch baking pan.
In a large bowl, mix the 2 sticks of softened butter, brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, and nuts until well combined. Drop evenly over cake batter by the Tablespoon and use a knife to marble and swirl through the cake. Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out nearly clean from center.
Place powdered sugar, milk and vanilla in a large bowl. Whisk until smooth. Drizzle over warm cake. Serve warm at room temperature.
