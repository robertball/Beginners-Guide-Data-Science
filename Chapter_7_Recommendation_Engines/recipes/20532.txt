Title:
Delicious Fish Smoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/923429.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (4 ounce) fillet salmon
1 cup strawberries, hulled
1 banana
3/4 apple - peeled, cored, and chopped
1/3 cup cold water
1 cup vanilla yogurt

Directions:
In a blender combine fish, strawberries, banana, apple, water and yogurt. Blend until smooth. pour into glasses and serve.
