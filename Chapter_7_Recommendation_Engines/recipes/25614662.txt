Title:
Tuscan Black and White Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15-oz. can Tuscan or any white beans, drained
1 quart chicken or vegetable  both
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 tsp. olive oil
1 tsp. butter
1/4 tsp. fennel seeds
1/2 tsp. fresh rosemary, stemmed and chopped
1 Tbl. basmati rice
1 lb. skinless, bonless chicken thighs, thinly sliced
1 cup white or yellow onion, finely chopped
1 cup Florence fennel, coarsely grated
1 cup sweet carrots, coarsely grated
4 cups Tuscan Black or any kale, syhredded
1/4 tsp. kosher or sea salt
1/4 tsp. fresh ground pepper
4 green onions, thinly sliced

Directions:
Rinse and drain beans well, then puree coarsely in a food processor with 2 cups broth and 1 clove garlic. Set aside.
In a heavy soup pan, heat oil, butter, remaining garlic, fennel seeds, rosemary and rice over medium-high heat and cook for 2 minutes.
Add chicken and cook, stirring, until opaque, 5 to 7 minutes.  Add onion, fennel and carrots, stir to coat, cover pan and cook until vegetables sweat their juices, 6 to 8 minutes.
Add beans and remaining broth, bring to a simmer, cover and cook over low heat for 30 minutes.  Add kale, cover pan and cook until barely wilted, 3 to 4 minutes.
Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve, garnished with green onions.
