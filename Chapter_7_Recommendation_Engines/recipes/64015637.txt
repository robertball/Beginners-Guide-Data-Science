Title:
Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 egg
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
3 tbsp cocoa
3/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup nuts or coconut (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Prepare 8"x8" pan.
Mix melted butter and sugar. Beat in egg and vanilla.
Sift in rest of ingredients except the nuts. Mix until blended.
Add nuts or coconut if desired.
Pour in pan and bake for 25 minutes.
Remove from oven and ice with chocolate icing.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
An all time favorite from the Mennonite Treasury of Recipes. The perfect texture for a Brownie - chewy and not cakey.
