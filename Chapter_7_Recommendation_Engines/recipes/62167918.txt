Title:
Quick Dippin Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Tahini paste
2 heading tablespoons Orange Marmalade
1 tablespoon water
1 teaspoon Rice Wine Vinager

Directions:
Mix together the Tahini Paste AND the Orange Marmalade
until well blended. Add the Rice Wine Vinager, stir and then add the water and stir until the water incorporates and the mixture is smooth.

Submitter: 
souplover Sue
Description: 
Raw veggies and crackers are my usual lunch, this takes off there harsh edge and add more nutrition as well as flavor. Now I dont reach for the Blue Cheese dressing.
