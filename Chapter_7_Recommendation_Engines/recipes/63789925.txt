Title:
Ginger Lemon Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter softened
1/2 to 1 cup brown sugar, packed
1 egg
1/4 cup molasses
3 lemons zested
2" fresh ginger, grated (or 3 tsp ground ginger powder)
2 cups 100% whole wheat flour
2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
*1/4 cup granulated sugar for rolling (optional)

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar. Add egg, molasses, lemon zest, and fresh ginger and beat until creamy.
Sift dry ingredients together and mix into dough until combined. Chill in fridge for 30 minutes.
Roll into 1/2 inch or 1" balls (.70 oz), roll in sugar if desired and chill for 30 minutes if you didn't chill before rolling.
Bake at 350 for 8-12 minutes

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
A fresh twist to a family classic. Fresh lemon and ginger give a zing to these delightful cookies.
