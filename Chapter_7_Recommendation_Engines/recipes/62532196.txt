Title:
Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cup Butter or Margarine
3 Cups Sugar
3 Cups mashed Bananas (about 6 medium)
4 Eggs well beaten
2 teaspoon Vanilla
4 Cups Flour
2 teaspoon Soda
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1 Cup Buttermilk
1 1/2 Cups Walnuts

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar throughly.  Blend in bananas, eggs, and vanilla.  Sift flour, baking soda and salt together.  Add banana mixture, alternating with buttermilk, mixing throughly after each addition.  Add nuts, mix.  Pour into greased and floured 2 9x5x3" loaf pans or 8 5x2 mini loaf pans.  Bake3 at 325 degreesfor 1 1/4 hr for 9x5 loaf pan or 36 minutes for 5x3 loaf pans   Freezes well.
