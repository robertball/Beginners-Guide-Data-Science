Title:
Autumn Crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c mashed acorn squash
1/3 c packed brown sugar
¼ c flour
1 egg
1 T milk
1 t each vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg
¼ t ground cloves
2 large apples, peeled and chopped
1 large carrot, shredded
½ c raisins
½ c rolled oats
¼ c wheat bran
¼ c brown sugar
2 T ww flour
2 T melted butter
1 t cinnamon

Directions:
Blend the squah, sugar, flour, egg, milk and spices in the food processor.
Add apples, carrot and raisins and spread in an 8x8 pan.
Mix together remaining ingredients and spread on top.
Bake at 350 for 30 to 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
LMLSCA
Description: 
A healthy recipe from my mother-in-law that includes both fruit and vegetables in a yummy fall dessert.
