Title:
Ajam Gula Jawa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs Chicken Wings
1 Onion chopped
2 tbsp sambal Djeroek
1 cup Ketjap
2 tsp Garlic powder
2 tsp Djahe or Ginger powder
1/2 cup Gula Jawa or Stroop (Dutch Molasses)
2 tsp Oregano
2 tbsp Sesame oil

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except sesame oil, in a bowl and stir to coat evenly.
Allow to marinate for at least half an hour.
Heat sesame oil in wok. preheat oven to 350 F
Remove wings from marinade, and reserve.
Brown chicken in oil.
Arrange wings in oven dish, and cover with marinade
Roast wings for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
