Title:
Colcannon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cabbage, ribs removed, or kale
1 pound potatoes
2 leeks
1 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste
1 pinch ground nutmeg
1/2 cup butter

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute cabbage and leeks (green & white parts) in butter until tender; remove and chop or blend well. Set aside and keep warm. Boil potatoes until tender. Remove from heat and drain.
Add seasonings & milk and mash potatoes well (add more milk if necessary). Stir in cooked leeks and cabbage (or kale) and heat until the whole is a pale green fluff. Make a well in the center and pour in the melted butter. Mix well.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
This is a recipe from County Mayo. It is traditional Halloween fare but good anytime! Kale can be substituted for cabbage.Modified from: Colcannon
