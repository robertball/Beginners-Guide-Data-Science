Title:
Ginger Molasses Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/684958.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C.  All purpose flour
1/2 C. All bran, flaxseed or wheat germ
1 tsp. Baking Powder
3/4 C. Granulated sugar
1 tsp. Cinnamon
1/4 tsp. Salt
2 eggs
1/4 C. Canola Oil
3/4 C. Milk
1/2 C. Fancy Molasses
2 Tbsp. Fresh Ginger (finely chopped)

Directions:
1.  Grease a 12 cup muffin pan and set aside.
2.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees
3.  Combine dry ingredients in a medium size bowl and set aside.
4.  Beat eggs, oil, milk and molasses in a separate bowl until well blended together.
5.  Add the ginger and stir until just incorporated.
6.  Pour batter into greased muffin cups and fill 2/3 full.
7.  Bake for 20 minutes and remove when nicely browned.
8.  Remove muffins on to a serving plate when nice and cool.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
Very closely related to the Bran Muffin with a subtle hint of ginger that makes it a very spicy muffin.
