Title:
Pecan Bars by EAGLE BRAND® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981355.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
4 eggs, divided
2 cups chopped pecans
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
1 cup light corn syrup
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. In large bowl, combine cake mix, butter and 1 egg; mix well (mixture will be crumbly).
Reserve 2/3 cup crumb mixture; press remainder firmly on bottom of 13x9-inch baking pan. Bake 10 minutes.
In medium bowl, combine reserved crumb mixture, 3 eggs, pecans, EAGLE BRAND(R), corn syrup, brown sugar and vanilla. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until center is set. Cool. Cut into bars. Store leftovers covered at room temperature.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
These quick and easy pecan bars are perfect for the holidays.
