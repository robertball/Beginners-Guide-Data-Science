Title:
Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Whole chicken
1 stalk of celery
4 carrots
1 large onion
1 (14.5oz) can of chicken broth
2 tbsp dried parsley
2 tsp chicken bouillon (w/out msg)
1 tsp pepper
water
2 cups of flour
1&1/5 tsp baking powder
3 tsp cream of tartar
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup of milk
4 tbsp oil

Directions:
Combine chicken, celery,carrots, onion, broth, parsley, bouillon, salt, pepper and enough water to cover all.
bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer for 2 hours
shred chicken, removing all skin and bones and return to pot.
return soup to simmer
Combine dumpling ingriendients and mix well to form a stiff dough.
Drop by TBSP into simmering soup. Cover and allow to simmer for 20 minutes.
Serve!
