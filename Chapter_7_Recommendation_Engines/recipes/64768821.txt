Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 onion, diced
1 green pepper, diced, optional
Salt and pepper to taste
Garlic clove or garlic powder to taste
1 - 28 oz. can Stewed or diced tomatoes
1 lb. cooked noodles (elbow, etc.)
Mozzarella cheese for garnish

Directions:
Brown ground beef with diced onions and green peppers (if using garlic clove, go ahead and add this now as well). Drain fat. Return to pan. Add salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste. Add in tomatoes, heat and let simmer. Once your noodles are cooked mix them together with your meat and tomato mixture. Serve. *We like to serve this with Mozzarella as a garnish.

Submitter: 
Sheridan Saltonstall
Description: 
An easy and quick family favorite meal! You could also serve with garlic or cheese bread.
