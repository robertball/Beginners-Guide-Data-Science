Title:
Steph's Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/662396.jpg
Ingredients:
1 6 oz can tuna packed in water
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 Tbs butter
3/4 cup milk
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1 lb elbow noodles
1/2 cup Italian Style breadcrumbs
Salt and Pepper to Taste
cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cook elbow noodles according to package directions
Combine cooked noodles with remaining ingredients, Except for the breadcrumbs.
If mixture seems dry add more milk.
Spray casserole dish with cooking spray.
Pour noodle mixture into casserole and top with breadcrumbs.
Bake for 35-40 minutes.
