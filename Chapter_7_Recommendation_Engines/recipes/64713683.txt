Title:
Air Fryer Garlic and Parsley Baby Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound baby potatoes
1 Tablespoon Avocado or Olive Oil
1/4 Teaspoon salt
1//2 Teaspoon garlic granules
1/2 Teaspoon dried parsley

Directions:
Heat Air Fryer to 180
Quarter the potatoes into a medium bowl
Add the oil and toss to coat
Add half the garlic and parsley, turn to coat. Repeat with the other half.
Place the basket in the fryer and cook for 20-25 minutes, tossing occasionally until golden brown.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
These are very similar to roast potatoes, crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside. Enjoy!
