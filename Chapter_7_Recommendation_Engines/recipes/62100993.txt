Title:
Baked Shrimp Wontons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 wonton wrappers
1 cup cocktail shrimp
1/2 cup diced water chestnuts
1/4 cup chopped green onion
2 teaspoons sesame oil
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon finely chopped fresh ginger
salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven at 300 degrees
Spray mini (or regular size)muffin tin with cooking spray.
Put a wonton wrapper in each space - the edges will come above the rim
Mix the rest of the ingredients in a bowl leaving 1/2 the green onions aside
Fill each wonton with the shrimp mixture - about 3/4 or more full
Bake for about 18 mins (give or take a couple minutes) until the edges are dark brown
Remove from muffin tin and garnish with rest of green onions
Optional: garnish with toasted sesame seeds

Submitter: 
MAUREENVANCOUVER
Description: 
These baked wonton cups filled with shrimp are an easy and delightful appetizer for your next party.
