Title:
Aunt Faith's Clam Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can minced clams (with juice)
1 block softened cream cheese
1 small sweet onion, minced
Tabasco

Directions:
With a fork, mash the cream cheese, adding a little bit of the clam juice at a time until smooth.  Add clams and onions, stir.  Add a few drops (or more) tabasco to taste.

Submitter: 
Sharon
Description: 
An old family favorite, simple and quick, fantastic with chips or crackers.
