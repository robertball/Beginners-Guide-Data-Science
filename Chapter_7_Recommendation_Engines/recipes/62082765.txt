Title:
Perfect Cranberry Cashew Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme or 7 ounces of mini marshmallows
1-1/2 cups granulated sugar
2/3 cup (5 ounce can) evaporated milk
1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups milk chocolate chips
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup whole salted cashews
1/2 cup cherry flavored craisins
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Line an 8x8" pan with aluminum foil or waxed paper; set aside.
In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine marshmallow cream (or mini marshmallows, sugar, evaporated milk, butter and salt.  Bring contents to a full boil and cook for exactly 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
Remove from the heat and pour in all of the chocolate chips.  Stir until all of the chips are melted and mixture is smooth.  Add cashews, craisins and vanilla.  Pour into the prepared pan.  Allow to cool at room temperature or in refrigerator for 2 hours or until firm.  Cut into 1" squares and enjoy.  Merry Christmas all year long!

Submitter: 
Karen H.
Description: 
Turn into a complete Christmas Tree Hugger with this updated version of yo Mamma's fudge.  The ultra smooth and creamy consistency of this fab fudge paired with the tart and tangy cherry flavored dried cranberries and the tender crunch of the whole cashews makes this treat one more reason to leave the decorations up all year long.
