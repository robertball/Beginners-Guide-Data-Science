Title:
Spaghetti Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs ground beef
2 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon sugar
8 oz tomato sauce
1 clove garlic
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon italian seasoning
8 oz cream cheese
1 cup sour cream
2 scallions/green onions sliced thin
12 oz spaghetti(cooked and drained)
8 oz cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 deg.
Spray 9x13 pan
Saute salt, seasoning, sugar and garlic in butter for 2 min.
Add gr beef to saute mix and brown.
Add tomato sauce. Set aside.
Cream the cream cheese til smooth and mix in the sour cream. Fold in the sliced onions.
Spread the spaghetti into the pan. spread the crem cheese mixture evenly over the pasta. Spread the beef mixture on next. Finally sprinkle the cheddar cheese on top of the beef.
Bake for 30 min
