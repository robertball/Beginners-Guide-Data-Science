Title:
Moms Favorite Rolled Omlet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/835057.jpg
Ingredients:
Egg mixture:
4oz cream cheese
3/4 cup milk
2 tblsp flour
1/4 tsp salt
12 eggs
Filling:
1 1/2 cup shredded cheese - I like smoked gouda
1 cup cooked & crumbled bacon
1 cup fresh spinach leaves
1 cup sauteed sliced baby bella mushrooms
1 cup fire roasted red peppers

Directions:
Preheated oven to 375
Line a 15x10x1 jellyroll pan with parchment extendind over short ends
Spray parchment with cooking spray and set aside
In a blenders add cream cheese and milk blend until smooth
Add flour and salt to the blender and blend till smooth
with the blended still blending add eggs one at a time till blended well
Place pan on middle oven rack and preheated pan for 5 min
Pour egg mixture into pan and bake for 15-20 minutes until eggs are puffed and set
Remove pan from oven and working quickly sprinkle with 1 1/4 cup of cheese and the rest of the toppings
Starting fron the short side of the pan lift the parchment and roll the egg over the filling while removing the parchment paper contuning until it is all rolled. Leave omelet on pan and sprinkle with remaining cheese
Place back in oven until cheese is melted then remove and serve

Submitter: 
Nikki Wilson
Description: 
This is a very easy and adaptable recipe for everyones taste. The filling I listed just happen to be my favorite. Be daring and try new fillings every time you make this oven baked rolled omelet.
