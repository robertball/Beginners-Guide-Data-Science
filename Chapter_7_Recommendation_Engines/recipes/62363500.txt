Title:
Golden Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. oil/shortening
1/3 cup finely chopped onion
1 Tbsp. salt
dash pepper
1 cup water/broth
1 cup leftover mashed potatoes (or more for thicker soup)
1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup grated cheese

Directions:
Saute oil and onion until yellow.
Blend in flour, salt, and pepper.
Add water; boil 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
Add mashed potatoes, milk and cheese.
Heat slowly until cheese melts.  Do not boil.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
I often crave this creamy soup on rainy days.
