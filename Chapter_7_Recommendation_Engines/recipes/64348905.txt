Title:
Avocado Salad with Sour Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 green onions, finely chopped
1/2 cup tofu sour cream
1 tablespoon lemon juice
salt to taste
2 avocados - peeled, pitted, and cubed
red leaf lettuce

Directions:
In a large bowl, gently combine green onions, sour cream, lemon juice, and salt.
Put in refrigerator for a few hours (or as long as possible to marry the flavors)then just before ready to serve, gently fold in avocado.
Arrange salad leaves on plates, and spoon avocado salad on top.
