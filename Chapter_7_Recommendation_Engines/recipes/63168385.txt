Title:
Steve's green bean casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans french style green beans
1/4 cup dried onion flakes
1 large can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 jar cheese whiz
4 cups rice chex cereal

Directions:
pour the beans into a 9x13" pan add in the onions and mix. Spoon in the cheese and mushroom soup Stir again mixing the cheese in well. Top casserole with chex cereal and bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes
