Title:
Deb's Clean Eating Lemon Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups whole grain spelt flour
1 cup light (white) spelt flour (or you can use whole wheat pastry flour)
2/3 cup Sucanat
2 tsp. baking soda
1/8 tsp. unrefined sea salt
2 eggs
1 cup low fat buttermilk
1/2 cup all-natural or organic honey
1/2 cup extra virgin coconut oil (coconut oil is liquid at 75 degrees, but will gradually solidify at cooler temperatures)
Juice and zest of 1 fresh lemon (use more for a more distinct lemon flavor)
1 1/2 cups fresh blueberries (organic if available)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Brush muffin tin with coconut oil, or spray with non-stick spray.
In large bowl, whisk together the flours, salt and baking soda. Set aside.
In a medium bowl, beat the eggs, then whisk in the Sucanat, coconut oil, honey, buttermilk, lemon juice and lemon zest.
Pour the wet mixture into the dry and mix just until well blended. Do not beat.
Gently fold in the blueberries.
Divide equally among muffin cups, filling about 3/4 full.
Bake at 350 degrees for 18-20 minutes. Let cool in pan for 5 minutes; remove from pan and finish cooling on wire rack.
