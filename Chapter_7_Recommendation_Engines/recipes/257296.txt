Title:
Bulldog Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
ice
1 cup tequila
1 (8 ounce) can frozen limeade concentrate
1/2 cup triple sec
2 (12 ounce) bottles Mexican beer (such as CoronaÂ®)

Directions:
Fill a blender 3/4 of the way with ice. Pour in tequila, limeade concentrate, and triple sec. Blend until ice breaks down to a slushy consistency.
Pour mixture into a 24-ounce margarita glass. Invert beer bottles into the mixture so that they lean against the rim of the glass.

If you watch Jersey Shore then you have seen this massive and amazing drink! The bulldog margarita is easy to make and everyone is guaranteed to love it!

 It's a great conversation piece and will get the party started!
