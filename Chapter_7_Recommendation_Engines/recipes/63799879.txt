Title:
Beef Bourguignon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of quality cubed beef(Lean Stew Meat or Beef Chuck Roast) Salt & Pepper the meat to taste
1/2 lb of blanched pearl onions
2 medium Carrots, peeled and chopped
3/4 tsp Marjoram
3/4 tsp Thyme
1/4 Cup chopped Fresh Parsley or 4 tsp Dried Parsley
4 Cloves of Minced Garlic
1 Bay leaf
3/4 Cups of Red Wine
_________________________
4-5 Slices of bacon
1/2 Lb Mushrooms, Cleaned and quartered
1/4 tsp Thyme
1/4 tsp Marjoram
2/3 Cup Red Wine
2/3 Cup Beef Broth
2 Tbl Tomato Paste
Season with Salt & Pepper
_________________________
1/4 Cup Flour
1/3 Cup of reserved Beef Stock from the slow cooker

Directions:
Mix the first 9 ingredients together and place into a Ziploc bag and let marinate in the refrigerator at least 1 hour. Longer if possible.
Meanwhile, cook the bacon and slice into pieces.
Add the ziploc mixture to the slow cooker. Add the cooked bacon and the next 7 ingredients to the slow cooker. Stir everything together. Cover and cook on low between 4-8 hours depending on your schedule.
Near the end of the cooking process, remove 1/3 cup of beef stock from the pot. Let cool a bit and mix with flour to make a slurry. Add to slow cooker and stir together and continue to cook until the broth has thickened.
Serve over egg noodles, polenta, orzo, rice or mashed potatoes.
