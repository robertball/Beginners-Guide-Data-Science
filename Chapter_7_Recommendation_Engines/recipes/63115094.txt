Title:
Randy's Amazing Avacado Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pt. Bobby Salazar's Chile Verse Salsa
1 pt. sour cream
8 ozs. grated cheese
3 Avocados
1/4 red onion
1 seeded Jalapeno pepper
1/2 tomato

Directions:
Mash Avocados in bowl.
Mix in Sour Cream,  Jalapeno pepper and Chile Verde Salsa
Add tomato and onions.
For extra flavor add green salsa of your choice
Serve or refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
caquilter
Description: 
This is an amazing and delicious Avacado dip. Randy, a Nurse I work with, gave me his original recipe and said I could share it. It makes alot so you might cut in half but not the avacados.
