Title:
Autumn Salad (Thanksgiving 2014) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb Brussels sprouts
3/4 lb cauliflower florets (1 small head)
3 c butternut squash in 1/2" cubes
2 T olive oil
1 T balsamic vinegar
1 t salt
1/2 t nutmeg
1 c cooked cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 c pomegranate seeds
10 oz arugula or mixed salad greens
horseradish vinaigrette

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F.
Trim and halve sprouts lengthwise. Trim cauliflower into similar sized pieces. Peel and cut squash. Toss sprouts, cauliflower, and squash with oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Roast on parchment-lined baking sheet for 30 min until beginning to brown at edges.
Remove from oven and toss with beans. Cool.
Just before serving, toss vegetables and pomegranate with greens and horseradish vinaigrette.

Submitter: 
meghanmacrae
Description: 
A WOW salad. Enough for 10 generous portions. A meal in itself. Best if served just after roasted vegetables have cooled to enjoy their contrasting textures.
