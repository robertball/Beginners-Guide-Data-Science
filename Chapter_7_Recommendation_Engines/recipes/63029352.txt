Title:
Chicken Ranch Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup apple
2 Tbs. onion
1 cup cooked chicken leftovers (even lunch meat)
2 tbs. walnuts
splash of chicken stock (optional)
1-2 Tbs. fat-free Ranch dressing
2 slices bread

Directions:
Add ingredients, except bread, in order to Short Cup and twist on Cross Blade.
Quick Pulse until you've reached a good consistency.
Spread between two slices of bread.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Spread between two slices of bread or place on top of mixed salad greens.
