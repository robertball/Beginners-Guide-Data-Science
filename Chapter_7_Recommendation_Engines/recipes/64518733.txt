Title:
Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz of Macaroni Noodles
1 cup of celery
4 hard boiled eggs
1 red bell pepper
1 can of sliced olives
1 1/2 cup of light Mayonnaise
2 teaspoons of mustard
1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
2 tablespoons of splenda
2 teaspoons white vinegar
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
cook Macaroni as directed by package. Mix in a large bowl the cooked Macaroni, olives,celery, hard boiled eggs, and red peppers. In separate bowl mix Light Mayonnaise, Mustard, salt, pepper, vinegar, and splenda together. Then add to pasta mixture. chill in refrigerator over night.
