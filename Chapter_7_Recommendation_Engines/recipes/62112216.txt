Title:
Lemon Flounder Almondine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 flounder fillets
Salt and pepper
1 egg
2 tbsp. 1/2n1/2 or milk
3 tblsp. extra virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons butter
1/3 cup of flour with a few shakes of salt
1/3 cup dry white wine
juice of 1 lemon or 1/4 cup of lemon juice
1/8 tsp sugar (optional)
1 clove of garlic, minced
1 tsp. of capers (optional)
1/2 sliced toasted almonds
1 tbsp. butter
some sprigs of parsley finely chopped

Directions:
Dredge the flounder in the flour. Beat the egg with milk and salt and pepper to taste. Heat a sautee pan on med. heat with olive oil and 2 tbsp. butter. Brown for about 3 minutes or so on each side. Remove the flounder. This may have to be done in 2 batches. In the same pan, add the extra tbsp. of butter add the garlic for a few seconds. Do not brown. Add the wine and let it reduce for about 1 minute. Add the lemon juice and capers. Sprinkle in the optional sugar to counter act the possible bitterness of the lemon and some chopped parsley. Place the cooked flounders back in the pan to sop up the juices and top with the toasted almonds.

Submitter: 
joannemerc
Description: 
A light delicious seafood dish we all love. I would serve it over a bed of garlic sauteed escarole or spinach. Any fillet of fish can be substituted. Tilapia works very well also.
