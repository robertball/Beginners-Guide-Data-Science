Title:
Cheese Rolls for the Bread Machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. yeast
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 butter or margarine
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
3/4 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Put all all ingred. into bread machine. Press dough cycle and start.
When cycle is complete, preheat oven to 400. Roll dough out on a floured surface to a 12-in. circle. Cut into 8 wedges. Roll each wedge up from the wide end. Put rolls on a greased baking sheet and bake a 400 for about 15-20 min.

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
A yummy cheese bread made with the ease of a bread machine. I modified this from a biscuit recipe. Try a blend of shredded cheese for added flavor. Great with soups.
