Title:
Colorful Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
4 tablespoons milk
2 tablespoons KOOL-AID Sugar-Sweetened Soft Drink Mix, any flavor

Directions:
Beat sugar, butter, milk and soft drink mix in large bowl with electric mixer on low speed until well blended and smooth. Use immediately.
