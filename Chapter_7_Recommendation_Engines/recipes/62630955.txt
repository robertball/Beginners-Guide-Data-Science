Title:
Smokey Sweet Pepper Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/577035.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of cooked chickpeas, rinsed and drained
4 cloves garlic
1 red bell pepper, cut into quarters
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp smoked paprika
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
1/4 tsp cumin
3 tbsp lemon juice
2 tbsp olive oil
dash liquid smoke
salt to taste

Directions:
Puree garlic and peppers in a food processor.
Add remaining ingredients and enough water to help the food processor blend it all together.
Puree until very smooth.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
I came up with this one night when I was making hummus to eat with chicken and pitas.  I had a red pepper to use up and decided to go different with my seasonings, I'm so glad that I did!
