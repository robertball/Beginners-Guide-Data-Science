Title:
Israeli-Style Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dried chickpeas
1 tsp baking soda
6 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
4 garlic cloves, grated
1 cup tahini (roasted sesame seed paste), stirred well
1/2 cup ice water, or more as needed
1 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp ground cumin

Directions:
Place chickpeas in large bowl; cover with water to 2 inches above chickpeas. Soak overnight at room temperature. Drain chickpeas.
Place chickpeas and baking soda in large Dutch oven. Add enough water to cover chickpeas by 4 inches (about 5 cups); bring to boil. Skim any residue that rises to the surface.
Reduce heat to medium; cover and simmer 50 minutes or until chickpeas are very tender. Drain.
Combine juice and garlic in medium bowl; let stand 10 minutes. Add tahini, stirring with whisk. Add ice water,  1 tablespoon at a time, stirring with whisk after each addition until tahini mixture falls off whisk in thick ribbons.
Place chickpeas in food processor. Add tahini mixture; process until very smooth. Add salt and cumin; process to combine.
Place hummus in airtight container; refrigerate 2 hours or up to overnight.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
A little baking soda softens the chickpeas for an ethereally smooth texture.
