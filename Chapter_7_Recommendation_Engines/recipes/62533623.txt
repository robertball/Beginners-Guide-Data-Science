Title:
Spaghetti Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/519152.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pts. frozen corn (thawed)
6 oz. angel hair pasta (broken up)
1 stick butter cut up
1/2 loaf Velveeta cut up
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in microwavable bowl.  Microwave until spaghetti is tender.  Be sure to mix every 5 minutes or so.  Move to a 350 degree oven for a few minutes to get a little brown on top.
