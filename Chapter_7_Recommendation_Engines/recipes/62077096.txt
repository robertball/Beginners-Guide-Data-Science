Title:
Amazing Cheddar-Onion Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large sweet onion, such as Vidalia, chopped
1/4 c. butter or margarine
1 c. sour cream
1/4 t. salt
1/2 t. dill weed
1 pkg. (8 1/2oz.) corn muffin mix
1/3 c. milk
1 can (8 1/4oz.) cream-style corn
2 drops hot pepper sauce
1/2 to 1 c. shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Saute the onion in butter until it becomes translucent. Cool for several minutes.
Mix sour cream, salt and dill weed with onions. Set aside.
Mix corn muffin mix, milk, cream-style corn and hot pepper sauce together. Put in a greased 9" square baking pan.
Pour onion mixture over top of corn muffin mixture.
Bake at 425 degrees for 15 minutes. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese on top and bake for another 15 minutes.
Cool slightly... best served warm.

Submitter: 
Diana S.
Description: 
This is a simple and delicious cornbread recipe that makes a meal in itself. Just serve with a crisp green salad and you're all set! FYI: I've had good results using low fat sour cream and cheddar cheese.
