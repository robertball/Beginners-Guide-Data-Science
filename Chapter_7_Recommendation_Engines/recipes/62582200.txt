Title:
Cranberry Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling: 3 C peeled, cored, sliced apples
2 C fresh or frozen thawed cranberries
1 C Equal Spoonful (substitute 24 pks Equal sweetener
Topping: 1/3 C all-purpoose flour
1/4 C chopped pecans
1/4 C stick butter or margarine, melted
1/2 C Equal Spoonful (substitute 12 pks Equal sweetener

Directions:
For filling, combine apples, cranberries, and 1 C Equal in an ungreased 10-in pie plate.
For topping: combine flour, pecans, melted butter and 1/2 C Equal until well blended. Sprinkle flour mixture over apples and cranberries in pie pan.
Bake in preheated 350 degree overn 55-60 min. or untl fruit is tender. Serve warm or at room temperature.
