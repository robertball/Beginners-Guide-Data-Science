Title:
scalloped potatoes 4 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 to 8 potatoes
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 of a green pepper chopped
1 medium onion chopped
1 soup can milk
3 tablespoons flour
Salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 cup butter
1 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Peel and slice potatoes. Boil potatoes for 10 minutes drain well. Spray casserole dish with Pam. Place potatoes, green pepper and onion in casserole dish. Top with cheese. Mix soup, milk, flour and seasonings and pour over casserole. Dot with butter. Cover and bake at 350 for 1 to 1-1/2 hours until potatoes are tender. Stir often while cooking.
