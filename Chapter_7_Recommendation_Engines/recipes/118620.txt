Title:
Glazed Baby Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984551.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup light brown sugar
1 (16 ounce) package ready-to-eat peeled baby carrots, cooked
1 packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix

Directions:
Melt butter and sugar in large skillet.  Add carrots and seasoning & salad dressing mix.  Stir well.  Cook over medium heat until hot and glazed, about 5 minutes, stirring frequently.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Ranch dressing mix adds zest to this quick and easy side dish.
