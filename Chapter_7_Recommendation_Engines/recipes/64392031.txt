Title:
Browned Butter Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2228200.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 Cup Browned Butter, salted
3 Very ripe Bananas
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1 large egg
1 Tablespoon of Vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1.5 Tsp Baking soda
1 teaspoon cinnamon(optional)
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg(optional)

Directions:
Place butter in a small saucepan over medium heat and melt slowly. Bring butter to a low simmer and continue to simmer,stirring frequently, until butter browns (this can take anywhere from 5-10 minutes) (butter will be foamy, and there will be brown bits in it.)it will also smell FANTASTIC.
Remove butter from heat and cool to room temp.
Preheat oven to 350, and grease a loaf pan
In a small bowl whisk together flour and baking soda (cinnamon and nutmeg can be added in too if you decide to use it)
In another bowl mash bananas.Stir in sugars, egg, vanilla, and cooled Browned Butter. Mix well.
Fold Dry Mixture slowly into wet, DO NOT OVERMIX.
Pour into loaf pan and bake for 45-55 Mins(depending on oven)
Enjoy HOT fresh from oven.

Submitter: 
Chef V
Description: 
Browning the Butter really add a depth of flavor, not to mention smells EXTRA AMAZING when in the oven. This is my go to recipe when I have ripe bananas, and has taken me a while to get it right.
