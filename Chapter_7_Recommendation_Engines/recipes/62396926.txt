Title:
Apple Berry Salsa & Baked Cinnamon Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Apple Berry Salsa:
2 medium Granny Smith apples
1 pint strawberries, diced
2 kiwi, peeled and diced
1 small orange
2 tablespoons packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons apple jelly or apricot jam
Baked Cinnamon Chips:
8 (7 inch) flour tortillas
1 tablespoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Peel, core and slice apples.  Coarsely chop apple slices.  Dice strawberries and kiwi.  Place fruit in bowl.
Zest orange to measure 1 teaspoon zest.  Juice orange to measure 2 tablespoons of juice.  Add orange zest, juice, brown sugar and jelly to fruit mixture; mix gently.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
To make baked cinnamon chips, preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Lightly spray each tortilla with water.  Combine 1 tablespoon sugar and 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon and sprinkle evenly over each tortilla.  Cut each tortilla into 8 wedges and bake for 8 - 10 minutes or until edges are lightly browned and crisp.  Remove from baking pan and cool completely.  Repeat with remaining tortillas.
Spoon Apple Berry Salsa into serving bowl and serve with baked cinnamon chips.
