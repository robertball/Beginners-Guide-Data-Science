Title:
Smucker's® Apple Raspberry Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986213.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup SMUCKER'S® Seedless Red Raspberry Jam
4 cups apple juice
2 teaspoons lemon juice

Directions:
Gradually add apple juice to Smucker's jam while stirring with a wire whisk; mix well.
Stir in lemon juice.

Submitter: 
Smucker's®
Description: 
Raspberry jam is the secret ingredient in this refreshing twist on apple juice.
