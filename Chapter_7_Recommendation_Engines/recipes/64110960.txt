Title:
Chile & Cheese Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white rice, uncooked
1 pint sour cream
1 to 2 cups shredded jack/cheddar cheese
7 oz. can diced chiles (Ortega)
Salt to taste

Directions:
Cook rice and then combine with sour cream. Layer half of mixture in a greased 9 X 13
casserole dish. Sprinkle cheese and chiles on top then evenly spread the rest of the
rice mixture on top. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees until hot. Serves 8.

Submitter: 
LaurenSyd
Description: 
This is a tangy rice casserole perfect for serving with steak or any Mexican meal.
