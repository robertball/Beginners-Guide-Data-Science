Title:
Moussaka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egglpants (about 1 1/2 pounds). sliced crosswise 1/4 inch thick.
4 zucchini (about 2 pounds), sliced crosswise 1/4 inch thick.
Salt and pepper
1/4 cup extra-virgin loive oil
1 bunch scallions, thinly sliced
3 gloves garlic
1 15 oz crushed tomatoes
3/4 lb lean ground beef
3 oz gruyere cheese, shredded
1 pint low-fat cottage cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 500. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper. Place the egglpant and zucchini in two large bowls and season with salt; let stand for 40 minutes. Rinse the vegetables and pat dry. Arrange in a single layer on the prepared pans and toss with 3 tbl olive oil. Roast until golden, about 20 minutes. Transfer to a 9 x 11 baking dish. Lower the oven temperature to 350.
Meanwhile, in a large skellet, heat the remaining 1 tbl olive oil over medium heat. Add the scalions and garlic and cook, stirring for 2 minutes; add the ground beef and cook, breaking up the meat.
Add the can of crushed tomatoes and simmer until thichened about 10 minutes; season with salt and pepper.
Spoon the meat sause over the eggplant and zucchini half of the gruyere.
Using food processor puree the cottage cheese. Spoon the puree onto the moussaka, and top with the remaining gruyere and bake for 17 minutes.
Let sant for 15 minutes before slicing.
