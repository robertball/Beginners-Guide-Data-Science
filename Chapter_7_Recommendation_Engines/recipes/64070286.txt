Title:
Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz. can crushed pineapple
21 oz. can cherry pie filling
1 box super moist butter recipe cake mix
1 cup (2) sticks margarine

Directions:
Dump pineapple in 9 x 13 pan, spreading evenly
Dump cherry pie filling on top, do not mix
Dump cake mix,spreading evenly
Cut margarine into pads & cover top of cake mix
Bake in pre-heated oven at 325 degrees for 45 minutes.
