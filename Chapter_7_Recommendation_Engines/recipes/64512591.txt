Title:
Angel Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
half a stick of butter
1 pkg. dry Italian salad dressing mix
1/2 cup white wine
10 3/4oz. condensed golden mushroom soup
half an 8 oz. pkg of cream cheese with chives
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
In large saucepan, melt butter over low heat.  Stir in salad-dressing mix.  Blend in wine and soup.  Mix in cream cheese and stir until smooth.  Do not boil.
Arrange chicken in slow cooker.  Pour sauce over top.
Cover.  Cook on low 5-6 hours, or until chicken is tender but not dry.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
Not alfredo!  Close to a chicken stroganoff!  Serve over pasta.
