Title:
Hawaiian Delight Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
2 cup cinnamon graham crumbs
1/2 cup melted butter
Filling
2 pkgs. banana or vanilla instant pudding
3 cup cold milk
3/4 cup shredded coconut
1/2 tsp. rum extract
3 ripe bananas, sliced
1 can (20oz) crushed pineapple, well drained
1 cup whipping cream
toasted coconut for topping

Directions:
Combine crumbs and melted butter and pat down in 9 X 13 pan.  Prepare pudding with 3 cup milk as per package directions.  Stir in coconut and rum extract.  Arrange banana slices over crust and spoon pudding mixture over bananas to cover completely.  In bowl, whip the cream and add pineapple before spreading over pudding mixture.  Top with toasted coconut.  Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
