Title:
Linguine (pasta) salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/362324.jpg
Ingredients:
Marinade for chicken breast
1 tbsp honey
Juice from 1  lemon
1 clove garlic, minced
1 Skinless, boneless chicken breast
Salad dressing
Juice from 1 lemon
1 tbsp brown sugar
½ tsp dried tarragon
2 tbsp olive oil
1 small clove of garlic, minced
Linguine salad
½ pound linguine
½ large sweet red pepper, diced
½ large sweet orange pepper, diced
1/3 cup sun dried tomatoes in oil, julienned
1 1/2 cups fresh spinach chopped
Fresh ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Marinate chicken for ½ hour. Cook on grill over low heat till done.  Allow to cool and cut into bite sized pieces.
While chicken is marinating, place  ½ pound linguine in lightly salted boiling water, cook for 10 minutes after water has come back to the boil.
Combine dressing ingredients in a small bowl and whisk till blended, set aside.
Pour cooked pasta into strainer, rinse in cold water till pasta is cold. Cut into small pieces.  Chop spinach and place into salad bowl with peppers, linguine, sun dried tomatoes and chicken. Toss with dressing.
