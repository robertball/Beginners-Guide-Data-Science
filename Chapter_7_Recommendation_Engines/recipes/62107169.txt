Title:
Traceys flavorful meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 to 1 1/2 pound ground beef
half of a package of dry onion soup mix
1/2 tbsp. dried minced onion (from spice section in your grocery store)
1 tsp. Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 small can (5 oz.) evaporated milk
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
3 tablespoons ketchup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a small bowl, mix together the onion soup mix, evaporated milk, minced onion, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic powder until well blended.
In a medium bowl, crumble uncooked ground beef.
Pour milk mixture over ground beef, and mix with your hands until well blended.
Place meat into a loaf pan and press down firmly, pushing meat to the edges, to make the loaf level.
In a small bowl, mix together the ketchup, mustard, and brown sugar.
Pour over the meat, and spread evenly over the top.
Place the loaf into the preheated oven uncovered and bake for 55-60 minutes.

Submitter: 
T. Kent
Description: 
This meatloaf uses only 8 ingredients with no breadcrumbs and no egg.  It stays together better then any other recipe I have tried before! It is very flavorful (hence the name)and original. The glaze over the top will make your mouth water.  Let me know what you think!
