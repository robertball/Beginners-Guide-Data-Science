Title:
Amish Friendship Bread Starter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cup Sugar
3 cup Milk
3 cup Flour

Directions:
On day 22 stir and add 1 cup flour, 1 cup sugar and 1 cup milk. Stir/squish again.
On days 23, 24, 25 and 26 stir/squish.
On day 27, add 1 cup flour, 1 cup milk and 1 cup sugar. Stir/squish.
You should have about 4 cups starter. Give 1 cup each to two friends (with instructions below) and keep the remaining two cups for yourself.
