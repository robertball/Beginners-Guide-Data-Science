Title:
Amish Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine
1cup brown sugar 1 cup white sugar
2 cups buttermilk
3 cups flour
2 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp salt
Topping:
6 Tbl. butter
4 Tbsp milk
1 cup brown sugar
1/ cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Cream together Margarine, brown sugar, white sugar. Add buttermilk. Add flour, salt and last, vanilla. Pour into a greased 9 X 13 greased pan. Bake 350 degrees 1 hour. then spread topping.
Topping:
Cream together  butter, milk, broen sugar then stir in nuts.
Spread on top of cake and return to oven until bubbly, about 5 min.
Remove cake from oven and cool

White cake with brown sugar and nuts on top
