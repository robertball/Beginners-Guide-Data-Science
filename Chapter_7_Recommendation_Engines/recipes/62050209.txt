Title:
Ham & Cheese Souffle' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 slices white bread - cut off crusts & cube
1 lb. ham, cubed
1 lb. sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1 1/2 cup Swiss cheese, cubed
6 eggs
3 cups milk
1/2 tbsp. onion salt
1/2 tbsp. dry mustard
3 cups cornflakes, crushed
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Spread 1/2 of the bread cubes evenly in a baking dish.  Add ham and both cheeses.  Cover with remaining bread cubes.  Mix eggs, milk, onion salt and dry mustard.  Pour evenly over bread cubes and refrigerate overnight.
Combine cornflakes and butter for topping; spread evenly over top.
Bake at 375 degrees for 40 minutes.
Serve immediately.
