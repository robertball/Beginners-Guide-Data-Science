Title:
Chicken and Artichoke in a Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/349981.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbs butter
6 skinless boneless chicken thighs (or 4 breasts)
I can quartered artichoke hearts
1/2 red bell pepper, cut into strips
1/2 large onion chopped
1 clove garlic crushed
1/4 cup dry white wine
1 tsp thyme

Directions:
Melt 2 tbs butter in skillet over medium heat (be careful not to burn)
Saute Chicken until done/brown (5-7min). Remove from skillet and set on plate.
Melt remaining tbs butter and add artichoke, pepper, onion, garlic into skillett. Saute for 3 minutes.
Pour in wine and sprinkle thyme over vegetables. Place the chicken over the vegetable, simmer for 15 min on low heat
