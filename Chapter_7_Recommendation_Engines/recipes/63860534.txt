Title:
pumpkin cream cheese mini loaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
bread part:
2 cups canned pumpkin
3 eggs
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp baking soda
2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
cream cheese filling:
8 oz cream cheese softened to room tempature
4 Tbsp sugar
1 egg
2 tsp flour

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease pan
bread layer:
In a bowl of your stand mixer or hand mixer combine pumpkin and eggs until smooth.
In a separate medium bowl whisk together flour, sugars, baking soda, and pumpkin spice.
Slowly add dry ingredients into wet mixing until just smooth, then set aside.
Cream Cheese layer:
In another bowl combine all ingredients until smooth.
Next the bread assembly:
Pour or spoon half of pumpkin bread mixture into bottom of each loaf
divide cream cheese mixture evenly into each loaf and smooth out.
pour remaining pumpkin bread mixture over each cream cheese section
then back about 25-30 minutes.
let stand about 10-15 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
A very unique and moist pumpkin bread. Our family loves these. great for anytime snacks.
