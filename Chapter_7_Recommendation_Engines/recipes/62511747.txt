Title:
SCD Fried Almond Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. SCD yogurt
4 eggs
2 c. almond flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 c. cheddar cheese

Directions:
Mix ingredients together in food processor. Fry like pancakes on a griddle at 250 until golden brown on both sides.

Submitter: 
lovetoeat
Description: 
A pancake like bread that can be used for sandwiches.
