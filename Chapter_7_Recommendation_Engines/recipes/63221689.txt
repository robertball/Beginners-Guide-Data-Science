Title:
Ziplock Freezer Smoothie Packs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/836802.jpg
Ingredients:
Per Ziploc bag:
3-Frozen Strawberries
3-cubes Frozen Mango Pulp
3-cubes Frozen Plain (or Vanilla) Yogurt
1/2 banana sliced
To Make:
Milk (any kind--soy, almond, 1%, 2%, etc)

Directions:
Freeze mango pulp in ice cube trays
Freeze yogurt in ice cube trays
Freeze hulled strawberries
In each ziploc bag place 3 strawberries, 3 cubes of frozen mango pulp, 3 cubes of plain (or vanilla) frozen yogurt, and half a sliced banana.
Freeze.
When ready to prepare smoothie add contents of ziploc bag to blender along with 1 cup of milk. Blend until smooth.
Each ziploc bag holds 1 serving.
Make several frozen servings ahead of time for quick  smoothies anytime!
