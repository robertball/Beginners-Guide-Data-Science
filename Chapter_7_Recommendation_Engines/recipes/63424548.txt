Title:
Jen's Cocunut Bon Bons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 ounces sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup butter or margarine
2 cups powdered sugar
3 cups (12 ounces) coconut flakes
24 ounces semisweet chocolate
4 tablespoons shortening

Directions:
Mix together condensed milk, butter, sugar and coconut.
Cover with wax  paper and chill for 24 hours.
Roll coconut mixture into balls and return to freezer on cutting board or cookie sheet.
When coconut balls are firm, melt chocolate with shortening and using a fork, dip the frozen balls into the chocolate.
Place on clean wax paper and return to freezer to cool and dry.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
So delicious, you won't BELIEVE how easy they are to make! Semi-sweet chocolate coats a creamy coconut filling.
