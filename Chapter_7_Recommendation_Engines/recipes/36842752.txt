Title:
Easy Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# ground beef
1 med. onion, chopped
2T all purpose flour
1/2t garlic salt
1/4t pepper
10 3/4 oz. cream of mushroom soup
8oz sour cream or soup can of skim milk mixed with 1T lemon juice
hot cooked noodles, can use whole wheat

Directions:
Cook ground beef and onion in large skillet until meat is browned, stirring to crumble meat; drain. Stir in flour and next 3 ingred.; cook and stir 1 minute. Stir in soup. Simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add sour cream, and heat thoroughly. Serve over noodles.
