Title:
Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635550.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup sugar
3 T. Flour
3 T. Cocoa
2 egg yolks
2 cups Milk
1 Pie crust

Directions:
Add above ingredients. Cook until thick and add 1 T. butter and 1 T. Vanilla.Pour in cooked pie shell. Top with Meringue or whipped cream
