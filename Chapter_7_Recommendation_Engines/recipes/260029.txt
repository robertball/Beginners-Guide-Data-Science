Title:
Easy Blended Mocha  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4557976.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1 cup cold coffee
2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 cup cold water
3/4 cup sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Place ice, coffee, and cocoa powder in a blender; blend until no big ice chunks remain. Add water and sweetened condensed milk; blend until smooth.
