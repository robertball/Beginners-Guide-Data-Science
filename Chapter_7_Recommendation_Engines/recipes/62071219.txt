Title:
Judys Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 C Warm Water
1 Package Rapid Rise Yeast
1- Teaspoon salt
1-Teaspoon Oregano
1-Teaspoon Garlic Salt
1-Tablespoon Sugar
2- Tablespoons Olive Oil
4 Cups- Flour

Directions:
Mix 1-cup flour and remaining ingredients in food processor for one minute. Add remaining 3-cups of flour and mix till dough leaves sides of container.  Oil a large glass bowl and place dough ball into bowl and oil top. Cover with plastic wrap and let rise at least 2 hours. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Roll dough out to form one  large or two regular size pizzas. Place on baking sheets and top according to taste. Bake in oven for 10 minutes. Turn off oven turn on broiler and broil for ONLY 2 minutes. IF making the extra large size pizza increase baking time 5 minutes longer. Do not change broil time. Slice and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Judy
