Title:
Shrimp enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon butter
5 or 6 chopped green onions (scallions)
1 pound of already cleaned shrimp about medium size
1 can ( 4oz.) chopped green chilis
1 jar (4oz. pimeintos (diced)save some for topping
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can of cream of shrimp soup
1 package of cream cheese ( low fat is fine)
1/8 tsp. white pepper
10-8 inch flour tortillas
8 oz. of shredded Monterey Jack Cheese, save some for topping.

Directions:
Saute shrimp in butter until tender. Add shrimp and cook til done. Stir in 1/2 of the cpimientos and the green chilis. Add, both soups and cream cheese and white pepper. Stir until cream cheese melted into mixture. Remove from heat.
To assemble:
Add half of the shrimp mixture in equal parts onto the tortillas. Roll them and put them seam side down in greased  2 qt. baking dish.
Spoon remaining mixture over enchiladas. Sprinkle with remaining pimientos and cheese.
Bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes til hot and bubbley.
