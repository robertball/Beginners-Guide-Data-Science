Title:
Creamy Caramel Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796600.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Ounces Cream Cheese, Softened
3/4 Cup Brown Sugar, Packed
8 Ounces Sour Cream
2 Teaspoons Vanilla
2 Teaspoons Lemon Juice
1 Cup Milk, Cold
1 Package Vanilla Pudding Mix

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and brown sugar until smooth.
Add the sour cream, vanilla, lemon juice, milk and pudding mix, beating well after each addition.
Cover and chill for at least 1 hour.
Serve as a dip for fruit.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
A delicious caramel dip for your favorite fruit.
