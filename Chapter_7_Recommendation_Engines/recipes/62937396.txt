Title:
Italian Sausage and Potato Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/725100.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small yellow bell pepper, seeded and cut into strips.
1 small green bell pepper, seeded and cut into strips.
1/2 sweet onion, such as vidalia, sliced.
3/4 lb red potatoes, cut into quarters.
1 Tbsp olive oil
1/4 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. garlic salt
pepper
1 lb italian sausage (about 4 links) cut into chunks.

Directions:
preheat oven to 450 degrees.  Line a large baking sheet with foil and mist foil with cooking spray.  in a large bowl toss peppers, onion and potatoes with olive oil, oregano, garlic salt and pepper.  spread vegetables on baking sheet.
Distribute sausage chunks among vegetables.  Roast until veggies begin to caramelized and sausages cooked through, about 30 minutes.  stirring once or twice during baking.
Serve hot.
