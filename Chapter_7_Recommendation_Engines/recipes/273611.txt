Title:
Grandma's Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/348942.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup shortening
6 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
1/4 cup water
4 teaspoons meringue powder
1/4 cup milk, or more as needed
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar

Directions:
Place butter and shortening in a large mixing bowl; beat with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Gradually beat in confectioners' sugar.
Mix water and meringue powder together in a small bowl. Beat into the frosting. Add milk, vanilla extract, salt, and cream of tartar; continue beating until light and fluffy. Add more milk if frosting is too thick.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is my grandma's recipe for buttercream frosting. It works wonderfully for decorated cakes, and tastes great!
