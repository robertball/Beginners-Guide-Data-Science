Title:
Low-Sugar Raspberry Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5638282.jpg
Ingredients:
6 oz fresh raspberries
1 tbs sugar free raspberry syrup (such as Torani)
2 tbs sugar
6 pkts Truvia (or choice sweetener)
1 tsp fresh squeezed lemon juice
Note:  Add an additional 2-3 packets of Truvia for a sweeter jam

Directions:
Place raspberries in a small pot and mash with potato masher
Add syrup and bring to a boil
Stir in sugar and Truvia
Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10-12 minutes until thick, stirring constantly
Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice
