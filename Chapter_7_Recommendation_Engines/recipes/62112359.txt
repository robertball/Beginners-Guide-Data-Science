Title:
Christmas Wassail with Baked Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large cooking apples, core removed
1 gallon apple cider
6 whole cloves
6 whole allspice
2 tsp. nutmeg
1 small frozen orange juice (6 oz.)
1 small frozen lemonade (6 oz.)
1 C. brown sugar
short cinnamon sticks

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees.
Cut apples in half crosswise and place cut side down on baking sheet. Bake until soft but not mushy.
In a large kettle, heat 2 C. cider, cloves, allspice and nutmeg and simmer for 10 minutes.
Add the remaining cider, undiluted orange juice and lemonade and stir to blend.
Add the brown sugar and heat but do not boil.
Serve warm. Float the bake apple halves in the bowl with the cinnamon sticks.
Can keep leftover wassil in refrigerator and heat to serve next day.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Traditional Christmas Punch garnished with floating baked apples and cinnamon sticks. Serve warm or at room temperature. Add a spray of pine or cedar frons and some shiny red apples,etc around the bowl for a festive table decoration.
Exceptionally delicious Wassail served as is or add your favorite rum to spice it up for a more festive holiday.
