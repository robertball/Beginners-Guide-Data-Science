Title:
Turkey Sandwich Condiment Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7411051.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup whole berry cranberry sauce
1 teaspoon prepared horseradish

Directions:
Mix together cranberry sauce and horseradish in a small bowl until evenly combined. Refrigerate until ready to use.

Submitter: 
SHORECOOK
Description: 
Cranberry sauce mixed with prepared horseradish enhances an ordinary turkey sandwich. Great way to use your leftover cranberry sauce and Thanksgiving turkey.
