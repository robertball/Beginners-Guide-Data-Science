Title:
Candy Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 apples
1 8 ounce package frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 8 ounce package toffee bits

Directions:
Core and cut apples into bite sized pieces.  Combine apples, whipped topping and toffee bits in a bowl.  Serve immediately.

Submitter:

Description: 
This oh-so-easy recipe is great for apple season!
