Title:
Taffy Apple Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-18 oz pkg sugar cookie
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup walnuts
1/4 cup carmel topping
cinnamon
1- 8 oz cream cheese
1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
2 med. apples

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Line cooking stone with parchment paper.
Shape dough into a ball and flatten on a cooking stone.
Bake 16-18 min. Cool 10 min.
Combine cream cheese, brwon sugar, peanut butter, vanilla and mix well.
Spread over cooled cookie.
Peel core slice apples and arrange over mixture.
Sprinkle with cinnamon, warm caramel topping and drizzle over apples.
Top with nuts.
