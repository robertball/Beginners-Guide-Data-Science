Title:
Baked Spicy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 chicken thighs with skin
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp Italian herb seasoning
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Combine garlic, paprika, Italian herb seasoning and salt.  Using your fingers, rub chicken on all sides with paprika mixture.
Place chicken on greased cookie sheet, skin side down.  Bake for 20 minutes.  Turn chicken and bake 20-30 minutes longer or until done.
