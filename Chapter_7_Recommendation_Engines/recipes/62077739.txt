Title:
Malawach (Yemenite Bread) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups flour
1 cup warm water
1 tsp salt
1/4 lb margarine

Directions:
1. Use the blade on your food processor. Add flour, salt and water; process until it forms a ball. Add more flour or water as needed to make a soft but not sticky dough. Transfer dough to a boil, cover and let stand for 1 hour.
2. Divide dough in half. Roll out one half and spread with half of the margarine. Fold in thirds, roll out and fold in thirds again. Repeat one more time. Let stand covered with a damp towel for 1 hour. Repeat with the other half.
3. Divide each half od the dough in thirds and roll out each piece very thinly.
4. Heat a skillet and add 1 tsp butter and fry bread until golden brown. Flip over and cook until golden. Repeat with the rest of the dough.
