Title:
BBQ Broccoli Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 340g bag broccoli slaw mix
1 cup mayo
1 tbsp lime juice
2 tbsp cider vinegar
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp smoked chipotle
1 tsp mesquite
2 tbsp sugar
1 tsp white pepper
1/2 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1 tbsp dried onions

Directions:
Whisk mayo, vinegar and lime juice
Add sugar, whisk until desolved
Add onions
Add remaining spices and whisk until smooth
Toss in broccoli until well coated
For best results, let marinate for 1 hour in fridge
