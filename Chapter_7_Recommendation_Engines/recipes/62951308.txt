Title:
Frozen Fruit Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. can frozen Orange Juice concentrate
20 oz. can pineapple, crushed and drained
15.25 oz. can peaches, chopped and drained
16 oz. can apricots, chopped and drained
12 oz. can mandarin oranges, drained
6 bananas, sliced
10-15 maraschino cherries, cut in half
1-2 cups sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Place mixture in 5 oz. cups.
Freeze.
Let stand at room temperature 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
This is a delicious make-ahead fruit cup that makes for a sweet and refreshing end to a breakfast or brunch.
