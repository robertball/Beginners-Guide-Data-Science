Title:
Apple Oatmeal Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups quick cooking oats
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 lb. butter, at room temperature
1 1/2 cups sour cream
2 large eggs
1 tsp. cinnamon
2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
3 medium apples, peeled and finely chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. In a bowl, mix until well combined, the oats, brown sugar and butter. Pack all but a 1/2 cup of this mixture into a 9 x 13in. pan. In another bowl mix together remaining ingredients, and spread over the oat base. Sprinkle with remaining oat mixture. Bake 25 to 30 minutes. Cool, then slice into squares. When slightly warm these squares are nice served with vanilla ice cream.

Submitter: 
JAYDA
Description: 
This recipe is a delicious alternative from the classic date square. A perfect fall dessert.
