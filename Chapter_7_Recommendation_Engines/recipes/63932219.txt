Title:
Savory Slow Cooked Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium sized chuck roast (about 3 pounds)
2 packages of brown gravy mix
2 packages of ranch dressing mix
2 packages of Italian dressing mix
1 cup of water

Directions:
Mix all seasoning packages together with the water and pour in the slow cooker.
Place the chuck roast on top of seasoning mixture.  Cover with slow cooker lid and cook on low for 9 hours.
Serve over hot cooked rice or mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
Angela Naumann
Description: 
This slow cooker version of pot roast is quick and simple to prepare.  In less then five minutes in the morning before work you can have dinner on the way.  Just toss all the ingredients in the slow cooker and wait for the magic to happen. The gravy that this recipe makes is amazing.
