Title:
Spiced Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14-oz can garbanzo beans, rinsed
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup sesame tahini (solids only!)
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
3 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 cup chicken broth
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 tablespoon cumin
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 1/2 tablespoons paprika
2 teaspoons salt

Directions:
In a food processor (or large bowl, fork, and lots of patience), mash the garbanzo beans into a paste.
Add olive oil, tahini, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and chicken broth. Mix well.
Add spices, using more or less of each to taste.
Mix thoroughly and enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
A powerful blend of spices makes this hummus the most flavorful I have ever tasted!
