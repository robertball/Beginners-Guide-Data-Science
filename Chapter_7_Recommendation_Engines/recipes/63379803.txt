Title:
Make-ahead Crock-pot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs chicken breasts
1/2 cup water
Garlic powder
Onion powder
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Put water in bottom of crock pot. Season chicken to taste. Cook on low 8-10 hours. Shred and freeze for future meals.
