Title:
Best in Show Blackberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bottom layer:
1/4 cup margerine
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 cup milk
Top layer:
2 cups blackberries
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
For the bottom layer, mix the ingredients like cake batter.  Spread batter on the bottom of a 9 inch greased pan.
For the top layer:  Place blackberries on top of the cobbler batter.  Sprinkle the sugar over the blackberries then pour 1 cup water on top.
Bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour or until the top is brown.  Cool before serving.
