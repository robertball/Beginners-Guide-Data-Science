Title:
Pork Chops and Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 pork chops
3 lbs red potatoes, scrubbed and sliced
1 med onion, sliced
1 Jar Ragu Double Cheddar sauce
4 Tbsp flour, divided
3/4 Cup milk
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place pork chops on the bottom of a 5-6 qrt crock pot.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Layer 1/4 of sliced potatoes and 1/4 of sliced onion.  Sprinkle with 1 Tablespoon of flour and pepper to taste(about 1/8 tsp).  Repeat layers.  Mix milk and cheese sauce in medium bowl.  Pour over potatoes and pork.  Slow cook on High for 6 hours.
Tip:  You can also cook in a 350 degree oven for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Jellem
Description: 
This is my husbands favorite way to have pork chops.  They stay nice and moist in the crock pot.
