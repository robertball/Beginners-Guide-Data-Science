Title:
Zucchini Pie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/457337.jpg
Ingredients:
Filling:
7-8 cups sliced zucchini with seeds & skin removed
3/4 cup sugar
2/3 cup lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon - 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 cup reserved base & topping (below)
Base/Topping:
5 cups flour
1 3/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
3 sticks butter at room temperature

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients for topping/base mix. Cut in butter till nice & crumbly. Reserve 1/2 cup base/topping for filling. Divide the remainder in half. Set aside.
Peel zucchini, cut in half long way & remove seeds. Cut in slice as for apple pie. In large pan put in sugar, spices, lemon juice & stir. Add sliced zucchini. Bring to a boil stirring occasionally till zucchini begins to soften, about 6 minutes. Stir in reserved 1/2 cup base/topping mix, stirring for a couple of minutes & it will thicken. Remove from stove & set aside.
Press half the base/topping mixture in a 15 x 10  jelly roll pan. (greased sides only) Press in tightly.  Bake 10 minutes at 350 degrees.
Spread filling over hot base.
Lay remaining topping mixture on top of zucchini & press down lightly.Return to oven & bake 35-45 minutes more or until top is lightly browned. Cool & slice. Can be served warm with ice cream.
