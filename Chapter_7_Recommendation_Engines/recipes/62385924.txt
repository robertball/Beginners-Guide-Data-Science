Title:
Quick Whole Wheat Seed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 c + 1 T warm water
1 1/2 c bread flour
1 c whole wheat flour
1/2 c whole wheat pastry flour
3 T flax meal
1 T canola oil
1 T olive oil
1 T agave
1 pkg NuStevia
1 t salt
1 t yeast
2/3 c sunflower seeds

Directions:
In bread maker pan, add ingredients and bake according to manufacturer's directions.
Note:
Baking time is for my bread maker's "quick wheat" cycle.

Submitter: 
4givnCyn
Description: 
This delicious and healthy bread was adapted from another great recipe to suit my needs.  It turned out just right with my bread maker's quick wheat cycle.
