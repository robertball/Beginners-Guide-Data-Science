Title:
Peanut butter Bon Bons Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 8 oz graham cracker crumbs
2. 2 sticks butter
3. 1 box ( 16 oz) powder sugar
4. 12 oz crunchy peanut butter
5. 1 tea vanilla
6. 12 oz chocolate chips milk chocolate

Directions:
In large sauce pan on low...  heat and  mix together 1. 2. 3. 4. 5. ...mixed and melted together well...when cool enough to handle make small balls and place on wax paper on cookie sheet
melt 6. in small sauce pan and dip the balls...to cover with chocolate...place back on the wax paper and chill
