Title:
40 Clove Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. butter
2 tbsp. olive oil
2-3 lbs. chicken
4 tbsp. fresh parsley
1 cup flour
40 cloves of peeled garlic
1 cup dry white wine
4 cups chicken stock
1/2 cup heavy cream
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat butter and olive oil in a large skillet
Season chicken with salt and pepper then toss in flour to coat.
Add chicken to pan, skin side down, and saute until golden brown on both sides.
Remove from pan and add garlic and saute until light brown.
Add wine and chicken stock to pan and return chicken to pan.
Cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
Remove chicken and keep warm.
Turn heat to high, add parsley, and reduce liquid by a little over half.
Pour sauce in blender, add cream and puree.
Pour sauce over chicken and serve.
