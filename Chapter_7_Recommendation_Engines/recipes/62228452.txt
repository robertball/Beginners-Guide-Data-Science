Title:
Bruschetta with green olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Baguette bread sliced
1 jar Marco Polo Bruschetta Sauce
2/3 cup grated cheddar cheese
1 cup green olives cut in half
extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Prehead oven to 400 degrees.  Lay slices of baguette bread on a cookie sheet.  Drizzle lightly with olive oil.  Sprinkle lightly with grated cheddar cheese.  Toast in the oven for just 8 minutes until cheese is melted.  Cut green olives in half, add to the bruschetta sauce.  Place a spoonful of the bruschetta sauce on top of the bread making sure that each piece gets an olive.  Serve!

Submitter: 
Janet Larsen
Description: 
Quick and easy appetizer to fix for your family and guests.
