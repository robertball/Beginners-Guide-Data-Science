Title:
CHRISTMAS  SPARKLE COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4C. Butter or Margarine, softened
1C. Sugar
2 Eggs
2Tbsps. Milk
1Tsp. Pure Vanilla Extract
2 1/4Cs. All-Purpose Flour
1Tsp. Baking Powder
1/2Tsp. Baking Soda
1/2Tsp. Salt
1C. Walnuts, well chopped
1C. Pitted Dates, well chopped
2/3C. Frosted Flakes Cereal, crushed
1/3C. Maraschino Cherries, cut into quarters

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
In a large bowl, using an electric mixer, cream butter and sugar, until fluffy.
Mix in eggs, milk and vanilla until smooth.
In a medium size bowl, sift together next 4 ingredients and add to creamed mixture, until well mixed. Stir in nuts and dates, mix well and shape into 1-inch balls and roll in crushed cereal.
Place on an ungreased cookie sheet. Top with 1/4 of a cherry. Place in 375 degree F. oven and bake 12 minutes or until done.
Cool 15 minutes, remove from cookie sheet and place onto wire rack and cool completely!!

Submitter: 
Corwynn Darkholme
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by my Great Aunt June on my Mother's side. Easy to make, sweet, fruity and nutty!! This is a great Holiday cookie recipe!!
