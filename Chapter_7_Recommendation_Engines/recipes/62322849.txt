Title:
Lentil and Cactus Soup *Moms Recipe* Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/379434.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb bag small lentils
5 cloves garlic
6 tspn Knorr chicken bouillon (heaping)
1/2 yellow onion (small)
1 tomato (chopped)
3 small potatoes chopped
1 cup precooked cactus (nopales-opuntia ficus-indica)
1/4 teaspoon ground Cumin (to taste)
14 cups water

Directions:
Bring the water to a boil with 3 cloves cut in 1/3, meanwhile washing the lentils. Once water is boiling add lentils as well as 4 tsp of chicken bouillon. Boil for about 1hr (Lentils about 95% cooked)
Sofrito saute: In small pan put about rits cracker sized amt of EVOO (olive oil)and saute onions and garlic (minced)once sauted add tomato until they get a bit soggy. Once this is complete add sofrito to the pot. Add the additional bouillon (to taste), as well as cumin (to taste). Add potatoes for last 15-20 min... ENJOY!!
**It is key to continually taste as to not over season.
