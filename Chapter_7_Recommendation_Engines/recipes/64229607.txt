Title:
Cranberry Pear Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces fresh or frozen cranberries, thawed
2 large pears, peeled and sliced
1/2 cup sugar
6 teaspoons flour, divided
3/4 t cinnamon
1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
2 T brown sugar
2 T cold butter

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cranberries and pears. Combine the sugar. 1 t flour, and cinnamon. Sprinkle over the cranberry/pear mixture and toss to coat. Transfer to an 8 inch baking dish coated with cooking spray.
In small bowl, combine the oats, brown sugar, and remaining flour. Cut in butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over cranberry/pear mixture.
Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees, for 30-35 minutes or until topping is golden brown and pears are tender.
Service with vanilla frozen yogurt or ice cream.

Submitter: 
Carrie
Description: 
This is a great sweet and tart dessert that can easily be doubled and cooked in an 9x13 dish.
