Title:
Big Puff Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C flour
1 1/2 C milk
4 eggs plus 1 egg white
1 t vanilla

Directions:
Mix all ingred. well. Can use a blender. Melt 1-2T butter in 9-in cake pan or 2 qt. casserole. Pour batter into pan. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon. Bake 375 for 25-30 min, until center is set and sides are puffy. Cut into pieces of desired size. Serve with butter and syrup

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
A variation: pour a can of cherry or apple pie filling into pan before adding batter. Bake it a bit longer in recipe calls for.
