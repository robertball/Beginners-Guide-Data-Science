Title:
Baklava Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. finely chopped walnuts
1/2 c. sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. cloves
1 package phyllo dough, thawed
1 1/2 c. melted unsalted butter
Syrup:
1 1/4 c. sugar
1 c. water
3 inch stick cinnamon
1/2 tsp. finely shredded lemon peel
2 Tbsp. rose water
1/2 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1/2 c. honey

Directions:
Bring 1 1/4 cups sugar, water, cinnamon stick and lemon peel to a boil, reduce heat and summer for 30 minutes stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat. Stir in honey, rose water, and lemon juice. Allow to cool to room temperature.
Combine walnuts, sugar, cinnamon, and cloves. Brush large glass rectangular baking dish with some of the melted butter. Layer 1 phyllo sheet, brush with butter, repeat 9 more times. Then place 1/3 of nut mixture on top. Repeat 2 more times. Top with remaining phyllo sheets, brushing each with melted butter. Drizzle remaining butter over the top.
With a sharp knife, cut into diamonds or triangles. Do not remove from dish.
Bake at 350Â°F for about 45 minutes or until golden brown. Allow to cool about 15 minutes.
Remove cinnamon stick from syrup and spoon over Baklava. Cool completely before serving.
