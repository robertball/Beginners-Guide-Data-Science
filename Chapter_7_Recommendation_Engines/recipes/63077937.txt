Title:
U-Yen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/777036.jpg
Ingredients:
1. 2 lbs shredded Taro
2. 1-1/2 Cups Tapioca Starch.
3. 5 Tbsp Ground Dry Shrimp.
4. 1-1/2 Tbsp Bean Paste Chili
5. 1 Tbsp Sugar
6. 4 Tbsp chopped ginger
7. 1/2 Tsp Salt
8  1/2 Tsp Pepper
9. 1/4 to 1/2 cup of water

Directions:
Mixed all ingredients with hand.  Fry at medium temperature.  Put fired taro on the wire rack to keep it crunchy.  DO NOT use paper towel to drain the oil.
Chili Sauce:  2 Tbsp Oelek Chili, 1 Tbsp Chopped Garlic, 1 Tbsp Vinegar, 1 Tsbp Sugar- Add warm water.
