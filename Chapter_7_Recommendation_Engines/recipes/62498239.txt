Title:
Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123221.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. cranberries
1 c. water
1 c. sugar
1 small cherry jello
1/2 c. chopped apples
1/2 c. celery
1/2 t. salt
1/2 c. nuts (opt.)

Directions:
Add water to cranberries and microwave until it pops.
Add sugar & cook another 5 minutes.
Add jello while still hot & let set until starts to gel.
Add apples, celery, salt and nuts if desired.
