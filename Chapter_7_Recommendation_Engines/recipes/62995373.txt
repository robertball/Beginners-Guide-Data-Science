Title:
Best Foods WALDORF SALAD WRAPS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. Hellmann's速 or Best Foods速 Light Mayonnaise
1 heaping tablespoon Dijon mustard
2 Tbsp. white wine vinegar
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
1/4 cup dried cranberries, cherries or raisins
1/4 cup chopped toasted walnuts or pecans
1 small bunch
2 whole wheat wraps, heated or lightly grilled

Directions:
Whisk together the Hellmann's速 or Best Foods速 Light Mayonnaise, mustard, and vinegar in a medium bowl. Slowing whisk in the olive oil until emulsified, sprinkle with salt and pepper, if desired.
Combine the chicken, cranberries, blue cheese, and walnuts in a large bowl, add a few tablespoons of the dressing, toss to combine and sprinkle with salt and pepper, if desired.
Spread some of the dressing down the center of each wrap and top with some of the arugula. Divide the chicken mixture between the two wraps. Roll each tightly and slice in half.
