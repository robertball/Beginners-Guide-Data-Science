Title:
Chocolate Suicide Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chocolate Cookie Dough
Oreo Cookies
Brownie Mix
Vanilla Ice Cream
Chocolate Syrup

Directions:
Press cookie dough in the bottom of a 9x13 pan
Arrange oreos in a layer
Mix brownies and pour over cookie layer
bake brownies according to box
when cool top with ice cream and syrup
