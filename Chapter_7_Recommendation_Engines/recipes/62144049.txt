Title:
Moist Orange Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (18.25 oz) yellow cake mix
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups orange juice
3 eggs
2/3 cup vegetable oil
2 teaspoons orange extract
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour a 12-cup bundt pan.
Mix together yellow cake mix and flour in a large bowl. Add orange juice, beaten eggs, oil, orange extract and vanilla extract. Beat on low speed until batter is smooth.
Pour batter into prepared bundt pan. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the cake comes out clean.
Allow to cool in pan at least 30 minutes before inverting onto plate.

Submitter: 
Anne O. Nymous
Description: 
A moist, delicious cake that isn't too heavy or dense. Using a box mix as a base is a huge time-saver!
