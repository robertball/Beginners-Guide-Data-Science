Title:
Not-So-Boring Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Cooked chicken breast
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup chopped red onion
8 oz Slaw Dressing
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp celery salt
1 tsp lemon pepper

Directions:
In a food processor, combine the chicken, slaw dressing, celery, onion, onion powder, garlic powder, paprika, celery salt, and lemon pepper. Mix until well chopped.
