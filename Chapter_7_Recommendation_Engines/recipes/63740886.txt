Title:
SELF-RISING FLOUR Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 Tbsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Sift the three ingredients together TWICE to insure even blending. Remeasure quantity for the recipe.
Store any remaining mixture in a clearly labeled air tight container.

Submitter: 
lgmarge
Description: 
If you don't have any self-rising flour in the pantry, here is a quick solution.
