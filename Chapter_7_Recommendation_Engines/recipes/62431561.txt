Title:
Crispy Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451448.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. bonless, skinless chicken breasts
1 egg
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 tbs. vegetable oil
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/2 cup cornstarch
1/4 cup flour
Oil for frying
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 cup orange juice
1 orange for 1 1/2 tsp. of grated orange peel
1/2 cup hoisin sauce
1/4 cup sugar
Cayenne pepper dash (optional)

Directions:
Cut chicken breasts into small pieces and place in large bowl.
Stir in egg, salt, black pepper and vegetable oil.
Stir cornstarch and flour together in separate bowl.
Add chicken mixture to flour mixture stirring to coat chicken. Add more cornstarch and flour as desired for a thicker breading.
Pour oil to about 1/2 inch deep in wok or skillet, set at medium high.
Add pieces of chicken in small batches to fry until golden brown and crispy. Careful not to overcook.
Drain fried chicken on paper towels.
For Orange Sauce:
Heat oiled pan on medium-high and sautee garlic.
Add grated orange peel, orange juice, hoisin sauce, sugar, cayenne pepper, salt and black pepper.
Heat until boiling.
Reduce heat and simmer until sauce thickens.
Pour sauce over fried chicken pieces, coating chicken evenly.
Pour chicken over rice or lo mein if desired.
