Title:
Summer Cabbage Carrot Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2405528.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head of fresh green cabbage(sliced)
1/3 package of shredded carrots
1/3 cup of dry roasted peanuts (crushed)
1 package oriental ramen noodles
1 11 oz.can mandarin oranges
3/4 cup Coleslaw style dressing

Directions:
Combine sliced cabbage, shredded carrots, ramen noodles, crushed peanuts, and cole slaw dressing. Mix well. Then add mandarin oranges and toss lightly. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jenny's Kitchen
Description: 
A delightful summer salad alternative with fresh cabbage, juicy mandarin oranges and crunchy ramen noodles.
