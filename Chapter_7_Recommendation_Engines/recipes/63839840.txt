Title:
Broiled Tomato Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066911.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
6 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 teaspoon parsley
1 teaspoon oregano
1 teaspoon pepper
6 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, grated and divided
1 loaf French Bread, lightly toasted

Directions:
Preheat oven to broil.
In a shallow bowl, whisk together the olive oil and vinegar.
Marinate the tomatoes in the mixture, stirring occasionally.
Meanwhile, in a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, parsley, oregano, pepper and 4 teaspoons Parmesan cheese.
Spread mixture on each slice of toasted bread.
Place sliced marinated tomatoes on each slice of bread, and sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese.
Place on a baking sheet and broil for 5 minutes, or until cheese turns golden brown.
Serve immediately, open faced.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Fresh, ripe tomatoes are marinated in olive oil and balsamic vinegar, then broiled with Parmesan cheese on toast.  Serve warm with a bowl of soup, if desired.Modified from: Mama's Best Broiled Tomato Sandwich
