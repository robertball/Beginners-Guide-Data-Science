Title:
Raspberry Streusel Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4483414.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ½ cups flour
½ cup sugar
2 tsp. baking soda
½ cup milk
1 egg, beaten
½ cup melted butter
1 1/2 cups raspberries
Pecan Streusel Topping:
¼ cup chopped pecans
¼ cup brown sugar
¼ cup flour
2 Tbsp. margarine, melted

Directions:
Combine first 3 dry ingredients in a small bowl.
In a separate bowl, combine milk, egg and butter. Stir milk mixture into flour mixture, just until moistened.
Spoon 1 Tbsp. batter into greased muffin tins. Sprinkle raspberries (fresh or frozen) over batter and top with remaining batter. Then sprinkle raspberries over batter to top.
Combine streusel ingredients to resemble moist crumbs and sprinkle over muffins.
Bake at 375 for 20-25 minutes or until golden.
