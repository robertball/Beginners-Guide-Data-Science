Title:
JC's Shepard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FOR GRAVY
1/2 C. (1 stick) Butter
1/2 C. Flour
4 C. Beef Broth
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Pepper
2 tsp Kitchen Bouquet
FOR PIE
2# Ground Beef (browned and drained)
6-8 Potatoes (mashed)
8 oz. Cream Cheese
2 C. Cheddar Cheese (shredded/divided)
4 cloves Garlic (minced)
8 C. frozen Mixed Vegetables (thawed)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F
GRAVY
1.  Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add flour and whisk together, making sure to get all visible lumps.
2.  Add salt and pepper.
3.  Cook over medium heat for 5 minutes or until mixture starts to turn light brown. Be sure to stir constantly.
4.  Turn heat to LOW and SLOWLY add broth, stirring constantly.
5.  It will spit and bubble so be careful.
6.  Add Kitchen Bouquet.
7.  Turn heat up to MEDIUM.
8.  Continue stirring until gravy boils and thickens.
PIE
Mix potatoes, cream cheese, 1/2 C. of cheddar and garlic until blended.
Combine meat, vegetables and gravy, spoon into a 9-inch square baking dish.
Cover with mashed potatoes and remaining cheddar cheese.
Bake for 20 minutes or until heated through.
