Title:
White crumb cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
1 1/2 cups white sugar
10 tbl butter (1stick +2tbl)
2 tsp Baking Powder
pinch of salt
2 eggs
3/4 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 tsp nutmeg (to taste)

Directions:
bring oven to 375 degrees. Grease pan(s).
In a mixing bowl combine flour, sugar, butter, baking powder and salt. Mix into crumbs. Remove about 1/2 cup crumbs and set aside. to the crumbs in bowl add milk, eggs and vanilla.Mix all together until a smooth, creamy batter. pour batter into prepared pans, dividing evenly and smooth top with knife or spatula. Sprinkle crumbs evenly over top of batter then sprinkle nutmeg (to taste) on top.
Bake about 25-30 min. Until cake tests done and top is golden brown.
Cool and serve either slightly warm or fully cool. ( It never gets completely cold in our house, it is gobbled up warm)Yum!!

Submitter: 
Janice Peiffer
Description: 
This is one of my family favorites. We make it at least once a week. Great breakfast cake and also great with fruit on top
