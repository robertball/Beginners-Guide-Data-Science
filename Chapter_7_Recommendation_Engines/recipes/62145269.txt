Title:
Delicious Guava Mango Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 guava fruit, peeled and diced
2 bananas, peeled and diced
1/4 cup sugar
8 ice cubes
1 cup milk

Directions:
Puree the fruit, milk, sugar and ice cubes until smooth. Pour into a cup and serve.
