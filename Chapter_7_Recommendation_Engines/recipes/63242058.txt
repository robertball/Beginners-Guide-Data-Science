Title:
Corn on the Cobb Parm Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-corn on the cobb
4-teaspoons butter
4-teaspoons grated parmesan cheese
4- 1/4 teaspoon paprika
salt and pepper to taste
Tin foil for wrapping

Directions:
Take each individual corn and season with butter,parmesan cheese,paprika, salt and pepper. Wrap in tin foil then put on the grill and cook for 10 minutes on each side.
