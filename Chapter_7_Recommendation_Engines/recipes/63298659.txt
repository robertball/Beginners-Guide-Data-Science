Title:
Passion fruit Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
Makes: 1 drink
11/2 ounces citrus flavored vodka, Smirnoff®
11/2 ounces white cranberry juice
11/2 ounces passion fruit juice
1/4 ounce orange liqueur, Grand Marnier®

Directions:
Directions
Combine all ingredients, except fruit garnish, in a martini shaker filled with ice. Shake and strain into a martini glass. Garnish with an orange or star fruit slice.
