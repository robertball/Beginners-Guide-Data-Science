Title:
Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter
8 fluid ounces heavy whipping cream
salt to taste
1 pinch ground nutmeg
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup grated Romano cheese
1 egg yolk
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2 Tablespoons cream chesse

Directions:
Melt butter or margarine in a saucepan over medium heat. Saute' garlic. Add heavy cream, stirring constantly.  Stir in salt, nutmeg, grated Parmesan cheese, cream cheese and grated Romano cheese. Stir constantly until melted. Remove 1/4 of mixture into a bowl let cool of a few minutes then SLOWLY wisk in the egg yolk.  slowly return to main sauce pan and Simmer over medium low heat for 3 to 5 minutes.  Garnish with additional grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Submitter: 
Connie Pronk Remiger
Description: 
A satisfying sauce you can use on any type of pasta - dry or fresh.Modified from: Classic Alfredo Sauce
