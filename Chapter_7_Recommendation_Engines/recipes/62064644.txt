Title:
11 Can Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
2 small onions, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cans of red kidney beans
2 cans of pinto beans
2 cans of black beans
1 small can of tomato paste
3 cans of diced tomatoes
1 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
2 packs of chili seasoning
seasonings to taste

Directions:
Brown beef with onion and garlic.  Drain fat.  Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and let simmer about 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Susanna Joiner Boyd
Description: 
Good basic chili recipe.  I modified it from a Bush's brochure.
