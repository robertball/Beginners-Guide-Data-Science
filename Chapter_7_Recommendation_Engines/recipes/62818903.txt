Title:
Jim's Diner Tuna Melt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can chunk white tuna, drained
1/2 small onion, minced
2-3 tbsp mayonnaise
pepper to taste
1 or 2 shakes celery seed
1 tsp pickle relish
8 slices American cheese
3-4 tsp margarine
4 slices white bread

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine tuna, mayo, minced onion, celery seed, relish and black pepper. Set aside. Preheat skillet.  Spread margarine on sides of bread and lightly brown all 4 slices in skillet. Remove browned bread to a platter and place 2 slices each  of american cheese on 2 pieces of bread.  Top each with half of the tuna mixture.  Cover remaining 2 slices cheese each and 1 slice browned bread each. Return to skillet on medium heat and cook til cheese is melted and tuna is warm in center - about 4 minutes each side.  Return to plate, slide lengthwise and serve with sweet potato fries (homemade or frozen).

Submitter: 
cynnergyrl
Description: 
My son had a similar tuna melt at a local diner, server with sweet potato fries.  He's perfected it for himself, as he could never find another establishment that prepared it in this manner.Modified from: Simple Tuna Melts
