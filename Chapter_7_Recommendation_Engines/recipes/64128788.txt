Title:
Dog protein mash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6oz? can boneless, skinless salmon or tuna in water, not drained
1 cup cooked brown rice
1 regular can green beans, or peas, liquid drained
1 cup plain greek yogurt

Directions:
cook the brown rice as directed. let cool completely. once cool, combine all ingredients in one large bowl. mix well together, getting tuna or salmon into small pieces.
