Title:
Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695042.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lbs. red potatoes
1/2-3/4 cup chicken broth
10 oz. garlic-flavored spreadable cheese

Directions:
Boil potatoes 30-40 minutes until tender. Drain and transfer to serving bowl. Mash with potato masher.
Add chicken broth until desired consistency is reached. Fold in cheese until well-combined. Serve hot or make ahead and reheat before serving.

Submitter: 
lucylove
Description: 
Don't want to roast and chop the garlic? Take the easy way out with a spreadable cheese, like Boursin.
