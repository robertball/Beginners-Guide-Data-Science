Title:
Pork chop casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops cubed
2 cups of instant rice
1 onion diced
1 pepper (any color) chopped
1 can mushrooms (save the water)
1 jar of classic 4 cheese spagetti sauce
4 tablespoons of salsa
1 tablespoon of garlic powder

Directions:
place in pan, pork chops (cubed), onion and pepper.  While it cooks add the garlic powder amd salsa.  Stir and cook till all meat is cooked through.
In a bowl add the sauce, rice, water from the mushrooms and another half can of water, mushrooms.  Add the stuff from the pan.  Stir and then put in a casserole dish.  Bake on 350 for 45 minutes.
