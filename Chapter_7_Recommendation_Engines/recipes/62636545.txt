Title:
Sour Cream Top Sirloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/579941.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. Top Sirloin Steaks
2 tbsp. All purpose flour
1/2 tsp. Paprika
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/4 tsp. Pepper
1/4 c. Vegetable oil
1/2 c. Chopped Onion
1 clove Minced Garlic
2/3 c. Water
2 tbsp. Soy sauce
2 tbsp. Brown sugar
1/2 tsp. Dijon mustard
1/4 lb. Sliced Mushrooms
1/2 c. Sour cream
Rice or Buttered Noodles , as desired

Directions:
Cut meat into serving-size pieces. Dredge in flour, seasoned with paprika, salt and pepper. Heat oil in a heavy skillet. Sear steak on one side. Turn meat.
Add onion, garlic, water, soy sauce, brown sugar and mustard.
Cover. Simmer for 8 to 10 minutes. Add mushrooms. Continue to simmer for 5 minutes or until meat is tender.
Add sour cream. Simmer until thoroughly heated.
Serve.

Description: 
I made this for my mothers birthday some years ago and she loved it! You may have to add extra seasoning though, I added salt and pepper to the sauce. Serve on a bed of rice or buttered noodles with green beans.
