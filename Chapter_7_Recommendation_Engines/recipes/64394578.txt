Title:
Chipotle Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2234303.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1 cup Herdez chipotle cooking sauce or salsa
1 small can of black olives
1 can black beans
2 cups cooked brown rice (1/2 serving per total serving)
2 bell peppers (red & yellow preferred)
1/4 cup fresh cilantro
1 tsp cooking oil
1 1/2 tsp butter
1 pack sliced white mushrooms
2 limes
1 avocado
1-2 cups shredded Mexican cheese
Spices:
1/4-1/2 tsp each paprika, cumin, coriander, garlic powder, & onion powder
mixed ground black pepper
sea salt
dash of red pepper (to taste)
Optional:
1 bottle mole stout beer - for marinating chicken, if desired
1 onion (in place of onion powder)

Directions:
Optional:Soak chicken breasts in mole stout beer for a few hours prior to cooking.
Slice peppers (and onions if preferred), dice tomatoes, cut limes, chop fresh cilantro
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Lay chicken breasts in foil lined baking dish - top with chipotle salsa & black olives; close foil tightly around chicken - Bake at 400 degrees for 35 minutes
Cook rice: I use boil-in-bag brown rice, boil water and cook rice for 10 min. Set aside.
Skillet: In a skillet over medium - heat olive oil and butter
Add peppers (& onions if desired), mushrooms, remainder of olives to skillet and cook for 5 min.
Add black beans, diced tomato, cilantro, 1 lime, & spices to skillet, and cook 5 min.
Add rice & Â¼ cup salsa, and let simmer for 5 - 10 min on low.
Final bake: (Set oven to 375 degrees) Remove chicken/foil from baking dish.
Add skillet contents to baking dish; remove chicken from foil and return chicken to dish. Top with any salsa runoff from the foil.
Bake for 5 minutes at 375
Top with shredded cheese, return to oven, bake 5 more minutes or until chicken is done.
Serve with sliced avocado, lime, and fresh cilantro.

Description: 
Baked salsa chicken w/ skillet-cooked rice, beans, peppers, etc. Really easy, and one of my favorite go-to meals. Can easily adjust the skillet ingredients to include whatever you like/don't like!
