Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Halloween Cats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983997.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Directions:
In large microwave safe bowl, melt margarine and marshmallows on HIGH for about 2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute. Stir mixture until smooth.
Add vanilla and KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal, stirring until well coated. Allow to cool.
Shape half cups of mixture into balls. Best if served same day.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
Have the kids help decorate these Halloween cats using candies, frosting and licorice for your favorite trick or treaters.
