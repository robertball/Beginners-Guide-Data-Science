Title:
Leftover Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups leftover beef, pork or lamb, cubed
2 cups of gravy, leftover or from a jar
2 cups of corn, peas and/or carrots; leftover, canned, frozen or cooked to order
1/4 to 1/3 cup chopped onion
instant mashed potatoes, enough for 8 servings
1 tablespoon parsley flakes (optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. In a greased 2-quart casserole dish, thoroughly combine everything but parsley and instant mashed potato flakes. Set aside.
3. Prepare instant mashed potatoes according to package directions. Add parsley flakes to boiling water, if desired.
4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
MISSIVONNE
Description: 
An excellent way to use up leftover meat and vegetables. And it's so adaptable: You can boil fresh vegetables or use frozen, canned or leftover ones.
