Title:
Honey Mustard Chicken Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts - pounded flat
1/3 cup honey
1 tbsp thinly sliced green onions
1/2 tsp fresh tarragon leaves (fresh works best)
2 1/2 tbsp Dijon mustard
lettuce leaves
tomato
red onion (optional)
4 onion rolls

Directions:
Combine honey, green onion, tarragon, mustard and blend well.  Bring to a boil and reduce heat to low.
Simmer approximately 5 minutes or until sauce thickens.
Grill chicken over medium heat brushing occasionally with the honey mustard sauce until chicken is done.
Brush honey mustard sauce on toasted onion rolls.
Layer rolls with lettuce, tomato and chicken.
Add 1 to 2 tsp sauce to sandwich and enjoy.
