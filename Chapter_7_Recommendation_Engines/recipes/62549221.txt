Title:
Clam Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can minced clams, drained reserve juice
16 oz sour cream
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1 tbs worcestershire sauce
1 tbs lemon juice
1 tbs reserved clam juice
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
Open and drain clams, reserve juice. Mix softened cream cheese and sour cream with electric mixer until smooth.  All remaining ingredients are to taste, or just use my "perfected" mix. Stir in worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, clam juice, salt and pepper, then add clams.  Serve with ridged potato chips

Submitter: 
Anne M. Ficklin
Description: 
This dip marks the begining of the holiday season for my family.  It is easy and delicious.
