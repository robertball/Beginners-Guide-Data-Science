Title:
Outback Steakhouse's Marinaded Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound steak
1 cup Scottish Ale -- not beer
2 teaspoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon monosodium glutamate

Directions:
-Place your favorite cut of steak in a shallow pan and pour ale on steak and marinate for 1 hour in refregerator.
-Remove steak from ale.
-Combine dry ingredients and rub steak with mixture on both sides.
-Let marinate with dry ingredients for 1/2 hour.
-Preheat a skillet or grill to meduim high heat.
-Add vegatable spray or vegetable oil to skillet if using one and braize to perfection.
