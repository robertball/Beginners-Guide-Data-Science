Title:
Cajun Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter, melted
2 sticks margarine, melted
4 ounces Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
Lots of black pepper (4 tablespoons)
2 teaspoons Season-All
salt to taste
2 bay leaves
2 teaspoons Tabasco Sauce (red original)
3 cloves garlic, minced
4 lemons, 2 juiced & 2 sliced
6 pounds medium to medium large size raw shrimp in shells
French Bread baguettes heated, for serving

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
In a bowl mix all sauce ingredeints except the lemon slices and shrimp.
Pour about a half a cup of the sauce in the bottom of a large baking dish (9x13 pan).
Layer the shrimp and lemon slices in the pan.
Pour all remaining sauce over shrimp and lemon slices.
Bake uncovered, stirring once or twice until the shrimp are cooked through, about 15 to 20 minutes.
Serve in large bowl or baking dish with baquettes of hot French bread.  Let everyone spoon the shrimp onto their plates and peel the shrimp themselves.  Dip the bread into the sauce.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This wonderful dinner party dish makes for great conversation as everyone peels their own shrimp and 
dips bread into the yummy sauce.
