Title:
Italian Hoagie Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - refridgerated pizza dough
1/4lb capacola
1/4 lb genoa salami
1/4lb provolone cheese
2 cups shredded lettuce
2 lg tomatoes sliced
1 small onion sliced (optional)
2 tb red wine vinegar
2 tb olive oil
2 tb dried oregano
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Prepare pizza dough as directed on package without the toppings.
Layer capacola, genoa and provolone over one crust.
Add lettuce, tomato and onion
Sprinkle with oil, vinegar, salt, pepper and oregano.
Top with remaining crust.
Heat in oven just until cheese starts to melt.
Enjoy!
