Title:
BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup Cider vinegar
2/3 Cup Ketchup
2/3 Cup BBQ sauce
6 TBSP Brown Sugar
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a bowl and mix together.  Pour over meat and cook meat as you normally would.

Submitter: 
Johanna Van Ness
Description: 
I needed BBQ sauce, but didn't have enough in the bottle, so I made this with ingredients I had on hand.  I added this to a beef brisket the last 30 minutes.  It would work for other meats as well.
