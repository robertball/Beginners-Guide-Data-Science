Title:
Sweet and Spicy Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (16 ounce) packages kielbasa or smoked sausage
1 (16 ounce) bottle spicy barbeque sauce
1 cup seedless raspberry jelly
1 (15 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained

Directions:
Cook the sausage on a hot grill until it has nice grill marks; add the barbecue sauce, jelly and pineapple, then stir; simmer 20 minutes.
Alternatively, cook in crockpot on Medium until hot, about 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
This delicious appetizer with sausage, barbecue sauce, and jelly can be prepared in the slow cooker or right on the stove.Modified from: Sweet and Oh So Spicy Kielbasa
