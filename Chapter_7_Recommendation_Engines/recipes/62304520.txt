Title:
salmon with brown butter, almonds, and green beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/385099.jpg
Ingredients:
*4 tablespooons unsalted butter
*1 1/4 pounds skinless salmon fillet, cut into 4 pieces
*1 pound green beans, trimmed and halved crosswise
*1/4 cup sliced almonds
*2 tablespoons capers

Directions:
1. heat 1 tablespoon of the butter in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. season the salmon with 1/2 tablespoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper.cook until opaque throughout, 3 to 5 mins per side; transfer to plate.
2. meanwhile, fill a second skillet with 1/2 inch of water, bring to a boil, and add 1/4 teaspoon salt. add the green beans, cover, and steam until just tender, 4 to 5 minutes; drain and transfer to plates.
3. wipe out the green bean skillet and heat the remaining 3 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the almonds and cook, stirring frequently, until the almonds and butter are golden brown (but not burned), 2 to 3 minutes. stir in the capers. spoon over the fish and green beans.
