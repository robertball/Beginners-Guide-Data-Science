Title:
Marilyn's Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups flour
2 cups water
1 tablespoon yeast
1/2 teaspoon salt
garlic powder, optional
onion powder, optional

Directions:
Add yeast to warm water. Let stand for 5 minutes.  Add salt and flour.  Knead.  Let rise 1/2 hour.  Punch down.  Roll out.  For crispy crust coat pan with olive oil.  Can sprinkle pan with corn meal.  Top with sauce (spaghetti sauce), cheese, and favorite toppings.
Cook at 425 degrees for about 12 minutes until browned.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
I got this from my friend, Marilyn.  It is a good standard pizza crust.
