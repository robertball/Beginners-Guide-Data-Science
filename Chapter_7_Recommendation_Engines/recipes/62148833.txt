Title:
Easy Baked Stuffed Lobster Tails Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Lobster Tails, cooked
2 cups dry bread crumbs
1/2 pound butter
2 teaspoons of Worcestershire sauce
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325F
Remove the meat form the cooked lobster tails and combine with dry bread crumbs, butter, Worcestershire sauce and salt. Fill the tails with the stuffing. Brush stuffing with olive oil to keep moist while baking. Place in a foil lined baking pan and bring the edges of the foil over the tails. Bake 50 minutes; serve hot.
*You can add fresh garlic to the stuffing if you are a garlic lover.*
