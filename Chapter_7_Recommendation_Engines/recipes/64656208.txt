Title:
Amey's Crunchy Oriental Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. chopped cabbage
1/2 c green onions, sliced thin
1 carrot, grated
1/4 c. sunflower seeds
1 pkg. shrimp-flavored ramen noodles, broken
1 c. slivered almonds
Dressing:
2 T vegetable oil
2 T sesame oil
2 T cider vinegar
2 T sugar
flavor pack from noodles

Directions:
Mix cabbage, onion and carrot.
Add sunflower seeds, almonds and crushed ramen noodles.
Whisk dressing together in a separate bowl.
Shortly before serving, mix all together and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
Amey brought this to a potluck, and I fell in love.  It's tangy, crunchy and delicious
