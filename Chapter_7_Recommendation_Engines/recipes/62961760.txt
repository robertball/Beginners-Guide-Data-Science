Title:
Best Ever Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Bacon Strips, diced
3 Cups Cubed Peeled Potatoes
1 Can (12-14 oz) Chicken Broth
1/2 tsp. Onion Powder
1 Tbls. Parsley
1/2 tsp. Celery Salt
Salt and Pepper
3 Tbls. Flour
3 Cups Milk
8 oz. American Cheese Cubed

Directions:
In a large sauce pan cook the bacon until crisp, drain. Add potatoes, broth, onion, parsley, celery salt, salt & papper. Cover & simmer until potatoes are tender (about 15 minutes). Combine flour and milk until smooth. Add to Soup. Bring to a boil and stir for two minutes. Add cheese. Stir until cheese is melted and heated thru.

Submitter: 
Brad Miller
Description: 
This is a great fast potato soup for those cold afternoons.
