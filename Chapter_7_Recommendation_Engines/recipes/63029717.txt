Title:
Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
one fresh pie crust
2 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup ricotta
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
oven 375 degrees

Directions:
Set pie crust out for 15 minutes before laying in pie plate.
wisk together eggs, milk, ricotta, salt & pepper in bowl.  Add the "extras", see below.  Pour mixture into pie plate and cover the edges of dough with tin foil. Bake at 375 for 40 minutes.
Extras:
Saute onions, red pepper, mushrooms to egg mixture with crisp bacon and shredded cheese.
Leftover taco meat, cheese, sauteed onions.
crumbled breakfast sausage, cheese and sauteed onions.

Submitter: 
Carol Roberts

Very Good.  Always make two.
