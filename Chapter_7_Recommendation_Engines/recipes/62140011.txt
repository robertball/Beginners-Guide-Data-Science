Title:
Walnut Ham Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package fresh whole wheat linguine
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tsp hot pepper flakes
1/2 cup walnuts, coarsley chopped
1/2 pound fully cooked ham slices
1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
Salt to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, saute garlic and hot pepper flakes in oil for two minutes.
Add walnuts and saute for two minutes. Stir in ham slices.  Cook until heated through, about five minutes.
Drain pasta and toss with ham mixture. Season with sale and sprinkle with parmesan and parsley.

Submitter: 
KerriJ
Description: 
Delicious and healthy way to use up leftover roasted ham.  Also a really quick dinner solution for busy week nights.
