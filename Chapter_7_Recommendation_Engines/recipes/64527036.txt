Title:
Mashed Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Water
salt
2 sweet potatoes
4 tbsp butter

Directions:
In a small pot, boil water with some salt.
While water is boiling, peel potatoes and cut into cubes.
Once water starts to boil, place potatoes into boiling water.  Continue boiling for about 13 minutes, or until potatoes are fork-tender.  Drain potatoes and place into a bowl.
Mash potatoes with a fork.  Add butter.  Once butter melts, mix it into the potatoes.  Salt to taste.
