Title:
Stacy's Raspberry Chocolate Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/612064.jpg
Ingredients:
Hershey's Special Dark Chocolate - 4 large bars
Half and Half
Black Raspberry Liquor

Directions:
Melt chocolate in electric fondue pot or double boiler
Add little-by-little enough Half N' Half to make viscosity to your personal liking.
Once melted, add 1/4 cup liquor (or enough to your taste).  Set electric fondue pot on 3.  Set double boiler on simmer.  Cut up strawberries and bananas, oranges, kiwis, fresh pinapple  or other fruits into bite size pieces for dipping.  Whole roasted almonds or bite size pretzels and shortbread cookies are also tasty dipped.
