Title:
KFC Chicken Spices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon dried Rosemary
1 tablespoon dried Oregano
1 tablespoon powdered sage
1 teaspoon powdered ginger
1 teaspoon majoram
1 1/2 teaspoon thyme
3 tablespoons packed brown sugar
3 tablespoons dried parsley
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon paprika
2 tablespoons garlic salt
2 tablespoons powdered chicken bouillon or 4 cubes mashed
1 package Liptop Tomato cup of soup mix

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender, food processor and run for 3-4 minutes to pulverise, or rub through a fine strainer.  Store in an airtight container so it will not lose potency.  Makes about 3/4 cup.
To use:  add 1 oz. mix to 1 cup of flour for coating.
