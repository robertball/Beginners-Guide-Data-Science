Title:
Microwaved Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of water
1 tsp. of salt
1 cup of rice

Directions:
Pour two cups of water in a 1 1/2 qt. microwave dish that has a lid.
Boil the water in the microwave by cooking it for 5 minutes on high power.
Add the rice and salt to the water and stir.
Place a paper towel or waxed paper over the dish and then cover with the lid.
Cook the rice on 50% Power for 15 minutes.
Allow rice to sit for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Angela Morris
Description: 
This makes perfect rice every time.
