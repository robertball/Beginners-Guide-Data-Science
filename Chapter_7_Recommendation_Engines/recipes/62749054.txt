Title:
Easy Lemon Meringue Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. lemon pudding, (4 serving size), not instant
2/3 c. sugar
2 1/4c. water
3 egg yolks
2 tbsp. lemon juice
2 tbsp. butter
1 baked 9-inch pie shell, cooled
3 egg whites
6 tbsp. white sugar

Directions:
Combine pudding mix, 2/3 c. sugar and 1/4 c. of the water in saucepan. Blend in yolks and remaining water. Cook and stir over medium heat until full rolling boil. Remove from heat, blend in lemon juice and butter. Cool 5 min. stirring at least 2 times. Pour into shell. Beat egg whites until foamy, then gradually beat in the 6 tbsp. of sugar and beat until stiff shiny peaks form. Spread over pie, seal edges well. Bake at 425 for 5-10 min or until meringue is browned. Cool at least 4 hours before serving.
