Title:
Amaretto Creme on Fresh Fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986888.jpg
Ingredients:
4 egg yolks
1/2 cup sugar
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1 cup milk
1 tablespoon amaretto or other almond liqueur
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
Fresh fruit

Directions:
COMBINE first 3 ingredients in a medium saucepan; gradually add milk, whisking until smooth.
COOK over low heat, whisking constantly, 10 minutes or until thickened. Remove mixture from heat; stir in amaretto and vanilla.
COVER and chill. Serve over fresh fruit.

Submitter: 
Southern Living magazine
Description: 
For fresh fruit we used sliced strawberries, sliced kiwifruit, pineapple chunks, and chopped mango.
