Title:
Finger Lickin' Good "Honey" BBQ Sauce! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/549748.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Reduced Sugar Heinz Tomato Ketchup
1/2 cup HoneyTree's Sugar Free Honey (or can use agave)
1/4 cup prepared yellow mustard
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 tsp ground cayenne pepper (adjust to your liking)
1 tbsp Ideal Brown (brown sugar substitute)

Directions:
Pour ingredients into blender until throughly incorporated.  Easy.
Use as you want.
Blending time is cooking time

Submitter: 
WickedCreations
Description: 
This is so rich, so good that no one would know that it is really low-sugar, healthy bbq sauce!!  Very sweet, tangy with a kick, honey flavor thick bbq sauce that is great on all your bbq and then some!!It will fool your tongue and want more!!  It tastes like it came from the bottle even famous restaurants but better!!  Homemade is better made!!  Yum! Very diabetic friendly too!  It's another my creations made with Ideal Brown Sugar Substitute!!  A Truely TNT!! Easy to make in your blender!!
