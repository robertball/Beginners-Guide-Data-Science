Title:
Toasted Pumpkin Seeds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups pumpkin seeds, unwashed
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 T melted butter

Directions:
Spread mixture in a large shallow pan.  Bake one hour and 40 minutes to 2 hours.  Stir frequently.  They should be dry and light gold in color when done.
