Title:
Low Carb Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1451574.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
5 eggs
1 cup Splenda
15 ounce can pumpkin
1 teaspoon Pumpkin Pie Spice
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Beat the cream cheese in the mixing bowl of an electric mixer on high until smooth.
Add the remaining ingredients and beat well.
Pour into an 8"x8" glass baking dish sprayed with nonstick spray.
Bake at 350ยบ 40 minutes.
Serve room temperature or chill. Refrigerate.
