Title:
Easy Beef and Garlic Noodles Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 servings Japanese Udon Noodles
2 tbsp butter
4 green onions, chopped
4 garlic cloves, chopped
2 cap steaks, cut into bite-sized pieces
2 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp fish sauce

Directions:
Prepare Udon noodles and set aside.
In large sauce pan, melt butter and saut√© onions and garlic until soft.
Add beef and stir until it is to desired doneness.
Add brown sugar, soy sauce, and fish sauce, and stir until sugar is dissolved.
Toss in noodles and heat to serving temperature and serve.
