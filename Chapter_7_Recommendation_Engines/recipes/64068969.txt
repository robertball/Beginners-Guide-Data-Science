Title:
Bean Soup With Kale Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon avocado oil or olive oil
4 large garlic cloves, crushed or minced
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
4 cups chopped raw kale (1 bunch)
4 cups low-fat, low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
1 tablespoon Shiro Miso
2 (15 ounce) cans white beans, such as cannellini or navy, drained and rinsed.
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 rounded teaspoon dried Italian herb seasoning
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot, heat avocado oil. Add garlic and onion; saute until soft. Add kale and saute, stirring, until wilted. Add broth, miso,  beans, tomatoes, herbs, salt and pepper. Simmer 45 minutes. Partially blend in the pot to desired consistency. Ladle into bowls; serve topped with crushed tortilla chips.

Description: 
White beans, cannellini or navy, and chicken broth form the base of this soup made with fresh kale and canned tomatoes.Modified from: Bean Soup With Kale
