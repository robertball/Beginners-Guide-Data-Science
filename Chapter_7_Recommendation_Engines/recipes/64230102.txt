Title:
Sausage/Potato Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Jimmy Dean/Bob Evans Hot pork sausage
1/2 lb bacon cooked, crumbled, set aside
1 onion, diced
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
1 teaspoon mustard powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
8 eggs, beaten
1 cup Half & Half
3 cups Extra Sharp Cheddar Cheese, shredded

Directions:
1. Crumble sausage into a medium skillet.  Cook over medium heat until evenly brown; drain.
2.  In medium bowl, mix together, mustard powder, salt, eggs, and half & half.  Add sausage, bell peppers, potatoes, and 2 cups of cheese, and stir to coat evenly.  Pour into a greased 9x13" baking dish.
Sprinkle the leftover cup of Extra Sharp Cheese on top and then the bacon crumbles evenly on top of the cheese.
3.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
4. Cover and bake 45 minutes.  Uncover, and reduce temperature to 325 degrees.  Bake for an additional 30 minutes or until set.
