Title:
Trisha's Mexican Cream Cheese Rollups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/3 cup sour cream
1 small can mild green chilis
1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped
3-4 green onions, chopped
Natures seasoning to taste
Cilantro chopped (to taste)
6-8(10 inch) spinach tortillas

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese, sour cream, green chiles, black olives, green onions, cilantro and seasoning.
Spread cream cheese mixture in a thin layer onto each tortilla. Roll up tortillas.  Chill about 1 hour, or until the filling is firm.
Slice chilled rollups into 1 inch pieces. Serve with salsa for dipping if desired
