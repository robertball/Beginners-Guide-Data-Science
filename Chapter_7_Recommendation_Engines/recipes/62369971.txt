Title:
Lime-Cumin Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup fresh squeezed lime juice
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon dijon mustard
zest of a lime
1/4 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1-2 cloves garlic, pasted with coarse sea salt
3-4 tablespoons minced sweet onion or shallot
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon coarse ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium size bowl and whisk briskly to combine. You can also do this in a shaker bottle.

Submitter: 
witchywoman
Description: 
This is a refreshing and bright vinaigrette that goes great over a simple mixed green salad!
