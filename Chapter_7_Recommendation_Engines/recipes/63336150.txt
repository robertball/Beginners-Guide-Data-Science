Title:
Blueberry Grain Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 cup (190 g) brown sugar
1/2 cup oil
2 cups buttermilk
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup (85 g) wheat germ
1 cup (60g) wheat bran
2 cups (280 g) AP or white whole wheat flour
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups fresh bluberries (250g)

Directions:
Combine first 7 ingredients together in medium bowl.  Combine dry ingredients in large bowl.  Gently stir in the blueberries.  Pour wet ingredients onto dry ingredients and gently fold together.  Bake at 375 for 18-23 minutes.
