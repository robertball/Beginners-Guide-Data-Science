Title:
Chocolate Pecan Slice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/4 tsp salt
2 eggs
3/4 cup corn syrup
1 cup white sugar
2 tbsp butter, melted
1 tbsp vanilla extract
1 cup pecan halves
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease the sides and bottom of a small baking pan.
In a large bowl combine the flour, 1/4 cup sugar, butter and salt until the mixture resemble coarse crumbs. Press firmly into the prepared pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 15 minutes.
In a large bowl stir the eggs, corn syrup 1 cup white sugar, melted butter and vanilla until blended. Stir in the chopped pecans and chocolate chips.
Spread the filling evenly over the hot crust. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 minutes or until set. Let cool before slicing.

Submitter: 
Chicken
Description: 
A yummy slice for those wanting the taste of pecan pie, with a chocolate flavour burst and the convenience of slice form for potlucks and other occasions.
