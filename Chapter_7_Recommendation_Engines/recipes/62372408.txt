Title:
sweet sesame bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
leftover stale french bread (at least a half a loaf or more).
2 sticks of butter melted
1 tsp of each, added to melted butter and stirred up
italian seasoning
thyme
chili powder
ground pepper
onion powder
paprika

Directions:
mix spices into melted butter
dip each pi. of sliced bread into melted butter, on both sides.lay out on cookie sheet and sprinkle generously with sesame seeds and dust with paprika.. warm in oven until sesame seeds are toasty.(about 10 minutes or so in a preheated 350 oven)

Submitter: 
Diane Dizacomo
Description: 
sweet bite sized pieces of bread sliced from stale french bread, spiced up and sweetened with paprika and sesame seeds on top.
