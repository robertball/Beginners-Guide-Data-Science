Title:
Shrimp Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/879949.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. tri colored twist macaroni
1 lb. cooked medium shrimp - peeled
1 pint of cherry tomatoes -  halved
1/2 cup of sliced green olives (optional)
3 springs of green onions - sliced
Dressing -
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1cup lemon juice
1 clove fresh minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions. Drain and rinse macaroni.
In large bowl mix macaroni,shrimp,tomatoes,green olives, and onion.
In seperate bowl or mixing glass combine oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt and pepper.
Pour dressing over macaroni and refridgerate at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Renee Coley
Description: 
My mom made this one day for a picnic and it was such a hit, there were no leftovers and we have been making it ever since. I have even omitted the shrimp at times due to allergies of our guest and it is still everone's favorite.
