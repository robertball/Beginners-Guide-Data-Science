Title:
Ooey Gooey Chocolatey Puffed Wheat Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c butter/margarine
1/2 c corn syrup
1 c brown sugar
4 Tbsp cocoa
1 tsp vanilla
10 large marshmallows
8 1/2 c puffed wheat cereal

Directions:
Melt butter in sauce pan.  Add syrup, sugar, cocoa, marshmallows and vanilla.  When mixture begins to bubble, remove from heat.  Add cereal and mix well.  Put into a buttered 9x12 cake pan and press and smooth with the back of a buttered spoon.
