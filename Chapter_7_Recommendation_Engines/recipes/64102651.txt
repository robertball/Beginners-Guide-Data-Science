Title:
Cajun Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
1/4 cup water
2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 dash Worcestershire sauce
1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon minced jalapeno pepper
1/4 cup chopped onion
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until almost crisp, 5 to 7 minutes. Transfer bacon to a dish, leaving drippings in pan. Place chicken in hot bacon fat & sautée until goden brown.
Sauté onion, garlic & jalapeño in separate skillet.
Whisk 1/4 cup water, cream, lemon juice, and Worcestershire sauce together in a bowl. Add to onion mixture.  Heat and stir, adding water as necessary to thin the sauce.   Remove from heat and stir in parsley.
Ladle pasta into a bowl and top with chicken, crumbled bacon and sauce.
