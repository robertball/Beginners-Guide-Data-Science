Title:
Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pks vacuum sealed chicken breast from Costco cut into 1 inch pieces
1 large yellow onion chopped medium
4 large carrots 1 inch pieces
4 small peppers, chopped
2 tbl olive oil light
4 bay leaves
1/2 tsp herbs de providence
1 tbl dried garlic
poultry gravy mix
salt and pepper

Directions:
Put olive oil in dutch oven, add onions, carrots,peppers bay leaves and salt and peppers.  Cook on high for 5 minuets.  Add the chicken bay leafs and provendence and garlic  and continue to cook until the chicken is no longer raw. Sprinkle the poultry gravy mix in the mixture and stir.  Continue to cook for another 5 minuets.  Add the chicken broth to cover the mixture, almost.  Cook for 20 min and all more gravy mix.  Simmer for another 20 min.  Serve.
