Title:
Devilish Guac Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1002350.jpg
Ingredients:
1-dozen Hard boiled eggs, shelled and sliced in half. Place yolk halves in a dish and sit aside.
1-ripe avocado, cut in half, remove seed and remove peeling.
1-2 cloves minced garlic (strictly preference).
1-Tablespoon minced onion (mince with garlic mincer).
1/2 fresh lime juiced.
1/2 cup mayonnaise
Salt to taste.
1/2 cup of fine shredded cheddar cheese.
48- olive slices, 2 per egg half.
Chili powder to dust the egg halves prior to olives and cheese being applied.

Directions:
Mash the avocado till smooth in a medium size bowl. Add the egg yolk and smash until incorporated together well. (If you use a fancy tip for making the deviled eggs cream with a food processor.)                                           Add the garlic, onion and mix very well (or process).
Add in mayo and lime juice and season with salt to taste.
Put a nice heap of yolk mixture in each egg half, place them on a serving dish.
Sprinkle the eggs with chili powder , sprinkle cheese and two olive slices per egg half. Cover and  Chill approximately 1 hour

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
I came up with this after eating sliced avocado with deviled eggs. I made them and took them to a party and received good feedback!
