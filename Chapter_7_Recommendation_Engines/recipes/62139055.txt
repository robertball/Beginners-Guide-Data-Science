Title:
Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch broccoli, cut into florets (6 cups)
3/4 cup Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
2 Tbsp. sugar
2 Tbsp. vinegar
1 small red onion chopped
6 slices bacon, crisply cooked and crumbled
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Mix: dressing, sugar and vinegar in a large bowl.
Add: remaining ingredients; mix lightly and cover.
Refrigerate: at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Ray
Description: 
This dish has been a favorite of mine for a very long time. It is a great side dish that the whole family will love.
