Title:
Ginger-Yogurt Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole chicken breasts
2 cups homemade yogurt
1/2 cup nut flour
1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit.
Split each breast down the back into two pieces.
Heat frying pan, add butter and brown the raw chicken but do not cook it completely.
Salt chicken and remove from heat.
Mix yogurt, almond flour, and grated ginger.
Arrange chicken in single layer in oven-safe pan and spread yogurt mixture over the surface of each piece of chicken, using all of the mixture.
Place uncovered in a 325 degree Fahrenheit oven and bake 1/2 hour or until chicken is tender.
