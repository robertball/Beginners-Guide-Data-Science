Title:
Rajah's Jewels Curry Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of golden raisins (Sultanas)
1 8 ox package of cream cheese
1 jar of sweet marmalade
Curry powder to taste

Directions:
1.  Briefly chop sultanas until they are in thirds. Set aside
2.  Mix rest of ingredients together in a medium=sized bowl.  Add sultanas and stir until well-blended.

Submitter: 
Sunshine
Description: 
It is really hard to stop eating this dip - be warned!  The utter simplicity of making it is also attractive. i use this version if I don't want to use a food processor.Modified from: Rajah's Jewels Dip II
