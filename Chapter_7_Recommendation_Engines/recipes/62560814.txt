Title:
Feta Bruschetta Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pints of grape tomatoes
1 medium red onion
3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/2 tsp each finely ground sea salt, red peppers flakes, black pepper
2 tsp finely chopped fresh thyme
1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
Finely slice onion into strips. In a baking pan spread the onions evenly, add the tomatoes. Drizzle tomatoes  and onions with the oil. Season with the salt, pepper flakes and black pepper, coat the tomatoes and onions evenly. place in oven for 10 minutes, reduce heat to 350 and continue to roast for 10 more minutes. In a bowl, add the feta cheese add the tomato mixture and gently stir until combined.
Serve with crostini or warm pita wedges.

Submitter: 
Kimmi
Description: 
Oven roasted grape tomatoes and onions make this a sweet and tangy spread for toasts, crostini or warm pitas. This is a perfect party appetizer.
