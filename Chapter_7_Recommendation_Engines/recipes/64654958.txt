Title:
Fried onion chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken
Ranch
Crushed fried onions
Cheese
Bacon

Directions:
Crush your fried onions and set aside. Dip your chicken in ranch,  then cover it in the onions. Bake at 350 for about 40-60 minutes.  Add cheese and bacon on top. Back in the oven to melt the cheese,  then enjoy!

Submitter: 
Racheal
Description: 
Everyone I have made this for can't get enough of it!  It's easy to do for busy nights!
