Title:
Stuffed Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground sausage
3 Acorn squash
1/4 c butter
1 c diced American cheese
1 10oz pkg frozen veggies
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
a about two hours before serving;
preheat oven to 350Â°. In 10 inch skillet over medium-high heat, cook sausage until browned, drain off fat. Cut squashes in-half lengthwise and remove seeds, place cut side up in large shallow roasting pan. Brush inside of each half with butter.
In medium bowl combine sausage cheese mixed vegetables and salt, spoon mixture into squash halfs and brush mixture with remaining butter. Cover pan with foil and bake 1 1/2 hours or until squashes are fork tender.
