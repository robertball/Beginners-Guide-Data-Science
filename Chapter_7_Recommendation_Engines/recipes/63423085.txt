Title:
Bittersweet Chocolate Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz bittersweet chocolate
1 tbsp. unsalted butter
1/3 cup milk
3 large eggs
1 large egg white
1/8 tsp cream of tartar
1/3 cup sugar

Directions:
1. butter the ramekins and sprinkle with sugar
2. place the chocolate, butter, and milk in a large heatproof bowl in a large skillet of barely simmering water
3. Stir until the chocolate is melted and the mixture is smooth.
4. Remove the bowl from the water bath and whisk in the egg yolks
5. set aside (and set aside whites)
6. in a medium dry bowl, beat the egg whites an cream of tartar. Gradually sprinkle in the 1/3 cup of sugar and beat at high speed until the whites are stiff but not dry.
7.Fold one-quarter of the egg whites into the chocolate mixture to lighten it, then fold in remaining egg whites
8. fill the rameskins, 3/4 full
9. Bake at 375 F for about 22-25 min
