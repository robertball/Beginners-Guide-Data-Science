Title:
Bourbon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds boneless chicken breast, bite size pieces
1-2 Tablespoons olive oil
1 clove garlic, crushed
1/4 teaspoon ginger
3/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/4 cup apple juice
1/3 cup light brown sugar
2 Tablespoon ketchup
1 Tablespoon cider vinegar
1/2 cup water
1/3 cup soy

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet, brown chicken. Remove chicken. Add ingredients heating over medium heat until well dissolved. Add chicken and bring to hard boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes. Serve over steamed rice.

Submitter: 
Rin
Description: 
A sweet spicy chicken served over steamed rice. One of my family's favorites.
