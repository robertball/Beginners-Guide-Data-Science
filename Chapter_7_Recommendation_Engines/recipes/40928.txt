Title:
Creamy Holiday Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 packages READY CRUST® Mini Graham Pie Crusts
1 (3.4 ounce) package vanilla flavor instant pudding & pie filling
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 teaspoon brandy extract
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
3/4 cup raisins
1 cup whipping cream, whipped

Directions:
Mix together vanilla pudding, sweetened condensed milk, 3/4 cup cold water, brandy extract, nutmeg and raisins.
Chill 15 minutes to set. Fold in 1 cup whipped cream and spoon into crusts.
Garnish with remaining whipped cream. Sprinkle with raisins and nutmeg.

Submitter: 
Ready Crust® Kitchens
Description: 
Raisins, nutmeg, and more create the perfect holiday dessert.  Prepared with Ready Crust® Mini Graham Cracker Crusts, these single-serve pies are an irresistible part of any holiday gathering.
