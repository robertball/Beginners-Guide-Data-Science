Title:
Big Ray's Carmel Apple Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3940588.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of granola
1 can apple pie filling
1 pint heavy cream
1 cup sugar
8 oz cream cheese
1 Tbls cinnamon
caramel topping

Directions:
In a mixer mix heavy cream, sugar, cinnamon,and cream cheese. On high speed for 1-2 minutes or until a peak forms.
In mason jars, place granola covering the bottom, top with caramel, add half the cheesecake filling and top with granola, caramel
