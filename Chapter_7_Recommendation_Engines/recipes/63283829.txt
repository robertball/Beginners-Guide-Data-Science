Title:
Grandma's sore throat Medicine / Orange Ice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can (6 oz) Frozen Orange Juice
Half Juice Can Water
3-4 Tablespoons powdered sugar (and sometimes more)
7-10 Regular Size Ice cubes....15-20 Small size

Directions:
Blend all ingredients but ice cubes in a blender for 5 seconds
With blender running at crush speed, add ice cubes 2-3 at a time
Blend until thick and smooth
Turn off and stir down mixture frequently ( this helps blend the ice evenly)
Serve Immediately and enjoy

Submitter: 
Nathan Shinn
Description: 
When I was a kid I would be sick and this is what my grandmother would make....it really works!!!! please respect her by not copying this recipe and taking it as your own.
