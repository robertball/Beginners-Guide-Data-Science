Title:
Pat's Peach Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups sugar,
5 TBSP flour,
3 egg yolks,
1 large can peaches, cut up, and drained,
1 pkg graham cracker crumbs,
1 stick butter,
1 can sweetened condensed milk,
2 tsp vanilla.

Directions:
Combine 1/2 cup sugar with cracker crumbs, and add melted butter.  Form a crust in a large bowl, saving 1/2 cup of crumbs for top of pudding.  On top of stove, in a 3 quart pot, combine 2 cups sugar, flour, beaten eggs, and milk; bring to a boil; stir until thick.  Remove from heat.  Add vanilla; stir.  Add cut up peaches; mix well.  Pour into crust.  Sprinkle remaining crumbs on top.  Chill about an hour and serve.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
My friend Pat brought this to school when we were in nursing school together. It was a big hit.  We were a small class, so each week we would all bring a dish and have lunch. It was the best part of school.
