Title:
Fizzy Autumn Cape Codder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/8614464.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounces pear flavored Vodka
1/2 ounce apple cider
2 ounces cranberry soda ( like cape cod cranberry dry)
Cinnamon stick for garnish

Directions:
Fill a lowball glass with ice.
In a shaker add vodka, Apple cider. stir with a spoon.
Pour over ice in glass.
Add cranberry soda and swizzle with a cinnamon stick.

Description: 
Flavors of fall in a cocktail. Juicy Pear, sweet apple cider and tart cranberry. Don’t forget the cinnamon stick for a bit of spice.
