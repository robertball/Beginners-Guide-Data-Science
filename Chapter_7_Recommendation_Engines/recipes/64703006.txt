Title:
Duchess Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 Pounds Chicken Breasts, Boneless, Skinless
2 Celery Stalks, finely chopped
3 Cups Red Grapes, Seedless,  Halved
1.5 Cups Pecans, Chopped (Optional)
2 Cups Soy-Free Mayonnaise
1/4 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp White Pepper
2 Tbsp  Poppy Seeds
2 Tbsp Dill

Directions:
Boil the chicken in a large pot filled with water until done, about 45 minutes. Drain the chicken and set aside to cool.
Once the chicken is cooled, dice into small pieces and place in a large mixing bowl.
Add the celery, grapes, pecans (optional), mayo, salt, white pepper, poppy seeds and dill.
Mix until the salad is fully combined.
Chill until ready to use.
