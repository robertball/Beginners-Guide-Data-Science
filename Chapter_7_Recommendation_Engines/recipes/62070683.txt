Title:
classic red velvet cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
1/2 cup of shortening
2 eggs
1 bottle (1 oz) red food coloring
3 teaspoons of white vinegar
1 tsp butter flavoring
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 and a half cups of cake flour
1/4 cup of baking cocoa
1 tsp of baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 cup of buttermilk
Frosting
1 package (8 oz) cream cheese softened
1/2 cup of butter softened ( I prefer unsalted)
3 and 3/4 cups of powdered sugar
3 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large mixing bown cream, shortening and sugar, add eggs one at a time beating well after each addition. Beat in food coloring, vinegar, butter flavoring, and vanila. Combine the flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt in seperate bowl then add the shortening mixture with buttermilk as well.
Pour batter into three greased and floured 9 inch round baking pans. Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until toothpick inserted in middle comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes then remove from pans to cool for 5 more minutes.
In a large mixing bowl combine frosting ingredients and beat until it's completely smooth and creamy. Spread frosting in between layers and on top of the cake
