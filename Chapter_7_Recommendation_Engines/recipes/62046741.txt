Title:
Sue's Apple Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup oats
1 3/4 cup all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup apples, peeled and finely chopped
1 cup walnuts or pecans, chopped

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugars.  Add eggs and vanilla.  Mix well.  Combine oats, flour, soda, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.  Mix well and gradually add to sugar mixture.  Stir in apples and nuts.  Drop by spoonfuls onto greased baking sheets.  Bake at 350 degrees for 10-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
This is my husband's favorite cookie.   His mother made them, and has passed the recipe to the next generation. Now we all make them for our families.  They are great served with fresh apple cider.
