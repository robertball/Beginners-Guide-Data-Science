Title:
Chocolate Martini II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/754252.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 fluid ounces creme de cacao liqueur
1/2 fluid ounce butterscotch schnapps

Directions:
In a cocktail mixer full of ice, combine creme de cacao liqueur and butterscotch schnapps. Shake vigorously and strain into glass.

Submitter: 
Neuf
Description: 
This is my own version of a popular drink. Garnish with a small peppermint candy cane.
