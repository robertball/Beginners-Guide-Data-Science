Title:
FRESH Green Bean Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 small tortillas
8 slices provolone cheese
1 c. fresh green beans, minced in food processor
1/2 c. green onions, minced in food processor
1/2 c. bread crumbs
1/4 c. parmesan cheese, grated
2 t. garlic powder
1 t. dried basil
1 t. dried oregano
2 t. Italian seasoning
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
Sprinkling of sugar if beans are not freshly picked with full sweetness.
1/4-1/2 c. olive oil

Directions:
Combine beans, onions, bread crumbs and grated parmesan cheese.
Add seasonings and mix well.
Moisten with olive oil to form "pesto".
Place 2 slices of provolone cheese on tortilla.
Spoon green bean "pesto" on top of cheese.
Wrap tortilla begining with bottom fold.
Served with Italian soup and lemon ice.
The green bean "pesto" can be stored in refrigerator for future use in a variety of dishes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Finally, healthy FRESH green beans served FRESH and flavorfully! Quick, simple and uses few ingredients.
