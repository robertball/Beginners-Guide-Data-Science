Title:
Crispy Oven-Fried Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2147657.jpg
Ingredients:
1.2 lbs ground turkey
3-4 scallions, finely chopped
3 tbsp. finely chopped parsley
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
2 eggs
1 1/4 cups whole wheat bread crumbs
flour for dredging

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
In a bowl, combine turkey, scallions, parsley, garlic, salt, pepper, 1 egg and 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs.
Form into 6 patties using a 1/3 measuring cup.
Beat the remaining egg with a few dashes of hot sauce.
Coat the patties with flour, egg & hot sauce mixture, then the remaining bread crumbs.
Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.
Place the patties on the baking sheet and lightly spray the patties.
Bake until crisp, 18-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
Ground turkey patties breaded and fried in the oven. Serve on a bun with zesty sauce and shredded cabbage to make it a meal.
