Title:
Hot Cheesy Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese, 12 ounces sour cream, 8 ounces of shredded parmesan and provolone, 8 ounces of cheddar cheese, 8 ounces of mozzerella, 8 ounces of frozen spinach

Directions:
Soften the cream cheese.  Run warm water over the frozen spinach and drain.  Mix all the ingredients into a bowl.  Put the mixture into a baking dish and cook at 375 degrees for 20 minutes or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
HAULBUR
Description: 
This is a hot cheesy spinach dip that I made for the Holidays.  It turned out to be a hit!
