Title:
Absolutely Easy Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet of Lipton "Soup Secrets" broth and noodle soup
2 tsp. of lemon juice (you can use fresh squeezed lemon too)
1 7 oz. can of corn
1 half tsp. of pepper
Optional: Croutons or crackers.

Directions:
1. Cook packet of noodle soup as directed.
2. While soup is cooking, put your 7 oz. can of corn on the stove, on low heat.
3. Once both are finished, combine them and add your 2 tsp. of lemon juice and your half tsp. of pepper.
4. Put in a bowl and add croutons/crackers then, devour!
