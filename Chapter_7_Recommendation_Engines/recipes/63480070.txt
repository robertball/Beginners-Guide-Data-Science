Title:
Chocolate Jell-o Pudding!  Dairy-Free!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/949033.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box (102gram or 4 serving) Jell-o chocolate pudding mix (dairy free)
3/4 cup canned coconut milk (not lite) - shaken/stirred up
1 cup cold water

Directions:
Follow directions on the box of pudding powder mix - replacing the milk with the combo of coconut milk & water.
Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Kerri
Description: 
Again, for our chocoholic dairy-allergic boy!  This is the best way to do chocolate pudding!  Have tried the other way (cocoa, sugar, etc), but it's not nearly as creamy or sweet! :)  This version is heavenly!
