Title:
Easy Chocolate Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/391623.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups( 8 ounces) semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 can (14-ounce) of fat free (or regular) sweetened condensed milk
Dash salt
1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
1. In a heavy saucepan, over a low heat, melt chocolate chips with sweetened condensed milk and salt. Remove from heat; stir in vanilla extract. Spread evenly into wax paper lined 8- or 9-inch square pan.
2. Chill 2 hours or until firm. Turn fudge onto cutting board; peel off paper and cut into squares.  and enjoy.
