Title:
blondie swirls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
1 6oz pkg semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Melt butter in saucepan, and add brown sugar.  Stir till combined.  Remove from heat and let cool.
Once cooled off add eggs one at a time, beating after each addition.
Stir in vanilla.
Combine dry ingredients together, and add to brown sugar mixture.  Stirring well.
Stir in nuts, and 1/4 cup chocolate chips.
Spread batter in a greased and floured 9x13in pan.
Sprinkle top with remaining chocolate chips.
Bake at 350 degrees for 3 minutes.  Remove from oven and gently swirl batter with a knife causing a marble effect with melted chocolate.  Return pan to oven and cook an additional 20 minutes.  Cool and cut into bars.
