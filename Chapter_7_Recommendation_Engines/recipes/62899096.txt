Title:
Jarlsberg Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup jarlsberg shredded cheese
1 cup onions caramelized in butter
1 cup heavy mayo
Mix together and bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes and serve with crackers, french bread, or a nice baguette.
