Title:
Cheeseball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
250 g cream cheese, softened
184 g can flaked ham
1 cup grated cheddar or marble cheese
1 tsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup sour cream
1/2 tsp. dill weed
1/4 tsp. cayenne powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well.
Shape in a mound on a serving platter.
Surround with crackers to serve.
Store in fridge.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Ham & cheese on crackers - classic! Keeps a long time.
