Title:
Sarah's Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken cutlets
1 cup of flour
1 T dried parsley
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
4 T extra light olive oil
1 T butter
zest from 1 lemon
4 lemons

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
1. cut chicken cutlets into bite size pieces
2. zest 1 lemon into bowl
3. juice 4 lemons, add to zest
4. heat 4 T oil over med-high heat
5. mix flour and seasonings in ziplock bag
6. toss chicken in flour mixture and set aside
7. saute chicken until golden brown
8. place finished chicken in shallow glass baking pan
9. spoon the lemon juice and zest over cooked chicken making sure to cover each piece. Use all lemon juice
10. place pan in oven for 15-20 min. remove from chicken and gently toss chicken in thickened sauce
Serve and Enjoy!
