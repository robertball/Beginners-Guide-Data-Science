Title:
Cajun Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. dry red pepper
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/4 tsp. white pepper
1/4 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1- 4 to 5 lb. chicken cut up
1/2 c. cooking oil
3 tbsp. flour
1 large (or two medium) onions
1 medium bell pepper
1 stalk celery
2 cloves garlic
3 c. chicken stock
1/2 c. green onions

Directions:
Mix the first six ingredients (spices) together and rub all over clean, dry chicken. Pour oil in large skillet and fry rubbed chicken over medium/high heat until done. Remove to paper towel and set aside.
Lower heat to medium and add flour stirring frequently to make dark brown roux. Add vegetables (except green onions) and cook till softened.  Add stock and put the cooked chicken back into the pot.  Cover with lid and cook 2 - 2 1/2 hours till chicken falls off bone.  Before serving, throw green onions in and stir.  Serve over rice; top with tabasco if desired.
