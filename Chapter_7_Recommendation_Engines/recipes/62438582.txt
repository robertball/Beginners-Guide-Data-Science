Title:
Cookies....All Dressed Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/688910.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar, packed
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup old fashioned oats
1 cup crisp rice cereal
1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup toffee bits
1/2 cup broken pretzel bits
1 cup milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream together butter and sugars. Add in eggs and vanilla extract and beat to combine. Set aside.
In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking soda, salt, oats, cereal, coconut, nuts, chocolate chips, toffee bits and pretzel bits.
Mix wet and dry ingredients together until well combined.
Drop dough by tablespoonfuls onto ungreased, parchment lined cookie sheet, about 2 inches apart. Flatten with a fork. Bake approximately 12 minutes or until bottoms are just beginning to brown. Remove from oven and allow to cool a few minutes before removing to cool completely on wire rack.
