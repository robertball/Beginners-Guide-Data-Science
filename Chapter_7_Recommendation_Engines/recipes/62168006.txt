Title:
APPLE STRUDLE: Maya Indian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 qt. milk
1 1/2 c. flour
5 apples
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 c. sugar

Directions:
By hand mix milk, flour, baking powder and vanilla. Cut the apples in thin slices. Wash apples and use skin.
Put apples in dough but do not beat. Bake 300 degrees until medium brown.
