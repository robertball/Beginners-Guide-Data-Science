Title:
Apple Baked Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium butternut squash, cubed
1 medium apple, peeled and cubed
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup butter
1-1/2 tsp flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Arrange butternut squash in 8 inch square microwavable dish.  Place apple on top of squash.
Combine remaining ingredients until well blended.  Drop by spoonfuls over apple squash mixture.
Cover  and cook in microwave on medium for 17-20 minutes.  Let stand, covered, for 3 minutes before serving.
