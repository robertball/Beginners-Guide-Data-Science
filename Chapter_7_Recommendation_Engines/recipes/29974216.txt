Title:
Savoury Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups chopped vegetables (onions, peppers, mushrooms, celery, zucchini, broccoli, garlic, asparagus)
1 Tbsp. marjoram
1 1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. salt
3 Tbsp. pressed organic sunflower oil
7 eggs
2 1/2 cups cream
1 cup diced fresh herbs (parsely, basil, rosemary dill)
12 cups cubed bread
3/4 cups grated Romano cheese
3/4 cups grated aged cheddar

Directions:
On a baking sheet, mix vegetables, marjoram, salt, pepper amd sunflower oil.  Bake in the oven for 10 minutes at 500F and set aside.
Whisk together eggs and cream.  Divide into two parts and set one aside.
Add bread, herbs and Romano cheese to one part of the egg mixture.
Lay half of bread mixture into a well-greased 11"x17" baking pan. Layer with half of raosted vegetables.  Add another layer of remaining bread mixture. Add remainng vegetables and top with cheddar cheese.
Pour second part of egg mixture over bread pudding and bake at 350F for 25 minutes.
Cover baking dish with parchment paper or lid and continue to bake for another 30 minutes.  Serve hot.

Description: 
This can be suited to many tastes depending on the vegetables used. The stunning colours and fresh flavours make it a festive centrepiece for any brunch table.
