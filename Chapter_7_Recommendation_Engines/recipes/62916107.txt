Title:
Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # pkg linguine or skroddles or egg noodles
1 head fresh broccoli or 16 oz frozen broccoli
4 T butter
2-2 1/2 # skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
8oz pkg. fresh mushrooms
4 T flour
1 can chicken broth
1 C skim milk
1 C grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 t black pepper

Directions:
Prepare noodles according to package. Add broccoli during last 4 min of cooking time. Drain.
Heat butter in large skillet over med-high heat. Add chicken and cook until well browned and cooked through, stirring often. Add mushrooms and onions during last 10 mins.
Add flour and cook a short time. Stir in chicken broth and milk and cook until thickened. Add cheese, black pepper and noodle mixture. Cook until hot and serve or put in oven and bake 10-15 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Original recipe calls for cream of mushroom soup but taste much better if fresh mushrooms are used and chicken broth substituted for cream
