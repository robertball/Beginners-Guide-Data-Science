Title:
Cauliflower, Potato & Bacon Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1495952.jpg
Ingredients:
4 - 6 slices bacon
2 tbsp butter
1 med onion, chopped
2 ribs celery, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
3 med carrots, chopped
4 med sized potatoes, peeled & cubed
1 med head cauliflower cut into small pieces
1 tbsp parsley
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
6 cups chicken stock
3/4 cup half & half
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Cook bacon until crispy and place on paper towel to cool.
When cooled, chop into small pieces.
Melt butter in stock pot - add onion, celery and garlic until softened and fragrant (about 5 minutes).
Add carrots, potatoes, cauliflower, parsley, paprika, salt & pepper and continue cooking, stirring occasionally for 10 minutes.
Pour in chicken stock and bring to a boil.
Simmer until vegetables are fork tender (about 15 minutes).
Stir in chopped bacon, half & half and milk (reserve some bacon if you want for a garnish).
Puree with a hand blender, or in batches in a large blender (exercising caution due to hot contents).
Simmer over low heat another 10 minutes.
Add more chicken stock if you prefer a thinner soup.
Add salt & pepper to taste.
Serve with crusty rolls or bread.

Description: 
A quick tasty homemade soup - perfect for cold fall days.
