Title:
Quickie Chicken Quesadilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup cheddar (or your favorite) cheese
1/2 cup cooked chicken
1/4 cup tomato
2 flour or corn tortillas

Directions:
Add all ingredients, except for tortillas, into Tall Cup and twist on Cross Blade.
Pulse until you achieve a blended, but chunky mixture.
Spread the mixture between the tortillas and microwave on high for 1 1/2 minutes.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
You can use any kind of leftover chicken you have, bbq, fried, it all tastes great. Or replace the chicken with ground beef, lunch meat, or other left over meats. Veggie quesadillas are just as delicious. Just add your favorite veggies, like squash, onions and peppers, and dig in.
