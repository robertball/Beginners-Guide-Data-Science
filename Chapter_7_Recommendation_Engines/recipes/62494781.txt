Title:
Fluffy Pumpkin Cheesecake Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages (8 oz. each) cream cheese, softened
2/3 cup sugar
2 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
2 eggs
1 can (15 oz) pumpkin
1 Keebler Ready crust extra serving pie crust

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl beat cream cheese on medium speed of electric mixer until fluffy.  Add sugar and spice, beat until combined.
Add eggs, one at a time, mixing until just combined after each addition.  Stir in pumpkin.
Pour into crust.  Bake at 350 for 35 to 40 minutes or until center is almost set.  Cool for 1 hour on wire rack.  Refrigerate at least 3 hours.

Submitter: 
Pam Morin
Description: 
Off the Keebler 2 extra serving graham cracker pie crust - the easiest and yummiest pumpkin pie ever!
