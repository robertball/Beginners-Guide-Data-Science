Title:
Bread Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup olive oil
1 teaspoon garlic powder, OR
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
1/4 cup water, warmed
2/3 cup milk, warmed
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups flour, to 3 cups

Directions:
Mix olive oil, garlic, and cheese.  Set aside.  Mix yeast, water, milk, sugar, salt, and 2 cups flour.  Beat until dough pulls away from bowl.  Add more flour if needed.  Mix well for 3-5 minutes.  Place dough in greased bowl, turning in bowl to grease all sides of dough.  Cover and let rise until double.  Punch down on floured board.  Roll into small ropes and place on a cookie sheet greased with oil mixture.  Place 1" apart.  Brush with oil mixture.  Let raise once more.  Bake at 375-400 degrees for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
I got this from my sister-in-law Phyllis.  We so love breadsticks and these taste wonderful.  We love the parmesan cheese flavor!
