Title:
Ham appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 slice of Ham
Salt
butter

Directions:
First, take out a frying pan and grease it
Next, chop up the slice of ham into small bits
Then, heat frying pan until the butter bubbles
Next, put in all the ham
Finally, take the ham out and serve

Submitter: 
Izzy Fiona
Description: 
This ham will make an excellent side to any dish
