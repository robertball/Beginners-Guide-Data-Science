Title:
Almond Thins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
200g plain flour
200g icing sugar
3 whole eggs
1 egg white
Rind and Juice of 1 Lemon
100g melted butter
100g almond slice(toasted)

Directions:
Line aluminium cookie sheet with Parchment paper, preheat oven to 200 C
Mix all ingredients except almond slice well Add Almond slice
Spoon 2 tbsp and spread batter on a parchment paper as thinly as possible (Really really thin) Note:  Batter will be very sticky
Bake at 200 C for 8 to 10 minutes or until edges are brown
