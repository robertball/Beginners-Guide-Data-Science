Title:
Sandy's Avocado Salad Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1603164.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Avocados,chopped into bite sized pieces
1/3 red onion chopped
2 Plumb Tomatoes, chopped
1/4 Cup Sharp Cheddar Cheese, chopped or what ever cheese you desire
Olive Oil to drizzle
2 Teaspoons Red Wine Vinegar (more if you want)
2 to 3 Pinches of Dried basil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium sized bowl, add avocado, onion, tomatoes, cheese.
Drizzle some olive oil,add vinegar, dried basil to avocado mixture; mix well. Refrigerate for at leas 1/2 hour so flavors blend.

Submitter: 
Sandy Lafleur
Description:
