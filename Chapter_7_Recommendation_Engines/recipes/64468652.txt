Title:
Balsamic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb chicken breasts
1/4 c balsamic vinegar
2 clove garlic
2 Tbsp olive oil (divided)
2 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
3 Tbsp honey
salt & pepper

Directions:
Slice the chicken into strips.  Marinate for at least 30 minutes in balsamic vinegar, garlic and 1 Tbsp of olive oil.
Heat the 2nd tablespoon of oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  When hot, cook the strips until browned on both sides and cooked through.  Place on a clean plate.
Turn the heat to medium-low and add the butter and last 2 tablespoons of vinegar.  Deglaze the pan and once the mixture has combined add the honey, increase the heat  and allow the sauce to simmer until thickened.
Once the sauce has thickened, season to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Noel Keen
Description: 
Healthy, quick chicken dish that presents beautifully with steamed vegetables.
