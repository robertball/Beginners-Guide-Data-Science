Title:
Sloppy Joe Supreme (lighter recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985247.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Bisquick Heart Smart® mix
1 1/2 cups shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup water
1 egg
1 pound extra lean (at least 90%) ground beef
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F. In small bowl, stir together Bisquick(R) mix, 1 cup of the cheese, the water and egg; set aside.
In ovenproof 10-inch skillet, cook beef over medium heat about 8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until brown; drain. Stir in ketchup, salt and tomato sauce. Heat over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until hot.
Pour Bisquick mixture over top. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheese.
