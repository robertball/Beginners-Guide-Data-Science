Title:
Ice Cream Pie Shell Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup light corn syrup
4 cups chocolate flavored cereal puffs

Directions:
Mix peanut butter, corn syrup, and puffed cereal together.  Pat into a 10 inch pie plate.

Submitter: 
Terri Lutz
Description: 
A great big crust that holds up very well to a large ice cream pie.  Top with favorite ice cream and lots of gooey toppings.
