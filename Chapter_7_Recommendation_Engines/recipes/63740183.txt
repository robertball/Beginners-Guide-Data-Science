Title:
Raspberry Jelly Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Flour
1 Pound Butter
1 Cup Sugar
1 tbsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowel.
Chill in fridge for 30 min
Roll out dough and cut into cookie shapes
Place raspberry jelly in center of cookie
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes
