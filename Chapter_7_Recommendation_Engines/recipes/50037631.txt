Title:
Hot Chocolate Fondant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 TB Unsalted Butter - plus extra to grease
2 ts unsweetened cocoa
2 oz bittersweet chocolate (70% or higher)
1 large egg
1 large egg yolk
1/3 C Superfine sugar
1/3 C Flour

Directions:
Heat oven to 350F
Grease two 3" ramekins
Dust liberally with cocoa powder
Slowly melt chocolate and butter in small bowl set over a pan of boiling water. Take off heat and stir until smooth. Let cool for 10 minutes.
Using an electric whisk, whisk the whole egg, egg yolk and sugar together until pale and thick.
Mix into the chocolate mixture.
Sift flour over the mixture and gentle fold in using a large spoon.
Divide between the ramekins.
Bake for 15-17 minutes.
Turn the ramekins upside down onto warmed serving plates.
Let sit for 1-2 minutes then turn out onto plates. Serve immediately.
