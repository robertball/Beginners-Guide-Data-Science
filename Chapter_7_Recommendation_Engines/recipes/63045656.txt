Title:
Allergy Friendly Comfort Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs olive oil
2-3 yams
1 head cauliflower
1 jalapeno
1/2 tsp ginger
1 tsp, mustard
1/2 tsp cardamon
1 tsp coriander
1/2 tsp turmeric
1 tsp smoked paprika
handful fresh basil
1 can coconut milk
2 cup water
2 cup cannellini beans or any other bean
1 bay leaf
1 cinnamon stick
1 tbs soy sauce(omit for soy allergies or use tamari sauces)
salt, pepper, parsley
Goat Cheese for Garnish

Directions:
1. Peel and cube yams, cut cauliflower into bite size pieces.  Dice jalapeno. Saute yams in olive oil until fork tender. Add cauliflower and saute for another 10-15 minutes.
2.In stock pot add coconut milk, water and rinsed beans over medium-high heat as well as all spices, salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil then add in yams and and cauliflower and continue to simmer on low for 30-45 minutes depending on how soft you like your cauliflower.
3. Serve garnish with parley and a splash of soy sauce as well as some Goat Cheese crumbles depending on your taste.

Submitter: 
Allergy Friendly
Description: 
Coming from a family with many strange allergies this soup is friendly for virtually anyone and is the ultimate comfort food, you won't feel like you are missing any of your favorites! Gluten Free, Egg Free, Nut Free, Dairy Free, Fish Free, latex allergen free and Flavor Full!
