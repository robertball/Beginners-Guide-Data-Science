Title:
Quick and Easy Chicken Enchiladas I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/267628.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups pre-cooked chicken meat
1 onion, finely chopped
1 small can of mild green chiles
1/2 pint sour cream
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 tablespoon fresh cilantro or flat leaf parsley, dried if necessary
2 tablespoons taco seasoning from a packet
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
6 (10 inch) flour tortillas
1 (12 ounce) jar enchilada or taco sauce
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Warm plates.
In a medium bowl, combine cooked chicken, onion, green chiles, sour cream, Cheddar cheese, cilantro o rparsley, taco seasoning, and tomato sauce.  Stir and taste- adjust seasoning to your liking.
Lay 6 tortillas flat on the counter.  Evenly divide the mixture between the tortillas and roll.   Arrange, seam side down,  in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover with enchilada or taco sauce and 3/4 cup Cheddar cheese. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven 20-25 minutes. Cool 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Quick&EasySupermom
Description: 
This quick dinner is made even faster with store bought or left over pre-cooked chicken and taco seasoning.  I like to use the cooking time to prepare bowls of garnish to pass- turning this easy weeknight meal into a mexican fiesta.  Serve Mexcian chocolate or Dulce de Leche ice cream for dessert.Modified from: Chicken Enchiladas I
