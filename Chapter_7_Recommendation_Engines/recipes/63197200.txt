Title:
Vanilla Yogurt Strawberry Parfait Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/826707.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. vanilla yogurt
1/2 c. diced strawberries
2 chocolate graham crackers crushed

Directions:
spoon 1/2 c. of yogurt into a glass cup.
lay on top of yogurt 1 crushed chocolate graham cracker.
lay on top of graham cracker 1/4 c. of diced berries.
repeat.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Yogurt, fresh sweet strawberries and chocolate graham crackers make for a delicious and easy breakfast parfait that's good enough too for a healthy dessert.
