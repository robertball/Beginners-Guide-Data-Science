Title:
Grandma Nancy's Spritz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups butter
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp almond extract
4 cups sifted all purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
various colors of granulated sugar

Directions:
Bring butter to room temperature. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Cream together the butter and sugar, then add the egg and extracts. Sift together the flour and baking powder then add slowly to the other ingredients. Do not chill the dough like you might fr other cookies. Press in a cookie press onto an ungreased cookie sheet and decorate with colored sugar. Bake at 400 degrees for 8-9 minutes. Remove Immediatly and cool on a wire rack. Note that spritz don't brown on top like other cookies. They really are done after 8-9 minutes in a properly calibrated oven!

Submitter: 
Robert Loughrey
Description: 
Swedish almond butter cookies are very addictive and a Christmas favorite. My mother taught me to make these from an early age for our traditional Swedish Christmas meal.
