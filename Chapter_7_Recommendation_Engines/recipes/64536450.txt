Title:
Oreo Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 36 Oreo cookies, finely crushed, divided
* 1 Pkg. (8 ounce) Philadelphia cream cheese, softened
* 4 Pkg. ( 4 oz. each) baker's semi-sweet chocolate, broken into pieces melted

Directions:
1) Reserve 1/4 cup cookie crumbs.
2) Mix cream cheese and remaining cookie crumbs until blended.
3) Shape into 48 (1inch) balls. Freeze 10 minutes. Dip in melted chocolate; place on waxed paper-covered rimmed baking sheet. Sprinkle with reserved cookie crumbs.
4) Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm.
