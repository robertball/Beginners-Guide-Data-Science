Title:
Instant Pot Buffalo Ranch Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5736296.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds chicken breasts
1 onion, chopped
2 celery stalks, chopped
1 tablespoon garlic, minced
¼ cup full-fat coconut milk (see notes)
¼ cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon ranch seasoning
½ cup hot sauce
¼ cup salted butter
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
¼ cup + 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
Sliced celery, red and yellow peppers, and cucumbers for dipping

Directions:
Place chicken breasts in bottom of Instant Pot.
Cover with remaining ingredients except nutritional yeast.
Place cover on Instant Pot and cook on high pressure for 20 minutes (allow 10 minutes to reach pressure).
Preheat oven to 400 degrees (optional; see below).
Manually release pressure and using forks, shred chicken.
Set pressure cooker to saute setting and stir in nutritional yeast; allow to simmer for 5 minutes and transfer to serving dish* and top with remaining tablespoon nutritional yeast.
*If desired, transfer to an oven-safe baking dish and bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes or until mixture has thickened and edges are crispy.
Notes: ¼ cup coconut milk may be replaced with additional ¼ cup mayo or if not dairy-free, with cream cheese.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
This buffalo ranch chicken dip is Paleo and Whole 30 friendly.  It's a little more juicy than a traditional dip with cheese, but the flavor is rich and layered!  If you like, finish in an oven-safe dish to thicken and get crispy edges.
