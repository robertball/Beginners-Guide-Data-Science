Title:
Cheesy Asparagus Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. finely diced onion
1 garlic clove, minced
2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar
1/4 c. dry bread crumbs
2 Tbsp minced fresh parsley
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp dried oregano
1/8 tsp hot pepper sauce
4 eggs, beaten
1 lb fresh asparagus, cut in 1/2" pieces

Directions:
In a skillet, saute onion and garlic in oil until tender.
Combine cheese, bread crumbs, parsley, salt and pepper, oregano and hot pepper sauce.
Stir in the onion mixture and eggs.
Cook asparagus in a small amount of water until crisp-tender, about 3-4 minutes.  Drain well.
Stir into cheese mixture.
Pour into greased 9 inch square baking pan.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until knife inserted near chenter comes out clean.
Let stand 15 minutes.
Cut into small squares - serve warm.
