Title:
EASY Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground lean beef
40 oz can red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
30 1/2 oz can chili hot beans
21 1/2 oz can baked beans
15 oz can tomato sauce
6 oz can tomato paste
4 oz can chopped green chilis
2 cups water
1 t minced garlic
1 large onion, minced
1 packet cincinnati chili seasoning

Directions:
Crock Pot Version: Throw all into a crock pot and set on low for at least 5 hours.
Stove Top Version: Throw all into a large pot, bring to boil, set to low, stir occasionally, ready to serve in about 60 minutes.

Submitter: 
The Lazy Gourmet
Description: 
This chili is super easy to make - get the can opener and have dinner in about an hour!
