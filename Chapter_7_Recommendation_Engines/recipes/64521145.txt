Title:
Ratatouille sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 paper
1 tomato
1 clove garlic
1 tomato
1 eggplant
1 zucchini
1 red onion
4 slices sourdough bread
4 t. olive oil
4 slices mozzarella
salt, pepper, oregano

Directions:
1. Cut paper in slices and grill on a panini pan on high heat until soft. Cut tomatoes and garlic and blend with paper until smooth. Add salt, pepper and oregano to taste.
2. Cut tomato, eggplant, zucchini and red onion in slices and grill in panini pan.
3. Drizzle some olive oil on the bread and on the other side a generous amount of tomato souse. Than put one slide Olive oil side down on the panini pan. Start assembling with a slice mozzarella, than tomato, zucchini, eggplant and red onion, finish with another slice mozzarella and put a second slice of bread on top.
4. Cook for 5 min on medium high.
