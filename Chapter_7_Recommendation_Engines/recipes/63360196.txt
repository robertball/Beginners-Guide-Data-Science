Title:
Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894902.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 pounds round steak, 1" thick
2 Tbsp oil
Sauce:
1 16 oz can tomatoes
1/2 cup minced green pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Stir together flour, salt, pepper. Sprinkle on meat and pound in with meat mallet or the edge of a plate. Cut into 6 pieces.
Brown pieces in oil over medium heat for about 15 minutes.
Cover tightly and simmer for 1 hour, adding a bit of water as necessary to keep from drying out.
Sauce:
Mix together tomatoes, green pepper, salt, pepper, onion powder.
Pour over meat after it has simmered for an hour.
Cover tightly and simmer for 30 minutes longer.
Serve with potatoes, rice or noodles.
