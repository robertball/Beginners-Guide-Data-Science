Title:
Sauteed Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 cup butter
2 large tart apples - peeled, cored and sliced 1/4 inch thick
1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
1/4 cup cold water
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 t vanilla
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a large skillet or saucepan, melt butter over medium heat; add apples. Cook, stirring constantly, until apples are almost tender, about 6 to 7 minutes.
Dissolve cornstarch in water; add to skillet. Stir in brown sugar, vanilla and cinnamon. Boil for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and serve warm.

Submitter:

Description: 
Sweet, sauteed apples are great served at any meal. The syrup from the apples is delicious on homemade pancakes or waffles.Modified from: Sauteed Apples
