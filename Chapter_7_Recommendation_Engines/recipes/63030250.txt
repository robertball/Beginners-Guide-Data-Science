Title:
Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh backfin crabmeat
1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup mayonnaise
2-3 tablespoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons Worcesterchire sauce
3 cloves garlic, crushed
1 bunch scallion
2 cups extra sharp cheddar chees, shredded
1 cup monterey Jack cheese, shredde
1/2 teaspoon chili powder

Directions:
Mix first 8 ingredients until well blended. Transfer to oven-proof backing dish.  Top with Monterey Jack cheese. Sprinkle lightly with chili power. Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes until brown an dbubbly.
