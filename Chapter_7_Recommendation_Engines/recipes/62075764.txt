Title:
Pizza Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One box Kraft Macaroni and cheese, cooked to directions
one pound fresh lean ground beef, browned and cooked on stove, drained
one green bell pepper, diced
one small onion, diced
Hormel sliced pepperoni
2 jars Ragu Pizza-Quick traditional flavor sauce (in spaghetti sauce section of store)
Two packages shredded Mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook Kraft macaroni and cheese according to directions. Drain. Place in greased casserole dish. Brown the lean ground beef in skillet on stove until cooked. Drain and stir into macaroni. Pour jars of Pizza Quick on top. Add pepperoni slices, diced green bell pepper and onion. Stir thoroughly in casserole dish.  Bake at 325 degrees for 35 minutes. Add shredded Mozzarella cheese on top during the last 15 minutes until melted and forms a crusty, bubbly layer. Cool.  Serves 6-8.  Kids love this.

Pizza Casserole.  Has macaroni and cheese, lean ground beef, Ragu pizza-quik sauce, onions, green bell pepper, pepperoni, and mozzarella cheese.
