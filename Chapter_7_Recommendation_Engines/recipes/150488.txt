Title:
Apple Nut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980903.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups finely chopped all-purpose apples
1 (15.4 ounce) package nut quick bread mix
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
1 cup chopped nuts
3 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
Cream Cheese Frosting
2 (3 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 cups confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. In large bowl, combine all ingredients except frosting; mix well. Pour into greased and floured 15x10-inch jellyroll pan.
Bake 30 minutes or until golden. Cool. Spread with Cream Cheese Frosting. Cut into bars. Garnish as desired. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.
Cream Cheese Frosting: In small mixer bowl, beat 2 (3-ounce) packages cream cheese, softened, 1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened, and 1 teaspoon vanilla until fluffy. Add 4 cups confectioners' sugar; mix well. (Makes about 2 1/2 cups)

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
A delicious apple and nut bar with a cream cheese frosting makes the perfect fall treat.
