Title:
Chicken Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Boneless,skinless chicken breasts,
cut in bite size chunks.
1 1/2 cups frozen broccoli florets
no stems
1lb. linguine
1/4 C. butter or margarine
1/3 C. olive oil
2 garlic cloves,minced
1 large onion,sliced
1 large green pepper,cut in bite size
pieces
3 T. lemon juice
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. freshly ground pepper
1/3 C. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook broccoli according to pkg. directions. In meantime,boil linguine approximately 9 minutes,drain when done. In large frying pan,place butter and olive oil over medium temperature. Add garlic,onion and green pepper,saute about 5 minutes;remove from pan and reserve. Add chicken to pan and cook about 5 minutes. Return onion and green pepper to pan,add cooked broccoli and saute 2 more minutes,til heated through. Add lemon juice,salt and pepper. Place hot linguine in serving dish,top with chicken mixture and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
SABRYSON
Description: 
Delicious combination of garlic,lemon,chicken and broccoli all served over linguine. Simple,easy and tastes so good!
