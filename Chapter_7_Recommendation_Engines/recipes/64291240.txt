Title:
Turkey Lasagna with spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1932494.jpg
Ingredients:
750 - 1000 ml Homemade White Sauce
400 g leftover roast turkey
1 large carrot, chopped
2 large stalks celery, chopped
2 medium onions, chopped
30 ml extra virgin olive oil
1 500 g tub Gay-Lee 1% fat-free sour cream
1/2 300 g package GV chopped frozen spinach
15 ml black pepper
300 ml Butternut Squash Cheese Sauce
150 g  Thornloe cheese curds
12 Catteli Healthy Harvest Lasagna noodles cooked to package instructions
Cheese Sauce:
1000 g  Butternut Squash raw
500 ml, Reduced Sodium Chicken Broth
125 ml, 1% Milk
Â½ can Cream of mushroom soup
1 medium onion, chopped
100 g low fat cheddar cheese
100 g low fat Mozzarella cheese
150 g Moterey jack cheese
Homemade White sauce:
75 ml Chefmaster 52% oil margarine
90 ml white flour
1000 ml 1% or skim milk
30 ml Bulk Barn version Mrs Dash, or to taste
15 ml black pepper, or to taste
15 ml garlic powder, or to taste

Directions:
Butternut Squash Cheese Sauce:
dice and cook the squash, and onion in the chicken broth until squash is soft, take either a hand blender and puree the squash and onion, or in small batches in a regular blender.
add the mushroom soup, milk and the cheeses and stir until cheese is melted and mixture thickens
Makes about 750 ml of the sauce
Homemade white sauce:
in a medium saucepan melt margarine and add flour and stir to form a roux pour in the milk and spices stir then bring to boil then simmer until thickened if too thin then add more flour until desired consistency, if too thick then add more milk
Makes about 1000 ml
Lasagna:
in a large saucepan saute the carrots, celery, and onions in the olive oil, about 5 minutes, then add the turkey and white sauce, reserving approx 250 ml of sauce for bottom of pan, heat to boiling then let simmer about 25 minutes, meanwhile boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
in another saucepan or microwaveable bowl combine the Squash cheese sauce and cheese curds, heat until cheese curds are melted about 3-5 minutes on auto-sensor reheat
in a medium bowl mix the sour cream and spinach the pepper
Preheat oven to 350, then assemble the lasagna by layers with middle layer being the sour cream and spinach mixture, and spreading the squash cheese sauce mixture on top, bake at 350 uncovered for about 45 minutes or until top is golden brown, let stand 10-15 minutes
