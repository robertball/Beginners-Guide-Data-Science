Title:
Olive Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz green Greek olives, marinated in Italian spices, sliced
3 oz kalamata olives, sliced
2 green onions, sliced
150 g mayonnaise
6 oz Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
1 1lb loaf french bread, vienna bread, italian bread, or other artisan loaf, split lengthwise

Directions:
Mix together first 5 ingredients.  Spread onto bread.  Wrap each piece in plastic wrap and freeze or refrigerate until ready to bake.
Bake at 325 degrees for 25-30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and browned.
