Title:
pistachio crusted asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/322232.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. fresh asparagus (the fat ones)
2 cups pistahios
2 eggs
2 T water
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel the asparagus and set aside. Shell the 2 cups of pistachios and grind in a food processor till fine. Beat the eggs with the water to make an egg wash.
Roll the asparagus in the eggwash, then the nuts, and place on a sheet pan outfitted with a cooling rack.
Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees while allowing the crust to set.
Bake for 15 minutes.
