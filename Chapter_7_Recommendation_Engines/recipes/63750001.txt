Title:
Creamy Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz elbow macaroni
2-10 3/4 Campbells Cheddar Cheese soup
2 Tablespoons yellow mustard
4 tablespoons butter
2 cups milk

Directions:
Cook macaroni shortest time the package calls for. Drain and place in to a big mixing bowl. Mix soup, milk, mustard in with macaroni. Stir in 3/4 of the cheese and butter. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes. Put remaining cheese on the top and bake an additional 5 minutes.
