Title:
Mom's Cole Slaw Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Mayonaise
1 1/2 Tbsp Worchestershire Sauce
1 Tbsp Celery Seed
7 1/2 Tbsp salt
1/4 cup Prepared Mustared
1/4 cup Vinegar
1 cup sugar
1/8 teasp Pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredents and pour over one head of shredded cabbage.  Toss lightly.  Chill for an hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Woodbine United Methodist
Description: 
Borrowed from the middle school kitchen she worked at in the mid 1970's, this sweet and tart dressing has been a favorite for decades.
