Title:
Cabbage Caserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small head of cabbage
1 med. Sweet (Vidalia) onion
15 oz Cream of Mushroom Soup (1.5 cans)
2 Tubes Buttered Crackers (Ritz)
1 cup butter
1 Tbsp Salt
3 qt baking dish
Vegetable steamer

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350 degrees f
Grease the baking dish (I use butter)
Crush the Ritz crackers
Shred Cabbage (use the slicing disk on the food processor, not the shredder)
Prepare steamer
Once steamer ready, put cabbage in and salt the cabbage
Steam until soft but not mushy
Dice the onion
Mix steamed cabbage, onion, and mushroom soup and put in the baking dish
Melt the butter and mix in the crushed crackers
Put the topping on the cabbage mix
Bake for 35 minutes or until bubbling and brown

Description:
