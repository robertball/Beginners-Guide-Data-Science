Title:
Spiced Cranberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 ounce bag fresh cranberries
1/2 cup honey
5 Tablespoons to 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
2 3-inch cinnamon sticks
1 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
3/4 cup water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a pan.
Simmer covered, stirring occasionally until berries burst and mix is thickened.
Refrigerate.
This is best made at least 2 days in advance to let the flavors blend.

Submitter: 
Dutchgirl
Description: 
For those who prefer a cranberry sauce that doesn't slide out of a can in one piece.
