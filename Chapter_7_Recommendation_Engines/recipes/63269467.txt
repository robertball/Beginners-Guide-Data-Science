Title:
Poached Eggs Over Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C water
1 C tomoato, diced
6 C baby spinach, (stems removed)chopped
4 large organic eggs
pinch of sea salt and or pepper
1/2 tbs fresh thyme
splash of extra virgin oil

Directions:
Into the saute pan over medium high heat, place the water, tomato, and spinach.  Make small indentions in the spinach and gently crack the eggs on top.
Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and fresh thyme.  Cover and allow to cook until the eggs are done to your liking.
The spinach should be wilted and the water evaporated.
Serve topped with olive oil.
