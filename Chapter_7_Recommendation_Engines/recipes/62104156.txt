Title:
Hot Cheesy Seafood Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1.5 cup shredded cheddar cheese (medium or sharp)
1.5 cup shredded montery jack
2 cans (6 oz) crabmeat
1/4 lb. baby cocktail shrimp
Juice of 2 fresh lemons
1/2 tablespoon of garlic powder
3-5 dashes of hot sauce
3-5 dashes of Worcestershire sauce
salt and pepper to taste
paprika for garnish
1/4 cup parmesan cheese for garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Mix all ingredients together (expect for paprika and parmesan cheese)
Spread in a shallow baking dish.
Top with paprika and parmesan cheese
Cover with foil and bake for about 25 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
Serve with sliced sweet or sour baguette and/or crackers (such as Club or Ritz)

Submitter: 
Jackie Fernandez-Gonzalez
Description: 
Great for parties, this hot seafood dish is great with slices of baugette or crackers!
