Title:
Alyson's Beef Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cups broccoli
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup honey
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
1 1/2 pounds flank steak, cut into thin strips

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook onion broccoli and garlic in oil until tender-crisp, stirring frequently. Set aside.  Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.  Pour soy sauce, honey, and red wine vinegar in pan, then add beef.  Cook beef, stirring frequently, until done, about 10 to 15 minutes.  Stir in broccoli, and cook another 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Alley
Description: 
Beef and broccoli cooked in a mixture of soy sauce, honey, and red wine vinegar is delicious.  Serve over white or brown rice.Modified from: Maria's Pepper Steak
