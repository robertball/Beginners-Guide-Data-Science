Title:
Mini Wheels with Peas, Ham and Tomato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1098000.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) box BarillaÂ® Mini Wheels
4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
4 tablespoons minced onions
1/3 cup julienned fully cooked ham
1/4 cup frozen peas
4 medium plum tomatoes, diced
1/2 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
In a large skillet saute onions in olive oil over medium heat. Add the ham and cook until slightly crispy, 2 to 3 minutes.
Add the peas and saute for 2 minutes. Add the tomatoes; season with salt and pepper.
Cook pasta according to directions.
Drain the pasta and toss with sauce and cheese.

Submitter: 
Barilla
Description: 
Mini pasta wheels tossed with diced tomatoes, peas and ham are topped with grated cheese for this quick, kid-pleasing weeknight meal.
