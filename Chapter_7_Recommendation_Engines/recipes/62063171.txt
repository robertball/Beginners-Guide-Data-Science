Title:
Homemade 'Cream of' Soup Base Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4    c milk
2    T flour
2    T cooking oil (canola, vegatable, olive...)
2    T sugar
1/2 t  salt
1/2 t  pepper
1/2 t  garlic powder
OPTIONS:
-omit salt & garlic powder and use 1 t garlic salt
-add 2 T cornstarch and water mix to further thicken after 18 mins.
-replace milk with 3 c cream & 1 c water for richness

Directions:
1. Make a white sauce of milk or cream, flour, oil, sugar, salt, pepper, and garlic powder
2. Mix well and simmer for 20 minutes over low heat.
3. If  desired, mix cornstarch and water, add to soup, simmer for 10 minutes.
POSSIBLE ADD-INS
-2 c cooked, cubed chicken meat
-2 c cooked, white rice
-2 bunches of chopped celery added to boiled water, 1/2 t celery salt, and 2 T butter. Simmer celery mix 40 min before adding to soup base.
