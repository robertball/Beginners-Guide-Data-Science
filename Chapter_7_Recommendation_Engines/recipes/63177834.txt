Title:
cheesy eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 eggs
1 jar cheese whiz
butter

Directions:
scramble eggs. melt a small sliver of butter in skillet, add a small portion of eggs stir with spatula consistantly till done turn out into bowl repeat till all eggs are done. Stir in small spoonfulls of cheese whiz stir in about 1/3 of the jar of cheese whiz mix well and serve.
