Title:
teriyaki grilled chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. chicken
1/2 cup soy
5 green onions, chopped
1/4 cup lime juice
2 Tab. dark brown sugar
1 Tab. honey
1 tea. crushed red pepper
1 clove garlic, pressed

Directions:
place chicken in 11x7 baking dish
combine all and pour over..marinate 8 hrs
drain,reserve, bring to boil
grill, basting often
