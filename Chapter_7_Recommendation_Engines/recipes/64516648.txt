Title:
Tasty Turnips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
turnips, peeled
apple, peeled
brown sugar
butter (optional)
cinnamon (optional)
honey or maple syrup, if desired

Directions:
Boil enough turnips (covered in water) to feed your ppl. Can cook apple(s) with turnips or add later.
Mash turnips (& apple, if it was cooked with turnips), adding a tbsp. or so of butter if desired.
Add an apple (or 2, if you're feeding a lot of turnip-tasters!). The apple can either be cooked with the turnips or added after you've mashed the turnips.
Add enough sugar, honey and/or maple syrup, to your personal taste preference.
Sprinkle on some cinnamon.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Ok, so turnips usually aren't very tasty, but my Oma added some ingredients that made them more palatable. I also like to add some butter while mashing the turnips.
