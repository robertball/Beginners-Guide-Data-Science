Title:
Avocado Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4640729.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp coconut oil
1 tbsp almond flour
2 ripe avocados, pitted, skinned and sliced
1 lime, juiced
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup goat cheese
1/4 tsp red chili flakes
1 tbsp basil, chopped
1/4 cup skim milk
1/8 tsp salt
2 cups whole wheat elbow macaroni
1/4 cup low fat mozzarella cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Melt the coconut oil in a skillet over medium heat.  Toast almond flour in coconut oil until golden brown.
Add avocados, lime juice, garlic, goat cheese, chili flakes, basil, skim milk and salt to food processor.  Puree until fully blended and smooth.
Cook macaroni according to package instructions.
Mix avocado mix with macaroni.
Place mixture into a casserole pan.  Top with the almond flour and the mozzarella.  Bake for 7 to 8 minutes.
