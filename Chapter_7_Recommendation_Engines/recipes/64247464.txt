Title:
Menudo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp oil
1 small onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 lb pork tenderloin, cut in small cubes
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp paprika
1/2 cup tomato sauce
1/2 cup water
2 medium potatoes, diced

Directions:
In large saucepan, saute onion and garlic in oil.  Add pork and cook until done.
Add salt, pepper, soy sauce, paprika, tomato sauce and water.  Simmer for 20 minutes.
Add potatoes and cook for 20-30 minutes.  Serve with rice.
