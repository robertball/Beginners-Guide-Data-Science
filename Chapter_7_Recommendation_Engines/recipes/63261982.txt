Title:
Wild Rice with Mushrooms and Hazelnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
oil
1 1/2c mushrooms, sliced
1c green onion
1c hazelnuts, chopped
1t dill seed
4c wild rice, cooked
salt and pepper
2T fresh herbs of your liking

Directions:
Saute mushrooms and nuts until golden, add onions and dill seed and cook a little more. Add remaining until warm and serve.
