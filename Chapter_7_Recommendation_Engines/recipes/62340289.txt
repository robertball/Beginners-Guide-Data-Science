Title:
Red Velvet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. shortening
1 1/2 c. sugar
1 oz. red food coloring (plus enough water to make 1/4 c.)
2 tbsp. cocoa (heaping)
1 c. buttermilk
2 1/4 c. cake flour
1 tsp. each vanilla, salt, and baking soda
1 tbsp. vinegar
2 eggs
Frosting:
3 tbsp. flour
1 c. white sugar
1 c. butter
1 c. milk
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Cream shortening, sugar and eggs.  Make paste of food coloring and cocoa.  Add creamed mixture.  Add buttermilk alternately with sifted flour and salt, add vanilla.  Add soda to vinegar, but hold over bowl as it foams, then add this soda and vinegar to cake mixture, blending instead of beating.
Pour into two greased and floured 9" pans.
Frosting:
Cook flour and milk until thick.  Cream sugar and butter plus vanilla until very fluffy.  Add cooled mixture.
