Title:
Creamy Romano Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, cut into 1/2-inch chunks
1 cup fat-free reduced-sodium chicken broth
4 ounces regular Cream Cheese, cubed
1/2 cup Old-World Cultured Sour Cream
3 tablespoons freshly grated Romano or any aged grating cheese
1 large egg
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook potatoes in boiling broth in large covered saucepan 15 min., stirring after 8 min. Uncover; simmer 5 to 6 min. or until potatoes are tender and most of broth is absorbed.
With an emersion hand blender or food processor, mash potatoes with the egg.  Then add cream cheese, sour cream, Romano, and garlic powder and blend until smooth.  Add additional garlic powder, salt, and pepper to taste.

Submitter:

Description: 
Loaded with flavor, these potatoes are cooked in chicken broth, then mashed with sour cream, cream cheese and Romano or other aged grated cheeses, for a creamy, tasty side dish.Modified from: Creamy Parmesan Mashed Potatoes
