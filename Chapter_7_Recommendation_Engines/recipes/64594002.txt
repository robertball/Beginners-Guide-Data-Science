Title:
Aloha Slow Cooker Sweet and Sour Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3837055.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lbs Pork cubed into 1n1/2 squares
2-3 tbsp vegetable oil/sesame oil mixed
1 beefy onion soup mix
1 bottle catalina dressing
1 jar pineapple preserves
4 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbls granulated garlic -divided
2 tsp ground ginger -divided
1 tsp black pepper or more to taste
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 cup (or scant more) cornstarch
2 green bell pepper chopped- 1 inch squares
1 red bell pepper chopped -1 inch squares
1 onion chopped -1 inch squares
OPTIONAL:
Chopped green onions and sesame seeds for garnish (recommended)

Directions:
Mix pork with thoroughly with soy sauce, black pepper, 1 tbsp garlic, 1 tsp ginger in bowl. Marinade for 30 minutes or longer.
In separate bowl combine the soup mix, dressing, chopped pineapple, preserves, remaining garlic and ginger and the red pepper flakes.
Heat 2 tbsp oil in large pan, adding more oil as needed.
Coat pork with cornstarch and brown pork in batches. Place in crockpot when done.
Add pork, bell peppers and onion to crockpot and pour sauce over and gently mix.
Cook on low for 3n1/2 hours or until tender.
Optional: add bell peppers and onion 1/2 way through cooking to keep a firmer bite.

Submitter: 
Lizette
Description: 
Sweet, tangy and tender pork. Easy to put together and delicious. Great for parties, potlucks, larger groups or just having leftovers. Great with white rice!
