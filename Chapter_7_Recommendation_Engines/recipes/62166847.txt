Title:
best cornflake cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 C brown sugar
1 C shortening
1 C cornflakes
1 tsp. baking soda
2 C. flour
1tsp vanilla
walnuts and coconut(optional) to satisfy taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Drop by tablespoons on cookie sheet. Bake at 350 until done. Wait until completely cooled to eat.

Submitter: 
heatherkuehnel
Description: 
Crispy, extra crunchy cookies with the perfect combination of sweet and salty tastes. A friend and I were curious and wanted to try something new. So, we decided to try this recipe from her mom. Very addicting and tasty.
