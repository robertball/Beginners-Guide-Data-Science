Title:
Macaroni & Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz.uncooked elbow macaroni or medium shells
1/2 stick or 1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup all purpose flour
2 cups milk
6-8 oz. sharp cheddar cheese (or any type of cheese you like) cut into small chunks
1-2 cups of shredded sharp cheddar cheese (or any cheese you like)
salt and pepper, to taset

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
Cook the macaroni accordin to the package directions. Drain well.
Meanwhile, in a large, heavy saucepan, melt the butter. Whisk in the flour to make a paste.  Gradually whisk in the milk.  Bring to a simmer over medium heat, whisking, until thickened, about 1 minute.  Add the cheese, salt a pepper, and wisk until smooth
Stir the cooked macaroni into the cheese sauce.  Scrape into a 9x13 baking dish.
Bake in the 350 degrees oven, uncovered, until bubbly and lightly golden on top, 20-25 minutes.  Add the shredded cheese for the last 5 minutes of baking. *Let stand 10 minutes before serving.*
