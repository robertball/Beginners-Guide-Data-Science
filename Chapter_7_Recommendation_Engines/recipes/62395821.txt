Title:
Creamy Lemon Meringue Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8-9 inch) baked pastry shell (cooled)  or:
1 graham cracker crumb crust
3 eggs (separated)
1 14 oz. can sweetened condensed mild (not evap. milk)
1/2 cup (real lemon) lemon juice from concentrate
Few drops yellow food coloring
1/4 tsp. cream of tarter
1/3 cup sugar

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees
In medium bowl, beat egg yolks, stir in real lemon and food coloring.
Pour into prepared pasty shell
In small bowl, beat egg whites, with cream of tarter, until soft peaks form:
gradually add sugar, beating until stiff, but not dry.
Spread meringue on top of pie, sealing carefully to edge of shell
Bake 12 0 15 minutes or until meringue is golden brown
Cool or chill before serving.  Can refrigerate leftovers.
