Title:
Easy Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/465929.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1 1/4 c dark brown sugar
1/2 cup butter, softened
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cup combination of:
semisweet chocolate chips
milk chocolate chips
Ghirardelli 60% Cacao bitter sweet chocolate
1/2 cup  chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Pour the cake mix into a large bowl. Stir in the butter, eggs dark brown sugar and vanilla with an electric mixer until well blended.  Stir in the chocolate chips and nuts. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto cookie sheets.
Bake for 11 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until the edges are golden. Cool on baking sheets for a few minutes before removing to cool on wire racks.

Description: 
These chocolate chip cookies are so soft and chewy, it is hard to believe they are made from a cake mix. Adding the brown sugar makes them taste more like traditional chocolate chip cookies but easier!Modified from: Easy Chocolate Chip Cookies
