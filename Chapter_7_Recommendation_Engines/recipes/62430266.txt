Title:
Mom's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 large onion, chopped
1 (15 ounce) can beans
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (1.25 ounce) package chili seasoning mix
1/2 cup chopped carrots

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium-high heat, cook beef and onion until meat is brown.  Stir in beans, diced tomatoes, chili seasoning, carrots and water.  Reduce heat and simmer 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Jessica Weber
Description: 
Ground beef is slowly simmered with onion, ranch-style beans, diced tomatoes and green chiles and spices.Modified from: Mom's Chili
