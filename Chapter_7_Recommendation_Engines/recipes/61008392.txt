Title:
Good Old Fashioned Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277580.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 1/4 cups milk
1 egg
4 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Spray the inside of a flower cookie cutter with non-stick cooking spray. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Slide off cutter (use the tip of a paring knife to push it off) when pancake has browned. Repeat.
