Title:
Air Fryer Buffalo Ranch Chickpeas (Garbanzo Beans) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5598724.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
2 Tablespoons buffalo wing sauce
1 Tablespoon dry Ranch dressing mix

Directions:
1. Preheat air fryer to 350 degrees.
2. Line a baking sheet with paper towels. Spread chickpeas over the paper towels. Place a layer of paper towels over the chickpeas and gently press to remove excess moisture.
3. Place chickpeas in a mixing bowl. Add wing sauce and toss to combine.
4. Add Ranch dressing powder and mix well.
5. Place chickpeas in the basket of the air fryer in an even layer.
6. Cook for 8 minutes. Shake and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Shake and cook for another 5 minutes. Shake and cook for the final 2 minutes. Let cool for for 5 minutes and serve immediately.
