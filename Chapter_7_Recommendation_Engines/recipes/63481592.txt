Title:
Mom's Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
3/4 c. powdered sugar
1 c. creamy peanut butter
6 T milk
8 oz cool whip, thawed
9 inch graham cracker crust
crushed nuts as topping, if desired

Directions:
Whip the cream cheese, then add sugar and peanut butter.
Gradually add milk.
Fold in whipped topping.
Chill overnight.
Serve with crushed nuts (peanuts, cashews, etc.) as topping if desired.
