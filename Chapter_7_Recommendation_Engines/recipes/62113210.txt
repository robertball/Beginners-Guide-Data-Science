Title:
Slow Cooker Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
2 cups polish sausage, sliced  - any kind, your choice!
1 can tomato soup
1.5 cans water
1 lb pork & beans

Directions:
Put all ingredients into crock pot and stir well.  Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.

Submitter: 
BLONDIE1020
Description: 
Being mostly polish, I was ALWAYS disappointing my grandmother for never liking polish food! This soup was my only savior.  Her sausage soup, perfect for those cool fall days.
