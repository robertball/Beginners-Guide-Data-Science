Title:
Roasted potato, fennel, radish, turnips with lemon butter and goat cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bulb fennel, chopped
1.5 bunches radishes, quartered
1.5 lbs small red potatoes, quartered
1 turnip, sliced
olive oil
salt
2 T butter
1/2 lemon, juiced
1 clove garlic

Directions:
roast all veggies with olive oil and salt 40 min or until soft
melt butter in sauce pan, add garlic, lemon juice
combine sauce and veggies
season as needed

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
roasted potatoes, fennel, radish, turnip (or other root vegetables) with lemon butter and goat cheese
