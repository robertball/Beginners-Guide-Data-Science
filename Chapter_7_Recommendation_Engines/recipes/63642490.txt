Title:
Spicy Albondigas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs
2 pounds ground beef
1 egg
1/4 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup uncooked brown rice
2 teaspoons ground cumin
4 cloves crushed garlic
1/4 teaspoon salt
4 cups chicken broth
Soup
6 cups chicken stock
1 cup diced carrots
1 cup chopped yellow onion
1 cup diced celery
2 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (7 ounce) can diced green chiles
2 tbs cilantro in tube, or 1 bunch chopped
4 cloves of crushed garlic
1 teaspoon dried oregano
2 teaspoons ground cumin

Directions:
Combine ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, rice, cumin, garlic powder, and salt in a large bowl.  Form the beef mixture into 1 inch meatballs, using about 1 1/2 tablespoons of meat for each.  Turn the heat down under the chicken broth to a simmer and drop the meatballs into the broth.  Bring chicken broth to boil and simmer the meatballs in the broth for 20 minutes, until meatballs are cooked through.  Add more broth if need to cover meatballs.
Bring 6 cups (or more) to boil, stir in carrots, onion, celery, canned diced tomatoes with green chiles, tomato sauce, canned chiles, cilantro, garlic,, oregano, and cumin.  Add meatballs and simmer at least 1 hour to cook the vegetables and blend the flavors.
