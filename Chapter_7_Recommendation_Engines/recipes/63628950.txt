Title:
Yellow Curry Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 dried chilies soaked in hot water for 15 min
3 T chopped shallots
1 T garlic
1 T coriander seeds
1 t cumin seeds
1 T lemon grass
1 t shrimp paste
1 t salt
2 t curry powder

Directions:
Fry first 5 and then grind into a powder.
Add all to blender and grind into a fine powder.
Store in a glass jar in the fridge for 4 months.
