Title:
Dianna's Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Oil
One cup of rice
2 cups of water
One sazon packet
One flat teaspoon of comino
One tablespoon of chicken bouillon
One heaping teaspoon of minced garlic
One flat teaspoon of onion powder
A few shakes of minced onion
One can of corn
One can of diced potatoes

Directions:
Add enough oil to cover the bottom of the pan
Once the oil heats up, brown the rice for a few minutes until it turns white
Add a little under two cups of water (substitute the liquid from the can of corn for more flavor)
Add one flat teaspoon of comino
Add a tablespoon of chicken bouillon - taste if you should add more
Add a heaping teaspoon of minced garlic
Add a flat teaspoon of onion powder
Add a few shakes of minced onion
Add corn and potatoes
Stir and taste.  Add salt if needed.
Cover and turn the temperature a little over medium and under high - Simmer until the water boils even with the rice
Turn it way low and cook for 5 minutes.  Turn off the heat and let sit for 20 minutes with the lid on.
Don't stir.  Shake the pan if needed.
