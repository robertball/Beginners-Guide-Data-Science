Title:
Rosebud Finishing Salt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6931855.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sea salt, such as fleur de sel , divided
1/4 cup dried Moroccan rosebuds

Directions:
Set aside one tbsp of sea salt.
Grind the remaining salt and the rosebuds in a spice grinder until fine. Put the ground mixture in a bowl. Mix in by hand the remaining 1 tbsp salt. Store in an airtight container out of direct sunlight. It will keep for up to six months, depending on the expiration dates of the initial ingredients.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Dried rosebuds are ground with sea salt to make a floral finishing salt which is a lovely addition to cooked raw or foods. It is nice on the rim of cocktail glasses.
