Title:
Curried Snow Pea Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked white rice
1 tbsp olive oil
2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into strips
2 cloves minced garlic
3/4 cup chopped onion
1 tbsp curry powder
1 1/2 cups sliced carrots
12 ounces chicken broth
8 ounces lite coconut milk
salt and pepper to taste
2 cups fresh snow pea pods

Directions:
Put rice on to cook according to package directions.
Heat olive oil in a large skillet and saute chicken until cooked through.   Remove from skillet and keep warm.
Add garlic, onion, curry powder and carrots to skillet; saute until softened.
Add broth, coconut milk, salt & pepper.   Cover, reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for about 5 minutes.
Add chicken and snow peas to the skillet, stirring to reheat chicken and warm snow peas through, about 3 minutes.
