Title:
Creamy Crock Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans slice potatoes
1 can cream of mushroom soup
3 tbsp real crumbled bacon
1 cup sour cream
3 tsp dry ranch dressing mix
1 1/2 tsp parsley

Directions:
In a medium size bowl combine the cream of mushroom soup, bacon, sour cream, ranch dressing mix, and parsley together.  Mix well.  Add potatoes.  Pour mixture into a crockpot.  Cook on high 4-5 hours.
Can be thickened by adding some cornstarch prior to serving.

Submitter: 
Johanna Van Ness
Description: 
These creamy potaotes are made in the Crock Pot with convience items.  It goes together quickly and is a great side dish with ham, roast or chicken.  For different variations you can add more or less bacon, add ham, garlic powder, use different cream soups, or use different spices to suit your tastes.
