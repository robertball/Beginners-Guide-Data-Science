Title:
Aidan Special Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of Rye
2 slices of Tomatoes
4 slices of Swiss cheese
2 or 3  slices of bacon
1 oz of Russian or thousand Island dressing.
2-3 oz of Turkey breast

Directions:
1.Heat a pan on the to stove , big enough to fit 2  pieces of Rye.
2. Butter both sides of rye. butter side down on the heat.
3. 2 slices of Swiss on each piece of bread.
4. In a separate pan heat turkey ,and tomatoes.
bacon can be pre-done heated in  the oven
5. Add Russian dressing or Thousand Island to top and bottom of melted cheese
6. Place turkey on what will be the bottom piece then bacon and top off with tomatoes close it up, if you would like you can finish it off in the oven.These can even be done as an open face sandwich. Have fun with this sandwich add an subtract things. My boys love them.

deli
Description: 
Nice hearty sandwich, on a cold night, and a cup of soup.
