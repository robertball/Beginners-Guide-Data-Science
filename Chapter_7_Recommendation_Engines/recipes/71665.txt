Title:
BUSH'S® Zesty Bean Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984040.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) can BUSH'S® Maple Cured Bacon Baked Beans, drained
2 large flavored flour tortillas (chili, tomato, cilantro, etc.)
2 cups shredded Jalapeno Jack cheese, divided
12 red bell pepper strips, divided
12 green bell pepper strips, divided
Salsa, if desired

Directions:
Drain BUSH'S Maple Cured Bacon Baked Beans, spoon into bowl and mash with a potato masher.  Spread half the bean mixture on one side of each tortilla to within 1/2-inch of edges.  Sprinkle half the cheese over the beans on each.  Place half of the red and green pepper strips along one edge of each tortilla.  Roll tortilla tightly around peppers; repeat with second tortilla.
Wrap in plastic wrap; refrigerate several hours or overnight.  When ready to serve, slice about 1-inch off each end of tortillas.  Slice remaining portion of tortilla roll into approximately 15 slices each.  Serve with salsa for dipping, if desired.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
These delightful little wraps are packed with cheese, beans, and peppers. They are great appetizers with a Southwestern flair!
