Title:
Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces sour cream
1 teaspoon mayonnaise
1 onion, diced
1 envelope Knorr Vegetabel soup mix
1 10 ounce package frozen chopped spinach, thawed

Directions:
Mix sour cream, mayo, onion and soup mix.  Squeeze excess moisture from spinach.  Mix in with other ingredients.  Serve with crackers.
