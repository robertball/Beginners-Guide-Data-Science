Title:
Mona's Fresh Blueberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3930557.jpg
Ingredients:
40 Ritz crackers, crushed
1/2 c butter, melted
1/2 c sugar
3 tbsp cornstarch
1/8 tsp salt
2 c fresh blueberries
1/4 c cold water
1 tbsp lemon juice
2 c fresh blueberries

Directions:
Crust:
Mix crushed crackers and melted butter and pat into pie pan and set aside.
Filling:
Combine sugar, cornstarch, salt, water, and 2 cups blueberries. Cook, stirring regularly until mixture boils and becomes thick and no longer cloudy. Remove from heat and add lemon juice. Cool.
When mixture is cool fold in remaining blueberries and gently pour into crust.
Refrigerate at least 6 hours before serving.
Serve with a dollop of whipping cream.
