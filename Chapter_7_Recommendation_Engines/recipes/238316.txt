Title:
Tropical Teaser Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3576767.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups vanilla soy milk
1 cup fresh pineapple chunks
2/3 cup frozen cantaloupe chunks
1/2 cup soft tofu
2 tablespoons lime juice
1 1/2 teaspoons coconut extract

Directions:
Blend soy milk, pineapple, cantaloupe, tofu, lime juice, and coconut extract in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
This non-dairy smoothie was created on a cold snowy day so that I could imagine that I was on a tropical beach somewhere. Lime juice and coconut extract give it a colada taste.
