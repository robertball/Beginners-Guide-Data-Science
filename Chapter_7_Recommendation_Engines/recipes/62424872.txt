Title:
Grilled Spicy Orange Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 shelled,deveined,uncooked X Lg shrimp
1 Tbls Evoo to coat the shrimp.
1/2 C of orange marmalade
1 to 2 chipotle chilies in adobo sauce,Minced.
1 tsp.adobo sauce
1 tsp.ancho chili powder
1/2 tsp. each garlic powder,ground cumin,salt
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Add the evoo to the shrimp and set aside. Heat the grill and use a grill pan to hold the shrimp.
Combine the rest of the ingredients in a small bowl, reserve 1/4 c. Brush shrimp with marinade and grill till the shrimp turn pink. Brush on reserved sauce. Serve with rice or mashed potatoes.
