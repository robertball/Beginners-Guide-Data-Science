Title:
Broiled Mackerel or Mackerel Preserves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/843506.jpg
Ingredients:
Mackerel - 4 lb
Water - 2 cups
Salt - 2 teaspoons
Pepper - 2 teaspoons
Sunflower oil - 1/2 cup

Directions:
The process is simple: take 4 lbs of mackerel, clean and wash the fish. Cut into mid-sized chunks.
***Tip from Helen*** You can fillet the fish or leave it as is and just chop it with bones. ***
Put the fish into the clay saucepan. season the fish and stir it. Add  2 cups of water and sunflower oil. Put the saucepan on a medium heat and broil for 2 hours. Eead more here:
