Title:
Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 to 2 C Sugar
3 eggs
1 can Eagle Brand Milk(condensed)
Pt Fruit
tsp Vanilla
pinch salt
Milk to fill 2" below top

Directions:
Cook sugar, eggs, eagle brand. Add salt, vanilla and fruit. Add to freezer, add milk to 2" below top of freezer. Put in ice cream maker with salt and ice alternating. Crank
