Title:
Green beans with bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2879900.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 cups green beans
smoked bacon
3 tps olive oil
1 tsp garlic
1 tbs brown sugar
lightly salt with sea salt
4 tbs Chicken broth or stock

Directions:
Fresh green beans, washed in cold water, then cut the ends off of both sides (can use frozen too, I've used in a pinch!)
On the stove in a skillet, add enough olive oil to cover the pan, low heat
Add garlic
Add brown sugar
Add the green beans
Add sea salt
Add smoked bacon (cut with scissors into small strips)
Turn up heat to medium, stir frequently
Add chicken broth
Cover for 10 mins.
Stir, add more liquid if needed
Cover for another 10 mins.
Stir
Leave uncovered for 10 mins on low heat or until liquid evaporates
Ready to eat!
