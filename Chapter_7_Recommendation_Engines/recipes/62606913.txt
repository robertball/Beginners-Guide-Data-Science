Title:
Green Jamba Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 2 cups Fresh Spinach
- 1/2 cup Orange juice
- 1/2 cup Apple juice
- 1 tbsp Honey
- 1/2 cup canned Pineapple
- 1/2 cup Fresh Grapes

Directions:
After rinsing off the spinach and grapes with water, combine all of the ingredients in a blender. Blend until mixture is a smooth liquid, best if served imediatley.

Submitter: 
hobo on your street
Description: 
A strange looking smoothie that most people would be shy to test out; But it tastes amazing! So don't be fooled by the color. This smoothie provides optimal health if you drink it at least once a day.
