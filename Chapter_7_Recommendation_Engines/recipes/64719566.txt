Title:
Tzatziki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small cucumbers  peeled and grated
1 ¼ c. plain greek yogurt (with fat if possible)
2 T. olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 T dill, chopped
1 lemon, juiced
white pepper and salt, to taste

Directions:
Combine yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic and pepper in a bowl.
Cover and refrigerate for one hour.
Blend yogurt mixture with a whisk until smooth.
Dry cucumbers by patting with paper towels. PAT WELL!!!
Add cucumbers and dill to yogurt mix.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Serve with gyros or as a dipping sauce.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Kedney'
Description: 
A sauce of Greek origin, made from yogurt, garlic, and cucumbers.
