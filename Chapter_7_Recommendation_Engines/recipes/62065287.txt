Title:
Fried Eggplant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium eggplant sliced in 1/4 inch thickness
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
1 cup oil

Directions:
Season the eggs with garlic salt and beat well. Heat oil in a large pan on medium high heat. Dip each eggplant slice into the egg mixture until covered in egg. Fry the eggplant slices until golden brown, about 20 to 30 seconds on each side. Remove and drain on paper towels. Serve immediately.
