Title:
Rustic Winter Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/971664.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tilapia Fillets
2 tsp of lemon zest (about 1 large lemon)
1 can of Chick Peas/Garbanzo Beans
1 cup of tomatoes, diced
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1 cup of kale, chopped small
2 tbsp of olive oil, plus 1 t
2 tbsp of lemon juice (about 1 large lemon)
2 tbsp of Italian seasoning
1 tsp of red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions:
Preheat over to 425
Mix all but first two ingredients in a medium bowl
Place kale mixture in a baking dish
Lay tilapia fillets on top, lightly brush or drizzle with olive oil, dust with Italian seasoning, and top with lemon zest
Cook fix in preheated oven until done, about 12-15 minutes

Submitter: 
MalsGTown
Description: 
This is a simple, healthy, and delicious recipe that I threw together one night with what we had in the house.  I served it with my perfectly roasted winter vegetables for an easy weekday meal.
