Title:
Super Baby Food - Pizzazzy Pizza Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Preheat oven to 400
Combine these dry ingredients in a large bowl
1 3/4 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 tablespoon Oregano
Combine these wet ingredients:
1 beaten egg
1/2 tomato sauce
1 cup yogurt
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Mix wet ingredients into dry. Pour into greased or lined muffin tins.
option: place slice tomato on top of each muffin.
Sprinkle more shredded cheese on top of tomato slice on each muffin.
Bake 20-25 minutes
