Title:
Ranch Dipped Chicken Fingers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976600.jpg
Ingredients:
Chicken:
1 1/2 pounds chicken breast
1 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Dressing
2 cups Panko breadcrumbs
Dipping Sauce:
1/4 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Dressing
1/4 cup mustard
Honey and brown sugar to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Cut chicken breasts into 2-inch pieces.  Coat chicken in Ranch.  Dredge chicken in breadcrumbs.
Place on greased baking sheet and cook for 15-20 minutes or until golden.
For dipping sauce, mix Ranch with mustard.  Add honey or brown sugar to taste.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Chicken breasts coated with a Ranch flavored breading are baked and served with a mustard-Ranch dipping sauce.
