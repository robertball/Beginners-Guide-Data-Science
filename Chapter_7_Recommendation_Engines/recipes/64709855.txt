Title:
Big Ray's Easy Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5556974.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. round steak
2 Tbsp four
2 Tbsp butter
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 cup sour cream or yogurt
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 (10 3/4 oz.) can cream of mushroom soup
3 cups egg noodles, cooked
1 Tbsp catsup
1 tsp onion salt
1 cup mushrooms, sliced

Directions:
Cut round steak into small bite-sized pieces. In a plastic bag, place flour and steak to coat. In a large skillet, brown in butter on medium high for 5-10 minutes (until cooked through). Add the rest of the ingredients and blend well. Cook only until heated through. Serve over  egg noodles.
