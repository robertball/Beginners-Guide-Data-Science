Title:
Chocolate Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c. Flour
1/2 c. Cocoa
2 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1 tsp. Salt
1 1/2 Sticks Margarine
2 c. Sugar
3 tsp. Cinnamon
1 tsp. Nutmeg
3 Eggs
1/2 c. Milk
2 tsp. Vanilla
2 c. Zucchini

Directions:
Cream margarine, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and eggs. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix with a mixer. Grease and flour 2 bread pans. Bake in 350 degree oven 40-45 minutes for 2 loaf pans or 30 minutes for a 9x13 inch pan.
