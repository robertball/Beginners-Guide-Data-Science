Title:
Seven-Layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 cans of refried beans (~36 oz)
5 avocados
8 oz. Sour cream
1 T Taco Seasoning
3 roma tomatoes, chopped
2-3 green onions, chopped
1 small can chopped olives
Cheese, shredded

Directions:
Spread beans evenly in bottom of 9x13 BD.
Smash avocadoas to make guacamole and spread over beans.
Mix taco seasoning with sour cream and spread over guacamole
Spread tomatoes and onions over sour cream mixture
Spread olives over tomatoes and onions.
Top with desired amount of cheese. Refrigerate of eat right away!

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Mix and match the layers at will. Some like them in a different order, some like to substitute for other ingredients. You can pretty much do whatever you want with this. (Just make sure there's always enough cheese!) :)
