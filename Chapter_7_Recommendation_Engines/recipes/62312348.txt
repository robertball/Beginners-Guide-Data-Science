Title:
French Toast Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Slices plain bread
2 Eggs
3 TBS Milk
3 TBS Sugar
1/2 tsp Vanilla Essence
1 Cup Oil for frying
1 Cup Milk
2 TBS Custard Powder
1/2 Cup Heavy Cream
8 Strawberries Halved
1/2 Cup Icing Sugar

Directions:
Cut the bread slices into four parts
Beat together eggs, 3 TBS milk, sugar and vanilla essence.
Dip the slices into the egg mixture and fry over medium heat in 1/4 inch of oil till golden brown.
Boil 1 cup of milk in a saucepan. Dissolve custard in some milk and add to the boiling milk. Reduce heat and cook till the custard thickens. Set aside and cool.
Line 8 pieces of french toasts in a shallow tray. Spread custard over each piece and place the other 8 pieces to form a sandwich.
Lightly dust the sandwiches with icing sugar.
Put a dollop of heavy cream over each sandwish and top with strawberry. Insert a toothpick through the fruit to keep the sandwich in place.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Celestial
Description: 
A twist to the traditional french toasts with a little vanilla custard, icing sugar and heavy cream topped with fruit. Excellent for tea parties.
