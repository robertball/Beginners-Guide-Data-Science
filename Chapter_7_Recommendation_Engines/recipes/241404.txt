Title:
Antioxidant King Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2114378.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 fresh pineapple, peeled
1 Golden Delicious apple, quartered
1 small beet, top and bottom trimmed
1/4 cup fresh spinach, or to taste
1/4 cup mixed berries, or to taste
1/2 banana
1/2 avocado, peeled
3 ice cubes

Directions:
Process pineapple, apple, beet, and spinach through a juicer.
Blend juice, berries, banana, avocado, and ice cubes in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Kenneth Smith
Description: 
This is my favorite smoothie with many health benefits. It looks great and tastes the same. The avocado gives it a real creamy texture.
