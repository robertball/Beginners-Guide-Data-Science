Title:
Sausage Gravy II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3699714.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground pork sausage
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 teaspoon garlic powder to taste
1 teaspoon dried parsley
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 cup milk as needed

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium high heat, combine the sausage and onion and saute for 5 to 10 minutes, or until sausage is browned.
Season with garlic powder, parsley and salt and pepper to taste. Add enough flour to just make the meat somewhat sticky. Then stir in milk until mixture is to desired consistency. Allow to heat through and serve.

Submitter: 
Brandi
Description: 
Here's a simple recipe for sausage gravy to serve over hot biscuits. Season with your favorite herbs and spices to taste.
