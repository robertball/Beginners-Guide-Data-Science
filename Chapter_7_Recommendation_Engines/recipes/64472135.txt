Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2477782.jpg
Ingredients:
13 oz marshmallow fluff or marshmallow creme
16 oz plain cream cheese
8 oz strawberry cream cheese
2 Tbsp cocoa powder
2 boxes vanilla wafer cookies

Directions:
Whisk 8 oz plain cream cheese with 1 cup marshmallow fluff (1/3 of the container).
Whisk 8 oz plain cream cheese with 1 cup marshmallow fluff (1/3 of the container) and 2 Tbsp cocoa powder.
Whisk 8 oz strawberry cream cheese with 1 cup marshmallow fluff (1/3 of the container).
Pipe each flavor of the marshmallow cream cheese mixture on a vanilla wafer.
Top with a second vanilla wafer.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
Dinomite loves these fluffy bites for dessert after a filling meal of kale!
