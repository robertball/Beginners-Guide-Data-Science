Title:
WW Broccoli Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (14 1/2 ounce) cans chicken broth
2 (1 lb) bags frozen chopped broccoli (can sub one bag for 1 (1 lb) bag frozen cauliflower
1 (10 1/2 ounce) cans rotel brand tomatoes and green chilies
10 ounces Velveeta reduced fat cheese product

Directions:
Mix chicken broth, frozen broccoli and tomatoes and chilies together.
Simmer for 25 minutes or until veggies are tender.
Cube Velveeta and put into soup pot.
Simmer just until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Daisy K
Description: 
A wonderful version of broccoli cheese soup flavored with a bit of heat!
