Title:
Picante Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large white onion
2 cloves garlic, chopped
4 large chicken breast, cubed
1/4 cup dry white wine
2 cups picante sauce
1 tablespoon sage
2 tablespoons cumin
1 bay leaf
1 can pinto beans, undrained
1 can black beans, undrained
6 roma tomatos, chopped
1 can green chilies
1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 bunch cliantro

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic in pot.  Add Chicken. Cook until not pink anymore.  Add wine, picante sauce,sage, cumin, bay leaf and beans.  Simmer for 10 minutes.  Add tomatoes and chilies and simmer for 10 minutes.  Top with cheese, sour cream and cilantro.
NOTE: ALl of these ingredients can be modified to your taste. Everytime I make it I add different amounts depending on what I have on hand and it is ALWAYS great!
