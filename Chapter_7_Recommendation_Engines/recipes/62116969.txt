Title:
Easy Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c Oil
5 c Flour
1 c Sugar
1 t Baking Soda
4 eggs
4 c Buttermilk
20 oz Rasin Bran Cereal

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, carefull to not over mix.
Refrigerate over night.
Preheat oven to 350
Bake for 20 min in muffin tins
