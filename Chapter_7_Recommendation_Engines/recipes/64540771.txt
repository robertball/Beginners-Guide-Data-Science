Title:
Pretty In Pink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cherries
2 bananas
2 cups of sugar
Vanilla Extract
Cherry Juice
Milk
Ice

Directions:
First, put in your cherries, cut the bananas up into fours. Then, put in your Ice, Milk, Cherry Juice and vanilla extract. Finally, blend them all together.

Edward Thomas Spivey

Your smoothie should be pinkish
