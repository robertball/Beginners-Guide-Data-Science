Title:
BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Tomato Paste
1/2 Cup Stone Mustard
1/4 Cup Cider vinegar
1 TBSP Chili Powder
1 TBSP Paprika
1 TBSP Garlic Powder
1/2 TBSP Salt
1/2 TBSP Black Pepper
1/4 Cup Chopped Dates

Directions:
Whisk the ingredients together in a small sauce pot and simmer on low for 20 Minutes. Puree in the blender to combine.

Submitter: 
elizabethanderson1
Description: 
A simple, straightforward recipe that you can modify and personalize as you see fit. Sub out different vinegars, add more spices and herbs, or heat with some cayenne. Use it as a pre-grill slather, and afterward to coat the grilled meat.
