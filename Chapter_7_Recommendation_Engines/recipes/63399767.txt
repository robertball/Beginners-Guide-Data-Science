Title:
Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz. can pumpkin puree
2 tsp. baking soda
4 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. cinnamon
2 tsp. vanilla
4 cups flour
1 tsp. salt
2 cups sugar
2 eggs, lightly beaten
2 tsp. milk
1 cup vegetable oil
10 oz. package of chocolate chips
1 cup of nuts - optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Mix pumpkin with sugar then add eggs.
Add remaining ingredients except chocolate chips, with flour being the last ingredient.
Mix well then fold in chips.
Drop by teaspoonfuls on cookie sheet.
Bake for about 14 minutes or until done.
