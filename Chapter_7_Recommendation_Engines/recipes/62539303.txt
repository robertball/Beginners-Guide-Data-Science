Title:
Mr. Picky's Favorite Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/522761.jpg
Ingredients:
2 heads fresh broccoli
1 red onion
1/2 pound bacon
3/4 cup golden raisins
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sugar
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar

Directions:
Place bacon in a deep skillet and cook over medium high heat until evenly brown, cool and crumble.
Cut broccoli into very small pieces and cut onion into thin bite-size slices. Combine the broccoli, raisins, onion and toss.
To prepare the dressing, mix mayonnaise, sugar, and vinegar together, whisk until smooth. Stir into the salad, let chill, top with bacon and serve.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
This salad has bite, crunch and sweetness, combining raisins, crunchy broccoli with crisp bacon in a sweet and sour mayonnaise-based dressing.Modified from: Broccoli Crunch Salad
