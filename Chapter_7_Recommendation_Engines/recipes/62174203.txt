Title:
Pumpkin Pie Spice I With Cloves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons ground cinnamon
4 teaspoons ground nutmeg
4 teaspoons ground ginger
3 teaspoons ground allspice
2 teaspoons ground cloves

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well. Store in air tight container.

Submitter: 
btrhoads
Description: 
Use this mixture in recipes that call for pumpkin pie spice. A blend of cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, allspice and cloves that can be scaled to any size.Modified from: Pumpkin Pie Spice I
