Title:
Orange Dream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) box of orange Jello
2 (3 ounce) boxes of tapioca pudding
3 cups boiling water
1 (8 ounce) container of cool whip
2 (15 ounce) mandarin oranges, drained

Directions:
Mix the orange Jello and tapioca pudding powders together in a medium size bowl.
Add 3 cups boiling water and mix until dissolved.
Refrigerate, stirring every 15-20 minutes (to keep it from settling and lumping up) for about an hour until it's creamy.
Then gently fold in cool whip and mandarin oranges.
Enjoy!!! Must be kept refrigerated!!!

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a orange flavored,light and figure friendly recipe.  It tastes sinful but it's not! You can make this as a fat free dessert or even a sugarfree dessert for those diabetic friends!! A friend of the family made this years ago for a gathering and no one could get enough of it. It's sinfully delicious!!!!!!
