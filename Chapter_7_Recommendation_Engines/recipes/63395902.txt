Title:
Chicken Enchilada Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 onion, chopped
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 (32 ounce) carton of fat free, reduced sodium chicken broth
2 cups shredded, cooked chicken
2 (15 ounce) cans of black beans, drained
4 corn tortillas, torn into small pieces
1 can red enchilada sauce
1 cup red salsa
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1-1/2 tablespoons taco seasoning (or about 1/2 a package)
1 cup shredded mexican style cheese blend

Directions:
In a large pot, heat oil and saut√© the onion for about 5 minutes.  Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute.  Pour in the chicken stock and add the shredded chicken.  Add in the remaining ingredients and simmer for about 20 minutes or longer.  Serve in individual bowls topped with your favorite toppings, such as cheese, tortilla strips, chopped avocado, chopped tomatoes, or pico de gallo.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This chicken enchilada soup is delicious, warm and comforting on a cold night but so quick and easy to make using pantry ingredients.  It tastes great with cornbread or crusty bread.  Top with tortilla strips, cheese, avocado or your favorite topping.
