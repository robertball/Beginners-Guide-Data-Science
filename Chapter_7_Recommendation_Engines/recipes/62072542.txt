Title:
Old Fashioned Jam Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 cups sugar
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup buttermilk
4 cups all purpose flour
2 cups strawberry jam
5 eggs
1 teaspoon soda
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1 tablespoon cinnamon
2 cups chopped pecans
Filling:
1 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups cream or half and half
1 1/2 cups raisins
3 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups chopped pecans

Directions:
Cake:
Mix sugar, butter, jam, eggs, nutmeg, cinnamon, and chopped pecans.  Mix soda with buttermilk and then add to the other mixure.  Add flour a little at a time, mixing well.  Pour into 3 greased and floured 8 or 9 inch cake pans.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes.  Remove from pans and let cool.
Filling:
Cream butter and sugar; add cream, nuts, and raisins.  Cook until soft ball stage.  Spread between layers over top of cooled cake; let filling drizzle down sides.

Submitter: 
UCMOM
Description: 
This was my husband's great-grandmother's recipe.  We enjoy eating it at Christmas.  Make sure the humidity is low when making the filling.  This makes alot of filling.  If I have extra filling, I drop it on wax paper and we eat it like divinity.
