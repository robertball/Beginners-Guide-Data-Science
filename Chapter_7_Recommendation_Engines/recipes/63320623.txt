Title:
Salt Substitute Recipe

Ingredients:
1 tsp chili powder
1 T garlic powder
6 T onion powder
1 tsp oregano
3 T paprika
2 T pepper
1 T poultry seasoning
2 T dry mustard

Directions:
Combine and mix well.  Any mixture not placed in a salt shaker should be stored in a clean, dry, covered container.
