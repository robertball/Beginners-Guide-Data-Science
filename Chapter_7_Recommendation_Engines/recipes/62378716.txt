Title:
Perfect Corn on the Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ears corn, shucked
4 paper towels
water

Directions:
Wet all 4 paper towels and squeeze to get out most of the water.  Wrap each ear of corn with 2 of the towels, place in microwave.  Cover with microwave safe dish.
Microwave on HI for 8 minutes.  Enjoy!
