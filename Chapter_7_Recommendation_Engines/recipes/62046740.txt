Title:
Southern Pecan Pralines Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups brown sugar
1 cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups pecan halves
1 tablespoon butter, do not use margarine

Directions:
Combine brown sugar, soda, and buttermilk in a large heavy saucepan and stir well to disslove sugar.  Cook over medium-low heat stirring constantly.  Cook to soft ball stage- 230 degrees.  Remove from heat and add remaining ingredients.  Beat mixture briskly until it becomes glossy and starts to thicken.  Quickly spoon onto wax paper, making small patties.  Let cool completely.  (If mixture gets too hard before you've had time to get it on wax paper, return it to the heat for a few seconds.)

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
This recipe comes from the south, which is well known for its pralines.  This recipe is a bit time consuming, but it is worth every minute of your time.
