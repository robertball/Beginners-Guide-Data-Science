Title:
Up Side Down Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 envelope spaghetti sauce mix
1 16 ounce can tomato suace
1 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/2 cup sour cream
1 8 ounce can crescent rolls, unseperated
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
any other desired pizza toppings, optional

Directions:
Brown beef and onions.  Drain off fat, Add spaghetti sauce mix and tomato sauce.  Mix well.  Spread meat mixture in 9 by 13 inch pan that is sprayed with cooking spray.  Spread sour cream and cheese over meat mixture.  Add any other desired pizza toppings.  Unroll crescent rools and do not seperate them.  Place crescent rolls over meat and cheese mixture.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
Pizza toppings covered with crescent rolls are sure to be a hit with your family.  This is quick and easy.  You can prepare it the night before and come home from work, pop it in the oven, and have dinner in 30 minutes.  Serve it with a salad for a complete meal.
