Title:
Apple-Cinnamon Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup water
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 teaspoon cornstarch
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3 medium apples, peeled, thinly sliced
DLB;
Everything except the apples
add a little more cinnamon

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, bring brown sugar, water, butter and cornstarch and cinnamon to a boil; boil for 2 minutes until thick. Reduce heat to medium. Add apples; cook for 10-12 minutes or until apples are tender.

Submitter: 
cookless cook
Description: 
Around our house, Saturday breakfasts are a time to relax. So I like to serve my family's favorite French toast topped with this homemade syrup. After such a satisfying meal, everyone slowly eases into their hectic day ahead.Modified from: Apple-Cinnamon Syrup
