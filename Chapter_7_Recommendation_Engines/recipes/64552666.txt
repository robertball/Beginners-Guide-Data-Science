Title:
Salmon Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
3 tbsp. lemon juice
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 tbsp. butter
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 220g cans salmon
1 cup milk
2 cups crushed cheese crackers (like Ritz)
dill, to taste
cayenne, to taste
Worcestershire sauce, to taste

Directions:
Beat eggs.
Add lemon juice and beat again.
Add onion, butter, salt and pepper. Mix well.
Remove skin and bones from salmon. Mash with juice. Add to egg mixture.
Add milk and crumbs.
Add spices, to taste. Mix well.
Pack into 1.5L greased loaf pan or casserole dish.
Bake @ 350 x 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A moist tender way to serve salmon as a main course. You can spice it up as much as you like!
