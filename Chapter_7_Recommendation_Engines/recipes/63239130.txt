Title:
Cauliflower Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium cauliflower, chopped
4 cups chicken stock
1/3 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon basil
1 teaspoon garlic powder
4 cups milk
2 cups grated medium Cheddar cheese
buttered bread crumbs for garnish

Directions:
Cook cauliflower in chicken stock until tender. Do not drain. Mash with potato masher to desired texture. Set aside.
Melt butter in saucepan. Add onion and saute until limp. Do not brown.
Mix in flour, salt, basil, and pepper. Add milk. Heat and stir until it boils and thickens.
Add cheese and cauliflower mixture. Stir to heat and melt cheese. Garnish with buttered bread crumbs.
