Title:
Ultimate Breakfast Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/799433.jpg
Ingredients:
16 eggs
1 c sour cream
2 lbs breakfast sausage, cooked and crumbled
1 lb bacon, cooked and chopped
3 red bell peppers, diced and sauteed
1 small onion, diced and sauteed
4 medium sized red potatoes, diced and steamed with your choice of seasonings
4 c shredded cheese of your choice.

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
grease a 9x14 inch pan
1. Whisk the eggs together with the sour cream until fully mixed
If you haven't done so already, steam your potatoes with seasonings, and saute your onions and peppers with some salt and pepper
Place potatoes in the bottom of the pan and pour eggs over the top
Add the bell peppers, onion, and bacon
Bake about 10 minutes or until eggs are softly set
Add the sausage over the top and cover with cheese
Let bake another 30 minutes or until center is set
Serve and watch the devouring begin...

Description: 
I decided I wanted to kick up a baked omelet a few notches... And after making this, I knew I had a winner.  You can add or subtract as you'd like, and this is a great idea for pot-lucks or brunches.
