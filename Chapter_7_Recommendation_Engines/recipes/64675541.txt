Title:
Hot Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bag of split chicken wings
10 Tablespoons Red Hot Sauce
1/4 cup butter
1/4 teaspoon Tabasco
1/2 teaspoon Worchestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon cayenne
1/4 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Melt Butter and add all other ingredients except wings
Place in a large container with lid and put aside
Bake wings at 425, 12-15 minutes
Put wings into container and shake until coated
Bake for 10 more minutes and serve
