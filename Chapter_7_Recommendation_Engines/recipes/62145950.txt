Title:
Creamy Beef Mushroom Barley Soup~ Slow Cooker~ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups of homemade beef broth (you can substitute chicken or turkey broth and store bought, but remember homemade has the best flavor)
16 oz of ground beef
16 oz of barley
1 cup of dried shitake mushrooms (you can substitute fresh use double the amount)
1 medium sweet onion diced
4 cloves of garlic diced
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp celery seed
1 tsp dried parsley
1 cup heavy cream (this can be optional)
1 cup of frozen green beans (optional)

Directions:
In a large 6 quart crock pot add beef broth, garlic, salt, pepper, celery seed, parsley,  and barley.
In a blender or food processor chop mushrooms until you have small 1-1/2 inch pieces.
Add mushrooms to crock pot.
Brown ground beef and onions over medium heat in a large skillet. When browned add to crock pot.
Stir to mix thoroughly, cover with lid and cook on low for 5 hours.
Add heavy cream and green beans; allow to cook for 1 more hour.
Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Namaste Mama
Description: 
A wintertime comfort food at its best. I cook for 8 people with big appetites so half this recipe for a smaller group.  If you like green bean casserole try adding green beans and some friend onions on top for a tasty twist.
