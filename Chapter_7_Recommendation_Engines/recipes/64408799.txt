Title:
Creamy Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz tub Philadelphia Cream Cheese
2 Tbsp. milk
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1-1/2 lb. beef sirloin steak, cut into strips
1-2 chopped onions
1 Tbsp. Butter
1/3 cup beef broth
3 cups cooked noodles

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, milk, and garlic powder until blended
Cook meat and onions in large skillet on medium heat 5-6 min. or until meat is done.  Remove from skillet; drain.  Cover to keep warm.
add cream cheese mixture; cook and stir 2 min Return meat mixture to skillet; stir in broth.  cook 5 min.  Remove from heat; let stand 5 min.  Serve over noodles.  Add Sour Cream as necessary.
