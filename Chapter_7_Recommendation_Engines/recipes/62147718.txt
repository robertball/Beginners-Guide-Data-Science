Title:
Coconut Cardamom Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 13.5 oz. can of coconut milk
1/2 cup sugar
8-10 cardamom pods
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 tablespoon rum

Directions:
Halve the cardamom pods
Simmer the coconut milk, sugar, cardamom, vanilla extract, and 1/2 T rum on low heat for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
After taking the mixture off the stove, stir in the remaining rum.
Strain out the cardamom
Transfer to a freezer-friendly container and freeze.

Submitter: 
Monica
Description: 
The best part about this incredibly simple, vegan-friendly recipe is that you don't need an ice cream maker to make it!  It comes out soft and creamy and ready to eat.
