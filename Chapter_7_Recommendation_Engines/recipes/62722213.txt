Title:
The Maggot Mamba Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) bag of frozen raspberries
1 (6 ounce) frozen raspberry lemonade
12 ounces of water
6 ounces 7up or Sprite
tequila
beer
Cointreau (or other orange liquor)

Directions:
Mix first three items in blender. Stir in 7up or sprite. Add tequila, beer and orange liquor to taste.

Submitter: 
4GiddyGirls
Description: 
Fun Halloween drink and sooo yummy and creepy at the same time.  It's a raspberry margarita but the raspberry seeds drop to the bottom and look like little maggots.
