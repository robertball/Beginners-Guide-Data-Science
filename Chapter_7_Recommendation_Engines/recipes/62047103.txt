Title:
Creamy Vadalia Onin Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 lrg Vadalia onions, thinly sliced
4 Tbsp.butter
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 c.heavy cream
4 Tbsp.flour
1 c.milk
(Healthy verion is omitting cream and using 1 1/2 cups milk)
salt for your own liking

Directions:
cook onions and butter together on med-high heat until soft and slightly caramelized.Add flour and blend through.Slowly add chicken broth.Take off of heat and let cool.Add milk and cream and then liquify it all in a blender. Reheat. You can top one onion to use as a garnish if you like for the top, or parsley.

Submitter: 
A_RAVEN_FAN
Description: 
If you love vadalia onions, this is the soup for you. It is very rich in flavor and because of it being liquified in a blender it gives it a creamy texture.
