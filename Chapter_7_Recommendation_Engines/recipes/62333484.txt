Title:
Garden Fresh Pico de Gallo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/386550.jpg
Ingredients:
5 medium tomatos, diced
1 red onion, finely diced
1 jalape√±o pepper, finely diced with seeds
3 large cloves of garlic, minced or pressed
1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
1 teaspoon coarse salt, kosher salt or sea salt works well
2 whole limes

Directions:
Dice tomatos , red onion, and jalapeno and add to medium  sized bowl.
Add minced garlic, cilantro and salt.
Add zest of one lime. Then add the juice of both limes.
Throughly mix all ingredients, then refrigerate for 30 minutes to allow flavors to mingle.
Enjoy with chips or your favorite Mexican dish.
