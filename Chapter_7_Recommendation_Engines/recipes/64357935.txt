Title:
Mom's Custard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp cornstarch
6 Tbsp sugar
1/4 tsp salt
3 cups scalded milk
2 egg yolks
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In a double boiler, combine cornstarch, sugar and salt; gradually add scalded milk.  Cook over hot water, stirring constantly until thickened.
In a large bowl, beat egg yolks, gradually blend in hot milk mixture.
Return to top of double boiler; cook 3 minutes.
Cool.  Add vanilla extract.  Serve over Apple Pan Dowdy.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Great poured over desserts or fruit.
