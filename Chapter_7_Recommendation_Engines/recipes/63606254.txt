Title:
Fast and Easy Israeli Tahini Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cup White Flour
1 cup Tahini
1 cup White Sugar (Honey or Brown Sugar can be used)
200 grams of Unsalted Butter
10 grams of Baking Powder
(Optional: Nuts, Dried Fruit or a touch of Vanilla are all great to add)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 180 C.
In a sauce pan or microwave melt butter
In a large mixing bowl add flour, sugar and baking powder
Next add melted butter and tahini
Mix until all ingredients are well folded in and the dough is crumbly
Prep a baking sheet with wax paper
With clean hands roll the dough into small balls and then press flat into cookie shape with your palms or  lay it on the cookie sheet and press with fork.
Put sheet in the middle rack of the oven and let cookie for 10 minutes or until golden
Let cool and serve

Submitter: 
Nuriti
Description: 
A quick and simple Middle Easter/Mediterranean hit. I've seen both little Jewish grandmothers in Jerusalem make these cookies and Bedouin women living in the middle of the desert. A great simple recipe that isn't too sweet, is very versatile and fast. Enjoy
