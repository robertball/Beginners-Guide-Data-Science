Title:
Moroccan Chickpea Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/447257.jpg
Ingredients:
2 onions, minced
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 teaspoons cumin seeds
2 teaspoons coriander seeds (lightly crushed)
4 garlic cloves, chopped
2 eggplant, cut into chunks
1 red chili pepper
1 teaspoon saffron strands
1 teaspoon salt
4 cups canned chickpeas
1/2 cup of basmati rice
1/2 cup of large green olives
1 thin-skinned lemon, sliced
2 1/2 cups vegetable broth
fresh coriander, chopped

Directions:
Saute onions in large skillet for 5 minutes
Stir in cumin and coriander seeds, the eggplant, chili pepper, saffron, and salt
Leave cook for a couple minutes while you drain and rinse chickpeas
Add chickpeas to the skillet along with rice, olives, and lemon slices
Pour broth into mixture
Bring to boil, cover, and cook gently for 20 minutes
Fork chopped coriander through mixture and serve
