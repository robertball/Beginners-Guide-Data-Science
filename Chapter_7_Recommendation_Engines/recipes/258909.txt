Title:
Quick Vegan Warm Sweet Cinnamon Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4555864.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon stevia powder
1 cup unsweetened vanilla-flavored almond milk
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 dash vanilla extract

Directions:
Put stevia in a microwave-safe mug; pour in almond milk. Cook in a microwave oven until hot, about 1 minute and 20 seconds. Sprinkle cinnamon on top, add vanilla extract, and stir to combine.

Submitter: 
tleks
Description: 
Delicious vegan drink, inspired by the taste of horchata. Adjust with more sweetener, if desired.
