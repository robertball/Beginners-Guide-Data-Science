Title:
Peanut butter balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C butter(softened)
1lb Powdered Sugar
2C peanut butter
1C rice krispies
8oz bar milk chocolate(melted)
1/2 cake paraffin wax
6oz semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Blend butter, peanut butter and powdered sugar together until well combined. Add rice krispies. Roll into balls and chill. Melt chocolate bar, chips and wax in a double boiler. Dip balls in melted chocolate and then put on parchment paper and let dry.
