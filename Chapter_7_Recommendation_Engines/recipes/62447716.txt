Title:
Crawfish Etouffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 bell pepper, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 (10 oz.) can cream of celery soup
1 (10 oz.) can cream of shrimp soup
3 Tablespoons paprika (for color)
1 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/4 teaspoon red or cayenne pepper (optional)
1 lb. cleaned crawfish tails with fat
1/4 c. parsley, chopped
1/4 c. green onion tops, chopped

Directions:
In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
Add onion, bell pepper, and celery and saute until translucent.
Add soups, paprika, seasoned salt, and pepper (if desired).  Stir until well mixed and simmer for 10 minutes.
Add crawfish tails with fat (the fat is very important to the flavor of the dish).  Stir well.
Add parsley and green onions; stir and simmer for 15 minutes more.
Serve over rice.
