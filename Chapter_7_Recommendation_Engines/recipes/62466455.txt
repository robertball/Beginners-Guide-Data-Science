Title:
The Chris Pumpkin Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1 sleeve  graham crackers, crushed
1/2 cup pecans
6 T melted butter
Filling
3  8 oz pkgs cream cheese
3 eggs, whisked
1 T lemon juice
1/2 t. nutmeg
1 t. cinnamon
1/4 t. all spice
1 cup pumpkin
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 t. vanilla
Topping
1/2 pint sour cream
2T sugar
1T vanilla

Directions:
Process nuts and crushed crackers  in  food processor. Stir in melted butter.  Press into  bottom of springform pan.
Beat cream cheese and sugar. Add eggs, spices  and pumpkin and mix until combined,  pour into springform pan.  Bake @350 for 30 to 40 minutes till firm,  rotate after 15 minutes.  Cool 20 minutes.
Whisk  sour cream, sugar, vanilla.  Top cheese cake.  Bake 5 minutes more.
