Title:
Saucy Baked Chicken over Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 or 6 chicken breasts
1/2 cup white cooking wine
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 small can of sliced mushrooms
1/2 container of garden vegetable cream cheese
3/4 cup milk
1/2 package dry italian seasoning

Directions:
For sauce:  In medium saucepan mix soups, cream cheese, wine & milk with seasoning until hot, but not boiling.  Add mushrooms.  Pour some sauce to cover the bottom of a 9 x 13 pan, place chicken breasts in pan, then pour remaining sauce on top.  Bake 350 degrees for about 40 minutes.
Serve over linguine.
