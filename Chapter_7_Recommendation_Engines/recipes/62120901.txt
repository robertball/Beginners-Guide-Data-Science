Title:
Simple and Tasty Chicken Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken breast, cut in pieces
3 cups of rice
1 bag of mixed vegetables
1 small can of sliced mushrooms
2 tsp. brown sugar
1/3 cup soy sauce
3 tsp. worceshtire sauce
1 tsp. garlic powder
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
3 tsp. hot sauce
3 tsp. balsamic vinegar

Directions:
In a bowl combine the brown sugar, worceshtire sauce, soy sauce, garlic powder, vinegar, and hot sauce.
Place oil in a pot, heat and place chicken in oil
Fry chicken until golden brown
Add 3/4 of the sauce on top of chicken on high heat until sauce thickens.
After chicken is browned and cooked, remove from pot.
In the same pot, put rice and fry for 5 minutes and add remainder of sauce on top of rice.
Add 2 cups of water on rice, cook until soft.
Before rice cooks completely, add vegetables and cover for 15 min.
Add chicken to rice and cook for 5 minutes, then serve.
