Title:
Hybrid Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
8 oz white mushrooms, sliced
1 tsp vegetable oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
8 oz red wine
8 oz beef stock or broth
1 cup sour cream
2 Tbsp corn starch
1-2 tsp salt
1 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Over medium-high heat, brown ground beef with the onion and mushrooms. Drain well and set aside.
Add oil and garlic to pan, and sautee garlic until golden brown.
Turn heat to high and add red wine and beef stock, scraping browned bits from bottom of pan.  Bring to a boil and simmer mixture for approximately 5 minutes.
Whisk cornstarch into sour cream, and mix into wine/broth mixture.
Stir in browned beef, and bring to a simmer, stirring frequently.
Add salt and pepper, serve over egg noodles.

Submitter: 
Mark Adams
Description: 
I created this as an amalgamation of about a half-dozen stroganoff recipes.  Really hits the spot on a cold day, served over egg noodles with peas and carrots.
