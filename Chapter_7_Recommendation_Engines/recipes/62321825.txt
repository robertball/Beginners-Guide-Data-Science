Title:
BBQ Chicken Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 to 7 pounds broiler/fryer chicken(s), cooked and shredded
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/2 cups water
2 1/2 cups ketchup
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. celery seed
1 tsp. chili powder
1/2 tsp. hot pepper sauce
1/2 tsp. ground mustard
1 Tbsp. molasses
2 tsp. garlic powder
Hamburger buns

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except buns, in a 3 qt. slow cooker or Dutch oven.
Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or simmer for 1 1/2 hours on the stovetop.
Serve on buns

Tangy chicken sandwiches, great for potlucks!
