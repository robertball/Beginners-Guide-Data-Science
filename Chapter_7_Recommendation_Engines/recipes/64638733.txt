Title:
Potato Salmon Cakes  with Homemade Tartar Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. leftover mashed potatoes
1 7.5 oz tin of salmon, drained
2 tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
2 tbsp. chopped scallions or chives
1 egg, beaten
1 cup Panko bread crumbs
1/2 tbsp olive oil
Tartar Sauce
1/4 c light mayo
1/4 c. non fat greek yogurt
1 tsp. tangy dill relish
1/4 tsp.  Old Bay Seasoning
1 tsp. lemon juice
1tsp. pickle juice
Combine all ingredients in a small bow. Refrigerate til use.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 deg. Line a cookie sheet with foil and spray lightly with cooking spray.
in a medium mixing bowl, combine mashed potatoes, drained salmon, parsley and scallions or chives . Set aside.
In a small bow, beat one egg, set aside.
On a dinner plate combine Panko bread crumbs with olive oil and mix with fingers until bread crumbs are moistened.
Using an ice cream scoop, scoop out some the potato/salmon mixture and lightly pat (do not pack it) into a patty, dip into beaten egg, then lightly pat into Panko til all coated, place on prepared cookie sheet.
Bake in pre-heated oven for 25-30 mins.
If cakes haven't browned enough, put under the broiler for a couple of mins and watch them carefully.

Submitter: 
debbie eckstein
Description: 
Canned salmon and  left over mashed potatoes made into little cakes, baked in the oven makes for a great light meal, served with Tarter Sauce and  a nice green crisp salad.
