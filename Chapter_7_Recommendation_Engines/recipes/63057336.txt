Title:
Cream of Spinach Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups water
3 cubes  chicken bouillon
1 (10 ounce) package fresh spinach
3 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
3 cups milk
1 tablespoon onion, chopped
1 tablespoon thyme
1/2 teaspoon garlic, chopped
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine water, bouillon, and spinach. Bring to a boil, and cook until spinach is tender.
Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour, and cook for 2 minutes. Gradually whisk in milk. Season with onion, garlic, thyme, cayenne, salt, and pepper. Cook, stirring constantly, until thickened. Stir in spinach mixture.
