Title:
Pan-Seared Chicken with Mushroom Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1083352.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbls Olive Oil
3 chicken breasts
onion powder
salt
pepper
1 Package fresh sliced white mushrooms
2 cloves garlic sliced
1/4 cup white wine
1/4 cup water
1 envelope of chicken gravy powder

Directions:
Sprinkle both sides of the chicken breasts with salt, pepper and onion powder.
Heat oil over medium high heat.  Add chicken cook until golden brown- about 10 minutes.
Add garlic and mushrooms- cook 5 minutes.
Add wine and water- scrape pan and bring to a rolling boil.
Add gravy packet to the pan and mix well.
Cover and reduce heat to medium low for 10 minutes.
