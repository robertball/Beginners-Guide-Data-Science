Title:
Perfect Mixed Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bunches collard greens
2 bunches turnip greens
2 bunches mustard greens
1/2 cup oil
3 Tbs Salt
Salt and Pepper to taste
4 clove sliced garlic
1/2 chopped cooked bacon
1/2 cup sugar
4 cups chicken broth

Directions:
Cut and discard stems.  Wash leaves, rubbing and squeezing gently in warm water.  Fill sink with warm water, 3 Tbs salt and 2 cups of the chicken broth and soak for 10min. Place greens in collander to drain excess liquid, do not rinse or dry.  Place greens in large pot.  Pour remaining broth over greens.  Place remaining ingredients in pot and gently mix.  Bring to boil, cover, turn to simmer and cook until greens are tender mixing occassionally for 45 min. to 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Mama Luvs Papa
Description: 
A delicious mixture of greens. The washing process is the most important part for these tender, tasty greens.  Don't forget the sugar!  If you follow directions exactly, you'll have the perfect greens ever!
