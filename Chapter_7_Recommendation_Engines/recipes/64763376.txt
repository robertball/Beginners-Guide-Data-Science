Title:
Big Ray's Spanish Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7222803.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken (3 to 3 1/2 pound size), cut in 8 pieces
1 medium onion, chopped
1 large green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
1 can (14 1/2 ounce size) stewed tomatoes
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 cup chopped salad olives (green olives and pimentos)

Directions:
Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper. Cook over medium heat 10 minutes per side. Remove and set aside. Discard all but 1 tablespoon fat.
Add onion and cook 6 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in green pepper, cook 4 minutes. Add tomatoes, oregano, and 1/4 cup water and bring to a boil.
Return chicken to skillet, cover, and simmer 10 to 15 minutes, turning after 5 minutes. Stir in olives and simmer 5 minutes.
