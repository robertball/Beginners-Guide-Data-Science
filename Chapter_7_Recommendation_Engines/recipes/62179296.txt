Title:
Crowd Pleasing, Bacon-Wrapped Smokies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (16 oz) packages of smokies
1 (1 lb) package of bacon
1 bag (32 oz) brown sugar

Directions:
Cut each strip of bacon into thirds.
Wrap each smokie in the 1/3 of a piece of bacon and 'fasten' with a toothpick.
place one layer of wrapped smokies in a crock pot and pour enough brown sugar on the layer to cover it.
Repeat steps 2 and 3, making sure to cover each layer with brown sugar, until you run out of smokies.
Cook in a crock pot on high for 3-4 hours, or until the brown sugar has melted.
