Title:
Easy Cheesy Beefy Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (6oz) Cornbread Stuffing Mix
3 eggs
2 slices of bread crumbled
1 1/2 cup milk
2 tbl spoons butter
2 tbl spoons Cream Cheese
1 cup shredded colby cheese
1/3 cup shredded colby cheese
1 pound uncooked seasoned beef (hamburger meat)

Directions:
Mix all ingredents except beef and 1/3 cup cheese in a bowl and set aside.
Brown seasoned beef giving the mixture time to soak up all the liquid. Stir in drained cooked beef.
Transfer mixture into a 3x9 baking pan, and bake for 25 min.
Top with remaining cheese and place in oven approx 5 min or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Christy Lane
Description: 
This is an easy no fuss dish that my family goes crazy for. You can easily substitute the seasoned beef for sausage to make this a breakfast dish to die for, or throw in that left over Thanksgiving turkey and make a gobbling good after the holidays meal.
