Title:
My Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/813170.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 cups fresh basil or cilantro
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1/3 cup olive oil
2 tbsp. pine nuts
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
1 lemon juiced
salt to taste (@ 1/2 t.)

Directions:
Combine, cilantro, roasted pine nuts, Parmesan, garlic, lemon juice, salt. Start food processor/blender, and start to add olive oil until it comes together. Continue to blend until throughly pureed (5-10 seconds).
