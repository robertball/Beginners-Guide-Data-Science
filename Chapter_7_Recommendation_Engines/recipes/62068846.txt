Title:
Slowcooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 lb bottom roast
1 can jellied cranberry sauce
1 package dry onion soup mix

Directions:
Place roast in bottom of slowcooker.
Spoon cranberry sauce over roast.
Sprinkle soup mix on top.
Cook on high for 6 hours.
Option:
After an hour and a half you can add,
whole mushrooms, cut up potatoes and baby carrots if you like.  Sprinkle one more soup mix over veggies.  They come out great.

Submitter: 
Laura Moody
Description: 
Simple 3 ingredient recipe.  Tastes fantastic and makes it's own gravy.
