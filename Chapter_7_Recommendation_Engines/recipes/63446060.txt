Title:
Toscana Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065489.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package Johnsonville HOT Italian Sausage (18-20 oz)
1 TBL vegetable oil
3/4 C diced onion
1 1/4 tsp minced garlic
1 Qt College Inn chicken broth
2 potatoes, quartered and sliced about 1/3"
May leave peel on, if desired.
2 C chopped kale
1 C Heavy Cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (150 degrees C).
GENTLY Remove Casing and cut into slightly larger tan "bite-size" and fry in a 4 Qt pot until gently browned. Remove sauage. Drain and reserve 1 TBS of the pork fat. Saut√© onions in reserved fat until translucent; add garlic and cook 1-2 minute.
Add broth and potatoes; simmer 15 minutes.
Thicken with flour as desired to coat the back of a spoon.  Reduce heat to low and add sausage, kale and cream; simmer until heated through and serve.
This Soup is BETTER on the 2d or 3d Day!

Submitter: 
Jack
Description: 
This soup is sure to warm your bones on a chilly day and won't leave you hungry.  Potatoes, sausage and kale are simmered in chicken broth and cream, with onions and garlic.Modified from: Toscana Soup
