Title:
Rye Drop Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/944336.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c all-purpose flour
1 c rye flour
3 tbs brown sugar
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 c vegetable oil
4 eggs
1/4 c milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400Â°
In a medium bowl combine the dry ingredients
Stir in the eggs, milk, and oil until smooth
Drop large spoonful 1 in. apart onto a greased baking sheet
Bake for 7-10 minutes or until golden brown
Remove from pan to a wire rack

Submitter: 
Michael Zick Doherty
Description: 
This is a modification on a recipe found online. I started making it because I wanted to have a quick easy bread recipe to have for breakfast.
