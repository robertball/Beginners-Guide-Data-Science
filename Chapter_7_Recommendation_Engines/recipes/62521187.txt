Title:
Spicy Red Pepper Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/557807.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ounces dry rigatoni
4 strips cooked bacon, crumbled
2 half chicken breasts
Splash of white wine
1 red bell peppers, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1.5 tablespoons cayenne pepper sauce
1 cup low sodium chicken broth
0.5 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 cup reduced fat sour cream
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Meanwhile, fry bacon and set aside to crisp.
Saute chicken breast in large non-stick skillet with white wine over medium heat for 5 minutes.
Add in bell pepper and garlic; cook for additional 3 minutes.
Stir in cayenne pepper sauce and broth; simmer uncovered for 5 minutes. During final minute stir in parmesan, sour cream and bacon.
Toss hot pasta with sauce and season pepper to taste; serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
Tender chicken and crispy bacon enveloped by a creamy sauce of spicy cayenne balanced by sweet bell pepper. Toss with warm pasta and sprinkle with Parmesan.Modified from: Fettuccine with Sweet Pepper-Cayenne Sauce
