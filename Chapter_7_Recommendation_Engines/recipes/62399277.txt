Title:
Lemon-Blueberry Corn Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C white flour
1/2C whole wheat flour
1C yellow cornmeal
1/3C sugar
1/3C Splenda
1T baking powder
1/2t salt
Zest from 1 lemon,finely chopped(1 1/2t)
1/4C canola oil
4T lemon juice
3/4C fat-free milk or 1/2 & 1/2
1/3C fat-free sour cream
1 lg egg
1/4C egg substitute
2C frresh or frozen blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line 15 muffin cups with foil or paper liners.
Whisk together dry ingred. w/lemen zest in bowl
In lg. bowl, add remaining ingred. except blueberries and mix at med. speed
Gently stir in blueberries and spoon into muffin cups
Bake until tester pushed into thickest part of muffin comes out clean (about 18 min)
