Title:
Perfect Cuervo Frozen Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976518.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila
3 ounces Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix
4 ounces crushed ice
1 teaspoon salt
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Mix all ingredients (except lime and salt) in a blender.
Rub the rim of a glass with lime and then dip into salt to frost.
Pour into the glass and garnish with lime wedge.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with the gold standard in tequila, Jose Cuervo Especial® tequila.
