Title:
Best Roasted Beef Bone Broth Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs beef bones
2-3 celery stalks, coarsely chopped
2-3 carrots, coarsely chopped
1 onion, quarted
1 head of garlic halved OR 5 garlic cloves smashed
1 bunch of herbs (parsley, cilantro, thyme, etc.)
2 bay leaves
1 tsp black peppercorns
2 TB apple cider vinegar
1/2 tsp coriander seeds (optional)
Salt to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 450. Roast bones on a parchment paper lined baking sheet for 30 minutes.
2. Turn bones and add vegetables and garlic. Roast for an additional 30 minutes.
3. Transfer bones & vegetables to a 6 qt Slow Cooker.
4. Add 10-12 cups water. Add herbs, bay leaves, peppercorns, ACV and coriander seeds (optional.)
Cook for 12-72 hours.
5. Strain stock through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a large bowl. Discard solids.
6. Let continue to cool until barely warm, then refrigerate in smaller containers overnight. Remove solidified fat from the top of the chilled broth.
*Broth can be stored for up to 5 days in the refrigerator and up to 6 months in the freezer.

Submitter: 
Cindi Fortmann
Description: 
Roasting the bones & the vegetables makes this the best bone broth ever!
