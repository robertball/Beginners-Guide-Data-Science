Title:
Avacado Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup mashed Avacado
8 Oz cream Cheese
3 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
Dash of Worcestershire
1/3 cup minced onion
Salt

Directions:
gradually add avacado to cream cheese, blending till smooth.  Add remaining ingredients and mix till well blended.  Chill and serve with corn chips
