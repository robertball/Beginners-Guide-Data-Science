Title:
Marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb Italian Sausage
1/2 lb ground beef
1 medium onion(diced)
2 cloves garlic (minced)
1 (28oz) can diced tomatoes
2 (6oz) can tomatoe paste
1 (14oz) can tomatoe sauce
1/2 cup water
2 TBL sugar
1 1/2 tsp dried basil
1 tsp italian seasoning
2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
In large sauce pan.  Fry ground beef, sausage and onion until meat is browned, drain oil.  Add garlic and all other ingredents. Simmer at leatst 1 1/2-2 hrs on low, stirring as needed.

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
Basic marinara, works great as a spaghetti or pizza sauce.  Make it meatless by omitting the sausage and ground beef, still has lots of flavor. Can be prepared on the stovetop in a large sauce pan or in a slowcooker.
