Title:
Greek Mango Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1000816.jpg
Ingredients:
3 English Cucumbers
9 Tomatoes
2 Red Peppers
2 Yellow Peppers
2 Orange Peppers
3 Mangos
2 Avocados
1 Cup Balsamic Vinegar
4 to 8 Tablespoons Greek Seasoning

Directions:
Cube all vegetables and fruit into bit size pieces.
Sprinkle on seasoning to taste and add Balsamic vinegar to taste.
Mix together let stand 10 minutes
Serve on a bed of Romaine lettuce leaves
