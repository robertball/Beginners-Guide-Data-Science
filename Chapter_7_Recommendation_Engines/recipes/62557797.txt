Title:
Grandma's Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 - 10  Cut up Peaches
1  Cup Sugar
1/2  Cup Flour
1  Teaspoon Cinnamon
6  Slices Butter
Topping:
2      Cups Flour
1/2    Cup Sugar
3  Teaspoons Baking Powder
6   Tablespoons Shortening
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1   Cup Milk

Directions:
Peaches:
Cut the peaches into small pieces.
Fill an 9x13 pan about three fourths full with the cut up peaches.
Mix in the sugar, flour and sprinkle the cinnamon on top.  Put several slices of butter randomly on top of the entire mixture.
Topping:
Fork together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and shortening.
Mix in the milk.
Fork onto the top of the peaches.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes or until medium brown on the top.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
EMET
Description: 
A quick and very tasty Peach Cobbler.  We make this all summer long and it always disappears quickly!
