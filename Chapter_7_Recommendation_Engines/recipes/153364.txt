Ingredients:
1 quart milk
1 pkg. (4 serving size)  JELL-O Brand Gelatin, any flavor
1/2 cup vanilla ice cream, softened

Directions:
Place milk, gelatin and ice cream in large pitcher with tight-fitting lid; cover. Shake vigorously 1 minute or until well blended. Pour into 4 glasses. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
You don't even need a blender to make this delightful milk shake. Try different flavors of gelatin until you find your favorite creamy concoction!
