Title:
Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2lb. bag frozen hashbrowns, thawed
1/2 cup onions, chopped
1 10oz package shredded cheddar cheese
16 oz sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
salt and pepper to taste
2 sticks melted butter, divided
1/2 box corn flakes, crushed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients in a 10x13 baking pan, except for 1 stick butter and corn flakes.
Cover with crushed cornflakes and pour melted butter over top.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour and 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ashley
Description: 
This is the most delicious hashbrown casserole I've ever tried! My family created this recipe for Christmas, and it has become something we make for every family get-together!
