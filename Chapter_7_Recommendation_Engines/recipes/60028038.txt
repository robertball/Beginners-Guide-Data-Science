Title:
Granny's Southern Buttermilk Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T Shortening
1/4 C Shortening, melted
2 Cups Buttermilk Cornmeal Mix
2 Cups Buttermilk

Directions:
Place 2 T Shortening in iron skillet and preheat oven to 475 degrees allowing the skillet to get hot.  Melt 1/4 cup of shortening.  Mix buttermilk into cornmeal mix and then add shortening.  Allow to sit 5 minutes.  Pour into hot skillet and bake for 25 - 30 minutes until a brown crispy crust forms.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
I remember as a child the smell of Granny's old fashioned cornbread cooking in her iron skillet.  The taste was true southern perfection with a tender inside and a crunchy outside.
