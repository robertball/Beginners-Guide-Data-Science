Title:
Quinoa with veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cooked quinoa (with chicken broth)
1 small onion, diced
3 mini sweet peppers (red yellow orange)
Olive oil
2 cups mini carrots, sliced lengthwise
2 cups spinach leaves, cut in small strips
2 garlic cloves, minced
"Emeril's Essence"

Directions:
Cook carrots in small amount of water. Add spinach and garlic when the carrots are nearly done and a little more water. Cook and stir.
Fry onions and peppers in olive oil..   Sprinkle the precooked quinoa with the seasoning mix.  Mix together the onion mixture and the carrot mixture.  Add the quinoa.   Heat (oven, microwave or stovetop) and serve.  May add cheese.

Submitter: 
None
Description: 
A flavorful healthy side dish or vegetarian main dish, if quinoa is cooked without the chicken broth (in water or veg broth).
