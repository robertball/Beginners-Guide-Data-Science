Title:
Stout Slow Cooker Corned Beef and Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(6 or 7qt) slow cooker.
4 pounds corned beef brisket (discard spice packet or save for other uses).
1 1/2 cup brown sugar (splenda).
1 1/2 - 12 ounce can/bottle Guinness Irish stout beer.
1 med size head of cabbage
2 large sweet onions
6 large carrots
3 medium red potatoes
3 medium sweet potatoes

Directions:
Start by pouring the first bottle of stout in the slow cooker.
Rinse the Beef completely and pat it dry.
Rub the brown sugar (splenda) on the corned beef, including the bottom, and slowly place in the stout in slow cooker.
Add all other ingredients but the remaining beer, on and around the beef in the slow cooker.
Pour the remaining stout beer slowly on and around the beef and veggies to wet the sugar.
Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.
Allow to rest 5 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
RayPappy Bailey
Description: 
A Slow Cooker recipe for some traditional style Irish Corned beef and cabbage that smells as good as it tastes. Sweet Potatoes added for those whose diet prefers them.
