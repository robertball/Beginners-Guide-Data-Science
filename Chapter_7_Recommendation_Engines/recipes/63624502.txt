Title:
Whiskey Spritzer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz whiskey
1 oz amaro, like Averno (Italian herbal liquor)
Splash of lemon juice
2 oz lemon lime flavor soda water

Directions:
1. Place ice in a cocktail shaker. Add ingredients except soda water. Shake vigorously. 2 Pour over ice in a low boy. Add soda water.
