Title:
Machi's Cream Of Broccoli Soup!!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Leaks
Garlic
Olive Oil
Boiling water
parsnips
Broccoli
Celery
Salt
Pepper
Garlic Powder
Sugar
Heave Cream

Directions:
Cut the dark green part of the 3 leeks off
Cut the Leaks
Put cold water in a bowl and put the leaks in to get the sand off them
Peel and mince five cloths of garlic
Over medium heat in a dutch oven saute the leaks and garlic in olive oil
Add ten cups of boiling water into the soup
Peel and cut two parsnips and add them into the soup
Add 4 cups of broccoli stalks into the soup
Add salt, pepper, garlic powder and a spoonful of sugar
Put the soup to high heat till it boils
Once it boils simmer the soup (covered) for 40 minutes
Put the heat back to medium
Blend the soup with a hand blender or a food prosesser
Add the rest of the broccoli florets
Put the soup to high heat till it boils
Once it boils simmer the soup (covered) for 5 minutes
Then stir in 1 cup of heavy cream
Enjoy :D
