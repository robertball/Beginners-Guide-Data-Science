Title:
thai rice noodle salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pkg rice noodles, 1/4 of a red bell pepper, 1/4 cup red onion, 3 green onions, 2 fresh garlic cloves ,thumb size of fresh ginger, 1/4 piece of jalapeno, handfull of basil and cilantro, 1/4 of a cucumber ,1/4 of a head of romaine lettuce, 1/4 tsp turmeric and 1/4 tsp of paprika, juice of 1 fresh lemon and 1 fresh lime , 1/4 cup of rice vinegar and  1/4 cup soy sauce, 1tsp salt, 1/4 cup of sugar, 1/3 cup of olive oil

Directions:
cook rice noodles according to package. drain add oil toss gently they will be sticky. As they cool they will become less sticky toss a few more times while preparing the vege  .mince garlic,ginger,jalapeno. dice bell pepper red and green onions,cucumber,romaine lettuce, roughly chop basil and cilantro add this to noodles will still be sticky but become less sticky as cools. mix the last 9 ingredients together and make a sauce add everything together toss WELL enjoy!
