Title:
Protein Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rolled oats
2/3 cup coconut
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 tbsp. chia seeds
1/2 cup wheat germ or ground flax
1/3 cup honey or maple syrup
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix together well.
Roll into balls.
Store in fridge or freeze.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Tasty little nibbles packed with protein and goodness. Keep a batch in the fridge for munching on.
