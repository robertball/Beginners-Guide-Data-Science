Title:
Beef Stew Recipe

Ingredients:
Beef rib chuck steak
onions
garlic
beef bouillon
carrots
potatoes
corn
thyme
rosemary
pepper
monkeydust
plain flour
tomato paste

Directions:
Season meat with monkey dust.  spirkel meat with very light plain flour.  Brown well in dutch oven.  remove meat and add onions and garlic.  Cook until translucent.  Add 2 cans of chicken broth and a lot of better than bouillon .  Add 1 can of tomato paste and add seasonings.  simmer for several hours.  Add the veges and cook at simmer for several more hours.  Thicken with plain flour and water.
