Title:
Cauliflower Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower, broken into florets
1C sour cream
1C shredded cheddar cheese
1/2C cornflake crumbs
1/4 C chopped green peppers
1/4 C chopped onions
1t salt
1/3C grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Put cauliflower in saucepan, add 1C water, cover and cook on med for 8 min., drain
Mix cauliflower with sour cream, cheddar cheese, crumbs, peppers, onions and salt
Put in greased 2qt. casserole
Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
Bake, uncovered at 325 for 30 min

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Can add up to 1 cup of green and red peppers as well as using seasoned salt unstead of plain salt. Add black pepper and paprika. Bake 350 for 30-35 min.
