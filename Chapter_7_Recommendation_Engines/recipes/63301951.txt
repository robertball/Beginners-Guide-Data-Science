Title:
Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 tablespoons flour
2 tablespoons cup chili powder
8 oz. can tomato sauce
1-1&1/2 cups water
1/4 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
2 tablespoons brown sugar
salt to taste

Directions:
1. Heat oil over medium heat, stir in four and chili powder. Cook until smooth, stirring constantly.
2. Gradually stir in the tomato sauce, water, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, and brown sugar. Reduce heat to low and cook for about 10 minutes or until thickened.

Submitter: 
Mary Kate
Description: 
I use this enchilada sauce for homemade enchiladas.
