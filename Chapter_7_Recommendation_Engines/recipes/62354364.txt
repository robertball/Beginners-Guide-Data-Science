Title:
Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (12oz.) pasta (shells or manicotti)
4 C Ricotta cheese
2 C mozzarella cheese, shredded
3/4 C Parmesan cheese
2 eggs
1 T parsley, chopped
3/4 t. dried oregano leaves
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
3 cups pasta sauce.

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions
Preaheat oven to 375Â°
In large bowl, stir together cheeses, eggs, parsley, oregano, salt and pepper
In 9x13 dish, spread 1/2 C sauce
Fill shells (or manicotti) with cheese mixture.
Layer 1/2 of shells on sauce and cover with 1 1/4 C sauce.
Layer other 1/2 of shells and spread remaining sauce on top.
Sprinkle with parm. Cheese if desired.
Cover with foil and bake for 35 min.
