Ingredients:
8 cups Corn, Rice & Wheat Chex速 cereal
1 cup mixed nuts
1 cup bite-size pretzels
4 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons (Lawry)seasoned salt
3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Combine margarine, Worcestershire sauce and seasons and mix well.  Pour margarine mixture over cereal mixture. Can put in big zipper bag. Preheat oven to 250 then bake 1 hour stirring every 15 minutes.  Spread on absorbent paper to cool then store in airtight container.
