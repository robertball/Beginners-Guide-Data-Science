Title:
Mediterranean Chicken and Orzo Salad In Red Pepper Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ cup olive oil
¾ t. garlic powder
¾ t. dried oregano
¾ t. dried basil
¼ t. pepper
½ t salt
¾ t. onion powder
1 t. Dijon mustard
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
2 sweet red peppers, halved
½ lb. orzo pasta, cooked
½ cup grape tomatoes, halved
¼ cup black olives, halved lengthwise
2 oz. crumbled feta cheese
1 chicken breast half, grilled, cut into small cubes

Directions:
In small bowl, combine oil, seasonings, mustard, and vinegar. Stir together orzo, tomatoes, olives, cheese, and chicken. Season to taste with dressing.
Spoon into pepper halves.
Garnish with fresh oregano.
