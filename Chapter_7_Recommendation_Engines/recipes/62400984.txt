Title:
Kickin Slow Cooker Carnitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/431463.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds boneless pork shoulder
2 can ( 10 oz) La preferida red Chile sauce
1 jar ( 12 oz ) La victoria Salsa Jalapena extra hot
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon oregano
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
1 teaspoon salt
10 oz water

Directions:
Combine all in a slow cooker for 8-10 hours on low. Shred the pork for sandwiches,tortillas or serve over noodles or rice

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
A pork should simmers all day in a slow cooker in red jalapeno extra hot salsa and red chile enchilada sauce.
