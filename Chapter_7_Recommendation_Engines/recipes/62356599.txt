Title:
SUGAR FREE 15 MINUTE JAM Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 CUPS STRAWBERRIES
1/4 CUP SUGAR (OR SPLENDA)
1 TABLESPOON LEMON JUICE

Directions:
IN BLENDER, BLEND FRUIT TIL SMOOTH (OR CHUNKY)YOUR CHOICE
ADD SUGAR/SPLENDA
ADD LEMON JUICE
pOUR MIXTURE INTO NON STICK SKILLET
BRING TO BOIL AND BOIL ABOUT 10 MINUTES TIL THICKENED
POUR OUT INTO GLASS JARS AND LET COOL
