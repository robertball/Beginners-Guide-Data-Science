Title:
Teriyaki Resturant Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/948680.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Tbsp Mayonaise
1 Tbsp pickled ginger + 1 Tbsp juice
1 large clove garlic
1 tsp Sesame seed oil
1/3 cup white sugar
1/2 cup Rice Wine Vinegar
1 cup Salad Oil (Vegetable, Olive, Canola - Whatever)

Directions:
Using a hand blender, mix together mayo, ginger, ginger juice, garlic,sesame seed oil, rice wine vinegar.  Using a whisk (preferrably from your hand blender) whisk in your salad oil slowly until it emulsifies. Serve!

Submitter: 
Judy Bialek
Description: 
My desperation for good Teriyaki Resturant style salad dressing led me to this. It's pretty good!
