Title:
Mildly Spicy Red Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag Dried Red Kidney Beans
1/2 cup Onion, minced
2 tsp Salt
2 tsp Old Bay Seasoning
1 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp Black Pepper
1/4 tsp Cayenne Pepper
2 Hot Sausages (your choice, I prefer Hot Italian), cut into chunks
4 cps Minute Rice

Directions:
In a large pot, cover beans with 8 cups of water.
Bring to boil on high heat.
Remove from heat and let soak for about 1-2 hours.
Drain and rinse beans thoroughly.
Return beans to pot with remaining ingredients, EXCEPT FOR THE RICE.
Cover with water 8 cups of water and heat to boiling again.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer for another 1-2 hours (or to desired tenderness); stirring every 20-30 minutes.
Drain liquid again, reserving 2 cups of bean liquid for the rice.
In another large pot, bring the 2 cups of bean liquid and 2 cups of clear water to boil.
Remove from heat and stir in rice.
Let stand until liquid is absorbed, then fluff with fork.
Stir in as little or as much beans as desired.
