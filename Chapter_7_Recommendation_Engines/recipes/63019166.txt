Title:
Grandma's Molasses ginger cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup shortening
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup molasses (dark, robust Brer Rabbit is best)
1 egg
1 tablespoon vinegar
2 tablespoon water
4 1/2 cups flour
1 tablespoon baking soda
1 tablespoon ginger

Directions:
Cream together shortening and sugar. Add egg, then molasses, vinegar and water
Sift together flour, soda and ginger.
Roll out thick and cut out with simple round cookie cutter.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes at 350.

Submitter: 
SJRJA
Description: 
A family recipe made by my grandmother Opal, not too sweet, but a big soft cookie you could eat for breakfast.
