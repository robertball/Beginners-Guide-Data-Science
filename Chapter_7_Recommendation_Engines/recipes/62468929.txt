Title:
Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz fresh spinach
1/2 C sour cream
1/2 C shredded pepperjack cheese
4 cloves garlic, minced
4 chicken breasts, pounded thin (1/2 in. thickness)
pinch black pepper
8 slices bacon (or 4 slices)
Can add sundried tomatoes, mushrooms, onion, red pepper flakes, and/or other cheeses.

Directions:
Preheat oven 375
Heat spinach in microwave for 3 min. or saute in pan with mushrooms, onion.
Stir in sour cream, cheese(s), garlic, (sundried tomatoes, pepper flakes).
Spoon mixture onto each chicken breast, roll up and then wrap with one or two bacon slices. Secure with toothpicks and put on foil lined dish.
Bake uncovered for 35 min and increase oven temp to 500 degrees and cook additional 5-10 min to crisp bacon.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Uses fresh spinach, sour cream, pepperjack cheese, and bacon. Pound chicken thin and stuff.
