Title:
Thai Noodles with Peanut Sauce and Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz package linguine noodles
4 T sesame oil
1/2 C green onions, chopped
1 C carrots, shredded
4 C frozen stir fry vegetables
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 T ground ginger
2 C cooked chopped chicken
1/4 C honey
1/4 C creamy peanut butter
1/4 C soy sauce
3 T rice vinegar
2 T Thai chili garlic sauce

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions. Drain. Return to pot, add 2 T sesame oil. Toss to coat.
Heat remaining oil in heavy pot over medium-high heat. Add onion, carrot, stir fry vegetables. garlic & ginger. Saute about 6 minutes.
Add chicken, honey, peanut butter, soy sauce, vinegar and chili garlic sauce. Stir and simmer about 2 minutes.
Pour sauce over pasta and toss to mix.

Submitter: 
cara1997
Description: 
This is a very simple Thai noodle dish that is very easy to make and not to overly spicy. I found it in a local newspaper and made some modifications of my own. My daughter, who loves any kind of noodle dish says it is one of her favorites.
