Title:
Pressure Cooker Sweet and Spicy Pinto Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups dried pinto beans
5.5 cups water
1.5 Tbls veggie oil or oil of your choice
1/2 an onion
8 oz chopped green chilies
8 oz crushed pineapple
19 oz hearty (chunky) tomato soup
2 TBLS chili Powder
2 tsp cumin
1 tsp oregano
2 tsp salt
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Pick through the beans, discarding any discolored or shriveled beans or anything foreign.
Rinse thoroughly.
Place beans, water and oil in a pressure cooker and cook on high pressure for 25 minutes.
Place the rest of the ingredients in the pressure cooker and pressure cook on high for another 10 minutes.
Remove lid and simmer for on medium for 15 minutes.  You may adjust cooking time based on how firm you like your beans.  Taste and add salt/pepper accordingly.

Description: 
The slight heat from the green chilies followed by a slight sweet finish makes these beans to die for.  I came up with this as I needed a side dish to go with my grilled pork tacos. Our friend kept raving about how good these were and when back for 3rd's.
