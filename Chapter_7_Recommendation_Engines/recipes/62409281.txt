Title:
Farina -Nut Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/436444.jpg
Ingredients:
Topping:
1/4 cup sugar
2 teaspoons granulated sugar
1/4 cup chopped pecans, almonds, or walnuts
Batter:
1 cup farina
1 egg
3/4 cup flour
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon course salt
1 cup buttermilk/sour cream or yogurt
1 teaspoon almond extract
1 stick butter, melted
2 cups blueberries
1 teaspoon orange zest

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Grease 12 muffin cups and combine topping ingredients.
Blend farina, flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder and salt.  In a separate bowl, whisk buttermilk, egg, almond extract, melted butter and orange zest.  Combine with dry ingredients.  Fill each cup to 2/3 full and sprinkle with topping.
Bake for 12-15 minutes, until muffins are golden brown.  Serve warm
