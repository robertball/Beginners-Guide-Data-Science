Title:
Orange Ganache and Berries Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FOR CAKE:
1 package white cake mix
1 1/4 cups water
1/3 cup oil
3 whole eggs
FOR FILLING:
8 ounces chocolate candy melts, milk/semi-sweet/dark as desired
2/3 cup whipping cream
1/4 tsp orange extract, or more to taste
FOR FROSTING:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup shortening
4 cups powdered sugar
2 tbsp milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
orange extract to taste (about 1 tsp.)
FOR BERRY SAUCE:
1 pint fresh strawberries, sliced
1 small jar seedless raspberry jam

Directions:
Bake cake as directed, using ingredient measurements for cake above.
To prepare filling, melt chocolate gently.  When chocolate is melted, stir in whipping cream until blended.   Stir in orange extract.
When cake is cooled, spread filling on bottom layer.  Settle top layer into place over filling.
For icing, cream together butter, shortening, sugar, milk and vanilla extract until smooth.   Stir in orange extract a little at a time.   Frost the cooled cake with icing.
Arrange berries on top of cake.  Melt the raspberry jam and pour over berries and cake before serving.    Alternatively, mix berries together with jam and spoon over cake before serving.
