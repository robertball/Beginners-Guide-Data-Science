Title:
Bean Chimichangas with Onion Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 ounce can refried beans
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1 cup grated cheddar cheese, divided
1 16 ounce can tomato sauce
2 onions, peeled and sliced
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1 tablespoon pickled jalapenos, drained and minced (or more, to taste)
6 12" flour tortillas
4 tablespoons canola oil, divided
1/2 cup sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Mix refried beans, garlic and onion powders,and 1/2 cup cheese in a microwave safe bowl.
Microwave until cheese has melted, stiring as necessary.
Set aside.
Saute onion in 3 tablespoons oil until tender and lightly browned.
Stir in tomato sauce, cumin, onion and jalapenos and simmer for 5 minutes.
While salsa simmers, preheat oven to 375 degrees and heat the tortillas in microwave until warm and pliable.
Place 2 tablespoons of the bean and cheese mixture in each warm tortilla and roll tightly, tucking in the sides.
When all tortillas have been rolled, place seam side down on cookie sheet and brush with canola oil.
Bake 25 minutes or until brown.
Serve chimichangas with onion salsa on top.
Sprinkle remaining cheese over salsa.
Top with a dollop of sour dream.
