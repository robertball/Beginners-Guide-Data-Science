Title:
STRAWBERRY PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh strawberries
1 (6-ounce) graham cracker crust
1 (4-ounce) package JELL-O® Vanilla Pudding
1 (4-ounce) package JELL-O® Strawberry Gelatin
1 ½ cups water
1 cup Cool Whip
½ tsp. vanilla extract (optional)

Directions:
Arrange strawberries in pie crust.
In large saucepan, combine dry pudding mix dry gelatin and water.
Cook over medium heat until mixture thickens and starts
to boil.
Stir often.
Spoon hot sauce over strawberries.
Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
Mix topping and vanilla and cover pie evenly.
