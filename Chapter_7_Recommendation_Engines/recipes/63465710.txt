Title:
Pork Tenderloin with Dijon Marsala Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 to 4lb boneless hatfield pork tenderloin
2 tablespoons butter
2 shallots or 1/4v onion, minced
1 cup Marsala wine
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup unsalted butter
cornstarch

Directions:
Cook pork as directed on package-
Meanwhile, melt butter over medium heat. Cook shallots in butter until soft. Stir in Marsala, mustard, milk and butter, and cook until volume of liquid is reduced by half. Thicken with cornstarch if needed.
Slice pork, and place on a serving dish. Serve sauce on the side.

Submitter: 
paigekowolewski
Description: 
This absolutely delicious pork tenderloin is prepared simply by first browning in a skillet, and then finishing in the oven. Served with a Marsala cream sauce, prepared while the pork is baking, the dish can be prepared in under an hour. The sauce is tasty over steamed vegetables as well.Modified from: Pork Tenderloin with Dijon Marsala Sauce
