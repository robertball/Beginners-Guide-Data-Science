Title:
Fideo Fantastico (Scrumptious Tex-Mex spaghetti) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 onion, minced
1 teaspoon cumin
2 teaspoons chili powder
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon paprika
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes and green chilies, undrained
1 (5 ounce) box Fideo (Vermicelli) pasta
1/2 cup water
1 cup chicken broth

Directions:
1. In a large skillet, season ground beef with salt and pepper. Add minced onion and cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain fat, reserving 2 tablespoons of fat in pan.
2. Season meat with cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, and paprika. Add Fideo pasta and stir. Add diced tomatoes and green chilies, water, and chicken broth.
3. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until pasta is tender and liquid is absorbed.

Description: 
This tasty Tex-Mex spaghetti is a flavorful and wonderful Mexican pasta dish, especially when you don't feel like Italian. It's also quick and kid-friendly!
