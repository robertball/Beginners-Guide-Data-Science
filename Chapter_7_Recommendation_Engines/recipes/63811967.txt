Title:
Susan's Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados
1 TBSP sour cream
1/2 tsp lime juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp onion powder
1/8 tsp cumin
sprinkle of chili powder
chopped tomatoes (optional)

Directions:
Mix all together.  Feel free to play with ingredients.  Throw in the avocado seed to keep it fresh if not serving immediately.
