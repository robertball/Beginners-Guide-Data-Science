Title:
Bacon, Spinach and Turkey Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Thick cut Bacon 1 pkg.
ground turkey or chicken 1/2 - 3/4 lb.
chicken taco seasoning 1/2 a packet
1 large tomato diced
1 large onion diced
1 jalapeno pepper seeded and chopped fine (optional)
cilantro chopped 2 Tbs
Garlic minced 1 tsp
Fresh Spinach 1 bag
Green enchilada sauce 1 can
sour cream 1 regular tub
1 package of shredded cheese (taco blend works well)
salt 1 tsp
12 corn tortillas

Directions:
First prepare filling
Fry bacon in large pan-once crisp and brown remove bacon from pan and set aside
Add ground turkey to the same pan on Medium heat
Mix in taco seasoning and allow to brown a little then add tomato, onion, garlic, cilantro and jalapeno
Slowly add washed and dried spinach to pan
Add salt and stir mixture on Low heat
Blend 1 part sour cream and 1 part enchilada sauce
Spread a little of this sauce mixture on the bottom of a casserole dish
Next you will put it all together
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Warm tortillas so they are easier to work with
Place shredded cheese in tortilla
Crumble 1/2 a bacon strip over cheese
Spoon some of turkey and spinach mixture
Roll and place seam side down in prepared casserole dish
Repeat until done then cover with the rest of the sauce and cheese
Bake for 25 minutes
