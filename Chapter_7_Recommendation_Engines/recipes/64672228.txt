Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of tart apples
2/3- 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup oats
1/3 cup butter, softened
3/4 tsp cinnamon
3/4 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
Heat 375 degrees.
Arrange apples in greased 8x8 pan.
Mix remaining ingredients.
Sprinkle over apples
Bake until topping is golden brown, and apples are tender, about 30 min.
