Title:
Peanut Butter Meltaways Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521178.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
1 1/2 cups white chocolate melting wafers
1 cup smooth peanut butter
2 tbsp margerine

Directions:
Place 4 dozen foil mini cupcake liners on a baking sheet or other flat tray.
Combine all ingredients in a glass dish and microwave on high for 2 minutes, stir mixture until smooth (if it will not smooth out microwave again for 30 seconds at a time stirring until the desired consistancy is acheived.)
Spoon into foil liners.
Place tray in refrigerater and allow to chill until set.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
This delicious little chocolate that is a staple on my Christmas trays is incredibly simple to make, other nut butters can be subbed in place of the peanut butter for different flavors.
