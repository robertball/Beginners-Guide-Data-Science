Title:
Yummy Sweet Potato Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sweet potato, cubed
1/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons butter, softened
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. clove
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1 cup packed brown sugar
2/3 cup all-purpose flour
6 tablespoons butter, softened
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C.  Bake sweet potatoes.  Remove skin.
In a large bowl, mix together the sweet potatoes, sugar, eggs, salt, butter, milk, vanilla and spices. Mix until smooth. Transfer to a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In medium bowl, mix the sugar and flour. Cut in the butter until the mixture is coarse. Stir in the pecans. Sprinkle the mixture over the sweet potato mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes, or until the topping is lightly brown.
