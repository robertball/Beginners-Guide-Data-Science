Title:
Mexican Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb pulled pork
3 16oz jars of salsa
6 large tortillas
1 packet taco seasoning
2 cans Kwuner's Southwestern Black Beans (with cumin & chili spices)
3 cups Mexican Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Pre-Heat the Oven to 375
1. Heat the pork in a pan. When heated add seasoning packet and handful of cheese. Combine and turn off heat.
2. Overlap two tortillas in bottom of casserole dish. Spread half of the pulled pork onto the tortillas.
3. Open and Drain 1 can of beans. Spread the beans over the pork.
4. Cover the beans with salsa and cheese.
5. Repeat
6. Add the final two tortillas to the top. Cover liberally in salsa and cheese.
7. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes.
Serve with guacamole/sour cream/etc.
