Rujak Bebek Recipe

Ingredients:
Bahan:
-Ubi kira kira 1 lb.
-Nenas 1/2 biji, dipotong kecil kecil
-Mangga muda 3 biji.
-Bangkuang 1/2 besar
-Pepaya muda, 1/2 biji
Bumbu:  Gula jawa 1 1/2 potong
Cabe Serano 4 biji
Asam Jawa 2 - sebesar bola pingpong.
Garam 1 Tbsp
Terasi 2 Tbsp

Directions:
-Boil brown sugar in 2 cups of water.
-Add all other ingredients
- Blend serano chili in 1/2 cup of water.
-Wait till it cold down, pour to the fruits
