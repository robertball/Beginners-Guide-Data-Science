Title:
Cheater Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8.5 oz Box Corn Bread Mix
1 egg
1/3 c milk
1/3 c sour cream or mayonnaise
3/4 c frozen corn, thawed

Directions:
Prepare cornbread mix as directed, adding the sour cream with the milk.  Fold in corn.
Bake as directed for cornbread or corn muffins.
This doubles well for a small oblong pan.

Submitter: 
REALCOOKIE
Description: 
We love cornbread and really enjoy this souped up version of the boxed mix.  It's wonderfully moist and super quick.
