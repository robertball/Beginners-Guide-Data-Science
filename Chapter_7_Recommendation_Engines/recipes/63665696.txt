Title:
Carrot salad with ginger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups shredded carrots
1 cup raisins
1/4 cup shredded candied ginger
1/4 cup orange juice

Directions:
Mix together and refrigerate.
Though good right away, it improves with age when ingredients have had time to blend.

Submitter: 
Anne
Description: 
Quick and easy gingered carrot salad.
