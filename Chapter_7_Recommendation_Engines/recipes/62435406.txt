Title:
Marinated Barbequed Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small eggplant, cut into 3/4 inch thick slices
2 small red bell peppers, seeded and cut into quarters
3 zucchinis, cut in half lengthwise
2 portobello mushrooms, stems removed
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup basalmic vinegar
1/4 cup coarsely chopped fresh basil
2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Place eggplant, red bell peppers, zucchinis and fresh mushrooms in a medium bowl.
In a medium bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, basil and garlic. Pour the mixture over the vegetables, cover and marinate in the refrigerator at least 1 hour.
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
Place vegetables directly on the grill or on skewers. Cook on the prepared grill 2 to 3 minutes per side, brushing frequently with the marinade, or to desired doneness.
Place in serving dish, sprinkle with feta cheese and serve.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
The garlic, lemon and basil marinade infuses veggies with an unmistakably delicious flavor. These veggies go great with just about anything!Modified from: Marinated Barbequed Vegetables
