Title:
Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 TBL Butter
3/4 Cup Diced Onion
1/4 Cup Celery
1/2 Cup Carrots
2 Cans of your favorite beans (Kidney, Cannellini, Green)
1 Small Zucchini
1 1/2 Cup of Cubed Potato
1/4 Bag of Spinach
1 Can Diced Tomato
1 TBL Tomato Paste
1 QT Broth (Chicken or Vegetable)
2 TBL Parsley
1 TEA Salt
1/2 TEA Basil
1/2 TEA Black Pepper

Directions:
Saute onion, celery, and carrots in butter.
Add all other ingredients and bring to a boil and then reduce heat and let simmer for an hour.
