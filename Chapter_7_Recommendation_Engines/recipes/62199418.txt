Title:
Hearty Steak Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/302435.jpg
Ingredients:
5 steak fillets (tenderized then cubed)
2 cans kidney beans (drained)
2 cans pinto beans (drained)
1 can chilli beans (drained)
3 cans fire roasted tomatoes
2 cans tomato sauce
1 can tomato puree
2 large green peppers (diced and sauteed)
3 garlic cloves
1 large white onion (sauteed)
3 tsp mexican chili powder
4 tsp crushed red peppers

Directions:
tenderize and cube your meat. saute with a bit of olive oil till its nearly cooked through. start adding to the crock pot. add in all beans,  tomato sauce and puree. add 2 cans of the fire roasted tomatoes with juice & the last one drained. saute (optional)and add your green peppers & onions. finely chop the garlic and add it in. lastly your spices.  i add my chili powder and red peppers by taste but its about 3-4 tbs of each. cook on low all night long and eat the the next day for dinner. i find the longer i let it cook the better it tastes.

Submitter: 
purplemoment
Description: 
chili is my favorite comfort food. with some left over  steak fillets i put together this hearty meal in my crock pot. its very satisfying with 3 different kinds of beans. *recipe is for a 6quart pot*
