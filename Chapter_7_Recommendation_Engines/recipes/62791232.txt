Title:
Refrigerator Lemon Margarita Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
24 graham crackers, finely crushed
1/4 c butter, melted
1/4 c brown sugar
Filling:
1/2 c lemon juice
1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
3 eggs, beaten
6 T tequila
8 oz sour cream
1 T granulated sugar

Directions:
Mix crushed graham cracker crumbs with melted butter and brown sugar.  Press into 8-inch pie pan to form crust.
In a medium sized bowl, whisk the eggs and sweetened condensed milk together.  Stir in lemon juice and tequila.  Pour into heavy saucepan and stirring constantly, slowly bring mixture just to a boil.  Remove from heat and let cool slightly.  Pour mixture into pie shell.  In a small bowl, whisk together sour cream and granulated sugar.  Spoon this over pie filling and refrigerate pie for at least one hour.
