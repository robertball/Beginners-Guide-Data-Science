Title:
Chicken Monterey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894501.jpg
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken breasts
200 grams (1 1/2 cups) Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
1 can mushroom soup
1/2 cup water
1 pkg. chicken Stove top stuffing
1/4 cup melted margarine

Directions:
Cut chicken into smaller pieces and put into baking dish. Cover with shredded cheese.
Combine soup and water. Pour over chicken.
Melt margarine and combine with Stove top crumbs. Sprinkle over chicken.
Cover and bake for 35 minutes at 350. Uncover and bake for 30 additional minutes.
