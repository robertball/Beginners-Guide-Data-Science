Title:
ABC Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1117411.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 - 1 pound ground beef
1 (1.5 oz.) packet of McCormick's beef stew dry seasoning mix
1 (28 oz.) can diced tomatoes
2 (14 oz.) cans Veg-All, drained
2 Veg-All cans of water
a handful of dry noodles, optional

Directions:
Brown ground beef and drain well.
Add beef stew seasoning packet and tomatoes.  Stir well.
Add Veg-All and water. Adjust water depending on how "soupy" you like your soup.
Bring to a boil.
Add a handful of noodles of your choice and simmer for 15 minutes or until noodles are done.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
My children made this recipe in their kindergarten class.  It's easy as ABC...and tasty too! It's even better the next day after all the flavors meld.
