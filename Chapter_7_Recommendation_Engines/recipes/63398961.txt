Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 c shortening
2 boxes of brown sugar
8 eggs
4 c cooked pumpkin
1 1/3 c water
7 1/2 c flour (use 3 1/2 whole wheat and 4 white flour)
1 T baking soda
1 T baking powder
2 T cinnamon
2 T cloves
2 T nutmeg
optional:  1 c nuts, 1 c raisins,pinch of salt

Directions:
Stir all ingredients until well mixed.  Spoon into muffin tin lined with cupcake papers.  Bake 20 minutes at 350'.  May be frosted with cream cheese and honey frosting.  Make about 5-6 dozen.
