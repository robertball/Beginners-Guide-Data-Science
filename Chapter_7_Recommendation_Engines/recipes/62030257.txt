Title:
BRUNSWICK STEW Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 {14 1/2Oz.} can peeled Tomatoes, undrained
1 {17oz.} frozen Green Lima Bean
2 1/2lbs. Chicken, cut up
2Cs. Chicken Broth
2Cs. Tomato Paste
2Tbsps. Red Wine Vinegar
2Tbsps. Worcestershire Sauce
1/4tsp. ground Red Pepper
2Cs. cubed cooked Pork

Directions:
Drain tomatoes and add lima beans, reserving liquid; coarsely chop tomatoes. In large heavy saucepan, oven medium-high heat, combine chicken, chicken broth, tomato paste, reserved tomato liquid, red wine vinegar; Worcestershire sauce and pepper; heat to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 20 minutes. Add tomatoes, lima beans and pork; cover. Simmer 20 to 30 minutes more or until is done!!

Corwynn Darkholme
Description: 
This is a very savory soup, very hearty as well!!!
