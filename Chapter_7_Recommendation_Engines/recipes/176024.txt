Title:
Simply Potatoes® Chili Dog Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package Simply Potatoes® Homestyle Slices
2 (15 ounce) cans chili no beans
1 (16 ounce) package jumbo hot dogs, cut into 1-inch pieces
1 (1 pound, 3 ounce) package refrigerated large flaky layer biscuits
2 cups Crystal Farms® Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 13x9-inch glass baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.  Spread potatoes in thin layer in bottom of baking dish.  Top evenly with one can chili.  Cover with aluminum foil; bake for 30 minutes.
Uncover; top with hot dogs and remaining chili. Separate biscuits; place over chili. Sprinkle with cheese.  Continue baking, uncovered, for 15 to 18 minutes or until biscuits are golden brown.

Submitter: 
Simply Potatoes®
Description: 
Kids and adults alike will not be able to resist this casserole. Hot dogs, chili, cheese… all-American ingredients!
