Title:
Pumpkin Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 cup dark corn syrup
1/2 cup sugar
4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) melted butter
1 cup canned pumpkin
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup chopped pecans
1 9-inch unbaked pie shell
Whipped cream

Directions:
With a hand beater, beat eggs well.
Beat in corn syrup, sugar, butter, pumpkin and vanilla until well-blended.
Arrange pecans in bottom of pie shell.
Slowly pour egg mixture over them. Bake for 1 hour at 350 or until knife inserted 1 inch from edge comes out clean.
Let cool completely before serving to allow the filling to set up.
