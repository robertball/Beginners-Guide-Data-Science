Title:
Strawberry Surprise Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2578795.jpg
Ingredients:
8 fluid ounces almond milk
5 frozen strawberries, or more to taste
2 scoops vanilla protein powder
1 large lemon, juiced
1 tablespoon ground flax seed
6 basil leaves

Directions:
Blend almond milk, strawberries, protein powder, lemon juice, ground flax seed, and basil in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Joseph Alvarado
Description: 
This is a quick breakfast drink for on the go.
