Title:
Nanna Bufalini's Lazy Daisy Toasted Coconut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CAKE:
2 eggs
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup All Purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup scalded milk
1 tablespoon butter
TOPPING:
2 tablespoons heavy cream
2 tablespoons melted butter
5 tablespoons sugar
1/2 cup coconut

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350
grease 9x13 pan and set aside
In medium bowl sift together all dry ingredients
In small saucepan scald 1/2 milk stirring as needed, remove from heat
In large bowl beat eggs until fluffy
add sugar to eggs and beat well
add vanilla and all the the dry ingredients to wet ingredients and beat well
add butter to scalded milk and allow to melt
mix milk and butter mixture into the rest of the batter
bake in a 13x9 pan at 350 for 20 minutes
While cake bakes mix Coconut, sugar, melted butter and heavy cream.
After you remove cake from oven allow to rest for 5 minutes.  While still warm, spread topping over cake.  Turn on your broiler and move the rack one level below highest rack height.  Broil cake until coconut is golden brown.  Allow to cool and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Chrissi Guarnieri
Description: 
This is a recipe from my husband's Nanna Bufalini.  It was his favorite dessert growing up and now a favorite in our family.  Absolutely delicious!  Double the topping and enjoy!
