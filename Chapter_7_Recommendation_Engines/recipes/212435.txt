Title:
Jif® Dream Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976659.jpg
Ingredients:
1 serving Crisco® Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
1/2 cup CRISCO® Butter Shortening
1 cup JIF® Extra Crunchy Peanut Butter
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1 large egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup Pillsbury BEST® All Purpose Flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 cup milk
2 cups 100% natural oats, honey and raisins cereal
1 cup chocolate covered toffee-candy chips
1 cup milk chocolate covered peanuts
1/3 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray a 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan with no-stick cooking spray; set aside.
Combine shortening, peanut butter, brown sugar and corn syrup in bowl of electric mixer; beat at medium speed until creamy. Add egg and vanilla; beat well.
Combine flour and baking powder in medium bowl; add alternately with milk to creamed mixture at medium speed. Stir in cereal, 1 cup chocolate chips, chocolate covered toffee-candy chips and chocolate covered nuts with spoon.
Spread in prepared pan. Bake 20 to 25 minutes.
Remove pan from oven. Sprinkle remaining 1 cup chocolate chips over top immediately. Move pan to cooling rack. Let stand about 3 minutes or until chips become shiny and soft. Spread over top. Sprinkle with chopped nuts. Cool completely. Cut into 2 x 1-inch bars.

Submitter: 
Jif®
Description: 
Make a batch of these rich, nutty, and chocolatey cookie bars for your next big event, and watch them fly off the platter.
