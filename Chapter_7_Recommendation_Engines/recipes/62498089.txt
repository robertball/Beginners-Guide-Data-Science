Title:
Rice & Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123189.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 c. instant minute rice (uncooked)
1 small jar cheese whiz
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 10oz. pkg. frozen broccoli
1/2 c. diced celery
1/2 c. chopped onion
1 stick softened margarine

Directions:
Mix together the rice, cheese whiz and soup in a large bowl.
Add the broccoli, celery and onion.
Blend in the softened margarine.
Put in buttered casserole at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until rice is done.
