Title:
Bagels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups water
3 TBS. sugar
2 tsp. salt
4 cups bread flour
2 tsp. rapid-rise yeast
For Boiling and Baking:
3 quarts water
1 TBS. sugar
2-3 TBS. cornmeal
1 egg beaten
1-2 TBS. sesame seeds, poppyseeds, etc.

Directions:
Measuring carefully, place all ingredients except those required for boiling and baking in bread machine pan in order specified by owner's manual.  Program dough cycle setting; press start.
Turn out dough onto floured surface; knead breifly.  Cut into 9 pieces.  Shape into balls.  Place on floured surface, let rest 10 minutes.  Poke thumb through center of each ball to make a hole.  Stretch into doughnut shapes.  Place on floured surface.  Let rise, uncovered, 15 minutes or until puffy.
For boiling and baking preheat oven to 400.  Bring water and sugar to a boil in large, deep skillet or wok.  Spray making sheets with nonstick cooking spray; sprinkle with cornmeal.  Carefully lower bagels, 3 at a time, into boiling water.  Boil 5 minutes, turning often.  Remove bagels using slotted spoon, drain breifly on paper towels.  Place 2" apart on prepared baking sheets.  Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with desired seed.  Bake 25-30 minuted or until golden brown.  Remove from baking sheet; cool on wire racks.
