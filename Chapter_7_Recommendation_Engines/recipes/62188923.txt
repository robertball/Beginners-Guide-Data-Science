Title:
Chicken cordon bleu cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 chicken breasts (seasoned with garlic, onion, salt, and peper.)
2 cups of diced ham
1/2 tsp garlic powder (or 1-2 fresh cloves diced)
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp paprika
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup shredded swiss cheese
1 cup shredded colby jack
1 cup of milk
2 cups cooked elbow macarroni noodles
3/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1/4 cup grated parmesan

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Saute seasoned chicken until no longer pink. In a bowl mix soup, milk, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and both cheeses. Add in chicken, ham, and noodles stirring until all mixed. Pour into sprayed 9x13 baking dish (or cassarole dish of your choice), and top with bread crumbs and parmesan. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kristen Klimper
Description: 
All the great flavor of chicken cordon bleu without the time and fuss. A great dish with kids!!
