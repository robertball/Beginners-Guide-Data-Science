Title:
Lacy's Mexican Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 9oz. "NO BOILING NEEDED" lasagna noodles
2 lbs. ground beef
1 small onion finely diced
salt and pepper to taste
12 oz mild salsa
3 tbsp taco sauce(eg La Victoria)
1 10.75 oz tomato soup
2 8oz can tomato sauce
1 1/4 cup french fried onions (eg frenchs)
2 cups monterary jack cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Start by browning your ground beef and onion. Drain.
After draing stir in Salt, pepper, salsa, soup, 1 can tomato sauce, and french fried onions. Bring to a bubble for 5-10 minutes.
Put a layer of the sauce mixture on the bottom of 9x13x2 so noodles don't stick. Put a layer of noodles. Then a layer of sauce. Then a layer of cheese. Repeat about 2 more times saving some cheese for the top. When you have your top layer pour the second can of tomato sauce all over it. Sprinkle with cheese. Cover with foil and bake about 45 min, take the foil off the last 15 minutes to allow cheese to brown. let stand about 15- 20 minutes.

This is diferent from the usual Mexican lasagna...no taco seasoning and no refried beans. It is truly a yummy dish. Kids will love it!
