Title:
Short Cut Cucumbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 empty jar of pickles like dill or whatever with juice
1 or 2 (depending on size of jar) cucumbers

Directions:
Slice cucumbers with skin on
Throw into pickle juice jar
Chill overnight

Submitter:

Description: 
Time baby!  It's all about saving time!  Great refreshing side for Spring and Summer.
