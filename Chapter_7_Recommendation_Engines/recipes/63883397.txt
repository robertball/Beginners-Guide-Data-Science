Title:
Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077339.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can crescent rolls
1 lb. sausage
6 eggs
1/4 c milk
1 bag cheddar cheese
non-stick cooking spray

Directions:
Brown sausage, drain sausage. Beat eggs with milk. Spray a 9x13" baking pan with cooking spray.
Spread crescent rolls in bottom of the pan. Crumble sausage over crescent rolls.
Pour egg mix over top & put the cheese on last.
Bake at 400 for 20 mins & serve.
