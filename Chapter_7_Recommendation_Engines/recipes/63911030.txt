Title:
Loaded Baked Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084801.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp butter
1/2 onion, chopped
1/4 C butter, melted
1/2 C flour
2 C milk
2 C chicken broth
1 1/2 tsp sriracha
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
¼ tsp red pepper flakes
¼ tsp garlic salt
¼ tsp celery seed
2 C broccoli florets
2 C carrots, finely chopped
3 C cheddar cheese, shredded
2 large potatoes, baked and cubed
8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled

Directions:
Melt 1 Tbsp butter in soup pot.  Stir in onions and cook over medium-low heat until softened.
Add melted butter to pot.  Stir in flour until well combined.  Slowly add milk and chicken broth to pot until mixture is smooth.
Add sriracha, black pepper, worcestershire sauce, red pepper flakes, garlic salt, celery seed, broccoli, carrots, cheese, potatoes, and bacon.  Continue to heat over medium heat, stirring often, until vegetables are cooked through and cheese is melted, about 20 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
This hearty soup is perfect for a chilly evening and full of flavor!  Bacon, potato, and broccoli make this a favorite in our home.
