Title:
Smoked Salmon Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 flour tortilla
1 package cream cheese, softened
50g smoked salmon, cut into strips
2 tablespoons fresh dill, roughly chopped
2 tablespoons lemon rind, finely grated
10 red oak lettuce
30 toothpicks

Directions:
1. Spread evenly the cream cheese on each tortilla.
2. Distribute evenly between 5 tortilla the smoked salmon strips, dill and lemon rind. Top with the lettuce, leaving the leafy ends a little over the edge of each tortilla.
3. Roll up each tortilla tightly and chill for about 1 hour (or more if necessary), covered with a clean tea towel or paper towel.
4. Cut each tortilla into 6 slices and secure each slice with a toothpick.
