Title:
Sauerkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/519676.jpg
Ingredients:
50 lbs cabbage
Jugs of water
10 gallon crock
Salt
Caraway seed
Old bath towel
Kraut cutter
Boards cut to fit inside of crock

Directions:
Clean & core cabbage.  Save outside leaves.  Shred into large metal bowl.  Put in bottom of crock and mix with handful of salt and Â¾ handful of caraway seed.  Hand pack tight into bottom all the way to edge outside edge.  Repeat layers until crock 2/3 full.  Water should be forming on top of layers.  Put old wet towel on top.  Layer outside leaves on top of towel 2-3 layers deep.  Put boards on top and weigh down with plastic jugs of water.  Put in warm location for 3 weeks.
