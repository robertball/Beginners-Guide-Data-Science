Title:
Keith's Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of Frozen Tater Tots
1 lb Breakfast Sausage
1 Clove Minced Garlic
1 Green Pepper Diced
1 Onion Diced
2 1/2 c Milk (divided)
6 Eggs
2 c Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 can Condensed Broccoli Soup

Directions:
Heat a skillet over medium heat
Brown the sausages remove from the pan and cut into small pieces
Add the garlic and diced onion to the pan and heat until fragrant and lightly browned
Spread the tater tots evenly in a 9" X 13" pan
Spread the sausage, onions and green peppers over the tater tots
Mix 2 cups of milk with six eggs
Spread the egg mixture over the tater tots
Sprinkle the Cheese over the egg mixture
Mix 1/2 cup milk with the broccoli soup
Spread the soup mixture over the shredded cheese
Refrigerate overnight
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour

Submitter: 
Sapper26
Description: 
Easy breakfast casserole that transports well and is perfect for when friends and family spend the night.
