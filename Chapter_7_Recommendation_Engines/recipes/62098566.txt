Title:
Praline French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 1/2 cup half & half
1 tablespoon brown sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 inch sliced French bread to fit baking dish single layer
Topping:
1 tablespoon butter (no margerine)
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup maple syrup
3/4 cup chopped pecans, toasted

Directions:
Combine base ingredients in large bowl, stirring with a wire whisk until well blended.  Pour 1 cup egg mix into a greased 9x13 baking dish.  Place bread in dish and pour remaining egg mixture over bread.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.
The morning of prepare the topping.  Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat.  Add brown sugar and maple syrup, stirring until smooth.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for one minute, stirring constantly.  Stir in pecans and pour or spoon over the bread slices in the baking dish.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until set and golden.  Serve with additional maple syrup or as is.

Submitter: 
SACHABEAR
Description: 
A wonderful baked french toast prepared the night before and baked the next morning.  This makes an excellent holiday breakfast!
