Title:
Thai Green Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1033855.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp Peanut Oil
1/4 Cup Thai Green Curry Paste
3 Cloves Garlic, minced
1/4 Inch piece of Ginger, finely grated
2 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts, thinly sliced
27 Oz (2 Cans) of Coconut Milk
15 Oz Can Baby Corn Pieces - Preboiled and Strained - See Note
8 Oz Can Bamboo Shoots - Preboiled and Strained - See Note
6 Kaffir Lime Leaves, minced
2 Tbsp Fish Sauce
2 Tbsp Palm Sugar
2 Bell Peppers (1 Red, 1 Orange) - sliced into thin strips
1/4-1/2 Cup Thai Basil, sliced into thin strips

Directions:
Heat oil in a wok until it starts to shimmer
Add curry paste, garlic, and ginger, stirring together and letting fry until fragrant, Approx 2-3 mins.
Add chicken breast slices, stirring until coated.  Let cook halfway, with some pink still showing - 3-5 minutes.
Add coconut milk to chicken and spice mixture, and let it come to a slow simmer, stirring occasionally - 5 minutes
Stir in baby corn, bamboo shoots, kaffir lime leaves, fish sauce, and palm sugar, let come back to a simmer and cook an additional 5 minutes
Stir in bell pepper strips and cook until crisp-tender, 3 minutes.
Take curry off heat, stir in basil, and serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Galen Dobbs
Description: 
This is a recipe I've cobbled together over the years.  The vegetables aren't completely traditional, but they give the whole thing a good texture and variety.

Often asked for in my household, hopefully It'll become a favorite in yours.
