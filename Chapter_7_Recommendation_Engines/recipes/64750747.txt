Title:
Chicken Escabeche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp evoo
12.5 oz skinless, boneless chicken breast
3.5 oz carrots, julienned
3.5 oz red onion, thinly sliced
3.5 oz white onion, thinly sliced
1 garlic clove, minced
2 bay leaves
1/4 cup dry white wine
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/4 cup water
1 tsp achiote powder
1/2 tsp chili powder

Directions:
In a dutch oven, heat two tablespoons of oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and brown on one side, about 5 minutes.
Flip the breast. Add the carrot, two types of onion, and minced garlic. Stir well. Allow the carrot and onion soften and become fragrant while the chicken browns. Add the bay leaves, white wine, vinegar, and water. Mix well. Sprinkle the achiote powder and chili powder over everything and mix the ingredients until the powder mixes completely through.
Reduce heat to medium, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes or until the internal temperature of the chicken is 165Â°F.
