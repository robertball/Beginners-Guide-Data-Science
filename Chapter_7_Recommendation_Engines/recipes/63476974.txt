Title:
Potato Fish Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. butter
1 small onion, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
3 tbsp. flour
2 8oz. bottles clam juice
4 med potatoes, cubed
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp. thyme
1 lb. cod or other firm white fish, chunked
1 cup whole milk
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a 5 quart sauce pan, melt butter.  Add onion, celery and garlic cooking until tender. Add the flour, stiring to coat.  Add clam juice and 2 cups of water, whisk until smooth.
Add potatoes, thyme and bay leaf. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to a simmer and cook until potatoes are tender.
Add fish pieces.  Cover and cook until flaky, about 8 minutes. Pour in milk and cook until heated through. Remove and discard bay leaf.
