Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Scaredy Snacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983192.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
1 tablespoon shortening
2 cups chocolate chips
12 wooden sticks
Candy sprinkles

Directions:
In large microwave safe bowl, melt margarine, one cup of chocolate chips, and marshmallows on HIGH for about 2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute.
Stir vanilla into mixture until smooth. Add KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal, stirring until well coated.
Shape half cup of mixture into ball, inserting a wooden stick. Refrigerate until firm. Melt remaining cup of chocolate and shortening in a small sauce pan over low heat. Dip the bottom of each ball into chocolate and decorate with candy sprinkles. Refrigerate until firm.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
Crispy, chewy snacks on a stick are dipped in chocolate and decorated with your favorite candy sprinkles.
