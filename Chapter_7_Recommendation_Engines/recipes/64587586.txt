Title:
Sorghum, Quail Egg, Avocado, Kumato速and Buffalo Mozzarella Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3729779.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked sorghum grains
1/2 tsp Sriracha salt (or to taste)
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp vinegar
2 Tbsp avocado oil, divided
5 quail eggs
1 Buffalo mozzarella (125g)
1 Kumato速, diced
4 radishes, sliced
1 avocado
4 fresh basil leaves

Directions:
Boil the quail eggs for 4 minutes. Cover and let sit for 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a mixing bowl, add the sorghum, Sriracha salt, and cumin. Toss well. Drizzle the vinegar and half the oil over the sorghum mixture and toss well. Divide the mixture into two bowls.
Rinse the eggs with cold water. Peel and cut in half. Set them on top of the sorghum by putting five halves of egg in each bowl. With you hands, shred the mozzarella into pieces and put half in one bowl and the other half in the other bowl. Tear the basil leaves and divide them evenly over the two bowls. Evenly distribute the radish, avocado, and Kumato速 between the two bowls. Drizzle remaining oil over the two bowls.
Serve at room temperature.

Description: 
Sorghum grains tossed with Sriracha salt, vinegar, avocado oil, and cumin then topped with avocado, quail eggs, Kumato速 and buffalo mozzarella cheese for a one bowl lunch or dinner.
