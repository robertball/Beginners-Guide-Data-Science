Title:
Caesar Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb turkey burger
2 tablespoons fresh parsley
1/2 teaspoon onion salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 cup caesar dressing
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
4 slices of lettuce
4 slices of tomato
4 slices of onion 1/4 inch thick
4 kaiser rolls

Directions:
Combine turkey, parsley, onion salt, pepper and 4 tablespoons of caesar dressing in a bowl. Make into four 1/4lb patties. Cook patties and onion on a grill for 8 to 10 minutes until patties are no longer pink in the center. When patties are done mix parmesan cheese and remaining caesar dressing in a bowl and microwave for 30 seconds until cheese melts. Assemble the patties, tomato, lettuce, and onion on the buns. Top with the caesar dressing.

Description: 
Moist turkey burger and a cheesy caesar dressing is a healthy alternative to the everyday hamburger.
