Title:
Easy Vegan Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4614612.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
3 cups soy milk

Directions:
Bring water, sugar, and cocoa to a boil in a small saucepan. Pour in soy milk and stir until combined and heated through, about 3 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rose H.
Description: 
Easy vegan hot chocolate to keep you warm during the winter!
