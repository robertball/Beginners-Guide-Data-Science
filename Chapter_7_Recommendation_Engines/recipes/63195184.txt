Title:
Creamy Tortellini and Chicken Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups Swanson chicken broth
4 cups water
2 tablespoons chicken base
2/3 cup diced celery
2/3 cup diced onion
1/2 tablespoon minced garlic
4 chicken sausage links (garlic and herb flavor), chopped
4 cups fresh baby spinach
1 regular size package cheese tortellini (fresh from the refrigerated section)
1 cup light cream
1 cup milk
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
1.  In a large stock pot, combine the broth, water, chicken base, celery, onion, and garlic.  Turn burner on to medium high heat until just barely boiling, and then reduce heat to medium low.  Simmer for 25 minutes, stirring occassionally.
2.  Add the remaining ingredients, and continue to cook over medium low heat for another 15-20 minutes, or until the tortellini is al dente.
3.  Serve and enjoy!
