Title:
Chocolate Kahlua Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/562309.jpg
Ingredients:
Non-stick cooking spray for pan
Flour for dusting pan
1 pkg Devil's Food cake mix
1 3.9 oz. pkg chocolate instant pudding
3/4 cup Kahlua
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup vegetable oil
4 large eggs
For glaze:
1 cup sifted confectioners sugar
1/4 cup Kahlua

Directions:
Preheat over to 350F.
Lightly mist a 12-cup Bundt or tube pan with vegetable spray; then dust with flour.
Place cake mix, pudding mix, Kahlua, water, oil, and eggs in a large mixing bowl.  Blend with mixer. Batter should look thick and smooth.
Pour batter into pan and smooth with rubber spatula.
Bake cake 40-45 minutes.
Cool cake on wire rack.
For glaze, place confectioners sugar and Kulua in a small bowl and stir until well combined.
Pour glaze in icing bag, cut a small hole in tip of bag, and drizzle glaze over the top of cake.
