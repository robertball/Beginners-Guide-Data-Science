Title:
Spanakhopitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, peeled
2/3 cup olive oil
2 tsp ground cumin
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 lb frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well drained
4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup cream cheese or ricotta cheese
pepper
14 oz package filo pastry, containing 19x10 inch sheets

Directions:
Mince onion. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a frying pan, add onion, and cook gently until softened. Sprinkle in the cumin and stir 2 minutes, then stir in garlic and spinach. Remove from heat and stir in the 2 cheeses and pepper to taste. Brush a 12x9 inch dish with olive oil. Place 1 sheet of filo pastry in the dish, bringing up the sides ans letting the short edges hang over at either end. Brush the pastry with oil, the layer half the filo sheets this way. Spread the spinach and cheese mixture over the filo. then cover with the remaining sheets, brushing olive oil between them as before. Trim the overhanging edges of filo, then tuck in the edges to seal. Brush top with oil. With a sharp knife, cut through the layers to make 16 squares. Bake in a preheated 375 oven 30 minutes. Let cool 10 minutes, then cut the marked squares and remove from the dish. Serve hot or warm.
