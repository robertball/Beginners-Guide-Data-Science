Title:
Baked brown rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup long grain brown rice, uncooked
1 (10 3/4 oz.) can french onion soup
1 1/2 cups beef broth
1 (4 oz.) can sliced mushrooms
2 Tbsp. butter
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
1/2 cup frozen peas
1 Tbsp. chopped fresh thyme

Directions:
In 2 quart casserole dish, combine rice, soup, beef broth, mushrooms and butter.
Bake in preheated 350 degree oven, covered, 50 minutes.
Stir in cheese, peas and thyme.  Continue baking 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
DarcieA
Description: 
This recipe came from the back of store bought long grain brown rice.  It immediately caught my eye because it can be served as a side dish, or a vegetarian main dish if vegetable broth is used in place of beef broth.
