Title:
Easy Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1040747.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 onion diced
1 to 1 1/2 lb sharp cheddar cheese grated
2 16 oz cans chili beans
1 16 oz can kidney beans
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 teaspoon Old El Paso taco seasoning
1 cup Pace Picante (medium)
4 flour tortillas
4 green onions chopped
salt & pepper
sour cream

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease 9 x 13 casserole dish.
In large deep skillet, brown ground beef & onion, season with salt & pepper; drain grease.
On medium heat, Add taco seasoning & stir to coat, add picante sauce & heat through.
Add chili beans, kidney beans & cream of mushroom; mix well.
Place 2 tortillas to cover bottom of casserole dish & add meat mixture & a layer of cheese; Repeat.
Bake uncovered for 45 min or until cheese browns slightly.
Serve topped with sour cream & chopped green onion.
