Title:
Daddy's Cliff Like Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/818451.jpg
Ingredients:
2-1/4 cups rolled oats
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup crunchy peanut butter
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 cup raisins
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup honey
2 egg, beaten
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Line a sided cookie sheet with wax paper. Spray with non-stick cooking spray, set aside.
In a large bowl, mix together the oats, flour, salt, and raisins, set aside.
In a medium sauce pan pour in the honey, eggs, peanut butter, oil, vanilla, and brown sugar. Turn on burner to medium-low heat and stir until well combined. Add Oat/Flour mixture to sauce pan and continue to stir until the dough start to pull away from the sides, stir for 1 minute more.
Pour mixture into the prepared pan, spray another sheet of wax paper with non-stick cooking spray and place it on top of dough. Pat dough until even and smooth. Remove top piece of wax paper and allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving.
