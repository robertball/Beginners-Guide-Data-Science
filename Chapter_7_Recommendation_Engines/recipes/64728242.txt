Title:
Asian pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 1/2" pork chops
1/3c soy sauce
1/3c vinegar
1/2c sugar
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/3c water

Directions:
Brown chops.
Mix sauce together. Add to chops and simmer 1/2 hour.
