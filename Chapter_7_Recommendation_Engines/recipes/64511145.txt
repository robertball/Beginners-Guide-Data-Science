Title:
Cheesy Garlic Veggie Pasta & Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box Ronzoni Garden Delight Pasta (12 oz)
1 Pound Ground Sweet Sausage (16 oz)
4 Tablespoons Butter (1/4 Cup)
2 Tablespoons Minced Garlic
1/2 Cup Grated Cheese
Small Amount of Garlic Powder
1 Tablespoon Italian Seasoning
Small Amount of Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
Cook Pasta as per instructions on box.
While pasta is cooking, cook sweet sausage.  Crumble up while cooking.  When finished add Italian season and small amount of garlic powder.  Toss to coat.  Set sausage aside.
Drain pasta and set aside.  In pasta pot add butter and minced garlic.  Cook on med-high for a few minutes or until butter is melted.
Add pasta back into pot with the butter/garlic mixture.
Add grated cheese to pasta.
Stir pasta well until coated with butter, garlic and cheese.
Serve individual servings topped with some shredded mozzarella cheese
OPTIONS:
Add dried crushed red peppers to individual serving
Add Salt and/or pepper to taste
