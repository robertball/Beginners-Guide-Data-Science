Title:
Tito's Hammock  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1129774.jpg
Ingredients:
ice cubes
1 1/2 fluid ounces vodka
1/2 fluid ounce simple syrup
1/2 fluid ounce lemon juice
5 slices cucumber
2 slices lemon, or more to taste
1 splash club soda

Directions:
Fill a shaker with ice. Pour vodka, simple syrup, lemon juice, cucumber slices, and lemon slices into shaker; cover and shake until chilled. Pour cocktail into a Collins glass and top with a splash of club soda.

Submitter: 
Allrecipes
Description: 
To make this refreshing summer cocktail, vigorously shake vodka, simple syrup, and lemon juice with lemon and cucumber slices. Pour into a Collins glass and top with a splash of club soda. This simple and refreshing cocktail is a signature drink at the Tiny Lounge in Chicago.
