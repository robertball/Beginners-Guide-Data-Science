Title:
MINI CHIP SNOWBALL COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 STICKS BUTTER
3/4 CUP POWEDERED SUGAR
1 TBLS VANILLA
1/2 TSP SALT
3 CUPS FLOUR
2 CUPS SEMI SWEET CHOCOLATE CHIPS
1/2 CUP FINELY CHOPPED NUTS
POWEDERED SUGAR

Directions:
PREHEAT OVER TO 375
BEAT BUTTER, SUGAR, VANILLA AND SALT UNTIL CREAMY
GRADUALLY BEAT IN FLOUR; STIR IN NUTS AND CHOC CHIPS
SHAPE INTO TEASPOONS OF DOUGH INTO 1 1/4 INCH BALLS. PLACE ON UNGREASED COOKIE SHEET AND BAKE FOR 10-12 MINUTES UNTIL LIGHTLY BROWNED. SIFT POWEDERED SUGAR OVER THEM
