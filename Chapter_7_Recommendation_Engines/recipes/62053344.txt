Title:
RaRa's Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces softened cream cheese,
1 small tub cool whip,
1 box confectioners sugar,
1 small jar marshamllow cream,
food coloring (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together til smooth.  Chill 1 hour.  Serve with favorite fruit.  Strawberries are wonderful with this.  The food coloring makes a pretty presentation.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
I was wanting something different with my fruit and this just popped up, with several combinations from different recipes. Hope you enjoy it.
