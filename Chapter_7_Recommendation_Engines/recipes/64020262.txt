Title:
Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10-ounce packages frozen broccoli florets, thawed
2 cups cooked chicken breasts, torn in large pieces
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon curry powder
1/2 cup grated extra-sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Arrange broccoli in a greased 9X13 casserole dish. Arrange chicken pieces on top. Combine remaining ingredients except bread crumbs and cheese. Mix cheese and crumbs and sprinkle over top. Bake 25 minutes or until all the cheese has melted and is beginning to brown. Allow to stand for 10 minutes before serving.
