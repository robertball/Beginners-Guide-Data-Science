Title:
Easy Pleasing Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. ground beef
1 pkg. (6 oz.) Stove Top Stuffing Mix (Chicken or Turkey)
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup BBQ sauce or ketchup

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375ÂºF. Mix meat, stuffing mix, egg, and BBQ sauce/ketchup in bowl until just blended.
2.  Shape into loaf either in a 13x9-inch baking dish or a bread loaf pan.
3. Bake for 1 hour or until done.  At about the last 10 minutes of baking, top with remaining BBQ sauce/ketchup.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
This recipe never fails in my house.  It's very quick and easy to prep before putting in the oven.  Dinner gets done while I help with homework!
