Title:
Fluffy Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg, separated
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup flour
1 tablespoon sugar
3 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1.	Beat egg whites and milk at top mixer speed for 3 minutes. In separate bowl, beat egg yolk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Combine the ingredients at low speed until well blended.
2.	Sift the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. At low speed slowly add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Continue mixing for another 2 minutes or until all the ingredients are combined.
3.	Preheat ungreased pan or griddle to medium-high heat. Pour 1/4 c of the batter onto the griddle. Flip pancake after bubbles begin to form and the outer rim of the pancake appears cooked.
