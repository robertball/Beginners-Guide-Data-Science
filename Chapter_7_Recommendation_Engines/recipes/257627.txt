Title:
Keto Brie Snack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
2 ounces Brie cheese
1/4 cup fresh raspberries

Directions:
Preheat toaster oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Place walnuts in an oven-proof bowl; top with Brie cheese. Sprinkle raspberries over the top.
Bake in the preheated toaster oven until cheese has melted, 5 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
G Nani
Description: 
Melted Brie cheese with walnuts and raspberries make a quick keto diet-friendly snack.
