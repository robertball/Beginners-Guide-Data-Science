Title:
Casa Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2906756.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup milk
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, milk, and lime juice. Season with cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir in cilantro just before serving.

Description: 
Creamy dressing that you sometimes see as the house dressing at Mexican restaurants!
