Title:
Light Italian Lasagna Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 tortilla chips
1 cup meat sauce from the World's Best Lasagna recipe from this site made with 99% extra lean ground turkey breast and sweet italian turkey sausage.
1 cup reduced fat italian blend shredded cheese
1 jalapeno, seeded and chopped (optional)
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Place half the chips on one plate and the other half on a seond plate.
Spoon meat sauce on top of chips on each plate.
Sprinkle the cheese on top of each plate.  Spray with cooking spray and microwave each plate for 1 minute.  Sprinkle with chopped fresh jalapeno, if desired.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This is a delicious italian lasagna version of nachos.  It is a nice change of pace from the usual nachos.  Substitute light italian flatbread crackers for a healthier option to tortilla chips if desired.
