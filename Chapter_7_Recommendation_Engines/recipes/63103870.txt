Title:
Quick Chicken Divan ala Barbara Neely Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped broccoli
2 cooked boneless chicken breast halves, chopped
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon curry powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place cooked broccoli in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Top with the chicken.
In a small bowl, blend the cream of chicken soup, cream of mushroom soup, mayonnaise, and curry powder. Pour the mixture over the chicken. Top with buttered bread crumbs.
Bake 35 to 40 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and lightly browned.
