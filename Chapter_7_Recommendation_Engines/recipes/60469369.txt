Title:
Artichoke Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14oz artichoke hearts, chopped
8oz lump crab meat
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
1/4 cup ramano cheese, grated
1 cup mozarella cheese, shredded
1 garlic clove minced
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup sour cream
paprika

Directions:
Rinse and drain artichoke hearts; chop. mix all of the other ingredients, blending well in a 1-1/2 qt casserole dish. Dust top with paprika; bake at 325 for 25 minutes and serve hot with pita chips, veggies, crackers or sliced baguette.
