Title:
Moscow Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/186627.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce vanilla flavored vodka
3/4 fluid ounce amaretto liqueur
1 tablespoon instant hot chocolate mix
4 fluid ounces hot milk

Directions:
Into a coffee mug, pour in the vanilla vodka, amaretto, and add the hot chocolate mix. Pour in the hot milk and stir to blend well.

Submitter: 
Gordon
Description: 
This is hot chocolate with vanilla-flavored vodka and amaretto. It keeps you warm on a cold day. Try it topped with whipped cream!
