Title:
Lime Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups whole milk
1 cup frozen limeade concentrate, thawed
3 Tbsp. sugar
2 drops green food coloring

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a food processor or blender.
Pour into freezing bowl, turn machine on and let mix until mixture thickens (about 25-30 minutes).
If desired, put in freezer for 2 hours.
