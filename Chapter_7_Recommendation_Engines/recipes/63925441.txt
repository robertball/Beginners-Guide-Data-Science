Title:
Asian Cabbage & Tea-Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 green tea bags
1c long-grain white rice
1lb cabbage, chopped
1 md red bell pepper, finely chopped
1c edemame
1c slivered almonds
water
salt
sesame seeds

Directions:
Tie the teabags together and onto a wooden spoon.  Fill a small pot with 2c water and place on high heat.  Place the spoon in the pot so that the teabags are sitting in its water.
When the water starts boiling, remove the teabags, add rice, bring to a simmer, and cover.  Cook until the rice has absorbed all of the tea, which should take about 20 minutes.
Add sesame seeds and salt to the rice.
Meanwhile, pour water into a large pot until its bottom is covered with about 1" of water.  Place a steamer in the pot and the cabbage in the steamer.  Cover and bring to a boil.  Continue to boil for about 5-10 minutes or until the cabbage starts to cook but it still crisp.  Remove from heat.
Also meanwhile, place almonds and edemame in a small pot and add water until they are well-covered.  Bring to a boil, then remove from heat and drain.
Chop the edemame and almonds well but do not mash or puree.
Combine the edemame, almonds, pepper, and cabbage.  Serve over the rice.
