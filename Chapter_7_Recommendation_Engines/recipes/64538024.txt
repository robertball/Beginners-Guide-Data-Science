Title:
slow cook tater soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of potatoes and chopped
3/4 cup of onions
1 cup celery
1 cup carrots
4 cups of chicken broth
1/2 Tbsp. of salt
1/2 Tbsp. of pepper
4 Tbsp. of butter
1/4 cup of flour
1 cup milk
1 small sour cream
Shredded cheese
Bacon shredded

Directions:
Add the potatoes, onions, celery, carrots, chicken broth, salt and pepper to the slow cooker. Set it to low for 6 hours or high for 4 hours.
In a saucepan, combine the butter, flour, milk and sour cream. Whisk until thickened. When the slow cooker is done, pour in the thickened mix. Stir, cover and set the slow cooker to low for another 30 minutes.
Serve with shredded cheese, bacon
