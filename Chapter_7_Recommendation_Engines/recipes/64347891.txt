Title:
Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz fresh mushrooms
4 Tbsp butter
1 medium onion, chopped
1/4 cup flour
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp white pepper
1-1/4 cups water
10 oz can condensed chicken broth
1 cup half and half

Directions:
Slice 1 cup mushrooms and chop the rest.  Saute sliced mushrooms in 2 Tbsp butter until golden brown.  Remove with slotted spoon.
Saute chopped mushrooms and onion in remaining 2 Tbsp butter until onion is tender.  Stir in flour, salt and pepper.  Cook until bubbly.
Stir in water and broth and heat to boiling.  Boil for 1 minute.  Stir in half and half and sliced mushrooms.  Heat, but do not boil.
