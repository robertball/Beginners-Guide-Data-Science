Title:
Tortellini Soup III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6999844.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1 (16 ounce) package prepared cheese tortellini, thawed
2 teaspoons minced fresh chives
salt to taste

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, bring the water and chicken broth to a boil. Mix in the cheese tortellini, chives and salt. Cook 10 minutes, until the tortellini floats to the top.
