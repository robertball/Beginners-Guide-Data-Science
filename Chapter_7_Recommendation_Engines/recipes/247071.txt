Title:
Blueberry Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4472292.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup frozen blueberries
2/3 cup Shiraz red wine
1/3 cup grape juice

Directions:
Place frozen blueberries in a glass. Pour in red wine and grape juice.

Submitter: 
Criscelda
Description: 
My husband threw this together after a hot afternoon of cleaning the house. I loved it! It's a great alternative to lemonade.
