Title:
Avocado Breakfast Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 avocado
1.5 tbsp. lemon juice
2 tsp minced garlic
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp turmeric
Dash of cayenne pepper
2 tsp garlic salt
Salt and pepper to taste
1 Small roma tomato
4 pieces of turkey bacon or regular bacon
2 slices of bread
2 tbsp butter
2 slices of colby jack cheese
2 eggs

Directions:
1.  Pull out 4 pieces of bacon and fry on stove.  (No need to add oil, butter or pam.)  Fry until slightly crunchy.  Then cut into tiny pieces.
2.  Lightly butter both sides of bread, set aside.
3.  While waiting for bacon to cook; cut up tomato and set aside.
4.  Mix together ingredients 1-8 until avocado spread is creamy.  Then add chopped up bacon and tomato. Stir all together.
5.  On same pan that fried bacon in, fry eggs to over easy in leftover bacon grease.  Lightly salt and pepper.  Put eggs to side when they are done.
6.  Turn stove down slightly and place bread on skillet and lightly toast 1 side, then turn over and place a slice of cheese on toasted side, slather a generous portion of your avocado spread on top of the cheese and cover cheese toast with pan lid so cheese can melt quickly.  Cook until bottom is light golden brown.  Remove toast from pan with spatula and place over easy egg on top.

Submitter:

Description: 
My own twist on the traditional popular breakfast avocado toast.
