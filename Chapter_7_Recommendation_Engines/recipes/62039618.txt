Title:
Nonfat Lemon Frozen Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. fresh lemon juice
1 c. plain nonfat yogurt
2 tbsp. grated lemon peel
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. almond extract
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
3/4 c. white sugar
1/4 c. unbleached white flour

Directions:
Combine ingredients in saucepan and cook over medium high heat, stirring constantly, until thickened and reduced, about 15-20 minutes.  Place in airtight plastic container, then chill in refrigerator for 1 hour.  Then stir and place into freezer.  Stir again after 1 hour, then freeze overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
CLOUDSSUNRAIN
Description: 
Smooth, rich, and delicious nonfat lemon yogurt made by cooking lemon juice with nonfat yogurt until reduced, then chilling and freezing.  The best part is that you don't need a fancy ice cream freezer for this recipe!  This can also be used as a pie filling for a cream pie if it is not frozen.
