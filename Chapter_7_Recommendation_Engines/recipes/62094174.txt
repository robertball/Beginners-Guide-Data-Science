Title:
Italian Seafood stew w/Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup olive oil
1 large onion chopped
6 garlic cloves chopped
1/4 cup chopped celery
1 Tablespoon crushed red pepper flakes
3 Tablespoons chopped fresh thyme
1 28oz can whole tomatoes, crushed by hand
1 15 oz can diced tomatoes
2 cups dry white wine
8-12 Live mussels scrubbed and rinsed
1/2 lb medium shrimp, shelled and deveined
1/2 lb Bay scallops
1 dozen LIVE littleneck clams
1 lb linguine, cooked al dente
3 Tablespoons chopped fresh Italian Parsley
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large casserole dish or large deep skillet, heat olive oil over medium high heat. Add celery, onion, garlic and pepper flakes and saute for 6-8 minutes.
Add tomatoes, thyme and wine. Bring to a boil and then simmer for 30 minutes or until sauce has thickened.
Turn heat back up to medium high and add mussels, clams and scallops. After 7-8 minutes add shrimp and stir. Once shrimp have cooked through (2-3 minutes), take off heat. Discard any mussels or clams that have not opened
Serve over linguine, garnished with parmesan cheese and and chopped parsley.

Description: 
Zuppa Di Pesce means Fish Soup. My husband doesn't like fish, so I substituted some of his favorite shellfish with more tomatoes to make it chunky enough to serve over pasta.
