Title:
Mandarin Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/408405.jpg
Ingredients:
1 7 ounce container of spring mix lettuce
2 11 ounce cans of mandarin oranges
1 cup of toasted slivered almonds
1 cup of edamame (soy beans)
1/2 cup of snow peas
3 grilled and chopped chicken breasts
Trishie's Chinese-Style Salad Dressing

Directions:
in a large bowl place spring mix lettuce and grilled chopped chicken, toss together with Trishie's Chinese-Style Salad Dressing. Then top the lettuce and chicken in this order: snow peas, edamame, mandarin oranges, and slivered almonds.

Submitter: 
Sally Rogers
Description: 
This is my rendition of a salad from a very famous burger joint. Of course it's better, and has more toppings. Pair it with Trishie's Chinese-Style Salad Dressing recipe from this site, and you have a wonderful light dinner. Enjoy!
