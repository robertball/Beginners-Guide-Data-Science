Title:
Spinach Supli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 24 oz frozen chopped spinach
1 c shredded asiago
10 pieces uncooked bacon
6 garlic cloves
flavored bread crumbs, fine
6 egg yolks, beaten, add water till not thick
oil
Marinara Sauce

Directions:
Thaw Spinach, squeeze out excess water, set aside
Cook bacon until just crisp, reserve greese
In Food processor, mince together garlic and bacon
In Skillet, cook garlic bacon mix just until garlic is aromatic, remove from heat
Add garlic mix, and asiago to spinach, then add bacon greese until mix sticks well together.
Roll spinach into pin pong size balls, then roll in bread crumbs.
Once all balls are breaded, dip balls in yolk mix, and roll in crumbs again, repeat process once more, so that in the end the balls have been breaded 3 times.
Heat oil, and fry spinach balls until deep golden brown, cover with marinara sauce and serve hot.

Garlicy, Spinach and Asiago combined for a wonderful and addicting appetizer.
