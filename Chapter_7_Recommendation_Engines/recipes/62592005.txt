Title:
Bill's Beef Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup olive oil
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon minced garlic or garlic powder.(not salt)
3 tablespoons dried basil
1 1/2 tablespoons dried parsley flakes or Italian seasoning
1 teaspoon ground pepper
1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (optional)
8 ounces fresh sliced mushrooms

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend on high speed for 30 seconds until thoroughly mixed. Can also just be placed in a large zip lock bag and mixed thoroughly.
Pour marinade over desired type of meat (or add meat to the zip lock bag) and refrigerate for a minimum of 4 hours. Cook meat as desired.

Submitter: 
Billrob_15
Description: 
A Garlic and Soy based marinade excellent for steaks and roast beef.  Add 8oz. of fresh sliced mushrooms to the left over marinade and cook down for an extra tasty topping.Modified from: Best Steak Marinade in Existence
