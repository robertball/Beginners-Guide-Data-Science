Title:
Quinoa Salad Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/403328.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons quinoa
6 Cups green and red cabbage
1 package ramen noodles
1 can mandarine oranges
Top with your favorite citrus salad dressing

Directions:
Toast quinoa in a frying pan, until golden brown, about 5 minutes.  Shake frequently
Chop cabbage
Pour your favorite dressing on top
Top with toasted quinoa, broken Ramen noodles
and mandarine oranges

Submitter: 
Heidi Heninger Shaw
Description: 
This is a beautiful side dish that can be made in a jiffy.  Simply toast the quinoa and break up some ramen noodles and you'll have a dish that everyone will be asking about.
