Title:
Paige's Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 onions, chopped
1 ½ lbs. boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
2 (4 ounce) cans of chopped green chile peppers, mild
2 teaspoons ground cumin
2 teaspoons dried oregano
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon pepper
4 (14.5 ounce) cans great northern beans, drained
1 cup Sour Cream
1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream or Half n’ Half
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese - optional

Directions:
Saute` chicken and onion in olive oil until chicken is cooked through. Remove chicken and cut into ½ inch cubes.
In a large stock pot, combine chicken broth, chicken, onion, beans, green chilies, cumin, oregano, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Bring to a full boil, then simmer on low for 1 -3 hours.
30 minutes before serving, add sour cream and heavy whipping cream. Bring to boil to thicken, then reduce heat. Serve in bowls topped with shredded Monterey Jack cheese (optional).

Submitter: 
Paige
Description: 
A delicious twist on an old favorite! Add a little cayenne pepper if you like it hot! Hint: You can make and serve imediately, but the additional simmer time allows the flavors to blend.
Serve with garlic bread and a salad and Voila! You have a meal!
