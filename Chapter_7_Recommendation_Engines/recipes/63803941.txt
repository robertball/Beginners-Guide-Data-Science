Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup(s) uncooked onion(s), chopped
2 cup(s) canned yellow corn
4 cup(s) fat-free reduced-sodium chicken broth or vegetable broth
16 oz canned black beans, undrained
16 oz fat-free canned refried beans
16 oz canned diced tomatoes
1¼ oz reduced-sodium taco seasoning
10 oz Diced Tomatoes with Green Chilies

Directions:
1. Sauté onions until soft in large pot sprayed with nonstick cooking spray.
2. Add all ingredients to pot and simmer for 30-40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Debala
Description: 
Warm & spicy soup that can be vegetarian if you use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth.
