Title:
Vegan Pumpkin Spice Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/492671.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. gr. flaxseed
1/2 Cup water
1 1/2 Cups turbinado sugar (raw sugar)
1/2 Cup applesauce
1/2 Cup canola oil
2 Cups fresh pumpkin
3 Cups whole wheat flour
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. gr. ginger
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. baking powder
3/4 tsp. salt
1 cup walnuts (optional)
Sprinkle with oats or raw sugar for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix flaxseed and water until thinkened (about 2 min.)
In a large bowl, combine flaxseed mixture, raw sugar, applesuce, oil, and pumpkin.
In a medium bowl, stir together, flour, spices, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
Stir dry ingredients into wet until incorporated.
Fold in walnuts. (Optional)
Lightly grease (2) 8" X 4" or 4 mini loaf pans.
Divide the batter evenly into pans.
Sprinkle with oats or raw sugar, if desired.
Bake 8" X 4" pans for 60 min.
Bake mini loaf pans for 45 min.
Let stand for 20 min. before removing from pans
