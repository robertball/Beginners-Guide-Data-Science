Title:
Dog heads spaghetti sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
3 T olive oil
3 cloves of garlic chopped
1 large onion chopped
1 lb ground beef
1 lb mild sausage
4 t italian seasoning (dried)
2 t rosemary (dried)
1 1/2 t oregano
2 t salt (opt)
1/2 t pepper
1-29 oz can of tomatoe puree
1-18oz can of tomatoe paste (or small cans that = 18oz)

Directions:
Grab the biggest pot you can find REALLY BIG Melt butter with olive oil, chopped garlic and chopped onion Add meats and brown. Crumble them up as they cook Add spices Let simmer for 20 minutes Add 29 oz can of tomatoe puree AND two cans of water Add 18 oz can of tomatoe paste AND one can of water Cook on low for two hours or more - more is better! This can serve 12 and still have left overs. It freezes well in plastic bowls that can be thawed later and used in other pasta dishes. Enjoy!
