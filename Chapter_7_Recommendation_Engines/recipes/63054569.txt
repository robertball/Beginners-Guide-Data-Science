Title:
Tropical Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed cooked chicken
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 to 1 tsp. curry powder
1 can (20 oz.) pineapple chunks, drained
2 large firm bananas
1 can (11oz.) mandarin oranges, drained
1/2 cup flaked coconut
3/4 cup salted peanuts or cashews

Directions:
Place chicken and celery in a large bowl. Combine mayonnaise and curry powder and add to chicken and mix well. Cover and chill for at least 30 minutes. Before serving add fruits and coconut. Toss gently. Serve in a lettuce cup and sprinkle with the nuts.
