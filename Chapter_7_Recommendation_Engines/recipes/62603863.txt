Title:
Thai Beef Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds sirloin or tenderloin steak cut in strips
1/2 yellow onion sliced very thin
1/2 green pepper and red pepper sliced 1/4 in strips
1 T peanut oil
2 T fish sauce
1 T. sugar
3 T lime juice
1 can coconut milk
2-3 T. Red Curry paste
1/3 c peanuts
1/8- 1/2 t. garlic salt
1/8-1/4 t. curry power
1/8-1/4 t. garam masala
1/4 t. turmeric
couple drops (Sriracha)red rooster sauce
1 T. soy sauce
1 t. Worcestershire sauce
1-3 T peanut sauce if desired (we thought it really added a lot) or a couple tablespoons peanut butter

Directions:
In large Wok, heat 1 T peanut oil until it sizzles.  Add  onion and peppers.   Stir fry until tender.  Remove from pan.
Pour in 1 can coconut milk.  Heat to low boil  for 3-5 min.
Add:
fish sauce
Thai red curry paste, sugar and rest of spices to taste.
add beef and veggies cook about 10 min over med heat until it thickens and reduces and add peanuts and serve over cooked rice.

Submitter: 
Betty
Description: 
My son and I doctored up a recipe from a local upscale restaurant using what we had and liked.  We were both impressed and will make this again.
