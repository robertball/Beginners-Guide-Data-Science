Title:
Banana Split Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
ice as needed
2 fluid ounces white chocolate liqueur
1 fluid ounce strawberry vodka
1/2 fluid ounce banana liqueur
1/2 teaspoon chocolate syrup, or to taste
1 maraschino cherry

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; add white chocolate liqueur, strawberry vodka, creme de banana liqueur, and chocolate syrup. Cover and shake until the outside of shaker has frosted. Strain into a chilled glass and garnish with a maraschino cherry.
