Title:
Resa's Chocolate Peanut Butter Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. Can of Pumpkin
1 12 oz. Evaporated Milk
1/2 tsp. of salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
1/4 tsp. ground cloves
2 eggs
1  8-.55oz chocolate peanut butter cups
1  9-inch graham cracker pie crust

Directions:
Mix sugar, salt, cinnamon, ginger and cloves in small bowl. Beat eggs in large bowl. Stir in pumpkin and dry mix. Slowly pour in evaporated milk and mix.
Open up individual chocolate peanut butter cups, lay one by one upside down into bottom of graham cracker crust to fill bottom.
Pour mixture on top of chocolate peanut butter cups to fill pie crust but not overfill shell.
Bake in preheated 425 degree F over for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees, bake for 40-50 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Cool on wire rack for 2 hrs. Serve immediately or refrigerate.
