Title:
Sauteed scallops and vegetables over rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked jasmine rice
4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 large or 4 small shallots, copped
2 large cloves garlic, minced
1 cup matchstick carrots ( found in the salad aisle of the grocer)
1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1" pieces
1 pound sea scallops
4 tablespoos white wine (optional)
2 tablespoons lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Prepare rice according to package directions, adding the optional butter or margarine.  Set aside.
Heat oil and butter in  a large, nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add shallots, garlic, carrots and asparagus and saute until the shallots are translucent and the asparagus is crisp-tender.  Do not allow shallots and garlic to brown.
Using a slotted spoon, remove vegetables from pan and reserve.  Increase heat to medium high . Add scallops to same pan and sear , turning once, until browned on both sides.
Add white wine to pan  and cook for one minute (optional).
Reduce heat to low.
Return the shallot/garlic/carrot/asparagus mixture to the pan, along with the cooked rice.  Stir to coat rice thoroughly and heat through.

Description: 
Sauteed sea scallops, matchstick carrots,fresh asparagus and jasmine rice combine to make a beautiful and quick one dish meal equally  suitable for a  fast weekday family meal or  for a special occasion.
