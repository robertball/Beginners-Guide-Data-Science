Title:
Clean Eating Oatmeal Protein Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/676261.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dry, uncooked oatmeal
1 Tbsp almond butter
1 scoop protein powder, vanilla is best
1 Tbsp honey or agave nectar
1 Tbsp flax seed - whole or meal
1 cup almond milk/soy milk
1/2 Tsp cinnamon
ICE

Directions:
Throw it all in a blender with some ice and blend!

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description:
