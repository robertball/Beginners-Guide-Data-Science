Title:
Laura's Power Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Quinoa (2 cups dry)
Kale (2 bags)
2 green peppers
2 red or yellow peppers
3 oz goat cheese
2 granny smith apples
1/2 C dried cranberries
1 TB Champagne Vinagrette

Directions:
Cook quinoa - layer in bottom of tupperware
Dice all other ingredients (except Kale) and mix- 2nd layer
Lightly chop Kale and add to top and put on lid

Submitter: 
Laura Leh
Description: 
This is a copy of a wawa salad I had.  Loved it.
