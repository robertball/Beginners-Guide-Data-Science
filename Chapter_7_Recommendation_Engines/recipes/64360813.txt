Title:
Fried Cabbage II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 slices bacon, chopped
1 chopped onion
1 head cabbage, cut into thin wedges
2 tablespoons water
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 pinch white sugar
salt and pepper to taste
1 tablespoon cider vinegar

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Remove bacon, and set aside.
Cook onion in the hot bacon grease until tender. Add cabbage, and stir in water, chicken broth, sugar, salt, and pepper. Cover to help wilt cabbage. Cook until cabbage wilts, about 15 minutes. Stir in bacon. Splash with vinegar before serving.
