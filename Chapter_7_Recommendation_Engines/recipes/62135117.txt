Title:
Strawberry Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces frozen canned lemonade concentrate, unthawed
3/4 Cup Daily's strawberry mix (for daiquiris)(found with other mixers in grocery store)
Water
fresh or frozen strawberries, optional

Directions:
In a 2-quart container, add lemonade concentrate and strawberry mix.  Stir to combine.  Fill container with water, stir, and refrigerate to chill.  May float strawberries in pitcher, if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a simple and delicious beverage that all seem to enjoy.
