Title:
Decorating Caramel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1/4 c. water
1/4 c. corn syrup

Directions:
Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring only until the sugar is dissolved.  Cook until the mixture is golden brown.  Immediately remove from heat and place pan in cold water to stop cooking.  Remove and let caramel cool slightly.  Spray parchment paper with non stick cooking spray and make different designs as you wish.  Freeze for 2-3 minutes and remove.  To make bowls, flip over a custard bowl and spray with non stick cooking spray.  Then drizzle over bowl back and forth.  Freeze for 2-3 minutes and remove from bowl.

Submitter: 
Traci Caha Myers
Description: 
For use on cakes, to make candy baskets, etc..
