Title:
Rummy Bears Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Gummy Bears
1 C Captain Morgan Spiced Run

Directions:
Pour gummy bears into a sealable container (such as a Gladware bowl).
Pour the Captain Morgan over the gummy bears until the are covered.
Store in the refrigerator overnight to allow the gummy bears to drink, er absorb, the rum.
Enjoy!
