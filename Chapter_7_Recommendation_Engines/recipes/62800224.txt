Title:
KOREAN BBQ WINGS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 LBS.WINGS
12 CUPS WATER
MARINADE:
1 CUP HOISIN SAUCE
4 TBSP.SOY SAUCE
3 TBSP.WATER
3 TBSP.HONEY
4 TBSP.FRESH GINGER
3 CLOVES GARLIC,MINCED

Directions:
Boil wins in water for 30 min.then remove from heat and set aside.Place all ingredients in a heavy bag.
Place cooked wings in the bag and massage untill sauce has evenly coated wings.Refridgerate overnight or no less than 4 hours.
Heat BBQ to 400.Spray grill with cooking spray and grill wings untill golden and crisp,about 20 min.
