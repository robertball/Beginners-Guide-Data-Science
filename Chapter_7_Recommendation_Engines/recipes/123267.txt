Title:
Savory Biscuit Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985795.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon dried minced onion
1 1/2 teaspoons dried parsley flakes
1 (12 ounce) package refrigerated biscuits

Directions:
In a bowl, combine butter, cheese, onion and parsley. Cut biscuits into quarters; roll in butter mixture. Place in a greased 15-in. x 10-in. x 2-in. baking pan; let stand for 25 minutes. Bake at 400 degrees F for 8 minutes or until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Wendy  Chilton
Description: 
'These light, golden puffs are super simple to make, and their flavor is oh-so-good,' informs Wendy Chilton of Bookeland, Texas. 'Their small size makes them easy to munch, and they're wonderful warm or cold.'
