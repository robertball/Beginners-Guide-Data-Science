Title:
Glazed Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 egg whites
1 Cup sugar
1 lb. pecans
1 stick butter or margarine

Directions:
Beat the egg whites until stiff.
Add 1 Cup of sugar gradually and continue beating.
Fold in the pecans.
Melt the stick of butter in a jellyroll pan or cookie sheet with sides.
Spread the pecan mixture evenly in the pan over the melted butter.
Bake 325 degrees for 45 minutes.  Turn every 15 minutes. and sprinkle lightly with salt.
Store in air tight container.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
A bowl of Glazed Pecans, a few tea sandwiches and a fruit salad and you have treats for the bridge club, planning committee or your ladies tennis team.
Great for hostess gift in a pretty bowl or attractive glass jar.
