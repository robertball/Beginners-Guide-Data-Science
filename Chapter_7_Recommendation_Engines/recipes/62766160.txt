Black Bean, Corn and Turkey Chili Recipe

Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef, lean
1/3 cup PaceÂ® Chunky Salsa
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 1/3 cup frozen whole kernel corn
1 can red kidney beans
1 can pinto beans
1 sm can chopped green chiles
3 T ranch seasoning mix
1 cap full Tone's Rosemary Garlic Seasoning

1 sm can tomato juice
splash of red wine

Directions:
Brown the ground beef in a fry pan.
Meanwhile, heat in large saucepan: everything else.  When ground beef is done add it to the saucepan.  Heat and simmer for aprox 45 mins.
