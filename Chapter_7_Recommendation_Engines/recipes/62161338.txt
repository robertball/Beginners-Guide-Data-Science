Title:
Mocha Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bananas, frozen and sliced
3/4 cup plain non-fat yogurt
3/4 cup milk or soy milk
3 tsp. espresso powder
1 1/2 tbsp malted milk powder
1 tsp. honey, agave nectar or sweetener of your choice
1 1/2 tbsp ground flaxmeal
Whipped cream and cocoa powder for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend for 30 seconds or until desired consistency.
Pour into glasses and garnish with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa powder, if desired.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
A quick and easy, frosty pick-me-up breakfast or snack, this smoothie is flavored with frozen banana, espresso powder and chocolate.
