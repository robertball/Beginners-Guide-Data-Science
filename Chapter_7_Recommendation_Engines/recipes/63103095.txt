Title:
Egg Drop Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1036867.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 t. olive oil
1 T. minced onion, fresh or dried
1 whole egg + 1 T. egg white
chives or green onions

Directions:
Bring chicken broth, olive oil and minced onion to a boil. Simmer 5 minutes until onion is tender.
Lightly beat eggs until well mixed.
Reduce broth to a medium simmer and slowly stream in the egg mixture, stirring constantly.
Adjust seasoning if necessary.
Sprinkle with fresh chives or green onions.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
Delicious, simple soup w/o the addition of the traditional corn starch.
