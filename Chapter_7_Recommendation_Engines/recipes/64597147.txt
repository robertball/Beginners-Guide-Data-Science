Title:
Loaded Baked Potato Chunks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Russet Potatoes
1 TBS Olive Oil
salt / pepper
Toppings
2 TBS Real Bacon Bits ( or to taste )
1/4 Cup Sour Cream ( or to taste )
1/4 Green Onion ( or to taste )
1/4 Grated Cheddar Cheese ( or to taste )
1/2 Cup Chopped Broccoli ( optional )

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400
Scrub potatoes and poke a few times with a fork.  Please potatoes in the microwave for 6 minutes.  Remove from microwave and let cool enough to cut , about 15 minutes.  Once cooled,  cut potatoes into bit sized pieces.  Toss with olive oil and salt and pepper.  Place on a greased cookie sheet and bake in the oven for 35 minutes turning every 15 minutes.  Sprinkle the cheese over the potatoes and bake for another 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Let cool for a few minutes and top with Real Bacon Bits, Sour Cream, Green Onion and the Chopped Cooked Broccoli ( optional )

This loaded potato recipe has it all.  The crispy oven baked potato skins with soft inside is loaded up with all your favorite baked potato toppings.
