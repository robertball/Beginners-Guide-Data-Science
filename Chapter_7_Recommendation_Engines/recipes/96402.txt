Title:
Light Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
Cooking spray
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 cup diced green or yellow bell pepper
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 (20 ounce) package JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE® Extra Lean Ground Turkey Breast
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon tomato paste
3/4 teaspoon salt (optional)
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
5 kaiser rolls, split, toasted

Directions:
Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray; place over medium heat. Add onion, bell pepper and garlic; cook 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Crumble turkey into skillet; cook 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add tomato sauce, Worcestershire, tomato paste, salt and pepper. Simmer uncovered 10 to 15 minutes or until sauce thickens. Spoon mixture onto bottom of rolls; close with roll tops.

Submitter: 
JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE®
Description: 
What could be better than a classic sloppy joe made with turkey? Served on delicious rolls, these joes will please everyone in the family and keep them coming back for more.
