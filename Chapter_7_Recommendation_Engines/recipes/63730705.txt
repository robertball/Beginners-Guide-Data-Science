Title:
Stuffed Green Pepper Caserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1035934.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger, browned/drained
2 14.5 oz cans diced tomatoes w/garlic & basil
2 green peppers, diced
1 onion, diced
1 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. oregeno
1/2 tsp. salt
2C Minute Rice
1C water
2C Cheddar Cheese, shredded

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350 degrees. In a large skillet combine browned hamburger, canned tomatoes, green peppers, onion, galic powder, oregano and salt.  Simmer for 15 minutes.  Add water and rice.  Stir.  Turn off heat. Add 1 1/2 cups cheese and stir.  Put mixture in 8x8 pan.  Cover and bake in oven for 15 minutes.  Remove cover. Add remaining cheese and bake for 15 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Kat
Description: 
This is one of my favorite recipies that my dad used to make.  We ate it as a main dish with bread on the side but it is also great as a side dish.
