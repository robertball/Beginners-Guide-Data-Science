Title:
A better smoky martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4524775.jpg
Ingredients:
3oz dry gin
3/4oz dry vermouth
1/4oz scotch
2 drops of orange bitters
2 inch piece of lemon peel

Directions:
Add first four ingredients to shaker bottle.
Fill with ice, cover, and shake with all your might until freezing cold.
Drain into chilled martini glass.
Twist lemon peel over the glass to release the oil, and skim peel along rim of glass.
Float lemon peel in drink.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kristina Pauliukonis McGrath
Description: 
A spin on the classic smoky martini, by adding vermouth and a dash of orange bitters. Oh, so good and smooth! Perfect for that much needed cocktail. It's a big one though, so you likely won't need a second!
