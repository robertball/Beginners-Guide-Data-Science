Title:
Sweet Grass Special Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 2 large red apples
*1 large orange
*1 teaspoon certified organic honey
*pinch finely ground cinnamon
* 1 ounce shot of freshf wheatgrass juice

Directions:
Combine apple, orange and wheatgrass juices in a pitcher and stir to combine. Warm honey until it liquefies and mix into juices. Pour into individual glasses and dust lightly with cinnamon. Enjoy!
