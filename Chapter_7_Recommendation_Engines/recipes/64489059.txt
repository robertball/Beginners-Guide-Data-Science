Title:
Dilly Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2572461.jpg
Ingredients:
Two Large Cucumbers (peeled and sliced thin)
2 Tbsp White Vinegar
Â¼ Cup Sour Cream
One Small Onion (minced)
One Bunch of Dill (chopped)
1 tsp Sugar

Directions:
Place slices of cucumber in a shallow bowl.
In another bowl, combine remaining ingredients for the dressing. Make sure that everything that needs to be chopped or minced is chopped or minced!
Pour dressing over cucumber slices, stirring to coat well.
Refrigerate for one hour and serve cold.

Submitter: 
Chef AidF
Description: 
This is one of my only cold recipes, but it's very refreshing and crisp and can be served to accompany a variety of main dishes.
