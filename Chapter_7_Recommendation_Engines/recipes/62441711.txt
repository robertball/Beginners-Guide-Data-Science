Title:
Lemon-Glazed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lemons
5 chicken breasts
Salt and pepper
4 garlic cloves
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1 small onion, sliced
Lemon Glaze:
1 cup lemon curd
1- 1 ½ cups white wine vinegar (depending on vinegar strength)
3 Tbsp. honey

Directions:
Zest lemons, catch zest in a bowl, and slice lemons ¼ inch thick.
Put tin foil on cookie sheet and brush with olive oil. Place chicken breasts on cookie sheet.
Salt and pepper chicken breasts and drizzle with a little olive oil.
Scatter lemon slices, garlic bulb cloves and onion on chicken.
Cook chicken for 30-40 minutes at 400 F.
Make glaze and pour 1/3 on chicken breasts (let it cook and thicken on chicken).
Cook remaining sauce in saucepan until thickens and pour over chicken just before serving.
