Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds fresh ground beef
1 quart tomato juice (29 ounce)
1 can tomato puree (15 ounces)
1 can red kidney beans (15 ounces)
1 can pinto beans (15 ounces)
1 1/2 cups of onion (medium-large)
1/4 cup dices green bell pepper
1/4 cup chili powder (use less for milder chili)
1 teaspoon ground cumin (use more for real flavor)
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic power
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried orgeano
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown ground beef.  Drain off the fat (if any).  Mix beef and ingredients in a pot (6 quart pot) and cover.  Simmer for 1 to 2 hours, stirring frequently.
