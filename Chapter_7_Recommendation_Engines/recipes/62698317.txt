Title:
Sky High Brunch Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package frozen puff pastry - thawed (2 blocks)
6 eggs
1 cup ricotta cheese
dash hot pepper sauce
2 package frozen spinach - thawed and well drained
1 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheddar cheese
1 red pepper cut up in pieces
1/4 cup real bacon bits

Directions:
Roll 1 crust to 12 inches
Roll other crust to 11 inches
Spray with oil in 12 inch spring form pan
Layer 12 inch crust in pan (including sides)
Whisk the eggs and reserve 1 tbsp
Mix eggs, spinach, riccotta cheese, hot sauce
Layer 1/2 bacon bits
1/2 cheese
1/2 spinach mixture
1/2 red pepper
Repeat all layers
Add top crust and seal
Brush egg on top of crust
Make 4 slits on top
Cook at 400F for 45 to 55 minutes
