Title:
Blackberry-Cucumber Vodka Tonic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7424152.jpg
Ingredients:
4 slices cucumber
5 fresh blackberries
1 cup crushed ice
1 1/2 ounces vodka
3 ounces tonic water

Directions:
Muddle 3 slices of cucumber and 4 blackberries in the bottom of a rocks glass. Fill glass with crushed ice and add vodka. Top with tonic water and stir to combine. Garnish with remaining slice of cucumber and blackberry.

Submitter: 
France C
Description: 
This vodka and cucumber cocktail is a refreshing and colorful spin on the classic cocktail!
