Title:
Refreshing Cucumber Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1493463.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 bunch fresh spinach, minced
1 cucumber, cubed
1 tomato, cubed
1/2 small red onion, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 lemon, juiced
1/4 cup olive oil
1 teaspoon sea salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Mix spinach, cucumber, tomato, red onion, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate, 1 hour to overnight.

Submitter: 
Walter Masten
Description: 
Easy to make low calorie salsa that is great with fish tacos.
