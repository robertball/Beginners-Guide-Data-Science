Title:
Upside Down Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3c AP flour
1T sugar
1t salt
3/4c cold butter, cubed
1/3c shortening, cubed
4-6T cold water
1/2c packed brown sugar
1/4c butter, melted
1c pecan halves
1c sugar
1/3c AP flour
2T butter, melted
1/4t cinnamon
8c thinly sliced peeled tart apples

Directions:
Combine flour, sugar, and salt in food process and pulse until blended.
Add cold butter and shortening.
Pulse until mixture resembles crumbs.
Gradually add water until dough forms a ball.
Divide dough in half so that one portion is slightly larger than the other.
Wrap each half in plastic wrap.
Refrigerate dough 45 minutes.
Coat a 9" deep dish pie plate with cooking spray.
Line bottom and sides of plate with parchment paper.
Spray paper and set aside.
In a small bowl, combine brown sugar and butter.
Stir in pecans.
Arrange in the bottom of pie plate with rounded side of pecans face down.
Roll out larger piece of dough on floured surface to fit pie plate.
Press crust against pecans and plate; trim edges.
Combine flour, butter, and cinnamon in large bowl.
Add apples and toss to coat.
Fill crust.
Roll out other dough and fit to top of pie; place on top.
Trim and seal edges; cut slits in pastry.
Place a foil-lined baking sheet on a rack below the pie.
Bake @ 375 for 60-70 minutes.
Loosen parchment paper and invert pie onto serving plate.
Cool 15 minutes before serving.
