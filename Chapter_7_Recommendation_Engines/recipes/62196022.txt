Title:
Thanksgiving Molded Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 env. unflavored gelatin
1-1/2 cup hot cranberry juice cocktail
1/3 cup sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/3 cup lemon juice
1 cup cold cranberry juice cocktail
3/4 cup chopped celery
1-1/2 cup chopped unpeeled apple
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Soften gelatin in 3/4 cup cold water. Add hot cranberry juice, sugar, and salt; stir until dissolved. Add lemon juice and cold cranberry juice; cool slightly.
Poor 3/4 cup of mixture in bottom of 2-1/2 quart mold. Chill until firm.
Chill remaining mixture until it begins to set, then fold in remainder of ingredients except mayonnaise and sour cream. Add to mold and chill.
Mix together mayonnaise and sour cream for dressing.
