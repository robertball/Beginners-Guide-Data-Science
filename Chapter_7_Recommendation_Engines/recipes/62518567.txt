Title:
Candied Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/511259.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pinches salt
3 pound carrots, cut into 2 inch pieces
6 tablespoons butter, diced
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
3 pinches ground black pepper
Cinnamon to taste

Directions:
Fill large pot with water, stir in salt.
Place carrots in water and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to a high simmer and cook about 20 to 30 minutes.  Do not cook the carrots to a mushy stage!
Drain the carrots, reduce the heat to the lowest possible setting and return the carrots to the pan. Stir in butter,  brown sugar, salt and pepper. Cook for about 3 to 5 minutes, until sugar is bubbly. Serve hot!
