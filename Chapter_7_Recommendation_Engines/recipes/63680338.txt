Title:
Pear Crush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 med. pears, stalks removed
4 radishes, trimmed
3 stalks of celery
1c. crushed ice

Directions:
Process pears, radishes, & celery through Juice Fountain™ using spedd 5.
Scoop ice into 4 glasses, pour in juice, mix well to combine. Serve Immediately.

Submitter:

Description:
