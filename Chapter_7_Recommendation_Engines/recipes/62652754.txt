Title:
Warm Orange Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/589175.jpg
Ingredients:
Juice of two medium oranges (approx 3/4 Cup)
1/3 C white sugar
3 TBSP butter
pinch of salt
3/4 C cold water
2 1/2 TBSP cornstarch
2 TBSP finely shredded orange rind
2 oz of Triple Sec or other orange flavored liquor (optional)

Directions:
Bring the juice, sugar, butter and salt to a hard simmer. Mix the water and cornstarch together (no lumps) and add to the lightly boiling juice mixture. Once it begins to thicken, take it off the heat and add the triple sec and the orange rind. Serve warm.
