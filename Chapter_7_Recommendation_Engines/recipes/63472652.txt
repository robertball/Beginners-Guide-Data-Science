Title:
Barbecue Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb whole shrimp
1/2 ts cayenne
1/2 ts pepper
3 tb Worcestershire
1/2 cup olive oil
6 cloves garlic
1 stick of butter
1 ts salt
1 tbs Italian seasoning
2 lemons
French bread

Directions:
Melt butter in a shallow baking pan. Add 1 1/2 squeezed lemon & everything except shrimp. Let simmer 5 minutes.
Wash shrimp (leave shells on) place in pan & baste. Bake at 350 degree until shrimp are pink on top 5-8 minutes.
Turn shrimp, salt & pepper lightly, and bake 10 minutes.
Squeeze 1/2 lemon & a few dashes Worcestershire on top, serve with French bread for dipping that great sauce.
