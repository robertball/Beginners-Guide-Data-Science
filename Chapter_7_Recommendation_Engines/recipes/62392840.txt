Title:
Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups apple cider
3/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 cup packed dark brown sugar
2-12 ounce bags fresh or frozen cranberries
2 1/2 teaspoons ground allspice
orange zest

Directions:
In a saucepan, stir together the apple cider, white and brown sugar, allspice, cloves, nutmeg and cranberries. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 15 minutes, until most of the cranberries have popped.
Use a stick or immersion blender to smooth out the consistency if desired.
Continue cooking until the sauce reaches the desired thickness.
Top with orange zest before serving.

Submitter: 
Drizzler
Description: 
This cranberry sauce is extremely flavorful and great for the big turkey day feast.
