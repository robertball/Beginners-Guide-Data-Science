Title:
Mustard Based BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup prepared yellow mustard
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup light brown sugar
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
ground black pepper to taste
1/2 cup diced onions
1 tsp liquid smoke
1 tbls garlic pwdr or 2 cloves
1 tbls worcestershire

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, stir together all ingredients. Season with black pepper. Bring to a boil, and let simmer for 5 minutes. Pour over cooked pulled pork or beef. If you want more flavor, let the meat simmer in the sauce for about 30 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a great version of the traditional mustard based bbq sauce.  Most sauces are ketchup based, but in the South we prefer the mustard variation.  You oughta try it at least once.Modified from: Mustard Based BBQ Sauce
