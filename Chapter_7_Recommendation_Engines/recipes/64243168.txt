Title:
Pumpkin Butter Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dry pancake mix
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 egg
1/2 cup Pumpkin Butter (private selection)
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
1/3 cup milk

Directions:
In a large medium bowl, stir together pancake mix and cinnamon. Add in the egg, pumpkin butter, lemon juice and milk; stir until smooth.
Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using about 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

Submitter: 
Frida Rodriguez
Description: 
This is a very quick, yet delicious recipe for when you want something other than some simple or regular pancakes. Anyone who loves pumpkin anything will love this.
