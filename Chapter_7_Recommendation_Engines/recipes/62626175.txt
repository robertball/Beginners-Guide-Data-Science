Title:
Light As Air Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
3 1/2 tsp baking powder
pinch salt
1/4 cold butter
1/2 cup ginger ale
2 Tbsp sour cream
2 Tbsp mayonnaise
Topping
1 Tbsp melted butter
1 Tbsp sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Mix flour, baking powder, salt in a bowl. Cut in butter until mixtures resembles coarse crumbs.
In another bowl, mix ginger ale, sour cream and mayo.
Stir wet ingredients into dry ingredients until just moistened. Turn onto a lightly floured surface and knead 4 - 5 times.
Pat out to 3/4 inch thickness, cut with biscuit cutter or sharp edge cookie cutter.
Place 2 inches apart on parchment lined cookie sheet. Brush with melted butter and sprinkle with sugar.
Bake 9 - 11 minutes at 400 degrees, or until lightly browned. Best served warm.
