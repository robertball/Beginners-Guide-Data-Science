Title:
Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 pound pot roast
Flour mixture: Flour, garlic salt, dried rosemary
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 to 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
1 cup beef broth
4 carrots
4 golden potatoes
1 stalk celery
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup red wine (optional)

Directions:
Lightly coat pot roast with mixture of flour, garlic salt and dried rosemary. Sear on all sides in pan with unsalted butter.
In a slow cooker, mix all remaining ingredients. Place pot roast in slow cooker.
Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.

Submitter: 
Laurie Sueko Lau
Description: 
This simple pot roast makes its own gravy in the slow cooker.Modified from: Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast
