Title:
Grandma Elizabeth's Peanut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white sugar
1 cup white Karo syrup
1/4 cup water
1 1/2 cups raw peanuts (may add up to 2 cups peanuts)
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Combine sugar, syrup, and water.  Cook until forms a thread.
Add peanuts and cook until mixture turns dark.
Remove from heat.  Add soda, salt and butter (mixture will foam up).
Stir well and pour immediately pour into a greased cookie sheet.
Allow to cool and break into pieces.

Submitter: 
Patricia Canerdy
Description: 
My husband's grandmother made this every year at Christmas.  It has become a family tradition at our house.
