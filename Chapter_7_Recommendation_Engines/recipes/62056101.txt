Title:
Chicken Breast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups uncooked saffron rice
2 1/2 cups chicken broth
4 boneless chicken breast halves
1 can cream soup (chicken, mushroom or celery)
1 packet dry onion soup mix

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 375.
2.  Spray bottom of of 9 x 13 baking dish with non-stick spray.
3.  Pour rice on bottom of pan.
4.  Lay chicken breat over rice.
5.  Pour chicken broth in pan.
6.  Spoon cream soup over chicken breasts.
7.  Sprinkle dry onion soup mix over mixture.
8.  Bake for 1.5 hours or until chicken and rice are done.  Stir rice occassionally during baking time to ensure rice cooks evenly and chicken does not dry out.

Submitter: 
Michelle A
Description: 
Chicken, cream soup and rice meld together for the perfect comfort casserole.  I usually double this recipe because the rice is so delicious!
