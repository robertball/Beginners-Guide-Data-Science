Title:
"Best of Both Worlds" Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/449411.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Roma Tomatoes
1 Bell Pepper (Small)
2 Avocados
2 Jalapenos
1 Serrano Pepper
1 Small Red Onion
1 Can Black Beans
1 Can Whole Kernel Corn
Olive Oil
Garlic Salt
Garlic Pepper
Garlic Powder
Season All

Directions:
Dice your tomatoes and avocados in large pieces (about 1/4" pieces) and add to bowl.
Dice your red onion, bell pepper, serrano, and jalapenos into small pieces (about 1/8" pieces")and add to bowl.
Stir in corn, black beans.
Add olive oil until you reach your desired consistency.
Add season all, garlic powder,garlic salt and garlic pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Cory Price
Description: 
I took my favorite parts of both Tomato and Bean Salsa and combined them to make my new favorite!
