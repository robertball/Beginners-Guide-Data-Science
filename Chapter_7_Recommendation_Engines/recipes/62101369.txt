Title:
Raisin Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups distilled white vinegar
5 1/4 cups water
2 1/2 cups packed light brown sugar
2 tbsp + 1 tsp dry mustard
2 tbsp + 1 tsp flour
1 1/3 cups raisins

Directions:
Mix the brown sugar, dry mustard, and flour in a mixing bowl and set aside. In a medium saucepan, combine the raisins, vinegar, and water and then stir in the dry ingredients.  Cook uncovered over medium high heat until sauce comes to a boil.  Lower the heat to medium low and simmer for about 1 hour or until the sauce becomes thickened to a syrup consistency.

Submitter: 
BECKY M
Description: 
Raisin Sauce has always been a tradition in my Lithuanian family when ham is served. This recipe has been passed down 3 generations. It takes a little time but is well worth it!
