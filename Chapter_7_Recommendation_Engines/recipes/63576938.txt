Title:
Molasses Crinkle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c soft shortening
2 c packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 c molasses
4 1/2 c flour
4 tsp soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cloves
2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp ginger
7 oz crystallized ginger (The Ginger People)

Directions:
Mix shortening, sugar, eggs and molasses.
Blend all the dry ingredients, including crystalized ginger, together before stirring them into the above mixture.
You may wish to chill dough for an hour or so before rolling nto balls the size of large walnuts.
Roll in granulated sugar.
Place on cookie sheet and bake at 375* or 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Classic molasses cookies which children and men especially seem to love.  I remember making dozens and dozens of these for The Harvest Crew  every July and August during our Pacific Northwest Wheat Harvest.
