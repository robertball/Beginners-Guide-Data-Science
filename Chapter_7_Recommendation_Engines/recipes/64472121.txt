Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2477694.jpg
Ingredients:
Smoothie filling:
1 banana,
1 cup orange juice,
2,5 cup kale
Â½ cup yoghurt
8 gelatin sheets
Crust:
10 digestive cookies
6 tbsp apple sauce

Directions:
In a blender blend banana, orange juice, kale and yoghurt until smooth.
Crush cookies and mix with apple sauce in separate bowl.
Line a cake tin with parchment paper and smooth the crust till it's thin and even.
Add the gelatin sheets as instructed on the package and refrigerate for few hours until the cake has set.
Cut to bitesized and enjoy!
