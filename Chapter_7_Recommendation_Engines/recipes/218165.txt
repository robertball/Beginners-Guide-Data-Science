Title:
Honey Peanut Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980615.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup JIF® Creamy Peanut Butter
1/4 cup seasoned rice vinegar
1/4 cup water
1/2 teaspoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon grated gingerroot
3/4 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
3 tablespoons honey
3/4 teaspoon chili pepper flakes, or to taste
1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Combine peanut butter, vinegar, water, soy sauce, gingerroot, garlic, lemon juice, honey and chili pepper flakes in blender container or food processor. Stir in chopped cilantro.

Submitter: 
Jif®
Description: 
With fresh ginger, lemon, chili pepper, peanut butter, and fresh cilantro, this dressing complements Asian-style salads.
