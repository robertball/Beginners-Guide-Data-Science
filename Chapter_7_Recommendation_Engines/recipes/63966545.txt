Title:
Baked Italian Chicken Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken breasts
4 medium potatoes
1 package frozen broccoli
4 Tbsp butter
1 package Italian dressing mix

Directions:
Dice chicken and potatoes.  Spray a 9x13 baking dish with nonstick spray.  Place ingredients in rows - chicken, broccoli, then potatoes.  Melt butter and drizzle over entire pan.  Sprinkle seasoning over ingredients.  Bake, uncovered, for 45-60 minutes, or until chicken is done.
