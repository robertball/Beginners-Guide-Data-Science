Title:
Black Forest Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 store bought bakery sugar free angel food cake
2 jiggers of cherry liquor ( kirsch )
2 1.3 oz pkgs sugar free chocolate pudding
4 C fat free evaporated skim milk
2 cans light ( reduced sugar ) cherry pie filling
1 Extra large container of Lite Cool Whip or other frozen whipped topping
1 small sugar free chocolate bar

Directions:
Slice and cube the angel food cake.
Toss the cake cubes in the kirsch to distribute evening.
Fix the chocolate pudding according to directions using the fat-free evaporated milk.
Use a trifle bowl or another clear glass serving bowl
1st Layer: 1/2 angel food cake, 1 can pie filling, 1/2 chocolate pudding, and 1/2 the whipped topping.
Repeat the layering: cake / filling / pudding/ topping.
Shave small slivers of the sugar free chocolate bar on the top of the trifle ......... and present !

Submitter: 
NONA
Description: 
An elegant dessert that can be put together quickly, and with these modifications can save some calories !  This works great on vacation when you need something on quick notice.
