Title:
Rangpur Rickey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979436.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Tanqueray Rangpur™ Gin
3/4 ounce fresh lime juice
1/2 ounce simple syrup
1 tablespoon soda water
1 slice lime

Directions:
Pour the Tanqueray Rangpur(TM) Gin, fresh lime juice, and simple syrup into a Collins glass filled with ice.
Top with soda water and garnish with a wheel shaped slice of lime.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
The renowned flavor of Tanqueray® London dry gin gains added mixability by the added distillation of Rangpur limes - which are actually orange.
