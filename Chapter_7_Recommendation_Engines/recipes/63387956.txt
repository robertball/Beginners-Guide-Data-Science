Title:
Maggie's Chicken Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons butter
2 teaspoon garlic, minced
6 chicken breast tenderloins
Black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°.  Generously butter bottom and sides of a small glass baking dish.  Arrange chicken in baking dish.
In small skillet over medium heat, melt butter.
Add garlic and saute until tender.  Pour garlic butter over top of chicken.
Sprinkle black pepper over chicken. Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour until chicken is white and juices run clear.
