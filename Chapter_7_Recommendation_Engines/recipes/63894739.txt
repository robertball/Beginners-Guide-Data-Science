Title:
Maple Walnut Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1080874.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1/2 C. Butter
1/2 C. Maple syrup
1/2 C. Maple sugar
2 Eggs
1 C. Quick oats
1/2 C. Walnuts, chopped
1/2 C. Chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
Blend flour, b. powder and b. soda together in a small bowl. Set aside
Mix butter, syrup and sugar with an electric mixer on medium speed and blend well.
Add eggs to butter/sugar mixture and beat on medium speed until creamy and smooth.
Miix in oats, chocolate chips and walnuts combining all ingredients together well
Stir in dry ingredients and coat mixture evenly with flour to make a nice soft dough that comes away from the sides of the bowl.
Take a small amount of dough and roll into a ball then place 2 inches apart on parchment paper. Flatten out rounds with a fork pressing down evenly
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes until lightly browned
Remove from oven and place on wire rack to cool completely before storing away in a container

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very soft textured cookie with the goodness of maple syrup and sugar with added oats, chocolate chips and walnuts to make a delectable cookie for snacking on at any time of day.
