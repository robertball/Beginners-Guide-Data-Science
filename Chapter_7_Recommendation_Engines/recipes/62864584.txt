Title:
Smoked Sausage and Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Onion,chopped
2 carrots,thin sliced
1 stalk celery, thin sliced
1/2 red pepper,chopped
4 Tbls. butter
3 Tbls. Flour
1 1/2 Cup Chicken Broth or Stock
1/4 Teas. Pepper
2 lbs. Cabbage,chopped
1 Lb. Smoked Sausage, sliced

Directions:
Saute first 4 Veg. in butter. Add flour and stir until smooth. Cook about 1 min. constantly stirring. Gradually add stock and stir and cook over medium heat until thick and bubbly. Stir in cabbage and pepper;cover,reduce heat and simmer 5 min. Add sausage;cover and simmer an additional 10 min.

Description: 
Easy recipe for smoked sausage besides grilling.
