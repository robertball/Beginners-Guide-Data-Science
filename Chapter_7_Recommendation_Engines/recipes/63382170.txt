Title:
Benja's Spring Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/906079.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked ground pork OR
1 cup cooked chicken (or turkey) OR
1 cup cooked shrimp OR
1/4 cup cooked crab meat
1 tsp. of chopped garlic (satay in oil)
2 Tbsp. soy sauce (Maggie or Golden Mountain)
2 cups bean sprout
2 cups chopped cabbage
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped carrot
1 cup bamboo
2 cups bean thread
1 pkg. spring roll skin
cup of cornstarch and water
Sprinkle of white pepper

Directions:
Put all ingredients together, mix well with hands, and roll in spring roll skin (seal with cornstarch and water mixture). Seal tightly and deep fry in oil.
