Title:
Teriyaki Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4539428.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken tenders
1 Tbsp sesame oil
1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
1/2 cup mukimame (cooked and shelled edamame beans)
10 cooked snow peas
1 tsp white sesame seeds
1 tsp black sesame seeds
1 1/2 cups of steamed jasmine rice

Directions:
In a small skillet over medium heat, add the oil and teriyaki sauce and place the chicken tenders on top. Cover and cook until the chicken is cooked throughout, turning over once when half way through. (About 5 minutes each side). Reserve the teriyaki sauce. Allow the chicken to cool and cut into bite-sized pieces.
In a portable lunch bowl, add the rice. Top with the snow peas and the mukimame finally adding the cut chicken on top. Drizzle the reserved teriyaki sauce. Top with sesame seeds and cover. Keep refrigerated until ready to eat.

Description: 
A quick, tasty one bowl meal to keep lunch from getting boring.
