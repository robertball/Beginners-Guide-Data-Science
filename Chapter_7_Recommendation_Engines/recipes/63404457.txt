Title:
Texas Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/916705.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Chili Powder
1 teaspoon Paprika
1 teaspoon Cumin
1 teaspoon Oregano
1 Tablespoon dried Onion (or 1/4 cup fresh onion)
1 teaspoon minced Garlic
1 teaspoon Kosher Salt (more or less to taste)
2 Tablespoons Masa
1 teaspoon Red Pepper
2 pounds lean Ground Beef
1 can (8 oz.) Tomato Sauce
16 oz Water
1 can (15.5 oz.) Pinto Beans
1 can (14.5 oz.) Petite Diced Tomatoes
12 oz. frozen Corn

Directions:
Brown 2 pounds Ground Beef, drain off fat.
Add Chili Powder, Paprika, Cumin, Oregano, Onion, Garlic, and Salt.  Stir to incorporate.
For Hot Chili add 1 teaspoon Red Pepper, for Medium  Chili add 1/2 teaspoon Red Pepper, for Mild Chili omit Red Pepper.
Add Tomato Sauce and Water.  Stir again and cover.
Simmer at medium low temperature for 30 minutes, or until meat is tender, stirring occasionally.
Mix Masa with 1-2 Tablespoons water.  Stir into Chili.  Add Beans, Tomatoes and Corn and simmer for 15 - 20 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Spicy, meaty chili.  Easy to make for a family or party.
