Title:
Snickerdoodle Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 2/3 cups Flour
2 tsp Baking Powder
1 tsp Salt
2 cups Packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tbsp Vanilla
1 cup Butter, softened
Topping
2 tbsp White sugar
2 tbsp Cinnamon
1/8 tsp Ginger
Glaze
1 Cup Confectioners Sugar
1 tbsp Cinnamon
1/4 cup Heavy Cream
1 tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven 350
Mix Flour, Bking Powder and Salt.  Set aside
Beat Butter, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla until smooth.
Add flour mixture, mix until well combined.  Batter will be very thick.
Spread into greased 9x13 baking pan
Combine Cinnamon, sugar and ginger and sprinkle onto batter.
Bake 25-30 minutes.
Combine Confectioners sugar, cinnamon, heavy cream and vanilla until smooth.  Drizzle onto cooled blondies.
