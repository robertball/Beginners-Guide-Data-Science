Title:
Taco Salad (Amber's Version) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces lean ground beef
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
1 red onion, diced
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
2 large tomatoes, chopped
8 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (16 ounce) package corn chips
1 (16 ounce) bottle Catalina salad dressing
Salsa & Sour Cream, if desired

Directions:
Prepare the ground beef as directed by taco seasoning package and set aside.
MIXED:  In a large bowl, combine the beef mixture, lettuce, red onion, green onion, kidney beans, tomatoes and cheese. Mix well.  Add enough dressing to coat.  Serve over the corn chips or mix corn chips in last and serve immediately.  Add salsa and sour cream on top, if desired.
LAYERED: Layer ingredients (eyeball how much to add) in individual serving bowl as follows; chips, meat, kidney beans, lettuce, onion, tomato, cheese and dressing.  Add salsa and sour cream on top, if desired.

Submitter: 
Kemberli Paes
Description: 
This is a robust and yummy taco salad.  Taco seasoned beef w/ kidney beans.  Served with corn chips and catalina dressing.Modified from: Taco Salad III
