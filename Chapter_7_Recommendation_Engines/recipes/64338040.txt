Title:
Chef Erin's Gluten free Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooking spray
1 3/4 C. Namaste gluten free all purpose baking mix
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. ground saigon cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
1 T. chia seeds
1/2 C. honey
1/4 C. sunflower oil
2 eggs separated into whites/ yolks
3 very ripe medium bananas
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/4 C. chopped nuts or chocolate chips

Directions:
1-spray pan
2- preheat oven to 325
3- stir together flour, salt, baking soda, cinnamon in a medium bowl and set aside.
4- Stir together honey, oil, egg yolks, chia seeds, mashed banana and vanilla; mix well.
5- Whisk egg whites until stiff peaks are formed and set aside.
6- Mix dry ingredients with the wet and finally fold in the egg whites and any optional mix ins until everything is equally incorporated.
7- put batter into the prepared pan and bake for approximately 1 hour, or until a tooth pick comes out completely clean.

Submitter: 
Erin MaLoon Rowley
Description: 
Wow! the chia seeds combined with whisking and folding in the egg whites make THE texture! Ya know, the one we all remember, miss, and love!
