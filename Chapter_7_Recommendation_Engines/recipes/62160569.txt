Title:
baby lima bean soup with hamhock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz bag baby Lima beans (rinse beans in cold water)
2 ham hocks
1 Tea-Spoon each of salt and pepper or to taste afterwards
1 large chopped yellow onion
1 cup ham chopped into large pieces
8 cups water (enough to cover ham hock)
1 T-Spoon of garlic powder
1 package of corn bread mix (i use jiffy)
5 potatoes cubed into small peices
3 tablespoons of oil for frying the potatoes
1 T-Spoon of salt for the potatoes
1 T-Spoon of pepper for the potatoes

Directions:
Rinse Lima beans in cold water. Drain. In Dutch oven on top of stove, add beans, ham hock, onion, chopped ham,garlic powder,and salt n pepper. Add water and bring to boil, turn down flame and simmer until beans are tender approx. 1 hour. You may need to add a little more water depending how thin you like your soup.....while cooking the beans fry your potatoes in the heated oil til the last 20 min of cooking time for the beans also put your cornbread in the oven according to the package directions.....serve your soup over the fried potatoes and crumble your cornbread on top and enjoy yummm

Submitter: 
Karen Steinke Reilly
Description: 
I serve this over fried potatoes and crumble corn bread on top very southern comforting dish...
