Title:
Easy Spinach Stuffing Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981554.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups Pepperidge Farm® Herb Seasoned Stuffing
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Celery Soup or Campbell's® Condensed 98% Fat Free Cream of Celery Soup
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mix 1/2 cup stuffing and butter.
Mix soup, sour cream, onion powder, spinach and cheese.  Add remaining stuffing.  Mix lightly.  Spoon into 1 1/2-quart casserole.  Sprinkle stuffing mixture on top.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 35 minutes or until hot.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
This rich, tasty spinach casserole is a fine side dish for the holiday table.
