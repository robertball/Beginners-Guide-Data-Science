Title:
Pecan Tassies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough
8 ounces cream cheese
1 cup softened butter
2 1/4 cups flour
Filling
1 egg
1 cup brown sugar
dash of salt
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 crushed nuts (fine)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Dough:
Blend cream cheese and butter.
Stir in flour
Shape into a ball, cover and chill
Divide into fourths
Pinch off dough into 1" balls
Press into tassy tins (ungreased)
Filling:
Beat together ingredients, except nuts
Sprinkle nuts into pastry lined cups
Add filling, sprinkle more nuts
Bake for  25-30 minutes
Cool and remove with a knife or spatula
Sprinkle with powdered sugar
