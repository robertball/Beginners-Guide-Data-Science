Title:
Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. chuck roast
1 tsp. salt
4 quarts water
1 cup sliced celery
1 onion, diced
1 carrot, peeled and sliced
3 tomatoes, peeled and chopped
2 TBSP minced fresh parsley
6 oz. tomato paste
1 TBSP salt
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 tsp. dried basil
16 oz. dark red kidney beans
15 oz. garbanzo beans
16 oz. baked beans
10 oz. frozen chopped spinach
3 small zucchini, sliced
1 lb. Italian sweet sausage, sliced
8 oz. kluski noodles

Directions:
In a large kettle, combine chuck roast, salt and water. Cover and simmer for 3 hours or until beef is tender. Remove meat from kettle cool in refrigerator and then lifting hardened fat off top and discarding. Break meat into 1 inch pieces and combine with broth in a very large stock pot. Add the remaining ingredients. Simmer, covered, until vegetable and sausage are tender, about 1 hour. Soup may be frozen at this time.
To serve, cook noodles according to package directions. Rinse, drain and add to hot soup. Sprinkle each serving with Parmesan cheese.
