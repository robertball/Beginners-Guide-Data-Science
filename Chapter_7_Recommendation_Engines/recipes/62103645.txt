Title:
Taco Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of chicken wings
1 packet taco seasoning
1/2 cup flour
Crushed Tortilla chips
1 stick of butter

Directions:
Mix 1/2 cup flour and taco season in a small bowl.  Melt 1 stick of butter in a small bowl.  Crush tortilla chips in a bowl.  Roll chicken in flour and taco seasoning.  Dip chicken in butter.  Roll in tortilla chips.  Bake on a greased cookie sheet for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Jaime Paugh
Description: 
Yummy treat, perfect for a football game.  Fast and easy!  Works great with a chicken breast too, just cook for an hour instead of 45 minutes.
