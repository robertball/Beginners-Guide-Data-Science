Title:
Chinese Sweet and Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448589.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup of white sugar
1/4 cup rice vinegar
2/3 cup of pineapple juice
1 tablespoon of soy sauce
3 tablespoons of ketchup
4 tablespoons of maraschino cherry juice
1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder
3 tablespoons of cornstarch

Directions:
Mix sugar, and vinegar until sugar is dissolved.  Then add in the pineapple juice, soy sauce, cherry juice and stir.  Finally add in the garlic powder and ketchup and stir until ingredients are well-combined.
Turn your stove on to medium heat and add sauce mixture to a sauce pan.  Once it begins to bubble, turn heat to lowest setting, and gradually add in the cornstarch while gently stirring.  Stir until all of the cornstarch is mixed in, and finally turn off the heat. ENJOY!!

Submitter: 
Victoria
Description: 
Chinese restaurant-style Sweet and Sour Sauce!  A sauce that's not overly sweet, and just tangy enough to satisfy your cravings!!
