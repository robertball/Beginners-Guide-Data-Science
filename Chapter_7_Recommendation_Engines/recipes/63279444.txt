Title:
Honey Garlic Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg. white onion, chopped
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 tsp mustard powder
1/2 tbsp garlic
5 lb. chicken wings

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except chicken and cook in saucepan for 5 minutes on medium heat. Spread wings evenly in shallow pan and pour sauce over wings. Let marinate anywhere from an hour to overnight. Bake at 350 for 1.5 hours, turning wings occasionally.
