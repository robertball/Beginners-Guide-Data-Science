Title:
Ed's Grilled Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 cup Italian Style Vinaigrette
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
ground black pepper to taste
seasoning salt to taste
2 teaspoons dried parsley

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly oil grate.
Marinate chicken in vinaigrette for 3o minutes, and sprinkle with the garlic powder, ground black pepper, seasoning salt and parsley. Discard any remaining dressing.
Cook on the prepared grill 10 to 15 minutes per side, or until no longer pink and juices run clear.
