Title:
Butterscotch & Choclate Rice Krispie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup corn/caro syrup
1 cup sugar
6 cups rice krispies
Topping
6 ounces butterscotch chips
12 ounces milk choclate chips

Directions:
Reserve 6 heaping cups of rice crispies in large bowl
In saucepan bring p-nut butter, syrup & sugar just to a boil, remove pour over rice crispies and stire until mixed.
Spread into 9 x 13 pan - press down w/ buttered hand.
Topping
Microwave in 30 second intervals until melted then spread ontop of rice krispies, let cool, cut and serve.

Submitter: 
cookinmama
Description: 
A no bake treat! A great addition to the holiday treat table or great as a gift! All of my friends say these are the 1st gone out of their treat bag! These are NOT healthy so they are very special and only appear at Christmas time in our house.
