Title:
Exotic Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast
1/4 cup freshly diced tomatoes
1/2 cup soy sauce
2 garlic cloves, crushed
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/4 cup chopped green onions

Directions:
Combine tomatoes, soy sauce, garlic, olive oil, and onions in a glass baking dish.
Place chicken in baking dish, and let marinate for an hour.
Preheat oven to 400 F
Place dish in oven, and bake for 25 minutes
