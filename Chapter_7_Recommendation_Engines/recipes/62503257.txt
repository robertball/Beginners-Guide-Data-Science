Title:
Chinese Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
225g (1/2 pkg.) steamed noodles
3 tbsp. cooking oil
2 tbsp. sesame oil
1 garlic clove
½ cup bean sprouts
¼ cup grated carrots
½ cup chopped bock choy
½ cup pea pods
Meat Marinade:
1 ½ cups thinly sliced meat
2 tbsp. oyster sauce
2 tbsp. soya sauce
2 tsp. sugar
4 tsp. cornstarch
1 tbsp. cooking oil

Directions:
Marinade meat in a bowl full of measured oyster sauce, soya sauce, sugar and cornstarch.
Heat 1 tbsp. of cooking oil in frying pan at medium high heat. Add meat and stir fry until meat is golden brown. Set aside.
At medium heat add 3 tbsp. cooking oil and minced garlic into another frying pan. Add sprouts, carrots, bock choy and pea pods to fry pan. Pour 2 tbsp. sesame oil over vegetables. Add noodles to 4 cups boiling water. Boil for 15-30 seconds
Pour noodles into colander and rinse noodles with cold water to cool. Drain well. Add cooked noodles and vegetables to meat frying pan and brown until hot and crisp. ENJOY!!
