Title:
Three Ingredient Buttermilk Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2203970.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Corn Meal
1/2 Cup All Purpose Flour
1 1/2 Cups Buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. In a non-stick skillet pour enough vegetable oil to coat the bottom and put into oven as it preheats. In a bowl mix all ingredients together. Take out skillet and pour mixture in. Put back into oven and bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Jillian Horton
Description: 
This is a quick and easy, savory cornbread recipe. I learned this from my Grandmother and it is my all time favorite corn bread.
