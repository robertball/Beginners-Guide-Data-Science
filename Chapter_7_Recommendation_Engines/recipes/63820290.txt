Title:
"All Grown Up" Mini Caramel Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mix together in a small pot:
1/2 cup Calvados Brandy
1 cup water
½ Cup white sugar
1 Bay leaf
½ cinnamon stick
½ lemon , sliced
2-3 orange slices
For the sauce:
1 cup Brown Sugar
½ cup water
25 g butter
pour in 1/2 Cup sweetened condensed milk
Additional:
lollipop sticks
2 crisp apples

Directions:
Using a melon ball tool , make mini apple rounds , 1 apple = apprx 15 small apple balls
Take the mixed ingredients in the pan and bring to a boil.
Remove from heat and  allow to cool.
Place apple balls into liquid in the pan , place on the stove and bring to a boil, then remove from the heat – it is very important that do this step correctly ( to avoid over cooking) , as soon as liquid comes to a boil, take off heat, let apples cool in mixture . This step can be done a day
before , or 4 hours ahead , this allows the flavors to absorb into the apples.
Remove apples onto a paper-towel , allow to dry – before dipping into warm caramel sauce.
For Sauce :
In small sauce pot - melt the three ingredients, 1 cup Brown Sugar, ½ cup water, and 25 g butter
Then stir in ½ cup sweetened condensed milk with candy thermometer bring mixture to softball stage 230/240 F
Take a lollipop stick and pierce the middle of the apple ball , then dip into warm caramel , roll into nuts or other desired toppings, place on sprayed waxed paper.
Place in fridge to help harden the caramel – then enjoy !!! Happy Halloween !

Submitter: 
The Homestretch
Description: 
Liana Robberecht, executive chef at the Calgary Petroleum Club, has a recipe for mini brandy-poached caramel apples.
