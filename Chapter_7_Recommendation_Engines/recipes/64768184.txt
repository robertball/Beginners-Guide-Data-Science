Title:
Spaghetti Milanese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Carrot
1 Onion
18 oz Tomato paste
1 qt of water
2 tbsp Flour.
2 tbsp Oil.
Salt;  ground:  Pepper, Basil, Oregano. Cloves.
3 oz of grated  Parmesan Cheese
1  8 oz package of spaghetti Noodles.

Directions:
Grate and Mince the Carrots and Onions Sautee in oil. Sprinkle with the Flour
mix and add the Tomato paste.  stir continually, until Lightly Brown then Transfer to a Small stock pot and add the water or (Broth).  Adjust to desired consistency Cook until all is Tender and absorbed.  Break the Noodles in Half and Boil in Salted Water for 11 min. Drain and Oil.  Serve with the Tomato Sauce and a tablespoon of Grated Cheese.
