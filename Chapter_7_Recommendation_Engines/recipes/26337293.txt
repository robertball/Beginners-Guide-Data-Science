Title:
Pink Ambrosia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces Cool Whip
1 can Eagle Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 tsp Vanilla
1 Small Can crushed/drained Pineapple
1 Cup Coconut
1 cup Pecans finely chopped
1 Can Cherry Pie Filling
1 Cup Tiny Marshmallows (Optional)

Directions:
Mix Cool Whip, Eagle Brand Milk, and Vanilla well.
Fold in the remaining ingredients and cover.
Store overnight in refrigerator.
Mix well before serving.
