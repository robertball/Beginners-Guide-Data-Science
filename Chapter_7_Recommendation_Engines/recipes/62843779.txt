Title:
Carrots that Zing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093845.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb carrots cut in 1" pieces
1/2 cup water
1/8 tsp salt
1 tbs honey
1 tbs butter
1/4 tsp cayenne

Directions:
Combine the carrots, water and salt in a medium sauce pan cover and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for about 10 minutes.
Uncover and increase heat to medium cook until water is reduced by half and carrots are just tender.
Add the honey, butter and cayenne, stir to coat the carrots until all are glazed.

Submitter:

Description: 
These carrots have a great balance of sweet and spicy.
