Title:
Spinach Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 no cook lasagna noodles
1 zucchini thinly sliced lengthwise
2-10 oz bags of baby spinach - chopped
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 cup chopped onion
5 cloves chopped garlic
1/4 cup chopped bell pepper (red or yellow)
1 cup sliced bella mushrooms
2-3 cups ricotta cheese
1 cup cottage cheese
salt, pepper & herbs to taste
1 jar marinara sauce
1 cup shredded mozzarella

Directions:
Lightly grease 9x13 baking dish (glass works well).
Blanch spinach in large saute pan with 1/4 cup water.  (about 5 mins) and set aside to cool.
While spinach is blanching, saute remaining veggies in olive oil until tender. Set aside to cool.
After veggies have cooled, combine with ricotta/cottage cheese and remaining spices and herbs.
Spread 1/2 cup sauce on bottom of dish.  Layer 4 noodles, spinach mixture, 4 slices zucchini, sauce,  and cheese in 3 layers, ending with noodles, sauce and cheese.  Cover lightly with foil and bake on 350 for 50-60 minutes.  Remove foil for the last 15 minutes, adding additional cheese if desired.  Let rest for 5 minutes before cutting.
