Title:
Skinny Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups eggs
1-1/2 cups cottage cheese
1/8 cup flour
1/8 cup oil (butter, olive, coconut)
1/2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a blender and blend until very smooth.  Cook on hot griddle (350-375 deg).  Flip when bubbles appear and edges are slightly set.  Serve with fruit topping and whipped cream.  Store leftovers in fridge and eat cold or warm.

Submitter: 
Lisa S
Description: 
Full of protein and keeps you satisfied all morning long.  Our kids beg for more and guests always love them.
