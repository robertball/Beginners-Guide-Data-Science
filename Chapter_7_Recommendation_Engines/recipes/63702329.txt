Title:
Taco Bell Crunchwrap Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 tablespoon onion flakes
1 teaspoon beef bouillion
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne
1/4 teaspoon sugar
3/4 cup water
6 10-inch flour tortillas, warmed
6 tostadas
1 cup greek yogurt
2 tomatoes, chopped, tossed with 1/4 cup of the taco sauce
2 cups shredded lettuce
12 oz Nacho cheese
Cooking spray, for frying
Taco sauce

Directions:
Brown the meat in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Add all the spices, including cornstartch. Cook for 2 minutes while stirring.
Add water.
Simmer for 5 minutes until meat is cooked through and sauce is thick and flavorful.
Place 1 flour tortilla on a work station.
Add a spoonful of meat mixture to the center and top with a spoonful of nacho cheese.
Top with a tostada and and a tablespoon of greek yogurt on top.
Sprinkle tomatoes and shredded lettuce on top.
Working counter-clockwise, fold the edges of the flour tortilla up over the meat, cheese, tostada, tomato, and lettuce pile until completely wrapped and in a hexagon shape.
Heat another medium skillet over high heat and grease with cooking spray.
Fry each side of the Crunchwrap Supreme for 1 minutes.
Serve with side of taco sauce.
