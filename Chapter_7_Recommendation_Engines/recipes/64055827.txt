Title:
Jalapeno  Popper Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup bread crumbs
2 TBSP EVOO
1 tsp taco seasoning
2 chicken breasts
2 jalapenos, chopped
2 oz cream cheese
1/4 cup cheddar cheese
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Brown bread crumbs in EVOO, add taco seasoning, stir and set aside.
Cut Chicken breasts almost in half horizontally, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Mix Jalapenos, cream cheese and cheddar cheese together. Place in chicken breasts secure with a tooth pick. Roll in egg and then in bread crumbs.
Bake 30 minutes at 375 degrees.
