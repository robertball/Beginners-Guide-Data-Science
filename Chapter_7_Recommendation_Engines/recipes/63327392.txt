Title:
Cocktail Weiners Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package weiners, chopped into bit size pieces
3/4cup ketchup
3/4cup brown sugar
2tsp liquid smoke (or sub. hickory BBQ sauce)
1/4tsp garlic powder
1tsp Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in slow cooker. Stir occassionally.

Submitter: 
Jewels
Description: 
Affordable, delious and easy to make! Perfect for taking to gatherings or for those sports events..and little fingers can't get enough of them!
