Title:
Wonton Hamburger Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332601.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (25 count) package wonton wrappers
1 pound hamburger meat, cooked & well drained
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
3/4-1 cup spagetti sauce, any variety

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Press one wonton wrapper into each muffin-tin cups. Bake 5 minutes. Remove from the oven & place the wonton cups on a cookie sheet.
Mix hamburger, cheddar cheese, mozzarell cheese and spagetti sauce until well blended.
Spoon mixture into cups. Bake 5 to 10 minutes or until bubbly. Remove from oven, let cool slightly and serve.
