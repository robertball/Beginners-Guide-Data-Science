Title:
Ham Salad II jodys way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups mayonnaise
1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
2 teaspoons mrs dash
2 pounds smoked boneless ham, chopped in food processor
1 small onion, chopped in food processor
1 small green bell pepper, chopped in food processor
1 cup chredded cheese
4 hard boiled eggs chopped

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together all ingredients.  Store the salad, covered, in the refrigerator.
Give 4 days shelf life.

Submitter: 
Jody Comerford
Description: 
This sweet ham salad is great served on bread as a sandwich or crackers as a snack.Modified from: Ham Salad II
