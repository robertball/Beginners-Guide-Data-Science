Title:
creamy macaroni salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb large elbow macaroni - cook til done (not soft and mushy)
1/2 c chopped celery
1/2 c diced onion
1/4 c diced pepper
1/4 c diced carrots
1/4 c corn
3/4 c mayonaise
3/4 c miracle whip
2 teas mustard
1 teas apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup sweet relish
1/2 tea salt
1/2 teas black pepper
2 teaspoons sugar

Directions:
Prepare the seasoning, mayo(s) and such in a small bowl, then mix it in a larger bowl with the already prepared macaroni.  Chill and serve.  (but beware, people love it as soon as you make it.  warm or not)

Description: 
Even when you don't like macaroni salad, you will love this one.
