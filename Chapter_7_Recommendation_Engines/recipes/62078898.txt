Title:
Kibby (Kibby-Bel-Saneeya) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pine nuts
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion (chopped)
1 cup cracked wheat
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 teaspoons cinnamon
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Soak cracked wheat in warm water for 25 minutes. While u wait take 1 pound ground beef in large bowl add 1 small onion (chopped) cinnamon lemon juice salt and pepper. After your Wheat has soaked squeeze the water out and add to beef mixture blend well. Add to greased baking dish make sure mixture is even in pan (10x14). If u wish add small pieces of butter(optional) to the top of kibby along with pine nuts bake 30 min last 5 minutes on broil
