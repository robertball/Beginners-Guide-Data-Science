Title:
Nutella crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
frozen crepes or phyllo pastry
nutella
chopped hazelnuts or pecans
butter
whipped cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Defrost the crepes. Spread nutella generously in the middle. Sprinkle on chopped nuts. Fold up crepes. Brush butter over the top. Bake for 15-20 minutes. Top with whipped cream and enjoy!!
