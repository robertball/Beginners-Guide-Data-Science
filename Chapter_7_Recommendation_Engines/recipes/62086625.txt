Title:
Lazy Cheese Kenephels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small package seashell style pasta
1 large onion
margarine (enough to saute onion)
2 cups  cottage cheese
1 cup sour cream
3 eggs
1/4 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook and drain seashell pasta. Set aside. Saute onion. In large bowl mix sauteed onion, cottage cheese and sour cream. In seperate bowl beat eggs with fork and add milk. Add to the onion mixture. Mix with the pasta. Bake until set (approx 30 minutes) at 350.

Submitter: 
J
Description: 
A delicious casserole using seashell pasta and cottage cheese that bakes up creamy and delicious passed down to me from my Great Grandma. Also good if you add a little bit of dill and/or bacon.
