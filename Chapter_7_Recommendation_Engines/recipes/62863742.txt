Title:
Avocado Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked corn
2 avocados
1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
1/2 red onion, finely diced
1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
Vinaigrette
2 T olive oil
1 T lime juice
1/2 tsp lime zest
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl mix corn, avocado, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro. In a separate mix olive oil, lime juice, lime zest, salt and pepper. Stir the vinaigrette into the salad and serve. I add a little extra cilantro garnish on top.
