Title:
Poppy Seed Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 chicken breasts, cooked, skinned, boned and cut-up
2 cans cream of chicken soup
8 oz. Sour Cream
6 oz. Ritz cracker, crumbled
2 sticks butter or margarine
2 TBS Poppy Seeds

Directions:
Mix crackers with melted butter and poppy seeds. Line 9 x 13 glass pan with 2/3 of  cracker mix. Mix chicken with soup and sour cream. Put chicken mix on top of crackers. Place remaining cracker mix on top. Bake for 1 hour at 350*

Submitter: 
Ladybug
Description: 
I love this recipe. I got this from a  friend who made it when my mom was sick.  She made a double recipe and froze half of it.  It was even better after it had been frozen.
