Title:
Andy's Jalapeno Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3c flour
1c white sugar
1c brown sugar
3 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp jalapeno powder
3 eggs
1/2c veg oil
1/2 applesauce
2 tsp vanilla
2c shredded zucchini
2 sm - med jalapenos, unseeded and chopped

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients together then add eggs,oil,applesauce and vanilla mix together until smooth, then add 2 cups shredded zucchini and chopped jalapeno. Split batter between 2- 9x5x3 loaf pans. Bake 350 for one hour. Or make 24 muffins and bake at 400 for 20 minutes. Allow to cool on wire racks.

Submitter: 
CNM CATERING
Description: 
I love zucchini bread and jalapenos and with the bounty of both coming out of my garden I figured I'd mix the two together. The jalapenos add just the right amount of heat.
