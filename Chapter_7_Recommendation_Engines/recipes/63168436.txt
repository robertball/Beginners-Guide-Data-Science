Title:
Crockpot Broccoli Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/813168.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup green pepper , chopped
1/2 cup onion , chopped
3 tablespoons butter
3 (10 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
3 cups milk
2 lb Velveeta cheese , cubed
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli
1 cup finely chopped celery
1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
1 cup small cubed potatoes
1 single package or powdered Ranch Dressing mix

Directions:
Saut√© onion and green pepper in butter.
Put cans of soup, milk and dressing in crockpot and wisk until mixed than add cheese (cubed), then all the vegtables.
Cook low in crockpot for  3-4 hours, stirring occasionally until cheese is melted.
