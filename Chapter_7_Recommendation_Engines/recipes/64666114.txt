Title:
Baked Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups rolled oats
1 cup Dark/light brown sugar
4 eggs
2 cups milk
1 Tsp vanilla extract
1-2 cups rasins or your favorite fruit (fresh or dried)
1-2 cups Walnuts or Pecans
1 stick + 1 TBS unsalted butter
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 TBS Cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 deg.
Take 1 TBS butter and coat the bottom and sides of a 9x13 baking dish.
Melt 1 stick of butter and set aside.
In a large mixing bowl add eggs,  milk, vanilla,  salt,  and sugar and mix thoroughly.
Add remaining ingredients and fold until well combined.
Pour into greased baking dish and bake for 35-40 minutes or until the top is nicely browned. Set on cooling rack and allow to cool for a few minutes. Serve hot or warm.

Submitter: 
Kevin Douglas
Description: 
An easy delicious breakfast you can make ahead of time.  Even the kids will love this one.
