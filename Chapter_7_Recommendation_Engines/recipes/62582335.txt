Title:
Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Stew Meat
1 Onion Chopped
1 bag Carrots Sliced
7 Potatoes Quartered
2 cans of Beef Broth
1 can Tomato Sauce
1 can of peas
2 tsp butter
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Brown Stew Meat and Chopped Onion in butter on Medium/High.  Add Beef Broth.  Bring to a boil, add carrots and simmer for 1-1/2 hour, until carrots are almost soft.  Add potatoes and continue to simmer until potatoes are soft.  Finally, add Tomatoe Sauce and peas.
