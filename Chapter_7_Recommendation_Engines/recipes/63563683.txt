Title:
Honey Barbecue Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 T olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
cracked black pepper and sea salt
1 c. water
1 c. ketchup
1/4 c. brown sugar
2 T honey
2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 "squirt" mustard
1 clove garlic
sprinkle of chili powder
1 lb. meatballs

Directions:
Heat olive oil over medium high heat in heavy skillet.
Saut√© onion, salt and pepper in olive oil until translucent.
Add water, ketchup, honey, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, garlic and chili powder.  Mix well and simmer over medium heat until hot.
Add prepared meatballs and heat through.
