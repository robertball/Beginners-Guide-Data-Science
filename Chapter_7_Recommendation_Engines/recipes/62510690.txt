Title:
Macaroni, Pineapple & Ham Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb Whole Wheat Macaroni
2 plum tomatoes, diced
1/2 onion, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
8oz ham steak, cubed
1c Italian dressing
2 short cans crushed pineapple, drained

Directions:
Cook macaroni as directed.  Don't run under cold water when done.  Pour the Italian dressing and pineapple over the macaroni and stir to prevent sticking. Add all other ingredients, mix well and allow to chill. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jodi
Description: 
This delicious macaroni salad is so refreshing. My husband and I came up with it using ingredients we prefer and we make this regularly during the summer to go with lunches or as a meal on a really hot day.Modified from: Macaroni & Ham Salad
