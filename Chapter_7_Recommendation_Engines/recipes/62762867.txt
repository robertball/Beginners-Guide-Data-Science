Title:
Sugar Free Hot Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/639345.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Water
3/4 cup Xylitol (equivalent to 3/4 cup Sugar)
4 scoops Kal Pure Stevia Extract (equivalent to 4 tbsp Sugar)
1/2 cup Cocoa Powder
1 pinch Salt
3/4 pkg Cream Cheese, softened (6 oz)
1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract (No Corn Syrup, Gluten Free)

Directions:
Combine the water, xylitol, stevia, cocoa powder, and salt together in a saucepan over medium low heat
Once mixture is smooth, add Cream Cheese
Turn down to low
Stir until smooth and the mixture thickens to your liking
Remove from heat and stir the vanilla into the sauce
Serve warm or cover and refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Zan
Description: 
Diabetic friendly hot fudge sauce! For the most diabetic friendly use Stevia and not Sugar. I only mentioned sugar because every Stevia measures to sugar differently because most aren't pure. I use Kal Stevia, which is pure and very good tasting! I found it on Amazon.com.
