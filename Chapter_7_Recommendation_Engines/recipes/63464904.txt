Title:
Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ounces white sugar
4 ounces salted butter
6 ounces all purpose flour
1/4 t salt
1/2 t vanilla

Directions:
Use your hands and mix then plop into pan and press in, prick with fork, cook on 300 degrees for thirty minutes and when it starts to BROWN slightl it will be ready. Cut when still cooling and sprinkle sugar on top).
