Title:
Dutch Oven Chicken, Rice and Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. chicken breast
4 cups chicken broth
2 cups short grain brown rice
4 large carrots, 1/2 inch slices
6 ribs celery, 1/2 inch slices
1/2 lb. green beans
salt to taste
pepper to taste
2 tsp herbs de provence

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 deg.  Spray a 5 qt. dutch oven w/ cooking spray.  Add rice and 1/2 of the broth.  Layer chicken, then celery, carrots and beans.  Season w/salt, pepper and herbs.  Then pour the rest of the chicken broth on top.  Cover and cook for about 2 hours or until rice is done and liquid is absorbed.  You may need to alter the amount of liquid depending on your climate/altitude.
