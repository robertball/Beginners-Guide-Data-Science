Title:
Shrimp Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/343756.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 1/2 cups Tomato Clam juice (Motts Clamato)
1 cup cocktail shrimp, drained and rinsed.
3/4 cup shredded crab meat
3/4 cup chopped celery
1 tbsp chopped onion
5-7 drops of Tabasco sauce
16 large cooked prawns

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except Tabasco sauce and large prawns. After everthing is mixed up, taste a sample for flavour. Add Tabasco sauce drop by drop to add a little more flavour to your taste.
Put in refigerator overnight to allow flavours to blend. when ready to serve, pour into cocktail glasses and hang 4 prawns on the rims of each glass.
Serve cocktails cold.

Submitter: 
hoeguy
Description: 
My father used to make this every years for Christmas dinner. We would use it as an appetizer in a cocktail glass with large shrimp hanging off the rim and Ritz crackers around the base for dipping. If you like seafood, this is the cocktail for you.
