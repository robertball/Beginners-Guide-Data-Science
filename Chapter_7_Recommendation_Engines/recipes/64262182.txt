Title:
Healthy Beef & Vegetable Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Beef Stew Meat
1 Onion, cut into strips
1 Green bell pepper cut into strips
1 (14-1/2 oz) can fire roasted diced tomatoes
2 Cloves garlic, minces

Directions:
Brown beef in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.  Add onion and bell pepper; cook 4 to 5 minutes or until tender.  Place onion, bell pepper and garlic in slow cooker; arrange beef on top.  Pour tomatoes over beef.  Cover and cook on LOW 6 to 8 hours, or until beef is tender
