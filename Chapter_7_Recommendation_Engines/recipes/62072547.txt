Title:
Homemade Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups nonfat dry milk
1/4 cup cocoa
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1/3 cup powdered nondairy creamer
pinch of salt
1 tbl. malted-milk powder

Directions:
MIX: In a large bowl measure and mix ingredients thoroughly.
TO SERVE: Spoon 3-4 tbl. of mix into a mug and fill with boiling water. Stir until dissolved.
TIP: Add 1/3 cup marshmallows to dry cocoa mix. They will shrivel up when stored, but pop up when in hot water.

Description: 
Easy to make and store...my grandmother's special cocoa.
