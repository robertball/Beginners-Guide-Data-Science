Title:
Herbed New Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982813.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 pound small red potatoes
1 tablespoon butter, softened
1 tablespoon sour cream
2 teaspoons snipped fresh dill
2 teaspoons minced chives
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 dash lemon juice

Directions:
Remove a strip of peel from the middle of each potato. Place potatoes in a saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until tender.
In a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients. Drain potatoes; add butter mixture and toss gently.

Submitter: 
Vi  Neiding
Description: 
'I make these potatoes often for my husband and me. They're easy to prepare and have a nice dill flavor,' writes Vi Neiding of South Milwaukee, Wisconsin. 'We eagerly await the start of farmers market season to get garden-fresh new potatoes.'
