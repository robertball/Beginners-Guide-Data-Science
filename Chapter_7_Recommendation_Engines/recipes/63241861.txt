Title:
Red Velvet Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c unsweetened cocoa powder
2 eggs
1/3 c. vegetable oil
1 1/2 c water
3 c waffle mix
3 oz cream cheese, softened
3 tblsp powdered sugar
1/4 tsp vanilla
3 tblsp milk
Red food coloring (about 25 drops)
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/2 c chocolate chips
fresh strawberries

Directions:
Preheat waffle maker.
Mix waffle mix and cocoa powder until blended.  Add eggs, water and oil and stir until blended.  Add about 20-25 drops red food coloring and stir until blended.  Cook waffles according to your waffle maker's directions.  Makes about 5 belgian waffles or 10 regular.
Blend together cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla and milk in a blender or food processor.  Pour into saucepan and warm on medium low, stirring frequently.
Place chocolate chips in a resealable bag.  Seal and microwave 30 seconds.  Knead bag to melt chips.  Microwave 10-20 seconds longer if needed.  When melted, cut small corner off bag.
When waffles are done, top  with sliced strawberries.  Drizzle glaze over all and sprinkle with chopped pecans. Drizzle with melted chocolate.
