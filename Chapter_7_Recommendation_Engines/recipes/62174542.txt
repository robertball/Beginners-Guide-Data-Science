Title:
Southern Sweet Potato Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 2/3 cups sugar
1 cup mashed sweet potatoes
2 eggs
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg  pinch or2 cinnimon 1 tsp vanilla
Pinch salt
1/2 cup butter or margarine
3/4 cup evaporated milk
1 (9 inch) unbaked pastry shell

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and sugar. Add potatoes, eggs, corn syrup, nutmeg, salt, butter and evaporated milk; beat well. Pour into pastry shell. Bake at 350 degrees F for 55-60 minutes.

Submitter: 
Dee
Description: 
This recipe is very popular here in the South. It's a particular favorite at our house because we always have plenty of sweet potatoes in our garden. Try it with a dollop of whipped cream.Modified from: Traditional Southern Sweet Potato Pie
