Title:
Roast New Potatoes and Sage and Citrus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. small new potatoes
3 tbsp. olive oil
4 sprigs of fresh rosemary
1 packed cup of fresh sage leaves
zest of 1 lemon or orange
Kosher salt or sea salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Rinse and dry potatoes.  Put the oil into a roasting pan.  Strip the rosemary stems and add to oil along with sage and citrus zest.  Toss to coat the potatoes evenly with herbs and oil.  Roast 40 to 50 minutes shaking the pan a few times until fork tender and with a crisp golden brown coating.  Sprinkle with salt and serve.
