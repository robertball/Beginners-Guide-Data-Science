Title:
Garlicky Green Olive Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/353247.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese (room temp)
1 cloves of garlic (finely grated)
25 small green olives with pimento (Manzanilla)
1/2 TBS juice from olive jar

Directions:
Chop olives into small rings.
Finely grate or paste garlic.
Add both to cream cheese with olive juice.
Stir to combine and serve with crackers or crostini.
