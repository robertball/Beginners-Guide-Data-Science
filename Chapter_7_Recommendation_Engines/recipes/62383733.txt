Title:
Spicy Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can black-eyed peas
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
3 tsp fresh cilantro (finely minced)
1 (4 ounce) can diced jalapeno peppers
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
1/2 cup Italian-style salad dressing
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine black-eyed peas, black beans, corn, onion, green bell pepper, jalapeno cilantro peppers and tomatoes. Season with Italian-style salad dressing and garlic salt; mix well. Cover, and refrigerate overnight to blend flavors.

Submitter: 
Hana
Description: 
Also known as 'cowboy caviar', this twist on traditional salsa is enlivened with black beans, corn and black-eyed peas.Modified from: Spicy Bean Salsa
