Title:
triple layer bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c butter
1 1/2 c graham cracker crumbs
1 can condensed milk
7 oz flaked coconut
1 bag semi sweet chocolate chips
1/2 c creamy peanut butter

Directions:
in a 9x13 pan melt butter in the oven at 350.  Once melted sprinkle in graham cracker crumbs. Then sprinkle the coconut on top.  Pour the condensed milk over the cocnut and bake for 25 minutes.  While baking melt the chocolate chips and peanut butter in a pot over medium heat stirring constantly.  Let the coconut and graham cracker cool, then pour the chocolate mixture on top and refrigerate.
