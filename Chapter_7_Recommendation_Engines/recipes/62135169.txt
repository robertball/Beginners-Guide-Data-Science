Title:
Vareniky Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough Ingredients:
Milk - 0,3 liter
Sunflower oil - 2 table spoons
Egg yolk - 1
Flour - 0,5 kg
Salt - 0,5 tea spoon
Filling Ingredients:
Mushrooms (Boletus edulis) dried - 100 g
Potatoes - 0,5 kg
Onions - 2
Oil - 3 table spoons
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Filling:
Soak mushrooms in hot water for 45 min
Peel potatoes and boil for 20 min
Chop onions
Chop mushrooms after soaking
Fry onions and mushrooms in oil 10-15 min
Mash potatoes after boiling
Mix potatoes, mushrooms and onions
Add salt and pepper to taste
Dough:
Mix milk, oil, egg yolk, salt, flour with mixer or by hand
Roll the dough can sprinkle flour on table before you roll
Vareniky:
Cut the dough into discs (1 cm thick and 5 cm in diameter)
Flatten or roll each disc
Put one table spoon of filling in the centre of each disc
Mould each disc(future Vareniky) by folding in half and pinching the circular edge closed
Boil 4 liters of water with 1 tble spoon of salt
Put vareniky into the boiling water and stir once
Boil on low heat for 10-15 min or until all vareniky is floatting on top of the water.
Strain water out of pot
Ready to eat use toppings for vareniky like:
- cracklings with fried onions
- fried onions
- butter with dill or parsley
- sour cream with dill

Submitter: 
Ukrainian Chef
Description: 
Vareniky is like a big ravioli with different fillings (meat, potato, berries, fruits, vegetables, mushrooms, etc.) This recipe will have a vareniky with potato and mushrooms.
