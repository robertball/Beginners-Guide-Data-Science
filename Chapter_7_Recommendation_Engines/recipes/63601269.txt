Title:
Golden Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C. Almond Flour
1C. whole wheat flour
1C. splenda
2 tsp.baking powder
2.tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cloves
2 C. chopped apples(skin 0n)
1/2 C. carrots (shredded)
1/2 C. coconut
1/2 C. raisins 1/2 C. nuts (chopped) 3/4 C. veg, oil
1/4C. fat free milk
2tsps. vanilla
2 large egs (beaten)

Directions:
Preheat oven 350F  Bake 20/25 mins.
In a large bowl combine the first 7 ingredients.Then add the next 5 ingredients.Then add the rest of the ingredients. Mix just until all ingredients are combined. Fill cupcake tins 3/4 full.
