Title:
The Loft Bloody Mary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2455853.jpg
Ingredients:
1 46 oz can of V-8 Juice
2 Tbsp Celery Salt
1/2 can of Beef Bouillon Soup (Campbells 14 oz)
3 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 Tbsp Tabasco Sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and shake well. Pour 6 oz. in a glass with ice and 1.5 oz. of your favorite vodka. Serve with a lime, olives.

Submitter: 
Paul Nenni
Description: 
The Loft Restaurant in Middletown, Ohio was the place to go hear live music and socialize in the 70's and 80's. Their signature Bloody Mary was always in high demand.
