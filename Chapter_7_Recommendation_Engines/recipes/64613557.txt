Title:
Granny's Spiced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 18-ounce Tang
1/2 cup instant tea
1/2 cup light brown sugar
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1 teaspoon cloves, ground
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Stir ingredients together well and store in an air-tight container.
To serve, pour hot water over 3 teaspoons of tea mix and stir.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
Granny's famous recipe for holiday spiced tea. A standard in the Heath/Havard family. No Christmas would be complete without it.
