Title:
Pierogi Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 med. potatoes
1 pkgs lasangna noodles
1/2 lb. butter
2 med. onions
.75 lbs cheese, longhorn or cheddar
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop and brown onions in butter.
Cook noodles until soft.
Cooke potatoes and mash.
Season with salt and pepper.
Shred cheese and melt into potatoes.
In a large baking dish, put a layer of onions followed by a layer of noodles and a layer of potatoes until all ingredients are used.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes.
Serve hot.
