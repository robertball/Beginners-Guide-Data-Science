Title:
Tom Khaa Gai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp vegetable oil
3 lemon grass stalks
3 large shallots
8 sprigs fresh cilantro, chopped coarse
3 tbsp fish sauce
4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
2 (14 oz) cans Chaokoh brand coconut milk
1 tbsp sugar
1/2 pound white mushrooms
2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken
3 tbsp juice from 2 to 3 limes
2 tsp thai red curry paste

Directions:
Put chicken in freezer for 30 minutes before slicing it in half lenghtwise and then slicing it into very thin strips on the bias
Remove tough outer leaves of lemon grass stalks and use only the bottom 5 inches.  Half lengthwise and slice thin crosswise
Chop the shallots
Clean mushrooms, trim stems, and cut into 1/4-inch slices
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat until just shimmering.
Add lemon grass, shallots, cilantro, and 1 tbsp fish sauce. Cook, stirring frequently, until just softened about 2 to 5 minutes.
Stir in chicken broth and 1 can of coconut milk
Bring to a simmer over high heat.
Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer until  flavors have blended, 10 minutes.
Pour broth through a fine mesh strainer and discard solids in the strainer.
Rinse saucepan and return broth mixture to pan.
Return pan to medium high heat.
Stir in remaining can coconut milk and sugar into broth.
Reduce heat to medium and add mushrooms.  Cook until tender, 2 to 3 minutes.
Add chicken and cook, stirring constantly until no longer pink, 1 to 3 minutes.
Remove soup from heat.
Combine lime juice, curry paste, and remaining 2 tbsp fish sauce into small bowl. Stir into soup.
Garnish with cilantro, chiles, and scallions.  Serve with lime wedges.

Submitter: 
Sandy Williams
Description: 
A spicy coconut thai soup that is a perfect meal with rice.
