Title:
Suz's Salsa Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (16 oz) Acini de Pepe Pasta
2 large red bell peppers
1 can (15.5 oz) black beans, drained.
3 Large Tomatoes
1 Purple Onion
2 tbs chopped cilantro
Salt, Pepper and Garlic to taste.
1 tbs lemon juice
1 jalepeno pepper (optional)
1 tbs olive oil

Directions:
1. Cook pasta per box directions.
2. Brush peppers with olive oil, place in oven on cookie sheet and roast at 350  for 15 to 20 minutes.
3. While peppers are roasting, quarter tomatoes and remove seeds and insides.
4. Dice tomatoes and put them in mixing bowl.
5. Cut half of the purple onion and dice. Place it in the mixing bowl with tomatoes.
6. Chop cilantro, add to the mixing bowl with the lemon juice. Mix tomatoes, onions, cilantro and lemon juice together.
7. Add black beans, mix again.
8. When peppers are finished roasting let them cool in a paper bag or cover them with foil.
9. Stir pasta as well as seasonings into the mixing bowl.
10. When peppers are cool, remove skins and dice.  Add to the mix.
11. Chill well, then serve!
That's it!  You can make it more substantial by adding two grilled chicken breasts or some grilled shrimp with lemon pepper and garlic.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
SuzField
Description: 
This salad can be used as a side dish or as a main dish.  I Makes a great cool dish for those hot summer nights! You can use store bought salsa and/or roasted peppers, but I prefer to make mine fresh!
