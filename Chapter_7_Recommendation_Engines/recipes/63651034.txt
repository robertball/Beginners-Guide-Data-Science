Title:
Oatmeal Refrigerator Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. shortening * see notes
1 C. granulated sugar
1 C. packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla (or more)
1 1/2 c. flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 c.  oats, I use old fashioned, you can use quick
1/2 c. chopped walnuts optional

Directions:
Cream together shortening, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each, add vanilla. Stir together flour, salt, bakng soda, and cinnamon, stir into creamed mixture. Stir in oats and walnuts. Shape dough nto two 8 inch rolls. Wrap in waxed paper or clear plastic wrap. Chill thoroughly. Cut into 1/4 inch slices and bake on parchment paper (or greased cookie sheets). Bake at 350 for 8 - 10 minutes. Makes about 5 dzn.
