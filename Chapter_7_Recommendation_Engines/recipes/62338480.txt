Title:
Chile Colorado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Dried New Mexico Chilies (stems and seeds removed)
8-10 Dried Chile de Arbol Peppers (stems and seeds removed)
2-1/2 Pounds Stew Meat
1 Onion - Diced
3 Garlic Cloved - Minced
1 Tspn Cumin
2 Beef Boullion Cubes
2 Tblspns Oil
3-4 Cups Water
1-1/2 Cup water
Salt and Pepper
1 Tablespoon Corn Starch and 1 Tablespoon Warm Water (if needed, optional)

Directions:
Place Chilies in 1-1/2 Cup Warm water and cover to re-hydrate them.  About 15 minutes.
Place Remaining water into pot with stew meat and turn on medium heat.  Add beef bouillion cubes and let simmer.
In skillet heat oil add onions and garlic.  Saute until golden brown.  Add them to the pot with meat.
Place chilies into blender with water and blend until smooth.  Add this mixture to the pan with chilies. Add cumin also.  Let simmer for about 2-3 hours or until meat is tender. Salt and pepper to taste.
Sauce should simmer down, if it is to saucy you may mix 1 tablespoon corn starch and 1 tablespoon water together and slowy stir into sauce.  It should thicken it.
Serve with rice, beans and tortillas
