Title:
Monaco Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
4 fluid ounces amber beer
2 fluid ounces lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
1/4 teaspoon grenadine syrup

Directions:
Pour the beer, soda, and grenadine syrup in a tall glass. Stir and serve.

Submitter: 
pomplemousse
Description: 
This is a drink that you can order at any bar in France.  It's a little sweet and has a pretty color; I don't like beer, but I like this drink.  I use about 1/4 teaspoon grenadine, but use less or more depending on how sweet you would like your drink.
