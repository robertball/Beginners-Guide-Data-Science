Title:
Chile Rellenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Anaheim chile peppers, charred and peeled
1 package of Queso Fresca cheese, drained and sliced into strips
1 package cream cheese cut into strips
1 cup all-purpose flour for coating
4 eggs, seperated whites from yolks
1 cup cooking oil (Olive)
1 jar Spagetti Sauce (Ragu - Garden Supreme chunky)

Directions:
Broil peppers until blackened.  Remove from heat and put in ziploc bag to steam.
After cooling, working carefully, remove skins.  Cut incision down one side of pepper and remove the top, seeds and membranes from peppers.
Coat the stuffed peppers in flour
Stuff each pepper with a strip of each cheese.
Beat egg whites until very stiff and then while whipping, add yolks.
Pour enough oil in heavy frying pan to reach 1/2 inch in depth and heat over medium-high heat.
Dip each floured stuffed pepper in batter.
Fry until lightly browned on both sides.
Top with Spagetti sauce and cheese

Description: 
Delicious green chiles stuffed with cheese, dipped in a special batter and fried in canola oil.Modified from: Chile Rellenos
