Title:
Japanese Sesame Steak Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2605991.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Soy Sauce
1 cup Vegetable Oil
1/2 cup Sesame Seeds
1 Egg Yolk
1/2 tablespoon Dry Mustard
1 Clove Garlic, Large (Minced)
1/2 cup Chopped Onion (One small onion)
1/2 cup Whipping Cream

Directions:
Place soy sauce, sesame seeds, onion and garlic in a blender and mix on high for 30 seconds.
Add remaining ingredients and blend until fine.
