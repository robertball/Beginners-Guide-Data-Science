Title:
Honey Roasted Cinnamon Chickpeas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz can garbanzo beans
1/2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp honey
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/8 tsp sea salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
Drain and rinse chickpeas and place on paper towel to dry.
Spread chickpeas in a single layer on baking pan. Bake for 45 minutes or until crispy.
Place all ingredients but chickpeas in a bowl and mix well.
While still hot, toss chickpeas in bowl with honey mixture and return them to pan to bake another 10 minutes or so.
Cool before serving.
