Title:
Pique macho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1123344.jpg
Ingredients:
800 g of beef
8 Vienna sausage
4 eggs
6 big potatos
1/2 kg of tomato
1/4 kg of onion
2 chilli peppers (locoto)
1/2 can of beer
salt
garlic
pepper

Directions:
Pique macho is a successful composition of meat, sausage type Vienna, slices of fried potatoes, slices of tomato, onion cut feather and a very spicy locoto slices, you must choose 800 g. of tender beef with a small portion of fat, it is cut into small cubes to eat a single snack, which is seasoned with salt, garlic and pepper.
On a thick hot skillet, add a good amount of oil, when it has begun to boil the pieces of meat and you cover them immediately to preserve all its juice, once the meat is cooked, add the sliced sausage and removed it for 3 minutes, then add the beer, the tomato, onion and pepper, cook for 5 minutes more.
To serve put the potatoes fried, then the meat and finally the egg.
