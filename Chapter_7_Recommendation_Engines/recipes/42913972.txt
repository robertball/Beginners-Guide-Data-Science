Title:
Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 Cups Sugar
1/2 Cup margarine or butter, softened
1 tsp Vanilla
2 eggs
2 3/4 cups flour
1 tsp cream of tartar
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tbsp sugar
2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Heat oven to 400
In large bowl, combine sugar, margarine, vanilla and eggs;
blend well
Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup
level off
stir in flour, cream of tartar, soda, and salt
blend well
Shape into 1 inch balls
Combine 2 Tbsp sugar and cimmamon
Roll balls in sugar mixture
Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet
Bake at 400 for 8 - 10 min or until set
