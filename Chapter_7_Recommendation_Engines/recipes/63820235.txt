Title:
Chocolate Fudge made with Velveeta Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Velveeta Cheese
1 lb butter
4 lbs powdered sugar
1 1/4 Cup Cocoa
3 cups of Chopped pecans
1 tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Sift sugar and Cocoa together.
Melt cheese and butter together in double boiler.
Pour melted cheese and butter over sugar and Cocoa mixture Add pecans & Vanilla.
Mix together and pour* in pans.
Note: Takes a long time to harden. Keeps for a long time in refrigerator. The longer you keep it, the better it gets. *It is thicker than for pouring. ~Jennifer
