Title:
Gluten Free All Purpose Flour Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup sweet rice flour (white or brown)
¼ cup tapioca starch/flour
¼ cup cornstarch or potato starch

Directions:
Mix ingredients for use in your favourite recipe to begin to make it gluten free

Submitter: 
sparkytoo
Description: 
This recipe is on the livingwithout.com website, and can be substituted cup-for-cup for wheat flour in all your recipes to make it gluten-free.  (Just make sure that there is no hidden gluten a.k.a. wheat starch, wheat flour, whole wheat, or rye flour in any of the ingredients as gluten is the starch used to hold many processed food.)
