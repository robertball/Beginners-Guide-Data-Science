Title:
Pasta Slaw Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985289.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package Bow-Tie Pasta
2 cups Broccoli Slaw Mix
1 (16 ounce) can Light Red Kidney Beans
1/3 cup thinly sliced Green Onions
1/2 teaspoon Celery Seed
1 tablespoon coarsely grained Brown Mustard
1 1/3 cups Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Chopped parsley to taste

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions.
Rinse and drain kidney beans.  Combine pasta, beans, broccoli slaw mix and onions.
In separate bowl, combine slaw dressing, celery seed, and brown mustard. Toss pasta mixture with dressing mixture. Garnish with parsley.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
Sure to be a family favorite, this unique slaw combines two classic summer salads - pasta salad and coleslaw!
