Title:
Fast and easy pizza sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can crushed tomatoes
1 six oz. can tomato paste
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. dry basil
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a food processor and it's ready to go! As simple as that. Enjoy

Submitter: 
Patrick
Description: 
There are so many ways to make pizza sauce. My way is fast, simple and requires no cooking. Just toss it together and it's ready to go. As tangy and delicious as any NY pizza house sauce recipe, I guarantee it!
