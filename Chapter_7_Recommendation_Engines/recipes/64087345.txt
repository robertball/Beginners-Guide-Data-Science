Title:
Cauliflower Potato Soup (natural) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium size head cauliflower, stemmed and chopped
4 large potatoes, cut into 1-inch pieces
5 baby carrots, cut into 1/2-inch slices, or more to taste
1-2 onions, cut small, stirfry until just brown
2 cup chicken stock
1/2 cup milk
1/2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, or chives, more to taste
1/2 - 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Stir cauliflower, potatoes, carrots, and onion  together in a pot; add enough water to cover and stir in chicken stock. Bring to a simmer and cook until cauliflower is tender, about 10 minutes.
Transfer vegetable mixture and about 1 cup cooking liquid to a slow cooker. Stir milk and cream cheese, into vegetable mixture.
Cook on Low, stirring occasionally, for 2 1/2 hours.
Stir parsley into soup and continue cooking until vegetable are tender and flavors blend, 1/2 to 1 1/2 hours. Top with shredded Cheddar cheese.
