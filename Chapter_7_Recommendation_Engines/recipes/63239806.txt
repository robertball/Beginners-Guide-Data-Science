Title:
Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mixture #1:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup cocoa powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
Mixture #2:
1/2 cup sour milk (2 teaspoons vinegar + 1/2 cup evaporated milk)
1 cup boiling water
Mixture #3:
1 cup butter (or oil)
2 cups sugar (I always use less)
3 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Sift together Mixture #1; set aside.
Combine Mixture #2; set aside.
Cream butter until light and fluffy. Add sugar gradually while continue mixing. Add eggs, one at a time beating well after each addition.
Mix in Mixture #1 alternately with Mixture #2, beginning and ending with the flour mixture.
Pour in pan and bake at 350 degrees F for about 50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Frosting: Double boil the following until thick or of spreading consistency: 1 can condensed milk, 2 tablespoons cocoa powder, 1/2 teaspoon coffee, and 1/4 cup butter
