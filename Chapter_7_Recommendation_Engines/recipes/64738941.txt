Title:
Parmesan Crusted Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fish
1/4 c mayo
1-2 T sour cream
1/4 shredded parmesan cheese
2 T parsley
2 T basil
2 garlic cloves, minced/grated
squeeze lemon juice
salt
pinch of smoked paprika (optional)
drops of cholulah or other hot sauce (optional)

Directions:
combine everything except fish
spread over fish
bake fish until begins to flake (450? 400? 350?)
broil for couple minutes until topping is browned

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
good on cod, grouper, etc
