Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz. Cream Cheese, cold
5 Tbsp. Butter, softened
2 t. vanilla
2 C. powdered sugar, sifted after measuring

Directions:
Beat cream cheese with butter and vanilla until combined.
Gradually add powdered sugar. (Add more sifted powdered sugar
until taste and consistency is to liking.)

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
With carrot cake, there is just no exception, you have to have the cream cheese frosting.
