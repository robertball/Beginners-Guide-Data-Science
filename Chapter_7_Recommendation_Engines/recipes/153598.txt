Title:
Spice Islands Indian Curry Couscous Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977083.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
2/3 cup couscous
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 teaspoons Spice Islands速 Curry Powder
1 teaspoon Spice Islands速 Garlic Salt
1/2 teaspoon Spice Islands速 Crushed Red Pepper
1 cup chopped bell peppers
1/2 cup minced red onion
1 small zucchini, chopped
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained, rinsed

Directions:
Boil water in a small saucepan, remove from heat and add couscous. Cover; let stand 5 minutes. Uncover and cool 10 minutes at room temperature.
Combine oil, lemon juice, curry powder, garlic salt and crushed red peppers in a small bowl. Stir well and let stand 10 minutes. Combine vegetables, beans, and couscous in large bowl. Pour dressing over salad; toss to coat. Cover; refrigerate 1 hour to blend flavors.
