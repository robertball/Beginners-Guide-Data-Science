Title:
Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/325535.jpg
Ingredients:
8oz Cream Cheese
1 Cup (10x)Confectioners sugar
Small container of Cool Whip
1/2 Cup Peanut Butter
1 Box instant Chocolate Pudding
1 Box instant Vanilla Pudding
3 Cups Milk
1/2 Cup Ground Peanuts
1 Stick of Butter, melted
1 Cup Flour
Chocolate bar, optional

Directions:
Deep Dish 12" Pie Crust:  Preheat oven to 350.  Mix ground peanuts, 1 stick of butter, and 1 cup flour. Pat into pie dish.  Bake 15-20 minutes or until light golden brown. Let cool.
Whisk the box of chocolate pudding, box of vanilla pudding, and 3 cups of milk together.  Put in refrigerator- let set.
Mix 8oz. cream cheese, 1 cup 10x sugar, 1 cup cool whip, and 1/2 cup peanut butter.  Lay mixture in pie crust around sides too.  Next layer pudding mixture over cream cheese mixture.  Put remaining cool whip on top.  Crack and sprinkle a chocolate bar on top.  Put in refrigerator overnight. Enjoy!
