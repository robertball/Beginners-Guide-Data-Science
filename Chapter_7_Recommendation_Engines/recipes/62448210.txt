Title:
Creamy Ravioli with Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 tsp. olive oil
½ cup chicken stock
2 cups light cream
¼ cup parmesan cheese
1 lb (500 g) ravioli
2 cups chopped fresh spinach
8 oz. (250 g) crab
2 Tbsp. dill
¼ cup parmesan (for sprinkling)

Directions:
Saute garlic in oil, add chicken stock and let simmer.
Add cream and parmesan, stirring until cheese has melted.
Cook ravioli.
Add ravioli, spinach, crab and dill to sauce and heat through.
Stir until sauce is at desired consistency.
