Title:
Easy Crispy Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Paprika, Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder, dried Oregano, 1/4 cup dried parsley flakes, (3) Lemons, 1/2 cup oil, chicken pieces (10 - 12 chicken thighs)

Directions:
Season both sides of chicken with the following ingredients:
Paprika, Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder & dried oregano
Place chicken skin side up in pan.
In separate bowl, combine 1/2 cup oil with 3 freshly squeezed lemons (including pulp); add parsley flakes to lemon & oil mixture.  Pour over chicken and bake at 325 deg. for 1 hr. uncovered.  DO NOT COVER
ENJOY.....

Submitter: 
BETTYCOOK
Description: 
So moist and lemony with a crispy outer skin.  Also delicious cold.
