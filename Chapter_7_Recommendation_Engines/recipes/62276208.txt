Title:
German Rotkohl-Spiced Red Cabbage w/Apples created by Cathy, inspired by BecR Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon, thick sliced is best
1/2 lge red onion, finely chopped
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 tsp ginger, finely chopped
1/3 cinnamon stick
6 cps red cabbage, shredded
1 tsp marjoram
1 tsp garam masala
1/3 cp cider vinegar
1/3 cp brn sugar or real maple syrup
1 bay leaf
1 whole cloves
1 large apple, unpealed and thinly sliced

Directions:
1. Cook bacon in Dutch oven. Remove bacon.
2. Add the onion, minced garlic, ginger and cinnamon stick and cook for about 5 mins, until the onion has softened.
3. Add the cabbage, stirring and cooking, for ten minutes.Then add the marjoram, garam masala, vinegar, bay leaf, whole cloves and apple.
4. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 1 to 1 1/4 hrs.
5. Stir in brown sugar or maple syrup and cook for an additional 15 mins, or until most of the liquid has cooked away.
6. Remove bay leaf and cinnamon stick before serving.

Submitter: 
Cathy Myers
Description: 
Wonderful, aromatic side dish that pairs well with Schnitzel, Sauerbraten, Swedish Meatballs or Konigsberger Klopse (German Meatballs in Creamy Lemon Caper Sauce)
