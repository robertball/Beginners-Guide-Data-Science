Title:
Mochanut Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1987888.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut milk
1/2 cup brewed coffee, hot
1 tablespoon turbinado sugar (such as Sugar in the RawÂ®)
1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa

Directions:
Add the cocoa and sugar to the hot coffee and stir until combined well. Divide equally into 2 coffee cups.
Heat the coconut milk using a milk frother over the stove top, about 1 minute. When warm begin to froth the milk and remove from the heat source.
Divide the warm coconut milk equally into the two cups.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Warm and frothy coconut milk make this traditional coffee extra special. The coconut is subtle and the cocoa flavour is well balanced with the sweetness turning out a mochalicious dairy free treat for your morning coffee.Modified from: Mocha Smoothie
