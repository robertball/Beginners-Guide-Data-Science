Title:
Devine Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bananas
4 large strawberries
1/3 cup blue berries
1/3 cup raspberries
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup water
1 tbs cinnamon
1 tbs white sugar
2 tbs hazelnut liquid coffee creamer

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth. You may substitute black berries for blue berries and any flavor of liquid coffee creamer to suit your tastes.

This smoothie is really sweet and tasty.
