Title:
Morning Glory Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups grated carrot
1 apple, peeled and grated
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
1/2 cup raisins
2 cups flour
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. cinnamon
3 eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup plain, low-fat yogurt

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss together carrot, apple, pecans, coconut and raisins.
Measure in flour, brown sugar, soda, baking powder and cinnamon.
Stir until evenly mixed.
In smaller bowl, whisk together eggs, oil and yogurt.
Pour into carrot mixture and still till combined.
Spoon batter into greased or paper-lined muffin tins, filling to the top.
Bake @ 350 x 20-25 min. until springy when u touch the top.
Makes about 15 muffins.
Can be nuked from frozen x 30 seconds on high for a quick healthy snack ;)
