Title:
tomato garlic chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves (pounded to 1/4 thickness)
1 can of peeled and diced tomatoes 14.5 ounces
1/2 cup of white wine
1 tablespoon butter
2 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 tablespoon all purpose flour
salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Mix the flour, salt and pepper. Coat the chicken lightly
Heat the olive oil and butter in a skilled over medium heat.
Add the chicken and cook for until the chicken is lightly browned on each side.
Add the can of tomatoes, wine and paprika to the skilled, cover and let simmer until the juices run clear.
Wait 5 minutes before serving.
