Title:
Avocado Black Bean Salad over Arugula Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 red bell pepper, diced
2 scallions, thinly sliced
1/4 cup pumpkin seeds, toasted
2 tbs extra virgin olive oil
3/4 tbs fresh lime juice
pinch of sea salt
4 cups baby arugula
1 ripe avocado, sliced
1 15 oz can black beans, drained & rinsed
2 tbs fresh cilantro

Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients except arugula and cilantro, and toss gently to mix.  Let chill a bit if you prefer otherwise divide the arugula into four plates and top each with a portion of the avocado mixture.
