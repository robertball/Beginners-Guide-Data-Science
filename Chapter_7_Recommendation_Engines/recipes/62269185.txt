Title:
Bourbon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
1-2 Tbls Corn Starch
2 tablespoon olive oil
1 garlic clove, minced
1 teaspoon fresh grated ginger
3/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 cup apple juice
1/3 cup light brown sugar
2 tablespoons ketchup
1 tablespoon rice vinegar
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Dust Chicken in cornstarch then lightly brown in olive oil 5-10 minutes.  In separate bowl add all other ingredients, whisk to combine. Pour over chicken, heat to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
One of those meals you can whip up in a couple minutes! Made a few minor changes to a recipe I found on the internet.  Smells wonderful cooking. Great with rice!
