Title:
Nana's French Cherry Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon almond extract
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1 prebaked pie crust
1 can cherry pie filling

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla and almond extracts. Beat with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. In a separate medium bowl, beat whipping cream until stiff. Carefully fold cream cheese mixture into whipped cream. Spread into the bottom of a prebaked pie crust. Cover with cherry pie filling and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
GoodGoneGirl
Description: 
This recipe was handed down from my great grandmother. I don't know where she got it, but it is one of my favorites. Super fast, easy to make, and most importantly - delicious!
