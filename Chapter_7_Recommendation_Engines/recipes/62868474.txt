Title:
Soba Noodle Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/688695.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 Cup Oil
1 small onion, finely chopped
4 scallions, finely chopped
12 oz. soba noodles
4 Eggs, beaten
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper

Directions:
1 Heat 2 tbsp. oil in a saute pan. Add onions and saute on medium heat until translucent.
2 Cook noodles per package directions, rinse, drain and toss with 1 tbsp. oil, allow to cool for 15 minutes.
3 Mix eggs with salt and pepper and add to noodles.
4 Preheat oven to 375. Pour the rest of the oil into a 9Ã—9 inch baking dish or loaf pan. Heat pan with oil until hot.
5 Pour noodle mixture into pan and bake for 45 minutes or until golden.
6 Serve with a ponzu dipping sauce if dessired.

Submitter: 
Tamar Genger
Description: 
This is a great fusion of Jewish and Asian cuisine. The texture of the soba noodle works so well in this form. With the scallions and a dipping sauce it is a Jewish Scallion Pancake.
