Title:
Fruit Smoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 sliced banana
1 cup frozen fruit
2 Tbsp plain yogurt
1/4 cup quick-cook oats
1 cup low-fat milk/apple juice
1 cup ice

Directions:
Combine all in blender.
Blend until smooth.  If the mixture is too thick, add more milk or juice, and blend again.  Add a bit of honey if desired.
