Title:
Cream Sherry Poppy Seed Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix(18.25 oz)
1 box vanilla instant pudding (3.4 oz)
3 eggs
1 1/2 Cup sour cream
1/4 Cup vegetable oil
1/2 Cup cream sherry
3 Tbs. poppyseed
2 Tbs. powdered sugar for garnish

Directions:
Pre heat oven at 350*
Grease bottom and sides of a 10 inch bundt pan.
Beat cake mix,vanilla instant pudding,eggs,sour cream,oil,cream sherry and poppy seeds on low speed for 30 seconds, then on medium speed for 2 minutes scraping bowl occasionally.
Pour into greased bundt pan.
Bake for 50-55 minutes or until a tooth pick inserted in the middle comes out clean.
Invert immediately onto serving dish and sift powdered sugar over cake.
