Title:
Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg ( 10 oz) frozen mixed vegetables
1/3 cup butter
1/3 cup flour
1/3 cup chopped onion
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 ( 14 oz) can chicken broth
2/3 cup milk
2 1/2 - 3 cups of cooked cut up chicken
2 ready made pie crusts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Melt butter in sauce pan, stir in flour, onion salt and pepper.
Cook stirring until bubbly.
Remove from heat
Stir in broth and milk
Heat to boiling, boil for 1 minute.
Stir in vegetables and chicken.
Pour into a 9x9 pan with one of the crusts lining it.
Place second crust on top and seal the edges.
Bake for 35 minutes

Submitter: 
Lori McTavish Bizjak
Description: 
This is a quick and easy chicken pot pie that never fails to turn out delicious.
