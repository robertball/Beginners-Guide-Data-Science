Title:
Mango Craze Juice Blend Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/44007.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups diced mango
1 1/2 cups chopped fresh or frozen peaches
1/4 cup chopped orange segments
1/4 cup chopped and pitted nectarine
1/2 cup orange juice
2 cups ice

Directions:
Place mango, peaches, orange, nectarine, orange juice, and ice into a blender. Blend for 1 minute, or until smooth.

Submitter: 
Austin Geraldson
Description: 
A great smoothie for the true mango lover!
