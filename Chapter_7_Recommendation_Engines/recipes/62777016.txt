Title:
Grilled Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ears of corn, husked and cleaned
1/4 C. softened butter
2 T grated Parmesan cheese
1 t chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Grill corn that has been wrapped in foil on med-high heat for 15-20 min., turn occ.
Mix remaining ingred. and spread on corn

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Calls for corn wrapped in foil and grill for 15-20 min. Quick with simple ingeredients
