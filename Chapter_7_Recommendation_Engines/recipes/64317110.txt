Title:
Pea and Cheddar Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 strips thick-sliced bacon, cooked and diced
1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen green peas, thawed
1/4 cup diced cheddar
1/4 cup sliced scallions
3 tbsp plain Greek yogurt
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp Dijon mustard
salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine peas, cheese, and scallions in a medium bowl.
In a small bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, and mustard.  Stir into pea mixture.  Season with salt and pepper.
Stir in bacon right before serving.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
A light pea salad for summertime dinners.
