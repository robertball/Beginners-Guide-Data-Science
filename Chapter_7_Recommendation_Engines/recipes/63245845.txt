Title:
Streusel-Topped Blueberry Baked French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/847459.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf French bread, cut into 10-12 1-inch thick slices
4 eggs
1 cup milk
1/4 cup half and half
6 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla
Streusel Topping:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
pinch of salt
1/4 cup cold butter, cut into pieces
1/2 cup fresh blueberries

Directions:
Night before:  Grease a 9 x 13 inch baking pan.  Combine eggs, milk, half and half, sugar and vanilla.  Dip the bread sliced in the egg mixture and place, side by side, in the prepared pan.  Cover and store in the refrigerator.
Night before:  Mix together flour, brown sugar, cinnamon and salt.  Cut in butter using fork or food processor until mixture looks crumbly.  Cover and store in refrigerator.
When you're ready to bake the French Toast:  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Remove casserole from refrigerator and sprinkle blueberries over top.  Sprinkle streusel crumb mixture on top of bread and blueberries.
Bake 45-55 minutes, or until set and golden brown.

Submitter: 
FROGERINA
Description: 
I wanted a beautiful, make-ahead French toast that was easy, and delicious enough for company...so I created this!  No syrup needed....just enjoy!
