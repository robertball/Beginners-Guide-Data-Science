Title:
Tomato Clamart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289725.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Tomatoes
1/4 CUP Butter
1 1/4 CUPS Peas, Frozen
TT Salt
TT Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ÂºF.
Peel the tomatoes and cut off about a 1/4" of the top.  Remove the seeds and juice.  Season the inside with salt and pepper.
Place tomatoes in the oven until half-cooked (about 10 minutes).
Melt the butter over medium heat and add the peas.  Season to taste with salt and pepper and cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
Stuff the tomatoes with the peas and finish cooking in the oven for 5 to 10 minutes.
