Title:
Oven Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/658263.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
3 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
3-4 pounds boneless stew meat - cut into 1 inch cubes
1/4 cup vegetable oil
4 small cans tomato paste
1/4 cup sugar
6 cups vegetable broth or water
3 cups chopped onion
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
8 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
10 medium carrots, cut into 1 inch pieces
1 pkg. brown gravy (to be used 30 mins before serving)

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt and pepper. Add beef cubes, a few at a time, and roll in flour to coat.
In a Dutch oven (if available) or large pot, brown meat in oil.
Add the tomato paste, water, onion and basil; mix well.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 1 hour.
Add potatoes and carrots. Bake 1 1/2 hours longer or until meat and vegetables are tender.
