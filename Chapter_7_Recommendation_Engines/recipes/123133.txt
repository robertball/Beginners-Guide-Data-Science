Title:
Taco Seasoning Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/179104.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dried minced onion
1/4 cup chili powder
3 tablespoons salt
4 teaspoons cornstarch
1 tablespoon dried minced garlic
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon crushed red pepper flakes
2 teaspoons beef bouillon granules
1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
ADDITIONAL INGREDIENTS:
1 pound ground beef
1/3 cup water

Directions:
Combine the first nine ingredients.  Store in an airtight container in a cool dry place for up to 1 year. To prepare tacos:  In a skillet, brown beef; drain.  Add 2 tablespoons taco seasoning mix and water.  Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
Karen  Owen
Description: 
In Rising Sun, Indiana, Karen Owen creates her own zippy taco seasoning for spicing up Mexican dishes. It's handy and less expensive than buying envelopes of prepared mix from the store.
