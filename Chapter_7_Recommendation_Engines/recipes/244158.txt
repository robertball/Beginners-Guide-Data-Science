Title:
P.A.M. Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4037203.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 pineapple, peeled and cored
2 Granny Smith apples, halved
4 mint leaves, or more to taste
1 fluid ounce lime juice
1 splash water, or as needed for blending

Directions:
Blend pineapple, apples, mint leaves, and lime juice together in a blender until smooth. Add water, if desired, or strain to reach desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Brittany Curtis
Description: 
Great when used as part of a healthy cleanse. Use with clean green juice as a delicious beverage option.
