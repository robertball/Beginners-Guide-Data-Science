Title:
Oriental Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 head(s) cabbage, green, small
1 1/2 medium scallion(s) (green onions), chopped
1 tablespoon oil, dark sesame
1 tablespoon vinegar, rice wine
1 tablespoon sesame seeds, toasted

Directions:
Combine the cabbage, scallions, oil, and vinegar. Toss well and chill until ready to serve. Add the sesame seeds and toss again before serving.
