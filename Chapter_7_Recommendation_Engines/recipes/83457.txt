Title:
Crunchy Mandarin Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985287.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups Green Cabbage, shredded
1 (11 ounce) can Mandarin Oranges, drained
1 cup chopped Celery
3 Green Onions, chopped (tops included)
1/2 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
1 cup Chow Mien Noodles

Directions:
Mix together and refrigerate cabbage, mandarin oranges, celery and green onions.
Before serving add Marzetti(R) Slaw Dressing and chow mein noodles.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
This slaw has the sweetness of mandarin oranges and the crunchy texture of chow mien noodles.
