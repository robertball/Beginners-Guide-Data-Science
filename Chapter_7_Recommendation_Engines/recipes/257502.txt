Title:
Shiraz Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4501767.jpg
Ingredients:
2 small cucumbers, diced
1 tomato, diced
1 onion, diced
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon chopped fresh mint
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix cucumbers, tomato, onion, lemon juice, mint, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Marianne
Description: 
This is a traditional salad eaten as a side dish with almost every Persian meal.
