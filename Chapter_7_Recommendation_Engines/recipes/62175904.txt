Title:
Hot Artichoke and Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 16 oz. container sour cream
1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese (not grated)
1/2 cup grated Romano cheese
4 - 6 cloves garlic, peeled and minced VERY fine
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
salt and pepper to taste
3 - 5 dashes Frank's Hot Sauce
2 (14 ounce) cans artichoke hearts, drained
and chopped VERY fine
1/2 red pepper, chopped VERY fine OR 1 small jar diced pimento peppers, drained
2 boxes frozen chopped spinach, thawed and
drained and chopped, again, VERY fine
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese (not grated)
1/2 cup Panko bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a small baking dish with butter.
In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese, sour cream, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, Romano cheese, garlic, garlic salt, salt and pepper and Frank's Hot Sauce.
Mix in on low speed finely diced artichoke hearts, red peppers and spinach.
Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish.
Combine Panko bread crumbs, mozzarella & 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese.  Sprinkle over spinach mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven 25 - 30 minutes, until internal temperature reaches 165.
Crisp up a couple minutes in broiler until lightly browned.
