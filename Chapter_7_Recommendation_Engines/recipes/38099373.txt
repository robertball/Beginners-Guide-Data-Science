Title:
Fairy Dust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons garlic powder
2 tablespoons dried basil
3 teaspoons dried oregano
1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 1/2 teaspoons black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small jar with lid.  Shake well to mix.

Submitter: 
Christi S.
Description: 
Great seasoning mix for poultry, fish, beef or even on veggies.
