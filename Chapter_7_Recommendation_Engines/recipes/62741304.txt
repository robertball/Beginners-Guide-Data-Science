Italian Bread Crumbs Recipe

Ingredients: Yield 1 cup

1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley flakes

1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon dried basil

Directions:
