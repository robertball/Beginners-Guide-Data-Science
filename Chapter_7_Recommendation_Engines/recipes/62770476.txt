Title:
Crazy Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. Flour
1 C. Sugar
1 Tsp. Baking Soda
1/2 Tsp. Salt
3 Tbsp. Cocoa
1/3 C. oil
1 Tbsp. Vinegar
1 C. Milk
1 Tsp. Vanilla

Directions:
1. Sidt the dry ingredients into an ungreased 8 x 8 inch baking pan.
2. Make three little wells in the dry ingredients.
3. Pour the oil into the first well, Vinegar into the second well,and Vanilla in the third well.
4. Add milk and stir with a fork until the mixture is well blended.
5. Bake in moderate oven, 350 degrees F for 35 minutes.
