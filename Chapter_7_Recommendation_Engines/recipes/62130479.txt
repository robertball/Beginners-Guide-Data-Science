Title:
Gooey Fudgey S'mores Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Betty Crocker Low Fat Fudge Brownie Mix (20.5 oz)
1 c. unsweetened applesauce
1/2 c. fat free sour cream
1 c. mini marshmallows
1/4 c. chocolate chips
5 graham crackers
2 Tbsp. chocolate syrup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine brownie mix with sour cream and applesauce.
Stir in 1/2 c. of marshmallows.
Pour half of batter into 9 by 9 baking pan.
Top with graham crackers.
Pour remaining batter over top.
Sprinkle with remaining marshmallows and chocolate chips.
Bake for 30-40 minutes.
Drizzle wtih chocolate syrup.
Chill for 20 minutes before cutting.
