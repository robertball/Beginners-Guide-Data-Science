Title:
My Mom's Yummy Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados,mashed
1 Tablespoon Miracle Whip
1/8 tsp. garlic powder, or to taste
1/8 salt, or to taste (optional if using salty chips)
1 Roma tomato, chopped
your choice of corn chips

Directions:
Add first 5 ingredients in a small bowl and stir until well mixed. Eat immediately with corn chips.

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
This is my mom's recipe for avocado dip. She didn't like the taste of lemon or lime in hers, so she substituted Miracle Whip, which helps the avocados not to turn brown. I like to serve this with blue corn chips for an interesting color variation!
