Title:
Kickin' Turkey Club Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970798.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (6 inch) flour tortillas
1/2 (8 ounce) tub PHILADELPHIA Spicy Jalapeno Cream Cheese Spread
4 lettuce leaves
8 slices OSCAR MAYER Deli Fresh Smoked Turkey Breast
4 slices OSCAR MAYER Bacon, cooked, drained
1 avocado, sliced
1/2 red pepper, sliced into thin strips

Directions:
Spread tortillas with cream cheese.
Top with remaining ingredients; roll up.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia
Description: 
Spicy jalapeno cream cheese spread on tortillas is topped with turkey, bacon, avocado, and strips of red bell pepper in these quick snack or lunch wraps.
