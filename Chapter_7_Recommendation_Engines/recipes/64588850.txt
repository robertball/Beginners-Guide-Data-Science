Title:
Pork Butt BBQ Garlicky Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lb boneless skinless pork butt
1 white onion small thinly sliced
1/4 cup orange juice
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp lemon juice
2 Tbsp lime juice
6 medium garlic cloves minced
2 tsp kosher salt
2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Pork Butt, Shoulder Butt or Boston Butt are synonymous!
Using a paring knife, poke holes all over the pork.
In a 6qt slow cooker, add the onion and then the pork
In a medium bowl, mix the rest of the ingredients and pour the sauce over the meat, massaging into the holes
Put the lid on and leave fore 8 hours on low.
Using forks, shred the meat and toss it with the sauce.
Serve with simmered collard greens and roasted sweet potatoes.

Submitter: 
Kim
Description: 
8 hours on low or 4 hours on high turning occasionally.
Store leftovers in juice and use for other meals like: pork fried rice, carnitas burrito bowl, Hawaiian flatbread pizza, and shredded pork sweet potato hash
