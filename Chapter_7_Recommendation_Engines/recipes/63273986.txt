Title:
Caramel Nut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. quick oats
1 c. packed brown sugar
1 c. flour
3/4 c. butter or margarine, melted
1/2 t. baking soda
1/4 t. alt
1 pkg. (14 oz.) caramels
1/3 c. milk
1 c. (6 oz) semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 c. chopped walnuts

Directions:
Combine the first six ingredients; sprinkle 1 cup into a greased 9x13 pan (do not press). Bae at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
Using a double boiler/bain marie or CAREFULLY using a saucepan, cook and stir caramels and milk until the caramels are melted. Pour over the crust.
Top with chocolate chips and nuts, then the remainder of the oat mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes. Refrigerate to set the caramel.
