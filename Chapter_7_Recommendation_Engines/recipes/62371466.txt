Title:
Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
dab of butter (at bottom of each cup)
or use the pam with four

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Grease and flour six 6-ounce custard cups.
In a medium bowl beat eggs slightly, Beat in flour, milk and salt until just smooth; being careful not to overbeat. Fill custard cups 1/2 full.
Bake at 450 degrees F (230 degrees C) for 20 minutes. Decrease oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake for 10-20 minutes more. Immediately remove from cups and serve piping hot.

Submitter: 
Diane Manzella
Description: 
The secret to light and airy popovers is a very light touch in mixing and a very hot oven for baking.  These are best served hot from the oven.
Try serving with cinnomon butter.Modified from: Popovers
