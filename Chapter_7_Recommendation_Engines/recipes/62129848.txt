Title:
Parmesan Romano Oven Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium baking potatoes, unpeeled
1/2 cup butter, melted
2 teaspoons minced onions
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Cut potatoes into 1/4 inch slices. Grease a baking sheet and place in a single layer. Mix together the butter, minced onion, garlic salt, pepper, and paprika. Brush onto one side of the potatoes, then turn and brush the other side.
Bake at 425 for 15 minutes. Then broil for 5 minutes, or until potatoes are a nice crispy golden brown.
Sprinkle with mixture of Parmesan and Romano cheese.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Janine
Description: 
We eat these up as soon as they come out of the oven. There are never any leftovers! We hope you and yours enjoy them also.
