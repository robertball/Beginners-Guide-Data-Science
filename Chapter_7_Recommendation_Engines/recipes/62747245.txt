Title:
IBBZ Rice, Broccoli Cheese Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. leftover cooked rice, approximately
1 1/2 c. cooked, drained, chopped broccoli
1 c. Mozzarella Cheese, shredded
1/4 c. Ranch Flavored Dressing
2 eggs, lightly beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Exchange cheese for your favorite. This is very forgiving recipe.  Mix all ingredients until well blended. Spoon mixture evenly into greased muffin tins. Bake 25 min. or until lightly browned. Serve with extra dressing on the side or add to top of each as you serve.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
A fun and tasty way to make a family favorite a little more interesting to see and eat. Children will eat this just because they look fun!
