Title:
Baked Slow Cooker Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 to 3 pound) whole chicken
salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon paprika
Seasoning salt garlic onion
Olive oil or butter

Directions:
Wad three pieces of aluminum foil into 3 to 4 inch balls, and place them in the bottom of the slow cooker.
Brown chicken all over in olive oil.
Season witj veggies and spices and place in the slow cooker on top of the crumbled aluminum foil.
Set the slow cooker to High for 2 hours, then turn down to Low for about 5 hours, or until the chicken is no longer pink and the juices run clear.
