Title:
PB and J with Banana Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986627.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pieces Pepperidge Farm® Goldfish™ Honey Whole Wheat Sandwich Bread
3 tablespoons reduced fat peanut butter
2 tablespoons strawberry jam
1 banana, thinly sliced

Directions:
Spread 1 1/2 tablespoons peanut butter on each bottom bread half. Spread 1 tablespoon jam on each top bread half. Divide the banana between the bottom bread halves. Top with the top bread halves, jam-side down.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Our version of this comforting sandwich uses strawberry jam...but you can use your favorite PB & J ingredients and then add some sliced banana...it's a winning combination.
