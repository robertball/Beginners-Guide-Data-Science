Title:
Campfire Bundles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion
4 med. potatoes, sliced 1/2" thick
6 med. carrots, sliced 1/4" thick
1 small cabbage, sliced (optional)
2 med. tomatoes, chopped
1-1 1/2 lbs fully cooked sausage, 1/2" pieces (or use burger patties/chicken breast)
1/2 cup butter
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Place veggies in order listed on 3 pieces of double-layered foil (about 18" x 18").
Add meat.
Dot with butter; sprinkle with salt & pepper (or other spices you like).
Fold foil around the mixture and seal tightly.
Grill, covered, over med. heat x 30 min.
Turn & grill another 30 min. longer or until veggies are tender.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A full meal in foil packages - cooked over the campfire or BBQ
