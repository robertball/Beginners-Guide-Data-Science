Title:
Sazon Dry Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon ground coriander
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon ground annatto seeds (or paprika)
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust for your own liking)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix well.
Store in an airtight container.
1 1/2 teaspoons of this mix equals one packet of commercial Sazon
Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Teri Draga
Description: 
I couldn't find this seasoning in the store so I made my own. I also don't like to use a lot of salt and prefer being able to monitor my own intake.

Enjoy!!
