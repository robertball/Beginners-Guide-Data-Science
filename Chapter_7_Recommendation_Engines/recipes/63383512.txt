Title:
Mock and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup diced, cooked ham
4 oz cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoons butter
1 package extra firm tofu, chopped roughly
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded parmesan
1 chopped yellow onion
2 cups chopped cauliflower, steamed
1 tbls garlic powder
1 tsp white pepper
1 tsp mustard powder
1/2 tsp sea salt (or to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Melt butter in pan
Carmelize onion in butter
Remove from heat, add cheddar, cream cheese, tofu and spices
Mix in cauliflower and ham
Add ingredients to casserole dish
Top dish with parmesan
heat in oven for 10 minutes

Submitter: 
CookingBee
Description: 
Low carb and yummy cheese heaven. A good replacement for those craving homestyle mac and cheese without the pasta. Regular mustard can be used if you don't have mustard powder.
