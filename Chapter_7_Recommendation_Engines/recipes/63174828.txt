Title:
Pecan-Crusted Trout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 trout filets
flour
1 egg, beaten
1/2 c crushed pecans
1 t dried rosemary
1 t salt
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Mix together pecans and spices.
Coat filets in flour, shake off excess. Dip into beaten egg.
Press pecan mixture onto filets and place on a sprayed baking sheet.
Bake for 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Devin
Description: 
Freshwater trout coated in a delicious crunchy pecan coating. Serve this with a savory brandy or lemon butter sauce.
