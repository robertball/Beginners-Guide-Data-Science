Title:
Taco Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1336179.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
1/4 C. water
1/4 tsp. chili powder
1 1/2 tsp. cumin
1 1/2 tsp. onion powder
1 T. white distilled vinegar
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
1/4 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper

Directions:
Pour tomato sauce in a small pot, whisk in rest of ingredients.  Heat through, then let simmer, lidded, for about 15 minutes. Transfer to non-reactive container for storage.

Submitter: 
Abbey
Description: 
Skip the artificial and unnecessary chemicals and make your own taco sauce! This tastes even better than bottled, without the use of high fructose corn syrup.
