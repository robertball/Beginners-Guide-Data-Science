Title:
Chicken and Rice Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags of minute rice
1 can of 98% fat free cream of mushroom soup
1 can of peas; drained
1/2 lb chicken breast-diced
1 tbs butter spread
1 tsp garlic powder
pepper to taste

Directions:
sautee chicken in butter and garlic salt until cooked
add minute rice to boiling water;drain water
combine rice and chicken
add cream of mushroom soup- DO NOT ADD WATER
add drained peas
simmer until heated thoroughly

Submitter: 
Jen G
Description: 
This is a really simple recipe.  I personally don't like peas, but I promise they really add to this meal.  It is easy,healthy and quite filling!  Perfect meal when you are short on time.
