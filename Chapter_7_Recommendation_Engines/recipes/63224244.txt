Title:
Southwestern Dip for Avocado Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/837828.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup light sour cream
1/2 cup mayo
1 tbs diced chives
1/2 tbs diced parsley
1 tsp dill weed
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp cumin
1 clove of minced garlic
1 tsp hot sauce
pinch of salt

Directions:
Mix all together and chill.
