Title:
Pineapple Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz malibu rum
2 oz amaretto
8 oz pineapple juice

Directions:
1. Fill a tall tumbler with ice.
2.add malibu rum and amaretto.
3.fill glass with pineapple juice, stir and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jen Camilleri
Description: 
This is a great take on the typical malibu run and pineapple drink.  Just downsize the portions for a great shot as well!
