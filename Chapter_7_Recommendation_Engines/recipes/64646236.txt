Title:
LTF DTox: Lemon-Garlic Marinated Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 garlic cloves, crushed
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
Sea salt and black pepper to taste
4 chicken breasts
1/4 cup parsley, mint or cilantro, chopped (optional)

Directions:
1. Place crushed garlic in a small bowl.
2. Add lemon juice, olive oil, salt and pepper to make a marinade.
3. Place chicken in a container or large Ziploc bag; pour marinade over chicken; seal container and place in the refrigerator.
4. Marinate at least 3.5 hours up to 6 hours.
5. Grill chicken until no longer pink in the middle, or bake at 350 degrees for about 30 min.
