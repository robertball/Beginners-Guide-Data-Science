Title:
Hash Brown Crust for Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups frozen hashbrowns
1/4 c melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
Thaw and drain hash browns.
Lay hash browns between layers of paper towels to dry well.
Mix hash browns with butter.
Press mixture into and up the sides of  9-inch pie plate.
Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown and starting to crisp.
Cool.
Fill with your favorite quiche filling and bake at 350 degrees until egg is set or testing with knife comes out clean.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Crisp tasty and quick to make crust for quiche filling of your choice.
