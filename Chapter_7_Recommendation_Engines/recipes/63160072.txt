Title:
Pumpkin  pie with chocolate custard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/810196.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 C. Pumpkin puree
1 C. granulated sugar
2 eggs
1 C. Milk
3 oz. chopped semi sweet chocolate
3 Tbsp. cornstarch
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. cloves
Crust
1 C. All purpose flour
1/2 C. whole wheat pastry flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 C. lard
8 to 10 Tbsp. cold water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit
Put lard in freezer for 10 to 15 minutes before making pie crust
Assemble flours in a medium bowl and add lard into the mixture with a pastry cutter until the size of a small pea.
With a fork stir the dough adding the cold water a Tablespoon at a time until it comes together well.
Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface and place in a 9 inch pie plate and set aside.
Combine pumpkin puree and sugar in a large mixing bowl and stir together until sugar is completely dissolved.
Break eggs into a small cup and beat lightly before adding to the pumpkin puree
In a small saucepan over low heat melt chopped chocolate adding milk and cornstarch blending well until thickened. Store in refrigerator until cooled
Add cooled pudding to the pumpkin mixture and stir in spices mixing all together.
Pour into unbaked pie shell and place in preheated oven for approximately 1 hr or until pie tests done in the middle.
Serve with a dollop of whipped cream on top

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
Here is a pumpkin pie that has some extra flavour added in the pie with chocolate pudding blended in to make a very dense chocolate custard that adds moisture to the pumpkin and makes a very good blend together with regular pumpkin pie spices to give it a decadent taste.
