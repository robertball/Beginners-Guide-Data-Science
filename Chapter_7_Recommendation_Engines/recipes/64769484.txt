Title:
Sausage Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb sausage
1 package pepperoni
1 package (2 cups) shredded cheese
3 eggs
1 package pizza dough
1 tablespoon parmesean cheese
1 tsp garlic salt
flour

Directions:
Place dough in bowl and place damp paper towel on top, then allow time for dough to rise (approx 30 mins)
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cook sausage and then mix with 2 well-beaten eggs.
Spread flour so dough doesn't stick
Roll out dough in rectangle shape.
Place sausage and egg mixture on dough, then top with shredded cheese and pepperoni.
Roll up the doughand spread the rest of the egg mixture on top of loaf.
Top with parmesean cheese sprinkle and garlic salt.
Bake at 350 degrees until golden brown.
