Title:
Cookie Press Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
2 cups sugar
3 eggs (whipped)
3 cups all-purpose flour
3 tablespoons baking powder
2 tablespoons water
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions:
Mix shortening and sugar until creamy. Beat in eggs, baking powder and 1 cup flour. Add water and spices. Gradually ass remaining flour until dough is stiff.
Roll the dough and insert into Cookie Press. Take desired disc and make cookies on ungreased baking sheet. Before baking sprinkle granulated sugar on each cookie. Bake at 400* F for 6-10 minutes.
