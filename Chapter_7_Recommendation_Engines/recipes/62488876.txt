Title:
Mexican Tortilla Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/491408.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups shredded cooked chicken
1 (4 ounce) cans chopped green chilies
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted
1 10oz can tomatoes with green chiles(undrained)
1 14.5 oz can tomatoes with onions and peppers (undrained)
12 corn or flour tortillas
1 14.5 oz can black beans, drained
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
1.In a bowl, combine the chicken, chilies,soups, tomatoes and beans and 1 cup shredded cheddar. Layer half of the tortillas on the bottom of a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan, cutting to fit pan if desired. Top with half of the chicken mixture and half of the cheese. Repeat layers. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes.  For best results, broil dish for last several minutes of cooking.

Submitter: 
Christine W
Description: 
This incredible casserole dish is full of flavor and quick to make. Make as spicy as you want by adding hot peppers, sometimes I throw in a can of corn also!  Serve with a dollop of sour cream on top for a flavorfish mexican inspired dished!
