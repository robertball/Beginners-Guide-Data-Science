Title:
Cherie's Pumpkin Blood Pressure  Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box o' tofu
1 can o' pumpkin puree (not pie filling)
1/2 cup o' freshly ground flax seed (I use a small coffee grinder in 2 batches)
sweetener to taste
pumpkin pie spice to taste

Directions:
Blend in a blender. Refrigerate. It get's stiffer after a few hours. Eat 1/2 cup a day, see results in a few weeks.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Lower your blood pressure by eating this pudding.
