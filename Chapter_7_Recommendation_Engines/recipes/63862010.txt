Title:
Gluten Free / Dairy Free Chex Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups of Chex
3 Tablespoons of Margarine (we use Earths Balance or Nucoa) or Butter
3 Tablespoons of Brown Sugar (or 2 tablespoons of maple syrup)

Directions:
Directions
Preheat your oven to 350
Melt the margarine and brown sugar on the stove or in the microwave.
Pour the buttery and sugary mixture in a food processor. Then add the two cups of chex. Blend until crumbly.
Pour it in a greased pie tin. Press it evenly.
Stick the pie tin in the over and bake for 8-10 minutes until the crust has taken on a nice light brown color. Let it cool for at least 30 minutes to an hour before filling it.

Submitter: 
Tiffany Donnelly
Description: 
Easy fast pie crust for those gluten free / dairy free guests at the table.
