Title:
Skinny Creamy Dill Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1002281.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup nonfat plain yogurt
1/4 cup fat free sour cream
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
1 or 2 tablespoons skim milk

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl and stir.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
This is so yummy, no one will know it's healthy. Perfect over a leafy green salad at a BBQ or leave out the milk and use it as a dip.
