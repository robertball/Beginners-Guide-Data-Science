Title:
Crumb Topping for Pies II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup butter, diced
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together the sugar and flour. Mix in butter and vanilla with a pastry knife just until the topping is crumbly.  Top your pie before baking.

Submitter: 
Sidial
Description: 
A brown sugar and vanilla crumb topping or streusel that can be used on top of pies, muffins or even coffee cakes.Modified from: Crumb Topping for Pies
