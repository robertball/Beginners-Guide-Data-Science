Title:
Easy cheesy mexican rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups boiling water
1 cup uncooked rice
1 16 oz jar of salsa..i prefer pace chunky mild..but you can add or take away the heat
1 cup shredded mexican cheese

Directions:
Prepare rice as per directions on the package. When rice is done simply stir in jar of salsa and cheese.

Submitter: 
REDNECKGRL
Description: 
Easy rice recipe. Made this one up for my husband who hates rice, to go along with cheesy quesadillas i make for a quick dinner, lunch or snack.
