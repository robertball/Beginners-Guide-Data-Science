Title:
Better (for you) Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup light butter or margarine
1 cup fat free half and half
1 tsp cornstarch
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan and or Romano cheese
1 tsp fresh black pepper
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, optional

Directions:
In a large pot, melt butter.  Add garlic and saute approximately 3 min.
In a seperate dish, add cornstarch to half and half and mix well.  Add half and half mixture and black pepper to pot. Simmer 5 min.
Add cheese and whisk quickly until melted into creamy mixture.
Serve over pasta and top with optional fresh parsley.

Submitter: 
Christine W
Description: 
A creamy and wonderful alfredo sauce with a lot less fat but all the flavor of traditional alfredo.  Serve over your favorite pasta.
