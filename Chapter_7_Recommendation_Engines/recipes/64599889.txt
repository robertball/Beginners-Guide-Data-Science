Title:
Pani Puri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 can garbanzo beans
1 cucumber - peeled, seeded, diced
1 red onion - diced
2 small yukon gold potatoes
1/4 cup cilantro
1 lemon
1 green chile (optional)
salt (to taste)
To serve:
sev (to taste)
20 pani puri shells
mint chutney
tamarind chutney

Directions:
Microwave yukon gold potatoes for 3 min and mash (alternatively you can bake them)
Mix together filling ingredients and chill for 2 hours (or more)
When ready to eat, mix sev into the filling, spoon into pani puri shells and top with chutneys
