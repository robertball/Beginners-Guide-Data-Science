Title:
Cowboy Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 sweet bell peppers (red, yellow, orange, green...) minced to make about two heaping cups (530 grams)
1/2 of a medium red onion, minced (200 grams)
3 15 oz. cans of black beans, pinto beans, or black-eyed peas (make a medley using one can of each, about 710 grams total)
3 11 oz. cans of white Shoepeg corn (about 930 grams) (vacuum  packed white kernal corn, do not substitute or use frozen corn)
2 tbs vegetable oil
marinade:
2 cups water
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup Equal granular sweetner

Directions:
Rinse beans and let stand in colander until all excess water is strained off.
Mince the onion and peppers into corn-sized pieces.
In a large mixing bowl, toss beans, onions, and peppers together with 2 tablespoons vegetable oil.
In a seperate small mixing bowl pour in 1/2 cup Equal granular sweetner and pour vinegar over. Mix. Stir in 2 cups water and mix well until disolved.
Pour the marinade over the beans, corn, and onions, cover, and refrigerate overnight.
Before serving, let sit in a colander until all excess liquid is strained off.

Submitter: 
Dan Ader
Description: 
This sugar-free variation is sweet and tangy and great as a dip, on chicken or pork, or as a salad topping or side dish.
