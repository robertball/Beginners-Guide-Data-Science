Title:
Crock Pot Turkey Taco Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. ground turkey breast
1 medium onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 14.5-oz cans diced tomatoes (with or without chilis)
1 8-oz can tomato sauce
1 16-oz can pinquito beans
1 16-oz can kidney beans
1 pkg (10-12 oz)frozen corn
1 pkg (10-12 oz)frozen cubed butternut squash (optional)
1 pkg taco seasoning (or 2 T. Penzey's Taco Seasoning)
1 T. cumin
1 T. chili powder

Directions:
Heat a large heavy saute pan over high heat. Add meat and cook, breaking it up until white, about 5 minutes. When cooked through, add to crock pot.
Add oil to the saute pan, then onions, green pepper, and garlic, and saut√© about 3-4 minutes. Add to crock pot.
Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, beans, corn, squash, taco seasoning, cumin, and chili powder, and mix.
Cover and cook on high for 4 hours or low for 8 hours.
