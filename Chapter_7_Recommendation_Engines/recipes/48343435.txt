Title:
Coconut Custard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 oz) can Cream of Coconut
5 Eggs
1 (14 oz) can Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 (12 oz) can Canned Milk
1 TB Vanilla

Directions:
Preheat over to 300F (150C)
Whisk by hand the cream of coconut until is is smooth and well blended.
Add in remaining ingredients, whisking after each until well blended.
Pour into ungreased 9X13" baking dish
Bake at 300F for approximately 1 hour - until pick inserted in center comes out clean. Turn off oven and let dish rest 15 minutes in oven.
Remove from oven & cool completely.
Chill before serving
