Title:
Pumpkin Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz reduced fat sausage optional
1 onion chopped
1 red bell pepper chopped
2 cloves garlic minced
2 cans (15oz) chicken broth
1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
1 can (15oz) pumpkin puree (Libby's)
s/p
thyme, cumin, chili powder, paprika, cayeene pepper

Directions:
Brown the cooked sausage form the pot.  saute the onion in the same pot until tender adding the red bell pepper halfway through and finishing by adding the garlic during the final minute.  add the remaining ingredients and the cooked sausage to the pot and simmer gently for 20 mins.  make this soup vegetarian by omitting the sausage and add an extra can of black beans
