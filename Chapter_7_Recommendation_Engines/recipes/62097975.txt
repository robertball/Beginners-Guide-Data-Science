Title:
Sweet and Tangy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 chicken breast tenderloins, flattened
1 teaspoon Greek seasoning (Cavenders)
2 tablespoons butter
1/2 c. chicken broth
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 teaspoon cider vinegar
1 teaspoon dijon mustard
2 tablespoons water
1 teaspoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Season chicken with greek seasoning.
Melt butter in skillet.  Add chicken, cook 3 minutes each side until done.  Remove and keep warm.
Add next 5 ingredients to pan.  Mix and scrape pan drippings.  Heat over med-low heat.
Mix water and cornstarch in separate bowl.  Add to pan and mix until thickened.  Add remaining lemon and butter.  Add chicken and toss until heated.

Submitter: 
paynes
Description: 
This is a quick and easy recipe to serve.  Chicken is coated in a delicious thickened lemon sauce.
