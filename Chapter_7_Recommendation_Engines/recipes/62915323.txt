Title:
Biscuit Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Leftover spaghetti sauce
1 (12 oz.) can of biscuits
4 oz. (1 cup) shredded Mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup cottage cheese
1 T. dried parsley flakes
1/2 t. minced onion
1/2 t. dried basil leaves
2 T. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Cover bottom of 13" x 9" baking dish with spaghetti sauce.  Separate dough into 12 biscuits; press or roll each biscuit into a 4" circle.  In a small bowl, combine Mozzarella cheese, cottage cheese, parsley flakes, onion, and basil; blend well.  Place about 2 tablespoons cheese mixture on each biscuit circle.  Fold dough in half over filling; press edges with fork to seal.  Arrange filled biscuits on top of spaghetti sauce.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake at 400 degrees for 15 to 18 minutes, or until biscuits are golden brown.  Makes 5 servings.
