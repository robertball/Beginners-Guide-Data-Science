Title:
Brown Sugar Grits with Bacon and Apple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 cup heavy cream
3/4 cup white corn meal
1/2 cup cream cheese
3-4 pieces of bacon
1/2 apple, chopped
1/2 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Bring water and cream to a rolling boil. Using a spoon, spin the water and slowly add the corn meal. Add cream cheese and stir completely. Turn the heat down to low and cover.
While corn meal is sitting, fry bacon. Drain fat off of bacon, remove from pan and then crumble. Add the apples to the bacon pan. Fry apples. While apples are softening, add brown sugar to grits and stir until dissolved. Once apples are soft; mix apples and bacon into sweet grits.

Submitter: 
mkecupcakequeen
Description: 
A sweet and savory treat that I made up with goodies in my fridge. Perfect for a cool morning. Of course you can alter to taste. Add raisins, nuts, use lighter milk products. Have fun with mix-ins!
