Title:
Cookies and Cream Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/599867.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick butter, softened
1/2 cup shortening
1/3 block cream cheese (8 oz. size), softened
3 1/2 cups confectioner's sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 tsp. salt
2-3 tbsp. heavy cream, or milk
8 Oreos, finely crushed

Directions:
Cream together butter, shortening and cream cheese until smooth. Add vanilla and salt. Add in sugar, one cup at a time, until all incorporated. Add cream or milk, as needed, until desired consistency is reached. Fold in cookie crumbs.

Super yummy frosting that goes perfectly with deep, dark chocolate cake.
