Title:
Grandmas Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks unsalted butter
9 oz. bittersweet chocolate
4 oz. unsweetened chocolate
4 large eggs
1 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. white granulated sugar
1 tbsp. Vanilla
1 c. flour
1/4 tsp. salt
1 1/2 c. chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Directions:
Melt the two chocolates and the butter together in the microwave or double boiler; let this cool. In another bowl mix the 4 eggs with the 2 sugars. When well mixed, add the chocolate mixture, vanilla, flour, and salt. Do not overmix. Add nits and hand mix. Pour 9x13 inch pan and bake at 350 for 20 to 25 mins.
