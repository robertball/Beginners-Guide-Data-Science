Title:
Breakfast Fast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/781529.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound Breakfast Meat (sausage, bacon, etc.)
1 cup frozen shredded potatoes
8 Eggs
1/4 Milk
1 clove Garlic
1/4 Parsaman Cheese
1/2 cup Onion
1/2 Green Pepper
Freeze dried chives as garnish
Grated Pepper Jack Cheese to top

Directions:
Cook meat, onion and green pepper. In mixing bowl, combine all remaining ingredients with meat. Spoon into cupcake tin and top with pepper jack cheese and chives. Bake at 350 for 20 mins. serve with your own flare by adding salsa, ketchup, or con caso (the possibilities are endless!)

Submitter: 
Bluestrose
Description: 
Before school starts is a miserable time to try to get everything on the table at the same time. I make this beforehand and the kids grab one on their own timetable. It's like a mini casserole on demand! Larger children will need a few cupcakes to feel full.
