Title:
Holiday Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz Bricks of Cream Cheese
1 12oz Can of Crushed Pineapple (Drained)
1 Medium sized onion, (Chopped)
1 16oz Walnuts (Chopped)

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, crushed pineapple and onions in a mixing bowl until ingredients are thoroughly combined.  Shape into a ball, wrap with seran wrap, and place in a bowl in the refridgerator for 2-3 hours until the cream cheese has hardened and set enough to retain it's ball form.
Remove from refridgerator and roll the ball in the chopped walnuts until completely covered.
Serve on a platter with Wheat Thins, Ritz Crackers, or whatever your favorite cracker may be.  ENJOY!!

Submitter: 
EarlyRetirement
Description: 
My mom used to make this every year for the holidays and it is still one of my favorites to this day.  I love the salty/sweet conflict of the pineapple, onion and nuts.  I hope you enjoy it as much as my family does.  Happy holidays!
