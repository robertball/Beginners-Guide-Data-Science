Title:
Manhattan Clam Chowder II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (6.5 ounce) cans minced clams
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
2 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped celery
2 potatoes, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
1 teaspoon salt
ground black pepper to taste
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1 tbs old bay
splash worcest sauce

Directions:
Drain clams and reserve liquid.  Add enough chicken broth to reserved liquid to make 3 cups of stock.
In a large saucepan pour clam juice and broth mixture, undrained tomatoes, onions, potatoes,celery, old bay, carrots, salt, pepper and thyme.  Cover and simmer for 30 to 35 minutes.
Remove the pan from the heat. Mash the vegetables slightly to thicken the broth. Add clams to the saucepan and heat thoroughly. Serve hot.
