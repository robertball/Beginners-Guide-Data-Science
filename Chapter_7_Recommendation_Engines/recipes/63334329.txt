Title:
SEPT:  Cocojito Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•
2 shots coconut rum, Bacardi®
•
5 fresh mint leaves, torn
•
½  shot cream of coconut, Coco Lopez®
•
Splash lime juice, Rose’s®
•
Club soda, Schweppes®
•
Ice Cubes
•
Mint sprigs, for garnish

Directions:
1.Add the first 4 ingredients to a cocktail shaker filled with ice cubes.
2.Shake several times and pour into chilled highball glass. Top with club soda and garnish with a sprig of mint.

Submitter: 
Pam English
Description: 
Coconut Rum, fresh mint and coconut cream make this an amazing cocktail.
