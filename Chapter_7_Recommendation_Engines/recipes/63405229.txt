Title:
Broccoli Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white rice
4 tablespoons butter
1 boneless skinless chicken breast, cooked and chopped
1 cup chopped onions
1/2 a bell pepper, chopped
1 (14 ounce) package chopped frozen broccoli/cauliflower mix, cooked and drained
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of onion soup
1 8 ounce bag shredded cheese, taco blend
11 ounces 4% fat cottage cheese
pepper*
garlic salt*
Italian seasoning*
*to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a saucepan bring 2 cups water to a boil. Add rice, pepper, and garlic salt and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
Melt butter in a medium skillet over medium heat. Saute pre-cooked chicken breast, onions and bell pepper until tender.
In a 2 quart casserole dish combine onions, bell pepper, chicken, cooked rice, broccoli and cauliflower, onion soup, 11 ounces cottage cheese and 4 ounces taco blend shredded cheese; mix well.
last 10 minutes, remove from oven and add the rest of the shredded cheese on top, leave uncovered. place in oven for the remaining time.
Bake in preheated oven for 6o minutes.
cool ~5 minutes before serving.
