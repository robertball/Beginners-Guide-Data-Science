Title:
Chocolate Chip Irish Cream Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate mini-chips
1 tsp. cake flour
2 3/4 cups cake flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
3/4 cup fat-free cream cheese - softened
10 tbsp. butter - softened
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
3 large eggs
3/4 cup Irish Cream liqueur
2 tbsp. powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Combine chocolate chips and 1 tsp. flour and toss to coat.
Sift together flour, salt and baking powder.
Place cream cheese and butter in bowl and mix until fluffy.
Add sugars and vanilla and mix well.
Add eggs 1 at a time.
Beat on high speed for 1 minute, then with mixer on low, add flour mixture and liqueur alternately.
Fold in chocolate chips.
Pour batter into Bundt pan and bake for 55 min. or until toothpick comes out clean.
Cool 10 min. and remove from pan.
Cool completely and sprinkle with powdered sugar before serving.
