Title:
Maclaren's Imperial Sharp Cheese Hot Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (250g) Brick Cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 cups crumbled Maclaren's Imperial Sharp Cold Pack Cheddar Cheese Product
1/3 Cup Miracle Whip
3 Slices bacon, cooked, crumbled
2 Green onions, chopped
2 Tbsp, chopped toasted pecans (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Mix all ingredients except nuts in an ovenproof serving dish.
Bake 15 minutes, stirring after 8 minutes.
Top with nuts.

Submitter: 
Trish McQuhae
Description: 
If you like sharp cheddar, then this dip is for you!
