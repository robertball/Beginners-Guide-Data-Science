Title:
Smoky Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/1933247.jpg
Ingredients:
3  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded thin
12 slices smoked beef
½ cup ketchup
½ cup mayonnaise
½ cup apricot jam

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Trim the fat from the smoked meat, place a piece on each chicken breast slice and roll. Fasten with toothpicks and place seam side down in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Mix together ketchup, mayonnaise and jam. Pour mixture over chicken and bake uncovered in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
NIBLETS
Description: 
Serves 2 rolls per person. Smoky meaty rolls of goodness!
