Title:
The Best Cheeseball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pkg cream cheese
1 can (20 0z )crushed pineapple - strained
about 5 green onions
1 green bell pepper
1 TBL seasoning salt
crushed pecans

Directions:
Let cream cheese rise to room tempeature before mixing.  Put cream cheese in large bowl.  Chop green onions and bell pepper into fine pieces.  Add to the cream cheese.  Strain the pineapple and add to the mixture.  Add seasoning salt and mix.  This will make enough for two good size cheese balls.  Separate and roll in pecans.  Chill overnight for best flavor.
