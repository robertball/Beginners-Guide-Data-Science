Title:
Seafood Paella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982307.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound Chorizo or Italian sausage, cut into 1/2 inch slices
2 tablespoons oil
1 cup onion, chopped
1 cup red or green pepper, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 tablespoon chili powder
2 teaspoons turmeric
2 cups white rice
4 1/2 cups chicken broth
Salt and pepper
1 cup tomatoes, chopped
1 pound shrimp in shell
1 (10 ounce) package Birds Eye® Deluxe Baby Peas
Parsley and lemon wedges for garnish

Directions:
In large deep skillet cook sausage until brown. Remove and drain on paper towels. Discard fat. Heat oil in the skillet. Add onion, pepper, garlic, chili powder and turmeric. Saute for 3 minutes. Add rice and stir until coated. Add the sausage and broth, cover pan and simmer slowly for 15 minutes. Uncover pan, season with salt and pepper. Add the tomatoes, press the shrimp tails into the rice, scatter the peas. Do not stir the rice. Cover the pan and cook another 8 to 10 minutes until rice and shrimp are cooked.  Serve with parsley and lemon wedges.

Submitter: 
Birds Eye®
Description: 
Paella is a one pan dish that has it all and then some.  This Spanish themed dish is filled with flavor.
