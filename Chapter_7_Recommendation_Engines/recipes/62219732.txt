Title:
Baby BLT Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bacon, cooked and crumbled, or real bacon bits
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup Veggie cream cheese
1 tsp. Horseradish sauce
1/4 cup green onions, chopped
1/4 cup grated Parmesean cheese
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
24 cherry tomatoes

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat for 6 to 8 minutes, or until evenly brown. Once cooled, crumble and set aside.
In a bowl, stir together the mayonnaise,creaa=m cheese,horseradish bacon, green onions, and parsley until well blended. Chill overnite if possible.
Cut a small slice from the top of each tomato. Using a melon baller or small spoon, scoop out the inside of each tomato and discard.Season tomatoes with salt and pepper. Fill each tomato with the bacon mixture, and refrigerate for 1 hour. Serve chilled.

Description: 
Cherry tomatoes stuffed with a mixture of bacon, green onion, and mayonnaise-cream cheese mixture. Just a little Horseradish sauce adds zip!. Just a bit time consuming, but worth it to the last bite!Prepare filling day before and refrigerate to help with time.Modified from: Baby BLT
