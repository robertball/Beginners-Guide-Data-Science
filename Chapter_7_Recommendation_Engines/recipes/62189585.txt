Title:
Shrimp 'n Chicken Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken breast halves with bones
2 quarts water
1/2 cup all purpose flour
2 cups chopped onion
1 3/4 cup celery
1 1/2 cup green pepper
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 T vegetable oil
1 1/2  t dried thyme
1 t dried oregano
1/2 t pepper (or more, see note)
3 bay leaves
1 14 oz can of chicken broth
1 8 oz can tomato paste
1/2 pound smoked sausage, sliced
1/2 cup green onions
1 pound medium-size fresh shrimp, shelled & deveined

Directions:
Combine chicken and water in large pot. (See note about shrimp.)
Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered about 45 minutes.
Place flour in a jellyroll pan.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes.
Remove the chicken from the broth, set aside & cool.  Bone & chop the chicken when cool.
Strain broth & set aside.
Cook onions and next 3 ingredients in oil in Dutch oven over medium-high heat, stirring constantly, until tender (15-20 minutes).
Add browned flour, thyme, and next 3 ingredients stirring until smooth.
Add reserved broth, canned broth, tomato paste, and sausage.
Bring to a boil, then reduce heat.  Simmer an hour or two, uncovered, stirring occasionally.
When ready, add the chicken and green onions.  Let simmer 15-20 minutes.
Next, add the shrimp 15-30 minutes before serving.  Cover and turn off the heat.  Don't overcook your shrimp!
Serve over hot rice
