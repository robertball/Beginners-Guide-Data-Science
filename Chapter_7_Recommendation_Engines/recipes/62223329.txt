Title:
Grill Master Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/324476.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs Chicken Wings
1/2 cup soy sauce (for marinade)
3/4 cup italin dressing (for marinade)
Sauce:
1/4 cup butter
1 tsp soy sauce
1/4 red hot sauce
Condiments:
Ranch Dressing
Blue Cheese Dressing

Directions:
Separate pieces at joint, discard tips
Combine soy sauce and italian dressing in large (1 gal) plastic bag
Marinate wing sections in bag over night
Grill wing sections
Melt butter
Stir in soy sauce
stir in red hot sauce
Put cooked wings in large serving tray
Pour sauce on top
Serve with celery sticks
and ranch and blue ccheese dressings
