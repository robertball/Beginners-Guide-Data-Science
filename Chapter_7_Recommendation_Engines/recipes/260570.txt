Title:
Moisturizing Kitchen Hand Scrub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4577617.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 cup olive oil
1/3 cup kosher salt
1 extra-large lemon, zested
1/4 teaspoon pure lemon extract (optional)

Directions:
Mix sugar, olive oil, salt, lemon zest, and lemon extract together in a bowl until blended. Spoon into a 1-pint jar; tighten the lid.

Submitter: 
NYNABOO
Description: 
Not to eat! Beautiful citrus scrub for your hands. Keeps them soft and lovely when working in the kitchen. It's also great for removing onion and garlic smells.
