Title:
Easy Baked Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 tablespoons dry white wine
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1 1/3 cups uncooked minute rice
1 red onion chopped
1 red bell pepper chopped
2 garlic cloves chopped
1 cup frozen peas
1 pound sole filets (can use flounder or haddock)
2 plum tomatoes sliced thin
1 lemon quartered

Directions:
Heat oven to 450 degrees. Heat first 7 ingredients until butter is melted. Spray a 13x9 inch baking dish with cooking spray. Pour rice into dish. Stir in onion,pepper, garlic and peas.
Place fish on top of rice. Top fish with tomato slices. Pour butter mixture on top. Cover with foil and bake 20-25 minutes. Serve with lemon wedges.
