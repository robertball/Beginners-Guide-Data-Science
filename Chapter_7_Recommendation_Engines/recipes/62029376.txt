Title:
tortellini with cheese and tuna sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cheese tortellini
2 scallions thinly sliced
2 garlic cloves minced
1 tablespoon butter
1 cup ricotta cheese
1/2 cup milk
1 (6 1/2)oz. can tuna fish packed in water drained and cut into pieces
1/2 cup shredded mozzerella cheese
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 1/4 teaspoon dried basil

Directions:
cook tortellini and drain.
In saucepan saute scallions and garlic in butter for 2 minutes. Remove pan from heat, whisk in ricotta cheese and milk. Add tuna, mozzerella, parmesan and basil. Cook over medium-low heat until cheese is melted. Toss with tortellini.
