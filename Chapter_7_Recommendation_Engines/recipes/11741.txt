Title:
Poppy Seed Spaghettini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/379923.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces spaghetti
1/2 cup butter, melted
1/3 cup poppy seeds

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.  Drain.
Toss with melted butter and poppy seeds.

Submitter: 
Judy
Description: 
Here is a lazy man's pasta which is loved in my family. It is quick and easy. Even my husband can make it.
