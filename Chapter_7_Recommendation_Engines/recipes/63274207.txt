Title:
Blueberry Gin Martini from Pinot Grill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz of blueberry flavored gin
*(infused 4 baskets in one bottle of Bombay sapphire gin for about a week) - if not, can muddle 8 blueberries in shaker
1 oz of Briottet Breme de Mure
1 oz simple syurp
splash of lime juice

Directions:
add all in shaker with ice
shake
enjoy
garnish with 3 blueberries floating

Description:
