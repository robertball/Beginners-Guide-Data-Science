Title:
Roasted Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Oz. Green Beans
2 TBSP Olive Oil
2 Tsp. Vegetable Seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Pour Green Beans in a bowl, drizzle with Olive Oil and sprinkle with vegetable seasoning. Mix together.
Spread on a parchment covered cookie sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes.
