Title:
Chicken Portobello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
2 portobello mushroom, large diced, underneath scraped out
2 roma tomatoes, seeded and chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 cup chopped red pepper
3/4 cup white wine, divided
3/4 cup chicken stock, divided
1 tsp italian seasoning
2 tsp garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Cayenne pepper (optional) to taste

Directions:
Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium high heat until nearly smoking.
Saute chicken strips until no longer pink.
Remove chicken from skillet and keep warm.
Add 1/2 cup wine and 1/2 cup of chicken stock.  Maintain medium high heat.  Scrape up chicken from bottom of pan.
Cook until liquid is reduced to about 1/3 cup.  Add garlic, tomatoes, red pepper and portobello mushrooms.
Lower heat to medium and cook for 10 minutes, stirring frequently.
Stir in chicken, remaining wine and chicken stock, italian seasoning, and pepper.
Cover, lower heat to medium low and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve over pasta or rice.

Submitter: 
Randy Fortel
Description: 
A wonderful combination of portobello mushrooms, roma tomatoes and red peppers in a white wine sauce.
