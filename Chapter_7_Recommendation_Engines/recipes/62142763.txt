Title:
Ritz Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1/2 c. Miracle Whip or mayonnaise
1 1/2 sleeves Ritz crackers (about 50 crackers)
salt and pepper

Directions:
Crush Ritz crackers in a plastic bag with salt and pepper to your taste.
Place 1/2 c. Miracle Whip in dish
Place cracker crumbs into another dish
Use tongs to coat chicken with Miracle Whip one piece at a time, then placing in dish of crumbs.
Press chicken down to fully coat, flip and repeat.
Place coated chicken breast on baking pan.
Repeat with remaining chicken breasts.
Bake at 350 Degrees for 20 - 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
thebakedbaker
Description: 
Moist chicken breasts baked to a golden perfection with a crispy buttery coating.
