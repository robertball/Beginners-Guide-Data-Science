Title:
Spinach and Mushroom Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 eggs
1 16 oz. container cottage cheese
1/2 cup melted butter
1/2 cup flour
1 lb. shredded Monterrey Jack cheese
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
2 small cans mushrooms, drained
1 small pkg. frozen chopped spinach - thawed, drained, and squeezed of excess liquid

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Whisk eggs, then add remaining ingredients, and mix until blended.
Pour into a buttered 9 x 13 inch baking dish.
Bake for 35 minutes, or until eggs are firm and inserted knife comes out clean.
This can be made ahead of time. Refrigerate, and add an additional 20 - 30 minutes of baking time.

Submitter: 
Karen Hibbert
Description: 
A nice meat-less breakfast or brunch main course.
