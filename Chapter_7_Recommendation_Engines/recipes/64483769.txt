Title:
Dairy Queen Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 envelopes unflavored gelatin
3/4 c. water
5 c whole milk
3 c sugar
2 t vanilla
1 1/2 t salt
4 1/2 c cream

Directions:
Soak gelatin in cold water.  Heat milk until hot but not boiling.  Remove from heat and add gelatin, sugar, vanilla and salt.  Let mixture cool before adding cream.  Chill in fridge 5-6 hours before freezing.
