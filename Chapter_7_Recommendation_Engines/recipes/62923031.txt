Title:
cream cheese buttercream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 TBS meringue powder
2 lbs confectioner's sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
milk as needed to achieve desired consistency-at least 3 TBS

Directions:
using a stand mixer, cream together the cream cheese and butter until creamy. add meringue powder and vanilla. add 3 TBS milk and mix until incorporated.
slowly add sugar alternating with more milk as needed to reach desired consistency.

Submitter: 
sugarmama
Description: 
delicious paired with pumpkin cake, or red velvet, or warmed and used as a glaze!Modified from: Cream Cheese Frosting II
