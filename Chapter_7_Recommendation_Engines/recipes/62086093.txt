Title:
EDAMAME CORN CHOWDER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 SLICES (BACON) CUT CROSSWISE INTO 1/2 INCH STRIPS
1 MEDIUM ONION, CHOPPED
2 CANS (14.5 OUNCES) REDUCED-SODIUM CHICKEN BROTH
1 RED POTATO (6 OUNCES) SCRUBBED AND CUT INTO 1/2 INCH CUBES
1/2 TEASPOON DRIED ITALIAN SEASONING
2 CUPS FROZEN SHELLED EDAMAME
1 CAN (15 OUNCES) CREAMED CORN
1/2 CUP HALF AND HALF
SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE

Directions:
IN A LARGE SAUCEPAN COOK BACON UNTIL BROWNED AND CRISP. (TRANSFER BACON TO PAPER TOWELS TO DRAIN). POUR OFF ALL BUT 1 TABLESPOON FAT FROM THE PAN.
ADD ONION AND COOK UNTIL SOFT. ADD BROTH, POTATO, AND ITALIAN SEASONING. SIMMER UNTIL POTATO IS JUST TENDER. STIR EDAMAME , CREAMED CORN AND HALF AND HALF; SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER. SIMMER UNTIL EDAMAME ARE TENDER ABOUT 8 MINUTES.
SERVE CHOWDER SPRINKLED WITH CRUMBLED BACON.

Submitter: 
MONICA SELF
Description: 
A GREAT HEALTHY SOUP FEATURING EDAMAME,  AND CORN. EDAMAME  ARE HIGH IN PROTEIN AND VERY GOOD SOURCE OF IRON, ZINC AND B VITAMINS. ENJOY WITH CRUSTY BREAD.
