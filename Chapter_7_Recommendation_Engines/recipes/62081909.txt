Title:
Heavenly Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. cranberries, ground
2 c. miniature marshmallows
2 c. sugar
1 c. chopped pecans
2 c. chopped celery
2 c. whipping cream, whipped stiff

Directions:
In the blender or food processor, chop the cranberries.  Mix with sugar and let stand covered overnight in the refrigerator.  Add rest of ingredients one hour before serving.
