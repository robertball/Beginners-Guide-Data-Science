Title:
Corn Chowder with bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can creamed corn
1 can sweet whole kernel corn
2 large potatoes
1 large onion, chopped
2 ribs celery, diced
6 strips of bacon
2 T. flour
3 cups milk
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper, if desired

Directions:
Fry bacon until crisp cooked, set bacon to paper towels to drain. Reserve bacon grease.
Saut√© onion and celery, add diced potato in bacon fat.
Make a basic cream sauce using bacon grease, flour and 1 cup of the milk. Simmer over med low heat until it is bubbly and thickened.
Add remaining milk and corn, salt and pepper and red pepper if desired. Simmer about 30 minutes.
Crumble bacon into small pieces and serve a a few crumbles on top of each bowl.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
This recipe says Fall is here! to me. My mom always made it the first time we would get a good cold snap.
