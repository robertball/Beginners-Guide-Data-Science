Title:
LuAnn's Crescent  Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C milk
1 cube butter
1/2 C sugar
4 t. or 2 pkg yeast
1/2 t. sugar
1/2 C warm water
3 eggs, beaten
7-8 C flour, divided

Directions:
In sm SP, heat first three ingredients until dissolved and melted. Set aside to cool.
Pour yeast into 12 oz glass. Add 1/2 tsp sugar and 1/2 cup warm water.
Set aside; yeast should bubble.
Add milk mixture to beaten eggs. Add 4 C flour. Add yeast.
Stir together. It will be gooey. Let sit for one hour. Cover with towel.
Add 3-4 more cups flour and 1/2 tsp salt. Let sit for one hour.
Preheat oven to 375°
Take large handful and place on floured surface.
Roll out with floured rolling pin. Roll until flat and thin.
Cut like a pizza and roll outside of circle to the inside point.
Place rolls on greased cookie sheet and let sit for 20-30 minutes.
Bake at 375° for 12-17 minutes. Paint tops with melted butter.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
These are light and delicious. Letting them rise twice is essential, so don't try to cut any corners.
