Title:
Mex-Italiano Chicken with Bowtie Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. olive oil
2 lb. boneless chicken, cut into 1" cubes
Salt to taste
1 t. Pepper
1 t. Garlic powder
1 t. Poultry seasoning
12 oz. farfalle (bowtie) pasta
1 - 10.75 oz. can cream of chicken soup
1 - 10.75 oz. can cream of mushroom soup
1 - 14.5 oz. can of diced italian style tomatoes (drained)
1 - 4 oz. can of diced green chiles (drained)
1/2 t. pepper
1/2 t. garlic powder

Directions:
Start heating water for pasta.
Season chicken.
Add olive oil to large nonstick frying pan. Cook on medium high heat until oil shimmers.
Add cubed chicken and cook 8 - 10 minutes or until the chicken is no longer pink in the middle.
Meanwhile, cook pasta for 11 minutes until it is "al dente".
Remove chicken to a large bowl. Cover to keep warm.
Place the remaining ingredients in the same large frying pan and heat over medium heat. Stir thoroughly.
Cook for 5 minutes or until heated through. Add chicken to pan. Stir chicken into mixture and cook for 2 minutes.
Drain pasta and add to pan. Mix well and serve.

Submitter: 
Randy Fortel
Description: 
I came up with this delicious recipe as a way to incorporate different flavors from items in my pantry. It is easy to make and can be adjusted to taste.
