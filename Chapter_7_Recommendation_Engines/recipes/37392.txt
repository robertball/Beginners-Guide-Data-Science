Title:
Parmesan Corn on the Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987519.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
4 ears fresh corn, husks and silks removed

Directions:
COMBINE the first 3 ingredients in a small bowl.
WRAP each ear of corn in heavy-duty plastic wrap. Microwave at HIGH 10 to 13 minutes or until tender, turning twice. Unwrap corn, and brush evenly with butter mixture.

Submitter: 
Southern Living magazine
Description: 
If you don't own a microwave, wrap corn in aluminum foil, and bake at 500 degrees for 20 minutes.
