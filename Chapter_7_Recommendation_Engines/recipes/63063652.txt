Title:
Easy Pecan Sandies / Peach Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-16 oz pkg Pecan Sandies Cookies
1-large can sliced peaches in syrup
1- small container- 8 oz cool whip - thawed in fridge
6- glass dishes parfait or truffle bowl style

Directions:
set your 6 glasses out
in large zip lock baggie crush the pecan sandies leaving some nickle size pieces
place 1/3 cup in bottom of each glass( save 1/3 cup small crumbs for the top) -
spoon peach slices( about 6 per glass)  with some juice over the broken cookies
spoon generous spoonful of cool whip on top of peaches
place teaspoon small cookie crumbs on top of coolwhip
chill at least 1 hr before serving

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
Makes 6 individual Pecan Sandies Peach Desert - as seen on Paula Dean show- adapted by her son to be low cal- he used his mothers pecan sandies... fresh peaches and fat free whip cream....adapted by me by using pre-made Pecan Sandies and canned peaches and cool whip
