Title:
Broccoli salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup freshly sqeezed lemon juice
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon white pepper
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1/4 cup  oil
2 cups grated carrots
2 cups broccoli
1/4 cup seedless raisins

Directions:
In a bowl combine the lemon juice, salt, pepper, sugar and stir until the salt and sugar dissolve.
Stir in the mustard and the oil and whisk until everything has combined.
Place the veges in a salad bowl and pour in enough of the vinaigrette to coat the veges when all are tossed together.
Cover and chill thoroughly.
