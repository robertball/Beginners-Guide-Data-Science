Title:
Old Fashioned  Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C all-purpose flour
1 C granulated sugar
2 tsp baking powder
3/4 C evaporated milk
1 tsp salt
1/2 C butter
4 C peaches, peeled & thinly sliced

Directions:
Melt butter in 1 1/2 quart baking dish. In medium mixing bowl combine flour, sugar, baking powder, milk, and salt.  Pour into melted butter (Very important, DO NOT STIR!).  Pour peaches over mixture (DO NOT STIR!).  Bake at 325 degrees F for 60 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rochelle Brooks Thompson
Description: 
This wonderfully rich and decadent cobbler is perfect on a cold winter's night with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
