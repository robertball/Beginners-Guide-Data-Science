Title:
BLT Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982666.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked Market Pantry™ elbow macaroni
8 slices Market Pantry™ bacon, cooked and coarsely crumbled
1 cup grape tomatoes, cut in half
1/2 cup Market Pantry™ Thousand Island dressing
1 (10 ounce) package romaine lettuce

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions; chill in cold water and drain. In large bowl, combine macaroni, bacon, tomatoes and dressing.  Serve over lettuce.

Description: 
Your favorite sandwich is now a salad. The pasta makes it hearty, the thousand island dressing gives it zing.
