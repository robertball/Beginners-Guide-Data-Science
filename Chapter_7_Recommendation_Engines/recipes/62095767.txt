Title:
Old Fashioned Peppermints Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup margarine (or yellow crisco)
3 cups brown sugar
4 eggs
4-5 cups flour
1 tablespoon ground anise
1/2 teaspoon cloves
1/2 teasppon nutmeg
1 tablespoon salt
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 tablespoons hot water
1 pint dark karo syrup
1 lemon - juice and rind
2 cups chopped walnuts

Directions:
Cream together the first 3 ingredients.  Beat in the eggs.  Sift together the flour, anise, cloves, nutmeg and salt; add to the egg mixture.
Dissolve the baking soda in the hot water; add to batter.
Add karo syrup, lemon juice/rind and walnuts all at once to batter and mix well.
Let mixture stand overnight.
Roll into small balls.  Bake 10 minutes at 400 degrees.
