Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 package hot Italian sausage
I can pinto beans with spicy sauce
2 cans black beans rinsed and drained
2 cans kidney beans rinsed and drained
1 can corn
1 onion chopped
2 tbsp. garlic
1 green pepper
2 mini yellow sweet peppers
2 mini red sweet peppers
2 stocks of celery chopped
2 28 oz cans diced tomatoes
1 can beef stock
1 tbsp. better than bouillon
2 cans tomato paste
1/4 cup chili powder
2 tsp cumin
2 tbsp. Worcester sauce
2 dashes hot pepper sauce (Tabasco)
salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
In a large stock pot, brown ground beef and sausage drain and rinse. Add back to the pot along with
tomatoes, beef broth, Better Than Bouillon, tomato paste. onion, peppers, garlic, celery,chili powder, cumin, hot sauce and Worcester sauce. Bring to boil lower heat to simmer, add beans and corn and salt and pepper. Simmer until veggies are tender.
