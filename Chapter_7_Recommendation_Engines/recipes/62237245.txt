Title:
Easy Peasy Sweet Corn Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can creamed corn
1 stick butter, melted
1 box 8.5 oz Jiffy corn muffin mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Mix all ingredients together, pour into 8x8 dish and bake for 35-45 minutes until lightly brown on top. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
