Title:
Muffin Pan Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups self-rising flour
1 1/2 cups milk
2 tbsp sugar
2 tbsp butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Mix flour, milk, and sugar with a whisk until almost smooth.  Fill 12 muffin cups about 2/3 full in a well-greased muffin pan.
Bake for 14-16 minutes.
Remove biscuits as soon as possible and arrange in a single layer on a plate.  Drizzle melted butter over the top and let cool for 5-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Bryan
Description: 
Super easy, super fast biscuits, baked right in a muffin pan!  The family all says they're the best biscuits I've ever made.
