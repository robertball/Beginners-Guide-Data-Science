Title:
NiNi's Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (11 oz) tomato sauce
1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
1 T. Sugar
1 T. Sweet Basil
1 t. garlic powder
3/4 T. oregano
pinch of pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together & spread sauce on pizza crust and bake at 450 degrees for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kim Nance
Description: 
I made up this pizza sauce for my homemade pizzas.  My family and friends all love it. This recipe makes enough sauce for several pizzas.  Enjoy!
