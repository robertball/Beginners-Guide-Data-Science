Title:
Lemon Walnut Bread with White chocolate chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 1/4 cup flour
1 t. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup of walnuts, chopped
3/4 cup white chocolate chips
Juice of half to one lemon - to taste
Grated Peel of 1 lemon

Directions:
Cream butter with sugar: mix in eggs. In another bowl, mix flour, baking powder and salt. Alternately add the flour mixture and the milk to the butter mixture, stirring constantly. Mix in the nuts, lemon peel and white chocolate chips. Bake in  3 greased mini loaf pans at 350ยบ for 40 hour.
