Title:
Fruity Hot Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-16oz cans jellied cranberry sauce
4 cups water
1 qt. pineapple juice
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
3/4 tsp. ground cloves
1/2 tsp. ground allspice
12 cinnamon sticks
butter (optional)

Directions:
Place cranberry sauce in a slow cooker and mash with a potato masher.
Add all the remaining ingredients to slow cooker except cinnamon sticks and butter.  Mix well.
Cover.  Heat on low 4 hours.
Serve in mugs with cinnamon stick stirrers.  Dot each serving with butter if you like.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
Great for leftover cranberry sauce!
