Title:
3-cheese 30/min pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg shell pasta
1 lb ground meat
1 pkg racotta or cream cheese (I love cream cheese  best)
1 sm onion finely chopped
1 c fresh grated parmisian cheese
1 c grated mozzarella
1 16/oz can/jar marinara sauce

Directions:
boil pasta as directed and drain
brown beef and onion
fold racotta/cream cheese and parisian into meat mixutre
fold meat/cheese mixture into shell pasta and pout into buttered casserole dish - pour marinara over mixure and lightly toxx
top with mozzrella and bake to a bubbly brown goodness

Pam Rosterman
Description: 
this yummy cheesy pasta bake will be a hit with your family
