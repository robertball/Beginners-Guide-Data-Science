Title:
Pasta E Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/906557.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground round
1 c. onion, chopped
¾ c. celery, chopped
¾ c. carrots, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
28 oz. can fire-roasted diced tomatoes, undrained
2  14 oz. cans fat-free, less-sodium beef broth
16 oz. can red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
15 oz. can cannellini beans or other white beans, rinsed and drained
1 ½ tsp. dried Italian seasoning
½ tsp. black pepper
¼ tsp. salt
¼ tsp. crushed red pepper
¾ c. uncooked small ring macaroni
2 T. grated fresh Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook beef, onion, celery, carrots and garlic in a large stock pot over medium-high heat until beef is browned, stirring to crumble.  Drain well and return to pan.
Add tomatoes, broth, beans and seasonings; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered 15 minutes.  Add pasta; cook 15 minutes or until past is done.  Ladle into bowls and sprinkle with cheese.
