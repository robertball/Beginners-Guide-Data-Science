Title:
Low Carb Zucchini Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/799903.jpg
Ingredients:
2 zucchinis
1 tbsp olive oil
1/4 cup water
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel the zucchinis.
With a vegetable peeler, cut slices of the flesh lengthwise.
Stop when you get to the seeds, turn it over and continue to do it on all sides.
Discart the seeds
Cut the slices into thin strips to resemble real pasta
saute the zucchini slices in olive oil for a minute and then add the water
Cook until desired softness
Serve with your favorite sauce

Submitter: 
DinnerMomma
Description: 
If you are a pasta lover and need a low carb version closer to the real thing than spaghetti squash, you have found your match!
