Title:
No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 C oatmeal
5 T cocoa
2 C sugar
1/2 C butter
1/2 C milk

Directions:
Mix oatmeal and cocoa
Just bring sugar, butter, and milk to boil
Pour sugar mixture over oatmeal mixture. Stir well.
Drop on pan. Cool and serve.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
An easy recipe that combines oats and cocoa. This is a good recipe to do with young children who love to help in the kitchen.
