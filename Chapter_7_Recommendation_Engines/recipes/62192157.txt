Title:
Peanut Butter Bon Bon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups peanut butter
1 stick butter
4-1/2 cups powdered sugar
3 cups rice krispies
1 6oz package butterscoth chips
1 6oz package chocolate chips

Directions:
Melt butter and peanut butter together.  In bowl combine powdered sugar and cereal.  Pour butter mixture and blend.  Roll into balls and chill about 30 minutes.  Melt chocolate and butterscotch chips and dip balls into it.  Place them on a sheet of wax paper and set them into fridge to set.
