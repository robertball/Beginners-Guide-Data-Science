Title:
Hot Brie Bruschetta Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
1 teaspoon chopped fresh basil
1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup olive oil
1 (8 ounce) wheel Brie cheese

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix roma (plum) tomatoes, onion, garlic, basil, parsley and olive oil. Cover and chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour.
Cut brie in half and place in small dish.  Bake at 325 for 25 minutes.  Remove from oven and remove rind.  Allow to cool for 5 minutes and pour tomato mixture on top.
