Title:
Camembert Spread with Radishes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5341521.jpg
Ingredients:
6 ounces soft Camembert cheese, cubed
2 tablespoons cream cheese, softened
5 tablespoons unsalted butter, at room temperature
5 radishes, finely chopped
2 scallions, thinly sliced
1/2 teaspoon sweet paprika, or to taste
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
2 tablespoons alfalfa sprouts

Directions:
Mash Camembert in a bowl and mix with cream cheese, butter, radishes, and scallions. Season with paprika, salt, and pepper. Sprinkle with alfalfa to serve.

Submitter: 
Marianne
Description: 
This simple cheese spread with Camembert and cream cheese gets color and spice from the radishes. Tastes great on crusty bread.
