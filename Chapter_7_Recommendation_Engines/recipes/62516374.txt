Title:
Thai Mango Salad in a rice paper with Jalapeno Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2199553.jpg
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
Green mango 25 gm
Dried shrimps 3 gm
Chichen mince 10 gm
Coriandar 5 gm
Spring onion 2 gm
Tomato 1 gr
Jalapeno 3 pcs
Rice paper 8 '' 3 pcs
Seasame oil 20 ml
Soya sauce 2 ml
Rice viniger 1 tsp
Pepper 1/2 tsp
Ginger powder 1/4 tsp

Directions:
Method:
Prepare the mango-peeled and sliced,chopped onion,tomato,spring onion,coriander,add dried shrimps,chicken mince and mix all in a bowl with soya sauce and rice viniger.
Soak the rice paper in a warm water.in the centre of the sheet put somesalad,wrap like a spring roll and cut into two pieces.
Preapare the dressing with finely chopped jalapeno ,sesame oil and little soya sauce.
Present the roll in big plate ,put some dressing around and finesed  with pepper and ginger.
