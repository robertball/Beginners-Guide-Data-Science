Title:
Cheesy Chicken Cups (WWPP=5) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Buttermilk Biscuits (10 biscuits)
1 cooked chicken breast, cut into small pieces
1 can Healthy Request Cream of Chicken Soup
2/3 c. FF Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 TBS dried parsley flakes
1/2 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Place each biscuit into a muffin pan.
Press the biscuits into the holes (and up the sides) to form cups.
In a bowl, combine chicken, soup, cheese, parsley, and pepper. Mix well. Spoon evenly into cups.
Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
