Title:
Hot and Honey Pretzel'd Chicken Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978265.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into 1-inch cubes
1 1/2 cups crushed salt-free pretzels
1/2 teaspoon MortonÂ® Salt Substitute, divided
HONEY MUSTARD SAUCE
1/2 cup hot mustard powder
1/4 cup honey
3 tablespoons apple juice
2 teaspoons sugar

Directions:
Dip chicken cubes into Honey Mustard sauce, then coat with crushed pretzels. Refrigerate 30 minutes to set.
Discard any unused sauce. Place chicken cubes on large ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 375 degrees F, 10 to 12 minutes or until chicken is done. Sprinkle with Morton(R) Salt Substitute. Serve warm.
Honey Mustard Sauce: Combine ingredients until smooth.

Submitter: 
Morton Salt
Description: 
Tender chicken with a crunchy coating takes a dip in a zesty sauce. What a snack!
