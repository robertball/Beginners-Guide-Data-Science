Title:
Hot Broccoli Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped broccoli
2 tbsp. chopped onion
2 tbsp. chopped red pepper
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1 clove garlic, pressed
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/8 tsp. pepper
Crackers or chips

Directions:
Mix all together.
Bake in greased casserole dish @ 375 x 25 - 30 minutes or till heated through.
Yields about 2 cups. I often double it. Keeps well in fridge.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Cheesy goodness for scoop chips. Great appetizer. I've also baked it over chicken or ham for a main course.
