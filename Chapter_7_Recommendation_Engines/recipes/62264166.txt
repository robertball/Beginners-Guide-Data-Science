Title:
Spicy carrot cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box spice cake mix (about 18 ounces)
1 can condensed tomato soup
3/4 cup water
2 eggs
1/3 cup canola oil
1/2 cup shredded carrot
1/3 cup chopped pecans ( optional)
1 cup confectioner's sugar
2 tablespoons lemon / or unsweetened apple juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F / 190 degrees C
Combine cake mix, soup, water, eggs, oil, carrots, raisins, and pecans. Blend until smooth. Grease and flour "Bunt cake pan"; add batter.
Bake for apx. 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Remove from oven and empty from pan to cooling rack until cool.
Stir sugar and juice together until smooth.
Spread glaze over cake.
