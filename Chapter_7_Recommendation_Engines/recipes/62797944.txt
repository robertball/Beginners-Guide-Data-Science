Title:
COUNTRY STYLE MACARONI AND CHEESE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/717345.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cup dry macaroni
3/4 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped green pepper
2 cups mushrooms cut in chunks
1/2 cup bread crumbs
3 Tbsp.marg.
2 Tbsp.flour
1 tsp.dry mustard
3/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp.oregano
2 1/2 cup milk
2 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 sliced tomato OR
few slices of back bacon

Directions:
Cook macaroni,drain and turn into buttered casserole.
Saute onion,green pepper and mushrooms.
Add to macaroni with bread crumbs.
Melt marg.Add flour,dry mustard,salt and oregano.
Gradually add milk,stir well.Add cheese.Whisk till thickened,pour over macaroni.Stir well.
Sprinkle with a little extra cheese if desired.
Top with tomato slices or sliced back bacon.
Cover.Bake at 350 for 45 min.
