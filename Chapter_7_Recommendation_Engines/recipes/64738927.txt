Title:
Cream of Carrot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp. butter
1 tbsp. chopped onion
3 tbsp. flour
1 tsp. salt
dash pepper
3 1/2 cups milk
3 cups shredded carrots
2/3 cup boiling salted water
chopped dill, to taste
cayenne powder, to taste

Directions:
Melt butter in large saucepan. Add onion; cook till soft.
Blend in flour, salt, pepper & cayenne.
Whisk in milk, add dill; cook on med. heat, stirring occasionally until thickened.
Meanwhile, cook carrots in boiling water x 10 min.
Add to milk mixture.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A delicious creamy soup loaded with shredded carrots and as much dill as you want!
