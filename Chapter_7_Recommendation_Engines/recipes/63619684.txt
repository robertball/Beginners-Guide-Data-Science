Title:
Turkey Meatballs with Chili-Garlic Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Meatballs:
1/4 C. Corn Flake crumbs
1 lb. ground turkey or chicken
1 egg
1 TBS minced fresh ginger
1 minced garlic clove
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dried parsley
3 chopped scallions
1 TBS low sodium soy sauce
2 tsp. sesame oil
For the Glaze:
2 TBS low sodium soy sauce
1 TBS chili garlic sauce (sambal oelek)
1 TBS palm sugar
2 TBS water

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
Combine all the meatball ingredients and form into 1 inch balls.
In a separate bowl, combine the ingredients for the glaze.
Place the meatballs on a greased baking tray and bake for 10 minutes.
Coat the meatballs with the glaze and cook for an additional 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
CaraMeg
Description: 
My favorite recipes are easy, healthy and delicious and these meatballs are all of those things.  I find recipes with ground turkey tend to be bland, but the fresh ginger, garlic and sesame oil in these make them very flavorful.
