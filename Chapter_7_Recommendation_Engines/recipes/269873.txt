Title:
Sweet and Savory Board Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6178439.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sourdough pretzel nuggets
1 1/2 cups thin wheat crackers
1 cup candy-coated milk chocolate pieces (such as M&M;'s®)
1 cup cheese crackers
1 cup dried cranberries
1 cup mini marshmallows
1 (5.5 ounce) package dinner mints
1 (4.5 ounce) package sweet and salty snack mix (such as Palmer®)
1 (3.25 ounce) package cream-filled rolled wafer cookies
1 (3 ounce) bag mini chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo® Mini)

Directions:
Arrange pretzels, wheat crackers, candy pieces, cheese crackers, cranberries, marshmallows, dinner mints, snack mix, wafer cookies, and mini chocolate sandwich cookies on a 14-inch square platter.

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
This sweet and savory board is great for some snacks after your meal or as an appetizer on big game days.
