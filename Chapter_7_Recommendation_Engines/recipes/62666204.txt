Title:
Chocolate Mascarpone Whipped Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/594584.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces Mascarpone Cheese
1/2 Cup Heavy Cream
1 Teaspoon Pure Vanilla
5 Tablespoons Powdered Sugar
1 1/2 Tablespoons Cocoa Powder

Directions:
Place mascarpone cheese into mixing bowl and mix until smooth.
Add heavy cream, vanilla, powdered sugar and cocoa. Begin to whisk mixture together until it forms stiff peaks.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
This is a versatile chocolate mascarpone whipped cream. You can use as an frosting on cupcakes, cakes, or cheesecakes.
