Title:
Wayne's Favorite Tuna Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. seashell pasta
1 cup mayo
1 large or 3 small cans tuna in water
5 small sweet pickles
1 carrot, grated
1/8 tsp. ginger
1/8 tsp. pepper
4 tbsp. diced onion
1 tbsp. diced parsley

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed on package, and drain.
Drain tuna. Mix all ingredients.
Chill at least one hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Missouri Farm Wife
Description: 
This is one of my husband's favorite tuna salad recipes. I usually serve it with raw carrots, iced tea, and fruit for dessert. I created the recipe when I wanted to make a pastas salad for lunch, but was out of tomatoes and celery.
