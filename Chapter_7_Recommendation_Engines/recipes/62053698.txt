Title:
Pumpkin Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz pkg of Cream Cheese
2 cups confectioners sugar
1 15oz can pure pumpkin
1 tsp cinnamin
1 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
Mix softened cream cheese and confectioners sugar together until smooth.  Add pure pumpkin gradually until well blended.  Stir in cinnamin and nutmeg.  Ready to serve or can be chilled.

Submitter: 
SOMEONESWT
Description: 
A delicious easy-to-make Pumpkin Dip that goes especially great with Ginger Snaps, Spiced Wafers or Graham Crackers.
