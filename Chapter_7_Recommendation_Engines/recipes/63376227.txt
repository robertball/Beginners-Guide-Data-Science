Title:
Tortellini Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/903227.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
1/2 medium sweet onion, rough chopped
4 oz. crimini mushrooms, cut to 1/2 inch pieces
1 cup marsala wine
1 medium package cheese tortellini from the dairy counter
1- 14 oz. can quartered artichoke hearts
1/2 cup half and half
2 T. shredded parmesan cheese, divided
Fresh or dried parsley for garnish

Directions:
Cook tortellini in salted boiling water for 4 minutes. Drain, shock in ice water and set aside.
Sautee garlic and onions in olive oil. Add mushrooms and artichokes and season with salt and pepper. Stir gently.
Add wine, cook for 5-8 minutes
Add cream and half of the parmesan cheese, stir gently and cook for 5 minutes  more.
Add tortellini, combine the ingredients and heat on medium low until steaming and pasta is al dente.
Transfer to pasta bowl, top with parsley and remaining parmesan cheese, and serve.
