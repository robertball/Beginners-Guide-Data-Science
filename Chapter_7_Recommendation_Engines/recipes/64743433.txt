Title:
MA's Yummy Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 cup vegetable oil
1 cup long grain white rice
1/2 cup chopped onion
3 to 4 cloves minced garlic
1/4 cup, well drained Rotel tomatoes
1/4 cup chopped fresh Cilantro
2 Tablespoons chicken bouillon
1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 3/4 cup hot water + 2 Tablespoons tomato sauce

Directions:
Heat up oil In a non-stick skillet, over medium heat. l Add rice, cook and stir until lightly browned.  Add onion, garlic and cilantro; cook and stir 2 minutes more.  Add tomatoes, bouillon and seasonings; stir well.  Turn heat to medium high, add water and tomato sauce; bring to a hard boil.   Cover tightly, adjust heat to low; cook another 15 minutes.   Turn off heat and leave covered another 5 minutes.  Just before serving, fluff and stir with a fork.

Submitter: 
MA McBridges
Description: 
One day, my sweet neighbor, Mr Delgado, showed me how his Momma taught him to make Spanish rice.  I took what I learned and put my spin on it to make it my own tasty version.  Everyone who tries it says, great rice, so I'm sure you'll like it too  :)
