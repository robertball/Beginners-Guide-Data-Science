Title:
Artichoke and Spinach Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4540493.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil or coconut oil
1 (10 ounce) frozen box of spinach, thawed
1 (10 ounce) frozen box of artichokes, thawed
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1 shallot, chopped (about 1 tablespoon)
1 clove garlic, chopped
2 large eggs
2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes, optional
1/2 cup tapioca flour
1/4 cup almond flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.  Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Wrap spinach with a paper towel and squeeze out water.  Heat a large saut√© pan over medium heat.  Add in oil and let pan get hot.
Add in spinach and artichoke and season with salt/pepper.  Let cook for about 3-4 minutes.  Add in shallots and garlic while continuing to cook for another 5 minutes.
Put spinach and artichoke mixture in the food processor and pulse until it's all broken down into small pieces.  Then add in eggs and pulse a few more times.  Place in a bowl.
Add in Italian seasoning, red pepper flakes, tapioca flour and almond meal.  Combine into the mixture.  Use an ice cream scoop and form a ball then place on the parchment paper.
Bake for about 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.  Serve immediately.
Enjoy!
