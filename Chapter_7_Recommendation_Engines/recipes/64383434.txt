Title:
Calico Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup bacon, chopped
1 pound lean ground beef
1 (15 ounce) can pork and beans
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can butter beans
1 (15 ounce) can lima beans, drained
1/2 cup BBQ Sauce
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup chopped onion
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
1 teaspoon mustard powder
1/2 cup chopped celery

Directions:
Fry bacon and drain on paper towels. Cook ground beef over medium high heat until evenly brown. Add onion and cook till translucent. Drain and set aside.
In slow cooker, mix together the bacon, ground beef, pork and beans, kidney beans, butter beans, Lima beans, BBQ sauce, onion, vinegar, dry mustard and celery.
Cover and cook on low for 4 hours.

Description: 
Pork and beans, kidney beans, butter beans, and lima beans mix it up with browned ground beef and bacon in a sweet and tangy sauce for a crowd-pleasing one-dish meal.Modified from: Calico Beans
