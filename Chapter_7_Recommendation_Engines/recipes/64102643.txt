Title:
Tracey's Summer Ceviche (Fish Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Diced Avocado
1 Peeled, Diced Cucumber
1 Diced Onion
1 Diced 16oz Ball Fresh Mozzarella
2 Diced Tomatoes
8 Tbl Orange Juice
2 Tbl Olive or Canola Oil
2 Tsp Lemon or Line Juice
2 Tbl Minced Cilantro
Salt & Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Gently toss first 5 ingredients in a large bowl and set aside.
In small bowl, mix juices, oil and cilantro.  Gently toss into large bowl to coat.  Salt & Pepper to taste.  Refridgerate at least 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Tracey Antognazzi
Description: 
This light, refreshing dish is versatile enough to serve in a martini glass for an elegant appetizer or as the perfect side at a summer grill.
