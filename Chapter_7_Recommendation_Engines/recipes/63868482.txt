Title:
Pot Roast (dutch oven) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 lb boneless chuck roast
6-8 large carrots, peeled and cut into 2.5 inch pieces
2 small onions, peeled and cut in half
1 cup chopped celery
2 cups frozen green beans, heated and drained
1 pkg french onion soup mix
3 sprigs of fresh Rosemary
1 cream of mushroom soup
1 cup red wine
1 (14oz) can beef broth
3 tbsp olive oil
Salt & pepper & garlic seasoning
potatoes (mash and serve on side)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275 degrees.
Season meat on all sides with salt, pepper and garlic seasoning and set aside.
Heat olive oil over med-hi heat, on stove, in dutch oven till very hot. Place onions in oil and slightly brown all sides and then remove.
In same oil add carrots,  celery & onion until slightly browned and then remove.
Brown all sides of meat and remove.
In separate bowl combine beef broth, french onion soup mix, cream of mushroom soup and stir to mix, setting aside.
De-glaze pan with 1 cup of wine. Add meat back into pan. Pour enough of beef broth mixture to halfway cover meat. Then add back in onion followed by carrot and celery mixture on top of meat. Place sprigs of rosemary on top. Pour about 1/2 the amount of beef broth mixture remaining.
Cover dutch oven, place into preheated oven and cook for 4-4.5 hours. Heat green beans and drain and add to pot roast mixture last 10 min of cooking. Serve with mashed potatoes.
