Title:
Big Ray's Greek Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3477043.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb Yukon Gold Potatoes
1/3 cup olive oil
2 Tbsp white wine vinegar
1/2 tsp died oregano
1/3 cup fresh paresely, chopped
1/3 cup kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper
3/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Place potao in large pot and cover with water, cover pot and bring to a boil.  Cook until potaos can be piereced easily with a sherp knife, about 15 to 20 minutes.
Drain and allow to cool until you can handle easliy, but still warm.  Cut into 1 to 1 1 1/2 inch chunks.
In a large bowl, stir together olive oil, vinegar, oregano, parsley, olives, celery, onion, salt and pepper. Whisk well to combine.  Add potatoes and feta cheese and toss to combine.  Cover and allow to sit at room temperature for about 2 to 3 hours, or refrigerate for several hours to overnight.  Serve at room temperature.

Description: 
This Greek potato salad uses olive oil and feta cheese to go steps beyond a tradition potato salad with mayonnaise or sour cream.
