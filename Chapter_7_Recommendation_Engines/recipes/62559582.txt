Title:
Broccoli Salad 2 Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 bunches broccoli, cut into small pieces
1 bunch green onions, chopped
½ lb. bacon, fried, drained, and crumbled
1 cup finely shredded mild cheddar cheese
1 small bag sunflower kernals
----- D R E S S I N G -----
1 cup mayonnaise
½ cup sugar
¼ cup apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Combine broccoli, green onions, backon, cheese, and sunflower kernels in a large salad bowl.
Mix mayonnaise, sugar and apple cider vinegar together well.  Pour over salad and stir well
