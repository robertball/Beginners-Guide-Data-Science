Title:
Grandma Lutz's Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 cups flour
2 pkg. yeast
1 cup oil
2 cups lukewarm water
2 eggs
2 tsp salt
1/2 cup sugar

Directions:
Put all ingredients into the bread machine.
Set on dough cycle and start.
After cycle separate dough into 24 pieces.
Roll each piece into a ball.
Place in a 9x13-inch pan sprayed with PAM.
Let rise until double.
Bake at 350 degrees until browned.
Brush tops with melted butter.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
These are the preferred rolls of the Kramer family. They are dense and kind of sweet. The recipe was originally called Mazola Oil Rolls.
