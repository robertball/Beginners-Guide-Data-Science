Title:
Mommy's Custard Angel Food Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 egg yolks, beaten
3/4 c. sugar
3/4 c. lemon juice
3/4 c. orange juice
1 1/2 t. grated orange rind
1 pkg unflavored gelatin
1/4 c. cold water
6 egg whites
3/4 c. sugar
1 c. whipping cream, whipped
Angel Food cake
Extra whipped cream

Directions:
Cook egg yolks, sugar, and juices until mixture coats spoon.  Add orange rind.
Dissolve unflavored gelatin in 1/4 c. water and add to egg yolk mixture.  Let mixture cool.
Beat 6 egg whites until very stiff with 3/4 c. sugar.  Fold in whipped cream.
Break Angel Food cake into pieces and place in large serving dish.  Cover with custard and leave in refrigerator over night.
Serve with extra whipped cream.
