Title:
Favorite Turkey Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Casserole:
2 c diced turkey (or chicken)
2 c cooked rice
1 can diced, drained water chestnuts
1 can cream of chicken soup
2-3 diced hard boiled eggs
1/4 c mayonaise
1 tsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. salt (optional)
Topping:
3/4 stick melted butter
1/2 c toasted sliced almonds
1 c bread crumbs or crushed crackers

Directions:
Mix all casserole ingredients and put in greased casserole dish (9X12)
Mix topping ingredients and spread on top of casserole.
Bake @ 350 degrees for 30min.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Cline Backfisch
Description: 
Turkey and rice casserole with a few tasty twists inside and a crunchy topping
