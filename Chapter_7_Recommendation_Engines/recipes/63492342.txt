Title:
Moroccan Couscous With Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion, diced
3 cups vegetable broth
2 carrots, peeled and julienned
2 turnips, peeled and julienned
1 sweet potato, julienned
1 zucchini, julienned
1 red bell pepper, julienned
1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
1 pinch saffron
1 pinch curry powder
2 cups uncooked couscous
1 lb white fish (tilapia, mahi, etc.), cut in 4 pieces

Directions:
Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat; saute onion until golden.  Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil.  Stir in carrots, turnips and sweet potato.  Reduce heat to medium and simmer 15 minutes.
Reduce heat to low and add zucchini and red bell pepper.  Simmer for 20 minutes.
Stir in garbanzo beans, tomato sauce, cinnamon, turmeric, saffron and curry powder. Place fish on top of vegetables and simmer until fish is cooked through.
Meanwhile, bring 2 1/2 cups water to a boil.  Stir in couscous, cover and remove from heat.  Let stand 5 to 7 minutes.  Fluff with a fork and serve with vegetables on top.

Description: 
Carrots, turnips, sweet potatoes, zucchini and red bell pepper are simmered with garbanzo beans and tomato sauce, and flavored with cinnamon, turmeric and curry.  This beautiful, tasty concoction is served atop hot couscous.Modified from: Israeli Moroccan Couscous
