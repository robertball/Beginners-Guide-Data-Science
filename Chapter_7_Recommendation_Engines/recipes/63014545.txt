Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 frozen skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons butter
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.7 ounce) can cream of celery soup
1 (10.7 ounce) can chicken broth
1/2 tsp poultry seasoning
1/4 tsp each garlic powder, rosemary, thyme, parsley
1 onion, finely diced
2 cups frozen corn
1 (10 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough, torn into pieces

Directions:
Place the chicken, butter, soups, broth, seasonings, onion and corn in a slow cooker, and fill with enough water to cover.
Cover, and cook 4.5 - 5 hours on High. Shred chicken. Remove crock pot from the base add biscuit dough on top and bake in oven according to package directions on biscuits.
Bake until biscuits are golden brown.
