Title:
Magic Bullet® Banana Chocolate Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4457757.jpg
Ingredients:
5 scoops chocolate ice cream (such as Nestle® Real Dairy)
1/2 cup milk
1/2 banana
4 ice cubes
1/2 teaspoon white sugar
1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine chocolate ice cream, milk, banana, ice cubes, sugar, and vanilla extract in a Magic Bullet(R) party mug. Blend until smooth, 1 to 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
Shari
Description: 
A creamy chocolate shake with a nice light banana flavor that I came up with one night. This recipe is for one party mug-sized shake. I'm not normally a fan of banana-flavored drinks, but this milkshake tastes amazing!
