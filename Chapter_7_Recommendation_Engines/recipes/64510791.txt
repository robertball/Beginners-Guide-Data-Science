Title:
Parmesan Chicken THE ABSOLUTE BEST!!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 chicken brest halves
1 cup of parmesan cheese
2 cups spaghetti sauce
2 cups panko bread crumbs
2 cups of butter
1 TBS Italian seasoning or dried basil

Directions:
set oven to 350
wash and cut fat of of the chicken then cut in half
melt 1 1/2 cups of butter
mix together bread crumbs and Italian seasoning/basil
dip chicken in melted butter then dip into bread crumb mixture
heat up the rest of the butter
put chicken in oven safe pan and pour the rest of the butter on top of the chicken
put  in oven for 15-20 min or until golden brown
pull the chicken out and pour spaghetti sauce on top of the chicken and cover with as much cheese as you want!
put the chicken back in to the oven until cheese is melted
ENJOY!!!!!
