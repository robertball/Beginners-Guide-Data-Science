Title:
Fruit Crisp for Two Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups fruit of choice; apple, peach, pear, etc.
Add 1/4 cup berries, if desired,  but remember to reduce the other fruit  to 1 1/4 cups.
2 tablespoon graham crackers or oats, ground
1 Tbsp honey or maple syrup
1 Tbsp white flour
1 Tbsp brown sugar
1 Tbsp butter (cut it in)
1 tablespoon chopped walnuts
1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
pinch of nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Grease two small ramekins.
Mix together the fruit, honey or maple syrup and flour together.
Place equal amounts of fruit into each bowl.
Now in a small mixing bowl, combine the remaining ingredients together, crumble on top of the fruit.
Place the two filled ramekins in oven and cook for 35 minutes or until slightly brown.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Slowturtle
Description: 
This recipe is only a guide. Place the 2 ramekin bowls on a small cookie sheet before putting them into the oven so it won't dirty in your oven.
