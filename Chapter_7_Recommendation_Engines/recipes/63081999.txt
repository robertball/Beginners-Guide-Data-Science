Title:
Broccoli Salad with Cranberries and Sunflower Seeds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small heads broccoli
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp agave nectar or sugar
1 tsp rice vinegar or apple cider vinegar
3/4 tsp Dijon mustard
1/4 cup chopped red onion
1/4 cup dried sweetened cranberries
2 TBS roasted, salted sunflower seeds

Directions:
Cut broccoli into small florets.  Trim and peel stalks, then slice into matchsticks.  Place in steamer basket over simmering water and steam just until bright green but still crispy, about 1-2 minutes.
Whisk together mayonnaise, agave nectar, vinegar and mustard in small bowl.  Combine broccoli, onion and cranberries in larger bowl; combine with mayonnaise mixture.  Sprinkle with sunflower seeds.
