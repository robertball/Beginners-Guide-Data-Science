Title:
Southern Chicken & Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/689939.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (32oz.) container low-sodium chicken broth
1 (14.5oz) can low-sodium chicken broth
3 cups of shredded cooked chicken (about 1.5lb)
1 (10 3/4 oz.) can reduced-fat cream of celery soup
1/4 tsp. poultry seasoning
1 (10.2 oz.) can refridgerated jumbo buttermilk biscuits.

Directions:
Stir together first 5 ingredients in a Dutch oven over medium-high heat; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low; simmer, stirring occassionally, 15 minutes.
Place biscuits on a lightly floured surface. Roll or pat each biscuit to 1/8-inch thickness; cut into 1/2-inch-wide strips.
Return broth mixture to a low boil over medium-high heat. Drop strips, 1 at a time, into boiling broth. Reduce heat to low; simmer 10 minutes, stirring occassionally to prevent dumplings from sticking.

Submitter: 
Rose Herring Cobb
Description: 
Any true Southerner would be happy to accept a bowful of these chicken & dumplings! This recipt is quick, easy, and will have your guests begging for more! We use a deli-roasted chicken for this recipe. One chicken yields 3 cups.
