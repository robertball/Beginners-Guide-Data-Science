Title:
BLT Rancheros Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2497419.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
Shredded lettuce
2 tomatoes, seeded, diced
1 pound bacon, cooked and crumbled

Directions:
Blend dressing mix with mayonnaise and sour cream. Refrigerate for at least 1-2 hours, or until ready to use.
To assemble each wrap, spread about 1 tablespoon of the mayonnaise mixture on each tortilla. Top with lettuce, tomatoes and bacon pieces. Roll tightly and secure with a toothpick. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley®
Description: 
Bacon, lettuce, and tomato are rolled up in tortillas spread with a creamy blend of sour cream, mayo, and Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix.
