Title:
Amazing Mexican Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 cup crushed tortilla chips
3/4 cup shredded pepperjack cheese
1 small onion chopped
1 packet taco seasoning mix
2 eggs beaten
1/4 cup mild red taco sauce
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Thoroughly combine beef, onion, cheese, taco seasoning packet and crushed tortilla chips in a bowl.
In a separate bowl whisk eggs, taco sauce and milk together and add to meat mixture until well combined.
Press into a 9x5 inch loaf pan and top with a strip of taco sauce down the center bake in preheated oven for 45 to 60 minutes.

Submitter: 
Camille
Description: 
My Mexican grandmother would use left over corn tortilla chips in our meatloaf and everyone loved it! They all wanted seconds along with the recipe.  A delicious Mexican-American comfort food.
