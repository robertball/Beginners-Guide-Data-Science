Title:
Coffee Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
48 lg marshmallows quartered
1 1/4 c strong black coffee
1 c whipped cream
Grated chocolate

Directions:
Pour the hot coffee over the quartered marshmallows in a medium bowl
Stir or whisk until melted
Chill until thickened (about 20 minutes)
Fold in 1 cup of whipping cream and chill until ready to serve
Garnish with grated chocolate
