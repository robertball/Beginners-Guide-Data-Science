Title:
Mediterranean Chicken and Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
2 lbs. boneless, skinless chicken breasts
Salt and pepper to taste
4 large cloves of garlic - minced
1 medium onion - chopped
1/2 yellow pepper - sliced
1/2 red pepper - sliced
3/4 cup dry white wine
14 oz. can of tomatoes with basil and oregano
14 oz. can of tomatoes with chiles or jalapenos
1 tbsp. capers
14 oz. can of artichoke hearts, drained
1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
16 oz. box of pasta

Directions:
Place large pot of water on stove, add salt and bring to a boil.  Add pasta to boiling water and cook according to package directions.  Add olive oil to large saute pan and heat to medium.  While pan is heating, cut chicken into bite sized pieces and add to pan.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper and cook until lightly browned.  Add garlic, peppers and onion to chicken and cook 1 - 2 minutes, then add wine, capers and tomatoes.  Cover and simmer about 15 minutes and add artichoke hearts, then simmer about 15 more minutes or until chicken is tender.  Toss chicken mixture with cooked pasta and add shredded Parmesan.  If desired, top each serving with additional Parmesan before serving.  Enjoy!!
