Title:
garlic shrimp stuffed mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/421352.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb frozen raw shrimp
1/2 onion diced
4 cloves of garlick diced
1 stick of butter
2 Tbls ex virgan olive oil for sauce mix
1/2 tsp of salt
1/2 tsp of white peper
1/2 cup cream
1 Tbls flower
1/2 pack stove top stuffing 'sage' or 'herb'
4 large portabella mushroom caps
2 Tbls olive oil for mushrooms
4 deli slices of provalone cheese

Directions:
In large skillet add butter and 2tbls of oil peper and salt.   heat on medium.
when hot add onion and garlic to saute...
On greased sheet pan place mushrooms and cover with oil.this will bake at 400 for 30 mins. then take out and set aside.
Just befor carmalizing onions add the shrimp from bag, and also use the juices from the bag.
still in med heat cook shrimp for about 2 mins on each side or untill done.
Remove shrimp and set aside. Now transfer sauce to small sauce pan
Combind and smooth together cream and flower.
Bring sauce pan to a boil and add cream/flower mix, wisking through out.
Bring to boil again then add stuffing mix and allow to set.
When stuffing is ready place equal amounts of stuffing on caps, add shrimp to each cap and cover with one peace of provalone cheese.
You can now garnish the top with parsly flakes and some paprica.
place in preheated oven at 350 for 5-7min or untill cheese is melted......
Enjoy
