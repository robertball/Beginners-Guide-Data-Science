Title:
Alison's Slow Cooker Vegetable Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cubed beef stew meat
4 cups water
1 small onion, chopped
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
2 cubes beef bouillon, crumbled
2 garlic cloves
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 (16 ounce) package frozen corn
2 cans green beans
1 (16 ounce) package frozen peas
1 cup barley

Directions:
Place beef in a slow cooker.  Pour in water.  Stir in onion and tomatoes.  Season with bouillon, garlic and basil.  Cook on low 5.5 hours.
Add vegetables & barley; cook an additional hour and a half.

Submitter: 
Donni
Description: 
Vegetables, barley and stew beef are cooked in a slow cooker with bouillon cubes and diced tomatoes in this easy meal.Modified from: Alison's Slow Cooker Vegetable Beef Soup
