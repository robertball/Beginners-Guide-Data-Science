Title:
Grilled Hot Italian Chicken Sausage with Peppers & Onion Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz) pkg al fresco Hot Italian Chicken Sausage
1 Each Cooking Spray, olive, extra virgin
4 cup Vidalia onions, thinly sliced
3 tbsp vinegar, balsamic
(7 oz) roasted red peppers (drained)
1/4 tsp black pepper
(8 oz) French baguette, low sodium

Directions:
Heat a large grill pan over medium-high heat. Coat pan with cooking spray. Add onion and al fresco chicken sausage, cook 1 minute.
Sprinkle with vinegar; cook 10-12 minutes or until sausage reaches an internal temperature of 165&deg;f turning occasionally. Add bell peppers, cook 1 minute. Sprinkle with black pepper.
Arrange chicken sausage, peppers & onions mixture evenly over bottom half of bread. Cut into 5 sandwiches.
