Title:
Donell's Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5# Elbow Macaroni
2 # Velveets
3 cups shredded cheddar
1 pt half & half
1 pt - 1 qt milk
1/4 b butter
Salt * Pepper

Directions:
Heat butter, milk, 1/2 & 1/2 and velveeta til melted.  (Do not boil).  Add shredded cheddar, melt completely.  Add salt & pepper, cayenneadd 1/2 & 1/2 as meeded to acheive a "fluid gravy"
