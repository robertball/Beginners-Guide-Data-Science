Title:
Spinach Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
250 ml whipping cream
3 Tbsp grated onion (or 1 Tbsp dried)
1 tsp dry mustard
1/4 cup sugar
3 Tbsp vinagar
1/8-1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and refrigerate all day or overnight
Serve over Spinach, cooked eggs, chopped bacon     (optional croutons freshly toasted )
