Title:
Rice Flour Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c rice flour
1/4 c potato flour
3/4 c water

Directions:
Mix together in a big bowl until dough forms
Divide into 4 balls
Roll each one thin between plastic wrap or waxed paper
Heat non stick skillet, cook for 2 min on medium heat
Cook other side about 1 min
