Title:
Cabbage Beef Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
2 medium carrots, thinly sliced
2 lb cabbage, coarsely shredded
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
3/4 cup rice, uncooked
1 14 oz can beef broth
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In large saucepan, brown ground beef and onion.  Remove from heat.  Add carrots, cabbage, salt and pepper; toss to mix well.
Pour the rice in a 3 qt. casserole.  Spoon ground beef mixture on top of rice.
In a small bowl, mix beef broth and water and pour over mixture.  Bake uncovered for 1 hour.  To serve, mix well.
