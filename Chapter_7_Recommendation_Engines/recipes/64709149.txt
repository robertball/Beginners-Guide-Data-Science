Title:
Chicken Quinoa Alfredo Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5537263.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole rotisserie chicken
1 cup quinoa
2 cups vegetable broth
3 garlic cloves minced
5 grinds  from pepper grinder
4 grinds from salt grinder
1 cup matchstick carrots
3 cups roughly chopped spinach
1/2 cup jarred roasted red peppers, roughly chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
1 1/2  jar of Alfredo sauce (15 oz. jars)
1 tsp dried basil
Cooking spray
2 tbsp unsalted butter
3/4 cup panko bread crumbs
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Piece apart chicken and set aside
Add veggie broth,  garlic, salt and pepper to medium pot and bring to a boil. Add quinoa to pot, cover, lower heat and simmer for 15 minutes. Let stand for 5 minutes.
Roughly chop spinach and roasted red peppers. In a pan add olive oil, carrots, spinach and peppers and cook on ow to medium heat for about 5 minutes.
Mix chicken, quinoa, veggie mixture, Alfredo sauce and basil in a bowl.
Spray casserole dish with cooking spray.
Pour mixture into casserole dish, spreading it out into the dish and patting it down.
In a small sauce pan melt butter and then mix in panko bread crumbs until evenly coated. Remove from heat and add Parmesan cheese.
With a spoon, evenly distribute crumb and cheese over the top of the casserole.
Put in oven and bake between 25-30 minutes.
