Title:
Spiced Baked Apple with Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Large apples (Bramleys or Golden Delicious)
3 oz walnuts, roughly chopped
1 Tbs raisins
1 Tbs soft brown sugar
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 oz butter softened

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350
Core apples and make a horizontal cut in the skin around the middle os each. Place in lightly buttered shallow ovenproof dish.
mix together the walnuts,raisins,sugar and cinnamon with butter
Stuff each apple cavity with sone of the mixture. add 1/2 inch of water to the dish.
Place in overn and cook for about 30 min or until flesh is tender when pierced with point of a knife. Cool slightly serve warm.
