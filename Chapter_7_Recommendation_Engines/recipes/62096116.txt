Title:
Mom's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. lean ground beef
1 package Instant Dry onion soup mix
1 (10 3/4oz) Can Tomato soup
1/4 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp salt (optional)
1 Egg
2/3 Cup Milk
1/2 cup dry unseasoned bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 325'
Combine beef and all ingredients except tomato soup and mix well.
Shape meat mixture into the shape of a loaf and place in a glass baking dish
Pour entire can of undiluted tomato soup over loaf
Bake at 325' for 1 1/2 hours
Drain Off fat
Transfer to serving platter, Slice and Serve

Submitter: 
Jodster
Description: 
This is a simple but tasty way to make meatloaf. It's how my mom used to make it and I love it this way. Even my toddler cant get enough of it!
