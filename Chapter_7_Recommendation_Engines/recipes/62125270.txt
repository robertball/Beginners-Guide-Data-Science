Title:
Mom's Mystery Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white flour
1/2 cup wheat flour
2 cups fruit cocktail
1 egg
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
TOPPING:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Stir the dry ingredients together in a medium size bowl.
Stir in the beater egg and the fruit cocktail.
Pour into a greased 9X9 pan

Submitter: 
PREGOCOOK
Description: 
This is one of the first desserts I remember my mother teaching me to make when I was little.  The ingredients are simple and yet, together they are intriguing - the brown sugar carmelizes and the secret ingredient (fruit cocktail) confounds even the most knowledgable cook!
