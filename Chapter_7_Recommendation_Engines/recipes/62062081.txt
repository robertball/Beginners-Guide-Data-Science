Title:
Simple Oven Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes
1 large onion
1 package of dry italian salad dressing mix
olive oil

Directions:
wash potatoes and chop in to medium size pieces and place into a resealable container
roughly chop onion and add to potatoes.  (Be careful not to chop onion too small or they will burn during cooking time.)
drizzle potatoes and onions with olive oil.
sprinkle salad dressing mix over the potatoes and onions, seal the container and shake vigorously to coat evenly.
spray a 9 X 13 baking dish with non-stick spray and bake in a 375 degree oven for 90 minutes stirring occasionally or until potatoes and nicely browned and crunchy on the outside.

Submitter: 
JULIECT
Description: 
Dry salad dressing mix is the simple secret to this dish.  The result are nice crunchy brown potatoes that everyone will enjoy.  Be patient and let these potatoes bake in your oven for at least an hour for a sure winner.
