Title:
Healing Pumpkin Seeds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2736758.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup raw pumpkin seeds, unwashed with pulp
2 Tbsp melted coconut oil, or more to taste
1 Tbsp ground turmeric
1 tsp fresh ground black pepped
1 tsp ground cayenne powder, or more to taste
1 tsp ground ginger, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 150Â°C. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Mix the oil, turmeric, pepper, cayenne and ginger in a small bowl.
Put the  pumpkin seeds in a mixing  bowl  and drizzle the coconut oil mixture over them. Stir well making sure  all the seeds get coated with the mixture.
Spread the seeds evenly onto the parchment paper and drizzle any remaining oil on top.
Cook undistirbed for 15 minutes. Turn off the the oven and stir the seeds trying to flip them over. Leave the seeds inside the oven with the door open until cool.
Enjoy!
