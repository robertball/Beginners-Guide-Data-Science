Title:
The BEST beef Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 boneless beef roast 2 lbs or larger
2 10 3/4 oz cans Cream of Mushroom soup
1 13oz can Beef Broth
2 Beef Bouillon cubes
1 tbsp softened butter
1/2 sm red onion (if desired)
1 pk of Onion soup mix

Directions:
Brush butter on the inside of the slow cooker.
In a medium bowl mix the Cream of Mushroom soup and the Beef Broth until well blended.
Stir the Onion soup mix into the mixture unti well blended.
Pour 1/2 of the mixture into the slow cooker.
Trim any fat from the Beef and place in slow cooker.
Cover beef with remaining mixture.
Add bouillon cubes and onion.
Cook on low setting for 8 hours.
Begin cutting beef after it has cooked for at least 5 1/2 hours if desired.

Description: 
A very tender, unbeatable roast that cooks in a slow cooker while you do other things.  Fast, Easy, and Fabulous!
