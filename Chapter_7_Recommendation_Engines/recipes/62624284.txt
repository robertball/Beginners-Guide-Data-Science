Title:
Panko Chicken Tenders with Cajun Remoulade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6T mayonnaise
2T Dijon mustard
1t Cajun seasoning
1-1/2c Panko bread crumbs
1/2c buttermilk
1-1/2lb chicken tenders

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Combine mayo, mustard, and cajun seasoning to make sauce, adding salt and pepper to taste.
Season Panko with salt and pepper.
Place buttermilk in a shallow bowl.
Dip chicken in buttermilk, then panko.
Spray baking sheet and then spray chicken.
Do not have tenders touching while they cook.
Bake 25 minutes until done.
