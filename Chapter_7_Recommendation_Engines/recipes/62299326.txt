Title:
Look no further vegan brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/363529.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 cups raw sugar
1 cup Sucanat
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup soy or almond milk
1 cup apple sauce
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 ripe banana, mashed (optional)
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)
1/3 cup chopped chocolate/chips (optional)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In large bowl, mix flour, sugar/sucanat, baking powder, cocoa powder and salt.
Stir in milk, apple sauce, vanilla, banana, nuts and chocolate chunks until well blended.
Pour mixture into an 8x8 greased baking dish.
Bake for 30-35 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean when inserted to its center.
