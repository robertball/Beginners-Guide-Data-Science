Title:
Berries Romanoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2232582.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb strawberries - halved
2 cups raspberries
2 cups blueberries
1 cup sour cream
1 cup of heavy whipping cream
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoon BLACKBERRY (or any berry) brandy

Directions:
Mix sour cream, brown sugar and brandy in bowl.  Whip cream in separate bowl until thick.  Fold cream into sour cream mixture.  Layer clear glasses or bowls with 1 berry - 1 tablespoon of cream - 1 berry - etc.
