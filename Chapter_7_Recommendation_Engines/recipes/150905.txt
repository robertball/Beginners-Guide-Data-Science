Title:
PB Breeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977781.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ Coconut Rum
2 ounces cranberry juice
2 ounces pineapple juice
1 slice pineapple

Directions:
Pour Captain Morgan Parrot Bay(TM) Coconut Rum, cranberry juice, and pineapple juice into an ice-filled glass and stir: garnish with pineapple to serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Add fruit flavor to your drinks without all the slicing by using Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ in Coconut, Mango, Pineapple or Passion Fruit.
