Title:
Sweet Sloppy Joe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 can condensed tomato soup
1 onion (chopped)
butter
2 tbsp ketchup
2 tbsp mustard
1 tbsp worcester sauce
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 package burger buns

Directions:
Chop onion and caramelize (cook in butter until softened and browned)
brown ground beef
Keep heat on while you add the rest of the ingredients.
Drain grease and add onions, tomato soup, ketchup, mustard, worcester sauce, and brown sugar (adjust as needed for taste)
Serve!
