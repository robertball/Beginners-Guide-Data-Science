Title:
Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/4 cup icing sugar
6 Tbsp margarine
2 eggs
1 cup sugar
2 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp lemon zest
2 Tbsp flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
2-3 drops yellow food coloring
1 Tbsp icing sugar

Directions:
Combine 1 cup flour with icing sugar then cut in margarine then press into 8x8 glass pan. Bake 12 mins until lightly brown.
Beat 2 eggs 1 cup sugar, lemon juice and zest, flour and baking powder still smooth then pour over crust.
Bake 325 20 mins or until golden,
Cool and sprinkle with icing sugar
