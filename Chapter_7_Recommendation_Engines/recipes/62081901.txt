Title:
Grandma's Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb Peeled and sliced carrots
1lb sliced celery
1 bunch of dill
2 Chopped and seeded tomatoes
1 Large onion Chopped
1 tbs Kosher salt
1 tbs Pepper
1 5lb Chicken

Directions:
First cut up all over your vegetables and put into a 2qt soup pot. Clean your chicken take off some fat but leave some for the flavor. Put the chicken into the pot with the vegetables. Clean the dill but don't seperate it. Take white thread and tie it around the dill. Add the dill, pepper and salt to the pot. Put it warm water almost 2 inches above the chicken. Cook at med-low fire. Cook until you see the chicken just about done. Take the chicken out and cut it off the bone. Add the cut chicken back into the pot. Cook around a half hour longer.Throw away the dill. Serve with Egg Noodles.
(If you don't like to add tomato like some of my family we just put a whole tomato in the pot just for the flavor and take it out when the soup is done)
