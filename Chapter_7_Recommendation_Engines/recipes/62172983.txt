Title:
Festive fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3C chocolate chips
1 (14oz) eagle brand sweetened condensed milk
dash salt
1/2 -1C chopped nuts, optional
1 1/2tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
In heavy saucepan; over low heat, melt chocloate chips with milk and salt. Remove from heat;stir in nuts and vanilla. Spread evenly into wax paper or buttered foil lined 8-9inch pan.
Chill 2hours or until firm. Turn fudge onto cutting board and peel off the wax paper and cut into squares. Store in the refriderator.
