Title:
Mexicali Beer Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/856262.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can limeade concentrate
1 1/2 cups gold tequila
3 (12 fluid ounce) cans Mexican beer
1 whole lime, cut into 6 wedges

Directions:
Pour the limeade, tequila, and beer into a pitcher; stir. Fill tall glasses with ice and pour mixture into the glasses; squeeze a lime wedge into each drink.

Submitter: 
chefjeff
Description: 
This is a variation of another posted 'Beer Margarita' with a little extra 'oomph.' It's quite delicious.
