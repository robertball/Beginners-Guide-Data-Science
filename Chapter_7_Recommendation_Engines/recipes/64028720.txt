Title:
Roasted Potatoes with Rosemary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large Yukon Gold potatoes - peeled and quartered
2 cloves of garlic - halved
1 1/2 tsp. chopped fresh rosemary
1 tsp. chopped fresh oregano or 1/4 tsp. dried
2 tbsp. olive oil
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Place potatoes in a large baking dish.
Add remaining ingredients and toss to coat evenly.
Bake uncovered, stirring occasionally, until browned - about 1 1/2 hours.
Serve immediately.
