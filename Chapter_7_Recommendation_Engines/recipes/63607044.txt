Title:
Cheesy Vegetable Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp butter
1 clove garlic, minced
1 small crown broccoli, chopped
1 carrot, peeled and sliced into 1/4 inch pieces
1 c baby spinach leaves
2 tbsp heavy cream
1/2 c vegetable stock
2 oz queso fresco
1 tbsp parmesan reggiano
1 wedge Laughing Cow light swiss
1 c cooked quinoa
salt and pepper to taste
1 tbsp pepitas

Directions:
1. Melt butter in a heavy pan or skillet over medium-low heat
2. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, 3-4 minutes
3. Crumble queso fresco and Lauging Cow into pan with the butter and garlic
4. Add cream and vegetable stock, melt cheese for 2-3 minutes
5. Add vegetables, cover and simmer until vegetables are cooked through and spinach is wilted
6. While vegetables are cooking, toast pepitas over medium heat
7. Uncover vegetables and add quinoa. Cook until heated through and season with salt and pepper
8. Top wth toasted pepitas for serving
