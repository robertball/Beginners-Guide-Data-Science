Title:
Catalina Grilled Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 6 oz cut of lean steak
1 16 oz bottle  fat free catalina salad dressing

Directions:
Place steak in a glass baking dish. Place bottle over the steak let marinate over night in the refrigerator.
Grill next day to the degree of desired doneness.
