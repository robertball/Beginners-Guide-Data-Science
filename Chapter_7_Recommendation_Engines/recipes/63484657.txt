Title:
Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken boned and cubed
3 tbs butter
1 apple peeled and cored and chopped
2 bananas peeled and chopped
2 celery stalks chopped
2 cloves of garlic minced
1 14 1/2 oz can chicken broth
1 14 1/2 oz can tomatoes, chopped
2 tbs cornstarch
2 tbs curry powder
s/p
6 carrots, peeled and chunked
6 medium potatoes, peeled and chunked

Directions:
melt butter in dutch oven.  add chicken, apples and bananas, onion, celery, and garlic.  cook until chicken is white and onion is tender, stirring.
add chicken broth (reserving 1/2 cup), carrots, potatoes, tomatoes, curry powder, salt, and pepper.  bring to a boil and simmer 1 hour.
mix corn starch in reserved chicken broth until smooth.  stir in  stew and simmer until thickened.
serve over boiled rice
