Title:
Grandma's Special Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked egg noodles
1 1/2 lbs. ground beef
1 tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp. cumin powder
28 oz. can tomato sauce
2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 cup sour cream
4 oz. cream cheese, softened
2 cups cheddar cheese, grated
4 green onions, diced

Directions:
Prepare noodles as directed on package. Drain.
Spread noodles on bottom of 4-quart (5L) casserole dish.
Brown beef over medium heat in frying pan, breaking up chunks.(10-15 min.)
Drain fat from skillet.
Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, cumin powder, chili powder and garlic salt to the meat over med.-high heat.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat, and simmer 10 min.
Spoon over noodles.
Meanwhile in a medium bowl, mash cream cheese with sour cream and green onions.
Spread evenly over meat mixture.
Sprinkle cheese over top.
Bake, uncovered, in 350F oven for 35 min.

Submitter: 
Miss Ashley
Description: 
This is a delicious dish my granny would always make for us on weekend visits! Delicious and easy, there are always smiles when I make this casserole!
