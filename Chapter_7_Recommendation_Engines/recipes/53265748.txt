Title:
Sangria la Frambuesa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
750 ml Zinfandel
3oz rasberry liqueur
3 TBS sugar
1 lime sliced in 1/4 inch rounds
1 orange sliced in 1/4 inch rounds
juice of 2 oranges
1/2 dry pint rasberries
2 cups club soda

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except club soda in a large glass pitcher and chill, 2-24 hours.  To serve add club soda and place fruit pieces in each glass.
