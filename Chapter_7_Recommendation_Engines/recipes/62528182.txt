Title:
chorizo bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 or 5 chorizo sausages
Garlic
dates
toothpicks

Directions:
Cut the Chorizo into 1/2 inch slices, cut the dates in half and stuff with small size garlic pieces, and insert toothpick into dates and garlic and chorizo slices. Place on cookie sheet and bake and 400F for 10 minutes. take of cookie sheet and place on tray!!!
