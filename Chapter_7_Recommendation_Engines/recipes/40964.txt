Title:
Very Berry Strawberry Smoothie Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992838.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) READY CRUST® Graham Cracker Pie Crust
1/3 cup cranberry juice
1 (.25 ounce) envelope unflavored gelatin powder
3 cups chopped strawberries
1 (8 ounce) container raspberry yogurt
Whipped cream (garnish)
Strawberries (garnish)

Directions:
Pour juice into medium microwaveable bowl, sprinkle gelatin over juice. Let stand 5 minutes to dissolve.  Heat juice and gelatin in microwave on High for 15 seconds, stir, repeat.
Pour juice mixture into blender, add fruit, yogurt, and liquid gelatin.  Puree for 3 minutes until smooth.
Pour into pie shell and refrigerate 4 hours. Garnish with whipped cream and strawberries.

Submitter: 
Ready Crust® Kitchens
Description: 
Fresh strawberries (or your fruit of choice)  is combined with yogurt to create this refreshing dessert pie.
