Title:
Apricot Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2675659.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups sugar
3/4 cup butter or margarine
2 eggs
2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted
1/2 teaspoon grated lemon peel
1 (21 ounce) can LUCKY LEAFÂ® Premium Apricot Fruit Filling & Topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Grease an 8x12-inch pan.
Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs singly and beat until light and fluffy. Add flour and lemon rind.
Put 1/2 dough in prepared pan and cover with apricot fruit filling. Drop remaining dough over top.
Bake for 45 minutes. Serve with light cream or milk.

Submitter: 
Lucky Leaf Fruit Filling
Description: 
Apricot fruit filling is baked between rich, buttery layers for a delightful tea time snack.
