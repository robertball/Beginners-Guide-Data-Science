Title:
Stuffed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 skinless boneless chicken breasts
1 box stove top stuffing
1 C water
1 C shredded parmesan cheese
1 can cream of celery soup
1 C milk or half and half
pepper and garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Pat dry chicken breasts.  Make a slit legthwise in each piece.
In med bowl mix stuffing mix and water and 3/4 C cheese.
Divide the stuffing into 5 parts and stuff into slit in breasts.
Arrange in 9x13 baking dish and season with pepper and garlic powder.
In bowl wisk together soup and milk.
Pour over breasts.
Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the top.
Bake 45 minutes until bubbly and browning.
