Title:
Chocolate Banana Peanut Butter Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1054959.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
4 tablespoons chocolate instant breakfast mix
2 tablespoons crunchy peanut butter
2 bananas, sliced
4 cubes ice

Directions:
In a blender, combine milk, instant breakfast mix, peanut butter, bananas and ice. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
This shake with instant breakfast mix, peanut butter, bananas, and milk, is great for a morning breakfast, or a snack anytime during the day.
