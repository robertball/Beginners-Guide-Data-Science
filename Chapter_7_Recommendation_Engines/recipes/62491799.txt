Title:
Pumpkin Whoopie Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cookies:
BEAT TILL FLUFFY;
.    1/2 cup softened butter
.    1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
ADD & BEAT WELL
.    2 Slightly beaten eggs
ADD & MIX WELL
.    1 1/2 Cups solid pack pumpkin
.      1/2 teaspoon salt
.      1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
.    1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
.      1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
.    1 teaspoon baking powder
.    1 teaspoon baking soda
.    1 teaspoon vanilla extract
STIR IN;
.    2 cups all-purpose flour
Cream Cheese Filling:
Whip Together Until Fluffy
.    8 ounces cream cheese, softened
.      1/2 stick butter, softened
.      1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
GRADUALLY ADD THEN BEAT TILL LIGHT & FLUFFY
.    1 1/2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
For cookies: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease or line four baking sheets with parchment paper.
Drop batter by spoonfulls onto prepared baking sheets. (Judge size of spoonfulls so that you end up with a number of cookies x 2 (You need top & bottom)
Bake for 10 to 13 minutes or until springy to the touch. Cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
Spread filling onto flat side of one cookie; top with flat side of second cookie to make a sandwich. Repeat with remaining cookies & filling.
Store in covered container in refrigerator.
