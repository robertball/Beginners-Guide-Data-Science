Title:
Savory Florentine Oatmeal Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7983786.jpg
Ingredients:
½ cup quick or old-fashioned oats*
1 cup water
½ cup spinach, coarsely chopped
1-2 teaspoons butter (1 pat)
¼ cup tomatoes, chopped
1 tablespoon shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
1. Bring water to a boil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Stir in oats and cook: 1 minute for quick oats, 5 minutes for old-fashioned oats, stirring occasionally.
2. Spoon oats into bowl over chopped spinach and butter, and stir well to wilt spinach. Top with chopped tomato and  shredded cheese.
3. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.

Submitter: 
Jessie Furman
Description: 
Make oatmeal a multi-dimensional pantry staple. This recipe can be made into a larger meal by adding an egg on top, chopped cooked chicken/tofu, additional vegetables, or seasonings.
