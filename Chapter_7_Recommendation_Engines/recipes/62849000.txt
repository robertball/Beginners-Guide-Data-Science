Title:
Tomato and Crab Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/679406.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbs olive oil
5 Scallions, thinly sliced
1.5 stalks lemon grass peeled,trimmed thinly sliced
1 fresh thai chile seeded, minced
2.5 # beefsteak tomatoes,chopped
2.25 C chicken stock
1/2 C coconut milk
3 Tbs fresh OJ
1 Tbs fish sauce
1 Tbs fresh lime juice
1/2 # Lump crabmeat.
3/4 C pea shoots trimmed
6 snow peas trimmed and thinly cut at an angle.

Directions:
Heat oil in a large saucepan over Med hea. Add scallions,lemongrass and chile. Cook until softened 3-4 min.
Add tomatoes cook another 5 min. Stir in chicken stock,cocnut milk,OJ, and fish sauce.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 15min.Let cool slightly.
Puree in blender until smooth. Stir in lime juice season to taste with salt. Garnish with crab,shoots and peas.
