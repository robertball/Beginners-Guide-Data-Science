Title:
Chicken & Stuffing Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. stuffing
1 stick butter
3-4 chicken breasts
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 1/2 cans chicken broth

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
2. Melt butter and mix with stuffing, set aside.
3. Mix soups together.
4. Put 2/3 of stuffing on bottom of 9x13 dish, top with chicken, then soup, then rest of stuffing.
5. Bake for 40-45 minutes.
