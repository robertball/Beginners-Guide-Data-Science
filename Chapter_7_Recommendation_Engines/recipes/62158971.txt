Title:
Twinkie Strawberry Shortcake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 Twinkies
2 lb. fresh strawberries
1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 tsp. almond extract
1 gallon vanilla ice cream
1 container Cool Whip
sliced toasted almonds for garnish

Directions:
Wash, hull and slice the strawberries and place into a large bowl.
Add the sugar, vanilla and almond extract to the strawberries and stir well to combine; let sit for 15-20 minutes.
Slice each Twinkie lengthwise.
Use the bottom halves of the Twinkies to line a 13 x 9 baking dish, reserving the top halves.
Top the Twinkies with small scoops of vanilla ice cream and a layer of strawberries with juice.
Layer with the Twinkie tops.
Spread the Cool Whip in a thick layer over the Twinkies and top with the remaining strawberries and juice.
Top with toasted almonds to garnish.
