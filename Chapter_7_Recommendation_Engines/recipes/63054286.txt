Title:
Nana's Pineapple Chiffon Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 envelope gelatin
1/4 c. cold water
1 1/4 c. canned crushed pineapple
4 eggs (separated; beat yolks slightly & follow recipe for the whites)
1/4 tsp. salt
1 Tbs. lemon juice
1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp. corn starch

Directions:
Dissolve the gelatin in cold water and add the corn starch to the cold water as well.
Cook over boiling water, (double boiler), the crushed pineapple, salt, egg yolks that have been beaten slightly, lemon juic, and 1/4 c. of sugar until custard consistency.  Add the dissolved gelatin to the custard mixture and stir until dissolved.
Cool. When mixture begins to congeal, fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites to which the other 1/4 c. of sugar has been added.  Pour into a baked pie shell and chill until firm.  Serve with whipped cream.

Description: 
This is my Mom's favorite pie & when my Nana passed away, we thought the pie recipe went with her. Fortunately, I found the recipe in her Swedish cookbook, so I'm putting it in my recipe box so I never lose it again!
