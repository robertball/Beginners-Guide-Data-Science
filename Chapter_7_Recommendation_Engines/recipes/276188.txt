Title:
Dianne's Pomegranate Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/284717.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces vodka
1 fluid ounce pomegranate juice
1/2 fluid ounce triple sec
1 teaspoon frozen limeade
1/2 cup ice as needed
1 slice lime (optional)

Directions:
Combine vodka, pomegranate juice, triple sec, and limeade with ice in a cocktail shaker. Shake and strain into a martini glass. Garnish with lime.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
I created this tart and tangy martini to suit my tastes, I hope you enjoy it too! You can substitute silver tequila for the vodka to create a pomegranate margarita! Perfect for Thanksgiving.
