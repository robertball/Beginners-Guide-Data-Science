Title:
Ham Stock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ham Hock (sliced open) or Bone
Enough water to cover
8 mini carrots, sliced
1/4 onion, diced
Corn off a cobb and juice (optional)
salt and pepper to taste
parsley to taste

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a stock pot on high.  Bring to boil; turn down to simmer for 2.5-3 hours.  Strain out solids.
