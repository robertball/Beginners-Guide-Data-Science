Title:
Chicken Florentine Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/4 cup butter
3 teaspoons minced garlic
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
2/3 cup half-and-half
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 bags fresh spinach leaves, wilted in microwave
8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
2/3 cup bacon bits
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Boil chicken breasts in chicken broth. Cube the chicken. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Cook mushrooms in butter. Add the garlic, lemon juice, cream of mushroom soup, Italian seasoning, half-and-half, and Parmesan cheese. Spray pan with Pam. Drain spinach in colander and then tear up and arrange the spinach over the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Pour half the soup mixture from the saucepan over the spinach. Arrange cubed chicken breasts in the dish, and cover with the remaining sauce mixture. Sprinkle with bacon bits, and top with mozzarella cheese.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes in the 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) oven, until bubbly and lightly browned.

Description: 
Chicken Florentine casserole, which can also be adapted with either fish or shrimp, lies on a bed of spinach leaves and mushrooms, has a creamy white sauce mixed with garlic, Parmesan cheese, and Italian seasonings, and is topped with baked mozzarella.Modified from: Chicken Florentine Casserole
