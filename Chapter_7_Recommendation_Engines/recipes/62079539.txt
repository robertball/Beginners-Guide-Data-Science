Title:
Easy Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bananas
Vanilla Wafers
1- Large box of instant vanilla pudding mix
2 cups cold milk
2/3 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 8oz cool whip
1 8oz sour cream

Directions:
Combine the pudding mix, milk, sugar & vanilla and beat for approximately 2 min. until well blended.   Fold in the cool whip and sour cream and mix well.  Put a layer of the vanilla wafers in the bottom of a serving dish then slices of banana and half of the pudding mixture then repeat with another layer.  Top the pudding with a layer of vanilla wafers.

Submitter: 
Donna Leigh
Description: 
This is an easy no cook banana pudding recipe that is always a hit!
