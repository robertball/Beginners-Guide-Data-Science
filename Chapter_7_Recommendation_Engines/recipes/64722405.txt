Title:
Banana Pudding (Joanna Gaines) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese
14 ounce can sweetened condensed milk
1 cup whole milk
3.4 ounce package vanilla instant pudding  mix (not generic)
3 bananas
3 cups heavy whipping cream
1/2 cup granulated sugar
2 tsp vanilla
1 package vanilla wafers (minis are good)

Directions:
Combine cream, sugar and vanilla, beat on high until soft peaks form. Set aside.
In another bowl beat cream cheese, until smooth, add condensed milk, whole milk and pudding. Beat on high for 2 minutes. Fold in half of the whipped cream mixture
In a trifle dish, or individual glasses lay a single layer of wafers on the bottom. Add  a layer of  pudding mixture on top, then a layer of wafers, then a layer of bananas, then another layer of  pudding, wafers and bananas (final layers of wafers and bananas are better when chopped up). Top with whipped cream.
Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
