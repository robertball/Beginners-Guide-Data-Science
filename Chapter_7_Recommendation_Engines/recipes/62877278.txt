Title:
Vanilla Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/692580.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Milk
1 Teaspoon Simple Vanilla Syrup

Directions:
Pour milk into a glass cup. Mix simple vanilla syrup into the milk and stir until frothy.
Serve immediately or keep refrigerated.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
Pure vanilla flavored milk makes a nice treat and is an alternative to chocolate milk. Easy to whip up and pleasing to drink!
