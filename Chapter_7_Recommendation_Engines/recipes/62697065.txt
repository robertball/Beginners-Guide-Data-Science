Title:
Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans fat-free refried beans
1  pounds lean ground beef
1 cup shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese
8 (6 inch) flour tortillas, warmed
1 box hard shelled tacos
1 cup chopped lettuce
1 medium tomato, chopped
taco sauce
western dressing (for kids)

Directions:
Fry meat and add refried beans.  Spoon meat into the desired number of hard tacos and cook at 350 for 10 minutes.  Use additional meat to put on tortillas.  Top with desired toppings.
