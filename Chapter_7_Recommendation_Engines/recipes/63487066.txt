Title:
The Scottish Salmon Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sunflower Oil - for frying
350g/12oz fillet Salmon - skinned and deboned
100g/3½oz Smoked Salmon - roughly chopped
3 Spring Onions - finely sliced
450g/1lb Potatoes - cooked, drained, dried a bit and mashed with a little milk
Whole Milk
½ Lemon - Zest + Juice - to taste
1 Egg - lightly beaten
120g/4oz fresh White Breadcrumbs - for coating burgers
1tbsp fresh Dill - finely chopped
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Peel potatoes, cook in lightly salted water in a saucepan, until soft;
Drain, dry out a bit, season and mash with a little milk;
Combine and blend the fresh salmon with smoked salmon with a blender or in a food processor.
Mix in and combine well spring onion, dill, lemon zest and juice to taste, egg yolk and seasoning;
Add mashed potatoes to thicken mixture sufficiently to roll into balls;
Roll mixture into balls, coat with breadcrumbs and flatten into burger shapes;
Set aside on tray, cover with clingfilm and chill in the fridge for an hour or so;
Heat oil in large frying pan, add burgers and cook on medium heat, turning to ensure golden brown on both sides, until fish is just cooked through.
