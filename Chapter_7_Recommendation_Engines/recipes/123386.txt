Title:
Flavorful Sugar Snap Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984843.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh sugar snap or snow peas
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 cup finely chopped fully cooked ham
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a small saucepan, bring 1 in. of water and peas to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 3-4 minutes or until crisp-tender.
Meanwhile, heat oil in a large skillet; add the ham, garlic and thyme. Cook and stir for 2 minutes. Drain peas; add to skillet and saute for 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Connie  Moore
Description: 
'Our family enjoys the first peas from our garden stir-fried as a crisp treat,' notes Connie Moore of Medway, Ohio.
