Title:
Mae's Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large heads Broccoli(cut into small florets)
1 Medium red onion(chopped)
8 Slices crispy bacon(crumbled)
1 Large carrot(coarsely grated)
1 cup raisins
1 cup cashew halves
1 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup white sugar
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
In small bowl mix mayo, sugar and milk with whisk. Set aside.
In large salad bowl mix all ingredients together pour dressing over top and toss well. Refrigerate. Serve cold.

Submitter: 
Mae Long
Description: 
This salad is insanely addicting. You'd better make plenty, because my family can never get enough.
