Title:
Red Coach Horseshoe Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb white american cheese
1/2 lb cream cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 gal. milk
1/4 cup Worcestershire
1/2 lb butter
1/2 tbls. cayenne pepper
1/2 tbls. white pepper
1 1/2 cups flour

Directions:
Melt butter and add flour, Worcestershire, cayenne pepper & white pepper.
Cook 5 minutes
Add sour cream - blend until smooth.
Add room temp milk slowly while stirring.
Add cheese stirring constantly.
Add 1 cup room temp Stag beer.
Cook until thick & smooth approximately 10 minutes after cheese melts.
Freeze what you don't use by the next day.

Submitter: 
wkngmomof2
Description: 
This is the best cheese sauce for horseshoes ever created.  It is a large recipe because it came from a restaurant.  The sauce is meant to be served as following:  Place two slices of toasted bread on a plate, top with hamburgers, sliced ham, slice turkey, or pork tenderloin.  Place a layer of french fries on top of the meat and cover in sauce.
