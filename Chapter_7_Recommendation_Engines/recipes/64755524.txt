Title:
Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 c. brown sugar
1 Tbsp. prepared mustard
1/2 c. ketchup
1 1/2 Tbsp wine vinegar
1/4 lb bacon
1 medium onion
1 28 oz can pork & beans, drain most liquid
1 28 oz can baked beans, drain half of liquid
1 15.5 oz can butter beans, drained

Directions:
Brown hamburger. Drain & set aside.
Cook bacon. Cool & crumble. Saute onion in bacon grease until translucent.
Mix all ingredients in 9x13 and bake at 300 degrees for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Alyssa Long
Description: 
I received this recipe from my husband's aunt at my recipe shower before hubby & I got married 26 years ago. It's one of 2 that I received that day that I still make all of the time. These are very good & always requested for BBQ's
