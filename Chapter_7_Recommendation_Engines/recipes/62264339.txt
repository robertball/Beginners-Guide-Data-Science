Title:
Healthy Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Banana, mashed
1/2 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup low fat plain or vanilla yogourt
1 egg
1/4 cup canola oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
3/4 cup quick cooking oats
1 cup all purpose flour
3/4 cup whole wheat flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
3/4 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
In a medium mixing bowl combine banana, brown sugarm yogourt, egg, oil and vanilla.  Mix in rolled oats and allow to sit for 10 minutes.  In a small mixing bowl combine remaining ingredients.  Add to wet ingredients and stir gently until just combined.  Spoon into paper lined muffin cups and bake in a preheated 375 oven for 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Stacey
Description: 
I have been making this muffin recipe for years.  I always use chocolate chips for my husband and kids, but I prefer to put blueberries in mine.
