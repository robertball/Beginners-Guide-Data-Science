Title:
Fiber One Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/789777.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
1/2 cup Fiber One cereal, finely ground
3/4 cup hot milk
pinch salt

Directions:
Mix flour, ground cereal and salt together in large bowl.
Gradually add in hot milk
Stir until dough forms
Knead dough for 5 minutes.
Cut into desired shapes either by hand or using pasta machine
Boil in salted water for 4-5 minutes
