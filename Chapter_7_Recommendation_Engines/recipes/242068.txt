Title:
Not Your Average Hamburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3775016.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
6 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons dried parsley
2 tablespoons dried basil
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon ground black pepper
garlic salt, or to taste

Directions:
Mix ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, basil, brown sugar, black pepper, and garlic salt together in a bowl; form into 10 patties.
Cook patties in a skillet, working in batches, until browned and desired doneness is reached, 4 to 5 minutes per side.
