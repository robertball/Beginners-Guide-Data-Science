Title:
Rhonda's Curried Coconut Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-lbs tailed and deveined cooked shrimp
1-lg chopped onion
4-6 cloves garlic pressed
3-tbls butter
1-lg potato peeled and cubed
2-ribs celery chopped
1-carrot cubed
1-teas. salt and pepper
1.5-tbls oil
2-tbls curry powder
1-14oz can coconut milk
1- 10oz can rotel(I use mild)undrained
1-8oz can tomato sauce
3-tbls sugar
steamed rice

Directions:
In a small skillet saute curry powder in oil for 5 min.(important process to bring out curry flavor!)
Add garlic and chopped onion and saute 5 min till soft
Add shrimp and saute 2 min more and set to side.
In a Large skillet saute carrot, potato and celery in 3 tbls butter for 5 min
Add rotel to potato mix and simmer till reduced about 5 min
Add coconut milk, tomato sauce, sugar and curry/onion mix
Cover and simmer 20 to 30 min.
Serve over steamed rice

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This is mild on the spice but big on tropical flavor!
