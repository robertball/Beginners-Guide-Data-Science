Title:
Smoked Turkey with Havarti Cheese & Cranberry Sauce Sandwich1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 slices EARTH GRAINS® Extra Fiber Whole Wheat bread
2 ounces sliced smoked turkey
1 slice Havarti cheese
4 1/2 tablespoons cranberry sauce

Directions:
Toast Earth Graind Extra Fiber 100% Whole Wheat bread.
Spread each slice with cranberry sauce.
On top of one slice of bread, place 2 ounces of smoked turkey and 1 slice of Havarti cheese.
Top with other slice of bread and serve.

Submitter: 
EARTH GRAINS®
Description: 
Smoked turkey is perfectly accented with Havarti cheese and cranberry sauce in this delicious sandwich.
