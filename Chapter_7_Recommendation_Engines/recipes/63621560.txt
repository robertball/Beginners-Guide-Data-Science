Title:
Cranberry Apple Piecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1007318.jpg
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 apple, chopped (preferably Granny Smith)
12 oz cranberries, fresh or frozen
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup juice (orange, apple, or comparable)
3/4 t cinnamon
Crust:
2 eggs, whipped on high for two minutes
1 cup sugar
6 T butter, melted and cooled
2 t vanilla
1/4 cup sour cream
1 cup flour
1/4 t salt
cinnamon sugar for topping

Directions:
Make filling by cubing apple into chunks and mixing well with other ingredients.
Beat eggs on high 2 minutes
Add other ingredients except flour and mix well to incorporate
Add flour and mix gently, just until smooth.
Pour into pan (deep pie dish or other 10" round pan) on top of filling.
Sprinkle cinnamon sugar on top
Bake at 325 for 55 to 60 minutes
