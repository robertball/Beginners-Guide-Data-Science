Title:
Prawn Banh Mi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large Carrot, Peeled and Shredded
1 Celery Stick, Chopped
2 scallions (green onion), chopped
4 tbs. Rice Vinegar
1/3 cup Fresh Cilantro, Chopped
3 tbs low fat mayonnaise
3 tbs low fat plain yogurt
1 tbs lime juice
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
3 12inch baguettes (Cut in half) OR 12 slices of bread
1 lb peeled cooked prawns, thawed, tails removed
18-24 thin slices of cucumber

Directions:
Thaw prawns under cold running water.
Combine carrot, celery, scallions and rice vinegar and set aside to marinate (vegetables).
Combine cilantro, mayonnaise, yogurt and lime juice (Sauce).
Add 2 tsp sauce to each half of baguette, or bottom bread slice. Using a slotted spoon, divide vegetables among baguette/bread equally (discard vinegar). Combine the prawns with remaining sauce and place 10 prawns on each baguette half or bottom bread slice. Top with cucumbers. Serve.

Submitter: 
J9
Description: 
A french inspired Vietnamese sandwich. 250 calories per sandwich (when using bread that is 150 calories per 2 slices).
