Title:
Jana's pressure cooker lentil ham soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs. smoked ham shanks
1 large yellow onion chopped
3 medium carrots, thinly sliced
2 cloves garlic, pressed or diced
2 stalks celery diced
1.25 t. dried thyme
1 bay leaf
2 c. lentils rinsed and inspected
1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes
pepper
vinegar, sherry or hot sauce to taste

Directions:
Rinse ham shanks.  Place shanks and 10 c. water into pressure cooker.  bring to 15 lbs. pressure, reduce heat and cook for 20 min. release pressure immediately.
Remove shanks to dish and empty ham broth into medium bowl. When cool enough, remove meat from bones and dice. Set aside.
Saute carrots, celery,garlic and onions in olive oil in pressure cooker until reduced, about 20 mins.
Add lentils, 8 cups of ham broth, thyme and bay leaf to vegetables. Pressure cook at 15 lbs. for 6 minutes.  Immediately reduce pressure.  Add tomatoes,reserved meat and pepper to taste.
finish with vinegar.  Sherry or hot sauce can also be used.

Submitter: 
skewtour
Description: 
Create a delicious ham broth first and use it to cook the lentils along with herbs and sauteed vegetables. the pressure cooker cuts down cooking time, but preserves the flavor of each ingredient.
