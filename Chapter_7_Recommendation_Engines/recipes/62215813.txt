Title:
Oh-So-Good Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground turkey (browned)
28 oz can diced tomatoes
12 oz can tomato sauce
12 oz can kidney beans
1/4 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp ground oregano
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp sea salt
3/4 tsp ground cumin
1/8 tsp chili powder
2/3 cup onion (diced)
2/3 cup red, green, &/or yellow bell pepper (diced)
16 oz canned tomato juice
dash of hot sauce
shredded cheddar cheese
saltine or oyster crackers

Directions:
Brown the ground turkey in a skillet and drain.
In a large soup pot or crockpot add all the ingredients except the kidney beans.
Simmer 1 hour on low-medium heat in soup pot or 2-3 hours on low in crockpot.
Add drained kidney beans for the last 15 minutes in soup pot or last 45 minutes in crockpot.
Adjust to desired thickness and hotness by adding more tomato juice and hot sauce.
Serve topped with shredded cheese and enjoy with crackers.

Submitter: 
Sarah Van Sell
Description: 
This is a great, easy, not-too-spicy chili with lots of flavor.  I always freeze the leftovers for those cold winter days when you don't feel like cooking but want some comfort food.
