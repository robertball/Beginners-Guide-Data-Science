Title:
Caramel Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (1 ounce) sugar-free instant butterscotch pudding mix
2 cups reduced-fat whipped topping, divided
4 cups diced apples (red and green) with peel attached)
1/2 cup chopped salted peanuts, chopped
1 can crushed pineapple with juice

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk crushed pineapple (fruit and juice) and pudding mix for 1 minute (mixture will be thick). Whisk in 1 cup whipped topping. Fold in remaining topping. Fold in apples. Fold in peanuts. Chill until serving.

Submitter: 
Kymmie
Description: 
Kids of all ages will love this healthy delicious apple salad. At our house we eat it for dessert but it can go with a hot sunny day meal as well. Serve chilled and it always hits the spot!
