Title:
Spinach Orange Jicama Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small jicama, peeled & cut into strips
1 small purple onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup unsweetened orange juice
2 Tbl white wine vinegar (or rice vinegar)
1/4 tsp salt
5 cups spinach, torn
olive oil spray
2 oranges peeled & sectioned (or can mandarin oranges)
1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper
2 Tbl pine nuts or sliced almons

Directions:
Combine jicama & onion in plastic bag; pour next 4 ingredients into bag. Close bag & mix well. Refrigerate 1 hour, turning bag occasionally. Drain jicama mixture, reserving 1 Tbl juice mixture.
Place spinach in serving bowl. coat w/ olive oil spray, tossing well. Add jicama mixture with reserved juice, orange sections & nuts.
