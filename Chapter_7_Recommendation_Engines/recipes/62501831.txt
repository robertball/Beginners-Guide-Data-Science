Title:
Schweddy Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/500103.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound sage flavored Jimmy Dean Pork Sausage
3 cups Bisquick Baking Mix
1 pound Velveeta cheese
1 dash Cholula Hot Sauce if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â° F. Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl; stir well. Form into 1 inch balls.
Place on ungreased baking sheet; bake 18-20 minutes or until golden brown.
Remove from oven; cool 5 minutes before removing from pan. Serve with cocktail forks or toothpicks.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
Making appetizers is fun and easy with these tasty cheesy sausage balls. They'll be the hit of every Christmas Party you attend
