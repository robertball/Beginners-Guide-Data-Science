Title:
Chicken Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 Lb Ground chicken
2 Tsp Ground Fennel
1 Tsp Salt
1 Tsp Pepper
1 Tsp Garlic Powder
1 Tsp Onion Powder
2 Tsp Italian Seasoning
2 Tbs Fresh Parsley
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup Romano cheese
1/4 cup Spaghetti sauce
Shells:
1 Pkg Manicotti shells
5 1/2 cup Spaghetti sauce

Directions:
Mix all filling ingredients together
Fill Shells with filling
Put 3/4 cup of sauce on backing dish
Place filled shells in baking dish
Pour the rest of sauce over filled shells
Cover with foil
Bake at 400 degrees for 1 Hour
uncover
Sprinkle some grated Mozerella over top
Cook for 10 more minutes
