Title:
Easy Oven-Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 potatoes, cubed
1/4 c. olive oil
1/2 c. chopped sweet onion
garlic salt
basil
parmesan

Directions:
put cubed potatoes and onion in a large bowl.  Mix in olive oil to coat.  Pour out onto greased baking sheet.  Sprinkle liberally with garlic salt (garlic powder if you're watching your sodium), and lightly with basil.  Sprinkle parmesan over everything.
Bake at 400 for around 30 minutes or until potato edges are slightly crispy.

Submitter: 
Sarah Smith
Description: 
A quick and easy to prepare side dish for any meal.
