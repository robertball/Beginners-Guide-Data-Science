Title:
Lemon Ginger Sauce with Dill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/235158.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup DannonÂ® All Natural Plain Yogurt
1 large shallot, minced
1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
1 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest
1 tablespoon minced peeled fresh ginger
1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/8 teaspoon sea salt
1/8 teaspoon freshly cracked pepper

Directions:
Whisk together all ingredients and chill in refrigerator until ready to serve. Serve on top of cooked fish, or to the side.

Submitter: 
DANNON
Description: 
A light, lemony herb sauce, made with Dannon Yogurt, is refreshing on cooked fish.
