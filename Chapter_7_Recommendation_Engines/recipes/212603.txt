Title:
Broccoli Cheese New Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894947.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Broccoli Cheese Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1/2 cup sour cream or yogurt
1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1 1/2 pounds small new potatoes, quartered
2 medium onions, cut into wedges
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Stir the soup, sour cream and hot pepper sauce in a large bowl. Add the potatoes and onions and toss to coat.
Arrange the potato mixture in a 2-quart shallow baking dish. Sprinkle with the Parmesan cheese. Bake at 375 degrees F for 50 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Creamy broccoli cheese soup and sour cream bake into a fantastic sauce and turn new potatoes into a special treat.
