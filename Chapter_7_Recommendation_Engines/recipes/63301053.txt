Title:
bananna foster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
juice of 1 orange
1/4 cup buter
1/2 cup light brown sugar  packed
4 ripe banannas
1/8 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup white rum
1/4 cup bananna liquer

Directions:
melt butter and brown sugar in skillet add banannas in a single layer and saute turning once untill tender about 5 minutes, sprinkle with cinnamon,pour in rum and bananna liqueur light with a long match remove from heat and baste banannas untill the flame burns out serve at once with  vanilla ice cream or whip cream  and injoy

this is so good over vanilla ice cream and so tasty , its a mouth watering
