Title:
Persian Herb and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3717905.jpg
Ingredients:
3 green onions, quartered
1 sprig basil
1 sprig tarragon
1 sprig mint (optional)
1 sprig cilantro (optional)
4 pita breads, quartered
3 ounces crumbled feta cheese
1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened

Directions:
Arrange green onions, basil, tarragon, mint, and cilantro on a serving platter next to pita. Place feta cheese and butter in separate small serving dishes.

Submitter: 
moaa
Description: 
This is a very interesting Persian starter dish or side dish. Feel free to add different veggies such as cucumbers and radishes.
