Title:
Coca-Cola Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packages of Ribs (the large package)
2 cans Coke Classic - NO SUBSTITUTES ( If using 2L. bottle of coke 3 cups = 2 cans)
1 1/2 cups Brown Sugar
6 tsp Garlic Powder
1/2 cup Soy Sauce

Directions:
Cut Ribs into strips and boil 30 mins.
Drain and wash off ribs, than return to pan.
Add Coke, Sugar and Garlic and boil 10 mins.
Add Soy Sauce, reduce heat and simmer 1 1/2 - 2 hrs till thickened.

Submitter: 
Tabby Matherly
Description: 
A very sticky rib recipe but deliciously good. A neighbor of my mum gave me this recipe.
