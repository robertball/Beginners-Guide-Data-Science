Title:
Cracker Barrel Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (2 pounds) frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
1 can (10 3/4 ounces) Cream of Mushroom soup
1 container sour cream (16 ounces)
2 cups Cheddar cheese  I use Velveeta.
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl, mix together onion, soup, sour cream, cheese, seasoning, and butter.
Put in microwave for 2 minutes.
Add hash browns and mix thoroughly.
Spread potato mixture in a greased 9 x 13 baking dish.
Bake for 45 minutes – one hour, until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Tamala Henry
Description: 
This Cracker Barrel Hash Brown Casserole is a favorite side dish with any meal. It’s super easy to whip up, but grants you restaurant quality taste right in your own kitchen.
