Title:
Raspberry Syrup for Drinks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup sugar
2/3 cup water
1 cup fresh or frozen raspberries

Directions:
Bring water and sugar to a boil in small pan, stirring occasionally. Remove pan from heat.  Add berries and mash with a potato masher or fork. Let hot mixture steep for at least 1 hour.  Once flavor reaches desired strength, drain the mixture using a fine mesh strainer. Press on berry pulp with spatula to extract the most juice.  Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
CRYSTALSHOE
Description: 
This syrup is great for flavoring ice tea, but can also be used in cocktails, soda, and other drinks.
