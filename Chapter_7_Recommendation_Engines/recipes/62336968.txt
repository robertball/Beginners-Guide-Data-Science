Title:
Low Carb Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound breakfast sausage cooked and drained
6 eggs
4 oz sharp cheddar cheese shredded (about 1 cup)
1 tsp mustard powder
3 tsp sage
1 cup sour cream
salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
cook and drain sausage and place on bottom of casserole dish
mix 6 eggs, salt and pepper, mustard powder and 1 tsp of the sage in a bowl
pour egg mixture over top of sausage
dollup 1 cup of sour cream on top
dollup cheddar cheese on top
sprinkle with remaining 2 tsp sage
bake x 30 minutes
makes 6 servings
