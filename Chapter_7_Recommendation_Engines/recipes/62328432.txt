Title:
Hot Fudge Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/407621.jpg
Ingredients:
6 ounces semi-sweet chocolate (Bakers)
1 14 ounce can sweetened condensed milk
2 Tb unsalted butter

Directions:
In a heavy sauce pan, combine condensed milk and chocolate, stir over medium-low heat until chocolate is melted and milk is well combined. Remove from heat. Add in butter and stir until melted, use right away or cover surface with plastic and cool in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
A rich hot fudge sauce that is great over ice cream, fresh strawberries or as a cake filling when cooled.
