Title:
Latin Steak Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/444213.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Lbs Skirt Steak Sliced Thinly (ask butcher to run it through the tenderizer)
Juice of 1 Lime
2 Teaspoons Latin Inspired Seasoning
1 Teaspoon Sea Salt
1/2 Teaspoon White Pepper
1/3 Cup Canola Oil

Directions:
Sprinkle latin seasoning, white pepper & salt evenly on meat.  Squirt lime juice on top of meat. Let set for about 30 minutes at room temperature.
Heat skillet with oil.  Add peices of meat. Cook each side about 7-9 minutes.  Remove and lay on paper towels to drain oil.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
Made steak tacos trying to use up my seasoning.  Serve with Poblano cream sauce on top!
