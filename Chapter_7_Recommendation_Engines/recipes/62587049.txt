Title:
Cheesy Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Monterrey Jack cheese
1 c uncooked Minute rice
8 oz sour cream
8 oz small curd cottage cheese
8oz chopped green chilis
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion salt
milk
grated cheese for topping

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except topping cheese & add enough milk to make the mixture slightly soupy. Pour into a buttered 2 quart casserole dish. Top with 1 inch of grated cheese. Bake uncovered for 35 min at 350

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is a yummy comfort food style rice side dish that a lady in our church made for every potluck when I was growing up.
