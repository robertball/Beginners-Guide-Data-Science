Title:
Crock Pot Classic Chili (KC likes) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2-1 lb. ground beef
2 onions, chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped or use jarred minced garlic
2 (14 oz.) cans diced tomatoes, undrained
15 oz. can tomato sauce (could add another can)
1 Tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. dried oregano or 1/4 tsp. ground oregano
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1-2 (15 oz.) cans red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
For a thinner chili or less spicy chili add 1-2 cups of water.

Directions:
Saut√© ground beef in heavy skillet with onions until the meat is brown, stirring to break up meat. Drain well. Mix all ingredients in 4 quart slow cooker. For thinner or less spicy chili add more water.
Cover and cook on low heat for 6-7 hours until vegetables are tender
