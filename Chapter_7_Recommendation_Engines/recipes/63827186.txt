Title:
Teriyaki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbl cooking oil
1/2 inch cube of ginger, minced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup plus 2 tbl soy sauce
1 cup water
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
2 tablespoon honey
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1/4 cup cold water

Directions:
Add oil to saucepan and heat on medium heat for 2 minutes.
Add garlic and ginger to pan, and cook for 30 seconds. Do not let it burn.
Add all but cornstarch and 1/4 cup water to the sauce pan, stirring occasionally.
Mix cornstarch and cold water in a cup and dissolve. Add to sauce in pan, stirring consistently.
Simmer for 3 minutes on low heat.
Add water to thin if it is overly thick.
