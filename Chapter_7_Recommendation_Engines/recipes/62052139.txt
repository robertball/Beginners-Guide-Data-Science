Title:
Italian Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ziti
non stick cooking spray
1 onion chopped
4 garlic cloves chopped
1(14-1/2oz.) can diced tomatoes
1(14oz.) jar spaghetti sauce
1/3 cup fresh italian parsley chopped
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 (15oz.) container ricotta cheese
3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese divided
1/4 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese divided
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Cook ziti and drain. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Spray 3 quart casserole with spray. Spray skillet with cooking spray saute onion and garlic for 5 minutes.Stir in tomatoes, sauce, seasonings, parsley and salt and pepper set aside. Place ricotta, 2 cups mozzarella and 2 tablespoons parmesan in blender or food processor blend till smooth.Mix together pasta, sauce and cheese mixture. Place in baking dish Top with remaining 1 cup mozzarella and 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese. Bake covered for 15 minutes and uncovered for 10 more minutes.
