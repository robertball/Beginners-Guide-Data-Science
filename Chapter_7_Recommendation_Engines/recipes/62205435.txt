Title:
Chocolate Covered Peppermint Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. mashed potatoes
1 tsp. salt
2 tbsp. melted butter
2tsp. peppermint extract
8 c. confectioners sugar
8( 1 oz) squares semisweet chocolate
2 Tbsp. shortening

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together potatoes, salt, butter and peppermint extract. Gradually mix in confectioers sugar until you have a workable dough. Knead slightly, and roll into cherry sized balls. Flatten balls to form patties. Arrange on sheets of waked paper, and allow to dry overnight. Place chocolate in a microwave safe bowl. Heat in microwave, stirring ocassionally, until melted and smooth. Dip patties in melted chocolate and let cool on waxed paper.
