Title:
Oriental Stir Fry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2290540.jpg
Ingredients:
¾ cup low sodium vegetable broth
3 tbs low sodium soy sauce
1 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tbsp blackstrap molasses
1 tbsp chopped garlic (2-3 cloves)
½ tsp powdered ginger (or about 1 tbsp of chopped fresh)
1 tbsp corn starch
1 tbsp water

Directions:
Whisk all the ingredients except corn starch and water over med heat until it starts to bubble.
Mix the corn starch and 1 tbsp water in a separate cup and slowly pour it into the sauce.
It will start to thicken immediately.
Leave it on the heat and whisk continuously until you get the thickness you desire.
