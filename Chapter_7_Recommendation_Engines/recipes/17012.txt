Title:
Light and Fresh Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1889433.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups vegetable oil
1 cup white sugar
1 cup white wine vinegar
4 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 teaspoons dry mustard
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 teaspoons sesame seeds

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the oil, sugar, vinegar, salt, pepper, mayonnaise, dry mustard, onion, garlic and sesame seeds. Mix together well and pour enough over salad to coat.

Submitter: 
Shelly A.
Description: 
This is a refreshing and light salad dressing.  Very yummy!
