Title:
Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
rack baby back ribs
Sweet Baby Rays bbq sauce or your favorite
stock of choice:
we prefer beer and water
try sprite, coke, beef stock, juice

Directions:
cut ribs into singles
place in dutch oven or stock pot
cover with stock
if in dutch oven:
place in oven on 350 degrees x 2-3 hours
if in stock pot:
bring to boil
reduce and simmer
cook until tender
drain
cover wtih bbq sauce
stir
cook until sauce hot
serve

Submitter: 
Michele Weyland
Description: 
Ribs are difficult for me to ge right and this is fool proof
