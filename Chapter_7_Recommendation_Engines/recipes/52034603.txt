Title:
Cream Filled Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients are for filling only:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup shortening
2 cups confectioners' sugar
1 pinch salt
3 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Bake chocolate cupcakes using recipe of choice. Let cool completely. Using a pairing knife, cut straight down through the center of each cake twice so that you make an X.
To make filling, in a large bowl, beat butter and shortening together until smooth. Blend in confectioners' sugar and pinch of salt. Gradually beat in 3 tablespoons milk and 1 teaspoon vanilla. beat until light and fluffy.
Fill a pastry bag.  Push tip through the X on the cakes and pipe in the filling until cake starts to plump.
Frost cupcakes with chocolate frosting.
