Title:
Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 3 pounds potatoes, peeled and diced 1/2x1 in. (weigh 3 pounds after peeling)
* 1 cup chopped celery
* 2 1/2 quarts water
* 4 or so chicken bouillon cubes (add more to taste)
* 1/2 teaspoon pepper
* salt to taste
* 1 1/2 quarts milk
* 4 ounces melted margarine
* Parsley flakes (to taste)
* 1 cup flour

Directions:
1) In a large pot put first 7 ingredients in large pot and simmer 20 minutes.
2) Add milk and bring to 170 degrees F.
3) In bowl blend melted margarine and flour till smooth then add 1 quart of soup broth and blend. Using wire whip add back to pot and blend well. Simmer 20 minutes

Description: 
A great soup for a cold winter day.
