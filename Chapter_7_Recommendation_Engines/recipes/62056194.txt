Title:
Roasted Garlic and Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of garlic, broken into individual cloves
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp sherry
8 oz. mild, lean sausage
4 potatoes, peeled and diced
4 chicken boullion cubes
4 cps water
2 cps heavey cream
1/8 tsp white pepper
1/4 cp instant potato flakes (optional thickening)
chopped chives
cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Place garlic cloves in small, covered, oven proof dish.  Drizzle olive oil and sherry over all.  Roast til cloves are soft when poked with a toothpick.  Approx. 30 to 35 minutes.  Set aside to cool.
Meanwhile, brown sausage in a large pot. Drain fat if needed.  Add potatoes, water, and boullion.  Simmer until potatoes are soft.  Add cream, white pepper, and potato flakes. When garlic is cool enough to touch, peel each clove and drop into soup.  Simmer about 4 minutes longer.
Garnish with chopped chives and serve.

Submitter: 
Beth
Description: 
Rich, simple, satisfying soup for a cold night.  My husband and sons like to add cayenne pepper to add some punch.  I like the flavor of the garlic without the cloak of spice.
