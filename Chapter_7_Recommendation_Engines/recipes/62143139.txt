Title:
Oh Ho Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bar layer:
1 (18 oz) box devil's food cake mix
eggs (as needed for cake mix)
oil (as needed for cake mix)
Filling:
1 & 1/2 cup milk
7 Tablespoons flour
1 stick (1/2 c) margarine or butter, softened
1 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
Frosting:
1 cup sugar
1/2 c milk
1 stick (1/2 c) margarine or butter, melted
1 c (6 oz) semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat over as recommended on cake mix box.
Lightly spray two 9X13 pans with cooking spray.
Prepare cake mix according to package directions, then pour into prepared pans.
Bake for about 2/3 of time recommended for 9X13 inch pan, or until toothpick inserted in middle of cake comes out clean.
Cool cake completely.
Filling:
In a bowl, mix milk and flour together, microwave on High for one minute, stir with a wire whip, microwave one more minute, stir, microwave another minute, stir.
When the mixture is the consistency of thick glue, cool.
Cream together the margarine or butter, shortening and sugar.
Add vanilla and cooled milk-flour mixture.
Beat at high speed for seven minutes.
Spread over cooled cake.
Refrigerate one hour.
Frosting:
Combine sugar, milk and margarine or butter.
Microwave on High five minutes.
Do not stir during microwaving.
Add chocolate chips and stir until chips are completely melted and mixture is smooth.
Pour over filling.
Keep refrigerated.

Submitter: 
GBM
Description: 
Tastes exactly like the chocolate/cream filling snack cakes.  Recipe makes a lot - two 9X13 pans of bars - perfect for parties, holiday gatherings or bake sales.  Bars freeze rather well so you can enjoy one pan now and save one for later.
