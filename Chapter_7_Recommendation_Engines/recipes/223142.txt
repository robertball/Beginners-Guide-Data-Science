Title:
Breakfast-to-Go Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1111868.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fat-free milk
1/2 cup frozen blueberries
1/2 cup plain fat-free Greek yogurt
1/4 cup old-fashioned oats
1 tablespoon ground flax seed

Directions:
Blend milk, blueberries, Greek yogurt, oats, and flax seed in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
christophermynhier
Description: 
This is a smoothie for a hearty breakfast on the go.
