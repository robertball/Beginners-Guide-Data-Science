Title:
Veggie, Fruit, and Nut Nutritious Green Smoothie! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 sliced ripe banana
1 cup shredded carrots
1/2 cup sliced raw beets
1 1/2 cups baby spinach leaves
1/4 cup cottage cheese
2 tbsp greek yogurt
1/2 cup milk [cow, almond, coconut, etc]
1 tbsp honey or 2 tbsp applesauce
1/4 cup walnut halves
1/4 cup whole almonds
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 chopped pear

Directions:
Blend. Chill if desired. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Amna Kazmi
Description: 
Spinach, beets, carrots, nuts, fruits ... anything your heart desires! Tastes healthy but delicious, and a protein-packed drink that packs a wallop! Great for starting your day or workout.
