Title:
Chex Caramel Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cinnamon chex
3 cups rice chex
2 cups cinnamon cheerios
1 cup honey roasted peanuts
1/4 cup butter
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 tbs light corn syrup
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Put cereal and peanuts in a large microwaveable bowl
Microwave butter, brown sugar, corn syrup and vanilla uncovered on high about 2 min, stirring after 1 min.. until mixture is boiling. Pour over cereal and stirr until coated.  Microwave, uncovered for 5-6 min stirring an scraping bowl every min, Spread on wax paper to cool. Store in air tight container.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
Tastes like caramel corn with out the corn.
