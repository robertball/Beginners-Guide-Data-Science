Title:
Cajun Red Snapper with Red Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10 oz red snapper fillets
2 tsp creole or cajun seasoning
1 14.8 oz pouch cooked long grain rice
1 15 oz can red beans, rinsed and drained
2 lemons
2 tbs butter, melted

Directions:
Rinse fish; pat dry.  Cut each fillet in half crosswise.  Sprinkle snapper with 1 tsp of the seasoning.  Heat 12 inch heavy nonstick skillet over medium high heat.  Add fish, skin side up; cook 4 minutes; turn.  Cook 2-4 minutes more or until fish flakes when tested with fork.
Meanwhile, in large microwave-safe bowl, combine rice, beans, and remaining 1 tsp seasoning.  Cover and micro-cook on high for 3 to 3 1/2 minutes or until heated through, stirring twice.
Finely shred 2 tsp peel from one lemon; cut other lemon into wedges.
Serve fish with rice and beans.  Drizzle melted butter over top; sprinkle lemon peel and parself.  Pass lemon wedges.
