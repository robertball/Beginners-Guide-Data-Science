Title:
Savory Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fine dry bread crumbs
2 t. instant onion
2 cups milk
2 carrots, peeled
2 lbs ground beef
2 eggs
2 T. chili sauce
2 t. salt (opt.)
Pepper to taste
1 t. curry powder
1 t. ginger
2 t. anchovy paste (opt.)
Prepared mustard

Directions:
Mix 2/3 cup crumbs, onion, and milk in bowl; let stand a few minutes.
Finely shred carrots and add to the mixture, with remaining ingredients.
Mix well.
Shape into loaf in shallow baking dish.
Spread a thin layer of mustard over loaf and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 1 hour, or until done.
Can be thinly sliced (when cold) for sandwiches.

Submitter: 
LawrenceKansasPerson
Description: 
With a tang of mustard, a whiff of curry, and the interesting addition of shredded carrots, this meatloaf takes you to a very different place than the usual ketchup-topped loaf.
