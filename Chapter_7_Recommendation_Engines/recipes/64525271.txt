Title:
Banana Oreo crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Plastic bag (zip lock)
2 bananas
4 Oreo's
3 ts grape nuts cereal (optional)

Directions:
-First take out a plate and cut the banana into slices
-Arrange the slices on the plate in any style you want
-Open up the Oreo's and take out the icing
-Take the icing and roll them into small balls
-Take the Wafer part of the Oreo and put it into the baggie
-Slam the baggie against something hard until the wafer part is crushed
-Drizzle the wafer crumbs on top of the banana slices
-Place the icing balls on top of the banana slices
-Serve and enjoy!
