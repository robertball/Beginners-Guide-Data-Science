Title:
Pumpkin Shortbread Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter, softened
2/3 cup granulated sugar
3/4 tsp. vanilla extract
2 1/3 cup flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 cup solid canned pumpkin
1/2 cup chopped pecans (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Cream 1/2 cup butter, 1/3 cup granulated sugar and 1/4 tsp. vanilla. Add 1 cup flour, mix well. Press this dough mixture into bottom of 13 by 9 inch pan. Bake 10 min. Reduce heat to 350.
Combine 1/3 cup flour, baking powder, and salt. mix well. Combine eggs, brown sugar, pumpkin, and 1/2 tsp. vanilla. stir in dry ingredients and nuts. Spread over partially baked crust. Combine remaining 1 cup flour and 1/3 cup granulated sugar. Cut in 1/4 cup butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle over pumpkin layer. Continue baking 25-30 minutes. Cool. Cut into bars.
