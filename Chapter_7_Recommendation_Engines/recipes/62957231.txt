Title:
Tacos Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
shredded or diced meat
corn tortilla
taco cheese or cheddar
cilantro
tomatoes
lettuce
pickled onions and peppers

Directions:
chop or slice the vegies
warm the corn (yummy if roasted)
brown corn tortillas in a cast iron skillet
warm the meat
melt cheese on taco
build the tacos
