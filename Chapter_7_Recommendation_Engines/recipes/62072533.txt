Title:
Mexican Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. tub cream cheese
1 package dry ranch dressing
1 small can chopped green chilies, drain well
1 small can chopped black olives, drain well
1 medium jar chopped pimentos, drain well
6 inch flour tortillas
artichoke hearts, chopped
sunflower seeds

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and ranch dressing; refrigerate overnight.  Add chilies, olives, and pimentos; mix well.  Spread mixture on a tortilla. Sprinkle with artichoke hearts and sunflower seeds.  Cover mixture with another tortilla; cut into 8 wedges.  Repeat with the rest of the tortillas.

Submitter:

Description: 
My 7 year old son begs me to make these.  They are easy to make and different.
