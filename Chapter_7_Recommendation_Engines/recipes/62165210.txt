Title:
Crispy Seasoned Baked Potato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Medium Baking Potatoes
2 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil
1 Teaspoon Course Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Dried Parsley
1/4 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1/4 Teaspoon Onion Powder
1/4 Teaspoon Paprika
1/4 Teaspoon Black Pepper

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 400 degrees.
Pour oil into gallon bag.
Measure salt, parsley, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika and black pepper onto a plate.
Pierce each potato a few times with a fork.  Coat each potato with oil by placing in the bag and rubbing the potato around.
Remove potatoes from bag and coat with seasoning.  Rub all over potato to coat evenly.
Wrap potato in aluminum foil.
Bake for 1 - 2 hours in preheated oven or until potatoes feel tender when you squeeze them.
Remove from oven and split with a knife.  Add your favorite toppings.
