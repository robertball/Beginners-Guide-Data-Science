Title:
Apple Crumb Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c.sugar
2 Tbsp. flour
3/4 tsp.ground cinnamon
6-8 macontosh apples
one 9 inch unbaked pastry shell----Crumb Topping -
1/2 c.flour
1/4 c.sugar
1/4 c.SOFTENED butter

Directions:
combine first half of ingreadients, sugar,flour,cinnamon w/peeled thinly sliced apples and mix well. Put into pie shell. Combine topping ingredients and fluff up good with fork till you have a crumbly consistancy.Place on top of pie. Cook for 50 minutes on 400 degrees.Please check to make sure crumb topping is not getting too brown. Cover loosely with foil if this occurs.
