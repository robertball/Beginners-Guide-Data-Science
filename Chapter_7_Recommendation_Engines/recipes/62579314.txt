Title:
Asian Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/966596.jpg
Ingredients:
Sauce:
3/4Cup hoisen sauce
1 1/2 tsp rice vinegar
1 Tbsp honey
2 Tbsp water
1 Tbsp lime juice
2 tsp sesame oil
1 lrg garlic clove minced
1 tsp grated ginger
Wrap:
6 cooked chicken breasts, cubed
1 Cup grated carrots
2 green onions, chopped
2 Cups steam fried noodles
1/2 red pepper sliced
handful of sprouts, washed
2 Tbsp chopped peanuts
(Vegetarian Version):
85g deep fried tofu
Head of Iceburg lettuce, washed and leaves taken off carfully

Directions:
In sauce pan, combine/stir sauce ingredients until warm.
In large bowl, mix together wrap ingredients and toss with sauce.
Place mix into each lettuce leaf, wrap and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
Soooo delicious - do with chicken or tofu for vegetarian version
