Title:
Crispy Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter or margarine
3/4 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup oatmeal
3/4 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
2 tsp cinnamon or ginger
2 tbs milk
3 tbs maple syrup

Directions:
melt butter and place in bowl. Stir in sugar and oatmeal. Sift together flour and baking powder. Add to butter mixture. Stir in cinnamon, milk and syrup. With a teaspoon, drop small droplet of batter, about 3 inches apart, onto a lightly greased baking dish. Bake at 350 for 8 minutes or until cookies are thin, crispy and lightly browned around the edges. Remove cookies immediately. If you like cookies slightly rounded, place each one over a wooden spoon handle while still warm.

Submitter: 
Ursula
Description: 
Serve 2 cookies with a scoop of ice cream between them, or dip ends cookies in melted chocolate.
