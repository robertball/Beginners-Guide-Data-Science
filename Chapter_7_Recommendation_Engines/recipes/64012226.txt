Title:
Johnnycake Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1114080.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C. All purpose flour
1 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1/2 C. Cornmeal
1/4 tsp. Salt
2 C. Water
1/4 C. Butter
1/2 C. Light cream
1 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs, beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 12 cup muffin pan. Set aside
In a small saucepan over medium heat cook cornmeal and stir until nicely thickened. Remove from heat
When cornmeal has cooled place in frying pan with butter and cook over medium heat until butter is dissolved and cornmeal is of pudding like consistency
In a large mixing bowl place cornmeal, sugar, beaten eggs and light cream.  Mix all ingredients together and blend until nice and smooth
Add dry ingredients to mixture and stir to just combine together
Pour muffin batter into greased muffin cups filling 2/3 full
Bake in oven for 20 minutes until lightly browned on top
Remove from oven and allow to cool before placing them on serving plate

Submitter:

Description: 
A very light muffin with the goodness of cornmeal for those who like Johnnycake for their dessert
