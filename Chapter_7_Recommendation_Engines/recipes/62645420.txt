Title:
Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1/2 cup brown sugar packed
1 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup skim milk
1 1/3 cups mashed bananas
1/3 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Combine the first five ingredients in a large bowl.  In another bowl, combine the eggs and milk and stir in bananas, applesauce and vanilla.  Stir this mixture into dry ingredients until just moistened.  Spray muffin tin fill each about three quarters full.  Bake 375 degrees for about 20 minutes.
