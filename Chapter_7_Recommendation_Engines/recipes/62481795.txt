Title:
Easy Chicken Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 fryer, boiled and deboned
5 eggs, boiled and sliced / or mashed
3 cups chicken broth
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 stick of margarine
1 cup self rising flour
1 cup milk
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Pre-heat your oven to 425 degrees. Place de-boned chicken into a 9x13 pan. Sprinkle sliced egg over the chicken. Bring the broth and cream of chicken soup to a boil. Add pepper. Pour over mixture. Melt margarine. Add to flour and milk. Pour these ingredients over the chicken, eggs, and broth. Bake for 30 minutes.
