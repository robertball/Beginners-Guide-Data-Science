Title:
Uncooked Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup milk
3 tbsp cocoa
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/3 peanut butter
dash salt
1 tsp vanilla
3 1/2 cup oatmeal
M&Ms; (optional)

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, milk, cocoa and butter. Cook over medium heat. Bring to a boil and allow to boil for 1 minute (only).
Remove from heat. Add at once peanut butter, salt, vanilla. Stir in oatmeal until well mixed.
Drop by tsp. fulls onto wax paper. Press in M&M's before they cool if desired. Let stand until set.
