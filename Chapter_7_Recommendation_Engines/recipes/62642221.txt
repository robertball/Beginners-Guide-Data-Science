Title:
Individual Baked Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 slice bacon
1 teaspoon melted butter
1 egg
1/4 slice Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350&#0176;
Cook bacon in microwave on paper plate, covered with paper towels until browned, but still flexible,about 1 minute.
Wrap bacon slice around the inside of a muffin cup. Place a teaspoon of butter (or bacon grease) in the bottom of muffin cup.
Drop in egg.
Bake in preheated 350&#0176;oven for 10 to 15 minutes.
Place 1/4 slice of cheese over egg, and continue cooking until cheese is melted and egg is cooked.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
A very easy idea: Individual Baked Eggs surrounded by a strip of bacon, and topped with a square of cheese.  Great not having splatters all over, and being able to do 12 eggs at one time in a muffin tin.Modified from: Individual Baked Eggs
