Title:
Mahi Mahi Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds of mahi mahi fillets
2 ready to use deep dish pie crusts
1 10 oz. can condensed cream of potato soup
1/2 cup milk
1 can mixed vegetables (with potatoes)
salt and pepper to taste
1 t. thyme
1 T. vegetable oil

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Celsius.
2. Place mahi mahi skin side down in skillet with oil.  Season with thyme, salt and pepper and cook over medium heat.  Cover for 7 min.  Flip and cook for additional 5-7 min.  Fish should be throughly cooked.
3. In a saucepan mix soup, salt, pepper, milk and mixed vegetables while on medium heat.  Let simmer and stir occasionally for about 5 minutes.
4.  Cut mahi mahi into edible pieces picking out any visible bones.
5.  Place fish in soup mixture and gently mix.
6.  Place mixture in a deep dish pie crust.  Place the other pie crust on top, sealing the edges with wet fingertips.
7.  Place in oven for 40-45 min.  Let cool for 10-15 min.  Enjoy and watch out for any bones.
