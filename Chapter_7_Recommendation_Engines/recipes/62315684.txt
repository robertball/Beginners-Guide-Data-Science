Title:
Dad's Magic Bar Olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
21 oz jar of Queen size Green Olives (about 60 olives)
4 oz cream cheese
1 cup of finely grated pepper jack
2 eggs
1 TBS water
1 tsp Tabasco sauce
1- 1 1/2 cups bread crumbs
2 TBS flour
1 tsp black pepper
Oil for frying

Directions:
Drain olives and remove pimentos with a sharp knife.
Mix together cream cheese, pepper jack and Tabasco sauce.
Transfer mixture to a resealable bag or piping bag and fill all olives.
Freeze olives for 30 minutes.
Mix together egg and water. Set aside.
Mix together 1 cup of bread crumbs, flour and black pepper.
Dip each olive in egg then bread crumb mixture.
Freeze for 30 minutes.
Repeat dipping process (may need to use additional bread crumbs).
Store in the freezer.
Fry in oil of your choice until browned.
Drain and cool for about 5 minutes before eating.

Submitter: 
Kathleen
Description: 
Yummy, oozing, fried green olives! A very unique appetizer that is great to have on hand in the freezer for impressing unexpected guests!
