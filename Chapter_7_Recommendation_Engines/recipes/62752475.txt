Title:
Grape-free Haroset 1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/634779.jpg
Ingredients:
A handful of dried cranberries
Ground pecans
Dried apple rings
Blackberry Manischewitz
Honey

Directions:
Just like any haroset: put it all in the food processor and process it until it turns into a chunky paste.

Submitter: 
Meir Hoberman
Description: 
This is the first of three grape-free haroset recipes my fiancee devised this year.  All quantities are approximate: please feel free to experiment and adjust things to fit the tastes of your family!
