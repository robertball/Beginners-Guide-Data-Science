Title:
Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10lb. cans pears or mixed fruit
36 oz. lime jello mix
48 oz. cream cheese, room temperature
4 C cream or milk
6 C whipped topping

Directions:
Drain juice from fruit, reserving 6 cups of the liquid.  Bring the reserved juice to a boil. Remove from heat. Add jello mix and stir until dissolved.  Let cool.
Mix room temperature cream cheese and milk or cream until blended.  Fold in the fruit and whipped topping.  Place in a 4" hotel pan and chill overnight.
