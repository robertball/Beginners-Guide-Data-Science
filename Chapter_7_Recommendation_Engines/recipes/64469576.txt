Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz bag white chocolate chips
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
3 1/2 cups rice crispies
1/4 cup mini chocolate chips
1 1/2 cups mini marshmellows

Directions:
-Melt chocolate chips with peanut butter
-Move to bowl & mix with rice crispies
-Wait until it reaches room temperature
-Mix WELL.
-Grease 9x13 pan
-Press with lightly greased spoon
-Refrigerate for 30 minutes
-Cut when hardened
