Title:
Lemon Dill Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/672584.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups mayonnaise
1/2 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup dried dill weed to taste
1/2 cup buttermilk

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, lemon juice, dill and buttermilk.  Chill until serving.

Submitter: 
COBRA
Description: 
This creamy mayonnaise and buttermilk based dressing is perked up by tangy lemon juice and plenty of dill.
