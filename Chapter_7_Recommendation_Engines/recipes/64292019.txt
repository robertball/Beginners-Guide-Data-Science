Title:
Strawberry Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1934782.jpg
Ingredients:
Premaid crepes
1 Container of fresh strawberries
Cornstarch
1 small container Whipping cream
3 Chocolate candy wafers
Powdered sugar
Sliced almonds

Directions:
Chop all but 8 strawberries and put in a saucepan on the stove. Bring to a boil and lower to simmer for 10 minutes. Mix 2 tsp of cornstarch in cold water and pour into strawberries.
After it thickens remove and let sit at room temperature. Refrigerate.  Prepare whipping cream and refrigerate. Using prepared crepes, fill them with strawberry filling and whipping cream. Fold over and drizzle melted chocolate over the crepes.  Sprinkle with sifted sugar and sprinkle with almonds.  Place a Dollop of cream on top. Slice whole strawberries in 4 or 5 slits and fan it open as a garnish
