Title:
Chicken Etti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•1 pound spaghetti noodles
•3 cups diced, cooked chicken
•1/4 cup chopped green pepper
•1 cup chicken broth
•1 pound Velveeta
•8 oz. can diced tomatoes & green chilies
•1 Small Onion - Chopped
•8 oz. can cream of chicken soup

Directions:
Boil spaghetti noodles, drain, and put them into a large bowl. Add cooked chicken and chicken broth.
Add chicken, green pepper, onion, and broth to noodles.
In a smaller bowl, combine the cheese, diced tomatoes & green chilies, and cream of chicken soup. Stir, and melt mixture in the microwave.
Combine the cheese mixture with the spaghetti. Spread into a greased casserole dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jerimy Wallace
Description: 
Cheesy Chicken Spaghetti much like what was seen on the TV show 19 Kids and Counting. After making it I decided to add some onion. It's a winner!
