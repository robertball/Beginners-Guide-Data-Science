Title:
Oven Baked French Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions:
place potatoes in a large resealable bag.
ombine the parmesan cheese and seasonings. add to potatoes and shake to coat.
pour oil into a baking dish. pour potatoes into pans.
bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees farenheit for 20-30 minutes or until tender.
