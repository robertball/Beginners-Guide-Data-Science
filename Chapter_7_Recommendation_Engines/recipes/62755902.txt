Title:
Chicken Marbella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 C white wine
2 Tbsp brown sugar
1-1/2 tsp oregano
3 Tbsp red wine vinegar
S and P
6 cloves garlic, smashed
1 Tbsp capers
1/2 C prunes
1/3 C green olives
4 small chicken legs, split, skin removed
1/4 C fresh parsley, chopped
1 C long grain white rice

Directions:
In a 5- to 6- quart slow cooker, whisk together wine, brown sugar, oregano, 2 Tbsp vinegar, and 1/4 tsp each of S and P. Add the garlic, capers, and olives and mix to combine.
Add the chicken, nestling it among the olives and prunes. Cover and cook until the meat is tender and cooked through, on low for 5 to 6 hours or on high 3 to 4 hours; gently stir in the remaining Tbsp vinegar and parsley.
30 min before serving, cook the rice. Serve the chicken, prunes, olives and cooking liquid over rice
