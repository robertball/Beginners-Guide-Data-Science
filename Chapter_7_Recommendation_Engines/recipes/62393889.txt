Title:
Pina Colada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cream of coconut
2 cups of white rum
3 cups of unsweetened pineapple juice (canned)
3 to 4 cups of ice

Directions:
Bring all ingredients into a blender.
Blend at high speed for about 2 minutes.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Good
Description: 
Simple pina colada recipe.  (for virgin version, exclude the rum and add 1/2 cup of sugar)
