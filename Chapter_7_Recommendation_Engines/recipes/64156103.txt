Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar salsa (650 ml)
1 pkg taco seasoning - low sodium (35 gm)
900 gm boneless chicken breasts
24 taco shells
4 cups shredded lettuce
2 cups finely diced tomatos - roma
2 cups shredded tex-mex cheese

Directions:
Mix salsa and taco mix in a slow cooker until blended
top with chicken and cover with lid
cook on low for 6 to 8 hours
remove chicken from slow cooker reserving the sauce
Shred chicken with a fork
Return to slow cooker
Stir until chicken is coated with sauce
Fill taco shells with chicken and top with lettuce and cheese
