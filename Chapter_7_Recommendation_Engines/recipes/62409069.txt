Title:
Dr. Fisel's Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, chopped
1 T olive oil
2 cups vegetable stock
2 cups butternut squash (seeds removed), peeled and diced
2 cups sweet potatoes or yams, peeled and diced
1 apple, cored and diced
1 tsp freshly grated ginger
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
In large soup pot, saute the onions in oil on medium-high heat until translucent.
Add all other ingredients and bring to a boil.
Turn down the heat and simmer 30 minutes
Remove 2 ladles of vegetables and 1 ladle of stock; puree them together in a blender or food processor until smooth. Return to soup pot; stir soup before serving.
