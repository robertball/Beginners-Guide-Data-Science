Title:
Coconut Mojitos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 sprigs of mint leaves
1/2 lime, cut into four pieces
1 tea light brown sugar
1.5 oz white rum
1.5 oz coconut rum
2 oz.  cream of coconut
Ice
Seltzer or club soda (I used Corona Tropical Lime Seltzer)

Directions:
1.  Add mint leaves, limes wedges and light brown sugar to a cocktail shaker.  Muddle and twist.
2.  Add ice
3.  Add rums and cream of coconut.
4.  Shake vigorously for 30 seconds.
5.  Add seltzer/club soda and stir.
6.  Strain into ice-filled glass.  Garnish with additional lime and/or mint.
