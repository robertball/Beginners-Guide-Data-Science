Title:
Easy Tasty Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
1/2 cup water
1 tablespoon chilli powder
1 tablespoon paprika
1 tablespoon cumin
2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon cornstarch
1 teaspoon hot sauce

Directions:
Brown ground beef in pan over medium-high heat.
Drain grease.
Return pan to heat, add all spices and water.
Stir and reduce heat to Medium-Low.
Add beans and heat until mixture is warm throughout.
Serve in tortillas or taco shells with shredded cheese, salsa, sour cream and guacamole.

Submitter: 
Lin Snow
Description: 
A quick, delicious, and healthful meal that comes together in no time! A family favourite.
