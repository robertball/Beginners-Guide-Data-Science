Title:
Chili Seasoning Mix II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/805892.jpg
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons chili powder
1/2 tablespoon crushed red pepper
1/2 tablespoon onion powder
1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoons white sugar
2 teaspoons ground cumin
2 teaspoons paprika
2 teaspoons oregano. dried
2 teaspoons dried parsley
2 teaspoons Lawry's seasoning salt
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, stir together all ingredients, add to 1 - 1/2 lbs ground beef or turkey.  Store in an airtight container.
