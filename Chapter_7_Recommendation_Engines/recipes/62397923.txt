Title:
Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head of broccoli florets, washed and cut
1 small onion, minced
1/2 bag shredded cheese
10-12 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
Dressing:
1/c mayo
1/4 c sugar
1 Tblspoon vinegar

Directions:
add broccoli, onion, bacon, and cheese to bowl.
In a separate bowl, add mayo, sugar and vinegar together and blend.
Add dressing to salad, tossing to coat evenly.
Cover and refrigerate until serving.

quick and easy broccoli salad for cookouts and parties.
