Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs ground beef
2 large onions
1/2 t pepper
2 T brown sugar
2 T mustard
1 can tomato soup
2 c ketchup
4 T flour (if needed)

Directions:
1.  Brown the meat with the onion.
2.   Add the rest of the ingredients and continue to cook until everything ie heated throughly.
3.  Serve on buns, hot dogs, or nachos.
