Title:
Crockpot Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/998850.jpg
Ingredients:
- 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
- 16oz salsa (fresh or from jar)
- 1 can black beans, with liquid
- 1 can corn, drained

Directions:
Pour half the salsa in the bottom of crockpot;
Add chicken (place in a row along the bottom);
Add the rest of the salsa;
Add can of black beans with liquid;
Add can of corn after draining liquid;
Cook on low for 5-6 hours;
A half hour before serving, remove chicken breasts one at a time and shred on plate and return to crockpot;
Stir chicken and cook covered 15-30 minutes longer;
Serve over rice or alone! Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
EASY & HEALTHY Salsa Chicken! This simple meal is low fat, packed with protein, and too easy not to try. The crock pot does all the cooking for you!
