Title:
Agua Fresca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cups Seedless Watermelon -cubed
1/2 Cup Water
24 Mint Leaves
8 T. sugar (you may adjust this if the watermelon is very sweet)
4 Slices of Lime cut in half (moon shaped)
Ice

Directions:
Add the watermelon and water to the blender until smooth.  Divide the lime and mint between 8 glasses and muddle ingredients, add ice over top and pour the watermelon blend on top.  Stir and serve.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is a drink served throughout Mexico.  It is made with several different fruit.  This is a watermelon drink.
