Title:
Omelette Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen hash browns
1 cup diced bell peppers
1 cup chopped mushrooms
1 cup chopped ham
12 eggs, beaten
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Put hash browns into a cassarole dish.
Put bell peppers, mushrooms, and ham on the top of the hashbrowns.
Pour eggs over the mushrooms, peppers, and ham.
Put cheese on top.
Cover with foil and bake at 350 for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jamie Willhight
Description: 
An incredibly easy breakfast dish that is sure to please everyone! This is a great base dish.
