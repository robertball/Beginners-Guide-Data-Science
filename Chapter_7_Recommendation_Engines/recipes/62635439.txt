Title:
Pico de Gallo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
1/2  onion, minced
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
1  lime, juiced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 pinch garlic powder
1 pinch ground cumin
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Stir the tomatoes, onion, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, lime juice, garlic, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. Refrigerate at least 3 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Rick Falsetta
Description: 
This quick and easy pico de gallo is as an appetizer or addition to dinner.Modified from: Pico de Gallo
