Title:
Madras Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packs Tasty Bites Madras Lentils
1 tbsp cooking oil
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green peppers
1/2 lb ground turkey, cooked and crumbled (ensure turkey is thoroughly cooked)
1 cup canned, diced or fresh, diced tomatoes
dash of hot sauce or red pepper flakes or cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
1. Heat oil in saucepan, add chopped onions and cook till soft and translucent.
2. Add green pepper, continue cooking until soft.
3. Add 2 packs Tasty Bite Madras Lentils
4. Add ground turkey, mix in well. *
5. Add dash of hot sauce or red pepper flakes or cayenne pepper.
6. Simmer for 5 â€“ 10 minutes.
7. Serve with taco chips. Or over rice. Or, add shredded cheese and eat as is.
*for vegetarian version, omit turkey and use 3 packs Madras Lentils.
