Title:
Buttermilk Buscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups self-rising flour
1/3 cup butter
3/4 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Cut butter into flour.
Add buttermilk and mix until moist.
Lightly knead dough on a sheet of wax paper.
Place another sheet of wax paper over this dough and roll out to thickness of 1/2 to 3/4 inch.
Cut dough with glass rim of a drinking glass.
Put a piece of foil onto a baking sheet. Place dough rounds on baking sheet. Bake at 425 for 12 minutes.
Brush with butter and honey.
