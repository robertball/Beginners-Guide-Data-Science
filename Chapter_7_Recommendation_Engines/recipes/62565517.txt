Title:
Russian Tea Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/540003.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C. soft butter
1  C. confectioners’ sugar, divided
1 tsp. vanilla
2 ½ C. sifted all-purpose flour
½ tsp. salt
¾ C. finely chopped walnuts

Directions:
Cream the butter and 1/2 C. confectioners' sugar.  Add in the vanilla.
Sift the flour & salt together and stir into the butter mixture and mix very well.  Mix in the nuts.
Chill the dough.  Roll into 1 inch balls.  Place 2 ½” apart on an ungreased baking sheet.  Bake until set, but not brown, in at 350º oven for 10 to 12 minutes.
While warm, roll in confectioners’ sugar.  Cool.  Roll in confectioners’ sugar once more.  Makes 4 dozen.

Submitter: 
Deb C
Description: 
My daughter's absolute favorite cookie.  Little bites of flaky, scrumptious, melt in your mouth goodness.  For best results, chill the dough before rolling into one inch balls.
