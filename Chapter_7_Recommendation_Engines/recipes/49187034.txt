Title:
Spicy Mexican Salad Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package chopped romaine lettuce
8 oz low fat shredded Cheddar and Monterey cheese blend
1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
2 tomatoes, chopped
1/2 cup salsa
1/2 cup low fat ranch dressing

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the romaine, cheese, pinto beans, black beans, and tomatoes.
Add the corn chips and enough dressing to coat; toss and serve.
