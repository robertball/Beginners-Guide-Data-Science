Title:
Italian chopped salad with vigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vinagrette:
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon minced shallots
1 tablespoon dijon mustard
1 garlic clove minced
1/4 cup olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup olive oil
Salad:
5 plum tomatoes seeded and choppedd
1 head romaine lettuce chopped
1 small red onion chopped
8 ounces small mozzarella balls drained and quartered

Directions:
For vinagrette whisk together vinegar,, shallots, mustard and garlic. Slowly whisk in oil. season with salt and pepper. In bowl toss together salad ingredients. Just before serving toss with vinagrette.
