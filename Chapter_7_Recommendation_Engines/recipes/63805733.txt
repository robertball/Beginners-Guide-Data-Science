Title:
Eggless Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C butter, softened
1 C packed dark brown sugar
1/2 C granulated sugar
2 TBSP ground flax seed
6 TBSP hot water
2 tsp vanilla
1 C flour
1/2 C wheat germ
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp sea salt
3 C rolled oats
1/2 C finely shredded coconut (no sugar added)
1 C dark chocolate chips
3/4 C chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
In large mixing bowl, cream butter, dark brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth and fluffy.
In a small separate bowl, combine ground flax seed with 6 TBSP water, stir well and let sit about 5 min. or until egg-like.  If needed, heat for 15-30 sec. in microwave. Let cool.
Add to creamed mixture, 1/2 at a time and mix well.
In separate bowl, combine flour, wheat germ, baking soda, and sea salt.  Add to mixture.
Stir in rolled oats, coconut, dark chocolate chips, and walnuts.
Drop by spoonfulls onto ungreased cookie sheet and bake in oven for 12 minutes, cool for 5 min. on cookie sheet then transfer to cooling rack.
