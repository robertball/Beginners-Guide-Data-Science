Title:
Blueberry Lover's Muffins Original Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
4 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/3 cup sugar
3 1/2 cups Kellogg's® All-Bran® Complete® Oat Flakes cereal
1 3/4 cups fat-free milk
2 egg whites
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries unthawed

Directions:
Stir together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and sugar. Set aside.
Measure Kellogg's(R) All-Bran(R) Complete(R) Oat Flakes cereal and milk into large mixing bowl. Stir to combine. Let stand 2 minutes or until cereal is softened. Add egg whites and oil. Beat well. Add flour mixture, stirring only until combined. Gently stir in blueberries. Spoon batter evenly into  twelve 2 1/2-inch muffin-pan cups coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 400 degrees F about 23 minutes or until light golden brown. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® All-Bran®
Description: 
The addition of oat flakes cereal helps transform these blueberry muffins into a wholesome breakfast option.
