Title:
Salt & Sugar Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound medium sized shrimp (31-40 count), shelled & deveined (leave tails on)
1 tablespoon coarse ground sea salt
1 tablespoon sugar
Zest of a lemon
1/8 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 clove minced garlic
Coarse ground black pepper, to taste
Lemon wedges

Directions:
1) Rinse shrimp and drain. In a large bowl, combine salt, sugar and lemon zest. Add shrimp and toss to coat evenly. cover bowl and chill for at least an hour, but no more than two hours.
2)Soak wooden skewers in water. Prepare barbeque grill and preheat for direct-heat grilling.
3)Rinse salt & sugar mixture from shrimp and pat dry. Thread shrimp onto soaked wooden skewers.
4)Lay skewers in a rimmed pan or plate. In a glass measuring cup, combine oil, lemon juice and garlic, pour over shrimp and turn skewers to coat evenly. Let stand for 10 minutes.
5)Lay skewers on grill over direct heat, keep grill uncovered. Cook, turning once, until shrimp are bright pink on the outside and opaque in the center, no more than 5 minutes.
6)Serve with lemon wedges.
