Title:
Mom's Bread Stuffing for Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup butter
3/4 Cup finely minced onion
12 Cups (3 quarts) bread crumbs (coarse or cubed)
1 1/2 Cups chopped celery (stalks and leaves)
1 Tablespoon salt
1 Teaspoon pepper
1 Teaspoon thyme
1 Teaspoon sage
1 Teaspoon marjoram
2 Teaspoons poultry seasoning
1/2 Cup milk
1/2 Cup hot chicken broth

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet.  Add onion and celery and cook until soft.  Stir in some of the bread crumbs and continue stirring until covered with butter mixture.  Mix in rest of ingredients.  Moisten with 1/2 cup milk and 1/2 cup hot chicken broth.  Cool and stuff inside turkey.

Submitter: 
Alley
Description: 
This is the stuffing my mom makes every Thanksgiving. Delicious blend of spices.  Oh so good! Enjoy!
