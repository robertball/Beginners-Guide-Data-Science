Title:
Pizza Bread Wedges (also my Favorite Fast Pizza Crust) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. sugar
1 tbsp. instant yeast
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup warm water
3 tbsp. olive oil (or your favorite substitute)
Topping:
1/3 cup Italian salad dressing
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. basil
1/2 tsp. thyme
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 cup (or more) grated marble or cheddar cheese
1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Crust:
Mix dry ingredients. Add water & oil. Knead in flour to make a smooth ball. Spread with a bit of oil or spray with cooking spray. Cover & let rise x 30 minutes.
Spread on a greased pizza pan.
Topping:
Brush dressing on crust. Sprinkle spices on top. Cover with cheeses.
Bake @ 450 x 15 - 20 minutes. Remove from oven and let stand for 5 - 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A fast yeast-based pizza crust recipe, that makes great pizza bread wedges.
I'm going to try making Papa John-ish bread sticks with this recipe.
Makes a quick & easy pizza crust too.
