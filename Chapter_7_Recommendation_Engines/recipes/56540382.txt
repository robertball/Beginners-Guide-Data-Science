Title:
Cheryl's Spinach Cheesy Pasta Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 itialian turkey sausage links crumbled & browned
2 cloves garlic minced
1 (8 ounce) can mushrooms drained
1/4 cup bell pepper chopped small
1/4 cup peperroni slices quartered
1 (12 ounce) package medium seashell pasta
1/2  package fresh  spinach chopped
2 eggs
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 cup bread crumbs
2 (26 ounce) jars tomato basil pasta sauce
1 (8 ounce) package itialian blend cheese
1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain. Bring 1/2 cup water to a boil in a saucepan, and cook the spinach 4 to 6 minutes, until tender.
Place the cooked pasta in a medium bowl. In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs and oil. Toss the pasta with the cooked spinach, egg mixture, and bread crumbs,.
Cover the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish with 1/3 of the pasta sauce. Pour half of the pasta mixture into the baking dish, and cover with another 1/3 of the pasta sauce. Sprinkle with a third of the itialian cheese blend and a 1/3  of the mozzarella. Layer with remaining pasta mixture, and top with remaining sauce. Sprinkle with the rest of the cheese blend and mozzarella cheeses. top with parmesean cheese
Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or until bubbly and lightly browned.
