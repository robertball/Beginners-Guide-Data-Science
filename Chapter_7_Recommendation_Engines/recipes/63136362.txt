Title:
Orange Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup orange juice
1 tablespoon grated orange zest
3 tablespoon soy sauce
1 teaspoon powdered ginger
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
3 cloves garlic, chopped
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into thin slices
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 cup sugar snap peas
1 cup broccoli florets
1 package stirfry veggies
1 cup carrots, sliced or julliened
(or other various vegetable combinations)

Directions:
In a small bowl combine the orange juice, orange zest, soy sauce, garlic, ginger and red pepper flakes. Mix well. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium high heat. When oil begins to bubble, add chicken and sauce mix. Saute until cooked through (no longer pink inside), about 7 to 10 minutes.
Mix chicken broth and corn starch together, slowly while stirring add a little at a time, until sauce has thickened to your liking. Add veggies and cook until tender crisp; serve hot over rice.
