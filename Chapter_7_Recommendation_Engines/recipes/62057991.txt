Title:
Mortons Contest - Verbena-Brined Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8   1/2 inch thick pork chops, frenched
1/4 cup and 2 tablespoons sugar
4   quarts water
1   cup loosely packed fresh verbena leaves
1/4 cup and 2 tbsp Morton Kosher salt

Directions:
Bring all ingredients, except pork to a boil in and 8-10 quart pot. Cool brine to room temperature. Add chops and chill in brine, covered for at least 8 hours. Remove chops from brine and rinse, then bring to room temperature, about 1 hour. Prepare grill for cooking. If using charcoal grill, open vents on bottom grill, then light charcoal. If using gas grill, preheat burners on high. Pat chops dry. Grill chops lightly oil grilling rack, turning over occasionally, until thermometer inserted horizontally registers 170 degress, 15 to 20 minutes. Transfer chops to a platter and let stand, covered loosely with foil, 10 minutes.

Description: 
This is a recipe that I make for my family, and they enjoy it. I like using Morton Kosher salt because it has a bolder flavor than the other salts I've used.
