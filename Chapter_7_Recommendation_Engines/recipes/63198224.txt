Title:
Miso Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1097842.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Pine nuts, toasted
OR walnuts OR sunflower seeds
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
4+ cloves Garlic, pressed
1/4 cup Water
1 1/2 tbsp shiro miso
4 cups Loosely packed fresh basil
OR 2 cups Basil + 2 cups Parsley

Directions:
Toast nuts/seeds in a dry skillet until golden brown, being careful not to burn. Grind in a blender and set aside.
In a blender/food processor, combine olive oil, garlic, miso, and water; blend.
Chop the basil finely, add to blender/food processor and process.
Add the nuts and blend until creamy smooth.

Submitter: 
Phinneas Stone
Description: 
Great for vegans or those of us who simply don't like cheese. Or just because.

Adapted from Judy Brown's _Guide to Natural Foods Cooking_ (Eden Foods)
