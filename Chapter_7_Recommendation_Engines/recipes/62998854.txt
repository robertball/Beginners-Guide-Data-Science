Title:
Cake Doughnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup white sugar
3 tsp tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
2 eggs, beaten light
2 Tbs melted shortening
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 quarts oil for deep frying

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl beat eggs, add sugar, shortening, milk and vanilla.  Mix in dry ingredients until well blended. Put on floured board and roll 1/4 inCh thiCk.  Cut with a doughnut Cutter.  Fry in deep fat 375 to 380.  Fry doughnuts in hot oil until golden brown, turning once. Remove from oil to drain on paper plates.
