Title:
Microwave Peanut Butter S'Mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987056.jpg
Ingredients:
Graham crackers
JIF® Creamy Peanut Butter
Milk chocolate candy bars
Large marshmallows

Directions:
Spread graham cracker square with peanut butter.
Top with a square of chocolate and a marshmallow.
Microwave on HIGH (100% power) 5 to 10 seconds or until marshmallow puffs.
Top with another cracker square. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Jif®
Description: 
Here's a nutty variation on a classic snack, plus no campfire is needed!
