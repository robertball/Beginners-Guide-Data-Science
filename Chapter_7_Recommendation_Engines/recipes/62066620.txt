Title:
Scooter Date Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1 c. packed brown sugar
1 c. butter or shortening
3 eggs, beaten
3 c. flour
1 tsp. each salt, soda, baking powder
1 tsp. vanilla
2 c. chopped dates
2 c. chopped nuts

Directions:
Cream sugars and shortening until light and fluffy; add eggs, beating well.  Combine flour, salt, soda, and baking powder; add to creamed mixture, mixing well. Stir in dates and nuts.
Roll dough into walnut-sized balls.  Place on greased cookie sheets.  Bake at 300 degrees about 15 minutes or until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Mary Ann Lackey
Description: 
Very easy.  Can be made and frozen.  Anyone who loves dates will love these cookies.
