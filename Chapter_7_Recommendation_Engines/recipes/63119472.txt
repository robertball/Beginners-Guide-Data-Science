Title:
Jackie's Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/793766.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked rice
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2 inch wide strips
2 cups frozen stir fry vegetables
1/2 cup stir fry sauce

Directions:
Cook the rice according to package directions.  Keep hot.
Heat oil in wok or large skillet over medium-high heat until hot.  Stir fry chicken 3 to 4 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
Stir in frozen vegetables, reduce heat to medium.  Cover, cook two minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender, stirring once or twice.
Stir in sauce.  Serve immediately with hot rice.

Submitter: 
JLeavitt
Description: 
An easy to make stir fry that is done in 20 minutes and very delicious!
