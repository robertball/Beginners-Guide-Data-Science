Title:
Country Beens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups beans
6 cups water
1/2 lb bacon or ham
1 small can tomato sauce
3 large toes garlic
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp pepper
1 onion

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and bring to a boil.  Cover and simmer or put in preheated 250 degree oven for about four hours.  Use less time if beans were pre-soaked.  Test for done...beans will be creamy soft.
Serve with cornbread (a must).
