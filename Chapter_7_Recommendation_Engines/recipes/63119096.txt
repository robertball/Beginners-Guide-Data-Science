Title:
Butter Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5/8 cup water
3 cups bread flour
3 tbs dry milk
3 tbs Sugar
2 1/2 tsp salt
1/3 cup Butter
2 tsp active dry yeast
egg; (for glaze)

Directions:
Place all ingredients except the egg in your 1.5lb bread machine.
Select DOUGH mode and start the machine.
When DOUGH cycle is complete, remove the dough and place in a lightly greased bowl.
Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and place in a warm area for 20 - 30 minutes to rise.
Place dough on lightly floured surface and divide into 12 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a cone.
Cover all pieces with a clean cloth and let rise for 15 minutes.
Using a rolling pin, roll each cone into a flat, triangular shape. Roll each triangle from the wide end to the narrow into to made your crescent roll.
Place rolls on a lightly greased cookie sheet and spray lightly with warm water. Place the cookie sheets into a warm oven (90F) for 30 - 40 minutes.
Bake rolls at 400F for 12 to 15 minutes. Brush the rolls lightly with beaten egg about halfway through baking.
