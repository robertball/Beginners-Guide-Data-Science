Title:
Hot Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/650133.jpg
Ingredients:
3 (8oz) pkg Fat Free Cream Cheese
6 tbsp Low Fat Milk
2 tbsp Worchestershire Sauce
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
1 tbsp chili powder
1 tbsp minced dried onions
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 clove minced garlic
1 (6oz) can Crab meat
1 1/2 tsp Tabsaco (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Soften cream cheese to room temperature. Mix all ingredients together.
Place mixture in an 8x8 ovenproof casserole dish. Bake 20 minutes.
Serve with your favorite chips or crackers.

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
This warm inviting seafood dip makes for a great way to toss a few ingredients together and enjoy before serving a main course.
