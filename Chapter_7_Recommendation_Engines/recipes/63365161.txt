Title:
Tomato Soup with Orzo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbs Olive Oil
3 cup Onions chopped
1 tbs Minced Garlic
4 cups Chicken Stock
1 28oz Can Crushed Tomatoes
1/2 cup Orzo
1/2 cup Heavy Cream

Directions:
1. In a large pot heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions and cook over medium heat for 15 min. Add Garlic and cook for 1 more min. Stir in chicken stock,tomatoes and salt and pepper to taste. Bring soup to a boil, then lower heat and simmer for 15 min.
2. Meanwhile, fill a medium pot with water, add 2 tsp salt and bring to a boil. Add orzo and cook for 7 min. Drain orzo and add to soup. Stir in heavy cream, return soup to a simmer and cook for 10 min stirring frequently.
