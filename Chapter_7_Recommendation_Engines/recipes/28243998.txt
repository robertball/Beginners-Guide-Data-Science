Title:
Broccoli with Sesame Vinaigrete Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. butter or margarine
1 tsp. sesame seeds
1 lb. fresh broccoli
2 TBS. plus 1-1/2 tsp. white wine vinegar
1 TBS. water
2 tsp. olive or sesame oil
1/2 tsp. LAWRY'S Seasoned Salt
1/2 tsp. LAWRY'S Seasoned Pepper

Directions:
On shallow microwave-safe plate, place butter and sesame seeds.  Cover with plastic wrap.  microwave on high 1 minute or until seeds are toasted; set aside.  Trim off large ends of lower broccoli stalks; discard.  Place trimmed broccoli in shallow microwave-safe dish; cover.  Microwave on high 7 minutes or until broccoli is crisp-tender.  Place broccoli on serving platter; keep warm.  Combine seeds, vinegar, water, oil, seasoned salt and pepper - drizzle over broccoli.  Garnish if desired.
