Title:
Pecan Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Pecan halves
1 cup sliced almonds
8 cups Popped popcorn
1 1/3 cup white sugar
1 cup butter
1/2 cup corn syrup
1/2 tsp. cream of tartar
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
preheat oven to 300 degrees.  Place pecans and almonds on cookie sheet and tost in oven.  Mix with popcorn in buttered bowl.
Mix sugar, butter, syrup and cream of tartar in pan.  Cook to hard ball stage (252 degrees) It takes about 5 minutes.  Remove from heat.
Stir in soda and vanilla.  Pour over popcorn and nuts.
spread out on tin foil and set.
Break into pieces and just try to eat just one little piece.
