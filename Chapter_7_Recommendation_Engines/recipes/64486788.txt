Title:
Roast Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head Cauliflower
1/4 Cup Olive oil
Low Salt Seasoning (I use Ranchers Seasoning from Wildtree)
Smoked  Paprika
Oregano
Fresh Lemon juice
pepper
1 /4 Cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 400.  Slice cauliflower like you are slicing thick pieces of bread. Spread on cookie sheet. Drizzle with olive oil,juice of half a lemon, sprinkle with remaining herbs and bake for 20 minutes.  Flip cauliflower and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese cook 5 additional minutes.  And Serve.
Delicious served warm or cold!

Submitter: 
Lisa Ermgodts
Description: 
I am gluten free and this recipe mimics my husbands grandmothers family breaded cauliflower recipe without the gluten! Plus my kids will eat it!
