Title:
Zucchini & Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound zucchini
8 ounces mushrooms
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon cold water
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 onion, thinly sliced
2 cloves garlic, crushed or minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons dark soy sauce
1/4 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Cut zucchini lengthwise into halves; then slice. Slice mushrooms. Mix cornstarch and water; set aside. Heat skillet over high, when hot add oil. Rotate to coat. Add onion and garlic; cook until garlic is golden brown. Add zucchini, mushrooms and salt; cook 3 minutes. Stir in soy sauce and chicken broth; heat to boiling. Stir in cornstarch mixture and cook stirring frequently for i minute.

Submitter: 
PIPPYMOE
Description: 
Wonderful way to cook zucchini. The sauce is light and does not overpower the vegetables.
