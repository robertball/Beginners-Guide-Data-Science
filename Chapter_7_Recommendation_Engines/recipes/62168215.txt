Title:
Romaine and Blue Cheese Salad with Warm Tomato and Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
9 oz. Romaine Heart Leaves, torn into 1" pieces
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
6 slices bacon
Dressing:
3 oz. blue cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup buttermilk
2 Tbsp. sour cream
2 Tbsp. mayonnaise
2 tsp. white wine vinegar
1/4 tsp. fresh ground black pepper
pinch garlic powder

Directions:
To make dressing, whisk buttermilk, sour cream and mayonnaise together.  Add vinegar, pepper, garlic powder and blue cheese and stir to combine.  Cover and chill for one hour.
Place bacon in a large skillet over medium-high heat, and cook until crispy; remove to paper towels to drain, then crumble.  Drain grease from skillet.  Return bacon to skillet and add tomatoes.  Heat over medium heat until tomatoes are heated through.
Place romaine in a large bowl and add tomato and bacon mixture.  Toss gently and serve with dressing.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
I had a similar salad at a restaraunt and fell in love with it.  So I set out to recreate it and came up with this wonderful version.  With its delightful combination of ingredients, you will be sure to fall in love with it too!
