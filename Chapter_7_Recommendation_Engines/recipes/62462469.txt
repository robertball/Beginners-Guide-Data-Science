Title:
Big Daddy Slow Cooker Sausage with Chia Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 (4 ounce) links fresh Italian sausage
1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
1 onion, sliced
1 tablespoon Big Daddy's Chia Roasted Garlic
1 scoop Mila

Directions:
Place the Italian sausage links, Roasted Garlic, Mila, spaghetti sauce, green pepper and onion into a slow cooker. Stir to coat everything in sauce. Cover, and cook on Low for 6 hours. Serve on hoagie rolls, or over rice.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
