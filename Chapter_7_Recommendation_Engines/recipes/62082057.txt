Title:
Hungarian Pizzelles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup melted butter
3/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon anise extract
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to beat the eggs and sugar together.
Mix in the melted butter, anise, and vanilla.
Gradually add the flour and baking powder and beat until smooth.
Preheat your pizzelle iron according to the manufacturer's instructions.  Add a spoonful of batter to iron.
Close and cook for about 45 seconds or until steam stops coming out of the iron.
Carefully remove pizzelle and lay on flat surface to cool.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar and store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Jennifer S
Description: 
My Hungarian grandmother makes this thin and delicate version of the traditional pizzelle.
