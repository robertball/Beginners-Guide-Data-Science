Title:
Homemade Runzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough:
2 cups warm water
2 packages active dry yeast
1/2 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 egg
1/4 cup margarine, melted and cooled
6 1/2 cups flour
Filling:
1 1/2 lb hamburger
1/2 cup chopped onion
3 cups shredded cabbage
1/2 cup water
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
Dash of Tabasco

Directions:
For Dough:  In a large bowl, mix water, yeast, sugar and salt and stir until dissolved.
Add egg and melted margarine.
Stir in flour and refrigerate for 4 hours.
For FIlling:  While dough is refrigerating, brown the hamburger and onion.  Drain well.
Add cabbage, seasonings and water and simmer 15-20 minutes.
Allow  mixture to cool completely.
After 4 hours,  preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit.
Roll out the dough into an oblong shape and cut into 16 squares.
Divide meat mixture evenly  between the dough squares.
Pull up the four sides of each square, and press edges together.
Place on greased cookie sheet and bake 20 minutes, or until lightly browned.
Allow to cool slightly before serving.
