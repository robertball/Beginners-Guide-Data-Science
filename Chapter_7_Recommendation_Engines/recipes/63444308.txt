Title:
Hawaiian chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups pineapple juice
2 TBS. Worchestershire sauce
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 TBS. sugar
1 can pineapple slices
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts, poked with fork

Directions:
Mix first 4 ingredients together and put meat in to marinate for 24 hours. Remove chicken and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Top each breast with a pineapple slice. Bake at 350 until done.
