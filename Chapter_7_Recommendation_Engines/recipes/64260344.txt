Title:
DINNER ROLLS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1819058.jpg
Ingredients:
2 CUP FLOUR
¼ CUP SUGAR
¼ CUP SHORTENING (CRISCO OR OTHER MELTED)
½  TSP SALT
1 EGG (BEATEN)
PUT 1 PKG OF YEAST MIXED INTO ¾ CUP OF WARM WATER,

Directions:
STIR FLOUR, SALT AND SUGAR TOGETHER
ADD EGG AND YEAST MIXED IN WATER TO FLOUR THEN ADD MELTED SHORTENING MIX UNTILL DOUGH FORMS AND
COVER AND LET RISE FOR (1) HOUR
POUR OUT ON FLOURED SURFACE…BRIEFLY KNEAD
ROLL OUT CUT INTO CIRCLES (I USE MOUTH OF WATER GLASS)
DIP CIRCLES IN MELTED BUTTER AND FOLD IN HALF….PLACE IN PAN -COVER AND LET RISE AGAIN  (ABOUT AND HOUR)
-OR-  IF NOT BAKING IMMEDIATELY COVER AND FREEZE.
BAKE AT 375 DEGREES.  WATCH CLOSELY TILL SLIGHTLY BROWN…
SHOULD MAKE ABOUT 1 ½ TO 2 DOZ.
