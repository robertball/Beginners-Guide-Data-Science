Title:
Smoked Turkey and Green Chile Turnovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989409.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) jar ORTEGA速 Salsa (any flavor), divided
3/4 cup chopped smoked turkey
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (4 ounce) can ORTEGA速 Diced Green Chiles
1/3 cup sliced green onions
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1 (10 ounce) package puff pastry shells, defrosted
1 large egg
2 teaspoons water
1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh cilantro or parsley

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 400 degrees F.
COMBINE turkey, cheddar cheese, 1/3 cup salsa, chiles, green onions and 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese in medium bowl. Combine egg and water in small bowl; brush edges of each shell with egg mixture.
SPOON 1/3 cup turkey mixture onto half of each shell; fold shells in half over filling. Crimp edges of each turnover with tines of fork. Place turnovers on ungreased baking sheet. Brush with remaining egg wash; sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese and cilantro.
BAKE for 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown. Serve with remaining salsa.
