Title:
Classic Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. packed brown sugar
1/2 c. butter, melted (no substitutes)
2 eggs, beaten
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 c. flour
1/2 t. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
1 c. chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl, combine brown sugar, butter, eggs and vanilla until blended.
Combine flour, baking powder and salt.
Add to brown sugar mixture.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Spread into a greased 13X9" baking pan.

Submitter: 
MRSLAURIE84
Description: 
I make these bars when we have a houseful of teenage boys.  They are quicker to make than chocolate chip cookies and disappear just as fast.  I am not allowed to a carry in dinner without these babies.
