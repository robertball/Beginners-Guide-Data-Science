Title:
hollandaise sauce (military cook) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 egg yolks
15 ml of lemon juice
114g of unsalted butter
salt / cayenne pepper to taste

Directions:
-seperate yolk from 3 eggs
-cut butter in numerous sections
-combine yolks and lemon juice on top of double boiler beat well
-add 1/3 of the butter whille continously beating with wisk over low heat
-repeat until all of butter is added
-add salt and pepper
