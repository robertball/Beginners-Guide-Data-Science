Title:
Paleo Blueberry Pie Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/3 Cup Blueberries (frozen or 1/3 less for fresh blueberries)
1/3 Cup + 1 Teaspoon  Arrowroot Powder
2 Tablespoons Lemon Juice
1/2 Cup Honey
1/4 Teaspoon Table Salt
1/4 Teaspoon Coriander (optional)
Premade Pie Crust

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients and mix well
2. Refrigerate the  mixture for 30 minutes
3. Preheat oven to 400Â°F
4. Pour mixture into pie crust  and smooth until even
5. Bake in oven for one hour
Note: Honey can be substituted with other all natural sweetener

Submitter: 
Carson Crochet
Description: 
Yummy pie filling created only with ingredients found out of the Earth. Wholesome ingredients for a whole pie :) I wanted a pie that would taste good and not mess up my digestive system with processed foods or dairy.
