Title:
Moms Easy and Delicious Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 (8 oz.) container whipped cream cheese
* 1 (7 oz.) container marshmallow creme

Directions:
Put both ingredients in a bowl. now whip it and keep whipping, can use an electric mixer to do this. Whip it till it's smooth and fluffy. Serve with any fruit of choice such as; strawberries, bananas, pineapples, apples, grapes, blueberries etc.
