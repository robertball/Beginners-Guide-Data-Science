Title:
Creme Au Chocolat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 four-ounce bar sweet cooking chocolate
2 tbsp sugar
3/4 cup light cream
2 egg yolks, slightly beaten
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Heat chocolate, sugar and cream over medium heat, stirring constantly, until chocolate is melted and mix is smooth.
Gradually beat into egg yolks. Stir in vanilla.
Pour into small dessert bowls and garnish with whipped cream if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
This French custard cream is very rich but extremely delicious, and guaranteed to please your sweet-tooth!
