Title:
Turkey Stuffed Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large zucchini, sliced lengthwise with middle scooped out and reserved
2 cups lean ground turkey
1/2 cup chopped sweet peppers
1/4 cup fat-free shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 F.
2. Brown ground turkey, peppers, and the reserved zucchini in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Place "boats" on greased sheet pan.
4. Spoon mixture into each zucchini boat and bake at 400 F for 30 minutes.
5. Remove boats, sprinkle mozzarella cheese, and return to oven until cheese is melted.
