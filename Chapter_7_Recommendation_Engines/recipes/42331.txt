Title:
Creamy Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/648129.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium cucumber, diced
2 plum tomatoes, diced
4 green onions with tops, sliced
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 teaspoon celery salt or salt-free seasoning blend
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Refrigerate until serving.
