Title:
Beef Barley Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) beef chuck roast
1 cup barley
1 bay leaf
1 tablespoon oil
3 carrots, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1/2 to 1  cup each of frozen corn and peas
4 cups beef broth
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (28 ounce) can chopped petite diced tomatoes
salt to taste
additional ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut the chuck roast into small cubes and brown in one tablespoon oil. Transfer to slow cooker; add onion, celery, carrots, tomatoes, beef broth and seasonings and cook until very tender (usually 4 to 6 hours on High, but can vary with different slow cookers). Add barley, bay leaf and frozen vegetables during the last two hours.
