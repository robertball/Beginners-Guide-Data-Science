Title:
No Bake Energy Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup old-fashioned oats, dry
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup flax seed, ground
1/2 cup chocolate chips
1/3 cup honey
1 tablespoon chia seeds (optional)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
1. Stir all ingredients together until well mixed.
2. Cover and let chill in refrigerator for 30 minutes.
3. After chilling, roll into balls with approximately 1-inch diameter. Or, press mixture into a parchment lined baking sheet. Cut into bars.
4. Store in an air-tight container and refrigerate for up to one week.

Submitter: 
Kayla Janis
Description: 
Easy and quick. Make as breakfast bites for the whole week!
