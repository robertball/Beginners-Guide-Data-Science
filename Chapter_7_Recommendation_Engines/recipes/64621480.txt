Title:
Ham and Caulifower Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion diced
1 cup cut carrot
1 Tblsp olive oil
3 cups diced ham I use leftovers
1 head cauliflower cut into florets
1/4 cup coconut milk
14.5 oz chicken broth
1/4 teaspoon salt
4 cloves garlic
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
dash pepper
8 oz cheddar cheese freshly grated

Directions:
Sautee onion and carrot in olive oil until onion is soft.
Add garlic and spices..
Add florets and ham and chicken broth.
Put lid on IP.  Close steam vent.
Manual, high 2 minutes.
Quick release steam.
Add in coconut milk.
Garnish with grated cheese.
I served mine over shredded cabbage.  Nice crunch.
