Title:
Yummy Crunchies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Sticks Butter
2 Cups Brown Sugar
2 TBSP Light Corn Syrup
8 Cups Corn Chex

Directions:
Combine the butter, brown sugar and corn syrup in a pot. Boil for two minutes.  Mix in the 8 cups of Corn Chex.
Spread onto a cookie sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes at 250 degrees.
