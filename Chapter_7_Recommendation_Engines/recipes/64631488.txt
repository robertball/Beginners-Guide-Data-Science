Title:
Chicken Quinoa salad with Apples and Mozzarella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked quinoa
3 cups cooked chicken, chopped
3 large sour apples
8 oz mozzarella, shredded or cubed
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
2 tsp dried thyme
2 tbsp dried parsley flakes
1/4 cup fruity vinaigrette dressing

Directions:
Cook the quinoa and lay out on a platter to cool
Cut the apples (skin on) into thin 1" slices
In a large bowl, combine the apples, cooked chicken, mozzarella, salt, pepper, thyme, and parsley
Add vinaigrette dressing and mix well
Refrigerate the mixture for 20 minutes
Mix in the quinoa and refrigerate until ready to serve

Submitter: 
Bryan
Description: 
This is a variation on the popular farro chicken salad and is loaded with protein and healthy carbs.
