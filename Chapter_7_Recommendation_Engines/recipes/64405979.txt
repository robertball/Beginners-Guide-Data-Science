Title:
Creamy Garlic Pasta by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp butter
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
3 cups chicken broth, plus a little extra
1/2 pound angel hair pasta or fettuccini
1 cup Parmesan cheese
3/4 c heavy cream
1 1/2 tsp dried parsley
2 large chicken breasts (3-4 portions), cooked or grilled

Directions:
In medium pan heat olive oil over medium heat.
Add garlic and stir for 1-2 minutes.
Add butter and melt, stirring constantly.
Add 3 cups broth and salt & pepper.
Bring to boil, add pasta and cook according to package directions or until desired tenderness. Add a little more broth if noodles start to stick together.
Add cheese, cream and parsley. Cook until heated through.
Add cooked chicken and serve immediately.
I add or serve with steamed broccoli
