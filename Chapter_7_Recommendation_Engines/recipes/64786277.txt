Title:
Barbacoa Beef Tacos - Instant Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 Cup Beer
4 Cloves Garlic
4 Chipotles in adobe sauce (de-seed chipotles)
1 onion, diced
4oz can of chopped green chilies
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
2 TBL apple cider vinegar
1 TBL ground cumin
1 TBL dried oregano (Mexican or regular)
2 tea salt
1 tea black pepper
1/4 tea ground cloves
1 TBL olive oil
3-4# beef chuck roast with fat trimmed, cut into 2 inch chunks
3 bay leaves

Directions:
1.  Combine beer, garlic, chipotles, onion, green chilies, lime juice, vinegar, cumin, oregano, salt, pepper, and cloves in a food processor.  Puree for 30 seconds and set aside.
2.  Put Instant Pot in saute mode.  Add oil.  Add roast and sear, turning every 1-2 minutes until roast is browned on all side.   Turn off Instant Pot.
3.  Add bay leaves and pureed sauce and mix.
4.  Put Instant Pot on Manual Mode on High Pressure and set time to 60 minutes.  Once time is up, turn vent to quick release venting.    Remove lid and discard bay leaves.
5.  Using two forks, shred beef in juices.
6.  Serve in your favorite taco shells with your favorite taco toppers (refried beans, black beans, shredded lettuce, salsa, avocado slices, sour cream, etc.)
