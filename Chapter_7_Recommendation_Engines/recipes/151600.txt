Title:
Campbell's Kitchen Souper Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992590.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Tomato Soup
1/4 cup water
1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
6 Pepperidge Farm® Farmhouse™ Sesame White Rolls

Directions:
Cook beef in skillet until browned. Pour off fat.
Add soup, water and mustard. Heat through. Serve on rolls.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Ground beef simmers in a savory mustard-spiked tomato sauce to pile into sandwich buns.
