Title:
Apple and Pear Crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of apple, peeled and diced
2 cups of pears, peeled and diced
1/2 cup of flour
1 1/2 cup of rolled oats
1/2 to 3/4 cup of sliced almonds
1 1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 softened butter
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 350F
In a bowl, mix the flour, rolled oats, half of the required brown sugar and the butter
In a separate bowl, mix together the apples, pears, other half of brown sugar and the cinnamon
Put the apple-pear mix in the bottom of a glass pan, evenly.
Put the flour mix on top of the apple mix, evenly.
Sprinkle the sliced almonds on top of the flour, evenly.
Bake for 40 minutes, and enjoy!
