Title:
Veggie Breakfast Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 vegetarian sausage patties
4 ounces of fresh mushrooms, chopped
2 cloves minced garlic
¾ cup finely chopped fresh spinach
½ t Italian seasoning blend
Chili powder, cayenne, salt and pepper to taste
1 cup egg whites or 4 eggs, lightly beaten
4 English muffins, toasted
½ cup low fat or fat free cream cheese
Olive oil cooking spray

Directions:
Cook the sausage patties according to the package directions.
In a skillet sprayed with cooking spray, sauté mushrooms over medium heat until tender.
Add the minced garlic, chopped spinach, Italian seasoning and other seasoning to pan.
Stir until mixed.
Pour in eggs, stir lightly until mixed.
As eggs set, lift edges, letting uncooked portion flow underneath. When eggs are completely set, remove from the heat.
Cut into four wedges.
Spread toasted English muffins halves with cream cheese, top with a sausage patty and an egg wedge.

Submitter: 
Linda T
Description: 
Delicious veggie filled breakfast sandwich. I make large batches of these and keep them in the freezer. Take one out the night before and let degrost in fridge. In the morning, just nuke and you have a yummy and healthy hot breakfast
