Title:
The Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 to 8 large potatoes, peeled and chopped, or similar amt of boxed flakes
1 to 1 1/2 pound lean ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 cup frozen corn
1 cup frozen green beans or peas
or 2 cups of frozen mixed veggies
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom or celery soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
OR substitute a can of chicken gravy for one of the above, but add some milk or cornstarch solution to thicken.
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
garlic powder and other seasonings as desired.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish; set aside
Bring a large saucepan of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and mash, adding half the grated cheese.
Place lean ground beef and onion in a large skillet. Brown onion and beef. Drain fat and stir in veggies, cream soups.
Pour mixture into the prepared baking dish and top with mashed potatoes. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese and bake in preheated oven for 30  minutes, or until  potatoes are lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Mary E
Description: 
This casserole of ground beef, veggies of your choice, topped with mashed potatoes and Cheddar cheese pleases even die hard non-casserole eaters.
