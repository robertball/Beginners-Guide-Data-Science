Title:
Skillet Flipped Hash Browns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1848561.jpg
Ingredients:
1 30-oz bag frozen shredded hash Browns
2 sticks butter, melted and divided
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
Open the bag of hash browns and microwave for 6 minutes on high. Pour into large bowl.
Add 1 stick melted butter and generous amount of salt and pepper and combine well.
Heat a nonstick skillet on medium and melt 1/2 stick of butter.
Add the hash browns and pat down.
Allow the hash browns to cook without stirring until edges begin to brown.
Place a plate on top of the hash browns and turn the skillet over, flipping the potatoes over onto the plate.
Heat 1/2 stick of butter in the skillet and carefully slide the hash browns back into the skilled.
Continue to cook until the edges brown.
When browned, flip the hash browns back onto the plate and serve.
