Title:
Ham 'n' Swiss Ring Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
1 1/2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
1 cup chopped onion
2 cups fresh spinach
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1 cup chopped fully cooked ham
2 cups Cheddar/Monterey shredded cheese blend
pepper to taste

Directions:
Arrange crescent rolls on a baking sheet, forming two logs with wide ends overlapping and pointed ends facing the outer edge of pan. In a skillet, saute mushrooms, spinach and onion in butter for 8 minutes or until juices are absorbed. Add remaining ingredients; mix well. Spoon over wide ends of rolls. Fold points of rolls over filling and tuck under wide ends at center (filling with be visible). Bake at 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
