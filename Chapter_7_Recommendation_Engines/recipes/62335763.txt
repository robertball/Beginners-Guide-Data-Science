Title:
Orange Nut Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 16 oz pkgs refrigerated sugar cookie dough
1 cup of orange marmalade
1 cup of chopped pecans or walnuts
1 cup of chopped chocolate or chocolate baking chips

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350 f
Use your fingers to spread out cookie dough out onto a cookie sheet to 1/4 inch thickness.Bake until golden,about 25 minutes.Cool completely.
Spread orange marmalade over crust and sprinkle with nuts.
Place chocolate pieces in a small bowl and melt in the microwave (at low power). Scrape melted chocolate in a plastic zipper top bag.SNIP a tiny corner off the bottom of the bag and squeezze melted chocolate through to decroate the top of cookie squares.Cut into bars

Submitter: 
MrsAudirsch
Description: 
This is a really nice simple treat for get togethers or brunch.I like them at home for myself.It's a nice twist to use marmalade.
