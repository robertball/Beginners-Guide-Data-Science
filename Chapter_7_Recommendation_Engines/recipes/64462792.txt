Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves (you can even use frozen)
2 tablespoons butter
1 14-ounce can chicken broth
poultry seasoning, salt and pepper, to taste
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup
1 onion, finely diced
2 (10 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough, torn into pieces

Directions:
Place the chicken, butter, soup, onion and chicken broth in a slow cooker.
Cover, and cook for 6 hours on Low.  Add the torn biscuits, pushing them down into the chicken mixture, and cook on Low for 2 more hours.  Shred the chicken up before serving.

Submitter: 
Hudson's Mama

Easy, creamy chicken and dumplings made from refrigerator biscuits, slow cooked to comfort-food perfection.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings
