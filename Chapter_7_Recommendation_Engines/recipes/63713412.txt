Title:
Blueberrylicious Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dry
1-3/4 Cup.    Flour
4 Tbs.           Sugar
1 tsp.             Baking Powder
1/2 tsp.          Salt
1/2 tsp.          Baking Soda
Wet
2.                   Eggs
4 Tbs.            Butter (melted)
1 Cup.            Sour Cream
1 Cup.            Milk
1-1/2 Cups.    Fresh or frozen Blueberries

Directions:
Heat griddle to 375.
Whisk together dry ingredients in large bowl.
In small bowl, gently whisk eggs.  Whisk in butter, sour cream, and milk.
Gently whisk together until wet (don't over stir)
Gently fold in blueberries.
Grease griddle.  Ladle half cup of batter onto hot griddle. Turn after 2 minutes or when pancake releases easily. Cook until done in center. Serve with butter and warmed syrup.

Submitter: 
Cindy
Description: 
This is my family's favorite weekend brunch with a side of bacon.  Especially yummy with fresh blueberries in the summer.
