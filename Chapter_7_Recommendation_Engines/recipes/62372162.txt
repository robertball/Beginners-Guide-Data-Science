Title:
Gorgonzola Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground turkey
1/2 cup crumbled gorgonzola cheese
1/2 package onion soup mix
3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 egg, beaten
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 cups fresh spinach
1/2 red onion sliced
1/2 tsp olive oil
foccacia bread (2 pieces per burger)

Directions:
Mix turkey, egg, soup mix, gorgonzola, Worcestershire sauce and pepper in a medium bowl and form into 4-6 burger patties. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the burgers for 5 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
While burgers are cooking saute the onion in olive oil over medium heat until tender. Add spinach and stir until wilted.
Toast the foccacia bread and disperse the burgers and spinach/onion mixture.

Submitter: 
Jen
Description: 
Gorgonzola cheese, spinach and onions adds great flavor to these simple turkey burgers.
