Title:
Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Mayonnaise
1/3 cup Sugar
1/4 cup Milk
1/4 cup Buttermilk
2 1/2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
1 1/2 Tbsp White Vinegar
1/2 Tsp Salt
1/8 Teaspoon Pepper
1 head of Cabbage (finely chopped)
1 Carrot (shredded)
2 Tbsp Minced onion

Directions:
Combine first seven ingredients, blend well.
Add veggies and mix well.
For best results, Let stand a few hours before serving.

Submitter: 
April Ashley Ann Willison
Description: 
A sweet slaw, perfect to top a bbq sandwich.  Passed down from my mother!
