Title:
New Mexican Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CUP CORNMEAL
2 EGGS - BEATEN
1 CUP WHOLE MILK
1/2 TEASPOON BAKING SODA
3/4 TEASPOON SALT
1 no. 303 CREAM STYLE CORN
1/4 CUP BACON DRIPPINGS or COOKING OIL
1 CUP GRATED CHEDDAR
1 CHOPPED JALAPENÕ PEPPER
1 LARGE ONION CHOPPED
1/2 lb LEAN GROUND BEEF (SAUTÊD and DRAINED)

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 350°
MIX CORNMEAL, BAKING SODA, SALT
ADD MILK, EGGS, BACON DRIPPINGS or OIL, CORN
COAT LARGE CAST IRON SKILLET WITH OIL( PAM WORKS GREAT), HEAT OVER BURNER AND SPRINKLE WITH 1 TEASPOON CORNMEAL AND BROWN
POUR 1/2 OF BATTER INTO SKILLET, ADD COOKED BEEF, ONIONS 3/4 cup GRATED CHEESE AND PEPPERS
ADD REMAINING BATTER
BAKE AT 350° FOR 50 – 60 MINUTES
TOP WITH 1/4 CUP GRATED CHEESE, BAKE ADDITIONAL 8-10 MIN TILL CHEESE MELTS
