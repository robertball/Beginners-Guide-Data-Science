Title:
Sunny Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb butter
1/4 cup fresh minced ginger
1 med bulb garlic (yes the whole bulb!)
2 onions finely diced
1 tbsp garam masala
1 tbsp curry powder
1 tbsp tumeric
1 tsp cayanne pepper
1 tsp salt
12 pc. skinless bone in chicken thighs
1 small can tomato paste
2 cups water
1 large field tomato diced
1 green pepper diced
1/2 cup fresh cilantro chopped
1 cup plain yogurt

Directions:
On med heat melt butter add fine chopped ginger, garlic and brown for 5 min.  Add onions and simmer for 20 min.  Add masala, curry, cayanne, and tumeric.  Throw in chicken brown for 2 1/2 min on each side.  blend in a bowl the water, tomato paste, salt, green pepper, tomato and cilantro.  Pour over chicken mixture let simmer for 30 min over medium heat stiring every 10 min.  When chicken is cooked remove from heat and blend in the plain yogurt.  Serve over rice with a side of nan or roti.

Submitter: 
EBSEPKE
Description: 
This is the best curry chicken I've ever had.  Sunny, a guy my husband works with, gave us this recipe.  If you love spicy curry recipes this one is for you.  Serve with rice and nan or roti.  A perfect Indian meal!
