Title:
Creamy Gorgonzola Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045292.jpg
Ingredients:
1	teaspoon olive oil
2	tablespoons minced shallots
1	clove garlic, minced
2	teaspoon all purpose flour
2/3	cup 2% milk
1/3	cup (1oz) crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
1	teaspoon butter
2	teaspoons chopped flat leaf parsley.

Directions:
Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add shallots and garlic; cook 1 minute. Add flour; cook 30 seconds, stirring. Add milk; boil. Cook until reduced by half. Remove from heat; stir in cheese and remaining ingredients.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
Great quick easy sauce for Beef!  can be made with blue cheese or Gorgonzola very easily.
