Title:
Thousand Island Hot Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups mayonnaise
3 cups marinated artichoke hearts
2 cups Italian Blend or Mexican Blend Cheese
1/4 cup Kraft's 1000 Island Dressing
1 tbs. Tabasco Sauce
1 Tbs. Worcestershire Sauce
paprika

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Place all ingredients in a food processor, holding back 1 cup of the cheese. Process until the artichokes are minced. Pour the dip into a baking dish and top with the rest of the cheese and sprinkle on some paprika. Bake for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
COOKINCOWGIRLS
Description: 
This is a great party dip for a crowd. Serve it with tortilla chips, bagel chips, or assorted crackers.
