Title:
Baby Berry Cobblers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 t baking powder
pinch salt
1/4 c milk
1 T butter, melted
1 cup fruit/berries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Grease 2 custard cups
Combine flour, sugar, powder and salt.
Stir in milk and melted butter, just until moistened.
Divide half of the berries in cups, pour mix over, top with remaining berries.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown
Serve warm topped with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Original recipe called for 1 cup blueberries which was quite nice.  Could easily use peaches, apples, cherry pie mix, etc.  A snap to whip up.
