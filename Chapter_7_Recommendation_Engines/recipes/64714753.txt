Title:
Pan Seared Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
salmon filets
salt
ghee

Directions:
salt salmon filets and bring to room temperature
pat dry
heat cast iron pan to medium high heat
melt 1-2 T ghee in cast iron pan
carefully place salmon filets in pan, skin side down for extra crispy skin or skin side up for more browning on flesh; do not crowd fish
sear for approximately 5 minutes
carefully flip and continue searing for 2-4 min
fish should still be pink in center
serve with garlic butter, browned butter/ghee (in pan), or chimichurri/herb sauce
