Title:
Butternut Squash and Smoky Black Bean Salad (Cooking Light) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4C (1/2 inch) cubed peeled butternut squash
7tsp olive oil, divided
1/2C walnuts, chopped
1/2tsp salt
2Tbsp red wine vinegar
1Tbsp dijon mustard
1Tbsp honey
1Tbsp Adobo sauce (from can)
2 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1/4tsp pepper
1 (15oz) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (9oz) package baby arugula
1/2C (2oz) crumbled goat cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425
2. Toss squash with 1Tbsp olive oil, arrange on baking sheet.  Bake 25 minutes or until tender
3. Toast walnuts
4. Combine 1Tbsp olive oil, vinegar, mustarnd, honey and adobo sauce in bowl, wisk
5. Heat med nonstick skillet over medium heat.  Add 1tsp oil to pan, swirl to coat.  Add garlic; saute 1 minutes.  Add squash, salt epper, beans and cook 3 mins until heated through.  Remove from heat, stir in 3Tbsp of adobo dressing and toss to coat.
6. Combine remaining dressing and arugula, toss to coat..
7. Divide arugula among 4 plates, top with bean mixture.  Sprinkle evenly with walnuts and goat cheese.
