Title:
Buckwheat Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole hulled buckwheat
Â¼ cup raw pumpkin or sunflower seeds
2 Tbsp. ground flax seed
Blend separately:
1 Tbsp. raw cacao powder
1 Tbsp. raw honey
1 Tbsp. coconut oil or ghee
Pinch pink salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F.
Combine buckwheat, pumpkin seeds and flax seed into an 8x8 glass baking dish.
In a separate small bowl, combine cacao powder, honey, oil and pinch of salt. Mix together until thoroughly blended.
Pour chocolate mixture over buckwheat and seeds in baking dish. Stir to coat evenly.
Spread evenly then bake 25 minutes, stir halfway through baking time. Remove from oven when seeds are toasted.
Allow to cool then store in glassware in fridge.
