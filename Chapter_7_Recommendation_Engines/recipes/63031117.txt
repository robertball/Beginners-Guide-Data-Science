Title:
Apple Puff Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1028187.jpg
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup all-purpose flour
3 tablespoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
4 tablespoons butter
2-4 apples - peeled, cored and sliced
1/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Place 4T butter in 9x13 pan and place in oven to melt.  Meanwhile, cut and thinly slice 3-4 large apples.  Place them in the melted butter, give it a stir, and stick back in the oven for 10 minutes.
Remove apples from oven and sprinkle with mixture of 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1/2t nutmeg, 1/2t cinnamon.  Set aside.
In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to blend eggs, milk and vanilla. Add flour, sugar, salt and cinnamon; mix just until blended. Pour the batter over the apples. Bake at 375 for 35 minutes.
