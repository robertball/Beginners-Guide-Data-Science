Title:
Green Peppercorn Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30g/1oz Green Peppercorns  drained
120ml/4oz Brandy
300ml/10 fl oz Beef or Lamb Stock
100ml/3 ½ fl oz Thick (double) Cream
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Put the peppercorns in a heavy based pan and, at a very low heat, warm through for 1 to 2 minutes until dry, careful not to burn them;
Then roughly break the peppercorns against the side of the pan with a spoon;
Pour in the brandy, turn up the heat and boil for 1 to 2 minutes, or until the brandy has evaporated;
Stir in the stock, bring to the boil and boil for some 5 minutes or until the liquid has reduced by a quarter;
Add the cream and continue to reduce over a high heat until the sauce coats the back of the spoon;
Season, if required, with salt and pepper to taste;
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A pungent,full flavoured piquant pepper based sauce to enhance any red meat especially steak. Delicious.
