Title:
Dry Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/943474.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1/8 teaspoon fresh ground pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vegetable stock powder or 1 bouillon cube
1 teaspoon dried onions
1 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients together and use as required. The above amount replaces one 40 gram packet of soup mix (1.4 oz packet).

Submitter: 
Lori Bachner Carey
Description: 
Many recipes call for a 1.4oz packet of French onion soup mix or dry onion soup mix. This makes a great replacement.
