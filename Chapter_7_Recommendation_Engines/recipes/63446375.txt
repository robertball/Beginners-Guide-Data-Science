Title:
Giant Baked Pancake Puff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/936092.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp butter, melted in 9x13 baking dish, and swirled around to coat all sides
4 eggs
1 1/2 c milk
1 tsp vanilla
3 tbsp sugar
1 1/2 c flour
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Melt butter in 9x13 baking dish; swirl around to coat all sides.
In bowl, beat eggs with whisk for 2 minutes.
Add milk, vanilla, and sugar, and beat for another minute.
Sprinkle in flour and salt and whisk until flour is incorporated.
Pour batter into baking dish, and bake at 375 degrees for 35 minutes, until puffy and golden.
Cut Into squares and serve immediately with fruit, yogurt, or syrup.
