Title:
Baked Almond or Pecan! Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
spices to taste
1 egg, beaten
1/4 cup sliced almonds or pecans
4 - 6 chicken breasts, thawed
2 tbsp. butter, melted

Directions:
Mix bread crumbs, parmesan and seasoning.
Dip chicken in egg and then in crumb mixture.
Place in greased 9" x 13" pan.
Sprinkle almonds (or pecans) evenly over chicken.
Drizzle with melted butter.
Bake, uncovered, @ 350 x 45 minutes, or until done.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Parmesan in the crumb coating is delicious & nuts add a nice crunch.
