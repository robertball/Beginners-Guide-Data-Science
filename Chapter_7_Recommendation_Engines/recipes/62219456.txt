Title:
Easiest Sweet and Salty Christmas Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - 12 oz bags Hershey Hugs
1 - 24 oz bag Old Dutch Holiday Shaped Pretzels
1 - 16 oz bag Holiday M&M's (regular)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 175 degrees.
Place a single layer of pretzels on a cookie sheet.  Unwrap Hershey Hugs and set on top of the pretzels.
Bake for 2-3 minutes, until Hugs begin to melt.  Take out of oven and press a single M&M down in the center.  Cool completely before serving.

Submitter: 
munchmn
Description: 
Sweet and Salty combination along with a Christmas theme makes for a Festive Cookie that my family looks forward to every year.  Best of all, it's super easy and only takes 3 ingredients!
