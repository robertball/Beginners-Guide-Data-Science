Title:
Aunt Kaye's Rhubarb Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound rhubarb cut crosswise into 1/4" pieces
1 cup sugar
1 4-oz box straberry Jello
1 box yellow cake mix
1 cup water
1/4 cup margarine

Directions:
1. buttter the bottom of the cake pan.  Fill bottom of the pan with rhubarb.
2.  sprinkle on sugar, Jello and cake mix.
3. Pour on water and melted margarine.
4. NOTE: If you use strawberry cake mix omit Jello.
5. bake at 350 degrees until rhubarb is soft. About 30-50 minutes.

Submitter: 
SCOOBYVC
Description: 
This is an easy family desert. My Aunt always made it for family gatherings.  Serve warm with vanilla icecream. MMMMM
