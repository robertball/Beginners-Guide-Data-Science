Title:
Smothered Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork loins cut into 1/4 inch pork chops Or chops
4 cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
2 pkg. brown gravy mix
12 cups water
1/2 cup bacon fat (optional) but makes it better
3 small onion, sliced
4 tbls. chopped garlic
2 tbls. pepper supreme

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grill pork chops on a medium high grill for 2-3 minutes on each side. Set aside.
Saute onions in bacon fat until soft. Add garlic and continue to saute for an aditional minute.
Add water, brown gravy mix and seasoning; continue to cook until smooth. Add cream of mushroom soup and mix well.
Place pork chops in prepared cafeteria pans; Cover with mushroom soup mixture. Seal ONE pan with plastic wrap and foil and bake in oven for 50 minutes.For the second pan set label and date put in the fridge and BATCH COOK it later!!!!

Submitter: 
CrystalRE
Description: 
Savory baked pork chops in an onion and mushroom sauce. Easy, fast and my kids like them.  If out of fresh onions you can mix dried onions into the mushroom soup or dried onion soup.Modified from: Smothered Pork Chops
