Title:
Ultimate Healthy Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 cup white flour
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 cup wheat germ
1/4 cup oat bran
2 eggs
3 or 4 very ripe bananas
1/2 cup applesauce
1/8 cup vanilla
1/4 cup milk
1/8 cup honey
1 cup walnuts

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix all of the dry ingredients (salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, white flour, whole wheat flour, baking soda, wheat germ, and oat bran) in a bowl and set aside.
Mash the bananas in a second bowl and mix in the eggs applesauce, vanilla, milk, and honey.
Mix the dry ingredients into the banana mixture.
Add the walnuts and mix thoroughly.
Pour the batter into 3 greased mini loaf pans and bake for 40-50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Sara
Description: 
This is a wonderful moist banana bread that is full of nuts and banana flavor.  Enjoy!
