Title:
Firecracker Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1 med onion, chopped
1 15 oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
2 tbsp chili powder
3 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp salt
4 7-inch flour tortillas
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can tomatoes and chilies
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Brown ground beef with onion; drain.  Add black beans, chili powder, cumin,and salt to beef and onion.  Pour mixture into a greased 9X13 baking dish.  Arrange the tortillas over the top of the beef mixture.  In a small bowl, mix the tomatoes and chilies and cream of mushroom soup together and spread over the tortillas.  Top with cheddar cheese.  Bake uncovered for 25 to 30 minutes.
