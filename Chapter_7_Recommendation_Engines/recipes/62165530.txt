Title:
Hawaiian Tropical Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 bananas, mashed
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3/4 cup white sugar
1 1/2 cups heavy cream, chilled
2/3 cup cold milk
1/4 cup flaked coconut
1/4 cup pineapple tidbits
2 tablespoons pecans, chopped

Directions:
Mash bananas with a potato masher.  Bananas do not have to be perfectly smooth.  Add vanilla, sugar, cream and milk.  Mix well.  Pour into ice cream maker.  Half way during the freezing process when batter is thickened add coconut, pineapple and pecans.  Pour into container and let ripen for at least 12 hours.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
I love fruit and wanted to come up with a great ice cream that incorporated many different tropical fruits!  This ice cream is so creamy and perfect for summer!
