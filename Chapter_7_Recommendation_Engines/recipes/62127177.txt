Title:
Easy Mash & Hash Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound package frozen shredded hashbrowns
4 ounce package "homestyle" mashed potato flakes
4 cup package of shredded mexican cheese blend, divided
3/4 cup milk and/or cream
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F. Spray a 9" x 13" baking dish with cooking spray.
In a large mixing bowl, place frozen potatoes, breaking up any large clumps.  Toss potatoes with about 2/3 of the cheese and the mash potato flakes. Add salt & pepper to taste.
Place potato mixture in baking dish, drizzle milk or cream over it evenly.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on top.
Cover with foil that has been sprayed with cooking spray.  Bake for one hour.  Remove foil, bake 10-15 minutes more until top is golden.

Submitter: 
LARANEFF
Description: 
This is a super easy potato casserole, that turns out hearty, cheesy, creamy & delicious.  It's a crowd pleaser & requires almost no cooking ability.  It's creamier with heavy cream, but it is good with milk, I use whatever cream I have on hand & add milk if it is short.
