Title:
Whole Wheat Oatmeal Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup whole wheat flour
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cups quick cooking oats
3 tablespoons flaxseed
1 cups skim milk
1 1/2 eggs, beaten
1/8 cup unsweetened applesauce
1/4 cup frozen blueberries

Directions:
Preheat a lightly oiled griddle over medium heat.
In a large bowl, mix whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, flaxseed, brown sugar, baking powder, and salt.
In a small bowl, mix oats and skim milk. Whisk in eggs and applesauce. Pour into the flour mixture all at once. Continue mixing until smooth. Gently fold in blueberries.
Pour batter about 1/4 cup at a time onto the prepared griddle. Cook 1 to 2 minutes, until bubbly. Flip, and continue cooking until lightly browned.
