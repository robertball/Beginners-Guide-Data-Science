Title:
Classic Greek Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. Greek seasoning
2 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
2 Tbsp. white wine vinegar
½ cup olive oil
¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
1 head Romaine lettuce
½ cup black pitted olives
1 cup cherry (or other) tomatoes, cut in half
½ medium red onion, cut into rings
1 medium cucumber, peeled, seeded, halved and sliced
Feta cheese

Directions:
Blend together dressing and stir in cheese. Refrigerate 15 minutes.
Tear lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Pour in tomatoes, olives, and cucumbers.
At last second, stir in dressing. Garnish with onions and feta cheese.
