Title:
Parsley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tomatoes
4 T olive oil
1 leek, finely chopped
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1 bunch of parsley, chopped
1 C white wine
salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
Sautee veges in the olive oil add white wine and simmer.
Puree and serve hot.
