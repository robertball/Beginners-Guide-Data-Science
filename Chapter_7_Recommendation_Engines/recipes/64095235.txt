Title:
My BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup ketchup
1/2 cup molasses
6 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup white vinegar
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
dash liquid smoke
cayenne pepper (to taste)
cracked pepper (to taste)
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
corn starch (optional)

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over low heat, mix the ketchup, molasses, lemon juice, balsamic vinegar, white vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, liquid smoke. Blend in the brown sugar, dry mustard, cracked pepper, and cayenne pepper until dissolved. Simmer 10 minutes, until thickened. Remove from heat, and allow to cool before using on meats as desired.

Submitter: 
Rachael Welch
Description: 
Try this thick, tangy sauce on ribs, steak, or whatever you feel like tossing on the grill!Modified from: This and That BBQ Sauce
