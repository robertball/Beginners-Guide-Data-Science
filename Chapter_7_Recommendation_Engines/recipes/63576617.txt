Title:
Deep Fried Calamari Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups calamari
3/4 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup flour
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Let calamari soak in buttermilk for several hours.
Combine flour, salt and pepper.
Dip calamari in breading mixture, deep fry and enjoy!
Delicious with a spicy mayo dip.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
Great for a deep-fry party!
