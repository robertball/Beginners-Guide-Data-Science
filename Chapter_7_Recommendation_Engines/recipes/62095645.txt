Title:
Slushies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C cold water
20 ice cubes
1 packet unsweetened fruit drink mix
3/4 C. sugar

Directions:
Place 5 of the ice cubes in the water, to make it very cold.  Place ice cubes, sugar, and drink mix in blender.  Add ice water.  Blend for one or two minutes until ice cubes are completely crushed.  Pour into glasses and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Tanya Belt
Description: 
My husband and I have a summer addiction to slushes and we found a recipe to make them quickly and inexpensively at home!! So refreshing on a hot summer day or balmy evening.
