Title:
Big Ray's Cheese & Crab Brunch Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1213458.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbl Dijon mustard
6 English muffins, split
8 oz imitation crabmeat, drained
2 Tbl lemon juice
2 tsp grated lemon peel
2 cups (8 oz) shredded cheddar cheese
12 eggs
1 cup half-and-half cream
1 cup milk
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp pepper
2 cups (8 oz) shredded Swiss cheese
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
4 green onions, chopped
1/2 cup finely chopped bell pepper, chopped
Read more:

Directions:
Spread mustard over bottom half of muffins. Place in a greased 13x9-in. baking dish. Top with crab, lemon juice and peel. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese. Top with muffin tops; set aside.
In a large bowl, whisk eggs, cream, milk, mayonnaise, salt, cayenne and pepper. Pour over muffins; sprinkle with Swiss cheese, Parmesan cheese, onions and peppers. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before baking. Preheat oven to 375Â°. Cover and bake 30 minutes. Uncover; bake 20-25 minutes longer or until set. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
