Title:
Grandmama Fuller's panut butter cake and frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1/4 cup peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups self-rising flour
1 1/3 cups sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup butter
1/2-3/4 cup milk
Frosting:
1/4 cup peanut butter
1/4 cup butter
2 1/2 cups powdered sugar
3 TBSP milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Cake:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cream butter and sugar until smooth, add peanut butter, vanilla, and eggs.  Beat well.  Add flour alternately with milk to creamed mixture, mixing well after each addition.  Pour into 2,9-inch cake pans,that have been greased abd floured.  Bake for 35-45 minutes.  Cool 10 minutes then remove from pans onto cooling rack to finish cooling.
Frosting:
Cream butter.  Blend peanut butter, vanilla and salt to butter, add sugar alternately with milk. Beat until light and fluffy then spread on cooled cake.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
This is the best peanut butter cake I have ever eaten, and I love peanut butter. It tastes like a peanut butter cookie with a great peanut butter frosting that is not the glossy type.
