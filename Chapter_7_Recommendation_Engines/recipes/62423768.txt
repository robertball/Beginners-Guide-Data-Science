Title:
Linguine With Clam Sauce and Portobello Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz bottle clam sauce
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 shallots diced
4 garlic cloves chopped
1 8oz package slice mushrooms (portobello)
1 1lb box of barilla linguine
1/4 cup of white wine (Yellow Tail Chardonnay)
2 10oz Cento whole baby clams
2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup butter
1 tablespoon dried basil
1 tablespoon dried oregano
2 chicken bullion cubes
3 tablespoons kosher salt

Directions:
Bring large pot of water to boil. Meanwhile in a large skillet, heat olive oil, then add shallots, garlic and mushrooms. Cook until mushrooms are tender.  When water boils, add 3 tablespoons of kosher salt, then add linguine to boiling water, cook until aldente(about 8 mins).  De-glaze skillet with white wine, then add claim juice, basil, oregano, butter, worcestershire, bullion cubes and clams.  Simmer about 20 minutes.  Drain pasta & add to clam sauce.  Finish pasta in sauce about 2 minutes and serve. Salt and pepper to taste.
