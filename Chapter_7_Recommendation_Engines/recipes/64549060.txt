Title:
Taco Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs of ground beef
1 onion -chopped
10 oz can diced tomatoes w/ green chiles
28 oz can crushed tomatoes
1.5 cups frozen corn (thawed)
15 oz can black beans (rinsed and drained)
1 TBSP chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
Toppings - As desired --- Tortilla chips, cilantro, cheddar cheese, sour cream, avacado, etc

Directions:
1. Brown ground beef and onion together.  Drain
2. Mix all  ingredients including beef mixture in large pot except toppings and bring to a simmer
3. Simmer for 30 minutes
Server desired toppings

Submitter: 
Cheryl Thompson
Description: 
Thick like stew.  Can easily be made in crockpot instead of simmering.
