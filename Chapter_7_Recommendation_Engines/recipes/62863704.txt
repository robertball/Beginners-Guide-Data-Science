Title:
Cajun Chicken Strips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T AP flour
1/2 tsp poultry seasoning
3/4 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 1/2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 T butter
2 T olive oil

Directions:
Cup chicken breasts into 1/2" strips.  Combine all dry ingredients in resealable bag.  Add chicken in 2 batches.  Shake to coat.  In a large skillet, melt butter and oil.  Cook chicken 8-10 minutes.  Garnic with fresh parsley.
