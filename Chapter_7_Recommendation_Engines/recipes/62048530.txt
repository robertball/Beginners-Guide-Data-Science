Title:
Play Doh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Salt
2 Tbsp. Powdered Alum
2 cups Flour
1 1/2 cups Water
1 Tbsp. Cooking oil
Food Coloring

Directions:
Dissolve salt in water on slow heat. Add food coloring, alum, oil and then flour. Work with spoon until not sticky.
