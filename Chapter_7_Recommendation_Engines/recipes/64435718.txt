Title:
Giant Bubble Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups water
1/2 cup blue Dawn dish detergent - original
1/2 cup corn starch
1 Tbsp baking powder (not baking soda)
1 Tbsp Glycerine

Directions:
Mix cornstarch in the water, stirring very well.
Gently stir in the remaining ingredients without making froth
Allow mixture to sit for at least an hour.
Stir occasionally if needed
use giant bubble wands/rods

Submitter: 
PlaygroundHunt
Description: 
Make giant bubble mix to use with giant bubble wands: 100% cotton rope without center through straws
