Title:
Pumpkin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1571010.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2 cups all purpose flour
2 15oz cans pure pumkin puree
1.5 cups white sugar
2tsp pure vanilla extract, divided
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2tsp pumpkin pie seasoning
1tsp baking powder
1tsp baking soda
1tsp salt
3oz cream cheese, softened
2c sifted confectioners sugar
1/2 c butter, softened

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. In a large bowl, mix the eggs, sugar, oil, 1tsp vanilla and pumpkin with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, pie seasoning and salt. Stir into the pumpkin mixture until thoroughly combined.
3. Spread the batter evenly into an ungreased 10x15 inch jellyroll pan. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in preheated oven. Cool completely before frosting.
4.To make the frosting, cream together the cream cheese and butter. Stir in vanilla. Add confectioners' sugar a little at a time, beating until mixture is smooth. Spread evenly on top of the cooled bars. Cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Christine W
Description: 
I created this recipe when I longed for a pumpkin bar that wasn't cake like, but was more like a pumpkin pie brownie. These aren't too sweet and have been a huge hit!
