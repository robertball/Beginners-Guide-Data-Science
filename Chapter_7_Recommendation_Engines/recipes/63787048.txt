Title:
Salmon with Maple-Lemon Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp lemon juice
2 tbsp maple syrup
1 tbsp cider vinegar
1 tbsp canola oil
4 (6-ounce) salmon fillets
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
cooking spray
Large zip top plastic bag

Directions:
1. Preheat broiler.
2. Combine first 4 ingredients (lemon juice, maple syrup, cider vinegar, canola oil) in a large zip-top plastic bag. Add fish to bag; seal. Refrigerate 10 minutes, turning bag once.
3. Remove fish from bag, reserving marinade. Place marinade in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave at HIGH for 1 minute.
4. Coat broiler pan with cooking spray. Place fish on pan. Sprinkle fish with salt and pepper. Brush marinade evenly over fish. Place in oven and broil for 5 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork or until desired degree of doneness.

Submitter: 
Rachel Blum
Description: 
Someone told me about this recipe, I tried it and was surprised how good it tasted! It yields very moist salmon.
