Title:
Black Bean Guaca-salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 ripe avacados, pitted and diced
2 medium to large tomatoes (1 cup equivalent) seeded & diced
1/2 vidalia or sweet onion, diced
3/4 cup of canned black beans, drained and rinsed
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
juice of 3-4 limes
salt & white pepper to taste

Directions:
To a large non-reactive bowl combine diced,seeded tomatoes,diced onion, minced garlic and black beans.
Add diced avocado.  Juice fresh limes over the avacado and combine with other ingredients.  cover adn refrigerate for 1 hours to allow flavours to combine.  Serve with tortilla chips, crudite or pita.

Submitter: 
Ellen Violet-Muir
Description: 
This easy, HEALTHY appetizer has been requested by neighbours, co-workers and friends alike!  I like to serve it with multi-grain chips.
