Title:
Fruity Tapioca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups water
1 cup sugar
1/3 cup quick cooking tapioca
1 6oz can frozen orange jc concentrate, thawed
1 29oz can sliced peaches, drained and diced
1 11oz can mandarin oranges, drained

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine water, sugar and tapioca; let stand 5 minutes.
Bring to a full rolling boil.
Remove from heat; stir in o.j. concentrate.
Cool for 20 min.
Stir in fruit.
Transfer to a serving bowl.
Refrigerate till serving.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A light fruit-filled tapioca dessert.
