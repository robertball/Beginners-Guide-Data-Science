Title:
Swewet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked mashed sweet potatoes
2 eggs
1 stick margarine
1/3 cup milk
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
Topping:
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup flour
1/4 cup margarine
1 cup finely chopped pecans

Directions:
Whip potatoes, margarine and milk until fluffy. Beat in eggs, sugar and vanilla. Spread in a 9x9 inch pan.
Topping:
Blend ingredients until crumbly. Spread over top of sweet potatoes.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Brad Miller
Description: 
Sweet Potato casserole with crunchy pecan topping.
