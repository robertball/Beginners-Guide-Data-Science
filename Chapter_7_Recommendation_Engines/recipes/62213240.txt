Title:
Mrs. Baslow's Molasses Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup molasses
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp ginger
2 cups flour

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients.
Mix and stir in dry ingredients.
Drop by teaspoonsful onto cookie sheets.
Bake at 350 for 10 to 12 minutes.

Submitter: 
SIXMITTS
Description: 
These were Erin's favorite Vermont cookies. She would walk into Mrs. Baslow's house, roll her eyes toward the cookie jar on top of the refrigerator and say, I peaz have a little tookie?
