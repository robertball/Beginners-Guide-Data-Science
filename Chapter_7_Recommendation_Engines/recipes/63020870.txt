Title:
Carmel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. corn syrup
2 sticks oleo
1 t. salt
1 t. soda
6 qts. popped corn

Directions:
Cook first 5 ingredients over low heat for 5 minutes.
Remove and pour over popcorn in shallow pan.
Place in 200 deg. oven for 1 hour. Stir every 15 minutes
