Title:
Divine Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1056903.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
3 cups sugar
3 eggs
2 cups mashed ripe bananas
1 (10oz) can crushed pineapple
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 cup (vegetable or canola) oil

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in hand, bake at 350 for 1 hour.  Will fit in large (greased) pirex baking glassware, pampered chef lasagna stoneware, or at least 2 greased loaf pans.

Submitter: 
Melissa LaRose Strunc
Description: 
This recipe is extremely easy, and makes the most delicious, moist banana bread I have ever tasted!  My old Indian babysitter made it for us once years ago, and left this simple recipe with me.  It turns out fantastic every time!
