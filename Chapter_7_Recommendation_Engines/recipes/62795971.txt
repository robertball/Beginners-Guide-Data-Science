Title:
Best Blonde Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/654223.jpg
Ingredients:
2 2/3 cups sifted all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups packed brown sugar
1 cup butter, softened
3 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup whole walnuts

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Lightly grease a 9x13 pan.
Sift flour into measuring cup and then add baking powder and salt. Set aside.  In large bowl, beat together butter, sugar, egg and vanilla until smooth.
Sift in the flour mixture and mix well. Add the nuts and mix with a spoon until well blended. Spread evenly in prepared pan.  Bake 25-30 minutes or until surface springs back when gently pressed. Cool completely before cutting.
