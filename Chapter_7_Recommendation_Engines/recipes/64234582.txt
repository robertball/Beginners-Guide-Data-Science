Title:
Peanut Butter Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c butter
2 c graham cracker crumbs
2 c confectioner's sugar
1 c peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
1 1/2 c semi-sweet chocolate chips
4 Tbsp peanut butter

Directions:
Melt 1 c butter, combine with 2 c graham cracker crumbs...
Blend with fork until crumbly...
Add 1 c peanut butter...
Mix till well blended...
Press in 9x13 pan
In double-broiler, or you can use a pan of simmering water and a metal bowl...
Combine 1 1/2 c semi-sweet chocolate chips and 4 Tbsp peanut butter...
Stir until smooth...
Spread chocolate over graham cracker crust mixture...
Refrigerate 1 hour before cutting...
