Title:
Betty's Hot Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 yellow onion, diced
2 cloves chopped garlic (or to taste)
3 c chopped washed & dried fresh spinach
2 T olive oil
1/4-1/2 t garlic powder, or to taste
1/4-1/2 t black pepper, or to taste
salt to taste
4 oz light cream cheese (neufchatel)
3-4 wedges laughing cow french onion cheese

Directions:
Saute onion and bell pepper in olive oil until soft and beginning to brown.  Add garlic and saute 1 min. more.  Add spinach and garlic powder to taste; saute until wilted.  Add salt and black pepper to taste.
Turn heat to low.  Add cheeses and stir until melted and well combined. Delicious as is, or stir in your favorite shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, parmesan, etc.)
Serve warm with tortilla chips, crackers, or crusty bread, and enjoy!

Submitter: 
B L
Description: 
New combination of classic flavors I stumbled on while making frittata.  Makes a delicious dip and is super quick and easy using fresh ingredients.
