Title:
Tomato, Mozzarella, Basil Appetizer Skewerss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-One pack (about 40 or so) cherry, grape or sweet plum tomatoes
-One bunch basil leaves
-One container FRESH Mozzarella balls in olive/herb marinade (you can also buy plain fresh mozzarella and just drizzle olive oil, herbs over it yourself)
-sald and cracked pepper to taste
-40 toothpicks

Directions:
Prep:
1.) Cut the tomatoes in half (you can use all of them or just some depending on the # of people)
2.) Cut each Mozzarella ball in half (reserve marinade for later)
3.) Wash and pat dry basil leaves
Arrangement:
4.) Take one tomato half and skewer onto toothpick. Thread one half Mozzarella piece, one piece of basil folded to fit and then the second half of the tomato.
5.) Arrange on a colorful plate covered with basil leaves (or you can use parslet or romaine lettuce)
6.) drizzle all the skewers with a tiny bit of the remaining marinade or drizlle olive oil on top.
7.) Season with a tiny bit of salt and cracked pepper.
Serve and enjoy!
