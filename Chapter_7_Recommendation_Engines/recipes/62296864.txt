Title:
Texas Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 - 10 corn tortillas
2 cups chicken broth or bouillon
3 cups diced cooked chicken (1 stewed chicken)
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 (10 oz.) can Rotel tomatoes
3 jalapeno peppers, cut into small rings
1 medium onion, sauteed
garlic salt to taste
1/2 pound sharp cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
Soak tortillas in chicken broth till soft.
Line the bottom and sides of a 9 x 13 pan with half the tortillas.
Combine chicken, soups, Rotel, onion, jalapenos, and garlic salt.
Mix well and pour over tortillas.
Cover the top of the mixture with remaining half of tortillas.
Spread grated cheese over the top.
Bake in 350 degree oven until the cheese is melted and mixture is hot.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
This is one of my husband's favorite entrees.  You can adjust the heat by adding or decreasing the jalapeno peppers.
