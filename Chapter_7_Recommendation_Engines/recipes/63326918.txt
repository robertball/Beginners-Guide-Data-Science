Title:
Turkey Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. butter or shortening
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 C. celery, chopped
2 C. cooked turkey, cut in strips or bitesize pieces
6 oz. uncooked fine spaghetti
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1(10oz)can of cream of chicken soup
2 C. turkey broth or canned chicken broth
1(4oz)can sliced mushrooms, drained
1/2 C. grated Parmesan cheese
Paprika

Directions:
Turn electric skillet on 225 degrees or low heat.  Melt butter, add onion and celry and cook until clear. Arrange turkey in layers,add spaghetti.  Mix salt, pepper, chicken soup and broth.  Pour over spaghetti.  Place mushrooms over top.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and paprika.  Cover.  Cook for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Toni Ebert
Description: 
An easy way to use left over turkey.  The original recipie came from a Utah Power & Light cookbook published later 70s
