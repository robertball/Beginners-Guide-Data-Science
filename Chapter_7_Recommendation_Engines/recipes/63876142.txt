Title:
Cherry, Mango Salsa with Black Beans, Plantains and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075259.jpg
Ingredients:
Cherries
1 mango
2 plantains
1 15oz can black beans
1 cup rice
1 small onion
cilantro
vegetable oil

Directions:
1. Cook rice as directed on package.  Prep salsa: remove pits of cherries and roughly chop.  Roughly chop mango, onions and cilantro.  Mix together in a bowl and refridgerate.
2. Slice plantains in thin slices.  Fry in a pan with vegetable oil, flip over, and cook until both sides are golden brown.
3. Drain and rinse black beans.  Heat as directed on can.
4.  Plate black beans and top with rice.  Add Cherry, Mango Salsa and Plantains on top.
