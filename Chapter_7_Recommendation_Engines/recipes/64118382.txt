Title:
Perfect Lemon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 lemon cake mix
* 1 pkg. Jell-O (lemon Jell-O)
* Frosting for drizzle or cool whip
Frosting:
* 1 (8oz.) pkg. cream cheese
* 3 cups powdered sugar
* 1 teaspoon lemon extract

Directions:
1)Using 2 round cake pans, bake cake as instructed on box. When done at room temperature, dump on a pan. Then use toothpick or fork to punch holes in cake.
2)Make Jell-O as instructed on box. Pour liquid Jell-O into cake (there will be Jell-O left over, freeze for a snack). Put the cake in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour. Drizzle with frosting.
3)For frosting mix together the cream cheese, powdered sugar and lemon extract until well blended.
