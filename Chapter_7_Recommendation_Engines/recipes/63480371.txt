Title:
Mediterranean Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/949145.jpg
Ingredients:
Whole Wheat Pizza Crust
4oz Basil Pesto
1/2cup Artichoke Hearts (canned, 1/2 can)
1/8cup Kalamata Olives chopped
1/8cup Pepperoncini Peppers sliced
1/4cup Feta Cheese crumbled

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450F or follow Pizza Crust instructions. Spread approx 1/2 jar or approx 4oz Basil Pesto onto crust. Pull apart Artichoke Hearts and disperse evenly around crust. Layer Kalamata olives, Pepperoncini peppers, and then Feta cheese. Bake 10-12 mins.

Description: 
This is a tangy flavorful Mediterranean style pizza inspired by a restaurant version I loved.
