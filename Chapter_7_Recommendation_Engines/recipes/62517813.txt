Title:
Soft fluffy cookie frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 Tablespoons milk

Directions:
Cream butter and add remaining ingredients. Whip until fluffy.

Submitter: 
Rin
Description: 
Frosting will crust slightly just enough to stack for shipping. Yum!
