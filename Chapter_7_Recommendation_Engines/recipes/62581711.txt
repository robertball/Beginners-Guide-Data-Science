Title:
Summer Fettuccini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large tomatoes
1/2 bag of fresh spinach
1/c c fresh chopped basil
2 tsp EVOO
2 large garlic cloves
salt & pepper to taste
1 c sliced mushrooms
1 box fettuccini

Directions:
Chop tomatoes, spinach, basil & garlic & let sit at room temp for 30 min. Cook pasta to al dente. Simmer tomato mixture on med heat for 5-8 min, just long enough to soften. Toss with the drained pasta & top w/ fresh parmesan.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is an easy summer pasta recipe that is nice for sharing.
