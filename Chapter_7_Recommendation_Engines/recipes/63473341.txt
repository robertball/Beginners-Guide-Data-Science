Title:
Bread Machine Honey-Oat-Wheat Bread II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 teaspoons active dry yeast
2 tablespoons molasses
1 1/2 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup soy flour
1 cup rolled oats
1/2 cup sesame seeds
3 tablespoons powdered milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup vegetable oil
3 tablespoons butter, softened
cooking spray

Directions:
Place water in the pan of a bread machine. Mix the all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, soy flour, rolled oats, powdered milk, sesame seeds and salt in a bowl. Add the vegetable oil, honey, molasses, and butter to the flour mixture, and pour the flour mixture on top. Make small indent in flour and add yeast, being careful not to get it wet.
Select the Dough cycle; Set crust to light, press start. When bread is.done, remove from pan and brush crust with melted butter.
