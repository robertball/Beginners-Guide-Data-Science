Title:
Casasva Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/777040.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups grated, peeled yucca
2 eggs, beaten
1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
1 botol Macapona

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 375 degrees F.
Stir the yucca, eggs, evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk, and coconut milk together in a bowl until thoroughly combined; pour into a baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour until the top of the cake is browned, 2 to 3 minutes. Add Sugar coated Macapona, bake for another 15 min.
