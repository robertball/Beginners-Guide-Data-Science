Title:
Bagel Boy's Sour Cream Perogies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/837155.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sour cream
2-3 cups all-purpose flour
5 potatoes, mashed
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
3 slices bacon,crumbled

Directions:
Mix sour cream and flour until it forms a ball that you can roll. Add cheese and bacon to potatoes and mix. Rol out dough and cut into circles about the size of a large canning jar ring. Put a tablespoon of potato mixture on one side of the circle. Fold over and seal. Lay on tea towel for 15 min on each side. Boil for 5 min and then fry in butter. Serve immediately. Also can freeze in ziploc bags after letting them dry on the cloth.
