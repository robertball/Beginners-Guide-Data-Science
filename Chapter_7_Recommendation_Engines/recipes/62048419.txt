Title:
Pizza Tortilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 flour totillas
1  8oz package grated mozarella
1/2 cup grated romano or parmesan cheese
1 jar pizza sauce
Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pre  heat oven to 450 degrees.
On each tortilla spread a scant amout of sauce(to taste)
sprinkle mozzarella evenly over each tortilla
Sprinkle grated cheese evenly(romano or parmesan) over each tortilla
Splash scant amount of olive oil over each pizza.
Season each pizza with salt and pepper to taste.
You can also add anything else you might like.
Bake for 10 minutes on top rack with foil on bottom rack. Bake until desired doneness.
Also, these may be rolled up and baked on a cookie sheet for an additional amount of time. 20-25 minutes?
