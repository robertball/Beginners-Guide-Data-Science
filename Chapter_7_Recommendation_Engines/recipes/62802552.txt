Title:
INCREDIBLE~Raspberry Cheesecake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/656993.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8oz)pkg Cream Cheese, soften
1 egg
1 (17.5oz)pkg Betty Crocker Sugar Cookie mix
2/3 cups Raspberry Flavored Baking Tidbits

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Line baking pans with parchment paper. Set aside until ready to use.
In a mixing bowl, cream the cheese, thoroughly until smooth. Then add the egg and mix until well blended.
Add Cookie mix until cream cheese mixture is well incorperated. Then lightly fold in Raspberry chips, gently.
Using a small cookie dough scooper OR a tablespoon, scoop 1 TBSP dollups on to parchment lined pans. Spacing 2 inches apart. Lightly pat cookie dollup down.
Bake for 9-12 minutes or until lightly golden edges. Cool and enjoy!
