Title:
Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes
seasoning to taste (fresh herbs, seasoning salt, vegetable seasoning mix, etc.)
1/2 tsp. pepper
3 tbsp. (or more) olive or avocado oil
salt

Directions:
Place large cast-iron or other heavy ovenproof skillet in cold oven.
Preheat oven to 475.
Meanwhile, cut potatoes into wedges (approx. 8 wedges/med. or large & 6/small potato).
Toss potatoes with oil, pepper and seasoning.
Add to hot pan.
Roast, tossing occasionally, until browned and crisp, about 1 hour.
Sprinkle with salt to serve.
