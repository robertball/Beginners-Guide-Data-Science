Title:
Easy Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups, prepared chilled quinoa
1 cucumber, diced
1 tomato, diced
1/2 onion, diced
juice from 1/2 of a lemon
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup oil

Directions:
Combine the quinoa, cucumber, tomato and onion in a large bowl.
In a smaller bowl, whisk together lemon juice, salt, pepper and oil.
Pour the oil mixture into the quinoa mixture.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Julia L
Description: 
This is the fastest easiest quinoa salad you will ever make. It is delicious for anybody!!!
