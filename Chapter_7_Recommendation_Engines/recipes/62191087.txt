Title:
Montreal Smoked Meat Flat Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 12-14 inch flour tortilla
2 oz Montreal Smoked Meat (approx 6 thin slices)
1/4 cup Tex Mex Shredded Cheese
2 tbsp Mayo
1 tbsp yellow mustard (to taste)
4 tbsp cooking oil

Directions:
Heat oil in frying pan on Medium heat.
Warm flour tortilla in microwave for 20-30 seconds, until soft and pliable.
Mix mayo and yellow mustard in small bowl until combined. Spread mayo mixture in centre of tortilla, about 1 inch from edge.
Put montreal smoked meat along the middle of the tortilla, keeping away 2 inches from top and bottom and about 4 inches from the sides.
Put shredded cheese directly ontop of meat.
Fold bottom and top of tortilla over meat, then the sides. Place seam down in hot pan and cook on medium for approximately 2-3 minutes until golden brown. Press down on wrap with spatula to flatten while cooking.  Flip and repeat for other side.
Remove from pan and cut in half.
