Title:
Easy chicken tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. boneless skinless chicken breast
1 can Rotel tomatoes
seasonings (optional)
tortillas

Directions:
Spray the slow cooker with non stick spray.
Wash the chicken and place it in the bottom of the slow cooker.
Pour can of Rotel over the chicken.  I always add Tony's and some garlic powder. You can also add a packet of taco seasoning or other seasonings if desired.
Cook on low approximately 4 hours. Shred chicken with forks until all shredded and cook on low for another hour or two.

Submitter: 
Stacey Walker
Description: 
There is nothing to this and since it's cooked in a slow cooker, you don't have to spend much time in the kitchen.
