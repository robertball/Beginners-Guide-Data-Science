Title:
Chef John's Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds extra lean ground beef
1/2 onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 small Serrano pepper, finely chopped
1 cup water
3/4 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 dash Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 cup water
1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste

Directions:
Combine the ground beef and onion in a cold skillet
place the skillet onto a stove burner
turn the heat to medium
cook and stir until the beef is crumbly and browned. Stir the garlic and pepper into the beef mixture;
continue cooking and stirring until the vegetables are tender, 2 to 3 more minutes.
Add water.
Mix in ketchup, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire, salt, and pepper.
Pour in more water.
Bring to a simmer.
Reduce heat to low and cook until the mixture becomes very thick, 30 to 45 minutes.
Adjust salt and pepper as needed.

Submitter: 
Wonder Becky
Description: 
The secret to a great 'Sloppy Joe' is a thick, rich, almost dry consistency, which allows it to be eaten two-fisted, sans fork.Modified from: Chef John's Sloppy Joes
