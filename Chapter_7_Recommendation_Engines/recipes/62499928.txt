Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 to 2 pounds cubed beef stew meat
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed GOLDEN mushroom soup
1/2 tsp salt
ground pepper (to taste)
2 tsp minced garlic
3/4 cup chopped onion
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup beef boullion
1/2 packet onion soup mix
4 ounces cream cheese
1 tbl sour cream
4-6 oz. sliced fresh mushrooms (baby portabellas are great)

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine all ingredients except cream cheese, sour cream and mushrooms.  Cook on Low setting for 5 to 5-1/2 hours. Stir in cream cheese and sour cream.  Sautee mushrooms in butter or margine.  Add to pot and continue on low for another 30 minutes.  Serve over egg noodles.

Submitter: 
Nanci Krolik
Description: 
Stew beef, condensed golden mushroom soup, chopped onion and Worcestershire sauce simmer in a slow cooker. Cream cheese is stirred in just before serving.Modified from: Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff I
