Title:
Spinach & Cottage Cheese Stuffed Manicotti in a 4-Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5826688.jpg
Ingredients:
Filling:
2 1/2 cups 1% cottage cheese
3 cups fresh baby spinach
6 tbsp Derlea minced garlic
3 tsp dried oregano
1 medium onion, diced
1 medium green pepper, minced
18 uncooked manicotti
Sauce:
1 jar Classico 4 cheese Alfredo sauce
about 1 jar of water (to stretch and thin the sauce)
2 tsp oregano
8 tbsp cornstarch
2 cups mozzarella cheese

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 350
2.  in a food processor bowl combine  cheese, spinach, garlic, 3 tsp
oregano, onion and green pepper, and process until mixed.
3. stuff the manicotti until full then place them in the manicotti trays
keep going until all the filling is used up.
4'. take half the jar of sauce add same amount of water then add 4
tbsp of the cornstarch and 1 tsp of oregano into a cup or bowl to
mix then pour onto bottom of 13"x 9" rectangular dish.
5. arrange the filled manicotti into the sauce, the mix the other half of
sauce and water remaining cornstarch and oregano mix and pour
over the top. then top with shredded cheese.
6. bake for 45 minutes
