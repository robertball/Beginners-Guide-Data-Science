Title:
Honey Buns made with Bread Machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup honey
1 1/4 cup water
1 egg beaten
1/2 tsp salt
4 cups flour
2 tsp yeast
1/2 tsp baking powder

Directions:
Put everything in bread maker with the baking powder on top of the flour with the yeast. Set to make dough.
When dough is ready make 14 buns and brush with melted butter and let rise 30 mins then bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 min.
