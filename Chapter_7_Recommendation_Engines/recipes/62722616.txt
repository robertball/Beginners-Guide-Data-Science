Title:
Rhubarb Crunch Revised Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/620729.jpg
Ingredients:
6 c rhubarb, cut into small pieces
1 pint strawberries, sliced
1 peach, sliced
1 1/2 c white sugar
4 tbsp flour
1 c cooking oats
1 1/2 c all-purpose flour
1/2 c butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Lightly grease 9X13" pan.
IN large mixing bowl combine fruit, sugar and 4 tbsp. flour.  Stir well and spread evenly into baking dish. Set aside.
In large bowl combine brown sugar, oats and 1 1/2 c. flour.  Stir well then cut in butter until mixture is crumbly.  Sprinkle mixture over fruit layer.
Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes.
Serve hot with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or serve cold.

Description: 
Use all rhubarb or change it up with some strawberries and peaches.
