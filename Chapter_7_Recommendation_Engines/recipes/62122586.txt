Title:
Teresa's Italian Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of lean ground beef
1/2 lb of lean ground pork
1 egg
1/3 cup of plain bread crumbs
1/2 cup of chopped onion
1 Pkg dry beef seasoning.
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 large clove of garlic finely chopped
1 tbsp Soy based All Purpose Seasoning such as Maggi or Bragg.
1 tbsp dried oregano
1 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
1. Preheat oven 375 F. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.  Using your hands will mix the meatballs in a more evenly fashion.
2. Shape into walnut sized balls and place on a baking pan that will drain away fat and bake for 20 minutes or no longer pink inside.
Serve with your favorite pasta and sauce.

Submitter: 
Sneakyteaky
Description: 
Ground Beef and Ground Pork are used in this original recipe created my a wonderful neighbour who shared it with me.  I made a few changes to make it my own.  The meat balls are very moist and full of flavour.
