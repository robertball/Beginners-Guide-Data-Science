Title:
Baked Horseradish Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium carrots, cut into 3-inch julienne strips
2 Tbsp. mayonnaise
2 tsp. grated onion
2 tsp. prepared horseradish
1/8 tsp. salt
Dash pepper
2 Tbsp. crushed butter-flavored crackers
1 tsp. butter, melted
Dash paprika

Directions:
Place 1 inch of water in a small saucepan; add carrots.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 7 to 9 minutes or until crisp-tender.  Drain, reserving 1 tablespoon liquid.  Place carrots in a 2-cup baking dish.
In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, onion, horseradish, salt, pepper and reserved liquid.  Pour over carrots.
Toss the cracker crumbs, butter and paprika; sprinkle over top carrots.
Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes or until heated through.

Description: 
Source: Cooking for 2 (Aunt Janice).  This is a deliciously different way to dress up carrots on a wintry night.  A little horseradish adds just the right amount of heat.
