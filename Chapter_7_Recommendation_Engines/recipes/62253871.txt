Title:
Pan-fried Steak with Marsala Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/334207.jpg
Ingredients:
Filet or ribeye steaks
Marinade:
Teaspoon honey
Tablespoon dijon mustard
Tobasco
Nutmeg (optional)
Olive oil (substitute Italian dressing or vegetable oil)
3 cloves garlic, crushed
1/3 cup Marsala wine
2/3 cup chicken broth
1 tablespoons butter
1/4 teaspoon rosemary, crushed
Lemon
Mushrooms
Onions

Directions:
Maridade the steak for 24 hours (thanks IMVINTAGE).  Marinade in olive oil, vinegar, dijon, honey, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and a few dashes of tabasco.
Saute mushrooms, onions, garlic.
Heat a large, heavy skillet over high heat for 2 minutes, or until very hot.
Place steaks in hot skillet.  Sear both sides quickly.  Turn down the heat to medium high. Cook for 3-4 minutes each side.
Remove steaks from skillet and cover with aluminum foil to keep warm.
With skillet on medium low heat, add Marsala and chicken broth. Bring to a boil and reduce for 3 minutes. Stir in the brown bits.
Lower heat.  Whisk in butter and rosemary.  Add healthy squeeze of lemon.
Whisk in some flour to thicken.
Transfer the sauted mushroom, onion, garlic into the skillet.
