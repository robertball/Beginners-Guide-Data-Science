Title:
Easy Flourless Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup  peanut butter (crunchy or smooth)
1 cup white sugar
1 egg
3/4 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease a cookie sheet.
In a large bowl, stir together all ingredients. Mix completely, but do not over blend.
Drop tablespoon sized balls of dough onto cookie sheet. Using a fork, lightly press on balls to flatten slightly. Do this twice so that a checkered pattern is formed on surface of cookie.
Bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until golden brown.
