Title:
Grilled Shrimp Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup light olive oil
1/4 cup lemon juice
3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup minced garlic
ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1 1/2 lbs shrimp
Change Measurements: US | Metric

Directions:
Directions:Prep Time: 10 minsTotal Time: 45 mins
1In a medium, non-reactive bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, parsley, garlic, black pepper and crushed red pepper.2Set aside.3Thread thawed shrimp onto skewers and cover with marinade for 30 minutes.4Preheat grill for high heat.5Grill for about 2 to 3 minutes on each side, or until done and brush with remaining marinade leftover in the pan.
