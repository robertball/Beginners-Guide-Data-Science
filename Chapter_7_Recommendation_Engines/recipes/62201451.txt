Title:
Pot Chocolat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 C milk
2 C Chocolate chips
2 Eggs
1/4 C Almonds crushed (optional)
Whipping Cream- whipped, unsweetened

Directions:
Boil Milk
While milk is boiling, add chocolate chips to eggs in a food processor and pulse twice.
Add boiling milk to chocolate and egg mixture and blend for one minute, until chocolate chips have melted.
Funnel mixture into small cups or ramekins and chill for at least two hours
Add crushed almonds and unsweetened whipped cream before serving.
