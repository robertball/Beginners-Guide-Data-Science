Title:
Indoor BBQ Hamburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 hamburger patties
1 T + 1 tsp. worcestershire sauce
2 T white vinegar
1 T + 1 tsp. granulated sugar
2/3 c. ketchup
1/4 c. chopped onion

Directions:
In a small bowl, thoroughly mix the Worcestershire, vinegar, sugar, ketchup and onions.  Set aside.
Pan fry the burgers until they become brown on both sides.
Pour the sauce in with the patties and reduce heat.  Continue cooking until the sauce reduces and thickens.
Serve on Potato Hamburger Buns spooning the sauce over the burgers.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
BBQ Hamburgers cooked indoors completely so you don't have to stand in the cold.
