Title:
Golden Pumpkin and Coconut soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans coconut milk, shaken
1 thai kibacha squash,peeled and cubed
1 spaghetti squash, peeled and cubed
3 red potatoes, peeled cubed
2 yellow potatoes, peeled, cubed
1 sweet onion (hermiston) chopped
6 cloves garlic, crushed
2 stalks lemon grass, finely minced
1 cup basil, torn
10 mint leaves, torn
1/2 cup cilantro, torn
3 cups chicken stock
3 small pieces ginger, peeled and minced
2tbs sesame oil

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large pan or wok.
Cook over medium heat until kibacha squash is done and spaghetti squash has started to separate.
Serve hot with finely sliced scallions and a dollop of coconut cream.
Soup can be pureed as well.
