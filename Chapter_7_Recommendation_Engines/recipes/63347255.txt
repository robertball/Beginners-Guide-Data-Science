Title:
Trailer Trash Three Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/889591.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium onions diced
1 cup chopped cilantro
1 - 14.5 oz. can Diced Tomatoes with Basil, Garlic and Oregano
2 - 14.5 oz. cans Diced Tomatoes with Zesty Mild Green Chilies
2 - 15.5 oz. cans Garbanzo Beans
2 - 15.5 oz. cans Cannellini Beans
2 - 15.5 oz. cans of Black Beans
Black pepper to taste
Make it spicy if you like by adding one 4 oz. can of hot diced green

Directions:
In a large bowl add diced onions and cilantro. Open all 10 cans, dump tomatoes in bowl with your diced onions and cilantro. Dump all the beans in a strainer and rinse then add these to the bowl and mix well with a big old spoon. Taste and add black pepper till you know it's right. Should not need salt, but that's up to you and your Doctor!

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
Always be sure your vehicle is mobile and your home isn't. Just open up 10 cans and dump'em! Excellent snack for those back door guest you didn't expect. Break open the case of beer Baba brought home. Add some chips with this giant bowl of salsa and you have made friends for life.
