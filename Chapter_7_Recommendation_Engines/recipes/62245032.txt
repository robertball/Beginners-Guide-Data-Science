Title:
Chilli poulet en papillote Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 tbsp chilli powder
1 tbsp paprika
1/2 tsp Cayenne pepper (or more or less depending how spicy you like it)
1 tsp dried oregeno
1/2 tsp black pepper (optional)
4 12X12 pieces of parchment paper

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix together.
Evenly and generously coat the chicken breast and place 1 breast per parchment paper.
Wrap the chicken breast in the parchment paper like you would a burrito.
Place on a baking pan in the oven at 350F for 25 mins until chicken is done.
let cool for 5 mins.
careful when unwrapping the chicken as you don't want to get any hot juices on you, it will burn.
Bon appetite!

Submitter: 
aenebe
Description: 
This parchment paper baked chicken is great hot or cold, on salads rice or on its own, you can make this as spicy as you like. Tastes amazing really juicy, quick, and it's healthy!!!
