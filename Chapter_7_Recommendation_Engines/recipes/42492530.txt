Title:
Toffee Nut Chocolate Clusters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1079149.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk chocolate chip morsels
1/2 cup pecan pieces
1/2 cup walnut pieces
1/2 cup sliced almonds
1/2 cup toffee bits
1/4 cup dried cherries

Directions:
Place chocolate chips in a medium microwave-safe bowl.
Microwave on 50 percent power (or defrost setting) for 2 to 4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until chocolate is melted.
Stir in remaining ingredients.
Spoon heaping teaspoon-size portions of the chocolate clusters into 1-inch foil or paper candy cups.
Refrigerate for 15 to 30 minutes, or until firm.
