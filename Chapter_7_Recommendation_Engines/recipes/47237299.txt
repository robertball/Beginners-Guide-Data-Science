Title:
Grandmother's Buttermilk Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 pound butter
3/4 cup honey
2 eggs
1 cup buttermilk
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8 inch square pan.
Melt butter and honey in large skillet. Quickly add eggs and beat until well blended. Combine buttermilk with baking soda and stir into mixture in pan. Stir in cornmeal, flour, and salt until well blended and few lumps remain. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
