Title:
Curry Pasta - Pakistani Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 Red Bell Peppers
3 Curry Leaves
1/2 KG Boneless Chicken Cubes
1 TBS Ginger Garlic Paste
1 tsp Turmeric Powder
2 tsp Red Chili Powder
4 TBS Roasted Chick Pea Flour
1 Cup coconut powder
3-4 Green Chilies
1 Packet Spagetti (or pasta of your choice)
Fresh Coriander
Lemon

Directions:
Fry whole red bell peppers with curry leaves and add the bonless chicken.
Fry the chicken till it is white and the juices run clear.
Add ginger garlic paste to the fried chicken and fry further more.
Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, chick pea flour and cook well till all the spices are well-cooked. Add a little water to ensure that the spices do not stick to the bottom of the pan.
Dissolve the coconut powder in one cup of water and add to the chicken.
Prepare spagetti according to the package instructions and take them out in a serving bowl. Pour the chicken mix all over the spagetti and garnish with finely chopped green chilies, fresh coriander and lemon slices.
