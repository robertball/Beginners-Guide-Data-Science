Title:
Le Poulet Royal de Louisiane Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3Tbs extra virgin olive oil
2Tbs unsalted butter
2 Lbs Boneless chicken breast in 1" cubes
1 med yellow pepper cut in strips
1 med green pepper cut in strips
1 med red pepper cut in strips
1 large onion roughly chopped
2 cloves of garlic minced
2 Tbs Luzianne Cajun Seasoning
2 C heavy cream
16 oz Penne rigate pasta cooked al dente

Directions:
Add olive oil and butter to large fry pan on medium heat. Brown chicken. Add onions, garlic and peppers, and cajun seasoning (You may want to adjust this to taste, we like it spicey) . Sautee until onions and peppers are softened, yet still firm. Add heavy cream and reduce untill sauce is thickened.  Add pasta and enjoy!
