Title:
Cherry Yum Yum Pie Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
1 (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
1 (5 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
Â½ cup chopped walnuts
1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
2 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crusts

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cherry pie filling, pineapple, sweetened condensed milk, and chopped nuts. Fold in whipped topping until no streaks remain. Spoon into pie crusts. Chill for 1 hour.

Description: 
A quick and delicious pie. It's a fluffy, cherry, pineapple, and nut pie, not too sweet and not heavy. In a graham cracker crust.
