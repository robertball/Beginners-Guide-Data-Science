Title:
Pina Colada Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup crushed pineapple
1/4 cup milk
2 cups vanilla frozen yogurt
1/2 teaspoon coconut extract

Directions:
Place pineapple, milk, yogurt and coconut extract in a blender container.  Make sure the lid is securely attached.  Whirl until all ingredients are well-blended.
