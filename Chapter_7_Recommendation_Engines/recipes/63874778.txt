Title:
Spinach Pomegranate Pear Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) bag baby spinach leaves, rinsed and drained
1/4 red onion, sliced very thin
1/2 cup walnut pieces
1/2 cup crumbled feta
2 Asian pears
1 pomegranate, peeled and seeds separated
4 tablespoons balsamic vinaigrette

Directions:
Place spinach in a salad bowl.  Halve and core the pear, then cut each half in slices. Top spinach with red onion, walnuts, feta, and pears. Sprinkle pomegranate seeds over the top, and drizzle with vinaigrette.
