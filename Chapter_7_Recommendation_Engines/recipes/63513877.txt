Title:
Greek Layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans garbanzo beans
sesame tahini
lemon juice
3/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
3/4 cup peeled and diced cucumber
1/2 cup diced fresh tomato
1/2 cup finely chopped Kalamata olives
1/4 cup olive oil
1 teaspoon dried oregano

Directions:
Combine garbanzo beans and tahini in food processor to make hummus.  Add lemon juice to taste.  Spread the hummus in a smooth layer in an 8x8-inch baking dish.
Layer the feta cheese, cucumber, tomato, and Kalamata olives on top.
Whisk together the olive oil and oregano in a small bowl, then drizzle over the Kalamata olives. Refrigerate up 24 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Ciara Gavin
Description: 
This 5-layer dip is full of bright and briny flavors. Serve it with pita chips or crackers for a deliciously Greek-inspired snack.Modified from: Greek Layer Dip
