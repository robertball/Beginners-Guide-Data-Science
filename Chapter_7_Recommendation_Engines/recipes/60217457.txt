Title:
Shrimp Cocktail Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz. +/- soft cream cheese
1 T. minced onion flakes
1 T. lemon juice
1 T. worcestershire sauce
1 T. horseradish
1 small jar shrimp cocktail sauce
Shrimp ( use canned that you rinse, drain well and chill, or use fresh, diced)

Directions:
Mix together the first 5 ingredients.
Spread on a large platter.
Chill.
Just before serving, spread cocktail sauce over cheese mixture.
Top with diced shrimp.
Serve with crackers for spreading.
