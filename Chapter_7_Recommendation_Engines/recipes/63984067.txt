Title:
Mediterranean Twist Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Salmon:
2 salmon filets
1 tsp. olive oil
For the Sauce:
1 clove garlic, minced
6 fresh basil leaves, chopped
2 tbsp. olive oil
1 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1/2-1 cup (depending on how much you like) fresh tomatoes, chopped

Directions:
In a medium saucepan heat the olive oil (for the salmon) for about one minute. Place salmon in saucepan and let cook completely.
In a separate medium saucepan heat olive oil (for the sauce) over medium heat for about 1 minute. Add garlic and let sit for another minute. Add tomatoes and let sit for 5 minutes. Add balsamic vinegar and finally add the basil. Let sit for 3 minuets over heat so flavors can blend.
To serve put the salmon on a plate and pour sauce over it. Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
A little twist on your basic salmon recipe. It's healthy and delicious.  A real crowd pleaser! Make extra, because everyone will want seconds!
