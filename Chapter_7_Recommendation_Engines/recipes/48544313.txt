Title:
King Ranch Casserole (Tex-Mex) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C chicken broth
1C skim milk
1/2C flour
14oz can diced tomatoes, drained
1/2C plain yogurt
4oz can chopped green chilies, drained
1/4C fresh chopped parsley
1T chili powder
1t oregano
1/2t cumin
salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2t oil
1 large onion, chopped
1 green or red bell pepper,diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2C chopped cooked chicken
10 corn tortillas, cut quartered
1/2C shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees
Bring chicken broth to simmer in med. pot. Whisk together milk and flour in sm. bowl to make smooth paste. Add to the broth and cook over med., whisking constantly, until thick and smooth, about 3 min. Remove from heat and stir in yogurt, tomatoes, chilies, parsley, chili powder, oregano and cumin. Season to taste w/salt and pepper. Set aside
Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over med-high heat. Add onion, bell pepper and garlic,stirring occ., until tender-crisp about 3 min.
Line bottom of shallow 3-qt dish with 1/2 of tortillas. Top with 1/2 the chicken, and 1/2 onion mixture. Spoon 1/2 of sauce evenly over top. Repeat layers w/remaining tortillas, chicken, onion mixture and sauce. Sprinkle w/cheese and bake until bubbly, 25-30 min.
