Title:
Garlic Spread for  Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/656891.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 tablespoon minced garlic (or 1 teaspoon garlic powder)
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (if on hand)
1 tablespoon garlic salt
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning (if on hand)
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground paprika (if on hand)

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine softened butter, minced garlic and parmesan cheese. Season with garlic salt, Italian seasoning, pepper and paprika. Mix ingrediants together.
Spread garlic butter on Texas toast buns, rolls or bread.
For toasted bread, bake uncovered in pre-heated 350 degree oven for 12 mins or until toasted.
For soft garlic bread, cover buttered buns in foil or in a pan covered with foil and bake about 15 minutes or until warmed.

Submitter: 
Philmont
Description: 
Use on Texas Toast rolls or buns to make garlic bread.
