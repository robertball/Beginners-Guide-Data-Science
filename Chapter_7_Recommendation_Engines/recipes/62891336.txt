Title:
PERESHKY [SAUERKRAUT BUNS] Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. yeast
1/2 cup warm water
1 tsp.sugar
1 cup sour cream
1/2 lb.shortening
1/2 tsp.salt
3 Tbsp.sugar
4 cups flour
3 eggs,beaten
FILLING:
1/2 lb.hamberger
2 cups chopped sauerkraut
1/2 cup chopped onion
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Dissolve yeast in warm water and 1 tsp.sugar.Let stand for 10 min.then stir well.
Combine sour cream and eggs.Mix shortening,salt,sugar and flour together as for pie crust.Combine with yeast,sour cream and eggs;mix with a wooden spoon.Beat well.Cover with wax paper and a dinner plate.Refridgerate overnight.
In morning prepare filling:
Fry hamberger and onions drain all grease.
Scald sauerkraut in boiling water,drain and press out all water.Add to hamberger mixture and set aside.
Roll out dough to 1/4 inch thickness and cut into circles,1-1 1/2 inches in diameter.
Place about 1/2 tsp. or so of filling in the centre,fold in half and pinch together.
Place on greased baking sheet with sealed edges down.Let rise in warm place for 20-30 min.
Bake in preheated 375 oven for 15-20 min.
