Title:
Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground hamburger
1/4 c chopped onions
1 1/2 pkg of tater tots
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1/2 pkg of mixed vegetables
1 soup can milk

Directions:
Brown the onion and hamburger; drain. Mix the soups, vegetables, and milk together. Put into a 9 x 13 pan. Top with tator tots. Back for 1 - 1 1/2 hrs at 350 degrees.
