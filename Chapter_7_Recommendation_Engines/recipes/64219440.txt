Title:
Olive Garden Toscana Soup-my version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp. olive oil
1 lb. Italian Sweet Sausage Patties crumbles
5 slices bacon, diced
1 small onion diced
40 oz. chicken broth
2 cups water
1 1/2 lb Russet Potatoes, scrubbed, rinsed and 1/16 inch thick slices
1 tsp. fennel seeds
1/2 tsp. salt or to taste
1/2 tsp. ground pepper
2 c. half and half
1 c.lightly packed kale leaves, cut up
2 chicken flavor boosts.

Directions:
heat olive oil in large saucepan over medium heat.  Saute onion and bacon. Crumble sausage and add to onion.  Let cook until sausage is no longer raw.  Add chicken broth, water, sliced potatoes, fennel seeds,salt and pepper.  Bring soup up to just before a boil.  Add chicken flavors.  Temper Half and half.  Add half and half, flavor boosts.  Add seasoning and simmer for 25 minutes.  Serve warm with Grated Italian cheese.

Submitter:

Very similar to Olive Garden's Zuppa Toscana Soup.
