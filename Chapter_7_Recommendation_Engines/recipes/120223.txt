Title:
Sesame Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/109315.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup water
3 tablespoons sesame seeds, toasted
1 garlic clove, minced
crushed red pepper flakes
4 bone-in chicken breast halves

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the first six ingredients; add chicken. Seal bag and refrigerate overnight. Drain and discard marinade. Grill chicken, skin side down, uncovered, over medium heat for 15 minutes. Turn; grill 15-20 minutes longer or until meat juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Julie  Lake
Description: 
'For flavorful, moist and tender chicken, try this marinade,' suggests Julie Lake from Anchorage, Alaska. 'The sesame seeds cling well to season the meat. We also like this mixture on chicken wings, beef short ribs and pork.'
