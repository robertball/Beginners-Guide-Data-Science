Title:
Thai Chicken and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp olive oil
1 1/2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
1 10oz bottle sweet chili sauce
3 tbsp creamy peanut butter
3 cloves garlic, minced
14 oz chicken broth
8 oz uncooked vermicelli noodles
1 cup shredded cabbage and carrots
chopped roasted peanuts

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.  Brown chicken in batches on all sides.  Transfer to a slow cooker.
Combine chili sauce, peanut butter, and garlic in a small bowl.  Pour over chicken and turn to coat.  Stir in broth, cover and cook on low 2 hours.
Add noodles and cabbage mixture.  Cook an additional 30 minutes or until vegetables and noodles are tender.
Garnish with peanuts.
