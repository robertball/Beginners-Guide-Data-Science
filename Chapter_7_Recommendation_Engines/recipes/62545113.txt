Title:
My Chicken Noodle Casserole I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
6 ounces egg noodles
2 stalks celery, diced
sliced carrots
frozen peas
1/2 C. onion
1 can mushrooms
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
3/4 Can milk
1 cup sour cream
ground black pepper to taste
1 T. chicken rub and spice
Chicken stuffing mix

Directions:
Bake or fry chicken.
Cook noodles until almost done. Cut chicken into small pieces, and mix with noodles.
In a separate bowl, mix together mushroom soup, chicken soup, and sour cream with 3/4 can milk.  Season with salt and pepper. Gently stir together cream soup mixture with the chicken mixture. Place in a 2 quart baking dish.
Add diced celery, onion, peas, carrots, and mushrooms.
Top casserole with the dry stuffing mix.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 45-50 minutes, until heated through and browned on top.
