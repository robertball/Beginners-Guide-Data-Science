Title:
Feta Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg coleslaw
4oz feta cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup toasted pine nuts
1/2 c Hidden Valley Coleslaw Dressing
1/4 c fresh basil, chopped

Directions:
Toss all ingredients together, chill 1 hour.

Description: 
Quick and flavorful coleslaw, my daughter's favorite.
