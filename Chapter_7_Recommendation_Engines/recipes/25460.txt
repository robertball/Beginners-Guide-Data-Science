Title:
Grandpa Bob's Spicy Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/250536.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup distilled white vinegar
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1/2 cup barbeque sauce
1 tablespoon ketchup

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix distilled white vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, hot pepper sauce, barbeque sauce and ketchup. Place desired meat in the marinade, and marinate in the refrigerator at least 1/2 hour before grilling as desired.

Submitter: 
NANCERINI
Description: 
My late step-father used to make this, and it is the best I've ever tasted! It is good for chicken, steak or venison. Making this brings back warm memories.
