Title:
Quinoa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120493.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa
2 cups chicken broth
1 1/2 lbs. ground chicken
1/2 onion
2 cloves of garlic (adjust to taste)
1 1/2 cans Rotel tomatoes

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, cook 1 cup of quinoa in 2 cups of chicken broth.  (boil, reduce heat, simmer about 20 min until liquid is absorbed)
Sautee 1/2 an onion and garlic in a separate pan.
Add 1 1/2 lbs. of ground chicken to onion/garlic and cook it completely.
Throw the cooked quinoa into the pan with the chicken and add 1 1/2 cans of Rotel tomatoes
Cook together for about 10 min. so flavors can meld.
Enjoy!
