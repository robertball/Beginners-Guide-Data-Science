Title:
Smoked Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brine  for a 12 pound salmon
1/2 C packed brown sugar
1/2 C.  of coarse salt
2 C.  soy sauce
1 C. of apple juice
1 t.   of onion powder
3 t. Garlic powder
1 t.  of pepper
1 t.  Tabasco
2 C.  Dry white wine
1/4 C pickling spices  optional

Directions:
Brine salmon Chuck's eight hours or overnight.
Remove  Salmon from Brine, wash off thoroughly under cold water. Place pieces on cookie sheet and air dry for at least two hours before placing in the smoker on racks.  Fill barbecue plan with Coals and once going well, drop on several cups of soaked Hickrey, alder, or cherry chips. Repeat this process of adding Coals and chips whenever necessary.  Salmon smokes for about six hours.
