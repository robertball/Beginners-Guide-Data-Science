Title:
Creamy Curried Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15oz. can white corn
1/4 - 1/2 c. half and half
1 clove garlic, chopped
1/2 tsp. curry powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1 dash ground black pepper
3 slices Schreiber's American Cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except cheese in a small kettle and heat on medium heat, stirring frequently, until heated through.
Add cheese and continue heating, stirring occasionally until all cheese has melted and sauce is thickened.
