Title:
Glazed Blueberry Coconut Cake-Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. almond meal                               1/3 c. blueberries
3/4 c. buckwheat flour                        1/4 c. coconut flakes
3/4 c. sugar                                               1/2 tsp. salt
1/3 c. oats                                                 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 c. coconut oil
1/4 c. grapeseed oil
1/4 c. yogurt
3 tbsp. milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Grease a 8"x12" baking pan.
In a large bowl, combine all dry ingredients. WIth a pastry blender, cut in coconut oil until it is well mixed and crumbly.
In a separate bowl, mix grapeseed oil, yogurt, and milk; add to dry and mix well. Add blueberries and stir until just combined. Pour into baking pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until tester inserted in the center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
The Writing Baker
Description: 
This was based off of a chocolate chip recipe, but it isn't at all like it! It onlys lasts two nights in our house, it's that good. The yogurt makes it deliciously moist.
