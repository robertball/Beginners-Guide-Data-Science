Title:
Power Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lg. Hand Full Kale
Lg. Hand Full Spinach
Lg. Hand Full Broccoli
1 Stalks Of Celery
1/2 Banana
1/2 Apple
3 oz Blue Berries
Water And Ice To Desired Texture

Directions:
Blend All Green Veggies Adding Water To Help Smooth
Add All Fruits And Add Ice To Desired Texture
Very Very Tasty, And So Good For You.
And It Is "0" Points On The Weight Watchers Diet.
And Very Filling.If You Like It Sweeter, You Can Add A Whole Banana And A Whole Apple. This Makes Alot Of Smoothie
