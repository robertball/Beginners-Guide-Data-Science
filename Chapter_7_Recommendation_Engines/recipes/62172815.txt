Title:
Marshmallow cream fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1jar marshmallow cream
1 1/2C sugar
2/3C evaporated milk
1/4C butter
1/4tsp salt
1 12oz pkg chocolate chips
1/2C nuts
1tsp vanilla

Directions:
In medium saucepan, combine marshmallow cream, sugar, evaported milk, butter and salt;bring to a full boil, stirring constanly over moderate heat. Boil 5minutes, stirrring constantly. Remove from heat. Add the chocolate chips and stir until melted and mixture is smooth. stir in nuts and vanilla.  Pour into aluminum foil-lined 8in square pan. Chill in refridgerator until firm(about 2hours)
