Title:
Ethiopian cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon whole milk
1/4 cup vinegar
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
boil the vinegar and salt together, then add the milk. turn the heat in a very low heat . cook it till you see the milk start separeted. then by using a fine fabric strain the milk. should separate the water and the curdle. live the fabric and keep it in the frigerator. use after 2 hrs.

this is a very simple home made cheese.
