Title:
Mexican Unstuffed Pepper Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
450 gm Lean Ground Beef
1 Red Pepper, chopped
1 Green Pepper, chopped
1 Onion, chopped
2 cups cooked long grain rice
2 cups salsa
1 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Brown meat with peppers & onions; drain.
Stir in rice, salsa and 1/2 cup of cheese.
Spoon into a 2 Litre casserole dish sprayed with cooking spray.
Top with remaining cheese.
Bake for 25 minutes.
