Title:
Makela Granola Bar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/811667.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup orange juice
3/4 cups honey
2 eggs
1 or 2 Tbsp molasses
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 Tbsp orange zest
1/2 cup grated apple
1 tsp cinnamon
3 cup rolled oats (old fashioned)
1/2 cup branflakes crushed
2 cups nuts chopped (pecans, almonds, walnuts)
2 1/2 cups dried fruit(raisins, dried cranberries)
1/4 cup sesame seeds
1/2 cup wheat germ
1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour (or enough to make mixture stiff)
Extra cinnamon for sprinkling on top

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees. In a medium bowl mix together first eight ingredients, set aside.
In a large bowl put the rest of ingredients. Mix together and add wet ingredients into dry. Put into a greased cookie sheet and flatten to 1/2 to 1 inch thickness. Sprinkle top with extra cinnamon. Bake at 300 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove from oven and cut while hot. Let bars cool and store in refrigerator

Submitter: 
Nancy Roy
Description: 
These are very filling granola bars that my moms friend used to make for us. Time consumming to make but worth the effort.
