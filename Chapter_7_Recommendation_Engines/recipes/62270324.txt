Title:
Polish Placki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large russet potatoes, grated
1/2 grated (medium) onion or 1 teaspoon onion powder
1 tsp. salt
2 beaten eggs
1/2 tsp. paprika
1 tbsp. flour
1/2 black pepper
3 TBSP vegetable oil-(do not use olive oil here)

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the grated potatoes, onion, salt, pepper, flour, and paprika and mix well.  Add the beaten eggs and mix well.
Heat oil in a pan on medium high heat.  Spread a thin layer of potato batter in oil and fry till golden, about 2 minutes.  Flip pancake and cook the other side.  Serve with sour creme.

Submitter: 
Vicky Gunkel
Description: 
A thin polish potato pancake.  Serve these as a meatless entree, or as a side to any meat dish.  Traditionally served with sour cream.
