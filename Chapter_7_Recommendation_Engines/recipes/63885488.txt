Title:
Potato Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package cream cheese
3 pounds of potato
4 cups chicken stock
1 can corn
1 can cream corn
1 amount white pepper
1 amount cayenne pepper
Suggested toppings:
1 amount bacon
1 amount chive

Directions:
Peel, cut, & boil potatoes in the chicken stock. Leaving the stock in the pot remove the potatoes. Add cream cheese to the chicken stock in the pot & whisk to break down the cream cheese. Add the potatoes back in with all the rest of the ingredient. Season white & cayenne pepper to taste.
