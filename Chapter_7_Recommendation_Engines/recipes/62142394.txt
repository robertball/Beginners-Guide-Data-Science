Title:
Southern Yam Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-9” pie shell
1-29 oz. can yams, drained
1-16 oz. container lemon supreme frosting
3 medium eggs

Directions:
Mash yams til smooth.
Add lemon supreme frosting and eggs.
Pour into pie shell.
Bake at 350° for 50 minutes.
Remove from oven.  Cool before eating.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
Sweet potato pie is a southern favorite. Most people interchange the names of yams and sweet potatoes. This is a very easy to make pie that my family loves!
