Title:
Pork Chops and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 pork chops
1 medium onion, chopped
1 cup uncooked rice
1 can chicken broth
1 cup water
1 T chicken base
1/2 tsp garlic
Salt and pepper (lightly)

Directions:
Brown pork chops slowly, but not too brown.  Saute onion in grease where chops were cooked.
In a large baking dish, mix all ingredients together and lay pork chops on top.
Cover tightly and bake 1 1/2 hours at 300 degrees.
