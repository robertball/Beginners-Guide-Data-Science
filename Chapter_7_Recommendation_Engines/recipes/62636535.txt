Title:
Super Easy Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked shredded chicken
2 cans cream of chicken soup
3 cans chicken broth
2 cans refridgerated biscuits

Directions:
Mix together the cream of chicken soup and the chicken broth in a large pot, bring to a simmer. Add the cooked chicken.  Cut each biscuit into quarters and drop into the chicken soup mixture. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes until soup is thick and biscuits are cooked.
