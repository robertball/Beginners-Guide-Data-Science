Title:
Eggs Benedict Oven style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices canadian bacon
6 eggs
6 slices bread or english muffin
butter
cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Coat large muffin tin with butter or cooking spray.
Cut a circle from each slice of bread large enough to fit in the bottom of muffin tin, using a small cup or glass.
Spread butter on each slice or bread or  english muffin.
Place canadian bacon slice on each slice of bread. Top with small mound of grated cheese. Crack egg open over each mound.
Cover muffin tin with aluminum foil, sprayed with cooking spray to prevent sticking.
Place in oven, bake for 10 minutes or until eggs are cooked to personal preference.

Quick, easy and oh so delicious. No more double boilers, over cooked eggs, etc. Use packaged hollindaise sauce or make your own, using a recipe from this site.
