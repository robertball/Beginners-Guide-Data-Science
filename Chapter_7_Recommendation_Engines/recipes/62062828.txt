Title:
Fried Quinces, in the Manner of the Countess Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 pounds fresh quinces
1 Tbsp. clarified butter
½ c. flour
1 Tbsp. sugar
1 pinch salt
Dash vanilla flavoring (not artificial
Ground peel of ¼ lemon, unsprayed
½ c. dark beer
Fat for pan frying; original recipe calls for coconut fat. Butter-flavored frying fat such as Crisco is a healthier alternative.
3 Tbsp. Demerara sugar

Directions:
Peel, stem and core quince as you would apples, cut into wedges. Steam quince wedges in clarified butter, covered, over low heat for 15 minutes. Remove from heat and cool.
Combine flour with sugar, salt. Combine beer with vanilla and lemon peel. Add flour mixture to beer mixture and stir to form smooth batter.
Dip quince wedges into batter and coat evenly, then pan fry in batches until golden brown. Reserve warm. After plating, sprinkle each serving with Demerara sugar and garnish as desired. Serve hot.
