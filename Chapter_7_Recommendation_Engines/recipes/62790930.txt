Title:
Blueberry Almond Streusel Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c slivered almonds
1/4 c firmly packed brown sugar
1 T all purpose flour
2 T butter or margarine
1/2 c regular oats, uncooked
2 c all purpose flour
2 t baking powder
1/4 t baking soda
1/4 t salt
1/2 t sugar
2 t grated lemond rind
1 1/2 c fresh or frozen blueberries
3/4 c buttermilk
1/4 c vegetable oil
1 large egg, slightly beaten

Directions:
Pulse almonds in food processor 2-3 times or until chopped.  Add brown sugar and 1 T flour; process 5 seconds.  Add butter; pulse 5 times or until mixture is crumbly.  Stir in oats; set aside.  Combine 2 c flour and next 5 ingredients in a large bowl; add blueberries, tossing gently.  Make a well in center of mixture, stirring just until moistened.  Spoon batter into greased muffin pans, filling two-thirds full, and sprinkle with oat mixture.  Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until golden.  Remove from pans immediately.
