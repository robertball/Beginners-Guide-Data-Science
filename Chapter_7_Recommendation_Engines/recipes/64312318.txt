Title:
Hard Boiled Egg Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large eggs

Directions:
Place egg in a medium saucepan and cover with cold water so egg is covered by 1 inch of water.
Place pan over medium heat and bring water to a boil. Once the water is boiling, cover the pan, remove from heat and let egg stand in hot water for 12 minutes.
Drain immediately and serve warm or cool completely under cold running water and refrigerate until serving.
