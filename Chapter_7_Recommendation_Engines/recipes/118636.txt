Title:
!Ole! Party Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/657763.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing
1/2 cup thick and chunky mild salsa
Fresh chopped tomatoes and diced green chilies (optional)
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Combine Ranch, salsa, tomatoes and chilies, if desired.  Chill 1 hour.  Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Looking for a new twist on salsa?  Try serving this Ranch flavored salsa at your next party.
