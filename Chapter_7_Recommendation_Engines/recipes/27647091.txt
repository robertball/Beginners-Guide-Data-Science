Title:
Salas Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pizza crust
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1 TBS. Dijon mustard
1 TBS. minced shallot
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp. sugar
1/2 tsp. dried basil leaves
1/2 tsp.driend oregano leaves
1/2 tsp. black pepper
2 TBS. olive oil
3/4 cup reduced-fat part-skim mozzarella cheese
2 TBS. grated parmesan cheese
4 cups mixed greens, washed and torn
1/2 cup cherry tomato halves
1/2 cup sliced red onion
1/4 cup chopped yellow bell pepper

Directions:
Prepare pizza crust.  Preheat oven to 450.
Combine balsamic, mustard, shallot, garlic, sugar, basil, oregano and black pepper in small bowl; whisk to combine.  Gradually whisk in olive oil.
Sprinkle prepared crsut with cheeses.  Bake 10-15 minutes or until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted.
Meanwhile combine salad greens, tomatoes, onion and bell pepper in large bowl.  Pour vinegar mixture over top; toss to combine.
Remove crust from oven and top with salad mixture.  Cut into wedges; serve immediately.
