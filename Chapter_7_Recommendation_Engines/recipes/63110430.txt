Title:
Cheesy Fudge Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB Velveeta Cheese
3 1/2 Sticks butter (the real stuff)
4 LBS Powdered Sugar
1 Cup Cocoa
4 Cups Chopped Nuts
2 Tbsp Vanilla

Directions:
Melt cheese and butter and melt one minute at a time until smooth and creamy. Add other ingredients and work together with hands . If the mixture is "too sticky" gradually add powdered sugar in small portions. Pour mixture onto a wax paper lined pan
(a large cookie sheet type pan). When the mixture is "Set", flip onto a counter or chopping block and
using a pizza cutter cut in to small squares.

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
Fudge is a favorite to all choclate lovers.This recipe is a little different than the grandma's.
Folks will never imagine that it has cheese in it.
