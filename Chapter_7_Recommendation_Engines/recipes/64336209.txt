Title:
Marie's Chocolate Peanut Butter Energy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¾ cup Pitted Dates
½ cup Peanuts or any other Nut
¼ cup Peanut Butter
2 tsp Cocoa Powder

Directions:
Add dates to the bowl of your food processor.
Pulse for 15-20 seconds, until dates begin to break down.
Add pecans, dried apple, apple butter and cinnamon.
Blend until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Roll mixture into one inch balls.
Store in the refrigerator for 5-7 days or in the freezer for up to 6 months.

Submitter: 
fordmarie
Description: 
These snack bites are so, so yummy! I Love eating a couple after my swim practice because they are satisfying and have a good balance of proteins, fats, and carbs.
