Title:
Quick Pumpkin Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1037281.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups milk, or more to taste
2 cups old-fashioned oats
1 cup canned pumpkin puree
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2 tablespoons brown sugar, or to taste (optional)
2 tablespoons pecan pieces, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Mix milk, oats, pumpkin puree, cinnamon, salt, ginger, allspice, and nutmeg together in a 4-quart microwave-safe bowl.
Cook on High for 12 to 15 minutes, stirring once. Sprinkle brown sugar and pecan pieces over the oatmeal to serve.
