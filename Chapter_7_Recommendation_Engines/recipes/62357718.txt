Title:
White Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (15 ounce) cans cannellini beans, rinsed and drained
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
1 Tbsp. garlic, crushed or to taste
1/3 cup olive oil
1/2 lemon, juiced
1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a food processor, combine the cannellini beans, parsley, garlic, olive oil and lemon juice, and crushed red pepper flakes. Process until smooth, or to your desired consistency. Taste, and season with salt and pepper.

Description: 
An awesome alternative to the usual refried beans and tortilla chips.  Serve this dip with pita chips or veggie wheat crackersModified from: White Bean Dip
