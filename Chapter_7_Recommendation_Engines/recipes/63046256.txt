Title:
Pineapple Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 frozen chicken breasts
¾ Cup of teriyaki sauce
¼ Cup of Soy Sauce
1 Can pineapples (Chunks).

Directions:
Place frozen chicken breasts in crock pot, pour in teriyaki sauce and Soy Sauce, and pineapple.
Cook on High for 4 hours or low 6-8 hours.
Shred chicken  (w/ 2 forks) serve over rice.
