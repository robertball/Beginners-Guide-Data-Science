Title:
Dawn's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/838002.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 pounds hamburger
1  egg; slightly beaten
1 cup oatmeal
1 small onion (optional)
1/2 cup milk
3 tablespoon mustard
1 tablespoon worcestershire
1 tsp. salt (optional)
1 dash pepper

Directions:
Combine ingredients. Shape into loaf pan. Bake at 350F for 50-60 minutes

Submitter: 
HOWEWASS
Description: 
I based this recipe on other meatloaf recipes, but it is uniquely mine. It is similar to one in the 1995 Swiss Colony Cookbook p 239. I wanted it made with oatmeal, and spices that I like.
