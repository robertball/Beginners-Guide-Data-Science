Title:
Chicken Stir-Fry with a kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/995781.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb of Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast Sliced into Strips
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
One fresh Broccoli Floret Chopped
1/4 cup of low sodium-soya sauce
1/8 cup of water
1/2 Lemon Squeezed
2 Cloves of Garlic Finely minced
1 dried ginger clove
1-2 Tbsp Dried Chili Peppers
1 Tbsp Cajun Spice
1 Tbsp of honey
2 Cups of Minute Rice prepared.

Directions:
Brown Chicken in Olive oil
Add broccoli, and liquids. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
Add in remaining ingredients (not the rice)
Let simmer for 10 minutes.
While spices are mingling, make your rice.
Add Rice into mix. and voila!

Submitter: 
Elyse Amber Greentree
Description: 
I love Stir-fry, and this is usually the base of what I go off of. We like to change it up depending on our mood. Overall Yum! Yum!
