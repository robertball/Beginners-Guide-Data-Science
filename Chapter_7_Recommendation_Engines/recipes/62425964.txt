Title:
Pub Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cheddar cheese (or extra sharp cheddar), grated
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup beer (stong flavor, example Guiness)
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon fresh parsley, minced (garnish)

Directions:
Place all ingredients except the parsley into a food processor.  Process to a smooth texture.  Spoon into serving bowl and top with parsley.
Cover and place in refrigerator to let flavors marry.
Remove from refrigerator and let cheese come to nearly room temperature and become spreadable.
Serve with buttery crackers.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
This cheese spread is similar to what is served with buttery crackers in pubs and restaurants.
