Title:
Colored Sugar Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 tablespoon butter
3 tablespoons water
2-3 drops food coloring
6 cups popped butter-flavored popcorn (I use one bag of microwave popcorn)

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients in a pot over medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring constantly until a thickened syrup forms. Take off heat and add food coloring.
Place popcorn in a mixing bowl and pour syrup mixture over popcorn. Toss with spatula/spoon to coat evenly.
Spread popcorn on a cookie sheet to cool.

Submitter: 
mlulay
Description: 
This is such a fun and tasty popcorn treat with so little effort! You can create any color you want (black and orange for Halloween, red and green for Christmas, ect. for holidays and parties)!
