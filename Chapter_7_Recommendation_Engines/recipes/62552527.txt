Title:
funfetti cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Funfetti cake mix
1/2 cup butter
1 egg

Directions:
soften or melt butter. Mix all ingredients. Roll into balls and place on cookie sheet about 1 1/2" apart. Bake at 350 for 10 min. or until they just start to brown and crack. Remove and let cool completely. Top with your favorite icing and sprinkles.

Submitter: 
Carol Wheeler
Description: 
Easy, yummy cookies made from a funfetti cake mix!
