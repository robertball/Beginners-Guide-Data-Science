Title:
Sausage Cassoulet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound dried navy beans
4 cups water
2 - 14 oz. cans chicken broth
6 medium yellow onions, chopped
4 celery stalks with tops, chopped
4 carrots, sliced 1/2" thick
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound diced canned tomatoes
2 bay leaves
1 tsp thyme
1/2 t. salt, or to taste
1/2 c. dry bread crumbs
1 # polish sausage
chopped parsley

Directions:
Rinse and sort beans.  Boil in water for 2 minutes then soak one hour.
Fry bacon until crisp.  Set bacon aside to drain.
Saute bread crumbs in 1 - 2 Tbs bacon fat, set aside
Bring beans to a boil.  Add oinions, chicken broth and spices to beans, cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
Add carrots and bacon to beans; simmer for 45 minutes.
Slice polish sausage about 1/2# thick and saute until brown.  Set aside.
Add browned sausage and tomatoes and their juice; bake uncovered in 350 degree oven for 45 minutes
Top with bread crumbs and bake for 15 minutes more.
Top with chopped parsley and serve.

Submitter: 
Chris D. Williams
Description: 
A French-inspired casserole bursting with flavor.  Originally published in 'Seventeen' Magazine, it is an easy recipe that makes a hearty meal.
