Title:
Daiquiri Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1060818.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces light rum
1 fluid ounce lime juice
1 teaspoon simple syrup
1 cup ice

Directions:
Combine light rum, lime juice, and simple syrup in a cocktail shaker. Add ice, cover and shake until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Submitter: 
Allrecipes
Description: 
The daiquiri is believed to have originated in Cuba. Shake rum, lime juice, and simple syrup with ice and serve.
