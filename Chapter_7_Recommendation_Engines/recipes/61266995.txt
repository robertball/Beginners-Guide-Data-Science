Title:
Pork and Green Chile Casserole/Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1  small onion -chopped (optional)
1 1/2 pounds boneless pork loin chops, cut into bite-size pieces
or left-over prk
1 1/2 cups instant brown rice, uncooked
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
or 1 can purred pinto beans
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
2 (4 ounce) cans diced green chile peppers
3 tablespoons chunky salsa
1/4 cup water
2 tablespoons ground cumin
1+ cup shredded Cheddar or Colby cheese

Directions:
Heat oil in oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.  Brown onions. Add pork (if uncooked), and saute until browned. Drain off grease.  Add the rice, cream of chicken soup, black beans, tomatoes, green chiles, salsa and water. Season with cumin. Stir to blend, then bring to a boil. Simmer over medium heat for about 15 minutes, or until the rice is tender.
Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. When the pork mixture is cooked, sprinkle cheese over the top.
Bake for 5 to 10 minutes, until cheese is melted. Serve with tortillas either to dip or wrap.
