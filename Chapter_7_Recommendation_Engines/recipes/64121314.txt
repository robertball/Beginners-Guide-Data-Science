Title:
Fruit salad smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*1 apple(sliced)  *1 Pear (sliced) *4 strawberries
*1hand-full of cherries *1 can of chopped pineapples
*2 spoonfuls of vanilla yogurt and 2 cups of milk
*********Type of the milk is optional*******

Directions:
Chop the apple the pear and the strawberries. Then put them in the blender.Add the the pineapples the yogurt and the milk.Blend it all together for 4 minutes on high, until its nice and  liquidy.
