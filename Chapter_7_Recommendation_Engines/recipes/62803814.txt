Title:
Russian Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup heavy cream
1 cup sugar
1 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Heat heavy cream and sugar over medium low heat stirring often until the sugar has dissolved.
Remove from heat and slowly add sour cream, stirring constantly.
Finally add vanilla and stir in well.
Best served at room temperature.
Store in refrigerator. Keeps well if stored in a tightly sealed container.

Submitter: 
Coralie
Description: 
Amazingly easy yet gourmet tasting sauce that goes best with fresh strawberries or other delicious fresh fruits!
