Title:
Strawberry Slushy (Smoothie) with a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3202523.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
1 (17.5 ounce) can coconut water
2 cups ice
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers rum, or more to taste
1 tablespoon white sugar, or more to taste
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Blend strawberries, coconut water, ice, rum, sugar, and vanilla extract together in a blender until frozen and slushy.

Submitter: 
DarenR
Description: 
Take a traditional strawberry smoothie and add a little extra kick with some front and back flavor. Use coconut water not coconut milk.
