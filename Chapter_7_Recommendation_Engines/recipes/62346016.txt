Title:
Apple And Cinnamon Crumble Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/394391.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup plus 2 tbsp of softened butter
1 1/4 cups superfine sugar
4 eggs
1 lb/4 cups self rising flour
3 large apples
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon for topping
3/4 cup raw sugar
1 cup plain all purpose flour
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 cup dry unsweetened shredded coconut
1/2 cup of butter

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350F.  Grease and base -line a 10in round cake pan.  To make the crumble topping,  Mix the sugar, flour, cinnamon and coconut in a bowl then rub in the butter with your fingers unit the mixture resembles bread crumbs. Set aside
Put the butter and sugar in a bowl and cream with an electric mixer unit light and fluffy.  Beat the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition and adding a little of the flour if the mixture starts to curdle.
Sift in half the remaining flour and ix well then add the rest of the flour and stir until smooth.
Peel and core the apples the grate them coarsely. Place the grated apples in a bowl and sprinkle with cinnamon. Set aside
Spread half the cake mixture evenly over the base of the prepared tin. Spoon the apples on top and sprinkle over half the crumble topping.
Spread the remaining cake mixture over the crumble and finally top with remaining crumble topping
Bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes cover the cake with foil if it browns too quickly.  Leave in pan for about 5 minutes. Then serve.
