Title:
Ham and Cheese Breakfast Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (12 ounce) packages frozen hash brown potatoes
1/3 cup butter, melted
1 cup cooked diced ham or sausage or bacon,cooked
1 small onion, chopped or green onions
1/2 red pepper, chopped
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
3 eggs
1/2 cup half and half

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Squeeze any excess moisture from the potatoes and combine them with the melted butter or margarine in a small bowl. Press this mixture into the bottom and sides of an un-greased 9X12 pan.
Bake at 425 degrees F for 25 minutes.
Remove pan from oven and arrange the ham, cheese, onions and red pepper evenly over the potatoes. In a separate small bowl, beat together the eggs and the half and half. Pour this over the ham and cheese.
Return pan to oven and bake for 425 degrees F for 30 minutes, or until the custard has completely set.

Submitter: 
Colleen Robertson Curtiss
Description: 
Bake a ham and cheese quiche in a pan lined with crispy hash brown potatoes instead of the usual pastry shell.Modified from: Ham and Cheese Breakfast Quiche
