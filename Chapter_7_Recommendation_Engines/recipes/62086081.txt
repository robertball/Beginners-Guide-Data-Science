Title:
chocolate cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 oz. of your favorite vodka; 1/2 oz. frangellico (hazelnut liquor).

Directions:
chill both ingredients together in a shaker with ice. wet shot glass, and dip the rim in sugar. strain alcohol mixture into shot glass.
some people like their sugar with a slice of lemon, but i suggest trying it plain first.

Submitter: 
Anastasia Beaverhousen
Description: 
for those who love chocolate cake and liquor, this shot is the one. can also be made larger in a martint sized version.
