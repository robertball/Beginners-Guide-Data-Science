Title:
Signature Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of Lea & Perrins Worcestershire sauce
3 tablespoons of balsamic vinegar
1 1/2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
1/2 teaspoon of pepper

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients.
2. In large, shallow non-aluminum baking dish or plastic bag, pour marinade over steaks; turn to coat.
3. Marinate in refrigerator for 30 minutes.  Remove steaks; discard marinade.  Grill or broil steak to preferred doneness.

Submitter:

Description: 
Flavorful and easy steak marinade using ingredients you typically have on hand.
