Title:
Glass Cleaner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz spray bottle
3 cups water
2 Tbsp rubbing alcohol
1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
5 drops lavender essential oil
1/2 Tbsp liquid castile soap

Directions:
In a well ventilated area, use a funnel to pour water, oil, alcohol and vinegar  in to a bottle.  shake well.  Add soap and give another good shake.
TO USE~ Spray windows and wipe with a news paper.

Submitter: 
TheBritishBaker
Description: 
Use rubbing alcohol and white vinegar to clean windows, mirrors and car windshields.  costs just $0.29 cents for 27 oz.
