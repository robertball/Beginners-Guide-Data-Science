Title:
Kathleen's Green Chile and Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/677835.jpg
Ingredients:
1 27 oz. can Hatch Whole Green Chiles
2 15 oz. can Hatch Green Chile Enchilada Sauce
1 15 oz. cans diced tomatoes
3 cups chicken, cooked and diced
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack Cheese
8 flour (or corn) tortillas
1 chopped onion
2 cups water

Directions:
Combine the ENCHILADA SAUCE, water, chicken, tomatoes and onion in a 4 qt. saucepan.
Stir, and heat at medium temperature until bubble.
Simmer for 15 minutes.
Preheat oven to 400° F.
Spread 1/3 of mixture in a greased 9 x 13 casserole dish.
Layer 4 tortillas over mixture(they may overlap or you can cut them to fit).
Spread 1/3 of mixture evenly over tortillas.
Layer the Hatch green chiles over the mixture.
Place another layer of tortillas over chiles.
Sprinkle 1 cup of cheese over tortillas.
Make another layer of remaining mixture and tortillas.
Bake at 400° for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
Carefully remove casserole from oven and top with remaining cheese.
Return to oven for 5 minutes, or until cheese has melted.
Let stand for 10 minutes; cut into serving sizes and top with sour cream and black olives, if desired.
Serve with rice or beans.
