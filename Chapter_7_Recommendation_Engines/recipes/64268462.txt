Title:
Dianna's Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1857547.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lb bag of Idaho or Russet Potatoes
1 stick Salted Butter
1/2 Milk
8 oz. Sour Cream
1 tsp Kosher salt
1/2 tsp black pepper (more or less depending on paste)

Directions:
Peel the potatoes and cut each potato into 4 pieces.
Place the potatoes in a steamer rack of a large pot.
Pour in 2" of water into the bottom, and bring to a simmer covered.
Turn heat to medium-high and steam the potatoes for 20 minutes or until they pierce very easily with a paring knife.
While the potatoes are steaming place butter and milk in sauce pan and warm over low heat until butter is melted.
Spoon entire container of sour cream into butter and milk mixture and warm over low heat until mixture is smooth.
Hand mash or rice the potatoes into a large bowl.
Add salt and pepper.
Slowly add sour cream mixture until the potatoes are the consistency you like.

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description: 
Creamy delicious mashed potatoes you can eat with or without gravy.
