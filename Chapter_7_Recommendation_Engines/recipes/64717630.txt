Title:
Big Ray's Polish Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5802712.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups uncooked penne pasta
1-1/2 pounds Polish Kielbasa Sausage, cut into 1/2-inch slices
2 cans (10-3/4 ounces each) condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 jar (16 ounces) sauerkraut, rinsed and well drained
3 cups shredded Swiss cheese, divided
1-1/3 cups heavy cream (or 2%  milk)
4 green onions, chopped
2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
4 garlic cloves, minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°. Cook pasta according to package directions; drain and transfer to a large bowl. Stir in sausage, soup, sauerkraut, 2 cups cheese, cream, onions, mustard and garlic.
Spoon into two greased 8-in. square baking dishes; sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake, uncovered, 45-50 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly. Freeze option: Cover and freeze unbaked casserole up to 3 months. Thaw in the refrigerator overnight. Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before baking. Preheat oven to 350°. Bake, uncovered, 50-55 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly.
