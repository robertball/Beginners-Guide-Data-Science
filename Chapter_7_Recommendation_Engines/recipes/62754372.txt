Title:
Aunt Netta's Breadmaker Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 7/8 c. water
2 T. olive oil or butter
2 T. honey or sugar
4 c. whole wheat flour from Hard White Wheat
1 tsp. salt
1/4 c. vital wheat gluten
1 t. yeast

Directions:
Add ingredients in order given to bread maker.  Each bread maker is different so you may need to adjust amounts.  Start with adjusting the water, then the flour, then the yeast.

Submitter: 
Miriam
Description: 
This is the yummiest bread I have every eaten!  Aunt Netta perfected this recipe and shared it with us.  This recipe is for a 2 lb. loaf bread maker
