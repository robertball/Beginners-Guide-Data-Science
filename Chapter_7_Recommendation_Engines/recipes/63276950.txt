Title:
Spinach, Pear and Nut Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups raw baby spinach, washed
1 Bosc pear, sliced lengthwise
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/4 cup crumbled feta
1/2 cup sliced celery
1/4 cup roasted sunflower seeds
1/4 cup seven-grain salad [or whatever is available]
squirt lemon juice
optional: blueberries or sliced strawberries
optional: chopped dates

Directions:
Mix. Layer sliced fruit on top. Enjoy!
