Title:
Pork Chop Grill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1-in thick pork chops, bone-in or boneless
3 cups water
2 1/2 tbsp sugar
1 1/2 tbsp kosher salt
1 sprig of fresh rosemary
2 sprigs of fresh thyme

Directions:
Mix the water, sugar, and salt in a gallon size bag until dissolved.  Add the fresh herbs.  Add pork chops.  Refrigerate four hours or more.  Grill 20 minutes, or until still pink in the middle.

Submitter: 
Marianne
Description: 
This is an easy grill recipe that requires very little preparation time outside of the marinating but delivers great taste!  This can be served with any grill side dish that suits your style!
