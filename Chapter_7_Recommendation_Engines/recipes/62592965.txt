Title:
Tender Glazed Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
4 boneless pork loin chops (4 ozs each)
1 cup apricot preserves
1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 1/2 teaspoons limne juice
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Combine the onion powder, garlic powder and oregano; sprinkle over pork chops.
In a small saucepan, combine the apricot preserves, lemon juice and lime juice; cook and stir over low heat for 10 minutes or until preserves are melted.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook pork in oil over medium-heat for 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned on one side.  Turn chops; generously brush with apricot glaze.  Cook 8-10 minutes or until juices run clear.  Serve with remaining glaze.
