Title:
HONEY BUTTER CARROT CAKE WITH TOASTED PECANS AND CITRUS CREAM CHEESE ICING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CAKE:
Butter or nonstick spray
2 cups all purpose flour
1-1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground clove or allspice
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 cup butter softened
1/2 cup honey
2 eggs
1 cup granulated sugar
3 cups finely grated carrots
1 apple, peeled and grated
1 cup toasted pecans, coarsley chopped
ICING:
One 250g package regular cream cheese
1/2 cup butter softened
2 tbsp honey
2 tbsp finely grated orange, lemon, or lime zest (or a minxture of all three)
2 tsp lemon juice
4 - 5 cups sifted icing sugar.

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 deg.
2.  Butter two 9-inch lay pans or coat with non-stick spray.  Sift flour with baking powder, baking soda, spices and salt.
3.  In another bowl, using an electric mixter, cream butter until lighter in colour and slightly fluffy.  Slowly beat in honey, then eggs, one at at time.  Beat in sugar and vanilla.
4.  Add flour mixture to butter mixture in thirds, stirring just until absorbed.  Stir in carrots, apple and pecans.
5.  Divide batter between pans, smooth tops.  Bake in oven centre for 35 to 40 min., or until cake tester comes out clean.  Cook on a rack for 10 minutes, then turn out to finish cooling on rack.
To make icing, beat cream cheese with an electric mixture until smooth.  Gradually beat in butter, then honey, zest and lemon juice.  Gradually add icing sugar until thick enough for spreading.  Spread between stacked layers, the over sides and top.  Sprinkle with any extra zest or chopped pecans.
NOTE:  YOU CAN ALSO BAKE IN A 10 INCH BUNDT PAN.   Coat pan with cooking spray.  Evenly spoon in batter and smooth top.  Bake in centre oven for 55 to 60 minutes.  Cook on a rack for 10 minutes, then turn out to finish on cooling rack.  Make only half of recipe of icing.  Spread over cooked cake.  Sprinkle with extra zest or chopped pecans.
