Title:
Spicy Raspberry Balsamic Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/971626.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1 tablespoon sesame oil
1 tablespoon raspberry preserves
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon dry mustard

Directions:
Whisk olive oil, balsamic vinegar, sesame oil, raspberry preserves, salt, onion powder, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and dry mustard in a bowl until thick and smooth.
Cover bowl with plastic wrap; refrigerate at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Chris Reynolds
Description: 
This sweet and tangy Balsamic dressing is perfect for spring mix!
