Title:
Cantaloupe and Burrata Salad  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1246923.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cantaloupe - peeled, seeded, and cut into large bite-sized chunks
1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 (4 ounce) ball burrata cheese, or to taste
1 pinch fresh mint leaves, or as desired

Directions:
Place cantaloupe chunks into a serving bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper; stir. Top with burrata cheese as desired. Garnish with mint.

Submitter: 
Corie Kessler-Bennett
Description: 
Fantastic dish brought to my attention at a potluck. A wonderful twist for plain old cantaloupe! Perfect accompaniment to any dish on a hot summer's day!
