Title:
Homemade Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4519220.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups ROLLED OATS  -  1/2 cup CHOPPED PLAIN ALMONDS -  1/2 cup PUMPKIN SEEDS  -  1/2 cup SUNFLOWER SEEDS  -   1/2 cup HAZELNUTS  -   1/2 cup UNSWEETENED COCONUT FLAKE -  1/2 tps SALT - 1/2 cup MAPLE SYRUP -   1/4 cup CANOLA OIL - 1/2  cup DRY RAISINS

Directions:
Put your even rack in the middle positionPreheat the oven to 325F - In a large bowl, Combine dry ingredients except dry raisin - Add the maple syrup and oil and mix well to combine - Spread mixture on a baking sheet.-  Bake for about 30 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes - Cool completely and and had the dry raisins to the mixture

Description: 
THE PERFECT BREAKFAST, THIS  HOMEMADE GRANOLA OFFER HEALTHY FATS FROM NUTS AND SEEDS AND JUST ENOUGH SWEETNESS TO SATISFY YOUR SWEET TOOTH.
