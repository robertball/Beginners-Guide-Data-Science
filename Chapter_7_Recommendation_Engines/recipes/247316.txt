Title:
Miami Street-Style Turkey Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3995634.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package JENNIE-OÂ® Lean Taco Seasoned Ground Turkey
3 cups shredded napa cabbage
1 mango, peeled and cut into thin strips
1/2 red bell pepper, cut into strips
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
8 soft taco shells

Directions:
Cook turkey as specified on the package. Always cook to well done, 165 degrees F as measured by a meat thermometer.
Combine cabbage, mango, red bell pepper, cilantro, lime juice, olive oil and salt in a medium bowl.
Spoon ground turkey mixture into taco shells. Top with slaw mixture.

Submitter: 
JennieO
Description: 
You don't have to hit Miami Beach to enjoy a tasty street taco. This easy turkey taco recipe features savory, pre-seasoned ground turkey topped with a homemade mango citrus slaw.
