Title:
Oven Fried Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb bag of frozen chicken wings (tips off) thawed/washed
1 cup Kraft parmesean cheese
1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1/8 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp onion powder
1/8 tsp black pepper
dash salt
1 stick melted butter
cookie sheet lined with tin foil and sprayed VERY well will cooking spray

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 400 degrees
Melt butter in a medium bowl
in a separate bowl mix all the dry ingredients together
dip chick wing in butter then in bread crumb mix
coat well.
place on cookie sheet that has been sprayed with oil.
Bake for 20mins or until brown.
turn over and cook additional minutes till all sides are a deep golden brown.
serve on a platter and enjoy!!!!

Submitter: 
Lisa
Description: 
Breaded chicken wings that consist of bread crumbs, parmesaean cheese, garlic powder, melted butter and a cookie sheet
