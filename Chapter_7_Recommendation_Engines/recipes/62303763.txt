Title:
BBQs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 small onion, finely diced
1 tsp. vinegar
1 Tbsp. brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 cup water
1/2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Brown ground beef with onion. Drain fat.
Add remaining ingredients and simmer on low until heated through.
Serve on buns.

Submitter: 
Kristin McNeil
Description: 
Perfect BBQ meat to serve on buns. Recipe can be easily doubled or tripled!
