Title:
Mom's Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Dozen Eggs
3 TBSP Mayonnaise
1/2 TSP Dry Mustard
1 TBSP Sweet Relish
2 TSP White Vinegar
Dash Worchestire Sauce
Dash black pepper
Sweet Relish for Garnish

Directions:
Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, and peel.
Slice eggs in half and set egg whites aside.
In a bowl, mash yokes with a fork, add mayo, dry mustard, sweet relish, white vinegar, worchestire sauce and pepper.
Fill egg whites with mixture, with either a spoon or pastry bag.
Garnish with several pieces of sweet relish.

Submitter: 
lisa j
Description: 
These simple deviled eggs have been a barbecue staple in my family for as long as I can remember.  Simple, yet classic.
