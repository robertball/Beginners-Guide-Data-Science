Title:
Lemon-Curry Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984675.jpg
Ingredients:
16 hard-cooked eggs
1/3 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon ground mustard
1/2 teaspoon curry powder
1 dash Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Cut eggs in half lengthwise. Remove the yolks; set whites aside. In a bowl, mash yolks. Add the remaining ingredients; mix well. Spoon into egg whites. Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Judith  Miller
Description: 
I enjoy prettying up a potluck or brunch buffet with a platter of these zippy eggs. You might also like to consider adding them to a salad plate or a soup and salad lunch. -Judith Miller, Walnut, California
