Title:
Smothered Steak and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. top round steaks cut into 3/4 in. strips
salt/pepper
1 tea. dried oregano
3 Tab. flour
4 Tab. butter
4 large vidalia (or any sweet) onions, sliced
1/4 tea. cayenne pepper
2 1/2 cups beef or chicken broth
1/2 cup heavy cream
noodles or rice for serving

Directions:
Place meat in a bowl and season w/ salt/pepper. Add the oregano and flour- toss to coat well. In a large skillet melt 3 Tab. of the butter. Brown the meat until golden, and drain on paper towels. Add the remaining 1 Tab. butter to the skillet and add the onions. cook until wiltedd and softened, about 10 min. Season w/ salt/pepper and cayenne. Return meat to skillet and add broth. Scrape up any browned bits on the bottom. Bring to a boil-reduce and cover. Cook until tender and thickened, about an hour. Por in the cream and simmer until hot.
