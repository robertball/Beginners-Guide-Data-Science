Title:
Cereal Slice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup corn syrup, light or dark
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup peanut butter
2 cups corn flakes cereal
1 cup rice krispies cereal

Directions:
Heat syrup and sugar in glass dish in microwave on high, stirring until sugar is dissolved.
Stir in peanut butter until smooth.
Add cereal, stirring to coat well.
Press into greased 8" or 9" square pan. Cool.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A quick, chewy slice. One of my Grandma's favorites. And with cereal and protein, it could almost pass for health food!
