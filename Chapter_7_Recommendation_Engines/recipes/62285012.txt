Title:
Ladybug's Salmon & Halibut Steak Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Fresh salmon steaks (1/2 lb each)
1/4 cup mayo
1 tsp Dijon Mustard (Grey Poupon)
2 Tbsp Italian Dressing
2-3 finely chopped sweet onion
2-4 tbsp Seafood Seasoning (Chef Paul Prudhomme's) Magic Salmon Seasoning is very good)
Progresso Italian Bread Crumbs
1/2 cup grated Cracker Barrel Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Blend the mayo, mustard, dressing and onion together and set aside
Sprinkle fish with Seafood Seasoning
Spread mayo mixture over fish
Sprinkle with Bread Crumbs (you can use Ritz Cracker crumbs if preferred)
Bake 15-20 minutes or until fish is flaky.
Remove from oven
Sprinkle cheese over fish
Return to oven and bake 10-15 minutes or until cheese is melted

Submitter: 
Lisa Schwartz-Gunnison
Description: 
Incredibly easy to make and crowd pleasing!  Originally served on fresh caught salmon when visiting relatives in Seattle Washington.
