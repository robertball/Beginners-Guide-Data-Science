Title:
Italian-Crescent Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground round
1 pkg. spaghetti sauce mix.
6 oz. shredded mozzarella
8-oz. can tomatoe sauce
8-oz. can crescent rolls
1/3c. grated Parmesan cheese
2 T.melted butter

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Brown ground round. Drain. Stir in sauce mix and tomatoe sauce.Heat until bubbly. . Pour hot mixture into ungreased 12x8 or 13x9-inch baking dish. Spoon cheese over meat. Seperate crescent dough into 2 rectangles. Place rectangles over cheese. Combine Parmesan cheese and butter. Crumble evenly over dough. Bake for 18-20 min., until crust is golden brown.
