Title:
Easy Apple Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 baking apples, pared and cored
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/8 tsp salt
1 egg, slightly beaten
4 tbsp butter
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Butter 4 custard cups large enough to set apples in. Mix 1/4 sugar and cinnamon. Roll apples in the cinnamon sugar and place in custard cups. Sprinkle remaining sugar on the apples. Stir 1/4 cup sugar with flour, baking powder and salt. Mix in egg, butter and vanilla. Spoon batter over apples, dividing evenly. Bake at 375 for 35-40 minutes. Crust should be golden brown. Serve with whip cream.
