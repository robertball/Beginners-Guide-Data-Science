Title:
Dilly Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds new potatoes, scrubbed and quartered
1/4 cup Italian-style salad dressing
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1 Tablespoon chopped green onions
1 teaspoon dill

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add potatoes, and cook for about 10 minutes, or until tender. Drain, and set aside to cool.
Meanwhile, in a large bowl, stir together the salad dressing, mayonnaise, green onions, dill, . When the potatoes are cooled, stir into the bowl until coated. Make sure to refrigerate for a couple of hours to blend flavors before serving!

Submitter: 
Lisa Garnier
Description: 
I love the tangy dill in this recipe. It's lighter than the original because of the reduced mayo, the smaller amount of Italian dressing adds just a little zing without being overpowering. Excellent on a hot day with barbeque!Modified from: Tangy Dill Potato Salad
