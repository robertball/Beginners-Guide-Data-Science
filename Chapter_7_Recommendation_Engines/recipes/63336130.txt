Title:
Joel's Steamed Mussels in Tomato Garlic White Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/911236.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Fresh Mussels (Scrubbed / De-bearded)
2 Tbl. spoons olive oil
2 Tbl. spoons butter or margarine
1 chopped red onion
4 Tbl. spoons fresh chopped parsley
4-5 garlic cloves chopped
1 can diced tomatoes
1 cup white wine
sea salt / cracked pepper to taste
freshly grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
Clean mussels by covering all with water in sink or large bowl.  (you can use 1-2 tbl. spoons cider vinegar to help separate sand/mud) also use brush to scrub under running water, also de-beard.  Discard any mussels that are not tightly shut.
Heat oil and butter in large pot over medium/high heat.  Add chopped onions and cook until tender (about 5 min.)
Add garlic and cook for another 2-3 minutes.  Add tomatoes, 2 Tbl. spoons parsley, and white wine.  Bring to boil.
Add mussels and stir well.  Cook over medium heat - covered for 8-10 minutes until all mussels are opened.  Discard any un-opened mussels.
Serve mussels with sauce in large bowel.  Sprinkle remaining 2 Tbl. spoons parsley, Parmesan cheese, and salt/pepper over dish.

Submitter: 
Joel Lacoss
Description: 
A great appetizer for sea food lovers.  This is a modified recipe I came up with by including all my favorite ingredients from similar recipes.  I usually get the mussels fresh from the ocean at low tide when we stay at Peaks Island, Maine each summer.
