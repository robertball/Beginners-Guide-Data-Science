Title:
Cocoa Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups steel-cut oats
2 cups each water and milk
1 mashed banana
3 tablespoons ground flaxseed
1 1/2 tablespoons cocoa
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1 cup water
1/3 cup each walnuts
Serve with maple syrup and blueberries.

Directions:
Boil steel-cut oats and 2 cups each water and milk. Simmer 15 minutes. Stir in mashed banana,  ground flaxseed, cocoa, cinnamon, nutmeg, and 1 cup water. Cook 10 minutes. Add walnuts and serve with maple syrup and blueberries.

Submitter: 
Karen Moorse
Description: 
Great comfort food for breakfast!  Nice on a cold winter morning.
