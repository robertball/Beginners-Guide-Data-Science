Title:
Beef Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/907443.jpg
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons kosher salt
1 tablespoon chile powder
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon majoram
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Mix together. Rub about a tablespoon on each steak or cut of meat, depending on size of cut.
