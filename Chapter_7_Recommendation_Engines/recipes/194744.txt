Title:
Cheesy Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991886.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (1-inch thick) center slices King's Hawaiian® Bread
1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar and Jack cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
3 stalks green onion, chopped
1/2 teaspoon garlic, minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Mix cheese, mayonnaise, green onion and garlic together. Spread onto 4 slices of bread.
Bake for 10 minutes or until cheese is bubbling. Slice bread into thirds and serve.

Submitter: 
King's Hawaiian®
Description: 
This easy toasted cheese bread recipe is perfect as a quick appetizer or to satisfy a sudden snack attack.
