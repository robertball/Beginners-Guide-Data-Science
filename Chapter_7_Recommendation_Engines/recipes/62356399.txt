Title:
Spaghetti with Fresh Tomato Sauce and Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Extra VirginOlive Oil
20 ripe plum tomatoes, peeled, halved and seeded
Pinch of crushed red pepper
Salt & Pepper
1 tbsp. unsalted butter
1/2 cup of freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
6 to 8 fresh basil leaves, sliced
1 lb. spaghetti

Directions:
1. In wide pan,heat oil over med. high heat. Add tomatoes and red pepper flakes, season with salt and pepper. Cook until tomatoes start to soften, then using a potato masher ,chop the tomatoes finely. Cook 20-25 min. until sauce thickens.
2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti until just al dente.Reserve a little of pasta water to use if sauce seems too thick. Drain pasta and add to sauce and cook over med. high heat gently mixing pasta with sauce until pasta is tender. Take off heat and toss in butter, cheese and basil. Serve immediately.
