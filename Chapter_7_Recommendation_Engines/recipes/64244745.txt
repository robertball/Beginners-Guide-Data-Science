Title:
Enchilada Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Beef 1+ lbs.
Ench. sauce (1 can mild and 1 can green chile) w/couple cans H2O
Refried Beans
Garbonzo Beans
Hominy Corn
Frozen Sweet Corn
Black Olives
Sour Cream
Mexican Shredded Cheese
Corn Chips

Directions:
Place frozen meat in sauces. Simmer on low until meat is cooked. Add misc. ingred. Add Sour Cream at end of cooking time. Serve over crushed corn chips. Add cheese and dollop of Sour Cream if desired.
