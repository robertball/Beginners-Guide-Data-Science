Title:
Orange 7 Minute Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1997751.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg whites
3/4 cups organic sugar blended in processor or blender
1 Tablespoon orange juice concentrate
2 Tablespoons water

Directions:
Put egg whites, sugar, water and orange juice concentrate in top of double boiler.  Beat until mixed well.  Place over rapidly boiling water.  Beat constantly with electric beater while it cooks for 7 minutes or until it will stand in peaks when beater is raised.  Remove from heat.  Makes enough for one layer cake.
