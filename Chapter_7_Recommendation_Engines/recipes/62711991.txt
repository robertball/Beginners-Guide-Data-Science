Title:
Hot Dog Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 sm. can Hunt's tomato sauce
1 can water
2 tsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp oregano
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 tsp red pepper
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 tsp salt
3 tsp paprika

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and simmer for 1 hour, stirring frequently.
