Title:
Roasted Spicy Mustard Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/518452.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 pounds small red potatoes

Directions:
Spray a shallow roasting pan with nonstick cooking spray three times to coat well; set aside. In a large bowl, whisk the mustard, paprika, cumin, chili powder, salt and cayenne. Pierce potatoes with a fork several times; add to mustard mixture and toss to coat. Place in prepared pan. bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees F for 30-40 minutes or until tender.

Submitter: 
Shirley  Glaab
Description: 
'Even mild red potatoes can be a 'standout' side dish when they're tossed with this mustard and spice coating, then baked,' noes Shirley Glaab of Hattiesburg, Mississippi. 'They perk up any meal.'
