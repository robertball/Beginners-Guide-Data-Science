Title:
Zucchini and Soy Bean Stir fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 zucchini, sliced
5 large mushrooms, sliced
1 medium onion, chopped
1 large carrot, chopped
1/2 cups shelled soy beans
1/3 cup cauliflower
1/3 cup broccoli
1 tblspn garlic, finely chopped
2 tblspn olive oil
1 1/2 tblspn reduced sodium terrakyi sauce
2 tspn light agava nectar
1 tblspn of water

Directions:
1.  Add broccoli, cauiflower, soy beans and carrots to a microwave-safe bowl with 1 tblspn of water, cover with plastic wrap, and nuke for 1 minute, until tender crisp. Set aside.
2. Heat oil over medium heat in a pan.
3. Add onion, allow to carmalize.
4. Add nuked veggies, zucchini and mushrooms to the pan.
5. Add garlic, stir on medium heat about five minutes.
6. Add terrakayi sauce and agava nectar, stir into veggies, abotu five minutes.
Remove from heat and serve warm, with toasted sesame seeds.

Submitter: 
Kierston
Description: 
I invented this while goofing around in the kitchen, it ended up being a hit with my family! If the veggies are steamed tender-crisp before thrown in the pan it cuts the cooking time in half and makes it a quick healthy lunch!
