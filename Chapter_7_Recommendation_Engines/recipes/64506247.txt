Title:
Spinach Lasagna Roll ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 lasagna noodles
2 cups finely chopped baby spinach, from about 3 ounces
1 cup low-fat cottage cheese
1 cup ricotta cheese
1 cup mozzarella cheese
1 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup spaghetti sauce

Directions:
Cook Lasagna noodles to al dente according to package
in a separate bowl, combine all ingredients except sauce and 1/3 cup of parmesan cheese.
spread noodles out, spoon cheese mixture on each noodle, roll noodle up and place in pan
top with sauce and remaining parmesan cheese,
bake at 350 degrees until cheese is bubbly, approx 20-30 min
