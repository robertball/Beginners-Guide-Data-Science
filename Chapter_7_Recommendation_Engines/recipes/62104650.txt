Title:
Easy Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
any type of pasta
1/3 cup chopped onions
1 clove garlic (minced)
1/3 cup of light cream
1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
1/3 cup of parmesan cheese
2 slices of cooked ham (cubed)
3 tablespoons water
salt, pepper and herbe de provence to taste

Directions:
Cook the pasta in boiling water with a bit of salt and oil until soft or al dente
Meanwhile, chop the onions and garlic and set aside. Shred the cheese.
Heat a skillet with about 1 tablespoon of olive oil and cook the onions and garlic for two minutes or until soft.
Add cream, cheese and water and stir occasionally until it forms a sauce.
Add salt, pepper and rosemary to taste
Pour the sauce over the pasta and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kali
Description: 
The easiest aflredo sauce ever. I made it on a lonely Sunday night with the left over contents of my fridge. It's an easy base so you can add to it whatever you like.
