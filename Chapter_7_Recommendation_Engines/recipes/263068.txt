Title:
Juli's Bee's Knees Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5292677.jpg
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cups pineapple juice
2 cups gin
1 1/2 cups lemon juice
1 1/2 cups white grapefruit juice
1/2 cup honey
1 grapefruit, halved and sliced

Directions:
Blend pineapple juice, gin, lemon juice, grapefruit juice, and honey in a blender until frothy. Pour into a pitcher; serve in glasses over ice. Garnish with grapefruit slices.

Submitter: 
Juliana Hale
Description: 
The classic Bee's Knees cocktail dates back to Prohibition, when the phrase became slang for the best. This updated version proves that the best can get better.
