Title:
Roasted Cauliflower with Lemon - KID APPROVED! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 large head of cauliflower OR 3/4 of a small head.
1 lemon zested
1 tbsp of water
2 tbsp of olive oil
Pepper to taste
Salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat your over 400 (electric) 425 (gas)
Cut up the cauliflower into bite sized pieces.  Place the cauliflowers into a baking dish big enough to fit it all on one layer.  Be sure to pick up those little crumbs because they will crisp up nicely in the oven.
Drizzle all of the olive oil over the cauliflower. Add pepper and salt to match your taste.
Sprinkle with lemon zest.
Get your hands in there and mix around until the seasoning, lemon and oil is spread mong all of the cauliflower.
Pop this baby in the oven for 25-35 minutes or until the edges get crispy and those little bits of cauliflower crisp up nicely.
Serve.
Note: Add a squirt if lemon if you like.  Be careful not to add too much or the dish may get bitter.
Enjoy

Description: 
This side dish serves as a perfect replacement for boiled or steamed cauliflower.  It goes perfect when served with a protien such as chicken or fish.
