Title:
Smoked Sausage Lasagna Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 lasagna noodles, cooked
6  4" chunks of smoked sausage
6 slices mozzarella cheese
1  8oz. jar spaghetti sauce
Freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Split each piece of sausage lengthwise, not cutting the whole way through.
Fold piece of mozzarella and place in split of sausage.
Roll up in one cooked lasagna noodle and place in baking dish.
Repeat with remaining sausage and noodles.
Pour spaghetti sauce over all and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Bake, covered, at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
