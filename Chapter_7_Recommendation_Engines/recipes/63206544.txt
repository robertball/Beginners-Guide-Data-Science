Title:
Crustless Spinach and Green Onion Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbs Vegetable Oil
1/2 an Onion chopped
small bunch of green onion
1 10 oz pkg chopped frozen spinach drained
5 eggs beaten
3 cups shredded cheese (gruyere, asiago, cheddar, etc)
1/4 teas salt
1/8 teas black pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. lightly grease pan/s (either a pie/ cake pan or muffin or mini muffin tins) Heat oil in large skillet over medium high heat. add onions and cook, stirring occasionally until they are soft. stir in green onions and then spinach until excess moisture has evaporated. Combine eggs cheese, salt and pepper.  mix in spinach mixture and stir and blend scoop into prepared pans.  for muffin pans, check after 35 minutes, should just be getting golden brown at edges. let cool for 5 minutes before removing from the pan.

Submitter: 
Christina S. Wall
Description: 
This quiche has no crust and contains no gluten making it ideal to take places where there might be people with a wheat/gluten allergy or are on the Atkins diet. we just love how they reheat well so you can make muffin sized ones and take them on the go reheated.
