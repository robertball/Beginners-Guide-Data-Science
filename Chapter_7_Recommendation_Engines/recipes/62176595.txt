Title:
Vanishing oatmeal rasin cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C butter
1C packed brown sugar
1/2C white sugar
2 eggs
1tsp vanilla
1 1/2C flour
1tsp baking soda
1tsp cinnamon
1/2tsp salt
3C oatmeal(old fashioned uncooked)
1C rasins

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. Blend together the butter and sugars until creamy. Add eggs and vanilla; beat well
Add the flour, soda,cinnamon,salt;mix well. Stir in oats and rasins. Drop by rounded teaspoons onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 10-12min or until golden brown. Cool 1min then remove to wire rack to cool.
