Title:
Judy's Fresh Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe tomatoes cut in bite-sized pieces
1/4 sweet onion, chopped
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup fresh basil julienned
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and stir to coat with olive oil.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Allow to sit on counter for at least 30 minutes to meld flavors and draw out liquid from tomatoes.
Serve as a side dish.

Submitter: 
Judy in Delaware
Description: 
This is a terrific side dish with grilled sausages, chicken or steak.  Easy, delicious and healthy!  It's especially good with your own vine-ripened tomatoes.
