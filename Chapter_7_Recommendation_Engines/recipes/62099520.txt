Title:
Pumpkin Pecan  Bread with Caramel Glaze` Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/3 cups flour
3 cups sugar
2 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg
1- 15 oz canned pumpkin
1 cup vegetable or cannola oil
4 eggs, beaten
2/3 cup water
1/2 cup chopped pecans
Glaze:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup whipping cream
1 tsp vanilla
2/3 cup powered sugar
1/4 cup pecans for decorating

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
grease and flour 2 loave pans or a bundt cake pan - set aside
In a large bowl combine the flour,sugar, soda,salt,cinnamon and nutmeg - set aside
In a second bowl combine the pumpkin, oil, and eggs till well combined
Mix the flour mixture into the pumpkin mixture alittle at a time until smooth
Once all the flour and pumpkin mixtures are combined - mix in the water - stir and then add the pecans.
Spoon into thegreased and floured loaf pans/bandt cake pan
bake for 55-60 minutes or until knife comes out clean
While the bread is cooling for 10-15 minutes - you can make the glaze
In a saucepan, over low heat, melt the butter
Stir in the sugars and whipping cream until the sugars are dissolved - remove from heat and allow to cool for 20 minutes
Remove the bread from the pans -set aside
After the 20 minutes are up and the sugar/butter mixture is cooled add in 1 tsp vanilla and the powdered sugar - beat together till smooth
Spoon the glaze over the cooled loaves and top with the chopped pecans

Submitter: 
Sylvia S.
Description: 
This is a VERY yummy pumpkin bread recipe that was given to me years ago. The bread is super moist and the caramel glaze makes it even more mouthwatering. We always wrap up loaves and add a bow and give as gifts over the holidays.You WILL NOT be disaapointed with this !!!
