Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
1 cup oil
3 cups flour
2/3 cups water
1 cup cooked pumpkin (or canned)
4 eggs
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Beat eggs
add pumpkin, oil, and water
sift all dry ingredients together
Mix dry with egg mix
Pour into loaf pans
Bake 350 for 20 min
