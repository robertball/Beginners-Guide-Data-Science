Title:
Hannah's Fruit Salad Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 cups fresh fruit (blackberries, blueberries, raspberries, strawberries, etc.)
3 envelopes unflavored gelatin
3 cups white grape juice
< 1/2 c sugar

Directions:
Fill a bundt pan with fruit.
Soften gelatin by sprinkling over 1/2 cup of grape juice.
Dissolve sugar in another 1/2 cup grape juice over medium heat.
Remove from heat and add in gelatin mixture, stir until gelatin is dissolved, and add in remaining grape juice.
Pour over fruit and refrigerate at least 4 hours before serving.
