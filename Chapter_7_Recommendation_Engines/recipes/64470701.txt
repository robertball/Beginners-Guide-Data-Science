Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2467629.jpg
Ingredients:
400g pkt jam sponge roll, sliced
60ml (1/4 cup) thickened cream
1 tablespoon Malibu liqueur
500g good-quality dark chocolate. 250g chopped and     melted. 250g for snacking on while your waiting
120g shredded coconut

Directions:
Line a large baking tray with baking paper.
Crumble the jam roll into a bowl. Stir in the cream and Malibu.
Roll tablespoonfuls of the mixture into balls. Place on prepared tray. Place in the fridge for 1 hour or until firm...hmmmm firm
Line a large baking tray with baking paper.
Place the coconut in a shallow bowl.
Use a fork to dip the balls, 1 at a time, in melted chocolate to coat. Gently tap the fork on the edge of the bowl to drain excess chocolate.
Roll in coconut to coat. Transfer to prepared tray. Place in the fridge for 30 minutes and wait impatiently to eat!!
