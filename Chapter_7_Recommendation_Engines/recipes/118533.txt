Title:
Cranberry Coffee Cake with Biscuit Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981885.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups biscuit/baking mix
2 tablespoons sugar
2/3 cup milk
1 egg, beaten
2/3 cup jellied cranberry sauce
TOPPING:
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
GLAZE:
1 cup confectioners' sugar
2 tablespoons milk
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the biscuit mix, sugar, milk and egg. Pour into a greased 8-in. square baking dish. Drop cranberry sauce by teaspoonfuls over batter. Combine topping ingredients; sprinkle over cranberry sauce. Bake at 400 degrees F for 18-23 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack.
In a small bowl, combine the glaze ingredients; drizzle over coffee cake.

Submitter: 
Doris  Brearley
Description: 
'This recipe was given to me by a former neighbor,' pens Doris Brearley from Vestal, New York. The yummy cake relies on baking mix, canned cranberry sauce and an easy nut topping, so it's truly a quick bread.
