Title:
Sweet Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 oz. can of sweet potatoes (drained and mashed)
*or use 4 medium fresh, peeled, boiled and mashed.
3/4 c Sugar
1/2 c Milk
1/2 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Vanilla
2 eggs-slightly beaten
2 1/2 tbsp. butter
Topping
3/4 c. brown sugar
1/3 c. butter
1/2 c. flour
1 c. pecans, chopped

Directions:
Mix first set of ingredients and pour into square
baking dish (buttered).  Usually a 9x9 will do.
Mix topping ingredients, crumble over potato
mixture.  (should be about 1/2in thick).
Bake in preheated oven at 350 degrees uncovered.

Submitter: 
TVESTA
Description: 
This is a family recipe.  It sounds 
iffy, but if you like sweet potatoes, 
this is up your alley.  Be aware, bake
for total time indicated.
