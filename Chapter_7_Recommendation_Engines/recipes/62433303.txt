Title:
Taco Seasoning in a bag Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/466159.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbs Chili powder
1 Tbs Onion Powder
1 Tbs Garlic Powder
1/2 Tbs Corn Starch
1/2 Tbs Paprika
1 tsp Cellery seed
1 tsp beef bullion (powder)
1 tsp Black pepper
1 Tsp Kosher salt
1 tsp Crushed Chillies
1 Tsp Oregano dryed
1 tsp Ground Mustard
1/2 Tsp Accent
1/2 Tsp Coca powder

Directions:
Combine all together in a zip top bag and shake.
To use Brown 2 pounds ground beef, Drain fat add 1/4 cup water and bag of seasoning, simmer until thickened.
