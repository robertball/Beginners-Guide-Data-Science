Title:
Green with Envy Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986470.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Margarita Mix
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
1/3 cup melon liqueur
5 cups ice
Limes for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Margarita mix, BACARDI(R) Rum and melon liqueur.
Add ice and blend for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with limes.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
Melon liqueur, rum and margarita mix really pack a punch in this fruity summer Margarita.
