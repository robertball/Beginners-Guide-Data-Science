Title:
No Added Fat Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oats
1/4 cup coconut flour
1/4 cup almond flour
(coconut & almond flour are defatted - different from ground coconuts/almonds. can be substituted with more oats/other flour)
1/4 cup applesauce
3tbsp sugar

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. Blend til crumbly in food processor. Pat into baking pan. Bake 20 min.
