Title:
Extraordinary Turkey Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground turkey
1 (10 oz) package chopped, frozen spinach, thawed and drained
1 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 cup shredded, reduced fat mozzarella cheese
1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1 egg
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 tbsp. olive oil for browning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Thoroughly mix all ingredients together, except for olive oil and then form into 1 inch balls.
Heat 2 tbsp. of olive oil in large skillet until hot, add formed balls and brown on all sides (approximately 5 - 10 minutes).
Remove browned meatballs from skillet and place in a large ungreased baking dish and bake uncovered for 30 minutes until done.

Submitter: 
Irish916
Description: 
A lighter meatball that's full of flavor using ground turkey, spinach, bread crumbs and 2 cheeses.  Great all on their own as an appetizer or for use in any recipe that calls for meatballs.
