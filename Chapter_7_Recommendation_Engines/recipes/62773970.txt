Title:
Grandma's Tossed Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:  Torn Romaine, torn endive, sliced carrots, sliced radishes, sliced cucumbers, quartered tomatoes, and sliced celery.
Dressing:  Vegetable oil, red wine vinegar, garlic salt, and salt.

Directions:
Mix salad ingredients together in a large bowl.  Add remaining ingredients to taste (2 oil: 1 vinegar).  Mix with hands until salad is coated with dressing.

Submitter: 
Gina Guerra
Description: 
I grew up on this salad.  My Grandma serves it with every holiday meal.  I, sometimes, have trouble finding endive where I live.  If I can't find it, I simply omit it.
