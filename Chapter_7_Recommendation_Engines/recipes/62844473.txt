Title:
Grilled Black Bean Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10-15 flour tortillas
cooked black beans
grated cheese
oil for grilling
vinaigrette

Directions:
Warm tortillas and beans.  Roll beans and cheese in tortillas to make burritos.
Heat cast iron skillet to medium heat.  Heat a small amount of oil in skillet for each burrito.
Grill burritos in skillet, browning each side.
Top with vinaigrette or other burrito toppings as desired.
