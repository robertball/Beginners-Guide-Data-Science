Title:
Mom's Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 Cup Butter/Margarine
1 lb Light Brown Sugar
3 Eggs
2 3/4 tsp Baking Powder
Pinch of Salt
2 1/2 Cups Flour
12 oz Bag of Chocolate Chips
1 cup Chopped Walnuts

Directions:
Melt Butter/Margarine and then mix with Brown Sugar - add eggs - mix - add in Baking Powder, Salt, Flour, Chocolate Chips and Walnuts - mix. Spread onto greased cookie sheet and Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Doug Matthews
Description: 
These are my Mom's Blonde Brownies - I keep losing the recipe so I thought that this was the best place to keep it - I've doubled the amount of chocolate chips from Mom's original.
