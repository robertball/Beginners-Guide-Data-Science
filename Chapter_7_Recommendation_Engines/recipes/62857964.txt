Title:
Red Velvet Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg. (8 oz. each) cream cheese, softened
Â½ cup sugar
1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
2 eggs
2 oz. semisweet baking chocolate, melted
1 Tbsp. red food color
1 prepared chocolate crumb crust (6 oz.)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating on low speed after each addition until blended.
Measure 1 cup batter into medium bowl. Stir in melted chocolate and food color. Pour into crust. Top with remaining cheesecake batter.
Bake 40 minutes or until center is almost set. Cool completely on wire rack.
Refrigerate 3 hours or overnight.
