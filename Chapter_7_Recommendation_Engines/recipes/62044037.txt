Title:
White Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meat Sauce:
1 lb ground beef
1 lg onion chopped
2 cloves garlic minced
1 can 14.5 oz diced tomatoes
2 tbl tomato paste
1 beef boullion cube
1 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
White Sauce:
2 tbl butter or margarine
3 tbl all purpose flour
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 cups milk
2 1/2 cups shredded mozarrella cheese, divided
10-12 oz uncooked lasagne noodles

Directions:
In dutch oven, cook beef, onion and garlic until meat is browned and onion tender.  Drain fat if you wish. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, beef boullion, and seasonings.  Cover and cook over medium heat 20 minutes stirring once or twice.
Meanwhile, melt butter in medium sauce pan over medium heat.  Stir in flour, salt and pepper. Gradually stir in milk.  Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally until thickened.  Reduce heat and cook 1 minute.  Remove from heat and stir in half of the mozarella cheese.
To assemble lasagne, pour half meat sauce in 13 X 9 X 2 inch baking dish.  Cover with half of the dry lasagne noodles,then cover with remaining meat sauce.  Top with remaining noodles.  Pour white mozarella sauce over all.  Sprinkle with remaining mozarella cheese.  Add fresh grated parmesan if desired.
Cover and bake at 400 degrees 45 minutes or until noodles test done.

Submitter:

Description: 
When you're hungry for lasagna and short on time, this makes an excellent choice for weekday cooking!  Serve with a green salad and some french bread.
