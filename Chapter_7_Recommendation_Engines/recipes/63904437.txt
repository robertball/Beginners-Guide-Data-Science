Title:
Jeff's Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1088115.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c oats
1 c coarsely chopped nuts
1/2 c unsweetened shredded coconut
3 T packed brown sugar
3/4 t cinnamon
1/2 t ginger
1/4 t salt
1/3 c honey
2 T vegetable oil
1 c dried fruit

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300F.  Line rimmed baking sheet with parchment.  Mix first 7 ingredients in large bowl.  Stir honey and oil in saucepan over med-low heat until smooth.  Pour honey mixture over oat mixture; toss.  Spread on prepared sheet.  Bake until golden, stirring every 10 mins, about 40 mins.  Place sheet on rack.  Stir; cool.  Mix in fruit.

Submitter:

Description: 
A simple way to make expensive store-bought granola on the cheap.
