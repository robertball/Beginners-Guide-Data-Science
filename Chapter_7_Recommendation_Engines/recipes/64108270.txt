Title:
Hot Milk Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
2 cups flour
2 t baking powder
2 t vanilla
2 T butter melted into
1 cup hot milk
Topping:
6 T butter
3/4 cup brown sugar
4 T milk
1 cup coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Beat eggs and sugar together until thick
Sift together flour and baking powder
Add vanilla to egg mixture
Melt the butter in the hot milk
Add all together and mix well
Pour into prepared 13x9 glass pan
Bake for 40-45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean
Allow cake to cool for 5 minutes.
Spoon topping on cake with a fork.
Toast under broiler until golden.
Topping:
Melt the butter and add to the milk.
Stir the brown sugar into the milk mixture.
Add coconut

Description: 
This is a very old recipe that was my Mom's. Regardless of where I take it, there are never any leftovers.
