Title:
Chicken Tika/Chicken Tika Masala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CHICKEN TIKA
==============
1 lb boneless chicken
1 tsp salt
juice 1/2 lemon
1/2 tsp tandoori color
2 cloves garlic chopped
1/2 inch piece ginger peeled and chopped
2 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp garam masala
1/4 whole nutmeg grated
1/2 tsp turmeric
5 oz natural yogurt
4 tbs oil
1/2 tsp chilli powder.
CHICKEN TIKKA MASALA
====================
1 lb Chicken Tika  (see above)
1/2 inch root ginger peeled and grated
2 cloves garlic peeled and chopped
1 tsp salt
2 oz unsalted butter
1 sm onion finely chopped
1/4 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp garam masala
4 fl oz liquid made up from the reserved juice from the
precooked Chicken Tikka and warm water
10 fl oz double cream
2 heaped tbs ground almonds

Directions:
CHICKEN TIKA
==============
Cut chicken into 1 inch cubes. Sprinkle with 1/2 tsp salt from the specified amt
and lemon juice mix well cover and set aside for 30 mins.
put rest of ingredients into processor and blend till smooth.
Put this marinade into a sieve and hold sieve over chicken pieces.
Press the marinade through the sieve till only a very coarse mixture is left.
coat the chicken thoroughly and cover. Marinate overnight in the refrigerator.
Pre heat oven to 230c/450f
Line a tin with foil
Thread chicken onto skewers leaving 1/4inch gap between pieces.
Place skewers in tin and brush with remaining marinade.
Cook in center of over for 6-8 mins.
Shake off excess liquid from chicken and keep aside if doing Chicken Tikka Masala
CHICKEN TIKKA MASALA
====================
Mix together the ginger, garlic and 1/2 tsp salt from the specified amt, and
crush to a pulp. keep the remaining salt aside for later use.
Melt the butter gently and fry the onions for 2-3 mins.
Add the ginger/garlic paste and cook for 1 min.
Stir in the turmeric and then the cumin, coriander, garam masala and chilli powders
stir and cook for 2 mins.
add liquid and stir gently.
Gradually add the cream and stir.
Add the remaining salt and simmer for 5 mins. then add the chicken.
Adjust heat to low cover and cook for 10 mins.
Stir in the ground almonds and simmer 5-6 mins
Remove from heat and serve.
