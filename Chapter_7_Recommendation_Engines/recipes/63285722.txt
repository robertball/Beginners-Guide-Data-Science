Title:
Britts Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs hamburger
1 box stove top stuffing
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 cup ketchup

Directions:
Soften stove top in milk and eggs.  Add rest of ingredients, top woth extra ketchup.
Bake for 1 hour and 15 min in a 350 degree oven.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
Stove top stuffing is used instead of bread crumbs
