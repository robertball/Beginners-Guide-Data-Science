Title:
BOCA® Breakfast Browns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 large red potato, peeled, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
2 frozen BOCA Meatless Breakfast Patties, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
2 mushrooms, chopped
1 tablespoon chopped onion
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
HEAT oil in medium nonstick skillet on medium heat. Add potato; cover. Cook 8 to 10 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally.
ADD remaining ingredients; mix lightly.  Cook and stir 5 minutes or until potatoes are lightly browned and mixture is heated through.
SERVE with ketchup.

Submitter: 
BOCA®
Description: 
A potato-perfect side dish to round out even the simplest of breakfasts.
