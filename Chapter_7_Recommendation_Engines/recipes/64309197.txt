Title:
Apple Oat Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups oat bran
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
3 Tbsp brown sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup apple juice
1/4 cup milk
1 egg
2 Tbsp oil
2 Tbsp honey
1 cup diced apple, peeled
2 Tbsp raisins, optional

Directions:
Combine oat bran, flour, brown sugar, baking powder, salt and cinnamon and set aside.
In another bowl combine apple juice, milk, oil, egg, and honey.
Add flour mixture, apples and combine until just moistened.
Fill 12 greased muffin cups and bake at 400 degrees for 18-20 minutes, or until golden brown and toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
A healthy tasty muffin for breakfast or for a snack.
