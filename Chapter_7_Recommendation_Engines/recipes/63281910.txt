Title:
Pizza Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices whole wheat bread
Pizza Sauce:
1 can tomato paste
1 tsp honey
1 tsp italian seasoning
1 large clove garlic, minced
Toppings:
2 cups shredded mozarella
1 cup chopped olives

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Place bread slices on a cookie sheet.
Mix ingredients together for pizza sauce in a small bowl, set aside.
Toast bread for 5 minutes.
Spread pizza sauce on toasted bread, top with cheese and olives.
Toast bread until cheese is melted and slightly golden.
Enjoy!
