Title:
Fat Boy's Smoked Baby Backs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Wet ingredients
1/2 cup vinegar
1/3 cup worcestershire sauce
Sauce ingredients
1 cup ketchup
1 tbsp hot sauce (sylvia's or crystal)
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 cup honey
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
3 tbsp A-1 sauce
1 clove chopped garlic
3 racks of baby back ribs

Directions:
remove membrane from ribs.
wash and pat dry.
coat with favorite dry rub.
marinate overnight with favorite marinating sauce (any will do)
place in a shallow baking dish with about 1/4 of water and tent dish with heavy duty aluminum foil, bake on 200 for about 1 1/4 hours.
Remove and place on smoker, or grill with wood chips (maintain a temp. of atleast 225 degrees).
mix wet ingredients and pour into spray bottle. spray ribs liberally every 30 min. so they don't dry out from smoke.
Sautee chopped onions and add ketchup as well as the rest of the sauce ingredients, and simmer for 20 minutes.  Add sauce to ribs 15 minutes before taking them off grill so that the sauce caramelizes.
