Title:
Black Rat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1060387.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ice, or as needed
1/3 cup pulp-free orange juice
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers dark rum
2 fluid ounces cola-flavored carbonated beverage, or as needed

Directions:
Fill a high ball glass with ice; pour in orange juice, dark rum, and cola. Stir.

Submitter: 
Dan Anderson
Description: 
Word of mouth is, this liqueur is thought to have been a staple drink among sailors who wanted to ward off scurvy and lighten the mood on long sea voyages.
