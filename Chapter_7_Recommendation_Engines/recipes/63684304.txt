Title:
Spinach Quiche Stuffed Portabella Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large portabella mushrooms
3 eggs
6 egg whites
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup nonfat milk
1 garlic clove, chopped
1 tbsp Italian seasoning
1 cup cooked spinach
1/2 cup parmesan shredded cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat 375 F
2. Remove mushroom stems, wipe with damp cloth
3. Spray baking sheet with cooking spray
4. Whisk together all ingredients except 1 tbsp parmesan cheese
5. Cook and scramble egg mixture until it starts to thicken
6. Scoop partially cooked egg mixture into mushroom caps
7. Sprinkle with remaining parmesan cheese
8. Bake 20 minutes
