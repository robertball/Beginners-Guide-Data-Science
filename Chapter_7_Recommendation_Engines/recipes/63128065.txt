Title:
Fresh Strawberry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. fresh strawberrie, washed and hulled, sliced in halves
2 tbsp. sugar
1/4 c. red wine vinegar
1/4 c. extra virgin olive oil
dash salt

Directions:
Place strawberries in food processor and blend 10 seconds, just until strawberries are lightly pureed.  Add sugar, vinegar and salt.  Blenderize 10 seconds.  Add oil.  Blenderize 10 seconds.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
Springtime, when the strawberry-picking season is upon us, this delectable dressing delivers the long-awaited flavor of the season.  Top your fresh salad with this dressing and a dash of toasted sunflower seeds.  Add fresh grilled chicken for a texture combination that is truly satisfying.
