Title:
my broccoli casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/308658.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bushel of broccoli cut into pieces.
1 small onion chopped
1/2 stick butter
3 C. cooked white rice
1 tsp. chopped garlic
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 can mushroom soup
1/3 block of velveeta
1 C. of milk
1 jar of cheese whiz

Directions:
1. add cut up broccoli onion and salt and pepper in a medium cooking pot. cook until broccoli becomes tender.
2. Add your garlic, mushroom soup, Velveeta and milk to your broccoli. cook over medium heat until Velveeta is melted. stirring repeatedly.
3. turn off heat and stir in your cooked rice.
4. put your broccoli mixture in two greased bread pans or one 9x13 cake pan.
5. Cook at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 min.
6. when done. poor your cheese whiz warmed up over top and serve.

Submitter: 
cookingmommy
Description: 
a recipe I came up with after eating at a local restaurant and trying there broccoli casserole. Decided to make my own, and everyone loved it, and went back for seconds!!
