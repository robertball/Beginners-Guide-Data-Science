Title:
Crock Pot Potato Soup with Hashbowns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 30 oz bag of frozen shredded hashbrowns
3 14 oz cans chicken broth
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/4 tsp pepper
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese

Directions:
In a crock pot, combine everything but cream cheese. Cook on low for 6-8 hours.
About 1 hour before serving, add cream cheese. Heat until thoroughly melted.
