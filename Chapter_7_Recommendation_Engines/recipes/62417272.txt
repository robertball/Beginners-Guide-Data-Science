Title:
Scottley's Avacado Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium-ripe avacados
1/2 cup prepared bacon bits
1/4 cup diced onion
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 cup cucumber ranch salad dressing
4 leaves of bib lettuce
1/2 cup shredded mild cheddar cheese
3/4 cup finely crumbled corn chips

Directions:
Remove avacodos from skin and cut into chunks after removing pit.  Gently toss with salt, pepper, onion, lemnon juice, salad dressing, bacon bits, and shredded cheese in medium size bowl.  Place equal portions of mixture onto leaves of bib lettuce on four individual serving plates.  Top with crumbled corn chips to garnish.

Submitter: 
Scottley
Description: 
Was messing around in the kitchen one day with some things I got on sale.  This quick, easy, and delicious dish was the result.  Makes a great appetizer before a steak dinner.
