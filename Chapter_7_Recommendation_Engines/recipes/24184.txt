Title:
Festive Nut Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/855881.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup macadamia nuts
1 cup cashews
1 cup shelled pistachio nuts
1 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together macadamia nuts, cashews, pistachio nuts and dried cranberries. Transfer to a nice serving dish.

Submitter: 
Diane K
Description: 
Festive Nut Bowl is so easy to make. Dried cranberries add color and a burst of tartness to the mixed nuts. It's always a big hit during cocktail hour.
