Title:
Hot Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T butter
2 T. minced onions
1 clove garlic, minced (or about 1/4 tsp granulated garlic)
8 oz. cream cheese
2 (12 oz.) cans sweet corn or white shoepeg corn, drained
2 T chopped jalapeño peppers
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350º.
Melt butter in a saucepan and sauté the onion and garlic until tender.
Add cream cheese, stir until it melts and mixture is smooth.
Stir in remaining ingredients and pour into a casserole dish.
Bake uncovered for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
Hot corn is always on the table for family gatherings on my husband's side. They love their spicy foods!
