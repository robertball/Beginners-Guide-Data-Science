Title:
Sesame Ginger Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1126160.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1/4 teaspoon sesame oil
1/4 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
2 1/2 teaspoons water

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, mustard, sesame oil, ginger root, and water.

Description: 
A great dipping sauce for chicken.
