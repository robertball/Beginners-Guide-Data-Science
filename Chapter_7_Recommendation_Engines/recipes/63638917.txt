Title:
Vinegar Barbecue Sauce & Mop* Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1025716.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups of distilled vinegar
1 teaspoon hot sauce
3 tablespoons sugar (white, light brown, or dark brown) OR 2 t. stevia
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoons crushed red pepper
1/2 teaspoon finely ground black pepper

Directions:
Pour all the ingredients into a jar and shake. Let it sit for at least 12 hours to allow the flavors to meld. A week is even better.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
This simple sauce is all you need on a properly smoked shoulder. Also great on eggs, vegetables and almost anything you want to add a kick to.
