Title:
Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/439801.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T Olive Oil
2 pkg Mushrooms (fresh)
2 lg Garlic Cloves (minced)
Salt (to taste)
1 pkg Cream Cheese (softened)
¼ c Parmesan Cheese (grated)
¼ t Pepper
¼ t Onion Powder
¼ t Cayenne Pepper
¼ c Bread Crumbs (seasoned)

Directions:
PREHEAT ovent to 350°
SPRAY a cookie sheet with cooking oil spray.
CLEAN and dry mushrooms.
BREAK of stems from caps and set aside.
SALT both the top and bottom of caps.
DICE stems up extremely fine (I used my Magic Bullet).
HEAT oil in a skillet over medium heat.
ADD mushroom stems and cook until most of the moisture has disappeared.
ADD garlic and continue cooking, making sure not to burn the garlic.
SET aside for a bit to cool (I popped it in the freezer for a couple of minutes).
MIX cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, pepper, onion powder & cayenne.
THEN add mixture of mushrooms and garlic.
PUT mixture into a ziplock bag, cut a hole and squeeze into mushrooms caps.
TOP with bread crumbs.
BAKE for 20 minutes.
