Title:
Butternut Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb butternut squash, 1/2 lb carrots, 1 medium baking potatoe, 2 cloves garlic, 1/4 cup chopped onion, 1 stalk celery, 4 cups chicken broth, 1/8 teaspoon ground ginger, 1/4 teaspoon cracked black pepper, 1/8 cup heavy whipping cream.

Directions:
1.  In large saucepan, combine squash, carrots, potato, garlic, onion, celery, ginger, pepper and chicken broth.  Bring to boiling, reduce heat.  Cover and cook 10-12 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
2.  In a food processor bowl or blender, carefully puree the squash mixture a half at a time until smooth.  Return to saucepan, add heavy whipping cream.  Cook and stir until heated through.
