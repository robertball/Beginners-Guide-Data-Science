Title:
Buttermilk Substitute in a Pinch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup plus 3 Tbs Milk
1 Tbs vinegar

Directions:
Place vinegar and milk liquid measuring cup.
Let stand for five minute.

Submitter: 
Muffin Mom N Garlic Girl
Description: 
Its a pretty common request AR exchange post for an emergency substitute for buttermilk. This recipe makes it easy with out a quick trip to the store for one item. You can also use lemon juice or 1 3/4 tsp cream of tartar.
