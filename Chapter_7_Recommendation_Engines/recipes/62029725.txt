Title:
SIMPLE KIELBASA SOUP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (7oz.) PACKAGE ELBOW MACARONI
1 TABLESPOON OIL
1TO 11/2 POUNDS KIELBASA SLICED AND QUARTERED
11/2 CUPS CHOPPED CABBAGE
1/2 CUP CHOPPED CARROT
4 SCALLIONS SLICED WITH TOPS
7CUPS  CHICKEN BROTH
1 TEASPOON DIED THYME
1/4 TEASPOON BLACK PEPPER
1/8 TEASPOON NUTMEG
2-3 DOPS HOT PEPPER SAUCE

Directions:
PREPARE  PASTA,DRAIN. SET ASIDE.
IN DUTCH OVEN COOK KIELBASA,CABBAGE,CARROT,ONION
IN OIL TILL TENDER.
ADD  BROTH AND SEASONINGS. SIMMER FOR 5 MINUTES.
STIR IN PASTA AND HEAT THROUGH.
