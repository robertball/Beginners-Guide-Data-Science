Title:
Cupola House Wassail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon apple cider
2 (12oz) cans frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
3 (3 inch) sticks cinnamon
1 Tbsp ground cloves
1/2 tsp ground allspice
4 dried apple rings
1 thinly sliced orange
1 thinly sliced lemon
4 cups white wine

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in a large non-aluminum pan.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 15 minutes.  Place apple rings, orange, and lemon slices in the bottom of a punch bowl.  Add the wine.  Stir in warm cider mixture.  Makes 22 cups.
