Title:
Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dried onion flakes (minced or chopped)
2 pounds ground beef
1/2 t. seasoned salt
6 slices cheddar cheese
24 small party rolls
24 dill pickle slices

Directions:
Evenly spread the onions on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Gently press the ground beef on top of the onion flakes. Press evenly making one giant pattie.
Sprinkle with seasoned salt to personal preference.
Bake in a 400 degree oven for 20 minutes.
Using a paper towel, pat the excess grease from the top of the pattie.
Top with cheddar cheese, return to oven for 2 more minutes.
Remove from oven, let set 5 minutes then cut into 24 patties. Slice open and separate each party roll. Place bottom of rolls on a large platter. Using a spatula lift pattie with onion layer onto each bun bottom. Top each slider with a pickle slice then the bun lid. Serve immediately. Makes 24 Sliders.

Submitter: 
Tina
Description: 
These White Castle like sliders are not really that healthy but boy are they yummy.  We serve them with fries at our house.  My husband says the dill pickle is what makes them. I prefer mine without the pickle ;o)
