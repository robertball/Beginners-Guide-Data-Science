Title:
Pina Colada Ambrosia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (20 oz.) can pineapple chunks, drained
1 (11 oz.) can mandarin oranges, drained
1-1/2 cups green grapes
1 cup mini-marshmallows
1/4 cup flaked cocount
8 oz. pina colada yogurt
1/4 cup chopped pecans, toasted

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and chill for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
healthnut
Description: 
This refreshing dessert salad is loaded with pineapple, mandarin oranges, grapes, mini-marshmallows, coconut, pecans, and pina colada yogurt.  It is great for picnics or hiking.
