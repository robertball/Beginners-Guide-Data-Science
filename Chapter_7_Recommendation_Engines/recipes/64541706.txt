Title:
Apple Sauce Dressing - 3 pts + Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup honey
1/2 small onion, finely chopped
3 Tbsp. vinegar (cider or red wine)
2 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
2 tablespoons apple juice
2 tablespoons oil
4 teaspoons prepared mustard
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon coarsely ground pepper
Poppy Seeds
Sesame Seeds

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a food processor. Each serving is 1/4 cup.

Submitter: 
Kimberly
Description: 
Low fat dressing for a spinach salad - 3 points plus per serving
