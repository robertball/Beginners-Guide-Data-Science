Title:
crab rolls - delete Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Flour tortillas
1 small container softened cream cheese (with or without chives)
1 pkg imitation crab flakes
1 can sliced black olives
2 Tbs mayonaise, optional

Directions:
Blend together, cream cheese and crab until well mixed.  Add olives and mayo, if desired.  Spread thin amount of crab mixture on flour tortilla.  Roll up jelly-roll style.  Slice approx 1/2 inch thick. Chill.

Submitter: 
anmlnrs
Description: 
Cream cheese, imitation crab, and flour tortillas, rolled together for a great quick appetizer.
