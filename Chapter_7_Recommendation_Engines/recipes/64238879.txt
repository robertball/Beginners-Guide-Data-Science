Title:
Thanksgiving Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup water
1 1/2 cups white sugar
12 oz whole fresh cranberries, washed/drained & picked over and any stems, etc... discarded
smidgen of McCormick Ground Allspice
1/2 smidgen McCormick Ground Cloves
1/4 tsp. McCormick Ground Cinnamon

Directions:
Bring Water and Sugar to Boil in Large Saucepan over medium heat.
Add Cranberries and stir.
Add Allspice, Cloves and Cinnamon and stir.
Bring back to a boil, lower heat and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring often (cranberries will pop).
Remove from heat and transfer to a bowl. Allow to come to room temperature, cover and fridge until serving.
