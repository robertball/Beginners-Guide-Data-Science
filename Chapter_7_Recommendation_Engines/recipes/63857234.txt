Title:
Big Ray's Egg and Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1070619.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups (24 ounces) 4% cottage cheese
3 cups frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
2 cups (8 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese
6 eggs, lightly beaten
1 can mushrooms pieces, drained
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 medium onion,chopped
8 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 Tbp oregeon
1/2 teaspoon salt
Additional shredded cheddar cheese, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl, ingredients except additional cheese.. Pour into a greased 9"x11" pan.
cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.  Remove foil and bake additional 15 minutes.  Sprinkle with cheese if desired.
