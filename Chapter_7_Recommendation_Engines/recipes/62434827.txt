Title:
Chocolate Caramel Toffee Cookies! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/453612.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter, soft
3 1/4 C flour
2 eggs
2/3 C sugar
1 C brown sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 C toffee Bits
3/4 C caramel Bits
3/4 C mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Cream the butter and sugars until pale in color
Add eggs one at a time
Add vanilla
Mix dry ingredients together
Add dry ingredients
Add toffee & caramel bits and chocolate chips
Chill dough
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Scoop onto parchment lined cookie sheets
Bake 10-12 minutes
Cool on cookie sheet for few minutes before removing

Description: 
The ingredients aren't fancy but the combination is delightful!  A great twist on a classic!
