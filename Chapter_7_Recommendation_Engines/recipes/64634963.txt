Title:
Quinoa Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
A few spoonfuls of cooked quinoa
A sprinkling of finely sliced green onions (the white and semi-white parts, not the dark green parts)
A small amount of oil & vinegar salad dressing (I used Newman's own)
A small amount of balsamic vinegar
A few handfuls of raw baby spinach
Diced tomato (optional)

Directions:
Mix quinoa and green onion with a little dressing and balsamic vinegar.  add spinach and add additional dressing if needed.

Submitter: 
HDN
Description: 
Super easy to make.  A little heavier than a salad, a little lighter than a meal.   Very good accompaniment for salmon
