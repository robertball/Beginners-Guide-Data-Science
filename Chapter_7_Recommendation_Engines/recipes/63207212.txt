Title:
Chicken Breasts with Lime Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded to 1/4 inch thickness
2 egg, beaten
3/4 cup dry bread crumbs
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 lime, juiced
3 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup half & half
1 teaspoon minced fresh chives
1/2 teaspoon dried dill weed
8 oz. fresh mushrooms
1/4 cup chopped red pepper

Directions:
Coat chicken breasts with egg, and dip in bread crumbs,mixed with salt and pepper. Place on a wire rack, and allow to dry for about 10 minutes.
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Place chicken into the skillet, and fry for 3 to 5 minutes on each side. Remove to a platter, and keep warm.
Drain grease from the skillet, saute mushrooms and peppers, squeeze in lime juice. Cook over low heat until it boils. Add butter, and half & half,  stir until butter is melted. Season with chives and dill. Spoon sauce over chicken, and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
Thin, breaded chicken breasts topped with a flavorful lime-butter sauce. Pounding the breasts doesn't take long and the resulting tender chicken is worth it.Modified from: Chicken Breasts with Lime Sauce
