Title:
Crispy Catfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/216498.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup finely crushed saltines
1 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
4 (8 ounce) fillets catfish
1/3 cup butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
In a shallow dish, combine the first four ingredients. Pat fillets dry; dip in butter, then coat with crumb mixture.
Coat grill rack with nonstick cooking spray before starting grill. Grill fillets, covered, over medium-hot heat for 10 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork, carefully turning once.
