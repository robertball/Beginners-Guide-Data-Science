Title:
Caramel Pancake Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4374635.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup light corn syrup
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Combine light corn syrup, heavy cream, and brown sugar in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until sugar is dissolved and syrup is warmed through, about 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
ely123
Description: 
The best syrup to put on pancakes, French toast or crepes. The best part is it's extremely easy and quick to make! 
This is a copycat recipe of the syrup from Kneaders Cafe in Utah.
