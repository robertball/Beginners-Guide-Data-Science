Title:
Crispy Dijon Parmesan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/663876.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast tenders
Flour Mixture:
1/3 cup flour
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
Egg Mixture:
1/4 cup egg substitute
2/3 cup hot sweet grainy dijon mustard
1/4 cup honey
1 tsp hot sauce (adjust to taste)
Breading:
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 TBL minced onion flakes
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp Italian Seasoning
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
1 cup corn flake crumbs
1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Spray a baking dish and cooking rack with cooking spray.
In a flat dish combine the flour, pepper and garlic powder and stir until evenly combined.
In a small bowl stir together egg substitute, dijon mustard, honey and hot sauce.
In a flat dish combine the corn flake crumbs, panko bread crumbs, parmesan cheese and the spices listed under "breading."  Stir until everything is evenly distributed.
Dip each chicken tender in the flour mixture and coat evenly.
Then dip the chicken in the egg mixture and shake off any excess.
Drag the chicken in the dry bread crumb mixture, covering evenly.
Place each piece of chicken on a cooking rack placed inside a baking dish.  Spray each piece of chicken with cooking spray.  Place the baking dish with chicken into a preheated oven.  Bake at 375 degrees for about 25 minutes or until the chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
After reviewing several oven fried, baked chicken recipes, I created this spicy, savory, slightly sweet crispy baked chicken without any added salt.
