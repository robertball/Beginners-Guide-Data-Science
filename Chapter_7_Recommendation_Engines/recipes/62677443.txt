Title:
Quick Cajun Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T canola oil
1 onion, diced fine
1 bell pepper, diced fine
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 cans kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 can pinto beans, rinsed and drained
1 can garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 t liquid smoke
1 1/2 t celery flakes
2 bay leaves
1/2 t cayenne pepper
1/2 t dried thyme
1/4 t dried sage
1 1/2 t dried parsley
1/4 t dried oregano
1/4 t paprika
1/4 t black pepper
water
2 bouillon cubes, vegetarian
2 c long grain rice
4 c water
1 t salt
1 T Smart Balance margarine

Directions:
Saute onions and bell pepper in oil. Add garlic, stir thoroughly and remove from heat to prevent burning.
Add beans, spices, and bouillon cubes to pot; add water to top of beans, and return pot to burner.
Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat and simmer 1 hour. Remove bay leaves; mash 1/2 the beans with a potato masher and stir well.
While beans are cooking make rice: rinse rice very well and drain.  Boil 4 c water + 1 t salt + 1 T margarine.  Add rice; stir, reduce heat and cover.  Simmer 15 - 18 min or until all water is absorbed, without removing lid.  Remove from heat, let stand 2 min, then fluff with fork.
Serve beans over rice.

Description: 
Simple recipe using canned beans, with authentic Louisiana flavor. Super healthy and flavorful. Serve over white rice.
