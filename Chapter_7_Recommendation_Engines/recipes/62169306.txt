Title:
Bacon wrapped peanut butter jalapenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 jalapenos, stems removed and seeded
1/2 cup peanut butter
8 pieces bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Cut jalapenos in half and remove seeds and membranes.
Fill jalapenos with peanut butter.
Wrap each jalapeno with half a slice of bacon, secure with a toothpick.
Place jalapenos on a cookie sheet and bake for 25 minutes, or until bacon is cooked.
