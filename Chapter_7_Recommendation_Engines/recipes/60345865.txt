Title:
Jalapeno Popper Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/273589.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup mayonnaise
2 ounces canned chopped green chilies, drained
1 ounce canned diced jalapeno peppers, drained
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
tortilla chips

Directions:
Stir together cream cheese and mayonnaise in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in green chiles and jalapeno peppers. Pour mixture into a microwave safe serving dish, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Microwave on High until hot, about 3 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cooks4Kids
Description: 
This is super yummy. I tone the spice down a little bit by cutting the chilies and peppers in half. Whenever I make it for my kids, I add a smile on top with chips. They love it!!Modified from: Jalapeno Popper Spread
