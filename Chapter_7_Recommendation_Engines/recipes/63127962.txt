Title:
Haddock Veracruz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 tsp black pepper
3 tsp garlic clove(s), minced
14 oz canned tomatoes, diced tomatoes
15 large olive(s),
1/3 cup pitted green olives drained
1/4 cup(s) capers
1 Tbsp canned tomato paste
1/2 cup(s) white wine
1 1/2 pound(s) uncooked haddock fillet(s)

Directions:
Preheat large saute pan on medium-high 2-3 min.
Spray pan with oil, then add garlic and tomatoes; cook and stir 2-3 min or until tomatoes are softened.
Stir in olives, capers, tomato paste, and wine; cook and stir 4-5 min or until thickened slightly.
Add fish to pan; spoon half of tomato mixture over fish.
Reduce heat to medium-low and cover; cook 5-10 min, without turning, or until fish is opaque and flakes easily.
Can use haddock at 5 points per serving, or grouper (6 points per serving), or halibut (6 points per serving).
