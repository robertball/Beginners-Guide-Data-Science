Title:
Kelly's Special Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes,cut in 1 inch chunks
1 onion, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
italian seasoning, just a little
garlic powder
salt
pepper
butter or margarine
8 oz. Velveeta (I use Light Velveeta)

Directions:
Put potatoes in 9x13 pan and sprinkle with onion, bell pepper, and spices.  Dot with butter, about 1/2 cup.  Cover with foil and cook at 350 degrees until tender, about 30 to 40 minutes.
Uncover, dot in several places with Velveeta, cover and put back in oven for 5 or 10 minutes.  Stir to mix in cheese and serve.
