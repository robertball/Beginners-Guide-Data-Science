Title:
Cheesy Broccoli Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless chicken breasts
2 large crowns fresh broccoli
1 tbs vegetable oil
1/2 sweet onion
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup mayonaise
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 packages Knorr butter & herb rice
1 tbs fresh or dried parsley
2 tspn garlic powder
salt & black pepper to taste
1 tbs salt free all purpose seasoning
1/4 tspn curry powder
1 tbs lemon juice
2 tspn dijon mustard

Directions:
-Season chicken breasts with salt,pepper and salt-free all purpose seasoning.Saute chicken in veggie oil until just cooked. Cool and cut up into bite size pieces.
-Cut up broccoli into small flowerettes and slice onion and steam both together for 3 minutes (do not overcook)
-Cook 2 Knorr rice packages as directed, except reduce amount of water by 1/2 cup.
-Put cooked chicken, broccoli, onion and rice into large mixing bowl.  Add cream of chicken soup, mayo, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt and pepper, grated cheese, mustard, more salt-free all purpose seasoning and curry powder and mix thorougly.
Grease casserole dish and pour mixture in. Spread 1/2 cup grated cheese on top of mixture.
-Cook in oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees F.
