Title:
Sour Cream Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter softened
3 cups sugar
6 eggs
1 cup sour cream (8oz)
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
3 cups sifted flour
1 1/2 tsp lemon extract
1 1/2 tsp almond extract
ICING
8 oz cream cheese
1/8 lb butter (2 oz)
1 lb box of powdered sugar
1 heaping tsp vanilla extract
a little water

Directions:
For the cake batter, cream butter and sugar.  Beat at high speed, add eggs 1 at a time, add sour cream then add the rest of the ingredients.  Pour into well greased bundt pan.  Bake at 350 for 1 1/2 hours.  Let stand 10 minutes then remove.
For the icing, cream all ingredients together and ice cake when it is cool.
