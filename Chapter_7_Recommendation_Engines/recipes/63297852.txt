Title:
Yucatan Shrimp Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/870587.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cold water
1 cup roma tomatoes, chopped
1/4 cup red onion, chopped
1/2 TBSP jalepeno pepper, finely chopped
1/4 cup cilantro, finely chopped
3/4 cup ketchup
1 TBSP lime juice
1 tsp red Tabasco sauce
1/4 TBSP Worcestershire
1/2 tsp cajun or blackening spice
1 avocado, cut in 1/2 inch chunks
30 small/medium shrimp, about 1 1/4 cups (peeled, boiled, and chilled, tail removed)
_____________________________________________________
Tortilla chips
sprigs cilantro, no stems

Directions:
Combine ingredients for Yucatan Shrimp Cocktail in bowl or pitcher.  Mix well and chill.
Serve in large goblets or sundae dishes.  Garnish with cilantro and serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This recipe came from the chef of a very good fish restaurant.  It is the best Yucatan Shrimp Cocktail I have had.  You can make a little ahead and refrigerate, then add the shrimp and avocado just before serving.
