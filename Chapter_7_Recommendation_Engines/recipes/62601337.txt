Title:
Vegetarian Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 large flour tortillas
1 can black beans
2 cups cooked rice
1 large or 2 medium onions
4-5 cloves of garlic
5-6 diced plum tomatoes
3-4 cups cheddar cheese
1 can enchilada sauce

Directions:
cook the rice
line a 9x13 or larger casserole dish with a small amount of enchilada sauce
mix black beans, diced onions, minced garlic, 2 cups of cheddar cheese wit cooked rice
fille tortillas with mixture and line them in the casserole dish
pour remaining enchilda sauce, tomatoes and remaining cheese over the top and bake at 350 for 1 hour
