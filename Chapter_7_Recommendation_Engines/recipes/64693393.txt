Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Beef roast
1 packet of ranch seasoning
1 packet of beef au jus
1 cup of banana peppers
1 cup of mushrooms (optional)

Directions:
Place the roast in the slow cooker
Add seasoning packets and banana peppers, mushrooms (optional)
Put slow cooker on low and cook for 5-6 hrs
Serve over noodles

Submitter: 
Tammy Riddle
Description: 
Just add meat and seasonings before work and it's done when you get home
