Title:
Moroccan Chickpea Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2 teaspoon ground cumin
2 teaspoons ground coriander
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (or to taste)
1 teaspoon garam masala
1/2 teaspoon of curry powder
pinch of salt
3 medium potatoes, in 1/2 inch cubes
1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes (do not drain)
1 cup tomato sauce (or basic pasta sauce)
1 cup golden raisins
1 can chickpeas, rinsed
1 bunch of kale, ribs removed
1/2 cup cilantro, finely chopped

Directions:
Heat the olive oil on a large pot or dutch oven. Add the onions and garlic, and sweat until onions are clear.
Add the cumin, coriander, cayenne pepper, garam masala, curry powder, and salt. Cook for one minute, until fragrant.
Add the potatoes, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and raisins to the pot. Add enough water to cover.
Simmer for 10 to 15 minutes, until potatoes are soft.
Add the chickpeas and kale, and simmer until the kale is wilted, about 3 minutes.
Add the cilantro and remove from heat.

Submitter: 
Mari
Description: 
This recipe came to be as a way to use the kale that was flourishing in my garden. It could also incorporate all kinds of other vegetables - whatever is in your fridge that needs to be used.
