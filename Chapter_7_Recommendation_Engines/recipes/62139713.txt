Title:
Corned Beef Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sour cream
4 (2 ounce) pkgs. budig corned beef, diced
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup onions, minced
1 Tbsp worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Mix the Cream cheese with the sour cream.  Add the onions, worcestershire and garlic powder. Add in the corned beef. Refridgerate for at least one hour before serving.  Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Lindsey
Description: 
I am always asked to make this recipe everywhere I go.  All of the ingredients go together so well.
