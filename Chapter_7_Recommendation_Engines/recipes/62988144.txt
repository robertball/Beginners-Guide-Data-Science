Title:
Cinnamon Honey Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter
1 cup powdered sugar
1 cup honey
2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Whip softened butter in mixer. Add remaining ingredients; whip. Stir down and whip some more.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a spread similar to the one at Texas Roadhouse. I found this recipe on pinterest.
