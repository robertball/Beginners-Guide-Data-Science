Title:
Holiday Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter, softened
2 cups packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tbls cherry brandy (optional)
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup chopped walnuts, toasted
4 ounces white baking chips
1/2 cup candied cherries, roughly chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly grease a 13x9x2 inch baking dish.
In large bowl beat butter with electric mixer on medium speed for 30 seconds.  Add brown sugar; beat until well combined.  Beat in eggs, brandy, if using, vanilla, baking powder and salt.  Add flour, beat just until combined.  Stir in walnuts, white chips and cherries.  Spread in prepared pan.
Bake 30 minutes or until golden.  Cool completely in pan on wire rack.  Cut into bars.
