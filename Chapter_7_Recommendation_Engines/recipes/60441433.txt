Title:
Chicken Salad Balsamic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups diced cold, cooked chicken
1 cup diced red apple
1 cup diced green apple
1/2 cup diced celery
3 green onions, chopped -or- 1/2 cup chopped red onion
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
1/4 - 1/2 cup craisins (optional...
1/4 - 1/2 cup raisins ... to total 1/2 cup)
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
1 T honey
1 tsp. garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss together the chicken, apple, celery, onion and walnuts.
In a small bowl, whisk together the honey, garlic, vinegar and oil and pour over the salad.  Season with salt and pepper to taste and mix well.  Let rest 10 to 15 minutes, mix again and chill.  Serve on bed of greens, surrounded with slices of cucumber and tomato.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
The contrasting flavors of sweet apple, crunchy walnuts, savory chicken and tangy balsamic vinaigrette meld beautifully in this simple to prepare chicken salad.Modified from: Chicken Salad Balsamic
