Title:
Sun Dried Tomato Red Pepper Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3134069.jpg
Ingredients:
1 zucchini, peeled & chopped into 1 inch pieces
1 red bell pepper, chopped, seeded & cut into 1 inch pieces
2 garlic cloves
1/4 cup sun dried tomatoes
1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1/2 cup tahini
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp paprika

Directions:
Toss all ingredients in your handy food processor or blender
Blend until combined well
Place in a bowl & serve with your favorite veggies

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Made with zucchini, sun dried tomato, garlic & red peppers this No Bean Sun Dried Tomato Red Pepper Hummus is thick, creamy and has a spicy kick. A delicious side to serve with raw veggies, no one will ever miss the beans.
