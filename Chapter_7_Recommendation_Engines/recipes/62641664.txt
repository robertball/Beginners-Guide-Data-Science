Title:
What the ELLE...WHOLE WHEAT/HONEY PIZZA Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast
1 cup warm sparkling water
2 cups whole wheat flour or 1c wwf + 1c wf
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon honey

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water.  Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
In a large bowl combine flour and salt. Make a well in the middle and add honey and yeast mixture.  Stir well to combine.  Cover and set in a warm place to rise for a few minutes.
Roll dough on a floured pizza pan and poke a few holes in it with a fork.
Bake in preheated oven for 5 to 10 minutes, or until desired crispiness is achieved.
