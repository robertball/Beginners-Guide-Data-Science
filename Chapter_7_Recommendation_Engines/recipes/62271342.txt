Title:
Broccoli Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/344892.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Onion chopped
1 clove Garlic chopped
2 Stocks celery chopped
1/4 Cup Shredded Carrots
1 Lbs Broccoli fresh chopped
1 Med Potato 8oz chopped
1 tbs Olive oil (Butter Optional)
1/2 tsp Fresh crushed black pepper
4 tbls Corn starch
1 tsp Lemon pepper
1 tsp MSG (optional)
4 tbls Chicken base
1/2 cup Heavy cream or (fat free half & half)
6 cups water

Directions:
Place the Garlic, onion, celery and carrots in a 6 quart pot with olive oil and saut√© until onions become clear.
Add the remainder of the ingredients reserving the corn starch and cream. Boil for 20 min then use a potato masher and bring to a chunky pulp.
Mix together corn starch with cream and add. Stir until thickened
