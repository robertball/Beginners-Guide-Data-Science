Title:
Oriental Pepper Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. cooking oil
2 cups water
1 lb. boneless chuck or round steak, cut into strips
1 envelope onion soup mix (or one with mushrooms added)
2 medium bell pepper, cut into thin strips
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
2 tsp. soy sauce
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
1 medium tomato, cut into wedges
1 tbsp. cornstarch
1/2 can diced pineapple (optional)
Cooked rice

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil and brown beef; drain.  Stir in onion soup mix blended with 1 1/2 cups water.  Bring to a boil, then simmer covered 20 minutes.
Stir in bell pepper, soy sauce, ginger and garlic.  Simmer covered 15 minutes longer or until beef is tender.
Stir in tomato and pineapple.  Mix conrstarch with remaining 1/2 cup of water and add to beef mixture.  Bring to a boil, then simmer, stirring constantly until sauce is thickened, about 5 min.  To serve, arrange beef mixture over hot cooked rice.
