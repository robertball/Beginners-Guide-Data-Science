Title:
Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 Large potatoes
2 cups milk
1 stick margerine (1/2 cup)
1 packet onion soup mix
cheddar cheese(optional)

Directions:
Slice the potatoes thinly.
melt the margerine and add the milk and margerine- mixing well
lay the potatoes out in the pan and pour the margerine mixture over the top.
put it into the oven on 350 degrees for about 25 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Ro Oranim
Description: 
this is a recipe for creamy potatoes. I love this recipe.I like to put it in a oven-to-table dish.
