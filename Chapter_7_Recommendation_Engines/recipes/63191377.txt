Title:
Macaroni Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup uncooked elbow macaroni
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 teaspoon sugar
2 teaspoon cider vinegar
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon prepared mustard
Dash pepper
2 celery rib, chopped
3 hard-cooked egg, chopped

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions. drain and rinse in cold water. In a bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, salt, mustard and pepper. Stir in celery. Fold in macaroni and egg. Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description:
