Title:
Bourbon Glazed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds carrots, peeled and sliced diagonally into 1/2" pieces
1/4 cup Brown Sugar
1/4 cup melted Butter
2-4 Tbsp Bourbon

Directions:
Boil or steam a the carrots until al dente.
In a medium sauce pan, combine the sugar, butter and bourbon. Simmer over a medium low heat until the sauce thickens
Pour sauce over carrots and serve immediately

Sweet carrots glazed in a buttery sauce flavored with Bourbon
