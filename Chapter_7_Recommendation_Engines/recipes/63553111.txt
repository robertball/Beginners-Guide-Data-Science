Title:
Shrimp and Pad Thai noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pad Thai noodle Kit with sauce
3 Tbs. vegetable oil, divided
1 egg
20 raw shrimp
1/4 Cup crushed peanuts
1/2 Cup bean sprouts

Directions:
Bring 4 cups water to boil in a large sauce pan. Remove from heat.Add rice noodles; let stand 6 to 8 minutes. or until noodles are soft but firm. Drain. Rinse with cold water.
Heat 1 Tbs. of oil in large frying pan on medium heat. Add egg; cook until scrambled. Remove egg from skillet.
Heat remaining 2 Tbs. oil in the same skillet on medium heat. Add shrimp and cook until pink. Add softened nnodlesand Pan Thai sauce; stirfry 3 to 4 minutes until noodles are tender and sauce is absorbed into the noodles. (If noodles are still too firm, add 1 Tbs. water and continue to fry until tender.
Stir in bean sprouts ans scrambled egg. Serve and garnish with peanuts.
