Title:
Braised beef in red wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lb Braising or stewing beef - cubed
4 rashers of smoked bacon - chopped
1lb shallots
1lb mushrooms
1 bottle of red wine
6 tble spoons tomato ketchup
2 tble spoons tomato paste
4 large garlic cloves - crushed
1 bouquet garni
1 beef stock cube
seasoned flour
olive oil

Directions:
coat the meat in the flour and fry in the oil for approx 5 mins until brown and set aside in a large casserole dish.
fry the shallots in oil for 3-5 mins adding the crushed garlic for the last 2 minutes - add to the beef.
cut mushrooms into quarters - add to casserole.
Warm the wine till bubbling and add to the casserole.
add ketchup, bouquet garni beef stock and bacon with the other ingredients and mix together.
Cover and put in oven at 170c for approximately 21/2 hours, remove lid and cook for a further 1/2 hour.
Alternately put in slow cooker on low for 6-8 hours

Submitter: 
CharlieB
Description: 
An easy to prepare rich and tasty dish that is good enough for a dinner party of informal supper. Can easily adapt for a slow cooker and the flavours will improve if cooked in advance.
