Title:
Aunt Grace's Gingerbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cup molasses
½ cup butter
3 cups flour
1 cup milk
2 tsp. Ginger
2 tsp. Cinnamon
1 tsp. Cloves
¼ tsp. Nutmeg
2 eggs well beaten
1 tsp. Baking soda  in ¼ boiling water

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar, add eggs and molasses and beat well.  Sift flour and spices and add alternately with milk to first mixture.  Stir in dissolved baking soda.  Pour into greased pan lined with wax paper.  Bake @ 350 degrees for 30-40 (test for doneness at 30 min. with a toothpick).

Submitter: 
jmacsaunders
Description: 
This is a family recipe handed down by my great-grandmother.  A family favorite at Christmas time.  A very dark (almost black) moist cake.  Great with vanilla ice cream !
