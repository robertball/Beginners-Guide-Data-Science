Title:
Mung Bean Detox Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup green mug beans-soaked overnight
2 cups water + 1 tsp Salt to cook beans
1-2 cups water for soup broth
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1/4 tsp asefoetida (optional)
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp. powdered turmeric or fresh
1 tsp. cumin seed
1/2 tsp. coriander seed
1 tsp. raw sugar cane
1 pinch garam masala
1.5 tsp fresh ginger
1/2 tsp. fresh garlic
1 Tbs.olive oil
salt to taste

Directions:
Soak beans overnight in water
Drain beans and rinse well and cook 20-25 minutes
Chop ginger and garlic set aside
Heat oil in large saucepan add mustard seeds.
When mustard seeds pop, add Bay leaf and Hing mix well.
Place cooked beans including cooked water and 1-2 c. more
Bring to boil and add remaining spices. Simmer several minutes.

Submitter: 
Lynn
Description: 
It is beneficial with 3-5 day fast; than add cooked vegetables like pumpkins squashes and leafy greens for two days; finally add rice for another two days; then return to your normal diet. Even eating Mung Bean soup as a normal meal can be beneficial to the body giving rest and allowing elimination of unwanted toxins.
