Title:
Fiesta Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 tbl chili
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
2 tsp zesty pepper medley
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, minced
1 1/2 cups salsa
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
4 medium tomatoes, diced and seeded
1 green pepper, diced
1/4 cup onion, minced
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Add ground beef and all spices to a pan. Saute until browned, breaking apart in to small peices as you saute. Take off the heat and set aside.
Mix mayonnaise and sour cream together in a separate bowl.
Combine tomatoes, green pepper and onion in a separate bowl.
In a 7x11 pan, layer beef, salsa, sour cream mixture, tomato mixture and cheese.
Refrigerate until cool. Serve with torilla chips for dipping.
