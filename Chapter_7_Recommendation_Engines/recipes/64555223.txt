Title:
Easy Pan Roasted Red Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3477700.jpg
Ingredients:
3 or more red potatoes
EVOO to coat pan
Salt

Directions:
Slice potatoes either in half or quarters
Heat a pan on med heat.  Coat the bottom with some EVOO and add salt.
Add potatoes.
Cook for approx. 10 mins, turning to insure all sides are brown.
Turn heat down to low, cover and cook an additional 10 mins.

Submitter: 
Sheila Calnan
Description: 
Had some Red Potatoes and came up with this.  Crisp on the outside and creamy on the inside.
