Title:
Cinnamon Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg yolk
1/2 cup sugar
1-2 t cinnamon
1 cup half and half
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 t vanilla

Directions:
Lightly beat egg yolk. Stir in sugar and 1/2 cup of half and half in a small sauce pan. Cook over low heat until mixture thickens slightly. Whisk cooked mixture together with remaining ingredients. Cool and chill mixture for at least 1 hour.
Pour cold mixture into your ice cream attachment for a kitchen aid mixer and process for about 20 minutes. Transfer to a freezer safe container and put in freezer until ready to serve.
