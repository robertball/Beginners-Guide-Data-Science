Title:
White Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 3/4 teaspoons ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tablespoon orange juice
1/4 cup maple syrup
white sugar for rolling

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Sift together the flour, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until fluffy. Beat in the egg, then stir in the orange juice and maple syrup. Gradually stir the sifted ingredients into the maple mixture. Shape dough into 1" balls, and roll them in the remaining sugar. Place the cookies 2 inches apart onto an ungreased cookie sheet, and flatten to 1/2"-1/4".
Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until light tan. Do NOT over bake. Remove to a wire rack to cool. Store in an airtight container.
