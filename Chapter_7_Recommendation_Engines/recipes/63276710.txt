Title:
Low-Carb Almond Ricotta Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. almond flour/meal
1/8 t. salt
1/4 t. baking soda
1 T. flax meal
1/4 c. ricotta cheese
1 egg
1/2 t. vanilla
1 t. coconut oil
1/2 t. coconut oil for pan

Directions:
Heat a frying pan until drops of water "dance".  Put dry ingredients in a small bowl, then add wet ingredients and mix thoroughly.  Put 1/2 t. coconut oil in pan and spread around with a spatula.  Pour batter into pan, cook and turn when bottom of pancake is a light golden brown.  Makes 1 large or two small pancakes.  Recipe can be doubled.

Submitter: 
4givnCyn
Description: 
I came up with this easy recipe to satisfy a pancake craving while following my diet of low-carbs and higher protein. It tastes even more delicious with  blueberries!
