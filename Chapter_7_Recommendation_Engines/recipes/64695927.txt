Title:
Chicken Parmesan Pasta Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp Italian Dressing (not the creamy kind)
3 Chicken Breasts, cubed
1 White Onion, diced
1 Red Pepper, diced
2 Carrots, diced
16 Ounces Rigatoni Pasta
24 Ounce Marinara Sauce
24 ounce of water
1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese, shredded
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese, Shredded
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup White onion, diced
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp basil
1 clove garlic, smashed

Directions:
Heat chicken and Italian seasoning in a large skillet on medium-high heat
Cook chicken until browned and just cooked through, about 5 minutes
Add pasta, marinara sauce and water, lightly combine mixture
Add onion, peppers, carrots and spices
Bring mixture to a boil and then reduce to simmer and cover for 15 minutes
Cover with grated cheeses, no need to stir and continue to cook for another 2-3 minutes or until cheese is fully melted.  You can broil the entire skillet for 4 minutes to crisp up the cheese
Top with chopped basil and enjoy
