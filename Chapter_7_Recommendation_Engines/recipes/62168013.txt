Title:
Tomato Raita (Yogurt) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon mustard seeds
1/8 teaspoon asafoetida
1 teaspoon channa dhal
1 teaspoon olive oil
2 medium tomatoes
1 green chili-pepper or Â½ jalapeno pepper (optional)
1 Tablespoon chopped cilantro
2 cups plain nonfat or low-fat Yogurt
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Wash and chop tomatoes. and chop green chili,
In a skillet add oil, mustard seeds asafoetida & channa dhall.
Heat and stir until mustard seeds pop and channa dhal turns golden brown.
Add chopped tomatoes and green chillies and cook on medium heat for 7 minutes.
Let cool and add Yogurt and salt.  Mix well and garnish with cilantro to serve.

Description: 
Delicate tomatoey yogurt dish that you can serve along something spicy. This is my favorite raita recipe of all time. I think it goes particularly well with my crunchy potato curry.
