Title:
Potato, Leek and Spinach Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 yellow onion, chopped
3-4 leeks, chopped
1 tbsp butter
4 cups water (or chicken/vegetable broth)
4 bullion cubes (if not using broth)
3-4 cups potatoes, cubed
1-2 bunches spinach, chopped
2 c milk
1 tbsp pepper
2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp chili powder

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large soup pan. Add onions and leeks, simmer until tender (roughly 7 minutes).
Add potatoes, water and bullion cubes (or broth). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes.
Add spinach and cook 5-7 minutes until wilted and soft.
Add milk, salt, pepper, paprika and chili powder. Let cook 5 minutes. Add additional spices to taste.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
lawgirl
Description: 
I came up with this soup recipe when I got potatoes, leeks and spinach in my CSA box and didn't know what to do with them. It turned out delicious and is perfect for fall vegetables.
