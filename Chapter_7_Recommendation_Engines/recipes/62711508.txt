Title:
Mom's BBQ Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion minced
2 lbs ground beef
2 eggs, lightly beaten
2 cups crackers
3/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoon worcheschire sauce
1 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
8 ounces tomato sauce
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup vinegar
dash garlic
dash basil
1 teaspoon rosemary

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a medium baking dish.
In a bowl, mix meatloaf ingredients.  Form into balls and brown.  Add tom. sauce, b. sugar, vinegar, garlic, basil, and rosemary sauce and pour over meatballs.  Cook 350 for 60 minutes.
