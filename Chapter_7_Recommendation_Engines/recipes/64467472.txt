Title:
Ghiswhises  'Dinomite's Fluffy Bites' 5??Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2456838.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag Marshmallows
1 large block of Aero chocolate
Crushed pistachios nuts or coating of your choice - hundreds and thousands

Directions:
1 place all chocolate in a glass measuring bowl
2. Place measuring bowl in a pan of boiling water and ensure no water gets into the chocolate
3. Ensure chocolate is completely melted
4 using a cocktail stick dip the marshmallows in the chocolate until completely covered
5. Push the cocktail stick with the marshmallow on the end into half a grapefruit to allow the chocolate to dry until the chocolate remains a little sticky to the touch
6. At this point taken the chocolate covered marshmallow and roll in the crushed pistachio nuts or any thing of your choice
7. Enjoy
