Title:
Blender Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup mashed bananas
1 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
1/2 Tbsp molassas

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients together in bowl
Put wet ingredients into blender and blend
Add to dry ingredients in bowl mix gently and pour into a greased loaf  pan
Bake 350 for 1 hour or until done
