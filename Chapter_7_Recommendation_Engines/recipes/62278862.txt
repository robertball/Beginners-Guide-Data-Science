Title:
POACHED PEARS & RASPBERRY SAUCE w/SURPRISE!!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/349668.jpg
Ingredients:
POACHED PEARS…
(allrecipes.com)
½ (750 milliliter) bottle champagne
1 cup orange juice
1 cup sugar
4 slices orange
4 whole cloves
4 Bosc pears (peeled w/stems intact & bottom sliced to make level)
RASPBERRY SAUCE…
(allrecipes.com)
1 cup fresh raspberries
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon orange juice
1 tablespoon cornstarch
½ cup cold water
DARK CHOCOLATE MOUSSE  the SURPRISE...
(kraftrecipes.com)
1 tub (8oz.) Philadelphia Indulgence Dark Chocolate, softened
1 cup thawed Cool Whip Whipped Topping
1 pastry bag w/tip or quart size freezer Ziploc bag

Directions:
POACHED PEARS~In saucepan over medium heat,combine champagne,orange juice and sugar.  Add orange slices,cloves&vanilla.Bring to a boil&stir until sugar is dissolved.  Place pears in saucepan&reduce heat.Cover&simmer 15 minutes.Remove cover&simmer an additional 30 minutes. Remove pears from the liquid & cool completely.Core pear from bottom to middle of pear w/small melon-ball scoop, remove seeds & leave hole for ‘surprise’.
RASPBERRY SAUCE~Combine raspberries,sugar&orange juice in a saucepan.Whisk cornstarch into the cold water until smooth. Add mixture to saucepan&bring to a boil.Simmer for 5 minutes,stirring constantly until desired consistency is reached.Sauce will thicken as it cools. Puree the sauce&strain through a fine sieve.Serve warm or cold.Sauce will keep in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
DARK CHOCOLATE MOUSSE~Stir ingredients just until blended.
ASSEMBLY~With ‘Cooled’ pears (bottom up) fill hole w/mousse. Place right side up on plate w/wax paper.When ready to serve, place on dessert plate, spoon warm raspberry sauce over pears.
