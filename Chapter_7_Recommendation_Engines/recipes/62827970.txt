Title:
Blueberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 - 1 cup white sugar (depends on sweetness desired)
5 tablespoons cornstarch
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3 tsp lemon juice
4-5 cups fresh blueberries
1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (210 degrees C).
Mix sugar, cornstarch, salt, and cinnamon,lemon juice and sprinkle over blueberries.
Line pie dish with one pie crust.  Pour berry mixture into the crust, and dot with butter.  Cut remaining pastry into 1/2 - 3/4 inch wide strips, and make lattice top or use a full crust for top with several slits (to let steam escape).  Crimp and flute edges.
Bake pie on middle shelf of oven for about 50 minutes, or until crust is golden brown. To keep the crust from over cooking cover edges with tin foil and uncover for last 15 - 20 minutes.

Description: 
There is nothing to beat a fresh blueberry pie. It doesn't need much: sugar, a bit of cornstarch to thicken all the nice juices and cinnamon. It's perfect with fresh berries, but still mighty fine with frozen ones.Modified from: Blueberry Pie
