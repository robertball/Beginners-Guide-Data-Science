Title:
Real-Food Huevos Rancheros Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1913535.jpg
Ingredients:
1 link chorizo (locally sourced)
2 C. refried beans (organic or home-made)
1 C. salsa (organic)
8 corn tortillas (GMO-free)
8 eggs (cage-free)
1 avocado, sliced
Additional hot sauce or cilantro for topping, optional

Directions:
Remove chorizo from casing.  In a large skillet over medium heat, brown chorizo.  Add refried beans and salsa, stir to combine and heat through.
Fry corn tortillas in a skillet until toasted, flip onto plates.
Top each tortilla with 1/4 C. prepared sauce.
Scramble eggs and lay one atop each prepared tortilla.
Top eggs with avocado slices, and additional hot sauce or cilantro, if desired.
