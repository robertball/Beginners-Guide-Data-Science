Title:
Family's Favorite Mini Meat Loaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg, beaten
1 lb. ground beef chuck
1/4 cup bread crumbs
1 tsp. salt
2 stalks green onion
TOPPING:
4 T. ketchup
2 T. brown sugar
1/2 tsp. dry mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together.
Press into a muffin pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
Add the topping.
Bake 10 minutes longer or until done.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
This is one of my family's favorite meat loaves. They eat it much better in the mini shape rather than as a large loaf, even when it's the same recipe. Go figure!
