Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/822816.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1/4 cup chocolate syrup
1/4 cup milk
12 cubes ice

Directions:
In a blender, combine peanut butter, chocolate syrup, milk and ice cubes. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Description: 
This is an excellent chocolaty peanut buttery milk shake.
