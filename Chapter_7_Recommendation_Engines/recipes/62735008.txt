Title:
THE BEST STUFFING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/782957.jpg
Ingredients:
14 cups bread crumbs
3/4 cup butter
2 1/2 cup chopped onions
2 cups chopped celery
4 tsp.crushed dried sage
2 tsp.salt
1 1/4 tsp.crushed dried savory
1 tsp.crushed dried marjoram
1/2 tsp.pepper
1/2 tsp.thyme
1 cup fresh parsley or
2 Tbsp. dried
1/2 tsp.paprika
chicken broth to moisten

Directions:
Combine crumbs and spices.Melt marg. in skillet,add chopped onion and celery.Fry till softened.Add to bread crumbs and spices.Slowly add broth till moist not soggy.
