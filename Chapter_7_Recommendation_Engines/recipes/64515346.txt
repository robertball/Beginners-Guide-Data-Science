Title:
DVO-Willy-England: Cabbage Soup with Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TB butter
2 large onions, finely chopped
8oz bacon, finely chopped
1 small cabbage, shredded
3 potatoes
salt and pepper
4-5 cups vegetable stock

Directions:
melt butter in large saucepan
cook onions and bacon for about 3 minutes
add cabbage and potatoes, season to taste
add the stock/broth and bring to a boil
lower heat and allow to simmer for 30 minutes or until vegetables are tender

Submitter: 
KirbyZmom
Description: 
not a very healthy version of cabbage soup. LOL. i love cabbage, so i would be using a large cabbage. i prefer reduced sodium chicken broth.
