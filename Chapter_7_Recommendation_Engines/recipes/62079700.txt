Title:
Stuffed Burger Bundles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup herb stuffing
1/3 cup evaporated milk
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 can 1/2 fat cream of celery soup
1 tsp. worcestershire sauce
1 Tbsp catsup

Directions:
Mix meat and milk, make into 4 thin patties.  Put 1/4 cup stuffing in center and draw meat over.  Place in casserole and combine rest of ingredients.  Pour over meat and bake at 350 for 35-40 min.

Submitter: 
Lynn Mott
Description: 
This is excellent served with baked or mashed potatoes and a vegetable. It can be made in the morning and baked just before dinner.
