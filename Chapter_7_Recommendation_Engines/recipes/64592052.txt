Title:
Pumpernickel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Sunny Boy cereal (an uncooked raw cereal milled from wheat, rye and flax - used for porridge)
1 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
2 cups warm water
1/4 cup molasses

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients. Make a well in center.
Stir water and molasses in small bowl. Pour into well. Stir to moisten. Batter will be quite runny.
Turn into greased 9" x 5" loaf pan.
Cover with foil.
Bake @ 300 x about 2 hours.
Let stand 10 minutes before removing from pan.

Deena Kordt
