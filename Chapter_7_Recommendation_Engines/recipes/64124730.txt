Title:
Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough...
1/2 c butter, at room temperature
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg yolk
2 c flour
3 tbsp water
1 tbsp lemon juice
Custard...
2 eggs
2 egg yolks
3/4 c heavy cream
3/4 c whole milk
1/2 tsp salt
Pepper to taste
@ 2 c grated or crumbled cheese, herbs, cooked vegetables or cooked meats.

Directions:
Using an electric mixer, cream together butler and salt.
Add egg yolk and beat until creamy.
Reduce speed to low and add flour, water, and lemon juice and mix until just combined.
Shape dough into a disc, wrap with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
When ready to bake, preheat oven to 350 degrees. On a lightly floured surface, roll out dough to @ 12 round and transfer to 9 inch pie plate. Pat to bottom and fold / crimp edge.
Line with parchment paper, fill with pie weights and bake 30 minutes. Remove from oven, remove paper and weights. Prick bottom with fork and return to oven to bake additional 5-10 minutes until pastry is evenly brown. Remove from oven and cool.
To prepare custard, whisk eggs and yolks. Add cream, milk and seasonings and whisk well.
To prepare quiche, place baked pastry shell on baking sheet. Arrange cheese, meats, etc. evenly on bottom and pour egg mixture over fillings.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45-60 minutes. If quiche begins to get too brown, cover with foil.
Remove from oven. Serve warm or at room temperature.
