Title:
Grandma's Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lg butterflied pork chops
1 can Campbell's cream of Mushroom Soup
Oil

Directions:
In a large heavy skillet, brown pork chops in a little oil until done.
Gravy: Add a little water to the mushroom soup to thin to the consistency desired for gravy. Remove the pork chops from the skillet and add the soup mixture to the drippings in the skillet. Stir until mixed well. Relpace the pork chops and continue cooking, covered for about 20 minuted. Serve with rice.
