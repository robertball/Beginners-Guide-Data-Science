Title:
Jan's,  Potato Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/648645.jpg
Ingredients:
5 pounds peeled, russet potatoes
4 tablespoons melted butter
2 tablespoons onion powder
1 tablespoon garlic powder
15 ounce container ricotta cheese
16 ounce container sour cream
1 tablespoon salt (or to taste)
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon crushed, fresh rosemary
4 large eggs
Bacon Salt
1 teaspoon Paprika used as topping
3 tablespoons dry parsley flakes (optional)
foil cupcake pan liners

Directions:
Cube and boil potatoes until tender. About 35 minutes.
Drain well.
Add all the remaining ingredients (except Bacon Salt)and mix with electric mixer until creamy and no lumps.
Spoon or pipe using a large, open star tip, into foil cupcake liners.  Fill to the top.
Sprinkle tops with a generous amount of Bacon Salt, paprika and parsley flakes.
Bake on a baking sheet or in muffin pans at 400 degrees for 35 minutes or until tops are golden brown and slightly crispy.
Serve hot.

Description: 
Fun potato side dish just right for a buffet table.  Cupcakes are creamy inside with a crispy top.
