Title:
Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/4 c powdered sugar
3 1/4 c shortening
1 1/2 c butter
3 eggs
1/2 t salt
3 c flour
3 c cake flour

Directions:
In large mixing bowl, cream together butter and shortening.  Slowly add powdered sugar until well blended.  Beat in eggs, salt, and gradually add flours, blending well.  Roll dough out onto a floured surface, to about a 1/4 inch thickness.  Cut into bars.  Place onto ungreased cookie sheets and bake in a preheated 375' oven for 12 minutes.  Do not overbake.  Remove from cookie sheets to cool.
