Title:
Collard Green Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound package smoked sausage, sliced and browned
4 cups chicken stock, I used low-sodium
2- 10 ounce cans diced tomatoes with green chiles(I used Rotel)
2- 15.8 ounce cans great northern beans, drained
1-1.4 ounce package dry vegetable soup mix such as Knorr
2-16 ounce bags frozen collard greens, do not thaw
salt and freshly ground black pepper to your tastes
couple dashes of hot sauce if desired

Directions:
Combine all above ingredients in a large pot and bring to a rolling boil.
Reduce heat to low and allow to simmer for 30 minutes, stirring often.
Serve with cornbread or biscuits.

Submitter: 
Ashley Holman
Description: 
Kinda came up with this one on my own one day when I just didn't feel up to going to the store so I used what ingredients I had in my pantry and fridge and voila!! Its now in my daily meal rotation, especially in the Fall and Winter!
