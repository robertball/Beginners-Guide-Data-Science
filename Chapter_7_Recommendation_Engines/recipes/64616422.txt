Title:
Cast Iron Ribeye Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Ribeye steaks 1-14" thick
Olive oil or spray
Salt & Pepper or Montreal Steak Seasoning

Directions:
Bring steaks to room temperature
Heat oven to 500 degrees
Place heavy cast iron skillet in hot oven for 5 minutes
Coat both sides of steaks with olive oil (spray works great)
Season both sides of steak liberally with seasoning.
Turn on stove burner to high, carefully remove skillet from oven and place on burner for 5 minutes. (leave oven on)
Using tongs, place steaks in center of skillet for 30 seconds
Turn steaks and cook an additional 30 seconds
Immediately return skillet to oven for 2 minutes
Using tongs, turn steaks and cook an additional 2 minutes. (steaks should bee medium rare)
Add a minute or so if you want your steak cooked more thoroughly
Remove from oven and let stand for 2 minutes
Top with sauteed mushrooms and onions and enjoy!
