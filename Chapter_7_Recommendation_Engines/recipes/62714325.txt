Title:
Tangy Green Beans, from Pat Bradshaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ lbs. fresh green beans, trimmed
1/3 cup diced red bell pepper
4 ½ teaspoons olive or canola oil
4 ½ teaspoons water
1 ½ teaspoon white wine vinegar (or cider vinegar)
1 ½ teaspoon spicy brown mustard
¾ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Place beans and red pepper in basket over 1 inch of boiling water in a saucepan.
Cover and steam for 7-8 minutes or until crisp-tender.
Meanwhile; in a bowl whisk together the remaining ingredients.
Transfer bean mixture to serving bowl.
Add vinaigrette mixture and stir to coat.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This is a great green bean dish with just like the title says, 'a little tang' or is it twang?  Although it has a little mustard in it even those who don't like mustard like these green beans!
