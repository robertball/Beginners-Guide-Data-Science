Title:
Brown Sugar Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs
3 cups plain flour
1box brown sugar
1cup gran. sugar
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1cup milk
1tsp. vanilla
1 cup (2 sticks) butter
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup nuts (optional)

Directions:
Cream shortening, butter and sugars.  Add eggs one at a time.  Add milk alternately with flour.  Add vanilla and mix very well.  Add nuts.  Bake in tube or bundt pan at 350 for about 1 1/2 hours.
