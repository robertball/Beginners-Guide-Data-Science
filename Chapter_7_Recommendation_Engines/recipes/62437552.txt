Title:
Homemade Beef Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Pkg. Pillsbury roll-out refrigerated pie crust.
1 lb. Beef stew meat (cut into bite size pieces).
1 can peas
1 can mushrooms (fresh is better)
1 can sliced potatoes
1 small onion diced
2 Pkg. Chicken flavored gravy mix
Egg Wash(1 egg,2Tbsp. cold water mixed)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
1)Saute' beef, mushrooms and onion in med. sized pan until meat is slightly brown and onions are soft.Drain any grease from meat.
2)Combine beef,peas,mushrooms,potatoes and onion in large bowl, mix.
3)Roll out crust into pie plates, and add mixture.
In the meantime prepare gravy mix as directed.when done pour 1/2 each over 2 pies.
4) Brush the edges of your pie crust with egg wash. Add top layer pie crust (you will want to trim the edges as there will be quite a bit of dough left over)poke the top with a fork as you would any pie to let the steam out.Brush top of pie with egg wash(this will make it golden brown an flaky on top.
bake at 350 for 20-30 minutes until golden.

Submitter: 
Cathy Adams-Kane
Description: 
I was experimenting one evening with pasties and found this to be alot easier to make but with the same great taste..My husband loves this and asks for it often. We make two at a time because they go so quickly.Enjoy!
