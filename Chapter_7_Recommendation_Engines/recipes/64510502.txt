Title:
Tyson Chicken and Black Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
22 ounces Grilled & Ready® Fully Cooked Frozen Fajita Chicken Strips
15-1/4 ounces whole kernel corn, drained
15 ounces black beans, rinsed, drained
1 cup salsa verde or picante
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
1-1/2 cups Mexican cheese blend, shredded
1/2 cup tortilla chips, crushed, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°F. In a 2-quart casserole dish combine corn, beans, salsa, cumin, and coriander. Stir in Grilled & Ready Fajita Chicken Strips and 1 cup of the cheese.
Bake, uncovered, 35-40 minutes or until bubbly. Sprinkle with the remaining 1/2 cup cheese. Bake 3-5 minutes or until cheese is melted. If desired, sprinkle with crushed tortilla chips.
