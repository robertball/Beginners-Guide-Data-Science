Title:
Clare's Shortcake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Sugar
1   Cup Cake Flour
2   Teaspoons Baking Powder
1/4 Teaspoon Salt
1/4 Cup Shortening
1/3 Cup Milk
1   Egg
1   Teaspoon Lemon Juice

Directions:
Sift the sugar, flour, baking powder and salt into a medium bowl.
Add the shortening, milk, egg and lemon juice.
Beat with a mixer on medium for two minutes.
Pour into a greased and floured 8 inch pan.
Bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes.
Enjoy!

Description: 
A great shortcake to have with strawberries!
