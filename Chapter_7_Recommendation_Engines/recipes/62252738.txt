Title:
Sami's Vanilla Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/333623.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups milk
3 egg yolks
3 tablespoons vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3 tablespoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
5 tablespoons melted butter

Directions:
In a large bowl mix together :
1 1/2 cups   Milk
3            Egg yolks
3     TBLSP  Vanilla
Stir in until well blendd:
1 1/2 cups   Flour
3     TBLSP  Sugar
1/2   tsp    Salt
5     TBLSP  Melted Butter
Heat a crepe pan over medium heat until hot.
Coat with vegetable oil or cooking spray.
Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the pan & tip to spread the batter to the edges.
When bubbles form on the top & edges are dry, flip & cook until lightly browned on the other side & edges are golden. Repeat with remaining batter.
Fill crepes with your favorite fruit, cream, caramel or even ice cream or cheese to serve.
