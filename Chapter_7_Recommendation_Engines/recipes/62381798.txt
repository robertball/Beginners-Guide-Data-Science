Title:
Sauteed Fresh Summer Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium zucchini, peeled and sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 medium onion, sliced
3-4 small tomatoes, sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 clove garlic, minced
2 T olive oil or butter
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil or butter in a large frying pan. Add garlic and onion; saute over medium heat 2-3 minutes.  Add zucchini slices and tomatoes, sauteeing an additional 3-5 minutes, or until zucchini is crisp tender.  Add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
ELLENMEL
Description: 
This is a very quick, healthy, and super delicious way to enjoy summer zucchini fresh from the garden.  It is especially tasty served over steamed rice.
