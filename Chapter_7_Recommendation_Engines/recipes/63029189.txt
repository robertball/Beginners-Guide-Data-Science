Title:
Mom's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large Jar of Prego (the largest you can find)
1 pkg. Dry Spaghetti Seasoning
1 small can tomato paste
3 small cans tomato sauce
1 lb Hamburger meat
1 tablespoon minced garlic
frozen bell peppers
frozen onions
1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
3 tablespoons chili powder
salt & pepper
1 teaspoon oregano
1 teaspoon basil
1 large jar mushrooms or fresh

Directions:
Fry Hamburger meat, onion & bell pepper. Add garlic. Then add dry spaghetti mix and rest of spices. Add tomato paste and stir...then add all the tomato sauce and prego and mushrooms. Let it cook for about an hour or longer.
