Title:
Soft Dinner Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/4 cups bread flour
1 cup warm water
1/4 cup white sugar
1 large egg
2 tablespoons butter, softened
1 tablespoon active dry yeast
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
Place water sugar and yeast in bowl and let sit for 15 minutes. After the yeast becomes foamy add half of the flour, egg, butter and salt with a wooden spoon until combined. Place the bowl on the mixer fitter with a dough hook and add the additional flour at low speed. Switch the speed to a 2-3 and knead for about 3 minutes or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
Punch dough down and divide into 12-15 rolls and place in prepared dish. Brush with melted butter. 12 rolls for hamburger buns and 15 for dinner rolls). Cover dish loosely with plastic wrap; let rise until doubled in volume, about 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Bake rolls in the preheated oven until the tops are golden brown, 12 to 15 minutes.
