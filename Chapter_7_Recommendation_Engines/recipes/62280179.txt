Title:
Pumpkin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or shortening, softened
1 cup sugar
1 cup pumpkin, cooked fresh or canned
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted
1 tsp. baking powder, double acting variety
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. allspice
1 cup nuts, chopped
1 cup raisins

Directions:
Cream the butter and the sugar together. Beat the egg and the vanilla together and mix with the pumpkin. Add the butter and sugar to this. Sift together all the dry ingredients except for the nuts and the raisins and add to the pumpkin mixture. Stir in the nuts and the raisins and drop cookies on to a greased cookie sheet. Bake at 375 degrees for 15 minutes. Makes about five dozen cookies which should get you through the night without too many tricks.
