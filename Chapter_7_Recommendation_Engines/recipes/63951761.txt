Title:
Sauteed Brussels Sprouts w/ Orange & Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 navel orange
2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
1 shallot, minced
2 lb Brussels sprouts, trimmed & halved
1/4 tsp salt
1/3 cup walnuts, coarsely chopped & toasted
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Zest entire orange and juice half of it.
In a 12-inch saut√© pan heat 1 Tbsp olive oil over medium-high heat.  Saute shallot for 1 minute.  Add half of Brussels sprouts, cut-side down, in a single layer; sprinkle with salt.  Cook for 4 minutes.  Add half of orange juice, flip sprouts and cook for 4 minutes.  Transfer sprouts to a large serving bowl.  Repeat with remaining oil, sprouts, salt and juice.  Add second batch of sprouts to serving bowl.
Toss sprouts with walnuts and orange zest and season with pepper. Serve warm, at room temperature, or chilled as a salad.
