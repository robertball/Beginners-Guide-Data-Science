Title:
Glazed Lemon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg yellow cake mix
1 pkg lemon pudding mix
4 eggs
2/3 cup water
1/3 cup oil
For the glaze:
Juice of 2 lemons
grated lemon zest
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350. Grease a 13 x 9 inch baking pan.
In a large bowl, mix cake and pudding together.
In another bowl, mix the eggs, water and oil together until well blended. Pour into cake pudding mixture. Stir well.
Put in oven and bake for 35 minutes or until the cake is slightly browned. Remove from oven.
Meanwhile, in a small bowl combine the juice of 2 lemon, lemon zest and powdered sugar. Seat aside.
When the cake is done and while it is hot, place on counter and poke holes all over using a meat fork or something similar.
Pour the lemon sugar glaze mixture over letting it fill the holes. Cool and serve.
