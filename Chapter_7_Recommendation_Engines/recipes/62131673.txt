Title:
Easy Smoked Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. smoked sausage, diced
1/2 medium onion, chopped
2 12oz. cans green beans, do not drain
2 10oz. cans cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2 to 2 qts. diced potatoes
3/4 lb. Velveeta cheese

Directions:
Saute sausage and onions together in skillet till onions are tender.
Meanwhile, put potatoes in a large pot and cover with salted water.
Boil potatoes till tender, drain off water.
Add green beans, sausage and onions, and mushroom soup to pot of potatoes.
Add water to thin soup to your desired consistency.
Bring soup to a simmer.
Add cubes of Velveeta, stir till they melt into soup.

Submitter: 
SPEARL20
Description: 
A quick, easy and hearty soup to serve to company.  Uses ingredients you probably already have on hand, and you can sub in polish sausage or kielbasa for the smoked sausage.
