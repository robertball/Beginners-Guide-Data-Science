Title:
Chocolate Chip Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T butter
1/2 c sugar
1 lg egg
3 lg mashed bananas
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 c all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
2 T unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 c sour cream
2/3 c mini chocolate chips
flour for dusting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ยบ F. Lightly grease 9ร5 loaf pan. In large bowl, cream together butter and sugar.
Stir in egg, mashed bananas, sour cream, and vanilla until well blended.
Add baking soda, cocoa, and flour. Mix until well incorporated. Dust mini chocolate chips with a little flour; stir into batter.
Pour batter into loaf pan; bake 50 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick tests clean.
Remove from heat; let rest in pan 10 minutes. Remove to wire rack.

Description: 
Moist, chocolate banana bread that will melt in your mouth, especially when served warm. With just the right amount of chocolate chips flecked throughout, this treat is distinctly banana bread and satisfyingly chocolatey!
