Title:
Instant Pot Chicken Karahi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp oil
2 cups onion, diced
2 inch piece of fresh ginger, finely diced
4 Tbsp garlic, minced
3 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs, sliced into smaller pieces
3 cups canned diced tomatoes
2 tsp ground cumin
2 tsp ground coriander
2 tsp garam masala
2 Tbsp Kashmiri red chili powder
.5 tsp ground turmeric
3 tsp kosher salt
1 green pepper, sliced into chunks
.5 cups cilantro, chopped
.5 cups fresh ginger, julienned

Directions:
Turn your Instant Pot to Saut√©s and heat the oil. Add onions, diced ginger, and garlic. Cook until the onions turn translucent. About 4 minutes.
Add the sliced chicken, canned tomatoes. Add the cumin, coriander, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric and salt. Mix well to combine.
Close Instant Pot lid and set pressure value to sealing. Set Instant Pot to manual (Hi) with a 5 minute timer. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes then release remaining pressure.
Set to Saute  and cook for an additional 5 minutes. The sauce should thicken.
Serve with a side of jasmine rice and/or naan. Garnish with cilantro and ginger.
Enjoy.

JD Scheffee
Description: 
A spicy chicken curry. Easy to make in the instant pot.
