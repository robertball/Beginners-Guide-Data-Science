Title:
Baked Chicken Chimichangas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c chicken, cooked and shredded
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 c olive oil -used for coating before baking
1/2 c chopped onion
2 minced garlic cloves
1/2 tbsp chili powder
16 oz salsa
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp cinnamon
6 10inch flour tortillas -warmed
1 c refried beans
Sour cream if desired
Guacamole if desired

Directions:
Heat oven to 450 degrees.
In large saucepan saute onion and garlic over medium-high heat in 2tbsp oil unti tender.
Stir in chili powder, salsa, cumin and cinnamon.
Stir in shredded chicken, remove from heat and let cool.
Grease a large baking pan.
Spoon a heaping tbsp of beans down center of each tortilla.
Top with 1/2 c of chicken mixture. Fold up ends and sides of tortilla and secure with toothpicks.
Place cimichangas in greased baking pan, seam side down.  Brush all sides with olive oil.
Bake 20-25 mintues, turning every 5, or until golden brown.
Serve topped with sour cream and/or guacamole if desired.
