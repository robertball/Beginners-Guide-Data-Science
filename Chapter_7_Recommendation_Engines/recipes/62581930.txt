Title:
Easy Hershey Bar Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box chocolate cake mix
1 c water
1 small box instant chocolate pudding mix
1/2 c oil
4 eggs
Frosting:
1 lg tub Cool Whip
1 can Eagle milk
2 almond Hershey bars.

Directions:
Mix all cake ingredients together & pour into a greased 9" by 13" pan. Bake @ 350 for 25-30 min. While cake is hot poke all over w/ a fork only half way down. Pour the Eagle milk all over the cake. Let cool completely. Ice cake w/ cool whip & grate Hershey bars on top to garnish. Keep in fridge

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is a quick & easy recipe that's perfect for sharing, goes great to bar-b-q's & potlucks!
