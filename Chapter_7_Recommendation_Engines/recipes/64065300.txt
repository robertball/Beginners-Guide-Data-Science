Title:
Aushak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meat Sauce:
3 tbsp vegetable oil
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1/2 lb ground meat
1 clove garlic
1 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp diced or grated fresh ginger
1/2 cup water
2 tbsp tomato paste
1/2 tsp pepper
.
Yogurt Sauce:
1 cup full fat yogurt
1 tbsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp salt
.
Onion Mix:
2 cups scallions, choppped, discard whites (2 bunches)
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp re pepper flakes
1 tsp minced garlic
.
1 pkg rice rermacelli
.
Fresh mint

Directions:
Heat oil in pan, add onion. Cook until tender.
Add meat and garlic. Cook about 3 minutes.
Add water and reduce by half.
Add tomato paste and cook for 5 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper. Set aside.
Mix all Yogurt Sauce ingredients together. Set aside.
Saute all Onion Mix ingredeints together. Set aside.
Cook vermacelli noodles according to directions. Blend onion mix ingredients with rice vermacelli.
Layer in a square dish: 1/4 cup yogurt sauce. Cover with cut up vermacelli/oncion mix. Mound on rest of the yogurt sauce. Spoon meat sauce all around the noodles. Sprinkle with chopped mint.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
Delicious Afghanistani flavor. Great to eat with Naan bread.  Also works great to substitue brown rice for the rice vermacelli.
