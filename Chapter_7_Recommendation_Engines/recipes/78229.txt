Title:
Crispix Mix® Chili Cheese Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
5 cups KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX® cereal
2 cups SUNSHINE® CHEEZ-IT® Original snack crackers
1 cup pretzel sticks
2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions:
Stir together Parmesan cheese and spices. Set aside.
In 2-gallon zipper-type storage bag, combine KELLOGG'S CRISPIX cereal, SUNSHINE CHEEZ-IT crackers and pretzels. Pour oil over cereal mixture. Close bag and gently toss until evenly coated. Add Parmesan cheese mixture. Close bag. Gently toss until evenly coated. Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX®
Description: 
A chili powder spice mix brings an irresistible flavor to this party snack hit.
