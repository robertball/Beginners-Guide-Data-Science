Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Barley and Vegetable Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 Tbl. olive oil
Salt and Pepper
2 lb. boneless chuck roast, cut into four pieces
6 cloves of garlic, sliced
1 C. onions, diced
6 sprigs of fresh thyme, or 1 tsp. dry
6 Cups chicken broth
1 lb. baby potatoes, cut in half
1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cut into medium to large chunks OR four carrots, peeled and cut into medium chunks
1/2 C. pearl barley

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet.  Season meat with salt and pepper to taste and brown the meat on both sides.  Place the meat into the crock of a 5 to 6 qt slow cooker that has been sprayed with PAM nonstick spray.  Add the garlic, onions and thyme to the skillet and saut√© until lightly browned.  Add 2 cups of chicken broth, stir to loosen bits of vegetable and meat and add the entire mixture to the crock.  Add the remaining, vegetables, broth and pearl barley.  Stir and cover.  Cook on low 8 hours.  Before serving, pull the meat into small bites.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
This easy stew is adapted from a Martha Stewart recipe.  The use of chicken broth instead of beef broth adds a nice lightness to the dish and the addition of the winter squash and barley is a welcome change to traditional beef stew recipes
