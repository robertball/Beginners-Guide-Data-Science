Title:
Crumble-Top Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c all-purpose baking mix
1 c raisins, (optional)
2/3 c quick or old-fashioned oats
2/3 c granulated sugar
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 can (30 oz) LIBBY'SÂ® Easy Pumpkin Pie Mix
2 large eggs
Streusel topping
3 tbsp all-purpose flour
3 tbsp granulated sugar
3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
2 tbsp butter

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 400. Paper-line or grease 24 muffin cups.
COMBINE baking mix, raisins, oats, sugar and cinnamon in large bowl. Combine pumpkin pie mix and eggs in med bowl; mix well. Stir into oat mixture just until moistened. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups, filling 3/4 full. Sprinkle with Streusel Topping.
BAKE 14 to 16 min or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Remove from pans to wire rack; cool slightly. Serve warm.
FOR STREUSEL TOPPING:
COMBINE flour, sugar and cinnamon in small bowl. Cut in butter with pastry blender or two knives until mixture is crumbly.
