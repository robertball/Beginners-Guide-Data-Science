Title:
Debbie's Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
1 stick butter
small can evaporated milk
1 heaping cup of peanut butter
1 jar marshmallow creme
1 teas. vanilla

Directions:
Melt butter in 3 qt. saucepan. Add sugar and milk. Stir well and let come to full boil and then boil for 5 minutes.
Remove from heat and add peanut butter, marshmallow creme and vanilla.
Mix well and pour in a 9 x 13 pan. Cool

Submitter: 
Marjorie Taylor
Description: 
The best fudge ever and easy to make.
