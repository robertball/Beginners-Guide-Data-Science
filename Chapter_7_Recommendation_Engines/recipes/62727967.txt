Title:
Piada arrotolata con stracchino e mostarda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Flour 500 grams (4 cups)
Pork Fat (strutto) 60 grams (2.12 oz)
Baking powder 1 tablespoon (or fresh yeast)
Salt 1 pinch
Peanut oil
Warm water
Fresh stracchino
Mostarda-fruit chutney

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients and work for 10 mins. Put it in a plastic bag to rest at room temperature for at least 1 hour.
Bake it on a flat traditional terracotta or nonstick pan or grill.
Fill with fresh stracchino and mostarda
Serve hot
