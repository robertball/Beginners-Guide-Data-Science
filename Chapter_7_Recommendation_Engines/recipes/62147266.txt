Title:
Chicken with Roasted Red Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup flour
3 Tbs. olive oil
3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 jar of roasted red peppers, cut into thin strips
1 cup chicken broth
2 Tbs. red wine vinegar
1 1/2 tsp. sugar
1 Tbs. butter
Salt and pepper to taste
3 Tbs. parsley, chopped

Directions:
Pound chicken breasts between parchment paper to 1/2 inch thickness.
Rub chicken with salt/pepper.
Coat chicken with flour.
Warm 2 Tbs. olive oil in skillet over medium high heat.
Cook chicken until golden on both sides, about 8-10 minutes.
Transfer to plate and cover.
Heat remaining 1 Tbs. of olive oil in skillet.
Cook garlic until fragant, about 30 seconds.
Add peppers to skillet and cook for 1 minute.
Add broth, vinegar, and sugar.
Bring to a boil, scraping browned bits from pan.
Reduce heat and simmer until slightly thickened, about 4 min.
Remove pan from heat, stir in butter and parsley.
If the sauce is not thickened to your liking, use 1-2 tsp. cornstarch to thicken it.
Pour sauce over chicken before serving.
