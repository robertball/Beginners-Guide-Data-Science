Title:
Blueberry Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. flour
1/2 c. sugar
1 t. baking powder
1/4 t. baking soda
1/4 t. salt
1 egg
1/2 c. butter or margarine, melted
1/2 c. buttermilk
1 t. vanilla
1/2 c. flour
1/4 sugar
2 T. margarine or butter, softened
1/2 t. grated lemon peel
1 - 21oz. can blueberry pie filling

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, stir together first 5 ingredients. Combine eggs, melted margarine, buttermilk and vanilla and add to dry ingredients, mixing well. Pour into a greased 9x9 pan.
In a small mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, and soft butter until crumbly. Sprinkle half of the mix onto the batter.
Stir lemon peel into piue filling; spread over crumb layer and add the remaining crumbs.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes.
