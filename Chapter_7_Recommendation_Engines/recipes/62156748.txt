Title:
easy summer pasta salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz package bowtie pasta
1 tbsp fresh ground pepper
half a large bell pepper thinly sliced and chopped
half a medium red onion thinly sliced and chopped
1 jar marinated artichoke hearts chopped(can substitute with 2 tbsp capers)
2-3 tbspn pine nuts
1/4 cup olive oil
3 tspn sugar
5 tbsp lemon juice
2 tspn salt
1 tbsp salt for boiling pasta
1 tbspn dried basil
1/4 cup fresh minced parsley
1 1/2 cup halved cherry tomato
grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
boil pasta with salt
cool the pasta when done with cold water, drain
add all ingredients, adjust to taste
sprinkle cheese lightly on top

Submitter: 
birdstheword
Description: 
this is an immitation of a delicious pasta salad that i get from a french bakery, it's very flexible so you can adjust the ingredients and spices according to your taste. enjoy!
