Title:
Grandma's Oatmeal Cocoa Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2 tablespoons cocoa powder
1/2 cup milk
1 stick butter
2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
2 cups quick cooking oats
Pinch of salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix sugar, cocoa, milk and butter in pan. Bring to boil; boil 1 minute, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Drop on to wax paper and cool.
