Title:
Chinese Hamburger Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
2 medium onions, chopped
1 (10.5 oz) can of cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.5 oz) can of cream of chicken soup
1 cup of white or brown minute rice
1 cup water
2 T low sodium soy sauce
2 T worcestershire sauce
2 stalks of celery, sliced
1 (8 oz) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1 (4 oz) can of mushrooms, sliced & drained (optional)
1 (10.5 oz) can of peas, drained (optional), Chow mein noodles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Brown hamburger in skillet with onions. Drain off any grease.
Add all remaining ingredients except the chow mein noodles. Mix well.
Pour into a 2 quart casserole dish.
Bake for 45 mins
Sprinkle the chow mein noodles over the top and bake for another 10 minutes.
