Title:
THM Easy Mac 'n Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dreamfield's Macaroni
3/4 C. Sharp Cheddar
2 oz. Cream Cheese
2 T Butter
3/4 C. Half & Half
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook Dreamfield's pasta until al dente
Combine all other ingredients in saucepan.  Let simmer until combined.  Pour over pasta, top with shredded cheese, bake until hot & bubbly.
