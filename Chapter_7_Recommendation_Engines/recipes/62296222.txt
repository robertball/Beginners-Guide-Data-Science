Title:
Mom's Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shells macaroni
1 cup Velveeta, cut into 1 inch cubes
1/4 cup margarine
1 (10.75 oz) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Cook macaroni as directed.
Combine macaroni, Velveeta, margarine, soup, and milk in a medium baking dish.
Cover and bake at 375F for 30 minutes, stirring half way through.

Submitter: 
Rick Janssen
Description: 
This is the macaroni and cheese I grew up with. A creamy alternative to the neon orange box mac & cheese.
