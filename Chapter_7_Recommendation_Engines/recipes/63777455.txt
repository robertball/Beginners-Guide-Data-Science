Title:
Slow Cooker BBQ Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-1/2- to 4-lb. chicken
1 jar (1 lb. 8 oz.) Ragu® Old World Style® Pasta Sauce
1/2 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
2 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp. ground chipotle chile pepper
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1.	Season chicken, if desired, with salt and ground black pepper. In 4-1/2-quart slow cooker sprayed with nonstick cooking spray, arrange chicken.
2.	In medium bowl, combine remaining ingredients; pour over chicken. Cook covered on LOW 8 to 10 hours or HIGH 4 to 6 hours.
3.	Remove chicken and cool slightly. Remove skin and bones, then shred chicken. Return chicken to slow cooker; stir. Serve, if desired, on whole grain rolls.
