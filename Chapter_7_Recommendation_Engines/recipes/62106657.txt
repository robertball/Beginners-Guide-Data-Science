Title:
Chicken Budapest Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/4 cup real cooked bacon bits
8 oz sour cream
2 Tbsp Worcestire sauce
1 medium chopped onion
2 Tbsp butter
1/4 cup herbed bread crumbs
garlic salt
paprika

Directions:
Quarter chicken breasts, making 8 pieces total
Melt 2 Tbsp butter into a 8" x 10" casserole in oven and let melt.
Take out of oven and place the chopped onion in bottom of pan.
Sprinkle chicken with garlic salt and place on top of onion in pan.
Lift up the pieces of chicken and sprinkle the bacon bits underneath each piece.
Mix 2 Tbsp Worcestire sauce into the sour cream and spread on top of chicken pieces.
Sprinkle the chicken with the bread crumbs and then a generous amount of good quality paprika.
Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Christine R.
Description: 
Comfort food Hungarian style.  Chicken breasts bakes with onion, sour cream and paprika. An easy favorite that will keep you coming back for more.
