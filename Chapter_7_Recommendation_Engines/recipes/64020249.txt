Title:
Balkan Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups plain yogurt
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 tablespoon white vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon chopped chives
1 teaspoon chopped mint leaves
1 1/2 cups peeled, seeded, and diced cucumbers
1/2 cup green seedless grapes, halved
1/2 cup red seedless grapes, halved

Directions:
Beat yogurt in a small bowl until very smooth.  Add garlic, vinegar, salt olive oil, chives, and mint leaves; beat for 30 seconds.  Fold in cucumbers and grapes. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
