Title:
Swedish Meatball Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
2c half and half, divided
1c bread crumbs
1 onion, chopped
1-3/4t salt, divided
1-1/2lb ground beef
1T butter
3T flour
3/4t beef bouillon
1/2t pepper
1/8t garlic salt
3c water
1 pound red potatoes, cubed

Directions:
Beat egg, add 1/3c cream, bread crumbs, onion, and 1t salt.
Crumble beef over mixture and mix well.
Shape into small meatballs.
In a soup pout, brown meatballs in butter, half at a time.
Remove and drain.
Add flour, bouillon, pepper, garlic salt, and salt.
Stir until smooth.
Stir in water gradually and bring to a boil.
Add potatoes and meatballs.
Reduce heat and cover 25 minutes until potatoes are tender.
Stir in remaining cream and heat through.
