Title:
Nanny's Bakeless Ice Box Fruitcake(7 Pounds) nanny=Helen Van Hoozer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pounds mini marshmallows
1 pound candied cherries
1 pound chopped pecans
1 pound chopped walnuts
1 pound grated coconut
1/2 pound crushed vanilla wafers
1/2 pound crushed graham crackers
1/2 pound raisins
1 can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
mix all together except milk- mix thoroughly
add milk-mix thoroughly
hand press into 9 X 13 pan lined with wax paper
place in fridge overnite- remove from pan and cut into 2 inch squares
store in fridge

Description: 
since there is no cake- this is really more of a candy- very rich
