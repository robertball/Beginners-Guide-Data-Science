Title:
Sesame Coated Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c wheat germ
2 T soy flour
2 T whole wheat flour
1/2 t salt
1 t paprika
1/2 t pepper
1/2 t thyme
1/2 t marjoram
1/3 c sesame seeds

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.
Dip skinned chicken parts in coating and arrange on baking sheet.
Place about a teaspoon pat of butter on the highest part of the chicken part.
Bake at 350F until juices run clear when poked.

Submitter: 
meghanmacrae
Description: 
Coating can be used on chicken or fish. Can be made in advance, stored in refrigerator, and used as needed.
