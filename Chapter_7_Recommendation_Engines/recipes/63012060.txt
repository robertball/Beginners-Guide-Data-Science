Title:
Date Nut Finger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1/4 cup margarine
2. 1 cup sugar
3. 1 cup dates cut up small pieces
4. 1 egg
5. 2 1/2 cups rice krispie cereal
6. 2 cups flaked coconut in the bag

Directions:
in medium sauce pan cook 1. 2. 3. 4. medium heat stirring constantly till sugar melted and mixture blended...add 5. and stir well till cereal coated well.....when cool enough to handle take tablespoon  full and shape into finger shape ...roll in 6 cool and store in covered container

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
Made with rice krispie cereal and cook on top of the stove ( no baking)
