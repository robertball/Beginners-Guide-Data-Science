Title:
Chocolate Chip Tapioca Pudding Cookies ( Gluten free ) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1076142.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C. Gluten free flour blend
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1/2 tsp. Xanthan gum
1/2 C. Butter
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
1/2 C. Brown sugar
2 Eggs
1 C. Tapioca pudding, cooked
1 Tbsp. Premium dry cocoa
1 C. Chocolate chips
1/4 C. Flax seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper. Set aside
Blend flour, b. powder, b. soda and xanthan gum together in a small bowl. Set aside
Cook tapioca pudding according to package directions and allow to cool before adding to recipe
Mix butter and sugars together in a large mixing bowl and cream ingredients together to form a smooth paste
Add eggs to butter/sugar mixture and beat with an electric hand mixer until smooth and creamy
Stir cocoa into cooled tapioca pudding mixture and dissolve completely
Mix in tapioca pudding and chocolate chips into butter/sugar mixture and combine all ingredients together to form a smooth cookie dough
With a teaspoon take small amounts and place 2 inches apart on parchment paper
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes until lightly browned
Remove from oven and place on wire rack to cool completely before putting in a storage container.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A moist and delicious chocolate cookie with a fudge texture that is sure to please the cookie lover in everybody.
