Title:
Easy & Quick Chocolate Peanut Butter Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/504196.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Box of Devils Food Cake
1 can of sweetend condensed milk
1 cup of chopped peanuts
1 cup of peanut butter (crunchy or plain)
1 pk of 8 Reesee's Cups
1 tub of cool whip

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Mix cake mix as directed adding chopped peanuts and cup of peanut butter.  Spray pan, bake for 50 mins til it done.  Stick a toothpick in and it should come out clean when ready.  After poke holes in cake pour sweetend condensed milk on top of cake.  Let cool.  Add chopped or crumbled resee's cups on top then add cool whip if desired.  Then add a few more reese's cup for looks!  ENJOY!  My kids LOVE this dessert.
