Title:
Jacqui's Perfect Alfredo Sauce 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp salted butter
3 Tbsp unsalted butter
1 cup heavy cream
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp dried parsley leaves
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium low heat. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes, then add garlic and cheese and whisk quickly, heating through until cheese is melted. Stir in parsley and pepper and serve.
