Title:
Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 tsp freshly cracked black pepper
1/4 cup onion, diced
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
1/4 cup pimento or minced roasted red pepper
1 cup milk
24-32 oz frozen green beans, thawed
3 oz Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 small can french fried onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute onion and mushrooms  until very soft.  Whisk in flour until smooth, and cook for one minute. Add milk and cook until bubbling and thickened.  Remove from heat and stir in cheddar cheese.
Place the thawed and drained green beans in a sprayed  2 1/2 quart casserole dish.  Sprinkle with pimento.  Pour cheese sauce over the top.  Top with french fried onions.
Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the top is golden. .

Submitter: 
Melissa Gardner
Description: 
French-style green beans and chopped onion are stirred into a homemade sour cream sauce then topped with a cracker and grated cheese crust  in this baked casserole serving eight.Modified from: Grandma's Green Bean Casserole
