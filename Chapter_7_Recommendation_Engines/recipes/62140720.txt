Title:
Homemade Marshmellows Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup hot water
1 TBS. unflavored gelatin
1/4 cup cold water
few salt granules ( bout a pinch )
Powdered sugar for dusting

Directions:
soften gelatin in cold water.
combine sugar and hot water.
Boil to soft ball stage.
Add gelatin and salt.
Beat until stiff.
Add flavorings and beat thoroughly.
Pour into a pan which has been dusted with powdered sugar.
Allow to stand until stiff enough to hold it's shape.
Cut into strips, remove from pan and cut into sqaures.
Roll in powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Rachel Dobbs
Description: 
The title says it all, homemade marshellows. An old recipe from back in the 30's. This is a fun recipe I do with my kids from time to time. Where as marshmellows are readily available at the grocery store these days, it's a fun project.
