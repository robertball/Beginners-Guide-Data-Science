Title:
Fabulous NO ICE CREAM Peanut Butter Banana Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bananas
2 Tablesppons Peanut Butter
1 or 2 Tablespoons honey

Directions:
Slice bananas 1/4 inch thick, spread on cookie sheet and freeze (at least 1 hour til completely frozen)
In the bowl of food processor place frozen bananas, peanut butter and 1 tablespoon honey.
Whirl for about 30 seconds (add second tablespoon of honey to taste)
Serve

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
A deliciously easy, healthy and completely dairy-free frozen treat you won't believe is this good!
