Title:
Mango Pineapple Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Ripe Mangos peeled and cubed
1 pineapple cubed (or three cans tidbits drained)
1/4 red onion minced
1 clove garlic minced
1 habanero pepper seeded and chopped
1 serrano pepper seeded and chopped
2 limes juiced
1/2 cup chopped cilantro
salt to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.  Refrigerate for 1 or more hours.  Serve with chips.

Submitter: 
CHRISPENTINA
Description: 
This salsa is awesome with chips, or great with grilled spicy meats for a super cool down.  Try it with Jerk Chicken!
