Title:
Chocolate Trifle cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of chocolate cake mix
2 boxes of instant chocolate pudding
1 large tub of whipped cream
1 bag of chocolate chips

Directions:
Prepare chocolate cake mix according to package directions. Pour into a greased, flat pan. Bake at 350 degrees for about 20 min. or until done.
Prepare chocolate pudding according to package directions.
Once cake has cooled, layer cake, then pudding, the whipped cream, then chocolate chips. Keep doing this until you run out of cake.
Refrigerate until cold, then enjoy!
