Title:
Strawberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of Romaine lettuce
1 package of shredded cabbage
1 1/2 cups golden raisins
1 cup red grapes cut in half
1 package fresh strawberries, sliced
1/2 red onion sliced
2 small packages of pine nuts
2 small packages of sliced almonds
1 bottle Creamy Poppyseed dressing

Directions:
Combine all ingredients besides nuts and dressing in a large bowl or casserole dish.
Mix in dressing to your liking.
Top with nuts and enjoy!

Description: 
An easy, delicious salad that is sure to please!
