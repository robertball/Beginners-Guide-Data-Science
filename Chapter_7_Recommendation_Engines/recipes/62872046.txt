Title:
Apple Cider Butt Chicken with Maple Curry Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons dry roasted Mild Curry powder
1/2 cup Maple Syrup
Juice of 2 limes
1 TBSP parsley paste
1 TBSP minced ginger
1 Frying Chicken
1 can Apple cider
maple syrup to taste to reduce marinade to sticky glaze

Directions:
Combine first four ingredients
Loosen breast skin on chicken
Rub marinade over and in the chicken
Pour the rest of the marinade into a freezer bag with the chicken.
Let marinade for 3 to 4 hours
Remove chicken from marinade and allow to dry in the fridge for 1/2 hour
Pour 3/4 can of cider into the chicken roaster
Place chicken over the can roaster
Preheat BBQ
Reduce glaze by half and add maple syrup and 1/4 can cider
Turn the flame down to low on each side of the BBQ and turn the middle one off
Put the chicken and roaster onto several layers of tinfoil with the edges turned up
Cook for 1 hour basting with the glaze often
check for doneness
