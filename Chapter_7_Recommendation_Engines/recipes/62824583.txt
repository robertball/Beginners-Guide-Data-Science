Title:
Low Fat Carrot Cupcakes and Cream Cheese Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/667814.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box dry carrot cake mix
1 (20 oz) can crushed pineapple in its own juices, undrained
1/2 cup sweetened coconut
1 package 1/3 less fat cream cheese
1 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl mix together the dry cake mix, an undrained can of crushed pineapple, and the coconut until well blended.  Spoon the batter into lined cupcake tins so each one is 2/3rds full.
Bake the cupcakes according to the directions on the cake mix package.  After baking set the cupcakes aside to cool
In a separate bowl mix together cream cheese, powdered sugar and vanilla using an electric hand mixer on medium.
Ice the cupcakes with the icing once they have cooled.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This is a quick and easy deliciously moist low fat carrot cupcake recipe.  It is a great way to indulge in a favorite carrot cake while limiting fat and calories.
