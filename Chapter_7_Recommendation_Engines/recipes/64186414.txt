Title:
Janet's Slo Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb. Beef round roast
6 Carrots, sliced or 12 oz. baby carrots
6 medium to large potatoes. cut into bite-sized chunks
1 envelope Lipton onion soup mix
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
Pan fry the roast until seared on all sides. Season with salt and pepper. Place in crock pot. Add carrots and potatoes. Mix onion soup mix with a little water, then add to soup. Add mixture to crock pot.
Cook on low 6-8 hours.
