Title:
Blueberry Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Angel food cake
1 can Blueberry pie filling
1 can Eagle Brand milk
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1 large (16 oz.) Cook Whip
½ chopped pecans, divided

Directions:
Tear Angel food cake into bite-sized pieces and set aside.
Set aside 2 Tbsp of chopped pecans.
In a large bolw, mix together blueberry pie filling, Eagle Brand mile, lemon juice, and chopped pecans.  Mix thoroughly by hand (do not use an electric mixer).
Add Cool Whip to blueberry mixture, combining thoroughly (this works best using a long-handled spoon).
In tall glass bown (any large bowl will work), put ½ of the torn Angle food cake. Place ½ of the blueberry mixture over the cake. Repeat layers.
Top with the reserved pecans. Refigerate for 4 hours before serving.
