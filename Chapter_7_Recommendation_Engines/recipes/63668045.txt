Title:
Not your average potato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018844.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs baby red potatoes, quartered
2 chicken boullion cubes
1 small sweet onion, diced
1 Tbs dried rosemary, chopped & split in 2 parts
2 celery stalks, finely chopped
1 large lemon, juiced
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1/8 cup dijon mustard
1-1/2 Tbs red wine vinegar
2-3 oz blue cheese stuffed green olives, diced
sea salt and ground pepper to taste
1 Tbs olive oil

Directions:
Place potatoes in medium size pot with water covering by 2 inches
Add chicken boullion and boil until potatoes are soft and fork goes through easily.
Drain and place potatoes in large mixing bowl, add salt and pepper to taste, cover and put in fridge
In medium pan add olive oil, heat over medium heat.
Add onions and 1/2 of the chopped rosemary, saute until carmelized (onions are tender and golden brown in color)
Remove from pan and let cool.
In a small mixing bowl combine mayonnaise, sour cream, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, dijon mustard, rosemary and salt and pepper.
Mix well.
Take potatoes and add onions, olives and mayonnaise mixutre. Mix well and place back in fridge for at least 3 hours for flavors to meld. (Can make the night before)

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
This isn't anything like the plain old potato salad you find at your grocer! Its all dressed up with some great savory flavors and a nice little punch of citrus.
