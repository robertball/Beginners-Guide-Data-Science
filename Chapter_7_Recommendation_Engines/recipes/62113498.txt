Title:
Barley Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped
1/2 C. diced celery
4 tbsp. butter or margarine
3 C. barley
21/2 C. chicken broth
1 can chopped mushrooms

Directions:
Saute` the onion and celery in the butter.
Add the barley and brown slightly.
Butter a casserole dish and pour in the chicken broth and chopped mushrooms. I used a covered casserole.
Add the barley vegetable mix and blend.
Bake 350 degrees for 1 -1 Hr. 15 min. until the liquid is absorbed and barley tender.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Serve this with beef, chicken or fish instead of rice or potatoes. It's simple to put together and bakes in the oven along with the beef, chicken or fish. Saves time and energy when you can bake two or more dishes at the same time.
