Title:
Chistmas Morning Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 doz. Eggs
1/2 C. flour
1 lb. Cubed ham
2 can diced green chilies (optional)
1 pt. Cottage cheese
1 cube butter
1 lb. Monterey jack, shredded
1/2 t. salt
1 t. baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°
Beat eggs until light.
Add flour, baking powder, salt, cheeses, and butter.
Stir in chilies and ham.
Butter a 9x13 pan or line with aluminum foil.
Pour mixture into pan and bake 35 min at 350°
Top will be golden brown and the center will be firm.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
An egg cassarole much like a large quiche.
