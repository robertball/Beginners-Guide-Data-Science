Title:
Turkey-Biscuit Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C baby carrots (12 oz)
2 C cut-up fresh broccoli
3 T butter or margarine
1/2 C chopped onion (1 med.)
3 T all-purpose flour
2 C chicken broth
1 T chopped fresh or 1 t dried sage
2 C cubed cooked turkey or chicken
Biscuits- 4 slices bacon
1 C biscuit mix
1/2 C milk
1/2 C shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400. In 2-qt. saucepan, heat 1 C water to boiling. Add carrots and broccoli; cook about 4 min. or until carrots and broccoli are crisp-tender. Drain.
Meanwhile, in 3-qt. saucepan, melt butter over med.- heat. Add onion; cook about 2 min., stirring occ. until tender. Beat in flour with wire whisk. Gradually beat in broth and sage. Reduce heat to med-low; cook about 5 min., stirring occ. until sauce thickens. Stir carrots, broccoli and turkey into sauce. Spoon turkey mixture into ungreased 2-Qt. casserole.
Place bacon on microwavable plate; cover with paper towel. Microwave on high 4-6 min. or until crisp. Crumble bacon; place in med. bowl. Add remaining biscuit ingred; stir just until blended. Spoon biscuit batter around edge of turkey mixture.
Bake 25-30 min. or until biscuit crust is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Mixture of veggies (carrots and broccoli) and turkey with biscuit mix topping and bacon. Make with chicken or turkey.
