Title:
Chicken Delicious Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 mediun potatoes ,peeled and quartered
1 tsp. oregano
1 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 frying chicken, cut up
1 pound sweet Italian sausage links, cut up
1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Arrange potatoes in large , shallow 3 quart casserole/baking dish. Mix oregano,paprika,garlic powder,salt and pepper, and sprinkle half the mixture over potatoes. Arrange chicken and sausage on top. Pour oil over and spriinkle with remaining seasonings. Cover and bake at 425 degrees for 1 hour. Uncover and bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes or until golden brown

A delicious combination of chicken , sausage and potatoes.
