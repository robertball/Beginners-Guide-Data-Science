Title:
Carrot Poppy Seed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For 1 1/2 pound loaf
1 c water
1 c lightly packed finely shredded carrots
1 Tbsp butter or margarine
1 tsp sea salt
2 c white flour (ap)
1 c whole wheat flour
1 Tbsp packed brown sugar
2 tsp bread machine yeast
1 Tbsp poppy seeds

Directions:
Add ingredients according to manufacturer's directions adding the carrot with the water.  Select basic white bread cycle and medium/light colour crust setting.

Submitter: 
smitty
Description: 
A really nice 'loaf' not too sweet to have with morning coffee and is very easy because of the bread machine.  It also makes a nice 'gift' loaf.  I have had many requests for the recipe.
