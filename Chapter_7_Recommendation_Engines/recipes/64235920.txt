Title:
Judy's Baked Bean Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1798679.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pound Lean Ground Hamburg
1 Pound Thick Cut Bacon
1 can 40 oz dark red kidney beans
1 can 40 oz butter beans
1 can 40 oz baked beans
1 cup of ketchup
1 cup of brown sugar
1 cup of white sugar
1 medium sized chopped onion

Directions:
In a skillet, brown hamburg and do not drain.
Pour contents of skillet in large pot.
Fry bacon and drain grease.
Crumble bacon and place in large pot.
Open beans and pour into pot. DO NOT DRAIN.
Stir remaining ingredients in and cook over med-high heat, until sauce begins to boil.
reduce heat to low and let simmer for an hour.
Serve...
Corn Bread muffins really set this dish off properly.

Submitter: 
Dale Young
Description: 
Our dear friend Judy makes these for her family outings, and I got tired of trying to figure out how to get an invite each time she made them.  So I asked, and viola!  She gave me the recipe.  I use Hanover Dark Red Kidney Beans and Butter Beans, and Bush's Regular Baked Beans.
