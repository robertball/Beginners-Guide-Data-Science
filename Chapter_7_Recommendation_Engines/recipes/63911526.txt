Title:
Chocolate PB Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cubes of ice
2 cups sweetened, chocolate almond milk
1 frozen over ripe banana
1 heaping tbl smooth peanut butter

Directions:
In a good quality blender, crush ice until it becomes like snow.
Add remaining ingredients and blend until smooth.
Serve immediately.
