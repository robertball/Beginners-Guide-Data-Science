Title:
Orange-Honey Glazed Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984312.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cook's® brand Bone-in Spiral Sliced Ham, Butt or Shank Portion Ham, or Half Ham
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup orange juice
1 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Prepare and heat ham according to package directions.
Prepare glaze by combining honey and orange juice in small bowl; mix well. Stir in brown sugar.
Apply glaze and heat uncovered for the last 30 minutes of cooking time. Carve ham and serve.

Submitter: 
Cook's® Ham
Description: 
This recipe courtesy of Cook's Ham is a sweetly glazed ham with a bright citrus flavor.
