Title:
Mom's Best Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
3 tablespoons white sugar
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups milk
2 eggs
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt. Add milk, eggs, vanilla and oil; mix well.
Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron. Cook until golden brown.
