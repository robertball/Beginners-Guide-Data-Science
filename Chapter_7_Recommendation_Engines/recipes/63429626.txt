Title:
ultimate macaroni and cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups  macaroni
1 1/4 cups 1/2 inch (cube) extra sharp cheese
2 TBLS plus 2 tsp flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1/8 tsp nutmeg (optional)
1 1/3 cup half and half
1 1/3 cups heavy cream
2/3 cup sour cream
2 lg eggs
3/4 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 1/4 cups packed (grated) extra sharp cheese. (5oz)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 13 x 9 pan. Cook macaroni al dente.Transfer macaroni to cake pan.Mix in cubed cheese.whisk dry ingredients together,gradually add half and half to dry mixture and than heavy cream,sour cream.add eggs and worcestershire sauce. Whisk to blend well.pour over macaroni in pan. stir,sprinkle grated cheese over top.Bake 25 mins. let stand 10 mins
