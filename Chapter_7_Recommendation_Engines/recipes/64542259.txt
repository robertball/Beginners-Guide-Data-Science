Title:
Vegan Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3183349.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion peeled & diced
2 celery ribs, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup carrots, peeled & chopped
1/2 large sweet potato, cubed
2 cups dried split peas
6 cups Gluten Free vegetable broth
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp paprika
1 bay leaf
salt & pepper to taste
green onion for garnish

Directions:
Heat a large pot over medium-high heat
Add onion, celery, bell pepper & carrots with a splash or two or broth
Cook until veggies soften 10 minutes
Add garlic & spices saut√© another 5 minutes
Pour broth into pot with sweet potato, split peas & bay leaf
Turn heat down to medium-low
Simmer 45 minutes covered until green peas begin to soften & pop
Remove half the soup, place in blender & pulse to puree
Add pureed soup back to the pot & stir well
Serve immediately & garnish with green onions

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Vegan Split Pea Soup made with simple & budget friendly vegetarian ingredients, this low calorie, easy-to-make soup is a hearty bowl of comfort and health!
