Title:
Homemade Rice & 'Roni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup uncooked spaghetti, broken into 1-inch pieces
3/4 cup uncooked white rice
14 1/2 ounces chicken or vegetable broth
1 chicken or vegetable bouillon cube
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium skillet over medium heat. Add spaghetti pieces and saute, stirring constantly, until spaghetti begins to brown.
Add rice, stirring until the rice is well-coated with butter and the spaghetti browns more.
Carefully pour in broth and add bouillon. Cover and simmer about 20 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.
