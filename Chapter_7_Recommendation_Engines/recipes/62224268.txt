Title:
Sugar Cookie Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup confectioners' sugar
4 teaspoons milk for flood icing
4 t heavy cream for stiff icing (for lettering and such)
1 T light corn syrup
1 t teaspoon vanilla/almond extract
assorted food coloring

Directions:
In a small bowl, stir together confectioners' sugar and milk/cream until smooth.
Beat in corn syrup and extract until icing is smooth and glossy.
Divide into separate bowls, and add food colorings to each to desired intensity.
Dip cookies, or paint them with a brush.

Submitter: 
Kat G
Description: 
This icing dries hard and shiny and the colors stay bright.Modified from: Sugar Cookie Icing
