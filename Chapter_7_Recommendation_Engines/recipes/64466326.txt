Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2443348.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. Bisquick
3/4 c. sugar (divided)
3 t. cinnamon (divided)
1T chili powder (divided)
1/2 c. applesauce
1/4 c. milk
1 egg
2 T. oil
5 T. melted butter
Optional: Kale

Directions:
Combine Bisquick, ¼c. sugar, 1 t. cinnamon, and about half the chili powder.
Stir in applesauce, milk, egg and oil until well mixed.
Fill greases muffin pan ? full.
Back at 400 for 10 min.
In a bowl mix ½ c. sugar with 2 t. cinnamon and the other .5 T chili powder.
Dip hot puffs in bowl of melted butter and then in bowl of cinnamon sugar.
