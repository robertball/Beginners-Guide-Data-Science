Title:
Ham Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. pre-cooked ham, chunked
1 can celery soup
2 T. onion, cut fine (or use dried, minced onion)
3 c. raw potato, diced or small cubed
1 can sliced carrots
1 can peas
1 T. butter
1 T. brown sugar
1/2 c. water
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
1/2 c. evaporated milk

Directions:
Heat butter and brown sugar until melted in pan.
Add ham and cook until browned. Remove and keep warm.
In the same pan, mix water, soup, onion, salt and pepper.
Add potatoes, carrots and peas. Cover and cook over low heat until veggies are tender, about 25 minutes. Pour milk into mix and add ham. Cover until hot and serve.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
Delicious and easy to alter to your tastes, this chowder is a yummy meal for any cool day.
