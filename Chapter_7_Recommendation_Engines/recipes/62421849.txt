Title:
Guy's five pepper- stuffed skirt steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 red bell peppers
2 poblano chiles
2 jalapeno chiles
1 anaheim chile
2 3/4lb skirt steaks, halved and pounded 1/4 inch thick
Salt and black pepper
4 peices snack size string cheese, quarted lengthwise
1Tbsp Evoo
1 onion, finley chopped
3 Tbsp vodka
1/2C half and half
2Tbsp finely chopped cilantro
4 Green onions, chopped

Directions:
Preheat a grill to high. Grill the bell peppers and chiles, turning occasionally, until evenly charred about 10min. Transfer to a bowl and cover; let cool. Stem and peel and discard the seeds. Slice 1 bell pepper, 1 poblano, 1 jalapeno and the anaheim into strips. Chop the remaining bell pepper and chiles and transfer to a skillet.
Season the steak on both sides with salt and pepper.  Working  with 1 peice at a time, arrange the roasted bell pepper, poblano,jalapeno and anaheim stips and the string cheese on the steak. Starting from the short end, roll up the steak, jelly-roll-style. Truss with kitchen string.
Add the olive oil and onion to the chopped peppers and chiles and cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, for 4 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for 1 more minute. Add the vodka and bring to a boil.
Season with salt and pepper.
Lower the grill temperature to medium. Cover and grill the steaks, turning occasionaly, for 10-12minutes. Return the pepper sauce to a simer; stir in the cilantro and green onions. Slice each steak crosswise and serve on a bed of sauce.
