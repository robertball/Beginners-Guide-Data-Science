Title:
Chili Butter Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3885098.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Acorn Squash
1 Tablespoon Light Butter
2 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
½ Teaspoon Salt
1 Teaspoon Chili Powder
½ Teaspoon Garlic Powder

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
Cut acorn squash in half and scoop out the seeds.
Place squash in baking dish cut sides facing up.
Using your fingers, rub the flesh of each half with ½ tablespoon of butter for each side.
Mix brown sugar, salt, chili powder, and garlic powder together in a bowl.
Generously season each side with the seasoning mixture.
Roast squash in the oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour until the squash is tender.

Description: 
Weight Watcher friendly acorn squash with a decadent butter sauce that is sweet with a hint of spice.  This Chili Butter Acorn Squash is delicious side dish and only 6 SmartPoints!
