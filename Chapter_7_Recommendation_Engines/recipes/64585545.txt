Title:
Smoky Black Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz chopped cremini mushrooms
2 Tbsp olive oil, divided
1/4 cup chopped onion
3 chopped garlic cloves
1/2 cup unsalted chicken stock
2 tsp chopped chipotle chiles in adobo sauce
1 (15.5-oz) can drained unsalted black beans
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 cup reduced-fat sour cream
2 tsp lime juice

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.  Add mushrooms, onion, and garlic; cook 7 minutes.
Add stock and chipotle chiles in adobo sauce; cook 1 minute.
Combine mushroom mixture, beans, and salt in bowl of a food processor; pulse until well combined. Transfer to a bowl; swirl in sour cream, remaining oil, and lime juice. Serve with tortilla chips and crudites.
Cals: 96; Fat 4.4g (1.1g sat); Pro 4g; Fiber 3g; Sugars 1g; Iron 1mg; Sodium 155 mg
