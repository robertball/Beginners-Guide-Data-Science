Title:
Red Italian  Salad dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-8oz can tomato sauce
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup wine vinegar
1 Tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon oregano, crushed
1 clove of garlic minced
1/4 tsp coarse crushed black pepper
1/4 tsp basil, crushed

Directions:
In a screw top jar, combine all ingredient, cover and shake. Chill well.

Submitter: 
rlt11_NMC
Description: 
This dressing is good as a normal Italian Dressing but with a tomato tang to it as well. This is one that my Mom whipped up often for us. She had all the ingredient in her pantry as most of us do too.
