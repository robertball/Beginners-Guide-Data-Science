Title:
Simple Chicken Salad Sandwiches with Garlic Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 French rolls about six inches long
Butter,  softened
Garlic salt
1 Cooked chicken breast chopped
1 celery stalk chopped
1/2 cup mayonnaise  (or more to taste)
1/2 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Add chopped chicken, celery, mayonnaise, celery salt, black pepper and apple cider vinegar to a bowl. Mix well. Add more mayonnaise if desired.
Preheat oven to 350. Butter insides of the rolls and sprinkle garlic salt.  Toast in the oven for 5 minutes until insides of the rolls are golden. Remove from oven.
Spoon filling into rolls  and serve.

Description: 
This is a quick and tasty chicken salad.  It's a great way to use up a leftover chicken breast. The recipe is basic enough that you can add your favorite ingredients to it. The garlic rolls add another dimension to the flavor.
