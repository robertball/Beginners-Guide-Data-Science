Title:
Passover Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups grated apple (3small)
2 cups grated carrot (5 large)
1 cup grated sweet or white potato (2 23oz cans or 2 fresh)
1 cup chopped prunes
1 cup chopped raisins
1 cup sugar
1 cup matzo meal
4 Tbl lemon juice
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
1 cup (i use less) oil

Directions:
Combine ingredients. Put in 9 x 13 ungreased casserole. Bake 350 for 45min
