Title:
Graham Cracker French Toast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11 whole graham crackers, broken in half (1 wrapped package)
1 tbsp butter
1 tsp cinnamon
4 eggs
1 cup milk
1 tbsp Vanilla extract
Strawberry or raspberry preserves
Cinnamon sugar

Directions:
1.  Grease a 9x9 pan with the butter
2.  Put one layer the graham crackers in even flat layers in the pan.  Then spread a thin layer of fruit preserves over the next batch of crackers and put them jam side down.  Repeat with the last layer.
3.  Beat the eggs, milk, cinnamon and vanilla - pour over mixture
4.  Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.  Let the pan sit for about 10 minutes
5.  Bake at 325 degrees for 25 minutes, then boost the oven to 375 and bake for an additional 5 to 10 minutes
6.  Top with powdered sugar, syrup and/or fresh berries.

Submitter: 
Pam Morin
Description: 
I found this recipe on a box of graham crackers, but did some tweaking.  My kids love this, they think it tastes like a custard desert.
