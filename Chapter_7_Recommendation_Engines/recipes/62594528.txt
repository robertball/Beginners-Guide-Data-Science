Title:
Hawaiian Nut Loaf II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/555967.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1/2 cup margarine
1 cup sugar)
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/8 teaspoon) salt
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, with juice
1/3 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
In a large bowl, cream together eggs, butter and sugar. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda and salt. Stir the flour mixture into the egg mixture. Mix in the pineapple (with juice) and nuts. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 75 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.
