Title:
Cheesy Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 packages (10-ounce) frozen chopped spinach
1/2 cup spinach liquid, reserved from cooking spinach
1 onion, chopped
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 cup skim milk
6 oz. hot pepper cheese, cubed
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Cook spinach according to directions on package. Drain well saving 1/2 cup spinach liquid; set aside.
Coat a skillet with nonstick cooking spray, saute onion until tender. Add flour and stir. Gradually stir in the milk and spinach liquid to make a sauce. Cook over medium heat until mixture thickens. Stir in cheese, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic powder, cooking until cheese is melted. Add spinach and mix well.
