Title:
Fried Butterflied Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/813630.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined and butterflied
3/4 cup of water
1  cup cornstarch
2 eggs
2 cups panko crumbs
Salt
Garlic Powder
5 cups oil for deep frying

Directions:
Preheat deep fryer or skillet with oil to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, pour in water and mix in cornstarch and eggs.
Add salt and garlic powder into the panko.
Dip the shrimp into the mixture allowing them to be completely coated. Then roll the shrimp in the breadcrumbs. Coat the shrimp well with the breadcrumbs. Do the second coat of the wash and crumb after finished all the first coat of the shrimp to give time let the crumb dried up. Mix up the cornstarch batter again. Dip the breadcrumbs coated shrimp back into the cornstarch batter. Roll the shrimp in the breadcrumbs for a second time.  refrigerated the shrimp for half hour to help the breading stick on the shrimp when they are frying.
Drop shrimp, one at a time, into the hot oil and cook shrimp until they are golden brown.
