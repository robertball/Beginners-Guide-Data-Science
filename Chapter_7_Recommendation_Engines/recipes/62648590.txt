Title:
Barbecue Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/588490.jpg
Ingredients:
2 chicken breast
Organic barbecue sauce
1 cup water
Salt & pepper.

Directions:
Boil chicken in 8 inch skillet, about 8 minutes on each side. Season with salt & pepper.
Place on plate and pour barbecue sauce over top.
