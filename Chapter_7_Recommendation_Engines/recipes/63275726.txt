Title:
Chicken Cordon Blu Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Whole Rotisserie Chicken, deboned and shredded
1/2 lb very thinly sliced deli ham, roughly chopped
1/4 lb thinly sliced swiss cheese
4 T Butter
4 T Flour
3 1/4 cups Milk
2 T Fresh Squeezed Lemon Juice
1 T Dijon
1 1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp smoked paprika
1/2 tsp white pepper
6 T Butter
1 1/2 Cups Panko
3/4 tsp Seasoning Salt
1 1/2 tsp Dried Parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Butter a 9 X 13 Pan.  Place chicken on bottom of dish.  Cover with ham, then cheese slices.
Make white sauce using Butter - Pepper.  Pour over meat and cheese.
Melt butter and toss with Panko, salt, and parsley.  Sprinkle over casserole.
Bake 45 min, or until hot and bubbly.
