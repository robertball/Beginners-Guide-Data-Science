Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - 6 large Granny Smith apples
8 graham cracker squares (4 rectangles)
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup rolled oats (oatmeal)
1/2 cup flour
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon allspice or nutmeg
1/2 cup melted butter or margarine

Directions:
Prepare enough apple slices to fill deep dish baker 3/4 full (I use 15x10x2 deep dish stoneware pan)
Finely chop graham crackers.
Stir together graham cracker crumbs, sugar, oats, flour & spices.  Add melted butter and mix well.
Spoon mixture over apples slices.
Bake in 350 oven 35 - 45 minutes or until apples are tender.
Cool slightly.
Serve with ice cream or whipped cream.
