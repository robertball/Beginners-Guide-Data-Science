Title:
Peanut Butter Hemp Cookies Recipe
Image:
NA
Ingredients:
2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
½ cup raisins
¼ cup hemp seed hearts
1 teaspoon baking powder
½ teaspoon baking soda
¾ cup brown sugar
¾ cup white sugar
½ cup butter, softened
½ cup applesauce
1 cup natural peanut butter
2  eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Mix flour, raisins, hemp seed hearts, baking powder, and baking soda together in a bowl. Beat brown sugar, white sugar, butter, and applesauce together in a bowl using an electric mixer until smooth and creamy; add peanut butter and beat until smooth.
Beat eggs and vanilla extract together in a bowl; stir into peanut butter mixture. Mix flour mixture into peanut butter mixture until a smooth dough forms. Roll dough into small balls and place on a baking sheet; press each with a fork to flatten.
Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 9 minutes.

Submitter: 
Heather Hedstrom
Description: 
A very healthy peanut butter cookie full of protein. I have a very picky 3-year-old and was trying different ways to get some extra protein in his diet. He loves these cookies and they're extremely healthy.
