Title:
Three-pepper Quesadilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. finely chopped yellow bell pepper
1 C. finely copped red bell pepper
1/2 c. chopped sweet onion
1 T. finely chopped jalapeno
1/2 tsp. cumin
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
8 corn tortillas
1 C. Jack cheese

Directions:
Combine first seven ingredients.
Heat a large skillet coated with cooking spray.  Add 1 tortilla and top with 1/4 c. mixture. Sprinkle with 1/4 c. cheese; top with 1 tortilla.  Repeat with remaining tortillas.  Cook each quesadilla 2 minutes on each side, pressing down with a spatula.
