Title:
Turkey and Avocado Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983215.jpg
Ingredients:
4 leaves lettuce
8 thin slices deli turkey breast
1/2 peeled pitted avocado, cut into 8 slices
8 slices Pepperidge Farm® Whole Grain 15 Grain Bread, toasted
2 tablespoons Pace® Chunky Salsa

Directions:
Divide the lettuce, turkey and avocado among 4 bread slices. Top each with 1 1/2 teaspoons salsa and the remaining bread slices.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Just a few slices of avocado and a kick of salsa takes ordinary turkey sandwiches to a whole new level of deliciousness!
