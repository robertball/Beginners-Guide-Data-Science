Title:
Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 chicken breasts (any chicken pieces will work)
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 soup can of water
salt and pepper to taste
several pats of butter
1 cup uncooked rice

Directions:
Combine soup and water. Put uncooked rice in a casserole dish. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and put several pats of butter over rice. Arrange chicken over rice and then pour soup and water mixture over chicken and rice. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours.
