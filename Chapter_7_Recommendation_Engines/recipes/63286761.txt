Title:
Chicken Chop Suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, trimmed of all fat, cut into 1/2" wide 2" long strips
2 tbs evoo
1/2 C frozen organic edamame pods
4 stalks celery, chopped on a slant
8 broccoli florets, sliced in half
1 15oz can bean sprouts, drained
3 tbs tamari (or soy sauce)
1/2 C water
1 tbs arrowroot

Directions:
In a large saute pan over medium high heat add the evoo to the pan.
Once oil is hot add the chicken strips, and the soy sauce, stir frying until almost completely cooked.
Add in the frozen edamame pods, stir frying for 2 minutes.
Add in the celery and broccoli, stir to mix in and cover for 3 minutes.
Add in drained canned bean sprouts (fresh can be used also) and mix well. Leave uncovered and continue stir frying for another few minutes.
Mix the 1/2 C water with the arrowroot and pour into the mixture, stirring to thicken. If it gets to thick add hot water by a 1/4 C at a time.

Submitter: 
Dragonfly
Description: 
Simple go to recipe for BSS advanced plan. Combines canned and fresh vegetables with chicken strips in a light soy sauce and arrowroot glaze. Very fast & easy recipe.
