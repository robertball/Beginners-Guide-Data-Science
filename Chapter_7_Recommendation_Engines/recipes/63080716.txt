Title:
Kathy's White Chocolate Oatmeal Pecan Coconut Chewy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/778316.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 tablespoons milk
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 teaspoon coconut extract
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 cups rolled oats
10-12 ounces white chocolate
1 cup chopped pecans or walnuts (optional)
1 3/4 cups shredded coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar and white sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the milk, coconut extract, and vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; stir into the sugar mixture until well blended. Stir in the oats, chocolate, nuts and coconut until evenly distributed.  Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 14 minutes in the preheated oven.
Cool for 1 minute on the cookie sheet and then remove to wire rack.   Cool completely and then store in tightly sealed container.
