Title:
Lipton Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/484121.jpg
Ingredients:
5-6 Lb chuck roast
2 pkts of Lipton Onion Soup mix
1 bottle of beer, any kind you have on hand.

Directions:
Put the roast in a pan and sprinkle with soup mix. Add the beer and cover with lid or foil. Put it in the oven
and bake for 3 or 4 hrs.Let rest 15 mins after fork tender. Serve with mashed potatoes or rice and a salad.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is terrific and virtually self cooking. Please, if you need to, slow cook with my blessing!
