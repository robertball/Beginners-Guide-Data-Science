Title:
Side Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head of lettuce
1 tomato
1/2 bell pepper, any color
1/2 cucumber
Italian dressing
Ranch dressing

Directions:
Tear lettuce and place equal amounts in small salad bowls. Add diced vegetables. Serve dressing choices on the side.
