Title:
Simplest Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4229482.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups red wine (I prefer Cabernet sauvignon)
1 cup orange liqueur (triple sec/cointreau)
1 litre cran-rasberry juice
2 cups frozen field berries

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a pitcher and let stand in the fridge overnight

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
I love Sangria but not the time it takes making one so I came up with this.  Its so good people ask for the recipe.
