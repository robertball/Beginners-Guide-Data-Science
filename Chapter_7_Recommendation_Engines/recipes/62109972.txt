Title:
Fully Loaded Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lbs. red potatoes, sliced in half
1 lb bacon
2 cups light sour cream
1 cup light mayonnaise
1 packet Ranch dressing mix
1 bunch green onions, chopped
2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
Bring large pot of water to a boil over high heat. Add potatoes slowly to keep water boiling. Cover pot with lid, turn heat down to med-high. Boil for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside to cool.
In the meantime, cook bacon until crispy. Drain on paper towels, crumble when cool, set aside.
In a very large bowl, mix sour cream, mayonnaise, and ranch mix together. Add onions, 1 1/3 cups cheese, and bacon, reserving a handful for topping.
Cut potatoes into bite size cubes. Stir into Ranch mixture to coat. Top with remaining cheese and bacon. Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Mis' Kitchen
Description: 
This is not your typical potato salad. Goes fast at gatherings.Tastes just as good on the first day as it does the second, and vice versa. Enjoy!
