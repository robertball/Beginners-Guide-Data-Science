Title:
Overnight Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup steel cut oats
2 cups dried fruit (raisins, etc)
4 cups water
1/2 cup half-and-half

Directions:
Place an oven safe dish in the center of the crock pot. Make sure it will allow crock lid to seal correctly.
Add all ingredients to oven dish and stir to blend.
Add water to the crock until it is about halfway up the sides of the baking dish.
Turn on low or warm heat.
Cover and let cook for 8 to 9 hours (overnight).
Stir and remove to serving bowls.

Submitter: 
Joan
Description: 
The basic recipe is from Alton Brown, but has been adapted to suit our needs/tastes. The extra step of using a water bath and a interior baking dish prevents burning/browning along the sides.
