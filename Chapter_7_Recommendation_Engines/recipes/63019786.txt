Title:
Farm Fresh Beef Jerky Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lb round steak
1 tsp Adolphs non MSG tenderizer
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp fresh ground pepper
1/4 tsp ginger powder
1 tbsp worcester sauce
1 tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp hot sauce
3/4 cup soy sauce

Directions:
Slice meat (while partially frozen) against the grain into thin strips
mix spices and sauces until well blended
Marinate for 12-24 hours

Submitter: 
Pam Morin
Description: 
We participated in beef livestock CSA shares.  Though the meat was healthier, organic and raised sustainably it was a little tougher than most people are used to.  This was an experiment with my mothers Nesco food dehydrator
