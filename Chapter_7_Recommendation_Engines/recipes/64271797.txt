Title:
French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1/2c butter
2 tbsp light corn syrup
12 slices while bread
1/4c sugar
1tsp ground cinnamon, divided
6 eggs
1 1/2c milk
1tsp vanilla

Directions:
Bring brown sugar, butter and corn syrup to a boil stirring constantly, remove from heat
Pour into a greased 9x13 pan. Top with 6 slices of bread.
Combine sugar and 1/2 tsp cinnamon: sprinkle half over bread. Place remaining bread on top. Sprinkle with remaining cinnamon-sugar
In a large bowl, beat the eggs, milk, vanilla and remaining cinnamon. Pour over bread, Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Remove from fridge for 30 min before baking. BAke uncovered at 350 for 30-35 min
