Title:
Glazed Summer Rainbow Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/658614.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint of fresh blueberries
1 pound of fresh strawberries, chopped
4 fresh nectarines, chopped
3 kiwis, peeled and chopped
1 14 oz. container of peach pie glaze

Directions:
Gently stir all ingredients together in a serving bowl.
Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, then serve.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jennifer Baker
Description: 
When Summer fruits are in season, delight your friends and family with this easy and colorful fruit salad.  I like to chop the fruit into chunks for better visual impact. 
Garnish individual servings with whipped cream if desired.
