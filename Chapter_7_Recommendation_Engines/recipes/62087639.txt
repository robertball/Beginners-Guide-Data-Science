Title:
Chicken Tortilla Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, cut into bite size pieces
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 small can chopped green chiles
1 small carton sour cream
1 small onion, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
10 corn tortillas, cut into 1/2" strips
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet cook chicken until done; set aside.
In a mixing bowl combine soups, green chiles, sour cream and onion to make the sauce.
Grease a 9x13 casserole dish and layer tortillas, chicken and sauce; make two layers.
Sprinkle top with cheese.
Cover with saran wrap or foil.
Refridgerate for atleast 8 hours.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 mins covered and then 15 mins uncovered.
