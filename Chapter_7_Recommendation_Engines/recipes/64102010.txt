Title:
Big Ray's Blueberry Breakfast Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1234427.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsalted butter (1 stick)
2 tsp grated lemon rind
7/8 cup plus 1 Tbl sugar, divided
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 cup blueberries
2 cups flour, divided
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Greases a square baking dish with butter.
Combine butter, lemon rind and 7/8 cup sugar (1 cup less 2 Tbl) in bowl.  beat until fluffy.  Add egg and vanilla; beat until combined.
Toss blueberries with 1/4 cup flour.  In separate bowl whisk remaining 1 3/4 cup flour,baking soda and salt.
Add flour mixture to egg mixture a little at a time, alternating with buttermilk.  Fold in blueberries.
Spread batter in prepared pan.  Sprinkle batter with remaing 1 Tbl sugar.  Bake 35 to 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Let cool at least 15 minutes before serving.
