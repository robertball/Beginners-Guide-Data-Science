Title:
Parmesan Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 dry stove top stuffing mix
2-4 tbsp Parmesan cheese
2 chicken breasts
3 tbsp melted butter

Directions:
Mix stuffing and parm cheese in small baking dish.
Dip chicken in crumb mixture, coating on all sides.
Pour melted butter over chicken.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
