Title:
Crack Cocaine Casserole aka Chicken Potato Chip Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs	Boneless chicken breasts
1	green bell pepper
1	red bell pepper
1	yellow onion
1 pack	fresh sliced mushrooms
1 stick	butter
1 box	potato chips (2bags)
1/2 cup	all purpose flour
2 cups	milk
2 cups	chicken stock (use water from cooking chicken)
1 cup	American cheese (one pack of the sliced good stuff)

Directions:
Boil chicken in a stock pot until the chicken reaches 165 degrees.
While the chicken is cooking dice up the peppers and onion into small pieces.
Take one bag of chips and crush up in to smaller pieces.
Layer the chips in the bottom of a 9X 13 pan.
Once the chicken is cooked ~ DO NOT DISCARD THE LIQUID
Set aside chicken stock for later use.
Dice chicken into bit size pieces (after it has cooled and can be handled)
Layer the chicken over the chips.
Melt the stick of butter in the stock pot.
Once melted add the onions, peppers and mushrooms.
Saut√© till translucent.
Turn on oven to 350.
Add the 1/2 cup of flour to make a rouge.  (Should look a little brown)
Add the chicken stock and milk to the rouge.
Once the mixture is thickened ~ add one slice at a time of the cheese until cheese is melted
When sauce is done pour over the chicken and potato chips.
Take the 2nd bag of chips and crush up like the first.
Top dish with the chips.
Bake in the oven for 1/2 hour.
