Title:
Robinson Family Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510298.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Whole Turkey
3 large Chicken Stock
2 Sticks Margerine or Butter (softened)
Sage, Pepper, and Rosemary to taste

Directions:
Pre Heat Oven to 325
Clean Turkey and remove gibblets
Place on turkey rack and coat with Margerine or Butter.
Season Turkey to taste
Pour 2 Chicken Stock in bottem of roasting pan
Cover with Foil
Roast Turkey according to size until thigh reaches 175 or breast reaches 165
