Title:
Old-Fashioned Date Filled Cookie Turnovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c sifted flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 c soft butter
1 1/2 c sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
(or 1 tbsp fresh grated lemon peel)
FILLING:
1 pkg pitted dates, cut up
1/2 c sugar
1 tsp fresh grated lemon peel
1/4 c lemon juice
1/2 c chopped walnuts

Directions:
Sift flour with baking powder and salt; set aside.
In large bowl, beat butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla until light and fluffy.
Gradually stir in flour mixture until smooth and well combined. Divide into four parts, wrap with plasti and refridgerate at least overnight, or until ready to use.
FILLING: In a sauce pan combine dates and sugar with 1/2 c water. Cook over medium heat, until mixture has thickened-about 5 mins. Remove from heat stir in lemon peel, lemon juice, and walnuts. Cool completley.
Preheat oven to 375 degree. Line sheets with parchment.
On lightly floured surface, roll dough, one part at a time, into a 10"x12" rectangle. With a floured sharp knofe cut into 12 rectangles.
Using spatula, place 2 inches apart on cookie sheet. Spread 1 tsp filling on half of each; fold over. With a flooured fork, seal edges firmly; prick center of tops.
Bake 10 -12 mins, or until lightly browned. Remove to wire rack: cool.

Submitter:

Description: 
Sugar cookie turnovers filled with date filling.
These have been a tradition my mother brought over from Germany.
