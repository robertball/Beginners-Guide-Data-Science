Title:
California Sushi Unroll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sushi Rice: (Make first)
2 cups sushi rice
2 cups water, plus more for rinsing
2 tablespoons rice vinegar, subsitute: white vinegar
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon kosher salt
Salad:
1/2 lemon, juiced
1 ripe avocado, peeled and finely chopped
3 nori sheets, cut into strips
1 batch sushi rice
2 tablespoons sesame seeds, toasted
1/2 cucumber, peeled, seeded, finely chopped
12 oz. immitation crabmeat, finely rough chopped
Soy Sauce, for serving
1 nori sheet, torn, for garnish

Directions:
Sushi Rice: (make first)
Place the rice into a bowl, and cover with cool water-pour off and repeat 2-3 times, or until water runs clear.
Place the rice and 2 cups water into a medium saucepan, and place on high heat.  Bring to a boil, uncovered.
Once it boils, reduce heat to low and cover.  Cook for 15 minutes.
Remove from heat and let stand covered for 10 minutes.
Combine the vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small microwave safe bowl, and heat in the microwave fo 30-45 seconds.
Transfer the rice into a large wooden or glass mixing bowl, and add the vinegar mixture.
Fold thourougly to combine, and coat each grain of rice with mixture.
Allow to cool to room tempurature before using to make salad.
Salad:
Coat avocado in lemon juce, to prevent browning.
Combine the three cut nori sheets and prepared rice.
Add crabmeat, cucumber, and avocado.
Fold in sesame seeds.
Top with torn pieces of nori.
Serve with soy sauce on top.
