Title:
Deep Dish Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Rapid Dry Yeast
1 C Warm Water
1/4 C Canola Oil
2 T Olive Oil
1/4 C Cornmeal
2 3/4 C Flour

Directions:
In a stand mixer bowl, dissolve the yeast in the water. Add the remainder of the ingredients minus 1 1/4 C flour. Beat until well mixed. Attach the dough hook and add the remaining flour. Knead for several minutes w/ mixer. (doing this by hand would be very difficult)
For little time= remove and place on clean surface buttered or butter sprayed
For early prep= remove and place on buttered or butter sprayed plastic wrap cover and refrigerate
After removal for both= let rise until double in bulk, punch down and allow to rise again, then punch down a second time and spread into a buttered 12" Cast Iron Skillet.
