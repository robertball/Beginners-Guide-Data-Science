Title:
Pecan Sandies with a surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 cup canola oil
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 cup confectioners' sugar, sifted
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cream of tartar
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 cups chopped roasted pecans
1/2 cup white sugar for decoration
bag of chocolat covered pecans for topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a large bowl, cream together the margarine, vegetable oil, 1 cup white sugar and confectioners' sugar until smooth.  Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda, cream of tartar and salt; stir into the creamed mixture. Mix in the pecans. Roll dough into 1 inch balls and roll each ball in remaining white sugar. Place the cookies 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets.  Use thumb to slightly flatten balls of dough.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the edges are golden. Remove from cookie sheets and add one  cholocat covered pecan to each cookie and set to cool on wire racks.
