Title:
Shiitake Delight Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F1131034.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
4 cloves garlic
8  shiitake mushrooms, chopped
1 cup vegetable broth
2 tablespoons butter
Italian seasoning to taste
salt to taste

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, and cook the garlic just until golden. Mix in mushroom pieces, and cook 10 minutes, until tender.
Pour the broth into the skillet. Stir in butter until melted. Season with Italian seasoning and salt.

Submitter: 
PINAYCOOK
Description: 
This is a very delightful meal. Very simple, and my two-year old loves it! Interesting taste and texture. Serve with white rice.
