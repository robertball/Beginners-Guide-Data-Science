Title:
Cranberry-Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 and 1/2 cups water
4 TBSP honey (reduce if desired; or use white or brown sugar; or mix sugars)
2 TBSP margarine or butter, softened (or oil)
2 and 1/2 cups flour
1 and 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1 and 1/2 tsp salt (increase to 1 and 3/4 to bake in bread machine)
1 tsp mace (or cinnamon for cranberry version; can omit)
2 and 3/4 tsp yeast
2/3 cup cranberries

Directions:
Place ingredients (except cranberries) in bread machine in recommended order. If baking in the machine, select the "Sweet Bread" setting. Add cranberries when the "Mix In" beep indicates during the kneading stage.
Dough setting:  If desired, use the dough setting. When dough is complete, shape into 2 loaves. Place in oiled (sprayed) loaf pans. Allow to rise (about 20 to 30 minutes in a warm place). Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.

SIXMITTS
Description: 
Recipe from the Oster Bread Machine User Manual. 
Versatile:  decrease sugar, omit cranberries, or use as is.
2 pound loaf
