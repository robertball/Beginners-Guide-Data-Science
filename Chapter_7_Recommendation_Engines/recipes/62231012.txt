Title:
Lisa's Beer Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/324355.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups all purpose flour
3 teaspoons baking powder
1&1/2 teaspoons salt
1/3 to 1/2 cup sugar [according to taste]
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar,Colby or Jack cheese
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
6 tablespoons melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
In a large bowl, stir together flour,baking powder,salt, sugar, cheese and beer. Spoon batter into prepared loaf pan; spread batter to evenly fill pan.After 30 minutes baking,pour melted butter on top of loaf and sprinkle with garlic salt.
Bake for 30 to 40 more minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of loaf comes out clean.
