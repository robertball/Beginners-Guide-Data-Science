Title:
Crockpot white chili with chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/755926.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz dried white great northern beans
1 small onion, chopped
1 lb chicken (2 chicken breasts)
3 cups chicken broth
1/2 tsp oregano
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tbs chopped jalapeños
4 ounces shredded Mexican style cheese (optional)
1 tomato, chopped (optional)
Cilantro (optional)

Directions:
Cover beans amply with water and soak overnight at room temperature.
Rinse beans and add to crockpot, along with the chicken breasts, chopped onion, chicken broth, jalapeños, oregano, cumin, and garlic powder.
Cook for 4 hours on high or 8 hours on low.
Shred chicken breasts with a fork and mix up chili.
Serve chili and top with chopped fresh tomatoes and shredded cheese. Chop up cilantro for mixing in, or simply use as garnish.
