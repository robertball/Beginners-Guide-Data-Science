Title:
Garlic-Lemon Shrimp Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Fresh shrimp, cooked
2 medium tomatoes
8 oz. sliced mushrooms
1 large jar of artichoke hearts
2 medium lemons
3 C. heavy whipping cream
2.5 Tbs. minced garlic
1/4 C. flour
1 stick butter
Salt/Pepper/Oregano to taste
Olive oil
Parmesan cheese
Angel hair pasta

Directions:
Melt butter in pan. Add whipping cream, flour, juice from both lemons, salt/pepper/oregano, garlic.
Meanwhile, boil angel hair pasta.
In separate pan, sautee tomatoes and artichoke hearts in olive oil. Drain and set aside.
Sautee shrimp and mushrooms together.
Combine tomatoes, artichokes, shrimp, mushrooms, and cream sauce.
Spoon over angel hair. Sprinkle with parmesan. Garnish with lemon wedge, if desired.

Submitter: 
Sarah W
Description: 
Delicious! Lots of garlic and lemon with shrimp, mushrooms, tomatoes and artichoke hearts over angel hair pasta. Very hearty and flavorful!
