Title:
Megan's Tuda Duda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bag egg noodles or macaroni noodles
2 cans tuna fish
1 can diced water chestnuts
1 8 oz package cream cheese
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 medium onion diced
1/8 tsp ceyanne pepper
1/3 cup milk
6 slices american cheese
1 sleeve Ritz crackers crushed
3 tablespoons butter
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Boil water and add noodles and boil for 10 minutes. While noodles are cooking, saute diced onion, water chestnuts and ceyanne pepper with 1 tablespoon butter until translucent. When noodles have finished cooking, drain and return to pot. Dice brick of cream cheese and add to hot nooodles, stirring till noodles are coated. Add saute mixture, two cans drained tuna, mushroom soup, milk, salt and pepper. Stir till well combined. Grease 9 x 13 pan and layer mixure with american cheese. Bake casserole for 25 minutes.Meanwhile, melt last two tablespoons in a skillet and lightly toast cracker crumbs. Remove cassarole from oven and spread crumb mixture over top.  Broil for a few minutes, being careful not to burn, until golden brown.

Submitter: 
NUNPUNCH
Description: 
This hot, stick-to-your-ribs comfort cassarole is super rich and satisfying. Special ingredients include cream cheese and diced water chestnuts for a great crunchy texture.
