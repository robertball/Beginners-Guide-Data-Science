Title:
Carrot Soup with Rosemary and Herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2+ T olive oil
1 lb carrots
1 onion
1 apple
8 cloves garlic
2 stems fresh rosemary
dried sage
dried basil
dried herbs de provence
water
salt
lemon juice and/or zest to taste

Directions:
heat oil over medium heat
boil carrots to soften and drain
add garlic, onion, apple, carrots to oil
add rosemary, sage, basil, herbs de provence, (approx 1tsp each dried herbs), salt to taste
add water to desired consistency and to cook veggies
puree in blender
lemon juice to taste
