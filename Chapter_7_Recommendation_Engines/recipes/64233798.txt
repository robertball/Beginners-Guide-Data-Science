Title:
Broccoli Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Cooked Broccoli
2 Cups Cooked cubed chicken
3/4 cup corn
1 medium onion chopped and cooked
1 stick celery chopped fine, cooked very well
1 1/2 cup instant rice
5 large mushrooms cooked in butter
In a large bowl microwave 1 can each cream of chicken, mushroom soup and 3/4 can water and mix throughly. Add 8 slices of cheese and mix until cheese melted. Add all other ingredients, mix well and back 45 mins at 350 degrees
