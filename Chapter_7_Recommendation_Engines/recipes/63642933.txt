Title:
Sally Lunn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups warm water
1/4 teaspoon ginger
2 Tb sugar
2 Tb active dry yeast
2 cups flour
2 Tb gluten
1/2 cup powdered milk
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup soft butter
2 eggs
3 more cups flour

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients & set aside
In mixing bowl, mix flour, salt, powdered milk & salt. Pour yeast mixture in & beat well.
Add eggs & butter, and beat again. Then add remaining 3 cups flour and mix, using bread hook, until thoroughly blended.
Cover, and let rise for 1 hour.
Beat the dough down, and spoon it into a greased 10 inch tube pan, cover and let rise for 45 minutes or until within an inch from the top of the pan.
Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 40 to 45 minutes. Let cool in pan for 5 minutes, then remove.

Submitter: 
Yomama
Description: 
This wonderful bread is aromatic and showy, since it is baked in a tube pan. It is delicious served warm, or sliced thickly and toasted.Modified from: Golden Cake Batter Bread
