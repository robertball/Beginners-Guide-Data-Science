Title:
David Hall's Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup shortening
1/3 cup sugar
1 cup corn meal
1 cup flour
3 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
2 eggs, beaten
1 1/2 cups milk soured with 1/2 tsp. vinegar (or buttermilk)
1/2 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Heat iron skillet in over with shortening
Sift sugar, corn meal, flour, baking powder and salt
Add eggs, milk, and baking soda
Add melted shortening
Bake until brown (about 40 minutes)
