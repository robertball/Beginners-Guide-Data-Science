Title:
Awesome Baked Sea Scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 sea scallops, rinsed and drained
5 tablespoons butter, melted
5 cloves garlic, minced
2 shallots, chopped
1/4 c white wine
3 pinches ground nutmeg
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup panko bread crumbs
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
4 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup chopped parsley
lemon wedges for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Saute garlic and shallots in 1 tablespoon butter. Add wine and let reduce and then add rest of butter. Let cool.  Toast panko crumbs in non stick skillet and 1 Tablespoon butter until brown. Add paemesan cheese. Set aside. Place scallops in a bowlwith garlic and shallots. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Stir gently to combine. Transfer to a casserole dish. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top of and in betweenscallops.
Bake in preheated oven until crumbs are brown and scallops are done, about 11 to 14 minutes. Top with parsley, and serve with lemon wedges on the side.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
Large sea scallops are combined with garlic, shallots, butter, and bread crumbs then baked to perfection. Being from Maine, we love our seafood, and this is one of the greats!Modified from: Awesome Baked Sea Scallops
