Title:
Sweet Mustard Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
1 TBSP cup butter, melted
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
breadcrumb

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a shallow baking dish.
In a small bowl, stir together the honey, Dijon mustard, melted butter and pepper.  Drdge in Breadcrubm.  Place chicken in the greased baking dish, and pour the honey mustard sauce over it.
Bake for 45 minutes in the preheated oven, (basting if needed wiith the sauce), until chicken is firm and juices run clear.
