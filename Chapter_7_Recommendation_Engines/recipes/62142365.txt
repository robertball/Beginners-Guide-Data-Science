Title:
Chocolate Chip Pecan Banana Bread (without eggs)! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large bananas, mashed
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 1/2 c. flour
1 container: Hunt's snack pack - tapioca or vanilla pudding
3/4 c. sugar
1/2 c. butter
1 c. pecans
1/2 c. chocolate chips (semi-sweet)

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients together.
Add mashed bananas and melted butter.
Stir together until well-mixed (don't over-beat).
Spread mixture into greased and lightly floured small loaf pan.
Top with chocolate chips.
Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ashley Weinrich
Description: 
I ran out of eggs in my apartment and used a Hunt's Snack Pack of tapioca pudding as a substitute! Add some pecans and some chocolate chips on top... an amazingly light, fluffy, and delicious banana bread!
