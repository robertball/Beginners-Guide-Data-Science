Title:
Easy candy cane truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/324732.jpg
Ingredients:
1 16 oz package of candy cane Oreos or similiar chocolate sandwich cookie.
1 8oz bar cream cheese, softened.
1 24oz package of white bark coating
6 6" candy canes.
72 1 1/4" paper liners

Directions:
Chop cookies in food processor until uniform in size and texture.
Place chopped cookies into mixer, add softened cream cheese and mix until well blended.
Scoop mixture (see footnote) and place on wax paper covered cookie sheet.
Chill until firm.
Crush candy canes in food processor.
Sprinkle an even layer into the bottoms of the paper liners.
Dip chilled centers (see footnote on my suggestion for dipping) into melted white bark coating and place into liners.
Before coating hardens, sprinkle with crushed candy cane.

Submitter: 
JonG
Description: 
This recipe is a tweak on a truffle recipe given to me by a friend of the family. A wonderful Christmas treat.
