Title:
Calavasitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic
1/4 - 1/2 sm onion chopped
1 medium can stewed tomato
1 medium can mexi-corn
Jack cheese - garnish
Zuccini or Summer Squash
Optional: 1 lb hamburger

Directions:
Saute garlic and onion and meat (if any until browned)
Add Tomato & Mexi-corn
Slice 3-4 small Zuccini and add.
Squash will cook quickly so don't over cook.
Serve

Submitter: 
Susan Murdoch
Description: 
Rich dish that can be made with with Hamburger and Zucchini. Yumm.
