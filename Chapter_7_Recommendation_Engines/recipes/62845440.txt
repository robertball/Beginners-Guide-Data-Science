Title:
Cookies'n'Cream cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cups all purpose flour
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 cups butter
1 2/3 cups brown sugar
5 egg whites
2 1/2 tsp vanilla
1 1/4 cups milk
1 cup chocolate chips
1/2 cup chopped oreos
Frosting
8 oz cream cheese
1/2 cup butter or 1 stick of margarine
1 1/2 cups chopped oreos
350* 20min

Directions:
Start with mixing your frosting first; it's better for frosting to chill while cupcakes are in progress.
Soften and mix together butter and cream cheese then add confectioners sugar in blending it gradually so it keeps smooth. Use a sifter if you're using organic powdered sugar. Mix in oreos. Refrigerate while making cupcakes.
Grease cupcake tin or use cupcake foils or papers.
Preheat oven at 350 degrees. Mix together flour, baking powder, salt in a large mixing bowl; set aside. Cream together butter and sugar. Add egg whites to wet mixture gradually while blending with mixer, blend in vanilla. Combine and stir-in well dry mixture into the wet mixture 1/2 cup at a time. Then gradually add milk, blend once more. Roll in chocolate chips and oreo bits.
Scoop 1/4 cup of mix into each foil bake for 20-25minutes.
