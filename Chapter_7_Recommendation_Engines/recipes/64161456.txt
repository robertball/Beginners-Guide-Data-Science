Title:
Bill's Tomato-Curry Lentil Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cup dry lentils
6 cups water
3 large, or 6 small cubes chicken bullion
15 ounces stewed tomatoes
1 medium onion, diced
4 stalks celery, diced, with leaves
1 teaspoon curry powder or to taste
9 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine lentils, chicken bullion and water, bring to a boil.
Lower heat to simmer, add tomatoes, onion, garlic, jalapeno and celery. Cover and let simmer 45 minutes. Check every 15 minutes to stir, and add water if necessary. Add curry last 15 minutes to taste. Taste and re-spice if necessary before serving.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
In this recipe serving 6, dried lentils are cooked with garlic and stewed tomatoes in curry seasoned chicken broth with onions and celery.Modified from: Tomato-Curry Lentil Stew
