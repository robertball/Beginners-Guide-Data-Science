Title:
Petite blanquette de poulet à l'estragon (Tarragon chicken) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/565274.jpg
Ingredients:
7 ounces white wine, 6 cups water, 1 clove of garlic, 1 branch of thyme, 1 onion with a clove in it, 1 branch of celery, 1 branch of parsley, Salt and pepper, 1 chicken (cut), 2 carrots, 2,5 ounces butter, A little flour, 4,5 ounces Paris mushrooms, 1 egg yolk, 1 branch tarragon, 1 lemon, 3 table spoons heavy cream

Directions:
1	Boil the wine and water with the onion, celery, thyme, parsley, garlic, salt and pepper.
2	Cut the chicken and peal and cut the carrots.
Add them to the boiling stock, cover and cook for 20 minutes.
Remove the chicken and filter the stock.
3	Melt the butter in a sauté pan and colour the chicken in it.
Then pour 30 cl of the stock, ladle by ladle. Cover and cook for 20 minutes.
4	Clean the mushrooms cut them in two and cook them in the melted butter for a few minutes. Add them to the chicken pan. Cook for 10 more minutes.
5	Remove the chicken and mushrooms from the pan and heat the content so it reduces.
Whisk in a bowl the egg yolk with the lemon juice and the heavy cream.
Shut down the burner and add the egg mix to the sauce, whisking. Add in the chopped tarragon and serve the chicken with the sauce and some rice.
