Title:
Homemade KFC Bowls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs instant potatoes (any brand)
2 cups frozen white corn
1 can beef gravy
1 can turkey gravy
2 lbs breaded chicken nuggets (breast meat)
Fiesta cheese mix (grated in pkg)

Directions:
cook nuggets in oven as package requires, mix potates as per package, boil corn and mix the gravies.
Layer mashed potates, corn, chicken, gravy and cheese in a bowl and dig in.

Submitter: 
Kim Casey Nugent Roberds
Description: 
If you like the KFC bowls you will love this quick meal.
