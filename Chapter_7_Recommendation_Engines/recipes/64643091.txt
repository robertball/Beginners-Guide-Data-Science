Title:
BROCCOLI AND CHEESE NUGGETS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4519226.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups of broccoli - 2 cups of cheddar cheese - 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs - 2 eggs

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together in a large bowl -  With your hands , form small balls patties and place on a parchemin paper on the baking pan -  Cook at 375F for 25 mins turning patties after the first 15 mins

Submitter: 
Noble Recipes
Description:

YOU CAN SERVE IT AS IS, BUT ACCOMPANIED BY A DIP IT IS EVEN TASTIER. HERE ARE SOME EXAMPLES OF DIP THAT GO VERY WELL WITH THESE BROCCOLI AND CHEESE NUGGETS.
