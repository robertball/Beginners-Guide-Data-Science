Title:
Turkey and Feta Grilled Sandwhich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1124489.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices of wheat bread
2 slices smoked turkey lunch meat
1 1/2 tablespoons feta cheese, crumbled
1 tablespoon Italian salad dressing
1 tablespoon butter
2 leaves of fresh lettuce

Directions:
Place the turkey, lettuce, and feta cheese onto one slice of bread. On the other slice, evenly spread out the Italian salad dressing and stick the slices together.
Melt your butter in a medium size skillet on medium heat and place sandwich in the middle. Cook on each side until bread is toasted, about 2 minutes per side.
Remove skillet from heat and enjoy your delicious sandwich!
