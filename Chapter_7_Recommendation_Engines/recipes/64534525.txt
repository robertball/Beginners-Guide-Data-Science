Title:
Gail's  Lo Mein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042694.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks marg.
1 cup raw minute rice
1 lb fine egg noodles ( no yoke)
2 envelopes onion soup mix
2 cups water
2 pkg frozen pea pods(thawed)
2 cans chicken broth
8 oz diced mushroom (drained)
2 cans water chestnuts(drained)

Directions:
Melt marg. in large pot. Brown noodles, stir often.
then add rice, broth, onion soup mix,  water, mushrooms
and water chestnuts.
cook cover on stove 5-10 mins.
Put into greased 9x13 glass casserole
Bake 350 for 25 mins
