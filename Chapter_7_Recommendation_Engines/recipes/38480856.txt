Title:
Heidi's Carrot Cake Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1 1/3 cups vegetable oil
3 extra large eggs
2 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
3 cups grated carrots
1 cup raisins
1 cup chopped walnuts
3/4 pound cream cheese at room temperature
1/2 pound unsalted butter at room temperature
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract,(for frosting)
1 pound confectioners sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees.
Beat the sugar, oil and 1 teaspoon of the vanilla together with an electric mixer.
Add the eggs one at a time.
In another bowl sift flour, cinnamon, salt and baking soda.
With the mixer on low speed, add 1/2 the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.
Add the grated carrots, raisins, walnuts to the remaining flour mixture and mix well; add to the batter, mix until well blended.
Line muffin pans with paper liners.
Place batter into muffin cups until each is 3/4 full.
Bake @ 400 degrees for 10 minutes, then lower to 350 degrees and bake for 35 mins or until toothpick comes out clean.
Cool on a rack.
For the frosting, cream the cream cheese, butter and vanilla in electric mixer.
Add the sugar and beat until smooth.
When muffins are cooled, frost....and eat!!!!!
