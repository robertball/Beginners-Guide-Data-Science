Title:
Better than ice cream BAKED BEANS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
64 oz. baked beans
16 oz. kidney beans - drained
16 oz. pinto beans - drained
16 oz. chili beans - drained
1 lb. bacon - diced & cooked
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. ketchup
1 tsp. worcestshire sauce
1/4 c. parmeasn cheese

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except parmesean cheese. Pour into large baking dish. Top with parmesan cheese. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes. Let stand 20 minutes before serving.
