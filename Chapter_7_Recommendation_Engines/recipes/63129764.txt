Title:
Microwave Savory Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/8 c chopped walnuts
1/4 c shredded mozzarella cheese
1 t milk
To taste:
Marjoram, liberally
Salt
Thyme, sparingly

Directions:
In a microwave-safe bowl, whisk the egg and milk.
Microwave for 30 seconds.
The egg should be somewhat solidified but not thoroughly.  Break up the pieces of solidified egg and whisk again, stirring in the walnuts, cheese, salt, and herbs.
Microwave for 30 seconds or until the egg is thoroughly cooked.

Description: 
A quick and protein-packed breakfast or lunch with a savory, meaty flavor.
