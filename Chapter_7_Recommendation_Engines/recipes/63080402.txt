Title:
Cabbage Roll Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pounds ground turkey
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 1/4 pounds chopped cabbage
1/2 cup uncooked brown rice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 (14 ounce) can chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large skillet, brown turkey in oil over medium high heat until thoroughly cooked. Drain off fat.
In a large mixing bowl combine the onion, tomato sauce, cabbage, rice and salt. Add meat and mix all together. Pour mixture into a 8x8 inch baking dish. Pour broth over meat mixture and bake in the preheated oven, covered, for 45 minutes. Stir, replace cover and bake for another 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jennifer C. Martin
Description: 
This casserole combines chopped cabbage with ground turkey, tomato sauce, and rice. Preparation is much simpler than for standard stuffed cabbage rolls.Modified from: Cabbage Roll Casserole
