Title:
Not Your Best Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz. can diced tomatoes and green chiles, such as Rotel
1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
1 4 oz. can mild fire roasted diced green chiles
1/4 roughly chopped red onion
1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Blend all of the ingredients in a food processor or blender until desired consistency is reached.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
This may not be the best salsa you've ever made but it sure works when you're in a bind for a quick, easy and flavorful salsa.
