Title:
3 Ingredient Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/909109.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cans of Peaches in Light Syrup
1 Stick of Butter
1 Box of Cake Mix (Yellow Cake Mix)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Pour the canned peaches in a baking dish.
Sprinkle cake mix on top of the peaches.
Melt stick of butter and pour over the cake mix.
Bake cobbler in preheated oven for 50 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Kristi Ford
Description: 
Yummy, peach cobbler that makes you feel as though you are in the south on a warm sunny day.  This recipe is inexpensive, easy, and suprisingly delicious!
