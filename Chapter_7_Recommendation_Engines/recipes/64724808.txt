Title:
Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6062966.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken cut up into bite size pieces
2 medium zuchinnis in half moon slices
1 medium onion coarsely chopped
1/2 bell pepper chopped
2 14oz cans of tomato sauce
1 jar of medium or mild salsa
1 bag frozen or fresh tortellini
2 cups Chicken Broth
1/8 c. cilantro, chopped.

Directions:
In a soup pot, place 2 TBS Olive oil and cook chicken, onion, and peppers
add tomato sauce, a jar of salsa, chicken broth, and zucchini and cilantro
simmer for 20 minutes
add tortellini
simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
serve in soup bowls garnish with cheddar cheese.
