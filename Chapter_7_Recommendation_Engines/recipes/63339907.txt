Title:
Ginger Miso Seafood Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C vegetable stock
2 C water
2 tbs fresh ginger root
1/3 C firm tofu
2 tbs miso
6 oz salmon (1 large piece)
12 leaves baby bok choy
2 sheets nori

Directions:
Pour the vegetable stock into a wok over medium high heat. Add the water too.
Slice the peeled ginger on a diagonal and add it to the wok.
Cut the tofu into 1 inch cubes and add to the wok.
Add the miso and stir to combine.
Slice the salmon into 1 inch long by 1/2 inch square strips and add this to the soup, stirring gently to incorporate.
Add the baby bok choy leaves, stir again, then ladle into bowls.
Top with crunched nori.

Submitter: 
Dragonfly
Description: 
The salmon chunks, large pieces of ginger, and the small leaves of baby bok choy, mix well with the tofu in this vegetable broth based soup.  Enough protein to eat as a main meal.
