Title:
Bar Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound box Velveeta cheese
1 (5-ounce) jar horseradish
9 tablespoons mayonnaise (or salad dressing)

Directions:
Melt cheese on very low.  Remove from heat, blend in horseradish and mayonnaise.  Stir until smooth.
Store in airtight containers.  Keeps for months in refrigerator.
Can be frozen and used later.  (I have never frozen it.)
Serve on crackers.

Submitter:

Description:
