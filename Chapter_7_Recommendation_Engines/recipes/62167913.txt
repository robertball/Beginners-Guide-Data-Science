Title:
Brockles Special Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/880806.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint jar of real Mayonnaise (not Miracle Whip)
4 Tbsp of drained sweet pickle relish
2 tsp of paprika, up to 2.5 tsp.
1.5 ounces of crumbled Blue Cheese
1 Tbsp of garlic powder

Directions:
Spoon Mayo into a mixing bowl.
Put the relish in a fine mesh strainer and press with paper towel to remove excess liquid.
Add ingredients, each with a bit of stirring.
Hand mix to an even color and texture.
Put back into cleaned mayonnaise jar (or container of your choice).
Keep refrigerated. Should keep for at least 3 weeks.

Submitter: 
Jimmy the Greek
Description: 
Salad Dressing and dip.  Adapted from our family's signature restaurant dressing popular in Dallas from the 1950's through the 70's. Great on wedge or tossed salads as well as a dip with various crackers.  Even good on burgers or meat sandwiches.
