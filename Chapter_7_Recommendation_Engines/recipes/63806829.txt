Title:
Aunt Kathy's Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
3 T oil
2 x 8 oz cans stewed tomatoes, choppedd
4 C chicken broth
2 C beef broth
1 T cumin
chili powder to taste
1 whole chicken, cooked, deboned, cubed or shredded
1 can chopped green chilis
10-12 corn tortillas, torn (can sub. with tortilla chips)
garnish :
Mexican cheese, grated
Sour cream
Salsa
Avocado, sliced

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients except tortillas and garnish. 2. Simmer at least 1 hour. Add tortillas just before serving. 3. Spoon into bowls and garnish.

Submitter: 
Melissa S.
Description: 
This is now a favourite in my husband's family in Canada after my Aunt sent it with me when I moved up from California.
