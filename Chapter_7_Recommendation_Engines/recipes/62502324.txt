Title:
Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6868463.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. shortening
2 c. brown sugar, firmly packed
2 eggs
1 t. vanilla
1 1/2 c. flour
1 t. salt
1 t. baking powder
1 t. baking soda
3 c. quick rolled oats
powdered sugar
whole pecan halves

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugar.
Add eggs and vanilla and cream until fluffy.
Add dry ingredients and mix.
Add oats.
Chill dough and form into balls.
Roll in powdered sugar and put on cookie sheet.
Press down with pecan on top.
bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes.
