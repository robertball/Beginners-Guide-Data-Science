Grilled Hot Italian Burger with Fresh Mozzarella Recipe

Directions:
Heat a skillet over medium high heat and coat with cooking spray. Pan grill hot Italian chicken patties for 5 minutes on both sides, to an internal temperature of 165 f.
To assemble: slice the deli roll in half, top with the grilled patty, slices of cheese and roasted red peppers. Serve immediately!
