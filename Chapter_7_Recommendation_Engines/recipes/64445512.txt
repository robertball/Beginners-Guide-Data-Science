Title:
Fresh Fig Oatmeal Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2373869.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup applesauce
3 eggs
1 3/4 cup fresh figs/ 1/2 pureed and 1/2 chopped
1 cup flour
1 1/4 cup oatmeal (old fashioned, not instant)
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 cup flax seed (milled)

Directions:
Beat sugar, eggs, applesauce until frothy.
Mix in figs, spices, baking soda, baking powder, flax seed.
Slowly add flour on low speed.
Fold in oatmeal.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
