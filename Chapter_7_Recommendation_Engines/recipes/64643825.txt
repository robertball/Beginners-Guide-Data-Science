Title:
Edemame Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:1.5 lb frozen shelled edamame3 cups frozen organic corn2 diced red bell peppers1 cup thin sliced green onion1 cup finely chopped red onion13 cup chopped fresh Italian parsley2 Tbsps fresh chopped basilDressing:1/2 cup fresh lemon juice3 Tbsps prepared dijon mustard3 Tbsps avocado, or olive oil1 tsp sea salt1 tsp fresh ground black pepper

Directions:
Prepare edamame to package directions. Drain thoroughly.Combine salad ingredients in a large glass bowl.In a separate bowl, whisk lemon juice, mustard, olive oil, salt and pepper. Add chopped veggies to dressing and toss to coat.Refrigerate, covered until ready to serve.
