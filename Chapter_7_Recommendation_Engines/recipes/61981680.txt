Title:
Crockpot Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups catsup
1 large green pepper, chopped
1/2 cup water
4 Tbsp. brown sugar
4 Tbsp. prepared mustard
4 Tbsp. vinegar(pref. apple cider)
4 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
3 tsp. chili powder

Directions:
Brown the ground beef, onion, and garlic; drain. In the crockpot, combine the catsup, green pepper, water, brown sugar, mustard, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, and chili powder.
Stir in meat mixture. Cover and cook on Low for 6-8 hours, or on High for 3-4 hours.  Spoon onto hamburger buns.
