Title:
Big Ray's Garbage Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4113792.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup melted butter
15 ounces can of sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup peanut butter chips
1/2 cup milk chocolate chips (or semi-sweet)
1/2 cup white chocolate chips
1/2 cup butterscotch chips
1 cup coconut flakes

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Melt butter in microwave or small saucepan over low heat. Sprinkle graham cracker crumbs over bottom of 9x13 pan then pour butter over crumbs. Flatten out crumbs with fork and make sure butter is evenly incorporated.
Evenly pour two cups of flavored chips over graham cracker crust.
Pour condensed milk over chips.
Cover with coconut flakes.
Bake 25 minutes. Edges will be brown and center should not jiggle.

Submitter:

Description: 
The reason I  call them “garbage bars” is because I simply emptied out my pantry to make them.  You can use  2 cups of whatever you have in your pantry: left over candy bars, oatmeal, frosted flakes or snack bars.
