Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs of beef chuck, cubed
1 can cream of mushroom soup
8 oz sliced button mushrooms
1 packet of onion soup mix
1 can milk
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 cup sour cream
1 lb egg noodles, prepare as directed

Directions:
In slow cooker combine cream of mushroom soup, milk, onion soup mix, salt and pepper.
Add in beef and sliced mushrooms.
Cook on low at least 6 hours, on high at least 3.
Cook noodles according to package directions.
30 min prior to serving add in sour cream.
The longer it cooks, the more tender the beef

Submitter: 
Amber Twillmann Friedli
Description: 
Cubed chuck beef, onions soup mix, milk, cream of mushroom soup and a slow cooker and you are set.
