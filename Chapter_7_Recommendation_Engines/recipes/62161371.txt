Title:
Strawberry-Orange Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Fresh Spinach (washed and dried)
2 cups Fresh Strawberries (sliced)
1 Navel Orange (peeled and diced)
1 Kiwi (diced)
1/2 cup Feta Cheese Crumbles
1/4 cup Pecan Halves

Directions:
Wash and dry the spinach.
Peel and dice orange over large bowl to retain any juice that is squeezed from the orange.
Prepare the strawberries and kiwi and place in bowl.
Toss in the spinach and feta.
Serve with a sprinkling of Pecan Halves.
(No dressing needed.)

A light refreshing summertime salad.
