Title:
Guy's Smoked Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bacon Grease
2 TBL Butter
3 Poblano chiles (roasted, peeled & chopped)
3 Anaheim chiles (roasted, peeled & chopped)
2 Cubanella peppers, diced
2-4 Jalapenos, minced
2 Yellow Onions, diced
1 Head garlic
1 pound Stew Meat, cut into 1/4" cubes
2 pounds Ground Beef, coarse grind
1 pound Breakfast sausage
3 TBL Chili powder
2 Tea Cayenne pepper
2 Tea ground coriander
2 Tea ground cumin
2 Tea granulated garlic
2 Tea granulated onion
2 Tea hot paprika
2 Tea kosher salt
2 Tea freshly cracked black pepper
2 Tea Ancho chili powder
2ea 16oz Can Tomato Sauce
1ea 32oz Can Crushed Tomato
1 Large Can Tomato Paste
1 Can Beer
16 oz Chicken Stock
4 Cans Beans, with juice

Directions:
1. Chop all veggies, put on a aluminum foil covered cookie sheet.  Mix with bacon grease and butter.
2.  Add stand to middle of veggie pan and add ground beef and sausage on top (formed into baseball size balls)
3.  Smoke tray for 1-2 hours on High Smoke at 225 degrees
4.  Remove veggies and meat from smoker and add to large soup pot.   Add remaining ingredients.
5.  Cook for 3-4 hours on medium heat, stirring frequently.
6.  Serve with your favorite side (cheese, crackers, onions, sour cream)
