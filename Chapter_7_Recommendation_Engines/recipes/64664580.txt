Title:
Connecticut Lobster Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4567266.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lobsters (~ 1 1/2  lbs each)
1/4 pound  butter melted
Juice of 1 lemon
salt to taste
4 split-top hot dog buns lightly toasted

Directions:
Fill large steamer pot with about 2 inches of water
Add salt
Bring to boil
Place lobsters in pot
Cover & steam until shells turn bright red (~8-10 minutes)
Remove the lobsters & rinse under cool water
Remove the meat from the shells & roughly chop
Melt butter with lemon juice
Remove from heat
Stir in the lobster meat
Season with salt
Divide among hot dog buns
Serve warm
