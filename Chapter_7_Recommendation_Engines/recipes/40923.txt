Title:
Chocolate Toffee Cream  Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985323.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) READY CRUST® Chocolate Pie Crust
4 (1.5 ounce) bars milk chocolate candy bars, chopped
3/4 cup heavy cream, divided
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup light brown sugar, packed
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1 cup chocolate covered toffee bits, divided
2 cups frozen non-dairy whipped topping

Directions:
Place chocolate in a small bowl, set aside. Microwave 1/2 cup cream for 1-1/2 minutes on High (100%). Pour hot cream over chocolate, let stand 5 minutes, then stir until smooth.
Using an electric mixer, beat cream cheese, brown sugar and vanilla in a medium bowl until smooth. Add remaining cream and gently beat in chocolate mixture.
Fold in 1/2 cup of chocolate covered toffee bits.  Pour the filling into crust. Spread 2 cups whipped topping over filling, top with remaining toffee bits, and place in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Ready Crust® Kitchens
Description: 
Pair milk chocolate and toffee with a Ready Crust® Chocolate Pie Crust and you have the perfect combination for your next holiday get-together!
