Title:
Southwest Style Turkey Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977696.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (7 ounce) can diced green chiles, drained
2 cups prepared salsa
1 cup sour cream
16 (6 inch) corn tortillas, cut into strips
12 ounces cooked turkey, shredded
12 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Combine the chicken soup, mushroom soup, chilies, salsa and sour cream in a bowl.
Line the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish with corn tortillas. Follow with a layer of turkey. Pour 1/2 of soup mixture over turkey, sprinkle 1/2 of the cheese. Repeat layers and top with Cheddar cheese.
Bake for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Pam Anderson
Description: 
Make the most out of your leftover turkey with this easy Southwest casserole.
