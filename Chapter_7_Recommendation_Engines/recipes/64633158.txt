Title:
Crock Pot BBQ Chuck Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 â€“ 2 1/2 lb. beef chuck roast
1/4 C. BBQ sauce
1/4 C. ketchup
2 T. light brown sugar
1/4 C. apple cider vinegar
1/4 tsp. dry mustard
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp. chili power

Directions:
1. Place the roast in the crock pot, slice in half if using a 4 qt. crock pot.
2. Whisk together the remaining ingredients and pour over the roast.
3. Cook on low for 6 hours.
4. Remove the roast and shred with two forks (it just falls apart), place in a serving dish and ladle some of the juice over the top before serving
