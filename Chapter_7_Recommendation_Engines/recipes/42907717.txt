Title:
Jello Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg lemon Jello
1 Cup Boiling Water
1 8oz Pkg cream cheese, softened
1 Cup Sugar
1 Can Evaporated Milk, Chilled
1 tsp Vanilla
CRUST
1/2 LB Graham Crackers, Rolled
1/4 LB margarine, softened
1/4 Cup sugar

Directions:
Mix Jello and Water chill until thick, like syrup
Mix Cheese and sugar together til creamy
Add Jello and whipped milk
Add Vanilla
Line bottom of pan with crust and press into bottom of pan
pour jello mixture over the top
Chill until set.

Submitter: 
Susan Murdoch
Description: 
This a very light dessert with the flavor of cheesecake
