Title:
Oreo Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
35 chocolate sandwich cookies
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
4 package (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
4 egs
20 chocolate sandwich cookies, chopped

Directions:
1.  Place 35 cookies in food processor container; cover.  Process ~30 seconds or until finely ground.  Add butter; mix well.  Press mixture into bottom of 13x9-inch baking pan to form crust.
2.  Mix cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla with electric mixer until well blended.  Add eggs; mix until blended.  Gently stir in 1 1/2 cups of the chopped cookies.  Pour mixture into crust.  Sprinkle with remaining chopped cookies.
3.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 40 minutes or until center is almost set.  Cool.  Refrigerate 3 hours or overnight.  Cut into squares.  Enjoy!
