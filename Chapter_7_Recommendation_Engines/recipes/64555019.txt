Title:
Blueberry Blanket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg refrigerated crescent rolls
4 oz cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoon sugar
1/2 cup blueberries
Dash of powder sugar

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and sugar together.
Open crescent rolls and seperate into 4 rectangles and pinch together seams so you have 4 complete rectangles.
Spread some cream cheese mixture on the rectangle (stay away from edges) and sprinkle on a few blueberries.
Bring the 4 corners up and over and pinch corners and seams together. Bake 11-13 minutes at 375 degrees.
