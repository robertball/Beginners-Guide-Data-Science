Title:
Quick Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups macaroni
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1 large tomato chopped
1 med red pepper chopped
1/2 cup cheddar cheese cubed
1 tsp basil
1 cup salad dressing

Directions:
Boil the macaroni, drain and rinse.
Chop the rest of the ingredients and put in a bowl with basil and salad dressing.
Add cooled macaroni and stir.

Submitter:

Description: 
Fast, but delicious macaroni salad that will keep them coming back for more!
