Title:
Deli Skillet Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices of bread
4 oz. cooked ham, thinly sliced
4 oz. provolone cheese, thinly sliced
4 oz. cooked turkey, thinly sliced
1/2 c. milk
2 eggs

Directions:
On each of the 4 slices of bread, layer ham, cheese, and turkey.
Top with remaining bread and press lightly.
Cut each sandwich in half diagonally; set aside.
In a 9" pie plate, beat together milk and eggs.
Dip each sandwich into egg mixture, coating both sides.
Cook in a preheated greased skillet over medium heat until browned, turning once.
Transfer to a baking sheet.
Bake at 400 degrees for 3-5 minutes or until cheese melts and sandwiches are heated through.

Submitter:

Description: 
Your average sandwich but with that delicious skillet taste!
