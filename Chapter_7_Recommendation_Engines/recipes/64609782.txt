Title:
Big Ray's Great Pumpkin Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4133662.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (15 ounces) solid-pack pumpkin
1 can (12 ounces) evaporated milk
3 large eggs
1/2 cup sugar
4 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1 package yellow cake mix (regular size)
1/2 cup butter, melted
1-1/2 cups chopped walnuts
Vanilla ice cream

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat first five ingredients until smooth.
Transfer to a greased 13' x 9" baking dish. Sprinkle with cake mix and drizzle with butter. Top with walnuts.
Bake at 350Â° for 1 hour or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Serve warm with ice cream.

Description: 
Canned pumpkin and yellow cake mix make this dessert an effortless alternative to pumpkin pie. What a great way to welcome autumn.
