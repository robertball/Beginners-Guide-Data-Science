Title:
Filet Mignon with Rich Balsamic Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (4 ounce) filet mignon steaks
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper to taste
salt to taste
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp brown sugar
3 tbsp butter
Gorgonzola cheese
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup dry red wine

Directions:
Sprinkle freshly ground pepper over both sides of each steak, and sprinkle with salt to taste.
Heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat add olive oil.  Place steaks in hot pan, and braise or until browned. Set aside to rest.  Reduce heat to medium-low, and add garlic, butter, brown sugar, balsamic vinegar and red wine. Reduce until thick.
Heat oven broiler to low, and top steaks with cheese.  Broil until cheese is melted.
Remove steaks to two warmed plates, spoon one tablespoon of glaze over each, and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Deanna Latrice Payne-Schmidt
