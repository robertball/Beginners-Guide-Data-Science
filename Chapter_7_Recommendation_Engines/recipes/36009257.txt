Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
6 TBS sugar
2-1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
3/4 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
2 TBS reduced-calorie margerine, melted
1/4 cup egg substitute
2 TBS lemon juice
1 tsp vanilla extract
1-1/2 cup zucchini, uncooked, shredded and packed coarsley
2 tsp lemon zest, finely grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350, coat an 8" loaf pan with cooking spray.
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, salt and nutmeg; mix well with a fork, make a well in the center and set aside.
In a medium bowl, whisk together butermilk, brown sugar, margerine, egg, lemon juice and extract; stir in zucchini and lemon zest.  Add mixture to dry ingredients and mix until just blended.
Pour batter into prepared pan and bake until a wooden pick inserted near the center comes our clean, about 50 minutes.  Cool in pan on wire rack for 10 minutes.  Remove bread from pan and cool completely.
