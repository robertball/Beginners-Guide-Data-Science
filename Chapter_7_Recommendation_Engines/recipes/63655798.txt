Title:
Quick & Easy BBQ Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Quick & Easy BBQ Cups:
Ingredients:
•1 lb. lean ground turkey
•1/2 - 1 cup BBQ sauce (depending on how much BBQ sauce you like!)
•1/4 tsp. garlic powder
•3/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
•1 (12-oz) can Buttermilk biscuits (I used Grands Junior)

Directions:
Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Brown ground turkey in skillet. Add BBQ sauce and garlic powder. Set aside.
2.Separate biscuits and place in muffin tins, pressing dough up sides to edge of cups. Fill dough with cooked turkey mixture. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden brown. Serve and enjoy!!
