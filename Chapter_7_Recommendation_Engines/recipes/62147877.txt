Title:
Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Mangos, peeled and chopped
1 small can pineapple tidbits, drained
1/4 bunch cilantro, stems removed & chopped
2 Tablespoons fresh ginger, peeled & chopped
1/2 of a small purple onion chopped
1/4 cup rice vinegar
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste

Directions:
Prepare ingredients and mix together. Enjoy! This salsa has a lot of color and looks great with a mix of red, yellow, and blue tortilla chips.
Tip: do not use a food processor or salsa will be too mushy.

Submitter: 
KirklandCook
Description: 
This salsa is very simple, with few ingredients, but has so much flavor! Sweet but spicy, it goes well chicken, pork or just corn chips.
