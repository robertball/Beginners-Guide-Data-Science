Title:
Swiss Cheese Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices bacon, fried crisp and crumbled
2 cups soft white bread cubes with no crusts
1 3/4 cup milk
8 slightly beaten eggs
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8  teaspoon pepper
2 tblsp. butter
1/4 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/2 lb Swiss cheese, sliced
2 tblsp. melted butter
1/2 cup dried bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400, when ready to cook
Combine bread cubes and milk; drain after 5 minutes
Combine milk with beaten eggs, salt/pepper
Scramble the eggs in butter until soft, but not fully cooked
Add soaked bread cubes and put into a 9" square baking dish
Sprinkle with seasoned salt; arrange Swiss cheese slices over the top
Top with crumbled bacon
Combine melted butter and bread crumbs and sprinkle over cheese and bacon
Bake for 10-15 minutes (longer if it is still cold), until cheese in center is melted
Serve immediately
