Title:
Guacomole Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ripe pitted avocados, mashed
1-10oz can "Rotel Mexican Diced Tomatoes with Lime Juice & Cilantro", drained, liquid reserved
1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
1 teaspoon fresh lime juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 to 1 whole fresh Jalapeno pepper, optional, to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large glass bowl.  Add reserved Rotel liquid until dip is desired consistency.
Serve with tortilla chips.

Guacomole dip made from fresh avocados
