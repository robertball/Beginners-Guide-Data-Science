Title:
Steak Gorgonzola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3518808.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1 cup heavy cream
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1.5 oz Gorgonzola cheese
4 oz Sun-dried tomatoes chopped
1/2 cup rosted red peppers (sliced)
16 oz penne pasta
16 oz Ribeye steak (cooked to desired doneness)

Directions:
Cook steak to desired doneness. Let rest. Slice
Cook pasta according to directions. Drain.
Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium low heat. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes, then add garlic and cheeses and whisk quickly, heating through. Stir in parsley, red peppers, sun-dried tomatoes.
Combine all ingredients.
