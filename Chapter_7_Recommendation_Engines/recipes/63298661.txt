Title:
AUG - Ten Midori Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
Makes: 1 drink
11/4 ounces gin, Tanqueray® No. TEN®
1 ounce lime juice
1 splash melon liqueur
1 cup ice cubes
Maraschino cherry

Directions:
Directions
In a blender, combine gin, lime juice, and melon liqueur. Add ice. Cover and blend until slushy. Pour into a chilled glass. Garnish with cherry.
