Title:
Brussel sprouts with bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds Brussel sprouts
2 thick slices of bacon
1/2 small yellow onion chopped finely

Directions:
1. Cook bacon in a frying pan until crisp. Remove from pan. Leave bacon grease in pan.
2.Trim the end off the brussel sprouts and remove any outer leaves that are yellowed/damaged.
3. Steam brussel sprouts for 6-8 minutes.
4. While the sprouts are steaming, saute the onions and crumble the bacon.
5. add steamed sprouts and bacon to the pan. heat for 1-2 minutes and serve.

Description: 
Brussel sprouts tossed with crumbled bacon and sauteed onions.
