Title:
Green Apple Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/423578.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce vodka
2 fluid ounces melon liqueur
1 fluid ounce sweet and sour mix
1 maraschino cherry

Directions:
In a cocktail mixer full of ice, combine vodka, melon liqueur and sweet and sour mix. Shake vigorously and strain into glass. Garnish with a cherry.

Submitter: 
LSHERIDAN
Description: 
Not to be confused with the Big Apple Martini, this one tastes just like a green apple Jolly Rancher candy!
