Title:
Mustard dipping sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/397459.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons prepared horseradish
1/2 cup sour cream
1 dash Worcestershire sauce
3 tablespoons prepared mustard

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl and refrigerate until you're ready to use
