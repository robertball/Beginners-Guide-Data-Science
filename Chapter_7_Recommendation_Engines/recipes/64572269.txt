Title:
Big Ray's Lemon Roasted Tuscan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3594313.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 tsp Italian seasoning
8 chicken thighs
4 Tbsp olive oil, divided
3 lb. Yukon gold potatoes (about 6 medium), cut into 1-1/2-inch chunks
1 large onion, cut into wedges
1 Tbsp tomato paste
1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 lemon, sliced

Directions:
Heat oven to 400ºF.
Mix cheese and seasoning. Brush chicken with 2 tsp oil; rub with cheese mixture.
Heat large oven proof skillet to medium heat.  Cook potatoes and onions in remaining oil on medium heat 5 to 10 min. or until lightly browned, stirring occasionally.  Add  tomato paste, vinegar and broth; stir. Bring to boil, stirring occasionally. Add lemon slices; top with chicken.
Bake 50 min. or until chicken is done (165ºF).
