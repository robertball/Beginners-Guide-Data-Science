Title:
Honey Pinapple Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast
2 tbls vegetable oil
1 (8oz) can unsweetened crushed pinapple, drained
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup honey
1/3 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup butter, melted
2 tbls prepared mustard
2 teas. soy sauce

Directions:
Put chicken in slow cooker.  Combine the remaining ingredients; pour over chicken.  Cover and cook on low for 8 hours.
