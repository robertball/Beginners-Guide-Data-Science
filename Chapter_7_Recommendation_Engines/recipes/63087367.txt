Title:
Southwestern Chicken Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless chicken breasted cube
1 tblsp canola oil
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 jalapeño pepper seeded and finely chopped
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 cans 14 1/2 oz reduced sodium chicken broth
3 cups fresh or frozen corn
1 can 15 1/2 oz black beans rinsed and drained
2 tblsp lime juice
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp hot pepper sauce
1/2 cup fresh cilantro
16 baked tortilla chips crumbled
1/2 cup shredded reduced fat cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large pot saute chicken in oil until no longer pink. Remove with slotted spoon and set side. In the same pan saute onion, and jalapeño pepper until tender; add garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
Stir in broth, corn, beans, lime juice,salt, hot pepper sauce, pepper and chicken; bring to boil. Reduce heat, simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes. Stir in cilantro. Top each serving with crumbled tortilla chips and cheese.
