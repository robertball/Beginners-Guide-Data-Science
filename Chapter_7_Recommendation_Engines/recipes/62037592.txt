Title:
Heavenly Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 each medium potatoes -- peeled and cubed
1/2 cup cream cheese -- softened,lowfat okay
1/2 cup sour cream -- lowfat okay
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese -- lowfat okay
2 teaspoons salt
1 Tablespoon onion -- minced
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1/4 teaspoon parsley, freeze-dried
1/4 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
-Boil potatoes until soft.
-Beat together all ingredients, except for parsley and paprika.
-Place in greased casserole dish.
-Sprinkle parsley and paprika on potato mixture.
-Refrigerate at least 1 hour, or overnight (preferred ).
-Bake in 400ยบ oven for 35 minutes.
