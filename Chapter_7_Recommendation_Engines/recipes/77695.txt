Title:
Dirty Bedtime Story Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/139134.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce amaretto liqueur
1 fluid ounce Irish cream liqueur
1/2 cup milk
2 teaspoons chocolate syrup

Directions:
In a tall thin glass, stir together the amaretto, Irish cream and milk. Mix in chocolate syrup, and serve.

Submitter: 
galatea
Description: 
Like a chocolate milk for adults. Light on the chocolate with a strong alcoholic kick, but very little bite.
