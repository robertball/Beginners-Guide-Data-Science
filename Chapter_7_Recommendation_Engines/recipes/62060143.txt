Title:
Lemonade Shake Up Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Juice of 1 lemon
1/2 cup sugar (more or less depending on taste)
ice water

Directions:
Fill a 24 ounce glass,with a tight fitting lid, 2/3 full of ice. Put the sugar in. Pour in the lemon juice. Fill the cup with water. Cover the glass and shake. Put the squeezed lemon halfs in the glass for an authentic lemonade shake-up.
ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Just me
Description: 
This is like the carnival/fair shake ups you get n the summer. Its very good and takes just a minute to make. No need to pay high carnival prices again. Make them at home!!!
