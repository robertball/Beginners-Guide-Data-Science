Title:
Easy-to-Make Apple Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3210647.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter (optional)
2 slices apple
2 tablespoons granola, or more to taste

Directions:
Spread peanut butter over one side of one apple slice; top with granola. Place remaining apple slice atop the granola to finish the sandwich.
