Title:
Marshmallow Cake Filling from ToH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups miniature marshmallows
1 1/2 tbsp butter
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
3 oz cream cheese, softened

Directions:
In large saucepan, combine marshmallows and butter. Cook and stir over medium-low heat until melted. Remove from heat and transfer to a bowl.
Add cream and 3 oz cream cheese; beat until smooth.
Store in refrigerator.
