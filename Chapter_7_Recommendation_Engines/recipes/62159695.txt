Title:
Ally's Barbeque Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless Pork Chops
Pork Rub Seasoning (to taste)
3/4 cup barbeque sauce
1/2 cup chili sauce
1/2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
2 teaspoons chili powder

Directions:
1) Preheat grill to medium heat.
2) Take pork rub seasoning and rub generously to cover all sides of the pork chops.
3) Mix barbeque sauce, chili sauce, worcestershire, lemon juice, and chili powder in a small bowl.
4) Coat pork chops with sauce mixture and place on grill.
5) Cook until pork reaches a temp. of about 155, try to turn only once.
