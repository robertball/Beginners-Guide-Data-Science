Title:
Macaroni and Sausage Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1106486.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Bratwurst sausages, cooked and sliced
1 1/2 C. Dry macaroni noodles
1/2 C. Cream cheese, softened
1/2 C. Cheddar cheese, grated
2 Eggs, beaten
1 C. All purpose flour
1 C. Milk
1 tsp. Dry mustard
1 tsp. Dry ginger
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/4 tsp. Cayenne
1/2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Slice cooked sausages crosswise and set aside
Cook macaroni according to package directions and then pour macaroni into a colander and allow to cool
In a large bowl add softened cream cheese and grated cheddar cheese; mix together, add cooled macaroni and beaten eggs  to creamed cheese sauce  and combine well.
In a small bowl combine flour, b. powder, b. soda, salt, mustard, ginger and cayenne and blend all ingredients together.
Add milk to flour mixture and stir together to make a light batter.
Pour batter over sausages in loaf pan and spread macaroni mixture on top of white sauce and pat down well in loaf pan
Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes
Allow for loaf to cool and then loosen from around the edges of loaf pan and place on a serving plate.  Slice for dinner with your favourite vegetable

A very cheesy dish with a spicy flavour that would go well with a vegetable accompaniment for dinner
