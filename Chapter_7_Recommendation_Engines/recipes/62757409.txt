Title:
Stove Top Easy Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup hot water
1 pkg stuffing mix for chicken
1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
1/3 cup sour cream
3 cups frozen vegetables, thawed, drained

Directions:
Heat oven to 400F.
Add hot water to stuffing mix; stir until just moistened. Set aside.
Mix chicken, soup, sour cream and vegetables in 13x9-inch baking dish; top with stuffing.
Bake 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
