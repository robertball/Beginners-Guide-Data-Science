Title:
Grandma's Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. sliced Granny Smith apples (4-6 medium apples)
lemon juice
2/3 c. flour
2 c. old-fashioned oats
1 c. brown sugar
4 t. cinnamon
2/3 c. melted butter

Directions:
Peel and slice apples.
Preheat oven to 375.
Place apples in greased shallow baking dish. Sprinkle with lemon juice to keep apples from turning brown.
Combine dry ingredients; add melted butter. Mix until crumbly.
Sprinkle crumb mixture on top of apples.
Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes or until apples are tender.

Submitter: 
jandjwhite
Description: 
I learned from my grandma (the hard way) that for the best crumbly topping use real butter and not margarine.
