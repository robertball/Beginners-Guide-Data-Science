Title:
Mushroom Brie Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 large mushrooms
8 ounces Brie cheese
4 tablespoons butter
1/3 cup sherry
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 cup chopped fresh flat-leave (Italian) parlsey
1/2 cup crumbled bacon pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Carefully pull stem from each mushroom and set aside.
Clean the mushrooms and stems with a mushroom brush or clean, dry dish towel.
Trim the outer rind from the brie cheese and discard.
Slice brie into bite size pieces.
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in microwave safe dish.
Dip each mushroom cap in melted butter to cover completely.
Place mushroom caps on a baking sheet and place slices of brie cheese inside each mushroom cap.
Chop mushroom stems.
Heat remaining 2 tbls of butter on medium-high heat.
Add mushroom stems, sherry and garlic.
Briefly saute, do not allow liquid to completely evaporate.
Remove from heat, stir in parsley and bacon pieces.
spoon mushroom mixture on top of brie slices in each mushroom cap.
Bake for 10-15 minutes until mushrooms are soft.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
kimberly
Description: 
The soft, gooey, creamy texture of the warmed brie oozes decadence and elegance.  

This simple but elegant dish is sure to impress your friends and family. Perfect as an appetizer or a first course.
