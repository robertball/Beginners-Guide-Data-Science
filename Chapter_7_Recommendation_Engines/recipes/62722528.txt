Title:
No Garlic Red Pepper Hummas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can chickpeas
1 large lemon
1/4 c sesame seeds (optional)
3 T olive oil
1/2 large onion (or 1 medium onion)
1/2 large red bell pepper (or 1 whole small pepper)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain and rinse chickpeas
Chop onion and red bell pepper
In blender (or stick blender) container place the chickpeas, olive oil, sesame seeds, and lemon juice.
Option A: fill a covered pan with 1/2" water, add the onion and pepper, bring to boil, and cook covered for 2 to 3 minutes.
Option B: Use the onion and pepper raw. This will provide a more robust flavor.
Place (drained) onion and pepper in blender.
Blend to smooth consistency
Add salt, pepper, and/or additional oil to taste.
