Title:
Greek-style Coconut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the cake:
6 eggs
1 1/2 cups white sugar
250 gr. butter
2 cups coconut sprinkles
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 cups self-raising flour
vanilla extract
For the syrup:
3 glasses water
3 glasses suga
2 tablespoons lemon juice

Directions:
Beat the eggs with the sugar and add the melted butter.  Mix the flour with the coconut sprinkles, the vanilla extract and the baking powder and thenh gradually add to the egg mixture.  Oil a baking form and pour the mixture in the form carefully.  Bake for about 45 minutes in a pre-heated oven at 175 degrees.  In the meantime prepare the syrup by bringing to boil 3 glasses of water with 3 glasses of sugar and some lemon juice.  Once the cake is ready allow to cool and then pour the cool syrup on top of it.  Leave overnight.  The next day turn it upside down on a plate and sprinkle some icing sugar on top.  You can serve it with whipped cream or vanilla ice-cream.

Submitter: 
GATOULA
Description: 
This is a typical greek-styled cake with syrup only in this case I added coconut.  The result is simply delicious!!
