Title:
Cajun Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2020223.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb peeled and deveined shrimp
1 tsp. Fresh garlic, minced
1/2 stick butter
½ tsp. Worcestershire Sauce
1 rounded tsp. Cajun seasoning
1 pinch Parsley, chopped
Lemon wedges

Directions:
Preparation
1.	In sauté pan, melt butter, Sautee garlic for 30 seconds
2.	Add shrimp, Cajun seasoning, Worcestershire sauce
3.	Over medium heat, cook shrimp to medium, stirring often. Do not overcook.
4.	Garlic Bread- spread with butter and garlic until fully coated
5.	Cook garlic bread until golden brown and cut diagonally into 2 pieces. Place in serving platter on each side.
6.	Pour shrimp into center of serving dish, garnishing with chopped parsley. Place a lemon wedge on top of shrimp.
