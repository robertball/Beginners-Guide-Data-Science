Title:
Jill's Shrimp & Linguiça Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 red onions
3 stalks of celery
1 large jalapeno
green onions
1/2 lb of Linguiça sausage cut into 1/4 inch slices
1/4 lb of bacon, chopped
1 cup of parsley
1 bunch p/person shrimp ( I used 1lb of 31/40 raw w/tail)
1 can of diced tomatoes
1 box of Zatarain's gumbo mix with rice (if you don't used the box add:
- 2/3 cup of all-purpose flour and 2/3 C butter to make a roux.
- salt to taste
- black pepper to taste
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp dried Thyme
- 2 bay leaves

Directions:
Saute the bacon till crispy
remove the bacon and add the slices of sausage to the pan.
Saute the sausage sliced until the edges become crispy.rRemove and drain on a paper towel.
Add the onion, celery and jalapeno to the pan. Stir for 5 minutes.
Add the Parsley.
Return the bacon and sausage.
Add 3 cups of water, the can of diced tomatoes and the box of gumbo mix with rice to the pan.
Bring to a boil, turn down to a simmer and put the lid on for 15 minutes.
Add the Shrimp and cover for another 10 minutes.
Spoon into a bowl and top with green onions.
Enjoy!
