Title:
Fiesta Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1/3 cup milk
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
1/4 tsp. cumin
dash of hot pepper sauce
1 (10 oz.) can cream of chicken soup
1 (10 oz.) pkg. frozen spinach, thawed and squeezed of liquid
1 (4 oz.) can diced green chilies,
drained
2 tbsps. chopped pimento
2-3 cups cubed cooked chicken
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
Topping:
2 eggs separated
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup butter or margarine
paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375*F.  In a large bowl combine all ingredients with the exception of the chicken and cheese. Spoon 1/2 of the spinach mixture into bottom of a lightly greased casserole. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the chicken and cheese mixture. Repeat layers. For topping, beat egg whites until stiff. Set aside. In a separate bowl combine, flour, baking powder, milk, butter and egg yolks. Beat 4 minutes at high speed. Fold in egg whites and our over casserole. Sprinkle with paprika. Bake 40-45 minutes or until golden brown.
