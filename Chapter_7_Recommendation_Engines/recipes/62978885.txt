Title:
Cherry Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp Vanilla
1 Cup powdered Sugar
18oz Cream Cheese
1 pint Whipping cream
17.5oz Cherries with syrup
1 cooked pie crust/tin

Directions:
Cream vanilla, sugar, and cream cheese together
Then fold in whipping cream.
Empyt onto baked but cold pie crust. Spread cherries on top.
