Title:
Southern Style Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 center cut (bone-in) Pork Loin Chops
2 tblsp oil
1/2 onion sliced in rings and separated
1 Jar mushroom caps or pieces
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can golden mushroom soup
1 soup can filled with Chablis wine or other white wine

Directions:
Add oil to large skillet with lid
Brown Chops well (browner the better)
Near end of browning add onions and drained mushrooms
Saute until onions are limp/browned
Drain excess oil
Blend soups and wine together in bowl and pour over chops (in Skillet)
Cover and simmer over lowest heat between 1 and 1 1/2 hours
Stir occasionally
*Pork chops are done when meat falls off the bone!
*Serve over mashed potatoes or egg noodles
