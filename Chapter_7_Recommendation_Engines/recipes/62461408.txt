Title:
Apple Crazy Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 c. of sliced apples
3 c. sugar
2 tsp. soda
2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
3 c. flour
1 tsp. salt
3 eggs

Directions:
350 degees. bake 60 min. bundt pan; mix all ingredients in order given. Add vanilla ice cream when done.

Submitter: 
jessie
Description: 
This is not a beautiful cake.  It is absolutely delicious and very moist.
