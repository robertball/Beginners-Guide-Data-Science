Title:
EASY Brazilian Passion Fruit Mousse (Musse de Maracuja) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of sweetened condensed milk
1 can of passion fruit pulp
1 can of table cream (creme de leite)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a blender
Pour in serving dish
Refrigerate for 4 hours prior to serving

Submitter: 
Bruna Miller
Description: 
A little tart and absolutely delicious!
