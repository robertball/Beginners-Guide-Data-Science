Title:
Christmas Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986518.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
2 green onions, chopped
1 (2 ounce) jar diced pimientos, drained
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
Assorted crackers

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy.  Beat in cheddar cheese, onions, pimientos, butter and Worcestershire sauce.  Press into a small bowl; smooth top.  Cover and refrigerate.  Remove from the refrigerator 15 minutes before unmolding.  Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Sundra  Hauck
Description: 
'This rich creamy spread looks so festive with flecks of green onion and red pimiento dotting with cheddar cheese,' relates field editor Sundra Hauck of Bogalusa, Louisiana. 'It's a crowd-pleaser at any party.'
