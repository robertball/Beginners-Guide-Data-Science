Title:
Jill Hedman's Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb steak in strips
1/4 flour
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup diced onion
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/4 cup water
1 cup sour cream
Package Noodles

Directions:
Roll steak mixture in flour, salt and pepper.
Brown in butter.
Add onions, soup and water.
Simmer for 1 hour.
Add sour cream and serve over noodles.
