Title:
Steak Marinade - Flat Iron Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. Soy Sauce
1/4 c. teryaki sauce
1 tbsp minced FRESH garlic
1 medium yellow onion chopped small
1/4 tsp ginger root powder or 1/2 tsp fresh minced ginger root
1 jalapeno minced (seeded & deveined)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.
Let marinate into itsself over night.
In large ziploc or sealed bowl, add marinade then the meat of choice (Flat Iron, Sirloin, T-Bone, London Broil, etc. , virtually ANY beef or poultry). Marinate overnight for a good bathing!
Then take the meat to the grill!

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
Another throw together recipe! Just picked stuff out of the spice rack, and BINGO! We have marinade! Pretty darn delicious!
