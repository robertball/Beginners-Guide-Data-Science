Title:
Hot chicken enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bonless, skinless chicken breasts (cooked and shredded)
6 soft taco shells
8oz sour cream
diced onions-to taste
taco seasoning mix-to taste
green salsa-to taste
asparagas(pre cooked it alittle)

Directions:
Make the sauce with the sour cream and taco mix, onions and green salsa. Mix and add to taste. Set 1/2 of the sauce aside. To the other half of sauce add the chicken. On each soft shell, set 2 spears of asparagas, cover with chicken mixture and roll up. Place in coverd baking dish and cover with the remaining sauce and shredded cheese if desired. Bake at 350 for 20min.
