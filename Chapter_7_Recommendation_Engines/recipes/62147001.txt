Title:
Rainbow Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon rainbow sherbet
1 can frozen orange pineapple juice concentrate
2 liter bottle of lemon lime soda pop

Directions:
In a punch bowl dissolve frozen juice concentrate in lemon lime soda.
With medium ice cream scooper, scoop balls of rainbow sherbet into juice and soda mixture. Stir slightly.
Serve in punch cups being sure each cup has a portion of rainbow sherbet floating in it.

Submitter: 
Michelle Blum
Description: 
Easy delicious punch that is difficult to keep in the punch bowl for very long!
