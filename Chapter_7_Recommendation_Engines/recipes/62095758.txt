Title:
Old Fashion Fruit Salad from the 1960's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Sugar
1/2 Cup Water
1 1/2 tsp. Corn Startch
1/2 tsp. butter
1 1/2 tsp. white vinegar
1 tsp. Good quality pure vanilla
( you can double this recipe if you prefer more sauce  or if you have more fruit to use )
1 tsp Lemon juice
An assortment of bit sized pieces of:
apples, oranges, bananas, cherries, strawberries, raspberries, peaches, nuts etc.

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine the sugar and corn startch until blended then add the remaining ingredients and bring to a boil, stirring frequently.  Simmer for about 5  minutes until thicken and transparent in appearance. Cool to room temperature then pour over mixed fruit. Chill or serve at room temperature. Use mint leaves to garnish.
Try adding some grated lemon peel/ orange peel and juice in place of vanilla.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
A very tastey way to serve summer fruit during the season. Add a scoop of your favorite sherbert, Italian Ice, or low fat ice cream for a quick  light dessert. Peaches are fantastic in this fruit salad/dessert.
