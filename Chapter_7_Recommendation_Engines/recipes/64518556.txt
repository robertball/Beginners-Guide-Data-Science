Title:
Big Ray's Pumpkin Butter Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2862292.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
8 Tbsp (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted and cooled slightly
1 egg
(8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin
8 Tbsp (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted and cooled slightly
1 tsp vanilla
3 eggs
1 (16 ounce) box or bag powdered sugar
2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
Caramel sauce and candied pecans, for topping

Directions:
Heat oven to 350Â°F.  Grease a 13 x 9-inch pan (or line bottom with parchment paper).
In a large bowl or bowl of a stand mixer, beat cake mix, melted butter and egg until well combined. Spread and press batter evenly into bottom of prepared pan.
Filling: In another large bowl (or clean bowl of a stand mixer), beat cream cheese and pumpkin until well combined. Add melted butter, vanilla and eggs; stir until combined. Stir in powdered sugar and pumpkin pie spice until just combined. Pour batter evenly into pan over cake base.
Bake 1 hour, until center of cake is just set and slightly wobbly. Cool completely.
Serve slices of cake with caramel sauce and candied pecans on top.
