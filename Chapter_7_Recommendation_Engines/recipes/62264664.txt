Title:
White Cheddar Bacon Rosemary Biscotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/340578.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup shredded white cheddar cheese
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 large eggs
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1 Tbsp. dried rosemary
8 strips of bacon

Directions:
In a medium bowl mix flour, salt and baking powder.
In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, oil, cheese and rosemary.
Stir in flour mixture until just combined, then stir in bacon.
Shape dough on baking into a 12-inch-long log.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until golden and firm in center.
Remove log from oven.
Cool log about 15 minutes; reduce oven temperature to 300*.
Slice log crosswise diagonally into 3/8-inch-thick pieces.
Arrange slices on sheet pan and return to oven for 10 minutes or until crisp.
Cool completely and store in an airtight container.
