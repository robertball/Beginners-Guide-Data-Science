Title:
4 Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 egg

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in large bowl.
Flatten with fork marks.
Bake at 350 F. for 10 minutes.
