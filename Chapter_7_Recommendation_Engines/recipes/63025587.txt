Title:
Bountiful Basket Persimmon Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c sugar
1 c oil
4 eggs
2/3 c water
2 c persimmon puree
3 1/2 c flour
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nut meg

Directions:
Mix egg, oil and sugar. Add other ingredients and mix. Fold into greased and floured loaf pans. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes. If using mini loafs, back 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
We got a dozen persimmons in our Bountiful Basket. I combined recipes to create this one.
