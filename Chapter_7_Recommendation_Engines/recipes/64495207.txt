Title:
Spicy Sambal Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of frozen shrimp (large), thawed and de-tailed, if preferred
1/2 cup of sambal oelek (chili sauce)
3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
3/4 cup of chopped onions
1 stick of butter
Juice of 1/2 of a lemon
1/2 bunch of green onions, chopped
3 cups of sushi rice

Directions:
Mince garlic and chop onions while you thaw the shrimp in a bowl of cold water for 10-15 minutes.
Melt butter and mix in garlic and half the lemon juice in the microwave.
Saute onions in a pat of butter until transparent.
Drop garlic butter, shrimp and the sambal oelek in the hot pan with onions.
Cook on medium until garlic is cooked and shrimp are hot (approx. 7-8 minutes max)
Serve shrimp with sauce over steamed rice, finish with lemon juice and green onions.

A spicy pan-seared shrimp dish flavored with sambal oelek.
