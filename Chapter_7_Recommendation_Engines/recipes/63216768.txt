Title:
Vegetable Beef Soup with Barley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs. stew meat, chopped into small pieces
1 C. barley
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp. carrabbas seasoning
1/4 tsp. tyme
1/4 tsp basil
2 C. baby carrots, chopped
2 C. green beans, chopped
1 onion, chopped
3 potatoes, peeled and cubed
4 cups water
1 quart beef stock
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 can petit diced tomatoes
1/3 C. tomato paste
1 tsp. salt
additional salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except barley in slow-cooker. Cook all day on low or 6 hours on high. Add barley last hour and a half of cooking.
