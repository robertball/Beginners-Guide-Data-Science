Title:
Broccoli Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chopped fresh broccoli
1 1/2 cups cubed, cooked chicken meat
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of broccoli soup
1/3 cup milk
1 medium onion chopped and sauteed in butter and olive oil
1 garlic clove minced and cooked with the onion
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 tablespoon butter, melted
1/2 cup garlic butter crutons crushed in food processor
1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Place the broccoli in a saucepan with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, and cook 5 minutes, or until tender. Drain.
Place the cooked broccoli in a 9 inch square pan. Top with the chicken. In a bowl, mix the soup and milk,onion and garlic and pour over the chicken. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese. Mix the melted butter with the crushed crutons,and parmesan cheese and sprinkle over the cheese.
Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until bubbly and lightly brown.
