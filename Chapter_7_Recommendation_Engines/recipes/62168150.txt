Title:
Auntie Chelle's Christmas Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup light brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1 tsp salt
2 Tbsp + 2 tsp baking powder
2 Tbsp cinnamon
2 Tbsp ginger
1 tsp nutmeg
4 eggs, beaten
1-1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup molasses
1 stick butter, melted

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients in large bowl.
Mix wet ingredients in separate bowl.
Add wet mixture to dry mixture & mix until batter is smooth.
Heat an oiled griddle/frying pan over medium low heat.
Scoop the batter onto heated griddle, using approx 1/4 cup per pancake.
Brown on both sides.
Serve hot.
