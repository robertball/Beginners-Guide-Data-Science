Title:
Sweet Cajun Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1101564.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dehydrated brown sugar
1/4 cup kosher salt
1/4 cup paprika
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon ground mustard
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 tablespoon granulated garlic
1/4 cup popcorn kernels, popped
2 tablespoons salted butter, melted

Directions:
Place brown sugar and spices in a coffee grinder or food processor and grind to a powder.  Store spice mix in a dry shaker container (such as an empty spice container).
Pop 1/4 cup of popcorn kernels.  Air popped is the preferred method.
Drizzle melted butter on the popcorn, stir to mix.
Sprinkle spice mix liberally on popcorn and stir to mix.

Submitter: 
kkinpa
Description: 
This sweet, slightly spicy popcorn is based on the South of Beale popcorn featured on Food Network's Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives.  The spice is unique and delicious served on buttery, freshly popped popcorn.
