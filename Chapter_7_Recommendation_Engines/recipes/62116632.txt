Title:
Classic English Creamed Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb small white onions, peeled
2 T butter
2 T flour
1 tsp grated onion
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 - 1/4 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
1/8 tsp white pepper
1 1/2 cup light cream or half & half
1 tsp instant chicken bouillion
Green pepper garnish, if desired

Directions:
Cover and cook onions in boiling salted water 20 - 30 minutes or until tender, but not overdone & coming apart.
Drain well.
While the onions are cooking, prepare the sauce.
Melt butter over low heat.
Stir in four, grated onion, salt, pepper and nutmeg.
Cook until smooth, stirring constantly.
Boil 1 minute.
Remove from heat, and gradually stir in the cream.
Add bouillon.
Return to medium heat, and bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
Add well-drained onions.
Bring to a serving temperature.
Garnish with stars cut from a green pepper.

Submitter: 
NONA
Description: 
No Southern holiday table was complete without a bowl of these rich creamy onions.  My mother-in-law introduced me to this tradition more than 35 years ago, and it continues in our home today.
