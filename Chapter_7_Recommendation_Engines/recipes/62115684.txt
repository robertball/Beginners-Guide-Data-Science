Layered Rigatoni Recipe

Ingredients:
1 and Â½ box rigatoni pasta
1 lb Italian sausage
1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes (not chunky kind)
3 cloves of garlic
Basil and oregano
1 cup shredded parm cheese
3 cups mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed
Skin the covers off sausage and place in fry pan.  Cook until done and then add garlic finely chopped and tomatoes and simmer.
Heat oven to 375.  Grease baking dish and throw pasta, cheeses and tomato mixture in and toss with hands.  Yep
Bake covered for 35 to 40 minutes
