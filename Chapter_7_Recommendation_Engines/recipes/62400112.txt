Title:
Au Gratin Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lb - Baking Potatoes
2c - Heavy Cream
1t - Garlic Powder
2t - Thyme Leaves
2c - Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Peel and slice potatoes very thin. Pour cream over potatoes, sprinkle on garlic powder and thyme, pour on 1 cup of cheese, and mix together. Bake 35 min., pull out sprinkle on remaining cheese as a crust on top, and bake another 5 min.
