Title:
Sweet Potato Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup celery, chopped
1/2 cup onion, diced
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 t salt
1/2 t sage
1/2 t poultry seasoning
1/2 t pepper
6 cups dry bread crumbs
1 large can sweet potatoes, chopped

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, heat butter over medium-high heat. Add celery and onion; cook and stir until tender. Stir in broth and seasonings. Add remaining ingredients; toss to combine.
Transfer to a greased 3-qt. slow cooker. Cook, covered, on low 4 hours or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Carrie
Description: 
I make this instead of plain stuffing each year for Thanksgiving and it is always a hit.
