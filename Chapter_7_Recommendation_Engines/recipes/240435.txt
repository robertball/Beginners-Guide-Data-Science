Title:
Easy Watergate Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2189974.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple in juice (such as Dole®), undrained
1 cup miniature marshmallows (such as Kraft® Jet-Puffed)
1 (3.4 ounce) package instant pistachio pudding mix (such as Jell-O®)
1/2 cup chopped pecans (such as Planters®)
1 1/2 cups frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed

Directions:
Mix pineapple, marshmallows, pudding mix, and pecans together in a bowl; fold in whipped topping. Refrigerate salad until chilled and thickened, about 1 hour.
