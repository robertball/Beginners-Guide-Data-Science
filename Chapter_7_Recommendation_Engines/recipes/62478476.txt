Title:
Artichoke and Green Chili DIp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14 oz. can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
2 4 oz. cans diced green chilies
1 cup mayonnaise
2 cups grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and put in a shallow baking dish.  (Pie plate works well.)  Cook at 325 degrees for 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
maggie
Description: 
Great and easy dip that is always a crowd pleaser.  Serve with crackers or tostitos.
