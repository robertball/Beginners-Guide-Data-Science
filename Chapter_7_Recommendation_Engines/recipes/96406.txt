Title:
Turkey Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980915.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.48 pound) package JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE® Turkey Breast, cut into thin strips
3 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons flour
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup whole milk
1 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
Freshly ground black pepper
1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
2 cups thinly sliced mushrooms
6 green onions, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons CARAPELLI® Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/2 pound spaghetti or macaroni
2 cups frozen green peas
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Cook turkey breast according to package directions. Set aside. Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
In saucepan over medium-high heat, melt butter. Stir in flour. Cook 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly. Gradually add broth, stirring until smooth. Add milk, oregano and black pepper. In skillet over medium-high heat, saut mushrooms, bell pepper and green onions in oil 2-3 minutes or until tender.
Prepare pasta according to package directions. In large mixing bowl, combine cooked pasta, sauce, mushroom mixture, turkey and peas. Spoon into greased 13 x 9-inch baking dish. Sprinkle top with cheese. Bake 20 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
