Title:
Tuscan Fava Bean and Orecchiette Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1020273.jpg
Ingredients:
1 19 oz. can fava beans drained, rinsed
1 cup dried orecchiette pasta
2/3 cup red onion
1 cup halved grape tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
Dressing:
4 minced cloves garlic1/4 cup EVOO
3 Tbsp red wine vinegar
1 tsp  dried thyme leaves
1/4 tsp kosher salt
12 twists of freshly cracked pepper

Directions:
1. Cook pasta 10 mins, al dente. Rinse in cold water to stop cooking process.
2. Whisk dressing ingredients together in a small bowl.
3. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Toss with dressing. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour. Overnight is preferred.

Submitter: 
Cathy Myers
Description: 
We really love the texture, hardiness and earthiness of the fava beans, however, butter beans or large lima beans may be substituted.
