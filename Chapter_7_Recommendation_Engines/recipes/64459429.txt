Title:
Baked Vidalia Onion Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium sweet onions
2 cups shredded Swiss cheese
2 cups mayonnaise
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Chop onions. Place the chopped onions and the Swiss cheese and mayonnaise in a large mixing bowl and combine well. Transfer to a 2 quart baking dish. Sprinkle with cayenne. Bake until golden brown and bubbly, about 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ursula
Description: 
Serve with chips, pitas, veggies and yes, apples.
