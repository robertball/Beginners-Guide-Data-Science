Title:
Rachel's Raspberry Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/821525.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen raspberry lemonade concentrate
12 fluid ounces lemon-flavored vodka
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle lime-flavored beer
12 fluid ounces water

Directions:
In a pitcher or punch bowl, stir together the raspberry lemonade concentrate, lemon vodka, lime-flavored beer, and water until thoroughly combined.
