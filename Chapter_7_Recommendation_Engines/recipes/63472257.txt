Title:
Easy Caesar Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup real mayonnaise
1 tbsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 tbsp. milk or half and half, or to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl or mason jar and whisk or shake to combine. Keeps up to one week, covered in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Julie Rehagen
Description: 
Quick Caesar salad dressing using no raw eggs or anchovies.
