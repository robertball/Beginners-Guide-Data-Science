Title:
Darling's Egg Nog Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. sugar
1 c. unsalted butter
1 1/2 c. full fat egg nog
11 oz pkg. butterscotch chips
10 oz white chocolate chips
1 tsp. nutmeg
2 jars marshmallow fluff
2 c. finely chopped walnuts
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 tsp. black walnut extract

Directions:
Line 9 by 13 pan with foil. In extra large sauce pan, combine sugar, butter, and egg nog.  Cook stirring constantly until it comes to a full boil.  Insert candy thermometer and continue cooking, stirring constantly until you reach soft ball, or 234 degrees.  Remove from heat, immediately stir in nutmeg, butterscotch, and white chocolate until completely melted.  Add marshmallow fluff and extracts and beat until melted.  Fold in nuts. Pour into prepared pan.  Cool completely.  Cut into squares.

Submitter: 
DarlingDash
Description: 
Definately a winter holiday fudge.  I put this in my Christmas tins every year along with other confections.  It is my grandmother's favorite.
