Title:
Momma's Poor Mans Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/574995.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. Noodles - Wide, Cooked and Drained
1 1/2 lb. Hamburger-Lean
3/4 C. Chopped Sweet Onions
1-8 oz. cans Tomato Sauce - No Salt
Salt & Pepper to Taste
1 C. Cottage Cheese - Sm. Curd
1-8 oz. pkg. Cream Cheese - Room Temp.
1/4 C. Sour Cream
1/2 C. Chopped Green Onion
1/3 C. Chopped Green Pepper

Directions:
Cook noodles, drain and set aside.
At the same time you can do the following steps:
Cook the onions and hamburger in a large skillet.  Add tomato sauce and salt and pepper to the hamburger mixture and simmer.
In a bowl, mix cheeses, sour cream, green pepper and green onions.
Grease a large casserole dish.  Layer beginning with 1/2 of the noodles.  Spread cheese mixture on top of the noodles and layer the remaining noodles over that.  Layer the mean mixture on top.

Submitter: 
VH
Description: 
Simple and Quick comfort food made for us as kids by my Momma.  Good for making large batches and freezing.  An inexpensive alternative to traditional lasagne.
