Title:
Irish Herb Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
3 tablespoons butter (softened)
1 egg
3 cups bread flour
1 cup mashed potato flakes
1 tablespoon sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon (crushed)
1 1/2 teaspoon dried dill (crushed)
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
2 1/2 teaspoons yeast
teaspoon

Directions:
Add ingredients to your breadmachine in the order called for by the manufacturer.
Select manual cycle
When the machine is done, remove bread and punch down.
Place dough into 2 loaf pans, cover, and let rise until doubled
Bake 35 minutes at 350

Submitter: 
MomZilla (Evin)
Description: 
A savory loaf of potato bread, delicious with your lamb stew.
