Title:
Tempe Mendoan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2031226.jpg
Ingredients:
•1.5 block tempeh, cut into thin slices
•oil for deep frying
Spiced batter
•1/2 cup all purpose flour (Indonesian: tepung terigu)
•1/2 cup of  rice flour (Indonesian: tepung beras)
•1-1/2 teaspoon salt
•1 teaspoon sugar
•1-1/2 teaspoon coriander powder (Indonesian: bubuk ketumbar)
•2 cloves garlic (Indonesian: bawang putih), grated
*5 Candle Nut (Kemiri)
*1/4 teaspoon Tumeric (Kunyit)
•2 scallions (Indonesian: daun bawang), cut into thin sliced
.5 Kaffir Lime Leaves
•300 ml water

Directions:
1.Heat enough oil in a pot/frying pan for deep frying.
2.Combine all batter ingredients in a mixing bowl, make sure to remove all the lumps.
3.Once the oil is ready, dip each slice of tempeh in the batter and deep fry until crispy.
4.Arrange the fried tempeh on a serving plate and serve with the chili sauce.
