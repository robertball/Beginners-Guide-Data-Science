Title:
Big Ray's Baked Banana French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2275313.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf stale French bread (if fresh toasted)
1 package,8 oz., Neufchatel cheese, cut into 3/4-inch cubes
3 medium bananas, sliced
6 eggs
4 cups milk
1/2 cup sugar, divided
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/4 cup maple syrup
3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon,divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°. Cut bread into 1-in. cubes; in a small boll mix half the sugar and half the cinnamon together and roll the cubed Neufchatel cheese in cinnamon/sugar mixture.  Place half of the bread in a 13x9-in. baking dish coated with cooking spray. Layer with cream cheese, vanilla,  bananas and remaining cubed bread.
In a large bowl, whisk eggs, milk, syrup, remaining sugar and cinnamon; pour over top. Refrigerate, covered, 8 hours or overnight.
Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before baking. Bake, covered, 30 minutes. Bake, uncovered, 25-30 minutes longer or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
