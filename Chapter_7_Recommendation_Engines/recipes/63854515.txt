Title:
Big Ray's Crustless Broccoli Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1070048.jpg
Ingredients:
4  eggs
1 cups milk
1 cup cottage cheese
3 slices  whole wheat bread, crusts removed, cubed
4 cups  small broccoli florets, cooked, chopped
1 cup  chopped onions
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp salt
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350Â°F.
Whisk eggs and milk in medium bowl until blended. Add bread; mix lightly. Stir in remaining ingredients.
Pour into 9-inch cast iron skillet, sprayed with cooking spray.
Bake 45 to 60 min. or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Let stand 10 min. before cutting to serve.
