Title:
Smokin' Succulent Grilled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Hellmann's® or Best Foods® Real Mayonnaise
2 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 1/2 teaspoons crushed chipotle chili pepper
4 pork chops

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients except pork chops. Reserve 1/2 cup mayonnaise mixture.
Grill or broil pork chops (6 minutes on each side), turning once and brushing frequently with mayonnaise mixture, until done. Serve with reserved mayonnaise mixture.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
Plate up a pleasant surprise: grilled pork that is perfectly moist! Its a snap with this simple, slightly spicy, grilling sauce.
