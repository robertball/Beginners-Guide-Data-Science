Title:
Grandma Jane's Tomato & Cucumber salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 Large Tomatos  diced
2 large cucumbers peeled and diced
1 to 1 1/2 cups Mayo
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Fold the Mayo into the Tomatoes, cucumbers,  salt and pepper together and chill.  It's better if left to marinate over night.

Submitter: 
cj
Description: 
Traditional summer salad of diced tomato and cucumber with a light dressing.
