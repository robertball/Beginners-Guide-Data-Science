Title:
broccoli raisin salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups broccoli,1 lb bacon,1 small red onion,1 cup raisins,1 small pkg sunflower seeds, 1 cup mayo, 1/2 cup sugar, 2 tbsp white vinegar

Directions:
fry bacon crisp, then chop fine, chop broccoli and onion fine add 1 cup raisins, set aside,. sauce combine mayo,sugar and vinegar, stir until sugar is absorbed, add to other ingredients,chill for 1 hour,  sprinkle sunflower seeds over top just befor serving
