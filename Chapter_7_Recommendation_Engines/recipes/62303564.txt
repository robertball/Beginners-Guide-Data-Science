Title:
Thai-style steamed pumpkin cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/366418.jpg
Ingredients:
25g tapioca flour
60 rice flour
1/2 tablespoon arrowroot flour
125ml (1/2 cup) coconut cream
100g sugar
1 pumpkin (about 1 kg), peeled, seeded and grated
250ml (1 cup) coconut milk
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1. Mix the tapioca flour, rice flour and arrowroot flour together. Gradually add the coconut cream and stir constantly until all the cream has been used, about 10 minutes.
2. Add the sugar, pumpkin, coconut milk and salt. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
3. Pour the batter into a square 25cmx 25cm baking pan. Place into a prepared steamer and steam for 25 minutes.
4. Cool the cake and cut into squares. Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
wiley
Description: 
This is a slightly different take on the traditional Thai steamed pumpkin cake. You simply need to grate the pumpkin and mix all the ingredients together, place in a pan and steam. You can cool the cake and cut it into squares to serve at a later time - it is the perfect snack!
