Title:
Creamy Ham Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
2/3 cup milk
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
2 cups cubed fully cooked ham
1 (4.5 ounce) jar sliced mushrooms, drained
1 (2 ounce) jar diced pimientos, drained

Directions:
Cook the macaroni according to package directions.  Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, whisk soup and milk; stir in cheese.  Cook and stir over medium heat until cheese is melted.  Drain macaroni; add to the pan.  Stir in the ham, mushrooms and pimientos; heat through.

Submitter: 
Beulah Johnston
Description: 
At her home in Fort Myers, Florida, Beulah Johnston jazzes up macaroni with ham, cheese and mushrooms to make this family-pleasing fare.  'Sprinkle servings with chopped cashews, if you'd like.' she adds.
