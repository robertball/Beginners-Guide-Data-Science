Title:
Macaroons - No Bake Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C sugar
8 Tbl butter
1/3 C cocoa
1/2 C milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 C peanut butter
2 1/2 to 3 C oats
1/4 coconut

Directions:
Cook all but oats and coconut for 8 minutes to melt sugar. Add oats and coconut and mix well. Drop by Tbl on to parchment lined cookie sheets. Refrigerate overnight.
