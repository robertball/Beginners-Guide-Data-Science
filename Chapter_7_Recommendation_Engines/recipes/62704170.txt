Title:
Easy No bake Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of Egg noodles
2 can of Tuna- drained
1 can of peas- drained
1 lg can of condensed mushroom soup
2 pats of butter(optional)
half and half
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil package of medium egg noodles in salted water until tender.  Drain
Add butter,mushroom soup, and peas and mix.
slowly add cream until nice nice and creamy.
Make sure heated thourghly and salt and pepper to taste.
Serve and enjoy.
