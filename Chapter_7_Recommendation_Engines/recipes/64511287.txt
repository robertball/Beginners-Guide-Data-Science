Title:
Quick Parmesan Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
buns, halved (hot dog, dinner, burger or other)
butter
parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
Spread butter on bun halves.
Sprinkle with grated cheese.
Broil for a couple minutes, until toasted.
Serve with soup, chili, salad, stew, etc. Or enjoy as a snack on it's own.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Need an accompaniment to soup or salad? Want a quick, warm, cheese bread snack? Have leftover hot dog, burger or other buns? This could be the answer!
