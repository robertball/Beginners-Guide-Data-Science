Title:
California Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 can tomato soup
2 1/2 cans water
6 oz can tomato paste
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon oregano
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2/3 cup minute rice

Directions:
Saute onions and ground beef. Drain grease. Add remaining ingredients except rice. Simmer uncovered for 45 minutes. Add uncooked rice, cover, simmer 15 minutes.
Serve over tortilla chips. Add lettuce, fresh tomatoes, and cheese.
