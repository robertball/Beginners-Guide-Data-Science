Title:
Apple and Butternut Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs squash (medium)
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 sweet onion
2 large carrots
2 ribs of celery
5 Royal Gala apples (medium size)
6 cups vegetable stock
2 tsp smoked paprika (start with 1)
2 tsp cumin (start with 1)
1 tsp ground coriander
1 1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp ground cardamom
1/4 tsp thyme
1 tsp apple cider vinegar
1 tbs apple cider mix

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 C. Grease a pan with olive oil. Cut Squash in half and remove seeds. Place squash cut side down on pan and put in oven. Cook for 30 min or until skin can be pierced through with fork
Meanwhile, chop the celery, onion and carrot and cook in pan/pot with the 1/4 cup olive oil on medium-high heat until cooked through.
Chop and core the apples and add to pot along with the remaining ingredients. When squash can be pierced through the skin with a fork, remove from oven. Remove the skin and add the flesh to pot
Set heat on high until boiling. Reduce heat to simmer.
Using an immersion blender, blend the mixture until smooth.
