Title:
Piglets in a Blanket with Honey Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/942859.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can of refrigerated biscuits
1 package of little smokies
Honey, just a dab on each piece

Directions:
1. Open the can of biscuits. Pull all apart and then tear each biscuit piece in half. Set aside.
2. Grab a sheet of paper towels and fold in half then half again. Drain the smokies and pat dry with the paper towels.
3. Preheat  oven to  400 degrees.
4. Roll a biscuit piece in your hands to make long roll. Wrap around a smokie and seal well on the bottom. Line them on a foil covered cookie sheet or a casserole dish. Repeat this step with each piece.
5. Spoon a dab of honey on top of each piece.
6. Place sheet or dish in the oven and bake for 13 minutes or until it starts turning a golden brown.

Submitter: 
Lovely Mommy
Description: 
One of my kids favorites! So quick and simple to make and the honey is what makes this dish stand out. You can use store bought honey or local honey.
