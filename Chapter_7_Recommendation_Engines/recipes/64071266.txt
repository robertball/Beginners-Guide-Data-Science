Title:
Five Minute Chocolate Mud Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
3 TBSP milk
3 TBSP oil
4 TBSP flour
4 TBSP sugar
2 TBSP cocoa
3 TBSP chocolate chips
dash of vanilla

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients in a large mug, stir well. Add flour, sugar, cocoa, chips and vanilla to the mug. Mix well.
Microwave 3 minutes @ 1000 watts power.
