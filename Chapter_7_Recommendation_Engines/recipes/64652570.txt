Title:
Buttery Gnocchi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. Gnocchi (precooked package)
2 Cloves of Garlic Â– Minced
1/2 Cup Shredded Parmesean Cheese
2 Tablespoons Butter
3/4 Teaspoon Salt
1/4 Teaspoon Pepper
10 Oz. Fresh Spinach
Grilled Chicken

Directions:
1. Cook your gnocchi according to the directions on the package.
2. Once cooked, drain, set aside and grab a large nonstick frying pan.
3. Heat your butter add in your minced garlic and continue to cook for 1 minute.
4. Continue to stir and then add in your gnocchi and spinach.
5. Cook for 1-2 minutes and wait for the spinach to wilt.
5. At this point, add in your salt and pepper and stir well.
6. Remove from burner and let cook for 5-10 minutes.
7. When ready to serve sprinkle on Parmesan cheese and enjoy!
