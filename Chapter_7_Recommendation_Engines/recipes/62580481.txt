Title:
Karen's Prize-Winning Bisquick Topped Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of chicken soup
¾ c. milk
¼ tsp. seasoned salt
2 c. chopped cooked chicken
1 pkg. frozen broccoli, cauliflower, and carrots
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese (save some  for top)
1 c. French fried onions   (save some  for top)
Topping:
1 C. bisquick
1 egg slightly beaten
¼ c. milk

Directions:
Combine, in casserole dish, soup, 3/4 c of milk, seasoned salt, cooked chicken, frozen veggies, shredded cheese, and onion rings. Bake 10 minutes.
While casserole is baking, combine bisquick, egg, and milk.  Spoon over casserole and bake an additional 20-25 minutes.
Top with reserved cheese and onion rings and bake for an additional 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
katieo
Description: 
Cooked chicken,soup, cheese,frozen veggies, onion rings all baked into a yummy casserole topped with bisquick and more cheese and onion rings for a quick and tasty dish that the adults and grandchildren love!
