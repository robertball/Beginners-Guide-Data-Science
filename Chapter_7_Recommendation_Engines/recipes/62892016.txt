Title:
Chicken with goop Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken breasts
2 10.05 ounce cans cream of chicken soup
16 ounces sour cream
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
6 slices mozzarella cheese
1 box chicken Stove Top stuffing

Directions:
In a blender or food processor puree the stuffing to a fine crumb. In a medium size bowl mix together the soup, pepper and sour cream. Spray a baking dish with a non stick cooking spray. Add the chicken breasts and top each breast with a slice of cheese. Pour the sauce over the chicken and sprinkle with stuffing. Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until done.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
Chicken breasts covered with a slice of cheese and a lovely creamy sauce topped with stuffing. Can be served over steamed rice or egg noodles.
