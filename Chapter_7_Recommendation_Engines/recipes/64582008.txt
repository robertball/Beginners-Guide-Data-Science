Title:
Tasty Thai Zoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3660122.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large zucchinis, cut with a spiralizer
1 small red pepper, chopped
1 small yellow pepper, chopped
3 celery stalks, chopped
1/2 cup chopped onions
1 Tbsp. Sesame Oil
Sauce:
1/2 cup low sodium soy sauce
1 Tbsp. brown sugar
1 Tbsp. minced garlic
1/2 Tbsp. ground garlic
Optional Toppings:
Green Onions
Sriracha
Ground Peanuts

Directions:
1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add peppers, celery, and onions to pan for 5 minutes or until slightly softened.
2. Squeeze zucchini strands in between paper towels to reduce moisture and add to pan. Cook an additional 5 minutes tossing zucchini until they reach al dente. Season with salt & pepper.
3. While zucchini is cooking whisk together soy sauce, sugar, garlic, and ginger. Pour sauce into pan and toss for 1 minute until all vegetables are coated.
4. Serve warm with toppings to add crunch or spice!

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
Skip the carbs, but not the flavor with Zucchini noodles. Quick and easy with a great Thai taste! Add meat if you please or switch up the veggies.
