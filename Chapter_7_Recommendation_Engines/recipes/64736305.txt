Title:
Dandelion Flower Cookies I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6505119.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C butter, room temperature
3/4 C firmly packed brown sugar
1 C granulated sugar
3 eggs
1 T pure vanilla extract
1 1/2 C all purpose flour
1 t baking soda
1 1/2 t ground cinnamon
3 C oatmeal
1 C craisins
1 C dandelion petals

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Soak craisins in water and set aside
In a mixing bowl cream together the butter and the sugars
Stir in the eggs and vanilla
Fold in the dandelion petals into the egg mixture a tablespoon at a time until well mixed
Stir in the flour
Stir in the baking soda and cinnamon
Stir in the oats
Drain the craisins and fold them into the batter
Drop dough by rounded tablespoon fulls onto a baking sheet
Bake for 12 minutes or until edges are golden
Upon removing from oven allow to cool for a minute
