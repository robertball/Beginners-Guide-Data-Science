Title:
Simple Eggrolls by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5829078.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground turkey
1/2 bag cabbage mix
2tbsp water
2tbsp soy sauce
1tsp Ginger
1tsp Garlic powder
1/2 tsp Celery salt
8 eggroll wraps
Olive oil

Directions:
Brown turkey, remove from pan.
Cook cabbage with water and soy sauce And seasonings  until nearly tender.
Add turkey and mix well.
Fill wrappers with mixture and roll up. Seal corner  with a little water.
Smear or spray lightly with olive oil.
Cook in 425 over for 30-40 minutes until crispy, turn halfway through.
Or air fryer at 370 for 8-10 minutes, turn halfway through. Will probably need to do in two batching, when the first batch is nearly done then I wrap up the second batch.
