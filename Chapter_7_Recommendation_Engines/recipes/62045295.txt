Title:
Hot Dill Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. butter
1 Tbsp. flour
1 tsp. salt
1 to 2 tsp. dill weed
1/8 tsp. fresh ground pepper
1 cup milk
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 Tbsp. chopped yellow onion
4 cups (about 5 medium) potatoes, diced and cooked

Directions:
In a medium size saucepan, melt butter over low heat.  Stir in flour, salt, dill weed, and pepper.  Add milk.  Cook and stir until thick and bubbly.  Blend in mayonnaise and onion.  Remove from heat and gently fold in potatoes.  Serve warm.
Refrigerate leftovers and reheat in microwave oven.

Submitter: 
Cristi Zbella
Description: 
This recipe comes from my Grandma, Alice Devine.  It is my absolute favorite potato salad recipe.  It's best served warm, but it's still great cold.  Everyone loves this!  Try it!  You will too!
