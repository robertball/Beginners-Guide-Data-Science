Title:
Hot Dog Bourbon Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. hot dogs
½ cup bourbon
½ cup brown sugar
½ cup ketchup

Directions:
Cut hot dogs into small one-inch pieces
Mix with other ingredients and bring to a boil.
Cook/simmer till well coated.
Transfer to a crockpot to keep warm & serve with toothpicks.  The longer they sit in the mixture the better they will be.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
You can't get any easier than this.  I've served these hot dog appetizers at fancy occasions such as weddings.
