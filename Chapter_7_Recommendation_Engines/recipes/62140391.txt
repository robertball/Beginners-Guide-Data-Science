Title:
Oma's Soup (Dutch Soup) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. beef steak, cubed
1 Tbsp salt
2 pkgs. Honig spring vegetable soup mix
6 qt. water
1/2 Cup raw rice
3-4 large carrots, diced
1 Cup chopped leeks
2 curls vermicelli noodles, broken up
2 cans (14oz) Veg-All mixed vegetables
Meatballs*:
1 lb. ground beef
1 egg
2 rusks, crushed
Salt & pepper

Directions:
Simmer steak with salt in a large stockpot with water and dry soup mixes for one hour. Add rice, carrots, and leeks. Make meatballs about an inch in diameter and add to soup. Cook on low until all ingredients are tender, about 20 minutes. Add salt to taste. Thirty minutes before serving (or once vegetables are tender) add the vermicelli and the Veg-All.
