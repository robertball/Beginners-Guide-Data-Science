Title:
Spinach and Goat Cheese Stuffed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/407269.jpg
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
3/4 cup Kraft garlic and herb sandwich mayo
3/4 cup crumbled goat cheese
1/2 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
2 (10 ounce) package frozen leaf or chopped  spinach, thawed and drained well
6 slices prosciutto (or bacon)
1 lemon, cut for squeezing
Optional: sun-dried tomatoes, toasted pine nuts

Directions:
Thaw spinach and squeeze to remove excess water.
In a medium bowl, mix mayo, spinach, goat cheese. Add sauteed onion and warm garlic until well blended.  Set aside in refrigerator.
Carefully butterfly chicken breasts, making sure not to cut all the way through.  Spoon spinach mixture into chicken breasts.  Wrap each with a piece of prosciutto. (or bacon secured with toothpicks)    Place in shallow baking dish and squeeze lemon juice over chicken.   Cover with foil.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 45 min., remove foil and broil approx. 10 minutes more.
