Title:
Pumpkin Crumble Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2674458.jpg
Ingredients:
Muffin Batter:
4 cups of fresh pureed pumpkin (or canned)
4 eggs
1 2/3 cups sugar
2/3 cup vegetable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla
3 all-purpose flour
1/2 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground all spice
1/4 teaspoon cardamom
Crumble:
1/2 rolled oats
2 tablespoons all purpose flour
1/2 chopped walnuts
1/2 cup brown sugar
4 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Place oven rack to low position so that tops of tins will be in center of oven. Heat oven to 350ยบ. Grease bottoms only of 3 muffin tins with non-stick cooking spray.
Crumble Mix: Combine oats, flour, walnuts, brown sugar and butter Using a pastry blender, a fork or your hands cut in butter. Keep cold until ready to use.
Muffin Batter: Stir together pumpkin, eggs, sugar, oil, and vanilla in large bowl.
Mix in remaining ingredients. Pour into tins, filling each cup to the half mark.
Evenly distribute crumble mix on top of each muffin.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Remove from tins and place top side up on wire rack to cool.
