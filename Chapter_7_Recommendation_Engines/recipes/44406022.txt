Title:
Parmigian-Reggiano Crusted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken cutlets
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
2 egg whites, lightly beaten
1 1/2 cups grated or shredded Parmigian Reggiano cheese
Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Tenderize chicken cutlets with a mallot.
Place a large skillet over medium-high heat with olive oil.
Fill one dish with egg whites and the other with the Parmigiano Reggiano.
Season the chicken cutlets on both sides with salt and freshly ground black pepper, then dip them first in the egg white and then in the grated cheese.
Cook each side about 4-5 minutes or until juices run clear.
