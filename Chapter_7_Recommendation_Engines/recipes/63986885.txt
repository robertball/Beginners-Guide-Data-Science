Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter flavored crisco, softened
2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract or almond Extract

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream together the cream cheese and butter until creamy. Mix in the vanilla, then gradually stir in the confectioners' sugar. Store in the refrigerator after use.

Submitter: 
Douglas Johannes
Description: 
This cream cheese icing goes great on pumpkin bread, carrot cake, chocolate cake, and cookies. Just four ingredients!Modified from: Cream Cheese Frosting II
