Title:
Spicy Sausage Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. butter
1 tbsp. olive oil
3 cloves of garlic - minced
1 lb. hot Italian sausage - loose
2/3 cup dry white wine
14 oz. can diced tomatoes with juice
1 cup heavy cream
6 tbsp. chopped Italian parsley
1 cup grated Parmesan
1 lb. penne pasta

Directions:
Cook pasta until al dente.
While pasta  is cooking, melt butter and olive oil in a pan.
Add onions and garlic and saute until golden brown.
Add sausage and cook for about 10 minutes, breaking up large pieces.
If there is a lot of grease in pan, drain some off.
Add wine and cook until it reduces by half.
Add tomatoes with juices and cook until it reduces by half.
Add heavy cream and reduce by about half and until slightly thickened.
Stir in 4 tbsp. of parsley and add salt and pepper to taste.
Remove from heat and pour over cooked pasta.
Add 3/4 cup of Parmesan and toss to coat.
Serve sprinkled with remaining parsley and Parmesan.
