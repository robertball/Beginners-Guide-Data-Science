Title:
White cheese dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 oz. white american cheese, sliced or cubed
12 oz. monterey jack cheese, cubed
1 pint half & half
1 pint whipping cream
1 cup chopped spinach, thawed and with moisture squeezed out
1 (4 oz) can diced jalapenos, drained

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, adding spinach a little at a time, in a crock pot or large saucepan and stir.  Heat until melted, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
ecmcg
Description: 
A creamy restaurant-style white cheese dip.  Goes great with tortilla chips or as a topping for any Mexican dish.
