Title:
Cream Cheese Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/943372.jpg
Ingredients:
2 8oz rolls crescent roll dough
2 8oz packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar, divided
1 4oz stick butter, melted
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Lightly grease 9x13 glass baking dish (or line w/ parchment paper).
Roll out one can of crescent dough in bottom of pan, sealing perforations with fingers.
In a medium bowl, mix cream cheese, 3/4 cup sugar and vanilla extract.
Spread cream cheese mixture evenly over crescent dough.
Open second package of crescent dough, and place on top of cream cheese mixture, sealing perforations with fingers.
Pour melted butter evenly over crescent dough.
Mix remaining 1/4 cup sugar and cinnamon, and sprinkle evenly over buttered dough.
Bake 20-30 minutes.
