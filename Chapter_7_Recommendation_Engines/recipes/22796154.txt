Title:
Artichoke Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts (drained and chopped)
1 cup Hellman's Mayo
1 cup parmesan cheese
1 clove garlic finely chopped or 1/4 tsp. garlic powder with parsley
1 loaf french bread

Directions:
Combine all and spread evenly.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
