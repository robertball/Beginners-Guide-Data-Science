Title:
Rice with Bacon and Onion (Arroz con Tocineta y Cebolla) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans French Onion Soup
2 cans Consomme Beef
8 cups rice
1 lb bacon
salt and pepper to taste
water
olive or vegetable oil

Directions:
Cut the bacon in small pieces and fry. Add some olive oil. Pour the rice on top and saute a little bit. Open cans of soup and measure, add water to complete 9 cups of liquid. Add on top of rice. Add salt and pepper to taste. Cook until water is absorbed, then lower heat and cover.

Submitter: 
Milly Suazo-Martinez
Description: 
Very tasty rice with a smoky bacon flavor.
