Title:
Aunt Lena's Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. chopped yellow squash or zucchini
2 c. prepared cornbread, crumbled(or cornbread stuffing mix)
2 eggs
1 small chopped onion
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 stick butter, melted
2 Tbls. chicken granules or 1 cube chicken bullion
1 t. sage(optional)
1/4 c. chopped waterchestnuts(optional)

Directions:
Mix all together.  Spray a casserole dish, cover, and put in 350 degree oven for 40-45 mins. May remove cover last 10 mins.

Submitter: 
Mary Ann Lackey
Description: 
Great with roasted chicken or turkey without the time and effort of regular dressing.
