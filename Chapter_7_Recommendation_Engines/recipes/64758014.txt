Title:
Bean and barley soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
1 clove crushed garlic
2 medium carrots, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
I leek, chopped
3 1/2 cups vegetable stock
1 small can tomato puree
3 heaping teaspoons medium barley
I can navy beans (do not drain)
1/2 cup packed leafy greens, stems removed and chopped
2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1 tsp salt
A few dashes ground cayenne pepper

Directions:
Heat the oil in a big pot, add carrots, celery, and leeks and cook on medium high heat until soft. Add the vegetable stock, tomato puree and barley. Simmer for 20 minutes until barley is tender. Add the beans, undrained and the greens (I used fresh spinach), and simmer for 5 more minutes. Let rest for a few minutes, serve with fresh baked bread.
