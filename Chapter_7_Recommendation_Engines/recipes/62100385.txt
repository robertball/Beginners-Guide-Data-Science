Title:
The Perfect Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289010.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbs butter or margarine, melted
1 1/2 cups cornmeal
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking soda
1 1/4 cups buttermilk
2 large eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degree Farenheit.  Grease a skillet or a 9" by 9" metal baking pan.
Combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt and baking soda in large bowl.  In medium bowl, beat buttermilk and eggs with fork until blended.  Add melted butter to buttermilk mixture, then add the buttermilk mixture to the flour mixture.  Stir just until flour is moistened (batter will be lumpy).
Pour batter into prepared skillet. Bake until golden at the edges and toothpick instered in center comes out clean, about 25 minutes.  Serve warm.
