Title:
Cucumber, Onion and Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 meduim cucumber - sliced
3 meduim tomatoes (firm but ripe) - sliced
1 large red onion - sliced and kept in ice water with pinch of salt for 1 hour
12 fresh basil leaves
1 tbsp dry oregano
2 tbsp fresh Italian parsley - chopped
1/2 tbsp coarsely ground pepper
4 tbsp bread crumbs - fine
----------------------------
1 tbsp salt
1/4 cup olive oil
3-4 tbsp lemon juice
2 tbsp orange juice
1 clove garlic

Directions:
For presentation - a glass bowl is best.
Begin with the cucumber slices and make a layer in the bowl.  Sprinkle with black pepper and some oregano then follow with a layer of the sliced tomatoes.  Sprinkle with crumbs, fresh basil torn in pieces and follow with some of the well drained onion slices.  Top with more oregano, bread crumbs and chopped parsley.  Repeat the process to use up all the vegetables.
Mix the oil, juices, garlic, and salt.  Pour over the arrangement and let stand a few minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
Delicious and tangy.  A real treat when tomatoes are in season.  Use ground up brown rice crisps for a gluten free alternative.
