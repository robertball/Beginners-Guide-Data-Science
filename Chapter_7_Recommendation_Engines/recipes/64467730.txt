Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 bar of white chocolate
-Sprinkles
-1 stalk of the finest kale

Directions:
1. Melt your chocolate in a bowl over boiling water.
2. Once the chocolate is completely melted, spoon some onto a tray in small amounts to create Dinomite's tooth shape.
3. Mash up the kale into a liquid and pour it across the chocolate teeth to create a nice green colouring, this will definitely wow your dinner guests!
4. Add sprinkles for that extra wow factor.
5. Place in the fridge for 1 hour or until the beautiful treats are hard enough to eat.

Submitter: 
Olivia Heardman
Description: 
These melt in your mouth treats will be a sure fire way to end an excellent dinner! Shaped like Dinomite's tooth for that extra awesomeness!
