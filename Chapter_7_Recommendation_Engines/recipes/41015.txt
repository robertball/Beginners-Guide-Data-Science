Title:
Corn Chowder with Sweet Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982315.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1 cup chopped onion
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated skim milk
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 (12 ounce) can whole kernel corn with sweet peppers
1 tablespoon snipped fresh parsley (optional)

Directions:
In a medium saucepan combine broth, onion, and pepper. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes or until onion is tender.
In a small bowl stir together milk and flour. Stir into broth mixture. Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. Stir in corn. Heat through. If desired, sprinkle with parsley.

Submitter: 
Better Homes and Gardens
Description: 
Serve this flavorful side-dish soup with your favorite sandwich. Or to make 3 main-dish servings, add one 6-ounce can minced clams, undrained, or 1 cup chopped cooked chicken with the corn. (330 calories per serving).
