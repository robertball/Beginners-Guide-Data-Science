Title:
Texas-New Mexico Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
canola or vegetable oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 medium onion, chopped
1 pound lean ground beef
2 bay leaves
3 Tbs cumin
2 Tbs chili powder
1 to 1-1/2 tsp oregano
1 14 oz can diced tomatoes
3-4 cans chicken broth
3-4 Tbs. yellow corn meal

Directions:
Saute garlic and onion in oil until the onion is translucent.  Add the ground meat and saute until cooked.  Add the spices and fry, stirring to mix with the meat.  Add the tomatoes and chicken broth.  Bring to a boil and then lower the heat.  Simmer for one hour.  Sprinkle the corn meal over the top of the chili and then stir it to mix it in well.  Cook another 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
SHECOOKS2
Description: 
This is a delicious blend of chilis.  The cornmeal thickens the chili and givest he chili a bite.  Add hot peppers if you like your chili hot.  It is great served over corn tortilla chips with sharp cheddar cheese melted on top.
