Title:
Big Ray's West African Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4531828.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch cubes
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
3 tsp olive oil oil, divided
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
6 garlic cloves, minced
2 Tbsp minced fresh gingerroot
2 cans (15-1/2 ounces each) black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
1 large sweet potato, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes
1 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1-1/2 tsp minced fresh thyme or 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme, divided
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
Hot cooked brown rice or quinoa, optional

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper. In a Dutch oven, cook chicken over medium heat in 2 teaspoons oil for 4-6 minutes or until no longer pink; remove and set aside.
In the same pan, saute onion in remaining oil until tender. Add garlic and ginger; cook 1 minute longer.
Stir in the beans, tomatoes, sweet potato, broth, peanut butter, 1-1/4 teaspoons thyme and cayenne. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until potato is tender. Add chicken; heat through.
Serve with rice or quinoa if desired. Sprinkle with remaining thyme
