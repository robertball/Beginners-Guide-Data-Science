Title:
Strangely soft chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cup flour
1 t baking soda
1/4 t salt
1 t baking powder
1 3.4 oz pkg pudding of choice (vanilla)
1 cup butter
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 T vanilla
2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
cream together butter and sugar
beat in pudding and eggs
blend in dry ingredients
stir in chocolate chips
drop cookies rounded onto parchment paper on cookie sheet or ungreased cookie sheets
bake at 350 degreed for 10 to 12 min until edges brown
