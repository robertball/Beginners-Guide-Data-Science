Title:
Wild Mushroom Farfalle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz uncooked farfalle pasta
2 tbsp evoo
1/2 c chopped onion
5 garlic cloves, chopped
2 (4 oz) pkgs exotic mushroom blend
1 c unsalted vegetable stock
3/4 tsp kosher salt
2 tbsp chopped dill (alt. use parsley or thyme)
1 tbsp unsalted butter
2 tsp grated lemon rind
1 1/2 tsp sherry vinegar
3/8 tsp black pepper
1/4 c ricotta cheese
1/4 c shaved Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions, drain, and reserve 1/3 c liquid.
Heat large skillet over med-high. Add oil and onion and saut√© three minutes.
Add garlic and mushrooms and cook eight minutes or until mushrooms are brown.
Add stock and salt and bring to boil.
Reduce heat and simmer five minutes or until liquid is reduced by half.
Stir in 1/3 c reserved liquid, dill, butter, lemon, vinegar, and pepper.
Add pasta and toss to coat.
Serve in four bowls and top with cheese.
