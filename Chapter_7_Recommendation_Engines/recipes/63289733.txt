Title:
Low Fat Blueberry Cheesecake Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ice Cream:
8 oz fat free cream cheese, softened
1 C fat free half n half
1/2 C skim milk
3/4 C sugar
dash of salt
1 T lemon juice
splash vanilla
Blueberry Sauce:
1/4 C sugar
1 1/2 t cornstarch
cinnamon to taste
1/4 C water
1 C blueberries (fresh or frozen)

Directions:
blend all ingredients of ice cream well, let sit in fridge for at least 1 hour prior to machine
bring blueberry mixture to a boil and cook blueberries well, use stick blender to smooth mixture. Cool completely
Add small amount of blueberry mixture to ice cream at beginning of freeze, and remainder with about 5 minutes left on machine.
Allow ice cream to sit in freezer at least 1 hour before serving

Submitter: 
Jennie Cripps-Ludlum
Description: 
low fat version of ice cream for smaller (1.5) ice cream machine.
