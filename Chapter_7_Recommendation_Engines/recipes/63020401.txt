Title:
Furikake Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/756809.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (12 ounce) boxes Crispix cereal
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup corn oil
1/2 cup Karo Light
1 (1.9 ounce) bottle Aji Nori Furikake (seasoned seaweed & sesame seed rice seasoning)

Directions:
Pour the cereal into a large alumninum pan.
In a sauce pan on low heat, melt the butter and sugar. Remove from heat and add the oil and Karo.
Slowly pour the mixture over the cereal and mix to cover. Sprinkle the Furikake seasoning over the cereal while stirring.
Bake at 225 degrees for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Remove from oven and let cool. Store in air-tight container.

Submitter: 
Laurie Sueko Lau
Description: 
A sweet and salty mixture of crispy cereal with sugar and furikake seasoning makes this popular Hawaii snack a crowd-pleaser!
