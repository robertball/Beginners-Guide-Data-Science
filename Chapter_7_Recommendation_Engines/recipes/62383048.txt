Title:
Big Daddy's Orange Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
1 cup milk
1 orange peeled
1 cup water
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
10 cubes  ice
2 scoops of Mila
2 tablespoons honey

Directions:
In a blender, combine orange juice concentrate, orange peeled, Mila, milk, water, vanilla, honey and ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
A great drink for orange lovers, and a fun way to cool off! Orange juice concentrate is blended with milk and ice to produce a tasty slushy drink!Modified from: Orange Smoothie
