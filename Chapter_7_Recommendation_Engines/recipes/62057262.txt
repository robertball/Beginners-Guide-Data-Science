Title:
Spicy Carrot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
7 large carrots, peeled and sliced  into 1/4-inch coins
3-4 slices of fresh ginger
1 onion, chopped
3 1/2 C chicken broth
3/4 C coconut milk
1/2 tsp red curry paste
salt to taste
Chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
1. In a large pot, saut carrots, onion, and ginger slices for 5 minutes in olive oil on medium heat.
2. Add 2 cups of broth, cover and simmer for an additional 15 minutes (or until carrots are soft).  Remove ginger slices.
3. Add remaining 1 1/2 cups of broth to cool mixture and transfer to blender.  Add red curry paste and puree until smooth.
4. Return to pot, add coconut milk and heat through.  Add salt to taste. Sprinkle servings with chopped fresh parsley.

Submitter: 
Trina
Description: 
Pureed carrot soup is given a Thai kick with ginger, red curry and creamy coconut milk. Adjust the amount of red curry to cater to your level of spiciness.
