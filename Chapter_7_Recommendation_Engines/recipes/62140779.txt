Title:
Sweet and Spicy Sticky Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Chicken Wings
1 C. Soy Sauce
1 C. Granulated Sugar
1/2 C. Balsamic Vinegar
1/4 C. Asian Chili Garlic Sauce (like Sambal)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large ziplock bag
Marinade in refrigerator for at least 1 hour
Bake wings in a 450 degree preheated oven for 40 minutes
Remove wings from oven
Baste wings with any drippings or sauce from the pan and return to oven
Turn the oven to broil and broil wings for 3 minutes

Submitter: 
ANGELBEBEBOO
Description: 
With just 5 ingredients, this recipe is an Asian style spicy BBQ wings that easy to remember and simple to make.  The wings can be grilled or baked and make a delicious dish for potluck.
