Title:
Sausage and Broccoli stoup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salt and pepper
1 large head of broccoli(cut into 2inch peices)
2Tbsp evoo
1pd bulk hot or italian sausage
1 large onion,chopped
3 cloves galic, chopped
1/8tsp freahly grated nutmeg
1 32oz chicken stock
1 28oz can stewed or diced tomatoes, with there juices
1 15oz can white beans, rinsed
1 1/2C small, short-cut pasta
Grated ramano cheese, for topping

Directions:
Bring a medim pot of water to a boil, salt it, add the broccoli and boil for 3 minutes. Drain and let cool. In a soup pot, heat the evoo, over medium-high heat. Add the sausage and cook, crumbling with a spoon, until browned, 3-4minutes. Stir in the onion and cook until softened, about 5min. Season with the nutmeg and salt and pepper to taste. Add the chicken stock, tomatoes and the juices and 2 Cups water; cover and bring to a boil. Stir in the white beans and cooked broccoli and return to a boil. Add the pasta and cook stirring occadionally, until al dente. Top with the cheese.

Submitter: 
Libbie Remmel
Description: 
Very hearty soup! Great with a nice crusty bread!
