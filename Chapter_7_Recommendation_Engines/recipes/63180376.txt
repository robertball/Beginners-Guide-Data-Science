Title:
Baked Cream Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 garlic clove, minced
1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes (or any diced tomatoes)
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
2 teaspoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/8 teaspoon pepper
8 oz Cream Cheese
Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
In a nonstick skillet, cook ground beef, onion and garlic over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain. Add the tomatoes, tomato paste and seasonings. bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 1 hour or less. Meanwhile, cook Pasta according to package directions; drain.
Drain and place cooked spaghetti back in warm pot.  Add cream cheese, Italian Seasoning and minced garlic.  Stir until cream cheese is melted and the spaghetti is thoroughly coated. I add a little bit of Basil or Italian seasoning to this.
Lightly grease a 9x13" pan.  Spread a small amount of meat sauce in the bottom of the dish.  Put spaghetti on top of sauce and top with remaining meat sauce.  Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top.
