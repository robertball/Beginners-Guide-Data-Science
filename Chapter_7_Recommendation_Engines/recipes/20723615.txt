Title:
Chicken Waikiki Beach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup flour
1/3 cup oil
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
For the sauce:
1 can sliced or chunked pinneaple
1 cup sugar
2 tbsp cornstarch
3/4 cup cider vinegar
1tsp soy sauce
1/4 tsp ginger
1 chicken bouillon cube or 1 tsp granual chicken broth

Directions:
Wash chicken, pat dry.
coat chicken with flour, and brown on all sides in hot oil.
Move to shallow roasting pan and discard oil.
Sprinkle chicken with salt & pepper.
Preheat oven to 350F.
Make sauce: drain pineapple, pouring juice into 2 cup measuring cup.
Add water to make 1 1/4 cup of liquid.
In a medium saucepan, combine pineapple juice, sugar, cornstarch, vinegar, soy sauce, ginger & cube. Mix together untill well mixed.
Bring to a boil stirring constantly and simmer for 2 min.
Pour over the chicken and bake uncovered for 30 minutes.
Add pineapple chunks and bake for 30 more minutes, uncovered.
Serve over white rice.
