Title:
Cornbread Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510308.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag Cornbread Stuffing
1 box Chicken Stock
1 Stick of Margerine or Butter
1 egg
sauted Onion, Celery and Mushrooms
Pepper and Rosemary to taste

Directions:
Prepare Stuffing according to package directions
Add Egg and Sauted Vegetables
Season with Rosemary and Pepper
Bake in Baking dish for 25 to 30 mins
