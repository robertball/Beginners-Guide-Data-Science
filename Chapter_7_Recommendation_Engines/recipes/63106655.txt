Title:
Cheesy Chicken Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 13 oz. cans chicken breast
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup Velveeta cheese, cut into 1 cm cubes
1 Tbsp onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 dozen buns

Directions:
Shred chicken.
Put all ingredients into a crock-pot.
Cook on high for 2 hours or on low for 4 hours.
