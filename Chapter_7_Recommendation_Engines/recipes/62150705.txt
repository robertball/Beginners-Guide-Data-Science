Title:
Fiesta Ole Peppy Popcorn Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1074196.jpg
Ingredients:
3 oz butter
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp oregano
1/3 cup popped corn

Directions:
In a microwave safe bowl, add butter and all spices.
Cover and microwave for 1 minute.
Drizzle mixture over popped corn and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Jellybean
Description: 
This is a spice mix to give your popcorn an extra kick.  Also excellent as a dry rub for chicken too!
