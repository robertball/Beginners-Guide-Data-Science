Title:
Nanny's Newfoundland Tea Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups flour
5 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup butter
1 pinch salt
1 cup milk
3 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F and grease two baking sheets.
Mix flour, baking powder and raisins.
Cream together butter, sugar and salt in a separate bowl.
Beat eggs and milk.
Combine all ingredients.
Sprinkle flour onto countertop.
Flatten dough on counter with hands (doesn't need to be rolled or perfect...)
Use a juice glass to cut out biscuits.
Bake until golden brown and serve hot with butter and jam.

Submitter: 
carynliles
Description: 
My nanny used to make these tea biscuits all the time.  One of my favourite memories of visiting Newfoundland is standing at the kitchen table as she made these and gave us a little bit of the raw dough to taste. They're light and delicious!
