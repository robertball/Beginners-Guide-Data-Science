Title:
Cambodian Lemongrass Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/531553.jpg
Ingredients:
Soup Base:
4-6 stalks fresh lemongrass
1 head     garlic peeled
1/2 inch   ginger peeled
10         peppercorns
1          dry red chili
1 lime     zest and juice
2 lemons   zest and juice
4 drops    Fish Sauce (optional)
1 teas     soy sauce
4 cups     chicken broth/stock
Add to Soup Base after Strained:
6          green onions sliced thin
1/2 cup    mix of Thai basil, mint leaves, cilantro
to taste   chili-garlic paste
8 ozs      cooked chicken shedded/cubed
or  8 ozs  firm tofu cubed
optional   several dried chinese mushrooms
sliced cabbage/bok choy
mung bean sprouts
julienne carrot

Directions:
For the Soup Base:
Add the zest & liquids to a pot. Peel/trim leave off lemongrass till stalk is all green. Slice down the center of stalk for 2 halves. Cut in 3inch pieces. Put pieces in a sandwich bag and pound with hammer.
Fill bag with water and dump all in pot. Mince garlic, slice ginger thinly add to pot. Add peppercorns & chili to pot. Bring to boil then simmer for 20min. Use a small mesh strainer and pour soup base through. You want a clear liquid. Place liquid back in pot on med.
While soup is cooking: Cube cooked chicken/tofu, slice green onions, greens and optional ingredients.
When soup is strained and back in the pot add rest of ingredients to pot and simmer till veggies are tender. Add chili paste to each bowl then ladle soup on top, garnish with a green leaf.  Serve with warm bagette.

Submitter: 
KLemons
Description: 
My Khmer Sons both love this soup at our favorite Cambodian Restaurant Chez Sovan in San Jose, CA.
This is my attempt to match the flavors at home.
Can be made as vegan soup. Substitute Veggie broth and use tofu.
