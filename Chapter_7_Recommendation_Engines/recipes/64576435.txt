Title:
Pomegranate-Farro Breakfast Salad with Honey Ricotta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp part-skim ricotta cheese
1/2 tsp grated lemon or tangerine rind
1/2 tsp honey
1 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
1 tsp fresh lemon or tangerine juice
1/8 tsp kosher salt
2 cups fresh mache or baby spinach
1/3 cup cooked whole-grain farro
1/3 cup pomegranate arils
1 Tbsp coarsely chopped roasted almonds

Directions:
Combine first 3 ingredients in a small bowl.
Combine oil, juice, and salt in a medium bowl. Add greens and farro; toss to coat. Arrange greens mixture in a shallow bowl; top with pomegranate, almonds, and ricotta mixture.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
For a tangier citrus flavor, go with lemon; for more floral, sweet notes, try tangerine.
