Title:
zucchini blueberry whole wheat bead Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
2/3 cup white sugar
1 cup zucchini shredded
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp ground cinnamon
3/4 cup all purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour

Directions:
Combine oil, eggs, sugar, zucchini and mix well
In a separate bowl, combine baking soda, baking powder, flour, cinnamon and mix well. Slowly add to the first bowl containing the wet ingredients. Mix well.
carefully mix blueberries in
Put parchment paper on the bottom of a 9 x 5 inch loaf pan. Pour the batter.
Cook at 325 F for 55 to 60 min or until a toothpick comes out clean.
