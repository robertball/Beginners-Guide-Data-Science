Title:
Ham and Linguine Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992709.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces linguine or any pasta , uncooked
1 (16 ounce) package frozen broccoli florets
8 slices OSCAR MAYER Smoked Ham, cut into strips or chopped
1/2 cup MIRACLE WHIP Salad Dressing
1/4 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/4 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper (optional)

Directions:
Prepare pasta as directed on package, adding broccoli during last 4 minutes of pasta cooking time. Drain; return to pan.
Add remaining ingredients; toss to coat.
