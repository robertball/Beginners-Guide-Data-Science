Title:
Chunky Bleu Cheese Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894508.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup buttermilk
1/2 teaspoon white pepper
2 tablespoons grated onions
4 dashes Tabasco sauce
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cash cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon minced garlic
2 teaspoons sugar
6 ounces crumbled bleu cheese

Directions:
Combine mayonnaise, sour cream, and buttermilk, mixing well
Add remaining ingredients mixing thoroughly
Cover and refrigerate for at least 24 hours before serving for best flavor

Description:
