Title:
Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup margarine
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
3 cups quick or old fashioned, uncooked oats
3/4 cups raisins
3/4 cups craisins
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Heat oven to 35o.  In large bowl, beat butter and sugars on medium speed of electric mixer until creamy.  Add eggs and vainilla; beat well.  Add combineed flour, baking soda, and salt; mix well.  Add oats raisins, craisins, nuts; mix well.  Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light golden brown.
Cool 1 minute on cookie sheets; remove to wire rack.
Cool completely.  Store tightly covered.   4 dozen
