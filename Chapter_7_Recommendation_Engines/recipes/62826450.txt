Title:
Kayda's Shepard's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked ground hamburger or ground sirloin
1 1/2 cups white gravy
1 can whole kernel corn, drained
1 can green beans, drained
4-5 large potatoes
8 tbs butter
1 cup milk
6 slices bacon, cooked and crubled
1 cup cheese, shredded and divided
2 tbsp Montreal Chicken Seasoning

Directions:
Combine ground meat and gravy in bottom of baking dish.
Top with green beans and corn.
Cook potatoes until soft, mash with butter and milk.
Stir in bacon, 1/2 cup cheese, and montreal chicken seasoning with the potatoes.
Top meat and potato mixture with potatoes.
Cover with glass lid or foil and bake at 350 for 30 mins.
Remove from oven, top with remaining 1/2 cup cheese.
Return to oven, uncovered, for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
techgurl11
Description: 
My daughter doesn't like traditional shepard's pie, so I modified a basic recipe until I found one she (and the rest of the family) loves!  They devour every bite!  It also freezes well, I sometimes make up a double batch and freeze one for a busy day.
