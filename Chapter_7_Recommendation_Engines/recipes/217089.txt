Title:
Death of the Red Ant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
ice
1 1/2 fluid ounces rye whiskey
1/2 fluid ounce cherry liqueur (such as Cherry Heering®)
1/2 fluid ounce kirschwasser
1 tablespoon mezcal
2 dashes chocolate or mole bitters (such as Bittermens® or Fee Brothers®)
maraschino cherries, for garnish

Directions:
Put ice in a cocktail shaker. Pour in whiskey, Cherry Heering, and kirschwasser. Then spoon in the mezcal and add a couple dashes of bitters. Shake well and strain into a cocktail glass. Garnish with cherries.

Submitter: 
Lorem Ipsum
Description: 
Based on the Red Ant cocktail created by a NYC bartender. Be sure to use a good quality mezcal. The small amount of it here makes a big difference. I like brandied cherries for the garnish.
