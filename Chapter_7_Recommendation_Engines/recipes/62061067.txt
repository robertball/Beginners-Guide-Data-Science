Title:
Snow Capped Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups peeled carrots, sliced 1/4 inch on the diagonal
1/4 tsp. salt
1/3 cup cooking liquid from carrots
2 Tb. butter
1/4 cup minced onion
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 tsp celery salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 Tb. worcestershire sauce
Topping:
6 large potatoes cooked in salted water until soft
1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese softened
1 cup cheddar cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1 egg
1-2 tsp salt (to taste)
1/2 tsp. pepper
parsely flakes

Directions:
Cook carrots in water with 1/4 tsp. salt until tender. Drain, reserving 1/3 cup of the cooking liquid. While the carrots are cooking, saute the onion and butter in a skillet until soft. Add ground beef, celery salt, pepper and worcestershire sauce. Saute' until browned. Add the carrots and the reserved carrot liquid to the meat mixture. Place in a casserole dish. Top with potato topping. Bake 350* for 25-30 minutes, until the topping begins to brown on the edges.
To Make Topping:
Cook the potatoes in salted water until tender. Drain and mash. Using a mixer, add remaining ingredients and combine until smooth. Add parsely flakes for color. Instant potatoes may be used, but who would want to?

Submitter: 
SharFras
Description: 
Savory ground beef with worcestershire sauce, onions and carrots topped with cheesy mashed potatoes. A modern twist on the traditional shepherds pie.
