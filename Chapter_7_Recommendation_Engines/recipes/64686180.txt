Title:
Mexi-Broccoli Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 heaping cups of bagged broccoli slaw mix
1 jar (almost 2 cups) of Salsa con Queso (cheese dip with green chiles)
1/2 cup sour cream
1 tsp chili powder
1  1/2  cups Tex Mex Cheese Blend
2 cups taco, corn or doritos chips
1 bunch green onions, chopped

Directions:
Put slaw mix in large bowl.
Mix Salsa con Queso with sour cream and chili powder and then stir into slaw, mixing well.
Sprinkle Tex Mex Cheese blend on top followed by chips and green onions.

Submitter: 
Phoebe
Description: 
Forget the mayo, this will work well alongside your tacos or enchiladas or whatever Mexican fare you have prepared.
