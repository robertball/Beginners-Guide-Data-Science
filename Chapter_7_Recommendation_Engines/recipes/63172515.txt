Title:
Great-Grandma's Hawaiin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Graham cracker crusts
1 large can crushed pineapple
1 cup nuts
2 Tbsp. juice or 1 lemon
9 oz. frozen cool whip, thawed
1 can Eagle brand milk

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in large bowl thoroughly. Divide into 2 pie crusts; put in freezer.
Take out 15 mins. before serving or use frozen.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe has been passed down in my family for generations. Delicious summer pie!
