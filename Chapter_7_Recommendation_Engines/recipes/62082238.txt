Title:
Catalina Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs cooked ground beef seasoned to taste
1 head of lettuce shredded
1 can kidney beans drained and rinsed
1/2 cup shredded mexican style cheese blend
Half a bottle of catalina dressing (or to taste)
Crushed tortilla chips for garnish

Directions:
In a large salad bowl mix lettuce, ground beef, kidney beans and cheese.  Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat completely.  Serve immediately and top with crushed tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
GABESGIRL
Description: 
Delicious taco salad made with ground beef, kidney beans, cheese and cruchy tortilla chips all tossed with tangy catalina dressing.
