Title:
See's Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1072905.jpg
Ingredients:
½	pound margarine
5	ounces marshmallow cream
3	packages milk chocolate chips
2 ½	cups nuts (optional)
4 ½	cups sugar
1	can evaporated milk
½	teaspoon salt

Directions:
1.	In a large bowl combine chips, margarine, marshmallow cream and nuts.  Set aside.
2.	In medium saucepan combine sugar, milk, and salt.  Heat to boiling over medium high heat.  Boil together 8 minutes.
3.	Remove from heat and add to ingredients in bowl.  Stir until all chips are melted and smooth.  Spread on cookie sheet and let cool completely before cutting.
