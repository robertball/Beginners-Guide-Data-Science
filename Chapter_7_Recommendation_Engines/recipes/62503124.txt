Title:
oven pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup milk
2 eggs slightly beaten
1/4 teaspoon salt
pinch nutmeg
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup of such as blueberries
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup power sugar

Directions:
In a medium bowl blend together the first 5 ingredients
Place the butter in a 12 inch oven proof pan and place in the oven until the butter is melted/ semi boiling. When the butter is very hot pour in the blueberries, then the batter. bake in a 450 oven for 10 min CONVECTION or until golden brown sprinkle with power sugar.
