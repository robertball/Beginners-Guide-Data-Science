Title:
Leftover Thanksgiving Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oil or shortening or bacon drippings
1 cup flour
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped green onions
1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup chopped celery
1 gallon of stock, made from turkey carcass
1 T chopped garlic
1/2 t crushed dried mint
3 cups sliced okra
1 pound smoked sausage, cut bite sized
leftover turkey meat, chopped bite sized
Louisiana hot sauce or cayenne, to taste
1 T Worcestershire
Salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in large, heavy pot over medium heat.
Add the flour & make a dark roux (this will take about 45 minutes with constant stirring).
To the roux add onions, green onions, bell pepper, parsley and celery.
Cook until the onions are clear.
Add 1 cup of the stock and stir to form a thick paste.
Stir in the garlic, then the remaining stock.
Stir in the mint, okra, sausage and turkey.
Season with hot sauce, Worcestershire and salt; stir to mix well.
Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 3 hours.
Serve over cooked rice.
