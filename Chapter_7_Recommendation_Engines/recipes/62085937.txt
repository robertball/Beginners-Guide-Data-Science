Title:
Pecan Pie Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup flour
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup pecans

Directions:
Cream butter and eggs until creamy.  Mix dry ingredients and add to creamy mixture.  Fill ingredients in to mini muffin pan.  Bake at 350 for 10 to 12 minutes.

Description:
