Title:
Peanut Butter & Banana Popsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe bananas
3 large spoonfulls creamy peanut butter
milk

Directions:
place bananas and peanut butter in a blender.
fill blender with milk about halfway up the ingredients.
blend until smooth
pour into popsicle molds
freeze

Submitter: 
Sarah Rose
Description: 
A delicious and healthy way for kids (and adults) who love peanut butter to cool off in the summer!
