Title:
Easy Meatball Parm Hero Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 cups PregoÂ® Traditional Italian Sauce
16 (1 ounce) frozen cooked meatballs
4 long hard rolls, split
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
Grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat sauce and meatballs in 3 quart saucepan over medium-high heat to a boil. Reduce heat to low. Cook for 20 minutes or until meatballs are heated through, stirring occasionally.
Serve meatballs and sauce in rolls. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.

Submitter:

Description: 
Ready-made meatballs simmered in tomato-mushroom sauce, topped with mozzarella, fill crusty rolls to create these hearty supper sandwiches.
