Title:
Saffron Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked long-grain rice
3/4 teaspoon crushed saffron threads
1 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
6 whole cardamom seeds
4 whole cloves
3 cinnamon sticks
1 onion, chopped
3 cups boiling vegetable broth
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Cover rice with cold water and set aside to soak for 30 minutes.
Soak saffron threads in 2 tablespoons boiling water.
Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat; add cardamom, cloves and cinnamon and fry 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Stir in onion and saute, stirring occasionally, until golden brown.  Stir in the rice, reduce heat to low and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
Pour in the boiling broth and stir in the salt and saffron.
Cover and cook until rice is cooked and all liquid is absorbed, about 40 minutes.
