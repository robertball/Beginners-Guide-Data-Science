Title:
Crock Pot Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lb. brisket, trimmed
2 tsp. sea salt
2 tsp. dry mustard
2 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. fresh ground pepper
1 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Combine seasonings and rub into both sides of the brisket. Place in the crock pot with fat side up. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Hennes
Description: 
This is a wonderfully easy and tasteful recipe for tender brisket.
