Title:
Corn Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Cream Style Corn
2 TBL Flour
2 TBL Butter
1 1/2 TBL Brown Sugar
2 eggs
Doubled Recipe - Add an Extra Egg.

Directions:
Preheat oven 350F
Mix ingredients.
Pour into a greased casserole.
Bake until set in the center, about 60 mins
