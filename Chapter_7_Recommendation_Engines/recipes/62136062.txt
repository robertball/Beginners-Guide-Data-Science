Title:
Cracked Peanut Brittle - Cooked in the microwave Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups granulated sugar
2 cup light corn syrup
1/2 cup water
2 cups salted peanuts or pecans
2 tsp. butter or margarine
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
The bowl will get very hot, so be sure to wear cooking goves while doing this.
Combine sugar, syrup and water in a 2 quart microwave safe glass bowl.  Cook uncovered in microwave for 6 minutes on high, or until sugar is dissolved.
Stir the peanuts/pecans into the syrup.  Cook uncovered in microwave for 8 minutes on high. Stirring every 4 minutes.
Stir butter into mixture.  Cook uncovered to hard cracked stage for 8 to 10 minutes.  Stir every 3 to 4 minutes.
Add baking soda & vanilla and mix well(it will foam).  Pour onto a lightly buttered, large cookie sheet.  Spread as thinly as possible.
When firm & cool, break & store in tightly covered container.
