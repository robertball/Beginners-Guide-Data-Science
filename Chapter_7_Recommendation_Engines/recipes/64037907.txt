Title:
Sauerbraten - German Pickled Beef Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1122392.jpg
Ingredients:
MARINADE INGREDIENTS:
1 1/2 C white vinegar
1/2 C water
1 medium onion, diced
1 medium carrot, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
12 whole black peppercorns
6-8 whole cloves
6 whole allspice
3 bay leaves
1 C dry red wine (add when marinade has cooled!)
4 Lbs boneless beef roast, bottom round
ROASTING/SAUCE INGREDIENTS:
1 med onion, diced
1 med carrot, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
1/2 C water
Reserved Marinade
1/2 C gingersnap cookies, crumbled/powdered

Directions:
Place beef in bowl slightly larger than the meat.
Bring spices, water & vinegar to a boil. Remove from heat and cool. Add wine & pour over meat. Cover and chill 3-5 days, turning twice daily.
One -2 hrs prior to cooking, drain the marinade from the meat and discard vegetables - reserve marinade. Place on a plate to drain and warm up.
Preheat oven to 350F.  Place roast in a HOT Dutch oven. Brown beef well in 2 Tbls of oil, on all sides - about 30 mins.
Remove roast and saut√© new vegetables until tender. Return the roast pour-in the reserved marinade, cover and cook about 3 hours.
Transfer beef to carving board and keep warm.
Puree marinade and vegetables in blender and add Ginger Snap crumbs. Return to Dutch oven and boil until thick, stirring often, about 6 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Slice beef and arrange on platter. Pour sauce over meat.

Submitter:

Description: 
Traditionally, sauerbraten is served with dumplings, boiled potatoes,or spaetzle, and red cabbage. This classic, but easy, recipe requires advance planning and time (3-5 days!), but it has a flavor and aroma that is incredible.
