Title:
Yummy Berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups mixed berries
1 cup yogurt
1/4-1 cup milk (depending on desired thickness)
1 1/2 cups dry milk powder
1/4 cup berry juice CONCENTRATE (any flavor)
3 ice cubes

Directions:
put all ingredients in blender.
blend for 10-15 minutes.
Enjoy!!
