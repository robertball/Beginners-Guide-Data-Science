Title:
Sugar Cookie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460299.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 cup butter
8 oz. cream cheese, soft
1 egg
1/2 tsp almond extract
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
2 1/2 cups flour
Frosting:
1/2 cup soft butter
4 oz. cream cheese, soft
3 1/2 cups powder sugar
3-5 Tbl milk
1 tsp vanilla
food coloring

Directions:
Beat the butter, sugar, cream cheese, and egg until well mixed (about 4 min.) Add vanilla and almond extract and mix.  Add dry ingredients (flour, soda, and powder.)
grease an 18x13x1 baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 min.
let completely cool
frost with a fun color, cut into squares(6x8=48 cookies) and decorate with white frosting in a ziploc bag with the corner tip cut off.
