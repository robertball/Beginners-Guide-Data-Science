Title:
Company's Coming Pumpkin Chiffon Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
14 oz. canned pumpkin, without spices
1 (.25 oz) pkg unflavored gelatin
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
3/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/4 tsp. salt
3 egg yolks
3 egg whites, room temp.
6 Tbsp. granulated sugar
1 cup whipping cream
1 baked pie shell

Directions:
Heat milk and pumpkin in saucepan until it boils.
Sprinkle gelatin over water in small cup. Let stand for 1 min. Stir into pumpkin mixture. Return to a boil.
Mix next 6 ingredients in a small bowl.
Stir into boiling mixture and remove from heat.
Cool, then chill until it's a thick syrupy mixture that will mound. Beat egg whites in a small bowl until a stiff froth.
Add granulated sugar gradually, beating until stiff and sugar is dissolved.Fold into chilled mixture.
Using same beaters and bowl, beat whipping cream until stiff. Fold in. Turn into pie shell. Chill.
