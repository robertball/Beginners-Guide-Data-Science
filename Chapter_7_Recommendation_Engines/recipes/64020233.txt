Title:
Venezuelan Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can black beans, drained
1 teaspoon salt
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon oregano
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 large lime, juiced
2 cups hot cooked white rice
1 large tomato, diced
1 large scallion, chopped

Directions:
In a large saucepan with a lid, saute onions and garlic in oil over medium-high heat until tender. Reduce heat to medium-low and add beans and salt. Stir in cumin, oregano, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes, covered.  Stir in lime.  Place rice in serving bowls. Pour bean soup over rice. Tope with diced tomatoes and scallions.

Submitter: 
Laurel Patton
Description: 
This soup recipe is authentic enough that my Venezuelan exchange student said it tasted just like his mom's.
