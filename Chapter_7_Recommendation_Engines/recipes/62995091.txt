Title:
Crispy Garlic Breadsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 day-old hot dog buns
1/2 C butter or margarine, melted
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Split buns in half and cut each half lengthwise. Combine butter and garlic and brush over breadsticks.
Place on a greased baking sheet. Bake in a preheated 325 degree oven for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
A surprisingly easy way to use day-old hot dog buns. They are a nice companion to a meal or a crunchy snack.
