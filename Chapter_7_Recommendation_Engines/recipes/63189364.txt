Title:
Oven BBQ Chicken Drumsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup water
1/3 cup ketchup
1/3 cup white vinegar
1/4 brown sugar
4 tsp butter/margarine
2-4 tsp Worchestershire sauce (no need to be exact)
2 tsp salt
2 tsp dry/ground mustard
2-4 tsp chili powder (again, no need to be exact)
6 chicken drumsticks

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees F.
Mix ingredients together (except for chicken).
Put drumsticks into a baking dish and pour sauce on top.
Cover chicken and put into oven for 1 hour, turning over halfway through.

Submitter: 
Nichole S
Description: 
A wonderful chicken recipe that can either be nice and gentle, or have a bit of a kick!
