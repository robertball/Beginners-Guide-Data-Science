Title:
Dandelion Cookies III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6493546.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Stick of Butter
1 Cup Honey
2 Eggs
1 Tablespoon Vanilla
1 Cups Flour
1 Cup Acorn Starch (Could use just all purpose flour)
1 Cups Old Fashioned Oats
1 Tablespoon Baking soda
1/2 Cup Dandelion Petals

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Melt the butter, set aside allowing it to cool
Remove all green stems and brackets from dandelion flowers, set aside
Mix all dry ingredients in a medium size bowl
Mix the dandelion flowers with the honey and butter
Add eggs and vanilla and stir well
Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture and combine well
Place generous tablespoon fulls on to a baking sheet
Bake for 12 minutes or until golden brown on the edges

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Fun way to welcome spring with a sweet Dandelion Cookie activity with the kids!  My daughter loves making these and eating them with me even more!
