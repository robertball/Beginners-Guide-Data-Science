Title:
Chocolate Avocado Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 large avocados - peeled, pitted, and cubed
6 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
5 tbsp maple syrup or honey
1/3 to 1/2 cup almond or coconut milk
1 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 pinch e@ ground cinnamon and salt

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth. Dribble in more "milk" if necessary to get the right consistancy.  Refrigerate pudding until chilled, about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
LauriePat
Description: 
Creamy, low sugar vegan pudding.  You won't taste the avocado!Modified from: Chocolate Avocado Pudding
