Title:
Awesome Pina Colada Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
2 c. sugar
1 c. crushed pineapple
2 eggs
1 tsp. baking soda
dash salt
1 c. coconut
1 stick butter
5 oz. can evaporated milk
1 c. sugar

Directions:
Mix the first 6 ingredients and place in a greased 9X13 pan at 350 degrees for 35 minutes or til done.  In saucepan, mix butter, sugar, evaporated milk - bring to a boil and boil for 1 min.  Add coconut.  Pour over hot cake.
