Title:
Bacon Horseradish Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989190.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) container BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1 (2.8 ounce) package OSCAR MAYER Bacon Pieces
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon KRAFT Prepared Horseradish
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
RITZ Crackers

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except crackers until well blended; cover.
Refrigerate several hours or until chilled.
Serve with the crackers.

Submitter: 
RITZ
Description: 
Horseradish and hot pepper sauce add some heat to this zesty sour cream and bacon dip.
