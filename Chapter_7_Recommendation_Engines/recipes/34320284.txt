Title:
Chocolate Covered Strawberry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chocolate chips
1/4 cup oil
1 package chocolate cake mix
3/4 cup water
3 eggs
1 jar strawberry preserves
1 cup chocolate chips
1 quart strawberries
1/3 cup melted vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Brush pan with oil. Combine chocolate chips and oil, then melt, stirring until smooth. Combine cake mix, eggs, water, 1/2 cup preserves, and melted chocolate. Stir well to blend. Pour into pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 - 50 minutes.
Invert cake onto serving plate. Let cake stand with pan for 10 minutes. Remove pan.
Combine melted ice cream and chocolate chips, heat, stirring until smooth.
Melt remaining preserves, and brush onto cake.
Dip strawberries into chocolate mixture, and use them to decorate the plate. Slice any remaining strawberries, and add them to the center of the cake.
Drizzle cake with melted chocolate mixture.
Serve with vanilla ice cream.
