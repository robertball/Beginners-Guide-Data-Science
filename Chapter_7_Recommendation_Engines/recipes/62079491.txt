Title:
Chicken with Creamy Mushroom Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breast fillets
Plain Flour
30g Butter
2 Cups of chopped mushrooms
1 tbs Port
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/2 cup of cream
1 tbs chopped fresh chives

Directions:
Toss Chicken in flour; shake away excess flour
Heat butter in large fry pan, add chicken.  Cook until browned on both sides.  Remove chicken from pan; drain.
Add mushrooms to same pan, cook stirring until sof.  Stir in port, wine,chicken stock.  Return chicken to pan, bring to boil, simmer, covered for 10 minutes or until chicken is tender.
Remove chicken from pan; keep warm.
Add cream to pan, boil 1 minute or until sauce is reduced slightly.
Stir in chices.  Serve chicken with sauce.
