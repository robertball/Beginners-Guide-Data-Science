Title:
Banana Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Bananas

Directions:
Peel bananas . and cut them into slices. Put the slices in a freezer bag and put the bag in the freezer for an hour.
Take it out and empty the bag into a blender. Blend until creamy like ice cream.
Scoop in a bowl and serve.
