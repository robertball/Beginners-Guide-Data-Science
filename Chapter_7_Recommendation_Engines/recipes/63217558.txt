Title:
salmon burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# can salmon, drained
1 egg, beaten
3/4 c. quick oats, uncooked
1/2 c. chopped onion
1 T. lemon juice
1 tsp. mustard
salt and pepper

Directions:
combine all ingredients; form 4 patties.  Pan fry.  Serve on a bun with lettuce, tomato.
