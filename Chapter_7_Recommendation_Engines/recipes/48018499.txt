Title:
A Good Easy Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves (about 16 oz.)
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 cloves of garlic
1/2 white onion
1 cup of Chicken Broth
2 cups egg noodles
juice from 1/2 a lemon
3 tablespoons flour
= 7 pts. per serving

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium high heat. Dredge chicken in garlic powder and flour. Add garlic and onion. Saute until chicken is browned.  Add chicken broth and simmer until chicken is cooked. about 15 minutes. Serve over egg noodles.
After chicken is cooked, add flour to pan to create a gravy.
