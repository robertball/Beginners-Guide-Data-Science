Title:
Meltaway Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. Butter or Margerine
1/2 c. Sifted spconfectioners sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 1/4 c. Softasilk Cake Flour
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F (mod. Hot).  Mix butter, confectioners sugar, and vanilla thoroughly.  Measure flour by spooning into cup and leveling off or by sifting.  Stir flour and salt together; blend into shortening mixture.  Drop by teaspoonfuls on ungreased baking sheet.  (Cookies do not spread.). Bake about 8 min., or until set but not brown.  While warm, dip in confectioners' sugar.  Cool.  Makes 5 dozen cookies.
