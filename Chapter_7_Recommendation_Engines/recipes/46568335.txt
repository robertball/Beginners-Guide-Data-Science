Title:
Brunch Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked ham, chopped INTO 1/2" CUBES
3/4 cup sliced green onions
3/4 cup chopped RED AND green bell peppers (LESS GREEN PEPPERS, ADD FRESH GREEN CHILES)
3 T. BUTTER
3-4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided  (USE MEXICAN BLEND)
10 (7 inch) flour tortillas
5 eggs, beaten (6-7 LARGE EGGS)
2 cups half-and-half cream (1 1/2 C. CREAM)
1/2 cup milk  (USE 1/2 C. SOUR CREAM)
SALT AND PEPPER
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour  (UNNECESSARY)
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder  (OMIT)
1 dash hot pepper sauce

Directions:
IN SKILLET, MELT BUTTER, SAUTE HAM, PEPPERS AND ONIONS A FEW MINUTES TILL SOFTENED.  Spoon 1/3 cup of the ham mixture and 3 tablespoons shredded cheese onto each tortilla, then roll up. Carefully place filled tortillas, seam side down, in a greased 9x13 baking dish.
In a medium bowl, mix together eggs, cream,  1 CUP CHEESE BLEND, SOUR CREAM, SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE and hot pepper sauce.  Pour egg mixture over tortillas.  Cover, and refrigerate overnight.
The next morning, preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bake, uncovered, in preheated oven for (35-50) minutes, or until set. Sprinkle casserole with remaining 1 cup shredded cheese. Bake about 3 minutes more, or until cheese melts. Let stand a least 10 minutes before serving.

Enchiladas filled with ham, vegetables and cheese and baked in a creamy egg batter.Modified from: Brunch Enchiladas
