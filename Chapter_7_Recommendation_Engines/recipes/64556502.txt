Title:
Tomatoes With Basil Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C olive oil
1/2 C finely chopped fresh parsley
1/4 C finely chopped fresh basil
2 T white wine vinegar
2 T lemon juice
1 t sugar
1/2 t salt
1/2 t ground white pepper
5 large tomatoes, sliced 1/4-in thick
1 large sweet onion, thinly sliced

Directions:
In small jar, combine all ingredients except tomatoes and onions
Cover; shake to combine
Refrigerate at least 2 hours
Just before serving, arrange tomato slices and onion rings on serving platter
Drizzle dressing over to

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Good recipe to use up those fresh garden tomatoes and herbs
