Title:
Keto Chocolate Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6361947.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup heavy whipping cream*
2 tablespoons dark cocoa powder
½ teaspoon espresso powder
½ teaspoon almond extract
1 tablespoon powdered stevia or alternative low-calorie sweetener
Pinch of fine sea salt
*or substitute canned full-fat coconut cream, refrigerated overnight, cream only from top of can
Optional:
¼ cup unsweetened dark chocolate chips, to garnish
Additional cocoa or espresso powder, to garnish

Directions:
Combine whipping cream or coconut cream, cocoa powder, espresso powder, vanilla, sweetener, and salt in a large mixing bowl.
Using an electric mixer, beat until mixture is light and fluffy and holds its form.
If desired, serve with a 1 teaspoon chocolate chips, sprinkled cocoa or espresso.

A decadent, creamy dessert that's low in carbs and ready in minutes!
