Title:
Cedar Planked Brie with Roasted Berries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium Brie cheese (single cream)
1 pint mixed berries, fresh (raspberries, blueberries, blackberries)
Orange blossom honey, or favorite
Olive Oil
Sea salt
Smoked black pepper
1 French baguette
Cedar plank
Honeycomb (if desired)

Directions:
Preheat grill to 450 degrees.
Place cedar plank into water and soak 30 minutes to one hour.
Drizzle olive oil on the Brie cheese and rub over the rind.
Season with sea salt and smoked black pepper.
In a small bowl, lightly coat berries with olive oil.
Place cheese on plank.  Place berries on the Brie and drizzle with honey.
Place Brie on grill with cover down until it warms through, approximately 20 minutes.
Serve Brie with honey comb and toasted baguette.
