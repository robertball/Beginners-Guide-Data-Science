Title:
3 layer carrot cake with lemon buttercream frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/854906.jpg
Ingredients:
CAKE
1 lb. carrots, peeled,cooked, cut 1/2" pieces
1 cup corn oil
1 8oz. unsweetened pineapple, drained
4 large eggs
1 tbsp. vanilla
3 cup unbleached flour
2 1/2 cups sugar
1 tbsp. grated cinnamon
1 tbsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups coarse chopped walnuts
FROSTING
1 lb. cream cheese, room temperature
2 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
1 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
2 tsp. vanilla
5 2/3 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
*butter 3 - 9"cake pan sides.  Cut wax paper bottoms, butter. Place inside
*mix all cake ingredients together including  carrots in food processor.  Blend until smooth.  You can do this in batches if your processor is not big enough.
*pour 1/3's of mixture into each pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for approx. 35 minutes.
*mix butter, lemon, cream cheese, vanilla.  Gradually add sugar.  Beat until smooth

Submitter: 
Roland G. Mandish
Description: 
This is one of my all time favorite carrot cake recipes.  I inherited this recipe from a close friend about 12 years ago and it's still a favorite!
