Title:
Mom Hanson's Minnesotan Cranberry Nut Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup orange juice
2 tablespoons hot water
1 1/2 cups whole cranberries
1/2 cup crushed walnuts
2 tablespoons melted butter
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Blend egg with sugar. Add melted butter, orange juice, and hot water.
Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Add to egg mixture.
Fold in cranberries and nuts. Pour into greased and floured loaf pan.
Bake at 325 F for 1 hour and 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
This easy to make simple bread is the perfect breakfast or after school treat. Not too sweet (or tart!) it's great by itself. If you'd like to eat it like a true Northerner? Spread a little butter or margarine on top of your slice before eating!
