Title:
Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chopped, cooked leftover lamb
1 cup chopped onion
2 tablespoons ketchup
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 cup frozen peas, thawed
1 teaspoon beef bouillon granules or 1 cube
1/2 cup boiling water
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1/2 cup sour cream
3-1/2 cups mashed potatoes (prepared with milk and butter)
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Make mashed potatoes and set aside. Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Heat oil in skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion and meat, cooking for about 5 minutes until onion is tender.
Stir in ketchup, Worcestershire sauce and garlic
When onion is tender and meat is browned, stir in peas. Reduce heat and last simmer.
Combine bouillon granules or cube and water  in a 1 cup glass measure and microwave 1 minute to dissolve. Stir cornstarch into bouillon until blended and add to skillet. Stir well, and simmer, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat and stir in sour cream.  Pour into casserole dish.
Spread potatoes over meat mixture all the way to the edge of the casserole dish.
Sprinkle with cheese and heat in oven until hot and bubbly and cheese begins to brown.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
This is best made with left over lamb roast.  You can also use ground lamb or even ground beef (but then it's Cottage Pie not Shepherd's Pie)
