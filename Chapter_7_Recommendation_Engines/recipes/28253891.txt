Title:
Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of ground beef
1 29 oz of tomato sauce
1 6 oz can of tomato paste
1 cup of water
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. chili pepper
1 tsp. onion salt
1 tsp. garlic salt
2 tablespoons of chopped parsley
1 box of spaghetti

Directions:
Brown ground beef in a 3 qt. pot.
Add salt, pepper, chili pepper, onion salt, garlic salt, & parsley and stir.
Add tomato sauce, paste, & water.
Place a lid on the pot and cook on medium heat for 10 minutes.
Stir ocassionally.
Reduce the heat to low and replace the lid.
Allow sauce to simmer until spaghetti noodles are done.
In a separate 4 qt. pot bring 2 1/2 qts of water to a boil.
Add spaghetti noodles to the boiling water.
Do not cover with a lid.
Allow spaghetti to cook until they are white and limp.
