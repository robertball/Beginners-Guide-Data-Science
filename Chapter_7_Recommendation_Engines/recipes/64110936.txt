Title:
Corn Fritters with Raspberry Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/4 c. milk
1 c. Bisquick
1 can whole kernel corn, drained
Black pepper
1 C. mayonnaise
1/4 C. raspberry preserves

Directions:
Blend together egg, milk, and Bisquick. Stir in corn. Add pepper to taste.
In a frying pan, heat 2 inches vegetable oil. Using 2 teaspoons, gently drop a rounded teaspoon of fritter batter into hot oil. Fry 6-8 fritters at a time, turning until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
Raspberry mayonnaise: Heat preserves in a saucepan over low heat until melted. Remove from heat and mix with mayonnaise.
