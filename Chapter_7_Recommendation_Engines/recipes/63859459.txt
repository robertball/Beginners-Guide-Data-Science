Title:
Best Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1071124.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken cut into pieces, 3 eggs beaten, 4 Tbsp. oil, 2 cups flour, 4 tsp.paprika, 2 1/2 tsp. salt, 1 tsp. pepper, 1 tsp. poultry seasoning, 1 tsp. thyme, 1 tsp. oregano, 1 tsp. tarragon, 1/2 tsp.garlic salt, 1/2 tsp. onion salt, 1/2 tsp. celery salt.

Directions:
Sift together all the coating ingredients and place in a large plastic bag. coat each piece of chicken with egg then coat in mixture in bag. MAKE SURE TO COAT EACH PIECE COMPLETELY. Heat oil in a skillet and slowly brown chicken (uncovered) on both sides. Once browned, cover skillet and keep frying slow until chicken is fully cooked. Place on paper towel to drain excess oil
