Title:
Pickled Asparagus from Hi-Berry farm Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb asparagus
3 garlic cloves
6 tsp dill seeds
6 tsp mustard seeds
36 black peppercorns
8 cups water
2 1/2 cups white vinegar
3 tbsp pickling salt
1/2 cup granulated sugar

Directions:
Rinse and trim asparagus to fit pint size jars
Soak in ice water
Add following to each jar:
1/2 clove garlic
1 tsp dill seed
1 tsp mustard seed
6 peppercorns
Bring water, vinegar, salt and sugar to a boil
Put the asparagus in the jars and fill with the hot liquid to cover leaving 1/2 inch headspace. Seal and process, either hot water for 5 tp 6 minutes or pressure canner.
