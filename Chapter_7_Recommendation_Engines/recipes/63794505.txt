Title:
Guinness Beer Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick (1/2 cup) unsalted butter
1/2 cup flour
3 cups milk
2 cans (14 1/2 ounces each) chicken broth
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 can or bottle of Guinness
1 pound sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
hot sauce to taste
1/2 teaspoon salt
freshly ground pepper to taste
1/2 pound smoked sausage, diced or cooked rotisserie chicken, diced
4 green onions, thinly sliced

Directions:
1. Combine butter and flour in a large saucepan over medium heat; cook, whisking, until mixture is golden, about 5 minutes.  Slowly whisk in milk; cook stirring until thick about 5 minutes.  Whisk in chicken broth; add garlic and beer. Reduce heat to low.  Cook, stirring often, 30 minutes.
2.  Stir in cheese; cook, stirring, until cheese melts, about 5 minutes.  Stir in hot sauce, salt and pepper to taste.  Garnish with sausage or cooked chicken and green onions.
