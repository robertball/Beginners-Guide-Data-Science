Title:
Mae's Hearty Beef Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Lean ground beef
1 cup Pearl barley (washed)
2 Tbs. Dehydrated onion flakes
1 15oz. Can diced tomatoes w/peppers and onions
1 16oz. Bag frozen country vegetables (or 1 1/4 cups ea. corn, cut green beans, and carrot medallions)
1 1/4 cups Frozen peas
4 to 5 medium russet potatoes
1 1/2 tsp. Gebhardts chili powder
2 medium sized Bay Leaves(my secret ingredient)
2 cloves of garlic(minced)
1 tsp. salt (more to taste)
1/2 to 3/4 tsp. black pepper
4 quarts water
10 beef boullion cubes (crushed)

Directions:
Brown ground beef using a pinch of salt and pepper to season.
Add dehydrated onions, garlic, barley and 2 quarts of water.  Bring to boil then reduce flame.
Add remaining salt and pepper, chili powder, boullion and bay leaves. Simmer 15 minutes.
Add tomatoes and frozen vegetables and remaining water. Simmer 1 1/2 hours, stirring every 15 or 20 minutes.
(While the soup is simmering, peel potatoes and dice into 1/2 inch cubes.)
After 1  1/2    hours slowly add diced potatoes to avoid splashing. Stir.  Simmer 30 more minutes or until potatoes are soft and soup is thick.
Best served with hot crusty French bread.

Submitter: 
Mae Long
Description: 
A hearty soup full of vegetables and a special flavor secret to satisfy any palate.
