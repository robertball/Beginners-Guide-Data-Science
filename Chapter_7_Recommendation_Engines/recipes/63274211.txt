Title:
Ausukes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. shortening
2/3 c. sugar
2 eggs
1/2 c. milk
1 t. vanilla
3 c. flour
3 1/2 t. baking powder
1/2 t. salt

Directions:
Cream together shortening and sugar.
Add eggs and beat well.
Add milk and vanilla and mix well.
Sift together remaining ingredients and add to above mixture.
Chill dough and then roll out onto a floured surface to about 1/4" thick. Cut into strips about 1.5" wide.
Angle knife and cut diagonally so the strips are about 4" long.
Fry in deep oil or fat until lightly browned, turning once.
Drain on paper towel. Dust with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
Lithuanian bow-tie cookies that are deep-fried to a perfect consistency.
