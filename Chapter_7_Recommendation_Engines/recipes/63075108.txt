Title:
Tortellini in Chicken Broth Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups low sodium chicken broth
Half 16-oz package frozen cheese tortellini
1 cup slivered watercress leaves
2 scallions, sliced
3 drops hot red pepper sauce
2 tablespoons grated Romano or Parmesan cheese
Freshly ground pepper

Directions:
In a med saucepan, combine the broth and 2/3 cup water. Add the tortellini and cook according to package directions. Stir in the watercress and scallions; cook until the watercress wilts, about 2 minutes. Add the pepper sauce, if using. Serve, sprinkled with cheese and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
apotherix
Description: 
In Italy, this soup is called tortellini en brodo. If you keep your freezer stocked, this soup is a snap to whip up after a long day at the office.
