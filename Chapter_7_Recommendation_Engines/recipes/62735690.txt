Title:
Hot Dog Cornbread Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 hot dogs, try to use skinless if possible
1 10-oz. pkg. corbread mix
Mushroom gravy or Barbecue sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Heat dogs in water till warmed, remove from water and dry; slash at 1 inch intervals. Prepare corn-bread as directs on pkg. but do not cook. Alternate layer of dogs and batter in greased, floured loaf pan, or use spray oil. End with hot dogs on top for a nice presentation. Bake for 35 mins; cool to lukewarm in pan. Slice into about 1-inch thick pieces to serve. Add gravy or bbq sauce on top and serve.
