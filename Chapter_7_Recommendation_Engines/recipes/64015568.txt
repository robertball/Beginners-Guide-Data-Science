Title:
Carolina BBQ sauce- green egg Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c. vinegar
2/3c. catsup
2 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. Worcestershire
1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes

Directions:
In a small sauce pan mix together all ingredients.  Cook over low heat for 5 min stirring to blend
