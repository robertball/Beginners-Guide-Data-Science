Title:
Monster Munchies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of marshmallows
4 Tbsp Hazlenut spread (Nutella)
4 cups of rice krispies

Directions:
Cut Marshmallows in half.
In medium microwavable bowl, melt nutella in microwave for 30 sec.
pour cereal in large bowl.
Place a cookie sheet next to the cereal bowl.
cover marshmallows in nutella, then cover with rice krispies. Then place on cookie sheet and freeze for about 30 min. Then, you can put them in a baggies without them sticking to each other.

Delicious easy to make treat adults will love as much as kids!
