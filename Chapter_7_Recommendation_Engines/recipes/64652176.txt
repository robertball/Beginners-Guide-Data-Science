Title:
Pittsburgh Style Sausage Grinder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. package bulk Italian Hot Ground Sausage or 1 16 oz. pkg. links, casings removed
6 slices provolone cheese
1/2 of a 28 ounce bag frozen fries, cooked according to package directions
2 cups vinegar based coleslaw (can also use mayo based)
6 slices ripe tomato
2 9 inch hoagie buns

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Divide sausage into 2 equal parts and shape each into a thin, oblong patty the same size as the hoagie bun.
Cook patties on a preheated griddle or skillet until cooked through, about 4 minutes per side.
Cut hoagie buns in half and place on a cookie sheet.
Top bottom of buns with a sausage patty and 3 slices of provolone cheese per sandwich.
Bake until cheese is melted and bun is slightly crispy, about 10 minutes.
Remove from oven and top with fries, coleslaw and tomatoes.
Serve with plenty of napkins!

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
This sandwich is a play on a famous sandwich served here in Pittsburgh. Have your appetite and LOTS of napkins ready...This sandwich is not only HUGE, but it's also extremely messy, enjoy!
