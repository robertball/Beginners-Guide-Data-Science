Title:
Sour Cream and Onion Smashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb small red potatoes
4 Tbsp butter
4 scallions, white parts minced, green parts sliced thin
1 C sour cream
1/2 C halfandhalf
S and P

Directions:
Cook Potatoes- Cover potatoes by 1 inch with water and bring to boil in a large pot over high heat. Reduce heat and simmer until tender, about 30 min.
Saut√© Scallions- Meanwhile, melt butter in md saucepan over low heat. Cook scallion whites until translucent, about 5 min. Whisk in sour cream, half-and-half, 1 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper until smooth. Remove from heat, cover, and keep warm.
Smash- Drain potatoes and return to dry pot; let stand 5 min. Break into large chunks with spatula. Fold in sour cream mixture until incorporated and only small chunks of potato remain. Stir in scallion greens and season with S and P

Submitter: 
Lisa Harmon
Description: 
Bold yet balanced sour cream and onion flavor with the signature texture of smashed potatoes
