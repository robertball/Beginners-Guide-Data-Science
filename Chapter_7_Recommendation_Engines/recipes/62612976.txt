Title:
Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can corn
1 can creamed corn
8oz sour cream
2 beaten eggs
1 box corn muffin mix
1 stick melted butter

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and pour into greased casserole.
Bake at 350 for 55-60 minutes.
