Title:
Stuffed Jumbo Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz jumbo shells (1/2 box)
2 T extra virgin olive oil
1 c finely chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1.5 ground beef (or a mix of beef and Italian sausage)
6 oz baby spinach
1/3 c pasta cooking water
1 t salt
1/4 t freshly ground black pepper
1 egg
1/4 c parmesan cheese
1 jar light alfredo sauce
1 c shredded mozzarella provolone cheese mix

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2.  Cook jumbo shells according to the package directions; drain, reserving 1/3 cup of the pasta cooking water.
3.  Saute the onion and garlic in olive oil.  Cook the ground beef in the same large skillet over medium-high heat 4 minutes or until no longer pink, stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat.
4.  Add spinach, pasta water, pepper, and salt and blend well.  Let mixture cool.  Stir in egg and parmesan cheese.
5.  Pour one cup of the alfredo sauce into a 9 x 13 inch baking dish.  Fill jumbo shells with meat mixture; place in dish.  Pour remaining sauce over shells; sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the top, cover with foil.  Bake for 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
6.  These freeze well if frozen before baked.

Submitter: 
Renee Heston
Description: 
These are my favorite shells to take to families  for dinner or to freeze for an easy thaw and make dinner.
