Title:
Angel Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
1 tsp. cream of tartar

Directions:
Mix butter and sugars together well.
Beat in egg and vanilla.
Stir remaining ingredients together and add.
Mix well.
Shape into 1 inch balls. Arrange on ungreased cookie sheet.
Press with a fork dipped in flour.
Bake @ 350 x 10 minutes, until golden.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A light, crisp cookie from a favorite cookbook series. There's cream of tartar in this one & I've found the best price for it is usually @ Nutters or Bulk Barn.
