Title:
Terrific Turkey Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter
8 ounces sliced mushrooms
1/2 cup sliced celery
2 tablespoons cooking or dry sherry
1/2 cup chopped pimientos
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups milk
3 cups cubed cooked turkey
1 teaspoon salt
8 ounces cooked spaghetti
1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F.
Grease a large baking dish.  Heat butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat; add mushrooms and celery.  Cook until celery is tender about 5 minutes.
Add sherry and pimientos to skillet; stir.  Sprinkle flour over vegetables. Stir Well. Add milk to skillet and cook, stirring continually, until thickened, about 3 minutes.  Stir in turkey and salt.  Place hot spaghetti in prepared baking dish.  Top with turkey mixture; sprinkle with Parmesan.  Bake until sauce is bubbly and cheese is browned about 25 minutes.
