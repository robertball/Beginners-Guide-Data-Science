Title:
Crockpot Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - 15oz cans of pizza sauce with cheese
1 - 16oz package of egg noodles
2 - 16oz packages of shredded mozzarella cheese
3 to 4 lbs of any pizza toppings you wish have on your pizza

Directions:
Cook noodles until they are just slightly under cooked.
Cook all toppings as normal (i.e. brown hamburger meat & sausage, fully cook bacon or chicken toppings and drain out all grease.)
Combine sauce with all NON-meat toppings and set aside.
Lightly grease large crockpot along the bottom.
Layer the following in the crockpot in the order given; noodles, browned meat, sauce mixture, cheese and pepperoni (if desired).
Then do a second layer the same way ending with cheese and pepperoni on top.
Set crockpot on low and cook 3-4 hours until cheese on top is melted and bubbling
