Title:
Christmas Cheese Ball Recipe

1 large pkg (8 oz) Cream Cheese

Directions:
Bring cheeses to room temperature.  Drain olives (make sure to squeese all juice from them). Combine the cheeses and garlic in food processor until smooth.  Stir in olives.
Make into 2 balls and roll in dried parsley flakes.  Refrigerate. Serve with crackers.
