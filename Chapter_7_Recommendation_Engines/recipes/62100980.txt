Title:
Layered Cracker Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg non-instant chocolate pudding
1 pkg non-instant lemon pudding
2 cups of milk
salt top crackers

Directions:
- use two differnt pots, prepare pudding as directed on package, using one cup of milk for each pudding mixture.
-layer a glass casserole dish with crackers, pour half of the lemon mixture
on top. Add another cracker layer, pour remaining lemon mixture.
-Repeat wth chocolate mixture.
-Chill for one hour
ENJOY!

Submitter: 
DRAPOSO
Description: 
The women in my family use to make this for the holidays.Use any 2 flavours of pudding that you want,I always loved lemon and chocolate!
