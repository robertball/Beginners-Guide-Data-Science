Title:
Slow Cooker Country Scalloped Potatoes and Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2682954.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds potatoes, peeled and sliced
1 sliced onion
2 pounds Ham, sliced
1 1/2 cups colby jack cheese, shredded
2 cups prepared packaged country gravy mix
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
In your slow cooker, layer 1/2 the potatoes, 1/2 of the onion, 1/2 of the ham and 1/2 a cup of the cheese, repeat, keeping 1/2 cup of the cheese seperate
Pour the prepared country gravy mix over all
Turn on Low heat and let sit for 8 hours
With 10 Minutes left, top with remain cheese until melted
Salt and Pepper to taste when serving.
