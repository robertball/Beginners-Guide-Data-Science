Title:
Hummus and Turkey Pita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ea	Arab Bread - (Pan Arabo)
10 g	Hummus
20 g	Turkey slices
10 g	Red Pepper sliced
or
10 g	Tomato sliced
Iceburg Lettuce

Directions:
Spread hummus on bread, add turkey, pepper slices or a tomato slice, and a couple of leaves of lettuce. Wrap in plastic wrap.
