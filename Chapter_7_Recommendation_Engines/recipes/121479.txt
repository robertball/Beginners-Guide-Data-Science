Title:
Macaroni 'n' Cheese for Two Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990970.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sour cream
1/3 cup milk
1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
3/4 cup elbow macaroni, cooked and drained
2 tablespoons chopped onion
Paprika

Directions:
In a bowl, combine sour cream and milk. Stir in the cheese, macaroni and onion if desired. Transfer to a greased 2-1/2-cup baking dish; sprinkle with paprika. Cover and bake at 325 degrees F for 25 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Mrs.  O. Lick
Description: 
This is the simplest of entrees, so well received on cold, frosty evenings. The combination of sour cream and cheese gives this macaroni dish a distinctive taste of its own. -Mrs. O. Lick, Boyne Falls, Michigan
