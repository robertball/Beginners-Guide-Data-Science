Title:
BBQ Chicken Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 oz Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ Sauce
2 cloves garlic
1 yellow onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
1 yellow pepper, chopped
1/2 pound of mushrooms, chopped
1/2 pound bacon
4 boneless chicken breasts
1 lg pizza crust
Seasonings: seasoned salt, black pepper, chopped oregano, parsley flakes, chopped basil, crushed red pepper, paprika
mozzarella cheese
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Grill chicken breasts and season with seasonings and bbq sauce. Chop the chicken into bite size pieces or shred the chicken, whichever you prefer. Cook the bacon until very crisp and crush into crumbs. Save a bit of the bacon grease and saute the onion and peoppers for 3 minutes. Chop garlic into the pan and cook for 1 more minute. Smother the pizza crust with bbq sauce instead of pizza sauce. Place the chicken on the sauce, then the crumbled bacon and then the sauteed vegetables. Cover the pizza with mozzerella cheese and then sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Finally, on top of cheese, sprinkle daskes of the seasonings to taste. Bake at 350 degrees until the cheese begins to brown.
