Title:
Easy Ground turkey with Tomato Sauce and Zucchini Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb Ground Turkey
1 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 medium onion
2 cloves garlic
2 (28-ounce) cans whole tomatoes, drained, tomatoes chopped
3 tablespoons tomato paste
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 bay leaf
1 tsp Salt and pepper
2 Zucchini's

Directions:
Preheat oven at 375 degrees
Zucchini Pasta: Place the Zucchini in the vegetable noodle slicer. Drizzle olive oil and salt and pepper over the noodles and bake for 15 minutes.
Ground Turkey until cooked.
Tomato Sauce: In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until soft, about 5 minutes. Add the  minced garlic and cook for 2 minutes longer. Add the remaining ingredients and cook, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 30 minutes. Pour over ground turkey and season it with salt and pepper.
