Title:
Savory Southwestern Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs (separated)
1 3/4 c milk, warm
1/3 c butter, melted
2 tbsp honey or white sugar
1 1/4 c cornmeal, white or yellow
1 c all-purpose flour
2 tsp salt
4 tsp baking powder
1/2 c corn
2-4 green chiles, chopped finely and deseeded

Directions:
1. Preheat waffle iron.
2. In a large bowl, beat egg whites until fluffy, then add milk, butter, and honey/sugar. Slowly add cornmeal, flour, baking powder, and salt, until smooth. Then add corn and chiles.
3. Ladle onto waffle iron. Cook until golden brown and crisp, about 3-4 minutes (or until waffle iron stops steaming).
4. Serve hot and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Medea Wolff
Description: 
This hearty and spicy cornmeal waffle can be eaten any time of day and is a nice substitute for cornbread when it's too hot out to use your oven.
