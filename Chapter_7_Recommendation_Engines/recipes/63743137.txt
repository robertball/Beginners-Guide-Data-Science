Title:
Sweet Baby Ray's Crockpot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1038948.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6	chicken breasts
1 18 ounce bottle	sweet baby ray's sauce
1/4 c	white vinegar
1 tsp	red pepper flakes
1/4 c	brown sugar
1 tsp	garlic powder

Directions:
Mix everything together, but chicken.
Place chicken in crockpot (frozen is ok).
Pour sauce mixture over chicken.
Cook on low 4-6 hours.
