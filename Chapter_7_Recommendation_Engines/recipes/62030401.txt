Title:
Moms Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/747296.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lb. Extra lean Ground Beef.
2 Cans Beef Broth.
1 Large can Tomatoes.
1 2 lb. Package frozen Mixed Vegetables.
4 Soup Starters (vegetable beef).
2 Qrts. water (to cook meat)
6 Qrts. water (add to other ingredients)
4 small potatos

Directions:
Break up ground beef and cover with 2 qrts. water. Bring to a boil, turn down fire and simmer for 20 minutes.Add all other ingredients except potatos, cover and bring to a boil. Turn down heat and simmer for 2 hours. Cut potatos and add after first hour.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a recipe of my mothers. I love it.It makes enough to feed a large crowd.Put it on corn bread and enjoy !
