Title:
Green Chile Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lrg can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 1/2 cups milk
3 lbs Chicken Breast (cut up)
3 small cans Green chiles (diced)
2 ts minced garlic
5 Potatoes diced
1 can of Corn
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a electic roaster (250 degrees) add all the ingredients and cook for about 5 to 6 hours.  Stir and keep the lid on it, do not let it boil.

Submitter: 
Kim Casey Nugent Roberds
Description: 
Cream of Chicken soup base with chicken breast and green chiles with veggies.
