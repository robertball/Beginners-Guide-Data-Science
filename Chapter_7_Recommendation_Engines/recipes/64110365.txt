Title:
Quinoa Salad Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 cup quinoa
1/2 cup cooked peas
1/2 cup chopped red peppers
1/2 cup chopped cucumbers
1/2 cup grated raw carrots
1/2 cup black beans
Sides:
Lettuce for wrapping
1/2 cup chopped cilantro
1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
Sauce:
1/4 cup peanut butter
1/4 cup hoisin sauce
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Cook quinoa in 2 cups water for 15 minutes. Let cool.
In a sauce pan, cook peanut butter, hoisin suace and water until smooth.
Mix cooled quinoa with alls veggies and beans EXCEPT cilantro and tomatoes.
Put sauce, cilantro and tomatoes in their own dish.
Serve with lettuce.

Submitter: 
Amy Lee
Description: 
Can use any veggies on hand. Make sure it's chopped up really small (less than 1cm). Can also sprinkle on a mixture of seeds.
