Title:
WW 15-Minute Chicken Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 spray cooking spray
1 package frozen gumbo-style vegetables, 16 oz, a mixture of okra, peppers and onions
29 oz canned diced tomatoes, with mild chilies
12 oz refrigerated grilled chicken breast fillet, cubed, about 3 cups
1 Tbsp all-purpose flour
1 tsp Creole seasoning

Directions:
Coat a large skillet with nonstick cooking spray.  Add vegetables and saute over high heat, stirring frequently, for 2 minutes.  Stir in flour and cook 1 minute more.
Stir in tomatoes, chicken and Creole seasoning.  Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until hot, about 6 minutes.

Super quick, super tasty gumbo made from convenience foods.
