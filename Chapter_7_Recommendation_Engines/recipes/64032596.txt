Title:
Curried ground lamb with quinoa, swiss chard and fiddle ferns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120684.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground lamb
1 carrot cut in 1/4 inch rounds
1 stalk celery chopped
1/2 onion - chopped
1/2 cup frozen peas
4 c swiss chard - chopped leaves and stems
1 cup uncooked quinoa
2 cups lamb stock                                     2 tbsp curry powder
1 cup fiddle ferns - boiled through and rinsed
2 cloves garlic - pressed
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown lamb in 9 in cast iron pan.
Add carrot, celery, onion, peas and swiss chard.  Continue to cook, stirring occasionally until vegetables are slightly soft and chard is wilted, about 10 minutes.
Add quinoa, stock and curry powder, stir, cover pan and bring to boil.  Reduce heat to low simmer and cook until quinoa is cooked and most of the liquid is absorbed.
Remove from heat.  Stir in Fiddle ferns and pressed garlic.
Add salt and pepper if desired.
Allow to rest for about 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Anna Silvani-Morrison
Description: 
Awesome combination of a few of my favorite spring ingredients.... Lamb, swiss chard & fiddle ferns!
