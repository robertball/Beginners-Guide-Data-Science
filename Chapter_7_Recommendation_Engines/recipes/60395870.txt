Title:
Boo Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 stick butter or margarine
30 large marshmallows or 3 cups of mini marshmallows
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1/2 cup reese's creamy peanut butter
1/3 cup hershey's special dark chocolate chips or semi-sweet chocolate chips
4-1/2 cups rice cereal

Directions:
Line cookie sheet with wax paper.
Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat.  Add marshmallows. Cook stirring constantly, until marshmallows are melted. Remove from heat.  Add corn syrup; stir until well blended.  Add peanut butter and chocolate chips; stir until chips are melted and mixture is well blended.
Add cereal; stir until evenly coated. Cool slightly. With wet hands; shape mixture into 1-1/2" balls.  Place balls on prepared cookie sheet. Cool completely. Store in tightly covered container in a cool dry place.
