Title:
Pumpkin banana pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white flour
1/2 cup wheat flour
2 tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 cup pureed pumpkin (canned is perfect)
1 mashed banana
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
3/4 cup milk
1 large egg

Directions:
1. Sift the dry ingredients together and set aside.
2. Mix pumpkin, banana, oil, milk and egg together, then add dry mix.  Mix just to moisten and drop onto medium hot pan.
