Title:
Burgundy Beef Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb beef roast
2 bullion cubes
3 large garlic cloves, sliced
1/2 large white onion, sliced
bottle of burgundy wine

Directions:
Lightly sear the roast in a frying pan.
Place roast in a deep baking dish.  Pour burgundy over the roast until almost covered.  Place garlic and onion on and around the roast.  Place bullion cubes in the burgundy.
Bake at 350 degrees covered for about 45 minutes or until cooked to your preference.  Cooking time will vary depending on the size of the roast.
