Title:
Caramel Toffee Sqares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Base:
2 cups flour
1/2 cup icing sugar
1 cup butter
Filling:
1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 can sweetened condensed milk (Eagle Brand)
Topping:
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1/3 cup butter or margarine

Directions:
Base:
Crumble ingredients together. Pack into greased 9" x 13" pan.
Bake @ 350 x 20 minutes.
Filling:
Mix ingredients in large saucepan.
Bring to boil over med. heat. Boil x 5 minutes, stirring constantly as it burns easily.
Remove from heat.
Beat with spoon slowly until it shows signs of thickening.
Pour over base.
Topping:
Melt chocolate and margarine together over low heat (or in micro.). Pour evenly over filling.
Chill.
