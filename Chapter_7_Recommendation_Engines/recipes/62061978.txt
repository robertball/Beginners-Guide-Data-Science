Title:
Absolutely the Best Meatballs Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. thawed ground beef
3 eggs
6 slices white bread, soggy with water
grated parmesan cheese
2 Tbsp. basil
4 big cloves garlic
olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cover the soggy bread with parmesan cheese.  Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl by kneading with hands.  Roll meat mixture into 2-inch balls.  Brown the meatballs in olive oil.  Place browned meatballs into simmering tomato sauce and heat on very low heat for 1 to two hours.

Submitter: 
Traveling_Is_Love
Description: 
These are the real Italian meatballs according to our family recipe!!  They really are incredibly good, and go great with all sorts of types of pastas.  Give em a whirl.
