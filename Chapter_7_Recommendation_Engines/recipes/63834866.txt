Title:
Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065729.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
3/4 cup butter
2/3 cup evaporated milk
1 cup peanut butter
2 cups marshmallow creme
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Spray a 9x13 inch baking dish with vegetable spray.
In a large bowl combine peanut butter, marshmallow creme and vanilla.
In a heavy 4 quart sauce pan, combine sugar, milk, and butter or margarine.
Bring to a boil over medium heat.
Boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
Pour boiling syrup over peanut butter mixture.
Stir with wooden spoon until smooth.
Pour into prepared 9x13 dish.
Let stand until firm; cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
An easy recipe for peanut butter fudge. Using real peanut butter not peanut butter chips.Modified from: Peanut Butter Fudge II
