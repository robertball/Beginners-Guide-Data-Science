Title:
Peanut butter cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C shorting
1C brown sugar
1C white sugar
1C peanut butter
2 eggs
1tsp vanilla
1 1/2C flour
1tsp soda
1/2tsp salt

Directions:
Cream together the shortening, sugars and peanut butter. Add the eggs and vanilla. Once combined well add the dry ingreindents. Drop by rounded teaspoon on cookie sheet and bake at 350 for 12min.
