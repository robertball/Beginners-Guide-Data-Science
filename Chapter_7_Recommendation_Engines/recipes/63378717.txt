Title:
Baked Salmon in Mustard Crumb Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp white wine vinegar
2 tbsp sugar
3 tbsp coarse ground dijon mustard
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/2 cup olive oil
1 1/4 lb salmon filet
1/2 tsp thyme
Salt & Pepper
1 1/2 cups bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F.
Place vinegar, sugar, and mustards in blender.
With blender running on low, slowly pour in olive oil until well blended.
Place salmon skin side down in foil-lined baking pan and pat dry.
Season with thyme, salt, and pepper.
Pour mustard mix evenly over the salmon and use brush to evenly cover.
Sprinkle bread crumbs evenly over the mustard-coated salmon and gently press on.
Bake for 25-30 or until crumb crust is golden brown.
