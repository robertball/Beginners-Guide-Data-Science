Title:
New York-Style Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 1/2 oz) can diced tomatoes with juice
1 6-oz. can tomato paste
2 T extra-virgin olive oil
2 T chopped fresh basil (or 2 tsp dried)
1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
1 1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 to 1 tsp minced garlic
3/4 tsp salt

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the diced tomatoes with juice, tomato paste, olive oil, basil, oregano, sugar, garlic, and salt.
Taste and add more salt if needed.
Use immediately or store in an air-tight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days, or freeze for up to 2 months.
Bring to room temperature before using.
