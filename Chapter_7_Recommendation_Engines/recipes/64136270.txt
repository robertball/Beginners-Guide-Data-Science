Title:
Dilly Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1351622.jpg
Ingredients:
18 cups water
18 cups distilled white vinegar
2 cup pickling salt
1/2 cup pickling spice
10 tbs dill seed
4 tbs red pepper
60 cloves garlic
15 pounds fresh green beans, rinsed and trimmed

Directions:
Sterilize 30 (1 pint) jars in boiling water for at least 5 minutes.
Combine the water, pickling salt,,vinegar and pickling spice in a large pot, and bring to a boil. When it begins to boil, reduce heat to low, and keep at a simmer while you pack the jars.
In each jar place the following:
1 ts dill seed,
1/4 ts dried red pepper,
2 cloves of garlic,
Pack beans into the spiced jars in a standing position.
Ladle the hot brine into jars, leaving 1/2 inch of space at the top. Screw the lids onto the jars, and process in a hot water bath for 5 minutes to seal. Store for at least 2 weeks before eating.
