Title:
Crawfish Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 cup onion, chopped
1 cup green onion tops, chopped
½ cup bell pepper, chopped
3 lbs. frozen crawfish tails, thawed
3 cans cream of shrimp soup
1 pint whipping cream
1 lb. (4 cups) Colby/Jack cheese, shredded
1 Tbsp. Chili powder
1 Tbsp. ground cumin
25 Flour tortillas
½ lb. (2 cups) Montery Jack cheese shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°. In butter, saute onions and bell pepper until clear. Add crawfish and simmer 5 minutes.
Add soup and simmer 5 minutes. Add whipping cream, 1 lb. Colby/Jack cheese, chili powder, and cumin. Simmer 10 minutes, but do not bring to a boil.
Let mixture cool.
Fill each tortilla with mixture. Roll and place seam down in large casserole dish. When finished, spoon remaining sauce over top and spread shredded Monterey Jack cheese on top.
Bake at 350° for 25 minutes.
