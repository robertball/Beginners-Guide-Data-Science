Title:
Citrus Zest Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Pineapple chunks, drained (20 oz.)
2 C. red seedless grapes
1 Green pear (or red apple), cored and cubed
1 orange, peeled and diced
1 banana, sliced
1/2 c. strawberries, sliced
1 c. vanilla low fat yogurt
1 t. orange zest or lemon peel
1/2 c. granola, low fat

Directions:
1.  Combine pineapple chunks, grapes, pear, orange, banana, strawberries in medium bowl.
2. Stir yogurt and orange peel into fruit mixture.  Cover; refrigerate 15 min. to allow flavor to blend.
3. Sprinkle granola on top.  Garnish with orange curl and fresh mint, if desired.
