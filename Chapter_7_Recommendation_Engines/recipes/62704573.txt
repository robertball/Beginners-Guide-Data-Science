Title:
Noodles With Cheese and Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 a package of penne noodles
1/4 cup butter
Parmesan cheese, to taste

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package directions.  Drain.
Melt butter in hot pan, add noodles, and some cheese. Stir to coat.
