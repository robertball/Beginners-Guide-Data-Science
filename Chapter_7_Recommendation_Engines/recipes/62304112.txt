Title:
Country-Kitchen Potato Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg potato, peeled & diced
1 c water
1 t salt
1/2 c butter
1/2 c sugar
1/2 c powdered milk
3 eggs
2 pkgs active dry yeast
1/4 c warm water
6 c flour

Directions:
Boil potato, water & salt in a large, covered, saucpan until potato is tender. Remove from heat but do not drain.
Add butter & sugar- beat with an electric mixer until smooth. Beat in powdered milk and eggs.
Disolve yeast in the 1/4 c of warm water & add to mixture.
With a wooden spoon, beat in enough flour to make a soft dough, about 4 cups. Work in remaing flour until smooth, turning out on a floured surface if necessary.
Plave dough in a large generously buttered bowl, turning it over so the top is greased.
Let rise until doubled, about 1 1/2 hrs.
Punch down, cover & refrigerate overnight.
THe next day, let rise @ room temperature for several hours.
Divide dough into s 9"x5" buttered loaf pans. Cover and let rise again until doubled, about 1hr.
Bake @ 375 f for 35-40 min. Set of wire racks to cool.
