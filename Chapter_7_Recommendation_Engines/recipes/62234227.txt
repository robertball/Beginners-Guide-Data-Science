Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1 onion, diced
2 packets taco seasoning
1 14oz cans diced tomatoes, undrained
1 15oz can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 15oz can black beans, drained and rinsed
2 c. frozen corn
2 32oz cartons chicken broth
sour cream, if desired

Directions:
Heat a large pot over medium heat.  Add ground beef, and cook and crumble it until it's no longer pink.  Add the onion and cook until it's softened, about 5 minutes.  Add the taco seasoning, mixing it in well, and cook for 2 minutes.
Add the tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, corn, and chicken broth.  Bring it al to a simmer over medium-high heat, cover the pot, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 10 minutes.  Serve with sour cream.
