Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole III Recipe

Ingredients:
2 29oz cans Yams
1/2 cup butter melted
1/3 cup evaporated milk
2 eggs beaten
3/4 cup dark brown sugar
1 cup light brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/3 cup butter
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 350F
2. grease 9x13 pan
3.In a large bowl, mix together mashed yams, 1/2 cup butter, evaporated milk, dark brown sugar, vanilla extract, and eggs.
4. Spread sweet potato mixture into the prepared baking dish.
5. In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar and flour. Cut in 1/3 cup butter until mixture is crumbly, then stir in pecans.
6. Sprinkle pecan mixture over the sweet potatoes.
7. bake for 25 min or until golden brown
