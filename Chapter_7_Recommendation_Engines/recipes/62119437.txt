Title:
Authentic German Obsttorte (Fruittart) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
for the crust:
3.5 oz soft butter or margerine
3.5 oz sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 pinch salt
2 eggs
3.5 oz flour
1 tsp baking powder
for the topping:
1 pack of vanilla sauce or vanilla pudding
1 can of peachehalves (or other seasonal fruit or canned fruit)
some strawberries
2 bananas
for the glaze:
1 pack of white gelatin
2 tblsp sugar
1 cup water

Directions:
Using mixer beat butter until smooth.
Add sugar, vanilla extract and salz and continue beating till mixture has a very smooth consistency.
Add eggs one at a time.
Mix flour and baking powder and slowly mix with butter mixture.
Pour into tart pan (11'') and bake at 350F for about 20 minutes.
When the crust is done take out of the oven and put upside down on rack to cool.
Prepare vanilla pudding according to directions on pack and spread some over the crust (pudding should be cold).
Peel bananas and cut in half (lenghtwhise), wash strawberries and cut in half (lengthwise), drain can of peachhalves. Arrange on crust.
In a small saucepan mix gelatin, sugar and water (according to directions on gelatin pack). Bring to a boil. Pour over tarte. Let cool. Enjoy.
