Title:
Snack-It-Up Appetizer Board Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6178750.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (11.5 ounce) jar sweet pickles
3 ounces pimento-stuffed Spanish Manzanilla olives
2 bunches seedless red grapes
10 ounces sliced Colby-Jack cheese
2 small apples, sliced
1/2 seedless cucumber, sliced
3 ounces buttery round crackers (such as Keebler® Toasteds® Buttercrisp)
3 ounces Havarti cheese slices
2 3/4 ounces mixed nuts (such as Seapoint Farm® Energy Blend)

Directions:
Place pickles in a 6-1/4x3-1/2-inch rectangular serving dish; place olives in a 16x2-inch olive tray. Arrange both on a large cutting board and surround with grapes, Colby-Jack cheese, apple slices, cucumber, crackers, Havarti cheese, and mixed nuts.

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
This easily put-together board of appetizers is perfect for continuous snacking on big game days or at any gathering.
