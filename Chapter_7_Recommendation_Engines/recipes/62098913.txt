Title:
yummy veggie soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 carrots(chopped small)
2 sticks celery
1 large zucchini
1 onion
2 bundles bok choy
1/3 head of broccoli
2 vegetable bouillon cubes(or 2 cups vegetable broth)
2 chicken bouillon cubes(or 2 cups chicken broth)
7 cups of water
1 tbsp oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop veggies.
In large stock pot add carrots and onion.  cook until onion is tender.
Add celery and zucchini. Cook a couple of minutes.
Add bok choy and broccoli.  Pour 1 cup of water into the pot and cover.
Meanwhile in a small pot bring 6 cups of water to a boil.  Add the bouillon cubes.
Add broth to veggies.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Cook until vegetables are cooked(bok choy should be tender but still retain alittle of its crispness)

Submitter: 
babybluangel
Description: 
I made this soup one day while I was sick.  I wanted a homemade soup without waiting hours for it to cook and this was it.  You could also add potatoes, rice or pasta to make it heartier.
