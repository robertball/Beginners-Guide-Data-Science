Title:
Cheesy pasta bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
water 12 cups
salt 1 1/2 tsp
small shell pasta 2 cups
Chopped onion 1 cup
can of condensed cream of mushroom soup 284 ml
Mayonnaise 1/2 cup
milk or cream 1/4 cup
grated cheese 3 cups
Worcestershire sauce 2tsp
2 cans of tuna/turkey (optional)

Directions:
Combine water salt in dutch oven bring to boil. Add pasta and onion. boil uncovered for 8 to 10 minutes stirring occasionally. Drain and return to pot
In separate bowl mix, Can of soup, mayonnaise and milk. then add to pasta mixture.
Add Cheese, Worcestershire sauce, and tuna if you wish too.
Place mixture in casserole and then place in oven at 350F for 30 minutes and enjoy.....
