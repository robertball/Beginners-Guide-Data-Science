Title:
Indian Spinach with Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs fresh spinahc
3/4 cup ghee or 3 Tsp of vegetable oil
2 large onions, sliced thin
2 garlic cloves, minced
5oz ginger paste
2 tsp chili powder
2 tsp turmeric
2 tsp garam masala
2 tsp coriander seeds
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp cumin powder
1.5 tsp salt
2 tsp black pepper
14 oz tomatoes, canned

Directions:
Wash the spinach, shake dry and de-stem. Slice into 1 inch strips.
Heat the ghee or oil in a large saucepan and add the onions and garlic. Fry over low heat until golden and soft, about 5 minutes.
Add the ginger and toss gently. Stir in the chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, coriander seeds, ground coriander, cumin powder, salt ad pepper and cook for 1 minute on low heat.
Mix the spinach well to coat it in the spices and add the tomatoes with half the juice and bring to a rapid boil while stirring. If the spinach starts to stick add a little extra water or tomato juice. Simmer for 5-10 minutes, or until the spinach and tomatoes are cooked.

Submitter: 
Shana McCoy
Description: 
Decadent spices and vibrant colors combine to make a delicious vegetable dish. Vegetarian friendly, it can be 100% vegan just by replacing the ghee with vegetable oil.
