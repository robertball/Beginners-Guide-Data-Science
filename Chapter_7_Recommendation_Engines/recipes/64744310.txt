Title:
Toasted butter cheerios Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of plain cherrios
1 stick of unsalted butter

Directions:
On a medium heat setting melt your stick of butter into your pan
Once your butter has melted add your cheerios
Toast until your desired toastyness.
Let cool and enjoy !

Submitter: 
m. madera
Description: 
These cheerios are great for snacking on when you need something tasty and simple or even something to sneek into the theatre thats even better than popcorn
