Title:
MOCHA FUDGE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
2/3 cup evaporated milk
1-2/3 cups sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
2 cups mini marshmallows
1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup butterscotch chips
1 tablespoon instant coffee granules
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine butter, evaporated milk, sugar, and salt in a heavy saucepan over medium heat.  Bring to a boil.  Cook 4-5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat.  Stir in marshmallows, chocolate chips, butterscotch chips, instant coffee, and vanilla.  Stir briskly until all blended and marshmallows melt.  Pour into a buttered 8-inch square pan.  Cool well, and cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Diane Blanchard Angell
Description: 
A coffee lover's dream!  Sometimes I add even a little MORE instant coffee for extra mocha flavor!
