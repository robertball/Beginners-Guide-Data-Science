Title:
"If You Like Pina Coladas" Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Pina Colada Mix
1/4 cup water
2 tbs crushed pineapple (drained)
1 tbs + 1 tsp sweetened coconut flakes
2 tbs powdered sugar
1-1/2 tsp cornstarch
3 tsp water

Directions:
Mix Pina Colada mix,  1/4 cup water, pineapple, coconut and powdered sugar in sauce pan.  Heat on medium-low temperature til sauce begins to simmer, stirring frequently.  Allow mixture to slowly simmer 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Mix cornstarch and 3 tsp water together and add to sauce.  Whisk well.  Let mixture simmer 3-4 minutes longer while you stir, until it thickens.  Remove from heat and allow to cool to room temperature.  Serve at room temperature.

Submitter: 
DIANA F
Description: 
Great on coconut shrimp or coconut chicken, this dipping sauce tastes like one you'd get at that popular seafood restaurant.
