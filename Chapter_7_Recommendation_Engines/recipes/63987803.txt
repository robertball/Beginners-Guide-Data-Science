Title:
Sweet Potatoes with Bourbon & Maple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups strong coffee
9 Tbp Maple syrup
3 Tbsp dark brown sugar
1/2 tsp instant expresso powder
1/3 c bourbon
9 Tbsp butter, divided
5 lbs. sweet potatoes, peeled, cut into 2-3 inch pieces
3 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 cup chopped toasted almonds

Directions:
Stir coffee, maple syrup, sugar, & espresso powder over med heat until sugar dissolves. Bring to a boil. Cook until thickened & reduced by half (6-7 min.
Removed syrup from heat, add bourbon & 2 Tbsp butter. Reduced heat to med & simmer until sauce is only about 3/4 cup (40-45 min) think enough to coat a spoon but not sticky. Season with salt & pepper (Can make sauce ahead)
Oven 425. Melt 7 Tbsp butter in bowl. Add sweet potatoes, oil, salt & pepper. Toss to coat and divide between 2 rimmed baking sheets. Roast 30-3 minutes, turning potatoes often. Should be crispy around the edges.
Drizzle warmed sauce over potatoes & top with almonds.
