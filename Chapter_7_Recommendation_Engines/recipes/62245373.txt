Title:
Zucchini Loaf Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/329565.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. All purpose Flour
1 c. Whole wheat flour
1/2 tea. salt
1 tea. baking powder
1 tea. baking soda
3 tea. cinnamon
3 eggs
1 c. oil
1/2 c. apple sauce
1 3/4 c. sugar
3 tea. vanilla extract
2-2 1/2 c. shredded zucchini
1 c. chopped walnuts or pecans
Topping:
1/2 c. oats
1/2 c brown sugar
1/4 c. flour
1/4 tea. cinnamon
1/4 c. butter softened

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 325.
1. In a lg. bowl combine both flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda and cinnamon. Set aside
2. In another bowl combine eggs, oil, apple sauce, sugar and vanilla stirring well. Mix together with dry ingredients.
3. Grease and flour two  8"x4"x2 1/2" loaf pan. Pour half of the mixture into each pan. Bake 50-60 minutes until a tooth pick inserted into the middle comes out clean.
4. While loaf is cooking, combine the ingredients for the topping. The last 15 min. sprinkle topping over loafs. Enjoy!!!
