Title:
Cream Puff/Eclair  Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. water
1/2 C. butter
1/4 tsp salt
1 C. all purpose flour
4 lg. eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. In medium saucepan, bring water, butter and salt to boiling. Remove from heat. With a wooden spoon or electric mixer, beat in all flour. Return to low heat; beat until mixture forms a ball and leaves the sides of the pan. Remove from heat. Beat in eggs, one at a time, beating mixture harder after adding each egg. Continue beating until dough is shiny and satiny and breaks away in strands. For cream puffs, drop 12 rounded tablespoonfuls, about 2 inches apart, on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 45 to 50 minutes, until puffed and golden brown. Let cool. With sharp knife cut off tops.Scoop out any filaments of soft dough. Fill with ice cream, whipped cream filling or custard filling.
For eclair - make 1/2 cream puff recipe. Drop 3 inches apart 6 rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet with spatula, form into 4 x 1 inch strips. Bake as above for 35 to 40 minutes. Cool. Fill through one end.
