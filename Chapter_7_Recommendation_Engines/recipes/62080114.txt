Title:
Guera's Puerco con Nopales Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lbs. pork country style ribs cut into bite sized pieces
1 jar Nopales drained and rinsed(cactus found in your mexican section of your grocery store)
5 tomatillos cleaned, boiled
2 jalepenos boiled
1/2 small onion diced
3 stems of cilantro chopped
salt and peper to taste

Directions:
1.) Boil your pork ribs till cook through and tender. Then drain the water and brown.
2.) Boil tomatillos and jalepenos togeather once cooked through till soft, liquiffy them in a blender till smooth adding in onion, salt and cilantro until soup constinace.
3.) Add nopales to the browned ribsand sautee till heated through.
4.) Add in your tomatillo and jalepeno mix bring to boil then reduce heat and simmer for 10-15 min.
Serve with yellow rice,refried beans and warmed corn tortillas.

Submitter: 
GUERAGUERRERO
Description: 
This is a recipe my husband showed me, he is from Michucan, Mexico. It has become the only way I like to eat pork ribs anymore. My children enjoy & love this recipe!
