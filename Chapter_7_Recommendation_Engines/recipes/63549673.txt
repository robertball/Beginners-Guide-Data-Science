Title:
Red Velvet Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/969685.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chocolate ice cream
3 tablespoons red velvet cake mix
1 tablespoon cream cheese frosting (optional)
1/2 cup milk
Whipped cream to garnish

Directions:
Blend ice cream, cake mix, frosting, and milk in blender until smooth. Pour into glass, top with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
HTR
Description: 
Red velvet cake mix transforms the traditional milkshake recipe into a Red Velvet Lover's dream. This indulgent and brilliantly hued recipe is perfect for Valentine's Day, the holidays, or any day of the week.
