Title:
Cocoa Tea Mix Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3311210.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups boiling water
1 Earl Grey tea bag
3 tablespoons milk
1 1/2 tablespoons hot cocoa mix
2 teaspoons white sugar

Directions:
Pour boiling water into a mug and steep tea bag, about 2 minutes. Remove tea bag; add milk, hot cocoa mix, and sugar. Stir until well-blended, about 20 seconds.

Submitter: 
Morgan13
Description: 
Not everyone will be a fan of this earthy, sweet drink. Depends what tea you use really. I use Earl Grey Black Tea, but you can use any tea you love. Try out this unique drink.
