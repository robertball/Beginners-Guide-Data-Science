Title:
Creamy Champagne Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shrimp 20lb
Mayonaise 5c
Heavy Cream 12c
Champagne 5c
Garlic 2c (diced)
Red Onion 4 Large (diced)
Cherry Tomatoes 5lb
Mushrooms 5 lb (sliced)
Flour 4 1/2 c
milk 8 c
salt
parmesan cheese 4 c
butter 4 c

Directions:
Dice Garlic, Red Onions and Muchrooms. Place each in a seperate bowl, and set aside
In a large sauce pan melt butter and sautee Red Onions, when soft add garlic. When garlic is aromatic, take onions and gralic out of the butter, reserving butter.
Sautee shrimp in the same butter used to cook onions and garlic, adding 2c of Champagne. Add tomatoes and Mushrooms, Sautee until shrimp is bright red.
In a large seperate bowl combine the following: mayo, flour, remaining Champagne, Parm. cheese, salt, milk and cream. Whisk together.
Removing Shrimp mixture for saute pan, place flour mixture in the pan and simmer until thick, stuir constantly to avoid sticking and burning.
Once flour mix is thick, add shrimp mixture, stir and serve over pasta.
