Title:
Cheese Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2cans 15 oz each tomato sauce
1 1/3 cups water
2 Tbsp chili powder
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp ground cumin
16 8 inch flour tortillas
6 cups (24 oz) Mexican shredded cheese
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheddar cheese
2 medium onions, finely chopped
1 cup (8 oz) sour cream

Directions:
In a large saucepan, combine the first six ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 4-5 minutes or until thickened, stirring occasionally. Spoon 2 tablespoons sauce over each tortilla.
In a large bowl, combine the Monterey Jack, 2 cups cheddar cheese, onions, and sour cream. Place about 1/3 cup down the center of each tortilla. Roll up and place seam side down in two greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking dishes. Pour remaining sauce over top.
Bake, uncovered, at 350Â° for 20 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining cheddar cheese. Bake 4-5 minutes longer or until cheese is melted. Garnish with lettuce, olives and sour cream if desired. Yield: 16 enchiladas.
