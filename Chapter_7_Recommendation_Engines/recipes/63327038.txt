Title:
Manijeh: Curry Chicken Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/881729.jpg
Ingredients:
Chicken Thighs
1/2 C plain yogurt
1 Tb honey
1 Tb olive oil
1 Tb curry powder
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp salt.
Bamboo skewers

Directions:
Marinade meat for at least 30 minutes before grilling.
I used Greek yogurt and 2" cubed chicken thighs.
Skewered with red onion, yellow bell and tomatoes from the garden
