Title:
Chocolate Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup rolled oats
2 tablespoons cocoa
1 cup milk
1/2 t. vanilla extract
1 frozen ripe banana (peeled and cut in chunks)
4 ice cubes
2 tablespoons crushed walnuts - optional

Directions:
Place oats and milk in blender. Let sit 5 minutes for oats to soften. Add rest of ingredients and blend till smooth.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
A rich, thick and chocolatey smoothie with only four ingredients and no added sugar! Not only is it healthy, it's an ideal way to satisfy a chocolate craving without the guilt.
