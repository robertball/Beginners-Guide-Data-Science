Title:
Easy Vegan Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4786601.jpg
Ingredients:
2 avocados
5 teaspoons raw apple cider vinegar
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine avocados, vinegar, parsley, chives, dill, and garlic powder in the bowl of a food processor. Process until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
