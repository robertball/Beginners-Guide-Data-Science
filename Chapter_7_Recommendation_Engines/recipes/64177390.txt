Title:
100 Chocolate Chip Cookies the Grandma Linda Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1488382.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups oatmeal
2 c butter
2 c sugar
2 c brown sugar
4 eggs
1 tbsp + 1 tsp vanilla
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
24 oz chocolate chips
2.6 oz Hershey bar (grated)
3 c chopped walnuts
4 c flour
2 c sugar

Directions:
Blend oatmeal to a fine powder. Cream the butter and margarine and sugars. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix oatmeal, salt, baking powder and soda with flour. Add all ingredients together. Fold in chocolate chips, grated Hershey bar and nuts. Roll into balls and place 2 inches apart on multiple cookie sheets. Bake 12 minutes at 375F.

Submitter: 
Seth Kolloen
Description: 
Soft and fluffy cookies. Easy to make and feed an army! Grandma Linda knew how to make friends.
