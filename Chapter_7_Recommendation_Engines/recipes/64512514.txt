Title:
Sunday Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. salt
2 tsp. paprika
1 1/2 tsp. onion powder
1 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 1/2 tsp. dried basil
1 tsp. dry mustard
1 tsp. cumin
2 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1/2 tsp. savory
1/4 stick butter
2 cups chopped onions
1 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 roasting chicken
1/4 cup flour
1-2 cups chicken stock

Directions:
Combine seasonings in a bowl.
Melt butter over high heat in skillet.   When butter starts to sizzle, add chopped onions and peppers, and 3 tbsp. seasoning mix.
Cook until onions are golden brown, cool.
Stuff cavity of chicken with cooled vegetables.
Sprinkle the outside of the chicken with 1 tbsp. seasoning mix.  Rub it in well so it sticks.
Place chicken in large slow cooker.
Cover.  Cook on low 6 hours.
Empty vegetable stuffing and juices into saucepan.  Whisk in flour and 1 cup stock from slow cooker.
Cook over high heat until thickened.  Add more stock if you prefer a thinner gravy.

One way to roast a chicken in a slow cooker!
