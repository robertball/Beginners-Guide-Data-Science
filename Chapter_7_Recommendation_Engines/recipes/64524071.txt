Title:
Bacon Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 tablespoon canola oil
3 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
12 cup-muffin tin

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Combine the eggs, milk, and oil; whisk in o the dry ingredients just until blended.
Using  the muffin tin, grease and flour six alternating cups.  Fill 2/3 full with batter. Sprinkle with bacon. Fill empty cups 2/3 with water.
Bake at 450 degrees for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees (do not open oven door). Bake 15 minutes longer or until deep golden brown.
