Title:
Scalloped Potatoes for 18 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 large potatoes, peeled and sliced
2 cups finely chopped yellow onion
1/2 cup butter + 2 Tbsp. olive oil
3/4 cup flour
5 cups chicken broth
1/3 cup mayo
2 teas. salt
1/2 + teas. pepper
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Saute onions in butter and olive oil.  Stir in flour until smooth.  Gradually add broth, mayo, salt and pepper.  Stir and cook until thick and bubbly.
In a large bowl, mix together the potatoes and the sauce.  Pour into greased baking dish.
Cover and bake at 350&#0176 for 90 minutes.  Top with cheese and bake, uncovered, for 30 more minutes.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
This potato dish is excellent served with ham for that special holiday meal.  Plan ahead as it takes 2 hours to bake.
