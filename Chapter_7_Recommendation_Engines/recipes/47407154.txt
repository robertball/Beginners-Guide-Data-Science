Title:
Jalapeno Pepper Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 jalapeno peppers (fresh), chopped
1 cup cider vinegar
3.5 cups sugar
1/2 bottle or 1 package Certo

Directions:
Combine peppers, vinegar and sugar in saucepan, and boil for 2 minutes only.
Remove from fire and add Certo.
Cook for additional 5 minutes
Pour in jelly glasses and seal.

Submitter: 
daf
Description: 
A Foreman family favorite, poured over cream cheese and served with crackers.
