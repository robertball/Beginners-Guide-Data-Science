Title:
Ranch Wheat Chex and Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box wheat Chex
1 package Hidden Valley Ranch Salad Dressing mix
1/4 c extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp garlic salt
3-4 small pinches dried dill (probably 1/2 to 1 tsp)
big handful each of toasted pecans, peanuts and almonds

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except Chex and nuts, stir well and let sit a few minutes.  Pour Chex and nuts in to a large bowl, pour oil mixture over all and stir well then let sit at least 30 minutes.  Bake in single layer on a cookie sheet at 250 for about 20 minutes.  Cool to room temperature and serve or store.

Submitter: 
lovetoeat
Description: 
This is a simple recipe for chex that I got from my sister in-law. It's a good healthy snack. You can change up the nuts (I like to add macademia nuts) but she said walnuts taste funny so you might not want to throw them in this mix.
