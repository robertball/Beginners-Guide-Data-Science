Title:
Christmas Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large butternut squash, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
1 large delicata squash, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
3 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
1 (2 pound) rutabaga, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
2 medium red potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
2 medium carrots, sliced
1 large onion, sliced
1/8 cup of extra virgin olive oil
6 dried bay leaves
2 Tbsp. dried rosemary
2 Tbsp. dried thyme
1 tsp. dried oregano
A dash of lemon juice
A dash of red wine vinegar
A dash of salt
A dash of pepper

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
In a large roasting pan, mix the butternut squash, delicata squash, sweet potatoes, rutabaga, red potatoes, carrots, and onion together.
In asmall bowl, mix the rosemary, thyme, and oregano together.
Drizzle the olive oil over the vegetables. Add the rosemary, thyme, and oregano mixture to the vegetables. Toss to coat the vegetables.
Add the bay leaves and drizzle the red wine vinegar and the lemon juice on top.
Bake uncovered for 1 hour and and thirty minutes, or until tender, stirring every 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Snowbunny
Description: 
An exquisite dish of roasted, winter vegetables, flavored with seasonings. It's a great side dish for beef, as well as pork and poultry.
