Title:
Indonesian chicken skewers with peanut sauce (Satay ayam) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/802511.jpg
Ingredients:
400 gr boneless chicken thigh ( cut aprox 1,5cm x 1,5cm x 1,5cm)
3/4 tsp of salt
a pinch of white pepper / black pepper
1 tbsp of sunflower/ peanut oil
* Mix them all, Marinate for 1 - 2 hours
Peanut sauce:
5 tbsp of peanut sauce
1 cup of water
2 garlic cloves (minced)
1/2 tsp of salt
2 tbsp of kecap manis (sweet soya sauce)
1 tbsp of brown sugar
1 tbsp of lime juice

Directions:
Marinate the chicken for 1 - 2 hours.
Soak the skewers to prevent the burning during the cooking.
Prepare the peanut sauce. Mix all ingredients in a small saucepan, cook it until it´s started to boil. Put it aside and add the lime juice.
Thread marinated chicken onto skewers. You will get approximately 24 skewers.
Add 2 Tbsp of peanut sauce to the chicken skewers and mix well.
Cook them in a grill pan for 1-2 minutes each side.
Serve satay ayam with the peanut sauce on top. Sprinkle it with fried onions.
