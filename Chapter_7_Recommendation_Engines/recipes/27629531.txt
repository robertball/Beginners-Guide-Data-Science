Title:
Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 package(s) 8oz. whole mushrooms
8oz block(s) cream cheese
1 log(s) Jimmy Dean Breakfast Sausage

Directions:
Brown sausage in a skillet. While browning, boil some water to rinse the sausage when browned.
Clean and stem the mushrooms. When the sausage is cooked and rinsed, return it to the skillet and add the cream cheese.
Heat on low until the cream cheese melts. Mix well.
Stuff each mushroom cap with mixture. Place on a cookie sheet and bake at 350.
