Title:
CHICKEN PEPPER STEAK Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1LB BONELESS CHICKEN BREAST
2 TEASPOONS MINCED GARLIN
1/2 TEASPOON ONION POWDER
1 TEASPOON SEASONED SALT
1 SLICED GREEN PEPPER
1 LARGE ONION CUT INTO LONG SLICES
4 ROMA TOMATOES
2 TABLESPOONS CORN STARCH
8 TABLESPOONS SOY SAUCE
1 TEASPOON CORN OIL
2 1/2 CUPS WATER

Directions:
HEAT OIL IN LARGE PAN.  WHILE OIL IS HEATING SEASON CHICKEN WITH SEASONED SALT AND ONION POWDER.  PLACE CHICKEN IN SKILLET COOKING UNITL 1/2 DONE, ADD GARLIC, 4 TABLESPOONS SOY SAUCE AND 1/2 THE ONION.  COOKING UNTIL CHICKEN IS DONE. WHILE CHICKEN IS COOKING MIX WATER AND CORN STARCH TOGETHER.  POUR INTO PAN WITH CHICKEN, COOKING ON MEDIUM HEAT.  ADD SLICED GREEN PEPPER AND REMAINING ONION AND SIMMER.  COOK UNTIL GRAVY HAS YOUR DESIRED THICKNESS.

Submitter: 
TIARA BRYANT-STOWE
Description: 
IF YOU LIKE THE TASTE OF PEPPER STEAK BUT REALLY DON'T ENJOY RED MEAT, TRY IT WITH CHICKEN!
