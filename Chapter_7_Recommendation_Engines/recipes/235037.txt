Title:
Easy Kahlua Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060422.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) box brownie mix (preferably Ghirardelli®)
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 egg
1/4 cup Kahlua

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray a 9x9x2-inch baking pan with non-stick cooking spray.
Combine the brownie mix with the oil, egg, and Kahlua in a mixing bowl. Mix until well blended. Pour into prepared baking pan.
Bake until top looks dry, about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kahlua®
Description: 
Mix Kahlua into brownie batter for rich, moist brownies with a hint of coffee.
