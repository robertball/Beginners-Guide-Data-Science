Title:
Pork Chops with Fennel and Rosemary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tsp olive oil
4 pork loin chops (bone-in), cut 1" thick
SEASONING:
2 tsp fennel seed
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp rosemary (leaves)
1 1/2 onion powder
3/4 tsp salt
3/4 tsp medium grind black pepper

Directions:
In blender or mini food processor container, combine seasoning ingredients; process until fine. Stir in oil; spread evenly on both sides of pork chops.
Grill over medium coals 14 to 16 minutes for medium doneness, turning once.
