Title:
Best Mac n Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
3 small cans tomato bisque soup (tomato soup works too)
2 7.5 oz pkgs Sargento 4 state cheddar cheese
1 7.5 oz pkg Sargento 6 cheese italian shredded cheese

Directions:
Cook and drain pasta, mix with soup and cheese, salt and pepper to taste.  Place in oven deep dish.  Place a few pats of butter on top.  cook covered for approximately 1 hour at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Cwby2nz
Description: 
This was a favorite recipe of my sister's in laws.  I added the different types of cheese and the spinach and meat in order to make it more nutrient rich.
