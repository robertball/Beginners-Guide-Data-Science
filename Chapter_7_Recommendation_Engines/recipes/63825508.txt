Title:
Walter Sands favorite bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups water
1 tablespoon honey
1/3 cup dry milk
2 tablespoons butter
2 teaspoons vital wheat gluten
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
4 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast

Directions:
Add all the ingredients in the order listed above to your bread maker. Makes a 2 lb loaf.

Submitter: 
Colleen Sopp
Description: 
Walter Sands was the father of the CEO of King Arthur. This is a perfect recipe for sandwich bread and for french toast bread.
