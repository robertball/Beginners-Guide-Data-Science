Title:
Chicken Madeira Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Chicken Breasts
1 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 Tbsp Butter
½ lb. Mushrooms
2 Tbsp Flour
(2 cups Chicken Stock plus 1 ½ cup Madeira) or
3 ½ cups Chicken Stock
Asparagus, about 8 stalks
Swiss or Mozzerella

Directions:
Sprinkle Chicken Breasts with salt & pepper; beat to ¼”
Heat Oil & add chicken 4-5 minutes per side;
Remove, cover, set aside
Sauté Butter & Mushrooms about 3 minutes until wilted
Add Flour & cook 2 minutes
Add Wine or Stock & bring to boil; lower heat, simmer 20 minutes, stirring frequently. Meanwhile, steam Asparagus
Add chicken to sauce, coat both sides. Cook 3 minutes until warm.
Top with asparagus & cheese
Drizzle with sauce; Broil 5 minutes.
