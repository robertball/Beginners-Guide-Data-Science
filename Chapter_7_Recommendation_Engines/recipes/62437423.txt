Title:
White Barbecue Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 and 1/2 c mayo
1/4 c water (sometimes I forget!)
1/4 c white wine vinegar
1 T coarsely ground pepper (I eyeball!)
1 T Creole mustard  (please track this down and save)
1 t salt (I also forget this!)
1 t sugar (I always remember this!)
2 garlic cloves pressed (VERY IMPORTANT!!!)
2 t prepared horseradish (also up there on the important list!)

Directions:
Whisk together until will blended.  Store in fridge for about a week.

Submitter: 
Dog Fish Head aka Fabius
Description: 
Easy to whip together.  Goes great with fish or chicken and can be stored in the fridge for about a week.  Definitely adds some excitement to your meals!
