Title:
Chocolate Snowflake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box chocolate or devil's food cake mix
1 egg
1 8 oz. tub frozen whipped topping (thawed)
powdered sugar

Directions:
Mix cake mix, egg, and whipped topping.  Mixture will be sticky!  Drop by tablespoon into a bowl of powdered sugar, and roll into balls .  Place on a greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 13-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
dutchkidzx2
Description: 
This is a very easy soft and chewy chocolate cookie that uses only 4 ingredients!
