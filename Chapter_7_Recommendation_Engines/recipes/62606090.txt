Title:
Easy Smothered Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. butter
2 pieces bacon, chopped (or use bacon bits)
3/4 c. diced celery
3/4 c. chopped onion
1 t. salt
2 pieces salmon

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet. Add bacon, celery, onion and fry until light brown. Place salmon in center of veggies, cover and cook on med/low heat until fish flakes (about 10 minutes). Some water can be added if needed.
