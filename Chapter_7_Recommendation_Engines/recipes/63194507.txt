Title:
Roasted Beets with Feta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium beets
1 T olive oil
1 t salt
pepper to taste
4 chopped scallions or green onions
juice from 1 large lemon

Directions:
Peel 4 medium beets and cut into 1/2-inch pieces.
Toss with 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 teaspoon salt, and pepper to taste on a baking sheet or glass baking dish.
Roast at 450 degrees F, stirring once or twice, until tender, 35 minutes.
Transfer to a bowl; toss with 4 chopped scallions and 2 teaspoons lemon juice.
Top with crumbled feta - to make the cheese hot pink, stir everything together.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Nicole Hanes Kosanovich
Description: 
I LOVE this recipe for beets! The lemon and green onion give it a light touch, and the feta mixed with the beets turns bright pink - making it a kid pleaser. Now I wish beets were always in season!!!!!! Recipe courtesy Food Network Magazine.
