Title:
Kahlua Cappuccino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089850.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Kahlua
4 fluid ounces brewed espresso
2 fluid ounces steamed milk
Dash ground cinnamon for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Stir together the Kahlua and hot espresso.
Spoon the steamed milk on top of the coffee and Kahlua mixture.
Garnish with cinnamon, if desired.
