Title:
Low Carb Pie Crust Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
5 ounces ground pecans
5 teaspoons SWERVE brown sugar packed
4 tablespoons Kerry Gold unsalted butter melted
1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon cinnamon powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â° F.
Mix ingredients.
Press into 8" pie pan, bottom and sides.
Place pie pan on cookie sheet.
Bake for 12 minutes.
Do not let nuts brown.
Fill with filling and bake or cool as needed.
