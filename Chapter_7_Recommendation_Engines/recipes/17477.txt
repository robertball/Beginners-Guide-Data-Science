Title:
Yummy Apple Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/601445.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups white sugar
3 eggs
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 cups chopped apples
1 cup maraschino cherries, chopped
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan.
In a medium bowl, cream together the oil, butter and sugar. Beat in the eggs, one at a time. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt, stir into the egg mixture. Finally, fold in the vanilla, apples, cherries, and walnuts. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
Bake for 75 to 90 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a toothpick. Cool in pan for 10 minutes before inverting onto a wire rack to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Gigi
Description: 
This is a moist and versatile cake. Use any type of fresh fruit in place of the apples.
