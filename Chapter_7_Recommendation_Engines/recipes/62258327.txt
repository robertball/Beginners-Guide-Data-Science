Title:
Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Saltines
3/4 c. brown sugar
2 sticks butter
12 oz choc. chips
1 c. chopped almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Line cookie sheet (regular size) with foil.  Spray foil with pam.
Line pan with saltines.
Melt sugar and butter in a saucepan.  Boil 3 minutes, stirring continuously.
Pour mixture over saltines and spread evenly.  Bake for 6-8 minutes.
Sprinkle choc. chips and almonds on top.  Place pan back in the oven for a few minutes to melt slightly.
Cool in frig.
