Title:
Pumpkin Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (15 oz) cans pumpkin
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 Tablespoons Pumpkin pie spice
or 2 Tablespoons Cinnamon, 1 Tablespoon Ginger, 1/2 Tablespoon Ground Cloves
3 eggs
1 cup sugar
14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
Topping:
3/4 box of yellow cake mix
1 1/4 sticks melted butter
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix all the pudding ingredients together and place in a 9 x 13 greased baking pan.
Gently sprinkle the yellow cake mix over the top of the pudding.  Over the cake mix layer on the chopped pecans and drizzle the melted butter over the top.  Place in a 350 degree oven for 1 hour.  Serve with whipped cream
