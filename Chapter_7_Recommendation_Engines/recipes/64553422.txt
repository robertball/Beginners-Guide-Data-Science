Title:
Overnight-light PB&J; Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup rolled oats
1/2 cup almond milk
1/2 tbsp chia seeds
1 tsp sugar
1/4 cup raspberries
2 tbsp powdered peanut butter (such as PB2)

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a covered container and refrigerate overnight.
Enjoy cold or hot!

Submitter: 
BathtubNinja
Description: 
This grab and go breakfast has it all - quick prep, easily customizable, great flavor with tons of health benefits.  Coming in a little over 200 calories, these oats are packed with whole grains, calcium, protein and necessary vitamins.
