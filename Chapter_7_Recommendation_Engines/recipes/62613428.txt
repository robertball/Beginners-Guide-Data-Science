Title:
Apple Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
36 caramels
1T water
30 large marshmallows
1/3c butter, cubed
4 medium tart apples, peeled and cut into 1/4inch slices
1/3c chopped dry roasted peanuts
1/3c mini semisweet chocolate chips
3T sprinkles

Directions:
In a microwave safe bowl, melt caramels with water and stir.
In a large saucepan, melt marshmallows and butter. Arrange apple slices on a large platter.
Drizzle with caramel and marshmallow syrup.
Sprinkle with peanuts, chocolate, and sprinkles.
