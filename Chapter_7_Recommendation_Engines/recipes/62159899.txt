Title:
Pan Fried Chive Flowers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Fresh chive flowers washed and well drained
1 clove garlic, minced fine
1 Tbsp Butter
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 pinch of salt
1 pinch fresh black pepper

Directions:
wash and dry chive flowers
pre heat fry pan at medium high heat
add 1 tbsp olive oil
add 1 tbs butter
add chive flowers and garlic
add pinch of salt
fry over heat, turning often until soft and tender
grind fresh pepper on top and serve

Submitter: 
StevenRN
Description: 
This is a great side dish to meat or fish. Its very easy to make and a good way to use up all those purple flowers that chives makes. Make sure when you pick them the flowers are still young and tender
