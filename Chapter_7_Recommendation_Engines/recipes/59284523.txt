Title:
Banana Cream Pie III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked-I use deep dish
3 bananas
2 cups cold milk
1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
1/2 package instant banana pudding mix
1 cup fresh whipped cream (unsweetened)
1- 1 1/2 cups fresh wipped cream (sweetened)

Directions:
Slice 2 of the bananas and arrange 1-1 1/2 banana in bottom of baked and cooled pastry shell.
Pour cold milk into a medium bowl. Add pudding mix and using hand held mixer blend until thoroughly combined on high speed. Fold in 1 cup of the unsweetened whipped topping and mix together well.
Pour half into into pastry shell over bananas. Layer the leftover of your first two bananas over the pudding mixture and than cover with the rest of the pudding mixture.
Chill at least 3 hours before serving. Garnish with sweetened whipped topping and slices of the 3rd remaining banana.
