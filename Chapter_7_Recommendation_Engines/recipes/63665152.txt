Title:
Michele's Crunchy Berry Parfait Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018276.jpg
Ingredients:
6 oz vanilla non-fat greek yogurt
1 teaspoon lemon zest
1/3 cup granola
1/2 cup fresh sliced strawberries
1/2 cup fresh blueberries
1 teaspoon sugar (optional)

Directions:
Add 2 tablespoons of granola into the bottom of each parfait glass.  Spoon two tablespoons of yogurt on top of that.  Sprinkle 1/2 teaspoon of lemon zest on each one.
in a small bowl add the strawberries and blueberries.  Sprinkle with sugar and stir to coat the berries.
Place one-third of the berries on top of the yogurt layer.  Add 2 tablespoons of granola and repeat layers until parfait glass is full.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
Enjoy this delicious, healthy, versatile parfait for breakfast or as a snack or dessert.
