Title:
Easter Egg Nests Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups chow mien noodles (1 large bag)
1 bag butterscotch chips
3/4 c peanut butter
36 jelly beans

Directions:
Break chow mein noodles into small pieces; set aside in large mixing bowl.
Combine butterscotch chips and peanut butter in small pan and melt and stir over medium low heat.
Pour peanut butter mixture over crunched up noodles; toss until completely coated.
Cool slightly, then butter hands and take approximately 1/4 cup of mixture and roll into a ball.
Make indentation in center of ball so that mixture resembles a birds nest.
Place on wax paper and let cool completely.
Place 3 jelly beans in each nest.
