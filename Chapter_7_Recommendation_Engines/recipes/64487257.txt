Title:
MA's Coco Pecan Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cooking spray or butter
1/4 cup powdered sugar
1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
1/2 cup light brown sugar
1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
3 eggs
1/4 tsp almond extract
1 tsp vanilla extract
3 cups self rising flour - sifted
3 TBLs cocoa powder
2 cups finely chopped pecans
Kosher salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Butter or spray 9x13 baking pan and dust with powdered sugar
In a large mixing bowl cream butter with sugars until sugars are almost dissolved.
Add eggs and extracts, beating until just combined.
Combine flour and cocoa.
Using a large wooden spatula or spoon, stir in pecans, flour and cocoa, one cup at a time, until flour is incorporated.
Dump into prepared pan, spread evenly and sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup chopped nuts and kosher salt.  Press lightly to even out batter.
On a cookie sheet and on middle rack, bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes.
