Title:
Baked Chicken with Creamy Lemon Butter Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken, Rotisserie Chicken - Plain
1 tbsp(s), Smoked Paprika
1.00 grams (1/4 tsp), Coarse Kosher Salt
1 lemon, Juice of 1 Lemon or 1/4 c approx of white wine or combination
lemon zest as desired
4 tbsp(s), Butter - Unsalted, divided
2 cup, Low Sodium Chicken Broth
6 fluid ounce, Pasture Raised Heavy Whipping Cream
9 cloves, Garlic, raw
0.75 cup(s), Parmesan cheese
2 tbsp(s), Thyme, fresh
0.25 cup, Fresh Raw Parsley or basil

Directions:
preheat oven to 400
cut up rotisserie chicken and place in greased baking pan
season with smoked paprika, if desired
melt 3 T butter in saute pan
saute garlic until fragrant
stir in stock, cream,  lemon juice/wine, lemon zest
add cheese, thyme, parsley/basil, continue to stir
bring to boil then reduce heat to simmer until starts to thicken
salt lightly
pour sauce over chicken
bake 400 for 30 min
