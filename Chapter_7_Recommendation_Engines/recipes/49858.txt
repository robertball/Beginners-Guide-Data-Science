Title:
Baked Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3207354.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package soft style cream cheese with pineapple
3/4 pound shredded Swiss cheese
2 cups dried cranberries
2 tablespoons orange juice
1/4 cup apple juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, blend the cream cheese, Swiss cheese, cranberries, orange juice, and apple juice. Scoop into a 9 inch pie pan.
Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Kelly M.
Description: 
This warm and cheesy fruit dip has become a favorite with friends and family! The blend of cheese and fruit creates a wonderful flavor. Although it can be served with crackers, it's especially wonderful served with sliced apples and pears.
