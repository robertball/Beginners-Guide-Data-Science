Title:
Hoisin Hamburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 teaspoon sesame oil
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper (adjust to taste)
1 tablespoon sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
1/4 cup chopped green onion
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl mix together all ingredients until evenly distributed.  Then divide the meat mixture into 4 patties and grill over medium heat for about 10 minutes per side.
Serve on hamburger buns and top with your favorite burger toppings.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
These burgers include the asian flavors of hoisin, sriracha, and sesame oil with a little crushed red pepper for a little added heat.
