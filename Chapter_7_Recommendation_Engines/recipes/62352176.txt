Title:
Fried Bologna Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/398426.jpg
Ingredients:
4 slices bologna
2 slices american cheese
1 egg
1 tablespoon butter or olive oil
1 tablespoon Hot mustard
2 thick slices tomatoes
1/4 cup shredded lettuce
Salt and pepper, to taste, optional
2 thick slices Italian bread or regular white bread

Directions:
In a dry pan, over medium heat, fry the bologna until slightly browned on both sides; set aside.
In the same pan, fry the egg in 1 tablespoon of  butter or olive oil until done to your liking.
Place bologna, cheese and fried egg on one slice of bread.
Top with lettuce and tomotoes.
Season with salt and pepper, to taste, optional.
Spread other slice of bread with hot mustard, and place on top of sandwich and cut in half.
Serve and enjoy.
