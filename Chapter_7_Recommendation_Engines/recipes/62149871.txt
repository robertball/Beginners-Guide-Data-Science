Title:
Stuffed Artichoke Hearts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 artichoke hearts (canned, drained very well)
1 tsp. lemon pepper
1 tsp. garlic salt
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 Tbsp. sour cream
2 Tbsp. chives, chopped
1/2 c. butter, melted
1 c. grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Drain arthichoke hearts well and trim bottoms so they will stand. Press thumb into the center of each heart to form a pocket. Sprinkle with lemon pepper and garlic salt.
Mix the cream cheese, sour cream and chives together. Stuff each heart with a little of the cream cheese mixture. Dip the tops in the melted butter and roll in the parmesan.
Place on a cookie sheet or in a shallow baking dish. Bake for about 45 minumtes or until tops are golden brown.
