Title:
Sonte's Slow Cooker Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/438459.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons olive oil
6 - 8 potatoes, scrubbed and diced, or if you prefer, peeled
and diced
1 onion, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 celery stalks, with leaves, washed and diced
1 medium carrot, scrubbed and grated
2 litres water
2 generous Tablespoons Vegetable Stock powder
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
fresh ground pepper, to taste
1 1/2 teaspoons dried sage leaves
1 teaspoon crushed thyme leaves
2 bay leaves

Directions:
Sprinkle olive oil over bottom of slow cooker.
Dice potatoes,onion, celery and place in Slow Cooker.
Add grated carrot and minced garlic.
Pour water over all.
Stir in Vegetable Stock powder.
Add salt, pepper and herbs.
Stir.
Place lid on slow cooker, and cook on HIGH heat for 6 hours.
To serve, ladle Potato Soup into soup bowls, top with a bit of shredded cheddar cheese and cooked, crumbled Vegie Delights Bacon Rashers.

Description: 
While doin' time in Apple Tree Creek I found myself cooking without a stove! Thank the Heavens for Slow Cookers and Electric Frypans! Bannock done in the electric frypan is a nice accompaniment to this soup.
