Title:
Green Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2pkgs. (10 oz. each) frozen peas ,defrosted
1 cup celery,finely diced
1/2 cup scallions, finely diced
1pound bacon , cooked and crumbled
1 cup sour cream
Salt/Pepper

Directions:
Combine peas,celery,scallions,bacon,sour cream and salt&pepper to taste. Chill thoroughly

Submitter: 
Patti
Description: 
Serve as is or on a bed of lettuce
