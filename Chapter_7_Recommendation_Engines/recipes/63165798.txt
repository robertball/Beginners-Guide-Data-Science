Title:
Bread Pudding II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 gallons of day old bread
1 cup butter, melted
3 3/4 cups raisins
1 1/2 cartons liquid eggs
10 cups milk
5 cups of heavy cream
5 1/2 cups white sugar
3 tbls. ground cinnamon
3 tbls. vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Break bread into small pieces and place into greased baking pans. Drizzle melted butter or margarine over bread and sprinkle with raisins.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine eggs, milk,heavy cream, sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla. Beat until well mixed. Pour over bread, and lightly push down with a fork until bread is covered and soaking up the egg mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until the top springs back when lightly tapped.
