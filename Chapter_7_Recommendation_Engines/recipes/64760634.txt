Title:
Slow Cooker Bacon Ranch Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.75 lbs chicken breast
1 c chicken bone broth
16 oz cream cheese
1 c sour cream
1-2 T of ranch herb mix:
1 T garlic powder
1 T or less onion powder
1 T salt -- use LESS next time
1 tsp dill weed
1 T dried chives
1 T dried parsley
2-3 slices bacon chopped

Directions:
season chicken breasts with 1-2 T of ranch herb mixture
put in crock pot with 1 c bone broth
add cream cheese, sour cream
cook low for 6-8 hours
use immersion blender to blend cream cheese mixture
shred chicken and return to crock pot
continue cooking on low for 2-4 hours or until done
add water or salt if needed
sprinkle bacon on top, serve with green onion, parsley if desired
