Title:
Delicious Home made Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 lb Italian sausage
1 medium sized onion
3-4 lg cloves of garlic, minced
4 cups of fresh mushrooms, thinly sliced
29oz can of tomato sauce
29oz can of tomato puree
10.75oz can of diced tomatoes
1/2 tablespoon rosemary
1 tablespoon oregano
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1/2 tablespoon sweet basil
1/2 tablespoon garlic salt
1 teaspoon of pepper
1/8 cup white sugar (may be more or less, depending on taste)

Directions:
Chop onion
Cook onion, Italian sausage, and ground beef in frying pan until done, drain
In lage pot, combine tomato sauce, puree, diced tomatoes, garlic, mushrooms, meat mixture, and spices
Simmer on low at least until hot, if you're in a hurry, otherwise, the longer it simmers, the better the flavors go through
Stir in sugar about 10 minutes before serving, the amount may be more or less than 1/8 cup, depending on personal preference

Submitter: 
Jennifer Witt
Description: 
Flavorful, thick, hearty, yet simple and delicious sauce that the family loves!
