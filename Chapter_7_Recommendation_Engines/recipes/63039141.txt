Title:
Peanut Butter Chocolate Waffle Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Frozen Waffles
2 Tablespoons of Peanut Butter
1 Cup* of Chocolate chips
* The amount can vary

Directions:
Use a toaster oven or oven to make the waffles.
Put the waffles in the oven or the toaster oven and set for 2-3 minutes or until you think the waffles are half way done.
Take the waffles out and spread the chocolate chips on top of both the waffles and set them in the toaster oven for another 2-3 minute. Soon the chocolate chips should be melted. Use a fork or toothpick to make sure that they are fully melted.
Then apply the peanut butter smoothly on both waffles, mixing the chocolate in with the peanut butter.
Close the two waffles together and enjoy!
