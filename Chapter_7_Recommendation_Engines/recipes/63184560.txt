Title:
Tomato and Mozzarella Pasta al Forno Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/819723.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. evoo
2 cloves garlic crushed
2 14oz cans diced Italian plum tomatoes
1tsp. dries oregano
S&P;
1 lb. dried tube pasta
8 oz mozzarella cheese, cubed
2/3 c. Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet and cook garlic for 1 minute
Add tomatoes and oregano simmer rapidly, stirring occasional until thick. Add salt and pepper. Cook pasta, toss with sauce. Place half of pasta into a 9x13" Cover with half parm and half mozzarella. top with remaining pasta, mozzarella and parm. bake at 400 for 15 minutes.
