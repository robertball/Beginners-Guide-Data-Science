Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 - 10 tart apples, peeled
1 c white sugar
1 c brown sugar
1 c butter
1 1/2 c oatmeal
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 c flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Slice or chop apples and place in 9 X 13 pan.
Mix together remaining ingredients and spread on apples.
Bake 30 - 45 minutes or until apples are tender and topping is golden brown.
