Title:
Chunky Crockpot Chili Recipe

Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (16 oz) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes w/ jalape√±o, undrained
2 cups tomato sauce
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 green pepper, chopped
1 cup corn
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 teaspoons hot sauce
1 tablespoon chili powder
Salt/Pepper to taste
Shredded cheddar cheese
Dollop of sour cream

Directions:
In a skillet, lightly brown beef and drain. Transfer to a slow cooker. Add the next ten ingredients and stir. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours or on high for 4 hours. Garnish individual servings with cheddar and sour cream.
