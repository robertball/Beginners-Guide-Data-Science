Title:
Chicken-Parmesan Pasta Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/973546.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound fettuccine, uncooked
1 tablespoon oil
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
3 tablespoons tomato paste
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 (14.5 ounce) can Hunt'sÂ® Diced Tomatoes, undrained
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup KRAFT Zesty Italian Dressing
1/4 cup KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed on package, omitting salt.
Meanwhile, heat oil in large nonstick skillet on medium heat. Add chicken; cook and stir 8 min. or until done. Stir in tomato paste and garlic powder; cook and stir on medium-high heat 1 min. Add tomatoes, water and dressing; cook 2 min., stirring occasionally.
Drain pasta; place in large bowl. Add sauce; toss to coat. Sprinkle with cheese.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
Description: 
Chicken strips simmered in a chunky tomato sauce with cheese are tossed with hot cooked fettuccine--this dinner's on the table in 25 minutes!
