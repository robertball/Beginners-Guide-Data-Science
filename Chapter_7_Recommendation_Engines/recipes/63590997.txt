Title:
Onion Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup plus 2 Tbsp. water
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 Tbsp. margarine or butter, softened
3 1/4 cups white flour
2 Tbsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. milk powder
1 1/2 tsp. salt
2 3/4 tsp. yeast

Directions:
Place all ingredients in bread pan in order listed.
Select "white bread" setting on bread machine and "1 1/2 lb loaf size".

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
A nice savory bread that works great in meat and cheese sandwiches!
