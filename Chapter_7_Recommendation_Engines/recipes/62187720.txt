Title:
Pumpkin Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (29 ounce) can pumpkin puree
1 to 2 cups apple juice
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 TO 1 cup white sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250*
Combine pumpkin, apple juice, spices, and sugar in abowl; stir well.
Pour mixture into a 9x13 baking pan and place in oven.
Cook, stirring every 15 minutes until the pumpkin butter is thick, browned and the consistancy you like.

Submitter: 
Kat G
Description: 
This pumpkin butter is an excellent hostess present, and is a wonderful spread for toast and muffins.  It can also be used to make a spectacular holiday dessert, Pumpkin Pecan Cheesecake.Modified from: Pumpkin Butter
