Title:
Mother's Corn Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can creamed style corn
1 can drained whole kernal corn
2 eggs (3 if small)
1/4 cup sugar or Splenda
Dash of salt
2 tablespoons butter
1 cup cream

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and pour into a buttered casserole
Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes or until knife pulls out clean from center
**If doubling the recipe you will need to use a shallow baking dish. If using a deep dish it will not cook firm in the center.

Submitter:

Description: 
Creamy corn pudding that everyone requests for me to bring to their home for the holidays.
