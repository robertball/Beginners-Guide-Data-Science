Title:
Bisquick Yeast Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. yeast
3/4 cup warm water
2 1/2 cups Bisquick
1 T. sugar
1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Dissolve yeast in water, 10-15 minutes.
Add Bisquick and sugar.
Knead 12-15 minutes.
Shape into rolls and place in lightly greased pans.
Cover and let rise 1 hour.
Brush with melted butter.
Bake 12-15 minutes in a 400 degree oven.

Paula
Description: 
An easy roll recipe using Bisquick.
