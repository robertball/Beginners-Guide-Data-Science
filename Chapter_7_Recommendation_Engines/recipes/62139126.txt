Title:
italian style tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package firm tofu
3 cloves garlic finely chopped
5 sundried tomatoes diced
4oz feta crumbled
2 to 3 chopped roma tomatoes finely chopped
1 teaspoon italian seasoning
red pepper flake to taste
2 table spoons olive oil
3 to 5 basil leaves fresh basil chopped
marinara for dipping
2 table spoons parmisan cheese
1 to 2 cups italian style bread crumbs{enough to coat each piece}

Directions:
cut tofu into 1 inch cubes, press out excess liquid. Place all ingrediece [except bread crumbs, parm cheese, and marinara and one table spoon of olive oil} in large flat bowl.  marinate at least 8 hours mixing occasionaly. place breadcrumbs and parmisan cheese on a plate, take each piece of tofu making sure to keep the pieces of feta,garlic,tomatoe etc. on tofu as much as possible. roll in cheese and bread crumb mixture place on oiled pizza pan and cook on 350 for 20 to 30 minutes make sure to turn each piece one or two times. You may cook a little loner for crispier tofu. Serve with marinara dipping sauce.
