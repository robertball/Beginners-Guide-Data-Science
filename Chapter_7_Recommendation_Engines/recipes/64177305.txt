Title:
Uncle Lou's Caramel Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups Powdered sugar
1/2 cup salted butter
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
6 TB. heavy cream
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
place powdered sugar in large bowl melt butter over low heat and add brown sugar and cream, whisk until lumps are dissolved. bring to boil over med. heat. remove from heat and stir in vanilla. Por hot butter cream mixture into powered sugar. beat at high speed with electric mixer 2 min. or until blended. cool one hour stirring occasionallyl
