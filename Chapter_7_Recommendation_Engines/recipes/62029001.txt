Title:
Tar Heel Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Chocolate Chips
1 cube Butter/ Margarine--Melted
1 cup Chopped Pecans
1 tsp. Vanilla
1/2 cup Flour
1/2 cup Brown Sugar
1/2 cup Sugar
2 Eggs--Beaten
1/2 cup Shredded Coconut
1 9in. unbaked Deep Dish Pie Shell

Directions:
Pour warm melted butter over 3/4 cup Chocolate chips,to make a "chocolate syrup", blend dry ingredients and stir into Chocolate mixture, stir in beaten Eggs and Vanilla, add remaing 1/4 cup UNMELTED Chocolate Chips and optional Coconut.
Pour mixture into pie shell and bake in 350* oven 35 to 40 minutes.
Top will be set and begin to slightly crack, best if a bit underbaked.
To Serve warm in microwave for a few seconds to melt the unmelted Chocolate chips.
Can be topped with a dollop of whipped cream, but none is really needed.

Submitter: 
GRAYJETMAN
Description: 
This pie is best if underbaked and a bit gooey like a brownie inside. This is a very rich pie, so small slices are advised.
