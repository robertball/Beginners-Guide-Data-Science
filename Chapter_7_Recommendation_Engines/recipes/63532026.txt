Title:
Hodge Podge  WW Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Minestrone Soup
1 can mild Rotel
1 can Beans
8 oz ground Turkey
italian seasoning

Directions:
Brown ground turkey with salt, pepper and italian seasoning. Minced garlic is good also.
Dump cans into a pot with all the juice, and add the browned turkey.
Season to desired.
