Title:
Artichoke Heart Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 lb lean ground turkey
1/2 lb cherry tomatoes, halved
1 15 oz. can each of butter beans, pinto beans, garbanzo beans, red beans, and black beans
1 small glass jar of marinated artichoke hearts
1 small red onion, chopped OR 3 scallions, chopped
1/8 cup Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp garlic, minced
1 Tbsp spicy brown mustard
2 Tbsp Maple Syrup
2 tsp cayenne pepper
3-4 Tbsp cumin
1 Tbsp turmeric
1 Tbsp basil
1 Tbsp marjoram
3-4 dried ancho chilies
1/4 cup shredded sharp cheddar or jalapeno jack cheese
crushed red pepper to taste
cracked black pepper and sea salt to taste

Directions:
Drain beans and mix in a crock pot set on high heat
In a small frying pan, warm oil and onion or scallions until slightly translucent. Add to crock pot.
In the same frying pan, brown ground turkey with garlic and Worcestershire sauce. Add to the crock pot
Add artichoke hearts and spices.
Shred cheddar cheese into the mixture and top with crushed red pepper to taste. Mix and simmer until you can't resist the smell any more... then enjoy!
Note: You can also use diced jalepeno or habanero peppers to increase the heat of this dish as you please!
