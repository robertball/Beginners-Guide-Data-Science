Title:
French's® Crunchy Onion Chicken™ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990499.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups FRENCH'S® Original or Cheddar French Fried Onions
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
4 (5 ounce) boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Place French Fried Onions and flour into plastic bag. Lightly crush with hands or with rolling pin. Transfer to pie plate or waxed paper.
Dip chicken into egg; then coat with onion crumbs, pressing firmly to adhere. Place chicken on baking sheet.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 20 minutes or until no longer pink in center.

Submitter: 
French's®
Description: 
French Fried Onions make a crunchy and delicious coating for tender baked chicken breasts.
