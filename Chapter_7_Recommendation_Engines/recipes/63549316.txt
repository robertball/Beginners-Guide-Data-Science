Title:
Paul Prudhomme's Fork in the Road Oven fried catfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (4-5oz.) catfish fillets
1/2 cup bread crumbs
2 TBS. Finely chopped fresh parsley
3 TBS. Finely chopped green onion
1 TBS. oil
Seasoning mix:
1 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. dried basil
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. cumin
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 450 degrees.  Sprinkle fish with seasoning mix.  Mix bread crumbs with parsley, onion, and oil.  Press fish into crumb mixture and shake of excess. Cook 6 minutes presentation side up. Turn over and cook 6 more minutes. Turn over again and cook 4 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.

Submitter: 
iceemama
Description: 
This recipe for catfish is from chef Paul Prudhomme's Fork in the Road cookbook. It is my favorite way to have fish. It is called oven fried but it doesn't get crispy like fried fish really is.
