Title:
San Francisco-Style Sourdough French Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
PHASE 1
1 1/2 cups sourdough starter
1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup water
PHASE 2
1/4 cups + 3 T warm water
1 1/2 t to 2 t salt
2 T olive oil
1 T sugar
2 cups flour
PHASE 3
1/2 cup flour

Directions:
Mix Phase 1 and put in bread machine, let sit for an hour or more.
Then add Phase 2 ingredients and process on dough setting, when done in bread machine continue rising in a lightly-oiled bowl.  Let rise until double approx 4-6 hours, in a warm location.
After dough has risen, remove from bowl and Knead in remaining flour from Phase 3. Knead for time required for dough to feel smooth and elastic (fingertip in dough should spring back).
Shape dough into loaf shape and place on a cookie sheet.  Cover and rise until doubled, 1 to 3 hours.
350 degrees, DO NOT PREHEAT OVEN, place bread in oven and back until nicely browned, 30 to 45 minutes.
