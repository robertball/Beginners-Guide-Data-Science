Title:
Mushroom Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion, thinly sliced
2 lbs. mushrooms, sliced
2 large cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons cornstarch
2 cups veggie stock
2 tablespoons Braggs Liquid Aminos (unfermented soy sauce, available in health food stores)

Directions:
Heat olive oil to just smoking. Sauté mushrooms until it starts to get soft. Add garlic and cook until mushrooms are browned. Add the onions and sauté till soft.
Add 1 1/2 cups veggie stock and simmer.
Mix cornstarch with remaining 1/2 cup of veggie stock and add to gravy. Add Bragg’s Liquid Aminos. Bring to boil to thicken.
Serve immediately or reheat.
