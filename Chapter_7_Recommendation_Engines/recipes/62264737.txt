Title:
Olive Oil Flatbread Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c water
2 tsp salt
2 tblsp sugar
1/2 olive oil
6 c unbleached flour
4 2/4 tsp bread machine yeast
extra flour as needed

Directions:
In the bread pan add the liquid ingredients first, the salt, sugar and flour next. On top of the mound of flour place the yeast carefully in small indentations. Start the machine on dough setting.
After the dough is completed, divide into three equal parts, setting aside 2. on a lightly flour surface roll out the dough very thinly, makeing a rectangular shape of 17x11-inches. Dust the pan lightly with corn flour, and place dough on top.
In 350 degree oven, bake for 10 minutes (12 for crisper crust).
Place favored toppings and bake again for 10 mins or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
This recipe makes a very smooth, and easy to handle dough. It also uses a bread machine to make it!Modified from: Olive Oil Flatbread Pizza Dough
