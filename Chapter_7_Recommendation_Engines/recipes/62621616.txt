Title:
Mateo's mindblowing meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large bag of frozen meatballs
1 (12oz) jar of chili sauce
1 (12 oz) jar of grape jelly

Directions:
In a crockpot, add the bag of meatballs, a jar of chili sauce and a jar of grape jelly. Mix and cook in the crockpot until the meatballs are heated all the way through.
