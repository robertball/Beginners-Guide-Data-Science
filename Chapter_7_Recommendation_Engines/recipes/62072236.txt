Title:
Stuffed Reuben Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One recipe of bread dough (enough for one loaf of any kind. White, Rye etc.)
1/3  cup thousand island dressing
1  cup sauerkraut, drained
1/2  lb swiss cheese slices
1/2  lb corned beef

Directions:
Roll out dough into a rectangle as thin as you can get it, about 1/4 inch thick.
In order given, layer ingredients down the center third of the dough(long ways).
Slice the outer thirds into one inch intervals.
Starting at the top, weave the ends and tuck them under while working down to the bottom of the loaf.
Place on a greased cookie sheet and brush with beaten egg wash (1 tablespoons water and 1 egg) and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Knightcraft
Description: 
Delicious stuffed reuben bread loaf. You can mix and match your favorite sandwich stuffings and enjoy with little work. The prep time doesnt include making the bread dough.
