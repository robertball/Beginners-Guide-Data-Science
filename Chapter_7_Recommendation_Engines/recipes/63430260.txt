Title:
Craisen-Orange Chex Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6051266.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c Corn Chex cereal
3 c Rice Chex cereal
3 c Wheat Chex cereal
1 cup shelled pistachios
1/4 c butter, melted
1/4 c brown sugar
1/4 c frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
1/2 c Craisens

Directions:
In an ungreased large roasting pan, mix cereals and pistachios and set aside.
In microwaveable bowl combine butter, brown sugar, and orange juice; microwave on high for 30 seconds.
Stir and pour over cereal, stirring until evenly coated.
Bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes, stirring midway.
Stir cranberries.
Spread on wax paper to cool.
