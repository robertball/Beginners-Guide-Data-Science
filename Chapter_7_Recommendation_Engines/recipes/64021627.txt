Title:
Ricotta Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Golden Butter or Yellow cake mix - prepared but not cooked
1 lb. ricotta cheese
3 tsp. vanilla
3 eggs
1/2 cup sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Prepare cake as directed and pour into an ungreased 13x9 pan.
Mix remaining ingredients and pour evenly over top of cake mixture.
Bake for 1 hour or until golden brown.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar when cool.
Keep refrigerated.
