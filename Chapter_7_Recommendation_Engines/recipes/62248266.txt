Title:
Grilled Balsamic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons olive oil
6 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
3 cloves minced fresh garlic
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast

Directions:
Mix olive oil, balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, salt and fresh pepper until the olive oil and vinegar are no longer separated.
Add 4 boneless chicken breast and marinate overnight (the longer you marinate this the better it is).
Cook on grill over medium heat for 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Crystal S
Description: 
Wonderful, easy dish to prepare with gourmet qualities!  Serve with a crisp green salad and a glass of your favorite white wine!
