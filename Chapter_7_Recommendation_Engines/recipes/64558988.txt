Title:
Sunset Rum Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 oz spiced rum, such as Kraken®
.75 oz white rum, such as Captain Morgan®
.75 oz dark rum, such as Myer’s®
.75 oz. hibiscus syrup (see recipe)
.75 oz. almond orgeat syrup, such as Torani®
1 oz. fresh-squeezed lime juice
1 oz. fresh-squeezed orange juice
1 oz. fresh-squeezed grapefruit juice
Orange slice, for garnish
Maraschino cherry, for garnish

Directions:
Add spiced, white and dark rums, hibiscus syrup, almond syrup, lime juice, orange juice and grapefruit juice with ice to cocktail shaker.
Shake for 30 seconds to chill.  Fill tiki mug with crushed or chipped ice and strain cocktail from shaker into mug.
Add your favorite tiki garnishes, such as an orange pinwheel with maraschino cherry.

Submitter: 
Matt Wencl
Description: 
When it’s late and you’re hanging ‘round the fire pit with friends watching the sunset, this is the rum punch crowd-pleaser to pass around. Full of bright citrus, floral notes and plenty of good rum, you’ll all have a delicious time before the day is done.
