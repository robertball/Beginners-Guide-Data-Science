Title:
Annie's Fresh Strawberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint strawberries
1¾ cups flour
½ tsp. baking soda
½ tsp. salt
1/3 cup shortening
2/3 cup sugar
2 eggs

Directions:
Chop (don’t mash strawberries.
Sift together flour, baking soda, salt.
Cream together separately: shortening, sugar.
Add eggs and beat well.
Add flour mixture alternately with 1 cup chopped strawberries at a time and beat till smooth.
Turn the mixture into a well-greased loaf pan and bake in a pre-heated 350 degree oven for 70 minutes, or till a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
This is moist and delicious, especially when it's warm.  You can use overripe or even bruised strawberries without sacrificing the taste.
