Title:
Genevieve's Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2667120.jpg
Ingredients:
16 ounces ziti pasta, cooked per box instructions
3 T olive oil
1 onion
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 c fresh parsley (or dry  3:1 fresh to dry ratio)
1/2 c fresh basil
16 ounces sausage
24 ounces part-skim ricotta cheese
16 ounces part skim milk mozzarella cheese
8 ounces parmesan cheese, grated
4 ounces goat cheese
1 whole egg, beaten
32 ounces spaghetti sauce

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 375 degrees.Cook ziti per directions on box.  Heat EVOO and garlic. Brown onion. Add meat and cook until no longer pink. Mix meat with cooked ziti pasta, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, goat cheese,  1/2 parmesan cheese, egg,  and 1 1/2 cups sauce.   Place mixture in a 9x13 pan (or 2 9x9 pans- freezes well). Top with remaining sauce and Parmesan cheese. Bake for 30 minutes.  Let stand for 15 minutes before serving.  This is also really good with spinach.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
This is an versatile recipe! Any meat can be used and it always turns out delicious. A crowd pleaser and easy to double or triple for a large group.
