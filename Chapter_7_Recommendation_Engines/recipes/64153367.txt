Title:
Mango Ginger Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp neutral oil (canola)
1/4 cup chopped dried mango
2 teaspoons minced ginger
1 cup jasmine or basmati rice
2 cups water
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup green onion chopped
(2 tbsp cilantro optional)

Directions:
over medium low heat saute mango and ginger for 2 to 3 minutes until fragrant
add rice and saute stirring often, until rice appears translucent, 5 minutes or so
add water and salt
when water boils reduce heat and cover pot with lid
cook covered for 20 minutes or until all water is absorbed
transfer hot rice to serving dish and top with green onion and cilantro if using
