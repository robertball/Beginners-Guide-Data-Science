Title:
Skillet Beef and Twists Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 c chopped onion
1/2 c thinly sliced celery
1 c sliced mushrooms
1 can (16 oz) tomatoes in puree
1 t salt
1/2 t dried thyme leaves
1/8 t ground black pepper
1/2 c sour cream
6 oz. twist macaroni, cooked, drained

Directions:
In large skillet, stirring frequently, cook beef over medium high heat 7-8 min. or until browned.  With slotted spoon, remove beef.  Drain off all but 2 T. fat.  Add onion and celery.  Saute 3-4 min. or until tender crisp.  Add mushrooms, cook 1-2 min. or until tender.  Add browned beef, tomatoes, salt, thyme and pepper.  Stirring occasionally, cook 5 minutes.  Stir in sour cream; cook until heated through.  Do not boil.  Spoon over macaroni.
