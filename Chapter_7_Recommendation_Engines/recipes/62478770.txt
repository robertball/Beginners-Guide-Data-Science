Title:
Yummy Ugly Pumpkin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/484200.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1 cup pumpkin
1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)
2 cups flour
1 tsp soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well. Drop by the teaspoonful on an ungreased cookiesheet. Bake at 375 10-12 min.

Submitter: 
muddyh2os
Description: 
These cookies aren't shaped and therefore called ugly. But their light pumpkin flavor and cake-like texture makes them yummy!
