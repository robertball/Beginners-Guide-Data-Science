Title:
Thai Beef Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976463.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
1 tablespoon fish sauce
1 teaspoon Market Pantry™ Sugar
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 teaspoons serrano chiles, finely chopped
2 tablespoons Thai basil, chopped
1 pound Sutton and Dodge flank steak
3 cups Archer Farms™ green leaf salad

Directions:
Heat grill.
In a small bowl, combine vinegar, lime juice, fish sauce,  sugar, garlic, Serrano chile and basil; mix well.
In a 2-gallon resealable plastic food-storage bag, combine 1/4 cup  marinade with steak (reserve remaining marinade for dressing),  turn to coat. Let stand until grill is ready.
When ready to grill, remove steak from marinade. Discard plastic  bag with remaining marinade. Place steak over medium heat;  cover. Cook for 12 to 15 minutes, turning once, until steak is  desired doneness. Let steak stand for 5 minutes, slice diagonally  across grain.
Place mixed greens on serving plates. Top with steak. Drizzle with dressing.
