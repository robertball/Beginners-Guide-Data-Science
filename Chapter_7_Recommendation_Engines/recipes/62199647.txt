Title:
1 2 3 No Bake Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
You will need:
1 8 or 9 inch graham cracher crust
1 cup sugar
1 can of fruit topping (pie filling) of your choice ( I use strawberries) or fresh fruit
1 teaspoon lemon extract (optional)
1 8 oz. container cool whip ( or any whipped topping)
2 8 oz. packages cream cheese -cooled to room temp
3 tablespoons vanilla extract

Directions:
1. Mix together cream cheese, sugar and flavor in a bowl until smooth. Fold in whipped topping.
2. Pour mixture into crust. Smooth evenly. Top with fruit of your choice.
3. Refrigerate at least 2-3 hours until firm.

Submitter: 
cafecook
Description: 
This is my favorite easy cheesecake recipe. When I want cheesecake in a flash, I make this no bake style. I love to share it with friends and family. It's as easy as 1 2 3!
