Title:
Roasted Italian Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 bell peppers (red, yellow and orange), cut into 1 inche pieces
2 medium size zucchinis, sliced into 1 inch round pieces
1 large red onion, quartered
1 8 oz. box of white mushrooms, quartered
1 tbsp. dried oregano
1 tbsp. dried parsley
salt and pepper to taste
2 lemons, juiced
1/4 to 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Place the vegetables in a roasting pan.
In a small bowl add all the other ingredients and stir.
Pour over the vegetables and mix to combine.
Roast in the oven for 1 hour or until vegetables are tender, mixing half way through.
