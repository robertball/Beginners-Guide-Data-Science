Title:
Texas Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small bottle of Zesty Italian
2 cans black eyed peas, drained
Juice of 3 limes
1 medium red bell pepper
1 medium green bell pepper
1/2 large red onion
2 Avocados (not too soft)
1 large tomato
1 bunch of cilantro
1.5-2 TB chopped pickled jalapenos (or to taste)
2-3 TB jalapeno juice (or to taste)

Directions:
Chop everything into bite-size pieces and mix it all together.
Serve with Fritos Scoops or tortilla chips of your choice.

Submitter: 
Kemberli Paes
Description: 
Delicious, chunky, Mexican dip!  Serve with Fritos Scoops or regular tortilla chips.  Everyone asks for this recipe when I make it!  It's a favorite!
