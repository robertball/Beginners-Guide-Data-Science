Title:
Cajun Chex Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 stick margarine )or healthy alternative!)
1 T. parsley flakes
1 tsp. celery salt
1 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
4 to 8 drops hot pepper sauce
2 2/3 c. Wheat Chex
2 2/3  c. Corn Chex
2 2/3 c. Rice Chex
1 can (2.8 oz) french fried onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250ÂºF. In open roasting pan melt margarine in oven.  Remove.
Stir in parsley, celery salt, garlic, cayenne pepper and pepper sauce. Gradually add cereals, stirring until all pieces are evenly coated.  Bake, stirring every 15 min. Add onions; mix well. Spread on absorbent paper to cool. Store in airtight container.
