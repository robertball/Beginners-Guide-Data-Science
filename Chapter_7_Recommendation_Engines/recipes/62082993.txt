Title:
Hot Curry Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz. pkgs. cream cheese - softened
4 TBSP. Mayonnaise
2 TBSP. Worchestershire Sauce
3 TBSP. Curry Powder
1/2 C. chopped green onion
1 C. Small fresh cooked shrimp (may add more)

Directions:
Cream together first five ingredients.  Stir in shrimp.  Spoon mixture into a greased, medium sized, oven safe serving dish.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until top begins to bubble.
Serve hot with crackers, melba toast baguettes and/or fresh sliced baguette.
