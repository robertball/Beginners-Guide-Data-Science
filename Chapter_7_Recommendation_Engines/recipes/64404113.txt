Title:
Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups thinly sliced potatoes
3 tbs butter
3 tbs flour
1 cup milk
1/2 cup half and half
1 tsp salt
1 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 cup grated cheese to sprinkle on top

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a sauce pan, melt butter and blend in flour.
Let sit for a minute.
Add all milk and half and half slowly, stirring with a whisk.
Reduce heat and stir in cheese.
Place half of the potatoes in a lightly greased 1 quart casserole dish.
Pour half of cheese sauce over potatoes.
Repeat with second layer of potatoes and cheese sauce.
Sprinkle remaining cheese on top and sprinkle with paprika.
Bake for 1 1/2 hours.
