Title:
Shrimp in Sweet and Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound large shrimp, peeled, deveined and butterflied
1/2 red pepper, cut in 1 inch cubes
1/2 green pepper, cut in 1 inch cubes
1 medium onion, cut in lengthwise strips
1 20 ounce can of pinapple chunks, juice reserved
3/4 cup sugar
1/3 cup white vinegar
2/3 cup of reserved pineapple juice
1/4 cup lite soy sauce
1 tablespoon ketchup
2 tablespoons cornstarc
oil

Directions:
Make the sweet and sour sauce by mixing the sugar, vinegar, pineapple juice, soy sauce, ketchup and cornstarch in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil, stirring continuously until the mixture thickens. Keep warm.
In a wok or a large frying pan, add enough oil to coat and heat on medium-high. Add peppers and onions and stir fry until peppers begin to lose their bright color and the onion starts to become translucent (about 5 minutes). Add pineapple chunks and heat, stirring, for one minute. Place the ingredients into the warm sauce.
Add oil to the wok or pan and bring to medium high heat. Add the shrimp and stir fry until they turn pink. Do not overcook.
Add the shrimp to the sauce. Stir, and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
A flavorful sweet and sour sauce coats shrimp, peppers and onion. The dish is as colorful as it is delicious. This recipe is also quick and easy.
