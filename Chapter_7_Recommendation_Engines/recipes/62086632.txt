Title:
Hawaiian Haystacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked rice
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 tsp basil
1 tsp parsley
1 Tbsp olive oil
4 chicken breasts, cubed (or 1 can veggie fry-chik chopped and drained)
1 cup chow mein noodles
1 cup chopped tomatoes
1 cup chopped pineapple
1/2 cup chopped green onions
1/2 cup shredded coconut
1/2 cup toasted sliced almonds
1 cup chopped green pepper
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In medium skillet prepare sauce by sauteeing chicken in olive oil until cooked through. Add chicken broth and allow to simmer about 5 minutes. Add mushroom soup, parsley and basil. Transfer to small bowl and set aside. To serve: Begin with rice, then sauce, and top with remaining ingredients to set to individual tastes and enjoy!

Submitter: 
J
Description: 
Rice, sauce, chow mein noodles and veggies topped with coconut and pineapple make an interesting stackable meal that can be catered to almost anyone's tastes.
