Title:
Taco Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984171.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (1.25 ounce) package ORTEGA® Taco Seasoning mix
1 (16 ounce) package macaroni, cooked, rinsed and drained
1 (16 ounce) jar ORTEGA® Salsa Prima Homestyle Mild
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup sliced ripe olives
3/4 cup sliced green onions

Directions:
COOK ground beef and seasoning according to taco seasoning package directions. Combine macaroni, salsa, ground beef mixture, cheese, olives and onions; mix well. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
Serve with warm flour tortillas and tall glasses of Nestea iced tea. Ground turkey or chicken can be used in place of ground beef.
