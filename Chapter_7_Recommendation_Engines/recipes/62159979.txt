Title:
Carrot Raisin Salad (Bunny Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 carrots
2 apples, chopped
1 rib celery, chopped
1 tbl lemon juice
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup vanilla (or maple) yogurt
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Grate carrots in food processor. Add apples, celery, lemon juice, raisins, walnuts, yogurt, and cinnamon. Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
Vanessa
Description: 
This is great for Easter, but we love it anytime! My kids love making and eating Bunny Salad, and they have no idea they are voluntarily ingesting veggies. =)
