Title:
Jan's Cherry Vanilla Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3387441.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick unsalted butter, room temp
1 16 ounce bag of  powdered sugar
3-5 tablespoons cherry juice from a 10 ounce jar of maraschino cherries with stems
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
pinch of salt

Directions:
Beat softened butter until light and fluffy.  Add in half of the sugar and mix well.
Add in 3 tablespoons cherry juice and extract.  Blend well.  Add in the remainder of the sugar and add the salt.  Blend until smooth adding in the last tablespoons of cherry juice, one at a time,  if frosting is too thick. Whip until light and fluffy, about two minutes.
Frost two 8 inch cakes or about 30 cupcakes.
Use the cherries to top the frosting with.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
Maraschino cherry juice adds color and flavor to vanilla buttercream frosting.  Use the cherries to decorate your baked goods. This frosting is especially good on rich, dark chocolate cake!
