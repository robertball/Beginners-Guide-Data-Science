Title:
Baked Chicken wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs chicken wings cut into pieces, tips removed.
5 stalks of celery, 3 carrots, 1 Tablespoon honey, few drops of chili oil also to the wings before simmering
1.5 cups of chicken stock or water with better than bouillon added to it
paprika to season the wings 2 teaspoons
Texas Pete's Red Hot Buffalo Sauce to season wings 1/2 cup or so

Directions:
PreHeat oven to 425 F.  Place cut wings, 1.5 cups water (and better than bouillon chicken stock mixture) or chicken stock, 3 carrots, 3 celery stalks, a few drops like 1/2 teaspoon of hot chili oil,  honey, and paprika in a deep sided covered dish and simmer for 18 minutes.
Spay a baking sheet with Pam. Transfer the cooked wings to the baking sheet and bake for 30 to 35 minutes until crispy turning them once half way through. After they are baked, put wings and 1/2 cup or sauce mix in a pan and low simmer to coat the sauce to the wings to your liking. Stir frequently to coat.
Serve with Blue Cheese and Ranch Dressing with Celery sticks on the side.
