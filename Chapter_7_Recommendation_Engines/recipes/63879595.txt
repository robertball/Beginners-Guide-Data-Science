Title:
Simple Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1076237.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c cooked rice
1 1/3 c milk
2 eggs, beaten
1/3 c sugar
1/8 tsp salt (omit if the rice was salted while cooking)
1 tsp vanilla
nutmeg to sprinkle

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients well. Pour into a well-buttered 9x9 baking dish. Bake 45-50 minutes or until set. Sprinkle with nutmeg before serving.

Submitter: 
KateShaw
Description: 
This is a great way to use up leftover rice of any kind. It's good hot or cold. I love it because it's firmer than most rice puddings and it's not too sweet or too eggy.
