Title:
Honey Glazed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978238.jpg
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup honey
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F. Grease rectangular pan, 13x9x2 inches. Place chicken in pan. Mix remaining ingredients; pour over chicken.
Cover and bake 20 minutes; turn chicken. Bake uncovered 20 to 30 minutes longer or until juice of chicken is no longer pink when centers of thickest pieces are cut.
