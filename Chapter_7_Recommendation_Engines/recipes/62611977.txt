Title:
Quick Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken breasts from two rotiserie chickens
2 10 3/4 oz. cans cream of chicken
1 cup cheddar cheese
1 8.25 oz. can corn
1 8.25 oz. can sliced carrots
1 15 oz can sliced potatoes
2 deep dish pie crust
2 rolled pie crust (for top)
Garlic pepper to taste
Garlic salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425Â°.  Cut up the breasts from the two chickens into small chunks and add to bowl.  Combine the following with chicken: cream of chicken, cheese, corn, sliced carrots (sliced smaller if desired), sliced potatoes (sliced smaller if desired), and season to taste with garlic pepper and garlic salt.  Divide in half and pour into pie shells.  Cover each pie with one of the rolled pie crust and cut a slit in the tops.  Bake for 35 minutes.
