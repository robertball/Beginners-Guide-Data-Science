Title:
Chicken Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Chicken (cut up and skin removed)
5 cups Water
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 cup Celery, sliced
1 cup Carrots, chopped
1 cup Peas, frozen
7 Tbsp Flour
1 cup Milk
3 cups Chicken & vegetable broth, reserved
1/4 tsp Salt
1/8 tsp Pepper

Directions:
1. Place chicken, water, and 1/2 tsp salt in a large pot.  Bring water to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 2 hours or until chicken is tender and broth is flavorful.  Reserve chicken broth. Debone chicken and set aside.
2. Cook celery, carrots and peas in a small amount of salted water until tender.  Drain vegetables and reserve broth.
3. In another saucepan, combine flour, milk, 3 cups reserved broth, 1/4 tsp salt, and pepper.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until thickened.  Stir in chicken and vegetables.
4. Pour into 2 qt casserole and bake at 400 degrees until bubbly, about 20 minutes.  Serve over baking powder biscuits.
