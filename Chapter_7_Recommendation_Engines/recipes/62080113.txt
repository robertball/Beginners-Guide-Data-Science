Title:
Chicken Chowder Ole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken broth
2 medium white or red potatoes, cubed
1 carrot, shredded or chopped fine
1/2 cup chunky Salsa
1 T. diced green chilis
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 to 1-1/2 cups cooked chicken, in
bite size pieces
1-1/2 cups frozen corn
2/3 cup milk
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/4 tsp. ground Cumin
3/4 cup grated Cheddar Cheese
Optional:  Garnishes such as jalapeno slices, tortilla chips, avacado slices

Directions:
In large saucepan, bring broth, potatoes, and carrots to a rolling boil, cook until both are sfot.  With potato mashes, mash about 1/2 of the potato pieces to thicken soup.  In separate bowl, combine salsa, chilies, cream of chicken soup, pieces of cooked chicken, corn, black pepper, and ground cumin.   Add to pan with potato broth.  Add milk to pan.  Stir to blend, and cook on low for about 30 minutes.  When soup is warm, add grated cheddar, and stir often, until cheese is melted and blended into soup.  Serve in bowls with a garnish of tortilla chips, extra green chilis, avocados or jalapenos,if desired.

Description: 
Very quick to make, satisfying for a cold or rainy night's dinner.  Add a salad and some warm tortillas or bread for a satisfying warming meal.
