Title:
Liz Wendt German Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6802978.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Russet Potatoes
1 lb Bacon
1 cup Sugar
1 cup Apple Cider Vinegar
1 tablespoon Rustic Mustard
1 bunch Green Onion
1 teaspoon Pepper

Directions:
Start with a pound of bacon. Fried crispy ish. Save the grease. Add I cup sugar and one cup apple cider vinegar to the bacon grease and just bring to a summer add pepper. Let sit. Boil a pound of potatoes till very soft. Peel them and cut them them up and add the bacon mix, mustard and chopped green onion. Stir
