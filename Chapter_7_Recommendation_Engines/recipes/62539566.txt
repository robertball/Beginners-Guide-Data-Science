Title:
Eggplant "Caviar" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. oil
1 eggplant (1 1/4 lb.), cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 medium  onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 medium ripe tomatoes (3/4 lb.) cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1/2 c. basil leaves chopped
1/4 c. fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves, choppped
3  T. fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Heat 1 T. oil in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.  Add eggplant, onion, garlic and salt to taste, and cook, stirring often, about 7 minutes, or until eggplant begins to brown.  Reduce heat, cover and cook, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes more, or until eggplant is tender.  Transfer to bowl and let cool.
Add tomatoes, basil, parsley, lemon juice, and remaining 1 T. oil, and salt and pepper to taste.  Blend well and serve at room temperature.
