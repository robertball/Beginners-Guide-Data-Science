Title:
Salsa Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 8-oz pkgs cream cheese
1 c. salsa, any kind, fresh or frozen
1 12-oz can black beans, drained and rinsed
2 c. shredded cheddar  cheese

Directions:
Whip cream cheese until light and fluffy.
Add salsa; whip well scraping sides of bowl often.
Fold in beans and cheese.
Chill for 1-2 hours before serving.
Serve with tortilla chips, crackers, etc.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
Light and creamy, this salsa dip may be scooped up with tortilla chips or crackers. So good you'll want to lick the bowl clean.
