Title:
Eggplant & Tomatoes &  Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium eggplant sliced as thin as you can
1 large can diced tomatoes, drained
4 tablespoons parmesan cheese
6 tablespoons pecorino romano cheese, shredded
4 oz. sliced boneless skinless chicken breast
2 cloves garlic chopped
1 cup chopped fresh basil

Directions:
Slice the eggplant. Salt the slices and layer in a colander. Add two layers of paper towel and put a heavy object on top. Let drain for about 45 minutes
Layer 1/4 of tomatoes on bottom
eggplant
and 1/4 mixture of the cheese and basil
continue with chicken and top with remainder of tomatoes and cheese. Bake in 425 degree oven for 35 minutes covered. Take out and carefully drain as much liquid as you can and set the oven on broil. Broil uncovered until the cheeses brown.
