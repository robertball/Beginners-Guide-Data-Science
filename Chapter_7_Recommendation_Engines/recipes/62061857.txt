Title:
Apple Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- one apple (peeled and cored)
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 3 scoops vanilla ice cream
- 2 tablespoons sugar

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a blender.  Blend until smooth.
Serve.

Description: 
The most unique and refreshing milkshake around.
