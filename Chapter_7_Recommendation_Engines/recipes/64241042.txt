Title:
Swedish coffee Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
2 pkgs. yeast or 4 1/2 tsp.
1 cup sugar
6 1/2 cups bread flour
30 cardamom seeds or 4 tsp. ground cardamom
1/2 cup butter
1/2 tsp. salt
cinnamon sugar & butter for the tops

Directions:
Scald milk and let cool
Soften yeast in warm water & add 2 TBSP. sugar
Add yeast to milk and 3 cups of flour and beat well
Cover and let rise until double
Grind cardamom if using the seeds
Cream butter, sugar,& cardamom together then mix into dough
Add salt and remaining flour
Knead well, cover and let rise until double
Breakdown & knead again and shape into 3 loaves
Put into greased bread pans cover and let rise until double
Bake at 375 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes
Remove from pan and generously butter the tops of the bread then sprinkle with cinnamon sugar

Submitter: 
Suzanne
Description: 
This is a recipe that my Great Great Grandmother brought with her from Sweden.  It is a lot of work but worth every bit.  Everybody loves when I make this.
