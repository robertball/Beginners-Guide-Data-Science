Title:
Sauza® Raspberry Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979792.jpg
Ingredients:
9 ounces Sauza ® Silver Tequila
3 ounces Dekuyper® Razzmatazz
24 ounces Margarita Mix
GARNISH:
Lime Wedge

Directions:
Combine the ingredients without ice and shake vigorously. Then just pour the mixture over ice in a 14 oz glass, garnish and enjoy the raspberry sensation.

Submitter: 
Sauza
Description: 
Enjoy the fruity, tangy flavor of raspberry in this summery margarita made with flowery white tequila and served on ice.
