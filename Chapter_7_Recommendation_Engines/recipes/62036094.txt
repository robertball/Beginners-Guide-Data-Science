Title:
Cheesecake Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese
1 stick butter (no substitutes)
1/2 tsp vanilla
2 tbsp brown sugar
3/4 c powdered sugar
3/4 c mini semi sweet chocolate chips
crushed walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Cream cream cheese and butter.  Add vanilla, and both sugar and mix well.  Fold in chocolate chips.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.  Remove, form in ball and roll in crushed nuts.  Refrigerate for at least 1 more hour.  Place on platter and serve with graham crackers and chocolate graham crackers.

Submitter: 
Tina McKellar Musial
Description: 
This is great if you are looking for a sweet appetizer to share.  I have made this 3 times and brought home 3 clean plates!
