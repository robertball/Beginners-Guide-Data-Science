Title:
Dark Chocolate Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 cup water
2 tbsp water
1 cup sugar
Pinch of salt

Directions:
Whisk everything together in a large bowl.
Refrigerate the mixture until chilled.
Freeze following the directions for your ice cream maker.
