Title:
Potato Salad with Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Minnimum of 6 Red potatoes, boiled, sliced into bite size pieces, skin on.
About 1/2-3/4Cup Mayonnaise (to taste)
1/4-1/2tsp Cummin
1/2 tsp Garlic Salt
1/4 tsp Finely Ground Mustard
1/4-1/2tsp Fresh Ground Pepper
1/8tsp Celery Seed (optional)

Directions:
Boil Potates, set aside (leave skin on and do not mash). Cook bacon in a seperate pan, set aside to cool, chop into bite size pieces. Combine all ingredients in large serving bowl. Keep in mind all seasonings are to taste, sub in what you like best.Be careful when mixing not to mash potatoes ( i find it best to stop boiling just before they're too over done, helps them hold up better.) Keep refridgerated when not enjoying.

Submitter: 
Jessica Harper
Description: 
This dish never survives one day in our house. Forget about leftovers. Goes great with all grill items. Perfect for summer.
