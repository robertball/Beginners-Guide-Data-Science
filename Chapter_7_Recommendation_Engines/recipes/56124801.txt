Title:
Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
7 cups water
2 tea bags
2 cups water
1 can frozen lemonade
1 can frozen orange juice
2 cups vodka (not more than 3 cups)
ginger ale

Directions:
Boil 7 cups of water and add the sugar
Steep tea bags in 2 cups of water
Mix together in an ice cream pail
Add lemonade, orange juice and vodka
Freeze in ice cream pail stirring occasionally to distribute the slush forming  on the sides
Scoop into glasses and top with ginger ale

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
Nice summertime slush with flavours of tea and citrus.Modified from: Orange Cream Slush
