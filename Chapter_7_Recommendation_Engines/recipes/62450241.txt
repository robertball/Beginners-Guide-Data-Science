Title:
Cranberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
3/4 cup sugar
1-1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 cup fresh cranberries
1/2 cup of nuts (ie. walnuts or other meaty nut)
1 tsp grated orange peel
1 egg, beaten
3/4 cup orange juice
2 tbsp cooking oil

Directions:
mix all ingredients together in a large bowl.
Beat just until all ingredients are mixed.
pour batter into a greased and floured loaf pan.
bake at 350 for 50 - 60 minutes or until knife inserted in centre comes out clean.
**for high altitudes, bake at 375.

Submitter: 
Catherine Parnell-Proulx
Description: 
My mom's cranberry bread recipe from Nova Scotia. fairly moist and full of flavour - great for serving with coffee or tea!
