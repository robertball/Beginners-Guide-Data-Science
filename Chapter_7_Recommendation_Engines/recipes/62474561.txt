Title:
Suckling pig Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
13lb pig-remove intestines
evoo
16 garlic
thyme
8 bay leaves
1 apple
1/3 c wine (Marsala)
salt and pepper
roasted potatoes, garlic, veggies, and apple sauce to serve.

Directions:
Wash pig, pat dry, rub with oil, salt & pepper.
Wrap foil around ears and tail, place a rock in mouth.
Layer garlic and herbs on bottom of pan, bake 375* 30 min, basting.
Reduce to 350* bake 1 3/4 hours baste every 20 min.
Broil to crisp skin.
Replace stone with apple
Pour out excess fat and add wine, reduce to 1/2 to make gravy.
