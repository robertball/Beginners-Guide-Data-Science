Title:
Grecian Skillet Rib Eyes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (1/2 pound) well-trimmed beef rib eye steaks, cut 1 inch thick
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
1 tablespoon chopped, pitted Kalamata or ripe olives
Seasoning:
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves, crushed
1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano leaves, crushed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Combine seasoning ingredients; press into both sides of beef steaks.  In large nonstick skillet, heat oil over medium heat until hot.  Place steaks in skillet; cook approx. 10 to 14 minutes for medium rare to medium doneness, turning once.  Sprinkle with lemon juice.
To serve, sprinkle cheese and olives over steaks; garnish as desired.

Submitter: 
Cattlemen's Beef Board and National Cattlemen's Be
Description: 
Fran Yuhas won the 1995 National Beef Cook-Off Grand Prize with this Greek-inspired steak recipe. The steak is lightly seasoned with garlic, basil, and oregano, pan-fried in olive oil, and garnished with fresh lemon juice and feta cheese.
