Title:
Stuffed Pepper Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/971912.jpg
Ingredients:
6 small or 3 large green peppers
1 large onion
2 cloves of garlic
2 tablespoons butter
1 pound ground Italian sausage
1 pound ground beef
2 cans (14.5 oz) diced and peeled tomatoes
1 can tomato paste
2 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup
2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
2 cups cheddar cheese
1 cup cooked white rice
oregano, salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions and place to the side.
Chop up onion, pepper and garlic.
In a large pot melt butter add garlic, pepper and onion and saute for 2 minutes. Add sausage and ground beef and brown until cooked throughout (add spices to taste). Drain fat.
Add cooked rice, tomatoes, tomato paste and Worcestershire sauce heat for approx 5 minutes.
Pour green pepper mixture into a baking pan.
Add tomato soup into a small bowl and whisk with 1/4 can of water until it has a saucy consistency, pour on top of stuffed pepper mixture.
Top mixture with cheddar cheese and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
