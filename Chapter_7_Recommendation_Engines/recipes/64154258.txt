Title:
Marianne's Perfect Lemon Curd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsalted butter, cubed
3/4 cup sugar
3 eggs
3/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1 tablespoon grated lemon zest

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar together.
Add eggs one at a time.  Beating until fluffy each time.
Add lemon juice and zest.
When fully mixed, pour into 2 quart saucepan.
Cook over medium-low heat until thick enough to hold marks from whisk, and first bubble appears on surface, about 6 minutes.
Stir constantly.

Submitter: 
Shearone
Description: 
Wonderfully tart, classic English lemon curd ... perfect with scones and tea. Marianne says, You have to mix the ingredients like you are making a cake.  Using this method makes a perfect curd with no egg curdles.Modified from: Perfect Lemon Curd
