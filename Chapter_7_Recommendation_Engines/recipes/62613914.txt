Title:
Sticky Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2c balsamic vinegar
1/2c low sodium soy sauce
1/2c chicken broth
1/4c sugar
1/4c brown sugar
3T honey
3 minced garlic cloves
2T grated ginger
1/8t pepper
2-3lb chicken breasts

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except chicken until sugar is dissolved.
Refrigerate chicken in marinade 24 hours.
Place all in skillet and boil, cover, and simmer 15 minutes.
Remove chicken, cook off marinade to thicken.
Return chicken to warm.
