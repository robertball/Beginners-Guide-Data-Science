Title:
Wild Rice and Sausage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound  sausage
2 teaspoons Northwoods Seasoning
1 medium onion, chopped
1 cup celery, chopped
1 cup wild rice
1 cup brown rice
1 cup white rice
1 10 3/4 ounce can cream of mushroom soup
1 10 3/4 ounce can cream of celery soup
1 10 3/4 ounce can cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1 4 oz. can water chestnuts
1 package small real bacon bits
1/2 cup slivered almonds
1/2 cup sunflower kernels

Directions:
Brown sausage in large skillet.  Season with Northwoods seasoning. Set aside. saut√© onion and celery in pan drippings.
Meanwhile, cook rices according to package directions.
Combine sausage, rices, soups, sour cream, water chestnuts, bacon, almonds, and sunflower kernels and place in 9x13 baker. Bake for one hour at 350f.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
This hearty casserole is a wintertime favorite with us! It makes a lot and freezes well, too.
