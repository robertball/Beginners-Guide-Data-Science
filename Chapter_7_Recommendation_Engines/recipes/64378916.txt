Title:
Slow-Cooker Beef Short Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 1/2 lbs boneless beef short ribs
1/4 cup butter
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup beef broth
3/4 cup red wine vinegar
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons ketchup
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons minced garlic
1 teaspoon chili powder

Directions:
Put flour, salt and pepper in a bag.
Add ribs and shake to coat.
Brown ribs in butter in a lg skillet.
Put in slow cooker.
In same skillet, combine remaining ingredients.
Bring to a boil, stirring.
Pour over ribs.
Cover and cook on low for 9 hours.

Submitter: 
Melissa
Description: 
I found this recipe online while searching for a slow cooker meal that cooks for many hours for a mom who works for many hours.
