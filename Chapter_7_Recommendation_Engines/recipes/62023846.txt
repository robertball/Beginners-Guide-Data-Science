Title:
Janet's Chicken and Rice  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups rice
1 can cream of chicken
1 can cream of celery
1 can cream of mushroom
1 1/2 cans of milk
1/2 stick of butter, melted
6-8 chicken thighs or your choice of cut
salt and pepper

Directions:
Stir souup and milk together. Pour over rice in 9x13 inch glass casserole dish and stir to mix. Place chicken pieces on top and pour melted butter over chicken. Salt and pepper to taste. Bake at 325 degrees until chicken done, 1 to 2 hours, depending on cut.

Suzanne Cook
