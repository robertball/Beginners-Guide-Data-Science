Title:
Chocolate Plunge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup Karo light corn syrup
1/2 cup heavy or whipping cream
1 cup (8 oz) semi-sweet chocolate morsels
Assorted fresh fruit

Directions:
In medium saucepan stir syrup and cream.  Bring to boil over medium heat.  Remove from heat.  Add chocolate; stir until completely melted.  Serve warm as dip for fruit.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This chocolate dip is so amazing with fruit or just about anything you can dip in chocolate.  I like this recipe because it doesn't harden when it cools.  It also works great in a fondue pot.
