Title:
Chocolate Eclair Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
----- Eclair -----
1 box Graham Crackers
2 small boxes French Vanilla instant pudding
3 cups cold milk
12 oz. container Cool Whip, thawed
--- ICING ----
1 cup white sugar
1/4 cup milk
1/3 cup cocoa
1 stick butter
1 tsp. vanilla
½ cup pecans, chopped

Directions:
----- ECLIAIR -----
Combine budding mix and milk.  Let set.  Fold Cool Whip into pudding.
Lightly butter or spray 9" x 13" pan with cooking spray.
Line bottom of pan with graham crackers.  Layer on ½ of pudding mixture. Add another layer of graham crackers, then the rest of the pudding mixture.  Top with a lawyer of graham crackers.
-----ICING-----
Optionally, lightly toast pecans on a pan in a 250° oven for a few minutes.
Bring sugar, cocoa, and milk to a boil. Boil 1 minute; remove from heat.
Add butter and vanilla.  Stir until butter is melted.  Cool slightly.  Pour and spread over graham crackers.  Sprinkle with pecans. Refrigerate 2 hours to overnight and serve.

Description: 
Reminds you of a Chocolate Eclair - creamy filling on the inside and chocolate icing on top.
