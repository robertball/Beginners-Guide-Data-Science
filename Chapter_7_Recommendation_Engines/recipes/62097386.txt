Title:
Broccoli and Seafood Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. imitation crab, shredded
1/2 c. cooked broccoli, chopped
2 tablespoons real bacon bits
1/3 c. mayonnaise
dash dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Serve.

Submitter: 
SPICYGIRL
Description: 
A quick easy seafood salad made with imitation crab.  Can be eaten as is or as a sandwich.
