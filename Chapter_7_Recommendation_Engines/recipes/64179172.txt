Title:
Margie's Disappearing Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2080694.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C. sugar
1/2 c. butter (softened)
1/2 t. vanilla
2 eggs
2 c. flour
1/4 t. salt
1 t. baking soda
1/2 c. sour cream
1 c. mashed bananas (approx. 2 large)
OPTIONAL: 1/4-1/2 c. favorite nuts or wheat germ

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Add sugar to softened butter, then eggs and vanilla. Next add flour, salt, baking soda and bananas.
You may add wheat germ at the end if you are not fond of nuts or forget altogether.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes.
When a toothpick comes out clean it is done.
