Title:
Lettuce Wrap Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breast, minced
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp ginger, grated
1 tbsp garlic, minced
¼ cup onion, chopped
½ cup mixed nuts, chopped
½ cup shiitake mushrooms, chopped
2 tbsp hoisin sauce
3 tbsp honey
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
Lettuce leaves

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large skillet/wok over med/high heat. Add chicken, onions, ginger and garlic.
Saute until no longer pink. Add mixed nuts and mushrooms. Stir.
Cook for 5 minutes.
Mix together hoisin, honey, soy sauce, sesame and apple cider vinegar in small bowl.
Add sauce mixture to chicken. Stir until combined.
Cook until sauce is absorbed.
Season with salt if necessary.
Serve on lettuce leaves topped with pickled ginger, sriracha/tabasco, more hoisin if desired.
