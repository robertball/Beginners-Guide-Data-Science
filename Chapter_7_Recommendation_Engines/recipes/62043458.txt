Title:
Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. round steak
8 oz. tomato sauce
14 oz. diced tomatoes
1 large onion, diced
2 carrots, shredded
2 celery, chopped
2 TB brown sugar
1 tsp. worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup flour
4 TB butter

Directions:
Cut steak into individual pieces and dredge in flour and set aside.  Melt 2 TB butter in large skillet and saute onions, celery and carrots until crisp.  Remove from skillet.  Add remaining butter and brown steaks.  Return vegetables to skillet with meat.  In a small bown, mix tomato sauce, tomatoes, brown sugar, and worcestershire sauce together and pour in with meat and vegetables.  Pour into crock pot and cook on low for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
I've done this recipe in a crock pot or even my pressure cooker but I'm sure it could be simmered on top of the stove or in the oven.  The flavors are even better the next day.  It's great with mashed potatoes!
