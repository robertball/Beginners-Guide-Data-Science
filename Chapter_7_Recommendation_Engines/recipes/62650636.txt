Title:
Southwestern Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef or ground turkey
2 cups tortilla chips, crushed
1 16oz jar picante sauce (hot, med or mild), divided use
2 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
1/2 cup green onion, chopped
1 8oz can tomato sauce, divided use

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large mixing bowl combine ground meat, tortilla chips, 1/2 of picante sauce, eggs, chili powder, green onions and 1/2 of tomato sauce. Mix ingredients.
Place mixture in a 2 1/2 quart uncovered casserole dish and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until done. Drain grease.
Mix remaining picante sauce and tomato sauce in a small bowl. Pour mixture over meat loaf, return to oven and bake an additional 15 minutes
