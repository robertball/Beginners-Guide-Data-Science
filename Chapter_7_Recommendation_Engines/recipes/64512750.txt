Title:
Scalloped potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium size potatoes
1 can each cream of mushroom and cream of celery soup  (any two cans of cream soup would work)
2 cloves minced  garlic
1 medium chopped onion
Salt and pepper to taste
1 stick butter
1/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1-1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese  (your favorite cheese will do)
Paprika
1/2 soup can water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
Slice potatoes to 1/4 inch thickness.
Arranged on greased baking dish.
Melt butter in sauce pan on medium heat.
Add onion and garlic to melted butter.
Sautee until tender.
Add soups and 1 cup cheddar cheese.
Stir in 1/2 soup can of water.
Once cheese is melted and mixture is thoroughly combined, pour over potatoes.
Mix potatoes and sauce mixture so all potatoes are coated.
Top with remaining cheddar cheese.
Cover and bake in oven for 2 hours or until potatoes are tender when tested with a fork.
Remove from oven and sprinkle with bread crumbs and paprika.
Bake additional 15 minutes uncovered.
