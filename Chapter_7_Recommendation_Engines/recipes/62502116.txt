Title:
Cheesecake for a Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. white cake mix (+ eggs, water and oil)
3 cups icing sugar
2 pkg. cream cheese
1/2-3/4 cup orange juice concentrate
3/4 grated orange (use 1/4 to garnish)
1 container Cool Whip
1/4 tsp. almond extract

Directions:
Bake white cake in a cookie sheet for 20 minutes.
Mix icing sugar, cream cheese, Cool Whip, orange juice concentrate, grated orange and extract.
Let cake cool and spread icing on it.
Garnish with orange rind.
