Title:
Chocolate Chip and Bran Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1926907.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1/2 C. Butter, softened
1/2 C. Brown sugar
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs, beaten
2 C. Bran Flakes, crushed
1 C. Coconut, fine shred
1/2 C. Quick oats
1 C. Chocolate chips
1 Tbsp. Light cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
Blend flour, b. powder and b. soda together in a small bowl. Set aside
Combine softened butter and sugars in a large bowl and break down the butter with a spoon to cream it with the sugar and form a smooth paste
In a small bowl beat eggs until light and frothy. Add eggs to butter/sugar mixture and beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until light and creamy
Mix in bran flakes, coconut, oats and chocolate chips with mixer on medium speed
Slowly add dry ingredients to mixture and make sure all the flour is incorporated to make a firm cookie dough
Add one tablespoon of light cream to dough to help stabilize and hold together better
Take a small amount of dough and roll it into a ball placing them on the parchment paper 2 inches apart
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes until tops have lightly browned
Remove from the oven and place on a wire rack to cool completely

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very chewy cookie with the goodness of bran flakes, chocolate chips, quick oats and coconut to make a good energy cookie for breakfast or lunchtime.
