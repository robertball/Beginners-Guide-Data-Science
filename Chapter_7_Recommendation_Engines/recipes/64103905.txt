Title:
Chicken Ala King Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg fresh mushrooms
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup flour
1/8 tsp pepper
2 cups whole milk (or half and half)
2 cups chicken broth
2 cups cubed cooked chicken (or turkey)

Directions:
In 3 qt saucepan, saute mushrooms in butter for 5 minutes.  Blend in flour and pepper.  Cook over low heat, stirring until mixture is bubbly.  Remove from heat.  Stir in milk and broth.  Heat to boiling, stirring constantly.  Boil and stir one minute.  Stir in chicken and heat through.  Serve over noodles or rice.
