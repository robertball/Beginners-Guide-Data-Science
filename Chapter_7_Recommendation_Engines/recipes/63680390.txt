Title:
Lemon Impossible Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup lemon juice
1 cup white sugar
4 eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup flaked coconut
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 2/3 cups milk
2 tsp. lemon zest

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 10 inch pie plate. In a blender combine lemon juice, sugar, eggs, coconut, flour and milk.  When well blended, pour mixture into pie dish.
Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for about 35 minutes, or until set.
