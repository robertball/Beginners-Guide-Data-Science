Title:
Crawfish Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1798710.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (28 oz)cans of Yams or Sweet Potatoes
1 lb of frozen crawfish tails (thawed rinsed and seasoned with salt and pepper)
1 pint of heavy whipping cream
1 stick of butter
1 onion (diced)
2 celery stalks (diced)
1 clove garlic (pressed and minced)
5 sprigs of parsley (diced)
1 Tbsp. Cumin
1 Tsp. Salt (or to taste)
1/2 Tsp. Black pepper (or to taste)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 300 F.
2. In a separate bowl rinse and mash sweet potatoes (its ok if they come in a light syrup just rinse it and the small amount of sweetness left will only add to the contrast in flavor)
3. In a large skillet heat up stick of butter and add onion, celery, garlic and parsley. Cook 3-5 minutes or until softened.
3. Add cumin, salt and pepper then stir.
4. Stir in crawfish and simmer for 20 minutes.
5. Fold in mashed sweet potatoes and stir.
6. Stir in heavy whipping cream.
7. Spray bottom of 9 x 13 pan with non stick cooking spray and fold in crawfish and potato mixture.
8. Cover pan with foil and place in oven for 25-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
CC Bombet
Description: 
The mild sweetness of sweet potatoes combined with cumin and cream create a unique blend of flavors. The crawfish is a big hit but can be substituted with shrimp or left out completely.
