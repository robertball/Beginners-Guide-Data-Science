Title:
Pumpkin Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 9-inch deep dish, unbaked pie crust
1 8-oz cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 egg
1 1/4 cup pumpkin puree
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup evaporated milk

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, 1/4 cup sugar and vanilla.  Beat well.  Add 1 egg, and stir until incorporated.   Spread mixture on bottom of pie crust.
Combine pumpkin puree, 1/2 cup sugar, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and salt. Add 2 beaten eggs and evaporated milk.  Stir well.  Carefully pour over cream cheese mixture.
Place pie on a cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 65-70 minutes.  Cool on rack before cutting.

Submitter: 
Karen Somheil Schneiders
Description: 
Pumpkin Pie filling swirled with cream cheese mixture.  These are a big hit with friends and co-workers at Thanksgiving.
