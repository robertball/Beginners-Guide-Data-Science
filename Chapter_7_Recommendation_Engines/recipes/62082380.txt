Title:
Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 pounds beef round steak- 1 inch thick
1 15oz. can diced tomatoes
1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
1/4 cup green bell peppers, finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Stir together flour salt and pepper. Sprinkle one side of meat with half of the mixture and pound in. Turn meat and repeat. Cut into 6 pieces. Heat oil in skillet over medium. Brown meat 5 minutes on each side. Add a small amount of water, cover and simmer 1 hour. Add remaining ingredients and simmer covered for 30 minutes longer.
