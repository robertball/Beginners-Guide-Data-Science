Title:
Easy Peanut Butter Fudge 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick (1/2 cup) butter
2 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup milk
1 cup peanut better
1 tsp vanilla extract
3 cups confectioners sugar, sifted to remove lumps

Directions:
Melt butter in sauce pan over medium heat.  Stir in brown sugar and milk.  Bring to a boil and boil for two full minutes, stirring frequently.  Do not confuse air bubbles with boiling, do not start timing the two minutes until mixture reaches a full boil or fudge will not set properly.
Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter and vanilla.  Stir until mixture is completely smooth.
Pour over sifted confectioners sugar in large bowl and beat until smooth.  Pour into 8x8 baking dish and chill.

Submitter: 
Chris D. Williams
Description: 
This is an extremely easy and fool-proof recipe for peanut butter fudge, with careful instructions so that the fudge comes out perfect every time.  This recipe is adapted from another on this site incorporating feedback from many reviews.
