Title:
Aunt Ludy's Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked squash
6 tablespoons butter
2 eggs beaten
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 cup grated cheese
1 cup evaporated milk
1 cup cracker crumbs

Directions:
Mash cooked squash.Add remaining ingredients- mix well.Pour into a greased casserole dish and bake at 350 for 35-45 minutes. May add cracker crumbes to top before baking if desired. Serves 6. Freezes well
