Title:
Old Fashioned Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C Shortening
1 C Sugar
3 C Flour
1/4 tsp Salt
1/2 C Milk
1 Egg
1 tsp Vanilla
3 tsp Baking Powder

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugar, add egg well beaten.  Add sifted dry ingredients (flour, salt, baking powder) alternately with milk and vanilla.  Roll 1/8 inch thick on lightly floured surface.  Cut into shapes with floured cookie cutters.  Sprinkle with sugar.  Bake on greased cookie sheet about 15 minutes.  Bake at 375 degrees.  Makes 3 dozen.

Submitter:
