Title:
Untuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) can jackfruit, drained
1/4 cup vegan mayonnaise
1 tablespoon mustard
1 tablespoon pickle relish
1 tablespoon minced red onion
1 tablespoon minced celery
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Flake jackfruit in a bowl using a fork. Add vegan mayonnaise, mustard, pickle relish, red onion, and celery. Mix until combined. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Cassidy Carolino
Description: 
Jackfruit is used in place of tuna.
