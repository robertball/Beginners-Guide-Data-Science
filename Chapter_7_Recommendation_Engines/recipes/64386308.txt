Title:
Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C creamy peanut butter
1/3 C reduced sodium soy sauce
1/3 C water
1 large -3 small cloves of garlic- minced
2 T  brown sugar
2 T lime juice

Directions:
In a small sauce pan heat all ingredients except lime juice over medium heat.  Whisk until peanut butter and all ingredients are incorporated.  Remove from heat and mix in lime juice.
