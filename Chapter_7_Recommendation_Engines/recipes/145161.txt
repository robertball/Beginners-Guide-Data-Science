Title:
Crispy Cucumbers and Tomatoes in Dill Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/636955.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1 teaspoon SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh dill weed
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 cucumbers, sliced
1 cup sliced red onion
2 ripe tomatoes, cut into wedges

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix the vinegar, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, salt, dill, pepper, and oil. Add cucumbers, onion, and tomatoes. Toss, and let stand at least 15 minutes before serving.

Description: 
Fresh tomatoes and onion add spark to a cool cucumber and dill salad.
