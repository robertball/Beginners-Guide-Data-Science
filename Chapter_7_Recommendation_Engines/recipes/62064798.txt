Title:
Simple kedgeree Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
100g brown basmati rice
1x 125g can of mackerel in oil
2 eggs
25g-50g frozen peas
1 tsp curry powder
salt and pepper

Directions:
After rinsing the rice, boil for 25mins or as directed on the packaging.
Hard boil the eggs for 5 mins. Then cool under running water and roughly chop.
Add the frozen peas to the rice for the last 5 mins of cooking.
Drain the rice and peas and rinse under running water.
Return the rice and peas to the rinsed pan and flake in the mackerel and oil. stir to combine.
Mix in the curry powder.
Lighly stir in the eggs and season to taste.
Serve and eat!

Submitter: 
ButtercupBento
Description: 
Simple version of the scottish/raj dish kedgeree using tinned fish for convenience. Ideal for brunch.
