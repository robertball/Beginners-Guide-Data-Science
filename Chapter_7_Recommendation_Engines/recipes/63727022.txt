Title:
Shallot and Red Wine Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
120g/4oz Shallots - sliced
2tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 clove Garlic - lightly crushed
2 sprigs Fresh Thyme
3tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
200ml/7fl oz Red Wine
200ml/7fl oz Beef Stock
1oz Butter
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, saut√©, stirring frequently, the shallots in the oil over a low medium heat for some 5 minutes or so until softish and slightly browned;
Season with pepper and add the garlic and thyme;
Continue cooking for a further 3 minutes or so, stirring occasionally;
Pour in the vinegar and cook until it has evaporated to syrup;
Pour in the wine, increase heat to high, and cook until it has reduced by half to two thirds;
Pour in the stock and bring to the boil;
Turn down the heat and, stirring occasionally, simmer until reduced to required consistency;
Remove the garlic and thyme;
Season with salt and pepper to taste and whisk in the butter;
If available, add any juices from the meat.
