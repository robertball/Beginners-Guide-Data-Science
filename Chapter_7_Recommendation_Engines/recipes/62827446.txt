Title:
Wheat Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs (beaten)
1 cup sugar
2 Tbsp flour
Juice of 2 lemons and grated rind
2 1/2 cups cooked wheat
6 apples (cut up)
1 cup pineapple tid-bits drained
1/2 cup nutriwhip, cool whip or whatever

Directions:
Juice 2 lemons and some grated rind, sugar and flour - cook till like custard and add 2 1/2 cups cooked wheat.  You can freeze this or keep it in the fridge for awhile.  When ready to serve add:
6 apples - cut up
1 cup pineapple tid-bits drained
1/2 cup nutriwhip (whipped cream)
