Title:
Hearty Italian Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/771178.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 1/2 pounds bulk Italian sausage
2 large onions, sliced
2 large green peppers, sliced
2 large sweet red peppers, sliced
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
8 sandwich rolls, split
shredded Monterey Jack cheese (optional)

Directions:
In a skillet, brown beef and sausage; drain.
Place a third of the onions and peppers in a slow cooker; top with half of the meat mixture. Repeat layers of vegetables and meat, then top with remaining vegetables. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and pepper flakes. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours or until vegetables are tender.
With a slotted spoon, serve about 1 cup of meat and vegetables on each roll. Top with cheese if desired. Use pan juices for dipping if desired.
