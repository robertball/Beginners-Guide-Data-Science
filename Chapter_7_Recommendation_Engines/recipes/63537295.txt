Title:
Crispy Chipotle Chicken Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkt. Shake 'N Bake Chicken Coating Mix
1 TSBP parsley flakes
6 small boneless skinless chicken breast halves (1-1 1/2 lb)
1/3 cup Kraft Sandwich Shop Chipotle Flavored Reduced Fat Mayonnaise

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F
Mix coating mix and parsley on plate. Spread both sides of each chicken breast with mayo; dip 1 at a time in coating mixture, turning to evenly coat both sides of each breast.
Place on baking sheet sprayed with cooking spray.
Bake 25 min or until done (165 degrees F)
