Title:
Szechuan Stir Fry Beef and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982190.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 (20 ounce) package Simply Potatoes® Diced Potatoes with Onions
1/2 cup prepared Szechuan or stir-fry sauce
1 cup water
1 (16 ounce) bag frozen stir-fry vegetables
1/2 pound sliced deli roast beef, cut into strips
Hot cooked white rice

Directions:
Heat oil in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.  Add Simply Potatoes(R) and 1/4 cup Szechuan sauce; mix well.  Cover; cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes.  Add 1/2 cup water.  Cover; continue cooking 5 minutes.  Add frozen vegetables, roast beef, remaining 1/4 cup Szechuan sauce and 1/2 cup water.  Cover; cook 6 to 7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender.
Serve over hot cooked rice.

Submitter: 
Simply Potatoes®
Description: 
Dinner ready in 25 minutes.  Its quick and easy using Simply Potatoes®, deli roast beef and a purchased Szechaun sauce.
