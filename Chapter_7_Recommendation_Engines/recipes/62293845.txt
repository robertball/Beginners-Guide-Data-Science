Title:
Sarah's Yummy Peanut Butter Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One yellow cake mix
1 c peanut butter
1/2 c brown sugar
3 eggs
1 c water
1/4 c oil or applesauce
1 c peanuts
1 c chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix together cake mix, 1 c peanut butter, and 1/2 brown sugar.  Reserve 2/3 cup of mixture.
Add 3 eggs, 1 c water, and 1/4 oil or applesauce.  Mix.  Pour into greased 9 x 13 pan.  Blend reserved cake mix  with 1 c peanuts and 1 c chocolate chips (we prefer milk choc with this).  Sprinkle on top of cake batter.  Bake at 350F for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Elle
Description: 
A cake-mix cake with peanuts and chocolate chips.
