Title:
Grape-Nuts Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Butter
6 large eggs
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla
4 cups whole milk
Grape-Nuts cereal
whipping cream

Directions:
Butter a 2 qt baking dish and preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Whisk 6 eggs, sugar, nutmeg, cinnamon and vanilla in large bowl. Whisk in milk. Pour a thin layer of cereal in baking dish, barely covering bottom of dish. Pour in milk mixture. Bake 30 to 40 minutes, until mostly set but jiggly in center. Serve with whipped cream.
