Title:
Ma's Blackberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
2 C Flour, all-purpose
1 tsp Salt
2/3 C Shortening
1/2 C Milk
Filling:
12 C Blackberries (fresh or frozen)
1/2 C Butter, divided
1/8 tsp Salt
1-1/2 C Sugar, divided
1 tsp Cinnamon
Topping:
1/4 C Sugar
1 tsp Cinnamon
2 T Butter

Directions:
Crust:
Mix flour, salt, and shortening until crumbly. Stir in milk, and gather into a ball. Flatten into a 6-inch circle, wrap with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 30 minutes. Roll on a lightly floured board until the dough is slightly larger than baking pan.
Filling:
Preheat oven to 400. Bring berries, 1/4 C Butter, and salt to boil in a large saucepan, stirring occasionally. Stir in 1-1/4 C Sugar. Pour in 13x9 baking pan. Top with crust. Dot with 1/4 C Butter, sprinkle with 1/4 C Sugar and 1 t Cinnamon. Prick top with fork. Bake 30 minutes or until golden brown.
