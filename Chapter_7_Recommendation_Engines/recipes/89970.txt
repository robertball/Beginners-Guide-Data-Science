Title:
Last Minute Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/381137.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 1/2 cups fresh button mushrooms, sliced
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt

Directions:
In a skillet, over medium heat, melt the butter. Add mushrooms, and sprinkle with seasoned salt. Cook, stirring occasionally, until mushrooms soften and become darker in color, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Carrie Magill
Description: 
This recipe for a buttery, delicious, mushroom side dish couldn't be any simpler.  It goes well with fish fillets, baked chicken, steak, and just about any other main dish.
