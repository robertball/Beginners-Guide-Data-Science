Title:
Pesto chicken pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1266648.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package bow tie pasta
3 TB olive oil
2 TB chopped garlic
1 1/2 TB hot chile paste
1 pound chicken tenderloins cut into bite size pieces
1 c Parmesan cheese
5 TB pesto
1 zucchini sliced
1 c mushrooms
1/4 c chopped sun dried tomatoes
1/4 c chopped onions
1/2 c chopped cilantro

Directions:
Cook pasta al dente in salted water per directions. Drain and save 2 c pasta water
In Dutch oven, brown onions, garlic and olive oil.  Add zucchini, chicken and chili paste and cook for 15 min..  Add mushrooms. Cilantro,  sun dried tomatoes and  cook for 5 min.  Add Parmesan cheese, pasta water and pasta.  Heat til warm through out

Pasta and pesto ... Great combination.  Add chicken, shrimp., pork or whatever.
