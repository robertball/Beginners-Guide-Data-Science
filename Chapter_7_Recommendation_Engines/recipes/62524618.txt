Title:
SOUTHERN SURPRISE SWEET POTATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked, mashed sweet potatoes
10 Tbl butter, melted (divided use)
1 cup sugar
1 egg
3 Tbl flour
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 cup chopped pecans
1/4 cup packed brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Lightly grease 8" sq baking dish.
In mixing bowl, combine potatoes, 6 Tbl butter, sugar, egg, vanilla & flour.
Blend well & transfer to prepared pan.
In separate bowl, stir together remaining 4 Tbl butter, pecans, & brown sugar Sprinkle evenly on top of potatoe mixture.
Bake 25min or until hot & bubbly.
