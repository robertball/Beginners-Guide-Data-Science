Title:
Bar Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound processed cheese, cubed
4 ounces horseradish
3/4 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
6 drops Worcestershire sauce
5 drops Tabasco sauce

Directions:
Microwave cheese; add horseradish. Stir well and return to microwave for about 30 seconds.
Add mayonnaise or salad dressing. Then add Worcestershire sauce and Tabasco. Stir well and return to microwave for 30 seconds.
Stir again and microwave for another 20 seconds. Pour into container and chill for an hour. Pour into container and chill for an hour.
Cool slightly and pour into 4 or 5 containers. Seal well, then refrigerate.
Serve on your choice of crackers.
