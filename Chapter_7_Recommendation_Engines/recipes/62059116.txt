Title:
sausage hash brown bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. pork sausage
1 can of cream of chicken
1 container of french onion dip(8oz)
1/4 cup of diced green pepper
1/4 cup of diced red pepper
1/4 cup of diced onion
2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
1 bag of shredded hashbrowns (16oz)

Directions:
brown sausage. mix in soup, peppers, onions, dip, and 1/2 of the cheese in seperate bowl. add sausage to mixture..make sure hash browns are thawed, fold hash browns into mixture. Pour into 11x9 casserole dish. Add remaining cheese. Cover with Foil and bake at 350 for 30 mins. Remove foil and bake an additional 15 mins.
