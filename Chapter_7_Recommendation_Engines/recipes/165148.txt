Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Chewy Chocolate Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984377.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
6 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon chocolate flavouring

Directions:
In a large microwave safe bowl, heat margarine and marshmallows and cocoa powder at HIGH for 3 minutes, stirring after 2 minutes.
Add in chocolate flavouring and vanilla. Stir in KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal until well coated. Using lightly buttered spatula, press into buttered 3.5 L (13 x 9-inch) pan.
Allow mixture to cool slightly. Cut into squares.

The kids will love these easy, delicious treats in their lunch boxes or after school.
