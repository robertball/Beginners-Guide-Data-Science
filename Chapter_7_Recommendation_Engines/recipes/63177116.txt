Title:
Chia Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup chia seeds
2 cups milk (regular, or almond/soy)
1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1 tablespoon sugar or sugar substitute
2 tablespoons unsweetened coconut flakes (optional)
2 tablespoons dried fruit (optional)

Directions:
Combine chia seeds, milk and vanilla in bowl or jar. Stir or shake after 10 minutes. Refrigerate overnight. When ready to serve, stir well. Spoon into bowls and top with fruit and/or coconut

Submitter: 
nikkid
Description: 
Chia seeds are a wonderful alternative source of omega-3, fiber, and energy. This easy pudding- similar to tapioca, is great for you.
