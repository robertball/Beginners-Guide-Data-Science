Title:
Sweet Heat BBQ Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dark brown sugar
1/4 cup kosher salt
2 tablespoons smoked paprika
1 tablespoon all spice
2 tablespoons cayenne pepper
2 tablespoons red pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon cinnamon

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix well.
Rub liberally into your favorite meat before grilling, broiling or baking.

Submitter: 
Drizzler
Description: 
This rub iss perfect for beef, chicken or pork. The heat of the cayenne and red pepper flakes is balanced by the sweet of the brown sugar and cinnamon while the paprkia lends a smoky flavor to your favorite meat.
