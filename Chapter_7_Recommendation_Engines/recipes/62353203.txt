Title:
Hot and Sour Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/399775.jpg
Ingredients:
1-14oz. can chicken broth
2 broth cans of water
1-4oz. can sliced mushrooms, drained
2 tablespoons soy sauce (dark is best, but regular will work too)
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1 teaspoon tabasco sauce, or to taste
1 egg, beaten
3 green onions, chopped
2 Tablespoons cornstarch combined with a little water (for thickening)

Directions:
Combine broth, water, mushrooms, soy sauce, vinegar and tabasco sauce in a medium pot.
Bring to a boil over high heat.
Stir in cornstarch slurry and boil until slightly thickened.
Pour egg in a thin stream into soup while stirring in a circular motion.
Remove from heat and stir in green onions.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
This soup had been a family favorite for years. I know it's not an 'authentic' recipe, but we love it, enjoy!
