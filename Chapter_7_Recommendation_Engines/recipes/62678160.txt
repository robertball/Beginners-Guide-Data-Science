Title:
Cheesy Potato Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/599937.jpg
Ingredients:
1 TBSP EVOO
1/4 cup diced onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup thinly sliced carrots
2 cups chicken broth
2 cups chopped broccoli florets
2 cups leftover mashed potatoes
1 cup heavy cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
salt and pepper to taste
four sliced bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan, cook garlic, onion, and carrots in olive oil over medium heat until onion is soft, about 5 minutes.
Add chicken broth. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and cook until carrots are tender, about 10 minutes.
Add broccoli. Replace cover and cook another 5 minuts or until broccoli is tender.
Stir in mashed potatoes until completely incorporated.
Stir in cream and cheese. Heat until hot, but do not return to boiling.
Top with bacon.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
This creamy, delicious soup uses leftover mashed potatoes as a main ingredient.
