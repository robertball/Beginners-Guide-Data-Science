Title:
Wild west baked chili macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (14.3 each) chili no beans
1pkg (8.5oz) cornbread mix, batter prepared as directed on package

Directions:
1. PREHEAT oven to 375F. Prepare Dinner as directed on package. Stir in chili.
2.SPREAD  into 13x9-inch baking dish.Spread prepared cornbread batter on top of chili mixture to within 1/2 inch of edges.
3. BAKE for 25 to 30min. or until cornbread is golden brown and fully risen.
