Title:
Ann’s Chicken Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet enchilada sauce mix
1 15 oz. can of tomato sauce
15 oz. water
18 corn tortillas-6 for each layer
3 chicken breasts or ½ of a chicken, cooked and shredded
24 oz. cheddar cheese, shredded
1 onion, diced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a casserole type dish mix Enchilada sauce mix, tomato sauce and water, set aside (you will be dipping the tortillas into this)
Cut one of the edges off of the tortillas to make a straight edge, save the pieces that you have cut off.
In a large bowl, mix the shredded chicken, cheese and onion together
Coat the bottom of the 13x9 pan in enchilada sauce
Dip tortillas into the enchilada mix and place a single layer into the baking dish with the straight edge along the sides of the dish.  Use extra pieces that you set aside to fill in the gaps.
Put on half the cheese mixture evenly over the tortillas.
Ladle on some enchilada sauce.
Add another layer of the tortillas the same way as in number 2.
Put the remainder of the cheese mixture evenly over the tortillas
Ladle on some most of the enchilada sauce
Add another layer of the tortillas the same way as in number 2.
Pour the remaining sauce over the top or the enchiladas.
All of the ingredients are precooked, so heat it in the oven at 350 degrees for 30 min.  The enchiladas should be bubbling.

Submitter: 
Andrea Schmidt
Description: 
Our family has been making this for years, it is a family favorite.  If you want enchiladas, roll each enchilada separately, and bake as stated.
