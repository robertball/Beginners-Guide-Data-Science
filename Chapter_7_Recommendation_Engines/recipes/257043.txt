Title:
Chai Pomegranate Overnight Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4473988.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Quaker® Oats (Quick or Old Fashioned, uncooked)
1 chai tea bag
1/4 cup low-fat vanilla Greek yogurt
1/2 cup low-fat milk
1 teaspoon honey
1/4 cup pomegranate seeds

Directions:
Add Quaker(R) Oats to a container of your choice and pour in Chai Tea Latte.
Layer on yogurt and top with honey and pomegranate seeds.
Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning!

Submitter: 
Quaker®
Description: 
Overnight oats with chai tea latte, vanilla yogurt, honey, and pomegranate seeds make a perfect on-the-go breakfast.
