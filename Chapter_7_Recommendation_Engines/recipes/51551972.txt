Title:
Joyce's Scalloped Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 carrots, sliced and cooked
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1/4 cup flour
1/4 tsp dry mustard
2 cup milk
8 oz. Velveeta, cubed or shredded
1/4 cup margarine
1 tsp salt
1/3 tsp pepper
1 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Saute onion in butter until tender.
Stir in flour, salt, dry mustard and pepper.
Heat to boiling, stirring constantly.
Boil 1 minute.(I cook mine in the microwave until it thickens slightly)
Alternate layers of carrots and cheese in an ungreased 9x13-inch baking dish.
Pour sauce over layers.
Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
Bake at 350 degrees for 35-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
This is a good blend of carrots and cheese. Most people who don't like carrots like this if they will try it!
