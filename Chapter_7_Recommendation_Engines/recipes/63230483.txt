Title:
Awesome Breakfast Casserold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. frozen hash browns
1 diced onion
1 lb. bacon or sausage
3 eggs
2 oz. Velveeta cheese (2 slices)
2 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
1 (10-12 oz.) can cream of potato soup
salt and pepper

Directions:
Cook the hash browns with the onion until crisp. Fry the meat of your choice. Mix cooked ingredients in a 13x9" pan. Add the cheeses, soup, beaten eggs, salt and pepper. Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 to 50 minutes. Serve warm.

Submitter:

Description: 
This casserole is creamy, cheesy, and perfect for breakfast! It will definitely satisfy your morning hunger with its special flavor.
