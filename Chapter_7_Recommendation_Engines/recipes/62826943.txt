Title:
Mama's Cassoulet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. kielbasa sausage, sliced in half lengthwise, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 whole chicken breast, skinned, boned, cut into small chunks
1 small onion, chopped
1 15.5oz can red kidney beans, drained
1 14.5oz can stewed tomatoes, cut up
1 14.5oz can great white northern beans
1 tsp beef bouillon

Directions:
In Dutch oven or large pot, over medium heat, cook kielbasa, chicken and onion until chicken is no longer pink and onion is tender. Stir in beans, tomatoes, and bouillon. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer uncovered 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Jaimee Howard
Description: 
Our go to recipe for company or whenever we want the kids to eat well. EVERYONE loves this meal and I have yet to make this meal for a guest who doesn't ask for the recipe before leaving.
