Title:
Crunchy Chicken Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/545697.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup finely chopped cooked chicken
1/4 cup minced green onions
1/2 cup finely shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
3/4 cup finely chopped pecans or chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first six ingredients; mix well. Shape into 3/4-in. balls. Roll in pecans or parsley. Cover and chill.

Submitter: 
Jamie  Cox
Description: 
Now that I'm retired from teaching, I have plenty of time to entertain. These individual chicken balls are always on the menu...much to the pleasure of my guests.
