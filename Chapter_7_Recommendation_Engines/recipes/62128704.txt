Title:
Fuzzy Navel Slush II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz.) can frozen orange juice
1 (12 oz.) can frozen lemonade
1 1/2 cans peach schanpps (use OJ can to measure)
1/2 can vodka (use OJ can to measure)
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 cans water (use OJ can to measure)
2 (16 oz.) bags frozen sliced peaches, pureed
7-Up, Sunkist Orange, Sprite or Tangerine soda

Directions:
Let the peaches thaw awhile before putting them in the blender.  Once in the blender do not puree all the way - leave some small chunks.  Mix all ingredients (except the soda) together making sure the sugar has dissolved.  Put into a freezer proof container and freeze overnight.  To serve, mix one (1) part slush to one (1) part soda of your choice.

Submitter: 
Shults3
Description: 
My sister came up with this one - it is fabulous!  The pureed peaches make the difference.  It is very good mixed with diet sunkist orange soda.
