Title:
Fruit Fusion Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4520606.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sliced fresh strawberries
1/3 cup cherries, pitted
1/3 cup pineapple chunks
3/4 cup Unsweetened Vanilla Almond Breeze Almondmilk

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Fruity and bright is exactly what you'll taste with this mixed berry and pineapple smoothie.
