Title:
Fresh Spinach Fruit Nut Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 -  9 oz Bag Fresh Baby Spinach.
1 - 9 oz Fresh Assorted Salad Greens (optional)
1 small red onion
1 Pint Fresh Strawberries
3 Fresh Pears
Pine Nuts
Specialty Cheese is a Key Ingredient: Crandale (Wensleydale Cheese with Cranberries.  I may experiment with some of the other specialty cheeses with lemon peel or any other fruit component.)
1 bottle Greek Feta Salad Dressing

Directions:
Loosley chop fresh spinach to reduce size of leaves. (May also add some of the optional fresh assorted salad greens if you are not into complete spinach salads.)
Coursely chop the red onion.
Toss Spinach and red onion together and place into serving bowl.
Quarter or thickly slice strawberries. Spread on top of spinach and onion.
Cube or thickly chunk up pears into bitesize pieces. Spread on top of spinach and onion.
Cube up the Cranberry cheese about half the size of a sugar cube and sprinkle on top of fruit.
Sprinkle Pine Nuts all around.
Refrigerate until ready to serve. When ready to serve, tong out onto plate and dress with a good quality store bought Greek Feta dressing.
