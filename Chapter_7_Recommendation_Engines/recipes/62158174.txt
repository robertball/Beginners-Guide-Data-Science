Title:
Tomato and Pesto  Bagel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/485596.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bagel sliced in half
2 tbsp of cream cheese
1 tbsp basil pesto
1/2 roma tomato sliced

Directions:
Slice the bagel in half. Toast the bagel as this time if you wish. Spread the cream cheese over one half of the bagel and then spread the basil pesto over the cream cheese. Slice the roma tomato. Place the slices of tomato on top.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
You can eat with toasted or not toasted. Great mix of flavors with cream cheese, tomato, and pesto. Fantastic for breakfast, lunch or a snack.
