Title:
Mimi's Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups milk
3 cups soft bread crumbs/pieces
1/3 tsp salt
1 cup raisins
3 beaten eggs
3/4 cup sugar
Cinnamon Sugar

Directions:
In a pot, heat milk, but do not boil! blend in bread crumbs/pieces. Meanwhile, in  a bowl combine, sugar, eggs, salt and raisins. When milk has heated, blend in the egg mixture with the milk/bread mixture. Pour into a greased/Pam 8x11" casserole dish and then place that into a 9x13" casserole dish with water. Bake at 375 degrees for 60-120 minutes or until set.

Submitter: 
Robin McCaughey Garrett
Description: 
This is more of a custard type of Bread Pudding. No rum sauce goes on top. The flavor is phenominal!
