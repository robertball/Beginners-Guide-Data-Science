Title:
Slow-Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 (15 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes, mashed
1 (10 ounce) can Hatch enchilada sauce
1 medium onion, chopped
1 (4 ounce) can Hatch chopped green chile peppers
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 boxes chicken stock
2 teaspoon cumin
2 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cayenne
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 - 2 bay leaf
1 (10 ounce) package frozen roasted corn
dry black beans
1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
Harbor Greens Chilepeno tortilla chips
lemon or lime juice
shredded cheese
avocado
sour cream

Directions:
Place chicken, tomatoes, enchilada sauce, onion, green chiles, and garlic into a slow cooker. Pour in chicken broth, and season with cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper, and bay leaf. Stir in corn, beans and cilantro. Cover, and cook on Low setting for 6 to 8 hours or on High setting for 3 to 4 hours.
Serve with avocado, lime juice, shredded cheese, sour cream and tortilla strips.

Description: 
A quick, no-fuss version of chicken tortilla soup! All you do is put everything into the slow cooker, and turn it on. Then garnish with baked corn tortilla strips!Modified from: Slow-Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup
