Title:
Swiss Cheese Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons of butter
1/4 cup chopped green onions
3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
1 cup half and half
1/3 cup dry white wine
1/3 shredded swiss cheese
1/2 teaspoon of ground mustard
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a small saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add green onions; saute for about 1 minute. Add floor; cook for 1 minute longer. Slowly stir in half and half and wine. Simmer, stirring for about 10 minutes, until smooth and thickened. Add swiss cheese, mustard, salt, and pepper. Stir until cheese is melted.
