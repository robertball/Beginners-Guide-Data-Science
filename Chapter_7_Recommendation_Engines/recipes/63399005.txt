Title:
Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 or 4 small, firm cucumbers (1 1/2 pounds)
1 T coarse salt (Kosher style)
1/2 c white vinegar
1/4 c cold water
1 T sugar
1 t salt
1/4 t white pepper
2 T minced fresh dill or parsley

Directions:
1.  Pare cucumbers, if necessary, and score with tines of fork.  Slice cucumbers.
2.  Mix dressing.
3.  Combine dressing and cucumbers.
