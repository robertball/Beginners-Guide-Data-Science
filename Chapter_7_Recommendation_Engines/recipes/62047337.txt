Title:
Souper Crock Pot Chicken & Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 can reduced fat cream of chicken soup
1 can reduced fat cream of mushroom soup
1 envelope onion soup mix
1 can sliced mushrooms(with juice)
1 cup reduced fat or no fat sour cream
pepper to taste
cooked noodles

Directions:
Combine soups, sour cream, and mushrooms in crock pot. Place chicken breast on top of soup and push to the bottom.  Cook on low for 8 hours.
Serve over a bed of noodles.
