Title:
Broken Glass Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510130.jpg
Ingredients:
Powdered sugar
3 3/4 cups sugar
1 1/4 cup light Karo
1 cup water
food coloring
1 teaspoon oil flavoring (available at pharmacy in flavors such as cinnamon, clove, peppermint, butter rum and many more)

Directions:
Sprinkle powdered sugar on 2 (11 X 15 inch) cookie sheets.
Combine sugar, Karo and water in heavy saucepan and stir over medium heat until sugar dissolves.
Continue to cook without stirring, until syrup reaches 290 degrees F on candy thermometer.
Remove from heat and add food coloring to desired intensity.
Now add the desired oil flavoring.
Pour onto cookie sheets, cool and break into small pieces.
Makes 2 1/2 pounds candy.
