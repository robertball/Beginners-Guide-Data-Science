Title:
Spicy Creamy Russian Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Miracle Whip
1/2 cup sour cream
5 teaspoons minced onion
2 teaspoon hot sauce
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon dried parsley
3 tablespoons chili sauce
1 tablespoon ketchup

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together the Miracle Whip, onion, hot sauce, Worcestershire, parsley, chili sauce and ketchup.  Chill until serving.
