Title:
Instant Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 paper or plastic sandwich bags
FOR EACH BAG
1/2 cup instant or quick oats
1 tbsp brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
pinch salt
1/4 cup chopped dried apples

Directions:
In each of 10 paper or plastic sandwich bags, combine 1/2 cup instant or quick (1-minute) oats with 1 tbsp brown sugar, 1/2 tsp cinnamon, pinch of salt and 1/4 cup chopped dried apples.
When ready to eat, add oatmeal mix to bowl and top with 1 cup boiling water or milk. Stir and let sit for 5 min. For the microwave, add 1 cup water or milk, stir, then microwave on high for 1 to
1 1/2 min. Stir again and enjoy.
Variations
Maple-Pecan:
Omit cinnamon. Use 1 tbsp pure maple flakes instead of sugar plus 1/4 cup toasted chopped pecans instead of apples.
Cinnamon-Sugar Raisin:
Use 1 tbsp granulated sugar instead of brown sugar plus 1/4 cup raisins instead of apples.
Banana-Chocolate:
Use 1 tbsp cocoa powder instead of cinnamon and 1 tbsp granulated sugar instead of brown plus 1/4 cup chopped banana chips instead of apples.

Description: 
Easy to make individual oatmeal bowls. Make different flavours so everyone can choose something different.
