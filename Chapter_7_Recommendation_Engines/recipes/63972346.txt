Title:
Greek Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1102339.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup diced onion
1 teaspoon minced garlic
2 teaspoons olive oil (divided)
2 cups Egg Beaters (or 8 eggs)
1 (10 ounce package) chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
ground pepper to taste
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, quartered
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Heat oil over medium high heat in a medium skillet. Add onion and garlic. Heat until onion has softened. Remove from heat and put into a small bowl.
In a medium bowl stir together Egg Beaters, spinach, oregano, salt and pepper. Add onions and garlic.
In a separate bowl combine tomatoes and feta cheese.
Heat remaining oil in a 12 inch skillet over medium heat. Add egg mixture, cooking and lifting the cooked eggs to let uncooked eggs flow into the pan and cook. When eggs are almost done, add tomatoes and cheese, and cover the skillet. Cook until the egg mixture has set.

Description: 
This colorful and healthy frittata tastes as appetizing as it looks! It can be served as an elegant breakfast dish for guests, or for family as a tasty main course for breakfast or dinner. A weekend favorite!
