Title:
Cappuccino Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tsp instant coffee
1 cup butter room temp
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 3/4 cups flour
1/4 cup cornstarch
6 squares semi sweet chocolate melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 F.
Finely crush instant coffee.
Cream butter and sugar.
Beat in instant coffee and vanilla. Sift flour and cornstarch together, stir into butter mixture.
Mould into the shape of coffee beans, using one tablespoon dough for each cookie.
Using the back of a knife, press an indent about 1/8 inch deep, lengthwise, across the top of each cookie.
Place on greased baking sheet.
Bake at 325 F for 15 minutes.
Place on wire rack to cool. Dip both ends (or just one end) in chocolate. Place on baking sheet lined with wax paper and refrigerate.
