Title:
Chicken & Stuffing Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
6 slices cheese of choice
2 cans cream of chicken soup
2/3 cup milk
2 tsp dried parsley
6 oz instant stuffing mix
Paprika to taste

Directions:
preheat oven to 400 degrees
mix soup, milk and parsley in small bowl
spray 13 x 9 baking dish with Pam or equal
place chicken breasts in pan and top with slice of cheese of choice
Cover chicken with soup mixture
top coated chicken evenly with stuffing mix
cover tightly with foil, and bake approximately 30 minutes
remove foil and return to oven for approximately 30 additional minutes
remove from oven, sprinkle with paprika and serve
