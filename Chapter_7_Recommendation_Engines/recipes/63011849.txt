Title:
Spicy coconut shrimp bisque, Great! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/752734.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound shrimp, shells reserved
2 1/2 cups water
4 tablespoons butter, divided
1/3 cup chopped green onions
1/3 cup diced celery
1/4 diced jalapeno
1/4 cup flour
2 cups prepared tomato soup
1/2 cup coconut milk
1/4 teaspoon red curry paste, or to taste
salt and/or fish sauce to taste
basil chiffonade
rice crackers

Directions:
Shrimp stock- Take shells of the shrimp, 2 tablespoons of butter, and 2 1/2 cups of water. Bring to simmer for 15 to 20 mins.
While thats going ^ in another pan on medium heat add 2 tablespoons of butter, green onions, celery, jalapeno mix until translusent.
Medium low heat now add flour for 3-5mins and stir.
Add tomato soup and your shrimp stock bring to medium high heat and stir.
Now add your red curry paste, coconut milk, and fish sauce(I used salt). Last add your shrimp and your done!
