Title:
Morgan's Amazing Peppermint Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 candy canes
6 oz of white chocolate

Directions:
Melt the white chocolate for 1 minute to 1 min. 30 secs.  During this time, get two bags, put one inside the other and put the candy canes in.  Mash with a rolling pin. Take chocolate out and stir, place back in microwave, let stand until done with candy canes. Mix candy canes and chocolate. Put on waxed paper and put it in fridge (opt.) until hardened.
