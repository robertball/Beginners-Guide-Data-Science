Title:
Acorn Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/910956.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Acorn Squash
3 red peppers
1 small onion
3 T butter
8 oz of cream cheese
1 Cup fresh cider
ground cinnamon to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
2 acorn squash halved and roasted for 45 min
"when done deseed and discard seeds, scrape meet with large spoon"
Red peppers halved, length wise, and roasted for 20 min
Caramelize the onion with 3 T of butter
Put all ingredients in the food processor and puree
Combine in a sauce pan, heat, and serve. Add spices to taste.
