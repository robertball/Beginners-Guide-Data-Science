Title:
Tanya's pizza sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  8 oz. cans tomato sauce
1 tsp. Italian seasoning
1/4 tsp. sugar
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
2 tsp. olive oil
pinch of black pepper
pinch of rosemary

Directions:
Combine all ingredient in medium bowl.  For best flavor, cover and let stand for flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
Tanya Belt
Description: 
This is my no fail pizza sauce.  Not too overpowering with a smooth Italian taste, this basic recipe is easily adaptable to your individual taste.  I usually mix up this sauce while my pizza dough is rising.
