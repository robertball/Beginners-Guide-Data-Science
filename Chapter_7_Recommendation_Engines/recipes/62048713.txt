Title:
Quick and Easy Refried Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans refried beans
1 package taco seasoning
8 oz. Mexican Blend cheese
chili powder

Directions:
Blend together the beans and taco seasoning in a casserole dish. Top it with the cheese and bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Sprinkle some chili powder on top and serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
COOKINCOWGIRLS
Description: 
This easy to prepare and inexpensive refried pinto bean dip can be made with fat free beans.
