Title:
Memere's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lb lean ground beef
2 eggs
4 oz sour cream
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/4 chopped onion
2 Tbs snipped parsley
1 Tbs worcestershire sauce
1 Tbs Dijon mustard or Mrs. Dash
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
cubes of cheddar cheese
Vegetable soup

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees
Mix all ingredients except beef and cheese first
Add beef and cheese
Transfer to loaf pan
Bake uncovered at 350 for 1.5 hrs
Mix 2 cans of vegetable soup and bring to a boil
Mix 1/2 cup water with 4 Tbs flour and slowly add to boiling soup until thickened.
