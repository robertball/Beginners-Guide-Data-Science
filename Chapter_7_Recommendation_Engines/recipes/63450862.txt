Title:
Kate's Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2# Ground Beef
3/4 C Milk
1 1/2 C Bread Crumbs
1 small onion diced
2 Eggs beaten
1/4 C Ketchup
3 T Brown Sugar
2 t Salt
1/8 t pepper
1 M carrot grated
2 T prepared mustard

Directions:
pour milk over bread crumbles, add meat, salt, pepper, carrot, onion, & eggs. Mix, make into loaf and cover with brown sugar, ketchup and mustard.
Bake @ 325* 1.5 hours

Submitter: 
KRMoser
Description: 
this recipe is from my great grandmother Alice McCorkell Bowles' friend Kate...it was in her recipe box
