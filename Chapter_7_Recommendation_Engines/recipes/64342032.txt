Title:
Roasted and Stuffed Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2086947.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Acorn Squash washed, halved and seeds removed
5 Fresh Sage leaves finely chopped
1 Small onion chopped
1 clove of garlic minced
Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees
Wash and halve the acorn squash. Then remove the seeds.
Rub the olive oil all over the squash including the inside. Season with salt and pepper
Lay the squash on a baking pan covered with parchment paper (inside of the squash down). Bake for 25 to 35 minutes until flesh is soft.
Heat a saute pan over medium over heat. bottom of sauté pan with olive oil and onion and garlic until onion is soft. Add mushrooms sage and red pepper. Heat until mushrooms are soft.
Once squash is done cooking, stuff with sautéed ingredients and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
Let cool about 5 minutes and serve with your favorite salad.
