Title:
Hula's Chocolate Chip Blondes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks soft butter
1 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp vanilla
2 extra large eggs or 3 little ones
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts
1 1/4 pounds chocolate chip chunks

Directions:
Set oven to 350
Grease and flour 8 x 12 x 12
Beat 2 sticks butter + 1 cup light brown sugar + 1/2 cup sugar till light and fluffy
Beat in 2 tsp vanilla
Add 2 extra large eggs one at a time and beat well
Sift 2 cups flour + 1 tsp baking soda + 1 tsp salt mix together and then add into the butter mixture - blend well
Fold in 1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts and 1 1/4 pounds chocolate chip chunks
Bake NOTE: Do NOT overbake or will be dry and crappy. Toothpick trick won't work.
