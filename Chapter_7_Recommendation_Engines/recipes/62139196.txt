Title:
Chicken Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken thighs
8 oz. Vermicelli noodles
1 large bell pepper, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
salt and pepper to taste
2 1/2 Tbsp worchestershire sauce
12 oz cheddar cheese

Directions:
Boil chicken in large pot.
Remove chicken and debone.
Saute bell pepper and onion in 1 cup chicken broth until tender.
Bring noodles to a boil in remainder of chicken broth; reduce heat to medium and cook until the noodles have absorbed all of the broth.
Combine all ingredients in a large baking dish, and bake at 375 for 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ashley
Description: 
This dish is a little different than a traditional tetrazzini. It has no mushrooms and is made with cheese, onion, bell pepper, soup, and worchestershire. No one eats this and leaves my house without the recipe.
