Title:
Honey Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of flour
1 cup of cornmeal
1/4 cup sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt, if desired
1 cup whipping cream
1/4 cup oil
1/4 cup honey
2 eggs, lightly beaten

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2. Stir flour, cornmeal, sugar baking powder and salt in a bowl.
3. Stir in remaining ingredients.
4. Pour into a 9 inch greased baking pan.
5. Bake in 400 degree oven for 20 to 25 minutes. Toothpick should come out clean.

Submitter: 
TIM LASTRAPES
Description: 
A sweet cornbread that will crumble in your hand and melt in your mouth.
