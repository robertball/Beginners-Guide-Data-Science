Title:
Araxi's Carrot Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4018331.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Carrots
1 Clove of garlic
2 sprigs of fresh parsley
1/3 cup sour cream
Salt to taste

Directions:
1. Peel and shred the carrots in a bowl.
2. Use a garlic press to add garlic.
3. Finely chop parsley and add to bowl
4. Add sour cream and salt.

Submitter: 
Marie Wishart
Description: 
This was my mom's recipe, I'm not sure if it is an Armenian dish or Russian but it is super easy and delicious. It is also very nutritious since we're not cooking out any of nutrients from the carrots and garlic.
