Title:
Chicken tater tot casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10.5 ounce can of chicken gravy
10.5 ounce can of cream of chicken
1 cup of evaporated milk
1 cup of dry pepperidge farm stuffing (sage & onion)
4 skinless, boneless chicken strips
1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 tsp of celery seed seasoning
1/2 cup of minced onion
2 tsp of minced garlic
pepper to taste (optional)
32 oz bag of frozen tater tots

Directions:
Preheat oven at 375. Spray 9x13 pan with nonstick olive oil spray. Line pan with frozen tater tots. Fry chicken strips and cut into cubes. Mix together all remaining ingredients except for cheese. Mix two cheeses and sprinkle on top. Put dish in oven and bake for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Carolyn Coleman
