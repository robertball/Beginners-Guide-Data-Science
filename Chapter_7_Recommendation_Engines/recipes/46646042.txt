Title:
Potato-Crusted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup dry potato flakes
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/4 tsp. onion powder
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. cayenne
2 lbs. chicken breasts
1/3 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees.  Grease or line with foil a baking sheet or a 13 x 9 inch baking pan.
In a medium bowl, combine potato flakes, Parmesan cheese, and seasonings.  Stir until well mixed.
Dip chicken pieces into melted butter and roll in potato flake mixture to coat.  Place in prepared pan.
Bake for 45 to 60 minutes, or until chicken is tender and golden brown.
