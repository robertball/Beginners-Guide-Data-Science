Title:
Not Your Mother's Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. Butter or Olive oil
1/4 Cup chopped onion.(may use scallions with green part)
1/4 Cup finely chopped Green Pepper
1 small can mushrooms or 1/2 cup fresh mushrooms, sliced
2 cans Tuna (drain oil or use packed in water, also drain)
1 Tomato, chopped (may peel)
1 Tbls. fresh Lemon Juice
salt and pepper
1/2-3/4 Cup Mayonnaise (Hellman's preferred,may use reduced fat)
8 oz. pkg. Noodles, cooked al dente
1/2 C. Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
1. Saute onion, green pepper and mushrooms in butter/oil over medium heat 5 minutes.
2. Add tuna, tomato, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
3. Remove from heat and stir in the mayonnaise.
4. Add the noodles.
5. Transfer to a 2-quart casserole and top with shredded Cheddar.
6. Bake uncovered 20 minutes at 400 degrees F.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
A tasty baked tuna noodle casserole without canned soup. Very appetizing and lovely presentation. Fit for company.
