Title:
Nutty Honey Oat Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cup water
2 tablespoons olive oil
3 tablespoons honey
1 1/2 cup bread flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
3/4 cup old fashioned oats
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup flax seed
1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
1/2 cup salted sunflower nuts

Directions:
Place all ingredients except sunflower nuts (in order of manufacturer's recommendations) in pan of breadmaker.  Using white bread setting, start machine and add sunflower nuts when the machine beeps, indicating time is appropriate to add additional ingredients.

Submitter: 
Paula S.
Description: 
A light whole grain bread with a little added crunch.
