Title:
BEST Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5lb Red Potatoes;1 8oz container of Sour cream; 8 slices of American cheese (more or less to taste);  1/2 cup of butter; salt and pepper to taste; 1 &1/2 cup cream (or milk)

Directions:
Clean and cube Red potatoes
Boil Until tender, drain.
Add milk, butter, sour cream, salt and pepper,
Mash or whip (whatever texture you prefer)
Add cheese slowly and fold in until mixed well.

Submitter: 
Christal Hudson Ellard
Description: 
This is the recipe handed down from my grandmother, it is good simple down-home mashed potatoes.
