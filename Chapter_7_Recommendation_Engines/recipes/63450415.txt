Title:
Restaurant Style Queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup chopped yellow onion
1/2 cup chpped celery
1 fresh jalapeno finely chopped (optional)
2 large anaheim or hatch peppers, chopped
1 pound good quality white American cheese cubed
1/3 cup milk and more if needed

Directions:
Melt the butter in a sauce pan over medium heat. Add the onion, celery and peppers.  Saute until veggies are soft but not browned.  Add the cubed cheese and milk and heat over low heat until melted stiring to make sure it does not burn on the bottom.  Add additional milk if the mixture is too thick.

Submitter: 
Love2cook32
Description: 
I have been trying to duplicate the queso that a lot of mexican restaurants make.  This one is so rich and creamy and has a great flavor.
