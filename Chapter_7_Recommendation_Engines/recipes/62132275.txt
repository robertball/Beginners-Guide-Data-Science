Title:
West African Chicken and Ground Nut Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts (skinless) cut into small pieces
1 can chunk chicken
1 Tablespoon peanut oil (or you can use Veg. Oil)
1 medium onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 28 oz can tomatoes, undrained
1 15.5 oz can Northern or Navy Beans undrained
1 11 oz. can while kernal corn, drained
1 Sweet Potato, peeled and cut into small pieces
3/3 c water
1/4 to 1/2 cup peanut butter
1 Tablespoon tomato paste
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 tespoon ginger
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
3 cups hot cooked rice
1-1 1/2 cup dry roasted peanuts

Directions:
Start peeling and slicing the sweet potato first, because this takes the most time. In a 4 quart pot, saute chicken,onion and garlic in oil, until no longer pink. Stir in canned chicken, and remaining ingredients (except rice& peanuts).Mix well and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to med-low, cover and simmer 30 minutes or until Sweet Potato is tender.Serve over hot rice, and have peanuts to serve over the top.
