Title:
Dad's Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb sweet or hot Italian sausage (5 links)
1/2 lb ground beef
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, crushed
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon salt
1-1/2 tsp dried basil leaves
1/2 tsp fennel seed
1/4 tsp pepper
1/8 cup chopped parsley
4 cups canned tomatoes, undrained; or 1 can (2 lb, 3 oz) Italian-style tomatoes
2 cans (6-oz size) tomato paste

Directions:
Remove sausage from outer casings; chop the meat.  In 5-quart Dutch oven, over medium heat, saut√© sausage, beef (break up beef with wooden spoon), onion, and garlic, stirring frequently, until well browned - about 20 minutes.
Add sugar, 1 tablespoon salt, the basil, fennel, pepper, and parsley; mix well.  Add tomatoes, tomato paste, and 1/2 cup water, mashing tomatoes with wooden spoon.  Bring to boiling; reduce heat; simmer, covered and stirring occasionally, until thick - about 1.5 hours.
