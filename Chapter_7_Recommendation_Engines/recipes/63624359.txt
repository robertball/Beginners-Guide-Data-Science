Title:
Easy Peaches and Cream Cheese Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1008108.jpg
Ingredients:
4 beaten eggs
1 cup milk
3/4 cup buttermilk
Â½ c sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
1/8 tsp. salt
1 - 15 oz. can diced peaches, drained
6-8 cups day old bread, crusts removed, cubed
4 oz cream cheese, divided
Glaze:
1 T. cream cheese, softened
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup powdered sugar
2 tsp. milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 3 quart casserole dish.
Mix together eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla, pumpkin pie spice, & salt. Stir in bread and peaches.
Dollop 6-8 teaspoons of cream cheese on bottom of casserole dish. Add bread mixture on top. Add rest of cream cheese in spoonfuls on top, tucking into mixture with a knife.
Bake for 1 hour or until golden brown on top.
For glaze: Mix cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla. Add milk 1/2 tsp. at a time until you reach desired consistency. Spread glaze over top and enjoy.
