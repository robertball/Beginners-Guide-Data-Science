Title:
Best Mug Cake (Paleo) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1017996.jpg
Ingredients:
Large Mug
Chocolate or Chocolate Chips(enough to cover bottom of mug, I usually use 3 or 4 Tbsp. But you can add more)
1 Tbsp. Olive Oil
2 Tbsp. Water
2 Tbsp. Coconut Flour
1/8 Tsp. Baking soda
1 egg
Drop of coffee extract or coffee (Optional)

Directions:
1. Put chocolate and oil in mug and melt in microwave.
2. Add Water, coconut flour, and baking soda and stir well with a fork.
3. Add egg and coffee extract or coffee and stir well.
4. Place in microwave and heat for 1 and a half minutes. Allow to cool for a few minutes and enjoy!

Submitter: 
DenaMarie89
Description: 
This is my favorite Paleo Mug cake recipe that I altered. It's a quick dessert that satisfies any sugar or chocolate craving!
