Title:
pea wiggle Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
4 C milk
8 tbs butter
8 tbs flour
1/2 -1 bag frozen peas
1 can salmon or tuna

Directions:
over med-low heat,whisk together milk,butter + flour till there are no lumps. add peas + samlmon or tuna.
serve over saltine crackers.
