Title:
Apple Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
1 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/3 cup orange juice
1 cup chopped apples
1/3 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Directions:
Cream the butter and sugar in a bowl. Add eggs and vanilla and beat well. Mix the flour, soda and salt and add to creamed mixture, alternatly with the orange juice.
Fold in apples and walmuts and placed in a greased loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees for about 55 minutes.

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
A delicious fruit bread, warm from the oven, with fresh apples folded right in. Great bread for celebrating autumn!
