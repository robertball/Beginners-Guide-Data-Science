Title:
Tarkari Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp butter
1 tsp vegetable oil
2 bay leaves
1 tsp cumin seeds
2 cloves garlic-finely chopped
2 medium onions-diced
1 tsp crushed ginger
2 carrots-diced
1 tsp ground tumeric
1/2 cup green peas
3 tomatoes-diced
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp salt
1/2 head of cauliflower cut up
Optional-other vegetables like broccoli, cooked potato, asparagus, etc.  Tofu or cooked chicken can also be added.

Directions:
Heat butter and oil in large pan.
Add bay leaves and cumin seeds and cook until browned(just a minute or two)
Add garlic, onion and ginger and cook until onion is soft (about 3 minutes)
Add vegetables
Add tumeric, coriander and salt and mix well.
Cook for 3-4 minutes or until vegetables reach desired tenderness
Serve with flat bread or rice and chopped cucumbers and plain non-fat yogurt.

Submitter: 
mixingmedias
Description: 
This is a Nepalese vegetable dish.  I cook it as a vegan dish, but it's easy to add chicken to it if that's your thing.
