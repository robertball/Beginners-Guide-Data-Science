Title:
Spiced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Tang instant breakfast drink
1 1/2 cups instant tea
1 tsp. ground cloves
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
2 cups sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and keep in tightly covered jar.  Use two heaping teaspoons per cup of hot water.
