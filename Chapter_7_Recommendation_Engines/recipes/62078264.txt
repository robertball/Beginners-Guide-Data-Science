Title:
Baked Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can creamed corn
1 can whole kernel corn (drained)
1/2 cup ( 1 stick) melted butter
1 box corn muffin mix (dry)
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Beat eggs. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Put in 9" deep dish pie plate or 2 quart casserole. * I lightly spray dish with nonstick oil spray before filling* Bake for an hour.
