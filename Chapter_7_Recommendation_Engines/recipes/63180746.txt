Title:
Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup brown rice
2 1/2 cup water
1 T butter
1 t salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Bring water, butter and salt to rolling boil in saucepan.
Place rice in 8x8 square pan and pour boiling water over it.  Stir to combine.
Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Alton Browns base recipe - turns out perfect each and every time.  May substitute broth for water and, of course, add items such as broccoli, bell pepper, carrots, etc. if desired.
