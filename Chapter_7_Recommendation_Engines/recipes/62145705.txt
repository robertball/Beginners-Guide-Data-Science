Title:
Scalloped Sweet Potatoes Au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-12 Medium Sweet Potatoes
1 cup Milk
2 cups Heavy Cream
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
Salt and pepper to taste (about 1/2 tsp each)
2 tablespoons stick butter
1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
Peel and slice sweet potatoes very thin (preferably using a mandoline)
In a very large sauce pan combine milk, cream, nutmeg, cinnamon, salt and pepper.
Add sweet potato slices to pan and allow to simmer and sauce to thicken.
Butter a large baking dish.  Transfer potato mixture to baking dish.
In a seperate bowl, combine butter, bread crumbs and cheddar to make au gratin mixture.
Genergously sprinkle top of potatoes with cheese mixture.
Bake for about 35-45 minutes until potatoes are cooked all the way through.

Submitter: 
Jill H
Description: 
This is similar to regular scalloped potatoes, but uses sweet potatoes instead.  Went over great with Christmas dinner as an unusual subsitute.
