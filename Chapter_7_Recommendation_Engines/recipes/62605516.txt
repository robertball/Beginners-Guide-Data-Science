Title:
Mom's Chocolate Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg German chocolate
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 c boiling water
1/2 c sugar
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese
8 oz carton cool whip

Directions:
Break up pkg of german chocolate.  Pour boiling water over it to melt, let cool.  Add vanilla. Beat sugar into cream cheese.  Add chocolate and beat in.  Fold in cool whip.  Chill.  Best if made a day ahead.

Submitter: 
chefmom
Description: 
Thick and creamy as a dessert alone or put in pie crust.
