Title:
Stuffed peppers by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large green peppers
3/4 lb ground turkey
3/4 c brown rice
1/4 onion
1tsp Lowry seasonimg
1tsp Garlic powder
1tsp Onion powder
1tsp celery salt
1can petite diced tomatoes
Sauce
1can tomato soup
1/2 c 1%milk
1/4 c sour cream

Directions:
Cookrice separately until nearly done
Brown turkey with seasonings
When nearly cooked, Add onions & peppers
Add can tomatoes and rice
Stuff peppers and cook medium-med low. Add 1/2 water to pan.
Meanwhile, combine and heat sauce
When peppers have cooked about 20 minutes. Add sauce on top and around pepper. Cook another 15 minutes
