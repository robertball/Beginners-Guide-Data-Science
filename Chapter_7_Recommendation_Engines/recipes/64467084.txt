Title:
Pie People Loves Geeks for Giving Dinomite Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2453909.jpg
Ingredients:
3 bags Marshmallows
6 tablespoons butter
Red and Blue Food Coloring
3 Boxes Captain Crunch Cereal
Dark Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Melt 1 bag of marshmallows with 2 tablespoons of butter
Add 2-3 drops of red food coloring to mixture
Mix with box of cereal
Form into cube shapes
Repeat two more times to make blue and white cubes
Refrigerate over night to set
Mix all cubes together with chocolate chips to create a trail mix of Dinomite Fluffy Bites.

Submitter: 
Amanda Kalnins
Description: 
A sugary treat perfect with a kale smoothie.
