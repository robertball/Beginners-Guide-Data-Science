Title:
Taco Salad for a Crowd (100) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 pounds hamburger
22 oz pkg taco seasoning
½ - #10 can tomato puree
1 lg baking pan
¼ case tomatoes -- diced
½ bunch green onions
1 red onion
2 - 5# bags salad mix, without add-ins
4 - 2# bag tortilla chips
½  - #10 can black beans
½ - #10 can kidney beans
1 - #10 can black olives, sliced
1 gallon salsa
5 pounds cheddar cheese
6 pounds sour cream (thin sour cream 6 parts sour cream to 1 part milk OR use mexican sour cream

Directions:
Brown hamburger and drain.  Spray baking pans and move meat to baking pans.  Mix taco seasoning with tomato puree, blend in with meat.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until 165 degrees.
Portion: ½ cup meat per person
