Title:
Dave's North Side Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/884550.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of a light vegetable oil
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/2 cup lemon juice
2 tsps sugar
1tsp salt
1/2 tsp oregano,
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp paprika

Directions:
The volume of this recipe fits nicely into a magic bullet. Put all the ingredients into the bullet and blend for at least two minutes, the more the better. Refrigerate and you're done, it keeps very well.
