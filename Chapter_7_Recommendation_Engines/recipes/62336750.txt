Title:
Loaded Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/494761.jpg
Ingredients:
tortilla chips
1 cup prepared manwich
1/2 cup salsa
1 small tomato seeded and diced
3 green onions sliced
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Heat manwich in microwave for 60 seconds or until hot.
Spread tortilla chips over lined pizza pan, covering pan completely.
Spread the manwich evenly over tortillas, followed by salsa, tomato, green onion, and cheddar cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
These nachos are good as a snack, but also make a great meal.
