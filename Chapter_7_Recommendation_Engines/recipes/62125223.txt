Title:
Berry Popsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2	cup raspberry (or your favorite) juice
1 	envelope gelatin (pick a flavor that compliments your juice)
1 	cup vanilla yogurt
1 	cup berries (fresh or frozen)

Directions:
Pour the berry juice in a saucepan and add the gelatin. Warm on the stove on low heat. Stir until the gelatin dissolves. Pour into a blender and add yogurt and berries. Blend for about 2 minutes.
Pour the mixture into molds and poke a wooden stick in the middle. Put in the freeze for a few hours. Take out and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sarah Waller
Description: 
Nothing beats a lazy summer afternoon, relaxing with a homemade treat. Experiment by putting ripe fruits and mixing juices with your popsicles.
