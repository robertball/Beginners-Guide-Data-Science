Title:
Sonora Corn Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBL butter
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cans (14 1/4 oz each) whole kernel corn, drained
1 medium red bell pepper, chopped
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded, finely chopped
2 cans (14 1/2 oz each) ready-to-serve chicken broth
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp chili powder
tortilla chips (optional)

Directions:
In large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat until hot; cook and stir onion 5 minutes or until tender. Add corn, bell pepper and jalapeno pepper; cook and stir 5 minutes. Stir in broth, cumin and chili powder; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 20 minutes. Garnish with tortilla chips, if desired.
