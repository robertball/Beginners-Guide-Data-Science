Title:
Rynea's Beef and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Ground Beef
1 Onion
1 pkg Mushrooms
2 Tablespoons Beef Bouillon
6 cups Water
1/4 cup Parsley chopped
1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
Pepper

Directions:
Brown ground Beef , Onion ,Mushrooms and drain. In a pot boil water, beef bouillon parsley and garlic powder add ground beef mixture and noodles. Serve over mashed Potatoes  Home made noodles are the best.

Submitter: 
Rynea Greathouse-Browning
Description: 
This is a Favorite recipe that I came up with when I was a kid. Quick and Easy and Yummy too.
