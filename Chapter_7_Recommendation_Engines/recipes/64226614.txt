Title:
Ham and Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (8 ounces) sliced pineapple
2 boneless fully cooked ham steaks (about 2 pounds)
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
4 maraschino cherries
Read more:

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving 1 tablespoon juice; set pineapple aside (discard remaining juice or save for another use). Cut each ham steak in half; place in an ungreased baking pan.
Combine brown sugar, mustard and reserved juice; spread over ham. Top with pineapple and cherries. Bake, uncovered, at 350Â° for 20-25 minutes or until heated through. Yield: 4 servings.
