Title:
Toblerone Chocolate Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- (100g) Toblerone chocolate bar
1- (300 mL) can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Break chocolate bar and melt in saucepan
Stir in condensed milk. Cook on medium until heated and well blended.
