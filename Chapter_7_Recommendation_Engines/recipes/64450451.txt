Title:
Deep South Okar & Tomatoes (INCOMPLETE) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slices bacon
4 cups okra, fresh or frozen, sliced
1 onion, finely chopped
1 tsp. salt
½ tsp. black pepper
½ tsp. ground cumin
¼ tsp. garlic powder
1 16-oz. can tomatoes,undrained
Hot cooked rice

Directions:
Fry bacon until crisp.  Drain and crumble, reserving drippings in pan.
