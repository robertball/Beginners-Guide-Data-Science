Title:
Melt in Your Mouth Marinade - modified by Stan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons minced garlic
2 tablespoons mustard powder
1 tablespoon ground ginger
1/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
In a small, nonporous bowl, combine the soy sauce, olive oil, garlic, mustard powder, ginger, and brown sugar. Mix well, cover and refrigerate until ready to use on your favorite meat.

Submitter: 
StanDiego
Description: 
Following advice from others,I reduced the soy sauce and ginger, added brown sugar, will try this on pork sirloin roast.Modified from: Melt in Your Mouth Marinade
