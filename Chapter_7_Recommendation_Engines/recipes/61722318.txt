Title:
Healthier Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/281859.jpg
Ingredients:
2 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
3/4 cup crumbled tofu
1 lime, juiced
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup diced onion
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
Place tofu and one avocado in a food processor and blend until smooth. In a medium bowl, mash together the remaining avocado, lime juice, and salt. Mix in tofu-avodaco mixture, onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper, if using. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.

Submitter: 
MakeItHealthy
Description: 
We decreased the fat and added protein to this recipe by substituting tofu for one avacado! Keep the healthy trend going by serving with multigrain or baked tortilla chips.Modified from: Guacamole
