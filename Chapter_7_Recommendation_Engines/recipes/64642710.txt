Title:
Amelia's German Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4518268.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of flour
2 cups of milk
2 eggs
1/2 teaspoon of salt.

Directions:
Mix flour and milk until smooth.
Add eggs and mix, then add salt.
Heat a little oil in a medium sized frying pan.
Put a little bit of batter on the pan and swirl around to spread. It should be very thin.
Fry it until golden brown then flip it to let the other side cook.When ready, put fruit, Nutella, or other spreads to put on it then roll it up.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sarah Klein
Description: 
A crepe-like pancake that looks and tastes delicious.
