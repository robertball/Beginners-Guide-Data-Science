Title:
Seabeck "Secret Sauce" for Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. butter
1/2 cup lemon juice (unsweetened)
2 Tbl. Liquid Smoke
1/4 cup yellow prepared mustard
Pinch pepper
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup chopped onion

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in pan, heat over low heat.  DO NOT boil.
Baste fish before grilling and again just before serving.

Submitter: 
daf
Description: 
This is the wonderful salmon sauce on salmon roasted on open fire pits, every Memorial Day weekend, next to Hood Canal, with the Olympics in the background.
