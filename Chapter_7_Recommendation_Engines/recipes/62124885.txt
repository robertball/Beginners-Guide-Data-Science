Title:
Great Grandma Patterson's Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 Large tomatoes, peeled & chopped
6 onions, chopped
5 peppers, chopped
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2-1/2 cups sugar (we often only use 1 cup)
1 cup vinegar

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a large pot over medium heat.
cook 2 hours.
Pour into mason jars and can.

Submitter: 
BOILERMISH
Description: 
This sauce has been in my family for many generations; we use it for pot roasts or tenderloin in the crock pot.  Mason jars work best for canning.
