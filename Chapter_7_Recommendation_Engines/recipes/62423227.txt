Title:
Sun Dried Tomato and Feta Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/446202.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (3 ounce) Tilapia Filets
2 Tbs Sun Dried Tomatoes (in oil)
2 Tbs Crumbled Feta
1/8 tsp Ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375 F
Dice Sun dried tomatoes.
In a small bowl, mix the sun dried tomatoes, feta and pepper into a paste.
Place Tilapia filets in a glass baking dish and top with tomato and feta mixture.
Bake in oven for 15-18 minutes or until Tilapia flakes easily with a fork.

Submitter: 
My4boys
Description: 
Sun dried Tomatoes mixed with feta and a pinch of pepper is mixed and baked on top of Tilapia. The flavor is the perfect mixture of of sweet, salty and a bit of 'tang'. I like to serve mine over a bed of baby spinach for a light lunch.
