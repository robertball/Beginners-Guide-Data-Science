Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts boiled and shredded
12 oz Frank's hot sauce
2 - 8 oz packages cream cheese
1 16 oz bottle ranch or bleu cheese dressing
16 oz shredded monterey jack cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a crock pot and heat until cheese is melted. Serve with chips.
