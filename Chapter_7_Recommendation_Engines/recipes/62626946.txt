Title:
Cup-a-cup-a-cup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 cup sugar
1 cup self-rising flour
1 cup milk
1 can fruit (your choice: peach, blueberry, cherry), not drained

Directions:
Melt butter in square cake pan or casserole dish, In mixingbowl, combine sugar, flour, milk, then pour in pan/dish over melted butter. Add canned fruit, and juice on top. Do not stir. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes until brown.
