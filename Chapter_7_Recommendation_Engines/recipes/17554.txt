Title:
Meatballs in Chile Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976260.jpg
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
2 eggs, lightly beaten
3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoon ORTEGA速 Thick & Chunky Salsa
1/2 teaspoon salt
SAUCE:
1 (24 ounce) jar ORTEGA速 Thick & Chunky Salsa
1 cup water
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 canned chipotle chile in adobo sauce, finely chopped (optional)
1 MAGGI速 Chicken Bouillon Cube
cooked rice

Directions:
FOR MEATBALLS: COMBINE beef, onion, bread crumbs, eggs, parsley, chile sauce and salt in large bowl; mix well. Form mixture into 18 meatballs.
FOR SAUCE: COMBINE salsa, water, tomato paste, garlic, chiles and bouillon in large saucepan. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low. Place meatballs in sauce; cook, stirring occasionally, for 20 to 25 minutes or until meatballs are no longer pink in center. Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Ortega速
Description: 
Your family will love this meatball recipe, made extra delicious by the addition of smoky chipotle chiles. Chipotles are smoked jalapenos, available dried, pickled, or canned in adobo (mild red chile) sauce. You can find chipotle chiles at Mexican groceries, most major supermarkets and gourmet food stores.
