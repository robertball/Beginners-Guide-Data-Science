Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886995.jpg
Ingredients:
5 sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced
1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
1/4 cup low fat margarine
2 tablespoons orange juice
2 pinches ground cinnamon
1 (10.5 ounce) package miniature marshmallows

Directions:
Boil sliced sweet potatoes in water until tender. Drain.
In a large bowl, blend the potatoes until creamy.
Stir in the butter, brown sugar, orange juice to taste and a dash of ground cinnamon.
Spread the sweet potato mixture into a 9x13 inch pan.
Sprinkle the miniature marshmallows over the top.
Bake at 350ยบ until golden brown.
