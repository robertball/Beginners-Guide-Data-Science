Title:
Cinnamon Sugared Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 egg white
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 cups pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325ยบ F.
Whip egg white and mix with pecans. In a separate bowl mix sugar, cinnamon and salt. Add to pecans. Spread in baking sheet and bake for 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Erin
Description: 
My grandma makes these every year for the holidays. It's always handy to make extra for later snacking! They are relatively quick and easy to make and taste wonderful. I love the smell of these when they're in the oven.
