Title:
Turkey Cheeseburger Soup in Instant Pot by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6429757.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb ground turkey
1 medium onion, diced
2 carrots, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
2 Yukon gold potatoes, diced
1/4 head cauliflower (leave whole or in big chunks for easy removal)
1 tbsp butter
2 tbsp flour
2 tsp Lowry seasoning
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp dill weed
3.5 c broth, I used beef bullion cubes to make the broth
8 oz sharp cheddar cheese, shredded or cubed

Directions:
In Instant Pot, high sauté setting: brown turkey with Lowry seasoning, remove.
Add butter, sauté onion, carrots, celery until just starting to get tender about 5 minutes.
Add flour, garlic, pepper and dill, cook about 1 minute.
Add turkey, potatoes and broth. Add cauliflower on top, close lid.
Manual, high pressure for 10 minutes, quick release.
Remove cauliflower and blend with 1 c soup liquid until smooth and creamy.
Add cheese and more seasoning if desired.
Add cauliflower purée and stir, if necessary heat on medium until cheese is completely melted.

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
Great diet friendly variation of an old favorite. Uses puréed cauliflower to thicken and make the soup creamy. 
220 cals, 11 g fat, 12 carbs, 18 g protein per serving
