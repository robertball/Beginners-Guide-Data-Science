Title:
Candied Salmon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
Lettuce
Onion - sliced
Cranberries
Roasted/Candied pecans
Avacado - sliced
Goat or Feta Cheese
Candied Salmon
Bruschetta (if we have any)
Dressing:
1/2 Tbsp fresh or 1/4tbsp dry dill
1/3 Cup lemon juice
1 Tbsp dijon mustard
2 Tbsp honey
2 cloves garlic crushed
1 Tbsp white vinegar
1 Tbsp olive oil

Directions:
1. Mix together salad ingredients in a large bowl.
2. Mix together salad dressing ingredients in a small bowl.
3. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
This is delicious and so good for you!  The dressing is very low in fat.
