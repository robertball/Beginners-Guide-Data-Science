Title:
Mona Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
2 cups brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. soda
2 cups rolled oats
2 cups chocolate chips (or combo of chip flavors - salted caramel, white, PB, etc.!)
3/4 cup coconut

Directions:
Cream butter & sugar together.
Add eggs & vanilla.
Add remaining ingred., mixing well.
Drop by spoonfuls onto baking sheet, flattening a bit with a fork dipped in flour.
Bake @ 350 x 8-10 min. depending on size.
