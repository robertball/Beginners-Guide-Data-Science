Title:
NO Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 3/4 cups white sugar
-1/2 cup milk
-4 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
-1/2 cup crunchy peanut butter
-3 cups quick-cooking oats
-1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
1. In a meduim saucepan, combine sugar, milk, butter, and cocoa powder. Bring to boil, and cook for 1 1/2 minutes. Remove frome heat.
2. Stir in chunky peanut butter, oats, and vanilla.
3. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto wax paper.
4. Let cool until harden.

Submitter: 
live_love_cook
Description: 
These are the easiest cookies to make ever!!! You don't have to bake the actual cookies but you do have to heat up some of the ingredients!!! These are the easiest and most delicous cookies EVER!!!
