Title:
Spicy Apple and Onion Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large white onion, sliced thin
2 Tbsp real butter
1 large clove garlic, chopped fine
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
3 MacIntosh apples, peeled and sliced medium (not too thin)
1/4 cup chopped canned jalapenos
1 Tbsp honey

Directions:
Heat skillet on medium low.
Add butter to skillet.
When butter is melted, add onions.  Turn heat up to medium on skillet.
Saute onions until transparent and begin to turn brown. (Approximately 10-15 minutes)
Add salt and garlic.
Saute one more minute.
Add apples and jalapenos, then cover with lid. Turn heat back down to medium low.
Simmer until apples are soft and transparent. (Approximately 4-5 minutes)
Add honey.
Simmer 2 minutes.
Serve.
