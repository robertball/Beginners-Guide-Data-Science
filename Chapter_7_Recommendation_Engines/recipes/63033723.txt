Title:
Potato Bread/Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, or more if needed
1/4 cup sugar
4 tsp Rapid Rise yeast
2 teaspoons salt
1 1/2 Cup hot water
1 Cup Milk
3/4 cup butter OR margarine
2/3 cup mashed potato flakes
2 eggs

Directions:
In large bowl, combine 1 cup flour, sugar, un-dissolved yeast, and salt
Heat water, milk, and butter until very warm (120o to 130oF)
Stir potato flakes into liquid and let soften 1 minute
Stir liquid into dry ingredients and stir until combined
Stir in 2 eggs and enough remaining flour to make a soft dough
Knead on lightly floured surface until smooth and elastic (about 4 to 6 minutes), OR knead with electric mixer using dough hook.
Cover with damp paper towel; let rest on floured surface 10 minutes.
Punch dough down; divide in half to make 2 large loaves or divide into 24 pieces for rolls (or Bierocks)
Let rise 20 to 45 minutes until nearly doubled
Bake in preheated 350 degrees F oven for 25 to 30 minutes
Cool and serve
