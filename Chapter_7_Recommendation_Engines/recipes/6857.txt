Title:
Jalapeno Bread I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/305032.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons white sugar
1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
6 tablespoons fresh chopped jalapeno peppers
3 cups bread flour
1/2 tablespoon active dry yeast

Directions:
Add ingredients according to the bread machine manufacturer's directions.
Set your machine on regular basic bread.

Nicole Cassidy
