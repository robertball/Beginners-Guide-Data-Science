Title:
Loomi Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6427212.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Syrian dried limes (dried black limes)
4 cups water
2 tbsp light honey, or to taste

Directions:
Crush to the limes with a pestle in a mortar until the lime pieces resemble small stones, not too fine.
Bring the water and crushed lime to a boil. Reduce the temperature and simmer about 5 minutes.
Strain into 4 tea cups. Add honey as desired. Serve hot or cold.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Refreshing sour tea I found in Dubai. Great for settling upset tummies, too. Serve hot or cold.
