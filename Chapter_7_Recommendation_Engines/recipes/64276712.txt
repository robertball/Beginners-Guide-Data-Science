Title:
Slow-Cooker Pepper Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef sirloin, cut into 2 inch strips
garlic powder to taste
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 cube beef bouillon
1/4 cup hot water
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1/2 cup sliced onion
2 large green or red bell peppers, roughly sliced
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, with liquid
3 tablespoons soy sauce
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 teaspoon brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Sprinkle strips of sirloin with garlic powder to taste. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat the vegetable oil and brown the seasoned beef strips.
Transfer to a slow cooker.
Mix bouillon cube with hot water until dissolved, then mix in cornstarch until dissolved. Pour into the slow cooker with meat. Stir in stewed tomatoes, soy sauce, sugar and salt.
Last 2 hours of cook time, add the onions and peppers.
Cover, and cook on High for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low for 6 to 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Crystal S
Description: 
Tasty strips of sirloin are seasoned with garlic powder, then slow cooked with onion, green pepper, and stewed tomatoes for this easy and comforting dinner.Modified from: Slow-Cooker Pepper Steak
