Title:
English Muffin Hors d'oeuvres Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 C. Mayonnaise
1 1/2 C. Sharp Cheddar Cheese Shredded
6 Green Onions Diced
Dash of Garlic Salt
Dash of Salt
Dash of Pepper
1 Package of English Muffins

Directions:
Mix the first five ingredients together.
Cut the English muffins in half along the seem.
Spread the mixture on the English muffins.
Cut the English muffins in quarters.
Broil 4-6 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
