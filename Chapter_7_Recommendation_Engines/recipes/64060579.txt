Title:
Pureed Carrots with Orange and Ginger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lb carrots, peeled and 1/2" sliced
4 c vegetable broth
2 T sugar
1 t kosher salt
1/2 c olive oil
2 T ginger, fresh, peeled and finely grated
4 garlic cloves, minced
2/3 c orange juice, fresh

Directions:
Simmer carrots, broth, sugar, and salt over medium heat until carrots are very tender. Drain and reserve 1/2 c cooking liquid.
Saute ginger and garlic in oil briefly. Puree with carrots and orange juice in food processor (or use hand blender). Add cooking liquid as needed for smooth consistency. Adjust salt and pepper.
