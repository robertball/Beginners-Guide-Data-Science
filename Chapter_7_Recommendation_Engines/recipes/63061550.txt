Title:
A Twist on Dixie Stampede's Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/771119.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon corn syrup
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon white pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 1/2 cups water
1 pint half-and-half cream (I've also tried it with heavy cream for a richer taste, mmm)
6 Large potatoes, peeled and cubed
1/2 can of Delmonte Sliced Carrots

Directions:
Peel potatoes and cut into cubes. Then boil in a pot until soft.
In another pot, melt margarine. Add onion, garlic, and corn syrup. Simmer 1 minute. Do NOT brown.
Add flour, white pepper, salt, chicken broth, water, carrots and cubed potatoes. Simmer 3 minutes.
Add half-and-half. cook until desired consistency. Stir constantly - do NOT bring to boil.
