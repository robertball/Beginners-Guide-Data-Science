Title:
Bacon Cheese Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 uncooked jumbo pasta shells
1c chopped onion
1T plus 1/4c butter, divided
1-1/2c ricotta cheese
8oz cream cheese, softened, divided
1-1/2c shredded Asiago cheese, divided
1c shredded Parmesan Acheese
1c crumbled cooked bacon
2T minced fresh parsley, divided
1/2t garlic salt
1/2t ground nutmeg
1/4t pepper
2T flour
2c heavy cream
1/2c chicken broth
1/2c 2% milk
2c shredded Romano cheese
1-1/2c shredded mozzarella

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed.
Saute onion in 1T butter.
Beat ricotta and half of cream cheese in a large bowl.
Stir in 1/2c Asiago cheese, Parmesan, bacon, 1T parsley, garlic salt, nutmeg, pepper, and onion.
Spoon into shells and place into greased 9x13 dish.
Melt remaining butter in large saucepan.
Stir in flour until smooth.
Gradually add cream, broth, milk.
Boil and cook 1-2 minutes until thickened.
Stir in Romano cheese, remaining cream cheese, asiago and parsley.
Pour over shells.
Sprinkle with mozzarella.
Cover and bake 350 for 30 minutes.
Uncover and bake 10-15 minutes longer.
