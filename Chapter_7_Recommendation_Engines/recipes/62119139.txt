Title:
Almond Joy Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11/2 cups butter
1(14oz) can sweetened condensed milk
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 pound (51/3 cups)sweetened shredded coconut
2 cups toasted almonds
2 cups (12 oz) milk or semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 tablespoon vegetable shortening

Directions:
1. Line a 9X13 inch baking pan with buttered wax paper or aluminum foil, and set aside.
2.  Melt butter in large saucepan, add milk and vanilla and mix well.  Stir in coconut, and almonds and mix well.  Press into prepared pan and refrigerate until firm or 1hr.
3.  Melt chocolate and shortening in double boiler.  Invert candy from pan, and cut into squares.  Dip candy piece in chocolate and sit on wax paper until chocolate is set(30 mins).
