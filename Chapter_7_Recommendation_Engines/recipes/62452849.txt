Title:
seven minute frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg whites
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/4 tsp. cream of tartar or
1 tbsp light corn syrup
1/3 cup water
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
combine egg whites, sugar, cream of tartar and water on top of double boiler.
Beat on high speed 1 minute with electric mixer.
Place over boiling water - beat on high speed 7 minutes.
Remove pan from boiling water;  add vanilla.
Beat 2 minutes longer on high speed

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
white and fluffy frosting.  No icing sugar
