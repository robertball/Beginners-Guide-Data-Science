Title:
Chinese Scallion Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/388444.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon yeast
1/2 cup warm water
1/2 cup hot water
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt (or garlic salt)
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 cup finely chopped scallions

Directions:
Sift the 2 cups of flour and separate the flower into two equal portion. In a bowl, combined and stir the warm 1/2 cup of water, yeast, and sugar in a bowl. Combine the yeast mixture with 1 cup of flower and stir with a spatula. With the other cup of flower, add the 1 teaspoon of salt and 2 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Stir the mixture till is a nice dough like texture (add flower if necessary).
Combine the two dough from above and knead them together. Continue to dust with flower to avoid the dough from sticking to the bowl. Dusk a table area with flower and place the dough in a round ball and cover it with a warm damp towel for 30-40 minutes.
Pinch the dough into two pieces. Roll each dough out and add sprinkle 1/2 of the scallion to each dough piece. Roll up each dough and pinch each dough again so that you get 4 pieces. Dusk each dough pieces out as necessary and roll them out into big circles.
over a medium heat with oil, place each scallion pancake and heat till browned on each side. serve as ready!
