Title:
Country Style Fried Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 table spoons. Olive oil
6 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
In a 2 QT. bowl mix potato's and 2 table spoons. Virgin Olive Oil.Heat a large cast iron skillet, heat on medium-high, add potatoes and cook until potatoes are golden brown. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder and paprika. Serve hot.
