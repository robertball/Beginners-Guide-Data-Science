Title:
Sloppy Joes II (and a half) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 clove garlic minced
1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup favorite BBQ sauce
1 Tbl brown sugar(or to taste)
1 Tbl. Worchestershire sauce
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef, onion, garlic and green pepper; drain off liquids.
Stir in the mustard, ketchup, BBQ sauce, Worchestershire and brown sugar; mix thoroughly. Reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Debi Blair McGinness
Description: 
The addtion of BBQ sauce & Worchestershire makes the perfect Sloppy Joes. I use Rasta Joes Spicy BBQ sauce to give it a little kick.Modified from: Sloppy Joes II
