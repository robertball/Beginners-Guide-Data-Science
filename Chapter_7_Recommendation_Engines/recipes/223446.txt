Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups finely chopped mango or fresh pineapple
1 large avocado, halved, seeded, peeled, and finely chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 tablespoon lemon juice or lime juice
1 tablespoon finely chopped jalapeno pepper (optional)
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
40 Keebler速 Town House速 Original Crackers

Directions:
In small bowl toss together mango, avocado, onion, cilantro, lemon juice, jalapeno pepper (if desired), salt and pepper.
Spoon mango mixture on top of KEEBLER TOWN HOUSE Original crackers. Serve immediately.

Description: 
Avocado and mango salsa with the kick of chopped jalapenos is served with crackers for a colorful, zesty appetizer.
