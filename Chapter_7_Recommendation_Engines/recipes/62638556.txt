Title:
Jessica's Rotel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # hamburger
2 # velveeta
1 can rotel
dash paprika
dash garlic powder
dash red pepper

Directions:
brown meat and drain.  Place rotel tomatoes in crock pot then cut up velveeta and place on top.  Turn crock on low and let melt, stirring occasionally.  Add meat and stir.  Serve with tortilla chips, Tostitoes scoops are the best!
