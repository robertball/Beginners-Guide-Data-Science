Title:
Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
1 1/2 cups granulated white sugar
2 large eggs
1 tsp pure vanilla extract
COATING: 1/3 cup granulated white sugar + 2 tsp ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a large bowl whisk together 2 3/4 cups flour, 1/2 tsp salt and 2 tsp baking powder.
In electric mixer bowl, beat 2 sticks butter and 1 1/2 cups sugar until smooth, about 2-3 minutes.
Add 2 eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Scrape down sides of bowl.
Beat in 1 tsp vanilla.
Add flour mixture and beat until smooth dough is formed.
Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours, or until firm enough to roll into balls.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees and place rack in center of oven.
Shape dough into 1-inch round balls.
Roll the ball of dough in cinnamon sugar and place 2 inches apart on pan.
Gently flatten each cookie to about 1/2-inch thick with bottom of glass.
Bake for 8-10 minutes or until light golden brown around the edges.
Place on a wire rack to cool.
