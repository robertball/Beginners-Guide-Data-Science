Title:
Salmon - How to cook in oven Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 salmon filet
2 TBSP butter
favorite herbs

Directions:
Choose center cuts. Remove salmon 20 minutes before cooking. Preheat oven to 475 degrees
If there are any bones, remove them.
Put butter in ceramic pan, preheat pan for 5 minutes. Season flesh side with herbs.
In the preheated pan place salmon skin side up, bake 5 minutes. Remove skin (if the skin does not come off bake longer). Season skin side with salt and pepper, turn over and bake 3 to 5 minutes longer
