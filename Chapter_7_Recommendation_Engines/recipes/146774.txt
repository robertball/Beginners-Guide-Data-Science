Title:
Chilled Strawberry Angel Food Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 inch) angel food cake
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 quart fresh strawberries, sliced
1 (18 ounce) jar strawberry glaze

Directions:
Crumble the cake into a 9x13 inch dish.
In a medium bowl, cream the cream cheese and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener until light and fluffy. Fold in whipped topping.  Mash the cake down with your hands and spread the cream cheese mixture over the cake.
In a bowl, combine strawberries and glaze until strawberries are evenly coated.  Spread over cream cheese layer.  Chill until serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
Angel food cake pieces are topped with a sweetened cream cheese topping and fresh strawberries in glaze in this chilled summer dessert.
