Title:
Jim B's Famous  Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/946330.jpg
Ingredients:
•	3 lbs. Ground beef, drained
•	1 cup pepperoni, chopped
•	1 white onion, chopped
•	4 garlic cloves, minced
•	5 celery stalks, chopped
•	2 reg. cans tomato sauce
•	3 can tomato paste (small)
•	2 beef bullion cubes
•	15 hot peppers (add ¼ the juice)
•	2 cans mushrooms, chopped
•	1 large can tomato juice
•	3/4 can 24 oz V8 juice
•	12 dashes of Frank’s Hot Sauce
•	1 beer
•	¼ cup olive oil
•	1 ½ TB crushed red pepper
•	½ cup Spanish olives
•	½ cup water
•	½ tsp. minced capers
•	palm full of Italian seasoning

Directions:
Fry the ground beef then drain it.
Saute the onions, celery, and garlic
Add everything and start cooking.  Bring it to a boil then keep it at a simmer.  Stir occasionally.

Submitter: 
Michael Bacon
