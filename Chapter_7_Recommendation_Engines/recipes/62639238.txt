Title:
Orange Dream Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/581398.jpg
Ingredients:
For cupcakes:
3/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup mascarpone cheese
1 tbsp. orange zest (zest from one large or 2 small oranges)
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. almond extract
2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/3 cup fresh squeezed orange juice
For Frosting:
1/2 (1/4 cup) stick butter or margarine, softened
1/2 block cream cheese, softened (4 oz.)
1/4 cup shortening
3 1/2 cups confectioner's sugar
1-2 tbsp heavy cream
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tbsp. orange juice
1 tsp. orange zest
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
For cupcakes:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line muffin tins with cupcake cups (24). Beat softened butter and sugar until light an fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, mascarpone, zest and extracts until  just combined. In separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder and salt. Also separate, combine heavy cream and orange juice. Slowly add dry ingredients and liquids to butter mixture, a little at a time, alternating mixtures. Mix until all ingredients are just combined. Fill paper cup lined muffin tins. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in the center of a cupcake comes out clean (Do not overbake). Place pans on cooling rack for 5 minutes. Remove cupcakes from pans and allow to cool on rack completely before frosting.
For frosting:
Cream together butter, cream cheese and shortening until smooth. Gradually mix in 2 cups of powdered sugar. Mix in extract, juice and zest. Gradually mix in remaining 1 1/2 cups of sugar and cream (as needed). Beat until smooth and fluffy (2-3 minutes). Frost cooled cupcakes.
