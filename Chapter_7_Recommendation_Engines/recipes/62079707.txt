Title:
Cucumber and onion relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/665740.jpg
Ingredients:
7 large cucumbers
5 large onions
1 handful salt
3 cups white sugar
1 cup water
3 cups white vinegar
1/2 cup flour
3 tsp tumeric
1 tsp ginger
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
Grind the cukes and onion and mix with the salt.  Let sit overnight.  Drain.
Mix remaining ingredients and boil 5 minutes or til thick,  then add cucumber and onions and boil another 10 minutes.   Put into hot sterilized jars and seal.

Submitter: 
Lynn Mott
Description: 
This has been handed down for generations and it is my families favorite relish.   Great with any beef dish or in a sandwich.
