Title:
Country Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz pork sausage
1 1/2 c hashed brown potatoes
1/4 c chopped onion
6 eggs
1/3 c milk
1/2 t parsley flakes
1/2 t salt
1 c shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1.  Brown sausage in 10 inch cast ion skillet over med heat, stirringuntil crumbly, remove with slotted spoon.
2.  Add potatoes and onions to drippings in skillet, cooking over med high heat for 5-7 min or until potatoes begin to brown and stirring occasionally.  Reduce heat to med.
3.  Beat eggs with milk, parsley flakes and salt in bown.  Pour over potatoes.  Add sausage.  Cook until eggs are set, stirring occasionally.
4.  Top with cheese.  Cook, covered, until cheese melts.
