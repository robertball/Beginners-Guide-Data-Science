Title:
Apple Rice (Apfel-Reis auf Deutsch!) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups water
1 cup rice
1/3 cup sugar, or more - to taste
1 apple, cut up (with or without peel - your preference)
cinnamon to taste
cloves to taste
nutmeg to taste
raisins, optional

Directions:
Put all ingredients in large saucepan.
Bring to boil.
Reduce heat to cook as directed for the rice you use.
Can be served at any temp., with or without a creamy topping, milk or additional cinnamon.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This apple flavored rice pudding has been known to be served as a very late night supper for a tired, hungry farmer who didn't want a heavy meal before bed! Lots of room for creativity with this recipe :)
