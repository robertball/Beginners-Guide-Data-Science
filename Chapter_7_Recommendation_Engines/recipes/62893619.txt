Title:
Bottled Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 bushel fresh tomatoes
3 lbs. onions
2 bulbs garlic (peel each individual clove)
2 cups olive oil
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup salt
2 Tbsp. dried oregano
1 Tbsp. dried basil
6-10 small cans tomato paste (use for thickening)

Directions:
Wash and puree in blender the tomatoes (do not remove skins), add to a large pot.  Puree onions and garlic, add to the tomatoes.  Add oil, sugar, salt, oregano and basil.  Bring to a boil and then add 6 to 8 cans tomato paste.  Let simmer about 2 hours.  If it's still quite runny, add more tomato paste.  Let thicken while simmering.  Pour into quart jars and process 45 minutes.
