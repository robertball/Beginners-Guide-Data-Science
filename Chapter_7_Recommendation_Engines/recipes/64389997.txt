Title:
"Coconut" Macaroons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 apple grated
Enough milk powder (try grated coconut or coconut flour) to make a paste.
Coconut extract.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Mix all together.
Spoon onto baking sheet.
Bake for 15-17 minutes (start checking at 10).
