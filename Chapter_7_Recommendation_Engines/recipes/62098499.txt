Title:
Ultimate Double Chocolate Crinkles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips, divided
3/4 c. granulated sugar
1/2 c. margarine
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups oatmeal, any kind
1 1/2 c. all-purpose flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 c. powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In saucepan over low heat, melt 1 cup chocolate chips. Let cool slightly.
Beat sugar and margarine until light and fluffy.
Blend in eggs, vanilla, and melted chocolate.
Combine oats, flour, baking powder, and salt in separate bowl. Add to chocolate mixture; mix well.
Stir in remaining 1 cup chocolate chips. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
Shape into one inch balls. Roll each ball in powdered sugar, coating heavily. Place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake for 10-12 minutes. Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet, then remove to wire rack to cool completely. Store tightly covered.
