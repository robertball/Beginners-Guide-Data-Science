Title:
Hamburger Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
2 packages of Onion soup mix (dry)
2 cans Cream of Mushroom soup
16 oz. + sour cream
1 cup water or beef broth (omit bouillon cube if using broth)
beef bouillon cube
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
3 cans sliced mushrooms, drained
1 package of wide to extra wide egg noodles

Directions:
Brown ground beef in a large skillet and drain.  Dissolve beef bouillon cube in 1 cup of boiling water (unless using beef broth).  Mix in dry soup, broth, salt and cream of mushroom soup to pan.  Simmer on medium heat for 10 minutes. Add sour cream and heat thoroughly.  Add more sour cream according to preference.
Cook egg noodles according to package directions.
serve over noodles.
