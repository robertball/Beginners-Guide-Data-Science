Title:
Pork Chops with Mushroom Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pork chops
1/4 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 onion, chopped
1/2 cup fresh sliced mushrooms
Garlic to Taste
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed golden mushroom soup
2 tablespoons Worsterchire

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
Rinse pork chops, pat dry, and season with seasoned salt and pepper.  Brown the Pork Chops, onions and mushrooms with a little garlic in a skillet until browned on each side.  Place them in a 9x13 inch baking dish. In a separate small bowl, combine the onion, mushrooms garlic Worsterchire and soup. Mix together well and spoon over the chops.
Cover, and bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 45 minutes. Uncover, and bake for 15 more minutes. (Note: Time could be less or more depending on the thickness of the chops.) Pork chops are done when their internal temperature has reached 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
