Title:
Pizza Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/334645.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1/4 cup yellow sweet onion
6 oz. tomato paste
1/2 teaspoon oregeno
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup chopped ripe olives
1 teaspoon garlic salt
Dash of pepper
1 cup Mozzarella cheese
1 loaf French bread split legnthwise

Directions:
Brown the ground beef & drain well. Mix together ground beef with onion, tomato paste, oregeno, parmesan cheese, olives, garlic salt & pepper. Spread mixture evenly on each half of the french bread and cover with Mozzarella cheese.  Bake open-faced in 450 degree oven for 10 minutes. Let cool for 5 minutes and cut in 1/3 sections. Serve with potato chips.

Submitter: 
Paul Nenni
Description: 
Growing up in an Irish-Italian family we ate these burgers on a regular basis from the 1960's on. They are easy to prepare, less messy than pizza, and delicious. I don't know where my mom got the recipe but my 5 younger brothers and 1 sister loved the burgers when we were young.
