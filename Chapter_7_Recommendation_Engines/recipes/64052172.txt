Title:
Smoky Chipotle Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (15.5 ounce) cans garbanzo beans, drained
1/4 cup tahini (sesame-seed paste)
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 canned chipotle pepper in adobo sauce
3 cloves roasted garlic
1/2 cup roasted red bell peppers, drained
6 oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained
1/2 teaspoon salt
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Place the garbanzo beans (remove hulls), tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, chipotle pepper, garlic, in the bowl of a food processor;  blend until smooth. Add the red peppers, sun-dried tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Pulse the mixture until the ingredients are coarsely chopped into the hummus base. Transfer to a serving bowl, cover, and chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Ruth Rae
Description: 
Wonderful for entertaining, this high volume hummus recipe is made with chipotle pepper, roasted red peppers, and sun dried tomatoes. The Middle-Eastern-inspired dip is a beautiful color and has a smoky, bright flavor. Serve with pita chips and fresh vegetables.Modified from: Smoky Chipotle Hummus
