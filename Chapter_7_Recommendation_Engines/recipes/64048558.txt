Title:
Purple Moose - Canadian Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1125569.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup Fresh Blueberries
2 Tbsp Peanut Butter - (Fresh ground/simple is best)
1 ripe banana
2 Tbsp Maple Syrup
1/2 cup Vanilla Rice Milk
15 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a blender. Starting at low speed, blend to med/high until all ingredients are combined. Pour into cups.

Submitter: 
The Jazz Chef
Description: 
The Purple Moose is an easy smoothie that tastes great to carnivores and vegans alike.
