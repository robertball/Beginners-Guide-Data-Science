Title:
Rhubarb Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
1C sugar
4C sliced rhubarb
1/2C water
3Tbsp cornstarch
Crust:
1C graham cracker crumbs
2Tbsp sugar
4Tbsp melted butter
Topping:
1 pkg instant vanilla pudding
1 carton cool whip
1 1/2C mini marshmallows

Directions:
Cook filling until thickened then let cool. Prepare the crust; Mix altogether and reserve 2Tbsp crumbs. Pat rest of crumbs in a 9x13in pan and bake for 5-10min at 350. Let cool. Put the cooked rhubarb mixture on top of crumb mixture. Mix 1 pkg instant vanilla pudding according to directions on box. Then  add 1-8oz carton of cool whip and 1 1/2C mini marshmallows. Mix well. Put this on top of rhubarb. Sprinkle rest of crumbs on top and refrigerate.
