Title:
LINGUINE WITH BRIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. olive oil
4 lg. tomatoes peeled and chopped
3 cloves minced garlic
1 lb. Brie cheese with rind off
1/2 c. fresh chopped basil
1 to 1 1/2 lb. linguine

Directions:
In a large bowl combine olive oil, chopped tomatoes, basil, garlic and Brie cut in small pieces. Let mixture sit for 2 hours. Cook linguine, drain, add to mixture, toss and serve.
