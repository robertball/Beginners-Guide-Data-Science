Title:
Caramel Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c cold milk
1 package SF instant butterscotch pudding
2c lowfat coolwhip
3 1/4 chopped apples, unpeeled
1/2c chopped salted peanuts

Directions:
Whisk pudding mix and milk.
Whisk in 1c cool whip
Fold in remaining cool whip and apples.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
Sprinkle with peanuts.
