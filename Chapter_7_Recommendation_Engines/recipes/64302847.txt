Title:
Spinach and Cheese Fritter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1961166.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box of jiffy cornbread mix
1/3 cup of milk
1 egg
2 cups shredded cheese
1 cup chopped spinach
1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Use one full box of Jiffy cornbread mix following the instructions on the back (1/3 cup of milk & 1 egg).
After mixing in a bowl, add 2 cups of shredded cheese, 1 cup of chopped fresh spinach, & 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder.
Mix well.
Then put on pan to medium heat & cook until edges are golden brown (like a pancake)
