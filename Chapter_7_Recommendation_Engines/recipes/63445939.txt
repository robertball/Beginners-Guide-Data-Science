Title:
Parisian Herb Baked Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 tsp mined fresh garlic
1/8 tsp minced fresh thyme leaves
1/8 tsp minced fresh rosemary leaves
1.5 tsp fresh minced parsley
1.5 tsp parmesan
3 eggs
1 tbsp heavy cream
1/2 tbsp butter
kosher salt
freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Preheat broiler on high for 5 minutes.  Place rack 6 inches below broiler
Combine herbs and cheese
Place 1 tbsp cream and 1/2 tbsp butter in dish and place under the broiler for 3 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
Pour eggs onto cream mixture.  Sprinkle with herb mixture and salt and pepper.
Broil 5 -6 minutes or until the whites are almost cooked.
Allow to rest 1-3 minutes to cool (eggs will continue to cook).
Serve with toasted bread.

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
Similar to a dish my mother enjoyed in Paris the summer of 1999.  You can make the herb mixture the night before and have it ready for guests in the morning.
