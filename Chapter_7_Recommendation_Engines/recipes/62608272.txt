Title:
Roasted Apples and Brussel Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 big bag of frozen brussel sprouts
- 2 sweet apples of your choosing
- 2 sweet onions
- extra virgin olive oil
- salt and pepper
- garlic powder
- juice of 1 lemon
- zest of 1 lemon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Empty the entire bag of frozen brussel sprouts onto a cookie sheet (preferably with lip to prevent spillage).
Chop apples and onions into "brussel sprout sized" chunks and add to the tray.
Drizzle apple, onion, sprouts with olive oil.  Add salt and pepper to taste as well as 1/4 tsp of garlic powder. Toss with your hands.
Make sure veggies are in a single layer on the cookie sheet.
Roast at 425 for @20 minutes.
Remove from oven.  Add juice and zest from 1 lemon.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Jacob French
Description: 
Apple, onion, and brussel sprouts are roasted together with a hint of lemon.  My wife and I can go through an entire tray of these by ourselves.  Yummo!
