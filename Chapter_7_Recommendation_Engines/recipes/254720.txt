Title:
Super Garlicky Paleo Tzatziki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4508854.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups plain coconut milk yogurt
1/2 cucumber
1 lemon, juiced
3 cloves garlic, or more to taste
1 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon dill

Directions:
Combine coconut milk yogurt, cucumber, lemon juice, garlic cloves, sea salt, and dill in a food processor; blend until well mixed.

Submitter: 
Natasha Farrell
Description: 
This is like garlic sauce times tzatziki sauce. To the power of garlic. I like mine super garlicky but you may adjust your garlic to taste. This tastes amazing on everything.
