Title:
Caramel glazed apple cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CAKE
1 cup packed light brown sugar
1 cup granulated sugar
1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
3 eggs
3 cups A P Flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon salt
5 granny smith or Golden delicious apples.  Cored, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
CARAMEL GLAZE
4 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup light brown sugar
pinch of salt
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
preheat oven to 325F.  Butter and flour a 9-by-13 inch pan.
To prepare the cake, beat both sugars and oil in a mixing bowl until well blended.  add eggs one at a time, beating well after each.  Sift together flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.  Gradually add to egg mixture, mixing just until blended.  Stir in apples, pecans and vanilla.  Pour in to pan.
Bake for 50-75 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.  Let cool in the pan while preparing the glaze.
To prepare the glaze, melt butter in a saucepan over low heat.  Stir in both sugar and salt, cook over medium-low heat for 2 minutes.  Add cream and boil for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
Poke holes in the cake with a toothpick or skewer.  Pour on the glaze.  Serve warm or at room temperature.  Top with ice cream or whipped cream if desired.
