Title:
Bagel Spread or Bagel Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces softened Cream Cheese
8 ounces Sour Cream
1 1.4 ounce packet Knorr Vegetable Soup mix

Directions:
Let Cream Cheese soften to room temperature for easier mixing, then add Sour Cream and Knorr Soup mix.
Whip with electric mixer until all ingredients come together, and enjoy.
For better results, refrigerate overnight.

Submitter: 
EARLMAN
Description: 
A wonderful easy spread for bagels, or thin the spread as desired with more sour cream for an interesting dip.
