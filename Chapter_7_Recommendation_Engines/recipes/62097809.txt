Title:
Double Chocolate Mocha Triffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (18.25 oz) fudge brownie mix (plus ingredients to make cake-like brownies as instructed on package)
4 tsp instant coffee granules (we've used French Vanilla flavored Folgers)
1/2 cup warm water
1 3/4 cup cold milk
2 pkgs (3.4 oz ea) white chocolate instanat pudding/pie filling (you can use vanilla, too)
2 cups whipped topping, thawed (8 oz Cool Whip or similar product works)
3 toffee bars (1.5 oz ea), coarsly chopped (Heath Bars work great)

Directions:
Prepare cake-like brownies as instructed on package.  Cool completely.
Cut brownies into 1 inch squares.
Dissolve coffee granules in water in large bowl.  Add milk and pudding mix, whisk until mixture is smooth and begins to thicken.  Gently fold in whipped topping.
Layer one third of the brownies in triffle bowl (or any serving bowl).  top with 1/3 of pudding mixture.  Sprinkle 1/3 of chopped toffee on top.  Continue layers until all ingredients are used.
Chill 1 hour, serve cold.
