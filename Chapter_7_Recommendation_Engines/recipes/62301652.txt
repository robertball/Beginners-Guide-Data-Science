Title:
Mashed Irish Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/371221.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs mashed potatoes(I use red potaotes)
1/4 cup butter
1 cup finely chopped onion
1/3 cup milk
1 1/2 cups chopped, cooked cabbage
1 Tblsp. parsley
1 cup chopped scallions

Directions:
Saute onion in butter in a large pot. Add mashed potatoes, milk, parsley, scallion, salt & pepper. Mix & heat. Add cabbage and mix & heat.

Submitter: 
Blazer
Description: 
Creamy, flavorful potatoes that go excellent with corned beef and cabbage.
