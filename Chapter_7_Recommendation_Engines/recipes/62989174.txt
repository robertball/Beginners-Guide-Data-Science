Title:
Leftover Turkey Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/742837.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped celery
1 tbsp butter
2 cups shredded or chopped leftover turkey
2 cups cooked rice (1 cup raw)
5 cups leftover gravy
32 oz. chicken broth

Directions:
Saute onion and celery in 1 tbsp melted butter.
Combine all ingredients in crockpot.
Cover and cook on low for 3 hours or until heated through.
