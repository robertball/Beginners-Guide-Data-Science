Title:
Oatmeal Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup soft butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp.vanilla
1 cup flour
1 tsp.baking soda
2 cups steel-cut oats or Scottish oats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
In medium bowl,cream butter and brown sugar.
Add vanilla and beat until light and fluffy.
In small bowl,blend flour,baking soda and oats.Add to butter mixture.
Roll into balls[about 1 in.]and flatten with a fork.
Lightly sprinkle with a little white sugar.
Bake on ungreased cookie sheets for 12 minutes.
