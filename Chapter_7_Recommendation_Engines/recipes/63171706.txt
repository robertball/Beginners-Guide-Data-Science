Title:
Pasta Quick Sauce with Ham and Artichokes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced fully-cooked ham
1 yellow onion, diced
5 cloves of garlic, peeled and minced
12 oz. package frozen artichoke hearts, thawed
29 oz. can of diced tomatoes with juice
1/2 C. sliced black olives
1/2 C. chopped fresh parsley
1 Tbl. chopped fresh basil
1 tsp. chopped fresh marjoram or oregano
1 tsp. chopped fresh thyme
1/2 tsp. hot paprika or more to taste
Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
Olive oil

Directions:
In a 5 quart pot drizzle enough olive oil to cover the bottom of the pan.  Over medium high, heat the oil and then add the ham.  Stir until the ham sizzles and then add the onions and garlic.  Sautee' until the vegetables begin to soften slightly.  Add the tomatoes, artichokes, olives and fresh herbs and seasonings.  Once the sauce simmers, reduce heat, cover and lightly simmer for 30 minutes, stirring several times.  Serve over rigatoni or other shaped pasta.  Grate fresh Parmesan over each plate.  Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
A hearty and savory sauce that is easy to prepare. A great way to use leftover holiday ham.
