Title:
Classic Southern Red Velvet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/584207.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 ½ cups self rising flour
1 cup buttermilk
1 ½ cups vegetable oil
2 eggs
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp pure vanilla extract
(2) 2 ounce bottles red food coloring
1 ½ cups sugar
1 tsp white vinegar
Frosting:
1/3 lb butter, softened
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
10 oz cream cheese
1 lb confectioners sugar
2 cups chopped pecans

Directions:
Sift and measure flour, then mix with remaining dry ingredients in a large bowl.  Thoroughly mix the eggs and all wet ingredients (brought up to room temperature) in a separate bowl, then add wet mixture to the dry mixture.  Beat well with an electric mixer.
Divide batter into three (3) greased and floured  9” pans ... bake @ 350
For frosting, thoroughly beat butter and cream cheese together with an electric mixer at high speed, then add vanilla….gradually add conf. sugar and mix until fully blended….fold in chopped pecans at the end.
