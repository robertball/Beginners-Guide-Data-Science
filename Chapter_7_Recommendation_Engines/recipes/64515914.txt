Title:
Cook'n Wishbone- Root Beer Float Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18.25 oz package of cake mix (yellow recommended)
12 fl oz bottle of root beer
1/4 cup vegetable oil
3 eggs
1/2 cup confectioner's sugar
3 TB root beer

Directions:
to make glaze-combine sugar and 3 TB root beer in a small bowl. beat until smooth
preheat oven 350F (or 175 C).
grease and flour 10" bundt pan
in a bowl, stir together cake mix, 12 oz root beer, oil, and eggs until smooth
pour batter into prepared pan
bake for 35-40 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick comes out clean.
cool the cake in the pan for 15 minutes before inverting onto wire rack to completely cool.
poke holes in the cake with clean skewer at 2" intervals.
pour root beer glaze evenly over cake.
