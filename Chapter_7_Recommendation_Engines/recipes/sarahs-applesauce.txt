Title:
Sarah's Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/25840.jpg
Ingredients:
4 apples - peeled, cored and chopped
3/4 cup water
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine apples, water, sugar, and cinnamon. Cover, and cook over medium heat for 15 to 20 minutes, or until apples are soft. Allow to cool, then mash with a fork or potato masher.

Submitter: 
PHATCAT
Description: 
This applesauce is delicious! I make it for my younger brother all the time and he loves it - and he hates canned applesauce!
