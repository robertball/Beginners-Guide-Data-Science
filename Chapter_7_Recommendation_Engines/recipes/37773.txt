Title:
Classic Baked Mostaccioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977196.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
4 cups mostaccioli, cooked, drained
1 (30 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
3/4 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Romano Cheese, divided
1 (8 ounce) package KRAFT Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
Brown meat in large skillet; drain.
Stir in mostaccioli, spaghetti sauce and 1/2 cup of the Romano cheese. Spoon into 13x9-inch baking dish. Top with mozzarella cheese and remaining 1/4 cup Romano cheese.
Bake at 375 degrees F for 20 minutes.
