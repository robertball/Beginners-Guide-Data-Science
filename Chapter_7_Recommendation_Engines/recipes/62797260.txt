Title:
5 Star Chicken Tikka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
450g/1lb boneless Chicken Thighs - skinned and cubed
10ml/2tsp Fresh Ginger - grated
1 clove Garlic - crushed or finely chopped
7½ ml/1 ½ tsp ground Chilli Powder
2½ ml/ ½ tsp ground Turmeric
2½ ml/ ½ tsp salt
150ml carton Greek Style Yoghurt
75ml/5tbsp Lemon Juice
Bunch of Fresh Coriander (Cilantro) - 2tbsp chopped and the rest torn to taste
15ml/1tbsp Sunflower Oil

Directions:
In a bowl, mix together the chicken pieces, ginger, garlic, chilli powder, turmeric and salt;
Add the yoghurt, lemon juice and 2tbsp chopped coriander;
Stir and mix well to ensure everything well coated;
Marinate in fridge for a minimum of 2 hours (overnight is better);
Remove from fridge and thread the chicken on to skewers for cooking;
Place over BBQ, turning and basting with marinade as necessary, until cooked through;
OR
Place in oiled foiled lined grill pan or flame proof dish and cook under medium grill for 15 to 20 minutes, turning and basting with oil and/or marinade as necessary, until cooked;
Serve on bed of salad leaves with rest of garnish and, if desired, a raita, popadums,chappatis, naan bread, Basmati (scented)? rice.
