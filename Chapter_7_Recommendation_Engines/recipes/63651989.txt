Title:
Apple Betty Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C. sliced apples
1 C. sugar
3/4 C. flour
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 C. butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Mound apples in a buttered 9" pie pie or a 9 x 9 square pan.  Combine the rest of the ingredients. Sprinkle over the apples.
Bake at 375 degrees for 45 min. or until done.
Serve hot with whip cream or ice cream.
