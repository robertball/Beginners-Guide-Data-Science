Title:
Old Bay® Spicy Marinated Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977507.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 tablespoons cider vinegar
2 teaspoons Old Bay® Seasoning
1 teaspoon McCormick® Parsley Flakes
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Black Pepper, Ground
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
In a self-closing plastic bag or a glass bowl, combine ingredients; mix well.
Add chicken and marinate 30 minutes.  Grill or broil 6 to 8 minutes per side, or until done.

Submitter: 
Old Bay®
Description: 
Old Bay® gives grilled chicken the flavor of the Chesapeake Bay.
