Title:
Patty's Christmas Cookie  butter Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup confectioners' sugar
2 teaspoons milk
2 teaspoons light corn syrup
1/2 teaspoon butter extract
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
assorted food coloring

Directions:
In a small bowl, stir together confectioners' sugar and milk until smooth. Beat in corn syrup and butter extract and vanilla extract until icing is smooth and glossy.  If icing is too thick, add more corn syrup.
Divide into separate bowls, and add food colorings to each to desired intensity. Dip cookies, or paint them with a brush.

Submitter: 
Nana
Description: 
This icing dries hard and shiny and the colors stay bright.Modified from: Patty's Sugar Cookie  butter Icing
