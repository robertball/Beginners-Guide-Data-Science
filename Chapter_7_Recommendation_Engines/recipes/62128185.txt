Title:
Tricia's Tuna Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. box of medium pasta shells, cooked according to package, drained and cooled
12 oz. can tuna, drained
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 green onions, chopped
1 C frozen baby peas
1 C Miracle Whip
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper

Directions:
Combine tuna, celery, green onions, peas, Miracle Whip, salt and pepper.
Add pasta and combine.
Chill several hours before serving.

A pasta salad loaded with veggies and tuna.
