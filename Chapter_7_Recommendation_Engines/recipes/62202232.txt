Title:
Leftover Turkey Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked turkey shredded
1 cup prepard dressing
1 cup green beans
2 cups gravy or to taste
2 cups mashed potatoes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat over to 375 F. In a large bowl mix dressing,turkey, green beans and gravy. Add to an  8X8 inch casserole dish spread mashed potates over the top add salt and pepper to taste. Bake uncovered for 45 minutes or until top is golden brown and everything it heated through.

Submitter: 
singlemomcooking
Description: 
This was a last ditch attempt to not waste anything this past Thanksgiving and I have to say the results where quite tasty. This recipe can be used with any leftovers you might have.
