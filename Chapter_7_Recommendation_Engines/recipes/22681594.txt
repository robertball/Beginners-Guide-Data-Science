Title:
Julie's Favorite Artechoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz. can artechoke hearts, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 c. shredded paremsan cheese
6 oz. feta cheese, crumbled
1/2 small jar capers, rinsed, drained and chopped
1 c. mayonnaise
3 Tbsp. real bacon bits
2 Tbsp. dried chives
1/2 bunch green onions, chopped
1 medium tomato, chopped
tortilla chips

Directions:
Mix all ingredients (besides grn. onions and tomatos). Put in corelle dish. Microwave 5 minutes, mix. Continue to microwave until warm. Top w/green onions and tomatoes. Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
JulezinWI
Description: 
This is a delicious warm cheesy artechoke dip. It's a favorite with my friends and family.
