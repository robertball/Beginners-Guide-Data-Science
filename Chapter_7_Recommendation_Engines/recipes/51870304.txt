Title:
My Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds ground beef
3 onions, chopped
6 cloves garlic, minced
3 tablespoons chili powder
3 teaspoons salt
5(14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes
2 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
2(15 ounce) can chili beans with liquid

Directions:
Combine ground beef, onion, and garlic in large stockpot.  Cook and stir over medium heat until beef is brown.  Drain.
Stir in chili powder, salt, oregano, tomatoes, and tomato sauce; break up tomatoes while stirring.  Heat to boiling, reduce heat to simmer, and cover.  Cook, stirring occasionally,  for 1 hour.
Stir in beans.  Simmer, uncovered, for 20 minutes; stir occasionally.

Submitter: 
Jack Pleugers
Description: 
This is the easiest chili recipe, and it is delicious and sure to please everyone. Using home canned tomatoes gives the best flavor, but is not necessary.  Enjoy.Modified from: My Chili
