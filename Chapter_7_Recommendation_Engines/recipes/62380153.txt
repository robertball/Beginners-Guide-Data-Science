Title:
Chocolate Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg chocolate cake mix
1 4 oz chocolate instant pudding
4 eggs
1 1/2 cup water
1 cup oil
1-2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°
Grease and flour bundt pan.
Combine all ingredients except chocolate chips. Mix together.
Add chips. Bake at 375. Check after 50-60 minutes.
Cool for 25 minutes in pan. Put plate on top of cake and flip over.
Then sprinkle with powdered sugar.
