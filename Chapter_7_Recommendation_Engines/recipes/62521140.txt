Title:
Roasted Feta With Tasty Toppings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Roasted red peppers, from a jar, (approx. 8, 2 inch strips)
Sundried tomatoes, packed in oil, (approx. 8, 2 inch strips)
Feta cheese, sliced (approx. 10 oz)
Fresh oregano - 1 tsp
Fresh ground pepper - 1/4 tsp
Kalamata olives, 8-10
Olive oil - 1 Tbsp

Directions:
Lay red pepper slices onto paper towel to drain off excess moisture.  Trim to about 2 inch slices.
Lay sundried tomatoes onto paper towel to drain off excess oil.  Trim to about 2 inch slices.
Place the cheese slices in a shallow baking dish and broil until the cheese is bubbling and starts to brown, about 3 minutes.  Remove from oven.
Sprinkle warm cheese with oregano and ground pepper.
Top cheese with red pepper strips and sundried tomato strips.
Toss the olives around the dish.
Drizzle with olive oil.

Submitter: 
Leanne Cooke
Description: 
A warm chunk of cheesy deliciousness that will have your guests reaching for every available pita chip or cracker!
