Title:
German Potatoe Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds of red potatoes
8 slices of thick bacon
1/2 cup sugar
3 Tbsp flour
2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1 1/2 cup water

Directions:
Peel and boil 5 pounds of red potatoes.
(Do not over-boil; keep them somewhat firm for cutting). Cook for 15 minutes and check
Let potatoes cool before cutting into slices.
(I think I usually put them in the fridge)
Brown 6 slices of bacon.
Set aside.
Saute onion (to taste) in bacon drippings.
Set aside.
Reserve drippings (approx. 1/2 cup)
(I use more bacon then recommended and less drippings).
Mix together:
1/2 cup sugar
3 Tbsp flour
2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
Then whisk into bacon drippings to make a paste
Mix together:
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1 1/2 cup water
Add to bacon drippings paste.
Cook for 2-3 min until it thickens
Coat potato slices with bacon drippings paste.
Stir in bacon and onions.
Let stand at least 3-4 hours before serving.
