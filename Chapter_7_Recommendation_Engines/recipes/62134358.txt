Title:
Gigi's Cinnamon Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Duncan Heines Butter Recipe Golden Cake Mix
8oz. Sour Cream
4 Eggs
1/2 cup sugar
3/4 cup oil
1 Tablespoon Sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 370 degrees.
In small bowl, mix the 1 Tablespoon sugar and cinnamon. Set aside.
In large bowl, beat eggs well.  Add the remaining ingredients and beat on medium speed until smooth (about 4 minutes)
Pour 1/3 of batter into greased bundt pan. Sprinkle with 1/2 of sugar mixture.  Add another 1/3 of batter followed by remaining sugar mixture. Pour in the last 1/3 of batter.
Bake 50 minutes or until a toothpick entered into the middle of the cake comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Roni J
Description: 
My grandma always makes this cake when we go fishing or camping. It can be made a day or two ahead of time and stored in a container until ready to serve. It is always a hit and can be served for brunch or as a dessert!
