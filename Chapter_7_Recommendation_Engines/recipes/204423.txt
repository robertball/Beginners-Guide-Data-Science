Title:
Caribbean Health Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/664095.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped carrot
1 banana
1 kiwi, peeled
1 apple - peeled, cored, and sliced
1 cup chopped pineapple
1 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Blend the carrot, banana, kiwi, apple, pineapple, and ice cubes in a blender until smooth.
