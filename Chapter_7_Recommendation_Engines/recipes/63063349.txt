Title:
Peas and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 tablespoon olive oil
1 large onion, diced
1/2 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
salt and pepper to taste
dash of cayenne
1 (15 ounce) can pigeon peas, with liquid
1 2/3 cups water
1 1/2 cups uncooked long-grain white rice
1 sprig dried thyme, chopped

Directions:
Heat oil in a large, heavy saucepan over medium high heat. Stir in onion and cook until tender. Mix in  tomato paste. Season with cayenne, salt and pepper. Reduce heat to low, and continue cooking about 15 minutes.
Stir pigeon peas and with liquid, water, rice, and thyme into the saucepan. Bring to a boil, cover, and reduce heat. Cook 40 minutes on low, or until all liquid is absorbed. Fluff rice with a fork.
