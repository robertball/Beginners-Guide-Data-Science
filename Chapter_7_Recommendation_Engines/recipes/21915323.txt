Title:
Pork, Rice & Bean Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded pork
1 cup cooked brown rice
1 can black beans
1 can diced tomatoes
1 small can tomato sauce
fajita seasoning
cheddar cheese
tortillas

Directions:
Combine rice, beans, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce and fajita seasoning in bowl and microwave for 8 minutes.
Fill tortilla shells with pork and rice mixture. Add cheese inside and on top.
