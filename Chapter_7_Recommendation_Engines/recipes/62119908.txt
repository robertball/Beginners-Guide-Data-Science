Title:
Cherry Triple Chunk Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 cups quick-cooking oats
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
2 eggs
3/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 tsp almond extract
1 cup maraschino cherries, drained & chopped
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chunks
1/2 cup chopped pecan pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.  Combine oats, flour, baking
powder & salt and set aside.  In a large mixing bowl
beat margarine, almond extract & sugar until fluffy, then
add eggs one at a time, beat until well blended.
Gradually add flour mixture and mix well.  Remove from
mixer and stir in chopped cherries, chocolate chunks
and pecan pieces by hand.
Spread into sprayed 13x9 pan.
Bake 27-30 minutes, or until center is set.  Cool completely before cutting; makes 32 bars

Submitter: 
Marsha Leigh Bogardus
Description: 
Combining 2 of my favorites, cherries & chocolate, you can't go wrong!  These are a new favorite of the whole family and are gorgeous on a plate during the holidays!
