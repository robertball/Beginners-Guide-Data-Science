Title:
Anne's Curried Halibut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb halibut or any white fish filets boned and skinned
1 C Mayonaise
1/2 C Cream
1/2 C Sour Cream
1/4 C Dry Sherry
2 T Lemon Juice
2 t. Curry Powder
1 C Thinly Sliced Onions
Green Onion Stems - sliced at an angle

Directions:
Wash and pat dry fish
Cut into serving size portions
Butter baking dish.
Add onions
Lay fish on top of onions
Combine rest of ingredients except green onions in a bowl
Pour mixture over fish
Bake 350 for 30 minutes
Sprinkle green onion over fish
Serve with rice

Submitter: 
jalexandropoulos
Description: 
Halibut topped with a creamy curry sauce and onions.  The delicious sauce keeps the halibut moist.
