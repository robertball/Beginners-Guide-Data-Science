Title:
Groovy Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451121.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana, frozen and cut in chunks
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
1 1/2 cups fresh spinach leaves
1 3.17 oz. apple carrot fruit sauce (Trader Joe's)
1 tbsp. almond butter (optional)

Directions:
Place everything into a blender. Cover, and blend until smooth, stopping frequently to push down anything stuck to the sides. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
My daughter begged for these apple carrot fruit sauce tubes she spotted at Trader Joe's...and naturally, refused to eat them when we got home. However, they make a great addition to smoothies!Modified from: Groovy Green Smoothie
