Title:
Martha's Glazed Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 pounds (or so) Martha's Ground-Mix (see
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
3 tablespoons ketchup
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425-degrees with rack in center position.
Gently mix together meat and mustard. Form mixture into 4-by8-inch oval (about 1-1/2 inches thick) on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
Combine ketchup and Worcestershire sauce in a small bowl; brush over surface of meat loaf to coat. Bake, rotating halfway through, until just cooked through and an instant-read thermometer inserted into center reaches 160-degrees, 40 to 45 minutes. Let rest 5 minutes before slicing.
