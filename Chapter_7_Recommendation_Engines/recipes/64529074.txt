Title:
Classic Tiramisu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs - separated
3/4 cup white sugar
16 oz. cream cheese (Marscapone is better)
1/2 cup coffee (expresso is better)
1/4 cup Kahlua
1 package Lady Fingers or vanilla wafers
2 - 1 oz squares semi-sweet chocolate - finally grated

Directions:
Beat yolks in medium size bowl
Add Sugar gradually until mixed well (No gritty feeling left)
Add cream cheese and mix well
Beat egg whites in a separate metal bowl until soft peaks are formed
Fold egg whites into cheese mixture
Spread 1/4 of cheese mixture into 12 cup Large bowl
Dip Lady Fingers in the mixture of coffee and Kahlua and cover the cheese mixture
Cover Lady Fingers with finally grated chocolate
Repeat 3 times using chocolate on each layer ending with cheese and chocolate on top
Refrigerate 6 hours before serving
Serve chilled
