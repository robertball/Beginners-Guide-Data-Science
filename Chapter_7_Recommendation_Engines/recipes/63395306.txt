Title:
Lemon Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 Tilapia filets, margarine, lemon juice, salt, lemon salt, pepper, ground basil

Directions:
melt margarine, add lemon juice. Place tilapia in skillet add other seasoning. Cook 5 - 7 minutes, turning filets a couple of times. Spoon sauce in skillet over fish.
