Title:
Crispy Cheddar Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large chicken boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 sleeves Ritz crackers
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 cup milk
3   cup cheddar cheese, grated
1   teaspoon dried parsley
1   14 ounce can cream of chicken soup
2   tablespoons sour cream
2   tablespoons butter

Directions:
Cut each chicken breast into 3 large chunks.
In a small food processor, grind up the crackers.
Pour the milk, cheese and cracker crumbs into 3 separate small pans.  Toss the seasonings into the cracker crumbs and stir the mixture around to combine.
Dip each piece of chicken into the milk, then the cheese, and finally the cracker crumbs.
Spray a 9x13 pan with cooking spray and lay the chicken inside the pan.
Sprinkle the dried parsley over the chicken.
Cover the pan with tin foil and bake at 400 degreese for 35 minutes.  Remove the tin foil, bake for an additional 10 minutes or until the edges of the chicken and golden brown and crispy.
Into a medium sized sauce pan combine the cream fo chicken soup, sour cream and butter with a whisk.  Stir it over medium high heat until the sauce is nice and hot.  Serve over the chicken.
