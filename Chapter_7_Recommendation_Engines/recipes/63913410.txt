Title:
new years waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 1/2 c milk
1 t vanilla
1 T baking powder
4 T buttermilk powder
1 1/2 c flour
1/4 c sugar
1/2 t salt
3 T melted butter

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients together, mix all wet ingredients together
Combine and immediately cook until brown in waffle iron.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
I came up with this for Chris on New Years day-we all liked them. First homemade waffle I ever liked
