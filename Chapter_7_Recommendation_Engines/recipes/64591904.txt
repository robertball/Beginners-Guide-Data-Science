Title:
Gingerbread Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1 tbsp. baking powder
3/4 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
2 tsp. ground ginger
2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. ground cloves
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
3 eggs
1/3 cup molasses
2 1/4 - 3 cups buttermilk (use less for thicker pancakes)

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients well.
Add beaten eggs, molasses and buttermilk (use less buttermilk for thicker pancakes)
Stir just until combined.
Cook on a hot griddle

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Pancakes sweetened with molasses and flavored with gingerbread spices
