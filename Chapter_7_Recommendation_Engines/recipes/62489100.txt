Title:
Pumpkin Pecan Biscotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cup flour
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
pinch salt
1 tsp cinammon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp ginger
1/4 cup finely chopped pecans
2 eggs
1/2 cup pumpkin puree
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Stir together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and spices. Combine well.
In a small bowl, mix the eggs, pumpkin, pecans, and vanilla.
Combine the egg and flour mixtures together. Mixture will be very thick. Add more flour if needed.
Divide dough into 2 portions. Roll both portions into logs about 10 inches long and 2 inches wide. Place on parchment lined cookie sheets.
Bake for 28 minutes. Remove from oven and let cool on tray for 5 minutes. Remove one loaf and slice into 1/2 inch thick slices. Place cut slices back on parchment lines cookie sheet. Cut other loaf.
Return to oven for 8 minutes. Turn pieces over and bake another 8 minutes. Remove from oven.
