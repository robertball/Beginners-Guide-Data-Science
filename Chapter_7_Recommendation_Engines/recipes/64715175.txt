Title:
Shanda's Wild Edibles and Basil Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5725434.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Basil
1  1/2 Cups Mixture of Dandelion, Cats Ear and Wood Sorrel
1 1/2 Cups Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 Cup Lemon Juice
3/4 Cup Pine Nuts
3 Tablespoons Minced Garlic
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Chili Powder
1 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Place the greens in a blender. Pour in about 1 tablespoon of the oil and lemon juice, then blend greens into a paste. Gradually add pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, garlic, chili powder, salt and remaining oil. Continue to blend until smooth.
Add extra lemon juice if it is too thick.
