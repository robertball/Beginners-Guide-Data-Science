Title:
Lemon-Coconut Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976364.jpg
Ingredients:
35 NILLA Wafers, finely crushed
1 cup sugar, divided
1/4 cup butter or margarine melted
1 teaspoon grated lemon peel
2 eggs
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 tablespoons flour
1/2 teaspoon CALUMET Baking Powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup BAKER'S ANGEL FLAKE Coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Mix wafer crumbs, 1/4 cup of the sugar, the butter and lemon peel until well blended. Press firmly into 8-inch square baking pan. Bake 8 min.
Beat eggs and remaining 3/4 cup sugar in small bowl with wire whisk until thickened and well blended. Add lemon juice, flour, baking powder and salt; mix well. Pour over crust; sprinkle with coconut.
Bake 25 to 30 min. or until center is set and top is lightly browned. Cool completely. Cover and refrigerate several hours or until chilled before cutting to serve. Store in tightly covered container in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
BAKER'S Chocolate
Description: 
A perfect balance of tart and sweet with a bit of crunch, these lemon-coconut bars make a great dessert or picnic treat.
