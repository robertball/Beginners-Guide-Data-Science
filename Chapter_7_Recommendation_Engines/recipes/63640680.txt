Title:
Hot Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup coconut milk
2 tablespoons soy sauce
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon curry powder (optional)
3 teaspoons lemon juice

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and heat on low until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Donna M. MacLean
Description: 
A quick and easy peanut sauce you can serve warm or cold.  Delicious over chicken stir fry with either rice or noodles.Modified from: Hot Peanut Sauce
