Title:
Chicken Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796565.jpg
Ingredients:
8 each chicken breasts
4 each bell pepper, sliced
2 each onion, sliced
1/2 cup olive oil
1 package sour cream
2 cups cheddar  cheese, shredded
2 teaspoons Lemon Pepper
2 teaspoons savor salt
1 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Skin chicken breasts and slice thinly.Season meat with meat tenderizer, lemon pepper, salt. And grill in a large skillet with a little oil.
Sprinkle flour tortillas with a little water and heat for 1 minute.
Heat Olive Oil and add butter to taste in large skillet and add onion and bell pepper. Season vegetables with savor salt and lemon pepper. Cook until tender. Add chicken and season with savor salt. Add chicken and enjoy a delcious dinner. Ole'
