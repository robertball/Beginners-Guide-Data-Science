Title:
Chicken Cacciatore for the Instant Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 skinless, bone in chicken thighs. Surface fat removed.
1/4 cup ap flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 medium onion, finely chopped
2 cloves of garlic, micro planed or finely chopped
30 oz tomato sauce
1 bay leaf
1 tsp each oregano, basil and parsley
A generous pinch of red pepper flakes, more if you like more heat.
2 tbs olive oil, or more as needed
1/4 cup of dry red wine

Directions:
Mix the salt, pepper and flour in a shallow bowl.
Preheat the Instant Pot's saute function, normal.
Place 1 tbs of oil in the pot, enough to cover the bottom of the pot.
Dredge 4 chicken pieces in the flour and shake off any excess.
Saute until golden brown, 4 or 5 minutes per side. Move the chicken to a plate.
Repeat with the remaining chicken pieces.
Add 1 tbs of oil to the pot and add the onion, stirring until translucent, about 4-5 minutes.
Add the garlic and cook until just fragrant, about 1 minute.
Deglaze the pan with the wine, scraping up any bits from the bottom.
Continue cooking until the wine has almost evaporated.
Add the tomato sauce and stir in the herbs red pepper flakes and bay leaf.
Add the chicken to the pot and turn to coat.
Put the lid on the pot and seal, vent closed.
Push the chicken feature and adjust the time for 10 minutes.
It will take 15-20 minutes for the pressure to build.
Use the quick release method.
Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
Chicken thighs cooked in a mixture of tomato sauce, herbs and a generous splash of red wine.
