Title:
Twice Baked Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 small sweet Potatoes
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp Coriander
1/2 tsp Cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/4 cup maple syrup
2/3 cup dried cranberries
18 pecan halves

Directions:
preheat oven to 450
wash, pierce with knife, and wrap sweet potatoes in foil
bake for 1 hr.
unwrap potatoes and let cool to touch
cut off top of potatoes, scoop out the insides, and add to mixing bowl
add the spices to the mixing bowl and mix well, stir in syrup
put mixture back in skins and top the cranberries and pecan halves
toast in a 350 deg oven for 15 min
