Title:
Lori's Quick and Easy No Bean Chili Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1982115.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (15 ounce) cans chili without beans
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened

Directions:
Mix chili and cream cheese together in a slow cooker set to High, stirring often, until cream cheese is completely melted and dip is hot, about 10 minutes. Reduce heat to Low and serve.
