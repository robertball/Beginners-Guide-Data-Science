Title:
Healing Cabbage Soup 3 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 onion, chopped
1 cloves garlic, chopped
2 cups water
2 cups chicken stock
1/2 teaspoon sea salt, or to taste
1/4 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste
1/4 head cabbage, cored and coarsely chopped
1 (8 ounce) can Italian-style stewed tomatoes, drained and diced
1/2 cup carrots sliced
1/2 cup kale chopped

Directions:
Combine all ingredients; cover and cook on low for 8 - 10 hours (HIGH for 4 - 5 hours). Makes about 6 to 8 servings

Submitter: 
Nick
Description: 
Comfort food on a cold winter's night, cabbage simmered in chicken broth is also an age-old folk remedy for curing the common cold.Modified from: Healing Cabbage Soup
