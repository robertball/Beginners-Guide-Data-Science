Title:
Chicken Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. pesto sauce mix or jar of ready made pesto
8 boneless skinless chicken breasts
breadcumbs or panko
4 slices provolone cheese, cut in half
fresh mushrooms, sauted in a little olive oil, r canned

Directions:
Prepare pesto sauce mix accoring to package instructions
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Spray rectangular dish (9" x 12") w/non-stick spray
Moisten chicken w/water, shake off excess (or coat with margarine or cooking spray), and coat w/ breadcrumbs
Place chicken in baking dish
Cover each chicken breast with 1/2 slice of cheese
Bake 40 min. Cover w/foil for first 20 min.
Serve w/ pasta

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Frozen boneless skinless chicken breasts can be used. Prepared pesto or package pesto sauce mix, mushrooms and provolone cheese makes this yummy.
