Title:
My Favorite Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1485361.jpg
Ingredients:
1 and 1/2 cups Flour
3 Tablespoons Sugar
2 and 1/2 teaspoons Baking Powder
1/2 teas Baking Soda
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1 teaspoon Orange Zest (or just the zest from 1 orange)
5 tablespoons cold unsalted Butter
1/2 cup Dried Cranberries
2/3 cup low fat Buttermilk plus 2-3 tables more if needed
Topping: 1 Cup Confectionery Sugar
1 Tablespoon orange juice or 1 teaspoon orange flavoring.
1 to 2 Tablespoons milk

Directions:
Preheat oven 400 degress. Line baking sheet with parchment. In large bowl stir together first 6 ingredients. Using a pastry blender or two knives blend butter into the dry mixture until it resembles a course meal. Then add the Cranberries.
Make a well in the center add the buttermilk and stir until combined (Do Not overmix) use a little more buttermilk if you think its too dry.
Using a big spoon scoop and drop 6 or 8 evenly sized pieces onto your parchment paper. (Depending if you want them larger or not.)
Bake for 18 to 20 minutes.
Once completely cooled mix some confectionery sugar (1 cup) with some orange juice (1 Tablespoon) or orange flavoring (1 teaspoon) and as much milk (probably about 1-2 tablespoons) you need to make it a frosting type consistency and drizzle over the scones.
