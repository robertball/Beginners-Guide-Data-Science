Title:
Roasted eggplant with ricotta and pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant
olive oil to coat
salt
premade pesto sauce
part skim ricotta
1 pint of cherry tomatoes
salt and pepper to taste
1 lemon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Peel eggplant and then cube.
Salt eggplant and put in a strainer for an hour.
Squeeze eggplant and pat with paper towel.
Toss the eggplant and whole cherry tomatoes in olive oil and juice from 1/2 of a lemon and place on a grease baking sheet.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Bake in oven @ 40 minutes or until the eggplant is tender.
Transfer to a cassarole dish and add about 6 tbsp of premade pesto sauce and toss.
Add about 6 tablespoons of ricotta in little droplets and broil 5 minutes
Once done drizzle the other half of lemon on top.

Submitter: 
Toni Losada
Description: 
This eggplant has pesto, ricotta, tomatoes and lemon! Very delicious as a side dish or a main vegetarian dish...yum!
