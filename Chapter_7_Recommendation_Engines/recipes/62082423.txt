Title:
Peanut Butter Caramel Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 - 6 1/2 c. corn chex
2 1/2 - 3 cups wheat chex
4 cups chocolate peanut butter cereal (such as Reese's)
1 1/2 cups mixed nuts
1 1/2 c. window pane shaped pretzels, coarsely broken
1 cup packed light brown sugar
1 stick butter
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 cups color coated chocolate candy(plain or peanut)
1 cup raisins
1 cup peanut butter chips

Directions:
-Preheat oven to 250.  Grease large roasting pan.  Mix first 5 ingredients and pour in pan.
-In heavy saucepan combine brown sugar, butter, corn syrup.  Bring to a boil over med. heat, stir constantly.  Let boil without stirring for 5 minutes.
-Remove from heat add in vanilla and baking soda.  Pour over cereal mix and stir to coat.
-Bake 45 min, stir every 15 min.
-Cool in pan, stir often.  Stir in raisins and peanut butter chips while still slightly warm.  Cool completely, stir in chocolate candy and store in airtight container for up to 2 weeks (if it lasts that long).

Submitter:

Description: 
This mix has the flavor of caramel popcorn, it's extremely addictive.
