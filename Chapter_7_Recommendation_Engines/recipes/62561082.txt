Title:
Smoky Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon, diced
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
3 carrots, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
1/2 jalapeno
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
2 bay leaves
4 cups vegetable stock
2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
1 teaspoon smoked salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In large saucepan, saute the diced bacon over medium heat until beginning to brown; cook onion, garlic, carrots, celery and jalapeno with the bacon  stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes or until onion is softened. Add chili powder and cumin and bay leaves; cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Add stock,  and 1 the beans; bring to boil.  Continue to simmer for one hour.
Meanwhile, in food processor or blender, puree together tomatoes and remaining can of beans; add to pot. Reduce heat and simmer for one hour.
