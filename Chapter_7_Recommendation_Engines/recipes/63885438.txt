Title:
Citrus Asada Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1   pound asada/skirt steak
2   oranges (juiced) about one cup of juice
1   tablespoon cumin
1   chipotle pepper canned (minced) or  1 - 2 teaspoons powdered
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1  tablespoon Asada, Fajita, or taco seasoning

Directions:
Grill Directions:
Keep pieces as large as possible, Mix all ingredients in a bowl and refrigerate 24 hours. Thoroughly mix the meat a couple of times while in the bowl. Grill on high heat.
Do not over cook, medium rare! Let sit 10 min & slice to size.
Stove top:
Cut meat into bite size pieces, mix all ingredients in a bowl and refrigerate 24 hours. Thoroughly mix the meat a couple of times while in the bowl. Over medium-high heat add about 2 tablespoons of butter into fry pan then add meat.  Do not over cook, medium rare!
