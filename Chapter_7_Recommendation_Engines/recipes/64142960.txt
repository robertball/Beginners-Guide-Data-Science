Title:
Tomato Basil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbls Unsalted Butter (or 2 Tbl Canola oil)
2 C Chopped Onion
1 Carrot- Chopped
8 Fresh Basil Leaves- Slivered (2-4 Tsp dried basil to taste)
4 C Chicken Broth
Salt/Pepper to taste
1 c Cream (use reduced fat or FF) add enough for desired consistancy
2 lg cans- Plum Tomatoes (Muir Glen Organic)
6 Cloves Garlic- Chopped
Grated Parmesean

Directions:
1. Saute onion and carrot at low-med until soft (about 10 min)
2. Add Garlic for the last minute
3. Add smashed tomatoes- cook 10 min
4. Add broth and basil- cook 50 min
5. Puree and add cream (about 3/4 c) to desired consistancy and taste
Serve with grated parmesean

Submitter: 
Cynthia Burks Anderson
Description:
