Title:
Pickled Beets and Hard-Cooked Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium beets
1 medium white onion, peeled and sliced
1 T black peppercorns
1/4 t whole cloves
2 T horseradish, fresh, peeled and grated
3 c cider vinegar
3 c water
4 T sugar
6 hard-boiled eggs, peeled

Directions:
Trim all but 2" of stalks off the beets. Wash beets, put into medium pot, cover with water, boil until soft, 45-60 min.
Drain, pull off stalks, slip off skins, slice beets, and put into large glass jar.
Add onions, peppercorns, cloves, and horseradish.
Bring cider vinegar, 3 c water, and sugar to boil. Pour into jar. Set aside to cool, cover and refrigerate until chilled. Store in refrigerator up to one month.
Add eggs to jar at least 4 hours and up to 5 days before serving.
