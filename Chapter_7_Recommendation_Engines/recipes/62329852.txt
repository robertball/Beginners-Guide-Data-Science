Title:
The BEST EVER Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of Gemeli pasta
1 8oz block of sharp cheddar (cut into small cubes)
1 cucumber seeded and small dice
1/2 red onion (if large) finley diced
2 or 3 ribs celery diced
1 cooked ham steak diced or 1/2 lb cooked bacon chopped
1 jalapeno pepper deseeded and finley diced
1/2 cup mayo ( do not use miracle whip )
1/4 cup honey mustard
salt pepper to taste
1 Tbsp spoon onion powder
1/2 Tbsp garlic powder
parsley for color

Directions:
Cook pasta al dente acording to directions on box, meanwhile mix together mayo, honey mustard, garlic and onion powder, parsley ,salt and pepper stir until combined.* If it has a mayo taste then add a little more honey mustard. set aside. In a large bowl add the cooled pasta, meat, cheese and diced veggies, add the dressing and mix to combine.

Submitter: 
Verity Tripke
Description: 
This is by far the best Pasta Salad you will ever eat!!! What kind of veggies and meat you add is your choice but this is how I do it!! Enjoy
