Title:
Buttercream frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (room temperature)
1/2 cup Crisco shortening (room temperature)
1 cup sugar
1/8 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup warm milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
food coloring (optional)

Directions:
Cream butter and shortening together until blended.
Beat in sugar, 1 tablespoon at a time, and salt. Add milk one tablespoon at a time and add vanilla extract. Mix all ingredients until completely blended.
Add a couple drops of food coloring until it reaches the desired color. Spread onto cakes, cupcakes, brownies, etc.

Submitter: 
Erin
Description: 
This is my mom's recipe and it always comes through for me if I need a quick frosting for a cake or other treat.
