Title:
Indian Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon red chili powder
1/8 teaspoon garam masala
2 teaspoons sunflower seed oil
1 onion, sliced
1 tomato, chopped
1/2 cup fresh spinach
1/4 cup water
2 eggs, well beaten

Directions:
Mix salt, garlic powder, black pepper, chili powder, and garam masala together in a bowl. Set seasoning mix aside.
Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onion. Cook, stirring occasionally, until starting to brown, about 5 minutes. Add tomato and cook until mushy, about 3 minutes more. Add spinach and water and cook for another 2 to 3 minutes.
Add eggs to the pan and let set for about 3 minutes. Evenly sprinkle the seasoning mix on the surface of the eggs, then stir to scramble. Cook over low heat until eggs are done to desired level, 2 to 5 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Neha
Description: 
This Indian-flavored version of scrambled eggs isn't leaking with dairy and fat. Some simple vegetables and seasonings make your morning scrambled eggs a sure delight! Serve with toast or chapati bread.
