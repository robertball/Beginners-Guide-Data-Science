Title:
Roasted Potato Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound russet or yukon gold potatoes, medium dice
1 medium white onion, medium dice
1/4 c EVOO
1 red bell pepper, medium dice
2T chives, finely chopped

Directions:
On a rimmed baking sheet toss together the potatoes, onion and EVOO.
Season with coarse salt and ground pepper.
Arrange vegetables i na sale layer and roast until onion begins to soften, about 10 minutes.
Add bell pepper to sheet; toss.
Roast until potatoes are tender and pepper is soft, about 13 minutes.
Sprinkle with chives.
