Title:
Cheesy Chili Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 dozen sourdough French rolls
1 pound Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
8 ounces tomato sauce
4 ounces green chiles, diced
6 green onions, finely chopped
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 ounces Parmesan chese, grated
Minced green onion for garnish

Directions:
1.  Split rolls in half lengthwise.  Scoop out soft bread leaving about 1/2 inch crust as the shell.
2.  Combine Jack cheese, tomato sauce, chiles, chopped green onions, oil, and garlic in a large bowl.
3.  Spoon equal amounts of cheese mixture onto bread shells, sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Arrange on baking sheet.  Bake at 425 degrees for 10 minutes.  Garnish with green onions and serve hot.

Submitter: 
kirklandcook
Description: 
This recipe was served to me as a luncheon entree along with a side salad---absolutely delicious!  Served in that way, it could serve 8-10 people.  I think this recipe would make a great appetizer as well.
