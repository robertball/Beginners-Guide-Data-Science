Title:
Kyle's PB&J Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C Sugar
1 C Peanut Butter
1 Tbsp Butter
1 tsp Vanilla
1/2 tsp Cinnamon
1/2 tsp Ginger
1 Egg
Jelly

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well. Roll into walnut size balls and place on greased cookie sheet. Make an indent in each cookie and fill with favorite jelly. Bake at 350 degrees until lightly browned, 10-15 minutes.
