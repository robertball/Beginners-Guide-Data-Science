Title:
Homemade Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack of chicken
2 cans of chicken broth
1 stalk of celery
2 carrots
2 bullion cubes
dash of poultry seasoning
dash of cajon seasoning
1/2  large onion
1/2 pack of egg noodles
tablespoon butter
Pressure cooker

Directions:
Brown chicken in butter and onions untill browned , stirring  occasionally . Add in chicken, veggies,  can of chicken broth and seasonings.  Put lid on pressure cooker and cook on medium heat until the "diddle" sound. Reduce heat slightly and cook for 15 mins . Cool lid of until pressure is out of the cooker then add your egg noodles and cook those without lid for 10 mins. Serve and enjoy.
