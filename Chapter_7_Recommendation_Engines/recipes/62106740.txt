Title:
Jim's Simple Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz can refried beans
8 oz can refried beans
16 oz jar of salsa
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1 cup grated monterey jack cheese

Directions:
Microwave beans in casserole dish for three minutes.
Stir well.
Continue to cook in microwave for an additional three minutes.
Stir in salsa.
Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
Remove and spread cheese on top.

Submitter: 
hils
Description: 
A very simple, quick and yummy bean dip. The spicyness of it can vary depending on what type of beans and salsa you use.
