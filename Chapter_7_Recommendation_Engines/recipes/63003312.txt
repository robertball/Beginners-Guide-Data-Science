Title:
Healthy Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749304.jpg
Ingredients:
4 hard boiled eggs
1/2 avocado
1 tbsp chopped italian parsley (fresh)
1/2 tbsp yellow mustard
1 tbsp plain greek yogurt
1 tbsp + 1 tsp pickle relish
1 1/2 tbsp chopped onion
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil eggs in hot water for 10-15 minutes. Rinse in cold water and peel shells.
Mash eggs in a medium bowl with a fork (or a fancy mashing tool if you have one). Dice avocado, onion and parsley and mix until combined. Stir in wet ingredients. Season with salt and pepper as desired. Serve on toast or with crackers.

Submitter: 
foolwithflour
Description: 
Egg salad made with greek yogurt instead of mayo to cut calories without cutting flavor!
