Title:
Hash Brown Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
3 cups hash browns
1/2 cup chopped green peppers
1/2 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup cheddar cheese
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown ground beef in large skillet; drain and remove from pan. Brown hash browns on medium-high heat until browned on both sides. Sprinkle in salt & pepper. Add beef back to pan and stir in green peppers and onions. Add more salt & pepper to taste. When heated through, top with cheese until melted. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
MRSMTAYLOR
Description: 
A quick, hearty dinner that even my husband can make! Simple seasonings (just salt & pepper) and simple ingredients make it a snap to make.
