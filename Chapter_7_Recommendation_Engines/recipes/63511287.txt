Title:
Hot and Sour Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans Vegetable broth (14 oz each)
1 can sliced mushrooms or 4 oz. of fresh assorted mushrooms
2 T Vinegar
1/2 t hot pepper sauce (tabasco)* to taste for spice
2 T cornstarch
2 T soy sauce
1 egg, beaten
2 green onions, chopped
If desired, canned shredded chicken

Directions:
Bring to a boil the vegetable broth, mushrooms, vinegar, hot sauce and green onions.
Mix cornstarch and soy sauce, then add to broth.  Stir until thick over medium heat.  If desired, add chicken to add thickness or heartiness to the broth.
Just prior to serving, add one egg, beaten to the soup and serve immediately.  (You can eliminate the egg if a thinner soup is desired)

Submitter: 
Adina Ryan
Description: 
A quick, warm, sweet and spicy soup for a cold day.  Enjoy as an prelude to any Chinese or Asian dish you are preparing.
