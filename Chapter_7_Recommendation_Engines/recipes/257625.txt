Title:
Keto Strawberry Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5670719.jpg
Ingredients:
5 strawberries, hulled
2 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
2/3 cup low-calorie natural sweetener (such as SwerveÂ®)
1/2 cup water
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon vodka
1 pinch salt

Directions:
Place strawberries in a food processor or blender; puree until smooth.
Whisk strawberry puree, cream, sweetener, water, vanilla extract, lemon juice, vodka, and salt together in a bowl; pour into an ice cream maker.
Process ice cream according to manufacturer's instructions. Transfer to a freezer-safe container and freeze until firm.

Submitter: 
FoodUnl.com
Description: 
Keto-diet-friendly strawberry ice cream. I experimented with lots of sweeteners, combos of cream and other liquids, and finally arrived at this very simple combo that tastes like the real thing, if not better.
