Title:
Texas Chili with Meat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean hamburger meat
1 large onion
1 medium bell pepper
2 cloves garlic
1-16 oz. can minced tomatoes
2-16 oz. cans dark red Kidney Beans
1-8 oz. can tomato sauce
2 1/2 tsp. chili powder
1/2 tsp. dried basil
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Brown meat, onion, bell pepper, and garlic till done.
Add spices to release flavors, tomatoes, tomato sauce, and beans.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
Serve over rice.
