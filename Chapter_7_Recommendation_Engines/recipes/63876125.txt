Title:
Peppermint Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075248.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tbsp. Unsweetened cocoa
1 1/2 tbsp. Sugar
1 cup Milk
Peppermint Ice Cream

Directions:
1. In a sauce pan, heat your milk.  Stir in cocoa and sugar. Frequently stir until boiling.
2. Add Peppermint Ice Cream on top.

Submitter: 
Shelly Culp
Description: 
I love peppermint especially in this winter time.  Add this to hot chocolate for a wonderful holiday drink!
