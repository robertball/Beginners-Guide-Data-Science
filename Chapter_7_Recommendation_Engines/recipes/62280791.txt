Title:
Cindy's Stk & Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Green bell pepper, sliced
1 Red bell pepper, sliced
1 Banana pepper, sliced (optional)
1 lrg onion, thinly sliced
2 lbs chopped stk
1 loaf Italian bread, cubed
2 cups shredded lettuce
1 cup Provolone cheese, shredded
1 cup Monterrey jack, shredded
1 tsp ground pepper
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp garlic powder
oil & vinegar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F. Cook peppers & onion first, saute them on stove medium-high heat. Add salt/pepper/garlic pepper. Cook until tender, set aside when done. Cook stk in 1 tbsp oil, on medium heat. Salt/pepper to taste. 5 min cook time or until no longer pink. Remove from heat immediately. Arrange cubed bread on bottom of casserole dish, add 1 cup Monterrey cheese. Than add the pepper/onion/stk mixture on top. Add provolone to top it off. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until cheese melts.
