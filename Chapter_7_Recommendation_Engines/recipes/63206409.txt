Title:
Chicken Korma Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/830029.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 small onion, diced
1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 boneless chicken breasts, chopped into bite-size pieces
1 fresh jalapeno pepper, seeded and sliced
3 tablespoons ground unsalted cashews
1 (4 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 teaspoons salt
1 1/2 tablespoons curry powder
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
1 cup heavy cream
1 bunch fresh cilantro for garnish

Directions:
Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the onion, and cook until tender. Mix in ginger and garlic, and continue cooking 1 minute. Mix chopped chicken, jalapeno, cashews, and tomato sauce. Season with salt and curry powder. Cook and stir 10 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.
Stir red bell pepper, and cream into the skillet. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 10 minutes or until chicken is done. Garnish with cilantro to serve.
