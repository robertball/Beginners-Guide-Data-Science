Title:
Marcia"s red onion salad Recipe

Ingredients:
1 red onion
can of chickpeas drained
can of white beans drained
Lemon juice 1/2 cup
Olive oil 1/2 cup
tomatoes seeded
parsley
cilantro
crushed garlic

Directions:
Mix oil and lemon juice.  Mix all ingredients and toss with oil and lemon juice.
Put in refridgerator to chill.
