Title:
Delicious Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 medium head of cabbage, finely chopped
1/3 cup white vinegar
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 tablespoons granulated sugar
1 teaspoon very finely diced sweet onion
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/4 teaspoon finely ground black pepper
1/4 cup mayonaise

Directions:
Combine all ingredients (except cabbage).  Mix until blended and smooth.  Pour over shredded cabbage.  Refrigerate for 3 hours prior to serving.

Submitter: 
sunnie
Description: 
A refreshing and tangy cole slaw that is easy to make.
