Title:
Homestyle Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. light sour cream
1 c. mayo
1 pkg chopped spinach, defrosted and drained
1/2 c. chopped parsley
2 TBS grated onion
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp dill weed
2 tsp ground celery seed (or 1 tsp celery salt)
1 tsp Lawry's seasoned salt
2 tsp Tarragon leaves
1 can water chestnuts, chopped (optional)

Directions:
1.  Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
2.  Cover and refridgerate for at least 45 minutes.
3.  Serve with bread chunks or crackers.

Submitter: 
LouiseCol
Description: 
This delicious spinach dip was created when I had everything but the dry soup mix part of this classic.  Try this version and you will never go back!
