Title:
China Lake Barbequed Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/180466.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 lemon, juiced
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
1 1/2 pounds flank steak

Directions:
Mix soy sauce, lemon juice and oil together in a large resealable plastic bag. Rub garlic power into meat, place in bag, and seal. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours; turn bag after 2 hours.
Preheat grill for medium heat.
Oil grate lightly, and place meat on grill. Discard marinade. Cook steak for 5 to 7 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.

Submitter: 
Bobbie
Description: 
I made this for my family when I was a Navy wife with three children with healthy appetites. This recipe will make the most inexpensive cuts very tender.
