Title:
Crispy Garlic Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 cloves crushed garlic
1/2 cup olive oil
juice of 2 limes
1 1/4 cup dry bread crumbs(panko)
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
4-6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Directions:
Mix the garlic and olive oil in a plastic zip bag to blend the flavors. let sit a few minutes then add juice of 1 lime, add chicken and marinate a few hours.  Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). In a separate dish, combine the bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese. coat with bread crumb mixture. Place in a shallow baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
