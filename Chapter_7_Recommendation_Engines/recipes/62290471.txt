Title:
Spicy BlackBean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 15oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
* 1 15 oz can whole corn drained
* 2 tomatoes chopped small
* 2 avocados chopped small
* 1/2 onion chopped small
* 3 tbsp lime juice
* 2 tbsp olive oil
* 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
* 1 tbsp salt

Directions:
1. Combine all the vegetables, add remaining ingredients and pour over the top. Store in the fridge in an airtight container, chill for at least 1 hour.
2. When I want more spice I add Smoky Hungarian Paprika and chili lime powder. Or Substitute garilc oil for the olive oil.

Submitter: 
Kathryn Weidenheimer
Description: 
Easy Bean Salsa, Great for Pot-Lucks, I generally make this and eat straight out of the bowl. My family Loves it and its one of my most requested meals to potlucks.
