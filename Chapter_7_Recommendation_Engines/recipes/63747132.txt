Title:
Jan's Jalapeno Popper Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1051559.jpg
Ingredients:
1 recipe of prepared pizza dough for a 16 inch pie
8 slices bacon cut into 1 inch pieces
3/4 cup chopped onion
2 cloves chopped garlic garlic
1- 8 ounce package low fat cream cheese, cubed
1/4 cup sour cream
1/2 cup shredded low fat Cheddar cheese
1-2 cups shredded Mozzarella cheese (depending on your preference)
4 jalapeno peppers cleaned and cut into thin rings
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon dry parsley flakes

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees.
In a large frying pan cook the bacon until crisp.  Remove to plate, covered with a paper towel, to drain off fat.
Add onion to bacon drippings and cook until soft and translucent.
Add garlic and cook over low heat until golden.  Just a few minutes.
Add cumin, oregano and pepper, sour cream and cream cheese.  Cook until soft and spreadable.
Spread cheese mixture over prepared pizza dough.
Sprinkle shredded cheeses over cream cheese mixture.
Top with the bacon then the pepper rings and parsley flakes.
Bake pizza until crust is firm and crispy and the toppings are browned and bubbly.  Approximately 30 minutes or until preferred crust browning is reached.
