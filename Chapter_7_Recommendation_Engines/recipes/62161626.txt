Title:
Green Garlic and Ham Scrambled Eggs with Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6     - Large Eggs
1     - Onion, chopped
2-3   - Green Garlic Stalks - sliced tops removed
1/2c  - Ham, cubed
1 tbsp- butter
1/4c  - milk
1 tsp - salt
1 tsp - ground black pepper
1/2c  - Old/white Cheddar Cheese - shredded

Directions:
Combine onion, green garlic, ham and butter in a skillet and saute until vegetables are tender.
Mix eggs with milk, salt and pepper.
Add egg mixture to ham and vegetables and scramble until almost done.
mix in half the cheese and finish cooking
Top with the rest of the cheese after serving onto plates
Serves 2-3

Submitter: 
Soupster
Description: 
I recently was given some stalks of Green Garlic.  I had no idea what to do with them so I started looking for recipes and found this delicious egg recipe.  There are endless variations but the key ingredients are the same.
