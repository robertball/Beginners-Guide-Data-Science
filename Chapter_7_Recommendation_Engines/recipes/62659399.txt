Title:
Mostaccioli with Spinach and Feta with Papere's changes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces penne pasta
2 tablespoons olive oil
3 cups chopped tomatoes
10 ounces fresh spinach, washed and chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
8 ounces tomato basil feta cheese
salt to taste
ground black pepper to tast
Mushrooms
Onions
Toasted Pine Nuts
Try red chili pepper
Try adding Shrimp

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.  Drain, and set aside.
Heat oil in a large pot.  Add tomatoes, spinach, and garlic; cook and stir 2 minutes, or until spinach is wilted and mixture is thoroughly heated.  Add pasta and cheese; cook 1 minute.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Papere
Description: 
Flavorful feta cheese, studded with bits of tomato and basil, is tossed with hot pasta into a saute of fresh tomatoes, baby spinach and minced garlic for a colorful, aromatic meal.Modified from: Mostaccioli with Spinach and Feta
