Title:
Hearty Split Pea Soup - modified Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 ounces dried green split peas with ham flavor packet
2 cups diced fully cooked ham
1 medium onion, chopped
4 celery stalks, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
6 cups boiling water
3-5 Tbsp minute tapioca

Directions:
In a slow cooker, layer the ingredients in order listed (do not stir). Cover and cook on high for 4-5 hours or until vegetables are tender.
Last hour of cooking combine tapioca with 1/2 cup hot broth, blend well with fork then add to soup.
