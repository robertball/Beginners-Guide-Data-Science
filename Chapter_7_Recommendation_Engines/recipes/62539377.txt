Title:
Sesame Shrimp Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tablespoon sesame seeds
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons canola oil
1 red bell pepper, sliced into thin strips
3 green onions, sliced
5 tablespoons teriyaki sauce, divided
4 cups broccoli florets (fresh)
3/4 tablespoon arrowroot
3/4 cup chicken broth
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 cups cooked brown basmati rice

Directions:
Combine shrimp, 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce, ginger, cayenne pepper, garlic, sesame seeds and black pepper in a large plastic food storage bag. Allow to marinate in the refrigerator.
Heat canola oil in a large wok or skillet. Add red bell pepper and green onions; saute 3 to 4 minutes to soften slightly  Add teriyaki sauce. Add broccoli and shrimp with seasoning; saute 4 minutes or until shrimp are opaque.
Stir arrowroot into chicken broth and add to wok; cook, stirring until mixture boils. Sprinkle with salt. Spoon shrimp mixture over rice.
