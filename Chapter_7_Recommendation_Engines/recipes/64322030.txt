Title:
Tomato Stuffed with Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounce(s) fish, tuna, light, packed in water
2 tablespoon mayonnaise
2 teaspoon mustard, Dijon
2 tablespoon celery chopped
2 tablespoon scallion(s) (green onions) chopped
salt to taste
pepper, black ground to taste
2 large tomato(es)
2 cup(s) lettuce, mixed greens
2 teaspoon oil, olive

Directions:
Drain tuna fish and mix with mayonnaise, mustard, celery, scallions, salt and pepper. Remove top of tomato and hollow out as much as desired, as you would a pumpkin.
Scoop tuna salad into tomato and top with minced tops of green onions or celery leaves. Serve with mixed greens tossed with salt, pepper and oil.
