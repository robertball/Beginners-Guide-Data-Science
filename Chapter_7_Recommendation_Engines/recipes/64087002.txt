Title:
Whole Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-1/4 cups water
1 Tbsp yeast
1-1/2 Tbsp molasses
1-1/2 Tbsp brown sugar
1/2 Tbsp salt
1 egg
2 Tbsp oil
3/4 cup cracked wheat
4 cups whole wheat flour
3 cups bread flour

Directions:
Heat 2- 1/4 cups water to 120- 130 degrees F.  In a large bowl, add 1/2 cup of this water to yeast and mix to dissolve yeast.  Add molasses, brown sugar, salt, egg, oil and remaining warm water.  Mix well.
Add cracked wheat and whole wheat flour, mixing well.  Add bread flour one cup at a time.
Place dough on a floured surface. Knead bread for 5 minutes, adding more flour as needed.  Place in large greased bowl and turn over so greased side is up.  Cover with a towel and let rise until double in size.  Punch down, divide in two, form into loaves and place in two greased bread pans.  Cover with towel and let rise until loaf size.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Bake for 30 minutes or until done.
