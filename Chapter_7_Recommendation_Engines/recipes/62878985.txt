Title:
Pink Angel Rider's World's Best Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/693285.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2  pound sweet Italian sausage links
1 1/2 pound lean ground beef
1/2 cup minced onion
2 tablespoons of ready to use minced Garlic
1 (28 ounce) diced garlic & onion tomatoes
2 (6 ounce) cans garlic & onion tomato paste
2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
1/2 cup water
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1 tablespoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 tablespoons dried parsley
2 boxes oven ready Lasagna Noodles
32 ounces ricotta cheese
2 eggs
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 16oz packages skim milk mozzarella cheese, grated
1 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium/low heat until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with sugar, basil,   Italian seasoning, 1 tablespoon salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons parsley. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with eggs, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. shake pan to get even cover. Take noodles straight from box  Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a third of grated mozzarella cheese . Spoon 1 3/4 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.  Repeat layers in opposite direction top with ricotta cheese mixture, one third of mozzarella cheese, spoon 1 3/4 cups of sauce, Last, 1 layer of noodles then  top  with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, spray foil with cooking spray, make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil,the extra meat sauce top lasagna with it and sprinkle mozzarella and  parmesan cheese  and put foil back on bake  for 15 minutes remove foil and bake an additional 10 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
