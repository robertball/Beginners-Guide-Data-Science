Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Chayote Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/450375.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken - cubed
1 white onion diced
4 garlic cloves diced
2 tablespoons oil
4 Cups Chicken broth
1 4oz can green chile peppers
2 chayotes
1/2 red bell pepper diced
1/2 green bell pepper diced
1 cup corn
1/2 cup carrots diced
1 can black beans drained and rinsed
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon mexican oregano

Directions:
Heat oil.  Saute diced chicken, onion and garlic in skillet. Once chicken is cooked on all sides transfer all to the crock pot.  Add remaining ingredients to crock pot.  Cook on low for 6 hours.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Serve over rice if desired.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is a great way to use chayote (aka merlins).  Let is simmer and have a nice light meal.  Serve over rice.
