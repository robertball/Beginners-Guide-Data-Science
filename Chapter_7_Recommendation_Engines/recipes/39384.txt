Title:
Cheesy Skillet Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980826.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
1 (4.5 ounce) can mushrooms, drained (optional)
1 (16 ounce) jar ORTEGA® Salsa Prima™ - Homestyle Mild
1 (4 ounce) can ORTEGA® Diced Green Chiles
2 cups SARGENTO® 4 Cheese Mexican Blend or ChefStyle Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese, divided
1 cup crumbled tortilla chips

Directions:
COOK beef, onion and mushrooms in large skillet until beef is browned; drain. Stir in salsa and chiles; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low; cook, stirring occasionally, for 2 to 3 minutes.
SPRINKLE with 1 1/2 cups cheese and tortilla chips. Top with remaining cheese. Cover; cook for an additional 2 to 3 minutes or until cheese melts.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
Your family will ask for seconds of this savory dish bursting with ground beef, shredded cheese and mushrooms.
