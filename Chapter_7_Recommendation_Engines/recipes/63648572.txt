Title:
Creamy Pomegranate Poppy Seed Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
2 T honey
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup Greek yogurt
2 Tbsp pomegranate juice
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tsp poppy seeds

Directions:
Put all ingredients EXCEPT the poppy seeds into a blender and blend until emulsified. Once everything is creamy and smooth, stir in the poppy seeds and serve over your favorite salad
