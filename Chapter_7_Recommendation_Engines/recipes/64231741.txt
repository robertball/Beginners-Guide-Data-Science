Title:
Tortellini in brodo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 kilo fresh tortellini (beef or prosciutto filled)
3 litres stock (chicken, veggie or beef)
1/4 cup shredded parmigiano reggiano

Directions:
Bring the stock to a boil.
Add the tortellini and simmer according to package directions.
Serve hot with parmigiano reggiano on top.
