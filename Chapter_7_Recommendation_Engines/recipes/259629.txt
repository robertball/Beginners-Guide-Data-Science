Title:
Kid-Approved Bento Toolbox Lunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4548558.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons peanut butter, or to taste
1 (7 inch) whole wheat tortilla
1 apple, sliced
4 grape tomatoes, or to taste
4 yellow grape tomatoes, or to taste
3 celery sticks, or to taste
4 baby carrots, or to taste
2 tablespoons ranch dressing, or to taste

Directions:
Spread peanut butter over tortilla. Arrange apple slices end to end down the middle of the tortilla. Roll tortilla up tightly; cut into small pieces.
Assemble bento box with tortilla pieces, grape tomatoes, golden tomatoes, celery, and carrots. Serve ranch dressing alongside.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Every little one enjoys a healthy lunch complete with toys. This toolbox lunch comes complete with a tractor loader, dump truck ,and utensil tools for the lunch break. Eating healthy was never more fun.
