Title:
Wendy's Cranberry Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounce bag of cranberries
1 garlic clove
1 or 2 jalapeno chilis
4 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro
3 scallions, minced
1/2 cup lime juice
1/2 cup sugar
Salt and freshly ground pepper,to taste

Directions:
Boil cranberries for 1 minute in 1 quart water. Drain well.  Mince the garlic. Seed and mince the chiles. Combine with chopped cilantro, scallions, and the cranberries in a mixing bowl. Mix by hand, squeezing some of berries to pulp and leaving the rest whole. Add lime juice, sugar, salt, and pepper as needed. The mixture should be a little sweet, a little sour and very spicy.
