Title:
Chiavetta's Chicken BBQ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts or 4 thighs
Chiavetta's BBQ Marinade

Directions:
Marinade Chicken in BBQ overnight, forking the meat to allow juices to enter the meat.
Grill until cooked through.
