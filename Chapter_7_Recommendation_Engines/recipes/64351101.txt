Title:
Crockpot Creamy Mushroom Chicken and stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 LBS Cooked chicken breast coarsely chopped
1 cup Chicken Broth
2 12oz cans Cream of Mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
2 4oz cans sliced mushrooms
1 cup chopped onion
2 6oz boxes of stove top stuffing
1 cup water

Directions:
Layer the bottom of the crockpot with the chicken and broth
Stir together the soups and the sour cream in a large bowl.
Blend the rest of the ingredients into the soup mixture until well incorporated.
Spread the soup  and stove top mixture over the chicken layer.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This is one I can make out of the pantry and is easily done with left over chicken. It can be cut in half for a family of four. Comfort food!
