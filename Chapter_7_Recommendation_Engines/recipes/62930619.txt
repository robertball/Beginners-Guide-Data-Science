Title:
Spicy bean burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/717145.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 c brown rice, cooked
1/2 c frozen corn kernals
1 egg
1/2 c flour
1 tbl chili powder
Cayenne pepper (optional)
Oil for frying
1/4 c cheddar cheese , grated

Directions:
Mash beans with potato masher or food processor.  Stir in egg with fork.  Gradually add in flour and spices.  Mixture should become a thick paste.  Fold in rice and corn.  Heat oil in skillet, then spoon bean mixture into pan, forming it into the size and shape of hamburger patties.  Fry until browned, about 5 minutes each side.  Place fried burgers on cookie sheet and sprinkle with cheese.  Broil in oven until cheese is hot and melted.

Submitter: 
DiaperBrigade
Description: 
Everyone will enjoy these meatless burgers.  Great topped with guacamole or grilled onions.  For extra kick, add a much cayenne pepper as you dare!
