Title:
Chicken of the Pea (Vegan Tuna) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4801548.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans 15 oz organic garbanzo beans (drained and rinsed)
1/4 red onion chopped
2 stalks celery chopped
6 flat tablespoons vegan mayo
1 table spoon olive oil
1 table spoon pickle juice
1 table spoon apple cider vinegar
1 table spoon sea salt
1 table spoon garlic powder
2 table spoons non active yeast
1 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon finely crushed dried seaweed
1/4 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning
2 dashes hot sauce
pinch of cayenne pepper
pinch of dry dill
lemon juice (1/4 lemon hand squeezed)

Directions:
use a potato masher to crush garbanzo beans in large mixing bowl. add rest of ingredients and mix thoroughly. refrigerate before serving on your favorite cracker or sandwich
