Title:
Chilies Rellenos Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg whites
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 cup flour
1 cup evaporated milk
16 oz of Monterey Jack cheese or the Mexican blend
3 small cans whole green chilies or 1 large can

Directions:
Heat oven to 350
Spray a 9 x 12 pyrex dish with cooking spray.
In medium bowl beat egg whites till fluffy.  Stir in milk, salt and flour.  Fold until well blended.
Place a layer of green chilies in the baking dish.  Cover with 1/2 the egg mixture & about 1/3 of the cheese.  Repeat layers saving the last 1/3 of the cheese.  Bake at 350 for 25 - 30 minutes.  Add the last of the cheese and return to oven for another 5 minutes or until cheese is melted and casserole is bubbly.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
This is a fluffy casserole version of Chili Rellenos.  Serve with the Mexican Chili Sauce.
