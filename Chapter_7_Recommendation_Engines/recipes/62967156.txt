Title:
Baked Chili Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chop mushrooms
1 cup chop onion
1/2 cup chop tomatoes
1-15oz can vegetarian chili
2/3 cup baking mix (heart smart)
1/4 cup skim milk
2 Tbl egg beaters
salt & pepper
Opt: FF sour cream

Directions:
preheat oven to 375. spray 9x9 baking pan with Pam
Medium skillet spray with Pam over med heat. Add mushrooms, onion & corn. stirring occasionally, cook until veggies soften, about 3 min Add tomatoes & continue to cook until soft, about 2 min
Remove skillet from heat. Add chili & mix well. Transfer to baking pan.
In med bowl, combine baking mix, milk & egg beaters. Whisk vigorously until smooth. Evenly pour mixture over chili mixture.
Bake until top is gold brown---about 25min. Cool 5 min before serving
