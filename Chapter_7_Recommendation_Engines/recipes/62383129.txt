Title:
Big Daddy's Banana and Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 banana
10 frozen strawberries
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup cold milk
1 scoop Mila

Directions:
Combine the banana, strawberries, cinnamon, milk, and Mila in a blender; blend until smooth; serve immediately.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description:
