Title:
Apple Cranberry Compote Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/362341.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp margarine or butter
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup orange juice
2 medium McIntosh apples, peeled and chopped
1 cup fresh or frozen whole cranberries
1 cup sweetened dried cranberries
1 Tbsp orange liquer
1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
1/8 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
1 tsp orange zest

Directions:
In a heavy medium-sized sauce pan, melt margarine or butter over medium-low heat. Stir in brown sugar and orange juice until smooth.
Cook for 3 minutes, then stir in apples and cranberries. Cook, stirring   occasionally, for 10 minutes.
Stir in orange liquer, ginger, nutmeg, and orange zest. Continue cooking an additional 10 minutes or until whole cranberries pop and apples have softened.
Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly. Serve warm or room temperature.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
Who says cranberries are just for Thanksgiving? Serve this warm, tangy fruit compote with pork or poultry for a festive and delicious touch any time of year.
