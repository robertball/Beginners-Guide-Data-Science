Title:
Almond Roca, Frugal Version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 sleeve Saltine crackers
1 cup (2 cubes) butter or margarine
1 cup brown sugar(you can use white sugar, but brown sugar is much better.
2 cups chocolate chips, milk chocolate or Semi-sweet
Sliced toasted almonds, pecans or walnuts, optional

Directions:
Prepare jelly roll pan (or large flat baking pan) with foil covering sides and bottom and sprayed with cooking spray.
Preheat oven 350*
Arrange crackers on foil fully covering the bottom of the pan.
Put butter and sugr into sauce pan
Cook to melt butter and sugar together and bring to a boil and boil for 2 minutes.
Pour sugar/water mixture over crackers.
Place in oven and bake for 10 minutes.
Remove pan from oven and sprinkle chococlate chips evenly over the cracker mixture.
Return to oven and bake for another 2 or 3 minutes or until chocolate chips are almost melted.
Remove from oven and carefully spread chocolate evenly over all.
Sprinkle with nuts if you are using them
Cool and break into pieces and try to leave some for the others.

Submitter: 
Kitten
Description: 
Crunchy sweet Almond Roca, but also kind to the budget. As the recipe says, try to leave some of the Almond Roca for the others.
