Title:
Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Individual oatmeal cups in various flavors
Water

Directions:
1. Boil water in large pot or use tea kettles or keurig if available
2. Laddle out water into each cup for students
3. Students stir, cover with paper lid, and wait until cooked

Submitter: 
SST
Description: 
Oatmeal cups are a great, quick breakfast. If you are in a hurry, be sure to purchase disposable spoons too. Also, this is a great breakfast to buy ahead of time to bring to the outreach location so you don't have to shop before the first breakfast. Serve with fruit.
