Title:
Whole Earth Kale Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. Brown Rice
1 package of Firm Tofu - diced
3/4 c. Tamari almonds
1 large bunch of Kale with stems and ribs removed - chopped
1/2 head large Red Cabbage - chopped
1 c. shredded carrots
1/2 c. lemon juice
1/4 sesame seeds
1/8 c. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 c. chopped flat leaf parsley
8 cloves fresh garlic - chopped
1/4 c. stone ground mustard
1/2 c. tamari soy sauce
1/2 c. fresh dill - chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook brown rice according to directions.  Let cool.  Mix all the ingredients together and enjoy!  Delicious!!!
