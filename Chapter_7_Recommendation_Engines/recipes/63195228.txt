Title:
Chicken with Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 4oz. boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup, undeluted
1 1/4 cup of chicken broth or water
1/4 c. butter melted
1 6oz. package of your favorite stuffing mix

Directions:
In a greased slow cooker, place chicken down first, top with soup.  In a bowl, combine the broth/water, butter and stuffing mix;  spoon over the chicken.  Cover and cook on low for 4 hours or until chicken juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
a very moist chicken dinner.  with the right sides it makes you think of Thanksgiving.
