Title:
Italian Style Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1LB ground turkey breast
1 egg
1/4 cup italian style bread crumbs
1/4 tsp salt or to taste
1/2 tsp pepper or to taste
1 tsp italian seasoning
1/2 clove garlic
1 + 1 cup of tomato sauce

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400
Ccmbine all ingredients except tomato sauce.
Shape into loaf.
Place on a baking pan with rack or in any baking dish sprayed with cooking spray.
Bake for 40 minutes.
Spoon 1 cup of tomato sauce over loaf and bake another 10-15 minutes.
Remove from oven, check for doneness, let stand for 5-10 minutes.
Slice and serve with extra tomato sauce.
