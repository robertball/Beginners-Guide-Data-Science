Title:
Three Bean Chilli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2166761.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Hot Italian sausages, cooked and sliced
1 14 oz can Chilli beans, drained
1 14 oz can Pinto beans, drained
1 14 oz can  Pork and Beans, drained
1 14 oz can stewed tomatoes
1 Sweet Red pepper, seeded and chopped into squares
1 Jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped finely
1 14 oz container,  Diced tomatoes

Directions:
In the bottom of a medium stock pot spread 2 Tbsp. of Canola oil to coat bottom of pot.
Throw in sliced cooked sausages, assorted beans, tomatoes and diced tomatoes,
Cook over medium heat and prepare peppers for stock pot.
Continue to cook over medium heat until all ingredients are heated through. Stir all  ingredients together and simmer until ready to serve with white or brown rice

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very lightly spiced chilli with hot Italian sausages to add some spice to the mixture with an Jalapeno pepper to make it a little hotter.
