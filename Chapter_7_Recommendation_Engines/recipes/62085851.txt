Title:
Gracie's Iowa Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole cooked chicken
16 oz. macaroni, uncooked
2c. chicken broth
8oz can water chestnuts, chopped
2 10.75oz. cans cream of mushroom soup
2c. shredded cheddar cheese
1 small onion, chopped
1/2t. salt
1/4t. black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Lightly grease a 9x13 dish.  Remove the skin and bones from the chicken and shred the meat.  Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix well.  Put mixture in prepared 9x13 pan, bake 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Christy Weber Stout
Description: 
My favorite casserole that my grandmother used to make.  It's comfort food!
