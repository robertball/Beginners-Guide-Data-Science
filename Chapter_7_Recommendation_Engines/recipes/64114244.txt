Title:
Roasted Beats and Yams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 beats, chopped
3 yams, chopped
2 onions, chopped
6 T. butter
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. chopped pecans
2 T. salt
2 T. olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. While the oven heats up, chop all the vegetables into 1 inch cubes. Put everything into a baking pan coated with the olive oil. Next, distribute the brown sugar, butter, pecans, and salt evenly over everything.
Bake for 45 minutes. Stir before serving.

Submitter: 
Sandra Holding
Description: 
Perfect for Vegetarians, this recipe is made with two Superfoods. Beets and Yams are both dense with anti-oxidants and essential vitamins and minerals. I make some version of it every Thanksgiving and Christmas. Given the colors though, it could be the perfect dish for Halloween. :))
