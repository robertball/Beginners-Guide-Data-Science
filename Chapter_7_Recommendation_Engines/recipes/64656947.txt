Title:
Joanie's Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box elbow macaroni
1 can tiny shrimp (drained)
1 cup of hellmans mayonnaise
1 tbsp of fresh or dried parsley
1 tbsp of grated horseradish
1 cup of  diced celery
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions.  Combine mayo with shrimp, salt, pepper, parsley, and horseradish.  Drain macaroni and rinse under cold water. Add mayo mixture to cooled macaroni and gently fold in. Add celery. Chill for at least 2 hours.
