Title:
Big Daddy's Slow Cooker Dumplings with Cheesy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 (11 ounce) cans condensed cream of Cheddar cheese soup
2 scoops of Mila
1/2 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon Big Daddy's Roasted Garlic rub

Directions:
Spray slow cooker with cooking spray. Place chicken breasts inside. In a medium bowl mix together Mila,soup and milk, and pour mixture over chicken. Season with salt and pepper to taste and garlic rub.
Cook on High for about 6 hours. Note: Do not lift lid while cooking!

Cheddar soup and chicken, let the cooker do the work for you.Modified from: Slow Cooker Dump and Go Cheesy Chicken
