Title:
"Da-Da-Li" (Stuffed Crepes) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1071249.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground pork.
1/2 lb.ground beef.
1-8-oz can sweet peas.
1/4 chopped sweet onion.
4-stripes of smoked bacon.
2-cloves garlic.
1-tbl. salt.
1/2 tbl. pepper.
1/2 red pepper.
1-egg.
1-tub sour cream.
For the crepes.
2-cups all purpose flour.
2-eggs.
1 tbl. fast rising yeast.
1-teaspoon salt.
1/2 teaspoon white pepper.
1/2 cup milk or soda water.

Directions:
Chop your bacon, onions, and garlic fine.
Fry bacon and onions garlic.
Add the meat and fry until done.
Strain off excess fat.
In a large mixing bowl mix all your spice and egg with strained peas and the meat mixture.
Mix your batter for the crepes .
Hot oiled skillet make your crepes and fill with mixture. put in greased baking dish and spread the sour cream on top.
Bake in 400 degree oven for 20-25 min.Or until heated through and golden brown.
