Title:
Chicken Enchilada Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 store bought pizza crust
3/4 cup prepared red enchilada sauce
1 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
1 cup Mexican Blend cheese, shredded
1/2 cup prepared Pico De Gallo, drained
3 TBLS salsa (optional)
1/4 cup sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Place pizza crust on a pizza pan or baking sheet sprayed with cooking spray.  Spread the enchilada sauce over the pizza crust, and add the shredded chicken on top.  Sprinkle the cheese on top of that and finally sprinkle the pico de gallo over the pizza.
Bake on the middle rack in the oven for about 10 to 15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown.
Optional:  If you like top with salsa and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This is a delicious, quick and easy chicken pizza with a Mexican twist.
