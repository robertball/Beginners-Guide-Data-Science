Title:
strawberry banana smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh or frozen strawberrys hulled and cut in half.1/2 medium lage banana,peeled cut into 1/2-inch slices 1/2 cup orange juice 1cup fat free vanilla yogurt

Directions:
place all ingredients in blender.blend on low speed 15 to 20 seconds then on high additional 15 to 20 seconds until creamy and thick
