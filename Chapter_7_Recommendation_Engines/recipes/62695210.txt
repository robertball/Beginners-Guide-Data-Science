Title:
Sweet & Sour Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp margarine
½ cup chopped onion
1 cup water
1 c ketchup
2 Tbsp vinegar
2 Tbsp lemon juice
2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp dry mustard
salt & pepper
4-6 bone-in pork chops

Directions:
Brown pork chops in frying pan.
Mix sauce ingredients in a pot – simmer for 15 minutes.
Put browned chops in roaster, pour sauce over them.
Cook, covered, in oven for 90 minutes at 350°F.

Submitter: 
mom2threelittleones
Description: 
Cooking these for a long time in the oven makes for very tender pork chops.
