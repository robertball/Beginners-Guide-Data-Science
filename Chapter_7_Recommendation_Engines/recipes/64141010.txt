Title:
Apple & Onion Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium apples, sliced
2 large onions, thinly sliced
1 Tbsp butter
6 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
3/4 cup Swiss cheese, shredded
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup bread crumbs
1/2 tsp minced fresh thyme
2 Tbsp apple juice

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute apples and onions in butter for 10 minutes or until tender.  Transfer to a baking dish coated with cooking spray.  Top with chicken; sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Combine the cheeses, bread crumbs and thyme; sprinkle over chicken.  Drizzle with apple juice.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 30-35 minutes or until chicken is done.
