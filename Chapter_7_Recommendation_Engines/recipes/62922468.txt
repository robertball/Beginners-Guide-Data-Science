Title:
Mouth-Watering Chicken Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken parts (thighs or legs) enough to fill a 9x13 pyrex baking dish.
2 tbsp Olive oil
1-2 tsps garlic salt (your option for more, too)
1 tbsp Italian herbs
1 fresh lemon, squeezed
6-8 little red potatoes

Directions:
Clean and remove skin on chicken pieces.
Drizzle olive oil in bottom of pyrex baking dish.
Place chicken pieces in baking dish.
Sprinkle a small amt of lemon juice on chicken.
Sprinkle half of garlic salt (remember, you can add more or less here) over chicken.
Sprinkle half of Italian herbs over chicken.
Cut red potatoes into halves. Place halves around chicken (perimeter of the baking dish) This should frame the entire baking dish.
Cook in low oven at 325 for 45 mins
Reduce oven to 300 and cook very slowly for 2-3 hours.
Turn chicken as needed. With each turn, add lemon juice with a little water. (do not over do)
Add the rest of your garlic salt and italian herbs with second turn.
No need to touch potatoes, they will cook nicely staged around this dish.
