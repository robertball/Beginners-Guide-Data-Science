Title:
Patty's Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10-oz. cans Rotel Mexican Festival Diced Tomatoes with Lime Juice
and Cilantro (blue can)
49 oz. Swanson’s chicken broth (large can)
1 dozen corn tortillas
oil for frying
8 oz. shredded Monterey Jack or cheddar/jack cheese
Avocado chunks and sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Combine chicken broth and diced tomatoes in saucepan over medium heat.
Cut tortillas into ½ inch strips.  Heat 1-2” oil in large frying pan.   Fry tortilla strips in single layer in batches until crisp and just beginning to brown.  Drain on paper towels.
In four large soup bowls, place handful of tortilla strips then a handful of cheese.  Continue layering strips and cheese until all are used.
Pour hot broth/tomato soup mixture in four equal portions over strips and cheese in bowls.  Top with a few avocado chunks and a dollop of sour cream, if desired.
Serves 4.

Submitter: 
Patty Pelfrey
Description: 
A simple recipe for a great-tasting Sonoran-style tortilla soup.
