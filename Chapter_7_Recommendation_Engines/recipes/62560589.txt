Title:
BBQ Turkey Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536126.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. Ground turkey
1 cup Green Giant® Niblets® Frozen Corn
1/2 cup barbecue sauce
4 (7-inch) flour tortillas, heated
1 cup chopped tomato
1 oz. (1/4 cup) shredded reduced-fat cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat medium nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Add turkey; cook and stir 4 to 6
minutes or until browned.
Stir in corn and barbecue sauce. Reduce heat to medium; cover and cook 5 minutes or until
hot, stirring occasionally.
Place tortillas on 4 individual plates. Spoon 1/4 of turkey mixture down center of each tortilla;
top each with 1/4 cup chopped tomato and 1 tablespoon cheese. Fold tortillas in half or roll up

Description: 
Ground turkey cooked with barbecue sauce and corn, and served in tortillas with tomatoes and cheese.
