Title:
Coke Peppermint Float Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/863492.jpg
Ingredients:
coke bottle or can
large cup
ice cream
peppermint
whipped cream

Directions:
Pour desired amount of coke in cup. Grind up peppermint and add to coke. Put in icecream scoop and stir. Top with whipped cream. Enjoy!
