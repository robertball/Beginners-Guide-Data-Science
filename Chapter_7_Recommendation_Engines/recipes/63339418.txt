Title:
DISH DESSERT Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LG PKG OREOS
12 OZ COOL WHIP
8 OZ CREAM CHEESE
1 STICK MARGERINE
2 CUPS WHOLE MILK
1 CUP POWDERED SUGAR
3 OZ BOX INSTANT CHOCOLATE PUDDING
1 TSP VANILLA

Directions:
CRUSH OREOS AND PLACE IN BOTTOM OF 11 X 17 PAN
MIX TOGETHER COOL WHIP, CREAM CHEESE,  POWDERED SUGAR & MARGERINE.
PREPARE INSTANT PUDDING AND COMBINE W/1ST MIXTURE AND POUR OVER OREOS.  SPRINKLE REMAINING OREOS OVER PUDDING MIXTURES.
REFRIDERATE 1 HOUR AND THEN TOP W/GUMMY WORMS
