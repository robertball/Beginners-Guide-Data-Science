Title:
Cream Cheese Yogurt Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package  neufchatel cream cheese, softened
1 cup Splenda
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup yogurt (any flavor)
Assorted fresh fruit

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese, Splenda, yogurt and vanilla; mix well. Serve with fresh fruit for dipping. Refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
suetoyou
Description: 
Color it Christmas with a platter of red and green apple slices piled 'round a bowl of this yummy no-fuss fruit dip! The dip also goes well with strawberries, grapes and kiwi.Modified from: Cream Cheese Fruit Dip
