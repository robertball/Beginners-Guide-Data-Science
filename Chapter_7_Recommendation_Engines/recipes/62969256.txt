Title:
Quick & Easy Chicken, Broccoli and Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. vegetable oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 can (10 3/4 oz.) Campbell's Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
1 1/2 cups water
1/4 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
1 1/2 cups uncooked instant white or brown rice
2 cups fresh or frozen broccoli flowerets

Directions:
Heat oil in 10in skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until well browned on both sides. Remove chicken from skillet.
Stir soup, water, paprika and black pepper in skillet. Heat to a boil.
Stir rice and broccoli in skillet. Reduce heat to low. Return chicken to skillet. Sprinkle additional paprika and black pepper over chicken. Cover and cook 5 min. or until chicken is cooked through and rice is tender.
