Title:
Easy sous vide dulce de leche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6055633.jpg
Ingredients:
1 -can Condensed sweetened milk
1- 8 ounce mason jar

Directions:
-Pour condensed milk in mason jar
-Heat water to 185 degrees Fahrenheit with sous vide
-Add jar to water for 14 hours
-Cool In fridge for an hour
-Ready to serve
