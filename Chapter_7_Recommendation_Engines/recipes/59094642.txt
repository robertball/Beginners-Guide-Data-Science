Title:
Quick and Easy One Dish Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/266118.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder (preferably dutch cocoa or Hershey's Special Dark)
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In an ovensafe 8X8 square pan, melt the stick of butter.  Swirl to coat the bottom of the pan.  Add the sugar, eggs and vanilla directly to the dish and stir with a fork until well blended.
Add the 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder to the pan.  Stir until combined.  use a paper towel to wipe the sides of the dish clean, if desired.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
