Title:
Avacado Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium avocado
lemon wedge
1 (5 oz.) can light tuna in water (drained)
1 stalk celery, chopped
2 green onions, chopped
1/3 cup grape tomatoes, quartered
1 tablespoon Greek yogurt
Salt and pepper to taste
Salt free seasoning, to taste

Directions:
Remove avocado seed and spoon avocado out of its shell into a medium bowl. Squeeze lemon wedge over avocado and crush with a fork until you have slightly lumpy paste.
Add in tuna and mix. Add celery, green onions, grape tomatoes, and yogurt. Season to taste and mix completely.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
Great for sandwiches, wraps, or just by itself.
