Title:
Mom’s Delicious Russian Vegetable soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 Cabbage Chopped
1 Large Onion Finley Chopped
3 Cloves Garlic Minced
1/3 Zucchini Thinly Sliced
2 Carrots cut in small pieces
2 Celery stalks cut in small pieces
1/3 Red bell pepper Coarsely Chopped
2 Large Idaho Potatoes cut in small pieces
1 Small Tomato Chopped
About 1/2 Teaspoon Black Pepper
About 1 1/2 - 2 Tablespoons Salt
1 1/2 Teaspoon Vegeta Soup Mix
Or 1 Bouillon Cube
About 1 Gallon of Water Or less
3 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil

Directions:
In a large soup pan heat the vegetable oil. Add onion, garlic, zucchini, carrot, cabbage, celery, bell pepper. Put some of the salt and pepper. Sauté on low heat for 20 min.
Cover With Water and add some more of the salt and pepper. On a low heat Cook covered for 40 min stirring occasionally.
After 40 min add the potatoes, Cook for 25 min.
After 25 min add the tomato, continue cooking for 10 min.
After 10 min, add the remaining salt and pepper and the Vegeta or Bouillon cube. Check for enough salt and pepper. Cook for 5 min then shut off.
Serve hot with a big spoon of sour cream as desired.
