Title:
Grilled Cipollini Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Cipollini onions
1 TBS olive oil
1tsp seasoned salt like Lawry's
1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, coarsely chopped (about 8 large leaves)

Directions:
Peel skin off of onions and place in a plastic bag or small bowl. Add remaining ingredients. Place in refrigerator for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
Soak wooden skewer in water for 30 minutes.
Thread onions onto wooden skewer entwining basil between onions.
Preheat outdoor grill medium hot.
Place skewer over heat. Grill for 10 minutes. Turning once.
