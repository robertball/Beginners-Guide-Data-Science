Title:
Elsie's french waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 White bread slices
2 eggs
1/2 cup bisquick waffle mix
1 tsp. ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat waffle maker to Hi.
Mix together the eggs, cinnamon, and waffle mix. When the waffle maker is heated, dip a slice of bread in the egg mixture and place it in the waffle maker.
Remove from the waffle maker when it is slightly browned. Repeat for the remaining 2 pieces.
