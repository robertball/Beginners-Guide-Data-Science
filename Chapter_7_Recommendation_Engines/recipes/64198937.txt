Title:
Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup white flour
1/4 cup wheat germ
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/3 cup oil
1/2 cup brown sugar
3/4 tsp vanilla
1 egg
1 cup mashed bananas
1/4 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix flours, wheat germ, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  Set aside.
With an electric mixer mix oil and brown sugar.  Add vanilla and egg, mixing well.  Add banana and blend until smooth.
Mix in flour mixture.  Stir in walnuts.  Pour batter into greased loaf pan and bake 45-60 minutes, or until done.
