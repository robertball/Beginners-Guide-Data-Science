Title:
Make Ahead - Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2741885.jpg
Ingredients:
8lbs russet potatoes
3/4 cup butter
1 pkg cream cheese, softened
1/2-3/4 cup half and half
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Peel and cut potatoes into even pieces and boil in a large pot of water until tender. Mash. Add butter, cream cheese, half and half and salt and pepper. Put into casserole dish and cut 5 pcs of butter and lay on top cover with Saran Wrap once cooled. Can be refrigerated for up to 2 days.
TO HEAT and SERVE: remove from fridge 2 hours before putting in the oven. Preheat oven to 350. Bake casserole uncovered for about 45 min. Make sure to check every so often so top of potatoes don't burn.

Submitter: 
Jackie
Description: 
This is a great make ahead recipe to use for Thanksgiving or another holiday.
