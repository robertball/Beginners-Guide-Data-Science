Title:
The Brown Diner Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 onion
1 15 oz. can red beans (drained)
1 15 oz. can black beans (drained)
1 15 oz. can whole kernel corn (drained)
1 28 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 4 oz. can green chilies
1 packet of ranch dressing mix
1 envelope of taco seasoning
shredded cheese (optional)
sour cream (optional)
Mission Strips (optional)

Directions:
Chop onion & brown in skillet with ground beef.
In crockpot, combine tomatoes, drained beans and corn, chilies, and seasoning packets.  Add browned ground beef and 2 tomato cans of water.  Cook in crockpot for 6 hours on low.  Serve with sour cream, shredded cheese and mission strips, if desired.

Submitter: 
Angie Brown
Description: 
After researching taco soup recipes I finally decided to make my own.  With the blend of beans and tang of the ranch, it's sure to be a crowd pleaser!!
