Title:
Jess' Party Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3172268.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen pink lemonade concentrate, thawed
9 fluid ounces vodka
3 fluid ounces lemon-flavored rum (such as Bacardi Limon®)
1/2 lime, juiced
1 packet sugar substitute (such as Truvia®)
5 (12 fluid ounce) cans beer
32 ice cubes, or as desired

Directions:
Mix pink lemonade concentrate, vodka, rum, lime juice, and sugar substitute together in a pitcher. Add beer slowly; stir gently. Serve over ice.
