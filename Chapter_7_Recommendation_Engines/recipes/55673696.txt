Title:
Sushi Rice (Alton Brown) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked glutinous white rice (sushi rice)
2 cups water
2 T rice vinegar
2 T white sugar
1 T kosher salt

Directions:
Rinse the rice in a strainer or colander until the water runs clear. Combine with water in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover and cook for 15 minutes. Remove from burner and let stand for 10 min.
Microwave vinagermixture for 30-45 seconds, stir into the cooked rice. Cool to room temp.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
Here is my recipe for the perfect sushi rice. You can eat this alone or roll into your favorite sushi roll with ingredients of choice. I use strips of carrots, cucumbers and slices of avocado. You can adjust the amount of vinegar in this recipe to suit your taste.Modified from: Perfect Sushi Rice
