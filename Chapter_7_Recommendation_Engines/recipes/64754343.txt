Title:
Blackberry Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6884063.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint fresh blackberries
2 cups heavy cream
1/2 cup whole milk
1/2 cup white sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp grated lemon zest

Directions:
Place the blackberries, lemon zest and sugar into a food processor and puree. Let sit for 10 minutes.
Strain the seeds through a fine mesh strainer and return puree to the food processor.
Add cream, milk, and vanilla. Pulse for 30 seconds to whip the mixture.
Transfer to an ice cream freezer and churn for 20 minutes. Then place into an air tight container and allow to ripen in the freezer for 2 hours to overnight.

Capture the fresh taste of ripe summer berries in this no-fail ice cream.
