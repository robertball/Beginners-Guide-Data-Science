Title:
Secret Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds unpeeled red potatoes, quartered
11/2 cups butter, room temperature
5 ounces Romano cheese, grated
1/4 cup minced garlic (about 7 medium cloves)
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dried oregano

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm,  about 20 minutes; drain.  Stir in butter, then add cheese, garlic, and oregano.  Mash with a potato masher or with an electric mixer. Stir in salt.

Description: 
Red potatoes mashed with butter, Romano cheese, garlic, salt and oregano.  A new twist on a familiar favorite!Modified from: Garlic Mashed Potatoes Secret Recipe
