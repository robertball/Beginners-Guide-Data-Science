Title:
Wholesome Whole Grain Blueberry Oat Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup skim milk
1 large or jumbo egg, beaten
3 tbsp + 1 tsp corn oil
1 cup old-fashioned oatmeal, uncooked (do not use instant)
1 cup pureed blueberries
3/4 cup Gold Medal whole wheat flour
1 tbsp double-acting baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp ginger
1/8 tsp nutmeg
No-stick cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
In a medium bowl, combine oatmeal, milk, egg, oil, and blueberries; set aside.
In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, ginger, and nutmeg.  Make a well in the center of the flour mixture and pour in the blueberry mixture.  Stir until well combined but do not over-beat.
Spray 12-serving (3-in each) muffin pan with no-stick spray.  Spoon in mixture.
Bake for 15 minutes, or until a tester comes out clean.  Cool for 15 minutes before removing from pan.

Submitter: 
EMILY052005
Description: 
These muffins are perfect for those people watching their sugar intake- there is no added sugar OR artificial sweetner.  They make a tasty snack and are incredibly healthy!
