Title:
Strawberry shortcake Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. frozen strawberries
1/2 c water
1/4 c sugar or Splender
2 T lemon juice
10 ounces Cake Volka

Directions:
Thraw Strawberries
Whilte thawing mix water and sugar in a saucepan and cook until sugar disolves and fruit is softened.  Allow to cool.
Puree cooled strawberries and syrup until completely smooth.
Mix 10oz of Cake Volka with the puree and freeze.
It will not freeze hard.
Using a serving fork, scrap and serve with a spoon in a champaign glass.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
A semi frozen slush drimk that takes like strawberry shortcake.
