Title:
Cherry White Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 apples, chopped
2 lemons, sliced into wheels
1 orange, sliced into wheels
1 lime, sliced into wheels
1 (16 ounce) jar maraschino cherries
2 (1.5 liter) bottles moscato wine
1 cup pineapple juice
1 cup orange juice
1/2 cup apple juice
1/2 cup apricot brandy
1/4 cup triple sec
1 (2 liter) bottle lemon-lime soda (such as SpriteÂ®)

Directions:
Combine apples, lemons, orange, and lime in a large bowl; add maraschino cherries and liquid from jar. Top with wine, pineapple juice, orange juice, apple juice, brandy, and triple sec. Refrigerate for at least 24 hours and up to 48 hours.
Pour lemon-lime soda over fruit mixture.
