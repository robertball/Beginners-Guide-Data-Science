Title:
Slow Cooker Oktoberfest Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2699780.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds potatoes
2 large onions
24 oz Jar Sauerkraut
6 Bratwurst's
6 German Sausages
8 oz German Beer

Directions:
Peel and Slice potatoes and layer in a large crockpot
Slice Onions and layer on top of potatoes
Dump, un-drained, the jar of Sauerkraut over potatoes and onions
Cut Brat's into 1 inch pieces and place, firmly, on top of Sauerkraut
Poor beer over all and heat on low for 8 hours
2 hours prior to service, cook sausages on grill until well heated and place in crock pot on top of all.
Serve with a good German mustard

Submitter: 
Richard Dasen Jr
Description: 
All the best of a great German Oktoberfest!
