Title:
Cranberry Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh cranberries
1 (16 oz.) can mandarin oranges chopped
1 cup pecans
3/4 cup white sugar
1 (16 oz.) can crushed pineapple, drained
2 (3 ounce) packages cranberry flavored gelatin mix (or raspberry jello)
1 cup boiling water
1 cup cold water

Directions:
1. In an electric food processor or meat grinder, grind cranberries, oranges, and pecans. Pour mixture into a large mixing bowl.
2. In a separate bowl, combine sugar, pineapple, gelatin and both cups of water. Mix the ingredients from the two bowls together. Pour the entire mixture into a mold and chill overnight before serving.
