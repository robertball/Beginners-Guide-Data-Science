Title:
Duckies Overnight Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup milk
1 cup shredded chedder cheese
1 cup uncooked macaroni
3 Tbls. chopped onion
1 cup cut-up chicken
2 eggs (boiled, and chopped)

Directions:
Boil eggs, cool, and peel.
Stir soup, and milk until well blended.
Add all other ingredients EXEPT eggs.
Turn into a 1 1/2 quart greased casserole dish.
Fold in eggs.
Cover, and refrigerate onernight.
Bake covered @ 350 degrees F for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Tiffanie DeMayo
Description: 
A great recipe from R. Griffith. Make it for that night, you don't have very much time to cook.
* I cut up, and use frozen chicken, so i can make this dish 2 night ahead.
