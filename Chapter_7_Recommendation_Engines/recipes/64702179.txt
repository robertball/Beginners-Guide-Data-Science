Title:
Maple Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 Cups     Flour
2 cups     Sugar
1 tsp     Salt
1 Cups     Butter, Soft
4 large     Eggs
2 tsp     Maple Extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Cream Butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
Mix in eggs and maple extract to creamed butter and mix until incorporated.
Add flour and salt ingredients and mix just to combined and smooth
Bake on a 9x13in baking dish for 30 min or until golden on top
While still warm, glaze by mixing 1 Cups Confectioners Sugar, 1 Tbsp Maple Extract, and 1 tsp Vanilla and pouring over blondies in an even coat.
