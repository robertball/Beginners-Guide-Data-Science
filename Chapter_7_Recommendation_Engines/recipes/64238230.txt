Title:
Grandma Shafer's BEST baked corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 17oz can whole corn, undrained
2 - eggs beaten
1 - small Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix
1 - 17oz can creamed corn
1- stick of margarine, melted
1 - cup sour cream

Directions:
Melt margarine in a bowl, add beaten eggs, corn and sour cream. Mix well and then add muffin mix. Pour into an oiled 7"x10.5"x 1.5" baking dish. Bake at 350-375 degree oven for 35 minutes or until browned and set.

Christine Denstaedt

This recipe is a family tradition.
