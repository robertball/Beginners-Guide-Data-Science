Title:
Zucchini Caprese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/891002.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp olive oil
dash salt and pepper
2 Tbsp chopped basil
1 medium zucchini, chopped
2 roma tomatoes, diced
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella

Directions:
Sautee onion and garlic in olive oil until the onion is translucent. Sprinkle with a dash of salt and pepper, to taste. Add chopped basil and zucchini. Sautee until zucchini is soft, about 5 minutes. Add tomatoes and mozzarella. Stir to melt cheese. Serve as a side dish or on its own.

Submitter: 
Patrice Ramsay
Description: 
Zucchini sauteed with basil and tomatoes is delicious as a side dish or on its own as a healthy meal. Goes great with grilled chicken.
