Title:
Creamy Strawberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Eagle Brand Milk
Juice of 3 Lemons
1 Pint Strawberries
1 Pre-prepared graham cracker pie crust

Directions:
Wash, hull and coarsely chop strawberries. Drain well.
Mix milk and lemon juice.  Stir mixture until it thickens, then fold in strawberries.
Pour into graham cracker crust and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
GLAMM
Description: 
My family had a large strawberry farm when I was growing up so we have a ton of strawberry recipes.  This pie is very refreshing on an early-Summer day, and a great alternative to the traditional gelled-strawberry pie.
