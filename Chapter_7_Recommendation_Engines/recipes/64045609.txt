Title:
Buttermilk Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c buttermilk
1/2 c reduced-fat sour cream
2 T mayonnaise
1 T cider vinegar
2 T chopped fresh chives
1 clove garlic (I used one large clove and one smaller one)
Dash hot sauce
Kosher sauce and freshly ground black pepper
1/2 t worcestershire sauce
1/4 t garlic salt
1/2 c crumbled blue cheese (divided)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients with 1/4 c blue cheese in a small food processor.  Pulse until smooth.  Pour into serving container and add rest of blue cheese crumbles.
