Title:
Elaine's Classic New York Italian Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744778.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 lb Lean Hamburger
1/2 lb of ground pork or sausage
1 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Pepper
1 TB Parsley
1/3 C Ramono Cheese
1 Clove of garlic minced or Approx 1/4 tsp of garlic powder. (We use the powder for simplicity)
1 egg
3 slices of stale bread

Directions:
Soak bread in water & squeeze out excess.  In a large bowl, add bread and all the remaining ingredients together (Except if using the clove of garlic. Saute garlic clove olive oil to flavor the oil. Use the flavored oil to cook meatballs in.) Form mixture into 2‚Äù meat balls or your preferred size. Brown in oil on medium heat for approximately 15-20 minutes (depending on meatball size) until done.  Serve with your favorite sauce and pasta.
