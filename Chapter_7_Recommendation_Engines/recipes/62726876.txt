Title:
Family Night Chicken Cornbread Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
1 onion, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn drained
1 (15 ounce) can cream-style corn
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1 (8.5 ounce) package corn bread mix
5 dashes Tabasco
2 1/3 cups chopped poached chicken breast
1 can green chile peppers, chopped
1 can Rotel drained and chopped
3/4 cups sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (8 ounce) package Monterey Jack cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease 9x13 inch baking dish.
Melt margarine in a small skillet over med-high heat. Saute onion and garlic until tender, 4 to 6 minutes; set aside. In a large bowl, combine corn, cream style corn, salt, tabasco and eggs. Beat in muffin mix. Fold in cooked onion mixture. Pour into prepared baking dish.
In a large bowl, combine chicken, green chiles, rotel, sour cream, cream of chicken soup, salt and pepper. Spoon over corn mixture to within 1 inch from edge.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, cover with cheese return to oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until edges are golden brown.

Description: 
This is a mildly spiced, Mexican Cornbread-inspired casserole with a cornbread bottom, and a spicy creamy chicken filling.Modified from: Chicken Suiza Cornbread Bake
