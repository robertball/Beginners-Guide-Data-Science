Title:
Black Dream Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977778.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Smirnoff® Black Cherry vodka
1 ounce Godiva® Cappuccino Liqueur
1/2 ounce milk
1 tablespoon grenadine

Directions:
Pour Smirnoff(R) Black Cherry Vodka, Godiva(R) Cappuccino Liqueur, milk, and grenadine into a shaker with ice.
Shake the cocktail and strain into a martini glass.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Add that bit of extra flavor to this or other cocktail recipes easily with the natural fruit flavors of Smirnoff® Flavored Vodkas.
