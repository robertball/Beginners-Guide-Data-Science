Title:
Peach Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ice
2 oz tequila
2 oz triple sec
3/4 cup peach nectar
1 cup of fresh or frozen peaches

Directions:
Add all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Pour and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Kim
Description: 
Very yummy adult summer treat. Subtle peach flavors.
