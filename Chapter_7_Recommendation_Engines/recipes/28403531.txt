Title:
Sesame Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
8oz. long fusilli
1 cup snow peas
1 cup shredded cooked chicken
1/2 cup chredded carrots
1 TBS. sesame seeds
1 scallion, thinly sliced
Dressing:
1/4 cup creamy penut butter
2 tsp. brown sugar
2 TBS. reduced sodium soy sauce
1 tsp. sesame oil
1 tsp. rice or apple-cider vinegar

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions; add snow peas to boiling water about 1 minute before pasta is done.  Drain and rinse under cool water.
Mix the peanut butter, brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil and rice vinegar in a large bowl.  Add the pasta and the snow peas; mix well.  Add chicken and carrots; toss to combine.  Top with sesame seeds and scallions.
