Title:
Green Beans Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Beans
2 boxes French Style Frozen Green Beans
2 tblsp butter
2 tbsp flour
1 c sour cream
1 tblsp parsley
1/2 tsp lemon zest
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
Topping
1/2 c grated cheddar cheese (sharp or mild)
2 tblsp butter
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs

Directions:
Cook beans until just tender. Saute onion and parsley in butter. Mix with all other ingredients and put in a greased casserole dish.
Add toping. Bake at 350 for about 20 minutes
Serves 4-6

Submitter: 
MSLIB5
Description: 
A much nicer alternative to traditional green beans casserole.
