Title:
Veggie White Deep Dish Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Loaf of Frozen Bread dough
2-3 Tblsp Olive Oil
Garlic Salt
2 Tblsp Dried or Fresh Basil
1/3 of an Onion chopped
1/2 cup fresh or 1 small can of sliced mushrooms drained
Ten Whole Black Olives cut in half
1/2 of a medium Zucchini sliced
1 small Tomato sliced
Black Pepper
1-1/2 Cups Shredded Colby Jack Cheese
1/3 Cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Unthaw frozen bread dough and let rise according to instructions
Spread dough out on the bottom of a greased round metal cake pan.
Brush Olive Oil on the dough so that it is evenly covered
Sprinkle dough with basil and garlic salt
Spread chopped onions on the dough
Lightly cover the rest of the dough with the remaining chopped veggie ingredients.
Sprinkle black pepper and some garlic salt over veggies
Sprinkle colby jack cheese over veggies
Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on last
Bake at 400 degrees for 25 minutes or until crust looks medium brown and pizza looks done.
Cut into squares and serve.
Very good the next day too.
(If you don't like so many toppings, you can adjust the amount you put on the pizza to your liking)

Submitter: 
Heidi Habeck-Johnson
Description: 
White Veggie Pizza uses thawed frozen bread dough and veggie toppings. It is quick and easy and so good!
