Title:
Spicy Beef Cous Cous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T cooking oil
1 1/2 lbs beef in 1/2" cubes or larger (marinade in a little soy
sauce, Worcestershire, and red wine vinegar)
2 medium onions, thinly sliced
1 small shallot (if available)
1 medium jalepeno pepper, diced
Water (see couscous pkg for amount)
1 can (6oz.) tomato paste/sauce
Salt and Pepper
Steak Seasoning
Pinch of Cinnamon
1 cup couscous

Directions:
Brown beef in oil and marinade then set aside.
Add onion and pepper
and saute until tender.
Add water, beef, tomato paste, salt, pepper, allspice, and
cinnamon
Simmer, covered, until meat is tender.
Stir in couscous,bring to a light boil, reduce heat and simmer until cous cous starts to come to surface.
Add water or broth if too dry.
