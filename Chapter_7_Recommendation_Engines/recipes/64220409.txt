Title:
Caledon Baked Oatmeal (Eat Clean Diet) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups/220g of Old Fashioned Oats
2 Cups of 35 Cal/cup almond milk
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2C (65g) of slivered almonds
1/4C (40g) of dry cranberries
1/4C (30g) of raisens
1 large unpeeled apple, grated
4Tbsp maple syrup
*Optional - 1/2tsp cinnamon
1 tsp of oil to grease casserole dish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F
Grease casserole dish
Mix all ingredients in bowl (if premixing, leave out moist ingredients till ready to cook)
Add to dish and bake for 45 mins (uncovered)

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
This is so good and would be perfect for xmas morning or when you are having a large group for breakfast
