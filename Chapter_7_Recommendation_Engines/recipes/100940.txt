Title:
Quick Pizza with Thai Flavorings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987815.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust, such as Boboli
1 tablespoon creamy peanut butter
1/3 cup Thai peanut sauce
1 1/2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
1/2 thin-sliced red bell pepper
1 large carrot, peeled and shredded
4 green onions, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Adjust oven rack to center position and preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Whisk peanut sauce and peanut butter in a medium bowl until smooth. Spread sauce over the pizza crust. Spread chicken then the red pepper over pizza; bake until hot and crisp, 12 to 14 minutes. Remove pizza and sprinkle with carrots, green onion and cilantro. Cut into pieces and serve.

Submitter: 
Pam Anderson
Description: 
This is a unique and tasty combination of Thai sauce, chicken, fresh carrots, green onions and cilantro.
