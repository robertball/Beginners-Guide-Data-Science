Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu with lucious pineapple sherry sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken breasts
6 slices deli ham
2 cups swiss cheese, grated
1 egg
1 1/2 tsp oil
1 tbl water
1/2 cup flour
1 cup bread crumbs
sauce
1 can crushed pineapple (14oz)
1 cup brown sugar
1 tbl lemon juice
1 tbl cornstarch
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup sherry
4 tbl butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. 1. Pound chicken breasts to approximately 1/4" thickness (without tearing), top with 1 slice ham and 1/3 cup cheese.  Roll up carefully, tucking in ends as you roll. 2. Combine egg, oil, and water in a small bowl.  Roll chicken in flour, then in egg mixture and then in bread crumbs. 3. Place in baking pan and bake for 45 mins. 4. While chicken is baking, prepare sauce by combining pineapple, brown sugar and lemon juice.  Cook on medium heat until bubbly; turn down heat to simmer. 5.  Add cornstarch with water slowly until slightly thick. 6. Add sherry and butter. Serve hot over cooked chicken.

Submitter: 
Lorriane Clarkson
Description: 
This sauce is what makes this different from your regular cordon bleu. Everyone, with much different tastes loves this.  It can take a little while to make but can be make ahead and popped in the oven the day of.
