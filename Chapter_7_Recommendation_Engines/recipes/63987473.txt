Title:
AnnieBells peach cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pillsbury pie crust
2 cups brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
1 stick salted butter
1Tablespoon cinnamon
2 cans sliced peaches in heavy syrup
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ginger
Nutmeg to taste
1/3 cup water

Directions:
Drain peaches
Mix dry ingredients blend together until smooth
Add juice from peaches with dry ingredients
Add 1/2 stick of melted butter
Add 1/3 cup of water
Mix with spoon until sugar is dissolved
Spread mixture onto pie crust
Cover with other pie crust
Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon
Cut pieces of butter on top of cobbler
Bake on 375 degrees until crust browns lightly approximatley 45 minutes
