Title:
Spinach Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz bag of frozen Spinach
1 cup egg beaters or 4 eggs
2 tsp. minced garlic
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp dill weed
1/4 cup ff parm cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
spray cookie sheet with non-stick spray
Thaw and drain spinach-squeeze dry
mix egg beaters with spinach
minced garlic, salt, ff cheese and dill weed
Press evenly into cookie sheet
Bake for 15 min check to see if set
turn in oven and bake another 10 min until dry and set.
If using for pizza crust top with your favorite legal toppings
If using as bread let cool and cut into squares
really good with tomato lettuce and chicken breast
I have also used for breakfast under poached egg and feta cheese.
