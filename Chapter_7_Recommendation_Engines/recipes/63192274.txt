Title:
Rancho Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/823532.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
2 cups chopped onion
2 (1 lb.) cans pork and beans
1 (1 lb.) can kidney beans, drained
3/4 cups brown sugar
1 cup ketchup
2 TBSP mustard
1 TBSP white vinegar
1 TSP salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Brown the meat in a skillet with the chopped onion until meat is cooked through and onions are translucent.  Drain well.  Combine the other ingredients well in a large bowl, add the browned meat and onion mixture.  Mix well.  Spray a casserole dish well with non-stick spray.  Pour mixture into the dish, bake uncovered for 45 minutes.  Freezes well.

Submitter: 
SusanV.
Description: 
Great dish to serve at a BBQ or summer dinner.  Easy and tasty!  This recipe has been used by the women on my husband's side of the family for years.
