Title:
The BEST Carrot Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs carrots
3 eggs + 1 egg white
3 TBS flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp vanilla extract
4 tbsp butter (melted)
pinch cinnamon and nutmeg
Topping:
1 c. crushed corn flakes
6 TBSP brown sugar
4 TBSP melted butter
3/4 c. crushed pecans

Directions:
Steam carrots until soft.
Puree them in a food processor.
Add eggs and egg white one at a time and process.
Add remaining ingredients and blend well.
Pour into a souffle or casserole dish.
Ina  seperate bowl, Mix all topping ingredients except butter with a fork. Add melted butter and mix with fork.
Spread topping over casserole and bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes or until set.

Submitter:

Description: 
This sweet and delicious souffle is sure to become a holiday favortie, my family fights over who is to take home the leftovers! The topping sets this recipe apart!  Steam the carrotts the day before to save time on your holiday.
