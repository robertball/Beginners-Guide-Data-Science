Title:
Lunchbox Pita Pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 circle of pocket bread (pita), cut in 2
1/2 cup lunchmeat, chopped
1/2 cup shredded lettuce
1/4 cup shredded carrot
1/4 cup ranch dressing

Directions:
Put lunchmeat, lettuce and carrot together in a ziploc bag.
Put salad dressing in a small tupperware container.
Make sure to put a plastic spoon in the lunchbox, so kids can spoon lettuce mixture into pita and dressing on top.
Variations:
-chopped pepperoni, mozzarella cheese, and pizza sauce.
-chopped bacon, shredded lettuce, chopped tomato, and ranch dressing.
-sliced apples and peanut butter.

Submitter: 
GivingTree
Description: 
Kids can assemble these pita meals themselves, right there at the lunch table!
