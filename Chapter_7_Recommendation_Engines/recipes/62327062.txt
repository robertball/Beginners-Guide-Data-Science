Title:
Meatball Nirvana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb pound extra lean ground beef
1/2 lb pound lean ground pork
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1 small onion, diced
2 -3 cloves fresh garlic mashed in garlic press
1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
3/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1 Tablespoon fresh Italian basil
1 1/2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1/3 cup  milk - or ricotta cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Place the beef into a mixing bowl, and season with salt, onion, garlic, Italian seasoning, oregano,  and Worcestershire sauce; mix well. Add the milk, Parmesan cheese, and bread crumbs. Mix until evenly blended, then form into 1 1/2-inch meatballs, and place onto a baking sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 20 to 25 minutes.
