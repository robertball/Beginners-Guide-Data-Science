Title:
Sweet Potato and Black Pepper Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c flour
2t baking powder
1t sugar
1/2t black pepper
1/2 t baking soda
1/4t salt
3/4c butter
1c sweet potato, cooked (or pumpkin or winter squash)
2T heavy cream

Directions:
Heat oven to 425.
Combine flour, baking powder,salt, pepper, and soda. Cut in butter until coarse crumbs.
Stir in pureed potato and cream until dough is soft.
Turn out on a floured surface and knead turning over about 5 times.
Roll out to a 1/2" thickness, cut out circles about 2" in diameter.
Place on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for 12-15 min, or until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
Sweet and peppery biscuits, great for ham sandwiches with a little honey mustard.
