Title:
Creamy Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz. package Italian seasoned grilled chicken breast strips
1 15 oz. can "Garlic & Olive Oil" flavored petite diced tomatoes (or any Italian seasoned variety)
1 8oz tub soft spreadable Chive & Onion Cream Cheese
8 oz Penne, Fusilli, Bowtie, or other small shaped pasta, cooked and drained
Fresh grated Parmesan cheese and fresh cracked pepper, if desired

Directions:
Dice chicken strips into bite-sized pieces. In nonstick skillet over low heat, combine undrained tomatoes and cream cheese.  Cook, stirring constantly, until cream cheese is melted and sauce is creamy.  Add chicken strips and heat thoroughly.  Add hot cooked and drained pasta and toss to coat.  Top each serving with freshly grated parmesan cheese and fresh cracked pepper, if desired.

Submitter: 
Christine Hanson
Description: 
Packaged grilled chicken strips, canned tomatoes and flavored cream cheese combine in a sauce so rich and flavorful you won't believe you did it with 3 ingredients in 10 minutes!
