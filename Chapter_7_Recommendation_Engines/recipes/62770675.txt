Title:
Macaroni Ham Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked elbow macaroni
7 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, diced
1 cup diced fully cooked ham
1 cup chopped dill pickles (or sweet relish)
3 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 cup celery, chopped
1 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

Directions:
Combine mayonnaise and mustard in a bowl, then add remaining ingrediants. Toss well, cover and chill until serving.
