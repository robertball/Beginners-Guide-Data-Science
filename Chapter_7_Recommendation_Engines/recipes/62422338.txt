Title:
Spezziello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
3 tbsp flour
1/2 tsp salt
3 beaten egg yolks
1 3/4 c pineapple juice (or more)
1 pkg pastina or spezziello macaroni
2 small cans mandarin oranges, reserve juice
1 tall can pineapple tidbits, reserve juice
9 oz cool whip
small pkg mini marshmallows

Directions:
Cook sugar, flour, salt, egg yolks, juice until custard-like.  Cook pasta as directed and DRAIN WELL.  Pour sauce over and mix well.  Store in fridge overnight.  Next day add chopped and drained mandarin oranges and pineapple tidbits.  May need to add some of the juice.  One hour before serving add cool whip and marshmallows.  Mix well.

Submitter: 
chefmom
Description: 
A sweet refreshing salad, made with tiny pasta, cool whip, fruit and marshmallows.
