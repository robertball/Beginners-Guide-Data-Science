Title:
Spudnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mashed potatoes
1 cup sugar
1/3 cup butter
2 tsp. salt
6 eggs
4 cups milk, scalded
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. lemon extract
12 cups flour
4 TBSP dry yeast
Glaze
2 lbs. powdered sugar
2/3 cup butter
1/3 cup water, scant

Directions:
Mix potatoes, sugar, butter, salt into milk.  Add yeast and stir. Add eggs, flavorings and beat well. Add flour and mix well. Let raise 30 minutes. Roll out and cut into donut shape.
Let rise until double in bulk. Fry in 370 degree oil (add 1/2 tsp. of ground ginger to oil to prevent grease from soaking into the donuts).
Glaze
Melt butter in water, then add powdered sugar and beat well.
