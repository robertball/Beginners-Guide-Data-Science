Title:
Autumn Warm Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bottle brandy
1 bottle muscato
1 bottle pear wine
3/4 gal apple cider
6 cinnamon sticks
8 whole cloves
1 apple
1 orange
1 pear
cranberries

Directions:
add all liquid ingredients into slow cooker
cute up apples, oranges and pears into small bits (no need to peel oranges). add to slow cooker with a handful of cranberries
heat at high, then set to warm
