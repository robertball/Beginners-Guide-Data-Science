Title:
Creamy  Spinach Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium potatoes thinly sliced
3 cups baby spinach
4 oz. Neufchatel cheese
1/4 C grated parmesan cheese
2 tbs soft butter, divided
2 tsp lemon pepper, divided

Directions:
In a microwave safe 8 x 8 dish layer half of the potato slices along the bottom of the dish.  Dollop 1 tbs of softened butter randomly and sprinkle 1 tsp of lemon pepper over potatoes.
Cut Neufchatel cheese into 6 sections and place on top of the potatoes.
Next, add the spinach and top with parmesan cheese.
Layer on the remaining potatoe's slices and top with remaining butter and lemon pepper.
Loosely cover and place in the microwave on high for 7 minutes.
Check potatoes with a fork for softness.  Cook in increments of 2 minutes until potatoes are cooked and cheese is melted.  Mix well just before serving.
