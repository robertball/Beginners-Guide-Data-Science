Title:
Tuna Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz cream cheese  4 -6 oz cans tuna 1 large onion chopped fine 1cup mayonnaise stalks celery minced 2 pkts gelatin 1can tomato soup

Directions:
dissolve gelatin in soup let cool.Beat cream cheese mayo. Add onion and celery and tuna.Mix with soup and spread in fish mold.put in refrigerator for 1hr and chill
