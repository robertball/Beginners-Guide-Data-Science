Title:
Chicken Puttanesca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448406.jpg
Ingredients:
olive oil
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves.salt & pepper, to taste
1/4 teaspoon red chile flakes, or to taste
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 pint cherry tomatoes -- halved
1 tablespoon fresh oregano leaves
2/3 cup kalamata olives (Greek olives)-- pittted
Fresh mozzarella cheese -- sliced

Directions:
Heat oven to 450 F. Heat oil in a large ovenproof frying pan over high heat.
Sprinkle chicken with S&P.. Brown chicken in hot oil. Add chile flakes, garlic, tomatoes, oregano, and olives.
Transfer pan to oven and cook until chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes. Lay cheese over chicken and bake just until melted and browned.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
This dish is loosely based on pasta puttenesca, except for the addition of chicken. It is a tamer version of Puttenesca - no capers and/or anchovies.
