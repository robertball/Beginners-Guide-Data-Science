Title:
Sweet Peanut Butter Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/601216.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8-ounce)pkg fat-free cream cheese
1 (7-ounce)jar marshmellow creme
1 (8-ounce)jar plain greek yogurt
1 (14-ounce)can fat-free sweetened condensed milk
1 cup low-fat peanut butter

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream the cream cheese well, until soft and smooth.
Add remaining ingredients to the creamed cheese, mixing thoroughly until smooth and creamy.
Cover and chill in refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
Serve with an assortment of fruits and/or cookies.

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
A light and fluffy dip for fruit or cookies.
