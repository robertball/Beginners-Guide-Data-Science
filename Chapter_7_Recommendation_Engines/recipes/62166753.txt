Title:
Strassburgare Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/752098.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cubes of butter, softened
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1 1/2 T. vanilla sugar
1 c. all purpose flour
1/2 cup potato flour
food coloring - optional

Directions:
Prehaet oven to 325
Beat the butter and the powdered sugar until creamy.  Blend in vanilla sugar and set aside.
In another bowl, mix the flour and the potato flour.
Gradually add this mixture to the butter sugar mixture and mix until blended
Color the cookie dough if desired
Place dough in a cookie press and press into desired shapes onto an ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake for approzimately 10 minutes - You don't want these to brown but you don't want them doughy.
Let cool on cookie sheets a few minutes then tranfer to wire racks to cool completely
You don't want these to brown

Submitter: 
Linda T
Description: 
Delicate and mouth watering Swedish cookies. Great for holidays or anytime. Vanilla sugar and potato flour is available at a good German or Scandnavian deli
