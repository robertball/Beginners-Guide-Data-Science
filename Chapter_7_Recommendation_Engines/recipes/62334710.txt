Title:
Straight to the oven Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/387269.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups white whole wheat flour or unbleached all purpose flour
2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons honey
2 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)

Directions:
Combine water, honey, and yeast in a large bowl. Allow yeast to dissolve and start to ferment, about 5 minutes.  Add in oil, salt and flour. Divide into 2 pieces. Lightly spray pizza pans with non stick spray. Press dough into the 2 10" pizza pans.  Top as desired.
Bake at 500 degrees C (190 degrees C) for 15 to 20  minutes, move move to lowest rack for 5 minutes for crisp crust.

Description: 
Quick to put together, this dough does not need to rise but goes straight from the mixing bowl to teh pizza pan. Makes enough for 2 10 pizzas.Modified from: Pizza Dough I
