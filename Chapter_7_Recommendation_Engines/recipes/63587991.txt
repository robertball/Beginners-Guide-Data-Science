Title:
Albondigas-  with Turkey Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup long grain brown rice
1 Tbsp sunflower, EVOO, or coconut oil
1 onion chopped
2 stalks celery cut into 1/4 inch pieces
1 large carrot- diced
4 cloves garlic- minced
4 cups water- add chicken base to taste
1 24- 26 oz jar of chopped tomatoes. (I used the ones we froze from our garden last fall.)
1/2 jalapeno pepper- (I used 1 1/2 peppers for more heat.)
1 lb lean ground turkey
1/2 cup chopped fresh mint
1 large egg
fresh ground pepper to taste
sea salt to taste
1 cup fresh or frozen peas
3/4 cup coarsely chopped cilantro to garnish

Directions:
Make rice in advance.
Meanwhile, in a large bowl, heat oil on medium. Add onion and cook for 2 minutes. Stir occasionally.Add celery, carrot, and garlic adn saute until crisp tender. Add broth, tomatoes and jalapenos. Increse heat and simmer.
To turkey, add rice, mint, egg, black pepper and sea salt. Stir gently and shape into small meatballs.
Reduce heat, add meatballs to soup one at a time.Simmer, stirring gently until meat balls are cooked through- about 30 minutes. Add peas and cook 5 more minutes.
Add salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
