Title:
Fresh Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978975.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sliced celery
2 cups thinly sliced cauliflower
2 cups halved cherry tomatoes
2 cups thinly sliced carrots
2 cups sliced cucumber
1 medium onion, thinly sliced, separated into rings
3/4 cup olive or vegetable oil
1/2 cup minced fresh parsley
3 tablespoons white wine vinegar or cider vinegar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground mustard
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 garlic clove, minced

Directions:
In a large serving bowl, combine the celery, cauliflower, tomatoes, carrots, cucumber and onion. In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine the remaining ingredients. Pour over vegetables and toss gently. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight. Serve with a slotted spoon.

Submitter: 
Harriet  Stichter
Description: 
This crisp colorful salad is full of garden goodness. The light dressing lets the fresh flavor of the vegetables come through. For variety, I sometimes substitute sliced zucchini for the cucumber. -Harriet Stichter, Milford, Indiana
