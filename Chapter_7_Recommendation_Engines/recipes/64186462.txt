Title:
Vicksburg Brunch Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups toasted rice  cereal, divided
2 lbs ground sausage, hot or mild
2 tbsp chopped onion
2 cups cooked rice
2 (10-oz) packages sharp cheddar cheese, grated
2 (10 3/4-oz) cans cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1/2 cup milk
6 eggs, slightly beaten

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 casserole dish.  Spread 3 cups cereal in dish.  Brown and drain sausage and onion.  Layer sausage and onion, cooked rice, and cheese in dish.  Combine soup, milk, and eggs.  Poke holes all over casserole.  Pour coup mixture over top.  Sprinkle top with remaining 1 cup cereal.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
