Title:
Maidrite Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds hamburger
2 T sugar
1 T vinegar
1 pint tomato juice
water
onion salt
celery salt
2-3 ounces soy sauce or Worcestershire sauce
1 cup oatmeal
3 teaspoons chili powder

Directions:
Brown burger and place in crock pot. Add other ingredients. Cook on low for 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
Almost as good as a real maidrite.
