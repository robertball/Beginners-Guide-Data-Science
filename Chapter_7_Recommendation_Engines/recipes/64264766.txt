Title:
A Very Special Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup onion, diced 1/2 " to 3/4" size pieces
1/4 cup red bell pepper, diced
1/4 cup diced celery, very fine
1/4-1/2 cup Anaheim or Pasilla chiles, chopped
1 - 2 Tablespoons Jalapenos, seeded,  diced
1 Tablespoon Cayenne, seeded, diced
1 Tablespoon minced garlic
1 14 oz can fire-roasted diced tomatoes w. green peppers, drained
1/2 of small can of tomato paste
1 12-14 oz. can beef broth
6 Tablespoons of California or New Mexico chili powder (mild Anaheim or Numex Pasillas)
1 Tablespoon Hot New Mexico, De Arbol or Cayenne Chili powder
2 Tablespoons San Antonio or Texas Chili powder
1 Tsp. salt
1 Tsp. Tabasco or Cholula sauce
2-3 lbs. Tri-tip beef (Best) or Sirloin Tip Roast (Better) or Flank Steak (Fair), cubed into 3/4 to 1" pieces
1/4 Lb. Ground Pork Sausage
1 Can dark beer
1 Tablespoon Cocoa powder
2 Tablespoons Cumin
2 Tablespoons Lime Juice

Directions:
*Mix up onion,garlic, chile pepper mixture well in bowl and saute in 1 Tablespoon veg. oil until tender
*Add can of diced tomatoes,tomato past, broth, 4 of the Tablespoons chili powder, salt and hot sauce to the sauteed onion/pepper mixture and simmer on very low for 30-45 minutes
*In large skillet, brown beef and pork, drain and set aside
*Add remaning 2 Tablespoons California Chili powder with 2 Tablespoons Texas Chili powder and 1 Tablespoon Hot Chili powder to pot
*Add Beef and Pork Sausage, simmer for 30 minutes
*Add 1/2 can dark beer to pot
*Add any remaining chile powder, 2 Tablespoons Cumin and 1 Tablespoon Cocoa powder to pot and simmer another 30-40 minutes
*Add 2 Tablespoons Lime juice
*Cook for another 30 minutes, adjusting as necessary for a 'saucy' texture rather than a watery' texture.
Enjoy with your favorite toppings, cheese, sourcream, etc. Know that you are eating Real Honest-to-God Chili.
