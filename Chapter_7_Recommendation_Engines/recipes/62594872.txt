Title:
Cantonese Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/570400.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 eggs, lightly beaten with salt and pepper
2 tablespoon butter
2 tablespoon shallots, finely chopped
1 glove garlic, minced
3 green onions, tops and greens chopped
4 cup cooked white rice
2 teaspoon soy sauce
2 teaspoon Dynasty Cantonese fried rice seasoning mix
1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
1/4 cup real bacon bits or 3 strips of bacon (pre-cooked and crumbled)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet, med/high heat. Add eggs and scramble until cooked. Scoop out eggs to place in a paper plate and set aside. Scrape off the bits that are stuck to the skillet with paper towel.(Be careful its hot).
In the same skillet, turn down the heat on low/medium, add butter to be melted then add shallots and garlic until fragrant.
Add green onions and give it a quick stir, then add rice, soy sauce and seasonings, stirring to combine.
Add peas, bacon, and put back the eggs. Heat thoroughly.

Description: 
My secret is to cheat with the addition of Dynasty's Cantonese fried rice seasoning mix. It gives it an extra oomph to your cooked or leftover rice.
