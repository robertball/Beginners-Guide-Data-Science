Title:
Oven Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077243.jpg
Ingredients:
6 med. baking potatoes, sliced lengthwise w/ skins on
1/2 c oil
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp paprika

Directions:
Mix ingredients & pour over potatoes on cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 for 45 mins or until done.
