Title:
Blueberry Cheesecake Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1125563.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1 8 oz block cream cheese, softened
1 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs, beaten
1 C. Fresh blueberries
1/2 C. Table cream or half and half

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease  a 12 cup muffin pan. Set aside
Blend flour, b. powder and b. soda together in a small bowl.  Set aside
Beat eggs with an electric mixer on medium speed until light and frothy
In a large mixing bowl soften cream cheese and then add granulated sugar to the cheese and mix well to form a smooth paste
Add beaten eggs to cream cheese/sugar mixture and beat well with mixer for about 4 to 5 minutes to make a smooth creamy batter
Fold table cream and blueberries into mixture and mix until just combined
Add dry ingredients to wet mixture and mix until all the flour has been absorbed into the batter
Pour batter into greased muffin cups filling 2/3 full and bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes or until lightly browned
Remove from oven and allow to cool before removing from pan to serving plate

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very light muffin with juicy blueberries that give the muffins a nice fruity flavour for breakfast or brunch.
