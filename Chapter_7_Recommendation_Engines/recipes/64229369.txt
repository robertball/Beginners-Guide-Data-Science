Title:
Lumpia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 onion, minced
1 lb ground beef
1 lb ground pork
3 potatoes, peeled and finely diced
1 can French cut green beans, canned
1 can bean sprouts, drained
4 Tbsp soy sauce
1/8 tsp black pepper
36 lumpia wrappers
Oil for frying

Directions:
In large pot, saute garlic and onion in oil.  Add ground beef and ground pork and cook until brown.  Add potatoes and cook for 5-10 minutes until tender.  Drain off fat.
Add remaining ingredients and mix.  Heat through.
Place mixture in lumpia wrappers and roll up like egg rolls.  Seal edges with water.
Fry in oil until brown and crispy.
