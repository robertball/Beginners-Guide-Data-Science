Title:
Stuffed Pasta Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 (16 ounce) package jumbo pasta shells
* 4 cups cottage cheese
* 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
* 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
* 2 eggs, lightly beaten
* 1 teaspoon garlic powder
* 1 teaspoon dried oregano
* 1 teaspoon dried parsley
* 1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce (or more for added moisture)
* 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
* 1-2 cans (or frozen) of spinach with liquid squeezed out.
* 1 lb. hamburger fried and drained

Directions:
1)Cook shells according to package directions. Place in cold water to stop the cooking process. Drain.
2)Mix together the cottage cheese, mozzarella cheese, 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese, eggs, and garlic powder. Rub the dried herbs in the palms of your hands to pulverize them, add to mixture and add the spinach and stir well. Stuff the mixture into the shells.
3)In another bowl add the spaghetti sauce and fried hamburger and stir well.
4)Spread some spaghetti sauce in bottom of pan and add stuffed shells on top. Spread remaining sauce mixture over shells and top with extra Parmesan cheese and mozzarella cheese.
5)Bake at 350 degrees F for 25 minutes to 35 minutes, or until bubbly. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
