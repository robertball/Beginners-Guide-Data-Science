Title:
Rockin's Ground Turkey Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons olive oil
1 onion chopped
3 pounds ground turkey
1-12 ounce can of tomato sauce
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup vinegar
1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon hot sauce
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute onions in oil till they just start to brown on the edges. Add Turkey to onion mixture and brown. Break up all the meat clumps. Once the moisture is cooked out and the meat is browned add the rest of the ingredients. Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This Sloppy Joe recipe is high in nutrition and big on taste! A half cup serving is 160 calories and has 15 grams protein.
