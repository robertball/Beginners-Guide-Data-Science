Title:
Hot Artichoke Goat Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1097398.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 oz) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 cup lite mayonnaise (I recommened Hellman's)
1 cup lite sour cream
4 oz goat cheese, softened
1/4 C shredded parmesan cheese + more for topping
1/2 t Italian seasoning
Salt & pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a large bowl combine artichoke hearts, mayo, sour cream, goat cheese, 1/4 C parmesan, Italian seasoning, salt & pepper.
Mix well leaving some texture.
Spread into an oven safe pie pan/ small casserole like bowl.
Bake uncovered for 25-30 mins (checking at 20 mins).
Serve with fresh baguette slices.

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
Besides the taste you'll enjoy the smell of this dip when it comes bubbling out of the oven! I like to use lite (not non fat) ingredients to lighten up this hearty dip a tad. It's incredibly easy and comes together in a snap!
