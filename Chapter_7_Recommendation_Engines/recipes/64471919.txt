Title:
Toasted Pistachio Muesli with Apple and Fig Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups rolled oats
1 cup roasted pistachios
1/2 cup pure maple syrup
1/4 cup poppy seeds
1/4 cup sesame seeds
2 tsp pure vanilla extract
1 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp fine salt

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine oats, pistachios, maple syrup, poppy and sesame seeds, vanilla extract, ginger, and salt.
Bake on 2 rimmed baking sheets, stirring and rotating once, until golden brown, 18-24 minutes.
Serve 3/4 cup muesli with milk, almond milk, or plain yogurt, topped with sliced apples and figs.

Submitter:

Description: 
This muesli makes it easy and delicious to eat more whole grains!
