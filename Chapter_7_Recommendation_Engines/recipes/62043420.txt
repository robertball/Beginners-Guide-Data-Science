Title:
Sour Cream Pear pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Graham cracker crust
(filling)
2 15 oz. cans pears, drained
3/4 C. sugar
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1 egg, beaten
1 Tbs flour
1 C. sour cream
dash of salt
(struesel topping)
1/2 C. sugar
2/3 C/ flour
1/4 C. butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Drain pears and dice them. Combine all filling ingredients. Pour into crust. Bake for 25 min, at 350. Combine sugar and flour for topping. Cut in butter until crumbly. Take pie out and sprinkle the struesel topping all over pie. Return to oven for an additional 30 minutes. Cool. Served chilled.

Submitter: 
Tanya Belt
Description: 
This Pie is a favorite with my family.  I make it alot for mother's day and birthdays.  It's a different flavor, and a nice change from the ordinary.
