Title:
Pumpkin Pie Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1/8 teaspoon salt
1-3/4 cup half and half
1 container egg substitute like Egg Beaters(equiv. to 2 eggs)
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup canned pumpkin
1 teaspoon ginger
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Combine first six ingredients in a large bowl.
In a small bowl, combine canned pumpkin, ginger, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
Add pumpkin mixture to large bowl, stirring until combined.
Refrigerate until ready to pour into one and a half quart ice cream maker.
Freeze until desired consistency, or until ice cream maker stops churning.

Submitter: 
Stephanie Zartman
Description: 
Creamy pumpkin pie flavored ice cream for a one and a half quart ice cream maker
