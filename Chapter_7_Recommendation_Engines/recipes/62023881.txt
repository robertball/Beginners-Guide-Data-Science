Title:
Butterflied Chicken Breasts with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 boneless chicken breasts
1/2 cup flour
salt & pepper
1 1/2 sticks butter
lemon juice
1 1/2 lbs fresh sliced mushrooms
1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Lightly spray a large baking dish with cooking spray.
Pound each piece of chicken between wax paper until thin like a cutlet. Season flour with salt and pepper and dredge each piece of chicken in flour. Shake off excess.
Melt 1 stick of butter in a large skillet and saute each piece of chicken 1-2 minutes, drizzle 4 to 5 drops of lemon juice on each side of chicken in skillet. Transfer chicken to baking dish. Set aside.
Melt remaining 1/2 stick butter with the remaining butter in skillet and saute mushrooms in skillet until soft. Spoon mushrooms and all drippings from skillet over chicken in baking dish. Pour cream over chicken and mushrooms. Sprinkle with Parmesan.
Bake uncovered 25-30 minutes.
