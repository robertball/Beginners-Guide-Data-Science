Title:
Cheryl's Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large heads cabbage, chopped (I used two bags already chopped!)
1 large onion, chopped ( I used 1 bunch of green onion finely chopped)
1 green or red bell pepper, chopped
1 tbsp black pepper
1 tbsp salt ( I used 1/2)
1 tbsp celery seed
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
3 carrots, shredded
1 cup mayonnaise
3-4 tbsps sugar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl, cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
I put the dressing on about an hour before dinner.
