Title:
Angel Food Cake With Light Lemon Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4472774.jpg
Ingredients:
Angel food cake
1 pkg. Jello lemon pie filling
Vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Mix pie filling and let set in fridge.
Once filling is set, use hand mixer to slowly mix in vanilla ice cream to desired consistency.
