Title:
Parmesan Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. large shrimp
1 cup bread crumps or panko
2 tablespoons dried parsley
1 & 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder or to taste
1 cup shredded parmesan
2-3 tablespoons of melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees & spray 9 x 13 glass casserole dish with non-stick spray. Peel and devein shrimp. In large zip lock bag combine bread crumbs, parsley, garlic powder, & parmesan. Shake to mix ingredients together. Add shrimp and shake to coat shrimp. Lay shrimp flat in dish. Sprinkle with remaining bread crumb cheese mixture from bag. Drizzle with melted butter- melt more if needed. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes. Then Broil for a couple of minutes until topping is light brown. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Perri
Description: 
Delicious crispy shrimp coated in a bread crumb parmesan mixture. My family can't get enough of it! I would suggest doubling recipe if you have very large eaters because this is addictive! Serve with pasta and salad.
