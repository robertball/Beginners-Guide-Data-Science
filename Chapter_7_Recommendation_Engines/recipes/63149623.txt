Title:
Avacado pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 peeled avocados
.5 cup fresh lemon juice
14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
9" graham cracker crust
whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
In a blender put the peeled avocado and .5 cup lemon juice. Pulse a couple of times.
Add condensed milk and blend till creamy.
Pour into the pie shell and refrigerate for 1 hour.
Add whipped cream when serving.
