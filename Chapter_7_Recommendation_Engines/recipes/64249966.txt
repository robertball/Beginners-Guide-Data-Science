Title:
Spicy Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1777109.jpg
Ingredients:
½ C unsalted butter, melted
1 C butter flavored shortening, melted
2 C lightly packed brown sugar
½ C molasses
2 eggs
1t salt
1T baking soda
1T cinnamon
1Tginger
1t cloves
½ t cayenne pepper
4 C all purpose flour
coarse sugar for rolling

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients except flour until combined and glossy
Fold in flour.
Divide into 48 balls and roll in sugar.
Bake at 375 for nine minutes or until the tops crack.
