Title:
Caramel Corn with Peanut M&M;'s Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. bag peanut M&M;’s
2/3 cup salted cocktail peanuts (no skins)
1 cup brown sugar
1 stick butter (not margarine)
1/8 tsp. cream of tarter
¼ cup light corn syrup
Dash salt
½ tsp. baking soda
5-1/2 T. popcorn  POP IN AIR POPPER

Directions:
Combine all ingredients – except candy, nuts, popcorn and baking soda.
Bring to boil.  Once mixture is boiling, turn to low and let set for 5 minutes.
Remove from heat and add ½ tsp. soda.
Put nuts and popped popcorn in a 9 x 13 or on a cookie sheet and pour mixture over top.  Stir and bake for 1 hr. at 250 degrees.  Stir every 15 minutes.
Will be sticky at end of 1 hour, but will harden.
When still SLIGHTLY WARM, add M&M’s.
