Title:
Chicken Tostadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 chicken breast (depending on # of people)-baked or boiled and shredded.
3-5 roma tomatoes-diced
1/3 onion-diced
1/2-1 jalapeño-sliced into rings (optional)
1 can refried black beans-canned or homemade
shredded lettuce
sour cream
mexican cheese (queso blanco)
hot sauce (optional)
tostadas-homemade or store bought

Directions:
Fry your tomato, onion, and jalapeño in 1 Tblsp. veg. oil about 10 minutes. Add chicken and heat thoroughly. Remove from heat. On tostada, smear refried black beans, pile on the chicken mixture, add the lettuce cheese and sour cream to your liking. Top with hot sauce.
To make homemade tostadas, simply fry corn tortillas in about 1/2 to 1 inch of very hot vegetable oil. Place on paper towel or rack to drain. salt while still hot.

Submitter: 
shana dominguez
Description: 
I originally made this recipe using plain chicken breast, but found that it turned out somewhat boring, but by frying some onion, tomato, and jalapeño in with the chicken, it changed the flavor completely. Now its a family favorite!
