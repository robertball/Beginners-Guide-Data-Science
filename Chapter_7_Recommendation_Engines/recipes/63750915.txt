Title:
Brisket with Coffee and Rum Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-7 lb brisket, first-cut
4 c onion, diced
4 c red bell pepper, diced
4 cloves garlic, peeled
2 c strong brewed coffee or espresso
3/4 c dark rum (Bacardi select)
1/4 c olive oil
beef broth
2 t salt
1 1/2 t pepper
4 t cornstarch

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300F.
Rub brisket with salt and pepper.
Heat 2 T oil in roasting pan on stovetop. Brown brisket on all sides. Remove brisket.
Heat remaining 2 T oil in roasting pan. Saute onions, peppers, garlic until onion is transparent.
With vegetables still in pan, deglaze with rum and coffee.
Return brisket to pan and cover with vegetables. Add beef broth until level is half the height of the brisket.
Cover with foil, tenting as necessary to keep foil off food.
Roast undisturbed 5-7 hours, 1 hour per pound.
Cool completely and refrigerate overnight.
Remove cold brisket and slice. Make gravy with defatted pan juices and cornstarch. Return brisket to pan and cover with gravy and vegetables.
Reheat brisket in pan, foil covered, 45-60 min in 350F oven.
