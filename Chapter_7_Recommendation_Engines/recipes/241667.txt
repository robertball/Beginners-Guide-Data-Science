Title:
Salted Caramel S'more Brownie Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2155895.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package Duncan Hines® Salted Caramel Brownie Mix
1 cup mini marshmallows
1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Line two 24-count mini-muffin pans with baking cups.
Set aside salted caramel pouch.
Prepare the brownie batter as directed on the back of the box.
Use a spoon and fill the baking cup half way with the brownie batter.
Push 3 mini marshmallows halfway down into the center of the batter.
Sprinkle graham cracker crumbs over the marshmallows.
Bake until the marshmallows are puffed and light golden, 20 to 23 minutes.
Cool for 20 minutes.
Drizzle salted caramel pouch over brownie bites.

Submitter: 
Duncan Hines® Canada
Description: 
Memories of summer camp. Duncan Hines Salted Caramel Brownies come together with mini marshmallows and graham cracker crumbs.
