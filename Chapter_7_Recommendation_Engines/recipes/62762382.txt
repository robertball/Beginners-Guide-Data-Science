Title:
Marinated Mozzarella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/639101.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ball 340 g Tre Stelle® Mozzarella Cheese, cut into 3/4” cubes
1 tbsp 15 mL Italian parsley, finely chopped
2 tbsp 30 mL fresh basil, finely chopped
1 small clove garlic, minced
1 tsp 5 mL red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp 2 mL salt
1/2 tsp 2 mL black pepper
1 tbsp 15 mL grated lemon rind
2 tbsp 30 mL lemon juice
1/2 cup 125 mL olive oil

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a medium bowl, cover and allow to marinate in refrigerator overnight or up to 2 days. Remove from refrigerator 1 hour before serving. Makes approximately 2 cups
