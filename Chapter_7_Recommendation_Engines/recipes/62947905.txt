Title:
Hoon Bang Slow Cooker Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 boneless pork chops (1-2 lbs.)
1/3 c Heinz ketchup
1/3 c soy sauce
3T Worcestershire sauce
3T brown sugar
2T peanut oil
2T water
1T ground ginger
½ tsp. ground black pepper
½ tsp. Chinese style mustard (extra hot)
¼ tsp. sesame oil
½ to whole yellow onion
3 cloves of garlic
1 can sliced water chestnuts

Directions:
Clean and dry pork chops, set them in the slow cooker.
Put all of the ingredients, except for the onion, the garlic, & the water chestnuts, into a food processor/blender, blend together very well.
Pour the mixture on top of your pork, make sure the pork is coated well.
Slice the onion and garlic cloves and add them to the slow cooker, along with the water chestnuts.
Cover and turn on low, cooking for about 5 hours, or until cut-with-fork tender.
Stir occasionally, make sure the pork is covered in sauce, and not drying out.

Submitter: 
DanSadler
Description: 
A very flavorful sauce that has an Asian flair, goes great with the tender pork.  It's easy to make & will have your house smelling awesome!  Try it over a bed of white rice.
