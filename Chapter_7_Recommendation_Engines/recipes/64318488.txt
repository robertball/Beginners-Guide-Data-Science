Title:
Jennifer's Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2009111.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen meatballs
Yoshida's original gourmet sauce

Directions:
Empty frozen meatballs into crockpot.
Pour Yoshida's sauce over meatballs (enough to cover).
Cover with lid and cook on high for 4 hours

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
This is such a quick and easy recipe that tastes great! Perfect for gatherings. If You'd like to make this into a main dish, serve over rice with green onions and sesame seeds
