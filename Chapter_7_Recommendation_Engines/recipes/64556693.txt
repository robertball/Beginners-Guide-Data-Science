Title:
Sweet and Savory Peanut Butter Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3389591.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Hard Boiled Eggs
8 Pickle Slices (Spicy Maple Bourbon*)
3 Tablespoons of Crunchy Peanut Butter (Honey Flavored*)
2 Tablespoons Hot Sauce (Louisiana*)
2 Tablespoons Mayo (Olive Oil added Mayo*)
3 Slices of Bacon
1 Tablespoon Reserved Bacon Grease
Salt and Pepper
Ground Red Pepper
*=my variety choice, feel free to substitute.

Directions:
1. Boil, cool, and peel the eggs.
2. Separate the whites from the yolks, reserve the yolks in a medium mixing bowl, whites for serving.
3. Chop the bacon into bits and pan fry, reserve the grease.
4. Mash the yolks, add crunchy peanut butter and the bacon grease - MIX THOROUGHLY.
5. Dice pickle slices, add with mayo and hot sauce to mixture and continue to mix and incorporate.
6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
7. Spoon mixture into egg whites
8. Top with bacon piece(s)
9. Sprinkle ground red pepper over the top of all eggs.
10. ENJOY!

Joey Partee
