Title:
Bananas in Caramel Sauce with Coconut Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup plus 1 teaspoon heavy cream
1/4 teaspoon vanilla
smidge of salt
1 banana sliced
2 scoops coconut ice cream
Toasted Pecans, Optional

Directions:
Slice Bananas.  In a large, heavy skillet over medium heat, melt butter.  Stir in sugar and cook, stirring, until sugar is melted and light brown.  Slowly stir in the cream (mixture will bubble up).  Let boil 1 minute, then remove from heat.  Add vanilla and salt.   Place sliced bananas over ice cream and pour over sauce.  Serve hot.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
A delicious, fast dessert. Impressive served when the sauce is still bubbling!  Serve with coconut ice cream, if desired.Modified from: Bananas in Caramel Sauce
