Title:
Hot Pepper Jelly BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1293991.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup of Hot Pepper Jelly
1/4 cup of White Vinegar
3/4 cup of Ketchup
1 TBSP of Mustard
2 TBSP of Worcestershire Sauce
1 Clove of minced garlic

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a saucepan and heat until just to boiling. Let cool a bit and brush on a meat of your choice.

Submitter: 
AuntE
Description: 
A sweet but spicy BBQ Sauce. Just thick enough to slab on any meat over the grill or in the oven. The whole family enjoyed the stickiness and flavor.
