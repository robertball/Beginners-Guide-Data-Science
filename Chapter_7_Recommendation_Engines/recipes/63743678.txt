Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head lettuce
1 large bottle of Russian dressing
1 cup green pepper
1 cup red pepper
3 med tomatoes
1 lb lean ground beef
1 pkg Clubhouse Hot
chili Mix
1 pkg shredded Tex Mex Cheese
1 bag taco tortilla chips

Directions:
Night before:
Brown ground beef
Season with salt, pepper and seasoning mix,
Place mixture in fridge overnight
In a large bowl, cut up lettuce into bite sized pieces
Add peppers, tomatoes, and meat mixture
Stir together
Add in cheese
Crumble the chips on top
Pour bottle of dressing over entire salad
Mix all together and salad is ready
