Title:
Marinara & Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/541121.jpg
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS
1 pound lean ground beef
1 cup fresh bread crumbs
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1 egg, beaten
SAUCE
1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 finely diced yellow onion
1 cup white wine

Directions:
Combine ingredients for meatballs, form into balls and brown.  Set aside.
Saute onions and garlic in olive oil.  Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, wine, chopped parsley, oregano, salt, and pepper.
Add back in meatballs and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
