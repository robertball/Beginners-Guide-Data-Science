Title:
One Bowl Microwave Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 15-oz cans sweetened condensed milk
2 1-oz squares unsweetened chocolate
24 oz semisweet chocolate chips
2 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix milk, chocolate squares and chocolate chips in a large microwave safe bowl.
Cook in microwave on high for 1 minute, then stir.
Cook in microwave on high for 30 more seconds then stir. Repeat in 30 second intervals until all chocolate is melted and smooth.
Once chocolate is melted, cook in microwave for 6 minutes on high stirring every 2 minutes.
Add vanilla and salt.
Beat with mixer for 2 minutes.
Pour into 10 by 13 pan lined with parchment paper.
Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before cutting into squares.

Submitter: 
Alicia
Description: 
A quick, classic fudge recipe that can be done in the microwave. Takes a fraction of the time and uses 1 bowl!
