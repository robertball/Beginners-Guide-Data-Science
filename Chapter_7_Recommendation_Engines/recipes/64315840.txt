Title:
Cheese and Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz processed American cheese
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup evaporated milk
1 tsp dry mustard
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/3 cup chopped roasted red pepper
1 can artichoke hearts, drained and coarsely chopped

Directions:
Combing all ingredients in small crock pot.
Cover and cook on low for 2-3 hours, until melted.
Stir well and serve with assorted crackers, bread cubes, or chips.

Submitter: 
Beth Mower Dodge
Description: 
This makes a great game-day (or any party day) dip. It works well as a dip for tortilla chips.
