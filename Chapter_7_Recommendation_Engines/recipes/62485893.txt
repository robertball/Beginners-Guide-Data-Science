Title:
Nichole's Chicken Cauliflower Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488919.jpg
Ingredients:
5 medium red potatoes, cubed
3 carrots, peeled and diced
3 stalks of celery, diced
4 tbsp of olive oil
1 medium onion, diced
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2 tsp of salt
2 tsp pepper
6 cups of chicken broth
2 cups of skim milk
1 head of cauliflower cut into small florets
2 medium chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
1 cup reduced fat Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.  Place cubed potatoes on a lightly greased cooking sheet and bake for 30 mins.
While potatoes are cooking coat bottom of large soup pot with olive oil.  Add celery, carrots, garlic, onion, salt and pepper.  Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes or until vegetables soften.  Add chicken broth and skim milk.  Bring to a boil and add cauliflower.  Reduce to medium heat and cook for 10 minutes.  Add potato, chicken and Parmesan cheese.  Reduce to simmer.  Let simmer for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Nichole Phillips McVicker
Description: 
A wonderfully light but filling soup.  Perfect on a cold night with a loaf of sour dough bread.
