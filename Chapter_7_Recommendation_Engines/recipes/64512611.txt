Title:
Hot Cranberry Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon apple cider
2 cups cranberry juice
1/2 -3/4 cup sugar
2 cinnamon sticks
1 tsp. whole allspice
whole orange studded with cloves

Directions:
Put all ingredients in slow cooker except for orange studded with cloves.
Cover.  Cook on high 1 hour and then on low 4-8 hours until thoroughly hot.
Float clove-studded orange in slow cooker.  Serve hot.
Serving suggestion- To garnish cider with an orange, insert 10-12 1/2" long whole cloves halfway into orange.   Place studded orange in flat baking pan with sides.  Pour in 1/4 cup water.  Bake at 325 for 30 minutes.  Just before serving, float orange on top of hot cider.
