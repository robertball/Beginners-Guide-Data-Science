Title:
The Best Lemon Drop EVER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz. citrus vodka
1 oz. FRESH lemon juice
1 oz. triple sec
1 oz. simple syrup (equal parts sugar and water, boiled)
Lemon juice and granulated sugar (for rimming glasses)
Lemon drop candies (for garnish)

Directions:
Moisten edge of cocktail glasses with fresh lemon juice.  Dip into granulated sugar to rim.  Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker with lots of ice.  Shake hard, at least 20-30 times.  Strain into prepared cocktail glasses, garnish each with a candy lemon drop.

Submitter: 
Kathleen Kleinhuizen
Description: 
My husband and I have tried lemon drop martinis at many restaurants and bars, and we always agree that mine are the BEST  : ) .
