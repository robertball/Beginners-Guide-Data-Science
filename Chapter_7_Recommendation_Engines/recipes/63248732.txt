Title:
Zumma Borsht Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 cups cut up potatoes
7 cups water
2tsp salt
1/3 cup fresh dill
1 yellow onion
1/2 bunch green onions
2 cups buttermilk
1 cup half and half cream
peppr
mennonite farmer sausage
Ham bone*optional

Directions:
Fry sausage. Cut into bite sized pcs.
boil potatoes (with bone if you have it)until potatoes are tender. Do not drain.
Add onions, dill, salt and pepper to taste.
Add sausage with drippings.
Add buttermilk and cream.
simmer 10 min.

Submitter: 
Eirene
Description: 
Zumma meaning summer, and Borsht meaning soup. A delicious german mennonite soup.
