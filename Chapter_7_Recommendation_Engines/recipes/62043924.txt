Title:
Mom Sykes' Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Round Steak or Chuck Cubes
3 Potatoes
1 pkg Lipton Onion Soup Mix
3 Carrots

Directions:
Brown meat with oil.  Drain oil, add water to cover meat, onion soup mix and simmer for 1/2 to 3/4 hour.  Add peeled and cubed raw potatoes, and peeled and sliced carrots for about 1 hour or until the potatoes and carrots are cooked.  Thicken with flour or cornstarch mixture.  Serve with biscuits.

Submitter: 
Gail Laulette
Description: 
Nothing beats a serving of hot stew on a cold winters night.
