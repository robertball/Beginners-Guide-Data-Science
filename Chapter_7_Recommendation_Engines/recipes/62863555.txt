Title:
Patio Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sugar
1 T cornstarch
1 to 1 1/2 t ground mustard
1 t salt
1 t dill weed
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup vinegar
1 egg, beaten
1/4 cup butter, cubed
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup mayonnaise
7 medium red potatoes, cubed and cooked
3 hard cooked eggs, chopped

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine sugar, cornstarch, mustard, salt and dill.  Stir in the milk, vinegar and egg until smooth.  Add butter.  Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened and bubbly.  Cool.  Stir in onior and mayonnaise.  In a large bowl, combine potatoes and hard cooked eggs.  Add dressing and toss gently to coat.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
