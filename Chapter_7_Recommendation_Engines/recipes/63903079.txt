Title:
Becky's Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1 clove garlic minced
6 Tbsp olive oil
3 Tbsp red wine vinegar
1 tsp sugar (or agave/honey)
1 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
Salad:
Spinach (or spring greens), sliced mushrooms, Padano parmesean shredded (I also add flat leaf parsley and green onions - MMM)

Directions:
Mix salad dressing ingredients in jar and shake.  Toss over salad

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
The padano really makes this recipe!!  Don't sub with anything else.
