Title:
Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2399179.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup shortening
1 1/2 cup sugar
2 whole eggs
4 mashed bananas
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
3 1/2 cups flour
16 oz package of walnuts (or to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°
Butter and flower bread pan
Blend the shortening and sugar in large bowl
Add the rest of the ingredients
Bake for 45 minutes or until golden brown on top and dry inside (check with toothpick)

Submitter: 
markfernandez
Description: 
Banana nut bread is good!  Serve hot with butter.  I like mine with fewer nuts, but you can decide how nutty you want it to be.
