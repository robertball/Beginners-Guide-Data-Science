Title:
Egg cup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 eggs
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
12 slices canadian bacon

Directions:
dice canadian bacon
beat eggs in bowl add remaining ingredients, pour into greased muffin pan and bake at 375 until set
