Title:
Jan Hagels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup butter
2 eggs, separated
1 cup sliced, unblanched almonds

Directions:
Mix flour, 1/2 cup sugar, cinnamon, and butter till crumbly. Mix in egg yolks till evenly blended.
Press firmly into parchment paper lined 13x9 pan. Brush with slightly beaten egg whites. Sprinkle with remaining sugar and the almonds, pressing them into the dough.
Bake 18-20 minutes at 350 or until lightly browned. Cool slightly, then lift out paper onto cutting board. Cut into 1 1/2 inch squares. Cool completely and store tightly covered, for one week to let the flavors blend, before eating.
