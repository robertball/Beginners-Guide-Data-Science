Title:
Mr. Dell's Original Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 cup shredded hash browns
1/4 cup butter
1 can cream of chicken soup
12 ounces sour cream
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup chopped green onion
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Melt butter in 9 x 13 inch baking dish, then add 1/2 potatoes, no need to thaw.
In a separate bowl, mix soup, sour cream, milk and onions, pour 1/2 of this mixture over the potatoes. Spread the mixture around over the potatoes evenly using a spatula. Salt and pepper to taste.
Sprinkle 1/2 cheese on top
Repeat layers.
