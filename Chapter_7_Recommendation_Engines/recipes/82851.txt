Title:
Ham Salad Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups reduced-fat mayonnaise
1 cup sweet pickle relish
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
2 pounds smoked boneless ham, diced
1 onion, diced
1 small green bell pepper, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
32 slices SARA LEE® Soft & Smooth™ Whole Grain White Bread
2 red or yellow peppers, cut into strips

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the mayonnaise and relish. Season with pepper. Gently mix in the ham, onion, green pepper, and celery. Spread 8 slices bread with the salad, and top with strips of red or yellow bell pepper and remaining bread to make sandwiches.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee Soft & Smooth™
Description: 
This lighter version of ham salad is made with low-fat mayonnaise and smoked ham, and served on whole wheat bread. This recipe makes enough sandwiches to feed a small army.
