Title:
Deliciously Delightful Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1115540.jpg
Ingredients:
12 hard-boiled eggs, sliced lengthwise, eggs separated from yolks
1/2 C mayo
2 Tbsp sweet pickle relish
1 1/2 Tbsp prepared mustard
1 tsp sriracha
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp dill
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Use an electric mixer to mix yolks, mayo, sweet relish, prepared mustard, sriracha, black pepper, dill, onion powder, and salt until smooth.  Fill empty egg whites with egg yolk mix.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
The flavors in these eggs are a wonderful twist on classic deviled egg flavors.  Sweet and tangy with a little heat, these are always a crowd pleaser!
