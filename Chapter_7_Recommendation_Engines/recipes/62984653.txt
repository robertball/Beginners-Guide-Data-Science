Title:
Tracy's Horseradish Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 large carrots, cut into stick-sized strips
2 T onion, finely diced
2 T fresh horseradish
1/2 c mayonnaise
1/2 c grated parmesan cheese (Kraft-type)
Panko or Ritz crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Place the carrots in a medium sauce pan with enough water to cover.
Cook 6-8 minutes, or until just soft.
Drain, reserving 1/4 c of the liquid.
Mix onion, horseradish, mayo, parmesan cheese and reserved liquid.
Toss with carrots and then place in a shallow backing pan.
Salt & Pepper to taste.
Sprinkle with bread crumbs to cover.
Bake uncovered for 20 minutes.
