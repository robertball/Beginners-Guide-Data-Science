Title:
French Cherry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*9" Graham Cracker Pie Shell
*1 cup sour cream
*1 cup milk
*1/2 teaspoon almond extract
*1/2 - 3/4 cup mini marshmallows
*1 can cherry pie filling
*1 package instant vanilla pudding

Directions:
Beat sour cream and milk together until smooth.
Add instant pudding and almond extract; beat 1 minute more.
Fold in marshmallows.
Pour into pie shell.
Chill until set.
When set, spoon pie filling over top.
Keep chilled.
