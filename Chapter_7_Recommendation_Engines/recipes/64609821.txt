Title:
Big Ray's Crock Pot Chickpea Stew with Apricots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4218615.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (15 oz) cans chickpeas/garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
1 (28 oz) can diced tomatoes
1 (15 oz) can chicken broth
3 Tbsp butter
1 onion, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
3/4 cup turnip, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup dried apricots, chopped
zest of 1 lemon
1 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
pinch of cayenne pepper

Directions:
Toss all the ingredients into a crock pot and stir to combine. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours or low for 6 hours. Taste for seasoning and add more salt as needed. Serve with fresh cilantro and lemon wedges over steamed couscous or rice.
