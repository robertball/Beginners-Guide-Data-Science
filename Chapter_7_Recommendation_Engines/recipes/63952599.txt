Title:
Quinoa Edamame Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dry quinoa
1 cup shelled edamame
8-10 grape tomatoes halved
1/3 cup chopped onion
3 tbs vege broth
2 lemons
2 tsp dijon mustad
1 tsp agave nectar
cumin
black pepper

Directions:
Cook quinoa in a pot with 1 cup water untilt it becomes like rice.
in a heated pan, pour the veggie broth, let simmer, add onions, tomatoes and edamame.
Transfer quinoa to pan and stirfry
make lemon dijon sauce.
Top on quinoa.
