Title:
Chicken Angelo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite size pieces
2 egg, beaten
1 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
3/4 lb. ounces Muenster cheese, sliced
2T melted margarine or butter
8 OZ mushrooms, sliced
1/4 c onion soup
1/4 cup white wine
1/4 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Rinse chicken pieces and pat dry. Dip chicken in egg, then roll in bread crumbs to coat. Lay in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Saute mushrooms in butter until brown, set aside
Combine wine, chicken broth and onion soup.  Add to mushrooms.
Layer cheese on top of chicken. Spread soup mixture on top of cheese. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
