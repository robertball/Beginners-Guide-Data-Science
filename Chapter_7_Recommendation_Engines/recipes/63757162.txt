Title:
Chicken Wing Sauce, Smoky Barbecue Sauce, Sweet and Spicy Thai Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Buffalo
1/2 Cup hot sauce
4 Tbs. unsalted butter melted
1 Tbs. Molasses
Smoky
1/4 cup Chicken Broth
1/4 cup Ketchup
1 Tbs Molasses
1 Tbs. cider vinegar
1 Tbs. minced canned chipolte chile in adobe sauce
1/4 tsp. Liquid Smoke
Thai
1/2 Cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup lime juice (2 limes)
1 Tbs toasted Sesame oil
1 tsp red pepper flakes
1 Garlic Clove
2 Tbs. fish sauce

Directions:
Chicken wing and smoky barbecue,  Mix all ingredients.
Thai Combine sugar, line muice, oil, pepper flakes and garlic in a small sauce pan, bring to a simmer over medium heat.  Cook until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.  Off heat add fish sauce and pour into large bowl.

Ed Canipelli

Wing sauce for your homemade wings.
