Title:
Strawberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
2 Tablespoons sesame seeds
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon minced onion
1 container strawberries
1 lb. bag of spinach

Directions:
For the dressing, pour all ingredients into a blender or food processor, excluding the olive oil.
Once ingredients are well blended, slowly pour in olive oil.
Slice strawberries lengthwise and mix in with spinach. Pour desired amount of dressing in large bowl with spinach and strawberries.
Serve cold.

Submitter: 
AppleChef
Description: 
A crisp and refreshing salad perfect for a light summer lunch or dinner!
