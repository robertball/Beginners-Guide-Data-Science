Title:
Amanda's Awesome Stir-Fry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup barbecue sauce
4 T. soy sauce
4 T. peanut butter
1/2 cup hot water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in small saucepan and whisk constantly over medium heat until smooth consistency.  Pour over a bed of rice and stir-fried vegetables.
Microwave method:
Combine all ingredients in microwave-safe dish and microwave at 50% power for 3 minutes, whisking at 1-minute intervals.

Submitter: 
Amanda Raposo
Description: 
A super-easy, delicious sauce that adds great flavor to standard white rice and stir-fried vegetables.
