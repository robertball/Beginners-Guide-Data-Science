Title:
Grilled pineapple butterscotch sundaes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 fresh pineapples
6Tbsp butter, plus 1/2C butter, divided
2Tbsp sugar
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1C packed brown sugar
1/2C heavy whipping cream
1tsp vanilla extract
dash salt
3C vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Peel, core and cut each pineapple into six spears. In a small sauce pan, melt 6Tbsp butter with sugar and nutmeg. Brush over pineapple. Grill, covered, over medium heat or broil 4in from the heat for 7-10min, or until lightly browned, turning occasionally.
For sauce, in a small saucepan, melt remaining butter. Stir in brwon sugar and cream. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Remove from heat; stir in vanilla and salt. Serve sauce with ice cream and grilled pineapple.
