Title:
Buttermilk Chicken Fried Chicken Tenders with Country Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Skinless Boneless Chicken Breasts cut into strips
1.5 cups buttermilk(divided)
1.5 cups flour
3 teaspoons seasoning salt
peanut oil for frying
deep fryer
Gravy:
4 Tablespoons oil chicken was fried in
4 Tablespoons flour
2 cups milk
salt and pepper

Directions:
Soak chicken strips in 1 cup buttermilk for at least 30 minutes
Combine flour and seasoning and slowly blend in 1/2 cup buttermilk with a fork. The mixture is very crumbly.
Coat the chicken strips with flour mixture. Lay them on a plate. Continue till all the chicken is ready to cook.
Heat oil till a tiny drop of water sizzles or 375.
Drop in chicken strips in small batches and fry till golden and crispy. About 3 minutes.  Drain on a paper towel.
Gravy: Add oil to a heavy skillet and heat.
Wisk in flour and brown for about 1 minute.
Slowly drizzle in milk stiring as you add.
Season to taste!
