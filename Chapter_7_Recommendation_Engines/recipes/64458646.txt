Title:
Orange Poppy Seed Zucchini Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. all-purpose white flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp. ground cloves
1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp. ground ginger
1 tsp. salt
3 large eggs
1-1/2 c. white sugar
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 c. vegetable oil
1 c. dried fruit (raisins,Craisins, apricots, etc.)
1 orange, zested
2 c. grated zucchini
1/4 c. poppy seeds
GLAZE:
1 c. powdered sugar
1/4 c. freshly squeezed or prepared orange juice

Directions:
In medium mixing bowl, blend all dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, baking soda, spices and salt.
In a separate large bowl beat eggs.
To beaten eggs, add white sugar and brown sugar; beat well.
Add oil; beat well.
Add orange zest; beat well.
Fold in remaining ingredients (fruit, zucchini and poppy seeds).
Pour into prepared (sprayed) 8 x 12" pan.
Bake at 325 degrees for approximately 45-55 minutes, or until toothpick inserted comes out clean.
Cool 10 minutes.
While cooling, make glaze.
Glaze: Mix powdered sugar and orange juice together until smooth.
Poke warm cake all over with fork and drizzle glaze over all.
Allow to cool completely, then serve.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
A wonderful burst of citrus in a quick bread that takes no time at all to make! Rich batter fills an entire 8 x 12 pan and uses up that wonderful garden zucchini that is overflowing gardens in the fall. Freezes extremely well!
