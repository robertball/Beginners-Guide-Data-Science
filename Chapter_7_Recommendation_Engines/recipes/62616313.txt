Title:
Vegetable Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon salt
1 pound stew meat
1 large can (15oz) tomato sauce
1 large can (14oz) beef broth
1 large can (14oz) stewed tomatoes
1 potato (peeled and sliced)
½ cup frozen green peas
½ cup frozen lima beans
½ cup crinkle cut carrots
1 potato (peeled and sliced)
1 cup frozen corn
1 large onion (chopped into medium sized pieces)
1 package (14oz) frozen whole petite onions
1 cup elbow macaroni

Directions:
Cut stew meat into ½ inch cubes add to pot of 6 cups of water. Add 2 tablespoons of
salt. Bring water to boil and add tomato sauce and beef broth. Cook until meat is well
done and tender. Add in green peas and lima beans. Cook until peas and beans begin to
soften. Add in sliced carrots, sliced potatoes, and corn. Cook until potatoes are soft. Add
chopped onion and whole petite onions. Cook until onions begin to soften. Add 2 cups of
water and the elbow macaroni. Cook until the soup slow boils for at least 5 minutes. Turn
off the heat and let stand for 10 to 15 minutes. Add salt to taste.
