Title:
Vidalia Onion Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/627921.jpg
Ingredients:
5 - 6 large Vidalia Onions
1 stick butter
2/3 cup Parmesan Cheese
1 1/2 cups Ritz Crackers, crumbled

Directions:
Slice onions into half rings. Saute in butter in a large skillet until opaque, but not browned. Place half of the onions in an 8-inch square baking dish. Sprinkle with half of the cheese; and then half of the cracker crumbs. Repeat layers. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
