Title:
Big'N'Chewy (whole wheat) Chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
125 ml  Butter, softened
125 ml  sugar
125 ml  Brown sugar
1     egg
2 ml  Baking powder
5 ml  Vanilla
2 ml  Bakin soda
1 ml  salt
250 ml  Rolled oats, ground
250 ml  Whole wheat flour
125 ml  Chocolate chips

Directions:
Pre-heat your oven to 375 F
Measure flour, remove about 50 mls and set aside.
Add remaining 200 mls flour to powdered/ground oats; stir to combine well. Add, baking powder, soda and salt.
Cream butter and sugars thoroughly. Add egg and vanilla and beat.
Add dry ingredients gradually to creamed mixture and blend evenly with wooden spoon. Dough should be quite thick. Add remaining 50 ml, flour only if necessary. Stir chocolate chips in.
Shape into balls the size of golf balls and press on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 10 minutes or until light gold.
Let stand 2-3 minutes before transferring to cooling rack.
enjoy.
