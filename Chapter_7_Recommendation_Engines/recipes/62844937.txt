Title:
Grilled Swordfish with Tomatillo Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/677627.jpg
Ingredients:
6 (6 oz) swordfish fillets (mahi mahi, halibut or another firm white fish), skin removed
1 lime, juiced
1 tbsp olive oil
Salt and pepper
2 semi-ripe mangos, peeled, seeded, and small diced
1 jalapeno, seeded and finely diced
2 limes, juiced
1 orange, juiced
4-5 medium sized tomatillos peeled, washed, and finely diced
1 small red onion, finely diced
1/2 cup chopped cilantro (or parsley if you are not a fan of cilantro)
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat grill.
Prepare both mangos and place in a bowl. Scoop 1/4 of the mango pieces out of the bowl and place in a blender with the lime and orange juice.
Blend until smooth and set aside.
Prepare the rest of the salsa ingredients (jalapeno, tomatillos, red onion, herbs) and place into the bowl with the mango pieces.
Sprinkle with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
Pour pureed mango and juices over the salsa and mix well.
Set aside.
Lay fillets on a plate and sprinkle with lime juice, olive oil, salt & pepper.
Place fillets on hot grill cook on each side for 4 minutes.
Serve salsa over fish and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
The fresh tomatillo mango salsa is simple, quick and a perfect compliment to the grilled fish. You can also enjoy the salsa with grilled chicken, tortilla chips or fish tacos.
