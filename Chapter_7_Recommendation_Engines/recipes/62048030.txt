Title:
Basic Beef Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 egg beaten
1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
1 cup of crushed saltine crackers

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degress C).
2. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, combine all ingredients and mix well.
3. Bake in preheated oven for 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
polarbyte
Description: 
This is a basic recipe for beef meatloaf. What's great about it, is what you can add to it to make it your favorite meatloaf.  Or leave it be for those who are unable to eat spicy foods.  Explore the possibilites and enjoy!
