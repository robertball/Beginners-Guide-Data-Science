Title:
Cheater Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/615883.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can of cream of (celery, chicken,or mushroom)soup
1 cup water
1/3 cup milk
3/4 cup brown rice
2 skinless chicken breasts, halved
seasonings of choice

Directions:
preheat oven to 375 degrees. In a medium mixing bowl combine soup water and milk, set aside. Pour rice in bottom of baking pan/dish, spread out fairly evenly. Lay chicken on top of the rice. Pour soup gently and evenly over the top of the rice and chicken. Sprinkle seaonings over chicken mixture. Cover tightly with aluminum foil. place in the oven for 45 minutes. Do not stir. Serve.

A super easy and quick chicken rice bake.
