Title:
bnetty's Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1228266.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (28 Ounce) Whole Tomatoes With Juice
2 cans (10 Ounce) Rotel (diced Tomatoes And Green Chilies)
1/2 cup Chopped Onion
1 clove Garlic, Minced
2 whole Jalapeno, Sliced Thin (one with seeds one without)
1/2 teaspoon Sugar
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/2 teaspoon Ground Cumin
1 cup Cilantro
1 whole Lime (Juice)

Directions:
Place whole tomatoes into large blender or food processor. Pulse until a good consistency. Poor half of blended tomato contents into a large bowl. Add remaining ingredients to blender. Pulse again until desired consistency. Combine contents into bowl with blended tomatoes. Mix with a spoon.
Refrigerate salsa for at least an hour.

Submitter: 
Ben Nettesheim
Description: 
Some restaurant style salsa that has some kick! Hai-Yah! It's a modified version of the Pioneer Woman's version. Many thanks to her!
