Title:
French Toast, "Mexican style" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 thick bread slices
8 eggs
1 14oz can La Lechera
1/4 cup Hershey's Dulce de Leche
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 stick of butter, for pan coating

Directions:
Heat a skillet to medium-high.
Beat eggs, then add the can of La Lechera. Stir in just until combined.
Next stir in the Dulce de Leche. Add vanilla extract and cinnamon.
Take two bread slices and dip them in the egg mixture, on both sides, for 5-10 seconds each.
Using the butter, quickly coat the pan. Put bread on pan, cooking for about 1-2 minutes each side.
Repeat process for other slices.

Submitter: 
Ashbow
Description: 
This is my fiance's recipe. He didn't know what to put in original French Toast, so he took what he had in his mother's kitchen, who is Mexican. Serve with your favorite syrup. You won't be disappointed!
