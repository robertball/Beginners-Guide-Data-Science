Title:
Very Veggie Pork Lo Mein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332611.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. pork loin (roast,chops or tenderloin) trimmed and chopped
2 Tbs peanut oil
1/4 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
1 Tbs. Hot Garlic Chili Paste (more or less to taste)
1 tsp. ground ginger or minced fresh gingerroot
1/2 head green cabbage, chopped
2 cups sliced fresh white mushrooms
1/2 cup matchstick carrots
1 cup fresh brocolli florets
1 pkg. whole wheat linguine
sesame oil
1/2 cup Stir Fry Sauce

Directions:
Combine Soy Sauce, Garlic Chili Paste and Ginger in gallon zip top bag or covered dish.
Add pork and marinade 2 hours to overnight.
Cook linguine per package directions past al dente.  Put in pasta bowl and dress lightly with sesame oil to prevent sticking.
Meanwhile over med-high heat, heat peanut oil in wok or large skillet until hot.
Add pork and stir fry 4-5 minutes until no longer pink.  Remove from wok.
Add a little more oil to wok and when hot, add cabbage.  Cook and stir until it begins to reduce (wilt).
Add remaining veggies.  Cook and stir 3-4 minutes until tender crisp.
Add pork back in and heat through.
Pour meat and veggie mixture over noodles.
Toss to combine.
Top with Stir Fry Sauce and toss lightly until incorporated.
