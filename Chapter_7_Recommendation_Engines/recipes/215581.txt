Title:
Summer Sweet Smoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/664244.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cranberry juice
2 cups strawberries
1 cup blueberries
1 cup watermelon chunks
1 banana
2 fresh figs

Directions:
Process the cranberry juice, strawberries, blueberries, watermelon, banana, and figs in a blender until smooth and creamy. Enjoy immediately or keep cool in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
souplover Sue
Description: 
Packed full of sweet summer fruity goodness. Great for a breakfast or lunch when you're on the go.
