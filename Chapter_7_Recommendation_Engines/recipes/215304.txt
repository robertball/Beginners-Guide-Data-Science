Title:
Slow Cooker Chinese Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2216995.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds baby carrots
1/2 cup orange juice concentrate
1/4 cup tamari sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger
1 teaspoon grated orange zest
1 tablespoon Asian (toasted) sesame oil
1 tablespoon honey

Directions:
Place the baby carrots, orange juice concentrate, tamari sauce, garlic, ginger, orange zest, toasted sesame oil, and honey into a slow cooker, and mix well. Set the cooker to Low, cover, and cook for 8 hours. Turn setting to High, and cook for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Aliza Finley
Description: 
A simple side dish with bold flavor.  I made a huge tray for a friend's fancy party and it was gone in no time.  Everyone asked me for the recipe, but I had made it up! Serve with your favorite Chinese food.
