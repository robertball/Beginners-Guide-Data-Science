Title:
Rhonda's chicken tortilla casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked, boneless and skinless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
4 tablespoons chicken stock
9 (6 inch) corn tortillas, cut into strips
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
8oz sour cream
1-10 oz can rotel not drained
1 onion, shredded
16 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a bowl mix Chicken, soups, sour cream, rotel, 2 cups cheese and onion.
Put 3 to 4 tablespoons of chicken stock in a 9 x 13 inch baking dish.
Add a layer of tortilla strips, a layer of chicken mixture and then top with cheese and repeat.  Continue to layer in this order until dish is full.  Top with cheese.
Cover and refrigerate for 24 hours.
Bake at 300 degrees F (150 degrees C) for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
Alternating layers of  tortillas, chicken and a tangy green chile cream sauce (easily made with canned soups), and oozing with melted Cheddar cheese.Modified from: Chicken Tortilla Casserole
