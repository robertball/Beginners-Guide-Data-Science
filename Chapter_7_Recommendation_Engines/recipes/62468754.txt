Title:
Mexican Seitan Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package seitan, about 8 oz., cut into strips
1/2 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, diced
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp cumin powder
2 tbsp soy sauce
3 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Sautee onions and garlic in olive oil until onions are soft, about 3-5 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients and allow to cook, stirring frequently for 7-10 minutes, until seitan is thoroughly cooked.
Wrap in corn tortillas and serve plain or with bottled green tomatillo salsa.

Submitter: 
cookiecutter
Description: 
These seitan fajitas look and taste like the real thing. Wrap these vegetarian fajitas in a corn tortilla and drizzle with green tomatillo salsa for a satisfying main dish.
