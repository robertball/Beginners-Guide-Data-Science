Title:
Zucchini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1042394.jpg
Ingredients:
2 small zucchini(green & yellow), sliced into 1/4"-thin round; 1Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil; 1Tbsp fresh lemon juice; 1Tbsp mint leaves, coarsely chopped

Directions:
Sprinkle salt over the sliced zucchini and leave for 5min.
Before serving, drizzle evenly with oil, then sprinkle lemon juice and black pepper.
