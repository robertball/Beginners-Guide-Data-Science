Title:
Slow Cooker Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups Wheat Chex® cereal
4 cups Cheerios® cereal
3 cups pretzel sticks
1 (12 ounce) can salted peanuts
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
2 tsp. Worchestshire  sauce

Directions:
In a 5-qt. slow cooker, combine cereals, pretzels and peanuts. Combine butter, Parmesan cheese, celery salt and seasoned salt; drizzle over cereal mixture and mix well. Cover and cook on low for up to 3 hours, stirring every 30 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature.Take lid off last 30 minutes

Submitter:

Description: 
'This mildly seasoned snack mix is always a party favorite,' reports Dana Hughes of Gresham, Oregon. Served warm from a slow cooker, the munchable mixture is very satisfying.Modified from: Slow Cooker Party Mix
