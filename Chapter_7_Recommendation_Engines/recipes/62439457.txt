Title:
Mexican Style Shredded Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) boneless pork loin roast, cut into 2 inch pieces
1 Tsp Mrs.Dash extra spicy
1/2 c chopped pickled jalapenos
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup red taco sauce
1 lime, juiced
1/4 c water

Directions:
Place the roast in a slow cooker, and season with Mrs.Dash. Place chile peppers and garlic on top of roast. Pour in the taco sauce, lime juice, and water.
Cover, and cook 7 hours on Low.
