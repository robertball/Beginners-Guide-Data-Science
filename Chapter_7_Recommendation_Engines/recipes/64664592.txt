Title:
Sea Bass Veracruz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. roma tomatoes, seeded, diced
1/2 C . pimento stuffed olives, chopped
1/2 C. cilantro
1/4 C. jalapeno, seeded and diced
3 T. lime juice
2 T. capers
4 Sea Bass fillets, 6 oz each
salt
pepper
1/4 C Flour
6 T. EVOO
2 C onion, sliced
1 T. garlic minced
2 bay leaves
1 t. thyme, dried
1/4 C chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Combine tomatoes, olives, cilantro, jalepeno, lime juice and capers.
Season fillets with salt and pepper and dredge one side with flour.
Heat oil in a large, oven proof skillet over medium high heat.
Add fish, flour side down and saute 3 minutes and remove from pan.
Stir in broth to deglaze pan.
Add in half of the tomato mixture.
Add onion, garlic, bay leaves and thyme into pan.
Saute 3 minutes.
Nestle fillets into tomato mixture and roast in oven 5-7 minutes, or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.
Remove and discard bay leaves.
Add remaining tomato mixture, salt and pepper to taste.
Serve over fillets.
Mexican rice is a great die for this dish.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
A delicious, light dish with yummy Mexican flavors. A great change to grilled or roaster fish.
