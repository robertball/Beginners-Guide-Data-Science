Title:
Breakfast Delicious! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tube refrigerated crescent rolls
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup butter
1 cup chopped bacon (cooked)
6 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1 cup shredded Montery Jack Cheese

Directions:
1) Spread rolls into the bottom of a square pan to make the crust. Saute onion in butter and add chopped bacon. Mix together and pour over the crust.
2)Combine and beat the eggs and milk.  Can salt and pepper to taste.  Pour over the meat and onion.  Sprinkle cheese on top.
3) Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until brown.

Description: 
Light and fluffy eggs sit on top of baked crescent rolls with bacon, onions and cheese!  So yummy even the kids ask for seconds.
