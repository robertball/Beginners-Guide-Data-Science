Title:
Healthy low-fat  chocolate chip banana bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/630941.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup wheat germ
3 over ripe bananas, mashed
1 egg
1/4 cup low fat yogurt
2 tbsp ground flax seed meal
2 tbsp sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 cup  semi sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
2. Combine mashed bananas, eggs and yogurt and mix until well blended.
3. Add dry ingredients and chocolate chips and mix well.
4. Pour into prepared loaf pan and bake in preheated oven for 60 minutes.
