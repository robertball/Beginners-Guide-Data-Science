Title:
Pumpkin Spice Latte Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089863.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Kahlua Pumpkin Spice
1 fluid ounce vanilla vodka
1 fluid ounce half-and-half
Dash cinnamon (optional)
Cinnamon stick (optional)

Directions:
Place Kahlua, vodka, and half-and-half in cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake until well blended and very cold. Strain into a martini glass. Garnish with a dash of cinnamon and a cinnamon stick.
