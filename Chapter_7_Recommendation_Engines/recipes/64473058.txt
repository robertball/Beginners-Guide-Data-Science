Title:
Mushroom, Ham and Cheese Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 pkg refrigerated pizza dough, thick or thin crust
2 tbsp oil
6 oz fresh mushroom, Shiitake or Portabella are best
1/2 cup sliced red onion
1 tsp rosemary
1/4 lg ham, julienned
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup Gruyere cheese, finely shredded
1/8 tsp crushed red-pepper flakes

Directions:
Grease a 10 inch round pizza pan and sprinkle with cornmeal.Push dough into pan making an edge. Remove stems from mushrooms and thinly slice. In large skillet heat oil over medium heat. Add mushrooms, onion and rosemary, saute 7 minutes. Add ham and garlic, saute 1 minute. Sprinkle crust with 2 tbsp Parmesan cheese, spread topping over crust to edging. Sprinkle with Gruyere cheese, the remaining Parmesan cheese and the pepper flakes. Bake 8-12 minutes or until crust is a light golden color and cheese is bubbly.
