Title:
Beef Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, finely chopped
2 jalapenos, seeded and finely chopped
1 1/2 tsp minced garlic
3 Tbsp chili powder
2 tsp cumin
3 tsp sugar
1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
1 cup water
1 large tomato, chopped
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 can chopped green chiles
1 cup extra sharp white cheddar shredded
1 cup Monterey jack, shredded
1 pkg soft-taco size flour tortillas
Cooking spray
Pepper to taste
Green onions for garnish

Directions:
Heat oven to 425. Grease 9x13 casserole dish. In large skillet, saute onions and jalapenos until soft. Stir in garlic, chili powder, cumin and sugar. Cook 1 minute; stir in water, pepper, tomato sauce and tomato. Meanwhile. brown ground beef and drain in another skillet. When sauce is done, mix half in with the beef; add chiles. Lay tortilla on plate; sprinkle mixture of both cheeses in middle and top with beef mixture. Roll tightly and put in casserole seam side down. Repeat. Spray tops of enchiladas and bake, uncovered for 7 minutes. Reduce temp to 400. Pour remaining sauce over enchiladas and top with remaining 1/2 cup cheese. Cover and bake 20 minutes. Remove foil and bake additional 5 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving. Top with green onions.
