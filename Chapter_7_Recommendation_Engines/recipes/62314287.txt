Title:
Cucumber Raita with Mint Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
570 ml (2 1/2 cups) plain yoghurt.
12-14 cms (about 5 inches) cucumber, peeled and coarsely grated. If you have time, remove the seeds before grating.
2 Tablespoons fresh mint, finely chopped.
1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin seeds, finely ground (see footnotes).
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper.
1 teaspoon salt.
Freshly milled black pepper.

Directions:
Put the yoghurt into a bowl and beat lightly with a fork (or whisk) until smooth and creamy.
Add all the other ingredients, stir well to mix them in thoroughly.
Cover and refrigerate until required.

Submitter: 
Sonya Jane
Description: 
A cooling Indian yoghurt dish that can be served as an accompaniment or as a dipping sauce. Excellent with samosas. And very healthy.
