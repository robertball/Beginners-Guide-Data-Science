Title:
Bacon Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980989.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups torn spinach
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion wedges
4 slices OSCAR MAYER Bacon, crisply cooked, crumbled
2 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
1 cup KRAFT CATALINA Dressing

Directions:
Toss all ingredients except dressing in large bowl. Serve with dressing.
Special Extra:  Prepare as directed, adding 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, grilled, cut into strips.
