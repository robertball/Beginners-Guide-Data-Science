Title:
Chicken and Wild Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6.2 oz) pkg wild and long grain rice
2 Tbsp butter
2 celery ribs, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 tsp minced garlic
1 can chopped water chestnuts, drained
2 1/2 cups cooked chopped chicken
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
1 can cream of mushroom soup
8 oz sour cream
1 cup milk
1/2 tsp pepper
1 cup breadcrumbs or chow mien noodles
1 oz sliced almonds, toasted

Directions:
Prepare rice according to package. Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat. Saute celery, onion and garlic until tender. Combine water chestnuts, rice, celery, onion, garlic, chicken, 1/2 cups cheese, soup, sour cream, milk and pepper in large bowl. Spoon into greased 9x13 casserole dish and top with breadcrumbs. Bake, uncovered, for 35 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining cheese and almonds; bake 5 more minutes.

Description: 
Doubles easily for freezing; leave off breadcrumbs and top just before heating.
