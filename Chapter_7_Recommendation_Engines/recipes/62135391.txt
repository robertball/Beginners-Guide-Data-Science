Title:
Broccoli Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pizza Dough (Pizza Dough I from allrecipes.com works great)
1-1 1/2 cups fresh broccoli florets
1 large red onion sliced
2 cups grated mozzarella cheese
Rosemary

Directions:
Spread dough on oblong or round baking stone.
Place broccoli on dough.
Top with onion.
Sprinkle with rosemary.
Top with cheese.
Bake at 375 until dough and cheese are golden.
