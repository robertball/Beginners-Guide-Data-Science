Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz cream cheese
1 tbl spoon onion powder, garlic powder, chili powder, and ground cumin
2 tbl spoons Olive oil
4-6 cups of cooked chicken
3/4 cup salsa
Enchilada sauce
Cheese
Tortillas

Directions:
Heat and mix the spices an cream cheese, when it is mixed well add salsa chicken and cheese, heat and mix, then put it in tortillas, put enchilada sauce on pan before putting the tortillas in the pan then pour more on top and then put cheese on the top of it all, bake at 350 until warmed and cheese is bubbly.

Submitter: 
Brad Miller
Description: 
This is a nice, easy to make chicken enchilada. It is a common request with my kids.
