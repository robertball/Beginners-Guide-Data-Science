Title:
BACARDI速 Pomegranate Mojito Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987258.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces BACARDI速 Superior Rum
12 fresh spearmint leaves
1/2 lime
1 ounce pomegranate juice
2 tablespoons simple syrup
Top with club soda

Directions:
Muddle mint leaves and lime in a shaker. Cover with BACARDI(R) Rum, pomegranate juice and simple syrup. Shake with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Top with a splash of club soda. Garnish with lime wedge and sprigs of mint.
