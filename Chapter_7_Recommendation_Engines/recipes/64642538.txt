Title:
Bacon Cheddar Ranch Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4517738.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz) carton sour cream1 (1 ounce) package of Ranch Dressing Mix8 ounces cream cheese, softened6 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded3 green onions, finely sliced

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the sour cream with the Ranch Dressing Mix. Stir together thoroughly. Add in the cream cheese and mix with a handheld mixer until well incorporated.Add in the bacon, cheese and green onions. Cover with plastic wrap and place in the refrigerator for 1 hour.If you prefer hot dip: Preheat oven to 400F. Place the mixture into a dish and bake uncovered for 30 minutes until hot and bubbly. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Culinary Envy
Description: 
Bacon Cheddar Ranch Dip is an irresistibly starter thats full of bacon, cheese, green onions, cream cheese and ranch dressing mix!
