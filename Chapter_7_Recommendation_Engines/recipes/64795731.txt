Title:
Air Fryer Sourdough Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sourdough starter
1/2 cup spelt flour
1 cup bread flour
1/2 tsp fine sea salt
1 tbsp evoo
1/2 cup water, divided

Directions:
Combine the starter, flours, salt, and evoo in a mixing bowl of a stand mixer. Begin to knead using the kneading hook. Add water until the ingredients combine and begin to pull together. You may not need all the water. Knead on low speed for 5 minutes.
Fold the dough into a ball. Place inside an air fryer safe baking dish. Cover with plastic wrap and allow to double in volume, about five hours or overnight.
Preheat air air fryer to 390°F. Remove the plastic wrap from the bread. Score the loaf and bake inside the baking pan for 20 minutes or until browned on the outside and the internal temperature reaches 190° F.
Allow to cool before slicing. The bread should be stored wrapped in a clean cloth then inside a plastic bag. It will keep for about 3 days.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Fragrant sourdough bread made in an air fryer. This method is perfect for the hot months when you don’t want to heat up the kitchen but still want fresh homemade sourdough.
