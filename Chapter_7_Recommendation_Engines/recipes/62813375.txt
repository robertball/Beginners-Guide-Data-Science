Title:
Healthy Choc Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Unsweetened Chocolate Almond Milk
1 Banana
2 Tbsp. Peanut Butter
1/2 cup oatmeal(uncooked)
4 Ice Cubes

Directions:
Blend all ingredients. Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Shay Pauley
Description: 
A healthy delicious breakfast smoothie with no sugar added.
