Title:
Delicious Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
1 cup water
1/2 cup butter - softened
3 eggs
2 t. rum extract
1 small box vanilla pudding

Directions:
Set oven to 325.
Blend all the ingredients together and mix on low for one minute than 2 minutes on medium.
Pour into sprayed bundt pan - use the baking spray with flour.
Bake 45 - 55 minutes or until done.
Remove from oven, turn pan over and allow cake to cool.
Right before serving, dust cake with powdered sugar.
