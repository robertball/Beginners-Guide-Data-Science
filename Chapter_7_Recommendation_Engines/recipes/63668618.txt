Title:
Chicken Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup unsalted butter
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 quart cold chicken stock
1/3 cup heavy cream
salt and ground pepper to taste
1 pinch cayenne pepper

Directions:
Melt butter in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Whisk in flour, 2-4 minutes.
Gradually whisk in cold stock. Bring gravy to a simmer, and cook and stir until thick enough to coat the back of a spoon, 5 minutes.  Stir in heavy cream, and season with salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper.

Submitter: 
Toni
Description: 
Make an easy homemade gravy out of chicken stock, heavy cream, butter, and flour.Modified from: Homemade Chicken Gravy
