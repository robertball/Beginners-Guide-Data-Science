Title:
Easy Breakfast Nacho Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123071.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. bulk sausage
4 lg. eggs
1/4 c. sliced green onion
2 T. butter
1 pkg. tortilla chips
1 c.. shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook, drain and crumble the sausage.
Beat the eggs and add to the sausage with the green onions.
Melt the butter in a medium baking dish.  Add the sausage mixture and top with crumbled chips.
Sprinkle the cheese over all and bake for 10-15 minutes or until eggs are set and cheese is melted.
Top with salsa and sour cream.
