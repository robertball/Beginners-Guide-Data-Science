Title:
Customized Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 (12 ounce) package rice noodles
2 tablespoons butter
1.5 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-sized pieces
1/3 cup sesame oil
4 eggs
3 tablespoon white wine vinegar
6 tablespoons fish sauce
6 tablespoons white sugar
3/4 tablespoon crushed red pepper
1/2 tablespoon cayenne
2 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
2 cups bean sprouts
1/4 cup grated carrots (or sticks)
1/4 cup crushed peanuts
3 green onions, chopped
1 lime cut into wedges

Directions:
Soak rice noodles in cold water 30 to 50 minutes, or until soft. Drain, and set aside. (Or can also just put into hot water for a bit before using - depending on noodle)
Make sauce (ingredients vinegar to sweet chilli sauce).  Heat butter in a wok or large heavy skillet. Saute chicken until browned. Remove, and set aside. Heat oil in wok over medium-high heat. Crack eggs into hot oil, and cook until firm. Stir in chicken, and cook for 5 minutes. Add softened noodles, and vinegar, fish sauce, sugar and red pepper. Adjust seasonings to taste. Mix while cooking, until noodles are tender. Add bean sprouts, and mix for 3 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jodi
Description: 
This is a traditional Pad Thai recipe used by a friend's mother. You can use chicken, pork, beef, tofu, or a combination. You may want to start with less pepper, and work your way up.Modified from: Pad Thai
