Title:
Orage Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces frozen orange juice concentrate
1 medium banana
3 cups ice water
1/2 cup milk, * see note
1 tablespoon sugar or honey

Directions:
Put in blender and blend until foamy.
* 3 tablespoons non-fat dry milk to 1/2 cup water
