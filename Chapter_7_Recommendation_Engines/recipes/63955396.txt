Title:
Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon of butter
4.5oz of fresh spinach chopped
7oz of quartered artichokes chopped
2oz of low fat cream cheese
2oz 2oz chive cream cheese
1 single cup (5.6oz) of fat free plain greek yogurt
1/3 cup of grated Parmesan
1 teaspoon of powdered garlic

Directions:
Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat.
Stir in the spinach and artichoke hearts. Cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
Mix cream cheese and greek yogurt into the spinach mixture.
Stir in Parmesan cheese and garlic. Cook, stirring, until thickened, 10 to 15 minutes.
Serve warm.
