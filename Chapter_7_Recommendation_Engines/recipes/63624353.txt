Title:
Cheese and Grape Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
a recipe of your favorite pie dough
a bag of washed grapes
1 1/2 cups of cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350
Take your pie dough and mix in the cheese. Shape it in a pan.
Cut your grapes into thin slices. layer them on top of the pie crust
Bake in the oven for 17 minutes. Serve warm or cold.

Submitter: 
Julia L
Description: 
This is a savory pie, based on the concept cheese and wine. I hope you enjoy!
