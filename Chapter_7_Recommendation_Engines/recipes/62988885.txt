Title:
Fancy-Fancy' Dinner Green Bean Onion Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/856125.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (14oz) cans cut green beas
6oz can french fried onions
1/3 c cream
1 can cream of mushroom soup
3 tbsp toasted sliced almonds
1/2 c shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Put alternate layers of drained beans and ff onions in 11/2 qt baking dish.  Mix light cream, soup and almonds.  Pour over top.  Place final layer of ff onions over all.  Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Slightly atpical version of this favorite holiday side dish, this recipe has been in my collection since 1964.
