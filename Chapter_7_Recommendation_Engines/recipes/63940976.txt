Title:
Pork Chops with Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless pork chops
1 pkg premade stuffing mix
2 tb brown sugar
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/3 can water

Directions:
On high heat in a large pan, brown the pork chops for 1-2 minutes on either side, drain and set aside.
Prepare stuffing as directed on the box, adding brown sugar.
Combine Cream of Chicken soup with Worcestershire sauce and water.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a casserole dish or oven safe pan, layer the stuffing and pour 1/4 of the soup mixture evenly over the stuffing. Place the pork chops over the stuffing and pour the remaining soup mixture evenly over the whole casserole. Bake 25 minutes and serve.
