Title:
Rum Berry Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz Rum
2 tsp sugar
3 rasberries
2 oz mango or pineapple nectar
2 oz cranberry juice cocktail
ice

Directions:
1. Crush raspberries and sugar in a hi-ball cocktail glass
2. Add ice
3. Add rum, nectar and cranberry juice
4. Stir and enjoy

Submitter: 
MrMomz
Description: 
This is a fruity rum cocktail that has complex tones of raspberry, cranberry and mango.
