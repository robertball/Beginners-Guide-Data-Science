Title:
Potato Gnocchi with Mushroom Basil Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/668078.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package Archer Farms Potato Gnocchi
1/4 cup Dry White Wine
2 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/2 Cup Vegetable Broth
4 Green Onions
8 oz. Mushrooms
2 Tablespoons Earth Balance Margarine
1/2 cup Chopped Fresh Basil
1/4 Cup Daiya Shredded Vegan Cheese

Directions:
Boil gnocchi until it all floats to the top (about 3 minutes)
In a large skillet, sautee mushrooms in olive oil until brown. Add green onions and wine and simmer until liquid is almost completely absorbed. Add vegetable broth and simmer until sauce thickens a bit. Add cooked gnocchi to mushroom sauce, along with margarine, basil and cheese and simmer for about 1 minute.

Submitter: 
Tracy Bradley
Description: 
A delicious gnocchi recipe that can be served as a main dish or as a side dish.
