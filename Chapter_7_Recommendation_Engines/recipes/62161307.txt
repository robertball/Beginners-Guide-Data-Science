Title:
Honeydew Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium sized honeydew mellon
1 tablespoon honey
1/4 teaspoon sugar or sugar substitute

Directions:
slice honeydew mellon in half
carve out honeydew mellon
discard shell
dump mellon into blender
add honey and sugar
blend on low for about 1 1/2 minutes
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Junoir Chef
Description: 
A refreshing summer drink - easier than a piece of cake!
