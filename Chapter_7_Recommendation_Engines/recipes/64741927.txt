Title:
Dairy and Gluten Free Sweet Oatmeal Flax Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tbsp. of coconut oil
3 tbsp. brown sugar
1/4 cup molasses
2 and 1/2 cup gluten and lactose free rolled oats ( I use Bob Mills Brand)
2 tbsp. ground flax
1/2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to  350 degrees.
In a medium sized pot combine coconut oil, molasses and sugar.
Heat over medium heat until coconut oil has dissolved.
Boil for 60 seconds.
Remove from heat and add vanilla.
Stir in rolled oats and  flax and mix until all is combined.
Press into well greased or parchment paper lined pie pan.
Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes, until edges begin to darken.
Cool completely in pan.
Cut into wedges while still in the pan or upend on a cutting board to slice.
