Title:
7UP Cherry Pomegranate Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985928.jpg
Ingredients:
1 quart pineapple sherbet
1 (2 liter) bottle 7UP® Cherry
6 ounces Rose's Cocktail Infusions® Pomegranate
1 cup white rum (optional)

Directions:
Add all ingredients to 1 gallon punch bowl.

Submitter: 
7UP
Description: 
This festive holiday punch with pineapple sherbet and 7UP Cherry makes a colorful cupful with the addition of pomegranate cocktail mix.
