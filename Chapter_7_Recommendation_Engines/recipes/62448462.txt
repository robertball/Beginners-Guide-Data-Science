Title:
Mediterranean Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/462976.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups canned garbanzo beans, drained
reserve liquid for thinning out dip if need be)
1/3 cup tahini (sesame seed paste)
2  3 cloves of garlic
¼ cup lemon juice (1  2 lemons)
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
Toppings according to your taste!
Paprika
Calamata olives, roughly chopped
Sun Dried Tomatoes, in olive oil (you can also drizzle oil over dip)
Roasted Garlic Cloves
1/ 4 cup Feta Cheese, crumbled
1 Tbsp. Chopped, Fresh Parsley
1  2 tsp. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Pita Bread

Directions:
Place beans, tahini, garlic, lemon juice, salt and pepper in blender or food processor.  Blend until smooth, you may need to add a little liquid to thin out dip if you see the consistency is too thick.
Refrigerate for about 1 – 3 hours or overnight for flavors to blend.
Transfer mixture to a serving platter and garnish with the above toppings according to your taste.
Wrap up in aluminum foil and warm in the oven – remove and cut into triangles to serve with dip.

A delicious hummus recipe that can be served with warm pita bread or crackers.
