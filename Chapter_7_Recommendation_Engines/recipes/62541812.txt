Title:
Anynight Chili Mac Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup elbow macaroni
1 1/2 pound ground beef
1 Tb. vegetable oil
1 small onion, chopped
1 cup chopped celery
1/2 large red bell pepper, chopped
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
2 tsp. chili powder, or to taste
1 tsp. garlic powder
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup
1 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Heat vegetable oil in large heavy skillet.  Saute chopped onion and celery (and bell pepper, if using) over medium-high until tender.  Reduce heat to medium.
Add ground beef to skillet and cook until evenly brown. Drain excess fat.
Stir in kidney beans, condensed tomato soup, diced tomatoes, chili powder and garlic powder. Season with salt and pepper, and stir in macaroni.
