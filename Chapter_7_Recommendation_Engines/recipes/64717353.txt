Title:
Beef Pot Roast Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb grass fed beef roast
1 T salt
1 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
1 medium onion sliced
4-5 garlic cloves chopped
olive oil/ghee
1 c beef bone broth
2 T coconut aminos
1 T red wine
1 c water (or as needed)

Directions:
combine salt/spices and rub into meat
put garlic and chopped onion in bottom of slow cooker
sear all sides of meat in ghee/olive oil
put meat in slow cooker
fry onions in remaining drippings or add more ghee/olive oil
deglaze pan with coconut aminos, wine
add bone broth and simmer for a few minutes
put broth/onions in slow cooker
marinate overnight
cook on low for 12+ hours
add water as needed if too salty (added later in cooking)
(was not quite falling apart after 11 hours, so turned to high; also added water; next time maybe 1/2 c bone broth and 1/2 c water to start; next time try marinate overnight with herbs and sear in morning or cook overnight)
