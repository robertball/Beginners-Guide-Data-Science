Title:
Beef Taco  Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/748892.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cream cheese
2 cups sour cream
2 pkg.taco seasoning
1/2 lb.hamberger
3-4 cups shredded lettuce
3 chopped green onion
1 chopped green pepper
1 chopped jalapeno pepper
3 cups grated cheddar,OR marble cheese
2 chopped tomatoes

Directions:
Cream together cream cheese,sour cream with 1 pkg.taco seasoning.Cover,refridgerate.Brown hamberger add 1 pkg.taco seasoning,drain all fat,refridgerate till cold.In 9x13 baking pan,spread cream cheese mixure.Top with lettuce,green onion,green pepper,jalapeno[if desired]and tomatoe.Top with cold hamberger and sprinkle cheese on top.Serve with your favorite taco chips.

Submitter: 
koko
Description: 
When my son was young he called this taco pie.He's 33 now and always asks for it.Huge hit at BBQ's and we have it every Christmas too.
