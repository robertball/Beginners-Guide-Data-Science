Title:
Asian Pear and Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1535424.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ice
1 Asian pear, cored and cubed
2 large strawberries, hulled
2/3 cup vanilla fat-free yogurt
1/4 cup fat-free milk
2 teaspoons white sugar

Directions:
Place the ice, Asian pear, strawberries, yogurt, milk, and sugar into a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Alysha Meeks
Description: 
This light, delicate smoothie is perfect for those hot summer days. The Asian pear flavor really comes through.
