Title:
Reduced Sugar Sweet and Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Splenda granulated sugar
1/3 cup white vinegar
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup reduced sugar ketchup
2/3 cup water
2 Tbsp. cornstarch

Directions:
Place the sugar, vinegar, soy sauce and ketchup in a medium saucepan, and bring to a boil. Stir together water and cornstarch. Whisk into sauce, stirring continuously until the mixture has thickened.
Serve as a dipping sauce with tempura or use as a substitute in any sweet and sour recipe.
