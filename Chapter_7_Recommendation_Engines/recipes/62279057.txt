Title:
Taco Bites!!!:D Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of Tostito's Tortilla chips
1 can of refried beans (or 2 optional)
1 can of black beans (or 2 depends, how much beans you like)
1/2 packet of taco seasoning
5 T. salsa
LOTS OF CHEESE :)(recomended Kraft "mexican" cheese"
some Sour Cream

Directions:
Put Refried beans, and Black Beans, in a small pot...add half packet of taco seasoning...Meanwhile, get 2 cookie sheets out, layer with tortilla chips. Once beans are done, pull out layer ontop of chips, add cheese, sour cream (optional) and salsa..Preheat oven to - 350 F Bake until chips are toasted and cheese melted!!!
