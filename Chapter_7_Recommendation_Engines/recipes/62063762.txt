Title:
veggie stir fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-21/2 T olive oil, 1/4 to 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes( to desired heat),  4 to 5 zuchinni, 3 slices yellow sweet onion chopped, 3tsp. garlic minced in the jar, 1 small red shallot, 3 plum tomato, 1/2 tsp. lemon pepper, 1/4 tsp seasoned salt, squirt lemon juice, parmesan cheese grated.

Directions:
Slice zuchhinni, chop onion and shallot, slice up tomato. heat pan and add oil, red pepper to infuse(until you smell the spice). Add zuchinni, onion, garlic,shallot. stir fry until zuchhinni and onion are tender.  Add lemon juice, lemon pepper, seasoning salt and toss. Place sliced tomoto on top and let heat for about 3mn. Grate fresh parmesan over top as much or little as you want.  I sometimes mix parmesan and cotija mexican cheese. cotija cheese can be found at mexican markets and is wonderful.

Submitter: 
Bethany Bambenek
Description: 
This is a spicy veggie stir fry. I love it for lunch, side dish or can make it a meal by adding your choice of noodles. This is topped with fresh parmessian cheese.
