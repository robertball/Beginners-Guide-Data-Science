Title:
Chicken and Four Cheese Ravioli with Spinach and Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991465.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package BUITONI® Riserva Refrigerated All Natural Chicken & Four Cheese Ravioli
1 1/2 cups baby spinach leaves
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon shredded fresh basil leaves
1 tablespoon pine nuts, toasted

Directions:
Combine prepared pasta, spinach, oil and basil in large serving bowl; toss gently. Sprinkle with pine nuts before serving.

Submitter: 
Buitoni, courtesy of meals.com
Description: 
Fresh basil and baby spinach bring the fresh flavors of summer to BUITONI® Chicken and Four Cheese Ravioli tossed with toasted pine nuts.
