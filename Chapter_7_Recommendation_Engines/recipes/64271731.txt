Title:
Rice Noodle Cups (Gluten Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch dried gluten free rice vermicelli
2 tsp homemade vegan broth
1/4 tsp chili powder
1/8 tsp white pepper
1/8 tsp ground ginger
Dehydrated veggie mix (peas and carrots, etc), optional

Directions:
Put the noodles inside a heat resistant jar with hermetic seal.
Add all the other ingredients in layers.
Cover and keep sealed until ready to use.
When ready to eat,
Add 1.5 cups of boiling water, cover for 5 minutes.
Stir and eat!

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
A healthy and gluten free substitute for those unhealthy ramen cups.
