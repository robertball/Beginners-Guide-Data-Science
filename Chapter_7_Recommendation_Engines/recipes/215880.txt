Title:
Saucy Slow-Cooked Italian Sausages Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990267.jpg
Ingredients:
8 (4 ounce) links fresh Italian sausage
26 ounces Classico® Tomato and Basil Sauce
1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
1 onion, sliced

Directions:
Place the Italian sausage links, sauce, green pepper and onion into a slow cooker. Stir to coat everything in sauce. Cover, and cook on Low for 6 hours. Serve on hoagie rolls, or over rice.
