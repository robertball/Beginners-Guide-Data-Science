Title:
Honey Oatmeal Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11/2 cup water
1/3 cup butter
1/2 cup honey
2 teaspoon salt
1 cup quick rolled oats
6 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoon active dry yeast
2 eggs
1 egg white
1 tbsp water
2 tbsp oatmeal for topping

Directions:
In saucepan, water, honey and butter, low heat to dissolve to 115 degrees.Add Yeast 'till frothy.
Mix 5 cups flour, oats, salt. Combine.
With mixer and dough hook, add warm liquid mix to flour and mix for about 1 minute.
Add eggs and mix 1 minute longer.
Add remaining flour as needed and mix about 2 minutes 'till dough clings to hook and cleans side of bowl.
Knead on speed 2 for 2 minutes.
Place dough in greased bowl. Let rise in warm place for about 1 hour or doubled in size.
Punch down, divide in half.
Place in 9 X 5 loaf pan. Cover and let rise.
Beat egg white and tbsp water with fork. Brush top of loaves.
Sprinkle with oatmeal topping.
Bake at 375 degrees for 40 minutes.
Remove from pans and cool on wire racks.
