Title:
Mint Icing for Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter
1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
dash salt
2 cups sifted powdered sugar
2 tablespoons milk
few drops green food coloring

Directions:
Pour the powdered sugar in a mixing bowl, add the butter (make sure it is soft), mix and add peppermint extract, milk, salt, and food coloring. Just mix as you go and add a little more milk if needed.

Submitter: 
Suzanne Harris
Description: 
This recipe has been used in my family for many years. I am sure there are some like it on the web, but I can't find them. This is delicious on Brownies.
