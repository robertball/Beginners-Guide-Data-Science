Title:
Nana's Strudel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/784444.jpg
Ingredients:
dough:
½ lb melted butter
1 TBS sugar
¼ tsp salt
1 cup sour cream
2 ¼ Cup flour
filling:
6 TBS orange marmalade
6 TBS apricot preserves
Combine:
½ cup brown sugar
1 cup + chopped walnuts
1 TBS + Cinnamon
½ Cup + white raisins

Directions:
Mix together dough ingredients. Stir until it forms a ball. Cut into 4 equal parts. Wrap in wax paper. Refrigerate or freeze until firm (refrigerate overnight is preferable).  Roll out each on floured board to create about 9X13 inch rectangle.
Combine the orange marmalade and apricot preserves.  Spread equally on the 4 parts rolled out dough.
Combine the brown sugar, chopped walnuts, cinnamon, and white raisins. Sprinkle on the rolled out dough.  Roll up each like a jelly roll.
Place on a greased and floured cookie sheet. Bake in a 350 degree oven for about 45 minutes.
Cool and slice into 3/4 inch slices.

Submitter: 
happycooker
Description: 
This is my grandmother's recipe.  It was never in written form until I finally followed her around the kitchen and wrote everything down.  It freezes well.
