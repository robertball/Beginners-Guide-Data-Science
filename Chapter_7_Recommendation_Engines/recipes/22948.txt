Title:
St. Michael's Irish Americano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/807090.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers espresso coffee
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers Irish whiskey
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 tablespoon heavy cream
6 fluid ounces hot water
2 tablespoons whipped cream, garnish

Directions:
Pour the espresso into your favorite mug, add Irish whiskey, sugar, tablespoon cream and hot water. Garnish with a dollop of whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Michael D
Description: 
After downing many an Irish coffee while in Ireland, I adapted this recipe to suit my Northwest coffee loving taste buds!
