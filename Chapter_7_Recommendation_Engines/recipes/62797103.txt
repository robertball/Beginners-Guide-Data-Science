Title:
Portuguese braised short ribs cooked in pressure cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef short ribs
1 cups red wine
3 cups water
salt and pepper to taste
5 cloves of garlic, chopped
1 onions, sliced
1 bay leave
1 tablespoon paprika
a few springs of fresh mint (tied together for easy removal)
a few springs of fresh parsley (tied together for easy removal)
1 teaspoon cinnamon
8 ounces tomato sauce
1/3 head of green cabbage, cut into 2 wedges

Directions:
Place all ingredients except cabbage into pressure cooker.  Cook for 30-40 minutes.  Let cool to release the pressure.  Add cabbage and simmer for additional 15 minutes. Serve with mashed potato or with thick crusty bread.

Submitter: 
soulgraft
Description: 
Adapted from Elle restaurant recipe: “One of those family traditions for Elle, a Wine Country Restaurant’s chef, Antonio Cardadeiro, was a dish his grandmother, born in Portugal, made for cold weather family dinners. This is his version served at Elle.”
