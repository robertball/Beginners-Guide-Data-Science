Title:
Chocolate Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large ripe banana (about 1 c. mashed)
3 T. peanut butter
2 heaping T. unsweetened cocoa powder
2 T. sugar
pinch salt
1/4 - 1/3 c. milk
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Using a fork, blender, or mixer, mash banana until it's the consistency of baby food.
Add peanut butter and mix until creamy.
Add cocoa, sugar, and salt and stir until well mixed.
Add milk and vanilla and stir until well mixed.
Eat right away or store covered in fridge.

Description: 
Super easy and healthy pudding - kids love it!
