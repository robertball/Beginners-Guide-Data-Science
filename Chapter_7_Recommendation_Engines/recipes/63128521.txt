Title:
Raw Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/797816.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup vegetable oil
2 each eggs
2 cups white sugar
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3 cups Granny Smith apples , chopped
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 cup pecans
1/2 cup raisins (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.
Chop apples finely and place into a large bowl. In a separate bowl, beat eggs and oil together and pour over apples. Let stand while mixing the dry ingredients.
In a medium bowl, mix sugar, flour, 1 teaspoon salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking soda, and pecans (raisins can be added if desired). Fold into apples, eggs and oil and mix well. Batter will be thick.
Pour into prepared 9x13 inch pan, and bake at 350 degrees F ( 175 degrees C) for 45 minutes. Once cool frost with Cream Cheese Icing.
