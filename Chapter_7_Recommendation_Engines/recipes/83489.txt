Title:
Gingerbread Cake with Lemon Cream Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981148.jpg
Ingredients:
Your favorite gingerbread recipe (box or scratch)
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
1 cup powdered sugar
4 tablespoons lemon juice
1 lemon, zested
4 tablespoons half and half

Directions:
Make your favorite gingerbread recipe and set aside.
To make the sauce, cream together the other six ingredients.  Serve over warm gingerbread.  Sprinkle with additional lemon zest.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
Gingerbread is topped with a sweet, creamy sauce, featuring a unique blend of cream cheese, powdered sugar, half and half, lemon juice, and the secret ingredient -- Marzetti® Slaw Dressing.
