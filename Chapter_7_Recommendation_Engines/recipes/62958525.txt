Title:
Tourtiere Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 lbs ground beef
chopped celery - cooked
2 tsp salt
2 tsp savory
3 tsp celery salt
1 tsp ground pepper
1/4 tsp ground clove
1/2 tsp thyme
Dash of tabasco
1tsp Worcestershire
1 cup water
1 can beef broth
1 to 2 cups bread crumbs

Directions:
Heat olive oil.  Add hamburger and cook until no longer red.  Drain fat. Mix all ingredients (except bread crumbs) together in large pot.  Bring to boil and cook for 20 minutes with no lid.  Remove from heat.
Add a few tablespoons of bread crumbs and let stand for 10 minutes.  Repeat until the liquid has been completely absorbed by the bread crumbs.
Let cool and put meat between two pie crusts.
Put foil around edge of pie crust for first 15 minutes.
Cook at 350 degree until pie crust is golden approximately 30-40 minutes.
Serve hot
