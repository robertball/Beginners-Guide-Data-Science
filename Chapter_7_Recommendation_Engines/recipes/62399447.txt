Title:
coconutty cereal bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/430410.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups corn flakes
2 cups rice crispies
1 1/2 cups sweetened flaked coconut
1 cup salted peanuts
1/4 cup margerine
1/4 cup smooth peanut butter
30 large marshmallows
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 inch bakign pan.
Pour cornflakes into a bowl and crush into small pieces with your hands.
Stir in rice crispies, coconut and peanuts, set aside.
Melt margerine and peanut butter together over medium heat in a sauce pan.
Stir in marshmallows and stir constanly until all of the marshmallows have melted and the mixture is smooth.
Stir in vanilla and pour into dry mixture.
Use a long handled wooden spoon and mix until well combined.
Press mixture into prepared pan and allow to cool before cutting into squares.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
Two classic cereals, sweet coconut and creamy peanut butter come together in my husbands favorite squares.
