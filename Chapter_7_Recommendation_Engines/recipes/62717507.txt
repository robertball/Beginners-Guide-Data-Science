Title:
Slow Cooker Latin Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
3 pounds skinless chicken thighs
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 bunch cilantro, stemps removed, chopped
2 large sweet potatoes, cut into chunks
1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
2 (15.5 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
2 cups chicken broth
1 bunch cilantro, chopped
2 cups Joe's homemade salsa
4 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon ground allspice
6 large cloves garlic, chopped
lime wedges, for garnish

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a large skillet; season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper. Brown the chicken in the frying pan, 3 to 5 minutes each side.
Arrange the chicken in the bottom of a slow cooker. Sprinkle with half of the cilantro. Place the sweet potatoes, red bell pepper, and black beans on top of the chicken. Mix together the chicken broth, remainder of the cilantro leaves, salsa, cumin, allspice, and garlic together in a bowl; pour into the slow cooker. Set slow cooker to LOW and cook for 6 hours. Garnish with lime wedges to serve.

Spicy cumin, chicken, salsa, black beans, and sweet potatoes combine into a flavorful stew. The thigh meat has unbelievable flavor and tenderness.Modified from: Slow Cooker Latin Chicken
