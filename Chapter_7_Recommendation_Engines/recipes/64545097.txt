Title:
Breakfast Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tablespoon olive oil
3 small corn tortillas, cut into strips
4 eggs, beaten
1 green chili, diced
1 oz tomatillo salsa
3 tablespoons cilantro
salt and pepper to taste
1 small Roma tomato, diced
sour cream to garnish

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet.
Add tortilla strips and cook until softened. Add the eggs, green chili, salsa and cilantro.
Cook for about five minutes until set.
Season with salt and pepper.
Top with diced tomatoes and a dollop of sour cream.

Submitter: 
Nathan Hendrix
Description: 
Quick, delicious breakfast dish. Serve alongside something tangy and sweet like blueberry lemon quinoa.
