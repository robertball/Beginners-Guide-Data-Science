Title:
The Nutty Banana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup(s) milk, fat-free
6 ounce(s) yogurt, fat-free plain
1 small banana(s)
3 tablespoon peanut butter, creamy, natural
6 whole ice cubes

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in food processor or blender. Process until smooth..
Divide between glasses and serve.
