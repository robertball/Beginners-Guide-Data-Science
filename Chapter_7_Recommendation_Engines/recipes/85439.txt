Title:
Pierced Fuzzy Navel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/267500.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce peach schnapps
1 fluid ounce vodka
3 fluid ounces orange juice
1 dash grenadine (optional)
ice cubes

Directions:
Pour the peach schnapps, vodka, orange juice into a shaker with ice. Shake, then strain into a glass. Top with a splash of grenadine if you like.

Submitter: 
Ashley Jones
Description: 
Actually stumbled across this when making Fuzzy Navels. It was a suggested recipe. Thought it might be good to share...interesting. Great party drink. Scales well.
