Title:
Orange Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked, shredded chicken
3 cup chicken broth
8 ounces tomato sauce
1 package taco seasoning mix
1-1/2 cups brown rice, uncooked
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
corn chips for serving

Directions:
Mix cooked chicken, broth, tomato sauce, taco seasoning, and rice in a Dutch oven.  Cover and simmer until rice is done (about 40 minutes).  Fluff with a fork, sprinkle with cheese, and serve with corn chips.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Michael Ramey
Description: 
Taco-flavored rice with chicken.  We usually eat it with corn chips, but it could also be used as a burrito filling or a side dish.  If you use white rice, cut the simmering time in half.
