Title:
Ravioli Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pam
1 C frozen loose cut leaf spinach
2 bags mini raviolis
1 (14.5 oz can) diced tomatoes, drained well
1 1/2 tsp dried italian seasoning
1 C low fat cottage cheese
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 C shredded italian cheese

Directions:
1.  Pre-heat oven to 375.  Spray 8 x 8 ubcg gkass bajubg dusg w ciijubg sorat.  Place spinach in microwave safe bowl.  Cover w plastic wrap & microwave on high 2 min, squeeze spinach dry
2.  Combine ravioli, tomatoes & italian seasoning in microwave safe bowl on high for 3-4 min or until hot.  Combine cottage cheese, spinach & black pepper in bowl.
3. Place half of ravioli mixture in bottom of baking dish.  Top w half of cottage cheese mixture & 1/2 C shredded cheese.  Repeat layers.  Bake 15 min or until cheese is bubbly
