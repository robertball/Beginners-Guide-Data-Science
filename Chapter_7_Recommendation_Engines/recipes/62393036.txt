Title:
Mushroom and Swiss Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/433685.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp. butter
2 eggs
1 Tbsp. milk
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup chopped fresh mushrooms
1/4 cup shredded swiss cheese

Directions:
In a small bowl, beat the eggs and milk together.  Season with salt and pepper.
Pour eggs into a small non-stick skillet coated with cooking spray over medium-low heat.  Sprinkle mushrooms and cheese on eggs and cook until eggs are set and no longer runny.
Fold omelet in half and turn out on a plate to serve.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
Growing up, we would often have Breakfast for Dinner Night, when my mom didn't feel like cooking.  My mom always requested that my dad make this omelet for her.  It's a great blend of flavors and so easy to make, and now my husband loves it too!
