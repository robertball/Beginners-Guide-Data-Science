Title:
Spinach and Carmelized Onion Feta Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/322198.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sheet puff pasty dough, thawed
1 box frozen spinach, thawed or microwaved according to box.
1/2 cup jarred roasted red peppers, julliened
2 medium sized yellow onions, sliced
5 button mushrooms, sliced
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
1 cup shredded fontina cheese
1/2 tsp of salt
3 tbsp oil.

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400.
Lay pastry dough on a greased baking sheet. Fold over edges to create a crust. Poke with fork and place in the oven for 15 minutes. Squeeze as much water from thawed spinach as possible. Heat oil in a saute pan and carmelized onions till golden about 15 minutes. Remove puff pastry dough from the oven and scatter spinach in an even layer. Top with mushrooms and sprinkle with salt. Scatter with red peppers and then with the fontina and parmesan cheese followed by the feta. Finish with the carmelized onions. Bake for an additional 20 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden brown.
