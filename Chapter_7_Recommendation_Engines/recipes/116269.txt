Title:
Cream Cheese Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/806231.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Assorted fresh fruit

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese, brown sugar and vanilla; mix well. Serve with fresh fruit for dipping. Refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Renner
Description: 
Color it Christmas with a platter of red and green apple slices piled 'round a bowl of this yummy no-fuss fruit dip! The dip also goes well with strawberries, grapes and kiwi.
