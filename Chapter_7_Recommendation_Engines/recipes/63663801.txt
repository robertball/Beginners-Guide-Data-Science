Title:
Big Ray's Cumin-Coriander Pork Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1017913.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Tbl Ground Cumin
4 Tbl Chili Powder
2 Tbl Kosher Salt
2 Tbl Ground Coriander
1 Tbl Paprika
1 1/2 tsp Ground Allspice
1 1/4 tsp Ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Combine in a medium bowl and mix well.  Store in an airtight container in a cool dark place until ready to use.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
A great rub for pork; chops, loin or even ribs that can be used on beef as well.
