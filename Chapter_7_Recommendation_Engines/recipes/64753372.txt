Title:
Big Ray's Savory Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6845830.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken thighs (about 1 pound)
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 Tbsp butter
10 garlic cloves, peeled and halved
1/4 cup white wine or chicken broth
1 1/2 teaspoons minced fresh rosemary
1/2 teaspoon minced fresh sage
1 cup chicken broth
Hot cooked rice or couscous, your choice

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat butter over medium-high heat; brown chicken on both sides. Remove from pan, reserving drippings.
In same skillet, saute garlic in drippings over medium-high heat until light golden brown. Add wine and herbs; bring to a boil, stirring to loosen browned bits from pan. Cook until mixture is almost evaporated. Add broth and chicken; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, covered, 10-12 minutes.
To serve, spoon pan juices over chicken. Serve with rice/couscous.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Searing the chicken thighs crispy brown help seal in the juices while pan-roasting the garlic cloves turns them into rich, creamy deliciousness. This chicken is fantastic with rice or couscous. Complete the meal with a green leafy salad on the side.
