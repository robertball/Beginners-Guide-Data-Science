Title:
Jalapeno Popper Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup mayonnaise
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies, drained
8 large fresh jalapeno peppers, roasted, peeled, seeded, and diced
1 tsp garlic powder
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1 1/2 cups shredded extra sharp cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
Stir together cream cheese and mayonnaise in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in green chiles, jalapeno peppers, garlic powder, 1/2 cup of the parmesan, and one cup of the shredded cheddar. Pour mixture into a microwave safe serving dish, (it fits perfectly in a 1.5 quart crock pot) and sprinkle top with remaining parmesan and cheddar.
Microwave on High until hot, about 3 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jay Lynne Stuart
Description: 
This is an easy cream cheese spread to make, and it tastes just like the jalapeno poppers you wish you could make yourself!  The hardest part of this recipe is not eating it all by yourself!Modified from: Jalapeno Popper Spread
