Title:
Greek Baked Macaroni (Pastitsio) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/880962.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/4 sticks butter
1 medium diced onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 lbs. ground chuck
1/2 lb. ground pork
1-1/2 tablespoons seasoned salt
1 tablespoon pepper
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1-8 oz can tomato sauce
2-8 oz cans of water
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1 cup bisquick flour
2 cups milk
8 eggs, beaten in blender or with mixer
1-1/4 lbs. mostaciolli (penne pasta)
1 cup grated romano cheese

Directions:
In large frying pan, saute' onion and garlic in 1/2 stick of oleo.
Add ground chuck and ground pork.
Season meat with salt, pepper and soy sauce.
After meat has browned, add tomato sauce, water, parsley and basil. Let simmer 15 minutes.
In large sauepan, cook bisquick and milk until thick, stirring constantly. REMOVE FROM HEAT, add beaten eggs and remaining butter to paste.
In large dutch oven, boil pasta in salted water unil 3/4 done, approximately 6 minutes. Strain and set aside.
Grease 9x11 pan. Layer bottom of pan with 1/2 of the partially cooked pasta. Follow with 1/2 of the sauce and meat mixture. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the romano cheese. Add a layer of about 1/3 of the cream sauce.
Repeat the above layers with the remaining pasta, sauce and meat mixture, cream sauce and romano.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Let set approximately 15 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Denise Phillips Sarigianopoulo
Description: 
This dish can be described as a greek lasagna. It can be served as a main dish with a garden salad or as a side dish. It's great for holidays. Ever since I can remember, this has been a staple at our family functions. Once your family tastes it, it will become a tradition at your family events too.
