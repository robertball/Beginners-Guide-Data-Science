Title:
Last Smokies You'll Ever Make Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bottle chili sauce (12 oz)
2 packages cocktail weiners
3 TBLS grape jelly
1 large can chunked pineapple (approx 20 oz)

Directions:
Drain 1/2 of the pinapple juice.
Combine all ingredients into a crock pot.
Cook on low for about 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Toast
Description: 
If BBQ sauce just isn't doing the trick try this blend of pineapple, chili sauce, and a special ingredient to shake things up!
