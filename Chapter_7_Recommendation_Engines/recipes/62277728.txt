Title:
Hamburger Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
3 cups peeled and thinly sliced potatoes
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
1 tbl chopped garlic
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
salt to taste
freshly ground pepper, to taste
1/2 envelope onion soup mix
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef, drain fat.  Sautee onions, bell peppers and garlic until tender.  Combine cream of mushroom soup, milk, sour cream, onions, bell peppers, salt and pepper. Alternately layer the potatoes, soup mixture and meat in a 11x7 inch (2 quart) baking dish. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, or until potatoes are tender. Top with Cheddar cheese, and continue baking until cheese is melted.
