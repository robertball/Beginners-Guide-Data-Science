Title:
Zorro Masked Bandit Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 beet
6 leaves beet greens
1 lemon
1 cucumber
2 carrots
1 tbsp size fresh ginger nub
2 large carrots
1 red delicious apple

Directions:
Wash and peel fruits and veggies if desired. Run all ingredients through the juicer (wrap the carrots in the beet greens)pour over ice and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
If you are not a fan of beets, this is the juice for you. The lemon, cucumber & ginger are a delightful mask for the beet bandit!
