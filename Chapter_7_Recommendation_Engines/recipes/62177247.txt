Title:
Kims Garlic Breadsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package or 2 1/4 tsp. yeast
4 1/4 cups flour
2 TBS butter softened
2 TBS sugar
1 TBS salt
Topping:
3 TBS melted butter
1/2 tsp. kosher salt
1/4 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Place yeast, sugar, and 1/4 cup warm water in bowl and let set for 15 minutes to activate the yeast.
Add flour, softened butter, salt, and 1 1/4 cup warm water after yeast has activated.
Knead dough for about 3 minutes.
Roll dough into a 2 ft log. Cut log into 16 pieces about 1 1/2 inches wide.
Knead each piece and shape into 6 inch long breadsticks.
Let them rise for about an hour.
Preheat Oven to 375 degrees
Melt Butter and mix in garlice powder and brush the breadsticks with it.
Bake for 15 minutes.
Immediately brush the breadsticks with a little bit of the butter mixture as soon as they come out of the oven.
