Title:
A Good Easy Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon olive oil
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 T. minced garlic
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 T. seasoned salt
1 cup chicken broth
1 T. cornstarch

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add chicken, onion,garlic and seasoning. Add chicken broth and saute about 10 to 15 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and juices run clear.
Remove chicken and keep warm in low oven. Add 1 T. cornstarch to 1/2 cup cold water and add to remaining juice in pan. Stir until thick.
Serve over brown rice and pour gravy over all.

Description: 
Sprinkle chicken breasts with garlic, onion and seasoning salt - then sautee and enjoy. Couldn't be easier! Great recipe for quick and easy meal. Very flavorful as well as a healthy way to fix chicken.Modified from: A Good Easy Garlic Chicken
