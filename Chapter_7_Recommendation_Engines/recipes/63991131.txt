Title:
Easy Roast Beef (Venison) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb Sirloin tip roast (works well with other roasts as well)
Seasonings such as Worchestershire sauce, seasoning salt, garlic powder, onion powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 500 degrees.
Rub roast with chosen seasonings.
Place roast on rack in pan.
If doing vegetables place around roast.
Place roast in preheated oven for 12 min/lb.
At end of time turn the oven off DO NOT OPEN THE OVEN DOOR.
Leave in the oven for another 12 min/lb.
Remove from the oven to serving plate.

Description: 
This is a very easy and foolproof ways to roast beef.
