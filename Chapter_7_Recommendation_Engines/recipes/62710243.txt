Title:
Reuban Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon granulated sugar
1 envelope dry yeast
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup hot water
1 tablespoon butter, softened
1/4 cup Thousand Island dressing
3/4 cup drained sauerkraut
6 slices swiss cheese
2 (6 ounce) packages thinly sliced corned beef
1 egg white, beaten

Directions:
Preheat ove to 400 degrees F
Grease baking sheet
Combine 2 1/4 cups flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl and mix well
Stir in hot water, butter, and enough remaing flour to make a soft dough
Knead dough on floured surface for 4 minutes
Roll doll into a 14 x 10 inch rectangle
Place dough sheet on baking sheet
Spread dressing over dough
Layer cheese, sauerkraut, and corned beef onto dough
Fold in each long edge of dough to the center, pinching seam and ends to seal filling in
Brush top of loaf with egg white
Cut 4 small slits in top of loaf
Bake 25 minutes, or until golden
Cut into slices to serve
