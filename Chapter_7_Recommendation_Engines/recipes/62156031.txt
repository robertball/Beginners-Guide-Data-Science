Title:
Left Over Pernil (Roast Pork) Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups of left over Roast Pork
4 Cloves of Garlic
3/4 of a cup of Basil
1 Large onion
2 Table Spoon of Oil
1 Small hot pepper or 1 tsp Chilli Oil
1 1/2 cups of frozen mixed vegitables
4 Table Spoons of Soy Sauce
2 Table Spoon of Hosin sauce
1 tsp of Chinese 5 spice (Optional)
2 cups cooked white rice or what ever rice you want

Directions:
On high heat in a large skillet heat oil add Garlic, Onion, Basil, Pepper or chilli oil and stir for about 1 minute. Next add pork, 5 spice and fozen Vegis stir again for about another minute then add Soy Sauce and Hoisin Sauce and let simmer for about 3 Minutes or until all the ingredients are coated with sauces. Lastly add the rice a little at a time and stir ingredients together until rice is brown. Do Not let rice sit because it will burn. Remove from heat and serve with scallions on top as presentation, serve with duck sauce on side. Hope you enjoy from my table to yours
