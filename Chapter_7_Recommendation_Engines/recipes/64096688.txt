Title:
Big Ray's Reuben Braids Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1213506.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz sliced corned beef, chopped
1-1/2 cups (6 oz) shredded Swiss cheese
3/4 cup sauerkraut, rinsed and well drained
1 small onion, chopped
3 Tbl Thousand Island salad dressing
1 Tbl Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp dill weed
2 packages (8 ounces each) refrigerated crescent rolls
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 Tbl sesame seeds
Read more:

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the first seven ingredients.
Unroll one tube of crescent dough onto an baking sheet lined with parchment paper; seal seams and perforations.
Spread half of corned beef filling down center of rectangle. On each long side, cut 1-in.-wide strips to within 1 in. of filling. Starting at one end, fold alternating strips at an angle across filling; seal ends. Repeat with remaining crescent dough and filling. Brush egg over braids; sprinkle with sesame seeds.
Bake at 375Â° for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on wire racks for 5 minutes before cutting into slices. Refrigerate leftovers.

Description: 
An easy way to make a sandwich to feed a large group at once. The braid design will impresses almost as much as the taste. Have some Thousand Island dressing on the side for dipping.
