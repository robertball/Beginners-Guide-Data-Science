Title:
Fusilli with Creamy Tomato and Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz ground beef or turkey
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 14-once cans peeled Italian style tomatoes, cut up
1 tsp dried Italian seasoning, crushed
1/2 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
8 oz packaged dried fusilli, vermicelli, or spaghetti
1/2 cup whipping cream
2 tblsp snipped parsley
Fresh rosemary sprigs (optional)

Directions:
For sauce, in a large saucepan cook beef or turkey onion, and garlic till meat is brown.  Drain fat.  Stir in undrained tomatoes, Italian seasoning, sugar, salt, and pepper.  Bring to boil then reduce heat.  Simmer, uncovered, about 40 minutes or until most of liquid has evaporated, stirring occasionally.
Meanwhile, cook pasta according to directions.  Drain & keep warm.
Gradually stir the whipping cream into the sauce.  Heat through, stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat.  Stir in parsley.
Spoon the sauce over pasta.  Garnish with fresh rosemary sprigs if desired.
