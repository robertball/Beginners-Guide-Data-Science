Title:
Rhubarb pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6C young chopped rhubarb
4Tbsp dry tapioca
1 or 2 eggs
3C sugar
2Tbsp butter
1 9in pie crust

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except butter. Then pour into pie curst(pre baked) dot the pie with butter. Bake in oven at 425 for 50-60min.
