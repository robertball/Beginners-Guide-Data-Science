Title:
Grandma Ebinger's Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1 Tablespoon Flour
2 Eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 15 oz can pumpkin
1 can evaporated milk
Unbaked pie shell

Directions:
Take out unbaked pie shell and set aside. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Combine sugar, flour, salt and spices together in a medium mixing bowl. Add the pumpkin and mix thoroughly. Add milk and combine. Lay out the pie crust in a 9 inch pie pan and add the mix. Bake at 425 for 20 minutes then reduce to 375 and bake for another 40 minutes. Test with a knife, when it comes out clean the pie is done. Cool to room temperature and serve with whipped cream.
