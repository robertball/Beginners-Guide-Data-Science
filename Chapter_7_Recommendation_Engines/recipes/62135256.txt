Title:
Hawaiian Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/442004.jpg
Ingredients:
2 - 16oz cans of lima beans (drained)
1 - 16oz. can kidney beans (drained)
2 - 16oz. cans of regular baked beans
1 - 15.5oz. can pineapple chunks
1/2 lb. bacon
3 medium onions
1 large green bell pepper
1/4 C. brown sugar
1/4 C. white vinegar
1/4 C. molasses
1/2 C. ketchup
2 T. mustard
1 T. garlic salt

Directions:
Mix together all beans & pineapplie in a VERY large bowl; set aside.  Cook & crumble bacon; set aside.  Cook onions in bacon fat.  Add bacon & onion to bean mixture.
In a separate bowl mix brown sugar, salt, vinegar, molasses, ketchup, mustard and green pepper; add to bean misture and stir well.  Pour into a large ovenproof dish or metal pan.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
