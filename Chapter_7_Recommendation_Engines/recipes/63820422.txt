Title:
Slow cooker Chili with Chuck Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C. Tomato paste
1/2 C. brewed coffee
2 Lbs. beef Chuck, cut into 1 1/2 in pieces
1 1/2 Tbs. Chili powder
Salt
Pepper
2 (15 oz.) cans pinto beans (do not drain)
1/2 C. crushed tortilla chips
1 1/2 C white rice
shredded cheddar cheese, avocado, scallions for topping

Directions:
Mix the tomato paste and coffee in a small bowl, set aside.
Toss the meat with the chili powder,1 1/2 tsp. salt and 1/4 tsp pepper in a 6 Qt. slow cooker. Stir in the beans (with the liquid),the coffee mixture, and the tortilla chips.
Cover and cook on low for 8 hours. Serve with rice; top with scallions and avocado.
