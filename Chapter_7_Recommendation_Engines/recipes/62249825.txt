Title:
Polenta with rosemary and parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cups low salt chicken broth
2 cups water
1 1/2 cups milk
3 garlic cloves
1 1/2 tsp. chopped rosemary
1/ tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups yellow cornmeal
8 tbsp. parmesan

Directions:
Bring first 5 ingredients to a boil and gradually add cornmeal, whisking until smooth.
Reduce heat and cook until soft and thick and creamy, stirring occasionally.  About l5 minutes.
Remove from heat and stir in parmesan and season with salt and pepper.
Butter a 2-quart casserole,  stir in more parmesan if desired,  then put into casserole and bake until golden.
375 degrees for 30 minutes

Submitter: 
food-dancer
Description: 
Creamy polenta seasoned with parmesan and rosemary -- delicious served with osso buco (braised lamb or veal shanks)
