Title:
Mounds Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C graham cracker crumbs
1/2 C butter, melted
1/3 C sugar
2 C flaked coconut
1 can sweetened condensed milk
2 C semi-sweet choc. chips
1 T shortening

Directions:
Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar and melted butter in 9 x 13 pan. Bake 5 min at 300. Combine coconut and condensed milk and spread over cooled crust. Bake 15 min at 325. Melt together chips and shortening. Pour and spread over cooled coconut layer. Refrigerate until chocolate is firm.Cut and serve
