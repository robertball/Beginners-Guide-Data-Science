Title:
Pop's Blackberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cobbler
2 quarts blackberries
3 Tbl Tapioca (quick cooking)
1/2 c. sugar
2 c. biscuit mix
3/4 c. milk
Syrup
1/2 c. sugar
1/2 c. water
1 Tbl. butter
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Combine fruit with sugar and tapioca in 13x9 in. pan.  Mix biscuit mix with milk, and cover fruit (makes about 6 large or 9 smaller biscuits).
Bake at 450 for 15 minutes.
While cobbler is baking, make the syrup. Bring water and sugar to a boil.  Stir in butter and vanilla and gently boil for several minutes. Keep warm.
When done baking, remove cobbler from oven and pour syrup over entire dish, making sure to cover all of the biscuits. Return pan to oven and bake for 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
ShellandSteve
Description: 
A simple sugar syrup gives a golden sheen to this juicy berry cobbler that's a summer staple at our house.  Also great with just peaches or a mix of both. Best with vanilla ice cream!
