Title:
Classic Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/839981.jpg
Ingredients:
1 oz lime juice (juice from 1 lime)
1 1/2 oz tequila
1 oz triple sec (or other orange flavored liquor)
1 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)
1 cup ice cubes
lime wedge, optional
course salt, optional

Directions:
For salted rim, place a small layer of course salt in a small plate.  Rub lime wedge around the rim of a glass.  Dip glass rim in salt to coat.
For a frozen Margarita, combine all ingredients in blender and blend on highest setting for 15-20 seconds.  Pour into glass.
To serve on the rocks, combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker. Shake for 20-30 seconds. Strain into ice filled glass.

Submitter: 
Patrick Washburn
Description: 
Can be served frozen or on the rocks.
