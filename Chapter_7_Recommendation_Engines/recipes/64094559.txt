Title:
Marinated Flank Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oil
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1 clove garlic, minced
1-1/3 lb flank steak

Directions:
Mix first 6 ingredients for marinade.  Put flank steak in marinade and marinate for at least 8 hours.
To cook, remove from marinade and grill 8-10 minutes per side.  If broiling, broil steak 6 inches from heat until brown, turning once, 6 minutes on one side and 4 minutes on the other.
