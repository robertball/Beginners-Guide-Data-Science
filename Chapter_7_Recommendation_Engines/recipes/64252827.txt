Title:
Pomegranate-Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1786643.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Pomegranates,
2 medium bananas
1 cup SunRype Activ Pineapple Orange Guava Juice
1 cup SunRype Tropical Twist
1 cup SunRype Strawberry Kiwi 100% Juice
2 tsp Realemon juice
2 tsp Realime juice

Directions:
1 Peel and seed the Pomegranates, saving the seeds, put the seeds in a blender or food processor and process until pulp (add up to 1/2 cup water if needed while pureeing seeds) (some seeds may still show that's ok), then pass the mixture through a fine sieve, return juice back to blender. 2. add the juices then blend till smooth adding the bananas while blending
