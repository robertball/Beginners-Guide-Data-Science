Title:
Northwest Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Romaine hearts, rinsed, drained and chopped
4 oz. crumbled blue cheese
2 ripe pears, cored and cut into bite-size pieces
3/4 cup walnuts (toasted in 350 degree oven 10 minutes, if desired.)

Directions:
Place above ingredients in a bowl.  Drizzle lightly with olive oil and a few splashes of white wine vinegar.  Add salt and freshly ground pepper to taste.
Toss and dive in!
