Title:
Holiday Smoked Salmon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (8 oz) light dairy sour cream
1 cup reduced fat mayonnaise
2 oz smoked salmon, cut into strips
1 tsp Beau Monde Seasoning
1/2 tsp dill weed
Steamed fresh artichoke petals
Assorted cut-up vegetables
Crackers or chips

Directions:
In food processor or blender container, place sour cream, mayonnaise, salmon, Beau Monde seasoning and dill weed, if desired. Paulse on-and-off until blended and salmon is coarsely chopped. Refrigerate for several hours or overnight before serving. Serve with artichoke, vegetables, crackers or chips.
