Title:
Fresh Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. whole wheat flour
3 c. raw apples, small chunks, not peeled
1 1/2 c. sugar
1-1/2 c. oil
1/2 t. salt
1 t. baking soda
1 t. cinnamon
2 t. vanilla
2 eggs
1 c. pecans, broken up

Directions:
Mix by hand.  Pour into greased 9 x 12 cake pan.  Wait 20 minutes.
Pat down gently.  Bake 50-60 minutes at 350 degrees.
