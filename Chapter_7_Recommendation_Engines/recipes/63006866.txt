Title:
Moist Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
2 tbsp. white sugar
1 cup (3 medium) bananas, very ripe and mashed
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup pureed banana baby food
1/4 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 deg. F. Coat either one 9x13 pan or two 7x3 loaf pans with cooking oil. In a small bowl combine 1/2 tsp. cinnamon and 2 tbsp. sugar. Sprinkle pans with sugar mixture.
In a medium bowl combine mashed banana, sour cream and pureed banana baby food.
In a large bowl use a mixer to cream the butter, white sugar and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time. Stir in the vanilla and cinnamon. Then stir in the banana mixture.
In a medium bowl combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and cinnamon. Stir into the banana mixture. Stir in the chopped nuts. Pour batter into pans until 2/3 full.
Place pans in preheated oven and bake for 50-60 min. or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the pan comes out clean.

This banana bread uses a couple secret ingredients to add moisture and a tangy banana flavor.
