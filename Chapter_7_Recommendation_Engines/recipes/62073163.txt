Title:
Skor Bar Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bottom:	1 1/2 cups graham crumbs	1/2 cups sugar
4 Tbsp cocoa
1/2 cup	margarine
Middle:	6 skor bars
8 oz cream cheese		1 cup icing sugar		1 lg frozen cool whip

Directions:
Prepare bottom in 9X13.  Crush 5 bars in 1 bowl.  Mix softened cream cheese, icing sugar, skor and 1/3 (about 1 cup) of the cool whip.
Spread over bottom.
Spread remaining cool whip on top.  Top with remaining skor bar.

Submitter: 
Mia
Description: 
I am not a cheesecake fan normally - but I could sit down with this cake and a fork and eat the whole thing!!
