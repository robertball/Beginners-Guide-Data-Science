Title:
Almond Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup raw almonds
3 cups water
4 ice cubes
4 dates
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Blend all ingredients except ice cubes in Vitamix for 1 minute. Add ice cubes and blend until dissolved.
Strain through cheesecloth.
Serve.
This will keep for about 3 days in the fridge.
