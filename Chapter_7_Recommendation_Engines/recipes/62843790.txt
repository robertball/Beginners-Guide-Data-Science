Title:
Rote Grutze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 oz cherries, stoned
6 oz raspberries
5.5 oz red currants
4 oz blackberries
4 oz strawberries quartered
1.75 oz golden caster sugar
2 Tbs cornstarch
5 oz water

Directions:
Place all the fruit in a saucepan with water and bring slowly to a boil. Reduce and let simmer.
Combine sugar and cornstach with 2Tbs cold water to make a smooth paste. Slowly add to sauce stirring until mixture begins to thicken.
Let cool serve with a little whipped cream.
