Title:
Tortilla Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- Vegetable cooking spray
> - 2-4 cups chopped or shredded cooked chicken breasts (I used approx 4 c
> and grilled chx w/ herb seasoning)
> - 4-5 cloves garlic, smashed/chopped
> - 1 large onion, chopped
> - 1-2 c mushrooms, sliced (optional)
> - 1 c frozen corn (optional)
> - 1 (10-ounce) can diced tomato and green chiles
> - 1 c salsa (optional)
> - 1/2 to 1 c green onion (optional)
> - 1 (10-ounce) can cream of chicken soup, undiluted (can use fat free,
> etc)
> - 1 (10-ounce) can cream of mushroom soup, undiluted (can use fat free,
> etc)
> - 1 to 2 teaspoon chili powder to taste
> - 1-2 tsp garlic powder/seasoning to taste
> - 10-12 (6-inch) corn tortillas cut into 1 inch pieces
> - 1 (8-ounce) block Cheddar (or jack or mixed -- can also use reduced
> fat) cheese, shredded
>
>

Directions:
Preparation
>
> Sauté garlic, onion in a large skillet coated with cooking spray over
> medium-high heat; add mushrooms, saute until tender.
>
> Meanwhile, grill chicken with garlic seasoning (or use ready made or
> leftover chicken).
>
> Add chicken, corn, green onion, tomato/chiles, salsa, chx soup, cr of
> mushroom soup, chili powder, other seasoning to skillet and cook just until
> combined. [Note: Add water or milk if you want creamier/juicier -- I would
> add 1/2 to 1 c of water/milk next time, esp if freezing or if expect to have
> leftovers.]
>
> Remove from heat.
>
> Cut or tear tortillas into 1-inch pieces; layer one-third tortilla pieces in
> bottom of a 13- x 9-inch baking dish coated with cooking spray. Top with
> one-third chicken mixture and one-third cheese. Repeat layers twice, topping
> with cheese.
>
> Bake at 350° for 30 to 40 minutes or until bubbly.
>
> Note: Freeze casserole up to 1 month, if desired. Thaw in refrigerator
> overnight, and bake as directed.
