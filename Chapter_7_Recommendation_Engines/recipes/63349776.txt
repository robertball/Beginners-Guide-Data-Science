Title:
Soup dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 1/2 cup flour
2/3 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Beat all ingredients together till well blended.  Use spoon and knife to cut off dough into hot boiling soup.  Cook for 5 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Volk
Description: 
Soup dumplings. Great for all those soups you want to add dumplings
