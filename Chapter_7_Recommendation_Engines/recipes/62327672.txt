Title:
Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounded chicken breast
1 Tablespoon Southwest seasoning mix
1 cup salsa
1 cup shredded Monteray Jack cheese
Cilantro Lime Rice (See Southwest Chicken Casserole recipe)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C)
Pound Chicken Breasts and coat with seasoning mix. Spray long, regular pan and put 2 blue scoops of Cilantro Lime Rice in bottom. Place seasoned chicken breasts on top, spread 2 green ladles of salsa evenly over chicken and top with cheese. Cook covered 25 mins, uncover and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 25 to 35 minutes, or until chicken is tender and juicy and its juices run clear.
Sprinkle chicken evenly with cheese, and continue baking for an additional 3 to 5 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly. Top with sour cream if desired, and serve.
