Title:
Protein added Peaches N' Cream Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/885420.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons soy milk
2 tablespoons vanilla yogurt
1/2 banana, broken into chunks
1/2 packet peaches and cream or any other favorite flavor instant oatmeal, original works too!
1 packet granular no-calorie sucralose sweetener (such as SplendaÂ® (optional)
2 and 1/2 ice cubes
1 Scoop EAS Soy Protein Lean & Toned Chocolate Protein Powder

Directions:
Add soy milk, vanilla yogurt, banana chunks, instant oatmeal, artificial sweetener, ice cubes, and protein powder into the blender and cover. Blend on High till smooth, pour into glasses, and enjoy while getting filled up!

Submitter: 
Val's Mom
Description: 
Quick and extremely filling. Great for before or after workouts and is great as a meal replacement especially when the protein powder is added.
