Title:
fried manchego cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jalapeno chili
2 tablespoons fresh cilantro
2 6 ounce wedges of manchego cheese
vegetable oil for deep frying
1/4 cup crushed ice
1 cup cold water
1 cup all purpose flour

Directions:
Finely chop and cilantro and jalapeno in a small bowl.
Coat the cheese with the cilantro and jalapeno.
Add enough oil to a large saucepan about 1 1/4 cup.
In a large bowl add ice water, ice, and flouer.
Mix with your fingers carefully until there are no lumps of flour.
dip cheese into batter.
add cheese to hot oil.
deep fry until batter is crispy.
put cheese into paper towel to drain excess oil.
