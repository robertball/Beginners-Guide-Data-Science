Title:
Simple Skillet Chicken Parm Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless chicken breasts
2 tbl dried oregano
2 cloves garlic
1 medium white onion
1 small green pepper
1 package shredded mozzarella cheese (I like the five cheese combo bag myself)
1 tbl dried basil
1 jar of your favorite pasta sauce
As much crushed red pepper flakes as you can stand

Directions:
1. Spray your pan with nonstick spray but don't turn on burner
2. After washing and drying chicken, cut into bite size pieces directly into pan. (leave out the fatty trim)
3. Cut peppers and onions into strips BUT leave off to the side.
4.  Dice garlic and add to chicken along with the oregano, basil, and crushed red pepper.
5. Turn the stove onto medium high heat stirring ingredients as you cook.  Coat each piece of chicken as evenly as possible with seasonings.
6. Cook for approximately 7-8 minutes or until outsides of chicken is cooked and the inside of chicken is just about done.
7. Pour in sauce, onions and peppers and stir until all ingredients are well mixed.
8. Cover and simmer for approx 5 minutes until chicken is cooked completely through.
9. Finally, cover the enire top of skillet with cheese (don't stir!) and cover for 3 minutes or until melted.
10. Savor the flavor with some garlic bread and salad.  Enjoy!
