Title:
Easy Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar,
2 sticks butter,
1 TBSP vanilla,
2 cups flour,
6 eggs.

Directions:
Put all ingredients together, and mix on medium speed for 10 minutes.  Pour batter into 2 greased and floured loaf pans.  Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour.  Batter for this cake is very thick.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
Very easy to make, and much better than store bought.
