Title:
screwy beef skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked corkscrew pasta
1 lb ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
2 cans tomato soup (undiluted)
1/2 cup milk (or to desired consistency)
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 TBSP worchestire sauce
1 cup frozen corn or 1 can corn
1 tsp minced garlic

Directions:
Brown ground beef with onion.
Mix ground beef, soup, milk, worchestire sauce, cheese, corn, minced garlic and pasta together.
Heat throughly.

Submitter: 
Johanna Van Ness
Description: 
Tomato soup, ground beef and corkscrew pasta. This recipe is great for a quick dinner on busy nights.  I brown all of my ground beef on the weekend for the upcoming week so it makes this even quicker.  Great served with garlic bread.
