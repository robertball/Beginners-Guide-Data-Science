Title:
Corn and Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500 grams frozen corn
6 strips bacon

Directions:
Dice bacon and place in a large skillet. Cook over medium high heat until crisp. Move to paper towels when desired level of crisp is obtained.
Add the corn into the left over bacon grease and cook until hot, then add the bacon and serve immediately.
Add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
nyxnx
Description: 
A very simple, quick side dish of corn and diced bacon.  The salty zing of the bacon with the freshness of the corn is very delicious. This is a favorite of mine to prepare due to the ease of cooking and the low carb nature of the dish.Modified from: Corn and Bacon
