Title:
Pam's Milk Tart (Traditional South African Tart) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
PASTRY
2T Castor Sugar
8T Margarine
2T Cooking Oil
1 Egg
1/4 t Salt
1/2 t Vanilla Essence
2 Cups Flour
2t Baking Powder
FILLING
1 Tin Condensed Milk
2t Vanilla Essence
3 Heaped T Maizena
3 1/2 Tins (condensed milk tins) of Milk
1 Egg

Directions:
FOR PASTRY
Cream sugar and margarine. Add oil and well-beaten egg. Mix well. Add vanilla essence then flour, salt and baking powder. Press into 2 greased pie plates. Bake at 180 deg. cel. for 15 minutes. Let them cool.
FOR FILLING
Bring condensed milk and 2 1/2 tins of the milk to the boil, stirring all the time(you don't want milk burnings at the bottom of the pot). Mix the maizena with the leftover 1 tin of milk, egg and vanilla essence. Remove the mixture from the stove, add the maizena mixture, put back on the stove and stir fast until thickened (on low heat). Pour into pie plates. Sprinkle with cinnamon and refrigerate.
