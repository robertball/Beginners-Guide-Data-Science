Title:
Cold Italian Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 boxe rigatoni pasta
1 large bottle Italian salad dressing
1 large green pepper or 2 medium
6 green onions
.5 pkg of sliced ham jullianned
.5 pkg. of pepperoni jullianned
1 large can pitted black olives
Parmesan cheese
2 cans sweet peas
2 cups of mozzerella cheese shredded
sprinkle of mint
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta and cool for 15 before adding to rest of ingridients
add half dressing and chill 2 hours then right before serving add rest. Keeps from getting mushy.
