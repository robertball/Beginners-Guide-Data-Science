Title:
Crock Pot Italian Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  46 oz Can Tomato Juice
2  29 oz Cans Tomato Sauce
1  6 oz Can Tomato Paste
1  Tbs Dried Basil
2-3 Tbs Sugar
1/4 tsp Pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredents in crock pot cook on low all day. DO NOT COVER. (As covering will make watery)
