Title:
Easy Gourmet Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 eggs
2 tbsp milk
1 tbsp onions
1 tbsp shredded cheese
butter
garnish (such as basil leaves) (optional)

Directions:
Preheat stove to medium heat, butter on whole bottom of pan
In a cup, stir eggs and milk with a fork
Slowly pour egg mix in pan
Stir gently, adding in onions
Sprinkle on cheese
When firm, scrape onto plate
Place garnish where ever desired
Enjoy immediately!

Submitter: 
Junoir Chef
Description: 
These eggs are quick, easy, and healthy. Plus, they can be modified to your likings.
