Title:
Mrs. McEwen's Squash Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked cornbread cooled & crumbled
1 stick butter - melted
8 ounces sour cream
2 TBLS pimento-drained and chopped
one can cream of chicken soup
2 1/2 cups cooked and mashed squash
1 tea sugar
1 medium onion -chopped

Directions:
mix all together bake in greased pan at 350 degrees for 30-40 minuted-- ** note- can sprinkle with shredded cheese for the last 10 minutes...

quick dressing type dish- can add a bit of sage if you wish- any squash will work:-)
