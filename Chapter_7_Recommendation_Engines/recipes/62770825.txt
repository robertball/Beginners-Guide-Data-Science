Title:
Cashew Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken in marinade:
1 lb chicken chunks
1 t soy sauce
2 t cornstarch
2 t sherry
1 T oil
Sauce:
3 T water
1.5 t cornstarch
1 t soy sauce
2 t sherry
Vegetables:
4 carrots
1 head broccoli
2 stalks celery
any other veggies you want, e.g.,
snow peans, green beans, green onions, water chesnuts
1 c cashews (roasted, unsalted is nice)
cooked rice
fried noodles

Directions:
Combine chicken in marinade ingredients in bowl or ziplock bag and refrigerate up to 1 day.
Combine sauce ingredients and refrigerate up to 1 day.
Cook the rice if you haven't already.
Heat about a tablespoon of oil in a skillet or wok on high heat. Stir fry each vegetable separately until crisp tender and remove to serving dish. A splash of water and dash of salt added to wok with each vegetable helps; add oil as needed.
Stir fry chicken in a bit more oil until cooked through.
Add vegetable juices back to wok. Add reserved sauce ingredients. Add cooked vegetables and cashews. Stir and reheat until sauce thickens.
Serve over cooked rice and fried noodles.
