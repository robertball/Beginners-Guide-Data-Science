Title:
Bonnie's Tuna Waldorf Salad Sandwich Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 fat free cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon prepared Dijon-style mustard
1/4 teaspoon curry powder
salt and pepper to taste
1 (6 ounce) can oil-packed tuna
1 shallot, finely chopped
1 Granny Smith apple, cored and diced
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup diced celery
1 teaspoon pickle relish or green olives topenade
4 leaves lettuce

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, mustard, curry powder, salt and pepper.  Add tuna, shallot, apple, walnuts, celery and pickle relish or topenade and toss until all ingredients are coated with dressing.

Submitter: 
Bonnie May
Description: 
Tuna salad gets the all-star treatment when studded with tart apples, crunchy walnuts, crisp celery and savory shallots.Modified from: Darra's Famous Tuna Waldorf Salad Sandwich Filling
