Title:
Prime Rib Rotisserie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Lbs. Boneless Prime Rib

Directions:
Pre-heat Grill to 325 degrees F
Put roast on rotisserie
Cook for 1 hour at 325F (internal temp should be about 105F)
Cook for about 10 more minutes (to internal temp of 125F)
Take off grill, let rest for 10 mins.
Serve
