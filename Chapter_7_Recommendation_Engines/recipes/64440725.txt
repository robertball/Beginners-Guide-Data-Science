Title:
To Die For Blueberry Muffins by Jeff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/4 cup butter, cubed
*****************************
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 egg
1/3 cup milk
1 cup fresh blueberries (or any fruit)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Grease muffin cups or line with muffin liners. Spray the top of the pan for overflow.
To Make Crumb Topping:
Combine sugars, flour, butter,  and cinnamon.
Mix in food processor, and sprinkle over muffins before baking.
To Make Muffins:
Combine flour, sugar, salt, cinnamon and baking powder.
Place vegetable oil into a 1 cup measuring cup; add the vanilla, egg and enough milk to fill the cup.
Mix this with flour mixture. Fold in blueberries.
Fill muffin cups right to the top, and sprinkle with crumb topping mixture.
Bake for 15 minutes in the preheated oven, then push the temperature up to 400 and continue for another 10-15 minutes, or until done.
