Title:
Mapo Tofu - Microwave Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 block firm tofu (300 grams)
1 Tbsp miso
2 tsp soy sauce
1 tsp oyster sauce
1 tsp sesame oil
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp doubanjiang
1 clove garlic, grated
1 tsp ginger, grated
100 mL water
150 grams mixed ground pork and ground beef
1 Tbsp cornstarch mixed with 1 Tbsp water
1/3 pc green onion, minced

Directions:
Drain the tofu then wrap it with a kitchen paper. Place in a microwaveable bowl then microwave for 2 minutes. Remove the wrap then slice the tofu into 2-cm cubes and set aside.
In a small bowl, combine miso, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, oyster sauce, doubanjiang, sesame oil and sugar. Mix the sauce ingredients very well.
In a big microwaveable bowl, combine the ground meat, water and the sauce ingredients then mix well.
Cover then cook in the microwave for 4 minutes using 600W.
Take the bowl from the microwave then drain the meat. Keep the liquid in one bowl then pour the cornstarch water on it.
Add the liquid, green onions, and tofu to the meat then mix carefully. Cover then bring back to the microwave and cook for another 4 minutes.
Mix carefully then serve.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
Mapo tofu is one of the most common food at Japanese homes. It's very easy to cook in a stove top but cooking it in a microwave cuts the cooking time in half. This recipe is very hard to resist. It always makes me want to eat more rice.
