Title:
Chocolate Zucchini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c sugar
3 T vegetable oil
2 large eggs
1 c applesauce or mushed bananas
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
2 T unsweetened cocoa
1 1/4 t baking soda
1 t. ground cinnamon
1/4 t salt
1 1/2 c finely shredded zucchini
1/2 c semisweet chocolate chips
cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a large bowl, combine the sugar, oil and eggs until pale and creamy. Add in the fruit puree and mix well.
In another bowl, combine the flour, cocoa, baking soda, cinnamon and salt. Add this dry mixture to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Add in the zucchini and chocolate chips and mix well.
Drop batter into muffins tins sprayed with oil. Fill about 3/4 full. Bake for about 20 minutes or until done. Makes about 18 muffins.
