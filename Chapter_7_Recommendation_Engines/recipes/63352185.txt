Title:
Broccoli and Chedder Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 deep dish pie crust
2 cups of broccoli chopped small.
4 eggs
1 TBSP Dijon Mustard
1 TSP Dried Oregano,
1/2 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 TSP Tyme
1/2 Cup milk
1/2 Cup Grated chedder Cheese
1/4 Cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
Prick pie crust, cover with oil and bake 8 minutes at 350 degrees
Place broccoli in a microwaveable bowl and microwave for 4 mins.
In a large bowl mix 4 eggs,mustard, oregano,salt, pepper, tyme, milk
Add broccoli
Place crust on a baking sheet, you can place the tin foil under it to catch anything.
Pour mixture into crust
Bake 40 minutes or until a knife inserted near the centre comes out clean.
Let stand 10 minutes before cutting
