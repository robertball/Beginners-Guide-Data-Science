Title:
Creamy Swiss Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/342820.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups uncooked medium pasta shells
2 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1 1/2 cups shredded Swiss cheese

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in boiling water for 9 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
In a 2.5 quart casserole dish, thoroughly mix cooked pasta shells, tuna, mushroom soup, sour cream, pepper, garlic powder and 1/2 cup Swiss cheese.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes in the preheated oven.  Sprinkle remaining 1 cup Swiss cheese on top and bake 5 minutes, or until cheese has melted.

Description: 
This version of the classic tuna casserole is extra creamy and flavorful!
