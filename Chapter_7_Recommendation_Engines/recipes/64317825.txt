Title:
Best, Unique Grilled Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2006817.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon of Water,
1 Tablespoon of Rice Vinegar,
1 Tablespoon of Sugar,
1/2 Teaspoon of Minced Garlic,
1 Teaspoons of Chili Flakes or Chili paste,
Two slices of Bread (Preferably White),
1 Tablespoon of Butter,
2 Slices of Cheddar Cheese,
2 Tablespoons of Cream Cheese,
One layer of Pickles (Dill or Bread and Butter),
4 Tomato Slices

Directions:
Stir Water, Vinegar, Sugar, Garlic and Chili's in a small pot on medium heat for 5 minuets.
Prepare a pan on medium to low heat.
Spread the butter on one side each of the bread slices.
Spread the cream cheese on one side of one of the slices of bread, opposite the butter.
Place cheese and pickles on top of the cream cheese.
Drizzle 1-2 Tablespoons of the sauce prepared in step 1 on both pieces of bread on the face opposite the butter.
Place the Sandwich together with the buttered sides on the outside.
Cook sandwich in the pan, 5 mins each side, or until cheese is melted and bread is browned.
After cooking, remove one slice of bread and place tomatoes in.
Enjoy!
