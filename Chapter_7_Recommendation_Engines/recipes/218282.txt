Title:
Ham and Swiss with Peanut Honey Sandwich Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977293.jpg
Ingredients:
Peanut Honey Sandwich Spread:
1/3 cup JIF® Creamy Peanut Butter
3 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon lime juice
2 teaspoons reduced-sodium soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
Sandwich:
4 honey whole wheat sandwich thins
Green leaf lettuce
4 slices Swiss cheese
1/2 pound honey ham, thinly sliced

Directions:
Combine peanut butter, honey, lime juice, soy sauce and garlic powder in food processor or whisk in medium bowl.
Spread 1 tablespoon Peanut Honey Sandwich Spread on both cut sides of the sandwich thins. Layer ham, cheese and lettuce between halves of sandwich thins and serve.

Submitter: 
Jif®
Description: 
The classic ham and Swiss sandwich gets an update with a peanut butter and lime spread on sandwich thin rolls.
