Title:
Chocolate Chip and Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120630.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1 C. Chocolate chips
1/2 C. Fresh blueberries
1/2 C. Quick oats
1 C. light cream
1 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs, beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 12 cup muffin pan. Set aside
Blend flour, b. powder and b. soda together in a small bowl. Set aside
Combine cream, sugar, beaten eggs, oats, chocolate chips and blueberries. Mix all ingredients together with a wire whisk until light and creamy
Add dry ingredients to wet mixture and stir until just combined.
Pour batter into muffin cups filling 3/4 full and bake for 20 minutes in preheated oven
Remove from oven when lightly browned and allow to cool before placing muffins on a serving plate to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very scrumptious muffin with lots of chocolate chips and blueberries in every bit of this muffin which would be good for breakfast or brunch.
