Title:
Tracy Ann's Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/474651.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/2 can  black beans, drained (not rinsed)
1/2 cup milk
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cans  cut green beans, drained
1 container French Fried Onions

Directions:
Whisk soup, milk, soy sauce, pepper in a 2-qt. casserole. Add black beans, green beans, and 2/3 container of French's(R)French Fried Onions & gently stir until evenly blended. Bake at 350 degrees F. for 25 min. or until hot. Stir and top with remaining onions. Bake for 5 min. more.
