Title:
Chicken, Ham and Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package fresh shredded hash brown potatoes from the refrigerated section
1 lb of diced ham
2 lbs cooked chicken breast, diced
1/4 cup melted butter
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup (healthy choice)
1 (8 ounce) container light sour cream
2 tablespoons minced dried onions or 1/3 cup chopped onions
2 cups shredded Velveeta Cheddar cheese
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cups crushed cornflakes cereal or crackers
1/4 cup melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine 1/4 cup melted butter, cream of chicken soup, sour cream, chopped onion, Cheddar cheese, and pepper. Mix in hash browns, ham, and chicken.  Place mixture in a 9x13 dish.
Mix cornflakes or crackers in remaining 1/4 cup melted butter, and sprinkle the mixture over the top of the casserole.
Bake covered in preheated oven for 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
This is an adaptation of our favorite easy hash brown casserole, modified into a hearty meal.  Just add a salad or vegetable side!
