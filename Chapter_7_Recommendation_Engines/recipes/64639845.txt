Title:
Filipino Spring Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4511379.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbsp cooking oil
3 cloves of minced garlic
1/2 chopped onion
1 package of ground pork or ground chicken
3 tbsp oyster sauce
3 big carrots cut into thin strips or shredded
1 bag of bean sprouts
1/2 medium sized cabbage shredded
1 package of large spring roll pastry (50 pieces)
a pinch of salt and pepper

Directions:
In a big pan or wok, add cooking oil then saute garlic and onions. Add ground pork or ground chicken and cook thoroughly then drain fat. Add oyster sauce then lower heat.
Add carrots, bean sprouts and cabbage. A pinch of salt and pepper. Mix together.
Wrap spring roll filling in pastry one at a time. Keep pastries covered.
Fry wrapped spring rolls until golden brown.
