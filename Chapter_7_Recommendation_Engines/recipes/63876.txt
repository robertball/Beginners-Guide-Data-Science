Title:
BUSH'S® Country Style Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987148.jpg
Ingredients:
6 medium red bell peppers
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 cups diced smoked turkey
1 cup diced green peppers
1 dried chipotle pepper, minced (or substitute with jalapeno pepper)
1 clove garlic, minced
2 (28 ounce) cans BUSH'S® Country Style Baked Beans
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Slice off pepper tops and set aside. Remove seeds from bottoms, set bottoms aside. On grill, heat an aluminum pan* and add oil, turkey, green peppers, chipotle pepper, garlic, salt and pepper. Cook over moderate heat for 5 minutes. Add BUSH'S Country Style Baked Beans and bring to a boil. Spoon baked beans mixture into peppers and cook on medium/hot grill for 5 minutes. Place tops over indirect heat and cook 5 minutes. Remove peppers and tops from grill. Replace tops and serve.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
Thick and rich baked beans are grilled inside red bell peppers.
