Title:
PECAN TASSIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C. Butter or Margarine
1 {8oz. Pkg.} Cream Cheese, softened
2 1/8Cs. All-Purpose Flour
2 Eggs
1 1/2Cs. Light Brown Sugar
2Tbsps. Butter or Margarine, melted
1 1/8Tbsps. Pure Vanilla Extract
Salt to taste
1 1/2Cs. Pecans, finely chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In food processor, cut butter and cream cheese into flour until dough forms.
Divide dough into 48 1-inch balls. Press each ball into a miniature muffin tin cup.
In a small bowl, combine remaining ingredients and mix well, spoon into cups and place into 350 degrees F. oven and bake 12 to 15 minutes or until golden brown.
Remove from oven and let cool 15 minutes and then place onto wire racks and let cool completely!!

These cookies are great for a Holiday gift. Simple to make and very yummy!!
