Title:
Beefy Porcupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground beef
1 egg
1 box beef flavored rice
5 cups of water split in half
2 tbl spoons beef bouillon
1 cup flour

Directions:
Open rice and set aside seasoning pkt for later use.
Mix ground beef, egg, and rice in a med bowl.
Form 1 to 1 1/2 inch meatballs and brown them on all sides.
Mix 2 1/2 cups of water, seasoning pkt and 1 tbl spoon of beef bouillon and pour over meatballs.
Bring to a boil; reduce heat to med and cover;  Cook for 30 min
Mix 1 cup of flour, 2 1/2 cups of water and 1 tbl spoon of beef bouillon.
Remove meatballs from pan and add mixture
Bring to a boil and cook untill desired thickness.  Return meatballs to sauce to coat them and serve.

Submitter: 
Donna Cuellar
Description: 
This is my kids favorite meal and there is never any left over.  The gravy is wonderful on mashed potatoes or serve the whole dish over egg noodles.
