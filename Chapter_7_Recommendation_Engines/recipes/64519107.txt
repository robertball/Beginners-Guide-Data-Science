Title:
Baked Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2879915.jpg
Ingredients:
1 20 oz can crushed pineapple
2 - 3 TBSP tapioca
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
cinnamon to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Grease loaf pan
Mix all ingredients except cinnamon until blended
Pour into loaf pan and top with cinnamon
Bake for 30 minutes
