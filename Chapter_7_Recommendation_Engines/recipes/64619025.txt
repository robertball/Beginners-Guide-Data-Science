Title:
Sauteed Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 small portobello  mushroom, cut into 3/4 inch pieces
1/2 cup sliced scallions
1/2 cup canned navy beans, drained
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 tbsp unsalted butter
1 tbsp canola oil
1/2 tsp salt
4 skinless tilapia fillets (about 5 oz each)
juice of 1/2 lemon

Directions:
1. Heat olive oil in a 12 inch non-stick skillet.  Add mushrooms and cook over high heat for about 1 1/2 minutes.
2.  Add scallions, beans, salt and pepper and stir well.
3. Transfer to bowl and set aside.
4.  Heat the butter and oil in the same skillet.
5. Sprinkle salt on fish fillets.
6.  When butter and oil are hot add the fish fillets in one layer and cook for 1 to 1 1/2 minutes each side.
7. Transfer to plate and add garnish to drippings in pan.  Saute for 1 minute.
8. Spoon garnish over fish and sprinkle with lemon juice.
