Title:
Jan's Cake Flour Substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2234168.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups all purpose flour (preferably unbleached)
1/4 cup corn starch

Directions:
Mix both ingredients together and sift 4 times to thoroughly incorporate and aerate.
Store in an airtight container.

Simple and easy way to make your own cake flour at home!
