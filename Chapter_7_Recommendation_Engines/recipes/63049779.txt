Title:
Bourbon Balls/Cajun Chef Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Equal parts crushed pecans, crushed vanilla wafers, powdered sugar
2 tsp cocoa
Tbsp Karo
Bourbon

Directions:
Equal parts pecans, wafers, powdered sugar(say cup of each)with cocoa in fp, add Karo, bourbon to a workable mix. Shape into balls, roll in sugar or cocoa powder, plain, refrig.
