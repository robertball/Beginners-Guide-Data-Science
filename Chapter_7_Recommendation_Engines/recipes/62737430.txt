Title:
Surprise Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
1/3 c. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. baking soda
2 1/4 tsp. baking powder
1/2 stick butter, melted
1 egg, beaten
3/4 c. real buttermilk
marmalade, jam, dates, prunes, peanut butter, etc. your choice
Sweet butter
1/2 c. softened butter
2 tbsp. honey
Whip items until thoroughly incorporated.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Sift flour, sugar, salt, soda and powder. Add butter, egg and buttermilk mixing only until moistened. Place 1/2 of mixture in bottoms of 12 muffin tins that have been buttered. Place your filling on top of each muffin tin over the batter until gone. Cover with remaing batter and bake for about 20 min. Top with Sweet Butter when cooled a bit.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
You can add so many different items to these muffins that you can think of. Base muffin, make it your own.
