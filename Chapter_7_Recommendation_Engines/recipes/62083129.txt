Title:
Peach Coconut Blizzard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of french vanilla betty crocker cake mix (prepare as directed...using milk in place of water)
1/4 tablespoon vanilla
2 large cans of peaches
2 cups of coconut
1 container of betty crocker whipped vanilla frosting
1 container of ultra light cool whip

Directions:
Mix cake as directed on box, substituting milk for water where indicated. Add vanilla to mixture and beat for 2-5 minutes until smooth. Drain peaches and dice into small cubes. Add one can of chopped peaches to the mixture and bake the cake as directed. (the cake might take a bit longer then indicated on the box due to the moisture of the peaches). Once cake is cooked remove and let cool completely.
Separating the cake into two layors using a string. Use the frosting for the centre of the two layors only. use the entire container. Using the second can of peaches drain them completely and place slices on top of the frosting. Place the top layor of cake over the peaches. Use the cool whip to frost the entire cake. When that is done cover the cake with coconut and place in the refrigerator.
