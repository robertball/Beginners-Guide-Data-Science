Title:
Heath bits Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. shortning
3/4 c. pnt bttr
1-1/4 c. brwn sugar
3 TBs Milk
1 TBs vanilla
1 egg
1-1/2 c. flour
3/4 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp salt
1-1/3 c. (8oz) Heath mildk chocolate toffee bits, divided

Directions:
Oven 375
Beat shortning, pnt bttr, brwn sgr, milk, and vanilla until well blended
add egg; beat just until blended
Combine flour, bakng soda, and salt; gradually beat into pnt bttr mixture
stir in 1 c. toffee bits
Drop by heaping tsp about 2" apart onto ungreased cookie sheet; top each with reserved toffee bits.
Bake 7-8 minutes or until set. Do not overbake.
Cool 2 minutes. Remove to wire rack. Cool Completely
