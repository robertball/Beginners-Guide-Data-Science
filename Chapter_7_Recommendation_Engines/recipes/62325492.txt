Title:
Lemon Coconut Cake 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups self raising flour
125 gm unsalted butter, chopped
1 cup caster sugar
4 eggs, beaten lightly
1 cup milk
2 tsp finely grated lemon rind
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 cup desiccated coconut
Icing: 2 1/2 cups icing sugar, 75gm butter, 2 tsp lemon rind, 2 tblsp lemon juice, 2 tblsp coconut milk powder. Extra shredded coconut to sprinkle on top.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 180, grease & line a 23cm round tin.
Place flour, butter & sugar in food processor, pulse 20 seconds until fine & crumbly.
Add eggs, milk, lemon rind, & juice, process 10 seconds.
Transfer to large bowl, fold in coconut. Spoon mix into tin, smooth surface. Bake 1 hour. until skewer comes out clean. Cool & Ice with lemon buttercream.
Beat butter, add icing sugar, lemon rind, coconut milk powder, add lemon juice to spreading consistency. Ice cake & sprinkle coconut on top!
