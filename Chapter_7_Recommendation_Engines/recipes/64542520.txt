Title:
Almond Puff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup flour
2 Tbsp water
1/2 cup butter
1 cup water
1 tsp almond extract
1 cup flour
3 eggs
Glaze
1 1/2 cup icing sugar
2 tbsp butter
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1-2 Tbsp warm water
3/4 cup roasted slivered almonds

Directions:
Cut 1/2 cup butter into 1 cup flour
Sprinkle 2 tbsp water over mixture and mix with fork, round into a ball and divide in half. On ungreased baking sheet, pat each half into a strip 12x3 inches and 3 inches apart
In medium saucepan heat 1/2 cup butter and 1 cup water to a roiling boil. Remove from heat and quickly stir in almond extract and 1 cup flour. Stir vigorously over low heat until mixture forms a ball (about 1 minute). Remove from heat. Beat in eggs all at one time until smooth. Divide in half and spread each half evenly over strips covering completely.
Bake about 50-60 minutes at 350. Cool and frost with glaze and sprinkle generously with roasted slivered almonds
