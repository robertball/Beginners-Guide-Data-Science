Title:
Garden Vegetable Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 unbaked pie crust
1 1/2 cups half and half
3 eggs
1 Tbsp olive oil
1/4 cup sliced scallions
1/2 cup shredded carrots
1 cup chopped broccoli florets or spinach
1 tsp salt
1 Tbsp flour
6 oz cheddar, shredded
2 roma tomatoes, sliced into rounds

Directions:
Place pie crust in pie pan and line with double thickness of foil.  Bake at 400 for 5 minutes.  Remove foil and bake another 5 minutes.  Saute scallions, carrots and broccoli or spinach in oil until softened.  In large bowl, whisk eggs, half and half, and salt together.  In a small bowl, toss cheese with flour.  Spread veggies onto pie crust, then top with cheese.  Pour egg mixture over top.  Place tomato sliced on top and place in oven.  Turn oven down to 325 and bake 45-50 minutes.  Cool at least 15 minutes before cutting.
