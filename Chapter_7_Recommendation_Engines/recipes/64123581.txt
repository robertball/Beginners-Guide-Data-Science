Title:
Strawberry Margarita Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c crushed pretzels
1/4 c melted butter
1 can (14oz) sweetened condensed milk
1 c pur√©ed strawberries
1/2 c lime juice
1 8 oz carton Cook Whip
Extra strawberries for garnish

Directions:
Combine pretzels and melted butter in 9x13 inch pan, pressing firmly to form crust.
Mix sweetened condensed milk, strawberries, and lime juice in large bowl until well blended. Stir in thawed cool whip. Pour over crust. Freeze 6 hours or overnight.
Let stand for 10-15 minutes at room temp before cutting. Garnish with extra strawberries.
