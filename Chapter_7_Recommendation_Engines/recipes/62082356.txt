Title:
Susan's Low-Fat Spinach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of chopped frozen spinach, thawed, with all liquid squeezed out
2 eggs
8 oz of 1% (skim) cottage cheese
1- 8 oz. package of finely shredded lowfat cheddar cheese (divided)
1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees and grease a pie pan.
Defrost spinach and squeeze all liquid out of each package, using papertowels if necessary.
In a bowl combine the drained spinach, eggs, cottage cheese, 7 oz of cheddar cheese and pepper.
Stir ingredients until mixture looks homogenous.
Pour into a greased pie dish and Sprinkle remaining cheese on top
Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
Let pie cool and solidify for 10 minutes before serving.
