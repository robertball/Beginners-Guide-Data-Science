Title:
Garlic & Garlic Parmesan Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Turkey
1 Bag Large Elbow Macaroni
1 heaping tbspn diced garlic
2 tbspn diced dried onions
6 tbspn Johnny's Garlic Seasoning Powder
1 can diced tomatoes (optional, flavored or not)
1/3 cup butter
3/4 grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brown Turkey with garlic, onions and 1/2 powder. Set aside.
Boil Pasta, drain and return to pot.
Add butter and 1/2 powder and stir till melted. Add turkey and tomatoes, mix, then add cheese.

A garlic masterpiece which includes pasta and ground turkey. If you love garlic, you will not be disappointed!!
