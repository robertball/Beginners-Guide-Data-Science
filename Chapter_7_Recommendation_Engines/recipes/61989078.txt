Title:
Crockpot Chicken in Mushroom Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. chicken pieces
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup dry white wine(may use
chicken broth)
1(10 3/4-oz.)can condensed cream of
mushroom soup
1(4-oz.)can sliced mushrooms,
drained

Directions:
Place chicken pieces in crockpot. Season with salt and pepper. Mix wine and soup; pour over chicken. Add mushrooms. Cover and cook on Low for 7-9 hours.

Submitter: 
Christine Westrom
Description: 
good with rice or mashed potatoes-a mixed green salad would round this out nicely
