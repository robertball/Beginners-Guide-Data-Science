Title:
Jalapeno popper with prosciutto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042769.jpg
Ingredients:
4oz fresh goat cheese
1oz grated parmigiano cheese
1/4 c finely chopped green scallion
2tsp grated lemon zest
6 jalapeno
3oz (12 slices of prosciutto)

Directions:
heat oven to 450. line a rimmed baking sheet with foil, then coat it with cooking spray.
In med bowl combine goat cheese , parmigiano, scallions and lemon zest
Halve the jalapenos lenghwise and carefully remove the ribs and seeds. Stuff each half with cheese mixture.
wrap 1 slice prosciutto around each jalapeno half. arrange the popper on baking dish bake about 15 minutes. serves 6
