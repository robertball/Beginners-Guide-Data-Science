Title:
Baked Boursin & Apple Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large eggs
3/4 cup milk
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 tsp salt
2 tbsp unsalted butter
1 apple, peeled, cored, and cut into 1/4-inch pieces
1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tbsp sugar
1/2 package (2.6 oz.) Boursin Garlic & Fine Herbs, crumbled

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F. Spray 9-inch glass pie plate with nonstick cooking spray; set aside. In blender or food processor, blend eggs, milk, flour and salt; set aside. In 10-inch skillet, melt butter over medium heat and cook apple, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes or until apple is tender. Stir in lemon juice and sugar. In prepared pie plate, add apple mixture, then evenly top with Boursin. Slowly pour egg batter over apples. Bake uncovered 25 minutes or until eggs are set and golden.
