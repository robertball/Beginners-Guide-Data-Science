Title:
Beefy Mushroom Barley Soup Personalized Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cubed beef stew meat (try beef shank!!)
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 cups broth
2 large carrots, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 onion, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
6 cups water
3 cups beef broth
1 cup pearl barley
Parsley, thyme, bay leaf
1/4 cup sour cream

Directions:
In soup pot brown floured meat in olive oil over medium heat until juices become a rich brown color.  Add  2 cups broth, and simmer meat while preparing the vegetables.
Add meat, 6 cups water, broth, parsley, thyme, bay leaf and barley to pot.  Cook until barley is soft. Adjust liquid and season with salt and pepper as desired.  Remove from heat, and stir in sour cream. Serve immediately.

Description: 
Immediately before serving, sour cream is stirred into this beef and barley soup made with carrots, onions, celery and mushrooms cooked in beef bouillon.Modified from: Beefy Mushroom Barley Soup
