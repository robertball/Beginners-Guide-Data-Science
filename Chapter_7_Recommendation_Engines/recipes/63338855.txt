Title:
Low Calorie Blender Frappe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 tray of ice cubes (approx 16-20 cubes)
- 2 TBSP name brand instant coffee (decaf or regular, I like folgers)
- 3 TBSP Torani sugar free Chocolate Flavoring Syrup
- 3 TBSP Torani sugar free Classic Caramel Flavoring Syrup
- 1 1/2 TBSP Vanilla Extract (artificial is fine)
- 1 1/4 cup skim milk
- Optional: 2 packets of saccharin or stevia for added sweetness. Mixing different artificial sweeteners enhances the sweetness.

Directions:
Blend all the above mentioned ingredients in a blender until the ice is the consistency you like. If you want a richer flavor subsititute skim milk for whole milk or even heavy cream.

Description: 
Similar to the drink people pay a premium price for at a fast food establishment, but with a fraction of the calories and cheaper too.
