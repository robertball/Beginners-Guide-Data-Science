Title:
Russian Black Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 C Water
2 2/3 T Olive Oil
1 1/3 T Molasses
1 1/3 T Vinegar
1 T Sugar
1 t Salt
2 t Unsweetened Cocoa
1 T Minced Dried Onion
1 t Instant dried coffee granules
3 t Caraway Seeds
1/4 t Fennel
2/3 C Oat Bran
2 C Bread Flour
1 1/3 C Rye Flour
2 1/2 t yeast
Egg Wash
1 Egg + 2 T Water beaten together

Directions:
Proof the yeast in lukewarm water and sugar.
In a large bowl combine the rest of the dry ingredients.
Make a tunnel in the middle and add the wet ingredients including the yeast-water mixture slowly while mixing.
Kneed dough until when you press thumb into bread dough the indentation pops right back up.
Add additional flour until dough is not sticky.
Place damp cloth over bowl and allow to rise for 1 hour.
Punch back down and form into a loaf placing loaf onto greased and dusted with cornmeal bread stone.
Cover with damp cloth and allow to rise 2nd time for 30 minutes.
Brush with egg wash.
Place in pre heated 350 degree F oven.
Bake for 45 minutes and until when knocking on loaf it sounds hollow.
