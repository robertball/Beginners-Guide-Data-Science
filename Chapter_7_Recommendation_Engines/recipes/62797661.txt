Title:
IRISH CREAM Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Bordens condensed milk
1/2 pint whipping cream
4 egg yolks
2 Tbsp.sugar
2 Tbsp.quick liquid chocolate
1 1/2 cup.Irish whiskey

Directions:
Beat cream till slightly thickened,about 2 min.Add beaten egg yolks.Strain.Add rest of ingredients.Stir well.Store in fridge.
