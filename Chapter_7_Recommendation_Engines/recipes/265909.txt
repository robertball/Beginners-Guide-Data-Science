Title:
Green Glow Smoothie Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5490985.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large banana, cut into chunks and frozen
1 cup packed baby spinach leaves
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup frozen pineapple chunks
1/4 cup raw whole almonds
1 stalk celery, cut into chunks
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon spirulina (blue-green algae) powder
1 teaspoon maple syrup, or to taste (optional)
5 blackberries
3 strawberries, sliced
1 tablespoon cacao nibs, or to taste
1 tablespoon sliced almonds, or to taste
1 tablespoon large coconut flakes, or to taste
1 teaspoon bee pollen, or to taste

Directions:
Place banana, spinach, water, pineapple, whole almonds, celery, vanilla extract, and spirulina in a large high-speed blender. Blend until smooth, stopping to scrape the jar as necessary. Taste and stir in maple syrup.
Pour mixture into a bowl. Garnish with blackberries, strawberries, cacao nibs, sliced almonds, coconut flakes, and bee pollen. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Sonja Overhiser
Description: 
This green glow smoothie bowl will have you glowing from the inside out! Spirulina powder adds a hit of nutrients to this satisfying way to get your greens.
