Title:
Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar Aldi Traditional Italian spaghetti sauce
1 lb ground round

Directions:
Brown ground meat in large sauce pan on low/med heat, stirring often to break up the meat. Drain the fat and rinse with hot water. Drain all of the water. Stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer on low for atleast 20 min.

Submitter: 
Jessica Schultz

Ground round and traditional italian spaghetti sauce.
