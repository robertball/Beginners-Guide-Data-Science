Title:
Mandy's Yummy Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of lean ground beef
1 tomato
1 white onion
1 pkg. of taco seasoning
Couple handfulls of Taco Doritos
8 oz. Western Dressing
2 cups Mexican Taco cheese
1 head of lettuce

Directions:
1) Brown the beef until no longer pink, drain the grease
2) Combine the meat and the taco seasoning per the seasoning directions
3) Cut up all of your vegetables
4) Combine all of the ingrediants right before you serve it to ensure the chips do not get soggy
5) Mix it all up with tongs and enjoy

Submitter:

Description: 
I discovered this recipe from a co-worker and I thought I would pass the recipe along to you. This is the best Taco salad ever and has won many awards!!!
