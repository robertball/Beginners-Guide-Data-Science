Title:
Night-Before Oatmeal-In-A-Jar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Quick-Cooking Oatmeal
1 Tablespoon Almond Butter (or other nut butter)
2 Tablespoons Coconut Sugar (or other sweetener
3/4 Cup Hot Water

Directions:
Add the oatmeal, almond butter, and coconut sugar to a small, tightly-lidded jar. Mason/Ball canning jars are perfect.
Store overnight in refrigerator (most almond butter requires refrigeration)
Add hot water, put lid back on oatmeal, and wait 3-5 minutes.
Stir and eat.
Notes: how much hot water you need depends on they type of oats you use and how think you like your oatmeal.

Submitter: 
Noel
Description: 
Mornings are not my thing, so if I want anything beyond a cup of coffee before heading out the door, I have to prep it the night before. My overly-expensive love of the oatmeal at Starbucks let me to start making this tasty Mason Jar Oatmeal.
