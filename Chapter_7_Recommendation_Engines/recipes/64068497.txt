Title:
Big Ray's Spiced Peach Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1131760.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 can (28 oz) peaches, drained
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/3 cup sugar
1 egg
1/3 cup molasses
1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 tsp ground ginger
3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup warm water

Directions:
Pour melted butter into a 9-in. square baking pan; sprinkle with nuts and brown sugar. Arrange peaches over nuts.
In a large bowl, cream softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in egg and molasses. Combine the flour, ginger, cinnamon, salt, baking powder and baking soda; add to creamed mixture alternately with water, beating well after each addition.
Spread batter over pears. Bake at 350Â° for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes before inverting onto a serving plate.

An intriguing variation on pineapple upside-down cake have the flavors of sweet peaches and gingerbread blending beautifully. Goes great by itself or a big scoop of ice cream.
