Title:
Easy, Cheesy Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 strips of hickory smoked bacon
10 cups frozen shredded hashbrowns (1 bag)
12 oz fat free sour cream
1/4 cup 2% milk
2 cans cream of chicken soup
5 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup + 1TBLS melted butter or margarine
seasoning salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop bacon into small pieces and fry in a skillet.
Save about 2 TBLS of the bacon grease and drain the rest.
Preheat oven to 375.
In a bowl mix cream of chicken soup, milk, sour cream, and 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese.
In a 9x13 glass baking dish pour 1/4 cup melted butter and 1 TBLS bacon grease, coating bottom of pan.
Pour 5 cups of hashbrowns into greased pan.
Pour 1/2 sour cream mixture over the hashbrowns and spread out with a spoon.
Sprinkle 2 cups cheese over this.
Add remaining 5 cups of hashbrowns, then 1/2 mixture, then last 2 cups cheese.
Sprinkle chopped bacon over the top of the cheese.
Pour remaining 1 TBLS butter and 1 TBLS bacon grease over the top.
Bake in oven uncovered for 55 minutes.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Toast
Description: 
Hickory smoked bacon really adds zip to this simple but delicious hasbrown casserole. And it's so versatile!
