Title:
The Best Banana Split Cake Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1 1/4 cups suger (divided)
1 stick of butter, melted
2 pkg. 8oz cream cheese, softened
1 20 oz can crushed pineapple, drained
6 bananas
2 cups cold milk
2 pkg vanilla flavor instant pudding
2 cups whipped topping, divided
1 cup chopped pecans (optional)
Maraschino cherries
Shaved Chocolate

Directions:
Step 1. Mix crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar & butter; press on bottom of 13 x 9 inch baking dish. Freeze for 10 minutes
Step 2. Mix with blender cream cheese and remaining 1 cup of sugar in bowl until well blended. Spread cream cheese mixture over crust, top with pineapple. Slice 4 bananas and arrange over pineapple. Tip: soak bananas in pineapple juice so the bananas don't brown.
Step 3. Mix milk and dry pudding mixes together in a large bowl until well blended. Stir in 1 cup of whipped topping. Spread over bananas in baking dish. Top with remaining whipped topping, chopped pecans. Refrigerate for 4-5 hours. * Before serving arrange over dessert the remaining two bananas sliced, chopped pecans, maraschino cherries and shaved chocolate.
Step 4. Enjoy!
