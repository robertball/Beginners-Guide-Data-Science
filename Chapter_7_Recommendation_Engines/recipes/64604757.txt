Title:
Easy Dorito Casserole! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4014447.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (Party Size) bag of Doritos
1lb ground beef
1 package of taco seasoning
1 cup of salsa
1 can of cream of chicken or cream of mushroom
2 cups of shredded cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook ground beef; drain, then add taco seasoning and follow directions on taco packet until it's done.
In a large bowl, combine salsa, cream of chicken (or mushroom), 1 cup of cheese and cooked taco meat and stir well.
Get an 8x8 casserole dish and spray the bottom with Pam or other non stick cooking spray and crush up Doritos on the bottom of the dish, then add some of the beef mixture and repeat the layers. Finally top it off with adding more crushed up doritos and the remaining shredded cheese.
Spray a piece of foil with cooking spray and cover the casserole. Bake casserole for 30 minutes covered, remove foil and bake uncovered for an additional 20 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
