Title:
Queso Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Velveeta cheese
1 Lg. Package Cream Cheese
1 lb. Hamburger (cooked and drained)
2 Can Ro-Tel tomato's w/ Chili's

Directions:
Melt together cheeses, add tomato's w/ chili, add Hamburger let cool in fridge or serve warm with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Cathy Adams-Kane
Description: 
 This is the best recipe I have tasted, It was handed down to me from a co-worker who makes it every year for our Christmas party.
