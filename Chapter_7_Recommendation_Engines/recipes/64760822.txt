Title:
Dilled Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head dill per pint (or 1/2 tsp. dill seed & some dill weed)
1 small garlic clove per pint
small whole carrots or larger ones cut into spears to fill 1 pint
1 tbsp. course (pickling) salt per pint
1/4 cup boiling vinegar per pint
boiling water to fill jar

Directions:
Put dill and garlic in hot sterile pint jar.
Fill with carrots to within 1" of top.
Measure salt over carrots.
Add vinegar which has been heated 1 min. in microwave.
Fill with water to within 1/4" of top.
Seal tightly.
Let stand 6 weeks before using.
