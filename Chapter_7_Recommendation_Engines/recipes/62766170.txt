Title:
Crustless Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
A couple good sized tomatos on the firm side
Toppings of your choice. ( I used black olives, mushrooms, and bacon. )
Shredded Mozarella
Italian Seasoning
Sea Salt
Black Pepper
Piece of aluminum foil
Baking sheet

Directions:
Set your oven to 400Â°
Slice tomatos medium thick (1/4-1/2 inch)
Cover baking sheet with foil
Arrange tomatos in single layer
Lightly sprinkle tomatos with italian seasoning, salt, & pepper
Top tomatos with toppings of your choice
Put cheese on top
Bake in oven

Submitter: 
SHANYCHRIS
Description: 
Though I know that this will help satisfy the pizza craving for the Low-carb enthusiast out there I created this recipe because I'm not into bread or crust. I eat the stuff occasionally but I wanted the choice to go crustless. Oh and no this recipe does not incude a big meat patty.
