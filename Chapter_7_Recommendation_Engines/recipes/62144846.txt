Title:
Pineapple Upside-Down Cake Mixed Shot / Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz. vanilla vodka
1 oz. pineapple juice
1 drop grenadine syrup

Directions:
Pour vanilla vodka and pineapple juice into a shot glass.
Sink a drop of grenadine syrup to bottom of glass.
Cheers!
