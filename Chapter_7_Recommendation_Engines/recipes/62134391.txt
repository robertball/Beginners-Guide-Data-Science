Title:
Mushroom & Bacon Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz package white mushrooms (sliced)
1 cup frozen green beans
2 TBS butter
1 tsp minced onion
1/2 tsp minced garlic
Sprinkle lemon pepper
Sprinkle seasoning salt
2 TBS crumbled bacon

Directions:
Saute on medium heat butter, onions, garlic, and mushrooms until they just begin to get tender.
Add lemon pepper, seasoning salt, and crumbled bacon and continue cooking until mushrooms are fork tender.
Add frozen green beans and stir occasionally until green beans soften and heat through.

Lee Ann Girard
