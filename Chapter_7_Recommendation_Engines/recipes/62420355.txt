Title:
Latin Inspired Spice Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/444194.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup Cumin Seeds
3 Tablespoons Whole Black Peppercorns
1 Tablespoon Coriander Seeds
1-1/2 Tablespoon Sugar
1-1/2 Tablespoon Sea Salt
1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder

Directions:
Combine cumin, peppercorn and coriander in heavy medium skillet.  Stir over medium heat until fragrant and toasted, about 8 minutes.  Cool slightly.  Finely grind toasted spices in the blender, add salt, sugar and garlic powder.  Transfer to bowl or container.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This seasoning is a great combo of latin flavours. Toasting the spices adds an extra punch to the spice mixture. It works great for meats, soups and rice.  I usually add about 1 teaspoon per pound of meat.
