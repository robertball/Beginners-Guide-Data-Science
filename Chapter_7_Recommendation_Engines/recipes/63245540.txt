Title:
Garlic and Mustard Rubbed Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon ground mustard
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 2 pound pork tenderloin roast

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
Mix together the garlic powder, ground mustard, salt and pepper.
Rub the seasoning mixture on the pork tenderloin.
Bake the seasoned pork roast for 1 hour (30 minutes per pound) or until the internal temperature reaches 170 degrees. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
A pork tenderloin roast is coated with a simple but flavorful rub and baked. The recipe is quick and easy. The amount of the rub can be adjusted to individual taste.
