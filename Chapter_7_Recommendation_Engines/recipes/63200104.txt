Title:
Oven-Roasted Turkey Breast (3-lb) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/827165.jpg
Ingredients:
2.5-3.0 lb Turkey Breast, boneless or bone-in
1/4 C butter, melted
1 clove Garlic, crushed
1 tsp Paprika
1 tsp Italian Seasoning
1/2 tsp Mrs Dash seasoning
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 F
Mix all the seasonings together with the melted butter.
Set turkey, skin side up, in a small roasting pan
Brush 1/2 of the butter/spice paste all over and under the skin
Tent loosely with foil
Bake for 60 minutes, remove foil, brush with rest of butter/spice paste
Roast until inner temp reaches 165 F (approx 30 minutes), remove and allow to rest for 10-15 minutes so juices settle.
