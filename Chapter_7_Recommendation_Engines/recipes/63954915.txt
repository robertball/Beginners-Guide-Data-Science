Title:
Best Ever Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large ripe bananas
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1/3 cup butter, melted
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups flour

Directions:
Oven 375. Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. In large, separate bowl mash bananas, add sugar, mix. Add to this cooled melted butter and slightly beaten egg. Mix well and add dry ingredients. Once mixed you can add a handful of chocolate chips and a handful of white chips if desired. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full and bake for 20 minutes until golden and toothpick comes out clean.
