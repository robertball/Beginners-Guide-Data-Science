Title:
Crustless Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 Cup Splenda
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour OR wheat flour
3 eggs
2 cups canned pumpkin
3/4 cup fat free evaporated milk
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoom pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the Splenda and flour. Add eggs; mix well. Stir in the pumpkin, milk, vanilla and spices; mix until well blended. Pour into a greased 9-in. pie plate. Place pie plate in a 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan; add 1/2 in. of hot water to pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 50-55 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Cynda Baker
Description: 
Baked in a water bath, this pie has a texture that's more like a custard than a traditional pumpkin pie. In place of canned or fresh pumpkin, I sometimes use cushaw, a type of crookneck squash.Modified from: Crustless Pumpkin Pie
