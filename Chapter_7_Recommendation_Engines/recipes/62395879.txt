Title:
Tuna On Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream mushroom soup
1 can tuna
4 slices of toast

Directions:
In saucepan combine soup and tuna; heat.  Pour 1/4 mixture over each slice of toast.

Description:
