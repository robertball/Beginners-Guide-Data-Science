Title:
Broccoli Slaw w/ Poppy Seed Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, diced - Chicken
Celtic sea salt to taste - Chicken
Pepper to taste - Chicken
(Makes 1 cups)
¼ cup and 2 T organic sugar - Dressing
1-½ teaspoons Dijon mustard - Dressing
½ teaspoon Celtic sea salt - Dressing
2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons raw organic apple cider vinegar - Dressing
1/4 cup onion, grated - Dressing
½ cup vegetable oil (I used grape seed oil) - Dressing
2-1/4 teaspoons poppy seeds - Dressing
1 bag of broccoli slaw - Salad
1 bag of baby spinach - Salad
1 cucumber, diced  - Salad
1 red pepper, diced  - Salad
1 inch piece of ginger, minced  - Salad
1 small package of basil (leaves only), julienned
1-3 clove garlic, minced  - Salad
Cashews, garnish  - Salad

Directions:
Chicken - Dice chicken and place into a pan.
Chicken - Salt and pepper chicken to taste.
Chicken - Once chicken is cooked thoroughly, set aside to cool.
Dressing - In a blender, blend all ingredients together.
Salad - Place all ingredients in a large bowl and mix together.
Salad - Pour on dressing and mix well.
Salad - Serve with cashews and cold chicken as a garnish.
