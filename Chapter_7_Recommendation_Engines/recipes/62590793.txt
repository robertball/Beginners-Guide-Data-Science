Ingredients:
12 oz bag of whole wheat noodles
1 lb of meat (you can use beef, turkey, or chicken) I get a lean ground cut.
15 oz of marinara sauce (jar of Ragu or whatever brand you fancy)
10 oz package of frozen spinach
2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
1 large egg
3-5 scallions or green onions (personal preference to taste)

Directions:
Coat a 2 qt oven save dish with a non stick spray or cover oven save dish with foil and spray with a non stick spray. Preheat oven to 350.
Microwave the frozen spinach for 3-5 minutes and allow it to cool and squeeze extra liquid from waxy box.
Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook the pasta alone for 5 minutes.
Cook meat in a separate pan until it is no longer pink; remove from heat and stir in marinara sauce.
Drain pasta and place into oven safe dish. Combined the noodles, cottage cheese, egg, cheese, scallions/green onions, and well drained spinach; Stir/toss lightly. Pour the meat sauce over the top and sprinkle with remain cheese from the package.
Put it in the oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.  Allow it to cool a few minutes before serving.
