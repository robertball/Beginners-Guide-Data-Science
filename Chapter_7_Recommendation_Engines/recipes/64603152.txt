Title:
Italian salami sandwich loaf made with bread machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4  cup milk (or 3/4 cup water with 2 Tbsp milk powder)
1 Tbsp oil
1 Tbsp sugar
1 tsp salt
2 cups flour
1 1/4 tsp yeast
Topping
2 cups sliced peppers
1/2 cup onions sliced
1 cup salami
1/4 cup mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Put the ingredients for the bread in bread machine and make the dough.
When the dough is ready, cut into 3 pieces and let rise 15 mins then roll each out into 7 inch circles.
Put 1/2 of the toppings on one circle then top with another circle then the rest of the topping then place the remaining circle on top and fold over edges to seal securely.
Let rise 30 minutes then bake on lowest rack in oven at 375 degrees for about 40-45 mins.
