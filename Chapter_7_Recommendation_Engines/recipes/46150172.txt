Title:
Best Drop Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. all-purpose flour
2 t. baking powder
1/2 t. baking soda
1 t. sugar
3/4 t. salt
1 c. cold buttermilk
8 T. unsalted butter, melted and cooled slightly (about 5 mim.) + 2 T. melted butter for brushing biscuits

Directions:
Adjust oven rack to middle position and heat oven to 475 degrees. Whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar and salt. Combine buttermild and 8 T. melted butter in med. bowl, stirring until butter forms small clumps
Add wet mixture to dry and stir w/rubber spatula until just incorporated and batter pulls away from sides of bowl. Using greased 1/4-C. dry measure, scoop level amount of batter and drop onto parchment-lined rimmed baking sheet. Space biscuits about 1 1/2 in. apart. Bake until tops are golden brown and crisp, 12-14 min.
Brush tops w/remaining 2 T. melted butter. Cool 5 min. before serving

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
If buttermilk not available, powdered buttermilk added according to pkg. instructions or clabbered milk may be used. Clabbered milk-mix 1 C milk w/1 T. lemon juice and let stand 10 min. A 1/4C scoop can be used to portion batter. To refresh day old biscuits, heat in 300 degree oven for 10 min.
