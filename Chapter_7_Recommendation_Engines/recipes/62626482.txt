Title:
Red Pepper Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 roasted red peppers
1 clove roasted garlic
2 Tbsp cream cheese
1 tspn flour
1 tspn butter
1/4 cup milk
1 1/2 cups cream
1 tbsn choppes chives
Salt, pepper, red pepper flakes
Chop garlic and peppers.

Directions:
Chop garlic and peppers.
Melt butter and add flour, blend.
Add milk, blend, then pepper and garlic
Season to taste
Pour mixture in blender-blend until smooth
Return to pot, add cream, cream cheese and chives and heat until cheese melts.  If you need a bit more flour whisk it in now.
Reduce temp, heat until it thickens
