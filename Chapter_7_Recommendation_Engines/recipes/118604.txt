Title:
Bagel Smiles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977281.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Whole wheat bagels
1/4 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing
1/4 cup whipped cream cheese
Face Decoration Ideas:
American cheese slices
Asparagus spears
Shredded carrots
Red bell peppers
Raisins
Broccoli florets
Canned beans (black, garbanzo, pinto)
Grape tomatoes

Directions:
Split bagels in half; lightly toast.  Mix Ranch and cream cheese in small bowl; spread even amounts of mixture over each bagel half.  Use imagination to assemble faces.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
These healthy Ranch flavored sandwiches are so fun to make your kids will enjoy helping out in the kitchen.
