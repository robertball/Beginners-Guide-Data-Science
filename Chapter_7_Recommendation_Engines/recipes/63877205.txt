Title:
Easy Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075559.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger
1 lb block Velveeta cheese
1 medium jar salsa
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Brown hamburger & drain. Cut cheese into chunks and place on hamburger.
Add salsa to hamburger & cheese, Cook slowly over medium until cheese is completely melted.
Mix thoroughly & serve with tortilla chips.
