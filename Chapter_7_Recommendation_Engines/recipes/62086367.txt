Title:
Anise Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Rogers corn syrup
2 cups of brewed coffee
4 cups all purpose
flour
1 Tbsp ground anise seed
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Combine syrup, sugar, coffee.
Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, ground anise seed and salt together.  Stir wet ingredients with dry ingredients and pour into 2 greased loaf pans.  Bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes.  Let cool and enjoy!
Tip if using glass loaf pans reduce temperature 25 degrees and cook for 5-10 minutes longer or until a tooth pick comes out clean from the centre of the loaf.

Submitter: 
EBSEPKE
Description: 
I got this recipe from my brother-in-law who is Dutch.  This cake reminds him of home and has become a family favorite of ours as well.  A dark dense loaf with the mild flavor of black licorice.  A great loaf  on it's own or paired with a nice cup of coffee.
