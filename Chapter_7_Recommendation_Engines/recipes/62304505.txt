Title:
Coconut Pecan Brownies Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/367081.jpg
Ingredients:
1 family size box of brownie mix (18.3oz)
1/2 can of coconut pecan frosting
1 cup of chocolate chips
6 T butter
1/3 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Bake your brownies according the directions on the brownie mix using an square 8x8" pan; bake at 325 degrees for 52-54 minutes.  Let brownies cool for 10 minutes in the freezer.
Spread the coconut frosting over the brownies and place them back in the freezer while you make the fudge topping.
Heat the chocolate chips and butter in a microwave safe bowl on med heat until melted, stir occasionally.  Mix well until smooth and all the chips are melted then pour over the coconut pecan frosting. Sprinkle the chopped pecans over the fudge and chill in the refridgerater until fudge topping is set.
Cut into squares and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Traci-in-Cali
Description: 
I came up with this easy and delicious brownie while trying to use up some leftover frosting.  It's a thick chocolate brownie covered in coconut pecan frosting then topped with a fudge topping and more pecans; scrumptious!
