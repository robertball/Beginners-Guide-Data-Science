Title:
Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1Can - Large Buscuits
1lb - Ground Sausage
3c - Souther Style Hashbrowns
2c - Casserole Cheese
8 - Eggs (scrambled)

Directions:
Preheat the oven at 350 degrees. Spread buscuits covering botton of baking dish like a crust. Brown sausage, and spread on top of buscuit crust. Brown hashbrowns, and spread in with sausage. Sprinkle cheese on top as well. Beat eggs (however you prepare your scrambled eggs), and pour over the casserole. Bake for 30 min. or until eggs fully set.
