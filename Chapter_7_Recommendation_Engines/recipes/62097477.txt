Title:
Happyhouse Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Packet Taco Seasoning (1.25 oz.)
1 Pound Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts (About 2 breasts)
2 C chicken broth
8 Soft Taco Size Tortillas or Hard taco shells
Taco Fillings:
Shredded Cheese
Diced Tomatoes
Salsa
Shredded Lettuce
Sour Cream

Directions:
Place the boneless skinless chicken breasts in the crock pot.
Empty packet of taco seasoning into 2 cups of chicken broth, and pour over chicken.
Cook on low 6 hours.
After 6 hours, shred the chicken with two forks.
Continue cooking shredded chicken on low for 3 hours.
Scoop chicken into tortillas or taco shells and cover with choice of toppings.

Submitter: 
happywife05
Description: 
All you need for these yummy shredded chicken tacos is a crock pot and a packet of taco seasoning. It is very easy for a weekday meal.
