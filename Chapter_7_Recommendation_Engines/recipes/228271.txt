Title:
Watermelon and Pineapple Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2073126.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped seedless watermelon
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
3 tablespoons orange juice
1/8 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHotÂ®)

Directions:
Toss watermelon, pineapple, onion, cilantro, orange juice, and hot pepper sauce together in a bowl. Refrigerate 1 hour before serving.
