Title:
Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags of instant mashed potato
5 1/3 c water
2 c coconut milk
1 stick of butter
Sea salt
Black Pepper

Directions:
Bring water to a boil with butter, salt and pepper
Pull off the heat
Add contents of packets while stirring
Stir well
Serve
