Title:
Crock Pot Chili with Beef and Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs grass fed ground beef
2-4 T olive oil or more as needed
1 medium yellow onion diced
1 small red onion diced
5-10 cloves garlic minced
1 large carrot shredded
1/2 summer zucchini shredded
1 red bell pepper diced
1 28 oz can crushed fire roasted tomatoes plus more if needed
1/2 can tomato paste
beef bone broth or stock
3/4 c gluten free lager beer (Omission) or use beef bone broth or stock
2-4 T cumin
2-4 T chili powder (use less if using chipotle)
2-3 T smoked paprika
2 T tsp cacoa powder
2-4 T oregano
dash cinnamon
dash cloves
1-2 tsp chipotle powder (1 tsp might be enough)
salt to taste
little smoked salt/ribs spice rub

Directions:
saute onion, garlic in olive oil, add salt
add red pepper, carrots, squash
saute until golden, soft
add spices/herbs and salt
deglaze pan with beer, cook off alcohol
add tomatoes and tomato sauce, bring to simmer
meanwhile, brown ground beef in little olive oil, salt and additional spices
put all in crock pot on low for 4-5 hours (started on high and reduced; be careful not to cook too long/high because meat deteriorates)
serve with cilantro, green onion, cheese, full fat greek yogurt
