Title:
Roasted Curried Cauliflower Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1010655.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head of Cauliflower, chopped into florets
1 onion, sliced into thin rings
2 tbsp olive oil
1 1/2 tsp curry powder
1 tsp kosher salt
2 1/2 cups vegetable broth, low sodium or make your own.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Toss Cauliflower and onion into a baking pan.
Mix Olive oil, curry powder and salt together.
Drizzle the mixture over the Cauliflower and onions, tossing or brushing to coat.
Roast for 20-25 mins.
In a blender, add 3 cups of vegetable broth.
Then add 3 cups of the roasted cauliflower & onions.
Blend using puree/soup option.
Pour 1 cup of soup into each bowl with approx. 1/2 cup of the remaining roasted vegetables.
