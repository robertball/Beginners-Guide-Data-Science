Title:
Chicken Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
12 oz. noodles (any kind)
2 cloves garlic
1/2 c. chopped carrots
1/2 c. chopped celery
1/4 c. butter
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 c. sour cream
1 Tbsp dried parsley
Other herbs (to taste)
Salt and pepper (to taste)
1 sleeve of Ritz Crackers, crushed

Directions:
In a large pot, boil salted water and drizzle in olive oil.  Boil chicken until down, about 20 min.  Cook noodles according to package directions.  While noodles are cooking, cut up the chicken into 1/2 inch pieces.  Drain pasta and set aside.  Saute veggies in butter, add soups and sour cream, stirring well.  Salt and pepper as needed.  Add noodles, chicken and herbs to soup mixture and mix well.  Transfer to a greased casserole or 9x13 pan.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until bubbly.  Top with cracker crumbs and bake 5-10 min more.
