Title:
Frauline's 2 Layer Icecream Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Boxes icecream sandwiches
1 small jar crunchy peanut butter
1 large Cool Whip
1/2 box chocolate pudding (dry)
2 8 packs single Reeses PB cups
1 jar hot fudge & caramel sauce

Directions:
Layer 1 box of sandwiches in 9x13 dish.
Melt peanut butter & spread.
Mix 1/2 packet of pudding into 1/2 tub of Cool Whip & spread.
Dice up 1 8 single pack Reeses PB cups over top.
Warm hot fudge & caramel sauce and drizzle.
Repeat steps for 2nd layer - minus adding dry pudding mix to cool whip.
Freeze 1-2 hours.

Submitter:
