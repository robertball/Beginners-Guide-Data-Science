Title:
Peach Clafouti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. melted butter
1/2 cup flour
1/3 cup light brown sugar
1/4 cup old fashioned oats
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
2 eggs, lightly beaten
3/4 cup milk
3 cups peeled, sliced fresh peaches or nectarines.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Grease bottom and sided of 1 1/2 qt. baking dish.
Combine flour, sugar, oats, cinnamon, salt and nutmeg in a large bowl and stir well.
Add beaten eggs and mild and stir until smooth.
Place fruit in prepared dish and pour batter over fruit.
Bake about 25 minutes or until puffy and golden brown.
