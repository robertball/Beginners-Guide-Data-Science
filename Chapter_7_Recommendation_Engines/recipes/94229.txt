Title:
FRITOS® Chili Pie Chili Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7925.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup FRITOS® crushed corn chips
1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL® Chili No Beans
1 cup grated American cheese
1/2 cup chopped onion
10 hot dogs
10 hot dog buns

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. In bowl, combine corn chips, chili, cheese and onion. Split hot dogs lengthwise down the middle (but not completely through). Place chili filling down the center of each dog. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until warmed through. If desired, sprinkle with additional chips before serving.

Submitter: 
HORMEL® NATURAL CHOICE® Deli Sandwich Meats
Description: 
Everyone will love these dogs stuffed with a delicious and crunchy mixture of chili, cheese and chips!
