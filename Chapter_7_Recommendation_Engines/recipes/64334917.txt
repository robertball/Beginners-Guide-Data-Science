Title:
Marie's Dark Chocolate Chip Yogurt Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Greek Yogurt
1/4 cup Cocoa Powder
1 Tbsp Honey, Maple Syrup, OR 1 packet Sweetener
1/2 tsp Cinnamon
1/4 cup Chocolate Chips

Directions:
In a bowl, mix all but the chips together and chill in fridge. Then top with chocolate chips.

Description: 
Just a simple and healthy chocolate Greek yogurt dip.
