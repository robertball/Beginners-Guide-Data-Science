Title:
Welch's Sparkling Holiday Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cold water
3/4 cups sugar
5 cinnamon sticks
1 bottle Welch's Sparkling Red or White Grape Juice Cocktail, chilled
1 cup orange juice
1/2 cup lemon jucie
3 bottles (16 oz each) lemon-line soda, chilled
Ice cubes made with Welch's Sparking Red or White Grape Juice Cocktail

Directions:
In medium saucepan, combine water,sugar and cinnamon stickes to make cinnamon syrup. Heat to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly.
Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes. Remove cinnamon sticks, cover and refrigerate 2 hours.
In a large punch bowl, combine cinnamon syrup, Sparkling Grape Juice Cocktail, orange juice, lemon juice and soda. Add ice cubes. If desired garnish punch with additional cinnamon sticks or float fruit filled ice cubes, or orange, leamon or lime slices.
