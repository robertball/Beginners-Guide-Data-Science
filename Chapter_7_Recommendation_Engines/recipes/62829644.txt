Title:
Slow Cooker pulled pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1069179.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 lb) boneless pork shoulder
1 envelope Lipton Recipe Secrets Onion Soup Mix
1 cup ketchup
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup Apple Cider Vinegar
OPtional: Hot Sauce to taste
10 Kaiser rolls or Hamburger buns or about 20 Hawaiian Rolls

Directions:
1. Arrange pork in slow cooker. Combine remaining ingredients, except for rolls, and pour over pork. Turn to cook pork evenly.
2. Cook covered on LOW 8-10 hours or HIGH 4-6 hours until pork is tender.
3. Shred pork with fork(s) & mix with remaining juices. Serve on rolls/buns.
