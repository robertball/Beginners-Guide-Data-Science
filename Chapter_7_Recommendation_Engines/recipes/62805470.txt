Title:
Sugar-Free Ice Cream II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lg. boxes sugar free pudding
4 eggs
2 cans milnot or low cholesterol milk
2 tbsp. vanilla
Dash of salt

Directions:
Mix pudding as directed on box then add eggs, milk, vanilla and salt. Add enough 2% milk or whole milk if preferred to fill freezer can 2 1/2 to 3 inches from top. Freeze and enjoy. Makes 1 gallon.
