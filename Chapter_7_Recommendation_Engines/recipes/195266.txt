Title:
Unstuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990814.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (26 ounce) jar RAGÚ® Old World Style® Traditional Sauce
2 large green and/or red sweet peppers, coarsely chopped
1 1/4 cups water
1 cup instant rice

Directions:
In 12-inch nonstick skillet, brown ground beef; drain, if desired. Stir in remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook covered 20 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.

Submitter: 
Ragu®
Description: 
An easy-to-make version of a classic that's light on labor, this hearty meal smells heavenly as it simmers.
