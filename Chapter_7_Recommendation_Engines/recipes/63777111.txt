Title:
Shrimp Pasta Salad With a Creamy Lemon Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
• 10 ounces penne pasta
• 1/2 cup chopped scallion
• 1/2 cup cheddar cheese, grated
• 1/2 lemon, thin sliced (for garnish)
• 40-50 medium shrimp
• 2 tablespoons of olive oil
Dressing
• 4 tablespoons greek yogurt
• 4 tablespoons chicken broth
• 2 tablespoons lemon juice
• 3 teaspoons Dijon mustard
• 2 teaspoons minced garlic
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 teaspoon kosher salt, the dish ( to season)

Directions:
1. Dressing -- mix all the ingredients and set to the side until the pasta is cooked.
2. Salad -- In a large pot of salted water, boil the pasta according to directions. Approximately 3 minutes before the pasta is done. Now drain the pasta. Rinse under cold water for just a few seconds to stop the cooking process and cool slightly.
3. Shrimp -- While pasta is cooking, toss shrimp in 2-3 tablespoons of olive oil, add seasoning. Put in glass baking dish in the oven for 10-12 minutes on 450.
4. Combine -- Now toss the still slightly warm pasta with the dressing. Add in the scallions, extra salt and pepper and serve garnished with the cheese fresh lemon slices. A nice simple salad!

Submitter: 
Brandi Starr
Description: 
Very light flavor and low calories (224 per serving). Originally meant to be a side dish I made some healthy substitutions and added shrimp. I usually serve with asparagus, the entire family loves it.
