Title:
Ann D's Delicious Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup granulated sugar
5 tbsp. butter
1/3 Cup milk
1 small pkg (8oz.) chocolate chips

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, mix the sugar, butter, and milk and bring to a boil. Stir and boil for 1 minute.
Add the small package of Chocolate Chips and mix well.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
The lady who gave this recipe to me has since passed away but her memory lives on with her delicious recipes.  An easy to prepare frosting for cakes and cupcakes.
