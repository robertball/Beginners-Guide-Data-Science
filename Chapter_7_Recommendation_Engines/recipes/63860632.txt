Title:
Garlic-Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 1/2 pounds fresh Brussels sprouts, halved
2 Tablespoons olive oil
3 garlic cloves, minced
1/cup heavy or half-and-half cream
3 Tablespoon Dijon mustard
1/8 teaspoon white pepper
Dash salt

Directions:
1. Place Brussels sprouts in an ungreased 15-in x 1-in. baking pan.  Combine oil and garlic; drizzle over sprouts and toss to coat.
2. Bake, uncovered, at 450 degrees for 10 - 15 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally.
3. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, combine the cream, mustard, pepper and salt.  Bring to a gentle boil; cook for 1-2 minutes or until slightly thickened,.  Spoon over Brussels sprouts.
