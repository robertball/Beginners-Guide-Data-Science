Title:
Tiramisu Toffee Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) pound cake, cut into slices
3/4 cup strong brewed coffee (6 T coffee per 1 cup water)
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup chocolate syrup or Irish Cream
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
2 cups heavy whipping cream
2 (1.4 ounce) bars chocolate covered English toffee, chopped

Directions:
Beat sugar, chocolate syrup, and cream cheese in a large bowl with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.
Beat whipping cream till stiff peaks form.  Fold in sugar, chocolate, and cream cheese mixture.
Layer everything twice in a trifle bowl...cake, coffee, cream, toffee, cake, coffee, cream, toffee. Load the top with toffee and drizzle with chocolate syrup or even ribbons of chocolate.
Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, but no longer than 24 hours to set dessert and blend flavors.  Best if made the day before.

Submitter: 
Jeannine Kuck
Description: 
This is a nice version of the popular Italian pick-me-up dessert. The toffee candy in this recipe adds a delightful crunchiness to the smooth creamy whipped cream quality of an already perfect dessert.Modified from: Tiramisu Toffee Dessert
