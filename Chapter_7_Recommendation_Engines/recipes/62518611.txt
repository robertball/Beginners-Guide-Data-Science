Title:
Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs. stew meat, cut into cubes
1 bag baby carrots
3 medium russet potatoes, cut into chunks
1 onion, diced
1 can Italian stewed tomatoes
2 tbs. flour
Pepper, to taste
1/3 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp basil
1/4 tsp oregano
1 large (46 oz) can tomato juice
1 tbs worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Place half of the vegetables in the slow cooker.
Add beef.
Mix flour, pepper and spices; sprinkle over the beef.
Add the rest of the vegetables.
Top with stewed tomatoes and their juice.
Pour tomato juice over the top.
Sprinkle with Worcestershire sauce.
Cover and cook on low 8 to 10 hours, or until meat is very tender and vegetables are done.
