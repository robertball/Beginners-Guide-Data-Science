Title:
Ruben's Diablo Ranch Pickled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4540663.jpg
Ingredients:
1 64 oz. Mason jar with lid
12 hard boiled eggs, peeled
1 cup water
1 1/2 cups white vinegar
1/2 small onion, sliced thin
5 oz. hot sauce; such as Widow No Survivors
1/2 teaspoon of oregano
1 habanero pepper, sliced thin (optional)

Directions:
Place the eggs in the jar with the remainder of the ingredients. Shake well and refrigerate for 5 days.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
The bold flavors that come from the spicy sauce make these eggs addictive. Pickled eggs like no others you've ever tasted that will have you coming back for more in no time.
