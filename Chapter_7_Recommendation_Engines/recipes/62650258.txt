Title:
Ginormous Fruit Salad Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup oj
1 tsp seasoned rice vinegar
1 tsp granulated sugar
2 cups peeled & diced cucumber
1 cup blueberries
1 cup diced strawberries
1 large Fuji apple, chopped

Directions:
In small bowl, combine oj, vinegar & sugar. Stir until blended & set aside
Place cucumber, blueberries, strawberries & apple in large bowl. Top with oj mixture & stir gently to coat
Cover & refrigerate at least 1 hr (overnight is best) Stir gently just before serving
