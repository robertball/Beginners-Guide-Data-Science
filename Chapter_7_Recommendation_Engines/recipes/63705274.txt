Title:
Garlic Parmesan Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 large shrimp
1/4 c olive oil
1/4 c chopped fresh parsley
3 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 c melted butter
1/2 c toasted italian breadcrumbs
1/2 c freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Arrange shrimp in a 11 x 7 inch baking dish.
Coat shrimp with olive oil and sprink with parsley, garlic, red pepper and black pepper.
Cover and bake at 300 degrees for 15 minutes.
Turn shrimp over, drizzle with butter, and sprinkle with breadcrumbs and parmesan cheese.
Bake uncovered for 5 -10 more minutes.
