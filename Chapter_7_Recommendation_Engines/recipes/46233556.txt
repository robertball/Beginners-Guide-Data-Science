Title:
Vivian's Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package cottage cheese
12 ounces whipped cream or 1 small container cool whip - thawed
1 (8 ounce) can pineapple tidbits, drained
1 (1 ounce) package strawberry flavored gelatin mix

Directions:
In a bowl, stir together cottage cheese, whipped cream, pineapple, and gelatin. Cover, and refrigerate at least an hour before serving.
