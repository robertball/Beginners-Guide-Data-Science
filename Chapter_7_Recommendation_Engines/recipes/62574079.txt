Title:
Tomato Basil Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 small tomatoes, diced
2 T chopped basil
1 T chopped onion
2 t minced garlic
2 t olive oil
1 t balsamic vinegar
salt and pepper
1/2 sourdough baguette

Directions:
Mix together tomatoes, basil, onion, garlic, olive oil, vinegar, salt and pepper.
Preheat to 425. Bake bread 5 minutes. Add topping.
