Title:
Boiled Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup cocoa
3 tbsp cornstarch
1 1/3 cup sugar
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix together in saucepan. Add 1 1/2 cup milk. Cook until thick stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Add 1 tbsp butter and 1 tsp vanilla. Cool.
