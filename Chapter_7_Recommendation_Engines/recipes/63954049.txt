Title:
Green Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups raw baby spinach leaves (or 2 cups frozen chopped spinach or collard greens)
1 cup water

Directions:
If using raw spinach, wash thoroughly.  Bring spinach and water to a boil in a medium pot.  Turn heat to low and allow to simmer for 10 minutes.  Pour into a fine mesh strainer over a container or bowl, pressing with the back of a spoon until all the liquid is released.
Store in refrigerator for up to 3 days or freeze in 1/4 cup portions.

Submitter: 
goodeats
Description: 
A nutritious additive to add a boost to foods such as pudding, milkshakes, etc.  1 tablespoon of Green Juice is comparable to eating 1/4 cup of spinach leaves.
