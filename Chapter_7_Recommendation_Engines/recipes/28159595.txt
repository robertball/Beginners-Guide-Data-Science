Title:
Oliebollen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups buttermilk
3 Tbsp of oil
1 1/4 cup of sugar
2-3 eggs (I usually use 3)
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon (or less)
1 cup raisins
4 cups of flour

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.(The batter should be very stiff.)
Take one mounded spoonful and deep fry until golden.
Poke with a fork to make sure they're cooked.
Coat with powdered sugar or sugar!

Submitter: 
Cindy Yonkman
Description: 
A New Year's tradition that I am passing on to my kids and grandkids. This recipe ALWAYS turns out perfect. I LOVE it because I don't have to worry about yeast~ me and yeast do not get along LOL!
