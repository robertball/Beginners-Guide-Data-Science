Title:
Wildtree Crockpot Chipotle Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup onion, sliced
1 cup celery, diced
1 cup carrot, diced
1 pound chicken thighs, boneless, skinless
1/4 cup Wildtree Chipotle Lime Rub
1/4 cup lime juice
1(15 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 avocado, diced

Directions:
Arrange onion, celery and carrot in bottom of slow cooker.
Liberally season chicken with Chipotle Lime Rub and arrange over vegetables.
In a small bowl, whisk together, lime juice, tomato sauce and garlic, and pour over chicken.
Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours or on high for 3 to 4 hours. Top chicken with avocado
