Title:
Cuervo Especial Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984380.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Cuervo Especial® Gold
8 leaves fresh mint
1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon lime juice
1/3 cup chilled club soda

Directions:
Fill a glass halfway with ice.
Crush mint leaves using back of a spoon. Add to the glass with sugar and lime juice.
Add Cuervo Especial Gold. Top with chilled club soda. Stir well. Garnish with mint leaves (optional).

Submitter: 
Cuervo®
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with the gold standard in tequila, Jose Cuervo Especial® tequila.
