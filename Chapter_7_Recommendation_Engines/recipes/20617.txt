Title:
The Best Steak Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1918457.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup raisins
2/3 cup ketchup
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon seasoning salt
1/2 cup applesauce
2 tablespoons Italian-style salad dressing

Directions:
Combine raisins, ketchup, chile powder, seasoning salt, applesauce and Italian-style dressing in a blender or food processor.  Blend until smooth.
