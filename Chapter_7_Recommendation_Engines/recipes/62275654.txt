Title:
Chocolate Chocolate Chip Pudding Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/347462.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package butter cake mix
1 package of vanilla pudding mix
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and sugar a Bundt pan - makes a nice crunchy crust
Prepare batter according to instructions on cake mix package. Stir in pudding and vanilla. Add 2 cups chocolate chips. Pour batter into Bundt pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour, or until toothpick inserted into center of cake comes out clean.
Cool for 10 minutes in pan, then turn out onto a wire rack.
