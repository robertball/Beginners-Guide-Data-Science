Title:
Tisa's Yogurt Tiramisu No Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Greek Yogurt
1/2 cup whipped cream
2 tbsp Cointreau
1 cup ready-made espresso
7 tbsp sugar
Chocolate sponge cake Â– 3 layers per ramekin
Cocoa powder for dusting

Directions:
Combine Greek Yogurt, whipped cream, Cointreau and sugar.
Put one layer of sponge cake in ramekin, and soak with espresso.
Put yogurt mixture in to fill by one-third, and layer up twice more. Chill for 1 hour to set.
Dust with cocoa powder.
Serve with chocolate chip cookies.
