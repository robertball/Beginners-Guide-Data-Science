Title:
Easy Cheesy Scallop Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package cream cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup broth
9 cups sliced potatoes
3/4 cup sliced ham
1 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese plus 1/2 cup for topping
1 cup peas

Directions:
Warm up broth and melt in cream cheese and whisk together . Remove from heat and add sour cream. set aside
Mix together potatoes, ham, cheddar and peas. Slowly add cream cheese sauce and combine until blended.
Pour into 9x13 glass pan and sprinkle top with shredded cheddar. Bake 1 hour at 350
