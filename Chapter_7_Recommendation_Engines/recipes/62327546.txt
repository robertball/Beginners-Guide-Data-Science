Title:
Ham and Potato Drop Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large potato peeled and diced.
1 bag of shredded carrots. (or 2 cups of cut carrots.)
1 package of cream cheese
2 cups of sour cream
2 packages of Ham flavor concentrate
2 cup of cut celery
1 medium onion diced.
9 cups of water
2 lbs of lean cut up ham (diced or sliced. )
2 cups of instant potato flakes

Directions:
Get a large pot ready by placing the 6 cups of water in to it.
Add potatoes celery onion and carrots in to the pot and allow the water to boil.
Chop the the ham and the cream cheese. Add those to the boiling mixture and bring down to a low boil. Combine the rest of the ingredients in to the pot and stir every 10 minutes.
You can add a tsp of cornstarch or more potato flakes to thicken the broth.

Submitter: 
Cyn Determination
Description: 
This is best served up with home made bread. There is a Irish Soda Bread and the two of them together was fantastic. I have to feed a large family of 6.
