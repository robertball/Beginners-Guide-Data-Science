Title:
Grandma Dixon's Fudge Cake Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Sugar
2 Squares Unsweetened Chocolate
1 C Milk
1 Tbls Butter
1/2 tsp Vanilla
Pinch - Cream of Tarter

Directions:
Mix Sugar, Chocolate, Milk in a double boiler.
Boil to Soft Ball stage - 234 - 242 F
Add Butter, Vanilla and Cream of Tarter.
Ice Fudge Cake while Icing is warm.

Submitter: 
Jack
Description: 
Don't over cook or you'll end-up with Chocolate concrete!
