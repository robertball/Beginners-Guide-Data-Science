Title:
BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
4 14oz bottles ketchup
7 cups cider vinegar
1 oz black pepper(2 TBS)
1/4 cup Tabasco(4 TBS)
Juice of 2 lemons(4 TBS)
1/2 cup margarine
1 5oz bottle worcestershire sauce
1 6oz jar prepared mustard
6 TBS salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in heavy saucepan
bring to boil and simmer 30 min.
pour in bottles and store on shelf.
The conversions are for whole recipe
I always 1/2 this recipe and it makes 2 bottles
