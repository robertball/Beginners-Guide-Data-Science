Title:
Peanut Blossoms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag Hershey's Kisses milk chocolate
1/2 cup shortening
3/4 cup creamy or crunchy Peanut Butter
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1/3 cup packed light brown sugar
1 egg
2 tbsp milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
Granulated sugar

Directions:
heat oven to 375 Remove wrappers from chocolate pieces.
Inlarge mixing bowl beat shortening and peanut butter until well blended
Add 1/3 cup granulated sugar and brown sugar; beat until light and fluffy.  Add egg, milk and vanilla; beat well
Stir together flour, baking soda and salt; gradually add to peanut butter mixture.  shape dough into 1 inch balls.  Roll in granulated sugar; place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 10 to 12 min or until lightly browned.  Immediately place chocolate piece on top of each cookie, pressing down so cookie cracks around edges.  Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack.
Cool completely
