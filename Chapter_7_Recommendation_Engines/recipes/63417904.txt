Title:
Hamburger Corn Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/923051.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans of canned sweet creamed corn
2 cans of whole sweet corn
1 lbs. of ground beef
1 large onion
3-4 medium potatoes - peeled, cut into small peices
2 tbs. dried parsley
2 tbs. salt - to taste
1 tbs. ground pepper - to taste
Water enough to completely fill crock pot.

Directions:
Peel and chop onion into small pieces and pan fry along with ground beef to brown. Place contents into crock pot. Do not drain grease. Add 2 cans of cream corn and 2 cans of whole corn to crock pot. Do not drain the whole corn. Add peeled and cut potatoes and all other ingredients. Add enough water to fill crock pot to full. Cover crock pot and cook on low heat for 6 hours. Before serving, adjust salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy! Easy as that!
