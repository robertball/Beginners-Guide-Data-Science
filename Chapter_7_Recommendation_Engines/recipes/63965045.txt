Title:
Hot Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups apple cider
1/4 cup real maple syrup
2 cinnamon sticks
6 whole cloves
6 whole allspice berries
1 orange peel, cut into strips
1 lemon peel, cut into strips

Directions:
1.	Pour apple cider and maple syrup into large stainless steel saucepan.
2.	Place the cinnamon sticks, cloves, allspice berries, orange peel and lemon peel in the center of cheesecloth; fold up the sides of the cheesecloth to enclose the bundle, then tie ends.  Drop the bundle into the cider mixture.
3.	Place the saucepan over moderate heat for 5 to 10 minutes, or until the cider is very hot but not boiling.
4.	Remove the cider from the heat.  Discard the spice bundle.  Serve.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
So warm and yummy without being overpowering like some mulled ciders.
