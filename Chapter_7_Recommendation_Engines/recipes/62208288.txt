Title:
Grandmas Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 medium potatoes
9 large boiled eggs
1 medium onion chopped fine
1 1/2 C. miracle whip
1/4 C. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/4 C. mustard
2 Tbls. of vinegar
4 stalks of celery finely chopped

Directions:
boil potatoes little less boiled then what you would do for mashed potatoes so they don't fall a part. Dice the potatos into cubes. peel and chop your boiled eggs. combine your potatos, onions, and celery in one bowl.
In another bowl combine the rest of your ingrediants to make your dressing.
Then mix together and put in fridge. Serve cold
