Title:
Spaghetti and meatballs All Amarician Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz smokey bacon-processed into a paste
2 garlic
2 lb ground beef
2/3 c chopped red pepper from a jar
2/3 c panko
2 eggs
1/2 c grated onion
1/2 c parmesan
1 T marjoram
2 t red pepper
1/2 t kosher salt
Sauce
2 28oz diced tomatoes-puree with garlic till smooth
2 garlic
6 oz bacon, cut and crisped
1 T oil
3 c chopped onion
1 1/2 t red pepper
2 c dry white wine
1 T marjoram
1 1/2 lb spaghetti
2-3 T oil
1 1/2 T marjoram
Parmesan

Directions:
Mix first and make meatballs.
Cook bacon and remove, add oil and brown meatballs till golden.
Add sauce ingredients up to wine, reduce 1/2. add rest and meatballs cook covered 15 min.
Cook noodles and serve.
