Title:
Scarlet Kiss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/856050.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger raspberry vodka
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger strawberry vodka
1 fluid ounce lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Chambord (raspberry liqueur)
1 twist orange zest

Directions:
In an old-fashioned glass over ice, combine raspberry vodka, strawberry vodka, and lemon-lime soda. Float raspberry liqueur on top. garnish with a twist of orange zest.

Submitter: 
KENINNY
Description: 
This is the all-time best vodka drink. It was invented by myself and my girlfriend at the time. It was such a hit, the bartender made us name it and immediately added it to the special board that evening. But beware! This tasty libation is all alcohol and not for the faint of heart.
