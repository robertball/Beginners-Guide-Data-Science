Title:
Piadine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 piadine
1 250g Gouda cheese
3 blocks frozen spinach
Other
Roasted peppers
sundried tomatoes

Directions:
Heat piadina, add cheese to one side and other ingredients.  Fold over and brown on both sides until cheese is melted.  Serve warm.
May use olive oil to add a yummier taste to the piadina.
