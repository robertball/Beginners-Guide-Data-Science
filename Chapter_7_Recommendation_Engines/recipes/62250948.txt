Title:
Creamy Leek,Chicken and Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/495840.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken stock
1 can chicken broth
2 cup water
2 packets leek soup mix
2 cups cooked shredded chicken
1 8 oz carton half and half
1 package microwave ready  seasoned long grain and wild rice
1 cup diced carrots
2 stalks diced celery
1 teaspoon black pepper
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons flour

Directions:
Cook packaged rice as directed and set aside.
To a large pot add chicken stock,water,broth and soup mix. Stir until all of the soup mix is disolved add carrots,and celery simmer 10 minutes Add chicken and rice cover and simmer 20 mins or until carrots are tender.  Add half and half and pepper  simmer 10 mins very slowly careful not to scorch.
In a small pot melt butter and add flour stirring to make a paste cook 1 min. Slowly add 2 ladles of liquid from soup and wisk until no lumps.Add this mixture to soup and stir well. continue to simmer until soup thickens slightly  Turn soup off and serve

Submitter:

Description: 
This soup won me 1st prize at our annual soup off. It is a creamy blend of a leek broth with seasoned chicken and wild rice blend.Seved with bread it is a meal.
