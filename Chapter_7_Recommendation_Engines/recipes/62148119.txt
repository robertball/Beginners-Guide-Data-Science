Title:
Mom's Au Gratin Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 med potatoes
3-4 green onions chopped
1/2 butter or marjarine
1 can cream of chicken condensed soup
1 pt sour cream
1 cup sharp cheddar cheese
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Boil 6 med potatoes until tender, drain and let cool.
Melt marjarine in large sauce pan over medium heat
add chopped green onions, cook 3 or 4 minutes until tender
add remaining ingredients and cook until cheese is melted and all ingredients are blended.
Peel potatoes and slice into 8x8 pan.
Pour sauce over potatoes and blend.
Bake at 375 for 45 minutes or until brown around the edges.
