Title:
Pebre Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6703173.jpg
Ingredients:
1 dried aji pepper
1 roma tomato
1 small onion
1 clove of garlic, peeled
1/2 cup cilantro, stems removed
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1/2 lime, juiced
Salt and pepper to taste
1 jalapeno (optional)

Directions:
Remove the stem and seeds from the dried aji pepper. Pour hot water over the dried pepper, just enough to cover. Allow to soak for 15 minutes.
Cut the tomato and onion into fourths. Put the tomato, onion, cilantro into a food processor fitted with a blade. Add the garlic. Pulse a few times.
Drain the aji pepper.
Add the drained pepper, red wine vinegar, olive oil, lime juice. Blend until the consistency desired has been achieved. Salt and pepper to taste. Stir to combine. Top with sliced jalapeno before serving.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
My take on pebre, the Chilean salsa. Mine uses a rehydrated aji pepper This is delicious served with bread or alongside eggs, meats, fish, or cheese.
