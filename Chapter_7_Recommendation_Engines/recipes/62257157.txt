Title:
Easy-to-Make ABC Mac & Cheese Menu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/336071.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces dried cavatappi, ziti tagliati, or elbow macaroni (2 cups)
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1-2/3 cups whole milk, heated
1 cup shredded American cheese (4 ounces)
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese (3 ounces)
2 tablespoons to 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese (1/2 to 1 ounce)

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain; set aside. Meanwhile, prepare cheese sauce.
For cheese sauce: In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour and pepper. Cook and stir for 3 minutes. Add milk in a steady stream. Cook and stir over medium heat for 3 minutes more or till slightly thickened and bubbly. Add cheese, stirring till melted. Stir in cooked pasta. Transfer pasta mixture to a 2-quart casserole.
Bake, uncovered, in a 350 degree F. oven for 20 to 25 minutes or till bubbly and heated through. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
