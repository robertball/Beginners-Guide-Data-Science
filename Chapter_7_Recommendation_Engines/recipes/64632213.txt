Title:
Creamy, White Enchilada Sauce by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4481198.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbls butter
3 tbsp flour
1 t cumin
1 t garlic powder
2 c water, broth or green enchilada sauce
1 c sour cream
1 small can green chili (can omit if using enchilada sauce instead of broth)
2 c cheese
2 c cooked chicken
Onions and green peppers optional
6-8 tortillas
Sour cream, salsa, cilantro, lettuce, tomato for topping

Directions:
Preheat over to 400
If using onions and green peppers saute until nearly cooked, add chicken. If desired, add 1/2 c cheese and a little broth, green sauce or salsa to chicken mixture.
Tightly roll mixture in tortilla shells and top with sauce and cheese.
Sauce:
Melt butter, add flour and cook 1-2 minutes.
Gradually add 2 c broth or sauce, mix well with whisk.
Cook until thick, whisking often (5-6 minutes).
Add cumin, garlic and sour cream and chilis if using.
Pour over rolled enchiladas, top with cheese and bake 20-25 minutes until heated through and top is bubbly and brown.
Let rest 5 minutes, add topping

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
Adaptation from a Facebook recipe, added green enchilada sauce instead of the broth and green chilis and it was delicious!
