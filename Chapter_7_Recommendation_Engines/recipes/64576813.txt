Title:
Peanut Butter Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/4 cup peanut butter
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/8 tsp. salt
2 cups icing sugar
2-3 tbsp. milk

Directions:
Mix all ingredients until smooth. Adjust milk and sugar to get desired consistency.
Makes about 1 1/3 cups (enough for 12 cupcakes).

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Add some peanut butter (& protein..!) to your sweet treats. Tastes great on chocolate cake & cupcakes.
