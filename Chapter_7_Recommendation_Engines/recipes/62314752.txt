Title:
Pizza Dough I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups whole wheat flour
1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon honey
1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)

Directions:
Combine flour, salt, honey, and yeast in a large bowl.  Mix in oil and warm water.  Spread out on a large pizza pan.  Top as desired.
Bake at 375 degrees C (190 degrees C) for 20 to 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
This one is a quick recipe that merely involves mixing a few basic ingredients and patting the dough into the pan. No need to wait for the dough to rise with this approach.Modified from: Pizza Dough I
