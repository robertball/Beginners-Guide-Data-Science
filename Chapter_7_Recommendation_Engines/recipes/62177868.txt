Title:
spiced maple chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4C maple syrup (sugar free)
1Tbsp soy sauce
1/4tsp ground ginger
2Tbsp white wine
1Tbsp canola oil
1 large clove garlic(miced)
1/4tsp red pepper flakes
1/8tsp salt
2 whole bonless, skinless chicken breasts, halved
Chopped parsley for garnish

Directions:
Mix first 9 ingredients in bowl, add the chicken. Let sit refrigerated 4 hours or overnight. Discard marinade after removing the chicken. Broil 5-6in away from heat about 7minutes per side. Sprinkle with parsley to serve.
