Title:
Shrimp and Cream Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices of bacon
1 small onion, diced
1 tablespoon garlic, minced
4 Roma tomatoes, diced
Salt & Pepper to taste
1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
2 cups 1/2 & 1/2
16 ounces peeled & deviened shrimp
3 tablespoons butter
If needed:
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon flour

Directions:
In a large skillet fry bacon until crisp, remove & crumble.  Reserve 4 tablespoons of bacon drippings in the large skillet.
Fry the onion and garlic in the drippings until soft, 4-5 minutes.
Add tomatoes, basil, salt & pepper.  Cook until tomatoes are tender, 3-4 minutes.
Add the 1/2 & 1/2 and stir to combine. Once combined and comes to simmer bubbles, add the butter and shrimp.  Cook until shrimp has turned pink, do not overcook.
If the sauce is too thin, melt the 1 tablespoon of butter and mix in the 1 tablespoon of flour until smooth and then mix into the cream sauce.
Serve over your favorite pasta.
