Title:
Vegan Multigrain Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/932950.jpg
Ingredients:
½ cup Spelt Flour,
½ cup Amaranth Flour
½ cup Brown Rice Flour
½ cup Almond Meal
¼ cup Oats (Quick )
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
8  Dried figs
1/8 teaspoon Stevia (liquid)
2 Tablespoons Ghee (or oil)
1 cup Vanilla Almond Milk
1 Flax egg  (can substitute regular egg)
2 Tablespoons Honey

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350 F.
Blend in blender:  Flax egg, Figs, Stevia, Ghee, Almond Milk & Honey.
In mixing bowl, mix together dry ingredients.
Add liquid to dry ingredients, stir together until nicely blended.
Drop into oiled muffin tins, smooth the tops over to make a nice finish. Bake for 15-20 minutes. Remove muffins immediately and let cool on a rack.
