Title:
Paleo Chipotle Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/966004.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup paleo mayonnaise
2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 lime, juiced
1 clove garlic

Directions:
Combine paleo mayonnaise, chipotle peppers, fresh cilantro, olive oil, lime juice, and garlic in a food processor or blender. Pulse or blend mixture until smooth and well combined, about 1 minute.

Submitter: 
sammyc14
Description: 
This can be kept in the refrigerator for at least a week. You can also omit the mayonnaise from the recipe if you need to. Tastes great on fish tacos or burgers!
