Title:
Super easy, quick and delicious Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breasts
1/3 cup butter
2 tbls flour
1 3/4 cups warm water
2 packages chicken oxo
2 tbls lemon pepper
2 tbls lemon juice
salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
Grill or bake chicken breasts. While chicken is cooking melt butter in saucepan. Turn burner down to medium heat and whisk in flour. Dissolve chicken oxo in warm water and add to butter and flour mixture. Add the lemon pepper and lemon juice, salt and pepper. Simmer until chicken is done. Add more flour to thicken. Pour sauce over chicken and rice.

Submitter: 
Tegan LeComte
Description: 
Quick and easy and always a favourite. Goes very well with rice and a spinich salad. Also goes well with salmon.
