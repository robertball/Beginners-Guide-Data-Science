Title:
Spicey n Sweet Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/778792.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
1/4 Cup Paprika
1 Tablespoon Black Pepper
1 Tablespoon Chili Powder
1 Tablespoon Onion Powder
1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
1 Tablespoon Cayenne
1/2 Tablespoon Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Cinnamon
1/4 Teaspoon Dry Mustard
1/8 Teaspoon Ground Thyme

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl
Taste and adjust to YOUR liking
Dry off the meat you are using and place on a piece of plastic wrap larger the your cut of meat
Rub season mixture into meat and sprinkle a bit extra over it
Wrap tightly and place in refrigerator till ready to cook
Unwrap plastic and place meat on grill or in oven.  Cook to proper safe internal temperature

Submitter: 
Lori Krol Rothfuss
Description: 
AN easily adaptable rub I have used on ribs and other pork cuts.  Makes a great caramelized coating.  It can easily be adjusted to add more heat and less sweet...or less rub if doing a smaller piece of meat
