Title:
Yummy Cherry Pineapple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (21 oz.) cherry pie filling
1 can (8 oz) crushed pineapple (undrained)
1 yellow cake mix
1/2 C. butter, melted

Directions:
Heat the oven to 350 degrees.  Spread pie filling in an ungreased 9x13 pan.  Layer the pineapple over the cherry filling.  Stir together dry cake mix and butter in a bowl until crumbly.  Sprinkle evenly over fruit.  Bake 35 min. or until golden brown.  Serve warm with vanilla ice cream.  Can also add chopped nuts to the cake mix if desired.

Submitter: 
Cheryl S.
Description: 
Super easy and quick to make and very delicious served warm with vanilla ice cream.
