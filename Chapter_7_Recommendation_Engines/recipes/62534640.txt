Title:
Ancient Honey Cakes (Rice Flour Cookies with Nuts or Poppy Seeds) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/519750.jpg
Ingredients:
•	1 Stick (1/4 lb) butter or ¼ cup coconut oil (vegan version)
•	1/2 c Honey
•	2 lg Eggs, slightly beaten
•	2 c Rice flour or barley flour (Egyptian version)
•	1/4 ts Baking powder
•	1/4 ts Cardamom
•	2 ts Hemp Nuts or Crushed Almonds or Poppy seeds

Directions:
1.	Cream the butter and honey together until smooth. Add the eggs and mix well. Add the rice flour, baking powder and cardamom and blend together. 2. Oil a baking sheet lightly. Take 1 heaping teaspoon of dough for each cookie, roll it into a ball. Press into the nuts or poppy seeds ½ way, then press it down to 1/4 inch thickness on the baking sheet. Bake in a 350*F oven for 20 minutes.

Description: 
In Ancient Temples, they offered Honey Cakesto the Goddess.  I Imagine that they were something like this!  I adapted the rice flour for modern tastes, although you can try barley flour if you wish.
