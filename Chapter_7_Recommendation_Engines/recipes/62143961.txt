Title:
Sausage Linguine with Artichoke Hearts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound dried linguine
1 pound bulk sage sausage
2 teaspoons minced fresh garlic
1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 (15.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1/2 cup grapefruit juice
1/2 cup half-and-half
1 (7 ounce) jar marinated artichoke hearts

Directions:
Cook linguine according to package directions.
Meanwhile, brown sausage, garlic and ginger in large skillet over medium-high heat. Drain off fat. Add dried basil, diced tomatoes, and grapefruit juice. Let simmer until pasta is finished, and then stir in half-and-half and artichoke hearts. Heat through, and serve sauce over linguine.
