Title:
Dutch Oven Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/747849.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
3 pounds ground beef or turkey
2 medium onions, chopped
2 14.5 ounce cans diced tomatoes
1 29-oz. can tomato sauce
3 tablespoons soy sauce
2 teaspoons dried basil
2 teaspoons dried oregano
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon garlic powder
3/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cups uncooked elbow macoroni

Directions:
In a large Dutch oven, heat the oil over medium-high heat.
Brown the meat in the oil about 10 minutes, breaking up the meat as it cooks.
Add the onion; cook and stir for 5 minutes.
Add the remaining ingredients except the macaroni.
Reduce heat; simmer, covered for 20 minutes.
Stir in the macaroni; simmer, covered for 20 minutes.
Let the goulash stand for 20 minutes before serving
