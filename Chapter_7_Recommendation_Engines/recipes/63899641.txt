Title:
Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 good sized mushrooms for stuffing
1/4 lb. small mushrooms for filling
1 or 2 green onions
1/2 c. sharp cheddar, grated
1 1/2 tsp. Worcestershire
Tabasco
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp. garlic salt
3 T. bread crumbs
1/4 c. butter
small pinch Italian herbs

Directions:
Clean and de-stem large mushrooms.  Finely chop stems and small mushrooms, onions, and cheese in food processor.  Add Worcestershire, Tabasco (to taste), salt. pepper, garlic salt, herbs, and bread crumbs.  Melt butter and place a small amount in each mushroom cap.  Pour the rest into the mushroom filling.  Fill caps with filling and put any leftover filling into a shallow oiled dish.  Broil at 350 until caps are hot but not mushy.
