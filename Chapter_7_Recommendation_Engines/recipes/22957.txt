Title:
Toy Soldiers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/969308.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (46 fluid ounce) cans fruit punch
1 pint sloe gin
1 pint vodka
8 cups ice

Directions:
In a large punch bowl or a cooler, combine fruit punch, sloe gin and vodka. Stir in the ice.
