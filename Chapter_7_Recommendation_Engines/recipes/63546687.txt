Title:
Canned Black Beans Made Awesome in 30 Minutes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/968706.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15oz can black beans, drained
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tablespoon Worchestershire sauce
salt to taste

Directions:
Scoop drained beans into a small saucepan.
Pour chicken broth over beans.
Add cumin, chili powder, oregano, minced garlic and Worchestershire sauce and stir to mix.
Bring beans to a simmer over medium-high heat. Turn heat to medium low and simmer gently for 25 minutes. If liquid gets low, add more broth.
Add salt to taste and serve on tortillas, chips, in burritos, as a side dish, or any other way you love to eat beans!

Submitter: 
Seth Kolloen
Description: 
I have all of the ingredients in my pantry, so I'll make these beans for a weekend lunch, quick dinner, or late-night snack when I'm out of fresh stuff but too lazy to go to the store. Saves me from getting take-out or fast food.
