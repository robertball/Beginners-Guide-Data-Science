Title:
Gashtel  (Ribble) Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 cup beaten eggs...(not sure how many eggs you need to make a cup)
2. 3 cups flour
3. Chicken stock for the soup or 3-4  large cans of chicken broth

Directions:
Bring 3. to a boil
In food processor that has a blade at the bottom mix 1. and 2. Put the flour in first and slowly add the eggs with the processor on ...when the mixture starts to form a ball add a little flour and pulsate until the ribbles are the size you desire...grate on the grater so that there are smaller and larger ribbles
add to boiling chicken broth ...cook till ribbles done
