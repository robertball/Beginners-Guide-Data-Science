Title:
Udon Noodles in teriyaki glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp sesame oil
2 Tbsp teriyaki glaze
1 Tbsp tamari sauce
4 Tbsp water
1 Tbsp sugar
8 oz. beef or chicken , thinly sliced strips
2 garlic gloves, chopped
4 oz. broccoli florets
2 pouches udon noodles

Directions:
Add 1 Tbsp, sesame oil to wok or skillet on high, mix in teriyaki glaze, tamari sauce, water, sugar and heat for one minute. See aside.
Heat 1 Tbsp. sesame oil on high, stir in meat and garlic and cook for 5 minutes.
Add udon noodles and broccoli; stir and cook for 2 minutes.
Combine with prepared sauce from step one and stir for one minute.
