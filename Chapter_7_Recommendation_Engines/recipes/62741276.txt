Title:
Turnip Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium turnips or 1 large
1 cup applesauce
2eggs
4tsp brown sugar
6 TBSP melted butter
1 ¾ cups soft bread crumbs
3 tsp salt
¼ tsp pepper
2TBSP melted butter

Directions:
Cook thoroughly and mash turnip
Combine all ingredients except ½ of bread crumbs and 2TBSP butter. Mix together well. Place in greased 2 qt casserole & top with remaining bread crumbs and melted butter.
Cover and refrigerate. Take out 1 hr before serving & bake uncovered at 350F for 45 min. until heated through.

Submitter: 
Carol
Description: 
Nice side dish for Turkey or Chicken that can be made the day before serving. The applesause takes a little of the bite out of the turnip.
