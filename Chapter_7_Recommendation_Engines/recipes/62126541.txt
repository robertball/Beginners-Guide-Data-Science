Title:
Easy Tomato & Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9oz) package cheese tortellini
1 (32oz) container Organic creamy tomato soup
1 cup frozen chopped spinach, unthawed
1 tsp minced garlic
1 tsp olive oil
1 tsp dried basil
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook tortellini according to package instructions (boil for 7-9 minutes.
Meanwhile saute garlic in olive oil.  Be careful not to burn garlic.
Add frozen spinach to garlic and saute until spinach is warmed through.
Add tomato soup and cook until heated through.
Add basil and salt and pepper.
Drain tortellini and add to tomato soup.
Simmer for 5-8 minutes to allow flavors to combine.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lygia Waters
Description: 
A quick and filling tomato soup that can be served as a main dinner course or accompanied by a crisp green salad for lunch.
