Title:
Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 English cucumbers
1 medium red onion
1/3 cup reduced-fat mayo
2 TBS. reduced-fat sour cream
2 TBS. cider vinegar
1 TBS. honey
1 tsp. poppy seeds
1/2 tsp. coarse salt
1/4 tsp. ground pepper

Directions:
Halve cucumbers lengthwise.  Using a teaspoon scrape out seds; slice cucumbers very thinly on the diagonal.  In a medium bowl, combine cucumbers with onion halved and thinly sliced.
In a small bowl stir together mayo, sour cream, cider vinegar, honey, poppy seeds, salt and pepper.  Add to cucumber mixture; toss, and serve immediately.
