Title:
Lemony Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2# fresh green beans, trimmed
3T EVOO
3 med. shallots, thinly sliced
4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
1/2t salt
1/4t black pepper
2t lemon zest (set aside)
3T fresh lemon juice

Directions:
In lg, heavy pot, cook beans in boiling lightly salted water for 4 min., until just crisp-tender. Drain; submerge in bowl of ice water. Drain well
In same pot, heat oil for 1 min. over med-high heat. Add shallots, garlic, salt and pepper. Cook and stir for 2 min. or until just softened. Add green beans and cook and stir for 4 min. or until heated through. Add lemon juice; cook and stir until well coated. Transfer to a serving dish. Sprinkle w/lemon zest.
