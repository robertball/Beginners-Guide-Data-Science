Title:
Mediterranean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz. sun dried tomatoes with garlic
4 oz. pitted and cut up kalamata olives
Garlic to taste
2 tbsp. fresh parsley
1 bunch green onion
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. lime juice
4 oz. crumbled feta cheese
olive oil
pine nuts

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and add olive oil and pine nuts to your desired level.  Cut up soft pitas and use to dip.  This dip is scrumptious!!

Traci Caha Myers
Description: 
Great with cut up soft pitas!
