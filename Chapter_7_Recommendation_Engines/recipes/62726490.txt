Title:
24 Hour Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head iceberg lettuce
1 bunch spinach
1 pkg. frozen peas
4 hard boiled eggs
1 bunch green onions
1/2 C mayonnaise
1/2 C salad dressing (Miracle Whip)
1/3 C milk
1 pkg. dried sour cream mix (Hidden Valley Ranch)
1 C Monerey Jack cheese, grated
8 slices bacon, fried

Directions:
Layer lettuce and spinach (torn in pieces) into bowl.
2nd layer: Peas (separated), eggs (sliced), bacon (crumbled), chopped green onions with tops, then frost with dressing. Top with grated cheese. Frost the salad dressing mixture which is mayo, salad dressing, milk and sour cream mix. Looks very pretty in a large glass bowl.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
A little different version of the 24 hr. salad
