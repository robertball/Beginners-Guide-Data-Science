Title:
Rice & Green Chili Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c uncooked rice
1/4 c butter
2 c sour cream
6oz can chopped green chilis
salt to taste
1 lb Jack cheese

Directions:
Cook rice according to directions. Drain & mix w/ 1/4 c butter, sour cream, chilis & salt. Put half the rice in a baking dish & top w/ 1/2 lb cheese. Add remaining rice & top w/ 1/2 c grated cheese. Bake 30 min @ 350.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
Tastes like sour cream chicken enchiladas, you can add cooked chicken to make it a casserole.
