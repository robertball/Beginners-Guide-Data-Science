Title:
Hunt's Easy Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983237.jpg
Ingredients:
1 18-inch French baguette, cut into 3/4-inch slices
1/4 cup virgin olive oil, divided
1 (14.5 ounce) can Hunt's Petite Diced Tomatoes, drained
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1/3 cup fresh basil, chopped

Directions:
Place slices of bread on cookie sheet; brush tops with olive oil.  Bake in a preheated 350 degree F oven for about 10 minutes or until lightly toasted.
Combine tomatoes, garlic, 2 tablespoons olive oil, and basil in a small bowl.  Season to taste.
Spread tomato mixture evenly over toasted bread.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Hunt's
Description: 
Hunt's Petite Diced Tomatoes are the perfect size for bruschetta, and make assembling this delicious appetizer a breeze!
