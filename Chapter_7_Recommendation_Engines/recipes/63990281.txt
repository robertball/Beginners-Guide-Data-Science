Title:
Mom's Fluffy Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb. butter
8 T. Crisco
1 c. granulated sugar - superfine
3 T. flour
1/2 c. milk (regular or evaporated)
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla or other flavoring

Directions:
Cream butter, Crisco, & sugar.
Add flour 1 T. at a time.
Combine milk and flavoring and add to butter mix.
Beat at high speed until sugar granules disappear.
