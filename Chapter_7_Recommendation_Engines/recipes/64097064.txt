Title:
Big Ray's Breakfast to Go Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1215100.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (15 ounces) corned beef hash
6 eggs
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Grease a 6-cup  jumbo muffin pan. Divide hash into the cups; press onto the bottom and up the sides to form a shell. Bake for 5 minutes then remove.
Break an egg into each shell; season with salt and pepper.
Bake at 400Â° for 10-15 minutes or until eggs are set the way you like them.
