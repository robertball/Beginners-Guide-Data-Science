Title:
Beefy Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. chopped carrots
2 1/2 c. chopped potatoes
1 c. tomato sauce
1 lb. ground beef
1 tbsp., or to taste, salt
4 Tbsp. corn starch
1 Tbsp. No salt seasoning
Water

Directions:
Cover carrots and potatoes with enough water to cook. Cover and cook for 30 minutes or until tender on medium heat. Meanwhile, brown hamburger. Add salt and no salt seasoning. Add more water if it all boiled out. Mix corn starch and 1/4 c. water. Add to stew, stir, and cook 2 more minutes until thickened.
