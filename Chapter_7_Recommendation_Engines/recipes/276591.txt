Title:
Turkey Gravy from Scratch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup turkey drippings
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 cups chicken broth
turkey giblets and neck

Directions:
Mix drippings and flour together in a small bowl to make a paste.
Combine broth with turkey giblets and neck in a pot. Bring to a boil. Whisk in the paste. Reduce heat to low and let simmer, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 10 minutes. Remove turkey giblets and neck before serving.

Submitter: 
Randy Finger
Description: 
Simple, quick, easy gravy flavored with the giblets and neck of the turkey. Delicious! Doesn't use very many drippings.
