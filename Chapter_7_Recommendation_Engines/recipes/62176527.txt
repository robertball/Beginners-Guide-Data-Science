Title:
Peanut butter fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2C sugar
1/2C milk
2Tbsp corn syrup
1tsp vinegar
1/2C peanut butter

Directions:
Stir sugar, milk, vinegar, corn syrup. Cook to soft boil stage; then cook until luke warm. Add the peanut butter and heat until creamy. Pour into 9in pan.
