Title:
Black Bean and Beef Quesadilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lbs 95% lean ground beef
2 Tbsp yellow onion, minced
15 ounce can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 tsp cornstarch
1/4 cup water
8, 7-in flour tortillas
1 cup shredded Mexican four cheese
1 tomato, chopped
1 cup shredded lettuce
light sour cream

Directions:
Cook ground beef and onion over medium low heat. Drain.
Mix chili powder through cornstarch in a small bowl.
Add black beans to ground beef. Sprinkle with chili powder seasoning.
Add water and cook over medium low heat for 10 min.
Mash ground beef mixture with a potato masher.
Heat 1 tsp oil over medium heat. Heat one tortilla for approx 20 seconds. Flip and heat for another 20 seconds. Repeat with second tortilla. After flipping the second tortilla, spread 1/3 cup ground beef mixture on tortilla, sprinkle with cheese, place the first tortilla on top and heat through. Remove from heat and cut into quarters.
Repeat with remaining tortillas.
Serve with lettuce, tomato, and sour cream.
