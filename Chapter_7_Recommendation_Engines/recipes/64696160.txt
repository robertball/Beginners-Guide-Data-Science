Ingredients:
Ham - cut into large chunks. Leftover or precooked dinner ham ~2 pounds.
Head of green cabbage cut into large chunks.
2 large cans of 23.2 oz tomato soup.
Optional: 1 kielbasa sliced.

Directions:
Put large cabbage chunks into large sauce pot.
Add both cans of soup.
Fill up both empty cans with water and add to pot.
Add ham chunks.
Add kielbasa - optional.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Bring to a boil.
Let boil for about 20 minutes.
Reduce heat and let let cook until cabbage is tender.
Serve with pirogies.

Submitter: 
Amy Standaert
Description: 
Warm and comforting. A great way to use leftover ham.
