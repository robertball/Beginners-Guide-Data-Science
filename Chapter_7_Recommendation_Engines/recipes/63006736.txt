Title:
Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz spaghetti noodles
1 can (10 3/4 z.) Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
2/3 cup milk
2 tbsp. onion, minced
2 cans (5 oz.) chunk tuna in water, drained
1 can (14.5 oz.) cut green beans, drained
Buttered Bread Crumbs -
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup dried bread crumbs

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 deg. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot according to the directions for the shortest time listed. Drain.
Return spaghetti to pot and add cream of mushroom soup, milk, onion, tuna and green beans. Mix well and scoop into 1 1/2 qt. casserole dish. Top with buttered bread crumbs (recipe below) and bake 30-40 min. at 350 deg. F.
Buttered bread crumbs - Melt butter in pan. Add bread crumbs and stir to coat.
