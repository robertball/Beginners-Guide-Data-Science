Title:
Pork Chops and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops, bone in
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, diced
8 ounces mushrooms, chopped
1/2 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Salt and pepper pork chops, lightly dredge in flour. Saute in oil and butter on medium high heat until nicely browned. Remove to plate.
Add some more butter and oil saute onion, garlic and mushrooms in the same pan until nicely browned. Remove from pan.
Put Chicken broth and 1 Tbsp of butter to same pan and heat until boiling.
Place half of the mushrooms in a 9 x 13 inch oven proof pan. Place pork chops on top of mushrooms and place the remainder of the mushrooms over the pork chops. Pour the chicken broth over all and bake.
