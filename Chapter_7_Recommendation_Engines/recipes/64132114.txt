Title:
Katie's Cool Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
1 tbsp white sugar
1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 tbsp kosher salt
Fresh ground black pepper to taste
3 medium cucumbers

Directions:
Cut up the cucumbers into long slices.
Cut seeds out of the center of each slice, and then cut the cucumbers into about 1/2" chunks.
Sprinkle kosher salt the cucumbers and let them sit in a colander for 15-20 minutes. Rinse off salt, pat dry.
For dressing, Stir together the Greek yogurt, sugar, vinegar, lemon juice, dill, and salt in a small bowl.
Season with black pepper to taste.
In serving bowl, add the cucumber cubes and the cucumber cubes, toss to coat.

Submitter: 
katie
Description: 
Cucumber cubes are tossed with a creamy cider vinegar, Greek yogurt, and dill dressing in this lowfat, tasty summer side-dish.
