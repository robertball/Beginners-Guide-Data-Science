Title:
Homemade Holiday Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large Jonagold or Gala apples (cored, peeled, and cut into small chunks)
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1 tsp. lemon zest
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup light brown sugar
2/3 cup water
pinch salt
1/2 tsp allspice
1/2 tsp cinammon

Directions:
Combine water, granulated sugar and lemon zest. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to med.
Add apples, cranberries and pinch of salt. Stir frequently until apples are tender (about 15-20 minutes).
Add brown sugar, allspice, and cinnamon.
Using a potato masher or a wire wisk mas apples until little or no chunks remain. (I like a few chunks for texture).
Let stand until room temp. Then refrigerate. Serve cold.

Submitter: 
Emily H.
Description: 
I made this off the cuff from some leftover Jonagold apples I had used for a pie. It was a hit at my Christmas party. Especially for those who find cranberry sauce a bit too tart.
