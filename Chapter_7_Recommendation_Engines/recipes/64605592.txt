Title:
GF Whole-Grain Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rice (brown, black, red or whatever)
1/2 cup whole flaxseed
1 cup buckwheat groats, divided
1/2 cup tri-colored quinoa
1/2 cup fonio grain
1/2 cup sorghum grain
1/2 teff flour
1/2 cup amaranth grain

Directions:
Grind rice, flaxseed, and 1/2 cup of the buckwheat groats  in a mini chopper until course.
Empty the mixture into a large bowl.
Add the other ingredients and mix well.
Store in an airtight container.
Ratio 1:3 mix:liquid

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
GF whole grain mix for breakfast or a side.  To be cooked in milk (veggie or dairy) or broth in a ratio of 1:3 (mix:liquid). Adapted feom GF Hot Breakfast Cereal
