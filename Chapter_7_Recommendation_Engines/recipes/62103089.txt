Title:
Quick N Easy Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Boneless Chicken Breasts
1 Pkg Frozen Brocoli
1 Can Cream of Mushroom
1 Can of Cheez Whiz
1 Cup Instant Rice
1 Can French Fried Onion Rings
1 can of milk

Directions:
Cook chicken breasts in water on the stove until done, season to taste. Chop chicken into bite size pieces. Mix cream of mushroom soup, milk, cheez whiz, rice, chicken, brocoli and 1/2 can of french fried onions together in baking dish. Salt and pepper to taste. Top with remaining french fried onions. Bake for 1 hour at 350.
