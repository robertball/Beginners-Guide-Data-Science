Title:
Taramousalata (taramasalata) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Tarama (carp roe)
1/2 Cup Olive oil
1/4 Cup Fresh Lemon Juice (possibly more to taste)
1 small onion
1 medium potato - cooked and chilled
8 pieces of white bread
Milk

Directions:
Soak bread slices in milk and then squeeze out as much milk as you can.
Add tarama, olive oil, bread, onion, and potato to food processor for about one minute.  It should be fluffy. If not add a little more olive oil.
Garnish with Greek olives, if desired.
Serve chilled with pita or crunchy bread.
Leftovers can be frozen and thawed in the refridgerator when you have a craving again.

Submitter: 
Tracy Tobias

Yummy Greek caviar spread that will impress your guests and leftovers freze well!
