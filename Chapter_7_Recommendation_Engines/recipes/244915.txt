Title:
Green Smoothie with Maca Powder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2920827.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped frozen pineapple
1 cup fresh spinach
1 frozen chopped banana
1/2 avocado
2 tablespoons almond butter
1 teaspoon maca powder
3 cups water

Directions:
Layer pineapple, banana, spinach, avocado, almond butter, and maca powder in a blender; pour in water. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Alli Shircliff
Description: 
Spinach, avocado, banana, almond butter, and maca powder come together in this energizing green smoothie. Carpe diem!
