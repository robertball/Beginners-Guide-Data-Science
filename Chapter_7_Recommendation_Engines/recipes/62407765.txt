Title:
Great Steak Marinade from food.com Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 tsp. olive oil
1 tsp. ketchup
1 tsp. minced garlic
1/2 tsp. dried oregano

Directions:
Place all ingredients in ziplock bag and squeeze to mix.  Add 2 nice steaks.
Refrigerate for about 3-4 hours, remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking.
Grill to you liking.. YUMMO!

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
I asked on the RE for a good marinade for 2 nice sirloin strips.  Coot226 recommended this one and it's a keeper!
