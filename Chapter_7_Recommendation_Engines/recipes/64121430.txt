Title:
Chicken Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1304642.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lbs - Ground Chicken
2 - Shallots
Salt & Pepper
---------------Sauce Mixture--------------
2 tbsp - Soy Sauce
1 tbsp - Honey
1 tbsp - Water
1 tbsp - Sesame Oil
1 tbsp - Peanut Butter - (Creamy or Chunky)
1 tbsp - Rice Vinegar
2 tbsp - Chili Garlic Sauce
------------------------------------------------------------
3 Cloves Garlic - Minced
4 Green Onions
8 oz - Water Chestnuts (Chopped)
1/2 cp - Peanuts

Directions:
Prep ingredients and set aside:
Dice Shallots, pinch of salt & pepper to taste
Chop Green Onions
Chop Water Chestnuts
Combine:
In a microwave safe bowl combine all sauce mixture ingredients. Warm mixture in microwave and mix to combine, set aside.
Cook Ground Chicken add shallots, salt, pepper mix.
When chicken is almost cooked, add Garlic and  sauce mixture.
Cook another 3 min.
Add Green Onions and Water chestnuts.
Cook till ingredients are heated.

GREAT, quick and easy alternative to PF Chang's famous chicken wraps with a mild kick.
