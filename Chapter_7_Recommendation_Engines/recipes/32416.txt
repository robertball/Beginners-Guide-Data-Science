Title:
Orange Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/870657.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
1 cup fresh lemon juice
1 cup sugar

Directions:
In a half-gallon pitcher, combine orange juice concentrate, lemon juice and sugar. Stir in enough water to make 1/2 gallon.
