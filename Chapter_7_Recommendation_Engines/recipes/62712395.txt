Title:
Mom's Cheeseball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 jar smoke-flavored cheese spread
1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 can chopped black ripe olives
chopped parsley

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, cheddar cheese, cheese spread, worcestershire sauce, dry mustard, salt and olives.  Form into a ball using saran wrap, place on a platter and sprinkle with parsley,  Chill for several hours.
