Title:
Magelby's Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package frozen Rhodes rolls , 36
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup melted butter
parmesan cheese

Directions:
1.	Take the frozen ball of dough and dip the top of the frozen roll into mayonnaise, then melted butter, and then parmesan cheese.  Place the roll with the dipped top up in a well greased muffin tin.  .
2.	Sprinkle with garlic salt and parsley and let dough rise about 4-5 hours
3.	Bake at 350 degrees for 12-15 minutes, until browned.
