Title:
Baileys Raspberry Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976917.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces Baileys® Irish Cream
1/4 ounce Smirnoff® Raspberry vodka
1 raspberry

Directions:
Pour the Baileys(R) Irish Cream and Smirnoff(R) Raspberry Vodka into a shaker.
Shake with ice and strain into martini glass. Garnish with the raspberry to serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Adding a touch of richness to any drink, Baileys® Irish cream liqueur mixes fine Irish whiskey, fresh dairy cream and a hint of chocolate flavors.
