Title:
Lemon Ice Box Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cool whip,
3 1/2 ounces lemonade concentrate, half frozen,
1 can sweetened condensed milk,
1 graham cracker pie crust.

Directions:
Mix lemonade, and milk; mixing well.  Fold in cool whip.  pour into crust, and refrigerate for 2 hours.  Serve garnished with lemon zest.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
My husbands grandmother used to make a pie similar to this for all of our summer get togethers.  He played around, and came up with a much simpler and quicker version.
