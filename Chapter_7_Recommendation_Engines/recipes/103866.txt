Title:
Cantaloupe Aqua Fresca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984615.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cantaloupe, peeled, seeded, and cubed
4 cups water, divided
1/3 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
Optional garnishes: cantaloupe wedges, lime slices

Directions:
Process cantaloupe and 1 cup water in a blender just long enough to make a coarse pulp; pour into a large pitcher. Add remaining 3 cups water, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, and lime juice, stirring until sweetener dissolves. Serve over crushed ice. Garnish, if desired.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
This cantaloupe scented water is refreshing on a hot summer day.
