Title:
Naan Ham and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4563081.jpg
Ingredients:
1 naan bread
1 slice ham, or to taste
1 slice Cheddar cheese, or to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place ham on naan bread; top with Cheddar cheese.
Bake in the preheated oven until naan is warmed through and Cheddar cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
