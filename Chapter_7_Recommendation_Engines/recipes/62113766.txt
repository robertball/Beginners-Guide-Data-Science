Title:
Geschnetzeltes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
21 ounces veal, sliced (preferably by hand)
2 Tbsp butter
salt and pepper to taste
1 onion
3/4 cup white wine
3/4 cup cream
a few drops of lemon juice
1 Tbsp chopped parsley

Directions:
Saute the thinly sliced veal in very hot butter.
Remove from pan, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and keep warm.
Slightly reduce heat, finely chop onions, and add to fat from meat.
Saute briefly and add the wine. Simmer until the liquid is reduced by half.
Add the cream and bring to a boil.
Add the meat juices and season with salt, pepper, and a few drops of lemon juice.
Reheat the meat in the sauce but do NOT let it boil.
Serve topped with parsley.
This is traditionally served with roesti or maybe pasta.
