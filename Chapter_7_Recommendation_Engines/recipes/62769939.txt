Title:
Paneer Tikka Masala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/642594.jpg
Ingredients:
TIKKA MARINADE
1/2 Cup Dahi Yogurt (Substitute Greek or American)
1 TSP Turmeric
1 TSP Grated Ginger
2 Cloves Garlic Minced
1 TBSP Garam Masala
1 TSP Tandoori Masala
Juice of 1 Lime
Salt to Taste
Cilantro Chopped as Desired
2 Cups Paneer Cubed
1/2 Onion Diced
1 Tomato Diced
GRAVY
1 TBSP Ghee (Substitute 2 TBSP Butter)
2 Cups Tomato Puree
1/2 Onion Diced
2 Cloves Garlic
2 TSP Tandoori Masala
2 TSP Garam Masala
2 TSP Suger
Salt to Taste
1/2 Cup Heavy Whipping Cream
Cilantro Chopped for Garnish
Spritz of lime

Directions:
TIKKA MARINADE
1. Combine Yogurt, Turmeric, Ginger, Garlic, Garam Masala, Tandoori Masala, Lime Juice, Salt, and Cilantro. Mix well.
2. Add Paneer, Onion, and Tomato. Mix well.
3. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Longer is better up to 4 hours.
4. Put marinated Paneer on skewers and bake at 350 for 5 minutes or until browned.
GRAVY
1. Heat Ghee in sauce pan. Add Onions and Garlic. Saute on medium for 5 minutes.
2. Add Tomato Puree cook for 5 minutes or until oil separates.
3. Add Tandoori Masala, Garam Masala, Suger, Salt, and Cream. Mix well.
4. Remove from heat. Blend till smooth.
5. Add Paneer. Fold gently into the Gravy.
6. Garnish with Cilantro. Spritz with Lime wedge if desired.
