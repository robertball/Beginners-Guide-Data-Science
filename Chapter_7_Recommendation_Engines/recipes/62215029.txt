Title:
Kathy's Pecan Tassies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Tassie Dough:
1 c. butter
6 oz. cream cheese
2 c. flour
Tassie Filling:
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1 1/2 c. brown sugar
1 c. chopped pecans
1 t. vanilla
2 T. butter

Directions:
Tassie Dough - Mix dough ingredients in food processor until dough form large ball.  Form into 1 inch balls and press into mini muffin pans.
Tassie Filling - Simmer all ingredients in medium saucepan until slightly thickened.  Spoon into shells.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.  Reduce temperature to 250 degrees and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
