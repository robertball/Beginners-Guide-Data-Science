Title:
Maple Walnut Banana Bread for Bread Maker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. milk
3 Tbsp. margarine (melted)
2 eggs
1 1/2 tsp. maple flavoring
2-3 medium bananas, quartered
1/3 cup sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
3/4 cups flour
3/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Place ingredients in greased bread maker pan in order listed.  Select 1 1/2 lb loaf size, sweet bread cycle.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
The maple flavoring adds an interesting twist to this delicious banana bread.
