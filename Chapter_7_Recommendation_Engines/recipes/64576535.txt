Title:
Mocha Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Angel Food Cake Mix
1 cup Sugar
1/2 cup Chocolate Syrup
8 oz pkg Cream Cheese at room temp
2 Tbsp Instant Coffee grounds
8 oz tub Whipped Cream
1/2 cup crushed chocolate graham crackers

Directions:
Make Angel Food Cake according to recipe on box, but bake in a 15 x 17 jelly roll pan.  Bake on lowest rack in oven for 25 minutes at 375 degrees.
Whip cream cheese with sugar.   Mix in chocolate syrup and instant coffee.
Fold in tub of whipped cream.
When cake is cooled, cut in half so you have  two 8 1/2" x 7 1/2"  cakes.  Place one cake into a 9" x 13" cake pan.
Evenly spread 1/2 the frosting onto cake.  Top with 2nd cake.  Spread remaining frosting on top.
Evenly sprinkle crushed chocolate graham crackers.   Cool in refrigerator for at least one hour before serving.  Store left overs in refrigerator.
