Title:
Swedish Pastry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. flour
1/2 c. butter
1/4 t. butter flavoring
2 T. water
Filling
1 c. water
1/2 c. butter
1/4 t. butter flaovring
1 c. flour
3 eggs
1 t. almond extract
Frosting
1 c. powdered sugar
1/4 t. butter flavoring
1/2 t. almond extract
1 T. milk

Directions:
Mix the first 4 ingredients together and spread on a 10x13 cookie sheet.
Heat the first 3 filling ingredients to boling, remove from heat and beat in the flour.  Add eggs one one at a time while beating.
Stir in almond extract and pour over crust.
Bake at 350 for 55 min.
While pastry is baking, make frosting and spread on when it comes out of oven.
