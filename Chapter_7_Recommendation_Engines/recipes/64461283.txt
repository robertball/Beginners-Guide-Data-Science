Title:
Shrimp Curry in a Hurry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
3 tbs sweetened coconut flakes
2 tbs barbecue sauce
1 1/2 tsp curry powder
1 tsp hot pepper sauce
1/2 lb peeled and deveined medium size shrimp
1 cup frozen peas

Directions:
Place mushroom soup,milk, coconut, barbecue sauce and hot pepper sauce in a 2 quart saucepan. Cook over medium heat until boiling, about 2 minutes. Add shrimp and peas and continue to cook and stir until shrimp are pink and cooked through, 4 to 5 minutes. Serve.
