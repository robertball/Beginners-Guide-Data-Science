Title:
Sugar Free Vanilla Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vegetable Shortening_______1/2 Cup
Sucralose/Splenda__________5 Cups
Cornstarch_________________1 Teaspoon
Salt, Kosher, Fine_________1 Teaspoon
Heavy Cream________________1 Cup
Vanilla Extract, Pure______1 Teaspoon

Directions:
Gather all ingredients and equipment.
Add the vegetable shortening to the mixing bowl and sift the Sucralose, Cornstarch, and kosher salt into it.
Using the paddle attachment, beat the ingredients together with the vegetable shortening on medium low speed until the mixture forms a coarse texture.
Add the eavy cream and vanilla extract; Increase the speed to medium high and continue to beat the mixture until it forms into a smooth frosting.
