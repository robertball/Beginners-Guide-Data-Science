Title:
Cheesy Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Mayo
1/2 Cup Sour Cream
8 Oz cream Cheese
7 pickled jalapeno slices
1 can black beans drained rinsed
1 can mexicorn drained
1 can black olives chopped up
1 can Hot Rotel drained
1 can Original Rotel drained
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped cilantro
4 to 6 chopped green onions

Directions:
Add cream cheese, sour cream, mayo and jalapeno slices to a food processor and mix until jalapenos are chopped up. Transfer to 9 x 12 dish.  add remaining ingredients and chill.  Serve with Frito Scoops.
