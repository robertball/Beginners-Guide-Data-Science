Title:
Mexican Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups of flour
2 Cups of sugar
2 tsp. of baking soda
1 (20 oz.) can of crushed pineapple including juice
2 eggs
1 cup coarsely chopped pecans or walnuts
1 tsp. vanilla
Frosting
1 (8 oz)pk. cream cheese (softened)
1/2 Cup butter (softened)
2 Cups powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Combine flour, sugar, baking soda, crushed pineapple w/ juice, eggs and chopped pecans.  Mix together and pour into a greased & floured 9x13 dish.  Bake in preheated 350 degrees oven for 35 t 45 minutes until done.
Frosting-
Combine the cream cheese, butter,  powdered sugar and mix well.  Add the vanilla.  Poke holes in hot cake with meat fork and frost cake while still hot.

Submitter: 
Nandabear
Description: 
This recipe came from a friend of mine and it is easy and delicious.  Some people mistake it for carrot cake.  When I take it anywhere for pot luck I always get requests for the recipe.
