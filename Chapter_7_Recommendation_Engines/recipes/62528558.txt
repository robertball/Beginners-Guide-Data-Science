Title:
Frozen Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c sifted flour
4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp cream of tartar
2 T sugar
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 c shortening
1 egg, beaten
2/3 c milk

Directions:
Combine flour, baking powder, cream of tartar, sugar and salt in a bowl.  Cut in shortening until crumbly.  Add egg and milk; mix well.  Turn dough onto floured surface.  Roll 1/4 inch thick.  Cut with biscuit cutter.  Place on baking sheet.  Freeze until firm.  Store in plastic bag in freezer.  Dip frozen biscutis in melted shortening.  Place on baking sheet  Bake at 450F for 10 to 15 minutes.  Yeild:  12 to 15 biscuits.

Baking powder biscuits with directions on freezing, then baking.
