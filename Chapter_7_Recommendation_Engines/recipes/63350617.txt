Title:
Spicy Baked Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. elbow macaroni
3 tbsp. olive oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 (14.5 oz.) can diced tomatoes
1 (10 oz.)pkg. frozen spinach, thawed and drained of excess liquid.
1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup and 1/3 cup grated Parmesan
1/4 cup plus 1/3 cup grated Romano cheese
4 tbsp. butter, softened
12 oz. mozzarella cheese, cubed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cook pasta to al dente and drain.
Heat olive oil in a large skillet and add tomatoes, spinach and red pepper flakes.
Cook, stirring occasionally for about 5 minutes.
In a small bowl, mix together bread crumbs and a 1/4 cup of each cheese.
Spread 2 tbsp. of butter in a baking dish.
Sprinkle half of bread crumb mixture to coat dish.
Combine cooked pasta with vegetable mixture, mozzarella and remaining cheeses.
Spoon into baking dish and top with remaining bread crumb mixture.
Dot with remaining butter and bake until golden brown, about 30-40 minutes.
