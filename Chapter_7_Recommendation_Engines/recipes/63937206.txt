Title:
Ginger Shrimp with Steamed Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1091568.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup uncooked white rice
1 teaspoon sesame oil
1 pound large raw shrimp, butterflied
2 tablespoons vegetable oil, for frying
1 red pepper, sliced
1/2 pound green beans
1/2 cup orange juice
1/3 cup lime juice
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon minced ginger
1 clove minced garlic
1 cup chicken broth
4 teaspoons cornstarch

Directions:
In a saucepan bring broth to a boil. Add rice, stir and cover. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes or until rice has absorbed the broth. Fluff with a fork, stir in sesame oil and keep warm.
In a large pan or wok saut√© the shrimp over medium high heat until the shrimp are pink. Remove shrimp.
Saute the red pepper and beans. Return shrimp.
In a mixing bowl whisk together the orange and lime juices, honey, ginger, garlic, chicken broth and cornstarch. Add to the shrimp and vegetables. Turn heat to medium high. Heat, while stirring, until shrimp and vegetables are heated through and sauce has thickened.
Serve shrimp and vegetables over the steamed rice.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Shrimp, red pepper and green beans are stir fried and mixed with a ginger sauce to create a tasty and colorful dish served over steamed rice.
