Title:
Cherry Coconut Bars by Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989789.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
5 cups Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal
1 cup flaked coconut
1/2 cup peanuts, coarsely chopped (optional)
1/2 cup maraschino cherries, well drained, chopped

Directions:
In large saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
Add Kellogg's(R) Rice Krispies(R) cereal, coconut, peanuts (if desired) and cherries. Stir until well coated.
Using buttered spatula or wax paper evenly press mixture into 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan coated with cooking spray. Cool. Cut into 12 bars. Best if served the same day.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
Description: 
Dried cherries and coconut make these bars a great family snack that's part sweet and part tart.
