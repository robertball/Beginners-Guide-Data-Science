Title:
Cream Cheese Cookies with Preserves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7469421.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1  8oz package of cream cheese, softened
1 cup granulated sugar
1 egg yolk
1/2 tsp vanilla
3 cups flour
Jar of preserves (I typically use raspberry, but strawberry is good too!

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
Lightly grease cookie sheet
Cream butter, cream cheese and sugar
Add egg yolk and vanilla, stir until well mixed
Gradually add three cups flour
Roll 1 tablespoon of dough and place on cookie sheet
Using a 1/2 tsp measuring spoon, gently press a dent into the center of each cookie.
Fill with 1/2 tsp of preserves
Bake 14 min, cookies will still be light

Submitter: 
Sarah Eve Sanders
Description: 
I hate cookie presses with the passion of a thousand firey suns. But I love cream cheese cookies! This is my personal take on creating a successful cream cheese cookie without a cookie press!
