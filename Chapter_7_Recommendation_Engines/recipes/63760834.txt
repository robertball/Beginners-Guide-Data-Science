Title:
Spinach and Green Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123193.jpg
Ingredients:
2 T. olive oil
1 T. cider vinegar
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
Salt and freshly ground pepper
5 ounces baby spinach leaves (about 5 cups)
1 Granny Smith apple
1/3 cup walnut pieces, toasted in dry skillet over a med.-high flame, about 2 minutes

Directions:
In a small bowl whisk together the oil, vinegar and mustard.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Toss the spinach with the dressing until evenly coated then divide the spinach among 4 serving plates.
Core the apple and slice it into thin slices.  Put on top of spinach and follow with the walnut pieces.
Serve immediately.
