Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag tortillia chips
2 heads of lettuce
1 small onion
2 pounds shredded cheddar cheese
1 pound ground beef
1 packet taco seasoning mix
1 16oz bottle Western dressing

Directions:
Cook beef then drain and add 1 package taco mix
completly cool meat (put in baggie and place in fridge)
put lettuce, onion and cheese in another bag or dish
crush chips and put in another ziplock bag
bring western dressing and put together right before serving

Submitter: 
cookinmama
Description: 
a cold taco salad, great to bring to parties and for cook outs.
