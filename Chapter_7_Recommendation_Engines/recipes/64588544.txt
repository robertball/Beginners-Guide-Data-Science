Title:
Luscious No-Cook Strawberry Lavender Freezer Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup boiling water
2 tbsp dried lavender flowers (or 4 tbsp fresh lavender flowers)
1 3/4 cups crushed strawberries
4 cups sugar
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 pouch CERTO Liquid Fruit Pectin

Directions:
POUR boiling water over lavender flowers in a small bowl. Let steep 15 minutes; strain and discard flowers.
MEASURE prepared fruit into a large bowl.
ADD sugar to fruit and mix well. Let stand 10 minutes.
STIR in lavender water, lemon juice and liquid fruit pectin. Stir 3 minutes. Some sugar crystals may remain.
POUR into clean jars or plastic containers. Cover with tight lids and let stand at room temperature until set (may take 24 hours).
STORE in freezer. If used within 3 weeks, jam may be stored in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Jody
Description:
