Title:
walnut and craisin coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
coleslaw ingredients:
2 cups chopped green cabbage
2 cups chopped red cabbage
1 cup craisins
1 cup rough chopped walnuts
1/4 cup diced red onion
dressing:
1/3 cup white vinegar
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1tablespoon celery seed

Directions:
mix coleslaw ingredients together in large bowl with lid
put dressing ingredients in container with tight fitting lid
shake until mixed
pour dressing over coleslaw mix and stir.
place in fridge for 3 hours for ingredients to blend together.
pour off extra dressing, and enjoy this crispy coleslaw.  Can be refrigerated for up to 5 days

Submitter: 
Janet Grella
Description: 
This is not your usual mayo coleslaw, a vinegar dressing along with walnuts and craisins, make this the best, crispy coleslaw ever!!!
