Title:
Chocolate Marshmallow muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/827597.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. All Purpose flour (unbleached)
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
1 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1/4 tsp. Salt
30 large marshmallows (approximately)
1 C. Milk
2 eggs
1/4 C. Canola oil
chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit
Grease a 12 cup muffin pan and set aside
In a medium size bow combine dry ingredients and put aside
Melt marshmallows in a large saucepan along with milk and stir until all marshmallows are melted in the milk mixture and allow to cool
Take cooled marshmallow mixture and pour into a large bowl
Add the eggs, and oil whisking until it is nice and creamy
Put the dry ingredients into the milk mixture a small amount at a time stirring until nice and smooth
Pour muffin batter from a measuring cup into containers filling 2/3 full
Sprinkle a few chocolate chips into each container and then bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes
Remove from oven and let cool 10 minutes before removing from pan

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very light muffin with gooey chocolate chips in the mix that melt real nice and make a very superb muffin for chocolate chip lovers
