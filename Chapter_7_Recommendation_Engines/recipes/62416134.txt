Title:
Prawn Curry with Coconut and Tamarind Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500 grams medium prawns, shelled and de-veined
1 can coconut milk
1 medium onion chopped
2 medium tomatoes chopped
1 tlbs tamarind paste dissolved in 1 cup of water
1 handful kari pata leaves(optional)
3 tlbs ginger paste
3 tlbs garlic paste
1 tlbs rye
1 tsp red chilli powder or cayenne pepper
1 tsp coriander powder
1 tsp turmeric
1 tlbs dried coconut pieces
2 tlbs oil, vegetable, canola or sunflower
1 tsp -salt

Directions:
Heat oil in a pan or stockpot on medium heat
Add rye, kari pata, dried coconut, mix till they release their fragrance
Add onion, ginger and garlic paste. Stir till translucent.
Add chopped tomatoes, heat till pasty
Add dry spices such as chilli, turmeric, coriander powders, stir.
Add the prawns, and allow them to brown, without draining all the moisture
Add cup of tamarind water
Add coconut milk
Replace lid on pot and let it cook on medium till the liquid has the consistency of stock.
Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves and serve with white rice.

Submitter: 
sugarFiend
Description: 
A traditional seafood preparation along the Malabar Coast of India right up-to Goa, known for its unusual flavours. Using the natural oils from the coconut, the tartness of tomato and tamarind, it provides a healthy meal without excess salt or oil. It is best eaten alone with white rice, Jasmine or Basmati.
