Title:
Homemade Crispy Coating Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. cornflake crumbs (approx. 8 cups cornflakes)
1 c. wheat germ
1/2 c. sesame seeds
4 tsp. dried parsley leaves, crushed
1 tbsp. paprika
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dry mustard
1 tsp. celery salt
1 tsp. onion salt or powder
1/2 tsp. ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients with a wisk until well blended. Store in a 5 c. container with well fitting lid. Store in a cool, dry place. Use within 2 months.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
A crunchy, flavorful, healthy coating you'll find very useful for chicken, fish, pork chops, you choose.
