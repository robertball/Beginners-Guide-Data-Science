Title:
Roasted Yam Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 kg yams (cut up like french fries)
2 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 Tbsp Sugar
1 Tbsp Chili Powder
1 Tsp Salt

Directions:
CUT up yams into french fries (4" long X 1/4" thick)
TOSS fries in large ziplock back with oil and rest of ingredients.
ARRANGE in single layer on parchment paper lined baking dish
BAKE @ 400F for 20 mins, turn over and bake another 10 mins.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
A healthy and delicious alternative to french fries
