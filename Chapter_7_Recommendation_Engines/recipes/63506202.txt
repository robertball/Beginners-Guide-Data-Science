Title:
Spiked Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Galons Apple Juice
1 Cup Sugar
1 Jar of cinnamon sticks
1 bottle of Everclear

Directions:
Bring the apple juice up to a boil, add sugar and cinnamon, let simmer 15 minutes then remove from heat
Let cool completely, pour into containers, finish by adding the liqueur
