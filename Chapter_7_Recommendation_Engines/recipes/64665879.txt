Title:
Goulash (Instant Pot) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic
2  - 15oz cans tomato sauce
2 - 15oz cans diced tomatoes
2 tbsp Italian seasoning
3 tbsp soy sauce
2 cups noodles
2 1/2 cups water
3 bay leaves
Salt and pepper to taste
1 pint mushrooms, sliced

Directions:
Turn the instant pot onto saut√©.
When hot, add beef, onion, garlic, mushrooms, salt and pepper. Cook until meat is browned. Drain fat.
Add tomato sauce, tomatoes, Italian seasoning, soy sauce, water, noodles and bay leaves.
Set the instant pot to manual high pressure for 4 minutes.
Quick release pressure.
Remove bay leaves.
Stir, top with Parmesan and enjoy!
