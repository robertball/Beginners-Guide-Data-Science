Title:
Creamy Cauliflower Asparagus Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4505648.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
2 small leeks, chopped
1 medium head cauliflower, chopped
1 pound asparagus, chopped
6 cups chicken stock
1 teaspoon thyme
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
handful fresh dill
handful fresh parsley

Directions:
1) Heat oil in pot
2) Add leeks saute 5 mins
3) Add cauliflower, asparagus, stock, thyme, salt and pepper; cover; bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer 20 mins
4)Puree
5)Stir in chopped dill and parsley
