Title:
Grilled Cheese Shooters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4571639.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (18.7 oz.) Tomato Bisque soup
4 Slices Sourdough bread
2 Tablespoons softened butter
4 Slices aged Cheddar cheese (1 oz. each)
Sour Cream to garnish
Chive sprigs to garnish

Directions:
Heat Tomato Bisque soup according to can recommendations.
While soup heating up, butter one side of each slice of bread. On un-buttered side of bread place 2 slices of cheese. Top with the other slices of bread (buttered side up).
Heat a large saute pan (high to med-high heat) and add sandwiches. Heat on one side till grilled golden toasted then flip and toast same other side.
Remove and cut off crust (if desired) into 18 -(1 1/2 in) squares then cut diagonal.
Place toothpick through each sandwich.
Pour heated soup into heat proof shooter glasses and top with skewered grilled cheese halves.
Garnish with dollop of sour cream and chive sprigs

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
My favorite Fall/Winter cold weather snack. Great with the family or friends over to watch the game or enjoy that book club review. Easy and quick fix that will always please. Forgive me for saying...who doesn't love a great grilled cheese sandwich topped with tomato soup.
