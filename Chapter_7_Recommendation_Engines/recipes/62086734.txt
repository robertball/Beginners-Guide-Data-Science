Title:
Mediterranean Chicken Salad II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. chicken breast, diced or shredded
2 tbl. light mayo
3 tbl. roasted red pepper hummus
2 tbl. sunflower seeds
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl.  Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to eat.

Submitter: 
Michelle
Description: 
This flavorful and simple chicken salad has been a huge hit!  This is fantastic in a whole wheat pita with some feta or pepper-jack cheese.  Great for people on high-protien diets.
