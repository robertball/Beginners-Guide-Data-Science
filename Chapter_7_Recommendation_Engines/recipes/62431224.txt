Title:
Sweet & Savory Tailgating Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Â½ cup Domino Dark Brown Sugar
2 Tbsp. Paprika
1 Tbsp. Black Pepper
1 Tbsp. Chili Powder
1 Tbsp. Salt
1 Tbsp. Onion Powder
1 Tbsp. Garlic Powder

Directions:
1. In a medium size bowl, mix together all ingredients.
2. Rub onto chicken, beef or pork; allow to rest 5 minutes before grilling, or wrap meat in foil and refrigerate overnight to grill the next day.
*Note: Yields about one cup, covering 2 to 3 lbs. of meat. Rub can be prepared in advance and store in an airtight container.
