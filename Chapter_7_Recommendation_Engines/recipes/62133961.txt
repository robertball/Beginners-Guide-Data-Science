Title:
Broccoli Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBL butter
2 TBL flour
¼ tsp. onion powder
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 ½ cups milk
14-1/2 oz can chicken broth
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 cups frozen broccoli
8 oz Velveeta Cheese
garlic salt to taste

Directions:
Melt butter over medium heat.  Stir in flour, onion powder, pepper, garlic salt, milk, chicken broth, and Worcestershire sauce.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Stir in cheese.  Cook over low head until cheese is melted, stirring constantly.  Cook broccoli in microwave, add to soup.
