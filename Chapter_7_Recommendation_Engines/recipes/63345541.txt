Title:
Mounds Candy Bars Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/888888.jpg
Ingredients:
2 2/3 cups Unsweetened Shredded Coconut
1 1/3 cups Coconut Oil
1/2 cup Sugar (I use equivalent stevia)
1 tsp Vanilla Extract Vanilla Extract (No Corn Syrup, Gluten Free)

Directions:
In a double boiler, combine shredded coconut, coconut oil and stevia
Melt and stir until smooth (don't cook the coconut!)
Pour in storage container
Freeze or Refrigerate for 10 minutes or more to harden
Store in Refrigerator

Submitter: 
Zan
Description: 
This goes great with Chocolate Bars - Hard - Sugar Free. I like to put the chocolate in a pan. Let it cool a bit (but not too long because it won't stick then) and then add the filling. Let it cool again and finally add the hard chocolate over top.
