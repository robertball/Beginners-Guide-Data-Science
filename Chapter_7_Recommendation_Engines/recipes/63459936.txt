Title:
Englands 3 lb sandwich spread /  appitizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb pork sausage
1 lb ground beef ( or ground turkey)
1 lb velveta cheese cut into cubes
2" x 2" dark rye bread slices...or ritz crackers or tortilla scoop chips or frito scoop chips

Directions:
fry meat till dome and drain off fat...add cheese and stir  till cheese melted and heated through...
1. place tablespoon full on top of sliced 2" x 2" and broil till cheese bubbles
or serve in a bowl with crackers, chips on the side

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
this recipe brought to a curch function by Beth who got this in England when husband was stationed there,,,,served it on tiny slices dark rye bread or with ritz crackers or tortilla chips or frito scoop chips
