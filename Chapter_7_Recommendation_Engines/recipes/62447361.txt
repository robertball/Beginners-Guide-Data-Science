Title:
MOCK "SALMON" PATE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups walnuts
2 celery stalks
1 lg red pepper
¼ c red onion
½ - 1 tsp salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients together in a food processor. Blend until smooth.  Can be served as pate for Nori rolls, rolled up in green leaf, or with crackers.

Submitter: 
Pat
Description: 
This is a surprisingly yummy raw foods recipe.  Chock full of protein.  It's so easy and simple. Enjoy!
