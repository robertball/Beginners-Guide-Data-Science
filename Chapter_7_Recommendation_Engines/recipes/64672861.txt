Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4830660.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Crisco Shortening (one stick)
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups plus 4 Tablespoons flour
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 pkg chocolate chips
1 cup nuts (pecans or walnuts)

Directions:
Cream shortening with sugar and brown sugar in mixer.  Add eggs and vanilla and blend well.  Mix together flour, baking soda and salt.  Add dry ingredients in batches, mixing well after each addition.  Add Chocolate chips and nuts and blend well.
Scoop onto ungreased cookie sheets.  Bake 11-12 minutes at 350 degrees.
