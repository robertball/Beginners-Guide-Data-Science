Title:
ATK Kansas City BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 t vegetable oil
1 onion, minced
4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
1 cup root beer
1 cup cider vinegar
1 cup dark corn syrup
1/2 cup molasses
1/2 cup tomato paste
1/2 cup ketchup
2 T brown mustard
1 T hot sauce
1/2 t garlic powder
1/4 t liquid smoke (optional)

Directions:
Heat oil in saucepan over medium-high heat until shimmering.
Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
Whisk in remaining ingredients, except for liquid smoke, and bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until mixture is thick and has reduced to 4 cups, about 1 hour. Stir in liquid smoke, if using.
(Sauce can be refrigerated in airtight container for up to 1 week.)
