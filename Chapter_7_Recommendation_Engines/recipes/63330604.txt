Title:
Quick Herbed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon EVOO
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Chicken soup
1/3 cup white wine
1/2 tsp tarragon
1/2 tsp parsley
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp marjoram
Broth Simmered Rice (see note)

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned.
Add soup, wine, and seasonings. Heat to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 10-15 minutes or until done. Serve with Broth Simmered Rice.

Submitter: 
Robin Whitworth
Description: 
Quick to make and tasty, this chicken recipe features Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Chicken with Herbs Soup for a warm and comforting meal.Modified from: 15-Minute Herbed Chicken
