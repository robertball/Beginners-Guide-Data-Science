Title:
Salad for gatherings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SALAD
2 bags of romaine hearts - torn up
1 bag spring mix
3 apples; chopped with skin on
1/2 can cashews
8 ounces of cheese cubed
1 bag of dried cranberries or cherries
DRESSING
2/3 cup canola oil
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
little less than 1/4 cup sugar or combo with Spenda
1 TBS onion powder
1 tsp dried mustard
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix, shake, and refrigerate dressing at  least 1/2 hour before serving. (makes more than needed)
Serve the salad with a portion of the dressing or serve dressing on the side for guests to apply as wanted.

Submitter: 
Jane
Description: 
Lettuce salad with dressing will be a winner at gatherings.
