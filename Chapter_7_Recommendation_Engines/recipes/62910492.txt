Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. Cooked Rice
1 C. Chopped Green Pepper
1 C. Chopped Mushrooms
1 Med. Onion Chopped
1 Can Stewed Tomatoes
2 C. Salsa
2 t. Celery Salt
2 t. Salt
1-1.2 t. Dried Chives
2 t. Splenda
1 t. Minced Garlic
1 t. Ground black pepper
3 Links Hot Italian Sausage
2 Pounds 80% Ground Beef

Directions:
Cook the rice. Put the ground beef and sausage in a large pan along with the green pepper, onion, and mushrooms. Cook off the liquid. Do not drain. Cook till meat is browned. Now add everything else except the rice.Stir well to mix and then add the rice.
