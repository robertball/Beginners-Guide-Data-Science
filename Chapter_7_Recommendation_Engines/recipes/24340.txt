Title:
Piggy Wiggys Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4031115.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated crescent roll dough
1 (16 ounce) package cocktail sausages

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a large baking sheet.
Separate crescent rolls. Flatten rolls and cut into triangles which measure 1 1/2 inches on each side. Wrap each sausage in a triangle of dough. Place on prepared baking sheet.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, until golden.
