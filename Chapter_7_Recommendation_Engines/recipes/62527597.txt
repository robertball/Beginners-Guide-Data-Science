Title:
Ritz Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup crushed corn flakes
1 box ritz crackers crushed (12oz)
7 tbs oatmeal
6tbs veg oil
1/2 cup of grapenuts
1 med onion grated
2 med carrots grated
season to taste
2 cups chicken broth or more

Directions:
Add all ingredients except the broth. Add broth gradually until moist. Put into a 9x12 dish and bake until crispy on top. Around 45 minutes. You can add more broth to reheat.
