Title:
Kathleen’s Toasted Ravioli with Basil Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/708517.jpg
Ingredients:
20 oz pkg of Beef Ravioli (Thawed if frozen)…any flavor ravioli can be used
1 ½ cups bread crumbs (If using Seasoned Bread crumbs, omit Italian Seasoning)
2 tsp Italian Seasoning
1 tsp Dried Basil
1 tsp Garlic Powder
½ cup Fresh grated Parmesan Cheese
2 Eggs
2 TBS water
Cooking Spray
Basil Cream Sauce
½ cup prepared Basil Pesto
3/4 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
½ cup Fresh grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
In a shallow bowl, combine Bread Crumbs, Seasonings and ½ cup cheese.
In a second bowl- beat 2 eggs with the 2 TBS of water.
Dip thawed ravioli first in egg mixture, then in bread crumbs until evenly coated.
Put a baking rack on a cookie sheet and place ravioli on baking rack.
Spray ravioli with cooking spray and place in a 400 degree oven for 25 minutes.
While ravioli are baking, heat Heavy Cream over Medium High heat in a sauce pan until it begins to thicken (about 2 min).
Stir in pesto and Parmesan Cheese.
Serve Ravioli as a main dish or appetizer with sides of the hot Basil Cream Sauce.
