Title:
Quick Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
3 cans kidney beans
1 can Rotel(optional)
2 TBSP red wine vinegar
1/3 cup onion flakes
1 tsp salt
2-3 TBSP chili powder
dash garlic powder
8 oz can tomato paste

Directions:
Brown meat with seasonings.
Add beans, tomatoes, and  vinegar.
Simmer 45 minutes.
Garnish with corn chips, sour cream, or cheese as desired.

45 minute chili.  Red wine vinegar brings it together.
