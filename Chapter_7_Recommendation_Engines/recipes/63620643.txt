Title:
Vegetarian Midnight Moon Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound penne
1 Tbsp butter
6 cloves of garlic, minced
10 oz. grape tomatoes, cut in half
6 oz. Cypress Grove Chevre
8 oz. Midnight Moon, grated
1/4 cup heavy cream
salt and pepper
1 tsp fresh thyme

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 375
2) Cook penneal dente, according to instructions on the package.
3) While the penne is cooking, heat butter in a large frying pan or Dutch oven. Saute garlic for a couple of minutes.
4)Add tomatoes and gently stir until warmed.
5) Stir the cooked penne into the garlic butter mixture.
6) Add the cream, thyme, and most of the cheese, reserving two ounces of Midnight Moon .
7) Stir until the cheese is melted and the pasta is well coated. Add salt and pepper to taste.
8) Transfer the entire mixture to a 9 x 9 baking pan, or another equivalent casserole dish.
9) Sprinkle the remaining two ounces of Midnight Moon on the top and bake for 25 minutes. Serve hot.

Submitter:

Description: 
I love cypress grove cheeses and pasta, and this is a great combination of the two.
