Title:
Hash Brown Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/364835.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/3 cup milk
1 3oz package cream cheese
4 cups hash browns
1 small onion chopped
1/2 cup shredded cheese
1/2 package onion soup mix

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine soup, milk and cream cheese. Cook and stir over medium heat until smooth.
Combine potatoes and onions; stir in soup mixture. Pour into 6x10 inch baking dish. Cover with onion soup mix and cover with foil. Bake at 350 degree F for 1 hour or until potatoes are tender.
Remove foil, then top with shredded cheese. Return to oven to melt cheese.
