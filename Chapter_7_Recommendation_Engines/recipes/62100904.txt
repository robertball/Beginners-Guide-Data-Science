Title:
Pampushki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds potatoes, peeled
2 2/3 cups potatoes, mashed, cooked
1/2 teaspoons salt
1/2 cups cottage cheese
2 tablespoons chives
2 teaspoons black pepper
Peanut oil, to deep fry them in

Directions:
Grate the raw potatoes and squeeze as much water out as possible. Combine with mashed potatoes, salt and pepper.
Combine chives and cottage cheese.
Scoop up a chunk of the potatoe mixture a little larger than an egg - flatten out, placing 1 teaspoon of cheese filling into the middle. Fold over and seal.
Repeat with the other dumplings.
Deep-fry in 340F oil for 15 minutes or until brown and crisp. Drain excess oil and serve
