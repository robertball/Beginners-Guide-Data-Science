Title:
7 Layer Jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package sugar-free Strawberry or cherry jello
1 package sugar-free lemon jello
1 package sugar-free lime jello
1 package sugar-free orange jello
2 packages gelatin
16 oz sour cream
1 cup sugar
2 cups milk
2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
4 small bowls, empty 1 package jello in each bowl with 1 cup boiling water, let sit 5 minutes then add 1/2 cup cold water to each bowl
in saucepan, heat milk until hot (remove from heat)  add sugar stir until disolved
soften gelatin in 1/2 cup cold water add to mild mixture til disolved
in seperate dish blend sour cream with vanilla then add to milk mixture and mix well
in a 9 x 13 pan
1st layer: red jello - put into fridge (15 min) or until set (once set remove from fridge and move to next layer
2nd layer: 1 1/2 cup of white mixture - put in fridge until set
3rd layer: lemon jello - put in fridge until set
4th layer: 1 1/2 cup of white mixture - put in fridge until set
5th layer: orange jello - put in fridge until set
6th layer: 1 1/2 cup white mixture - put in fridge until set
7th & last layer: green jello
return to fridge atleast an hour before serving.

Submitter: 
cookinmama
Description: 
This is great for a hot summer day, very refreshing and also beautiful. You need to have some time to make this but it's worth the effort.
