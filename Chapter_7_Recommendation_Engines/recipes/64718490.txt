Title:
Keto Peanut Butter Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c peanut butter (chunky, no sugar, runny)
1/3 c plain whey protein powder
1/8 to 1/4 c (used less than 1/4 c) powdered Swerve/erythritol
1/4 c melted butter (unsalted Kerry's)
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
pinch sea salt
1/2 c keto chocolate chips (Lily's dark chocolate)
1 T peanut butter
1 T coconut oil melted

Directions:
combine peanut butter, whey protein, erythritol
add butter, vanilla extract, pinch sea salt
stir well
divide among muffin/cupcake papers
melt chocolate chips, coconut oil, peanut butter
pour over peanut butter mixture to cover
freeze/refrigerate until hardened (approx 1 hour)

Description: 
Easy keto peanut butter cups with protein powder; could use almond butter too
