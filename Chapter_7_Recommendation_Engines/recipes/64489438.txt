Title:
Nancy's Fumi Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head of cabbage, chopped
3-4 T finely chopped onion, to taste
4 T slivered almonds
4 T toasted sesame seeds
1 pkg. chicken flavored ramen noodles
2 T sugar
3 T vinegar
1 t salt
1/2 t ground pepper
chicken flavor packet from ramen noodles
1/2 c salad oil

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingredients well. Refrigerate.
Mix the last 6 ingredients to make a dressing.
Just before serving, pour dressing over salad and mix well.
Note: This salad is not good left over.  The noodles get soggy.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
The crunch in this Asian-flavored salad comes from ramen noodles, crunched up. Thanks, Nancy for a change of pace in the salad department!
