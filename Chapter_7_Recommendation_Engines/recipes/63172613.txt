Title:
My Mom's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground veal
1 1/2 cups seasoned Italian breadcrumbs
3 cloves garlic minced
1 small onion minced
2- 3 large eggs beaten
1 cup carrots very finely minced
1 cup broccoli very finely minced
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 cup tomato ketchup
1/2 cup reggiano parmegiano cheese
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 small onion sliced
2 tbps oilve oil
1 1/2 cup of water

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 375F
mix all ingredients in large glass bowl til well blended. Mixture should be moist not dry. If dry add one more beaten egg.
Form meat mixture into loaf and place in 9x 13 inch baking pan sprayed with cooking spray.
saute sliced mushroomes and sliced onion in 2 tbsp of olive oil until slightly browned.
Pour mushroom and onion saute over meat mixtre in pan.
Add 1 1/2 cup of water to pan.
Bake 1hr 15 min  at 375F or until meat is no longer pink.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a staple dish we grew up on at my house. Mom used to sneak in the veggies and we never knew.
