Title:
Veggie Quesadilla For One Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large flour tortilla
1 t olive oil
1 T onion
1/4 c sliced mushrooms
1/2 c thinly sliced zucchini or yellow squash
1/2 c fresh spinach
1/4 c salsa
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 c cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Heat oil in a skillet; saute onion and mushrooms until tender, 1-2 minutes.  Add zucchini and cook another minute.  Add spinach and salsa and cook til zucchini is tender, spinach is wilted, and all ingredients are warm. Season with salt and pepper.  Slide vegetables onto one half of a tortilla and place tortilla in skillet.  Top with cheese and fold tortilla in half.  Cover and cook until the cheese is melted.
Options: Vary vegetables, using peppers, garlic, broccoli, etc.
Add a sprinkle of taco seasoning or red pepper flakes for more flavor.
Serve with sour cream.
