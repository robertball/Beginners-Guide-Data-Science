Title:
Stuffed mushrooms with Spinach and Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30 medium sized mushrooms wiped clean
1 package frozen spinach , thawed.
1 cup mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
4 cloves garlic minced.
1 package reduced sodium stuffing mix for chicken(Stove Top)
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp paprika
1tsp parsley,
1 tsp oregano

Directions:
Remove stems from mushrooms and finely chop,
In small saute pan, saute garlic and chopped stems in a bit of oilve oil . Season with salt and pepper Saute til translucent and fragrant.Remove to cool.
Squeeze spinach of excess water and add to large bowl along with stuffing mix, cheeses, and stem and garlic mixture, add parsley and oregano. Mix until well blended.
Stuff mushrooms, sprinkle with more parmesan cheese and place on baking sheet.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 min til browned and heated through.
