Title:
Crunchy Cucumber Rounds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 finely chopped red delicious apple
1 can (8 oz) unsweetened crushed pineapple, drained well
1/4 c. finely shopped pecans, toasted
1/2 cup low fat sour cream
pinch of salt
2 english cucumbers

Directions:
In a small bowl combline, the apple, pineapple, pecans, sour cream, and salt. Cover and refrigerate until chilled. Cut each cucumber into 1/2 inch round slices. Blot with paper towels to remove moisture. Spoon 1 tsp. apple mixture onto each slice.

Submitter: 
BECKY M
Description: 
Cucumber slices topped with a apple and pineapple mixture. Great party appetizer recipe!
