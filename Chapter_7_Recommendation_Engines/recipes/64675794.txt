Title:
Hearty Tortellini Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups quartered mushrooms
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped carrots
1 cup chopped zucchini
1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon  olive oil
2 small garlic cloves, minced
4 cups fresh spinach leaves
3 cups canned stewed tomatoes
2 quarts canned chicken broth
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
Tortellini (cheese stuffed is best)

Directions:
Saute vegetables in olive oil
Add all ingredients
Add cooked tortellini at the last ( I recommend keeping the cooked pasta separate; add to hot soup upon serving)
