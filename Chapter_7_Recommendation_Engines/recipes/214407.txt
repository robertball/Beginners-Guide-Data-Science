Title:
Smirnoff Classic Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989785.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 fluid ounces Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka
1/4 fluid ounce Vermouth

Directions:
Combine Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka and vermouth with ice in a shaker.
Shake well and strain into a chilled martini glass.
Garnish with lemon twist.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
Elegant, cool, and classic, the vodka martini -- shaken, not stirred -- is served straight up with a lemon twist.
