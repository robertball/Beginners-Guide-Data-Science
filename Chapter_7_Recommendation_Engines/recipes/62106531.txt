Title:
Easy Lemony-Dilly Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Long English Cucumbers
1 Lemon
2 tbsp dry dill weed, or to taste
4 tbsp mayonnaise
1 tsp sugar, or to taste(if it's a little bitter or sour add a bit more)

Directions:
peel and dice cucumbers, place in bowl
zest the entire lemon into same bowl
add mayo and sugar(or use equivalent measure of sugar substitute)
squeeze lemon into bowl
add dill weed, or double if you are using fresh dill
stir, and you can enjoy it right away, or enjoy it twice as much by letting it sit about 10 minutes before serving

Submitter: 
MyFoodWontKillYou...Really
Description: 
Zesty lemon zest, tasty dill, and a teensy bit of sugar all combine with cucs to make a super duper tasty combo.  The recipe says 4 servings, but I ate it all myself. Does not keep well, so only make as much as you will be eating right away.
