Title:
Cheese and Potato Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) package of frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
1 (8 ounce) package of shredded cheddar cheese
8 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
1/2 cup diced onion
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup diced green pepper (optional)
4 eggs
2 cups cottage cheese, small curd
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Lightly grease a 3 quart baking dish.
In a large bowl lightly combine potatoes, shredded cheese, bacon, onion, parsely and green pepper. Transfer to baking dish.
In a mixer, blend eggs and cottage cheese until smooth. Pour mixture evenly over potatoes.
Bake uncovered for 30 minutes or until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Cheese, hash brown potatoes, bacon and eggs come together to make a deliciously different and hearty breakfast casserole. Great for company or a special brunch!
