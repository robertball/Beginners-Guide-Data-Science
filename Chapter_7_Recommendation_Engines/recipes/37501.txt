Title:
Grilled Ham and Cheese for One Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/12012.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices bread
2 KRAFT Singles
3 slices OSCAR MAYER Smoked Ham
Butter or margarine, softened

Directions:
Top 1 bread slice with 1 Singles, ham and second Singles. Cover with the Second bread slice.
Spread outside of sandwich with butter.
Cook in skillet on medium heat until lightly browned on both sides.
