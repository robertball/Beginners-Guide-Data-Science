Title:
Bourbon Yule Luvit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980261.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Bulleit Bourbon™
1/2 ounce hazelnut liqueur
1 pinch nutmeg

Directions:
Layer the Bulleit Bourbon(TM) and hazelnut liqueur into a shot glass. Sprinkle nutmeg on top to serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
The hints of vanilla and honey, and long, smoky finish of Bulleit Bourbon™ Kentucky straight bourbon whiskey make it ideal for classic cocktails.
