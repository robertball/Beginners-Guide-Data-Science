Title:
Tuscan White Bean and Tuna Salad with Fennel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 scallions, chopped
1 medium carrot, diced (about 1/2 cup)
1 cup diced fennel bulb
1 tbsp chopped fennel fronds
1 tbsp chopped basil
2 tbsp capers, drained and rinsed
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp white balsamic vinegar
1 tsp Tuscan seasoning blend
1/4 tsp pepper
1 6-oz can tuna in water, drained and flaked
1 15 1/2 oz can cannelini beans, drained and rinsed
4 cups chopped romaine or other lettuce

Directions:
Mix everything except lettuce together.  Serve on top of or tossed with lettuce.
The tuna/bean mixture will keep covered in the fridge for a few days

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
A variety of flavors and textures make this salad a satisfying light meal.  Weight Watchers PointsPlus value of 6 pt per serving, plus it counts towards your daily health oil requirements!
