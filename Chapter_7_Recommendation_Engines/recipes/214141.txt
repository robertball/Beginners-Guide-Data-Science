Title:
Strawberry-Peach or Banana-Peach Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988806.jpg
Ingredients:
5 ounces canned peaches in heavy syrup*
2 fresh limes
3 ounces premium light rum
3 large fresh strawberries*
2 tablespoons sugar
Ice
Whipped cream for garnish

Directions:
Do ahead: In a blender, puree the entire can of peaches. Set peach puree aside in a separate bowl/pitcher. Rinse blender bowl and return to base.
To make 2 cocktail drinks: Juice two limes, and pour juice into blender. Add rum, 5 ounces canned peach puree, strawberries and sugar. Fill serving glass with ice, add ice to blender and repeat, adding another glass of ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into cocktail glasses. Garnish with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Mealtime.org
Description: 
Enjoy a taste of the islands in your backyard. This fruity daiquiri is refreshing and cool, and made with canned fruits that are great sources of vitamins A and C. This recipe can also be served with or without alcohol so the whole family can enjoy.
