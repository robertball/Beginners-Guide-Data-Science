Title:
Tomato-Cheese Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
1/4 cup soy sauce
tbsp dried onion
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1 large tomato, cut into 6 slices
6 slices cheese
6 hamburger buns

Directions:
Preheat grill.  Throughly combine ground beef, soy sauce, onion, and garlic salt in a medium bowl.  Shape into 12 patties.  On each of 6 patties, place slice of tomato and slice of cheese.  Top with remaining patties, pinch edges together to seal.  Cook on grill 4 inches from hot coals 5 minutes on each side, or until throughly cooked.  Serve on hamburger buns.
