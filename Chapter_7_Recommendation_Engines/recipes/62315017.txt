Title:
Black-Eyed Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/548829.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup cooked or canned black-eyed peas
3/4 cup diced green bell pepper
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 cup diced red onion
1/4 cup diced yellow onion
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons cider vinegar
1 clove minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon tabasco sauce (optional)

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix the black-eyed peas, green bell pepper, celery, and both onions.
In a small bowl, mix the oil, sugar, vinegar, garlic, salt, black pepper, and tabasco.
Pour this dressing into the large bowl and mix all the ingredients.  Let stand overnight for maximum melding of flavors.
