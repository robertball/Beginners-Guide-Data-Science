Title:
Keto Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6315345.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ½ cups almond flour
? cup coconut flour
1 tablespoon flax meal
½ cup cocoa
? cup baking stevia
2 teaspoons espresso powder or instant coffee (optional)
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
¼ teaspoon fine sea salt
½ cup (1 stick) salted butter, at room temperature
2 eggs at room temperature
1 cup unsweetened almond milk
For frosting:
½ cup (1 stick) salted butter, room temperature
8 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
1 teaspoon espresso powder
¼ cup cocoa
½ cup baking stevia
6 drops liquid stevia
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
One pinch of salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
*Prepare an 8” round cake pan by spraying and dusting with cocoa.
Whisk together almond flour, coconut flour, flax, cocoa, stevia, espresso, baking powder and soda, and sea salt.
In a large stand mixer or mixing bowl with electric mixer, beat together eggs and butter until thoroughly combined.
Reduce speed to low and gradually add dry ingredients, mixing until combined.
Continually mixing and add almond milk, starting with ¼ cup and adding ¼ cup at a time until mixture is thick frosting-like consistency.
Carefully spread into prepared pan and bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes, or until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean.
Meanwhile, combine all frosting ingredients in a large bowl with electric mixer.
Allow cake to completely cool and frost.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
A gluten-free, low-carb cake with an espresso cream cheese frosting that is delicious and decadent!  Bake in small layer cake pans for this pretty layered presentation.
