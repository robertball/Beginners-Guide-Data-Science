Title:
Baked Pork Chops and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion (diced)
2 Tbsp Butter
6 pork chops (thin/bone-in)
seasoned salt (to taste)
1 can Beef Consomme
water
1 1/2 cups White Rice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
In large skillet melt 2 Tbsp butter. Over medium heat caramelize onions. Set aside onions.  Sprinkle pork chops with seasoned salt. In same pan brown the pork chops on both sides.
Prepare beef consomme and water to make 3 cups.  Pour liquid into a 9x13 baking dish. Sprinkle rice over liquid.  Sprinkle caramelized onions over liquid.  Gently lay pork chops over rice in a single layer.
Cover baking dish with foil and bake for 45 to 60 minutes.

Submitter: 
rev'd up chef
Description: 
Growing up this was a staple in my home.  This is a great recipe because the pork and rice cook in the same dish.  The rice is flavorful and the pork chops come out nice and tender.
