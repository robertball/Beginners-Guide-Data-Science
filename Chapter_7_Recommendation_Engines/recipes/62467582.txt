Title:
Wheat Germ Whole-Wheat Pumpkin Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/4 cup canola oil
1 1/2 cups buttermilk
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup pumpkin puree
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 cup wheat germ
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups whole wheat pastry flour
1 tbsp. pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix eggs with oil, buttermilk, orange juice and pumpkin. Combine dry ingredients in a separate bowl; stir into wet ingredients until well blended.
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides, turning once.
