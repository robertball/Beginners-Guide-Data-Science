Title:
Creamy Salmon Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz penne, gemelli or rotini pasta
1 shallot, chopped
1-2 garlic cloves, minced
2 T butter
2 t flour
1/2 c dry white wine
1 c half-and-half
1/2 c water from cooking pasta
1.5 c coarsely chopped fresh spinach
1/3 c chopped fresh basil leaves
3 T chopped chives
1/3 c grated parmesan cheese
6-8 oz previously cooked salmon, cut into bite-sized pieces
salt and pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions, saving 1/2 c water for sauce.
In a dutch oven, melt butter over medium high.
Add shallots and garlic until softened and fragrant (1-2 minutes).
Sprinkle with flour and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
Add the white wine, stirring with a whisk until smooth. (It should boil almost immediately)
Add the cream and boil, stirring with a whisk, until the mixture starts to thicken.
Stir in parmesan cheese.
Add some or all of the reserved pasta water as needed to achieve desired consistency.
Stir in spinach and herbs and cook until fully wilted and dark green.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Add salmon and combine until heated through.
Distribute onto serving plates and sprinkle with additional parmesan if desired.

Description: 
My new favorite way to use leftover salmon.  I now buy whole fillets just so I can be sure to have leftovers for this recipe.  Rich and satisfying with lots of color.
