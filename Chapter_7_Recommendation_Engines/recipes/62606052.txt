Title:
Pumpkin Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
3 Tbsp brown sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp allspice
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups milk
1 cup canned pumpkin puree (pure pumpkin)
1 egg
2 Tbsp vegetable oil
2 Tbsp vinegar

Directions:
Mix the wet ingredients and stir.
Combine the dry ingredients and stir into the wet just until combined.
Make as normal pancakes.
*more milk might be needed if it gets too thick.
