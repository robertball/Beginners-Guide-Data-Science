Title:
Vanilla Pear Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/568550.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 egg
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
1/2 cup canola oil
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 firm ripe pear (such as D'anjou or Bosc) peeled, cored & diced
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Grease a muffin tin or line with paper liners.
In a medium bowl whisk together flours, sugar, baking powder and salt.
In a small bowl whisk together the egg, yogurt, oil and vanilla extract.
Pour wet ingredients into dry, add in diced pear and pecans, and mix just until combined.
Divide mixture evenly between the 12 muffin cups and put into preheated oven. Reduce temperature to 350 degrees and bake for 20-25 minutes or until tops are browned and toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Let cool 5 minutes then remove to cool completely on a wire rack.

Description: 
Vanilla and pear are a flavor match made in heaven. Tender, light and moist, and with the added goodness of whole wheat flour, these sweet vanilla pear muffins are great for breakfast or a snack.
