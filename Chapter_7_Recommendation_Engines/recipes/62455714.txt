Title:
Harold's Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C. shredded cabbage
1C. sliced potatoes
1C. slices carrots
1C. chopped onion
1 1/2 lbs. ground beef
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 can of water

Directions:
Grease a casserole dish. Layer cabbage, potatoes, carrots and onions. Salt and pepper each layer. Brown and drain ground beef. Place on top of onions. Mix water and soups in bowel then pour over beef. Bake covered at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes. Remove cover and bake 1 hour longer. while baking push butter knife to bottom randomly leaving holes for soup mixture to drain down to bottom.

A Hearty casserole that is easy to prepare.
