Title:
Pizza Style Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 medium onion, finely chopped
2 tbsp shortening
2 cans tomato soup or tomato sauce
2 cups macaroni
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
1/4 - 1/2 lb grated cheese

Directions:
Brown first four ingredients in shortening.
Add tomato soup or sauce. Cook stirring occasionally 15 minutes until thickened.
Cook macaroni, drain well.
Beat milk and egg; add to macaroni.
Spread macaroni mixture on greased cookie sheet. Cover with tomato-beef mixture. Top with grated cheese.
Bake at 350 F for 20 minutes.
