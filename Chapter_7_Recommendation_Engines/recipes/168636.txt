Title:
Fuzzy Brite Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/394781.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cubes  ice
1 1/2 fluid ounces brandy
1 tablespoon peach schnapps
1/2 cup diet ginger ale

Directions:
Fill a glass with ice, and pour in the brandy, peach schnapps, and ginger ale. Stir to mix.

Submitter: 
Karetha Palmer-Dellgrottaglia
Description: 
Always looking to lighten up my cocktails, I found a great way to make a fun beverage. And it's a great way to use brandy on those hot summer nights. Throw it all in the blender for a frosty, frozen beverage. Garnish with your choice: lime wedge, orange slice, or maraschino cherry.
