Title:
Double Chip Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981485.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (no substitutes)
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup peanut butter chips

Directions:
Place butter in a 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan; place in a 350 degrees F oven until melted.  Remove from the oven.  Sprinkle the cracker crumbs evenly over butter.  Pour milk evenly over crumbs.  Sprinkle with chips; press down firmly.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.  Cool on a wire rack before cutting.

Submitter: 
Victoria Lowe
Description: 
'Our two children love these sweet, rich dessert bars,' writes Victoria Lowe of Lititz, Pennsylvania. 'They go together so quickly that I can even make them on hectic days.'
