Title:
Pixie's Herb Roasted Potatoes/Baked French Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/696258.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Idaho baking potatoes cut into  cubes or fries
¼ cup olive oil
1 pack of sazon seasoning mix
1 tsp Kosher salt
1 tsp dried parsley
½ tsp chili powder

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 375 degrees
Put potatoes in a pot of water with sazon and salt and bring to boil
When the potatoes are cooked for about 8 mins where they are ½ cooked drain them
Foil a baking tray
In a bowl mix the olive oil, salt, parsley and chili powder
Pour mixture over the potatoes and put in a single layer on baking tray while the potatoes are still warm
Bake for about 40 mins until the potatoes are crispy outside

Submitter: 
Pixie2
Description: 
I came up with this because my husband doesn't really like mashed potatoes or potato salad so I needed something different to make with potatoes.
