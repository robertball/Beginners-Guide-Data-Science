Title:
Nicole's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 to 1 c. chopped carrot
1/2 red onion
5-6 cloves garlic or more
1 c or handful of chopped parsley
1 egg
3/4 c or more bread crumbs (1 toasted, 2 ends)
2 T agave
2 T BBQ sauce (optional -- or adjust other for sauce)
2 T balsamic vinegar
1 marzano tomato plus 1/4 c tomato sauce
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp curry powder
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp dijon or to taste
1 tsp oregano
salt
pepper
Glaze
2-3 garlic cloves
2 T balsamic
2 T apple cider vinegar
2 T agave
2 marzano tomatoes plus 1/4 c sauce
2 T BBQ sauce (optional -- or adjust other for sauce)
1 tsp approx dijon
2 tsp cumin
1 tsp curry
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1-2 tsp oregano
salt
pepper

Directions:
preheat oven 350
use fork to break apart ground beef
finely chop garlic, carrot, onion, parsley and blend/process in blender until chopped very finely
add vegetable mix to ground beef
add egg
add bread crumbs to make loaf
glaze
mix all glaze ingredients in blender and season to taste
make loaf and put in 9x12 baking pan
bake 10 min while preparing glaze
spoon glaze over meatloaf and continue baking for total of approximately 45-60 min
