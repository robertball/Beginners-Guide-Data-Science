Title:
Chicken Marsala 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour for coating
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon Italian Seasoning
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded 1/4 inch thick
4 tablespoons butter
4 tablespoons olive oil
2-3 cloves of chopped garlic
2 cups sliced mushrooms
1/2-3/4 cup Marsala wine
1/4 cup cooking sherry

Directions:
In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together the flour, salt, pepper, oregano & Italian seasoning.   Coat chicken pieces in flour mixture.
In a large skillet, melt butter in oil over medium heat. Place chicken in the pan, and lightly brown on both sides.  Remove from pan and keep warm in 200 degree oven.
Add mushrooms & chopped garlic to pan and saute a couple minutes.  Add back chicken and pour in wine and sherry.  Cover skillet; simmer chicken 10 minutes, turning once, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
Thicken with a mixture of heavy cream & cornstarch or chicken broth.

Submitter: 
Kathleen Farrell
Description: 
A delicious, classic chicken dish -- lightly coated chicken breasts braised with Marsala wine and mushrooms. Easy and ideal for both a quick weeknight entree AND serving to company.Modified from: Chicken Marsala
