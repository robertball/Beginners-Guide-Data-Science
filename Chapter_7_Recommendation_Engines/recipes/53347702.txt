Title:
Cream Cheese Pound Cake with Lime Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/593432.jpg
Ingredients:
CAKE:
3 1/4 c cake flour
1/4 t baking soda
1/4 t salt
2 1/4 sticks butter
8 oz. cream cheese
3 3/4 c sugar
6 eggs
1 t vanilla
3 T plus 1/4 c fresh lime juice
2 t lime zest
GLAZE:
1/4 c fresh lime juice
3/4 c sugar

Directions:
Have all ingredients at room temperature.
Preheat oven to 325.  Grease and flour a fleur-de-lis pan or a 10-cup Bundt pan.
Sift flour, baking soda, and salt over a sheet of parchment paper.
Cream butter and cream cheese together until fluffy.  Gradually add sugar and continue to beat for approx 5 min.
Add eggs one at a time.  Beat in vanilla and lime juice.
Fold in flour mixture in 3 additions using parchment paper to guide mixture.  Scrape sides after each addition.
Using a rubber spatula, fold in lime zest.
Place in cake pan making sides about 1-inch higher than the center.
Bake for 90 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
FOR GLAZE: In a small bowl, whisk lime juice and sugar until blended.  Brush over warm cake.

Submitter:

Description: 
Very dense and buttery.  The lime glaze makes it taste lighter.
