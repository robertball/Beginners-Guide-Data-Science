Title:
Festive Franks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup finely chopped onions
1/3 cup finely chopped celery
1/2 clove garlic
3 tablespoons shortening
1 can tomato soup
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons white vinegar
2 teaspoons dry mustard
4 drops Tabasco sauce
1 pound frankfurters

Directions:
In skillet, brown onion, celery and garlic in shortening.
Stir in remain g sauce ingredients and simmer a few minutes.
Add frankfurters.
Cover and simmer 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
aunt star
Description: 
Mom never used celery, and my sister uses much more hot sauce. Adjust to your taste. We always serve this over mashed potatoes.
