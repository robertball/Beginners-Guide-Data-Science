Title:
Bacon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 chicken breasts
1/2 lb. partially cooked bacon (less salt)
2 cans cream of chicken soup (low sodium)
8 oz. container of sour cream

Directions:
Wrap bacon around chicken and put in 9 x 13 pan. Mix soups and sour cream and pour over chicken.
Bake at 350 for 1-1.5 hours. Serve over rice. May cook in slow cooker for 3-4 hours on low.
