Title:
Sparkling Harvest Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 qts chilled apple cider
1 C lemon juice
1/2 C sugar
30 oz bottle ginger ale, chilled
cinnamon sticks, optional

Directions:
Combine 1st 3 ingred. and stir until sugar dissolves. Just before serving, add ginger ale. Serve over ice

Judy Bodnar
