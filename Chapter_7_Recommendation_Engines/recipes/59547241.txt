Title:
Budget To Die For Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/269997.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 egg
1/2 cup buttermilk (or milk)
1 cup frozen blueberries
zest of one lemon (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease muffin cups or line with muffin liners.  Sift together dry ingredients.
Beat together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.  Mix in egg.  Using spatula fold dry ingredients, adding blueberries in after a few folds.  Be sure not to over-mix.  Fill muffin cups.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until done.

Submitter: 
CookingOnABudget
Description: 
These moist muffins are packed with blueberries, if fresh blueberries are in season by all means bypass the frozen ones.  These muffins are perfect just the way they are and don't need the extra sugary streusel on top.Modified from: To Die For Blueberry Muffins
