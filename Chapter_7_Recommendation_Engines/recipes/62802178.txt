Title:
Bite Sized BLTs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 slices sandwich bread
1/4 head iceberg lettuce, shredded (1 1/4 cups)
1/2 lb ripe tomatoes, seeded, juiced and drained
1/2 lb bacon, cooked, crumbled
3 Tbsp mayo
s&p;

Directions:
Preheat oven 350. Flatten bread with rolling pin. With 2 1/2" cookie cutter, cut rounds from bread. Put in mini muffin pan and bake until toasted about 6 minutes. Cool a few minutes, then invert and cool completely on baking rack. Combine, rest of ingredients and fill cups.
