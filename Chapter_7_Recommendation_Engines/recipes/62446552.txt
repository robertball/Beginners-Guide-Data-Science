Title:
Microwave green beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11/2 lbs green beans
2 cups cherry tomatoes sliced
1/2 stick butter
1T sugar
1tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1T basil
crumble feta cheese

Directions:
Boil green beans for 3 mins. Drain. Lay in a sprayed oblong pyrex dish. Add the sliced cherry tomatoes to the beans.
melt the butter, sugar, salt, pepper and basil. Pour over the beans and tomatoes. Add the feta cheese. Cover and refrigerator until ready to cook. Cook in microwave on high for 4-5 minutes
