Title:
FRESH PEACH CRUMB CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2580629.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lg. Red Haven ripe peaches, peeled and sliced
¼ c. brown sugar
½ tsp. cinnamon
2 Tbs butter, melted
1-1/2 C. Bisquick
½ C. sugar
2 Tbs. butter, melted
1 egg
1/2 C. milk
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven 350°.  Grease and flour 8X8 pan (used Baker’s Joy spray).
Toss peach slices with the brown sugar, cinnamon, and melted butter.
Mix Bisquick, sugar, egg, milk, melted shortening, and vanilla.
Beat 4 min. medium speed with mixer.
Put peaches in bottom of baking dish.
Pour batter over.
Bake + or - 40 min.   Check w toothpick for doneness.
