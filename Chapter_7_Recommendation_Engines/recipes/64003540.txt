Title:
Stovetop Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
12oz can of evaporated milk
2 tsp table salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp ground mustard dissolved in 1 tsp water
1/4 tsp hot sauce
8 oz elbow macaroni (apx 2 cups)
4 T butter
12oz sharp cheddar, American, or Monterey Jack, shredded

Directions:
Mix eggs, 1 cup of evaporated milk, 1/2 tsp salt, pepper, mustard, and hot sauce in a small bowl and set aside.
Prepare pasta in 2 quarts of boiling water using remaining 1 1/2 tsp salt.  Cook until tender, but still firm to bite.  Drain and return to pan over low heat.  Add butter and toss to coat.
Pour egg mixture over buttered noodles along with 3/4th of the cheese.  Stir until thoroughly combined and cheese begins to melt.
Gradually add remaining 1/2 cup milk and remaining cheese, stirring constantly, until mix is hot and creamy, apx 5 minutes.  Serve immediately.
