Title:
Apple Sausage Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 mushrooms - quartered
5” Kilbasa Sausage - sliced
½ Onion – sliced & quartered
1-2 med potatoes – cubed
½ - 1 Apple – cored & cubed (leave peel on)
½ to ¾ c. water (add more throughout if pan gets dry)
salt, garlic salt and pepper to taste
2 Tbl. Maple syrup

Directions:
Put everything, except the maple syrup, into a pot and cook on medium high until potatoes are done  (about 20 min).  Add maple syrup just before serving.
