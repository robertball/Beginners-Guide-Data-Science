Title:
Cucumber Chicken Salad with Spicy Peanut Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5541266.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chunky peanut butter
1 tablespoon sambal oelek (chile paste)
1 tablespoon low-sodium soy sauce
1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
4 tablespoons water, or more as needed
1 cucumber, peeled and sliced into thin strips
1 cooked chicken breast, shredded into thin strands
2 tablespoons chopped peanuts

Directions:
Combine peanut butter, sambal oelek, soy sauce, sesame oil, and water in a bowl to create a sauce that is thin enough to drizzle.
Layer cucumber slices on a platter. Top with shredded chicken and drizzle with sauce. Sprinkle chopped peanuts on top.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
We call this bomb bomb chicken. Adjust the spice level by adjusting the sambal. 
Perfect for a hot summer day: Cool cucumbers with chilled chicken are topped with a spicy peanut sauce. Great as an appetizer or a cool lunch.
