Title:
Baked kale Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh kale leaves
olive oil cooking spray
salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Place kale leaves on baking sheet sprayed with olive oil non-stick spray. spritz leaves with spray & sprinkle with salt
bake until crispy, 5-8min
