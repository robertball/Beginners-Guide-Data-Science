Title:
Dill Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/386706.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups dill pickles, roughly chopped
1/4 cup dill pickle juice, or more to taste
1/4 cup canola oil
3 sprigs fresh dill
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place the dill pickles, pickle juice, canola oil, fresh dill, and salt and pepper in a blender, and blend until smooth.
