Title:
Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123104.jpg
Ingredients:
2# frozen shredded potatoes, thawed
4 c chichen broth
1 pkg MC Cormicks sausage country gravy mix
Salt, pepper, parsley and onion to taste

Directions:
Saute potatoes in soup pot.
Add broth and gravy mix and seasonings. (May add more broth)
Simmer.
