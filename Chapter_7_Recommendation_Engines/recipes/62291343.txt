Title:
Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c onion, chopped
1 c celery, chopped
1 large green pepper, chopped
1/4 c garlic, minced
1/4 t thyme
1/4 t basil
1/4 t red pepper
1/4 t black pepper
1/2 t paprika
14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
2 c chicken broth
1 c brown rice
2 bay leaves
1/2 lb cooked cajun, smoked or andouille sausage, cut into bite-sized pieces
1/2 lb ham, cut into bite-sized pieces
1/2 lb cooked chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
1/2 lb shrimp, cleaned

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, sautee onion, celery and green pepper until crisp tender.
Add garlic, thyme, basil, black and red pepper and paprika and cook until fragrant, about one minute
Add tomatoes, broth, rice, bay leaves, sausage, ham and chicken.  Mix well, cover and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low and cook until rice is done and liquid is absorbed, about two hours.
Remove bay leaves, add shrimp and cook until shrimp is heated or cooked through, depending upon whether cooked or raw shrimp were used.

Description: 
Hearty ingredients combine with a spicy combination of herbs and spices to create this New Orleans favorite.
