Title:
Poblano Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1557675.jpg
Ingredients:
4 seeded and rough chopped fresh Poblano peppers
2 T real butter
1 t each onion powder & garlic powder
Chicken broth
JASMINE rice

Directions:
Saut√© peppers in butter until slightly soft.
Add onion and garlic powder.
Add 1 1/2 cup chicken broth and bring to boil.
Add 1 cup raw rice.
Heat down to low.
Cover tightly (no peeking) for 12 mins.

Submitter: 
MitchJGray
Description: 
This recipe allows you to taste what poblano brings to the table. Most poblano recipes use the pepper as a container. This showcases the pepper and it's incredible stand alone flavor. GREAT with fish!
