Title:
Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077018.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can "cheap" baked beans
2 t ketchup
2 t dark brown sugar
1 c crumbled bacon
1 tsp mustard
1 tsp worcestershire
Green pepper
Onion

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together & cook in a 350 oven until sugar melts & onion & pepper is tender, about 30 mins for 3 cans.
