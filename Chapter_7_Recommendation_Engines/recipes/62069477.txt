Title:
Chicken, Tomato and Artichokes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 tbsp. olive oil
-4 chicken breasts, cut up into bite sized pieces
-1 medium yellow onion, chopped
-3 cloves garlic, minced
-2 12 oz cans stewed or diced tomatoes
-1/4 cup chicken broth or white wine
-1 tbsp basil
-2 tsp oregano
-1 tsp red pepper flakes (or to taste)
-2 bay leaves
-1 can quartered artichokes, drained
-3 cups cooked white rice
-salt and fresh cracked pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat.
Add chicken and onions and season generously with pepper.  Saute until chicken is no longer pink in the middle and is lightly browned.
Add garlic and saute 2-3 minutes.
Add the tomatoes, broth or wine, bay leaves, pepper flakes, oregano and basil. Stir well, scraping up browned bits from the bottom of the pan and reduce heat to medium.
Simmer for 20-30 minutes or until thickened. Taste for salt & pepper and adjust accordingly.
Add artichokes and cook until heated through.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
CHRISTYG
Description: 
Sauteed chicken, garlic and onions with a savory tomato sauce partnered with tart artichokes. Serve over rice or pasta!
