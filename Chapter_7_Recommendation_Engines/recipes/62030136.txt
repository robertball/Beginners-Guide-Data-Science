Title:
CORN CHOWDER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4C. chopped Onion
2Tbsp. Margarine or Butter
1C. frozen Hash Brown Potatoes
1C. diced, Cooked-Ham
1Pkg. {10ozs.} frozen Whole Kernal Corn
1C. Cream-Style Corn
1 can {10 3/4ozs.} Cream Of Mushroom Soup, undiluted
2 1/2Cs. Milk
Salt
Black Pepper
Parsley Flakes

Directions:
Combine onion, margarine, potatoes, ham, corn, cream of mushroom soup and milk in Crock-Pot. Cover and cook on High 4 to 5 hours. Salt and pepper to taste.
Garnish with parsley flackes and parmesan cheese.
Serve hot and with Herb Toast!!

Submitter: 
Corwynn Darkholme
Description: 
Quick and very easy to make!! This is also a very savory recipe!!
