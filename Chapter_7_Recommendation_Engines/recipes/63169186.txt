Title:
Dana's Milkalicious Energy-Booster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-1/2 cups of low-fat milk
1 teaspoon of liquid vanilla extract
2 ripe bananas
5 slices of canned pineapple
1/4 cup frozen blueberries
1/4 cup frozen blackberries
2 tablespoons granulated sugar
2 scoops of mango or strawberry ice cream
1/4 cup granulated sugar (optional for decoration)
3 drops of any food coloring of your preference (optional for decoration)
4 teaspoons of water

Directions:
Pour the milk into the blender.
Add vanilla extract, bananas and pineapple slices. Make sure to chop your bananas into halves or quarts. Blend for a minute or two.
In a strainer rinse your blueberries and blackberries to remove extra ice and soften a little bit. Put into the blender and mix miX MIX!
Scoop in your ice cream and add the sugar. Blend well.
For decoration, you will need two small flat plates. Mix the water with the food coloring and pour into a plate. Put your sugar into the other.
Now holding your glass upside down dip the whole glass' drinking edge into food coloring solution first then quickly into the sugar to get a colored crystal like look!
Pour your smoothie into the glasses, and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Dana
Description: 
This is a super easy, anytime milkshake that I usually enjoy with my family on weekends. It is healthy and super fruity and milky at the same time. A great source of proteins, calcium and vitamins.
