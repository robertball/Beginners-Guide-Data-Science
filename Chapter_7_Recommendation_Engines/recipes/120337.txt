Title:
Melon with Minted Lime Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981991.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup water
6 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons minced fresh mint
2 teaspoons grated lime peel
1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger
8 cups melon balls or cubes
MINTED LIME DIP;
1 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon lime juice
2 teaspoons grated lime peel

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the sugar, water, lime juice, mint, lime peel and ginger. Add melon balls. Cover and refrigerate for 1-6 hours. Thread melon onto wooden skewers or toothpicks. In a bowl, combine dip ingredients. Serve with melon.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home Test Kitchen
Description: 
For a refreshing summer side dish for any meal, our Test Kitchen recommends these marinated melon balls with a cool, creamy dip. You can also serve this fruity treat as a light dessert.
