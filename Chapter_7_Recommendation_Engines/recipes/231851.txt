Title:
Easy Mango Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6236089.jpg
Ingredients:
2 mangos - peeled, seeded, and sliced
2 bananas
2 cups vanilla yogurt
2 cups milk

Directions:
Blend mangos, banana, vanilla yogurt, and milk in a blender until smooth.
