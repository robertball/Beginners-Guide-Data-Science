Title:
Old Virginia Wassail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 qts Sweet Apple Cider
2 cups Orange Juice
1 cup Lemon Juice
1 20-oz. can Sweetened Pineapple Juice
1 stick Whole Cinnamon
1 tsp. Whole Cloves
3/4 to 1 cup Sugar

Directions:
Combine ingredients and bring to a simmer.  Strain and serve.

Submitter: 
Kevin G
Description: 
This warm, spiced juice beverage is traditionally made during the Christmas season.  But it is so good you want to make it all year long.
