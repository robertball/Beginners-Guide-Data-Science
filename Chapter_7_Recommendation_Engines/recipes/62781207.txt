Title:
Couscous Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box instant couscous
6oz marinated Artichokes
2 cup cherry tomatoes
3 scallions
1 can chick peas
1 large cucumber
5-10 mint leaves
1 rotisserie chicken
2-3 lemons
1/2 cup olive oil
salt, pepper, cayenne pepper
Pine nuts, if desired

Directions:
Cook couscous according to package and let cool.
Drain artichokes and reserve liquid, cut tomatoes in halve, slice scallions on a diagonal, de-seed cucumber and cut into large diced pieces
Combine the above ingredients in a large bowl. Add chunks of chicken until the mixture is to your liking.
In a separate bowl, combine 1/4 cup artichoke liquid, juice from 2-3 lemons, 1/2 cup olive oil. Mix together, then combine into other mixture.
Add salt, pepper, cayenne pepper to taste
Add torn pieces of 5-10 mint leaves and mix throughly.
