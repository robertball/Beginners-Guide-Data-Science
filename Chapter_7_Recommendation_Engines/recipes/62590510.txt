Title:
Cornbread Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (8oz) cornbread mix
1 envelope ranch dressing mix
1c milk
6 boneless chicken breasts (thin)
2T vegetable oil

Directions:
Combine cornbread mix and ranch in paper/plastic bag.
Dip chicken in milk and then in bag.
Brown chicken in oil over medium heat.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
Can be finished in the oven if needed!
