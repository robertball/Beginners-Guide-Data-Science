Title:
Citrus Carrot Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded carrots.
1/4 cup dried raisins.
1 teaspoon lemon juice.
1 teaspoon white sugar.
1 teaspoon lime juice.
1 tablespoon marsh mellows(optional)

Directions:
1. Mix carrots, raisins, lemon & white sugar & lime juice. 2. Toss with a Salad fork.3. Place in the microwave for 30 seconds on low, bowl covered. When timer beeps, take out salad and place in a glass bowl. Sprinkle marsh mellows on top of salad to garnish.

Submitter: 
TheGlamChef
Description: 
This is a healthy, sweet, and even seasonal dish! It can be made for 4th of July instead of coleslaw, and it's perfect for a cool afternoon picnic!
