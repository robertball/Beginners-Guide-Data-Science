Title:
Easy Apricot Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breasts
12 oz jar salsa
8 oz jar apricot jam
1 packet taco seasoning mix

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a 9x13 baking dish, and bake at 350 F for 20 minutes, or until chicken reaches 165 F.
To make ahead:
Place all ingredients in freezer bag, and lay flat in freezer.  When ready to use, thaw overnight in fridge.  Empty contents into 9x13 baking dish and bake at 350 F for 20 minutes, or until chicken reaches 165 F.

Submitter: 
Sissy's Momma
Description: 
So easy and quick, this takes less than 30 minutes to make! I serve this over rice.
This is excellent to make ahead and have ready as a freezer standby.
