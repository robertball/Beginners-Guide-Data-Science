Title:
Curry Pineapple Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp curry powder
2 tbsp fish sauce
2 tbsp pineapple juice
1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 lb. chicken, cubed
1 medium onion, sliced
1 20-oz. can pineapple chunks
3 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Combine the curry powder, fish sauce, and pineapple juice in a small bowl. Mix thoroughly.
In a wok or large skillet, cook the oil, chicken, and onions until the chicken is white and the onions are soft (approx 5 minutes).
Add the rice, pineapple, and curry mixture. Stir fry until everything is hot.
