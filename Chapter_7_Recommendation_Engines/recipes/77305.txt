Title:
Chocolate Swizzle Nog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977600.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups milk
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
4 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract or peppermint extract
Whipped cream or whipped topping

Directions:
In medium-sized saucepan over medium heat, combine milk, EAGLE BRAND(R), and cocoa. Heat through, stirring constantly. Remove from heat; stir in vanilla or peppermint extract.
Serve warm in mugs topped with whipped cream. Store covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Warm up on a cold winter night with this tasty, cream-topped nog.
