Title:
Banana & Persimmon Cinnamon Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1566425.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Hachiya persimmon
2 ripe bananas
1/3 C coconut oil - melted
3/4 C sugar plus 1 Tbsp
1 egg
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 C flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
-Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees
-Line muffin pan with muffin cups or grease with coconut oil
-Peel and mash the bananas & persimmon in a med size bowl of a standing mixer
-add oil, 3/4 C sugar, egg and vanilla,  mixing well after each ingredient is added
-sift together flour, baking power and salt in a small bowl
-add dry ingredients to wet, stir by hand just to combine
-fill muffin pan 3/4 full
-combine remaining sugar with cinnamon and sprinkle evenly over batter
-bake for 18-23 minutes until a tooth pick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Anna Silvani-Morrison
Description: 
I put this recipe together to use up some bananas & persimmon that were very very ripe!  As soon as they began to bake my daughter said she could smell Autumn.
