Title:
Chicken with Mushroom Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb chicken breasts
1/2 cup almond flour
1/2 tsp tarragon
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp butter
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 cup shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and sliced
1/4 cup dry sherry
3/4 cup chicken stalk
2 tsp chives

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Pound chicken breasts until theyre 3/4 inches thick
On a large plate, combine almond flour, black pepper and tarragon. Dredge chicken breasts in seasoned flour.
Preheat large skillet over medium-high heat. Add in olive oil and once its smoking slightly gently place chicken breasts in skillet. Sear 4-5 minutes per side. Remove to baking dish and bake 10-12 minutes.
Reduce heat in skillet to medium. Add butter to skillet, stir in garlic one minute and then add mushrooms. Saute 1-2 minutes then add sherry and chicken stock. Simmer until mushrooms are soft.
Add chives just before serving. Serve over baked chicken.
