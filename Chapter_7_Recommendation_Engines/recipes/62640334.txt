Title:
Strawberry Shag Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/581994.jpg
Ingredients:
1 to 2 large sprigs fresh basil
1/4 cup Sugared Strawberries with juice
1 1/2 ounces vodka
1 ounce fresh lemon juice
3 to 4 ounces chilled soda water
Fresh strawberry for garnishing
Small basil leaf for garnishing

Directions:
In a cocktail shaker, press the basil and strawberries together with a muddler to release the basilâ€™s flavor. Fill the shaker with ice. Measure in the vodka and lemon juice. Cap and shake vigorously. Pour into a large glass, top with soda water, and stir. Garnish with a strawberry and basil leaf
