Title:
easy shrimp and butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of shrimp or more. peel and deveined leaving tails on if you want
1 stick of butter
1 package of good seasoning Italian dressing mix
1 lemon sliced

Directions:
Melt a stick of butter in a flat roasting pan.
Slice one lemon and layer it on top of the butter.
Put down the fresh shrimp , then sprinkle one pack of seasoning
on top of shrimp.
put in oven and bake at 350 for 15 mins.
best shrimp you'll ever eat.
