Title:
Mother's Chocolate Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup butter
3 cups sugar
5 eggs
3 cups plain flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup cocoa
1 1/4 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Cream 1st. 3 ingredients together, add eggs, alternately add flour and other dry ingredients cocoa and milk. Grease and flour tube pan, bake at 350* for 1 hr. 15 min.
