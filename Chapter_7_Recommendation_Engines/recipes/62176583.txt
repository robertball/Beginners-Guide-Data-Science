Title:
Grandma's Molasses Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c shortening
1 c sugar
1/4 c molasses
1 egg
2 tsp
2 c flour
1/2 tsp. cloves
1/2 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp salt
1 T cinnamon

Directions:
Melt shortening.  Let it cool.
Add sugar, molasses and egg.
Beat well.
Sift dry ingredients, then add to the wet.
Chill in refrigerator.
Form 1 inch balls.  Roll in sugar.
Place on greased cookie sheet.
Bake for 8-10 minutes, but watch, they burn easily.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
Crunchy flavorful cookies.  They don't last in our house!
