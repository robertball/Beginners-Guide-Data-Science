Title:
Grandma Raap’s Chop Suey Instant Pot version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6365461.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter
1 lb beef, pork or chicken cut into 1” cubes
1 onion diced
2 celery stalks diced
2  28 oz can chop Suey vegetable (rinsed & drained)
1 small can water chestnuts (drained)
1/2 cup uncooked rice (not minute rice)
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 1/2 cup water
2 tsp Molasses
Asian noodles
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Turn Instant Pot on to Sauté
When display reads hot, melt butter
Brown meat on all sides
Add  celery,  and onion
Once the vegetables are cooked, stir in the soy sauce, molasses, water, and rice.
Turn the Instant Pot to off. Secure the lid and set the valve to sealing.
Select pressure cook and set time to 10 minutes.
When timer goes off,usually natural venting.
Stir and serve.
Top with Asian noodles.
