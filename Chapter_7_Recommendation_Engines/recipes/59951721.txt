Title:
Angel's Chunky Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cooked, boneless chicken breast half, chopped (2-3 c chicken)
2 stalks celery, chopped
4-5 tablespoons mayonnaise or Miracle Whip
1/2 onion, finely diced
2 dill pickles, chopped or sweet relish
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste
pine nuts

Directions:
Combine the chicken, celery, mayonnaise, onion and pickle and pine nuts, if desired; mix well.
Season with the garlic powder, salt and pepper. Serve with lettuce on fresh crusty bread or bun.
