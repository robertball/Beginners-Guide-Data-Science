Title:
Berry Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup oats
1/4 cup mixed berries (raspberries, blueberries, blackberries)

Directions:
Prepare oats by mixing with 1 cup of water and microwaving until it reaches desired consistency, stirring every 30 seconds.
Stir in mixed berries.

Description: 
This should keep you full until lunch!
