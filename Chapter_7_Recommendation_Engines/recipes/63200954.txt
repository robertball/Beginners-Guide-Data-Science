Title:
Leftover Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Left over meat, veggies, and mashed potatoes
Golden mushroom soup/use cream of chicken when using chicken or turkey
Shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Cut meat into bite size pieces
Put in a baking dish
Spread soup over meat
Add vegetables, minus the juice
Mix potatoes with shredded cheddar cheese
Spread over the top
Cover with foil
Bake for 30 to 45 min til it's hot

Submitter: 
KENDRU58
Description: 
This is a nice use of leftovers, and offers a different taste for the second night
