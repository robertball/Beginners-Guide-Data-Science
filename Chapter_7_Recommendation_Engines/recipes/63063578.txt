Title:
Chicken Tortilla Soup NMR Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1/2 teaspoon olive oil
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
2 (32 ounce) cans/boxes chicken broth
1 cup frozen corn kernels
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 1/2 cup chunky salsa (Embassa, etc)
8 ounces corn tortilla chips
3/4 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese (optional)
1 bunch fresh cilantro (optional)

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, saute the chicken in the oil with the garlic and cumin until breasts are done. Let breasts cool then shred by hand and return to pot. Then add the broth, corn, onion, chili powder, lemon juice and salsa. Reduce heat to low and simmer for about 20 to 30 minutes.
Break up some tortilla chips into individual bowls and pour soup over chips. Top with cilantro to taste, the Monterey Jack cheese and a little sour cream.

Submitter: 
StanDiego
Description: 
Lemon juice brightens the flavors in this chicken, corn and salsa soup seasoned with cumin and chili powder.  Garnish with tortilla chips, grated cheese and a dollop of sour cream.Modified from: Chicken Tortilla Soup V
