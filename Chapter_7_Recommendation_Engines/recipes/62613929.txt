Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/603476.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package onion soup mix
1 pound ground beef
1 pound sausage
3/4c bread crumbs
2 eggs
3/4c water
1/3c ketchup
1/3c brown sugar

Directions:
Combine everything except ketchup and brown sugar and make into loaf.
Bake at 350 for 50 minutes.
Mix ketchup and brown sugar and top loaf, bake 10 minutes.
