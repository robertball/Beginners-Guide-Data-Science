Title:
Warm Blue Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8-oz) package of Neufchatel Cheese, softened
5 oz. crumbled blue cheese
1/2 cup milk
4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
2 T finely chopped green onions
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 cup chopped hickory smoked almonds (optional)

Directions:
1. In small saucepan over low heat, combine Neufchatel cheese, blue cheese, milk, bacon, green onions and garlic powder.
2. Cook, stirring frequently, until cheese is melted.  Transfer to a 1-quart slow cooker on low to keep warm.
3. Top with chopped almonds (opt) and serve with pita chips and assorted vegetables.

Submitter: 
BajaGirl61
Description: 
This dip is great for blue cheese lovers.  It is great served with celery sticks and pita chips.
