Title:
Sparkling Peachy Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bottle - Ocean Spray White Cran-Peach (don't substitute brands, tried generics and they don't measure up)
1 liter - Sprite or Ginger Ale (your taste)
2 cans (12 oz) - Peach Juice (try in Mexican section of store)
2 bags - frozen Peach slices
1 bag - frozen Raspberries (optional but beautiful)

Directions:
All liquid should be cold before starting.
Mix together in punch bowl. Start with one can peach juice and add the second to your taste of sweetness. Place as much frozen peaches as to make a nice layer on top. Sprinkle raspberries to add color. Place remaining fruit back in freezer and add as needed.

Submitter: 
Cindy
Description: 
This is a beautiful, tasty punch that is simple to make.  Frozen peaches makes this punch stand out on the table.
