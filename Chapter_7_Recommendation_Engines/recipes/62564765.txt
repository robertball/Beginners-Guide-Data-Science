Title:
Gluten Free Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cornstarch
1 1/2 cups icing sugar
1/3 cup fine white rice flour
2/3 cup sorghum flour
1 cup tapioca starch
1 cup sweet rice flour
2 1/4 cups softened butter

Directions:
Sift together the cornstarch, icing (powdered) sugar and flours.
Blend in butter until a soft smooth dough forms.
Shape into 1" logs and chill 30 -60 minutes. Slice into 1/8" rounds.
Place 1 1/2" apart on a cookie sheet.
Bake 300' for 15-20 minutes. Transfer to a rack and cool

Submitter: 
Carriescows
Description: 
Adapted from an old family favorite you'll need to make these in large batches to keep them around long enough for company!
