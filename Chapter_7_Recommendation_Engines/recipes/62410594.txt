Title:
Cherry Bounce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
apx 4 cups pitted cherries
1/2 c sugar
1 quart vodka

Directions:
Pack the cherries and sugar in a large glass jar with a lid.  Cover with vodka.  Set aside for at least one month, occasionally turning the jar upside down and back to mix.  Alcohol content increases the longer you let it sit.

Submitter: 
Karyn Watson Woodward
Description: 
Easy cherry flavored vodka.  Perfect for Christmas gifts.  Called Cherry Bounce because after you drink it you're bouncing!
