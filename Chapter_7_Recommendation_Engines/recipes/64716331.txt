Title:
Oatmeal Raisin Coconut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups margarine/butter
2 1/2 cups brown sugar, packed
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk
3 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
dash of Mace or nutmeg
3 1/2 cups quick cooking oats
2 cups raisins
3/4 cup coconut, shredded

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees.
Cream margarine/butter well.
Add brown sugar to the creamed margarine/butter, mix well.
Add eggs to sugar/margarine mixture, one at a time mixing well after each addition.
Add milk, mix well.
In a separate bowl add the flour, baking soda, salt and mace, mix well.
In small additions (about 3/4 cup) add dry mixture to the sugar mixture mixing well after each addition.
Fold in the quick cooking oats, then the raisins and coconut
Cook about 1 tbsp full drop on the cookie sheet, cook for 10-12 minutes at 375.
