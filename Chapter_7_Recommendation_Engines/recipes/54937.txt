Title:
Cornflake Crunch Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981581.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 (10 ounce) packages TysonÂ® Individually Fresh Frozen Boneless Skinless Chicken Tenderloins
2 cups finely crushed cornflakes
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 large egg
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
2 cups hot marinara sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Line a jelly roll pan with foil; spray with nonstick cooking spray.
Combine cornflakes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper in a shallow dish. Lightly beat egg in another shallow dish.
Wash hands. Rinse ice glaze off chicken. Pat dry. Dip chicken in egg, then in cornflakes mixture. Place on pan. Wash hands
Drizzle chicken with butter. Bake 20 minutes or until done (internal temp 170 degrees F).

Submitter: 
Tyson Foods
Description: 
Cornflakes aren't just for breakfast! Here, they make a tasty, crispy coating for tender chicken strips.
