Title:
Cheesy Summer Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Medium Summer Squash
1 large vidali onion, diced
6 cooked strips of bacon
2 Cups grated cheddar cheese
bread crumbs
2 eggs
salt and pepper

Directions:
Boil your fresh Summer squash till extremely tender
while its boiling, sauté your onions in leftover bacon dippings
when your squash is tender mash in large mixing boil, with sautéed onions, grated cheese and crumbled bacon, add two eggs, beaten well, mix all ingredients together, and then add 1 1/2 Cups of bread crumbs and salt and pepper, mix together and pour in greased casserole dish. Bake at 400 for 30 minutes, until cheese is bubbling.
