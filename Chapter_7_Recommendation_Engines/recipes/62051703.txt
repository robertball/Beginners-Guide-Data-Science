Title:
Grape Jelly Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs extra lean ground beef
1 package onion soup mix
1 medium onion finely diced
2 cups grape jelly
1 bottle of chili sauce

Directions:
-mix ground beef with onion soup mix
-roll into 1 inch balls and place in a 9"x13" baking dish, bake at 400 F for 30 min until browned and cooked through.
-while meatballs are baking lightly saute onions in a pot and when done add grape jelly and chili sauce.
-add cooked meatballs to sauce and boil gently for 5 min.
-serve over rice and your favorite vegetable on the side.  Enjoy!
