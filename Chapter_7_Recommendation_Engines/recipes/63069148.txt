Title:
Savoury Tallarines with Ginger and Spring Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
10cm/4in fresh Ginger - cut into long thin strips
4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped
6 Spring Onions - finely sliced
250g/9oz Fine Egg Noodles - cooked according to packet instructions, drained and refreshed in cold water and drained again
3 tbsp Sesame Oil
2 tbsp Soy Sauce
2 tbsp Rice Wine Vinegar
Â½ Lemon - juice only - to taste
2 tbsp Sesame Seeds
4 tbsp fresh Coriander - chopped

Directions:
Heat the vegetable oil in a wok over a high heat;
When the oil is smoking, add the ginger, garlic and spring onions and stir fry for 2-3 minutes, or until softened;
Add the cooked noodles and stir fry for a further minute, or until warmed through;
Add the sesame oil, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar and lemon juice and mix well to combine;
Add the sesame seeds and stir fry for a further 1-2 minutes;
Sprinkle over the coriander and stir well to combine.

A savoury slightly piquant, packed with flavour delight from China. A great accompaniament for stir fries of all varieties.
