Title:
Steak Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups extra virgin Olive Oil
1/2 cup lime juice
2 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
4 lbs flank steak
2 cups finely chopped white onion
8 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a zip lock bag, seal bag and with hands massage beef with marinade to coat evenly. Place in fridge to marinate for 1-6 hours.
OVEN COOKED:
after beef has marinated, position the top oven rack to be 3-4 inches away from the broil element. Place an oven safe grill pan (i use cast iron) in the oven and heat for 10-15 minutes on hi broil. Once preheated, place beef onto hot grill pan and cook for 12-20 minutes (6-10 minutes each side). After beef hits an internal temp of 145 degrees F, remove from oven and let rest on cutting board for 10 minutes.
Finally, slice beef crosswise in 1/8" strips and serve
