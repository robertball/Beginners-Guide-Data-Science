Title:
Wasabi Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp wasabi paste
1 T honey
1/4 c seasoned rice vinegar
1/2 c vegetable oil
2 tsp sesame oil
1 T cilantro, chopped
salt
pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a jar and shake until combined.
