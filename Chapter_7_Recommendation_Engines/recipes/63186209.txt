Title:
Ris Bollar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/820378.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup of Butter
0.5 Cups of sugar
2 Teaspoons of vanilla sugar
1 Cup of Oats
1 Cup of puffed rice

Directions:
Beat with electric mixer, preferably butter and sugar until very fluffy. Stir in oats and rice. Shape the balls and then roll them in more puffed rice. If possible store in the refrigerator
