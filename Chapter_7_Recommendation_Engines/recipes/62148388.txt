Title:
Ceviche de Jícama (Vegetarian) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6566551.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Large Jícama
1/2 Onion
1 Hass Avacado
1 Large cucumber
2 Limes
Small bunch Cilantro
Salt & Pepper to taste (Herbal Salt optional)**

Directions:
Peel and grate Jícama
Peel and grate onion
Wash and chop cilantro
Peel and cut in small cubes hass avocado
Peel de-seed and chop cucumber
Combine all ingredients in large bowl
Squeeze Limes and pour juice over top of mixture.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Mix
Garnish with sprig of cilantro (optional)
Taste's better the longer you wait, the next day is great but everyday after it is even better.
Delicious served with tortilla chips and or topping on tacos.
* Jícama = Large root, peeled similar to a orange, but it looks NOTHING like one. Usually you can locate in specality section of produce.  Look for smooth skinned, firm ones for freshest flavor.
**Recomend Herbamare herbal salt
