Title:
Orange BBQ Sauce for ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c. molasses
2/3 c. ketchup
!/2 c. fresh-squeezed orange juice
Approx. 2 T. orange rind (zest only), diced into very small pieces
5 cloves
2 medium cloves garlic, minced
1 t. worcestershire sauce
2 T. chili sauce
1 t. mustard
1 bay leaf
1T butter
Tobasco or Tapatio sauce to taste and desired level of hotness

Directions:
Combine al ingredients in a heavy saucepan and simmer on low for approx. 1 hour.  Cook baby back ribs and brush with sauce only in the last hour.  Serve a bit of extra sauce on the side.

Submitter: 
Helen Ann Hebert
Description: 
A slightly tangy, sweet, tomato-based sauce for ribs.
