Title:
Mom's Peanut Butter Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine or butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 cup flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 cup rolled oats
1 cup chocolate chips (for topping)
1 cup powdered sugar (for glaze)
1/4 cup peanut butter (for glaze)
2-4 tbsp milk (for glaze)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine brown sugar, sugar, margarine, egg, 1/2 cup peanut butter, and vanilla.
Mix flour, salt and baking soda together.
Stir flour mixture into sugar mixture slowly.
Stir rolled oats into mixture slowly.
Layer mixture into 9x13 pan and bake for 20-30 minutes.
Once browned, remove from oven and top with chocolate chips.
Allow chips to melt, then spread along top.
Then allow time to cool and chocolate on top to harden.
For glaze, mix powdered sugar, 1/4 c peanut butter, and 2-4 tbsp milk.
Spread glaze on top of chocolate topping.
