Title:
Savory Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2tbsp Olive Oil
1 small onion (1/4 cup)
1 cup uncooked regular long grain brown rice
2.5 cups chicken broth
1/4 tsp salt
shake of turmeric
carrot shavings
handful of spinach

Directions:
Add oil and onion to saucepan over medium heat. Cook onion in margarine about 3 min, stirring occasionally, until onion is tender.
Stir in the rice. Cook 5 minutes, stirring frequently. Stir in broth and salt.
Heat to boiling, stirring once or twice; reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 35min without stirring. Add carrots and cook 5 more minutes. Add Spinach; Cover and let steam about 5 minutes.

Jessica Schultz

Delicious, creamy and simple rice dish.
