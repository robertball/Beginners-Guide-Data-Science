Title:
Charlies on the Lake Seafood Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp butter
1 tsp red onion chopped
1 tsp prepared salsa
1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp oregano
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup green onions, chopped
5 oz salad shrimp (about 70)
5 oz bay scallops
2 Tbsp flour
2 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp sour cream
2 Tbsp heavy cream
1 cup Monterey Jack cheese
1 cup prepared Alfredo sauce
1/2 cup Monterey Jack cheese
10 tortilla shells
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup salsa
1 avocado sliced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a small saucepan, combine 1 tsp butter, red onion, salsa, red pepper, oregano, garlic and green onions.  Saute until onions are tender.
Dust the seafood with flour.
In a separate pan, saute seafood in 2 Tbsp butter until 80% cooked.
Add onion mixture to seafood and allow to cool.
Add sour cream, heavy cream and 1 cup Monterey Jack cheese to seafood mixture.  Chill in the refrigerator.
To assemble the enchiladas, roll 2 oz of the seafood mixture into each soft tortilla shell.  Place in the baking dish, seam-side down.  Cover with Alfredo sauce and additional cheese.
Bake for 15 minutes.
Serve with sour cream, salsa and avocado.
