Title:
Tofu Fruit Julius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/3348177.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package silken tofu, cut into cubes, or to taste
2 cups fresh strawberries
2 cups soy milk
4 cups crushed ice

Directions:
Layer tofu, strawberries, and soy milk in a blender; blend. Add ice cubes, 1 cup at a time, while blender is running; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Katheyjo
Description: 
Silken tofu cubes blended with various fruits to create a delicious sweet summer drink. You can add more fresh or frozen fruit, if desired.
