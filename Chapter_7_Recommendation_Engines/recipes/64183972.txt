Title:
Sweet Potato Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1510511.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 Cups Bisquick
3 tbsp Brown Sugar
2 1/2 tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice
2 tbsp oil
2 tbsp white vinegar
1 3/4 Cups Milk
1 Cup Mashed canned Sweet Potatoes or Yams

Directions:
1. Mix together all dry ingredients, then gradually add wet ingredients and mix until somewhat smooth.  (There maybe small lumps of sweet potato in the batter.  This is fine,
2. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat. Pour approximately a 1/4 cup of batter onto the hot pan for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve.

Submitter: 
ReynnaRose
Description: 
My family wanted pumpkin pancakes for breakfast.  I was all out of pumpkin, but I DID have a can of yams and this recipe was born. It's a perfect fall breakfast choice!
