Title:
Leche asada (baked milk) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
2 cups milk
1/3 cup white sugar
1 cinnamon stick
½ cup white sugar

Directions:
1.- Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2.- Melt in a saucepan ½ cup of white sugar without stirring. When sugar is melted put it in a 8” x 8” pan.
3.- Mix eggs, milk, 1/3 cup of white sugar, and cinnamon in a bowl. Beat until well mixed (try to don’t make spume).
4.- Pour mixture into melted sugar pan.
5.- Bake for about 30 to 35 minutes, or until toothpick inserted come out clean.
