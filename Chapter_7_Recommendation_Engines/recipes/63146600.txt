Title:
SEASONED BAKED CHICKEN with SOUR CREAM SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 Boneless skinned breast/thigh chicken
½ pkg. Herb Seasoned Pepperidge Farm  Stuffing,finely crushed
½ cup dried minced onion
½ cup dried parsley
8 oz sour cream
2 cans Cream Mushroom Soup
½ can milk (pour into emptied can)

Directions:
Mix stuffing, dried onions & parsley. Put in pie plate. Set aside.
Mix 1 can Cream of Mushroom soup and 1/2 can milk.  Dip Chicken breast/thighs into mushroom mixture and coat in crumb mixture.  Lay in 9x13 buttered glass pan.
Bake @ 375 for 45 to 50 min.
Take remaining mushroom mixture, add 2nd can cream of mushroom soup and mix with carton of Sour Cream.  Heat on low on top of stove.
Top Chicken with sour cream sauce.

Submitter: 
Vicki Wilson
Description: 
My family loves this chicken dish.  This sauce is very rich. I usually serve this with whipped potatoes and asparagas. This smells so heavenly, everyone will think you have been in the kitchen cooking all day.
