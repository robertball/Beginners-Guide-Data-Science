Title:
Nutella Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Nutella
1 cup flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350.
2. Mix all ingredients together well.
3. Roll into 1 " balls and place on a cookie sheet. Press flat with the bottom of a glass.
4. Bake for 7-8 minutes.
