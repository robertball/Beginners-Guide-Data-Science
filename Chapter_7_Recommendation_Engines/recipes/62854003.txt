Title:
Tarragon Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 13-ounce can chicken breast
1 small onion, diced
2 ribs celery, diced
1 teaspoon dried tarragon
1/2 cup mayonnaise (more or less...to your liking)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain the chicken breast pieces well and chop or shred into small pieces.
Mix all ingredients together and store in the refrigerator to combine flavors; at least 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Judy in Delaware
Description: 
This chicken salad is great as a sandwich, as a wrap, or on crackers.  The tarragon is a perfect pairing with chicken, and the flavors meld wonderfully.  Best if made a few hours in advance to really combine the flavors.
