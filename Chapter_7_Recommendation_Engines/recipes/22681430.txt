Title:
Vegetarian Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. large tortilla shells
8oz. sour cream
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese
4 oz. minced black olives
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 can green chilis
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese

Directions:
Mix ingredients, spread on tortilla shells, roll up, refrigerate for a couple of hours, cut into bite size pieces and serve.
