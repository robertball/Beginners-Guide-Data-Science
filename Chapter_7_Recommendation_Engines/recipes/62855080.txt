Title:
Never fail Noodle recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Eggs,
1 Tbs Oil
1 1/4 Cups Flour,
Salt to taste

Directions:
Crack 2 Eggs in Food Processor, Oil and Salt, Mix for 5 seconds add Flour mix until a ball forms. Need ball on a floured surface, let rest 30 min. make noodles

Submitter: 
Rick Falsetta
Description: 
A never fail noodle recipe I learned from Maryann Esposito, that is made in your food processor
