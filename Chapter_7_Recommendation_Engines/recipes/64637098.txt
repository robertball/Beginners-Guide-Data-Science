Title:
Fried Plantains and Sausages Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon virgin coconut oil
2 yellowish-green plantain, sliced
2 organic chicken sausage, sliced
raw sauerkraut

Directions:
1) Heat oil over medium heat.
2) Add plantains and saute both sides for 5 mins total
3) Push plantains to the side and add the sausage cook for 2 mins
4) Mix together and serve with sauerkraut
