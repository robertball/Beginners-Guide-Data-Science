Title:
Philly Cheesesteak Dog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 hotdogs of your choice
8 hotdog buns or club kaiser rolls
1/2 lb shaved ribeye chopped
8oz Cheese Whiz
1/2 large diced onion
1 TB olive oil

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Toss in onions and saute until tender.
Remove onions place to the side.
Cut chip steak into pieces and place in the skillet. Cook until meat is no longer red, and all liquid is cooked away. Stir frequently, not allowing the meat to burn.
Add onions back to the skillet and mix thoroughly.
Cook or BBQ hotdogs per package directions.
Place Hotdogs on buns and top with steak,onion and cheese mixture.

Hot Dogs topped with a Philly Cheesesteak what could be better. Perfect for your 4th of July cookout!
