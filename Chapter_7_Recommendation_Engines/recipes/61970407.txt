Title:
Hawaiian Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large cream cheese
1 C chopped macadamia nuts
2 T Lowrey's season salt
12 oz can crushed pineapple, drained well
2 T finely chopped onion
1/2 C green bell pepper
finely chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and refrigerate overnight. ROll into ball and roll in chopped pecans until covered. Makes one large ball or two small ones. Freezes well.
