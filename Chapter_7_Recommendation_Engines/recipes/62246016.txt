Title:
Chocolate Fudge Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/795581.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Box Devil's Food Cake Mix
4 eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup warm water
3.5 oz instant vanilla pudding
1 cup chocolate chips (6 oz)
8 oz plain yogurt
GLAZE:
2 T cocoa
2 T water
1 T oil
2 T corn syrup
1 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Combine cake mix, eggs, yogurt, water, oil, and dry pudding mix in bowl.
Beat on high for 5" (batter will be very thick);
Fold in chips.
Bake at 350 in lightly greased bundt pan for 1 hour
Cool for 10";  invert and turn out
Glaze;  Heat cocoa, oil, water and corn syrup over low heat until well mixed.  Add sugar and drizzle over warm cake.

Description: 
Ooeeyy Gooeey - this is Jonathon's favorite cake.  Dense, rich, and deeply chocolaty. Have substituted sour cream for yogurt with no problem - also good with adding peanut butter chips or vanilla chips as well.  Yummmmm...
