Title:
Asparagus and Pea Salad with Tomato and Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 teaspoon salt
plus more to salt water
1/4 pounds asparagus
2 cup(s) peas, snow
1 cup(s) tomato(es), cherry
1/2 teaspoon basil, fresh
peppercorns, black freshly ground, to taste

Directions:
Put a medium pot with 3 cups salted water on to boil.
Immerse the asparagus in the boiling water for 2-4 minutes or until bright green with a tender crunch. Remove the asparagus (keep the water) and cool immediately under cold water.
Repeat the process with the snow peas. Toss the tomatoes and basil together with the peas and asparagus, salt and pepper.
Serve immediately or chill for up to 15 minutes, tossing again before serving.
