Title:
Buffet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 1 1/2 lb bag shredded frozen hashbrowns
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 pint sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 stick of butter (melted)
10 oz. cheddar cheese (grated)

Directions:
Thaw potatoes slightly.  Combine all ingredients and turn into 9X13 pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
