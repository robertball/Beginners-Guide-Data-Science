Title:
Mom's Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lb. Hamburger
1 Egg Beaten
1/2 C. Milk
1/2 C. Bread Crumbs
6 Tbs. Onion (diced)
2 Tbs. Sugar
1 1/4 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Nutmeg
1/4 tsp. Allspice
2 Tbs. Butter
Gravy:
3 Tbs. Flour
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/4 tsp. Pepper
2 C. Milk

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and shape into 1" balls. Brown in 2 Tbs. Butter. When cooked, remove into a casserole dish. Prepare Gravy by adding first 3 ingredients to fat in skillet. Heat until it bubbles. Stir in 2 Cups of Milk and cook until it thickens. Do not boil. Pour over meatballs - Keep warm

This is a recipe that my mother used to make that I FINALLY found. I have tried other recipes and none have tasted like hers. ;)
