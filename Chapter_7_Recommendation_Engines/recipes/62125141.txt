Title:
Eggplant Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant, sliced into 1 inch thick rounds
1/2 C sundried tomatoes (not oil packed)
1/2 C water
1 egg
1/8 C water
2 C bread crumbs
1 C goat cheese
1 C mozzarella cheese
1/2 large onion, sliced
1 T molasses
1 tomato, sliced
3 leaves of fresh basil, chopped
oil
2 T butter

Directions:
Soak 1/2 C sundried tomatoes in 1/2 C water for 1 hour.
Heat oven to 350.
Slice Eggplant into rounds.
Lightly oil a baking sheet.
Whisk egg and 1/8 C water and dip eggplant rounds into mixture, then into bread crumbs. Place on baking sheet.
Bake eggplant rounds in oven for 10 minutes, flip and bake another 10 minutes. Remove from oven.
While eggplant is cooking, cook sliced onions in frying pan in butter on low until golden. Stir in Molasses and remove from heat.
After sundried tomatoes have soaked, process in food processor until desired texture for sauce.
Spread tomato sauce on eggplant rounds and sprinkle with chopped basil.
Top pizzas with goat cheese, mozzarella cheese, tomato slices and caramelized onions.
Bake in oven for 10 - 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
MUTHERMCREE
Description: 
A delicious vegetarian substitute for pizza, using Eggplant as the crust and a delicious sundried tomato sauce. The topping choices are endless! Serve with a side salad.
