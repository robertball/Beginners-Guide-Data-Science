Title:
Kat's Hearty Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lb beef stew meat, cubed
1/4cp all purpose flour
1t garlic powder
1t onion powder
1/2t pepper
3T olive oil
2cps. red wine (I use Merlot)
2cps. chicken broth
1T ketchup
1T A1 steak sauce
1T worcestershire sauce
1t dried rosemary
1t dried parsley
1/2t ground black pepper
4-5 large potatoes, peeled & cubed
4-5 carrots peeled and cut into 1" pieces
1 large onion, chopped
4 celery stalks, cut into 1" pieces
2t cornstarch
2t cold water

Directions:
1. Toss beef cubes in garlic powder, onion powder, pepper and flour.
2. In a dutch oven or large pot, cook beef in oil over medium heat until browned.
3. Add wine and  broth. Stir frequently and bring to a boil.
4. Stir in ketchup, A1, Worcestershire, rosemary, parsley and pepper.
5. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 1 hour.
6. Stir potatoes, carrots, onion and celery into the pot.
7. Dissolve cornstarch in cold water and stir into stew.
8. Cover and simmer 1 additional hour.

Submitter: 
Katzmeow
Description: 
A delicious and hearty stew, perfect for those cold, winter nights. Serve with some crusty french bread.
