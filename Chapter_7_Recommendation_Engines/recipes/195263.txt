Title:
Mama's Easy Awesome Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993241.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 large onion, thinly sliced
1 (24 ounce) jar RAGÚ® Old World Style® Traditional Sauce
1 pound spaghetti, cooked and drained

Directions:
Brown ground beef with onion in 12-inch nonstick skillet; drain, if desired.
Stir in sauce and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer covered, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes. Serve, if desired, over your favorite hot pasta.

Submitter: 
Ragu®
Description: 
Ground beef and onion are simmered with robustly flavored sauce for a delicious, cooked-all-day flavor.
