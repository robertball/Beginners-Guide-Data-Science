Title:
Poppy Seed Dressing II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1928331.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon mustard powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup cider vinegar
1 onion, grated, juice reserved
1 cup vegetable oil
1 1/2 tablespoons poppy seeds

Directions:
Whisk together the sugar, mustard, salt, vinegar, onion juice, oil and poppy seeds. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Cathy
Description: 
This dressing is wonderful over fresh fruit or shredded cabbage and halved grapes.
