Title:
Mexican Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp finely chopped fresh cilantro leaves
2 tsp finely chopped yellow onions
2 tsp minced jalapeno or Serrano chilies, seeds and membranes removed, if desired
course salt
2 large ripe Avocados, peeled and seeded
2 Tbsp cored, seeded and finely chopped plum tomato
2 tsp freshly squeezed lime juice

Directions:
Mash together 1 Tbsp of the silantro, 1 tsp of the onion, 1 tsp of the chile, and 1/2 tsp of salt in the bottom of a bowl.
Add the Avocadoes and gently mash wtih a fork until chunky-smooth. Fold the remaining cilantro, onion, and chile into the mixture.
Stir in the tomato and lime juice, adjust the seasonings to taste and serve with a basket of warm corn tortilla chips.
