Title:
Linda's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 to 1 1/3 pounds Ground Beef
2 cloves Garlic
1/2 cup Onion, chopped
2 1-pound can Whole tomatoes
2 8-ounce cans Tomato Sauce
3 ounce can Mushrooms, drained
1/4 cup Dried Parsley
1/2 teaspoons Oregano
1 teaspoon Salt
1/2 teaspoon MSG
1/4 teaspoon Thyme
1 Bay leaf
1 cup Water

Directions:
Brown ground beef and drain.
Add all ingredients, sprinkling the dried parsley over the top of the sauce.
Simmer uncovered for 2 to 2 1/2 hours.

Dale Tadlock

Granny's famous spaghetti sauce!
