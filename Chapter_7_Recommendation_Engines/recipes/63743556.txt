Title:
Banana Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 cups milk
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp butter
2 tbsp cornstarch
pinch salt
2-3 sliced bananas
1 pie shell

Directions:
Heat milk....do not boil
Mix all ingredients except butter
Add ingredients when milk starts to form film
Keep stirring
Add butter when mixture thickens
Line pie shells with the bananas and pour thickened mixture on top
Let cool....refrigerate and then serve with whipped cream
