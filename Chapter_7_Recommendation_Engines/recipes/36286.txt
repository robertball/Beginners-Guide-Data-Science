Title:
Old Bay® Catfish Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985336.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup cornmeal
1 tablespoon Old Bay® Seasoning
1 teaspoon McCormick® Parsley Flakes
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Garlic Salt
4 (4 ounce) fillets catfish fillets
1 egg, beaten
3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions:
Place cornmeal on large piece of wax paper.  Add OLD BAY(R) seasoning, parsley and garlic salt.  Stir with fork until well combined.
Dip fish fillets, 1 at a time, in beaten egg.  Allow excess egg to drip off.  Coat with cornmeal mixture.
Heat oil in large skillet.  Panfry fillets 5 to 6 minutes on each side, or until fish flakes easily with fork.

Submitter: 
Old Bay®
Description: 
Spice up your next fish fry with OLD BAY® Seasoning.
