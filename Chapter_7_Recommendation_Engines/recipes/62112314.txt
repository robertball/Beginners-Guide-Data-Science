Title:
Raspberrry Cinnamon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg. pkg raspberry gelatin mix
4 C.water
1/2 pkg Cinnamon Candies (Red Hots) 1/2 + C.
1 lg. can crushed pineapple, well drained
3 medium apples, diced small
3/4 C. celery, diced small (optional)

Directions:
Boil cinnamon candies in 2 cups water until candies are dissolved.
Add jello and stir to dissolve.
Add 2 Cups cold water and stir well and pour into a oblong pan or mold and chill until partially set.
Fold in  apples, pineapple and celery, if using and chill until firm.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Uses Red Hots for the cinnamon flavor.
