Title:
marshmallow squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 cup semi sweet and caramel chips (1/2 and 1/2)
Bag of colored minimarshmallows

Directions:
Melt butter with peanutbutter in a large saucepan.
Stir in chips until melted.
Cool so that ou can hold your hand on the bottom of the pan.
Add marshmallows and stir until all are coated.
Pack in 9 x 9 pan
Refrigerate
