Title:
Apple Pie Moonshine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon apple juice
1 gallon apple cider
4-8 cinnamon sticks (personal preference)
2 cups white sugar
2 cups brown sugar
dash of apple pie seasonings
1 750ML bottle of 190 proof grain alcohol
1 750ML bottle of vodka

Directions:
combine all ingredients except the alcohol in a large pot and heat just to a slight boil. cover, reduce heat, and simmer for about an hour. remove from heat and let cool completely.
add the alcohol and then transfer finished product into jars, bottles, or whatever container you see fit.
longer it sits in the fridge the better it seems to get.

Submitter: 
STASH
Description: 
Great tasting apple pie in a jar but it does pack quite a kick if your not careful.
