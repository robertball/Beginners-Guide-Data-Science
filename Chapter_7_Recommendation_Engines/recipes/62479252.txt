Title:
Kellie's Healthy Shrimp Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/491498.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large lettuce leaves, chopped
4 cups spinach, chopped
4 roma tomatoes, chopped
1 large cucumber, chopped
1 pound cooked shrimp
2 avocados, pitted, peeled and diced
4 large hard boiled eggs, thinly sliced
fat free thousand island dressing

Directions:
Combine lettuce, spinach, tomatoes, and cucumber in a large bowl. Toss. (If making ahead, cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.)
Divide salad among 4 bowls. Top with shrimp, avocado, and egg slices. Drizzle dressing over salad and enjoy!
