Title:
Cranberry Butternut Squash a Yum! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4899180.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken stock
1 small to med butternut squash (peeled and cubed in small bite sized)
1/2  sliced red onion (small to medium)
4 tablespoons dried cranberries
Zest of one small orange
2 tablespoons margarine
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1 Tablespoon brown sugar
2 Tablespoons sliced almonds
1 Tablespoon  shredded Parmesan cheese to taste
(recommended)
Salt and pepper to taste (i do like a little more pepper)

Directions:
bring chicken stock, butternut squash cubes, red onion, and cranberries to boil in saucepan, reduce to low and simmer until squash tender approximately 15-20 min. Drain but reserve at least 1/4  cup stock.
Heat margarine and olive oil and  1/4 remainder stock in large skillet over medium heat. Stir in butternut squash mixture in to butter and oil and remainder stock, coat the sprinkle with brown sugar.
Coat and gently flip veges when browned, approximate 5 -8 minutes per side.
Atop with shredded almonds and Parmesan chesse
