Title:
Croth's Lemon Curd (in the microwave) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/384944.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg, large
1/3 cup sugar
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
zest of 2 lemons
1 tbl butter, unsalted

Directions:
In a small bowl beat together egg and sugar.
In a (2 cup measure) Pyrex glass combine egg-sugar mixture and lemon juice. Beat until smooth.
Stir in the zest.
Add butter.
Microwave on high for 30 secs, stire until smooth.  Contine to heat at 30 second intervals until curd is bubbling, about 5-6 times.
Reduce to 15 second intervals and heat 3 times, stirring until smooth after each interval. When curd can coat the back of spoon it is finished.
Refrigerate until thoroughly cooled, it will thichen upon cooling. Makes 1 cup

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
This a tart, refreshing curd that is made in the microwave. Very simple and makes the perfect amount of lemon curd for a batch of fresh scones or to add to a pie, 1 cup.
