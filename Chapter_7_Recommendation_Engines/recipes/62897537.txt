Title:
Campbell's Kitchen Broccoli and Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 onions chopped
6 ribs celery chopped
2 (10.75 ounce)cans Campbell's Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/2 cup milk
2  tablespoons yellow mustard
3(16 ounce) package frozen broccoli flowerets, thawed
3 cup shredded Cheddar cheese or cubed American Cheese
1 cup dry bread crumbs
2 teaspoons butter, melted

Directions:
Saute onions and celery in small amount of oil.
Stir in broccoli cook until tender add the soup, milk, mustard, broccoli and cheese in a 1/2 hotel pan.
Add ________ cups cooked rice _____________ minutes.
Mix the bread crumbs with the butter in a small bowl and sprinkle over the broccoli mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes or until hot.
