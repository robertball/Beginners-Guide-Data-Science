Title:
Toffee Turtle Cheesecake Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 personal sized chocolate covered toffee candy bar
16 ounces cream cheese, softened
2 eggs, beaten
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
3/4 cup of sugar
27 chocolate sandwhich cookies (I.E. Oreo)
1/2 cup melted butter
9 teaspooons premade chocolate fudge sauce, heated.
9 teaspoons premade caramel sundae sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Place cookies in a bag, and pound with meat pounder to crush. Transfer to a mixing bowl, and pour in melted butter. Mix until well blended.
Place 18 baking cups into cupcakes pans.
Use cookie mixture to form a crust on the bottom of each cup, pressing down firmly.
In a large bowl, beat together cream cheese, eggs, vanilla, and sugar. Pour batter into each cup.
Bake for 12-15 minutes.
Cool for 1-2 hours, then top with chopped toffee and drizzle with chocolate and caramel sauce.

Submitter: 
CUPYCAKESMURF
Description: 
These perfectly sized mini cheesecakes are laid on a chocolate cookie crust and topped with toffee chips and then drizzled with chocolate and caramel sauce.
