Title:
Creamy Cheesey Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
1/2 cup vegetable broth
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon flour
1/3 cup heavy cream
1 teaspoon granulated sugar
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon white pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 cup Parmesean cheese(optional)

Directions:
Divide head of cauliflower into florets.
Steam cauliflower pieces over boiling water for 15 to 20 minutes or until cauliflower is tender.
Drain the cauliflower.
When the cauliflower has cooled, put the florets in a food processor along with broth.
Puree the cauliflower on high speed until smooth, but with some very small pieces of cauliflower remaining for just a bit of texture.In a saucepan, make a roux(see Blonde Roux recipes) of flour and butter,cooking approx. 3-4 mins.
Add the cream, sugar and remaining spices and stir.Add pureed cauliflower.
Set the saucepan over medium heat and cook, sirring often for 5 to 10 minutes or until thick. Place into lightly buttered casserole dish and sprinkle with parmesean cheese. Bake at 400 F. for 10-15 mins.

Submitter: 
RockingItCountryStyle
Description: 
A delicious Creamy Cauliflower dish. For quick version serve without final step of baking! Also  wonderful with a combo of cauliflower and broccoli-just steam and mash broccoli and cauliflower,leaving both veggies with more rustic texture!!
