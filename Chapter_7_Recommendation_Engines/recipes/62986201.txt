Title:
Soul Style Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sugar
6 medium size sweet potatoes; peeled & cut into 1/4-inch slices
1/2 water
1 stick butter

Directions:
Rinse the potato slices in ample water and drain. Place in a 2-quart covered saucepan and add the water, sugar, and butter. Do not mix or stir. Cover and simmer until very tender. Do not stir during the cooking process.

Submitter: 
Karen Waters
Description: 
I have been making these for years. My family loves them. I cut the sugar by one cup from the original recipe. These are the most delicious sweet potatoes you can imagine. The dish makes its own syrup and it will be a hit with your family year-round.
