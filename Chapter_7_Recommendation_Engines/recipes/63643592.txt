Title:
7 up Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups Bisquick plus 1 Tbs. for cutting surface
1 cup sour cream
1 cup 7-up
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Mix bisquick, sour cream and 7 up. Roll out on floured surface and cut with cookie cutter.
Melt butter in bottom of cookie sheet pan, and put shaped biscuits in, then Bake at 425 until golden
