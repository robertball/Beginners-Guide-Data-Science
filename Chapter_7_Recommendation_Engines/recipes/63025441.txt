Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookie Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 uncooked 9 inch graham cracker crust or deep dish pie crust
2 large eggs
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup butter softened
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup chopped nuts
Ice cream recommended

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
beat eggs for about one minute then add flour and sugars.  beat well.   Add butter, beat, then stir in chocolate chips and nuts
spoon mixture into unbaked pie crust
Cool on wire rack completely top with ice cream.

Submitter: 
Nannette Turner
Description: 
Bake pie in oven on cookie sheet for 55 minutes
