Title:
Quick Taco Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Boxes Kraft Mac and Cheese
1 lb. Ground beef
1 onion (chopped)
2 bell peppers (chopped)
1 clove garlic (minced)
1 pack Taco seasoning
1 bag shredded lettuce
1 jar salsa
Fiesta cheese 8 oz
1 container sour cream (optional)
2 Avacados (optional)
1 pkg. Guacamole seasoning (optional)

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown combine ground beef, onions, peppers, and garlic. When fully cooked, add taco seasoning mix, and following the directions on the back of the package.
In a pot, cook macaroni, setting the cheese packets aside for later.  When fully cooked, add to the hamburger mix. Add the cheese packets, stirring thoroughly.
Serve: In a bowl, place lettuce, then taco mac and cheese, sprinkle with fiesta cheese, top with salsa. If you wish to add guacamole and sour cream, see optional instructions below.
Optional: we top ours with guacamole and sour cream. For the guacamole, follow the instructions on the back of the guacamole seasoning package.

Description: 
Need something quick and easy? Family can't agree on Tacos or Mac and cheese? This recipe is great combo.
