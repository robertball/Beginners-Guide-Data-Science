Title:
Semi's Chilli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1476105.jpg
Ingredients:
2	pounds ground beef
1 	rib celery, thinly sliced
1 ½	cups chopped yellow onion
¾	cup diced green bell pepper
2	to 3 cloves minced garlic
1	teaspoon garlic powder
2	teaspoons minced pickled jalapenos or fresh jalapenos
2	teaspoons liquid from jar of pickled jalapenos
1	(46oz.) bottle of low sodium V8 juice
1	(10.75oz.) can of tomato puree
2	cans dark kidney beans, rinsed and drained
2	cans great northern beans, rinsed and drained
2 	cans diced tomatoes with juices
1	teaspoon salt
¼	teaspoon black pepper
¾	teaspoon sugar
1 ½	teaspoon Pasilla Chile powder
¼	cup Ancho chile powder
3	teaspoons cumin
1 ½	teaspoons dried oregano

Directions:
Brown beef and crumble in a large stock pot.  Once browned remove as much grease as possible.
In same pot with the beef; add all other ingredients.  Stir and bring to a boil, turn down; and simmer for 2 hours stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
this is a full bodied Rich chilli that will warm you on those Cold Winter Nights!
