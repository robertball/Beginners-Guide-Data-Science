Title:
Crack chicken bubble up Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2506949.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked, chopped chicken
1 (10.75) oz can cream of chicken soup
8 oz sour cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1.5Tablespoons ranch dressing mix
1/4 cup cooked, chopped bacon
12 oz grands jr biscuits

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°
Lightly spray 9x13 baking dish and set aside.
Combine chicken, soup, cheese, ranch mix and bacon. Toss to combine.
Cut each biscuit into 4 and toss in chicken mixture to combine.
Pour mixture in prepared baking dish.
Bake 25-30 minutes or until bubbly and biscuits are golden brown.
