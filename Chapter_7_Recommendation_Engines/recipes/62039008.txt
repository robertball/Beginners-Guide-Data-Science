Title:
Pistachio Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
60 plain Ritz crackers, crumbled
1/4 lb. margarine, melted..... Mix these together and press into large baking dish and bake at 350 for 10 min.
Mix together and beat well:
2 pkgs. Pistachio pudding (6 oz.)
1 1/2 cups milk
Add 1 litre of soft vanilla icecreamtopudding mixture and spread over bottom. Top with 1 large Kool Whip and then grate Aero chocolate bars over the top and refrigerate. Will keep for a week in the fridge. Good frozen also.

Directions:
All in the above.
