Title:
Pasta with Mizithra Cheese, Pancetta and Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound spaghetti
1/4 lb pancetta, sliced into matchsticks
1 cup butter
3/4 pound Mizithra cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta in large pot of salted, boiling water until al dente. Drain.
Brown pancetta in a medium skillet; drain well.
When pasta is almost done, start slowly melting butter over medium heat until golden.
Skim butter solids off top.
Toss pasta with pancetta and then the melted butter.
Sprinkle with cheese and toss again.
