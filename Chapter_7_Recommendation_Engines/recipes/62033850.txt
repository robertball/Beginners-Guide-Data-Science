Title:
Croned Beef Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked corned beef, chopped fine
1 cup cooked, peeled,& Chopped potatoes
1 Tb instant minced onion, or 1/2 cup cooked and chopped onion
1/4 cup left over stock from corned beef, or beef stock
Pepper, to taste
Butter or margarine

Directions:
Heat heavy skillet,Prefer cast iron, add butter,
Mix all the vegies and stock, add to pan , and cook over med. heat.  Season to taste.
You can add, turnip & cabbage that has been cooked, also.  Add 1 cup of beets, or 1 can of sliced beets, with liquid, to make red flannel hash
Heat and serve

Submitter: 
Rosemary
Description: 
Save and use all your leftover vegies from your dinner,even the cabbage.  To make it Red Flannel Hash add 1 cup of cooked beets.
