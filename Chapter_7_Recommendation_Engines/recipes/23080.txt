Title:
Strawberry Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/962637.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen sweetened strawberries
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle caffeinated citrus-flavored soda
3 cups ice

Directions:
In a blender half full of ice, combine strawberries and citrus soda. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
jpd222nc
Description: 
An ambrosial blend of ice strawberries and citrus soda. Excellent on a hot summer day. It could also be made with rum if desired.
