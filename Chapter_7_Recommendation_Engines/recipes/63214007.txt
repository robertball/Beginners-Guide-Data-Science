Title:
Tripe - less Menudo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30oz can white hominy - add liquid too
(yellow hominy will work too)
4 cubes beef boullion
4 cups water
1/2 a large white onion, minced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp fresh basil, minced (bit more if using dried)
1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/4 tsp red chile pepper flakes
Salt to taste - wait 'till boullion dissolves though.
Garnish Options
1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
Dashes of chile pepper flakes
Squeezed lemon or lime juice

Directions:
In a medium sized pot over medium high heat, begin adding all the ingreadients in the order listed except for salt & garnish items.
Stir together,  and once its come to a low boil reduce heat to a simmer and cover - continue cooking for about 20 - 30 minutes, stirring a couple of times.  Don't overcook or hominy becomes mush.
Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro, red pepper flakes and a squeeze of lemon or lime juice.
