Title:
AWESOME PUMPKIN ROLL Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C All-Purpose Flour
1 Tsp Baking Powder
1 Tsp Baking Soda
2 Tsp Cinnamon
1/2 tsp Nutmeg
1/2 Tsp Salt
3 Eggs
1 C Sugar
2/3 C Canned Pumpkin
1 Tbsp Lemon Juice
1 C Walnuts or Pecans, finely chopped
Filling
1 C Powdered Sugar
1 8 oz Cream Cheese, softened
6 Tbsp Butter
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 F. Grease the jelly roll pan and line with parchment or wax paper and grease that also.
Mix dry ingredients together.
In a large bowl beat eggs on high for 2 minutes. Beat in sugar. Stir in the pumpkin puree and lemon juice.
Fold in the dry ingredients. Then add the nuts.
Spread in pan evenly and bake for 15 minutes.
Sprinkle a kitchen towel with enough powdered sugar to cover the towel. Turn out the roll onto towel immediately after it is finished baking. Roll up the pumpkin roll by rolling the towel inside cake and place seam side down to cool in the refridgerator.
Filling
Beat all of the filling ingredients until smooth. Unroll the cooled roll and spread the cream cheese filling over the roll. Roll up again with the  with plastic wrap and return to refidgerator until time to serve.

Description: 
This is the most wonderful pumpkin roll I have ever eaten! It is rich, moist and very addictive. I have gotten many compliments and request for this recipe. It is a great substitute for the traditional pumpkin pie at Thanksgiving.
