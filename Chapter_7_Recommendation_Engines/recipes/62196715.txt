Title:
Dad's Double Whole Grain Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 1/3 cups dry milk powder
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups whole wheat flour
3/4 cup white sugar
4 eggs, lightly beaten
3 cups water
1/4 cup butter, melted
or 1/3 cup applesauce
3 tablespoons vinegar
Option: blueberries, 1/2 tsp cinnamon, pureed squash, 1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In a large bowl, sift all-purpose flour, milk powder, baking powder, baking soda and salt, cinnamon. Stir in whole wheat flour. In a small bowl, combine sugar, eggs, water, butter (or applesauce) and vinegar, (vanilla), (squash). Make a well in the flour mixture, and pour in the egg mixture. Fold in until smooth (don't stir).
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Cook until pancakes are golden brown on both sides; serve hot.
