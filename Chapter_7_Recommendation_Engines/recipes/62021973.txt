Title:
Brownie Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup white sugar
3 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup flour
1 cup walnuts or pecans
Glaze
1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon light corn syrup
1 tsp. vanilla
Slice strawberries for garnish (optional)
Butter and flour a 9 inch round cake pan. In a pan heat butter and corn syrup until butter is melted.Stir in chocolate chips until melted. Add sugar and eggs, stir well until blended. Stir in vanilla , flour and nuts.
Pour into pan..bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until center springs when touched.
Cool in pan 10 minutes. Then place on a cooling rack.
Glaze...Combine chocolate chips, butter and corn syrup in pan. Stir over low heat till chocalate chips are melted. Remove from heat, stir in vanilla.
Frost tops and sides. Chill until set.
Garnish with sliced strawberries.
This torte is delicious and very rich...:)
