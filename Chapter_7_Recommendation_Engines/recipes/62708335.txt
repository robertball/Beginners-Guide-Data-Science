Title:
Zucchini Tea Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614277.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded zucchini
2 egg whites slightly beaten
½ cup grandulated sugar
1/ cup milk
¼ cup vegetable oil
1 ½ cups all purpose flour
½ cup whole wheat flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp ground cinnamon
¼ tsp baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
Place zucchini in colander drain for 15 minutes. Press with double layer of paper towels to squeeze out excess liquid.
Place zucchini in a bowl. Add egg whites, sugar, milk, and oil. Mix well.
Prepare another bowl combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon and baking powder, mix well.
Add dry ingredients to zucchini mixture and stir until dry ingredients are moistened.
Pour batter into prepared pans.
Bake 30-35 minutes or until the tops are golden and toothpick inserted comes out clean
