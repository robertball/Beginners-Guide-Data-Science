Title:
Candy Apple Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz vodka
1 oz apple schnapps
splash of ameretto
splash of grenadine
cinnamon-sugar for garnish

Directions:
Combine alcohol and grenadine over ice.  Serve shaken, not stirred.  Garnish glass with cinnimon-sugar if desired.

Submitter: 
MHIGHFILL
Description: 
A fun martini for apple schnapps lovers.  It may remind you of those old carnival treats!
