Title:
Cajun Seafood Shells n Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4480523.jpg
Ingredients:
1C Small Shells or Orecchiette pasta, dry
3Tbsp butter
1Tbsp flour
1 C Milk
3 slices deli style American Cheese
3 slices smoked Gouda or smoked white cheddar
1 wedge white cheddar spread (or1Tbsp cream cheese)
2 Tbsp grated asiago cheese
2 dashes each-White, black, and Cayenne pepper
3 dashes Cajun Seasoning
3 dashes Creole Seasoning
1 tsp smoked Paprika
1 tsp good quality garlic powder, or one clove minced fresh garlic
1 C Imitation Crab, chopped
1/2 C boiled shrimp, chopped

Directions:
Boil pasta till al dente, set aside.
Melt butter in pan over med heat.
Stir in flour and let cook while stirring, one minute.
Add in milk
Tear sliced cheese, add, stirring well
Blend in cheese spread/cream cheese.
Add spices, to taste.
Add in seafood and let cook, stirring well, 2 minutes.
Stir in the cooked pasta.
ENJOY!
