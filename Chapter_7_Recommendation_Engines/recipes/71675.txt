Title:
BUSH'S® Slightly Southwestern Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985360.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (16 ounce) cans BUSH'S® Dark Red Kidney Beans, rinsed and drained*
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chilies, undrained
1 (11 ounce) can niblets corn, drained
1 cup chunky salsa
Shredded Cheddar cheese
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Combine BUSH'S BEST beans, tomato sauce, chicken broth, tomatoes, corn and salsa in large saucepan. Heat to a boil, stirring frequently. Simmer, uncovered, 10 minutes, stirring frequently. Top with cheese and serve with tortilla chips.

Full of flavor, this soup takes only 20 minutes to make.
