Title:
Pizza Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz uncooked rotini pasta
16 oz. bottle Italian dressing
14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes in juice, undrained
8 oz. mozzarella cheese, shredded
5 oz. package pepperoni, chopped (or sliced)
1 onion, chopped
4 oz. can mushrooms

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions. Drain. Rinse and cool.
Mix dressing (start with half the bottle, and add more until it is as wet as you like) and tomatoes (including liquid) with pasta. Add cheese and pizza toppings. Mix all ingredients to taste.
Chill.

Submitter: 
HOWEWASS
Description: 
I tasted this salad at a party and loved it. The recipe given to me just had a list of ingredients without measurements - just add to taste. I made it and wrote down the amounts that worked well for me. Adjust with your favorite pizza toppings - add green pepper, olives, etc.
