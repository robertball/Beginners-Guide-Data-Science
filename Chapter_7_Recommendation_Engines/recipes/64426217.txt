Title:
BBQ lemon chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup lemon juice (or mix with lime juice)
1/2 cup soya sauce
1/2 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp ground pepper
4 chicken breasts

Directions:
Mix together and put in a sealed plastic bag.   Add chicken and refrigerate for at least 20 minutes.   Cook on barbcue.   Meanwhile, add 1 tbsp cornstarch and mix well.   Heat the marinade and let boil for approximately 5 minutes.
