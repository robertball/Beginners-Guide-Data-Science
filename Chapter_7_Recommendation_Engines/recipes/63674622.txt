Ingredients:
Tangy Party Punch...
1 can (46 ounces) pineapple juice, chilled
1 can (46 ounces) orange juice, chilled
1 can (12 ounces) frozen limeade concentrate, thawed
1 can (12 ounces) frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
3 liters ginger ale, chilled
1 pint each orange, lemon and lime sherbet

Directions:
In a large punch bowl, combine the first four ingredients. Stir in ginger ale. Add scoops of sherbet. Serve immediately. Yield: 8
