Title:
Strawberry-Avocado-Mint Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6936428.jpg
Ingredients:
16 fresh mint leaves, torn in half
2 cups frozen strawberries
2 (5.3 ounce) containers nonfat strawberry-flavored Greek yogurt (such as DannonÂ® Light & Fit)
1 cup vanilla-flavored almond milk
1 small avocado, cut into chunks

Directions:
Place mint, strawberries, yogurt, almond milk, and avocado into an electric blender and blend until smooth.

Description: 
The combination of strawberry and mint is a flavor explosion, but add avocado to it, and you'll have the creamiest, tastiest, most wholesome smoothie you've ever had. Great way to start the day.
