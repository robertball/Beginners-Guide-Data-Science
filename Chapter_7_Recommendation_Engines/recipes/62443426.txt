Title:
Ellie's Dessert Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I box yellow cake mix
1 c. light brown sugar
1/2 c. butter
2 eggs
2t. vanilla extract
6 ounces semi sweet chocolate chips
3/4 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except the chocolate chips and the nuts.
Beat well.
Pour into grease 13X 9 pan
Sprinkle chocolate chips and nuts on top
Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes
These are chewy and may not look done but they are.
Cool in pan and cut into 24 bars
