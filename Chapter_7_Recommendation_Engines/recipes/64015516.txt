Title:
Baked Potato Chex Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5c. Rice Chex               1 pkg. ranch dressing
3c. corn Chex               1/2c parmesan cheese
4c. shoestring potatoes
2c. gold fish
1/4 c bacon bits
1/4c butter

Directions:
1. I large microwave dish melt butter, mix in cereal, potatoes. crackers and bacon bits.  Mix until coated.  Add ranch dressing and cheese stir until coated.
2.  Microwave on high 3 min. stirring after every min.
