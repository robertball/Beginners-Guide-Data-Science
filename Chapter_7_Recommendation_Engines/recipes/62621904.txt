Title:
Bacon Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices of bacon
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 medium onion, roughly chopped
1 cup frozen French-cut green beans, thawed
1 egg, scrambled
3 cups cold, prepared rice
2 teaspoons sesame oil
soy sauce to taste

Directions:
Cut bacon into 1/2" squares
Fry bacon in a large, deep, heavy-bottomed skilled until most of the fat has been rendered and the bacon is just beginning to brown
Drain all but a teaspoon or two of the bacon fat from the skillet
Add green beans, onion and garlic to the bacon and saut√© until the bacon is crisp and the vegetables are cooked through
Push the vegetable and bacon to one side of the pan and add the sesame oil to the empty side. Allow it a second or two to heat through then cook the scrambled egg in the sesame oil until just set then combine with the rest of the ingredients
Add the cold rice to the pan, on top of the other ingredients and begin to mix
Add soy sauce, a tablespoon at a time (to taste), making sure the rice is evenly coated
Heat through and serve

Submitter: 
Christi Checca
Description: 
This is a fried rice recipe that I luckily created from practically threadbare cabinets one evening. It's the epitome of comfort food and is a breeze to make. The green beans really compliment the flavors but feel free to use any veggie you have on hand. Enjoy!
