Title:
5 Minute Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1270543.jpg
Ingredients:
2 T. Tahini
2 T. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
3 T. Lemon Juice
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. cumin
1/2 clove of garlic, minced
1 cup cooked Garbanzo Beans
1/4 water

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, add tahini, olive oil and lemon juice. Mix for about 15 seconds. Add salt, cumin and minced garlic and blend an additional 15 seconds. Add beans and enough water to make it the consistency you desire.
That's it, you're done! Use this as a dip for vegetables, crackers, chips, pita bread...

Submitter: 
Stephanie Wentzlaff
Description: 
Our family loves hummus. To buy it from a grocery store is not always cheap. Making it saves you bundles and there is no comparison in the taste. You can add peppers, more garlic, onions or your favorite spice to make it your own.
