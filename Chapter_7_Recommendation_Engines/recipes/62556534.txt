Title:
Germen Beef Cakes ( Fricadellen) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Boxed Mashed Potatoes ( 4 servings)
1/4 cup sliced green onions
1 lb ground beef, cooked and drained
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1 tbsps all purpose flour
2 to 3 tbsps butter

Directions:
Prepare mashed potatoes as directed on package for 4 servings except- decrease water to 3/4 cup.
Stir in onions, beef, garlic salt, and pepper. Shape into eight patties; coat with flour.
Heat butter in 10 inch skillet until melted. Cook patties in butter until brown, four to five minutes on each side.
