Title:
Slow Cooker Barbecue Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds beef ribs
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ sauce
1 cup Ketchup
2 TBSP brown sugar
1 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Season ribs with salt and pepper. Place in a shallow baking pan. Brown in oven 15 minutes. Turn over, and brown another 15 minutes; drain fat.
In a medium bowl, mix together the ketchup, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce,  and salt and pepper. Place ribs in slow cooker. Pour sauce over ribs, and turn to coat.
Cover, and cook on Low 6 to 8 hours, or until ribs are tender.  During last 1/2 hour cook on hight with lid ajar .
