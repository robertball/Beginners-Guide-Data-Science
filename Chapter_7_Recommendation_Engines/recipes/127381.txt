Title:
Bacon and Tomato Presto Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978203.jpg
Ingredients:
8 slices OSCAR MAYER Bacon, chopped
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
1 (8 ounce) tub PHILADELPHIA Chive & Onion Cream Cheese Spread
1 cup milk
1/2 cup KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
6 cups hot cooked penne pasta

Directions:
Cook bacon in skillet 5 min. or until bacon is crisp, stirring occasionally. Drain skillet, leaving bacon in skillet. Stir in cherry tomatoes.
Add cream cheese spread, milk and Parmesan cheese; mix well. Cook until hot and bubbly, stirring frequently.
Stir in pasta.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia
Description: 
A flavorful dish of penne pasta prepared with bacon, cherry tomatoes, and chives is hot and fast to make when you use chive-flavored cream cheese spread.
