Title:
Best Ever Tropical Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 mandarin oranges, divided (for salad and dressing)
2 c strawberries, sliced
1 pineapple, cut into bite-sized chunks
5 kiwi, sliced
3 mangoes, cut into bite-sized chunks
1 medium lemon
1 lime
1/4 c honey
1/2 tsp ginger paste or fresh grated  ginger
1/2 tsp poppy seeds

Directions:
Wash and dry lemon, lime and two oranges.
Zest washed citrus fruit and place zest into small bowl.
Squeeze juice of washed and zested fruit into same bowl.
Add honey, ginger, and poppy seeds, whisk, and set aside.
Prepare remaining fruit and place into large bowl.
Pour dressing on top and gently toss to coat.
Chill at least 30 minutes before serving.
