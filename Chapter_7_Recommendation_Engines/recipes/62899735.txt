Title:
SICILIAN PIZZA SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp.olive oil
2 medium onions,finely chopped
1-28oz.can tomato puree
1/2 cup water
2 tsp.oregano
1/2 tsp.basil
1/2 tsp.salt
1/4 tsp.pepper

Directions:
Heat olive oil.Saute onions till just golden.
Add tomato puree,water and remaining spices.
Simmer covered for 30 min.
This is enough for 2 pizzas.
