Title:
Pasta Con Broccoli With Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 # dry fettuccine pasta (or whatever)
3C heavy whipping cream
6 T butter
8oz tomato sauce
1 T minced garlic
1 chopped red pepper (can omit or use other color)
2C broccoli florets (or more)
1 C fresh sliced mushrooms
1/2 of chopped onion
salt to taste
black pepper to taste
1/2C grated parmesan cheese (or other cheese)

Directions:
Cook noodles in large pot of boiling salted water until "almost" done. Drain, and return noodles to pot
Stir cream, butter, tomato sauce, garlic, and broccoli into noodles. Simmer until noodles are done. Stir in mushrooms and remove from heat. ( I sauted red pepper, onion, garlic and mushrooms sepately and then added to noodles) Sprinkle with cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
I used canned milk, sour cream and nonfat milk instead of heavy whipping cream. Original recipe used 3 oz. tomato sauce and I used 8.
