Title:
White bean, Kale and Kielbasa Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch kale or collard greens
1 TBS olive oil
8 OZ Kielbasa thinly sliced crosswise.
1 large leek (white and light green part only)
15 oz can of white beans (cannellini or navy) rinsed and drained
3 cups reduced sodium chicken broth
1/4 tsp salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
1-2 oz Parmigiano Reggiano cheese grated for garnish

Directions:
1. Rinse kale, shake dry, cut out and discard tough center stems. Roll lengthwise into a tight tube and cut crosswise into 1/4 inch strips and set aside. Use discretion on greens, some bunches are really big.
2. Heat oil in a large heavy saucepan or dutch oven over medium heat and kielbasa and cook until browned (about 5 minutes)
3. Add leeks and cook over med heat for about 3 min.
4. Add kale and cook until wilted about 3 min
5. Stir in beans and broth, bring to boil, reduce and simmer until the kale is tender 10-15 minutes. Add salt and pepper
6. Ladle soup into bowls. Grate cheese on top. Serves 4
7. Cooked rice can be added to soup as you eat it.
310 calories per serving. 1500 sodium
