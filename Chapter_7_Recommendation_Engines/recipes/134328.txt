Title:
Chilean Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 cups ice cubes
1 cup pisco
1 cup orange juice
1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
1/4 cup sweet vermouth
4 lemon twists for garnish

Directions:
Place ice, pisco, orange juice, milk, and vermouth in the bowl of a blender. Blend for a minute until smooth, then pour into serving glasses and garnish with a lemon twist.

Submitter: 
damasio
Description: 
A touch of sweet vermouth with pisco lend a distinctive flavor to this unusual cocktail.
