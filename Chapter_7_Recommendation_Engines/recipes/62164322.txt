Title:
Cool Avocado Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ripe Avocados-should be soft
3 tablespoons of Cilantro, chopped
1 lemon
3 tablespoons of red onion, chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut Avocados in half length-wise.  Remove pits.  Scoop out Avocado in one piece.  Cut each Avocado into 1/4 inches slices.  Lay out on serving dish.  Sprinkle 3 tablespoons of Cilantro evenly over slices.  Sprinkle red onion over slices.  Cut lemon in half and squeeze over Avocados to taste.  Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.  Serve with tortilla chips or crackers.

Submitter: 
Nichole Phillips McVicker
Description: 
This a a great appetizer to put out on a hot day.  Nothing creamy to drag it down.
