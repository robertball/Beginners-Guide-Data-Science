Title:
Coffee Smoother Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6780206.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup coffee
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup chocolate ice cream
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1/4 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Place the coffee, milk, ice cream, cinnamon, and ice cubes in the bowl of a blender; puree until smooth.

Submitter: 
Rosie D
Description: 
This is a smooth coffee drink, but not smooth enough to be a smoothie.  A very strong winter drink.
