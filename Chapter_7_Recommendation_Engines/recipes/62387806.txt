Title:
CRUNCHY SASSY CHINESE SLAW Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz pkg broccoli slaw mix or cabbage mix
1 cup  water chestnuts, sliced into strips & drained
1 cup scallions, chopped
1/2 cup slivered almonds
1/2 cup sunflower seeds
dressing: 1/2 cup olive oil mixed with 1/2 cup water, 2 Tbl soy sauce, 3/4 cup Splenda, 1/2 cup red wine vinegar. Mix well to emulsify

Directions:
Toss all ingredients together, mix well Refrigerate for at least 2 hrs. Stir well before serving
Can be made in advance---next day is best.
