Title:
Homemade Rice-a-Roni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup orzo
3/4 cup rice
2 tablespoons olive oil (approximately)
2 cups chicken stock or broth
about 1 teaspoon better than bouillon (chicken flavor) (optional)

Directions:
Heat olive oil over med in frying pan (not non-stick)
Meanwhile, heat the stock.
Add to the pan, browning slightly. One rice is starting to turn golden, add orzo.
Once all is golden brown, add stock and bouillon and bring to a boil
Reduce to a simmer, cover, and cook 25 minutes.
Let sit off heat 5 minutes
