Title:
sourdough french toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb loaf of sourdough
1 1/2 cup milk
2 large eggs
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp butter or nonstick spray

Directions:
Cut bread into thick slices.
Whisk together milk and eggs.
add salt, cinnamon and vanilla extract.
Heat a pan over medium high heat and coat with butter or nonstick spray.
dip bread slices into egg mixture, just enough to coat the bread.
Cook the bread slices until browned on both sides.
