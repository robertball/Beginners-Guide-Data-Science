Title:
Dry Rub for Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1 1/2 tablespoons paprika
1 1/2 tablespoons salt
1 1/2 tablespoons ground black pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Mix together the brown sugar, paprika, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder. Rub into pork ribs. For best results, allow ribs to marinate overnight. Bake at 250 for 3 1/2 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
Here's a super simple dry rub for a slab of pork ribs. Works great with chicken, too.Modified from: Dry Rub for Ribs
