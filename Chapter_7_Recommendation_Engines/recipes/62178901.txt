Title:
Low Calorie Chocolate Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (2.1 oz) instant sugar-free, fat-free Jello chocolate pudding mix
2 C light or fat-free milk
2 C light or fat-free Cool Whip
1/2 t vanilla
1/4 t ground cinnamon (opt.)

Directions:
Beat pudding mix into 2 cup cold milk in bowl, wsing wire whisk for about 2 min.
Fold in Cool Whip, vanilla, and cinnamon. Stir well
Pour into 6 individual serving dishes and chill until set

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
When you have a chocolate craving, this light, creamy mousse fills the need.
