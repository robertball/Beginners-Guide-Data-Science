Title:
Chocolate and Banana Protein Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 scoop chocolate flavored protein powder (I use Muscle Milk light)
2 cups almond milk (I use Silk brand)
2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
1 banana

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in the food processor until smooth.

Submitter: 
lovetotravel
Description: 
This shake has a lot of protein and will keep you full for hours.
