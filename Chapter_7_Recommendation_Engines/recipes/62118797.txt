Title:
Greek Holiday Cookies - Kourabiedes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C butter
1/2 C confectioners sugar
2 egg yolks
2/3 C blanched almonds, toasted and finely chopped
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 tsp. almond extract
1 1/2 oz brandy
1/3 C orange juice
1 tsp. baking powder
4 1/2 C flour
confectioners sugar for topping

Directions:
Cream butter until light and fluffy
beat in sugar, egg yolks, almonds, vanilla and almonds extracts, brandy and orange juice
Sift baking powder with flour
slowly add to butter mixture
shape into 2 in. balls
bake at 400 for 17min or until very lightly browned.
Upon removing from oven, roll hot cookies in powder sugar
after all cookies have been rolled in sugar and cooled, roll in sugar again for a thick coating

Submitter: 
jalexandropoulos
Description: 
My husband grew up eating these cookies for nearly all celebrated Greek holidays. Similar to what we know as pecan balls or Mexican wedding cakes, these are even tastier with orange juice and brandy.
