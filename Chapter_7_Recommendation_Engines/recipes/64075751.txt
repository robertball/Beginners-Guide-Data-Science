Title:
MARSALA PAN-ROASTED POTATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1136623.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. New Potatoes, small/evenly sized
1 large bunch Fresh Basil, thinly sliced
6 oz. Marsala
Â¼ cup Olive Oil
2 T. Butter
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel the potatoes.  In a large skillet, melt half the butter and brown the potatoes for about 10 minutes over medium heat.
Add the remaining butter and continue to brown, rotating the potatoes.
Deglaze the pan with the Marsala and cook down slightly.  Add more butter if needed, salt as desired and continue browning approx. 20 minutes turning occasionally.
Add the fresh basil, season with pepper and salt, serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Executive Chef Jack Stierer
Description: 
This is a great side for any traditional or american style Italian entree.
