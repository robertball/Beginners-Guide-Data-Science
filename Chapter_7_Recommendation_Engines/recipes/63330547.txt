Title:
MeMe's Beef Tips & Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 PKG OF JOHN SOUL’S FAITA BEEF STRIPS (SOMETIMES I CHEAT)
OR
1 TO 2 1BS OF SIRLOIN STEAK CUT INTO THIN STRIPS (EASIEST IF YOU
FREEZE THE MEAT FOR ABOUT 10 OR 15 MINUTES BEFORE SLICING)
1 MED ONION, CHOPPED
1 LARGE CAN OF SLICED MUSHROOMS (OR ABOUT 2 CUPS FRESH,
SAUTEED WITH ONION)
1 TO 2 CELERY STALKS, CHOPPED (OPTIONAL)
1 TBLS OF STEAK PEPPER MIX (CANDIAN OR MONTREAL BLEND)
1 TSP GARLIC POWDER OR 2 TSP MINCED GARLIC (NOT GARLIC SALT!)
1 CAN OF DICED TOMATOES (OPTIONAL)
1 TO 2 PKGS OF LIPTON SOUP MIX (BEEFY-ONION OR BEEFY-
MUSHROOM)
2 TO 3 TBLS OF MARGARINE (TO SAUTE VEGGIES IN)
2 TO 4 CUPS OF WATER (DEPENDS HOW MUCH SOUP MIX YOU USE)
1/3 TO 1/2 CUP  OF CORNSTARCH WITH ABOUT 1/3 TO 1/2 CUP OF WATER
OR AMOUNT THAT YOU NEED, MIX & SET ASIDE UNTIL NEEDED)
4 CUPS OR SO OF COOKED RICE OR EGG NOODLES

Directions:
**IF USING ALREADY COOKED FAJITA MEAT, THAW MEAT THEN PLACE IN LARGE POT & SAUTE ONIONS, MUSHROOMS, CELERY & SPICES.  , SOUP MIX & WATER.  BRING TO BOIL, TURN DOWN AND SIMMER FOR HALF AN HOUR, THEN TURN UP HEAT AND ADD IN CORNSTARCH MIX, STIR UNTIL THICKENED.  TURN DOWN HEAT TO SIMMER, ADD TOMATOES. SIMMER ABOUT 10 MORE MINUTES. SERVE OVER COOKED RICE OR NOODLES
**IF USING SIRLOIN STEAK CUT INTO STRIPS, SAUTE WITH ONIONS & MUSHROOMS, CELERY AND SPICES, ADD WATER & SOUP MIX BRING TO A BOIL AND THEN TURN DOWN AND SIMMER ABOUT AN HOUR TO 1 ½ HOURS  (UNTIL MEAT IS TENDER). THEN TURN UP HEAT AND ADD IN CORNSTARCH MIX, STIR UNTIL THICKENED.  TURN DOWN HEAT TO SIMMER, ADD TOMATOES. SIMMER ABOUT 10 MORE MINUTES. SERVE OVER COOKED RICE OR NOODLES
**GOES GREAT WITH A SALAD AND ROLLS OR WHATEVER VEGGIE OR FRUIT YOU WANT**
