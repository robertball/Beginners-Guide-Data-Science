Title:
Thyme Roasted Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 med sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 1/2. " thick rounds1
3 tbsp olive oil
4 large garlic cloves
1/3 c thyme leaves or herbs de Provence with thyme or rosemary sprigs to garnish
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp pepper flakes

Directions:
Preheat to 450
In large mixing bowl combine all ingredients and toss
Arrange potato slices I'm single layer om rim bed baking sheet or 13x9 baking dish
Place on top rack of oven and roast until tender and slightly borrowed, about 40 min.
Serve warm or at room temp with sprigs for garnish
