Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground turkey
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. flour
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 1/2 cup ketchup
1 1/2 Tbsp. prepared mustard
1 1/2 Tbsp. barbecue sauce
1 1/2 Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp. red pepper sauce
1 1/2 Tbsp. chili sauce (optional)
2 tsp. horseradish (optional)
6 sandwich buns

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook the turkey onion and garlic over medium heat until turkey is no longer pink; drain if necessary.
Stir in the sugar, flour and pepper.
Add the ketchup, mustard, barbecue sauce, worcestershire sauce, chili sauce (opt.) and horseradish (opt.).  Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 5-10 minutes or until heated through.
Serve on buns.
