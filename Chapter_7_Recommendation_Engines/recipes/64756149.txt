Title:
Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C mayo or yogurt
1/2C sour cream
1/2C buttermilk
1/2tsp dried chives
1/2tsp dried parsley
1/2tsp dill weed
1/2tsp garlic powder
1/4tsp onion powder
1/8tsp salt
1/8tsp pepper

Directions:
In a large bowel whisk all ingredients together. Refrigerate 1/2 hour before serving. Add 1T oil to thin out for dressing
