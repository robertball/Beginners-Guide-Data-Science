Title:
Chicken Soup with Escarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes(32 oz.)chicken broth
1 chicken, cut up and skin removed
1 lb. potatoes, peeled and cut into
1 1/4-inch chunks
1/2 lb. carrots, cut into 1-inch
pieces
3 ribs celery, cut inti 1-inch
pieces
1 medium onion, chopped
2 bay leaves
1 tsp. dried thyme
1 1/2 tsp. salt
few grinds black pepper
3/4 cup uncooked brown rice
1/2 lb. escarole, chopped(about 4
cups)

Directions:
In a large pot, combine broth with chicken, potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, bay leaves, thyme, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium-low.
Simmer, skimming foam from surface of the soup, for 1 hour. Remove chicken; let stand until cool enough to handle, about 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, add rice to soup. Cover and simmer 30 minutes. Remove chicken meat from bones. Sstir chicken and escarole into soup 5 minutes before rice is done.

Submitter: 
Christine Westrom
Description: 
better if carrots, celery and onion are sauteed prior to proceeding with recipe, but not required
