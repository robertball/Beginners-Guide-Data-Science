Title:
Peppermint Rice Crispies Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/323487.jpg
Ingredients:
5 1/2 cups Rice Crispies
24 Large Marshmallows
1/2 cup Crushed Peppermint Candy
1/4 cup Butter
1 TSP Vanilla

Directions:
In a large bowl, melt the butter, vanilla, and the marshmallows for 50 seconds, or until melted, then mix together. Add the peppermint and mix until well combined. Add the Rice Crispies and mix until the Rice Crispies are covered in the marshmallow mix. Once mixed, spread into a 13X9X2 IN pan. Sprinkle more peppermint ontop.

Submitter: 
CLField
Description: 
Classic rice crispies square with a hint of peppermint flavoring and peppermint chunks.
