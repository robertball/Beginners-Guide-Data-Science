Title:
Chicken and Brie Sandwiches with Roasted Cherry Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tsp olive oil
Italina dressing for marinade
2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
3 T balsamic vinegar
1 T chopped fresh basil
1/4 tsp kosher salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
1/4 c mayo
1 T. dijon mustard
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 loaf french bread
3 ounces Brie cheese
1 lb. cooked chicken breast, sliced
2 c. fresh spinach leaves

Directions:
Marinate your chicken in Italian dressing overnight.  Grill, slice and spread brie cheese on top to melt. Cover and set aside.
In a small bowl, mix mayo, garlic, and dijon mustard.  Spread over one half of your sliced french bread.
In a small skillet add 1 tsp. olive oil over medium heat.  Add halved tomatoes and kosher salt.  Cook 4 minutes, stirring once.  Remove from heat and add 2T balsamic vinegar.  Cook tomatoes in a 300* oven for 15 min. then set aside.
In a small bowl, coat spinach leaves with 1 T balsamic vinegar, 2 tsp olve oil and kosher salt.  Place spinach leaves on top of mayo dressing.  Add cooked tomatoes on top of spinach leaves.  Place cooked chicken with cheese on top.  Top sandwich with other half of french bread.  Place in oven for only 5-10 minutes.  Cut into 4 huge sections, serve warm.
