Title:
Baked Crab Rangoon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (6oz.) white crabmeat, drained, flaked
4oz. reduced fat cream cheese
2 green onions, thinly sliced
1/4C. light mayo
12 won ton wrappers

Directions:
Heat oven 350 degrees.
Mix first 4 ingred.
Place one wrapper in each of 12 muffin cups sprayed w/cooking spray, extending edges of wrappers over sides of cups.
Bake for 5-7 min. or until lightly browned.
Fill w/crabmeat and bake 6-8 min. or until filling is heated through.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Quickly made, ready in a jiff and only 5 carbs and 70 cal. each.
