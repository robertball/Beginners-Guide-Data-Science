Title:
Dee-Licious Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons of EVOO
1 Tablespoon of Soy Sauce
2 Tablespoons of Red Wine Vinegar
2 Tablespoons of Worcestershire
1 Tablespoon of grainy mustard (spicy brown works great!)
1/2 Tablespoon of garlic powder
1/2 red or white onion, finely chopped or minced
1 Clove of Garlic, minced

Directions:
Blend together well.
Pour over steak and seal in Ziplock bags for up to 24 hours, depending on the cut of the steak (the thicker the steak, the longer you should marinade)
Grill or pan-fry.

Submitter: 
LaurenSyd
Description: 
A tangy steak marinade that brings out all the right flavors! Just mix, marinade, and enjoy. Happy Grilling!
