Title:
Wondertime Chocolate Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/632905.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup half-and-half
1 teaspoon vanilla
Assorted cut up fruit, pound cake cubes and marshmallows (for dipping)

Directions:
Melt together chocolate chips and half-and-half either by sittting in the top of a double boiler over simmering heat or by heating in microwave for a couple of minutes, stopping every 30 seconds or so to stir. Stir in vanilla, then serve.
