Title:
Grandma Jean's Cinnamon Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4483413.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups warm milk
½ cup warm water
2 eggs, slightly beaten
½ cup sugar
½ cup veg. oil
2 pkg. yeast
2 tsp. salt
¼ cup margarine (melted)
8 cups flour
1 pkg. vanilla pudding mix (Instant)
Filling:
1 cup brown sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup raisins (optional)
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Mix well, but do not knead. Place in greased bowl and let rise until doubled (1 hour).
Divide like a jelly roll to make 24 pieces.
Put in 9 x 13 pan and let rise 45 minutes.
Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
Rub with margarine and sprinkle with cinnamon filling. Drizzle icing over baked buns.
