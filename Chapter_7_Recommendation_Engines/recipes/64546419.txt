Title:
Santa Maria style Ranch Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB Chopped Bacon
2 Chopped Onions
2 Chopped Red Peppers
1/4 cup Minced Garlic
1 TBS Ground Black Pepper
1 TBS Oregano
1 LB Cooked Sausage
2 bags of dried Pinto Beans (pre-soaked)
2 cans Ranch Beans
1/4 TSP Cayenne Pepper
1 TSP Paprika
1 TSP Cumin
1/2 bottle BBQ Sauce

Directions:
Put the bacon pieces at the bottom of a large cooking pot
Put half the onions, half the red peppers and all the garlic on top
Add black pepper and oregano on top
Add the cooked sausage on top
Heat until ingredients become oily
Add pinto beans and ranch beans
Add cayenne pepper, paprika and cumin
Stir in BBQ sauce
Add remainder of onions and green peppers
Cook on medium for 30 minutes stirring occasionally
Move to smoker and smoke for 90 minutes

Submitter: 
Troy Adam
Description: 
Just like my Grandpa Fred used to make. He was a wildcatter near Goleta in the 60's and learned a lot about Santa Maria style BBQ
