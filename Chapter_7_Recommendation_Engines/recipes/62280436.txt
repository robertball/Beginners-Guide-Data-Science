Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
2 cups of sugar
1 cup of oil
3 1/2 cups of flour
3/4 tsp. baking soda
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
2 cups grated zucchini
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup raisins, optional

Directions:
Beat eggs. Gradually add sugar, then oil. Combine dry ingredients; add to egg mixture alternately with zucchini. Stir in vanilla, nuts and raisins. Pour batter into two greased and floured loaf pans. Bake on lowest oven rack 55 minutes at 350 degrees. Let stand about 10 minutes then turn onto wire rack to cool.
