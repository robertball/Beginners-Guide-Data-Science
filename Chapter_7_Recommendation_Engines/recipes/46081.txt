Title:
Russian Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/596266.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sweet onions, chopped
2 tomatoes, chopped
3 green bell peppers, chopped
1 cup sour cream
1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill

Directions:
In a medium bowl, toss together sweet onions, tomatoes, and green bell peppers.
Blend sour cream and dill in a small bowl. Lightly toss into the salad mixture. Cover, and chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Submitter: 
Alexandra Romanov
Description: 
A wonderful and easy Russian salad. Outstanding with Russian Stew.
