Title:
Apple Lemon Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups diced Apples peeled and cored.
4 tablespoons Lemon and 1 Tablespoon grated lemon peel
1 Cup all purpose flour or whole wheat flour
1 Cup firmly packed brown sugar
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 Cup Butter or Margarine

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350. Pour diced apples into 8in" Pie pan and sprinkle grated lemon peel on top. Then pour the lemon juice evenly on top of apples, let the lemon juice saturate into the apples for 30 minutes. While apples are being saturated in a small bowl stir together sugar,flour,and 1 teas. of the cinnamon. Then cut in butter until mixture is crumbly. After 30 minutes is over, spread flour mixture evenly over apples. Bake,uncovered, for 50 to 60 minutes or until top is crisp or lightly brown, let cool for at least 25 minutes before serving. Serve at room temperature.

Submitter: 
*Cozycook*
Description: 
Easy and not time consuming!.....Nutritious and only lightly sweetened!!! Delicious, apples saturated in lemon juice with a sprinkle of crunch added on top! Enjoy.
