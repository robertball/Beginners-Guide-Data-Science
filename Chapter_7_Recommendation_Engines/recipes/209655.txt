Title:
Dirtier Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991026.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 medium onion, diced
1 medium red bell pepper, diced
1 stalk celery, diced
1 (5.7 ounce) package Knorr® Cajun Sides™ - Dirty Rice
2 cups water

Directions:
Brown ground beef in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally; drain.
Stir in onion, red pepper and celery. Cook, stirring occasionally, 3 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender. Stir in Knorr(R) Cajun Sides(TM) - Dirty Rice and cook, stirring frequently, until rice is lightly toasted, about 2 minutes.
Add water and bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer covered until rice is tender, about 7 minutes. Serve with hot pepper sauce.

Submitter: 
Knorr
Description: 
A Cajun-inspired take on dirty rice achieves main-dish status when made with ground beef, onions, peppers, and celery, and a flavored rice mix.
