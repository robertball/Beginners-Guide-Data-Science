Title:
Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 c milk
1 T melted butter
1 c flour
1/4 t salt

Directions:
Beat 2 eggs until light.
Add remaining ingredients and beat until evenly blended (30 seconds with an electric beater.)  The batter should be like heavy cream.
Pour into buttered muffin pan, having them half full.
Bake 20 minutes at 450, reduce heat to 350 and bake 20 minutes longer.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
Wonderful, light and airy popovers.
