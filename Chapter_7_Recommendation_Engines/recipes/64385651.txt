Title:
Jambalaya Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2213581.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1 inch cubes
1 pound andouille sausage, sliced
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice
1 large onion, chopped
1 large green bell pepper, chopped
1 cup chopped celery
1.5 Litres chicken broth
1 can kernel corn
2 teaspoons dried oregano
2 teaspoons dried parsley
2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1 pound frozen cooked shrimp without tails (optional)

Directions:
In a large pot, add the olive oil and brown the chicken, sausage, onion, pepper and celery for a few minutes.  Then add tomatoes with juice,corn and broth. Season with oregano, parsley, Cajun seasoning, cayenne pepper, and thyme.
Cover, and cook 1 - 2 hours on Low, or till all veggies are cooked through. Stir in the shrimp during the last 30 minutes of cook time.

Submitter: 
Phyllis Trinke Funk
Description: 
Shrimp and chicken simmer with classic jambalaya ingredients in this easy, hearty soup meal.Modified from: Colleen's Slow Cooker Jambalaya
