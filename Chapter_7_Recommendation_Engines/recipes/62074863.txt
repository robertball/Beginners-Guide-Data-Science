Title:
Blackberry Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Topping:
1/4 Brown sugar
2 Tbs. Butter
2 Cups Blackberries
3/4 Cup Sugar
Cake:
1/2 Cup Butter or Margarine
1 Cup Sugar
2 Eggs
1 1/2 Cups Flour
2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Vanilla
1/4 Cup Milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Topping:
On stove heat brown sugar and 2 Tbs. butter in 9 inch cake pan over medium heat. Add berries and continue cooking, stir until mixture bubbles. Add 3/4 cup sugar and crush berries slightly with fork. Cook for 5 more minutes then remove from heat and set aside.
Cake:
Beat 1/2 cup soft butter and 1 Cup sugar until fluffy. Beat in eggs. Mix dry ingredients in small bowl. Alternately add dry ingredients and milk to butter and sugar mixture cream well. Add vanilla and mix.
Pour batter (more like droplets) over cooked fruit in cake pan and bake for 35-40 min.
Let stand in pan till warm (about 1/2 hour or more) do not let cake cool completely. Run knife along the edge of pan, place cake plate over top of the cake and flip. Lift slowly, may take a min for the suction to release.
*Use cake plate that is wider than cake, the berries and juice run down the sides but is gorgeous when done!

Description: 
Easy and beautiful cake to make with ingredients you have on hand. Great to take to a party or picnic.
