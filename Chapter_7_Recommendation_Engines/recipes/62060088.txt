Title:
Simple Spicy Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb peeled and deveined shrimp
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tbsp chopped, fresh parsley
1 tsp. Adobo seasoning
sprinkle of chili powder
fresh pepper
1-2 tsp of your favorite hot sauce
3 tbsp. olive or corn oil

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet over medium heat. Rinse shrimp and add to skillet. Season with adobo and  chili powder. Cook for 2 minutes and then turn. Add garlic to skillet and mix well. Sprinkle with pepper. Cook for an additional 2 minutes. Add hot sauce, if desired and stir. Sprinkle with parsley. Lower heat, cover and simmer for additional 5-7 minutes or until shrimp are cooked through. Serve over white rice.

Submitter: 
Angie Duffy Hernandez
Description: 
This is an easy way to make shrimp. It's spicy but can be made without the hot sauce if preferred. It is good served over white rice.
