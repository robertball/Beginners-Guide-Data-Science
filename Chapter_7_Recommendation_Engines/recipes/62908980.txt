Title:
Kai Kang Dang (Chicken Curry with Coconut Milk) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (2 cups)LITE coconut milk
2 tablespoons red curry paste
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut in bite-sized pieces
4 tablespoons fish sauce
1 1/2 tablespoon brown sugar
3/4 cup bamboo shoots, drained
2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
1/2 orange bell pepper, sliced
3/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chiffonade
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice

Directions:
Bring 1/2 cup lite coconut milk to a boil in a heavy saucepan. Whisk in the curry paste and cook until fragrant, about 5 minutes. Add the chicken and cook until browned, about 5 minutes. Stir in the rest of the coconut  milk, fish sauce, sugar, frozen vegetables and bamboo shoots; simmer briefly until the chicken is cooked through and vegetables are cooked but still brightly colored and firm, about 5 minutes. Add the fresh sliced peppers and remove from heat. Squeeze fresh lime juice over and add basil chiffonade. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Lorelli
Description: 
This South Asian stir-fry recipe tastes great on Basmati rice. Increase or reduce the red curry paste to your tastes.Modified from: Kai Kang Dang (Chicken Curry with Coconut Milk)
