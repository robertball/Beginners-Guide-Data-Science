Title:
Buffalo Chicken Sausage Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz package al fresco Buffalo Style Chicken Sausage
1 cup Celery, chopped into bite-sized pieces
1 cup Low Fat Blue Cheese dressing

Directions:
In a non-stick pan with cooking spray, cook chicken sausage until browned on all sides and internal temperature reaches 165 degrees.
Remove sausage from pan and slice into bite sized pieces.
Skewer sausage pieces and celery pieces and serve with blue cheese dressing for dipping.
