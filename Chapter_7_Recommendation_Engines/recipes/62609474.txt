Title:
Pasta mama - lighter version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  1/2 package of Tofu Shiratki noodles
1/2 cup egg beaters
2 cloves minced garlic
2T chopped parsely
2 T of water ( divided)
IT fresh parmesan
1/4t garlic powder
1/4t oregano
1/4t  seasoning salt
1/4t onion powder

Directions:
Pull out, set up and prepare all ingredients for the dish
2) Prepare pasta –  Boil for three minutes
3) Turn on medium flame. Pour 1T water in 10 inch
sauté pan
4) Add minced garlic and 1 tsp mixed seasoning; sauté together 1-
2 min.
5) Add parsley; add pasta with 1 TBS hot water (Make sure
leftover pasta or cold pasta is hot before adding parsley or
eggs)
6) Add beaten eggs. Mix thoroughly.
7) Add Parmesan, cook through and serve
Variations: Pasta Mama has as many variations as you can imagine.
Add any vegetables you would put in an omelet, other cheeses
