Title:
Super Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce or soy sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons McCormick速 Season-All速 Seasoned Salt
1/4 teaspoon McCormick速 Black Pepper, Ground
1 (11.5 ounce) can mixed nuts
2 cups wheat squares cereal
2 cups rice squares cereal
2 cups corn squares cereal
2 cups small pretzels, such as twists or rings

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees F. Combine butter, Worcestershire sauce, Season-All(R),and pepper in a large bowl. Stir in remaining ingredients and gently stir until evenly coated.
Spread mixture evenly onto two 15x10-inch jelly roll pans. Bake 45 minutes, stirring halfway through cook time. Cool completely. Store in an airtight container.
