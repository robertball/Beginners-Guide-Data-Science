Title:
Spicy Gingerbread Overnight Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5833908.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup rolled oats
1 tbsp flax meal
1 tbsp brown sugar, or to taste
1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt (or regular plain yogurt)
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp molasses
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground ginger
pinch ground nutmeg
pinch ground cloves
pinch ground allspice
pinch salt
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
3 tbsps raisins (optional)
3 tbsps walnuts (optional)

Directions:
1. In a glass container, combine oats, flax meal, brown sugar, yogurt, milk, molasses, spices, salt, and vanilla. Stir until thoroughly combined.
2. Refrigerate mixture 8 hours, or overnight. Top with raisins and walnuts just before serving, if desired.
