Title:
New Year's Day Baked Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter
6 eggs
1/3 cup milk
2 tbsp sour cream
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp dried thyme
Freshly grated black pepper
2 cups chopped or thinly sliced vegetables (any combination of broccoli, zucchini, summer squash, mushrooms, green and red bell peppers)
1 cup shredded/julienned  hard cheese (any combnation of cheddar, monterey, provolone, American, swiss)
2 plum tomatoes, sliced, or about 6 cherry tomatoes, halved
1/2 cup grated or shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Grease a 2 quart casserole dish with butter.
Whisk together eggs, milk, sour cream and herbs.  Season with black pepper.
Reserving sliced tomatoes and parmesan cheese, stir chopped vegetables and cheese into egg mixture.
Pour into casserole dish.
Arrange sliced tomatoes on top and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
Bake for 35 minutes or until golden brown set.
Let cool at least 10 minutes before serving, or cool and refrigerate.
Can be served, warm, room temperature or cold.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
A New Year's Day tradition, I make good use of the leftovers from our veggie trays and cheese platters from the New Year's Eve festivities!  This versatile dish is easy to prepare and can be serve warm or cold - it will also keep well in the refrigerator for a couple of days, and tastes just as good reheated.
