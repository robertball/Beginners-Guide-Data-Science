Title:
Southern Skillet Fried Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ears of corn
3 tablespoons flour
3 teaspoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1-1/2 cups water
3 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Shuck corn and pull off silks.
In a large bowl, cut the corn from the cob with a sharp knife.
Scrap up and down the cob with a spoon or knife to get the milk.
Combine flour, sugar, salt and pepper in a small bowl, then add 1-1/2 cups of water.
Stir with a fork or whisk to combine.
Melt butter over medium-low heat in a large, heavy bottomed skillet.
Add corn and flour mixture and cook over medium-low heat for 20 to 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Watch close it will stick..
