Title:
Awesome Tuna and Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of Tuna (6oz cans) - drained
1 16oz package of dry extra wide egg noodles
1 15oz can of small sweet peas - drained
2 10.75oz cans of Cream of Mushroom soup
1/2 c butter
7 oz Lays Original Potato Chips - coursely crushed
1 soup can full of milk
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cook noodles per package instructions
Drain noodles
Stir 3T of butter into noodles
Add peas, tuna, soup, and milk to noodles
Stir all ingredients together until well mixed
Add salt and pepper to taste - go light on the salt, as the chips will add salt
Grease 9x13 dish
Pour noodle mixture into baking dish.
Dot top of noodles with remaining 5T butter
Spread potato chips evenly across top of casserole
Bake for 20-25 minutes until chips are lightly browned

Submitter:

Description: 
Tuna and Noodle Casserole is my daughters favorite.  With the potato chips, kids eat this up and ask for more!
