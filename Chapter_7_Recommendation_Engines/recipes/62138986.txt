Title:
Easy Damn Good Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box chocolate or white cake mix
1 can condensed sweetened milk
1 jar caramel topping
1 container frozen whipped topping, thawed
3 heath bars (crushed)

Directions:
1. Bake cake mix as directed and let cool completely.
2. Take a wooden spoon and poke holes in the top of the cake all the way through to the bottom.
3. Mix together the caramel and condensed sweetened milk. Pour the mixture on top of the cake filling the holes.
4. Top the cake with whipped topping and crushed heath bar.

Submitter: 
Dana C.
Description: 
A yummy cake topped with cool whip and crushed heath bar. This dessert is delicious and makes a great cake for anyone's birthday! It's also so easy to make.
