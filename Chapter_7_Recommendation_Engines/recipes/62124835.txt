Title:
Chicken Patty Ann Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large chicken
1 1/2 cup chopped celery
2 T butter
16 oz pkg mozarella cheese
1 can pizza sauce w/cheese
1/4 cup red wine
1 can whole tomatoes, lightly smashed
2 cups chicken broth
1 1/2 tsp celery salt
2 tsp oregano
1 lb rigatoni pasta

Directions:
Boil chicken until done, cool and remove from bone.
Saute celery in butter
Mix mozarella, pizza sauce, red wine, whole tomatoes, chicken broth, celery salt and oregano.
Add cooked celery and chicken, stir in cooked rigatoni.
Place in casserole dish or individual dishes, sprinkle with additional cheese.
Bake 1/2 hour covered at 350 degrees (be sure not to touch cover to cheese)
Bake 15 min uncovered to brown cheese.

Submitter: 
ChefJoe
Description: 
This delicious combination of italian flavors is a family favorite.  It freezes well too, for those last minute dinners.
