Title:
Yummy baked fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 sleeve of Club Keebler Crackers
2 eggs beaten
1/2 teaspoon of garlic salt
pepper to taste
4 halibut steak loins
2 tablespoons of butter

Directions:
Crush the crackers into a bowl and add the garlic salt and pepper.
Dip the fish into the egg beat and then dip into the cracker mixture.  Add the butter into the dish that you are baking the fish in.  Cook at 375 degrees for 25 minutes
