Title:
Andy's Bacon Smothered Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/301873.jpg
Ingredients:
6 thick slices of bacon
1/2 cup onions, chopped
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 pound of fresh or frozen green beans, trimmed
Ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook chopped bacon in a large skillet over medium-high heat until crisp.
Steam fresh or frozen green beans to desired crisp/softness.
Remove bacon and chop.
Stir in onions and garlic and saute until caramelized.
Add bacon and green beans and pepper and continue to cook for about 15-20 minutes.
