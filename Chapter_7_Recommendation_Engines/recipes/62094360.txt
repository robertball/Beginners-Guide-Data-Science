Title:
Spicy-Sweet Chicken Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup olive oil
3 1/2 T. chopped cilantro
2 T. lime juice (fresh is best)
1 1/2 tsp. sugar
2 garlic cloves
3/4 tsp. cumin
1/2 tsp. chili powder
1/2-3/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 3/4 c. chopped, cooked chicken
1 15 oz. can black beans, drained & rinsed
1 c. frozen extra sweet corn, thawed
1/2 medium red bell pepper, chopped
1 can mild green chilies, chopped
4-5 green onions (include just a little of the green part)

Directions:
Combine oil, lime juice, cilantro, sugar, garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt and pepper in a small bowl. Mix well with a wire whisk.  Set aside.
Mix together chicken, beans, corn, red pepper, green chilies and green onions in a large bowl.  Combine dressing with the chicken mixture and toss gently.  Chill until ready to serve.  Tastes best at room temperature.

Submitter: 
SUE1956
Description: 
This is a recipe I've made for years. Spicy, but with a hint of sweetness from the corn. It's perfect for a light summer meal with garlic bread!
