Title:
Beer-Butt Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3-5lbs fryer chicken, thawed
1 stick butter/margarine
4 TBLS garlic -or- lemon pepper
1 "tall-boy" can of beer, half full

Directions:
1] remove innards from fryer chicken
2] boil for 10-20 minutes or till barely pink
3] meanwhile, melt stick butter, mix with seasonings, set aside
4] heat grill, remove top shelp if nessacery
5] shove beer-can up chickens rear-opening
so chicken appers to stand upright
6] place beer canned chicken, (acting as stand) on botton shelf in grill
7] close lid
8] every 10 min baste chicken with butter mix
9] repeat step 8 6 times
10] done when chicken skin is crisp

Submitter: 
MRSREAMESNOODLE
Description: 
Quick, simple, no-fail, juicy, grilled, rotissarie-style chicken. (pre-boil speeds the cook time and eliminates pink grilled syndrome within meat.
