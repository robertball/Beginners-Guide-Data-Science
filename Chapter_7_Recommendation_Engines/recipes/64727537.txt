Title:
Longhorn cheese casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 pound pasta medium shells
1 pound of longhorn cheddar shredded
2 jars of original Ragu sauce

Directions:
Brown beef
Boil shells in salt water 20 minutes drain
In casserole baking dish put a thin layer of Ragu sauce
Shells in a layer
Then beef and sauce
Then cheese
Repeat until ingredients are gone
Cover well baking @ 350Â° for 35 minutes remove lid last 10  minutes
