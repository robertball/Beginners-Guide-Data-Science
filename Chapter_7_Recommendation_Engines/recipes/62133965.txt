Title:
Cream Cheese Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 oz) pkgs cream cheese
1 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 pkgs crescent rolls
1/3 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix cream cheese, sugar, vanilla together.  Spread 1 pkg crescent rolls in bottom of 13 x 9 inch pan.  Seal perforations.  Spread with cream cheese mixture.  Cover with second pkg of crescent rolls.  You donâ€™t have to seal perf. on this layer.
Sprinkle with 1/3 cup sugar mixed with 1 tsp cinnamon.  Bake 25 min.
