Title:
Orange-Honey Game Hens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4463427.jpg
Ingredients:
2 game hens, cut in half
Â¼  cup pure honey
2 Tbsp soy sauce
I large orange, juiced
Himalayan Pink Salt, to taste
Freshly cracked black pepper, to taste

Directions:
In a gallon size plastic zipper bag, combine the orange juice, soy sauce, and honey. Shake to mix well. Add in the game hens and seal the bag. Turn to coat and marinate for at least four hours.
Arrange the game hens on the CIO grill pan, spooning some of the marinade over top. Generously season with salt and pepper.
Set to autocook, Poultry and vegetables 1.

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
Game hens are marinated in a sweet orange juice and honey sauce then roasted to perfection in the Panasonic Countertop Induction Oven. Serve with a side of white rice for a special dinner.
