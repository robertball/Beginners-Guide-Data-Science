Title:
Honey-Glazed Ham Slice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Brown Sugar (96g)
1/2 cup Honey (168g)
1/2 teas. Gr. Mustard
6 whole Cloves
1" thick Ham Slice
2 slices Pineapple (optional)
8 Marachino Cherry halves (optional)

Directions:
Combine brown sugar, honey, mustard, and cloves in a small sauce pan. Bring to boil over med-low heat. Stir occasionally and be careful not to let boil over.
Bake ham slice at 325F. for 10 min. Arrange pineapple slices and cherries (if using) on top of ham slices. Spoon glaze over ham slice and bake for 15 min. basting every 5 min. with drippings.
