Title:
Chicken and Spinach Fettucini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless boneless chicken breasts, chopped
8oz frozen spinach, thawed and drained
13oz tin diced tomatoes
garlic to taste
handful sliced mushrooms
1/2 onion, sliced
1 large tablespoon light sour cream
vegeroni (colored) fettucini

Directions:
1. Cook fettucine until al dente, drain.
2. Fry garlic in a little olive oil and add chicken and onion.  Cook till chicken cooked through.
3.  Add spinach, stir through well.  Add tomatoes (in juice) and stir, then add the mushrooms.  Cook a few minutes to heat well.
4.  Stir in the sour cream and serve over fettucini.
