Image:

Ingredients:

Directions:
CRACKER SALAD
3 stacks Saltine crackers
3 or 4 tomatos, seeded and diced
1 medium onion finely diced
1 medium green bell pepper, minced
8 ounces grated sharp Cheddar cheese
Mayoinaise to moisten
METHOD:
Break Saltines in a bowl, add other ingredients. Mositen with mayoinase to taste. Salt & Pepper to taste. Chill before serving
OPTIONS:  Can be topped with crumbled criap bacon; can add 2 hard boiled eggs; can add green olives or sweet pickle chips, or diced & seeded cucumber.
