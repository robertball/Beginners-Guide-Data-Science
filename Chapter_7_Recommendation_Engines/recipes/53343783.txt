Title:
Perfect Grilled Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C soy sauce
1 t peeled and minced fresh ginger
1/2 t. minced garlic
1 T honey, molasses or hiosin sauce
fresh ground pepper
juice of 1/2 lime
16-24 oz boneless steak

Directions:
Mix first 6 ingred., taste and add more of anything you like. Turn in sauce once or twice, then let sit in sauce until grill is hot.
Turn once more before placing on grill, spoon any remaining sauce over steak.
For rare, grill about 3 min. ea. side for steak under an inch thick. For larger or more well-done steak, increase the time slightly.
