Title:
Awesome Apple pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling
(10 apples total)
6 c. granny smith apples sliced 1/2 inch
1 c. golden delicious apple sliced 1/2 inch
1 tbsp butter
1 c light brown sugar packed
3/4 tsp cinnamon
4 tbsp flour
2 tsp lemon juice
Topping
1/2 c plus 2 tbsp flour
5 tbsp cold butter cut into pieces
5 tbsp caramel sauce

Directions:
First prepare filling.
Place 1 tbsp butter in a large skillet.
Heat butter over medium heat.
Combine brown sugar and cinnamon.
Pour apples in hot skillet
Pour sugar/cinnamon over apples.
Coat apples with sugar mixture.
Cook apples about 5 minutes.
Stir occasionaly.
Remove from heat
Stir in 4 tbsp flour.
Stir in lemon juice.
Spoon mixture into crust.
Prepare topping
Lightly spoon 1 c. flour into measuring cup.
Level flour in cup.
Combine flour and 1 c. brown sugar cut in 5 tbsp butter it until resembles crumbs.
Drizzle 5 tbsp caramel syrup over apple mixture.
Sprinkle topping over syrup.
Bake 375 for 30 minutes
or until apples are tender.

Submitter: 
Melissa Carmela
Description: 
An apple pie that is sure to please everyone.  This pie is a staple in our house through out the year.  I am sure you will be pleased.
