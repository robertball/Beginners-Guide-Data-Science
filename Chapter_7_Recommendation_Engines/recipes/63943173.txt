Title:
Mediterranean-Inspired Creamed Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093062.jpg
Ingredients:
1 10 ounce box of frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 tablespoon flour
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
2 tablespoons pine nuts
2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
sea salt, to taste
ground pepper, to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium high heat. Add onions and saut√© until onions are soft, about 5 minutes.
In a small bowl, blend flour into almond milk. Stir in nutmeg, salt and pepper. Transfer to the saucepan and heat until sauce thickens.
Add spinach and cook until heated through. Mix in pine nuts and feta cheese. Serve.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Pine nuts and nutmeg give a Mediterranean flare to a creamy yet healthy spinach dish that can be served either as a hot dip or elegant side-dish. It is excellent when served with lamb.
