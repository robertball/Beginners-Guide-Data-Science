Title:
Easy Thai Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rotisserie chicken
1 can light coconut milk
1-2T red curry paste
4t sugar
salt and pepper to taste
1c fresh basil
cooked rice

Directions:
Pull meat from chicken in large pieces.
Spoon a few T of coconut milk into skillet.
Stir in 1-2T curry paste, sugar, remaining milk, and s/p.
Simmer, add chicken, and heat through.
Stir in basil.
Serve over rice.
