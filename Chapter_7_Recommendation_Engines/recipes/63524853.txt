Title:
Millie's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/962370.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Italian sausage, casings removed (mild or hot)
1 small red onion, chopped
3 - 4 garlic cloves, minced
1 (14 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
1 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
1 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
1 ½ cups water
1 ½ teaspoons basil
1 teaspoons dried parsley flakes
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
1/4 teaspoon fresh coarse ground black pepper
5 teaspoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
A little over 1/4 cup of red wine
1 box thin spaghetti or favorite noodles
Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
In large, heavy stockpot, brown Italian sausage, breaking up as you stir.
Add onions and continue to cook, until onions are softened.
Add garlic, tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce and water.
Add basil, parsley, sugar, salt, crushed red pepper, and black pepper.
Stir well and bring to a boil.  Stir in red wine.
Simmer on low, stirring frequently for at least an hour or longer.
(making sure not to let it burn)
Cook spaghetti or favorite noodles according to package directions.
Spoon sauce over drained noodles and sprinkle Parmesan cheese
