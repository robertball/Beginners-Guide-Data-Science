Title:
Texas Hot Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1051070.jpg
Ingredients:
1 gallon apple cider
1 quart pineapple juice
1 pinch ground nutmeg, or more to taste
20 whole cloves
1 orange
5 cinnamon sticks, or more to taste

Directions:
Mix apple cider, pineapple juice, and nutmeg in a large pot.
Press cloves into orange in the shape of Texas. Float orange and cinnamon sticks in the cider mixture.
Heat cider mixture over medium heat until warmed and flavors have combined, 15 to 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Michelle
Description: 
This Texas hot apple cider will warm your boots on a cold night! There is enough for the state of Texas in this recipe. It's big! Perfect for parties! Plus it will make your house smell so good!
