Title:
Roast pork Tenderloin with Shallots & Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pork tenderloin
salt and pepper
1 Tbsp.butter
1 Tbsp.oil
1 cup brown and white mushrooms
2 garlic cloves,minced
2 shallots,minced
1/4 cup red wine
2 tsp.dried thyme
8 sheets phyllo pastry,thawed
1/4 cup melted butter
2 Tbsp.feta cheese
1 egg,beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.Season pork with salt and pepper and roast in oven for about 15 minutes.Remove from oven and let cool.
Heat butter and oil in skillet set over medium-high heat.
Add mushrooms and saute for 5 minutes.Add garlic and shallots to pan,continue to saute for another 2-3 minutes,or untill mushrooms are softened.
Add wine and thyme to pan and continue to cook untill most of the liquid has evaporated.Remove from heat and let cool.
Roll out one pastry at a time and lightly brush each with butter.Lay next sheet on top,repeat untill all are used.
Spread mushroom mixture evenly over phyllo,leaving 1" border.
Sprinkle mushroom mixture with crumbled feta and season with salt and pepper.
Place pork tenderloin on one side of phyllo and roll up.
Brush phyllo with egg wash and bake pork,seam side down,in oven for about 20 minutes.

Description: 
This is tenderloin done a little different.The phyllo pastry keeps it so moist.Serve with pasta.I make this for just my husband and I.All you need is a great bottle of wine.
