Title:
Quick Thai Fried Bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1944153.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
4 firm bananas, peeled and cut into 1- to 2-inch pieces
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 lime, juiced
1 teaspoon black sesame seeds

Directions:
Melt butter in a wok over high heat. Add bananas, brown sugar, and cinnamon; saute until bananas are golden brown and sugar is bubbling, about 5 minutes. Stir in sesame seeds and lime juice.
