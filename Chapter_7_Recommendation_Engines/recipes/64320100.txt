Ingredients:
1 box of Brownie Mix
1-11 oz. jar of Hot Fudge Sauce
3 large Eggs

A  bread loaf pan

Directions:

Pour it into a non-stick sprayed laof pan, about 3/4 of the way up.
Bake it at 350 degrees for approx 45 minutes, or until itâ€™s cooked all the way through
