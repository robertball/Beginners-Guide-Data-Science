Title:
Marge's Princess Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/4 c. sour cream
2 t. instant onion
1/4 t. crushed red pepper
1/2 t. salt
3 oz cream cheese, cubed
steamed asparagus spears

Directions:
Beat together all ingredients except asparagus.  Pour into hot buttered skillet.
When eggs set, arrange asparagus spears on omelet.  Fold over.
