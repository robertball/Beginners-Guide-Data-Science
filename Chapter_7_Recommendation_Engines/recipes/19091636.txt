Title:
Mrs. Nato's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Soy Sauce
1 Cup water
1/2 Cup vinegar
1/2 Cup molasses
1 large clove garlic peeled and hit once to flatten
Fresh ginger, peeled and cut into small pieces
Chicken pieces with skin, about 2 - 3 pounds

Directions:
In a saucepan bring all ingredients to a boil. Pour hot marinade over chicken to cover and
soak overnight. Place chicken skin side up in baking dish. Pour marinade over chicken and bake 15 minutes at 450. Reduce oven temerature to 350 and continue to bake 15 - 20 minutes longer, basting occasionally. Remove chicken from dish, cover tightly with foil to keep warm. Pour marinade into skillet (remove ginger and garlic if desired) and boil over medium high heat until sauce is reduced to 1/2 and thick. Serve chicken over rice with sauce.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
A delicious terriyaki like mixture that acts as a marinade, cooking liquid and a sauce for serving.
