Title:
Caramel Chocolate Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg. devil's food cake mix (9 oz)
2 pkgs instant chocolate pudding (3.9 oz)
1 carton frozen whipped topping, thawed (12 oz)or    real whipping cream
1 jar caramel ice cream topping (12 1/4 oz)
1 pkg Engleish toffee bits or almond brickle chips (7-8 oz)

Directions:
Prepare cake as to package directions (8 in. square Pan) I use the larger mix and use half or more. The other half I ice and freeze for later use)
Prepare pudding as to directions.
Cut cake into 1 1/2 inch cubes, place half of the cubes in a 3 qt. trifle bowl or large glass serving bowl. Press down lightly to fill in gaps.
Top with half of the whipped topping, pudding, carmel topping and toffee bits.
Repeat the layers
Cover and refrigerate until serving

Submitter: 
Mara
Description: 
Layered with cake, pudding, whipped cream, and caramel sauce makes a cake everyone devours.
