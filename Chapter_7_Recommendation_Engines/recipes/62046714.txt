Title:
Spiced Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 cup whole-wheat flour
1 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
1 Tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon ground ginger
4 egg whites
1 cup canned pumpkin
1/2 cup buttermilk
3 tablespoons unsweetened apple sauce
2 tablespoons molasses
1/3 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray a loaf pan with no-stick spray.  In a large bowl, stir together the flours, brown sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, nutmeg, and ginger.  Add egg whites, pumpkin, buttermilk, applesauce, and molasses.  Mix with an electric mixer on low speed just until blended.  Then mix on high speed for 2 minutes.  Stir in walnuts.  Poor batter into prepared pan.  Bake for 55-65 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.  Cool bread in the pan for 10 minutes.  remove bread from pan and cool completely before slicing.
