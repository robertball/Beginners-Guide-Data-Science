Title:
Beverly's Green Chili Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/974113.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package Jimmy Dean hot sausage
1 small onion
1 4 oz. can diced green chili's
9 to 12 eggs mixed up
1 cup cheddar cheese
8 flour tortillas
hot sauce if needed

Directions:
Fry sausage and onion, when fried add green chili's and eggs, scramble until eggs are cooked. Warm up tortillas and fill with meat mixture, add cheese and hot sauce.
