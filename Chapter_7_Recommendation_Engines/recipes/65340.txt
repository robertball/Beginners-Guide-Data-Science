Title:
Sweet and Sour Smoky Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981720.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup prepared mustard
1/2 cup SMUCKER'S® Grape Jelly
1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
1/2 pound frankfurters, cooked

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine mustard, SMUCKER'S(R) jelly, and relish.
Heat over very low heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is hot and well blended.
Slice frankfurters diagonally into bite-size pieces. Add to sauce and heat thoroughly.

Description: 
SMUCKER'S® Grape Jelly is the secret ingredient in these tantalizing sweet and sour snacks.
