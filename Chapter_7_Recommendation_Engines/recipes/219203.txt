Title:
Tex Mex Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982473.jpg
Ingredients:
12 fresh mushrooms
1 tablespoon KRAFT Calorie-Wise Zesty Italian Dressing
1 small onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup finely chopped red peppers
1 clove garlic, minced
3 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro
1 tablespoon 40%-less-sodium taco seasoning mix
1/4 cup KRAFT Tex Mex Light Shredded Cheese, divided

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F.
Remove stems from mushrooms; chop stems. Heat dressing in medium skillet on medium heat. Add stems, onions, peppers and garlic; cook and stir 5 min. or until onions and peppers are crisp-tender. Stir in cilantro, seasoning mix and 2 Tbsp. cheese.
Spoon into mushroom caps; place in single layer in shallow pan.
Bake 15 min.; top with remaining cheese. Bake 5 min. or until mushrooms are tender and filling is heated through.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese
Description: 
Mexican-inspired stuffed mushrooms filled with cheese and red bell peppers make a quick great appetizer or snack.
