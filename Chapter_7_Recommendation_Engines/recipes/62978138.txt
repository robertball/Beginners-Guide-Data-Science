Title:
Raspberry Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/737813.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup white sugar
3/4 cup frozen raspberries, thawed, mashed, and strained to remove seeds
1 medium overripe banana
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon lemon extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Grease one loaf pan.
In a medium bowl, mix together flour, baking soda, cinnamon, salt, and nutmeg.
In a large bowl, combine eggs, sugar, raspberries, banana, and oil. Beat with an electric mixer at low speed for 2-3 minutes. Add flour mixture and stir just until moistened. Stir in lemon extract.
Bake for 1 hour, or until the loaf tests done. Cool on wire rack.
