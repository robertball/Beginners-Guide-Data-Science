Title:
Fruit Salad Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cherry pie filling or sour red cherries
3 T. Corn starch
1 1/2 c. water
1 3 oz. pkg. raspberry jello
1 1/2 c. sugar
1 small can crushed pineapple
Red food coloring
3 bananas
1 c. chopped pecans
2 pie shells (baked)
Whipped cream or Dream Whip

Directions:
Drain juice from cherries. Add corn starch and stir. Add water; place on fire and cook until thick and clear.
Remove from fire and add jello. Stir and add sugar. Add cherries and pineapple and small amount of red food coloring.
Let cool, and add bananas and chopped pecans.
When mixture just starts to congeal, pour into baked pie shells.
Chill until ready to serve.  Serve with whipped cream.
