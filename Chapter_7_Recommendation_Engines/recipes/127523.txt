Title:
Lantini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/859581.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ice cubes
2 fluid ounces vodka
4 fluid ounces pink lemonade
1 fluid ounce maraschino cherry juice

Directions:
Fill a highball glass with ice cubes, and pour in vodka and lemonade. Stir to mix, then gently pour maraschino cherry juice on top, and serve.
