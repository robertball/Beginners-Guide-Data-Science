Title:
Marinated Cucumbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c apple cider vineger
1/2 c water
sugar to desired sweetness
2 Tbsp. oil
Spice varieties: salt/pepper/garlic powder/dill/celery seed, whatever you like
2 cucumbers,peeled & sliced
1/2 to 1 onion, sliced, red,yellow or white

Directions:
Blend the first 4 ingredients plus any of the spices that you want or have on hand.
Add the cucumbers and onions and immerse as well as you can in the liquid.
Refrigerate.
