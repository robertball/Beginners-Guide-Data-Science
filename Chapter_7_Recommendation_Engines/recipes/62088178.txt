Title:
BBQ Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean ground beef
1 egg, beaten
1 cup fresh bread crumbs
1 medium onion, chopped
8oz can tomato sauce
1 1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 1/4 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce

Directions:
Saute chopped onions till soft. Set aside. Combine ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, salt, pepper and half the can of tomato sauce.  Mix well. Add onion and mound into a loaf pan.  Bake 30 mins at 350. Pour BBQ sauce over meatloaf and bake 1 hour longer.

A moist, delicious meatloaf that can utilize your favorite BBQ sauce!!
