Title:
Spinach and cheese pasta idea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One can of Hunts tomato paste (about a cup)
Half a bag of shredded mozzerella cheese (about a cup)
Half a stick of butter
Salt and pepper
Water (varries)to thickness
Half a cup of baby spinnach
Half a box of your favorite pasta

Directions:
Open tomato paste and pour it into a medium bowl.
Mix it with the butter
Microwave it until the butter has completly melted
Mix in the cheese
Microwave it until the cheese has gotten a thick and sticky consistionsy
Heat pasta as usual
Place the pasta into a large bowl
Mix in the spinnach into the tomato sauce
Use salt and pepper to season to your likeings
Pour the sauce into the bowl with the pasta and stirr
Enjoy!
