Title:
Jana's kielbasa and greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T + olive oil
1 lb greens (collard, mustard, chard etc., to your liking)
1 lb kielbasa or smoked sausage, sliced 1/4 in rounds
1 151/2 oz can white beans,  drained
1 onion chopped
1 red bell pepper chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 c chicken broth or stock
1 c tomato juice or tomato cocktail juice
1 t marjoram or Italian  herbs
Salt and pepper
Parmesan cheese (optional)
Hot sauce (optional)

Directions:
In a heavy dutch oven, brown kielbasa in olive oil on medium heat.  Remove to bowl.
Add more olive oil if pot is dry.
Add red bell pepper, onion, and garlic to pot and saute on medium until soft. (5-7 minuets). Remove vegetables to bowl with kielbasa.
Deglaze  pot with chicken broth, scraping crusty bits off bottom of pot.  Pour liquid over vegetables and kielbasa.
Stuff greens into Dutch oven and pour contents of bowl over top of greens.  Pour tomato juice over all and crush herb over top.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Cover and simmer for 35 minuets. Add beans and gently heat through. Serve with optional hot sauce and parmesan cheese.

Description: 
Hearty and delicious, this is a quickly prepared meal.  To save even more time, purchase a bag of already washed and chopped greens.  Simmering the greens keeps them nutritious and delicious.
