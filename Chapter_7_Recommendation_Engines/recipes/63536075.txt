Title:
Cinnamon Rolls Waffles with Cream Cheese Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/965822.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can store bought cinnamon rolls with cream cheese frosting
FROSTING:
Cream Cheese Frosting (from cinnamon roll can)
1 Tablespoon Corn Syrup
1 Tablespoon Water

Directions:
Spray waffle iron with cooking spray.  Place cinnamon roll slices on pre-heated waffle iron and close to cook.  You may want to "lock" your waffle iron (if available) to prevent the lid from coming up while they are cooking.
For the syrup, combine cream cheese frosting from can with 1 Tablespoon Corn Syrup.  Add 1 Tablespoon of Water.  Drizzle over hot waffles.
