Title:
NALLEY's Pasta/Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Spiral, Seasell or Elbow Macaroni
3/4 cup diced celery
3/4 cup diced green bell pepper
3/4 cup diced red onion
1/2 cup grated carrot
1/2 cup peas, frozen
1 1/2 tsp seasoning salt
1/2 cup diced dill pickles
1 1/3 cup Light Mayo
1 Tbsp mustard
1/4 cup sweet relish
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Boil Pasta, rinse and drain.
In a large bowl, combine pasta celery, pepper, onion, carrot, peas, salt and pickles, toss well.
Add remaining ingredients, mix well.
Refrigerate and serve, best made the day before!
