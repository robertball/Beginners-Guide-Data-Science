Title:
Candy Apple Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 HoneyCrisp apples, peeled & cored
No Sugar Added Apple Juice
1/4 c Red Hots Cinnamon Candies
1 tbsp Ground Ginger
Scant 1/8 tsp Ground Clove
Juice of one small lemon
Brown sugar to taste

Directions:
Chop apples medium-fine, toss in lemon juice and divide in half.
In slow cooker combine apples, spices, candy and just enough apple juice to not quite cover. Allow to cook, covered, on low to med heat stirring once in a while until candies are dissolved or they will burn. Increase heat to high for approx 4 hours.
When first half of apples have begun to lose shape, add remaining apples and cook uncovered on low to very low heat until desired thickness is reached.

Submitter: 
Kitchen Witch
Description: 
A slightly sweet sassy apple butter kids are sure to love!

Cinnamon Oil and red food coloring may be used in place of the candies.
