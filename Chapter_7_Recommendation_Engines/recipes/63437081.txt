Title:
VEGGIE LOVERS PIZZA DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. chopped broccoli
8 oz. veggie cream cheese
1 clove garlic (pressed)
1/4 c grated Parmesan Cheese, (divided)
1/2 c. mozzarella cheese, divided
1 c. speghetti sauce.

Directions:
Mix broccoli, cream cheese, garlic, 1/2 of the cheese and spread in small baking dish.
Cover with speghetti sauce
Bake 15-20 minutes
Remove from oven.
Cover with remaining cheese
Bake 5-10 minutes until cheese is bubbly and slightly browned
