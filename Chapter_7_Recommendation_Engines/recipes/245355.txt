Title:
Pumpkin Pie Pudding Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4563249.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
4 ounces cream cheese
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg, or to taste
1 cup milk
3/4 cup spiced rum
1 (3 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Beat pumpkin, cream cheese, cinnamon, and nutmeg together in a bowl with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. Mix milk, rum, and vanilla pudding mix into pumpkin mixture until combined; fold in whipped topping until blended.
Freeze pudding until set, 10 to 15 minutes. Spoon 2 tablespoons pudding into each glass.

Submitter: 
TINATED
Description: 
Spiced rum helps to create a not-so-traditional addition to your table or a fun adult treat to hand out Halloween night. These pudding shots are extra delicious with a layer of graham or spaced wafer crumbs.
