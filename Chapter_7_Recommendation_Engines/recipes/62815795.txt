Title:
Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup Mayo
2/3 cup Sour Cream
2/3 cup Buttermilk
1 tsp Parsley
1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Pepper
1/2 tsp Onion Salt
1/2 tsp Celery Salt

Directions:
Crumble Parsley in hand(rub together)
Combine all ingredients
Ranch is good for as long as the buttermilk- maybe a little longer
