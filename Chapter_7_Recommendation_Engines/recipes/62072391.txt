Title:
Basil Pesto Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Artichike hearts (drained)
Black olives (chopped)
Basil Pesto sauce (ready made is fine)
Pizza crust (pre cooked)
Mozarella Cheese (good quality, fresh or from the deli section)
Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 or according to crust directions.
Place crust on cookie sheet.
Spread basil pesto sauce on crust (completely cover).
Place Artichoke hearts and black olives on pizza.
Cut and cover pizza with Mozarella slices.
Bake for 8 minutes. For crisper crust remove pizza from cookie sheet at 8 minutes and place back on rack for remaining 2 minutes.
Cut and serve.
