Title:
Elsie's Potatoes - Deb's Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Lbs potatoes or 8 Lg potatoes
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese, softened
1 C sour cream
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp salt
pinch of pepper
1 Tbl butter

Directions:
Cook and mash potatoes very fine. Add all ingredients. Put into large, greased casserole. Dot with butter. Bake, covered at 350 for 30 minutes. If making ahead, cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate or freeze. Thaw before baking.
