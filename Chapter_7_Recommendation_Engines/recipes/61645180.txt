Title:
Healthier Four Cheese Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/281249.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
8 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup shredded Jarlsberg cheese
1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat Monterey Jack cheese
1/2 cup shredded part-skim Mozzerella cheese
1 (8 ounce) can evaporated milk
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain well and return to cooking pot.
In a small saucepan over medium heat, melt 8 tablespoons butter; stir into the macaroni.
In a large bowl, combine the Jarlberg cheese,  Cheddar cheese, Mozzarella and Monterey Jack cheese; mix well.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Add the evaporated milk, 1-1/2 cups of cheese mixture and eggs to macaroni; mix together and season with salt and pepper. Transfer to a lightly greased deep 2 1/2 quart casserole dish. Sprinkle with the remaining 1/2 cup of cheese mixture.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes or until hot and bubbling around the edges; serve.
