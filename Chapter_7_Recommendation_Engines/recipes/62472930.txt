Title:
Rivel Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped onion
2T butter
8 cups beef broth
1/8 tsp. black pepper
1# hamburger, fried

Directions:
Combine the flour, salt, and beaten egg and mix together with a fork until mixture is crumbly.Saute onion and celery in butter in a large pot for about 5-7 min. Add broth and bring to a boil.
Pour flour mixture into the gently boiling broth,  and cook about 10 minutes. The rivels will look like boiled rice when cooked.
Add fried hamburger and pepper.

Submitter: 
Maria Joy Schrock
Description: 
This is a Pennsylvania-Dutch recipe for homemade rice-shaped egg noodles cooked in broth.Modified from: Rivel Soup
