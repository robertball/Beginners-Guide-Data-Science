Title:
Key-Style Citrus Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tblsp. butter
4 skinless, boneless cicken breast halves (1 1/4 lbs total)
2 to 3 cloves garlick, peeled and thinly sliced
1 tsp. finely shreeded leme peel
2 tblsp. lime juice
1/4 tsp. ground ginger
1/8 tsp. crushed red pepper
1 orange

Directions:
in a large skillet, melt buttter ove medium high heat.  Add chicken and garlic.  Cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until chicken is tender and no longer pink (170 degrees) turning chicken once and stirring garlic occaisionally.
Meanwhile in small bowl, combine lime peel and juice, ginger and crushed red pepper; set aside.  Peel orange, reserving juice.  Cut orange in half lengthwise; cut croswise into slices.  Add lime juice mixture and any reserved orange juice to chicken in skillet.  Place orange slices on top of chicken.  Cover and cook for 1 to 2 minutes or until heated trough.  Serve an reserved drippings over chicken.
