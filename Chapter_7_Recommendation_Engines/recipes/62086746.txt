Title:
Country Apple Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large Granny Smith apples
2 cans crescent rolls
2 sticks butter
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
12 oz. Mt. Dew soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease 9 x 13 baking dish.  Peel and cut apples into 8 pieces each.  Seperate crescent rolls into 16 triangles.  Starting at the smallest end of the triangle, roll each apple wedge in dough.  Place in baking dish.  Melt butter in small saucepan and mix in sugar and cinnamon.  Pour mixture over dumplings coating well.  Pour Mt. Dew over dumplings.  Bake for 30-45 minutes or until golden brown.
