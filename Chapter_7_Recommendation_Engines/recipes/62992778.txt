Title:
Brown sugar body scrub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup sea salt
1/2 cup jojoba or olive oil
1tbsp of freshly squeezed lemon juice
2tsp of freshly grated lemon peel(zest)
1 tbsp honey
1vitamin e capsule

Directions:
Mix the sea salt and sugar in a bowl.  Add the oil, lemon juice, lemon peel, and honey.  Stir until completely blended.  Poke a hole in the vitamin e capsule and squeeze into the mixture and stir until blended well.
