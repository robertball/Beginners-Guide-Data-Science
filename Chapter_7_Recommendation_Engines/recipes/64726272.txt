Title:
Hungarian Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon oil
1/2 lb. boneless beef sirloin steak, cut into thin strips
1 medium onion, coarsely chopped
1 medium green bell pepper, coarsely chopped
1 ½ cups water
1 (14 1/2-oz.) can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 (8-oz.) can tomato sauce
1teaspoon sugar
3 teaspoons paprika (sweet Hungarian paprika where available)
½ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon caraway seed
¼ teaspoon pepper
4 oz. (1 1/3 cups) uncooked rotini (spiral pasta)

Directions:
Heat oil in 12-inch skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat until hot.
Add beef strips; cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until browned.
Add all remaining ingredients except rotini; mix well.
Bring to a boil.
Add rotini.
Reduce heat to medium; cook 10 to 15 minutes or until rotini is tender, stirring occasionally.

Hungarian stew of meat seasoned with paprika and other spices.
