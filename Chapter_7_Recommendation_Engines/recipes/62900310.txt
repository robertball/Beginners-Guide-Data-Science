Title:
Home-Made Dark Brown Sugar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/702894.jpg
Ingredients:
4 c. granulated white sugar
1/2 c. molasses

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl beat sugar and molasses till completely blended. Store in a sealed container.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Making your own brown sugar is easy, economical and stays fresh so much longer than commerical packaged brown sugar. This yields a moist dark brown sugar. For light brown sugar decrease the molasses by half.
