Title:
Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup shortening
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg
1/4 cup water
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup flour
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. soda
3 cup oatmeal, uncooked
2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Grease cookie sheets.
Cream together shortening, brown sugar, and white sugar.
To shortening mixture, add egg, water, and vanilla; beat until creamy.
Add flour, salt and soda; stir until blended.
Add oatmeal and chocolate chips; stir until blended.
Drop by heaping tablespoonfuls onto cookie sheets.
Bake until lightly browned; 8-10 minutes.
