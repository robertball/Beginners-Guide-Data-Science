Title:
German Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. vegetable oil
1 c. sugar
1 c. brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp. baking soda
2 c. flour
4 c. apples, peeled & diced (about 6 small apples)
Topping:
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. flour
3 T. oleo

Directions:
Mix cake ingredients together by hand.  Batter will be thick.  Spread into 9x13 greased & floured pan.
Mix topping ingredients together and until crumbly.  Mix and sprinkle on top of batter.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
