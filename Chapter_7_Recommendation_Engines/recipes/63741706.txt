Title:
Butter Finger Fudge - No Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese @ room temp
2 sticks butter @ room temp
5 1/2 cups powdered sugar
2 1/2 cups dry milk
10 oz bag Butterfinger bites / finely crushed
2 tsp vanilla
1 reg size Butterfinger candy bar / finely crushed

Directions:
1. Bring cheese and butter to room tempature
2. Add first six ingredients to a large bowl; mix w hands until thoroughly combined.
3. Press into buttered 9 x 13 pan
4. Top w additional Butterfinger candy bar
5. Refrigerate until firm. Cut into squares to serve. Store in refrigerator.
