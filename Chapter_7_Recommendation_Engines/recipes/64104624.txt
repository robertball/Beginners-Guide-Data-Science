Title:
Loose Meat Sandwich (Maid Rites) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 Tbsp Crisco
2 tsp salt (just enough to cover bottom of skillet
1 medium onion
1 Tbsp prepared yellow mustard
3 Tbsp Black pepper
1 Tbsp vinegar
1 Tbsp sugar
1 cup water

Directions:
Use cast iron skillet
Melt Crisco over medium hear and lightly salt bottom of the skillet
Break ground beef up in skillet and start crumbling into small crumbles.
Add chopped onion while browning meat
When meat is browned, drain off any fat and return to skillet.
Add mustard, vinegar, sugar, and just enough water to barely cover the meat in the pan.
Cook at a simmer, until water is all cooked out (15-20 minutes)
Adjust salt and pepper to taste
Steam buns and serve with a bit of mustard and pickles - can add cheese
