Title:
Dubliner Stuffed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/486716.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces of Dubliner cheese
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
4 ounces of bread crumbs
1 ounce of Mediterranean herbs
2 eggs beaten
1/2 teaspoon of salt

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 400 degrees
Grate half of the Dubliner cheese and cut the remainder into thin slices
In a bowl mix together bread crumbs, spices and shredded cheese
Slice the chicken breasts length wise down the middle so it resemble a pita pocket and insert the sliced cheese
Dip the chicken in egg batter, then into the bread crumb mixture.
On a lightly greased Pyrex or shallow cake pan bake for 35-40 minutes or until cooked through, then enjoy!

An authentic Irish dish that is perfect for any occasion.
