Title:
Cabbage Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head green cabbage, roughly chopped
1-2 lbs smoked sausage, sliced in rounds
4 baking potatoes, peeled and sliced
1/2 cup butter
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet (14" round or larger), layer sausage, potatoes and cabbage.
Add butter in spots til all gone.
Add salt & pepper to taste.
Cover and simmer stirring occasionally until potatoes are browning and tender. (Lid will NOT fit well until cabbage is cooked down.)

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
My husband grew up with this dish. Its easy all in one dinner.
