Title:
Fall Pumpkin Dip for fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz of cool whip
3 small boxes instant vanilla pudding *** dry mix only*** DON'T make the pudding***
one small can of pumpkin
serve Cut Apples, Graham Crackers...can hollow out a pumpkin and serve the dip in that

Directions:
You use 16 oz of cool whip, 3 small boxes instant vanilla pudding *** dry mix only*** DON'T make the pudding*** , one small can of pumpkin. Mix everything together and then add some pumpkin pie spice. Serve with graham crackers. SOOO soooo good!!!
