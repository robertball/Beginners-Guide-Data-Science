Title:
Pork Barbacoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. pork roast
1 can enchilada sauce
2 cloves minced garlic
1 small can diced green chilies
1 can Coke (not diet coke)
1 cup brown sugar
salt to taste

Directions:
Put pork in a crock pot with a little water and cook until very tender (about 8 hours on low).
Shred pork and add remaining ingredients to crock pot.
Cook one more hour on low.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Not unlike Café Río's Sweet Pork Barbacoa, this is tender and delicious and also incredibly versatile.
