Title:
Spiced Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123116.jpg
Ingredients:
2 T. cold water
1 egg white, slightly beaten
1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. cloves
1/4 tsp. allspice
2 c. pecans

Directions:
Mix the water with the egg white.
Dissolve the sugar into egg mixture.
Add the salt, cinnamon, cloves, and allspice.
Mix well and pour over the nuts.  Stir.  put on greased cookie sheet and bake at 250 degrees until golden - about 1 hour.  Stir in between.
