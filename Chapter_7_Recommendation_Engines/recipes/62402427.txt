Title:
Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked yellow squash, drained and mashed
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 teaspoon black peper
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups crushed Ritz crackers
3/4 stick butter or margarine
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup finely chopped onions
1 cup evaporated milk

Directions:
Cook, drain and mash squash.
Add remaining ingredients except cheese and mix well.
Pour into a greased baking dish.
Bake for 30 minutes at 375 degrees.
Add cheese and bake 15 additional minutes.

Submitter: 
Patricia Canerdy
Description: 
I have prepared this dish for many years and even my pickiest eaters love it.
