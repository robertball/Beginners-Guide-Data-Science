Title:
Dalton's Beans Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F932096.jpg
Ingredients:
½ pound lean ground beef
1  onion, chopped
½ pound bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 (15 ounce) can baked beans
1 (15 ounce) can lima beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
½ cup ketchup
½ cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon prepared mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a Dutch oven, saute the ground beef and onion for 5 to 10 minutes, or until beef is browned; drain excess fat. Stir in the bacon, baked beans, lima beans, kidney beans, ketchup, brown sugar and mustard. Mix all together well and cover.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 1 hour.

Description:
