Title:
Flourless brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1313070.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15oz can rinsed & drained black beans
1/3 cup chocolate cocoa powder
2/3 cup honey
1/3 cup coconut oil
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/8 tsp salt
2 eggs
1/2 package of semi sweet chocolate chips
1 milk chocolate bar

Directions:
In food processor, add all ingredients, except chocolate chips and bar, blend until smooth.
Pour into a pan, stir in chocolate chips
If you are adding walnuts-now is the time!
Cut up milk chocolate and sprinkle on top
Bake at 350 for 35 minutes - let cool for at least 10 minutes
