Title:
Sweet & Sour Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb pork tenderloin, 1 inch cubes
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup cornstarch
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 cup water
Oil
1/4 cups pineapple chunks
1/2 cup green pepper, cubed
1/2 cup carrots, diced

Directions:
Combine egg, flour, cornstarch, salt and water.  Dip pork cubes in egg mixture and drop in hot oil.  Fry 6-8 pieces at a time until golden brown.  Drain on paper towel and set aside.
Meanwhile, prepare sweet and sour sauce (see recipe).
Cook carrots in boiling water for 2 minutes.  Add green pepper and cook for 30 seconds.  Remove from heat and drain.  Combine with pineapple chunks.
When ready to serve, reheat oil and fry pork cubes for 2 minutes until crisp.  Place pork on serving plate.  Heap carrot mixture over pork.  Pour sweet and sour sauce over all.  Serve immediately over rice.
