Title:
Cherry Almond Biscotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 C. dried cherries
1/2 C. Amaretto
3 C. flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
4 tbsp. butter, softened
1 C. sugar
3 eggs, plus 1 for egg wash
2 tsp. vanilla
3/4 C. whole blanched chopped almonds
3 tbsp. sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven 375.
Heat cherries with Amaretto on medium low heat.
Stirring occasionally, 8 minutes.
Drain and reserve 2 tbsp. liquid.
Sift together the flour, baking powder, salt on a sheet of waxpaper.
Beat the butter and sugar until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
Stir in the eggs, one at a time.
Add the cherry liquid and vanilla.
Gradually mix in the flour mixture.
Fold in the cherries and almonds.
On a lightly floured surface, divide the dough in half.
Shape in 12 inch by 2 1/2 inch logs and flatten to about 3/4 inch thickness.
transfer to two baking sheets lined with parchment paper.
Brush logs with egg wash and sprinkle with sugar
Bake 35 minutes.
Transfer to two wire racks and let cool 20 minutes.
Cut each log on the diagonal.
Transfer to baking racks lay on sides.
Set racks on baking sheets and bake 300 degrees for 8 minutes then flip and bake again for 8 minutes.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Usually served with dessert wine, this Cherry Almond Biscotti is perfect with espresso or cappuccino.
