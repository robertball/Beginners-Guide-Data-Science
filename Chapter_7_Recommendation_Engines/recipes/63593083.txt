Title:
Turkey-Pepper Jack Panini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices whole wheat bread
1 TBSP butter
4 slices pepper jack cheese
4 oz deli sliced smoked turkey
2 large tomato slices
2 TBSP chopped cilantro

Directions:
1. Lightly spread one side of each slice of bread with butter. Place buttered side down on sheet of waxed paper. Layer 2 of the slices with 1 slice cheese, turkey, tomato, cilantro and remaining 1 slice od cheese; top with remaining slice of bread, buttered side up.
2. Cook in Panini maker 2 to 3 minutes or until golden brown and cheese is melted.
