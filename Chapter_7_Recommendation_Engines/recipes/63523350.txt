Title:
AUNT ELLIE'S CHEESECAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST:
1 1/2 cup graham crackers
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
dash of salt
1/4 cup melted butter
pack on bottom and partially up the sides of a 9x13 pan.
2ND LAYER
Cream 3 pkgs 8oz cream cheese
1 cup sugar
5 eggs (beat in 1 at a time)
1 tsp vanilla
Pour on top of crust and bake at 300 for 1 hr
3RD LAYER
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 pints sour cream
beat together and put on cream cheese after you take it out of the oven and then put back in the oven for 5 minutes
