Title:
Chicken Macaroni Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 medium onion, sliced
1-1/2 cups cooked chicken, diced
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp salt
6 cups chicken broth
1 cup macaroni, elbow or shell
1/2 cup milk
1/8 tsp black pepper
2 stalks green onions, minced

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute garlic and onion in oil until light brown.  Add chicken.  Season with soy sauce and salt.  Saute for 5 minutes.
Add chicken stock and bring to a boil.  Add macaroni, cover and lower heat to medium.  Cook for 15 minutes until macaroni is done.
Stir in milk and pepper.  Remove from heat.  Garnish with green onions.
