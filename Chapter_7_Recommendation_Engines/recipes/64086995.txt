Title:
Tiny Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium baking potato
1 egg
1 teaspoon chopped celery
1 teaspoon chopped onion
1/4 teaspoon dried dill
2 Tablespoons mayonaise
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Wash the baking potato, prick in 4 places.  Microwave for 5 minutes or until tender.  Cut the potato in cubes when cool enough to handle.  Place in a bowl large enough to hold all the ingredients.  Place bowl in an ice bath.  Break egg into a microwave safe bowl and break the yolk.  Place paper over top of bowl as it may pop.  Microwave 30-60 seconds or until completely cooked.  Place bowl in an ice bath to cool while you prepare the rest of the ingredients.  Chop celery and onion and set aside.  Once the egg and potatoes are cool, add the celery, onion, dill and mayonaise.  Stir well.  Chill until completly cool and serve.

Description:
