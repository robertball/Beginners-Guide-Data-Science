Title:
Easy Grilled Spicy Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1254668.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound raw peeled and deveined shrimp
¼ cup Sriracha
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon smoked paprika

Directions:
Set your grill to medium-high heat.
Place prepared shrimp in a large bowl then add in remaining ingredients and toss together.
Using large tongs place shrimp on top rack of the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side or until cooked through.
Prepare your guacamole and enjoy a great appetizer!.

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
I am not a big shrimp fan!  Not sure why but it’s true..   However my new easy grilled spicy shrimp has been a summer favorite and so easy with all of the entertaining we do at the lake house!
