Title:
Fried Bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 very ripe large plantain bananas
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 cup fresh orange juice
1/3 cup honey
1 tsp cinnamon
cr√®me fraiche

Directions:
Peel bananas and cut lengthwise. Saute in batches in olive oil, transferring with a slotted spoon to an oblong pyrex dish. Make a mixture of orange juice, honey and cinnamon. Pour over the bananas and bake at 300 degrees for about 15 minutes. Serve in bowls with a dollop of cultured cream.
