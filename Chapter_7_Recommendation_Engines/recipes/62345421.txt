Title:
Sundried tomato Blue cheese spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese such as Gorgonzola
2/3 cup sour cream
4-6 sun-dried tomatoes packed in oil, finely chopped
2 tablespoons finely chopped dill
1-2 tablespoons milk,optional

Directions:
Combine sour cream and blue cheese in a small bowl.
Add chopped sun-dried tomatoes and dill, stir until well combined.
Depending on the consistency, add milk to make a thinner spread.

Submitter: 
Kimmi
Description: 
This dip is perfect for veggie platters or spread on baguette for the most delicious appetizers.
