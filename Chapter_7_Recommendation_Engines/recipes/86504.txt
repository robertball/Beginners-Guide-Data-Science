Title:
Zesty Herb Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981254.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.06 ounce) package McCormick® Grill Mates® Zesty Herb Marinade
1/3 cup water
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
3 tablespoons white wine vinegar
2 pounds chicken parts*

Directions:
Combine marinade mix, water, oil and vinegar in small bowl. Place chicken in large resealable plastic bag. Add marinade; turn to coat well.
Refrigerate 30 minutes or longer for extra flavor. Remove chicken from marinade. Discard any leftover marinade.
Grill over medium-high heat 30 to 40 minutes or until chicken is cooked through, turning frequently.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Great taste is easy when you start with a Grill Mates Marinade. The blended herb flavor is accented by the perfect amount of heat.
