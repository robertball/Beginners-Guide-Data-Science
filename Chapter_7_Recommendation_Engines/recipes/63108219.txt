Title:
Hearts of Palm Salad (Fall Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
2 Tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
8 cups green leaf lettuce, torn
6 cups baby spinach, thinly sliced
2 pears, cut into wedges
1 (14 oz.) can hearts of palm, drained, rinsed, and sliced
1/3 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
To prepare dressing, in a small bowl vigorously whisk together olive oil, vinegar, and pepper
In a large bowl, combine lettuce, spinach, pears, hearts of palm, and cranberries
Pour dressing over salad and toss. Adjust ingredients as needed.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Lebow
Description: 
Try this with mixed leafy greens, added nuts, and different dried fruits.  Just a different salad when your starting to feel like it's gotten a little monotonous.  ;)
