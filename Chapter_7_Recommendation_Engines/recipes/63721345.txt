Title:
Honey Garlic Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
2 tbsp. corn starch
3/4 cup water
3 tbsp. soya sauce
1 tbsp. honey
2 cloves of garlic minced fine

Directions:
Wisk water, cornstarch,and soya sauce together in small sauce pan.
Add brown sugar, honey, and garlic.
Bring to a boil stirring constantly.
Pour sauce over cooked meat and serve.
