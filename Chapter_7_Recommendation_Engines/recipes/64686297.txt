Title:
Vermouth Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp olive oil
3 Tbsp butter
1 large onion finely chopped
1 lb mushrooms, thinly sliced
3 Tbsp tomato paste
5 C. Beef Broth
1/2 C sweet vermouth
salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place olive oil and butter in a large saucepan over medium high heat.
Add onion and mushrooms and cook until onion is soft and translucent, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes.
Stir in tomato paste.
Add broth and vermouth.
Bring to a boil and simmer 10 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper.
Ladle into bowls and serve with parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
PTRICK
Description: 
A simple yet elegant soup as a classy start to a meal.   The vermouth combined with beef broth give this a unique and pleasing taste.  Be prepared to be asked to share the recipe.
