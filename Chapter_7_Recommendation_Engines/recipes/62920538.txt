Title:
Mediterranean Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup chicken broth
3/4 cup uncooked rice
2 tbsp oil
1/4 cup raisens
1/4 cup almond slivers
1/2 tsp tumeric
3/4 tbsp soy sauce
3/4 tsp curry powder
1/2 cup pineapple chuncks (or chopped)

Directions:
Bring chicken broth to a boil.
Add all ingredients except the pineapple.  Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 20 minutes (if needed, adjust cooking time for the type of rice you are using).
Stir in pineapple and serve.

Submitter: 
NicMock
Description: 
A sweet and savory rice pilaf with pineapple, raisins, and almonds.
