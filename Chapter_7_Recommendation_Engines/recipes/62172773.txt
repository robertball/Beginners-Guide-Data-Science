Title:
Declicious Home Made Cherry Cheesecake. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 Inch) Graham Cracker Pie Crust
2 (8 ounce) packages Cream Cheese Softened
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 Cup of Sugar
2 Eggs
1 Can Cherry Pie Filling (or other filling over your choice)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Combine Cream Cheese, Vanilla Extract,  Sugar, and Eggs in mixing bowl. Mix until smooth and well blended.
Pour into pie crust and bake at 450 degrees for 45 minute OR until the top cracks.
Cool on top of the stove for a couple of hours, or in the fridge for 45 minutes to an hour, top with cherries (or other filling of your choice).

Description: 
Cherry cheesecake, made, as my mom taught me. Best I've ever had!
