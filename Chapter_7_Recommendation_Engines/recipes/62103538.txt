Title:
Cherry Bombs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lemon-lime soda, 2L
Pineapple juice, 6 oz.
Fruit Punch Flavored Beverage with a blend of Proline, Creatine and Taurine, 16 oz.
Grenadine, 1 cup
1 Lime
1 Pineapple, sliced with rind left on
Granulated Sugar, 4tblspn on a saucer

Directions:
In large pitcher, pour in the soda, pineapple juice, and fruit punch flavored beverage.
Stir together well
Slice lime into wedges. Set aside.
Using a long glass, wipe a wedge of lime around upper brim.
Dip into sugar.
Fill glass with ice.
Pour liquid mix into glass, leaving about ½” of space at top.
Top off drink with a few splashes of grenadine.
Place a slice of pineapple and a wedge of lime on brim.
Serve with straw

Description: 
Non-alocoholic blast of fruity. Nice on cold and hot days!
