Title:
Kendra's Easy Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of chicken gravy mix prepared OR 1 cup of gravy
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 tsp poultry seasoning
3 cups cooked chicken, cubed
1 1/2 cups frozen mixed veggies (peas, carrots and corn)
1 potato, precooked and cubed
1 cup bisquick (or other variety baking mix)
1/2 cup milk
1 egg

Directions:
1. Pre-heat oven to 375
2. Prepare the gravy mix or heat up 1 cup left over gravy on the stove. Add the can of condensed cream of chicken soup and poultry seasoning. Whisk until smooth.
3. In a 9X9 inch, lightly greased baking dish toss the chicken, the frozen mixed veggies and the potato with the gravy and soup mixture.
4. In a separate bowl mix together the baking mix with the milk and the egg until smooth. Spread this evenly on top of the chicken and veggies.
5. Bake in preheated oven for 40 mins and enjoy!
