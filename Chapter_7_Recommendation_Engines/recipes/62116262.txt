Title:
Starbucks Caramel Frappuccino Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup double strength coffee chilled (2 tablespoons of ground coffee per cup in your coffee maker)
3 tablespoons sugar
1 cup low-fat milk
2 cups ice
1/3 cup of caramel

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend on high speed until drink is smooth.
Fill two 16 ounce glasses, Top each glass with whipped cream and drizzle additional caramel over the whipped cream. Serve with a straw.
Leftovers keep well in the refridgerator!!
