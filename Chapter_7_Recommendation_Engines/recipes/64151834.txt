Title:
Honey Garlic Sauce for Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons corn starch
1 tablespoon honey
2 cloves of garlic
3 tablespoons sauce sauce
3/4 cup water

Directions:
preheat oven to 375
cut ribs, cover and back in oven and bake for 60 minutes - uncover at 45 minute mark and add water
mix all ingredients together except water and boil, set aside
when cooked pour sauce over ribs - keep warm or serve immediately
