Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked sweet potatoes, mashed or 40 oz. can
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 cup canned milk
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 tsp. of nutmeg, cinnamon, salt and vanilla
Topping:
1 cup corn flakes, crushed or Morning Tradition Cranberry
1/2 cup pecans, chopped
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Spray baking dish with Pam.
Blend well ingredients from Sweet Potatoes to vanilla. Place in baking dish and bake 30 minutes.
Reduce oven to 400 degrees. Spread topping over sweet potato mixture. Bake an additional 15 minutes.
