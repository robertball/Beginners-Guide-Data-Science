Title:
Fruit-Infused Teriyaki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/771869.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups soy sauce
1 1/2 cups mirin (Japanese sweet wine)
1 orange, juiced
6 ounces chopped fresh pineapple with skin
1 Granny Smith apple, cored and chopped
2 cloves garlic
1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
4 tablespoons SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine soy sauce, mirin, orange juice, pineapple, Granny Smith apple, garlic, ginger, and half of the SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes. Adjust sweetness to taste with remaining SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener. Strain through a fine mesh sieve; cool.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Especially nice with chicken, this Japanese style teriyaki sauce uses fresh fruit.
