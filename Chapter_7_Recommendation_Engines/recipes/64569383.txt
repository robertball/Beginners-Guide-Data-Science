Title:
Protein Packed Power Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp Chia Seeds
1/2 Tbsp Psyllium Husk Powder
31 grams Protein Powder (preferably Isopure Zero Carb Creamy Vanilla)
1 cup cold Unsweetened Vanilla Almond Milk (preferably Almond Breeze, 30 calories per cup)
1 1/2 cup cold, filtered water
16 grams roasted & salted almonds
45 grams of raw baby spinach (1 1/2 cups)
105 grams frozen pineapple bits
1 medium banana (broken into 2-3 pieces)
5 ice cubes

Directions:
Put cold almond milk and filtered water into blender pitcher. Add in chia seeds, psyllium husk powder and protein powder. Add in spinach, pineapple, banana and roasted almonds. Top with ice cubes. Blend for 1 to 1 1/2 minutes until all items are fully blended (use XL Smoothie setting on Blendtec).
Serve immediately. Do not store in fridge as items in smoothie will settle. Best if drank immediately.
