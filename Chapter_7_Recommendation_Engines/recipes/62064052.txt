Title:
cheesy mexican rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tblspns real butter or margarine
2 cups long grain white rice
1 can mild rotel
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
2/3 jar of salsa con queso
salt to taste

Directions:
melt butter.  saute rice in melted butter until golden; add chicken broth, rotel and bring to a boil.  stir in salsa con queso, cover, and bake in pre-heated oven at 375 for 30 minutes.  remove, stir, and test for doneness.  if rice isn't yet tender, return to oven for five minute intervals until rice is meltingly soft.  remove, stir, and serve.  great with taces, enchiladas, or any traditional mexican dish.
