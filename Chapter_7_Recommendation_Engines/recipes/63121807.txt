Title:
Pork Medallions (personal) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 ounce pork tenderloin (5 3oz slices)
1/2 cup wheat germ
1/2 tsp lemon rind
1/4 cup parmesean cheese
1 tbsp dried tarragon
1 black pepper
2 tbsp olive oil
5 ounces cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
mix wheat germ, lemon rind, parmesean, Tarragon & black pepper in a bowl.
Dredge tenderloins in wheat germ mixture.
transfer to plate.
Heat oil in skillet.
Add tenderloins and cook until no longer pink
remove to clean plate
add water to pan
add cream of mushroom soup
whisk and scrape up brown bits.
return tenderloins to skillet and simmer in sauce.
