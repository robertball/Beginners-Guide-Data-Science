Title:
My Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 (15 oz) can Tomato Sauce
1 (8 oz) can Tomato Paste
1 Tbs ground oregano
1 1/2 tsp basil
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp ground paprika
2 tsp onion powder
sugar
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp thyme

Directions:
Mix together in sauce pot and evaporate as much water as possible to reach desired thickness.  Add sugar or honey to taste
