Title:
Jan's Chocolate and Peanut Butter Stacked Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1099779.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, melted
1 and 1/2 cup sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla
4 large eggs
1 and 1/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup peanut butter
1 cup chocolate chips
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1 cup peanut butter chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Grease an 8-inch square baking pan.
Beat butter, sugar, and Vanilla together until smooth.
Add eggs one at a time beating well after each.
Add in flour, baking powder, and salt.  Mix until combined.
Remove half of the batter to another bowl.  To this bowl add 1/4 cup peanut butter and the chocolate chips.  Mix until well blended.
To the other bowl add the cocoa powder and the peanut butter chips.  Mix until well combined.
Spread the brownie mixture in the bottom of your prepared pan.  Top this with the peanut butter mixture - by spoonfuls.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 - 30 minutes OR until set and edges begin to brown.  Cool completely before slicing.
