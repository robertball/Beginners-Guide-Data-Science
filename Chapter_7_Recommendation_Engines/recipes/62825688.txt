Title:
Green Chile Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/668293.jpg
Ingredients:
4 sm. cans ortega green chiles drained
1 cup cooked sausage (optional)
1 lb. jack cheese
1 lb. Cheddar cheese
3 eggs
3 cups milk
1 cup Bisquick
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 13x9 baking dish. Drain chiles and dump into baking dish top with sausage and all of the cheese. Beat the eggs, add milk and Bisquick salt & pepper wisk until blended well and pour over cheese. Bake 40 min covered and uncover for the last 20.
