Title:
Chinese chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of pineapple juice
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup of soy sauce
3 cut chicken breasts
1 tablespoon of coconut oil
onions
bell peppers

Directions:
mix pineapple juice, brown sugar, and soy sauce together. Heat a skillet with coconut oil. add chicken. When the chicken is almost cooked, add sauce mix and chopped onions/peppers. Cover and cook until done. Serve over white/jasmine rice.
