Title:
Asian Dipping Sauce, My Own Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4568878.jpg
Ingredients:
1 jar all fruit spread, flavor of your choice
1-2 T. tamari sauce
1/2-1 t. Szechuan sauce (careful - Hot)
1-2 t. sesame oil
2 T. chili sauce
2 T. apple cider vinegar
2 or more t. Truvia, to taste

Directions:
All measurements are approximate.
Mix all ingredients together.
Adjust for sweet/spicy.
Store in a glass jar.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
The perfect blend of sweet, sour and spicy. Great for spring rolls, stir fry and pad Thai.
