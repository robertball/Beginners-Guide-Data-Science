Title:
Cilantro Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz container sour cream
1 bunch cilantro - stems included
2 teaspoons chipotle pepper powder
salt to taste

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a blender and process till smooth.
Refrigerate for at least one hour prior to serving and the cream will thicken.

Submitter: 
NUNPUNCH
Description: 
Cool, creamy sour cream blended with spicy cilantro makes a great topping for many Mexican dishes or as a dip for tortilla chips.
