Title:
Campfire Cones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cake cone
2 marshmallows
Hershey's kiss (optionsl)
Rolo candy (optional)

Directions:
Roast marshmallows over fire.  When cooked to desired doneness, place into cone.  If you want, slip a chocolate candy between hot marshmallows.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sweet Apron
Description: 
My son created our new favorite treat for bonfire nights.  Simple, fun and A LOT less messy than s'mores (even though we love those, too).
