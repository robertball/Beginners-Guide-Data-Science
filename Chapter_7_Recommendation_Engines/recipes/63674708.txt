Title:
Almond Butter and Fruit Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices whole-grain bread, toasted
4 tsp raw almond butter
1/2 ripe pear, such as Comice, cored and thinly sliced
1 tsp wheat germ
1/4 cup fresh raspberries
2 tsp whipped honey

Directions:
Spread 1 slice of bread with almond butter.
Layer pear slices over almond butter and sprinkle with wheat germ and raspberries.
Spread remaining slice of bread with honey.
Sandwich the slices together and enjoy!
