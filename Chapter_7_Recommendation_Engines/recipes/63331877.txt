Title:
ocean water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons water
3 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon artificial coconut extract
2 drops blue food coloring
2 (12 ounce) cans Sprite

Directions:
1 combine water and sugar.
2 microwave for 30-45 seconds and stir.
3 allow to cool, then add coloring and extract.
4 stir well.
5 mix into sprite and pour over ice. Print Recipe
