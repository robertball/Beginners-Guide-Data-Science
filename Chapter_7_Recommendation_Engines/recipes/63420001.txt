Title:
Sweet Potato Mallow Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound sweet potatoes (about 3 medium), or 1 17 ounce can
1/2 cup Sour cream
1 Egg yolk
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/4 teaspoon Ground mace
3/4 cup Miniature marshmallows

Directions:
Prick sweet potatoes and microwave for 8-9 minutes and peel. Beat sweet potatoes, sour cream, egg yolk, salt and mace in small mixing bowl on medium speed until smooth. Pour into 1 quart casserole dish sprayed with no-stick cooking spray (I use Pam, butter flavor). Top with marshmallows. Cook uncovered in 350 degree oven for about 30 minutes until marshmallows are puffed and golden brown.
