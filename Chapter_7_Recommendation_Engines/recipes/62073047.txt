Title:
cheesy garlic potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 medium potatoes
2 cloves of garlic
200 grammes cheddar cheese
50 grammes butter / margarine

Directions:
1. peel and chop potatoes into little bits.
2. boil until soft (about 20 mins)
3. drain water and set aside.
4. grate cheese
5. mince garlic and add to cheese
6. add cheese / garlic mix to potatoes with butter.
7. mash until creamy and serve!
