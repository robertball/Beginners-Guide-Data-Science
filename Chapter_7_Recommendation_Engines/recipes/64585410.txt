Title:
Hot Eggnog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, separated
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 c sugar
6 c milk, scalded
1 tbsp almond flavoring
1 tbsp vanilla flavoring
Whipped cream

Directions:
Beat egg yolks until light.
Add salt and sugar and blend.
Add hot milk and seasonings
Beat egg whites until stiff and fold into mixture.
Chill and serve with a dollop of whipped cream if desired.
(From JoAnne.  She says she doesn't separate the eggs...just beats them well.)
