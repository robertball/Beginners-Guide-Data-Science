Title:
Marmite and mushroom Jacket potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large mushrooms
1 large baking potato
1 tablespoon marmite
1 tablespoon butter
1 cup of grated cheese

Directions:
* Cook the baked potato to your liking.
* Cut open the potato in two directions.
* Mix the butter and Marmite together and spread inside the potato.
* Add the mushrooms to the potato.
* Lastly add the cheese.
* The potato can then be returned to the oven/microwave to melt the cheese if perfered.

Submitter: 
RICHMUSK
Description: 
One of the best toppings for jacket potatoes ever!!  So tasty you'll end up having them for lunch every day!
