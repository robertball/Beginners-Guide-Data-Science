Title:
Italian Beef Hoagies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978601.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (4 pound) boneless sirloin tip roast, halved
2 (.7 ounce) packages Italian salad dressing mix
2 cups water
1 (16 ounce) jar mild pepper rings, undrained
18 hoagie buns, split

Directions:
Place roast in a 5-qt. slow cooker. Combine the salad dressing mix and water; pour over roast. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours or until meat is tender. Remove meat; shred with a fork and return to slow cooker. Add pepper rings; heat through. Spoon 1/2 cup meat mixture onto each bun.

Submitter: 
Lori  Piatt
Description: 
You'll need just five ingredients to feed a crowd these tender tangy sandwiches. 'On weekends, I start the roast the night before, so I can shred it in the morning,' says Lori Piatt of Danville, Illinois.
