Title:
Angel Food Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 tablespoons corn starch
3/4 cups sugar
1 1/2 cup boiling water
3 egg whites
3 teaspoons sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Mix corn starch and sugar in saucepan.  Add boiling water, stirring constantly.  Cook until thick and clear. (about 10 minutes)  Add salt to egg whites; beat until stiff, then add 3 teaspoons sugar; then add vanilla.  Beat until egg whites are creamy.  Pour hot corn starch slowly into egg whites (beating constantly) and until stiff.  Pour into 9 inch pie shell.  Decorate with coconut. (colored coconut optional)  One large pie.

Submitter: 
Sue
Description: 
This was my mothers famous Easter dessert. I always looked forward to having it at our Easter dinners with the different colors of coconut for the holiday.
