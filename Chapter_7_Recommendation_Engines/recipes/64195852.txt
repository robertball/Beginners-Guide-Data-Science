Title:
Buffalo Chicken Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Sliders
2 tsp onion powder
2 tsp granulated garlic
1 tsp celery salt
3 lb boneless chicken thighs
2 lbs boneless chicken breasts
2 tsp olive oil
Sweat:
1 cup diced onions
1/2 cup diced carrot
1/2 cup diced elery
1 tbsp unsalted butter
1/2 cup low sodium chicken brother
1 cup hot sauce divided
Melt
4 tbsp unsalted butter
salt and black pepper to taste
40 cocktail buns - halved
For the Slaw - process
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup blue cheese crumbled
1 tsp white wine vinegar
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 tsp dijon mustard
Toss:
6 cups thinly sliced celery
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
For the sliders combine:  onion powder, granulated garlic and celery salt, sprinkle over chicken
Sear chicken in oil, 3 batches in a large pan over high heat until browned on both sides, adding more oil as necessary - transfer to pot or slow cookerSweat onions, carrot and celery in 1 tbsp butter int he same pan over medium heat until softened, 3-4 minutes - deglaze the pan with broth, scraping up any browned bits - transfer to pot or slow cooker - pour half the hot sauce over top
Cook in slow cooker on high for 2 hours, until chicken in fork tender - transfer chicken to a cutting board and shred with two forks - strain the liquid from pot or slow cooker and discard solids - return the chicken to the stock
Melt 4 tbsp butter and remaining 1/2 cup of hot sauce in a saucepan over medium heat, stir into slow cooker, set to warm and heat chicken for 30 more minutes, season with salt and pepper
For the slaw, process mayo, sour cream, blue cheese, vinegar, worchestershire and dijon in a food processor until smooth
Toss celery with dressing and parley just before serving
Serve on buns topped with slaw
