Title:
Mixed Green Salad with Strawberries & Kiwi Fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
2 bags (8 oz) bagged Riviera salad blend
1 quart strawberries, sliced
4 kiwis, peeled and sliced
Dressing
1/4 cup sugar
3 tablespoons each balsamic and raspberry vinegar
3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon poppy seeds
1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Directions:
1. Whisk dressing indgredients into glass container
2. Remove stems and slice strawberries thinly. Toss with dressing and marinate 1-24 hours
3. Right before serving peel kiwi. Cut in half lengthwise and then into thin slices
4. Place salad blend in large bowl. Pour strawberries with dressing over salad and toss to coat.  Top with kiwi, poopy seeds, and sesame seeds.  Enjoy!
