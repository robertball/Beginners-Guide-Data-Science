Title:
Mashed Potatoes with Horseradish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
2 tbs butter
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 cup sour cream
3 tbs horseradish

Directions:
Place potatoes in a large saucepan. Cover with cold water and bring to a boil. Cook 8-10 minutes, until tender. Drain and place in a large bowl. Add butter, salt & pepper. Beat until smooth. Add sour cream and horseradish, mix well.
