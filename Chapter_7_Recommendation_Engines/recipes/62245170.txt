Title:
Garlic Mashed Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lbs of yukon gold potatoes
1/2 cup chopped fresh green onion or chives
1/2 cup heated milk
1/2 cup cream cheese ( plain or herbed )
3-4 cloves of garlic crushed and chopped ( or to taste )
3 tbsp butter
1 egg lightly beaten
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup old cheddar cheese shredded

Directions:
Boil potatoes until tender, about 20 minutes.
Drain and return to pot.
Add 1/3 cup of the green onion or chives, milk, cream cheese, garlic, butter, egg, and salt.
Mash together until mixture is smooth.
Spoon into a greased glass baking dish.
Sprinkle with cheese and remaining green onion or chives.
Bake  at 375'F uncovered for about 40 minutes or until lightly browned.
( To make ahead, cool, cover and refrigerate for up to 24 hours, you will need to increase baking time to 55 minutes. )

Submitter: 
Chef Kris
Description: 
A great make ahead recipe, and a nice amount of extra flavor instead of your standard mashed.
