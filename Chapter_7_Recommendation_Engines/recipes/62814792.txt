Title:
Marzetti® Hawaiian Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups Cabbage, shredded (pkg slaw mix, w/purple cabbages and carrots
1 (8 ounce) can Pineapple, drained and crushed (fresh)
1/4 cup Raisins and 1/4 c. craisins
1/4 cup Nuts, sunflower seeds chopped
1/4 cup Coconut, flaked
3/4 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing

Directions:
In mixing bowl, combine ingredients.  Mix well.  Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Culicerto
Description: 
This tropical delight features pineapples, raisins, and flaked coconut. Close your eyes and you can hear the wind rustling through the palms.Modified from: Marzetti® Hawaiian Slaw
