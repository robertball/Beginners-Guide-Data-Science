Title:
Sam's Fresh Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 6oz. container of strawberry yourgart
3/4 C thawed whipped topping (sugar free for a healthier and low carb version)

Directions:
Mix ingredients until well blended, cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Serve with fresh fruit
