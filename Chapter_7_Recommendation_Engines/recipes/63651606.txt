Title:
Hawaiian Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag Cole slaw mix
15 oz can crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 Tbs brown sugar
1 tsp dried cilantro

Directions:
Mix Cole slaw mix, pineapple and onion in a large bowl.  Whisk remaining ingredients in a separate small bowl and pour over slaw. Mix well and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Momof2andPup
Description: 
This is excellent served with pulled pork. Its a nice mix of sweet, tangy and crunchy.
