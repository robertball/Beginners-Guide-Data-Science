Title:
Chocolate-Raspberry Martini by SEM Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/860066.jpg
Ingredients:
1 shot Chocolate Krystov vodak
1 shot Chambord raspberry liquor
1 shot regular non-flavored vodka (any brand)
1 shot club soda
float 2 fresh raspberries - or add to rim.
Can rim glass with chocolate flakes and sugar for extra presentation - but not needed since drink is sweet :-)

Directions:
Add 4 shots of liquid in shaker full of ice
shake long time
pour and enjoy!
