Title:
Hawaiian Haystacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1/2 to 1 cup of Milk
3 cooked chicken breasts
1 can pineapple tidbits
2 chopped bell pepper
3 chopped green onions
3 stalks celery, chopped
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup dried coconut
1/2 cup slivered almond
1 package chow mein noodles
4 cups cooked rice
Soy sauce to taste

Directions:
Cook chicken and cut into bite size pieces.
Add 2 cans of Cream of Chicken soup to chicken.
Add milk to soup mixture to dilute into a gravy-type sauce
Simmer
Cook rice with rice cooker, amount for number of people to feed.
Layer on plate: Rice, Chow mein noodles, Soup mixture, Pineapple, Cheese, Celery, Bell Pepper, Green onions, Coconut, Almonds, and Soy sauce.
Add more toppings to suit your taste.

Yummy, easy-to-make meal that can feed the masses.
