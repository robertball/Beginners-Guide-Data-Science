Title:
Banana Blueberry Mini Loaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1/2 c. oil
1 c. mashed ripe bananas
1/2 c. plain yogurt
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
2 c. all purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 c. fresh or fozen blueberries (do not thaw)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 deg.  Grease and flour bottoms of three 6x3 1/2 in. loaf pans.  Beat together sugar and oil, then add bananas, yogurt, vanilla and eggs, mixing well.  Add dry ingredients and stir just until blended.  Fold in blueberries.  Pour into prepared pans and bake 40 - 50 minutes.  Cool in pans 5 minutes then remove from pans and cool completely.

Submitter: 
Melissa Burnett Mandrona
Description: 
The mini loaves are a great size for gift-giving or for a small family.
