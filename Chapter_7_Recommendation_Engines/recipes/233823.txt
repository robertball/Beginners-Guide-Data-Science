Title:
Cranberry Juice Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1060467.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cranberry juice
1/2 cup bourbon whiskey
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle lemon-lime flavored soda
ice cubes, as needed

Directions:
Stir cranberry juice and bourbon whiskey together in a small pitcher. Pour lemon-lime soda into pitcher and stir gently.
Put ice into 2 pint-sized tumblers. Pour cranberry juice mixture over the ice.
