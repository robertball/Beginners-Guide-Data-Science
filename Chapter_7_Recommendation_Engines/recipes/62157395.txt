Title:
Buffalo Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14.5 ounces rotini pasta
1 lb. grilled chicken strips (cut into small pieces)
1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
1/2 cup green bell pepper, diced
1 cup red onion, diced
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 cup of chunky blue cheese dressing
1/2 cup buffalo wing sauce
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Cook and drain pasta. Set aside.
In a mixing bowl add chicken, pasta and veggies.
Add remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.
Refrigerate for 1hr. or more then serve.

Submitter: 
Andrew C
Description: 
For anyone who like buffalo wings this is a great change of pace from normal pasta salad. Really popular as a side dish for a barbeque or just about any gathering you can think of. You can substitute or add many different vegetables with this recipe.
