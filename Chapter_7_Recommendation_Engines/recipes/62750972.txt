Title:
Cinnamon Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
1 18 oz white cake mix
1 small (4 serving size) instant vanilla pudding mix
4 eggs
3/4 cup vegetable oil
3/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon cinnamon
Glaze
1/3 cup water
1/2 cup sugar
1 tablespoon cinnamon
6 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour bundt pan.
Combine all cake ingredients. Beat for two minutes. Pour batter into bundt pan. Bake for 50 minutes. Remove from oven. Cool cake in pan for 10 minutes on a rack. Remove cake from Bundt pan and continue to cool on rack.
Put glaze ingredients in small pan. Place pan over low hit; stir constantly and heat until butter and sugar are melted.
Move cake onto cake plate. Poke holes in cake and spoon glaze over cake using all of glaze. Brush glaze that accumulates at bottom of cake onto cake. Covering cake enirely.
