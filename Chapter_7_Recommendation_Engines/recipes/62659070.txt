Title:
Grilled Veggie & Cheese Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/590932.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large carrot
1 medium onion
1 cucumber
1 yellow pepper
1 clove of garlic

Directions:
Shred the carrot. Dice the cucumber. Throw the remaining veggies under your hand chopper. Mix in a package of reduced fat cream cheese.
Butter two slices of bread, add the mixture and grill. It is that easy...and that delicious!

Submitter: 
Army Wife
Description: 
This is my go-to quickie sandwich mix. It takes only minutes to whip up and can be refridgerated to grab in a pinch. Use any veggie you have laying around...my ingredient list is only a suggestion
