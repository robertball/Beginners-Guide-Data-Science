Title:
Basic Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1971276.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lbs ground beef
1 medium onion
1 medium bell pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 TBSP chili powder
2 tsp Salt
2 tsp Oregano
4 (14.5 oz) crushed tomatoes
1 (15 oz) tomato sauce
1 can light kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 can dark kidney beans, drained and rinsed

Directions:
Cook ground beef through and drain off fat.
Cut up onion and green pepper into bite size chunks.
Add all ingredients, except kidney beans into crock pot and cook on high for 6 hours.
Add beans about 20-30 minutes before serving to warm through.
