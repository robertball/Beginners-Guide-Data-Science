Title:
Tuna Whole Wheat Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/387985.jpg
Ingredients:
1 5 oz. can tuna
1 tsp oil (grapeseed or avacado or olive)
1 tbsp. low fat mayonnaise
1/4 red bell pepper
1 stalk celery
3 scallions or 1/4 small onion
1 c. whole wheat pasta
1 tbsp. lemon juice
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta to desired tenderness.  Drain and add 1 tsp oil and toss. Cover to stay warm.
While pasta is cooking...in a bowl break apart tuna into small pieces.  Finely chop red pepper, onion, and celery, add to bowl and mix.  Sprinkle with lemon juice and mix.  Add mayo and mix thorough.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Toss the tuna mixture into the pasta and eat.

Submitter: 
Elaine
Description: 
This delicious, high-protein meal is nutritionally balaned and works with the Eat Clean system and the Zone system.  This is 11 points on the WeightWatchers system.  You can reduce the points by reducing or removing the mayonnaise.
