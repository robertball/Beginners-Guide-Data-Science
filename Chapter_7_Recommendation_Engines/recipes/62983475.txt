Title:
Texas Applesauce Fruit Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cream together and set aside:
3/4 cup shortening
1 1/2 cups sugar
Dissolve 1 tsp baking soda in 2 cups applesauce then mix with shortening/sugar mixture
Sift together then mix with applesauce mixture:
2 1/2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp each: allspice, cinnamon, ground cloves
Stir in:
2 cups raisins
2 cups pecans

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugar and set aside. Dissolve baking soda in applesauce then stir in shortening/sugar mixture. Sift flour, salt, spices together then mix with applesauce mixture. Stir in raisins and pecans. Pour in prepared tube pan. Bake in tube pan in 325 degree oven for 1 1/2 hours. Cool before removing from pan.

Submitter: 
Sarah Story
Description: 
Granny Whitefield's Texas Applesauce Fruit Cake. A spicy, moist applesauce cake that tastes great with coffee for breakfast or as a sweet dessert after a meal.
