Title:
Smoothie for a Boss! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2308161.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
15 slices frozen peach, or more to taste
1 banana
3 1/2 ounces fruit yogurt
2 tablespoons chocolate sauce (optional)

Directions:
Pour milk into a blender; add peaches, banana, and yogurt. Blend mixture until smooth; add chocolate sauce and blend until smooth.
