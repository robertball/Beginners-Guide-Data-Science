Title:
One Bodacious Cheese Ring Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16oz sharp cheese, shredded
2C mayo
1lb fried, drained and crumbled bacon
4 finely chopped green onions
3/4t cayenne pepper
1C sliced almonds
1C strawberry jam

Directions:
Line a ring mould with almonds.
Mix all other ingredients except the jam together and pour over almonds.
Cover and chill overnight.
Turn out onto platter place jam in a dish in the centre.
Serve with onion crackers.

Submitter: 
SAJA2003
Description: 
Although an unlikely combination, the strawberry jam adds a touch of magic to this much asked for appetiser.If you don't have a ring mould, place in a dish and top with almonds.
