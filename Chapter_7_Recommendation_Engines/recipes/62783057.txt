Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c.- Butter
1t. - Salt
1t. - Vanilla
1/2t. - Baking Soda
3/4c. - Packed Brown Sugar
3/4c. - Granulated Sugar
1 - Egg
2c. - Flour
1pkg. - Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Cream together all the ingredients minus the flour and chips. Mix in the flour and then the chips. Scoop out onto pan. Cook 10-12 min.
