Title:
Regrigerator Cucumber Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4# cucumbers, about 6 large, cut into 1/4-in slices
3 med. onions, cut into 1/8-in slices
3 C sugar
3 C cider vinegar
4 t canning/pickling salt
1/2 t alum

Directions:
In large container, combine cucumbers and onions. In lg. bowl combine the remaining ingred., stirring until sugar is dissolved. Pour over cucumber mixture, mix well. Cover and chill overnight. May be refrigerated for up to 2 weeks. Yields about 2 1/2 qts
