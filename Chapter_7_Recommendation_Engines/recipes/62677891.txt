Title:
Kicken Chicken Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/648749.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8in. tortilla (flavored)
1 Tbl. flavored mayo (Chipotle or Hot & Spicy)
1 slice pepper jack cheese
6 slices shaved chicken (flavored)
2 slices, tomato
1 lettuce leaf
Red onion, thinly sliced

Directions:
Place all ingredients inside and wrap
Place into panini grill for three minutes
