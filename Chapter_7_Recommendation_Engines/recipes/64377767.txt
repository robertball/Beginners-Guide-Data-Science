Title:
Indian Spiced Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2192742.jpg
Ingredients:
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes, drained
2 Tbsp tomato paste
2 Tbsp grated fresh ground ginger
1 Tbsp curry powder
Kosher salt
Pepper
1 1/2 boneless , skinless chicken thighs, cut in half
1 small head cauliflower, cored and cut into florets
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1/4 raisins
1/2 cup cilantro, roughly chopped
greek yogurt and lemon wedges for serving

Directions:
In a 6 quart slow cooker, whisk together tomatoes, tomato paste, ginger, curry powder, 1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper.
Add chicken, cauliflower, onion and raisins to the slow cooker and stir to combine.
Cook covered until chicken is cooked through, 4 to 5 hours on high or 6 to 7 hours on low.
Serve with yogurt and lemon wedges topped with cilantro. if desired.
