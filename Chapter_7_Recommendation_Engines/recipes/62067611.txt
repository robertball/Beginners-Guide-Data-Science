Title:
Yummy Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package Mission Wraps Jalapeno Cheddar ( any other flavor will Do but this has been my Favorite!!
2lb ground beef browned
2 pks Taco Seasoning
Taco Sauce
2 Cans mexican Rotel
1 Head lettuce
2 cups Cheddar cheese shreded
4 small ripe avacodos
1 bunch Green onion tops diced
8 oz Sour cream
Jalopenos (optional)

Directions:
1. Brown ground beef, Strain meat, Add Taco seasoning per package directions.
2. Put tortias between to damp paper towels and warm in mirowave about 10 secs.
2. TAke tortia and spread some sour cream down center.
4. put cheese on top of sour cream
5. add meat, rotel, lettuce, avacados, green onion, and top with taco sauce.
6 pull little portion of bottom wrap up fold sides in. Eat!!!

Submitter: 
Dale Tammie Collins
Description: 
Tacos gone wild with rotel, Avacados, green onions and flavored wraps!!!!!!
