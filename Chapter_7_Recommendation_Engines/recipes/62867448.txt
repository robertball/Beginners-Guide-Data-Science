Title:
Rhubarb-Strawberry crsip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/688284.jpg
Ingredients:
Fruit mixture:
4 c. fresh, diced or 1 pkg. frozen rhubarb
1 pt. fresh strawberries
1 Tablespoon Honey
Crisp topping:
1/2 c. butter softened to a consistency to mix
1/2 c. brown sugar, packed
1 1/2 c. old-fashioned oats
1/4 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Mix fruit mixture with honey and put into 8x12 inch baking dish
Mix crisp topping together until it is mixed thoroughly, it will be crumbly. Spread over fruit mixture. If needed sprinkle some more oats to completely cover.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes or until brown.
Serve with yogurt or ice cream!
