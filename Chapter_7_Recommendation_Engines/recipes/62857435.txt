Title:
Chicken Macaroni Italiano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/683229.jpg
Ingredients:
13oz Jar Spaghetti Sauce
4oz Cream Cheese
1/4 cup Zesty Italian Dressing
1lb Chicken Breast cut in strips
1/4 cup chopped Red Onion
16oz Uncooked Macaroni
1tbl Oil
Garlic Salt to taste
Fresh Shredded Cheese to taste

Directions:
In a medium pan over medium-high heat brown chicken strips in oil. Sprinkle with garlic salt and add dressing. Reduce heat and simmer 8 minutes.
Add cooked chicken, spaghetti sauce, cream cheese, and onions to crock pot and cook on high for 2-3 hours stirring occasionally.
30 minutes before serving, boil noodles in water until al dente (about 8 minutes). Drain and add to crock pot.
When serving top with fresh shredded cheese.
