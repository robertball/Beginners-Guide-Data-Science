Title:
Imogene McQuade's Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, hard-cooked, chopped
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 teaspoon horseradish
1/4 teaspoon prepared mustard
salt and pepper

Directions:
Place eggs in saucepan; cover with cold water .  Heat to boiling; reduce heat and allow to barely simmer about 15 minutes.  To stop cooking, place eggs under cold water a few seconds.  Peel hard-cooked eggs and chop. Mix with other ingredients.  Adjust amounts to taste.

Submitter: 
Amy Coats
Description: 
Wonderful egg salad that my Mom and Grandma used to make.  Perfect for Spring.
