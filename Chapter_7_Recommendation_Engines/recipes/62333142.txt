Title:
Lemon Chiffon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 oz) package Lemon Flavor Gelatin
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon grated lemon rind
3/4 cup boiling water
1 1/2 cups crushed ice
1 1/2 cups thawed Cool Whip, (or prepared Dream Whip or whipped cream)
Thin lemon slices (optional, for garnish)

Directions:
1.  Combine gelatin, sugar, lemon juice, lemon rind, and boiling water in electric blender container.
2. Cover and blend at low speed until gelatin and sugar are dissolved, about 1 minute.
3.  Add ice and whipped topping; cover and blend at high speed until ice is melted, about 1 minute.
4.  Pour into serving bowl or individual dessert glasses. Chill until set, about 30 minutes.
Garnish with additional prepared whipped topping and thin lemon slices, if desired.
