Title:
Mashed Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T onion, chopped
1 T margarine
2 cup milk
1 1/2 c mashed potatoes
1/8 t celery salt
1/8 t pepper
1/4 c cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
In a saucepan, saute the onion in butter until tender. Add milk, potatoes, salt and celery salt if desired and pepper; heat through. Garnish with chive
