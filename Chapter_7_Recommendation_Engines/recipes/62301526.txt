Title:
Spicy Cayenne Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tsps cayenne pepper
1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
1/4 cup minced garlic
2 tbsp lemon juice
salt to taste
1 tsp sugar
1 tbsp chopped dill
2 tbsp chopped parsley

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine all ingredients except butter and stir until spices absorb lemon juice and a loose paste is formed.  Add butter and stir until mixture is smooth and pink in color.  Allow to sit 15-20 min for flavors to intersify.  Butter can be prepared up to 7 days ahead and refrigerated or frozen up to 1 month.
