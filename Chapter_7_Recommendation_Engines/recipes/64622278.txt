Title:
Sausage Broccoli Cheese soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion diced
1-2 lbs sausage
2 carrots grated
5 T butter
5 T flour
2 cups milk
8 cups chicken broth
24 oz broccoli (frozen is fine)
8 oz cream cheese
8-16 oz cheddar cheese
2 t mustard powder
1 t fresh grated nutmeg
1 package frozen ravioli
1-2 cans white beans

Directions:
Cook sausage, onions, and carrots in your soup pot. Drain
Melt butter, and flour to form a roux, slowly add milk until incorporated, add chicken broth.
Bring to a boil stirring often, turn down to a very low simmer , add in your cheeses.
Add everything to the pot except ravioli, bring up to a very slow simmer, add ravioli and cook until they float.
Serve hot with rosemary bread, croutons, or crackers.
