Title:
Really Rich Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup unsalted butter at room temperature
1 Cup (packed) dark brown sugar
6 Large eggs
2 Cups all purpose flour
2 Tsp. baking powder
1/4 Tsp. ground nutmeg
Pinch ground allspice
Pinch of salt
3 Ripe bananas, mashed
1 Tsp. pure vanilla extract

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350F.  Grease and flour a 9X5 inch loaf pan.
2. Cream the butter and sugar in a large bowl.  Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
3. Combine in a small bowl all of the dry ingredients and then add them to the butter and egg mixture. a bit at a time, beating well after each addition.
4. Add the bananas and vanilla and combine well.
5. Pour the batter into the loaf pan and bake for 1 1/2 hours, until a skewer comes out clean.
6. Cool the cake in the pan for 10 minutes and then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.

Submitter:

Description: 
My sister gave me this.  It is so decadently rich and wonderful and especially nice toasted and spread with a little salty butter, or even cream cheese.
