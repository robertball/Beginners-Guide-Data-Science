Title:
Toffee Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/404832.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg yolk
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups flour
nuts
chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
mix together the butter, brown sugar, egg yolk, vanilla and flour.
spread and pat flat on jelly roll pan. (it does not come out nearly as smooth as a brownie batter..it takes a little more effort to spread evenly)
Bake in oven for about 15 minutes
Take pan out and spread desired amount of chocolate chips on top (about 1 cup is good)
Put pack in oven for 1-2 minutes-when chocolate chips are shiny.
Take pan out of oven and spread chocolate with spatuala and spread nuts on top if desired. wait a while (a half hour or so) for the chocolate to harden. enjoy!
