Title:
Chicken Cheese Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
1	Med.	Onion, chopped
1	Clove	Garlic, minced
½	Cup	Butter
½	Cup	Flour
1	Tsp.	Salt
2	Cups	Chicken Broth
1 ½	Cups	Milk
4	Cups	Mozzarella Cheese, shredded and divided
1	Cup	Parmesan Cheese, grated, and divided
1	Tsp.	Dried Basil
1	Tsp.	Dried Oregano
½	Tsp.	White Pepper
2	Cups	Ricotta Cheese
1	Tbsp.	Fresh Parsley, minced
9		Lasagne noodles, cooked, and drained
20	oz.	Frozen Spinach, thawed and well drained
2	Cups	Cooked Chicken, cubed

Directions:
Instructions:
1. In a saucepan, melt butter.
2. Add Onion and Garlic.
3. Sauté until tender.
4. Stir in Flour and Salt.
5. Cook until bubbly.
6. Gradually stir in Broth and Milk.
7. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
8. Boil 1 minute.
9. Stir in half (2 cups) of the Mozzarella Cheese.
10. Add half ( ½ cup) of the Parmesan Cheese, stir.
11. Add Basil, Oregano, and Pepper, stir.
12. Set aside.
13.	In a bowl, combine Ricotta Cheese, Parsley and remaining Mozzarella Cheese.
14.	Set aside.
15.	Spread ¼ of Cheese Sauce into greased 13x9 baking dish.
16.	Cover with 1/3 of Noodles.
17.	Top with ½ of Ricotta Mixture.
18. Top with ½ of Spinach.
19. Top with ½ of Chicken.
20.	Cover with ¼ of Cheese Sauce.
21.	Top with 1/3 of Noodles.
22.	Repeat layers in order.
23.	End with Noodles and Cheese Sauce.
24.	Sprinkle remaining Parmesan Cheese over all.
25.	Bake at 350 F, uncovered for 35-40 minutes.
26.	Let stand 15 minutes.
