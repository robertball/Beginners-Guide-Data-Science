Title:
spicy pineapple upside down cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz can drained sliced pinapple (reserve all the juice)
8-10 marshino cherries
1 cup dark brown sugar
1/2 cup melted butter
gingerbread:
1 cup sugar
1 cup butter
2 eggs
2 cups molasses
5 cups all-purpose flour
1tbs baking soda
2 tsp ground cinnamon
21/2 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp ground cloves
1 tsp salt
1 cup hot water
1 cup reserved pinapple juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  prepare 13x9 pand with melted butter, darkbrown sugar pinapples, and put cherriesin middle of pineapple slices...set aside
In a large bowl cream together the butter and sugar, beat in the eggs and molasses.
Mix all the dry ingredients add to the creamed mixture, stir in the hot water and the reserved pineapple juice.  pour into the prepared pan.
Bake for about an hour or until tooth pick comes out semi-clean, cool for about 10 minutes then turn upside down on your favorite platter.
Serve warm and with whipped cream

Submitter: 
Michelle Satterfield
Description: 
this very easy and yummy cake is an old New England recipe from my grandmother using gingerbread instead of the standard yellow cake
