Title:
Grilled Tilapia with Smoked Paprika Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
2 Teaspoon Smoked Paprika
1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Freshly Ground Black Pepper
4 (6-ounce) Tilapia Fillets
Cooking Spray

Directions:
1. Heat a large nonstick grill pan over medium-high heat.
2. Combine oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a small bowl, stirring well.
3. Brush fish evenly with oil mixture.
4. Coat pan with cooking spray and add fish to pan. Cook 4 minutes on each side or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
