Title:
Darn Good Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 2lb 10oz package Costco chicken, DIced
1 Cup Pecans
1 head celery cut into 1" slices
2 rounded teaspoons curry powder
1.5 teaspoon Fresh dill weed minced
mayo to taste 2 cups +/-

Directions:
Add pecans to food processor and pulse until minced.  Add pecans to large bowl.
Add chopped  celery to food processor and pulse until finely chopped and add to nuts.
Add dillweed and curry powder to nuts and celery and mix.  Finely dice chicken and add to bowl and mix.  Add Mayo top taste.
