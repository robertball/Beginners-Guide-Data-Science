Title:
Baked Pimento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
1 (4 oz) jar diced pimento, drained
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 tsp finely grated onion
1/4 tsp ground red pepper
1 (8 oz) block extra sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 (8 oz) block sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Stir together first 5 ingredients in large bowl, stir in cheeses. Spoon minture into a lightly greased 2-quart or 11 x 7 baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
