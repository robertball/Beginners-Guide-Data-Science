Title:
Curry Roasted Chickpeas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can chickpeas
1/2 tbs olive oil
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp curry powder
1/4 tsp black powder
pinch of garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Strain and dry off chickpeas
Mix together chickpeas, spices, and oil
Bake chickpeas for 45 minutes and mix every 15 for maximum crispiness
