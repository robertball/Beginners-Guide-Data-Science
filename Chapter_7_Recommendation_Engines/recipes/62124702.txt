Title:
Shrimp and Lemon Cream with Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pd large or jumbo Shrimp, peeled and deveined
1-2 shallots, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbs olive oil
1/2 cup wine
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 cup heavy cream
Juice and zest from 1 lemon
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1/2 cup sundried tomato, chopped
1/2 cup fresh Basil, torn
2 tsp crushed red pepper
salt and pepper
1 pd penne, cooked and drained

Directions:
heat oil in pan, add shallots and garlic
add chicken broth and lemon juice
add wine and simmer until reduced by half
add shrimp and cook just through
add crushed red pepper, s&p, and zest
lower heat and add cream, simmer but don't boil
add parm cheese, stir as it thickens slightly
remove from heat
toss with pasta, tomato and basil
sprinkle with more cheese, and crushed red pepper
serves 4 main courses

Submitter: 
KMOMMYZ
Description: 
Here is a decadent pasta dish perfect for the most elegant occasion yet simple enough for a weeknight meal!  A creamy sauce gets heat from wine and crushed red pepper all tossed with Shrimp and fresh Basil...Delicious!
