Title:
Soft Cut Out Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup soft butter
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 eggs
3 cups of flour
3 teaspoons baking powder
1 tablespoon milk
2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Cream together the butter and sugar. Add the eggs and mix. Add the flour and baking powder and mix. Add the milk and vanilla and mix. Chill over night. Roll out and use cookie cutters. Place on baking sheet and bake at 350 for 6-8 minutes
