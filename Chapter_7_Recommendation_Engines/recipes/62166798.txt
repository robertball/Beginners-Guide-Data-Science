Title:
Bionico (Mexican Fruit in Cream) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/329329.jpg
Ingredients:
Cream  fruit topping:
1-14oz can La Lechera (sweetened condensed milk)
1-16oz sour cream
1 Squirt honey
1/2 Teaspoon Cinnamon
Fruit: (You can use what you like)
2 Fuji Apple
1/2 Cantalope
1/2 Pint Strawberry
3 Banana
Toppings:(optional)
Granola
Shredded Coconut
Raisins

Directions:
In a bowl combine Sweetened Condensed Milk, Sour Cream, a Squirt of honey and tspn of cinnamon together, mix together. Cut fruit up and place in bowl & top with the cream mixture. Then sprinkle raisins, granola & coconut on top. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is served at many local mexican ice cream shops.  It has Stawberries, Cantalope, Fuji Apple and Banana all topped with a cream that you cant resist.  You may sub different fruits.
