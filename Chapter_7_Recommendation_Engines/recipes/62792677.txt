Title:
Honey Balsamic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/652674.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp dried rosemary
salt and pepper to taste
2-3 lbs bone-in chicken (I prefer thighs)

Directions:
Combine the first five ingredients in a gallon-size resealable bag.
Season chicken Add salt and pepper to chicken pieces (to taste), then add to marinade. Marinate chicken in the refrigerator for 2-8 hours.
Bake at 375 for 35-40 minutes, until chicken juices run clear.

Submitter: 
BAGRUBA
Description: 
Short on ingredients, this marinated chicken bakes up tender and flavorful.  It's good enough for company.
