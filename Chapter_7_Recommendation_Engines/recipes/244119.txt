Title:
Green Hornet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3227357.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 fluid ounce whiskey
3/4 fluid ounce vodka
3/4 fluid ounce gin
3/4 fluid ounce green creme de menthe
1/2 fluid ounce cola-flavored carbonated beverage (such as Coca-Cola®), or more to taste
1/2 fluid ounce lemon-lime carbonated beverage (such as 7Up®), or to taste
1/2 fluid ounce sweet and sour mix, or to taste
1 cup crushed ice, or as desired

Directions:
Mix whiskey, vodka, gin, creme de menthe, cola-flavored beverage, lemon-lime flavored beverage, and sweet and sour mix together in a cocktail shake. Fill a glass with ice; pour drink over ice.

Submitter: 
KIM
Description: 
A recipe from my Grandma Briere who was a bartender for 30 years. Original price for this drink in the 70s was $1.60. Garnish with an orange slice, cherry, or any other fruit.
