Title:
Christmas cookies-pecan fingers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2lb butter
2Tbsp powdered sugar
2C sifted flour
2tsp vanilla
2Tbsp cold water
3/4C chopped pecans

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar, add flour adn cream again. Add water, vanilla and pecans. Roll into date shape and bake at 325 for 20min. Roll in powdered sugar while hot.
