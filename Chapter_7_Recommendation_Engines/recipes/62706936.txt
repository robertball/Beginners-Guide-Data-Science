Title:
Greek Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box bowtie pasta
1 english cucumber
2 medium tomatoes
1/4 cup diced red onion
1 tsp minced garlic
1 cup hummus
4-6 leaves fresh basil
1/2 cup feta cheese
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Cook bowtie pasta, drain and chill. Peel and dice cucumber. Seed and dice tomatoes.  In a medium bowl, mix diced cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and garlic.  Add pasta.  Toss with hummus.  Add finely chopped fresh basil, gently mix.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Increase or decrease the amount of hummus to adjust the wetness or dryness of the salad.
