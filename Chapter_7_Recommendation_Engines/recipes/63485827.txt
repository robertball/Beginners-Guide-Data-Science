Title:
Pumpkin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2 cups shortening
2 eggs
2 cups pumpkin (15oz can)
dry ingred:
1tsp cinammon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
4 cups flour
4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp allspice, cloves
1 12 oz package of chocolate chips

Directions:
Cream together sugar and shortening, then eggs and pumpkin.  Sift together dry ingredients and combine.  Fold in chips.  Splat cookies on sheet.
Refrigerate at least 1 hr to firm up dough.
bake at 325F for 15 mins
use an icecream scoop or large spoon
use parchment paper on cookie sheet
