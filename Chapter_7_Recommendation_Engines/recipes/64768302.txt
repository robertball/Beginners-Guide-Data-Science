Title:
German Ground Steak  / " Fleisch Pflanzerl" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Ground Pork/Beef
16 oz Finely Chopped Onion
3 tbsp Butter
Oil for Frying
1 Teaspoon of Salt
1/4 Teasponn of Pepper
1 oz of Chopped,  Fresh Parsley
1/2 oz of Celery Green
5 oz of  Bread Crumbs;  or ( * day old bread;  minced )
2 Eggs
1/2 cup Water.

Directions:
Fry the Onion in the Butter until light Brown. Too the Ground add the Chopped Parsley, Salt and Pepper, Onions, Bread crumbs,  Scrambled Eggs.  Mix a bit; check for consistency;  add 1/2 the water,  mix. add the rest if necessary.  Set aside for 1/2 hour.  Next;   Form 4 rounds,  smooth with wet Hands and press Slightly down to an 1 1/2 Thickness.   Fry on both sides until Brown but not done.  Finish in a med- Hot oven for another 20 min. or until Done "Through"  Garnish with Chopped Parsley ; Serve with Macaroni Noodles & Tomato Sauce, or  Mashed Potatoes and Butter.   Along;  a White Rye Bread.
