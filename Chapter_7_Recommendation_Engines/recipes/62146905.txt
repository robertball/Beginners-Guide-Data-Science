Title:
Nonnie's Incredible Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/693368.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c. white wine vinegar
1 tbsp. lemon juice
Pinch cayenne pepper
Ground pepper, to taste
1/2 c. extra virgin olive oil
7 tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
1 clove garlic, peeled and halved
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1/2 tsp. ground mustard
1 large head Romaine Lettuce and other ingredients of choice

Directions:
In a stainless steel bowl, combine vinegar, lemon juice and spices.  Whisk until thoroughly blended.  Gradually whisk in olive oil.  Mix in grated cheese and garlic.  Let stand at room temperature at least 2 hours.
Wash and drain 1 large head Romaine lettuce.  Tear leaves into bite-sized pieces.  Pour dressing over salad and toss well.  Serve immediately.  Add, if desired:  tomato pieces, mushrooms, cucumber, red onion, croutons, dried cranberries.

Submitter: 
Beth Zelle
Description: 
Throw away that bottled salad dressing!! This is so simple and as good as any quality Italian Restaurant Salad Dressing.
