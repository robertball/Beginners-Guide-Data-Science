Title:
Taco Soup I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound lean ground beef or ground turkey
1 chopped onion
1 1/2 cups water
1 (28 ounce) can chopped  tomatoes, with juice
1 (14 ounce) can kidney beans with liquid
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 package taco seasoning mix
1 (16 ounce) can kernel corn, with juice
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese (optional)
1 (12 ounce) package corn tortilla chips (optional)
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream (optional)

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook ground beef and onion until meat is evenly brown; drain excess fat. Mix in water, tomatoes, kidney beans, tomato sauce, corn and taco seasoning mix. Cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
Ladle hot soup into serving bowls. Pass cheese, tortilla chips, and sour cream to top each serving.
