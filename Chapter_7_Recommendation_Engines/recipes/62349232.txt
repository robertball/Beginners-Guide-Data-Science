Title:
Omelet Muffins "Dr. Gott's No Flour, No Sugar Diet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large eggs
1 1/2 cups egg substitute
4 ounces diced ham or canadian bacon
1 cup fresh or frozen corn kernels
3 green onions, thinly sliced
1 tsp. crumbled dried thyme
3 ounces soft feta cheese, crumbled
Season to taste (up to quantity state)
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
1.  In large bowl, lightly beat eggs and whisk in egg substitue. Add ham, corn, green onions, thyme, cheese, salt and pepper, and stir until well combined.
2.  Spray a 12 cup muffin tin with cooking spray.  Ladle egg mixture into cups, dividing evenly.
3.  Bake in preheated oven for about 25 minutes, until tops are puffed and golden and eggs are set.  Allow to cool slightly, then run a knife around the edge of the eggs and remove them from the tin.  Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate up to 3 days.  to serve, heat on hgih in microwave for 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
Maureen
Description: 
These are tasty, flourless baked scrambled eggs.  Try adding leftover vegetables such as sauteed mushrooms, steamed broccoli or roasted peppers for variation. Can be reheated.
