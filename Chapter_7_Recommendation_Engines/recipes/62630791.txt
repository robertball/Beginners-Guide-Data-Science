Title:
WHOLESOM GRANOLA BARS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 CUP (1&1/2 STICKS) BUTTER, SOFTENED, DIVIDED
2 TABLESPOONS POWDERED SUGAR
1 CUP ALL-PURPOSE FLOUR
1/2 CUP SEEDLESS RASBERRY JAM
1/4 CUP HONEY
2 TABLESPOONS PACKED BROWN SUGAR
1 TEASPOON VANILLA EXTRACT
1 CUP QUICK-COOKING ROLLED OATS
1 CUP GRANOLA(WITHOUT DRIED FRUIT)
1/2 CUP CHOPPED PECANS
1/2 CUP SEMI-SWEET CHOCOLATE CHIPS

Directions:
PRE-HEAT OVEN TO 350 DEGREES F, 175 DEGREES C). IN MIXING BOWL CREAM 1/2 CUP BUTTER AND POWDERED SUGAR, STIR IN FLOUR. WITH LIGHTLY FLOURED FINGERS, PAT DOUGH INTO UNGREASED 9X9 INCH BAKING PAN. BAKE 10 MINUTES. REMOVE, COOL 10 MINUTES. SPREAD JAM EVENLY OVER CRUST. COMBINE IN BOWL REMAINING 1/4 CUP MELTED BUTTER, HONEY, BROWN SUGAR AND VANILLA. STIR IN OATS, GRANOLA, PECANS, AND CHOCOLATE. PAT FIRMLY OVER JAM LAYER. BAKE 30 MINUTES OR UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN. COOL AND CUT INTO SQUARES.
