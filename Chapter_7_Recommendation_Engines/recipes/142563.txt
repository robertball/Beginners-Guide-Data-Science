Title:
Cinnamon Apple Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986639.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (3/4 inch thick) boneless pork chops
Vegetable oil
1 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 1/2 cups applesauce
2 tablespoons cinnamon red hot candies

Directions:
Heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Brush chops lightly with oil and brown chops on both sides, turning once, about 3-4 minutes. Sprinkle chops with pepper, add broth and lemon juice. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer gently for 5 minutes. To serve, top with applesauce and cinnamon candies.
Chunky applesauce with cinnamon red hot candies: In small saucepan, stir together 1 1/2 cups applesauce and 2 tablespoon candies. Heat over medium heat, stirring often, until candies are melted; stir well to thoroughly combine.

Submitter: 
National Pork Board
Description: 
Give your applesauce a red hot twist to update this family favorite standard. Serve with roasted red potatoes and steamed green beans.
