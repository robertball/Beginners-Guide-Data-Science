Title:
Hubby's Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3lbs. or so pot roast
1 bag baby carrots
3-4 large white baking potatoes, cut into bite-sized chunks
1 large can cream of mushroom soup (family size)
1 packet onion soup mix

Directions:
This can all be dumped into a slow-cooker and cooked on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours or can be baked in oven for 2 hours (or until meat and potatoes are cooked).
