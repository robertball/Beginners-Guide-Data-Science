Title:
Creamy Pineapple Pepper Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/946589.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C mayo
4 T salsa
8 1/4 ounces crushed pineapple, drained, juice reserved
2 T pineapple juice
2 1/2 T sugar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix on medium speed for 2 minutes.
Refrigerate for an hour or more.
Use as dipping sauce with southwest eggrolls or other finger foods.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
We used to love getting the Southwest Egg rolls at Benningan's, but since they have closed, we haven't been able to find anywhere that makes the sauce. This is a pretty good copy!
