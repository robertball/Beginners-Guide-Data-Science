Title:
Hasselback Gratin Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz finely grated Gruyere or comte
2 oz finely grated Parmigiano-reggiano
2 c heavy cream
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 T fresh thyme leaves, roughly chopped
kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
2.5 lbs russet potatoes, peeled and sliced 1/8th inch thick on mandoline slicer
2 T unsalted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F. Grease a 2 qt casserole with butter.
Combine cheeses. Set aside 1/3 of cheese mixture. Add cream, garlic, and thyme to remaining 2/3. Season generously with salt and pepper.
Add potato slices and toss until each slice is coated. Stack potato slices and place in casserole with slices perpendicular to bottom. Pour remaining cheese/cream mixture over potatoes.
Cover tightly with foil. Bake 30 min.
Remove foil. Bake 30 min longer until pale golden brown.
Sprinkle with reserved cheese. Bake 30 min longer until deep golden brown.
