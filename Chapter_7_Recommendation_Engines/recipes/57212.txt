Title:
Hot Hunan Hoagies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef top round steak, cut 1 inch thick
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup dry sherry
1/2 cup dark soy sauce
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 tablespoon grated ginger
8 Hoagie rolls
RELISH:
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
1 teaspoon dark sesame oil
1 teaspoon white wine vinegar
1/2 teaspoon dark soy sauce
1/4 teaspoon sugar
1/8 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1/2 cup sliced green onions
1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper

Directions:
Place beef steak in a plastic bag.  Combine garlic, sherry, soy sauce, oil and ginger; reserve 1/4 cup.  Pour remaining marinade over the steak.  Close bag securely and marinate in refrigerator 4 to 8 hours, turning at least once.
Meanwhile prepare Relish:  Combine vegetable oil, sesame oil, vinegar, soy sauce, sugar and hot pepper sauce.  Add green onions, mushrooms and bell peppers.  Let stand for 30 minutes.
Remove steak from marinade; discard marinade.  Place steak on rack in broiler pan so surface of meat is 4 inches from heat.  Broil for 15-18 minutes, turning once and basting with reserved marinade.  Let stand for 5 minutes.
Meanwhile split hoagie rolls and toast under the broiler.  Carve steak diagonally across the grain in two slices.  Place beef in hoagie rolls.  Top with relish and serve.

Submitter: 
Cattlemen's Beef Board and National Cattlemen's Be
Description: 
National Beef Cook-Off winning recipe from 1986 by Bette Phillips of New Jersey. Steak is marinated in a zesty Asian sauce, then served on toasted rolls with a spicy relish.
