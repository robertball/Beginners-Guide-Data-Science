Title:
Debra's Blueberry Pineapple Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can blueberry pie filling
1 can ( 1 cup) crushed pineapple drained
1 8 oz cream cheese softened
3 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp milk
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 9 inch graham cracker crust
1/2 cup cream whipped

Directions:
Blend cream cheese with 3 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp milk and vanilla.
Reserve 2 tbsp of crushed pineapple and stir in remainder into cheese mixture.
Spread over bottom of graham cracker crust.
Chill pie.
Pour over blueberry pie filling. Chill
Just before serving whip cream and spread on top, garnish with reserved crushed pineapple

Submitter: 
Debra Rose
Description: 
I have been making this delightful cheescake for 25 years now.  Everyone always asks for the recipe.
