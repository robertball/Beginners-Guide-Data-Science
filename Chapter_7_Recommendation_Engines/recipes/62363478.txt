Title:
Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup oil
1 cup sugar
1-2 eggs, slightly beaten
1 cup mashed bananas
1 tsp. vanilla or lemon extract
1 1/4 cups flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda (or 1 tsp. if you use sour milk)
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup milk (regular or sour)

Directions:
Combine oil, sugar, and eggs; mix well.
Add bananas and vanilla/lemon extract.
Combine flour, soda, and salt separately.  Add alternately to first mixture with milk.  Beat well.
Pour into well-greased 9x9 pan.
Bake at 350 for 20-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
This healthier-than-average cake is great on its own or with peanut butter frosting.  Well, okay, so then it isn't so healthy.  But it's delicious, especially with the chocolate chip option!
