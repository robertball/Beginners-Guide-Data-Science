Title:
Beer Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can frozen limeade concentrate
1 can tequila (use the limeade can)
1 bottle corona
1 12 oz can 7up
1 shot triple sec

Directions:
Mix all together in a pitcher and add ice.
Salt rims of glasses.
Serve over ice.
