Title:
Thai Red Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
coconut oil (approx 1 T)
4-5 garlic cloves
1 onion, sliced
1 small head cauliflower, chopped into florets
1 red pepper, sliced
6-8 cremini mushrooms
4 T Thai red curry paste
2 cans coconut milk
2 T tamari
salt
2-4 T chopped basil
fresh cilantro, green onion, lime to serve

Directions:
melt coconut oil; saute garlic, onion until soft
add curry paste
add vegetables
add ccconut milk
add soy
add chopped basil
simmer 15-20 min
add sugar/agave if needed
serve with cilantro, green onion, rice
