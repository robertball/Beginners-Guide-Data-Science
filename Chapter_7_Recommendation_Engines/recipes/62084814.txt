Title:
Blue Ribbon Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Over-ripe Bananas
1/2 Cup Butter Flavor Crisco
1 Cup Granulated Sugar
2 Eggs
1/4 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Baking Soda
1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1 tsp. Vanilla
1 3/4 Cups Flour
1 Cup Chopped Nuts (optional)

Directions:
First, mash bananas in medium bowl with mixer.  Then add shortening and sugar to the bananas and mix.  Next, add eggs and mix well.  Finally, add remaining ingredients and mix just until blended.  Pour into greased bread pan and bake at 300 degrees fahrenheit for 50 minutes to 1 hour.  Bread is done when toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  For perfect, moist bread, do not over bake.
