Title:
Lori's Freezer Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Slaw:
1 Head of Cabbage - Shredded
1 tsp Salt
1 Carrot - Grated
1 Green Bell Pepper - Chopped fine
1 Onion - Chopped Fine
Dressing:
1 Cup Cider Vinegar
1 Cup Water
2 Cups Sugar
1 tsp Whole Mustard Seed
1 tsp Celery Seed

Directions:
Slaw: Combine the first 5 ingredients and let stand 1 hour. Squeeze out as much moisture as possible.
Dressing: Combine the last 5 ingredients in a sauce pan and bring to a boil for 1 minute.  Cool completely.  Pour over slaw mixture and mix it up.  Put slaw into freezer bags.
Thaws quickly and can be re-frozen.
