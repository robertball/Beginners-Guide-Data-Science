Title:
grilled corn on the cob salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 corn on the cobs
30 ml/2 tbs melted butter
4 tamatoes
8 spring onions (scaillions)
1 garlic colve
2 tbspfresh lemon juice
2 tbsp olive oil
tabasco sauce (to taste)
salt and ground black pepper

Directions:
1.  remove the husks and silky threads covering the corn on the cob. brush he cobs with melted butter and gently barbecue or grill(broil) them for 20-30 minutes,turning occasionally,until tender and tinged brown.  2. to remove the kernals,stand each cob upright on a chopping board and use a large heavey knife to slice down the lenth of a length of the cob. 3. plunge the tomatoes into boiling water for 30 sec. Remove with a slotted spoon and plunge into cold water to cool. then peel the tamatoes (the skins should slip off easily) and finely dice the flesh. 4. place 6 spring onions on a chopping board and chop finely. Chrush and chop the garlic, and then mix together with the corn and tamato in a small bowl.5. stir the lemon juice and olive oil together, adding tabasco sauce,salt pepper to taste.6. pour this mixture over teh salsa and stir well. cover the salsa and leave to steep at room temperature for 1-2 hr. before serving garnish with the remaining spring onions.
