Title:
Peach & Beet Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Portion ingredients according to amount of servings desired:
mixed greens
cooked or roasted beets, cut into bite size chunks
canned peaches, drained and cut into bite size pieces
red bell pepper, chopped
feta cheese, crumbled
pecan pieces
balsamic dressing
pesto

Directions:
Mix salad ingredients together, using proportions as to your personal taste and quantities for # you're serving.
Serve with pesto and a balsamic dressing (there are many varieties from sweet to sour).

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
I tried to replicate a restaurant salad with mixed greens, peaches, beets, pecans, feta, etc., which I serve with a balsamic dressing and pesto.
