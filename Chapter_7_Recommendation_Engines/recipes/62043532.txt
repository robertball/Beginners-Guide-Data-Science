Title:
Sinful Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 flour tortillas- 9" size
2 cups chopped cooked chicken
7oz. can chopped green chilies
4 chopped green onions
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
8oz. softened cream cheese
1-1/2 cups sour cream
1-1/2 t. chili powder
2 cups shredded cheddar or mexican blend cheese

Directions:
Lightly grease 13x19" baking dish.  Combine 1 cup sour cream, cream cheese, green chilies, green onions, cilantro and chili powder.  Stir in chicken and 1 cup of the shredded cheese.  Spoon approximately 1/3 cup of filling down the center of tortilla.  Foll up each tortilla, enclosing filling and arrange in dish seam side down.  Pour bottle of picante sauce over enchiladas.  Cover and bake at 350 for 45 minutes until sauce bubbles and enchiladas are heated through.  Uncover and sprinkle with remaining shredded cheese and bake another 5 minutes until cheese is melting.  Top with remaining sour cream.

Submitter: 
LILYBABY
Description: 
The combination of cream cheese and sour cream in this recipe makes it a favorite at our house.  Add extra green chilies and hot picante sauce if you prefer a spicier dish.
