Title:
Scalloped Polenta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tube premade polenta (680gm)
3 tbsp butter
2 cups fine chopped onion
2 cloves garlic minced
2 teaspoons flour
1 & 1/2 cup whole milk
Salt and white pepper
1 & 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or favorite)
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1/2 cup bread crumbs
Extra butter and parmesan to coat pan

Directions:
Prepare 8 x 8 pan by greasing with butter and coating with parmesan chese
Mix Shredded cheddar with 1/2 cup parmesan cheese and bread crumbs and set aside
Slice premade polenta in 1/8 inch thin even slices and set aside
Saute onion and garlic in butter, over medium heat, until tender
Add flour and stir until well combined
Remove from heat and add milk while whisking briskly
Return to reduced heat and simmer over low heat until thickened, 5 minutes
Season with salt and white pepper to taste *
In the prepared 8 X 8 baking dish layer half the sliced polenta
Then top with half the sauce
Repeat with the remaining polenta
Top with the remaining sauce
Top with Cheddar, Parmesan and bread crumb mixture
Bake in a 425 Degree Oven for 25 minutes or until chesse is melted and the cheese is golden brown..
