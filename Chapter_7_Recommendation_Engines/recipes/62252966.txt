Title:
Caramelized Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds chicken wings or other parts
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 cup soy sauce (reduced sodium)
3 tablespoons ketchup
1 cup honey
1 medium onion chopped fine
2 clove garlic, minced
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Place chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish lined with foil and sprayed with cooking oil. Mix together the oil, soy sauce, ketchup, honey,onions, and garlic, salt and pepper, in a pan and bring to a boil for about 5 minutes and then Pour over the chicken.
Bake in preheated oven for one hour, or until sauce is caramelized and chicken is tender.

Description: 
Soy and ketchup, laced with honey and garlic, make for a potent sauce.  This chicken is ideal for appetizers or a main course.Modified from: Caramelized Baked Chicken
