Title:
Dublin Kiss (St. Patrick's Day Shot) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6396158.jpg
Ingredients:
ice cubes
1 fluid ounce Irish whiskey (such as Jameson®)
1/2 fluid ounce Irish cream liqueur (such as Baileys®)
1 splash creme de menthe liqueur

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Pour in Irish whiskey, Irish cream liqueur, and creme de menthe. Cover and shake until the outside of shaker has frosted. Strain into a shot glass.

Submitter: 
fabeverydayblog
Description: 
This creamy St. Patrick's Day shot has a kiss of mint with the addition of a splash of creme de menthe.
