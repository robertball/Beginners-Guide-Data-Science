Title:
Sheet Pan Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4576318.jpg
Ingredients:
12 apples, cored and cut into 8 wedges
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 teaspoons cinnamon, divided in half
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup flour, divided in 2
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups old fashioned oatmeal
1/3 cup maple syrup
1 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Sprinkle brown sugar,  1 teaspoon cinnamon and 1/2 cup flour over the sliced apple wedges and toss. Add flour and mix well. Place on a greased sheet pan.
For the topping, cut the butter into flour until it's pea sized pieces. Add the oatmeal, walnuts, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, pumpkin pie spice, salt and maple syrup and mix well. Spoon mixture over the apples and bake for 45 to 50 minutes, until the fruit mixture is bubbling.
