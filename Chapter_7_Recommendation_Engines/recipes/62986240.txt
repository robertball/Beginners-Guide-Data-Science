Title:
Mom G's Cranberry Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3oz packages lemon Jello
1 1/2 c. boiling water
2 c. cold water
1 12 oz bag fresh cranberries
1 c. sugar
1 c. crushed pineapple, well drained
3/4 c. celery, chopped
1/2 c. walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Dissolve the jello in boiling water, add cold water.  Refrigerate until thickened.  Meanwhile, chop cranberries in food processor, add sugar and allow sugar to dissolve.  Add remaining ingredients and mix into thickened jello.  Pour into jello mold or serving dish and chill until set.

Submitter: 
chefdeb
Description: 
A delicious alternative to cranberry relish.  A colorful addition to the holiday table!
