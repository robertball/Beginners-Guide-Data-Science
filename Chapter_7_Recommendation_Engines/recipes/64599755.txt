Title:
White Sauce / Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. butter
2 tbsp. flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 cup milk
Optional cheese variation:
stir in 1/4 tsp. mustard (optional)
3/4 cup shredded med. cheddar cheese (or approx. 1/4 cup Cheez Whiz)

Directions:
Melt margarine in a micro-safe bowl.
Whisk in flour, salt and pepper till smooth.
Whisk in milk just to blend a bit.
Heat in micro. x 1 min. @ a time, whisking between, until thickened to desired consistency.
Whisk in cheese (& optional mustard) to melt, if using variation.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A classic white sauce that's quick & easy to make in the microwave. It's so versatile - use it over veggies or anywhere you'd like to add a sauce; or as a base for cheese sauce, etc. Flavor options are only limited by your imagination ;)
