Title:
dry rubbed ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chili powder
1/4 cup paprika
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 TBS mustard powder
2 tsp curry powder
1 TBS Seasoned Salt
1 TBS Pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp cinnamon
dash of cayenne pepper
2 full Racks of pork baby back ribs

Directions:
Blend all dry ingredients in a medium tupperwear with a tight fitting lid. Shake well.
Thouroughly coat ribs with a generous amount of dry rub seasoning.
Place in a roasting pan and cover loosely with foil.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for 1 hour 30 minutes. Finish on a prepared bbq until the ribs are nicely browned.

Submitter: 
LisaD.
Description: 
This is a terrific dry rub that is easy to make and adds a ton of flavor to ribs. This dry rub is equally delicous on roasted chicken and grilled salmon.
