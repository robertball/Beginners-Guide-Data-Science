Title:
Salsa Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/293467.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb lean round steak
1/4 tsp of salt
1/4 tsp of black pepper
1 onion, sliced thin
1 green pepper, sliced thin
1 can of cream of mushroom soup
1 cup of chunky salsa

Directions:
Place round steak in slow cooker.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Arrange onion and green pepper slices on top.  In a medium bowl combine soup and salsa.  Spread over top of peppers and onions.  Cook on low for 6-8 hours.
Excellent served over rice or mashed potatoes.
