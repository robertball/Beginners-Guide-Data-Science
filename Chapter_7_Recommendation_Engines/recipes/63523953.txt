Title:
Cindy's Breakfast Burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/962132.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/2 c. diced green pepper
1/2 c. of sausage crumbles
1/2 c. diced tomato
1 c. Sargento "Off the Block" Sharp Cheddar, shredded cheese
6 soft flour tortillas,10 inch size

Directions:
1. Saute green pepper til soft.
2.Scramble eggs in bowl, add to fry pan along with sausage crumbles.
3.Stir to scramble this mixture on medium heat until eggs are cooked thru and dry.
4.Split this cooked mixture between 6 soft flour tortillas.
5.Sprinkle diced tomatoes and shredded cheese on top of hot egg mixture and wrap each tortilla into a burrito shape.Do not overfill.
6.Place onto bake sheet, seam side down. Bake for 10 minutes at 350 to melt the cheese.
