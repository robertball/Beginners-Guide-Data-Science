Title:
Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup cocoa
2/3 cup oil
1-1/2 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1-1/4 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease baking pan, 13x9x2 inches.
Mix cocoa and oil.  Mix in sugar, eggs and vanilla.  Stir in remaining ingredients.
Spread in pan. Bake for 30 minutes or until brownies start to pull away from side of pan.  Do not overbake.  Cool slightly and cut into bars.
