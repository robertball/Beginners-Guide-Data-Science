Title:
Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065813.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can Cream of Celery Soup and Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 1/4 cups milk
1 can (15 ounces) peas
1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
1 (12 ounce) cans tuna, drained and flaked
1 (12 ounce) hot cooked medium egg noodles
1 1/2 cups potato chips, crushed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400Â°.
Mix soup, milk, peas, cheese, tuna and noodles in 1 1/2-quart baking dish.
Bake for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
Sprinkle crushed chips on top.
Bake 5 minutes or until chips start turning brown.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Tuna tossed with noodles, peas and cheese in a creamy sauce is baked until bubbling with crunchy topping.Modified from: Classic Tuna Noodle Casserole
