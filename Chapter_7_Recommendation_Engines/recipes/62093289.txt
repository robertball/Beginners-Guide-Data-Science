Title:
Katie's Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 avocados
1/4 c. chopped onion
1 jalapeno, diced fine
1/4 c. chopped tomato
1/2 t. red pepper flakes
1/2T - 1T garlic salt (to taste)
2t. lemon or lime juice

Directions:
Remove avocado from skin, chop, and mash.
Mix all ingredients.
The beauty of this recipe is that you can change the amounts based on your taste.  This is just the starter recipe- feel free to add cilantro, more jalapeno, less juice, whatever!
