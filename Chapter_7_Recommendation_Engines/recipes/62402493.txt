Title:
Pickled Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med. red onion, thinly sliced
1/2 C white vinegar
1 t chopped garlic
1/4 t red pepper flakes
1 t. sugar
1 t. salt
1/4 t. freshly ground black pepper
2 cucumbers, thinly sliced

Directions:
Add all ingredients, except cucumbers, to small pot and bring to boil over low heat. Turn of heat, add cucumbers and let sit for 1 hr. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
