Title:
Hand lotion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 gm Sodium Lauryl Sulfate
15 gm Anhydrous Lanolin
120 gm Cetyl Alcohol
200 ml. Gycerin

Directions:
In a double boiler over gentle heat, melt all of the above ingredients
Once all the ingredients are in a  liquid state, add 1125 ml warm (not hot) water (4 1/2 cups)
Stir briskly
Remove from heat and continue to stir occasionally
Place in jars or storage containers.
