Title:
Spaghetti Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cucumbers
4 tomatoes
1 or 2 green peppers
1 large onion
1 pound spaghetti noodles
1 bottle Salad Seasoning (I think its actually called Salad Supreme and it found with the spices in the grocery store.)
1 bottle wine vinegar & oil

Directions:
Must make this all the night before you want to serve it.
Cut all the vegetables and cook the spaghetti.  Mix togother.  Use the whole bottle of dressing & the whole bottle of Salad Seasoning.  Mix well before serving.

Submitter: 
Sue
Description: 
This is a delicious classic spaghetti salad Mom made often.  Make this the night before so all the flavors have time to blend.
