Title:
Easy Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1040761.jpg
Ingredients:
2 baking potatoes, peeled OR
Simply Potatoes to cover bottom of dish
1 medium onion
ham or sausage, optional
cheese, optional
2 cups milk or substitute
2 t. chicken bouillon granules
3 T. flour
3 T. butter, cut into pieces
Pepper to taste and a light sprinkle of Cajun seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Spray a medium size casserole dish with non-stick spray.
Slice potatoes and onion.
Layer in dish, with optional cheese and ham/sausage.
Wisk flour and pepper into milk and mix together with bouillon. Pour over potatoes.
Lay butter pieces on top.
Cover with foil and bake for 1 1/2 hours @ 350.
Uncover and continue  baking 15 more minutes.  Bake 50 minutes if using Simply Potatoes.
