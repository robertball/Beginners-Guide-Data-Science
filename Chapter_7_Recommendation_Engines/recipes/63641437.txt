Title:
Crockpot Chicken & Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 or 4 chicken breasts
Vegetables - green beans, broccoli, potatoes(white or sweet), carrots, etc
Italian seasoning
butter

Directions:
place chicken breasts in crockpot sprinkle with seasoning.  Place veggies on top and sprinkle again.  Dot top with butter.  Cook on low 4-6 hours
