Title:
Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup water
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 tablespoon white sugar
3 1/3 cups bread flour
2 1/2 teaspoon active dry yeast
2 T parma cheese
garlic powder
onion powder
Sauce
16 oz tomato sauce
8 oz tomato paste
garlic powder
onion powder
1/2 t salt
1/2 t sugar
1 t Italian seasoning

Directions:
Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select Dough cycle; press Start.
Remove dough from pan after rise cycle and use for your favorite pizza recipe.
Mix sauce ingredients together.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
Nothing beats a homemade pizza crust! Crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. Using your bread machine makes mixing the dough a breeze.Modified from: Pizza Crust for the Bread Machine II
