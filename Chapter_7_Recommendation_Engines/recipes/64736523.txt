Title:
Blueberry Latte Breakfast Smoothie Jars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6303047.jpg
Ingredients:
VANILLA CHIA PUDDING LAYER
1 cup full-fat coconut milk
1 cup brewed coffee, cooled
3 tbsp maple syrup
Pinch of salt
Optional: 2 scoops collagen peptides or protein powder
1/2 cup chia seeds
1 tbsp vanilla extract
BLUEBERRY YOGURT LAYER
2 cups vanilla yogurt
2 cups frozen blueberries
2  tablespoon pure vanilla extract
ADDITIONAL INGREDIENTS
2 cups vanilla yogurt
2 cups fresh blueberries
1 cup fresh raspberries
(or fruit of choice)

Directions:
VANILLA CHIA LATTE PUDDING LAYER
Combine all the ingredients in the Vitamix blender, in the order listed.
Pulse/blend briefly on low speed to mix.
Let it sit for a couple of minutes.
The seeds will sink to the bottom.
Pulse to re-mix.
Let it sit again. Pulse again. Repeat 3-4 times every few minutes until seeds remain evenly distributed and pudding is starting to thicken.
Pour into a jar. Refrigerate overnight.
BLUEBERRY YOGURT LAYER
Place vanilla yogurt, vanilla and frozen blueberries into the vita-mix blender and puree
TO COMBINE
In two large mouth jars place 1 cup of the chia pudding in the bottom
Add a half cup fresh blueberries on top of the pudding
Top the fresh blueberries with the blueberry yogurt mixture
Top the blueberry yogurt mixture with vanilla yogurt
Top that with a 1/4 fresh blueberries and 1/2 cup fresh raspberries (or fruit of choice)
Serve!
