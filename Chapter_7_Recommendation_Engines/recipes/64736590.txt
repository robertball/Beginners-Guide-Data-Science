Title:
Honey-Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup flour
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
3 lbs bone-in chicken pieces or ~ 1.5 lb chicken breasts**
Glaze
1/2 cup butter or margarine, divided
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 tbsp. soy sauce
1 1/2 tsp. curry powder

Directions:
Place flour, salt & cayenne pepper in large plastic bag
Add chicken pieces and shake to coat.
Pour 4 tbsp. melted butter into 9" x 13" x 2" baking/cake pan
Place chicken in pan, turning pieces once to coat.
Bake, uncovered, @ 350 x 30 min.**
Mix brown sugar, honey, lemon juice, soy sauce, curry powder and remaining butter; pour over chicken.
Bake x 50 minutes more or until chicken is tender, basting several times with pan drippings.
**If using chicken breasts, reduce both baking times.
