Title:
Texas Queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups shredded sharp cheddar
2 cups Monterey Jack cheddar
1/4 block of velveeta cheese
1/3 cup milk
1 T cornstarch
1/3 cup of finely chopped white onion
1 large jalapeno seeded and chopped
1/3 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
1 habanero pepper seeded and chopped
1 T olive oil
1 4 oz can of mild green chiles

Directions:
Heat up the olive oil in a medium skillet on a medium-high temperature. Once the oil is hot, add the onion and chopped peppers.
Sautee the onions and peppers for about 3-5 minutes or until the onions are slightly translucent. Set aside.
Put all three cheeses into a crock pot and toss with the cornstarch.
Set the crock pot on high and add the milk, sauteed onion and peppers and the can of green chiles and juice.
Let the mixture heat and stir every 5-10 minutes.
Serve when melted, usually about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
NomNomDelicious
Description: 
A nice, thick queso great for chips, quesadillas, enchiladas, fresh veggies and just about anything you can think of!
