Title:
Easy Fettuccine Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990393.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (26 ounce) jar RAGÚ® Old World Style® Traditional Sauce
1 (16 ounce) package frozen assorted vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers, onion, zucchini), cooked
1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
1 (16 ounce) package fettuccine, cooked and drained

Directions:
Heat Pasta Sauce in 2-quart saucepan until heated through. Stir in cooked vegetables and basil.
Spoon vegetable mixture over hot fettuccine and sprinkle, if desired, with grated Parmesan cheese.
