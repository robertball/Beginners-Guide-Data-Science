Title:
Nana's Tourtière (Meat Pie) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pastry:
Flour	      4 cups
Butter           1 lb
Sour cream  1 cup
Filling:
Ground pork		1 lbs
Chicken stock.       1/2 cup
Onion			1/4 cup finely chopped
Garlic			1 clove
Salt			        1/2 tsp
Thyme			1/2 tsp
Dry mustard		1/2 tsp
Sage			1/2 tsp
Poultry seasoning      1/2 tsp
Potato			1 medium  mashed

Directions:
Put flour into a bowl and cut in butter in large chunks.  Use a pastry cutter until it is the consistency of course meal. Then add sour cream and stir with a fork until blended. The dough will be soft. Chill for 2 hours in the fridge. Divide the dough in half and form two balls.
Cook one medium sized potato and mash then set aside. In a sauce pan add ground pork, chicken stock, onion, garlic, salt and seasonings. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 25 minutes, until the liquid is gone. The stir mashed potato into the meat mixture.
To make small meat pies roll out pastry and cut out circles using a small bowl (about 2-3 inches across).  Scoop out 2 tsp of meat mixture onto to one side of the pastry circle. Fold the pastry in half and crimp edges with a fork.  Bake at 375°F for 15-20 minutes. Or put the whole amount in a pastry shell for a whole meat pie.
