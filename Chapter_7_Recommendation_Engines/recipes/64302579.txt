Title:
Mushroom Sausage Scallopini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb sausage
1/4 all-purpose flour
1/4 C butter
1/4 C canola oil
1 C white wine or chicken broth
1/2 C water
1 onion
1-2 garlic cloves
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp thyme, oregano, and rosemary each, crushed
1lb fresh sliced mushrooms
hot cooked fettuccine

Directions:
1. In a large skillet, heat butter and oil. Brown sausage. Sprinkle flour in pan. Remove and keep warm. Stir in the wine/ broth, onion, garlic, and seasonings into drippings. Return sausage to skillet. Top with mushrooms.
2. Cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes or until meat is fully cooked. Serve with fettucine.
