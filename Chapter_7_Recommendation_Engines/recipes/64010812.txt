Title:
Potato Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium Russet potatoes
1 pint of sour cream
10 oz. sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
1 bunch of scallions, chopped
3 tbsp. milk
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
2 tbsp. melted butter
1/3 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300.
Clean potatoes then cook in salted boiling water until just tender.
Remove from water and let cool.
Peel and grate potatoes coarsely.
To grated potatoes, add cheese, sour cream, scallions, milk, salt and pepper and mix thoroughly.
Put mixture into a buttered 13x9 casserole and smooth with spatula.
Combine bread crumbs and melted butter and sprinkle over the top.
Bake for 50 minutes or until golden brown and hot.
