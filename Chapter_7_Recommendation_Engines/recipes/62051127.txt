Title:
Mouthwatering Buttermilk Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 C. Flour
1/2 tsp. salt
2 tsp.   baking powder
3 Tblsp. sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon  (optional)
3 eggs
1/3 C. vegetable oil
1 C.  Fresh buttermilk
1/2 C. milk
1 tsp.  vanilla

Directions:
Sift the dry ingredients into a bowl.  Then, separate the eggs and put the egg whites in a smaller bowl.
Beat the whites until stiff peaks are formed. (make sure you beat the whites first before you beat the rest of the batter)  Then,  add the yolks, both milks, oil and vanilla to the dry ingredients.  Beat well and make sure there are no lumps.  Fold the whites into the batter using a flat untensil or wooden spoon.
Pour about 1/2 to 3/4 C. of batter in your preheated waffle iron.
Makes 6-8 9 inch waffles.
