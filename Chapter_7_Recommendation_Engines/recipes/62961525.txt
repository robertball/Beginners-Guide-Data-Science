Title:
Mom's Vanilla Pound Cake with modification for chocolate pound cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
2 sticks butter
½ cup Crisco
5 eggs
3 ¼ cup all purpose flour
½ cup evaporated milk and ½ cup water OR 5oz evaporated milk and 3oz water
3 tsp vanilla
For Chocolate Pound Cake add ½ cup cocoa

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Grease and flour bundt pan.  Cream together sugar, butter, and Crisco until light yellow in color.  Add eggs one at time mixing each in completely before adding the next.  Mix in flour and milk/water alternating, starting with a third of the flour and then half of the liquid and so on finishing with flour, mixing completely between each addition.  Add vanilla.  Continue to mix another minute.  Pour into prepared bundt pan.  Bake at 325 for 1 ½ hours in bundt pan.  Cool in pan for 10 minutes prior to turning out on a wire rack.  Note: I use a bundt pan but you can also use 2 loaf pans.

Description: 
This pound cake combines butter and crisco and has a wonderful buttery vanilla flavor. This classic is always a hit!
