Title:
Marinara Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
About 6 cloves of garlic
28 ounce can of whole tomatoes
Olive Oil
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Basil, garnish
Parmesan, garnish

Directions:
Crush garlic cloves with the side of a knife blade.
Put some Olive Oil in a pan.
Add garlic.
Turn heat on medium.
Cook until garlic is slightly brown. Do not over cook.
Add tomatoes.
Cook about 30 minutes.
Smash tomatoes with a wooden spoon about half way into cooking.
Add a little more Olive Oil
Turn on high for about 3 minutes.
When Oil turns red it's done.
Use on toasted Italian Bread, with meatballs, pasta, . . .
Garnish with Basil & Parmesan.
Enjoy

Submitter: 
Loretta Ann Zazo
Description: 
Sauce does not need to cook all day to be great.
