Title:
Beefy Nachos Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups crushed tortilla chips,divided
2 jars processed cheese spread
2 cups fat-free sour cream
1 cup sliced jalapeno peppers
1 red onion,chopped
1 package(30 oz.)frozen hash brown potatoes,thawed
1 cup shredded sharp-cheddar cheese
3/4 cup shredded monterey jack cheese
3/4 cup shredded swiss cheese
1 1/2 lb ground beef
1 cup chili sauce
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
2 teaspoons seasoned pepper
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 cup shredded white cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Place 1 cup crushed tortilla chips on greased 3-quart baking dish.
In a large bowl combine cheese spread,sour cream,jalapeno peppers and red onion.Stir in potatoes and cheeses;transfer to the baking dish.
In a large skillet,cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink;drain.
Stir in chili sauce,brown sugar and seasonings;spread over the potatoes.Sprinkle with remaining tortilla chips and shredded white cheddar cheese over the top.
Bake,uncovered,at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes or until bubbly.
