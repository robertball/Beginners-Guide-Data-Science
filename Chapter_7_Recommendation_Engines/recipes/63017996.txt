Title:
Zesty Shredded Beef Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2-3 lb) beef roast
3/4 cup bottled Italian dressing
2 cups water
2 beef bouillon cubes
2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
2 tsp herbs de provence
2-4 bay leaves
2 loaves of french bread cut into thirds

Directions:
In a slow cooker combine all ingredients except the beef.  Place beef into slow cooker.  Cover and cook on low for 8 hours until beef shreds easily with two forks.  Remove bay leaves and serve on french bread.
