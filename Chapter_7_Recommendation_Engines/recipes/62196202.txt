Title:
Seven Layer Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick margarine or butter
1 c graham cracker crumbs
6 oz chocolate chips
6 oz butterscotch chips
1 c coconut
1 c chopped walnuts
1 14 oz can condensed milk

Directions:
Melt butter or margarine in a pyrex pan (9x13.
Sprinkle the graham cracker crumbs over the butter.
Layer all the remaining ingredients, and pour the condensed milk over the top.
Bake at 350.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
My mother used to make these at the holidays, so they always feel like Christmas to me.
