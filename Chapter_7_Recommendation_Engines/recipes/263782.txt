Title:
Raw Raspberry Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups freeze-dried raspberries (such as Karen's Naturals Just RaspberriesÂ®)
1/2 cup agave nectar
1/4 cup water, or more as needed

Directions:
Combine raspberries, agave, and water in a high-speed blender; blend until raspberries are softened and jammy. Pour into a jar. Store in the refrigerator.
