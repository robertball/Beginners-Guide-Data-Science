Title:
Bill''s Mango Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 Cups Heavey Cream
1 Cup Milk
3/4 Cup Sugar
6 Large Egg Yolks
1 tsp Real Vanilla Extract
5 Large Mangoes, medium ripe

Directions:
Peel, pit and slice mangoes.
Place sliced mangoes into a food processor and process until pureed.
Place mango puree in a 2 qt suacepan and bring to a simmer.
Simmer mango puree slowly, uncovered for 2 hours or until volume has been reduced to 2 cups of puree.  Set aside to cool. Then chill undtil cold.
Bring cream, milk, and sugar just to a boil in a 2 quart heavey sauce pan, stirring occasionally.
Lightly beat yolks in a large bowl, then add hot sweetened cream mixture in a slow stream, whisking.
Pour cream and egg custard mixture into the saucepan and cook over a moderately low heat, stirring constantly.
Continue cooking until custard mixture reaches a temperature of 170* or the custard mixture coats the back of wooden spoon.
Pour custard mixture through a sieve into a clean bowl and then stir in the vanilla extract.
Cool custard, stirring occasionally, then chill until cold.
Add chilled pureed mango reduction to chilled custard mix.
Freeze custard mixture in an ice cream maker.  Transfer to an air tight container and place in freezer until hardened.
