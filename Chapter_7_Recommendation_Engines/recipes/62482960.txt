Title:
Cream Cheese Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488248.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese
2 sticks butter
2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
5 eggs
2 tsp Vanilla
Other flavoring of your choice

Directions:
Cream together the cream cheese and butter.
Add sugar and beat well until fluffy.
Add eggs one at a time, beating well
Add self-rising flour
Add flavorings.
Bake at 350 until done.

Submitter: 
Milly Suazo-Martinez
Description: 
Wonderful pound cake with buttery consistency. This recipe has been in our family for over 25 years.
