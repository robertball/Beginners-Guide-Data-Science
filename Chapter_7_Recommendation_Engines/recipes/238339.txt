Title:
Simple Cantaloupe Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1221609.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vanilla yogurt (such as Dannon® Light and Fit®)
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup sliced cantaloupe
4 ice cubes, crushed, or as needed

Directions:
Blend yogurt, orange juice, cantaloupe, and ice cubes in a blender until smooth, about 30 seconds.

Submitter: 
Beth
Description: 
This is a very simple recipe! A sweet, healthy way to start your day with a delicious, unique flavor.
