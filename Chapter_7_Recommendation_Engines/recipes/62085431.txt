Title:
Pumpkin Pie Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup water
2 tablespoons instant coffee
3 tablespoons sugar
1/4 teaspoon canned pumpkin
1/4 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
dollop whipped cream

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients together in a small, microwaveable bowl. Heat for 3 minutes in the microwave, on high. Pour or ladel into 2 mugs. Top with a dollop of whipped cream.

Submitter: 
spiceisnice
Description: 
Quick and easy, creamy and cozy, a nicely spiced hot coffee drink for the holidays or anytime.
