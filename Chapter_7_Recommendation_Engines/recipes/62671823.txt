Title:
Scalloped Potatoes w/ Ham & Shallots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. potatoes, peeled and sliced thickly
4 small or 2 large shallots, peeled and sliced thinly
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided in half
1 1/2 cups cooked ham, cubed or cut into narrow 1-inch strips
1/2 cup butter
1 cup half and half
2 tablespoons cooking sherry
1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped finely

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Toss potatoes, shallots, 1 cup of cheese, and ham pieces in a large bowl to evenly distribute all the solid ingredients. Arrange in a rectangular Pyrex glass dish.
3. Melt the butter in the microwave. Whisk in the half and half and then the sherry, followed by the parsley.
4. Pour the butter mixture evenly over the potato mixture. Sprinkle the top with remaining cheese.
5. Bake in preheated oven for 60  to 90 minutes, or until slightly browned and bubbly.
