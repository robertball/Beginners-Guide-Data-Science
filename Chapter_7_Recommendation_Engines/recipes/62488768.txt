Title:
Madeover French Toast with Spiced Pears Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup liquid honey, divided
1 TBS lemon juice
1/2 tsp. each ground cinnamon and ginger, divided
2 large ripe pears, cored and thinly sliced
2 large eggs
1/3 cup milk
4 slices of bread
1/4 cup toasted sliced almonds

Directions:
1) Preheat over to 350 degrees. Stir 3 TBS of the honey with lemon juice and 1/4 tsp. each of cinnamon and ginger. Spread evenly into greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange pears in two rows.
2) Beat eggs in shallow dish with milk, remaining honey, cinnamon and ginger. Soak each piece of bread in egg mix. Place on top of pears.
3) Bake for 20 minutes or until set and golden.
4) To serve, cut into squares and use a thin, flat spatula to lift each portion out of the pan and turn pear side up. Sprinkle with almonds and drizzle with any syrup left in the baking dish.
