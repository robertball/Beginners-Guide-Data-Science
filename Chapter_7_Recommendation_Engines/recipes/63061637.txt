Title:
SWISS BEANS (*/+) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/770863.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (16 oz) french style green beans
2 T butter or marg
2 T flour
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp grated onion
1 cup sour cream
1/4 pound grated swiss cheese

Directions:
Heat green beans; drain.
Melt butter and when melted, blend in flour, salt, pepper, sugar and onion.
Add sour cream gradually and cook, stirring constantly, until thickened.
Fold in beans.
Pour mixture into greased 1 1/2 qt casserole.
Sprinkle grated cheese over top.
Place casserole under broiler for five minutes or until cheese is melted and browned a bit.
