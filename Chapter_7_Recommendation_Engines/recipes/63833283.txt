Title:
Chili Sauce Revisited Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup tomato sauce
2T brown sugar
2 tablespoons vinegar
1/4 teaspoon allspice
1 T chili powder

Directions:
Stir the tomato sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, chili powder and allspice together in a mixing bowl until thoroughly blended. Pour into a covered container, and refrigerate until ready to use.
