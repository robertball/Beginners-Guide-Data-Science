Title:
Glazed Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 apples
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
pie crust (bottom and top)
Glaze: 2 tablespoons cream or milk
1 tablespoon sugar

Directions:
preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
place piecrust in pie plate.
cut apples into slices.  toss slices in the sugar and the spices.
put filling in bottom crust.
cut the top crust into strips.
arrange the strips into a lattice pattern.
glaze the top crust.
cook in oven at 425 degrees F, for 30 minutes.
turn oven down to 350 degrees, cook for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Melanie
Description: 
A family favorite, classic apple pie with a new twist...glazed top!
