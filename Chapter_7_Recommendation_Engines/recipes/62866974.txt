Title:
Strawberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
3 tsp. cinnamon
2 c. sugar
2 (10 oz.) frozen strawberries, thawed
1 1/4 c. chopped nuts
1 1/4 c. oil
4 eggs

Directions:
Mix flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt together. In a second bowl, blend the sugar, strawberries, nuts, oil, and the eggs. Add moist batter to the flour mixture and blend them together. Grease 3 loaf pans. Spoon batter into pans and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
