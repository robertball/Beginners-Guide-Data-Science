Title:
Homemade Sour Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. Vinegar
2/3 C. milk powder
3/4 C. Water

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a mixer and blend to your desired consistency. This produces one cup of sour cream.

Submitter: 
Lisa Garnier
Description: 
I found this on the web when I was needing sour cream for a cake recipe but didn't have any.
