Title:
Signature Caesar Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/418127.jpg
Ingredients:
2 TBS Light Mayo
½ tsp Dijon Mustard
½ tsp Worcestershire Sauce
1 clove of Garlic- grated
2 TBS Fresh grated Parmesan Cheese
½ tsp Fresh Cracked Pepper
1 ½ TBS Anchovy Paste
1 TBS Fresh lemon juice
3 TBS Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, mix all ingredients except the olive oil.
After ingredients is combine, slowly stream in olive oil.
Place in a dish and refrigerate until using.
Best if tossed with lettuce before serving, as this dressing is very thick.

Submitter: 
Kathleen
Description: 
A garlicky Caesar Dressing that does not use raw eggs. Perfect with Romaine lettuce and grilled chicken.
