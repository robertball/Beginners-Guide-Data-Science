Title:
Easy Baked Chicken Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 8 oz chicken breasts, sliced in half to make 8
3/4 cup seasoned breadcrumbs
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tbsp butter, melted
3/4 cup mozzarella cheese
1 cup pasta sauce
cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450Â°. Spray a large baking sheet with spray.
Combine breadcrumbs and parmesan cheese in a bowl. Melt the butter in another bowl. Coat each piece of chicken with the melted butter, then dip into breadcrumb mixture.  Place on baking sheet and repeat with the remaining chicken.
Bake in the oven for 20 minutes. Turn chicken over, bake another 5 minutes.
Remove from oven, spoon sauce over each piece of chicken and top each with shredded mozzarella cheese.
Bake 5 more minutes or until cheese is melted.
