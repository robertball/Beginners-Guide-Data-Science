Title:
Sophie's Stuffed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Baked Potatoes
3 Chicken Breasts, Grilled and Diced
2 Heads Brocolli, Blanced and Seasoned to Taste
2 Cups Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1/2 Cup Milk
1/4 Cup Butter
Chives
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Split potatoes
Sprinkle with milk, butter pats, salt and pepper.  Mix gently, being careful not to break the skin.
Fill with chicken and brocolli.
Sprinkle with Cheese.
Broil until cheese is melted and lightly browned.
Sprinlke with chives and serve.
