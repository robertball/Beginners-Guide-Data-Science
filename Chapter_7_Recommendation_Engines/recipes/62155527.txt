Title:
No-Bake Oatmeal Fudgies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup cocoa
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup peanut butter
1tsp. vanilla
2 1/2 cups quick-cooking oats

Directions:
In 2 quart saucepan over medium high heat,mix milk ,cocoa,sugar,and butter, Bring to boil and boil for 2minutes. Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter and vanilla. Then stir in oats. When dough is well blended, put into greased 8"x8" pan and cut into bars, or drop by teaspoonsful on waxed paper. Let cool.
