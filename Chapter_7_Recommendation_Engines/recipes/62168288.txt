Title:
Slow Cooker Turkey and Potatoes! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large Turkey legs (uncooked)
1/2 cup of chopped yellow onion
1/2 cup of chopped red and green bell pepper
2 medium size potatoes cut into large cubes
2 cans of Campbell's Cream of Chicken & Mushroom Condensed Soup
1 can of Campbell's Cream of Mushroom Soup
Salt
Ground Pepper
Season All Meat Tenderizer
Garlic Powder
2.5 cups of water
2 cups of long grain white rice

Directions:
Place water, all cans of Condensed Soup, Onions, Bell Peppers, water in Slow Cooker and stir together.
Add Turkey Legs and make sure they are covered by the liquid.
Leave Slow Cooker on high for 3 hours, stirring occasionally
After 3 hours, add potatoes and stir into Slow Cooker. Season to taste with Salt, Season All Meat Tenderizer, Ground Pepper, Garlic Powder
Continue to stir occasionally for another 2 hours on high.
Take a knife, and cut meat off of the bone. Continue to stir occasionally on high for another hour.
Cook 2 cups of rice seperately.

Submitter: 
RiceLover
Description: 
A very comforting and hearty slow cooker meal over rice. This was a big hit with my Husband and of course very easy since it is made in that trusty slow cooker! :)
