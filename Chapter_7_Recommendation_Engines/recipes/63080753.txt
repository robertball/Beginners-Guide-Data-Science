Title:
Whoppie Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
4 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1 cup shortening
2 eggs + 2 yolks
3/4 cup cocoa
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup (sour) milk
1 cup hot water

Directions:
Combine ingredients in large bowl.
Bake like cookies for 7-8 minutes @ 375 degrees
Cream Filling:
2 egg whites, unbeaten
2 tsp. vanilla
4 T.  confectioners sugar
4 T. milk
Beat all ingredients together well.
(add 5-6 conf. sugar (depending on the consistency)
Spread filling between two of the cake pies
