Title:
Kindness Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1069474.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup honey
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 eggs
1 tablespoon orange zest
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups rolled oats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Stir together the  honey, oil, and eggs. Add the orange rind and salt.  Mix in the oatmeal and stir well.
Drop on cookie sheets and bake for 8 to 10 minutes.
