Title:
Easy Red Beans & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/600706.jpg
Ingredients:
1  pound  dried red beans
7  cups  water
1  green bell pepper, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
3  celery stalks, chopped
3  garlic cloves, chopped
1/2  pound smoked andouille sausage, sliced & browned
3  tablespoons  Creole seasoning
Hot cooked rice
Garnish: sliced green onions

Directions:
Place first 8 ingredients in a 4-quart slow cooker.
Cook, covered, at HIGH 7 hours or until beans are tender. Serve with hot cooked rice. Garnish, if desired.

Submitter: 
Knightcraft
Description: 
Easiest Red Beans & Rice recipe ever. Throw it in a slow cooker and eat when you get home from work!
