Title:
One Dish Chicken and Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954887.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. of chicken pieces
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup melted butter
2/3 cup undiluted evaporated milk
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 lb. sliced mushrooms

Directions:
Coat chicken with flour.  Arrange chicken in single layer with skins down in melted butter.  Bake uncovered at 425 degrees for 30 minutes.
Turn chicken and bake until brown for 15 to 20 minutes or until tender.
Pour off excess fat.
Combine milk, soup, cheese, salt, pepper and mushrooms.
Pour over chicken.
Cover with foil and bake for 15 to 20 minutes at 325 degrees.
