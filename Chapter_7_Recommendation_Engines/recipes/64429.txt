Title:
Honey-Lime Fruit Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981751.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) can DOLEÂ® Pineapple Chunks
1 (11 ounce) can DOLE Mandarin Oranges, drained
1 large DOLE Banana, sliced
1 DOLE Kiwi fruit, peeled, halved and sliced
1 cup quartered DOLE Strawberries
1/4 teaspoon grated lime peel
2 tablespoons lime juice
1 tablespoon honey

Directions:
Drain pineapple; reserve 1/4 cup juice.
Combine pineapple chunks, mandarin oranges, banana, kiwi fruit and strawberries in bowl.
Stir together reserved juice, lime peel, lime juice and honey.  Pour over salad; toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Dole Food Company
Description: 
The sweetness of honey mellows the tang of lime in this colorful fruit combo.
