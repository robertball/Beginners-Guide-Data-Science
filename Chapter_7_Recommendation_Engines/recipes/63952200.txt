Title:
Honey Whole Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/8 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
3 tablespoons honey (reviewers say increase to 1/4 cup)
1/3 teaspoon salt (rev. says increase to 3/4 or 1 tsp)
1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1 1/2 cups bread flour
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Add ingredients according to the manufacturer's directions to your bread machine. Use the wheat bread cycle and light color setting.

Submitter: 
Judy Maurer
Description: 
The bread machine makes this classic combination pretty easy to come by.Modified from: Honey Whole Wheat Bread
