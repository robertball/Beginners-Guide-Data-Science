Title:
Pomegranate Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup pomegranate seeds (arils from one large pomegranate, white membrane removed)
2 large ripe avocadoes
1/4 cup chopped green onion
1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander
1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
1 serrano chile (optional)
S&P;

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and serve with tortilla chips. Easy!

Submitter: 
Chocolate
Description: 
Pomegranates add a great twist to guacamole!
