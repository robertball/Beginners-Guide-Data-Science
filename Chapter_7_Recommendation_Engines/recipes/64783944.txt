Title:
Air fryer  Mini Meatloaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground chuck
2 tsp onion powder
1 tsp salt
1 egg
2 tbs mayonnaise
1/4 C seasoned bread crumbs
Ketchup

Directions:
Spray the basket of a 10 quart airfryer  with oil, and preheat the temperature on an air fryer for 390 degrees and a time of 30 minutes.
In a large bowl mix all ingredients together and form into 6 mini loaves.  Once preheated, place the loaves into the basket making sure that they don’t touch.
Cook for 30 minutes for well done meatloaf.
Smooth ketchup on top and cook another 3 minutes.
