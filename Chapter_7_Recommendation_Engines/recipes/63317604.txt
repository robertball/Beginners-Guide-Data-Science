Title:
Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 heads of Cabbage
1 1/4 Cup of Mayo
1/4 Cup of Sour Cream
1/8 White Balamic Vinegar
1/4 t. Pepper
1/4 Cup of Sugar
1 T. of Orange Juice

Directions:
Sherred the cabbage and carrots. Set aside. Mix the remaining ingredients and taste. If its on the thin side add a 1/2 cup of mayo. If too thick thin out with the orange juice.
