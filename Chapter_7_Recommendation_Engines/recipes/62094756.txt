Title:
FRANKFURTER CASSEROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 cups diced potatoes, uncooked
1-1lb. pkg franks, sliced 1/2" to 1" thick
2-8 oz.cans tomato sauce
1-small onion, diced
1/2-bell pepper, diced
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/2-cup water

Directions:
Boil the diced potatoes for 7 minutes and drain.
Mix with remaining ingredients.
Pour into a greased 2 qrt. baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 min or until top is lightly browned and potatoes are done.

Submitter: 
BARBARA1940
Description: 
My mother-in-law gave me this recipe when I was a new bride in the '50's. She knew how to economize and this is a quick, easy and economical dish to make but still a tasty one. My family has loved it for the past 50 yrs. It is good with a side salad and breadsticks.
