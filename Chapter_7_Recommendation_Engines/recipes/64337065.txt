Title:
Chocolate Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp raisins
1/2 cup warm water
1/2 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
3 eggs
1 1/2 cup low-fat milk
1 tbsp butter/margarine
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/8 tsp salt
8 slices bread - cut into cubes (wholewheat is yummy)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Place raisins and water in a small bowl and let stand for 10 minutes or until raisins are soft. Drain raisins and discard water.
Whisk the sugar with the cocoa and eggs until smooth and set aside.Heat the milk and butter over low heat until butter melts. Whisk the milk mixture into the cocoa mixture along with vanilla, nutmeg and salt.
Place the bread cubes in a lightly greased 8x8 dish. sprinkle with the raisins, then pour in the cocoa mixture. Land stand for about 10 minutes.
Set the pan in a larger baking dish, then pour enough hot water into the larger pan to come halfway up the sides of the pudding pan.
Bake the pudding, uncovered,in the hot water bath for 25 minutes or until toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Serve warm or at room temperature.
