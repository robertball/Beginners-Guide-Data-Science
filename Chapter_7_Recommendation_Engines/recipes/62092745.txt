Title:
3C (Couscous, Carrots, and Chicken) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 c. whole wheat couscous
1-3/4 c. water
1 tsp. turmeric
2 Tbsp. butter
1 c. cooked, shredded chicken
4 carrots
1-2 cloves garlic
extra virgin olive oil
salt

Directions:
Peel carrots and slice thinly.
Peel and chop garlic.
Boil water with turmeric and butter.  Add couscous, turn off heat, and let sit, covered, for at least 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, heat oil in skillet.  Add carrots and garlic and cook until soft.
Add chicken during the last couple of minutes, just to heat through.
Salt couscous, stir in veggies and chicken, and serve.
