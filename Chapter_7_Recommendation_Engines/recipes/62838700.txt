Title:
Salted Caramel Party Snack Mix for a Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package crispy rice square cereal
1 (12 ounce) package crispy corn square cereal
1 (12 ounce) package crispy wheat square cereal
1 Pound pecans
1 Pound almonds
1 Pound cashews
1- 14 oz package of sweetened flaked coconut
Optional- 2 1/2 cups of raisins or dried cranberries
1 pound of butter
2 pounds of brown sugar
1 1/4 tsp salt
2 tsps vanilla

Directions:
Place Cereals, nuts and dried fruit into 2 large brown paper bags or 2 large microwaveable bowls and stir or shake to mix.
In a saucepan over medium heat- Melt Butter, Brown Sugar and salt.
Bring to a slow boil then simmer 5 minutes stirring frequently.
Remove from heat and add vanilla
Pour 1/2 of liquid into each container over the cereal mixture and stir to coat.
Microwave each bag or bowl for 1 1/2 minutes on high three times for a total of 4 1/2 minutes Stirring between each microwaving. Add as much Coconut as you'd like 1/2 bag to a full bag.
Spread out to cool and break apart.
Freezes well.
This makes 3 gallons of mix and can be bagged up and frozen until you are ready to eat it yourself or give it away.

Description: 
This is a great recipe to make for gifts during the holidays or for a big crowd! Makes a lot and it is always a crowd pleaser. Everyone loves it and always asks for the recipe. I know you'll love it too!
