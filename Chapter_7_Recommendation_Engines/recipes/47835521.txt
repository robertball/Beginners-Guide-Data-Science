Title:
PAVLOVA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg whites
1 cup sugar (use berry sugar)
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1tsp. vinegar
2 cups of whipping cream
Fresh fruit, Kiwi, Strawberries, Blueberries
1/2 cup toasted slivered almonds

Directions:
Beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Continue beating while adding sugar slowly, 1 Tbsp. (15 ml) at a time. Add vanilla and vinegar. Beat until very stiff. Place waxed paper or parchment paper on a cookie sheet and spread mixture in a circle, slightly smaller than desired size. Bake 1 hour at 275 F (140C). Turn oven off and leave meringue in oven overnight to dry. Peel off paper. Top with shipped cream, fresh fruit and toasted almonds. Cut in wedges to serve.
