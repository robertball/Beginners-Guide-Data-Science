Title:
Zombie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983490.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Fuzzy Navel mix
1/4 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Margarita Mix
1/4 cup BACARDI® Rum
1/4 cup BACARDI® Limon Flavored Rum
1/4 cup pink grapefruit juice
1/4 cup Minute Maid® Orange Juice
5 cups ice
Limes for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Fuzzy Navel mix, BACARDI(R) Mixers Margarita mix, BACARDI(R) Rums, grapefruit juice, Minute Maid Orange Juice and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with limes.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
Wake up a lifeless party with these tropical citrus Zombies. Everyone will moan with delight.
