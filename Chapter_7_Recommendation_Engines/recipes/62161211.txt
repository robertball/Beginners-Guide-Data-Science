Title:
Chicken Loaf or Ring Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups scalded milk          1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 eggs, lightly beaten       3 cups diced cooked chicken
1 cup soft bread crumbs      1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 tsp. salt                1 green pepper chopped
1/4 tsp. paprika             1 1/2 tblsp. lemon juice

Directions:
Pour hot milk slowly in a thin stream onto eggs, stirring constantly.  Add bread crumbs, salt,paprika,Worcestshire sauce,chicken,celery,green pepper, and lemon juice. Mix well, and pour into buttered loaf pan or ring mold. Bake at 300 degreesuntil knife inserted in center comes out clean - 45 to 60 minutes approximately. The ring will cook in less time than the loaf. Do not overbake. Remove from oven and le t stand 10 minutes before unmolding.

Submitter: 
Patti
Description: 
Like it's first cousin, meat loaf,good hot or cold. Fill a hot chicken ring with rice or macaroni, a cold ring with fresh salad
