Title:
Shrimp Scampi Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/475562.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons butter
4 cloves garlic (chopped)
2 pound shrimp (shelled and deveined)
1/4 cup white wine
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon butter
1 pizza dough
1 cup asiago cheese
1 cup mozzarella (grated)

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 550
Heat the oil and melt the butter in a pan.
Add garlic and saute until fragrant, about 1 minute.
Add the shrimp and saute until just cooked, about 1-3 minutes per side.
Remove the shrimp from the pan.
Add the wine and lemon juice and bring to a boil.
Simmer the liquid until reduced by about half, about 5+ minutes.
Add the 1 tablespoon of butter, wait for it to melt and turn off the heat.
Pour the mixture onto the pizza crust, add the shrimp and top with the mozzarella and asiago cheese.
Bake in a preheated 550F oven until golden brown, about 10 minutes.
