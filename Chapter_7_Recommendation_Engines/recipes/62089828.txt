Title:
"To DIE FOR"  Bean Lovers Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz can Rosarita beans (or other)
1 16 oz can Denison's Chili (hot or mild to your taste preferences)
1 4 oz can mild diced can chili peppers with juice
2  4 oz cans sliced black olives/drained
2 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup salsa or taco sause(mild, med, or HOT)
4 diced green onions including stems
1/2 cup minced fresh cilantro(a must!)
Serve with/on: tortilla chips, flour tortillas, hot dogs, baked potato or potatoe wedges

Directions:
In large bowl mix all beans, chili peppers, 1/1/2 cup cheese, salsa, cilantro, and 1 can olives.
Add salsa to get desired "spiciness"
Heat on low stirring occasionally until cheese is melted in a small-med sized crock pot.
Continue heating until dip is  melted and bubbly.
Reduce heat to simmer.
Sprinkle remaining cheese and olives before serving and as needed.
Serve with/on: tortilla chips, flour tortillas, potatoe wedges, on hot dog, or on a baked potatoe.
YUM!
