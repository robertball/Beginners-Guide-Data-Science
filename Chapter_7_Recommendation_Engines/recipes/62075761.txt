Title:
fennel corn muffin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 Cup all purpose flour
2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
pinch of salt
1 Cup Soy milk
1 egg
1 eggyolk
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup yellow cornmeal
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 1/2 Tablespoon dried thyme
1 1/2 Tablespoon dried fennel seeds
pinch of black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
1. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, thyme, fennel seeds, black pepper and salt. Stir with a whisk to break up all the lumps from flour. make a well in center of the bowl.
2. In a medium bowl with a whisk, combine rest of the ingredients.
3. pour in the wet mixture into the dry mixture. Stire just until combined.
4. Fill up till the 3/4th on well oiled muffin tins. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes, until golden on top.
5. Cool on wire rack for 5 minutes, take the muffins out from the tin and cool on wire rack for another 5-8 minutes until its cool enough to eat.
