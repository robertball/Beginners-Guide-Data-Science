Title:
Cheesy Burger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1028683.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. Butter, melted
2 C. Celery, chopped
1 Medium onion, chopped
3 Cooked Burgers, chopped
2 C.Aged Cheddar cheese, grated
3 C. Milk
2 C. Light cream
1 C. V8 juice
2 tsp. Cumin
1 tsp. Salt
2 tsp. Curry Powder
2 tsp. Celery Seed
Roux:
5 Tbsp. All purpose flour
1/4 C. Butter
1 C. Milk

Directions:
In a medium dutch oven melt 2 tablespoons butter and saute celery and onion together until tender.
Add milk, light cream, V8 juice,  chopped burger and cheese  to the vegetable mixture and heat just to the boiling point
Season the mixture with salt and spices stirring constantly and blend ingredients together well
In a small saucepan heat butter and flour and meld together adding 1 cup of milk to the sauce and stir until thickened
Add roux to the soup mix and stir until roux dissolves into the soup and heat just until mixture thickens.
Serve in soup bowls with crackers

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A cheesy soup with big chunks of burger in a tangy cheese sauce with added spices.
