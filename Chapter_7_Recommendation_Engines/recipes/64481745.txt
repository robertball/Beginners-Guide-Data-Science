Title:
New England Lobster Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2529212.jpg
Ingredients:
1 - live and kicking chicken lobster (up to 1 1/4 lb.)
2-inch piece of celery, thinly sliced (optional)
2 tsp. mayonnaise
1 squeeze fresh lemon juice
Salt and pepper to taste
1 top-split New England style hot dog roll
1 tsp. salted butter, softened
Shredded iceberg lettuce (optional)

Directions:
1.  Steam or boil lobster for 15 minutes; remove from pot and let cool. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
2.  Remove all meat from shell, discarding intestinal tract, roe and tomalley (green stuff) from the tail.  Cut meat into chunks or coarsely shred (your preference).
3.  Thoroughly mix meat with the sliced celery, mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
4.  Meanwhile, spread softened butter evenly on each side of hot dog roll and place on hot skillet or griddle and toast roll for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until nicely golden.
5.  Place shredded lettuce in bottom of roll; place dressed lobster meat on top and enjoy one of the best things about summer!
