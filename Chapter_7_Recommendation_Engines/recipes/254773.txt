Title:
Rainier Cherry Berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4458168.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
3/4 cup blueberries
1/2 cup fat-free vanilla yogurt
6 large fresh strawberries, halved
12 Rainier cherries, pitted and halved
1/4 cup fat-free milk

Directions:
Combine banana, blueberries, vanilla yogurt, strawberries, cherries, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth, about 60 seconds.

Submitter: 
courtneyswife
Description: 
This is the best smoothie I've ever concocted! The Rainier cherries taste like a blend between a peach and a cherry. They're big, juicy, and sweet.
