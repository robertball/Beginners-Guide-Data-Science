Title:
30 minute Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 can tomato soup
1 T worcestershire sauce
1 t dried thyme
1 bag frozen mixed veggies
1 6 serving package mashed potatoes, prepared
2 cups cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet saute ground beef until nearly browned, drain, add onion and saute until soft.
Add soup, worcestershire sauce, and thyme, heat on medium high until bubbly, turn heat down to medium, stir, and simmer for 5 minutes.
Add veggies, raise heat to medium high, return to a soft boil, lower heat to medium and simmer 5 minutes, stir.
Drop potatoes by large spoonful all over top, then top with cheese.  Cover with a lid (do not allow to stick to cheese) and simmer 5 more minutes or until the cheese is melted.
