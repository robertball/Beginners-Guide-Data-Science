Title:
Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Bell Peppers cut in 1/2 and cleaned
5 lbs Ground Beef
20 oz Ketchup
1/4 cup Lawerys Seasoned Salt
1/4 Season Mix
5 slices White Bread - Diced
1 Medium Onion
7 Celery Sticks - Diced
2 Tbsp Thyme
5 Eggs

Directions:
1. Cut Bell Peppers in half and clean, set them in 2-2 inch pans
2. In mixing Bowl with paddle, put all ingredients in bowl and mix on low speed for 2 minutes or until will mixed.
3. Stuff Bell Peppers
4. Cook on HIGH fan for 35-40 minutes
