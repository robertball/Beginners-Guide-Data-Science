Title:
Oriental rice vinegrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unseasoned rice vinegar
1/4 cup soy sauce
4 tsp fresh garlic
1 tbsp dry cilantro
1 tbsp dry basil
2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
2 Tbsp sesame oil (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a container.  Seal with plastic wrap or lid.  Refridgerate at least 2 hours to let flavors blend.  Serving size is 2 Tbsp.

Submitter:

Description: 
A light dressing that goes great on any green salad
