Title:
Soup's Sirloin Tips and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/365234.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. beef sirloin
3 Tbsp Olive Oil
2 Cloves garlic, minced
1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
3/4 cup red wine (I used Francis Coppola Merlot)
1/2 chipotle pepper in adobo, minced
1/2 lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/8 tsp. cinnamon
ground pepper and season salt to taste

Directions:
1. Cut beef into cubes and brown in a large skillet with olive oil and garlic
2. Add remaining ingredients and simmer over medium heat approx. 40-45 minutes or until beef is tender
