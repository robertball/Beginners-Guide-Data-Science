Title:
Vegetarian Frito Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (15-ounce) cans Hormel® Chili With Beans
1 (10-ounce) package FRITOS® corn chips

Directions:
1.In microwave-safe bowl, place chili; cover loosely. Microwave on HIGH (100%), stirring once, 3 to 5 minutes or until hot.
2.In 13x9-inch serving dish, place corn chips. Pour warm chili over chips. Top with favorite toppings as desired.
