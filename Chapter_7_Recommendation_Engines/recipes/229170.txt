Title:
Beau Monde Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982540.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup mayonnaise
2/3 cup sour cream
1 tablespoon chopped green onion
1 tablespoon Spice Islands® Dried Parsley
1/2 teaspoon Spice Islands® Beau Monde® Seasoning
1/2 teaspoon Spice Islands® Dried Dill Weed

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise, sour cream, green onion, parsley, Beau Monde Seasoning, and dill weed in a small bowl.
Cover and chill a minimum of 30 minutes to blend flavors.

Submitter: 
Spice Islands®
Description: 
Whip up this delicious Beau Monde® dip in just 5 minutes, chill for a bit, then serve with your favorite crackers, chips, and veggies.
