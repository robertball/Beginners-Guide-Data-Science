Title:
Slow Cooker Italian Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, cooked & shredded
1 onion, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 tsp chopped garlic
2 15oz cans cannellini beans, drained & rinsed
7 oz chicken broth
1 8oz can tomato sauce
2 tsp tomato paste
2 14oz canned diced tomatoes, undrained
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
4 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp dried parsley
2 bay leaves
chopped fresh parsley
shredded mozzerella cheese

Directions:
Put all ingredients in slow cooker. Cook on high for 2 hours, then low for 5 hours. Discard bay leaves.
Garnish with fresh choppped parsley and shredded mozzerella cheese if desired.

Submitter: 
Christine Foley Wanner
Description: 
An Italian twist on chili... I came up with this recipe while trying to use up leftover chicken and canned goods in my pantry.
