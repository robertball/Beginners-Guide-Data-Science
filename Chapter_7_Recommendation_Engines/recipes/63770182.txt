Title:
Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 split chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
1 small can green chilies
1 bag frozen corn, 8 oz.
1 can crushed tomatoes, 28 oz.
1 TBSP chili powder
1 TBSP ground cumin
1 tsp cayenne pepper (or less for milder soup)
2 cans chicken broth
1 can vegetable broth
1 can beef broth
-crushed tortilla chips
-shredded Monterey jack cheese
-diced avocados

Directions:
Mix all above ingredients together in large soup pot or crock pot.  Simmer on low for 2-3 hours.
