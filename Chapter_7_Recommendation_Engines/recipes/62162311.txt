Title:
Coconut Cholcolate No-Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups sugar
1/2 Cup Butter
1/2 Cup Milk
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1 Teaspoon Vanilla
3 Cups Oatmeal
1/2 Cup Cocoa powder
1 Cup Coconut flakes

Directions:
In Large sauce pan, over medium heat, mix sugar, butter, milk and salt, until it comes to a boil.
Remove from heat.
Stir in Vanilla, Oatmeal, Coconut, and Cocoa powder, until blended.
Drop by teaspoonfulls unto wax paper.
Let cool and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cynthia
Description: 
Simple No-Bake Cookies with coconut. Can be made in about 10 min. Kids love them.
