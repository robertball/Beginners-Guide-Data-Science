Title:
Scrod a la Rarebit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lb. haddock or cod
Salt and pepper to taste
2 Tbs. butter
2 Tbs. flour
1/2 Tbs. mustard
1 C. milk
1 C. Shredded cheese

Directions:
Place fish on oven-proof platter or baking dish. Make a cream sauce as follows: Melt butter over medium heat. Add flour and mustard and blend. Add milk and cook until thickened, stirring constantly. Add cheese and cook until melted.Spread over fish and bake.
