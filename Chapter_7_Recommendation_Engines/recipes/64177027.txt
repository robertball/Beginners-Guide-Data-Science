Title:
Pumpkin Patch Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
2 tsps pumpkin pie spice
1 tsp vanilla
2 large eggs
1 28 ounce can pure pumpkin puree
1 12 ounce can evaporated milk
42 3 inch tart shells

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Prebake empty tart shells for 1 minute.
Place all ingredients into a large bowl and whisk until well combined.
Bake for 22 minutes turning pan halfway through baking.
Cool for 10 minutes, refrigerate until chilled, serve with whipped cream.
