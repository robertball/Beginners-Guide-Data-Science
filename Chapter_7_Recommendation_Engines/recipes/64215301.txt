Title:
Cardi Alla Parmigiana (Cardoons With Parmesan Cheese) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small cardoon
juice of 1 lemon(medium
1/2 cup butter melted
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.

Directions:
Wash the cardoon thoroughly, discard the outer, prickly stems. Remove the strings from the inner stalks(like peeling celery)and cut into pieces about 4 inches long. Place the prepared cardoon pieces in a bowl of water adding the lemon juice straight away to prevent discoloration. Cook in boiling salted water for about 30- 40 minutes then drain.Butter a oven proof pan and cover the bottom with a layer of cardoon pieces.Pour over a little melted butter and sprinkle with some Parmesan cheese. Continue to layer the cardoon pieces,butter and cheese until all have been used up. Cook in preheated oven (350) for 20-30 minutes until lightly browned. Serve hot with extra Parmesan cheese
