Title:
Spirit in the Desert Breakfast Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Butter
2 1/2 cups Brown Sugar, packed
4 Large Eggs
2/3 cup Molasses
3 1/2 cups All-Purpose Flour
2 teaspoons Baking Soda
2 teaspoons Salt
2 teaspoons Ground Cinnamon
4 cups Oatmeal
1 cup Chopped Walnuts
2 cups Raisins, Dried Cherries, or Dried Cranberries
2 cups Semi-Sweet Chocolate Pieces or Butterscotch Chips

Directions:
Combine butter, sugar, eggs, and molasses in a large mixing bowl. Stir together flour, soda, salt, and cinnamon; add to first mixture and stir to mix well.
Stir in oatmeal, walnuts, raisins, and chocolate pieces. Spoon into a  greased 1/2 sheet baking sheet and bake in a 400 degree oven until done. Cut into bars to serve.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
I got this recipe from the chef at Spirit in the Desert Retreat Center in Carefree, Arizona. We were all blown away by the flavor (they use butterscotch chips). They were great with a cup of coffee.
