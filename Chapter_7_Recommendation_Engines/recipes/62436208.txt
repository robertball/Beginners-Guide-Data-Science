Title:
Sausage Cheese Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Jimmy Dean hot sausage
1/2 cup roasted red peppers, chopped
1/2 cup green onion, chopped
1 8 oz. pkg. Sargento 6-cheese Italian cheese
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon dried basil
3 pkgs. fillo shells

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
In a skillet over medium heat, cook sausage until evenly brown. Drain and crumble into a medium mixing bowl. Mix in roasted pepper, green onion, cheese, mayonnaise and basil. Fill each shell with the sausage mixture.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven , or until golden brown.
