Title:
Midg's Flaming Rib Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4483970.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups white sugar
1 cup seasoned salt
1/4 cup paprika
1/4 cup chili powder
1/4 cup ground black pepper
2 tablespoons ground cumin
2 tablespoons mesquite-flavored seasoning
1 tablespoon onion powder

Directions:
Mix sugar, seasoned salt, paprika, chili powder, black pepper, cumin, mesquite-flavored seasoning, and onion powder together in an airtight container. Store in a cool, dark place.

Submitter: 
Midg
Description: 
Dry rub for ribs 
and any other meats! This will keep for 3 to 4 months.
