Title:
Easiest Enchilada Casserole Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/390837.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12 oz. pkg. plain corn tortilla chips.
1 Lg. (24-28 oz.)can enchilada sauce, red or green.
16 oz. Mexican cheese shredded
2-3 C. refried beans. (canned or make your own)
2.lb. pre-cooked meat of your choice (ground beef w/taco seasoning, left over roast pork or beef or shredded chicken.)
1 16 oz. jar of salsa desired heat.

Directions:
Crush corn chips slightly, you don't want all crumbs. Spread them evenly into a 3-4 quart casserole sprayed with nonstick oil.
Pour enchilada sauce over chips evenly.
Layer 1/2 the cheese on top of sauce.
Heat the beans to a spreading consistancy and pour over cheese.
Layer meat of your choice evenly.
Pour salsa over meat and the top with remaining cheese.
Cover and bake at *350 for 45 min. - 1 hr.
Offer sour cream, pico de gallo and shredded lettuce for a complete Mexican meal.

Submitter: 
Buffy Ehlers Whitney
Description: 
Delicious layers of mexican ingredients used in a make ahead form of a casserole will feed a croud without all the fuss of traditional enchiladas yet you still get the full flavor with eaze and convenience.
