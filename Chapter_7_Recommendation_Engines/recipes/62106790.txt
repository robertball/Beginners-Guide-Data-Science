Title:
Doggie Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cornmeal
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups unbleached flour
1 egg
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 teaspoons chopped parsley
Â½ cup chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Combine cornmeal, salt, and unbleached flour in a medium bowl.
In a separate mixing bowl, beat egg with vegetable oil, then add chopped parsley and chicken broth. Mix well.
Gradually add the flour mixture. Knead dough, then roll out to half-inch thickness.
Using a knife or cookie cutters, cut dough into shapes your pet will like.
Bake for 15 minutes or until firm.

Submitter: 
Allie G.
Description: 
This is a dog biscuit recipe my Pembroke Corgi loves to eat again and again! For more fun, use cookie cutters with shapes like cats, fire hydrants, shoes, or bones.
