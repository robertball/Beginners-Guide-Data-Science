Title:
Fiesta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/664842.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies, drained
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 tablespoon lemon or lime juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
3 1/2 cups cooked rice, chilled
2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
1 green onion, sliced
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
Lettuce Leaves
1 medium tomato, chopped
1 medium avocado, peeled and sliced
1/4 cup ripe olives, sliced
Tortilla chips

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine first six ingredients. Stir in rice, chicken, green onion and green pepper. Cover and chill for 1-2 hours. Serve on a lettuce-lined platter; top with tomato, avocado, olives and chips.

Submitter: 
Connie  Bolton
Description: 
If you're tired of the same old chicken salad, try this slightly spicy version. It's attractive and tasty, so it's perfect for every day and special occasions.
