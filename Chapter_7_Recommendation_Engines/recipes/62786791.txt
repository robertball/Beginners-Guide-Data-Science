Title:
Pineapple Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups diced fresh pineapple
1/2 cup finely chopped sweet onion (such as Vidalia)
2 or 3 plum tomatoes, seeded and chopped
1/2 cup bell pepper, red or green, chopped
1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
2 Tbsp fresh lime juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients together.
Best if made a day early so the flavors can blend.
Serve with tortilla chips or with grilled chicken or fish.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
My version of a fruit salsa we made in a Knife Skills class.  You can use pineapple, mango or even peaches!
