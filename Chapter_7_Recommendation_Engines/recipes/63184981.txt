Title:
Apple Pecan Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8cups thinly sliced apples
1 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 1/2 cup chopped pecans
2 cup all-purpose flour
2 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 egg, beaten
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1/3 cup butter, melted
3/4 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Generously grease a 2 quart baking dish.
Arrange apple slices in an even layer in the baking dish. In a small bowl, mix together 1 cup sugar, cinnamon, and 1 cup pecans. Sprinkle mixture over apples.
In a medium bowl, mix together flour, 1 cup brown sugar, baking powder & soda, and salt. In a separate bowl whisk together egg, evaporated milk, and melted butter. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture all at once, and stir until smooth. Pour mixture over apples, and sprinkle top with 1/4 cup pecans.
Bake in the preheated oven for 55 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
Sliced apples are layered in a baking dish, sprinkled with sugared pecans, and topped with a mixture of flour, butter, evaporated milk, sugar and baking powder. The top of the cobbler is then sprinkled with more pecans, and it 's baked until bubbly and beautiful.Modified from: Apple Pecan Cobbler
