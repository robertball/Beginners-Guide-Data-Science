Title:
Easy Chicken Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken Breasts
Onions
Bell Pepper (Green and Red)
Fajita Seasoning (preferably from Mexico)
Olive Oil
Mexican Cheese shredded
Sour Cream
Guacamole
Tortillas (Optional)

Directions:
Nonstick spray a casserole dish
Place desired number of chicken breasts in bottom (can make into strips or not)
Sprinkle with Fajita Seasoning on all sides
Top with Onions, Bell Peppers
Drizzle with Olive Oil
Bake 350 degree oven for 40 minutes or until juices run clear and chicken is 165 degrees internally.
Remove from oven and serve on or off tortillas with shredded cheese, sour cream and guacamole.
