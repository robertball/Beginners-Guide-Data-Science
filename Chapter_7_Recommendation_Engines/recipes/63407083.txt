Title:
Three Cheese Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 container (15 oz) part-skim ricotta cheese
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
2 eggs, beaten
1 jar (28 oz) spaghetti sauce
1 (16 oz) box ziti pasta, cooked and drained
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In large bowl, combine ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese and eggs; Set aside.  In another bowl, combine pasta and spaghetti sauce.
In 13x9-inch baking dish, spoon 1/2 of the pasta mixture.  Evenly top with cheese mixture, then remaining pasta mixture.  Cover with foil and bake 30 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This is a good basic ziti recipe.  You can add meat or fresh vegetables if you like.
