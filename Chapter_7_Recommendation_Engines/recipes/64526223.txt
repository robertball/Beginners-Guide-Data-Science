Title:
Big Ray's Pepper Jack Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2991535.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 Cup shredded pepper jack cheese
4 Tbsp cold unsalted butter cut into pieces
2-3 Tbsp half-n-half

Directions:
Preheat oven to400 degrees.
Flour baking sheet.
Put  flour, salt, cheese and butter into food processor fitted with steel blade.  Pulse until flour and butter are well combined.  Add 1 Tbsp half-n-half at a time while machine is running.  Add just enough for the dough to come together., but not too sticky.
Place dough on sheet of wax paper and flatten with hands.  Cover dough with another sheet of wax paper and roll into thin circle, about 1/8" thick.
Transfer dough to floured baking sheet.  Score with knife or sharp pizza cutter into small squares or shape desired.  Move crackers so there is room between each one.
Bake 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.  Remove from oven and immediately transfer to wire rack.  Serve warm or store in tin for up to 3 days Makes 48 crackers.
