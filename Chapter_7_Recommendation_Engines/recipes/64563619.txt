Title:
Meatball Sub Simmer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar (26 oz) spaghetti sauce
36 frozen cooked meatballs (1 lb)
6 sandwich buns partially split
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup whater

Directions:
Mix spaghetti sauce and 1 cup water in a slow cooker; stir well. Add meatballs; stir gently until evenly coated. Cover and cook on LOW for 6-8 hours (or on HIGH for 2-4 hrs). Fill buns with meatballs and sauce; sprinkle with cheese.
