Title:
Mini Dump Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3596452.jpg
Ingredients:
Canned fruit pie filling
1 package Yellow Cake Mix
Butter or coconut oil, chilled
Chopped pecans, walnuts, or macadamias; and/or sliced almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Fill bottom of mini baking dish with fruit pie filling.
Cover with a layer of cake mix – about 1/4 inch thick.
Top with sliced pats of butter, covering the cake mix as much as possible.
Sprinkle with nuts.
Place mini cakes on a cookie sheet or foil and bake for about 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into cake layer comes out clean.
Let cool slightly and serve with Reddi-wip topping.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
This recipe doesn’t have specific measurements. It’s all about the layers and balance, and will depend on how large your mini bakers are. Serve them while they’re still warm, and top with Reddi-wip topping {and ice cream if you like} for a delicious dessert treat!
