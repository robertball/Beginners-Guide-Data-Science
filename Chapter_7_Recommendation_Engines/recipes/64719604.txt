Title:
Redi Burger (Quick Vegetarian Burgers) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6174201.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C Hot Water
1/4 C Oil
1/4 C Soy Sauce
1 C Rolled Oats
1/4 C Yeast Flakes
1/4 C Soy Flour
1/4 C Wheat Germ
1/2 C Vital Gluten or Do Pep
1 T Garlic Powder
1 T Onion Powder
1/2 t Celery Seed
1 t Sage or Poultry Seasoning

Directions:
Mix well.
Greese wide mouth jars and place sterilized canning lids and rings on jars.
Place in pressure cooker and pressure on light rock (10 lbs pressure) for 50 minutes.
Serve by sliding out of jar and slicing into thickness of burger you would like and you can fry to rewarm or just serve out of jar.
Makes great sandwiches.
