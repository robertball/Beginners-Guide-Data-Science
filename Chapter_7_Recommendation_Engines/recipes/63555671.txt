Title:
Great Grandma Electra's Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter, softened
8 oz. cream cheese, room temperature
3 cups sugar
6 eggs
3 cups flour
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 T.  vanilla

Directions:
Heat oven to 300 degrees.  Spray a bundt pan with non-stick spray.  Place oven rack to lowest level.  Beat together the butter, cream cheese and sugar until creamy.  Add the eggs, one at a time until smooth.  Sift together the dry ingredients and stir into the batter.  Stir in the vanilla.  Pour into the bundt pan and bake at 300 degrees for 90 minutes or until toothpick inserted comes out clean.  Apply a thin layer of butter on top while hot.  Best right of the oven!  Enjoy!
