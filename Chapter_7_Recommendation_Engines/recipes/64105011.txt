Title:
Paper Towel Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. sifted powdered sugar
1/2 cup or 1 stick salted butter, softened
1 1/2 cups solid vegetable shortening
2 tbsp. clear vanilla extract
1/4 tsp. almond extract (Optional)
1/3 cup water for icing cake -OR- 1/4 cup for stiff consistency
VIVA Paper Towels

Directions:
Cream butter, shortening, and extracts until creamy and smooth.
Gradually add powdered sugar and water while mixing thoroughly.  Use medium speed for hand mixers, low speed for stand mixers, until smooth and creamy. Do not overmix or mix on high speed.
Apply frosting.
Let set for 15 minutes before smoothing
Lay paper towel across area to be smoothed, tap and smooth the area and peel away the paper towel.

Submitter: 
Scott Kallen
Description: 
This is an amazing frosting recipe using VIVA (apparently only VIVA).  An incredibly tasty, smooth and durable recipe.  When you use the paper towel in conjunction with this frosting, the results appear as smooth as fondant.  Amazing.
