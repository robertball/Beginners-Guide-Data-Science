Title:
French Vanilla Coffee Creamer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/762084.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (14 oz.) Sweetened Condensed Milk
14 oz. milk (your preference)
2 tsp. pure vanilla extract

Directions:
Pour condensed milk in to a medium mixing bowl. Using condensed milk can, measure milk, and add to bowl. Add vanilla. Mix well. Refrigerate in a sealed container.
