Title:
Ready Crust速 Chocolate Banana Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976387.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) READY CRUST速 Chocolate Pie Crust
1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding and pie filling
1 cup cold milk
1 (8 ounce) container frozen non-dairy whipped topping, thawed
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 bananas, sliced
chocolate sundae syrup (optional)

Directions:
In mixing bowl, combine pie filling, milk, vanilla, and 1/2 of the whipped topping; mix on low speed until blended.  Increase speed and beat until thick, about 2 minutes.
Pour 1/3 of mixture into crust; layer sliced bananas on top, reserving 1/2 banana for garnish. Pour remaining filling into crust.  Chill 2 hours.
Garnish with remaining bananas, whipped topping and optional chocolate sundae syrup.
