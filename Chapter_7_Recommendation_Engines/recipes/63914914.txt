Title:
Bills Low Sodium Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds petite sirloin
3 Tablespoons Olive oil
1 Teaspoon Minced onion
1/4 Cup cooking sherry
1/2 Teaspoon Mrs Dash Table Blend
1/2 Teaspoon fresh Rosemary (substitute dry)
1 Quart Pacific Low Sodium Beef Broth
1 1/2 Teaspoons Fresh chopped Rosemary (substitute dry)
1 1/2 Teaspoons Fresh chopped parsley
1/2 Teaspoon fresh ground rainbow pepper (substitute black if needed)
1/2 Teaspoon Mrs. Dash Table Blend
3 Large Potatoes
1/2 lb. Baby carrots chopped
4-5 Celery Stalks very finely chopped
1 Large white Onion very finely chopped
Create a flour rue and thicken to desired consistancy

Directions:
Cube Sirloin to about 1/2" cubes
Place in a Large glass covered skillet (vented) or a dutch oven, add Olive oil, minced onion, 1/2 teaspoon Mrs Dash, 1/2 teaspoon rosemary (chopped finely).  Simmer until sirloin is fairly brown and add in cooking sherry, brown an additional 15 minutes.  Add the remaining spices and beef broth, bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 1.5 hours.  Stir in your vegetables, bring back to a boil and simmer an additional 1 hour.  Stir in your rue slowly until it reaches your desired consistency, let cool briefly and serve hot.
