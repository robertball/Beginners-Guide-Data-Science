Title:
Pico de Gallo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018601.jpg
Ingredients:
6 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
1/2 red onion, minced
3 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
1/2 lime, juiced
1 pinch garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Stir the tomatoes, onion, cilantro, lime juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. Refrigerate at least 3 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
