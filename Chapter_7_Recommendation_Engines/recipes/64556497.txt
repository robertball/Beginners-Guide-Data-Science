Title:
Barbecued Wings Atlanta Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 chicken wings (about 2 1/4 #)
1/2 C cola-type soda (not diet)
1/2 C Maull's barbecue sauce
2 T brown sugar
1-2 T hot pepper sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
Rinse wings and pat dry
Divide at joint, discarding wing tips
In small saucepan, combine remaining ingredients
Bring to boil over medium-high heat
Reduce heat and simmer, stirring frequently, for 15-20 minutes or until sauce reduces and thickens
Place wings on grill over medium hot heat
Grill, turning wings frequently, for 20-25 minutes or until done, brushing wings with sauce during last 10-15 minutes

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Spicy-sweet sauce made with cola and barbecue
