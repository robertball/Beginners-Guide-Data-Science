Title:
Dairy-Free Chocolate Peanut Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5548822.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 frozen banana, cut into chunks
2 tablespoons smooth peanut butter
2 teaspoons cocoa powder

Directions:
Blend almond milk, banana, peanut butter, and cocoa powder together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Ariel Burn
Description: 
This is a delicious smoothie that works great as a breakfast! It's dairy-free too.
