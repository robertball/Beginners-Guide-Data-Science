Title:
Creamy Avocado and Greek Yogurt Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Plain Fat-free Greek Yogurt
2 Ripe Avocados, peeled and seeded
1 Clove Garlic, minced
1/4 cup Chopped Fresh Cilantro
1 Tbsp Finely Chopped Jalapeño pepper
2 Tbsp Lime Juice
1/4 tsp Cumin
Salt, Cayenne, and Pepper, to taste
Pita Chips, Tortilla Chips, or Crudite serving

Directions:
Place the yogurt, avocados, garlic, cilantro, jalapeño, lime juice, and cumin in a blender or food processor. Mix until smooth. Season with salt, cayenne, and pepper, to taste.
2. Scrape dip into a serving bowl and serve with pita chips, tortilla chips, or crudite.

Submitter: 
fordmarie
Description: 
Avocados make such smashing dips, plus when you add some Greek yogurt, you add a good kick of protein into it.
