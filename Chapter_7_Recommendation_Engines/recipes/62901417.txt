Title:
Chicken Mole Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fat-free chicken broth
1 onion, small
1 clove garlic, fresh diced
4 teaspoons chili powder
1 oz bittersweet chocolate
4 pieces walnut halves
1/8 teaspoon cinnamon, ground
1/4 teaspoon table salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 1/2pounds chicken breasts, uncooked boneless/skinless, sliced into strips
6 flour tortillas
2 cups shredded lettuce
2 medium tomatoes
1/4 cup low-fat sour cream

Directions:
Heat 1/4 cup broth in a large skillet. Add onion and garlic.  Saut√© 2 minutes.
Pour into blender; add remaining broth, chili powder, chocolate, walnuts, cinnamon, salt and pepper.  Blend to a puree.
Pour mixture into skillet; simmer 1 minute to heat.  Add chicken strips. Cover and simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
Assemble wraps consisting of tortillas wrapped around chicken, lettuce, tomatoes, and sour cream.

Submitter: 
rev'd up chef
Description: 
Chocolate in a chicken dish?  Absolutely! It's delicious when combined with cinnamon, chili powder and other seasonings.  This Mexican specialty more than proves the point.
