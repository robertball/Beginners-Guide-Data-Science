Title:
Marinated Baked Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon soy sauce
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 tablespoons cider vinegar
2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoon lemon juice
4 tablespoons brown sugar
4 tablespoons ketchup
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon black pepper
6 boneless pork chops

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small bowl, thoroughly blend soy sauce, vegetable oil, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, brown sugar, onion powder, garlic powder, black pepper and ketchup.
Place pork chops in a medium baking dish, and spread with 1/2 the sauce.
Cover and Marinate at least 1 hour in refrigerator.
Remove from refrigerator at least 30 minutes before time to put in the oven.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cover dish with aluminum foil
Bake pork chops 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Turn, and spread with remaining sauce. Recover and continue baking 15 minutes (remove foil for last 5 minutes), or until internal temperature of the chops has reached 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
