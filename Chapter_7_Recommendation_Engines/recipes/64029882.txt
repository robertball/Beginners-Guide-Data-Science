Title:
bisquick substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 cups flour
7 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp salt
1 cup shortening

Directions:
Using a pastry blender, cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse meal. Store sealed in pantry or refrigerator.
BISCUITS - add 1/3 cup milk to cup of mix.
PANCAKES - 3/4 cup milk and 1 egg to a cup of mix
