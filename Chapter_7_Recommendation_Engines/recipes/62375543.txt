Title:
Vegan Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans artichoke hearts
2 boxes frozen spinach
1 cup Tofutti sour cream
1 cup Veganaise
6 oz Tofutti cream cheese
2/3 cup white Daiya cheese
1/2 cup nutritional yeast
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Cook spinach according to the directions on package and drain. Drain the artichokes and chop them. Mix the artichokes and spinach with the remaining ingredients. Put in greased 1 quart dish and bake at 325 degrees for 20-30 minutes. Serve with tortilla chips.
