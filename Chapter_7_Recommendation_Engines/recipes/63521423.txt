Title:
Barbecue Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 TBL; Paprika
5 TBL; Garlic Powder
3 TBL; Chili Powder
3 TBL; Pepper ... black, ground
3 TBL; Pepper ... red, flakes
2 TBL; Salt ... kosher
2 TBL; Celery Seed ... crushed
2 TBL; Oregano ... dried, crushed
2 TBL; Thyme ... crushed
2 TBL; Allspice ... crushed
2 TBL; Coriander Seed ... crushed
2 TBL; Mustard Seed ... preferably equal parts yellow & brown
1 tsp; Accent

Directions:
Using mortar & pestel, crush all ingredients together.  Take workable amounts, crush together & remove to storing bowl.  Repeat multiple times until all ingredients have melded together completely.
As an alternative to hand crushing, the use of a coffee grinder (or small food processor) is okay.
