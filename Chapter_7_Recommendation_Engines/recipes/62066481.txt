Title:
Pina Colada Sauce and Coconut Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. raw jumbo shrimp - peeled, deveined, butterflied
1 3/4 cups bread crumbs
2 cups  coconut flakes
1 1/2 cup corn starch - divided
1 1/2 cup bottle pi単a colada drink mix - divided
2 Tbls. powdered sugar
1/3 cup Rum
1/3 cup canned crushed pineapple - drained
1/3 cup sour cream

Directions:
Place 1 cup corn starch in a bowl- put aside.
Combine bread crumbs, 1/2 cup cornstarch, and coconut flakes in a separate bowl- put aside.
combine 1 cup pi単a colada drink mix, powdered sugar, and rum in a separate bowl-put aside.
Coat shrimp in cornstarch, then dip in pi単a colada mixture, then in coconut mixture, back into pi単a colada mixture, and back into coconut mixture.
Fry until golden brown in oil, 375 degrees and drain.
For the pina colada sauce, combine sour cream,  1/2 cup pina colada drink mix, and pineapple.

Description: 
This recipe takes you some place tropical! Enjoy it with the leftover pina colada mix and rum and have a Hawaiian night to remember! After kids are asleep of course!
