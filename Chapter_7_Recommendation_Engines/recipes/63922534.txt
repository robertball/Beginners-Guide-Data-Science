Title:
Salmon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1087709.jpg
Ingredients:
250g Drained boneless skinless canned salmon, flaked (or better yet grill your own)
3 Celery stalks chopped fine
1/3 Small red onion, chopped fine
1 Stalk green onion, chopped fine
1tspn Chili powder (absolutely crucial)
2 tbsp Mayo
Optional: Baguette if you want to make sandwiches with this salad, which can be topped with fresh sliced cucumber to add some freshness

Directions:
1. Mix ingredients.
2. Eat as is, or top on baguette slices and top with cucumber

Submitter: 
NomNomNomz
Description: 
A quick and easy salmon salad, full of flavour. Can be eaten as a side, or spread over baguette slices and topped with fresh cucumber. Created and tested after reviewing all salmon salad recipes and reviews. Yum!
