Title:
Big Ray's Stuffed Sweet Pepper Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3929475.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
8 cups water
4 cups tomato juice
3 medium sweet red pepper, diced
1-1/2 cups chili sauce
1 cup uncooked long grain rice
2 celery ribs, diced
1 large onion, diced
2 tsp browning sauce, optional
3 tsp chicken bouillon granules
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, over medium heat, cook beef until no longer pink; drain. Add the remaining ingredients; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 1 hour or until rice is tender.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Tomatoes, peppers, garlic and onions are the mainstays of my garden. I love stuffed peppers but they can be a lot of work.  This soup takes all the taste without all the work.
