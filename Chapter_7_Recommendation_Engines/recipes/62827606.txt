Title:
Sweet and Sour Spare Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs spare ribs
1/3 cup flour
1 tsp dry mustard
1/3 cup soy sauce
1 tsp vegetable oil
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 inch fresh ginger, diced
1/3 cup vinegar
1.5 cups water
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp salt
1 small onion, diced

Directions:
Chop ribs into small pieces. Mix flour, mustard and soy sauce and marinate ribs in this mixture for 30 mins to 1 hour.
Heat oil. Add crushed garlic and ginger.
Brown ribs, add vinegar, water, sugar, salt and onion.
Simmer for 1 hour.
