Title:
Blackberry Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. peeled and sliced fresh peaches
1 -2 c. fresh blackberries
1/4 c. white sugar
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1 Tbl. lemon juice
1/2 c. gluten free flour
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. quick oats
1 1/2 c. gluten free flour
3/4 c. butter

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Lightly grease a 9 x 13-inch baking dish.
2. Mix peaches, blackberries, cinnamon, lemon juice, sugar and flour in a bowl; pour into the prepared baking dish and spread into an even layer.
3. Stir the brown sugar, oats, and 1 1/2 c. flour together in a separate bowl.  Cut the butter into the flour mixture until the mixture resembles course crumbs; sprinkle evenly over the fruit mixture.
4. bake in the preheated oven until golden brown on top, about 45 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
the best crisp I have ever made!  Easily substituted to gluten and/or dairy free.
