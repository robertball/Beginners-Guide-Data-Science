Title:
KUTIA-- Ukrainian wheat delicacy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup pearled wheat
6 cups water
pinch of salt
1 cup honey
1/2 cup poppy seeds
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Wash wheat well.Place in a heavy pot,add the water and soak overnight.Do not drain.Add salt.Bring to a boil and skim the top.Turn to low heat,cover and simmer 4-5 hours or until kernels pop open.Stir occasionally.
Wash the poppy seeds.Scald and simmer for 5 minutes.Drain.Grind with a coffee grinder.Combine poppy seeds,honey and 1 cup of boiling water.Stir into wheat.Cool.Add nuts before serving.

Submitter: 
koko
Description: 
Kutia is only made for Ukrainian Christmas Eve dinner when there will be 12 meatless dishes,symbolizing the twelve apostles.When the first star appears,the meal begins with kutia[cooked wheat with honey and poppyseed.]Kutia represents peace,prosperity and good health.
