Title:
Cheddar Garlic Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Biscuit Mix
2/3 Cup Milk
1/2 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
2 Tablespoons Margarine or Butter, Melted
1/4 Teaspoon Garlic Powder, Divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  Stir biscuit mix, milk, cheese and 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder until soft dough forms.  Drop by tablespoon fulls onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.  Stir together margarine and remaining 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder, brush over warm biscuits.

Submitter: 
Jackie C
Description: 
Great savory biscuits that go awesome with a steak or seafood dish...or just about any dish in my house...heck we even eat them by themselves.
