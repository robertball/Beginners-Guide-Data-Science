Title:
Sweet Teriyaki sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/811932.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup Mirin
1/3 cup Soy sauce
1/3 cup Rice vinegar
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 tbs Honey
1.5 tsp fresh ginger minced
1 Clove garlic minced
1 tsp minced onion
2 tsp sliced chives
1 tsp sesame seeds toasted
1 pinch black pepper
1/3 cup water
1-1/2 tbs cornstarch

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small sauce pan except cornstarch and water. Bring to a boil and reduce to a simmer for 5 min.  Mix cornstarch with water and wisk in until thick. Serve hot or place in jar and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Pitchfork
Description: 
This is the sweet, sticky, yummy sauce you would find at a American restaurant like Panda or Yoshinoya. It goes great on chicken or beef. I was trying to replicate the sauce my favorite terry bowle place makes and could find nothing online that was close.
