Title:
Smarter Fettuccine Alfredo (Kraft recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
220g whole wheat fettuccine, uncooked
2 Cup low sodium chicken broth (1 tbsp epicure veg broth)
1.5 Tbsp flour
2/3 Cup Light cream cheese
4 Tbsp Lite parmesean cheese
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cracked pepper
1/4 tsp garlic powder
3 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley (or pinch of dried parsley)

Directions:
COOK pasta as directed
MIX broth and flour with wisk in medium saucepan.  Stir in cream cheese, 2 Tbsp parmesean cheese, nutmeg, pepper and garlic powder; bring to boil, stirring contantly. Cook for about 2 minutes or until thick.
DRAIN pasta; return to pot.  Add sauce; toss to coat.  Top with remaining parmesean cheese and sprinkle with parsley for garnish.
