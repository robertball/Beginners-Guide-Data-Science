Title:
Dreamsicle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 oz. amaretto
3/4 oz. vodka
1.5 oz. half'n'half cream
3 oz. orange juice

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients with ice in a shaker. Shake well and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with a wedge of orange.

Submitter: 
JeanieMomof3
Description: 
Very yummy! You can substitute vanilla ice cream and make this in a blender for a frozen drink.
