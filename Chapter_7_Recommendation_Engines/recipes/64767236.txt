Title:
Duck in Walnut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  Duck
4 oz. Mayonnaise
3 oz of Ground Walnuts
1 clove of Garlic
1/2 Tsp. of Herbs Provence
pinch of: ground paprika, Curry, Coriander
Salt & Pepper.

Directions:
Boil the Duck in Salted water until done and cut into portions. keep warm in
a serving Dish.  While it is still warm prepare the sauce. Take the Ground walnuts and Garlic and add the Mayonnaise  and Herbs. Then;  add enough cooled Duck Fond to make a suitable sauce and pour over the Duck;  Serve.
