Title:
Korean Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5653111.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs baby potatoes cut into bite size quarters (or smaller if your potatoes are large)
2 T vegtable oil
1/2 c water
2 1/2 T low salt soy sauce
2 T corn syrup
1 1/2 T sugar
2 T ketchup
1 T minced garlic
Red pepper to taste (couple shakes to 1/8 t)
1 t sesame seeds

Directions:
Heat wok with oil and stir fry on high/med high for 5-7 min, turning  and checking potatoes until they are half cooked and slightly seared.
While potatoes are cooking, mix together the water, soy sauce, corn syrup, sugar, ketchup, garlic and red pepper in a bowl.
When potatoes are half cooked, add sauce mixture, reduce heat, and simmer for 6-8 min until cooked through, and sauce thickens
Sprinkle with sesame seeds and toss to coat.
