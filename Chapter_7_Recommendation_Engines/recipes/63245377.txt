Title:
Coconut Crepes / Love Letter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11/2 cups all-purpose flour
1tablespoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups milk
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 eggs
1/2 cups coconut grated
1-2tbs sugar
1/4tsp cardamom powder

Directions:
1.In a large bowl, sift together flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Stir in the milk, eggs and vanilla extract; mix until smooth.
2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
3. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, turn and cook the other side.
4. mix together coconut grated, cardamom powder and sugar.
5. Spread the coconut mixture on a crepe and fold as envelope or in half.
6.Tranfer to plates and serve as it is or with some sauce.
