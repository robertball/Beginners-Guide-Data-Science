Title:
Cheesy Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups [500mL] All Purpose Flour
2 cups [500mL] grated Cheddar cheese, divided
1 tsp [5mL] salt
1 can [370mL] Carnation® 2% or Fat Free Evaporated Skim Milk
½ cup [125mL] water
4   eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ºF (180ºC). Place 12 cup muffin pan in oven while it is heating up.
Combine flour, 1 cup (250mL) grated cheese and salt in a large bowl.
Combine evaporated milk and water and heat to 125ºF/60ºC (approximately 1 minute on high in the microwave) and whisk with eggs. Add to flour mixture. Do not over mix. Don’t worry if there are a few lumps.
Remove hot pan from the oven. Spray with non-stick cooking spray. Put 1/3 cup (75mL) batter into each cup. Sprinkle with remaining 1 cup (250mL) grated cheese.
Bake in preheated oven 35-40 minutes until puffed and browned. Serve hot.
