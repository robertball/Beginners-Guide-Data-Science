Title:
Hamberger Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500 gm lean Hamburger
1/2 cup chopped onion
3 tbsp flour
1 clove garlic, smashed
16 oz can tomatoes
8 oz can corn
8 oz green beans
Dumplings
1 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp dry mustard
2 tbsp shortening
2/3 cup milk
3 oz shredded cheese - optional

Directions:
Fry hamburger, garlic and onion till brown, drain the fat
Add the flour, salt and pepper .
Stir in the tomatoes and cook over medium heat until thick
Add corn and beans making sure they are drained first, then add to meat mixture
Transfer to a casserole dish
Sift the flour, baking soda and mustard.
Cut in shortening with a pastry knife
Blend in milk and half of cheese to form oft dough
Drop dough on top of meat mixture, then top with remaining cheese
Bake uncovered at 350 for 30 minutes
