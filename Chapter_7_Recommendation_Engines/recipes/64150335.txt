Title:
Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 tsp smoked paprika
2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
5lbs boneless pork shoulder
1 cup water
soft sandwich buns
Frank's Red Hot Sauce
Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ sauce
Cole Slaw

Directions:
Combine seasonings in a small bowl and rub evenly over pork.
Place meat in 6qt slow cooker.  Add water.  Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 4-5 hours, until pork is very tender.
Remove pork to large cutting board and let it rest for 10-15 minutes.  Pull, slice, or chop, removing fat along the way.
Serve on buns with hot sauce, BBQ sauce, and cole slaw.

Basic pulled pork recipe - easy to make into BBQ pulled pork sandwiches, or can be used in pork tacos or other recipes that require pulled pork.  Recipe from Costco Connection Aug 2014 www.costcoconnection.com/connection/201408/?pg=61#pg61
