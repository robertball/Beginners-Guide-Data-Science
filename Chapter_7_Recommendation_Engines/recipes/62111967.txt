Title:
Pineapple French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter or margarine
1/2 C. brown sugar
1 can 20 oz. crushed pineapple(Drain and reserve juice)
3 eggs, beaten or (8 oz Egg substitute)
3/4 C. milk
cinnamon
Day old sliced bread, may use day old French

Directions:
Preheat oven 425
Place the stick or butter or margarine on a cookie sheet with sides or jelly roll pan and melt in preheated oven.
Sprinkel brown sugar over melted butter/margarine.
Add the drained crushed pineapple and spread evenly over sugar/butter mixture.
Beat eggs and milk together.  Add 2 tbsp. pineapple juice to the egg mixture.
Pour mixture into a pie dish and dip bread to quickly coat both sides. Place bread on top of pineapple mixture and sprinle with cinnamon.
Bake 12-15 minutes.
When ready to serve, Flip over bread slices so that it looks like pineapple up-side down cake.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Children love this recipe and it's easy to prepare. Nice for overnight guests to be served this for breakfast or brunch. Just add bacon, ham or sausage to complete the menu.
