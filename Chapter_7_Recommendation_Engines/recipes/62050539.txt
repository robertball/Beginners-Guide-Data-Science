Title:
Strawberry Pretzel Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups crushed pretzels
3 Tablespoons white sugar
1/2 cup melted butter
12 oz cream cheese, softened
1 cup white sugar
8 oz tub whipped topping, thawed
2 cups boiling water
1 (8oz)package strawberry gelatin
1 1/2 cups cold water
2 pints fresh strawberries, sliced

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 350. Mix crushed pretzels, sugar and butter. Press into 13x9 pan and bake 10 minutes. Cool completely.
2. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.  Gently fold in whipped topping. Spread over pretzel crust and chill for 30 minutes.
3. Dissolve gelatin in bowl with boiling water. Stir in cold water and refridgerate until thickened, about 1 1/2 hours. Stir in sliced strawberries and spread over cheese layer.
4. Refrigerate at least 3 hours or until firm.  Cut into squares and serve with additional whipped topping if desired.
