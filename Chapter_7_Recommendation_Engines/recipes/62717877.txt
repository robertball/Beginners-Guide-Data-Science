Title:
White Chocolate Cranberry Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C flour
2/3 C sugar
1 C butter, softened
2 eggs, beaten
1 (300 ml) can sweetened condensed milk
1 C white chocolate chips
1 C dried cranberries
1 (200 g) pkg shredded coconut
1/2 C semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 350
In food processor or large bowl, combine flour and sugar. Pulse or cut in butter to form fine crumbs. Press firmly in to 13x9-inch baking pan. Bake about 12 minutes, until lightly browned.
In large bowl, combine eggs, milk. Stir in white chips, cranberries and coconut. Spread over crust, return to oven; bake 18 to 22 minutes, until set in centre.
Melt semi-sweet chocolate chips in microwave in small plastic bag on MEDIUM-HI. Cut small corner in bag; drizzle chocolate over top. Let cool completely, cut in to squares.
