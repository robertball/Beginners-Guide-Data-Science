Title:
Maggiano's Spaghetti and Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1854706.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp garlic, finely minced
3/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, shredded
1 tsp Salt
1 1/4 lb ground beef
1 1/2 TBSP fresh parsley, chopped
1 large egg
6 TBSP milk
1 TBSP extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Combine the garlic, bread crumbs, cheese and salt together.
Mix beef, parsley, egg & milk together with bread crumb mixture.
Mix well by hand until all ingredients are evenly distributed.
Mix only until combined and form into 16 evenly sized meatballs (approximately 2 oz per meatball.
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Add meatballs and brown on all sides (approximately 1 min/side).
Finish cooking in a 350 degree oven (about 10 minutes).
Add meatballs to pasta sauce and cook on medium heat for 10 minutes to marry the sauce with the meatballs.
