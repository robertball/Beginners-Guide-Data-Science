Title:
Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1702977.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tube refrigerated crescent rolls. 8 oz
1 lb breakfast sausage
2 cups frozen shredded hash browns thawed
6 eggs
1/2 c milk
Salt/pepper
2 c shredded pepper jack cheese

Directions:
Arrange rolls on bottom of 9 x 13 baking dish
Layer... Browned sausage and potatoes
Beat eggs, milk, salt/pepper in bowl
Pour egg mixture over potatoes
Top with cheese
Bake in 375 degree oven for 30 minutes
Let set before serving

Submitter: 
Lynn Mauk
Description: 
Great breakfast casserole for entertaining.  Use leftover Holiday Ham or Sausage.  Lots of flexibility.
