Title:
Easy Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/833678.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cube butter
chicken tenders or chicken breasts
Italian Seasonings Salad Dressing Mix
Lemon Juice from fresh lemons.

Directions:
Put cube of butter in bottom of crock pot.
Lay chicken in pot.
Sprinkle dressing over chicken, drizzle with lemon juice, pop the lid on.
Cook on Hi  4-5 hours, or Low â€“ 6-8 hours.
Optional:
Take  the chicken out, shred with 2 forks, then ladle some of the juice over your shredded chicken. It will soak it up, and make it sooo juicy.
