Title:
Bumbleberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5280714.jpg
Ingredients:
Pastry for 2 crust pie
1 cup sugar
1/3 cup flour
1 cup sliced apples
3 cups mixed berries (raspberries, blackberries strawberries etc)
1 cup rhubarb, in short lengths
(if you don't have rhubarb add more apples and berries)
1/2 tsp granulated sugar

Directions:
Roll pastry and line 9 inch pie plate.  Roll top crust
Stir first amount of sugar and flour together in large bowl
Add apples, raspberries, blackberries and rhubarb.  Toss well. Turn into pie shell.  Dampen crust edge.  Cover with pastry.  Trim and crimp to seal.  Cut vents in top.
Sprinkle with remaining sugar
Bake on bottom rack at 350 F oven about 45 minutes until brown and apple is cooked.
