Title:
Broccoli Crunch Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads fresh broccoli
1/2 red onion
1/2 pound bacon
3/4 cup raisins
3/4 cup sliced almonds
1 cup mayonnaise
8 packets of Stevia Sweetener, Granulated
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar

Directions:
Place bacon in a deep skillet and cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Cool and crumble.
Cut broccoli into bite-size pieces and cut onion into thin bite-size slices. Combine with the bacon, raisins, and almonds and mix well.
To prepare the dressing, mix mayonnaise, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, and vinegar together until smooth. Stir into the salad, let chill and serve.

Submitter: 
Diana
Description: 
This salad has bite, crunch and sweetness, combining raisins, nuts and crunchy broccoli with crisp bacon in a sweet and sour mayonnaise-based dressing.Modified from: Broccoli Crunch Salad
