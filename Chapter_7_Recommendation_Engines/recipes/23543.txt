Title:
Tanya's Ginger Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1/2 cup white sugar
2 (1 inch) pieces crystallized ginger
4 cinnamon sticks
3 cups orange juice
1 cup pineapple juice
1/2 cup lemon juice
1 liter lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Directions:
In a sauce pan, combine water, sugar, crystallized ginger and cinnamon sticks. Bring to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
Pour syrup into a pitcher. Add orange, pineapple and lemon juice. Chill in refrigerator. When ready to serve, pour into a punch bowl and slowly add the lemon-lime soda.

Submitter: 
Tanya McGill
Description: 
This punch is always a huge hit.  You can serve this with vodka or just on it's own.  Wonderful holiday punch and very refreshing in the summer.  I always freeze sliced oranges and lemons in a mold pan with water.  It makes a more decorative floater than ice cubes.
