Title:
Keto Peanut Butter Brownie Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 C peanut butter made with 100% ground peanuts as the only ingredient
1/3 C Cocoa Powder
1.25 TB 0 calorie 0 carb sweetener (I use Whole Earth Stevia & Monk Fruit)
1/4 C unsweetened coconut milk
1 TB vanilla extract (I use my homemade double-fold Madagascar bourbon vanilla)
pinch of salt, or more to taste

Directions:
Blend all ingredients with a food processor, scraping sides as needed, or use an electric hand/imersion blender. Use small scoop to measure and roll into balls. Should make about 20 balls.
Note: If desired, roll in unsweetened coconut (may add calories).

Submitter: 
GoldenBelly
Description: 
Oh so satisfying! This deliciously low-carb treat delivers vegetarian protein, is completely guilt-free, and gives you the perfect healthy fat bomb when you need one. Best of all, one batch lasts and lasts.
