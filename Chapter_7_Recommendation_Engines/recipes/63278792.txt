Title:
Chicken with Creamy Chive Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, bonely chicken breasts (about 2 lbs total)
1/4 c. butter
1 envelope italian dry salad dressing mix
1 can golden mushroom soup
4 oz. cream cheese with chives and onion
1/2 c. chicken stock

Directions:
Place chicken in a 3 1/2 t 4 qt slow cooker.  In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium eat.  Stir in dry salad dressing mix.  Stir in golden mushroom soup, cream cheese, and chicken stock.  Pour over chicken.  Cvoer and cook on low-eat setting for 4 to 5 hours.  Serve chicken with sauce.
