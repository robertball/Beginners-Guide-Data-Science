Title:
3 ingredient Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans pie filling
1/2 white cake mix
1 stick of margarine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
pour pie filling into a 9x13 pan and spread evenly.  take 1/2 of the cake mix and sprinkle evenly over the pie filling and store the remaining half.  Cut stick of margarine into small chunks and distribute evenly over dry cake mix and bake 30 to 40 minutes till golden and bubbly.  Serve warm.

Dawn Raegene Mendenhall Scott

for a quick and easy cobbler recipe here is one that is quick easy and fun to make.
