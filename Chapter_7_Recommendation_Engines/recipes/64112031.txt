Title:
Coconut-Chocolate Sugar Free Popsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1271519.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4  13.5 oz can lite coconut milk
1/4 cup almond milk
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
2 t vanilla
dash salt
3 ripe bananas
6 dates

Directions:
Soak dates for 6-10 minutes in hot water until soft, then drain.
Combine all ingredients and puree in blender or Vitamix.
Place in popsicle molds and freeze overnight.

Submitter: 
JoyFullGrl
Description: 
A cool low-guilt summer treat flavored with coconut milk, bananas and cocoa.  Vegan, no refined sugars and quick to whip up.
