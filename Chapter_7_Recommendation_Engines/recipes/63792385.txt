Title:
Garden Fresh Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One gallon bag frozen cored tomatoes
1 small can of tomato paste
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon crushed fennel seeds
1/2 teaspoon basil
1/2 teaspoon parsley
1/4 teaspoon thyme
1/2 cup red wine
1 tablespoon sugar
3 cloves garlic sliced
1 onion diced
5 or 6 large mushrooms sliced
1 pkg mild Italian sausage

Directions:
1. Skin frozen tomatoes by running them under hot water. The skins should slip off easily. Place in large pot and heat until boiling.
2. Using a emulsion blender, blend the tomatoes until smooth. Add the tomato paste the wine, spices and sugar, simmer for a few min. while you first cook the sausage and then saute the  onion, garlic and mushrooms. Add to the sauce and simmer for several hours.

Submitter: 
Aven
Description: 
Here is a good way to use up garden grown tomatoes. I core and freeze several bags so I can make this sauce all winter long.
