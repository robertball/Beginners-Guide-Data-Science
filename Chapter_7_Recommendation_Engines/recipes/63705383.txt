Title:
Creamy Soft Polenta with Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon olive oil
2 teaspoons chopped garlic
2 cups chicken broth
2/3 cup cornmeal
1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
1/3 cup milk
1 tablespoon butter
1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped

Directions:
In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium-high heat until hot. Add garlic and saut√© for about 2 minutes.
Add the broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low. Gradually wisk in the cornmeal. Cook until the polenta is thick, about 5 minutes. Lower the heat to low and add the cheese and milk.
In a separate pan melt the butter over medium heat until it begins to froth. Stir in the chopped basil and cook for about 1 minute.
Add the basil to the polenta mixture. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Soft polenta can be made quickly and easily and it is a nice change of pace from regular polenta or side dishes such as mashed potatoes. Fresh basil gives the dish color and a wonderful hint of flavor.
