Title:
Very Creamy Potato-Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T Butter
2 C Chopped Onions
3 Large Cloves Garlic
2 C Chopped Celery
2 Large Carrots, peeled and chopped coarsely
4 large Potatoes, unpeeled and chopped
3 C Vegetable Stock
2 t dried dill
8 ounces Cream Cheese
1 C Half and Half
1 C Sour Cream
2 Cups Grated Sharp Cheddar Cheese
Salt and Black Pepper to Taste
*Chopped Parsley for garnish

Directions:
In a large soup pot sauce the onions in butter until translucent then add the minced garlic for a couple more minutes.  Add potatoes, celery, and carrots and saut√©ed for 5-10 minutes more.
Add cream cheese, sour cream and stir in half and half.  Use immersion blender and puree.  Warm soup to almost boiling but taking care not to boil then turn off burner and pour in grated sharp cheddar cheese while stirring.  Let sit about 10 minutes to merge the cheese with the rest of the soup, add salt and pepper to taste.
Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with warm bread and a green salad
