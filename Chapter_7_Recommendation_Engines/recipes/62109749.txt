Title:
Cheese & Garlic Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Bisquick
1/4 cup Butter, melted
2/3 cup Milk
1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
1/2 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven 450F
Mix cheese, Bisquick, and milk until formed into soft  dough.
Drop dough by spoonful onto lightly greased cookie sheet.
Bake 8-10 minutes.
Mix melted butter with garlic powder. Brush over baked biscuits
