Title:
Starbucks Light Frappuccino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1024301.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbs. instant coffee granules (regular or decaf)
2 Tbs. fat free vanilla flavored coffee creamer, powdered
1/2 C. fat free milk
8-10 ice cubes

Directions:
Blend all ingredients together in a blender and process until smooth. If desired, sweeten with Splenda. ( I use Stevia)

Submitter: 
Coffee Cup
Description: 
Enjoy a homemade version of Starbucks Light Frappuccino from the convenince of your own kitchen.
