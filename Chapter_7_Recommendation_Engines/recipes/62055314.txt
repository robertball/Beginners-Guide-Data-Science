Title:
red velvet balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
red velvet cake mix
1 can cream cheese icing
2 pkgs White almond bark

Directions:
Make cake mix according to directions on box with any of the recomended ways, it does not matter you will just crumble it up anyway. Cool after baking until cool enough to handle.
Crumble cake mix in large bowl, mix with cream cheese icing until well blended. Chill mixture about 30 min.
Melt the almond bark in double broiler, microwave, or small crockpot.
Dip balls in chocolate and put on wax paper until set. If you want them to look fancy drizzle them with melted chocolate chips.
