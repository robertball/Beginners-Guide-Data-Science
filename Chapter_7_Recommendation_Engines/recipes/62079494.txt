Title:
Coconut Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup sugar
1 egg
2 tsp evaporated milk
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup coconut
Jam
12 unbaked tart shells

Directions:
Melt butter and mix in sugar and egg.  Then add evaporated milk, vanilla and coconut.  Place 1/2 teaspoon of jam in each tart shell. Fill shells with mixture and cook for 20 mins at 375degrees F.

Submitter: 
Freya
Description: 
My Grandma used to send these to the soldiers in the 2nd world war.  I've modified their sugar content as they used to be quite sweet.
