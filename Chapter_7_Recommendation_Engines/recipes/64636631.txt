Title:
Yellow Rice in Rice Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons butter
1 Onion grated
4 cups chicken broth
3 cups long white rice
2 packs of Sazon
1 teaspoon oregano
1 teaspoon cilantro
1/3 cup tomato sauce
1 tablespoon garlic powder

Directions:
Turn the cooker on cook. Melt butter in the bottom of the cooker and saute grated onion for 3-4 minutes. Turn the cooker off and back on again to reset the time. Add the rest of the ingredients. Close the lid and let it cook. Stir and serve!
