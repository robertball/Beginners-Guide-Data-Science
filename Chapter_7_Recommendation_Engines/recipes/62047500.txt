Title:
The Boys Ice Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups all purpose flour
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
1 cup oatmeal
1 cup melted butter
2 litres ice cream (any flavour)
Squeezable chocolate sauce (ie, quick)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Mix first 5 ingredients together.  Spread mixture onto cookie sheet to fill.  Bake for 15 minutes.
In 9x12 baking pan, spread 1/2 of baked mixture on bottom of pan and gently pat.  Drizzle some chocolate sauce top.  Cut ice cream to fit onto the 9x12 pan and layer over the crumb layer.  Put remaining 1/2 of crumb mixture over ice cream. Drizzle with chocolate sauce.  Cover with tin foil and let set in freezer for 5 hours.
Cut and enjoy.  This is excellent using mint chocolate chip ice-cream, chocolate ripple, butterscotch ripple, neopolitan, etc.
