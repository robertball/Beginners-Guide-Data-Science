Title:
Philly Cheesesteaks, vegetarian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Thinly sliced Portobello mushrooms (one per sandwich)
olive oil
thinly sliced onion and green pepper to taste
a few slices of of a good, hard cheese
six-inch sandwich roll

Directions:
Cook the mushrooms in a large frying pan, chopping it up with a spatula.  Add the onion and green pepper, and mound all the food into a shape slightly smaller than the roll.  Put the cheese slices on top.
Slice the roll and place the roll over the whole thing.  Using the spatula, remove it from the grill, fold into a sandwich, and wrap in foil, or serve on a plate.
