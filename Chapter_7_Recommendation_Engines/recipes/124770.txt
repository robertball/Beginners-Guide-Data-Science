Title:
Party Spinach Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983119.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach
1/3 cup chopped parsley
2 tablespoons chopped onion
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
Thaw spinach and drain thoroughly, squeezing out extra liquid. Wash parsley; pat dry on paper toweling. In food processor, chop parsley using steel blade. Add spinach and remaining ingredients; pulse until combined. Store in airtight container in refrigerator. Serve with thin wheat crackers, buttery crackers or celery and cheese sticks.

Submitter: 
Maire  Macy
Description: 
This appetizer is delicious, easy and pretty, too! Everyone who tries it asks for the recipe. The spread keeps for several days, so it's great for making in advance for a party. But once you put it out, it won't last long!
