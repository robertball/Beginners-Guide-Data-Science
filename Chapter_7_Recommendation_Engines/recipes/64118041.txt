Title:
Broccoli Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz pkg frozen broccoli, defrosted
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/t tsp onion powder
Salt and pepper to taste
2 eggs
1 cup panko bread crumbs
1 cup cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400. Squeeze broccoli in dishtowel to remove as much excess water as possible. Mix in bowl with spices, eggs, cheese and breadcrumbs. Line baking sheet with parchment. Form broccoli mixture into patties. Bake 30 minutes, turning once.
