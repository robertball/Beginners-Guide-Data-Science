Title:
Bread and Butter Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 quarts sliced cucumbers
1 quart sliced onion
1 cup salt
9 cups water
BRINE
4 1/2 cups brown sugar (or white)
1 1/2 cups water
3 1/2 cups vinegar
1 tsp celery seed
1 tsp tumeric
1 tbsp. mustard seed

Directions:
Cut cucumbers and onions crosswise in coarse slices.
Make a brine of 1 cup salt to 9 cups water.  Pour over cucumbers and onions each in separate jars.  Let stand over night.  Drain the next morning.
Combine vegetables with weak vinegar solution made of equal parts vinegar and water.
Heat vegetables in this for 10 minutes, do not let this come above simmering.  Then drain off, pack the vegetables in jars and pour pickling syrup over them to cover then seal.
If cauliflower is used prepare it the same as cucumbers and put them in vinegar solution for a few minutes before putting in cucs and onions.
