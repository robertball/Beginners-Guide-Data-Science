Title:
De-lite Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups baby spinach, washed & dried
1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
1/4 cup reduced feta cheese, crumbled
1 tart apple, pink lady or fuji, thinly sliced
1 tablespoon pine nuts
1 teaspoon sunflower seeds
1 tablespoon lite poppyseed dressing

Directions:
Toss all ingredients together and serve.

Submitter: 
Carolyn Hilliard Nelson
Description: 
A nice filling lunch or dinner salad that is low in calories and high in fiber and vitamins.
