Title:
Four Cheese Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
1 16 ounce shell macaroni
1 cup butter
1 cup shredded Muenster cheese
1cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
3 cups half-and-half
16 ounces cubed processed cheese food
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain well and return to cooking pot.
In a small saucepan over medium heat, melt 8 tablespoons butter; stir into the macaroni.
In a large bowl, combine the Muenster cheese, mild and sharp Cheddar cheeses, and Monterey Jack cheese; mix well.
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Add the half and half, eggs season with salt and pepper & wisk, set aside. layer pasta, shredded cheese, and cubed cheese  in lightly greased deep pan. Pour milk mixture over all and Sprinkle with the remaining 1/2 cup of cheese mixture.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes or until hot and bubbling around the edges; serve. ?? approx cooking time
