Title:
Claire's shrimp and crab gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 stalks celery with leaves, halved
1 large yellow bell pepper, seeded and quartered
3 roma tomatoes sliced
2 medium cucumbers, peeled, seeded, and halved
1 medium sweet onion, peeled and quartered
1 shallot peeled and halved
2 tbs. extra virgin olive oil
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
sea salt & fresh ground pepper to taste
4 cups vegetable and clam juice
1 lb.. cooked medium shrimp, cut into bite size pieces
8 oz. unstrained crab meat
cilantro &/or sour cream if desired
i half of a lemon,juiced and zested

Directions:
With a blender or kitchen processor puree vegetables (all ingredients except shrimp and crab).
Add shrimp & crab meat.
Chill for a few hours, but if in a hurry, add an ice cube to your bowl.
Garnish with cilantro and/or sour cream. goes great with grilled meat.
