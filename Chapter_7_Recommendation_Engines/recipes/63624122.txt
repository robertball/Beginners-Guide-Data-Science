Title:
shrip and chicken sesame toasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken thighs
3 1/2 oz cooked, shelled shrimp
1 egg
3 scallions
2 garlic
2T cilantro
1T fish sauce
1/2 t pepper
1/4 t salt
12 slices white bread ,crusts removed,
1/3 c sesame seeds
Oil for frying

Directions:
Process chicken, shrimp until Finley chopped. Add remaining.
Spread over bread right to edges. Place sesame seeds on a plate and press spread side down.
Use a sharp knife and cut 6 per slice.
Pan fry 6-8 min turning once.
Drain and serve.
