Title:
Breakfast Before Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. pork sausage
1 c grated cheddar cheese
2 c milk
1 tsp dry mustard
2 slices bread, cubed
6 eggs
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
1.) Saute the sausage and drain.
2.) Beat the eggs with the milk, salt, pepper, and mustard.
3.) Layer bread cubes, cheese, and sausage into greased 9x13 in. baking pan.
4.) Pour egg mixture on top and refridgerate overnight.
5.) Bake at 350 for 45 minutes the next morning.

Submitter: 
Alexa
Description: 
This is a great dish of mainly eggs and sausage. It's very convenient if you're having company!
