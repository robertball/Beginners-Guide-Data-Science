Title:
Cream Cheese-Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3 cups sugar
2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp salt
2 cups canned unsweetened pumpkin
1 cup vegetable oil
2/3 cup water
4 large eggs
Cream Cheese Filling

Directions:
Stir together 3 1/2 cups flour, sugar, and next 4 ingredients in a large bowl.  Whisk together pumpkin and next 3 ingredients; add to flour mixture, whisking just until dry ingredients are moistened.  Spoon 3 cups of batter into each of 2 greased and floured 9x5 inch loaf pans.  Spoon Cream Cheese Filling evenly over batter.  Spoon remaining batter evenly over Cream Cheese Filling.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour and 15 minutes or until a wooden pick inserted in center of bread comes out clean.  Let cool in pans 10 minutes.  Remove bread from pan, and cool completely on a wire rack.
