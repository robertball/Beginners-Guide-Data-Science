Title:
Savory Apricot Honey Dijon Chicken Meal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken:
Goya Mojo Criollo Marinade
4 chicken breasts
2 cups apricot preserves
3 tblsp honey
3/4 cup Grey Poupon dijon mustard
3 tblsp Ginger (ground)
Vegetables/Starch:
4 Fresh corn (shaved off stalks)
1/2 to full pack of grape tomatoes (halved)
2 large yellow squash, diced
1 package of Baby Portobello mushrooms (sliced)
1/2 package of fresh spinach
Brown Rice
Toppings:
Black olives, (sliced)
Feta cheese

Directions:
Instructions:
1.	Marinade chicken for apx. 2 hours in Goya Mojo Criollo Marinade.
2.	Mix together apricot preserves, Grey Poupon dijon mustard, ginger and honey. Set aside.
3.	Combine corn, tomatoes, squash and mushrooms in large stock pot; sauté on low in butter (can substitute chicken stock). Add a few shakes of garlic salt (can also add minced garlic).
4.	Sautee chicken breasts in apricot mixture on low until slightly browned.
5.	While chicken is sautéing, cook brown rice.
6.	Right as chicken breasts are done, add spinach to corn/squash/mushroom/tomatoes to wilt.
7.	Suggested plate composition:  Place rice on plate first, add vegetables, then chicken. Top with feta cheese and sliced black olives. Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Alisa Butler Kinsaul
Description: 
This recipe has an awesome blend of both sweet and savory flavors. The men in my house love it! Perfect meal for the summer while squash, tomatoes and corn are in season.
