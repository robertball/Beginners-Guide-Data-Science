Title:
Easy Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3605227.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
3 cloves garlic, pressed
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1/8 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Stir soy sauce, garlic, vegetable oil, red wine vinegar, and ground ginger in a bowl together until ginger is dissolved.
