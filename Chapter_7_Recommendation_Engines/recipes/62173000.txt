Title:
Peach-Pear crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C packed brown sugar
1/3C flour
1/4C butter(softened)
1/4tsp cinnamon
1/8tsp salt
1 can (29oz) sliced peaches, drained
1 can (16oz) sliced pears, drained

Directions:
Mix well sugar,flour,butter, cinnamon and salt;set aside. Arrange fruit in grease 9inch pan. Broil the fruit 6inches away from the heat for 3min. Sprinkle with sugar mix. Broil 3 more min. longer or until bubbly and carmelized. serve immediatly
