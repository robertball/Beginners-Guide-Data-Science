Title:
Easy spinach pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb penne pasta, cooked and drained
1 jar prepared spaghetti sauce
16oz ricotta cheese
1 box frozen chopped spinach, thawed
1 1/2c mozzerella cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients but mozzerella cheese and spread into 9x13 baking dish. Top with mozzerella cheese and bake at 350 until heated through.

Submitter: 
ZOPOOH
Description: 
Spinach combined with penne, ricotta, cheese, and spaghetti sauce make for a quick weekday meal.
