Title:
Healthy Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit and grease or line a baking tray. In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients and stir until smooth. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
Scoop 1 to 2 tablespoons of dough at a time and roll into balls. Place balls evenly spaced on baking tray and flatten tops with a fork. Bake for 10-15 minutes until set and slightly golden brown.
Allow to cool for a few minutes before removing to a  wire rack.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
For a healthy cookie you can indulge in guilt-free, bake a batch of healthy peanut butter cookies. No additional ingredients are needed.
