Title:
Missouri Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/540595.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. sugar
1/2 c. butter
3 tbsp. powdered cocoa*
1/2 c. milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 c. peanut butter
3 c. quick cooking oatmeal
*If you do not have powdered cocoa, you can substitute 1 oz. unsweetened chocolate bar and reduce butter by 1 TBS*

Directions:
Put sugar, butter, cocoa, milk, and vanilla into saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Boil 2 1/2 minutes. Stir in the peanut butter until melted/mixed then remove pan from heat and add the oatmeal. Mix well. Drop by teaspoons onto wax paper. Let cool. Store in an airtight container once cooled.

Submitter: 
JoAnna Hewett
Description: 
Easy no-bake chocolate, peanut butter, oatmeal cookies.  My Mom made these when I was little, and I'm still making them for MY family :)
