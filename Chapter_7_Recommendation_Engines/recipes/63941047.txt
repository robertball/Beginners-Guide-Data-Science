Title:
Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
French Bread
Stick of Butter
Minced Garlic
Garlic Salt
Oregano
Parsley
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cut french bread length wise. Melt butter add minced garlic, garlic salt, oregano and parsley to the melted butter. Spread generously on the bread. Cover top with parmesan cheese. Broil in the oven until crisp

Submitter: 
Kevin Gorton
Description: 
Simple to the point but decadent garlic bread!
