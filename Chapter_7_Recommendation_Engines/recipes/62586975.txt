Title:
Two Layer Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 c softened butter
1 c sugar
3 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/4 c flour
1/4 tsp baking soda
3/4 c chopped nuts
Cookie Dough Layer:
1/2 c butter softened
1/2 c brown sugar
1/4 c sugar
3 Tbsp milk
1 tsp vanilla
1 c flour
1 c semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
In a microwave safe bowl melt the chocolate chips for the brownie layer. Cool slightly. Cream butter & sugar. Add eggs & vanilla & mix well. Stir in the melted chocolate. Combine flour & baking soda & add to batter. Stir in nuts. Spread in a 9in by 13in pan. Bake at 350 for 16-22 min or till a toothpick inserted near the middle comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack. In a mixing bowl cream the butter & sugar for the dough layer. Beat in milk & vanilla & gradually add the flour. Stir in chocolate chips & drop spoonfuls all over the cooled brownies & carefully spread evenly. Store in the fridge.

Description: 
These brownies are for those who love to eat cookie dough, the top layer tastes just like dough & the fudgie bottome is a perfect compliment.
