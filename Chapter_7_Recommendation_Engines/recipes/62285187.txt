Title:
Guava Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups lemon juice (about 4 lbs lemons)
5 1/3 lbs guavas
2 2/3 lbs sugar
Pinch of salt

Directions:
Chop guavas (leave peel on), and boil with lemon juice.  Strain out the seeds.  Then return to pot, add sugar and salt, and boil till of desired consistency.  Pour into hot sterilized jars and seal.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
This is a convenient recipe to use where I live in Taiwan, since it doesn't call for pectin, and guavas are readily available.
