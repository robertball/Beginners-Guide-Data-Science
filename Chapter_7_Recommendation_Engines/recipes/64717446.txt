Title:
Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup short-grain rice
1/4 cup yellow onion, diced
1/4 cup green pepper, diced
1/4 cup celery, diced
1 tsp parsley, minced
1 can beef broth
1/2 tsp dried thyme leaves
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
Pinch cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp salt
1 bay leaf
1/4 cup water
1 (14oz) can diced tomatoes, drained
1 (8oz) can tomato sauce
1/2 lb smoked sausage, butterflied, cooked and cut into bite-size pieces
1/2 lb medium, fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
In a large stockpot, combine jambalaya mix ingredients, water, tomatoes, tomato sauce and smoked sausage. Bring mixture to a boil over medium-high heat. Cover, reduce heat and let simmer for 20 minutes. Remove bay leaf from jambalaya. Add shrimp and cook for an additional 5 to 7 minutes, or until shrimp are pink.
