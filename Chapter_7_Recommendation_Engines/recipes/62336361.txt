Title:
Meaty Peppery Slow Cooked Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef.
2 green peppers - thick chopped
1 red pepper - thick chopped
2 spanish onions - thick chopped
5 cloves garlic - chopped
2 cans tomato paste
1 large can whole tomatoes
1 can tomato sauce
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 tblsp butter

Directions:
You will need 2 Skillets and a large slow cooker.
In one skillet brown ground beef, drain fat
In the other add butter, onions, red and green peppers, garlic, black pepper cook until tender about 3 - 5 mins.
In the slow cooker add tomatos, tomato paste, tomato sauce, crushed red pepper, garlic powder
Add beef, sauteed onions, red and green peppers to the slow cooker and cook on low for about 4 hours, the longer it cooks the better it tastes.
