Title:
Two Minute Spinach Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large egg(s)
1/2 cup(s) egg substitute
1/2 cup(s) soy milk
1 cup(s) spinach, baby
4 tablespoon cheese, cheddar, low-fat shredded
1/2 cup(s) beans, black drained and rinsed
1 teaspoon red pepper sauce
1 pinch salt, Kosher
1 pinch pepper, black ground

Directions:
Beat egg, egg substitute, and milk with salt and pepper in microwaveable bowl. Stir in vegetables and microwave on high for 2-3 minutes.
Run a knife around the edge of the bowl to release the frittata. (Soak the bowl immediately to facilitate clean-up).
Sprinkle cheese on top of frittata and serve with the black beans heated on the stove in a small sauce pan with the hot pepper sauce.
