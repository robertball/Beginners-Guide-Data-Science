Title:
Easy Spaghetti Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package lingunini
2 tablespoons butter
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 eggs, beaten
1 1/2 pound lean ground beef
2 tsp of Frahm seasoning
1 tsp garlic juice
1 tbsp minced onion
1 jar of spagetti sauce
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook and drain spaghetti. Stir in butter, parmesan cheese and eggs, while spaghetti is hot.  Form spaghetti mixture into a crust in a buttered casserole dish.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a skillet cook the beef, garlic and Frahm seasoning, and onion. Drain off the fat and stir in the spagetti sauce.  Top the linguninie with the hamburger mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the top of the pie then bake for 5 minutes longer, until cheese melts.
