Title:
Apple Spinach Salad 1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon poppy seeds
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
10 ounces fresh spinach - rinsed, dried and torn into bite-size pieces
1 cup chopped green apple or golden delicious
1/4 cup almonds or cashews

Directions:
In a medium bowl pour honey and vinegar. Mix until dissolved then add oil, paprika, worcestershire sauce, and poppy seeds. Chill for one hour.
In a large bowl, combine the spinach, apples and almonds/cashews. Pour amount of dressing desired over salad, and toss. Refrigerate 10 to 15 minutes before serving to allow flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
Amanda Nye
Description: 
The dressing in this spinach salad is sweet. Choose to pair it with a sweet or sour apple. YUMM!!Modified from: Apple Spinach Salad
