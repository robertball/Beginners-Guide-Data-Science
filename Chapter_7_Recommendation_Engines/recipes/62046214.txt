Title:
Cucumber Dill Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz pkg. of cream cheese softened
1 C. Mayonnaise
2 medium cucumbers peeled, seeded, and chopped
2 T. sliced green onion
1 T. lemon juice
2 tsp. snipped fresh dill or 1/2 tsp. dill seasoning
1/2 tsp. hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Beat the cream cheese until it is smooth.  Stir in the cucumbers, green onion, lemon juice, fresh dill and hot red pepper.  Mix until well blended.  Cover and Chill.  Makes 2-1/2 cups.
