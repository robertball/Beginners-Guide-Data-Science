Title:
Sticky Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 extra large box Golden Grahams cereal
1 extra large box Corn Chex cereal
1, 7 oz. bag coconut
8 oz. sliced or slivered almonds
2 c. light corn syrup
2 c. sugar
3/4 c. butter

Directions:
Combine cereal, almonds and coconut in big bowl and set aside.
In large saucepan, heat sugar, butter and corn syrup over medium to medium-high heat. Bring to a boil and boil for 5 min.
Pour over cereal mixture and toss or stir until well coated.
Let cool on waxed paper.
Store in an air-tight container for up to a week.
