Title:
Snow Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of clean snow
1 cup any flavor liquid coffee creamer

Directions:
Steal some snow from the foot or so Mother Nature just dumped on us!  Pour the coffee creamer into snow and mix.  Enjoy!
