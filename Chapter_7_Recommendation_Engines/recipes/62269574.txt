Title:
Sun-Dried Tomato Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/343593.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15-ounce can garbanzo beans (drained and rinsed, reserve 1/3 cup of the liquid from the beans)
3 ounces reduced fat cream cheese
1 ounce sun-dried tomatoes, minced
1/4 cup sliced green onion (white and pale green parts only), sliced thin
2 teaspoons prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a food processor or blender and puree until smooth. Check taste and add additional salt, pepper and horseradish, to taste.

Submitter: 
Scott Koeneman
Description: 
A two tablespoon serving of this creamy, delicious hummus is only 79 calories, with 3 grams of fat and 2 grams of fiber.
