Title:
Refreshing Tomato Cucumber Salad  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/876706.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, sliced
2 tomatoes, sliced
1 onion, sliced
1/3 cup Italian-style salad dressing
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Combine cucumbers, tomatoes, and onion in a bowl. Add Italian dressing and Parmesan cheese and toss until coated. Refrigerate until chilled, about 1 hour.

Submitter:

Description:
