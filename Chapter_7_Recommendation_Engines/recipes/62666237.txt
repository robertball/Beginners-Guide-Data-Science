Title:
Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of tomato soup
Colby Jack cheese
whole wheat sandwich bread

Directions:
Liberally butter grill and heat to medium-high heat.  Place sliced or grated cheese between two slices of bread per person.  Add garlic salt to taste Grill bread on each side until nicely browned.  Meanwhile, whisk wataer or milk into soup and heat through.
