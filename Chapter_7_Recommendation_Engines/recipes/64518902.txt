Title:
Joan's Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Frito-Lay bean dip (or refried beans, if you can't find the bean dip)
1 cup of sour cream
1/2 cup of mayonnaise
1 small can of sliced black olives
1/2 package of Taco seasoning
3 avocados
1 t. lemon juice
3 chopped tomatoes
3 scallions, chopped
1-2 cups cheddar cheese, grated
Scooping size Fritos or tortilla chips

Directions:
Spread dip/refried beans in a flat dish
Mix sour cream, mayonnaise and Taco seasoning together and layer over beans.
Mash avocados, mix with lemon juice and layer over beans and sour cream.
Mix chopped tomatoes, olives and scallions together and layer over beans, sour cream and avocado.
Sprinkle all with generous layer of cheese.
Serve with large Fritos or tortilla chips.

This is a family holiday favorite. Easy to make and delicious!
