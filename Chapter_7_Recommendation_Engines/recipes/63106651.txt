Title:
Crock-Pot Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 13 oz. cans chicken breast
7 cups water
4 cups cooked egg noodles
1 cup chopped celery
1 26 oz. can cream of chicken soup
1 cup chopped carrots
8 cubes chicken bouillon
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Shred chicken, if desired.
Pour all ingredients, except egg noodles, into crock-pot.
Cook on high for 2 hours or on low for 4 hours.
Add noodles before serving.
