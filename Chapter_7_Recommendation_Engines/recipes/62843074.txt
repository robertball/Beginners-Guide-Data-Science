Title:
?Three (3) envelope Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken
1 envelope dry chicken gravy
1 envelope dry italian dressing
1 envelope of hidden valley ranch dressing
Water

Directions:
Mix all 3 dry ingredients with 1 to 1 1/2 cups water . Mix well. In the Crock pot put your favorite chicken parts (we like thighs) pour wet mix on top of chicken and cook 6-8 hours on low

Submitter:

Description: 
Easy, delicious slow cooker recipe. Serve with egg noodles or rice.
