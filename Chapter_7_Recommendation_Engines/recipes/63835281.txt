Title:
Apple pankakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 Tbs  brown sugar
2 tsp  baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp allspice
1/4 tsp nutmeg
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 tbs vegetable oil
2 small apples peeled,cored,cut into small pieces (microwave apples for three minutes)

Directions:
1. Combine dry ingredients
2. mix wet ingredients
3. add wet ingredients into dry, stirring until thoroughly combined.
4. Heat griddle, use your favorite oil and pour pancake batter the size you like. When they are bubbly all over they are ready to flip!

Submitter: 
Sheryl Cole
Description: 
These are cakey delicious spicy pancakes! Great for a chilly Fall Morning!
