Title:
Salmon Cakes III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz fresh salmon
1 slice bread, micro planed
2 shallots, grated
1 clove garlic, grated
2 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1 egg
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 tsp parsley

Directions:
Micro plane shallots,parsley and garlic into frying pan. Add 1 tbs avacado oil and warm. Add salmon and cook over med heat until no longer pink, breaking up salmon as you go.
Remove from heat and allow to cool. Add bread crumbs,flour and egg. Form into patties and place in fridge for one hour. Remove from fridge 30 mins prior to cooking.
In a large skillet heat oil over high heat. Cook the patties until browned on both sides, about 8 minutes. Drain briefly on paper towels before serving.
