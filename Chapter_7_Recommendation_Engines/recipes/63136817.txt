Title:
Lela's Teriyaki Chicken Rice Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/801116.jpg
Ingredients:
Sauce:
2 cups of low sodium beef broth
2 T. cornstarch
2 T. soy sauce-I used worcestershire sauce
1 T. brown sugar
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 tsp. ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
Main Dish Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons of canola oil (1 T. for the vegetables and 1 T. for chicken)
5 medium sized boneless chicken breast tenderloins  chopped
1/2 teaspoon of poultry seasoning and onion powder to taste
2 Tablespoons of lemon juice
1 cup broccoli
1/4 cup shredded carrots
4 green onions chopped
1/4 head of chopped cabbage
3 cups of cooked white rice or brown rice

Directions:
Cook the rice.
Sauce: Add beef broth, cornstarch, worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ground ginger, and red pepper flakes to a sauce pan.
Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
Reduce heat, and simmer 5 minutes.
Main Dish Directions: Chop the cabbage and onions. Shred the carrots.
Heat 1 T. of  oil in a nonstick wok on medium heat. Add broccoli, carrots, and onion. Stir-fry until tender-crisp. Add the lemon juice.  Remove from the wok and cover with aluminum foil to keep warm.
Add 1 T. of oil and turn the heat to high to stir-fry chicken. Add the chicken and cook until tender.
Turn down heat to medium-low and add the poultry seasoning and onion powder to the chicken. Toss the chicken to mix seasonings.
Add the prepared sauce. Simmer until it bubbles.
Serve over a bed of white rice.

Submitter: 
Lela
Description: 
A take on the Chicken rice bowls served in Japanese Restaurants. Stir fried vegetables, chicken, and rice served with a homemade Teriyaki sauce.
