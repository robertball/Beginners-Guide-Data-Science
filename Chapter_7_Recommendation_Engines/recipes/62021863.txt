Title:
Pumpkin Cranberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ¼ cup all purpose flour
1 tbs. Pumpkin pie spice
2 tsp. Baking powder
½ tsp. Salt
2 eggs
2 cups granulated sugar
1 ¾ cup (15 oz. Can) pumpkin
¼ cup lighter bake (prune butter) or ½ cup oil
1 cup fresh, frozen or sweetened dried cranberries

Directions:
Combine flour, pumpkin pie spice, baking powder and salt in large bowl. Add eggs, sugar, pumpkin and lighter bake (or oil), beat until just moistened. Fold in cranberries. Spoon batter into 2 (spray with cooking spray first) 9¡¨ X 5¡¨ loaf pans.
Bake in preheated 350„a oven for 55 to 60 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pans for 5 to 10 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
