Title:
Linguine with Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons butter
2-3 cloves of garlic, crushed
1 teaspoon flour
1 cup chicken broth
1 tablespoon parsley
1 teaspoon lemon juice
Pepper
1 cup artichokes, not in oil, quartered
2 cups minced clams, canned and drained
2-3  tablespoons Parmesan cheese
1/2 pound of cooked linguine

Directions:
Heat oil and butter, add garlic
Stir in flour; cook 3 minutes
Stir in broth; cook 1 minute
Add parsley, lemon juice, pepper
Stir in rest of ingredients
Serve over linguine
