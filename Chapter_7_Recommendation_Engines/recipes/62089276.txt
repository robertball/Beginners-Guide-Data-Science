Title:
Best Ever Streusel Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FILLING
1/2 c. chopped walnuts or pecans
1/4 c. brown sugar
1 T. butter, melted
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
CAKE
1/2 c. butter
7/8 c. sugar
2 eggs, separated
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 c. flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/8 tsp. salt
6 oz. sour cream
TOPPING
1/8 c. flour
1/8 c. sugar
1 T. cold butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour a 9x5 loaf pan.
For filling combine nuts, brown sugar and cinnamon, cut in butter and set aside.
Beat egg whites in a small bowl until stiff peaks form and set aside.
In another larger bowl cream butter and sugar together.
Beat in egg yolks one at a time scraping the bowl to combine well.
Add vanilla, beat well and scrape bowl.
Combine dry ingredients and add to creamed ingredients alternately with sour cream.
Fold in egg whites (they will deflate just make sure they are all combined).
Put half of the batter into the pan, top with the filling and the other half of batter and topping.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes, remove and cool for 10 minutes before turning out of pan to cool completely.

Submitter: 
D. Storm
Description: 
Moist and not excessively sweet with a cinnamon nutty filling. This is the best homemade coffee cake ever.  It is gone in my house before I even turn it out of the pan.
