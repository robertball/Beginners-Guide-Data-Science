Title:
Nuclear Screwdriver Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/167857.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
1/2 fluid ounce 190 proof grain alcohol
2 fluid ounces fresh orange juice
1 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Fill a rocks glass with ice, and pour in vodka, grain alcohol, and orange juice; stir and serve.
