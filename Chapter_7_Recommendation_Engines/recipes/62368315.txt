Title:
Loaded Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb - Ground Beef
1lb - Bacon
2c - Grated Cheese
1t - Worcestershire Sauce
1/2t - Ground Black Pepper
1/2t - Salt
1/8t - Ground Cayenne Red Pepper
1/8t - Liquid Smoke

Directions:
Chop up bacon into small pieces then add all ingredients into bowl mixing well. Form into patties and grill.
