Title:
Roasted Butternut Squash Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/909158.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium butternut squash, halved lengthwise and seeded
4 teaspoons butter
4 teaspoons brown sugar
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Place butternut squash halves on a large baking sheet flesh side up. Place 1 teaspoon butter in the middle of each squash. Sprinkle brown sugar over each squash. Season with salt and black pepper. Roast 25-45 minutes, until flesh is fork-tender. Reserve 2 halves for future meal.
