Title:
Strawberry Spinach Salad I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons sesame seeds
1 tablespoon poppy seeds
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon minced onion
10 ounces fresh spinach - rinsed, dried and torn into bite-size pieces
1 quart strawberries - cleaned, hulled and sliced
fresh blueberries
1/4 cup almonds, blanched and slivered
You can also add blue cheese or feta or both

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk together the sesame seeds, poppy seeds, sugar, olive oil, vinegar, paprika, Worcestershire sauce and onion. Cover, and chill for one hour. (Can also be combined in blender)
In a large bowl, combine the spinach, strawberries and almonds.  Add a few red onions on top. Refrigerate 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
