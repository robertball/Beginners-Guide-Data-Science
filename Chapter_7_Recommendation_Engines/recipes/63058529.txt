Title:
Steak a la Piziolo (Pizziola or Pizzaiola) with Linguini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/776297.jpg
Ingredients:
Steak:
8-10 blade steak** (preferred) or chicken steaks (you can use fewer or more steaks to accommodate your family size)
Olive oil
1 onion sliced
Salt & pepper
Sauce:
28 oz can crushed italian tomato
6 oz can tomato paste
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp basil
Salt & pepper
1 clove garlic
1 beef bouillon cube
1 onion sliced
2 cups water
3 cloves garlic crushed.
Pasta:
1 lb linguini, cooked (or more to accommodate family size)

Directions:
Heat oven to 400F. For steak, coat a 9x13 baking dish with olive oil.  Lay steaks in dish then flip steaks over so that both sides are soaked with oil.  Put onion slices over the steaks.  Season with salt & pepper.  Brown steak in oven for 15 minutes on each side.
For sauce:  brown onion and garlic in olive oil in 4qt sauce pan.  Add crushed tomato, tomato paste, water, crushed garlic, oregano, basil, beef bullion .  Cook sauce while steak is in oven.
Pizziola:  remove steak from oven.  Pour sauce over steak.  Allow to marinade 1 hour if using chicken steak. For blade steak marinading is optional). Put dish back in oven loosely covered with foil for 45 minutes.
Pasta:  cook linguini to desired doneness.  Put some sauce over linguini to keep it from sticking.
Serve linguine and steak with some sauce from the baking pan.
