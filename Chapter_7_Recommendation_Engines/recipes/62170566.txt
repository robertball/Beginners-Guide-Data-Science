Title:
Buttery Mushroom Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/338942.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbs. olive oil
1 yellow onion, diced
1 clove of garlic, minced
2 carrots, sliced
2 stalks of celery, sliced
1 c. mushrooms, sliced
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked and diced
1 26 oz. container chicken stock
2 c. water
1 tbs. chicken base
1 tsp. dried thyme
1 tsp. dried rosemary
salt and pepper to taste
splash of white wine
1/2 c. butter
3/4 c. flour
1 c. heavy cream
1 c. milk
1 1/2 c. egg noodles

Directions:
Sautee onion and garlic in olive oil for about 5 minutes in large stock pot.
Add carrots and celery, mushroom, chicken, chicken stock, water, chicken base, thyme, rosemary, salt, pepper, and splash of white wine.
Cook on medium heat until veggies are tender, about 15 minutes.
In a separate pot, boil egg noodles until tender.
Drain, set aside.
In a medium sauce pan, melt butter.
Add flour 1 tbs. at a time, whisking after each addition until smooth.
Whisk in heavy cream a little at a time until smooth.
Whisk in milk a little at a time until smooth.
Add to stock pot making sure to mix well.
Cook for 10 to 15 minutes until thickened.
Add egg noodles and serve with some crusty bread.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
This is a complete comfort food soup.  It's a heartier version of traditional chicken noodle soup that I made after having some at a local restaurant.  I love to make this on cold, fall weekends.
