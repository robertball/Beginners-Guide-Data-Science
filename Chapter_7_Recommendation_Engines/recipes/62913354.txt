Title:
Goldie's Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups granny smith apples, thinly sliced
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon Cinnamon
1/2 cup raisins
Zest and Juice from one Orange
1 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1 tablespoon Baking Powder
1 egg

Directions:
Toss together apples, sugar, cinnamon, raisins and orange juice.  Place into greased 9X13 pan.
Mix together zest, flour, sugar and baking powder with a fork.  Work egg into mixture.  Sprinkle crisp mixture over apples.
Bake at 350 for 40 - 55 minutes, or until apples are soft and top is brown and crispy.
Serve with cream, whipped cream, or ice cream.
