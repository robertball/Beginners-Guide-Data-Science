Title:
Jennifer's Ultimate Pancake Mix  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup oat flour
7 teaspoons baking powder
7 teaspoons white sugar
5 teaspoons egg replacer (dry)
4 teaspoons salt

Directions:
Mix all-purpose flour, cornmeal, whole wheat flour, oat flour, baking powder, white sugar, egg replacer, and salt together in a bowl. Pour into an airtight container; shake well and store.

Submitter: 
Jennifer J
Description: 
Tastes better than the store-brand mixes and can be altered easily. 
Note: You will need large airtight container (3- to 4-quart size) for storage.
