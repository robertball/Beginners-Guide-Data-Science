Title:
Sweet and Tangy Slow Cooker Bar-B-Q Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons of chili sauce
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon hot sauce
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon celery seed
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 tablespoons brown sugar
2 pounds boneless pork
1 onion, sliced into rings
1 red bell pepper, cut into rings
1/4 cup cornstarch
1/4 cup water

Directions:
In a bowl, mix the ketchup, chili sauce, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, Dijon mustard, chili powder, celery seed, black pepper, and brown sugar.
Place the pork in a slow cooker, and cover with onion and red bell pepper. Pour sauce over all.
Cover, and cook 8 hours on Low.
Add mixture of cornstarch and water at end of cooking to thicken.

Description:
