Title:
Bacon Horseradish Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Mayonnaise
1 Cup Sour Cream
1 Cup Real Bacon bits
1/4 Cup Horseradish

Directions:
Mix the mayonnaise, sour cream, bacon bits and horseradish together until well blended. Cover and chill.

Submitter: 
SBURBANK
Description: 
An easy to make dip, that my grandmother used to make for the holidays.
