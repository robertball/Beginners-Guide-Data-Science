Title:
Best Steak Marinade in Existence Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 tablespoons garlic powder
1 1/2 tablespoons dried parsley flakes
1 teaspoon ground pepper
1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1 teaspoon paste garlic

Directions:
Place the soy sauce, olive oil,  Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, parsley, and pepper in a blender. Add hot pepper sauce and garlic, if desired. Blend on high speed for 30 seconds until thoroughly mixed.
Pour marinade over desired type of meat. Cover, and refrigerate for up to 8 hours. Cook meat as desired.
