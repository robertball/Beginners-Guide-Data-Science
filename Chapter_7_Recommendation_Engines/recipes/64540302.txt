Title:
Just Like A Mac Attack Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3156582.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C mayo
4 tsp sweet pickle relish
1 Tbsp minced white onion
1 Tbsp ketchup
2 tsp olive oil
2 tsp sugar
1 tsp white vinegar
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 teaspoon lemon juice
1/8 teaspoon dry mustard
1/8 teaspoon paprika
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
8 C salad greens
6 cooked hamburger patties, chopped
1/2 C shredded cheddar
1/4 C onion, diced
1/4 C dill pickle chips, chopped

Directions:
1. Whisk together sauce ingredients; chill for at least 30 minutes.
2. Toss together salad ingredients, top with sauce before serving.

Description: 
A tasty, lighter way to enjoy the classic burger at home!
