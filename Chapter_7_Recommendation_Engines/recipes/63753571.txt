Title:
Loretta Cooper's Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120276.jpg
Ingredients:
8-12 Potatoes (any size)
Mustard
Miracle Whip
Dill Relish (not sweet)
Onions (optional)

Directions:
Make Boiled Potatoes in a Pot. Boil about 30 minutes
Mix up potatoes with an electric mixer. Put mustard with potatoes. It does not matter how much mustard you put in. Pour Miracle whip in potatoes. You may decide how much you pour in. Put relish in potatoes, at least 1/4 quart.

Submitter: 
Cooper
Description: 
It is my favorite potato salad of all time. 

Please try this when you are ready.
