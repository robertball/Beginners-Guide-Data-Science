Title:
Pepperoni Hotdish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked elbow noodles
1  pound lean ground beef
2 T onion, minced
garlic to taste
oregano to taste
1 bag pepperoni
15 oz tomato sauce
1/4 cup water
2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cook noodles according to package directions.
In a medium skillet over medium-high heat, brown the ground beef with the onion, garlic and oregano. Drain excess fat.
Combine noodles, meat mix, tomato sauce, and water.  Place in 9x11 pan.  Cover with cheeses, pepperoni, and any other choice of topping.
Bake 40 minutes.
