Title:
Plantation Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018710.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C. All purpose flour
1 C. Whole wheat pastry flour
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1 3/4 C. Oat bran
1/2 C. Butter
1 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs
1 C. Canned pineapple, (juice drained) diced
1 C. Banana, mashed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
In a large bowl combine butter and sugar then cut butter into small chunks and mix with the sugar
In a medium size bowl combine dry ingredients and mix together then set a side
Add eggs one at a time to sugar mixture and beat with an electric mixer until creamy and smooth
Pour in the oat bran and beat with mixer until oat bran makes a firm paste
Stir in pineapples and bananas evenly distributing them throughout the batter
Add the dry ingredients to the mixture to make a drop cookie dough
Drop by teaspoonful onto a greased cookie sheet 2 inches apart and bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes or until lightly browned around the edges
