Title:
Poppy Seed Bundt Cake II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 - 1 1/4 cups light sour cream
2 T. poppy seeds
1/2 cup white sugar
4 eggs
1 tsp each of: vanilla, almond, lemon extracts
Glaze:
1 1/4 - 1/2 c sifted powdered sugar
2 T butter, melted
1 tsp each of: vanilla, almond, lemon extracts
1-3 T water, or to desired consistency

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and sugar a 10 inch Bundt pan.
In a large bowl, combine cake mix, oil, melted butter, sour cream, poppy seeds, water, sugar, and eggs. Mix with hand mixer on low speed until blended. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
Cool in pan 10 minutes; then invert onto a platter covered with wax paper.  Prepare glaze and spread on top of cake while still warm.
