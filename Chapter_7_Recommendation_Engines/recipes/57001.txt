Title:
Brie Cheese Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/544652.jpg
Ingredients:
8 1/2 ounces Brie cheese, thinly sliced
2 cups sliced almonds
1 (14 ounce) package purchased fully baked pizza crust (such as BoboliÂ®)

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Arrange Brie slices on pizza crust; cover with sliced almonds.  Bake until cheese is melted and almonds are toasted, about 10 minutes.  Slice into small wedges to serve.

Submitter: 
JERSIE
Description: 
This is a great appetizer that tastes great and takes seconds to prepare! Just layer Brie cheese and almonds on a  pre-made pizza crust, and bake!
