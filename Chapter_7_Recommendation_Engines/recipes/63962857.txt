Title:
Tomato Basil and Gouda Cheese Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces gouda cheese, cut in 1" cubes
1/3 cup fresh tomatoes, diced
1 tablespoon fresh basil, chopped
1 baguette loaf, sliced and toasted

Directions:
1.	Place cheese in a bowl and microwave on high for 1 minute.  Stir cheese and continue cooking for 30 second intervals until cheese is thoroughly melted.
2.	Stir in tomato and basil to blend.  Serve with toasted or cubed bread.

Karen Monson
Description: 
A great savory fondue.
