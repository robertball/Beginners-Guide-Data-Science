Title:
Dave's Panagoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lime
250 ml Cherry Tomatoes
1 Avocado
125ml frozen corn, defrosted
125ml kidney beans (canned, rinsed)
125ml Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
250ml cooked brown rice
100g lettuce
100ml bbq sauce (sweet and smoky is best)
250g chicken breast cooked, seasoned with salt and pepper - thinly sliced
Hot sauce (as desired)

Directions:
If rice not cooked, cook rice
If chicken not cooked, bbq or pan fry chicken until cooked.
Prep veggies, shred cheese
Combine rice, chicken, rice and squeeze lime juice and hot sauce as desired into large individual salad bowls. Add about 50ml of bbq sauce per salad and toss
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
DVD
Description: 
A great combination of ingredients that is fresh and yet almost comfort food. I serve this in giant salad bowls for each portion. If you make chicken and rice on weekend, this dish can be made quick during the week!
