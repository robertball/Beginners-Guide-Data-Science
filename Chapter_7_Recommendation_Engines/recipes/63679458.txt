Title:
Spaghetti Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1023740.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 pound ground beef
1 onion, diced
1 green pepper, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 small jalapeno, minced
1 14.5 ounce can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 14.5 ounce can stewed tomatoes, undrained
1 15 ounce can tomato sauce
1/4 cup chili powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 15.5 ounce can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 pound spaghetti (or pasta of your choice)
Shredded sharp cheddar cheese
Sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Heat oil in a Dutch oven and cook ground beef with onion, green pepper and garlic until ground beef is no longer pink.
Add jalapeno, diced tomatoes, stewed tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder and salt.  Bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat and simmer for at least one hour.
Add kidney beans, cover and simmer an additional 30 minutes or more.
Cook your choice of pasta as directed on package.  Drain but don't rinse.
Serve chili over pasta.  Top with shredded cheddar cheese and optional sour cream.

Submitter: 
Judy in Delaware
Description: 
We absolutely love chili over any type of pasta smothered in shredded cheese.  Our favorite is linguine but any type will work.  Any leftover chili is great on hot dogs, too.
