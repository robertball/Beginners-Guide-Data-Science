Title:
Pumpkin Cream Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz. pack cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup canned pumpkin
1/4 cup sugar
3/4 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
1/2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Beat all ingredients in a medium mixing bowl with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Spread on bagels, etc.
