Title:
Pineapple Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box white or yellow cake mix
1 small box vanilla instant pudding
1 (8oz) cream cheese
1 (8 oz) can crushed pineapple, drain juice
1 (8oz) tub cool whip

Directions:
Bake cake as directed.  Cool.  Make pudding as directed.  Mix pudding and cream cheese.  Put pineapple on the cake.  Spread on pudding mixture.  Cover with cool whip.  Chill.
