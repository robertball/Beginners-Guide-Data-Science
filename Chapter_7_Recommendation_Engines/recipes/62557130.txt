Title:
Teriyaki Chicken Brochettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts - boneless and skinless
1 green pepper
2 tomatoes - cut in pieces
1 zucchini
1 onion
Marinade:
3 tbsp soya sauce
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp fresh grated ginger or 1 tsp ground ginger

Directions:
Mix together marinade ingredients
Cut chicken in chunks
Bring chicken in marinade and refrigerate 30 minutes or more.
Put chicken and vegeatables on a skewer.
Cook at 350 degrees in oven or  barbecue until chicken is cooked.
Boil marinade for approximately 5 minutes and serve with chicken brochettes.

Submitter: 
cads550
Description: 
Can cook in the oven or on the barbecue.  Best if marinated a few hours before.
