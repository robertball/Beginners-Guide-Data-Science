Title:
Spicy Tortilla Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/704589.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
1 (4 ounce) jar sliced pimento peppers, drained
2 green onions, minced
3 tablespoons hot pepper sauce
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
10 (10 inch) flour tortillas

Directions:
In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, olives, chiles, pimentos, green onions, hot sauce and fresh cilantro. Spread the mixture onto tortillas. Roll the tortillas up and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Slice the roll ups and serve.

Submitter: 
SHECOOKS2
Description: 
These are delicious, easy, and can be made ahead of time.  No leftovers, either! I always serve them on a platter with hot sauce for dipping. You may serve them with toothpicks in each spiral for easy eating!!
