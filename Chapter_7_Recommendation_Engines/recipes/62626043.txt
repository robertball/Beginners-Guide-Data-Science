Title:
Stuffed Cabbage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # ground beef
3/4 C rice
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic
1/4 t pepper
1/2 t salt
1-28 oz can tomato sauce
1/4 C cider vinegar
1 T dry mustard
8 C coarsely chopped cabbage

Directions:
Brown meat, onion, garlic. Drain well.
Add rice, salt and pepper.
In small bowl, mix together tomato sauce, vinegar and dry mustard.
Layer 1/3 of cabbage in 3 qt. deep casserole dish. Arrange 1/2 beef mixture on top, cover with another 1/3 of cabbage. Top with remaining beef mixture and remaining cabbage.
Pour tomato sauce mixture over to top. Do not stir. Casserole will be quite full. Let it stand at room temperature for about 20 min.
Bake in 325 degree oven for 2 hours without stirring.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Can to changed to your liking.  Instead of tomato sauce, use Rotel tomatoes or both.
