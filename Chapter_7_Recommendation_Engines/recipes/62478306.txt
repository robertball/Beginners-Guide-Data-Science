Caramel Corn Recipe

Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2 cups brown sugar
1 cup light corn syrup
1 can eagle brand milk
1 tsp. vanilla
10 quarts popped popcorn

Directions:
Mix together and Stir constantly
take off just before soft ball stage.
Pour over popped pop corn
