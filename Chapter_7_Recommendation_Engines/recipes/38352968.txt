Title:
Stuffed Jalapeno Poppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 medium to large jalapeno peppers
1 8oz package cream cheese
1 pound bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to broil.
Cut off stem tops of peppers.
Slice jalapeno's in half, lengthwise and remove seeds.
Fill cavity of jalapeno with cream cheese.
Wrap one piece of bacon around each pepper, spiralling bacon to cover pepper entirely.  Cut bacon in half if peppers are small.
Place in oven on ungreased cookie sheet.
Broil for 10-12 minutes, checking periodically.
Poppers are done when bacon is cooked through.
