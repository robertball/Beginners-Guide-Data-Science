Title:
A Spicy Rubdown Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons chili powder
2 tablespoons dried oregano
2 tablespoons garlic powder
2 tablespoons ground coriander
1/4 cup ground cumin
1 tablespoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon ground red pepper (cayenne)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl. Store in small jars such as baby food or recycled spice jars.

Submitter: 
Albertsons
Description: 
Add a powerful punch to grilled chicken, seafood, or vegetables with this make-ahead spice blend with a Tex-Mex accent. It makes a terrific gift!
