Title:
Best Ever Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 cups chopped onion
2 cups chopped carrot
2 cups finely chopped celery
1/2 tablespoon minced garlic
1 cup yellow split peas
1 cup green split peas
8 cups fat-free chicken broth
HAM HOCK
1 1/2 teaspoons salt-free seasoning blend
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat, heat olive oil.  Cook onion, carrot, celery and garlic until onion is translucent.  Stir in yellow and green split peas, broth, seasoning and salt.  ADD HAM HOCK.  Cover, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 2 1/2 hours, stirring frequently.  REMOVE HAM HOCK BONE.  WHEN SIMMERED LONG ENOUGH THE VEGETABLES AND PEAS BREAK DOWN SO PUREEING THEM IS NOT NECESSARY.
