Title:
Pecan Date Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
filling:
1.1 lb ( 3 cups) pitted chopped dates
2. 1 1/2 cups orange juice
Crust and topping:
3. 1 1/2 cups flour
4. 1 1/2 cups o;d fashioned oats
5. 2/3 cup light brown sugar
6. 1 1/2 cup pecans chopped
7. 2 1/2 sticks cold butter cut up
8. powdered sugar

Directions:
maake filling in 3 qt saucepan bring to boil 1. and 2. reduce heat to low and simmer stirring often 15 min till dates are tender and juice is absorbed and mixture thickens set aside to cool
Heat oven to 350 ...line 13" x 9" pan with foiland coat with non stick spray
Crust and topping : Put 3. 4. 5. 6. in food processor pulse to blend add 7. and pulse till mixture is crumbly...transfer 1/2 of this mixture to pan lined with foil and press down spoon filling over the crust and spread evenly...spoon remainder of topping over the top of filling
Bake 350* 35 min till edges are brown
cool in pan...lift foil out of pan and cut bars on cutting board - dust with powdered sugar store with wax paper between layers
