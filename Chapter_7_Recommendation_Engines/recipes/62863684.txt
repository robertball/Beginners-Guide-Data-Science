Title:
Italian Lentil Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb green lentils
2 scallions, sliced thin
1 cup halved seedless grapes
1 small cucumber, peeled, seeded and diced
2 stalks celery, diced
1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
vinaigrette of choice

Directions:
Bring large pot of water to boil.  Add lentils and cook until tender, about 20 minutes.  Drain and let cool 5 minutes.  Toss remaining ingredients together.  Serve warm or cold.
