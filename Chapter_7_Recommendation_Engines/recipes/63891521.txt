Title:
Cracker Stuffing for Shrimp or Flounder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- stick butter melted ( might need more to hold crumbs together)
1- onion chopped
2 cloves garlic minced
2 talb gingerale or white wine or sherry)
salt & pepper
3 sleeves ritz crackers crushed
12-20 etra large jumbo shrimp thawed with the body shell removed ( keep tail shell on) slice in the middle be careful not to go all way through - take out black line in the shrimp - - or 10-12 flounder

Directions:
In fry pan saut√© onion and garlic.
In large bowl crush ritz crackers add rest of ingredients ...  add enough butter to be able to hold crumbs together...
for shrimp- stuff the center of the shrimp place on buttered baking pan ( sheet pan/ jelly roll pan)
for flounder - place 2 talb stuffing on the end and roll the flounder- place seam side down on buttered baking pan....4 talb water in the pan...
Bake 350* covered with tin foil first 10 min then remove the foil total 20 -30 min
serve with melted butter
for flounder - bake 350 * pour a little milk over the flounder cover tin foil covered 10 min then take off foil and bake 10 more min
