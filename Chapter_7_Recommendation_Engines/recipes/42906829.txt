Title:
Frozen Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz Cream cheese
2 tbap Mayonnaise
2 Tbap Sugar
1 16oz Can Cranberry Sauce
1 Cup Crushed Pineapple
1/2 Cup chopped nuts
1 Cup Whipping Cream Whipped

Directions:
Soften cheese
Blend in Mayo, sugar, fruit, and nuts
Fold in whipped cream
Pour into greased pan and freeze 6 hours or more
Slice and serve
