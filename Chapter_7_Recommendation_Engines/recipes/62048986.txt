Title:
Baked 4 cheese Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound imported conchiglie rigate (shells)
2 cups heavy cream
1 cup canned tomatoes, puréed
1/4 pound thinly sliced mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup (1 1/2 ounces) freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese, plus more for garnish
1/2 cup (1 1/2 ounces) coarsely shredded fontina cheese
1/4 cup (1 1/2 ounces) crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
2 tablespoons ricotta cheese
1/4 teaspoon coarse salt
6 basil leaves, coarsely chopped
4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, cut

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 500°. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta, and cook for 4 minutes. Drain in a colander.
2. While pasta is cooking, place cream, tomatoes, cheeses, salt, and basil in a large bowl. Stir to combine. Add pasta to cheese mixture, and toss to combine.
3. Divide among six to eight shallow ceramic gratin dishes (1 1/2 to 2 cups in capacity). Dot with butter, and bake until bubbly and brown on top, 7 to 10 minutes. Sprinkle with Pecorino Romano and scallions. Serve immediately.
