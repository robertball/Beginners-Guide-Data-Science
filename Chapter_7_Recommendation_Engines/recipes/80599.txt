Title:
Spice Sachets for Mulled Cider and Wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 inch) cinnamon stick
1 teaspoon whole cloves
1 teaspoon whole allspice berries
1 teaspoon dried grated orange peel
3 cardamom pods
One 6-inch square double layers of cheesecloth
Kitchen twine
Ribbon
Sprig of evergreen or holly
1 gallon apple cider or one 750ml bottle red wine

Directions:
Place spices in the center of each cheesecloth square. Gather edges and neatly tie each sachet with kitchen twine. Tie each bag with a ribbon, and tuck a sprig of evergreen or holly into the bow.
For a gift: Attach a sachet to a jug of apple cider or bottle of wine. Include recipe on an index card or other recipe card as follows:  <p> Remove the ribbon and greens from sachet. Combine l gallon of apple cider or 750ml bottle of wine with l spice sachet in a large nonaluminum pot. Simmer over low heat 15 minutes if using wine or 30 minutes if using apple cider. Ladle into mugs and serve hot, garnish with a cinnamon stick or orange slice, if desired. Makes approximately 32 servings.

Submitter: 
Albertsons
Description: 
Cinnamon sticks, whole cloves, allspice berries, and cardamom pods are all wrapped up in cheesecloth and tied up with a holiday ribbon to give as a present. This recipe makes one sachet, but you can easily make dozens of these simple gifts. Instructions for using a sachet to make mulled wine or cider are included.
