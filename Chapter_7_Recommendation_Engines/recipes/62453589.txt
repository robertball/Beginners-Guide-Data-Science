Title:
Roasted Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs. fresh tomatoes, washed and cored, preferably homegrown or from a farmer's market.
1/2 onion, minced
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tbl olive oil
1 cup stock (vegetable or chicken)
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Place the tomatoes on a cookie sheet and roast at 400 degrees until the skin darkens and splits, about 30 minutes.
While the tomatoes are roasting, saute the onion and garlic in the oil until translucent.  Puree the tomatoes and any juices, onions, garlic, stock and seasoning.
Taste and adjust the seasoning.  Serve with fresh croutons.

Submitter: 
TexasJG
Description: 
This is a great way to use up garden tomatoes.  The roasting gives the tomatoes a rich flavor not found in other tomato soups.  It is a purist soup, without a lot of other flavors to mask to rich tomato flavor.
