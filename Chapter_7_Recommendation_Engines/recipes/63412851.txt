Title:
Broccoli and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/926482.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz. of raw broccoli, steamed
3 cups cooked rice
1/2 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup chopped celery
2 Tablespoons butter
1 small can mushrooms
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup butter
8 oz. pasteurized prepared cheese product, such as Velveeta
1/2 cup water
1 8 oz. can water chestnuts, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Saute onions and celery in 2 T. butter until softened.
Add mushrooms and cook 2 minutes.
Add soup, 1/2 cup butter, Velveeta, water, and water chestnuts until all is melted together.
Add the cooked broccoli and rice and stir well.
Pour mixture into a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
This is a casserole usually reserved at my home for holidays, but I don't really know why. It's perfect for any occasion and can be put together and frozen for an easy side dish. I prefer this dish using regular rice, not instant.
