Title:
Black bean quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15-oz. can black beans, rinsed and drained (1 3/4c.)
2-3 canned chipotle chiles, chopped
8 corn tortillas
1 c. jack or quesillo cheese, grated
2 T. coarsely chopped cilantro leaves
16 1/8-inch-thick slices white onion

Directions:
Blend beans and chiles to coarse paste, adding water if necessary.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Place 4 tortillas on a warmed ungreased griddle.  Arrange1/4 of cheese on each tortilla.  Top with 1/4 of cilantro and 4 onion slices.  Spread remaining 4 tortillas with bean mixture and turn over onto tortillas with cheese.  Cook 2 minutes, or till cheese begins to melt.  Flip and cook till browned.
