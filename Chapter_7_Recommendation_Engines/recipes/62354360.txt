Title:
Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. Beef
3 eggs, beaten slightly
1/2 C milk
4 slices bread, broken up (or 1 C. bread crumbs)
1/2 C finely chopped onion
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425Â°
Combine ingredients
Chill mixture if too soft to form balls.
Shape meat balls and place on cookie sheet.
Bake until nicely browned
Remove from baking sheet and use as desired.
