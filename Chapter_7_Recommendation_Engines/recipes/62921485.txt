Title:
Raw Vegan Beet Slaw with Mint Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 tablespoons coconut vinegar
5 teaspoons organic unpasturized miso paste
1/2 teaspoon organic ground black pepper
3/4 cup goji berries
3 beets, peeled and grated
1 organic fennel bulb, sliced very thin
1 bunch organic green onions, chopped
2/3 cup organic mint leaves, sliced

Directions:
1. In a large bowl, whisk together vinegar, miso and pepper.
2. (Optional) Soak goji berries in enough water to cover for 1 hour or longer. Drain.
3. Add goji berries, beets, fennel, onions and mint.
4. Mix well.
