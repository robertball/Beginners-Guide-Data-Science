Title:
Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head cauliflower
8-16 oz mushrooms
olive oil
12 oz spaghetti/fettucine
2 oz bacon or pancetta
1/2 c parmesan cheese
1/2 c or more parsley
2 eggs

Directions:
roast cauliflower and mushrooms in olive oil, salt, pepper at 450 for 30 min
prepare pasta
fry pancetta, add garlic
combine egg, parsley, cheese
drain pasta and save some pasta water
combine pancetta, garlic, cauliflower, mushrooms with pasta; add egg cheese mixture and pasta water as needed
season with salt and pepper
serve with Parmesan, parsley
