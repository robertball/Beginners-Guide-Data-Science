Title:
Spicy Cajun 15 Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  16 ounce package of Cajun 15 bean mix
1 lb. chopped ham or sausage or [1/2 lb
chopped bacon]
1 tablespoon garlic powder [not salt]
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups chopped green onions
1 cup chopped celery
1/2 can of 10 ounce can Rotel Tomatoes
3 quarts water

Directions:
In a 6 quart pot, put all ingredients, except the package of spices that come with the beans and the rotel tomatoes.  Bring to a simmer for about 2 hours, add package of spice mix and Rotel tomatoes and cook for about 30 minutes or until the larger beans are done.  If
you like really spicy hot food, put the
whole 10 ounce can of Rotels in it.
