Title:
Jim's Tastiest Slow Cooker Flank Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds Flank Steak
1-Cup all purpose flour
3-Cans diced tomatoes
1-Can tomato sauce
2-Tbs minced garlic
1-Cup soy sauce
2-Tbs worchester sauce
1-tsp onion powder

Directions:
Cut the flank steak into 2" strips. Put in a gallon ziplock baggie with one cup of flour. Shake well. Put 4-T of olive or vegetable oil in a pan and brown the flank steak. add it to the slow cooker with the rest of the ingredients. Serve over rice or egg noodles
