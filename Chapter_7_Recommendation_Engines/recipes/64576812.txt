Title:
Almost a Scratch Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cake mix (2 layer size) (your choice of flavor)
1 4serving size instant pudding mix (flavor to complement cake mix)
1 cup water
1/2 cup canola oil OR applesauce (or combo)
4 eggs
Glaze:
1 cup icing sugar
1 tbsp. water
1/4 tsp. flavoring, or more to taste (ie. vanilla, rum, lemon, cherry or orange juice, peanut butter, cocoa powder)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Beat all ingredients slowly until moistened. Beat on med. x 2 minutes.
Turn into greased AND floured 12 cup bundt pan (or other pans, greased or paper lined, as indicated above).
Bake in oven x 45 - 50 minutes for bundt or 9"x13" cakes, or until tests done with toothpick.
Let stand in bundt pan x 20 min. before turning out onto rack of plate to cool.
Can be drizzled with glaze(s) in flavor(s) to compliment.
Flavor combo suggestions:
*rum nut - white cake mix & vanilla pudding; add rum flavoring & 1/3 - 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (rum or vanilla glaze)
*chocolate cake mix & chocolate pudding; chocolate chips optional! (vanilla, chocolate, rum, mint or peanut butter, etc. glaze)
*spice cake mix w/vanilla or butterscotch pudding (cream cheese glaze)
*lemon cake mix w/lemon pudding (lemon flavored glaze)
*butter pecan cake mix w/butterscotch pudding (rum flavored glaze)

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
There are so many flavor variations for this moist (tastes-like-homemade) cake mix based cake - use your imagination! I usually bake is as a bundt cake, but you can make a 9 x 13 or cupcakes as well.
