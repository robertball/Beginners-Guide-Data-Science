Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 to 1.25 lbs skinless, boneless chicken strips
2 tablespoons butter
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 oz) can condensed cream of celery soup
1 onion, finely diced
2 (10 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough, torn into pieces
1 (14 oz) can of chicken broth

Directions:
Place the chicken, butter, soups, and onion in a slow cooker, and fill with enough broth to cover.
Cook on low while at work. Tear, slightly flatten, and create strips from the biscuit dough.  Place the torn biscuit dough in the slow cooker, and finish cooking on high for 90 minutes.

Submitter: 
Mona Bowen
Description: 
Easy, creamy chicken with delicate dumplings made from refrigerator biscuits, slow cooked to comfort food perfection.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings
