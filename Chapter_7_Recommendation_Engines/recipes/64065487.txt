Title:
Caramel Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 German chocolate cake mix
3/4 cups melted butter
2/3 cup evaporated milk, divided
14 oz. caramels
6 oz. chocolate chips
3/4 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9x13 cake pan.
Mix together cake mix, butter and 1/3 cup evaporated milk. Spread 1/2 mixture in the pan; bake 6 minutes.
Melt caramels and 1/3 cup milk in a small saucepan.
Spread caramel mixture on baked cake, sprinkle with chocolate chips and nuts. Cover with remaining cake mixture. bake an additional 20 minutes.
