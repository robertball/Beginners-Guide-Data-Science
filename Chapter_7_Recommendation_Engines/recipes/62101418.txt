Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C sugar
1/2 C shortening
2 eggs
2 C flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1/2 C buttermilk
1 C mashed bananas

Directions:
Cream sugar & shortening, add eggs one at a time.  Beat until fluffy.  Add dry mixture and mashed bananas.  Bake at 350 degrees F 60-70 minutes in well greased loaf pan.  Dough is very versatile...add chocolate chips (as much as you like) with or instead of bananas.
