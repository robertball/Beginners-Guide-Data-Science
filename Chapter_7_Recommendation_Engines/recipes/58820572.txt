Title:
Chocolate Earthquake Cake II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package dark chocolate fudge cake mix with pudding
1 cup flaked coconut
1 cup chopped pecans
8 ounces cream cheese
3 cups confectioners' sugar
1/2 cup butter soft
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Spray 9x13-inch pan with cooking spray.
Spread coconut and pecans over bottom of pan.
Mix cake mix according to package directions. Pour 1/2 over the pecans and coconut.
Melt butter, add with cream cheese, vanilla, and confectioners' sugar to a medium-sized bowl and mix well. Spoon with a teaspoon over the cake pour remaining cake batter over cream cheese mixrter bake.
Bake cake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 50-55 minutes or until done. let cool 5 min. invert onto cookie sheet
