Title:
Roast Beef with Chive Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs red potatoes, cut into 1 inch cubes
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 tsp minced chives
3/4 tsp salt, divided
2 medium onions, havled and thinly sliced
1 lb sliced fresh mushrooms
1/4 cup butter, cubed
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tsp dried rosemary, crushed
1/4 tsp pepper
1/3 cup dry red wine or beef broth
2 cups cubed cooked roast beef
1 cup beef gravy

Directions:
Place potatoes in a greased 15X15X10 baking pan.  Drizzle with oil and sprinkle with chives and 1/4 tsp salt; toss to coat.  Bake, uncovered, at 425 for 25-30 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, saute onions and mushrooms in butter until tender.  Add the garlic, rosemary, pepper and remaining salt; cook 1 minute longer.  stir in wine. Add beef and gravy; heat through. serve with potatoes.
