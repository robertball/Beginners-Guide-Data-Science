Title:
Egg and Cheese Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. baking mix (bisquick or jiffy)
1 1/2 c cottage cheese
8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
1 tsp dried onion, or 2 tsp fresh, chopped
1 tbsp chopped parsley
1/4 tsp salt
6 eggs, lightly beaten
1 c milk
3/4 c butter or margarine

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Put margarine or butter in 9x13 baking dish and put in the oven to melt the butter while you mix the other ingredients.  Mix all other ingredients, then pour into the melted butter.  Do not stir.  Bake 40 min.

Submitter: 
7woolfords
Description: 
A great combination of eggs and cheese, great for a brunch or weekend dinner.
