Title:
Ham and Cuke Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoon cream cheese, whipped low-fat
2 ounce(s) ham, deli-style lower sodium, thinly sliced
1 medium cucumber(s) sliced into sticks

Directions:
Spread the cream cheese onto each ham slice. Lay cucumber sticks on top of cheese and roll the ham into logs. Slice into smaller pieces, secure with toothpicks, if necessary.
