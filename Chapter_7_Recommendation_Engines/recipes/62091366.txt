Title:
Perfectly Melted S'mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package large marshmallows
1 package milk chocolate chips
1 package graham crackers

Directions:
Make a small slit in one flat end of the marshmallow with fingers or a paring knife.
Push 3 chocolate chips into the middle of each marshmallow (more if you can!)
Poke a stick through the rounded side of the marshmallow
Roast beside the blue portion of the campfire flame or over red-hot coals, turning often for even toasting.
When marshmallow is soft on the inside, sandwich one graham cracker on either side of the marshmallow and remove from stick.
Enjoy the perfectly toasted marshmallow with perfectly melted chocolate inside!

Description: 
The perfect technique for getting the chocolate to melt for your campfire s'mores.
