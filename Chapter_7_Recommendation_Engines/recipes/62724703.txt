Title:
Avocado and Corn dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Medium corn on the cob, grjilled with pam and Lowry's seasoning, cooled to room temperature.
1/2 cup scallions, white and green parts, chopped.
1 Medium Jalapeno pepper, cored, seeded, and minced
1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes, optional.
1 large clove of garlic minced
1/4 tsp ground cumin powder.
1/4 tsp. sea salt
1/8 tsp. black pepper
2 Medium aocado, ripe, peeled, pitted adn cut into 1/2-inch cubes.
2 Tbsp fresh lime juice, from 1 medium Lime
2 tbsp cilantro, fresh, minced

Directions:
Slice corn kernels off of cob. Put corn in bowl, add scallion, jalapeno, red pepper flakes, garlic, cumin, salt, pepper, avocado, lime juice, and cilantro.
