Title:
Deb B's Panang Meat Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422442.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 cup flour
2 tbsp. Vegetable oil
1 cup coconut milk
2 tbsp. red curry paste
1 tbsp. fish sauce
2 tbsp. peanut butter
1 tbsp sugar
10 basil leaves, optional

Directions:
Shape the ground beef into 1" balls and roll in the flour.
Stir-fry in the vegetable oil until brown, then remove from pan and set aside.
Using the oil left in the pan, stir-fry the curry paste over low heat for 2 minutes.
Add coconut milk, peanut butter, sugar and fish sauce and mix thoroughly.
Return meatballs to the pan and simmer until cooked through.
Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with basil leaves if desired.
