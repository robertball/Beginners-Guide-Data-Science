Title:
Grandma's Wassail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 gallons apple cider
2 cups fresh orange juice
1 cup lemon juice
1 cup pineapple juice
1 teaspoon whole cloves
1 stick cinnamon
1 cup sugar

Directions:
Mix all liquids in a large pot on medium-low heat.
Combine spices and add mixture to a tea ball. If you don't have a tea ball, place spice mixture on a small piece of cheesecloth and tie with a string, forming a small pouch.
Set tea ball or spice pouch in pot gently.
Simmer until warmed. Serve from pot with large ladle.

Submitter:

Description: 
An easy recipe for the traditional holiday drink.
