Title:
Sweet Perfection Chili - award winner! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb	Ground Turkey
1 lb	Ground Maple Sausage
15 oz	Kidney beans
15 oz	Tomato Sauce
30 oz	Diced tomato with chilies
1 jar	Newmans pineapple salsa
1	Sweet Onion
1 tbls	Chili powder
1 tsp	Ground Cumin
¼ cup	Pancake Syrup
4 dots	Hot Hot sauce

Directions:
Sauté the onions, then add and cook the meat
Put meat and onion mix in the crock pot, along with the beans, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, salsa, cumin and chili powder.
Cook on low for about 4-5 hours , until mixture looks like chili.
Add the syrup and dots of hot sauce about one hour before serving
Continue keep warm until ready to serve

Submitter: 
davekippen
Description: 
Sweet and spicy, not too thick or thin, just a really good hearty chili. Ground turkey and maple sausage instead of beef adds a nice change of flavor. Won first prize at an annual chili cookoff!
