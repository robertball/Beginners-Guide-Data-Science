Title:
Tofu Lemon Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. soft tofu, drained, room temp
2 tbsp. lemon juice
4 tsp. soy sauce, your choice, to taste
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
1/2 tsp. dry mustard

Directions:
Combine ingredients in blender and blend until smooth, approx. 30 seconds. Refrigerate about 2 hours to blend flavors. Bring to room temp to use. Approx. 9 calories per tbsp.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
A wonderful tasting spread that is healthy and good tasting.
