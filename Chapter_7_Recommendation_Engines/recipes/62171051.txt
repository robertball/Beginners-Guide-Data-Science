Title:
Pumpkin Pie-licious Milk Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283617.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vanilla ice cream
1/2 cup skim milk
1/4 cup canned pumpkin puree
1 tsp vanilla flavoring
1 Tbsp. sugar
1/2 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 pinch nutmeg

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and secure lid.
Blend until smooth. Pour into a tall glass and garnish with whipped cream if desired.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
Can't get enough pumpkin? This creamy, delicious shake should hit the spot! I created this one evening when I was craving pumpkin pie, but lacked the time and energy to bake one. The result has become a family favorite. This recipe is easily doubled for sharing.
