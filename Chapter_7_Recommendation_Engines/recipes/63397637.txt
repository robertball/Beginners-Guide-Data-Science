Title:
Nutty Bacon Cheeseball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 package (4 oz.) cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup milk
1 cup (8 oz.) shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 cup (8 oz.) shredded monterey jack cheese
1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
5 slices bacon, cooked, crumbled and divided
1/3 cup plus 1 tbsp. Chopped pecans divided
1/8 cup finely minced green onions (white parts only)
1/2 jar (1 oz.) diced pimiento, drained
salt and pepper
2 tbsp. Minced fresh parsley
1/2 tbsp. Poppy seeds

Directions:
Beat cream cheese and milk in bowl with electric mixer at low speed until blended.
Add cheeses. Beat at medium speed until well mixed. Add half of the bacon, half of the pecans, green onions and pimiento. Beat at medium speed until well mixed.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Transfer to large piece of plastic wrap. Shape into ball; wrap tightly.
Refrigerate at least 2 hours or until chilled, or ready to serve (up to 24 hrs.).
Combine remaining bacon, and pecans with parsley and poppy seeds in pie plate.
Remove plastic wrap from chilled cheese ball.
Roll in bacon mixture until well coated.
Serve and enjoy!
