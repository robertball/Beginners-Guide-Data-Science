Title:
Crock Pot Lemon Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/461911.jpg
Ingredients:
3 quarts water
3 boneless skinless chicken breasts
3 lemons, juiced
1 (10 oz)box frozen chopped spinach
1 cup uncooked brown rice
3 medium carrots, coined
3 stalks celery, sliced
1 small onion, chopped
3T chicken boullion granules
1T salt
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Add chicken breasts, water, carrots, celery, onion, boullion granules, salt and pepper to crock pot.  Cook on low for 6 hours. Add rice and lemon juice.  Cook 2 hours more.  Remove chicken breasts and shred them with forks.  Return chicken to crockpot and add thawed spinach.  Leave in crock pot until spinach is warm.

Submitter: 
Andrea Patton
Description: 
Chicken, veggies, rice and lemon juice make a tangy, healthy (and gluten free!) soup.Modified from: Lemon Chicken Soup II
