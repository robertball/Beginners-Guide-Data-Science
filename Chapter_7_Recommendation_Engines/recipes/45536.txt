Title:
Newfoundland Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/926779.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce dark rum
1 fluid ounce coffee flavored liqueur
8 fluid ounces hot brewed coffee
1/2 fluid ounce Irish cream liqueur
1 maraschino cherry

Directions:
Measure rum and coffee liqueur into a coffee mug. Pour in hot coffee. Top with Irish cream, and garnish with a cherry.

Submitter: 
MIKECRAWFORD
Description: 
A wonderful coffee treat with a kick. If you live in Newfoundland, you can use Screech instead of rum. Add a dollop of whipped cream if you like.
