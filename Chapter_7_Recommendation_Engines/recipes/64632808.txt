Title:
Sous Vide Beef Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
tenderloin
thyme
rosemary
salt pep per
butter

Directions:
Let beef stay in refrigerator for 24-3 6 hours uncovered and heavily salted.  After, heavily season the meat put 1/4 stick butter cut length wise and vacuum seal.   Let sit minimum for 2 weeks to months.
Let beef come to room temp, Cook at 131.0 for 3.5 hours.  Put in a 500 degree oven for 10 maybe 15 min.   Salt fairly heavy  when puttingh in oven..
