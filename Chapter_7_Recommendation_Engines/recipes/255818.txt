Title:
Super Fresh Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4483401.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
4 leaves kale
1/2 lime, juiced
1 tablespoon honey, or more to taste (optional)

Directions:
Combine banana, kale, lime juice, and honey in a blender; blend until smooth.

A very healthy grab-and-go smoothie that is good for any occasion.
