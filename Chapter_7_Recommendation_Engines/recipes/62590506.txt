Title:
Reuben Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
salt and pepper
1 can sauerkraut
Swiss cheese, 2-4 slices or shredded
1/4c Thousand Island dressing
Chopped parsley

Directions:
Spray 9x13 baking dish
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper.
Top chicken with sauerkraut and cheese.
Pour dressing over and cover with foil.
Bake 90 minutes at 325. Garnish with parsley.
