Title:
Revised Watergate Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) packages instant pistachio pudding mix sifted. (If you don't like the hard nuts)
8 oz sour cream
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, most of juice drained
1 can diced pears
1 can diced peaches
1 (4 ounce) jar maraschino cherries, drained (optional)
1 (8 ounce) containers whipped topping (such as Cool WhipÂ®)
2 cups miniature marshmallows, or more as needed

Directions:
Combine sour cream and pie filling.  Stir in fruit until combined. Gently stir in whip topping and then add marshmallows.  More marshmallows can be added if desired.
