Title:
Steve's Famous Pico De Gallo/Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2960323.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Red onion diced
1 29oz can of petite diced tomatoes, drained OR  3 cups of chopped fresh tomatoes
3 cloves minced garlic
1/2 cup of fresh chopped cilantro
1 Jalapeño pepper (or other hot pepper) finely chopped.
1 tsp salt
1/2 lime

Directions:
In a mixing bowl placed diced tomatoes and add onion garlic, jalapeño, cilantro and salt.
Squeeze 1/2 lime into mixture
Stir and refrigerate 3-4 hours for flavors to develop
To make into a salsa, place above ingredients into a food processor and chop until it reaches desired consistency.
Customize recipe with different peppers, tomatillos, black beans, corn, fresh mango etc.

Submitter: 
Steven Dallwig
Description: 
This easy and customizable recipe that I have created over the years is a party favorite, loved and requested time and time again. The recipe can be made as either a pico (with fresh or canned diced tomatoes) or a salsa (run through a food processor)
