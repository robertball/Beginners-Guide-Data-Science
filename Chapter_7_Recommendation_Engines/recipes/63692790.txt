Title:
Fruit/Cracker Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1 stick real butter, softened
1 tsp. vanilla
2 Tbsp. brown sugar
3/4 - 1 C. powdered sugar
3/4 C. mini choc. chips

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, butter, and vanilla with beaters for 2 minutes until fluffy.
Add sugars and mix for another minute.
Spoon stir in choc. chips.
Refrigerate overnight.
Serve with graham cracker sticks and strawberries.
