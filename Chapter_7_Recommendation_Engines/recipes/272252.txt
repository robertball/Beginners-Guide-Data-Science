Title:
4-Ingredient Strawberry Vinaigrette  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6696057.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
4 large strawberries, pureed (1/3 cup pureed)
1 1/2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
1 teaspoon poppy seeds

Directions:
Combine apple cider vinegar, strawberry puree, and honey in a mini blender; blend until smooth. Pour into a glass container and add poppy seeds. Let rest on counter for an hour for flavors to marinate, then store in fridge.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
Slightly tangy, easy vinaigrette with fresh strawberries, poppy seeds, apple cider vinegar, and honey - great for a spinach salad.
