Title:
THM Brownie in a Mug Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Egg
1 T. Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
1 T. Plain Greek Yogurt
1 T. Coconut Oil
1 T. Unsweetened Peanut Butter
5-6 tsp Truvia
Pinch of Salt
1/2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Stir all ingredients until blended well.  Put into a mug and microwave for 1 min (cooking time may vary due to microwave wattage).  Turn it upside down onto a plate and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kristen Bowman
Description: 
This is a quick & easy way to enjoy a gluten free, sugar free brownie for 1!
