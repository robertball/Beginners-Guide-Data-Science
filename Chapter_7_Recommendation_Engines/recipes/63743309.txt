Title:
Orange Crunch Cake - Bubble Room, Captiva, FL Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
Duncan Hines Orange Supreme Cake
1/3 c oil
1 1/3 c water
3 eggs
Crunch layer
1 cup graham crackers (7 sheets)
1/2 cup sliced almonds
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
Frosting
8ox pkg cream cheese
3 c powdered sugar
1 box instant vanilla pudding
1/2 tsp vanilla
1tsp orange extract

Directions:
Prepare crunch layer. Press into bottom of greased and floured pan(s) like a cheesecake crust. Prepare cake mix as directed on box. Pour on top of crunch layer. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
While baking, cream the cream cheese and powdered sugar, add in vanilla and orange extract. Finally, add in pudding mix. (Stir in food coloring at this point if you'd like 12drops yellow/4 drops red).
Cool cake, then frost. Keep refrigerated

Submitter: 
Colleen Hibbitts
Description: 
Beautiful cake, don't mix coloring completely into frosting to give marbled effect
