Title:
All Shrimped Up! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
3 tbsp. liquid honey
1/4 cup sweet chili sauce
1 tsp finely grated lemon zest
1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
2/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
2 finely chopped green onions
1 tsp. seasoned salt
2 tsp. dried crushed chilies
2/3 cup white wine
about 1 lbs. raw jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
bamboo skewers soaked in water

Directions:
Combine first 10 ingredients in large bowl.  Add shrimp and stir to coat.  Cover and marinate in refrigerator for about 3 hours, turning several times.  Remove shrimp.  Pour marinade into small saucepan.  Bring to boil for 5 minutes.
Thread shrimp lengthwise onto skewers, starting at head end.  Grill or broil shrimp for 2 minutes per side, brushing with marinade several times, until shrimp are just pink and tender.

Description: 
Marinated shrimp on a stick makes a perfect appetizer!
