Title:
Refreshing Watermelon Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1032280.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups cubed seeded watermelon
2 sprigs peppermint, leaves plucked off
2 teaspoons honey (optional)
10 ice cubes

Directions:
Place the watermelon, peppermint leaves, honey, and ice cubes into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Serve immediately.
