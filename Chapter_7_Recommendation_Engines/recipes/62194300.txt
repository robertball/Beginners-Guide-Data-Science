Title:
Leftover Halloween Candy Brownies! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/299744.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup plus 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
1/3 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 large egg
2 cups of your favorite chocolate candy. Feel free to use variety!

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350Â° F. Grease 9-inch square baking pan.
COMBINE flour, baking powder and salt in medium bowl. Beat sugar, butter and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl until creamy. Beat in egg. Gradually beat in flour mixture. STIR in candy. Do not blend! Press into prepared baking pan.
BAKE for 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown. Cool for 10 mins. in pan on wire rack. Cut into bars. ENJOY!
