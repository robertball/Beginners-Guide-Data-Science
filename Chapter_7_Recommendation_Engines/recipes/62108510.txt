Title:
Mama's Gnocchi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
17 oz package Gnocchi
16 oz tomato sauce
1/2 C shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 C grated fresh parmesan, plus additional to pass at the table (you can substitute the green can kind!)
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt (or to taste)

Directions:
Prepare gnocchi according to package directions, then drain.
Add tomato sauce, grated cheeses and seasonings.
Stir to combine and serve. Pass grated parmesan at the table. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Dana Hall
Description: 
Gnocchi in a basic red sauce that your kids will love!
