Title:
Nutter Butter Raindeer Cookies Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
What an easy and fun recipe to put together with your kids! Just some Nutter Butter cookies, frosting, pretzel twists, M and M's and you're set! Feel free to SHARE this post with your friends.

Directions:
Nutter Butter cookies, use the frosting as glue to glue a  pretzel twists at the top ( antlers) and  M and M's for eyes, nose and mouth
