Title:
Tapas Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup (50 mL) sugar
1 1/2 tsp (7mL) crushed fennel seeds
1 tsp (5mL) red pepper flakes
1 tsp (5mL) salt (I use sea salt)
2 cups natural whole almonds
1 tbsp (15mL) water

Directions:
Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper or nonstick foil. Combine sugar, fennel seeds, red pepper flakes and salt in a bowl. Add almonds and water; stir to combine. Spread almond mixture in a single layer in prepared pan. Bake at 325 F (160C) stirring twice, for 25-30 minutes or until sugar is melted and almonds are richly glazed; cool. Store in an airtight container in a cool dry plance for up to 1 week or freeze up to 1 month.
