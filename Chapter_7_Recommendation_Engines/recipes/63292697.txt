Title:
Chicken Rice Casserole Vegetarian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
1 Cup chopped celery
1 Cup chopped Onions
1 can (10 3/4oz.) condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 can (8 oz) water chestnuts, drained and sliced
1/2 cup low fat mayonnaise
2 Cups cooked brown rice
2 cans diced Worthington FriChick
1 teaspoon Chicken McKay's chicken style seasoning
2/3 Cup potato chips (optional)

Directions:
1. In a medium frying pan, heat oil.  Add celery and onion and saute until softened.  Remove from heat.
2.  In a large mixing bowl, combine soup, water chestnuts, mayonnaise, rice, worthington FriChick, and seasoning.  Stir in sauteed celery and onions.  Spread into 1 1/2 quart glass baking dish coated with cooking spray.  Sprinkle with potato chips if desired.
3.  Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 50 minutes until thoroughly heated.
