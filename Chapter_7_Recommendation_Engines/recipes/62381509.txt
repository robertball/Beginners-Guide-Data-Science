Title:
Crunchy Onion Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
French Fried Onion rings (4oz)
2 Tbl flour
4 boneless chicken breasts
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Crush onion rings in bag with flour
Dip chicken into egg, then coat in onion crumbs
Bake 15-20---until done.
