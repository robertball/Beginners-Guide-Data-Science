Title:
Provencal Beef and Olive Daube Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MARINADE
1/2 cup olive oil
1 onion sliced
4 shallots, sliced
1 celery stalk, sliced
1 carrot, sliced
2/3 cup red wine
6 peppercorns
2 garlic cloves, sliced
1 bay leaf
1 sprig of thyme
2 parsley stalks
3 pounds topside beef
8 oz. thick bacon, chopped
8 oz. carrots, sliced
1 sprig of thyme
2 parsley stalks
3 garlic cloves, sliced
8 oz. pitted black olives
14 oz. can diced tomatoes

Directions:
To make marinade- heat oil in pan over medium. Add onion, shallots, celery and carrots; cook 2 minutes. Lower heat and add wine, peppercorns, garlic, bay leaf, thyme and parsley stalks. Cover and simmer 20 minutes. Place beef in a large dish. Pour completely cooled marinade over beef, cover, and refrigerate for 12 hours, turning once. Preheat oven to 325. Lift meat out of marinade and place in baking dish. Strain marinade and add to beef. Add bacon, carrots, herbs and garlic. Cover with grease proof paper, then lid, and cook 2 1/2 hours. Remove and stir in olives and tomatoes. Recover and bake 30 minutes longer. Serve with beans or pasta.
