Title:
Hot Christmas Cranberry Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12oz) package fresh or frozen cranberries
2 1/2 quarts water
2 c orange juice
1 c sugar
1 c pineapple juice
2 Tbsp lemon juice
3 cinnamon sticks

Directions:
In a large pot bring cranberries and water to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5-7 minutes or until the berries pop. Stir in remaining ingredients and return to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered for 30 minutes. Strain through a cheesecloth and throw away pulp and cinnamon sticks. Serve warm.
