Title:
Seafood Alfredo (low sodium) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Spaghetti or Fettuccine noodles
8 oz shrimp
8 oz scallops
1/2 lb salmon fillets, cubed
2 TBLS olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
3 TBLS flour
1 1/2 cup non fat, non dairy creamer
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1/4 cup Parmesan

Directions:
Cook noodles according to directions.
Saute shrimp, scallops, salmon & garlic with the olive oil in large sauce pan on medium heat.
Shake together flour & creamer; add to pan along with Italian seasoning.
Cook and stir until thickened and just starting to boil; add Parmesan and cook until melted.
Serve over noodles of choice

Submitter: 
limegreendog
Description: 
Because of the shrimp & cheeses this is kind of high for those with a very restrictive low sodium diet, so be careful with this. But, this is a great way to have a lighter version of the meal.
