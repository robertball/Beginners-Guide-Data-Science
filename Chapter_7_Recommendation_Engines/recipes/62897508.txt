Title:
Easy Lasagna Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound turkey sausage
1/4 C  chopped onion
4 garlic clove, minced
2 jars spaghetti sauce
1 package frozen spinach
32 oz ricotta cheese
4 oz goat cheese
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3 egg, lightly beaten
4 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
1 teaspoon onion powder
24 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
In a skillet, cook beef, onion and garlic until meat is no longer pink; drain. Add spaghetti sauce, simmer for 10 minutes. Spoon half of the meat sauce into three greased 9-in. square baking dish. combine cospinach, ricotta,goat cheeese,  Parmesan cheese, egg, parsley and onion powder; spread 1/4 cupful on each noodle. Carefully roll up and place seam side down over meat sauce. Top with remaining meat sauce. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Freeze two containers. Cover and bake third container at 375 degrees F for 30-35 minutes or until heated through. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
I saw a recipe that rolled lasagna noodles into individual servings, and I adapted my original recipe to this efficient way of preparing it. Now my husband and I enjoy this entree often.Modified from: Lasagna Roll-Ups
