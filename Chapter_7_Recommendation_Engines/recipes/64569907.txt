Title:
Big Ray's Asian Chicken Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3526870.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (3 oz.) ramen noodle soup mix
1 pkg. (14 oz.) coleslaw blend (cabbage slaw mix)
2 cups shredded or chopped cooked chicken/turkey
4 green onions, diagonally sliced
1/3 cup salted peanuts
1/2 cup  Asian Toasted Sesame Dressing
1 Tbsp creamy peanut butter

Directions:
Break Noodles into small pieces in large bowl. Add coleslaw blend, chicken, onions and nuts; mix lightly. Discard Seasoning Packet from soup mix or reserve for another use.
Beat dressing and peanut butter with whisk until blended. Add to salad; toss to coat.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
I always have some shredded cooked chicken/turkey in the freezer so this fabulous-looking Asian Chicken Noodle Salad is a snap to make, thanks to a coleslaw blend, a ramen noodle soup mix and a spoonful of peanut butter.  You could always just use a  rotisserie chicken.
