Title:
April's Spicy Chickpea Soup with Kale Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2206847.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T. olive oil
2 t. cumin seeds
1/2 t. red chili flakes (optional)
3 cloves garlic (minced)
1 medium onion (minced)
1 large carrot (peeled and diced)
2 stalks celery (diced)
1 (15 oz.) can lightly smashed chickpeas (optional to drain and rinse)
4 cups chicken stock
1 small bunch kale (chopped)
1/2 c. parsley (finely chopped)
salt and cracked black pepper
parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
In medium sized pot heat oil on medium heat till warm.
Add cumin seeds and red chili flakes and sauté till fragrant (about 30 seconds).
Add garlic, onion, carrot, and celery and sauté about 5 minutes till onion in translucent.
Add chickpeas, chicken stock, and kale.
Bring soup to simmer and cook till kale is tender (about 10-15 minutes).
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Add parsley.
Turn off heat.
Serve.
Grate fresh parmesan cheese over top steaming hot soup to taste.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
My friend April made this soup for me one afternoon when visiting. I loved it so much I asked her how she made it and now make it often for myself. It's easy, healthy, and can be adjusted to personal tastes easily. Enjoy!
