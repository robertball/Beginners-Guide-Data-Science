Title:
Best-Ever Amish Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12-ounce box elbow macaroni prepared according to package - rinse in cold water, drain well, set aside
4 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1 small red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
1 small Vidalia onion, chopped (sweet onion)
3 stalks of celery, chopped
2 cups Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
2 tablespoon dill pickle relish
3 tablespoons yellow mustard
2 teaspoons white vinegar
1/4 cup sugar
3/4 teaspoon celery seed
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1. Prepare pasta according to package, rinse in cold water, drain well, set aside.
2. On a large cutting board, chop veggies and eggs.
3. In a large bowl, mix all of the dressing ingredients and stir until blended well. Pour veggies and eggs on top, mix well. Add prepared macaroni and stir until well blended.
4. Place covered in fridge and chill for 1 to 2 hours. Stir before serving.
5. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe makes a special tasting, creamy Amish macaroni salad that will blow your mind!
