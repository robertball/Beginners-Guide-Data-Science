Title:
Best Fresh Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
1-1/2 cups cooking oil
2 cups sugar
2 eggs
3 cups peeled, cored, chopped apples
1 cup English walnuts
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 teaspoon cinnamon
Icing
1-1/2 cup sugar
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
1-1/2 teaspoon white syrup
3/4 cup buttermilk
3/4 teaspoon vanilla
6 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Cake
1. Combine oil, sugar, and eggs. Beat together and add chopped apples, vanilla, and cinnamon.
2. Mix together with nuts, flour, baking soda, and salt.
3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, grease 9x13 pan.
4. Bake for one hour.
Icing
1. Mix all ingredients together and cook for about 2 minutes.
2. After it comes to a boil pour over your Fresh Apple Cake and allow it to cool before cutting.

Submitter: 
Tracie
Description: 
Mmmmm... Fresh Apple Cake with homemade Buttermilk Icing.  In our family this is everybody's favorite!  On birthdays, it's the only request.  And of course all the holidays.  It will be a wonderful addition to your recipe box!!
