Title:
Macaroni Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Elbow Macaroni
1 lb. Ground Beef (or 1/2 Beef, 1/2 Sausage)
1 1/2 cups Tomato Sauce
1 (6 oz) can Tomato Paste
1/2 cup Water
1 TBSP Mild Chili Powder (or to taste)
1 tsp Dried Parsley
1 1/2 cups Medium or Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Cook Macaroni to preference.  Set aside  In a large skillet brown the meat, drain off excess fat, add tomato sauce & paste, water, chili powder and parsley to meat.  Stir until combined.  Bring mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer or five minutes.  Mix pasta and cheese into meat mixture.
