Title:
Lettuce and Egg salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head iceburg lettuce, chopped
2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
Dressing ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 egg
1/3 cup sugar
1 tablespoon vinegar
2 tablespoons mayonnaise (optional)

Directions:
Place lettuce and egg in a large bowl.  In a small saucepan, melt butter over medium-low heat, and add sugar.  Add egg and mix quickly, then add vinegar.  Cook dressing mixture or until thick. Place in refrigerator and let cool for 15 minutes.  Mixture can be thinned with mayonnaise, until desired consistency.  When ready to eat, toss dressing with lettuce and egg mixture.

Submitter: 
Irlandes
Description: 
This is a very easy recipe and goes well with as a side to any type of pasta.  Bacon and tomato can be added for a more filling salad.
