Title:
Bacon Wrapped Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (1 inch thick) boneless pork chops
1 c. shredded sharp cheddar/jack cheese, divided.
8 slices bacon

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Fry the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until cooked through but still flexible. Sear pork chops in bacon fat.  Wrap two slices of bacon around each pork chop and top with a partial handful of cheese. Place the pork chops in a baking dish.
Bake for 25 minutes.  Add remaining cheese and bake 25 minutes more.
