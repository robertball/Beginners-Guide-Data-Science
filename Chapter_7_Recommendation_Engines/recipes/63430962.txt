Title:
Memeres' Pork Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5lbs  ground pork
3lbs  ground beef
2 lg onions
1 TB  Cinamon
1 TB  ground cloves
12-15 medium potatoes
salt&pepper; to taste

Directions:
Brown ground pork, ground beef, and onions in a large dutch oven or skillet. Drain,and add enough water to just cover mixture. Add ground cloves, cinnamon, and salt and pepper. Cover and simmer for 90 min- 2hrs. Remove excess liquid with a large spoon, but not all liquid, (leave moist)
Add potatoes that have been mashed with only butter, or margarine (no milk).
Check seasoning. Place mixture in a two pie crust shell, make 5 slits in top pie crust.
Bake at 400 degrees for 45 min.
