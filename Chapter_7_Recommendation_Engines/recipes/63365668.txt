Title:
Aunt Susan's Apple Desert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-10 C sliced apples
2 C melted butter
2 eggs
1 1/2 C sugar
2 C flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Spread apples into a greased 9x13 pan. Combine remaining ingredients and spread over apples. Sprinkle sugar on top. Bake at 350degF/175degC for 30 min. Serve hot or cold topped with whipped cream or ice cream.

Submitter: 
sixsnowflakes
Description: 
This is really sweet, but it's a delicious way to quickly turn your fall bounty of apples into a delicious desert. Aunt Susan always makes this from golden delicious apples from her yard.
