Title:
Mexican Green Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh tomatillos
3 large jalapeno peppers-seeded
1 medium onion-chopped
2 cloves garlic
6 sprigs cilantro-stems removed
2 cups chicken broth
1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions:
Husk the tomatillos and cut in half
Stem and half jalapenos
Boil tomatillos and jalapenos in chicken broth for 10 minutes
Strain and reserve broth
In a blender, combine tomatillos, jalapenos and remaining ingredients, except for the oil, and mix until almost smooth
Heat oil in frying pan until very hot
Add the tomatillo mixture and cook for 4-5 minutes, stirring constantly until dark and thick
Add reserved broth to mixture and bring to a boil
Reduce heat and cook until thickened, about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally

Submitter: 
Great Sommelier
Description: 
Great sauce for just about any Mexican dish.  Not too spicy, so it is good for just about anyone.
