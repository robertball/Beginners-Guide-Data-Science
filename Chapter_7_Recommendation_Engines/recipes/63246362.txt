Title:
Nesquick® Chocolate Whip Topping I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/858373.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tbsp confectioners' sugar
3 tbsp Nesquick® Chocolate Drink Mix
1 pkg Whip IT® whip topping stablizer(optional)

Directions:
With a electic mixer or hand-held mixer, pour whipping cream in a small bowl, then add the vanilla, confections' sugar, drink powder & optional Whit It(R)whip topping stablizer.
Mix on high until soft but firm peaks firm. Then top on your favorite dessert item and store unused portion in the refrigerator in a covered dish.
Stores well in refrigerator for a few days no problem.

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
I came up with recipe to wow my husband around our anniversary. Was looking for a sweet chocolate whip topping that didn't taste chalky. This worked in my favor and it's been super secret until now. Enjoy.
