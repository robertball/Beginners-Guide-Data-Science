Title:
The Original Fred's Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
red wine
triple sec
spiced rum
apple puckers (the green stuff)
pineapple juice
cranberry
Ginger ale
brandy (optional)
cut fruit

Directions:
Fill pitcher 1/2 full of red wine
For the remaining 1/2, fill accordingly (decreasing in amount the further down the list)
Lots of pineapple
triple sec
puckers
smaller amount of rum
cranberry to taste (should cut the tartness)
ginger ale to taste
splash of brandy
Leave room to taste and adjust.
Add fruit
Add ice to the cups, not the pitcher

Submitter: 
bluedaisy
Description: 
The recipe for Sangria that Freds in NYC used to use.
