Title:
REESE'S PEANUT BUTTER BARS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Graham Cracker crumbs
2 sticks melted butter
2 1/3 C powdered Sugar
1 C Peanut Butter
12 oz Chocolate Morsels

Directions:
Melt butter. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar and peanut butter. Add melted butter and stir until well mixed. spread in 9 x 13 pan (thinly). Melt chocolate chips and pour over the top. Cool in the refrigerator and cut into bars.....verry rich!
