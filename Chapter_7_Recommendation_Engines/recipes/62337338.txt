Title:
Hot Fudge Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups white sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup cocoa
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 large can (14 ounces) evaporated milk
2 tbsp butter
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Combine sugars, cocoa, flour, and salt in saucepan.
Add milk and whisk until smooth.
Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until boiling. Cook 5 minutes longer.
Remove hot fudge sauce from heat. Cool slightly then stir in butter and vanilla.
