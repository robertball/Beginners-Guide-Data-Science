Title:
Margarita Grilled Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ears of corn with husks
2 t finely shredded lime peel (1-2 limes)
1 t kosher salt or sea salt
2 T melted butter
1/2 t chili powder
small limes (opt)

Directions:
Peel back husks and remove silk. Pull back husks and soak in lg. pot with water covering corn. Soak 2-4 hrs., drain well. Pull back husks and pat corn dry with paper towels.
Combine lime peel and salt in small bowl. Sprinkle over corn and tie husks with string.
Grill for 20 min. on med-high heat
To serve, remove husks and spread butter, chili powder and squeeze of lime over corn
