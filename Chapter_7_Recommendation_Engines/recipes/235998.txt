Title:
Sour Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1090133.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1 orange, peeled
2 limes, peeled
1 lemon, peeled
1 kiwi, peeled
1 teaspoon honey, or more to taste

Directions:
Blend ice, orange, limes, lemon, kiwi, and honey together in a blender until smooth.
