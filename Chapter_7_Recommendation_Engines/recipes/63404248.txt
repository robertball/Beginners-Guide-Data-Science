Title:
Anne's Calabacita's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ onion chopped
1 bell pepper chopped
1 yellow or red pepper chopped
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 large can Ortega Green Chiles chopped
3 different squash (zucchini, yellow, patty pan  and/or summer squash) cut into1/2 inch squares
1 ½ cup frozen (or fresh cut off the ear) white corn
Dash of oregano
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
2/3 cup crumbled Cojita cheese
½ cup sour cream
1 cup minced cilantro
Salt and pepper to taste
Optional:
1 large tomato chopped and drained
1 can whole pinto beans drained
1 cup chopped spinach

Directions:
In large skillet, heat olive oil and saute the onions and bell peppers over medium heat until tranparent and golden brown, then add the chopped green chiles. Add the prepared squashes and cook until soft. Stir in corn (and tomatoes, if desired) and simmer until the liquid has cooked down. Salt and pepper to taste and add a dash of oregano.
If you want to add drained whole pinto beans or chopped spinach, do so here and simmer until heated through and then add the remaining ingredients, the cheddar and Cojita cheeses, sour cream and minced cilantro.
Serve immediately and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Dylan's Nana
Description: 
Even kids will love this recipe. I learned it from my wonderful mother-in-law who would add or subtract different ingredients everytime. This version was my favorite!
