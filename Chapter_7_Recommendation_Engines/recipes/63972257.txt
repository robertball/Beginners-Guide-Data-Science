Title:
PEANUT BUTTER-NUTELLA PIE COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 CUP PEANUT BUTTER
1/2 CUP NUTELLA
1/4 CUP HONEY
1/4 CUP AGAVE SYRUP
8 OUNCES LIGHT CREAM CHEESE
1 EGG
1 TEASPOON VANILLA
1/2 TEASPOON BAKING SODA
1/4 TEASPOON SEA SALT

Directions:
BLEND ALL INGREDIENTS THOROUGHLY.
DROP BY TABLESPOON ON PARCHMENT LINED BAKING SHEET.
BAKE AT 350 DEGREES FOR 12 MINUTES.
ALLOW TO COOL ON BAKING SHEET FOR 3 MINUTES BEFORE TRANSFERRING TO COOLING RACK TO COOL COMPLETELY.
