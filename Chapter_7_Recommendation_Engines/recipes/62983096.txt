Title:
Quick & Fresh Cranberry-Orange Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole orange, skin washed and orange quartered and seeded
1 package (12 oz) fresh cranberries, washed
1/2 c chopped walnuts or nuts of choice (to taste)
Sugar to taste-about 1/2 to 1 cup (we prefer 1/2 cup)

Directions:
Place washed, quartered orange the food processor and pulse to a coarse chop. Add washed, drained cranberries and pulse to a coarse chop-about five seconds.  Add chopped nuts.  Turn into a bowl and stir in sugar to taste. Refrigerate several hours or overnight.  Makes 2 1/2 cups.  May be frozen for several weeks.

Submitter:

Description: 
My mother's fresh cranberry-orange relish, updated for the food processor.
