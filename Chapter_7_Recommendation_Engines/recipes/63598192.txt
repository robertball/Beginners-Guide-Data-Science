Title:
Cherry Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14-0z cans sweetened condensed milk
1 c light brown sugar
2 8-0z jars maraschino cherries, chopped, including juice.
7 cups milk
1 1/2-2 cups walnuts, chopped (opt)
1 1/2-2 cups mini chocolate chips (opt)

Directions:
Mix sweetened condensed milk and brown sugar in mixer.  Add maraschino cherries and juice.  Mix well.  Pour into freezer can.  Fill to the freezer fill line with milk-about 7 cups.  Stir well.  If using walnuts and/or chocolate chips, add at this time. Freeze according to manufacturer's directions.  Makes about one gallon.
