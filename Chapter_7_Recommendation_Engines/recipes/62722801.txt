Title:
Oriental Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
***Salad***
1 # shredded cabbage for coleslaw
2 pkg. Ramen uncooked noodles
3 oz. slivered almonds
1/3 C sunflower nuts
1 sm. white or purple onion
***Dressing***
1/2 C sugar
1/2 C oil
1/3 C cider vinegar
Both flavor packages from Ramen noodles

Directions:
Mix salad ingred together. Set aside.
Mix dressing ingred together and chill.
Mix together salad and dressing. Chill and serve

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
No meat in this salad. Quick to go together
