Title:
Parmesen Cod Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Topping:
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
8 oz. sour cream
1/4 C dill
juice of 1 lemon
1 Tbsp. seasoning mix
Crust:
2 C Panko
2 C parmesen, grated
1/2 C butter, melted
Cod:
1ea 5 lb. block cod, thawed

Directions:
Topping:
Whip all ingredients together until smooth.  Set aside.
Crust:
Mix all ingredients together in a small steel bowl.  Set aside.
Cod:
Cut the cod into 2-2 1/2 oz. pieces, about 35 pieces total.  Lay on sheet pan lined with parchment paper.
Place a dollop of topping ix on each piece of fish and then cover with crust.  Place in oven on low fan at 350 degrees F. for 10-15 minutes or until crust has browned.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
Moist and tender on the inside, and crispy on the outside!
