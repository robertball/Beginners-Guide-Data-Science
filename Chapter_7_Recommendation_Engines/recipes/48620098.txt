Title:
Yagaman's South Texas Carne Guisada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6T Vegetable oil
4lbs. meat (you pick it) cubed
4T all purpose flour
1 bell pepper (color doesn't matter) finely chopped
1 yellow onion diced
2 large tomatoes chopped
2 fresh jalepenos minced
7 cloves minced garlic
1t ground cumin
2t chilli powder
S&P
Beef Stock as neded
*1 1/2 lbs. Diced potatoes if desired

Directions:
Heat the oil in a big pot or dutch oven.  Add the cubed meat and saute until lightly browned, 8-10 minutes.  Sprinkle with flour then add bell pepper, onion, and tomato.  Stir well and then mix in the jalepeno, garlic, cumin, chili powder, S&P, and Beef Stock as needed.  Turn the heat down low and simmer for 2-21/2 hours. Check periodically to make sure the meat ain't sticking.  Add stock if needed.  *If adding potatoes wait until last hour of cooking.
