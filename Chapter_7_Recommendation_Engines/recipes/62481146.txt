Title:
Gooey Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten
1 cup raisins
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup butter
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 cups rolled oats
1 cup chopped pecans
1-2 tablespoons honey

Directions:
Combine eggs, vanilla, raisins and honey in a small bowl;  cover and let stand for 1 hour.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a large bowl, cream the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar together.  Sift together the flour, cinnamon, and baking soda;  stir into the creamed mixture.  Then stir the raisin mixture, rolled oats, and nuts.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto an unprepared cookie sheet.  Bake for 10 minutes, or until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Christal Kuchera
Description: 
The best oatmeal raisin cookies I have ever had!  Mmmm!  The secret is the honey and soaking.
