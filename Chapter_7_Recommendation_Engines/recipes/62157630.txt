Title:
Old Bay Seasoning Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. ground bay leaves
2-1/2 tsp. celery salt
1-1/2 tsp. dry mustard
1 tsp. ground black pepper
1/2 tsp. ground white pepper
1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp. ground cloves
1/4 tsp. ground allspice
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
1 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 tsp. ground mace
1/4 tsp. ground cardamom

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well and store in an airtight container.  This recipe will yield 1/4 cup of Old Bay Seasoning Mix.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Pota
Description: 
A seasoning mixture used on seafood and chicken.  If you don't have it on hand this is the perfect recipe to make your own at the last minute or to just tuck away in your spice cabinet.
