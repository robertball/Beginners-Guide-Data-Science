Title:
Paleo Turkey-Cashew Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/580381.jpg
Ingredients:
1.25 lbs. ground turkey (not just breast meat)
3 tbsp olive oil
1 white onion, chopped
2 tbs minced garlic
1 tsp sofrito (homemade is best)
1 can San Marzano tomatoes
1 can tomato paste
1 cup chopped unsalted cashews
2 cups water
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp sea salt
2 -3 tsp Chili powder
1 tsp Cumin
1 tsp unsweeted cocoa powder

Directions:
Heat oil in heavy-bottomed saucepot (make sure there is a lid available)
Add onion, saute until fragrant
Add sofrito and garlic, saute about 3 min
Move onion mixture to one side
Add turkey, brown well.  Keep lid on to keep moisture in.
Add can of tomatoes, and 3/4 of the tomato paste.  Add 1 cup water.
Mix well.  Add cashews, chili powder, cumin, cocoa powder.
Let simmer on low-med heat for at least 1 hour.   Keep the lid on and stir frequently.
Add water as needed (chili will cook down and become thick)
Salt and pepper to taste
Serve over Cilantro-Lime Cauliflower Rice.
