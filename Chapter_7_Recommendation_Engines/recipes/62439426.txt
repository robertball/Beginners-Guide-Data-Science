Title:
Grandma Clark's Fried  Mush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup yellow cornmeal
1 tablespoon butter
1 cup milk

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, heat water to boiling. Reduce heat to medium; stir in salt and cornmeal. Cook, stirring regularly, until mixture is thick.
Spoon cornmeal mixture into a lightly greased 9x5 inch loaf pan. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
In the morning, peanut oil in a skillet over medium high heat. Slice cornmeal mush into 1 inch wide slices. Cook in peanut oil until golden brown on both sides.

Submitter: 
Chuck Clark
Description:
