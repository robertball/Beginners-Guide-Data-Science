Title:
Triple Chocolate Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/3 cup unsweetened Ramstadt-Breda Dark cocoa powder
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 cup white chocolate chips
Frosting:
3 tablespoons butter, softened
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 tablespoon honey
1 cup confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
Line an 8-inch square pan with parchment paper and spray with non-stick spray.
In a microwave safe bowl, microwave 1/2 cup butter for about 45 seconds until melted. Stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup dark cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Fold in semi-sweet chocolate chips and white chocolate chips. Spread batter into prepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook. Edges should not be crunchy
To Make Frosting:  Combine 3 tablespoons softened butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.
