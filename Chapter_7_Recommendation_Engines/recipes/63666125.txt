Title:
Feta Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018449.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs red potatoes, well scrubbed, with skins
1/2 tsp salt
6 cloves of garlic, peeled with thick end removed
1 tsp olive oil
6 to 8 oz Greek Fat free yogurt
4 oz crumbled Feta cheese
1 tbs butter

Directions:
Cut each potato into 6 equal pieces and place in a pot with water to cover.
Add salt and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer until tender about 15 minutes.
Meanwhile place the garlic cloves in a small glass dish and drizzle with oil.
Microwave on high for 1 1/2 to 2 minutes until fragrant and tender..  Set aside.
When potatoes are ready, drain and place back over low heat for a moment to dry them.
Put through a ricer or mash thoroughly.
Add the garlic and 6 oz of yogurt and mix.  Add more yogurt if they are too stiff..
Stir through the feta cheese.
Serve topped with butter.

Submitter: 
BigShotsMom
Description: 
This is an easy way to give mashed potatoes a new spin.  Leaving the skin on saves time and there are a lot of nutrients in the skin.
