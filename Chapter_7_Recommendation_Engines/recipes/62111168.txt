Title:
Tasty Tuna Dip for Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 oz can white albacore tuna, drained and flaked
3 Tablespoons reduced fat mayonnaise
1 small onion, chopped
1 cup reduced fat sour cream
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp cayenne powder
4 oz reduced fat cream cheese, softened

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients together until blended.  Chill for several hours to allow flavors to meld.  Serve with vegetable dippers.

Submitter: 
Dianemwj
Description: 
This is a deliciously different dip for vegetables.  I used to take it to work for potlucks and everyone wanted the recipe.  Serve with radishes, carrot sticks, scallions, celery or any other raw vegetable you like.
