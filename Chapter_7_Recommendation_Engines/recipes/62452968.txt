Title:
Fruit Lax Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg dates
2 cups raisins
2 cups bran
1 cup molasses
1 can apple sauce
2 oranges
2 cups prune juice

Directions:
Put first three ingredients into saucepan and cover with water.
Boil together till soft
Add remaining ingredients & blend together
Keep in fridge
