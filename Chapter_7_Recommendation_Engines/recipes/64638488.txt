Title:
Roast Chicken a la Slowleigh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken (3 lbs)
3-4 cups of cold water
2 Bay leaves
2 Naval oranges, sliced
1/3 cup brown sugar
Ground salt & pepper to taste (in brine & on chicken after brine)
3 tablespoons butter
1 large freezer bag (brine)

Directions:
1. Remove chicken from packaging, remove any string/giblets, rinse under cold water and place into large freezer bag
2. Add brown sugar, bay leaves, orange slices, salt and pepper to freezer bag, fill with cold water (approx. 3-4 cups).  Seal bag and place in refrigerator.  Minimum 6 hours, overnight ideal.
3.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees, spray roasting pan & rack with kitchen spray (optional) for easier clean up
4. Remove chicken from  freezer bag, discard water but keep the orange slices and bay leaves
5. Rinse chicken under cold water, and pat dry, inside and out.
6. Liberally apply butter to chicken, both inside and out, making sure to rub butter under skin as well.
7. Place slices of orange under skin on breast and legs of chicken,  place remaining slices inside cavity of chicken with the bay leaves.
8. Grind salt and pepper over skin of chicken, place on rack in the roasting pan.
9. Roast chicken for 1.5 hours, or until a meat thermometer reads 180 degrees
10. Remove chicken from roast pan to cutting board, tent with tin foil and let sit for 20-30 minutes.
11.  Cut as appropriate and serve with roasted potatoes and vegetable assortment or salad.
