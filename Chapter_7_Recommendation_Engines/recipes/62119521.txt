Title:
Low Fat Creamy Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TB butter
1 TB olive oil
1 medium onion, chpped
10 oz pkg sliced baby portobello mushrooms
1 medium carrot, shredded
1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt, if desired
1/4 tsp pepper
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup skim milk
1/2 cup fat free half n half
1/2 cup uncooked wild rice
4 oz fat free cream cheese, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
curry powder

Directions:
In large pot over medium heat, add butter and olive oil.  Saute onions and carrots 4 minutes.  Add mushrooms and saute until just tender - add a splash of water if needed.  Stir in flour, salt and pepper.  Whisk in broth.  Heat to a boil stirring constantly.  Stir in milk, cream and wild rice.  Turn to low and simmer 40 minutes, until rice is tender.
Turn up to medium heat and add cream cheese and stir until melted and soup is heated through.
Garnish with a dash of curry powder on each bowl.

Submitter: 
WebsByMegan
Description: 
I have been making this soup for years and thought I would share.  It's very thick, add more broth if you want to thin it out. It's amazing how well fat free ingredients work in this recipe.  Even my 10 year old loves it!
