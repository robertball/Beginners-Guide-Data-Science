Title:
Chicken Lojano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken, cut up
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup red wine
2 cloves garlic, cut into thin strips
1 1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 medium onions, quartered
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
1 tablespoon salt
2 medium tomatoes, quartered
3 tablespoons chopped fresh chives

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a saute pan and brown chicken pieces on all sides. Place chicken in a greased 9X13 casserole dish. Puree remaining ingredients, except for  the chives, in a food processor, then pour over chicken. Bake for 1 hour. Garnish with chopped chives and serve.

Submitter: 
Laurel Patton

This recipe is from the Loja Province of Ecuador.
