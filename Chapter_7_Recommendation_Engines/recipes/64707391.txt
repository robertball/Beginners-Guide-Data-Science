Title:
Rhubarb-Walnut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
3/4 cups brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 egg
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup chopped rhubarb
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
Topping:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven 325 degrees F.
Prepare muffin pan by greasing pan or adding paper muffin cups
Combine dry ingredients.
Add wet ingredients, mixing only until moist.
Fold in rhubarb and walnuts and place batter in prepared muffin pan.
Combine topping ingredients and sprinkle over muffins.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.

Submitter:
