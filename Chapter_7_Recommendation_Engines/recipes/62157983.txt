Title:
Caramelized Onion Tartlets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large sweet onion (such as Vidalia)
½ stick butter
1/4 cup red wine
1/6 cup sugar
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella (4-ounces)
salt, pepper, cayenne pepper - flavor to taste
1 package ready-to-use refrigerated pie crust

Directions:
Slice and chop 1 large sweet onion (such as Vidalia), add ½ stick butter and enough red wine to cover bottom of pan and sprinkle with 1/6 cup sugar, cook until onions are tender and translucent.
Combine 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream, 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella (4-ounces), flavor with salt and pepper and cayenne pepper.
Once onions are ready, stir into the mixture.
1 package ready-to-use refrigerated pie crust – cut with a cookie cutter into shapes (or circles) and put at the bottom of a muffin tin (or mini muffin).
Spoon the mixture on top of the pie crust pieces. Bake at 375 for 20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool for a few minutes and pop the tartlets out.
