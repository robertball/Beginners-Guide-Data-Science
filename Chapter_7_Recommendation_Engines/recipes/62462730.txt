Title:
Hot dog chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground meat
T. vinegar
1 c. ketchup
T. chili powder
2 T. mustard
T. sugar
salt & pepper to taste
water

Directions:
Cook meat in 1/4 c. water until brown. Drain
Mix all ingredients.
