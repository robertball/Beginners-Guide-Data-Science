Title:
Spicy Unstuffed Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Hot or spicy pork sausage in a tube.
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
28 oz. can diced tomaotes
6 oz. tomato paste
2 tbsp. vinegar
1/4-1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
2 tsp. chili powder
1/2 tsp. salt
10 cups shredded cabbage

Directions:
In a large kettle or soup pot, brown the sausage, ground beef and onion.  DRAIN WELL.
Add the remaining ingredients, except the cabbage and mix well.
Stir in the cabbage and simmer 20-25 minutes or until the cabbage is tender, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Valerie Kasper
Description: 
This is a huge hit at potluck suppers. You can adjust the spiciness to your own palate. I also think it just gets better the next day!
