Title:
Cherry Cheesecake Candies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds confectioners'(powdered)sugar
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
pinch of unsweetened cherry kool aid packet
1/2 c granulated sugar

Directions:
Soften cream cheese by letting it get to room temp. Cream together cream cheese and confectioner's sugar adding a small pinch of the cherry kool aid. Roll into small balls, roll balls in granulated sugar, then press into circles by using fork tines or press into candy mold. Let air dry dry for several hours. Refrigerate or freeze.

Submitter: 
Petunia Applebee
Description: 
Family favorite for many years. These make wonderful quick and tasty candies for parties or the holidays.
