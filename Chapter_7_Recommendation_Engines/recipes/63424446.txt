Title:
Turkey Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Turkey carcass and bones
1/2 of bunch of Celery, chopped
1 bag Carrots, shredded
1/2-1 bag Onion, frozen chopped
Corn (optional)
Green beans (optional)
Garlic (4 cloves or 4 tsp.)
2 Tbs. kosher salt
2 tsp. each sage and thyme
1 bay leaf
1 1/2 cups Wild Rice

Directions:
Day 1
Put turkey carcass and bones in large stock pot. Cover with water.
Bring to a boil and cook for one hour.
Pull out carcass and bones and set aside in a pan to cool.
Strain the broth (need a second large pot to do this).
Put strained broth in fridge to cool overnight.
When bones have cooled, pick off remaining meat, cut into bite size pieces and store in fridge.
Day 2
Skim fat from broth.
Put broth on stove to boil and add chicken, veggies and seasonings.
Bring to a boil.
Add wild rice and simmer for about an hour.

Submitter: 
KimmerG40
Description: 
Make with bones from Thanksgiving turkey. Great way to use the whole bird, and also freezes well to pull out when someone is sick.
