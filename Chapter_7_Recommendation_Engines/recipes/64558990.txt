Title:
Hibiscus Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup hibiscus flowers
2 cups water
½ cup sugar
¼ cup brown sugar
½-inch knob of ginger root, thinly sliced
Peel of 1 lemon

Directions:
Bring hibiscus flowers, water, sugar, brown sugar, ginger and lemon peel to a boil, then turn down heat to a simmer for 10 minutes.
Steep mixture off the heat for 5 minutes.
Strain through a fine mesh strainer, pressing mixture to extract every last drop of flavor. Discard solids and save syrup in refrigerator for 2 weeks.

Submitter: 
Matt Wencl
Description: 
Keep this sweet, tart and floral syrup on hand for adding extra zing and beautiful ruby red color to margaritas, rum punches and daiquiris. Or just pour over ice and add club soda for a delicious fresh soda.
