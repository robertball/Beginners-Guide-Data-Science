Title:
Buffalo Chicken Enchiladas with Creamy Ranch Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (10.75 oz) condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup refrigerated sour cream ranch di
2/3 cup chopped green onion
3 cups chopped cooked chicken
3/4 cup buffalo sauce
1 package (10.5 oz) Old El Paso flour tortillas for soft tacos & fajitas (6-inch tortillas)
3 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1) heat oven to 350*. Spray 13x9 glass baking dsh with cooking spray. in a medium bowl, mix soup, dip and 1/3 cup of the onions. In large bowl, mix chicken and buffalo sauce until coated.
2) Spoon 2 tablespoons soup mixture down center of each tortilla; set remaining mixture aside. Reserve 1/2 cup cheese for carnish. Top each tortilla with about 1/4 cup chicken mixture and scant 1/4 cup cheese. Fold sides of tortillas over filling; place seam side down in baking dishh. spoon remaining soup mixture over filled tortillas.
3) Cover dish tightly with foil. Bake 40-45 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
4) Remove from oven. Sprinkle with reserved 1/2 cup cheese and remaining 1/3 cup onions. Bake uncovered about 5 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
Note: to increase the heat, add 1 tsp red pepper sauce to the buffalo wing sauce.
