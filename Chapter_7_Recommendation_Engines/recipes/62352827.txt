Title:
Chicken Drumsticks/Thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 chicken leg pieces
olive oil
S/P to taste
Mix & Match:
herbes de provance
sunny paris
lemon pepper

Directions:
Wash and dry chicken; arrange in a baking dish so the chicken isn't touching
Pour a little olive oil over chicken
Season with S/P and any other desired seasonings.  Mix everything together so chicken is well covered with seasonings.
Cover with foil and bake at 350F for 40 minutes
Uncover, increase temp to 400F and bake 15 min, and then another 15 min or so on 450F until chicken is golden, brown and delicious.
