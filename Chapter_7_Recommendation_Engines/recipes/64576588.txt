Title:
Red Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large red cabbage, cored, thinly sliced
1 1/2 C beef stock (**)
1/2 C DARK brown sugar, packed
1/2 C cider vinegar
4 Tbls butter
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
1.	Combine all ingredients in a 10-12 quart stainless steel soup pot.
2.	Bring to a boil, stir, cover, and simmer over low heat, stirring occasionally--about 1 hour.
3.	Salt to taste.
4.	NOTE ** replace with water and two beef bouillon cubes.
DON'T Over Cook - you want some SNAP/Tooth to the cabbage
