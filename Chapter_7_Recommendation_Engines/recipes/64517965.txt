Title:
Thanksgiving Leftover Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2851281.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 c. shredded turkey or chicken
Â½ - 3/4c. barbecue sauce
1 lb. leftover vegetables
3-4 c. mashed potatoes
1 c. shredded cheese
Optional- cranberry sauce and/or stuffing

Directions:
1.  Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
2.  Grease a rectangular baking dish. Then, cover the bottom of the dish with shredded turkey. Pour barbecue sauce over the turkey and stir with a fork or spoon.
3.  Next, place a layer of leftover vegetables over the turkey. If desired, layer cranberry sauce and stuffing. Finally, pour a layer of mashed potatoes and sprinkle with shredded cheese.
4.  Bake for 25-30 minutes.
