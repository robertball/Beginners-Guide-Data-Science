Title:
Sweet 'n Simple 'Powdered Sugar Glaze' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/4 cup cornstarch
5 teaspoons water

Directions:
Place SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener and cornstarch in blender jar.  Cover and blend until SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener is a very fine powder.  Pour into a small bowl.  Add water and stir well.  This recipe makes a thick glaze.  Add more water for a thinner glaze.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
This cookie glaze is so simple and versatile, it's sure to become your 'old stand-by.'
