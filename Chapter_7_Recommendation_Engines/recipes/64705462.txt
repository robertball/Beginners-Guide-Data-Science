Title:
Debaucherry Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5470438.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz White Chocolate Chips
1/4 cup heaving whipping cream (or soy based creamer)
Vanilla

Directions:
Fill your fondue pot with one 12 oz bag of white chocolate chips
Turn your fondue pot on medium and stir in 1/4 cup heaving whipping cream (or soy based creamer) and a teaspoon of vanilla
Stir continuously for 5 minutes until silky smooth
Turn fondue pot down to warm setting
Serve with cherries

Submitter: 
Rachael
Description: 
Cherries popped and savored in a sweet silky white chocolate.
