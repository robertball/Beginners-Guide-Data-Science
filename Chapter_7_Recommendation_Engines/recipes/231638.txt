Title:
Thick and Creamy Banana Yogurt Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/1060379.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup plain yogurt, or more to taste
1  banana, sliced and frozen
3 tablespoons honey
2 tablespoons orange juice
1 tablespoon almond butter

Directions:
Blend yogurt, bananas, honey, orange juice, and almond butter in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Jill Jakubiszen
Description: 
This smoothie is healthy, delicious, and filling. Use peanut butter in place of almond butter if you don't have the almond butter.
