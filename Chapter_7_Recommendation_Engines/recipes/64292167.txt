Title:
Melt - In - Your - Mouth Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1/4 cup cornstarch
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
can add
1 tsp almond extract, 2 tsp vanilla extract, 1 T packed brown sugar.

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Whip butter with an electric mixer until fluffy.  Stir in the confectioners' sugar, cornstarch, and flour. Beat on low for one minute, then on high for 3 to 4 minutes. Drop cookies by spoonfuls 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake for 12 to 15  minutes in the preheated oven.  Watch that the edges don't brown too much. Cool on wire racks.

Submitter: 
Jacque
Description: 
This quick and easy shortbread will literally melt when you take a bite. Great for Christmas parties with a little bit of decorating.Modified from: Melt - In - Your - Mouth Shortbread
