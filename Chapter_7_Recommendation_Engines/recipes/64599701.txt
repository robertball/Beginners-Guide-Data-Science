Title:
Pumpkin Pie Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 of an 8" pumpkin pie
6 scoops vanilla ice cream
2 tablespoons bourbon (optional)
pinch of pumpkin pie spice + more for garnish
1 cup whole milk
whipped cream

Directions:
Put entire 1/4 slice of pie into blender.
In the following order, add ice cream, bourbon, pumpkin pie spice and milk.
Blend until combined.
Pour into two tall milkshake glasses.
Top with whipped cream and garnish with a sprinkle of pumpkin pie spice, a straw and a small sliver of pumpkin pie.

Submitter: 
Matt Wencl
Description: 
This outrageously delicious pieshake combines your Thanksgiving favorite, pumpkin pie, with plenty of vanilla ice cream,  ice cold milk and a shot of bourbon to become a tall glass of pumpkin spice heaven. Top this crazy shake with too much whipped cream and a small sliver of pumpkin pie, for dunking!
