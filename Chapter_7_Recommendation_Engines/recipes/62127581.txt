Title:
NP's Spicy Thai Basil Chicken & Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp fish sauce
4 tbsp chili & garlic sauce
2 tbsp oil
1 lb of chicken, cut into bite size pieces
1 large carrot, thinly sliced
1 green bell pepper, sliced into strips
1 red bell pepper, sliced into strips
1 can bamboo shoots, drained
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 1/2 cups fresh basil, coursely cut
1 cup snow peas, whole
1 cup green beans, cut into 2 inch pieces
1 tsp red pepper flakes
salt to taste

Directions:
Mix oil, chili & garlic sauce, and fish sauce in medium skillet over medium-high heat. Cook for 1 minute making sure not to burn the sauce.
Stir in basil, chicken, bamboo shoot, and vegetables into the skillet and cover.
Cook covered over medium heat for 10 minutes, or until chicken is cooked thoroughly.
Add red pepper flakes and salt to taste. Stir in.
Once chicken is cooked, reduce heat to low and let simmer for 5 minutes.
Serve with brown rice for a healthy and filling meal.
