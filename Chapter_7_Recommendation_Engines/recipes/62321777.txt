Title:
Jim's Mediterranean Seafood Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/378856.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups dried small pasta shells
1 16 oz can of crab or lobster meat
1 8 0z can of baby shrimp
2 stalks celery, finely chopped
1 12 oz can black olives (cut in half)
1 1/2 cups miracle whip ( mayonnaise can be used too)
1/3 cup Catalina salad dressing
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoon hot sauce
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain, and place pasta in a large bowl. Stir in crabmeat,shrimp, celery, and olives. Mix in miracle whip, Catalina dressing,cayenne pepper, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, and Dijon. Stir in Cheddar cheese, cover, and chill at least 1 hour.

Description: 
Shell pasta, real crab (or lobster meat),baby shrimp and black olives are blended with a unique dressing of Dijon mustard,cayenne pepper, Worcestershire, hot sauce, miracle whip, and Catalina dressing. Shredded Cheddar cheese completes it. It has a little kick to it!Modified from: Mediterranean Seafood Salad
