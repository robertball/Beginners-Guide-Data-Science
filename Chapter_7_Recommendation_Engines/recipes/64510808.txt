Title:
Microwave Chocolate Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. butter, melted
1 cup flour
3/4 cup sugar
2 tbsp. cocoa
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
Sauce:
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup cocoa
1 1/4 cup boiling water

Directions:
Mix flour, 1st sugar, 1st cocoa, baking powder and salt.
Stir in milk, butter, vanilla & nuts.
Pour into 8" round baking dish.
Mix sauce ingredients. Pour over batter. Do not stir.
Cover with paper towel.
Cook on high in microwave x 9 - 10 minutes.
Remove from microwave and let stand 10 minutes.
Serve with ice cream, milk or whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A quick rich chocolate cake with a fudgy sauce
