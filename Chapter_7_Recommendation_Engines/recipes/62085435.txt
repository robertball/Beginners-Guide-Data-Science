Title:
Pecan-Crusted Red Snapper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 red snapper fillets, (8 ozs. each)
1/2 cup pecan pieces
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees.
Prepare the red snapper with salt and pepper to taste, then coat the fillets with pecans.
Bake for approximately ten minutes.
Makes 4 servings.
