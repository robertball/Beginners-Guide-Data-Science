Title:
Chocolate-Banana Chia Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups vanilla flavored Flax Milk.
1 large ripe Banana, cut into chunks.
7 table spoons Chia Seeds.
3 tablespoons local Honey.
1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
1/8 teaspoon Sea Salt

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a blender in listed order.  Blend until smooth. Pour the pur√©ed mixture into a bowl. Refrigerate for at least two hours to thicken. Spoon into small bowls for serving.
