Title:
Good Eats Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups crushed croutons or breadcrumbs with garlic to season
1/2 tsp each black and cayenne pepper
1 tsp each chili powder and dried thyme
1/2 onion
1 carrot broken up
3 cloves garlic
1/2 red pepper
18 oz hamburger meat
1 1/2  tsp salt
1 egg
Sauce:
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 Tbsp each cumin and chili powder
shot each Worcestershire  and Tabasco
1 Tbsp honey

Directions:
mix breadcrumbs pepper chili powder and thyme in a food processor, add onion, carrot, garlic and red pepper  and mix until crumbly (not powdered) .
add to meat, salt, and egg . Press  into a bread pan then turn meat out to parchment paper on a baking pan.
Cook 15 mins at 325 degrees then spread on  sauce and cook for 1 hour 20 minutes
