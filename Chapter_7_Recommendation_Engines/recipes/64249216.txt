Title:
Delicious Ham and Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# diced ham
4 cups Low Sodium Chicken Broth
3 1/2 cups washed and diced potatoes
1/3 cup diced celery
1/3 cup finely chopped onion
1 teaspoon ground white or black pepper, or to taste
1/2 t dried thyme
5 tablespoons butter
5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups milk

Directions:
put ham in crock pot. Add potatoes, celery and onion to ham and chicken broth in crockpot.Stir in thyme and pepper. Cook on low until potatoes are tender.  Add instant mashed potato flakes to thicken it up a bit.
In a separate saucepan, melt butter over medium-low heat. Whisk in flour with a fork, and cook, stirring constantly until thick, about 1 minute. Slowly stir in milk as not to allow lumps to form until all of the milk has been added. Continue cooking on high until thick, Add 1/2 cup shredded cheese.
Stir the milk mixture into the stockpot, and cook soup until heated through. Serve immediately.
