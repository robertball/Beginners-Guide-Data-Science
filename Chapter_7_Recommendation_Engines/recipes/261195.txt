Title:
Orange Panties (with Spiders) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4060244.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
1 cup whipped cream
1/2 cup Champagne
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers citron liqueur
1/2 teaspoon orange food coloring
2 plastic spiders
1 tablespoon whipped cream
black sprinkles

Directions:
Combine ice cubes, orange juice concentrate, whipped cream, Champagne, citron liqueur, and orange food coloring in a blender; blend until smooth.
Pour into glasses and garnish with plastic spiders, 1 tablespoon whipped cream, and black sprinkles.

Submitter: 
Rosalind Brazel
Description: 
This rip on the popular pink panties drink is fun, fizzy, and packs a punch. Serve this Champagne drink at your adult Halloween party or at any fall brunch.
