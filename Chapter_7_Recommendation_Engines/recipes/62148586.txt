Title:
Meat Pillows Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (2 oz.) packages of Buddig Beef
1 (4.25oz.) canned chopped black olives
2 (8 oz.) packages of cream cheese (softened)
1 tsp. garlic powder (or to taste)

Directions:
Mix softened cream cheese, chopped olives, garlic powder. Place a tablespoon of mixture into a beef slice and fold up like a pillow. Fold sides in, then top and bottom. To make it hold, place a tiny amount of cream cheese as glue.
