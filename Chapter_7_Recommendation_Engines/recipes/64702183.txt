Title:
Bacon Tuscan Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz     Bacon, Chopped
1 – 12 oz Bag     Brussels Sprouts (Shaved)
2 Tbsp.     Tuscan Seasoned Olive Oil (If you can’t find it add 1tsp Garlic and 1tsp Italian Herbs to Olive Oil)
1 tsp     Salt
1 tsp     Pepper

Directions:
Cook bacon on medium heat until cooked through but not yet crispy, reserve bacon fat.
In a large bowl, toss sprouts, olive oil blend, salt, pepper, and bacon (including rendered fat) until the sprouts are evenly covered.
Spread evenly on a cookie sheet
Roast at 400*F for 25-30 minutes or until sprouts start to lightly brown.
Serve immediately
