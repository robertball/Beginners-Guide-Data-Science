Title:
Baked Cod Fish with Boursin Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2-2 lbs fresh Cod Fish
2 Tbsp. butter, melted
1 small can chopped tomatoes with garlic, onion and peppers
1 pkg. Boursin heab cheese or Alouette cheese with herbs
Salt
Pepper
Parmesan cheese for topping

Directions:
Preheat oven 400.
Place one tablespoon melted butter and the fish in a casserole or baking dish.
Pat the fish with paper towel to absorb excess moisture then spread the Boursin or Allouette Herb cheese over the top of the Cod fish.
Pour the canned tomatoes over and then sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Top with 1-2 tablespoons of grated Parmesan Cheese, drizzle the remaining butter over the fish and bake uncovered for 25-30 minutes. May substitute Olive oil for the butter.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Use Boursin or Allouette Cheese and canned tomatoes packed with onion, peppers and garlic. Outstanding fish recipe for a quick and easy to prepare dinner.  Add a toss salad, a green vegetable side dish and the dinner is complete.
