Title:
Lori's 15 Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of Hurst 15 bean  (dry bag )
1 Polish Kielbasa sausage link, cut into bite size pieces
1 can of stewed tomatoes (with garlic, basil)
Water

Directions:
Rinse beans and soak in enough water to cover them for at least 24 hours before cooking.  Rinse the beans after soaking and place in crockpot with 6 cups of water.  Add seasoning packet, sausage, and tomatoes.  Stir enough to mix.  Cook on high for 8-10 hours, adding more water if necessary.

Submitter: 
Kisha McDaniel Logsdon
Description: 
This could be made in many variations.
