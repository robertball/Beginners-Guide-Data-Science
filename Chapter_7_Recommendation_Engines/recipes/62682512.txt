Title:
egg for sushi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
5 T dashi
1/2 t salt
2/3 t soy
2/3 t sugar

Directions:
Add all, gently beat, do not foam.
Lightly oil pan and cook very thin layer, rolling up for shape of sushi
