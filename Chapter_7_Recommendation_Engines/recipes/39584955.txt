Title:
Spicy Cabbage With Ground Beef Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
1/2 bell pepper, chopped
6 t garlic, chopped
1 t salt
1/2 t pepper
1/2 t oregano
1- 18oz can tomatoes w/green chilies
1-8oz can tomato sauce
1/2 C raw rice
1-2# cabbage, shredded
1 C grated American or cheddar cheese

Directions:
Fry together first 7 ingred. after mixing them well together. Fry in sm. amount of light oil. When completed, remove from fire and set aside
Mix tomatoes, sauce and rice together thoroughly in bowl and set aside. Mix together all fried ingred and tomatoes and rice mixture real well
In buttered 9 x 13 pan, layer 1/2 meat mixture, 1/2 cabbage and 1/2 cheese. Repeat layer, ending with cheese. Cover pan with aluminum foil. Bake 1 1/2-2 hrs at 350
