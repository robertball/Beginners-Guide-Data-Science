Title:
Tropsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/849238.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups low-fat vanilla yogurt
1 cup sliced mango
1 (8 ounce) can pineapple chunks, undrained
1 small banana
1 orange, juiced

Directions:
Blend yogurt, mango, pineapple chunks with juice, banana, and orange juice in a blender until smooth. Pour into ice pop molds; freeze until set, 4 to 6 hours.

Submitter: 
KJR
Description: 
My 19-month-old son loves yogurt and squishy fruit but they're soooo messy. This way he can get both in a cool treat with no unnecessary sugar, and I can just hose him off afterward!
