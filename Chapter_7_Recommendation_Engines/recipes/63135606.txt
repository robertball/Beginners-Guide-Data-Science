Title:
Toilet Bowl Cleaner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Baking Soda
10 Drops tea-tree essential oil
1/2 cup distilled vinegar

Directions:
Pour baking powder directly into the toilet bowl, add oil then vinegar (in that order).  let water effervesce for several minutes, then scrub the bowl with a toilet brush.  Flush.

Submitter: 
TheBritishBaker
Description: 
This recipe harnesses baking soda's gentle abrasiveness vinegar's acidity and the stain lifting powder of the fizz created by mixing the tow, plus tea tree oils antibacterial capabilities.  Costs approx $0.48 (enough for 1 use)
