Title:
ricotta cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 1/2 c whole milk
1 1/2 c heavy cream
1 t salt
1 T fresh lemon juice
2 1/2 T vinegar

Directions:
1. Line a sieve with cheese cloth and set it over a large bowl.
2. Bring milk, cream, and salt to simmer in a large pot. Stirring occasionally.
3. Add lemon juice and vinegar, stirring until curdled, about 1 min.
4. Pour through sieve and let drain until cheese it the correct thickness.
Store in an airtight container up to 4 days.
165*F
