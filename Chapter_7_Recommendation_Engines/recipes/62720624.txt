Title:
Stir-fried Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teas sesame seeds
2 pounds asparagus, cut in 1 inch pieces
2 Tbls peanut oil
2tsp grated fresh ginger
1/2 teas salt
1 tsp sesame oil

Directions:
Plunge asparagus in boiling salted water for 2-3 min. Drain and plunge into a bowl of ice water. When asparagus is cool drain and pat dry.
Toast sesame seeds in dry skillet till light brown. Put in a dish to cool. Pour peanut oil in the skillet. Saute asparagus, ginger and salt for about five minutes.
Add sesame oil and seeds and serve
