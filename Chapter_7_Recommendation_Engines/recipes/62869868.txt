Title:
Apple Pie Moonshine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon apple cider
1 gallon apple juice
2 cups sugar
4 cinnamon sticks
1 quart White Lightnin'

Directions:
Boil first 3 ingredients until sugar dissolves.
Remove from heat
Add jar of White Lightnin'
Store in quart jars.

Submitter: 
Patricia Canerdy
Description: 
Good old White Lightnin' spiced up.  Makes a wonderful hot sipping drink.
