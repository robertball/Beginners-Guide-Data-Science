Title:
Mussaman Beef Curry-Gaeng Mussaman Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c coconut cream
3 T mussaman curry paste
1 t oil
1 lb beef sirloin
2 c coconut milk
5 cardamom seeds-dry roasted until fragrant
1 cinnamon stick
2 potatoes
2 T chopped peanuts
5-10 green onions
3 bay leaves
2 T chopped palm sugar
2 T fish sauce
3 T tamarid juice
Sweet and Spicy pickled Vegetables
1 c vinegar
1/2 c sugar
3-5 chilies
3-5 green onion
2 T finely sliced cauliflower
2 T baby corn or cabbage
2 T sliced cucumber
1 T grated ginger

Directions:
Heat coconut milk and curry paste for 5 min. Turn off heat and set aside.
Stir-fry beef 8-10 min, add coconut and bring to a boil, reduce and simmer for 10 min.
Add remaining ingredients and cook until potatoes are tender.
Serve with rice and pickled veggies.
To pickle veggies, boil sugar and vinegar, set aside to cool. Add remaining and set aside.
