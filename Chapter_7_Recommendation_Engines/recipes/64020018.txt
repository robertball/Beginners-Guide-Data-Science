Title:
Corn Bread Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup melted butter
1 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 cup chopped corn
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese or 1 cup cottage cheese
1 cup white flour
1 cup corn meal
1/8 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees, lightly spay baking tins. Mix together wet ingredients in bowl, then add dry ingredients and mix just until moistened. Bake 15-18 minutes. Let cool 1 minute before removing from pan.
