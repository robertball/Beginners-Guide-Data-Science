Title:
Cinnamon Roll Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
3 cups flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup sugar
4 teaspoons baking powder
1 1/2 cups milk
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
1/2 cup melted butter
Topping:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons flour
1 tablespoons cinnamon
Glaze:
2 cups powdered sugar
5 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Mix cake ingredients except the melted butter.  Slowly stir in melted butter last and pour in a greased 9X13 pan.  Mix topping ingredients until well combined and drop evenly over the top of cake and swirl with knife
Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 30 minutes
While warm, drizzle glase over the cake and enjoy!
