Title:
Cheeseburger Meat Loaf 1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c ketchup, divided
1 egg
1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
1/2 cup onion, chopped fine
1 lb lean ground beef
2 teaspoons prepared mustard
2 teaspoons dill/sweet pickle relish
6 slices American cheese

Directions:
In a bowl, combine 1/4 cup ketchup, egg, bread crumbs and onion.  Crumble beef over mixture and mix well.  On a large piece of waxed paper, pat beef mixture into a 10 in.x 6-in. rectangle. Spread remaining ketchup over meat to within 1/2 inch of long sides and 1 1/2 in. of short sides. Top with mustard and relish.  Place four cheese slices on top;set remaining cheese aside. Roll up loaf, jelly-roll-style,starting with a short side and pulling away waxed paper while rolling.  Seal seams and ends well.  Place loaf, seam side down, in a greased 11 x7 x2 baking pan.  Bake at 350' for 45 minutes or until meat is not longer pink and the meat thermometer reads 160'  Cut the reserved cheese slices in half diagonally place on top of loaf. Return to the oven for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Let stand for 10 minutes before slicing.
