Title:
Spicy Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/111294.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
dash salt
1 egg
1 teaspoon McCormick速 Pure Vanilla Extract
1 teaspoon McCormick速 Ground Cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon McCormick速 Ground Nutmeg
2 cups all-purpose flour

Directions:
Beat together butter, sugar, salt, egg, vanilla, cinnamon and nutmeg.  Stir in flour.  Chill 60 minutes.
Shape into 1-inch balls.  Place on baking sheet.  Flatten with fork.
Bake in preheated 350 degree F oven 12 minutes.  Cool on wire rack.  Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
McCormick速 & Company
Description: 
Spicy Butter Cookies are great with coffee or tea throughout the year. This cookie is easily shaped by rolling the dough into balls, then flattening with a fork before baking.
