Title:
Easy Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups Miracle Whip salad dressing
1 two-layer size yellow cake mix
4 eggs
1/4 cup cold water
2 tsp. ground cinnamon
2 cups finely shredded carrots
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1 16 oz. container ready to spread cream cheese frosting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Beat salad dressing, cake mix, eggs, water and cinnamon in large bowl with electric mixer at medium speed until well blended. Stir in carrots and walnuts. Pour batter into greased 13 x 9 inch baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes or until wooden toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely. Spread cake with frosting. Garnish as desired. Makes 12 servings.
