Title:
Eggs Benedict from Galbani® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4556111.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
2 English muffins, toasted
10 fresh basil leaves
8 ounces Galbani® Mozzarella Fresca™, sliced
2 slices tomato
2 poached eggs
4 tablespoons prepared hollandaise sauce
Chopped fresh chives
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Butter the muffins, top with the fresh basil, add some Galbani(R) Mozzarella Fresca(TM) slices and a slice of tomato. Add the poached egg.
Drizzle with hollandaise, add some chives, add salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Galbani Cheese
Description: 
These Eggs Benedict use fresh mozzarella cheese and prepared hollandaise sauce so your favorite brunch dish is on the table is just minutes.
