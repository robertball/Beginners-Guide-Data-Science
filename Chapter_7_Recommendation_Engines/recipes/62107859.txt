Title:
Chocolate Peppermint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cup flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
2 eggs
1 Tbs. vanilla
1 cup chocolate morsels
1 cup soft peppermint chunks (not the hard candy)
1/2 cup coconut
1/2 chopped nuts

Directions:
Mix flour, baking soda and salt together in a small bowl. Set aside
Cream butter and sugars until smooth, add vanilla and eggs.
Slowly add flour mixture, mix well
Fold in chocolate morsels, peppermint chunks, coconut and nuts.
Drop by tsp. on ungreased baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 13 minutes or until lightly browned. Do not over bake.  Cool on pan for 3 minutes then move to cooling rack.

Submitter: 
Becky Bates Myers
Description: 
An original recipe that is a soft chocolate, peppermint cookie that my daughters eat up before anyone else gets any.
