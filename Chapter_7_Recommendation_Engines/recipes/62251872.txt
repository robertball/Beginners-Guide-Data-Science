Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Boneless Chicken Breast halves
6 Slices Deli Ham
6 Slices Deli Swiss Cheese
1 Egg
1/4 Cup Milk
2 Cups Italian Bread Crumbs
2 Tbsp Unsalted Butter
1/2 Cup White Cooking Wine
Dash of Salt
Dash of Black Pepper
1 Can Cream of Mushroom Soup

Directions:
Mix egg, milk, in a shallow bowl.
Mix the bread crumbs, salt and pepper in a second shallow bowl.
Pound Chicken breasts flat.
Lay a slice of cheese and ham on each breast half.
Roll chicken, cheese and ham and secure with 2 to 3 toothpicks.
Dip rolls into the eqq mixture and then coat with bread crumb mixture.
Saute rolls in butter on medium heat until golden brown.
Mix wine and soup mix.
After the rolls have browned pour in sauce,cover and simmer for 1/2 hour.
