Title:
Antipasto Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box tri-color fusilli pasta
1 bunch seasoned/roasted asparagus
1 1/2 cups marinated kalamata olives quartered
1 1/2 cups marinated artichokes quartered
1/2 cup marinated cherry peppers julienned
1/2 bell pepper julienned
8 oz salami julienned
1 cup finely shaved pecorino
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
1 cups brown sugar
Salt, pepper & granulated garlic to taste

Directions:
1. Cook pasta per instructions on box.
2.  Cook asparagus, let cool, cut into fourths.
3. Combine pasta, asparagus, olives, artichoke hearts, cherry peppers, bell pepper, salami and pecorino.
4. In a separate bowl, mix red wine vinegar, olive oil, brown sugar, salt, pepper and granulated garlic to create a vinaigrette.
5. Poor vinaigrette over pasta salad, mix well.
6. Let stand in refrigerator for 1 hour before serving.
