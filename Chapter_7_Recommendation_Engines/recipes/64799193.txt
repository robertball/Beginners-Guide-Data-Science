Title:
Zucchini Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups grated zucchini
2 large slightly beaten eggs
2 T. chopped green onion, or minced red onion
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
dash dried oregano
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Directions
Blot off excess moisture on zucchini with paper towels. Mix eggs and add zucchini. Add onion. Mix dry ingredients. Add to zucchini. Stir JUST until moistened. Heat about one half inch vegetable oil in a heavy skillet to a medium high heat. Be sure it is hot enough. Drop batter by rounded tablespoons into the frying pan. Saute 2 to 3 minutes on each side, until golden. May drain on paper towels. Serve with sour cream. (May keep warm as you are cooking in a low oven, 25o degrees. Makes about 20.
