Title:
Hot german potato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3Tbsp oil
4green onions sliced thin
1Tbsp each: flour and parsley
2Tbsp dijon mustard
1/2tsp salt
1/4tsp pepper
2 (160z)cans sliced potatoes (drained) save 1/2C of the liquid.
1/4C vinegar (cider)
2Tbsp bacon bits

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet. Add the green onions to bacon drippings and saute until tender. Stir in flour, sugar, parsley and mustard, salt and pepper. Gradually add potato liquid and vinegar. Cook and stir until mixture thickens and boils. Stir in potatoes and heat through. Sprinkle with the bacon bits and serve.
