Title:
Caramel bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package caramels (candies)
1/2 cup cream
1 1/2 cups oatmeal
1 1/2 cups flour
1 cup margarine or butter, melted
1 cup brown sugar
3/4 tsp. baking soda
3/4 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
3/4 cup nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Lightly greased a 9x13 inch baking pan.
Melt the caramels and cream in a saucepan over medium heat; set aside.
Mix oatmeal, flour, margarine/butter, brown sugar, baking soda, and salt; place half of this mixture in baking pan.  Bake for 10 minutes.
Cool oatmeal crust; spread with caramel mixture.
Sprinkle cookies with chocolate chips and nuts; sprinkle remaining oatmeal mixture on top.
Bake for an additional 15 minutes.
