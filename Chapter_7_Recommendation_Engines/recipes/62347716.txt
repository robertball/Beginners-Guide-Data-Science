Title:
Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/397038.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
1 can cream of chicken, or cream of mushroom
1 cup of frozen vegetables (corn, carrot, green bean mix, or similar)
3/4 can of milk (use soup can to get residual soup out of can)
pinch of salt
pinch of pepper
garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
brown turkey in pan.  Add salt, pepper, garlic powder to turkey.  When done, add frozen vegetables, and cook for a couple of more minutes.
Add mixture to 9 x 12 pan.  Add soup, and milk. Then place a layer of tater tots on top of casserole.
Optional, sprinkle wtih shredded cheddar cheese.
Cook for 35 minutes.
