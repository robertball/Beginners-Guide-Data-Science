Title:
Spaghetti Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground chuck
2 cups onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, crushed
2 T. sugar
2 t. salt
2 t. bosil leaves
1/8 t. red pepper
1 t. organo leaves
2 cans italion tomatoes, undrained 3 oz. size (or diced tomatoes works just as well).
1 can tomatoe paste, 6 oz.
1 can on tomatoe puree, 6 oz.

Directions:
Cook meat, onion & garlic until brown (about 10 min.).
Add rest of ingredients and bring to a boil, then simmer 3 hours and ENJOY!
