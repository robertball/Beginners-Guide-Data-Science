Title:
Egg Boats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 eggs
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon mustard
1 tablespoon dill pickle juice
3 roma tomatoes
dash of paprika
6 pieces of toast

Directions:
Hard boil the eggs.  Remove eggshells and use an egg cutter to dice eggs.  Mash eggs further with fork to create egg salad consistency.  Add mayonnaise, mustard, and pickle juice.
Toast 6 pieces of bread.  Put egg salad mixture on the bread, open faced.  Slice tomatoes and place on top of your boat.  Add salt, pepper and paprika to taste.

Submitter: 
Trisha Brummer
Description: 
This is a long-standing family breakfast tradition at my house, but I have had them for lunch and dinner as well. You can add more or less mayonnaise depending on how you like your egg salad. This recipe does not work with cold, pre-made, egg salad.  The key is to serve it warm!
