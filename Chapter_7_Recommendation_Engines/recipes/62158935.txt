Title:
Blended Mojitos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 mint leaves
juice of 1 lime
4 oz Bacardi Limon Citrus Rum
1.5 tbsp sugar
2-2.5 cups ice cubes
1 355 mL can lime sparking seltzer water

Directions:
In a blender, add mint leaves, lime juice, rum, sugar and ice cubes.
Blend until smooth.
Pour equal amounts into two 16 oz glasses
Top glasses off with lime seltzer water
Place a lime wedge on the glass rim and a fresh sprig of mint leaf in the drink
Stir and enjoy!

Submitter: 
CANADIANCOOK1
Description: 
Refreshing homemade mojito without having to spend time muddling!  Blending ingredients makes a smooth, well mixed mojito!  Quick, easy and delicious!
