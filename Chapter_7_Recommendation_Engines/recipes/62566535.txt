Title:
Jan's Snowman Hats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/539723.jpg
Ingredients:
1 16 ounce package Oreo sandwich cookies
1 10 ounce package jumbo marshmallows
1 tablespoon ready made chocolate frosting
16 ounce bag milk chocolate chips
4 tablespoons shortening
powdered sugar

Directions:
Glue marshmallows to Oreo with a small amount of frosting.  Set aside.
Melt chocolate in a double boiler pan.  Add shortening 1 tablespoon at a time until smooth and silky.
Dip hats into chocolate covering well.
Set on wire cookie rack placed over cookie sheet to drip.
Before chocolate completely sets, sprinkle with powdered sugar to look like snow.
Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
No bake cookies you can make with your kids.  Easy and fun!  You can change it up to fit the season -- just dip in green melting chocolate for leprechaun hats!
