Title:
Crock Pot Chili Queso Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1128023.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp salted butter
2 (15 ounce) cans chili without beans
1 (16 ounce) jar medium salsa
3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, cubed
2 tablespoons minced jalapeno pepper, or to taste (optional)
2 (13 ounce) bags tortilla chips

Directions:
In a skillet, saute onion and garlic in butter until tender. Transfer to a slow cooker. Stir in the chili, salsa, cream cheese, and jalapeno (if desired). Cover and cook on low for 4 hours or until heated through, stirring occasionally. Stir before serving with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Heather Santos
Description: 
This recipe is easy to throw together and goes over great at potlucks. Doubling the recipe makes the perfect amount to fit in my 5 quart crock pot. Turn up the heat by using hot salsa and jalapenos.
