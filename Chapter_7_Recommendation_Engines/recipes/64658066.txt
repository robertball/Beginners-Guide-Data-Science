Title:
Corn tortilla, chicken, cheese, black bean, verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz pre-roasted chicken breast. shredded
8 oz Tillamook Medium Cheddar shredded
7 yellow corn tortillas
7 oz medium green enchilada sauce
15 oz canned black beans

Directions:
Spray an 8X12 casserole with Pam or other cooking spray
Create layers tortilla, chicken, beans, 1/2 of the cheese, tortilla
Wet it all with the enchilada sauce
Top with remainder of the cheese
Bake in 350 oven for 35 minutes or until top cheese begins to brown.
