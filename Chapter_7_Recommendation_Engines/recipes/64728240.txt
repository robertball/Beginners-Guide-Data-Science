Title:
Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb hamburger
1 onion
green pepper
2 cloves garlic
1 tbsp parsley
1 tbsp basil
1 1/2 tsp salt
dash of pepper
3/4 tsp sugar
5 shakes Tabasco
5 shakes Worcestershire
28oz can diced tomato
1 large can tomato paste
9 noodles
500g cottage cheese
2 beaten eggs
1 tsp salt
dash of pepper
3/4c parmesan
2 tbsp parsley
mozzarella

Directions:
Brown meat. Add ingredients. Mix cheese together. Layer bottom with noodles in 9x13 dish. Layer with meat sauce, then cheese mixture.
Bake at 350 for 1/2 hour.
