Title:
Captain's Pina Colada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981086.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Captain Morgan® Original Spiced Rum
6 ounces pina colada mix
1 spear pineapple
1 maraschino cherry

Directions:
Add Captain Morgan(R) Original Spiced Rum and pina colada mix over crushed ice in a blender
Blend until smooth and pour into a specialty glass.
Garnish with pineapple spear and maraschino cherry.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
The mellow tropical spices in Captain Morgan® Original Spiced Rum add a little island flavor to any drink.
