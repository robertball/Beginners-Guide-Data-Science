Title:
Curly Noodle Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 (11 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (3 ounce) package beef ramen noodles
1/4 cup water (optional)

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink; drain. Stir in the tomatoes, corn and contents of noodle seasoning packet. Bring to a boil. Break noodles into small pieces; stir into beef mixture.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10-12 minutes or until noodles are tender, adding water if desired.

Submitter: 
Joanne  ShewChuk
Description: 
Looking for a hearty meal that calls for only a few items? This beefy entree from Joanne Shewchuk fits the bill. 'We have three active children, so this simple skillet dinner is ideal,' writes the St. Benedict, Saskatchewan reader.
