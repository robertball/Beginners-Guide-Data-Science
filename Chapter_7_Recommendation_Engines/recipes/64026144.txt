Title:
Brownie Refrigerator Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of brownie mix
1 extra large egg
1 8oz. package of cream cheese - softened
1 cup powdered sugar
2 8oz. containers of Cool Whip
1 3oz. package of instant chocolate pudding
1 3oz. package of instant vanilla pudding
3 1/2 cups milk
1 chocolate candy bar or chocolate syrup for garnish

Directions:
Mix brownie mix according to directions, add egg and bake in 13x9 pan.
Mix cream cheese, powdered sugar and 1 container of whipped topping.
Spread mixture on top of cooled brownies.
Blend puddings and milk together and spread on top of cream cheese mixture.
Spread remaining container of whipped topping over pudding mixture.
Garnish with chocolate shavings or syrup.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
