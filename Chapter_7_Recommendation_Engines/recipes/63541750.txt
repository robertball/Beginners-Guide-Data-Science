Title:
Vegetable Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2[8 ounces] cans refrigerated crescent rolls
1 cup sour cream
1 [8 ounce] package cream cheese softened
1/2 package ranch dressing powder
2 1/2 cups sliced vegetables [your choice]
1/2 cup sliced ripe olives
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
3/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
1.Unroll the crescent rolls and place in an ungreased 15-in.x10-in.x1-in baking pan.Flatten dough to fit the pan,sealing seams and perforation.Bake at 375 degrees F for 10 minutes or until golden brown.Then cool.
2.In a small mixing bowl,beat the sour cream,cream cheese and ranch dressing powder until smooth.Spread over crust.Top with vegetables of your choice.Sprinkle with olives and cheeses.Press lightly.Cover and chill for at least 1 hour.Cut into squares.
