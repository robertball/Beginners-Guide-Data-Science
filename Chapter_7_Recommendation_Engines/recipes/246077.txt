Title:
Chia Colada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3292821.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut water
2 tablespoons pineapple juice
1 tablespoon chia seeds
1/4 cup chopped pineapple

Directions:
Blend coconut water, pineapple juice, and chia seeds together in a blender. Set mixture aside until chia seeds expand, about 30 minutes. Top mixture with chopped pineapple to serve.
