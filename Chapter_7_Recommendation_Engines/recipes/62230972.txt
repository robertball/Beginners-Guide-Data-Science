Title:
Slow Cooker Spicy Black-Eyed Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans chicken broth
1 pound dried black-eyed peas, sorted and rinsed
1 onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, diced
1 green bell pepper, stemmed, seeded, and diced
1 can green chiles
1/2 lb smoked turkey
2 can dice tomatoes
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
salt, to taste
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Pour chicken broth in crock pot. Combine the black-eyed peas, onion, garlic, bell pepper, green chiles, smoked turkey, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper; stir to blend. Cover the slow cooker and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours until the beans are tender.
