Title:
Appel Fritter  " Apfel Kuecherle"  / "Beignets" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large. Apples
Batter:  4 oz Flour
3 oz milk
1 Egg
1 tsp. Oil.
pinch of Salt.
powdered Sugar or Vanilla Sauce.
Oil,  for baking/frying

Directions:
Peel and core the apples.  Slice across into 5 Slices.  Pat dry and put into prepared Batter. leave for 20 minutes.   Carefully Fry on on Both sides in enough Oil and when done put on Paper for a moment.  Sprinkle with Powdered Sugar.  Garnish with;  Berries, Whipped Cream, Vanilla Sauce
Cinnamon Sugar . any combination.!!
