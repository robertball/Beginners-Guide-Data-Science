Title:
Japanese Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Regular Cucumber, sliced
1 Persian Cucumber, sliced
1/2 tsp minced Garlic
1 tsp minced Ginger
1/2 tsp dried onion/sea salt milled
1/8 tsp Sesame oil
Full bottle rice wine vinegar, spiced
1 tsp honey
1 tsp dill weed

Directions:
slice cucumbers, mix other ingredients and cover cucumbers with dressing.
allow to sit overnight.
sprinkle with fresh dill weed and serve.
