Title:
Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads of Cabbage
1 pound of bacon
1 pound of real butter
2 Yellow Onions
pinch of salt to taste

Directions:
Boil the 2 heads of cabbage til tender
sautee the bacon and chopped onion in the real butter
when cabbage is cooked add the bacon and chopped onion to cabbage mixing it all together let the combination simmer for 30 min stirring occasionily
salad is served hot
