Title:
Food - The Edible Kind Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups beef broth
2 (12-ounce) cans of vegetable juice (like V8Â®)
1 pound frozen mixed vegetables
1 (1.25-ounce) package onion soup mix
1 (7-ounce) package alphabet shaped noodles, uncooked
2 tsp onion powder (or more to taste)
2 tsp garlic powder (or more to taste)
salt and pepper to taste
Shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large saucepan, bring the beef broth, vegetable juice, onion soup mix, and frozen mixed vegetables to a boil.  Cook until vegetables are almost tender, approximately 10 minutes.
Stir in alphabet noodles and return to a boil.  Allow to boil gently, stirring occasionally, until noddles are al dente, approximately 8 minutes.
Season with onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
Ladle into bowls and top with cheddar cheese to serve.

Description:
