Title:
Bleu Cheese Phyllo Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup crumbly bleu cheese
1/4 cup half and half
1/3 cup commercially prepared apricot or peach chutney
Package of 15 Athens pre-baked phyllo cups (freezer section)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Open the package of phyllo cups and arrange on a baking sheet.
Mix the crumbly bleu cheese with the half and half in a 2-cup glass measuring cup.
Spoon the mixture into the phyllo cups until they are almost full.
Top each filled cup with a dollop of the chutney. Bake for 15 minutes and served warm.
