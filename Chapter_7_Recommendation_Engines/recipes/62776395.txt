Title:
Cowboy Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 ounce can of Limeade concentrate
6 ounces tequila
3 ounces raspberry liquer
1 12 ounce can of light beer
ice
sugar (for garnish)

Directions:
Combine limeade, tequila and raspberry liquer in blender until well mixed. Add light beer and stir to combine, but be careful of foam. This may be served frozen or on the rocks in a sugar rimmed glass.

Submitter: 
hlmartin81
Description: 
This is a fun cocktail that will appeal to many different tastes because it balances the tart, sweet and tangy flavors of the liquors beautifully.
