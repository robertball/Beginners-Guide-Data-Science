Title:
Big Ray's Soda Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1092630.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box cake mix
20 oz. soda
8 oz. whipped cream
sprinkles, if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Pour cake mix into medium bowl and slowly add soda, mix until smooth.  Pour mixture into greased 13x9 baking dish.  Bake for 30 minutes or until cake tests done.  Allow to cool and spread whipped cream on top, adding sprinkles if desired.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This quick and tasty cake can be changed to suit your own tastes by changing the flavor of the cake mix and the soda flavor, the  combinations are almost endless.
