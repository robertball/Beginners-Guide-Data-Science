Title:
Fireman's Bento Box Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4540577.jpg
Ingredients:
6 hot wings; http://allrecipes.com/recipe/166638/baked-buffalo-wings/
6 jalapeno poppers; http://allrecipes.com/recipe/240785/baked-jalapeno-poppers/
1 cup cottage cheese garnished with chopped black olives; representing a dalmation
Spiralized jicama; representing a fire hose
4 baby bells; representing fire bells
Carrots; cut in the shape of a fire hydrant
Pretzel stick; representing a ladder
sliced radishes with black olives; representing a fireman's hat
Ranch dressing in red and blue shot glasses; representing emergency lights
3 green onions; representing fire hose spray
Carrot sticks
Celery sticks

Directions:
Prepare hot wings as directed in the recipe
Prepare poppers as directed in the recipe
Assemble remaining ingredients and place in a bento style lunch box

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Cool ranch dressing helps put out the fire of this spicy bento style lunch complete with hot wings, jalapeno poppers and other goodies that represent a fireman's needs. Enjoy this lunch at work cold or reheated in the microwave.
