Title:
Homemade Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground hamburger
1/4 lb Italian sausage
1 lrg red onion
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 Green Pepper
2 celery stocks, chopped
2-3 jalapeno peppers, finely chopped (optional, reduce chili powder if used)
1 12 oz can of tomato juice
1 28 oz can of peeled & chopped tomatoes
1/2 c ketchup
2 tbls brown sugar
2 tbls molassis
1 tbls warcestershire sauce
4 tblsp chili powder
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp thyme
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 oz bittersweet chocolate, broken into chunks
1 15 oz can red kidney beans
1 15 oz can white kidney beans
1 cup beer

Directions:
In large pot over medium heat, brown hamburger and sausage. Add onions, garlic, celery, and jalapenos and cook until celery is tender and onions are clear.
Add tomato juice, chopped tomatoes, ketchup, sugar, and spices. Bring to a boil, add beans and bittersweet chocolate; mix well. Continue to boil and add beer, simmer for 30-45 min.
