Title:
Creamy Chocolate Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983087.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons granulated sugar
1/4 cup NESTLE® TOLL HOUSE® Baking Cocoa
1/4 cup cornstarch
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 (12 fluid ounce) can NESTLE® CARNATION® Evaporated Fat Free Milk
1/2 cup water
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
COMBINE sugar, cocoa, cornstarch and salt in medium saucepan.  Add evaporated milk and water; whisk to blend.
COOK over medium heat, stirring constantly, for about 7 minutes or until pudding thickens (do not boil).  Remove from heat; stir in butter and vanilla extract.  Serve warm or cold.

Submitter: 
Nestle® Carnation®
Description: 
This velvety soft chocolate pudding will delight 'kids of all ages'. Serve warm or cold with a dollop of whipped cream!
