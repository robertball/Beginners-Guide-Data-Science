Title:
Gluten Free Cinnamon Raisin Bread (Breadmaker) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 Cups Pamela's Gluten Free Bread Mix
1 egg + 3 egg whites (or just 2 eggs)
1/3 cup of vegetable oil
~1 1/4 cups of water
1 TBL Cinnamon
1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
1 1/2 Cups of raisins
2 1/4 tsp Active Dry Yeast

Directions:
1) In a 2 cup measuring cup, fill with 1/3 cup vegetable oil.
2) Add the egg and egg whites.
3) Fill the remainder of the 2 cup measuring cup with water to just over the 2 cup line (~1/8th of an inch).
4) Pour the contents of the 2 cup measuring cup into breadmaker pan.
5) Add 3 1/2 cups of Pamela's Gluten Free Bread Mix to the breadmaker pan on top of the liquid ingredients.
6) Add 1 TBL of Cinnamon to breadmaker pan.
7) Add 1/4 cup of brown sugar to breadmaker pan.
8) Add 2 1/4 tsp of active dry yeast to the breadmaker pan.
9) Load raisins into fruit/nut tray
10) Set breadmaker for "Basic" loaf (Do NOT use the gluten free setting!!)
11) Select Medium crust
12) Select 2 lbs. loaf
13) Start

Submitter: 
Adam Glenn Thomas
Description: 
A great gluten free raisin bread.  People love it and I don't even tell them it's gluten free.
