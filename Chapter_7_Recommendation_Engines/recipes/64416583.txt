Title:
honey garlic rosemary lamb chops with honey mustard mayo dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs rack lamb chops
1/2 c olive oil
2 tbsp honey
1 tbsp grainy mustard
zest of 1 lemon
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp, rosemary,
freshly ground pepper
sea salt to taste

Directions:
place whole lamb rack into a glass or ceramic container to marinate overnight
in a large bowl, stir in olive oil, honey, mustard, lemon zest, garlic, rosemary and pepper
pour the honey marinade over the chops a bit at a time, making sure to cover the meat well.  make sure you put the marinade under the chops too, - cover and refrigerate for 24 hours
let chops reach room temp before cooking
preheat oven to 450 or grill
oven - place a cast iron skilet over medium heat, when pan is hot, shake off excess, sear to a golden crust, about 2 minutes per side, pop pan into over and roast for 10 minutes, then let rest
grill, preheat grill to 350
wrap the bare bones with aluminum foil
shake off marinade, grill to medium rare about 10 to 15 minutes, turning every so often
honey-mustard mayo dip
1/2 c good quality mayo
1 tbso honey
1 clove garlic minced
juice of half a lemon
1 tsp grainy mustard
freshly ground sale and pepper
