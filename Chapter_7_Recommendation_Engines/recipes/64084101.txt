Title:
Banana Peanut Butter Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ¾ cups flour
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. baking soda
½ tsp. salt
½ cup chocolate chips
2 eggs at room temperature
½ cup butter or margarine, softened
¼ cup sour cream (low fat is fine)
2-3 mashed bananas
½ cup peanut butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Prepare muffin tin by greasing cups or using paper liners.
Using a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar, baking soda, salt and chocolate chips.
In a separate bowl, add the eggs, butter, sour cream, mashed bananas and peanut butter. Mix together thoroughly using an electric hand mixer or stand mixer.
Add the wet mixture to the flour mixture. Combine thoroughly but ignore lumps. Scoop into prepared muffin tin and place in oven.

Tender and moist - peanut butter, bananas and chocolate - what more can you say! A favorite of kids and adults, alike.
