Title:
Awesome and Healthy Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quick cooking oats
2 tblspn brown rice flour
1 tblspn unsweetened coco powder
1 tblspn white sugar
1 tblspn brown sugar
2 tblspn splenda
2 tblspn slepnda brown sugar
1 tspn baking powder
1 tspn each of cinnamon and nutmeg
1/8 tspn ground cloves
3 tblspn crushed walnuts
1 tspn vanillia
3 tblspn reduced fat margarine
1/2 of a ripe banana
1 tblspn unsweetened applesauce
1 egg white

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
2. In a bowl mix together oats, brown rice flour, unsweetened coco powder, baking powder, white sugar, brown sugar, splenda, splenda brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and ground cloves.
3. Mix in egg white, ripe banana, margarine and applesauce. Add walnuts and raisins.
4. On a greased baking tray form dough into small balls and place about one inch apart. Bake 10 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into center of cookie comes out clean.
5. cool on a wire rack for 3-5 minutes.

Description: 
I recently discovered I am gluten and dairy sensitive, so I created this cookie while experimented with healthy substituions from another recipe on this site. It ended up being a hit with the whole family, including my brother, the cookie connusor!
