Title:
Best Ever Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
2 tsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. vanilla
4-5 very ripe bananas, mashed
2 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. baking soda
3 1/2 cups flour
1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease 2 loaf pans.
Cream together butter, cream cheese, and sugar.
Add eggs one at a time.
Add lemon juice, vanilla, bananas, baking powder, baking soda, flour, and nuts.
Bake for 55-60 minutes or until knife comes out clean.
Remove from pans immediately and cool on racks completely.

Description: 
I am a nurse.  This recipe was given to me by a patient I cared for in the hospital.  I have never found a banana bread that tops it- EVER!
