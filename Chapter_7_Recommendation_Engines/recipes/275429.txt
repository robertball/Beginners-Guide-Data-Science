Title:
Air Fryer Rosemary Garlic Baby Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7009268.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds multi-colored new potatoes, halved
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon finely chopped fresh rosemary
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon lemon zest

Directions:
Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Toss together potatoes, oil, garlic, rosemary, and salt in a large bowl. Arrange potatoes in a single layer in the air fryer basket without overcrowding; work in batches, if necessary. Cook until potatoes are golden brown and tender, about 20 minutes. Sprinkle with lemon zest before serving.

Submitter: 
Allrecipes
Description: 
Transform new potatoes into a satisfying side dish via air fryer with the subtlest sprinkling of garlic, rosemary, and lemon zest.
