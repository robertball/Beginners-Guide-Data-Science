Title:
Spinach Artichoke Ravioli Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp butter
2 cloves garlic minced
3 Tbsp flour
2 C milk (preferably whole)
2 Tbsp Cream Cheese softened
1 C shredded mozzarella, divided
1/4 C freshly grated Parmesan
1 14 oz can artichoke hearts chopped
2 C frozen spinach defrosted & chopped- I use fresh
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes optional
2 lb frozen cheese ravioli
Kosher Salt & Freshly ground black pepper
2 tbsp Freshly chopped parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat
Add garlic and cook until fragrant about 30 secondes
Whisk flour into butter/garlic and cook until bubbling and golden, 1 minute more
Gradually pour in milk whisking constantly
Simmer then stir in cream cheese, 2/3 mozzarella and Parmesan
Let simmer until thickens slightly 2-3 minutes
Fold in Artichoke and Spinach, then add lemon juice, red pepper, salt & pepper
Remove from heat
Spread thin layer in bottom of 9/13, lay ravioli in single layer in bottom, top with 1/2 sauce then repeat ravioli and sauce.  Top with remaining mozzarella
Bake 30 minutes
Can broil 2-3 minutes if you want top to be golden
Garnish with parsley
