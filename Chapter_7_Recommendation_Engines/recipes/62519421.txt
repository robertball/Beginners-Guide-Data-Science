Title:
Deer Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs deer
1 lb sausage (plain pork)
3 eggs
1 c crushed ritz
1/2 c plain breadcrumbs
1/2 chopped onion
1 c honey BBQ sauce
1 t worchestershire
1/4 t liquid smoke
1 t salt (or less)
1 t savory
1 t garlic powder
topping
1 c ketchup
1/2 c brown sugar
1 t worchestershire

Directions:
Mix all ingrediants together
Top will ketchup
Make into 2 loafs and bake at 350 for 45 min.
