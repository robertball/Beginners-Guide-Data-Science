Title:
Chocolate Lover's Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978625.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
4 (1 ounce) squares BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Baking Chocolate, melted, cooled slightly
1 (6 ounce) OREO Pie Crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended. Add eggs; mix just until blended. Stir in melted chocolate. Pour into crust.
Bake 40 minutes or until center is almost set; cool.
Refrigerate several hours or overnight. Store leftover cheesecake in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
BAKER'S Chocolate
Description: 
This easy to prepare chocolate cheesecake is sure to become a family favorite.
