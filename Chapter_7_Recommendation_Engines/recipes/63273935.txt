Title:
Creamy Chow Mein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can package chow mein noodles, divided
1 case pork hamburger patty's unbreaded
10 celery ribs, chopped
13 large onions, chopped
4 cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
4  cans  condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted
10 cups milk
2 #10 cans sliced water chestnuts, undrained
2 #10 jar sliced mushrooms, undrained
1/2 bag cashews

Directions:
Sprinkle one can of the chow mein noodles into a greased 2 small hotel pans. In a bowl, combine the meat, celery, onion, soups, milk, water chestnuts, mushrooms and cashews. Spoon over the noodles; top with remaining noodles. Bake, uncovered, at 325 degrees F for 35-40 minutes or until heated through.
Remember to put in small pans and batch cook.
