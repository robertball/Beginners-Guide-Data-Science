Title:
Superfast Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound asparagus
1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
OR any other seasoning you may prefer
Olive oil cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Snap the asparagus at the tender part of the stalk. Arrange spears in one layer on a baking sheet. Spray lightly with nonstick spray; sprinkle with the Cajun seasoning.
Bake in the preheated oven until tender, about 10 minutes. (13 minutes if you like your asparagus more tender).  OPT. - You can sprinkle a little shredded white cheddar over them when they are done and broil to melt.
