Title:
Lime Meringue Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 graham cracker crust- make or store bought
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup lime juice- fresh or bottled
3 egg yolks
3 egg whites
6 TBLS sugar

Directions:
pre heat oven @ 350
separate eggs- set aside -
in mixing bowl put 1 can sweetened condensed milk
add th 3 eggs yolks- mix well( i mix by hand with wire whisk)
add the juice- mix well
pour into pie crust- set aside
for meringue:(i mix with electric hand mixer)
in mixing bowl put the 3 egg whites- mix til foaming
add 6 TBLS sugar ( 1 TBLS at a time- mixing well between each addition)
continue mixing til stiff peaks form- top pie with meringue
bake @ 350- 10 minutes or til evenly browned
