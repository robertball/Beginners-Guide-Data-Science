Title:
Chicken Caprese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988657.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons Mrs. Dash® Tomato Basil Garlic Blend
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
8 cherry tomatoes, cut in quarters
4 low fat tiny mozzarella balls, cut into quarters
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 teaspoons red wine vinegar
Cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray a 9 x 9 x 2 inch baking tray with cooking spray.
Toss tomatoes with 1 Tbsp. of Mrs. Dash(R) Tomato Basil Garlic Blend.
Lightly flatten each breast to 1/4 inch thickness. Place tomatoes and four quarters of mozzarella ball on each chicken breast. Roll up and secure with a toothpick.
Place breasts on baking tray. Drizzle with the olive oil, vinegar and remaining 1 Tbsp. of Mrs. Dash(R) Tomato Basil Garlic Blend.
Bake for about 30 minutes. Remove from oven, cover lightly with foil and let rest for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Dash®
Description: 
For hearty chicken wrapped in fantastic flavor, roll tomatoes tossed in Mrs. Dash® Tomato Basil Garlic Blend with mozzarella into a chicken breast. Then bake. Juicy and tender, it's a mouthful of deliciousness in every bite.
