Title:
Cheesey Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24-oz jar navy beans, with the liquid
3 chicken breasts cut into 1/2" cubes
1 4-oz can diced green chilies
1 12-oz jar salsa
4 oz. shredded pepper jack cheese
8 oz. shredded Monterey jack cheese
1 cup chicken broth
1/2 tsp. white pepper
1/2 tsp. garlic salt

Directions:
Place all ingredients into the slow cooker and stir.  Cook on low for 6-7 hours or until the chicken is cooked and the cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Mother Ann
Description: 
This winter has been so cold I have been wanting warm, comfort food.  One morning I threw this together in the crock pot.  The house smelled delicious and our dinner was fantastic!
