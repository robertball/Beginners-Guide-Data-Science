Title:
Jim's Chille' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds ground beef or stew meat
4-5 cloves chopped fresh garlic
2 medium onions
8 cups diced green pepper
1 Habenero pepper seeded and halved ( flavor of pepper is a must, use like a bay leaf)
(options)
3- jalapeno peppers seeded for extra flavor and heat or adjust seasoning for similar result
3- whole fresh cayenne peppers or halved and seeded
3 TBSP Chocolate to even the burn
2 -28 oz cans stewed tomatoes
3- cans bushes mild chili beans
1- 2 quart can tomato juice
3- pkg McCormick original or mild chili seasoning
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown beef iand diced rawn an 8-10 quart stock pot with salt and pepper on med high heat
Add garlic onion and peppers and saute' approx.  10 min
Add remaining ingredients and simmer 20 min
Serve with homemade bread or oyster crackers, sour cream, sharp cheddar cheese, and raw diced onion

Submitter: 
Jim Vaughan
Description: 
Heavily flavored with peppers as a true tribute to the Chille'
