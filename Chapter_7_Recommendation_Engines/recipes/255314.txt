Title:
Sweet and Savory Ramen Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3978760.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles (such as MaruchanÂ® Instant Lunch), seasoning packet discarded
1/4 cup maple syrup
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1 teaspoon ground paprika

Directions:
Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a saucepan. Remove from heat and add ramen. Let stand until softened, about 2 minutes. Drain excess water.
Mix maple syrup, cumin, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, and paprika together in a bowl. Mix in noodles using 2 forks.
