Title:
Stuffed Dates Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/753977.jpg
Ingredients:
- Bacon
- Whole Dates (Pitted)

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees!
1) Wrap the bacon around the date (You could cut the bacon slice in half to use more bacon) And toothpick the bacon & date together! Place on a greased cookie sheet! Place in the oven 15 min or until bacon is cooked (Don't over cook the bacon!)
SERVE & ENJOY!!

Submitter: 
Mrs Sarah
Description: 
This is probably the easiest appetizer to make, and once will never be enough! These are just the perfect combination of salty and sweet! So good!
