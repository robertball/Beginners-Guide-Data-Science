Title:
Bananas Foster French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package of “King’s” brand Hawaiian rolls (slice into 12 even slices) DO NOT BREAK ROLLS APART
3 Eggs
3/4 CUP Milk
1 TBSP Cinnamon
1 ½ TBSP Vanilla Extract
2 Bananas, sliced 1/8" Thick
1/4 CUP Chopped Pecans
½ CUP Brown Sugar
¼ CUP Butter, cube in 1/4" piece

Directions:
STEP 1:
Preheat oven to 350° > Spray 9x13 baking dish with butter flavored Pam. > Batter: beat eggs smooth and add milk, cinnamon, and vanilla.
STEP 2:
Dip 6 slices of bread in batter, coating both sides, place in baking dish. > In a casserole style placement, place sliced bananas, covering bread. > Cover with brown sugar, sprinkle with pecans and dot with butter pieces. > Dip the remaining 6 slices of bread in batter, coating both sides and place on top.
**Optional: Sprinkle a small bit of brown sugar and chopped pecans on top for extra crunch!
STEP 3:
Bake for 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
marie_1283
Description: 
This baked french toast recipe blends french toast and banana's foster into one casserole-style dish, and it is positively AMAZING!  This is great for breakfast or brunch... You could even serve this for dessert with a bit of vanilla bean ice cream on the side!
