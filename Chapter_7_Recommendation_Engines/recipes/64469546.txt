Title:
'Dinomite's Fluffy Bites' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2460174.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz liquid mud
4 T fat
1 box of dino biscuits
1/2 cup fluffy powder
1 scoop of unicorn poop

Directions:
Melt your mud and fat together for 2 minutues.  Pour over your dino biscuits. Mix. Toss in the fluffy powder and unicorn poop and mix again.  Time to munch. For an even tastier treat eat on a kale leaf!
