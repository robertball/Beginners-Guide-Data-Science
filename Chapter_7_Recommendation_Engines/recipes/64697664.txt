Title:
Baguette Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5300708.jpg
Ingredients:
1 baguette (or french bread) cut into 1/2 inch slices
1/2 cup melted butter or margarine
1/2 cup chopped garlic flakes
1 cup cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Place slices on cookie sheets lined with parchment paper.  Spread butter on each slice, top with chopped garlic flakes, and cover with plenty of cheese.  Bake in pre-heated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
