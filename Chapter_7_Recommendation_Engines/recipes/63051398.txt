Title:
Cheeseburger Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 cup milk
1/2 cup egg substitute
1/2 cup bisquick
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 tomatoes, sliced

Directions:
Brown beef & onions together, drain & place in greased 8x8 baking dish.
Mix salt, pepper, bisquick, milk & egg sub together and pout over beef slowly.  Back 25 min @ 400.
Sprinkle with cheese then top with tomatoe slices and bake for 5-8 min longer.
