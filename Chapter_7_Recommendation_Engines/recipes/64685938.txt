Title:
Cocktail Sausages (Drunken Pollacks) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5040812.jpg
Ingredients:
2 or 3 lbs Kielbasa sausage fully cooked
1 bottle BBQ sauce, like Sweet Baby Ray's
1/4 cup Bourbon
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup real maple syrup
1/4 cup grape jelly
1/4 cup molassas
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup sweet chili sauce
1/4 cup garlic chili sauce
Sriracha sauce to taste (careful, very hot)

Directions:
Cut Kielbasa into 3/4" slices.  Brown in a saute pan.
Combine all ingredients in a slow cooker and cook on low for 1-2 hours.  Serve warm with toothpicks.
