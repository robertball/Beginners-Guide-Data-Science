Title:
American Chop Suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 lb. lean ground beef (or turkey)
1 medium onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
1/2 cup grated or finely minced carrot
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 28-oz can crushed tomatoes
1 14-oz can tomato sauce
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp dried basil
1 box elbow macaroni*
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
Add beef, break up with fork and cook until browned.
Add onion, garlic, peppers, and carrots, and cook until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes. Add mushrooms and cook another 3 to 4 minutes.
Add crushed tomatoes, sauce and seasonings, and bring to a simmer.
Add uncooked macaroni.  Cover and simmer, stirring occasionally, until pasta is cooked - about 20 minutes.  (add water if necessary)
