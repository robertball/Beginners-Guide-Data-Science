Title:
'Deena' Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2743260.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine, melted
2 cups brown sugar
4 tbsp. cocoa
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour

Directions:
Mix in order given (don't over-mix).
Spread in a greased 9" x 13" pan.
Bake @ 350 x 20 minutes or until tests done with a toothpick.
Recipe can be doubled in an 11" x 17" jelly roll pan.
Ice, dust with icing sugar or serve plain.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A family (& community!) favorite - often used for potlucks, as 'Birthday Brownies' and hostess gifts. This cocoa powder (vs chocolate squares) recipe tastes wonderful, with or without icing and freezes very well. Why not make a double batch?!
