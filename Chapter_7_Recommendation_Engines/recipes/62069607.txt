Title:
Sweet and Tangy Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tart apples peeled, cored, and sliced (aprox. 6 cups)
1/4 cup granulated sugar
2 tbsp cinnamon
2 tsp lemon juice
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
1 tsp baking soda
3 tsp baking powder
1 1/2 tsp salt
juice and finely grated ring of 2 oranges (aprox. 2/3 cup of juice)
5 eggs
1 1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 tbsp vanilla

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 F
Grease and flour a 9 inch tube pan; set aside
Combine apples, sugar, cinnamon, and lemon juice; set aside.
In a large bowl, sift together all dry ingredients; blend in orange rind.  Make a well in the centre.
Wisk together wet ingredients; pour into well and beat together until smooth.  Lightly spread 1/3rd of the cake batter on bottom of cake tin.  Layer with 1/2 of apple mixture, repeat layers once.  Spread remaining batter over the top.  Bake for one 65 minutes until puffed and golden.  Check for doneness by inserting a cake tester and when pulled out is free of uncooked batter.  Let cool for five minutes ang remove from pan.  sprinkle with icing sugar if desired.  Enjoy warm or cold with wipped cream.

Submitter: 
EBSEPKE
Description: 
A warm and homey blend of apples and citrus in a cake that your guests will love.  No need to ice it.  Just a dolop of fresh wipped cream on a warm slice, Ahh heaven!
