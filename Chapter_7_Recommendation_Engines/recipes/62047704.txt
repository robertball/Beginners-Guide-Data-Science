Title:
Slow Cooker Party Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lbs. party wings
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup spicy barbque sauce
1 cup honey mustard barbque sauce
1 teaspoon Worshire Sauce
1-5 tablespoons bottled hot sauce
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
2 garlic cloves, minced

Directions:
1. Season Party Wings with Salt and pepper and put them either in the toaster oven on Broil or in a preheated oven on Broil for ten to fifteen minutes.  The wings will not be fully baked but just a little toasted.
2. Mix together all of your remaining ingredients.
3. Place wings in the slow cooker in layers, pouring enough of the sauce mixture to bearly cover the wings.
4. Cook on low for 6 hours or on high for 4.

Submitter: 
Mommy of Five
Description: 
This is a simple, gooey, put in the slow cooker and forget about it recipe!  Sure to entice the pickiest of eatters.  (Be prepared, the final product is delicious, a bit messy, but so worth it!)
