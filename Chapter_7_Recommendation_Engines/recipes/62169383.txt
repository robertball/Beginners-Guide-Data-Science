Title:
Apple and Spice Healthy Breakfast Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large apples, peeled, cored, and chopped
1/2 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
1 tbs. water
1 1/2 cups granola
1 1/2 cups fat free vanilla yogurt

Directions:
In a microwave safe bowl, combine apples, water, and pumpkin pie spice. Microwave on high for 1 minute, or until apples are soft. In three large glasses, layer 1/4 cup granola, 1/4 cup yogurt, and a spoonful of the apple mixture into each glass. Repeat layers. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
larkspur
Description: 
This yummy and unexpected combination is quick to come together on a busy morning, yet pretty and delicious enough to serve to company. It tastes the best with gingersnap granola, but any other kind is great too!
