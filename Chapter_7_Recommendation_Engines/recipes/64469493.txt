Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2459850.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup broccoli; cooked and chopped
1 cup cauliflower; cooked and chopped
2 cloves garlic; minced
3 eggs
1 ½ cups grated cheese
3/4 cup breadcrumbs
1/3 cup flour
1 tsp Italian seasoning
¼ tsp salt
¼ tsp black pepper

Directions:
1) In a large bowl, beat eggs, salt, black pepper and Italian Seasoning.
2) Stir in flour and breadcrumbs.
3)Add remainder of ingredients to bowl and mix all the ingredients together.
4) With your hands, form small patties and lay on a parchment lined baking sheet.
5) Bake at 375F for 20 minutes, turning the patties after the first 15 minutes.
