Title:
Not yo mommas egg salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/889562.jpg
Ingredients:
4 hard boiled eggs
2 Tbs mayonnaise
1/4 tsp curry powder
4 green olives
1 tsp capers
1 Tbs jarred roasted red peppers
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop eggs, capers, olives and roasted red peppers and place in bowl.
Add mayonnaise and curry powder and mix well.
Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
yvonnelong
Description: 
This curried egg salad is kicked up a notch with olives, capers and roasted red peppers.
