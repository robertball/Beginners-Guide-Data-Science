Title:
Cabbage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head cabbage, shredded
1 onion, chopped
EVOO to taste
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
4 ounces shredded sharp cheddar, plus about 2 T more
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup dry bread crumbs

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute onion in about one tablespoon butter until tender. Add cabbage and saute, covered, until soft.  Make a well in the center and add garlic, sauteing about 30 seconds, then mix.  Add soup and mix well. Add 4 ounces of cheese; heat and stir until melted.
Remove from the heat. Add thyme, marjoram, salt and pepper. Transfer to an ungreased 2-qt. baking dish. Mix bread crumbs and remaining cheese, sprinkle on top of casserole.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 20 to 30 minutes or until heated through.
