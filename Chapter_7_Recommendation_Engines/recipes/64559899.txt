Title:
Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1/4 cup molasses
2 tsp ginger
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp cloves
1/8 tsp salt
2 1/4 cups flour
sugar for dusting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Beat butter until softened, add sugar and blend until combine. Add egg; molasses and blend.
Add dry ingredient in order given and mix until well combine.
Shape into 1 inch balls; roll in sugar. Place 1 1/2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8 to 9 minutes or until lightly browned and tops are puffed (do not over bake). Cool on cookie sheet for 1 minute. Transfer to a wire rack and let cool.
