Title:
Old Fashioned Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box lemon Jello
1 can crushed pineapple in heavy syrup
1 8oz package cream cheese
1 can chilled evaporated milk
2 eggs
1/4 cup sugar
1 brick graham crackers, crushed
1 stick melted butter or margarine
2 tablespoons sugar

Directions:
Combine crushed crackers with 2 tablespoons of sugar and melted butter.
Press mixture into bottom of 9x13 baking dish.
In medium saucepan, beat eggs with sugar then add entire can of pineapple and mix well.
Cook over low to medium low heat until thickened, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and stir in Jello mix; let cool slightly.
Beat evaporated milk until whipped; add softned cream cheese and mix well.
Fold pineapple mixture into cream cheese mixture, then pour over crust; evenly distribute.
Cover with plastic wrap and chill overnight.
Prior to serving, sprinkle top of cake with crushed graham cracker.
