Title:
Aunt Meg's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds Italian Sausage (not HOT italian)
1 pound Pepperoni slices
1 box Oven Ready Lasagna Noodles
1 X-Large Jar Tomato & Basil Tomato Sauce (divided)
1 Regular Jar Traditional (no meat) Tomato Sauce
3 packages (2 cups each) Italian Blend Shredded Cheese
1 small can Parmesan Cheese
Spices (all to taste)
Garlic Powder
Onion Powder
Black Pepper
Oregano (dried)
Italian Seasoning Blend
Parsley (dried) - used as garnish on top

Directions:
Brown sausage.  Drain.  While the sausage is frying - pour a thin layer of tomato & basil sauce on the bottom of your deep dish 9x13 pan (helps to prevent burning) - sprinkle with each spice.  After sausage is drained combine the remaining tomato/basil sauce in with the sausage and mix in spices to desired preference. Place first layer of noodles over plain sauce, cover noodles with a layer of sauce, pepperoni and cheeses.  Repeat layering. After using the meat sauce (tomato/basil/sausage) use traditional sauce for remaining layers. Making sure you end with cheeses and sprnkle parley on top.  Cover with foil*.  Bake covered for 25-30 min. at 350 degrees.  Remove foil and continue baking for 15-20 min. or until cheese is melted and golden.  Remove from oven and let stand for 10 min. Cut, serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Aunt Meg
Description: 
The Pepperoni and Italian Sausage combination make this lasagna stand out from all others!  Serve with a salad and garlic bread - YUM!! :)
