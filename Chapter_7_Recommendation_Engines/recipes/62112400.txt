Title:
Blackberry Brain Freeze Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint vanilla ice cream
1 jigger blackberry brandy
Sprig of mint (optional)

Directions:
Scoop ice cream into blender or food processor, add liquor
and process till smooth. Pour into chilled tumbler and garnish with mint.

Submitter: 
Charley
Description: 
This is a delicious way to a brain freeze. Any flavored liquor that suits your taste will do. Very simple and easy to make.
