Title:
Clone of Wendy's Spicy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/663218.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
4 hamburger buns
mayo
romaine lettuce
tomato
1/3 cup Frank's Red Hot Sauce
2/3 cup water
1 cup flour
4 tsp cayenne powder
2 1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp season salt
1/8 tsp garlic powder
Oil for frying

Directions:
Mix the Frank's Red Hot with the water and set aside.
Combine the flour and spices in a seperate dish.
Pound chicken breasts to 3/4 of an inch thickness and cut to fit hamburger bun if needed.
Coat chicken with the flour mixture, then the sauce mixture and then flour mixture again.
Fry until crispy, about four minutes on each side.
Place cooked chicken on paper towel lined dish to absorb any extra oil.
Prepare buns with mayo, lettuce and tomato, adding one chicken breast to each burger.

Description: 
A spciy chicken fillet that is very close to the one served at Wendy's restaurants.  A great burger that you can make for yourself at home!
