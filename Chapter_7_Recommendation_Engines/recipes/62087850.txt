Title:
Banana Bread Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large bananas
box of yellow cake mix
box of instant banana pudding
1/3 cup oil
1 and 1/3 cup water
4 eggs

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350
Combine cake mix, pudding mix, oil, water and eggs in a large mixing bowl.
Mix with an electic mixer on high until smooth.
Chop or break bananas into small pieces and add to mixture. Continue to mix on high until there are no visible chunks.
Grease and lightly flour a bundt pan.
Pour mixture into the pan and bake for 40 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
