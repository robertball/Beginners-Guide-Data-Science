Title:
Cumin,Garlic And Red Pepper Pork Loin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/782949.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb.pork loin roast
4 minced cloves garlic
2 tsp.ground cumin
2 Tbsp.dried oregano
1 tsp.dried thyme
1 Tbsp.paprika
1 tsp.red chile flakes
1 tsp.chili powder
1 tsp.kosher salt
1 Tbsp.olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine all ingredients in mixing bowl.
Rub roast with seasonings and place on rack.
Bake for 1 1/2 hours or untill thermometer reaches 155f[75c]
Remove from oven and let stand 10 mins.before carving.
