Title:
Firecracker Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup sugar
2 Tbs. Dijon mustard
2 Tbs. Vegetable oil
2 Tbs. juice from jar of pickled jalepenos
1 to 2 tsps. of our favorit Louisana hot sauce
1/2 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
1/2 tsp. celery seed
slaw:
1 small green cabbage cored and shredded (8 to 10 cups)
2 carrots peeled and grated or shredded
1 red bell pepper, seeded and thinly slivered
1 green bell pepper, seeded and thinly slivered
2 to 4 fresh jalapeno chilies, seeded and thinly sliced (for extra hot, leave seeds in)
2 Tbs. minced pickled jalapeno chilies

Directions:
Prepare the dressing.  Combine the salt and garlic in a large mixing bowl and mash to a paste with back of the spoon.  Add the remaining dressing ingredients.
Toss the dressing with the prepared slaw and let stand for at least 1 hour and over night is best.

Submitter: 
Fooddude
Description: 
We got this from friend several year ago and we love it.  A spicey alternative to creamy slaws.  This is an excellent choice for folks who like a little 'heat' in their lives.
