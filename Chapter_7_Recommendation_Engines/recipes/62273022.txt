Title:
Spicy Good Gingersnaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/345823.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup whole wheat pastry flour
1 t baking soda
2 t ground ginger
3/4 t cinnamon
1/2 t salt
1/4 t cayenne pepper
1/4 t ground cloves
1/4 t chili powder
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup oil
1/3 cup molasses
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  In a bowl mix the flours with baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, salt, cayenne pepper cloves and chili powder.
In a large mixing bowl add sugar, oil, molasses and egg; mix until combined then mix in dry ingredients gradually until dough is formed.
Roll into 1 1/2" balls and place on a greased cookie sheet.  Bake for 12 minutes; move to cooling racks.
