Title:
Greek Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4   fresh tomatoes, chopped
1   cucumber, peeled and chopped
1   green bell pepper, cut into 1/2 ",pieces
1/2 cup  chopped red onion
1  tablespoon  red wine vinegar
1  tablespoon  balsamic vinegar
1  tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1  teaspoon salt
1  tablespoon  chopped fresh oregano
1/4 cup kalamata olive
1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled

Directions:
Mix together vinegars, oil, salt and oregano.
Add other ingredients.
Marinate several hours in refrigerator to allow flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
goodeats
Description: 
Every summer I get cravings for this zesty, cool marinated salad. I can't take credit for it as I pulled it off another recipe site years ago.
