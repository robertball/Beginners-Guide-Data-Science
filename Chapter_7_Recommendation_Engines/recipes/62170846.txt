Title:
Fettuccine Alfredo with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz fettuccine
1/2c butter
1/2c mushrooms
1c milk
1 can of cream of chicken soup
1/2c grated cheese
3 skin less chicken breasts
2tbs olive oil
2tbs crushed garlic
salt/pepper

Directions:
Pasta:
bring 4 quarts of water to a boil add 1 tablespoon salt, cook fettuccine for 10-12 mins - stir to separate pasta
drain dont rinse
set aside over medium heat
Chicken:
heat olive oil in frying pan and saute the chicken and garlic until chicken is done (no more pink)
Sauce::
In a large saucepan, melt butter saute mushrooms in butter. add milk gradually, reduce heat, cheese and soup.
In large dish (9x13) pour pasta sauce mixture and chicken together then stir
to add more flavor add salt pepper and Parmesan, you can even add some minced onion or red pepper flakes
