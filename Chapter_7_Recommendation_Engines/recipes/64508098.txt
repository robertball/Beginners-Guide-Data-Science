Title:
Mongolian BBQ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp peanut oil
1 tbsp sesame oil
1/4 head cabbage, shredded
1 whole onion, sliced
2 whole carrots, shredded
1 lb mushrooms, sliced
2 large tomatoes, quartered and sliced
1 cup bean sprouts
1/4 head nappa cabbage, shredded
1/2 lb meat, sliced very thin
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp ginger juice (from a bottle of sliced fresh ginger)
4 tsps garlic, minced with juice
2 tbsps brown sauce
1 tbsp peanut oil

Directions:
Heat peanut oil in a large skillet or wok.   Add vegetables and meat to skillet.   Pour sauces over the top and stir fry until vegetables are tender.
