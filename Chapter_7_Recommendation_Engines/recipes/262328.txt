Title:
Chipotle Tofu Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice
2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
1 (14.5 ounce) can black beans
1 (12 ounce) package firm tofu, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 cup sliced bok choy
1 tablespoon chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, chopped
1 teaspoon adobo sauce from chipotle peppers (optional)
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
salt to taste
1/2 lime, juiced
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, or more to taste

Directions:
Heat a saucepan over medium heat and add onion. Cook and stir until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic; cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add diced tomatoes, broth, beans, tofu, bok choy, chipotle peppers, adobo sauce. paprika, oregano, and salt. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until heated through and slightly thickened, about 30 minutes. Pour into bowls and add lime juice and cilantro.

Submitter: 
KenK
Description: 
Simple, tasty, and healthy tofu soup. Great with a nice hunk of artisan bread!
