Title:
J-Lyn's Awesome Salmon Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/930011.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Elbow Macaronni
2 3-oz cans Salmon
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
1/2 can Milk or Water
1/2 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/4 Cup French Fried Onions
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Boil 1 Cup Elbow Macaroni in water until al dente, drain.
Open cans of salmon and drain.
Grease a 9x9 casserole dish with your choice of butter, oil, or cooking spray.
Combine macaroni, salmon, cream of mushroom soup, 1/2 can milk, parmesan cheese, and salt and pepper in casserole dish. Mix until well blended.
Sprinkle top with 1/4 cup French Fried Onions.
Place in middle of oven and bake for 30 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
I improvised this casserole together with items I had on hand in my pantry. I've never been big on casseroles (if you've had my mom's cooking, you'd understand why), but they are definitely an easy item to use up items that would otherwise be sitting in the pantry forever.
