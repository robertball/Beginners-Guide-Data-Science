Title:
Apple Mincemeat Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (27oz) jar of None Such classic or original mincemeat pie filling
4 apples cored, peeled, and diced
2 tbl spoons of butter
2 tsp lemon juice
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1 1/2 tsp of vanilla
1 cup of chopped walnuts (optional)
1 box of pilsburry prepared double crust (in refrigerator section of the grocery store
1 egg yolk
1 tbl spoon of water

Directions:
Combine the apples, butter and lemon juice in a saucepan over medium heat and cook for about 8-10 minutes or until tender. Cool slightly. Add Sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and nutmeg to the apples. Next, add 3/4 of the None Such classic or original mincemeat pie filling to the apple mixture and set aside. May add walnuts if you like.
With one of the pie crust place in the 9" pie pan remove and excess. Fill the pie crust with the filling. Place the other pie crust over the pie and seal with a little pat of water in between. Cut 4 slits in the middle to allow to vent.
Preheat oven to 450. Beat the egg yolk with 1 tbl spoon of water. Brush the egg mixture over the pie crust. Cook the pie for the first 15 minutes then drop the temperature to 350. Remove the pie from the oven and cover the edges with aluminum foil. Place back into the oven and bake for another 30-35min or until golden brown.

Description: 
An easy and very tasty mincemeat pie with an apple base. My stepdad asks for me to make him this pie every Friday!
