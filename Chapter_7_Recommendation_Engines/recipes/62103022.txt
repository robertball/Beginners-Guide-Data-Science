Title:
Brocheribean Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c fresh broccoli
5 c cooked rice
1 (10.5 oz)can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 c milk
2 c shredded cheddar
1 (14.5 oz) can black beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper
2 t dried parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F.
Chop Broccoli heads into 1/4 to 1/2 inch chunks.
Set aside 1 cup of the cheddar cheese.
Combine broccoli, rice, cream of mushroom soup, milk, 1 c of the cheddar cheese, beans, salt, pepper, and parsley in an ungreased 9 x 13 casserole dish. Combine well, then sprinkle with remaining 1 cup of cheddar. Place in oven for 15 minutes, until cheese is melted or browned (however you like it best), and casserole is heated through.

Description: 
A fast, easy, filling and surprisingly hearty vegetarian casserole that just may get kids to eat broccoli.
