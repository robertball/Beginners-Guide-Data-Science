Title:
MA's Southern Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/862444.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large can porknbeans (3lb/slightly drained)
1/2 lb hickory smoked bacon (chopped)
1 large white or yellow onion (chopped)
1/4 cup green(Bell)pepper (minced)
2 tablespoons chopped garlic
1/3 cup catsup
1 tablespoon yellow mustard
1 tablespoon worchestire sauce
1/2 cup packed dark brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 325.
In a skillet cook bacon over med/hi heat until almost done.  Drain all except 2 tablespoons of bacon drippings. Lower heat to med, add onion and continue cooking 7-8 minutes.  Add garlic and green pepper, cook stir an additional 5 minutes.
Combine remaining ingredients in a large bowl. Add bacon mixture, mix well.  Pour into a, greased or sprayed, 9" or 10" baking dish and bake uncovered for an hour (stir once after 30min).
