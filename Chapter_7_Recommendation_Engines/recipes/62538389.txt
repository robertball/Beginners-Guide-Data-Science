Title:
Sopa Seca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (10 oz.) twisted vermicelli
4 tbs cooking oil
2 large onions chopped
1 large clove garlic, crushed
1 green pepper, chopped
2 pounds ground round
1 can (1lb) tomoatoes
Half of a 10 oz can of Rotel Tomatoes and green chilies
1 tsp chili powder
Salt and Pepper
4 cubes beef stock

Directions:
In 12" skillet, brown vermicelli "nests" (only) in 3 tablespoons of oil. Remove, drain and set aside. Add remaining 1 tbs oil to skillet and saute onion, garlic, green pepper, and ground meat. Add tomatoes, Rotel tomatoes, cumin, chili powder, salt and pepper. Simmer 10 minutes. Arrange vermicelli in 3 qt casserole; cover with meat sauce. Pour stock over all. bake, uncovered at 350 for 1 hour. Remove form oven and mix well before serving. Much better if made at least 1 day before serving. When reheating add more stock if necessary. Freezes well.

Description: 
My mother used to make this every time my husband and I came over for dinner. We haven't had it in years so I got the recipe form her. It originally came from the Houston Junior League Cookbook.
