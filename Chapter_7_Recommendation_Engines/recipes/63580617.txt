Title:
Big Ray's Beer Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1007514.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
3 Tbsp white sugar
1 Tbsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1 Tbsp diced garlic
1 tsp season salt
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 12 oz can beer  room temperature
4 oz cheddar cheese shredded
1Tbsp melted butter

Directions:
Adjust oven rack to lowest position and preheat to 425 degrees.
Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.  Add beer and mix with wooden spoon and then with hands until all dry is combined, being careful not to deflate the beer.
Gently mix in the cheese. Transfer to  a greased 12 in cast iron skillet and slit an "x" with a very sharp knife.  Brush with melted butter and bake for 20-25 minutes till golden brown.  Transfer to wire rack to cool.
