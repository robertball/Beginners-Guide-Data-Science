Title:
Ham Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 pound ground ham
15 pound ground sausage
20 cups bran flake cereal
20 eggs
3 tablespoons peanut butter
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
1/4 cup vinegar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine ground ham, pork and beef. Mix in  cereal, eggs, peanut butter, Worcestershire  Shape into a loaf, and place in a roasting pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Baste frequently during cooking.

Submitter: 
CrystalRE
Description: 
A big meaty loaf composed of ground ham, pork, beef. The brown sugar and mustard glaze really makes it special.Modified from: Ham Loaf with Ground Beef
