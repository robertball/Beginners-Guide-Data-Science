Title:
CANNELLONI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz spinach ( I use ½  pk frozen)
¼ cup onion, chopped, or more
½ pound ground beef
3 italian sausages, casings removed
1 pound ricotta cheese
2 eggs
½ cup parmesan cheese *optional
salt and pepper
1 box cannelloni (cook 8 minutes)
tomato sauce , 2 large cans of hunts
2 cloves garlic or more
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp oregano

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a pan, brown onions,  ground beef, garlic and sausage meat, drain. Cool.
Stir in ricotta eggs. Season. Gently mix in spinach .
Stuff cannelloni with the mixture.
Pour 1 cup sauce in baking dish .
Place stuffed cannelloni in dish and cover with sauce.
Sprinkle basil and oregano on top of sauce.
Sprinkle with mozzarella and bake 60 minutes at 350.Increase heat and brown 10 minutes. Let rest 10 minutes
* Cut corner off a zip lock bag to stuff pasta

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
A wonderful stuffed pasta that will have your mouth watering while waiting.
