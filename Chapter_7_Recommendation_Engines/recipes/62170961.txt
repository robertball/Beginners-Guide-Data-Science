Title:
Lemon Blueberry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box pudding included lemon cake mix
1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
1 tsp lemon zest
6 Tbsp. softened butter
1 Tbsp. lemon zest
6 cups powdered sugar
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
2-4 Tbsp. water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Set aside 1/4 cup dry cake mix in bowl.
Prepare rest of cake mix according to box directions.
Toss the blueberries with the reserved cake mix.
Mix in with the rest of the wet ingredients.
Divide batter between two greased 9 inch round pans.
Bake according to directions on box.
Let cakes cool.
Meanwhile, beat butter with powdered sugar, gradually adding.
Add in lemon juice.
Add water until you get your desired consistency.
Frost cooled cake and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jessica
Description: 
This is a wonderful cake mix cake that gets a homemade touch from frozen blueberries and a lovely lemon frosting.
