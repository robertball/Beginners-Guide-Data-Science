Title:
Morning Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 eggs, at room temperature
2 teaspoons vanilla
1/3 cup sugar
1 1/2 cup shredded carrots
1/4 cup canola or sunflower oil
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
1/2 cup sunflower seeds
2 tablespoons flax seed
2 tablespoons poppy seeds
2 tablespoons wheat germ
1/2 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
Line 6 muffin molds with paper cup liners. Combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon and sugar in a medium bowl.
In another bowl place eggs, oil, applesauce and vanilla and wisk together.
Stir into flour mixture and mix until joist moistened (don't over mix).
Then stir in carrots, seeds and dried cranberries.
Fill muffin cups 3/4 full and bake for 30 minutes or until toothpick inserted into the center comes our clean, about 30 minutes.
Cool muffins in pan for 5 minutes, then remove from pan and cool completely on a rack.

Submitter: 
Patti Smith
Description: 
This is a low fat, low sugar recipe that is loaded with lots of good stuff and tastes wonderful in the morning with a cup of hot tea or coffee. It isn't real sweet but still has lots of flavor. You can easily add or subtract items to make to your liking.
