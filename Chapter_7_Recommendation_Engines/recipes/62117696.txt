Title:
Easy As Pie Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tube crecent rolls
8 oz. diced ham, cooked sausage, or bacon cooked & crumbled
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons finely chopped onions
1 tablespoon chopped green pepper
6 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*
Unroll crescents from package.
Pat evenly into bottom and up the sides of a 1 1/2 quart casserole or pie pan.
Place the meat that you are using into unbaked shell, top with chopped vegetables and cheese.
In a medium bowl, beat eggs with a mixer until foamy.
Add seasonings and milk, mix well.
Pour over meat and cheese mixture.
Bake aprox.45 minutes until eggs are set and top is nicely browned.

Submitter: 
Billie Miller
Description: 
Versatile, easy to put together for breakfast, brunch, and beyond, Serve with a fresh fruit or green salad.
