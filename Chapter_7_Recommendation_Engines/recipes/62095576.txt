Title:
Chicken and Bell Pepper Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 4 skinless boneless chicken
breast halves
* 6 tablespoons vegetable oil
* 2 limes, juiced
* 1 tablespoon minced garlic
* 1 tablespoon worcestershire
sauce
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
* 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
* 8-6 inch diameter flour
tortillas
* 1 red onion, sliced thinly
* 1 red bell pepper, cut into
1/4 inch wide strips
* 1 green bell pepper, cut into
1/4 inch wide strips
* shredded cheddar cheese
* sour cream
* salsa

Directions:
Lightly pound chicken breasts between two pieces of waxed paper to a thickness of about 1/2 inch. Place chicken in a glass baking dish. Whisk together 3 tablespoons oil, lime juice, worcestershire sauce, garlic, cumin, salt and pepper. Pour over chicken, turning to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Wrap tortillas in foil and heat in oven until warmed through, about 10 minutes. Turn oven off and leave tortillas in oven to keep warm. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and saute' 3 minutes. Add bell peppers and saute'about 4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Place pepper and onion in a large bowl and cover with foil.
Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in the same skillet over medium-high heat. Place chicken in skillet and saute' about 3 to 4 minutes per side.
Cut chicken into 1/2 inch wide strips and mix together with vegetables. Spoon mixture into tortillas. Serve with shredded cheese, salsa and sour cream.
