Title:
Sourdough Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package frozen chopped spinach
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
.5 cup chopped green onion
1 tsp lemon juice
.5 tsp seasoning salt
.5 tsp dried parsley flakes
1 or 2 large round sourdough bread

Directions:
1. Defrost and drain spinach, chop it to fine pieces.
2. Add spinach and all remaining ingredients to a bowl. Stir well.
3. Refridgerate dip for a few hours (if possible) to blend flavours.
4. Remove top and insides of one bread roll to create a "bowl" for the dip. Chop up all remaining bread into bite-sized pieces.
5. Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lin Snow
Description: 
A sourdough breadbowl houses a delicious creamy spinach dip. Be sure to have extra bread for dipping!
