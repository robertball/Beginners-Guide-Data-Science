Title:
Kansas City Ice Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/848455.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce gin
1 fluid ounce vodka
1/2 fluid ounce lime juice
1/2 fluid ounce triple sec
3 fluid ounces lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Directions:
Combine the gin, vodka, lime juice, and triple sec in a shaker with ice. Shake, and strain into a chilled pint glass with ice. Pour in lemon-lime soda, and serve.

Submitter: 
DarkStar11
Description: 
A very refreshing drink for hot summer days. Be careful, though - it is easy to have too many of these.
