Title:
orange creamsicle cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
graham cracker crust
1/2 cup melted butter
2 tablespoons granulated sugar
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
cheesecake
2 8 oz. packages of softened cream cheese
1/2 cup granulated sugar 1 teaspoon vanilla
2large eggs
1/2 cup sour cream
orange creamsicle layer
1 3 0z. box of orange gelatin
1 1/2 cups boiling water
1 8 oz. container of whipped topping such as cool whip.

Directions:
pre heat oven to 325 degrees
in a mixing bowl combine the ingredients for the graham cracker crust and pat into a 9" springform pan then set aside.
in a medium mixing bowl whip the cream cheese , sugar, and vanilla beat the eggs in 1 at a time then beat the sour cream in, pour into the crust and bake for 40 minutes turn your oven off after the 40 minutes and crack door and leave cheesecake in for an additional 30 minutes allow to completely cool.
once cooled mix the creamsicle layer by stirring the gelatin into the boiling water until it dissolves and then gently whisk in the whipped topping until mixture is completely combined then place a plate or receptacle under the cheesecake and pour mixture over the top of cheesecake immediately place in the freezer for 1 hour then remove and refridgerate until serving its best if it has been chilled overnight.
