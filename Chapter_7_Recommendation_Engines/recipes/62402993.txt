Title:
Limeabalemon Glaciate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup crushed ice
1 banana
1/2 lime
1/2 lemon
Coconut shards or a cherry, garnish

Directions:
Blend ice and banana together on low speed, adding juice of lemon and lime to keep ice from freezing to the side of the blender.
Serve in a tall glass and garnish with coconut shards or a cherry.
Best to do this 1-2 servings at a time.

Submitter: 
SouthernSon
Description: 
Slightly bitter and extremely refreshing, this is one instance of what I have dubbed blender-miracles. Throw everything in and blend away!
