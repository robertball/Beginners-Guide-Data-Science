Title:
Pineapple Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283099.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
4 eggs
1 (20 ounce) can unsweetened crushed pineapple, drained
5 slices white bread, cubed

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in pineapple. Gently fold in bread cubes.
Spoon into a greased 2-qt. baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 35-40 minutes or until top is lightly golden. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Sandy Moyer
Description: 
Perfect for Easter Sunday, Sandy Moyer sends Pineapple Casserole. It is a wonderful side dish with ham, she writes from Gilbertsville, Pennsylvania.
