Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Squares with Chocolate Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987368.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
6 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
In large saucepan over low heat, melt margarine. Add marshmallows; stir until melted and well blended. Remove from heat.
Stir in vanilla. Add cereal, stirring until coated. Add chocolate chips, stirring until thoroughly coated.
Using lightly-buttered spatula, press into buttered 3.5 L (13 x 9-inch) pan. Cool. Cut into squares.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
Classic KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES Treats* with chocolate chips will satisfy everyone's sweet tooth--with coffee, after school or for dessert.
