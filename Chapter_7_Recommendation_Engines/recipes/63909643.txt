Title:
Chunky Monkey Crepe Filling (sugar free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. peanut butter
1 T. vegetable oil
3-4 T. Splenda
1 T. cocoa powder
1/4 c. softened chocolate ice cream
Whipped topping to taste
1 banana, chopped

Directions:
Heat peanut butter and vegetable oil for approximately one minute in microwave, just until peanut butter begins to soften.
Cream peanut butter and oil together; stir in Splenda and cocoa powder.
Mix in chocolate ice cream.
Add whipped topping to desired consistency.
Combine filling with diced banana.
Serve with basic crepe recipe from this website.

Submitter: 
Mrs.Morgan
Description: 
I created this rich crepe filling when I couldn't find another recipe for chunky monkey crepes!  Sugar free is an added bonus.
