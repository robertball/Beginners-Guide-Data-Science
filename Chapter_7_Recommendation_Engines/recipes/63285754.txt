Title:
Dried Beef Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz pkgs cream cheese
2 sm jar or pkg dried beef chopped up reserve 1 jar for rolling cheese ball in.
3 tbs chopped onion
1 large dill pickle chopped
1 tbs garlic powder
2 tbs mayonaise

Directions:
Mix all together and chill.  Form into a ball and roll in reserved dried beef.
