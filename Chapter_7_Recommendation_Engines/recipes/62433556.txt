Title:
Jocelyn Orr's White Bread (in a Bosch) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups warm water
1/2 cup honey (or sugar)
1 Tbsp. salt
2/3 cup Crisco (melted)/oil
3 Tbsp. yeast (active/dry)
8 + cups flour

Directions:
Add water, honey, salt, Crisco and 4 cups flour to Bosch. Mix together. Then add yeast with 2 more cups of flour.
Add flour (3-4 cups at a time)until dough is off the walls of the Bosch bowl.
Then mix/knead for 6 minutes on 2nd setting.
Let rise. Then punch down and form into loaves.
Let rise again. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes.
