Title:
Whole Grain Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, beaten
1 3/4 cups buttermilk
1/4 cup canola oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup whole wheat pastry flour (or 120 grams whole wheat flour + 2 Tbsp cornstarch)
1/2 cup flax seed meal (55 grams)
1/4 cup wheat germ (30 grams)
1/4 cup all-purpose flour (35 grams)
1 Tbsp + 1 rounded 1/4 tsp baking powder
0.875 tsp baking soda
2 Tbsp brown sugar (30grams)
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. In a separate bowl, whisk together the whole wheat pastry flour, cornstarch, flax seed meal, wheat germ, all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and salt.  Blend two mixtures until batter is smooth.
Cook on waffle iron that's been liberally buttered.

Submitter: 
Melissa Gardner
Description: 
This filling waffles made with whole wheat flour, flax seed meal, and wheat germ are quite delicious. Serve with homemade blueberry syrup.Modified from: Whole Grain Waffles
