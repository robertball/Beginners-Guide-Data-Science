Title:
Minnie's Chicken Piccata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/925946.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Whole Chicken Breasts, Skinned, Boned, and halved
1/4 Cup Flour
4 Tablespoons of Butter
1 Tablespoon oil
1 Teaspoon of pepper
1 Teaspoon of salt
3 Tablespoons of Lemon Juice
1/4 Cup White Wine
1 Tablespoon of Butter
3 Tablespoons of Parsley
4 Thin Slices of Lemon

Directions:
Flatten Chicken Breasts with Mallet between sheets of wax paper, then sprinkle lightly with Flour.
In a skillet, heat 4 Tablespoons of butter with one tablespoon of oil.
When Hot and Foaming, saute breasts for 2 - 3 minutes on each side until browned. Remove and keep warm.
Add Salt, Pepper, lemon juice and white wine to skillet.  Stir and deglaze to dissolve all brown particles; if needed add a spoonful of cold water.
Add remaining butter and parsley to skillet, blend and return breasts to pan and heat through, simmering until chicken juices run clear.
Place chicken on Serving platter, spoon pan juices over them and top with lemon slices.
