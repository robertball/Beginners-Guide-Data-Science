Title:
Mofongo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large green plantains
1/3 cup oil for frying
1 smallish onion, diced
8 ( or more !! ) fat garlic cloves, completely crushed
1 medium size bag of fried pork rinds, crushed fine
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 13oz. can chicken broth
1/3 stick margerine or butter

Directions:
Peel the plantians and cut into 1 inch pieces.
Place plantains chunks in heated oil and saute until golden.
Remove the plantains from the oil and set aside.
Saute the onion and garlic next in the heated oil.
A few at a time, use a fork to crush and mash the plantain pieces to the texture of corn bread stuffing.
Add the pork rinds to the plantains, and mix in the onion, garlic and salt.
Pour the can of chicken broth over the plantain mixture, dot with pieces of butter ( or margerine ) and cover tightly in casserole dish.
place in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes
Serve with roast pork or chicken but delicious all on it's own!!!

Submitter: 
steph
Description: 
From Puerto Rico to Cuba, mofongo is an extremely savory side dish or stuffing that accompanies pork, sea food and chicken making any meal a heartfelt home experience.
