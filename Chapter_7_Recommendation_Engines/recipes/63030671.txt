Title:
Coconut Macaroons IV Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg whites
1 1/2 cup white sugar
1 bag sweetened flaked coconut
1 1/4 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lay parchment paper onto cookie sheets
In a large glass or metal bowl, whip egg whites and vanilla to soft peaks. Add sugar into egg whites in small doses while mixing.  Mix until whites for hard peaks. Fold in coconut. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto the prepared cookie sheets.
Bake for 15 minutes in the preheated oven, or until coconut is golden.

Submitter: 
Lisa Combest
Description: 
This is a recipe my aunt made in the 30's. A meringue cookie with coconut and cornflake cereal. I could never eat enough of them.Modified from: Coconut Macaroons IV
