Title:
Tomato Aspic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 envelope or 1 Tbsp unflavoured gelatin
¼ C boiling water
2 C vegetable juice e.g. V-8
1 bay leaf
2 cloves
½ C chopped celery
½ C chopped celery
Worcestershire Sauce (optional-to taste)

Directions:
Fully dissolve gelatin in ¼ C boiling water
Heat to boiling the vegetable juice with the bay leaf and cloves
Allow to simmer for 10 minutes
Remove seasonings
Add chopped onion and celery to jiuce mix
Pour over dissolved gelatin
Stin gently until fully blended
Refigerate until set and cool
Serve as a side dish

Submitter: 
Barbara Zavadil
Description: 
This is a colourful addition to a summer salad plate and goes nicely with my Ham Salad for Two. Very tasty and light.
