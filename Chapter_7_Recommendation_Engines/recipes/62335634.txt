Title:
Creamy Buffalo Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C mayonaise
2 T buffalo sauce (like Franks Red Hot)
1 t honey

Directions:
Mix well and serve!

Submitter: 
MRSPARKERII
Description: 
I came up with this recipe to serve with chicken fingers.  I was tired of honey mustard, but didnt want all of the heat of straight buffalo sauce.  Heres my creamy and slightly sweet version that is a perfect dipper for anything from chicken fingers to fried shrimp!
