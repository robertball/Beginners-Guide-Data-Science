Title:
Fried Chicken Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065874.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 cups Italian-style seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 eggs, beaten
2 tablespoons water
24 chicken tenderloins
2 quarts oil for frying
1 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons prepared horseradish
1/2 cup sour cream
1 dash Worcestershire sauce
3 tablespoons mustard

Directions:
Place flour in a shallow bowl.
Place breadcrumbs in a separate shallow bowl, and mix with pepper and cayenne pepper.
Place eggs and water in a small bowl.
One piece at a time, coat the chicken in the flour, the eggs and the bread crumbs, and set aside.
Heat oil in a deep fryer to 375Â°.
In small batches, fry chicken 6 to 8 minutes, or until golden brown.
Remove chicken, and drain on paper towels.
In a separate small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, horseradish, sour cream, Worcestershire sauce and mustard.
Mix well, and serve with the chicken for dipping.
