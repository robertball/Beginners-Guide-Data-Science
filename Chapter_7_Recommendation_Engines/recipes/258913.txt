Title:
Refreshingly Tart Lemonade Slushie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4538370.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large lemons, peeled and seeded
10 ice cubes
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup white sugar, or to taste

Directions:
Place lemons, ice cubes, water, and white sugar in a blender; blend until a slushie consistency is reached, about 1 minute.
