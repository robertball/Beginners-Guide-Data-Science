Title:
Gourmet Vegan Flourless Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/962487.jpg
Ingredients:
For Chocolate:
1 cup Cacao Butter
6-8 Tablespoons Cacao powder
2 Tablespoons Agave
pinch of salt
1/3 cup choc. chips
1/2 cup Cacao powder
2 1/4 cup almond flour
3/4  teaspoon raw vanilla powder
4 Chia Seed Eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 cup Agave
1/2 cup raw sugar
For the Sauce:
In blender:
2/3 cup raspberries,
5 tablespoons agave
3 tablespoon water

Directions:
Pre heat oven 375
Make Chia Eggs (4 tablespoons Chia Seeds + 1 cup boiling water let stand 5 minutes, in blender put 4 tablespoons lecithin granules)
Add Agave, vanilla and raw sugar blend in blender.
To Make Chocolate:
In Double Boiler:
Melt Cacao Butter
Add 6-8 tablespoons Cacao powder, agave, salt stir till fully blended
Add Choc Chips melt and blend
In large bowl: Mix together Almond flour, Cacao Powder and Vanilla
Combine wet and dry ingredients mix thoroughly.
Bake in a round cake oiled pan.
Make the sauce:
In a sauce pan heat dried raspberries and agave over low flame, stirring.
Bake for 35-40 minutes
