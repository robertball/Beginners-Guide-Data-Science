Title:
3 - 2 - 1 Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds powdered sugar
2 pounds peanut Butter
1 pound stick butter

Directions:
In large bowl mix powdered and peanut butter until crumbly then set aside.Put wax paper on cookie sheet.In saucepan melt your butter.Pour melted butter over peanut butter mixture.Mix well then pat onto cookie sheet and cool for one hour cut and keep in air tight containers.

Submitter: 
Beadlady2
Description: 
I got this recipe from a friend and it's become our favorite holiday candy. Also makes enough to give as gifts.
