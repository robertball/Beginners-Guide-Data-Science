Title:
Marinated Berry Fizz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984276.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons honey
1 teaspoon grated lemon rind
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
2 cups fresh blackberries
2 cups fresh raspberries
2 cups fresh blueberries
2 cups sliced peeled peaches
2 2/3 cups Asti Cinzano or other sweet sparkling wine
16 amaretti cookies

Directions:
Combine first 3 ingredients in a 2-cup glass measure; microwave at high 11/2 minutes or until the mixture boils. Cover and chill.
Combine the blackberries, raspberries, blueberries, and peaches in a large bowl. Add the honey mixture, stirring well. Cover and marinate in refrigerator at least 4 hours.
Spoon the fruit mixture into each of 8 bowls, and add the wine. Serve with amaretti cookies.

Submitter: 
Cooking Light magazine
Description: 
A virtually fat-free indulgence: Succulent berries and ripe peaches in a 'marinade' of honey, lemon, and sweet sparkling wine.
