Title:
Chocolate Oatmeal Coconut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks unsalted butter
1 cup brown sugar packed
6 tablespoons sugar
2 large eggs
1½ teaspoons vanilla
½ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon baking soda
1 cup flour
2¼ cup oats
1½ cups coconut
12 ounces semi-sweet mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°F. Beat butter and sugars at high speed until fluffy. Add eggs and beat until blended. Beat in vanilla, baking soda, and salt. Add flour and mix at low speed until blended. Stir in oats, coconut and chocolate.
Arrange ¼ cup of dough in mounds about 3 inches apart on large buttered baking sheets. Pat down to ½ inch. Bake in upper oven, rotating occasionally, for 15 to 18 minutes. Remove and cool for 1 minute before transferring to cooling rack. Enjoy!
