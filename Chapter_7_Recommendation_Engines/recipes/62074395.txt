Title:
Sinfully Rich Rice Pudding Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cream OR half-and-half
4 eggs, slightly beaten
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup unsweetened baking cocoa
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup cooked rice (prefer arborio), cooled
4 oz. semisweet chocolate chopped
Chocolate-Molasses Sauce
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tbsp cornstarch
1/3 cup water
3 tbsp chocolate syrup
1 tbsp molasses

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. In large bowl, beat together eggs, cream, sugar, cocoa and vanilla with a rotary beater. Stir in rice and chopped chocolate. Pour mixture into a 2 quart casserole. Place dish in 13 X 9 pan set on oven rack. Carefully pour in 1 inch of boiling water into the 13 X 9 pan to create a water bath for the pudding. Bake, uncovered for 55 to 65 minutes or until a knife inserted near center comes out clean. Prepare Chocolate-Molasses sauce by stirring together in a saucepan the brown sugar and cornstarch and then stirring in the water and chocolate syrup and the molasses. Cook this mixture over medium/low heat for 2 minutes or til thick and bubbly. Serve this sauce over the rice pudding, and top with whipped cream if you like.

Description: 
A decadent rice pudding delight that features cream, cocoa, semisweet chocolate and a Molasses-Chocolate Sauce. A real comfort food with some whipped heavy cream on top!!
