Title:
Frozen Caramel Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of strong brewed coffee
1 cup of milk
2 Tablespoons of Sugar or 3 Packets of artificial sweetener
3 Tablespoons Caramel Syrup
1 Cup Ice
Whipped Cream (optional)
Caramel Syrup for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Combine milk, coffee, sugar and syrup into shaker cup.  Shake well until combined.
Place in freezer for 2 hours or until sides of mixture begin to freeze.
Pour mixture into blender making sure any frozen parts of mixture is added.  Add ice.  Blend until smooth.
Pour into 2 glasses.  Garnish with whipped cream and drizzle additional caramel syrup over whipped cream.  Serve with a straw.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
I was addicted to the frozen coffee drinks at a popular coffee chain.  After moving to an area where I couldn't get these drinks I decided to create my own at a fraction of the price.  I actually think these are better!
