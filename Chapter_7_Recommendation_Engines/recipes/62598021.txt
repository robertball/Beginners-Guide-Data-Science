Title:
Marinated Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
1/2 lb baby carrots
1/2 head broccoli
1 red pepper
1 shallot, wedged
2T apple cider vinegar
2T Dijon mustard
1/2t salt
1/8t pepper
2T olive oil

Directions:
Boil salted water in skillet.
Cook cauliflower for 3 minutes and remove.
Cook carrots for 3 minutes and remove.
Add broccoli and pepper, cook 2 minutes and drain.
Combine with all other ingredients in large bowl and toss to coat.
Refrigerate before serving.
