Title:
South Philly Peppers and Eggs (and Potato) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/495915.jpg
Ingredients:
8 eggs
1 green bell pepper, diced
1 large potato, peeled, diced
2 tbs butter
4 crusty italian rolls
ketchup for condiment
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
melt two tablespoonfuls of butter in a skillet
add potatoes and peppers, cover and let soften. do not brown peppers
when soft, beat eggs with salt and pepper
pour eggs over vegetables and let set before scambling to make large curds.
while still hot, pile onto crusty italian rolls, top with ketchup
