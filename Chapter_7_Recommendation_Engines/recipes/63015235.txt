Title:
Reindeer Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/754233.jpg
Ingredients:
One box cake mix any flavor
Bag of red and green M&M;'s
2 bags chocolate covered pretzels
1 can of store bought icing
bag of chocolate chips
brown food coloring

Directions:
Bake cupcakes according to package directions. While cupcakes cool color your store bought icing until you get the right shade of brown. Once cupcakes are completely cooled cover in brown icing, add two pretzels standing up to make ears, add two chocolate chips to make eyes, then add red M&M for nose.
