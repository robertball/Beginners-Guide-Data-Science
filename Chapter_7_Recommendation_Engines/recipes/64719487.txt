Title:
Hearty Lentil Pumpkin Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6316317.jpg
Ingredients:
4 C Sliced Onion
1 Stick  Butter
2 C Dried Lentils
10 Vegetable Bullion Cubes
5 C Water
3 C Canned Pumpkin
1/4 t Marjoram
1/2 t ground thyme
4 t curry powder
1 t Franks Red Hot
2 C light cream
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute onion in butter, stir in lentils, bullion, pumpkin, spices and Franks Red Hot.  Simmer 1 1/2 hours or until lentils are very tender.  Use a immersion blender to puree.  Reheat slowly and then add cream.  Season to taste.
