Title:
Lentils & Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Onions
3 Cloves Garlic
2 Celery Stalks
2 Carrots
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1 1/2 cups lentils
1 bunch greens Swiss Chard
2 cups frozen Spinach
2 Un-Chicken Bouillons
1 Tablespoon Chevril
1 Tablespoon Basil
6 cups water
1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Directions:
Chop Onions, Garlic & Celery
Saute in Olive Oil until onion is translucent
In pot, combine,water, lentils, carrots, onion mixture, frozen Spinach, Bouillons, salt, basil.
Bring to a bowl, reduce heat and cook for 30 minutes.
Add Chevril and Swiss Chard.   cook until done.
Serve over rice.
