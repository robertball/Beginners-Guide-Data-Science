Title:
Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1 med sized onion (finely diced)
1/4 cup flour
1 tsp dry mustard
1 can chicken broth
2- 8oz jars clam juice (I used “Snows”)
1/2 cup shredded carrots
3 med sized potatoes (peel and cut into small pcs)
4 (6.5 oz) cans chopped clams (juice and all, don’t drain)
1/2 tsp dill weed
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1-2 cups half and half
1-2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
Salt & pepper (to taste)
Celery leaves (for garnish)

Directions:
Sauté onion and celery in butter for several minutes (until tender)
Stir in Flour and dry mustard and continue to sauté for a few minutes
Add clam juice, chicken broth stir until smooth
Add potatoes &amp; carrots and simmer until tender (15 minutes)
Add canned clams (juice and all) simmer another 10 minutes
Add dill weed &amp; cayenne
Add half and half and cheddar cheese, stir until smooth
Season with salt and pepper (to taste)
Sprinkle with finely chopped celery leaves

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
I wanted a recipe I could make year around.  I used Snows brand chopped clams and juice.
