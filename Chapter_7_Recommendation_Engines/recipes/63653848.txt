Title:
Skillet Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 small baked potatoes
6 eggs
1/2 c shredded cheese
1/4 c FD spinach
1/2 c salsa
1-2 T baco bits
rosemary to taste
salt to taste
pepper to taste
lawrys to taste

Directions:
Chop potatoes.  Put in skillet with olive oil. Toss with herbs.  Set aside use skillet to cook eggs.  Add salsa and spinach and potatoes and cheese.  Stir until melted.  Serve.
