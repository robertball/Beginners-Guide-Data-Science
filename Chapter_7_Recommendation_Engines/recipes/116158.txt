Title:
Chunky Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1008898.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup creamy peanut butter
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup quick-cooking oats
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup raisins
1 cup dry roasted peanuts, chopped
1 cup flaked coconut

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars. Beat in peanut butter. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Combine the flour, oats, baking powder, salt and baking soda; gradually add to creamed mixture. Stir in the chocolate chips, raisins, peanuts and coconut.
Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake at 350 degrees F for 12-14 minutes or until golden brown. Cool for 2 minutes before removing to wire racks.
