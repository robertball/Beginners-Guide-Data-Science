Title:
Apple Brie Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large apple, cored and cut into ½-Inch wedges
4 tablespoons maple syrup(divided)
8 cups fresh baby spinach
1 round (8 ounces) Brie o’ Camembert cheese, rind removed and cubed
½ cup pecan halves, toasted
¼ cup apple cider or juice
¼ cup vegetable oil
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 garlic clove, minced

Directions:
Place apples on ungreased baking sheet; brush with 2 spoons of the syrup. Broil 3 to 4 Inches from heat for3 minutes.  Turn; brush with remaining syrup. Broil 3 to 5 minutes longer or until crisp tender.
In large salad bowl, combine spinach, cheese, pecans and apples.
In small saucepan, combine cider or juice, oil, mustard and garlic; bring to a boil. Pour over salad; toss to coat Serve immediately.

Description: 
The first recipe is from the new “The Taste of Home Cookbook” (Relman Media Group, $29.95) for a salad featuring the classic pairing of Brie and apples.

Makes 10 servings
