Title:
Chicken, Sweet Potato, Spinach Lime Summery Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons soy sauce
5 Tablespoons fresh lime juice (split, into 3T and 2T)
2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 garlic clove, minced
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
1 small-med sweet potato
1/2 red onion
Olive Oil

Directions:
Mix together soy, 3T lime juice, worcestershire, garlic - marinate chicken in it and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 425
Cut S. Potatoes into wedges and thinly slice onion, toss with Olive Oil, S&P. Bake for 20-25 min.
Wash Spinach, preheat grill.
Mix Spinach, Sweet Potatoes, Onions, and 2T Lime.
Grill Chicken (about 10 min)
Slice and serve over the salad.
