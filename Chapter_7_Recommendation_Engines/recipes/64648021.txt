Title:
Apple-Raisin Peanut Butter Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup unpeeled, diced apple
1/4 cup raisins
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a small bowl, stir together the ingredients.
If too stiff to spread add a little apple cider and blend ingredients together.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This spread from Breaking the Vicious Cycle can be used as dip.
