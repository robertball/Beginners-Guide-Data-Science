Title:
Date Night Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz (1.25 lb) 90% Lean Hamburger
5 Poblano Peppers (roasted, skinned and seeded)
5 oz. Goat Cheese
12 Pitted Medjool Dates
1 Egg
1/4 cup Plain Bread Crumbs
1/4 cup, + 2 tbsp Jack Daniels BBQ Sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine peppers and dates into food processor, pulse until well combined and dates are fully incorporated into the peppers.
Mix remaining ingredients (except 2 tbsp BBQ sauce) into mixing bowl or kitchen aid and combine.
Spray non-stick coating into single bread pan.
Pour meat mixture into bread pan and spread evenly.
Cover with remaining 2 tbsp of BBQ sauce.
Bake in oven for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Tera Willingham
Description: 
This meatloaf steps above in flavor and juiciness. With the spice of the peppers, sweetness in the dates, and the tang from the goat cheese it is the perfect combination. I am sure you will love it!
