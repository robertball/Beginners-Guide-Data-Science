Title:
Apple Fritter Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 2/3 c Sugar
1# Butter
8ea Eggs
1T Vanilla
6c All Purpose Flour
1T Baking Soda
2c Milk
4c Cubed Apple Pie Filling

Directions:
Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.  Add eggs and vanilla until just incorporated and scrape down the bowl. Add flour and baking soda, mix until just incorporated then add in the milk while the mixer is running. Remove from mixer and fold the apple pie filling in by hand.
Bake at 350 for at least 45 min.

Description: 
A light variation of pound cake with the addition of apples
