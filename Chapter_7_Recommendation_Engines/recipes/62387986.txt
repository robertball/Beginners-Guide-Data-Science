Title:
Zesty Garden Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of crisp iceberg lettuce
1/2 cup of sweet corn
1/2 cup of sugar snap peas
1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard
1 sprig of dill (optional)
1/3 cup of edamame beans
1 tablespoon of diced green oninon
2 tablespoons of shredded cheddar cheese
A dash of salt and a pinch of pepper

Directions:
Combine Dijon mustard, dill(optional), cheese, and onion in salad bowl. Add lettuce, corn, peas, and edamame. Toss gently for thirty seconds and add salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
MiniChef
Description: 
An excellent, refreshing salad discovered one hot summer day. If you're tired, hot, or just need a little spice in your life, it won't take more than fifteen minutes to create this classic pick-me-up.
