Title:
Hearty Italian Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hot Italian sausage
1 medium red or green bell pepper, diced
1 medium onion, diced
28 oz. can diced tomatoes
2 8 oz. cans tomato sauce
2 8 oz. cans water
1 T or 3 cubes chicken bouillon
3/4 tsp. garlic salt
3/4 c. uncooked shell macaroni
shredded mozzarella for topping

Directions:
Brown sausage, onion, and pepper together; drain.  Stir in remaining ingredients except macaroni and cheese and simmer on low at least 15 minutes.  When ready to serve, add dry macaroni and simmer until tender.  This soup improves on sitting in fridge.  Top individual servings with shredded mozzarella.
