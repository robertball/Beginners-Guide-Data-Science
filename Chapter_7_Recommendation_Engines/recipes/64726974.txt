Title:
Baked BBQ Rodeo Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6131998.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz tortilla chips
1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
2 cups precooked pulled pork
3/4 cup BBQ sauce
1/8 of red onion, finely sliced
1/2 cup chopped Bread and Butter Pickles

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2. Spread the tortilla chips across the bottom of a baking sheet.  Sprinkle cheese on chips, then spread pork evenly on top. Spoon BBQ sauce over pork and top with red onions.
3. Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes. Top with chopped pickles and serve.

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
This recipe takes BBQ pork to a different setting in quick, tasty nachos baked in 5 minutes.
