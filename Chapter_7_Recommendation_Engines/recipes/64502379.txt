Title:
Golden herb orzo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups orzo
1 tbs butter
1tsp turmeric
1 tbs salt free herb mix (like Mrs. Dash  garlic and herb flavor)
3 1/2 cups chicken broth
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
In a medium pot melt butter and add orzo. Cook until golden.
Mix in turmeric and herb mix.
Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium and  continue to  cook on a high simmer until broth is  reduced and orzo is tender.
Salt and pepper to taste.
