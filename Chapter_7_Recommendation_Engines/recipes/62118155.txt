Title:
LUSH LEMON PECAN TART Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 1/2-cups flour
* 1 1/2-sticks margarine or butter
* 2/3-cup finely crushed pecans
* 1/2-cup pecan pieces
* 1-8 oz pkg cream cheese,softened
* 1-cup powdered sugar
* 3-cups milk
* 1 1/2-tsp lemon juice
* 1-16 oz container whipped topping
* 3- 3.4 oz pkg Instant lemon pudding

Directions:
1.  Melt butter
2. Mix flour and crushed pecans together
3. Blend in butter to form a dough
4. Spray a 9x13 pan with non-stick
5. Spread pecan dough in 9x13 pan
6. Place in a preheated oven at 350 deg, for 20 minutes or until lightly browned and cookie like
7. Allow to cool
8. Blend cream cheese, 1 cup whipped topping and powdered sugar in a large bowl
9. Blend lemon pudding, milk and lemon juice into the bowl with cream cheese etc
10. Beat until thick
11. Spread pudding mixture over top of pecan crust.
12. Cover with balance of whipped topping
13. Sprinkle 1/2 cup pecan pieces over top

Submitter: 
POPS OHIO
Description: 
Buttery pecan crust. Lemon cream cheese pudding topped with whipped topping and pecans. Sure to please your family for generations.
