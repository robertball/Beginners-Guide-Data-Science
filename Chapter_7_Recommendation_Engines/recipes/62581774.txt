Title:
Crescent Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can crescent rolls
1 lb sausage
6 eggs
2 c grated mozzarella cheese
2 T picante sauce
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Press rolls into a 9*13 in pan. Brown & drain sausage & sprinkle across rolls. Beat eggs w/ salt, pepper & picante sauce. Pour over the sausage. Sprinkle top w/ cheese & bake @ 350 till set, 25-30 min.
