Title:
Insalata Caprese II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced 1/4 inch thick
1/3 cup fresh basil leaves
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 clove fresh garlic, minced
balsamic vinegar to taste
fine sea salt to taste (optional)
freshly ground black pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
Evenly distribute minced garlic on tomato slices.  Arrange slices on platter & drizzle with balsamic vinegar.  Top tomato slices with  mozzarella cheese slices and basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil. Season with sea salt and pepper.
