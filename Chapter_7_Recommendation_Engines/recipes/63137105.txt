Title:
Banana Buckwheat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Buckwheat groats
1/2 cup Water
Sea Salt
1 Banana, mashed
1/2-1 Tbl. Coconut oil
1/8 tsp. Cinnamon
1/8 cup Golden Flaxseed, ground (optional)
1/2 Tbl. Chia seeds (optional)

Directions:
Bring Buckwheat to a boil in 1/2 a cup of water with a pinch of salt.
While Buckwheat is cooking, mix Banana, Coconut oil, Cinnamon, Flaxseed and Chia seeds in a small bowl.
Cook Buckwheat until water is mostly absorbed (around 15 minutes). Remove from heat.
Add Buckwheat to banana mixture and stir until everything is combined.

Submitter: 
halfnotes
Description: 
Buckwheat is a delicious, chewy seed that can be used in place of rice in many dishes.  Because it has a different flavor than most people are used to, adding a banana makes the transition a little easier.
This is a dish that my kids request on a regular basis.
