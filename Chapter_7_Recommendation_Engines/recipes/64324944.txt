Title:
Tomato and Chick Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup diced mushrooms
1/4 cup diced garlic
1/2 cup diced onions
1/2 cup diced tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped carrots
I can tomato puree
1/4 tsp. oregano, basil, chives, parsley, red pepper, salt (fresh or ground)
1/2 cup of cooked/steamed chick peas

Directions:
Drizzle olive oil in a pot and saute onions, mushrooms, garlic, and tomatoes until browned.    Turn heat to very low and blend together carrots, tomato puree, and the rest of the mushrooms, onions, garlic, and tomato together in a blender until semi-smooth.  Combine chick peas and mixture in blender to sauted veggies and mushrooms, turn heat up to medium and stir for a few minutes until soup begins to boil.  Turn heat down to a simmer and add herbs and spices.  Let sit for about 15 minutes and enjoy with bread or crackers.
