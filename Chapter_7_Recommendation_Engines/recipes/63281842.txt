Title:
Scrumtious Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/863547.jpg
Ingredients:
•	1 cup butter, softened
•	1 cup white sugar
•	1 cup packed brown sugar
•	2 teaspoons vanilla extract
•       2 eggs
•	3 cups all-purpose flour
•	1 teaspoon baking soda
•	2 teaspoons hot water
•	1/2 teaspoon salt
•	2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
•	1 cup chopped walnuts (if desirable)
•	Any desirable toppings (M&Ms, etc.)

Directions:
1.	Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2.	Cream together the white sugar, the butter, and the brown sugar until the batter is soft. Beat in each egg one at a time, and then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water. Add to batter with the salt. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfulls onto ungreased pans two inches apart.
3.	Bake for about 10-15 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned. Cool on rack before eating your delicious snack!
