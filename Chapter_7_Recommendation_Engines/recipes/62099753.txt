Title:
Apple Avocado Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups romaine lettuce, cut up
1 avocado, chopped or sliced
1 apple, sliced
1 cup sliced celery
2 hard boiled eggs, sliced
a few tablespoons vegetable oil
1-2 tablespoons italian spices
same amount of oil of lemon juice

Directions:
Toss lettuce, avocado, apple, celery and eggs in a serving bowl. In another small bowl, whisk vegetable oil, italian spices and lemon juice. Add to salad and toss again.

Submitter: 
JULICHICKY
Description: 
The ingredients in this salad come together to create a healthy yet slightly sweet main or side dish. Very tasty! The serving number is intended if the salad is served as the main meal.
