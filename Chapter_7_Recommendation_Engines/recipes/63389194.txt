Title:
Susan's Vinaigrette Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBSP apple cider vinegar
3 TBSP canola oil
3 TBSP sugar
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp poppy seed
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp hot pepper sauce (optional)
4 cups shredded green cabbage
2 medium shredded carrots

Directions:
Combine vinegar, oil, sugar, mustard, poppy seed, pepper, salt, and hot pepper sauce (optional) in bowl.  Whisk until well blended.
In large bowl combine cabbage and carrots.  Pour vinaigrette over cabbage and carrots.  Stir to coat well.  Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
