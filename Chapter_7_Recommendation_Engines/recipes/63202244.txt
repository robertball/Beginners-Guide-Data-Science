Title:
Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 large potatoes
1 stick lactose free/gluten free margarine (soy or coconut)
1/4 c Lactaid milk
Seas Salt
Coarse ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Bring water to a boil. Peel the potatoes and chunk into equal size pieces. Boil the pototoes until you can stick a fork into a chunk. Drain the water from the pan, add 1/2 stick margarine and allow to melt. Add in 1/8 cup milk, dash of salt and 1/8 tsp pepper. Beat with electric beaters on low for 30 seconds, then med/high for 1-2 min. Taste and adjust as needed by adding more milk, margarine, and/or salt.
