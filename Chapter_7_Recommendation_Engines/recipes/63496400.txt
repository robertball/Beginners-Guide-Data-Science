Title:
Oreo White Chocolate Mousse Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (1 b 4 oz) pkg holiday oreos divided
6 T butter, melted
1 envelope knox unflavored gelatin
1 1/4 cups milk divided
1 (11oz) pkg white chocolate chips
1 pint heavy cream, whipped
Raspberries for garnish

Directions:
1. Finely crush 24 cookies.  Mix crushed cookies & butte, press on bottom & 1 inch up side of 9 inch springform pan.
2. Sprinkle gelatin over milk in saucepan; let stand 1 minute.  Stir over low heat about 3 mins or until gelatin completely dissolves.
3. Add white choco chips, continue heating until chips are melted and smooth. Refrig 30 mins o until slightly thickened.
4. Coarselt chop 24 cookies. Gently fold cookies & whipped cream into cooled choco mixture.  Spoon into crust. Refrig 6 hrs or overnight.
5. Halve remaining cookies. Garnish with cookies & raspberries.
