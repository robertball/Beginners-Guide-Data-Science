Title:
Paleo Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large Eggplant sliced into 1/4 inch thick slices
1 1/2 c non-gmo canned tomato sauce or spaghetti sauce
1 pound Italian sausage cooked
1/2 c shredded almond cheese and Parmesan
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat broiler
Line 8x8 dish with foil spray wihth cooking spray
Place eggpant slices in even layer on baking sheet spray with olive oil cooking spray and sprinkle with salt. Broil for 7 minutes until browned about 5 min
Meanwhile measure tomato sauce and combine with Italian sausage. In the prepared baking dish layer 1/2 c sauce, 1/2 the eggplant slices, 1/2 c sauce 1/4 c cheese, 1/2 the eggplant  slices, 1/2 c sauce and 1/4 c cheese. bake at 425 for 25 minutes
