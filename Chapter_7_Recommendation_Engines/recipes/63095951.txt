Title:
Mediterranean Chicken and Spinach Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Boneless chicken breasts
1/4 tsp salt and pepper
2 tbsp olive oil
8 oz cremini mushrooms,thinly sliced
1 tsp oregano
1/3 cup dry white wine
1/3 cup chicken broth
4 cups packed baby spinach
12 oz spaghettini
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with half salt and pepper. In large nonstick skille1 tbsp of the oil over medium-high heat; brown chicken 4 minutes.  Remove and keep warm.
Heat remaining oil in skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms and oregano; cook until mushrooms are slightly softened, about 4 minutes.
Pour in wine and chicken broth, scraping up browned bits.  Add chicken and remaining salt and pepper. Simmer until chicken is no longer pink inside, about 5 minutes.
Stir in spinach until wilted.
Meanwhile, in large pot of lightly salted boiling water, cook pasta, about 5 minutes.  Drain and return to pot, reserving 1/3 cup of the cooking liquid.  Add chicken mixture and reserved liquid; toss to combine.  Serve topped with feta cheese.
