Title:
Old Fashioned Pressure Cooker Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5279004.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup long grain rice (not instant)
3  1/3 cups whole milk
1/2 cup granulated sugar
2 large eggs
1 teaspoon of vanilla
1/4 cup raisins
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Set pressure cooker on sauté.
Add milk and sugar into the pressure cooker.
Warm mixture, stirring continuously until sugar dissolves - 3-4 minutes.
Turn pressure cooker off.
Add rice.
Pressure cook for 15 minutes.
Let pressure naturally release for at least 10 minutes.
Beat eggs in a bowl.
Remove lid of pressure cooker, and scoop a cup of the pudding mixture out.
Add it to the beaten eggs, whisking until completely blended.
Pour mixture back into the pressure cooker.
Add rasins, cinnamon  and vanilla.
Turn pressure cooker back on sauté and cook 3 minutes until mixture thickens.
Serve warm or chilled.
