Title:
LOW  FAT  ARTICHOKE  DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. nonfat plain yogurt
1/2 c. fat free mayonnaise
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 diced onions
3 cans artichokes, chopped
Dash hot sauce, optional
2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c. Romano cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and bake in casserole dish for 20 to 30 minutes at 325 degrees.
