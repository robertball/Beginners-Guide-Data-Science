Title:
Healthy Bananaberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 roasted almonds
1/2 cup sliced carrots (or 6 baby carrots)
3/4 cup frozen wild blueberries
1 banana cut into slices and frozen
1 tbsp flaxseed
2 oz soft tofu
1 cup orange juice
1 cup vanilla soy milk

Directions:
Place all the ingredients in a blender.
Blend on low until carrots, almonds, and the frozen fruit are ground.  Blend on medium until smooth.

Submitter: 
Tikeren
Description: 
This naturally sweet smoothie is a delicious way to get your daily dose of soy, anti-oxidant rich berries, citrus, beta-carotene, nuts, and flaxseed!
