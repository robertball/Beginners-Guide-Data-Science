Title:
Open Face Leftover Thanksgiving "Sloppy Joes" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2883240.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5-2 cups chopped turkey (breast is best)
1.5-2 cups stuffing (sausage/apple/walnut is good)
3/4 cups turkey gravy
Rolls to serve on

Directions:
Combine turkey, stuffing, & gravy in microwave safe bowl.
Heat in microwave until hot.
Serve over open rolls.
Enjoy.
