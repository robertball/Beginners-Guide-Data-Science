Title:
SPICY BAKED SHRIMP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup 	olive oil
2 Tbsp.	Cajun or Creole seasoning
2 Tbsp.	fresh lemon juice
2 Tbsp.	chopped fresh parsley
1 Tbsp.	honey
1 Tbsp.	soy sauce
Pinch 		cayenne pepper
1 lb.		uncooked large shrimp, shelled, deveined
garnish		lemon wedges
as needed	French bread

Directions:
Combine first 7 ingredients in 9x13-inch baking dish. Add shrimp and toss to coat. Refrigerate 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 450Â°F. Bake until shrimp are cooked through, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes. Garnish with lemon wedges and serve shrimp with French bread.
