Title:
Vanilla Glaze for Momma's Wine Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups icing sugar
2 1/2 tablespoons milk
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon white corn cyrup

Directions:
Melt the butter and add to rest of ingredients.
Mix until creamy.
You may have to add more milk if the consistency is too thick.
