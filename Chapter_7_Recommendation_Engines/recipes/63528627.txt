Title:
Pork Flautas with Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shredded Pork (seasoned with taco seasoning) (precook in crockpot)
Green peppers (chopped)
Onions (chopped)
Refried beans
Shredded cheese
Salsa
Flour tortillas (halved)
Mangos (diced)
Olive oil

Directions:
Heat olive oil in skillet.
Place refried beans, salsa, pork, onions, green pepers, and cheese on tortilla.
Roll tortilla and secure with toothpicks.
Fried in skillet.
Add mangos to salsa and use it as a dip.
