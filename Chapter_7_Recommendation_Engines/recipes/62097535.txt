Title:
Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small French baguette loaf, cut diagonally in 8 1-inch slices
3 Tblsp extra virgin olive oil
2 garlic cloves, minced and divided
1 garlic clove, whole
8 thin slices of fresh mozzarella
6-8 Roma tomatoes, seeded and diced
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
1/4 tsp salt
Balsamic vinaigrette
1/4 c shredded parmigiana

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
Combine 1 part minced garlic and 1-1/2 tbsp olive oil in small dish. Brush both sides of baguettes w/ oil mixture and place on foil lined baking pan. Place on middle rack in oven and bake 5 minutes or until lightly browned. Remove from oven and rub each side w/ whole garlic clove. Place a slice of mozzarella on top of each baguette and replace in oven for an additional 3-5 minutes or until cheese has melted.
Meanwhile, combine remaining 1-1/2 tbsp of olive oil and garlic in a bowl. Add diced tomatoes and basil, sprinkle w/ salt and toss.
Spoon tomato mixture onto baguette slices, drizzle w/ balsamic vinaigrette, top w/ shredded parmigiana and serve.

Easy, cheesy, tomato bruschetta with a perfect blend of garlic and fresh basil.
