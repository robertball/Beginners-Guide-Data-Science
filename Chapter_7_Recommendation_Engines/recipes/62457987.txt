Title:
Russian Slow cooker Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/469805.jpg
Ingredients:
6 chicken breasts or equal amount chicken tenders
1 (8oz) bottle Russian salad dressing
3/4 c. apricot preserves
1 envelope onion soup mix
cooked rice

Directions:
Put chicken, salad dressing, apricot preserves, and dry onion soup mix in a crock pot. Heat on high 4 to 5 hours or until chicken is tender. Serve over cooked rice. Save your juice to top off your chicken and rice. Enjoy!
