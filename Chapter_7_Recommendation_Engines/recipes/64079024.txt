Title:
Beer Batter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Cups all purpose flour
6 Eggs, beaten
2 Tbsp Garlic Powder
1 Tbsp Black Pepper
9 Cups Beer

Directions:
1. In Medium bowl, add flour, eggs, Garlic powder and black pepper
2. Add Beer - more can be added to moisten

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
This is a great ground up batter
