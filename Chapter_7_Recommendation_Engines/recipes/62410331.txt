Title:
Bell Pepper & Cottage Cheese Stirfry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 red or green bell pepper chopped
500 grams cottage cheese (as dry as possible, drain out excess water with cheesecloth)
1/2 medium onion chopped
2 cloves of garlic
1 tsp turmeric
1 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp salt
handful chopped coriander for garnish
2tlbs oil, vegetable, canola or sunflower

Directions:
Heat oil in a frying pan on medium heat
Add cumin and wait for bubbles to appear
Add onion, garlic and pepper, and stir gently till soft and translucent
Stir in cottage cheese
Add salt, pepper and turmeric
Stir gently till the cottage cheese is coated well with spices
Reduce heat to low, gently stirring every 2 minutes, till excess water has disappeared
Add coriander (or cilantro) for garnish and serve

Submitter: 
sugarFiend
Description: 
Easy and fast to prepare, a flavorful dish, served best with pita bread, tortillas, naan or flatbread. Its bright appearance adds cheer to what is already a healthy meal.
