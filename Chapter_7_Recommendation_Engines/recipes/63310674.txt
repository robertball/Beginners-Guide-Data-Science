Title:
Carrot Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/875296.jpg
Ingredients:
Carrots
Olive Oil
Salt

Directions:
Peel carrots into thick slices.
Toss in olive oil & salt.
Bake @ 350 degrees for 12 minutes.
Cool.

Submitter: 
Jeff Harrell
Description: 
You can do this with sweet potatoes too. Yummy, with cinnamon, nutmeg and honey drizzle
