Title:
Peanut Butter Apple Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/880297.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Mix peanut butter, brown sugar, cream cheese, and milk together in a bowl until smooth; refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
Shay
Description: 
A great apple dip that makes enough for at least 6 apples.
