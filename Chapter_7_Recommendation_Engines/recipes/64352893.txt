Title:
Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs
6 ounces frozen dumplings
1 table spoon dried minded onion
1 teaspoon mince garlic
1 can of condensed Healthy Choice cream of mushroom soup
1 can of condensed Healthy Choice cream of chicken soup
1 can of condensed cream of potato soup
16 ounces of frozen mixed vegetables

Directions:
Bring chicken and enough water to fill a 6 quart pot to the 5 quart line to boil.
Add frozen dumplings, broken into quarters, cover and reduce heat.  Allow to simmer for 45 minutes.
Remove thighs from pot.
Remove at least half of the liquid, add soups, and mix. Add frozen vegetables.
Shred thighs and add back to pot.
Heat on low until bubbling.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
This is a super simple comforting meal to put together for a cold night!
