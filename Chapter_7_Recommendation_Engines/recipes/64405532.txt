Title:
Oven Baked Breaded Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Chicken Breast, 1 Cup Mayo, 2 large eggs, 1-1/2 cups bread crumbs,

Directions:
Thaw Chicken Breast. While thawing, prepare mayo, eggs, and bread crumbs, on separate plates. Once Chicken is done thawing, dip each chicken into egg, then mayo (Make sure to very much cover the chicken). Once covered in egg and mayo, completely cover your chicken in bread crumbs. (repeat for the rest of the chicken.) Bake at 350 degrees for about an hour.
