Title:
Dijon Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/489135.jpg
Ingredients:
* 1 clove garlic, grated
* 1 tsp. Dijon mustard
* 3 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
* 3 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
* Salt (to taste)
* Freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
* Small bowl
* Wire whisk

Directions:
In small bowl, whisk together garlic, mustard, vinegar, salt, and pepper until mixed. Continue whisking while adding olive oil in a small, steady stream. Continue until oil and vinegar mixture are completely blended. Can be made ahead of timeâ€”store in refrigerator in shaker bottle with lid and shake well before serving.
