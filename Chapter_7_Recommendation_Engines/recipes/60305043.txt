Title:
Budget Monkey Bread I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/275224.jpg
Ingredients:
3 (12 ounce) packages refrigerated cinnamon roll dough
1 stick butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 9 or 10 inch tube/Bundt&#174; pan.
Cut cinnamon rolls into quaters and layer into prepared bundt pan.  Pour melted butter over the top of  of the cinnamon rolls and place in pre-heated oven.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 minutes. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a plate. Drizzle with the frosting that comes in the cinnamon roll tubes.  Do not cut! The bread just pulls apart.
