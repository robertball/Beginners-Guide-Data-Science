Title:
Quinoa Salad with Crispy Tofu Cubes and Lime Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1577088.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large sweet potato, large diced
1 cup quinoa, rinsed
2 cups chicken broth
2 Tbsp olive oil
salt
pepper
1 block very firm tofu, cut into 1 inch cubes
2 Tbsp flour
2 Tbsp corn starch
2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 cup raw broccoli head, diced into bite size pieces
1/2 cup red pepper, large dice
1/4 cup capers
1 avocado, diced
7-10 fresh basil leaves, chiffonaded
2 big handfuls mixed greens and spinach
3 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450*
Toss sweet potato cubes in olive oil, salt, and pepper then roast until soft (~20 minutes)
Boil chicken broth, reduce heat to low, stir in quinoa, and cook covered for ~30 minutes.
Cool quinoa in refrigerator until ready to use.
Boil a pot of heavily salted water, turn off heat, and add tofu cubes. Let soak for 15 minutes.
Mix flour, corn starch, garlic powder, salt, and pepper
Remove tofu from water and pat dry with towel, then toss in flour mixture to evenly coat.
Heat large skillet on medium-high heat, then add 1/4 cup oil. When hot, add tofu cubes in two batches, turning to brown each side for about 20 seconds.
Place olive oil, lime juice, mustard, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper in a jar and shake well to combine.
Toss greens, sweet potato, broccoli, red pepper, capers, avocado, and tofu cubes in lime vinaigrette
Place a 1/2-3/4 cup scoop of quinoa on the plate and top with salad mixture.
