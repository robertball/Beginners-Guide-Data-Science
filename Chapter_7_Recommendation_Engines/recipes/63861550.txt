Title:
Maple Glazed Roasted Butternut Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
About 4 cups butternut squash, peeled and cut into 1" cubes
1/4 cup pure maple syrup, preferably Grade B
3 Tbsp orange juice
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tsp dijon mustard
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbsp finely chopped rosemary
Salt & pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F
Combine maple syrup, orange juice, oil, mustard and garlic in small bowl.  Pour over squash and toss to coat,  Add rosemary and season with salt & pepper.
Spread cubes in large baking pan or jelly roll pan.
Bake for about 40 minutes, stirring occasionally, or until squash is cooked, yet still slightly firm.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Nice change from our usual pureed squash.  Grade B maple syrup gives stronger maple flavor.
