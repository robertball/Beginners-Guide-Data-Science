Title:
Mooma's Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup raisins
2 cups brown sugar
1 cup butter/margarine
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
4 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 c. liquid from boiling raisins

Directions:
Put raisins in a small saucepan, cover with water, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer covered for five minutes. Set aside to cool. Heat oven to 375 degrees. Cream sugar and margarine/butter. Add egg and vanilla. Sift dry ingredients together in a medium bowl. Add dry ingredients and liquid from boiling raisins alternatingly. Fold in raisins. Drop by teaspoons onto cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes. DO NOT OVERBAKE! To store, place wax paper between cookies.
