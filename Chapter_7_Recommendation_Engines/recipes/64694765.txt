Title:
Erishta (Lenten Noodle and Lentil Soup) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5227669.jpg
Ingredients:
Noodles
5 cups white whole wheat flour
1.75-2 c water
4 Tbsp canola oil
Salt
Soup
1.5 c brown lentils
1 head garlic
1/2 c extra virgin Olive oil
1/2 c Lemon juice
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Start lentils boiling with about 8 c water.
Mix flour, salt to taste, 1.5 c water and oil. Add more water if needed, dough should be slightly sticky. Knead about 5 minites until smooth and dough bounces back when pressed.  Make 2 balls of dough and let rest about 10- 15 min.  Put some flour or counter and roll 1 dough ball to aboit 1/4 in ch thick, or thinner.  Dust with flour, fold sheet of dough over itself about 3 times. Slice into 1/4 inch wide noodles. Unroll noodles and drop into hot pot of lentils. Noodles need to cook about 5 minutes. Smash the garlic in mortar and pestle with a Lot of salt. When it is pretty smooth, add about 12 cup olive oil and 1/4 c lemon juice. Take pot off heat and add in Garlic mixture and additional lemon juice. Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Leila Demian
Description: 
This is a tradtional Lebanese Lent dish. The broth should be garlicy with a kick of lemon.
