Title:
Fruity Summer Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
6 ice cubes
1/2 cup cubed cantaloupe
1/2 cup pineapple chunks
1/2 cup cranberry juice
1/3 cup sliced banana
1/4 cup pineapple juice
1 tablespoon honey
3/4 teaspoon lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon grated lemon peel

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender or food processor; cover and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home
Description: 
When fresh melons first come in, make this delightful thirst-quenching cooler.
