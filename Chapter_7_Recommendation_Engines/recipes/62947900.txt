Title:
Cilantro Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped or ground
3 TBLS olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 1/2 cup rice
1 (14 oz.) can chicken broth
1/2 cup white wine
1/4 cup water
1 (28 oz.) can diced tomatoes
1 tsp oregano, cumin, salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 cup cilantro leaves; washed & chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Brown rice in Dutch oven with 2 TBLS oil until golden; remove from pot
Add remaining oil, onion & red pepper, saute until tender crisp; remove from pot
Add rice back to pot, stir in broth, wine, water & spices; bring to boil on high; reduce heat; simmer until 1/2 liquid is absorbed
Stir in chicken, peppers & onions; cover Dutch oven with lid
Bake at 350 for 30-45 minutes
stir in cilantro just before serving

Submitter: 
limegreendog
Description: 
Before I made this recipe I had never cooked with Cilantro. This is a great way to start. Now I am not afraid of sprinkling it into tacos, or any Mexican type of dish. This is also a great to have for left overs.
