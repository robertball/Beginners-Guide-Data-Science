Title:
Stuffing Topped Chicken and Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6oz. pkg. stuffing mix
2 cans (10 3/4 oz.) cream of chicken soup
1 cup water
3 tbsp. sour cream
3 1/2 cups cooked chicken, cubed
2 cups cooked instant rice
2 10oz. pkgs. frozen broccoli - thawed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Prepare stuffing mix according to directions.
In a bowl, combine soup, water and sour cream.
Stir in chicken, rice and broccoli.
Pour into a greased 3qt. baking dish and top with stuffing.
Cover and bake for 30 minutes.
Uncover and bake 15-20 more minutes or until bubbly.
