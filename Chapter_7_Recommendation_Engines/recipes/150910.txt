Title:
Rangpur Cran Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975698.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Tanqueray Rangpur™ Gin
3 ounces cranberry juice
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Add cranberry juice to Tanqueray Rangpur(TM) Gin and stir.
Serve cocktail over ice; garnish with a lime wedge.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
The renowned flavor of Tanqueray® London dry gin gains added mixability by the added distillation of Rangpur limes - which are actually orange.
