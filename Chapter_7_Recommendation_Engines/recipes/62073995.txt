Title:
Raspberry Jell-O Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6-oz, package Raspberry Jell-o
1 package Cook-N-Serve Vanilla pudding.
Milk

Directions:
Make Raspberry Jell-O according to package directions and set aside.
Make Vanilla Pudding according to package directions.  When pudding has thickened set it aside.  Pour Raspberry Jell-O into serving glasses or a 9x13 pan.  Spoon vanilla pudding into the Raspberry Jell-O. (If you are using serving glasses add about 3 Tbs. pudding per glass).  Refrigerate until mixture is firm.

Submitter: 
SDHARKER
Description: 
My Mom made up this recipe to eat during our hot Nevada summers.  It was always a special summer treat.
