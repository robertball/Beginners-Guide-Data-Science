Title:
Rowdy Red Rooster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/613206.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (750 milliliter) bottle vodka
1 (32 fluid ounce) bottle cranberry juice
2 cups orange juice
1 (15 ounce) can mandarin oranges

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine vodka, cranberry juice and orange juice. Place in freezer for 2 hours, or until slushy.
When slushy, mix in mandarin oranges and serve, or replace in freezer.
