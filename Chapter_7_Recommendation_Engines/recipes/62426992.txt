Title:
Spiced Beef Stew--Revised Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbl olive oil
2 lbs beef stew meat
1/4 cup flour
1-14oz can mushroom or beef soup
1 cup beer
1 onion, sliced into thin rings
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 Tbl brown sugar
1 tsp thyme
1 bay leaf
potatoes & carrots (or 15 oz mixed veggies, drained)

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
In large pot, cook beef in oil until browned, stirring often. Remove from pot & set aside. Stir in flour
Add broth, beer, onion, garlic & br sugar, thyme & bay leaf. Heat to boil. return beef to pot & mix well.
Pour all this into casserole dish, add potatoes, carrots. cover & bake for
2 1/2 hours. If using canned or frozen mixed veggies, add after first 1 1/2 hr.
