Title:
Yogurt Popsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 16oz containers plain yogurt
1 small can concentrated oj or grape or tangerine juice
1/2 t vanilla

Directions:
Mix together well.  Pour into popcicle containers.
Place popsicle stick in each container and freeze.
