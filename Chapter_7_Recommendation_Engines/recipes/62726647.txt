Title:
Homemade Beer Bread In Under An Hour Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 C. all-purpose flour
3 T sugar
1 T baking powder
1 1/2 t salt
12 oz beer
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Adjust oven rack to lower middle position and heat oven to 375 degrees.
Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a lg. bowl.
Add beer and stir with a fork until just combined.
Turn dough onto a floured surface, knead quickly to form a ball.
Place bread on a baking sheet and mark an X on top with a serrated or very sharp knife.
Brush loaf with egg wash.
Bake until golden brown, about 45 min. Transfer to a wire rack to cool.
