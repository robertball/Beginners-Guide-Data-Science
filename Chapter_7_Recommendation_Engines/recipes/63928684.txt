Title:
Bazinga Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1097624.jpg
Ingredients:
4 baked potatoes
1/2 tsp. crushed rosemary
Salt, pepper, garlic powder
2 green onions
1/2 cup shredded Asiago cheese
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray a 2 quart casserole dish with butter flavored baking spray.
Cut cooled potatoes into cubes. Place into dish.
Add rosemary, salt, pepper, garlic powder and onions.
Stir to coat evenly.
Sprinkle with cheese.
Top with small slices of butter.
Cover and bake for 30 minutes, stirring once.

Submitter: 
DebbyJean
Description: 
Great dish to use up any leftover baked potatoes. Can sub  parmesan cheese for the asiago. For a nice crunchy potato, leave skins on.
