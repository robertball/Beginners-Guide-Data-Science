Title:
Goat Cheese Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5 oz. container of goat cheese
1 brick of cream cheese
1 cup of pecans, chopped
Several green onions, chopped
1 jar of red pepper and onion relish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Mix cream cheese, goat cheese, and chopped green onions, form into a ball and place into freezer to solidify
Toast pecans
Roll cheese ball in toasted pecans and bake for 6-8 minutes until heated through
Top with relish and serve with Stacy's Naked Pita Chips
