Title:
Baked Brie with Quebec Maple Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 wheel of brie
1 cup of chopped walnuts
1 can of finest light AA grade Quebec maple syrup
1 baguette

Directions:
Lay brie at bottom of baking dish. Sprinkle with walnuts and pour maple syrup all over. Bake in oven for 10 minutes at 200 degrees C. Serve with baguette pieces to dip.
