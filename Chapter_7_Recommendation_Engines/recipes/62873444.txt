Title:
Creamy  Banana Soft Serve Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bananas, frozen
Optional Additions:
1Tbs Honey
2Tbs Cocoa Powder and/or
2Tbs Peanut Butter

Directions:
Break bananas into chunks and throw into food processor. If using, also add optional ingredients at this time. Blend on high until a creamy consistency is reached. Spoon out and enjoy!!

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe is so simple and delicious!! Perfect guilt-free treat when your craving something sweet.
