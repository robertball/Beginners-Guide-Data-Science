Title:
Fruit dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar marshmallow creme (15oz)
3/4of 8oz carton sour cream
juice from 1/2 lime
1/2tsp cinnamon
Fruit: melon
strawberries
pears
apples
pinapple
peaches

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except the fruit.
Cut the fruit and then dip and eat!
