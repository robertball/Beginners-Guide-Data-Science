Title:
Layered Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8oz) pkg cream cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 cup cheddar cheese
1 pkg of taco seasoning
head of lettuce
3 tomatoes
1 lb ground beef

Directions:
brown beef and let it cool for 10 mins
Mix taco seasoning, sour cream and cream cheese together
Prepare the taco salad on 12" or bigger pie plate
1st layer: sour cream mixture
2nd layer: ground beef
3rd layer: shredded lettuce
4th layer: chopped tomatoes
5th layer: graded cheddar cheese

Submitter: 
Maryet Mann
Description: 
Made this in high school for a party and never stopped making it.
