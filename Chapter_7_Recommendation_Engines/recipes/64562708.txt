Title:
Sour Cream Raisin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, separated
1 cup sugar
1 cup sour cream
1 cup raisins, chopped a bit
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/8 tsp. salt
9" unbaked pie shell

Directions:
Beat egg yolks.
Add sugar, sour cream, raisins, nutmeg & salt to egg yolks.
Beat egg whites till fluffy.
Fold beaten egg whites gently into first mixture.
Pour into unbaked pie shell.
Bake @ 350 till set, approx. 40 min.  Cool before serving.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This rich, creamy pie will satisfy your sweet tooth with even a small sliver. Store leftovers in fridge.
