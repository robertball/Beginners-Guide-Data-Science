Title:
Tomato sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
25 Lbs. ROMA Tomatoes
5 onions
4 large red bell peppers
1 large green bell peppers
4 (6oz) cans tomato paste
1/4 c. soy sauce
3 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2/3 c. packed brown sugar
1/4 c. salt
10 cloves garlic, chopped or minced
3 Tbsp. dried oregano
3 Tbsp. dried basil
2 tsp. red bell pepper flakes
2 bay leaves
2 tablespoons sirachi
1 1/4 c. Lemon juice (for jars)

Directions:
In Bullet food processor,  grind onions ans peppers.  Saute the peppers and onions in a little oil and a pinch of salt before processing. Then grind all tomatoes.
In a large stockpot, combine the tomatoes (do not discard excess juices from the tomatoes), onion/pepper mixture, tomato paste, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, salt, garlic, oregano, basil, and pepper flakes.
Use Ninja and  blend the combined mixture until smooth with no large chunks.
Place in slow cookers, on high, add bay leaves, then reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 4-5 hours, stirring every 15 minutes,and draining water from top. Add sirachi to taste. Discard bay leaves.
Add 2 tablespoons lemon juice 9 or 10 hot 1-quart. jars (depending on how much you have). Ladle hot mixture into jars, leaving 1/2-in. headspace at the top. Remove air bubbles; wipe rims and adjust lids. Process for 40 minutes in a boiling-water canner.
