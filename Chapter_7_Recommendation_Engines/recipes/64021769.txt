Title:
roasted salmon with kale and cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small bunch Tuscan kale, stems removed and leaves thinly sliced (3 cups)
2 cups savoy cabbage, thinly sliced
3 T. olive oil, divided
2 skinless salmon fillets (4 to 6 oz. each)
1/2 tsp. lemon zest puls 1 T. juice
2 T. fresh dill, chopped
1/2 tsp. dijon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450. Toss kale and cabbage with 1 T. oil on a rimmed baking sheet; spread evenly; season with salt and pepper. Bake 6 minutes. Stir.
Season salmon and add to baking sheet. Bake till cooked through, about 10 minutes.
Meanwhile whisk together lemon zest and juice, dill, mustard and 2 T. oil. Drizzle salmon and veggies with dressing before serving.
