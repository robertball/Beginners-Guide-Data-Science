Title:
Barbecue Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large Can Pork 'n Beans
½ cup Ketchup
1 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
Dash of Tobasco sauce
Few strips of Bacon
½ tsp Gaarlic Salt
½ cup Brown Sugar
1 Tbsp Lemon Juice
1 Onion, diced

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients except bacon. Place in a casserole dish and top with bacon.  Cook at 375° for 1½ hours.
