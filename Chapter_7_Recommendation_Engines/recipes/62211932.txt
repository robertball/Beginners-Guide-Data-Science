Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole pre-cooked chicken, deboned (Rotisserie one from store is perfect or make your own)
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 can black beans (undrained) OR kidney beans (undrained)
1 can ranch style beans with Jalapeno’s (undrained)
1 can whole kernel corn (drained)
1 ½ cans chicken broth
1 oz tomato sauce
1 pkg taco seasoning
1 can diced tomatoes with green chili’s

Directions:
Combine above ingredients and simmer on low for 4-6 hours in crock pot.  You can put together the night before or morning of.
Serve with chips, sour cream, grated cheese and avocado
No crock pot? Put in pot and simmer on low for 2-4 hours.
Make extra by doubling the canned goods and taco seasoning only.
