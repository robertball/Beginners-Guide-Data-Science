Title:
Mimmie Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5-2lbs of 75% lean hamburger
2 cans light red kidney beans
2 32oz cans tomato sauce
3 TBSP. chili powder (to taste)

Directions:
Brown and drain hamburger
Drain beans and add to hamburger
Mix in all ingredients
Cook on low for about 20 min.
