Title:
pickled okra (mama's) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
fresh okra
fresh garlic
fresh hot peppers
dill seed
alum
water
white vinegar

Directions:
fill jars with okra(stems on)-1/2 pint jars work best
to each jar add:1 or two hot peppers
1 tsp. dryed dill seed
pinch of alum
boil together 2 quarts each- water and white vinegar
pour hot mixture over okra and seal

nice crunchy addition to a great vegetable dinner.
