Title:
Hot Chicken Salad Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/436006.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz shell noodles, cooked
4 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and diced
1 cup chopped celery
1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts or a jar of pimentos
1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
1 can cream of chicken soup
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 c sour cream
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 teaspoons grated onion
1/2 cup sliced almonds
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups crushed potato chips or ritz

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a large bowl mix the everything but Cheddar and chips. Transfer to the baking dish, and top with Cheddar cheese and crushed potato chips.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, until lightly browned
