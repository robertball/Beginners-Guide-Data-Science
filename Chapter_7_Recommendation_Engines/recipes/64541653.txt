Title:
Skinny Black Forest Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3174859.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen cherries
1 cup unsweetened almond milk
2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 cup GF rolled oats
2 cups baby spinach
1 packet Truvia sweetener

Directions:
In a blender, place all ingredients and blend until smooth
Add to a glass, top with chia seeds if desired & enjoy

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
A thick & creamy Skinny Black Forest Smoothie made with blended chocolate, cherry, milk and spinach. A delicious smoothie packed with antioxidants and fiber. The perfect post workout smoothie that doubles as dessert!
