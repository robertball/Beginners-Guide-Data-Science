Title:
Adrienne's Pumpkin Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
-----
2 Cups Sugar
1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
1 Cup Pumpkin Puree
1/4 Cup Milk
4 Eggs
2 Tsps Vanilla
2 Cups Flour
2 Tsps Baking Powder
1/2 Tsp Salt
2 Tsps Cinnamon
3/4 Tsp Ginger
1/4 Tsp Ground Cloves
-----
-----
Glaze:
-----
1oz Cream Cheese, softened
1/4 Cup of Butter, softened
2 Cups Powdered Sugar
1/4 Cup of Milk or as needed

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, blend together sugar, vegetable oil, pumpkin, milk, eggs and vanilla.
In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, ginger and cloves. Stir or mix into wet ingredients.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Let cool a bit before glazing.
To make glaze:
Combine cream cheese, butter, and powdered sugar. Add milk as needed to thin. Pour over slightly cooled but still warm cake.

Submitter: 
twogingers
Description: 
A moist, spicy and delicious pumpkin cake with a simple cream cheese glaze. Perfect for fall!
