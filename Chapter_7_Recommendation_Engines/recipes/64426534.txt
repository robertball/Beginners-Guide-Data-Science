Title:
Chewy Gluten/Grain-Free Gingerbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2317304.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening* (NUTIVA Brand preferred)
1/2 cup coconut sugar
3 eggs
3/4 cups raw honey (or molasses)
2 cups almond flour**
1 cup coconut flour
1 teaspoon xanthan gum
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 tsp ginger
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ground cloves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Using a food processor, cream together sugar and shortening, then beat in eggs. Scrape down sides.
Add honey and blend well. Scrape down sides.
Then add flours, xanthan gum, baking powder, and salt. Scrape down sides.
Last add in spices. Scrape down sides.
Blend until a dough forms.
Dump dough out of processor into a bowl. Optional: Refrigerate for about 20 minutes.
On cookie sheet with parchment paper or silicone mat, you can make cookies (roll into a ball and flatten with wet fingers) or shape into donuts.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 10 minutes. (+/- 2 min, depends on oven).

Submitter: 
Jackie HealthyWitness
Description: 
Eating healthier does not mean eliminating fun, yummy treats! These soft, chewy cookies are challenging for portion control. The recipe was created to eliminate white flour and white sugar for people trying to eat more whole and real food while increasing protein and fiber! Enjoy these for a special occasion!
