Title:
Spicey Green Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cucumber, peeled, seeded and chopped
4 green onions, chopped (green and white parts)
2 cloves of garlic, peeled and sliced
2 jalapenos, seeded (if desired) and chopped
1 cup chopped fresh cilantrao
1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
1/4 cup white vinegar
1 teaspoon sugar
3 tablespoons canola oil
salt to taste

Directions:
Combine in a food processor and blitz to the consistency of pickle relish.
Allow to sit for minimum of 20 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
Can be made a day ahead and served as a side condiment.

Submitter: 
Liz and Sylvan
Description: 
Is it Indian syle mint sauce?  Is it salsa verde? Is it argentinian chimichurri?  YES YES YES 

And whether you pair it with flat breads, steak or tortilla chips, this vegetarian yum yum is a great addition to Caribbean, Argentinian, Mexican, South American or Indiana dishes.
