Title:
Quinoa Salad with Avocado, Corn, Tomato and Cumin Chili Citrus Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
2 c cooked Quinoa
1.5 ears corn (approx. 1-2 cups)
1-2 cups cherry/grape tomatoes (halved or quartered)
1 avocado, chopped
1-2 green onions, chopped
1 small bunch fresh cilantro, finely chopped
Handful fresh basil, finely chopped
Vinaigrette:
1 clove garlic
1-2 limes, juiced
1 lemon, juiced
Lemon zest from ½ lemon
Lime zest from 1 lime
¼ c orange juice
¼ c olive oil
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
½ tsp paprika
Little agave nectar to taste
Salt to taste

Directions:
Combine all vinaigrette ingredients in blender until smooth; salt to taste
Combine all salad ingredients and toss with vinaigrette
Season to taste with more salt, lemon/lime juice
