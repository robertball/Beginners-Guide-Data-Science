Title:
Minty Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C almonds
1 15 oz can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1 tbs sesame seeds, toasted
2 garlic cloves
2 tbs fresh mint leaves
1/4 C fresh lemon juice
1 tsp cumin
pinches of sea salt & pepper
1/2 C water

Directions:
Blend the almonds, chickpeas, sesame seeds, and garlic, in a food processor until well mashed.  Add remaining ingredients, blend until smooth.
