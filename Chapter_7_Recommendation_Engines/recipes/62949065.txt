Title:
Slow Cooker Tropical Pork Shoulder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9.5 pound) Pork Shoulder
1 can of Mandarin oranges
1 can of Pineapple tidbits
1 large onion cut into Â¼ cubes
1 clove garlic, minced
1 1/2 tablespoons of sea salt
1 1 1/2 tablespoon of worcestershire sauce

Directions:
1. Place the chopped onion pieces around the perimeter of the slow cooker, reserving the middle area of the slow  cooker for the meat.
2. Place all of the juice from the Mandarin oranges and Pineapple tidbits and half of the can of Mandarin orange and half of the can of Pineapple tidbits around the perimeter of the slow cooker, reserving the middle area of the slow cooker for the meat.
3. Spread half of the minced garlic evenly in the middle area of the slow cooker.
4. Place the pork shoulder into the center of the slow cooker.
5. Distribute the Worcestershire sauce, salt, remaining garlic, Mandarin oranges and Pineapple tidbits evenly on the top of the pork shoulder.
6. Put the lid on the slow cooker and set the cooker on low for 20 hours.
7. When the 20 hours are up, you will be able to grab the bone and the meat will fall right off of the bone.
8. Place the meat on a presentation platter and take two forks and shred the meat. Once you have piled the meat in the center of the platter, strain the Mandarin oranges and Pineapple tidbits, reserving one cup of the broth around the edge of the meat and then pour the cup of broth evenly over the shredded meat.
