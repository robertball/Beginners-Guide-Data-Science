Title:
Slow cooker chicken lentil burrito filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2b skinless/boneless chicken
2 package of taco seasoning
2 can chicken broth
1 can whole corn

Directions:
mix broth and season packages in crockpot.
add chicken and lentils.
put on low for 8 hours.
in last 1.5 hours, add can of corn.
serve in whole wheat tortilla.
Save in refrigerator and make available for quick on the run meals.  Sorry Taco Bell!

Description: 
This is a great meal for on the go! A much healthier alternative to Taco Bell.
