Title:
Feta Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded cooked chicken breasts
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
1/2 cup chopped seedless cucumber
1/2 cup chopped sweet yellow pepper
4 tsp onlive oil
4 tsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp Greek Seasoning
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the first five ingredients. In a small bowl, whisk the lemon juice, oil, greek seasoning, salt and pepper. Pour over chicken mixture; toss to coat. Refridgerate for at least 1 hour. Just before serving, sprinkle with cheese.
