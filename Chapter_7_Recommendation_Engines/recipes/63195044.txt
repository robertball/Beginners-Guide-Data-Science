Title:
Creamy Santa Fe Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup light mayonnaise
1/2 cup low-fat buttermilk
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon smoked paprika
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon sea salt
1 tablespoon chopped cilantro, to taste

Directions:
Mix ingredients in a blender and chill until for at least one hour before used.

Submitter: 
Souper-Shannon
Description: 
A creamy dressing that is best served on a bed of greens with roasted corn, chopped tomatoes, salsa, and crunchy tortilla chips.
