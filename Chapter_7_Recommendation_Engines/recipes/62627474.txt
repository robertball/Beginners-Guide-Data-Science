Title:
Vegan Red Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon peanut oil
1 small onion, chopped
(1.5 or to taste) tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
3 clove garlic, chopped
1/2 tsp. fenugreek seeds
1 cup dry red lentils (try a mix of different types)
1 cup butternut squash - peeled, seeded, and cubed
1/3 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro
3-4 cups water or *homemade chicken stock*
1/2 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
2 tablespoons tomato paste
1-2 teaspoon curry powder
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp mustard seed
1 pinch cayenne pepper
1 pinch ground nutmeg
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat, and cook the onion till sweet and tender. in a small skillet toast mustard seed till pops and cumin seed stir 10 sec. and remove from heat. grind to powder. Add mustard, cumin, ginger, garlic, curry powder, cayenne pepper, and fenugreek to onions. Stir briefly on low heat till fragrant. Add to soup pot with the lentils, squash, and cilantro. Stir in the water/chicken broth, coconut milk, and tomato paste. Season with  nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer 30 minutes, or until lentils and squash are tender.
