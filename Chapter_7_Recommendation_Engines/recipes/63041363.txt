Title:
Herb Burgers with Cucumber Yogurt Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
1/2 cup unseasoned dry bread crumbs
2 TBL milk
2 tsp lemon pepper marniade
1/2 tsp oregano (leaves)
4 whole pita breads, top 1/3 cut off, warmed
sliced tomato, sliced onion and crumbled feta cheese
SAUCE:
1 cup plain lowfat yogurt
2/3 cup finely chopped cucumber
1 tsp onion salt
1 tsp sugar

Directions:
In small bowl, combine sauce ingredients; mix well. Set aside.
In large bowl, combine turkey, bread crumbs, milk, lemon pepper and oregano, mixing lightly but thoroughly. Shape into four 1/2" thick oval patties.
Grill patties over medium coals 10 to 14 minutes or until cooked through and juices run clear, turning once. Serve in pitas with sauce and tomato, onion and cheese, as desired.
