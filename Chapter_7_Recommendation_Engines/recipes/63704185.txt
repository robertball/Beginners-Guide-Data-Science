Title:
Chicken Enchilada Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030796.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb chopped boneless chicken
1 tbsp canola oil
2 tbsp dried minced onion flakes
1 tsp garlic powder
1 can (12oz) enchilada sauce
1 (15oz) can low-sodium black beans, drained and rinsed
1/4 cup low-sodim chicken broth
1 cup salsa (optional)
1/2 cup of greek yogurt
8 oz campanelle wheat pasta (or penne)
1 cup Pepper Jack cheese, shredded
2 tbsp parsley or cilantro

Directions:
Heat oil over medium-high heat in 12-inch oven safe skillet until hot.
Add and cook chicken until browned. (It doesn't need to be completely cooked because it will continue to cook with the pasta)
Add onion flakes, garlic powder, enchilada sauce, black beans, broth, yogurt, and pasta.
Bring to boil.
Reduce heat to low and cover skillet.
Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
Remove skillet from heat.
Top with cheese and sprinkle with parsley or cilantro.
