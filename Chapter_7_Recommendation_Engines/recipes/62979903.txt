Title:
THM Escalloped Cabbage #S Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1986056.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head of Chopped Cabbage
1 1/2 C. Half & Half
3/4 C. Sharp Cheddar Cheese
2 oz. Butter
2 oz. Cream Cheese
Salt
Papper

Directions:
Chop head of cabbage & steam.  Once cooked, drain it & place in buttered casserole dish.
To make white sauce mix: Half & Half, Butter, Cream Cheese, Sharp Cheddar Cheese.  Cook until slightly thickened.  Season with salt & pepper.  Pour over cabbage.  Sprinkle more sharp cheddar cheese over the dish.  Bake at 350* until hot & bubbly.

Kristen Bowman
Description: 
This rich cheesy dish is my favorite cabbage recipe...  perfect for fall!
