Title:
Rajisthani Pumpkin & Potato stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. Pumpkin cubed
1 c. Potatoes cubed
3 Tbsp Ghee (clarified butter)
A pinch Asafoetida (Hing)
¼ Tsp Mustard Seeds
¼ Tsp Jeera Seeds (cumin seeds)
¼ Tsp Kalonji Seeds (onion seeds)
¼ Tsp Fenugreek Seeds
2 Bay Leaves
1 Tsp minced Green Chillies
1 & ½ Tsp Salt
1 Tsp Dhania (Coriander) Powder
1 Tsp Amchur (Mango) Powder
1 Tsp Red Chilli Powder
½ Tsp Turmeric Powder
½ to ¾ c. Water to Pressure Cook
4 Tbsp chopped Dhania (Coriander) Leaves

Directions:
Cut pumpkin and potatoes into bite sized cubes.
Heat ghee in the pressure cooker and fry the asafoetida, bay leaves, onion seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds and mustard seeds.
Once the mustard seeds begin to splutter, add green chili, potatoes, pumpkin, salt,turmeric, and water.
Pressure cook for 1 whistle. Open the cooker and add coriander, mango, and red chili poweders. Mix gently and add more water if needed to get the desired consistency of a thick stew.
Add coriander leaves and mix well. Serve hot garnished with chopped coriander leaves.
