Title:
Veggie smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup kale
2 cups spinach
1 frozen pear (slice before freezing)
1 cup chopped mango
1/2 orange (no seeds)
1 tablespoon flax seeds (optional)
1/4 cup water
1 cup ice

Directions:
combine all ingredients and blend

Submitter: 
George. P. Cooper
Description: 
Here is a great tasting kale and spinach smoothie that can be an excellent source of nitrate.
