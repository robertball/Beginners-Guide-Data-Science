Title:
Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crumbled fried bacon
2T butter, melted
1/2 cup chopped onion
3 eggs, beaten
8 oz cream cheese, cut in small pieces
1 (15 ounce) can cream-style no salt added corn
2 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn no salt added, drained
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chilies, drained
1 chopped bell pepper
1 t pepper
1 t onion powder
1 t cumin
1 t garlic powder
1 T mayo
1 (8.5 ounce) package dry corn muffin mix

Directions:
Saute onion in butter.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a 2 quart casserole dish.
In a large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients, in order. Stir in the corn muffin mix until just moistened. Place in a 9 x 13 dish.
Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven for  55 minutes;or until knife come out clean. Turn off oven. Add additional cheese on top and let stand 10 minutes before serving.
