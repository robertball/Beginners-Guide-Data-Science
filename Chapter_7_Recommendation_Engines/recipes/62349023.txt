Title:
Mighty Mulligatawny Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/403315.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons Oil
2 tablespoons curry powder
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 medium onion, diced
3 garlic cloves
1 large Granny Smith apple, cored but not peeled, and diced.
1 large sweet potato, peeled and diced
6 Cups Chicken broth
1 lime juiced
1 (14 ounce) can of diced tomatoes
2 Cups cooked white beans
1 (14 ounce) can of coconut milk
1 cup of heavy whipping cream
2 Cups cooked millet
3 Cups cooked, shredded chicken breast
Salt, pepper and sugar to taste

Directions:
Heat oil, add onion, spices, 6 tablespoons of water, garlic, apple and sweet potato. Cook until onion is softened.
Stir in stock, lime juice, tomatos, beans, salt and pepper to taste.  Reduce to simmer and cook 30 minutes until the veggies are tender.
Stir in coconut milk, cream, millet. If you want a smooth consistancy use an immersion blender to thicken.  Add chicken.
