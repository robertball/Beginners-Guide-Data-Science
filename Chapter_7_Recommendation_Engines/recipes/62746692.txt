Title:
Italian Teala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/637428.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. can tomato sauce
3 cups peeled and thin sliced potatoes
2 cups thin sliced zucchini
1 small red or yellow pepper, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
3/4 cups bread crumbs
1/4 cup water
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup grated Romano cheese
1 teaspoon each: dried basil, oregano, garlic powder
3 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Oil 13 x 9 pan(sides and bottom) with the olive oil. Layer potatoes on bottom of casserole dish, sprinkle with 1/3 of bread crumbs and cheese. Repeat layers of zucchini, bread crumbs, cheese, onions and peppers.
Mix seasonings into tomato sauce and pour over layers.  Add water, salt and pepper.
Bake covered at 350Â° for 50 minutes, or until potatoes are soft when pierced with a fork. Stir occasionally, add water if needed.
