Title:
Boo's Crock Pot Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 or 4 boneless chicken breasts
1 box chicken broth
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 small bell pepper, chopped
2 stalks of celery, chopped
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 cups Bisquick
2/3 c. milk

Directions:
Place chicken in crockpot along with box of chicken broth, onion, bell pepper, celery, salt and pepper. Coook on high for 5-6 hours.
"Shred" the chicken breasts. Pour one can of cream of chicken soup into crockpot, mixing well.
Mix the Bisquick w/ milk  until moistened. Drop by tablespoons into crockpot. Gently fold some of the broth over the dumplins. Let it cook for another 20 minutes. It will make a bisquit-like top over the chicken.....just cut into it and fold broth over it.
