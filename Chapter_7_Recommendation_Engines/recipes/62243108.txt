Title:
Vanilla Bean Whipped Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/328364.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package softened cream cheese
2 cups confectioner's sugar (or 1 cup granulated)
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
1 Vanilla Bean (seeds and extract)

Directions:
Chill beaters and metal bowl in freezer for 10 minutes.
Use chilled beaters and bowl to beat heavy cream until stiff peaks form (consistency of whipped butter); set aside in fridge to stay chilled.
In a large bowl combine cream cheese, sugar, salt and vanilla.
Split the vanilla bean in half length wise. Scrape seeds and extract out of bean and add to cream cheese mixture.
Beat until smooth, then fold in whipped cream.
For additional firmness, refridgerate for an hour before using.

Submitter: 
Erin
Description: 
A light whipped cream frosting with a hint of cream cheese for flavor and firmness. Perfect for summer desserts or people who prefer lighter frostings.Modified from: Whipped Cream Cream Cheese Frosting
