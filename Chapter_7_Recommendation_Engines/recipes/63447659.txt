Title:
Pumpkin Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can pumpkin
1 12 oz can evaporated milk
4 eggs
1 1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 box yellow cake mix
2 cup chopped pecans
1 1/2 stick salted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.  Mix pumpkin, evaporated milk, eggs, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Pour into greased 9x13 pan.  Sprinkle dry cake mix over the pumpkin, covering the top. Top the dry cake mix with the pecans.  Melt the butter and pour over the pecans.
