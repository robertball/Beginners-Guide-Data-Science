Title:
Cool Fruit Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cherry pie filling
1 large can crushed pineapple, drained
1-2 cups fresh blueberries, washed and dried
1 8 oz tub Cool Whip
1 can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Place in dish (depends on your preference for thickness of finished product). Freeze. Remove from freezer 30 minutes before serving. Can also just serve straight from freezer. Serve slices or blocks, as preferred.
Can also add chopped nuts if crunch in texture is desired.

Description: 
Cherries, pineapple and blueberries in a light, cool base.
