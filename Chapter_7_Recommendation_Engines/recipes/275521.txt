Title:
Strawberry-Lime-Cilantro Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6950247.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen strawberries, slightly thawed and chopped
1 frozen banana, chopped
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
1/2 cup ice
1/4 cup chopped cilantro, leaves and stems, lightly packed
1 tablespoon lime juice
1/2 teaspoon stevia powder (optional)

Directions:
Combine strawberries, banana, almond milk, ice, cilantro, lime juice, and stevia powder in a blender. Process until smooth, adding more almond milk or water for a smoother consistency.

Submitter: 
Tammy Lynn
Description: 
This refreshing strawberry and cilantro smoothie is packed with vitamins and minerals with the addition of a savory herb. A frozen banana makes it creamy and sweet.
