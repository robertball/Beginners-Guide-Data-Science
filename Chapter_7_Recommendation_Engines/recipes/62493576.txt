Title:
Sweet Strawberry and Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Banans
6 Strawberries
1 1/2 cups of Yogurt
1/2 Cup milk
2 tablespoons of sugar

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients in the blender for about a minute.

Submitter: 
Nick Gibietis
Description: 
This is a strawberry and banana smoothie using fresh fruit. Sweet taste and not bitter at all. It has sugar so it sweetens the taste
