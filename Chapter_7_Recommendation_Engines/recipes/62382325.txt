Title:
Spinach/Red Pepper Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons olive oil
2 cups spinach leaves or chard leaves
half red pepper (chopped)
salt and pepper (freshly milled)
3 egg whites, beaten with
1/2 teaspoon chopped marjoram or chives

Directions:
In a small nonstick skillet, heat one teaspoon of oil, add the spinach or chard and chopped red pepper, and cook until it's wilted.
Season with salt and pepper, then move it to a plate.
Add the second teaspoon of oil to the pan, then pour in the whites.
Tilt the pan to spread them out, then cook over medium heat until they're set. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper, herbs
arrange the spinach over 1/3 of the eggs, then gently prod the eggs over it to make a rolled omelet.
