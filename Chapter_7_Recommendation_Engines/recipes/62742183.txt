Title:
Old Bay Cod Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1 tbs Old Bay seasoning
1 1/2 pound cod fillet
2 tbs olive oil

Directions:
In a shallow dish mix the flour and Old Bay seasoning well.
Dry the fillets with a paper towel then dredge each in the flour mixture and shake off any excess.
Heat the olive oil in a skillet and add the fillets.
Cook on medium heat for 2-3 minutes or until the bottom is golden then turn and cook for another 2-3 minutes.  Fish is cooked when it flakes easily.
Serve immediately with a lemon garnish.

Submitter: 
BigShotsMom
Description: 
Sometimes the simplest preparation yields the tastiest result.  This will work with any type of fillet.
