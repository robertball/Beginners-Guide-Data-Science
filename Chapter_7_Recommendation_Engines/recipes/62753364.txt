Title:
Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635164.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 cup sugar
3 eggs
1 2/3 cups milk
2 1/3 cups flour
1 cup cornmeal
4 Â½ tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
Honey to taste

Directions:
In mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Combine eggs and milk. Combine flour, cornmeal, baking powder and salt; add to creamed mixture alternately with egg mix.
Pour into a greased 13x9-inch baking pan. Bake at 400F for 22-27 minutes or until toothpick inserted near center comes out clean. Cut into squares; serve warm.
