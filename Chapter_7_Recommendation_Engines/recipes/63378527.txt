Title:
Red Cabbage Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/904329.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small red cabbage, thinly slice and chop
1/2 cup grated carrot
1/4 craisins or raisins
1/4 cup chopped walnut, pecan or sunflower seeds
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 to 2 tsp. sugar
splash of milk
1 TBSP of apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Red Cabbage can be chopped to your desire whether long slices or small pieces. Add all other ingredients in a medium bowl, stir and cover for several hours.

Submitter: 
AuntE
Description: 
A nice light creamy side dish to bring on a picnic, or to go with a nice comforting fall dish like chili or pulled pork sandwich. Adds beautiful color to your plate.
