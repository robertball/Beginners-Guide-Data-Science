Title:
Coconut Lemon Poppyseed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1 3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup sweetened coconut flakes + 3 -6 Tablespoons (divided)
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/2 Tablespoon poppy seeds
1 lemon, zest and juice
3 eggs
1 1/8 cup coconut oil, melted
1 1/2 cup coconut milk (full fat unsweetened from a can)
1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 teaspoon almond extract (or imitation)
1 1/2 teaspoon butter flavor
GLAZE:
1/4 cup lemon juice
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
1/2 teaspoon butter flavor

Directions:
BREAD:
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease and four 6 mini loaf pans
Put everything in a mixing bowl and beat together until smooth. this batter is much thicker than the traditional recipe and cannot be poured. The batter must be scooped into the prepared bread pans. Sprinkle 3-6 tablespoons of sweetened coconut flakes over the tops of the loaves and cook for 1 hour.
GLAZE:
While bread is cooking make glaze by stirring all ingredients together. When bread is out of the oven, stir glaze then brush 2 coats on bread while bread is in the pan and still hot.
