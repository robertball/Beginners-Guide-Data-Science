Title:
Strawberry Sacue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Frozen whole strawberries
1tsp white sugar (or more)
1/2 tsp of icing sugar
1/4 tsp of brown sugar
1/4 tsp of medium coconut
pinch of cocoa

Directions:
Put 3 or more frozen strawberries in a dish and put them in the microwave for 50 seconds. Then take them out and cut them up in the same dish, then put them back in the microwave for 20 seconds. After, take them out and put the white sugar, icing sugar, brown sugar, and cocoa in, mix it up and put back in the microwave for 15 seconds. Lastely, add the coconut in the sauce.

Submitter: 
yummyfood
Description: 
This is a warm or cold sauce that can be drizzled on fruit, or on desserts!
