Title:
Potato Salad with bacon & sour cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 medium potatos
1 Tbs salt
2 cups mayonnaise
1 & 1/2 Cups Sour cream
1/4 to 1/2 Cup Cider Vinegar
4 Tbs Worcestershire sauce
1/2 to 1 lb Bacon, fried crisp broken into 1/4 to 1/2 inch pieces.
6 green onions cut into 1/4 inch pieces

Directions:
Boil potatoes until tender.
Remove skins & cool.
Dice into rough Cubes.
Sprinkle with salt & green onions.
In a separate bowl, Mix Mayonnaise, Sour cream, vinegar & worcestershire sauce.
Add half of crisp bacon to sauce, mix.
Pour over potatos & green onions.
Mix , Top with remaining bacon pieces.
