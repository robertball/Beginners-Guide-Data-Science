Title:
Bread Machine Wheat bread Master mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 five pound bag bread flour
1 five pound bag whole wheat flour
1 3/4 cups dry milk
1 1/4 cups sugar
4 1/2 tablespoons salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in extra large bowl until well blended. Store tightly covered in bulk, or measure into quart jars or bags with recipe for gift giving.
TO MAKE 1 1/2 pound loaf: Measure 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons warm water, 2 tablespoons butter or margarine, softened, 3 1/2 cups Bread machine mix, and 1 1/2 teaspoons bread machine or quick active dry yeast into bread machine pan in order recommended by manufacturer. Select Basic/White or Whole Wheat cycle and medium or light crust. Remove baked bread from pan, and cool on wire rack.
TO MAKE 2-pound loaf: Measure 1 1/2 cups warm water, 3 tablespoons butter or margarine, softened, 4 1/2 cups Bread machine mix, and 1 1/4 teaspoons bread machine or quick active dry  yeast into the bread pan in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select Basic/White or Whole Wheat cycle and medium or light crust. Remove baked bread from pan, and cool on wire rack.
Makes 8 2-pound loaves.
