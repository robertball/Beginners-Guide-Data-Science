Title:
Glogg Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/933312.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Sugar
1 Cup Water
3 Liters Port
1 Liter Brandy
20 Cardimum seeds
3 Cinnamon sticks
Handful of sliced almonds
handful of raisins
10 Whole Cloves

Directions:
Boil Water and sugar until sugar is disolved.
Add the Port Wine and Brandy.
Make a bundle of the spices and tie up in cheese cloth.
Drop into heated mixture.
Boil until you smell the spices
Remove the spice packet
Add the Brandy
Cool and ladle with a funnel into decorative bottles wait untill cooled thoroughly before corking.

Submitter: 
Susan Murdoch
Description: 
Traditional Skandinavian Holiday Beverage.  I usually bottle this up and give to friends and family.
