Title:
Tex-Mex Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 onion, diced
1 packet taco seasoning(I use one whole recipe of Taco Seasoning I from this site)
3 cups cooked rice
1 cup salsa
2 cups shredded monterey jack cheese, divided
6 green bell peppers, tops cut off and seeds removed
1 (15oz.)can enchilada sauce

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350F.
In large skillet, saute ground beef and onions until beef is no longer pink.
Add taco seasoning, rice, salsa, and 1 cup of the cheese. Stir until well combined.
Place peppers in deep casserole dish.Fill peppers with rice mixture. Pour enchilada sauce over each pepper, then top with remaining cheese.
Cover casserole dish with lid or foil and bake 40-45 minutes, or until sauce is bubbly.
