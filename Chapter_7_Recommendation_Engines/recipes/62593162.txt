Title:
Mexican Beef Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/558782.jpg
Ingredients:
1 boxe Pillsbury Refrigerated Pie Crust
1 15 oz can refried beans
2 large tomatoes, diced
1 lb ground beef
1/4 small onion, chopped
1 jalapeno, minced
1/4 green pepper, chopped
1 packet low-sodium taco seasoning
16 oz sour cream
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 TB milk
lettuce, chopped
salsa

Directions:
Prepare pie crust in pan, according to directions.  In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, green pepper, and jalapeno until meat is no longer pink.  Drain well.  Add taco seasoning mix to beef mixture and cook according to package directions.  Meanwhile, prepare pie pan with crust.  When meat mixture is done, add beans and mix well.  Simmer until warm, about 5 to 10 minutes.  In prepared pie pan, layer ingredients in order:  Meat, cheddar cheese, sour cream, diced tomatoes.  Cover with top crust.  Brush top of crust with milk.  Make 2 slits in crust. lightly cover with foil. Bake at 350 for for 35 minutes.  Remove foil and bake 15 more minutes, or until top of crust is brown according to your liking.  When serving, top each slice with lettuce and salsa to taste.
