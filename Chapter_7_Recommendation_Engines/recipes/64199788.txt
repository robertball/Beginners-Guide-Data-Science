Title:
Quick and Easy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion, chopped
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/4 cup ketchup
5 tablespoons soy sauce
3 tablespoons Splenda
5 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
10 oz frozen broccoli cuts

Directions:
Saute onion in oil until translucent.
Add chicken, and brown lightly.
Combine ketchup, soy sauce, sugar, lemon juice, and pepper; mix well.  Pour over chicken, and bring to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat, and simmer for 25 to 35 minutes.
Add broccoli during the last 5 minute of cooking.
