Title:
nutella buttercream frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/723434.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks unsalted butter, softened
1 13-ounce jar of Nutella
dash of salt
2 TBS meringue powder(optional)
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
2 pounds confectioners’ sugar
6-8 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Directions
1. in a large mixing bowl, cream butter, nutella and meringue powder until well combined. slowly add in confectioner’s sugar, and continue creaming until well blended, alternating with milk.
2. add salt and vanilla. blend on low speed until moistened. add additional milk until you reach the desired consistency. beat at high speed until frosting is smooth and fluffy.
