Title:
Bean Salad with Tarragon Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976052.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can green beans
1 (14.5 ounce) can yellow beans
1 (19 ounce) can black beans
1 (12 ounce) can corn
1 white onion, sliced into rings
Tarragon Vinaigrette:
1/2 cup canola oil
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 garlic clove, minced
1 teaspoon dried tarragon
1 teaspoon dried basil, crumbled

Directions:
Strain green beans, yellow beans, black beans and corn. Mix in a large bowl.
To prepare vinaigrette, whisk together canola oil, vinegar, sugar, mustard, garlic, tarragon, and basil. Pour onto bean mixture prior to serving. Garnish with onion rings.

Submitter: 
The Canola Info Virtual Kitchen
Description: 
This quick and easy salad tastes great the next day too! Making your own vinaigrettes not only saves money but also allows you to control the fat content!
