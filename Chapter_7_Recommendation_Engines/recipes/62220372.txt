Title:
David's favorite Cheese Srtaws Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/314917.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups (8 0z.)shredded Extra Sharp Cheddar Cheese,
3/4 cup margarine.
2 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 tablespoons extra virgan olive oil
3 to 4 tablespoons water as needed.

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 400 degrees F,(205 degrees C).Grease or line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Sift togeather the flour ,baking powder, cumin, paprika ,garlic powder. Set aside.
Grate 2 cups of extra sharp cheddar cheese.
Cream togeather butter and cheese. It helps if cheese and butter are room temp.
Add flour and blend togeather on medium adding olive oil and water till soft dough froms.
Using a cookie press (any shape you like) press onto parchment paper or greased baking sheet. Bake at 400 degrees F, for 8 to 10 min. till just starting to brown on edges and bottom .

Submitter: 
Robograndma Davis
Description: 
Easy to make with just the right spice. Well my family thinks so. Even the grandbaby.This recipe is for a cookie press but you could roll them by hand if you cut back on the water.
