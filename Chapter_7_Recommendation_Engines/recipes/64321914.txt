Title:
Red Leaf, Avocado, and Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon mustard, coarse-grain
1 pinch salt, plus more to taste
1 pinch peppercorns, black, freshly ground, plus more to taste
4 cups lettuce, red leaf, torn into bite-size pieces
1/2 medium avocado pitted, peeled, and thinly sliced
1/4 medium apple(s), Granny Smith, thinly sliced

Directions:
Combine oil, lemon juice, and mustard in a jar. Add a pinch of salt and pepper, close tightly, and shake vigorously to combine.
Toss dressing with lettuce; add salt and pepper to taste. Distribute salad among plates, top with avocado and apple slices, and serve.
Each serving is 2 cups.
