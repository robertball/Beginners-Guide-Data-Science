Title:
CHEDDARY CHICKEN, RICE AND TOMATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (16 oz)whole tomatoes, chopped
1 can (11 oz) Cheddar cheese soup
2 cups quick cooking rice
1 onion, finely chopped
1 Tsp basil
1 tsp salt
1/8 Tsp pepper
2to3 lb broiler-fryer chicken pieces

Directions:
In a 12 x 8-inch dish, combine tomatoes , soup, rice,  onion and seasonings.  Arrange chicken pieces on top, bony side upand meatiest portions toward outside of the dish.  Cover with waxed paper.  Microwave on high for 15 minutes.  Stir rice mixture and turn chicken pieces over.  Microwave, uncovered 11 to 15 minutes longer or until chicken is fork tender and meat near the bone is no linger pink.
