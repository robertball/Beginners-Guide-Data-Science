Title:
Heart Healthy Choco Banana Oat Walnut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c dry regular oats (not quick cook)
1 1/4 c lowfat milk or soy milk
2 eggs (or 1 egg + 2 egg whites)
2 med over-ripe bananas, mashed
1/4 c canola or other heart healthy oil
3/4 c brown sugar
1 t vanilla extract (not imitation)
1 c semisweet chocolate chips
3/4 c chopped walnuts
1 1/4 c flour (all white, or 1/2 c wheat + 3/4 white)
2 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
1 t salt

Directions:
Combine dry oats and milk in large bowl; let stand for 5 min.
Add eggs and beat well with fork. Add bananas, oil, sugar, vanilla, chocolate chips and walnuts; stir until well mixed.
Sift in flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; stir until just combined.
Fill muffin cups using 1/4 c measuring cup and bake at 350 14-16 minutes, or until done.

Description: 
I created this recipe to use ingredients I had on hand.  The results were wonderful: delicious, moist, heart healthy muffins - rich in fiber but not too heavy.  Definitely a keeper!
