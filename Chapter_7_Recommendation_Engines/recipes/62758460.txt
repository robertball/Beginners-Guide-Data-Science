Title:
Easy German Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. pecans chopped
1 C. coconut
1 box chocolate cake mix prepared according to directions
1 8 oz. cream cheese softened
1 stick butter melted
3 1/3 C. powder sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350^
Using 9 x 13 pan, spray with PAM, evenly sprinkle pecans and coconut on bottom
Prepare cake mix according to directions and pour over nuts/coconut mixture
In seperate bowl combine softened cream cheese and melted butter to mixed well
Slowly add powdered sugar until smooth and creamy
Dallop cream mixture on top of cake batter and swirl in with a knife to create a marble look.
Bake for 35-40 min. or until tooth pick comes clean and cake no longer jiggles in the middle.

Submitter: 
Too Hot Tamale
Description: 
A moist and rich german chocolate cake that has no need for frosting as there is a cream mixtue that has been marbled throughout the cake to replace the need for frosting.
