Title:
Healthier Pumpkin Muffin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/901976.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Whole Wheat Flour
1 Cup Sugar
3/4 Teaspoon Baking Powder
1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1 Teaspoon Salt
1 Tablespoon Ground Flax Seed
1/2 Teaspoon Cloves
1/4 Teaspon Ground Ginger
1 Tablespoon Cinnamon
1 Tablespoon Nutmeg
1 Large Egg
1/4 Cup Coconut Oil
1/4 Cup Water
3/4 Cup of 100% Pumpkin Puree (Half of a 15 Oz. Can)

Directions:
Start by mixing the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, flax seed, spices) in a large bowl.
Next, in a medium bowl whisk the egg and then mix in the coconut oil and water. Once combined, stir in the pumpkin puree.
Next, slowly stir the pumpkin mixture into the large bowl of dry ingredients. Once combined let rest for 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 400F. Pour the mixture into a lightly greased muffin pan or liners. Place in the oven for 20 mins and then check with a toothpick if they come out clean.
Let cool for 5 minutes.
