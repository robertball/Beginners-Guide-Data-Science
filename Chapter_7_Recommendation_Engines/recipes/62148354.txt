Title:
Trishie's Banoffee Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
2/3 bananas sliced
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
2 cups sweetened whipped cream
1 tablespoon grated semisweet chocolate, for garnish

Directions:
Fill a large pot with water and bring to a boil over medium heat.
Place the can of sweetened condensed milk (unopened) in the boiling water.
Cover and let simmer 3 hours (adding more water if needed)
Turn the can every half hour to prevent scorching.
Arrange sliced bananas onto the prepared graham cracker crust
Carefully open can of condensed milk and pour toffee sauce over the bananas
Refigerate for 30 mins
Cover with the whipped cream
Sprinkle the top with the grated chocolate.

Submitter: 
Trishie
Description: 
A  Graham Cracker Crust filled  with Bananas, Toffee Sauce and topped off with Whipped Cream and Chocolate shavings!!
