Title:
Amber's Hash Brown Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/591133.jpg
Ingredients:
2cups frozen hash browns
1 ½ cups milk
2 Tbsp. butter
1 tsp. salt
½ tsp. pepper
½ tsp. garlic salt
1 Tbsp. diced ham
2 tsp. seasoned salt
Handful shredded sharp cheddar

Directions:
1.	Mix together milk, butter, salt, pepper, and garlic salt; put in microwave until butter melts and mix again
2.	Add in ham and hash browns and mix
3.	Poor into a 8x8x5 in pan and spread out evenly
4.	Evenly spread cheese on top and put in 375* oven
5.	Bake until cheese melts and is golden

A delicous and easy breakfast to make on a cold, rainy day to keep you warm and cozy.
