Title:
Manly Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2303657.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ice cubes, or as desired
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers tequila plata
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger triple sec
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger lime juice

Directions:
Fill a rocks glass with ice; add tequila, triple sec, and lime juice. Stir. Let margarita rest for 2 minutes.
