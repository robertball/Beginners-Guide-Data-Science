Title:
Prawn Saganaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 red onion, halved and thinly sliced
3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons tomato paste
1/2 cup white wine
1 jar Papayiannides Tomato & Olive & Ouzo Sauce
1/2 cup crumbled Greek feta cheese
2 tablespoons chopped flat leaf parsley
Crusty bread to serve

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
In a large frying pan over medium heat, saute the onion, stirring, until soft. Add the garlic & cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Add the tomato paste & cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
Add the wine & simmer until reduced by about half. Add the tomato sauce & simmer for 10 minutes or until the mixture is thick.
Spread the tomato mixture over the base of a 6-cup oven proof dish. Top with the prawns & feta.
Bake for 10 minutes or until cooked through. Divide among serving dishes. Top with parsley and serve with fresh bread.
Serves 4 as an appetizer.

Submitter: 
Angie
Description: 
In Greek cuisine, Saganaki is any one of a variety of dishes prepared in a small frying pan, the best-known being an appetizer of fried cheese. We're raising the bar for this recipe by adding prawns & lots of fresh Feta!
