Title:
Ham and Cheese Bowties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces farfalle (bow tie) pasta
1/4 cup butter
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
dash of cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasonings
2 cups milk
1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard
2 cups shredded Italian cheeses
4 ounces cooked ham, julienned
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup breadcrumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a large saucepan over medium heat, melt butter.  Saute garlic 30 seconds.  Whisk in flour, salt, peppers, and Italian seasonings.  Cook and stir until smooth.  Pour in milk, a little at a time, stirring constantly.  Bring to a boil for 1 minute.  Stir in mustard and cheese.  Continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until cheese is melted.  Remove from heat and stir in pasta and ham.
Pour into a 2 quart baking dish.  Sprinkle with Parmesan and breadcrumbs.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes, until bubbly and golden.
