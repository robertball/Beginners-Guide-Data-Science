Title:
Old Style Gravey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbs Butter, 4 Tbs Flour, 1 3/4 - 2 Cups Beef or Chicken Stock,

Directions:
Melt Butter on stove low heat, Mix flour until blended well. Add Stock turn up heat and stir until sauce comes to a boil and thickens.
