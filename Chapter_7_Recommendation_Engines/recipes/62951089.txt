Title:
Slow Cooker Barbeque Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/726166.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 pieces boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 bottle of BBQ sauce (whatever you prefer will do, but i used sweet baby ray's, and it was great)
1/4 cup vinegar
1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Mix bbq sauce with all ingredients listed under it.
Place chicken in crockpot. Pour sauce over it and cook on LOW for 4-6 hours.

Submitter: 
mrs.pie
Description: 
A little spicy barbeque chicken that simmers all day in the crock pot. Super easy preparation.
