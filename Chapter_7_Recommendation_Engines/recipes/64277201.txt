Title:
Melissa's Succulent Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken (about 3 lbs)
1 Tbsp of onion powder or garlic powder
1 med onion (quartered)
4-6 garlic cloves (sliced)
1-2 stalks celery (cut into thirds)
1 lemon (quartered)
1/2 cup butter
2-3 cups of beer or chicken broth
salt and pepper (to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*.
Place chicken in a roasting pan on rack, and season generously inside and out with salt and pepper.
Sprinkle inside and out with onion powder.
Place 1/4 cup of butter in the chicken cavity.
Arrange dollops of 1/4 cup of butter on top of chicken and under skin. Rub butter into chicken's skin.
Place cut celery, onion, lemon and some garlic pieces into the chicken cavity.
Slide rest of garlic pieces under skin.
Put some broth, beer, or other liquid in the bottom of roasting pan and arrange any leftover vegetables or lemon slices in broth.
Bake uncovered about 2 hours in the preheated oven, to an internal temperature of 180*.
Baste half way through cooking time.
Remove from heat, and baste with drippings.
Lightly cover with aluminum foil, and allow to rest about 20-30 minutes before serving.
