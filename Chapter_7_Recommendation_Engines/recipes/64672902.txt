Title:
Baked Parmesan Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 large beefsteak tomatoes, cut into 1/4” slices
1 c shaved Parmesan cheese
1 tbsp dried basil
1 tbsp dried oregano
2 tbsp fresh Italian parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400°.
On a greased baking sheet, arrange tomato slices.
Top with Parmesan cheese, and sprinkle with mixture of basil and oregano.
Bake for 10 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
Top with parsley, and serve immediately.
