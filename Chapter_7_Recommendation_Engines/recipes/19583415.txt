Title:
Mud Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of dry black beans
3 tablespoons of olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
1 bay leaf
salt

Directions:
Wash the beans.
Put beans in a pot with 6 cups of water.
Soak them for 4-8 hours.
Drain beans and put them back in the pot.
Add 6 cups of water.
Add the oil, the onion, and the bay leaf.
Simmer for 102 hours, until the beans are tender. Be sure they are always covered with water. Add salt and stir.
