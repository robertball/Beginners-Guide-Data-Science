Title:
Creamy Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SAUCE:
1 can (10.5 oz) cream of chicken soup
12 oz sour cream
FILLING:
3 c cooked, shredded chicken (lightly season with taco seasoning)
2 c shredded cheese
1/2 c (4 oz) sour cream
1 (4.5 oz) can chopped green chiles
1/3 c fresh cilantro, chopped
1/2 c mild taco sauce
6 (8") flour tortillas
TOPPING:
1 c shredded cheese
1 Tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350º.
Spray a 9 x 13 baking dish with non stick spray.
Mix together 12 oz sour cream and chicken soup to make the sauce. Spread a layer in the bottom of the baking dish. Set aside.
Stir together the filling ingredients in a large bowl, and divide it evenly into 6 portions.
Spoon each portion of filling down the center of a tortilla. Roll tightly and place seam down in the baking dish.
Spread remaining sauce on top of enchiladas.
Bake, uncovered at 350° for 35-40 minutes or until bubbly.
Sprinkle cup of cheese and cilantro on top, and return to oven till the cheese is melted.
Garnish with salsa, tomatoes, avacado. Eat with chips.

Submitter: 
Becca Owens
Description: 
Deliciously creamy enchiladas. Serve with freshly diced tomatoes and sliced avacado on top, and a side of rice to top it off.
