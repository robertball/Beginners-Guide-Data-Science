Title:
Blueberry Banana Oatmeal Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1411127.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup soy milk
1 frozen banana, sliced
1/4 cup frozen blueberries
1/4 cup oats
1 tablespoon chia seeds
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon white sugar, or more to taste

Directions:
Blend soy milk, banana, blueberries, oats, chia seeds, vanilla extract, and white sugar together in a blender until smooth.
