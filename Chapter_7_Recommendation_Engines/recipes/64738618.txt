Title:
Nacho Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups diced cooked chicken
1 can (15 oz) pinto beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 cup Old El Paso Thick 'n Chunky salsa
1 can (10.75 oz) condensed nacho cheese soup
1 1/2 cups (6 oz) shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded lettuce
1 medium tomato, chopped (3/4 cup)
1 can (2.25 oz) sliced ripe olives, drained (optional)

Directions:
1) Heat oven to 375*. In large bowl, mix chicken, beans, salsa and soup. Spoon evenly into ungreased 8-inch square (2 quart) glass baking dish. sprinkle with cheese.
2) cover dish with foil. bake 30-40 minutes. uncover dish; bake 5-8 minutes longer or until edges are bubbly. Top with lettuce, tomato and olives.
