Title:
Pork Noodle Skillet/Pampered Chef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium carrots
1 medium red bell pepper
5-6 green onions with tops(about 1 1/4 cups divided)
2 pork tenderloins(about one pound each)
2 Tablespoons toasted sesame oil
2 teaspoons each of garlic and onion powders
and ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground pepper
4 Packages ramen noodles oriental flavor
1 Tablespoon oil
4 cups water

Directions:
Cut carrots and peppers in thin strips. Slice onions and set apart tops for garnish.
Trim fat off potk tenderloins. Slice each tenderloin in 4 strips the long way. Thinly slice strips cross ways.
Combine pork with 2 of the seasoning packets from the soup, sesame oil and the other seasonings and mix well.
Add oil to large skillet and brown half of the pork at a time 2-3 min. Remove from the skillet and keep warm. Repeat with the second half.
Add carrots and peppers to the skillet ant saute 1-2 min. Add water and remaining seasoning packets; stir to loosen any brown bits. Add ramen noodles and white parts of onions; bring to boil and cook 4-5min. Add Pork and breakup noodles; remove from heat and allow to stand 2-3min. Garnish with reserved tops.

Rhonda Holtz Hill
