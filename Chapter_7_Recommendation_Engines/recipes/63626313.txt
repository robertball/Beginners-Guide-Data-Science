Title:
No Oil Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tb freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/4 tsp salt
1 15.5 oz. can garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
1 garlic clove, cut into a few pieces
1/4 water or low sodium broth

Directions:
Puree all ingredients in a food processor until smooth.

Submitter: 
Cindi Fortmann
Description: 
A great tasting hummus recipe, from The Biggest Loser's Bob Harper.
