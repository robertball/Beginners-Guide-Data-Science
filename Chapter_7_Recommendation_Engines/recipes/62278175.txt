Title:
Garden Vegetable Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. quinoa, rinsed
1 c. water
2 T. oil
1 med. onion, julienned
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 med. red bell pepper, diced
3 summer squashes or zucchinis, sliced into 1/4" thick rounds
1 c. sweet peas
6 oz. tomato paste
1 T. each--dry oregano, basil, thyme
2 eggs
2 c. provolone cheese, shredded
1 pound cottage cheese
1 bunch parsley leaf, chopped fine
1 large tomato, sliced

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine the quinoa and water; bring to boil.  Reduce to simmer, cover, and gently cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Meanwhile, heat oil in large skillet.  Saute onions, garlic, pepper, squash, peas, and spices till squash is soft.  Mix in tomato paste till smooth.  Let cool.
In a small bowl, combine eggs, cottage cheese, parsley and 1 cup of Provolone.
Combine cooked quinoa, sauteed veggies, and cheese mix together and press into a lightly greased 9 x 13 pan.  Top with tomatoes and remaining provolone.
Bake uncovered for 50 minutes at 350.  Remove from oven and let set 15 minutes before serving.
