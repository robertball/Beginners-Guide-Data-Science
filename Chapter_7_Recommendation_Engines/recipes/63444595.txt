Title:
Blueberry - Cheese Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. 8 oz refrigerated crescent dinner rolls
4 oz. Philadelphia Neufchatel Cheese, softened
2 Tbsp Sugar
1/2 cup blueberries, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven 375.  Unroll dough into four rectangles; firmly press perforations together to seal.
Combine Neufchatel and sugar; spread onto dough rectangles to within 1/2 inch of edges.  Top evenly with blueberries. Bring opposite corners together; press together to seal.  Place on ungreased baking sheet.
Bake 11 - 13 minutes, or until golden brown.
