Title:
Bacon Cheddar Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 egg
1/2 - 3/4 cup quick cook oats
1/4 cup BBQ sauce
1 tbl Montreal steak spice
Salt and pepper to taste
3 slices cheese
4-6 slices bacon

Directions:
- preheat oven 375 C
- mix hamburger and all ingredients ( besides cheese and bacon ) together in a bowl
- spray cooking spray on a loaf pan
- take half of hamburger mixture and put on the bottom of pan
- place cheese on top of hamburger them top with remaing hamburger
- line the top of meatloaf with bacon strips
- cook for 45 min then broil for additional 5 minutes

Submitter: 
Carolyn Showman
Description: 
Come on Bacon makes everything better!!!! This meatloaf is really moist and a hit with the whole family.
