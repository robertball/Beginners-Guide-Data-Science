Title:
Shelly's Special Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/753019.jpg
Ingredients:
3 blocks of cream cheese, softened
1 jar dried beef, minced
1 small can black olives, minced
1 small can of mushrooms, minced
1 bunch of green onions, minced
2 dashes of accent (msg)

Directions:
Place all ingredients into a bowl and mix until homogenous.
Serve with Ritz Crackers, or your favorite brand.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
This is a delicious cheese dip packed full of goodness!
