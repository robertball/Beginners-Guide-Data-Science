Title:
Patriotic Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990731.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups boiling water
1 (6 ounce) package JELL-O Berry Blue Flavor Gelatin
2 cups cold water
1 (8 ounce) tub COOL WHIP Whipped Topping, thawed, divided
1 cup fresh strawberries, halved

Directions:
Add boiling water to gelatin mix in large bowl; stir 2 minutes until completely dissolved. Stir in cold water. Pour into 9 parfait or dessert glasses.
Refrigerate 4 hours or until firm.
Spoon 2 cups COOL WHIP over desserts; top with berries and remaining COOL WHIP.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
Hooray for these red, white, and blue parfaits made with berry flavored gelatin, fresh strawberries, and whipped topping.
