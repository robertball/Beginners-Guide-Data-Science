Title:
New England Boiled Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb Corned Beef
1 Tsp peppercorns or 1/4 teaspoon pepper
6 whole cloves
1 bay leaf
6 potatoes, peeled and quartered
6 carrots, Cut in chunks
1 Medium head cabbage, cut into 6 wedges

Directions:
In large Saucepan, cover corned beef with water.
Add Pepercorns, cloves and bayleaf.
Simmer covered, 3 - 3 1/2 hours or until tender
Add potatoes and carrots
Simmer Covered, 15 min
Add Cabbage; Cook 15 min or until vegetables are tender.
Remove Bay Leaf.
Cut meat into pieces.
Serve with cooking liquid and vegetables
