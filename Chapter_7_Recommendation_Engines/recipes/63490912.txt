Title:
Spicy Peanut-Chicken & Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Grilled Chicken Breast (precooked) chopped
8 oz baby kale (half of the typical bag/container)
1/2 head green cabbage- shredded
1 red pepper- sliced very thinly
1 head romaine lettuce- shredded thinly
3 to 4 cups bean sprouts
1 apple- thinly sliced
2 cups shredded carrots and/or broccoli slaw
1 cup sliced radish (any kind)
1 cup thawed edamame (soy beans not in pods)
1/2 cup roasted salted peanuts
Dressing:
4 Tb sweet chili sauce or honey w/ red pepper flakes
1/2 lime juiced
1 Tb toasted sesame oil
1 cup prepared satay sauce (any)

Directions:
Prepare all the ingredients for the salad portion in advance, but toss with the sauce only when you're ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Karen Barris Calabro
Description: 
After an evening family workout, this meal answers the question, What do I feel like making for dinner tonight?  Lazy, healthy, and quick, it is one of my husband's favorite composition salads.
