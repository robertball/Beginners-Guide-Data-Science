Title:
Spaghetti Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2# Angel Hair Pasta
1 T. Italian Seasonings
3 cups Creamy Italian Dressing
2 cups real mayonnaise
1 cup chopped red & green bell peppers
2 cups shredded Cheddar Cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
3 T. sugar
1 cup Creamy Italian Dressing
1 3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup sugar
2 cups chopped tomatoes or sliced cherry tomatoes

Directions:
Cook & drain spaghetti. Don't overcook.
Add next 2 ingredients while still warm.
Cool & add next 5 ingredients.
Cover & refrigerate over night.
Next day, add the next 4 ingredients.
Wait until last minute to add tomatoes. Refrigerate until all is cold again.

Submitter: 
Peggi Weaver Tebben
Description: 
This is a great salad for catering. It makes a lot & people really love it. The longer you let it marinade, the better it tastes.
