Title:
Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6356005.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c flour
2 3/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 Tbl Italian Spice
1 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1 Tbl parm cheese or nutritional yeast
1 Tbl oil
1 c water
Pizza stick butter:
1/4 c melted butter
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp salt
1/2 Tbl parmesan or nutritional yeast

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients. Add water and oil. Stir until it forms a ball.
The dough should be soft, not sticky. Knead on a floured surface for 3-4 minutes, adding 1 TBL of flour if sticky, and repeating as necessary.
For pizza add sauce and toppings and bake at 400°F for 15-25 minutes, lifting pizza and ensuring that middle has browned.
For pizza sticks, roll into 1/2 inch ropes about 3" long and brush with oil. Bake on a parchment lined baking sheet at 400°F for 10 mins flipping after 5. Remove from oven, brush with pizza stick butter. Serve with pizza sauce.
