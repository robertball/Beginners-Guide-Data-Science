Title:
Baked Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4.5 lb potatoes, peeled and cut up
1/4c olive oil
2 pkg Italian dressing mix
1 green pepper, chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
1 bunch green onions, chopped
2 large tomatoes, chopped
4 boiled eggs, chopped
5 strips bacon
1-1/2c mayonnaise
1T vinegar
1T lemon juice
2t basil (dried)
1/4t garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Toss potatoes with oil and dressing mix.
Bake potatoes at 400 for 45 minutes.
Toss other ingredients and dressing.
Mix dressing with potatoes once cool.
Refrigerate at least an hour.
