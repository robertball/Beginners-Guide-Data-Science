Title:
Sam's Orange sauce for angle food cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C water
3/4 C concentrated orange juice
1 pkg instant vanilla pudding (3.4 oz.)
1 C whipped topping
1 Angel food cake

Directions:
Combine water, orange juice and pudding, beat on low until mixed, then beat on high for 2 minutes.  Whisk in whipped topping.
Serve over angle food cake.
