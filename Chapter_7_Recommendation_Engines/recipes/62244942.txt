Title:
Dianna's Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
handful of baby carrots, diced small
1 stalk celery, diced small
1/4 medium onion, chopped fine
2 cartons of chicken stock
2 1/2 to 3 lbs potatoes, diced
Parsley
Garlic powder
Italian seasoning
salt & pepper to taste
1 can evaporated milk
1 heaping tablespoon cornstarch
3 thick slices velveeta, cubed
1/2 cup cubed ham
Instant potato flakes

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large pan.  Saute carrots and celery until they have a little color.  Add onion to mixture and saute until soft.  Add salt & pepper.
Add chicken stock and potatoes.  Add all seasonings until broth is to your taste.  Cook until potatoes are tender.
In a bowl, whisk together evaporated milk and cornstarch.  Pour into hot broth and stir to mix.  Add velveeta and ham.  Stir all together until hot and cheese is melted.
Slowly add instant potato flakes until thickened the way you want it.
