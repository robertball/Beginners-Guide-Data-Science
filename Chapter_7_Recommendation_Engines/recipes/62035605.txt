Title:
Corwynn's Ham Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2Lbs. Honey Baked Ham, shredded
2Cs. Sour Cream
1C. Miracle Whip or Mayo
1/8C. or to Taste. Yellow Prepared Mustard {me, I love French's}
1/4C. or to Taste. dried Onions
1Tsp. or to Taste. Garlic Powder
1/2C. Sweet Pickle Relish, drained of excess juice
12 to 24 Spanish Olives with Pimiento, sliced width-wise

Directions:
In a food processor grate ham, set aside.
In a large bowl, place sour cream, mircale whip, mustard, onions and garlic powder. Mix well!!
To dressing mixture, add ham, pickle relish and olives, mix well.
Place in a air tight container and place in refrigarator and chill for at least 24 hours!!
Serve chilled with flavored crackers, triscuit or in a bread bowl with bread cubes.
