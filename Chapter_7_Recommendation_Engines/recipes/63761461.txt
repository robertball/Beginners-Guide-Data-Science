Title:
Linguine with White Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1043005.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Lbs linguine cook according to instructions
1/3 cup  extra virgin olive oil
2 Anchovy fillet or paste
6 cloves of garlic (minced )
2  10 oz can Bubble Bee Whole Baby Clams with 1 -1/2 can of clam juice set aside
1/4 stick unsalted butter
1/3 cup  white wine/ cooking sherry
1/2 cup parsley (chopped)
1-1/2 tsp dried Oregano
1/2 tsp red pepper flacks
1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Over medium-high heat up 1/4 cup of extra-virgin olive oil in a large saute pan. Add anchovies and cook until dissolved into the oil. Next add your  garlic and cook until it starts turning light brown. Add butter until it melts and stared to bubble then add parsley and seasonings,cook for about 1 minute. Next add wine and clam juice cover and reduce heat to medium - medium low until it starts to simmer. Add clams until heated through and pour over pasta and top with Parmigiano-Reggiano
