Title:
Smoked Salmon Pizza Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/569472.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sheet of puff pastry
1/3 cup reserved tomato soup (from previous recipe)
1/3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup crumbled smoked salmon
1.5 tsp chopped chives

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Roll out puff pastry to 1/4'' thick. Cut out circles with round cookie cutter and place them on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake in a preheated oven for six minutes.
Top the puff pastry with the tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and smoked salmon.
Turn the oven temperature up to 450 degrees and put the pizzas back in for another five minutes.
Take out of the oven and serve warm.
