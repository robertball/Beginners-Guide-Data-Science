Title:
BACARDI速 Rum Balls Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/4 cup BACARDI速 Gold Rum
2 cups ground walnuts
1 1/2 cups vanilla wafer crumbs
1/2 cup honey
Confectioners' sugar

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine walnuts and wafer crumbs.
Stir in BACARDI(R) Gold and honey. Shape into 1-inch balls.
Roll in confectioners sugar.
Store in tightly covered container.
