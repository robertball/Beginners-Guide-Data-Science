Title:
Roasted Garlic and Fontina Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of garlic, roasted
4 chicken cutlets
1/8 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dried basil
1/2 tsp. olive oil
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/4 cup shredded Fontina cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Sprinkle chicken with basil and pepper.
Oil baking dish and bake chicken for 15 minutes or until done.
Squeeze garlic out of head and mix with salt.
Spread 1/4 of the garlic over each peice and sprinkle with cheese.
Bake for 3-4 minutes of until cheese is melted and bubbly.
