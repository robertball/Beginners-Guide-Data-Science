Title:
POPPYCOCK Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/755192.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups brown sugar
1 cup marg.
1/2 cup cornsyrup
pinch salt
pinch of cream of tarter
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. vanilla
16 cups popcorn
1 cup almonds

Directions:
Make popcorn,put 16 cups in roaster,add the 1 cup of almonds.             Melt marg. add brown sugar and syrup.BOIL-5min.Remove from heat.Add salt,cream of tarter,baking soda and vanilla.Pour over popcorn and almonds.Bake at 200 for 1 hour.Spread wax paper on table.Remove popcorn from oven,spread on wax paper and quickly separate popcorn before it hardens.
