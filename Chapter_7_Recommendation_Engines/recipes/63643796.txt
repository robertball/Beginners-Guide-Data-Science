Title:
Sweet Italian Pita Pizzaz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz) pkg al fresco Sweet Italian Chicken Sausage
1 tbsp Colavita Extra Virgin Olive Oil 6 ea Whole wheat pita bread, large ( 6 1/2")
1 cup Tomato Sauce, low sodium

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400&deg;.
Heat olive oil in a medium skillet over med-high heat. Saut&eacute; chicken sausage until evenly browned, about 10 - 12 minutes.
Remove from heat and cut &frac14;" slices. Set aside.
Spoon 2 - 3 tbsp tomato sauce over each pita bread, Divide and spread chicken sausage over each pita. Cover with shreeded cheese.
Place in oven, bake 5 minutes or until cheese melts. Optional: Use your favorite al fresco Chicken Sausage flavor, and finish with mushrooms, peppers, onions or different toppers.
