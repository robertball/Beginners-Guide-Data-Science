Title:
Blueberry French Toast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
French Toast:
1 loaf  French bread (cubed)
1 pkg   cream cheese (8-oz)
1 cup   blueberries (fresh or frozen)
1 doz   large eggs
1/3 cup maple syrup
2 cups  milk
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cinnamon
Blueberry Syrup:
1 cup  water
1 cup  sugar
2 tbsp cornstarch
1 cup  blueberries (fresh or frozen)
1 tbsp butter

Directions:
French Toast: Arrange 1/2 of the bread cubes in a greased 9 x 13 pan. Sprinkle cream cheese cubes and blueberries evenly over the bread cubes. Top with the remaining bread cubes.
Whisk together eggs, milk, maple syrup, salt, nutmeg and cinnamon. Pour over bread cubes and cover pan with aluminum foil. Let chill in refrigerator overnight.
Bake covered with foil at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake an additional 30 minutes or until fluffy and golden.
Serve with blueberry syrup on the side and top with whipped cream.
Syrup: In a large saucepan cook water, sugar and cornstarch until thickened stirring frequently. Stir in the blueberries and simmer for 10 minutes. Add butter and stir until melted.
