Title:
Sausage Rotel Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz reduced fat breakfast sausage, cooked, fat drained
16 oz reduced fat cream cheese
2 cans Rotel tomatoes

Directions:
Add everything together to the crock pot and heat on low, stirring every 30 minutes or so, until fully blended and heated to desired temperature.
Serve with corn chips.
