Title:
Diced Vegetarian Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Chopped Celery
Olive Oil
1 C Chopped Onions
1/3 C Milk
1/2 t Salt
2 C Diced Fri-Chick
1 Can Mushroom Soup
4 oz Can Mushrooms
1/2 C Mayonnaise
1 Medium Grated Onion
2 C Cooked Rice
1 t Chicken McKays Seasoning
Crushed Potato Chips

Directions:
Braise celery and chopped onions in a small amount of olive oil.  Make sauce of mushrooms, mushroom soup, milk, mayonnaise and grated onion.  Combine all ingredients and pour into a shallow casserole dish.
Sprinkle with crushed potato chips and bake at 350 degrees F.
Bake for 45-50 minutes
