Title:
Cream of Anything Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
2 T  onion, chopped
3-4 cups chicken broth (or boullion)
1 carrot, diced (optional)
1 celery stalk, diced (optional)
1/2 cup flour
2 cups anything diced; broccoli, cauliflower, potatoes, mushrooms, chicken & rice is yummy!
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 cups milk, scalded
Dash of cayenne pepper

Directions:
Melt butter.
Saute onion (carrots & celery) until crisp and tender.
Mix flour, salt, black pepper, and cayene pepper. Add broth (or boullion) stir constantly.
Continue to stir while bringing to a  boil.
Cook for 1 minute. This mixture will be very thick. Stir in "anything."
Cover and cook over low heat for 30 minutes, stirring
occasionally.
Remove cover and stir in scalded milk.
Cook, uncovered, at low heat for 5-10 minutes. Serves 4-6

Submitter: 
Krystal
Description: 
Given to me by my very good friend Christie, this warm, healthy and delightfully creamy soup will warm your bones and put a smile on your face.
