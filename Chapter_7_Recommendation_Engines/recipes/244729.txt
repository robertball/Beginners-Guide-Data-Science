Title:
Roasted Garlic Hummus from Hidden Valley® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2497410.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (15 ounce) cans garbanzo beans, drained
1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds
1 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Roasted Garlic Dressing
Paprika (optional)

Directions:
In food processor fitted with metal blade, process beans, sesame seeds, and dressing to desired consistency.
Dust with paprika just before serving. Serve with pita chips or fresh vegetables as dippers.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley®
Description: 
Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Roasted Garlic Dressing makes a creamy, delicious addition to this quick and easy hummus.
