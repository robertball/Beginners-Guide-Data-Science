Title:
Stuffed Cabbage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean ground beef
1 large onion, diced
1 head cabbage, cut into 1 inch pieces
2 cups cooked rice
2 cans condensed tomato soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 lb bacon
8 cups water
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot brown ground beef.  Add onions and cook until onions are tender.
Add cabbage and 2 cups of water.  Simmer until cabbage is soft.
While meat and cabbage are simmering, cut bacon into 1/4 inch pieces and fry until crispy.  Drain and set aside.
When cabbage is soft, add remaining water, soups, rice, and crispy bacon. Bring to boiling, reduce heat and simmer for about 45 minutes.  If soup is too thick, add additional water.
Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Suzey Veitch
Description: 
Comforting soup without the long prep of stuffed cabbage.
