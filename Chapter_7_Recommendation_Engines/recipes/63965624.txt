Title:
Strawberry Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ice cubes
16 strawberries, fresh
1/2 cup frozen concentrated limeade
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Place ice cubes in a blender and pulse until crushed.  Add the strawberries, limeade and water.  Puree until smooth and thick
