Title:
Layered Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2591777.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. block cream cheese, softened
1/4 c. sour cream
1 T. taco seasoning
1 - 1 1/2 c. fresh pico de gallo or salsa
1 14 oz. can black beans, drained and rinsed
I - 1 1/2 c. finely shredded lettuce
1- 1 1/2 c. finely shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, sour cream and taco seasoning, using an electric mixer to make it very smooth and free of lumps. Spread evenly in a large loaf pan. Wipe any smears from sides of dish with paper towel.
Add each additional ingredient in the order listed, taking care to make layers even.
Serve with tortilla chips.
