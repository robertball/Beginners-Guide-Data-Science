Title:
Garlic Parmesan Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 tsp. onion salt
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp black pepper
15 chicken wings
1 cup flour

Directions:
Add 1/2 tsp garlic powder and to 1 cup flour and shake in large bag to coat chichen wings. Deep fry as desired. Mix butter, remaining garlic powder, onion salt, black pepper, parmesan cheese in large bowl (with cover) and mix. Place cooked wings in bowl, cover and skake to coat.

Submitter: 
Charles Millwood Jr.
Description: 
Savory garlic parmesan chicken wings for snack or meal. Great while watching your favorite football game.
