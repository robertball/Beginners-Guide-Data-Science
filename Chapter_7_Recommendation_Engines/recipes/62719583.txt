Title:
Wieners and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp brown sugar
1 Tbsp molasses
1/4 cup ketchup
1 tsp mustard
2 (16 ounce) cans baked beans with pork
8 beef frankfurters

Directions:
Heat beans on medium and add all the ingredients except the hot dogs.  Stir gently or over cook.
Wrap hot dogs in paper towel and microwave on high for 55 seconds
Unwrap hot dogs on cutting board.  Using a knife and fork, slice them into bite sized chunks
Add hot dogs to beans and heat through and serve right from the pan.

Submitter: 
Katrina Freed
Description:
