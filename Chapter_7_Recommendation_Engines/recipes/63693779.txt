Title:
Wild Berry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1026882.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. All purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1/4 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Cinnamon
1/2 tsp. Ginger
1 C. Granulated sugar
1 C. Almond milk
2 Eggs
1/4 C. Canola oil
1/2 C. Oat bran
1 1/4 C. Berries (blueberries, Saskatoon berries, huckleberries,)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 12 cup muffin pan and set aside
Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, sugar and spices all together and set aside
In a large bowl put milk, eggs, oil and berries together. Stir in oat bran with a wire whisk and blend well.
Add flour, sugar, salt and spices to the wet ingredients and mix until just incorporated into the batter.
Pour muffin batter into greased muffin cups filling 2/3 full and bake in oven for 20 minutes or until lightly browned on top.
Remove from oven and let stand for 5 minutes before putting on a serving platter.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A pleasantly sweet berry with oat bran adding a nice chewy texture to the muffin
