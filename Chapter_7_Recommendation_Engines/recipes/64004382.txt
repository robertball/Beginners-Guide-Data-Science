Title:
Half Hour Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C brown sugar
1 Tbsp. butter
2 cups hot water
1/2 C raisins
1/2 tsp. vanilla
/2 C sugar
1 Tbsp. butter
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 C flour
1/2 C milk

Directions:
In a saucepan put 1 C brown sugar, 1 Tbsp. butter, 2 cups hot water, 1/2 C raisins
Boil together for 2 minutes. Remove from heat and add 1/2 tsp. vanilla.
While that's boiling, in an ovenproof 2 qt. dish (fairly deep), cream together 1/2 C sugar and 1 Tbsp. butter.
Add 2 tsp. baking powder,1/2 tsp. salt, and 1 C flour. Blend together, then add 1/2 C milk and beat til smooth.
Pour the sauce over the dough and bake for 30-40 minutes at 375 degrees
Serve warm.
