Title:
TINY PECAN PIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
** DOUGH =
1/2 CUP BUTTER
1  3OZ PKG CREAM CHEESE
1 CUP ALL PURPOSE FLOUR
** FILLING =
1 EGG
3/4 CUP BROWN SUGAR
1 TABLSEPOON MELTED BUTTER
1 TEASPOON VANILLA
1/2 CUP FINEL CHOPPED NUTE

Directions:
** FOR DOUGH, MIX BUTTER, CREAM CHEESE
ADD FLOUR, CHILL MIX ONE HOUR MIN.
****ROLL IN 1 INCH BALLS
PRESS UP SIDES OF ungreased MINI MUFFIN TINS
*** FOR FILLING ****
MIX ALL TOGETHER
FILL TIN W/1 ROUNDED TEASPOON FILLING..
BAKE AT 325 FOR 25-30 MIN
