Title:
Corn & Carrot chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slices of bacon
1TB olive oil
8 baby carrots diced
1 small onion diced
3 stalks of Celery diced
1 med red pepper  diced
2   jalapeños  seeds and membranes removed
1/4 cup & 1 TB  real butter
1/3 flour
3 small golden potatoes diced
2 bay leaves
1 bag frozen corn
1 1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Add bacon, olive oil,carrots, onion, celery,  jalapeños, & red pepper cook until all veggies soften on med heat.
Add butter and flour mix with a wisk until blended
Add potatoes and bay leaves cook 10 minutes
Add corn and heavy cream cook additional 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Carla Lybarger
Description: 
Flavorful veggie chowder that is quick and easy to make.
