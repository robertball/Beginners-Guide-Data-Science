Title:
Green Apple Curried Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 bunch spinach
1/4 cup chopped green onion
1 tart green apple chopped into medium chunks
1 cup whole almonds
Dressing:
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 tbsp red wine vinegar
4 tbsp mango chutney
1 tsp curry powder
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp tumeric
1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 C. light vegetable oil

Directions:
Combine salad ingredients in bowl.
Combine dressing ingredients in separate bowl and blend until smooth and well mixed.
Drizzle on salad just before serving.

Submitter: 
PTRICK
Description: 
This salad has a unique blend of spinach, green apples, and almonds drizzled with a mango chutney infused curry dressing.  The flavours come together for a wonderful eating experience.
