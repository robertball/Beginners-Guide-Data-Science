Title:
Collard Greens Southern Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/742621.jpg
Ingredients:
4 lg. bunches of collards
1/2 cup sugar
8 chicken bouillion cubes
2 smoked neck bones
1 tbs garlic powder
2 tbs. salt
1 tsp. back pepper
1 tsp. red pepper flakes
2 tbs. olive oil
1/4 c. red wine vinegar

Directions:
Devein collards
Stack leaves and roll
cut in 1 inch strips
wash collards
place in large pot with above ingredients
fill pot with water until collards start to float
bring to a boil, covered
reduce heat and cook on low heat for 3 hrs, covered
stir occaisionally.
