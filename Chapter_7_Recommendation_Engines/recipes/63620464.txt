Title:
Greek Chicken Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (32 ounce) package uncooked Penne Pasta
8 (6 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breasts
3 (10 ounce) bag chopped fresh spinach
2(8 ounce) can sliced black olives
4 cups crumbled feta cheese
2 cups Italian Dressing

Directions:
Fill a large pot with lightly salted water, and bring to a rolling boil over high heat. Once the water is boiling, stir in the pasta, and return to a boil. Cook the pasta, stirring occasionally, until the pasta has cooked through but is still firm to the bite, about 10 minutes. Drain well, and return pasta to the pot.
Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a Deep baking dish.
Lay Spinach on bottom of baking dish. Put Chicken breasts on top.
Sprinkle with Feta cheese and pour bottle of Italian dressing over all.
Mix in Balck Olives if desired.
Bake 350 2 hours covered tightly.
mix with Pasta before serving

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
The lively Mediterranean flavors of tangy feta cheese and briny black olives transform this simple chicken and pasta bake into a company-worthy dish.Modified from: Greek Lazy Lasagna
