Title:
Poppy Seed Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1/4 cup poppy seeds
4 eggs
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. vanilla
2 cups sugar
1 cup buttermilk
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 sticks of butter - softened

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Cream sugar and butter until fluffy.
Add buttermilk, vanilla and eggs and mix well.
Add dry ingredients and mix well.
Pour into greased Bundt pan and bake for 55-60 minutes or until cake tests done.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar before serving.
