Title:
Our Favorite Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987367.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons oil
3 tablespoons water
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
2 teaspoons McCormick速 Minced Onions
2 teaspoons McCormick速 Worcestershire Ground Black Pepper Blend
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 pound steak, such as New York strip, rib-eye or sirloin steak

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, except steak, in small bowl.  Place steaks in large resealable plastic bag or glass dish.  Add marinade; turn to coat well.
Refrigerate 30 minutes or longer for extra flavor.  Remove steaks from marinade.  Discard any remaining marinade.
Broil or grill over medium-high heat 6 to 8 minutes per side or until desired doneness.  Sprinkle with additional Worcestershire Pepper, if desired.
