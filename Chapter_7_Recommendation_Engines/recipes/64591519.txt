Title:
Banana Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium bananas,  firm to ripe,  peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 red onion, finely diced
2 ripe avocados
juice of 1 lemon
juice of 2 limes
1-2 red chillies, deseeded and diced
1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves, roughly chopped (or to taste)

Directions:
Put the diced banana in a bowl with the onion.
Stone, peel and dice the avocados and add to the bananas.
Mix in the lemon and lime juice, chillies and cilantro.

Submitter: 
mikekey
Description: 
Great accompaniment for jerk pork or fish.  Do not make more than an hour ahead of serving or bananas will start to brown.
