Title:
Imitation Lobster Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg imitation lobster chunks
1 stick celery finely chopped
3-4 jalepeno rings finely chopped
1 Tbsp green onion finely chopped
1/2 cup mayo
1 tsp maple syrup
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl, mix well.  Better chilled, but you can also eat it right away. Spread on rolls and enjoy.  OR you can also spread the filling on rolls and cover with shredded cheese ie: Monteray Jack, and warm in oven til cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
CraZee A
Description: 
Close your eyes food snobs!  This uses the more affordable imitation lobster, but if that's what you have on hand it's not a bad quick meal or snack, and the price is right.  It's also quick and easy :-)
