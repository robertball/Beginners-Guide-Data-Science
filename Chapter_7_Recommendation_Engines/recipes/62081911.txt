Title:
Chocolate Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ c. flour
¾ c. margarine
¾ c. finely chopped pecans
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 c. powdered sugar
large container of Cool Whip
2 boxes instant chocolate pudding
3 c. milk

Directions:
Mix together flour, margarine and pecans.  Press into 9 x 13 pan and bake 15 minutes at 375.   Let cool.
In mixer, beat cream cheese and  powdered sugar.  Fold in 1 ½ c. Cool Whip.  Spread over crust.  In a large bowl, mix pudding with milk.  Beat 2 minutes or more.  Spread over first layer.  Spread Cool Whip on top.  Sprinkle with chopped pecans and grated unsweetened chocolate.  Chill overnight or until chilled through.
