Title:
Hot Mulled Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 qts. apple cider
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 cinnamon stick
4 cloves

Directions:
Combine ingredients in slow cooker.
Cover.  Cook on low 5 hours.  Stir.
