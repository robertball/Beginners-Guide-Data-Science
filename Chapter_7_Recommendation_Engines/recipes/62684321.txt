Title:
Whole Grain Pancake Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
***Dry Mix ingredients:
4 C. Whole Wheat flour
2 C. All-Purpose Flour
1 C. Wheat Germ
1 C. Oat Bran
1 C. Buttermilk powder (found near powdered milk)
3 Tbsp. Baking Powder
2 Tsp. Baking Soda
1 Tsp. Salt
***To 2 C. dry mix, add:
2 Eggs
1 C. Water
2 Tbsp. Vegetable Oil
2 Tbsp. Honey

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients in gallon ziploc bag for storage in fridge.
When ready to make, add wet ingredients to 2 C. dry mix and cook on hot griddle.
