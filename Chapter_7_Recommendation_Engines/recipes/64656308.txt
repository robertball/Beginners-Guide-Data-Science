Title:
Instant Pot® Double Bean and Ham Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4551432.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups dry navy beans
2 cups chicken broth or stock
2 cups water
1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes, undrained
2 medium carrots, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 cup chopped ham
1 (16 oz) can pork and beans, undrained
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Put navy beans, chicken broth, water, tomatoes,  carrots, celery, and onion into the Instant Pot® and stir.
Put the lid on the pot, lock in place, and turn the valve to Sealing.  Press the Manual button, high pressure, and set the timer for 45 minutes.  When countdown is complete, do a Natural Pressure Release.
Remove lid, stir in the  ham, pork and beans, and season to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
lutzflcat
Description: 
No pre-soaking beans required.  I know it sounds strange, but my mother-in-law always added a can of pork and beans to her homemade bean soup and, seriously, it adds another layer of flavor.
