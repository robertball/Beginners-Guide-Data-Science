Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2 cups sugar
3/4 cups oil
1 Tbsp. vanilla
2 1/2 cups flour
3 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
2 cups grated zucchini
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Beat eggs until frothy.
Add sugar and beat until creamy.
Add oil and vanilla.
Blend in dry ingredients, then the zucchini.
Add raisins and nuts if desired.
Pour into 2 greased loaf pans.
Bake 1 hour in 350 degree preheated oven.
