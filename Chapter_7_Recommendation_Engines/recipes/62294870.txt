Title:
Sausage Quiche Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. bulk pork sausage
1 c. (4 oz) shredded cheddar cheese
1 c. (4 oz) shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 tsp. chili powder
1/2 c. finely chopped onion
1 can (4 oz) can of chopped green chilies
10 eggs
1 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook sausage until no longer pink, then drain. Place cooked sausage in a greased 13x9 baking dish. Layer with cheese, onion, chilies. In a separate bowl, beat eggs and seasonings. Pour over cheese. Bake uncovered at 375 degrees for 18-22 minutes, or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes. Cut into one-inch squares.
