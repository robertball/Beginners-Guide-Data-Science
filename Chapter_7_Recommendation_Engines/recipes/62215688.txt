Title:
Cabbage and Vegetable Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small head of cabbage
1 small head or broccoli crown
1/2 cup chopped english cucumber
1 medium onion
2/3 cup creamy salad dressing (Hellmans)
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/3 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon white vinegar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon poppy seeds
1/2 cup chopped green onion, white and green parts

Directions:
Shred the cabbage in food processor, and place in large mixing bowl. Cut up the broccoli and cucumber in small chunks and mix with cabbage.
In the food processor, combine onion, Hellmans dressing, vegetable oil, sugar, vinegar, salt and pepper.  Blend until onions are almost pureed (about 30 seconds).  Mix in poppy seeds and pour dressing mixture over cabbage/broccoli/cucumber and toss to coat.  Chill at least 2 hours before serving. Sprinkle with green onion before serving.

Very easy to make. Adding the broccoli and english cucumber is a nice change to standard coleslaw.Modified from: Sweet Restaurant Slaw
