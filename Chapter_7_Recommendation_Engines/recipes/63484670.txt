Title:
Huntington Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz package of egg noodles (5 cups noodles)
1 rotisserie chicken (1 1/2 lbs chicken breast)
1 onion, chopped
3 stalks celery, sliced
1 red bell pepper, chopped
2 4oz cans sliced mushrooms
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can chicken broth
1 lb velveeta cheese, cubed

Directions:
bring a pot of water to a boil and cook noodles as the package directs.  shred meat from the rotisserie chicken or poach chicken breasts, cool, and cube.  In a large pot saute onion, celery, and pepper in 1 tbs olive oil.  Add mushrooms (including liquid), soup, chicken broth, and cheese.  Heat slowly until cheese melts.  Drain the cooked noodles and add with the chicken to the vegetables and cheese sauce.  Let stand for a few mins to thicken.  Season as desired with salt and pepper.  Freezes well.  Reheats well.
