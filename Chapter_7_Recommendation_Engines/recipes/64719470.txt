Title:
Creamy Mexican Chicken in Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.75 lbs chicken breast
4-5 cloves garlic, chopped
1 small white onion, sliced
4 T Mexican seasoning rub (approx half of recipe; see below)
3 small bay leaves
2 4oz cans hatch green chiles (mild)
1 c chicken stock, divided
8 oz cream cheese
3/4 c sour cream
cheese, cilantro, green onion for serving
Mexican Seasoning Rub
2 T cumin
2 tsp chipotle powder
2 tsp smoked paprika
2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp chile powder
1 T sea salt

Directions:
place sliced onion and garlic in bottom of slow cooker
put chicken on top of onions
rub 4 T of seasoning over chicken on each side
pour 2 cans of hatch green chiles over chicken
put 3 bay leaves in
add 1/2 c to 1 c chicken stock
cook on low for 6 hours
after 6 hours, combine approx 1/2 c broth/juice from chicken, 8 oz cream cheese, 3/4 c sour cream, 1/4 to 1/2 c more chicken broth
blend with immersion blender
shred chicken with forks
pour cream cheese mixture back into slow cooker and cook for another 2 or more hours
serve with shredded monterey/cheddar cheese, cilantro, lime, green onion, red chile flakes as desired
