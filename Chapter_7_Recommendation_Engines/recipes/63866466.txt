Title:
Hershey's Kisses Candy Cane Blossoms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
48-hershey's kisses brand candy cane candies
1/2 cup-butter,softened
1 cup- sugar
1-egg
1/2 tsp- vanilla extract
2 cups- all purpose flour
1/4 tsp- baking soda
1/4 tsp- salt
2 tbsp- milk
Red or green sugar crystals, granulated sugar, or powdered sugar.

Directions:
1. Heat the oven to 350. Remove wrappers from candies.
2. Beat butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla in large bowl until well blended. Stir together flour, baking soda, and salt; add alternately with milk to butter mixture, beating until well blended.
3. Shape dough into 1 inch balls. Roll in red (or green) sugar crystals, granulated sugar, powdered sugar, or a combination of any of the sugars. Place on a cookie sheets.
4. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until edges are lightly browned and cookie is set. Remove from oven; cool 2 to 3 minutes. Press candies into the center of the cookies.
5. Remove from cookie sheets, place onto wire racks. Cool completely
