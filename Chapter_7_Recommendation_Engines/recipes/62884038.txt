Title:
BEET LEAF BUNS [DOG BONES] Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Your favorite bread recipe.
50 or so beet leaves,may have to cut in half[remove stem]
1 Tbsp.marg.
2 chopped green onions
2 Tbsp.chopped fresh dill
2 cups whipping cream
1/4 tsp.each salt and pepper

Directions:
Take about a Tbsp.of dough roll into small roll about 3 inches long.Wrap with beet leave.Grease cookie sheet. Once cookie sheet is full,bake for 25 min.at 350.
The buns can be frozen to use at a later time.Start with 30-40 made buns.In large skillet melt the marg.Cook green onion till just soft.Add cream and bring to just gentle boil,simmer for 10 min.Add dill,salt and pepper.Add buns and cover.Cook for 3-5 min. Serve immediately,as they get tough quickly.They should swell as they suck up the cream.[I've also tried fat free cream and no one knew the difference]
