Title:
Canned Corned Beef Hash Spruced Up. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 pieces of bacon cut up
1 Small onion diced
1 Tablespoon minced garlic
1 small green pepper diced
1 can corned beef hash
1 4 oz can of mushrooms
Salt and Pepper to taste
4 Eggs beaten
1/4 cup milk
3/4 cup shredded cheese (I prefer an italian blend)

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute bacon, onion, minced garlic, and green pepper.  Once bacon is cooked and onion is translucent, mix in can of corned beef hash and mushrooms.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Brown the corn beef hash by flipping in sections occasionally. Once corned beef hash is near done, combine the beaten eggs and milk and pour into hash mixture, stirring occasionally.  Add cheese and serve.

Submitter: 
HollyG
Description: 
This is a quick and easy way to spruce up canned corned beef hash.  Makes a great comfort meal. Adjust ingredients according to preference.
