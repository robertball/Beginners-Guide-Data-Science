Title:
Cheddar cheese sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups milk (heated until warm)
3 cups sharp grated cheddar cheese
2 Tbl. butter
l or 2 Tbl. flour
l/2 tsp. dry mustard
pepper to taste

Directions:
In a sauce pan melt butter then stir in flour to make a roux. Slowly pour in milk stirring constantly until thickened. Add dry mustard and pepper and finally cheese until melted. You can add more cheese if you wish.

Submitter: 
LINDA MCLEAN
Description: 
A creamy cheese sauce great with vegies or a base for macaroni and cheese
