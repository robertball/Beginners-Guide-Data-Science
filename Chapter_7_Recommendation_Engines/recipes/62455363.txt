Title:
Renee's Bread/Cinnamon Roll/Maple Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pkg Yeast
2c lukewarm water
1 egg, well beaten
2 tsp salt
5 Tbsp vegetable oil
5c to 6c flour
4 Tbsp sugar

Directions:
Mix together yeast and water in a large bowl.  In a small bowl, mix egg, salt, sugar, oil, and flour.  And dry mix and water to yeast and knead.  Cover dough with thin layer of oil arise in large bowl, 1 or 2 times. Punch down.  Divide to make loaves or rolls, let rise then bake.
Cinnamon Rolls
Roll out 1/2 of the dough; spread on generous amount of butter.  Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.  Roll up and cut in 1 1/2 inch portions.
Cinnamon Roll Glaze
1/2c butter
1/2c brown sugar
1 Tbsp corn syrup
2 Tbsp water
1/2c pecans
Melt together in the bottom of a cast iron skillet.  Put rolls in the skillet and bake at 375 for 25 to 30 minutes.
Maple Bars Glaze
1c powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
Maple favoring
Milk (enough to make it creamy)
Roll out dough and cut in to strips, fry the strips cover with icing.
