Title:
Pancake or Waffle Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744809.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cup Water
1 tsp. Vanilla
1/4 Cup Pure Maple Syrup

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a sauce pan and then bring it to a boil.  Boil gently until sugar is completely dissolved. I store mind in an air tight bottle in the cupboard.  Keeps for a long time.
