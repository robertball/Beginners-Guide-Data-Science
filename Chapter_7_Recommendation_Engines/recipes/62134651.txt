Title:
Vegetable Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 lb ground beef
-cayenne pepper, italian seasoning, sugar(just to season beef with, so you dont need a lot)
-1 onion chopped(optional)
-1 tsp garlic salt
-3 cups beef bullion
-1 small bag of frozen vegetables
-8 oz. can tomato sauce
-1 can diced tomatoes
-1 cup elbow macaroni

Directions:
1.place ground beef and chopped onion in pan, turn to med. high, sprinkle on seasonings, and garlic salt. cook till done; drain
2.pour the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, frozen vegetables in a dutch oven, or large pot, or even crock pot. Put in beef bullion with correct amount of water, according to instructions.
3. after draining beef, add it into the soup mixture, cook on low- med, for about 4-5 hrs or until vegetables are no longer crunchy stir every once in a while to prevent it from sticking. For crock pot about 8 hrs on low
4.if making in a crock pot, cook the noodles in a seperate pot then add to the soup, but if in a regular pot, you can either add uncooked noodles to mixture, or seperatly cook them.
***to make extra just add more beef bullion with the water, if you use more than 5 cups beef bullion...then add another can of tomato sauce. ***
