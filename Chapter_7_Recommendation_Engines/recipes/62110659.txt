Title:
Really Yummy Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28oz can of vegetarian baked beans
1 large onion (preferably vidalia or red)coarsely chopped
1 large green pepper coarsely chopped
2 tbsp of olive oil
3 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp mustard
2 tbsp of ketchup
2 tbsp barbecue sauce

Directions:
Saute onion and green pepper in olive oil over medium high heat till wilted.
Reduce heat to low and add remaining ingrediants and stir.
Cook for approximately one hour stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
NainInCandia
Description: 
Easy,sweet and savory beans cooked on the stove top that will be a hit at any gathering.
These are really delicious served over brown rice.
