Title:
Navy Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2017628.jpg
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. package dry Navy Beans rinsed and soaked overnight in cold water to cover.
8 cups cold water
1 cup diced ham
1 large white onion, chopped
1 carrot, chopped
1 chicken bouillon (packet/cube)
1/2 tsp salt
6 whole peppercorns
1 bay leaf
1/2 cup garlic mashed potatoes

Directions:
1.  Rinse Soaked Navy Beans in cold water.  Place Navy Beans in 5 qt. pot.  Add 8 cups      cold water.
2.   Add all ingredients, except the mashed potatoes.
3.   Bring to a boil.  Cover and reduce heat to simmer.
4.   Simmer 3-31/2 hours.  Remove bay leaf.
5.   Add 1/2 cup garlic mashed potatoes last 1/2 hour.
