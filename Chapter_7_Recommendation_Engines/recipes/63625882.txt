Title:
Turkey meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb extra lean ground turkey
1 egg, beaten
1/2 c cooked brown rice, cooled (I subbed oat fiber)
3 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 c onion, finely chopped
1/4 c chopped fresh parsley or 4 tsp dried
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp oregano

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and shape into 30 meatballs 1 inch across.  Bake @ 400 for 20 minutes.  Alternatively, spray a large nonstick skillet with olive oil.  Cook the meatballs for 5-6 minutes, occasionally moving them around to brown on all sides.

Submitter: 
Cindi Fortmann
Description: 
Great healthy meatballs.  Awesome source of protein.
