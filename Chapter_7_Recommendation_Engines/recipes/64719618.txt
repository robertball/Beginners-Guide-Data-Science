Title:
Jeannie's Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cube melted butter
1/4 cup cocoa powder
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
dash of salt
1 cup sugar
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp Aluminum-Free Baking Powder

Directions:
Melt 1 cube butter and mix with  cocoa powder.
In a separate bowl beat 2 eggs  with  1 tsp vanilla.
add butter and cocoa powder mix to the eggs and vanilla.
Add salt, sugar, flour and baking powder.
Mix well.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes in a 9x9 pan.
