Title:
Spinach Pesto Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package fresh baby spinach, stems removed
2 ounces pine nuts
6 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup fresh chopped basil
3/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup grated Parmesan
16 ounces tri-color pasta

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions, reserve a small amount of cooking liquid
Place spinach in large saucepan
Pour boiling water over spinach- enough to completely cover spinach
Let stand, covered, for 1 minute, then drain
Rinse spinach in cold water, press to remove excess moisture
Combine pine nuts, garlic, and salt in food processor and process until smooth
Add spinach and basil to food processor, process until smooth
Add olive oil in a thin stream while processing constantly
Process until mixture forms a smooth paste
Add Parmesan nad mix well
Combine sauce and pasta and toss to mix
Add enough reserved cooking liquid to reach desired consistency
