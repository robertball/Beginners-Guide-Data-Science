Title:
Peas with sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/741833.jpg
Ingredients:
2lbs  petite frozen peas ( I like the ones from Trader Joe's)
1 medium onion
1 small can diced tomatoes
Kelbasa sausage (such as Hilshire Brand or any smoked sausage you preffer)
Salt, Pepper, Smoked Paprika, and Garlic powder to taste
Fresh baby dill
Olive oil to sautee

Directions:
In a large pan dice the onion and sautee in olive oil on medium heat until slightly brown, add the sausage cut into slices and sautee until sausage is slightly browned.  Add the frozen peas, can of diced tomatoes, salt, pepper, generous amount of smoked paprika and garlic powder.  Mix with wooden spoon, when it starts simmering lower the head to low and cover pan.  Cook approx 30 mins or until peas are tender, adjust seasoning as needed.  When done remove from fire, add chopped baby dill ( approx 2 Tbsp) mix and serve. Enjoy!
