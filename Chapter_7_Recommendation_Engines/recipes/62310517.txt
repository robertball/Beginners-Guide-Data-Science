Title:
Salsa-Chicken Tostadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2-in. pieces
1/2 C water
1 pkg. (1-1/4 oz.) taco seasoning mix
1 C refried beans, warmed
4 tostada shells (6-in)
1/2 C Mexican style four cheese, shredded
1 C shredded lettuce
1/4 C chunky salsa
1/4 C sour cream

Directions:
Cook and stir chicken in large nonstick skillet on med-high heat 3 min. or until no longer pink
Add water and seasoning mix; simmer on med-low heat 5 min. or until chicken is done and sauce is thickened
Spread beans onto tostada shells; top with layers of chicken mixture and remaining ingredients.
Garnish with chopped cilantro or green onions before serving, if you wish
