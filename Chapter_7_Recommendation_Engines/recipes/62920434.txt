Title:
Stacey's Chicken and Stuffing Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Rottessarie Chicken meat removed
2 Tablespoons Butter
1 medium onion, chopped
10.75oz can cream of celery soup
5oz can evaporated milk
Large bag cornbread stuffing mix
1 T Sage
1/2 t poultry seasoning
1 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Remove chicken from bones.   Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In skillet melt 2 T butter and add diced onion.  Cook until translucent.  Add chicken and mix well.  Pour evaporated milk and cream of celery soup into chicken mixture.  Mix well.  Spread into 13x9 baking dish.  Combine chicken broth and melted butter.  Empty cornbread stuffing into large bowl and add spices.  Pour  broth mixture over.  Toss to combine.  Pour cornbread stuffing evenly over chicken.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
