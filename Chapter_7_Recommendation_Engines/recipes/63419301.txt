Title:
Roasted Pear Salad with Chevre and Fig Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/923705.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Bosc, Comice, Concorde, or Bartlett pears, halved and cored (Vons & Staters)
8 tsp plus 2 Tbs fig jam, divided (Riley's Ranch)
5 oz. soft goat cheese, cut into 8 slices (Vons)
1 Tbs olive oil, plus more for drizzling, divided
2 Tbs lemon juice
1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
4 cups watercress or baby arugula (Vons)
1/2 sm red onion, thinly sliced (like shreds 1/2 cup)
1/4 cup chopped toasted walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Place pear halves cut-side up in 2 large baking dishes
Spoon 1 tsp jam in center of each pear half. Top with goat cheese rounds, and lightly drizzle with oil. Bake pears 30 mins, or until cheese begins to brown.
Whisk together remaining 2 Tbs fig jam, lemon juice, and mustard in bowl. Whisk in 1 Tbs olive oil
Divide arugula among 8 serving plates. Sprinkle with onion and walnuts. Top each with pear half, and drizzle with dressing.

Description: 
This is an easy, elegant fall salad that can be served as a light supper
