Title:
Eggrolls, the best Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground meat
1 onion, grated
black pepper
1/2 head cabbage, shredded
3-4 carrots, shredded
soy sauce, to taste
1 good handful of fresh bean sprouts, rinse and drain
1 package of large egg roll wrappers
oil for frying

Directions:
In large pot brown ground meat; drain well.
Add grated onions; cook and stir.
Add black pepper and soy sauce to taste.
Stir in bean sprouts.
Add carrots & cabbage stirring until heated keeping them a little bit crisp.
Place 1 heaping tablespoon of meat mixture on each egg roll wrapper; wrap according to package directions.
Deep fry (you can use a skillet) until golden brown on all sides.  Drain on paper towels.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
Homemade egg rolls, you can add/omit what you like and follow this basic recipe.  (I serve these with my Sweet & Sour Sauce, see recipe box)
