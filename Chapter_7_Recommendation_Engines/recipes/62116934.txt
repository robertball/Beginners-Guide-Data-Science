Title:
Greek Layered Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Hummus
1/2 C diced red onion
1 C finely diced cucumber
1 C finely diced tomato
1/2 C crumbled feta
1/2 C diced kalamata olives
1 C greek yogurt
2 cloves minced garlic

Directions:
Mix yogurt and garlic, set aside.
In large pan or flat bottomed bowl, spread hummus across bottom.  Layer red onion, then cucumber, then tomatoes.  Spread yogurt mixture across top.  Sprinkle feta then olives over the top.  Serve with pita chips or crackers.
