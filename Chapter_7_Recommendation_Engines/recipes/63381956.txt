Title:
Longhorn Steakhouse Like Parmesan Crusted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless chicken breasts, cut in half
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
1/4 cup plain bread crumbs
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup ranch dressing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
1. Using a pastry brush brush chicken tops with mayonnaise.
2. Mix cheese and bread crumbs together and place on chicken tops (saving some for later.)
3. Place in oven and bake for 40 minutes.
4. Open oven and put ranch dressing and remaining bread crumbs on top of chicken.
5. Cook for 5-10 minutes more until browned.

Submitter: 
Carol Marie
Description: 
Tastes like your eating the famous Longhorn Steakhouse Crusted Chicken, but home.
