Title:
Iced Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups water
4 cups brown sugar
8 - 10 cups double strong brewed coffee
To serve:
1/2 & 1/2 or milk
ice

Directions:
Boil water and sugar until sugar is dissolved.
Add coffee.
Cool and refrigerate.
To serve, mix @ ratio of 2/3 coffee mixture, 1/3 milk and add ice.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Keep this on hand for a quick refreshing summer drink. Just add milk or 1/2&1/2 with ice.
