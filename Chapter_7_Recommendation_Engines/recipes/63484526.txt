Title:
Coronation Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mayonnaise
2 tbsp mango chutney
2 tbsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp white vinegar
3/4 tsp onion powder (or diced onion)
1-2 tbsp curry powder
salt
pepper
2 cooked chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized cubes
1.5 stalks celery, finely diced
1/4 cup raisins and/or dried cranberries
1/4 cup almonds, chopped
Sandwich bread or crackers (optional)
Sliced cheddar cheese (optional)
Rocket/Arugula (optional)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix mayonnaise, mango chutney, lemon juice, vinegar, onion powder, curry powder, salt, and pepper.  Stir until mixed well.
Add chicken, celery, raisins, and dried cranberries.  Stir to coat all ingredients.  Refrigerate overnight to let flavors mix together.
Before serving, add chopped almonds.
Serve with crackers or as a sandwich - add cheddar cheese and/or arugula if desired.
