Title:
Southwestern Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 t vegetable oil
1 t paprika
3/4 t chili powder
1/2 t pepper
1/2 t cumin
1/4 t coriander
1/4 t cayenne

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and marinate a 4 pound chicken. Bake at 325 for 35 minutes per pound. The gravy made from the drippings taste like Swiss Chalet Dipping Sauce if you add a little pepper sauce and salt.

This recipe from a magazine was a family favorite 30 years ago.
