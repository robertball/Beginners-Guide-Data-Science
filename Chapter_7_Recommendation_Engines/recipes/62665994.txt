Title:
Chicken Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/594242.jpg
Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts cooked and cut into small pieces
1/2 lb. bacon chopped
1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
6 cloves garlic minced
1/4 cup white grape juice
1 1/2 cup chicken broth
3 egg yolks
1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup freshly grated asiago cheese
1/2 lb. spaghetti noodles

Directions:
In a large pot cook spaghetti noodles while preparing chicken carbonara
In a large skillet cook bacon until crisp on medium heat.
In a separate bowl whisk egg yolks until smooth.
Add cooked chicken breast to skillet and mix with bacon, grease and all.
Add crushed red pepper flakes and minced garlic to skillet and cook in bacon grease about two minutes.
Add grape juice and chicken broth to skillet and reduce heat to low.
Take several spoonfuls of hot juice from skillet and add it to egg yolk and whisk together.
Pour egg yolk mixture into skillet being careful not to cook yolks. You'll need to keep stirring the mixture now.
Stir in parmesan and asiago cheese until sauce is thick. A gravy consistency.
Add drained spaghetti noodles to skillet and mix well.

An Olive Garden inspired recipe. This creamy parmesan sauce with chicken and bacon is a delicious end to a long day. If you're not counting calories, this will be a hit.
