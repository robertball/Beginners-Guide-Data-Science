Title:
CASTAGNOLE  FRITTE ( LEMON FRITTERS) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2eggs
3Tbsp.sugar
1 and 3/4cups flour
4Tbsp. olive oil
3Tsp.brandy
pinch of salt.
1/2Tsp.vanilla extract
grated peel of 2 medium sized lemons.
Oil for deep frying (About 2-3 cups.)
1 cup of icing sugar,more if needed.

Directions:
In a large bowl,beat the eggs with the sugar until light and fluffy.Add the flour,olive oil,brandy,salt,vanila extract,and lemon peel.Mix with a wooden spoon until well blended.Heat the oil in a large deep pot for deep frying.Drop by teaspoons of the batter mixture into the hot oil.Use 2 teaspoons to achive the round shape of the fritter.The fritters will puff up,so fry a few at a time.When golden brown,transfer to paper towels with a slotted spoon,and dust with icing sugar.

Submitter: 
manella
Description: 
Growing up these were made at Christmas and Easter,as a treat,now we enjoy them all year long.Easy and quick to make.
