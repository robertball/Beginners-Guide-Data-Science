Title:
Newman's Tuscan Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (5 ounce) bag mesclun mix or baby lettuce
1/2 pears - peeled, cored and sliced
1/2 cup toasted pine nuts or pecans
1/3 cup Newman's Own® Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing, or more to taste

Directions:
Rinse and fry mesclun mix and place onto plates. Divide pears and nuts evenly between two servings. Drizzle with Newman's Own Balsamic Vinaigrette dressing to taste.

Submitter: 
Newman's Own®
Description: 
For this Tuscan inspired salad, toasted pine nuts and fresh sliced pears are served on a bed of greens with a drizzle of balsamic vinaigrette.
