Title:
Buffalo Chicken sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 rotisserie chicken, deboned and chopped
2 stalks celery, finely chopped
1/2 cup matchstick carrots, chopped
8 oz crumbled blue cheese
2/3 cup wing sauce
1 package of dinner rolls

Directions:
Mix all ingredents in a baking dish. Heat at 400 degree for 20 minutes. Serve on dinner rolls.

Submitter: 
Mmmmichele
Description: 
Just like the mini burgers, but with a buffalo style twist. Great for football parties! So simple to make!
