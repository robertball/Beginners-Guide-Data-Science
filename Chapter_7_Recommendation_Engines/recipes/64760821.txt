Title:
Dill Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads dill per qt
1-2 garlic cloves per qt
small pickling cucumbers
Brine for 1 qt:
1 1/2 cups water
1/2 cup vinegar
1 1/2 tbsp. course (pickling salt)

Directions:
Place heads of dill and garlic in hot sterilized jars. Fill with small cucumbers to within 1" of top.
Brine: Combine ingred. in saucepan. Bring to boil.
Pour over cucumbers to within 1/4" of top. Seal.
