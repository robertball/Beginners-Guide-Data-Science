Title:
Baked Hawaiian sandwhiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ligonberry jam
Ham
swiss
1/2c butter
1/4c dried onion flakes
1/4c sugar
2T poppyseeds
2T sesame seeds
1T honey mustard (or just mustard)

Directions:
Place buns on a cookie sheet with parchment paper under neath. Layer with jam, ham, and cheese.
Top with melted butter, sugar, seeds, and mustard
Bake 350* for 15-20 min.
