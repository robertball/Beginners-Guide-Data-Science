Title:
Chowmein tuna noodle casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkj. (14 oz.) steam fried chinese noodles
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
one large onion diced
2-4 stalks celery diced
1 cup frozen peas
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms (opt.)
1 large shredded carrot
cracker crumbs or shredded cheese for topping.

Directions:
Simply mix all ingredients in a large bowl,(except for cracker crumbs) then put into a casserole dish. Spread cracker crumps on top, cover and bake for 45 min.  Take off cover off for the last 15 min.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
PATRICIA53
Description: 
This is a delicious and very simple tuna casserole.  Great for those busy days when we want something easy and hearty.  Adjust the vegetables to your personal taste.
