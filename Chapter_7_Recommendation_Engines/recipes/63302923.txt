Title:
Rainbow Pasta Salad for the Church Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-16 oz tricolor spiral pasta
2 med carrots (julienne)
1 lg red onion (chopped)
1 med bell pepper (chopped)
½ tsp celery seed or 2 celery ribs (chopped)
½ 6 oz can black olives (sliced)
7-oz stick pepperoni (chopped)
4 oz Monterey Jack w/horseradish (cubed)
½ 16 oz frozen petite peas
1-14 oz sweet condensed milk
1 cup vinegar
1 cup Miracle Whip
¾ cup sugar
1 tsp NaCl
½-1 tsp coarse black pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta per pkg directions.  Rinse in cold H2O + drain; place in lg container.  Add carrots, onion, bell pepper + celery.  Add olives, pepperoni, cheese + peas.  In another bowl, combine remaining ingredients.  Pour over pasta mixture; toss to coat.  Cover.  Regrig 8 hr or o’nite.  Serves min 12-14 Just fits lg round Rubbermaid bowl.

Submitter: 
eaglerock777
Description: 
My husband and I loved to prepare dinners for  various church celebrations. This is a pasta salad filled w/veggies + meat and with a sweet tart dressing that will leave no leftovers!
