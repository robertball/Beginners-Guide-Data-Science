Title:
Campbell's Kitchen Easy Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (regular, 98% Fat Free, or 25% Less Sodium)*
1 (10 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
1 cup cubed cooked chicken
2 pillsbury refridgerated pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Place one crust in bottom of 9 inch pie plate.
Mix soup, vegetables and chicken in bowl then pour into pie plate.Cover with second pie crust. cut several slits into top crust. Bake 30 minutes or until golden.
