Title:
Breakfast Ham Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
12 slices Sara Lee® Soft and Smooth 100% Whole Wheat Bread
1 (14 ounce) bag frozen broccoli florets
3 cups cooked ham, diced
1/2 pound processed pasteurized cheese
6 eggs (well-beaten)
3 cups milk
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon dry mustard

Directions:
Cut bread into 2 inch squares and place into well-greased 9 x 13 inch casserole dish. Add broccoli, ham and cheese (cut into bite-size pieces).
In a separate bowl, add milk to eggs and beat well. Add salt, pepper and mustard. Pour egg mixture over bread mixture in casserole. Refrigerate overnight.
Bake, uncovered, for 1 hour in 350 degree F oven.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee®
Description: 
Filled with broccoli and ham, this cheesy breakfast casserole is perfect for entertaining, but easy enough for everyday.
