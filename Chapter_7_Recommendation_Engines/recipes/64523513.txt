Title:
Chinese Almond Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3016959.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon almond extract
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup sliced almonds
1 egg white
1/2 teaspoon water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar; beat in egg and extract. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture. Chill in refrigerator for 30 minutes.
Roll into 1-in. balls. Place 2 in. apart on un-greased baking sheets lined with parchment paper.  Flatten balls of cookie dough with a fork. Place sliced almonds on top of the flattened dough. In a small bowl, beat egg white and water.  Brush over cookies.
Bake in upper 1/3 of oven for 16-18 minutes or until edges and bottoms are lightly browned.  Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks. Yield: about 3 dozen
