Title:
Easy as Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/475570.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup oil
pinch of salt
1 tablespoon milk
1/4 cup boiling water
2 cups flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Heat water in the microwave until it boils (usually a minute 30 seconds does it.
In a bowl add oil, salt, milk and water together and stir well.
Add 2 cups of flour and stir with a fork. Divide the dough into 2.
Place one piece between two pieces of waxed paper and roll into a circle.
Place the rolled out dough into a pie pan.
Cook for about 5-8 minutes while rolling out other piece of dough between same waxed paper.
Fill the pie with favorite filling and top with second crust.
Pinch edges of pie together and make slits in the top of the pie for vents.
Cook additional 35-45 minutes until crust is golden brown.
