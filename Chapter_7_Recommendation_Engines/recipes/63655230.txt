Title:
Rhubarb Crisp Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1015801.jpg
Ingredients:
Wet:
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
1 egg
1/4 cup oil
Dry:
2/3 cup white flour
2/3 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1-2 cups rhubarb (diced)
Topping:
1/4 brown sugar
1/4 cup large flake oats
2 Tbsp melted butter
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 and line 12 muffins cups.
Combine dry ingredients, including rhubarb, in large bowl. Make well in middle.
Combine wet ingredients (yogurt, egg, oil) in small bowl. Pour into dry ingredients and mix JUST until blended. Should be a thick dough-like consistency.
Combine topping mix and pat about 1 Tbsp on top of each muffin.
Bake for 20-25 minutes until done. Allow to cool in pan 15 mins before removing to cooling rack.
