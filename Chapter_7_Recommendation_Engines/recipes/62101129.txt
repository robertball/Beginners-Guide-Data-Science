Title:
Easy Cheese Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup flour
1 1/4 cup milk
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 cup white wine
200g cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
Melt the butter in a saucepan and mix in the flour. Stir in the milk and continue stirring until thickened and boiling. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the garlic, wine and cheese. Keep warm, but do not reboil. Serve with chunks of French bread for dipping.

Description: 
This cheese fondue is extremely easy, but delicious.
