Title:
Cheese Meat-less Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups colby longhorn cheese, grated
1 medium onion, finely chopped
4 eggs
1 cup bread crumbs
3/4 cup walnuts
Vegetable oil for frying
2 teaspoons boullion mixed with 2 cups water (or 1 15 oz. can vegetable, beef, or chicken broth)

Directions:
Mix together cheese, onion, eggs bread crumbs and walnuts. Form into 1-1/2 inch balls.  Fry lightly in vegetable oil.  Put into baking dish. Pour broth over meatballs and bake for 45 minutes in a 350 degree oven.

Submitter: 
KGora
Description: 
This is a tasty vegetarian alternative to meatballs.  These meatless balls tastes great served with noodles and gravy.
