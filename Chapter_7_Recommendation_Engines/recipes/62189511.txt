Title:
Angel on Vacation Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/450491.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 Angel food cake mix
1 20 oz.can crushed pineapple undrained
Frosting:
1 tub SF cool whip
1 sm box instant banana pudding
Toasted coconut/chopped almonds

Directions:
Mix the cake and the pineapple.
In a 9 x 13 in pan,Do Not spray with Any oil, add the batter and bake @ 350 degrees for 45-50 mins. Cool
Mix the cool whip and pudding together and frost the cake. Decorate with toasted coconut/almonds.

Submitter: 
Patty Cakes
Description: 
I heard about this cake when I was a Weight Watcher. I have changed it a little. The cake is very light and a different way to have angel food cake.
