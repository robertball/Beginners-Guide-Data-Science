Title:
Banana bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bananas
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup buttermilk or heavy whipping cream
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a loaf pan with cooking spray.
Mash the bananas with a fork or a potato masher until smooth.
Melt butter in saucepan or microwave. Cool for five minutes.
Crack eggs in the bowl with the bananas. Add the melted butter, sugar, buttermilk or whipping cream, and vanilla.
Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir until the flour is wet. Pour into prepared pan.
Bake for about 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until a fork poked into the middle of the bread comes out clean. Cool for five minutes.
