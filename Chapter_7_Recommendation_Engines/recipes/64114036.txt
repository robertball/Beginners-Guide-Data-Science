Title:
Corn Avacado Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1278835.jpg
Ingredients:
3 ears sweet Corn (not cooked)
1 Avacado, cubed
30 cherry Tomatoes, cut into 2-3 pieces each depending on size
1/2 medium sweet Onion, diced
Juice of 1/2 Lime
20 Basil leaves, sliced and halved

Directions:
Shuck the corn and remove as many silks as possible.  In a medium bowl, with high sides, cut the kernels off the ears of corn.  Remove any silks from the bowl.  Add the avacado, tomatoes, onion, lime juice, and basil leaves.  Stir ingredients gently.  Place salad in the refrigerator to cool until just before serving.

Submitter: 
LonestarNR
Description: 
Fresh summertime vegetables make this salad a very refreshing side dish on a hot day.
