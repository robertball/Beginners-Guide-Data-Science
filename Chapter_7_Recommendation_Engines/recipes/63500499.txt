Title:
Rum Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. semi-sweet chocolate pieces
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup rum
3 Tbsp. light corn syrup
2 cups crushed vanilla wafers (about 60 Weis brand)
1 cup very fine ground walnuts
Granulated sugar for rolling

Directions:
In a sauce pan, melt chocolate pieces over low heat.
Remove from heat and stir in sugar, rum and corn syrup.
Fold in vanilla wafers and nuts together.
Shape the mixture into 1 inch balls using about 2 tsp. of the mixture for each.  Roll in additional sugar.  Use some sugar to take moisture out of hands as needed.
Store in an air tight container for several days.
