Title:
Mulled Wine (Vin Chaud) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 750mL Bottle Red Wine (Cabernet Sauvignon, Beaujolais, Burgundy, or similar)
2 Cups Apple Juice
3 Tbsp Honey or Agave
8 Whole Cloves
10 Cardamom pods
3 Cinnamon Sticks
1/2 tsp Anise

Directions:
Place everything in a pot & simmer over medium-low heat
Can be kept warm on the stove over low heat
Optional - pour into glasses through a strainer or use a spice bag to keep the spices in the pot and out of your glass

Submitter: 
Liz
Description: 
This is an easy mulled wine recipe that fills the house with an amazing aroma!
