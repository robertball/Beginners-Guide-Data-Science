Title:
Roasted Brussels Sprouts and Grapes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
1 pound red or green seedless grapes
3 tbsp olive oil
Fresh thyme to taste
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 375F.  On a baking sheet or Pyrex pan, toss the Brussels sprouts and grapes with the oil, thyme, salt and pepper.
Occasionally toss to brown on each side. Roast until golden brown, approximately 30-45 minutes.
