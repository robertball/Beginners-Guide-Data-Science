Title:
Designer Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
choose 4 cans of beans from the following list:
black beans
dark kidney beans
light kidney beans
yellow wax beans
green (cut) beans
****************************************
1/2 cup celery (medium dice)
1/2 cup red bell pepper (medium dice)
1/2 cup green bell pepper (medium dice)
1/4 cup chopped green onion (green part only)
1/4 cup white or yellow onion (small dice)
*********************************************
3/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 Tablespoon dried parsley
3 Tablespoons granulated sugar
3 Tablespoons water
1 package dry Italian salad dressing mix (about 1/2 ounces) i buy the Kroger 4 pack
toss together beans with vegetables- add dressing mixture- mix lightly-
cover and chill  - overnight is best

Directions:
choose 4 cans of beans - rinse and drain-put in large mixing bowl- set aside
chop and toss vegetables together - set aside
whisk dressing ingredients together- set aside
toss vegetables with beans- add dressing mixture- mix lightly
cover and chill at least one hour-overnight is best
