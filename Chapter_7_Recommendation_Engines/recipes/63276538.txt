Title:
Easy Bbq Southwestern Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless chicken leg quarters
1 (cup) bbq sauce
1 1/2 (teaspoon) southwestern seasoning
1 (teaspoon) each salt and pepper
1 (teaspoon) chicken fajita seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Marinate leg quarters in all ingredients for 15 minutes.
Place a sheet of foil on a cookie sheet, and wrap leg quarters in foil. Seal it up tight to make sure there are no openings.
Bake for 45 minutes. When done spoon remaining sauce over chicken till glaze appears, then serve.
