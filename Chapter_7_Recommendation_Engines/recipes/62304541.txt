Title:
baked chicken parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/385102.jpg
Ingredients:
chicken cutlets
egg
salt & pepper
bread crumbs
olive oil.
1 24 ounce jar of marinara sauce
1 lb sliced fresh mozzarella

Directions:
season the chicken with salt and pepper, dip in beaten egg, then bread crumbs. in batches, cook in olive oil over medium heat until golden. pour in 1 24 ounce jar of marinara sauce in a large, shallow baking dis; top with the chicken, then 1 pound sliced fresh mozzarella. bake at 400 degrees F until golden, 25 to 50 mins.
