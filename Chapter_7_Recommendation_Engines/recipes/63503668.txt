Title:
Easy Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar (or Splenda), divided use
3 cups peaches, sliced (frozen works well)
1 stick marg
1 cup flour
3/4 cup milk
salt to taste
cinnamon to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Melt margerine in 9x11 baking pan
Sprinkle 1 cup splenda over peaches; mix well & set aside
Mix remaining cup of splenda w/baking powder & flour. Mix in milk. Pour over melted marg. Place peaches on top of batter; do not sir. Bake for 1hr.
