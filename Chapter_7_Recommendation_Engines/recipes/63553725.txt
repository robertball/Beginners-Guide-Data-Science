Title:
Purple eggs and beets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans whole beets
1 cup vinegar
1 cup white sugar
8 whole cloves
1 tsp cinnamon
1 dozen hard boiled eggs

Directions:
Cook eggs until hardboiled.
Remove shells.
Pour the juice from the beets into a medium sized bowl.
To the juice add the vinegar, sugar, cloves and cinnamon. Stir until sugar dissolved.
Put the juice into a large glass container with lid or a large plastic or glass bowl with a lid.
Add the beets and eggs.
The eggs will turn purple and you eat them with salt and pepper on them. The beets are good too.
The eggs will keep for 5-6 days in the liquid.
