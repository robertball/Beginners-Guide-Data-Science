Title:
EAGLE BRAND速 Chocolate Chip Cheesecake  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978650.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups finely crushed cream-filled chocolate sandwich cookies
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND速 Sweetened Condensed Milk
3 large eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 teaspoon Pillsbury BEST速 All Purpose Flour

Directions:
Heat oven to 300 degrees F.  Combine cookie crumbs and butter;  press firmly on bottom of ungreased 9-inch springform pan or 13x9-inch baking pan.
In large bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Gradually beat in sweetened condensed milk until smooth.  Add eggs and vanilla; mix well. In small bowl, toss 1/2 cup chocolate chips with flour to coat; stir into cream cheese mixture.
Pour into prepared pan.  Sprinkle remaining 1/2 cup chips evenly over top.  Bake 55 to 60 minutes or until set. Cool. Chill. Garnish as desired. Refrigerate leftovers.

The addition of a cup of miniature chocolate chips results in a cheesecake sensation!
