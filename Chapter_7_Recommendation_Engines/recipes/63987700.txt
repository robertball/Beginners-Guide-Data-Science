Title:
Sweet Potato, Pineapple and Cranberry Marshmallow Casserole! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Sweet Potatoes
1/4 cup Brown Sugar
1 tsp of Cinnamon
1 tsp of Nutmeg
1 pkg of dried Cranberries
1 tbs of Butter
1 can chunk Pineapples
1 Bag of mini Marshmallows

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350 dg.
Peel sweet potatoes then Heat potatoes in microwave until soft, 3 minutes at a time in microwave turning bowl or plate each time until fork easily slides to the middle of potatoes.
Once soft slice potatoes into half dollar size squares then place in a casserole dish.
Add 1-tbs of butter to the middle of dish.
Sprinkle A handful of Cranberries into dish.
Drain 1 small can of pineapple chunks and spread over potatoes.
sprinkle Brown Sugar on tops of potatoes.
sprinkle Cinnamon all around the brown sugar.
Sprinkle the Nutmeg.
Place in oven for 15 minutes.
Remove after 15 minutes and add enough marshmallows to cover the dish, place back into the oven for another 10-15 minutes or until marshmallows are puffy and light brown in color then remove.
Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
