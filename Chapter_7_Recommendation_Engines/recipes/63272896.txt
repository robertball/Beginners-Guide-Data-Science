Title:
Rhubarb Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. brown sugar
1 egg
2/3 c. vegetable oil
1 t. vanilla
1 t. baking soda
1 c. sour milk
3/4 t. salt
2 1/2 c. flour
1 1/ c. diced rhubarb
1/2 c. sugar
1 T. butter
cinnamon

Directions:
Mix all ingredients (except the last 3) together.
Mix the remaining three ingredients and sprinkle on top of batter.
Bake in a greased loaf pan at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
