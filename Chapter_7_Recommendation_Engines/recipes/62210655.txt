Title:
Almond Spekulatius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
350 grams Flour
140 grams Sugar
170 grams Butter
1 Egg
100 grams ground Almonds
1 teaspoon Cinnamon
1 teaspoon Baking Soda
1/2 package Spekulatiusgewurz
1 teaspoon cocoa

Directions:
Mix ingredients together.
Knead into a soft dough.
Refridgerate the dough for 2-24 hours.
Piecewise, roll the dough into thin, 1/4 inch sheets, and cut-out cookies with cookie cutters.
Place on parchment paper on a baking sheet.
Bake at 180ÂºC.
Check after 10 minutes and rotate if oven cooks unevenly.
Cookies are finished when slightly brown on the edges.

Andreas's mother taught me this traditional German Christmas cookie recipe. The crispy cookies smell heavenly and keep well, thus making them ideal for gifting.
