Title:
Late night blueberry cream pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/565643.jpg
Ingredients:
1 single deep dish pie crust
custard:
2 cups milk
1/2 cup white sugar
3 tbsp cornstarch
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
1 tbsp butter
topping:
2 cups frozen blueberries
1/4 cup orange juice
1/4 cup white sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsbp cornstarch

Directions:
Bake the pie crust as directed for a custard or pudding pie.
Make the custard:
Whisk the sugar, salt,and cornstarch into the cold milk.
In a heavy bottom saucepan, heat over medium heat until small bubbles form, reduce heat.
Stir until the mixture is thick and coats the back of a spoon, about 15 minutes. Do not boil!
Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla. Pour into a new container and place in refrigerator to chill for 5 minutes, stirring twice.
Topping:
In a heavy bottomed pot, combine blueberries sugar and cinnamon and heat, stirring.
In a small bowl, whisk cornstarch into orange juice.
When the berries are hot, add the juice/cornstarch mixture and bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
Cook until the berries break down, and the mixture is thick.
Remove berries from heat and set aside to cool a bit.
Pour the custard into your baked pie shell and chill 5 minutes, until the top is set.
Pour the berry mixture over the custard, and return the pie to the fridge. Let chill until full cooled and set.
