Title:
Three Cheese Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
3 Tblsp butter
3 Tblsp flour
1/2 tsp salt
dash pepper
3 cups milk
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup swiss cheese
1/4 cup grated cheese
1/4 cup plain bread crumbs or panko crumbs

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions and drain.  In a sauce pan melt butter on medium high heat.  Mix together flour, salt and pepper.  Slowly add the flour mix to the melted butter and stir until smooth.  Gradually add milk stirring constantly.  Cook and stir until thick and bubbly.  Remove from heat.  Add the shredded cheeses and stir until melted. Stir in grated cheese.  Mix macaroni and cheese sauce.  Pour into a greased 2 quart casserole.  Sprinkle bread crumbs on top and bake uncovered 35 - 40 minutes until top begins to brown.

Submitter: 
Suzanne
Description: 
This is a great old school homemade mac n cheese.  It is easy to make and it comes out creamy and delicious with a nice crunchy topping.  You can substitute different types of cheeses, I have also made with is colby/jack cheese instead of swiss cheese.
