Title:
Award Winning Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985902.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup Parmesan cheese (grated)
8 ounces grated mozzarella
1 pinch garlic salt

Directions:
Chop artichokes and mix together with all other ingredients.
Put in shallow pan and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
Serve with tortilla chips, bread or crackers.
