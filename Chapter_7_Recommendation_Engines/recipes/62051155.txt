Title:
Spicy Spinach & Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter or margarine
1 cup onion, chopped
2 - 10 oz. pkgs. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well drained
14 oz. can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
8 oz. sour cream
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 cup grated romano cheese
2 tbsp. hot sauce
salt to taste
assorted crackers & bite size sliced pumpernickel bread

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet, add onion, cook about 5 minutes.  Stir in spinach, artichokes, cream cheese, sour cream 3/4 monterey , 3/4 romano, hot sauce and salt.  Stir till well blended and heated through.  Pour into 1/2 quart casserole dish, top with remaining monterey and romano cheeses.  Bake in 350 oven until cheese starts to brown, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
Great appetizer for any gathering all year round!!!  I receive requests to bring this dish to friend and family functions.
