Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. - Ground Beef
2 - 6 oz. cans Tomato Paste
1 - 6 oz. can Water
1 - 14.5 oz. can Beef Broth
2T - Chili Powder
1/2T - Onion Powder
1t - Ground Cumin
3/4t - Garlic Powder
1/2t - Salt
1/2t - Brown Sugar
1/4t - Ground Black Pepper
1/4t - Dried Oregano
1/8t - Ground Cayenne Red Pepper

Directions:
Brown meat, combine all ingredients in slow cooker. Stir occasionally, and let simmer until your ready to eat it.
