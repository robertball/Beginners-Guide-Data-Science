Title:
Gluten Free Pumpkin Date Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4684007.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups gluten free rolled oats
1 cup chopped, pitted dates
1/2 cup chopped almonds
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup coarsely chopped gluten free pretzels
3 tablespoons chia seeds
1 cup chopped hazelnuts
2/3 cup water
1/2 cup ground flax seed
2 ripe bananas
2 tablespoons honey
1 cup pumpkin puree

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.  Place parchment paper on a large cookie sheet and set aside.
In a large bowl, gently mix together oats, dates, almonds, raisins, dried cranberries, pretzels, chia seeds, and hazelnuts.  In a small bowl, mix together the water and ground flax.  Let sit for 5 minutes.
In a medium sized bowl, mix together bananas honey and pumpkin puree.  Gently mix into oat mixture until soft and even.  Spread onto the cookie sheet to form a 1/2 inch even layer.
Bake for 25-33 minutes, or until browned and slightly gooey.  Let cool, and cut into rectangles.  Enjoy!
