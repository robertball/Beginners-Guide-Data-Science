Title:
Linda's Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 lb. mild pork sausage (bulk)
1 medium onion, chopped
1 c. rice, uncooked
1 c. celery, chopped
1 can cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 can cream of chicken soup, undiluted
2 T. soy sauce
2 c. water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Brown the ground beef and sausage approx. 5 minutes, breaking into small pieces as it cooks. Add the onion and cook until meat has lost its pinkness and onion is soft. Drain off the fat. In a 2-quart casserole dish, combine the rice, celery, soups, soy sauce and water with the cooked meat and onion. Stir to combine ingredients. Cover and bake 60 minutes. Uncover and bake 15 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Diana S.
Description: 
This is an easy to prepare casserole with lots of flavor. You can substitute hot sausage for the mild if you prefer.
