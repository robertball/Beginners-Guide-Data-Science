Title:
Perfectly Easy Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/655882.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of crushed ice
1 cup of slightly frozen fruit of your choice
1 cup of vanilla yogurt
1 cup of milk of your choice - fat free or whole
4 tbls. sugar or honey

Directions:
Place ice and slightly frozen fruit in a blender and pulse until well crushed.
Then add remaining ingredients and blend on high until smooth and well mixed. You may need to stop and scrape the sides of the blender a few times.
Pour into a glass, add a straw and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
This is a fresh and healthy quick drink to make anytime! You can create your own flavors by mixing in any of your favorite frozen fruits! If you have a blender and a few ingredients, you can easily make this refreshing smoothie!
