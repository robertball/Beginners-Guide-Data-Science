Title:
Apple Carrot Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1832393.jpg
Ingredients:
7 Granny Smith Apples
12 Gala Apples
12 Carrots

Directions:
Rinse and scrub apples and carrots. Removed stem from carrots. Remove stem and seeds from apples, cut into slices. Run through juicer. Strain and store in gallon pitcher.
