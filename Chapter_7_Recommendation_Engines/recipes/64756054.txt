Title:
Hibiscus and Pink Pepper Finishing Salt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6931870.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup red hibiscus flowers
1/4 cup unrefined sea salt (such as Fleur de Sel), divided
1 tbsp whole pink peppercorns

Directions:
Put aside two tbsp of the salt.
In a spice grinder, add the remaining salt, hibiscus flowers, and peppercorns. Grind until fine.
Put the ground mixture into a bowl. Add the remaining two tbsp mixing until well combined. Put the finished mixture into an airtight container away from direct sunlight. It will keep for up to six months, depending on the expiration date of your initial ingredients.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
This is a finishing salt with a tangy, peppery flavor.  It’s delicious on avocados, popcorn, steaks, fish, and watermelon. It’s also a fantastic salt for cocktails with rum, vodka, or tequila. Mixed with butter is also another way to enjoy it.
