Title:
Chicken Cheese & Wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8  Chicken breast's
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can mushrooms
3 minced garlic cloves
salt & pepper
1tsp curry
1 cup white wine
2 cups shredded old cheddar
paptika

Directions:
place chicken in a baking dish. In a bowl mix soup, mushrooms, garlic, salt, pepper, & curry. Blend in wine & pour over chicken. Cover with cheese & sprinkle with paprika. Bake at 350.  serve with rice, and enjoy
