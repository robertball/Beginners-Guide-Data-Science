Title:
Fiesta Chicken and Rice Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980152.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's速 Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup or Campbell's速 Condensed 98% Fat Free Cream of Chicken Soup
1 cup Pace Thick & Chunky Salsa Mild,Med,Hot CAM
1/2 cup water
1 cup uncooked instant white rice
2 cups cubed cooked chicken
4 flour tortillas (10-inch)

Directions:
Mix soup, salsa and water in saucepan. Heat to a boil.
Stir in rice and chicken. Cover and remove from heat. Let stand 5 min.
Spoon about 1 cup chicken mixture down center of each tortilla. Fold tortilla around filling.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description:
