Title:
Crispy Chicken with Honey Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979103.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup MIRACLE WHIP Salad Dressing
1/4 cup honey
2 tablespoons GREY POUPON Dijon Mustard
2 tablespoons peanut butter
4 (5 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into strips
1 1/2 cups finely crushed potato chips

Directions:
Mix salad dressing, honey, mustard and peanut butter.  Remove 1/2 cup of the salad dressing mixture; set remaining aside.
Brush chicken with 1/2 cup of the salad dressing mixture; coat with crushed chips.  Place on greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 425 degrees F for 9 minutes.  Turn.  Bake an additional 4 to 5 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and lightly browned.  Serve with remaining salad dressing mixture as dipping sauce.
