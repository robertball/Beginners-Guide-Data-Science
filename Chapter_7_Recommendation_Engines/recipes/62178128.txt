Title:
Cranberry apple mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2C boiling water
1pkg cranberry flavored gelatin
1Can whole cranberry sauce
1C cold water
1/2tsp ground cinnamon
1 medium apple,(chopped)

Directions:
Stir boiling water into gelatin in large bowl 2 min. Stir in cranberry sauce, cold water and cinnamon. Refrigerate about 1 hour and 30min. Stir in apples. Spoon into 5C mold. refrigerate 4 hours or until firm. Unmold, garnish as desired
