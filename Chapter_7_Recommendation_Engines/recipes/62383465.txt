Title:
Vegan Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cooked white rice, still warm
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 teaspoon sesame oil
4 tablespoons soy sauce
3 green onions, diced
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 medium white onion, diced
4 mushrooms, sliced

Directions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large pan at a medium-high heat, and add the rice.
2. Stir in soy sauce and sesame oil.
3. Add vegetables, fry for 5-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
thenottakenname
Description: 
A quick and easy fried rice for vegans! Works great when you have a few things around the house that you need to use up. Only the rice, soy sauce and sesame oil are mandatory, throw in whatever else you want; this is only my favorite way!
