Title:
Basil-Avocado Chicken Salad Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1215642.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Ripe Avocados
1 Lime, juiced
2 Tablespoons chopped fresh basil
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
4 cups chopped cooked chicken
1/4 cup raisins
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
2 heads Bibb or Butter lettuce, leave separated.

Directions:
Pit and peel avocadoes and mash with a fork until chunky.
Add lime juice, basil, garlic salt, and pepper. Mix well.
Add chopped chicken, raisins, and walnuts; mix well to coat.
Spoon chicken mixture onto lettuce leaves; roll up.
