Title:
Scrumptious 5-Layer Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989120.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15.5 ounce) can refried black beans
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1 cup KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
3 green onions, sliced
1/3 cup sliced black olives
1 tomato, chopped

Directions:
Mix beans, chili powder and cumin; spread onto bottom of 9-inch pie plate.
Top with layers of remaining ingredients.
Refrigerate several hours or until chilled. Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
KNUDSEN
Description: 
Spiced black beans are layered with sour cream, Cheddar cheese, green onions, black olives and chopped tomato for this delicious, crowd-pleasing 5-layer dip. Serve with tortilla chips.
