Title:
Mom's  Yummy Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 med. ripe bananas - mashed
1 c. sugar
2 eggs
2 c flour
1 tsp baking soda
1tsp salt
1/2 c cooking oil
1/2 c nuts or raisins
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cream together cooking oil and sugar.  Add eggs and beat well. Sift together dry ingredients and add to creamed mixture alternately with bananas blending well after each addition.  Pour into well greased large sized loaf pan.  Bake in 350 degree oven for 45-60 min. Cool 5 min. Remove from pan.  Cool on rack.  Wrap in foil to keep moist.
Enjoy...this bread gets better every day that it lasts.
