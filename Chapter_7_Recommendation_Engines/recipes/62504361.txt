Title:
Roasted Butternut Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small butternut squash, peeled and chopped in 1/2 inch pieces
4 Tbsp. olive oil
3/4 tsp cinnamon
1/2-3/4 tsp cadamom
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp chili powder
pinch of salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450. Mix oil and spices together in a medium sized bowl. Add the chopped butternut squash and thoroughly coat.
Bake for 15 minutes at 450 degrees.  Continue baking for 5-10 minutes based on your preference for texture.
