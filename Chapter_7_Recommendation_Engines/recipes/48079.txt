Title:
Sweet Potato Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979508.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package TysonÂ® Frozen, Fully Cooked Diced Chicken Breast
1 pound small red potatoes, cooked and cubed
1 (1 pound) sweet potato, cooked, peeled and cubed
1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed, cooked and drained
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup nonfat plain yogurt
2 tablespoons milk
2 teaspoons dried dill weed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
PREP: CLEAN: Wash hands. In large bowl, combine chicken, potatoes and green beans. In small bowl, combine mayonnaise, yogurt, milk, dill weed, salt and pepper.
SERVE: Pour mayonnaise mixture over chicken mixture; stir gently to coat evenly. Serve with warm pita bread, cut in triangles.
CHILL: Refrigerate leftovers immediately.

Submitter: 
Tyson Foods
Description: 
Serve this delicious Sweet Potato Chicken Salad made with diced chicken and sweet potatoes in a warm pita.
