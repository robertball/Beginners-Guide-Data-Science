Title:
Bacon Caesar Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan
6 tablespoons olive oil
6 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1 teaspoon Savory Market™ Crispy Bacon Flavor
1 teaspoon Savory Market™ Oven Roasted Garlic Flavor
2 garlic cloves
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in blender or processor. Blend until smooth. Season to taste with salt and pepper (Can be prepared 2 days ahead. Cover and refrigerate.)

Submitter: 
Savory Market
Description: 
This is a unique twist on a traditional Caesar dressing.
