Title:
Mama's Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. sugar
2/3 t.salt
5 T. cocoa
1/2 c. flour
4 c. milk
6 eggs (separate, save whites for topping)
1/2 stick buttet
1 cap of vanilla

Directions:
Wisk and cook in large sauce pan all the above till thick like merange. pour into pre-baked pie shells.
TOPPING:
beat the 6 egg whites til stiff then add slowly 1/3 c. sugar and 1/2 t. vanilla. Beat til stiff peaks. spread on top of pies (til no filling is showing).
Bake: 350* til peaks brown
