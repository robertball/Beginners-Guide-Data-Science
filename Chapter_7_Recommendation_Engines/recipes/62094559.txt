Title:
Jerk Chicken - not too hot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dry ingredients:
1 tbsp pepper
1 tbsp curry powder
1/2 tbsp cayenne pepper
1 tbsp salt
1/2 tbsp cinnamon
Wet ingredients:
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 tbsp chicken bouillon
2-4 tbsp jerk seasoning (depends on the intensity of sauce)
4 tbsp ketchup
1 tbsp hot sauce
2 tbsp asian 5 spice sauce
2 tbsp plum sauce
2 tbsp real mayonnaise
2 tbsp pancake syrup
6 pieces of chicken/ any of thighs/legs/backs

Directions:
Mix all of the dry ingredients in a ziplock back and put aside.
Cover a shallow baking dish in foil. Add all of the wet ingredients and mix well with a spatula or plastic spoon.
Shake the chicken in the dry ingredients until well coated. Put the seasoned chicken in the foil covered dish and turn it around until all sides are covered in the wet ingredients.
Leave to marinate for 3 hours or more.
Preheat oven to 350degrees.
Cook for 45 minutes - 1 hr (until juices run clear).
Alternatively, take foil out of dish and place chicken and foil on BBQ and cook for same amount of time.
