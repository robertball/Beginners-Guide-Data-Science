Title:
Ann's Cornbread Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/544192.jpg
Ingredients:
1  Package Jiffy Cornbread Mix
1/2 Cup Milk
2  Eggs
1  Can Yellow Corn
1  Can Creamed Corn
1/4 Cup Vegetable Oil

Directions:
Combine the Jiffy cornbread mix, milk and eggs.
Stir in the corn, creamed corn and vegetable oil.
Pour into a greased baking dish.
Cook at 350 for 45 - 50 minutes.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
EMET
Description: 
A great side Casserole which has been a family favorite for many years!  Easy to make and quickly disappears!
