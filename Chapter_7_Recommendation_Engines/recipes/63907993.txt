Title:
Apple Cinnamon Slow Cooker Steel Cut Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123048.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. steel cut oats
4 c. water
1/2 c. silk
1 apple (Honeycrisp - chopped)
1 T. cinnamon
1/2 t. nutmeg
1/2 c. br.sugar
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. vanilla
1 T. butter

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a slow cooker and cook over night on low (7 hours).
