Title:
Best BBQ Beef Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/876408.jpg
Ingredients:
4-5 lb. Brisket
1 Can (10 1/2 oz.) beef consomme
1 Bottle (6 oz.)soy sauce (low sodium is fine)
1/4 Cup lemon juice
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 Tablespoon liquid smoke
1 Bottle (18 oz.) BBQ sauce

Directions:
Prepare marinade by combining all ingredients (except for the BBQ sauce) in a medium bowl.  Place brisket in a pan.  Pour marinade over the brisket.  Cover pan with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.
Remove brisket from the marinade and place in clean baking pan.  Cover pan with foil and cook at 250 degrees 9-10 hours.  During the last 1/2 hour of cooking, baste brisket with bottled BBQ sauce.  Uncover for the last 15 minutes.
