Title:
Crockpot Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts milk
2 T live active yogurt
1/2 cup powdered milk
2 T gelatin

Directions:
Homemade Yogurt
1.	Wash insert of crock pot in hot, soapy water.
2.	Mix 2 quarts of milk, Â½ cup powdered milk, and up to 2 T gelatin.  Turn crock pot to low, and monitor temp.  Add the milk and heat to 180 degrees.  Unplug crockpot and wait for milk to cool to 116 degrees.  At 116 stir in 2 tablespoons of live active yogurt culture yogurt.
3.	Turn the oven on to heat to about 100, then turn off.  Place the crockpot into the oven to incubate.  It will take between 4 and 8 hours for the yogurt to set.  The longer you let it incubate, the tangier your yogurt will be.
4.	It finishes setting up in the refrigerator
