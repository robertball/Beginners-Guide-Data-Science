Title:
Yakh dar Behesht Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup rice flour
1/4 cup starch
2 cup water
2 cup milk
1 cup whipping cream or coconut milk
3/4 cup sugar
5 tablespoons rosewater
2 tablespoons crushed pistachios
1 tablespoon rose pedals

Directions:
Dissolve the rice flour in cold water and place in a saucepan over medium heat. Meanwhile mix the starch with the milk and add to the rice flour mix. Bring to a slow boil.
Add sugar and rosewater and continue cooking over low heat until the consistency thickens and becomes pudding like. Don't forget to stir constantly.
Fill the mixture into little bowls and decorate with almonds, pistachios and rose pedals. Place the dessert into a fridge until cooled down completely.
