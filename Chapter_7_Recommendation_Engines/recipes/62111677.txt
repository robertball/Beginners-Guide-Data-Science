Title:
Sizzling Hot Beer Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C oil
1-2-or 3 dried chili peppers, broken up
1 small onion, diced
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
1/3 C + 1 tbsp lemon juice
1 (12 oz.) can beer
1 jar chili sauce 12 oz.
1 tbsp Worchestershire Sauce
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Heat the oil over medium heat and add the onion. Saute until onion is soft but not brown.  Add hot peppers and minced garlic or onion juice, continue simmering for 2-3 minutes.  Add the remaining ingredients and simmer 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Use as many chili peppers as you like for the heat, or eliminate this ingredient if you aren't into HEAT. Great over chicken, pork ribs, or beef. The addition of beer in the sauce gives it a really tasty flavor.
