Title:
Sweet Potato Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can refried beans
1 cup cooked and mashed sweet potatoes
4 (10 inch) flour tortillas, warmed
4 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Divide bean mixture and mashed sweet potatoes evenly between the warm flour tortillas. Top with cheese. Fold up tortillas burrito style, and place on a baking sheet.
Bake for 12 minutes in the preheated oven, and serve.

Submitter: 
Jessica Weber
Description: 
Refried beans, mashed sweet potatoes, and cheese rolled into a burrito.Modified from: Addictive Sweet Potato Burritos
