Title:
Chicken Fajita Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15.5 oz jar salsa con queso
1 8 oz package cream cheese
1 jar taco sauce
2 cups seasoned and shredded chicken breast
1 package flour tortillas
Red, green, yellow peppers cut into strips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  In saucepan, combine salsa con queso, cream cheese, taco sauce and shredded chicken.  Heat over medium heat until warmed through, about 10 minutes.  Place in small slow cooker or fondue pot to warm. Cut tortillas into wedges, spray on both sides with nonstick cooking spray and place on baking sheet.  Place in oven and bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown and crisp.  Serve dip with tortilla wedges and pepper strips for dipping.
