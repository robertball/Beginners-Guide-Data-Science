Title:
Antioxidant Trail Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2908749.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup almonds
1/4 cup walnuts
1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup goji berries
1/4 cup raisins
1/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Combine almonds, walnuts, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, cranberries, goji berries, raisins, and chocolate chips in a bowl.

Submitter: 
srtickle
Description: 
This trail mix is full of antioxidants and will give you a boost of energy to start your day, or keep you going if you're on the go!
