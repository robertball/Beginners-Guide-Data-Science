Title:
Peg's Summer Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 – 10 small to medium red potatoes
5 or 6 stalks of celery, chopped
1 medium onion, diced
1 red or yellow Bell pepper, diced
2 ears of corn (can substitute frozen)
1 small can of whole black olives
4 T fresh basil, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
Dressing
½  C mayonnaise
2 T yellow or brown mustard
¼ C balsamic vinegar
¼ C white balsamic vinegar
¼ C olive oil

Directions:
Scrub potatos, slice into quarters leaving skin on, and boil until just soft.
Bring the 2 ears of corn to a boil, let cool, and then slice corn off the cob.
Chop celery, onion, pepper and basil
Slice olives in half, or can leave whole.
Combine all with the dressing
Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
cathaire
Description: 
A versatile potato salad that uses fresh sweet corn, bell peppers and fresh basil but can be modified with the addition of other ingredients such as cooked chicken, hard boiled eggs, or tuna to become a one dish meal.
