Title:
Jalepeno Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 fresh jalepeno peppers
1 medium white onion (or 1/4 cup chopped green onion)
2-3 cloves of fresh garlic, skinned
2 medium tomatoes
1 lime
1/4-1/2 cup fresh cilantro/coriander
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Cover and boil the jalepeno peppers until they lose their shine, approximately 10-12 minutes.
Quarter the onion and chop the garlic; place in a blender.
Add cilantro to the blender.
Squeeze the lime juice into the blender.
Add salt and pepper to blender.
Add tomatoes to boiling water for 2-3 minutes.
Strain peppers and tomatoes and discard the water.
Peel skin from tomatoes, dsicarding skin; add to blender.
Add boiled jalepenoes to blender.
Chop briefly in blender (use slow setting).
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Trey
Description: 
I wanted a verde style salsa, but living in Germany, I was not able to find Tomatilla tomatoes.  This green salsa is great on eggs, used as a marinade, or just eaten with tortilla chips.  Its heat is somewhere in the middle between mild and hot.  Hope you enjoy it as much as we do.
