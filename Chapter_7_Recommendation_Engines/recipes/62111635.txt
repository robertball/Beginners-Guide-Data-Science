Title:
Ham Salad for Two Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ a small fully cooked ham steak - julienned or cold ham of choice
½ C chopped celery
½ C chopped Vidalia onion (or other sweet onion)
¼ C chopped sweet red pepper
1 single-serving size tin of diced pineapple fully drained
¼-½ C chopped sweet pickle
2 Tbsp salad dressing NOT mayonnaise
1 Tbsp honey mustard
freshly ground pepper
1 tsp dried dill weed or chopped fresh dill
curry powder (optional to taste)
garlic powder (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in bowl, toss gently until fully blended.
Refrigerate for at least an hour before serving.
Can be served on a bed of lettuce with sliced cucumber and tomatoes, (suggestion only)

Submitter: 
Barbara Zavadil
Description: 
A delicious blend of ham strips and fresh crunchy celery sweetened with sweet pickle.  Suitable enough for a meal in itself or with other salads.
