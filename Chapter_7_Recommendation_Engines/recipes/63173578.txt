Title:
Macaroni Casserole (Mac & Cheese) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/817187.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup elbow macaroni, cooked
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup flour
2 cups milk
2 cups grated old cheddar
1/4 tsp dry mustard
Salt & pepper to taste (half teaspoon each)
TOPPING:
1/4 cup fine bread crumbs or crushed soda crackers
1 tbsp butter
1/4 cup grated old cheddar
Sprinkle of paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F.
On low/medium heat, melt butter and stir in flour until bubbly
Very gradually add milk, stirring constantly until smooth and thickened.
Add cheese, stir till melted then add seasonings.
Stir in macaroni and mix well.
Pour into a 1.5 qt casserole dish.
Prepare topping by mixing together bread crumbs and butter.
Sprinkle on top of macaroni mix, then add cheese and paprika.
Bake for 25-30 minutes or until heated through and crumbs are browned.

Submitter: 
Anisa
Description: 
Delicious homemade mac and cheese my mom used to make when I was a kid. Serve it with a meat/veggie dish or simply have salad on the side. Yum!
