Title:
Vegetarian Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
1/2 med. onion, chopped
2 bay leaves
1 t ground cumin
2 T dried oregano
1 T salt
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 green bell peppers, chopped
2 jalape√±o peppers, chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
2 4oz cans chopped green chili peppers, drained
2 12oz packages of vegetarian burger crumbled
3 28oz cans crushed, peeled tomatoes
1/4 c chili powder
1 T ground black pepper
1 15oz can kidney beans, drained
1 15oz can garbanzo beans, drained
1 15oz can black beans
1 15oz can whole kernel corn

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large pot over medium heat.
Stir in onion, bay leaves, cumin, oregano and salt.
Cook and stir until onion is tender.
Mix in celery and peppers.
When veggies are tender, mix in veggie burger.
Reduce heat to low, cover pot, let simmer 5-minutes.
Mix in tomatoes, season with chili powder and pepper.
Stir in beans and bring to boil.
Reduce heat to low and simmer for 45-minutes.
Add corn, cook for five more minutes before serving.

Deb Jenkins

Delicious, hardy veggie chili.
