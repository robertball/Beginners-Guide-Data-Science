Title:
Collard Greens with Smoked Turkey Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds collard greens
2 cups chicken broth
½ cup white wine
1 cup water
1 large onion sliced
4-6 chopped garlic cloves
3 small green onions, chopped
½ pound smoked turkey bacon, chopped
½ cup olive oil
½ tsp of baking soda(optional) *I use this if collard greens are tough
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large pan then add turkey bacon, onions, garlic and green onions.
Stir occasionally
Rinse and clean collard greens, then roll leaves and slice into thin strips(I use prepacked and already cut collard greens)
Add collard greens to pan
Pour in white wine, chicken broth, and water
Cover pan and let cook for 1 hour on medium heat or until desired tenderness is reached
Stir frequently to distribute the ingredients evenly
Add salt and pepper to taste

Submitter: 
CrondaT
Description: 
A nice variation for serving collard greens. There is a lot of bacon in this recipe so I served this as a  main dish with cornbread and it was very filling.
