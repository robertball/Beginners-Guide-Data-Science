Title:
Big Ray's Unstuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1002152.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
3 medium green peppers, coarsely chopped
1 medium onion, coarsely chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups cooked white rice
1 jar (24 oz.) spaghetti sauce
1 can (14.5 oz.) diced tomatoes
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese(divided)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Brown ground beef, adding onions, peppers and garlic as meat browns until onions and peppers are softened.
Stir in tomatoes, spaghetti sauce, rice and 1 cup of the cheese.
Spoon into a 11" x 13" greased baking dish.  Spread remaining cheese over top and bake for 25 minutes or until heated through and cheese is bubbly.

Submitter:

Description: 
Love stuffed peppers but they're too much work?  This simple and fast casserole takes little time and tastes like the real thing.
