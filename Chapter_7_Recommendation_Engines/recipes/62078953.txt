Title:
Pumpkin Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups pumpkin
2 ½ cups flower
6 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
6 egg whites
3 teaspoons baking powder
½ teaspoon each ground cloves, cinnamon, and ginger
1 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix pumpkin with ½ cup flour in a small bowl.
Set aside.
Mix oil and sugars in large bowl.
Add egg whites two at a time.
In separate medium bowl mix remaining dry ingredients.
Add dry ingredients and milk alternately to eggs and oil, starting and ending with the dry ingredients.
Add pumpkin and flour to cake mix last.
Pour in two greased nine-inch round cake pans and bake for 30-40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sam Gray
Description: 
A moist sweet holiday cake with no need for icing.
