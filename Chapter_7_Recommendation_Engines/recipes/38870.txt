Title:
Bacon Ranch Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982890.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2.8 ounce) can OSCAR MAYER Real Bacon Pieces
2 cups KRAFT Ranch Dressing
1 cup BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1/2 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese
2 green onions, sliced

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in large bowl. Cover. Refrigerate until cool.
Serve with assorted cut-up vegetables or chips.
