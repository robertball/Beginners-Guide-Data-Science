Title:
Hidden Valley Ranch Oyster Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch Salad dressing Mix (powder)
1/2 tsp. dill weed
3/4 c. salad oil
5 c. Plain Oyster Crackers
1/4 tsp. lemon pepper
1/4 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees.  Combine salad dressing mix, dill weed, and oil.  Pour over crackers in a large bowl.  Stir to coat.
Spread crackers out on a cookie sheet into a single layer.  Place in oven for 20 minutes.  Stir gently halfway though baking.
