Title:
Key West Shrimp Boil with Key Lime Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Key West Shrimp Boil
2-3 Lbs Raw Shrimp
2 - Cans Beer
Juice of one lemon
2 Tbsp Old Bay Seasoning
1 Tbsp Chopped Garlic
1/2 Tsp Cayenne Pepper
1/2 Tsp Black Pepper
12 Shakes Tabasco
Key Lime Mustard Sauce
1 Cup Mayo with 1 tsp sugar added
3 Tbsp Dijon or Spicy Brown Mustard
1/2  Cup Lime Juice
Few Shakes Tabasco
Salt and Black Pepper to taste

Directions:
For Shrimp Boil, add all ingredients to a large stock pot.  Bring to boil and add shrimp.  Stir and cook until JUST pink...around 2 minutes.  Strain in collander.  At this point, you can either chill them down or serve warm.
For the Key Lime Mustard Sauce, mix all ingredients in a small bowl.  Chill for at least 2 hours, or longer.
