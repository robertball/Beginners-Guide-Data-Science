Title:
Healthier Shake and Bake Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 cup cornmeal
4 tsp ground cumin
2 tsp onion powder
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp dried oregano, crushed
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
In a heavy, plastic zip lock bag, combine the above. Shake bag to mix ingredients.
Makes two cups or enough to season four 3 ½ pounds of chicken.
To use:
Remove ½ cup  and place in a bag. Cut the chicken into serving pieces and place into bag, a few at a time, and shake to cover.
Arrange in a lightly oiled pan and bake in a preheated oven 375F for 40 minutes. Turning the chicken once during baking.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
No need to buy the pricey, on the shelf coating mix. By making your own you can control the sodium which is very important for many people.
