Title:
Curry Crab Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can or 1/2 cup of cooked crabmeat
1/3 cup grated swiss cheese
1 ~ 1.5 tsp curry powder
3 spring onions chopped fine
2 tsp fresh parsley chopped finely chopped
1 sqeeze of lemonjuice
s&p to taste

Directions:
~Mix all these ingredients together in a small bowl
~Add just enough real mayonaise to hold it together
Spread a small amount on slices of baquette and broil from a middle rack until it just starts to bubble a bit
~Serve Warm!
~Play a bit and add finely chopped red pepper or other bits that appeal to you

Submitter: 
Carrie Elizabeth
Description: 
Melted crab and cheese with a hint of curry make for a delicious appetizer or light dinner!
