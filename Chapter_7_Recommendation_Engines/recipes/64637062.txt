Title:
Simple Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
1 cup chopped leeks
1 cup diced onions
2 cup chopped carrots
2 cup chopped celery
2 cup chopped mushrooms
2 cup chopped zucchini
6 cups chicken stock
3 cups chopped kale
1/2 cup chopped parsley
salt and pepper to taste
(chicken optional)
(1 tablespoon dried hijiki or arame)
(1 tablespoon fresh ginger)

Directions:
1) Heat oil in pot over medium heat.
2) Add leeks and onions; saute for 5 mins.
3) Add vegetables and stock (plus any additions); cover and simmer for 10-15 mins until vegetables are tender.
4) Add the kale and parsley; simmer for 5 mins.
5) Season with salt and pepper.
