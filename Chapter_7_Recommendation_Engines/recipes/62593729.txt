Title:
Jan's Cauliflower Pizzaiola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
1 - 2  cups pizza sauce (depends on if you like a little or a lot)
1 - 8 oz bag shredded, low fat mozzarella
1 tsp. parsley flakes
1- 8 oz bag mini pepperonis (optional)

Directions:
Clean cauliflower and cut into  pieces.
Place in a foil covered roasting pan sprayed with PAM.
Pour sauce (a little or a lot) over cauliflower and top with mozzarella and pepperoni.
Sprinkle parsley flakes on top.
Bake 375 degrees until al-dente and cheese begins to brown and bubble. About 20 - 30 minutes.
Serve hot.
