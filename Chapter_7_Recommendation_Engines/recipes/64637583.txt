Title:
2 Ingredient Strawberry Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4504787.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups of Frozen Strawberries
1/4 cup of Condensed Milk

Directions:
Put the strawberries and condensed milk in a vitamin and blend. Remember to take time out to stir the sorbet. After completely mixed put in a freezer container and freeze for 2 hours. Then Serve.
