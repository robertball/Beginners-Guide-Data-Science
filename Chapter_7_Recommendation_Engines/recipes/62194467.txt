Title:
Baked Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head of cauliflower
1/4-1/2 C water
1/2 C mayonnaise
1 TB Dijon Mustard
1 TB milk
1/4 tsp pepper
1 C bread cumbs
1 TB melted butter
1 C shredded cheese greuyre or cheddar

Directions:
Trim leaves & cut large stem off of cauliflower so it will lay flat.  Place in microwaveable dish with the water & cover with plastic wrap-vented.  Microwave approximately 10 minutes.
Mix together the mayo, mustard, milk, and pepper.  Spread on cauliflower.
Mix together the bread crumbs, melted butter and cheese. Put on top of cauliflower.
Bake 10 minutes in 400 degree oven until brown.
