Title:
Grandpa's World Famous Spinach and Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bunches of fresh spinach, washed and drained
2 8 oz jars quality artichokes
1 1/2 c heavy cream OR half-n-half
8 oz parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In batches using a food processor, chop spinach, garlic and artichoke with juices. (Be careful not to pulverize)
Add the spinach-artichoke mixture to a porcelain baking dish.  Add cream, parmesan cheese, salt and pepper.  Mix well.
Bake at 375 for 45 - 55 minutes stirring occasionally.
