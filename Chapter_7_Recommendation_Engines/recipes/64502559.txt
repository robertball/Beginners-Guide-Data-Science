Title:
Tamarind Sauce Fish Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Preparation:
1 tablespoon red chilli powder
1/2  tablespoon salt
1 tablespoon oil
Cooking:
1 large Onion finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds
1.5 tablespoon Garlic paste
2 Pounds White Carp (or Tilapia) fish cut into large chunks
3 tablespoon Salt
2 tablespoon red chilli powder
1 tablespoon ground turmeric
1 big lemon sized Tamarind pulp
2 tablespoon Coriander Powder
1/4 cup oil
Chopped Coriander leaves
2 cups water

Directions:
Preparation:
Clean fish pieces and marinate them in salt, chilli powder,turmeric powder & oil.
Soak tamarind in 1 cup of warm water and extract juice by squeezing tamarind.
Cooking:
Heat oil and add cumin seeds.
Add finely chopped onions to the oil and fry them till they turn transparent on medium heat.
Add garlic paste and cook for 3 minutes.
Add fish pieces , cover the pan and cook for 5 minutes.
Add the tamarind juice and bring it to boil.
Turn the fish pieces over and cook.(Do not stir too much)
Add salt,red chilli powder, coriander powder.
Cook on low heat for 10 minutes till the sauce thickens and oil separates.
Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.

Submitter: 
Sushama
Description: 
Indian style tangy fish curry. This is famous Chepala Pulusu from Andhra.
