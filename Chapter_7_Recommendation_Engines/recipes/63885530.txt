Title:
Slow Cooker Cocktail Smokies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077988.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packages mini smoked sausages
1 jar grape jelly
1 bottle bbq sauce

Directions:
Combine grape jelly & bbq sauce in a slow cooker. Stir in the little smokies.
Cook on high, until heated through, 2-3 hours.
