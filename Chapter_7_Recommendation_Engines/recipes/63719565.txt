Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Fruit Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 huge spoon of peanut butter
1 normal spoonful of cocoa
agave
vanilla extract
almonds
About half cup almond milk

Directions:
Mix and heat everything except almond milk. Whisk in almond milk depending on how runny of a sauce you want, eat on fruit(nectarines & bananas)! Hot or cold
