Title:
Lo Mein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lo Mein Noodles rinsed and set aside
3-4 stalks of celery
1/2 head cabbage
3 garlic cloves
2 chicken breasts
2 T Light Soy Sauce
2 T Dark Soy Sauce
1 1/2 tsp salt
3-4 T sugar

Directions:
Slice celery on an angle, slice onion thinly on an angle. Slice carrot thinly and diagonally. Slice cabbage thinly. Crush garlic, dice chicken breasts. Cut noodles. Mix all this together. Mix soy sauces, salt and sugar together. Cook diced chicken and add the sauce mixture, add in vegetables and noodles, cook all ingredients together for 3-5 minutes. Serve while hot.
