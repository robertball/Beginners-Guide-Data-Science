Title:
Elephant Ear Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of puffed pastry sheets (thawed)
5 oz. sugar

Directions:
Sprinkle sugar on the pastry sheets
Stack the pastry sheets one on another and roll them into a rectangle.
Fold the short side in half.
Open it up again and fold the sheet to the half, but leave a centimeter between the ends.
Where you left the 1cm space fold it in half again.
Cut the sheets into one centimeter wide slices
place the slices cut side up onto a baking sheet at least 2 inches apart.
put them into the oven heated to 400 degrees farenheight.
when they get slightly brown flip them over (approx. 6 minutes)
when both sides are brown take the cookies out of the oven and let them cool.

Submitter: 
Melanie
Description: 
Cookies originated in Germany, however in Germany they are called pig ears.
