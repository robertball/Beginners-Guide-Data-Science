Title:
White Pizza Grilled Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbs. Butter
1/4 Small Sweet Onion thinly sliced
2 Tbs. Softened Butter
1 tsp. Crushed Rosemary
1/8 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/8 tsp. Onion Powder
Salt
Pepper
2 Slices Thick Italian Bread
1 Slice Provolone Cheese
1/4 c. Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1 Slice Cooper Sharp Cheese

Directions:
Heat non-stick skillet over medium heat, melt 1 Tbs. of butter, add onion and saute until softened and slightly brown.
While sauteeing onions, in a small bowl, mix together Softened Butter, Rosemary, Garlic and Onion Powder, and Salt and Pepper to taste.
After Onions have browned slightly, remove from pan(keeping skillet over medium heat)
Spread Butter mixture on one side of each slice of Italian Bread.
Place one sice of bread, butter side down in skillet.
Top bread with the Cheeses and sauteed onion.
Place the other slice of bread butter side up on top of cheese and onions.
Flip sandwich with spatula when bottom is golden brown(being careful not to burn)
Remove from pan when other side is brown and cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Anissa
Description: 
If you love Double Crust White Pizza, this sandwich will satisfy your craving without making a whole pizza or ordering out.
