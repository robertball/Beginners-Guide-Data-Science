Title:
Sausage Potatoe Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Sweet Italian Sausage( sliced into 1/2 inch pcs.)
2 lbs of Red Skinned Potatoes( sliced )
1 Pint Heavy Whipping Cream
1 Pint Light Cream
4 Cloves Fresh Garlic
8 oz Shredded Parmesan Cheese
8 oz Shredded Romano Cheese
1 Tspn Salt
1 Tspn Pepper
1/2 Cup butter
1/2 Cup Feta Cheese
1/4 Cup Italian Bread Crumbs

Directions:
Pan fry the Italian sausage and slice it up into 1/2 inch pieces and set aside.
Slice the potatoes and put them aside as well.
In a medium sauce pan take 1/4 cup butter and put over low heat. Mince garlic cloves and put in with the butter. Cook over low heat for about 10 minutes.
Add the heavy and light creams and bring to a boil. Once boiling turn down to medium heat and add Parmesan and Ramona Cheeses. Stir until cheese is all melted and the sauce is a good consistency then put back on low heat and stir in salt and pepper.
Put the potatoes and the sausage in a large mixing bowl and mix in the sauce.
Dump mixture into a 9 X 11 Baking dish.
Then mix 1/4 cup melted butter, bread crumbs and feta cheese in a bowl and spread on top.
Bake 30 375 for 90 minutes.
Let cool for 10 minutes before serving
