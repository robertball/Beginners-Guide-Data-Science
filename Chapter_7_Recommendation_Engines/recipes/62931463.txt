Title:
Fiesta Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/717606.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1 large spanish onion
3 cloves minced fresh garlic
1 4 oz can diced green chiles
1 14 oz can chili beans
1 14 oz can seasoned black beans
1 14 oz can sweet corn
2 14 oz cans fire roasted diced tomatoes
1 packet taco seasoning
1 packet ranch seasoning
Frank's hot sauce
1 tsp salt
1 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large soup pot add ground beef and chopped onion. When ground beef is browned and the onion is translucent, add minced garlic, green chiles, chili beans, black beans, sweet corn, tomatoes, ranch and taco seasoning packets. (Note...do not drain liquids from canned vegetables). Stir thoroughly. Cover and simmer for 1 hr, stirring occasionally. Add hot sauce, salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter:

Description: 
South of the border ingredients give this zesty soup lots of flavor. Hearty and wholesome comfort food with a Tex-Mex twist. Goes great with a dollop of sour cream and tortilla chips.
