Title:
Chicken with Creamy Shallot Tarragon Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
2 tbsp. olive oil
2 tbsp. shallots - minced
1 cup dry white wine
1 tsp. dijon mustard
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/2 tsp. tarragon
3 tbsp. fresh parsley - chopped

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper then saute in olive oil until golden brown.
Add shallots and wine and cook 4 minutes.
Remove chicken then add tarragon, cream and mustard.
Cook sauce to reduce until it thickens.
Add parsley and pour sauce over chicken.
