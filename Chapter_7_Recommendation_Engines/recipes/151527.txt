Title:
Campbell's® Creamy Dijon Chicken with Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977389.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Celery Soup or Campbell's® Condensed 98% Fat Free Cream of Celery Soup
2/3 cup water
1 tablespoon Dijon-style mustard
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 cups hot cooked regular long-grain white rice

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned.
Add soup, water, mustard and pepper. Heat to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or until done. Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Browned chicken simmers in a creamy mustard sauce, then served with hot rice.
