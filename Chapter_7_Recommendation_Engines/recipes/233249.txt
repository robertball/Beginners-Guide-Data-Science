Title:
Hot N Spicy Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) can crabmeat
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) package sharp Cheddar cheese spread
3 tablespoons lemon juice
3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
3 tablespoons beer
2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
2 tablespoons grated onion
1 tablespoon chile-garlic sauce (such as SrirachaÂ®)
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix crabmeat, cream cheese, Cheddar cheese spread, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, beer, chives, onion, chile-garlic sauce, cayenne pepper, and garlic together in a bowl; spread evenly into a 9x9-inch baking dish. Sprinkle crab-cheese mixture with paprika. Cover baking dish with aluminum foil.
Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes; remove aluminum foil and bake until bubbling, about 5 more minutes.

Submitter: 
KailynJoy
Description: 
This is super easy and super delicious! I'm always asked to make it for parties. And I never have any leftover! It's great on crackers, toasted bread, or veggies! Even good as filling for stuffed mushrooms!
