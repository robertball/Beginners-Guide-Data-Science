Title:
Sausage and Egg Tostadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/623468.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9.6 ounce) package Jimmy DeanÂ® Original Hearty Pork Sausage Crumbles
1/2 cup chopped onion
6 tostada shells, warmed in oven
6 cooked eggs, such as poached, fried or over-easy
1 1/2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese
3/4 cup salsa
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Cook sausage and onion in large skillet over medium-high heat 8-10 minutes or until sausage is thoroughly cooked, stirring frequently; drain.
Place tostada shells on serving plates.  Top with sausage mixture, eggs, cheese, salsa and cilantro.
