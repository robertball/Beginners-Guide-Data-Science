Title:
My Smother-fried Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 baker potatoes cut into thick fries or chips
1 small onion cut into rings
1 lb. package Eskridge Smoked or Keilbasa sausage sliced
salt & pepper
butter
oil

Directions:
Heat about 1/2 cup oil and 1/2 stick butter in skillet.  Brown potatoes just a little about 7 minutes, then turn the heat down, put onion rings on top and sausage if you desire, then cover with a lid and continue to "smother" for approximately 15 to 20 minutes.  Salt and pepper to taste

Submitter: 
ttkc
Description: 
If you were to add Eskridge Smoked sausage to this recipe you would die and go to heaven! My adult kids are picky eaters, but they all go for this easy dinner.  Just throw in a salad and bread.
