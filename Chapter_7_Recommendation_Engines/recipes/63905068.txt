Title:
Porcupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
¼ cup chopped Onion
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
½ cup Long Grain Rice
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 soup can of Water
Cooking Oil

Directions:
Mix meat, rice, onion, salt and pepper together.  Form into small balls.
Heat cooking oil. Brown meatballs fast in hot cooking oil.  Remove from heat and drain skillet.
Mix soup and water together.  Place in skillet or saucepan.  Drop meatballs into soup mixture and simmer about 30 minutes.
