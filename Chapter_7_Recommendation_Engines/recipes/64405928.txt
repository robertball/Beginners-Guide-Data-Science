Title:
Grandma's Chocolate Cake by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 c flour
2 eggs, beaten
2 c sugar
1tsp vanilla
3 tbsp lard
3/4 c sour cream
6 tbsp cocoa
1 1/2 c boiling water
2 tsp baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Beat  eggs, add 2 c sugar. Add 1 tsp vanilla, 3 tbsp lard, 3/4 c sour cream, 6 tbsp cocoa, beat well.
Dissolve 2 tsp soda in 1 1/2 c boiling water (it should fizz up so make sure you have it in a large enough container).
Alternate adding soda water mix and flour to batter mix.
Pour into greased and floured 9x13 cake pan.
Bake 40-45 minutes at 350.

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
Dense, moist and delicious chocolate cake. Grandma usually did not put frosting on this cake. We topped it with butter or whipped topping.
