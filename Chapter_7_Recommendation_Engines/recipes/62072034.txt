Title:
Aunt Kaye's Date Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped dates
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup hot water
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon margarine
1 egg
1 ½ cups flour
¼ teaspoon salt
½ cup nutmeats or walnuts
Topping:
1 cup chopped dates
2/3 cup water
½ cup sugar
½ cup nutmeats or walnuts

Directions:
Pour hot water over dates and baking soda. Let cool. Combine sugar, margarine and egg.
In another bowl combine flour, salt and nutmeats.
Combine the two mixtures.
Bake for 45 minutes.
Topping:
Boil until think and spread over date pudding.
