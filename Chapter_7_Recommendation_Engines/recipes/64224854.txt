Title:
Chili Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Wolfe brand chili witout beans
8 oz jalapeno  cheese whiz
8 oz. cheddar cheese (My original recipe says "regular' cheese and I don't remember what I used.)
1 can french fried onions

Directions:
Put everything except onions in crock pot and let cook long enough to get hot and let flavors blend.

Submitter:
