Title:
Grilled portobello sandwich with roasted red pepper and mozzarella. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 portobello mushroom caps, cleaned
1 red pepper, roasted on grill
1 onion, sliced and caramelized
16 basil leaves
4 slices buffalo mozzarella
4 tomato slices
4 rolls, whatever kind you like
4 tsp mayonnaise, can add roasted garlic if you want
0.5 cups olive oil
salt
pepper

Directions:
1) Heat your grill to med-high heat.
2) Add salt and pepper, as per your taste, to enough olive oil to brush over all sides of the mushroom caps about twice.
3) Brush the smooth side of cleaned mushroom caps with seasoned olive oil.  Place on the grill oiled side down.  Grill over direct heat ~3minutes.  Brush gill side with oil, flip, grill 3 more min.  Brush smooth side again, flip, grill 3 more min.  You can add cheese at the end if you wish it melted. You can grill the red pepper in the same way as the mushrooms, although they will need longer to cook. After roasting slice peppers thinly.
4)Toast roll/buns/focaccia.  Spread with a bit of regular mayo or garlic mayo if you made that.  Construct sandwich: grilled mushroom, add mozz if not already melted to mushroom, add tomato slices, basil leaves, caramelized onions, and roasted red pepper slices.  enjoy.
