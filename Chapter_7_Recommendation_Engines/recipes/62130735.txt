Title:
Vegan Morning Blueberry Cheesecake Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-3/4 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1 egg
3/4 cup vanilla or plain soy milk
1/3 cup veg. oil
1 tsp. grated lemon peel
4 oz. Tofu cream cheese (like Tofutti brand)
3/4 cups fresh blueberries
------
1 tablespoon lemon juice
3 tablespoons sugar

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 F.
Mix the flour, 1/3 cup sugar and baking powder in large bowl.
Beat the egg and then add the soy milk, oil, and lemon peel.
Add to flour mixture and hand mix until just moistened. Add the blueberries mix slightly and then add the tofu cream cheese in separated pieces, 1/4 tsp at a time so that there are small chunks all around the batter. Gently blend and spoon into muffin pan filling each cup 2/3 full.
Bake for 18-20 minutes or until lightly browned.
Mix the lemon juice and 3 tblsp. of sugar and brush on warm muffins.

Submitter: 
joannemerc
Description: 
Being lactose intolerant, I have adjusted my Moms old recipe to make it non-dairy. Here is the perfect alternative using Tofutti cream cheese. You would never know its not real cream cheese. Serve this for breakfast or brunch on a sunny spring day.
