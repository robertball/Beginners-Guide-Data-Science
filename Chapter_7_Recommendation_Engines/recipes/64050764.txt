Title:
Gluten Free Mini Cornbread Cocottes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1471216.jpg
Ingredients:
145 grams cornmeal
31 grams rice flour
35 grams tapioca flour
20 grams buckwheat flour
13 grams baking powder
4 grams baking soda
3 grams salt
3 tablespoons butter or lard, softened
2 tablespoons honey
1 tsp vinegar
2 eggs - beaten
¾ cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 175°C.
Grease and flour muffin tin or ceramic pots.
Sift the cornmeal, all the flours, baking powder, baking soda and salt into a large mixing bowl.
Blend in the butter until well combined.
Add the eggs and milk.
Blend till the milk is absorbed and the batter is just combined.
Pour into tins or moulds.
Bake 20-25 minutes till the tops are firm and lightly browned.
Let them cool for a few minutes before letting them cool on a rack.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Homemade sweet style gluten free cornbread.  Fluffy and tasty, just without the gluten.  Enjoy.
