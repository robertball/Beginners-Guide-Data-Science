Title:
Rice Krispy Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
One 10-1/2 oz package marshmallows (6 cups or 40 large)
6 cups Rice Krispies

Directions:
Microwave margarine in a large bowl until melted.  Add marshmallows and mix.  Microwave 1-1/2 minutes on high, stirring twice.  Immediately add cereal and mix well.  Press into greased 9x13 inch pan.  Cool and cut into squares.
