Title:
Ice Cream Sundae Taco Delights Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5912634.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Hard Taco Shells
1-2 scoops Vanilla Ice Cream
Melted Chocolate (optional)
Chocolate Sauce
Caramel Sauce
Sprinkles
Mini M&M;
1 Marciano Cherry, halved
Whip Topping

Directions:
Dip ends of taco shell into melted chocolate (or chocolate sauce), then dip into sprinkles.
Scoop ice cream into taco shell. Top with favorite toppings, such as, chocolate & caramel sauce, sprinkles, M&Ms, Hershey's (New) Chocolate whip, whip, cherries, etc. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
tcasa
Description: 
We always have left over taco shells after taco Tuesdays, so, this is how we use them up. Add what ever ice cream toppings you prefer inside to create your own delightful ice cream taco.
