Title:
Easy Chicken Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/354777.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c. peanut oil
1/4 c. flour
1/2 c. chopped sweet onion
1/2 c. chopped green pepper
1/2 c. chopped celery
3/4 - 1 1/2 t. Cajun seasoning (make sure it's not the salty variety)
1 t. minced garlic
1/2 14 oz.) can chicken broth
1/2 lb. andouille sausage, cut in 1/4" slices
2 c. chopped cooked chicken
Okra Pilau

Directions:
Heat oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat; gradually whisk in flour, and cook, whisking constantly, 5 minutes or until chocolate colored. (Do NOT burn)
Reduce heat to medium.  Stir in onion, green pepper, celery, cajun seasoning, and garlic and cook stirring constantly, 3 minutes.  Gradually stir in chicken broth and sausage.  Increase heat to medium-high and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low, and simmer, sturring occasionally, 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, prepare Okra Pilau.  Stir chicken into gumbo; cook stirring occasionally, 5 minutes.  Serve with Okra Pilau.
