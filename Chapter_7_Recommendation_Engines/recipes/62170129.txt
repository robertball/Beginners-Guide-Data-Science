Title:
Kitchen Sink Vegetable Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 pounds cubed stew beef
2 quarts beef broth
1 28 ounce can diced tomatoes
1/2 cup diced onion
1/2 cup diced bell pepper
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 tablespoon creole or cajun seasoning
1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
1/2 cup frozen lima beans
1 pound cubed potatoes
1/2 pound sliced carrots
1/2 pound elbow pasta

Directions:
Sprinkle raw beef with creole seasoning.
In a 10 quart or larger saucepan, brown cubed beef in olive oil.
Add onions, green pepper, and garlic. Brown for 3 minutes.
Add Beef broth, and canned tomatoes.  Simmer for 30 minutes.
Add potatoes and carrots. Simmer 20 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients, and simmer until pasta, potatoes and carrots are tender.
Enjoy with buttermilk biscuits or your favorite bread.
