Title:
Iceberg Stawberry Salad w/Blue Cheese Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 thick cut bacon, cooked until crispy, crumbled
1 head of iceberg lettuce, trimmed, cored, cut into 1" slices
6 oz strawberries, hulled, sliced
1/4 med red onion, thinly sliced
Vinaigrette:
1/2 c crumbled blue cheese
1/2 t very finely minced garlic
Pinch of sugar
3 T cider vinegar
3 T olive oil
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Place lettuce slices on plates, top with crumbled bacon, sliced strawberries and onions.
Prepare vinaigrette in a medium bowl. Use fork to mash blue cheese, garlic and sugar into cider vinegar until cheese has "melted" into vinegar. (Do not need to completely mash; bits 'n pieces are good.)
Add the olive oil and whisk.  Add salt & pepper.
Drizzle vinaigrette over salad. Top with crumbled blue cheese, if desired
