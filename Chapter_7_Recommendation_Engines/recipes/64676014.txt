Title:
Caribbean Coconut Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound large shrimp
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup white wine
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 cup coconut
Vegetable oil
Sauce:
2 tablespoons marmelade
2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
2 tablespoons orange juice

Directions:
Mix together flour, wine,salt/pepper
Add shrimp to the batter
Remove shrimp from batter; dredge in coconut
Fry until lightly browned; drain on paper towel
Make sauce and serve with shrimp
