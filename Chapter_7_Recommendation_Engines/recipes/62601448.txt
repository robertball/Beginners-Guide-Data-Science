Title:
Salmon Grilling Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp honey
2 Tbsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp rice vinegar
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 clove of garlic, crushed
1 tsp ginger root, grated (or powdered ginger)
dash of salt
dash of pepper

Directions:
Combine in saucepan and simmer on medium for 6 minutes or until reduced by half. (slightly thickened)
Spoon over 1 lb salmon on foil.  Grill 4 to 6 minutes per side, basting as needed.
