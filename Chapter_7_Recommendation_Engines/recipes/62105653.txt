Title:
Marietta's Pork Tenderloin with Balsamic- Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Table spoon butter
1 8-10 oz. pork tenderloin
1/2 chopped onion
1 Tablespoon fresh rosemary
1/2 cup low salt chicken broth, or white wine
1/3 cup canned whole berry cranberry sauce
1 Tablespoon balsamic vinegar
Salt & pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450. Melt 1/2 tablespoon of butter in heavy ovenproof skillet. Sprinkle pork with salt & pepper. Sear pork in skillet on all sides, about 2 min. Place skillet with pork in oven. Bake until pork is at 155 degrees, about 10 min.
Meanwhile, melt remaining butter in another pan. Add rosemary and onion; saute until onion softens. Add broth (or Wine), cranberry sauce and vinegar, wisk until cranberry melts.
Add juices from pork skillet into sauce and reduce for about 6 min. Salt & pepper sauce to taste. Slice pork & spoon sauce over.
