Title:
stuffed yellow squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 summer squash cut in half longways.
2 small squash to dice
1 onion
2 or 3 bell pepper
2 cucumbers
3 or 4 tomatoes (cherry or grape)
1 box uncle ben's wild rice
1 cup cheese (i used gouda)

Directions:
cut squash in half, leave a few for the stuffing.
sautee onions, bell pepper, tomatoes, squash and cucumbers.
cook rice
bake squash for about 20 mins @ 350 or until tender.
put spoon full of rice into the squash, add sauteed vegetables on top.
bake for about 30 more mins
sprinkle cheese all over and leave in oven for 5 mins.
delicious veggie dinner is served. cook with porkchop or what ever meat, and you have plenty of veggies, meat, and a side dish all in one!

Submitter: 
crys
Description: 
I grew everything here in my garden, it was from the first harvest. So it was a lot cheaper on the budget, because we all know fresh fruit and veggies can get expensive. All in all great time cooking our own food. even my 4 year old had fun helping.
