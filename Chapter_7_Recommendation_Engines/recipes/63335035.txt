Title:
Not Your Average Potato Salad by chairlifter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes
1 medium sweet onion
1/4 cup sunflower oil
1/4 cup vinegar
1/3 cup water
Salt and Pepper to taste
Fresh shredded parsley
Optional:  bell peppers, jalopenoes, cucumbers, bacon bits

Directions:
Boil potato's until tender, peel and then chop into chunks
Mix remainder ingredients, except for the parsley.
Marinate  several hours or overnight(refridgerated) Stirring periodically.
Add shredded parsley before serving.
