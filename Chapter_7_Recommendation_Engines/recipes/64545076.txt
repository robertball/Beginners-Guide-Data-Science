Title:
Indonesian Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T. soy sauce
1 1/2 T. lime juice
1 1/2 T. brown sugar, packed
1  T. Sriracha
2 garlic cloves, minced
Salt and fresh-ground white pepper
4 T. peanut oil (or other oil that can take heat), divided
8 oz. extra-firm tofu, patted dry and cut into 3/4-inch cubes
3 large eggs
2 medium carrots, diced
1 red bell pepper, diced
6 scallions (1 large bunch), cut crosswise into 1/2 inch lengths
3 c. cooked brown rice
2 c. thin-sliced green cabbage
1/4 c. chopped peanuts

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, lime juice, sriracha, garlic, and 2 T. water. Set aside.
Whisk eggs with 2 T. water and salt and pepper. Set aside.
Heat a T. of oil in a large skillet (cast iron or non-stick) over high heat until shimmering. Add tofu, season generously with salt and pepper, and cook until browned on all sides. Transfer to large bowl.
Add another T. of oil to pan, then add eggs.  Cook till just set, stirring occasionally,  and transfer to the bowl.
Add another T. of oil to pan and heat till smoking. Add carrots, pepper, and scallions and cook until crisp-tender and well browned. Transfer to bowl.
Add remaining T. of oil and rice to skillet. Cook, stirring, till rice is browned in spots. Stir sauce then add to rice (mixture will steam).Cook till sauce is reduced to sticky glaze.
Add rice to bowl, then stir in cabbage and peanuts. Serve!
