Title:
Georgia Peach Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1269330.jpg
Ingredients:
2 small peaches, pitted and diced
1/2 cup coconut milk
1/2 cup Greek yogurt
1/4 cup 1% milk

Directions:
Blend peaches, coconut milk, yogurt, and milk together in a blender until smooth.
