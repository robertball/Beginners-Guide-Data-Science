Title:
Creamy Pumpkin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/889916.jpg
Ingredients:
CRUST:
2 cups flour
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 cup unsalted butter, cold & cut into pieces
1 cup chopped walnuts
_______________________
FILLING:
16 oz cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
4 eggs
1 cup pumpkin puree
2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Blend the crust ingredients in a food processor until ball forms - Press into 9 x 13 pan.
Bake for 15-20 minutes - Remove and cool slightly.
Meanwhile, mix all filling ingredients with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.
Pour over crust.
Bake for 35-40 minutes until set.
Chill in refrigerator until cool, then cut into squares.
Store leftovers in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
These cheesecake style bars are moist and delicious. They make a perfect after dinner treat for Fall and are great on holiday dessert trays. This recipe can easily be cut in half and baked in an 8x8 pan for smaller crowds.
