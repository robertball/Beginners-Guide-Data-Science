Title:
Whole wheat and mint salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1)1 cup whole wheat grain pearls
2)2 cups mint leaves
3)Juice of 1 lemon
4)Finely grated rind of 1 lemon
5)1 green chilli
6)small piece of ginger (optional)
7)Salt and pepper to taste
8)Cherry tomatoes and mint leaves as garnish
9)1 cup roasted walnuts

Directions:
1) Boil wheat pearls in salted water till they are Alla dente. Drain and keep aside.
2)In a blender, blend mint leaves, lemon juice, green chilli and ginger to a fine paste.
3)Toss wheat pearls and mint paste together. Add walnuts and lemon rind.
4)Add salt and pepper to taste
5)Garnish with cherry tomatoes and mint leaves.
