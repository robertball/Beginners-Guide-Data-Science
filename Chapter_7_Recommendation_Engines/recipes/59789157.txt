Title:
Budget Cream Cheese Frosting II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/271133.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream together the cream cheese and butter until creamy.  Then gradually stir in the confectioners' sugar.Beat until light and fluffy.  Store in the refrigerator after use.

Submitter: 
CookingOnABudget
Description: 
This recipe will frost the top and sides of a single layer 9 inch round cake or a 9x13 pan of carrot cake.  Use store brand cream cheese, butter, and powdered sugar. Try this recipe with the Budget Carrot Cake III recipe.Modified from: Cream Cheese Frosting II
