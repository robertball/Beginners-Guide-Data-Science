Title:
Apple Danish Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup (1 stick) soft butter
1 box yellow cake mix
1/2 cup coconut
1 can apple pie filling
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 cup (8 ounce container) sour cream
1 egg

Directions:
Mix butter and cake mix. Will be crumbly.
Add coconut.
Pat into 9x13 cake pan.
Bake 10 minutes at 350.
Arrange apple pie filling on warm crust.
Mix sugar and cinnamon, sprinkle over apples.
Blend sour cream and egg.
Drizzle over cake. Topping will not cover.
Bake another 25 minutes.
Serve warm or cold.
Store in fridge.
