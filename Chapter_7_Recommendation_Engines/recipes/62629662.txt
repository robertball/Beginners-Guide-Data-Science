Title:
Pork Chops for Two Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbsp	butter or oil
2	bone-in pork chops, 1/2” thick
1	small onion, sliced
8	baby gold potatoes, halved
.
1 c	heavy cream or nonfat condensed milk
1 c	broth
1/4 tsp	salt
1/8 tsp	red pepper
12 drops	red Tabasco
1/4 tsp	garlic powder
.
8 oz	canned mushrooms, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350º.
Melt the butter in a large, ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat.
Add the pork, onion and potatoes and cook until well browned on both sides.
In a medium bowl, stir the cream, broth, salt, pepper, Tabasco and garlic powder together.
Pour the cream mixture into the skillet and bring to a boil.
Cover the skillet and place in the oven and bake for 15—25 minutes or until the pork is cooked through.
Remove skillet from oven and add the mushrooms.
