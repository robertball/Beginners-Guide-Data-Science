Title:
Elsy's Lebanese Burghol with Tomatoe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2 cups coarse burghol (cracked wheat) rinsed and left to dry a bit in colander.
- 5 large ripe tomatoes
-1 large onion finely chopped
-3 cloves garlic finely chopped
- 1/2 green pepper finely chopped
-2 tablespoons Tomatoe paste
-1 1/2 teaspoon salt
-1/2 teaspoon cayenne  pepper
- 4 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Fry the onions in 3 tablespoons of olive oil  on medium heat until translucent
Add the garlic and 1/2 teaspoon salt and continue to fry until onions slightly golden.
Meanwhile finely dice 2 tomatoes and whizz the rest in a blender.
Add the chopped bell pepper and fry for 1 minute and then add the diced tomatoes and fry for around 3 minutes.
Now add the burghol and mix well with the rest of the ingredients and keep on frying for another 3 minutes.
Add the remaining salt, cayenne pepper, olive oil and the blended tomatoes.
Add the tomatoe paste and enough hot water to cover the burghul and lower the heat.
Cook for around 15 minutes till  burghol is tender, add more water if needed.
Serve with a side of Greek yoghurt.
