Title:
Bamboo Steamed Stuffed Grape Leaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/463519.jpg
Ingredients:
1 10 oz package yellow rice, cooked
1 lb ground lamb
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp rosemary
1 egg
1 8 oz jar grape leaves, unrolled and rinsed
1 qt chicken broth, optional

Directions:
Prepare yellow rice according to package directions. Let cool 30 minutes. Mix in ground lamb, garlic, rosemary and egg. Lay grape leaf on a flat surface and add 3 tablespoons of rice mixture to the center. Fold each leaf side inwards and then roll. Repeat. Place the rolled grape leaves in a single layer into a bamboo steamer. Add 1 qt chicken broth or water in an electric wok and turn setting to steam. Place bamboo steamer with lid on inside the wok. Steam for 30 minutes. Serve grape leaves hot or cold.

Submitter: 
The Green Goddess
Description: 
I have found that sometimes when I bake stuffed grape leaves they tend to dry out so my husband suggested that I steam them instead. Steaming them was the perfect solution and now my stuffed grape leaves are always moist.
