Title:
Nanny's Chicken & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
about a 3 pound chicken cut up- or the pieces you enjoy
1 1/2 cup uncooked rice
1 can cream of chicken soup
water

Directions:
In deep casserole dish- place chicken pieces in dish- spread uncooked rice over chicken
in seperate bowl- dilute one can cream of chicken soup with one can water- spread over rice
add 1 1/2 cups water evenly over mixture
bake at 325 til well done- about 1 1/2 hours-

old fashioned chicken & rice dish- real comfort food!
