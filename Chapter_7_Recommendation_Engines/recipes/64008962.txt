Title:
Sweet Pepper Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6856713.jpg
Ingredients:
16 hella Pinoy peppers or sweet peppers, small,cut in half
1 pound hot spicy sausage
8 oz. cream cheese
1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brown sausage and mix with cheeses.
Melt together and fill pepper halves
Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes. Mixture should bubble.
Do not bake first if going to freeze! Bake straight from freezer @ 325 degrees for 25 minutes (check often).
