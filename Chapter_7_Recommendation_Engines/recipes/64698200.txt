Title:
Injection for Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. Apple Juice
2 c. Pineapple Juice
2 TBLE. Worcestershire Sauce
2 TBLE. Soy Sauce
1 TBLE. Salt
1 TBLE. Brown Sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well at room temperature so the salt and brown sugar can integrate into the liquids. Inject into the pork of your choice, and then place the pork in the fridge for 12 hours or over night. Prepare and cook, smoke or grill your pork to your liking.

Submitter: 
Julie Gilbert
Description: 
This is an all purpose injection to add to any pork loin, butt or shoulder meat.  Remember to inject under the bone if you are using a pork butt.  You can adjust the measurement  amounts to your liking.  Keep in mind that pork tends to be a saltier type of meat by itself.
