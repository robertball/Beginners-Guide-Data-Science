Title:
Classic Vinaigrette or Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup  red wine vinegar (or white wine vinegar)
1 tsp  salt
1-1/4 tsp  sugar
1-1/4 tsp  fresh ground pepper
2 tbsp Dijon mustard
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 small shallot, minced
1 tsp dried onion
2 tbsp dried tarragon (or any other dried herb)
1 cup  olive oil

Directions:
In small bowl, whisk vinegar with salt, pepper and sugar to dissolve. Add all other ingredients except olive oil and whisk to blend.
Add olive oil in a fine stream whisking constantly to form an emulsion.
Pour into squeeze bottle or container. Store in refrigerator for 1-2 weeks.
