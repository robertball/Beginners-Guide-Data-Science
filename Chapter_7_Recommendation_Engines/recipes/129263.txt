Title:
Avocado, Chicken and Mandarin Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
8 iceberg lettuce leaves
24 fresh cilantro leaves
1 1/2 cups cooked chicken
1 (15 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
1 green onion, chopped
1 ripe Hass Avocado, peeled, pitted, chopped and tossed with lemon juice
Prepared Asian dipping sauce

Directions:
Trim lettuce leaves at stem end making about 6 to 8 inch long wrappers. Wash, drain and chill.
In bowl, combine cilantro, chicken, mandarin oranges, green onion and avocado to make filling.
To serve: Place a spoonful of filling in lettuce wrap. Drizzle with dipping sauce.
Roll and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Fresh Chilean Hass Avocados
Description: 
Chicken, avocado, and mandarin oranges wrapped in lettuce leaves make a delicious salad you can eat with your hands.
