Title:
Fresh & Fast Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1655432.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup red onion, chopped
1 bunch cilantro, stems cut off and leaves chopped
1 jalapeno, minced
1 lime, juiced
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 roma tomatoes, seeded and diced
4 Avocados from Mexico, cubed

Directions:
Mix diced onion, cilantro, jalapeno, lime juice and garlic. Add tomatoes and mix. Gently fold in avocados. Serve with fresh veggies or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Avocados from Mexico
Description: 
This classic guacamole with creamy avocados, fresh cilantro, jalapeno, and red onion is ready to serve in just minutes.
