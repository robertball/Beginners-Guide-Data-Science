Title:
Warm Brie Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package mini puff pastry shells (frozen)
1 tsp dried basil
1 package brie cheese
1 package walnut halves
1 jar red pepper jelly

Directions:
Bake shells according to the package directions.
Sprinkle each shell with basil. Pack each shell with small cubes of brie cheese. Top each shell with 1/2 walnut.
Bake at 375 degrees for ten minutes.
Top each shell with jelly

Submitter: 
KerriJ
Description: 
My mother gave me this recipe that she has used over and over - everyone loves it!  Be sure to only use mini puff pastry shells - not tart shells - the pastry will overwhelm the brie & basil mixture.
