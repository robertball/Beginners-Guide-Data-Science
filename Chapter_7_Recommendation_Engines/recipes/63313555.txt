Title:
Pomegranante-Apple Spritzer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 c sparkling water, chilled
3 c apple juice, chilled
3 c pomegranate juice, chilled
3 c lemon-lime-flavored soda, chilled
Garnish:  apple slices, orange slices

Directions:
In a large pitcher, combine sparkling water, apple juice, pomegranate juice, and soda.  Garnish with apple and orange slices, if desires.  Serve immediately.
