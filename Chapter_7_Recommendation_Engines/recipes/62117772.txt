Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*  2- pkg 8 oz cream cheese soft
* 1- pkg ranch for salad dressing (not the dip style)
* 3- cloves garlic,minced
* 3- boneless chicken breasts
* 1- 4 oz bottle "Franks Red Hot Sauce" to taste
* 2- cups cheddar cheese

Directions:
1.  Boil Chicken breasts, cool then cube
2. Mix both packages of cream cheese,garlic and ranch dressing together
3. Spread the mixture in the bottom of a 9x13 pan
4. Place diced chicken on top evenly
5. Sprinkle "Franks" sauce on top to taste
6. Cover with Cheddar cheese
7. Cover with tin foil heat at 350 degrees for 20 minutes

Submitter: 
POPS OHIO
Description: 
Cream cheese, cheddar cheese, bubbly out of the oven with scooper chips! Perfect for those Ohio State Buckeye Football Saturdays!
