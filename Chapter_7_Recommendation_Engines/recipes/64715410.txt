Title:
Biddy's Butternut Squash and Turkey Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5727199.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey 93/7%
1 Large butternut squash peeled and cubed
20 oz spicy red pepper marinara sauce (this is approx)
1 small yellow onion diced
4 cloves minced garlic
16 oz fresh spinach
10-12 oz skim milk ricotta
2  lg eggs
9 lasagna noodles
1 cup shredded low fat mozzarella
½ cup shredded Parmesan
1 tsp ground sage
1 tsp Italian seasoning
½ tsp Crushed red pepper
Olive oil

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375. Toss cubed butternut squash in olive oil, sage and S&P. Place on baking sheet and bake until soft (about 20 minutes) (once squash is done, pull it out but keep the oven on).
2. In large saucepan, simmer marinara on low.
3. Saute 2 cloves garlic and diced yellow onion until caramelized. Add to marinara.
4. Wilt spinach in saute pan with olive oil, then place on plate with paper towel to absorb some of the moisture (this might take several paper towels).
5. Brown turkey in olive oil with the last of the garlic, add italian seasoning and red pepper as desired, S&P. Place in bowl with wilted spinach and mix.
6. Boil large pot of water to cook lasagna noodles (about 8 minutes) al dente (when noodles are done separate and lay out to keep them from sticking).
7. In large bowl mix soft squash, ricotta, and eggs together until smooth.
8. In a 9x13 baking pan, put a thin layer of marinara covering the bottom.
9. Begin to layer your lasagna. Place 3 noodles on the marinara, add layer of ricotta/squash mixture, add layer of ground turkey, followed by marinara, followed by 3 noodles….. Etc.
10. Once lasagna is built, layer the shredded mozzarella and sprinkle with parmesan.
11. Bake in oven for 45 minutes, let cool 10 minutes before cutting.
