Title:
Big Ray's Go to Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1841463.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach - thawed, drained and squeezed dry
1 (14 ounce) jar marinated artichoke hearts, drained and well chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream
1 (8 ounce) packages Neufchatel cheese, softened
2 Tbp lemon juice
1/4 tsp red pepper
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 7x11 inch baking dish.
In a medium bowl, mix together the Neufchatel cheese, sour cream and mayonnaise until smooth. Mix in the artichoke hearts, spinach and 2/3 cup Parmesan cheese. Season with red pepper, garlic and lemon juice. Spread evenly into the prepared baking dish.
Mix remaining Parmesan cheese, cheddar cheese and bread crumbs.  Sprinkle over dip.
Bake covered for 20 minutes. Remove the cover, and let the dish bake uncovered for 5 more minutes, or until the surface is lightly browned.
