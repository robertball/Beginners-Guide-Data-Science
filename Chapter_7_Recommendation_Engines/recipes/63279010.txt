Title:
Remoulade Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 table spoon ketchup
1 clove garlic; pressed
2 tea spoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
1/8 teaspooncayenne pepper; (1/8 to 1/4)
1/2 to 1/4 tea spoon capers (optional)

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, ketchup, garlic, lemon juice, parsley, and cayenne; mix until well blended. Rough crush capers add to mixture mix well.

Submitter: 
dcoy88
Description: 
Use on french fries, on top of roast beef items, and as a hot dog condiment, although there are a multitude of other applications. Great with most fish.
