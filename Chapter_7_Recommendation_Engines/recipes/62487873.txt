Title:
Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups corn oil
1 3/4 cups sugar
3 eggs
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
.5 tsp nutmeg
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
2 cups grated carrot
2/3 cup minced walnuts
1/2 cup flaked coconut
1 8oz can crushed pineapple, drained

Directions:
Beat oil, eggs and sugar until well combined.
Sift flour, soda, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg.
Beat into sugar mixture.
Stir in vanilla.
Add carrots, walnuts, pineapple and coconut.
Mix until well blended.
Pour into greased & floured bundt or 2 round or 2 square pans
Bake at 350 degrees- bundt: 1 hr, 2 pans: 45 minutes
Cool on cake rack.
Frost with cream cheese frosting and garnish with walnuts.
