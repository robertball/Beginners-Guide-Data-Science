Title:
Pie Crust Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 1/2 C. flour
1 C. shortening
1 egg
1 T. vinegar
1 t. salt
1/4+ C. water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425Â°
Add salt to flour. Cut in shortening until it is pea-sized
Beat together egg, vinegar and water
Add egg mixture to flour mixture and toss together with fork
Knead a little then roll out.
Place in pie plate crimping edges and fill with whatever. Bake 10 mmin..

Pretty basic pastry pie crust. You can get fancy with the top if you want to make it look special.
