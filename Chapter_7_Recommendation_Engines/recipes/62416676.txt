Title:
Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 mango - peeled and diced
1/4 cup chopped red onion
2-3 green onions (with tops) chopped thinly.
1 serrano chile, minced (optional or use one or two canned jalapeno slices)or leave chile out completely.
2-3 large basil leaves (freshly chopped)
2 tablespoons lime juice (or fresh lime)
2 tablespoons olive oil, divided

Directions:
For the mango salsa, sautee onions with a splash of olive oil until onions are slightly softened. Set aside. Sautee the mango and chopped basil with a splash of olive oil. When the mango is slightly browned, remove from heat. Add together with the onions, squeeze fresh lime juice overtop, and serve over your favorite dish.
