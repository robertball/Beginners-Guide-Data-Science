Title:
Avocado Deviled Eggs with Grilled Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4329023.jpg
Ingredients:
12 hard-boiled eggs
24 peeled and shrimp
cilantro, fresh, small bunch
1 lime, juiced
1 teaspoon cumin
1/2 cup mayonnaise
4 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 avocado
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon gralic powder
1/2 stick butter
salt & pepper

Directions:
Place eggs in a single layer in a large pot.  Cover with water by 1 inch.   Bring the water to a simmer over high heat.  Remove from heat.  Cover and let stand for 10 minutes.
Let cool and then peel.  (To get the full egg out out of the shell, place the eggs in ice cold water for 5-10 mins) Slice eggs in half and gently remove the yolks into a bowl.
Add avocado, juice from half a lime, cumin, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, cumin, chopped cilantro salt & pepper and mash.
Pipe or scoop into the egg white halves.
In a bowl, whisk lime juice, chopped cilantro, garlic powder, butter, salt & pepper.  Add shrimp and let marinate for 15 minutes.
Using a grill pan on high heat, grill shrimp on both sides until opaque with grill marks, about 2-3 minutes each side.  let rest to room temperature.
Top each deviled egg with a shrimp
