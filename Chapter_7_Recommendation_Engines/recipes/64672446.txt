Title:
Maws  Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4587021.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce ) Cans of condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup of milk
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
8 cups cooked cut green beans
2 2/3 cups French Fried Onions

Directions:
Stir soup, milk, pepper, beans and 1 1/3 cups onions in 3-qt. casserole.
Bake at 350 degrees F. for 25 min. or until hot. Stir.
Top with remaining onions. Bake for 5 min. more.
