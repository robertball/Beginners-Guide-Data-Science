Title:
Frito Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can corn
1 bell pepper
1 c Miracle Whip
1 bag chili cheese fritos

Directions:
Drain corn & chop pepper. Mix both w/ Miracle Whip. Just before serving stir in fritos.
