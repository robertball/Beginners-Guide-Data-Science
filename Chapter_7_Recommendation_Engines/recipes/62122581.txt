Title:
Canadian Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 Chicken Drumsticks (any part of the chicken may be used)
1 250 ml bottle Italian Dressing
1 package Extra Crispy Shake-and-Bake
1/4 cup Flour
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp Paprika
Bread Crumbs
Canola oil for frying

Directions:
1. Poke many holes in chicken using a fork.  Marinate in Italian dressing for 2 hours turning frequently.
2. Pour Shake-N-Bake into a one cup measure.  Add Flour, salt, paprika and top off measuring cup with bread crumbs.
3. Toss chicken in bread crumb mixture pulling back skin to make it extra crispy.  Deep fry 8-10 minutes in hot oil until done.  Drain on paper towels.
