Title:
Addictive Healthy Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole wheat wrap
6 cherry tomatoes
6 olives
a good 1/2 cup fat free cottage cheese
1 scallion
1/4 medium green pepper
salt to taste

Directions:
1.(optional)
Insert tomatoes and olives in a boat shaped piece of foil and place it in broiler on 350F. (bottom part of oven)
2.
In youre oven(350f) warm up the wrap on  foil for 3 minutes should be crisp but rollable.
Slice scallion and cube pepper into a small bowl mix with cottage cheese and salt to taste.
3.
Take out the warm wrap place cheese mixture lengthwise in middle,top with tomatoes and olives fold two ends slightly over and the other two ends all the way to the middle flapping over.  Turn the wrap over and cut in half with a sharp knife.
