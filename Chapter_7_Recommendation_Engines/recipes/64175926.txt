Title:
Connie's Zucchini "Crab" Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups grated zucchini
egg replacer for 2 eggs
1 1/2 cup gluten-free bread crumbs, split
1/4 cup minced onion
1 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning TM

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees.
In a large bowl, combine zucchini, egg replacer. Stir in 1 cup seasoned crumbs, minced onion, and seasoning. Mix well.
Shape mixture into patties. Dredge in 1/2 cup crumbs.
Put on parchment lined baking sheet and bake 25 min. (15 min on one side, 10 on the other)until light brown.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
These really taste like crab cakes but without the crab, and are a really good way to utilize that bumper crop of zucchini!Modified from: Connie's Zucchini Crab Cakes
