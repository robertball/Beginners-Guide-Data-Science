Title:
Berry Lemonade Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5478952.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle white wine
2 (12 ounce) packages mixed frozen berries
1 1/2 cups peach-flavored vodka
1 (12 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate

Directions:
Mix wine, berries, vodka, and lemonade concentrate in a large pitcher; chill for flavors to blend, at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Ailee
Description: 
Lemonade and berries make this sangria a refreshing summer staple! The longer you chill, the more the flavors blend, so I always try to chill overnight.
