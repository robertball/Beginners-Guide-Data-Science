Title:
Dad's juicy porkchops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Garlic salt, season salt, olive oil, thin cut pork chops.

Directions:
season both sides of pork chops with garlic and season salts.  Heat pan on medium high heat, pour in olive oil coating the entire pan.  Grill until you see meat begin to crown and turn to finish for 1 minute.

Submitter:

Description: 
Simple, juicy pan grilled pork chops.
