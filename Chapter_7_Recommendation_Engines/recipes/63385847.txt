Title:
Spinach and Orzo Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package uncooked orzo pasta
1 (10 ounce) package baby spinach leaves, wilted
3/4 cup crumbled gorgonzola cheese
1/2 red onion, caramelized
1/2 cup pine nuts
1/2 cup craisins
2 T Balsamic Vinegar
2 T Rice Vinegar
2 teaspoon sugar
2 teaspoon salt
1 cup olive oil
4 T fresh lemon juice
1/2  tsp pepper
2 cloves garlic minced
1/2 tsp dry mustard

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.  Add orzo and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and rinse with cold water.  Transfer to a large bowl and stir in spinach, gorgonzola, onion, pine nuts and craisins.  Mix remaining ingredients together to make dressing.  Use 1/2 of dressing for salad.  Refrigerate and serve cold.

Submitter: 
Tami Regan
Description: 
Orzo pasta is tossed with spinach, red onion, gorgonzola cheese, pine nuts, craisins, olive oil and vinegar salad dressing, creating a delicious, colorful cold salad.Modified from: Spinach and Orzo Salad
