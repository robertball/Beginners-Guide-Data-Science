Title:
Venison woth Mushroom Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs venison, cut up
pepper
1 1/2 pkgs. dry onion soup mix
1 1/2 cans cream of mushroom soup
dash of garlic powder (opt)

Directions:
Place meat on a sheet of heavy duty aluminum foil large enough to wrap  securely around meat. season meat with pepper, soup mix, and garlic powder. Pour soup over meat, wrap securely; bake at 350" for 2-3 hours or until tender.

Submitter: 
Jen Lee
Description: 
My Grandad taught me how to cook this one at the hunting club. Easy to throw together and ready when the guys are back from a long day of hunting.
