Title:
Quick Pina Colada Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5024804.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup pineapple juice
1/2 cup unsweetened coconut cream
1/4 cup ice cubes, or as desired

Directions:
Blend pineapple juice, coconut cream, and ice cubes together in a blender on high speed until smooth, 30 to 40 seconds.
