Title:
Best Ever Secret Ingredient Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs very lean ground beef
1 1/2 cups water
3 tbsp Dave's brand liquid seasoning (or soy sauce)
1 tbsp chopped garlic
1 tsp cumin
6 drops liquid smoke
1 tbsp powdered type beef bouillon
1 cup finely chopped celery
1 cup finely chopped sweet yellow onion
1/2 cup finely chopped green bell pepper
1 7oz jar diced pimentos
1 can stewed tomatoes with juice, smashed
1 can mild tomatoes and green chilis with juice (like Rotel)
2/3 can Bush's chili beans (mild or hot)
1/2 large package William's original Chili Seasoning (to taste)
1/2 Hersheys chocolate bar
1/2 tsp creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup brewed coffee
1 tbsp dark molasses (optional)

Directions:
In a large iron pot combine first seven (7) ingredients and cook covered, on high, stirring occasionally - keeping meat in larger, bite-sized chunks until almost browned.
Add the celery, onions and green pepper to the meat and cook until tender; drain.
Add the remaining ingredients to the pot and cook uncovered on medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, until flavors mingle well. (about 20 minutes).

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
This is a rich, super meaty and VERY delicious home-made chili, with some surprise ingredients.
Perfectly scrumptuous!
