Title:
Grilled Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of cabbage
4 TBSP. butter, cubed
5 bacon strips
1/4  parmesan cheese
2 TBSP of galic

Directions:
Hollow the core from the head of cabbage  and fill with parmesan cheese butter and garlic layer these until the hollow part of the cabbage is full then wrap cabbage in bacon the wrap a few times in foil be sure to keep the open core to the top and grill or you could bake for 1 hour or longer if needed

Submitter: 
Chelle Walls
Description: 
This is a great twist on Cabbage smells wonder and is Sinfully delicious!
