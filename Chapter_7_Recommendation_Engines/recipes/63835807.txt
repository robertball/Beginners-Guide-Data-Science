Title:
Twice-Baked Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Base
4 cups, (approx. 3#) sweet potatoes whole with skin on
1/2 c white sugar
2 eggs beaten
1 t sea salt
4 T, 1/2 stick salted butter, melted
1/2 c whole milk
1/2 t vanilla extract
Topping
1/2 c brown sugar
1/3 c AP flour
4 T butter softened or melted
1/2 chopped pecans

Directions:
For Base;
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Place whole, unpeeled sweet potatoes in a casserole dish with a glass cover.
Bake for 1 hour until soft to the touch.
Let cool 2 hours or overnight.
Peel sweet potatoes and discard the skins.
Combine remaining ingredients with potatoes.
Pour and spread in a 9x13 casserole dish.
For topping;
Combine all topping ingredients and sprinkle over top of base.
Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 30 minutes until toasty and golden.

Submitter: 
Ed Price
Description: 
A holiday classic.  For those who have boiled their sweet potatoes, twice baked sweet potatoes are a new level of decadence.
