Title:
Chipotle Taco Plates Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1003525.jpg
Ingredients:
10 flour tortillas
1 lb. ground beef
8 oz. HERDEZ® Traditional Chipotle Mexican Cooking Sauce
8 oz. sour cream
8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
1/4 c. cooking oil

Directions:
1. Cook ground beef in a medium skillet on medium heat until browned, drain. 2. Add HERDEZ® Traditional Chipotle Mexican Cooking Sauce, garlic powder, salt and pepper to ground beef and simmer on low-medium heat for 4-6 minutes. Set aside in a bowl 3. Clean skillet and heat cooking oil over medium heat. 4. Place one tortilla at a time in skillet and fry until one side is golden brown, then turn and lightly brown other side. 5. Remove and place on paper towels to drain. 6. Top each tortilla with 1/4 cup ground beef, 1/8 cup cheddar cheese and 1-2 tablespoons sour cream. Fold sides upwards (or leave flat) and enjoy while warm.

Description: 
A new flavor for an old family favorite. HERDEZ® Traditional Chipotle Mexican Cooking Sauce took this simple dish to a new level adding a burst of smoky flavor followed by a satisfying heat that will leave you wanting another. Quick, easy and extremely flavorful!
