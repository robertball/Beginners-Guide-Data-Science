Title:
Original KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Squares with NEW VANILLA RICE KRISPIES* Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985057.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Vanilla cereal

Directions:
Melt margarine in large saucepan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
Stir in vanilla. Stir in KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal until well coated. Using lightly buttered spatula, press into buttered 3.5 L (13 x 9-inch) pan.
Allow mixture to cool. Cut into bars. Best if served same day.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
Use KELLOGG'S* VANILLA RICE KRISPIES* for the original crunchy, chewiness of these snack treats but with a taste of vanilla.
