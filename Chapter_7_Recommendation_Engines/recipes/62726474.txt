Title:
Chicken and Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pkg. cooked chopped broccoli
4 chicken breasts (precooked and cut into bite size pieces)
1 t poultry seasoning
1 t salt
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 C sour cream
1 T butter or margarine
1 small can of mushrooms
1 1/2 t lemon juice
Paprika

Directions:
Mix all ingred. except paprika in casserole dish. Top w/paprika.
Bake 45 min at 350 degrees. This will not be a solid casserole

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Broccoli, chicken breasts, mushroom soup, sour cream, cheese, and mushrooms are all thrown together and baked
