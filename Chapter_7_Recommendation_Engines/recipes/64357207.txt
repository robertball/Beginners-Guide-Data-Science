Title:
Baked Caramel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup unpopped popcorn (makes 6 cups popped)
1 cup butter
2 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup corn syrup
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Pop popcorn and set aside.
Melt butter,  then stir in brown sugar, corn syrup and salt.  Bring to a boil stirring, then boil without stirring 5 minutes.
Remove from heat, stir in baking soda and vanilla.
Pour over popped popcorn.  Turn into 2 cake pans and bake at 250 degrees F., for  1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
Cool and store in airtight container.
