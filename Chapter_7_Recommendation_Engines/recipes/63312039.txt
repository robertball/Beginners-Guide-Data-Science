Title:
Thai Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch cilantro
3 Tablespoons EVOO
1 1/2 Tablespoons Fish sauce
1/4 cup peanut butter
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon brown sugar

Directions:
Put all ingredients in the blender and blend until smooth.  Serve immediately or refrigerate in an air-tight container until needed.

Submitter: 
she_cooks
Description: 
Add the taste of Thai to noodles, salad, rice, or any dish with this versatile, full-flavored sauce!
