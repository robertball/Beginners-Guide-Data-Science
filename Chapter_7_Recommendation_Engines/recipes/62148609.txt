Title:
Butternut Squash Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4 pound butternut squash (peeled and cubed)
1/3 finely chopped yellow onion
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/2 plus 1/4 cup italian bread crumbs
1-2 tablespoons fresh thyme finely chopped
1 6 oz. container of crumbled blue cheese
sea salt
freshly ground pepper

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, toss cubed butternut squash, chopped yellow onion, olive oil, 1/2 cup of italian bread crumbs,(save the 1/4 cup for topping) chopped thyme, and entire container of blue cheese.
Season to taste with sea salt and fresh ground pepper.
Place squash mixture in a baking dish.
Sprinkle squash mixture with remaining 1/4 cup of italian bread crumbs.
Bake at 425 degrees for 35-40 minutes, or until lightly browned on top.
Serve immediately!

Submitter: 
Too Hot Tamale
Description: 
A savory side dish or vegetarian main dish made with butternut squash, and accented with fresh thyme and blue cheese.
