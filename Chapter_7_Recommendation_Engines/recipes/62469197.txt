Title:
Bonniata's Veal Picatta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/477470.jpg
Ingredients:
4 veal scallopini
2T butter
1/2 c. flour
1 garlic clove
2T lemon juice
1/4 cup dry white wine (if you wouldn't drink it don't cook with it!)
1/4c. chicken broth
1 and 1/2 T. Capers drained
pinch of paprika
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Dredge veal scallopini in flour.
Melt butter.  Add floured veal and cook 30 sec each side, then transfer veal to a warm platter.  Add garlic, saute 30 seconds.  Add wine boil two minutess.  Stir in broth, simmer until reduced by half.  Wisk in 2T. butter.  Add capers.  Spoon over veal.
