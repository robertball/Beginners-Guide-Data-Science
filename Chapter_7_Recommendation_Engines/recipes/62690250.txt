Title:
Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For cinnamon sugar coating:
2 tablespoons sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
For cookie dough:
1 cup margarine or butter, softened
1 1/3 cups sugar
2 eggs
3 cups flour
1 1/2 teaspoons cream of tartar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In a large bowl, beat together the margarine or butter, sugar, and eggs until light and fluffy.
Add the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Beat with an electric mixer until well blended.
In a small bowl, mix together 2 tablespoons sugar and 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon. Shape dough into 1 inch balls and roll in cinnamon and sugar coating. Place 2 1/2" apart on cookie sheets and bake for 10 minutes or until edges are golden.
