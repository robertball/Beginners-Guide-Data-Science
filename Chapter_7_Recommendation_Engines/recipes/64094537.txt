Title:
Hangchow Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups water
2 lb whole fish
2 Tbsp oil
2 Tbsp ginger, shredded
1 scallion, shredded
1 cup chicken stock
1/2 tsp salt
6 Tbsp red wine vinegar
6 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp cornstarch, dissolved in 2 Tbsp water
Black pepper

Directions:
Pour water into a large roasting pan.  Bring to a boil over a range unit.  Cover and poach fish in boiling water 20 minutes.  Turn off heat.
Heat oil in wok to 275 degrees F.  Stir fry ginger and scallion until aroma comes, about 1 minute.  Remove.
Combine sauce ingredients (except for cornstarch and black pepper) in wok and bring to boil.  Add cornstarch.  Add ginger and scallions.  Bring to boil again.
Transfer fish to platter.  Pour sauce over and sprinkle with black pepper.
