Title:
PC Lemon Pepper Mushroom Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lemon
1 can (10.75 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup 2% milk1 cup
(4 oz) shredded cheddar cheese
1 garlic clove, pressed
6 slices soft white sandwich bread,
1/2 oz Parmesan cheese, grated (about 2 tbsp)
2 tbsp butter, melted
Mushrooms - Portabella
2 tbsp Lemon Pepper Rub
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 large head broccoli, cut into small florets (about 3 cups)2 cups uncooked instant white rice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400°F. Zest lemon using Microplane® Adjustable Grater to measure ½ tsp. Juice lemon to measure 2 tbsp. Combine lemon zest, juice, soup, milk, cheddar cheese and pressed garlic in Classic Batter Bowl; whisk until smooth. Coarsely chop bread using Food Chopper. Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese and butter in Small Batter Bowl; mix well.
2
Sprinkle  mushrooms with rub. Heat oil in (12-in.) Skillet over medium-high and sautee. Add mushrooms; cook 6-8 minutes or until golden brown, turning once. Remove Skillet from heat; add broccoli. Pour soup mixture evenly over mushrooms and broccoli. Top with bread crumb mixture. Bake 15-17 minutes or until bread crumb mixture is golden brown and sauce is bubbly. Meanwhile, cook rice according to package directions. To serve, divide rice among serving plates; top with mushrooms and sauce.
