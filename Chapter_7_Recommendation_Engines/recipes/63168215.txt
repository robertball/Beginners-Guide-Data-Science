Title:
Scalloped Potatoes and Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 3 lbs potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/3-inch slices
1 lb cooked ham, cubed
1 small onion, chopped
2 cups shreded cheddar cheese
1 can cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
Spray slow cooker with cooking spray.
Layer one-third of the potatoes, ham, onion and cheese into the slow cooker.
Repeat layers twice.
Spread soup on top.
Cover and cook on low 6 - 7 hours.
