Title:
Million Dollar Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 9" Graham Cracker Pie Shells
1 can Eagle Brand Condensed Milk
1 small can frozen lemonade or limeade
1 med. can drained crushed pineapple
1 large can drained mandrian oranges
1 large tub Cool Whip
1 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup chopped maraschino cherries (drained)

Directions:
Beat milk, lemonade together with mixer, fold in remaining ingredients
Pour into pie shells & let set 3-4 hours in refrigerator before serving
