Title:
Cheesy Beef Sausage Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb Ground Pork Sausage
1 lb Ground Beef
1 Cup Savory Herb stuffing crushed
1 Tbsp Savory Herb stuffing crushed
1 Tbsp Seasoning Salt
Salt and Pepper to taste
1/4 Cup Worshire Sauce
1/4 Cup Ketchup
2 Cups small cubed Velveeta cheese
1 egg
1/4 Cup Milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Combine in a large mixing bowl
1/2 lb Ground Italian Sausage
1 lb Ground Beef
1 Cup Savory Herb stuffing crushed
1 Tbsp Seasoning Salt
Salt and Pepper to taste
1/4 Cup Worshire Sauce
1/4 Cup Ketchup
2 Cups small cubed Velveeta cheese
1 egg
1/4 Cup Milk
Mix well and form into log in a glass baking dish or place in a loaf pan. Sprinkle 1 Tbsp Savory Herb stuffing crushings over top and place in oven for 60 minutes, take out of oven and try to pour out as much juice as possible. Return to oven for 20 more minutes until middle of meatloaf is cooked
