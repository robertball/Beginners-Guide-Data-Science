Title:
Chicken Croquettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. coarsely ground cooked chicken
1 c. soft bread crumbs (about 2 slices)
2 eggs, beaten
2 T. milk
1/2 tsp. salt
1 T. minced onion
1/2 c. dry bread crumbs or cracker crumbs
1/2 c. milk
vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
Combine chicken, soft bread crumbs, eggs, 2 T. milk, salt, and onion.
Chill about 2 hours.
Shape into logs about 1 inch in diameter and 2 - 3 inches long.
Roll in dry bread crumbs, milk, then bread crumbs again.
Fry croquettes in hot oil until browned on all sides, about 2 min.
Drain on paper towels.
Serve with Veloute Sauce.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
This makes delicate, soft chicken patties.  Prep is messy but a great job for the kids.

Serve with Veloute sauce (saved separately).
