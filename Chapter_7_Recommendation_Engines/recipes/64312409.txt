Title:
Cottage Cheese with Tomato and Cucumber Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cucumber(s)
1 cup cherry tomatoes
1/2 cup(s) cottage cheese, low-fat, low sodium
peppercorns, black freshly ground, to taste

Directions:
Wash and slice vegetables. Arrange on a plate.
Top each piece with a little cottage cheese. Season to taste with pepper or other herbs.
