Title:
Sweet Hickory Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982076.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 yellow onion, sliced, separated into rings
1 (1 pound) boneless beef sirloin steak
1/4 cup A.1. Sweet Hickory Steak Sauce

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Melt butter in medium skillet on medium heat. Add onions; cook 8 to 10 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally.
Meanwhile, grill steak 5 minutes on each side or until medium doneness (160 degrees F).
Stir steak sauce into onion mixture; cook an additional 5 minutes or until heated through, stirring occasionally. Cut steak into four pieces. Serve with the onion sauce.
