Title:
All-American Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976598.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon Lawry's® Seasoned Salt
1 tablespoon Lawry's® Seasoned Pepper
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into serving pieces
Vegetable oil

Directions:
In large resealable plastic bag, combine Lawry's(R) Seasoned Salt, Lawry's(R) Seasoned Pepper and flour.  Add chicken, a few pieces at a time, and toss to coat.
In 12-inch deep skillet, heat 1-inch oil over medium-high heat and cook chicken, turning twice, 25 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked; drain on paper towels.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
This simply seasoned classic fried chicken recipe will become a family favorite.
