Title:
Parmesean Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup parsley
1 cup white rice
2 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons grated Parmesan
coarse salt

Directions:
In medium saucepan with lid, bring 1 1/2 cups water to a boil.  Add rice and teaspoon salt.  Cover and reduce to a simmer.  Cook until water is absorbed and rice is tender, about 20 minutes.  Remove from heat.
Add butter, Parmesan and parsley, fluff with a fork.
