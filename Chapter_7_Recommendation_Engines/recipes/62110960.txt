Title:
k.o. coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cold coffee
1 cup milk
2 scoops vanilla ice cream
2 tbsp hazelnut flavored liquer
2 tbsp kahula
2 tbsp carmel flaveored syrup
2 tbsp confectioners sugar
2 tbsp chocolate syrup
1 tbsp REAL maple syrup
1/4 cup chocolate chips
1/4 cup white chocolate
16 ice cubes
chocolate covered expresso beans

Directions:
in large blender add ice, coffee, milk, hazlenut liquer, syrups, sugar, chocolate chips blend
add ice cream blend
in empty serving glass add chocolate covered expresso beans to taste pour coffee drink over the beans serve with a wide straw to suck up expresso beans
serve immediately
