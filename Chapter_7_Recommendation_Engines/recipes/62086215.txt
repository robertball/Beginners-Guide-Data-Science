Title:
Super Zuppa! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove of garlic, minced
1 small zucchini, julienned
6 baby carrots, julienned
4c beef stock
1c tomato sauce
3/4t italian seasoning
15oz can cannellini beans, drained
3oz small pasta
parmesean cheese

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic in olive oil over medium heat a few minutes until soft and fragrant.  Add zucchini and carrots and saute until slightly softened, about 5 minutes.  Add stock, tomato sauce and italian seasoning and bring to a boil.  Drop heat and simmer 15 minutes.  Add pasta and beans and simmer until pasta is al dente.  Top with grated parmesean cheese and with a crisp salad and warm bread.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
extrayummystuff
Description: 
Talk about quick!  This toss-together soup ended up being a primo staple in our house with warm bread and a salad.  We've used various vegetables and pastas and it's always great.  Tiny pasta like orzo or ditallini works great.  When my kids were little they asked for alphabet pasta.
