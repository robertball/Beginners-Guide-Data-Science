Title:
White Chocolate Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
18 shortbread cookies, broken
3/4 cup chopped pecans
3/4 stick of butter, melted
Filling:
4 eight-ounce packages cream cheese (room temperature)
1 1/4 cups sugar
4 eggs
4 ounces imported white chocolate (finely chopped)
4 tablespoons hazelnut liqueur

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Crust:
Using a food processor, chop shortbread cookies until they turn to fine crumbs, add pecans and melted butter. Process until crumbs begin to stick together. Press mixture into bottom and 1 inch up the sides of a 9" spring form pan.
Filling:
Using an electric mixer, beat cream cheese which is at room temperature in large bowl until light and fluffy (beat well for creamier cake). Gradually beat in sugar. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in white chololate and hazelnut liqueur. Pour filling into crust.
Bake cake approximately 1 hour and 20 minutes or until edges are set and center moves only slightly when pan is shaken.  Cool completley in pan on cooling rack. Cover and refrigerate at least 24 hours. Run small sharp knife around edges of pan to losen cake from pan, release pan sides, cut into wedges and serve.

Submitter: 
CindyT
Description: 
This recipe is always a hit with family and friends and relatively easy to make! I often take it along to Christmas get togethers. The hazelnut liqueur and white chocolate, along with the shortbread, pecan crust sets this cheese cake apart from all others!
