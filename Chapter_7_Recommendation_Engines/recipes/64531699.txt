Title:
White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3056260.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Small onion, chopped
1 Small green pepper, chopped
1 Jalapeno, seeded and chopped
1-2 Tbl Olive Oil
1 Tbl cumin
1 Tsp. chili powder
Salt and Pepper to taste
1/2 Tsp.  basil
1 Can Chicken Broth
1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Can Drained cannelloni Beans
3-4 Tbsp. Chopped Cilantro
2-4 Chicken breasts

Directions:
Cut up chicken and cook in a frying pan with olive oil.
Mix all ingredients (except cream of chicken soup) in  a pot.
Simmer on stove for 1 hour.
10 minutes before you serve stir in Cream of Chicken soup.
Serve (over rice if desired) topped with sour cream, cheddar cheese and chopped cilantro.
