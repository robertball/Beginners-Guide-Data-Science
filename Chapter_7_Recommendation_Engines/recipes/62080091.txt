Title:
Laban ib khyar (yogurt with cucumber) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cucumbers, peeled, and finely diced
1 container of yogurt
2 tablespoons of dried mint.
2-3 cloves of garlic, mashed.
salt, to taste

Directions:
open the container of yogurt and place in a medium bowl.
add the rest of the ingredients and mix.
serve.

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
This arabic version is great on rice or even eating by itself.  It consists of mint, cucumber, garlic and yogurt.
