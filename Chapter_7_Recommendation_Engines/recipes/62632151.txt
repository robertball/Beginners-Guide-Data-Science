Title:
Aunt Sara's Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1   Cup Shortening
2   Cups Sugar
2   Eggs
1   Cup Sour Cream or Buttermilk
1   tsp Vanilla
1   tsp Nutmeg
4 1/2 Cups Flour
1   tsp Baking Powder
2   tsp Baking Soda

Directions:
Mix well,Cover and Refrigerate at least 3 hrs or over night. Preheat oven to 350,  Roll thick, use mason jar large mouth lid to cut, sprinkle w sugar place a raisin in center bake 10 to 12 min. Let cool 5 min.
I have always used sour cream my aunts orignial recipie has sour crean or buttermilk
