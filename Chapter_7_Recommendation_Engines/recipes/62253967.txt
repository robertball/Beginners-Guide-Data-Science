Title:
Dijon Croque Madame Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbl dijon
Bread
Ham
Gruyere cheese
3 eggs
1/4 cup milk
Pepper

Directions:
Cut hole for egg.
Spread mustard over bread.
Layer each slice with ham and cheese.
Dip both sides of sandwich into egg and milk mixture.
Heat skillet medium heat.  Butter pan.
Place in pan.  Insert egg.
Cook until cheese melts.
