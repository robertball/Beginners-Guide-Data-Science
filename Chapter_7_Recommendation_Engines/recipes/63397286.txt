Title:
Easy Spaghetti Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 spaghetti squash
1/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan (packed)
2 Tbl. olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cut spaghetti squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds
Salt and pepper the inside of both halves and place face down in a 9X13 pan.  Fill pan with 3/4 inch of water.
Place pan in oven and bake for 45 minutes or until tender.
While squash is baking, grab a small cup or bowl and add Parmesan and Olive oil.  Stir to combine the sauce.
Scoop out the spaghetti squash from the shell and place into a large bowl.  Add Parmesan sauce and stir to combine.
Sprinkle a generous amount salt and freshly ground pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
halfnotes
Description: 
With a little Parmesan and Olive Oil, you can turn a bland spaghetti squash into something special.
