Title:
Split Pea Soup with Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/828090.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. (2 cups 1/4) dried split peas, rinsed and sorted
2 quarts chicken, beef, or vegetable broth or 8 c. water + 8 tsp. chicken or vegetable bouillon or soup base
1 lb. bone-in ham, diced into small pieces and trimmed of excessive fat
2 medium carrots, peeled and chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
4-5 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp. Italian seasoning
1/2 tsp. marjoram
1/2 tsp. smoked paprika
pinch of thyme
1 bay leaf
1 tsp. malt vinegar

Directions:
Place all the ingredients in a slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 4 hours. You can also cook it on high until it starts to boil and then turn the setting on low until youâ€™re ready to serve the soup. Top with Sourdough Garlic-Herb Croutons. Makes 8 1 1/2-cup servings.
