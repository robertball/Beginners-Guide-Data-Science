Title:
Tomato, Cheese and Basil Pretzel Crisps® Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3467928.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7.2 ounce) package Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
1 pint cherry tomatoes, sliced
1 (4 ounce) package fresh basil, thinly sliced
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Arrange Pretzel Crisps(R) on a baking sheet. Top with basil, Parmesan cheese, and tomato slices.
Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
Place baking pan in preheated oven. Broil until cheese has melted, 2 or 3 minutes. Let cool and serve.

Submitter: 
Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
Description: 
Basil, fresh Parmesan cheese and sliced cherry tomatoes on Pretzel Crisps® make delicious appetizers.
