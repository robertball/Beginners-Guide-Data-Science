Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar (can be decreased, but will affect rise and moisture)
1 cup oil
4 eggs
1 1/2 t salt
1 t cinnamon
2/3 cup water
1 t nutmeg
2 t baking soda
2 cups pumpkin
3 1/2 cups flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease/flour three small loaf pans and set aside.  Mix all ingredients.  Distribute batter evenly between prepared loaf pans.  Bake for one hour.  Done when toothpick comes out clean.  Cool in loaf pan for at least twenty minutes.

Submitter: 
Jackie
Description: 
This is soooo good.  I look forward to it all year long (not that I really have to wait, since I could make it any time).  This is so easy and freezes really well too.
