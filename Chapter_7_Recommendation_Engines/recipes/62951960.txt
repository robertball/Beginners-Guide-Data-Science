Title:
Magic  Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups peanut butter
1 cups white sugar
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheet.
Combine peanut butter, egg, and sugar and mix until smooth. Spoon dough by tablespoons onto a cookie sheet.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned. Let the cookies cool on the cookie sheets for 5 to 10 minutes before removing.
