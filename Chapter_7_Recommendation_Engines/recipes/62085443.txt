Title:
Nicfries Egg Salad and Spinach Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs
8 slices bread of your choice
1/2 small red onion, minced
1/2 cup fat-free mayo
a pinch of cayenne pepper
fresh ground pepper to taste
1 tsp oregano
24 fresh spinach leaves
4 queen pimento stuffed green olives

Directions:
1. Place eggs in a saucepan with enough cold water to cover them. Bring to a boil.
2. Remove saucepan from heat, cover, and let eggs sit in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.
3. Place eggs in a bowl with cold water and ice for a few minutes to cool. Peel under cold running water.
4. Mash eggs in a large bowl using a fork. Add mayo, onion, oregano, cayenne pepper and black pepper.
5. Evenly distribute spinach leaves over 4 slices of bread. Spoon egg salad on top of spinach leaves. Cover with the remaining slices of bread to create four sandwiches.
6. Garnish with green olives.

Submitter: 
Nicole Evans
Description: 
A little bit of a twist on the traditional egg sandwich. A bit of cayenne pepper for an added kick and fresh spinach leaves makes this a flavorful egg salad sandwich.
