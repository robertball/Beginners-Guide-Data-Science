Title:
Gluten Free Vanilla Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1832217.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Gluten Free F&E; Box Vanilla Cake Mix
2 Box F&E; Vanilla Bean Frosting Mix
28 Tablespoons Butter
8 Large Eggs
1 1/3 Cup Unsweetened Almond Milk
8 Tablespoons Water
24 Cupcake Liners
Sprinkles (optional)

Directions:
Prepare cupcakes as directed on Gluten Free F&E Box Vanilla Cake Mix box. While cupcakes are baking prepare frosting as directed on Box F&E Vanilla Bean Frosting Mix box and chill. Once cupcakes are done baking and have completely cooled, frost and top with sprinkles.
