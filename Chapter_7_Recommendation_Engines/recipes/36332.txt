Title:
Sizzlin' Steak Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon McCormick® Grill Mates® Montreal Steak Seasoning
1 pound lean ground beef
4 slices Gouda cheese
4 hamburger rolls

Directions:
Preheat grill.
Mix steak seasoning into meat and form meat into 4 equal-size patties.
Grill patties 5-6 minutes on each side, or to desired doneness.  Add a slice of cheese to each patty, 1 minute before cooking is completed.  Serve on a roll.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
McCormick® Grill Mates® add a bold, zesty flavor to burgers.  To add another flavor twist, top burgers with a gourmet cheese, such as Gruyere, Havarti, or crumbled blue cheese.
