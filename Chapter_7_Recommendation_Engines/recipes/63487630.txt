Title:
Banana Nut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups flour
1/4 cup sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg beaten
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup cooking oil
1 cup bananas mashed
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
pre heat 400F mix flour sugar, baking powder and salt make well in center.  add egg, milk and oil all at once to well.  add banana and stir till just moisened.  batter si lumpy
fill tins 2/3 and bake for 20-25 mins
