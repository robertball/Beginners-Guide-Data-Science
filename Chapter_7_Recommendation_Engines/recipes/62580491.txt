Title:
Grace's Cole Slaw Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C oil
1 C Sugar
1 Small Onion cut fine
1 tsp dry mustard
Add 1/2 cup vinegar
1 tsp celery seed

Directions:
Blend first 4 ingredients together in a blender. Add remaining and dress right before serving
