Title:
Wet Burrito Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz brown gravy (pkg or jar work fine)
12 oz enchilada sauce (homemade or canned)

Directions:
mix both ingredients together and warm in a saucepan.
Fill burritos as desired and plate.  Pour wet burrito sauce over the top and garnish with shredded cheddar cheese.

Submitter: 
Amy Randy Smith
Description: 
This is my FAVORITE recipe for sauce for Macho burritos!  The sauce is super flavorful and so easy and inexpensive to make.  It takes regular burritos to restaurant level!
