Title:
Polenta Parmesan with Sauteed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283926.jpg
Ingredients:
Cheese flavored polenta - cut into 1/4" thick slices
3 Chicken cutlets - cubed into bite size pieces and sauteed in olive oil, garlic, onion , salt and pepper.
1 1/2 cups shredded Italian blend cheese
1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese
32 oz. of your favorite pasta sauce

Directions:
Grease a 9 x 13" baking dish. Put a layer of sliced polenta covering the entire bottom of baking dish. Sprinkle with half the Italian blend shredded cheese. Add half the sauteed chicken on top. Then add half the pasta sauce. Repeat layers one more time.
Top with fresh grated parmesan cheese.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for about 25 minutes or til cheese is browned.
Remove from oven and let it set for about 15 minutes, before cutting and serving.

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
This is a delicious Italian comfort food! I use homemade polenta and pasta sauce for this one.
