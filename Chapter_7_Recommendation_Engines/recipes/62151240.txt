Title:
Upside Down Inside Out Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can buttery Grands buscuits
2 cans cream of mushroom soup, divided
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
1 can mixed vegetables, WELL drained
2 cups rotisserie chicken, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
Cut each biscuit into 8 pieces
Toss dough pieces with 1 can of cream of mushroom soup and half of the cheese.
Spread dough mixture in a sprayed 2 1/2 quart glass baking dish.
Sprinkle mixed veggies over dough pieces.
Mix beef and remaining soup.
Spoon chicken mixture over veggies.
Sprinkle casserole with remaining cheese.
Bake for 30 to 40 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly.
Let rest 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
A combination of buttery Grands buscuit pieces are combined with other savory ingredients creating the easiest, most unusual perfect pie.
