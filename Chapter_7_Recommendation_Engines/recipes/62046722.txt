Title:
Creamy Chicken Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups instant rice
2 cups hot water
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 tablespoons margarine or butter

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Pour in microwave safe dish.  Cook for 7 minutes.  Mix well. Let sit 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
This quick and easy side dish is full of flavor.  It is great served with chicken and stir fry vegetables.
