Title:
Strawberry filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 cups coarsely chopped, hulled strawberries
.5 cup sugar
2.5 tbsp. cornstarch

Directions:
Bring all ingredients to a boil in a heavy small sauce pan, stirring constantly and crushing the berries slightly with the back of the spoon.
Boil 2 minutes to thicken, stirring constantly (mixture will be slightly chunky).
Pour into bowl and cool completely.

Submitter: 
Holly
Description: 
Used this once as a layer in a giant strawberry shortcake!
