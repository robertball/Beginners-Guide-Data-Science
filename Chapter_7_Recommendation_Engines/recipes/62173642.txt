Title:
Pot roasted pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bonless pork roast 1-1 1/2lbs
salt and pepper to taste
dash of garlic powder
1 small onion
1 bay leaf
2tsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp water

Directions:
Trim excess fat from the roast. Sprinkle with salt,pepper, and garlic. Place onion in slow cooker. Add the roast; add the bay leaf, soy sauce and water. Cover and cook 8-10 hours.
