Title:
Tissy's Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1244902.jpg
Ingredients:
-3 tomatoes
-3 cucumbers
-1onion
-1tbs salt
-1 tbs pepper
-1 tbs garlic
1/2 cup zesty Italian dressing
3/4 cup mayo

Directions:
Chunk tomatoes & cucumbers
Dice onions
Mix vegetables together adding salt,
pepper, garlic, mayo, & zesty dressing.
Stir all together
Refrigerate
Enjoy!

Description: 
My momma's spin on cucumber salad. A personal favorite of mine!
