Title:
Dipped Marshmallows Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 toffee bars (Macintosh)
large marshmallows (not sure how many? - 1 bag should be enough!)
1/2 cup butter
1 can sweetened condensed milk (Eagle Brand)
rice krispies

Directions:
Melt first 3 ingredients in large saucepan, stirring constantly.
Partially crush rice krispies.
Dip marshmallows in toffee mixture and then roll in rice krispie crumbs.
Cool.
These also freeze well.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
These always bring fond memories of Grandma! Large marshmallows, dipped in caramel and rice krispies. MMM :) I haven't attempted them yet - maybe worried they won't taste like hers, but I don't want to lose the recipe.
