Title:
Cheddar Chicken Bacon Ranch Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (16 oz) penne pasta, cooked
1 package (16 oz) bacon, cut into bite size pieces
2 chicken breast halves, cut into bite sized cubes
1 Tbsp butter
Salt and pepper to taste
2 Tbsp flour
2 cups broccoli florets, cut into bite sized pieces
1 packet ranch dressing mix
2 cups milk
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet cook bacon over medium heat until desired crispiness is reached, set aside
Add butter and chicken (seasoned with salt and pepper) to the bacon fat left in the skillet and cook until chicken is no longer pink (about 5 minutes)
Add flour and mix until well incorporated
Add ranch dressing mix, broccoli and cook until broccoli is tender
Add milk and reserved bacon, heat
Add cheese and pasta
Once cheese is melted, serve and enjoy!
