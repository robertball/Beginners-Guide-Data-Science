Title:
Tandoori Masala Spice Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/776921.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp Garlic (Lasun) Powder
1 tsp ground Ginger (Adrak)
1 tsp Cloves (Lavang) Powder
½ tsp grated Nutmeg (Jaiphal)
1 tsp Mace Powder (Javitri)
1 ½ Tblsp Cumin (jeera) Powder
2 Tblsp ground Corriander (Dhania)
1 tsp Fenugreek (Methi) Powder
1 tsp ground Cinnamon (Tuj/Dalchini)
1 tsp fresh ground Black Pepper (Kalimirchi)
1 tsp ground brown Cardamom (Elaichi) Seeds

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together.  Store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
TheBritishBaker
Description: 
I needed this mix for a Butter Chicken recipe, rather than another trip to the store I decided to make my own!
