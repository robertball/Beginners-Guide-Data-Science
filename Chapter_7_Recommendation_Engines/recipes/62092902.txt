Title:
German Quick Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. unsifted graham flour
2 c. unsifted all-purpose flour
1 c. sugar
2 t. salt
1 quart buttermilk
2 t. baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F.
Grease two 9x5 loaf pans.
Combine the flours, sugar and salt.
Comine the buttermilk and baking soda.
Stir buttermilk mixture into dry ingredients.
Turn into prepared pans.
Place pans in oven and immediately turn heat down to 350 F. Bake 70 min. or until done in center when tested with a toothpick.
Remove from pans and cool on a rack.

Submitter: 
Diana S.
Description: 
A delicious & easy bread recipe. It calls for graham flour but I've also made it with whole wheat. I love it toasted and spread with butter and honey. This recipe came from a German woman who my friends stayed with while vacationing in that beautiful country.
