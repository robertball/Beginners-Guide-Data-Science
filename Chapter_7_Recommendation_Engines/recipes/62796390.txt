Title:
Sesame Broccoli Ramen Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 packages Ramen noodles (any flavor - save flavor packets for another use)
2 lbs. Broccoli or Asparagus
1/3 C chopped scallions
1/4 C chopped cilantro
TBSP Fresh ginger minced
1/4 C sesame oil
3 TBSP sesame seeds
7 TBSP Soy Sauce
3 TBSP balsamic vinegar
3 TBSP brown sugar
2 tsp salt
2 tsp chili oil

Directions:
Cook Ramen noodles in boiling water for exactly 3 minutes.  Rinse with cold water and drain.  Chop and blanche broccoli or asparagus until crisp tender and bright green.  Mix remaining ingredients in bowl and toss with noodles and broccoli.  Chill for at least one hour.  Best served at room temperature.

Submitter: 
~Gina~
Description: 
This is a budget and picnic friendly salad with fresh ginger and sesame flavor combined with fresh blanched broccoli and ramen noodles.
