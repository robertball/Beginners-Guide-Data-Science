Title:
Chicken fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounce chicken breast
1/4 c. fresh lime juice
2 T. water
1 garlic clove, minced
1 large onion, sliced thinly
1 large green pepper, sliced thinly
tortillas
guacamole
salsa
grated cheese (optional)

Directions:
Marinate chicken with next three ingredients for several hours; drain and grill till tender. Slice thickly.
Meanwhile saute onion and pepper in hot oil till browned and somewhat soft.
Make guacamole.
Serve chicken in tortilla with toppings.

Submitter: 
Susie
Description: 
This simple recipe can be made vegetarian by using tofu or tempah.  Marinate either for a  short time, drain, reserving marinate. Fry in hot oil, then when the tofu /tempah is brown, add reserved marinade and cook till absorbed. Or just fry without soaking, then add marinade.
