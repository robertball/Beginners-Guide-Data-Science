Title:
Captain's Seafood Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970590.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups mayonnaise
1 cup fresh crabmeat
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup French salad dressing
1/2 lemon, juiced
2 teaspoons prepared horseradish

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise, crabmeat, Cheddar cheese, French salad dressing, lemon juice, and horseradish together in a bowl; refrigerate until the flavors blend, at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Cherry Baumgartner
Description: 
This was served to diners at Captain's Seafood, Murrell's Inlet, South Carolina. Serve with stone-ground wheat crackers or other mild (not flavored) cracker.
