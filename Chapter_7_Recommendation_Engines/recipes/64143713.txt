Title:
Basil Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh basil
3 cloves chopped garlic
1/2 c walnuts
1/2 c Italian parsley
1 tsp salt
1/2 to 1 cup EV Olive Oil
1/2 c grated parmesan

Directions:
Place all but the olive oil and parmesan in a food processor.
Add only 1/2 cup of olive oil and process.  Add up to 1/2 cup more of olive oil until you reach the desired consistency.
