Title:
Asparagus Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small bunches asparagus, cut into bite size pieces
1 can garbonzo beans rinsed/drained
1 cup diced red onion
1/2 cup diced celery
1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
1 tbls & 1 tsp minced garlic
approx. 1 tbls sugar
approx 4-5 'splashes' of white rice vinegar
2 tbls & 1/8 cup olive oil
1 tsp lemon pepper
optional:
crumbled feta cheese
dried cranberries or dried tomatoes
nuts
salt/pepper

Directions:
Saute asparagus in 2 tbls olive oil with 1 tsp minced garlic & 1 tsp lemon pepper until al dente.  Remove from heat, pour into a large salad bowl & mix with the following ingredients; garbonzo beans, onion, celery & red bell pepper.
For the dressing mix: 1/4 cup olive oil, vinegar, 1 tsp minced garlic and approx 1 tbls sugar.
*the vinegar & sugar amount(s) are hard to measure exactly,
they are mainly on a 'by taste' basis*
Pour over salad ingredients, toss and refrigerate for at least an hour before eating.
Add 1 or more of the optional ingredients, if desired.

Submitter: 
ahicswife
Description: 
While sauteing asparagus to be used as a side dish, I realized there wouldn't be enough, so I decided to use it in or as a salad.  Well, I came up with this by accident and we love it!  It reminds my of a 3 bean salad, only better.
