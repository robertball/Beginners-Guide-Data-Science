Title:
Cilantro Lime Rice by Marie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6040861.jpg
Ingredients:
•	1 cup white basmati rice
•	2 cups vegetable broth
•	1/2 bunch cilantro
•       2 teaspoons finely diced cilantro stems
•	Juice of 1 Lime (about 1 Tablespoon)
•	1 Garlic Clove
•       2 sliced green onions
•       1 small jalapeno diced
•       fresh ground black pepper
•        pinch of oregano
•	1/2 teaspoon unrefined salt
•	1 Tabelspoon Olive Oil
•	1 Tablespoon water

Directions:
1.	Bring 2 cups of vegetable broth, salt, cilantro stems, and oregano to a boil and add the rice.
2.	Cover and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to a low simmer and cook until the liquid is absorbed and rice is cooked.
3. Blend cilantro, garlic clove, lime juice, olive oil and water in a small food chopper.
4. Add cilantro mixture, diced jalapeno, and green onion to cooked rice.  Mix well and season with fresh ground black peper.   Serve on it's own or as a side with fish,  fried eggs, beans etc.
