Title:
Moist Oatmeal Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 C boiling water
1 C dry oats
1/2 C butter or oleo
2 eggs, beaten
1 C sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 C flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp nutmeg
3/4 tsp cinnamon
1 C raisins, optional
Topping: 1 C Brown sugar
1/2 C melted butter or oleo
5 tsp half & half or heavy cream
1/2 C chopped nuts, optional
1 C coconut, optional

Directions:
Mix boiling water and oatmeal together. Let stand 20 minutes.
In large bowl, blend together oleo, eggs, sugar, vanilla until smooth.  Pour in oatmeal mixture.  Blend together flour, baking soda, salt, nutmeg, and cinnamon.  Add to mixture.  Pour into prepared pan, bake at 350 for 50 - 55 minutes.
Blend together brown sugar, melted oleo, cream, nuts and coconut. Pour over warm cake.
