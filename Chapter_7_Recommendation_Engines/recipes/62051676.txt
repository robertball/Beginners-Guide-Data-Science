Title:
Cool Tuna Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of Cream of Celery soup
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tbsp. vinegar
4 cups cooked corkscrew pasta
2 cups cooked mixed vegetables OR peas
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 cans (about 6 oz each) tuna, drained and flaked
Salad greens
Tomato slices

Directions:
Mix soup, mayonnaise and vinegar.  Toss cooked pasta, vegetables OR peas, onion, tuna and soup mixture.
Serve on salad greens with tomato.

Submitter: 
Stefany Grider Baldwin
Description: 
Tuna on a fresh bed of greens.  A perfect lunch or light dinner dish!  Serve with crusty rolls as an accompaniment!
