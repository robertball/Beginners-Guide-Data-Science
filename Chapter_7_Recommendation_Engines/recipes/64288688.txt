Title:
Frozen Chocolate Peanut Butter Desert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup Chocolate Baking crumbs
2 Tbsp butter melted
1 pkg cream cheese (250 gram) softened
1/2 cup Peanut Butter
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups whipped cream topping thawed or homemade whip cream
1/3 cup chocolate chips melted

Directions:
Combine crumbs and margarine and press into bottom of foil lined 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Mix cream cheese, peanut butter sugar and vanilla with electric mixer until well blended. Gently fold in 1 1/2 cup whipped cream. Spoon 1/2 cup of the cream cheese mixture into a small bowl and fold in melted chocolate until blended, set aside. Spoon half of the remaining cream cheese mixture  over crumbs, top with chocolate mixture, cover with remaining cream cheese mixture then the 1/2 cup whipped cream and a dusting of chocolate crumbs for decoration
Freeze 4 hours or overnight until firm.  Invert onto plate. Remove foil then re-invert so that crumb layer is on bottom
