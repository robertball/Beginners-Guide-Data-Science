Title:
Crock  Pot Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Macaroni
2T. oil
1  13 oz.evaporated milk
1 1/2 cup reg. milk
1 teap. salt
4 oz.velveeta cheese
8 oz. sherdded cheese

Directions:
Boil macaroni and add the oil so it won't stick together. Put in crockpot and add the rest of ingredients.
Cook on low for about 3 hours.
