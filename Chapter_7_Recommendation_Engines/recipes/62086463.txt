Title:
Fresh Fried Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds of fresh Salmon
salt
pepper
1 cup flour
oil
1 bay leaf
1 lime cut in half

Directions:
Heat enough oil in skillet to cover bottom. Slice Salmon into half inch slices or chunks. Season both sides of  salmon with salt and pepper and roll in flour. Place bay leaf and salmon in skillet. Brown fish on both sides about 5 minutes each depends on thickness. Take fish out of the skillet and sprinkle with lime juice.

Submitter: 
SUDEMERS
Description: 
Living in Alaska my parents have tried many recipes and this one stands out to me. Easy to prepare and without the strong salmon flavor.
