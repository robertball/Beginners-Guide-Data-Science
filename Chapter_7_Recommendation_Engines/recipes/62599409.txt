Dirty Icing - Crumb Coat Recipe

4 Cups of Confectioners sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and ice cake very thin layer to contain the cake crumbs.  Allow drying time..once the icing is hardened you can then cover with your favorite icing

Submitter: 
Angel
Description: 
This is a icing I make to cover cake to keep in the crumbs or filing between layer.  Coating the cake in dirty ice eliminate crumbs from getting into your final coat of icing.  You can also use this for piping and decorating cake.
