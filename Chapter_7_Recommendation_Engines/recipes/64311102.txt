Title:
Joann's Mean Meatloaf Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Beaten Eggs
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs with seasoning(preferably italian)
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1.5 pounds of meat loaf mix (bef, pork and veal)
For Sauce:
1/4 cup catsup
2 tablespoon brown sugar
1 teaspoon dry mustard

Directions:
This very easy.  Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine eggs and mlk.  Stir in crumbs and onion, .  Add meatloaf mix and mix well.  Pat mixture into pan and bake for 1 and 1/4 hours (uncovered).  Spoon off excess fat.
For topping combine catsup, brown sugar and dry mustard; spread over meat loaf.  Return to oven 10 to 15 minutes.
This recipe make 2 loafs.
