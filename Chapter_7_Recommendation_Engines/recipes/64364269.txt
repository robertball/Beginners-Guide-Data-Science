Title:
Liat's Chicken Stirfry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2156897.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
1/2 cup brown sugar
4 Tbsp. soy sauce
3 Tbsp. lemon juice
4 Tbsp. fish sauce
1 Tbsp. grated ginger
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
2 teaspoons garlic
1 red bell pepper
1 yellow bell pepper
1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
1 Tbsp. corn starch to thicken sauce (optional)

Directions:
Combine sauce ingredients in a pot and bring to a boil.
Slice chicken breasts into strips.
Pour sauce, chicken and vegetables into a wok and cook or place in slow cooker and cook on low for 6 hours.
