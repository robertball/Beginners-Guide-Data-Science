Title:
Double Chocolate Brownie Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups butter flavor Crisco
2/3 cup light brown sugar
2 eggs
2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups of walnut or pecan pieces
1 cup sugar
1 tbsp. vanilla
2 1/4 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
3 tbsp. milk
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cream together Crisco, sugars and vanilla.
Add eggs one at a time.
Combine dry ingredients and add to creamed mixture alternately with the milk.
Stir in nuts and chocolate.
Drop 2 tbsp. full onto ungreased cooked sheet and bake for 9-11 minutes.
