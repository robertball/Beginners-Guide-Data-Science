Title:
Zesty Microwave Bread and Butter Pickles II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/326634.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large cucumber, sliced
1 teaspoon salt
1 Vidallia (or other sweet) onion, thinly sliced
1/4 Red bell pepper, seeded and thinly sliced
3-4 slices of banana peppers (mild to hot, according to your taste).
1/2 teaspoon mustard seed
2/3 cup white sugar
1/2 cup distilled white vinegar
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
1/4 teaspoon ground Turmeric
1/8 teaspoon ground Margoram
1/8 teaspoon dried Tarragon leaves, crushed

Directions:
In a medium microwave safe bowl, mix cucumber, salt, onion, bell pepper, jalapeño (OR jalapeño jelly), mustard seed, sugar, vinegar, celery seed, Turmeric, Marjoram, and Tarragon.
Microwave on high 8 to 9 minutes, stirring twice, until cucumbers are tender and onion is translucent.
Transfer to sterile containers. Seal and chill in the refrigerator for up to one month.

Submitter: 
Carol Hammond
Description: 
If you like your B&B pickles with a little zip, give this version a spin.  Experiment!  Maybe a couple of grates of fresh ginger root? (I haven't tried that yet, but you get the idea).  Make it your own!Modified from: Zesty Microwave Bread and Butter Pickles
