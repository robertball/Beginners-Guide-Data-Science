Title:
Toasted orzo chicken soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 32oz chicken stock
1pd bonless, skinless chicken breast or tenders
2Tbsp butter
3/4C orzo pasta
2Tbsp Evoo
1 Carrot, finely chopped
1 small zucchini, finely chopped
1/2 small red bell pepper
2 shallots or 1 small onion finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, finley chopped
salt and pepper
1C frozen peas
1/4C finely chopped parsley
2 tsp grated lemon peel

Directions:
In a medium pot, bring the chicken stock to a simmer. lower the heat and poach the chicken for 12 min. Heat a dutch oven or soup pot over medium heat. Add the butter to melt. Add the orzo and cook, stirring, until deeply toasted, 3 to 5 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
Add the evoo to the soup pan. Add the zucchini, carrot, bell pepper, shallots and garlic; season with salt and pepper. Cook stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are softened, 7 to 8 min.  Return the orzo to the pot.
Remove the chicken from the stock. Skim and discard the fat from the stock; transfer the stock to the soup pot. Chop, dice or shred the chicken and add to the soup with 2Cups of water. Bring the soup to a boil and cook until the orzo is just tender, 5 minutes.  Add the peas during the last minute of cooking. Turn off the heat; stir in the parsley and lemon peel. Serve immediately
