Title:
Big Ray's Alfredo from the Gods Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1119872.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup half & half
1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese (not pre-shredded)
3 Tbsp  of cream cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
2 tsp of garlic powder
1 Tbsp dried parsley
1/4 tsp salt
fresh ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat butter or margarine and half & half in a small saucepan over low heat until butter or margarine is melted. Stir in cheeses, parsley, garlic, salt and pepper; keep warm over low heat.
