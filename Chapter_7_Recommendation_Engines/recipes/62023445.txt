Title:
Chicken Wranglers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 whole, boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/4 lb deli ham
1/2 c milk
4 slices bacon
cheddar cheese
prepared bread crumbs

Directions:
Split chicken breasts and trim off excess fat.  Pound until thin enough to roll easily.  Cut a stip of cheese slightly shorter than the width of the chicken and about 1/2 inch thick.  Roll ham around cheese and place on pounded breast.  Roll chicken tightly around ham & cheese. Wrap the chicken roll with 1 strip of bacon.  Dip roll in milk and coat thoroughly with bread crumbs. Place in coated 9x13 baking dish.  Prepare remaining 3 breasts.  Bake at 350 for about 30 minutes.
