Title:
Linda's Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 large frozen cranberry/raspberry concentrated juice
1 large frozen lemonade
2 cans water
1/2 bag frozen raspberries or 1/2 pt. fresh
1 liter while wine
1 qt. Squirt or grapefruit flavored sparkling soda

Directions:
Have all the ingredients chilled before preparation.
Put all ingredients in an attractive punch bowl and serve.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Serve this ruby red punch in a glass bowl with a garnished ice ring for your next holiday gathering or cocktail party.
