Title:
Arroz Con Pollo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/466864.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cooked rotisserie chicken from any grocery store, meat pulled off
salt and pepper to taste
1 small onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 extra large tomatoes, diced (1 3/4 cups)
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup rice
1 tbl dry herbes de provence
2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions:
1.  Over medium-low heat, in a large, deep frying pan sauce onion and garlic until onions are soft, about 2 minutes.  Add bell peppers, stirring occasionally, until they soften, about 3 minutes.
2.  Add tomatoes, chicken broth, herbes de provence, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper and bring to a simmer.  Stir in the rice and simmer partially covered, over medium-low heat until the rice is done, about 20-25 minutes.  Add chicken about 10 minutes before finished.

Submitter:

Description: 
I have made this recipe many times and everyone that has tried it loves it!  It saves a lot of time buying a pre-cooked rotisserie chicken from the grocery store! and this is a great recipe to make variations on too!
