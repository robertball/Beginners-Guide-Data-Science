Title:
Tantelizing Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 flour tortillas
2 containers of whipped cream cheese
2 tbls hot sauce (not salsa)
1 cup diced green onion
1/2 fresh jalapeno or 1/4 cup jared jalapenos diced
1 small can diced green chillies
1 1/2 cup fresh sharp cheddar cheese shredded
18 thin slices of your favorite deli meat (Best with ham or Corned beef)

Directions:
Combined in Medium bowl cream cheese, hot sauce, green chillies, green onions, jalapenos mix well
Spread mixture over entire tortilla (top side only)
sprinkle layer of cheese over it. layer evenly with 3-4 peices of meat covering entire area of shell
Roll tightly
Chill if desired before slicing into 1/2 inch pieces and serve.
