Title:
Easy Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986091.jpg
Ingredients:
3 (14.5 ounce) cans Market Pantry™ chicken broth
1 cup Market Pantry™ salsa
1/2 package Market Pantry™ fajita chicken strips, thawed and cut in bite-size pieces
2 cups Market Pantry™ bite-size tortilla chips, coarsely crushed
1 cup Kraft four cheese blend

Directions:
Combine chicken broth, salsa and chicken in large saucepan; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes or until chicken is heated through.  Pour into individual bowls and top with tortilla chips and cheese.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
Add some south-of-the-border flavor to a delicious soup. It's a welcome change of pace and a great way to spice things up.
