Title:
Easy Carry Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz pkg rotini pasta
1 c. mayonnaise
1 tbsp vinegar
1 tbsp prepared mustard
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tbsp. celery seed
1 c. diced celery
1/2 c. diced red onions
1 10oz pkg frozen peas

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.
Combine all ingredients except peas in airtight container. Chill thoroughly.
Make a well in centre and pour frozen peas into well.
Cover and carry to picnic or table. Just before serving, stir to mix well. Frozen peas help keep salad safely cold while travelling.

Submitter: 
K. Lea
Description: 
A surprisingly good salad, with few ingredients. Don't skip the celery seed, it makes the salad. The subtle flavors change, if you leave overnight, the spices blend. An excellent pot luck or picnic salad. Kids approved.
