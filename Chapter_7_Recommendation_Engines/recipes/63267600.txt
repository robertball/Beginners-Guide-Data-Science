Title:
Cheesy vegetable and meat loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 carrots, diced
2 celery stalks, diced
1 onion, diced
2 mushrooms, diced *optional
1/4 c. Olive oil
1/4 c.& 1T minced parsley, fresh or dried
2 oz. shredded cheese
2c. Bread crumbs
2lb. ground beef
2 eggs, lightley beaten
1/2 t. minced thyme, fresh or dried
2t. salt
1t. pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Coat bottom of pan with oil.
Saute carrots, celery, and onions  for 8-10 min. Then add mushrooms and saute 1 or 2 minutes longer. Set aside to cool.
In a small bowl combine, oil, parsley, and cheese, mix.
In a large bowl combine bread crumbs, ground beef, eggs, thyme, salt, pepper, saute vegetables, and parsley/oil mixture; mix together with your hands.
Place in a casserole dish and form it into a loaf.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Aleina Cassian
Description: 
This is my families all time favorite meat loaf, especial my little brother! It serves well with mashed potatoes.
