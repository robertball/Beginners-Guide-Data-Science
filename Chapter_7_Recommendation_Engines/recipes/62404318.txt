Title:
Marzetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups uncooked whole grain egg noodles
1 lb. ground beef
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
2 cup shredded swiss cheese
16 oz tomato sauce
2 (8oz) cans mushroom stems and pieces
1 green pepper, diced
1/2 onion, diced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook noodles.
Brown ground beef and about half way done, add diced pepper and onion.  Season with garlic, salt and pepper.
In a 2 quart dish, layer cooked noodles and beef mixture.
Top with top with cheese, tomato sauce, and mushroom pieces.
Bake for 10 mins in oven or until cheese melts.
