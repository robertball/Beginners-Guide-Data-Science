Title:
Lavender Vanilla Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6463132.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C Butter
1 C Lavender Sugar
2 t Vanilla Extract
2 C All Purpose Flour
Pinch of Salt
*Lavender Sugar
1 C Sugar
2 T Finely Chopped Lavender

Directions:
Lavender Sugar:
Place the lavender in a food processor and run until lavender is in a powder
Add 1/2 cup sugar and pulse for 20-30 seconds
Pour into small bowl and add the rest of the sugar mixing well to combine well
Shortbread:
Preheat over to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
With a pastry blender cream the butter, half the lavender sugar, vanilla, flour and salt until crumbly
Transfer to two 9" round ungreased glass pie plates
Press dough down in each dish
Using a fork poke holes all round shortbread
Bake 25 minutes until pale golden
Cover with the remaining lavender sugar shaking to evenly spread it on top the shortbread.
Cut in wedges while still warm and let cool
Garnish plate of shortbread with a sprig of lavender
*Lavender Sugar
Combine ingredients in a bowl and store in a airtight jar overnight

Description: 
A light summery twist on shortbread by infusing the fragrance and taste of lavender
