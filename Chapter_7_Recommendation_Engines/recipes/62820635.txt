Title:
Best Ever Lemon Curd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/665841.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Sugar
1 Tlbs. finlely chopped lemon zest
2/3 Cups freshly lemon juice
8 Lrg. egg yolks
1/4 tsp. salt
1 1/4 sticks unsalted butter cut into 1/2 inch chunks

Directions:
In a medium saucepan off heat mix together sugar, zest, egg yolks. Wisk in lemon juice and salt.
Add butter and place over med. high heat. Cook wisking constantly until butter has melted, mixture is thick enough to coat the back of a spoon and small bubbles form around the edge of the pan. About 5 minutes. DO NOT BOIL.
Remove from heat while continuing to whisk. Pour curd into glass boil and lay plastic wrap on the surface of the curd and refrigerate to cool.

Submitter: 
Wayne
Description: 
This recipe featured on the Martha Stewart Show. Fantastic, Rich and very lemony. This curd can be used to fill cake, spread on toast, fill a donut. Add it to chicken stir fry.
