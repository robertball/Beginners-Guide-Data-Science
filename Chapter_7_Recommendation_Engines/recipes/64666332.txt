Title:
Simple Crockpot Beef Buffalo Ranch Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4571813.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag Rosina's frozen Italian Style Meatballs medium sized 26.0 oz (store bought or frozen)
1 bottle Frank’s Red Hot Wings Buffalo Sauce (12 oz.)
1 packet Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing dry mix (1 oz.)
Garnishing Options
Meatball Sauce
Blue Cheese Dressing
Parmesan cheese shredded
Baked Garlic Bread squares

Directions:
Combine Frank’s Red Hot Wings Buffalo Sauce and dry Ranch Dressing packet and mix well.
Add frozen meatballs to Crockpot, cover with sauce mixture, stir and mix together, then cover.
Heat on high for approximately 2 hours or on low for 4 hours,  stirring occasionally.
Let simmer on "warm"  setting once done
Garnish Option:  Serve in glass shooters layering with warmed meatball sauce then a layer of Blue Cheese Dressing. Skewer 2 meatballs and place in layered glass shooter.
Top with some shredded Parmesan cheese and sliced piece of baked garlic bread.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
Easy breezy spicy Beef Buffalo Meatballs for game day or any day! Serve right out of Crockpot or serve as individual shooters. Either way these meatballs are simple and just plain fantastic!
