Title:
hummingbird water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups tap water
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar

Directions:
put water in glass 8 cup pyrex mixing bowl
microwave 6 minutes
remove from microwave
add sugar-stir until dissolved
let cool
put in 3 clean hummingbird feeders

Submitter:

Description: 
feed the hummers healthy- no dye- no chemicals
