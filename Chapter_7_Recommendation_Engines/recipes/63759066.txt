Title:
Parmesan crusted Swai fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1042591.jpg
Ingredients:
3 swai fish filets (frozen) thawed
3 Tbls corn starch
2 Tbls flour
1 egg, beaten
1/4 C. ground pecans
1/4 C. Bell Stuffin' mix, pulsed in blender
2 Tbls. Parmesan
2 Tbls coconut oil

Directions:
1. Place flour/cornstarch mix in a large plate,stir with fork
2. Beat egg in another large plate
3. Place pecans/stuffin mix and Parmesan in another plate, stir with fork
Set aside plates.  Preheat electric skillet to 375 degrees
Pat dry flilets with paper towels. Add coconut oil to pan.  place fillet one at a time first in flour/cornstarch plate, shake then dredge in egg, then 3rd in  pecans/stuffin mix.  Place gently in pan for 5-7 minutes on each side depending on thickness of fillet.  Serve with lemon.

Submitter: 
rploker
Description: 
The coconut oil gave it it great light taste!  My husband said it was the best fish!  You can use tilapia too, just cut down the cooking time as it's a much smaller  fillet.  The swai fillets was much bigger and 2 fed us both quite well!
