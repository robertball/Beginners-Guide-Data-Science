Title:
Broccoli Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
2 packages frozen chopped broccoli
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup cream of mushroom soup
5 oz  slivered almonds
1 cup Durkee Onion Rings

Directions:
Thaw broccoli, drain.
Beat eggs, add cheese and mayo; mix. Add soup; mix. Mix in broccoli and let stand for 15 minutes. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Add almonds and onion rings to the top of the dish 15 minutes before it comes out of the oven.
