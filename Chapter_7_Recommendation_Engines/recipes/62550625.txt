Title:
Grandpa's Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1 teas. baking powder
1/2 teas. soda
1/2 teas. salt
1 cup soft butter or oleo
1 1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1 teas. vanilla

Directions:
Sift flour with baking powder, baking soda and salt. (Set aside.
Cream sugar, butter, eggs until light and fluffy.
At low speed beat in the sour cream and vanilla until smooth.
Gradually beat in flour mixture until well combined (chill for 1 hour.)
Bake at 375 degrees. Lightly grease cookie sheet.
Drop batter by T. --- 2 inches apart.
Sprinkle top with mixture of 1/2 c. sugar and 1 T. cinnamon.
Bake 10 - 12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Marjorie Taylor
Description: 
My father died on July 4th, 1998 and this was his favorite cookie. It is also my husband's favorite. It is a very easy and simple recipe.
