Title:
Date Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz package date pieces
1 stick margarine
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup chopped pecans
3 cups crisped rice cereal
Powdered sugar

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine dates, margarine, and sugar. Heat on low until sugar dissolves. Remove from heat.
Add vanilla, pecans, and cereal. Stir to blend very well.
Grease hands with margarine. Using hands, roll mixture into 1" balls.
Roll balls in powdered sugar. Cool thoroughly before storing.

Submitter: 
Melaniemg2000
Description: 
A holiday tradition, my mother made these date balls every year at Christmas time.
