Title:
Peach Cake With Glaze Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
PEACH CAKE
2 eggs
2 c, self rising flour
2 c, sugar
1  tbsp, cinnamon
1 tsp , soda
1 large  can  peaches drained  and chopped
1 stick butter  melted
ICING :
1 stick butter
1 small can  evaporated  milk
1 TSP  vanilla
1/2 cup  sugar

Directions:
mix  above cake ingredients  together and   bake  at  350 degrees  from  30  to  45  minutes.
Poke holes  in  cake  let  cool completely
Icing:
Heat in a saucepan  till it  comes  to a boil
cook  for 5 minutes .
Pour over cooled cake.
