Title:
Sweet & Spicy Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb sweet italian sausage (I prefer Johnsonville)
1 1/4 vadalia onions, chopped
28 oz Sauce of Eden (see my recipe)
1/2 lb whole wheat penne pasta
1 Tbs & 1Tsp EVOO (Extra Virgin Olive Oil)
freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat EVOO in 12 inch skillet over medium-high heat. Add sausage and cook until well browned. Add onions shortly after sausage and allow to saute.
Add Sauce of Eden and reduce heat to low. Cover and cook for 20 minutes or until sausage is fully cooked.
Serve over bed of penne and top with freshly grated parmesan to liking.

Submitter: 
Chef Eden
Description: 
This sweet and spicy dish is not only healthy but incredibly delicious. With just the right balance of sweet and heat, your sure fall in love with this Italian dish!
