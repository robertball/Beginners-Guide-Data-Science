Title:
Waldorf Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced apples
1 cup celery, sliced
1 cup seedless grapes
1/2 cup broken walnuts
1 cup Miracle Whip
1 Tablespoon honey

Directions:
Combine apple, celery, grapes, and nuts.
Blend together the Miracle Whip and honey.  Mix with the above ingredients
Refrigerate until serving

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
A true Holiday salad.  A family tradition.
