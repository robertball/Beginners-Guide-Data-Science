Title:
Amish Pumpkin Whoopie Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients: Cakes/Filling
Cakes: 2 egg yolks
2 c. Brown sugar
1 c. Vegetable oil
1 tsp each, cloves, cinnamon, ginger
Baking powder, baking soda, vanilla
2 c. Cooked pumpkin
3 c. Flour
Filling: 5 Tbsp flour
1 c. Milk
1 c. Granulated sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 sticks oleo (must be margarine, cannot substitute butter)

Directions:
Cook flour and milk over low heat until thickened. Set aside to cool.
You may do before mixing and baking pies.
Combine (filling) sugar, vanilla, margarine, flour in electric mixer, beating for 10 minutes until fluffy. (This is very good on chocolate cake, red velvet cake, whoopie pies, or carrot cake).
Cakes: Beat egg, brown sugar, oil until smooth. Combine remaining ingredients.
Bake 350* pre-heated oven for 12 min
