Title:
Slow Cooker Cabbage Roll Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2-2 pounds ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
1-2 cloves chopped garlic
1 28 ounce can diced tomatoes
3 pounds chopped cabbage
1/2 cup cooked brown rice or 1 cup uncooked white rice
1 teaspoon salt
2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown beef in oil over medium high heat until redness is gone. Drain off fat.
In a large slow cooker combine the onion, garlic, diced tomatoes, cabbage, rice and salt. Add meat and mix all together. Pour broth over meat mixture. Cook on high for 6 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
Casserole combines chopped cabbage with ground beef, diced tomatoes, onions, garlic and rice. This recipe is quick and easy compared to traditional cabbage rolls.Modified from: Cabbage Roll Casserole
