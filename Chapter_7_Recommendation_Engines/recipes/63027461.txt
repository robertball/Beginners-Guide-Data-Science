Title:
Instant Mashed Potato Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/758975.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. cold water
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg, beaten
3/4 c. instant mashed potatoes
Fresh chives (optional)
Freshly ground pepper (optional)
Cayenne, to taste (optional)
1 Tbs oil
1 Tbs butter

Directions:
Mix cold water and egg with a whisk.
Add instant mashed potatoes and mix in with the whisk.
Add optional ingredients.
Melt butter with oil in pan on medium heat.
Divide mixture into four parts.  Using wet hands, shape each part into a pancake shape and place in pan with butter/oil.  Cook until EXTRA crispy (about 10 mins).  Turn over, flatten slightly and cook for an additional 6-8 mins.
