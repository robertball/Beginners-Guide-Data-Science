Title:
Rosemary Ginger Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Honey
2 cups water
10 limes
2 cups Tequilla (repesado)
2 cups Grand Marnie
2 ounces chopped ginger
4 sprigs rosemary
1 cup orange juice

Directions:
Bring honey and water to boil with ginger and rosemary.  Turn heat off and let stand for 30 min.  Strain into pitcher discarding rosemary and ginger.  Add remaining ingredients and stir.  Serve over ice or add to blender with ice.
