Title:
Peaches 'n' Creme S'mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3566063.jpg
Ingredients:
2 HONEY MAID Honey Grahams, broken in half
4 tablespoons marshmallow creme
1 fresh peach, pitted and thinly sliced

Directions:
Top each of 2 cracker halves with 2 tablespoons marshmallow creme. Top with sliced peaches and remaining cracker. Gently press. Serve immediately.
