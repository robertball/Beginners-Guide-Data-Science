Title:
Chewy Cranberry Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
2 1/2 cups old fashioned oats
8 ounces unsalted butter slightly softened
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
2 large eggs, beaten lightly
1 tablespoon honey
2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
6 ounces dried cranberries (1 1/3 cups)
5 ounces chopped pecans  (1 cup)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Line pans with parchment
Mix dry ingredient together, then stir in the oats
Beat the butter and both sugars together until light and fluffy
Add eggs, scrape the bowl, add honey and vanilla
Add the flour mixture in two additions
Stir in cranberries and pecans
Bake 10 minutes
