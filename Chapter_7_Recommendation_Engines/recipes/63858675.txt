Title:
5 ALARM (OR LESS) CHILI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. ground beef
8 oz. tomato sauce
16 oz. water
6 tbsp. ground chili pepper
1 1/2 tsp. ground cumin
1 1/2 tsp. ground oregano
1 1/2 tsp. salt (optional)
1 1/2 tsp. minced onion
1 1/2 tsp. minced garlic
1 1/2 tsp. paprika
3/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 1/2 tsp. cornstarch
1 can petite diced tomatoes
1# dried kidney beans
1 medium chopped onion
1/3 c. baking soda

Directions:
Rinse beans.
Put beans in a container and cover with water.
Add baking soda and refrigerate overnight.
Next day-
Drain and rinse beans.
Place in pot and cover with water.
Simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
While beans simmer-
Brown meat and onion in large saucepan.
Add tomato sauce and water, and tomatoes.
Mix well.
Stir in all spices.
Drain and rinse beans and add to Chili.
In a small bowl, mix cornstarch with some warm water to make a paste.
Add paste to chili and stir.
Allow chili to simmer for 45 minutes.
Stir occasionally.
1/4 teaspoon = mild; 3/4 teaspoon = 1 alarm, spicy; 1 1/2 teaspoon = 2 alarms, hot; 2 teaspoons = 3 alarms, very hot; 3 teaspoons = 4 alarms, very, very hot.
