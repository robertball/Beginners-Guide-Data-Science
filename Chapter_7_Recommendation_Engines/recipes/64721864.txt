Title:
Dick's Famous Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6561868.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lbs Potatoes
12 Eggs, boiled
1 10 Ounce Jar Pickle Smack
2 C Mayonnaise
2 T Garlic Powder
1 T Lawry's Seasoning Salt
1 T Chicken McKays Seasoning

Directions:
Boil potatoes and eggs.  While still warm cut potatoes in bowl with knife and add pickle smack mixing together.  Slice eggs and add the rest of ingredients.  Mix together.
Adjust salt to taste.
