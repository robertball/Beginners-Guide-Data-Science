Title:
Tropical BBQ Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5091520.jpg
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
2 lbs Ground Beef
1/2 Cup Bread Crumbs
2 Eggs
1 Fresh or 1 Cup Frozen Mango Chunks
1 Tbsp Adobo Seasoning
1 Pack Lipton Golden Onion Soup Mix
1 Tsp Ground Thyme
1 Tsp Ground Ginger
1/2 Tsp Ground Black Pepper
1 Tsp Sea Salt
3 Fresh Garlic Cloves
Tropical BBQ Sauce:
1 Fresh or 1 Cup Frozen Mango Chunks
1 Cup of  Fresh or Canned Pineapple Chunks
1 Bottle Basic BBQ Sauce
1 Tbsp of Soy Sauce
1/2 Cup White Granulated Sugar
1 Tbsp Yellow Mustard
1 Tsp Cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Puree Mango for Meatballs
Place Ground Beef in large mixing bowl
Add all dry ingredients to bowl
Add crushed garlic cloves
Mix all of the above ingredients thoroughly
Add beaten eggs
Add Pureed Mango
Mix again
Begin shaping 1 1/2 inch meatballs
Place meatballs in baking pan
Place  pan in preheated oven
Bake for approximately 45 minutes
BBQ Sauce:
In a medium mixing bowl add:
Mango Chunks
Pineapple Chunks
BBQ Sauce
Soy Sauce
Mustard
Cinnamon
Sugar
Mix all ingredients and set to the side
Once meatballs are fully cooked:
Remove from the oven and drain excess oil/grease
Pour previously mixed sauce over the meatballs
Place Meatballs back in the oven
Bake at 400 degrees for 20 mins
Remove pan
Enjoy!
