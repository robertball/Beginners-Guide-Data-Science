Title:
Roasted Chicken Topped with Red Lentils & Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2-3 pound roasting chicken. Ideally antibiotic free & free range.
1 pint heirloom or cherry tomatoes
1 yellow onion
Â¼ cup red lentils (uncooked)
2 tbs curry power
Olive Oil
Balsamic Vinegar

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350
Rinse the chicken, remove anything inside the belly and place it in a roasting pan. Drizzle with olive oil & balsamic vinegar
Quarter the onion and slice the quarters into strips. Place on and around the chicken.
Place the tomatoes around the chicken
Sprinkle the dry lentils on top of the chicken and add the curry powder.
Place the chicken in the oven and cook for 1.5 hours or until there is no pink when cutting into the breast.
Remove and enjoy!
Pairs well with sides of roasted brussels sprouts and sweet potato.
For a lighter meal, try the chicken on a bed of fresh arugula.
