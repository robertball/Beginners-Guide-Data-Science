Title:
Super Easy Banana "Eggnog" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dash Cloves
1/2 cup Almond Milk
1 Frozen Banana
1/4 tsp Cinnamon
Dash Nutmeg

Directions:
Combine almond milk, banana, a light dash of nutmeg (about 1/8 tsp) and a tiny little bit of cloves in a blender and blend until smooth and creamy. Garnish with ground cinnamon and serve.

Submitter: 
fordmarie
Description: 
Who knew bananas could be so cunning? I like to serve this beverage with a drizzle of peanut butter on top..oh goodness so delicious.
