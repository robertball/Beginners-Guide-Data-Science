Title:
Food Processor Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 c all purpose flour
2 sticks (1 cup) unsalted butter
1 t salt
6-8 T ice water

Directions:
Cube the butter and put in the refrigerator until you are ready for it.  In a food processor, combine flour and salt.  Add butter cubes and pulse only until combined and resembles crumbs.  Add ice water 1 T at a time just until dough comes together.  Place dough immediately into two plastic bags, form into disks and place in refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.  Roll out on floured board and place in pie plate and refrigerate again until ready for pie preparation.

Submitter: 
It's A New Day
Description: 
Easy pie crust.  It is important to refrigerate after mixing and after forming.
