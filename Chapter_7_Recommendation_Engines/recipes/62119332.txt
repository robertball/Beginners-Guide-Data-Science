Title:
Caramel Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cp butter
1 1/2 cps packed brown sugar
3/4 cp light corn syrup
1 can sweetened condensed milk (14 oz)
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp cinnamon
pinch salt

Directions:
Combine butter, sugar, corn syrup, and milk in a saucepan.
Heat over medium heat, stirring to dissolve sugar, about 5 minutes.
Stir in vanilla, cinnamon, and salt.
Pour into a dipping bowl.
Serve warm with apple slices.

Submitter: 
Carol Potts
Description: 
This is a delicious warm caramel dip for apples.  I have also put it over ice cream.  I even considered just drinking it from a cup!
