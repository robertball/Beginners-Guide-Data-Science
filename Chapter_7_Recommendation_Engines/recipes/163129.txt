Title:
Ham Bagels With Honey Mustard Cream Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/468198.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup whipped cream cheese
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon honey
4 bagels, split
8 slices deli-style ham

Directions:
Mix the cream cheese, mustard, and honey in a small bowl.
Spread cream cheese mixture over cut sides of 4 bagel halves.  Top evenly with ham.  Sandwich with the remaining bagel halves.

Submitter: 
Ray
Description: 
This is a great twist on a sandwich; the honey blends well with everything and gives it a bit of a sweet taste.
