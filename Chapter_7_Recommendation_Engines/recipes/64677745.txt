Title:
Big Ray's Garlic-Rosemary White Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4772292.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp olive oil, divided
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp rosemary
1 15 oz can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp fresh chopped rosemary
pinch red pepper flakes

Directions:
Heat olive oil in medium skillet.  Add garlic and rosemary; cook for 1 minute, stirring, then remove from heat.  Add beans, lemon juice and salt.  use back of fork to mash some of the beans, leaving others whole.  Place in bowl and garnish with remaining olive oil, rosemary and red pepper.  Serve with crackers, pita chips or veggies.
