Title:
Mango Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4481923.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 mango, diced and frozen
3/4 cup vanilla yogurt
1 cup orange juice
1/2 banana

Directions:
Peel, dice and freeze mango.
Chop banana into small pieces.
Pour mango slices, yogurt, orange juice and banana into blender.
Blend until smooth.
Enjoy!
