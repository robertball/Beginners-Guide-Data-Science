Title:
Iced Pfeffernusse (Peppernut) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c + 2 tbsp flour
2 sp baking powder
1/2 c potatoe starch (or corn starch)
2 eggs
1 1/4c sugar
1 tbsp rum
1/2 lemon peel (freshley ground)
1/4 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp cardamom
1/4 cloves
1/4 mace
1/8-1/4 white pepper (finely ground fresh)
1 tsp cinnamon
1/3 c almonds (finely ground)
1/3 c citron (very finely diced)
ICING:
1 c powder sugar
2-3 tbsp hot water
or
2 c powdered sugar
sturdy paper sack

Directions:
Sift flour, baking powder, potatoe starch spices; set aside.
Beat eggs until foamy, add sugar and beat untill fluffy and light. Add rum, sifted flour, fruits and nuts. combine until it makes a thick dough. Let stand for an hour. .
Preheat oven to 400 degree, and line baking sheet with parchment paper.
Pinch off 1" of dough, with the fingers form into a ball and place on the baking sheet 2" apart.
Put into oven and turn temp down to 350 degrees, baking for 10-15 min lightly brown on the bottom, and firm to the touch.
Cool slightly on the wire rack (15 min).
Mix 1 c powdered sugar and water untill it will make a thick coat and drench cookies. After letting dry completly, pack in airtight conatiner. will keep for 1 week.
Put powdered sugar in sturdy sack and put a few cookies and shake until coated.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
This German version of the traditional pepper nut make a semi soft spicey nuggets that have been a family favorite at christmastime.
