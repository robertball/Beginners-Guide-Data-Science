Title:
Seared Sea Scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Beurre Blanc:
2 shallots, finely diced
1 lemon, juiced
2 cups white wine
2 fresh thyme springs
3 peppercorns
1 bay leaf
4 ounces roasted garlic
2 ounces heavy cream
16 ounces butter, cubed and cold
Kosher salt/pepper
Mushrooms:
1/2 cup canola oil
24 ounces mixed mushrooms
4 tablespoons minced garlic
4 tablespoons minced shallots
4 tablespoons mixed herbs (parsley, tarragon, chives, basil)
Kosher salt/pepper
Scallops
12 jumbo sea scallops
1 cups canola oil, divided
2 teaspoons Kosher salt
2 teaspoons pepper

Directions:
Beurre Blanc:
Combine shallots, lemon juice, wine, thyme, peppercorns, bay leaf and roasted garlic
Bring to a boil and reduce until almost dry; 1/8 cup
Reduce heat to low; add cream
Begin adding butter, 1 cube at a time, whisking continuously
Strain through a fine straineer
Cover and keep warm
Mushrooms:
Place a saute pan over high heat and add enough canola to cover bottom
Allow pan to heat until very hot
Add mushrooms and begin to saute, until half cooked
Add garlic and shallots
When cooked through, add the herbs and season with salt/pepper
Scallops:
Toss lightly with canola oil
Season each scallop evenly with salt and pepper
Cover bottom of large pan with oil, Saute scallots
Brown on each side; put into a hot pan to finish cooking
Serve over mushrooms; drizzle with beurre blanc
