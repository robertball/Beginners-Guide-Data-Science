Title:
Fresh Sweet & Sour Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds fresh green beans, trimmed
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1/4 cup sugar
1 ice cube
1 cup thinly sliced shallots
2 tablespoons canola oil
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
fit a large pot with a steamer basket; add 1 to 2 inches water and bring to a boil, add green beans, cover and steam until tender-crisp.  (about 5-8 minutes)  Drain.
Meanwhile heat vinegar and sugar in small saucepan over medium heat, stirring until sugar dissolves.  (1-2 minutes)  Remove from the heat.  Stir in ice cube until it dissolves.  Whisk in shallots, oil and salt.
Transfer the drained green beans to a large bowl, pour the dressing over beans and toss to coat.  Serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
A simple green bean salad-type dish when steaming the green beans to a tender-crisp.
