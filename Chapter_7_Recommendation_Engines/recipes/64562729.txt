Title:
Flavored Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
3 tbsp. preserves or other flavored additions - use your imagination!
Suggestions: jams to serve on pancakes, loaves or muffins; spicy chutneys or sauces for biscuits, corn bread or buns

Directions:
Mix butter & flavored addition(s).
Serve with desired base.
Store leftover in fridge.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This concept seems simple enough, but how often do we consider raising humble butter to a new level?! The possibilities are endless ;)
