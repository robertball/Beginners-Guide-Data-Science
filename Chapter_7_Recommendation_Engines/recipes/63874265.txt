Title:
Shelley's Yummy Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1074858.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick softened butter
1 1/2 cup flour
2 eggs
1-2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
3/4 tsp sea salt
1 cup raw sugar
2 tsp baking powder
2 1/2 mashed bananas (overripe)
1 tsp vanilla
3 tbsp milk or cream
1/2 cup sour cream
Frosting:
1/2 stick butter
1 1/2 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
pinch sea salt
milk as needed to thin

Directions:
Sift together dry ingredients, set aside.
Beat butter and sugar well.
Add eggs, vanilla, and milk; mix well on slow speed or by hand.
Fold in mashed bananas.
Mix in dry ingredients.
Fold in sour creme.
Pour into 1 greased loaf pan and bake at 325 degrees for 1-1 1/2 hours.
Frosting:
Melt butter, add powdered sugar, vanilla, and sea salt.
Add milk a tbsp at a time until desired consistency. Frosting should be thin enough to drizzle over bread.
