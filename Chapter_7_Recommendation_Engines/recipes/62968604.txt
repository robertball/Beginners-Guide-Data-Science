Title:
Greek Egg Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg whites
3/4 cup chopped red onions
1/4 cup diced tomato
2 tablespoons reduced-fat feta cheese
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk together all ingredients. Pour into a small frying pan that has been coated with vegetable cooking spray. Cook over medium-low heat until eggs are cooked through.
