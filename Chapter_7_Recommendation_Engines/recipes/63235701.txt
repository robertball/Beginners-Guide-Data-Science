Title:
Blackberries & Dumplins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp. sea salt
3 Tb. butter
2 Tb. hot water
1 Tb. corn starch
2 Tb. cold water
1/3 cup white sugar
1/2 Tb. lemon juice
3 cups fresh blackberries

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine first 5 ingredients & mix. With a fork or pastry blender, work in butter till mix is crumbly. Add hot water & mix till all blended into a soft dough.
In a glass or metal bowl, dissolve corn starch in cold water & add berries, 1/3 cup sugar & lemon juice. Stir well then pour into an 8 inch cast iron skillet over medium heat. Stir often & bring to a boil. Drop dough by small spoonfuls into boiling syrup & berries. Move skillet to a foil-lined cookie sheet and place in a preheated 400 degree oven for 20 to 25 minutes till dumplins are golden. Allow to stand at least 15 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
ccofer
Description: 
My husband's grandmother used to make this when he was a boy. I've reworked the recipe to a manageable amount since a little of this sweet treat goes a long way.  Great with ice cream.
