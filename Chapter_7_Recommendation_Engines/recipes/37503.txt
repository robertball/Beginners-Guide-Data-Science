Title:
Grilled Turkey Club Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 slices bread
2 KRAFT Singles
1 tomato slice
4 slices LOUIS RICH Oven Roasted Turkey Breast
2 slices OSCAR MAYER Center Cut Bacon , crisply cooked
2 tablespoons KRAFT Mayo Real Mayonnaise

Directions:
Top 1 bread slice with Singles, tomato, turkey, bacon and second bread slice.
Spread outside of sandwich with mayo.
Cook in skillet on medium heat until lightly browned on both sides.
