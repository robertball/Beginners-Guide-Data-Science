Title:
Dilled Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large heads broccoli cut into florets
1 large cauliflower, cut into florets
3-4 carrots, cut in ¼” rounds
Dressing
1/3 cup olive oil		2-3 Tbsp. white wine vinegar
1 Tbsp. Dijon mustard	1 Tbsp. sugar
1 ½ tsp. Dried dill		¾ tsp. salt
¾ tsp curry or paprika
In a large pot bring 4 cups of water to a boil.  Add all veggies and cook until tender-crisp (2-3 minutes).  Drain and immediately rinse under cold water.  Whisk together dressing ingredients.  Cover and set-aside until serving time.  Pour over vegetables and mix gently to coat.
