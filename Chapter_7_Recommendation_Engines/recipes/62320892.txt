Title:
Pan Fried Oysters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (eight ounce)jars or cans of oysters
2 eggs beaten
1 cup of bread crumbs
1 tablespoon of Old Bay seasoning
1 tablespoon of cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon of onion powder
1 tablespoon of garlic powder
1 tablespoon of ground black pepper
3 tablespoons of butter

Directions:
Empty the oysters into a collander and let them drain.
Mix together bread crumbs,Old Bay seasoning, cayenne pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, and black pepper in a small bowl and set aside.
In a separate bowl beat the eggs and set aside. After the oysters have drained, dip each one into the eggs then place into the bread crumb mixture and coat all sides of the oyster.  Repeat until all oysters are fully coated.
In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat and place oysters in pan leaving a little room between each one. Fry on each side for approximately four mimutes until golden brown on each side.
