Title:
Lemon Pepper Fiber Crusted Fresh Flounder Fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/787241.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Fiber One cereal
1 cup flour
1 egg
1 T milk
2 tsp. lemon pepper spice
4 (3-4 oz.) fresh flounder fillets
2 cups vegetable oil
4 lemon wedges
tartar sauce (optional)

Directions:
Using a blender, pulverize the Fiber One cereal.  Add 2 tsp. of lemon pepper and set aside.
Heat oil to 350 degrees in a deep skillet.
Whisk egg and milk together in a medium bowl and set aside.
Place 1 cup of flour in a separate medium bowl.
Coat each flounder fillet in flour, then dip in egg mixture, then dredge with Fiber One mixture.
Deep fry in hot oil for 3 minutes, turning once.
Drain on paper towels.
Serve with a wedge of lemon and tartar sauce.
