Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2450665.jpg
Ingredients:
10 Strawberry fruit roll ups
2 grape fruit roll ups
5 shoe string licorice
4 cups miniature chocolate marshmallows
4 cups crisp rice cereal
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 teaspoons butter

Directions:
Step one...make the chocolate rice crispy treats
Step two...roll them into a standard marker shape and size
Step three... roll said rice crispy sticks with the red fruit roll ups
Step four... Insert a 3 in. piece of shoe string licorice in one end of each delicious chocolate candy dinomite.
Step five...cut the grape fruit roll up in 1 in. strips and wrap the bundle of dinomite bites to resemble a group of dynamite
