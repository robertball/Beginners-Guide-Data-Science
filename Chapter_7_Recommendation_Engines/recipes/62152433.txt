Title:
Gluten Free Banana Bread with Chocolate Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of butter
1/2 cup of Brown Sugar
1/2 cup of White Sugar
2 Eggs
2 Large or 3 Medium Size ripe Mashed Bananas
1 tsp of Vanilla Extract
1 tbs of Rum (optional)
2 Cups of Gluten Free Self Raising Flour
1/2 Cup of Dark Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Cream Butter and Sugars
Add Eggs and mix
Add Bananas, Vanilla & Rum until combined.
Add Flour and mix well, then combine chocolate chips into batter.
Pour into a greased loaf pan.
Bake 45-50 minutes at 180 degrees.
Allow to cool before removing from pan.
Suitable to freeze.

Submitter: 
CZARDAS
Description: 
This is an easy to make banana bread which is suitable for anyone that has to eliminate Gluten from there diets.  No longer do you have to miss out.
