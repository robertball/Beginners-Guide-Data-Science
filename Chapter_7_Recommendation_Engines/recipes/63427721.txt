Title:
Dried Beef And Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/927905.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Oz Of Dried Beef (Riped into Pieces)
8 Cups Of Sliced Potatoes
5 Tablespoons Of Butter
5 Tablespoons Of Flour
2 Cups Of Milk
8 Oz Of Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
To Create The White Sauce (Bechamel) Melt Butter In A Small Pan
When The Butter Is Melted Add The Flour, And Mix The Flour Into The Butter.
Add The Milk To The Butter And Flour Mixture, And Mix Together.
Bring The Mixture Almost To A Boil Constantly Mixing As You Heat It, Then Remove From The heat.
In A Casserole Pan Add White Sauce, Potatoes, Cheese, And Meat in Layers, Top With Cheddar Cheese.
Place Casserole Dish (Covered) In A Preheated Oven At 400F For 60 Mins To 75 Mins.
Remove From Oven And Allow To Cool For 15 Mins.

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
One Of My Wife's Creations About 1980, When Dried Beef Was A Staple In A Military Home. This Is A Very Easy Meal To Make And Is Delicious. There is No Salt Added To This Because The Salt In The Beef Is All You Need To Properly Season The Potatoes.
