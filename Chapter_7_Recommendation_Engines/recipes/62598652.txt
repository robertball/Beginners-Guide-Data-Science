Title:
NANA'S TURKEY MEATLOAF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (lb.) ground turkey
1 (6 oz.) can tomato sauce
1 small onion (chopped or diced)
1/4 cup yellow pepper
1/4 cup red pepper
1/4 cup green pepper
1 egg
8 crackers (crushed)
Dash of garlic powder
Salt and ground black pepper to taste
3 (tbs.) olive oil
1/2 cup spaghetti sauce
1/2 cup mozarella cheese (shredded)

Directions:
Preheat oven 400 degrees. In a sauce pan, heat the olive oil and saute onion, yellow pepper, red pepper and green pepper. In a mixing bowl place ground turkey, egg, crackers and tomato sauce, mix well. Add garlic powder, salt and pepper to taste. Drain sauteed ingredients and add them to mixing bowl. Mix well. Place meatloaf mixture into a baking dish and into preheated oven for 40 minutes. Remove meatloaf and add spaghetti sauce to the top, sprinkle with shredded mozarella cheese. Place meatloaf back into the oven for additional 5 minutes or until cheese has melted. Remove from the oven allow 5 minutes, then serve.
