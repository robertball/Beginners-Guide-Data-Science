Title:
Creamy Cajun Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 large eggs
1 (3-oz.) package cream cheese, room temperature
2-3 tablespoons Miracle Whip
1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
1/4 teaspoon hot sauce
Salt and pepper to taste
Cajun seasoning

Directions:
Place eggs in a medium-size pan and cover with cool water over high heat.
When water comes to a boil, cover with a tight-fitting lid and reduce heat to lowest setting.
Allow to cook for 18 minutes.
Remove from heat and peel under cool running water; allow to cool slightly.
Cut the cooked eggs in half lengthwise and remove cooked egg yolks from egg whites.
7. In a small bowl, using an electric mixer, combine egg yolks, cream cheese, Miracle Whip, sweet pickle relish, hot sauce and salt and pepper to taste until smooth.
Using a teaspoon, refill egg white with the yolk mixture.
Lightly sprinkle with Cajun seasoning for garnish and chill until serving time.

Submitter: 
Melissa Dommert
Description: 
The secret ingredient in these creamy eggs is the cream cheese. C'est bon meaning It's so good!
