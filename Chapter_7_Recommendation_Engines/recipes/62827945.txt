Title:
Tijuana Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb turkey breast, cut into 3/4 inch cubes
1 small green bell pepper, finely chopped
1 small onion, minced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 T brown sugar
1 T chili powder
2 T cornmeal
2 T unsweetened cocoa powder
1 t cumin
1 t oregano
1/2 t salt
1/4 t cinnamon
1/8 t red pepper
1 8-ounce can tomato sauce
1.5 c water
1.5 T smooth peanut butter
3 c hot cooked rice
1/4 c chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Brown turkey with onion and garlic. Add to slow cooker. The remaining ingredients through peanut butter (not the rice or cilantro) should be whisked together in a bowl and poured over the turkey.
Cover and cook on Low for 4-5 hours.
Serve over hot rice garnished with cilantro.
