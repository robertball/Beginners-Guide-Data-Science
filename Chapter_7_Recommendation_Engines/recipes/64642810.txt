Title:
Chicken Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, cut into 4 cubes2 chicken breasts, cubed2 small zucchini, chunked1/2 lb. sliced mushrooms1-2 cloves garlic, minced1/3 cup grated provolone cheese1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese1/2 teaspoon dry mustard1 tablespoon cornstarch1 1/2 to 2 cups heavy cream (or use 1 can evaporated milk)1 lb. fettuccine noodles "al dente"salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta in boiling salted water until al dente, drain.Heat one cube butter, add garlic, saute vegetables, a few at a time, removing to a hot dish, and adding more butter in between batches.Continue with chicken and removing to hot dish.Pour cream into pan, and boil until somewhat reduced.Toss provolone with cornstarch, and add to cream a little at a time.Add half of the parmesan and stir well.Add chicken and vegetables to the pan.Stir well and season with salt and pepper while heating.Add fettuccine and toss while heating.Pour into serving dish and sprinkle with remaining parmesan.Serve immediately!

Submitter: 
Yomama
Description: 
My own twist on the traditional Alfredo Sauce. Easy and quick to make. I usually use canned milk because I like its creamy texture and reduced calorie input.
