Title:
Thai Kaang Ped Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c.(240 ml) Coconut milk
1 c. water
1 package (50 g) Thai Red Curry paste
250 g. Beef or chicken, sliced into small pieces
Fresh eggplant (1 cup diced)
Sweet basil to taste
1 small chili peppper, or 1 t. chili powder (depending on your tastes)
1-2 t. fish sauce
Cooked vermicelli or rice

Directions:
Heat skillet and add 1 c. coconut milk together with the Thai Red curry paste. Stir fry until oil appears on the surface.
Add the sliced beef or chicken and stir fry until done.
Add 1 c. water, bring to a boil.
Add the eggplant, sweet basil and chili. Cook until tender.
Add fish sauce to taste. Go easy and taste as you go. A little goes a long way.
Serve over cooked vermicelli or cooked rice.

Submitter: 
Skash
Description: 
A fantastic dish for beef or chicken with rice or vermicelli! A little kick, but tamed by coconut milk. If you've never chewed the aroma of coconut milk filling your kitchen, definitely cook this. Everyone will go wild in anticipation.
