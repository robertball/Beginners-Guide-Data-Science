Title:
Apple Burgers via MAM Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean ground beef
1 Tsp soy sauce
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1/2 tep onion powder
1 med apple

Directions:
Combine meat and spices then divide into 4 burgers
Fold meat over apple chunks and shape
Broil or grill about 3 inches from heat for 8 min
Turn once
