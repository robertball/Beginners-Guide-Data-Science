Title:
Mushroom Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. cooked rigatoni
1 tbsp. oil
1 1/4 lbs. sliced mushrooms
1 cup chopped onion
1 tsp. dried thyme
2 cloves garlic - minced
3/4 cup white wine
1 cup chicken broth
3 tbsp. mascarpone
Grated Parmesan cheese
Fresh parsley

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Add mushrooms, onion, garlic and thyme and cook until golden - about 7 minutes.
Add wine and reduce by half then stir in broth and mascarpone.
Toss sauce with pasta and top with Parmesan and parsley before serving.
