Title:
Fast and Tasty Chile Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Pounds Hamburger
1 Medium Onion Diced
1 15 oz Can Kidney Beans with juice
1 15 oz Can Pinto Beans with juice
2 15 oz cans Corn with juice
2 15 oz cans Tomatoes peeled and diced
2 15 oz cans Tomato sauce
2 Pkg of McCormich Taco Seasoning Mix

Directions:
Brown Hamburger meat with onion, add Taco Seasoning Mix and blend in. Add all remaining ingredients, bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 1/2 hour. Top with cheese or sour cream if desired..

Submitter: 
Veronica
Description: 
You should have everything in your pantry to make  quick savory chile beans. My mother in-law gave me the recipe and family and friends rave about the taste.
