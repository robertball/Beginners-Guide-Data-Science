Title:
Swiss Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
6 slices of swiss cheese
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup milk
2 cups of herbed stuffing
1/2 cup margarine or butter, melted
cooking spray

Directions:
Spray slow cooker with cooking spray.
Arrange chicken breasts in bottom of slow cooker.
Top each piece of chicken with a piece of cheese, layering cheese if necessay.
Combine soup and milk, stir well.
Spoon over cheese.
Sprinkle with stuffing mix.
Drizzle melted butter over stuffing mix.
Cook on low for 8 hours.
