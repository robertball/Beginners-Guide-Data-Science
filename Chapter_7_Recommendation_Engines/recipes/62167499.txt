Title:
ITALIAN PORKETTA ROAST Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 pound boneless pork shoulder -tied
10 cloves garlic
2-4 tablespoons Extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon@ lemon zest, fennel seed, dill seed, oregano, rosemary, onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper
1 bulb Fennel sliced with top greens chopped
4 carrots sliced
2 strong onion chopped fine
2-4 cups dry white wine or broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325* or get slow cooker ready
Sliver some of the garlic, slit roast with paring knife and stuff with garlic slivers,
Rub oil and zest into roast
In a spice grinder or blender, combine the dill seed, fennel seed, oregano, rosemary, pepper, onion powder and garlic powder. Grind well and rub into the roast
Add veggies and fresh garlic to 10x15 inch roasting pan or crock, add wine or broth.
Place roast on top of veggies
Cover with foil and roast at 325 degrees F for 1Â½ hours, uncover and cook 45 min  longer or until meat can be pulled apart with forks
Add more wine or broth to keep juices in pan
Serve w/ veggies and broccoli rabes and peppers or on sandwich rolls
