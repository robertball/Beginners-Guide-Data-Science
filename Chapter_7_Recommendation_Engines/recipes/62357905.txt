Title:
Quick and Easy Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/402180.jpg
Ingredients:
peanut oil
2 pkg. Kroger Stir-Fry frozen vegetables (green beans, broccoli, mushrooms, onions, carrots)
fresh asparagus
Very Very Teriyaki Sauce (to taste)
1 pkg. (41-60 count) Frozen, fully cooked shrimp, remove tails
minced garlice
1/4 t. cayenne pepper
1/2 - 1 t. crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
Heat peanut oil over medium high heat.  Stir-fry veggies for about 7-8 minutes or until almost al dente.  Add chopped asparagus. seasonings and some sauce.  Cook another minute or so.  Add shrimp until they are warmed through.  Add more Teriyaki sauce to taste.
