Title:
Creamed Green Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper
1 small head green cabbage (about 2 #), cored and shredded (10 C)
3 T unsalted butter
1/4 C all-purpose flour
2 C warm whole milk
1/4 C plus 1 T finely grated Pecorino Romano cheese (1/2 oz)
1 T finely grated lemon zest (from 2 lemons)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Bring 1 inch salted water to a boil in Dutch oven or large pot. Add cabbage. Cover, and steam, stirring occasionally, until tender, about 5 min. Drain, and let cool slightly. Squeeze out excess water using kitchen towel.
Melt butter in med. saucepan over med. heat. Stir in flour and cook until bubbling but not browning, 1-2 min. Slowly whisk in milk. Cook, stirring, until sauce is thick enough to coat the back of wooden spoon, about 2 min. Remove from heat; add 1/4 C cheese and lemon zest. Immediately fold cabbage into sauce. Season with salt and pepper
Spoon mixture into shallow (6-cup) casserole dish. Sprinkle with remaining tablespoon cheese. Bake until bubbling, 30-35 min. Let stand for 5 min before serving.
