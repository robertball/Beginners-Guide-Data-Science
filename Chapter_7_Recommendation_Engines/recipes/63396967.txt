Title:
Apple Crisp with Cranberry Compote Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Apple Crisp
5 cups granny smith apples, peeled and sliced
1/3 cup butter, softened
2/3 cup brown sugar, packed
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp all spice
1/2 cup oats
Cranberry Compote
1 - 12oz bag of fresh cranberries
1 cup sugar
1 cup water

Directions:
Apple Crisp - Preheat oven at 375 degrees.
Spread apples in the bottom of a 9" square baking pan.
Mix remaining ingredients (oat topping) until crumbly.
Spread evenly over apples.
Bake 30 to 40 minutes or until apples are soft.
Cranberry Compote - Bring sugar and liquid to a boil stirring frequently.
Add cranberries and reduce to a simmer.
Simmer 5 minutes or until cranberries soften and start to pop.
Remove from heat.  Sauce will thick upon cooling.
Serve apple crisp topped with the warm cranberry compote.

Submitter: 
funnybelle
Description: 
I like to make this delicious apple crisp recipe in the fall using fresh granny apples picked from our local orchard.  For a Thanksgiving like twist, top with a sweet cranberry compote.
