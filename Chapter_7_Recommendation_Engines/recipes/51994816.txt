Title:
Asian Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 Boston Bibb or butter lettuce leaves
1 pound thinly sliced chicken breasts
Cooking spray
2-3 cups of cooked rice
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
2 tablespoon soy sauce
1/4 cup hoisin sauce
2 teaspoons minced ginger
2 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
2 tablespoons of Garlic-Chili Sauce (optional)
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 teaspoon Asian (dark) sesame oil
small hanful of Vietnamese Basil - chopped

Directions:
Rinse whole lettuce leaves and pat dry, being careful not tear them. Set aside. Start some rice
In a medium skillet over med heat, spray with cooking spray, add sliced chicken breast and and cover, stirring often. Drain, and set aside. Cook the onion, garlic and ginger in the same pan, stirring frequently. Add the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, vinegar, and chile pepper sauce to the onions, and stir. Stir in chopped basil, green onions, and sesame oil, and continue cooking until the onions just begin to wilt, about 2 minutes. Add the chicken back in and about 1/2 cup water to make a little saucy. Cover and let simmer about 20 minutes stirring occasionally.
Place a generous scoop of warm rice in the lettuce leaf and top with chicken and the sauce. Carefully fold over and ENJOY!

Description: 
Delicious and bursting with flavor, Asian lettuce wraps are a great idea for entertaining or a family style meal. Each person can build their own fresh, delicious wrap. Just be sure to have plates or napkins available since the mixture can sometimes drip a little.Modified from: Asian Lettuce Wraps
