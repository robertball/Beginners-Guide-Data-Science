Title:
Scrumptious Shredded Roast Beef for sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4 to 5 pound roast (any cut)
3 pkgs Good Seasons Italian Dressing Mix
1 cup of water

Directions:
Place roast in crock pot -- sprinkle 3 pkgs salad dressing mix over roast. Pour water over this -- cover and cook for 6 to 8 hours on medium heat. When done, remove from juice. Trim off any fat, then pull meat apart (like shredded) and put back into juice until ready to use. Dip with slotted spoon onto hoagie buns, rolls, or hamburger buns. Condiments may be mayonaise or horseradish.

Submitter: 
Susan LeBlanc
Description: 
This is a great fix ahead recipe especially for tailgating and football games.
