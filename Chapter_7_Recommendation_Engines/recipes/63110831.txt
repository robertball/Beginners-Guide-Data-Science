Title:
Nutmeg Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Milk
a pinch of nutmeg
2 teaspoons sugar
*This recipe does not require a certain amount of milk. It all depends on the size of your mug. :)

Directions:
Turn on steamer until it gets to be 300 degrees Fahrenheit
Fill up a mug halfway with milk and steam the milk until it reaches 140-160 degrees F.
Mix in a generous pinch of nutmeg and the 2 teaspoons of sugar.
Drink while warm and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Froggy+Popovers=
Description: 
This warm winter drink calms you down and soothes your mind. With a perfect balance of nutmeg and sweetness, this is an ideal winter drink for everyone. Simple, quick, and fabulous!
