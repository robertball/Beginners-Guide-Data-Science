Title:
Super Easy Sirloin Beef Tips & Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/434023.jpg
Ingredients:
4 lbs Boneless Beef Loin Top Sirloin Steak
3 Tbs Vegetable Oil
3/4 Cup Cranberry Juice
26 oz Low Sodium Beef Stock (or homemade Beef Stock)
6 Tbs Low Sodium Soy Sauce
1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp fresh ground Ginger
Kosher Salt & Freshly Ground Black Pepper, to taste
Cornstarch & Water Slurry

Directions:
Cut Sirloin into 1 to 1 1/2" pieces.
Heat oil in large skillet on medium high.
When oil is hot, add sirloin and brown on all sides. (This may have to be done in batches, depending on size of pan.)
Season with Salt and Pepper, to your family's taste
When all meat is browned, add cranberry juice, beef stock, soy sauce, garlic powder and ginger.
Cover and simmer for 1  hour or until meat is very tender, stiring occassionally.
Uncover and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Mix cornstarch and water to make a slurry and stir into simmering beef and broth to make gravy. (Mixture must boil to reach desired thickness.)
Simmer for 1 - 2 minutes.
Serve over egg noodles, rice or mashed potatoes.
