Title:
Fajitas on a Stick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992527.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Lawry's® Tequila Lime Marinade with Lime Juice
1 1/4 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into 1 inch pieces
1/2 medium green bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
1/2 medium onion, cut into 1/2-inch slices
16 cherry tomatoes
8 fajita size flour tortillas, warmed

Directions:
In large resealable plastic bag, pour 3/4 cup Lawry's(R) Tequila Lime Marinade With Lime Juice over chicken; turn to coat. Close bag and marinate in refrigerator 30 minutes. Soak 8 wooden skewers in water 15 minutes.
Remove chicken from Marinade, discarding Marinade. On skewers, alternately thread chicken, green pepper, onion and tomatoes. Grill or broil, turning once and brushing frequently with remaining 1/4 cup Marinade, 15 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
Remove chicken and vegetables from wooden skewers. Serve on tortillas and, if desired, with sour cream and shredded Cheddar cheese.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
Marinated chicken and veggies are cooked on skewers and served in warm tortillas.
