Title:
Perfect Hard Boiled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Eggs

Directions:
Place eggs in a saucepan and fill with water until the eggs are just barely covered
Place on the stove and bring to a boil
Boil for four and a half minutes
Remove from heat and let the eggs sit in the hot water for twenty minutes
Remove to a bowl and either peel the shells and server or keep the shell on and place in the fridge until you are ready for them

Submitter: 
Dan.Ryan
Description: 
I've tried many different was to make a great ahrd boiled egg with a soft while and a nice fluffy yolk that's not grey on the outside.  After lots of trial and error I've finally done it.  After cooking I usually keep them in the shell until I need them.  Enjoy!
