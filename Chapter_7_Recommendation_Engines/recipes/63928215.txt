Title:
Peanut Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces spaghetti
1 bunch green onions, sliced (white parts only)
1 Pepper
2 tablespoons sesame oil
1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
*****I would 1/2 the sauce next time, but keep noodles and veggies the same*****
1/3 cup peanut butter
2 TBs honey (added)OR 1 teaspoon white sugar
1 TBs soy sauce (reduced from 1/4 cup)
1/4 cup hot water
1 tablespoon cider vinegar
1 Tbs Sweet Chili sauce OR 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1 Shredded Carrot
2 Tbs peanut dust and crushed peanuts

Directions:
Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until done. Drain.
Meanwhile, combine oil peppers and onions in a small skillet. Saute over low heat until tender. Add ginger; cook and stir for 1 to 2 minutes.  Mix in peanut butter, soy sauce, water, vinegar, sugar, and red pepper flakes. Remove from heat.
Toss noodles with sauce, carrots and peanut dust/crushed peanuts, and serve.

Submitter: 
Heather Peter Zelonis
Description: 
Ginger comes alive when its warmed, and it gets that opportunity in this tasty sauce. All the ingredients  - peanut butter to sesame oil -are warmed together in a pan until their flavors blend and the sauce becomes flavorful.Modified from: Peanut Noodles
