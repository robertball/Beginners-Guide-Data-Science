Title:
Carrot Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Italian arborio rice
2 cups water
1/2 cup roasted pine nuts
1 tablespoon margarine
1 onion, sliced
1 teaspoon ginger root, minced
3/4 cup grated carrots
salt to taste
cayenne pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Stir in 1 cup of rice. Cover and redduce heat to low, and allow to steam until tender, about 20 minutes.
While rice is cooking, toast pine nuts till golden brown, approximately 3 minuets and set aside. Melt margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute onions until golden brown. Stir in ginger, carrots, and salt to taste. Reduce heat to low and cover to steam 5 minutes. Stir in cayenne pepper and pine nuts. When rice is done, add it to skillet and stir gently to combine with other ingredients.
