Title:
Grandma Dee's No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups granulated sugar
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup milk
1 pinch salt
3 cups quick cooking oats
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a saucepan bring sugar, cocoa, margarine, milk, and salt to a rapid boil for 1 minute.
Add quick cooking oats, peanut butter, and vanilla; mix well.
Working quickly, drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper, and let cool.

Submitter: 
Corinne Villa
Description: 
My Mother gave me this recipe decades ago.  I used to make them for my children's classroom parties.  They were always a big hit.  My children still love them, now that they are adults.Modified from: No Bake Cookies III
