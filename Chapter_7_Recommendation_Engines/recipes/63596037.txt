Title:
Simple Carnitas by Keller Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 1/2) pound boneless pork shoulder, trimmed and cut into 2 inch chunks
2 oranges, quartered
6 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
2 tablespoons kosher salt
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
1 bottle light beer

Directions:
1. Place the pork shoulder, oranges, garlic, kosher salt, cumin, black peppercorns and beer into a large pot.  Add water to cover.
2. Bring to a boil over high heat, then cover loosely and reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 2 hours. Shred the meat with two forks.
3. Serve meat on a tortilla with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese and sour cream.
