Title:
Perfection Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons gelatine
1/2 cold water
1/2 cup mild vinegar
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 cups boiling water
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup cabbage finely chopped
2 cups celery finely chopped
2 pimentos chopped
1/2 cup sweet pickles, chopped

Directions:
Soak the gelatine in cold water for five minutes. Add the vinegar, lemon juice, boiling water, sugar, and salt. Cool, and when mixture starts to thicken add the remaining ingredients. Turn into a wet mold. Allow to chill until firm. Unmold and serve on a crisp lettuce leaf. Garnish with mayonnaise.
