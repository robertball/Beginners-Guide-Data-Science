Title:
Venison Pepper Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 pounds boneless venison steak (slightly frozen)
2 tablespoons cooking oil
1 garlic clove, minced
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
2 large green bell peppers, thinly sliced
1 large onions, thinly sliced
3/4 teaspoon beef bouillon granules
3/4 cup hot water
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup water
1/2 teaspoon sugar

Directions:
While still partially frozen, cut steak across the grain into 2-in. x 1/8-in. strips. In a large skillet or wok, brown steak in oil. Add garlic, ginger, salt (if desired) and pepper; cook 1 minute. Remove meat and keep warm. Add green pepper and onions to skillet; cook and stir for 5 minutes or until crisp-tender. Dissolve bouillon in hot water; add to skillet with water chestnuts. Combine cornstarch, soy sauce, cold water and sugar; stir into skillet. Add meat. Cook and stir until mixture boils; cook and stir 2 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
This is a delicious way to use those venison steaks my hunter husband is always filling my freezer with. Of course it works great with beef too. We serve it with sticky white rice at our house.Modified from: Pepper Steak
