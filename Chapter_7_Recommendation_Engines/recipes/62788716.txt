Title:
Low fat Waldorf Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. chopped red apples
1/4 c. chopped celery
1/4 c. halved seedless green grapes
2 T raisins
1 1/2 c. torn romaine lettuce
2 T low fat mayonnaise
2 T nonfat plain yogurt
1/2 t. honey or sugar
1/3 T lemon juice
salt
pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the first 5 ingredients.
In a small bowl, whisk together the Mayonnaise, yogurt, honey or sugar and lemon juice. Add salt & pepper. Pour over the salad, toss well and serve immediately.
