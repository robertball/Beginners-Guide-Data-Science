Title:
Pasta Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 cups favourite pasta, cooked and drained
1/4 cup vinegar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 cups assorted vegetables
Handful of cheeses, cut up
Cup of green olives, some chopped
Tin of black olives, sliced
Half tin of chick peas, rinsed and drained
Packet of Club House Pasta Salad Dressing Mix

Directions:
Combine vinegar, oil, dressing mix. Set aside. Rinse pasta until cold. Drain well. Toss pasta with dressing and add vegetables, cheese olives etc. Cover and chill a couple of hours. Toss again just before serving.

Submitter: 
Rosebud
Description: 
Very versatile salad that can be served as is or with a basket of breads and crackers. If desired, cooked chicken breast can be added or a tin of tuna.
