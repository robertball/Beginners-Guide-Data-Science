Title:
Glazed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs carrots, cut into sticks
1/4 C butter
1/4 C packed brown sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp white pepper

Directions:
Place carrots in a large saucepan. Add 1 inch of water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 8-10 minutes or until carrots are tender-crisp. Drain and set aside.
In the same pan,combine butter, brown sugar, salt & pepper. Cook and stir until butter is melted. Return carrots to the pan. Cook and stir over medium heat for 5 minutes or until glazed.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
Simple dish with tender carrots with a buttery glaze and a mild sweetness.
