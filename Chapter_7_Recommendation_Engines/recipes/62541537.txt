Title:
Spicy Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can diced tomatoes with green chiles
2 fire roasted red peppers
1 1 1/2 t. red pepper flakes
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375. Place the chicken breasts in a greased baking dish.
2. Sprinkle the pepper flakes evenly over the chicken.
3. Pour the tomatoes over the chicken, moving the chicken around a little so that some of juice from the tomatoes can get underneath the chicken too.
4. Tear the red peppers apart into strips and place them throughout the pan.
5. Bake the chicken for 25-35 minutes or until juices run clear and chicken is cooked throughout.

Description: 
This is a quick an easy chicken bake that is actually flavorful and healthy. Always a hit in my house!
