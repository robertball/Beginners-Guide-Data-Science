Title:
smash cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
3 eggs (divided use)
1 stick butter, melted
1/2 cup pecans
8 oz cream cheese
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup confectioners

Directions:
preheat oven to 350, mix cake mix, 1 egg, butter & pecans. Press into ungreased
9 x13 pan. set aside.
mix cream cheese, 2 eggs, vanilla & confectioners sugar.  spread over cake mix in pan. bake 30 min. cool & cut into squares
makes about 2 dz
