Title:
Apricot Honey Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups water
1/2 cup chopped dried apricots
1/3 cup honey
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt (optional)
2 cups Quaker® Oats (Quick or Old Fashioned, uncooked)

Directions:
In 3-quart saucepan, bring water, apricots, honey, cinnamon and salt to a boil.
Stir in oats; return to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium; cook about 1 minute for quick oats (or 5 minutes for old fashioned oats) or until most of liquid is absorbed, stirring occasionally.
Let stand until of desired consistency.

Submitter: 
The Quaker Oats Company
Description: 
Steaming bowls of whole-grain Quaker® oatmeal have been warming tummies and toes for over 125 years.  Tangy dried apricots, sweet honey and a sprinkle of cinnamon flavor the oats as they cook.
