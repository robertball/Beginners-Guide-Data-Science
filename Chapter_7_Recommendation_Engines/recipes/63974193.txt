Title:
Michelle's Tortilla Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 can chopped green chilis, drained
1/2 bunch green onion, chopped
2 c. finely shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 packet taco seasoning
1 package large flour tortillas

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix using hand mixer.
Spread evenly on flour tortilla all the way to the edges.
Roll tightly and immediately wrap in plastic.
Chill.
When firm, slice with a sharp knife.
Serve with picante sauce for dipping (salsa is too watery)
