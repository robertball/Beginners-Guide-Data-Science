Title:
Basic Cookie Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/454020.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups butter (no substitutes), softened
2 cups sugar
2 eggs
1/4 cup milk
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
8 cups all-purpose flour
4 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar.  Add the eggs, milk and vanilla; mix well.  Combine the flour, baking powder and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture, beating just until combined.  Divide dough into four 2-cup portions.  Cover and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Gloria  McBride
Description: 
In Payson, Utah, Gloria McBride turns everyday ingredients into a versatile dough that's the basis for five sensational sweets. 'You can bake some cookies right away, then pop the extra dough in the freezer for later,' she notes.
