Title:
Curry Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 lb bag of boneless skinless chicken thighs (frozen)
1 Tbsp Salt
1 tsp Accent Flavor Enhancer
1 13oz bag frozen broccoli flowerettes
2 cans cream of chicken with herbs soup
1 cups mayonnaise (not salad dressing)
1 1/2 tsp Curry Powder
1 1/2 tsp Lemon Juice
1/2 tsp pepper
1 Cup of Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place the frozen thighs in a large pot and cover with water.
Add salt and accent to the pot.
Bring water to a boil.
Boil the thighs for 30 minutes or until done.
Place frozen broccoli in a large collander.
Rinse well and let drain.
In a large bowl add soup, mayonnaise, curry powder, lemon juice, and pepper.
Mix well.
On a cutting board, chop broccoli into small pieces.
Line the bottom of a large (13"x9") casserole dish with broccoli.
When chicken is done, drain and cool slightly.
Shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces.
Layer the chicken on top of the broccoli.
Pour the sauce mix over the top of chicken and broccoli.
Smooth to seal edges.
Bake for 30 minutes.
Remove and cover with the 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese.
Bake an additional 5 minutes.
Serve over jasmine rice.

Description: 
The curry adds a wonderful aroma to this casserole of chicken and broccoli, and great over jasmine rice.  This is a family favorite, and it is easily doubled to serve to guests.  Not for the calorie conscious!
