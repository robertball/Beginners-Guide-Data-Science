Title:
Renee's Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box Devil's food cake mix
1 lrg box instant chocolate pudding mix
1 c. heavy cream
1 1/2c. semi sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Prepare cake mix according to package directions, adding in heavy cream and pudding mix. Mix well. Pour batter into a 4-6 quart crock pot. Sprinkle chocolate chips on top of batter.
Set crock pot on low and cook for about 4 hours. (try not to remove the lid during cooking)
Scoop cake into individual bowls using a large spoon or ice cream scoop. Serve warm with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream on top.

Submitter: 
ReneeW.
Description: 
If you LOVE chocolate you probably should not make this cake because once you take your first bite your hooked. My son requests this cake every year for his birthday, and it could not be easier to make. ( in a crock pot )
