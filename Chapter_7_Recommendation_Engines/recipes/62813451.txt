Title:
Dry Onion Soup Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dried onion flakes
1 tablespoons low-sodium beef bouillon granules or 3 cubes crushed
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon parsley flakes
1/8 teaspoon celery seed
1/8 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Stir the onion flakes, beef bouillon granules, onion powder, parsley flakes, celery seed, paprika, and black pepper in a bowl. Use as substitute for a 1-ounce envelope of dry onion soup mix.

Submitter: 
Julie60
Description: 
If you just like making your own, or if you don't want to run to the store, just whip up this dry onion soup mix substitute. You probably have most of the ingredients already. The recipe makes a substitute for a 1-ounce envelope of purchased mix.Modified from: Dry Onion Soup Mix
