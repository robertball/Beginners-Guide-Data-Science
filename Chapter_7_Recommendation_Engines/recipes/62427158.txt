Title:
Honey Almond Granola with Cranberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448511.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups rolled oats
1 cup chopped almonds
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 1/2 tbsp vanilla
1 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Combine oats, almonds, brown sugar and salt in a large bowl.  Mix by hand (or with a wooden spoon) until uniform.
Combine oil and vanilla in separate bowl. Add honey and mix well with a whisk.
Add the wet ingredients to the dry and mix together.
Spread the mixture evenly onto 2 sheets pans lined with parchment paper.
Bake at 325 for 10 minutes. Remove and stir.
Continue to bake 5-7 minutes until golden.
Remove from oven and allow to cool completely.
Add dried fruit and mix well.

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
This is a great way to start making granola, since its so versatile. It smells great when its baking in the oven!
