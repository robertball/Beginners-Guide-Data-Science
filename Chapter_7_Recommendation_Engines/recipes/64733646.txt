Title:
Galette des Rois Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Tablespoons of butter, softened
1/3 cup of sugar
1 egg, beaten
1 Tablespoon of apple brandy
2/3 cup of almond flour
2 Tablespoons of flour
Puff pastry ( I prefer the Trader Joe's kind) defrosted
1 egg (optional)
Powedered Sugar (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 400F
First the butter and sugar.
Then add the egg.
Next add in the brandy.
Last mix in the flours.
Lay out one sheet of puff pastry. Spoon out almond mixture onto the puff pastry sheet leaving 1 inch around the perimeter. Place second sheet on top and press down the edges to seal.
Either egg wash and place in over or place in oven and dust with powdered sugar.
Place in oven for 15 minutes and bake. Then lower to 350F and bake an additional 5 minutes.
