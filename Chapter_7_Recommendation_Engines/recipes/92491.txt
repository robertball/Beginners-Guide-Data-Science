Title:
All-Dressed Pizza Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup chopped pepperoni
1/2 cup chopped ham
1 green pepper, diced
10 ounces canned mushroom slices
1 medium onion, diced
1 cup grated marble cheese
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1 tablespoon minced fresh garlic
1 teaspoon dry crumbled oregano
1/2 teaspoon dry crumbled basil
1/2 teaspoon dry crumbled chilies
2 eggs
1/2 cup 1% milk
1/4 cup canola oil
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
In a large bowl, combine pepperoni, ham, green pepper, mushrooms, onion, cheese, pizza sauce, garlic, oregano, basil and chilies.
In another bowl, beat eggs, blend in milk and canola oil; mix into pizza mixture.
Combine flour and baking powder and mix into pizza mixture until well blended.
Spoon into non-stick muffin tins. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until lightly browned. Serve warm or cold. Store in the refrigerator or freezer.
