Title:
Confectioners White Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups icing sugar (9x9 = 1.5)
4 tbsp. milk (9x9 = 1.5)
1 cup shortening (Crisco), softened (9x9 = 1/3)
1 tsp. vanilla (9x9 = 1/2)
1 tsp. almond flavoring (9x9 = 1/2)
1/2 tsp. salt (9x9 = pinch)

Directions:
Put all ingredients into mixing bowl.
Beat together until smooth. Adjust icing sugar & milk to reach desired consistency for either spreading, filling or piping.
Makes about 3 cups (enough for about 4 dozen cupcakes).
For 8" or 9" square cake, quantities are in brackets.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A very white icing for decorating. Tints easily for truer colors.
