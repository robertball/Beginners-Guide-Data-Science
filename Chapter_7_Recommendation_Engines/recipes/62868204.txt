Title:
Cucumber Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers - peeled, seeded, chopped, drained
1 cup greek yogurt
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup chopped green onion
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 avocado, diced

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine the cucumber, yogurt, parsley, green onions, cumin and salt. Mix well. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours before serving. Mix in diced avocado just before serving.

Description:
