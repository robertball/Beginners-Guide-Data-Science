Title:
Snack Bar Cookies II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1015408.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C. All purpose flour
1 C. Whole wheat pastry flour
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1/2 C. Butter
1 C. Granulated sugar
1/2 C. Oat Bran
1 C. Dates, chopped
1/2 C. chocolate chips
2 Eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
In a medium size bowl combine flour, baking powder and baking soda then set aside
In a large bowl combine butter and granulated sugar together and beat with an electric mixer until butter is dissolved into the sugar
Add the eggs and beat until mixture is smooth and creamy
Stir in oat bran and dates mixing together well
Add dry ingredients  slowly to wet ingredients and stir until all the flour has been absorbed and then stir in chocolate chips distributing them evenly throughout  the dough
Drop by teaspoonfuls on a cookie sheet 2 inches apart and bake for 8 to 10 minutes watching them carefully as they tend to brown quickly

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very soft cookie with chocolate chips and dates together to make a good combination for a snack cookie.
