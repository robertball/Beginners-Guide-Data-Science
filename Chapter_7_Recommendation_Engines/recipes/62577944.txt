Title:
Healthy Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fat-free milk
1 1/2 tsp cocoa
2 tsp of organic maple syrup

Directions:
1. In a saucepan bring the milk to a boil
2. Add the cocoa and maple syrup stirring occaisionally until all the cocoa has blended into the milk
