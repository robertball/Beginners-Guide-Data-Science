Title:
Salsa Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
60ml White Wine Vinegar;
1 small clove Garlic – finely chopped;
20g flat leaf Parsley – chopped
10/12 Basil leaves – chopped;
2tbsp Spring Onion or sweet White Onion - grated;
3tbsp Capers – drained and rinsed;
1tsp Dijon Mustard
120/125ml Extra Virgin  Olive Oil;
Salt & freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the wine vinegar, garlic, onion, capers, mustard, parsley and basil;
With a fork, beat in the oil gradually, a little at a time, to avoid separating;
Season with salt and pepper - to taste;
Set aside at room temperature to marinate for 1 to 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
To quote a  much used AR phrase, this salsa is awesome with grilled/BBQ Steak/Chicken/Pork etc and a simple side salad, lettuce, rocket, cherry tomatoes, cucumber.
Fries and crusty bread also help.
