Title:
Wild Rice with Cranberries and Caramelized Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c chicken broth
1/2 c brown rice
1/2 c wild rice
3 tbsp butter
3 med onions, sliced in thin wedges
2 tsp brown sugar
1 c craisins
1/2 tsp orange zest

Directions:
Combine chicken broth and rices in medium saucepan and bring to a boil over med-high heat. Reduce to low, cover and simmer 45 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.
Meanwhile, melt butter in med skillet over med-high heat and add onions and brown sugar. Cook six minutes or until liquid is absorbed. Reduce to low and cook slowly for 25 minutes or until onions are caramel color, stirring often.
Stir craisins into onion mixture and cook over low heat for 10 minutes.
Gently fold craisins and onion mixture, and orange zest into cooked rice.
Serve.
