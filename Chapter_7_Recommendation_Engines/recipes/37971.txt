Title:
Coffee Mint Frost Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977037.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
4 teaspoons TASTER'S CHOICE® 100% Pure Instant Coffee
4 large scoops vanilla ice cream
1 cup ice cubes
2 tablespoons granulated sugar
1/8 teaspoon peppermint extract

Directions:
PLACE milk and Taster's Choice in blender; cover. Blend until coffee is dissolved.  Add ice cream, ice, sugar and peppermint extract; blend until smooth. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Taster's Choice®
Description: 
Tastes like Christmas, but is good any time of year.
