Title:
stuffed acorn squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package cornbread stuff and such
1 cup wild rice  mix
2 cups vegetable stock
2 t butter/or olive oil
1 onion diced
1 clove garlic
1 cup diced fresh mushrooms
couple sprigs fresh sage
two medium sized acorn squash

Directions:
cut acorn squash in half, clean out seeds, and bake in 350 degree oven cut side down for 25 minutes, or until just barely soft to the touch
During squash cooking time, make stuff and such dressing as per package directions.
Melt butter in medium sauce pan, cook onion and garlic till clear, about 10 minutes, add mushrooms continue cooking for another 5 minutes.
Add dry rice, and sage, stirring to brown rice for about 5 minutes.
Add two cups broth, bring to boil and cover and cook till rice is tender, 30-40 minutes.
combine cooked rice  mixture with dressing mixture and stuff in to centres of squash.  Bake for 15 minutes.
to serve cut squash half's in half again.
