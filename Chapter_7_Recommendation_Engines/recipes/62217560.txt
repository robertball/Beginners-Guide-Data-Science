Title:
Pecan Pie Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup flour
1 cup chopped pecans
2/3 cup melted margarine
2 eggs
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Combine sugar, flour and pecans. Set aside. Mix margarine, eggs and vanilla together then stir into flour mixture. Mix until just moistened. Bake in mini-tins. Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
