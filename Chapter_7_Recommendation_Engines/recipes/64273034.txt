Title:
Sparkling Cranberry Kiss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups cranberry juice
1 1/2 cups orange juice
3 cups ginger ale
ice cubes
orange slices, optional

Directions:
In a pitcher, combine cranberry and orange juices.  Just before serving, stir in ginger ale; serve over ice.  If desired, serve with orange slices.
