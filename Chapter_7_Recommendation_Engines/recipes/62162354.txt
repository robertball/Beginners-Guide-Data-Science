Title:
Cheese Wheel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. or 1 pkg of yeast.
2/3 c. warm water (not hot)
2 c. flour
2 Tb. oil
1/2 tsp. sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
Filling:
1 egg
4 c. cheese (brick, munster, mozz, etc)
1/4 c. parsley
1/8 tsp. pepper
3/4 tsp. garlic salt
Glaze:
1 egg
1 Tb. water
Sesame seeds

Directions:
Soften the yeast in the warm water.
Let set for 1 min, then add rest of the ingredients minus the filling.
Knead for 4-5 minutes.
Let rise for one hour.
Divide into 2 portions.
Roll portion 1 into a 12 inch circle and place on a greased pizza pan.
Combine all of the filling ingredients and place on top.
Roll the rest of the dough and place over filling.
Bake at 400 degrees for 18 minutes.
Remove and brush with the combined egg and water glaze.
Sprinkle with sesame seeds and return to oven for 2 minutes.

Submitter:

My mom used to make this a lot when we were kids. We adored it. Cheesy to the max and fun to eat!
