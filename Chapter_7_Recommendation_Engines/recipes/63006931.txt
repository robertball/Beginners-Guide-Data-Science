Title:
Roasted Jalapeno Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 Jalapenos
1/2 cup white cooking wine
pinch of white pepper
pinch of paprika
pinch of cumin
pinch of garlic, minced
1/4 cup white onion, chopped
2/3 cup cream
1/2 oz. chicken stock
2 cups rice, cooked according to direction on package

Directions:
Place the jalapenos in an ungreased skillet over medium-high heat. Turn them periodically until blackened on all sides. Remove from skillet, remove seeds and chop.
Combine jalapenos, white wine, pepper, paprika, cumin, garlic, onion in skillet. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and saute for a few minutes until the onion is soft. Add cream. Reduce heat and reduce down for a few minutes. Add chicken stock.
Combine with cooked rice.
