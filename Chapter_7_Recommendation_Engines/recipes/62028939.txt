Title:
Grannies Best Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken Pot Pie
2 0r 3 three large chicken breasts
1 small onion
1 medium potato, sliced
salt and pepper to taste
Pot Pie Dough
2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp crisco
1/4 cup cold water (more if necessary)

Directions:
Cook chicken, onion, salt, and pepper in water to cover until chicken is done.  Take pot off heat, remove chicken.  When chicken is cool enough to handle, remove meat from bones and return to pot onn low heat.  Add potato.  Mix dough as indicated; divide into 2 balls.  Roll out on floured surface, cut in squares and slowly place in pot which has been brought back to boiling.  Add only as much dough as broth will tolerate.  Adjust seasoning cook until potatoes and pot pie is tender.

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
This recipe was a childhood favorite of mine.  My grandmother was the best cook in the world.  She had some recipies that would make you remember the day that you ate it. For me, this is one of them.
