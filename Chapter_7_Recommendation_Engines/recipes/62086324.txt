Title:
Cookie Dough Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the brownie layer:
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips, melted
1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
3 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/4 cups flour
1/4 tsp baking soda
3/4 cup nuts (optional)
For the cookie dough layer:
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
3 Tbsp. milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup flour
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
For the brownie layer:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a 9 x 13 inch pan.  In a small saucepan, melt chips over low heat, cool.  In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs and vanilla.  Stir in melted chocolate.  In a small bowl, mix together flour and soda.  Stir dry ingredients into chocolate mixture.  Add nuts, if desired.  Spread batter into greased pan.  Bake in preheated oven for 16-22 minutes, or until knife comes out clean.  Cool completely.
For cookie dough layer:
Cream butter and sugars.  Stir in milk.  Stir in flour, mix well.  Add chocolate chips.  Drop by spoonfuls on cooled brownie lay and spread carefully.  Cut into squares and enjoy!
