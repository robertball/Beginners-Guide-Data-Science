Title:
very easy bechamel sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons flour
1 cup of milk
1/2 teaspoon garlic
salt,white pepper to taste

Directions:
melt butter in saucepan
add flour whisk constantly for about 3 mins.
add milk whiski constantly till smooth and thick.
add salt and white pepper to taste
