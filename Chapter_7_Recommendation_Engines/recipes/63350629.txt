Title:
French Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large yellow onions
2 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp flour
6 cups of beef broth
Salt & pepper to taste
6 slices of french bread
3/4 cup Swiss or Gruyere cheese grated
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese grated

Directions:
Peel onions and slice thinly.
Saute onion rings in butter until softened, then lightly stir in flour.
Add onions to beef broth and bring to a simmer covered for 20 minutes.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Ladle soup into oven proof bowls.
Place a round of french bread in each bowl and sprinkle with the combined cheeses.
Place bowls on a cookie sheet and brown under a preheated broiler for 3 minutes or until cheese is bubbling.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Great for lunch, before a light evening meal or a late snack.
