Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium to large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
1/3 stick melted butter
1 1/2 Cups half and half
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 Cup light brown sugar
1 Cup chopped pecans
1/3 stick melted butter

Directions:
Boil cubed sweet potatoes 10-15 minutes or until they easily mash with a fork.
Drain potatoes and mash in a large mixing bowl with 1/3 stick of melted butter.
Add half and half, and vanilla, and mix on low speed with a hand mixer until creamy.
Pour sweet potato mixtue into a 9X13 in casserole dish.
Mix pecans, brown sugar, and butter in a small bowl until the mixture is completely incorporated.
Sprinkle nut/sugar mixture over the top of the casserole.
Bake in a pre-heated 350 degree oven for 35 minutes.
Allow to rest at least 5 minutes before serving.
