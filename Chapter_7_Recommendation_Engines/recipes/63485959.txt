Title:
Marshmallow Rice Crispy Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine
1 101/2 oz bag mini marshmallows (40 big)
6 cups rice crispies cereal

Directions:
melt marg in 3 quart sauce pan over low heat.  add marshmallows. stir until melted and well blended. remove from heat. add cereal.  toss until well coated.  press mixture into greased 9x13" pan.  cool and then cut into squares
microwave: melt amrg into large mixing bowl on high for 45 s. add marshmallows toss to coat.  melt 1 1/2 mins stirring at 45 s
