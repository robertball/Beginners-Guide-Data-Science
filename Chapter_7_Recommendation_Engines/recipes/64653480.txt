Title:
Salsa-It Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs. -Ground beef
2-eggs (beaten )
1- 24Oz  pace Salsa
1 cup- crushed Cheez-It's (anyflavor)
Salt and pepper

Directions:
1. Pre-Heat oven to 350 degrees
2. Beat 2 eggs place in large bowl add ground beef, cheez-Its,and most of the salsa (leave enough for the top), salt and pepper.
3. Mix with hands until everything is well mixed together into a ball shape.
4. Place meat mixture in a meatloaf pan and into the oven for 1 hour.
5. Once done top remaining salsa on top and serve.
