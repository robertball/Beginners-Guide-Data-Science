Title:
pizzia fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion chopped
1/2lb ground beef
2Tbsp butter
2 10oz jars pizzia sauce
1Tbsp cornstarch
1tsp fennel seed
1 1/2tsp orgeno
1/4tsp garlic powder
10oz cheddar cheese-graded
1C mozzorella-graded
1 loaf garlic bread

Directions:
Brown the onion,meat and butter, drain fat. Mix cornstarch and seasoning with the pizzia sauce- cook until thickened. Then add the cheese. Mix everything together. Pour into fondue pot. I use garlic bread to dip.
