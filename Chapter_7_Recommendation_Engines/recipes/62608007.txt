Title:
Rosemary Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Several Sprigs fresh Rosemary
Popcorn
salt

Directions:
In a saucepan put in enough extra virgin olive oil to lightly cover bottom of pan.  Heat the oil and put the rosemary in oil.  Allow the rosemary to infuse in the oil.  Remove rosemary, and pour oil into another small container leaving enough oil in the pan to pop the popcorn.  Place as much popcorn in pan as you think you need without overfilling pan once popcorn is popped. Shake pan continuously until popped.  Dump the popcorn in a bowl and drizzel the saved oil over the popcorn and salt to taste.
