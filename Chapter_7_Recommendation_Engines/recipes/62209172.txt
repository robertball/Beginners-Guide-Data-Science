Title:
Antigonish Marble Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp, salt
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 square unsweetened chocolate, melted
1 tsp. butter, melted
1/4 cup chopped nuts
1/3 cup shredded coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F and grease an 8" square pan.
Beat eggs; add brown sugar.
Mix together flour, baking powder and salt; blend into the egg/sugar mixture.  Stir in vanilla.
Remove 1/4 of the batter to a small bowl and stir in melted chocolate, butter and nuts.
Add coconut to the remaining 3/4 of the batter.  Pour this batter into the pan, the dot with the chocolate mixture.  (You can swirl lightly with a knife if you like)
Bake 20-25 minutes.  Let cool before cutting into squares.
