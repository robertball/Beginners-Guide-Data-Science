Title:
Corn Mush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of whole corn
1 can of creamed corn
2 eggs
1 small can of evaporated milk (2/3 cup)
4 Tbsp of melted butter (1/2 stick)
2 Tbsp of minced onion
½ tsp salt
¼ tsp pepper
2 cups of crushed saltine crackers (About ½ a box)

Directions:
Drain a ¼ cup of the corn liquid into a measuring cup and set aside.
Crack eggs into a greased casserole dish and beat them slightly.
Stir in corn, a ¼ cup of the corn liquid, creamed corn, evaporated milk, butter, onion, salt and pepper.
Fold in the saltines.
Cover with lid and bake at 325 degrees for one hour.
Uncover and cook for 10-15 more minutes or until the top is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Christina Salinas
Description: 
This is a huge hit with my family.  It was originally called Scalloped Corn but we have since decided to call it Corn Mush instead because that's what it kind of looks like.
