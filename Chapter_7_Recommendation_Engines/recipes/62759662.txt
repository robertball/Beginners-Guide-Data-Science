Title:
Chicken Cacciatora Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/735765.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless,skinless chicken breast
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp.salt
1/2 tsp.pepper
2 Tbsp.olive oil
1 green pepper, cut in chunks
1 large onion,cut in chunks
2 cups fresh mushrooms,cut in chunks
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 large can of canned tomatoes
1 small can tomatoe paste
1/2 tsp.salt
1 tsp.pepper
1/2 tsp.Italian seasoning
1 tsp.oregano
1/2 tsp.basil
pinch[or more]red pepper flakes
1/2 cup red wine

Directions:
Cut up ckicken into chunks.In bowl combine flour with salt and pepper,roll chicken in flour.Heat oil in skillet.Fry untill golden.
Add onions,garlic, green pepper and mushrooms,cook untill soft.Add spices,cook 5 min.
Add can tomatoes and tomatoe paste.Stir well,cover and simmer for 45 min.
Add red wine,cook another 1/2 hour.
Serve with pasta.
