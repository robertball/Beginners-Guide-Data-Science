Title:
SUPER SIMPLE Meatloaf Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. oil
1 small onion, diced
1/2 tsp. paprika
2-4 slices leftover meatloaf, 1/2-3/4" thick
1 14 1/2 oz. can DelMonte diced potatoes, drained
salt & pepper to taste
1/3 cup ketchup

Directions:
Pour the oil into a medium skillet; add the diced onion and paprika.  Cook until translucent.  Add the remaining ingredients and stir to combine.
Cook until heated through.  Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Valerie Kasper
Description: 
Super,super, simple and fast!... and made from leftovers!  For those nights when you just don't have time to cook. Very kid friendly!
