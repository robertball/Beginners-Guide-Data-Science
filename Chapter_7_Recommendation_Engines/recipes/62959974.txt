Title:
No Peek Beef Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb stew beef; cut into 1-inch pieces
1 1 3/8 oz envelope dry onion soup mix
1/2 cup red wine or broth
1 10.75 ounce can cream of mushroom soup
1 can (4 oz) whole mushrooms; drained
1/4 cup quick cooking tapioca (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in crock pot. Stir together well.  Cover and cook on low 8 to 12 hours. (High 4 to 6 hours). Serve over noodles or rice.
