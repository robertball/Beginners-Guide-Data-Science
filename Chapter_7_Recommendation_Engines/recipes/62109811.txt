Title:
Easy Mojitos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 mint leaves
2 slices lime
1-2 tsp sugar
1 part Bacardi rum
3 parts Diet Sprite

Directions:
Place mint, lime and sugar in bottom of glass and muddle with a spoon until mint is crushed
Add ice to fill glass
Add rum and sprite and mix

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
It's hard to find mojitos at any of the restaurants in the small town I'm stationed in, and when I tried to make my own using club soda, they never seemed to turn out right.  Using lemon-lime soda also cuts down on the amount of sugar usually used.
