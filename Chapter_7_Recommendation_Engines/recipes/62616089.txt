Title:
Easy Caramel Praline Apple Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 oz container caramel apple dip
3T Kahula
3/4C chopped pecans

Directions:
Slightly warm apple dip in microwave according to package directions (1-2 min on high). Transfer to serving bowl.
Stir in Kahula and pecans.
Core and slice apples.
To avoid darkening, presoak in Fresh Fruit or use lemon water.
