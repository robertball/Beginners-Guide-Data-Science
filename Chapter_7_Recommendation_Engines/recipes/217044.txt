Title:
Habanero Heat Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/662767.jpg
Ingredients:
1 habanero pepper, seeded and chopped (wear gloves)
1/3 cup olive oil
3 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
1 tablespoon water
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 tablespoon black sesame seeds

Directions:
Place the habanero pepper, olive oil, rice vinegar, peanut butter, water, and garlic into a blender, and process until smooth. Add sesame seeds, and pulse once or twice just to mix. Pour into serving bowl.

Submitter: 
souplover Sue
Description: 
Slightly sweet salad dressing can sub as a dip, glaze, or marinade for chicken, seafood, or red meat.
