Title:
Egg Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4430234.jpg
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 red pepper
1 orange pepper
1 onion
1 pack mushrooms
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Heat olive oil in skillet.
Dice peppers, onion and mushrooms.
Cook veggies in skillet, till soft, 5-10 minutes.
Meanwhile, crack eggs, whisk.
Once veggies are cooked, add egg scramble.
Cook for 3-5 minutes, till eggs are cooked.
