Title:
Saffron Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rice
2 cups vegetable broth
1/2 cup diced onion
3 tbsp butter
3 saffron threads

Directions:
Preheat over to 375 degrees.
Heat the butter in a large frying pan over medium heat.
Add the onion and cook until slightly soft.
Add broth and saffron to the onions and bring to a slight boil.
Reduce heat and continue to cook for 5 minutes (mixture should start to turn yellow from the saffron)
Put rice in the bottom of a small, square casserole dish and cover with the broth mixture. Stir and cover.
Cook at 375 for 30 min.

Submitter:

Description: 
Very simple rice recipe that bakes in the oven. Great side dish!
