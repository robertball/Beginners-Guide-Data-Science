Title:
A Berry Beautiful Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup frozen raspberries, thawed
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoon honey
1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
1/2 cup olive oil
1 bag preprared, pre-washed baby greens
8 oz goat cheese, crumbled
3/4 cup toasted pistachios, chopped
2 cups mixed berries (raspberries, sliced strawberries, blueberries & blackberries)

Directions:
For the dressing:
Puree thawed raspberries in blender until smooth.  Drain, pressing on the solids to yeild 1/4 cup.  Whisk raspberry puree, vinegar, honey, and sugar in a bowl.  Gradually whisk in oil.  Salt & pepper to taste, and refridgerate for at least one hour.
For the salad:
Place greens in a large bowl and drizzle with 1/2 cup dressing.  Toss to coat.  Divide the greens onto four salad plates, and top with goat cheese, pistachios, and berries.  Serve remaining dressing on the side.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
Take advantage of all of those beautiful fresh berries this summer and enjoy this perfect mix of berries, greens, cheese and nuts.  Now that berries are available year round in most areas, you can even prepare this sweet-heart of a salad for Valentine's Day!
