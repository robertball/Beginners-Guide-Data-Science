Title:
Broccoli Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065889.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 pound broccoli florets, cooked
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
1 cup Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 cup dry stuffing mix

Directions:
Boil chicken until tender and shred or cut into bite size pieces.
Meanwhile, steam broccoli until crisp but tender.
In a small bowl mix together the soup and mayonnaise.
Set aside.
In a 9x13 inch baking dish layer the chicken, broccoli, soup mixture, and cheese. Sprinkle dry stuffing mix over the top.
Bake at 350Â° for 25-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Assembly time is minimal with this simple casserole combining chicken, cream of mushroom soup, cheddar cheese and broccoli. Dry stuffing mix is sprinkled over the top for a crunchy, flavorful topping.Modified from: Broccoli Chicken Casserole I
