Title:
Creamy Wild Rice and Mushroom Soup Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1	package (2.75 oz.) Country Gravy mix
2	teaspoons dried minced onion
2	teaspoons dried celery flakes
1	teaspoon dried parlsey flakes
½	cup white rice
¾	cup (rice select royal blend, rice blend)
2	tablespoons coarsely chopped dried mushrooms such as shiitake
For the Soup Instructions:  7	cups water
1	tablespoon chicken bouillon or chicken base
1	tablespoon olive oil
½ 	cup minced celery
1	jar soup mix

Directions:
Pour gravy mix into wide mouth pint jar.  In a small bowl combine, onion, celery, and parsley stir and pour into jar on top of the gravy mix making second layer. Add layers of white rice then royal blend. Then mushrooms. Put lid on jar and attach instructions.
Intructions: in a Dutch oven or stock pot, add olive oil and celery sauté for 5 minutes over medium low heat.  Once softened.  Add water and contents of jar and bouillon or base, bring to a boil.  Reduce heat cover and simmer for 25 to 30 minutes or until rice is tender. Stirring occasionally.  Garnish with fresh chopped parsley.
