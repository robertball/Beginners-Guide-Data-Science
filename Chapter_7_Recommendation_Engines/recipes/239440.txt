Title:
Slow Cooker Cranberry and Muscadine Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1634307.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (5 pound) boneless pork loin roast, trimmed of fat
1 (750 milliliter) bottle muscadine red wine
1 (12 ounce) package fresh cranberries
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Put the pork roast into the crock of your slow cooker. Pour wine over the roast; add the cranberries and garlic. Season everything with salt and pepper.
Cook on Low 6 to 8 hours.

Submitter: 
JLYNNNAMDG
Description: 
This is such an easy way to make a tender, juicy pork roast.  My family loves it . . . even my 5-year-old. It's fancy enough to serve to guests, but so easy to make, so why wait for company?
