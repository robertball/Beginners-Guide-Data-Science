Title:
Jalapeno Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413424.jpg
Ingredients:
Cooking spray
8 chicken thighs or four boneless chicken breasts
3 T jalapeno jelly
1 T olive oil
1 T lime juice
1 clove garlic
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Line jelly roll pan with foil. Spray with non-stick cooking spray
If using breasts, cut into halves.  Arrange chicken in single layer in pan. Bake 15 minutes and drain juices. Combine jelly, oil, lime juice, garlic, chili powder, pepper and salt in small bowl. Turn chicken. Brush with half of jelly mixture.  Bake 20  minutes. Turn chicken. Brush with remaining mixture.  Bake 10-15 minutes or until juices run clear and meat thermometer registers 180 degrees.

Submitter: 
Parizienne
Description: 
Spicy, sweet and delicious.  Perfect with a corn salsa or a tossed salad with a light vinaigrette.
