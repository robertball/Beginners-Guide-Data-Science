Title:
Banana Split Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521128.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups  Graham Crackers, crumbled
1 (20 oz.) can of crushed pineapples, drained
6 each bananas
1 stick  Butter, melted
1 pound Powdered Sugar
1 pound Cream Cheese
1/2 cup Sugar
2 pints Whipping Cream
1/2 cup Pecans
1/2 cup Maraschino Cherries

Directions:
Mix the crumbled graham crackers and melted butter together to form crust
for a 9" X 13" pan.
Next beat the powdered sugar and cream cheese for 7 minutes or until thoroughly mixed.
Pour mixture over crust and place bananas over this mixture. Place whipped cream on top of the bananas then pineapples and juice.
Add more whipped cream and top with pecans and maraschino cherries.
Put in refrigerator until chilled.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
This banana split-inspired dessert makes a great treat to share with friends and family.
