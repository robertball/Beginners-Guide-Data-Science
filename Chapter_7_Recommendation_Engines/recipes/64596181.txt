Title:
Italian Bow tie Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes of Bow Tie Pasta
3 lbs lean ground beef
1 lg can of mushrooms (pieces and stems)
1 lg  jar of Roasted Garlic and Parmesan Alfredo sauce
1 lg can of cream of mushroom soup
2 cp sour cream
1-2 cp milk
2 cp shredded italian cheese mix
3 pckg or 9 tbl of Ranch seasoning
3 tsp paprika

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
Salt water for boiling pasta. Prepare pasta according to directions for al dente  pasta. Brown ground beef; salt and pepper to taste. Drain ground beef, then stir in ranch seasoning and paprika. Combine Alfredo sauce, cream of mushroom soup, sour cream,mushrooms, ground beef, and pasta.  Add milk to desired consistency, then pour mixture into a large, deep baking dish. Spread cheese across the top of the mixture, then place in the oven to bake for 40 to 50 minutes until the center is heated through.
