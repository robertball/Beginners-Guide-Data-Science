Title:
Tuscan Italian Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1) 2 cups cooked al dente pasta
2) 3 celery stalks, chopped
3) 1/2 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese
4) 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
5) 1/2 cup red onion, finely diced
6) 1 1/2 cup grape tomatoes, sliced
7) 1 cup Veganaise
8) 1 cup Kraft Tuscan Italian dressing
9) 1/2 cup sweet banana peppers, diced
10) 1/2 cup sliced sweet or mild pepperocini peppers, diced
11) 1 whole cucumber, skin removed, chopped
12) 1/2 cup sliced pitted Kalamata olives
*Pepper to taste, salt optional; real bacon bits or diced meat can be added to create a robust Mediterranean meal.

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl or dish, combine all ingredients, mixing until dressing takes on a creamy consistency and all ingredients are thoroughly coated. Serve immediately, or refrigerate, mixing again just before serving when (oils in dressing may separate).

Submitter: 
A. Piatt
Description: 
A flavorful diversion from the run-of-the-mill pasta salad.
