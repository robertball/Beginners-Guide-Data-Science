Title:
Big Ray's Summer Squash Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3594284.jpg
Ingredients:
2 small zucchini, thinly sliced
1 small yellow squash, thinly sliced
4 eggs
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 cup chopped green peppers
1/4 tsp. each salt and black pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 375Â°F.
Cook zucchini and yellow squash in large skillet sprayed with cooking spray on medium heat 5 min. or until crisp-tender, stirring frequently. Remove from heat.
Whisk eggs and dressing in large bowl until blended. Stir in Parmesan. Add zucchini mixture and all remaining ingredients; mix lightly. Spoon into 8-inch square baking dish.
Bake 35 to 40 min. or until center is set and edges are lightly browned.

Submitter:

Description: 
When fresh zucchini and yellow squash overwhelm the garden, let them team up to make the perfect base for this cheesy oven-baked side. Super easy to make so you can soon get back to enjoying your summer evening.
