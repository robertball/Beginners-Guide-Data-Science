Title:
Best Ever Chili Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz brick Velveeta
1 pkg Jimmy Dean sausage (original or hot)
1 can Hormel chili,no beans
1 can Rotel Diced Green Chilis and Tomatoes,drained
1 bag Tortilla Chips

Directions:
Cook sausage according to the pkg directions.  Cube Velveeta for speedier melting.  Add all ingredients to crockpot, mix.  Turn crockpot on high until cheese is fully melted, stirring occationally.  Turn on low to keep warm.

Submitter: 
ARATMARIE
Description: 
A great recipe that everyone will love!! Easy to make. If you're wanting to feed a crowd, double the recipe.
