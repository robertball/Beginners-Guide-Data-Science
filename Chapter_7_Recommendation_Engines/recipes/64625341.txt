Title:
Tangy Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shrimp - 16-20 Large/Jumbo (4-5 per person); raw, peeled, deveined, with tail on.
Olive Oil - 3-4 TBL.
Garlic - 3-4 medium-large cloves; smashed.
Crushed Red Pepper - 1 tsp (adjust to spice tolerance).
Seasoning - 2-3 tsp; Old Bay or similar.
Lemon Zest - 3 tsp.
Lemon Juice - 2 TBL.
Parsley - 3 TB; chopped.

Directions:
Heat a large skillet (cast iron or stainless steel) over medium-low heat for 3 minutes, until warm.
Add in, simultaneously, the garlic, crushed red pepper, and shrimp.  Stir ingredients together.
Add seasoning plus salt & pepper.  Fold everything together.
Cook on medium heat until shrimp are fully cooked (approx 3-5 minutes depending on size).
Pour lemon juice over mixture.  Stir again.
Reduce heat to low.  Add lemon zest and parsley.
Remove shrimp (only) to serving platter.

Submitter: 
stephenhbossin
Description: 
This is a simple dish that can be used as a pre-meal snack or the appetizer course.  Stove top, easy and quick to make.  And, all the ingredients can be pre-measured ahead of time.
