Title:
Green Tea Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8-oz package of cream cheese, softened
1/2 stick butter, softened
2 tablespoons of whipping cream
1 cup sifted confectioners sugar
2 teaspoons of matcha (powdered green tea)

Directions:
Beat together cream cheese, butter and whipping cream until creamy.
Add the sugar and matcha and beat until glossy and smooth.

Submitter: 
Fugendake
Description: 
A tangy Japanese inspired frosting for Kurogoma Cupcakes courtesy of Delicious Coma.
