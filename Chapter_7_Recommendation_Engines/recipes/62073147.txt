Title:
Pennsylvania Egg Melt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices Texas Toast
2 eggs
4 slices pre cooked bacon
2 slices American cheese
2 slices tomatoes
2 lettuce leaves
creamy cole slaw
butter

Directions:
Heat two pans over medium heat. Spread butter over one side of each piece of bread and place butter side down in one large pan. Place a slice of cheese on two of the four pieces of bread. While the cheese begins to melt on the bread, fry the eggs over hard in the other pan. When the eggs are done place one over each of the cheese slices of bread. Top the egg slices with bacon. Remove all four pieces of bread from the pan when buttered side is golden brown. Place one plain piece of grilled bread and one egg, bacon, cheese piece of bread on two plates. Top the plain piece with lettuce, then tomato, then finish with a scoop of cole slaw. Serve open faced or sandwich style if desried.
