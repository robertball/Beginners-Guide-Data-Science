Title:
Rigatoni a la You! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976373.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (19.76 ounce) package Johnsonville Mild Italian Sausage Links
1 (16 ounce) package rigatoni pasta
3 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 large red bell pepper, chopped
2 tablespoons fresh Italian parsley, chopped
1 (26 ounce) jar of your favorite pasta sauce

Directions:
Cook sausage links according to package directions. While sausage is cooking, boil the rigatoni according to directions. In a large pan, heat olive oil, add garlic, and saute lightly for 30 seconds. Add peppers and cook until tender but still crisp.
Coin-slice cooked sausage and combine in pan with pasta sauce and peppers and heat until warm. Pour over cooked pasta, top with fresh parsley. Serve immediately. Enjoy immensely. And welcome to Johnsonville.
