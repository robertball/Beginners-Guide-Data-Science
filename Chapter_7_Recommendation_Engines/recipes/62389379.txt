Title:
Baked Rhubarb Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh rhubarb
1/2 cup sugar
1/8 tsp ground cloves
1/2 tsp cinnamon
pinch of salt

Directions:
WAsh the rhubarb, cut into 1 inch pieces and place in a covered casserole dish.
Mix the sugar and spices together and sprinkle evenly over the rhubarb.
Bake covered at 300F for 30 - 45 minutes.
Remove from oven and stir vigorously to break the cubes down to sauce consistency.
Serve warm, chilled or room temperature.

Submitter: 
BigShotsMom
Description: 
I can't believe I didn't like this as a child!  It cooks to a sweet, tart, spicy sauce that can be eaten alone, mixed with oatmeal, warm over ice cream or to accompany pork.  My daughter even spreads it on toast.
