Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup celery, chopped
1/2/ cup green bell pepper chopped
1 clove garlic minced
4 oz can mushrooms chopped
2 8oz can tomato paste
1/2 cup ketchup
1 tbs Worcestershire sauce
pepper, oregano, basil
hot pepper sauce, parmesan cheese
8 hamburger buns

Directions:
brown beef with veggies.  drain fat.  add remaining ingredients.  simmer 15 mins. fill buns
