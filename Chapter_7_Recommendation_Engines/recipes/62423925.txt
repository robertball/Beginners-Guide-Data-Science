Title:
Ann Rigdale 13x9 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 Cup Margarine or Butter
1 Cup Cocoa
3 Cup Sugar
1/2 tsp Salt
1 tsp Vanilla
1 1/2 Cup Sour Milk (add 1tbsp vinegar to regular milk)
1 tbls baking soda
3 cups Flour
1 egg

Directions:
1.   put cocoa, marg, sugar, salt and vanilla in bowl, pour boiling water over, stir and let cool slightly.
2. Add sour milk alternately with flour and soda
3. Beat egg in last
4. Batter will be runny, but not to worry
5. Bake at 350 (180 c) for 35 - 40 min. This makes an 13 x 9 pan. (33x23 for the Aussies)

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
this makes a nice thick 13x9. 2/3 of the recipe for a thinner one.
