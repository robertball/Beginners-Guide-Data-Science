Title:
Molasses Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/531503.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C Butter/Shortening
1 C Sugar
1 C Dark Molasses
4-5 C All-Purpose Flour
2 tsp Cinnamon
2 tsp Ground Ginger
1/2 tsp Ground Cloves
1/4 tsp Ground Nutmeg
4 tsp Baking Soda
1 tsp Salt
1 Cup Sour Cream
Icing:
5-6 Cups Powdered Sugar
1 Cup Butter/Shortening
2 tsp Vanilla
2-4 Tbsp Milk

Directions:
For Cookies:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream Butter, Sugar and Molasses in a large bowl. In seperate bowl combine all dry ingredients (4 Cups Flour through Salt.)Mix dry ingredients together well.
Add flour mixture to creamed mixture all at once. Mix on low until combined. Dough will be dry and crumbly.
Add Sour Cream. Mix on medium speed until well combined and moist. Add up to one extra cup of flour until dough is dry enough to be handled.
Scoop into heaping Tablespoon portions. Roll into balls then smash down gently into disks. Place on greased cookie sheets.
Bake at 350 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes.
Allow to Cool completely before icing.
For Icing:
Cream butter and vanilla together in medium bowl. Add Powdered Sugar one cup at a time, mixing well after each cup. Mixture will become dry and crumbly.
Add Milk one Tablespoon at a time until icing becomes proper speadable consistency.
Keep covered with a moist towel until ready to use.

Submitter: 
Alicia
Description: 
Chewy delicious holiday cookie topped with a sweet icing. Grandma always makes these for the holidays!
