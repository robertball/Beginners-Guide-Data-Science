Title:
Rockin Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pd of ground beef
1 med pearl onion or your choice of onion
2 pinches of salt or your desired amt
2 tsp of garlic minced
2 small jalapenos
1 can of mushrooms
1 1/2 of bell pepper
2 pks of chili seasoning
1 can of red kidney beans
1 can of black beans
1 can of seasoned crushed tomatoes, use your choice of taste
1 cup of cheddar cheese/ or your choice of chesse
1 tsp of butter

Directions:
first rinse your ground beef and drain. cook on medium heat until golden brown, then drain and set aside. cut  your onions, jalapenos and bell pepper to your desired size, i cut small and set aside. in a skillet over medium heat add butter, when butter melts add your onions, pepper, mushrooms, jalapeno and garlic and saute for about 5 to ten minutes.
in a large soup pan , add meat,the vegetable saute mix, add two pack of seasoning mix, add can of desired flavored crushed tomatoes, can of kidney beans and can of black beans. and heat to a boil, once it starts to boil turn on medium heat for about 30 mints so the all the flavorings have time to cook together. serve and top with desired your choice of cheese and crackers.
