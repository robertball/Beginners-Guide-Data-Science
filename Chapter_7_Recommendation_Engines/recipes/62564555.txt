Title:
Quick & Easy Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T Butter
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, pressed
2T yellow curry powder
1t ground ginger
1t cumin
3 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite sized pieces
2T tomato paste
1-15 oz. can coconut milk

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet.
Add onion & garlic and saute until onions are translucent.
Add all spices.
Add chicken, stir fry until chicken is done.
Stir in tomato paste.
Add coconut milk.
Simmer 6-8 minutes, until sauce has thickened.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Andrea Patton
Description: 
I can't take credit for this one.  A fabulous curry dish from my good friend Jami!  It's quick & easy & the whole family loves it.  All of the ingredients are easy to keep on hand.
