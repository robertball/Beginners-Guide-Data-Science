Title:
Rice Protein Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 scoops rice protein powder
1 tbs flax oil or borage oil
2 tbs ground flaxseeds
Ice if desired
6-8 oz filtered water
1/2 C fresh or frozen berries
Optional:  1/4 C nuts soaked overnight, drained

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a blender and combine.
