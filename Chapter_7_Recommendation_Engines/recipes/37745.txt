Title:
Apple Walnut Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987262.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf French bread, cut into cubes (4 cups)
3 medium apples, cored, chopped
1 cup chopped walnuts or raisins (optional)
4 cups 2% milk
2 (3 ounce) packages JELL-O Vanilla Flavor Cook & Serve Pudding & Pie Filling
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Place bread cubes in lightly greased 13x9-inch baking pan. Add apples and walnuts; toss to mix well.
Pour milk into large bowl. Add pudding mixes and 1 teaspoon of the cinnamon. Beat with wire whisk 1 minute. Pour over bread mixture; sprinkle top with remaining cinnamon.
Bake 50 to 60 minutes or until mixture comes to a boil. Remove from oven. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
