Title:
Ham Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/932874.jpg
Ingredients:
Ham Ball mix:
1 1/2 lbs Ground Smoked Ham
1 1/2 lbs Ground Pork
1 C. Cracker Crumbs (Soda or Graham)
1 C. Milk
2 Eggs
1 tsp. Onion powder
Sauce:
1 1/2 C. Brown sugar
1/2 C. Water
1/2 C. Vinegar
1 T Dry Mustard

Directions:
Mix the 6 Ham Ball mix ingredients together (using hands works best). Once thoroughly mixed form 1 1/2 in. balls which should yeild approximately 24.
Mix Sauce ingredients in bowl and pour over formed balls.
Bake uncovered for 1 1/2 hrs in 350 degree oven basting with sauce mix every half hour if desired.

Submitter: 
jadaripley
Description: 
These little gems are so good! I have been making my mothers Ham loaf for special occasions for years and this is the same recipe made into balls. It has been my experience that children and adult seem to get more excited over meat balls versus meat loaf.
