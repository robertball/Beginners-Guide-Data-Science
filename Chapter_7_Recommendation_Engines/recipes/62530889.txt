Title:
Good Seasons Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4470474.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
1 pkg. Good Seasons Italian Dressing mix
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
4-6 chicken breasts

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 F.
Mix cheese, dressing mix and garlic powder.
Moisten chicken with water; coat with cheese mixture and place in shallow baking dish (or on cookie sheet).
Bake 20-25 minutes.
