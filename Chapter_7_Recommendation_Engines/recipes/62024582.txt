Title:
Easy Mexican Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
- Plain tortilla chips
-2 cans cream of chicken soup
-1 can tomatoes with green chiles, drained
-1/4 c. chopped onion
-2 t. garlic powder
-1 T. chili powder
-2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
-Lettuce and Tomato

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a retangular glass casserole dish, cover the bottom with plain tortilla chips about 2 chips deep.  Slightly crush the chips to lay flat in the dish.  Top chips with cubed chicken.  In a mixing bowl, add tomatoes, onion, soup, garlic powder, and chili powder.  Stir till well mixed.  Spread soup mixture over top of chicken.  Sprikle cheddar cheese on top and bake 30-45 minutes or until hot and bubbly throughout.  Serve with chopped lettuce and tomato on top.

Description: 
This recipe was shared with us by our neighbor years ago.  Over time, the recipe has been lost but since it is so easy, I was able to remember the basics and re-create it.  I have made it for several friends through college and it has been a big hit with all!
