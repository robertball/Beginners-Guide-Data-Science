Title:
Quick and Easier Easy Baked Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/270915.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (4 ounce) fillets tilapia
2 teaspoons high temperature olive oil
1 teaspoon Spice Mix (such as Jane's Mixed-Up Pepper Spice)
1/2 teaspoon crushed garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 lemon, sliced
1 (16 ounce) package fresh, ready cut vegetable mix with broccoli and red pepper

Directions:
Preheat the oven on broil setting. Spray a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a bowl, combine olive oil. lemon juice, salt and crushed garlic. Place the tilapia fillets in the coated pan, brush with oil mix and season with Jane's Mixed-up Pepper spice or other spice mix of your choice.
Blanch pre-cut mixed vegetables for 3 minutes, arrange around fish and drizzle lightly with oil, salt and pepper.
Broil fish for 5 minutes, turn over, brush with oil mix and broil the other side for 5 minutes.
