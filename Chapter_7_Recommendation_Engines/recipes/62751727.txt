Title:
Tortilla Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635168.jpg
Ingredients:
10 inch torillas
8 oz sour cream
8 oz cream cheese, softened
4 oz. chopped green chiles
6 oz. chopped black olives
½ - 1 cup chopped onion
Garlic and onion powder to taste
1 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
Mix ingredients except tortillas and spread on tortillas. Roll and wrap in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
Slice ½-inch thick to serve. Serve with salsa.
