Title:
Boursin Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups whipping cream
1/2 cup boursin cheese (or one 5 oz. pkg.)
3 lbs. peeled baking potatoes thinly sliced
1 1/2 Tbsp. parsley
s/p to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Butter or spray a 9x13 baking dish
In a sauce pan, stir whipping cream and boursin on medium heat until smooth
Layer 1/2 the potatoes with half the sauce
Repeat
Sprinkle with parsley
Bake for 1 hour, uncovered
