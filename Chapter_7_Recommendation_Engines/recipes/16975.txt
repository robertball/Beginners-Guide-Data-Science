Title:
Tropical Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/425266.jpg
Ingredients:
1 mango, peeled and seeded
1 papaya, peeled and seeded
1/2 cup fresh strawberries
1/3 cup orange juice
5 cubes ice

Directions:
Place the mango, papaya, strawberries, orange juice, and ice cubes in an electric blender. Process until the ingredients are smooth.
