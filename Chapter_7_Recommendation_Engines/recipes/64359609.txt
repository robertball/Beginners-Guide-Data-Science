Title:
Lemon Garlic Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5774193.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fish fillets
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 Tbsp butter, melted
1/2 tsp garlic salt
2 Tbsp onion, minced

Directions:
Thaw fish fillets.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine remaining ingredients and pour over fillets.  Bake for 25 minutes, basting occasionally.
