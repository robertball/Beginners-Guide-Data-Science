Title:
Cinco De Mayo Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
2 bags triple washed sald mix
1 red bell pepper, diced
2 ears sweet corn, kernels removed or one small can corn, drained and rinsed
1 8oz can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 pint grape tomatos, halved
grated parmesan cheese
salad croutons
Dressing:
2/3 cup chopped cilantro
1/4 cup plain yogurt
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup chopped shallots
2 anchovy fillets, chopped
1 jalapeno chili, seeded, chopped
1 tablespoon lemon juice
3/4 cup olive oil
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Dressing:
Place all dressing ingredients in blender or food processor, except olive oil, and mix until smooth.  Gradully drizzle in olive oil until blended.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Refrigerate several hours or overnight to meld flavors.
Salad:
Combine all ingredients in large serving bowl.  Toss with enough dressing to coat.
Note: Salad may be assembled the night before, however, add croutons and dressing immediately before serving.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This salad is a fixture at my annual Cinco De Mayo party, and is delicious with bar-b-que year-round.  Salud!
