Title:
Cucumber Cilantro Raita (Yogurt) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium cucumber
1 green chili-pepper or Â½ jalapeno pepper (optional)
1 Tablespoon chopped cilantro
2 cups plain yogurt (you can use nonfat or low-fat)
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Peel Cucumber, slice in half and scoop out its seeds. Dice.
Chop green chili and add all ingredients together & mix.
Set aside for 10 minutes before serving cold.
Tip: The leftover cucumber seeds also taste great marinated in a little lemon juice with salt & pepper!

Submitter: 
vburrito
Description: 
Quick & tasty Indian yogurt side dish that can help cool down any spicy meal. You can also add other veggies like onion, tomatoes & bell peppers. Very healthy too. I ate this almost everyday growing up.
