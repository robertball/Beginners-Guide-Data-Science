Title:
Vegan Eggplant Caprese Bruschetta Toasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 frozen breaded eggplant slices, heated
1/2 lb firm tomatoes, diced
16 1/2" slices wide Italian bread (about 1 loaf)
1c Red Mill TVP
7/8c water
3T capers
olive oil
garlic mill

Directions:
Prepare the TVP with the water according to package instructions.
Meanwhile, paint the Italian bread with olive oil.  Place on a grill on medium heat for about 3 minutes or until lightly browned.
Microwave the tomatoes for 3 minutes.  Drain off excess liquid.
Combine the tomatoes with the TVP and capers.
Stack one slice eggplant atop each slice of bread.  Add an equal dollop of TVP mixture to the top of each toast.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
A hybrid of bruschetta and caprese makes for a quick summertime vegan dinner.
