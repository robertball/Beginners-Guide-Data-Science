Title:
Lawry's Steak Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975887.jpg
Ingredients:
Sandwich Spread:
1/4 cup Hellmann's® or Best Foods® Real Mayonnaise
2 tablespoons steak sauce
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
Sandwiches:
2 ribeye or NY strip steaks
1/2 teaspoon Lawry's® Seasoned Salt
1/4 teaspoon Lawry's® Seasoned Pepper
1 medium red onion, cut into 4 thick slices
2 tablespoons Bertolli ® Classico™ Olive Oil
1/2 cup steak sauce
4 hoagie or sandwich rolls, split and lightly toasted
2 cups shredded Romaine lettuce
1 medium ripe tomato, sliced

Directions:
For Sandwich Spread, in small bowl, combine  ingredients; set aside.
For Sandwiches, season steak with Lawry's(R) Seasoned Salt and Lawry's(R) Seasoned Pepper. Brush onion with Olive Oil. Grill or broil steak and onion, turning once and brushing with steak sauce, until desired doneness. Cover steak loosely with aluminum foil; let stand 10 minutes. Thinly slice steak; set aside and keep warm.
Evenly spread 1/4 cup sandwich spread on bottom of rolls. Top with lettuce, tomato, sliced steak and onion. Drizzle with remaining sauce, then top with remaining rolls.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
These big steak sandwiches slathered in a creamy special sauce are ready in no time.
