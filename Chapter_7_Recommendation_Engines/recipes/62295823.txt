Title:
MOMMA-PRENEUR's MILE HIGH DENVER OVEN OMLETE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices of bacon, fried crispy & crumbled
3 slices Co-Jack cheese
3 slices American cheese
8 Eggs, beaten
1 C. Milk
1/4 C. (total) green, yellow, red pepper & onion
6-8 Grape tomatoes (cut into small pieces)

Directions:
Generously butter a 9-inch pie plate. Saute all the veggies together with a little butter.  In a large mixing bowl beat eggs and milk briskly. Add sauteed veggie/bacon mix to eggs. Then add tiny amount (just enough to coat bottom of pie plate)with egg mixture. Lay cheese over that (tear to fit). Pour remaining egg mixture. Bake @ 350 degree oven for 30 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes, cut into pie pieces and serve. Enjoy!
