Title:
Fruited Oat Scone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1 1/4 cups oats
1/4 cup sugar
1 Tbsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/3 cup butter
1 1/3 cup diced dried fruit (6 oz package)
1/2 cup milk
1 egg, lightly beaten
sugar / cinnamon

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Cut in the butter with a pastry cutter.
Stir in the fruit.
Add milk and egg.
Mix just until moist.
Shape dough into a ball. Turn onto floured surface. Gently knead 6 times.
On lightly greased cookie sheet, pat dough into an 8-inch circle. Score with knift into 8 wedges. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
Bake 20-25 minutes at 375 degrees until golden brown.
Serve warm.
