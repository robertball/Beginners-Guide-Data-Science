Title:
Pablo's Quick and Easy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground sirloin
1 package chili-o seasoning mix
1 medium onion chopped
1 14.5 oz. can diced tomotoes undrained
1/2 15 oz. can tomato sauce
1 can black beans
1 tbspn cocoa
1 tspn salt
1 cup water

Directions:
Brown ground sirloin in a pot along with onion then drain.
Add remaining ingredients, black beans to taste (I usually use 1/2 can, approx 7-8 oz.) and heat on medium until simmering.
Reduce heat to med-low and simmer for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Tarco
Description: 
A quick and easy chili recipe for those days when you don't have the time to do it right. The cocoa and black beans are ingredients that I borrowed from a couple of my other favorite chili recipes.
