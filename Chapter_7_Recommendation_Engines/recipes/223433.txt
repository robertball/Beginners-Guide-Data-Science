Title:
Gourmet Gelly Shots: Berry Berry Good Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup boiling water
1 (6 ounce) package mixed berry-flavored Jell-O® mix
1 cup raspberry-flavored vodka (such as Smirnoff®)

Directions:
Arrange 12 2-ounce plastic cups on a baking sheet.
Slowly pour boiling water over gelatin mix in a bowl while whisking. Continue whisking while streaming raspberry-flavored vodka into the gelatin mixture. Pour mixture into plastic cups.
Refrigerate until set, 8 hours to overnight.
