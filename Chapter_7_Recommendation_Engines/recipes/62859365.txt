Title:
DOGS AND TORTILLAS   (*/+) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/662962.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large can chili with beans
1 can chili without beans
10 hot dogs
10 tortillas
2 pkg shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c. chopped onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Cook dogs in butter in frying pan and drain.
Cook onions in the butter left in the pan.
Spray Pam in a 9x13 baking dish.
Spread 1/2 of both cans of chili in bottom of dish.
Roll dogs up in tortillas and place seam side down in dish.
Sprinkle with onions.
Sprinkle with 1/2 the cheese.
Put the rest of the two cans of chili on top.
Sprinkle with the rest of the cheese.
Cover with foil and cook 30 minutes.
Take foil off and cook or broil for a few minutes to brown.
