Title:
Delicious Italian Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken cutlets - trimmed and cleaned
1- 32oz. box of chicken stock
3-4 chicken bouillon cubes
1 medium onion - chopped
4 cloves of garlic - halved
1 tsp. garlic salt
2-3 cups of water
6 oz. diced canned tomatoes with juice
1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning
1 cup carrots - diced
1 cup celery - chopped
4 oz. frozen spinach - slightly thawed
3 scallions - sliced into rounds
1 tbsp. dried or 2 tbsp. fresh, chopped parsley
8 oz. of cooked small pasta of your choice
Fresh grated Parmesan cheese - optional
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pour stock into large pot and add cleaned chicken breasts. Add enough water to almost fill pot, then add bouillon cubes, onion, garlic, garlic salt and pepper.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer covered for 30-45 minutes or until chicken is tender.
Remove from heat and take chicken out of pot and cover to keep warm.
Strain broth and return to pot.
Add carrot, celery, diced tomato, scallions, Italian seasoning and salt and pepper if needed.
Simmer until vegetables are tender.
Shred chicken while soup is simmering.
When vegetable are tender, add chicken, spinach and parsley to soup.
Check seasoning and adjust as desired.
To serve, place a portion of pasta in bowl and ladle soup over the pasta. Top with Parmesan cheese if desired and serve with a nice crusty bread!!
