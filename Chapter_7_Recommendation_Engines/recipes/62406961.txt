Title:
Wild Rice Casesrole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C wild rice
3 C bullion
1 C water
1/2 C chopped onion
1/2 C chopped green pepper
1C chopped mushrooms
1/2 C soft butter
1 C Cream

Directions:
Soak wild rice for 6 hours and drain well. Boil rice in boullion and water until liquid is absorbed. Saute onion, pepper and mushrooms in all but 1 T butter. Mix rice with sauteed ingredients and stir in cream and remaining butter. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Cover and bake at 325 for 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
