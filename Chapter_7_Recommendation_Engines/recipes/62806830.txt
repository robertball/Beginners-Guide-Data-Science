Title:
Banana Nut Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 pecans, chopped
9 red grapes, cut in half
3 well rounded teaspoons peanut butter
1 banana, mashed

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Stir well. Refrigerate for 10 minutes.  Ready to serve.
