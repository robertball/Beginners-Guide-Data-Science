Title:
Bratton Holiday Wassail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Apple Cider
Pineapple Juice
2 cinnamon sticks
1 Tbsp allspice
1 Tbsp cloves
1 Tbsp Redhots (small balls of cinnamon candy)
1 cup strong tea (peppermint, chai, whatever)
5" square piece of Cheesecloth

Directions:
1.	Add equal amounts of cider and juice along with the tea into the crock-pot.
2.	Wrap remaining ingredients in a cheesecloth and add to liquids.
3.	Heat in the crock-pot a few hours.
