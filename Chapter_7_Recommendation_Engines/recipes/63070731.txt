Title:
Herbed Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886986.jpg
Ingredients:
12 hard-cooked eggs
4 tablespoons chives, minced
4 tablespoons plain yogurt
4 tablespoons mayonnaise
2 tablespoon fresh tarragon, chopped
2 tablespoon fresh parsley, minced
5 teaspoons mustard
salt and pepper to taste
paprika

Directions:
Cut eggs in half lengthwise; remove yolks and set whites aside.
In a small bowl, mash yolks.
Stir in the remaining ingredients except for the paprika.
Pipe or stuff into egg whites.
Sprinkle paprika over the top of each egg.
Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
