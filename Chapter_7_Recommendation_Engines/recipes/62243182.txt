Title:
Easy Southwest Egg Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/328383.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole pre-cooked chicken
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 tablespoons minced green onion
2 tablespoons minced red pepper
1 cup chipotle salsa
1/2 cup frozen corn kernels
1/3 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
1/3 teaspoon salt
1/3 teaspoon pepper
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 package of egg roll wrappers
2 cups oil for frying

Directions:
Remove cooked chicken from bone and set aside in large mixing bowl.
Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Stir in green onion and red pepper. Cook and stir 5 minutes, until tender.
Shred chicken and mix into the pan with onion and red pepper.
Mix corn, black beans, parsley, salt and pepper. Cook and stir 5 minutes, until well blended and tender.
Transfer mixture to large mixing bowl and add Monterey Jack cheese and salsa.
Using egg roll wrappers, spoon even amounts of the mixture into each wrapper. Fold ends of wrapper, then roll tightly around mixture as instructed on egg roll wrapper package.
In a large, deep skillet, heat oil for deep frying to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Deep fry stuffed egg rolls 5 minutes each, or until dark golden brown. Turn half way through. Drain on paper towels before serving.
