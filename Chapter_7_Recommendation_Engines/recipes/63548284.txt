Title:
Pan Roasted Brussel Sprouts with Gorgonzola Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bag Brussel sprouts, cleaned and halved
2 Tbs butter
1/3 Cup gorgonzola cheese crumbles
1/4 tsp salt (to taste)
1/4 tsp pepper (to taste)

Directions:
Heat large pan over medium high heat.
Add butter and melt.
Add brussel sprouts and coat with butter.
Cover pan with lid and turn heat down to medium.
Stir brussel sprouts every 3-4 minutes allowing sides to brown.
Cook brussel sprouts until tender all the way through, about 8-10 minutes (Depending on size).
Sprinkle gorgonzola cheese evenly over brussel sprouts, stir and serve immediately

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
Savory brussel sprouts all dressed up! This recipe calls for very few ingredients but creates a punch of flavor your sure to love.
