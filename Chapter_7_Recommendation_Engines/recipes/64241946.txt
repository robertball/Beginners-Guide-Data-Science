Title:
Cherry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans Cherry Pie filling
White choc chips
1 box yellow cake mix
1 and 1/2 sticks of butter
1 cup Crushed pecans
Cinnamon sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Grease casserole dish, pour cherry pie filling evenly. Sprinkle white chocolate chips on top. Pour yellow cake mix evenly over cherries. Pour cake mix evenly over all, then pour melted butter on top. Sprinkle with pecans, then cinnamon sugar. Bake for 50 min.
