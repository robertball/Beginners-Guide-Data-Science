Title:
Chicken Pho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken thighs
8 cups water
Chicken bullion granules to taste
1 onion, thinly sliced
1 tsp minced garlic
1 package Pho noodles
Cilantro
Thai Basil
jalapeno (thinly sliced)
bean sprouts
green onions (sliced)
lime wedges
chili sauce (Sriracha)

Directions:
Boil the chicken in a stockpot along with the onions and garlic.
After the chicken has cooked through, remove it from the pan and chop.
Season the broth to taste with the bullion.
Add the chicken back to the pan.
Boil and drain the noodles in a separate pan, according to package directions.
Add noodles to each bowl. Top with chicken and broth.
Garnish with cilantro, Thai basil, green onions, bean sprouts, jalapeno, a squeeze of lime juice, and chili sauce.

Submitter: 
Nyki
Description: 
Vietnamese chicken noodle soup! So yummy on a cold day, without being too heavy. It's also super easy!
