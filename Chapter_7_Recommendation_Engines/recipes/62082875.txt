Title:
Strawberry Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup powdered sugar
10 ounces fresh (or frozen) strawberries

Directions:
Clean and hull strawberries.  Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
GLAMM
Description: 
My family had a large strawberry farm when I was growing up, so we had tons of strawberry recipes.  This one is delicious -- especially with fresh homemade bread.
