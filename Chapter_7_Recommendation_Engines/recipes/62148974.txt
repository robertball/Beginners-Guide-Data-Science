Title:
Tortellini Picnic Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages cheese filled tortellinis
1 large tub of Crumbled feta Cheese
1 Can Black Olives
3 Roma Tomatoes (Diced)
5 cloves chopped Garlic
1 bunch green onions-sliced
1/2 Cup Chopped Walnuts
Sauce:
1 Cup cup white vinegar
3/5 cup Olive Oil
1 teaspoon Basil
Salt to Taste

Directions:
Cook Tortellini's as directed on the package, drain and let cool for 30 minutes.
In a Large bowl mix Tortellinis,Feta Cheese,Olives,Tomatoes,Garlic, onions and walnuts. Mix Well.
In a smaller bowl mix White Vinegar, Olive Oil and Basil.
Pour Sauce mix in with other ingredients. Mix well. Refridgerate one hour. ENJOY!!
