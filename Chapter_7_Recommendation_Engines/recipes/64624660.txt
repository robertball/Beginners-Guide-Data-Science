Title:
Mango Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen mango
1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger

Directions:
Add ingredients to the single serving Ninja blender cup in the order of yogurt, mango, ginger.
Pulse 3-4 times and then blend until pureed. Serve immediately.
