Title:
Mark's Micheladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1023386.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz ice cold beer
3 oz lime juice
2-3 dashes Worcestershire sauce
2-3 dashes Frank's Redhot sauce
1 lime
Tajin Fruit Seasoning

Directions:
Using a lime wedge, moisten the rim of a glass.
Rim glass with Tajin seasoning.
Add enough ice to fill glass 1/2 full.
Add lime juice, Worcestershire and hot sauce to glass.
Pour beer into glass, and stir.
Lick Tajin from side of glass, and sip drink though straw.
Say " that's good!"

Submitter: 
Mark Adams
Description: 
My absolute favorite drink for cooking out in the summer.
