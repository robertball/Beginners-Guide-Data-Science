Title:
Mrs. Abbott's Lima Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of frozen fordhook limas
1 pint sour cream
1 tsp dry mustard
1 TBS sugar
salt and pepperto taste
2 garlic cloves on toothpicks

Directions:
Precook limas as directed on bag. Drain well.
Combine sour cream, mustard, sugar, salt, pepper
Add limas.
Put bean mixture in casserole lightly sprayed with Pam
Insert two garlic cloves into center.
Bake slowly @250 degrees for two hours.
Remove garlic cloves and serve.

Submitter: 
Anne Bauer-Wright
Description: 
Fordhook lima beans baked slowly in sour cream.  Delicious with grilled meats and chicken.
