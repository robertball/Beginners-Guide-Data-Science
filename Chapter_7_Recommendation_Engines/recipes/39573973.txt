Title:
Pasta Caprese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# campanela pasta or other short pasta
1 1/2# fresh tomatoes,cored,seeded,cut into dices
1/4 C good olive oil
small shallot, finely chopped
small clove garlic, finely chopped
12 oz fresh mozzarella cheese
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 C fresh basil, chopped
2-4 T fresh lemon juice (see note above)
1 t sugar (see note above)

Directions:
Whisk oil, 2 t. lemon juice, garlic, shallot, 1/2 t.salt, and 1/4 t. peper together in large bowl. Add tomatoes and gently toss to combine; set aside. Let set 1/2 hr.,but no more than 45 min.
Cut cheese into dices and freeze for about 10 min. until slightly firm. Bring 4 qts. water to rolling boil in stockpot. Add 1 T. salt and pasta, stir to separate,and cook until al dente. Drain well.
Add tomatoes and cheese to pasta and gently toss. Let stand for 5 min. Stir in basil; adjust seasonings with salt, pepper, and additional lemon juice or sugar, if desired, and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
This recipe comes from America's Test Kitchen. It's delicious! Depending on the ripeness of the tomatoes, additional lemon juice or up to 1 t. sugar can be added at the end to taste.
