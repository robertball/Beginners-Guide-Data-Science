Title:
Reese's Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box choc cake mix
16 oz sour cream
3 eggs
Bag of mini Reese's PB cups

Directions:
Mix all together, very sticky but it works.
Take mini-reeses peanut butter cups and cut into ½'s. Put ½ batter in 3 qt w/cone.
Put as many Reese 1/2 's as you can around cone but don't touch the cone or the sides.
Put rest of cake mix.
Let set 5 minutes.
Microwave on high for 12 minutes.
Let set a bit before you invert it.
