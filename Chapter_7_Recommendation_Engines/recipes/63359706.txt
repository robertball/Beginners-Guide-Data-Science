Title:
Wheat Germ Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs, lightly beaten
1/3 cup canola oil
1 quart buttermilk
4 teaspoons baking soda
1 cup wheat germ
1 teaspoon salt
3 cups whole wheat pastry or white whole wheat flour

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix eggs with oil and buttermilk. Stir in baking soda, wheat germ, salt and flour; mix until blended.
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides, turning once.

Submitter: 
Melissa Gardner
Description: 
Pairs wheat germ and whole-wheat pastry flour for a healthy, wholesome breakfast treat.Modified from: Wheat Germ Whole-Wheat Buttermilk Pancakes
