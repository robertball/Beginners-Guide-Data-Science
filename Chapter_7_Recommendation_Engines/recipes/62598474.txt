Title:
Heavenly hashbrown quick-ish quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 russet potatoes, washed, peeled, grated, and squeezed of as much liquid as possible.
1/4 c. melted butter
1 c. cooked, chopped ham
1 med. bell pepper, chopped
1/2 red onion, finely-chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 4 oz. can diced green chiles, drained
1 7.5 oz. can sliced mushrooms, drained (optional)
1/2 c. cooked, chopped broccoli (optional)
5 eggs
1/2 c. milk
1/2 tsp. salt
1 TB brown mustard
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1 1/2 - 2 c. grated sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 deg.
Pat grated potatoes into an 8X8 pan, or 9" pie plate. Press up the sides to form a "crust." Drizzle butter over the potatoes. Bake for 25 min. or until browned.
Meanwhile, lightly-saute onion, bell peppers and garlic in 1 tsp. EVOO. Mix in ham, and remaining veggies.
Reduce temp. to 350 deg., remove potato-pan and spread ham/veggies over crustt.
Whisk eggs, milk, and seasoning in sep. bowl and pour over ham/veggie layer. Sprinkle w/ cheese and bake covered for 15 min., uncovered for 10-15 min. so that cheese is bubbling, and knife inserted in center comes out clean.
