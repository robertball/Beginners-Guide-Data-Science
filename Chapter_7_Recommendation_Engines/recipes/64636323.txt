Title:
Princess Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds potatoes, peeled and cubed
2 tsp salt
1/2 cup butter
3 oz sour cream
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese, divided
1 bunch green onions, finely chopped
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
2 Tbsp milk or cream
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Boil potatoes in salted water until tender.  Mash with butter and sour cream.  Beat until smooth and add 1/4 cup cheddar cheese, onions,  Parmesan cheese, and salt.  Spread in a greased 9x9 square pan.  Bake for 40 minutes.  Spread remaining cheese on top and bake an additional 5 minutes.
This can be made ahead a day or so in advance.  Cover and chill in refrigerator.  When ready to serve, let stand at room temperature for 30 minutes before baking.

Submitter: 
Jacqueline Bedsaul Johnson
Description: 
Creamy mashed potato and cheese dish.  The perfect side to a prime rib roast.
