Title:
Ham Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 T mustard
4 T flour
1/2 cup pineapple juice

Directions:
remove ham from oven.  Increase pven to 400 degrees.  Pour 1/2 of mixture over scored ham.  Return to oven for 20 minutes.  Remove and pour on remander of mixture.  Decorate with pineapple slices and bake until golden brown.
