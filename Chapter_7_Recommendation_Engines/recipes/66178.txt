Title:
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies Treats® Holiday Lights Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979680.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package marshmallows
6 cups Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal
Food coloring
String licorice
Prepared frosting
Small round candies

Directions:
In a large microwave-safe bowl, melt margarine and marshmallows on HIGH for 2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute. Stir mixture until smooth. Divide marshmallow mixture into four or five portions. Add a few drops of food coloring to each marshmallow portion. Stir until well blended.
Divide Kellogg's(R) Rice Krispies(R) cereal into four or five portions and add to the colored marshmallow mixtures, stirring until well coated. Let cereal mixture slightly cool. With buttered hands, shape cereal mixture into cereal holiday lights and plug. Place on waxed paper.
Shape licorice into circle to form holiday lights wire. Attach cereal lights and plug to licorice with frosting. Decorate as desired.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
Make your own holiday lights! These Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal creations are fun and easy to make, and are a great holiday activity for the whole family.
