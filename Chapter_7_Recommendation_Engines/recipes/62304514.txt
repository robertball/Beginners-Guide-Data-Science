Title:
Mixed Vegetables with Noodles/chap ch'ae Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T. soy sauce, divided
2 tsp. sugar, divided
1/2 tsp. minced garlic, divided
4 tsp. toasted sesame seeds, divided **Toast sesame seeds in a dry hot pan, stirring constantly, for 3-4 minutes, till seeds are golden.
4 oz. firm tofu, cut into 3/4 -inch pieces
6 T. oil (safflower)
8 oz. fresh mushrooms (pref. Shiitake), sliced
1 large onion, chopped
3 to 4 medium carrots, cut into thin 2-inch strips
(other veggies and less carrots, if desired, like red peppers, zucchini)
1 c. bean sprouts
1/2 cup chopped fresh spinach
5 oz. cellophane (or other) noodles, cooked
5 tsp. sesame oil (important!)

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine 2 T. soy sauce, 1 tsp. sugar, garlic, 2 tsp. sesame seeds, and tofu. Set aside.
In a large frying pan or wok, heat 1 T. oil over high heat for 1 minute.  Add tofu and fry, stirring frequently, for 2 to 3 minutes.  Place tofu in large covered bowl.
In same pan, heate another T. oil and add mushrooms.  Cook, stirring, for 1 minute, or until soft.  Add to tofu.  Repeat with remaining vegetables, cooking each one separately and adding to bowl.
Cut noodles into 3-inch lengths and add to bowl.
Add 2 T. soy sauce, 1 tsp. sugar, 2 tsp. sesame seeds, and the 5 tsp. sesame oil to the mixture and mix thoroughly.  Serve warm.
