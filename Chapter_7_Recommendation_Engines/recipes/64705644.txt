Title:
Keto Vanilla Cinnamon Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pecans finely ground
1 cup Almond flour
1/2 cup Coconut flour
1 egg
1/2 tsp salt
1 stick butter
1/4 cup Erythritol
1/4 tsp Nutmeg
1 tsp Cinnamon
1 tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Chop pecans finely and mix with almond flour in skillet then heat over medium heat until golden and fragrant (3-6 minutes).
Remove from heat and whisk in salt nutmeg and cinnamon and let mixture cool.
Cream butter with Erythritol and add Vanilla and egg.
Mix butter mixture with flour mixture .
Form dough into a roll and wrap in Saran cling wrap and refrigerate for one hour or freeze for later use.
Divide dough into 24 cookies and place on cookie sheet that has been lined with parchment paper.
Bake for 12-15 minutes at 350 degrees
