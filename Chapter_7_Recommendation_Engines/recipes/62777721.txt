Title:
Peach Cake II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs,(ex-large or jumbo), beaten
1 cup white sugar + 3/4 cup applesauce
1/2 cup vegetable oil + 1/2 cup applesauce
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon baking soda
2+ cups fresh peaches - UN-peeled, pitted and sliced
1 cup sliced strawberries (or blueberries)
1/2 cup chopped pecans (optional)
unsalted butter and cooking spray for cakepans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 365 degrees F.
Spray two 9" cake pans with cooking spray, then lightly grease with unsalted butter and dust w/flour
In a large bowl, combine the eggs, sugar+applesauce, oil+applesauce, flour, salt, cinnamon, baking soda, peaches and pecans. Mix thoroughly by hand.
Pour into prepared 9x13 inch pan and bake at 365 degrees F for 30 minutes or until done.

Submitter:

Description: 
Fantastic cake that the whole family loved.  Though I made some lighter vanilla icing, this cake needs none, and we really preferred it without. Moist and a real treat, all the better because it's flexible, forgiving, and so quick to make.Modified from: Peach Cake II
