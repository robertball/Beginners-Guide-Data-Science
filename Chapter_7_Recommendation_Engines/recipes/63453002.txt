Title:
Miso, Chicken & Green Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked lean chicken breast or turkey breast or tofu
1 cooking onion
5 green onions chopped
1 1/2 cups portobello mushrooms sliced
8 cups chix/veg broth
2 tbsp miso
Sea salt & black pepper

Directions:
1. Large skillet - onions until golden brown - add mushrooms 5 minutes 1/2 cup of cooking liquid - let liquid almost disappear
2. whisk miso w/2 tbsp broth, pour into skillet - salt & pepper - add meat cook for 30 minutes over low heat
3. garnish with green onion
