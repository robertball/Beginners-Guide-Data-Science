Title:
Southern Ireland Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/994700.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces Irish cream liqueur (such as Baileys®)
1 1/2 fluid ounces peach-flavored bourbon liqueur (such as Southern Comfort ®)

Directions:
Mix Irish cream liqueur with bourbon liqueur in a small glass.

Submitter: 
KEHUGGINS2
Description: 
I prefer to mix this in a martini shaker over ice to make it very cold before serving. Mix together and serve as a shooter, or double amount and serve over ice. If you prefer it warm, you can stir it into hot chocolate and top with whipped cream. YUM!
