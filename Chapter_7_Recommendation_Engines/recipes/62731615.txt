Title:
Alioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Angel hair pasta
1 lb Chicken breasts grilled and chuncked
Fresh chopped tomatoes
6-8 frsh cloves garlic
Melted butter mixed with olive oil
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook past and chicken.
Toss pasta w butter/ olive oil mix and parmesan cheese.
Add garlic ( chopped or minced) and chicken.
Add tomatoes last
Salt and pepper to taste.
