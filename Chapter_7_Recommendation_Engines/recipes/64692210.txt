Title:
Stuffed Chicken Tomato Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6                          Chicken Breasts (Cut in Half and Tenderized) Season to taste
1/2                       Cup Parmesan Cheese
2 Jars (24 Oz)  Spaghetti Sauce (Prego)
1 lb                      Mozzarella Cheese
2 bags                 No Yolk Noodles
1 Lg                      Cottage Cheese
1                            Egg
(Use these to season chicken)
Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herb
Onion Powder
Garlic Powder
No salt

Directions:
1.  Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
2.  Brown Chicken
3.   Cook Egg Noodles to package directions
4.   Mix Cottage Cheese, Egg, and Parmesan Cheese put aside
5.   Put half of the sauce on bottom of lasagna pan
6.   Place chicken in same pan
7.   Add Noodles on top of chicken
8.   Add Cottage Cheese Mixture as next layer
9.   Add Mozzarella Cheese on top

Submitter: 
Michelle Hughes
Description: 
A variation of your favorite lasagna with delicious chicken, cottage cheese, and spaghetti sauce!
