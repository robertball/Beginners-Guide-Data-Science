Title:
Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SALAD:
250 gm bacon
500 gm broccoli florets
1 small onion, chopped
1 cup raisins
1 cup sunflower seeds
DRESSING:
3 Tbsp lemon juice
3/4 cup oil
2 tsp sugar
3/4 tsp mustard powder
3/4 tsp celery salt

Directions:
Fry bacon until crisp and crumble.
In large bowl, mix crumbled bacon with remaining salad ingredients.
Mix dressing ingredients and pour over salad.  Toss lightly and refrigerate 2-3 hours.  (You can use blanched broccoli- 1 minute)
