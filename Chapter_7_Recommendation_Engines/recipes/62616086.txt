Title:
Heavenly Caramel Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14oz) bag caramels (approx. 50 pieces)
2/3C half-and-half or light cream

Directions:
Melt caramels with half-and-half in large heavy saucepan on low heat, stirring frequently.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
With only 2 ingred, this is quickly made.  Serve warm or at room temp as a dip with fruit slices, cookies, angel food cake cubes, etc.
