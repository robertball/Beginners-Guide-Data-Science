Title:
Nutty Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart organic broth
1 1/2 cups filtered water
2 cups cashews
2 small onions, chopped
3 T olive oil
2 tsp marjoram
2 tsp thyme
1 T minced chives (for garnish)

Directions:
Grind the nuts in a coffee grinder until very fine.
In a large skillet, saute the onions in oil on medium-high heat until translucent.  Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly.
Transfer the nuts and onions to a blender with remaining ingredients and blend until smooth.
Transfer to a soup pot and simmer on medium heat for 20-30 minutes. Serve warm, garnished with minced chives.
