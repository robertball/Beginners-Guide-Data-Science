Title:
Juicy Pork Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2411924.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/4 cup dry breadcrumbs
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp sodium-reduced soy sauce
1 tbsp water
1 tsp minced ginger root
1 tsp hot pepper sauce
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 lb (454 g) lean ground pork
2 green onions, minced
4 hamburger buns

Directions:
In a bowl mix together all ingredients using your hands except buns. Shape into four 1/2-inch thick patties.
Place on greased grill over medium-high heat; close lid and grill, turning once, 10 to 12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
These are the juiciest burgers I have made yet and people love them. A great alternative for those who do not want beef
