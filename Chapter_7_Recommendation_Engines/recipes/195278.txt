Title:
Italian-Style Saucy Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991892.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds red potatoes, cut into bite-size chunks
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 (26 ounce) jar Ragu速 Chunky Pasta Sauce or Ragu速 Robusto!速 Pasta Sauce
1/2 cup shredded Parmesan or mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. In 13 x 9-inch baking dish, combine potatoes, olive oil and salt. Bake 30 minutes.
Spread sauce over potatoes, then sprinkle with cheese. Bake an additional 10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
