Title:
Slow Cook 3 Bean Chili- Vegetarian and GF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. Dried Kidney Beans
8 oz. Dried Lentils
8 oz. Dried Black Beans
1 Can Crushed Tomatoes
1 Can Tomato Paste
1 Can Kernel Corn
8 Cups Water
2 Tb Chili Powder
1 Tb Cumin
1 Tsp Paprika
1/2 Cup Sugar
2 Tb Olive Oil
3 Medium Onions
3 Cloves of Garlic

Directions:
1. Combine all the ingredients (except the oil, onions and garlic) together in a slow cooker.
2. Put slow cooker on high for 6 hours.
3. Saut√© the onion, garlic and oil. Add to the slow cooker when done.
4.Stir periodically. Depending on how tender the beans are you may want to leave it on low for another 4-6 hours.

Description: 
This is a wonderfully filling (high protein) yet yummy 3 Bean Gluten Free and Vegetarian Chili.
