Title:
Aquarium Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986490.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package JELL-O Brand Berry Blue Flavor Gelatin
3/4 cup boiling water
1/2 cup cold water
ice cubes
1/2 cup chopped strawberries
1 (4 ounce) package gummy fish

Directions:
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water completely.  Combine cold water and ice cubes to make 1-1/4 cups ice and water.  Add to gelatin, stirring until slightly thickened.  Remove any unmelted ice.  If mixture is still thin, refrigerate until slightly thickened.
Place fruit on bottom of 4 dessert dishes.  Pour thickened gelatin over fruit.  Suspend gummy fish in gelatin.
Refrigerate until set, about 1 hour.  Garnish with additional gummy fish, if desired.
