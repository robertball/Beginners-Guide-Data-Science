Title:
Creamy Avocado Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 avocado
1/2 lemon (juiced)
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 cup Fresh Basil
2 tbsp canola oil
6 oz of pasta
Pinch of pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package.
Add garlic powder, lemon juice, and oil into food processor.
Process until smooth.
Add potted avocado and basil. Process until smooth
When pasta is done cooking, pour on sauce and toss.
Garnish with lemon zest and black pepper.
Serve immediately.

Submitter:

Description: 
Creamy, thick, and rich with lots of garlic flavor and a hint of lemon.
