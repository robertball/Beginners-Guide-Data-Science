Title:
Southwest Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup onions chopped
2-1/2 cups skim milk
1-1/3 cup diced tomatoes and green chilies (can)
4oz monterey jack cheese, diced
8oz corn muffin mix
8oz Turkey Italian Sausage
3 pieces of LoCal wheat, thinly sliced
1tsp cumin
1/4tsp pepper

Directions:
mix corn muffin mix according to package directions. Let muffins cool. Crumble muffins into a large bowl. Stir in bread, set aside.
Cook sausage until done. Add onion and saute. Drain and set aside.
Combine milk, cumin, pepper and tomatoes in a bowl and mix well. Add the sausage and bread mixture.
Spray 11x7 baking dish with non-stick cooking spray. Spoon half of mixture into pan. Sprinkle with 1/2 the cheese. Repeat the steps.
Refrigerate overnight. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
