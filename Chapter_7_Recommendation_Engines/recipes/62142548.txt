Title:
Chai Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 butter flavored shortening
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1/4 cup cold chai tea (no milk added)
1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 cup sifted powered sugar

Directions:
1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
2. Mix together throughly the shortening, brown sugar, and egg.  Stir in the cold chai tea.  Sift together and then blend in the flour, baking soda, salt, nutmeg, and cinnamon.
3. Once dough is mixed throughly, cover and chill in the fridge for at least 1 hour.
4. Measure 1 tbsp. of dough and roll into ball, then roll dough ball in the sifted powdered sugar.  Place powdered sugar dough balls onto ungreased baking sheet about 2" apart.  Bake at 400 degrees for 8-10 minutes; until, when touched lightly with finger, almost no imprint remains.
