Title:
Greek Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
Juice of 1/2 large lemon
1 heaping teaspoons garlic powder
1 heaping teaspoons dried basil
1 heaping teaspoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1.5 teaspoon white sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 package cooked rotini pasta
1 cups fresh sliced mushrooms
2 tomatoes, diced
1/2 red or green pepper, diced
3/4 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup chopped green onions
1/2 (4 ounce) can whole black olives
Cooked, diced chicken (opt)

Directions:
Combine dressing ingredients, and toss with the rest of it (except chicken)! Chill overnight, and eat topped with warm chicken.
