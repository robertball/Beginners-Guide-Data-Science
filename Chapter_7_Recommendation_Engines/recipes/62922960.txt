Title:
Lulu's Slow Cooker Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet of Lipton Onion & Mushroom soup mix
3 or 4 cups of beef broth
1 can of tomato paste
1 lb. of beef stew meat
2 cups of cooked pasta (elbows preferred)
Flour for dredging
Garlic Powder, Salt and Pepper for dredging the meat
1 can of mushrooms
1 onion, diced
1 potato, cubed
1/2 package of frozen peas and carrots
Spicy Steak Seasoning
Red Wine Vinegar
Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Boil water for pasta, cook until al dente
Cook frozen peas and carrots according to instructions, put in slow cooker
Dredge stew meat in flour, salt, garlic powder and pepper, set aside
Chop onions and potato
Open can of mushrooms, put them in slow cooker
Heat up EVOO in skillet and brown stew meat in skillet with Worcestershire sauce and red wine vinegar (red wine if you have on hand)
When meat is cooked through, pour into skillet, but keep the drippings in the pan
Cook tomato paste, onions and potatoes in the meat drippings
Pour cooked veggies into crock pot
Pour beef broth into crockpot with steak seasoning
Cook on low for 5 - 8 hours.

Submitter: 
LaurenSyd
Description: 
Slow cooker beef stew, serve with bread or biscuits. Okay to cook in pot as well.
