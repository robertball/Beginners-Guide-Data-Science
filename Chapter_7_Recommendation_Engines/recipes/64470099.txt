Title:
Dinomite’s Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2463511.jpg
Ingredients:
Corn Bread
Kale (steamed + chopped) or 5 oz of frozen spinach
4oz Cream Cheese
1/4 Sweet Onion

Directions:
If using fresh kale, boil or sauté until tender. If using spinach, thaw and drain well, squeezing off excess moisture. Chop finely and add to large mixing bowl.
Add sautéed onions and softened cream cheese, then cream together with a fork.
Bake up a fresh batch of corn bread, or use 2-3 muffins. Crumble and add to mixing bowl, being sure to fully incorporate all ingredients.
Shape with a melon baller, spoon or by hand.
They care be eaten as-is, or pan/deep fried.  Optional ingredients:
Parmesan or Reggiano cheese
Panko (for breading and frying)
Bacon!
