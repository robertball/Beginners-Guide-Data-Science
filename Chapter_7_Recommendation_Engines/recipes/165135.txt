Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Pole Vault Triple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985058.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
6 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
32 regular-size marshmallows
32 fresh strawberries

Directions:
In a large microwave safe bowl, heat margarine and marshmallows at HIGH for 3 minutes, stirring after 2 minutes.
Stir in vanilla and KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal until well coated. Using lightly buttered spatula, press into buttered 3.5 L (13 x 9-inch) pan and let cool. Cut into 4 cm squares.
On each 36 cm skewer, alternately thread a square, a marshmallow, and a strawberry, ending with a square.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
These sweet 'kebabs' with cubes of KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Treats, fresh strawberries, and marshmallows are a special snack for after school or anytime.
