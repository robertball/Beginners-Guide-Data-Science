Title:
Ginger cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4C shortening
1 egg
1tsp ground cloves
1tsp cinnamon
2tsp baking soda
1c sugar
4tsp molasses
1tsp ginger
1/2tsp salt
2C flour

Directions:
Cream the shortening and sugar. Then add the egg molasses and dry ingredients. Roll into small balls. Bake at 350 for 10-12min
