Title:
Buttermilk Donuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/3 c all purpose flour
1 cup sugar
2 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
1/2 t cinnamon
1/4 t nutmeg
2 T shortening
2 eggs
3/4 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oil in deepfryer to 375
In large bowl, combine 1 1/2 cup flour and all other ingredients.
Blend 30 seconds on low speed, scraping bowl constantly
Beat 2 minutes at medium speed, scraping bowl occassionally.
Stir in remaining flour
Turn out onto well floured board and gently roll dough 3/8" thick.
Cut with floured donut cutter.
Slide into hot fat with wide spatula, turning as they rise to surface.
Fry 2 to 3 minutes then drain.
Serve plain, sugared, or frosted
