Title:
Ham and Cheese Crescent Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can PillsburyÂ® Refrigerated Crescent Dinner Rolls
8 thin slices  cooked ham
4 thin slices  Swiss cheese, cut into strips
or low fat Mozzerella
Honey mustard (optional)
Apples 2-3 cut into cubes
Brown Sugar
Apple Jelly

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Separate dough into 8 triangles. Spread a little honey mustard on each triangle. Place 1 piece of ham on each triangle; place 2 strips of cheese down center of ham. Fold in edges of ham to match shape of dough triangle.
Roll up each crescent, ending at tip of triangle. Place with tips down on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 15 to 19 minutes or until golden brown. WHile sandwiches are baking, saute apple in butter for 1 minute. Add brown sugar and stir until dissolved. Then add apple jelly and stir until it melts into a sauce. Serve apples and sauce on side or over sandwich. Serve warm.
