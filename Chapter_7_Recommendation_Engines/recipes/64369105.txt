Title:
Russian Black Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups warm water
2 tablespoons cider vinegar
2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
3 teaspoons vital wheat gluten
1 cup rye flour
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons margarine
2 tablespoons dark corn syrup
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1.5 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon instant coffee granules
1 teaspoon onion powder
1.5 teaspoons caraway seed - ground in spice mill
1/8 teaspoon fennel seed - ground in spice mill
2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Place warm water, brown sugar and yeast in bread machine pan, let proof for 10 minutes. Add remaining dry ingredients followed by remaining liquid ingredients. Dough will be sticky, add flour only if excessively so. Transfer to lightly floured surface, form into loaf and place in prepared bread pan. After loaf has risen place in 350F oven and bake for 35 minutes.
