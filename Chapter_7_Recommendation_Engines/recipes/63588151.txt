Title:
Grilled Vegetable Panina with Egg Salad and Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 1/2 inch thick hard country bread (inside lightly toasted.
12 stalks grilled asparagus
8 grilled scallions or carmelized onion relish
1 c. egg salad gribiche (recipe follows)
4 slices bacon (crumbled)
4 slices of feta cheese
Egg Salad Gribiche recipe:
6 lg eggs, hard boiled
2 tsp Dijon mustard
5 tblsp olive oil
1 tblsp red wine vinegar
1 tsp Kosher salt
1/4 to 1/2 tsp finely ground black pepper
1/2 c finely chopped cornichons
1 tblsp finely chopped bine-packed capers
1 tblsp fresh flat leaf parsley

Directions:
Grilled asparagus.  Lightly toast in griddle plate one side of country bread.  Apply 1/4 cup egg salad on toasted side.  Sprinkle bacon crumbles, one slice grilled scallion or 1 tsp. carmelized onion relish on top of onion.  Put two asparagus stalk and one slice on cheese.  Top with another slice of bread and insert in panini press until toasted and cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Keep it Tasty
Description: 
This is a very quick full meal within 5 minutes.  Just make the Egg Salad Gribiche the night before.  Add a cup of creamy bisque and a glass of wine for a delicious meal.
