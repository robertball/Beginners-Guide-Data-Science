Title:
Un-Bourbon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite size pieces
2 T coconut oil
3/4 c bone broth chicken
1/2 c coconut aminos
2 T tomato sauce (omit! or use small amount tomato paste)
1 tsp fish sauce (approx)
1 T apple cider vinegar or coconut vinegar
3-5 garlic cloves
1-2 tsp or more ginger minced
3/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1-2 tsp salt
1-2 green onions chopped
handful cilantro leaves
cauliflower rice

Directions:
salt chicken (used too much last time)
brown chicken in coconut oil over medium-high heat (next time use bigger pan instead of wok)
remove chicken
combine all sauce ingredients with hand mixer, leaving a few chunks of ginger/coconut
put sauce in pan and reduce a bit until thickened
remove some sauce for side
add chicken back to pan an simmer
add avocado oil to reserved sauce for side
serve with cauliflower rice
