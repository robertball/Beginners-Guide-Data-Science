Title:
Coquito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/759154.jpg
Ingredients:
2 can evaporated milk
1 can cream of coconut
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 can coconut milk
2 cup Don Q Gold
1 tablespoon ground nutmeg
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Cinnamon stick add one stick to each glass bottled filled.

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and process for 3 minutes at high speed until blend.  Store in a glass container in the refrigerator and serve chilled, dusted with a little cinnamon on top at serving.

Submitter: 
Milagros Irizarry Ocasio
Description: 
Creamy tropical coconut beverage made with spices and white rum. It is always requested at my holiday gatherings. (Thanksgiving and Christmas time) Serve in glass cups and sprinkle with more cinnamon if desired. Feliz Navidad!
