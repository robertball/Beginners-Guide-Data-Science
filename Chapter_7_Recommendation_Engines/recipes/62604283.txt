Title:
Sassy Bacon Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/561722.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Cherry Tomatoes
1 Scallion(pretend they are chives)
1/2 tsp minced Garlic
2 slices crumbled Bacon
5 oz jar Olde English Cheddar Cheese
8 Tbsp Mayonnaise
3 Tortillas
4 Tbsp melted butter
Salt

Directions:
Cut tortillas into wedges and brush with melted butter and sprinkle with salt. Place on rack over cookie sheet. Bake until crisp. *Don't wait till after baking to cut* Mix deseeded diced tomatoes, chives(scallion used, store was out), bacon, cheese and mayo together. Dip with tortilla wedges.
