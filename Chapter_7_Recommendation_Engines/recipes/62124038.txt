Title:
Ryan's Quick Tomato Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Pkgs Vegetable Soup Mix
2 Cans Stewed Tomatoes
3 Cups Water

Directions:
Boil Water and Stewed Tomatoes.
Add Vegetable Soup Mix
Remove from heat and cover
Let sit for 3-5 minutes or until vegetables are hydrated.

Submitter: 
Anne
Description: 
This is a very quick and easy vegetable soup that is great to take camping or backpacking.  We like it at home for just dinner too. Goes great with quick and easy flatbread recipes and the kids love it too!!
