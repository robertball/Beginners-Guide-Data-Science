Title:
Harissa Spice Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448373.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp sweet paprika
1 chipotle in adobo, minced
1 teaspoon adobo sauce
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 tsp ground cumin
2 tsp ground caraway
2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for brushing
Salt
Pepper (freshly ground)

Directions:
In a mortar, combine the paprika, chipotle, adobo sauce, garlic, cumin, caraway and olive oil.
Smash until a paste forms.
Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
This red pepper paste (the chipotle) is spicy! Use it as a condiment, or a component in a marinade. Add it to anything from vegetables to lamb!

Note: 1/2 of this blend is suitable for 1 pound of chicken, beef, pork, etc.
