Title:
Noemi's  Horchata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cup uncooked white long-grain rice
4 cups water
1 1/2 cups milk
1 5oz can of evaporated milk
1 Tablespoon vanilla extract
2 Cinnamon sticks
1/3 cup white sugar

Directions:
Pour the rice, cinnamon sticks and 2 cups of water into the bowl of a blender; blend until the rice and cinnamon sticks about 2-3 minutes. Let rice and water soak in the refrigerator for a minimum of 4-5 hours, overnight is best.
Strain the rice water into a pitcher and discard the rice. Stir the milk, vanilla, and sugar into the rice water. Chill and stir before serving over ice.

Submitter: 
Noemi - No way is she a
Description: 
A refreshing Mexican drink made with rice, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon. A great balance for spicy foods, enjoy this served over ice.Modified from: Lola's Horchata
