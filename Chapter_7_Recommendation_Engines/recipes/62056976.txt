Title:
Mom's Best Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 c. whole rolled oats
1 c. wheat germ
1 c. unsweetened coconut
1/2 c. sunflower seeds
1/2 c. sesame seeds
1 c. chopped nuts of your choice
1/2 c. vegetable oil
1/2 c. honey
1/2 c. boiling water
1/4 c. brown sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F.
In a large bowl, mix together the oats, wheat germ, coconut, seeds, and nuts.  Mix the remining ingredients in a small bowl.  Pour the liquid ingredients over the oat mixture and mix well.
Spread in a large cookie sheet and bake for 1 to 1 1/4 hours, stirring every half hour.

Description: 
A crunchy, nutty granola that never fails to get compliments from breakfast guests.
