Title:
Baked Potato Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060683.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large baking potatoes
1/2 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons butter
2 teaspoons dry onion soup mix
2 to 4 slices bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Clean potatoes and bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour or until soft.
Cut potatoes lengthwise and scoop out center, about the size of a large egg.  Place scooped potatoes in small bowl.
Mash scooped potatoes with butter, sour cream, salt and pepper (if desired), and onion soup mix.  Mix well.
Fill potatoes with mashed mixture.  Sprinkle bacon crumbles on top.  Brown in oven for 10 minutes.

Description: 
Baked potatoes are a staple or many meals, and this is an easy variation that is a great side for a hearty dinner.
