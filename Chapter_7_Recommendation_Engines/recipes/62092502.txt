Title:
Potato Stuffed Pakoras Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For patties :
3 cups mashed potatoes
1 cup finely chopped onion
3 green chillies finely chopped
2 tea spoon ginger grated
1 tea spoon garlic cloves finely chopped
3 tea spoon lemon juice
1/2 cup finely chopped corrinder
salt to taste
For batter :
2 cups gram flour
salt to taste
1/2 tea spoon chilli powder
1 tablespoon hot oil
1 tablespoon corn flour or rice flour
1 1/2 cups water
Oil to fry
For dip :
4 tablespoons roasted groundnut powder
1 cup yoghurt
salt to taste
1 tea spoon chilli powder
1 tea spoon corrinder leaves

Directions:
Mix ingredients for patties
With wet hands Make into lemon size balls& flatten them into 1/2 inch thickness
Mix all ingredients for covering to form a batter
Heat oil in a skillet
Dip the patties one by one in batter  & let them be covered  evenly DRop in hot oil
Fry over medium heat till deep golden colour
If making large quantites,Vadas you can be fried  to a light golden colour & kept
Just before serving,they should be fried again quickly in hot oil. Refried Vadas are still tastier
Mix all ingredients for the dip & serve with Vadas

Submitter: 
RADHIKA GHATAGE
Description: 
This is a exotic version of Pakora,Very popular in Bombay,where is is served as a quick meal sandwiched in a bun
