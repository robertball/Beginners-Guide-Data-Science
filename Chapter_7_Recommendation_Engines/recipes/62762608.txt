Title:
Skillet Toad in the hole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/639275.jpg
Ingredients:
i lb. sausage
2 eggs
1 cup flour
pinch of salt
1 cup milk
1/3 cup water
2 tbsp. chopped chives

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
Brown sausages in cast iron skillet
While they cook  prepare batter
Mix flour and salt.
Add eggs and milk.
Beat until smooth then add water and beat until blended.
fold in chives.
Pour batter over sausages and put in oven for !/2 hour.
let rest 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Anne Olson
Description: 
One cast iron skillet isall that's needed for this yummy brunch treat.
