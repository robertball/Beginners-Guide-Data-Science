Title:
Orange Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1486974.jpg
Ingredients:
1  box            Duncan Hines Orange Supreme Cake Mix
1  box(3.4 oz)    White Chocolate Instant Pudding mix
2  cans(15 oz)    Mandarin oranges, drained
1  stick (1/2 cup) Butter
1  cup            turbinado sugar
2  tbs            honey
4  lg             eggs
1  cup            water
1/2 cup           canola oil
1  10oz jar       maraschino cherries (no stem),                     drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Melt butter in a 9x13 pan.  (Glass pan in the microwave is easy.)  Sprinkle sugar over butter.  Spread drained oranges around pan.  Dot with cherries.  Drizzle honey over top.  Mix cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, water and oil for 2 minutes.  Pour over orange mixture.  Bake for 40-45 minutes, just until toothpick comes out clean.  Cool at least 15 minutes.  Invert onto a platter.
