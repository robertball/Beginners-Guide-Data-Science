Title:
Rick Gallop GI Crustless Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp non-hydrogenated margarine
1/4 c fresh whole wheat bread crumbs
1 bunch broccoli, trimmed and cut into florets
1 1/4 c liquid egg
1 c skim milk
1/4 tsp each salt and ground black pepper
2 slices fresh whole grain bread, cubed
1/4 c packed sun-dried tomatoes, re-hydrated in hot water, drained and chopped
2 green onions, chopped
2 oz low fat feta, crumbled

Directions:
-grease bottom and sides of a deep 10" pie plate with margarine. Add bread crumbs, tilting to coat bottom and sides evenly.
-steam broccoli for 5 minutes or until tender-crisp. Rinse under cold water, drain and chop. set aside
-in large bowl, whisk together egg, milk, salt and pepper. stir in broccoli, cubed bread, tomatoes, onions, and feta. pour into prepared pan
-bake in 350 oven for 50-60 minutes or until light golden, puffed and just set in center.
