Title:
Mini Meatloaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/508954.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Eggs
1½ c Milk
1 c Oats (quick)
½ pkg Saltines (crushed)
2 stalks Celery
2 cloves Garlic
2 md Onions (diced)
2 pkg Onion Soup Mix
3 lb Ground Beef
1½ c Ketchup
½ c Brown Sugar
2 T Mustard

Directions:
PREHEAT oven 350º.
COMBINE first 8 ingredients.
MIX in the ground beef without over mixing.
FORM mixture into eight miniature meatloaves.
PLACE these in two lightly greased 9"x13" baking dish.
COMBINE ketchup, brown sugar and mustard in another small bowl.
SPREAD about 2-3 tablespoons over each meatloaf. (There will be leftover sauce to eat with dinner)
BAKE, uncovered, for 45 minutes.
