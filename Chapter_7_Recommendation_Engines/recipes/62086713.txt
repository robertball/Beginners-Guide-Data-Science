Title:
Pizza Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1/4 cup water
2 tbsp fresh parsley
1 tsp each of salt, pepper, oregano and basil
1 tbsp oil
4 tbsp tomato sauce
1 cup of your favourite pizza toppings
1/2 cup mozzarella

Directions:
Beat together eggs, water, parsley and spices.
Heat oil in skillet on medium heat. Add egg mixture. Cook until eggs are almost set.
Spread tomato sauce over the eggs. Add toppings. Make sure that meats, such as bacon, ground beef, sausage are cooked before adding.
Top with mozzarella. Cover and heat until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
linda
Description: 
Great for pizza lovers who want to cut down on their carb intake.  Quick to make and easy to alter depending on what ingredients you have on hand.
