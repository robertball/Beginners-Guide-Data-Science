Title:
Quick Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1556595.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef, or to taste
1 (32 ounce) can chili beans
3 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato sauce
1 (15 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (1.25 ounce) package chili seasoning (such as BrooksÂ®)
1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder, or more to taste

Directions:
Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.
Stir chili beans, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, chili seasoning, and chili powder into the beef; bring to a boil.
