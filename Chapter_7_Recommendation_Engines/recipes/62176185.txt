Title:
The Best Ever, On This Earth, Chewy Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
225g Butter
75g Cocoa
4 Eggs
450g Castor Sugar
1tsp Vanilla
100g each Self raising flour,plain Choc Chips,Walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 180 C
Grease your tins
Melt butter over low heat or in Microwave.
Add cocoa & stir until well blended & smooth, set aside
Beat eggs & sugar until light & fluffy
Beat in butter & cocoa mixture and vanilla
Sift flour over top and fold in gently
Fold in chocolat & nuts
Bake 30-40 min (the outside is quite crunchy, but the inside is very chewy)
