Title:
Ultimate Chicken Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
½ pound bacon, rough chopped
1 medium onion diced
2 stalks celery diced
2 tbs flour
32 oz chicken stock or broth
4 gold potatoes, peeled and cubed
2 15 oz cans sweet corn, drained
1 can creamed corn
2 cups half-and-half
1 tsp paprika
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook and shred chicken, set aside.
In a heavy bottomed pot over medium heat, sauté bacon until crisp.
Remove bacon and set aside, reserve fat.
Sauté onion and celery in bacon fat until onions are translucent.
Add flour to make a roux, cook and stir for two minutes.
Add chicken stock and potatoes, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Add corn and creamed corn, half and half, paprika and chicken. Cook to heat through about 20 minutes on low to medium heat.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
