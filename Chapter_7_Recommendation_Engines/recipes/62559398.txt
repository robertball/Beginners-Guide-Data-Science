Title:
Spicy Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535356.jpg
Ingredients:
1 onion, diced
4 cloves of garlic, minced
3 (15 1/2 ounce) cans black beans-undrained
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 (14 1/2 ounce) can vegetable broth
1 (14 1/2 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chiles
1 (11 ounce ) can corn, drained

Directions:
Spray dutch oven with non stick spray and place over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic; cook, stirring occasionally, until the onion is softened, about 5 minutes.
Place the onion mixture, 1 can of the beans, cumin, and red pepper in a blender and pulse until smooth: return the mixture to the dutch oven. Place one more can of beans in the blender and pulse until smooth; add to the dutch oven. Add the remaining can of beans, the broth, tomatoes, and corn to the dutch oven; bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer, uncovered, until slightly thickened, 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
SlimCookins
Description: 
A great, healthy soup for the winter OR summer seasons. Versatile as either an appetizer or a meal of its own. Top with a dallop or sour cream and serve with a slice of warm italian bread.
