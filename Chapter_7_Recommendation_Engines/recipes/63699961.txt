Title:
Lemon Black Raspberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1028510.jpg
Ingredients:
Bread:
2 cups all purpose flour
1 Tbsp baking powder
1 c sugar
1/4 tsp salt
Zest of 1-1/2 fresh lemons
1-1/2 c Fresh Black Raspberries
2 large eggs
1 cup milk
Juice of 1 fresh lemon
3 Tbsp vegetable oil
Glaze:
Juice of 1 fresh  lemon
1/2 cup of granulated sugar

Directions:
Combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt into a bowl.
Stir in black raspberries and the lemon zest
In another bowl, beat eggs; add milk, lemon juice and oil, mix well.
Stir in the dry ingredients mixture, just until moistened.
Pour into a greased 9x5 loaf pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for one hour or until toothpick tests clean.
Prepare glaze ingredients. Combine all ingredients in a microwave safe bowl. Microwave at 20 second intervals until sugar is dissolved.
After removing loaf from the oven, while still warm, brush the glaze over the bread while its still in the pan. Let cool for 10 minutes, remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
Store any leftovers in the fridge.

Submitter: 
Miss B
Description: 
My nieces often describe this as having a tall cool glass of Raspberry Lemonade in a bread form! The sweet and tart fresh Lemon flavor combines well with the natural sweetness of the Black Raspberries! This has become a family favorite during berry picking season!
