Title:
Puffed Blueberry Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/546208.jpg
Ingredients:
3 TBL dark brown sugar
¼ tsp. cinnamon
1 TBL fresh lemon juice
1 Cup blueberries
2 TBL butter
2 large eggs, beaten
6 TBL all-purpose flour
Pinch of salt
½ C. milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 425º.  In a medium bowl stir together brown sugar, cinnamon, lemon juice and blueberries.  Toss to cover.  Divide the butter between 2 shallow 1 cup ovenproof dishes; heat in oven until butter melts.
Combine eggs, flour, salt and milk.  Mix well.  Divide the batter between the dishes.  Divide the blueberry mixture between the dishes, sprinkling the berries on the top.  Bake for 17 to 20 minutes in a 425º oven until puffed and golden.  Serve with syrup.
