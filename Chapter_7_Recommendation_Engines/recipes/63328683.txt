Title:
This Mom's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 lb ground round, coarse grind if possible
2 Tablespoons Chili Powder
1 Tablespoon cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1 can rotel, drained
1 can chopped anaheim chilies
1 8 oz can tomato sauce
1 can chili beans, drained and rinsed

Directions:
Sautee onions and peppers until edges turn golden.  Add garlic and cook until fragrant (30 sec).  Place into crock.
Brown beef in small amount of oil until browned.  Add spices and cook until spices have bloomed (about 1 min).  Place in crock.
Add canned goods and stir gently.
Cook on low heat 7 to 9 hours.
