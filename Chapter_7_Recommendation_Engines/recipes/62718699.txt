Title:
Hungarian pork and sauerkraut sandwich - peshene Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 thin cut boneless pork chops
1 small onion, thinly sliced
Garlic Powder
Salt
Pepper
Hungarian sweet paprika
1/2 stick unsalted butter
1 large can (27 ounces) sauerkraut, rinsed, squeezed dry
8 slices of the best rye bread

Directions:
Sprinkle chops with seasoning, cover with wrap and let them sit at least 15 minutes, longer for more flavor.
Gently brown the pork chops in the butter.  Do not over cook.
Set pork aside and add onions and sauerkraut to the pan. Saute the kraut until browned.
Make up 4 sandwiches being generous with the sauerkraut mixture.

Submitter: 
Cynthia Betancourt
Description: 
I grew up in a town with a lot of great Hungarian food.  This sandwich was a favorite!  Very delicious pork sandwich.  My family does not like sauerkraut, so I serve it with Cabbage and Noodles from this site.  The fresh rye bread is a must!
