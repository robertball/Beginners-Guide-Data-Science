Title:
BROCCOLI CHEESE SOUP WITH NOODLES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP OIL
1 CHOPPED ONION
3 LARGE CANS OF CHICKEN BROTH
4 CUPS OF NOODLES (REAMS HOMESTYLE)
1 TBSP GARLIC POWDER
1 OR 1 1/2 HEADS OF FRESH BROCCOLI
3 CUPS MILK
1/2 LB AMERICAN CHEESE CUBED
SHREDDED CHEDDAR CHEESE
VELVETTA CHEESE
SALT & PEPPER TO TASTE

Directions:
COOK ONIONS IN OIL, THEN ADD CHICHKEN BROTH, ADD NODDLES AND BOIL UNCOVERED FOR 3 MINUTES. STIR IN BROCCOLI & GARLIC POWDER FOR 4 MINUTES.  ADD MILK & CHEESES & STIR CONSTANTLY UNTIL CHEESE MELTS
