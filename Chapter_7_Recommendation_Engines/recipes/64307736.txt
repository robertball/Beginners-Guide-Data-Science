Title:
Greek Chicken Bake with Tzatziki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1974536.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Chicken Thighs
1 head of garlic, halved
2 red onions, cut into wedges
1/2 cup Calamata olives
olive oil to drizzle
2 tsp dried oregano
salt & pepper to taste
1 lemon juice
1/8 cup feta cheese

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 350 degrees f
2. Place chicken in an oven proof pan.  Add the red onion wedges, sliced head of garlic and calamata olives.
3. Sprinkle over the dried oregano, season to taste and drizzle over the lemon juice and olive oil.
4. Place it in the oven and roast for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is cooked throughout and the skin is golden & crisp.
To serve, sprinkle feta cheese.  Serve with Tzatziki, warm pita bread and extra lemon wedges.

Submitter: 
LaFonda Merrick
Description: 
Easy to make and perfect to put together the night before and put in the oven when you get home from work.  We add brown rice and salad as a side, but small red potatoes halved are perfect with it as well.
