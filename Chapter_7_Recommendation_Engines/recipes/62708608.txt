Title:
Slow Cooker Pork Chops II-REVISED Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil
1 small onion, minced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon paprika
1 tablespoon poultry seasoning
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 cup chicken broth
1 cup white wine
4 thick cut boneless pork chops
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Season chops with salt and pepper and brown in olive oil, remove to slow cooker. Saute onion and garlic in pan, deglaze pan with white wine.  Stir in chicken broth, garlic, paprika, poultry seasoning and basil. Pour into the slow cooker. Cook on High for 4 hours.
