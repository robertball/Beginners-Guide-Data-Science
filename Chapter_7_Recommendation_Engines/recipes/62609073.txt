Title:
Peachy Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package yellow cake mix with pudding
3 eggs
1 tbsp vanilla extract
1 21 oz can peach pie filling
1 c white chocolate chips
1 cup milk
1 3.5 oz package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 8 oz frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9x13 inch pan.
Mix together: cake mix, eggs, vanilla extract, peach pie filling, and 1 c white chocolate chips. Stir until just combined. Pour batter into prepared 9x13 pan.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 45-50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Remove cake from oven and let cool.
To make frosting: in a medium bowl, combine pudding mix with milk and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth. Gently fold in whipped topping until no streaks remain. Spread over cooled cake.
