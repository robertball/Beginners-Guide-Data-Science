Title:
Big Ray's New Orleans Creole  Red Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2302793.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. dried red beans
1 white onion, chopped
2 stalks celery, diced
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 lb.smoked sausage, thinly sliced
1 ham hock, optional
3 bay leaves
1/2 tsp. Creole seasoning
1/2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
6 cups water
Green onions, for garnish
Hot sauce or seasoned vinegar to taste, if desired
cooked white rice

Directions:
Add all ingredients except for green onions and hot sauce to the slow cooker and stir.
Cook on low heat for 8 hours.
Before serving, remove bay leaves and garnish with green onions. Season with hot sauce or a splash of seasoned vinegar, if desired.
Serve over white rice.

Description: 
The perfect meal to use the leftover ham and free up several hours away from the stove to come home from work to a hearty meal.
