Title:
Sweet and sour ground pork stir fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1107874.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground pork
1 onion sliced
2 carrots peeled and sliced
1/2 cup rice vinegar
1/3 cup ketchup
1/4 cup sugar
2 teaspoons soya sauce
1 green or red pepper chopped
2 cups broccoli chopped

Directions:
Brown ground pork in large frying pan on high until cooked through. Water may be added to prevent sticking.
Add onions and carrots and stir fry for two minutes.
Add vinegar, ketchup, sugar and soya sauce. Mix well.
Add green peppers and broccoli.  Stir to distribute sauce evenly. Cover and cook until broccoli is done.
