Title:
Meatball Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb ground beef
2 c chopped onion
1 t salt
10 cups beef broth
2 16oz stewed tomatoes
1 1/2 t. thyme
1/4/ t. pepper
2 c. chopped cabbage
1 16oz can garbanzo beans, drained
1/2 lb uncooked pasta
grated romano cheese

Directions:
Combine meat & 1 c onion & salt, make into 1/2 inch meatballs, brown in large stock pot.  Remove from pan, cook remaining onion in drippings, until onion is tender.  Add meatballs, broth, tomatoes, thyme & pepper.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer covered 1 hour.  Add cabbage, garbanzo beans & pasta.  Cook 15 minutes longer or until pasta is tender.  Sprinkle w/ cheese

Submitter: 
Marie Mako
Description: 
A hearty soup great for winter and large gatherings, serve with bread, and salad if you need to!
