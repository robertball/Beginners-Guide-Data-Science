Title:
Hot Bacon Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices bacon (avoid "flavored" - Maple, etc)
1-2 TBLS Bacon grease
1 1/2 C white sugar
3 tsp cornstarch
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground mustard
1/4 C water
1/2 C Cider vinegar

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Crumble and set aside.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, mustard and salt, and slowly pour in Bacon grease,water and vinegar, whisking constantly.
In a medium skillet, add the crumbled bacon and pour the dressing mixture over it.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens.

Submitter: 
Jack
Description: 
This warm dressing immerses crunchy bacon bits in a sweet, thickened vinaigrette for an old-time taste treat!Modified from: Hot Bacon Dressing
