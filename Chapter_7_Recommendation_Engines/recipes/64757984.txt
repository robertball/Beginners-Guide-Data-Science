Title:
KAY SAUER CHILI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	2 TBSP OLIVE OIL
•	1 LARGE ONION
•	2 TBSP MINCED GARLIC
•	½ POUND GROUND SIRLOIN
•	1 POUND CHORIZO
•	1 POUND GROUND BEEF
•	25 OZ BEEF BROTH
•	¼ CUP CHILI POWDER
•	2 TSP CUMIN
•	1 TBSP GARLIC SALT
•	1 TSP DRIED BASIL
•	4  14OZ CANS CHOPPED PETITE TOMATOES WITH JUICE, WITH GARLIC OR OTHER SEASONING
•	2 TBSP CILANTRO
•	1 TBSP CINNAMON POWDER
•	2 BAY LEAVES
•	2 JALAPENOS, SPLIT LENGTHWISE, ONE WITH SEEDS
•	1 TBSP YELLOW CORNMEAL
•	½ CUP PACKED BROWN SUGAR
•	2 CANS RED KIDNEY BEANS (ONE WITH HOT SAUCE, ONE MILD)
•	1 TSP CAYENNE PEPPER
•	1 CUP KETCHUP
•	1 CAN TOMATO PASTE
•	SALT & PEPPER TO TASTE

Directions:
Saute onions and garlic in the olive oil.
Add the meat and brown thoroughly.
Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer for three hours.
