Title:
Pumpkin Spice Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups oat flour
1 T baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 T cinnamon
1/4 tsp allspice
1/4 tsp nutmeg
4 egg whites
1/2 cup raw pumpkin
1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk

Directions:
Preheat griddle to medium heat. Mix oat flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, allspice, and nutmeg in a bowl.
Whisk egg whites and pumpkin. Mix in almond milk. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix together.
Spray griddle with nonstick spray. Scoop batter with a 1/4 cup measuring cup onto griddle. Cook 3-5 minutes per side.
