Title:
Thai Meat & Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups early potatoes halved
2 sprigs rosemary
2 pork tenderloins cut into 1 inch pieces
salt and pepper
olive oil
1/2 cup shallots finely minced
2 garlic cloves finely minced
1/2 cup finely diced pineapple
1 mango
juice of 1 lime
1 cup heavy cream
2 teaspoons Thai green curry paste
1/4 cup chiffonade of basil

Directions:
Boil potatoes in salted water with rosemary sprigs unil tender set aside
Pound tenderloin until 1/4 inch thick, set aside
Cut mango flesh away from pit and skin and puree with hand blender
Add juice of 1 lime to the mango puree, set aside
Heat 2 TBSP olive oil in frying pan
Add shallot and pineapple and saute over medium low heat until tender 3 to 4 minutes
Add garlic and continue to cook for 2 minutes
Reduce heat to low and add heavy cream and mango puree
Season tenderloin pieces with salt and pepper
In a heavy pan heat 3 TBSP olive oil, medium high heat
Quick fry tenderloin pieces 2 minutes per side, place pieces on paper towel as they are done
Add Thai green curry paste to cream sauce
Add water as needed to thin sauce, should have the consistency of good hollandaise sauce.
When all the meat slices are cooked
Add Chiffonade of basil and potatoes to the sauce, toss to re-heat..
Plate potatoes onto 4 plates reserving some sauce for the meat.
Cut the cooked pork tenderloin pieces in halves, arrange nicely on the potatoes and top with reserved sauce.
