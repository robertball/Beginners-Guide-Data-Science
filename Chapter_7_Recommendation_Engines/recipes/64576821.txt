Title:
Butter Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups icing sugar
1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
2 tbsp. flour
3 tbsp. cream, milk or water
1 tsp. vanilla (or other flavor ie. cherry, lemon or orange juice, coffee, liqueurs, etc.)

Directions:
Beat all together until smooth. Adjust liquid and sugar/flour to reach desired consistency.
Adjust flour/sugar for desired level of sweetness.
Makes about 1 1/3 cups.
Enough for about 12 cupcakes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This quick, easy icing is my go-to for cupcakes especially. It can be quite sweet, so I've toned that down with a bit of flour. This will ice 1 dozen cupcakes. Flavor to complement your cake.
