Title:
Vegetarian Mabo Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Enoki Mushrooms
Dried mushrooms
1 cup of warm water
Green and Red Bell Pepper
Vegetable Oil
3 Scallions
2 tsp crushed garlic
1 tsp fresh ginger
mabo sauce packet
1 block Tofu, drained & dried in oven for 20 min
Rice

Directions:
1. combine dried mushrooms with warm water - let soak
2. saute enoki mushrooms and peppers in oil.
3. Add garlic, ginger, onions  & saute
4. Add tofu, sauce packet, & water.
5. simmer for 15 minutes
6. serve over rice
