Title:
Roasted Cod with Garlic Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 unsalted butter, softened
1 1/2 tbs chopped flat leaf parsley
1 large garlic clove, minced
2 tsp minced shallots
1/2 tsp dijon mustard
1 1/2 tbs prosciutto minced
1 tbs flour
2 tbs freshly squeezed lemon juice
salt, to taste
ground pepper, to taste
2 tbs canola oil
4 skinless cod fillets

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Stir together first 10 ingredients in a small bowl, set aside.
Heat oil in a wide, deep ovenproof skillet over medium high heat.  Season the cod with salt and pepper and cook 4 minutes.  Turn fillets over; cook 1 minute.  Spoon 1 tbs butter mixture over the top of each fillet and transfer skillet to 450 degree oven for 2 minutes or until fish is just cooked through and opaque in the center.  Add any remaining butter to pan and let melt stirring with juices in pan; spoon over fish and garnish with lemon wedges if desired.
