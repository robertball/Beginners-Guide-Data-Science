Title:
Apple Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
? 1/2 cups applesauce or oil
? 2 cups sugar, raw or regular
? 2 eggs
? 1 tsp. vanilla
? 3 cups apple juice, chopped
? 1 cups walnuts, chopped
? 1 tsp. cinnamon
? 1 cups ww flour
? 2 cups flour
? 1 tsp. baking soda
? 1 tsp. salt
? 1/4 cups wheat germ

Directions:
Mix applesauce or oil with sugar, add eggs, vanilla, apples & walnuts
add rest
bake in muffins tins 25 min
or
9x9 pan 45 min to 1 hr  325
