Title:
Chicken Paprikash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken(usually thighs on the bone) with salt and paprika
Brown in butter
Turn and add sliced onion and more paprika cover and simmer for 20min.
Add 2 cups of water and 2 bouillon cubes cover and cook covered slowly for 1 hour or until chicken is tender.
Remove chicken
Blend some sour cream and a little water and 1/4 cup of flour until smooth.
Add to broth slowly, stir till thickened-don't boil.
Return chicken that has been removed from the bones.
Spaetzles:
2 eggs, 2 cups flour, 1/2 cup milk, 1/2 tsp salt
Mix with fork
Drop into boiling water by small spoonfuls or use spaetzle maker and cook for 5 min then strain.
