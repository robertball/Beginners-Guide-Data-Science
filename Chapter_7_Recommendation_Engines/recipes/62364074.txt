Title:
Lemon Lover's Layer Cake with Lemon Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium-size lemon
1 box plain yellow or vanilla cake mix
1 3.4 oz. box lemon pudding
1 6 oz. cup low-fat lemon yogurt
3/4 C water
1/2 C oil
4 eggs
Frosting:
4 oz. reduced fat cream cheese
1/2 C butter
4 C powdered sugar

Directions:
Zest lemon: 1 tsp. for cake and 1 tsp. for frosting.  Juice lemon:  1 T for cake and 2 t for frosting.  Bake cake for 25-30 minutes at 350.

Submitter: 
FoodFan
Description: 
A lemon cake plain and simple, flavored with lemon yogurt and pudding and fresh lemon juice and zest.
