Title:
Chicken Tortellini with a Mushroom Spinach Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/298492.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz. pckg.  chicken tortellini (I use Buitoni, but any kind will work)
1/2 yellow onion
2-3 cloves garlic
4 oz. sliced mushrooms
2 14.5 oz. cans diced tomatoes
1 bunch spinach ripped off stems
1/4 cup ricotta cheese
1 tsp. (or to taste) Italian seasoning
1 tsp. (or to taste) red pepper flakes
S&P; to taste
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook tortellini according to package.
Saute onion and garlic about 3 minutes until tender.  Add mushrooms and saute 1-2 minutes.  Add tomatoes and cook about 20 minutes.  Next add spinach and cook until wilted.  Stir in the ricotta and add more to desired thickness.  Last add in the spices and stir.  Add heaping mounds of sauce to the tortellini.
Garnish with parmesan after plated.
Enjoy!!!

Description: 
This dish is perfect for any occasion.  It's great for a family meal or a romantic dinner.  Serve with a crusty Italian bread to soak up the sauce!
