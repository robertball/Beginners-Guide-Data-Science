Title:
Ham and Swiss Cheese - That's a Wrap! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/643182.jpg
Ingredients:
16-24 slices of deli ham
12 slices of Swiss cheese
Dijon mustard
8 (10") flour tortillas

Directions:
Spread the Dijon mustard on each tortilla
Layer 2-3 slices of ham and 1 1/2 slices Swiss cheese on each tortilla
Roll and wrap tightly with waxed paper.  Secure with toothpick
Refrigerate overnight.
When ready to serve, remove toothpicks, cut each wrap in half and arrange on serving plate, placing a bowl of extra Dijon mustard in the center.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Easy to make and easy to serve, these wraps are a hit at any gathering.
