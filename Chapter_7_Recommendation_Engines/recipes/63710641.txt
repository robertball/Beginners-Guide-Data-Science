Title:
Sour Cream Cucumbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium cucumbers, thinly sliced
1 small onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup dairy sour cream
1 tablespoon vinegar
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine the cucumbers and onion. Stir together sour cream, vinegar, sugar and salt: toss with vegetables. Cover and chill, stirring occasionally.
