Title:
French Onion Soup (slow cooker) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Four Large Onions thinly sliced
four TBL butter ( or enough to saute onions well without making them greasy)
Four 8 oz cans beef consomme
garlic clove
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute onions in skillet, when clear and browned move to crock pot. Mix in rest of ingredients. Cook on high for four hours or low for eight.
