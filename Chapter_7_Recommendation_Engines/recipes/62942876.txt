Title:
Mac N. Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/722625.jpg
Ingredients:
15-16 oz. elbow macaroni
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 1/2 lb. cheddar cheese
3 1/2 c.  milk
4 eggs
1/2 tsp nutmeg
2 tbsp. butter
paprika

Directions:
Cook macaroni as directed on box.
Grate half of the cheese; cut the rest into small chunks.
Place half of the macaroni in a large ungreased casserole dish (4 quarts)
Sprinkle with half of the cheese, salt, pepper, onion powder.
Place the remaining macaroni in the dish.
Push cheese chunks into the macaroni; sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Blend milk, eggs, nutmeg, remaining salt, pepper and onion powder. Pour onto macaroni. Dot with butter.
Cover and bake 30-45 minutes. Uncover and bake 15 minutes longer.
