Title:
RICE ELENA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 cups cooked rice
2 cups sour cream
2-4 oz. cans chopped green chiles
1 # Monterrey Jack cheese, grated
Salt to taste
1/2 cup grated Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Butter a 2 quart casserole.  Mix all ingredients except Cheddar cheese.  Sprinkle Cheddar cheese on top.  Bake for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
bryantgal31
Description: 
This is a favorite of my family.  Goes great with Mexican dishes or other chicken as a side dish.
