Title:
Orange Cherry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/558922.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 C white whole wheat flour
1/2 C sugar
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp finely grated orange peel
1 egg
1/4 C applesauce(unsweetened)
1 tsp vanilla
1 6 oz orange yogurt(light,fat free)
2 oz skim milk
1 C dried cherries
1/4 C chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F and grease muffin tins.
Mix dry ingredients and orange peel together in a bowl.
In another bowl, beat together egg,applesauce,yogurt,milk and vanilla. Add to dry ingredients and stir just until combined and moistened. Fold in cherries.
Pour into prepared muffin pan, sprinkle with walnuts and bake 18-20 min.
Cool for 5 min and remove from pan.

Description: 
This is a healthier variation of a recipe from my BHG cookbook with a few changes as well. If you use paper liners they stick pretty good to the muffins.
