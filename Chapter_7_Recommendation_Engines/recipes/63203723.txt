Title:
Sweet Coffee BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C brewed coffee
1/4 C molasses
1/4 C dijon mustard
1/4 C soy sauce or coconut amino's
1 T Worcestershire sauce
1 T hot sauce
1/3 C tomato sauce or ketchup
1-2 T vinegar, white or apple cider (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and heat on med-low until reduced by half.  Taste, and adjust to your flavor.
