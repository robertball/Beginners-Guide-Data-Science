Title:
Almost No-Knead Bread with Olives, Rosemary & Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Addition: 4oz finely grated Parmesan cheese (about 2 C)
1 T minced fresh rosemary
1/2 C chopped green olives

Directions:
Follow same directions as Almost No-Knead Bread however add above ingred.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Use same ingred. as Almost No-Knead Bread but add 4oz. finely grated Parmesan cheese (about 2 C)and 1 T. minced fresh rosemary to flour, yeast and salt. Add 1/2 C chopped green olives (pitted)with water,beer and vinegar.
