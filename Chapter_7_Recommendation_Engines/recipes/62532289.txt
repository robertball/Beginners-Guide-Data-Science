Title:
Thanksgiving Yam Pecan Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Yams 3 1/2 pounds
Butter, melted 6 TBL
eggs, beaten  2
Milk, 2%  1/2 cup
Dark Brown sugar 1 cup
Softened butter  1/4 cup
flour 1/4 cup
Chopped pecans 1 cup
salt 1/4 tsp
vanilla 1 tsp
ground cinnamon 1/2 tsp
ground nutmeg  1/2 tsp

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2.  Bake Yams until tender - about 1 1/2 hour - remove & cool
3.  Increase oven temperature to 375 degrees
4.  Scoop pulp from yams. Place in large bowl and mash until smooth. Beat in melted butter, eggs and milk.  Place mixture into greased 11 x 7 inch baking dish.
5.  Mix remaining ingredients until crumbly. Sprinkle over yams.
6.  Bake 30 minutes or until heated through.
Too make healthier - they say you can leave out the melted butter.
