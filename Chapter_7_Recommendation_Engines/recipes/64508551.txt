Title:
Kandy's Beef Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042704.jpg
Ingredients:
5lb beef brisket untrimmed
1 large onion sliced
1 onion soup mix
1 bottle of chile sauce

Directions:
place brisket in a dutch oven with top
cover the top of brisket with sliced onions
mix soup and chile sauce together
and pour on top of brisket
bake a 300 covered for 3 hrs
take out of oven and cool. Slice thin and put back in dutch oven and refrigerate over night.
reheat and serve
