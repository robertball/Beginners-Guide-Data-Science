Title:
verna's cucumber relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 small or 8 large cucumbers
12 medium sized onions
1 red and 1 green pepper
1 cauliflower
BRINE
5 cups vinegar
1 cup water
6 cups white sugar
1 tbsp celery seed
6 tbsp. dry mustard
3/4 cups flour

Directions:
Grind vegetables together and soak 1 hour in 5 cups water and 1/2 cup salt.
Drain well.
Brine - cook vinegar, water etc. together till thick then add vegetables and cook till tender.  Bring to boil for just a few minutes.  Keep Stirring
Pour into jars and seal.
