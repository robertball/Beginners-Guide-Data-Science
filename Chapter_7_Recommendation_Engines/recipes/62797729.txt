Title:
Restaurant style Spinach-Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz.cream cheese
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1/4-1/3 cup gouda jalapeno cheese
1 clove minced garlic
1/2 tsp.dried basil
1/4 tsp.garlic salt
1 can[398ml/14oz.artichoke hearts,drained,coarsley chopped
1/2 cup frozen chopped spinach,thawed and drained
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup mozzarella cheese-grated

Directions:
Allow cream cheese to come to room temperature.
Cream together cream cheese with mayonnaise,parmesan cheese,gouda and jalapeno cheese,garlic,basil and garlic salt.Mix well.Add artichoke hearts and spinach,mix untill well blended.Add salt and pepper to taste.Spray pie pan with oil,pour in dip and and top with mozzarella cheese.
Bake at 350 for 25 min.untill top is browned.
Serve with melba toast or crackers.
