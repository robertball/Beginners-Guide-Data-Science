Title:
Accidental Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Stick of Butter (softened)
2 cups Flour
1 cup of milk for rolled biscuits. Add more milk for muffin pan biscuits.

Directions:
Take a softened stick of butter. Place in a large mixing bowl. With a hand held pastry blender -or- fork cut flour into butter. This mixture will have the consistency of course crumbs. Add milk to desired amount for type of biscuit and bake in a 325' oven till golden on top. Melted butter may be brushed over the top to give biscuits a golden glow.

Submitter: 
MIDNIGHT_ANGEL
Description: 
Easy, wonderfully light tasting and a very versatile biscuit mix. Milk can be altered to use as either rolled or muffin pan mixture consistency. The rolled variety can be made in advanced and frozen for up to a month.
