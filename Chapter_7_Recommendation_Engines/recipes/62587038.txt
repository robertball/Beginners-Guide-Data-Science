Title:
Garden Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med Vidalia onion chopped
1 clove of garlic minced
2 Tbsp unsalted butter
8 ears of fresh sweet corn
2 Tbsp flour
3 c chicken broth
1 sprig of fresh thyme
1 c diced potatoes
1 c milk
kosher salt
fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Saute the onion & garlic in butter over med heat till onion is soft, but do not brown or overcook. Cut the corn from cobs, add flour to the pot & cook for 2-3 min. Add broth slowly while stirring. Place corncobs & thyme in the pot & simmer for 15 min. Add the corn & potatoes & cook for another 10 min. Remove the cobs & thyme. Pour half the soup in a blender, being very careful to keep the lid securely on & blend until smooth. Pour back into the pot & add the milk. Simmer but do not allow to boil. Season with salt & pepper to taste & garnish with fresh thyme.

This is a great way to use fresh sweet corn from the garden all summer long
