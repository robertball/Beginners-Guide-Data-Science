Title:
Chicken Bacon Ranch Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3627286.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz ground bacon
2 lbs ground chicken breast
8 oz shredded cheddar
1/4 cup ranch
2 eggs
1 cup bread crumbs
2 tbsp coconut oil

Directions:
1) Cook the ground bacon in a pan/pot until browned, then empty it into a large mixing bowl
2) Add the ground chicken, cheddar, eggs, bread crumbs, ranch and coconut oil
3) Mix thoroughly by hand
4) Pour and spread into a baking dish
5) Cover and bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees

Submitter: 
Will Lee
Description: 
I had chicken bacon ranch pizza for lunch and realized how much more I would enjoy it in loaf form (as I do many things).
