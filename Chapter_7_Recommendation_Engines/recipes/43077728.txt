Title:
Big Chewey Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c + 2tbsp flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp bkg soda
3/4 c. butter
1 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. white sugar
1 egg
1 egg yolk
2 tsp vanila
1 c. chips

Directions:
mix flour, salt, soda and set aside
mix melted butter and sugars
add eggs and yolk
add vanilla
mix in dry ingredients
add chips
make 1/4 cup balls
break in two and smash together
bake at 325 for 12-18 minutes
