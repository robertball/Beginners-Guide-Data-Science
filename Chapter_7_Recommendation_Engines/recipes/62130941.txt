Title:
Easy Pumpkin Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4 oz serving of sugar free instant vanilla pudding mix
1 cup canned pumpkin
1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
2 cups skim milk
1/4 cup whipped topping
optional:
chopped pecans

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine pudding mix, pumpkin, spice and milk
Mix well using wire whisk or handheld beater.
Pour into 4 dessert dishes.
Top each with whipped topping and pecans.
Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Casey Vermeland Paris
Description: 
Easy to fix in the fall when your needing a pumpkin fix! The kids will love the creamy pudding and whipped topping and it's easy to clean up!
