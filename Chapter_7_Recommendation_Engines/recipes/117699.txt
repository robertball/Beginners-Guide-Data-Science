Title:
Hot Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/549403.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, mayonnaise and Parmesan cheese until blended.  Stir in spinach and mozzarella cheese.  Spoon into an ungreased microwave-safe 9-in. pie plate.
Microwave, uncovered, on high for 4-5 minutes or until bubbly, stirring twice.  Serve with crackers or vegetables.

Submitter: 
Janie Obermier
Description: 
For a different take on traditional spinach dip, Janie Obermier of St. Joseph, Missouri warms this rich mixture in the microwave.  It's terrific with crackers, tortilla chips or fresh vegetables.
