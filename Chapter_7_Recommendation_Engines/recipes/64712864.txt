Title:
Stott Christmas Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5640650.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c sugar
1 c butter
2 eggs
4 c flour
1 tsp. baking soda dissolved in 3T hot water
Grated rind of one orange
3 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar. Beat in eggs and orange rind. Work in flour and cinnamon. Add soda water. Chill and roll.
Bake 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes.
