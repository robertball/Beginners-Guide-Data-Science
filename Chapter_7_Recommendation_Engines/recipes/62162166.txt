Title:
Fig and Prosciutto Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pizza crust recipe
1 c fresh figs, pureed
1/2 c feta cheese, crumbled
1/4 lb sliced prosciutto
balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 500 degrees.
Make your pizza dough according to directions and create a large round crust on a baking stone.
Take the pureed figs and spread over the crust as the sauce.
Crumble the feta cheese across the top of the pizza evenly.
Place the strips of prosciutton evenly across the top of the pizza.
Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until crust is crisp and brown.
Drizzle balsamic vinegar over the top of the pizza.
Cut and serve.

Submitter: 
Ms. Munchie
Description: 
Taking a great appetizer and turning it into a pizza! Blend the sweetness of fig and balsamic with the saltiness of feta and prosciutto.
