Title:
Delicious and Easy Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar Kraft Marshmallow Creme
1 8 oz package original Cream Cheese

Directions:
Cream cheese must be at room temperature. Mix both ingredients together until very smooth. Serve with any type of fruit.

Submitter: 
Nicole
Description: 
This is a fabulous super easy fruit dip. With only 2 ingredients. Serve with any kind of cut up fresh fruit. Our favorite is strawberries, bananas, apples, and cantalope.
Everyone will wonder what it is. Make 3 or 4 batches and take to party, it will be a hit. I promise!
