Title:
Speedy Spaghetti Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 cup green pepper, chopped
1 cup sliced mushrooms
2 cups water
1 8oz package spaghetti, broken in 2" pieces
2 cups of your favorite pasta sauce
2 tablespoons minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook ground beef and onions, peppers, & mushrooms in a 10" skillet until beef is no longer pink.
Add water, bring to a boil, add spaghetti.
Cook spaghetti and beef for 10 minutes, stir occasionally.
Add pasta sauce and seasonings, stir to combine, bring to a boil, reduce heat.
Simmer 5 minutes to combine flavors.
Top with cheese and remove from heat, cover to allow cheese to melt.
