Title:
Chicken Long Rice (Hawaiian style chicken soup) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of bean thread
3-4 chicken breast
1 small onion
2 to 3 cups of chopped bok choy or shredded cabbage your choice
3 slices a ginger
3 cloves of garlic
3 bay leaves
1 tablespoon of soy sauce
1 tablespoon of Patis (fish sauce)
1 quart chicken broth
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Soak the beans to thread in a large bowl with warm tap water
In a large pot boil 6 to 8 cups of water enough to submerge chicken
crush and slice ginger and garlic
sliced your onions thin
Chop up the bok choy or shred your cabbage whatever you chose to use
After prepping your vegetables take out your chicken from boiling water and shred well with two forks then place the shredded chicken back into the boiling water and add your chicken broth
Bring your heat down to medium high add your onions garlic ginger fish sauce and soy sauce salt  pepper and bring to a boil
When your onions are translucent drain the water from the bean thread and cut with cooking  shears to desired length
Add the bean thread to the soup when the beans threads becomes translucent you're ready to add your choice of cabbage or bok choy
Give it a good stir and serve over steam rice
