Title:
The Great Grape Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/942577.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust
1 cup softened butter
3 cups Flour
1/2 cup Sugar
2 tablespoons Cinnamon
1  1/2 tablespoons Vanilla
Half and half
Filling
Diced apples about 6 gala
Cut grapes in half 2 cups seedless
Topping
1/2 cup Softened Butter cut into chunks or pieces
1 cup Flour
1 cup Sugar
2 tablespoons Cinnamon
2 tablespoons Vanilla

Directions:
The great grape apple pie
Crust
Mix together with mixer
1 cup softened butter
3 cups Flour
1/2 cup Sugar
2 tablespoons Cinnamon
1  1/2 tablespoons Vanilla
Half and half - add in while mixing... Until a soft dough forms
Sprinkle flour on counter pour dough on counter and sprinkle with flour. Knead lightly.
Cut into 2 pieces. Wrap in plastic. Let rest in refrigerator for 1 hour.
Remove from refrigerator and press into pie plate.
this is enough for 2 pies. Place a piece of foil on top the pie crust, shape it like a big bowl and put a layer of dried beans on top. This will keep the pie crust from bubbling up.
Bake for 45 mins.  350 degrees
Filling
Dice apples
Cut grapes in half
In a pan on medium heat put in 1  1/2 sticks butter cut up , pour the apples and grapes on top that, add 1 cup sugar, 2-3 tablespoons cinnamon (more If you want ;-), 2-3 tablespoons vanilla (more if you want ;-) , stirring occasionally, bring to a light boil. Cook 15-20 minutes till apples are softened up.
Pour into pre baked crust
Topping
1/2 cup Softened Butter cut into chunks or pieces
1 cup Flour
1 cup Sugar
2 tablespoons Cinnamon
2 tablespoons Vanilla
Mix all together with your hands until a crumble mixture is formed
Pile on top of apple and grape mixture
Bake for 1 hour 15 minutes at 350
