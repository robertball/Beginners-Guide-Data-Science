Title:
Guinness and Beef Irish Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 lbs lean beef stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes
1/4c flour
1 generous pinch each salt, black pepper, cayanne pepper
1TBS vegetable oil
4 peices cooked bacon sliced into small pieces, and fat reserved
2 large onions chopped into eighths and then layers seperated
2c. baby carrots
2-3 stalks celery, chopped
6oz can of tomato paste
2c beef broth
1.5c Guinness beer
1TBLS Worstechire Sauce
2 bay leaves
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1tsp paprika
1/4c brown sugar (can be increased or decreased dependig on chef's preference.)
*Optional:  3 red potatoes chopped into 1 inch cubes.

Directions:
Sift flour, salt, black pepper, and cayanne pepper together.  Dredge the beef cubes in this mixture.
Brown sliced bacon in a deep skillet. Remove bacon to a paper towel but retain the fat.  Add beef to the skillet and brown on all sides.  Add onions, celery and garlic to the same skillet.(Add oil if needed)
Add the tomato paste along with about 2 TBS of water.  Re-add bacon.  Stir to blend
Reduce heat, cover pan, and allow to cook for 5 minutes.
Pour half of the beer into the pan and scrape off any bits stuck on the bottom as it begins to boil.  Add the rest of the beer, beef broth, worstechire sauce, bay leaves, paprika, brown sugar, carrots and potatoes.
Cover, reduce heat, and simmer for 2-3hrs, stirring occasionally.
Taste and adjust seasoning before serving.  Can garnish with chopped fresh parsely if desired
