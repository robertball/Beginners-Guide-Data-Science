Title:
Nachos!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 bag of tortilla chips
1 lb. of ground meat browned with taco seasoning
1 small can of jalapeno cheddar cheese sauce
12 oz. of mexican shredded cheese
2 large tomatoes, diced
1/2 medium onion, chopped
shredded lettuce
salsa and sour cream to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees
1. Chop vegetables and put aside.
2. Brown ground meat and add seasoning.
3. Arrange tortilla chips on baking sheet.
4. Layer meat on chips, then spread jalapeno cheese and add onions.
5. Bake nachos for 5 minutes.  Then top with vegetables, salsa and sour cream. ENJOY!!
