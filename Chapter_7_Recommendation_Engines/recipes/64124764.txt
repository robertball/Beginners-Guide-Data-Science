Title:
Beef Tenderloin for a Dinner Party Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole beef tenderloin (it helps to have butcher cut off silver skin)
Garlic powder
Marinade....
1/3 c red wine
1/3 cup oil
1/3 c soy sauce
1 tsp ginger
1 tsp lemon

Directions:
Sprinkle garlic powder all over tenderloin.
Place 1/3 to 1/2 of marinate in plastic bag with tenderloin and marinate for at least two hours.
When ready to cook, preheat oven to 450 degrees. Place tenderloin on a wire rack in roasting pan. Bake for 45 minutes and baste regularly with remaining marinade. (Beef will be medium rare in the middle and medium on the ends.)
