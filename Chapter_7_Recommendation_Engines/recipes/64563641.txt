Title:
Panini Florentine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 small boneless, skinless chicken breast halves (1 lb)
1/2 cup Kraft Good Seasons Classic Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing made with EVOO
8 slices whole grain bread
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup fresh spinach leaves
2 roasted red peppers, cut in half
4 low-moisture part skim mozzarella cheese slices

Directions:
Marinate chicken in dressing in glass dish in refrigerator, 30 minutes; discard marinade.
Cook chicken in skillet on medium heat 5 minutes on each side or until cooked through (165F).
Spread bread with mayo, fill with remaining ingredients to make 4 sandwiches. Cook in panini maker on medium heat 5 minutes or until golden brown.
