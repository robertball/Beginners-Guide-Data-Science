Title:
Mom's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
1/2 medium onion, chopped
1/2 green pepper, chopped
1 16 oz can chopped tomatoes, with juice
1 16 oz can tomato soup, ready to serve
1 16 oz can kidney beans, drained
1/2 Tbsp chili powder
1/2 tsp paprika

Directions:
In a large saucepan, brown ground beef and onion.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer for at least 30 minutes.
