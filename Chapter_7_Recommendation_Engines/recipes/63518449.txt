Title:
Flank Steak with Bourbon-Cinnamon Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/960731.jpg
Ingredients:
3 fl. oz. bourbon
3 fl. oz. shoyu
1½ Tbsp. brown sugar
1½ tsp. minced garlic
¾ tsp. ground cinnamon
1 flank steak (about 2 lbs.)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a re-sealable plastic bag. Marinate in refrigerator for 4-24 hours.
Spray a large skillet with cooking spray and heat over high heat. Sear the steak for 5 minutes, turn, and sear for 5 minutes more. Allow to rest for several minutes, then slice across the grain.
