Title:
Codie's Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c flour
2 c sugar
5 T. cocoa
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
3/4 c. softened margarine
1 c. milk
1 tsp soda
1 c. hot water
Filling:
1/2 c milk
1/2 c sugar
2/3 c Crisco
1 c powdered sugar
Frosting:
1 stick oleo
3 T. cocoa
5 T. milk

Directions:
Mix the top ingredents well. Spread on a jelly roll pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 18-20 minutes. Let it cool.
Filling: Add the first three ingredents and beat for 5 minutes. Then add 1 c. of powdered sugar and beat for another 5 minutes. Spread over cooled cake.
Frosting: Bring all the ingredents to a rolling boil. Take off the stove and add 2 cups of powdered sugar and beat with a mixer. Spread over filling.
