Title:
South-of-the-Border Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 avocados
1/2 c. prepared salsa
2 tbsp. lemon or lime juice

Directions:
Cut avocados in half, remove pit and scoop out avocado meat.
In a bowl mash avocados with a fork to desired texture.
Add salsa and lemon or lime juice.
Serve immediately, or cover securely with plastic wrap and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Shannon :)
Description: 
It doesn't get any easier than this. Avocado, prepared salsa and lemon juice in a blissful combination to create a creamy zesty dip. Serve with tacos, flautas, fajitas, or ordinary tortilla chips for a taste sensation!
