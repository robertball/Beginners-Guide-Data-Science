Title:
Chinese Bhel Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup napa cabbage, julienned and liquid drained
1 cup chopped tomatoes, liquid drained
1 cup julienned yellow bell peppers
1 cup julienned orange bell peppers
1 cup beansprouts, washed and dried
1 seedless english cucumber, julienned and liquid drained
1 carrot, shredded
1 cup chopped green onions
1 cup chopped coriander (cilantro)
1 cup canton noodles, broken into small pieces
2 cup roasted peanuts
4 tablespoonfuls of sesame seeds
1 tablesppon freshly chopped red chili peppers
1 tablespoon sugar
6 tablespoons red chili paste
4 tablespoons ketchup
2 tablespoons seasoned rice vinegar
2 tablespoons sesame oil

Directions:
Make sure all liquids are drained from the chopped vegetables by using a salad spinner, toss together and place in serving bowl.  Top with coriander and green onions.
Make szechuan sauce: in a medium bowl, stir together freshly chopped red chili peppers, sugar, red chili paste, ketchup, seasoned rice vinegar and sesame oil until combined.
When serving, allow guests to help themselves to the salad and add their own sauce and garnishes (peanuts and sesame seeds) before tossing everything together in their own plates.

Submitter: 
moosie
Description: 
Inspired by the Indian snack Bhelpuri (puffed rice & onion in tamarind sauce) we decided to do a Chinese version using crunchy noodles, green onions and szechuan sauce.
