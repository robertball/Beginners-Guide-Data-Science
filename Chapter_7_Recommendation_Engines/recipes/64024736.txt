Title:
Creamy Avocado Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1118042.jpg
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, cooked and diced
1 ripe avocado, sliced
1/2 C sour cream
1 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp lime juice
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Pulse chicken breasts in food processor until finely chopped.  Add all remaining ingredients and process on low until fully combined and almost pur√©ed.
