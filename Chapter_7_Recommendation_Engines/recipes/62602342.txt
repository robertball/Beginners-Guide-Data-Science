Title:
Deli Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cooked and diced or chunked chicken-about 5 breast halves
1/2 jar of sweet gerkin pickles-diced with half the juice from the jar.
1/2 red onion-diced
2 cups mayonaise

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and chill

Submitter: 
Jeanni Kistler
Description: 
This is the chicken salad we make in the deli where I work.  It's simple and delicious.  I always hated chicken salad until I started working there.  Now I can't stop eating it!
