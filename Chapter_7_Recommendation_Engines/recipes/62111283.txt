Title:
Goddess Beet Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bunch of Fresh Beets or 2 cans of small whole beets
1/8 cup olive oil
1/4 cup chopped garlic
1/3 cup chopped onion
1  4oz package of Feta Cheese
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Boil the Fresh Beets until soft and cut into bite size chucks.  If you use the canned beets just cut into the size you'd prefer
In a medium saucepan sautee the onion and garlic in the olive oil till its nice and translucent ( cooked nice and clear)...don't burn the garlic..   :)
After your onions and garlic are cooked I recommend that you let the mixture cool for 1/2 hour then add the Feta Cheese. ( Let Cool So Cheese Doesn't Melt ).
Salt & Pepper to taste and serve on lettuce garnish.

Submitter: 
ADAMGODES
Description: 
This has got to be one of my favorite creations.  Its a simple beet salad that has a wonderful taste of garlic and onion with crumbles of Feta cheese.  I served this to a group of 20 and 19 wanted the reciepe.
