Title:
Patty's Dill Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups sour cream
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup and 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 Tablespoon onion powder
1/2 Teaspoon garlic powder
1 Tablespoon parsley
1/2 Tablespoon worchesteshire sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and chill. serve with veggies or try as a sauce for potato salad.
