Title:
Apple Ugly Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. sugar
3 c. flour
2 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 1/3 c. vegetable oil
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 c. raisins
4 c. peeled, cored, chopped apples

Directions:
In a very large mixing bowl, stir together sugar, flour, baking soda, salt & cinnamon.  Stir in oil, eggs, and vanilla.  Mix thoroughly, batter will be very thick.  Add apples and raisins.  Turn into a 9" x 13" pan.  Bake for 1 1/2 hours at 325 degrees.

Submitter: 
MTWOMAN
Description: 
Don't let the name of this recipe fool you.  It may not be the prettiest cake you've ever seen, but it sure is the delicious!  I've made this for a tea party, office luncheon, brunch and dessert.  It's always a hit.  Delicious served with vanilla ice cream.
