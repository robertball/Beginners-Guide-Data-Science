Title:
Cherry Ice Box Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup butter flavor shortening
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 teaspoon almond extract
2 3/4 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 10 ounce jars maraschino cherries, drained and chopped
Red food coloring, as needed

Directions:
Beat butter and shortening. Add sugar, cream well. Mix in egg, vanilla and almond extract. Sift together the flour, baking powder and salt. Stir into the creamed mixture. Pat cherries dry between paper towels. Stir in the cherries and enough red food coloring to make dough light pink. Cover and chill 2 hours. Shape cold dough into two logs. Wrap in waxed paper and chill again for about 2 hours. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Prepare cookie sheets with parchment paper. Slice cookies 1/4 inch thick and bake 8-10 minutes. Cool one minute on sheets, then remove to wire racks tom cool completely.
