Title:
Tex/Mex Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef.
1 cup panko bread crumbs.
1 large egg.
1 8 OZ can of chopped green chiles drained.
16 OZ your favorite mild salsa.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 Degrees. In a large mixing bowl combine ground beef, egg, chiles and bread crumbs. Mix well with your hands or a potato masher. Shape into a loaf and place into a 9x5 inch loaf pan. Cover loaf evenly with salsa. Bake for 45 min and take the temperature with an instant read thermometer. Continue baking if necessary until you reach 160 degrees.

Submitter: 
Bruce Stone
Description: 
This is a quick and easy meatloaf from the heart of Texas.
