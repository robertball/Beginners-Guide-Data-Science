Title:
Shirley & Johns butter tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1003679.jpg
Ingredients:
Pastry:
5 cups flour
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp sugar
1 egg & water to = 3/4 cup
1 lb lard
Filling:
3 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup melted butter
4 eggs beaten
2 tbsp vinegar
2 tsp vanilla
2 cup raisins (optional)

Directions:
Mix flour, salt and sugar together.  With pastry blener or two knives cut in lard until mixture is in small clumps the size of peas.  Beat egg and water together, with fork.  Add to flour mixture stirring with fork.  Mix together with hands and form into ball. Make 6 balls. Roll out on floured surface.  DO NOT OVERWORK THE DOUGH.
Mix all ingredients and mix until well conbined. fill tarts 2/3 full.  Bake 350 for 25 to 30 minutes.   or 375 for 18 to 20.
