Title:
HOT SPICED CIDER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 GALLON CIDER
2 CINNAMON STICKS
4 WHOLE ALL SPICE
10 TBS. BROWN SUGAR
4 WHOLE CLOVES
1/2 THINLY SLICED ORANGE, UNPEELED

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and cook on high 8-10 minutes.
Stir halfway between cooking time.
Serve steaming hot.
Keep cider on stove at low setting and serve from stove.  The aroma is appealing.

Submitter: 
Mara
Description: 
Your house will smell wonderful when your guests arrive. And the cider is perfect for a winter day.
