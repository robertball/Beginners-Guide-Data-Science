Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups rice (using the cup that comes with a rice cooker)
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup minced onion
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic
chicken stock to reach the 3 cup line on the rice cooker (approx  8 - 10 ounces)
3/4 cup chunky salsa

Directions:
Rinse rice and set aside to dry.
Sautee onion in  half the olive oil. Remove from skillet and set aside.
Add the other half of the oil and saute rice until lightly brown.  Stir in spices and place rice into the rice cooker.
Add enough stock to reach the 3 cup line on the side of the rice cooker (and a little more so that it's above the line)
Add salsa... stir.
Cover and turn on the rice cooker.
