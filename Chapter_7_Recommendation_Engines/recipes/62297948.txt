Title:
Quick Mexican Style Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 Cups Instant White Rice
1/2 Cup Instant Brown Rice
1-1/2 Tablespoons Chicken Bouillon Powder
1/2 - 15oz Can Tomatoes Diced (Blend in blender until smooth, freeze extra or discard)
2 Cups Water

Directions:
Bring water, chicken bouillion, and tomato puree to boil.
Once boiling add white and brown rice and stir together.  Cover with lid and remove from heat, let stand for 5-7 minutes. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This can be made in 15 minutes.  It is great when you need a quick side.
