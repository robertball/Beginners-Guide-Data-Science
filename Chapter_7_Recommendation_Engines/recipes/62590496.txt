Title:
Chicken Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
3-4 potatoes, cut into wedges
1/4 c Italian dressing
1/4 c Parmesan cheese
1t oregano

Directions:
Place chicken and potatoes in sprayed 9x13 pan.
Top with dressing and cheese and oregano.
Bake 30 minutes @ 400 with foil, 30 minutes without.
