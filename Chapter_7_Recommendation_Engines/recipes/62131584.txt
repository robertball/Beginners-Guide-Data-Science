Title:
Trishie's Toblerone Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6oz caster sugar
125 ml hot water
8oz unsalted butter
200g Toblerone bar
4oz SR flour
1oz plain flour
1oz cocoa
2 beaten eggs
2oz (60g) milk chocolate

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 160 *C
Put sugar butter and half toblerone with 125ml hot water in a pan.
Stir until all melted and cool slightly.
Sift flours and cocoa into another bowl.
Add melted chocolate mixture and whisk until just combined. Gradually add eggs, (do not over beat).
Pour into a lined 7 or 8 inch tin.
Scatter remaining toblerone on top
Bake for 45 mins
Melt the milk chocolate and pipe or drizzle on top when removed from oven.
Put in fridge for 10 mins to cool.
Cut into slices
Enjoy
