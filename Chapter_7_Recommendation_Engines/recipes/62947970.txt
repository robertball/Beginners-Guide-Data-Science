Title:
Honey Chicken Stir fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, sliced
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 onion sliced
1 green bell pepper, sliced
6 oz snow peas
1/4 cup honey
2 TBLS sliced almonds
2 TBLS olive oil
2 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Heat 1 TBLS oil in wok or skillet on medium high heat; cook chicken with ginger and garlic, stirring frequently until cooked through (about 4-5 min); remove from pan.
Add remaining oil, add onion, pepper and peas; cook until tender crisp (about 4 min)
Add honey and stir veggies until well coated
Add chicken and stir until heated through
Sprinkle almonds on top
Serve over rice with low sodium soy sauce on the side
