Title:
Pork Chops with Apples, Onions, and Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
salt and pepper to taste
1 onion, sliced into rings
1 sweet potato, sliced
2 apples - peeled, cored, and sliced into rings
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Season pork chops with salt and pepper to taste, and arrange in a medium oven safe skillet. Top pork chops with onions, sweet potatoes, and apples. Sprinkle with brown sugar. Season with pepper and salt.
Cover, and bake 50 minutes to an hour in the preheated oven, until sweet potatoes are tender and pork chops have reached an internal temperature of 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).

Submitter: 
Amy Cothran Gray
Description:
