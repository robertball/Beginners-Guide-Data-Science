Title:
corned beef dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can corned beef
1 pkg dry onioin soup mix
1 16oz container of sour cream

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl and chill for 1/2 hour. Serve with crackers
