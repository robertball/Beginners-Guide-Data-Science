Title:
Caramel Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine
2 cups white sugar
4 eggs, beat well
2 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cups cocoa
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups chopped pecans
SECOND LAYER
1 cup margarine
2 tbsp. corn syrup
1 can sweetened condensed milk
4 tbsp. flour
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
THIRD LAYER
3 tbsp. margarine - melted
1/4 cups milk
2 cups icing sugar
1/4 cups cocoa

Directions:
Cream together margarine and sugar, add eggs and vanilla.  Mix in dry ingredients
spread in large cookie sheet (10x15) and bake until just done at 350 F (20-25 min)
Cook second layer until thick and caramelized.  Pour over first layer - bake 10 min more in oven
Beat third layer well and spread on cooled cake
