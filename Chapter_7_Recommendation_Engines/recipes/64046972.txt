Title:
Mexican Ham and Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1125076.jpg
Ingredients:
1 20 ounce package of hash browns
1 cup cooked ham, chopped
1 cup mixed (green, red, yellow or orange) peppers
2 cups low fat Mexican cheese blend
3/4 cup Egg Beaters (or 3 eggs)
1 cup low fat sour cream
1/2 cup almond milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
salsa (optional)
sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a large (2.8 liter) casserole dish with cooking spray.
Combine the hashbrowns, ham, peppers and cheese in a large bowl.
In a medium bowl, mix together the eggs, sour cream, almond milk, salt, chili powder and onion powder. Add the egg mixture to the hashbrowns. Combine and add to the greased casserole dish.
Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until the top is a golden brown.
Let the dish cool for 5 minutes. Serve with salsa and sour cream on the side.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
This Mexican-inspired dish is a delicious combination of ham, cheese and peppers. An alternative to traditional ham and cheese casseroles, it can be served for breakfast/brunch or as a main dish, and it tastes as good as it looks! The use of low fat/low calorie ingredients also makes it a healthier alternative.
