Title:
Lorna's Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 Can cut Green Beans - drained
1 Can cut Yellow Beans - drained
1 Can mixed Beans - drained
1 Small onion sliced thin
Some yellow/red peppers cut up small
1 Small zuccini sliced and quartered
2 Stalks of celery sliced thin
Dressing:
2/3 Cup Vinegar
2/3 Cup Sugar
2/3 Cup Canola Oil
1/2 tsp dry dill

Directions:
1.  Mix together salad ingredients in large bowl.
2.  Heat dressing ingredients in sauce pan until sugar disolves.
3.  Pour dressing over salad and gently toss.
4.  Marinate for at least 2 hours
