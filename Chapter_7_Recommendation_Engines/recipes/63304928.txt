Title:
Ice Cubes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water (approximately)
addtional water, if needed

Directions:
Empty any ice cubes that are left in the trays into ice cube  bin.
Take trays over to the sink and fill them with cold water.
Carry carefully to the freezer, being careful not to spill on the floor.
Place water filled ice trays back into the freezer.
Place ice bin back into freezer, if you had to remove it.
Shut the door to the freezer.
They are ready to use when frozen solid.
Enjoy!
