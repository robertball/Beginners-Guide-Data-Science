Title:
Cinnamon & Honey Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4578179.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 1/2 tabelspoons white sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons vinalla extract
1 tablespoon honey
1 cup milk
2 eggs
1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients together in a large bowl
Mix all wet ingredients together in separate bowl
Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients slowly while stirring
Put small amount of butter in pan before each new batch of pancakes
Once bubbles start to pop, drizzle honey lightly on pancakes then flip to finish cooking.
