Title:
Fancy Pants Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of thin chicken breasts
Few slices of proscuitto
Half bag of baby spinach
8 oz package of fresh sliced mushrooms
1 envelop of Lipton Recipe Secret's Savory Herb & Garlic
1/2 cup white wine
1/4 cup olive oil
8 oz shredded Mozzarella

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Spray 13 x 9 pan with Pam
Put 2 layers of chicken in pan
Top with proscuito
Cover with spinach
Sprinkle with salt and pepper
Top with mushrooms
Whisk together oil, wine, and seasoning packet
Pour over all
Cover lightly with foil
Bake for 30 minutes
Top with cheese; bake 5 minutes more
