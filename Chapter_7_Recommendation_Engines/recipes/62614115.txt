Title:
Festive Layered Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3T canned artichoke hearts, chopped
2T pesto
8oz block cream cheese, well chilled
3T sundried tomatoes, chopped
2T smoked almonds, chopped
2t fresh parsley, chopped
Triscuits

Directions:
Mix artichokes and pesto.
Cut cream cheese into 3 layers.
Spread tomatoes between first two layers and artichoke mixture between next two layers.
Press nuts and parsley into top.
Wrap with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least an hour.
Serve with Triscuits.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
Cream cheese can be cut with dental floss
