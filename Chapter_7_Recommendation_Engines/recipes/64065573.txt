Title:
Applesauce Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups baking mix
6 tablespoons sugar
4 tablespoons applesauce
1/3 cup milk
cinnamon n nutmeg to taste

Directions:
Drop spoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet. (They will spread so space them with room to "grow".)
Bake some 5-8 mins in 350* oven.
Great warm.
