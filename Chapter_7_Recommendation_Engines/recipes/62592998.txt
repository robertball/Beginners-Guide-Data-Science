Title:
Pork and Sweet Potato Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked pork
.5 t black pepper
2 cups fat-free, low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
6 garlic cloves, crushed
1 T olive oil
3 c cubed (1/2-inch) peeled sweet potatoes
1 c chopped onion
1 c quartered portobello mushrooms
1 c chopped fresh red peppers
.5 c chopped celery

Directions:
Pre-cook pork in the crock pot.
Add olive oil to a large skillet and saute potatoes and onions for 6 minutes.
Add red pepper, celery, mushrooms and cook for 3 minutes.
Add cooking broth, black pepper, and garlic. Bring to a boil.
Add pork.
Reduce heat to medium and cook for 20 minutes or until liquid is gone.
