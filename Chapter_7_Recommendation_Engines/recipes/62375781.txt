Title:
Lemon Poppyseed Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp. apple cider vinegar
2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
2 tsp. dijon mustard
2 tsp. honey
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp kosher salt
1/2 cup grapeseed oil
2 tbsp poppy seeds

Directions:
Whisk together vinegar, lemon juice, mustard, honey, onion powder and salt.  Slowly drizzle in oil, whisking constantly, until combined.  Stir in poppy seeds.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Sweet and tangy with a little bite of dijon mustard, this is a perfect compliment to green salads with fruit, such as apples and berries.
