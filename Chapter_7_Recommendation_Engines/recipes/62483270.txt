Title:
Skillet Pork and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large Pork Shoulder steaks
3-4 potatoes
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup milk
2-3 slices of onion
3 tbl spoons Marg/Butter/oil for frying
2 tbl spoons Worcester sauce
1 tbl Spoon each dried Dill, Garlic Powder, Salt, Pepper
2 tbl spoons Beef or Chicken Bullion
1/4 cup white wine

Directions:
Add oil/butter/Marg to deep skillet with a lid and add half of onions.
Add Pork and top with Worcester and remaining onion.
Add half of the salt/pepper/garlic powder and dried dill.
Cook 5 minutes each side covered.
Slice potatoes and place on top of Pork.
Combine wine, bullion, water and milk and then add.
Add remaining salt/pepper/dill and garlic powder.
Cook for 35 minutes covered until potatoes are tender. Cook a few minutes uncovered to reduce liquid if desired.
Serve

Submitter: 
Holleta
Description: 
Tender Pork and potatoes in a rich sauce cooked together in one pan on the stovetop.
