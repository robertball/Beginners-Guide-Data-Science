Title:
Picadillo basic recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 onion
1 green bell pepper
2 garlic cloves, chopped
1 tsp cayenne pepper
2 Tbsp cumin

Directions:
Fry ground beef in frying pan over medium heat until fat is released.
Add onions, green bell peppers, and garlic. Fry until softened.
add remaining ingrediants and cook on high heat until meat is fully brown.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Jessica Weber
Description: 
ground beef, green bell pepper, and onion fried together.  Serve with rice.
