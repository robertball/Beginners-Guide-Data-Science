Title:
Soft Oatmeal Scotchies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup applesauce
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
3 cups quick cooking oats
1 bag butterscotch chips

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla and applesauce. Combine flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon; stir into the creamed mixture. Mix in oats and butterscotch chips. Cover, and chill dough.
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease cookie sheets. Drop the dough by rounded tablespoon 2 inches apart on cookie sheets.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in preheated oven. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
