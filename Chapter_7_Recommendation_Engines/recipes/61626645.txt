Title:
Healthier Slow Cooker Chicken Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/281454.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed
1/8 cup butter
1 (4 ounce) package neufchatel
1 cup natural cream of chicken soup
1 tablespoon chopped carrot
1 tablespoon chopped parsley
1 tablespoon chopped onion
1 clove garlic
1 teaspoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon lemon zest
1 cup frozen peas
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a small blender or food processor combine the carrot, parsley, garlic, onion, lemon zest, salt and pepper. Process until finely chopped and transfer to the slow cooker. Add the chicken and butter and mix. Cook on low for 5 to 6 hours.
Add the neufchatel cheese, soup and peas and cook on high for another 1/2 hour or until heated through and warm.

Submitter: 
MakeItHealthy
Description: 
Cubed chicken breast cooked in the slow cooker with a creamy sauce mixture.  This healthier version includes peas, lowfat cream cheese,  butter instead of margarine, and natural cream of chicken soup. You also make your own seasoning mix instead of using a packaged mix. Serve over hot cooked rice or noodles, if desired.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken Stroganoff
