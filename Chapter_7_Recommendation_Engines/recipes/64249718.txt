Title:
Instant (Dry) Hot Chocolate Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 qt.) box non-fat dry milk
1 (2 lb.) can Nestles' Quick
1 (1 lb.) box powdered sugar
1 (6 oz.) jar coffee creamer

Directions:
Mix ingredients together in large container.
Store in jars.
For each cup of hot chocolate use 1/2 c. instant mix to a cup of boiling water. Stir.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Johnson
Description: 
Good hot chocolate mix to add to a cup of hot water and stir.
