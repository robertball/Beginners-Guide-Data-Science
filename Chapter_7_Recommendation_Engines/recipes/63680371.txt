Title:
Orange Envy Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 med. tomatoes
1 lg. cucumber, peeled if desired
1 lg. bunch fresh parsley
3 med. carrots

Directions:
Process tomatoes, cucumbers, parsley, & carrots through Juice Fountain™ using speeds 1, 4, & 5. Serve Immediately.
