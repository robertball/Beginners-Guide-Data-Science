Title:
Orange Dreamsicle Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can orange juice concentrate, thawed
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 (8 oz) container whipped topping, thawed
1 9-inch graham cracker crust
Red Food Coloring
Yellow Food Coloring

Directions:
In a bowl mix orange juice concentrate and sweetened condensed milk.  Fold in whipped topping.  Add food coloring to get the desired orange coloring. Pour into crust.
Refrigerate overnight to set, or put in the freezer ro 1-2 hours.

Submitter: 
Aunt Meg
Description: 
Orange, creamy, refreshing pie.  Perfect for a Summer evening!
