Title:
Turkey and Beer Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/769898.jpg
Ingredients:
3 celery stalks, chopped
2 green peppers, chopped
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
2 tablespoons garlic, minced
2 14.5 oz cans diced tomatoes
2 15 oz cans tomato sauce
1 6 oz can tomato paste
1 cup water
1 can garbanzo beans
1 can corn
3 tablespoons chili powder
1 tablespoon cumin
2 teaspoons ground mustard
2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
2 lbs. ground turkey
1 package turkey sausage
1/2 bottle of beer

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a medium pan, add sausage when hot and cook over medium heat with a lid, shaking to brown all sides. Remove from pan and drain, set aside.
In a large pot, saute celery, peppers and onion in a little olive oil until soft. Add garlic. Add turkey meat and brown. Use a strainer to drain any liquid and return meat and veggies to the pot.
Pour in canned tomatoes, sauce, tomato paste and water.
Add turkey sausage, spices, beer, corn, garbanzo beans and stir thoroughly.
Bring to a boil, then cover, and simmer for 1 1/2 hours.

Description: 
A healthier take on an old favorite. Absolutely delicious and a small bowl is quite filling.
