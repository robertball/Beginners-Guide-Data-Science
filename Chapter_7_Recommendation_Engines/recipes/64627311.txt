Title:
Roasted Avocados Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
avocados sliced into quarters
fresh lemon, lime or orange juice
avocado oil or olive oil
sea salt and papper

Directions:
preheat oven to 400 degrees
drizzle slices with citrus, oil, etc
roast in oven for about 15 minutes.  Can broil for a minute or two at the end
