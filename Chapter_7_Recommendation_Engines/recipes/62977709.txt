Title:
Baked Fish Fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of any fish
1 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/4 cup butter, melted
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/8 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
-Sprinkle fillets with salt and pepper.
-Place in a single layer on a well-oiled baking pan.
-Combine margarine, lemon juice and paprika.
-Cover fillets with the sauce.
-Bake at 350 degrees F, for 20 to 25 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.

Submitter: 
jiffins45
Description: 
Good recipe for Mackerel. Or any filleted fish. If using other fish, times may vary depending on type and thickness of fillets.
