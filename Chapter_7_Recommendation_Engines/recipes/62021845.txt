Title:
Roman Honey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups unbleached flour
1 tb yeast
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/3 cup honey
1 cup warm water
1 beaten egg

Directions:
Combine all ingredients at once and knead the dough for 10 minutes. Let rest for 2 minutes, then knead for 1 more minute. Shape dough and place on an oiled (or nonstick) baking tray. Let rise for 3 hours.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
When the dough has risen, place in oven for 20 minutes.

The kind of bread which would have been eaten in ancient Rome.
