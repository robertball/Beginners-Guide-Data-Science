Title:
Cream Cheesy Strudel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz. tubes refrigerated crescent rolls
2 8 oz. pks cream cheese, softened
1 egg
1/2 cup plus 2 tbs sugar, divided
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Arrange 1 tube crescent rolls in the bottom of an ungreased 13x9 baking dish, Mix cream cheese, egg, 1/2 cup sugar and vanilla, spread over crescent rolls. Cover with remaining crescent rolls, sprinkle with cinnamon and remaining sugar. Bake at 375 for 11-13 minutes. Cut into squares to serve.
