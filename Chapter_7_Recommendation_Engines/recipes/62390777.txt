Title:
Vanessa's Horchata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/424694.jpg
Ingredients:
2 2/3 cups of uncooked long grain rice
10 cups of water
1 cup of plain soy milk
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
4 sticks of cinnamon
1 cup of white sugar

Directions:
Put 1 1/3 cup of rice in the blender.
Add 2 cups of water and blend until roughly ground.
Add 3 cups of water and 2 cinnamon sticks and blend until sticks are broken up.
Pour mixture into a pitcher.
Repeat.
Let mixture sit at room temp or in refrigerator 4-5 hours or overnight. (the longer the better)
Strain mixture through a sieve and discard rice and cinnamon sticks.
Add soy milk, sugar, and vanilla.
Stir.
Serve chilled.
