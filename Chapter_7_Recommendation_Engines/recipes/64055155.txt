Title:
Steakhouse Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBL vegetable oil
1 medium red bell pepper, coarsely chopped
1 medium onion, coarsely chopped
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken, cut into bite sized pieces
2 TBL prepackaged chicken rub seasoning
2 cups cooked white rice
1 can Bush's Steakhouse recipe grillin beans

Directions:
1. Toss chicken pieces in prepared rub. Discard remaining rub.
2. Heat oil in 10" skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken, pepper and onion. Cook for 10 minutes until internal temperature for chicken reaches at least 165 degrees and vegetables are tender.
3. Reduce heat to medium-low. Add beans. Stir and simmer for 10 mins or until heated completed through.
4. Remove from heat. Serve on a bed of rice.
