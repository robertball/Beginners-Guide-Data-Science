Title:
Sweet Potato Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs sweet potatoes
2 C butter, softened
4 C white sugar
2 C milk
8 eggs
2 tsp ground nutmeg
2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 Tbsp vanilla extract
4 unbaked pie crusts

Directions:
1.  Boil sweet potatoes whole in skin for 40-50 minutes or until done.  Run cold water over the sweet potatoes, and remove skin.
2.  Break apart sweet potatoes in a bowl.  Add butter, and mix well with mixer.  Stir in sugar, milk, eggs, nutmeg, cinnamon, and vanilla.  Beat on medium speed until mixture is smooth.  Pour into an unbaked pie crust.
3.  Bake at 350 degrees F.  for 55-60 minutes, or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.  Pie will puff up like a souffle, and then will sink down as it cools.
