Title:
Sweet and Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can can pineapple and sauce
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup vinegar
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 bag meatballs
white rice

Directions:
Put all ingredients into pan until sauce thickens
Add frozen meatballs and simmer covered for approx. 10 minutes
Serve over rice
