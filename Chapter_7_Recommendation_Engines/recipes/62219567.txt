Title:
Creamy Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/314814.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter
12 oz white mushrooms (shredded or sliced thin)
1/2 cup dry white wine
4 cups chicken stock
1/2 cup fat free half and half
salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
Shred mushrooms in Food Processor (or slice thinly).
In medium saucepan, heat butter over high heat until foam subsides.
Add mushrooms, saute, stirring until liquid is evaporated and mushrooms begin to brown.
Add wine, bring to a boil and boil 1 min.
Add stock, salt, pepper, simmer for 2 min.
Puree (with immersion blender).
Add the cream and simmer 2-3 min.
