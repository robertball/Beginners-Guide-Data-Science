Title:
Cinnamon Bread I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
6 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
2 tablespoons margarine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Mix together flour, 1 cup sugar, baking powder, baking soda, 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon, salt. In separate bowl, mix buttermilk, oil, eggs and vanilla. Genltly mix both together.  Pour half into prepared loaf pan. Add 2/3's of topping made below.Add rest of bread mixture, and sprinkle rest of topping over smoothed batter.
Combine topping ingredients, mixing until crumbly.Using a knife, cut in a light swirling motion to give a marbled effect.
Bake for about 50 minutes. Test with toothpick. When inserted it should come out clean. Remove bread from pan to rack to cool.

This is a lovely way to start off your morning when you want a little something different than your usual. Note: If you don't have buttermilk you may substitute milk with 1 tablespoon vinegar to measure 1 cup.Modified from: Cinnamon Bread I
