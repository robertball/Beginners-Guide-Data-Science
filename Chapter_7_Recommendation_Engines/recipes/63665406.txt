Title:
Baked Cheddar Bacon Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018284.jpg
Ingredients:
1-8oz. cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
4 green onions, chopped fine, divided
1/4 cup mayonnaise (such as Hellmann'sÂ®)
1/2 pound bacon, cooked and crumbled, divided
1-1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
Assorted crackers, pretzel sticks, and bread cubes

Directions:
In a medium bowl, beat the cream cheese, sour cream, garlic powder and green onions (reserving 1 Tablespoon), until well blended.
Fold in bacon (reserving 2-3 Tablespoons) and cheddar cheese (reserving 1/4 cup), until combined.
Transfer to a greased small baking dish.
Top with reserved cheese, bacon and green onions.
Bake at 375 degrees 20-30 minutes, until hot and bubbly.
Serve with crackers, pretzels and bread cubes.
