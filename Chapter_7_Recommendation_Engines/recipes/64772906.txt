Title:
Macaron Filling - Lemon curd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2/3 cup sugar
1/2 stick butter
1/4 cup lemon juice

Directions:
1. Whisk together eggs and 1/2 cup of sugar in a medium size bowl.
2. In a saucepan, combine the remaining 1/3 cup sugar, butter and lemon juice and bring to a boil.
3. Temper the eggs by slowly whisking some of the hot liquid into the eggs. Whisk the warmed eggs into the hot juice in the sauce pan and cook over a medium flame. Stir constantly until the mixture thickens.
4. Strain the curd into a bowl and cool. Chill with plastic wrap covering the surface.
