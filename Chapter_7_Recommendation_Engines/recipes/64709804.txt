Title:
Hula's Oreo Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Oreo Crumbs
6 tsp Melted Butter
2 packs Cream Cheese
1/3 cup Powdered Sugar
1 tsp Vanilla
Crushed Oreos
2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream
1/3 cup Powdered Sugar
1 pack Unflavored Gelatin
1/2 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
1 cup Choc Chips

Directions:
Make the base with about two cups of crumbs with the melted butter.  Press it into the cheesecake base and chill.
Beat the room temp Cream Cheese and Vanilla with the sugar in the big mixer till smooth.
In a separate bowl, whip the cream and the other 1/3 cup sugar with the hand mixer till stiff peaks form.
Soften the gelatin in a little cold water for a few minutes and then add 1/2 cup of hot water and stir till the gelatin is dissolved.
Fold the cream cheese, cream, gelatin and oreo crumbs together and then spoon over the base.
Heat the Cream but do not boil.  Take off the heat and pour over the choc chips.  Leave to stand a few minutes then stir till choc is melted and ganache is smooth.
Gently spread ganache over the top of the cheesecake and decorate with halved Oreos
