Title:
FIRECRACKER COLESLAW Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/3 cup sugar
2 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
3 Tbsp. vegetable oil
2 Tbsp. juice in jar, pickled Jal.
1 - 2 tsp. favorite hot sauce
1/2 tsp. ground black pepper
1/2 tsp. celery seed
Slaw:
1 Sm. green cabbage, shredded
2 carrots peeled and shredded
1 red bell very thinly sliced
1 green bell very thinly sliced
2-4 fresh jals.,seeded & thin slice
2 Tbsp. minced pickled jalepenos

Directions:
Prepare the dressing.
Combine the salt and garlic in large bowl and mash to a paste with the back of a spoon.
Add the next 8 ingredients, whisk until the sugar and salt are dissolved.  Correct the seasoning, adding salt or sugar to taste: the dressing should be highly seasoned.
Stir in the remaining ingredients and toss to mix.  Let the slaw stand for at least 10 minutes or as long as 4 hours before serving.
