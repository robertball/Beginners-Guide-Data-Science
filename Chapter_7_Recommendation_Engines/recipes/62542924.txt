Title:
Elegant Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T. olive oil
1   2-lb. boneless pork loin
15 shallots, peeled and thinly sliced
3/4 c. white wine
1/4 c. warm water
2 T. balsamic vinegar
2 tsp. brown sugar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place 3 T. olive oil in a Dutch oven or heavy baking pan. On a stove top burner, heat the olive oil on medium-high heat. Add pork loin and cook until brown on all sides. Remove from the pan. Add remaining 1 T. olive oil and shallots to the pan and cook until shallots are wilted, stirring occasionally. Add white wine, water, balsamic vinegar and brown sugar. Mix well. Return pork loin to the Dutch oven. Cover and roast in oven for 45 min. Salt and pepper to taste. Allow to cool about 5 min. before slicing. Drizzle remaining pan juices over pork and serve.
