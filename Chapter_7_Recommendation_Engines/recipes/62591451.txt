Title:
Tortilla Soup I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 cups low salt low fat chicken broth
6 cloves roasted garlic
2 cans rotel tomatoes
1/2 yellow onion, chopped
2 fresh jalapeno peppers, sliced into rings
1 teaspoon dried oregan
1 tb cilantro
1/2 yellow onion, sliced
1 can black beans
2 cups shredded, cooked chicken meat
1 lime, juiced
6 (6 inch) corn tortillas, cut into strips and toasted for garnish

Directions:
In heavy pot, bring the broth to a boil. Add garlic, tomatoes, chopped onion, jalapeno, and oregano to the stocks. Simmer uncovered for 30 minutes.
Broil the sliced onions until soft and a little brown. Add broiled onions, chicken, lime juice to soup, and simmer till chicken is heated.
Place toasted tortilla strips in each bowl and pour soup over strips.

Submitter: 
Hana
Description: 
Shredded cooked chicken and broiled onion rings are added to chicken stock with a chopped jalapeno pepper, tomatoes and lime juice in this soup served with crushed tortilla chips, cheese and avocado.Modified from: Tortilla Soup I
