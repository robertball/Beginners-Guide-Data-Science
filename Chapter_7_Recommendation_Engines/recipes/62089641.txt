Title:
Ham and Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz blocks of Cream Cheese softened
1 can of devilled ham spread
1 package of Hidden Valley Ranch party dip mix
1 Cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 Cup diced vegetables (i.e. green peppers, green onion)
1/2 Cup nuts or sunflower seeds

Directions:
Mix softened cream cheese with powdered ranch dip, add in ham, shredded cheese and vegetables, mix well. Form into ball and roll through the nuts or seeds. refridgerate for at least 1 hour. Serve with crackers or fresh veggies.

Submitter: 
lisascooking
Description: 
This is a very simple but great party recipe. I made it the first time for a friends baby shower. It has ham, cream cheese and ranch dressing.
