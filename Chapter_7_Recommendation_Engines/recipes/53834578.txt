Title:
Orange Curd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 egg yolks
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup frozen orange juice concentrate, room temperature
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 tablespoon grated orange peel (optional)

Directions:
In the top of a double boiler, beat eggs and sugar.
Stir in orange juice concentrate, butter and orange peel.
Cook over simmering water for 15 minutes or until thickened.
