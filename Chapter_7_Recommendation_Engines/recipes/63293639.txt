Title:
Olive Garden Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag iceburg salad mix
1/4 whole red onion, sliced very thin
4 ounces sliced black olives, drained
6 whole pepperoncini peppers
2 whole roma tomatoes, sliced
1/2 cup seasoned croutons
grated parmesan cheese, to taste
freshly ground black pepper, to taste
DRESSING:
8 fluid ounces Newman's Own Vinegar & Olive Oil dressing
1 tsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1 tbsp sugar

Directions:
Add salad dressing seasonings directly to bottle of salad dressing. Shake well & refrigerate for 24 hours.
Mix all salad ingredients, add dressing to taste & toss.
