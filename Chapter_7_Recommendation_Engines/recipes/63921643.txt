Title:
Lemon Ice Box Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1087493.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
2 Tbsp butter, melted
3-6 Tbsp milk
3 tsp powdered gelatin
2 cups prepared vanilla pudding
2 cups low-fat firm yogurt (like Greek yogurt)
10 Tbsp powdered sugar
¾ cup lemon juice
1 tsp lemon zest
2 cup low-fat whipped topping, divided
1 Tbsp powdered sugar

Directions:
In a food processor, process cookie crumbs, melted butter and 3 Tbsp milk. Mix until crumbs form a ball. If necessary, add more milk, but the mixture should not be soggy. Press crumbs in bottom and up sides of a 9-inch pie dish.
In a small bowl, combine gelatin with ¼ cup cold water. Let stand 5-10 minutes.
Mix pudding, yogurt, sugar, lemon juice, lemon zest and 1 cup of low-fat whipped topping. Add gelatin mixture and stir until gelatin is dissolved.
Pour into cookie crumbs crust and refrigerate until set.
Top with remaining 1 cup whipped topping before serving and garnish with lemon slices.
