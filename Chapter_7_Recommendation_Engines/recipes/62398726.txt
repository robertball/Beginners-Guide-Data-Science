Title:
Orange Marmelade Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Flour
1/2 C sugar
2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
3/4 c milk
1 beaten egg
1/4 c butter
3/4 c chopped pecans
2/3 c melted marmalade

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients together. Add milk, egg and butter.  Mix in pecans and marmalade. Pour into well buttered muffin tin and place in a preheated 400 oven.
