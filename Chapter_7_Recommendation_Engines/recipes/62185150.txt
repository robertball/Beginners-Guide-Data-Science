Title:
Bulk Turkey Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4.5 pounds ground turkey (3 packages)
1 tablespoon and 3/4 teaspoon ground ginger
2 tablespoons and 1 teaspoons salt
2 Tablespoons dried sage
2 Tablespoons Thyme
1 teaspoon chili powder
2 tablespoons and 1 teaspoons ground black pepper
1/4 cup maple syrup

Directions:
For precooked sausage, divide into two batches, and saute for about 10 minutes, until crumbly. Maximum amount to fit in kitchenaid.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
Description: 
A quick and easy recipe for classic tasty breakfast sausage patties.  Great with pancakes and eggs. This recipe has a mild to moderate zing.  Increase cayenne and black pepper if you like more of a punch.  Thyme may be substituted for sage, or use both.Modified from: Mom's Turkey Sausage Patties
