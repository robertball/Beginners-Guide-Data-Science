Title:
Allergen Free Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ¼ c. Sorghum flour
½ c. Tapioca flour
1 ½ tsp. cream of tarter
¾ tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. Xanthum gum
¼ tsp. salt
¼ x. applesauce
¾ c. non-dairy milk
1 Tbsp.  Flax seed mixed with
3 Tbsp. water
1/3 c. chopped, dried dates
2 tbsp. non dairy milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 Degrees F. Grease baking sheet and set aside. Mix all ingredients together to form a ball of dough then flatten to 8-inch circle, ¾ inch thick. Brush top with 2 tbsp. milk. Bake for 12-15 minutes. Cut into wedges. Serves 6-8.

Submitter:

Description: 
My mom intented this recipe for gluten free, dairy free, sugar free scones, when we wer off all of the above. It's DELICIOUS...as well as nutritious!
