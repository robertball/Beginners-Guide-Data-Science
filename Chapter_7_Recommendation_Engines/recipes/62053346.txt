Title:
Creamed Corn and Cheddar Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup self-rising cornmeal,
1 cup self-rising flour,
3/4 cup buttermilk,
1 egg, lightly beaten,
1 cup creamed corn,
1 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese,
2 RBSP butter.

Directions:
Place a large cast iron skillet in the oven.  Pre heat to 425 degrees.  Mix together all ingredients, except butter;  add the butter to the hot pan until just melted.  Pour the batter into the hot pan.  Bake for 30-35 minutes until lightly browned.  Cut pizza style into pieces.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
A friend gave me this recipe.  It is so easy and so simple, but has such a wonderful taste.
