Title:
Dutch Oven Bratwurst for the Oven Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bratwurst package (about 5 or 6)
Fingerling potatoes
2 large onions, roughly chopped
Olive oil
Stone ground mustard

Directions:
Mix up a little olive oil with the mustard
Lightly grease a dutch oven with olive oil
Chop the onions
Add brats to dutch oven along with the fingerling potatoes and cover with chopped onions.
Pour the olive oil and mustard mixture over all.
]Bake at 400 degrees for about an hour, stirring it every 20 minutes

Lorraine Friberg

A good way to do a dinner easily.
