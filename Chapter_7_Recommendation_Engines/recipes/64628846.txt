Title:
Curry Fried Chicken Drumettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4472911.jpg
Ingredients:
8 pcs chicken drumettes
1 tsp cornstarch
olive oil
lettuce
2 tsp tomato ketchup
1 tsp honey
2 tsp soy sauce
1 tsp curry powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
1. In a small bowl, combine the ketchup, honey, soy sauce, curry powder, salt and black pepper then mix well.
2. Spread the marinade mixture all over each chicken drumettes then marinate for 1 hour.
3. Put the chicken inside a microwave-safe plate then sprinkle the cornstarch on top of each chicken. Drizzle with olive oil. Make sure that there are spaces in between each chicken to prevent the pieces from sticking with each other.
4. Cover the plate/bowl the. Cook for 8 minutes using 600W.
5. Tear the lettuce into easy to-bite pieces then arrange in a serving plate.
6. Put the chicken on top of the lettuce then you can serve.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
My attempt to cook fried chicken in a microwave-oven. I didn't get the crunchy fried chicken like what a deep-fried chicken would offer me but I was happy after eating because I don't have oily pans to clean. This is delicious and tasty.
