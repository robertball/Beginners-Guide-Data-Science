Title:
Muffuletta Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf of French bread, sliced
12 slices of Genoa salami
12 slices of mozzarella cheese
12 slices of ham
1 cup olive spread
1 stick butter, softened
1 tablespoon roasted garlic
green onions to garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Spray foil with nonstick cooking spray.
Slice bread, making sure to not cut through the bottom of the bread.
Place bread on foil.
Stuff bread between each slice with cheese, ham, and salami.
Mix butter, garlic and olive spread together.
Stuff bread with butter mixture make sure you get between each slice and on top and sides of
bread.
Wrap bread and foil and bake for 25 minutes.
Open foil and broil for 5 minutes until top of cheese melts well.
Garnish with green onions and serve hot.
