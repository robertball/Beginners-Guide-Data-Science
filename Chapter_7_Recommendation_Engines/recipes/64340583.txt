Title:
Cilantro and Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless & skinless chicken breasts
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp butter
Salt and pepper to taste
Paprika to taste
1/ 12 oz (350 gr) can of crushed tomatoes
1/  8 oz (210 gr) can of green salsa
8 oz of cilantro dressing.  You will find many easy to make  cilantro dressing recipes on line.

Directions:
Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper and paprika.
Brown chicken breasts in oil and butter, approx 7 minutes a side or until just barely cooked through.
Remove chicken from frying pan and cut on a diagonal into 1" slices, return to fry pan.
Add crushed tomatoes, green salsa, and cilantro dressing.
Bring to a simmer, cover and cook for another 5 minutes.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
I had some chicken breasts, left over tomatoes, salsa and cilantro dressing in the fridge.  This is what happened when I combined them.  Spicy and delicious!
