Title:
Pork Piccata with Lemon and Capers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1-lb. pork tenderloin, trimmed
3 T all-purpose flour
3/8 t salt
1/4 t freshly ground black pepper
2 T olive oil
5 garlic cloves, 3 thinly sliced and 2 minced
1/2 c dry white wine
1/4 c low sodium chicken broth
1 10-oz bag spinach
1 T unsalted butter
2 T freshly squeezed lemon juice
4 t capers, rinsed and drained
2 T finely chopped flat leaf parsley

Directions:
Cut pork against grain into 12 1/2-in slices; press each to flatten slightly. Combine flour w/ 1/4 t salt and 1/8 t black pepper in a shallow dish. Dredge pork. Heat 2 t oil in large sillet over med-hi heat; add half the pork. Cook until lightly golden, about 2 minutes. Turn and cook 1 minute more; set aside. Repeat with remaining pork, using another 2 t oil.
Add mindced garlic to empty skillet, stir 20 seconds. Add wine; cook 3 minutes. Add chicken broth, cook 2 minutes.
Meanwhile, heat another large skillet over med-hi heat. Add remaining oil and sliced garlic. Saute 1 minute, add remaining slat and black pepper, then spinach in batches; saute, tossing, 3 minutes.
Remove skillet with sauce from heat. Stir in butter, lemon juice, capers and parley. Divide spinach among four plates, place 3 slices pork on each; top with sauce.
