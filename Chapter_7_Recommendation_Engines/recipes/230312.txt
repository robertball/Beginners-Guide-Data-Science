Title:
Marshmallow Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3855028.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces vanilla-flavored vodka
3 fluid ounces pineapple juice
1 splash sweetened lime juice (such as Rose'sÂ®)
3 miniature marshmallows, or to taste

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Pour vodka, pineapple juice, and lime juice over ice; cover and shake. Strain cocktail into a martini glass. Garnish with marshmallows.
