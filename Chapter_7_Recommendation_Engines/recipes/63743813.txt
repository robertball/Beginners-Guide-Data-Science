Title:
Mashed Sweet Potatoes with honey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. unsalted butter
1/4 tsp. salt or to taste (optional)
1 tbsp. honey
1 - 1/2 lb. sweet potatoes peeled, cut in half lengthwise, and cut in 5 inches slices crosswise
3 tbsp. light cream

Directions:
1. Place sweet potatoes in a steamer basket over boiling water. Cover and steam for 20-25 minutes or until tender
2. Drain and rinse under cold water
3. Heat butter, cream, and honey in same saucepan over low heat.
4. Add cooked potatoes and mash until smooth
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste
