Title:
BBQ Chicken Wings-Linda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. butter
1 small onion
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
4 Tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. prepared mustard
1 tsp. paprika
1/2 c. catsup
1/4 c. white vinegar
3/4 c. water
4 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
Chicken:
1 dozen wings

Directions:
Saute onion in butter until clear.
Add remaining sauce ingredients and simmer for 1 hour.
Place 1 dozen chicken wings in 2 quart casserole.
Pour barbeque sauce over wings.
Bake in oven 350ยบ for about 1 hour or until done.
Hint: the longer you bake them the better they taste.

Submitter: 
Lela
Description: 
A mild barbeque sauce without hot peppers.
