Title:
Cinnamon Chips Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose biscuit baking mix
1/3 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 cup HERSHEYÂ®'S Cinnamon Chips
2/3 cup milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 F. Grease or paper-line 12 muffin cups (2-1/2-inches in diameter).
Stir together baking mix, sugar, vegetable oil, egg, cinnamon chips and milk in medium bowl just until moistened. Divide batter evenly into prepared muffin cups.
Bake 15 to 18 minutes or just until browned. Cool slightly; remove from pan.
