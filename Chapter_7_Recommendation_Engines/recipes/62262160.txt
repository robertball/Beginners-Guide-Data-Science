Title:
Fruit Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package Strawberry cream cheese, softened
1 (7 ounce) jar Marshmallow Creme
2 T. Orange Juice
1/2 t. vanilla
1 C. Cool Whip

Directions:
Using an electric mixer, blend cream cheese and marshmallow creme until mixed thoroughly.
Mix in the 2 T. Orange Juice, and 1/2 t. vanilla.
Stir in l C. Cool Whip.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
This is a really light and fluffy fruit dip that is great at parties, holidays, or any time! Serve with fruit of your choice.Modified from: Fruit Dip II
