Title:
Buffalo Chicken Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 chicken tenders, uncooked, unbreaded
2 t. cajun seasoning
1 large Boboli pizza crust
1/4 cup buttermilk ranch
1/2 cup Franks hot sauce, more or less to taste
1 T. butter
1 1/2 c. shredded mozzarella
garnish: pickled banana peppers,  crumbled blue cheese, opt.

Directions:
1. Preheat oven and pizza stone (opt.) at 400 degrees.
2.  Lightly sprinkle cajun seasoning over chicken tenders.  Coat skillet with light layer of cooking spray.  Add tenders cook 4 minutes on med-high on each side until lightly browned. Turn heat to low Using spatula, shred chicken into bite size pieces and remove.
3.   Add butter to skillet and melt.  Add hot sauce and mix.  Add chicken to skillet and coat , remove from heat.
4.  Spread ranch dressing on pizza crust.  Only add enough to lightly coat, ranch will thin and spread out when heated.  Spread chicken mixture and spread.  Top with mozzarella cheese and garnish if desired.
5.  Cook at 400 degrees for 12-15 minutes until lightly browned.
