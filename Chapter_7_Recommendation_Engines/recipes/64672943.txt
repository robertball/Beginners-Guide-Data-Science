Title:
Best Ever Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4588645.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Cups cranberries
2 Cups water
4 Mandarin type oranges, juiced
1.5 cups honey
2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground nutmeg
1 tsp ground allspice
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

Directions:
Put water and cranberries in a large sauce pan. Bring to boil. Add all other ingredients and simmer until berries are popped.
Cool, and enjoy.

Submitter: 
GraceGloria
Description: 
Cranberry Sauce with mandarin oranges and spices. The best cranberry sauce I have ever had, said one of my Thanksgiving dinner guests.
