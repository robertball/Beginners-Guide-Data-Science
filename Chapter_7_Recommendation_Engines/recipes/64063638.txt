Title:
Big Ray's Creamy  Rigatoni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130181.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups   (1/2 of 16-oz. pkg.) rigatoni pasta, uncooked
1 Tbl olive oil
2 zucchini, sliced into 1 inch rings
1 lb. sliced fresh mushrooms
3 cloves  garlic, minced
1 1/2 Tbl  flour
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
1 1/2 cup chicken broth
8 Neufchatel Cheese, cubed
1/2 cup sour cream
1 pkg (6 oz.) baby spinach leaves
1/4 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1-1/2 cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese, divided

Directions:
Heat oven to 375ÂºF.
Cook pasta in large saucepan as directed on package, omitting salt.
Heat oil in large skillet on medium heat. Add zucchini, mushrooms and garlic; cook and stir 3 to 4 min. or until zucchini is crisp-tender. Add flour and seasonings; cook and stir 1 min. Stir in chicken and broth; cook and stir 2 to 3 min. or until thickened. Add Neufchatel and sour cream; cook and stir 2 to 3 min. or until smooth.
Drain pasta; return to pan. Add zucchini mixture, spinach, Parmesan and 1/2 cup mozzarella; mix lightly. Spoon into 9 x 13  casserole sprayed with cooking spray; top with remaining mozzarella.
Bake 10 min. or until mozzarella is melted.
