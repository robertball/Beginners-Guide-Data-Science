Title:
Keto Chocolate Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pks Truvia
4 cubes frozen Avocado
1/4-1/2 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
1/4-1/2 cup Coconut Cream
2 tsp Cacao powder
Cinnamon to taste

Directions:
Place all in blender and enjoy.
Note: Refrigerate coconut cream so fat solidifies and Pour off liquid.
