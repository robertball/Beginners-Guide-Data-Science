Title:
Mashed Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic
One head cauliflower , chopped
1-1/2 tablespoons olive oil, butter or combo
1/4 cup coconut cream
1/2 teaspoons crushed thyme
scant tsp salt
freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Rub garlic with salt and olive oil, roast at 375 about 45 minutes, in a foil wrap.
Steam cauliflower and set in colander to let water drain out.
Combine all ingredients and mix with immersion blender.

Submitter: 
donnacim
Description: 
Really yummy substitute for mashed potatoes!  Adapted from http://www.dummies.com/how-to/content/paleo-recipe-for-mashed-cauliflower.html
**Make extra garlic to spread on toast or mix into other recipes.
