Title:
Vegatable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbs vegatable  oil
1 large white onion
3 carrots, sliced
3 stalks celery, diced
32 oz beef broth
12 oz V8
2-3 yellow  potato, diced
1 (14.5 ounce) can fire roasted diced tomatoes w/juice
1 cup green beans
1 cup  corn kernels
1 beef bullion cube
salt and pepper to taste
1 tbsp Cajun seasoning or to taste

Directions:
In a large stock pot sauté onions, carrots sand celery until softened. Add broth, tomato juice and potatoes. Bring to a boil, reduce to simmer, add tomatoes, green beans, and corn. Season with salt, pepper, bullion and Creole seasoning. Simmer for 30 minutes or until all vegetables are tender.
