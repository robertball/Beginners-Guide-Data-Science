Title:
Three Cheese Baked Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. package penne pasta
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons flour
1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup half-and-half
1/2 cup shredded white cheddar cheese
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 cup shredded swiss cheese, divided
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cook pasta according to package directions.
Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in the flour and cook, whisking constantly for one minute. Gradually whisk in half-and-half and milk and continue to whisk constantly for 5-6 minutes or until thick.
Stir in cheddar, parmesan, and 1/2 cup swiss cheese, salt, pepper, and 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg until the sauce is smooth.
Pour pasta into 1 lightly greased 11x7 baking dish. Pour cheese sauce over pasta and stir together. Top with 1/2 cup remaining swiss cheese and sprinkle remaining ground nutmeg on top as well.
Bake for 15-18 minutes or until top is slightly golden and cheese is bubbly.

Submitter: 
Alisan
Description: 
A cheesy pasta bake that's great for both adults and kids! Swiss, parmesan, and white cheddar cheese make this insanely delicious. Great for a family dinner, a romantic meal for a couple, or for a block party!
