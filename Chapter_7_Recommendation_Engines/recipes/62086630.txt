Title:
Shepherd's Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 round loaf french bread, halved
1 cup mayonaisse
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients (except bread) together. Spread light layer spread over halved bread. Wrap bottom half of bread in foil and bake approximately 20 minutes at 350. Cut into wedges and serve.

Submitter: 
J
Description: 
A nice change from the usual garlic bread to serve with pasta or steak and baked potatoes. Also good as an appetizer.
