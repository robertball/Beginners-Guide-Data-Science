Title:
Holiday Turkey Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Whole Wheat english muffins, split
2 tbs butter or margarine, softened
4 tsp mayo or salad dressing
1 1/2 cups chopped leftover turkey meat, warmed
1 cup leftover cornbread stuffing/dressing, warmed
1/2 cup leftover whole berry cranberry sauce, warmed

Directions:
Spread butter on the inside of each split english muffin half. Place muffin halves buttered side down in a large skillet and cook over medium high heat until toasted.
To assemble...
Place 2 muffin halves toasted side up onto microwave safe plates.
Spread 1 tsp mayo on one muffin half and then top with one fourth of the warmed turkey meat.
On top of the other toasted muffin half, press 1/4 cup of warmed stuffing and then top that with about 3 tsp of warm cranberry sauce.
Microwave each open faced sandwich for about 30 seconds.
Close each sandwich by quickly flipping the stuffing side atop the turkey side before serving.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
These delicious hot sandwiches are prepared using leftovers from Thanksgiving dinner; including turkey, dressing, and cranberry sauce.
