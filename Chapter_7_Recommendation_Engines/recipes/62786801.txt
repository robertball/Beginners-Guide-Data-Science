Title:
Honey Dijon Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon honey
1 tbsp chopped shallot
1/2 tsp minced garlic (or small clove)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a blender.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
My version - I adjusted some of the ingredients and I like to add either a shallot or a clove of garlic (sometimes a bot of both)Modified from: Honey Dijon Balsamic Vinaigrette
