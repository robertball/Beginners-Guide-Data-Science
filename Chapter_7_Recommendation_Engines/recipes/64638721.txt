Title:
Chipotle lime chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
2 limes juice only
1 jar of salsa
Pinto or black beans
1 can diced tomatos (drained)
Colby jack cheese

Directions:
1. Mix all ingredients together in a slow Cooker
2. Cook for 5-6 hours on low

Bill Girolamo
