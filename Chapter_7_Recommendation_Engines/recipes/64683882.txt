Title:
Low Carb Light Snack Cheesy Broccoli Cauliflower Tots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4977701.jpg
Ingredients:
1      Broccoli
1/2   Cauliflower
3      Eggs
1      Teaspoon Garlic powder
1/2   Teaspoon Salt
1/2   Teaspoon Pepper
1      Tablespoon Onion powder
1/2   Cup Coconut flour
1 1/2 Cup shredded mixed Mexican cheese blend

Directions:
Preheat oven at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Break broccoli into florets. Boil for 5 minutes.
Place 1/2 of the broccoli florets into food processor or Ninja chopper. Pulse until it's broken down into smaller, finer pieces. Repeat with the remaining broccoli florets.
Transfer the finely chopped broccoli into a clean cheese cloth and place it in a large bowl.
Twist the cloth and squeeze out the liquid.
Break cauliflower into florets and chop into small pieces using the food processor.
Transfer the finely chopped cauliflower into a clean cheese cloth and place it in a large bowl. Repeat moisture removal procedure by twisting and squeezing.
Mix both processed broccoli and cauliflower into a large bowl.
Add shredded cheese, mix well.
Add onion powder, garlic powder, salt, pepper and coconut flour. Mix well.
Crack 3 large eggs and mix well.
Scoop approximately 1 tablespoon of the mixture, roll into 1 inch tall and 3/4 inch diameter cylinder tot. Repeat with the rest.
Place them on a prepared baking sheet. You may spray them with olive oil.
Bake in the preheated oven. Switch and turn them around until evenly golden brown for 25-30 minutes.
Serve with dipping sauce of your choice.
