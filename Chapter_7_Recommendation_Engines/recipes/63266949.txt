Title:
Farafel Pasta with Gorgonzola Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2 tablespoons flour
1 large shallot, minced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups reduced fat milk
1/2 cup heavy cream
8 ounces gorgonzola cheese, sheredded
2 tablespoons olive oil
6 ounces thin asparagus, cut into 1 1/2" pieces
1 sweet onion, thinly sliced
1 10 oz package sliced mushrooms
2 roma tomatos, diced
Dash salt
Dash pepper
8 ounced farafel pasta

Directions:
Cook pasta according to direction, then place cooked pasta on bottom of casserole dish.
Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in saute pan. Add onions and cook until caramelized. Add mushrooms & asparagus and cooked until soft. Drain liquid from vegetables, and set mixture aside.
Melt butter in large sauce pan. Saute garlic and shallots in butter. Slowly add flour to butter mixture and whisk together.
Reduce heat to butter mixture and slowly add milk, whisking throughtly. Add gorgonzola to mixture, and whisk together. When cheese is throughly melted, add 1/2 cup cream to mixture. Add salt and pepper to taste Heat through.
Prior to serving, add asparagus mixture and diced tomoatos to cheese sauce. Pour over farafel pasta in casserole.

Description: 
This was inspired by a frozen packaged pasta dish. I experimented with ingredients and came up with my own version. I found this version to be the closest to the original.
