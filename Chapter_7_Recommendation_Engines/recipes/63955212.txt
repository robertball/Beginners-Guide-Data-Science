Title:
Mary Janes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Saltine crackers
12 Tbsp peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
12 regular size marshmallows
1 regular Hershey's plain chocolate bar (optional), broken into pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 defrees F.
Spread each cracker with 1 Tbsp peanut butter
Place one piece of chocolate bar on peanut butter (optional)
Place a marshmallow on each prepared cracker and place on ungreased baking sheet.
Bake for approximately 5-10 minutes or until marshmallow becomes partially melted and desired brownness is obtained.

Submitter: 
Scottley
Description: 
Quick, easy, and better for you and your family than a lot of other prepared sugary snacks.  Easy enough for the kids to help and you probably already have the ingredients on-hand.  The salty sweetness of this snack is addictive!
