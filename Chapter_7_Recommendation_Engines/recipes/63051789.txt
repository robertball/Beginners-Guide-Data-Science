Title:
Beer Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1 lb Velveeta cheese, grated
3 cloves garlic, minced finely
3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
Â½ can beer
1/2 tsp salt (to taste)
1 tsp dry, powdered mustard
a few dashes Tabasco sauce (to taste)
paprika and chopped parsley, for garnish

Directions:
Combine all ingredients (except for garnish), mixing well.
Form into a ball, and wrap tightly with plastic wrap.
Refrigerate for at least 8 hours before serving, although overnight is best.
Garnish and serve.
