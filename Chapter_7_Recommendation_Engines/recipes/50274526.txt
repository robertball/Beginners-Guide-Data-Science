Title:
Allison's Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups farfalle pasta
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
4 tablespoons cider vinegar
3 tablespoons prepared Dijon-style mustard
2 teaspoon white sugar
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 can kidney beans
1 small can sliced black olives
3 hard boiled eggs, diced
2 cups diced ham
1/2 cup chopped green onions
1 1/2 cups diced sharp Cheddar cheese (or grated)

Directions:
Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until al dente. Drain, and rinse in cold water.
In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, sour cream, cider vinegar, mustard, sugar, black pepper, dill weed, and salt.
In a large bowl, combine pasta and other ingredients.  Toss with 1/2 the dressing.  Cover, and chill overnight or for at least 6 hours.  Add more dressing before serving if needed or use remaining dressing as a veggie dip.
