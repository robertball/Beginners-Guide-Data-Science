Title:
Crescent Pizza Pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/712886.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) can Pillsbury® Crescent Recipe Creations® refrigerated seamless dough sheet
1/4 cup pizza sauce
3/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
24  pepperoni, diced
1 teaspoon grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees F. Unroll dough on cookie sheet and cut into 4 rectangles; press each into 6x4-inch rectangle.
Spread 1 tablespoon pizza sauce on half of each rectangle to within 1 inch of edge. Sprinkle each with 3 tablespoons cheese; top with 6 (slices) diced pepperoni. Fold dough over filling; firmly press edges with fork to seal. Sprinkle each square with 1/4 teaspoon grated cheese. With fork, prick top of each to allow steam to escape.
Bake 13 to 15 minutes or until deep golden brown. Serve warm.
