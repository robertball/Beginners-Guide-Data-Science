Title:
Meatballs & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 egg
1 cup uncooked white rice
2 tbs worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Mix all ingredients. Shape into any size meatballs and place in 9 x 13 pan sprayed with cooking spray. Cook for 30 minutes. Serve with additional rice and brown gravy if desired.

Submitter: 
MRSMTAYLOR
Description: 
A great meal that kids and adults both enjoy! Meatballs can be made any size, so it's easy to adjust according to your needs.
