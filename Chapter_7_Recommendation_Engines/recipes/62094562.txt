Title:
Paper Bag Roast Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole turkey
1 1/2 sticks softened butter
pepper, coarse sea salt, sage & garlic to taste.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Remove neck and giblets and rinse turkey with cold water. Pat dry.
Combine butter and spices. Using about half of the butter mixture, liberally coat all sides of the inside of the paper bag.
Rub the remianing butter UNDER the skin of the turkey. Fill cavity with favorite stuffing. Place turkey into buttered paper bag, press it down tightly and roll opening to seal shut. Place in roasting pan, cover with foil and place turkey in oven on lowest rack.
After 3 hours, remove foil and split open bag to allow turkey to brown. Roast turkey until temperature reaches 180 degrees.
Platter the turkey and allow to rest for 30 minutes prior to carving.

Submitter: 
myka
Description: 
This self-basting method will make the juiciest, fall-off-the-bone bird you will ever taste.
Do NOT use a grocery bag. The local health food store should have clean, non-recycled brown bags available.
