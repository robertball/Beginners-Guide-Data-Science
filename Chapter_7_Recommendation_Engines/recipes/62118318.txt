Title:
peanut buster ice cream cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz) package oreo cookies crushed
1/2 cup butter
1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream
1 1/2 cup party peanuts
1/2 cup butter
12 oz can of evaporated milk
2 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
for the crust:
mix the crushed cookies and butter together.  press into a lightly greesed 9x13 inch pan
freeze for 20 minutes
for the filling:
slice ice cream into 3/4 inch thick pieces and place on top of cookies
spread peanuts on top of ice cream
for the topping: (make about 2 hours ahead of time)
melt butter, evaporated milk, powedered sugar and chocolate chips over meadium heat stirring constantly.  bring to a boil for 8 minutes.
remove from heat, stir in vanilla and chill until completely cool then spread over ice cream.
