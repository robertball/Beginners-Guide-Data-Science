Title:
Creamy Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 envelope Dream Whip
1 (5 1/2 oz.) pkg. instant vanilla pudding
1 1/2 cups canned pumpkin
1 cup milk
1/2 t. each of nutmeg, ginger, cinnamon
1 - 9" pie shell, baked and cooled

Directions:
Prepare whipped topping as directed on package.
Mix 1 cup of the prepared whipped topping with the pudding mix, pumpkin, milk and spices.
Beat slowly just until blended.
Pour into prepared pie shell.
Chill at least 2 hours, until set.
Top with remaining whipped topping.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
JennyD
Description: 
My aunt has made this recipe for Thanksgiving for as long as I can remember.  It's light and creamy and is a great substitute for those of us who do not care for traditional pumpkin pie.
