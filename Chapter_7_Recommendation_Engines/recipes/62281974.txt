Title:
Oaxaca Old-fashioned Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351902.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 oz Fine Mescal Joven
1 1/2 oz Fine Reposado Tequila
1 teaspoon Agave Nectar
1 Dash Angostora bitters
Strip of orange peel with pith, one inch wide.

Directions:
Combine liquids in a cocktail shaker with a generous handful of ice. Stir until well chilled. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice, or a rocks glass filled with one very large hand cut ice cube.
Hold a lighted match over drink in one hand and orange peel in other hand, with orange side facing flame and about an inch away. Carefully and quickly squeeze orange peel until oils spark. Put out match and drop peel into cocktail.

One of the best new drinks of the last decade.
