Title:
Bacon Snacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Thick sliced raw bacon

Directions:
Take thick sliced bacon and smear with honey  mustard or sweet mustard on one side.  Dip in brown sugar.  Put on rack of broiler pan lined with foil and cook at375 degrees  for 20 - 30 mins.  Cut in small bite size pieces.  Cool.  Harrinton Cooks, The Sequel - HARRINTON HOMEMAKERS
