Title:
Cinnamon-Streusel Cider Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the streusel:
1/3 C packed brown sugar
3 T all-purpose flour
2 t ground cinnamon
3 T cold butter
For the Muffins:
1 3/4 C all-purpose flour
1/3 C granulated sugar
2 t baking powder
1/4 t salt
1 beaten egg
3/4 C apple cider or apple juice
1/4 C cooking oil
2 apples, peeled and chopped

Directions:
For the streusel: Stir brown sugar, flour, and cinnamon in a small bowl. Cut in butter with pastry blender. Set aside.
For the muffins: Lightly oil twelve 2 1/2-in muffin cups or line with paper baking cups; set aside. In med. mixing bowl stir together flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the center. In a small mixing bowl combine egg, apple cider, chopped apples and oil; add all at once to flour mixture. Stir just till moistened. (The batter should be lumpy)
Spoon about 1 T of batter into each prepared muffin cup. Sprinkle 1 t. of the brown sugar mixture over each. Fill with remaining batter. Sprinkle tops of muffins with remaining streusel. Bake in a preheated 400 degree oven until golden brown, about 20 min. Remove muffins from pans; cool slightly on racks

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
A thick sprinkling of streusel tops a pan of these quick and easy muffins. Their mild apple flavoring I enhanced by adding chopped apples
