Title:
Uncle 'Sam'-wich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984108.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18 ounce) package refrigerated sugar cookie dough
8 large scoops ice cream, any flavor
1 (4.5 ounce) tube red decorator icing
1 (4.5 ounce) tube blue decorator icing
1 (4.5 ounce) tube white decorator icing

Directions:
Roll out sugar cookie dough, cut into 16 rectangles and bake as directed on package (or in recipe for homemade cookie dough).
Decorate half the cookies with red, white and blue icing to look like American flags.
Place a scoop of ice cream on undecorated cookies, spread with a small spatula and top with a decorated cookie.
Freeze until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
California Milk Advisory Board
Description: 
Make homemade ice cream sandwiches this holiday with sugar cookie dough, ice cream, and frosting in red, white, and blue. The whole family will have fun decorating these ice cream sandwiches like flags to celebrate!
