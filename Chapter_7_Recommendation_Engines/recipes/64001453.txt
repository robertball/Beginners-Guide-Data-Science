Title:
Spicy Garlic Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/8 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon dried parsley
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoons garlic powder
3 tablespoons lime juice
1/2 cup chicken stock

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together salt, black pepper, cayenne, paprika, 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder, onion powder, thyme and parsley. Sprinkle spice mixture generously on one side of chicken breasts.
Heat butter and olive oil in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Saute chicken until golden brown, about 6 minutes on each side. Sprinkle with garlic powder, chicken stock and lime juice. Cook 5 minutes, stirring frequently to coat evenly with sauce.
