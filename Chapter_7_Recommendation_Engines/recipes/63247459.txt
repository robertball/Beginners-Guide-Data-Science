Title:
Festive chocolate cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/848350.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All Purpose flour
1/2 C. Cocoa
1 C. Granulated sugar
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1/4 tsp. Salt
1 1/2 C. Milk
2 Eggs
1/2 tsp. Vanilla
1/4 C. Pure Maple syrup
1 C. Semi sweet chocolate chips
1/2 C. Softened butter
1/2 C. Granulated sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
Grease and flour a 13 by 9 by 2 inch cake pan then set aside
Combine flour, Baking Powder, Baking Soda,  sugar, salt, and cocoa and set aside
Pour milk into a large mixing bowl and add the eggs, maple syrup and vanilla to the batter
Whisk in the dry ingredients until thoroughly combined and nice and smooth
Pour batter into prepared cake pan and bake for approximately 20 minutes or until a knife or toothpick inserted in the centre comes out clean
For the icing melt 1 cup of chocolate chips in saucepan over low heat and then add to softened butter and sugar
Spread frosting over cooled cake and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very light cake with the subtle taste of maple syrup and a frosting with chocolate chips that adds up to a cake for a festive occasion or just for a Sunday brunch that goes well for dessert
