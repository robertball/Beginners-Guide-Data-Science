Title:
Smashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Baby red potatoes
Olive oil
Salt and pepper (garlic and other herbs) to taste
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Boil potatoes until fork tender.
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Drizzle olive oil on baking sheet, place potatoes on sheet and gently smash and twist similar to a cookie.
Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with seasonings and cheese.
Bake for 20 minutes.
