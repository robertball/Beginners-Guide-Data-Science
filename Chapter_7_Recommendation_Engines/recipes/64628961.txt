Title:
BLT Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4472779.jpg
Ingredients:
6 bacon strips, cooked and diced
1 package premium romaine bagged salad
1 medium tomato, chopped
8 Monterey Jack cheese sticks, cut into cubes
1/2 cup mayonnaise (or Miracle Whip, whichever you prefer)
3 Tablespoons sugar
4 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
1 teaspoon olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
1 cup croutons

Directions:
In a skillet, cook bacon until crisp and cut into small pieces.  In a large bowl, combine salad, tomato and cheese.  In a small mixing bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, olive oil and salt and pepper.  Pour dressing over your salad or serve on the side.  Sprinkle with bacon and croutons.
