Title:
Flounder Amandine over Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
2 Tbsp mayonnaise
2 lb flounder filets
1/2 c chopped almonds
2 c sliced mushrooms
2 Tbsp olive oil
10 oz baby spinach
2 c halved cherry tomatoes
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 Tbsp grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F.  Combine mustard and mayo.  Place fish fillets in a single layer on lightly greased baking sheet.  Spread mustard mixture evenly over fish.  Sprinkle with almonds.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until fish flakes easily with fork.
Meanwhile, heat oil and cook mushrooms in a large skillet on medium-high heat 3 minutes.  Add spinach, tomatoes and garlic powder; mix well.  Cook on additional 3 minutes or until spinach is wilted and mushrooms are tender.
Spoon spinach mixture onto plates, sprinkle with parmesan and top with fish.
