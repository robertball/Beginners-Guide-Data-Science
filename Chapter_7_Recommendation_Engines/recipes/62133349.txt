Title:
Bacon Ranch Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs baby red potatoes, cut into pieces, skins optional
16 oz sour cream
1 oz ranch dressing mix
1 1/4 cup bacon pieces
1 1/2 cup file shredded chedder
2 bunches green onion, thinly sliced

Directions:
Boil potatoes 10 minutes or until done. Run cooked potatoes under cold water until cool enough to handle.
In a large bowl add potatoes, onion, bacon, and cheese. In a seperate bowl mix sour cream and ranch dressing mix until well blended. Stir sour cream mix into the potatoes, coating the contents.
Chill for a couple hours and serve.

Submitter: 
KristenB
Description: 
This is the easiest recipe because it can be altered in so many ways. Add a little taco seasoning to spice it up or you can add other veggies for texture.
