Title:
Vegetarian Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small cans diced tomatoes
1 large can crushed tomatoes
1 tiny can green chilies
1 lb dried beans (soaked overnight and cooked before hand)
1 large yellow onion
2 green peppers
3 large stalks of celery
1/4 cup chili powder (archer farms brand ONLY)
2 tsp cumin
1 1/2 tsp garlic pdr
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
tsp sugar
1 tsp dried oregano

Directions:
Soak beans overnight
Cook beans until desired doneness on stove top
While the beans are cooking, combine other ingredients in crockpot, and put on low.
Add cooked beans to crockpot, and continue cooking on low for a couple hours for flavors to come together.

This is a chili I designed around things that I consistently have in my pantry.
