Title:
Pickapeppa marinated flanked steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup Pickapeppa sauce
1/4 Worcestershire
3 Tbl. Dry red wine
2 Tbl. Dark brown sugar
2 cloves garlic minced
2-1/2 pounds flank steak

Directions:
Mix and cover flank steak.  Marinate 12 to 24 hours, turning occasionally.
Grill 4 to 5 minutes per side over a singe layer of ashen gray coals.  Slice thinly across grain to serve.
Boil marinade until it is reduced by 1/3.  Spoon some over the meat.
