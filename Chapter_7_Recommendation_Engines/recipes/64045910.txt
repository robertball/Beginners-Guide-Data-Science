Title:
Lemony Caesar Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cloves garlic, minced
5 anchovy filets, chopped
1 1/2 tablespoon grated parmesan cheese
1 1/2 tablespoons grated asiago cheese
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
2 tablespoon lemon zest
2 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients into a blender and blend till smooth.
Store in lidded jar, and refrigerate.
