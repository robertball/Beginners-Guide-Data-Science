Title:
Pretzel Crisps® Creamy Broccoli Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4457786.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package broccoli coleslaw mix
1/2 red onion, chopped
1/4 cup Greek yogurt
2 tablespoons lime juice
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
1 (7.2 ounce) package Snack Factory® Original Pretzel Crisps®

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients (except Pretzel Crisps(R)) in a large mixing bowl. Refrigerate 10 minutes. Place a scoop of the broccoli slaw mixture on top of Pretzel Crisps(R) and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
Description: 
This broccoli slaw with red onion and a creamy yogurt-lime juice dressing is served over crunchy Pretzel Crisps®.
