Title:
Savory Oatmeal Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c cooked steel cut oats
1/4 c tomatoes, diced (canned or fresh)
4 tbsps basil, chopped
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 eggs
2 tbsps heavy cream
1/4 c crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 F.
2. In a medium bowl, stir diced tomatoes, chopped basil, salt, and pepper into the cooked oats.
3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and cream together until uniform.
4. Fold the egg mixture into the oats mixture.
5. Divide the mixture into two large ramekins.
6. Top with feta.
7. Put ramekins in the oven and cook at 375 for 30 minutes.
8. Remove and serve.

Submitter: 
Rachel Jargon
Description: 
I don't care for sweet oatmeal. On cold mornings, I make this hearty, low-calorie breakfast that tastes great and holds me over till lunch.
