Title:
Mexican Pizza, the best Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef or ground turkey
1 pkg taco seasoning
1 PIZZA CRUST
1 SMALL CAN EL PATO TOMATO SAUCE
1 SMALL CAN REGULAR TOMATO SAUCE
1 1/2 CUPS SHREDDED MEXICAN CHEESE OR MONTERREY AND CHEDDAR
1 CAN (40Z) DICED GREEN CHILIES, DRAINED...
1 CAN HERDEZ SALSA CASERA (MORE IF NEEDED....
1/3 CUP CHOPPED ONION (OPTIONAL)
2 TABLESPOONS CHOPPED CILANTRO

Directions:
HEAT OVEN TO 450 DEGREES
FRY MEAT WITH TACO SEASONING AND DRAIN...
FRY EL PATO & TOMATO SAUCE TOGETHER
LAYER ON PIZZA SHELL
(1)SOME OF EL PATO SAUCE (2) 1/2 CHEESE  (3) MEAT, (4)CHILIES, (5)  HERDEZ SALSA, (6) CHEESE
COOK AT 450 11-13 MIN.  SPRINKLE WITH CILANTRO
