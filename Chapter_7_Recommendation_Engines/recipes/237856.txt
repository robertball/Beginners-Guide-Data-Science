Title:
Mango Chipotle Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1150185.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados from Mexico, peeled and pitted
1 mango, peeled, pitted and diced
1 small clove garlic, minced
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro leaves
2 teaspoons lime juice
1/4 teaspoon ground dried chipotle pepper
Salt to taste

Directions:
Mash avocados in a small bowl, leaving chunks if desired. Mix in mango, garlic, cilantro, lime juice, chipotle powder and salt. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Avocados from Mexico
Description: 
A smoky twist on traditional guacamole.  Excellent with chips, or paired with carne asada or carnitas.
