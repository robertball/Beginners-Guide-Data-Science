Title:
Slow cooked Pork Roast Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2lb pork roast
1/2 cup chopped carrots
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup fresh chopped parsley
1 can (14 oz) cream of mushroom soup
1 cup low sodium vegetable broth
1 package dry onion soup mix

Directions:
Place Pork Roast in slow cooker.  Mix all ingredients in a bowl.  Pour over roast.  Cover and cook on low for approximately 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Helen Lang Rosenbaum
Description: 
I threw this together one night and my kids went wild over it.  They won't let me make pork any other way!  The gravy goes great with noodles or mashed potatoes.
