Title:
Marshmallow Creme Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4524947.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
1 tablespoon orange juice concentrate
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions:
Stir cream cheese, marshmallow creme, orange juice concentrate, and nutmeg together in a bowl until completely combined.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
Oh-so-good! Serve with fresh fruit.
