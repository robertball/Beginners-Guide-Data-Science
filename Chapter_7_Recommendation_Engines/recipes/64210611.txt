Title:
Egg and Cheese Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, slightly beaten
6 slices bread, cubed
4-6 slices ham, diced
4oz or 1 cup cheese, shredded (cheddar Monterey Jack)
2+ c milk
1 tsp Dijon
Salt
pepper

Directions:
mix all ingredients
pour into 9x13 inch greased pan
(sprinkle cheese on top)
bake at 350 for 35-45 min
