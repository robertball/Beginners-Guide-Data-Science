Title:
Butterscotch Delight Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
One box yellow cake mix
one box butterscotch instant pudding mix
one cup sour cream
4 eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup butter
4 tsp vanilla
1 cup butterscotch morsels
Glaze:
1 stick butter
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
mix pudding and cake mixes together.
add oil and butter, mixing until incorporated.
add sour cream, mix well.
add eggs, one at a time.
add vanilla.
mix in the butterscotch morsels.
bake in a well greased bundt pan for 1 hour and 15 min at 325 degrees.
check cake after one hour.
For Glaze:
in a saucepan, heat butter until melted.
stir in brown sugar and vanilla.
heat through until sugar dissolves and mix becomes thickened.
Pour over cooled cake.
Serve once glaze has set.

Submitter: 
SweetPea
Description: 
This cake is so moist and with the butterscotch morsels mixed into the batter, it a sweet surprise when you bite into it.  the glaze is just the best on top of this cake as well.  Serve for dessert or at an afternoon brunch.
