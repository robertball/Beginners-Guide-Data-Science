Title:
Keto Roll Out Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c butter, softened
1/4 c Swerve confectioners
1 egg
1/2 to 1 tsp vanilla extract (or other, optional)
1/2 tsp baking soda
1.5 c blanched almond flour
1.5 tsp gelatin
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
preheat oven to 325
In large bowl, mix butter and confectioners sugar, vanilla, beat in one egg
Slowly add dry ingredients to wet
Mix well
Form dough and refrigerate at least one hour
Roll to 1/4 inch thick, cut into shapes or roll balls and flatten with glass
Roll in sugar or add chocolate chips
Bake on parchment for 10-12 min at 325
Cool and frost
