Title:
Uli’s  Doerrfleisch mit Zwiebel- Sosse               Uli’s Onion-Bacon-Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
150 grams diced Bacon
150 grams diced Walla Walla Onions
100 grams of Butter or Lard

Directions:
Cut the bacon into small dice or strips. Melt Butter in a frying pan,  add the bacon and sauté until Golden Brown.
Peel and cut onions into small dice, set aside.
Shortly before the bacon is getting crispy, add the onions and cook until translucent. About 10 min.
When sauce is ready, pour over the Lengenberg potatoes and test.
Always test by tasting!
By Uli Lengenberg
