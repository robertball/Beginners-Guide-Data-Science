Title:
BBQ Sauce w/Beer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup homemade ketchup
1/4 cup beer
1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
2 TB. vinegar
1 TB. Worcestershire sauce
1/4 TSP. or to taste Dave's Gourmet Ghost Pepper Sauce

Directions:
Mix together ketchup, beer, brown sugar, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, and ghost pepper sauce in a bowl.
Heat for 1 minute in a microwave or until sauce thickens.
Stir again and it's ready to serve!
