Title:
Slow Cooker Tapioca Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups milk (one half gallon)
1 1/3 cups white sugar
1 cup small pearl tapioca
4 eggs, lightly beaten
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Stir together the milk, sugar, tapioca, and eggs in a slow cooker.  Cover, and cook on Low for 6 hours, stirring about once an hour.  Add vanilla after turning off slow cooker.  Stir well. Serve warm or put in individual containers and keep in fridge.

Submitter: 
Ellen Janet Nestler
Description: 
Classic tapioca pudding is made with very little hassle in a slow cooker. There is no need to presoak small tapioca pearls prior to cooking.Modified from: Slow Cooker Tapioca Pudding
