Title:
No Bake "My Take" Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3593394.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup of milk
1 stick of butter (half a cup)
5 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon instant coffee
3/4 cup peanut butter
2 teaspoons vanilla
4 cups  instant oats

Directions:
Bring first six ingredients to rapid boil for 2 1/2 to 3 minutes.
Turn off heat and mix in last three ingredients.
Drop by cookie scooper on to wax paper to cool, about 20 minutes.
