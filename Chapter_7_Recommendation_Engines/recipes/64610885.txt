Title:
White Northern Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (48-oz) jars of Great Northern White Beans
1 lb brown sugar
1 large chopped onion
1pkg of thick cut bacon
1 pkg of thin sliced bacon
1tbs dried mustard powder
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut the thick bacon into 1" chunks. Fry until crisp, set aside
Dice large onion, fry in bacon grease, set aside
Drain and rinse the beans
Combine beans, mustard powder, salt, pepper, brown sugar, cooked bacon and onions in a large baking dish or tin. Mix well
Layer the thin bacon across the top of the beans
Bake at 350 for 1 hour
