Title:
Nanas Waverly Cracker Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. box of Waverly Club crackers with salt
2. 1 stick butter
3. 1 stick margarine
4. 1/2 cup plus 2 tabl sugar
5. 1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans or almonds

Directions:
Line cookie sheet with crackers closly and NOT crumbled
oven at  350*
In a pan melt 2. 3. 4. bring to a boil for 1 min
pour over the crackers
sprinkle with 5.
Bake 7 min
put on racks right away " you have to work fast or have help"....
when cool store in containers in fridge
