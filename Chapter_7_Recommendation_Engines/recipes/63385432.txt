Title:
Lynda's Santa fe Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 can green chiles
1/4 cup water
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
3/4 cup Sour Cream, divided
1 16 oz bags of tortilla chips
1 1/2 cups KRAFT Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese
1 green onion, sliced

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Brown meat in large skillet; drain. Return meat to skillet. Add water and seasoning mix; stir. Cook 10 minute, stirring occasionally. Stir in 3/4 cup sour cream and green chilies
Place half the chips in 13x9-inch baking dish; cover with layers of half each of the meat mixture and cheese. Repeat layers.
Bake 20 minute or until heated through. Top with onions

Submitter: 
Lynda
Description: 
A quick snack that is a family pleaser, this hearty meaty taco sauce for this nacho bake is mixed with  Sour Cream and layered with tortilla chips and shredded Cheddar cheese.Modified from: Santa fe Bake
