Title:
Fiesta Lime Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/633493.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c. cooked cooked brown rice
1 1/2 canned black beans, rinsed and heated
1 1/2 c. corn (I like to cut it off the cob)
1 15 oz. can diced tomatoes with chilies
1 heaping tbsp. chives, diced fine
2-4 tbsp. chopped cilantro (to your liking)
1 tbsp fresh squeezed lime juice
Kosher salt to taste (about 1 tsp.)

Directions:
In large bowl, combine cooked rice, corn, beans, tomato, chives, salt and lime juice.  Toss and serve.
Servings: 6  Serving Size: 1 cup  WW Points+: 4 pt
calories:  152.8  Fat: 0.8g  Protein: 5.7g  Fiber: 4g Sugar: 1.7

Absolutely fabulous side dish by itself; but positively beyond that with fresh grilled chicken breast or capork carnitas, barbacoa beef, chicken ropa vieja, slow cooked sweet barbacoa pork.
