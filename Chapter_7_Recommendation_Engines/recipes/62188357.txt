Title:
CREAMY TOMATO SOUP (no cream) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion chopped
1 tb chopped garlic
2 tb canola oil
1 qt home canned tomatoes
3 slices bread cubed
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil (1 tb dried)
1 tb brown sugar
2 cups chicken broth
2 tb butter
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in dutch oven and add onion and garlic cooking until tender not browned about 5 minutes on medium low.  Add tomatoes crushing with potato masher. Stir in sugar, basil and bread.  Bring to simmer and cook until bread is completely saturated and starts to break down about 10 minutes.
Transfer to blender add butter and blend until smooth and creamy.  Return to Dutch oven, heat and add broth, return to simmer and season to taste with salt and pepper.
