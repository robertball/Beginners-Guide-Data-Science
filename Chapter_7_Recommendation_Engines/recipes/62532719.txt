Title:
White Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
filling:
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 pint sour cream
1/2 chopped onion
small can chopped olives
small can green chilis
mix above ingredients and separate in half
2 cups shredded cheese
3 cups diced cooked chicken
6 flour tortillas

Directions:
spray 13 x 9 pan with pam
separate filling in half
add chicken and 1 cup cheese to half of mixture
with other half put thin layer of sauce on bottom of pan
fill each tortilla with chicken and cheese w/sauce filling
place in pan
cover with remainder of sauce
cover with remainder of shredded cheese
cover with foil
bake 350 degrees for 30 min
