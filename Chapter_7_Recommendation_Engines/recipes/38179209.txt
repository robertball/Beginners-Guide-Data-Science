Title:
Crisp Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar (can also use 1/2 c. brown sugar 1/2 c. white sugar)
1 c. flour
1 c. oats (opt)
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 c. butter or margarine softened
1 tsp cinnamon (opt)

Directions:
Mix together dry ingredients. Cut in butter until mixture is coarse and crumbly. Sprinkle on top of sliced fruit in prepared pan.  Bake at 350 F until brown and bubbly.

Submitter: 
KELLY BENSON
Description: 
Buttery crisp topping great for apple crisp, cherry crisp, peach crisp etc...
