Title:
Atlanta Barbecue Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Coke (not diet)
1 packet onion soup mix
1/2 C. ketchup
1/2 C. barbecue sauce
1 large yellow onion diced
3 bay leaves

Directions:
Place all ingredients into a heavy sauce pan.  Bring to a boil and reduce to light simmer.  Cook, uncovered, until reduced by half--at least an hour or more.  Store in sealed jar in the refrigerator.  Serve over the protein of your choice.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
Adapted from a recipe in Gourmet Magazine.  This is a delicious twist on traditional barbecue.
