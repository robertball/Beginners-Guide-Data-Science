Title:
Chicken Tender Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lb chicken strips, cooked according to package directions.
5 lb potatoes, peeled and cut
1 lb bacon, chopped
1/2 cup all purpose flour
4 cups milk
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/4 cup butter
2 Tbsp milk
2 (16 oz) bags frozen corn niblets
2 (6 oz) packages french fried onions
16 oz shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In well salted water, boil potatoes until tender, approx. 15 min.
While potatoes boil, in a large skillet, cook bacon until crisp.
Stir in flour to make roux and cook, stirring constantly for 2-3 minutes.
Stir in milk and cook, stirring constantly until gravy is thickened.  add salt and pepper to taste.
When Potatoes are tender, drain in a colander and add back to pot.   Mash potatoes with the cream, butter and milk.
Spread mashed potatoes evenly in a well-greased casserole dish.
Spread gravy evenly over potatoes.
Sprinkle corn niblets evenly over gravy.
place cooked chicken tenders evenly over corn.
Sprinkle french fried onions evenly over chicken.
Sprinkle cheese evenly over french fried onions.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 15-20 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
