Title:
Slow-Cooker Thai Chicken (Betty Crocker's) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. chicken thighs (skinless) (~8 thighs)
3/4 cup hot salsa
1/4 cup peanut butter
2 tablespoons peanut butter
2 tablespoons lime juice
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 teaspoon grated fresh gingerroot
1/4 cup chopped peanuts
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 cup basmati rice
1/2 cup grated coconuts

Directions:
Place chicken in slow cooker. Mix salsa, peanut butter, lime juice, soy sauce and gingerroot and pour over chicken.
Cover and cook on low heat setting for 8 to 9 hours or until juice of chicken is no longer pink when center of thickest pieces are cut. Remove chicken from cooker using slotted spoon and place on serving platter.
Remove fat from sauce and pour over chicken. Sprinkle with peanuts and cilantro.
Serve chicken over rice mixed with grated coconut.
