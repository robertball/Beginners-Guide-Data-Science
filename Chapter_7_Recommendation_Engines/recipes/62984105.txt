Title:
Pecan pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch single crust pie
1 c. Karo syrup
3/4 cup white sugar
1 cube butter, melted
3 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3/4 cups coarsely chopped pecans
3/4 cups pecan halves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together the corn syrup and sugar,then add butter. Mix in eggs and vanilla until well blended. Let stand 1 hour. Place pecan pieces in pie shell then pour syrup over. Top with pecan halves.
Bake for 50 minutes or longer in the preheated oven until top is brown, and filling is set. Let pie cool.
