Title:
Slow Cooker Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup rice
½ cup water
Fork
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
1
Pour ½ cup rice and ½ cup water into the slow cooker for each cup of cooked rice you want to prepare. For example, to make three servings of cooked rice, or 3 cups, add 1 ½ cups rice and 1 ½ cups water to the slow cooker.
2
Cover the slow cooker and plug in the appliance.
3
Set the slow cooker on low heat and let the rice cook for six to eight hours, or until the rice has absorbed all of the water and tastes tender.
