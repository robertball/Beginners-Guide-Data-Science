Title:
Mexican Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 tbsp chili powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp red pepper
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 can(16oz)diced tomatoes
10-12 corn tortillas
2 c cottage cheese (drained)
1 c grated monterey jack cheese with jalapeno peppers
1 egg
1/2 c grated cheddar cheese
2 c shredded lettuce
1/2 c chopped tomatoes
3 green onions chopped
1/4 c sliced black olives

Directions:
Brown ground beef then drain thoroughly.Add spices and tomatoes and heat through.Cover the bottom and sides of a 9x13 pan with half the tortillas.Pour all the beef mixture over the tortillas,then place another layer of tortillas over top and set aside.Combine cottage cheese,monterey jack cheese and egg together. Pour over tortillas. Bake at 350 F for 30 minutes.Remove from oven and sprinkle cheddar cheese,lettuce,tomatoes,green onions and olives over top.Serve immediately and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Heather
Description: 
I prepare my toppings while the lasagna is baking.This is very filling and is like a meal and salad all in one.
