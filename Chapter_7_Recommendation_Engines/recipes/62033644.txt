Title:
Mustard Glazed Rib Eye Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs Rib Eye Roast (Boneless)
1/2C. Dijon Mustard (or yellow)
1-2 Cloves Garlic-pressed
Dash Worchester sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Place roast in an oven safe baking dish.  Score roast with a knife.
In a small bowl, combine mustard, garlic, sauce and spices.  Mix thoroughly.  Glaze top and sides of roast with a thick layer.
Place in oven uncovered, until internal temp is 150 degrees.
Remove from oven and sit for 5 minutes.  Slice meat in pan and transfer to plates.  In pan, add 1/2 cup of water to drippings and heat over medium heat just to boil.  Serve juices as Aujour.
