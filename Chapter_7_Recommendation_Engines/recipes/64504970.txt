Title:
Grilled Teriyaki Mahi Mahi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4- 6 oz filets of mahi mahi
1/4 C olive oil
1/4 C lemon juice
Salt
Pepper
1/2 C honey
1/4 C teriyaki sauce
1 tsp ground or fresh ginger
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Whisk together  olive oil and lemon juice in a large bowl. Place mahi mahi in the bowl and coat the fish with the marinade.  Salt and pepper the fish.
Place in refrigerator for 15 minutes.
Preheat an outdoor to medium.
In a small bowl, combine honey, teriyaki sauce, ginger and cayenne. Mix throughly.
Place fish on the grill and cook 5 minutes each side.  Discard the marinade.
Once fish is cooked,  generously brush fish with the sauce. Turn off heat or remove from direct heat and let rest for 2 minutes on the warm grill.
