Title:
Brazilian Pork and Beans Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices of thick bacon (chopped)
1 onion chopped
1 can tomato sauce
2 teas garlic
2 T chili powder
1 teas cumin
1 teas coriander
3 cups hot water with chicken bullion
3 cans beans (black, kidney, pinto)
2 bay leaves
1 pork tenderloin (cubed)

Directions:
Place ingredients in crockpot. Cook on high for 5-7 hours.
