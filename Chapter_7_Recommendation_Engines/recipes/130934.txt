Title:
The Fabulous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/856342.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1 1/4 fluid ounces raspberry vodka
3 fluid ounces club soda
1 fluid ounce lemon-lime soda
1 lime twist

Directions:
Fill a rocks glass with ice, and pour in the vodka, club soda, and lemon-lime soda. Top with a lime twist and serve with a straw.
