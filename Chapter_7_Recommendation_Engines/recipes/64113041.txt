Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups mixed salad greens
2 oz smoked turkey breast
1/2 cup sliced English cucumber
1/4 cup canned drained white beans
1 small sliced pear
10 seedless red grapes
1 1/2 T chopped roasted walnuts
2 T vinaigrette

Directions:
Top salad greens with the turkey breast, cucumber, beans, pear, grapes, walnuts, and vinaigrette. Enjoy!
