Title:
Crock Pot BBQ Spareribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/644397.jpg
Ingredients:
4 lbs ribs
Salt and Pepper to taste
Your Favorite BBQ sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Place ribs in shallow pan.  Brown in oven 15 minutes, turn and brown other side for 15 minutes. Place ribs in crock pot and add BBQ sauce.  Cook on low for 8 hours.
