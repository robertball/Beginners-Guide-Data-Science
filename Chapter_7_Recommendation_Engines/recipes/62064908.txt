Title:
Garlic Chicken Fried Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
2-3 boneless skinned chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
1/2 large sweet red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 cup green onion, chopped
4 to 6 garlic cloves, minced
3 cups cooked brown rice* cooked and cooled
2 tablespoons light soy sauce
1 tablespoon rice-wine vinegar
1 cup frozen peas, thawed
Black pepper,paprika, dried parsely, to taste
Optional 2 scrambled eggs

Directions:
Heat large nonstick skillet over medium  high heat.
Add 1 tbsp. oil.
Add chicken, red bell pepper, green onion, and garlic.
Cook 5 minutes or until chicken is cooked through, stirring occasionaly.
Remove to a warm plate.
Heat remaining Tbsp. oil in skillet.
Add cooked cooled brown rice, cook 1 minute.
Stir in soy sauce, vinegar, and peas, cook 1 minute.
Stir in chicken and onion, bell pepper, garlic mixture.
Add black pepper, paprika, and dried parsely to taste.
If you wish you can add 2 scrambled eggs.
*NOTE: Simmer, covered, 1 cup brown rice in 2 1/2 cups boiling water for 45 minutes, then chill for about 4 hours or overnight.
