Title:
Vegan Lemon-Tahini Dressing  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5971929.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup lemon juice
1/2 cup olive oil
8 tablespoons tahini
6 cloves garlic cloves, pressed
1 tablespoon coarse salt

Directions:
Combine lemon juice, olive oil, and tahini in a jar with a lid. Close and shake vigorously until combined. Press garlic into the dressing using a garlic press. Season with salt and shake well.

Submitter: 
Lily Garcia
Description: 
Makes two cups of delicious Lebanese-inspired dressing.
