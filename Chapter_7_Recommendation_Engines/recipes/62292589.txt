Title:
Greek lentil soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. olive oil
1 medium onion, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 ½  c. lentils
8 c. vegetable stock
1 tsp. oregano
1 bay leaf
1 ½ c. diced carrots (5 or 6)
2 medium potatoes, diced (1 ½ c.)
½ tsp. lemon zest
2 T. lemon juice
3 T. fresh dill, chopped (or 1 T. dried)
salt and pepper

Directions:
Saute onion, garlic, and celery in hot oil about 4 minutes.
Add lentils, stock, oregano, and bay leaf.  Boil, then reduce heat and simmer partially covered for about 40 minutes.
Stir in carrots and potatoes and simmer, uncovered, till lentils are tender, about 20 minutes.  Add salt, pepper, lemon juice and zest, and dill.
