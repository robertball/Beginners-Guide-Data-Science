Title:
Hot Asiago Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of Grated Assiago Cheese
2 cups of Hellman's Mayonaise
1 Large clove of garlic minced
1 Small jar of artichokes chopped (reserve oil marinade from artichoke jar)
1/2 bottle of the reserved marinade from the artichoke bottle mixed into mayo
Box or mini Breton Crackers and/or baquette cut up
Round sourdough bread - Hollow it out like a bowl (reserve bread for dipping)

Directions:
1. Stir together the first 5 ingredients in a bowl.
2. Pour cheese mixture into the hollowed out bread bowl.
3. Wrap foil around the bottom of the loaf of bread (don't cover the cheese)and bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until golden and bubbly on top.
4.  Serve hot with sliced up baguette or crackers. (for dipping or with a knife for spreading)

Submitter: 
Andrea Longo Policella
Description: 
This dip is the absolute best!  It is a quick and easy appetizer that can be prepared ahead of time and then just thrown in the oven before serving. I have never served it without having to give out the recipe after!
