Title:
Pineapple Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5601285.jpg
Ingredients:
Shell: 1 cup (2sticks) unsalted butter, softened
2 cups flour
3 tablespoons sugar
Filling:  1 cup crushed pineapple (in its own juice)
1 1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons corn starch
Topping:  4 egg whites, beaten until stiff

Directions:
Knead or use kitchenaid with paddle attachment  first 3 ingredients (for shell) until soft
Press in bottom and up sides of 9x12 pan
Bake at 325 for 20-25 minutes or until edges are light golden
While baking shell boil together 1 cup crushed pineapple with 1 1/4 cups sugar, thicken with 2 tablespoons corn starch mixed with a little water. Simmer until thickened. Remove from heat and cool slightly
Beat whites of 4 eggs until stiff. Add slightly cooled pineapple mixture and whip in at low speed just until mixed. Give a good stir to make sure all the filling is evenly mixed in
Pour filling into baked shell and bake 10 minutes at 325 or until topping is slightly golden brown
Cool and serve with whipped cream if desired

Light and refreshing  with shortbread crust. This was my childhood favorite (almost 60 years ago!). Everyone loves this dessert!
