Title:
Roasted Red Pepper Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 oz jar of roasted red bell peppers
2 teaspoons tomato paste
2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
1 garlic clove
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a food processor, puree the peppers and juice, the tomato paste, vinegar, garlic and cayenne until almost smooth.

Submitter: 
apotherix
Description: 
Keep a container in the fridge for snacks with vegetables or a baked potato topper.
