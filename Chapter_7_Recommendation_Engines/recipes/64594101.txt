Title:
Chicken Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil
5-6 garlic cloves, minced
1 onion (white), diced
1/4 to 1/2 head red cabbage, shredded/chopped
salt to taste
2-3 tsp cumin to taste
2-3 tsp smoked paprika to taste
2 tsp chili powder to taste
2-3 tsp oregano to taste
1/4 to 1/2 tsp onion powder to taste
1/4 tsp cayenne (or jalapeno minced -- optional, didn't use) to taste
1 lb ground chicken
1 tomato, diced
2 T apple cider vinegar
1/4 to 1/2 c water
cilantro, green onion, avocado, romaine lettuce leaves for serving

Directions:
heat oil over medium-high heat
saute garlic, onion, cabbage until softened and fragrant
add spices
move veggies to side and brown chicken, breaking up into small pieces
add chopped tomato
add apple cider vinegar and water
turn to low and simmer 10-15 min
serve on romaine lettuce leaves with cilantro, green onion, avocado or guacamole and cumin-cilantro-lime crema

Ground chicken tacos served on lettuce tortillas
