Title:
Grandma Helen's Orange Glazed Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 pkg yellow cake mix
1 pkg lemon instant pudding mix
3/4 cup vegetable oil
4 eggs
3/4 cup water
1/2 tsp lemon zest
Glaze:
1/4 cup orange juice
1 cup powdered sugar
2 tsp vegetable oil
1/2 tsp lemon zest

Directions:
1) Grease 13X9 or bundt pan.  Preheat oven at 350Â°F
2) Mix all cake ingredients together and beat with electric mixer for 10 minutes to fully incorporate the pudding mix.
3) Pour cake batter into pan and bake for 50 minutes.
4) While cake is baking, prepare glaze by mixing all ingredients together with electric mixer.
5) When cake is finished baking, remove from oven.  (If using bundt pan, remove cake from pan.)
6) Using a large fork, poke holes in the cake.
7) Pour glaze mix over cake.
8) Allow to cool and enjoy!
