Title:
Beanie Weenies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices of bread
8 hot dogs, sliced down the center lengthwise, but not cut in half
1 can pork and beans
8 slices of cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Place bread slices on cookie sheet.
Lay opened hotdogs on bread.
Fill and overfill hotdogs with pork and beans.
Lay cheese slices over hotdog/bean/bread
Bake until bubbly and cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Michelle Bovenkamp
Description: 
This was a quick and easy dinner that my mom would prepare on her way between jobs.  It remains a hit for my children!
