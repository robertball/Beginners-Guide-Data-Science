Title:
Schwarties Hash Browns - Deb's Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lbs frozen hash browns
1 500 ml carton sour cream
2 tins cream of mushroom soup
1/2 C melter butter
2 Tbl grated onion
2 C grated cheddar cheese
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Thaw potatoes slightly for easier mixing. Mix first 6 ingredients in a 9"x13" baking dish. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top. Bake at 350 for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
