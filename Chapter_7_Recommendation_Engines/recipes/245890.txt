Title:
Nuoc Cham (Vietnamese Sauce) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3175279.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons fish sauce, or more to taste
2 tablespoons white sugar, or more to taste
1 tablespoon water
1 red chile pepper, thinly sliced, or more to taste (optional)
1 clove garlic, thinly sliced (optional)

Directions:
Mix lime juice, fish sauce, sugar, water, red chile pepper, and garlic together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
vietlove
Description: 
This favorite Vietnamese dipping sauce goes well with so many things. It doesn't keep well, so make it fresh. The sauce should taste balanced, not too sweet, sour, or salty.
