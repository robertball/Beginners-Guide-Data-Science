Title:
Grilled Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bunch Asparagus - Thick are Best
Olive Oil
Sea Salt

Directions:
Snap ends off of stalks.
Toss with 1 Tablespoon olive oil and salt to taste.
Grill on Med Hot Grill 5 minutes, turning often.
Serve immediately.
