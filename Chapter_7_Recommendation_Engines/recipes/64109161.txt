Title:
Almond Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1260460.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
2 cups flour - divided
1/4 cup butter
2 eggs
2 tsp. baking powder
pinch of salt
3/4 cup milk
1 tsp. almond extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°.
Mix, by hand; sugar, 1 cup flour and butter until crumbly.  Set aside one-half of this mixture.  To the remaining mixture add; 1 cup flour, eggs, baking powder and salt.  Mix well.  To that mixture; add milk and almond extract.  Pour batter into a 9x9 or 7x11 lightly greased pan. Spread reserved crumbly mixture over the top of the batter.
Bake for 30 minutes.
Cool, cut and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Molly
Description: 
This is my aunt Pat's recipe.  She made it every weekend that she came to the lake when I was growing up.  It is still my favorite to this day.
