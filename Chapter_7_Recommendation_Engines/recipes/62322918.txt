Title:
Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1   can (15oz)black beans drained, rinsed
1   can (10 oz) ROTEL Original Diced Tomatoes
and green chilies, drained
1/2 cup whole kernel corn, canned
1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
1/4 finely chopped fresh cilantro
1   tablespoon lime juice
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
Tortilla chips, tacos or burritos (optional)

Directions:
1. Mix all ingredients in medium bowl.  Cover and     refrigerate 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
2. Serve with tortilla chips, tacos or burritos, if desired.

Submitter: 
Gina M. Ford
Description: 
Super easy delicious salsa which can be eaten with chips, in a burrito or in tacos! Yummy!
