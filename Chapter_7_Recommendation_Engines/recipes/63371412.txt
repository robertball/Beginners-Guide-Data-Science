Title:
Baked Steel Cut Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups Steel Cut Oats  (already cooked)
1/4 cup chopped nuts
1 egg
1/3 cup of milk
½ cup dried fruit or fresh or frozen fruit
1/2 TBS cinnamon
1 TBS canola oil
1 TBS maple syrup

Directions:
1.	Cook steel cut oats according to 1 cup of oats.  Usually ½ c oat 1 cup water (directions on box- the best brand of steel cut is organic brand)
2.	Preheat oven to 375. Grease pans.
3.	Drain oats in colander and place back in bowl.
4.	In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, milk and maple syrup until frothy.
5.	Add egg mixture to oats along with fruit, nuts, cinnamon, and oil. Fold until combined.
6.	Pour into pan.  8' pie pan works well,
7.	Bake for 20-30 minutes or until toothpick   inserted comes out clean or oatmeal has no liquid on top.
8.	Serve with maple syrup
