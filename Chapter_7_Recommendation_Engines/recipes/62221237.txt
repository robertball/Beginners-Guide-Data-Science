Title:
Nebraskan Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cubed (or sausage, shrimp...combo etc.)
1 pkg Zatarain's Jambalaya mix
2 1/2 cups water
Zatarain's Creole seasoning, to taste
1/2 cup Cajun Power marinating and basting sauce
2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
2 8oz cans tomato sauce
1 6 oz can tomato paste
1 15 oz can diced tomatoes (with green pepper, celery and onions), undrained
1/2 can (15 oz) corn, drained
1 15oz can black beans, drained
add your own fresh veggies:
celery, onion, bell peppers etc.

Directions:
Combine 2 1/2 cups water, 2 Tbsp. vegetable oil and chicken in large sauce pan (2 1/2 quart).
Bring to boil.
Add Jambalaya mix, creole seasoning, and marinating sauce in pan.  Mix and return to boil.
Reduce heat, stir, cover and simmer over low heat for 18 minutes.
Add cans of tomato sauce, tomato paste, corn and diced tomato.  Return to simmer and stir frequently for 5 minutes.
Add beans and mix frequently for 6 minutes.
Add additional marinating sauce and creole seasoning to taste.

Submitter: 
Christal Kuchera
Description: 
Sort of like a chili inspired by jambalaya...but not a true jambalaya.
