Title:
Hawaiian Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Large Sweet Potatoes
1 Tablespoon Butter
2 teaspoons of salt
6 firm ripe Bananas...sliced
1 cup firm packed light brown sugar...mixed with 1 teaspoon cinnamon
16 oz.crushed pineapple
1 cup pineapple juice..mixed with 1 teaspoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons of honey

Directions:
Wash,scrub,sweet potatoes and place in cold water.cover,bring to a boil and parboil until they still show a little resistance when pierced with the tip of a sharp knife.Drain, cool slightly,peel and slice potatoes 1/2 inch thick.Grease a heatproof casserole dish with a little of the butter.Arrange in alternate layers starting with the sweet potatoes dotted with butter and salt,then the Bananas sprinkle with brown suger,cinnamon mixture. Then add the crushed pineapple end with the sweet potatoes or the crushed pineapple...Combine the pineapple juice and lemon juice and honey and pour over the mixture..Bake in a preheated oven 350 for 40 minutes or until brown on top
