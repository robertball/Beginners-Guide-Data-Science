Title:
Ropa Vieja (Miami Style) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.75 lb Boneless Shoulder Clod Chuck Roast
2 T Olive Oil (Spanish if You Have It)
1 10oz Can "Hatch" Diced Tomatos w/ Chiles (Med)
1 4oz Can Of Diced Green Chiles (Mild)
3/4 C Beef Broth
1 5oz (Small) Can Tomato Paste
5 Lg Cloves Garlic Chopped (or more if you wish)
1 Jalapeno Pepper Seeded And Chopped
1 Large Bell Pepper Cut Into Strips
1 Med Onion Cut In Half And Sliced Into half Moons
1 5oz Jar Manzinella (Green Stuffed)Olives.Juice And All
11/2 tsp Cumin
1 tsp Ground corriander (Optional)
1 tsp Ground Pepper
Pinch Of Salt

Directions:
Season And Brown The Roast On All Sides in Oil
Place In Crockpot
Add Tomatos,Tomato Paste,Chiles,Broth,Chopped garlic,Jalapenos,Spices
Top With Bell Peppers,Onions Olives w/Juice
Put Lid On Crockpot And Cook On Low for 8 Hours
Remove All Tender Meat And Shred It With 2 Forks.
Add Meat Back To Crockpot
Serve With Black Beans, White rice And Corn Tortillas.
Even better The Next Day..... Enjoy !
