Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites by Team Union/Loves Foxes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2464521.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tb butter
1/2 cup marshmallows
1/3 cup oats
cranberries
coconut shavings
cranberries

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl mix the oats, cranberries, coconut shavings and a pinch of cinnamon.
On the stove melt the better and add the marshmallows.  Melt together.  Mix all ingredients together with large wooden spoon.  Wait a few minutes and allow mixture to cool.  Wet your hands and then separate the mixture into little balls.  Bake in oven for 5 minutes at 350 degrees.  Let cool. EAT!

Submitter: 
Almie
Description: 
These little bites pack a lot of flavor, little but yummy!  Wholesome little love bites.
