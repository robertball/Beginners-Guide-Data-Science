Title:
Sarah's Dark Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351863.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
3/4 cup butter, softened (NOT melted)
7 to 8 tablespoons half & half
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream butter and cocoa powder until smooth.
Add vanilla and gradually beat in confectioners' sugar alternately with half & half.
Beat several minutes, until light and fluffy. If necessary, adjust consistency with more milk or sugar.

Submitter: 
Sarah Dipity
Description: 
Very chocolaty and creamy frosting that is good for piping.  Add more half & half if you like a thinner consistency.
