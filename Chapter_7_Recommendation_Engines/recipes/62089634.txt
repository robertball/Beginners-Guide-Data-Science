Title:
Almond crusted Stuffed peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 red/yellow/orange bell peppers
1 cup fresh spinach
1 cup chopped fresh tomatoes
1 cup chopped fresh broccoli
1 large white onion, chopped
1/2 cup chopped fresh baby bella mushrooms
1 cup cooked brown rice
1 cup cooked black beans, smashed
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
2 cloves garlic, smashed and chopped
15 almonds, finely chopped
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
1 tsp madras curry spice
pepper
salt

Directions:
Slice tops of bell peppers, seed and core.  Flatten bottoms of bell peppers by slicing thin portion of flesh from the base of the pepper. Spray the insides of peppers with cooking spray, sprinkle with 1/2 tsp of the curry powder, salt and pepper.
Sautee garlic, onion and mushrooms over medium heat until garlic is just fragrant.  Place all ingredients (except almonds) in large mixing bowel, stir until just combined. Add additional curry powder, salt and pepper.
Portion mixture into pepper cups, sprinkle tops with almonds. Bake in preheated oven or broiler at 375 degrees, until tops are browned and pepper gives to touch.
