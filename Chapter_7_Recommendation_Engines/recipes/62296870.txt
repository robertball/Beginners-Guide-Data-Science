Title:
turkey lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/361838.jpg
Ingredients:
one lbs ground turkey
one lbs ground beef
whole wheat lasagna noodles
1 1/2 can tomato sauce
1 package low fat slice mozzarella  cheese (8oz)
1-2 package low fat shredded cheese (16oz)
1 garlic bulb
Italian seasoning
low fat ricotta cheese
hot sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to approximate 400-425 degrees. In a skillet cook ground turkey and ground beef separately from each other. Add italian seasoning, salt pepper, chopped garlic and hot sauce to desired amount. Once both are cooked add chopped garlic to skillet along with salt pepper to your liking. Add meat and sauce together and let simmer while you boil approximately 8 noodles. Begin layers with noodles, add ricotta cheese (thin layer) and then meat sauce. Add slice mozarella and shredded cheese and repeat layers. Place in oven for about 15 minutes or until top layer of cheese is golden brown and melted.
