Title:
MY FISH BREW Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
My fish brew
Victoria M Hamm
1 crock pot
5 fillets of sole (or whatever whitefish you prefer)
1 small onion
2 tablespoons of corn relish
1 tablespoon of sweet pickle relish
1 large garlic clove
1 cup of white beans (any kind of white bean)
3 tablespoons of butter
½ cup of flour
1 teaspoon of lemon herb
1 tablespoon of parsley
1 tablespoon of powdered/salted chicken stock
4 slices of presliced cheese (any kind you’d like)or the equivalent of any type of cheese. Parm or Provalone or monteray.

Directions:
a)Put the spices and fillets in the crock ½ filled with water along with the flour and 1 tbspn of butter – fill the remaining crock with water turn on HIGH
b)When fish is almost cooked in a frying pan cook down the onion with 2 tablespoons of butter and the relish along with garlic clove – put all in the crock pot -
c)Also in a separate frying pan cook down the dried beans until it’s almost completely soft (2o minutes maybe a little longer @ full boil) then drain & add to the pot.
d)Put one cup of the boiled bean water in the crock only and spoon in the rest/drained
e)Dump in everything from the onions and relish – it’s fine as it is
f)Add the sliced cheese and let it melt – you can add as much or as little as you like
You should have a nice creamy broth yummy

Submitter: 
Victoria M.H.
Description: 
This is a chowder, however I omitted potatoes and milk products. It is a very delicious packed with protein soup. I've become addicted to the crockpot.
