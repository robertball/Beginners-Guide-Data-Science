Title:
Baked Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package ground beef
1 small onion
1 bottle marinara sauce
1 can fresh tomato juice
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
1 1/2 packages spaghetti noodles
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
2 cups mild cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. Cook ground beef on medium heat; chop and add onion; cook ground beef until brown.
2. Drain beef and onion and place back on stove; add marinara sauce, tomato juice, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, and parmesan cheese; stir together and keep heat on low
3. Cook noodles
4. Spray a medium sized pan with cooking spray; combine noodles and meat sauce and place on the bottom of the pan
5. Sprinkle cheddar cheese on top and add more pepper flakes on top
6. Cook for 25-30 minutes on 375 degrees.
