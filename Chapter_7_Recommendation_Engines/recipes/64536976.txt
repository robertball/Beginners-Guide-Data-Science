Title:
banana choco-malt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup almond milk
1 med. banana
1/2 cup crushed ice
2 dates, pitted
1 T. unsweetened almond butter
1 T. cocoa powder
1 t. flaxseeds
1 cup baby spinach

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into blender.
Blend until smooth and thick.
