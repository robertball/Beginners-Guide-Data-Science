Title:
Turkey Tart Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/732564.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cropped onion
1/2 cup cropped carrot
1/2 cup cropped celery
1/4 stick real butter
1/4 cup flour
3 cans low fat chicken broth
1 family size envelope (or 2 cups cooked)Sucess Rice
2 cups shredded turkey
1/2 teaspoon white pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon basil
1/2 teaspoon tarragon leaves
1/2 teaspoon parsley
2 tabelspoons lemon juice
1 cup sour cream
4 slices cooked crumbled bacon for topping(optional)

Directions:
Heat butter in dutch oven or large saucepan, add onions, celery, and carrots until crispy tender, stir in flour, cook for 1-1 1/2 minutes. Stir in the broth until it thickens, add turkey, salt, white pepper, garlic powder, lemon juice, basil, tarragon, and parsley, open and pour in Sucess rice or cooked rice(do not put boing bag in). Cover and simmer for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally, then add the sour cream, stirring to blend.  Simmer for 5 more minutes on very low heat. Serve with bacon crumbled on top if desired.

Submitter: 
Judi Gesell Judge
Description: 
Delicious tangy soup that perks up left over turkey for a cold winter's warm up. Lemony tart flavor with sour cream creates this old passed down family recipe that I modified to my tastes.  If you freeze left over turkey from Thanksgiviing or Christmas, it really comes in handy.
