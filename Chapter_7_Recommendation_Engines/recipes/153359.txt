Title:
Hot Spiced Witches' Brew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986537.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups boiling water
1 pkg. (8 serving size) JELL-O Brand Orange Flavor Gelatin
1 quart boiling apple juice
8 cinnamon sticks

Directions:
Stir boiling water into dry gelatin in large bowl at least 2 minutes until completely dissolved. Stir in boiling apple juice.
Pour into 8 mugs.
Serve hot with cinnamon sticks.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
This hot, spiced brew will warm up the trick or treaters when they return from their rounds.
