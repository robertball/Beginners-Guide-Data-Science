Title:
Roasted Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups cauliflowerets
1/4 cup EVOO
1 TBSP garlic
2 TBSP lemon juice
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
2 TBSP parmesan cheese
1 chive, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 500 degrees.
Place cauliflower in a roasting pan. Mix remaining ingredients except parmesan and drizzle over cauliflower. Bake. Sprinkle cheese over all and serve.
