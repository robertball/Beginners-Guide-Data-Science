Title:
Quick and Easy Pumpkin Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/339443.jpg
Ingredients:
24 marshmallows
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup canned pumpkin
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
2/3 cup powdered sugar, separated
2 cups whipping cream. separated
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
In a large saucepan melt butter.  Add marshmallows, milk and pumpkin stirring frequently until mixture is melted and smooth.  Stir in vanilla and pumpkin pie spice.  Cool completely.  In a separate bowl beat 1/3 cup powdered sugar and 1 cup whipping cream until stiff.  Fold into pumpkin mixture.  Repeat with remaining sugar and cream to serve as an optional topping.  Pour into ramekins or prepared chocolate shells.  Cover and refrigerate 2 hours or until firm.

Submitter: 
Kristin_California
Description: 
This is a fast easy treat around the holidays.  Pour into chocolate shells purchased at any kitchen store for an elegant touch or with graham crackers for dipping.  You can also heat ingredients in the microwave on high for 30 second intervals if you are short on time.
