Title:
Mini Sour Cream Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985839.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup biscuit/baking mix
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
In a bowl, combine all ingredients. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls into greased miniature muffin pans. Bake at 425 degrees F for 15-18 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Sara  Dukes
Description: 
These buttery mini muffins are flaky, tender and a snap to make because you start with convenient biscuit mix. Sara Dukes of Bartow, Georgia shared the recipe.
