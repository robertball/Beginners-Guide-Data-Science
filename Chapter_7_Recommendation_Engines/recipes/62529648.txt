Title:
Stuffed Shells with Spinach and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package jumbo pasta shells
1 package fresh baby spinach
3 cloves garlic
3-4 fresh basil leaves
1 tablespoon fennel seeds eggs, beaten
1 (32 ounce) container ricotta cheese
1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
8 ounces grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1 tablespoon dried Italian Seasoning
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (28 ounce) jar pasta sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Finely chop the garlic cloves and saute in skillet with olive oil. Wash spinach and spin dry.  add to the sauted garlic.  Cover the skillet and allow the spinach to wilt for a few minutes.  Mix in the fennel seed and stir to blend.  Remove from heat and drain excess liquid.  Place spinach mixture in food process and quickly chop.
In a large bowl, mix eggs, ricotta, half the mozzarella, half the Parmesan, Spinach mixture, Italian Seasoning, salt and pepper until well combined.  Stuff cooked shells with ricotta mixture and place in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Pour the sauce over stuffed shells and sprinkle with reserved cheeses.
Bake in preheated oven 45 to 60 minutes, until edges are bubbly and shells are slightly set.
