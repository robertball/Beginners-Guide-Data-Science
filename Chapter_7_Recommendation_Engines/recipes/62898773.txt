Title:
Cottage Cheese Dill Buns - Pyrizhky Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 recipe of your favorite bread dough.[enough for a 2lb.loaf]
2 1/2-3 cups dry cottage cheese
1/2 cup chopped onions
1 Tbsp.oil
1 egg
3 Tbsp.whipping cream
1/4 cup finely chopped green onion tops
1/4 tsp.salt or to taste
CREAM SAUCE:
1 Tbsp.margarine
2 chopped green onions
2 Tbsp.chopped fresh dill
2 cups whipping cream

Directions:
Saute chopped onions in oil untill soft.Whisk egg in a bowl and stir in whipping cream.Add onions,dill,green onions and cottage cheese,stir well.Add salt to taste.
After first dough rise,divide dough into 24 pieces.Roll each piece into a 4-5 inch cicle.Place 2 Tbsp.of filling in centre.Pull sides in toward the centre and pinch together.Place seam side down on greased baking sheet.Cover and let rise in warm place for 60-90 min.Bake at 350 for 20-30 min.untill golden.Makes 24 large buns.
Melt margarine in skillet,add green onions and cook till soft,do not brown.Add cream,dill and salt and pepper to taste.Bring to a slow soil then simmer till cream begins to thicken.Add buns cover for 5-10 min.serve immediately.
