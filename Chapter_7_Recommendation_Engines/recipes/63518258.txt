Title:
Savory Tri Tip Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/960710.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup Olive Oil
1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
1 heaping teaspoon crushed garlic
1 Tablespoon Red Wine Vinegar
1 Tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a plastic zip lock bag.  Add up to 2 pounds of pierced steak or roast.  Place in refrigerator and marinate for a minimum of 4 hours.
Grill or roast meat to an internal temperature of 145 degrees for medium rare.
