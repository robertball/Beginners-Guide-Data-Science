Title:
Rumherzen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/8 c flour
2 tsp baking powder
2 eggs
1 c sugar
1 c almonds, finely ground
1/2 tsp vanilla extract (or 2 tsp of vanila sugar)
3 tbsp rum
9 tbsp butter
Icing:
1 c powdered sugar
1 bottle of rum flavoring
1-1 1/2 tsp hot water

Directions:
Sift flour and baking powder together and set onto a pastry board. Make a well in the center and pour in the sugar, ground almonds, vanilla, rum and eggs. Draw in some of the flour from the sides of the well to mix with these ingredients to form a thickish paste. Add butter, cut into small pieces, cover with more flour. Starting from the middle, knead the dough until firm and smooth. If it is sticky refridgerate for an hour.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees, and prepare baking sheets with parchment paper.
Roll out to 1/8" and cut out in 3" shapes. Transfer with spatula to baking sheets. Bake for 8-12 mins.
Let cool on wire racks. Ice the cookies while still warm and place 1/2 almond on top of each cookie.
ICING: Mix powderen sugar and rum flavoring with enough hot water to give the cookies a good coat.
Let dry complety before packing in air tight container, will hold for 1 week.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
A tradtional german christmas cookie that has a buttery rum and almond flavouring. They look simple but taste like a warm christmas on a cold snowy day.
