Title:
Vegetable Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
8 oz. mushrooms - sliced
1 clove of garlic - crushed
2/3 cup diced onion
1/8 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
10 oz. frozen spinach - thawed and drained
1 lb. frozen chopped broccoli - thawed and drained
1 carrot - shredded
1 lb. low-fat ricotta cheese
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
4 cups of marinara sauce
9-12 cooked lasagna noodles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Heat olive oil in a large skillet.
Add mushrooms, garlic and onion and saute until soft.
Add nutmeg, oregano, salt and pepper.
Remove from heat and stir in spinach, broccoli, carrot and cheeses.
To assemble, spread 1 cup of marinara in the bottom of a rectangular baking pan.
Place 3 or 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce and top with 1/3 of the vegetable/cheese mixture.
Top with 1 cup of marinara and sprinkle with Parmesan.
Repeat twice.
Cover lightly with foil and bake 45 minutes or until center is hot.
