Title:
Raspberry muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
150 g plain flour
1 tsp baking powder
100g sugar
50g butter
50g margarine
3 eggs
200g fresh raspberries
rind of 1 lemon, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 180 degrees of Celsius.
Sift flour and baking powder together in a bowl.
Beat eggs with butter, margarine and sugar.
Fold flour mix into the egg mix.
Add grated lemon rind.
Mix well for around 10 minutes.
Add fresh berries and mix well until the berries are broken and spread.
Divide the mix into muffin pan (greased or lined with paper muffin cups).
Bake at 180C for 40 minutes or until a toothpick stuck in the center comes out clean.
Take out and cool on a wire rack.
