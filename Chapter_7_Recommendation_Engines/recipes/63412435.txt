Title:
DR PEPPERED CROCKPOT CORNED BEEF` Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 LITERS OF DR PEPPER
1 3-5 LB CORNED BEEF BRISKET
2 CUPS OF LIGHT OR DARK BROWN SUGAR
2 ORANGES (PEELED)

Directions:
FILL HALF OF SLOW COOKER WITH CORNED BEEF, 1 CUP OF BROWN SUGAR AND TURN ON HIGH.  ADD CORNED BEEF FOLLOWED BY 1 CUP OF BROWN SUGAR OVER THE TOP OF CORNED BEEF.  SURROUND THE CORNED BEEF WITH ORANGE SLICES.  POUR ONE CUP OF DR PEPPER OVER THE BEEF COVER AND COOK ON HIGH FOR 3 HOURS.  CHANGE HEAT TO LOW AND COOK FOR ANOTHER 2 1/2 HOURS AND ENJOY!
