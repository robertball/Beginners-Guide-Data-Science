Title:
Far East Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup seasoned rice vinegar
1 TBL soy sauce
1 tsp ground ginger
1 cup cooked chicken, chopped
4 cups cabbage, shredded
1 carrot, shredded
2 green onions, sliced
2 TBL sesame seed, toasted

Directions:
Combine vinegar, soy sauce and ground ginger. Add chicken and marinate for 15 to 30 minutes. In salad bowl, combine remaining ingredients. Just before serving, remove chicken from marinade, reserve. Pour marinade into cabbage mixture; toss. Top with chicken.
