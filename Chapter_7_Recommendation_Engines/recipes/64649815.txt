Title:
Chicken Stuffing Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box chicken stuffing
1 stick butter
1 pkg mixed vegetables (thawed)
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 c milk
1 T finely chopped onion
2 lbs chicken, cooked in pieces

Directions:
1.  Combine stuffing and butter.  Put half of this mix in a 9 x13 casserole dish.
2.  In a large bowl mix soup and milk.  Then add veggies, onion, and chicken.  Mix well.
3.  Mix with stuffing in a casserole dish.  Top with remaining stuffing mix.
4.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes until edges are bubbly.

Submitter: 
Renee Heston
Description: 
My aunt made this for us when my baby sister was born and it was an instant family favorite!
