Title:
Easy Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 med. apples
1 c  water
3/4 c sugar
1/4 t cinnamon

Directions:
1. peel, core and slice apples
2. in stock pot bring to boil all ingredients.
3. cover, reduce heat to simmer until apples are tender (10-15 min.)
4. in blender puree to desired consistency
5. return to boil and simmer another 15 min
