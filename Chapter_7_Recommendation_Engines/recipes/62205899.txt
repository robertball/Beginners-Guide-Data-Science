Title:
Kick A$$ Spicy Turkey Beer Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/306573.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 12 ounce can or bottle of beer (recommended - Porter for smokiness)
1 large white onion, diced
3 garlic cloves, minced
4 stalks celery, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
2 jalapenos with seeds, diced
3 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, diced (can be purchased canned)
1 packet low sodium McCormick chili seasoning
a couple of drops of liquid smoke, to taste
1 28 ounce can (or 1 14.5 ounce cans) diced tomato
1 28 ounce can (or 1 14.5 ounce cans) tomato sauce
2 cans spicy chili beans
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon paprika
salt & pepper, to taste
1 tablespoon olive oil
TOPPING
light sour cream
shredded cheddar cheese
fresh cilantro
green onion, diced
SIDE
cornbread

Directions:
In a large pot, brown turkey in olive oil and season with paprika, salt and pepper.
Add jalapeno, celery, white onion, garlic and chipotle peppers.  Saute vegetables with turkey until onions are translucent, adding more olive oil if necessary.
Add diced tomato, tomato sauce, chili seasoning, beer, liquid smoke and spicy chili beans.
Stir and let simmer for at least 2 hours.
Serve topped with fresh cilantro, green onion, shredded cheddar cheese, and light sour cream on top with cornbread on the side.

Description: 
This delicious red chili has the right amount of kick and will warm you right up!  Healthified, with the use of ground turkey and veggies.  Perfect with a beer, during a football game, on a cold winter night, or on top of a hot dog.  This recipe is sure to please!  Can be made on the stove top or slow cooker.
