Title:
Quick pork loin cutlet with balsamic cranberry sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5lb Pork loin or boneless chop, 1/2 in thick
Several cloves garlic
Rosemary: 2 sprigs fresh or 1t & 1T dried rosemary
Some flower for coating pork
1T sea salt
1t whole pepper corns
1 can 'whole berry' cranberry sauce
2 - 3 T balsamic vinegar
Olive oil
2 t butter

Directions:
Over low heat, add 1t butter and cranberry sauce.
Break apart the blob of cranberry sauce, add rosemary (Dried 1t, 1/2 sprig fresh), and allow it to heat until it starts to bubble a bit, then add balsamic.
Crush together garlic, sea salt, pepper, remaining rosemary
Once crushed add a 1T or so of olive oil.
Coat pork with paste
Lightly dust pork with flower
Heat pan to HOTTTTT
drop in remaining butter, a couple glugs of olive oil, and then pork
cook 2-3 minutes per side. cook in batches so pan stays hot!
