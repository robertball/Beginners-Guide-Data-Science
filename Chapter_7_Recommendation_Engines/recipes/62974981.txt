Title:
Dutch Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C butter, softened
1/2 C brown sugar
1 C white sugar
1 egg, beaten
1 1/2 C flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
t tsp vanilla
3/4 C fine, unsweetened coconut
1 1/2 C rolled oats

Directions:
Preheat over to 325 degrees.
Cream butter and sugar, add egg and vanilla.
In separate bowl, combine all the dry ingredients.
Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix.
Drop by teaspoon on to parchment lined baking sheet. Flatten with fork.
Bake at 325 degrees for 11-13 minutes.
