Title:
Mary and Kims Summer Grilled Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Head cabbage
large green bell pepper
large onion
pack bacon (12 to 20 oz)
4-6 oz italian dressing
Tony Chachere's Creole Seasoning 1 tsp

Directions:
Slice cabbage, bell pepper and onion.
Cook bacon. Reserve just enough bacon grease to cook onion and bell pepper on medium heat until wilted.
Add cabbage and creole seasoning cook just until cabbage becomes translucent.
Add dressing till moist, not soupy. Mix well.
Stir in crumbled or cut bacon.
Can be covered and left on stovetop on low heat or put into dish and baked 30 minutes on 325 degrees or wrapped in heavy duty foil on the grill.

Submitter: 
MAREBELA
Description: 
Cabbage cooked with onion, green bell pepper, bacon. An interesting compliment to BBQ: Can be grilled, baked or stove top.
