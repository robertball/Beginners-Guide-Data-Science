Title:
Peanut Butter Dog Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 tbsp. baking powder
1 cup peanut butter (chunky or smooth)
1 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In a bowl, combine flour and baking powder
In another bowl, mix PB and milk, then add dry ingredients and mix well
Place dough on lightly floured surface and knead
Roll dough to 1/4 inch thickness and use a cookie cutter to cut shapes
Bake for 20 minutes on greased baking sheet until lightly brown
Let cool and then store in airtight container
