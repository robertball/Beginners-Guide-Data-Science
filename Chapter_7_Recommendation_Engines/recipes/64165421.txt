Title:
Apple Coffee Cake ala "Gram" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups peeled, cored and diced apples
2 cups sugar
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 cups flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon allspice
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine apples and sugar in a large mixing bowl.  Toss to coat  Add eggs, oil and vanilla and mix to combine.
In another bowl, combine flour, soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice and salt.  Mix to combine, then add to the large bowl of wet ingredients.  Mix well.
Pour into a greased 9"x13" glass or Pyrex baking dish.
Bake at 350ยบ for about 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.  Cool in the pan on a wire rack.  Cover with plastic wrap to keep moist.

Submitter: 
Amy Randy Smith
Description: 
My mom, or Gram as her 10 grandkids lovingly refer to her, made this for her 5 kids when we were growing up.  Even on our tight budget, she managed to convert those apples dropping off our tree into something wonderful every fall.
