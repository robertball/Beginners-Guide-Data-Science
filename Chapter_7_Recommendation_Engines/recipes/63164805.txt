Title:
Homemade Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 oz. lowfat milk
1-2 t. gelatine
3 T. plain yogurt (not Greek)

Directions:
Pour milk into large glass measuring cup.
Heat in microwave to 180 degrees. (scalds in pan)
Set measuring cup in a larger container of cold water and cool to 115 degrees.
Remove 1 C. of warm milk and stir in 3 T. yogurt until smooth.
Pour back into larger portion, stirring until smooth.
Sprinkle gelatine over surface and allow to soften for a few minutes, then stir into mixture.
Pour into clean yogurt jars (do not cover with lids), put into yogurt maker and allow to culture for 6-8 hours.
Remove jars, cover and refrigerate.
There will be a small amount of whey on top.
