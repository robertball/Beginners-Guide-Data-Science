Title:
Rum Runner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985367.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Rum Runner mix
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
5 cups ice
Blackberries for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Rum Runner mix, BACARDI(R) Rum and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with blackberries.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
This is a fabulous blackberry flavored drink with blended rum and ice. Perfect for a backyard barbeque.
