Title:
Homemade Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of Flour
2 tsp. of Baking Powder
1 tsp. of Salt
1 cup of Milk

Directions:
Mix all the dry ingredients together in a small bowl, slowly mix in milk. The mixture should be a stiff dough like consistency. Put 1 tbsp. full into stew at a time, make sure you have enough broth to do this. Let cook for 10 minutes on each side. They will double in size and become fluffy, that is how you will know that they are done.
