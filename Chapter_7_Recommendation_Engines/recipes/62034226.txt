Title:
Texas Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large onions, sliced
1 large green pepper, minced
1 lb ground beef
3 tablespoons olive oil
2 cups (1 can) diced tomatoes
1 cup rice (uncooked)
1 tsp. chili powder
2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper

Directions:
In a large pot saute onions and green pepper in oil.  Add ground beef and cook until mixture falls apart.  Stir in tomatoes, rice, chili powder, salt & pepper.
Lower heat and simmer 20 minutes, until rice is tender.
