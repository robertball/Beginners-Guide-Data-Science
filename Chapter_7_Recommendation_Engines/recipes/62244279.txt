Title:
Peppered Pancetta-or-prosciutto wrapped Shrimp with Curry Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. extre-virgin olive oil
3/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
1/2 tsp. minced garlic
1/8 tsp. freshly ground white pepper
12 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
12 thin slices pancetta or prosciutto
1 tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
Curry dip
1 cup mayonaise
1/2 tsp. curry powder
1/2 tsp. lemon juice
1/4 tsp. salt or to taste

Directions:
1.Combine first 5 ingredients in a bowl. Add your shrimp, and toss to coat. Let sit for 5 minutes. Wrap each shrimp with 1 slice of pancetta or prosciutto. Thread shrimp on skewer.
2. Grill or broil shrimp over medium high heat for 2 minutes each side. Pancetta or prosciutto should be browned and crisp. Srinkle with fresh chopped parsley and serve with curry dip on side.
