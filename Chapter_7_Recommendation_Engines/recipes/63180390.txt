Title:
Ham Tetrazzini 4.5 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/817990.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons chopped onion
1 tablespoons chopped garlic
1 tablespoon butter
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup with roasted garlic
1/2 cup milk or cream
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup diced ham
6 ounces angel hair pasta
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
2 tablespoons chopped pimento peppers
pepper to taste
sprinkle of red pepper flakes

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a large saucepan over medium-high heat, saute chopped onion, garlic, and mushroom in butter until tender.
Stir in cream of mushroom soup, water, and shredded cheese. Heat mixture until cheese melts, stirring often.
Add ham, cooked and drained pasta, chopped pimento, and chopped parsley to saucepan; stir.
Stir until heated through, serve hot!

Submitter:

Description: 
Here's a great idea for leftover ham! Stir it into a hearty sauce of cream of mushroom soup, sauteed onions and shredded Cheddar cheese. Add cooked spaghetti, some chopped pimento and parsley for snappy color, then serve piping hot.Modified from: Ham Tetrazzini
