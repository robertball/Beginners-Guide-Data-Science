Title:
Broccoli, Chicken, and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 stalks broccoli, cut into smaller spears
1/3 cup milk
1 (10.75 oz) can  Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 (10.75 oz) can  Cheddar Cheese Soup
1 cup uncooked regular rice
1 lb cooked, shredded chicken

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl, stir together the onion, broccoli, milk, soups, rice and chicken.  Pour the broccoli mixture into a lightly greased 9x13 in glass pan.  Cover with aluminum foil.
Bake for 45-60 minutes or until the mixture is hot and bubbling and the rice is cooked.
