Title:
Kooky Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 C vegetable oil
5 C butter, softened
5 C white sugar
5 C packed brown sugar
5 eggs
3 Tbsp. vanilla extract
17 C all purpose flour
2 Tbsp. baking soda
2 Tbsp. salt
5 C cornflakes
5 C rolled oats
7 C Nuts (pecans, almonds, walnuts)
5 C flaked coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Beat butter and sugars until fluffy.  Slowly add oil and beat until oil is well-incorporated.  Add egg and vanilla and beat until mixed.
In a small bowl combine flour, soda, and salt.  Add to butter mixtuer and stir just until mixed.  Combine cornflakes, oatmeal, nuts, and coconut, and add to cookie mixture.  Mix just until combined.
Scoop portions out onto a cookie sheet using a cookie scoop.  Bake 8 minutes or until lightly browned.
