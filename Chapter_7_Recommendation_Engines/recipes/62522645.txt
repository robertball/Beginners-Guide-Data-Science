Title:
Rosemary Lemon Grilled Goat Backstrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup fresh rosemary
3 cloves garlic
1 lemon, zested
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1 goat back strap
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a food processor, thoroughly blend together the olive oil, rosemary, garlic, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Season backstrap with salt and pepper. Put in a ziplock and pour marinade over, and refrigerate 3 hours.
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate. Grill to desired doneness.

Submitter: 
lovetoeat
Description: 
This simple, sensational marinade and sauce for grilled goat combines rich and zesty flavors of lemon, garlic, rosemary, and butter.Modified from: Rosemary Lemon Grilled Chicken
