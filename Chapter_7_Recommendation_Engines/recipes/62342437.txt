Title:
Agave Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/392223.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch fresh, raw broccoli, finely chopped (about 1 pound)
1 C sliced carrots
1 C chopped red onions
1 C unsweetened raisins
1 C chopped bell pepper (any color will do)
1/3 C agave nectar (or 1/3 C raw cane sugar)
1/4 C white vinegar
1/2 - 3/4 C Vegannaise (or Mayonnaise or Miracle Whip)
1/3 C walnuts (optional)

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together agave nectar, vinegar, and Vegannaise until well blended.  Add remainder ingredients and toss to coat.  Serve chilled.
