Title:
Onion, Garlic and Mushroom Pasta...Oh My! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/483829.jpg
Ingredients:
1-16oz. box linguine, or pasta of choice
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup olive oil (I use extra virgin)
2-3 medium onions, chopped
3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
1-8oz. package sliced mushrooms
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, or more to taste
3/4 teaspoon dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
1/4 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste
1/2 cup pasta water, or as needed
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese, optional

Directions:
Heat butter and olive oil in pan over medium heat. Add in the onions, garlic, mushrooms, red pepper flakes, salt, pepper and parsley, and saute until softened (about 8-10 minutes).
Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions, reserving some liquid; drain.
Mix pasta with onion mixture and taste for additional seasoning, optional.
Add pasta water, if needed.
Toss with parmesan cheese, optional.
Serve with extra parmesan cheese at the table.
Enjoy!
