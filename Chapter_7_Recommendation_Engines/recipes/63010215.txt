Title:
Banana raisin spice cake / bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cooking oil
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs
3 large, over ripe bananas
3 T sour milk
1 tsp baking soda
1 cup wheat flour
1 cup white flour
1/4 - 1/2 cup raisins
1 tsp allspice
Crushed nuts, if desired.

Directions:
Cream together oil and sugars until smooth. Add the bananas and one cup of the flour, mix until smooth. Add the sour milk, baking soda, the remaining flour, raisins and allspice. Mix until all the flour is incorporated.
Spread into a greased 9x9 or 8x12 pan (depending if you want cake or bars).
Bake at 335*F for 25-35 minutes, or until the top is a dark golden brown.
Frost with cream cheese frosting. Garnish with a sprinkle of allspice.
