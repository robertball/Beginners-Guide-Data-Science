Title:
Layered Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8oz) block cream cheese, softened
8 ounces sour cream
1 package taco seasoning mix
shredded lettuce
chopped tomato
shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, sour cream, and taco seasoning.  Spread in a square cake pan.  Layer with lettuce,tomatoes, and cheese.  Chill.  Serve with tortilla chips.  Any other desired taco toppings could also be layered on dip.
