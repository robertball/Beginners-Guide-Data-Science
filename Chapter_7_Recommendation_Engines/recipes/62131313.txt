Title:
Multigrain Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups water
2 tablespoons butter
2 1/3 tablespoons honey
2/3 tablespoons molasses
1 1/2 cups bread flour
1 1/3 cups whole wheat flour
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons vital wheat gluten
1 teaspoon active dry yeast
2 Tablespoons golden flax seeds
2 Tablespoons brown flax seeds
2 Tablespoons sesame seeds
2 Tablespoons soft wheat berries
1 tablespoon poppy seeds
1/3 cup sunflower seeds
Additional butter optional

Directions:
Place water and butter in microwave safe bowl and microwave for 30 seconds then add to bread machine.
Add honey and molasses.
Add flour, salt, vital wheat gluten, yeast, sesame seeds, flax seeds, wheat berries and poppy seeds. (Flax seeds may be lightly ground in coffee grinder prior to addition).
Select dough cycle and follow remaining instructions (may use basic dough cycle and add sunflower seeds when alert sounds during knead cycle).
When finished add sunflower seeds and restart dough cycle to incorporate, or knead in by hand.
Shape into log and place in greased 9x5 loaf pan.
Let raise 45 to 60 minutes in warm area.
Bake in 350 degree oven 30 minutes, turning after 15 minutes.
Immediately remove from pan, place on rack and butter top crust if soft crust is desired.

Submitter: 
Heather Lapp Cresmen
Description: 
An easy to make bread which looks like what you would buy in the store, but tastes amazing.  It will have you wondering why you ever spent so much on bread for your family.
