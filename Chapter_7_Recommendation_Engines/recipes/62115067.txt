Title:
THE Hamburger steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
3/4 cup plain bread crumbs
1 pck. onion soup mix
1/2 cup minced sweet yellow onion
1 egg
1 can cream of mushroom
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp. Kitchen Banquet
1/2 beef broth
1 tsp. cornstarch

Directions:
Mix first five ingredient well with hands, form into patties slightly thicker than a hamburger
Place in frying pan over med. heat and brown on both sides
Cover with lid and continue to cook, check for doneness (about 15 min.)
Uncover cook each side for an additional 2 min. each until a nice crust forms
Meanwhile mix Cream of mushroom, banquet, broth, cornstarch and milk, whisk
Remove patties and drain most of the grease from pan
mix cream of mushroom mixture into same pan on med. heat and whisk
After gravy thickens pour over steaks and serve

Submitter: 
Kristy
Description: 
Great recipe for a filling southern style meal at a low cost, scrumptious gravy finishes off these all star steaks, my husband requestes this twice a week
