Title:
Egg Strada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 eggs
6 pieces of white bread, cut into 1-inch squares
4 green onions
1 tsp. dried basil
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. black pepper
2 cups Hal and Half
1 lb. breakfast sausage, browned and drained
1 pkg. sliced fresh mushrooms
1 stick butter
2 cups grated cheese
Garlic salt

Directions:
Saute mushrooms with green onions in butter.  Season with garlic salt.
Mix together eggs, Half and Half, bread and seasonings.  add other ingredients.  Pour into a 9 x 13 sprayed pan.
Bake at 300Â° uncovered for 1 hour.
