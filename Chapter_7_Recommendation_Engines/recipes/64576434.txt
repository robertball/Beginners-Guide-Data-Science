Title:
Fried Egg and Crunchy Breadcrumb Breakfast Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz whole-grain bread (1 slice)
1 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil, divided
1 large egg
1 1/2 tsp white wine vinegar
1/8 tsp kosher salt
1/8 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 cups salad greens
4 radishes, halved

Directions:
Tear bread into small pieces. Heat 1 teaspoon oil in small skillet over medium-high heat. Add breadcrumbs to pan; cook 3 minutes or until toasted, stirring occasionally. Remove from pan.
Reduce heat to medium. Add 1/2 teaspoon oil. Crack egg into pan; cover and cook for 1 1/2 to 2 minutes or until desired doneness.
Combine remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Add greens and radishes; toss to coat. Top with egg and breadcrumbs.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
If you have access to whole-grain rye bread, definitely give it a try here; it's so flavorful on it's own that it adds special lift to this salad.
