Title:
Brocco-Cran Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of broccoli
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1 green onion, chopped
2 Tbsp bacon pieces
1/2 cup T. Marzetti's Spinach Salad Dressing
1/4 cup sliced almonds

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.  Best if let set overnight, but may be served immediately.

Submitter: 
Sarah Ripley
Description: 
This is a twist on the classic broccoli salad recipe.  I think the cranberries and almonds are a nice change.
