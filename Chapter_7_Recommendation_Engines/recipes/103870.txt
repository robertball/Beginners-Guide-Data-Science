Title:
Berry Simple Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985439.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups frozen unsweetened whole strawberries
1 cup plain fat-free yogurt
1/2 cup lite cranberry juice
1/4 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated

Directions:
Combine strawberries, yogurt, cranberry juice, and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener in a blender until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides.
Pour into three glasses and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
A splash of cranberry juice takes a strawberry smoothie to a new level of greatness.
