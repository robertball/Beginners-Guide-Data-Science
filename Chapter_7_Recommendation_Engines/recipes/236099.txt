Title:
Winter White Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089870.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces cold milk
3/4 cup Kahlua
3/4 cup chocolate liqueur
1 tablespoon frozen whipped topping
Pumpkin pie spice (optional)

Directions:
Add milk, Kahlua, chocolate liqueur, and whipped topping to a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake vigorously for about 10 seconds and strain into martini glasses. Sprinkle with pumpkin pie spice if desired.
