Title:
Jewish Coffee Cake I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake recipe
2 1/2 cups flour
1 1/4 cup sugar
3 eggs
1 1/4 tsp Baking powder
1 1/4 tsp Baking soda
1 1/4 cup sour cream
3/4 cup butter softened
1 tsp vanilla
Topping recipe
1/4 cup butter softened
1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Mix cake ingredients together adding flour last, a little at a time.
Batter will be thick.
Pour into greased 13x9 baking dish.
Mix topping ingredients with your fingers until crumbly and sprinkle over cake mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes

Submitter: 
Suzanne
Description: 
My mom got this recipe from the Jewish nursing home where she used to work.  The cake is very moist and easy to make with ingredients you are sure to have on hand.
