Title:
quick chicken mole poblano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. brown rice
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp. salt
1/3 c. chopped cilantro
1 T. olive oil
2 c. finely chopped onion
1 large poblano, seeded and chopped
1 T. chopped garlic
2 tsp. cumin
1 1/2 tsp. coriander
1 1/2 tsp. NM chile powder
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
3 c. chicken broth, divided
1 can fire-roasted crushed tomatoes
3. T. canned chipotle chiles, chopped
8 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
1 oz dark chocolate, chopped
1 T. tahini

Directions:
Start the rice cooking in 2 c. broth with bay leaf and salt. When rice is just tender and most broth is absorbed, remove from heat, add cilantro, and fluff with a fork. Cover and let stand off heat for 10 minutes to finish cooking.
While rice cooks, heat oil and cook onion and chile till onion is tender, 5 minutes. add spices and saut√© till fragrant but not browned, 30 seconds. add 1 c. of the broth, the tomatoes and the chipotle chiles.  Stir, then add chicken. Bring to a simmer, cover and reduce heat to low. Cook till tender, 30 minutes.
Transfer the chicken from the sauce to a cutting board. Shred the chicken. Add the chocolate and tahini to the sauce and blend with an immersion blender. Add shredded chicken to the sauce and return to a simmer. Serve over the rice.
