Title:
Mobridge Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/745210.jpg
Ingredients:
2 TBL brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp dried basil leaves
1 1/2 garlic powder
1/2 tsp seasoned salt
1/8 tsp red pepper
2 TBL extra virgin olive oil
A variety of fresh vegetables to cook. (ie. zucchini, yellow squash, green beans, cauliflower, mushrooms, carrots)

Directions:
Mix all the dry ingredients in a small bowl. Spoon mixture into a large plastic bag. Add 3-5 cups of any fresh vegetables. Toss lightly to cover all the vegetables with the stir fry mixture.
Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan. Add mixture from the bag and fry quickly on high until the vegetables are done to your liking. Between 5-10 minutes

Submitter: 
Stephanie Wentzlaff
Description: 
If your in the mood for a non-oriental stir fry, this is it! It is one we found traveling around the prairies of South Dakota. 
I quadruple the recipe and keep it on hand in a mason jar for quicker access.
