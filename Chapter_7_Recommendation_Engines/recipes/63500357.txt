Title:
Marazpan Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pie Pastry to cover 8 X 8 baking pan
Raspberry Jam
1/2 cup marjarine
2 eggs
2/3 cup sugar
2/3 cup rice flour
Icing
2 tbsp marjarin
1 1/2 cup icing sugar
2 tbsp warm milk
1 tbsp almond extract

Directions:
1. Put layer of pie pastry on bottom of greased pan
2. Spread raspberry jam on the top of the pastry
3. Mix marjarine, eggs, sugar and rice flour together to get a homogenous mixture
4. Divide step 3 in half
5. Add red food coloring to one half and green food coloring to the other half
6. Dab mixture on top of the raspberry jam, roughly inch thick alternating the red and green mixtures
7. Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes
8. Prepare the icing as follows, mix 2 tbsp marjarine, 1.5 cups icing sugar, 2 tbsp warm milk and 1 tsp almond flavouring togther until smooth
9. Spread step 8 onto a cool cake and chill
