Title:
Pumpkin Carrot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
2 onions, sliced or quartered
4 carrots, cut into large chunks
1 tbs curry
1 tbs asian five spice (optional)
1 tbs black pepper
8 cups chicken stock
2 cans pumpkin puree, 15 ounces each
1 quart half-and-half

Directions:
In a large, heavy pot, melt butter.
Add onions and cook over medium heat until translucent.
Add carrots, curry, five spice and black pepper. Mix well.
Add chicken stock.  Simmer until carrots are soft, about one hour.
Add pumpkin and heat through.
Remove from heat.  Cool enough to puree, using a food processor, blender, or immersion blender.
Refrigerate overnight.
The next day, reheat soup, stir in half-and-half, and continue until warmed through.

Submitter: 
Kate Horrell
