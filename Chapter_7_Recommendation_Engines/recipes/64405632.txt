Title:
Pink Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Chocolate wafer crumbs
2 tbsp melted butter
1 8 oz Philidelphia Cream Cheese, softened
1 can Raspberry frozen concentrate
2 tbsp icing sugar
1 later cool whip, thawed

Directions:
Combine first two ingredients to make the crust, freeze for 15 minutes.
Beat cream cheese, raspberry concentrate, icing sugar until smooth.
Fold in cool whip.
Pour over crust and freeze
