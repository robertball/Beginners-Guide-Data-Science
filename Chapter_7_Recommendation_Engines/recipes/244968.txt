Title:
Superfood Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2912530.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup nonfat plain yogurt
1/4 cup blueberries
2 tablespoons dried goji berries, or to taste
1 tablespoon ground flax seed
1 tablespoon ground walnuts, or to taste
1 tablespoon ground almonds, or to taste
1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon honey

Directions:
Mix yogurt, blueberries, goji berries, flax seed, walnuts, almonds, cocoa powder, cinnamon, and honey together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
This is a great tasting, quick breakfast concoction jam-packed with superfoods.
