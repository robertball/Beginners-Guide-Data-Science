Title:
Texas Trash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Sticks of real Butter
1 cup veggie oil
3 TBLS Garlic Powder
3 TBLS Onion Powder
3 TBLS Worchestershire Sauce
2 TBLS Allspice
1 Small Box Accent
1 bag of unseasoned chex snack mix or something similar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200 degrees.
Melt all above ingredients except snack mix in small saucepan.
Pour snack mix onto a cookie sheet and spread out evenly.
Drizzle melted ingredients over snack mix and bake in oven for 3 hours stirring every 15 minutes.
