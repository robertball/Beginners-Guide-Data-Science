Title:
Beer Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy cream
1 stick butter
6 oz beer (I used Rhinegeist Hustle IPA 6% APV and 50 IBU)
1/2 tsp white pepper
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/4 Red pepper, or more if you like it spicy
1 tsp mustard
4 cups shredded cheese

Directions:
Place cream, butter, beer and spices in sauce pan. Heat on medium low until butter is melted and it starts to boil, whisking often. Add cheese one cup at a time and whisk until melted. I used all sharp cheddar.
