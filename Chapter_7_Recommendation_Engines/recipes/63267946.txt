Title:
Sharon Lofdahl's Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/857327.jpg
Ingredients:
Cream below five ingredients together until fluffy.
2 c shortening (or butter or margarine)
2 c sugar
2 c brown sugar
4 eggs
1/2 tsp vanilla
Combine, then add the following dry ingredients to the creamed mixture.  Beat on medium speed with mixer until combined.  Do not over or under beat.
4-1/2 c flour
3 c oatmeal
2 tsp baking powder
2 scant tsp soda
2 tsp salt

Directions:
Drop by teaspoons full onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 400 degrees (F) for 8-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Chewy cookie enjoyed at our Church Potlucks in the early 1970s.  Sharon's home cooking was always delicious.
