Title:
Italian Dressing Mix like Good Seasons! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1073689.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon granulated garlic
1 tablespoon minced onion
1 packet of Splenda
1 tablespoons dried oregano
1 tablespoon of pizza seasoning
1 teaspoon lemon pepper
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
1 tablespoon of sea salt
1 tablespoon of ground dried tomatoes not in oil

Directions:
In blender mix together the garlic, onion, Splenda, oregano, pizza seasoning, lemon pepper, thyme, basil, parsley, celery seed and regular salt.  Store in a tightly sealed container.
To prepare dressing, whisk together 1/4 cup white vinegar, 2/3 cup oil, 2 tablespoons water and 2 tablespoons of the dry mix.
