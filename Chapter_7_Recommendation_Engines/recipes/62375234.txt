Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Apples
4 C apples, peeled and sliced
2 t. lemon juice
Topping
1/2 C. Flour
1 C Oats
1/2 C Brown sugar
1/2 t. salt
1 t. cinnamon
1/3 C butter, melted

Directions:
Apples: Mix ingredients together
Spread in thin layer in bottom of glass pan
Topping: Mix ingredients together
Put on top of apples
Bake at 375Â° for 30 min.
