Title:
Aunt Joanne's yummy corn casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can(15 oz) whole kernel corn, drained, such as Del Monte
1 can (15 oz)creamed style corn
2 Tablespoons butter
1 8oz brick of cream cheese, cut up into chunks
1 pkg (8.5 oz) corn bread mix, such as jiffy
2 eggs
1 to 1 1/2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 350* and grease a 13X9 baking dish.
2) In a microwave safe bowl heat butter for 30 seconds on high. Then cut up cream cheese and heat for 30 more seconds or til melted.Stir well.
3)In a large mixing bowl mix together corn bread mix, both things of corn, eggs, cream cheese mixture,and a little of shredded cheese.
4)Pour into prepared baking dish and top with shredded cheese.
5) Bake 45 minutes or til golden brown. Let cool 5 minutes then serve and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Amanda Lockwood
Description: 
I recently had this at my Aunt's with Christmas dinner and it was a huge hit. I just had to get the recipe and try it for myself and it was delicious.
