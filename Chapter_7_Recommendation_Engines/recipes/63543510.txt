Title:
Peter's Shrimp Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of shelled and de-veined shrimp
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup chopped cilantro minced
1/2 cup minced onion
1/4 cup fresh basil minced
Juice of 1 lemon
2 cloves garlic minced
1/8 tsp hot sauce
Cherry tomatoes
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix ingredients in a bowl.
Place shrimp in a ziploc bag and pour marinade over shrimp.
Place ziploc bag in refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
Soak 6-7 bamboo skewers in water for 30 minutes.
Thread shrimp and cherry tomatoes onto bamboo skewers and bbq over medium flame.
Cook skewers for 3 minutes a side or until shrimp is just done.
Serve immediately with your favorite rice dish

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This is our friend Peter Murphy's recipe for a delightfully light yet flavorful marinade for shrimp.
