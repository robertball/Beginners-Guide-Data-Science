Title:
Avocado and Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups avocados diced medium
2 cups grape tomatoes or 2 cups cherry tomatoes
2 cups cucumbers peeled and diced medium
1 cup red onion diced small
4 Tbs fresh cilantro chopped
2 tsp fresh garlic minced
2 Tbs lime juice
1/4 cup olive oil
salt
pepper
lettuce if you want

Directions:
toss all ingredients in a bowl and top on a bed of lettuce
makes 8 servings
