Title:
Cheddar Broccoli Chicken and Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981549.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Broccoli Cheese Soup or Campbell's® Condensed 98% Fat Free Broccoli Cheese Soup
1/3 cup milk
2 cups fresh broccoli flowerets or frozen broccoli flowerets
4 cups hot mashed potatoes

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet.  Add chicken and cook until browned.
Add soup, milk and broccoli.  Heat to a boil.  Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or until done.  Serve over potatoes.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Tender chicken, broccoli flowerets and a cheesy sauce are served over creamy mashed potatoes for a winning one-dish supper.
