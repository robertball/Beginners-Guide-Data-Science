Title:
Citrus Fir Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6126298.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 C fresh conifer needles or tips
1 C butter
2 C granulated sugar, divided
1 T orange zest
1/4 t salt
2 C flour

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
In a large bowl mix all the dry ingredients together.
Add the orange zest.
In a vita-mix, food processor or coffee grinder mince the pine needles taking care to remove any woody bits first.
Add minced needles to dry ingredients.
Add butter and combine with a pastry blender until a fine, even consistency.
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Evenly pour shortbread mixture onto cookie sheet.  Even mixture out on tray and then press down firmly.
Use a fork to make tong imprints all over dough.
Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit  for 30 minuets
Remove from oven.
Sprinkle one cup of sugar on top of cookies after removing from oven shaking tray to distribute sugar evenly.
Cut into squares while freshly out of the oven.
Enjoy

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Wild Edible take on a cookie favorite!
Pine needles are very high in vitamin C.
Use pine needles that are baby pine needles that were harvested when soft and tender in the spring.
