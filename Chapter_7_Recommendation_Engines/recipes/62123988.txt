Title:
Classic Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/3 cup sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 beaten egg
3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup cooking oil
3/4 cup blueberries

Directions:
pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees F
take the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt and mix in a bowl.
then mix the egg, milk, cooking oil, and blueberries in another bowl.
mix the two bowls together.
spoon batter into muffin tins
bake at 400 degrees F for 20 minutes
