Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick margarine
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup granulated sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
3/4 cup chocolate chips or 2 cups

Directions:
cream margarine, shortening and both sugars.  add eggs and vanilla.
mix dry ingred and stir into batter
add chocolate chips
refrigerate dough at least 1 hr before baking
grease cookie sheets
splat out using ice cream scoop. 6 per med tray
3 1/2 dozen for 10 mins
giant cookies 15-17 mins yield 20
ice cream scoops 22 mins yield 15
