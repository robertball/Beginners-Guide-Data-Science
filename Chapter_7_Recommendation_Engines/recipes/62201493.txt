Title:
Crust Bean Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 onion, finely chopped
1 small green bell pepper, chopped
2 garlic cloves
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained
3/4 cup salsa
1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
3/4 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (9 inch)  unbaked 9 inch pie crust
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat.  Saute onion, green pepper and garlic until tender.  Sir in beans, salsa, red bell pepper, chili powder, cayenne and black pepper.  Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes.
Spoon the mixture into the pie crust.  Top with cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 45-1 hour.
