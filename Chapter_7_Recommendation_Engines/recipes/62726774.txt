Title:
TGI Friday's Copycat Bruschetta Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (4 oz) chicken breasts
salt and pepper
1 # angel hair pasta
6-8 med. size roma tomatoes
4 T EVOO, divided
1/4 t salt
1/8 t pepper
2-3 minced garlic cloves
10 fresh basil leaves
2 garlic cloves, sliced into thin coins
1/2 C tomato sauce
1 C balsamic vinegar
1 T granulated sugar

Directions:
Chicken Breasts: Season both sides w/salt and pepper. Grill 3-4 min per side, 165 degrees. Grill right before serving.
Pasta: Boil pasta as per directions on box. Drain and transfer to bowl.
Tomato Sauce: Wash, core and dice tomatoies to 1/4" pieces, save juices and place in sm. bowl. Wash, dry and cut basil leaves into thin strips. Combine tomatoes, basil, 2 T EVOO, salt, pepper and minced garlic and hold for 2 hrs before use.
Heat heavy botton saucepan under med-low heat. Add 2 T EVOO, heat oil for 20 sec. Saute cloves of garlic coins in oil for 45 sec or each side until soft and tender-do not brown garlic. Increase heat and add tomato salad and stir.
Add plain tomato sauce to pan and bring to a boil. Add pasta to sauce pan and toss woth fresh sauce (sauce should just coat pasta). Transfer to a platter. Garnish with balsamic glaze.
Balsamic Glaze: Bring to a boil vinegar and sugar in sm . pain and tgurn doen flame to simmer. Reduce by 75% until sauce turns to a thick syrup, hold at room temperature.
To serve: Slice chicken breast into strips on a bias (45 degree angle) and place on top of pasta.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Grilled chicken in tomato salad served over angel hair pasta. Tomato salad needs to be made 2 hrs earlier.
