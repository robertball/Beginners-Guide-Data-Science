Title:
Chuck's Simple Grilled Portobello Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 portobella mushroom caps stem removed.
1/2 teaspoon olive oil per cap
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder per cap
1/2 teaspoon sea salt per cap
Fresh ground pepper to taste per cap

Directions:
Clean, rinse and pat dry mushrooms.
Twist stem and remove from each cap.
Add olive oil, garlic powder, sea salt and pepper to each cap.
Let sit for 30 minutes.
Add to a medium heat grill and cook for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Chuck Mac
Description: 
A very simple recipe for dressing up this awesome mushroom.
