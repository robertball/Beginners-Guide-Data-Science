Title:
Caramel Brownie's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MELT:
14 oz caramel's
1/3 cup evaporated milk
MIX:
1 box German chocolate cake mix
3/4 cup melted butter
1/2 cup evaporated milk
12oz pkg chocolate chips

Directions:
In a 9 x 13 greased pan press half the dough in the bottom of the pan.
Bake for 6min @ 350 degrees.
Put 12oz package of chocolate chips on top and cover with melted caramel.
Make patties with remaining dough to put on top. Continue to bake for 18-20min @ 350 degrees.
