Title:
Shrimp & Bokchoy Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs Shrimp (raw wt), peeled and deveined
Chicken broth, 49oz or equivalent
Rice noodles, such as Maifun, 5-8oz of dry wt.
Baby Portabella Mushrooms, 8oz
Yellow onion, 1 medium, chopped
Fresh ginger, 2" piece grated (~2 Tbls.)
Fresh garlic, 4 cloves, peeled and minced
Bok choy, medium, chopped

Directions:
Put chicken broth in large pot, cook over medium to medium high heat.
Make sure that shrimp is peeled or deveined or prep by hand if came otherwise.
Chop onion, ginger, and garlic - add to chicken broth.  Then chop bok choy & mushrooms - add to broth.
Weigh out or eyeball Rice noodles, cook in a separate saucepan in water.  Drain.  Cut noodles on cutting board in desired length.  Then add to broth.
Add Shrimp to broth and cook for about 3-5 minutes.  Take soup off of heat and allow to set until shrimp are completely done and pink.
Stir and enjoy!

Submitter: 
CBecker
Description: 
Delicious & healthy soup made with 8 ingredients!  This is also wonderful without the shrimp...but the shrimp are really good in it.  =)
