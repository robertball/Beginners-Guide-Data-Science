Title:
Vegan Banana Bread Machine Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Earth Balance butter, softened
2/3 cup almond milk
2 flax eggs (2 tbsp flax, 6 tbsp water, mixed and set)
2 1/2 cups Organic all-purpose flour
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 packed brown sugar
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 tsp vanilla
2/3 cup mashed bananas
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Spray bread machine pan with vegetable oil spray.
Premix ingredients in order of your machine. (important or it doesn't cook all the way through) Place mixture in bread machine pan.
Select the Quick Bread/Cake cycle.  Press Start. Check after 1 minute to see if mixture is well blended.
Cook until cake cycle stops.  Remove pan, and cool completely before removing bread from pan.
or you can do in oven for 60  mins or so on 375.
