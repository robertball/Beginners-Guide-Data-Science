Title:
Asian chicken salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
for salad:
1.5lbs chicken breast
1 bag mixed greens
1 small head napa cabbage, shredded
2 red peppers, julienned
1 c snow peas
3 Tbsp cilantro, minced
2 Tbsp green onions, sliced
for peanut sauce:
1c peanut butter
1/4c soy sauce
1/4c sesame oil
3 Tbsp rice vinegar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp hot pepper flakes
2 Tbsp minced fresh ginger
3/4c water
for sesame lime dressing:
1/4c sesame oil
1/4c rice vinegar
1/4c lime juice
1Tbsp honey
1tsp chili pepper flakes
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
optional garnishes:
crushed peanuts
fried wonton strips

Directions:
saute chicken breasts in equal parts sesame oil and soy sauce til cooked through, about 10 minutes. after chicken has cooked, chill in the refrigerator.
chop all vegetables for salad and toss together.
for peanut dressing, combine ingredients in blender, or jar with tight fitting lid and shake to blend, making sure peanut butter is fully incorporated.
for sesame lime dressing combine ingredients in tupperware container and shake to blend.
dress salad with sesame lime dressing, serve peanut sauce on the side.
if desired, to make wonton strips use about 10 sheets pre-made wonton wraps cut into 1/2 inch strips and fry in batches in peanut oil or other oil with high smoke point til crisp, about 2 minutes per batch.

a tangy, refreshing salad with thai-inspired dressings
