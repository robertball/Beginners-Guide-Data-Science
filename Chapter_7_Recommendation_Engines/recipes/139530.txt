Title:
Fresh Cranberry Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 quarts water
1 (12 ounce) package cranberries
2 cups SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2 oranges, juiced
2 lemons, juiced
12 whole cloves
2 cinnamon sticks

Directions:
In a large pot, combine water and cranberries. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes. Add SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, orange juice, lemon juice, cloves and cinnamon sticks. Cover, and steep for 1 hour.

Description: 
Fresh cranberries add an extra flavor dimension to this traditional tea.
