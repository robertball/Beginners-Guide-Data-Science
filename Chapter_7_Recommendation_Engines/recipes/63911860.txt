Title:
Big Ray's Crescent-Topped Cheeseburger Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084932.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1/4 cup chopped dill pickles
1/2 cup ketchup
1 Tbsp yellow mustard
1/8 tsp pepper
1 1/2 cups shredded American cheese (6 oz)
1 can (8 oz) crescent rolls
1 egg, beaten
1 Tbsp sesame seed

Directions:
Heat oven to 375Â°F. In 10-inch nonstick skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink, add onion cook additional 5 to 7 minutes, stirring frequently, until onions are translucent; drain. Stir in pickles,ketchup, mustard and pepper.
Spoon beef mixture into 11x7-inch (2-quart) glass baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese.
Unroll dough; press into 12x8-inch rectangle. Cut into 6 squares; place on top of cheese. Brush with egg; sprinkle with sesame seed.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until deep golden brown.
