Title:
BUSH'S® Best 3-Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984989.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground round beef
3 teaspoons chili powder
1 small yellow onion, chopped
1 small green pepper, chopped
2 (16 ounce) cans BUSH'S® Dark Red Kidney Beans
2 (16 ounce) cans BUSH'S® Pinto Beans
2 (15 ounce) cans BUSH'S® Black Beans
1 (14.5 ounce) can tomatoes
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
1/2 teaspoon cumin
Cinnamon, to taste
Sour cream

Directions:
Brown ground beef in heavy Dutch oven or soup pot. Add chili powder and mix well. Add onion and pepper to meat and cook for 2 minutes. Drain off excess grease. Stir in remaining ingredients except sour cream. Simmer on low heat for 10 minutes. Top with a dollop of sour cream.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
This hearty winter meal uses dark red kidneys, pintos and black beans. The cinnamon flavor gives this recipe a sweet kick.
