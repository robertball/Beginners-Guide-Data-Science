Title:
Crystal's Quick Avocado Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 avocado, quick cut into small pieces
1 handful of Cilantro, chopped
1/2 can Corn, drained
Juice of 1/2 Lemon or less depending on taste
Salt & Pepper to taste, if desired

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and allow to sit for 5 minutes.  Eat by itself or serve with pita bread or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Suzq777
Description: 
This is yummy dish from a great friend.  I hated avocado with a capital H and this made me love it.  It is a great party appetizer or a quick weeknight meal for one.
