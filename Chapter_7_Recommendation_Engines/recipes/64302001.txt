Title:
Chana Masala - Indian spiced Chickpeas - Lite Version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1958880.jpg
Ingredients:
1 to 1.5 cups Dry Chickpeas -  (you can use canned )
1/2 cup of oil divided
1 large onion
4 plum tomatoes
2 TBSP Ginger paste - divided
2 TBSP Minced Garlic - divided
3 tsp Coriander powder
1 tsp chili powder ( or to taste)
1 TBSP Garam Masala powder
Salt to taste
Chopped Coriander leaves for garnish

Directions:
If using dried chickpeas you will need a pressure cooker.
1. coat bottom of pressure cooker approx 1/4 cup of oil.  Once pressure cooker is heated and oil is warm put 1 TBS of ginger paste and 1 TBS minced ginger.  after sizzling put dried chickpeas that have been presoaked in pressure cooker with about 4 cups of water. you want about 1 to 1.5 inches of water above chick peas.  Place pressure cooker lid on top and cook.  Mine takes about 20 min.
2. While chick peas are cooking (skip step on if using canned) in a large pot put the rest of the oil approx 1/4 cup.  Once heated put remaining amount of garlic and ginger.
3. Once oil is hot add the chopped onion until starts to turn brown.
4. While that is cooking in a small bowl combine spices and set a side
5. Add tomatoes to pot once onions are brown. stir often and cook until tomatoes start to melt.
6. add dry spices to pot and stir.
7. By this time the chickpeas should be done.  Add whole pot including all liquid
8.  Cook on high stirring often until the liquid is reduced and the sauce is thick.
