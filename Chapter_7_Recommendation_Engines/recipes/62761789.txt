Title:
LEMON ROASTED POTATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/700157.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs.potatoes or 2 bags of small yukon
1/3 cup olive oil
juice of 2 lemons
2 tsp.salt
1/2 tsp.pepper
1 tsp.oregano
3 cups chicken broth

Directions:
Wash and peel potatoes and cut into thick wedges.Place in 2 inch deep pan,large enough to make one layer.Toss the potatoes with the olive oil and spices,add the broth.
Bake in a 400 oven for 1 hour or untill tender and golden brown,OR BBQ in a foil pan for about 30-40 min.
