Title:
Mama Marian's Sour Milk Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sift before measuring: 7/8 cup all purpose flour
Resift with 1 tsp. sugar, 1/2 tsp salt, 3/4 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp baking soda.
1 egg
1 cup sour milk (can be made by adding a drop or two of white vinegar to regular milk)
2 Tbsp. melted butter

Directions:
Beat milk and egg until light
Combine dry and liquid ingredients with a fork until JUST combined (do not over mix)
Mix in 2T of butter until JUST mixed (batter will be lumpy)
With griddle on medium, place some olive oil in the center and make sure the surface is coated.
Use 1/2 cup measuring cup to pour each pancake onto griddle.  When cakes are bubbling and light brown, flip.
Delicious served with butter and hot real maple syrup.

Submitter: 
Megan Fellows
Description: 
These thick, golden pancakes are easy to make and will always remind me of Saturday mornings in PJ's with my mom at the griddle.
