Title:
Garlic Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T extra virgin olive oil
3 medium garlic cloves (minced or pressed), apx 1 T
1/4 tsp table salt
6 pieces high quality white sandwich bread, cut into half inch cubes (apx 3 cups)

Directions:
Oven rack in middle position and set to 350
Combine oil, garlic and salt in small bowl and let sit for 20 minutes.
Pour into a large bowl through a fine mesh strainer and discard the garlic.
Add bread cubes to oil and toss to coat.
Spread bread cubes onto rimmed baking sheet and bake - shaking pan occasionally - until golden brown (apx 15 minutes).  Let cool then store in airtight container for up to 1 day.
