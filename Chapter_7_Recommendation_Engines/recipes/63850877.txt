Title:
SWEET & SOUR KIELBASA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (20 oz.) can pineapple chunks in juice
1/2 cup Smucker's Sugar-Free Apricot Preserve
2 tsp. cornstarch
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
1 Tbsp. cider vinegar
1 Tbsp. soy sauce
1 lb. Kielbasa, cut into 1/2" slices
1 large onion, cut into 1" pieces
1 bell pepper, cut into 1" pieces
1 Tbsp. vegetable oil

Directions:
Drain the pineapple, reserving 1/4 cup of the juice; set aside the pineapple. In a small bowl, mix together the apricot preserves, cornstarch and ginger. Stir in the vinegar, soy sauce and reserved pineapple juice.
In a large skillet, over medium heat, saute the sausage, onion and bell pepper in the vegetable oil until the vegetables a tender crisp, about 5-7 minutes. Add the pineapple and sauce mixture. Bring to a boil; cook and stir until thickened, about 2-3 minutes. Serve over steamed white or brown rice.
