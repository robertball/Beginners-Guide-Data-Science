Title:
Parmesan Alfredo Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018279.jpg
Ingredients:
1-8oz. cream cheese, cubed
3/4 cup heavy cream, or as needed
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper, or to taste
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, or to taste
2 small French baguettes, warmed and cubed

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, stir cream cheese over low heat, until just melted.
Add heavy cream and keep stirring to combine well.
Add parmesan cheese and stir until incorporated and smooth.
Add garlic powder, cayenne pepper, and parsley; stir.
Pour into small serving dish and top with freshly ground black pepper.
Serve warm with warmed bread cubes.
