Title:
Sausage-Corn Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6404063.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9.6 ounce) package Jimmy DeanÂ® Original Hearty Pork Sausage Crumbles
1 small onion, chopped
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 1/2 cups water
1 (15.5 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed, drained
1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
2/3 cup picante sauce or salsa
Optional Toppings:
Shredded Cheddar cheese
Sour cream
Chopped cilantro

Directions:
Cook sausage, onion and chili powder in large saucepan over medium heat 4-5 minutes or until onion is tender.
Stir in water, beans, corn, tomato sauce and picante sauce.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Ladle into bowls.  Add toppings, if desired.
