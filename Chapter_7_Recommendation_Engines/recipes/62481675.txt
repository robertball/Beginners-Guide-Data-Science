Title:
Double Layer Pumpkin Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3(8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 eggs
1/2 cup sour cream
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
8 ozs pumpkin puree
3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/21 pinch ground cloves
1 pinch ground nutmeg
1/2 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (165 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar and vanilla. Beat until smooth. Blend in eggs one at a time. Fold in sour cream.  Remove 1 cup of batter and spread into bottom of 10 in. spring form pan set aside.
Add pumpkin, cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg to the remaining batter and stir gently until well blended. Carefully spread over the batter in the crust.
Bake in preheated oven for 1hr, until middle jiggles like jello. Turn off oven,Prop open oven door and leave 1 hr. more. Allow to cool, then refrigerate for 3 hours or overnight. Cover with whipped topping before serving.
