Title:
Rest-easy Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp flour
1 large Reynolds Oven Bag
2 medium onions, quartered
1 stalk celery, sliced
2 baking potatoes cut in 1/2" cubes
4 carrots sliced
5-7 pound roasting chicken
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
Poultry seasoning
Salt & pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Shake flour in cooking bag and place in large roasting pan.
Place half of the vegetables in bag.
Rinse and pat dry chicken.
Place in bag and surround with remaining vegetables.
Brush chicken with oil.
Sprinkle chicken and vegetables with seasonings and spices.
Bake 2 hours or until tender.

Description: 
Moist chicken roasted with carrots, celery, onions and potatoes.
