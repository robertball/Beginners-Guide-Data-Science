Title:
Cinnamon Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/481154.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of butter or margarine, softened
2-3/4 cups of sugar, divided
2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
4 eggs
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon of baking soda
1 teaspoon of salt
2 cups (16 ounces) sour cream
2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
1/2 cup of chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter and 2 cups of sugar until fluffy. Add vanilla. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Combine flour,baking powder, baking soda and saly; add alernatley with sourcream, beating just enough after each addition to keep batter smooth. Spoon 1/3 of the batter into a greased 10-inch tube pan. combine cinnamon, nuts and remaining sugar; sprinkle 1/3 over batter in pan. Reapeat layers 2 more times. Bake at 350F for 70 minutes. Remove from pan to a wire rack to cool completely.
