Title:
German Chocolate Upside Doiwn Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box german chocolate cake mix
1 cup choped nuts
1 cup cocoanut
1 stick butter
1 8oz pkg cream cheese
3 cups powder sugar

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 cake pan. Mix cake mix as directed on package. Put chopped nuts in bottom of pan, sprinkle cocoanut over nuts/  Pour cake batter on top.  Cream the cream cheese, powder sugar and butter together.  Drop by spoonfuls on top of cake. Bake 350 for 45min to 1 hour.
