Title:
English Cheese and Crab Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz. packages cream cheese
2 5 oz. glass jars Old English Cheese from dairy case
1/2 Cup 1/2 and 1/2
2 Tbs Worcestershire Sauce
2 6 oz cans crab meat, drained
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
Loaf of day old French bread, cut into cubes
Baby carrots
Celery

Directions:
In Fondue Pot or double boiler, place cream cheese and stir until melted. Add Old English Cheese and, again stir until melted. Add Worcestershire Sauce, garlic salt, and cayenne pepper. Stir until blended. Add crab meat and stir.
Serve with bread and veggies.

Submitter: 
Andrea F
Description: 
This is a standard pub fare recipe from Jolly Old England. Old English Cheese and Crab combined and served with bread and veggies!! Delicious!!
