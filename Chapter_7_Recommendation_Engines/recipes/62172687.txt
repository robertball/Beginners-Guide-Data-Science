Title:
7min frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2C sugar
1/4tsp cream of tatar
1/4tsp salt
1/3C water
2tsp light corn syrup
2 egg whites
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In double boiler, combine all ing. except vanilla. Place over boiling water(pan shouldn't touch water) Beat on high about 7min or until mixture is at high peaks remove from heat. Add the vanilla, contiune beating util frosting holds a deep swirl(about 2min) add chocolate chips and swril if desired
