Title:
Carrot Pineapple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz carrots, shredded
1/4 cup pineapple chunks, drained and sliced
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup raisins, soaked in water to plump
2 T powdered sugar

Directions:
Combine carrots, pineapple, mayonnaise, raisins and confectioners' sugar;  mix well.
Chill until ready to serve.
