Title:
Acorn Meatballs (Vegetarian and Gluten Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6411109.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Acorn Meal
1 Cup Old Fashioned Oats
1/2 Cup Dehydrated Potato Flakes
1/4 Minced Dried Onions
1 t Cumin, dried
1 teaspoon Oregano, dried
1 teaspoon Basil, dried
1/4 Cup Chia Seeds
1/4 Cup Sesame Seeds
1/2 C Grated Cheddar Cheese
1 Peeled and Grated Carrot
2 Tablespoons Fresh Minced Parsley
1 teaspoon Baking Powder
1 Tablespoon Soy Sauce
4 Tablespoons Olive Oil
4 Eggs
6 Tablespoons Boiling Water
1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, stir together the acorn meal, potato flakes, baking powder, chia seeds, sesame seeds, spices
Add the oil, soy sauce and mix well
Add the boiling water and mix well
Add the carrots and grated cheddar cheese and mix well
Add eggs mixing making sure everything is combined well
Cover and let mixture sit for 30 minutes
Heat a cast iron skillet over medium heat add olive oil and heat
Form the mixture into walnut sized balls and add to the pan
Cook about 10 minutes or until browned turning occasionally to brown all of the sides

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
These wonderful meatballs have a delicious flavor.
Thanks to the potato flakes, oats, chia seeds they have several great binders so there is no need to products including gluten.
Easy to make and tasty.
Definite winner for all who tried it from a senior citizen to a 7 year old girl!
