Title:
Blue Ribon Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large eggs
2 TBL unsalted butter
3/4 cup flour
3/4 cup whole wheat flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cup vanilla low-fat yogurt
3/4 cup whole milk
1/2 cup pure maple syrup

Directions:
1. Let eggs get to room temperature
2. In large bowl, whisk together flours, baking soda & salt. Make a well in center & add yogurt, milk & eggs. Whisk until blended.
3. Heat nonstick skillet on medium, add butter to melt.
4. Measure out 1/3 cup of bather and cook on skillet. Flip after 2-3 minutes or until bubbles appear.
