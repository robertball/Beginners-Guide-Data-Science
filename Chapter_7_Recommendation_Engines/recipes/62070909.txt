Title:
FESTIVE PUMPKIN DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. cream cheese softened
1/2 cup canned or cooked pumpkin
2 Tablespoons Taco seasoning mix
1/8 tsp. garlic powder
1 pkg. chopped dried beef
1/3 cup chopped sweet green pepper
1/3 cup chopped sweet red pepper
1 can (2 1/4 oz.) sliced ripe olives    (drained)
1 round loaf Italian or Pumpernickel bread
idea: surround dip with fresh vegetables, crackers, corn chips or bread pieces

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, pumpkin, taco seasoning and garlic powder until smooth.  Stir in beef, peppers and olives.  Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.  Just before serving, cut tip off bread; scoop out the bread from the inside, leaving a half inch shell.  Fill the shell with the cream cheese mixture.  Yields 3 cups.

A tasty and colorful Mexican dip that you serve in a bread bowl.
