Title:
Chicken Enchillata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cream of chicken soup
4 cans diced chicken
1 sm. can chopped green chilies
1 lg. can evaporated milk
1 package broken Doritos
1 sliced sm. onion
10-12 slices swiss cheese

Directions:
Heat milk, soup and peppers
Line casserole dish w 1/2 of chips, chicken, onion and soup mix.
Add 1/2 of cheese.
Repeat layers.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
Let stand for 5-10 min. before serving.
