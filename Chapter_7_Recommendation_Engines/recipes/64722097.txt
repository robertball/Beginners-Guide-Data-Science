Title:
Poached Quince with Cranberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5964562.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups water
3/4 cup agave syrup
1/4 cup white sugar
1 cup fresh orange juice
2 quince fruits (about 14 ounces each)
1/2 cinnamon stick
2 cups fresh cranberries
1 tbsp orange zest

Directions:
Bring the water, agave, white sugar, and orange juice to a boil. Meanwhile, wash and peel the quince fruits. Cut out any dark spots.
Add the whole, peeled quince resting on one side to the boiling liquid. Turn down the heat. Simmer for 10 minutes. Turn the quinces over 180Â° and simmer another 10 minutes.
Add the cranberries. Simmer another 10 minutes or until the quinces are tender to a knife.
The quinces can be served cored or whole with the cranberries and syrup ladled over them. Top with orange zest.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Quince fruits poached in sweet syrup with tart cranberries. They make a beautiful presentation at your next get together.
