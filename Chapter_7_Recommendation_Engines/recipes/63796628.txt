Title:
Rost Chicken Legs with Potatoes, Garlic, and Rosemary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 lbs Chicken Parts
3 lbs Red Potatoes, Cut in 1" pieces
1 Bulb Garlic, broken into cloves, with extra paper removed (cloves should retail a layer)
1 Sweet Onion, Rough Chopped
2 T Fresh Rosemary Sprigs, Chopped
2 T Olive Oil
Salt to taste

Directions:
Toss all ingredients together, making sure that oil evenly coats all ingredients.
Spread evenly in large shallow pan.
Roast at 425 for 45 minutes.
Serve with crusty bread (for eating the garlic).
