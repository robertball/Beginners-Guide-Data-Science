Title:
Spinach and Shell Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
1 cup stewed tomatoes
4 1/2 small pasta shells
14 oz. can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup fresh spinach
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
3/4 oz. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large saucepot, bring chicken broth to boil
Add tomatoes and shells; cook 7 minutes
Add beans, spinach, oregano, basil; cook 8-10 minutes
Add water if too thick
Sprinkle with cheese upon serving
