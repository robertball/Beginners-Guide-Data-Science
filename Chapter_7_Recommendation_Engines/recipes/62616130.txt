Title:
Roasted Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # Brussels sprouts, washed and halved
1 T olive oil or cooking spray
3 cloves chopped garlic
1/4 t coarse salt

Directions:
Toss Brussels sprouts with oil and roast in 400 degree oven for 10 min. Remove from oven and add garlic and salt. Roast for another 5 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Can roast sprouts in olive oil or spray with cooking spray.  Add more garlic if you like
