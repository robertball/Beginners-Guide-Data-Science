Title:
Leftover Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup chopped sweet onion (i use frozen)
1/2 cup chopped green pepper (frozen)
1-1/2 t dried oregano
2 garlic cloves, minced (get the pre chopped)
1t ground cumin
1t olive oil
1 can (16 oz) kidney beans, rinse and drain
1 can (15-1/2 oz) cannellini beans, rinse and drain
1 can (26 oz) solid-pack pumpkin
1 can (26 oz) crushed tomatoes
1 can (14-1/2 oz) low salt chicken broth
1/2c water
4T brown sugar
4T chili powder
1t pepper
3c cubed cooked turkey breast
------------------------------------
child friendly chili:
after you cook the veggies, puree and follow directions
add half the beans whole, and half pureed, the less they see... the more likely they are to eat!  Lastly, serve to the kids with pita chips, tortillas cut into wedges, sprayed with cooking spray lightly seasoned with S,P garlic.
------------------------------------
for a spicier chili
add 1t cayenne
add 3T chopped cilantro
add 1/2t crushed red pepper

Directions:
For stove top:
-lightly toast the oregano, cumin and chili powder in dry pan @ 3min then add olive oil and garlic.
- add the onion and green pepper and saute till tender then add to large sauce pot with the cubed turkey.
- add in the beans, tomatoes, pumpkin, broth, water, brown sugar, and pepper, stir and bring to a boil.
- put lid on pot, lower heat and simmer for an hour to 1 1/2 hours, gently stir every 15 min.
For Crock pot:
-lightly toast the oregano, cumin and chili powder in dry pan @ 3min then add olive oil and garlic.
- add rest of ingredients and simmer low for 8 hrs.
