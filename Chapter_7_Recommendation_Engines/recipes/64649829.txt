Title:
Macaroni and Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 quarts water
5 t chicken bouillon cubes
1.5 c sliced celery
2 large carrots, chopped
1 large onion, diced
2 medium green pepper, diced
2.5 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
1 c butter
3/4 c flour
6 c milk
1 lb Velveeta

Directions:
1.  Both steps below should be done at the same time!
2.  In a soup kettle or Dutch oven, bring water and bouillon to a boil.  Add celery, carrots, onion, and green pepper; cook for 4 minutes or until tender.  Add macaroni.  Cover and return to a boil; boil for 2 minutes.  Remove from heat; let stand for 8-10 minutes or until macaroni is just tender.
3.  AT THE SAME TIME, melt butter in a saucepan.  Add flour, stirring until smooth.  Gradually add milk, stirring constantly.  Bring to a boil, cook and stir for 2 minutes.  Stir in cheese until melted, add to un-dranied macaroni mixture.
