Title:
Spicy Sweet hamburger dills Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gal. hamburger dills
2 pt jars jalepenos
4 c sugar

Directions:
drain dills and place in container.
drain jalepenos and layer on top of dills.
cover with sugar.
lid and put in fridge.
day 2- stir
day3- stir
day 4- jar up

Submitter: 
Jannabofanna
Description: 
spicy sweet pickles made out of a base of hamburger dill slices in the jar.
