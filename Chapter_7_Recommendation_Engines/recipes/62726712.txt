Title:
Tuna Casserole III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
2 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained and flaked
7 ounces crushed potato chips
1 (16 oz) bag of frozen peas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9 inch glass pie dish.
In a mixing bowl, combine soup, peas, tuna and 1/4 of the potato chips. Mix thoroughly. Pour into the prepared pie dish. Cover the top with remaining potato chips.
Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes or until potato chips on top start to brown.

Submitter: 
Audra Larimer
Description: 
Mushrooms soup, tuna and crushed potato chips. Quick and easy for students or busy moms and dads. Add a bag of frozen peas for extra veggies that add a little extra.Modified from: Tuna Casserole III
