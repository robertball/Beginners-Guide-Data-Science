Title:
Cookie Dough Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BROWNIES:
2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup baking cocoa
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup oil
4 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup walnuts (optional)
MOCK COOKIE DOUGH:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup sugar
2 TBS milk
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup flour
TOPPING:
1 cup or 6 oz chocolate chips
1 TBS shortening
OR
Nutella or any chocolate hazelnut spread
Chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
BROWNIES:
Mix dry ingredients. Mix wet ingredients. Combine dry and wet ingredients and beat 3 minutes. Add nuts. Pour into greased 9x13 pan. Bake 25-30 minutes on 350. Cool completely.
MOCK COOKIE DOUGH:
Cream butter and sugar. Add milk and vanilla. Beat in flour. Spread over cooled brownies. Chill until firm.
TOPPING:
Melt together until smooth. Spread over filling. Sprinkle with nuts and press slightly. If using the chocolate hazelnut spread simply spread over cookie dough. Top with chopped nuts if desired.

Submitter: 
Nonna Birdi
Description: 
A three layer brownie. Bottom layer brownie, middle layer is a mock cookie dough (no raw egg) and topping is melted chocolate or chocolate hazelnut spread. (I have used a boxed brownie mix for the bottom layer when I am being lazy!)
