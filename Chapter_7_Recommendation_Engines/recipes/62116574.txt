Title:
Pepper Jack Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups tube pasta
1/4  cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped red pepper
1/2 tsp minced garlic
1 tbs vegetable oil
1 (10 3/4 oz) can condensed Southwest style pepper jack soup, undiluted
1 (9 oz) pakcage ready-to-use southwestern chicken strips
3/4 cup water
1 (15 oz) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup shredded monterey jack cheese
chopped parsley, optional

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, saute the onion, red pepper, and garlic in oil until tender. Stir in the soup, chicken, and water. Bring to a biol. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 8 minutes. Stir in the beans; heat through. Drain pasta and place in a serving bowl; top with chicen mixture. Sprinkle with cheese and parsley.
