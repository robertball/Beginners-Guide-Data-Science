Title:
Shrimp Aspic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bottle Heinz chili sauce
1 pkg. lemon Jell-o
1 c. hot water
2 cans baby shrimp, drained
1 tsp. horseradish

Directions:
In a round glass baking dish, stir the hot water into the lemon Jell-o until dissolved.
Stir in the remaining ingredients until well combined.
Chill until set, about two hours.
Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
thislittlepiggy
Description: 
May sound a little strange, but is delicious and pretty! Serve as a cocktail party appetizer or take to your next pitch-in.
