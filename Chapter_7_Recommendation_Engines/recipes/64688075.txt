Title:
Jamaican Spiced Chicken Thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup minced red onion
1 T sugar
1 T finely chopped seeded jalapeno pepper
2 t cider vinegar
2 t low sodium soy sauce
1/2 t salt
1/2 t allspice
1/2 t thyme
1/2 t black pepper
1/4 t red pepper
8 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
Cooking spray

Directions:
Combine 1st ten ingredients in a large bowl; add chicken, tossing to coat. Heat grill pan over med-high heat.
Coat pan with cooking spray. Add chicken to pan; cook 4 min. Turn chicken over; cook 6 min or until done.
Complete the menu with mashed sweet potatoes and a mixed green salad
