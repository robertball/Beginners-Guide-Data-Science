Title:
Caesar Salad with Homemade Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the salad:
4 romaine lettuce hearts, cut into  bite sized pieces
1/4 cup grated parmesan
1 tablespoon olive oil
Black pepper, freshly ground
2 1/2 Tablespoons mayonnaise
2 Tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon worcesteshire sauce
For the croutons:
2 cups white bread, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
4 cloves garlic
1/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
To make croutons, in food processor or blender drop garlic cloves thru feeder lid while machine is running.  Add 1/4 cup olive oil and puree together 30 seconds.  Strain mixture, saving reserved garlic.
In large bowl, toss bread crumbs and add 1 Tablespoon of olive oil mixture.  Add a pinch of salt, toss together and add remaining olive oil mixture, coating bread cubes well.  Toast bread cubes in pan over medium high heat for about 6 minutes, shaking and tossing constantly.  Set aside.
To make dressing, whisk together mayonnaise, lemon juice, worcesteshire, and half of reserved garlic until creamy.
Toss lettuce with 1 Tablespoon of olive oil and freshly ground pepper.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese, mix in salad dressing.  Top with croutons.
