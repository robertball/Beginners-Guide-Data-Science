Title:
White Christmas Jewel Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975937.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups premium white chocolate chips
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup chopped green candied cherries (optional) (NOT Maraschino Cherries) (optional)
1/2 cup chopped red candied cherries (optional) (NOT Maraschino Cherries) (optional)

Directions:
In heavy saucepan, over low heat, melt white chocolate chips with EAGLE BRAND(r), vanilla and salt. Remove from heat; stir in cherries.
Spread into wax paper lined 8- or 9-inch square pan. Chill 2 hours or until firm. Turn fudge onto cutting board; peel off paper and cut into squares.  Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLEBRAND®
Description: 
This fudge recipe is easy enough to fit into anyone's busy holiday schedule. It looks beautiful with red and green candied cherries on a white chocolate backdrop, and it also makes a great gift.
