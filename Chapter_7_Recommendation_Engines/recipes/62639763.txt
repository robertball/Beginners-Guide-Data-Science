Title:
Perfect Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. packed basil leaves
1/4 c. grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan Cheese
2 tbsp. Toasted Pine Nuts or Walnuts
2 tbsp. Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
1 clove Garlic, chopped
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. ground Black Pepper
3 tbsp. defatted Chicken Broth

Directions:
In a food processor, combine basil, cheese, pine nuts, oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
Process with on/off turns until basil is chopped.
With the machine running, slowly pour broth through the feed tube until a smooth paste forms.

Submitter: 
hobbyzu
Description: 
This zesty sauce goes great with pasta, grilled chicken breasts, sauteed fish fillets, steamed veges and lots more.
