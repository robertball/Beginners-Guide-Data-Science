Title:
Crock Pot Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. Hamburger, seasoned to taste
1 onion, finely chopped
1/2 C. bread crumbs
1/2 C. Oatmeal
2 eggs
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
1 pkg. French onion soup mix
BBQ Sauce or Ketchup

Directions:
Butter Crock pot
Mix ingredients in Kitchen Aid or by hand
Pack mixture into crock pot
Cook 1 hr./lb.
Lift meat loaf out of crock pot.
Drip off grease into small saucepan
Mix grease w/ 1 tbs. Flour, boil
Mix w/ BBQ sauce or ketchup
Pour over meatloaf.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This is a simple recipe and very little work.
