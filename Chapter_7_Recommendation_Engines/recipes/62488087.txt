Title:
Smashing Pumpkin Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups chicken stock
1 1/2 tsp. salt
4 cups pumpkin puree
1 tsp. chopped fresh parsley
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 tsp. chopped fresh thyme
1 clove garlic, minced
ground black pepper
cream

Directions:
Heat stock, pumpkin, onion, thyme, garlic and pepper.  Bring to boil, reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes uncovered.  Puree the soup in small batches using a blender.  Return to pan and bring to boil again.  Reduce to low and simmer another 30 minutes, uncovered.  Pour into soup bowls and garnish with cream and herbs.
* I played around with the seasonings and added more thyme, parsley, some ginger and curry powder.
