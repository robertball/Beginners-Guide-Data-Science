Title:
Italion Oyster Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags oyster crackers
2 [0.7 oz] pkgs dry italion salad dressing mix
3/4 cup olive oil
2 tbsp italion seasoning
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Place crackers in a large roster, in a small vowl combine remaining ingredients, pour over crackers.  Toss gently to coat. Bake 30 min stirring twice during baking.  Cool completely and store in airtight container.
