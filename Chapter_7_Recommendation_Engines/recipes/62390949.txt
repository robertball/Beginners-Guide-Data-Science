Title:
Jess's Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
1 1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
2 1/2 tablespoons dill weed
1/4 teaspoons salt
1 1/2 teaspoons celerry salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 large onion, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 cucombers

Directions:
1.Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the macaroni, and cook until tender, about 8 minutes. Rinse under cold water and drain.
2.In a large bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, vinegar. Stir in all other ingredients. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving, but preferably overnight.

A basic macaroni salad with a little more flavor!
