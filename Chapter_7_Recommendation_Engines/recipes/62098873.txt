Title:
The best turkey gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
turkey neck & giblets
3 cans chicken stock
1 cup onion
1 cup celery
white pepper to taste
2 packs of turkey gravy mix
1/4 cup wondra flour
1/2 cup milk (optional)
pan drippings

Directions:
Add neck and giblets to stock pot
add onion & celery
ground pepper
turkey gravy packs
cover with chicken stock
bring to a boil-cover and simmer for 1/2 hour
remove liver and set aside
simmer another 2 1/2 hours uncovered
keep stock around 3 cups. add more chicken stock if needed
stain stock and set giblets aside with liver
Pour pan drippings from turkey pan into fat separater
Add pan drippings to stock pot and add flour
whisk until desired thickness and slowly add strained gravy to it.
Whisk until smooth and cook over med heat until it simmers. Add more pan drippings for extra taste
add pepper to taste
add chopped giblets (optional)
add milk if desired for extra smoothness.
Simmer and stir until desired thickness
