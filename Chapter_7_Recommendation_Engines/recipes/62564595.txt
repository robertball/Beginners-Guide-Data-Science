Title:
Chocolate Covered Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
2 cups powdered sugar
2 cups crunchy peanut butter
1 lb chocolate bark

Directions:
Mix butter, powdered sugar and peanut butter.
Form into bite-sized balls.
Dip into melted chocolate and place on wax paper-lined cookie sheets.
Cool in freezer for 15 minutes to set.

Submitter: 
Becca Owens
Description: 
Deliciously sweetened crunchy peanut butter balls dipped in chocolate. YUMMY! This recipe was passed on to me from my mother, and we usually make these for the holidays.
