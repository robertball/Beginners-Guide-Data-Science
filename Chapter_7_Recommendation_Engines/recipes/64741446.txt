Title:
Chummus, "Chickweed Hummus" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6583373.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 oz) can chickpeas
4 cloves garlic, with peel on it
1½ cups chickweed leaves and stems (chopped)
½ cup tahini
1 C lemon juice
1/4 cup of olive oil
½ tsp cumin
1/4 tsp black paper

Directions:
Place everything except the chickweed in a food processor and puree.
Add the chickweed and pulse until it is minced and well combined.
Taste to adjust for salt, pepper, cumin and lemon juice.
Serves: 8

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Spring is in the air and it tastes great too when adding chickweed to traditional hummus!
Packed with vitamins and minerals.
The bright green color shouts spring.
Enjoy
