Title:
Taco Soup VIII Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
4 onion; diced
1c taco seasoning mix
1/2 # 10  can crushed tomatoes
6c whole kernel corn
1/2 # 10  can kidney beans, drained
8 cups mild salsa
8 cups beef stock
6 cups corn tortilla chips
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped green onion

Directions:
In a pan over medium heat, cook beef and onion until beef is browned; drain.
Add salsa, beef stock, crushed tomatoes, taco seasoning, corn, and beans.
Cook on Low 1 hour and let flavors blend
To serve, garnish with tortilla chips, cheese, and green onions.
