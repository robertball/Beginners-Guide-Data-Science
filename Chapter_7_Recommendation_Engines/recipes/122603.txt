Title:
Oatmeal Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/800803.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (no substitutes), softened
3/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups quick-cooking oats
3/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Gradually add the flour, oats and salt. Press into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Prick with a fork if desired. Bake at 325 degrees F for 30-35 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool for 10 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Marion  Hall
Description: 
These rich buttery cookies from Marion Hall are great with a steaming cup of coffee. 'My family enjoys these chewy treats very much,' remarks the Etobicoke, Ontario cook.
