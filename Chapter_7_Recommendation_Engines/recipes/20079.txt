Title:
Easy Southwestern Salad Dressing Recipe

Ingredients:
1 cup Ranch-style salad dressing
1 cup honey barbecue sauce

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together the ranch dressing and barbecue sauce.  Refrigerate until serving.
