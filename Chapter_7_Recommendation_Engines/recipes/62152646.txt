Title:
Orzo with Spinach, Bacon and Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 pounds of bacon
1 cup olive oil
3 cups minced garlic
2 pounds diced onion
33 pounds fresh spinach leaves
10 pounds diced tomato
30 pounds chicken breast -cut into bite-size pieces
2 gallons half and half
1 gallon milk
10 pounds sour cream
18 pounds uncooked orzo pasta
10 pounds feta cheese
seasoning salt to taste
grated Parmesan cheese for topping

Directions:
Saute` bacon until crispy. Drain on paper towels and crumble. Return 2 cups of bacon grease to the pan, and add 1 cup olive oil. Saute` garlic and onion in the bacon grease and olive oil for 1 minute while stirring. Add spinach, tomato and chicken. Stir until spinach starts to wilt. Add half and half, milk, sour cream crumbled bacon and seasoning salt to taste. Stir together and simmer on low for 15 to 20 minutes.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add orzo pasta, cook for 8 to 10 minutes, until al dente, and drain. Add to the spinach chicken mixture. Stir in Parmesan Cheese. Let it stand for 5 minutes. Serve topped with crumbled feta cheese

Submitter: 
Karen Lanier-Shelton
Description: 
A creamy concoction of Orzo, tender chicken pieces, spinach and BACON BACON BACON!!
