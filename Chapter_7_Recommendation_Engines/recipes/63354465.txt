Title:
Cake Mix Pumpkin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow cake mix
1 16-0z can Libby's solid pack pumpkin
3 eggs OR 3/4 c liquid egg substitute
2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp cloves
OR
use pumpkin pie spice mix
Glaze:
1/2 c powdered sugar
1/4 tsp orange extract
1 tsp skim milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.  In a large mixing bowl, beat cake mix with pumpkin, egggs, and spices for 3 minutes.  Pour batter into a 15 x 8 jelly roll pan sprayed with Pam.  Bake for 23 minutes.
Cool bars before glazing.  Glaze only the portion of the bars that will be used, as glazed bars do not keep well.  Unglazed bars may be frozen.

A moist cake-like pumpkin bar from a cake mix.
