Title:
Cowgirl Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/840659.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
2 Tablespoons oil
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
Â¼ cup celery, chopped
Dash garlic powder
2 T. Chili powder
1 15 ounce can diced tomatoes
3 cups water
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
16 ounce pkg frozen corn
1 15 ounce can Ranch Style Beans (chili beans), undrained
6 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed

Directions:
Brown  beef and onion in oil, breaking up beef.
Add remaining ingredients, cover and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for about an hour, or until potatoes are tender.
