Title:
Kid Friendly Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med onion, chopped
3 tbsp. butter or margarine
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup chicken broth
1 small can green chillies, chopped
1 lbs boneless skinless chicken
1 package flour tortillas
1 lb cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
Cook chicken and onion in butter over medium heat until chicken juices run clear.  In a bowl, combine soups, broth, and chillies.  Add chicken and onion mixture.  Place about 1/4 c mixture in the center of a tortilla and roll. (easiest to do it in a 9*13 pan).  Repeat until tortillas are used up, then pour the rest of the soup mixture on the top of the enchiladas.  Sprinkle the grated cheese on top.  Bake at 325 degrees for 30 to 45 mins, or until cheese is melted.  Enjoy!
