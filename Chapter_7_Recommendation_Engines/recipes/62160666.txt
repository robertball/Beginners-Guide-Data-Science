Title:
Curried Goat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb.goat mutton
1 int stock
1/2 tsp.allspice(pimento)
1 oz. drippings
2 large onions sliced
1 tbsp.pure malt vinegar
1 large tsp.curry powder
1 desert dessicated coconut
1small scotch bonnet pepper

Directions:
1. Brown meat in smoking hot drippings.
2. Remove and add onions and when clear but not brown add curry powder,allspice and coconut,stirring a moment or two.
3. Add stock,pepper,vinegar and salt to taste.Return meat to pot ans simmer slowly for two hour.serve in a border of fluffy boiled or fried rice.
NB:THE ADDITION OF CARROTS AND POTATOES DURING THE FINAL 20 MINUTES MAKES GIVES IT A uniqueness,good with your favourite main dish and salad

Submitter: 
sunflowerBirmingham
Description: 
This is another traditional Jamaican dish served especially during our festival period over the summer,its one of my family's' favorite meal.
