Title:
Cream cheese Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter + 2 tabl.
8 oz cream cheese
2 portabello mushrooms thinly sliced
1 1/2 c. milk
6-8 oz shredded parmesan
1 garlic clove crushed
1 tabl. minced fresh basil
white pepper

Directions:
Melt together butter, and cream cheese.
While waiting for that to melt, saute your mushrooms in the extra 2 tabl. butter until tender.
Add milk, and parmesan cheese to the cream cheese mixture until melted. Toss in crushed garlic, basil, and white pepper to taste, and simmer 5 min. Remove garlic before serving.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Mommy bakes
Description: 
This is my spin on alfredo sauce. We love cream cheese, mushrooms and garlic. So I decided to try different ways of creating this tasty dish! Serve with fettuchini, and sliced crusty bread for mopping!
