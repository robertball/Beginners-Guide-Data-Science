Title:
FROGHOPPER's Candied Ginger Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/419672.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12 oz bag of baby carrots
1 cup water
3 TBLS butter
2 TBLS brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Bring water and carrots to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer until carrots are soft, but not mushy, about 10 minutes. Drain carrots.
Add butter, cover until melted. Then add brown sugar, ginger, and salt, stir.
Bring to boil over medium high heat for 2-3 minutes.
Stir and serve.

Submitter: 
FROGHOPPER
Description: 
Ginger adds a very nice layer of flavor and perks up basic candied carrots for a side that the whole family will love!
