Title:
NYS SPIEDIE SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TEASPOON DRIED MINCED ONION
1 TEASPOON GARLIC POWDER
1 TEASPOON SALT
1 TABLESPOON TABASCO
1 TABLESPOON MSG (McCORMICK FLAVOR ENHANCER)
1 TABLESPOON GROUND BLACK PEPPER
1 TABLESPOON ITALIAN SPICES (ITALIAN SEASONING)
4 Oz. WATER
7 Oz. VINEGAR
4 Oz. OLIVE OIL
6LBS MEAT(CHICKEN, BEEF, LAMB or PORK)

Directions:
COMBINE INGREDIENTS INTO 1 PINT CONTAINER THE SAUCE KEEPS WELL IN A SALAD DRESSING BOTTLE TYPICAL SPIEDIE MEATS ARE CHICKEN,BEEF,LAMB AND TURKEY. CHICKEN BREAST IS THE BEST. SPIEDIE CHICKEN 6 LBS ADD 1 PINT SPIEDIE SAUCE TO 3 QUART CONTAINER. MARINATE 6 LBS. BONELESS CHICKEN (THIGHS OR BREAST) PIECES (ABOUT 1/2 a GOLF BALL SIZE). LET MEAT MARINADE FOR 3 DAYS IN FRIDGE.
COOK ON A SPIEDIE STICK ON THE GRILL & SERVE ON BUTTERED ITALIAN BREAD.
