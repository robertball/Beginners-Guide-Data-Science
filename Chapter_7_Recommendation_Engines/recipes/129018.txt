Title:
Twisted Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/931854.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups apple cider
1/2 cup spiced rum
1/4 cup cinnamon schnapps
4 cubes  ice cubes

Directions:
Stir the cider, rum, and cinnamon schnapps together in a pitcher. Place 2 ice cubes in each of two 12-ounce glasses. Pour half of the cider mixture into each glass and serve.

Submitter: 
Ed Grivner
Description: 
I got bored with apple cider and decided to 'spice' it up with a few of my favorite liquors. It's sure to warm you up on a cold night!
