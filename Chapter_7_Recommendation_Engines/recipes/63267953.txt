Title:
Grandma Carstens' No Roll Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/857334.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c shortening
1/2 c salad oil
1/2 c granulated white sugar
1/2 c powdered sugar
1-1/2 tsp vanilla
2-1/4 c flour

Directions:
Cream shortening, oil, white sugar and powdered sugar until creamy and fluffy. (This is the secret).
Dip out on cook sheet and press tops with sugar coated water class bottom, or fork.  (If using fork, sprinkle small amount of granulated sugar on tops afterwards).
Bake 350 degrees (F) for 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Grandma loved to serve these up with a warm cup of tea in her good china Friendship Tea Cups - made a lasting impression and connection with her grand children and great grand children.
