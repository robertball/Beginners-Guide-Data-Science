Title:
Indoor Smore's Delights Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. box golden graham cereal
1 10.5 oz. bag mini marshmallows
1 11.5 oz. bag milk chocolate chips
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1 tsp vanilla or butternut vanilla
5 tbsp butter or margarine

Directions:
Spray a 13 X 9 pan with non-stick spray.
Add cereal to pan.
In a large microwavable bowl combine 5 cups of the marshmallows, chocolate chips, corn syrup, and butter.
Place in a microwave and cook on high setting for 3 minutes, stopping every minute to stir.
Then add the vanilla to the mixture and blend.
Pour this over the cereal, and toss until well coated.
Sprinkle the reserved cup of marshmallows over this, and stir in.
Using wax paper, press the mixture evenly into the pan.
Cover loosly and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Here's a delicious blend of graham crackers, marshallows, and chocolate just like we use to make around a camp fire. These are blended, and cut into bars. Kids everywhere love them.
