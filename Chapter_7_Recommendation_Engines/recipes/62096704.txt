Title:
Angie's Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz package cream cheese, softened
1-1/2 cups sour cream
1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper
1-1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
1-1/2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 cup salsa
8 ozs. Colby/Monterey Jack cheese, shredded

Directions:
Mix together cream cheese and sour cream until well blended.
Mix in red pepper, cumin, chili powder, garlic powder and salsa.  Mix well.
Spread mixture into casserole dish and top with cheese.
Refrigerate for 2 hours or overnight before serving.
Serve with Nacho Cheese Doritos or Tortilla chips.
Shredded Cheddar Cheese can be substituted for Colby/Monterey Jack.

Submitter: 
Angie Bowers
Description: 
This is a wonderful dip, a little different than most out there.  This goes over well with everyone and is often requested at parties.
