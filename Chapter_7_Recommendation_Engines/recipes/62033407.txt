Title:
Competition BBQ Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slabs pork loin back ribs
First Stage Dry Rub:
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
1/2 cup paprika
1/3 cup garlic salt
2 T. onion salt
2 T. chili powder
1 T. cayenne pepper
1 T. black pepper
1 1/2 t. dried oregano
1 1/2 t. white pepper
1 t. cumin
Second Stage:
1/2 cup apple juice per slab
1/2 cup grape juice per slab
Third Stage:
3/4 cup First Stage Rub
1/4 cup brown sugar
Finishing Glaze:
1 1/2 cup red sauce
1/2 cup honey

Directions:
Raw Preparation:
Place slab of ribs bone side down on table.  With a dry paper towel, grasp the edge of the thin membrane and pull.  The entire membrane should separate from the rib.
Preheat oven to 250 degrees.  Combine First Stage Rub and mix well.  Generously apply rub onto the front and back sides of ribs.  Gently pat to ensure that rub will adhere.  Place ribs meat side up on a broiler pan and bake 2 1/2 hours.
Remove ribs from oven.  Place each rib meat side down on its own doubled aluminum foil square.  Foil should be large enough to completely wrap rib.  Mix the Second Stage juices.  Pur 1 cup of liquid over each rib.  At the same time, wrap and seal each rib tightly.  Return to the oven for 1 hour.
Remove wrapped ribs from oven.  Remove from foil and apply medium coat of the Third Stage rub to the meat side of ribs.  Place uncovered in the oven or grill meat side up for 30 minutes.
Remove ribs from oven or grill and increase oven temperature to 350 degrees.  Brus finishing glaze on both sides of ribs.  Place ribs in oven or on grill for 10 minutes or until sauce carmelizes.  ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Joanie
Description: 
After years of searching for the ultimate BBQ rib recipe, I've finally found it!  This was featured on FoodTV about a year ago and has won several rib competition awards.  You'll have to devote a good part of your day to make these ribs just right but the time is WELL worth it!  Bake part way in the oven, then finish on the grill.
