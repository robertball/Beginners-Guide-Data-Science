Title:
Zucchini Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
3 eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup applesauce
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
3 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 cups grated zucchini
2 bananas (1 cup)
1 cup chopped macadamia nuts, chopped

Directions:
Grease and flour two 8 x 4 inch pans.  Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Sift white and wheat flours, salt, baking powder, soda, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
Beat eggs, oil, applesauce, vanilla, white and brown sugar and bananas together in a large bowl.  Add sifted ingredients to the creamed mixture, and beat well.  Stir in zucchini and nuts until well combined.  Pour batter into prepared pans.
Bake for 40 to 60 minutes, or until tester inserted in the center comes out clean.  Cool in pan on rack for 20 minutes.  Remove bread from pan, and completely cool.

Submitter: 
Julie Holden
Description: 
Very god and extremely moist.  Bread will freeze well, and keep in refrigerator for weeks.Modified from: Mom's Zucchini Bread
