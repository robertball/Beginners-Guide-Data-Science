Title:
Red Sauce with Pork Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbls olive oil
Baby back ribs
1 head of garlic, cloves peeled
2 large cans of crushed tomatoes
2 large cans of tomato sauce
Basil, fresh or dried
salt
crushed black pepper
crushed red pepper
Romano cheese, freshly grated
2 Lbs. Linguini, cooked al dente

Directions:
Brown ribs in the oil in a large pot.  Add whole garlic cloves and red pepper and slowly saute w/ the meat.  Remove the cloves with a spoon and put into a blender.  Add a cup or so of crushed tomatoes and fresh basil and parsley.  Blend until smooth.  Add to pot.  Pour contents of sauce and tomato cans into pot.  Add dried basil, salt, black pepper, and 1 cup of Romano cheese.  Simmer for two to four hours (or more; up to eight hours).  Remove ribs and put into a serving dish w/ tongs to serve.  Spoon sauce over pasta and serve with additional cheese.

Submitter: 
Gina Guerra
Description: 
The ribs flavor the sauce w/ a savory pork flavor and the meat is so tender it falls off the bones.Modified from: Red Sauce w/ Ribs
