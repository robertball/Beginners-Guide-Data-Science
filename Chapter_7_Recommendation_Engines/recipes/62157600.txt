Title:
Chocolate "Rolo" Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1 c. firmly packed brown sugar
1 c. softened margarine
2 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
2 1/2 c. all purpose flour
3/4 c. cocoa powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 c. chopped pecans
1 TBsp. sugar
48 "ROLOS" (chocolates with the caramel centers)

Directions:
Combine sugars and margarine, beat til light and fluffy.
Add vanilla and eggs, Blend well.  In a separate bowl, combine flour, cocoa and baking soda. Mix well.
Add to sugar mixture.  mix well.
Stir in 1/2 c. of the pecans.
Cover and refridgerate 30 minutes for easier handling.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
For each cookie, shape about 1 TBsp. of dough around the Rolo, covering completely.
In a small bowl, combine remaining nuts and 1 TBsp. sugar.
Press one side of each ball into nut/sugar mixture.
Place nut side UP, 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake for 7-10 minutes or until set and slightly cracked.
Cool 2 minutes.
Remove from cookie sheet
Cool completely, about 15 minutes
