Title:
Berry Yogurt Dessert Cup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup(s) berries, frozen
1 1/2 cup(s) yogurt, nonfat, vanilla
1 tablespoon coconut, shredded, unsweetened
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
2 tablespoon nuts, walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Thaw berries and reserve juices.
Combine yogurt, coconut and vanilla. Divide mixture between two bowls or stemmed glasses.
Just Before serving, top with nuts and reserved juices.
