Title:
Spicy eggplant puttanesca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T. oil
1 medium eggplant, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes
1 c. sliced red onion
1 c. chopped bell pepper
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 c. chopped kalamata olives
1 (28-oz) can diced tomatoes
4 T. capers, rinsed and drained
1 T. oregano
2 tsp. chili flakes
1 12-oz bag frozen cheese tortellini
1 c. shredded parmesan, optional
1 c. chopped Italian parsley

Directions:
Bring large pot of water to a  boil.  Meanwhile heat oil and saute eggplant, onion and bell pepper  about 10 minutes or till eggplant is soft.  Add garlic, saute 2 more minutes.   Add olives, tomatoes and capers, and simmer 5-10 minutes.  Stir in oregano and chili flakes.  Simmer while pasta cooks.
Cook tortellini, drain, return to pot, and stir in sauce.  Top with cheese and parsley.
