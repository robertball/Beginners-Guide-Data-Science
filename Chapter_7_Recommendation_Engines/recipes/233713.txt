Title:
Peach Salad with Raspberry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2399893.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package fresh spinach
1/2 cup chopped almonds
1/3 cup sliced red onion
1/4 cup grated Asiago cheese
1/2 cup raspberry vinaigrette dressing
salt and ground black pepper to taste
2 fresh peaches, sliced

Directions:
Combine spinach, almonds, onion, and Asiago cheese in a large bowl. Drizzle dressing over salad and season with salt and pepper. Add peaches and toss.

Submitter: 
Elaine Sammeroff
Description: 
Real light and refreshing either as starter or side salad. Easily doubles. I have used Parmesan instead of Asiago cheese. Nuts can be left out. I have also used pecans.
