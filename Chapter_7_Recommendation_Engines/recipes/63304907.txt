Title:
Sweet and Sour Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head bib or leaf letuce
1/2 head romaine lettuce
1 head iceberg lettuce
1 lb. fresh mushrooms
1 can peas, drained
4 slices bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled
4 oz feta, crumbled (optional)
__________________________________________
DRESSING:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup vinegar
1/4 cup sugar
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp tobasco (more if you wish)

Directions:
Mix dressing ingredients in food processor or whisk.  Pour over salad ingredients and toss until well coated.
