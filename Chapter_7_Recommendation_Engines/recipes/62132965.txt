Title:
Cucumber Zucchini Borscht Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, peeled, seeded, grated
1 zucchini, grated
10 radishes, grated
3 celery ribs, including leaves, chopped
4 green onions, chopped
1/4 red pepper, chopped, if desired
1/4 green pepper,chopped, if desired
12 ice cubes
2 cups half & half
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup water
3 hard cooked eggs, peeled, chopped

Directions:
Mix cucumbers, zucchini, radishes, celery, onion and peppers, if desired in a large bowl.
Put ice cubes on veggies, let sit at least 1 hour.
Remove ice cubes, drain liquid.
Add all other ingredients, and salt and pepper, if desired.
