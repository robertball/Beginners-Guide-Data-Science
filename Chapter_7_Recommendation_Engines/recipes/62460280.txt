Title:
dill pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1-qt. jars with tight sealing lids
4 c. water
3/4 c. vinegar
4 T. kosher rock salt
12 cloves garlic
4 dill heads or 20 sprigs dill weed
4 red chile pequin pepper (optional)
cucumbers (about a dozen)

Directions:
Mix water, vinegar, salt together until dissolved.  Pour 1 cup of solution into each jar.  In each jar place 3 cloves garlic, 1 dill head or 5 sprigs, and one pepper.  Pack each jar with young cukes.
Seal the jars and refrigerate for 3 days or better yet, a week.
