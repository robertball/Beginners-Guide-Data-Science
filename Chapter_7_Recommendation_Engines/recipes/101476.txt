Title:
Lawry's Seasoned Hamburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975888.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef, turkey or pork
1 teaspoon Lawry's® Seasoned Salt
1/2 teaspoon Lawry's® Seasoned Pepper
4 hamburger buns

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients, except buns. Shape into 4 patties. Grill or broil to desired doneness. Serve on buns with your favorite toppings.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
Hamburgers so simple and delicious, you will be tempted to prepare them every night of the week. Use your favorite meat: beef, turkey or pork.
