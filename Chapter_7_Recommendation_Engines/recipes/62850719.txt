Title:
Italian Rice Bowls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cannelini beans drained
1-2 cans diced tomatoes in Italian Seasoning
1/2 tsp Italian Seasoning
1/2 tsp Salt
1 lb ground turkey, browned
Rice
Optional- 1 clove minced garlic
Optional- Italian cheese

Directions:
Set up the smallest serving of rice in the rice cooker according to the manufacturers directions (1 1/2 cups rice in mine) and add all other ingredients except the cheese and turkey.  Follow the manufacturers directions for cooknig the rice.  Brown the turkey while the rice is cooking and set it aside.  When the rice is done mix the turkey in and top bowls with Italian cheese just before serving.
