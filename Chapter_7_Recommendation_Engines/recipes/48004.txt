Title:
Chicken Pita Pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977869.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package TysonÂ® Frozen, Fully Cooked Chicken Breast Strips
3/4 cup ranch-style salad dressing
1 teaspoon dried dill weed
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
1 medium onion, thinly sliced, separated into rings
1 cup shredded lettuce
4 large pita bread loaves, halved

Directions:
Wash hands. Blend salad dressing and dill in a small bowl. Set aside.
Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium. Add mushrooms and onion; cook 3 to 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Add chicken; cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until chicken is hot.

Submitter: 
Tyson Foods

The ever-popular pita bread is one of the oldest recipes known to man. The name comes from the Greek word 'pitta,' which means thin, flat layers.
