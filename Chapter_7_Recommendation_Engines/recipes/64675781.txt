Title:
Lemon Chicken with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken cutlets, pounded to 1/4" thickness
3 tablespoons flour
1 teaspoon each  butter and vegetable oil
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 tablespoons each lemon juice and white wine
1 teaspoon chopped parsley
Pepper

Directions:
Pound chicken cutlets between pieces of waxed paper
Dredge in flour, reserving flour
Melt butter and oil in nonstick skillet
Add cutlets and cook over medium heat, turning once
Transfer chicken to plate
In same skillet, combine mushrooms and garlic and saute
Sprinkle with reserved flour and stir quickly to combine
Stir in remaining ingredients until mix thickens; 3-4 minutes
Return chicken to skillet and heat through
