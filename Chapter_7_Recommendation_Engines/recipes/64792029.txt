Title:
Nanner Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tea baking soda
1/4 tea salt
1/4 cup melted butter
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
Dash cinnamon
1 egg, beaten
1 tea vanilla
3-4 overripe bananas
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350.  Grease small loaf pan.
2.  In large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt.
3.  In medium bowl, cream together butter and sugar.  Stir in vanilla, egg and mashed bananas until well blended.
4.  Stir banana mix into flour mix with chocolate chips until just moisten.   Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
5.  Mix 2 TBL of brown sugar with 1 TBL water in small bowl and brush over top of loaf.
6.  Bake in oven for 45 minutes until toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.   Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes and then remove to wire rack.
