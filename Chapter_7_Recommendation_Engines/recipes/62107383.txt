Title:
Layered Pineapple lush cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 prepared angel food cake (store bought or made yourself) I do a store bought prepared one!
1 box (4 serving size instant lemon pudding and pie filling mix
1 box sugar free instant vanilla pudding mix.
1 can (20 ounce) crushed pineapple, undrained
1 cup whipped topping such as cool whip
1/4 cup fresh blueberries (optional)
1/2 cup fresh raspberries (optional)
4 medium to large strawberries, hulled and sliced in half. (optional)

Directions:
Slice prepared angel food cake in 3 equal parts horizontally.
In a medium bowl, mix both packets of pudding mix, the can of crushed pineapple with the juice, and the whipped topping until well blended.
Place one of the cake slices cut side up, on a cake plate, or other flat surface that the cake may be served from.
Place an even layer of the pudding mixture on the slice of cake.
Place another slice of cake over top of the last layer of pudding mixture and repeat with the pudding mixture.
Place the last slice of cake on the top of the last layer and use the remaining bit of pudding mixture on the top.
Place fresh berries on the top of the cake and refrigerate until ready to serve.
