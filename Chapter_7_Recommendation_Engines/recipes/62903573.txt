Title:
Tex-Mex Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 whole wheat sandwich rolls
1lb. ground turkey
2 medium onions, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 large green bell pepper, chopped
1 jalapeno, minced
1 1/4 cup ketchup
1/2 cup whole kernel corn
4 Tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 Tsp. cooking oil
1 Tsp. chili powder
1 Tsp. dried oregano
1 Tsp. ground cumin
Dill pickle slices, optional
Splash of hot pepper sauce, optional

Directions:
In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat.  Add onion, pepper, corn, garlic and jalapeno.  Cook 5 to 10 minutes until onion is tender, stirring occasionally.
Stir in turkey, chili powder, oregano and cumin.  Cook another 5 to 10 minutes until turkey is no longer pink.  Stir in ketchup, Worcestershire sauce and hot pepper sauce (if using); heat through.
Divide mixture among the rolls.  Serve with dill pickle slices, if desired.

Submitter: 
tjduck85
Description: 
This is a one-pan wonder!  A healthy homemade version of a timeless classic!  These sandwiches are made with ground turkey and fresh vegetables for a warm and hearty meal.  I like to add a splash of hot sauce for an extra kick!
