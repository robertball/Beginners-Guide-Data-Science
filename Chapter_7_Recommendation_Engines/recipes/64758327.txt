Title:
Peanut Butter Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup peanut butter
4 Tb. soft butter
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2/3 cup all purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup peanuts (or peanut butter chips)

Directions:
Lightly spray bottom of 8 inch square pan with cooking spray.
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream peanut butter, butter, sugar & eggs.
Whisk together the flour & salt then add to creamed mixture.
Stir once or twice then add the peanuts (or peanut butter chips).
Mix just until the flour is all incorporated.
Spread into prepared pan.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes.
* Can substitute half the flour for cocoa powder to create a chocolate peanut butter brownie.

Submitter: 
ccofer
Description: 
Not all brownie goodness is chocolate.  And for friends who don't like chocolate at all, this is a great option.
