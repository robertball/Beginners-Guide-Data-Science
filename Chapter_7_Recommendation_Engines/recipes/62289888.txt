Title:
Asiago Chesse Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 slices bread
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
2 teaspoons garlic salt (optional)
2 cups shredded Asiago cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Remove crusts from stale bread slices. Brush bread on both sides with melted butterand oil. Cut bread slices up into small cubes. Sprinkle with garlic salt (if desired). Arrange cubes on an ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 15 minutes or until browned. Sprinkle with asiago cheese bake for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and golden  Let cool. Store croutons in a covered container or plastic bag. Serve in soups or salads.

Submitter: 
coffee house chef
Description: 
Stale bread that is buttered,oiled, seasoned, and baked till crispy and topped with cheese make these inexpensive croutons that everyone will surely enjoy.Modified from: Croutons
