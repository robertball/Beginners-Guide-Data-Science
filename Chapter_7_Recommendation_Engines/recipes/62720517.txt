Title:
Baked Asparagus with Butter and Parmesan Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1kg Asparagus - prepared with woody bits cut off
60g to90g/2 to 3oz (to taste) Unsalted Butter - cut into small bits
60g to 90g/2 to 3oz to taste) Parmesan cheese - grated.
Salt and freshly ground Black or White pepper â€“ to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 220c/440f/gas7;
Parboil asparagus in big pan 5 mins or so until softened but not mushy;
Drain and cool in cold water to stop the cooking process;
Drain again:
Butter a baking dish, layer asparagus, covering each layer with butter and cheese;
Cook for 15 to 20 minutes until cheese is browning;
Serve in the dish with masses of crusty bread and a cold dry white wine.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
An absolute delight, spring and summer as a Main, Side or Appetizer. Rich, packed with flavour and a joy to eat with crusty bread and a glass (or two) of dry white wine. Magic.
