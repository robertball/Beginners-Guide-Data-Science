Title:
Aunt Jannie's Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf Italian bread, split lengthwise
1 stick softened butter (1/2 cup)
1 lb pepperjack cheese, shredded
1 cup mayonnaise
3 cloves roasted garlic, mashed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Combine butter and garlic. Spread evenly over inside surface of bread.
In a small bowl, mix mayonaise and pepperjack cheese. Spread over butter.
Place on baking sheet and bake in preheated oven 25-30 minutes until bubbly.
Allow to sit 5-10 minutes before cutting and serving.
