Title:
Jerk Chicken Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs ground chicken
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 small red pepper
1 small red onion
4 green onions
1 egg
1 Tbsp jerk seasoning
1 Tbs minced garlic
1 can of peaches
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Finely chop red pepper and onions in a chopper.  Combine 1/2 of the mixture with all ingredients except the peaches.  Mix well, add 1/4 cup of peach juice, mix andshape into a loaf.  Place in pan, cover with foil, and bake on 350Â° for 50 minutes.  Uncover halfway through.
Add enough peaches to the chopper to equal two whole peaches.  Mix with the rest of the remaining chopper peppers and onions.  Spoon mixture on meatloaf and bake and additional 20 minutes.
