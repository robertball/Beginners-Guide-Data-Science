Title:
Steamed Mussels in Blue Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds mussels, cleaned and debearded
2 tablespoons butter
2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
1/4 cup shallots, minced
7 slices bacon, minced
2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
4 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
3/4 cup dry white wine
nutmeg
ground black pepper to taste
1 french bread, cut into pieces

Directions:
In a medium stock pot, heat butter over medium heat. Add shallots and bacon and saute until shallots are translucent.
Add chives and sautee until soft.
Pour in cream and stir. Then add blue cheese and stir until cheese has melted. Use low heat!
Pour in wine. Then add some nutmeg.
Add mussels. Raise heat to medium/high and steam mussels until shells open, about 5 minutes.
Pour mussels and cooking liquid into a serving bowl, sprinkle with parsley and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Serve immediately with toasted french bread.
