Title:
Hilly's Easy Bake Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package Frozen Ore Ida Southern Style Hash Brown Potatoes
1 Stick of Butter (melted)
1 Can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 16oz Container of Sour Cream
1 Pkg Shredded Cheese (mild Chedder - 2 cups)
1 Cup Chopped Green Onions (optional)
1 Pkg Pepperidge Farm Herb Seasoned Bread Crumbs for Stuffing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease or Spray with cooking spray a 9 x 13 x 2 inch pan and set to the side.  In a  seperate large bowl, pour the melted butter over the partially thawed hash browns and mix with a large spoon.  (DO NOT completely thaw hash browns as they will get mushy)  Add one can of cream of mushroom soup and one can of cream of chicken soup and mix, then add the sour cream and cheese and optional green onions and mix. Pour mixture into greased pan and bake in the oven for 1 hour.  10 minutes before the you take them out of the oven pour the seasoned bread crumbs evenly over the potatoes and let bake until time is up. (Do not put them on when you first place them in the oven or they will just burn and they basically ruin the taste)
Let the pan set for about 10 minutes before serving.
