Title:
Lemony Green Beans with Walnuts and Thyme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can green beans
1 1/2 tsp olive oil
1/4 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
2 tsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp thyme
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix oil, lemon juice, thyme, salt & pepper in medium bowl, while heating green beans.
Drain beans and toss with lemon mixture. Top with nuts. Serve.
