Title:
Tomato Basil Egg Salad Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/694605.jpg
Ingredients:
8 hard-boiled eggs (sliced)
1/4 cup mayo
1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/8 tsp salt
ground pepper
1 or 2 fresh tomatoes
4 pieces of lettuce
10 basil leaves (julienned)
4 toasted English muffins

Directions:
Hard boil eggs. Peel and slice. Place in a large bowl.
Add mayo, mustard, onion powder, salt and pepper.
Fold in julienned basil leaves.
Place sliced tomatoes and lettuce on top of toasted muffins.
Serve egg salad on top of muffins.

Submitter: 
Mei
Description: 
A great sandwich to make when you have a garden of fresh basil and tomatoes. It is very easy to make and very yummy. I would recommend this as an open faced sandwich for those who are watching their carbs.
