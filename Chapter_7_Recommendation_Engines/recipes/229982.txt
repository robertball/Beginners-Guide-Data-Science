Title:
Wonderful Cranberry Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package strawberry-flavored gelatin (such as Jell-OÂ®)
3/4 cup boiling water
1 quart ground cranberries
2 cups white sugar
2 cups minced apple
2 cups chopped grapes
1 cup drained crushed pineapple
1 cup finely chopped pecans

Directions:
Dissolve Jell-O(R) in boiling water.
Stir cranberries and sugar together in a large bowl. Pour water with dissolved Jell-O(R) over the cranberry mixture. Fold apple, grapes, pineapple, and pecans through the cranberry mixture.
Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate 8 hours to overnight.

Submitter: 
NCOLVER
Description: 
This cranberry sauce/relish is wonderful served with your Thanksgiving or Christmas dinner or with chicken any time of the year. It freezes very well.
