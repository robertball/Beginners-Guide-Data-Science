Title:
Garlic and Rosemary Aioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg yolks
2-3 garlic cloves
salt
1-2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp dijon mustard
3/4 c olive oil
1-2 tsp rosemary minced
water if desired

Directions:
combine egg yolks, garlic, salt with hand blender or Vitamix
add lemon juice and dijon mustard
slowly add olive oil
add water if needed to obtain desired consistency
add rosemary and blend briefly

Easy garlic and rosemary aioli
