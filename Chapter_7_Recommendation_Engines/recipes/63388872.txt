Title:
Low Calorie Root Beer Mocha Frappe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 12 Ounce Can of COLD diet or regular root beer
frozen into an ice cube tray with a bit of water to fill tray
- 2 TBSP name brand instant coffee (decaf or regular, I like folgers)
- 3 TBSP Torani sugar free Chocolate Flavoring Syrup
- 3 TBSP Torani sugar free Classic Caramel Flavoring Syrup
- 1 TBSP Vanilla Extract (artificial is preferred)
- 1 1/4 cup skim milk (or whatever kind you want)
- Optional: Extra artificial sweetener of your choice or sugar

Directions:
Blend everything together in blender and enjoy.
Get another can of chilled root beer frozen in an ice cube try for next time you make it.

Submitter: 
dissident
Description: 
A low calorie frappe that whips up to a nice creamy consistency thanks to the carbonation in the root beer.
