Title:
Blaine Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1017690.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon white sugar
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Stir tomato sauce, tomato paste, vegetable oil, garlic, salt, oregano, sugar, and pepper together in a bowl; refrigerate until chilled completely, at least 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Andrew Blaine
Description: 
Very conservative pizza sauce passed down from my great grandmother.
