Title:
Sushi Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 24.35 oz bottle Rice Vinegar (chikyu-uma brand if possible)
4 c. sugar
3 tsp. salt

Directions:
Mix together and heat on stove top until sugar and salt melt.
This makes alot which you can store in the pantry. Add 1 cup sauce to 5 cups cooked rice. Let sit for awhile to let rice absorb sauce and check taste, add more sauce if needed.

Submitter: 
lovetoeat
Description: 
Easy recipe for sauce to pour on white rice for making sushi.
