Title:
Chicken and Broccoli Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 soda crackers
4 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 cup small curd cottage cheese
1 cup diced cooked chicken
1 (10 oz) package chopped broccoli
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated or shredded
1 1/2 cup milk
4 eggs
1 tsp. basil leaves
1 clove garlic

Directions:
Lightly grease 9x13 baking dish.
Place all crackers on bottom dish and sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
Over crackers, spoon cottage cheese,chicken and broccoli.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
In medium bowl, beat together milk, eggs, basil and garlic.
Pour evenly over baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45-55 minutes.
