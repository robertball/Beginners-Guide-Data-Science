Title:
Spiced Tea (Russian Tea) Recipe

Ingredients:
2 cups tang
1 1/2 cup instant tea
1 envelope lemonade mix
3/4 cup sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamin
1/2 teaspoon cloves

Directions:
1. Put in according to taste (2-4 tablespoons)
2. put in microwave until warm/hot
3. put rest of mix in air tight container
