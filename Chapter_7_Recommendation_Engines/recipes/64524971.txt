Title:
Rice krispie treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c light karo
1c sugar
1c peanut butter
6 c rice Kristine
12oz chocolate chips
6 oz peanut butter chips

Directions:
Put karo and sugar in a large pan over medium heat just until it comes to a boil, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in the peanut butter until blended.  Stir in the rice krispies until well mixed. Press into an 8x8 greased cake pan or foil pan. Melt chocolate and peanut butter chips in a double broiler, stirring constantly, being careful not to burn. Spread over rice krispies. Refrigerate until chocolate is firm.
