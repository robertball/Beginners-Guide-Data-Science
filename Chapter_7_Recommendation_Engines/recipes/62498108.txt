Title:
Better Than Whatever! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6906004.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. flour
1 stick margarine
8 oz. cream cheese
1 c. powdered sugar
small cool whip
1/2 c. crushed pecans
2 pkgs. instant pudding (choc. & vanilla or 2 of same flavor)
1 1/2 c. milk for each package

Directions:
Mix flour, margarine and pecans.
Press in 9x13 pan & bake 15 min. @375 degrees.
Mix cream cheese, powdered sugar and 1 c. cool whip.
Spread over cooled crust.
Mix 2 pkgs instant pudding and milk.
Spread over cream cheese mixture.
Let stand 5 min.
Spread remaining cool whip over pudding & top with crushed pecans.
Refrigerate.
