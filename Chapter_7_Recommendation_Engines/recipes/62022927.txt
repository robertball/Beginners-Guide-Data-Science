Title:
14 Carrot Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 cups all purpose flour
2 cups sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1-1/2 cups oil
4 eggs
8-1/2 oz. can crushed pineapple (DO NOT DRAIN)
2 cups grated carrots, packed
1/2 cup chopped pecans
Frosting:
1/2 cup of butter
8 oz. cream cheese
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 pound box of powdered sugar
1/2 cup chopped pecans, optional

Directions:
Cake:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl.  Pour into a greased 9x13 pan or in two greased 8" pans.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.  Cool and then frost.
Frosting:
Cream the butter, cream cheese and vanilla together.  Then add the powdered sugar and beat until smooth.  Fold in chopped pecans, if desired.

Submitter: 
Denise Voltz Perkins
Description: 
This cake is easy to make and is very moist and delicious.
