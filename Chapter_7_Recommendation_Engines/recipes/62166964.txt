Title:
3rd World Beans for 1st class families Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups dried pinto beans
4 cups cool water
3 cups cool water
11/4 t salt
5 strips bacon, cut in 1/2" strips and slightly browned
bacon drippings

Directions:
Place beans and 4 cups cool water in slow cooker and soak at least 12 hours.
Drain and rinse in cool water.
To the beans add: 3 cups cool water, salt, bacon and drippings.
Cook in slow cooker on low for 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Claire Edwards Grover
Description: 
Pintos are slow cooked all day with bacon until tender. Serve with cornbread to sop up the potlickins'. You won't believe how such a simple recipe can change the way you look at simple foods. My kids request this weekly.
