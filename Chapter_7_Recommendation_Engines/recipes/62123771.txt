Title:
CHICKEN SAUCE PIQUANTE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Chicken, cut into pieces
1/4 cup oil
1 Large onion, chopped
1 Cup celery, chopped
1 cup chopped bell pepper
1 (16-ounce) petit tomatoes
3 tablespoons tomato paste
2 cups chicken broth
1 Teaspoon sugar
4 cloves garlic, minced
salt, black and ayenne pepper
1/2 cup green onions chopped
1/4 cup parsley chopped

Directions:
Brown chicken in oil for about 1/2 hour until tender. Take chicken out of pot and set aside. Add onions, celery, and bell pepper to oil and cook slowly until onions are wilted. Add tomatoes, paste, and broth . Cook over medium heat for about 25 minutes.Add chicken,surgar, and minced garlic. Season to taste with salt, black pepper and cayenne.Cook for 30 minutes. Add parsely and green onions. Serveover rice.

Submitter: 
michellej
Description: 
This is a cajun dish that is to die for! Lean heavy on the cayenne pepper to give the sting (which is the piquante).
