Title:
Frosty Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1   4 serving box of instant chocolate pudding
2/3  C. whole milk
1/3  C. vanilla vodka
1/3  C. Bailey's Irish Cream
8 oz.  Cool Whip

Directions:
Whisk pudding and milk until smooth.  Mix in vodka and Bailey's.  Fold in Cool Whip.  Spoon into disposable plastic shot glasses.  Freeze and serve frozen.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
A frozen treat with a kick!  Perfect for the holidays!
