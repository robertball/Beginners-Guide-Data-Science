Title:
Apple Cider Mimosa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5855852.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chilled apple cider
1 (750 milliliter) bottle chilled Champagne
4 cinnamon stick
4 dried apples

Directions:
Fill champagne flutes 1/4 full with chilled apple cider and top off with Champagne.
Garnish each glass with a cinnamon stick and dried apple slice.

Submitter: 
Sandra Garth
Description: 
This cider and champagne mimosa is perfect to serve for an autumn brunch or just because.
