Title:
Chopped Brussel Sprout Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2106588.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Shallot, minced
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1/4 cup Sunflower Oil
2 TBSP Dijon Mustard
1/2 tsp honey
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground pepper
6 cups brussel sprouts
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/3 cup each of sliced almonds, sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds
2 Gala Apples, cored and sliced thinly

Directions:
Whisk shallots, vinegar, oil, mustard, honey, salt and pepper in small bowl.
Trim ends of Brussel sprouts, cut in half and then into strips. Combine sprouts , cranberries, nuts/seeds and apples. Drizzle with dressing, toss gently
