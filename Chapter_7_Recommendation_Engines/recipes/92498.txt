Title:
Applesauce Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991928.jpg
Ingredients:
canola cooking spray
1/2 cup canola oil
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 egg
1 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 teaspoon allspice
1 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 5x9-inch (2 L) loaf pan with canola cooking spray and dust with flour.
Beat together canola oil, sugar and egg; add applesauce. Add dry ingredients and raisins, stirring until just combined.
Pour into prepared loaf pan. Bake for 35-40 minutes.

Submitter: 
The Canola Info Virtual Kitchen
Description: 
Whole wheat flour, raisins and canola oil add loads of good-for-you great taste to this moist loaf!
