Title:
Jamie's Cranberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/347738.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup almonds, blanched and slivered
1 pound spinach, rinsed and torn into bite-size pieces
1 cup dried cranberries
1 11-oz can of mandarin oranges
2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
1 tablespoon poppy seeds
1/2 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons minced onion
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Toast sesame seeds and almonds on two separate baking pans in a 250F oven for 10 minutes, and let cool.
In a large bowl, combine the spinach with the toasted almonds, mandarin oranges, and cranberries.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the sesame seeds, poppy seeds, sugar, onion, paprika, white wine vinegar, cider vinegar, and vegetable oil. Toss with spinach just before serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
Toast the almonds and sesame seeds in the oven for a quicker dish. Better yet, buy toasted almonds and omit the sesame seeds if they're not on-hand. Mandarin oranges are a must-have addition!Modified from: Jamie's Cranberry Spinach Salad
