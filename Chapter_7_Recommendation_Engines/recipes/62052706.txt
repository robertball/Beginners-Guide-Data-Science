Title:
banana nut cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow cake mix
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup oil
3 bananas, mashed
1 cup pecans
4 eggs

Directions:
mix all ingredients together. Bake in a bundt pan for 50 minutes to 1 hour at 350F.

Submitter: 
CHLOE.ALEXIS
Description: 
Easy and moist cake. Frost with canned cream cheese icing.
