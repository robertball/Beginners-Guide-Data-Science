Title:
Sour Cream Biscuit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups Bisquick Mix
2/3 cup Sugar
2/3 tsp. Baking Power
1 16oz. Sour Cream
2 Eggs
2 block Butter (Melted)

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients 1st, set aside.
Mix wet ingredients.
Add dry ingredients and mix.
Batter will be clumpy.
Use some of the butter to coat the pan.
Spoon batter into pan
Pour remaining butter on top of batter
Bake @ 400' for 13 minutes.
Biscuit will look under cook. Let stand for 15 minutes.
**Baking time may vary depending on you oven.

Simply and easy to make a 9x13 pan of biscuits
