Title:
A+++ Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4-1#  spicy italian sausage
4 T. butter, melted
8 oz. spaghetti
1/2 c. minced fresh parsley (do not use dried flakes)
3 well beaten eggs
1/2 c. freshly grated parmesan cheese (Do not use Kraft)
freshly grated black pepper
Additional cheese for garnish

Directions:
Prep 2 T. butter, parsley, eggs, and cheese and set aside in small dishes. Begin cooking spaghetti. Remove casings from sausage and brown with 2 T of butter.
(Note to chef:  Timing and adding ingredients in proper order is essential in this recipe.  All ingredients must be hot and/or fresh at the time of mixing.) Add spaghetti to meat.  Add butter, parsley, and mix quickly to blend. At once, pour in the eggs and continue lofting and mixing the spaghetti to coat well.  Sprinkle in the 1/2 c. of cheese and add ground pepper to your family's liking.  Mix again.  Serve with additional cheese for garnish.
