Title:
Chicken & Gruyere Turnovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sheets frozen puff pastry
1 1/2 C. shredded chicken
1 1/2 C. grated Gruyere
1/2 C. frozen peas
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Cut frozen pastry in half to form 4 rectangles. Dividing evenly, top half of each rectangle with chicken, cheese and peas. Seal pastries and brush tops with egg. Bake at 400 20-25 minutes or until golden brown. Serve with Dijon mustard.
