Title:
Honey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups flour
1 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon allspice
1 teaspoon nutmeg
4 eggs
1 1/2 cup honey
4 tablespoons butter, melted
1 cup black coffee
1 cup chopped pecans
1 cup golden raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease and flour two 9" loaf pans.  In a large bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda and spices.  In a separate bowl, beat eggs, honey, butter and coffee.  Blend the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients til all is just moistened.  Fold in raisins and nuts.  Pour into prepared pans and bke one hour.  Cool in pans 10 min before turning out onto wire racks.

Description: 
A sweet quick bread with spices, nuts and raisins.  A rich taste that is good with breakfast or a cup of tea.
