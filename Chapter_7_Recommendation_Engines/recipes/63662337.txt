Title:
Green Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Balsamic Dressing
1/2 cup Chopped Walnuts or Pinenuts
1 lb. French Green Beans, cut into 1-1/2 inch pieces
1/2 cup Thinly Sliced Red Onion
1/2 cup Feta Cheese
Freshly ground black pepper and salt to taste
1/3 cup Dried Cranberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Toast nuts for 5-7 minutes. Cool. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add beans and cook for 5-7 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water and drain again. Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl and gently toss together. Add balsamic dressing to serve.
