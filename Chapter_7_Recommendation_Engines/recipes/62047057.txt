Title:
Candied Butternut Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2-2/12 pound butternut squash, halved, seeded, peeled and cut into cubes
3 Tbsp butter melted
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp ground cloves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Place squash in a glass baking dish. Drizzle with butter, toss to coat. Blend sugar, cinnamon, salt, pepper & cloves in a small bowl.  Mix into the squash.  Bake until squash is tender. Stir every 10 to 15 minutes until done, approximately 45 minutes

Submitter: 
PSOCONNOR
Description: 
This is a new favorite that I started making last year. I don't know what it is about this squash but people who normally don't eat squash will love this.
