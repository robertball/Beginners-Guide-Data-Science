Title:
Artichoke Joe's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/706099.jpg
Ingredients:
Bag of artichokes from Trader Joe's OR fresh vegetables of any kind that equal a lb.
1/2 T of olive oil
2 T butter
1 tsp sage
1 tsp rosemary
1 tsp thyme
2 cloves garlic
pinch of salt
pinch of pepper
to go spicy add 1/2 tsp cayenne

Directions:
First strain the artichoke hearts after thawing.
Melt butter and oil
Press garlic and add
Add spices
Throw in artichokes/vegetables and cover for five minutes or until ready

Submitter: 
Mrs. Falvo
Description: 
If you have a Trader Joe's by you then check out their frozen section for artichoke hearts and add this to the mix! This also can be used for other vegetables.
