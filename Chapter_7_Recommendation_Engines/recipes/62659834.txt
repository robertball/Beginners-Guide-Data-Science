Title:
Berries & Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. berries (Rasp, peaches)
2/3 c. sugar
1/4 c. flour
1/4 t. salt
1/2 t. cinnamon
1 c. half & half

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients then add half & half. Put fruit in pie shell and pour mixture over. Bake 400 for 35-45 minutes, until set.
