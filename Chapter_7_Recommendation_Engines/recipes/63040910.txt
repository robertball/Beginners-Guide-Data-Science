Title:
Fix-It-Quick Chicken Caesar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breast halves, cut into 1/4" strips
1 TBL fresh lemon juice
1 pkg (10 oz) Caesar salad mix with dressing and croutons
Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
Lemon Wedges (optional)
Seasoning:
1 TBL olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 tsp crushed thyme

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine seasoning ingredients; add chicken, tossing to coat.
Heat large skillet over medium-high heat until hot; cook and stir chicken, 1/2 at a time, 2 to 3 minutes or until no long pink in center. Remove from heat; drizzle with lemon juice.
In large serving bowl, combine salad mix with dressing and croutons; toss to coat. Top with chicken. Service with Parmesan cheese and lemon, if desired.
