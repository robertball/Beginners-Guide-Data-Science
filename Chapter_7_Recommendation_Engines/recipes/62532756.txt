Title:
death by chocolate pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/518874.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oreo crusts
Layer 1:
1/2 cup of sugar
1/2 cup real butter
1 bag semi-sweet chips
2 eggs
Layer 2:
2 bags of white chips-Hershey or Nestle
4 tablespoons of heavy whipping cream
3 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
Layer 3:
2 bags of milk chocolate chips
2 eggs
1/3 cup heavy whipping cream
Topping:
remainder of quart of heavy whipping cream
1 teaspoon of vanilla
sugar to taste

Directions:
Layer 1:
slowly melt the chips in the microwave, 30 seconds at a time and stir. when chips are completely melted, mix in sugar, butter and eggs. blend with electric mixer until sugar is completely desolved. pour half into each pie crust. put in freezer to set.
repeat for next two layers, returning the pies to freezer after each time.
whip topping ingredients in a chilled glass bowl until firm, top each pie with whipped cream and shaved chocolate bar
