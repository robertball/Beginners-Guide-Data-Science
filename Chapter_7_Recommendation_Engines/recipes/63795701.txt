Title:
Paleo Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1053030.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup boiling water
1 (3") dried, smoked chipotle pepper
1 1/2 tsp. coconut oil
1 cup chopped yellow onion
1 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 cup chopped red bell pepper
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 lb. ground bison or beef
1/2 lb. spicy sausage, pork or turkey
1 T. chili powder
1 T. ground cumin
1 t. dried oregano
1 t. cocoa powder
1 t. Worcestershire sauce
1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
1 1/2 t. Kosher salt
1/2 t ground black pepper

Directions:
Bring 1 cup of water to a boil and add the dried chipotle pepper with the stem removed.  Let set for 10 min. to soften.
In a saucepan over medium heat, melt coconut oil, when melted add onions and peppers and saute until tender. Mince softened chipotle pepper and add along with the chopped garlic.  Saute for about a minute. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
Add chili powder, cumin. oregano, cocoa powder, and Worcestershire sauce and mix well. Add crushed tomatoes, salt and pepper.
Bring to a boil over medium high heat and then turn down heat to simmer uncovered for 10 minutes.
Serve with chopped green onions and sour cream.

Description: 
Paleo (*eating like our pre-agricultural revolution ancestors ate)chili is made with-out beans. In the west, we typically include beans, but I think even non-Paleo eaters will enjoy this deep smoky flavorful chili.
