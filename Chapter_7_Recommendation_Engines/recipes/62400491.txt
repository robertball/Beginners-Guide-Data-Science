Title:
Easy Oven Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup shredded swiss cheese
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons flour
6 slices bacon; fried & crumbled
3 tablespoons diced fresh sweet red pepper
2 tablespoons parsley leaves (no stems)
8 large eggs
1 cup milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Toss together cheeses and flour, spread cheese mixture in bottom of greased 8" baking dish or casserole.
Scatter crumbled bacon, diced red pepper and parsley over cheese.
Beat eggs and milk together, pour egg mixture over ingredients in baking dish.
Bake until puffed and completely cooked: approximately 45 - 50 minutes (DO not over bake as it gets rubbery)
