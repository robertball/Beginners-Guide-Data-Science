Title:
Grain Free Chocolate Walnut Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3073706.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup walnuts
2/3 cup almonds
10-12 pitted dates soaked in warm water 10-20 minutes
1/3 cup cocoa powder
2 tbsp maple syrup
1/4 tsp peppermint extract (*can substitute any extract)

Directions:
Soak dates in a bowl of warm water 10-20 minutes to soften them
If your dates are small, then add 2 extra otherwise 10 is sufficient
Add walnuts & almonds to a food processor and pulse until roughly chopped
Add dates, cocoa powder, maple syrup & mint extract
Blend until a dough mixture is formed (*add a few extra dates if it does not combine)
Scoop one tbsp of dough and roll into balls (*add water to your hands to help them stick in the rolling process)
Store in a container or ziploc bag in the refrigerator for a healthy snack

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Grain Free Chocolate Walnut Truffles made with almond, walnuts, dates & chocolate these decadent truffles melt in your mouth not in your hand!
