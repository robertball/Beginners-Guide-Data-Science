Title:
Church Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
In large bowl mix:
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 pint sour cream
1 onion chopped
2 cups cheese
2 bags shredded hashbrowns

Directions:
spray 13 x 9 pan with pam
place mixture in pan
top with crusted cornflakes or breadcrumbs
optional to drizzle butter on top
bake 350 degrees for 1 hour
