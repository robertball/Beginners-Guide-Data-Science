Title:
KAHULA CARAMEL CUPS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
DOUGH
2-3 OZ CREAM CHEESE
1 CUP MARGERINE
2 CUPS FLOUR
FILLING
14 OZ CARAMELS
1/2 CUP EVAPORATED MILK
1 TBSP KAHULA
FROSTING
1/2 CUP MARGERINE
1/2 CUP CRISCO
1/2 CUP SUGAR
2/3 CUP EVAPORATED MILK
1 TBSP KAHULA

Directions:
MIX THE DOUGH TOGETHER
MAKE INTO SMALL BALLS
PRESS INTO MINI MUFFIN CUPS & PRICK WITH A FORK
BAKE AT 350 FOR 12-15 MINUTES
USING A DOUBLE BOILER MELT THE CARAMELS
ADD THE EVAPORATED MILK & KAHULA STIRRING WELL
FILL THE TART 1/2 WAY
BEAT THE MARGERINE, 1/2 CUP CRISCO & 1/2 OF THE SUGAR UNTIL SMOOTH & FLUFFY
ADD 1 CAN SMALL EVAPORATED MILK & 1 TBSP KAHULA OR MORE TO TASTE
USE A PASTRY BAG AND PIPE THE FROSTING ON TOP OF THE CARAMEL FILLED CUPS
