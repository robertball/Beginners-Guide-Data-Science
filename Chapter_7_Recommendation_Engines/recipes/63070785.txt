Title:
Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887008.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (10.5 ounce) cans cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 teaspoon pepper
4 (14.5 ounce) cans whole green beans, drained
1 (12 ounce) can French fried onions

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 baking dish.  Stir soup, milk, pepper, beans, and half of the onions in a casserole dish.
Bake at 350º for 25 minutes or until hot.
Stir.
Top with remaining onions. Bake for 5 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
This traditional casserole made with cut green beans, cream of mushroom soup, and French fried onions.Modified from: Campbell's® Green Bean Casserole
