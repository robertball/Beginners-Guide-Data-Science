Title:
40-Minute Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/713237.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2 cups flour
1 stick oleo or butter
1/2 cup Crisco
4 T Cocoa
1 cup water
2 eggs
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 tsp soda
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Sift sugar and flour together.
In saucepan, place oleo (or butter), Crisco, cocoa, and water.  Stir and bring to boil.
Pour over flour mixture and stir well.
Add the eggs, buttermilk, soda, and vanilla.  Stir just until combined.
Pour in 9" X 13" oblong cake pan (buttered).
Bake at 400 for 20 to 30 minutes.
For icing:
In sauce pan: 1 stick oleo, 4 T cocoa, 6 T milk, 1 tsp. vanilla.
Bring to boil - remove from heat.
Add 1 box powdered sugar - beat with beaters until spreading consistency.  Add 1 cup nuts.
Pour over hot cake.
