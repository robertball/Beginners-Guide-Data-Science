Title:
Peanut Butter Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter or margarine
1/2 c. sugar
1/2 c. packed brown sugar
1/2 c. creamy peanut butter
1 egg
1 t. vanilla extract
1 c. flour
1/2 c. quick opats
1 t. baking soda
1/4 t. salt
1 c. chocolate chips

Directions:
Cream butter, sugars and peanut butter. Add egg and vanilla and mix well.
Combine dry ingredients and slowly add to creamed mixture.
Spread into a 9x13 pan and sprinkle with chocolate chips.
Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes.
Cool for 10 minutes; drizzle with a mixture of 1/2 c. powdered sugar, 2 T. peanut butter, and 2 T. milk.
