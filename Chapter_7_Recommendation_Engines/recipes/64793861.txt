Title:
Potato Bacon Soup With Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/8170705.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg . Beef Bacon (sliced into thin strips. Pork or turkey bacon may be substituted. For turkey bacon, please use high quality uncured. Do not use Jenny O's, Butterball, etc.)
Maple Sage season (To taste. Any Maple glaze season can be substituted)
1 lb. cooked salad shrimp
2  medium Vidalia onions chopped
2 lbs. red potatoes cubed
1 celery stalk sliced
2 fresh jalapeno peppers diced (seeded)
6 green onions chopped
1 12 oz. pkg. frozen sweet corn (fresh may be substituted)
1 16 oz. pkg. Daisy sour cream
2 cans cream of chicken soup
2 1/2 c. unsweetened Almond milk ( regular milk may be substituted)
1 t. dried Thyme leaves
1 t. black pepper

Directions:
Brown strips of bacon along with Maple Sage season on medium heat.
Add celery, onions and jalapeno peppers and stir constantly for about 5 minutes to add flavor.
Add potatoes and toss well.  Stir every 5 minutes to keep from sticking to the pan. (about 15 minutes).
Add sour cream, soup, milk, corn, Thyme, black pepper. Mix well stirring every 3 minutes for about 15 minutes.
During the last 5 minutes, add thawed shrimp and green onions.
