Title:
Pepperoni Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. box medium size pasta, like penne or mostacielli
1/2 cup pepperoni slices, quartered
1/4 cup Manzanilla olives, sliced
2-3 green onions (scallions), sliced thin
8 oz. shredded Mozzarella or your favorite cheese
2/3 cup mayonnaise
1 tbs. chopped parsley
1 tbs. vinegar
1 tsp. ground mustard
1 tsp. dry Italian seasoning
1 tsp. ground pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
Topping:
4 oz. favorite cheese
paprika

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions on package. Rinse in cold water. Combine all other ingredients in a bowl, blend, toss with the pasta. Sprinkle 4 oz. cheese on top, then paprika for color. Refrigerate.

This is a delicious pasta salad that can be served year round.
