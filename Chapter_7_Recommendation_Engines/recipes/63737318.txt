Title:
Southern Style Collard Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium-size sweet onions, finely chopped
2 smoked ham hocks or turkey necks
4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
3 (32-oz.) containers chicken broth
3 (1-lb.) packages fresh collard greens, washed and trimmed
1/3 cup of vinegar
2 tablespoon sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons salt or to taste
3/4 teaspoon pepper or to taste

Directions:
In a stockpot over medium heat Add onion, ham hocks or turkey necks, garlic, and broth cook for 2 hours if you are using hamhocks and 45 mins if you are using turkey necks. Add the remaining ingredients. Cook on medium low heat 2 hours or to desired degree of tenderness.
