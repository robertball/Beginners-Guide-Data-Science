Title:
Pea and Bacon Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/487356.jpg
Ingredients:
8oz spiral dried pasta of your choice
1 onion (minced)
1 garlic clove (minced)
1/2 cup frozen peas
1 1/2 Cup heavy cream
2 strips of raw bacon chopped into cubes
2 tbsp parsley
2 tbsp parmesan
Olive Oil for cooking

Directions:
Cook the pasta for 10 minutes or until done. Drain and rinse under cold water. Set pasta aside. Heat olive oil in a deep pan. Add onions and garlic and saute for a couple of minutes.  Add the bacon and saute until crisp.  Add the heavy cream and reduce by half.
Add the peas and continue to simmer for a couple minutes. Add the pasta and season with salt and pepper to taste.  Heat until dish is at a comfortable serving temperature.  Add grated parmasean and serve.

This is a wonderfully quick and tasty pasta dish that will soon become one of your family's favorite dishes!
