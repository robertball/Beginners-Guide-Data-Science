Title:
Spicy Avocado Crema Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1008737.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados
1 tablespoon lime juice
2 tablespoons Herdez Chipotle Cooking Sauce
2 cups plain Greek yogurt
1 tablespoon fresh cilantro, finely chopped

Directions:
Using a fork, mash the avocados in a bowl.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Use to top fish or chicken, or to garnish tacos.  Especially good on Roasted Salmon Tacos!

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
Healthier than sour cream, this Greek-yogurt based dish is perfect to lighten up and add spice at the same time to all your Mexican dishes!
