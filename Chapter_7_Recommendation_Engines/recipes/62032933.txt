Title:
Greek Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag shreded cabbage - angel hair cabbage or shred your own
4 ounces crumbled feta cheese
1/2 bottle Ken's Greek Salad Dressing

Directions:
Mix add cheese to cabbage, pour on salad dreessing and gently fold the mixture.  Allow to marinate at least 30 minutes prior to serving.  Overnight even better

Submitter: 
MLT
Description: 
Absolutely a favorite!  My husband hates slaw but loves this.  Easy to make if using mixes or you can easily adapt with your own homemade salad dressing. A must try!
