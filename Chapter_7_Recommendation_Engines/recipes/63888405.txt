Title:
Chris's Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs chicken wings separated, tips discarded
2 cups dark brown sugar
1 cup soy sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp ground ginger

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275 degrees F.
In a bowl, mix brown sugar, soy sauce, garlic, ginger.
Place chicken wings in a single layer in a 10 " x 14" pan.
Pour sauce over wings.
Bake in preheated oven until chicken is cooked, approx. 2.5 hours.
Remove pan from oven every 1/2 hour to turn wings during cooking.

Lisa Proctor

Tasty gooey fall off the bone goodness!
