Title:
Marinated Grilled Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/3 c. olive oil
1/8 c. tomato paste concentrate
4 T red wine vinegar
2 T chopped fresh basil, or 5-6 tsp dried
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp Sriracha sauce
1-2 pounds shrimp, peeled and deveined
skewers

Directions:
Stir together garlic, olive oil, tomato sauce, and red wine vinegar. Season with basil, salt, and cayenne pepper. Add shrimp to the bowl, and stir until evenly coated. Cover, and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour, stirring once or twice.
Preheat grill for medium heat. Thread shrimp onto skewers, piercing once near the tail and once near the head. Discard marinade.
Lightly oil grill grate. Cook shrimp on preheated grill for 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until opaque.
