Title:
Spicy Chicken Fettucine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Tbl. Medium hot chili powder
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1/2 tsp. dried marjoram
1/2 Tbl. onion powder
1/2 Tbl. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp. ground black pepper
1/2 Tbl. white pepper
3/4 tsp. salt (or to taste)
3/4 - 1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breast
1/4 cup olive oil
2 Tbl. fresh minced garlic
2 Tbl. butter
1/4 cup flour
2 cups half & half
3/4 lbs. fettucine or linguine
fresh shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cut chicken into 1/2 inch pieces.  Mix together in a medium bowl all the spices, chili powder through salt.  Add the diced chicken and stir to coat throughly.
Heat large pot of water to boiling and add pasta, cook according to package directions.  You can start the alfredo while it's cooking. When done, drain, but do not rinse.
Heat olive oil on medium high in a large deep skillet. Add chicken and saute until the chicken starts to brown on the outside and cook for about 3 minutes.  Add the garlic and cook another 1-2 minutes.  Add butter and heat until melted.  Stir in flour and cook for another minute.  Stir in the half & half and heat until it starts to thicken.  Add the fettucine and stir until throughly mixed.  Divide onto plates and top with shredded parmesean

Submitter: 
Debi Blair McGinness
Description: 
I've been making this for years. The spices give it just the right amount of heat. It's a family favorite.  Just serve with a side salad and some french bread.
