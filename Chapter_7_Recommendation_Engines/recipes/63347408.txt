Title:
Slow Cooker Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound beef for stew
1/4 cup flour
1 1/2 cup chicken stock
3 medium potatoes (chopped)
3 large carrots (chopped)
2 celery stalks (chopped)
5 sprigs of parsley (minced)
1 large yellow onion (chopped)
3 cloves of garlic (depending on how much garlic you like) (minced)
1/2 jalepeno (minced)
1 tsp salt
2 tsp fresh ground black pepper

Directions:
1. Mix flour and salt and pepper together
2. Toss beef in flour mixture in a medium bowl
3. In a large bowl, mix the carrots, celery, onion, parsley, garlic, jalepeno, and potatoes
4. Place everything into the crock-pot
5. Add chicken stock
6. Turn the crock-pot on low and let cook for 6-8 hours until meat and vegetables are cooked through, stirring occasionally if you are impatient and curious like I am.
