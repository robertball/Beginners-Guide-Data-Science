Title:
Vitamix: Green Apple Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces (180ml) apple juice concentrate, thawed
1 banana, peeled
1 1/2 cups packed fresh spinach
4 cups ice cubes

Directions:
1. Place all ingredients into the Vitamix container in the order listed and secure lid
2. Select Variable 1
3. Turn machine on and slowly increase speed to variable 10, then to High.  Use the tamper to press the ingredients into the blades
4. In about 40 seconds, the sound of the motor will change and four mounds should form
5. Stop machine, Do not over mix or melting will occur.  Serve immediately.
