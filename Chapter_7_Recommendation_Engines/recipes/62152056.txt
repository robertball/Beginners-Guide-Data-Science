Title:
Tasty Tomatillo Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tomatillos
1 medium white onion
2 cloves of garlic
2-4 jalepeno peppers
salt to taste
1/4 cup fresh chopped cilantro
1-2 tablespoons lime juice
3 ripe avocados

Directions:
Peel husks off tomatillos and rinse.
Quarter the onion.
Add tomatillos, onion, garlic, and chili peppers to medium saucepan.
Fill with enough water to cover vegetables.
Place on stove and heat on medium heat until vegetables soften.
Tomatillos will change color from bright green to a soft yellowish green.
Turn off heat and remove from burner.
With a slotted spoon remove vegetables and add to a blender.
Blend on high speed until pureed.
Add salt, cilantro and lime juice.
Blend again until mixed well.
Cut avocados in half and remove seed.
Scoop out avocado into blender.
Blend until well mixed and incorporated.
Taste and adjust seasonings as needed (add more salt, cilantro, or chili peppers as you like.)
Note:  dried cilantro works just as well as adjust to your taste preference.

Submitter: 
Keiko
Description: 
A flavorful guacamole that's easy to adjust to your heat or taste preferences.   A little untraditional to make but won't stay around long.   Great with fresh tortilla chips or as a condiment on many latin dishes.
