Title:
Breakfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
4 strips of bacon
4 flour totillas
Cheese
Salsa

Directions:
Cook bacon to desired level of crispiness.  Set aside on paper towels to soak up excess fat.
In a bowl, beat eggs until fully combined.
Pour eggs into heated skillet and cook, stirring frequently.
When eggs are done to desired consistency, remove pan from heat.
To assemble the burritos, lay a flour totilla on a plate.
Next, lay down two strips of bacon.
Spoon about a quarter of the eggs onto the bacon, keeping in line with the bacon.
Sprinkle shredded cheese and salsa to taste.
Wrap burritos and enjoy!
