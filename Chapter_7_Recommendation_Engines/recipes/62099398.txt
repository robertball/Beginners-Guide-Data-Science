Title:
Homemade Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. uncooked, large elbow macaroni
2 1/2 c. grated cheddar
2 c. grated mozzarella
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c. milk
1/2 c. chopped onion (optional)
3/4 c. diced ham (optional)
1 T. butter

Directions:
Oven 350.  Cook macaroni and drain. (Optional part: Saute onions in butter, add ham)  In large frying pan on med-low heat add soup and milk, mix well.  Add cheese and stir constantly until very smooth.  Pour in cooked pasta.  Blend well. Bake 30 minutes.  You could add bread crumbs or crushed potato chips to top if you like.
