Title:
Trinidad Style Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/696266.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb elbow macaroni
2 tbsp dried parsley
3/4 lb shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup vegetable stock
2 eggs
1 tsp mustard
1 shallot or 1 small onion finely minced
1/8 scotch bonnet pepper
1 stick butter
salt
pepper
For Topping
3 slices of day old bread toasted
3 tbsp shredded parmesean cheese

Directions:
Directions
1) Boil Macaroni in salted water add the parsley and let boil. Pre Heat oven to 350 degrees.
2)Drain and cool when aldente about 7 mins
3) In a bowl add shredded cheddar cheese, milk, vegetable stock,eggs, mustard, shallot and scotch bonnet pepper, Mix well.
3)In mixing bowl add 1/2 stick butter to the macaroni and the 1/2 stick to the cheese mixture.
4) Add salt and pepper to taste
5) Pour mixture into 13 X 9 greased baking dish
6) In a food processor add bread and cheese to make a bread crumb miture mix in parmesean cheese
7 Dot top of macaroni with butter and add bread crumbs and cheese mixture
8)Bake at 350 Degrees for 45 mins
9) Cool for 15 mins before serving will cut up in to small squares.
