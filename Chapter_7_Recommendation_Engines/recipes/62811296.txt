Title:
Daddy's Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/661266.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 small onion, chopped
1 TBSP of butter
2 packages of Chicken Flavored Instant Ramen, crush the noodles
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup (10.5oz)
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup (10.5oz)
1 Cup shredded Cheddar Cheese
1/2 Cup of Frozen Peas
1/2 Cup Frozen Corn
Salt and Pepper to taste
1/2 Cup of Water or chicken broth, ADD MORE AS NEEDED

Directions:
Melt butter in a pot on medium heat, add onions and cook until soft. Add peas and corn and cook for a few more minutes. Add the cans of soup, noodles, flavor packages that came with the ramen noodles and water, stir and cover the pot with a lid. Cook for about 10 minutes on Medium heat or until noodles are soft. While the noodles are cooking add water as needed to reach the consitancy of soup you want. Once the noodles are soft add the shredded cheese and salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Jessica Paternoster
Description: 
My dad use to make this soup when I was growing up, I still love it. A creamy, cheesy and filling Ramen, great thing is you can use any veggies, creamy soups and ramen flavors you want!
