Title:
Banana Crush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Sugar
3 Cups Water
1/2 12 oz can of pineapple juice concentrate
1/2 12 oz can of lemonade concentrate
1 12 oz can of orange juice concentrate
2 mashed bananas
Orange or Lemon-Lime Soda

Directions:
Combine water and sugar and bring to a boil. Boil 3 minutes.  Remove from the stove and allow to cool.  Add remaining ingredients (except soda).  Measure and add enough water to make 5 quarts.
Pour into ice cube trays and freeze over night.  Remove from trays and keep in freezer bags until ready to use.
TO SERVE:  Place several of the fruit cubes into a glass and pour orange or lemon lime soda over it.

Submitter: 
craftmomma
Description: 
Yummy, like a banana popsicle.  Makes a good punch or served singly in tall glasses.
