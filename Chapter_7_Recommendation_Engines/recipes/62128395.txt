Title:
Rolling Stones Cookies  - vegan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rolling Stones - vegan
1 cup all purpose flour
1 cup walnuts, ground fine
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup vegetable shortening
2 tbls. honey or maple syrup, warmed
1 tsp. coffee liqueur (Kahlua)
Powdered sugar for rolling, to roll cookies in.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.  Lightly grease
2 baking sheets.
Combine flour, walnuts and salt, stir to blend.
In a large bowl beat vegetable shortening, honey, and
liqueur until smooth.
Gradually blend in the dry ingedients, blend well.
Pinch off walnut sized pieces of dough and roll into
balls.
Place 1-inch apart on prepared baking sheets.
Bake for 23 to 25 minutes until lightly colored.
Roll in powdered sugar and transfer to rack to cool.
After cookies have cooled, roll in powdered sugar a
second time. Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
DragonShoes
Description: 
Unique little cookies, I named them after their shape.  They travel well.  I mail them to friends over the holidays and they are attractive to serve on the buffet table.
These are dairy and egg free.  Perfect vegan cookie.
