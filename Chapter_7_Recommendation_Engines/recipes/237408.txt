Title:
Gladiator Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1129525.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce amaretto liqueur
1 fluid ounce peach-flavored bourbon liqueur (such as Southern Comfort ®)
1 fluid ounce orange juice
1 fluid ounce lemon-lime soda (such as Sprite®)

Directions:
Stir amaretto liqueur, bourbon liqueur, orange juice, and lemon-lime soda together in a glass.

Submitter: 
Gevans
Description: 
Great summer drink. Try making this in a pitcher for multiple servings. You will drink it all.
