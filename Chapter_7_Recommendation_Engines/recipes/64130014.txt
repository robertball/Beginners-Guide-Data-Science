Title:
Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy cream
2 cups 2% milk
1 cup granulated sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix heavy cream, 2% milk and sugar until sugar dissolves.  Add vanilla extract and stir till blended.  Pour mixture into ice cream maker. Run ice cream makerfor approximately 1 hour or until thickened and starting to harden. Pour into freezer proof container and chill in freezer about 4 hours. Stir once half way through to ensure consistency. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Dairy Princess
Description: 
This is a very simple and easy to make vanilla ice cream.  My hubby asks for it at least once a week in the summer time. And occasionally in the winter too. 
It does not require eggs or cooking like some recipes, which makes it great for young cooks to make too!
