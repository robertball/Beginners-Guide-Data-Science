Title:
Olive Garden Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/764271.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fettuccine
1 1/2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup grated Romano cheese
6 jumbo egg yolks
Salt & cracked pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp parsley flakes

Directions:
1. Cook the fettuccine according to package directions
2. Heat the milk and cream in a large, heavy saucepan until the mixture comes to a simmer.
3. Slowly whisk in the cheeses and then remove from the heat.
4. Place the egg yolks in a bowl and slowly whisk in a portion of the hot milk & cream mixture
5. Slowly pour the egg yolk mixture back into the milk and cream mixture into the pan
6. Season to taste with salt & pepper.
7. Add the cooked fettucine to the saucepan. Sprinkle in the garlic powder and stir until the sauce thickens to the desired consistency.
8. Sprinkle with parsley.
