Title:
Colorful Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz penne or cellentani pasta
2 cups chopped basil
4 medium tomatoes, chopped
2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 bunch spinach, chopped
1 small squash, chopped
1/2 pound sweet italian turkey sausage

Directions:
Cook the pasta and set aside.
Sautee the mushrooms and squash for about 5 minutes, then add the chopped tomatoes and spinach and keep sauteeing for another 5 minutes.
Cook the sausage in a seperate frying pan and crumble into bits.
Mix the vegetables and the sausage in with the pasta and sprinkle with feta cheese and chopped basil.

Submitter: 
Sara
Description: 
This is a delectable pasta dish with a tantalizing blend of flavors.  Lots of basil is the key to this dish (remember, you can never have too much basil).
