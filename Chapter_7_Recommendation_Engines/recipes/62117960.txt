Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Sausage Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground chicken
3 italian sausages
3 14 or 15 oz cans stewed tomatoes (sliced)
2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
1/2 red onion chopped
1/2 cup beer
2 beef bouillon cubes
1 TBS brown sugar
1 tsp olive oil
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp hot sauce
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp oregano
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1 can kidney beans (optional)

Directions:
Begin by putting the stewed tomatoes, beer, beef bouillon, chili powder, cayenne pepper, hot sauce, brown sugar, paprika, oregano, and garlic powder in the slow cooker on high.  Simmer these ingredients on high for 1 hour to create a base.
Sautee onions and olive oil until slightly tender.  Add ground chicken and cook until brown.  Add to slow cooker.
Using the same pan, remove the italian sausage meat from the casing and break apart with spoon.  Cook until brown.  Add to slow cooker.
Add 2 cans of tomato paste.
Cook on high for another 2 hours.  Switch slower cooker to low and simmer for an additional 4 hours.
