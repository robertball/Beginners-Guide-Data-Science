Title:
Brie Stuffed Chicken with Carmlelized Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Tablespoons Unsalted Butter
1 Large Sweet Onion, sliced thin
2 Tablespoons Sugar
4 Each Chicken Breasts, thinly sliced and pounded, if necessary
1/2 Cup Flour
1 Each Egg, lightly beated with 1 tablespoon water
Salt and Pepper, to taste
4 Tablespoons Olive Oil
4 Ounces Brie - Triple Cream Preferred, thinly sliced, rind removed
1/2 Cup Vermouth
1 Tablespoon Thyme, dried or fresh
2 Tablespoons Arrowroot, to thicken sauce

Directions:
1.	Slice onions thinly and carmelize in butter.  Add sugar and cook onions until sugar is melted.  Set aside and cool completely.
2.	Add salt and pepper to flour.  Dredge cutlets in flour and then in egg.  Saute and brown lightly.  Remove and set aside.
3.	Layer a slice of brie and onions between cutlets.
4.	Make sauce by adding more butter to pan.  Let butter brown slightly then add vermouth.  Reduce by half.  Add thyme, salt and pepper and arrowroot to thicken.
5.	Bake cutlets in 350 oven for about 5 - 10 minutes to melt cheese.  Serve sauce over cutlets.

Submitter: 
Lia Soscia
Description: 
This recipe was developed as a simple alternative to typical Valentine's fare for one of my client's Valentine Dinner.  It was a great success and so easy to prepare.
