Title:
Blueberry Lemon Shortcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3736931.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c flour
1/4 quick cooking oats
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/8 tsp salt
3 tbsp cold butter, cut up
1 egg
3 tbsp milk
2 tbsp honey
2 tbsp plain Greek yogurt
2 tsp lemon zest
---
1 6 oz carton plain Greek yogurt
1 tbsp honey or agave syrup
1/2 c whipped cream
1 c fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Stir together first 5 ingredients (thru salt), then cut in butter until mixture resembles crumbs. Make a well in the center.
In a bowl lightly beat egg. Stir in milk, honey, yogurt, and lemon zest. Add egg mixture to flour mixture and stir just until moistened. Drop dough in 8 mounds on parchment-lined baking sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden brown. Cool.
Mix up the honey yogurt cream (carton of yogurt, honey and whipped cream) and set aside.
Split cakes in half  horizontally and place on plate. Spoon honey yogurt cream and blueberries on bottom half and cover with top half.
Drizzle with honey or agave syrup and dark chocolate savings and serve.
