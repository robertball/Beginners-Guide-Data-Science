Title:
Basic slow cooker chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes or diced tomatoes
1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 (15 ounce) cans kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 chopped onion (medium)
1  garlic clove, minced
3/4 teaspoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
1/4 cup chili powder
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon salt
Sour cream
Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cook ground beef till evenly browned in a deep skillet. In the slow cooker combine the ground beef, and all the remaining ingredients, except the sour cream and Cheddar Cheese, and cook for 4-6 hours on high. Do not cook for more 10 hours!
If you do not want to use a slow cooker, you can let it simmer on the stove top for 1 1/2 - 2 hours.
Top with a dollop of sour cream and some shredded cheddar cheese.
