Title:
English Pub Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
8 ounces Cheddar cheese, grated
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup beer (strong flavor, i.e. Guiness)
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon parsley, minced

Directions:
Preparation:
Put all ingredients, except parsley, into blender or food processor. Mix. Spoon into ramekins. Top with parsley. Serve cold with crackers and vegetables.

Submitter: 
Avon- status quo PRO
Description: 
English style pub cheese that goes well spread on crackers or toasted pumpernickel or any other dark rye breads.
