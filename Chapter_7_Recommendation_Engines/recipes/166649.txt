Title:
Tradicional Sunrise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986943.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Cuervo Tradicional
1/2 ounce grenadine
3 ounces orange juice

Directions:
Fill glass halfway with ice.
Add Cuervo Tradicional, grenadine, and orange juice.
Mix well.

Submitter: 
Cuervo®
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with the gold standard in tequila, Jose Cuervo Especial® tequila.
