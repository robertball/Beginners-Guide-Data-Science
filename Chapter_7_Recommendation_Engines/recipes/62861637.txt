Title:
Peach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 T cornstarch
2 t lemon juice
1 T orange juice
1 t cinnamon
sliced fresh peaches
1 crust

Directions:
mix peaches with other ingredients and place in pie pan.
Add crust on top.
Bake at 400 for 30 minutes.
