Title:
Garlic Roasted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977433.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (4 pound) roasting chicken, cut into serving pieces
2 tablespoons lemon or lime juice
1 tablespoon Bertolli速 Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 teaspoons Lawry's速 Garlic Salt
1 teaspoon Lawry's速 Seasoned Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
In large bowl, toss chicken, lemon juice and Olive Oil. Evenly sprinkle chicken with Lawry's(R) Garlic Salt and Seasoned Pepper. In 13 x 9-inch shallow baking or roasting pan, arrange chicken.
Roast chicken 45 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked. Garnish, if desired, with lemon slices.

Description: 
This simple preparation for a whole chicken makes for a delicious main dish and easy clean up.
