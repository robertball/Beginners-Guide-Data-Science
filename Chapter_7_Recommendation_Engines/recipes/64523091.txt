Title:
Olive Garden Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2940321.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
2 tbsp water
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
3 tablespoons Miracle Whip
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients in a blender until well mixed.
It will last up to 10 days if stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
It can also be used as a marinade for vegetables or even meats for the grill.
