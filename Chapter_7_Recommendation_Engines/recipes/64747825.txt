Title:
Borscht Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6639916.jpg
Ingredients:
10 cups chicken stock
3 medium beets, washed well
2 large potatoes, peeled and chopped into cubes
3 medium carrots, thinly sliced or shredded
1 small red onion, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 head cabbage, cored and shredded
1 large can of crushed tomatoes  (about 2 cups )
1 cup of left over cooked pork ( or beef ) chopped in small cubes
2 tablespoons sugar or to taste
Salt and papper to taste
Fresh dill chopped
Sour cream

Directions:
Method :
Place beets in a pot and fill with just enough water to cover them
Cover pot and boil until tender, about 45 minutes. Remove beets from pot to a plate or cutting board and set aside to cool. Then chop in small cubes.
In a large pot, add broth, carrots potatoes and boil covered for about 15 minutes, then add onions, garlic, cabbage, pork, diced beets and diced tomatoes and simmer 30 min.
When ready to serve, garnish with fresh chopped dill and dab of sour cream.

Submitter: 
Mikey's Kitchen
Description: 
While I was on tour in Russia and Ukraine, I had this delicious and comforting soup many times as it is a staple in those countries. This is my concocted version.
