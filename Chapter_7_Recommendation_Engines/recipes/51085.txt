Title:
Four-Cheese Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993442.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
2 1/2 cups hot water
1 cup milk
1 (8.5 ounce) package Hamburger Helper® three cheese
1 (16 ounce) package frozen broccoli, cauliflower and carrots
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1 cup frozen green peas
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook beef in 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until brown; drain.
Stir in hot water, milk, uncooked Pasta, Sauce Mix, broccoli, cauliflower and carrots and Italian seasoning.  Heat to boiling, stirring occasionally.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Stir in peas.  Cover and cook about 5 minutes or until pasta is tender.  Stir in sour cream; sprinkle with cheese.

Submitter: 
Betty Crocker®
Description: 
Want more cheese?  This pasta dish is loaded with vegetables and the delicious taste of four cheeses!  Hamburger Helper® dinner mix makes it one-skillet easy!
