Title:
Taco Chili or Perfect Nacho Topper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 lbs lean ground beef
1 can Black Beans
1 can Pinto Beans
1 can Kidney Beans
1 can Corn
2 cans Del Monte diced tomatoes with basil, oregano and garlic
1 can RoTel Diced Tomatoes with green chiles
1 can Beef Broth
3 tbls or 1 packet Taco Seasoning
3 tbls or one packet Ranch Seasoning

Directions:
Brown the lean ground beef and drain the fat. Add to stock pot.
Drain the black beans,  pinto beans,  kidney beans and corn. Rinse with water. Add to pot.
Add all remaining ingredients to pot (3 cans diced tomatoes, beef broth, ranch and taco seasoning).
Stir over medium heat until desired temperature is reached.
To serve as a chili: Top with nacho strips and a sprinkling of a Mexican cheese blend
To serve as Nachos: Drain most of the liquid from the chili. Pour over nachos and top with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkling of a Mexican cheese blend.

Submitter: 
Tammy Walker
Description: 
This recipe is quick and simple. Ready in minutes. Grab a stock pot, toss in your ingredients, heat and serve. You can either serve this recipe as-is as a chili (top with nacho strips and Mexican cheese if you're feeling fancy) or drain most of the liquid and serve it over nachos with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkle of a Mexican cheese blend.
