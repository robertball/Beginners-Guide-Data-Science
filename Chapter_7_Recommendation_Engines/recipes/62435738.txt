Title:
Spaghetti Sauce Thick and Meaty Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 whole red chopped onion
2 (14.5 ounce) cans Itailian style diced tomatoes
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 can hunts tomato sauce
1 1/2 bags baby bella mushrooms
3 tablespoons fresh basil (Approx.)
or
1 tablespoon dried basil
2 tablespoon minced garlic
1 tablespoon garlic & Parsley Powder
or
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon dried oregano

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook ground beef with onion and minced garlic until brown; drain.  Stir tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, basil, garlic powder, oregano and mushrooms into beef mixture.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer one hour.  Serve over hot pasta.

Submitter: 
Kevin Amy Kayla Ethan
Description: 
A simple tomato sauce with ground beef, onion, mushrooms, basil, oregano and garlic powder, simmers about an hour for a rich, blended taste.Modified from: Spaghetti Sauce III
