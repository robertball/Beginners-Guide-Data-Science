Title:
Iced Mocha Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986466.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fat free milk
1 cup ice cubes
1 packet No Sugar Added Rich Milk Chocolate NESTLE® CARNATION® INSTANT BREAKFAST®
1 teaspoon TASTER'S CHOICE® 100% Pure Instant Coffee
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)

Directions:
PLACE milk, ice, Carnation Instant Breakfast, Taster's Choice and cinnamon in blender; cover. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Carnation® Instant Breakfast™
Description: 
Here's a delightful change of pace from the usual coffee routine.
