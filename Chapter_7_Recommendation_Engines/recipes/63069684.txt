Title:
Chex Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887264.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, melted
1/3 cup Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon seasoning salt
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 cups pretzels
2 cups Cheerios
1 cup nuts
2 cups Wheat Chex cereal
3 cups Corn Chex cereal
3 cups Rice Chex cereal

Directions:
Combine butter, Worcestershire sauce and salts in a saucepan.
Heat until salts are dissolved.
In a large bowl combine remaining ingredients pour sauce over coating well.
Bake at 250ยบ for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes.
Store in airtight container.
