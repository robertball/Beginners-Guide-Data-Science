Title:
Ham and Pineapple Ranch Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980991.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound ham, julienne-cut
1 (8 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
1 cup fresh or frozen asparagus tips, blanched
1 (10 ounce) package salad greens
1 (8 ounce) bottle KRAFT FREE Ranch Fat Free Dressing
1/2 cup chow mein noodles

Directions:
Arrange ham, pineapple and asparagus over greens.
Serve with dressing. Sprinkle with chow mein noodles.
