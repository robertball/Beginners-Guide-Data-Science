Title:
Spicy Sausage & Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/462765.jpg
Ingredients:
1	pound hot Italian sausage
1	tablespoon olive oil
1	onion, chopped
2	to 3 cloves garlic, minced
4	celery stalks, diced
3	carrots, peeled and diced
2	medium russet potatoes, peeled and diced
8	cups chicken or vegetable stock
4	cups water
2	cups lentils
1 ½	to 2 teaspoons dried marjoram
1 ½	teaspoons dried savory
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh grated Pecorino Romano to garnish.

Directions:
1.	In large heavy saucepan, heat oil over medium heat; add onion and cook for
5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add celery, carrot and sausage; cook for 5 minutes, Stirring occasionally. Add Garlic, cook and stir for 1-2 minutes.   Add stock, water, lentils, marjoram and savory; bring to boil Reduce heat, cover partially and simmer for 30 minutes.
2.	Add Potatoes; cover and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 35 minutes Or until lentils are tender.  Salt and pepper to taste.

Description: 
Hearty, Flavortul, comforting soup.  Freezes well.
