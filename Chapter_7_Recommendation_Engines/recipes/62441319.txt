Title:
Healhty Banana Bread-Fanti's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/457973.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups of ripped bananas
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup Olive Oil
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients,  stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack
