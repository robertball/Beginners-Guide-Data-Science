Title:
Thai Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6oz fine egg noodles
1 1/2 tbl olive oil
1 1/2 tble white wine vinegar
2 tbl no salt peanut butter
2 tbl soy sauce
1 T honey
1t ginger
1/2 t red pepper flakes
1/2 t vege oil
1 1/2 t sesame oil
1 1/2 bell peppers, seeded, and cut into thin strips
4 green onions
8 oz shredded chicken
2 T chopped almonds

Directions:
Cook noodles.
Prepare dressing: Blend; olive oil, vinegar, PB, soy sauce, honey, ginger, pepper.
Stove: Heat vege oil & sesame oil; add bell pepper & green onions add chicken.
Add dressing and noodles.
