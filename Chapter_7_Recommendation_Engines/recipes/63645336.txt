Title:
Zesty Miracle Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonaise
2 tablespoons ketchup
2 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons milk
2 teaspoons chili powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon hot sauce
1/2 teaspoon worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Cover and chill.

Submitter: 
Barb0607
Description: 
I created this when I was looking for something zesty to put on chicken sliders.  My husband loves it, and started calling it Miracle Sauce.  He uses it on sandwiches, burgers, and he even dips his french fries in it.
