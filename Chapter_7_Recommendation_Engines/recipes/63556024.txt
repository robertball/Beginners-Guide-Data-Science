Title:
Healthy Crockpot Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/971834.jpg
Ingredients:
- 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts (thin sliced or regular);
- 3 cloves of garlic, minced;
- 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil;
- 1 cup marsala wine;
- 1 cup low sodium chicken broth;
- Â¼ cup all-purpose flour;
- 1 8oz package fresh mushrooms (use 2 packages if you LOVE mushrooms - any type will work);
- 1 teaspoon of dried basil or parsley;
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt;
- pepper, to taste

Directions:
Drizzle olive oil in bottom of crock pot
Put the chicken breasts in crock pot.
Sprinkle the flour over the chicken.
Sprinkle the salt, pepper, and basil or parsley, on top of the flour.
Pour the wine and broth into the crockpot.
Layer the mushrooms on top.
Cook on LOW for 6 hours.
When the meal is ready, take the chicken out and place on the serving dish. Stir the sauce and spoon over the chicken.
Serve over egg noodles or pasta of your choice
