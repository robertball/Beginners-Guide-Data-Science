Title:
Buttermilk Substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2113139.jpg
Ingredients:
Just under one cup Milk
1 Tablespoon white vinegar or lemon juice

Directions:
Place a Tablespoon of white vinegar or lemon juice in a liquid measuring cup.
Add enough milk to bring the liquid up to the one-cup line.
Let stand for five minute. Then, use as much as your recipe calls for.

Submitter: 
Kevin G
Description: 
Once you see how easy it is to make buttermilk, you'll want to make your own every time.
