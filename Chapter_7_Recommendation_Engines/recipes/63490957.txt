Title:
Bacon-Date Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices bacon; dice & cook, drain on paper towels, reserve drippings
2 celery stalks; chopped
1 onion; chopped
dates; you will need 3/4 cup of chopped/diced dates
1 garlic clove; minced
1 teaspoon dry thyme (1 tablespoon fresh)
Hawaiian bread; cut into cubes, 4 cups
1 to1 1/2 cups chicken broth

Directions:
Cook diced bacon, remove from skillet, drain on paper towels & reserve about one tablespoon drippings in skillet.  Add chopped celery and onion; cook until tender (do-not brown).
Stir in the dates, garlic and thyme.
In a large bowl combine the bread cubes with the bacon-date mixture.  Drizzle with chicken broth to desired consistency.
Spray a 2 qt. baking dish (if desired).  Place bread mixture into the baking dish; bake at325 for 40 minutes or until browned.
