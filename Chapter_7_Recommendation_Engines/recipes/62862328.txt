Title:
Jamaican Jerk Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/685660.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (5-oz.) cans Solid White Albacore Tuna in Water
1/3 cup Low-Fat Mayonnaise
2 teaspoons Sodium-Free Jamaican Jerk Spice
1/4 cup Onions, finely diced
1/4 cup Sweet Red Peppers, diced
3 Tablespoons Fresh Cilantro, chopped

Directions:
In bowl, combine mayonnaise, Jamaican Jerk Spice, onions, red peppers and cilantro. Gently flake and fold in Chicken of the SeaÂ® Tuna, mix until all ingredients are blended. Chill until ready to serve.
