Title:
pumpkin cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1 cup oil
4 eggs
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 cups flour
1 can pumpkin

Directions:
Combine sugar, oil and eggs in large bowl.  Mix well.
Mix dry ingredients in small bowl.  Add dry ingredients to sugar/oil/egg mixture and beat well. Stir in pumpkin. Pour batter into 2 greased/floured 9" cake pans. Bake in preheated oven 350 degrees 35-40 minutes.  Cool on wire rack 10 minutes before removing from pans.
Frost with cream cheese icing when completely cool.
