Title:
Sloppy Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Cocoa
1 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
1 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
2 cups Sugar
2 cups Flour
1 tsp Salt
1 1/2 cup Hot Water
3/4 cup Vegetable Oil
1 tsp Vanilla
2 Eggs

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients together.  Add wet ingredients and mix well.  Pour into greased and floured 13x9 baking dish and bake for 30-35 minutes on 350 degrees.  Cool cake and add your favorite icing or simply sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Suzanne
Description: 
This is a very moist chocolate cake that is easy and delicious with your favorite icing.  I usually bake in a 13 x 9 dish and ice with vanilla icing.
