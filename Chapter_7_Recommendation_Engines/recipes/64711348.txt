Title:
Big Ray's Orzo and Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5597071.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (16 oz.) orzo pasta, uncooked
5 cups small broccoli florets
1 cup cherry tomatoes, quartered
1 small red pepper, chopped
1 cup Italian Dressing, divided
1 pkg. (4 oz.) Crumbled Feta Cheese
1/2 cup chopped  almonds

Directions:
Cook pasta in large saucepan as directed on package, omitting salt and adding broccoli to the boiling water for the last 2 min.; drain. Rinse with cold water; drain well.
Place pasta mixture in large bowl. Add tomatoes, peppers and 3/4 cup dressing; mix lightly.
Refrigerate 1 hour. Add cheese, nuts and remaining dressing; mix lightly.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Italian dressing, feta and almonds give this orzo salad great flavor, but the festive color comes from broccoli and cherry tomatoes. It's perfect to take to your next picnic.
