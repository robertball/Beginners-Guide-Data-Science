Title:
Chocolate Buttercream Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup margarine, softened
1/3 cup cocoa
4 cups confectioners' sugar
2 tablespoons milk

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream together the margarine and shortening. Blend in the sugar, one cup at a time,and the cocoa, beating well after each addition. Beat in the milk, and continue mixing until light and fluffy. Keep icing covered until ready to decorate.

Submitter: 
Lisa Travis
Description: 
An easy to make egg-free frosting that works great for decorating cookies for holidays, birthdays and parties.Modified from: Buttercream Icing
