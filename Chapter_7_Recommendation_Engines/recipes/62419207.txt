Title:
Anna Banana Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/648744.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package Smoked Sausage (or Beel Kielbasa), cubed
2 Chicken Breasts, cubed
1 White Onion, chopped
1 Red Bell Pepper, chopped
1 C Celery, chopped
2 Tbl Cajun Seasoning (personal mix available)
1 C White Rice, uncooked
1 (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes (with juice)
1 Tbl miced garlic
2 C chicken broth
3 bay leaves
1/4 tsp dried thyme

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large saucepan, stir in sausage and chicken and cook for 5 minutes
Add onion, red pepper, celery and cajun seasoning and cook until vegetables are soft, approximately 6 - 8 minutes
Stir in rice until coated in the vegetable mixture then pour in tomatoes with juice, garlic, chicken broth, bay and thyme leaves
Bring to a simmer over medium-high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer for approximately 20 minutes (until rice is tender)
Remove Bay leaves before serving

Description: 
A tasty modification to a Southern Original for those who don't like seafood!
