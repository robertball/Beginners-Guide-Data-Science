Title:
Basil Vinaigrette Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup olive oil
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup honey
3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
2 cloves garlic, minced
Dash of salt
Dash of pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, apple cider vinegar, honey, basil, and garlic. Pour over or toss with your favorite salad to serve. Blend in blender

Submitter: 
Soncea Braden-McCann
Description: 
Easy to make vinaigrette dressing especially good with summer salads.Modified from: Basil Vinaigrette Dressing
