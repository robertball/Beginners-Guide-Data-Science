Title:
Carrots with Orange and Ginger Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1104704.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound baby carrots
1 cup orange juice
1 tablespoon honey
2 teaspoons corn starch
1 teaspoon minced ginger
1 pinch salt
fresh chopped parsley for garnish

Directions:
Bring salted water to a boil in a sauce pan. Add carrots and cook until tender. Drain carrots and set aside.
In a sauce pan mix orange juice, honey, corn starch, ginger and salt. Bring mixture to a boil, stirring until sauce has thickened.
Add back carrots and simmer on medium heat for 2 minutes.
Plate carrots on a serving dish and garnish with chopped parsley.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
The taste of orange, honey and ginger accentuate the sweetness of the carrots. This dish is a quick, simple and tasty alternative to glazed carrots.
