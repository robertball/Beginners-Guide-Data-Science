Title:
Steak Fajita Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 leftover KC Strips cut into bite size strips
1 can diced tomatoes
1 small can tomato sauce
1/2 cup milk
4 cups beef stock
1T minced garlic
1/4t crushed red pepper flakes
2T cumin
2T fajita seasoning
1T lime juice
1 small onion chopped
1 red bell pepper chopped
1 green bell pepper chopped
1 can seasoned black beans rinsed
8 oz freshly shredded Monterey-Jack cheese for garnish
1/2 cup long grain rice (optional)
1 sliced avocado for garnish (optional)
Tortilla chips (optional)

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients expect rice into large pot on medium high for 30 minutes covered.  Add rice cook until tender (30 minutes) Garnish with sliced avocado, cheese and tortilla chips.
