Title:
Kookie Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7462192.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.5 ounce) bag corn chips
1 cup light corn syrup
1 cup sugar
1 cup creamy peanut butter

Directions:
Spread corn chips in a greased 15-in. x 10-in. x 2-in. baking pan.  in a saucepan over medium heat, bring corn syrup and sugar to a boil. Remove from the heat; stir in peanut butter until smooth. Pour over corn chips. Cool. Break into pieces.

Submitter: 
Helen  Bachman
Description: 
'You won't be able to stop eating this sweet, salty, crunchy and chewy snack,' assures Helen Bachman of Champaign, Illinois. 'It's a quick way to dress up plain corn chips.'
