Title:
Easy Banana Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 frozen (peeled) bananas
1/2 cup of Skim milk
1 tsp. sugar (optional)

Directions:
Once frozen, take 2 bananas place them in a blender and add 1/4 cup of skim milk. Blend for 30 seconds. Add remainder of milk blend an additional 30 seconds on high speed and serve immediately.  What could be easier.  No need for sugar but if you like it sweet add 1 tsp of white sugar and blend for another 30 seconds.

Submitter: 
ANNE  MARSHALL
Description: 
When banana have over rippened don't throw them away, peel them, wrap them in plastic wrap and freeze them. Two ingredients is all you need to make this family pleaser.
