Title:
Elegant hoilday dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-6 1/2oz cans crab meat
(drained)
3 8oz pkg cream cheese
2Tbsp prepared mustard
1Tbsp powdered sugar
1/2C mayonnaise
1/2C white wine
1Tbsp onion jucie
1Tbsp lemon jucie

Directions:
Melt cream cheese in double boiler. Add all ingredients. Heat until bubbly. Serve from chafing dish with assorted crackers or fresh vegetables.
