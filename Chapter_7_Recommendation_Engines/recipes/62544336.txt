Title:
Fab Caramel Flan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/525907.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Whipped Cream
1/3 cup whole milk
1 egg
2 yolks
4 tbsp white sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
Caramel:
1/3 cup white sugar
1 tbs hot water
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp butter for greasing cups

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
2.Cream: Mix together cream, milk, egg,yolks,sugar,vanilla.
3.Caramel:In a small, heavy saucepan over medium heat, melt sugar, stirring, until dark golden, add hot water and lemon juice. Pour into butter greased baking cups, tilting to coat bottom.Pour cream to half full.
4.Place cups into baking dish, filled with warm water to half of cups.
5.Bake in preheated oven 50 to 60 minutes, until center is set and creamed. Cool one hour on wire rack, then chill in refrigerator 5 hours or overnight. To unmold, run a knife around edges of cups and invert on a rimmed serving platters.
