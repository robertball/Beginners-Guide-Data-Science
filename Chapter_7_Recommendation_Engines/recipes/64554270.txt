Title:
To Die For Whipped Creme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3348886.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pint Heavy Whipping Cream
1/4 Cup Powdered Sugar
1 Tbsp Vanilla
1 Tsp Cinnamon

Directions:
Power cream into a mixing bowl
Whipping the cream with a mixer on low speed
Carefully add powdered sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon
Once ingredients are combined, turn mixer up to high
Whip until cream forms stiff peaks
Don't whip beyond stiff peaks or your will get butter

Submitter: 
Andrew Taylor
Description: 
Sweet strawberry topping for your desserts, pancakes, or just eat it by the spoon full.
