Title:
Low Glycemic Sugar Free All Natural Flax Seed and Nut Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. Flax seeds
1 C. Sunflower seeds
1 C. Unsweetened shredded coconut
1 C. Chopped pecans
1 C. Chopped walnuts
1 C. Chopped Almonds
1 stick of butter, melted
2 t. cinnamon
2 t. vanilla powder (sugar & alcohol-free)
1/4  C. Fructevia, Stevia Brand Stevia Blend of or a tiny dash of Stevia Brand Stevia Powder

Directions:
In a large rosting pan, mix together flax seeds, sunflower seeds, coconut and nuts.
Drizzle with the melted butter.
Stir cinnamon, vanilla powder & Frutevia or Stevia Brand Stevia Blend.
Toast in the oven at 325 degrees for 30 minutes stirring every 5 or 10 minutes.

Susan Taylor

No wheat or sugar!!!  Granola made with nuts & seeds!
