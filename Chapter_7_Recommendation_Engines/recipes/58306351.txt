Title:
Snowman Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. Cream cheese, softened
2 C. shredded white cheddar
1 small onion finely chopped
2 t. worcestershire sauce OR
1 t. Tabasco sauce
1/8 t. garlic salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.  Chill.
Form into 3 balls-small, med. large
Roll in grated parmesan cheese
Use pepper corns, carrot, and pretzel sticks to complete snowman look.  Refrigerate until ready to serve with crackers.
