Title:
Baklava Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1029540.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce amaretto liqueur
3 fluid ounces gin
1 fluid ounce creme de cacao
1 1/2 fluid ounces simple syrup
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Combine amaretto, gin, creme de cacao, simple syrup, and cinnamon in a cocktail shaker full of ice. Shake well; strain into 2 martini glasses.

Submitter: 
blancdeblanc
Description: 
My friend had this at a Vancouver restaurant and begged the recipe off the bartender.  It's very tasty, and not as sweet as it sounds!
