Title:
Refreshing Brazilian Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3851886.jpg
Ingredients:
4 1/4 cups water
4 large limes, cut into wedges and seeded
1/2 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
ice cubes
5 tablespoons white sugar

Directions:
Combine water and limes in a blender; blend until smooth. Strain mixture through a strainer into a bowl; discard solids. Pour lime mixture into blender. Add condensed milk, ice, and sugar; blend until combined.

Submitter: 
Zainab Pervaiz
Description: 
In this hot summer season what can be better than a cool and refreshing splash of lemonade? Not just a typical lemonade; it's beyond that. It has an additional ingredient to enhance the sweetness level. Yet it is similar enough to traditional lemonade to please a crowd at summer pool party! Serve the way you want.
