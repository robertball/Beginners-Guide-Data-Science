Title:
Thai scallops and Vitamin Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130690.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head of Vitamin Greens, chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
1 yellow onion, chopped
1 lb fresh sea scallops
1/2 C avocado oil
1 T minced garlic
1 T fresh ginger
1/4 Serrano pepper, minced
1 T green curry paste
2 T cilantro
4 cups cooked brown rice

Directions:
Chop vegetables and set aside.
Heat avocado oil on high in a large wok and add onion, garlic and ginger. Saute for 2 minutes until fragrant.
Add scallops and cook until just white.
Add red pepper, Serrano pepper, and green curry paste. Cook for 2 minutes.
Add Vitamin Greens and cook until wilted.
Serve over cooked brown rice and top with cilantro.
Add Vitamin Greens

Submitter: 
Jennifer Jill Harman
Description: 
Delicious use of Vitamin Greens (which we get a lot of in the spring from our CSA) and green curry paste. High in flavor, low in carbs.
