Title:
My Hubby even ate this Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/365255.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. fresh asparagus, trimmed
3/4 cups water
2 tbsp. butter
1 tsp. Worchesterchire
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
1. Place asparagus and water in a skillet and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium, cover, and steam 3-4 minutes or until all water has steamed off
2. Add butter, worchestershire, garlic, and cook until asparagus is lightly brown.
