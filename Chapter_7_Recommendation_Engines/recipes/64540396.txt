Title:
Flat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup water
1/2 cup milk
1 Tbsp Oil
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt
3 cup flour
2 tsp yeast

Directions:
put in bread maker and make dough
when doughs done, remove and divide into how many flat breads are needed, let rest then roll out to desired size. Grill in a non stick pan 2 mins each side
