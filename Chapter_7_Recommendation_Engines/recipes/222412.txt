Title:
Frozen Daiquiri Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1011541.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces light rum
1 tablespoon triple sec
1 1/2 fluid ounces lime juice
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 cup crushed ice
1 maraschino cherry

Directions:
Combine light rum, triple sec, lime juice, and ice in blender with 1 cup crushed ice; blend until smooth. Pour into  a Champagne flute.
Garnish with maraschino cherry.

Submitter: 
Allrecipes
Description: 
Frozen daiquiris are a great way to cool off on a hot summer day. Blend rum, sugar, triple sec, and lime juice together with ice for a frosty cocktail treat.
