Title:
#1 Ultimate Berry Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/2 c. frozen berries1/4 c. white sugar2 tbsp corn starch3/4 c. melted butter2 c. brown sugar1 tbsp vanilla1 tsp cinnamon1/2 c. flour2 c. rolled oats

Directions:
Mix berries, sugar, corn starch lightly. Set aside.Cream butter, sugar and vanilla in separate bowl.Mix remaining ingredients in adding oats last. Stirring in oats until just mixed.Place berries in bottom of a 9x9 pan.Chill topping for 15 min.Place topping on berries, pressing lightly.Bake at 375 for 60 min.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
This recipe is for the juicy fruit. Even Courtney will eat this one.
