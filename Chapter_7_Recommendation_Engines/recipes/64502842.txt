Title:
Cooking With Plants Miso Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Broth
1 tbs White Miso paste
1 tbs Nutritional Yeast Flakes
1 whole Nori Sheet crumbled or cut into small pieces
2 cups Hot boiled water
Add Ins
1 large handful Bok Choy sliced thinly
2 large handfuls Bean Sprouts
0.25 cup Carrot grated
0.25 cup Tofu cubed
Sesame Seeds for sprinkling over the top

Directions:
Put miso into your bowl and gradually stir in hot water a little at a time to thin the miso paste.    Add all of the broth ingredients into a container and stir well. You want to get the miso paste well dissolved and mixed through. Let sit while you prepare the soup add ins.
Add Ins
Add bok choy to one side of your soup bowl.
Place bean sprouts on other side of your soup bowl.
Layer carrot across the middle of the bowl, and then top with tofu.
Pour hot broth mixture over the top.
Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve.
Recipe Notes
You can add any ingredients to your soup bowl that you like. eg. mushrooms, cooked rice, cooked noodles etc would all work really well.
