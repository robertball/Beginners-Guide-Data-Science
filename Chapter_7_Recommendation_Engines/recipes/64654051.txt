Title:
Alexander's Mouth-Watering Pizza Crescents Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can crescent rolls
shredded mozzarella cheese(judge the amount by how much cheese you like)
Rao's sauce or your favorite tomato sauce
1 beaten egg
toasted sesame seeds (toast in a dry skillet on med/low for a few mins.)
Your personal favorite toppings

Directions:
Unroll the crescent into triangles on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper.
Top each one with sauce and cheese.
Place your favorite toppings on them cheese.
Roll up starting at the wide end.
Brush the top with egg.
Sprinkel with sesame seeds.
Bake at 375 degrees.
15-20 mins until golden and cheese is melted!

Alexander Petrie

This recipe makes unbelievably tasty roll pizzas!
