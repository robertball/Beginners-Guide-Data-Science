Title:
Big Perm's Egg Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5329469.jpg
Ingredients:
16 Maple Sausage Links, chopped into pieces
1 lb of Turkey Sausage
6 Sweet Peppers, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 bag of baby spinach
2 Cups of Shredded Cheese
9-10 Farm Fresh Eggs
3 Slices of Whole Wheat Bread, crust removed

Directions:
Brown Turkey Sausage and chopped Maple Sausage in skillet. Drain and let cool.
Sautee Onions and Sweet Peppers in Olive Oil or Butter until caramelized.  Add Spinach, cover, and steam down until shriveled. Beat 9-10 Eggs lightly in a mixing bowl. Add cooled meat, Vegetables, and Shredded Cheese to Egg mixture. Spray bottom of a 9 x 9 Baking Dish with Cooking Spray. Layer sliced bread on bottom of pan. Pour Egg mixture over bread slices evenly. Bake at 350' uncovered for 46 minutes. Let sit for 10 minutes. Cut into 9 even squares. Refridgerate what you don't eat. Makes an easy, microwaveable breakfast for about a week.

Submitter: 
Big Perm
Description: 
Meaty Egg Bake. Healthy and hearty. Substitute bread slices for chopped Broccoli or Cauliflower for a low carb, breakfast masterpiece!
