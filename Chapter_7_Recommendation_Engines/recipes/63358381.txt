Title:
Country Grits & Country Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894184.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups water
½ cup quick cooking grits, uncooked
3 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
4 eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup milk
½ teaspoon dried basil
½ teaspoon dried sage
¼ teaspoon garlic powder
½ teaspoon dried onion flakes
1 lb pork sausage, cooked

Directions:
Bring water to boil, stir in grits.  Return to boil, then reduce heat and cook 4 minutes stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat.  Stir in cheese until melted.
Combine eggs and milk, beat well.  Stir in basil, sage, garlic powder & onion flakes.  Gradually add ¼ of the grits into the egg mixture, stirring well.  Then add remaining grits, stirring until combined.  Stir in sausage.  Pour into lightly greased 11x7 baking dish.
Bake at 350 for 45-50 minutes until set.

Description: 
This breakfast casserole is a holiday favorite in my family.  I recently adapted it by using chicken sausage.
