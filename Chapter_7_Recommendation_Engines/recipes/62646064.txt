Title:
Party Mimosa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) can apricot-mango nectar
1 (12 ounce) can pineapple juice
3/4 cup cold water
1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed and undiluted
1 (750 milliliter) bottle cold champagne
(Moscato D'asti) sweeter champagne

Directions:
Stir together apricot nectar, pineapple juice, water, and orange juice concentrate in a large pitcher until combined. Pour in bottle of sparkling wine just before serving.
One part mix with 3/4 C. champagne.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
This tropical fruit mimosa is a wonderful change, and will serve several people too!Modified from: Party Mimosa
