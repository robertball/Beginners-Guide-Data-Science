Title:
Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/694694.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
6 fluid ounces chilled lemon-lime soda
6 fluid ounces light rum
1/2 (10 ounce) package frozen sliced strawberries
2 cups ice cubes

Directions:
In the container of a blender, combine the lemonade concentrate, lemon-lime soda, rum and strawberries. Cover, and blend until smooth. Add ice and blend to desired thickness.

Submitter: 
flossi
Description: 
This refreshing drink is made with strawberries, rum and lemon-lime soda blended with ice for a great summer cool down.
