Title:
Garden Greens Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb. asparagus, trimmed
3 oz. goat or ricotta cheese
1/4 cup olive oil
3-4 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
2 tbsp. heavy cream
5 pepperoncini - rinsed, seeded and chopped
3/4 lb. rotini pasta
10 oz. fresh or frozen peas (thaw if using frozen)
5 oz. baby arugala
1 cup sliced radishes
1 cup chopped chives

Directions:
Cook asparagus until crisp-tender - about 3 minutes.
Transfer to a bowl of ice water and reserve cooking water.
Cut cooled asparagus into 1 1/2" pieces.
Whisk together cheese, oil, lemon juice, cream and 1/2 cup of cooking water in a large bowl.
Stir in pepperoncini.
Return water in pot to a boil and cook pasta.
Drain pasta and add to bowl with dressing and remaining ingredients.
Toss to coat and season to taste with salt and pepper.
