Title:
Irish Dairy Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups of sugar
3/4 lbs of butter softened
3 cups white flour sifted
1 8oz pkg cream cheese softened
6 eggs at room temperature
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar together.
Add 6 eggs one at a time.
Add cream cheese. Mix well
Add vanilla and slowly add flour.
The batter will be like velvet.
Pour into a butter and floured bundt pan.
Cook at 300 degrees for and  one hour and half or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Aven
Description: 
This supose to be an authentic Irish recipe. I got it from a dear Irish friend. It is not for the faint of heart.... but it is devine.
