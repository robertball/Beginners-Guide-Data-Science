Title:
Easy to Make Fudge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 sticks of butter, melted
1 cup chopped walnuts, divided
2 packages instant chocolate fudge pudding mix
3 cups milk
1 cup confectioners sugar
12 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 container (16 ounces) frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
1. Preheat ovn at 350 degrees F.
2. In a bowl, combine flour, butter and 3/4 cup of the nuts. Press mixture into a 9"x13" baking dish. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until a firm crust forms. Let cool.
3. In a bowl, whisk together pudding mix and milk until thick, let stand. In another bowl, combine cream cheese, confectioners sugar, and half of the whipped topping. Mix well.
4. Spread the cream cheese mixture over the cooled crust. Spoon the chocolate pudding over the cream cheese mixture. Top with the remaining whipped topping. Sprinkle remaining walnuts over the top.
5. Cover and chill for at least 6 hours or overnight if you wish. Keep leftovers stored in a refrigerator.
