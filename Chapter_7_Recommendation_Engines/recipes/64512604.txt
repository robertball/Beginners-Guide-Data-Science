Title:
Maple Mulled Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon cider
3-4 cinnamon sticks
2 tsp. whole cloves
2 tsp. whole allspice
1-2 tbsp. orange juice concentrate
1-2 tbsp. maple syrup

Directions:
Combine ingredients in slow cooker.
Cover.  Heat on low 2 hours.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
Serve at Halloween, after Christmas Caroling, or for sledding parties!!!
