Title:
Lowfat Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup pumpkin puree
2 eggs
1/4 cup oil
1/3 c. water
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 3/4 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground cloves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease and flour a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Mix together pumpkin, eggs, oil, water and sugars.  In a separate bowl sift together the flour, soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves.
Stir dry ingredients into moist until just mixed.  Pour into prepared pan.
Bake 45-50 minutes, until toothpick in center comes out clean.

Description: 
Very moist, flavorful pumpkin bread that is suprisingly low in fat.
