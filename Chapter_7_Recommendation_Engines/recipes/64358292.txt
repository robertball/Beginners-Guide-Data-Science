Title:
Sauteed Srting Beans with Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup slivered almonds
2 tsp olive oil
3 medium garlic cloves, minced
8 cups green snap beans, trimmed
1/2 cup chicken broth
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Place almonds in a large dry skillet and place over medium heat
Cook until nuts are golden brown, shaking pan frequently (about 2-4 minutes)
Remove nuts from pan & set aside
Heat oil in same skillet over medium heat
Add garlic and saute 1 minute
Add beans and saute 1 minute
Add broth to pan, cover and steam until beans are crisp-tender
Add salt and pepper, stir to coat
Remove from heat, stir in toasted almonds.
