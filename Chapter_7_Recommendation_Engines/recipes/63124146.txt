Title:
David's Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6857244.jpg
Ingredients:
2# Jumbo shrimp
Marinade: 1/4 # melted butter
1/2 cup orange juice
1/4 cup lime juice
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp ginger
1/2 cup honey
1 T seafood magic (Paul Prudhomme) (Old bay?)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except shrimp.
Add shrimp and marinate for 4-6 hours.
Sewer and grill.
