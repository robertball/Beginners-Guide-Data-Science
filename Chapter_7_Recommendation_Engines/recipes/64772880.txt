Title:
Yellow Dhal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3rd cup yellow moong dhal
1 tsp. cumin seeds
1 tsp. turmeric powder
Chopped coriander/cilantro
2 tsp. salt or to taste

Directions:
1. Cook the lentils using three cups of water.  Takes ~15 minutes
2. Heat oil in a saucepan and fry the cumin seeds for a minute.
3. Add the cumin seeds to the lentils.
4. Add turmeric powder, heat till the mixture starts to boil
5. Season with salt and turn off the heat.
6. Garnish with chopped coriander/cilantro.
