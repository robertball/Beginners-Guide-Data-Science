Title:
Glorious Glazed Ham Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 fully cooked ham steakm cut 1/2" thick (approx. 1 lb)
GLAZE:
1/4 cup apricot jam
1 TBL fresh lime juice
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/8 tsp ground white pepper

Directions:
In small bowl, combine glaze ingredients.
Place ham steak on rack in broiler pan so surface of ham is 3 inches from heat; brush tope with half of glze. Broil 2 to 3 minutes. Turn ham; brush with remaining glaze. Broil 2 to 3 minutes longer or until glaze is bubbly and golden.
