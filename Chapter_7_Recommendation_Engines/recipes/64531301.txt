Title:
Banana chocolate chip cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ c. Butter
1 c. Sugar
1 egg
1 c. Mashed banana
12 oz. Chocolate chips
1 tsp. Baking soda
1 tbsp. Water
1 ½ c. Unbleached flour
1 tsp. Nutmeg
1 tsp. Vanilla

Directions:
Cream the butter and sugar
Ad the egg and mashed bananas
Discover the baking soda in the water and add to the mixture
Beat in (low speed) the flour, nutmeg and vanilla
Stir in chocolate chips
Use paper liners In your pan
Bake at 375 degrees for 12-15 minutes
Cool and frost as desired
