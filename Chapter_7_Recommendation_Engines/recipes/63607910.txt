Title:
The Girls' Chicken Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked & diced chicken breast
3 green onions, minced
Refrigerated poppyseed dressing, to taste
12 Hawaiian Sweet rolls, sliced
1 tub whipped pineapple flavored cream cheese
6 Boston lettuce leaves
16 ounces ripe strawberries, hulled & diced

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine chicken and green onions. Add enough poppyseed dressing to moisten the chicken.
Spread both cut sides of rolls lightly with cream cheese.
Tear lettuce leaves to fit and place on each bottom half of roll. Place 1/4 cup chicken spread on top of lettuce. Spinkle diced strawberries on top and add top halves of rolls.

Submitter: 
HappyGrandma
Description: 
My girls love these fruity sandwiches for picnics at the park. We have them for girls' lunches & showers too.
