Title:
Tropical Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/667308.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c. Olive Oil
3 Limes
1 tsp. Black Pepper
1 tsp. Dried Crushed Rosemary
3 Cloves Garlic minced
2 Jalapenos minced
Fresh Mango cut into large chunks
Fresh Pineapple cut into large chunks
Pork Loin Chops cut into large chunks
Skewers

Directions:
Combine 1/4 C. olive oil, black pepper, rosemary, garlic, jalapenos and squeeze the juice from two of the limes into the mixture.  Marinate pork in mixture for 4 hours.  After marinating, alternate the pork and fruit chunks onto the skewers.  Grill on Medium heat for about 25 minutes or until pork is cooked thoroughly.
