Title:
Cherry Popping Scotsman Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces single malt Scotch whiskey
2 fluid ounces milk
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger creme de cacao liqueur
2 maraschino cherries

Directions:
Pour Scotch over ice in a chilled old fashioned or collins glass. Fill glass with milk, and slowly pour creme de cacao over milk. Garnish with 2 maraschino cherries.

Submitter: 
Anonymous
Description: 
Single malt Scotch is smoothed out with creme de cacao and milk, and two cherries are popped upon the top.
