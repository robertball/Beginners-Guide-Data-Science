Title:
Momma's Chicken Vegetable Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs Chicken Tenderloins
1Tbsp Oil
2 Sliced Medium White or Yellow Onions
1 16 oz bag frozen Vegetables California
1/4 cup Frozen Corn
1/4 cup Frozen Peas
2 Stalks 2in cut Celery
2Ibs Chopped Potatoes
1 can Cream of Celery or Cream of Mushroom
1Tsp Salt
1/4 Tsp Parsley or Basil or 1Tsp Chives
2Tsp Lemon Juice

Directions:
1. Heat oil in a large stock pot. Add chicken, onions, and celery. Sautee until chicken is lightly browned.
2. Add the rest of the ingredients and fill pot with water until vegetables are almost covered.
3. Heat to a rolling boil, then simmer for 45 minutes on low.

Submitter: 
Alicia Taylor
Description: 
My supermarket regularly has frozen chicken tenderloin cuts for sale, so I make this stew often.
