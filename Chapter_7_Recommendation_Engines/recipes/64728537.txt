Title:
Snack It Up Appetizer Board Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6178750.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bunches red seedless grapes
1- 2.75 oz Energy Blend Nuts
2 small apples
1- 3oz buttercrisp party pack crackers
1- 3oz Havarit Cheese Tiles
1- 10oz Colby Jack Sliced Cheese
1/2 seedless cucumber sliced
3 oz Spanish Manranilla Pimento Stuffed Olives
11.5 fl oz Sweet Pickles

Directions:
Using a 20 x 14 large cutting board arrange snacks to your liking. Place olives in a 2" x 16" Olive tray and place pickles in a 6 1/4" x 3 1/2" rectangle dish

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
Easy put together board of appetizers for continuous snacking
