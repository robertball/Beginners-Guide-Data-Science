Title:
Healthy Chocolate Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients:
1 frozen peeled banana
2 tbsp. chocolate milk powder
1/2 cup of soy or almont milk- vanilla flavored or original

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a blender.
Blend them
Voila!
