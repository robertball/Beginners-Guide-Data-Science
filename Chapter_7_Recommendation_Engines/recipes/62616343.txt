Title:
Jell-O Pudding Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Â½ cups cold milk
2 packages (4 serving size each) Jell-O Oreo flavor instant pudding and pie filling
1 tub (8oz) Cool Whip whipped topping, thawed, divided
1 Oreo pie crust (6oz)
5 Oreo chocolate sandwich cookies, halved

Directions:
Pour milk into large bowl. Add dry pudding mixes. Beat with a wire whisk for 2 minutes
or until well blended. Gently stir in half of the whipped topping. Pour into pie crust.
Refrigerate for 4 hours or until set. Garnish with remaining whipped topping and cookie
halves just before serving. Store leftover pie in the refrigerator.
