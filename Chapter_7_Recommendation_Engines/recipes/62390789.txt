Title:
Peach Pecan Upside down cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp maple syrup
3/4 lb fresh or canned peaches, pitted and sliced
1/2 cup butter or margerine
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 1/4 cups flour
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
a pinch of cloves
1/3 cup chopped pecans
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
grease a 9 inch round cake tin and put a round of waxed paper in the bottom.  drizzle the syrup over the paper and arrange the peaches over top.
Cream butter and sugar, beat in eggs.
Sift together flour, baking powder and spices.
Add dry mixture to creamed mixture and mix until just combined, mix in milk to make a soft dropping consistancy and genly fold in pecans.
Spoon batter over peaches and bake in a preheated 400 F oven for 35 - 40 minutes until golden brown.
Invert onto serving plate and serve warm.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
sweet and juicy peaches atop a light fluffy cake with crunchy pecan bits and warm spices, excellent with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
