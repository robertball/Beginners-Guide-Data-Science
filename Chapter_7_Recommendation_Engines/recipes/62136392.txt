Title:
Summer antipasto picnic salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Mozzarella cheese, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
2(6.5oz) jars quartered marinated artichoke hearts(including liquid)
2(6oz) cans pitted large olives,drained
2 bell peppers, cored and cubed ( any color)
1(8oz) chub italian dry salami, cut into 1/2 cubes
1 cup cherry or grape tomatoes
1/4 cup sliced fresh basil

Directions:
Toss together all ingredients in a large bowl. Cover and refrigerate for several hours to let marinate, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
tanya
Description: 
This is a yummy combo of salami,veggies,olives and basil.Great for picnics because no mayo, and it can be made ahead of time.Very versatile also, you can add whaterver you like. You can also drain the artichokes and use your favorite italian dressing.
