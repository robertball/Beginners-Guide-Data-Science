Title:
The Perfect Lemon Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 TBSP lemon juice
1 TBSP boiling water
2 TBSP butter, melted
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In small bowl, combine sugar, lemon juice, butter, and vanilla.  Gradually add water and mix until smooth.  Spread over cooled cake.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This lemon glaze is perfect for cakes and pastries, and can easily be changed to make an orange or lime glaze.
