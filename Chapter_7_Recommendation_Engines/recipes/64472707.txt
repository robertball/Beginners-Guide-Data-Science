Title:
Peanut Butter Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C half and half
3 T sugar
1/4 C light Karo
1 t vanilla
1/2 C creamy peanut butter

Directions:
1. Combine half and half, sugar, and Karo syrup in saucepan over medium heat, stirring until sugar is dissolved and sauce is smooth.
2. Let it cool for 20 minutes. Add vanilla and peanut butter and stir until smooth.
