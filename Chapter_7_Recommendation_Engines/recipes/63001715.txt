Title:
Cool & Crunchy Chicken Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749025.jpg
Ingredients:
12 hard yellow corn taco shells
1 small head romaine lettuce 2 cups shredded
1-2 limes, divided
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp cayenne
pepper (optional)
3 cups diced grilled chicken breasts
2 tbsp fresh cilantro
Chipotle salsa
1/2 small jicama*
1 small mango*
1 avocado*

Directions:
Grill chicken
Prepare taco shells according to package. Set aside.
Shred lettuce. Zest limes to measure 1 tsp. juice limes to measure 3 tbsp.
Mix zest, 1 tbsp of juice, mayo, cumin, and cayenne pepper. Spoon about 1/3 cup of mixture in a plastic bag and set aside.
Add remaining sauce to chicken.
Spread avocado in shells and place lettuce into taco shells. Top evenly with chicken mixture. Trim corner of sauce filled bag and drizzle over tacos.
Enjoy!
