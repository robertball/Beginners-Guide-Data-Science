Title:
Grandma's Ambrosia Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14oz can of crushed pineapple(save juice)
1/4 c. pineapple juice
1 14oz can of fruit cocktail, drained
8 oz cream cheese
1 tub Cool Whip
6 tsp sugar
2 c. pecans
1 c. shredded coconut

Directions:
Whip pineapple juice together with cream cheese until blended well.  Add all remaining ingredients.  Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Kristine Skaggs Frisone
Description: 
This is a deliciously, creamy fruit salad that my grandma always served at holidays.  She also called it Cloud Nine.
