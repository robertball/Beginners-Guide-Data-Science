Title:
Nasi Goreng with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rice
2 cups water
3 tbsp. soy sauce
1 tsp. sesame oil
2 chicken breast, cubed
3 tbsp. vegetable oil
1 carrot, thinly diced
1/3 cup white onion chopped
2 cloves garlic
2 green onions
1/3 cup mushrooms, chopped (optional)
2 tbsp. ketchup
1 tbsp. sirachi sauce
2 eggs

Directions:
Cook rice in water, adding 1 tbsp. of soy sauce and 1 tsp. sesame oil. Let rice sit for 3-4 hours, or use day old rice if available. Put chicken breast into hot pan with oil and allow outsides to turn white. Then, add carrots, onion, garlic, and mushrooms, cooking until onions have become translucent. Add remaining 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, ketchup, sirachi sauce. top with rice and stir for 3-5 minutes to break up rice if it is clumped together.
Push ingredients to side of pan and fry two eggs. Once eggs are cooked to your liking plate rice and top with fried egg.

Submitter: 
JessT
Description: 
When traveling in Nepal I had some of the most delicious nasi goreng I have ever tasted. This is my humble attempt at a duplication.
