Title:
Texas Chili by Puck Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1 LB Ground Turkey 1 LB Ground Sirloin 2 cans diced tomatoes 2 cans tomato sauce 2 cans chili beans 2 cans kidney beans 2 cans pinto beans 3 tbsp Worchestershire sauce 3 spoons chopped (jar) jalapeno's 1 tbsp chili powder 1 tbsp cayenne pepper 2 tbsp tobasco 1 tbsp crushed red peppers garlic powder 1/2 onion, chopped 1 can light beer black pepper

Directions:
Directions
Brown the turkey and sirloin in a pan then poor into into large pot. Add everything else to pot and simmer for about 1 hour. Adjust beans and everything else to your tastes.

This is the best chili I've created to date after years of working on it. Plenty of room for adjustments based on your tastes.
