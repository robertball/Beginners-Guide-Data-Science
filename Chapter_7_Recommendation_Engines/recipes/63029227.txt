Title:
Seven-Second Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup onion
1/6 to 1/4 jalapeno pepper
1/2 cup tomato
1-2 cloves garlic
cilantro (optional)
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup black beans

Directions:
Place all ingredients, except beans, into the Tall Cup.
Twist on Cross Blade and place cup onto Power Base
Give it several quick Pulses. Salsa should be chunky, so avoid over-processing.
Add beans.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Serve with tortilla chips, slices of Italian bread, or as a dip for triangles of quesadillas.
