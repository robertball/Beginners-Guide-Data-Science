Title:
Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/928963.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C Sugar
3 Eggs
1 tsp Vanilla
1/4 C melted Margarine
1/2 C Light Corn Syrup
dash salt
3 Tbsp Flour
1 C Pecans chopped
1/2 Pecans for top decoration
1 unbaked 9" deep dish pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Mix all ingredients except pecans. Add in cup of chopped pecans, pour into pie crust. Add 1/2 pecan pieces on top. Bake 55 to 60 minutes till set. Pie should spring back when done and still be a little jiggly. If edges or pecans start to brown too much tent foil over the top.
