Title:
Braided Cinnamon Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
125 ml warm milk
1 teaspoon sugar or honey
12 gr fresh yeast
200 gr pastry 00
100 gr bread flour
1 egg
30 g butter, melted
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Dissolve the yeast in warm milk with a teaspoon of sugar and let stand 5 minutes.
Mix the flour, add the milk, yeast, egg and melted butter. Knead by hand or in the planetary for about 10 minutes until dough is smooth, soft and smooth. If necessary, adjust with more flour or milk.
Let rise covered with foil about 30 ° for 1 hour and a half, I left the bowl with the dough in the oven at 50 ° C and allowed to cool.
For the filling whisk with a fork the butter with the sugar and cinnamon.
Deflate the dough spread it with a rectangular shape to 1 cm thickness and spread the filling on the surface. Keep a nut aside.
Roll with a knife and cut the roll in the middle in the longitudinal direction. Hold together the upper end to facilitate subsequent procedures.
Braiding taking up the part with the cut and join the two ends so as to form a crown. Spread with remaining filling.
Bake at 200 degrees for about 30-35 minutes.
Remove from oven and let cool on a wire rack or eat warm.

Submitter: 
Gregg Hollander
Description: 
Found on an Italian website titled kringel Estonia which I take to mean Estonian-style cake. I've chosen to simplify it to Braided Cinnamon Bread because I think that is more culturally relevant and out of fear of calling it
