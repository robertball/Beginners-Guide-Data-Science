Title:
Home made Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5585023.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Box Pillsbury roll out pie crust
1 Cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
Salt & pepper
2½ Cups cooked chicken
1 can Cream of chicken soup
Can or Veg-All

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°. Put one pie crust in pie dish. Mix cheese, salt & pepper, chicken & cream or chicken soup together. Put mixture in pie crust that's in dish. Roll out other pie crust on top & foldor pinch edges of both crusts together. Bake for one hour. Take out & let sit to cool a little bit (abt 45mins) to set up. ENJOY!!!!

Submitter: 
Brittany Harris
Description: 
The best home made chicken pot pie. Not necessarily quick fixing but it's a meal in one!! I don't always add mixed veggies it's good with or without!!
