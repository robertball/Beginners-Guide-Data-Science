Title:
Bread Machine Croissants Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup 80 degree water
3 cups bread flour ( can also do 1 1/2 cup wheat 1 1/2 cup white)
2 tablespoons dry milk
3 teaspoons sugar
3/4 teaspoons salt
3 tablespoons butter in 6 pieces
2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Mix all ingredients into bread machine. Set to kneed only. After dough has kneeded place on floured surface and roll out to 15 in. x 12 in. rectangle. Cut into 3rds lengthwise and crosswise. Roll into croissants. Cover and let rise 30 minutes before baking. Bake 10 minutes at 325 degrees.
