Title:
Strawberry-Melon Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup lemon yogurt
2 cups watermelon balls
1 teaspoon lemon juice
2 cups cantaloupe balls
1 tablespoon honey
2 cups halved strawberries

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice and honey and blend well.
In a large bowl, combine remaining ingredients. Pour dressing over fruit and toss very gently to coat.

Submitter: 
Drizzler
Description: 
This salad is perfect for summer BBQs.  The fruit makes it tasty and healthy.
