Title:
Grandma Kennedy's Irish Whiskey Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chocolate ready-to-use pie crust
5 egg yolks
1 envelope unflavored gelatin
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup cold water
1/2 cup Irish Whiskey, divided
1 1/2 cups heavy cream, divided
shaved chocolate for garnish

Directions:
Beat egg yolks until they are thick and lemon colored. Slowly beat in the sugar. Soften gelatin in water and then add 1/4 cup Irish whiskey. Heat over boiling water until gelatin disolves. Add gelatin mixture into the egg yolks, stirring quickly. Pour in the remaining Irish whiskey. Beat the cream until soft peaks form.  Fold in 1 cup of whipped cream to mixture. Pour filling into pie shell and chill. When the filling is set, decorate with a border of the remaining whipped cream and sprinkle shaved chocolate over the center.

Description: 
This tasty dessert is a must for St. Patrick's day celebrations!  It's a delicious and unique pie that will get rave reviews every time you serve it!  I like mine with a sip or two of Irish Cream.
