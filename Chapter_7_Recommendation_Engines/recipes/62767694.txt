Title:
Floyd Eudy's Henriettas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough Ingredients
1/2 C. Oleo
1 C. Sugar
2 Egg Yolks
1 C. Flour, Sifted
1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
A Few Grains of Salt
1/2 tsp. Vanilla
Topping Ingredients
2 Egg Whites, Beaten Stiff
1/2 C. Brown Sugar
1 tsp. Vanilla
1 C. Finely Chopped Nuts

Directions:
Cream oleo and Sugar until light and fluffy.  Add egg yolks and beat well, using lower speed.  Gradually add flour, baking powder, and salt. Add vanilla.  This makes a stiff dough. Press into shallow 9" X 13" baking pan.
To make topping
Beat egg whites with brown sugar, nuts and vanilla, then spread over the dough.
Bake at 325 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes. Let cool in pan, Cut into squares.
