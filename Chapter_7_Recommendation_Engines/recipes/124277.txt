Title:
Thousand Island Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1002404.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups mayonnaise
1/4 cup chili sauce
1/4 cup pickle relish

Directions:
In a bowl, combine all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate

Submitter: 
Darlis  Wilfer
Description: 
'It's almost unbelievable that a dressing so easy to fix can be so good,' says Darlis Wilfer, Phelps, Wisconsin. Darlis got the recipe from her daughter, Debbi.
