Title:
Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks (8 ounces) butter
2 cups flour
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
4 beaten eggs
2 cups sugar
4 T flour
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 T finely grated lemon peel
sifted confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325°
Blend butter, 2 cups flour and 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar.
Pat into ungreased 13x9x2-inch pan. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes.
Blend eggs, sugar, 4 T flour, lemon juice, and lemon peel.
Pour over first layer. Return to oven and bake at 325° for 20 minutes.
Cut into bars and sift confectioners' sugar over the top while warm.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
One of my most favorite treats. Tart and sweet with a delicious butter-cookie crust.
