Title:
Breta's 7 Layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/871495.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can refried beans
1 (9 ounce) can hot bean dip (such as FritosÂ® Brand)
6 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
1 cup salsa
1 1/2 cups sour cream
1 cup finely shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (4 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
2 tomatoes, diced
4 green onions, chopped

Directions:
Mix refried beans with hot bean dip in a bowl; spread the bean mixture in an even layer over a pizza pan.
Mix avocados with salsa in a bowl; spread mixture over the bean mixture. Place dollops of sour cream over avocado layer and spread into an even layer; top with shredded Cheddar cheese. Sprinkle black olives, diced tomatoes, and green onions over the dip.
Chill at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Kat Sorenson
Description: 
Beans, guacamole, sour cream and cheese layered to perfection! I am known for my 7 layer dip ever since I've been making this. Serve with your favorite tortilla chips.
