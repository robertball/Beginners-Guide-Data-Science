Title:
Mexican Shrimp Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/2 cup finely chopped green onion
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
1 1/2 cups tomato and clam juice cocktail
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce, or to taste
1/4 cup prepared horseradish
salt to taste
1 ripe avocado - peeled, pitted and chopped
1-2 jalapeno peppers, chopped
1/4 t. garlic powder

Directions:
Place the shrimp in a large bowl. Stir garlic, green onion, jalapeno and cilantro. Mix in tomato and clam juice cocktail, ketchup, lime juice, hot pepper sauce, and horseradish. Season with salt. Gently stir in avocado. Cover, and refrigerate 2 to 3 hours. Serve in one large bowl or ladle into individual bowls.

Submitter: 
Buffy Ehlers Whitney
Description: 
Cooked shrimp are chilled in a spicy tomato-juice cocktail with avocado, green onion, jalapeno pepper and cilantro.Modified from: Mexican Shrimp Cocktail
