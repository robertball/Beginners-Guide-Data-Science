Title:
Suzanne's AMAZING Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
3 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
2 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
3 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/8 tsp. ground cloves
1/8 tsp. ground ginger
3 cups grated carrots
8oz. can crushed pineapple in own juice (drained)
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease and flour 9x13 pan.
In a large bowl beat together eggs, oil, white and brown sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves,& ginger.
Beat in baking soda, baking powder, & salt.
Beat in flour.
Stir in carrots & pineapple.
Fold in walnuts.
Pour into prepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven 50-60 minutes or until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
Cool completely on wire rack and frost.
Bake in preheated oven 50-60 minutes

Submitter: 
Suzanne
Description: 
I've tried many carrot cakes, and none have been as good as this recipe.  Everyone flips over this carrot cake.  I frost it with Cream Cheese frosting.
