Title:
Lisa's Best Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2           cups  All-purpose flour
2          teaspoons  Baking soda
1           teaspoon  Salt
1/2      teaspoon  Baking powder
3               cups  Sugar
1           teaspoon  Cinnamon
1           teaspoon  Pumpkin pie spice
1/2           cup  Cooking oil
4                     Eggs
2 1/2           cups  Canned pumpkin
1/4           cup  Water

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl combine the flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder, sugar, cinnamon, and pumpkin pie spice.
In a seperate bowl, mix by hand the oil, eggs, pumpkin, and water.
Combine with the dry ingredients.
Bake in 9x5 loaf pans in a 350 oven for 90 minutes, checking after 1 hour.

Submitter: 
LCLARKE
Description: 
A moist and delicious quickbread, perfect for Fall.  This bread gets rave reviews from family and friends, and it's extremely versatile - I often replace some or all of the pumpkin with other fruits on hand and it's always delicious.
