Title:
SHRIMP EGGS FOO YUNG Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. bean sprouts, cleaned and drained
small amount of cooking oil + 1 Tbsp.
1 slice  ginger root, minced
6 chopped green onions
1 stalk very thinly sliced celery
1 c. shredded cooked shrimp or other fish or finely diced cooked meat
6 well-beaten eggs
1 tsp. salt
Â¼ tsp. freshly ground black pepper
soy sauce to taste

Directions:
Combine the eggs, salt and pepper and have them ready to mix with the other ingredients.
Heat small amount of oil in skillet and stir-fry the giner root, onions, celery, and meat/fish until translucent and crisp.
Add the egg mixture.
Heat the additional Tbsp. of oil in another small skillet.
Drop the mixture into it to form small omelets, golden brown on both sides.
Serve with soy sauce.

Submitter: 
Joan Wagner Teller
Description: 
Can be made with meat instead of fish. Best served with soy sauce.
