Ingredients:
3/4 cup Marzetti速 Honey Dijon Mustard Dressing
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, halved
1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees  F. Dip chicken breasts in dressing, until well coated. Combine crumbs and cheese; coat chicken. Bake 50 to 60 minutes, or until chicken is tender.
