Title:
Boozy Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 2 C. Flour (Sifted)
- 1 Tsp. Baking Soda
- 1 Tsp. Salt
- 1 Stick of Butter (Softened)
- 3/4 C. Brown Sugar
- 2 Eggs (or 1/2 cup of Egg Beaters)
- 5-6 Over Ripened Nanners (Mashed)
- Few drops of Almond Extract
- Few Drops of Amaretto Liqueur (99 Bananas might be better!)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350' - Spray Loaf Pan with Pam
Mix sifted Flour, Salt, & Baking Soda in bowl
Mix Butter, Mashed Bananas, Brown Sugar, Eggs and Booze/Extract in seperate Bowl
Add two mixtures together just enough to get all the flour wet. Put in Loaf Pan
Cook in preheated oven 350' for 60 minutes. Let cool. Serve with butter.
