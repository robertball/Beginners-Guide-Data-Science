Title:
Crixxie's Ranch Chicken Nuggets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Italian breadcrumbs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 cup prepared Ranch salad dressing
1 egg beaten
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
Additional Ranch dressing

Directions:
In a shallow bowl, combine the first five ingredients. Place dressing and egg in another bowl. Toss chicken cubes in dressing and egg mixture, then roll in the cornflake mixture. Place in a greased 11-in. x 7-in. x 2-in. baking pan.
Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees for 12-15 minutes or until juices run clear. Serve with additional dressing for dipping.
