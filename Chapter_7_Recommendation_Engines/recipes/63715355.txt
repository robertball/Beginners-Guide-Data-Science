Title:
Provencial Beans and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz fingerling potatoes, quartered lengthwise
12 oz fresh green beans, trimmed
2 tbsp kalamata olive juice
1/4 cup kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
3 tbsp Nocoise vinaigrette

Directions:
Cook potatoes in large pot of boiling water until fork tender, 5-8 minutes.  Transfer to a strainer and drain.  Boil beans in same water until crisp tender, 5 minutes.
Toss potatoes with olives and olive juice; let sit 10 minutes.  Add beans and vinaigrette, then toss to coat and season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
Perfect with Tuna Nocoise sandwiches.
