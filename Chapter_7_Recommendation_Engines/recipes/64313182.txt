Title:
Chicken and Biscuit Casserole-revised Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped baby carrots
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons white sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
5 cups chicken broth
1/4 cup peas, drained
4 cups diced, cooked chicken meat
10 Pillsbury biscuits-baked following directions
Rice

Directions:
Salt and pepper celery, carrots, onion, garlic and saute with half of butter until soft.  Remove to bowl.  Put remaining butter in pot and melt.  Add flour and mix.  Slowly add liquid.  Bring pot to boil then reduce heat.  Add sugar, basil, peas, and chicken.  Cook until flavors come together.

Submitter: 
Kristi Dockter Watkins
