Title:
Spanish Style Pork Chops (Casserole) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pork chops
Pepper and salt
2 (16 oz.) cans diced or whole tomatoes
1 c. uncooked rice
1 Green pepper
1 Onion
2 tbsp. butter
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. cumin

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Prepare Rice as per directions on package (long rice - not Quick rice).
Sprinkle meat with salt and pepper. Brown meat in hot shortening; drain.
In a 13"x 9" greased baking dish, arrange meat on bottom (put drippings from pork on bottom too).  Combine rice with tomatoes, green pepper, onion, butter, salt, cumin and chili powder. Spread rice over pork chops. Bake for 1 hour or until meat is tender. Serves 4.
