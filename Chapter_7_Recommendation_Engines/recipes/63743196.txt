Title:
Pear Honey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1   20-ounce can crushed pineapple with syrup (not juice)
16 cups (about 6 pounds) peeled, cored, and chopped pears
10 cups sugar
1 inch piece ginger, peeled and grated

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a heavy pot and cook until pears are tender and mixture thickens, approximately 30 minutes.  Ladle into sterilized jars and seal while mixture is hot.

Submitter: 
Karen Waters
Description: 
I found the original recipe in a Paula Deen cook book several years ago. I changed it adding the grated ginger. I make this as holiday gifts for my neighbors. My family helps with the making of the pear honey and assembling the gift baskets. Great family time.
