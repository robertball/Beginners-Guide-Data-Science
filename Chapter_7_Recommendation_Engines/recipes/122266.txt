Title:
Skillet Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979451.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
3 cups water
2 (11.5 ounce) cans tomato juice
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
2 tablespoons dried minced onion
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 (7 ounce) package spaghetti
grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink; drain. Add the water, tomato juice, tomato paste and seasonings. Bring to a boil. Add uncooked spaghetti. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes or until the spaghetti is tender, stirring frequently. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if desired.

Submitter: 
Sr. Mary Maurice
Description: 
This has always been a favorite dish of our Sisters who work in a hospital. When we get back to the convent, it's a quick and easy recipe we've used for years. Served with Italian bread and a tossed salad, it makes an excellent meal.
