Title:
Jarlsberg Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb brick of jarlsberg cheese ( a variety of swiss)
2 medium vidalia or sweet onions
Approximately 2 cups of Hellmans mayonaise
7 Tbls. Butter
Paprika
( None of these ingredients can be substituted !)

Directions:
Grate the cheese and set aside.
Slice the onions very thin and simmer in butter until transparent, set aside to cool.
Mix onions, mayonaise and cheese in a large bowl the spread into 2 shallow glass baking dishes. (like a pie pan or quiche dish)
Sprinkle the top very liberally with paprika.
Bake at 350 for 25 to 30 mins.  The dip will puff up about one half of an inch as it cooks.
Serve hot with sturdy crackers
