Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 or 3 cups of kidney or pinto beans
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 lb. ground chuck
1 medium onion
1 tbls minced garlic
1 beef boullion cube
10 ounces water
14 1/2 ounce can crushed tomatoes
1 tbls sugar
1 small can tomato sauce
1 or 2 tbls chili powder
1 tbls vinegar

Directions:
Saute onion and beef in 1 teaspoon olive oil. Drain off fat. Add beef boullion and water. Add cumin and garlic. Blend tomatoes and sugar. Add tomato sauce, beans, and other seasonings. Simmer for at least 1 hour. Better if simmered for 2 hours in slow cooker.

My grandmother's chili recipe - great on a cold night.
