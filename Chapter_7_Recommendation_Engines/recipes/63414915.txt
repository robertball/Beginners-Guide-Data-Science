Title:
Raspberry Vodka Jelly Jiggler Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 envelopes (1/4 oz. each) KNOX Unflavored Gelatin
1 Tbsp Splenda
1 1/4 cups cold Smirnoff raspberry flavored vodka, divided
1 cup cold lemon-lime soda
1 1/4 cups  boiling water
2 pkg (.3 oz each) JELL-O Raspberry Flavor Sugar Free Gelatin

Directions:
Mix unflavored gelatin and Splenda in a medium microwaveable bowl until blended. Stir in soda. Let stand 3 minutes for gelatin to soften.
Microwave on high for three minutes, stirring each minute, until gelatin and Splenda are dissolved.
Let mixture cool for five minutes, then stir in one cup of vodka.
Pour into 8-inch square dish sprayed with cooking spray and refrigerate for 30 minutes or until set but not firm.
Meanwhile, add boiling water to raspberry gelatin mixes in separate medium bowl; stir 2 minutes until completely dissolved. Let cool slightly, then stir in remaining cold vodka. Cool 10 min.
Pour raspberry gelatin gently over clear gelatin layer in dish. Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm. Cut into 16 squares. Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.
