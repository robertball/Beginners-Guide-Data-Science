Title:
British-inspired BBQ burger with cheese beer sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cheddar beer sauce
2 T butter, unsalted
2 T all purpose flour
1/2 t powdered mustard
1 t worchestershire sauce
1/2 t kosher salt
1/2 t freshly ground pepper
1/2 c porter beer, plus more to thin if necessary
2/3 c milk or cream
2 c shredded cheddar cheese
2 drops hot sauce, like tobasco
burger patties
1-1/2 pounds of medium ground beef
salt and pepper
4 large eggs, optional
4 burger buns
4 tomato slices
4 large lettuce leaves

Directions:
cheddar beer sauce
in a medium saucepan over low heat melt butter and whisk in flour, cook, whisking constantly for about 2 minutes, careful not to brown flour
whisk in mustard, worchestershire sauce, salt and pepper until smooth - add beer to combine
pour in milk/cream and whisk until combined and smooth, about 4 - 5 minutes
add hot sauce, remove from heat and allow to cool. refrigerate if not using immediately - to thin for serving, microwave for 10-15 seconds
burger patties
mix ground beef with salt and pepper, form thinner patties than normal, use top to make pocket in beef, fill with room temperature bacon jam - see separate recipe - press some more beef on top and press to seal.  repeat with all 4 burger patties, put in frig if not using immediately
heat bbq - cook patties until cooked through, toast your buns lightly, meanwhile fry 4 eggs and set aside - optional
to assemble burger, place lettuce on bottom bun, put a slice of tomato on top of lettuce, top with burger patty, top with fried egg and then spoon cheddar beer sauce over top and top with top bun

this bbq burger with a nod to the brits, filled with bacon jam and topped with a cheddar cheese sauce - decadent
