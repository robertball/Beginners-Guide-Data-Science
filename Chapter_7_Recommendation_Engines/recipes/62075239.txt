Title:
Sweet and Sour Baked Beans in a crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1 1/2 lbs. kielbasa cut into chunks
1 16 oz. can chunk pineapple, drained
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 large cans Bush's Baked beans
1 medium onion, diced

Directions:
Put all ingredients in the crockpot, cook on low 5-6 hours (may be left in longer if needed).

Submitter: 
Nancy Toth
Description: 
Very simple and quick dinner for busy Mom's and Dad's. I have brought this to many parties and I am always asked for the recipe. There are never any leftovers.
