Title:
Bourbon Street Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/831040.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bell pepper, julienned
1/2 sweet onion, julienned
2 lbs of chicken breasts, cubed
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 cup water
1/4 apple juice
1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 Tbsp ketchup
1 Tbsp apple cidar vinegar

Directions:
Heat saute pan and add 1 Tbsp of olive oil. Saute the bell peppers and onions for about 5 mins until slightly soft. Remove from the pan and set aside.
Add 1 Tbsp of olive oil to the saute pan and brown chicken cubes.
Once the chicken is browned, add all remaining ingredients, including the sauteed veggies.
Bring the sauce to a hard boil and continue boiling for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally until the sauce has thickened. Serve over steamed rice and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Misty Darnell
Description: 
This is a really easy and healthy dish. A nice change from all the same old chicken recipes! It is sweet and mildly spicey. It is delicious over steamed rice!
