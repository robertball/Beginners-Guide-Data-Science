Title:
Broccoli Cheese Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp butter
3 eggs, beaten
1 cup milk
1 cup all purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 head of fresh broccoli, cut up small & steamed (approx. 3-4 minutes)
1/4 cup onion, diced
4 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place butter in a 9 x 13 pan and put in preheating oven until melted; remove pan from oven and tilt to coat bottom and set aside.
In a bowl, stir together eggs, milk, flour and baking powder; fold in broccoli, onion & cheese.
Spread into the buttered pan.
Bake at 350* for 30-35 minutes.
Cut into squares to serve.
Makes 12-15 servings.

Submitter: 
KGora
Description: 
This easy broccoli recipe will have even the pickiest broccoli eaters asking for seconds. Great side dish to your fancy holiday dinners or your weeknight family meals. Also makes a yummy appetizer.
