Title:
Amaretto Eggnog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4341268.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup eggnog
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger amaretto liqueur

Directions:
Pour eggnog into a microwave-safe mug. Heat in the microwave until warmed through, 30 seconds to 1 minute. Stir in amaretto liqueur.

Submitter: 
kellieann
Description: 
Amaretto-spiked eggnog, perfect pick-me-up for the holiday season!
