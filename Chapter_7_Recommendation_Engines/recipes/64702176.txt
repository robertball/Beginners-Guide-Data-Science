Title:
Hearty Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5391100.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Large Cans Chicken Broth (49 oz each)
¼ Cup Flour
¼ Cup Butter
2 Stalks Celery, Chopped
4 Medium Sized Carrots, Peeled and Medium Diced
1 Tbsp Poultry Seasoning
1.5-2 lbs Diced Chicken Breast, Boneless Skinless
32  40 oz Frozen Egg Noodles

Directions:
On medium heat in a large pot, melt butter and whisk in flour to make a roux. Cook until the flour mixture starts to turn golden.
Add Carrots and Celery, mix in the roux and cook for 5 minutes with the lid on.
Remove the lid and add diced chicken and poultry seasoning.
Cook chicken until cooked through.
Add Chicken Broth. Turn to medium high and bring to a boil, stirring periodically.
Once at a boil, add noodles, time for 16 minutes.
Turn soup to low and continue to cook for 5 minutes.
Make sure to cool to a comfortable temperature before serving.

Submitter: 
ToriGeib
Description: 
This recipe makes a large amount, which is nice because it freezes well for future meals. As I mentioned above, you can also serve it over mashed potatoes or add more veggies to it to make it your own.
