Title:
Grandma's Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs of meatloaf mix (ground beef, pork and veal)
2 c italian bread crumbs
2 large eggs
2 tbsp garlic powder
2 tbsp parsley flakes
1 cup Romano cheese
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Add ingredients in a large bowl and mix very well with your hands.  Roll into 2 inch meatballs.  Place on baking sheet and bake for 18-20 mins, turning meatballs half way.

Submitter: 
Nichole Phillips McVicker
Description: 
My grandmother has been making these since I was a little girl.  It's the best meatball recipe I have ever come across.
