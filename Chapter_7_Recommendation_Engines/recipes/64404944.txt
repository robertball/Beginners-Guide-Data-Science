Title:
Fresh Tabbouleh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup lemon juice
2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 cup olive oil
1 cup hot water
3/4 cup whole wheat couscous
2 cups chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
2 cup diced tomato
1/2 cup diced green bell pepper
1 cup peeled and diced cucumber
6 green onions, thinly sliced
1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint

Directions:
Whisk lemon juice, kosher salt, in a bowl. Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking rapidly until dressing is thick and creamy.
Pour hot water over couscous in a bowl; let soak until water is absorbed and couscous is soft, about 30 minutes.
Mix parsley, tomato, green bell pepper, cucumber, green onions, and mint together in a large bowl. Add couscous; toss to combine.
Drizzle dressing over mixture; toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Deborah Terrill
Description: 
This fresh tabbouleh, made with bulgur, tomatoes, mint, and cucumbers has a refreshingly light dressing, perfect for warm summer nights.  Modified for Weight Watchers - 4 Point Plus per serving.Modified from: Fresh Tabbouleh
