Title:
Pumpkin Honey Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup softened butter
1/2 cup honey
3 TBS pumpkin puree
1 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
With a mixer whip butter for one minute or until fluffy. In another bowl combine the remaining ingredients and mix well. Add to the butter mixture and whip together to combine. Chill overnight in the fridge to allow flavors to combine. Enjoy~

Submitter: 
Namaste Mama
Description: 
This is a super yummy honey butter that is great on just about anything! Try it on pancakes, or rolls at Thanksgiving dinner!
