Title:
Healthy Green Turkey Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs olive oil
2 1/2 lbs lean ground turkey
1 tsp minced fresh garlic
2/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 1/2 tbs onion powder or 1/2 cup sliced scallions
2 1/2 tsps powdered chicken bouillon
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 cans green beans, juice reserved
1 cup reserved green bean juice
2 small cans mild green chilis
1 tsp chopped fresh garlic
1 1/2 tbs onion powder or 3 tbs sliced scallions
1 1/2 tbs sugar or honey
1 1/2 tsp chili powder
1 tsp ground cumin
2 tsp chopped fresh cilantro
1 tsp lime juice
Dash Tabasco
12 corn tortillas, warmed in microwave
1 cup shredded mexican blend cheese
Diced tomatoes and sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Brown ground turkey, parsley, onion powder, bouillon, and black pepper in olive oil.
Meanwhile place green beans, 1 cup bean juice, green chilis, garlic, onion powder, sugar, chili powder, cumin, cilantro, lime and Tabasco in a blender or food processor and blend until smooth.
In a skillet over meduim-low heat, warm 1/2 cup of green sauce and take one tortilla at a time and dip it in the sauce coating both sides. Place dipped tortilla on a plate and add a spoonful of turkey meat in a single line accross tortilla and roll up forming an enchilada.
Repeat untill all 12 tortillas are formed - placing three enchiladas per plate.
Top enchiladas with shredded mexican blend cheese.
Microwave each plate of enchiladas until cheese melts, about 1 minute.
Serve with chopped tomatoes and sour cream on top if desired.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
This is a delicious low calorie enchilada recipe that combines green beans, green chilis, and spices for the sauce. Its fast and easy to prepare.
