Title:
Cali Chile Relleno Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large poblano peppers (can also use pasilla peppers or Hatch green chiles)
3 tbsp butter
1/3 cup chopped onion
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp ground cumin
3 cups chicken broth - low sodium
Salt and pepper
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into 1/2 inch pieces or smaller
8 ounces cream cheese
3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
*1/4 tsp of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes (depending on the level of spiciness in your peppers)
For garnish:
Fresh chopped cilantro
Sliced avocado

Directions:
1.  Roast poblano peppers until skin is charred and blistered.  Place poblanos in a ziplock bag and seal. Once cool, the skins should peel right off. Finely mince the peppers and set aside.
2.  In a Dutch Oven over medium heat, melt butter. Add onion and cook, stirring frequently, until translucent, about 5 minutes.
3.  Add garlic, onion powder and cumin, and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
4.  Stir in chicken broth and season to taste with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to a simmer.
5.  Add chicken pieces and continue to cook until chicken is cooked through, about 10 minutes.  Stir in poblano peppers.
6.  Add cream cheese and stir until thoroughly incorporated.
7.  Add three cups of the cheddar cheese, stir until completed melted.
8.  Top with fresh cilantro and sliced avocado
Serving Hint:  serve in oven safe bowls and sprinkle each with shredded cheddar cheese. Sit briefly under the broiler until cheese is melted and browned.
