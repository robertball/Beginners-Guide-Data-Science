Title:
Grape Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp grape juice
1 vanilla Greek yogurt
1/8 teaspoon honey

Directions:
Stir all ingredients together in a bowl.
Serve with chunks of fruit, like apple chunks.

Submitter: 
2004eb
Description: 
Great dip with apple slices. This is not cooked or baked in any way.
