Title:
Perfect Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb sack of golden potatoes, washed and diced
garlic powder to taste
salt to taste
fresh cracked pepper to taste
6 boiled eggs, peeled and diced
1/2 cup green onions (chopped, whites included)
1/2 cup finely diced red onion
1/3 cup unfiltered apple cider vinegar
2 large garlic cloves, pureed or mashed
2 tbsp granulated sugar
1/2 cup yellow mustard
1 cup mayonnaise
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boiled diced potatoes in a pot with the garlic powder, salt and pepper.
When tender, drain and refrigerate in a large bowl until not not (warm or cold is fine)
In a bowl, mix garlic, sugar, vinegar, mustard and mayonnaise until thoroughly combined.
Bring potatoes out and add green onions, red onions and eggs. Pour mayonnaise mixture over the top and toss lightly with hands to coat all ingredients evenly.
Add salt and pepper if needed.
Cool and enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Sara Perry
Description: 
Don't let the fact that it has no dairy fool you, this potato salad is tangy, sweet and crunchy... just perfect.
