Title:
Rajma Masala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp canola oil
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 15 oz can tomatoes
1 1/2 cans (about two cups) kidney beans
2 tsp grated fresh ginger
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp garam masala
1/4 tp crushed red pepper flakes
1-2 Tbsp lemon juice
3 Tbsp chopped cilantro
2 cups cooked brown rice

Directions:
Heat oil in  skillet over medium heat.  Saute onion and bell pepper until soft.  Add garlic and ginger and cook another minute or two.  Add all remaining ingredients except lemon juice, cilantro and rice.  Simmer about 20-30 minutes.  Stir in lemon juice and cilantro and serve over rice.
