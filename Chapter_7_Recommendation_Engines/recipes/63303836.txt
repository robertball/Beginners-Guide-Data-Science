Title:
Honey Mustard Dressing II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
1 tablespoon honey
1/2 tablespoon paprika

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, mustard, honey, and paprika. Store covered in the refrigerator.
It should have a light consistancy.
