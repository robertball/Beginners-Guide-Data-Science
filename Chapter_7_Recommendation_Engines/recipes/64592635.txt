Title:
Sourdough Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups buttermilk
3 tbsp. canola oil
1 1/2 tbsp. sugar
3/4 tsp. soda
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
3/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Mix wet ingredients and stir in dry till just mixed and no lumps.
Fry on griddle or fry pan.
Serve with butter, syrup, bacon, sausage, fresh fruit, yogurt, coffee, juice...whatever u love for breakfast!
