Title:
Brown Sugar Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C brown sugar
Â¾ C carnation milk
1 C  butter
2 C icing sugar
1 C walnuts or peanuts (optional)

Directions:
Boil sugar, milk and butter for 10 minutes on medium heat.  Remove from heat and add icing sugar.  Beat with mixer for 5 minutes.  Add peanuts or walnuts.  Spread evenly into a 8 inch square pan.
Cooks Comments: This is a recipe of Fran Wass.  This fudge recipe has always worked for me as long as I Boil for exactly 10 minutes; and beat for exactly 5 .
