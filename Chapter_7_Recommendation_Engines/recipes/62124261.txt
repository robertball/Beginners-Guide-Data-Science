Title:
Breakfast Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pk of 12-raw, ready to cook flour tortillas
12 slices of cooked bacon
shredded cheddar cheese
6- cooked scrambled eggs

Directions:
In a deep fryer or deep frying pan, heat canola oil and keep on medium heat. Wrap the flour tortillas in a moist papertowel and microwave it only until they are plyable. Unwrap, keeping the others wrapped until you are ready to use them. Lay on a flat surface and fill the middle with egg, bacon, and shredded cheese. Seal by moistening the edges with a little water and pinch them closed, you can also use a fork. Do the same to the remaining tortillas. Fry them up till they are a nice golden brown. Serve with your favorite salsa. Enjoy!
