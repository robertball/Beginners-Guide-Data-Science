Title:
Salsa Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
60ml/4tbsp White Wine Vinegar;
1 small clove Garlic - finely chopped;
21g/Â¾oz flatleaf Parsley - finely chopped
15 Basil leaves - finely chopped;
2tbsp Spring Onion or sweet White Onion - grated;
3tbsp Capers - drained and rinsed;
1tsp Dijon Mustard
120ml/8tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil;
Salt & freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the wine vinegar, garlic, onion, capers, mustard, parsley and basil;
Gradually add the oil, pouring in slowly, whisking continuously to avoid oil/vinegar separation;
Season with salt and pepper - to taste;
Set aside at room temperature to marinate for 1 to 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A fresh, piquant, full flavoured salsa. The perfect accompaniament to Salads with grilled - griddled - BBQ steak, meat and fish dishes.
