Title:
Midwest Confetti Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/344093.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. can red kidney beans (rinsed and drained)
1/2 can yellow sweet corn (rinsed and drained)
1/2 large sweet yellow onion (diced)
1 whole avocado (diced)
3 large tomatoes (diced) or 1 large can (28 oz.) petite diced tomatoes (well drained)
1 small bunch finely chopped, fresh cilantro (approx. 4 Tablespoons)
2 Tablespoons Lime juice
1 Tsp. Salt
1 Tsp. Black Pepper

Directions:
Gently combine ingredients in large bowl, cover and refrigerate.
Tastes best served cold!

Submitter: 
ChereenH
Description: 
A mild, fresh tasting, chunky-style salsa. 
Delicious with your favorite tortilla chips, as a salad topper, a simple side dish, or to accompany your favorite Mexican entree.
For an added bit of crunch use fresh sweet corn right off the cob instead of canned.

Quick and easy!
