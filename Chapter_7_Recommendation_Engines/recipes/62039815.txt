Title:
Dad's Pineapple/Cherry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CAN PINEAPPLE CHUNKS (CRUSHED)
1 CAN CHERRY PIE FILLING
1 PACKAGE YELLOW CAKE MIX
2 STICKS MARGARINE (CUT INTO CHUNKS)

Directions:
Put crushed pineapple (with juice)into cake pan.  Spread cherry pie filling on top.  Pour dry cake mix on top and spread evenly.  Place the margarine chunks on top.  Cook in preheated 350 degree oven for 30 to 35 minutes or until cake mix is cooked.

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
I came home on leave from Germany and my dad has this delicious cake ready for me.  I like it served in a bowl with lots of milk and sugar mixed in.  I made it a few times and had great results.
