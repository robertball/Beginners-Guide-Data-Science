Title:
Seafood Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag durable tortilla chips (white Gold Tostitos)
1 jar canned alfredo sauce or similar
3 poblano peppers (seeded and diced)
1 pkg. imitation flaked crab meat
1 pkg imitation lobster
1/4 lbs shrimp
1 pkg finely shredded cheese( I use mexican)

Directions:
lay tortilla chips on a greased cookie or pizza pan.
Lightly drizzle with canned sauce until all chips are just coated.
Top with flaked crab, lobster and shrimp and then drizzle sauce over top.
Bake at 350 for 10 minutes.
After baked top with shredded cheese and peppers and what other toppings you prefer. We like guacomole and pico de gallo.
