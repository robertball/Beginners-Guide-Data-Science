Title:
Pierogi Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
pepper
4 oz butter
2 egg
8 oz sourcream
2 T chopped chives

Directions:
In a large bowl mix together the flour, salt. Make a well in the center.
Combine wet ingredents. Pour into the well of the dry ingredients. Knead dough for 8 to 10 minutes.
Cover dough and let rest for 2 hours. Roll out and fill as desired.
