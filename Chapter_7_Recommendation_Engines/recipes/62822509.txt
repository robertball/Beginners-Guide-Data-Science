Title:
Fresh Raspberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666856.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Vanilla Yogurt
1/2 Cup Fresh Raspberries
1/2 Cup Fresh Strawberries

Directions:
1.) Clean fruit and remove tops from the strawberries.
2.) Pour vanilla yogurt, raspberries, and strawberries into blender. Pulse until well blended and serve with a dollop of fresh whipped cream.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
Simple raspberry smoothie. This recipes uses fresh fruit instead of frozen. I serve mine with a dollop of homemade whipped cream to balance the flavors.
