Title:
Jan's Stay Soft Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1184729.jpg
Ingredients:
2 and 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
2 cups dark brown sugar
4 large eggs
3 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp salt
1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
3 and 1/2 cups BREAD flour
1 12 oz bags milk chocolate chips
1 12 oz bag dark chocolate chips
2 cups walnuts

Directions:
Beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
Add in vanilla, eggs, salt, baking powder and soda.  Mix until blended.
Mix flour in until fully incorporated.
Add chips, nuts and mix until combined.
Cover dough with plastic wrap and chill for 30 minutes.
Heat oven to 350.
When ready, drop 3 tablespoon sized balls of dough, 2 inches apart,  onto a cookie sheet lined with parchment or a Silpat.
Bake approximately 15 minutes until lightly brown and cookies are "set" and no longer shiny.
Let baked cookies rest about 1 minute then remove to cooling rack.
Store in a covered container.

Description: 
Chocolate chip cookies that stay soft for days. Cookies bake up looking like you bought them at the bakery. See notes on freezing dough for baking up a quick batch when cravings hit. :)
