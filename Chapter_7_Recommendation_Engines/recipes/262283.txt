Title:
Breakfast Fruit Smoothie with Kefir Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5043423.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup kefir
1/2 cup frozen blueberries
1/2 small banana
1 tablespoon almond butter
2 teaspoons honey

Directions:
Combine kefir, blueberries, banana, almond butter, and honey in a blender. Process until smooth.

Submitter: 
Adrian Izzy
Description: 
A sweet, yet tart smoothie. A healthy, energetic start to the day.
