Title:
Baked Apple Crisps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980051.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup T. Marzetti's® Old Fashioned Caramel Apple Dip
4 tablespoons butter or margarine, chilled
1 cup quick oats
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
6 large Granny Smith apples

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. For crisp mixture: Cut 3 tbsp. margarine into oats, sugar, flour, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg until crumbly. Cut 1/3 top off of apples and chop into small pieces. Core out center. Mix remaining apple pieces with crisp mixture. Spread remaining butter inside of apples. Fill apples with crisp mixture, pressing firmly. Bake 20 minutes. Top with apple dip. Bake an additional 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
T. Marzetti's® Old Fashioned Caramel Apple Dip
Description: 
Baked Granny Smith apples are filled with a sweet and crispy oatmeal mixture and covered with a sticky caramel sauce. Serve with whipped cream or ice cream for a delicious dessert.
