Title:
White Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/545350.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups self raising flour
1/2 cup white sugar
1/3 cup cocoa powder
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 1/2 cups white chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar and cocoa. In another bowl, combine eggs, milk, and butter. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Fold in the white chocolate chips.
3. Fill paper-lined muffin cups three-fourths full. Bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the centre comes out clean.
4. Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pans. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
melissa_nn
Description: 
These are like inside-out chocolate chip muffins. The generous amount of white chocolate makes them satisfying for those who have sweet tooth, but the cocoa helps to balance the sweetness. Warning: they are seriously addictive!
