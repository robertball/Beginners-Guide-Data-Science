Title:
Pineapple Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
4 eggs
1 (20 oz) can crushed pineapple drained
5 slices white bread cubed

Directions:
Preheat over 350 degrees; grease 9 inch baking pan
Cream together butter & sugar; beat in eggs one at a time; stir in pineapple & bread cubes
Bake for 1 hour

Submitter: 
XtinaU
Description: 
Sweet & buttery delicious! A must have for the holidays! Goes great with turkey or ham. Always a hit!
