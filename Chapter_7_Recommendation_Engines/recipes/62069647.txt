Title:
Apple Crisp Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 medium (Cortland) apples (peeled, cored, and sliced)
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup quick rolled oats
3/4 cup flour
3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 cup room temp. butter (or margarine) sliced and cubed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees (F).
Grease or butter a 8 or 9 inch round pie plate.
Layer apples in the pie plate to fill until slightly mounded.
In a medium bowl, cut butter into the remaining ingredients with a pastry blender (or with a knife and fork) until combined and mixture resembles small crumbs.  Sprinkle over apples.
Bake for 45-55 minutes until top is lightly golden brown and apples are tender and bubbling.
Remove from oven and allow to cool for 15 minutes.  Cut with pie server and serve warm with cream or ice cream.
Substitutions:
If desired, you may substitute apples with the following and reduce cooking time to 35-45 minutes:
Canned peaches (drained)
Canned apricots (drained)

Submitter: 
CAKEWOMAN
Description: 
This delicious dessert uses less sugar and more oats for a dense and crunchy topping.  It is served as bottomless pie in a plate or bowl, complimented with cream, whipping cream, or ice cream, and lightly garnished with sprinkled ground cinnamon.
