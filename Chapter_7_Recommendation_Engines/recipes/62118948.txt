Title:
Southwest Stuffed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
3/4 c freshly grated Monterey Jack cheese
1 can (4 oz) whole green chili peppers, torn in half (by Mexican food at the store)
1/2 c bread crumbs
1 egg white
1 T cayenne pepper
1 t. garlic salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°
Coat a baking dish with nonstick cooking spray
Mix bread crumbs with cayenne pepper and garlic salt
Put chicken between 2 pieces of plastic wrap, pound thin (about 1/4") with a meat mallet
Divide chilis and chicken between chicken pieces
Wrap chicken around filling, dip in egg white, then in breadcrumb mixture and set in baking dish
Secure with toothpicks, with the opening at the top so cheese does not bubble out
Sprinkle with leftover bread crumbs
Bake for 35-40 minutes and serve immediately

Description: 
We serve these at least a couple times per month in my house.  Pair with veggies or a southwest salad.
