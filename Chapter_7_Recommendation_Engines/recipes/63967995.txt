Title:
Artichoke Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) jar marinated artichoke hearts, drained, squeezed dry and chopped
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream
1 pinch garlic pepper
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, mix together the artichoke hearts, Parmesan cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream and garlic pepper. Place chicken in a greased baking dish, and cover evenly with artichoke mixture.
Bake, uncovered, for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
Chicken breasts, baked with Parmesan cheese and artichoke hearts, make for a quick and delicious weeknight meal.Modified from: Artichoke Chicken
