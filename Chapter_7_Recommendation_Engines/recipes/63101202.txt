Title:
FiberOneDish Breakfast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/786388.jpg
Ingredients:
The Crust:
2 cups of FiberOne Original Cereal
1 cup Country Gravy Mix
1/2 cup olive oil
2 Tablespoons minced onion or chives
1 teaspoon of pepper
Filling:
1 8 oz. package of low fat sausage
1/2 onion
4 eggs
1/8 cup water
Salt & pepper to taste
3/4 cup shredded Mexican Style Cheese

Directions:
In a food processor, blend the Fiber One and package of Country Gravy mix until very fine.
Add the olive oil, minced onion and pepper until blended well.
Empty out into a 9" pie dish and pat in place. Bake this at 350 for 15 minutes.
Remove from oven, and set aside to cool a bit. Increase oven temperature to 400 degrees.
In a skillet, brown the sausage and onion. Drain and empty onto the crust.
In a separate bowl, whip the four eggs together, and add 1/8 cup of water until frothy. Add salt and pepper. Pour the egg mix over the sausage.
Top with 3/4 cup of shredded Mexican style Cheese.
Bake for 20-25 minutes at 400. Remove, and let it rest for about 2 minutes before serving. Serves 8

Submitter: 
Kitchenista Jane
Description: 
All the yummy flavor of biscuits and gravy without all the calories! FiberOne teams up with low fat sausage and eggs to make a Onederful main dish breakfast!
