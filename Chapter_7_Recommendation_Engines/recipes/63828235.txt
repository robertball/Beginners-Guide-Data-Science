Title:
Italian Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1061905.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. extra lean ground beef
1 small onion diced
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1 1/2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1/3 cup milk
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees F.
Place beef into a mixing bowl with onion, salt, garlic salt, italian seasoning, and Worcestershire sauce. Mix well before adding milk, parmesan cheese, and bread crumbs. Mix until evenly blended the form into 1 1/2 inch meatballs. Place in a 9x13 baking dish.
Bake in pre-heated oven until no longer pink in the center, 20-25 min.
