Title:
slow cooker barbecued beef ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb beef ribs,
1 cup water
1 can tom paste
1 cup ketchup
3/4 c brown sug
1/2 c vinegar
2 tb mustard
1 tb salt
hot cooked rice

Directions:
mix all in crock pot, cook on low for 8 hours, serve over noodles or rice

Submitter:

Description: 
beef ribs with a homemade b b q sauce, in crockpot (or on top of stove - if you have the time)
