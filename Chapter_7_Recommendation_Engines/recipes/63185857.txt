Title:
SHREDDED POTATOE AU GRATIN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 CUPS SHREEDED HASH BROWN POTATOES (FROZEN ARE FINE-LET THAW
2 CUPS WHIPPING CREAM
1 1/2 CUPS SHREDDED CHEDDAR CHEESE
1/4 CUP SLICED GREEN ONIONS
1 TSP SALT
1/4 TSP BLACK PEPPER

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 350
SPRAY BOTTOM OF 4-1 CUP RAMEKINS WITH COOKING SPRAY
COMBINE THE HASH BROWNS, CREAM, 1 1/4 CUPS CHEDDAR CHEESE, ONION & SALT & PEPPER AND MIX WELL
SPOON THE POTATOE MIXTURE INTO THE RAMEKINS. SPRINKLE WITH THE REMAINING CHEESE. BAKE UNTIL BUBBLLY & GOLDEN BROWN ARUND 35-45 MINUTES
