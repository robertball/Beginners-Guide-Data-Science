Title:
Barbecued Beef Brisket Robs way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon celery seed
1/2 teaspoon pepper
5 pounds fresh beef brisket*, trimmed
SAUCE:
1 cup ketchup
1 cup chili sauce
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoon liquid smoke flavoring
1 teaspoon ground mustard

Directions:
Combine the first six ingredients; rub over brisket.  Place in a roasting pan with 2 inch; sides.  In a bowl, combine the sauce ingredients.  set  sauce aside. Add i cup water  Cover and cook at 325 allowing 1 hour per pound. At least 1 hour before it's done remove cover and slice. Return slices to pan and pour remaining sauce over the slices and leave uncovered for remaining time.
