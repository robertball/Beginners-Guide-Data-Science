Title:
Taco Soup VII Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 (1 ounce) package low sodium taco seasoning
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce or tomato soup
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes and chiles with juice
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans without liquid
1 14.5oz can chicken broth
water

Directions:
In a large pot over medium high heat, saute the ground beef and onions for 5 to 10 minutes, or until browned. Drain the grease, and remaining ingredients. Mix well, reduce heat to low and let simmer for 1 to 2 hours to allow the flavors to mesh, adding water as necessary to desired consistency

Submitter: 
Arizona Desert Flower
Description: 
A packet of taco mix is the seasoning used in this soup made with ground beef, kidney beans, whole kernel corn and diced tomatoes cooked in tomato sauce.Modified from: Taco Soup VII
