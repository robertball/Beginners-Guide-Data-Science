Title:
Christmas Wreaths (Cookie Press) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C butter
1/4 C sugar
1-1/2 oz cream cheese
1/2 t vanilla
1 C flour

Directions:
Mix first 4 ingredients; sift in flour.  Add green food coloring.
Chill 1 hour.
Form into wreaths using star-shaped press form.
Bake at 375 for 8-10 minutes.
