Title:
MAPLE-ORANGE MASHED SWEET POTATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/924595.jpg
Ingredients:
4 TBSP BUTTER, CUT INTO 4 PIECES
2 TBSP HEAVY CREAM
1/2 TSP SALT
1 TSP SUGAR
2 LBS SWEET POTATOES, PEELED, AND CUT INTO 1/4" PIECES
PINCH OF BLACK PEPPER
2 TBSP MAPLE SYRUP
1/2 TSP GRATED ORANGE ZEST

Directions:
COMBINE BUTTER, CREAM, SALT, SUGAR, AND SWEET POTATOES IN SAUCEPAN; COVERED, OVER LOW HEAT STIRRING OCCASIONALLY, UNTIL POTATOES FALL APART WHEN POKED W/FORK, 35 - 45 MINUTES.
OFF HEAT, MASH SWEET POTATOES IN SAUCEPAN WITH POTATO MASHER, OR TRANSFER MIXTURE TO HOPPER OF FOOD MILL AND PROCESS INTO WARMED SERVING BOWL. STIR IN PEPPER, MAPLE SYRUP AND ORANCE ZEST; SERVE IMMEDIATELY.
