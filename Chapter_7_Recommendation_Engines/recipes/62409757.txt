Title:
Jeanne's Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Pitted dates, (find in bulk section in your grocery store)
2 ripe bananas, peel needs brown spots
1/2 cup water
2 teaspoons vanilla (opt)
1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt
9 cups rolled oats
1 cup chopped nuts (opt)
1 cup unsweetened coconut (opt)
1/2 cup sunflower seeds (opt)

Directions:
Blend first 5 ingredients until smooth. Pour over remaining ingredients and stir well. Spread on cookie sheets to about 1/2 inch thick and bake at 200 degrees for about 80 to 90 minutes, stirring every 30 minutes, until golden and almost dry. Turn oven off and leave pans in oven to complete drying.
OPTION: Place pans of granola in oven at lowest setting, (150 degrees to 170 degrees) and leave on overnight, no need to stir.
Variations: Substitute rolled barley or rye for oats; Can use 1 1/2 Cups apple juice concentrate instead of first 3 ingredients; Add 2 Cups chopped fresh apple while baking or 1 Cup dried fruit after baking.

Submitter: 
idahocook
Description: 
A yummy healthy granola with no sugar or oil that our family has been making for years. We add or take away ingredients to fit our cravings. Enjoy!
