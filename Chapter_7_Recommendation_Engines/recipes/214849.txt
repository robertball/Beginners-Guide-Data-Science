Title:
PHILLY Salsa Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986825.jpg
Ingredients:
125 grams PHILADELPHIA Light Brick Cream Cheese Spread, softened
3 tablespoons salsa
2 (10 inch) spinach-flavoured tortillas or wraps
1/2 cup shredded CRACKER BARREL Cheddar Cheese Light
Chile powder

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and salsa. Spread evenly onto tortillas; top with shredded cheese. Sprinkle lightly with chili powder. Roll up each tortilla tightly.
Cut each crosswise into nine slices. Arrange, cut-sides down, on serving platter.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia Cream Cheese
Description: 
These tasty pinwheel roll-ups are always a party favorite.  They're simple to make, taste great, and look beautiful on a buffet table.
