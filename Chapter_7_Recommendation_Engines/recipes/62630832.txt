Title:
French Bread Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup chopped green onions tops included
1 cup mayo
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
3/4 cup sharp cheedar cheese
1 tsp worestorshire [or to taste]
1/2 tsp oregano
1 loaf french bread sliced

Directions:
1. Mix all ingrediants together in a food processer.
2. Spread on the french bread and broil. French bread may be cut the long way or in slices both work great. In my oven it takes about 8 mins.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
My best friends Mom used to make this all the time and we loved it.
