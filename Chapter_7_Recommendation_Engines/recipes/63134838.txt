Title:
Horseradish and Cucumber Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup low or no fat sour cream
1/3 cup low or no fat mayonnaise
1/4 cup prepared horseradish
1/4 cup minced onion
3 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon sugar
2 large cucumbers, peeled, seeded and diced
Sea salt and ground pepper, to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients. Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
A fresh tasting, flavorful sauce, with a bit of crunch. It is perfect when served with fish. Low fat ingredients make it more healthy.
