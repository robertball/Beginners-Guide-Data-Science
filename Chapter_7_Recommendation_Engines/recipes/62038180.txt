Title:
melted herb butter for corn on the cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 teaspoon dried chives
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black ground pepper
2 teaspoons grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
combine all ingredients but cheese in a microwaveable bowl. Cover microwave on high 2-3 minutes until melted. Stir in parmesan cheese.

Submitter:

Description: 
Gives a nice taste to your corn.
