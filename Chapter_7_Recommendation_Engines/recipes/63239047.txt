Title:
Banana Nutella Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bananas, Sliced
1 Cup Nutella
1 Tablespoon Cocoa Powder
1 Teaspoon Cinnamon

Directions:
Place sliced bananas, Nutella, cocoa powder, and cinnamon into your blender or food processor, and blend until smooth.
Place in freezer overnight until set. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lisa Lou
Description: 
No ice cream maker needed for this incredible spin on ice cream.
