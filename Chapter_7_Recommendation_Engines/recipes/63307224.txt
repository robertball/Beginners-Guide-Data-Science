Title:
Delicious Confetti Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/873917.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Peanut butter
1/4 cup Margarine
1 pkg (7 0z) Butterscotch chippits
2 cups Coloured miniature marshmallows

Directions:
1. Melt margarine and peanut butter in microwave.
2. Add chips and stir until blended.
3. Let cool
4. Fold in marshmallows.
5. Press into 8 x 8 square pan.
6. Chill
7. Cut into squares and serve.

Submitter: 
JADABUGGY
Description: 
i have been making this recipe for years now..its a big hit! its quick, easy and absolutly delish!! adding a little extra marshmallows makes it extra gooey and sweet. Replacing butterscotch chips with chocolate chips is recommended aswell. Enjoy;)
