Title:
Aunt Deanna's Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup shortening
3 eggs
2 1/4 cup all purpose flour
1 1/2 cup yellow corn meal
1 TBSP baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Cream sugar and shortening in a mixer.Add three eggs and continue blending. Add the flour, cornmeal, baking powder, salt, and milk beat until smooth. Pour into a greased and floured 9 x 13 baking dish and bake in the oven for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Kevin Gorton
Description: 
My husband's aunt Deanna gave my this recipe at my bridal shower. It's a very moist cornbread that goes great with soups or my chili.
