Title:
PHILLY CHEESEBURGER SKILLET DINNER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion, sliced, 3 ounces
8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 green pepper, cut in strips, about 5 ounces
1 red pepper, cut in strips, about 5 ounces
1 clove garlic, minced
Salt and pepper, to taste
4 ounces American cheese, cut up, 6 slices

Directions:
In a large skillet or wok, brown the meat with the onion; drain the grease. Add the mushrooms, green pepper and garlic. Stir-fry until the green peppers are tender-crisp, seasoning with salt and pepper. Stir in the cheese and cook over low heat until melted.
Makes 4 servings
Freezing not recommended
Per Serving: 386 Calories; 26g Fat; 30g Protein; 8g Carbohydrate; 2g Dietary Fiber; 6g Net Carbs
This is an easy one-pot dinner and it is very good. My husband said he liked it as well. Feel free to use any cheese you like in place of the American but American melts very nicely in something like this - it makes a creamy sauce that coats the meat and veggies.
