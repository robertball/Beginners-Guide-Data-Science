Title:
Spicy Buffalo Style Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup quick oats
1 large egg
1/4 cup water
1 tsp garlic powder
1 small chopped onion or onion flakes
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2-3 tbsp hot sauce
2 tbsp melted butter
1 tbsp honey
blue cheese dressing
carrot and celery sticks

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Mix ground beef, oatmeal, egg, water, onion, garlic, salt and pepper. Shape into about 30 1 inch meatballs and place in a large baking dish. Bake about 20 minutes.
Mix hot sauce, butter and honey. When meatballs are done, drain off any fat, and mix in sauce.
Serve with blue cheese dressing and veggie sticks.

Submitter: 
Sagitta
Description: 
Quick and easy gluten free dinner. My husband can eat all these spicy meatballs in one sitting!
