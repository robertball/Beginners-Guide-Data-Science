Title:
Ultra Sweet Celery Simple Treat Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 small Celery pieces
3-4 Containers Peanut Butter
4-5 Containers Fudge Sauce
4-5 Containers Caramel Sauce
4-5 Containers Raisins

Directions:
1. Place 3 pieces of celery onto each plate.
2. Fill the center with peanut butter.
3. Add caramel sauce to the top of the peanut butter.
4. Finally, top it off with both raisins and Fudge sauce.
5. Eat up!
