Title:
Cheddar and Roasted Garlic Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979663.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups all-purpose baking mix
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (14 ounce) can Swanson® Seasoned Chicken Broth with Roasted Garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
Mix baking mix, cheese and broth to form a soft dough. Drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets, making 24.
Bake for 10 minutes or until golden. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Swanson®
Description: 
Simply stir Swanson® Seasoned Chicken Broth with Roasted Garlic into baking mix along with shredded Cheddar cheese for flavorful, flaky biscuits every time.
