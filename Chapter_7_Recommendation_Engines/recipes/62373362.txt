Title:
Sue's Crockpot Barbeque Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, sliced (optional)
1 (4 pound) package country style pork ribs
Salt and ground black pepper garlic powder (to taste)
1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce
1/2 cup water
potatoes and carrots if desired.

Directions:
Place the ribs on a large baking sheet and sprinkle both sides with house seasoning(salt,pepper and garlic powder)add water to crockpot,then layer in crockpot with the onion,potatoes and carrots. Cover and cook for 4 hours.
After 4 hours, pour the entire bottle of barbeque sauce over the ribs. Cover and cook until tender, about another 4 hours. after 3 hrs. check ribs and remove for grill if meat is tender, but not falling apart.

Submitter: 
Suzanne Marzano
Description: 
Prepared in a slow cooker, these country style ribs made with your favorite barbeque sauce couldn't be easier. Put on grill for added flavor and color.Modified from: GrannyLin's Barbeque Ribs Made Easy
