Title:
Best Pumpkin Bisque Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1581473.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp oilive oil
1 medium onion
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp ground curry powder
½ tsp. ground coriander
Cinnamon to taste
Pumpkin pie spice to taste
¼ tsp cayenne pepper
¼ tsp sea salt
¼ tsp fresh ground pepper
3 cups reduced sodium chicken broth
1 can 15-16 oz pumpkin puree
1 apple cored and chopped
1 cup fat free half and half or heavy cream
1 tsp maple syrup or honey for sweetness
¼ cup toasted pumpkin seeds
¼ cup minced fresh chives

Directions:
In a large saucepan, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté for 20 minutes, until soft. Add the garlic, curry powder, coriander, cayenne, salt and  pepper sauté for 1 minute to enhance the seasonings.
Add the broth and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and simmer uncovered for 20 minutes.
Add the pumpkin  and apple and simmer for 10 minutes. Using an immersion blender, process the soup until smooth. Or carefully add the hot soup in batches to a blender and run batches until smooth; return the soup to the pot.
Add the cream or half and half  and the sugar or maple syrup and simmer 2-5 more minutes. Ladle into bowls to serve. Garnish with pumpkin seeds and chives.
