Title:
Banana Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 whole eggs, 2 egg whites
1 tbsp coconut oil
1 whole banana
2 T ground flax
cinnamon, to taste

Directions:
Preheat the pan to 300 degrees.
In a bowl, mix eggs and 1/2 of a mashed banana.
Melt coconut oil in the microwave and add to mix.
Stir in flax to a desired consistency. Add cinnamon.
Pour in pan and cook a few minutes on each side.
Once all pancakes are made, slice up the remaining 1/2 of banana and top the pancakes with it.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
These are great topped with toasted coconut, too!
