Title:
Poppy Seed Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 sleeve buttery round crackers (such as RitzÂ®), crushed
1 teaspoon poppy seeds, or more if desired
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Bake the chicken breasts with butter and seasoned salt for about 30 minutes.  Shred chicken.  Mix crackers and poppy seeds.  Set aside.  Mix sour cream and chicken soup together.  Pour half of chicken soup mixture in bottom of a 9" x 9" baking dish.  Place chicken on top of soup mixture.  Add the remaining soup mixture.  Put cracker mixture on top.  Pour over melted butter.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
