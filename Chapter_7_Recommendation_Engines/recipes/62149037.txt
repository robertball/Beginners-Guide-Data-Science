Title:
german shepards pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of ground beef
1 cup of diced tomatoes
24oz can of italian stewed tomatoes
2 cups of your favorite tomato sauce
2 cups of italian bread crumbs
salt and pepper to taste
2 eggs
6 servings of mashed potato prefferably home made.

Directions:
first make large meat balls
mix gently the beef, diced tomatoes, bread crumbs, eggs, 1 cup of the tomato sauce and pinch of salt and pepper.
this will be a wetter mixture than regular meat balls.
make 4 to 6 larger meat balls in the shape of hamburger patties.  not round but more flat.
place in large saute pan.
on med high brown both sides of meatballs just 2 minutes on each side.
add stewed tomatoes cover and simmer on med-low for 30 minutes.
make mashed potatoes.
to serve.  spoon out mashed potatoes in middle of plate, add a meatball and helping of stewed tomatoes with juices right on top.

this is another grandmothers recipe.  we are german and she made this quite a bit.  its my mothers favorite.  remindes me of home.   tender large german meatballs with stewed tomatoes on top of homemade mashed potatoes.  its different but good
