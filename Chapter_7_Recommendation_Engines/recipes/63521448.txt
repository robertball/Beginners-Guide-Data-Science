Title:
Tahini Chocolate Chip Cookies (Vegan) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/961536.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup spelt flour
1/2 cup oat flour
1/2 cup amaranth flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon Celtic sea salt
1/2 cup almond milk
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil (or Butter)
2 Chia eggs
1/4 cup agave
1/4 succanat
1/2 cup tahini sauce
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup choc chips

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 F
Stir dry ingredients together.
Blend Chia Egg, agave, tahini, vanilla, succanat, almond milk, in blender.
Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients.
Add chocolate chips.
Mix together.
Spoon out on parchment paper. With wet hands you can pat down the cookies so they lay flat.

Tahini and chocolate chips an interesting and innovative combination. These  delicious, vegan, wheat-free cookies are a real treat.
