Title:
Peach Cobbler Upside-Down Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4568295.jpg
Ingredients:
Topping:
3/4 cups brown sugar
3/4 salted butter "soften"
3 large peaches
1 tsp cinnamon
Cake batter:
3 cups flour
3 cups sugar
1 cup of salted butter "soften"
1/2 cup of butter flavor shortening
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp of baking powder
3/4 cups buttermilk
1/2 cup sour cream
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 tsp almond extract
Filling :
3 large peaches
4 tbsp salted butter
1/2 sugar
1/2 brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp almond extract
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg
1/2 cup water
2 tbsp flvorer vodka

Directions:
Preheat over @350
Peel pit & slice 3 large peaches
Grab a sauce pot and add all ingredients for the filling and cook down until you have a cobbler like texture around 25 minutes on medium.
Take 1/2 cup brown sugar 1/2 cup of soften salted butter & mix well then spread evenly around bunt cake pan. Peel pit & slice 3 peaches then add 1 tsp cinnamon. Arrange peaches neatly on top of the brown sugar butter mix.
Grab all of your wet ingredients for your batter i.e. butter shortening eggs vanilla almond buttermilk and sour cream and mix very well. Next you will mix all dry ingredients i.e. flour baking powder sugar cinnamon & nutmeg. You will now mix wet and dry ingredients.
Take half of the batter and pour into bunt pan now take your filling and put on top now take the remaining batter to top off. Put it in over @ 350 for 45 minutes after that turn your oven on 325 and bake for another 1 and 15 minutes you might need another 15 minutes depending on your oven. Once out turn out immediately onto your cake plate
