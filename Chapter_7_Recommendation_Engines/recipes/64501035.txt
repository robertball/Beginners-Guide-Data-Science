Title:
Brown sugar cinnamon apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2615462.jpg
Ingredients:
7 large apples peeled cored and sliced
1/2 cup water
2 tsp flour
1 tsp cinnamon
1 cup flour
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 stick melted butter
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 cup crushed walnuts

Directions:
1. Peel, core, and slice apples place in a casserole dish. Add 1/4 cup water. Sprinkle 2 tsp flour over top. Then sprinkle 1 tsp cinnamon over apples.
2. In separate mixing bowl add all dry ingredients, mix well.  Add melted butter to dry mixture and blend well, will become a damp crumble consistency.  Crumble over top of apples. Add crumbled walnuts over the top.
3. Place aluminum foil over baking dish. Cook 35 minutes , removing foil after 25 min. Let cool a little serve with vanilla ice cream.
