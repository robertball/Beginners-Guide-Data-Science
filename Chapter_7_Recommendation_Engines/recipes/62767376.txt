Title:
Irish Soda Bread I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 teaspoon ground cardamom
1 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 eggs
1 pint sour cream
1/4 cup milk
1 cup dried currants, soaked in boiling water to cover for about 10 minutes (or any dried fruit!)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease two 8x4 inch loaf pans.
Mix the flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder,cardamom, and salt. Add the eggs, sour cream, milk, and raisins/currants and mix until just combined. Distribute batter evenly between the two pans.
Bake loaves at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 1 hour.
