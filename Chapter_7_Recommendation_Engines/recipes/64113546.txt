Title:
Kong Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion pan fried
1 can of tomato paste
1 cup water
2 cans of kidney beans
1 lb. spinach cooked and drained
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
pan fry one large onion and cook one pound of fresh spinach and drain. Combine all ingredients and simmer on stove for about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Danielle Kroll
Description: 
Here is a really quick and easy soup recipe I received from one of my friends. It tastes great and is very easy to make.
