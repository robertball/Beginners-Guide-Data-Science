Title:
Blueberry Protein Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg whites
1 whole egg
1/2 cup oatmeal
1/3 cup blueberries
1 tsp cinnamon (optional)
1 tsp vanilla (optional)
1 tsp baking soda (optional)

Directions:
Combine ingredients.
Spoon pancakes into preheated skillet and cook until golden brown.
Flip pancakes and continue cooking to brown other side.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
You can leave off the blueberries and diversify this base recipe as much as you'd like.
