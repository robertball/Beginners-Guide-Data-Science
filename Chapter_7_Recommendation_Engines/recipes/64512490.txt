Title:
Christmas Sugared Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. walnut pieces
1 stick unsalted butter, melted
1/2 cup confectioners sugar
1 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp. ground ginger
1/4 tsp. ground allspice
1/8 tsp. ground cloves

Directions:
In slow cooker, stir walnuts and butter together until combined
Add confectioners sugar, stirring to coat nuts evenly
Cover.  Cook on High 15 minutes.  Then reduce heat to low.
Cook, uncovered, stirring occasionally until nuts are coated with a crisp glaze, about 2 hours.
Transfer nuts to a serving bowl.
In small bowl, combine spices.   Sift over nuts, stirring to coat evenly.
Cool nuts completely before serving.

Makes 3-4 cups
