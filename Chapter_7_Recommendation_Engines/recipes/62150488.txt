Title:
Pistachio Ice Cream Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
2 sleeves of crushed butter crackers
1/2 cup melted butter
Topping:
1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream
2 small pkgs. instant pistachio pudding
1 1/2 cups milk
1 large container Cool Whip
Heath Pieces or Butter Brickle Pieces

Directions:
Mix the crushed crackers and melted butter together and press into a 9x13 pan.
Bake at 350' for 15 minutes.
Set aside.
Mix together the ice cream (softened), pudding,                                                                                                                               and milk.
Spread on top of prepared crust.
Spread Cool Whip on top of ice cream mixture.
Top  with Heath Pieces.
Freeze until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
tracyc
Description: 
This is a refreshing, light dessert;a nice treat for St. Patrick's Day or any special occasion. My husband and son will eat a whole 9x13 pan in less than a week!
