Title:
Yummy Best Ever Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup flour
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp. baking soda
3/4 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. lemon juice
1 cup buttermilk
1 egg
1/4 cup canola oil
1/2+ tbsp. honey

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients & set aside.
Heat lemon juice a bit in microwave and stir in honey until dissolved.
Mix remaining ingredients and add to dry.
Bake in 8" x 8" greased cake pan @ 350 x 40 minutes or until tests done with toothpick.
Serve warm with butter or maple syrup.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
I loved Montana's sweet cornbread & tweaked recipes till I had one that came close. A perfect companion to chili or stew or soup.
