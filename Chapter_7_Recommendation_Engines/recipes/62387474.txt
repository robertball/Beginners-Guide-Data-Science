Title:
Cranberry Relish Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of fresh or frozen whole cranberries
1 seedless orange, first zested, then peeled and cut into sections
zest from orange
1 apple, cored and cut into sections
1 T. fresh minced ginger
1/3 cup Aspen Mulling Spices or mix 1/3 cup sugar, 2 tsp. cinnamon and 1/2 tsp nutmeg together
1/2 cup sugar, can use more or less to taste
1 cup pineapple tidbits, optional
1/2 cup toasted pecans, optional
1 cup mini marshmallows, optional

Directions:
Put all ingredients, except for optional pecans and marshmallows, in food processor. Process until everything is thoroughly combined and consistently diced.  Pour into serving dish and add pecans and marshmallows if desired.  Serve as a relish, next to meat or a salad.

Submitter: 
Stacey
Description: 
This is a mouthful of pizzazz! It is the taste of fall combined together and will compliment any holiday feast or meal.  It is very fresh and tart dish that goes a long way and keeps for days.
