Title:
Low-Fat Mornay Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups low-fat milk
1/4 cup flour
1 small onion, peeled
3 whole cloves
1 bay leaf
3 black peppercorns
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp nutmeg
2 slices reduced-fat Swiss cheese
2 tbs grated Parmesan cheese
pinch cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over low heat, whisk milk and flour. Stud the onion with the cloves and bay leaf and add to milk mixture. Stir in spices. Simmer, whisking frequently until thickened. Strain and discard solids. Return to heat, stir in cheeses and cayenne. Stir until cheese melts.
