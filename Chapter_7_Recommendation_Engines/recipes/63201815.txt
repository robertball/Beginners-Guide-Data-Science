Title:
Allie's Mother-In-Law's Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
1 cup hot milk
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup sherry wine
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/4 cup flour
1/4 teaspoon ground thyme
1/4 teaspoon basil
1/8 cup onion chopped
1/8 teaspoon pepper (I use white pepper)
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups (8oz by weight) cheddar cheese, diced (or shredded)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Lightly grease a caserole dish.
Cook macaroni according to package instructions.
Place cooked macaroni in a casserole dish.
Mix milk,  chicken broth and wine together and heat.
Place remaining ingredients in blender and pour the hot liquid over the ingredients in the blender.
Blend at high speed for 15 seconds or until smooth.
Pour blended mixture over cooked macaroni.  Mix with a spoon.
Bake uncovered for about 30 minutes or until bubbly and a bit crusty.

Submitter: 
AllieGeekPi
Description: 
This is one of several recipes I got from my MIL.  Everyone loves this homemade macaroni and cheese, especially my husband.  Sherry is a nice addition to the cheese and seasonings.
