Title:
Cheeseburger Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound ground beef (1 small package)
3/4 cup cheeze whiz (NOT other process cheese spread!)
1 baguette, or 6 small dinner buns

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Mix ground beef and cheeze whiz in a small bowl until completely mixed.
2. Slice baguette into 1/2 inch slices, or cut buns in half.
3. Spread a 1/4 inch of spread onto bread.
4. Place on a baking sheet and bake for approximately 15 minutes, or until ground beef is browned and cheese is bubbly.
5. Let stand for 5 minutes and serve hot.
