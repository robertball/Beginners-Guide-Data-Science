Title:
Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack Jimmy Dean sausage regular flavored (cooked crumbled and drained)
4 cups cubed day old bread
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
10 eggs slightly beaten
4 cups milk
1 tsp. dry mustard
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. onion powder
Fresh ground pepper to taste
1/2 cup sliced mushrooms (optional)
1/2 cup peeled, chopped tomatoes (optional)

Directions:
Place bread in well-buttered 9 x 13 baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese.
Combine the next 6 ingredients. Pour evenly over the bread and cheese. Sprinkle cooked sausage and optional ingredients over the top. Cover and chill overnight.
Preheat oven to 325. Bake uncovered for about 1 hour. Tent with foil if top begins to brown to quickly.
