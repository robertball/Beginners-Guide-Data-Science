Title:
Fresh Green Beans with Heaven Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Fresh Green Beans
2 Tbsp. Butter
1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
1 Tbsp. Fresh Minced Garlic
1/2 Cup Fresh Minced Onion
2 Tbsp. Mayo
1/4 tsp. Allspice
1 Tbsp. Sesame Seeds
1 tsp. Sriracha Sauce
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Steam 1 lb fresh green beans. Set aside.
Melt butter in a medium saucepan.  Toss in onions and garlic. Sautee over medium heat for 2 minutes then add the sesame seeds. Toast them until  golden brown. By this time your onions should be soft but not brown.
Add the soy sauce, allspice, mayo, and sriracha sauce. Turn heat to medium low.
Mix thoroughly.
Add freshly steamed green beans to the mixture and combine, or plate the green beans and pour sauce over the top.
