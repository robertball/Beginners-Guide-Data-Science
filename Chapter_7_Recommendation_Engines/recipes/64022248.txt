Title:
Creamy Spinach and Chicken Saute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken breasts
1 cup milk
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup chopped onion
7 cups chopped spinach (about 9 oz.)
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp. nutmeg

Directions:
Cook chicken in a skillet about 2 minutes on each side over medium heat.
Reduce heat to medium low and add milk, broth and onion.
Cook 5 minutes, turning chicken occasionally, until onion is tender.
Add spinach and cook about 3-4 minutes, until spinach is wilted.
Remove chicken from skillet and keep warm.
Increase heat to medium and cook spinach until almost all liquid is evaporated.
Add salt, pepper and nutmeg.
Serve chicken over spinach.
