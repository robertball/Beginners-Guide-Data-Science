Title:
World's Best Marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/841716.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 medium sweet onion, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
8 oz. mushrooms, chopped
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 c fresh flat leaf parsley, torn
2 tbsp oregano
2 tbsp sugar
2 tsp basil
1 tsp rosemary
1/2 tsp sage
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp salt
Pepper to taste
28 oz can tomato sauce

Directions:
1. Heat oil in saucepan over medium heat.  Add onion and garlic to pan and saute for 2 minutes.
2. Add mushrooms, pepper, and parsley and saute for an additional 2 minutes.
3. Add oregano, sugar, basil, rosemary, sage, and red pepper flakes.  Heat an additional 2 minutes.
4. Add tomato sauce.  Heat over low heat for at least 30 minutes.
5. Remove half of sauce from pan and puree in blender.  Add back to pan with remainder of sauce.  Stir to combine.  Serve with pasta.
