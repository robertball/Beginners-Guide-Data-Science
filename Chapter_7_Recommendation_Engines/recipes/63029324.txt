Title:
Almost-Instant Asparagus Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup asparagus tips (about 7-10 spears)
1 clove garlic
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/3 cup cream (milk, nonfat yogurt)

Directions:
Add asparagus, garlic, and chicken stock to the Tall Cup.
Twist on the Shaker/Steamer Top and microwave for 3 minutes, until asparagus is soft.
Open cup and add cream, and twist on Cross Blade.
Blend until you have a smooth souplike consistency.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
To make the most of your asparagus snap the end of each stalk off with your hands, it will break off at exactly the point you want to use.
