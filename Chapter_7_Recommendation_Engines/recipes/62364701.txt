Title:
Crockpot Smothered Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb boneless beef round, cut into 8 pieces
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp. salt
3/4 tsp. pepper
2 Tbsp. canola oil
1 large onion, chopped
3 medium carrots, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 cup beef broth
1/2 cup red wine (or additional 1/2 cup beef broth)
1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
1 tsp. dried minced garlic

Directions:
1. In 1-gallon resealable plastic bag, place beef, flour, salt and pepper. Seal bag and shake to coat beef evenly.
2. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Brown beef on both sides in two batches.
3. Spray 4-quart slow cooker with cooking spray. In cooker, place onion, carrots and celery; top with beef. In medium bowl, mix broth, wine, tomato sauce and garlic. Pour over beef.
4. Cover; cook on Low for 7-9 hours.

Submitter: 
Christine Westrom
Description: 
Makes great gravy; we like it over rice
