Title:
Creamy Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/216848.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (6.8 ounce) packages chicken flavored rice
4 cups cubed cooked chicken
2 cups sour cream
2 cups chow mein noodles

Directions:
Prepare rice mix according to package directions. Remove from the heat; stir in the chicken and sour cream. Transfer to a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Sprinkle with the chow mein noodles. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Sarah  Larson
Description: 
Sarah Larson only needs four items for this comforting meal-in-one. 'If you'd like, add chopped broccoli to the rice for color,' she writes from La Farge, Wisconsin. 'It's fast enough for weeknights yet tasty enough for company.'
