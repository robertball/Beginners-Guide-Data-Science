Title:
Granola Breakfast Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups rolled oats
1 cup flour
3/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup raisins (optional)
1/2 cup wheat germ
3/4 tsp salt
3/4 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup oil
2 tsp vanilla
1 egg

Directions:
Line 9 x 13 pan with foil and grease.
Mix dry ingredients, then add remaining ingredients.  Pat mixture into pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 30-35 minutes until pale golden brown around the edges.  Cool completely.
Transfer with foil to cutting board and cut into 24 bars.
