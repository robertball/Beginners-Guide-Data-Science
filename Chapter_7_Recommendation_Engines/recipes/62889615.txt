Title:
Finnish Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
2 Tablespoons butter
2 eggs
1/2 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp vanilla (optional)

Directions:
Add butter to milk that has been warmed slightly in the microwave.
Whisk melted butter into milk.
Beat eggs into mixture.
Whisk rest of ingredients together and finish blending briefly with an immersion blender.
Cook in skillet or electric griddle at moderate to high heat turning when the first side is done and golden brown.
