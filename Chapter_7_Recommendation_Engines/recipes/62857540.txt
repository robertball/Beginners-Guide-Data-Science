Title:
Chocolate chip muesli bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/683201.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups puffed rice cereal
1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
3/4 cup unsweetened medium grated coconut
1 1/2 cups chopped dried fruit (pineapple and bananas are amazing)
1/2 cup dutch-processed cocoa powder
1 cup chocolate chips
60g salted butter
2/3 cup honey
2 tbsp light corn syrup

Directions:
1. Combine first 6 ingredients
2. Place butter, honey, and corn syrup in small saucepan.  Heat slowly until butter melts.
3. Add melted mixture to dry ingredients and mix well.
4. Press mixture into lined 11x7 baking pan.
5. Put in fridge until firm then slice into bars.

A great no-bake energy bar.
