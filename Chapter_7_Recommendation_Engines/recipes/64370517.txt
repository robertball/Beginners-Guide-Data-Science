Title:
Pollo avocado e cipollotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 white onions (or 1 depending on the size)
3 chicken breasts
extra-virgin olive oil
1 ripe avocado
cherry tomatoes
juice of a lemon
salt and pepper
2 fresh green onions

Directions:
Cut the white onions in rounds and sauté them in a bit of olive oil until they become golden.
Remove the onions and put on a separate dish. Cook the chicken breasts (cut into strips or cubes) in the same pan where the onions were sautéed.
In a salad bowl, add the tomatoes sliced in 4ths and diced avocado.
Add the cooked onions and chicken to the tomatoes and avocado.
Mix together the lemon juice, oil, salt and pepper. Add this dressing to the chicken/avocado mixture.
Mix everything together, serve on a plate. Cover with thinly sliced, fresh green onions.
