Title:
Venus de Milo Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
1 envelope onion soup mix
2 large or 4 small beef Bouillon Cubes
1 small minced onion
1 tsp minced garlic
1 bay leaf
Salt and pepper to taste.
1 28oz can crushed tomatoes
3oz Tomato Paste (abt 1/2 small can)
2 qts water (aprox)
1 10-16oz package frozen mixed veggies
2/3 cup Orzo Pasta

Directions:
Brown ground beef in large pot. Drain off fat. Add Onion, and garlic. (do not brown) Stir in tomato paste then add Crushed Tomatoes, onion soup, Bouillon, bay leaf. Add water and veggies to pot. Bring to rolling boil over med/high heat. Add uncooked orzo to boiling soup and cook till tender. Lastly, add salt and pepper to taste since salt and sodium content vary depending on the brand of onion soup mix and bouillon you have used. Remove from heat and serve.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth Cornell Chedid
Description: 
This my version of a popular soup served at a well known banquet facility in South Eastern Mass. It is simple to prepare using common ingredients you may already have on hand in your pantry and freezer. Feel free to vary the basic recipe with inseason fresh veggies and low fat turkey or veggie/soy burger for the ground beef.
