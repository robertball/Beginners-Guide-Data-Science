Title:
Nana's Mexican Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744450.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cornmeal
3 tbs flour
1 tsp baking Powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1/4 cup milk
2 eggs
1/4 cup canolia oil
1 can creamed corn
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
4 oz can chopped green chiles (optional)
*optional add in 1/2 lb browned ground beef and 1 can drained corn.

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees.
Mix all ingrediants together in large bowl, transfer to lightly oiled iron skillet.
Bake for approx 25- 30 mins, until full cooked
