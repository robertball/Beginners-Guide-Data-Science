Title:
Roasted Carrots & Parsnips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
carrots
Parsnips
Olive Oil
Salt
Pepper
1 tsp dried Thyme leaves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Peel carrots & Parsnips, cut into slices about 4 inches long and 1/4 - 1/2 inch thick
Drizzle with olive oil and arrange in single layer on baking sheet
Season with salt, pepper and thyme leaves
Roast in oven, turning once until edges and tips begin to brown and vegetables are tender - about 30 mins
