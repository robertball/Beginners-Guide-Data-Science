Title:
Holiday Broccoli and Cauliflower Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package frozen broccoli
1 (16 ounce) package frozen cauliflower
1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup (8 ounces) sour cream
1-1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided
1 can french-fried onions

Directions:
Cook broccoli and cauliflower according to package directions; drain. In a large saucepan, combine the soup, sour cream, 1 cup cheese and 1 cup onions. Cook over medium heat for 4-5 minutes or until heated through.
Pour soup mixture over the brocolli and cauliflower and mix well.  Pour into a greased 2-qt. baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at 325Â° for 20 minutes. Sprinkle with the remaining cheese and onions. Bake 10 minutes longer or until cheese is melted. Yield: 6-8 servings.
