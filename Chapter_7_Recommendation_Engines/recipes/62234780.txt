Title:
Calico Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/323375.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb bacon
1 medium onion, chopped
1 lb ground beef
1 16 oz can dark red kidney beans
1 16 oz can Great Northern beans (can also use butter or lima beans)
1 30 oz can pork n' beans
1 c. ketchup
2 T. yellow mustard
1 c. light brown sugar

Directions:
Fry bacon; set aside on paper towel to absorb grease. Add chopped onion to bacon grease and saute over medium heat until soft. Add hamburger and brown. Drain grease.
Drain kidney and Great Northern beans and rinse in colander. Add kidney, Great Northern, and pork n' beans to the hamburger/onion mixture and mix together.
Combine ketchup, mustard, and brown sugar. Add to the rest of the ingredients and stir. Bring to a slight boil.
Transfer mixture into crockpot. Crumble bacon and sprinkle over the top. Heat on low until thoroughly warmed.

Description: 
Hearty three-bean casserole-like dish. great for potlucks. goes great with some corn bread.
