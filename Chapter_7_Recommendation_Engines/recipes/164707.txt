Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Spicy Raisin Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982064.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1/2 cup raisins
6 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
1/2 teaspoon vanilla (optional)

Directions:
In a large saucepan over low heat, melt margarine. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
Stir in pumpkin pie spice and vanilla. Add cereal and raisins, stirring until well coated.
Press into lightly-buttered 3.5 L (13x9-inch) pan. Allow to cool. Cut into bars.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
Pumpkin pie spices and raisins make these KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* bars a special treat that will remind you of autumn.
