Title:
Almost KFC Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag coleslaw mix
1/3 medium onion, finely chopped
1 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons vinegar
1/8 cup sugar (+/- depending on personal taste)
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon basil

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, salt and basil.  In large bowl, add coleslaw and onion.  Starting with one-half of the sauce, stir until vegetables are well coated, adding more sauce until desired consistency is acheived.  Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
goodeats
Description: 
I'm very picky about coleslaw and this is the only one I like.  Simple, fresh and tasty.
