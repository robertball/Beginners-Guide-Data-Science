Title:
Chipotle Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise (paleo, see my other recipes)
1/2 cup cilantro
1 clove garlic
2 T olive oil
2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
~ juice of 1 lime

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a mini blender or food processor until mixed well.
Keep refrigerated until ready to serve

Submitter: 
sammyc14
Description: 
Paleo dipping sauce. This can be kept in the refrigerator for at least a week. You can also omit the mayonnaise from the recipe if you need to. Tastes great on fish tacos or burgers!
