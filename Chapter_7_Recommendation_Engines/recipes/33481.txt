Title:
Killer Koolade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/694480.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 fluid ounce melon liqueur
1/2 fluid ounce amaretto liqueur
4 fluid ounces cranberry juice

Directions:
Fill a tall glass with ice. Pour in melon liqueur and amaretto liqueur. Top with cranberry juice.

Submitter: 
SAMMYSAM
Description: 
This is a wonderful drink that can be made in large quantities. People that don't like the taste of alcohol will love this one. If making in a punch bowl, throw in pieces of tangerines and other fruits as garnish.
