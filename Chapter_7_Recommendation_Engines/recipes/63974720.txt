Title:
Mama's Re-donkulous Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 3/4 t. cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
4-5 mashed overripe bananas
1 small bag smashed up pecans.
1 t.
pinch nutmeg
2 t. vanilla
3/4 c. orange juice
1/2 c. vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Grease and flour loaf pan.
In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt.
In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar.
Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
Add in OJ, vanilla and yogurt.
Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Add in nuts.
Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
