Title:
Mrs. Hobbs Blarney Stones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs, separated
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
1 TBS vanilla
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
pinch of salt
1/2 boiling water
Icing
1 1/2 TBS butter
1 TBS vanilla
Powder sugar
Milk
1 egg yolk
Crushed peanuts

Directions:
1. Beat egg whites until stiff and set aside.
2Combine flour, baking powder and salt and set aside.
3.Beat egg yolks. Add vanilla and mix in sugar gradually. Beat until creamy.
4. Mix in half flour mixture with eggs and sugar and then half of boiling water.
5. Mix in remaining flour and water.
6. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites.
7. Bake 325 for 30 minutes.
8. Cool completely.
9. Cut into 1 1/2" squares.
10. Frost on  all sides and roll in peanuts.
