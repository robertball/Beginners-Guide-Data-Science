Title:
Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups sour cream
1 10oz can cream of chicken soup (or other cream soup you have on hand)
1/2 cup cheez whiz OR I prefer ~ 1.5 - 2 cups shredded texmex cheese
1 kg. bag frozen hash browns
1/3 cup margarine
sliced onions (optional)
2 cups corn flakes, crushed

Directions:
Grease a 9" x 13" pan.
Heat and mix cream, soup and cheez whiz, until cheese is melted.
Put frozen hash browns in pan.
Pour cream mixture over top.
Melt margarine and stir in crushed corn flakes.
Sprinkle over top of casserole.
Bake, uncovered, @ 350 x 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This was a favorite meal-to-the-field. A nice comfort food accompaniment for almost any meat. I usually halved the recipe for our family of 4 and still had leftovers.
