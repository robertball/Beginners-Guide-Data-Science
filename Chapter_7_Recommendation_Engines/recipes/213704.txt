Title:
Mandarin Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/383480.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh tangerine juice
1 cup ice
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers premium tequila blanco
1 tablespoon agave nectar

Directions:
Blend the tangerine juice, ice, tequila, and agave nectar in a blender until the ice is thoroughly crushed. Dispense into 2 margarita glasses to serve.

Submitter: 
Crema
Description: 
An old friend of mine, famous tequila maker, made this margarita for me. I like it because the fresh juice makes me feel healthy, while the tequila makes my spirit feel better. The agave honey is a delicious alternative to sugar, which makes this margarita even better!
