Title:
Arabic salata (Arabic salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large tomatoes, diced
1 cucumber, diced and peeled
1 small onion, diced
1 bunch of parsley, chopped
the juice of one lemon.
salt to taste
1 teaspoon of mint (optional)
1 red or green bell pepper, diced (optional)
5 romaine leaves chopped (optional)

Directions:
add all chopped vegetables and mix.
when ready to serve add the lemon and salt, otherwise the lettuce will wilt.

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
This is the arabic salad we use to accompany in mixing to all our rice dishes.  Its good by itself, but the purpose of it is to mix with rice.

The basic recipe follows, its great when you add all sorts of vegetables.  if you dont like raw onions, use green onions and its just as great.
