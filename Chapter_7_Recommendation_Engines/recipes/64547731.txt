Title:
Elk Bolognese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lb ground elk
10 oz Pancetta
1 cup diced carrot
1 cup diced celery
1 cup diced red onion
1/2 cup butter
4 cups whole milk
1 tsp nutmeg
4 cups dry white wine
1 tbsp garlic powder
84oz of chopped tomatoes (3 cans)

Directions:
Saute carrot, onion & celery in 1/4 cup butter until translucent, deglaze pan with small amount of white wine.  Brown elk meat in 1/4 cup butter with salt and pepper seasoning throughout.   Lightly brown pancetta until just beginning to melt, remove from heat.  Combine vegetables and milk, add milk and nutmeg.  At a slow boil, reduce until most milk has evaporated (30 mins at least).  Add wine, again at a slow boil reduce until most has evaporated.  Add tomatoes, garlic powder and simmer for 3 hours (or more).  Add water if too much liquid boils off.

Submitter: 
TheCattlemansWife
Description: 
A blend of many recipes - comes out very tender, mild flavor and freezes well. Can be used with any kind of ground meat.
