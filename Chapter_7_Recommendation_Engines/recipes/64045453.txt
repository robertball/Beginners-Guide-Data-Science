Title:
Baking Powder Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chilled butter
2 cups flour
1 Tbsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425°
Combine dry ingredients; cut in butter.  Stir in milk until dough is soft and sticky.
Turn dough onto lightly floured surface and knead lightly about 10 times.  Roll to 1/2" thickness.   Cut with floured cutter and place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
Alternate baking method:
Preheat oven to 475°
Put 2 Tbsp. oil in a preheated iron skillet.  Place biscuits in skillet and flip immediately to coat both sides.  Bake for 8 minutes then broil for another 1 minute.

Submitter: 
Julie E.
Description: 
These biscuits are light and fluffy (as long as the butter is chilled).
