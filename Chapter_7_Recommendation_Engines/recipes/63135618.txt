Title:
Bathroom Tub and Tile scrub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp borax
1/2 tsp liquid castile soap
1/2 cup distilled white Vinegar
3 cups hot water

Directions:
Using a funnel, pour borax, soap, vinegar and water into a spray bottle.  Shake well to mix.

Submitter: 
TheBritishBaker
Description: 
Borax and Vinegar remove mold and mildew growth as well as soap scum in the cost effective cleaner.  Makes approx 280z at a cost of only $0.15
