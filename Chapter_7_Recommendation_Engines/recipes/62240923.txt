Title:
Rosy Glow Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 quart bottles Cranberry Juice Cocktail, chilled
1 can (12 oz.) frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
1/2 gallon Raspberry Sherbet
1 quart Ginger Ale, chilled

Directions:
Combine cranberry cocktail and lemonade concentrate.
Stir well and chill.
Just before serving, pour into 4 quart punch bowl.
Add scoops of sherbet.
Slowly add Ginger Ale.
