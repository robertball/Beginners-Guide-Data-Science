Title:
bacon ranch chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2cans cream of mushroom soup
4 chicken breasts
8 slices of bacon
2 cups sour cream
4 tblsp dry ranch dressing mix

Directions:
pre heat oven to 350 degrees.
cook bacon and set aside.
flatten each chicken breast to about a 1/4 inch thickness.
in center of each chicken breast place 2 strips of bacon.
wrap chicken around bacon and secure with toothpicks.
in medium size bowl, comine sour cream, soup, and ranch dressing mix.
place chicken in greased baking dish and pour soup mixture over chicken.
bake uncovered for 35-40 minutes or untill juices run clear.

Submitter: 
mistycolleen
Description: 
chicken breasts stuffed with bacon and baked in a creamy ranch flavored gravy.serve with steamed broccoli and parmesan flavored noodles. YUMMY!
