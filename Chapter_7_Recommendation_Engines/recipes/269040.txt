Title:
Vegan Thousand Island Salad Dressing  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5638374.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces unsweetened plain soy milk
1/2 cup soy-free vegan mayonnaise (such as Follow Your Heart® Veganaise)
1/4 cup shelled hemp seeds (such as Hemp Hearts®)
3 tablespoons ketchup
2 tablespoons sweet dill relish

Directions:
Whisk together soy milk, vegan mayonnaise, hemp seeds, ketchup, and relish in a large bowl.

Submitter: 
Charley
Description: 
This is the best thousand island dressing I have ever had and perfect for a vegan diet. Season with additional ketchup or dill, if needed.
