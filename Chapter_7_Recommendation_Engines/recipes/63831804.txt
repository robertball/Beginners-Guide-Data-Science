Title:
Thanksgiving Leftovers Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065040.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb package frozen bread dough, defrosted
1 C cooked, chopped turkey
1 C prepared stuffing
1 C prepared mashed potatoes
1/2 C mixed vegetables
1/2 C cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Break off 2 inch pieces of bread dough.  Flatten piece of dough as thin as possible without breaking.  Place approximately 1 tsp each of turkey, stuffing, mashed potatoes, and vegetables on dough.  Close dough around filling, sealing to form a ball.  Repeat with remainder of ingredients, placing each dough ball in baking dish.
Sprinkle dough with cheddar cheese.  Bake for 30-40 minutes or until dough is cooked through.  Invert onto serving dish.

Submitter:

Description: 
An easy and delicious way to use up Thanksgiving leftovers.  Serve with gravy or cranberry sauce!
