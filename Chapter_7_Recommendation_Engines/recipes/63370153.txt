Title:
Cherry Smash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/943944.jpg
Ingredients:
3 frozen cherries (can use fresh, not maraschino)
Angostura bitters
2 ounces of Rye whiskey (like Michter's)
1 ounce of sweet Italian Vermouth
1/2 ounce simple syrup
Lemon juice

Directions:
Place cherries in a low boy glass and splash with angostura bitters; muddle cherries. Fill a a cocktail shaker with ice. Add Rye, Sweet Vermouth, simple syrup and a splash of lemon juice.  Shake vigorously. Pour over cherries.
