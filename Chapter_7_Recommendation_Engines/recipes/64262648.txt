Title:
Pan Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large potatoes
1/3 cup margarine or olive oil
2 Tbsp Parmesan cheese
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp salt
2-1/2 Tbsp bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Cut potatoes into wedges.  Melt the margarine and pour over potatoes, toss until well coated.  Place potatoes on greased baking sheets.
Combine cheese, paprika, salt and bread crumbs. Sprinkle over potatoes.  Bake at 30-40 minutes.
