Title:
Vegetarian Thai-Style Eggrolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
1/2 cup mushrooms, chopped (optional)
1 container crumbled hard tofu
1 cup vegetable protein, soaked
1 cup fresh bean sprouts
1/2 cup mint leaves, chopped and slightly cooked
Fresh raw mint

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in pot and cook, mixing until warm.
Orienting prepared egg-roll wrap as a diamond toward you, place a large tablespoon full of mixture in the center of the wrap.
Place a few leaves of fresh mint on top of filling
Fold sides of wrap in toward center and roll the wrap forward. Use a wet fingertip to "glue" the seam.
Place, seam down, on a dish
Fry eggrolls on all sides until golden brown and crispy.
