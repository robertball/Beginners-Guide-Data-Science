Title:
Nutty Bar Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 Nutty Bars Wafer Bars
2-8oz pkgs. cream cheese
1-4oz pkg Inst. Vanilla pudding
2 C.Chunky peanut butter
2 C.Milk
1 C.whipping cream
1 tsp.vanilla extract
1/2 C.sugar
1/2 C.melted butter

Directions:
Unwrap 12 nutty bars chop in blender until finely crushed. Pour into bowl. Add butter to crushed bars and mix well. Press into bottom of 12" spring form pan. Set aside. Unwrap remaining Nutty Bars but in half. Line rim of pan with bars as shown above. In a separate bowl, whip cream cheese, milk, peanut butter and sugar together until fluffy. Add whipping cream, pudding mix and vanilla to cream cheese filling. Mix two minutes. Pour filling into crust. Chill overnight.
