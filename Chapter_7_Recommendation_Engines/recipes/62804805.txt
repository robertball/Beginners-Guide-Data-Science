Title:
Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup butter, melted
2 8oz pkgs cream cheese, softened
2 cups peanut butter
2 cups powdered sugar
2 T. vanilla extract
1 3/4 cups heavy whipping cream
3/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 T. whip. cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Make crust.
In a large bowl, combine chocolate crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar and 1/2 cup butter and press into a 10 inch round springform pan or fluted pie pan. Bake for 10 minutes. Remove from oven and cool.
In a large bowl, combine the cream cheese, peanut butter, sugar and vanilla. Beat on medium speed until smooth.
In a separate bowl, beat whipping cream until stiff peaks form.  Fold into peanut butter mix.  Pour into pie crust. In a microwave-safe bowl, microwave chocolate chips with cream until melted. Stir occasionally until chocolate is smooth. Drizzle melted chocolate on top of pie.
Refrigerate 4 hours or until set.
