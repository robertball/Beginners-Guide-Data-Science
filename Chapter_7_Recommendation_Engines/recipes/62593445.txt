Title:
Pickled Eggs & Beets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (15 oz. each) whole or sliced beets
12 hard-cooked eggs, peeled
1 onion, sliced
1 cup sugar (or less, to taste)
1 cup water
1 cup cider vinegar

Directions:
Drain beets, reserve 1 cup juice
Layer beets, onions & eggs in (2)
2 qt. jars.  Bring remaining ingredients
& 1 cup reserved beet juice to boil.
Pour over beets & eggs in 2 qt jars.
Cool and then cover tightly and
refirgerate for at least 24 hours
before serving.
