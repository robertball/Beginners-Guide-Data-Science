Title:
Simple Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard-cooked eggs
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
2 heaping Tbs sweet pickle relish
1 teaspoon prepared mustard (optional)
1 tablespoon finely chopped onion (optional
Shaking of paprika, (hot optional).

Directions:
Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks; set whites aside. Mash yolks with a fork in a small bowl. Stir in mayonnaise, relish, mustard, and onion; mix well. Stuff or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites. Sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate until serving.
