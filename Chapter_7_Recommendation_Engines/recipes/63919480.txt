Title:
citrus mint water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lemon
1 lime
handful of mint

Directions:
Thinly slice lemons and limes and put them in the bottom of a 2 qt. pitcher with the mint leaves. Cover with ice about 1/2 up then add water. Let sit for 4 hours (or overnight). Makes one big pitcher (2qts?). Refill water 3-4 times before replacing citrus & mint

Description: 
Flavored water to add a twist to the same old thang.
