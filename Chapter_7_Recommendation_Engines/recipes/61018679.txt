Title:
Best Spinach Dip Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277680.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1 (1.8 ounce) package dry leek soup mix
1/2 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained, and chopped again
food coloring
12 mini sourdough rolls

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise, sour cream, food coloring, dry leek soup mix, and chopped spinach. Chill in the refrigerator 6 hours, or overnight.
Remove top and interior of sourdough breads. Fill each with equal amounts of mayonnaise mixture. Tear removed bread chunks into pieces for dipping.
