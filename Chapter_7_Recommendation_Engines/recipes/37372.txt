Title:
Butterbeans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985632.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 ounce finely chopped lean ham
2 cups fresh or frozen butterbeans or lima beans
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
BRING2 cups water and ham to a boil in a saucepan; boil 5 to 10 minutes. Add butterbeans, salt, and pepper; return to a boil.
COVER, reduce heat, and simmer 45 minutes or until tender.

Submitter: 
Southern Living magazine
Description: 
Boiling a little lean ham in the cooking liquid really fools your tongue; you'll think you're feasting on bacon.
