Title:
Yummy Spaghetti and Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/357116.jpg
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
1 Package (1 lb) of ground beef or ground turkey
4 Crackers crushed
1/4 Cup Onion chopped
1 Egg
Salt and Pepper
Sauce:
2 Tbs Olive oil
1 Onion chopped
4 Cloves of Garlic diced
1/2 Pepper large chopped(Red, Green, Yellow or Orange)
1/2 Cup of Carrots chopped
1 Cup Broccoli cut into small crowns
1/2 Cup Celery chopped
1 Cup Mushrooms sliced
1 Jar Tomato Sauce - Your flavour choice
1 Can Tomato Paste
1 Tbs Oregano (optional)
Salt and Pepper
Pasta:
1 Box of Spaghetti Noodles
1 Tbs Oil
Optional:
Fresh Parmesan Cheese
Garlic Bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375ÂºF.
In large mixing bowl combine with your hands ground beef or turkey with crushed crackers, chopped onion, egg, salt and pepper until well combined.
Shape meatballs with hands into fair size balls.
Place meatballs on baking dish with rack and bake for 30min or until no longer pink inside.
While meatballs are cooking start chopping all ingredients for sauce.
Heat oil in large pan over medium heat and add onion, garlic, pepper, carrots, broccoli, celery and mushrooms.  Stir frequently.
Once vegetables are soft (about 10min of cooking)add tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, salt and pepper and simmer on low-med heat for about 15min stirring frequently.
While simmering sauce heat your water and oil for spaghetti noodles and cook according to package directions.
Once meatballs are done cooking add them to the sauce mixture and simmer for about 5 more minutes on low-med heat.
Drain noodles and serve with sauce on top and sprinkle with fresh parmesan cheese.
Serve with garlic bread.
