Title:
Minty Pea Soup with Parmesan Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 oz whole-wheat baguette, cubed
Cooking spray
2 Tbsp grated Parmesan cheese (or nutritional yeast)
1 Tbsp olive oil
2 cups chopped onion
4 garlic cloves, minced
5 cups unsalted chicken stock
4 cups fresh green peas
1/4 cup mint leaves, divided
2 tsp sugar (optional)
1/2 cup fat-free Greek yogurt
2 tsp grated lemon rind
3/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp kosher salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Arrange bread on a baking sheet; coat with cooking spray. Bake at 425 degrees for 6 minutes.
Place bread in a bowl. Add cheese; toss well.
Heat Dutch oven over medium heat. Add oil, onion, and garlic; cook 11 minutes. Stir in stock, peas, 3 tablespoons mint, and sugar. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; simmer 20 minutes. Place half of pea mixture and remaining mint in blender. Remove center piece of lid; secure lid on blender.
Place a towel over lid. Blend until smooth; pour into bowl. Repeat with remaining mixture.
Return soup to pan. Add yogurt, rind, pepper, and salt. Top with croutons.
