Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Dessert Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976300.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (1 ounce) squares semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
2 teaspoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Fresh Fruit, ice cream or cake

Directions:
In medium saucepan, over medium-low heat, melt chocolate and peanut butter with EAGLE BRAND(r) and milk, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat; stir in vanilla. Cool slightly. Serve warm as fruit dipping sauce or over ice cream or cake. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
This rich chocolate peanut butter sauce is delicious served over cake and ice cream, or use it as dipping sauce for your favorite fruits.
