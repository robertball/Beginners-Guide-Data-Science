Title:
Flapper Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST:
1 1/2 cups graham crumbs
1/2 cup melter margerine or butter
1/2cup sugar 1 Tbsp. cinnamon
FILLING:
3 cups milk
1/2 cup sugar
4 Tbsp. cornstartch
3 egg youlks
1tsp. vanilla
MERAUNGE:
3 egg whites
2 Tbsp. sugar

Directions:
Mix graham crumbs, sugar, cinnamaon and melted butter.
Reserve 1/4 cup for topping.
Press into 9" round deep pie plate.
Bake at 350 for 8 min.
Heat milk but do not boil.
Mix sugar,cornstartch, egg youlks and vanilla.
When milk is heated add 1/4 cup to the egg mixture to temper so the eggs don't scramble.
Add back to milk and stir until thickened.
Pour into crust.
Beat egg whites and sugar until stiff.
Drop on to top of filling.
Sprinkle with leftover crumbs.
Place under broiler until meraunge starts to brown.
