Title:
Strawberry Pecan Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box white cake mix,
1 small box strawberry gelatin,
1 cup canola oil,
1/2 cup milk,
4 eggs,
1 cup frozen strawberries, drained,
1 cup pecans.

Directions:
Mix cake mix and jello together dry.  Add oil, milk, and eggs(one at a time).  Add remaining ingredients.  Bake 3 layers at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
My husband's granmother used to make this, it was a big hit.  Very tast, but oh so rich.
