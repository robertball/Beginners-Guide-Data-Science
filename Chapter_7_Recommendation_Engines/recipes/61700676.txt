Title:
Beef Stroganoff III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds flank steak ( or round steak..or beef tenderloin!)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 ounces butter
3 tablespoons lemon juice
6 green onions, sliced (white parts only)
1-C  all-purpose flour
2 (10.5 ounce) can condensed beef broth
2-T garlic minced
2 teaspoon prepared mustard
2 (8 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
1- 8oz  sour cream
1/2 cup burgandy wine
3-T Worchester
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Remove any fat and gristle from the roast and cut into strips 1/2 inch thick by 2 inches long. Season with 1/2 teaspoon of both salt and pepper. Add 1-C flour to 1 gallon zip lock bag and coat meat. Shake well.
In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter and brown the beef strips quickly, then push the beef strips off to one side. Add the onions and cook slowly for 3 to 5 minutes, then push to the side with the beef strips.
Stir the flour left in the zip lock bag into the juices on the empty side of the pan. Pour in 2 -Cans beef broth, worchester and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Lower the heat and stir in mustard and garlic.Transfer to the slow cooker. Cover and cook on high for 1 hour or until the meat is tender.
Thirty minutes before serving, stir in the other 8 oz of mushrooms, sour cream, and burgandy wine.
