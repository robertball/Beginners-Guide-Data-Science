Title:
spicy asian pork tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup hoisen sauce
2 Tbsp chili-garlic sauce
1 tsp sesame oil
1 1/2 Tbsp rice vinegar
1 1/4 lb pork tenderloin

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium high.
combine hoisin, chili-garlic sauce, sesame oil and rice vinegar in small boul.
Brush Pork all over with sauce. Place pork on grates cover and grill, turning several times with tongs, untill it reaches 150'F  12-15 minutes.  Tent with foil and rest for 5 min.
