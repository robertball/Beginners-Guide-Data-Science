Title:
Sweet N Sassy Spicy Peachy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into large pieces
all-purpose flour to coat chicken pieces, about 1/2 cup
1 package dry onion soup mix
1-10 ounce bottle russian salad dressing
1/4 cup cane syrup or molasses
2 tablespoons hot sauce
1/2 cup peach jam(store bought or homemade)
1 cup fresh sliced peaches(if fresh is not available use canned, just drain them well)
2 tablespoons butter
1 small yellow onion, chopped
salt and freshly ground black pepper to tastes
white or brown rice for serving

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Dredge chicken in flour, salt and pepper.
Place chicken pieces in a large casserole dish. In a large bowl combine the soup mix, dressing,
cane syrup or molasses, russian dressing, hot sauce and peach jam.
Mix well and pour over chicken.
Cover and bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
While chicken is cooking saute the peaches and onions in butter until tender. Serve chicken over rice with the peaches and onions.

Submitter: 
Ashley Holman
Description: 
This dish is the perfect combination of sweet, savory and spicy!! It pairs very well with a nice garden salad and hearty bread.
