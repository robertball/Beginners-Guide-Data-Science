Title:
Barbecue Tempeh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. barbecue sauce
1 (8 oz) package tempeh, crumbled
1 green pepper, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
4 kaiser rolls, split and toasted

Directions:
Pour the sauce into a medium bowl; add tempeh and let it marinate while cooking vegetables.
Heat oil in a skillet, saute veggies till tender.  Stir in tempeh mix and heat.
Spoon mixture onto rolls and serve.
