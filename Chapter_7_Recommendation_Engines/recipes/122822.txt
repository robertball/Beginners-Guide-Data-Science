Title:
Lentil Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/218983.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 cup dried lentils
2 tablespoons dried minced onion
1/2 teaspoon dried minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/8 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1 small zucchini, chopped
1 cup taco sauce
1 cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
8 (8 inch) fat-free tortillas

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine the first sic ingredients; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until lentils are tender.  Drain if necessary.  Stir in zucchini, taco sauce and cheese.  Place about 1/2 cupfuls down the center of each tortilla.  Fold sides and ends over filling and roll up.

Submitter: 
Pam  Masters
Description: 
'I'm constantly trying to incorporate healthy but tasty meals into our menu,' writes Pam Masters of Derby, Kansas. 'Both kids and adults love these mildly spiced burritos that combine filling lentils with crisp zucchini.'
