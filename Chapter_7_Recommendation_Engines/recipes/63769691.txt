Title:
Grandma's Salmon Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1991998.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large can pink salmon (about 14oz)
1 slice whole wheat bread
1 small onion, minced
1 carrot, shredded
1 egg
salt and pepper to taste
1 large onion

Directions:
Drain and de-bone salmon.
In food processor, crumble bread (untoasted) to crumbs.
Mince onion.  Shred carrot.
Mix salmon, breadcrumbs, onion, carrot and egg in bowl.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Form 6 patties (or sized to your liking).
Cut large onion into 8ths.
Fry onion along with patties in small amount of oil until both sides are golden brown, and onion
is tender and golden.  (Aprox 4-5 min a side)
Can be served warm or cold, but best served at room temperature.

Submitter: 
Lindsay
Description: 
Grandma loves salmon patties.  She shared her recipe with me.  One of the unique things about this recipe is that they are best at room temperature.  She would often eat the leftover ones in a sandwich.
