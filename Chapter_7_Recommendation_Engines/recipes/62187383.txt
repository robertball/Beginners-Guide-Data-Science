Title:
Mocha Brownies with Coffee Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup butter
1 cup cocoa
2 cups sugar
1 T hot water
4 to 5 tsp instant coffee
4 large eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
Frosting:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups confectioner's sugar
1 1/2 T. milk
3 tsp instant coffee

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Grease a 13x9 inch pan.  Melt shortening and butter and pour into a mixing bowl. Add cocoa, blend well.  Add sugar and mix well.  Dissolve instant coffee in hot water and mix into creamed mixture.  Add eggs, one at a time, beat well, by hand after each addition. Stir in vanilla, flour and salt. DO NOT OVER BEAT!!!!  Pour into prepared pan and bake 25- 30 min.  Cool completely before frosting.
Frosting:
Cream butter and vanilla. add powdered sugar and mix well on medium speed. dissolve instant coffee in milk and add to mixture.  Beat on high until light and fluffy.
