Title:
Spicy Hummus chicken wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Flatbread
3 Tbs Sabra supremely spicy hummus (well mixed)
1 Tbs Zesty italian dressing
2 oz. cooked chicken breast, shredded (I use rotisserie chicken left overs)
6 grape tomotoes, quartered
1/4 cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
1 oz spinach leaves (or any lettuce you prefer)

Directions:
Lay flatbread out and smooth on spicy hummus to cover entire peice.
Layer shredded chicken, tomatoes, cucumber and spinach evenly over hummus.
drizzle with zesty italian dressing.
Start at one end of flat bread and roll up until it looks like a burrito. (use short side of flatbread to roll)
Cut in half and serve.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
This is a quick, delicious and healthy meal! Great for meals on the go. The spicy hummus adds a nice kick of flavor.
