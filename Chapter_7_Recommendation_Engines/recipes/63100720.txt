Title:
Basic Bread - Bread Machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886873.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup warm water (110ยบ)
2 tablespoons sugar
1 (.25 ounce) package bread machine yeast
1/4 cup vegetable oil
3 cups bread flour
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place the water, sugar and yeast in the pan of the bread machine.
Let the yeast dissolve and foam for 10 minutes.
Add the oil, flour and salt to the yeast.
Select Basic or White Bread setting, and press Start.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
This very easy-to-follow recipe promises to be foolproof. It makes a soft bread with a flaky crust.Modified from: Best Bread Machine Bread
