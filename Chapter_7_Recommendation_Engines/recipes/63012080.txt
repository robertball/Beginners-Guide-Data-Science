Title:
Butternut Balls Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 cup butter
2. 1/2 cup powdered sugar
3. 1 tea vanilla
4. 1/8 tea salt
5. 2 1/2 cups flour
6. 3/4 cup chopped walnuts
7. 1 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
in mixing bowl beat 1 till soft...add 2. 3. 4. 5. and 6 mix well ...form a ball and chill 1 hour
Form 1" balls bake 400* Ungreased cookie sheet for 10-12 min...let cool slightly then roll in 7. while still warm ...cool on plate
watch these cookies so they do not burn....
might want to double since recipe makes 2 doz

Delicate melt in your mouth cookies
