Title:
Creamy Chicken and Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth
2 cups water
2 cooked, boneless chicken breast halves, shredded
1/2 c. wild rice mix
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
chopped celery
chopped onion
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup butter
2 cups 1/2 & 1/2

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, combine broth, water and chicken.  Bring just to boiling, then stir in rice, cook for 20 minutes.
Saute celery and onion in butter.
In a small bowl, combine salt, pepper and flour.      Reduce heat to low, then stir in flour mixture by tablespoons, to form a roux.  Whisk in cream, a little at a time, until fully incorporated and smooth.  Cook until thickened, 5 minutes.
Stir cream mixture into broth and rice.  Cook over medium heat until heated through, 10 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
wild rice is cooked in chicken broth with shredded chicken, then combined with thickened cream for a quick soup.Modified from: Creamy Chicken and Wild Rice Soup
