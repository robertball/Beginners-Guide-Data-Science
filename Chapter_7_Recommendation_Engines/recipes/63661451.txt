Title:
M&m; Cookie S'more Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1 cup butter (softened)
1tsp vanilla extract
2 eggs
2 1/2 cups flour
3/4 tsp salt
3/4 tsp baking soda
2 cups m&m;'s
4 king sized hershey chocolate bars
2 jars marshmallow jet fluffed
pack and a half of graham crackers

Directions:
1. Beat sugar, brown sugar, and butter in a large bowl. 2. Beat in vanilla and eggs. 3. In a medium bowl mix together flour, salt, and baking soda, add to mix. 4. Mix in m&m's. 5. Put in oven at 375 for 6 minutes. 6. Remove from oven and place hersheys bars over cookie dough. 7. Spread marshmallow mix over chocolate. 8. Put crumbled graham crackers over marshmallow fluff. 9. Put back in oven for 2-3 minutes. 9. Remove from oven and freeze for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Marissa Getty
Description: 
M&M; cookie dough layered with the traditional s'more recipe. Quick and easy to make!
