Title:
Easy Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 medium sized apples
1/3 cup light brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp lemon juice
1 cup apple cider
salt

Directions:
Peel and cut apples into chunks.
Mix in brown sugar, cinnamon, lemon juice, a dash of salt and the apple cider. Bring to a boil and then reduce to simmer for approximately 20 minutes. Uses a potato masher while cooking to help breakdown the softened apples. Serve warm or cool.

Submitter: 
Doug Matthews
Description: 
A simple applesauce using brown sugar & apple cider
