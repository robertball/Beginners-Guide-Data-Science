Title:
Gin Rickey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/995590.jpg
Ingredients:
cracked ice
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger dry gin
1 lime, juiced
1/2 teaspoon superfine sugar
seltzer water
1 slice lime

Directions:
Fill a highball glass 3/4 full with ice. Pour in gin, lime juice, and sugar. Stir, and fill remainder of the glass with seltzer water. Garnish with a lime slice.

Submitter: 
GROSBACH
Description: 
A classic warm-weather gin drink that is deliciously different.
