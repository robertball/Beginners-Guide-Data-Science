Title:
King Crab Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
3/4 cup whipping cream or half n' half
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic, minced
12 oz. fettuccine, cooked and drained
1/2 teaspoon pepper
salt to taste
8 oz. imitation crab meat

Directions:
Melt margarine in large skillet on medium heat.
Saute' garlic until tender.
Add crab meat, Parmesan and whipping cream.  Cook over low heat, stirring frequently until heated through and thickened.
Add salt and pepper.
Toss with cooked fettuccine.
