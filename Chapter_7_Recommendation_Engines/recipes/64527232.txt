Title:
Slow Cooker Spinach & Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14oz cans artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 10oz pkg. frozen spinach, thawed and squeezed dry/chopped
8 oz sour cream
1 small onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, crushed
3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/3 cup mayo
1 tbsp. vinegar
1/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
8 oz cream cheese, cubed

Directions:
Place all ingredients, except cream cheese, into slow cooker.
Stir until well combined.
Top with cream cheese.
Cover & cook on low heat for 2 hours.
Uncover and stir until cream cheese is well combined.
Cover and cook on high heat for an additional 15 min.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A creamy, delicious dip, conveniently cooks in slow cooker. Serve with scoop chips or bread.
This is a large batch. I usually half it!
