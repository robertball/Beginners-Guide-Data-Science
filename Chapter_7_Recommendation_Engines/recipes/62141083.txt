Title:
Starbucks Curried Chicken Salad (Deb's Version) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Far East Couscous -
Mediterranean Curry flavor
1 20 oz. can pineapple tidbits - do not drain
1 pound boneless/skinless chicken thighs
2 teaspoons olive oil
1 large Granny Smith apple, unpeeled
1 teaspoon chives (fresh or dried)
12 oz. plain yogurt*
1/2 pecans, chopped
salt, pepper and curry powder to taste
lettuce leaves for serving

Directions:
Cook couscous according to package directions.
While couscous is cooking, brown the chicken in the olive oil...season to taste.
Cut chicken into bite size pieces.
Core and chop apple into bite size pieces.
Place all ingredients in to a large bowl.
Stir well and add extra curry powder if needed.
Cover and Chill at least 2 hours...even better overnight.
Serve on a platter lined with lettuce leaves.
* I use Silk Live! Soy Yogurt but any brand of PLAIN yogurt will do.
