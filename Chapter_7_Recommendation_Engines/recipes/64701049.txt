Title:
Pierside Grill Beach Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf French bread, sliced in half
4 tablespoons garlic butter melted
1/2 cup  crumbled blue cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup diced fresh tomatoes
2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Line a baking sheet with nonstick foil or parchment paper
Brush cut sides of French bread with melted butter
Top with blue cheese,mozzarella, tomatoes, chives, and parsley
Bake 10-15 minutes until bread is crusty on the outside and the top is melted and just begging to turn golden
