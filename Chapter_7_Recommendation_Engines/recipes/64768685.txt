Title:
Ranch Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 cups Crispix cereal
1 cup cheese crackers
1 cup pretzels
1 cup peanuts
2 packets Hidden Valley The Original Ranch Salad Dressing Mix - Buttermilk (0.4 oz each)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in 2 gallon or larger sealed bag
Toss until evenly coated

Submitter: 
Stephen Enochian
Description: 
Add some zesty flavour to your next gathering or enjoy for whenever you want it. I love making it around the holidays.
