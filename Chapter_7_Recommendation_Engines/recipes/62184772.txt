Title:
Lemon Curd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup *lemon juice (NO bottled)
4 tsp lemon zest
1cup sugar
Pinch of salt
5 eggs
1 stick salted butter, melted, no substitutes.
*Meyer lemons add an incredible flavor.

Directions:
In blender mix first 5 ingredients very well. Add melted butter and mix another 10 seconds
Pour into sauce pan. Over medium low heat whisk non-stop til mixture comes to a low boil, appx 5 mins. Let simmer for about 30 seconds while continuing to whisk.
Set pan in refrigerator and let cool for one hour. Pour into container(s).
Lemon Angle Pie;
1 batch of lemon curd
2 cups heavy whipping cream
1 Tb lemon zest
Beat whipping cream and lemon zest til cream is very stiff.
Gently fold cooled lemon curd into whipped cream til thoroughly incorporated. Spoon into either baked pastry pie shell or Graham cracker pie shell.
Let cool in refrigerator at least 2 hours before serving.
