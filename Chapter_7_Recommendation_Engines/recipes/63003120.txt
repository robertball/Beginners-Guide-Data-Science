Title:
Cindy's Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz bag of precut coleslaw (in the produce refrigerator section)
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sugar (then add to your taste preference)
1/8 cup cider vinegar
1/2 tsp granulated onion
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Pour the coleslaw mix in a large bowl.
In a small bowl, combine ALL the dressing ingredients. Pour over cabbage mixture and toss to coat. Place in fridge to chill & absorb the flavors for at least a 1/2 hr.
Serve with a slotted spoon. Enjoy!!!
