Title:
Four Bean Salad with Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can each of:
light kidney beans
wax beans
cut green beans
garbanzo beans
whole kernal corn
1 small can of mushrooms
1 green pepper cubbed
1 onion chopped
1/2 cup of cider vinegar
1/2 cup of sugar
1/4 cup of veg. oil
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano

Directions:
Drain canned vegetables add all ingredients and mix. Then put in refrigerator overnight. After it sits i add another 1/4 to 1/2 cups of sugar. Taste before you add if it has to much of a vinegar taste add the sugar it balances out perfect.
