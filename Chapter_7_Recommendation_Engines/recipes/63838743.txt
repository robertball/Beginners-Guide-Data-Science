Title:
Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066606.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
2 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
2 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
6 cups cold milk
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
8 bananas, sliced
1 (12 ounce) package vanilla wafers

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy.
Beat in condensed milk, pudding mix, cold milk and vanilla until smooth.
Fold in 1/2 of the whipped topping.
Line the bottom of a dish with vanilla wafers.
Arrange sliced bananas evenly over wafers.
Spread with pudding mixture.
Repeat 2 more times.
Top with remaining whipped topping.
Chill.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Less than ten ingredients, including condensed milk, whipped topping, and vanilla wafers, are combined in this quick and easy banana pudding.Modified from: Banana Pudding IV
