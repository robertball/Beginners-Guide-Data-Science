Title:
Yummy cheeseball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/945209.jpg
Ingredients:
2-8oz pkg. cream cheese (softened)
3 pkg. sliced beef or ham chopped, I use the budding brand they are like 59cents
4-6 green onion, chopped
3 Tbls. worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except one package of chopped meat. shape into a ball and cover with remaining meat. wrap and chill for at least an hour. Serve with  crackers

Submitter: 
Mary Bailey Kidder
Description: 
This is my go to for parties, it is super easy and I never have any left.
