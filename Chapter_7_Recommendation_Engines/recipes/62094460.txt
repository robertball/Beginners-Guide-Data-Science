Title:
Cheesy Chicken and Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lbs. Rotini
1 can (10.5 oz) Cream of Chicken Soup
1 can (10.5 oz) Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 can (15 oz) Veg-All
1 jar (16 oz) Ragu Cheese Sauce
1 can (10 oz) Chicken
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Bring Rotini to Boil in a pot of salted water.
Mix all other canned/jarred ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
Drain pasta and place into a nonstick 9x13 pan.
Add mixed ingredients to pasta and stir through.
Top with shredded cheese.
Bake until heated through.
Serve with tossed salad and biscuits.

Submitter: 
Starla Middlebrooks
Description: 
Tender chicken and veggies tossed with Rotini in a cheesy sauce.
