Title:
Chocolate Tofu Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-12 oz bag of milk chocolate chips
1-12 oz block of silken tofu
1/4 cup strongly brewed coffee
1-2 Tbsp vanilla flavoring
1-Graham cracker pie crust
Raspberries or other berries for garnish

Directions:
Melt chocolate chips in the microwave or on stove top. Blend together tofu, chocolate chips, coffee and vanilla.
Pour into graham cracker crust and chill 1-2 hours in the freezer or 4 or more hours in the refrigerator.
Garnish with fresh raspberries or whichever berries you have chosen.
Serve and enjoy. Peanut butter recipe in the notes section.
