Title:
Chocolate Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¾ cup flour
¾ cup sugar
1 ½  t baking powder
½ t baking soda
¼ t salt
1/3 cup plus ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa
½ cup milk
2 1/2 T butter, melted
1 tsp vanilla extract
½ cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 ¾ cups boiling water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a bowl, mix flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt and ? cup cocoa.  In a cup, combine milk, butter and vanilla.  Stir into dry ingredients until just blended.  Batter will be thick.  Spoon batter into a buttered or sprayed 8 x 8 inch baking dish.
In a small bowl, combine brown sugar and remaining ¼ cup cocoa.  Sprinkle evenly over batter.  Pour boiling water over entire mixture.  Do not stir.  Bake until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, about 30 minutes  Cool slightly.  Serve warm with ice cream or whipped cream and a sprinkle of nuts.
