Title:
Creamy Fra Diavolo with Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (5.5 Oz) Tomato Paste
1 cans (28 Oz.) Peeled Tomatoes (Or two 15 Oz Can)
½ cups White Wine
¼ cups Heavy Cream
3 Tablespoons Minced Garlic
2 Teaspoons Red Pepper
2 Tablespoons Oregano
2 Tablespoons Basil
¼ cups Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 box of any spaghetti or angel hair pasta
Optional
1 pound of cooked lobster (chopped) or 1 pound
of shrimp
2 teaspoons Red Pepper
¼ cups Heavy Cream

Directions:
Preparation Instructions
In a food processor add the tomato paste, tomato sauce, and peeled tomatoes. Pulse in the food processor for just a few seconds until the mixture is blended but chunky.
Pour mixture into a large sauce pan and add the remaining ingredients. Simmer for a mimimum of 20 minutes. The longer you let it marry together, the better.
Cook the pasta as instructed.

Submitter: 
Nick Gibietis
Description: 
This is a great slightly spicy sauce that is great with any pasta dish. This is also a changes on the traditional Fra Diavolo sauce in that it adds cream for a richer variation on the original. 

You can add lobster or shrimp to the mixture for a seafood addition to the dish.
