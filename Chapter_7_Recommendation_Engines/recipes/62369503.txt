Title:
8 Layer Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 10" flour tortillas
1 lb ground beef
1 can pinto beans
3 c cheddar cheese
2 cans cream of chicken soup
2 c salsa

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Cook ground beef till done and crumbly, drain. Spray 9x13 baking pan with non stick cooking spray. Cover bottom and sides with 4 tortillas, they will overlap. Make a layer with the beef. Sprinkle on the pinto beans with juice. Sprinkle on 1 c cheese. Cover with 4 tortillas. Spread cream of chicken soup over tortillsa,then salsa on top of soup. Sprinkle with rest of cheese. Cover with tented foil and bake 1 hour. Let sit 15 minutes before serving. Slice into squares.
