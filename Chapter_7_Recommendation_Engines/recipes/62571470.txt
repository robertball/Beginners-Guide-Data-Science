Title:
Oatmeal Applesauce Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
2 cups sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp. baking soda
2 cups applesauce
2 tsp. nutmeg
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. salt
4 1/2 cups flour
1 package chocolate chips
2 cups oatmeal

Directions:
Cream shortening, sugar and eggs together.
In a different bowl add baking soda to applesauce, then mix this with sugar, shortening and egg mixture.
Add remaining ingredients, but add oatmeal last.
Make 1-2" balls of dough, place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake at 425' for 10 minutes.
