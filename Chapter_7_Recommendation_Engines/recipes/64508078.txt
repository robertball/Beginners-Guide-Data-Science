Title:
Chicken Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cooked chicken, coarsley chopped or shredded
1 whole onion, chopped
1 1/2 tsp cumin
2 tsp minced garlic
1 1/2 tsp salt
4 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp pepper
1 c water
12 whole corn tortillas
16 ounces enchilada sauce
1 c light sour cream
8 ounces reduced fat cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Grease 13x9 baking dish.
In skillet, saute chicken and onions.   When onions are tender, add next 6 ingredients.  Simmer for 10 minutes or until liquid mostly evaporates.
Pour 1/2 cup enchilada sauce into baking dish.  Arrange 1/2 of tortillas to cover bottom of dish (easier if you cut them in half).  Pour 1/2 cup sauce over tortillas.  Spoon into meat mixture.  Top with sour cream and sprinkle on 1/2 of the cheese.  Arrange remaining tortillas overlapping on top of cheese.   Spread remaining sauce on top and top with remaining cheese.
Cover with foil and bake for 40 minutes.
Remove foil and bake an additional 5 minutes.
