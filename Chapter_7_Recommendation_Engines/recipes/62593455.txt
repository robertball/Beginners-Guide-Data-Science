Title:
Mom's Refrigerator Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 c. Sugar
1 1/3 c. Vinegar
2 T. Pickling Salt
1 Onion
Cucumbers Sliced thin (enough to fill jar)
1/2 t. Turmeric
1/2 t. Celery Seed
1/2 t. Mustard Seed

Directions:
Combine and pour over onions & cucumbers.
Refrigerate at least 5 days.  Will keep for months if refrigerated.
Makes 1 quart.
