Title:
Chicken & Black Bean Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871613.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (pound) Ground Chicken Breast
1 package taco seasoning
2/3 cup water
1 (15 ounce) can Black Beans
7 ounces crema mexicana
8 low carb tortillas
1 (10 3/4 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
1 (2 ounce) can sliced black olives
3/4 cup medium salsa

Directions:
Cook ground chicken. Add taco seasoning and water and black beans. Then add 1/2 the olives and cream. Stir until creamy, wrap in tortillas. Mix the rest of the olives, soup and salsa together in a medium bowl. Pour over tortillas. Bake for 20 minutes at 350 until heated through.
