Title:
root beer tritip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 3 lb tri tip
1 liter of root beer
10 bay leaves
1 tbs of kosher salt

Directions:
pour root beer in a big bowl add salt, bay leaves.
mix, pour over tri tip, cover place in frig.
take tri tip out of pan place on grill or place in oven proof pan.
cook to 135 or more. take tri tip when ready out of grill, oven. place in clean pan let it rest for 10 to 14 minutes.
