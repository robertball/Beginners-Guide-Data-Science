Title:
BBQ  Finishing Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup crushed ginger
1/2 cup crushed garlic
1/2 cup orange marmalade

Directions:
mix all ingredients in a bowl. Brush on your favorite BBQ dish just prior to removing from grill. Makes an excellent dipping sauce for shrimp.

Submitter: 
Luchy Luchtenberg
Description: 
A tasty easy finishing sauce for ribs, chicken and shrimp.
