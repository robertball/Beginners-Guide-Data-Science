Title:
Jan's Fruity Cookie Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 and 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg yolk
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
2 and 1/4 cups flour
1 - 8 oz jar of Lemon curd or a 12 oz can of cherry cake and pastry filling (this is not jelly, jam or plain pie filling)
powdered sugar

Directions:
In mixer bowl beat the butter, sugar and salt until light in color.  Add in egg yolk and extract beat well.
Blend in flour just until dough forms.
Divide dough into 4 pieces.  Roll into a 12 inch rope.  Place on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper.
Using your the side of your pinky finger make an indent down the length of the dough.  Keeping in about 1/2 inch from ends.  Widen this indent with your index finger to a depth of approximately 1/3 of the dough. Repeat with the remaining dough placing 2 cookies per sheet.
Fill the dough with the pastry filling keeping level with the dough.  Be careful not to over fill.
Refrigerate filled cookies for at least 30 minutes.
Bake in a preheated oven at 350 degrees for about 20 - 25 minutes.  Cookies are done when golden brown and dough feels "set".  Cool on cookie sheet for 10 minutes then remove to wire rack to finish cooling completely.
Once cool slice on the diagonal in 1 inch in width.  Dust with powdered sugar.
Store in airtight container.
