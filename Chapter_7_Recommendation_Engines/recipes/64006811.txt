Title:
Broccolious Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1112167.jpg
Ingredients:
Seedless green grapes 1-2 cups
Cucumber 1 (6 inches long)
Broccoli 2 cups
Lime 1
Cold water 1/2 - 1 cup approx.

Directions:
Chop cucumber and cut broccoli.
Place everything in the blender, soft ingredients at the bottom. Blend until smooth.
