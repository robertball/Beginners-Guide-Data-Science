Title:
Meatball Sub Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 C. chopped green onion
1/4 C.sceasoned bread crumbs
3 T. grated parmesan cheese
1 lb. ground beef
1 loaf Italian bread
1 8 oz. package of cream cheese, softened
1/2 C. mayonnaise
1 t. Italian seasoning
1/4 t. pepper
2 C. shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
1 28 oz. jar of spaghetti sauce
1 C. water
2 cloves of minced garlic

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the green onion, bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, and ground beef.  Mix well and shape into 1" meatballs.  Place on a baking sheet and bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes (or until no longer pink).
While the meatballs are baking, cut the Italian bread into 1" slices and arrange in a single layer in an ungreased 9x13 in. baking dish.
Combine the cream cheese, mayonnaise, Italian seasoning, and pepper.  Spread the mixture over the bread slices.
Sprinkle 1/2 if the cheese over the bread.
Combine the spaghetti sauce, water, and garlic.  Add meatballs to the sauce and poor over the bread.  Sprinkle casserole with remaining cheese.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
