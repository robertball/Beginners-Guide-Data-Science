Title:
Big Ray's Skillet Parmesan Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3771810.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 small red pepper, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
4 small zucchini (1 pound), thinly sliced
1 14 oz can diced tomatoes
1 8 oz can tomato sauce
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp oregano
1 /2 tsp granulated onions
2 cloves garlic, minced
3/4 cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
2 Tbsp Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef until no longer pink, drain and set aside.  In same pan, cook  peppers and onions. Add zucchini; cook and stir 2 min.
Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato sauce and spices; cover. Cook 8 min. or until vegetables are crisp-tender, stirring occasionally.
Sprinkle with cheeses; cook, covered, 1 min. or until mozzarella is melted.
