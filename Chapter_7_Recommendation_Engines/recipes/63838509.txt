Title:
Rich Chocolate Cookies (Low Glycemic) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066550.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup rye flour
1/2 cup brown rice flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup cacao powder
3/4 cup cacao nibs
1 tablespoon lecithin
1/2 cup walnuts
3 tablespoons flax seeds
1 cup warm water
2 ounces unsweetened chocolate
3/4 cup agave
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 F.
Add warm water to flax seeds.
Let sit for 5-10 minutes.
Add unsweetened chocolate, agave and vanilla.
Blend at a high speed in blender.
In a mixing bowl, combine flours, baking powder, salt, cacao powder, cacao nibs, lecithin, walnuts.
Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients. Mix well.
Spoon out onto parchment paper.
Bake on parchment paper for 10-12 minutes at 350 degrees.
