Title:
Betty Crocker Brown Mix Clone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129266.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Sugar
1/2 Cup Flour
1/3 Cup Cocoa
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1/4 teaspoon Baking Powder

Directions:
Mix all these ingredients together with a whisk and dump them into a zipper bag.  On the bag, add the following instructions:
To Prepare:  Add 2 Eggs, 1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil, 1 teaspoon Vanilla.  Bake @ 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes in a 9 x 9 pan (30 to 33 minutes in 8x8).
