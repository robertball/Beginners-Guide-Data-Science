Title:
Pumpkin Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/299278.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
1 1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup pumpkin puree
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1 T cream of tartar
1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp. ground ginger
1/4 tsp. ground allspice
1/4 tsp. ground cloves
extra sugar for rolling cookies in

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.  Place parchment paper on cookie sheets.
Cream shortening and sugar.
Add eggs and pumpkin.
Combine all the other ingredients except the extra sugar, add to mixture in bowl.
Shape in small balls (the mixture is somewhat sticky). Roll in extra sugar, place on cookie sheets.
Bake at 400 about 8 minutes, remove from oven, cool. Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
Light, airy, sweet spice cookies.  I developed this recipe as a way to enjoy the rest of the can of pumpkin.
