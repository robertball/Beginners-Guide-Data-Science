Title:
Skeleton Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3807523.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
2 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup cocoa
1 tsp. baking soda
1 1/2 cups icing sugar
2 tbsp. milk

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
Beat in egg and vanilla.
Combine flour, cocoa and soda; gradually add to creamed mixture and mix well.
Cover and refrigerate x 1-2 hours or until easy to handle.
On a lightly floured surface, roll to 1/8" or a bit thicker. Cut with gingerbread man cookie cutter.
Bake on cookie sheets @ 375 x 7-8 min or until set.
Cool a few minutes before removing from pan to wire racks to cool completely.
For icing, combine icing sugar and milk until smooth. Pipe skeleton bones on cookies.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
These are a fun Halloween cookie and a clever way to use a gingerbread man cookie cutter! AND, they're chocolate!!
