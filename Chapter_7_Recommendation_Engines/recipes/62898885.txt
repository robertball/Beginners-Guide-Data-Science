Title:
Chicken Tamale Casseole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/833821.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup (4 oz.) preshredded 4-cheese Mexican blend cheese, divided
1/3 cup fat-free milk
1/4 cup egg substitute
1 tsp ground cumin
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1 (14 3/4 oz.) can cream-style corn
1 (8.5 oz.) box corn muffin mix
1 (4 oz.) can chopped grene chilies, drained
Cooking spray
1 (10 oz.) can red enchilada sauce
2 cups shredded cooked chicken breast
1/2 cup fat-free sour cream

Directions:
Preaheat oven to 400 degrees.
Combine 1/4 cup cheese and next 7 ingredients (through chilies) in a large bowl, stirring just until moist.  Pour mixture into a 13x9 inch baking dish coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes or until set.  Pierce entire surface liberally with a fork; pour enchilada sauce over top.  Top with chicken; sprinkle with remaining 3/4 cup cheese.
Bake at 400 degreees for 15 minutes or until cheese melts.  Remove from oven; let stand 5 minutes.  Cut into 8 pieces; top each serving with 1 tablespoon sour cream.
