Title:
Baked Ranch Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460594.jpg
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/3 cup teriyaki basting sauce
1/3 cup light Ranch salad dressing*
1 cup light aged shredded Cheddar cheese
3 green onions, chopped
1.5 oz real bacon bits
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Brown chicken breasts in a large nonstick skillet. Place chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Brush with teriyaki sauce, then spoon on salad dressing. Sprinkle with cheese, green onions and bacon bits.
Bake for 25 to 35 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear. Garnish with parsley and serve.
