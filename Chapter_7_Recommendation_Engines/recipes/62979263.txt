Title:
Frog Eye Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/738351.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. package acini di pepe pasta
1 package (6 serving size) vanilla pudding mix
8 oz. package whipped topping
20 oz. can pineapple tidbits, drained
10 oz. jar maraschino cherries, chopped
1 c. mini marshmallows
1 c. sweetened shredded coconut flakes

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain pasta. Cool.
Prepare pudding according to package instructions. Cool.
Mix cooled pasta, pudding, whipped topping, pineapple, cherries, marshmallows, and coconut in a large bowl.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Chris Cavanaugh
Description: 
This simple side dish is like a pasta salad version of a vanilla ice cream sundae.  It comes together very easily and tastes great.  Large yield is perfect for parties or family gatherings.  Prep time includes cooling time.
