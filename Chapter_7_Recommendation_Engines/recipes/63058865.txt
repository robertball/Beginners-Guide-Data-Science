Title:
Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123079.jpg
Ingredients:
1 white or yellow cake mix
1 pkg (3.4 oz.) instant vanilla or butterscotch pudding
1 c. sour cream (non fat)
1/2 c. applesauce (in place of oil)
4 eggs
1/2 c. nuts
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. white sugar

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingredients together (batter).
Mix last 4 ingredients together (filling).
Put 1/2 of batter in lightly sprayed 9x12 cake pan.
Put 1/2 of filling over batter.
Put remainder of batter then the last of the filling on top.
Bake @ 350 degrees for 35-45 minutes. (Cupcakes- 28 min.)
