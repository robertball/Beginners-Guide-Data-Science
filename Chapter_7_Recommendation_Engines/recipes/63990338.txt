Title:
Hamburgers with Quinoa & Black Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1107253.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs Lean ground beef
1 Cup cooked Quinoa
1 Cup black beans pureed
1 Egg
2 Tbsp Minced Onion

Directions:
1) Mix all ingredients together in a mixing bowl
2) Form into patties
3) Cook in frying pan, covered on med high approx 10 min on each side.
Makes 8 burgers

Submitter: 
Bernadette Feakes
Description: 
Quinoa is a great substitution for bread crumbs. The black beans add extra nutrients to the burger. Add your favorite seasons to the mix before cooking. Top with your favorite burger toppings. The family loved it and they had no idea what was in it!
This is also a gluten free recipe.
