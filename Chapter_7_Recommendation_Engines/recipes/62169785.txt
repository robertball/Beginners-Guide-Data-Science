Title:
Easy Spicy Ketchup Dip for Sweet Potato Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. Ketchup
1/2 tsp. Garlic Salt
1 1/2 tsp. Cayenne Pepper
2 tsp. brown sugar or 1/2 tsp. molasses
1 TBSP. Worcestershire Sauce
1 TBSP. spicy Horseradish

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in bowl.
Serve with a plate of oven-baked sweet potato fries.
The longer you let this sit, the better.

Submitter: 
BrookeMT
Description: 
A Ketchup based dip that is sweet & spicy with plenty of zing that is best served with sweet potato french fries.  My friends and family can't get enough of this stuff!
