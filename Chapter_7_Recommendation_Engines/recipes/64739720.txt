Title:
Low-carb, chicken mushroom meal with white-wine creamy garlic sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6372089.jpg
Ingredients:
1 chicken breast (8 oz)
1/4th cup flour
1 Tbsp blackening seasoning, or equivalent salt/pepper/paprika
1/4th red pepper cut into small pieces
1/4th green pepper cut into small pieces
2 pats butter
3 slices yellow onion
1/2 tsp crushed garlic
Handful of sliced mushrooms
White cooking wine
Chicken stock
Lite cream or half-and-half

Directions:
Pound the heck out of the chicken breast until it's flat
Slice it up thinly into strips
Cut in half or thirds to bite-sized pieces
Season with blackening seasoning, both sides
Put flour into mixing bowl
Put chicken chunks into mixing bowl, coat everything with flour
Heat up pan, put in sesame oil and one pat of butter
Put in chicken, peppers and onions, cook till golden and tender
Add second pat of butter
Keep flipping and stirring until chicken properly cooked (165 degrees)
Take off heat
Add 1/8th cup white wine, let it sizzle for 30 seconds
Add 1/4th cup chicken stock
Add 1/2 tsp crushed garlic
Put back on heat
Slowly add in 1/8th cup lite cream/half-and-half, stirring in slowly, bit-by-bit so the sauce doesn't separate.
Garnish with green onion.
Salt to taste.

Submitter: 
Jessica Myers
Description: 
This is a low-carb (1.6g) meal that is fulfilling and tasty. Also only takes 15 minutes to make. Serves two.
