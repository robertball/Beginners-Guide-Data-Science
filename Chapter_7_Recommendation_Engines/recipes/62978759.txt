Title:
Cranberry Apple Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/751539.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Eggs
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup packed dark brown sugar
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup 1% Milk
1/2 cup I can't believe it's not butter, softened
1 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
3 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
3 apples, peeled, cored and finely chopped
1 cup of Cranberries, chopped
2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 teaspoon ground allspice
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Butter a 10 inch bunt pan.
Whisk together flour, spices, and salt, powder. Set aside.
Cream together butter and sugar. Mix in eggs and vanilla. Stir together soda and olive oil, sour cream. Stir in flour mixture, apples, and cranberries until well blended.
Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake for 1 hour 20 mins mins, or until a tester comes out clean.
Let Cool in pan about 30 mins. Once cool, shake pan to loosen cake. Flip over and turn onto plate. Decorate!
