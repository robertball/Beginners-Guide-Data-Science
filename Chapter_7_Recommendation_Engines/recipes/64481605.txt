Title:
Crockpot Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can refried beans
1/2 jar medium salsa
3 Tbsp sour cream
8 oz grated cheese (any type)
1 Tbsp taco seasoning ( I use homemade)

Directions:
Mix all together in crockpot.  Heat on low until cheese is melted and dip is hot.  Serve with tortilla chips.
