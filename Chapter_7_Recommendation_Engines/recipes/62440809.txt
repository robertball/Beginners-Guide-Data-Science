Title:
Pumpkin Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/457576.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake
* 1 1/8 cup all-purpose flour
* 3/4 cup white sugar
* 2 teaspoons baking powder
* 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon Cinnamon
* 1/4 teaspoon Ginger
* 1/8 teaspoon Allspice
* 1/8 teaspoon Cloves
* 2 whole eggs
* 1/4 cup shortening
* 1 cup Pumpkin Puree
* 1/2 cup milk
* 1 teaspoon pure maple syrup
Filling:
* 1 large apple, peeled and finely chopped
* 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
* 1/8 teaspoon allspice
* 2 tablespoon butter
Frosting:
* 1 Cup Confectioners Sugar
* 2 Tablespoons Butter, Melted
* 1.5 tablespoons milk (or half-n-half)
* 2 tablespoons pure maple syrup

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. For the cake. Mix all of the dry ingredients together in a bowl. In a separate bowl cream together shortening and eggs. Combine all of the dry and wet ingredients, and mix thoroughly.
Place half of the mixture in a greased pie pan, and place in the oven. Cook until a butter knife comes out clean about 15 minutes. Repeat for the remainder of the batter.
4. While baking prepare the filling, in a saucepan melt butter over medium heat, add remainder of ingredients, mix well and cover; allow the mixture to simmer for about 15 minutes or until apples have become very soft.(When its finished you can choose to puree the apple mixture or leave it whole)
5. For the frosting mix all ingredients until the mixture if very smooth.
To assemble, place one part of the cake down, place the filling on top, place the other cake on top. When cooled, lightly spread the frosting and place in the fridge or enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sarah Garland
Description: 
I make this to eat for breakfast over several days. I use pure maple syrup and not an extract, but it shouldn't matter. It will make a fairly flat cake.
