Title:
Easy Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bowl taco seasoned ground beef
4 oz Seven Seas salad dressing
1 bag Doritos chips, crushed
1 bag ready made salad mix
1 chopped tomato

Directions:
Heat taco meat then combine all ingredients in a large bowl, expect doritos, and toss until well coated.  Serve with doritos.

Description: 
A very simple throw together taco salad with zing.
