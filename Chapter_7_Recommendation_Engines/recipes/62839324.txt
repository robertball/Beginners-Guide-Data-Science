Title:
No Bake Strawberry Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ready made graham cracker pie crust
1 (3 ounce) package strawberry flavored gelatin mix
1 cup boiling water
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 (5 ounce) can cold evaporated milk

Directions:
Dissolve strawberry gelatin in boiling water. Let cool in the fridge until thick, but not set. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese, white sugar and vanilla until smooth. Set aside.
In a separate bowl, whip evaporated milk, til it thickens . Pour in strawberry gelatin and keep mixing until well blended. Fold in cream cheese mixture.  It is liquid-y, but it will thicken and set in the fridge.
Place pie shell on pan.  Pour filling into crust. Be careful, it will be very full. Chill in refrigerator for at least 3 1/2 hours before serving.
