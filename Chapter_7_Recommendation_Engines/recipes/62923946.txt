Title:
Pudding Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small pkg. INSTANT chocolate pudding
3/4 C. milk
1/4 C. Vodka
1/2 C. Irish Cream
8 oz. Extra Creamy Cool Whip

Directions:
Mix pudding and milk for a couple of minutes with an electric mixer, add alcohol, and then mix well.  Now it's time to add the Cool Whip.
Then all you have to do is put the pudding mix into individual serving cups with lids.  You may want to provide some plastic spoons.  Keep in the freezer.

Awesome alternative to jello shots
