Title:
Big Ray's Parmesan Butternut Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6828571.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium butternut squash (about 3 pounds), peeled and cut into 1" cubes
2 Tbsp water
1/3 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Place squash and water in a 9 x 13 baking dish. Cover with foil and roast squash for 30 minutes, or until tender.
Preheat broiler.  Toss bread crumbs with cheese, salt ,pepper, cinnamon and nutmeg; sprinkle over squash. Broil 3-4 in. from heat 1-2 minutes or until topping is golden brown.
