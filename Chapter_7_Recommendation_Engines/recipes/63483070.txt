Title:
Fresh Cranberry Orange Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz fresh or frozen cranberries
1 med orange
3/4 to 1 cup sugar
may add: 1 orange, 1/2 lemon, 1/2 lime -seeded chopped, rind and all

Directions:
slice unpeeled orange into eights. remove seeds.  place half the cranberries and half the orange slices into food processor container.  process until mixture is evenly chopped.  transfer to bowl. repeat with remaining cranberries and orange slices.  stir in sugar to desired sweetness.  store in refrigerator or freezor.  makes about 2.5 cups
