Title:
Soft Peanut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white sugar
1/2 cup white corn syrup
1/2 cup water
2 cups salted peanuts
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda

Directions:
In a 4 quart saucepan over medium heat, combine the sugar, corn syrup and water. Heat to 295 degrees. Remove from heat and quickly add combined remaining ingredients. Stir well and pour onto 12 X 18 buttered cookie sheet, quickly spread and make even with forks. Cool, break into pieces. Store in air tight container.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description:
