Title:
Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 C chocolate chips
3 14oz. cans sweetened, condensed milk
3/4 C butter
4 C nuts, any kind (optional)

Directions:
Place chocolate chips, sweetened condensed milk, and butter in a large microwaveable container.  Microwave on medium power until chips are melted, about 3 minutes, stirring once or twice.  Stir in nuts if desired.  Pour into a well-greased hotel pan and refrigerate until set.
