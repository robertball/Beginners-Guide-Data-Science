Title:
Nappa Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Head of Nappa Cabbage
4 spring onions,diced
1 pkg of Ramen Noodles
1C Sunflower Seeds
1/2 stick butter
Dressing:
1/2C vinegar
1/2C oil
2T sugar
2T soy sauce

Directions:
Saute Butter with Ramen noodles and sunflower seeds until brown...let cool, chop up nappa cabbage into chuncks place in large bowl with diced onions,ramen noodles and sunflower seeds and toss with mixed dressing.........
