Title:
Banana and Nutella French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 banana, sliced
4 slices of bread
4 tbsp nutella
2 tbsp butter
2 eggs
1/4 cup chocolate milk
1 tbsp vanilla
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
1.  Spread nutella on one side of each slice of bread.
2.  Put banana slices on two of the slices of bread, and top to form two sandwiches with banana and nutella in the middle.
3.  Combine and whisk eggs, chocolate milk, vanilla and cinnamon.
4.  Slowly heat butter on a frying pan.
5.  Dip each sandwich in the french toast mixture, turning if needed to cover both sides with the mixture.
6.  Cook on low-med heat, turning as needed until both sides are golden brown and banana and chocolate has softened.

Submitter: 
Nico
Description: 
Excellent for breakfast or dessert, best served warm.  Serve with icecream or fruit.
