Title:
No-Bake Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/751823.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups white sugar
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup whole milk
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup of natural peanut butter (or nut butter of your choice)
3 cups quick oatmeal
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Mix sugar, cocoa, butter and milk in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Boil for one minute
Remove from heat and add peanut butter. Mix until smooth
Add oatmeal and vanilla. Mix well.
Drop spoonfuls on wax paper
Let them cool and harden up for about 60 minutes

Submitter: 
KitkatJohn
Description: 
No need to bake these very delicious cookies. They do contain peanut butter, but any nut butter can be substituted
