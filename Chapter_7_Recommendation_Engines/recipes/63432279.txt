Title:
Potato Leak Latkes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/929993.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium Russet potatoes
1 leak
3 eggs
1/4 cup flax seed meal
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup grated parmesan or romano
sour cream as topping

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350Â°
Cut potatoes and leak into small pieces
Process chopped vegetables in food processor until they become a mash
Add eggs, flax, and salt then process until incorporated
Spoon mixture on to baking sheet in 3" flat circles
Sprinkle cheese on top and bake for 20 minutes
Serve with a dollop of sour cream

Submitter: 
Michael Zick Doherty
Description: 
This recipe is quick and gluten free meal using late fall crops.
