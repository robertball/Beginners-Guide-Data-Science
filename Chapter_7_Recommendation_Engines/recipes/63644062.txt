Title:
Hickory Smoked Turkey Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz al fresco ABF Hickory Smoked Turkey Breast thinly sliced
1 Ciabatta Sandwich Roll sliced in half
1/4 leaf red leaf lettuce
1/4 fresh tomatoes, thinly sliced
1 slice low sodium cheddar cheese

Directions:
To assemble the sandwich: Place lettuce leaf on the bottom of ciabatta roll (or your favorite bread), top with sliced Hickory Smoked Turkey Breast meat, tomato and cheese. Use your favorite sandwich accompaniment to complete this gourmet sandwich such as: Dijon mustard, reduced fat mayonnaise, bread & butter pickles, sliced cucumber, roasted red peppers.
