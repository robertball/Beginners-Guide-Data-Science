Title:
Cheese sauce for vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2Tbsp butter
2Tbsp flour
1/4tsp salt
1/8tsp pepper
1C milk
4 slices of american cheese

Directions:
Melt butter in saucepan. Blen in flour salt and pepper and milk. Melt in cheese.
