Title:
Flavorful Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups water
1-1/2 cups uncooked white rice
1 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 pinch salt
1/2 pinch ground black pepper
1/2 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained

Directions:
In your rice steamer combine the water, rice, olive oil, vinegar, basil, oregano, salt, pepper and tomatoes.  Cook according to steamer's settings.
