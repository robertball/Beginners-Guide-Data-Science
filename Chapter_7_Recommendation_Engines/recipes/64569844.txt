Title:
Turkey and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3524907.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 cup finely diced carrots
1/2 cup finely diced celery
1 medium onion, finely diced
1 lb diced turkey (or chicken)
1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
1/4 teaspoon turmeric
6 cups turkey or chicken broth
salt & pepper to taste
1 tablespoon flour
250	g	self-raising flour
125	g	cold butter, grated
100 - 200 ml of cold water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Put oil in hot pot, add the diced carrots, celery and onions, saute over medium-low heat for 3 to 4 minutes.
Stir in the poultry seasoning and turmeric, saute for 1 min.
Whisk together flour and broth then pour into pot, stir in and bring to a boil.
Add the turkey.
Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
Put self-raising flour in a mixing bowl.
Add grated butter and rub together until it is the the size of a pea.
Add 100ml - 200ml of cold water and bring into a ball of dough.
Divide into 10 - 12 dumpling balls.
Put stew in an oven proof dish and drop dumplings on top.
Bake, uncovered for 30 min.

Submitter: 
Jordan VanDijk
Description: 
Great option for left over turkey or even chicken. A yummy take on my mom's turkey pot pie. Can't resist that fluffy topping.
