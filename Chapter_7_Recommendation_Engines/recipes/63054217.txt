Title:
Broccoli and Cauliflower Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh broccoli
2 cups fresh cauliflower
2 eggs
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 medium onion finely chopped
1 can cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 cup melted butter
1  box seasoned croutons

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*F. Cook vegetables separately for 3minutes and drain. Distribute evenly in a lightly buttered casserole. Beat eggs and combine with mayonnaise, onions and soup and 1/2 of the cheese. Pour evenly over vegetables. Sprinkle remaining cheese over top. Place croutons on top of cheese and drizzle with melted butter.  Bake  40 minutes.
