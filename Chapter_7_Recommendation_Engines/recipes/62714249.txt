Title:
Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups canned peaches, do not drain
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup milk
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon corn starch
1 cup hot water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Pour peaches into a large 9 by 13 baking pan.
In a bowl mix by hand; 3/4 cup sugar, butter, milk, cinnamon, egg, salt, flour and baking powder.  Pour mixture over peaches.
In a small bowl combine the 1 cup sugar and corn starch.
Pour the corn starch mixture as the next layer.
Then pour the hot water on top.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
The crust/dough cooks into the peachy flavor.  This is an easy and very good Texas peach cobbler.  Serve  with  scoop of French Vanilla or Homemade Vanilla ice cream.
