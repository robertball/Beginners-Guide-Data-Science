Title:
Kaylee's Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1791500.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cup butter
2 cups white sugar
2 cups brown sugar
4 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
6 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk chocolate chips
1/2 cup white chocolate chips
1/2 cup Skor bits

Directions:
Cream together butter and sugars in Bosch
Add eggs one at a time
Combine dry ingredients in a separate bowl
Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients in Bosch (add half, mix lightly, add remaining dry ingredients and mix)
Add chocolate chips and Skor bits
Roll into small balls (1 1/2 tbsp.)
Bake at 350 for 10-11 minutes (once cookie cracks and bottoms are golden brown)
Let cool 10 minutes on cookie sheet then transfer to cooling racks.
Freeze in freezer Ziploc bags
