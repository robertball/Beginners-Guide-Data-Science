Title:
Juicy Deer & Bacon Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground deer
1/2 lb lean ground beef
6 slices Hickory Smoked bacon
1 can Guinness
dash of Lea & Perrins
dash of kosher salt
dash of fresh cracked pepper

Directions:
Combine ground deer and lean ground beef and in a small bowl until well blended; make patties.
Put onto preheated skillet on med/low heat
Sprinkle salt, fresh cracked pepper, a dash of Lea & Perrins, and a dash of Guinness.  Cook burgers without flipping for about 5-10 mins.
On a separate skillet, cook 6 strips of bacon until done and set aside.
Now, flip and add another dash of Guinness and a dash of Lea & Perrins.
Continue to cook until done, then add 1 strip of bacon broken in half on top.
Serve with or without cheese on a hamburger bun.
For an additional topping idea, try sauteed onions by slicing a med sized onion in 1/2 inch slices and cook over 1 TBS olive oil with a dash of salt.
