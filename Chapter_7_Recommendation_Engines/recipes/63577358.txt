Title:
Caraway Cabbage, Carrots and Leeks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBSP BUTTER
3 1/2 CUPS OF LEEKS
4 CARROTS
1 CUP CHICKEN BROTH
1/2 WHITE GRAPE JUICE
1 TSP CARAWAY SEEDS
2 BAY LEAVES
1/2 TSP SALT
1/4 TSP PEPPER
2 LBS CABBAGE

Directions:
CUT LEEKS LENGTHWISE IN HALF AND CROSSWISE INTO 1/2" SLICES
CUT CARROTS INTO 1 1/2" PIECES
CUT CABBAGE INTO WEDGES
IN LARGE POT, MELT BUTTER OVER MEDIUM HEAT
ADD LEEKS AND CARROTS; COOK, STIRRING OCCASIONALLY, UNTIL VEGETABLES ARE SOFTENED, ABOUT 5 MINUTES.
STIR IN BROTH, JUICE, CARAWAY SEEDS, BAY LEAVES, SALT AND PEPPER; ADD CABBAGE.
COVER AND COOK OVER MEDIUM-LOW HEAT UNTIL CABBAGE AND CARROTS ARE TENDER, 20 - 30 MINUTES.
