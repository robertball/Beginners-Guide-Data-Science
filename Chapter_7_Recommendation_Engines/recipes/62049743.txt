Title:
Twinlow Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups brown sugar
3 cups butter or margarine
6 eggs
3 cups peanut butter
7 1/2 cups AP flour
2 Tablespoons baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
granulated sugar as needed

Directions:
Heat oven to 350*F.  Cream together the butter, peanut butter and sugar.  Add the eggs.  Add the flour mixed with soda and salt.  Roll Tablespoon size balls of dough in granulated sugar, place on a parchment lined baking sheet and flatten and cris-cross with a fork.  Bake 12 Minutes.

Submitter: 
KARINKAY
Description: 
This summer camp favorite makes a delicious Crisp-CHEWY peanut butter cookie.
