Title:
Chicken Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz Philly orginal flavor cooking cream cheese
2 Taco packets
1/2 cup Mayo (not miracle whip)
1/2 cup Sour cream
1/2 cup Parmasan cheese
2 cups Mexican cheese
5 chicken breast
2 tomatoes, diced(opt)
Bagette slices

Directions:
boil and chop chicken into small peices. stir together philly cheese and taco packets, slowly add the rest of the chese an stir. Add chicken last. Bake at 350 for 30 min. serve with bagette slices. Top with tomatoes for added flavor.

Submitter: 
Stacey Gilbreath Randle
Description: 
Easy to make, Kids love it. Its not much to look at but you'll be fighting over the leftovers, if there is any! My family eat this as a meal w/ some choped tomatoes and tortia scoops.
