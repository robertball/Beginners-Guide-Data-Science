Title:
Orange-Rosemary Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 TBSP Chicken Broth
2 TBSP Red Wine Vinegar
2 Tsp Dijon Mustard
1/4 cup Frozen Orange Juice Conentrate, thawed
2 garlic loves, minced
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp Paprika
1/8 tsp Cayenne Pepper
1 TBSP fresh Rosemary, minced

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients together in a small bowl and set aside.  Cover and refrigerate until needed.  Will keep in refrigerator for up to two weeks

Submitter: 
Shannon Campbell
Description: 
So tasty.  Awesome on a fresh spinach salad sprinkled with Feta Cheese and crumbled walnuts.  A strong flavor, so a little bit goes a long way and you don't have to use much.
