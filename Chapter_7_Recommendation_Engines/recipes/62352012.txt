Title:
Flounder with Tomato - Onion Ragout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 teaspoons olive oil
1 medium red onion, thinly sliced
2 cup grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
2 to 4 teaspoons white-wine vinegar
Coarse salt and ground pepper
4 flounder fillets (5 to 6 ounces each)
8 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Tomato-onion ragout
1.	In a 10-inch skillet,  heat 2 1/2 tsp. olive oil over medium.  Add onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until light browned.  Add tomatoes and cook, stirring occasionally, until slightly collapsed.
2.Remove skillet from heat. Add vinegar; stir to combine.  Season with salt and pepper.  Remove to a bowl; cover and keep warm.
Flounder
1.	Wipe skillet clean with paper towels. Place 3 1/2 tsp. olive oil in pan, and heat over medium-high.  Season fish with salt and pepper; place in skillet.  Cook, until lightly browned.
2.Carefully turn fish over; cook until opague throughout..  Transfer fish to a dinner plate.  Spoon tomato-onion ragout over fish; serve with couscous or rice, if desired.  Sprinkle parsley over top.
