Title:
Italian Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz wide egg noodles
3 C whipping cream
1 1/2 C whole milk
1 T all-purpose flour
1/2 t. salt, plus more for pasta water
1/4 t. freshly ground black pepper
2 C (packed) grated Fontina (can be substituted with Mozerella)
3/4 C (packed) finely grated Parmesan
3/4 C (packed) grated mozzarella
4 oz cooked ham, diced, optional
2 T finely chopped fresh Italian parsley leaves

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 450Â°
Grease 9x13 glass BD. Set aside.
Cook the noodles in a large pot until slightly firm. About 5 min.
Drain well, but do not rinse.
Whisk the cream, milk, flour, salt, and pepper in lg MB.
Add the noodles and toss to coat.
Mix cheeses in med. MB. Add most of mixture to noodles.
Transfer the noodle mixture to the prepared baking dish.
Sprinkle remaining cheese mixture over the noodle mixture.
Bake 20 minutes or until sauce bubbles and cheese begins to brown on top.
Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
