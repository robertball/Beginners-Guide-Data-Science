Title:
Vidalia Onion Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (16 oz) fresh coleslaw mix
6 oz red cabbage, thinly sliced, 2 cups
1 scallion, chopped, abt 1/4 cup
3/4 cup golden raisins
2/3 cup bottled Vidalia onion salad dressing
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1 TBL white vinegar
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
1. In large bowl, toss together coleslaw mix, red cabbage, scallions and raisins.
2. In separate bowl, whisk together salad dressing, mayo, vinegar, salt and pepper until smooth.
3. Power dressing mixture over coleslaw mixture until evenly coated. Serve immediately or cover and refrigerate until ready to serve
