Title:
Hard Cider Onions for Hot Dogs or Sausages Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp butter
2 large whole sweet onions, sliced longways root-to-root (approx 2 cups)
1/2 cup hard cider (such as Woodchuck Amber)
1/8 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp garlic sea salt (such as Morton's)
sugar as needed, OPTIONAL

Directions:
Fry onions in butter and garlic salt 5-10 minutes or until tender and a tiny bit of brown on the bottom of the pan. Add cider and pepper and simmer covered 10 minutes, uncovered 5 minutes or until the liquid evaporates. Add sugar as necessary to taste. Use tongs to serve on hot dogs or other sausages in buns with spicy mustard.
(Can use any beer plus additional sugar as necessary to suit your tastebuds.)

Submitter: 
JARRIE
Description: 
Sweet onions cooked in butter and hard apple cider til tender and flavorful make a sweet & sassy complement to your favorite frank-on-a-bun!
