Title:
Green Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T extra virgin olive oil
1 large onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
4 green chiles (Hatch)
2 T flour
1/4 cup sherry
2 cups chicken stock
1/8 t caraway seed
1/4 t ground cumin

Directions:
Prepare the chiles:
Heat broiler to high
Line sheet pan with foil and place chiles on pan
Roast chiles about 4" from heat source, until blistered and blacked, apx 3 minutes each side
Place roasted chiles in bowl and cover with plastic wrap.  Let sit apx 10 to 15 minutes to loosen skin.
Making sauce:
Once chiles have cooled, remove skins and seeds, and coarsely chop.
In a large saucepan, heat oil over medium high heat.
Caramelize onions in hot oil, stirring occasionally, until lightly brown, apx 5 to 7 minutes.
Add garlic and cook until aromatic, apx 1 minute
Lower heat to medium and whisk in flour and stir constantly for 3 or 4 minutes.
Add sherry, chiles, chicken broth, caraway, and cumin, and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes or until slightly thickened.
Carefully pour sauce into a blender and process until smooth.

Submitter:

Description: 
A mildly spicy green chili sauce with an out of this world smokey sweetness.  Perfect for enchiladas, tacos, burritos, or (my personal favorite) as a dip for tortilla chips.
