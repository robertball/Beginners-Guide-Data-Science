Title:
Healthy Mozzarella Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006392.jpg
Ingredients:
12 sticks of string cheese in your choice of cheese cut in halves and frozen
1 egg
2 tbsp of flour
5 tbsp of bread crumbs
2 tbsp of parmesan cheese
olive oil

Directions:
Put flour into bowl. Beat the egg in a separate small bowl. Use another separate bowl to combine the bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese.
First coat frozen cheese stick in flour, then egg, and lastly the bread crumb and cheese mixture.
Once all of the 24 sticks are coated, place them on a baking sheet. Coat the top of each stick with olive oil and cover pan with aluminum foil.
Bake in the oven at 400 degrees for about 4 minutes, or until crisp.
