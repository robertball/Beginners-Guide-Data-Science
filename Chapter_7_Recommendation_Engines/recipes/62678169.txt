Title:
Gluten Free, Dairy Free, Peanut Butter Cookies, Batch #2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
~ 1 cup peanut butter
~ 1/3 cup white sugar
~ 1/3 cup brown sugar
~ 1/4 cup egg white
~ egg substitute
~ tsp extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
Combine ingredients and drop by teaspoonfuls on cookie sheet.  Bake for 8-12 minutes until starting to brown.  Let cool on the cookie sheet for at least 10-15 minutes before removing.
