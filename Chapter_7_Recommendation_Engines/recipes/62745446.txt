Title:
Super Easy Scalloped Potatos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs. Margarine
1/2 a small red onion diced.
1 clove of garlic
1 tbs flour (all purpose)
1 tbs Garlic and herb seasoning
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup Unsweetened  Almond milk
2 tbs. Sour Cream
1cup cheese
3 medium potatos peeled and sliced
a touch of key lime juice

Directions:
Boil potatos for 20 minutes.
Drain & toss with lime juice. Set aside.
You will need a corning ware (the older ones work on the stove too, otherwise start in a sauce pot) loaf pan for the following.
Set loaf pan on burner at medium heat.
Sautee onions and garlic in margarine until just transparent. Add flour and sautee till flour just turns yellow. Immedialy stir in milk and sour cream. When combined slide off burner and add 2/3 of the cheese, garlic seasoning salt and pepper. Fold in potatos. Top with remaining cheese bake for 15 minutes at 350.

Rich and creamy scalloped potato recipe with a hint of garlic. Feeds four.
