Title:
Edna's Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5918764.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
1 tablespoon freshly grated orange zest
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Beat cream cheese in a bowl until smooth and soft. Fold marshmallow creme, orange zest, and ground ginger through the cream cheese.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Gran28540
Description: 
A very nice sweet and rich treat for dipping your favorite pieces of fruit or as a fruit cup topping. If you don't want to use as a dip for fruit, a dollop on top of fresh fruit served in a glass is especially nice to serve after dinner as a refreshing dessert.
