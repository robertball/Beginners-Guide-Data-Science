Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breasts
½ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
¼ teaspoon garlic powder
1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese (Neufchatel)
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
½ cup Buffalo wing sauce (such as Frank's® Red Hot)
½ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

Directions:



Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
Prep, cook, and serve this delicious Buffalo chicken dip in 1 bowl - your slow cooker! Cook the chicken breasts, shred with a hand mixer, then add the rest of the ingredients and voila: 4 hours later, this yummy dip is ready.
