Title:
Crockpot Green Chili Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs Pork Tenderloin - cubed
2 tablespoons chili powder
2 tablespoons minced garlic
16 oz Green Chili Sauce
6 Diced Jalapenos
14 oz Chicken Broth (1 can)
2 medium onions, chopped
2 baking potatoes, cubed
Lard as desired
Butter as desired
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Put the pork cubes in a large ziplock bag and sprinkle with 1 tb chili powder.  Shake until all pieces are covered evenly. Brown the pork in lard or any kind of oil.
Place chopped onion, pork, garlic, green chili sauce, jalapenos and broth in crockpot. Sprinkle the remaining chili powder and mix all well.  Cook on low for 6 hours or high for 3 hours.
Season the potatoes with salt and pepper, brown in butter.  Add to stew and cook for an additional 2 hours on high, or 3 on low.
