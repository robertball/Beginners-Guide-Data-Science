Title:
Rich Turkey Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 to 1/3 Cup of unsifted flour
1/4 cup turkey pan drippings or butter
2 Cups water
2 tsp. chicken bouillon cubes
(Can use chicken broth instead of water and cubes)

Directions:
In a medium skillet, over medium heat, stir flour into drippings; cook and stir until dark brown.  Stir in water and bouillon; cook and stir until thickened and bouillon is dissolved.  Refrigerate leftovers.
Makes 1 1/2 Cups.
