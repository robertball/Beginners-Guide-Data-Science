Title:
RaRa's chicken and Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped,
1 large bell pepper, seeded, and chopped,
2 TBSP olive oil,
3 cans chicken broth,
16 ounces spaghetti,
1 large jar pimentos,
1/2 pound velveeta cheese,
2 tsp salt,
1 tsp black pepper,
1 pound bonesless chicken, cooked, and cubed.

Directions:
In a medium skillet, saute onion, and bell pepper in olive oil til tender.  Add chicken and broth.  Bring to a boil, and add salt and pepper.  Bring to a boil again, and add spaghetti.  Cook until spaghetti is tender; drain.  Add velveeta, an mix til cheese is melted.  Just before serving add chopped pimentos.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
A friend and me came up with this with what we had laying around the kitchen.  It has been a hit every since.
