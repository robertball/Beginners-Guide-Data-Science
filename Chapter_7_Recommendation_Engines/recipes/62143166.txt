Title:
Connecticut Beef Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef stew meat
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1/2 t garlic salt
2 T margarine or butter
1 cup water
1 onion, thinly sliced
4-5 potatoes, peeled and sliced
1 can low fat Cream of Mushroom Soup
3/4 cup half-and-half
8 oz light sour cream
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
Crushed butter crackers

Directions:
Season meat with salt, pepper, and garlic salt.
Brown meat with onions in the butter until onion is tender.
Add water, stir, heat to boiling.
Reduce heat, simmer covered for 1 hour.
Pour meat mixture and liquid into lightly greased 9x13 pan.
Arrange sliced potatoes over meat.
Mix soup, half-and-half, and sour cream, and pour evenly over meat. Sprinkle cheese evenly over the top.
Bake 1 hour, uncovered, at 350 degrees.
Sprinkle with cracker crumbs and bake additional 30 minutes.

Description:
