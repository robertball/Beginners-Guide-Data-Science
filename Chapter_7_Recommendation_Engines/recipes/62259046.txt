Title:
Cabbage Diet Soup 'Cioppino Style' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bulb fennel (or 4 stalks celery)
4 stalks celery
1 cup carrots
1/2 head cabbage
1-2 tbls. olive oil
1 leek
1 large red pepper
6 cloves garlic, crushed or minced
2 Tbls. fresh Thyme leaves, or 2 teaspoons dried
1/2 small can tomato paste
1-2 tbls. anchovy paste (optional)
1 cup red wine
1/2 8 oz. bottle clam juice
1-2 cans roma tomatoes, pureed
1/2- 1 carton vegetable broth (32oz.)
1 32 oz. carton seafood broth
1 pkg. 'Knorr' Leek Soup mix
1-2 cans crab meat
1-2 bay leaf
1 tsp. crushed red pepper
salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop all of the veggies into small, bite-sized pieces.  Add olive oil to a large pot and lightly saute fennel, celery, carrots, garlic and thyme.  Stir in tomato paste and anchovy paste, mix into veggies, over a medium heat.  Add red wine, mix well.
Now add all of the liquid ingredients, soup mix, pureed tomatoes, red pepper flakes and bay leaf. Stir togeter well.  Mix in the rest of the veggies and simmer for 25-35 minutes.  If you like a heartier and less chunky soup, use an immersion blender and blend to desired texture.  Drain crab meat and add to soup, cook for another 10 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Debi Richner
Description: 
I added some ingredients from my Cioppino to this soup for more flavor... very tasty and low calorie!
