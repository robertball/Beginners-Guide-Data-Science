Title:
Liz's Homemade Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 32oz. can diced tomatoes
2 4oz. cans of tomatoes with green chilis
1 4oz. can diced green chilis
1/4 sweet onion, chopped
salt to taste
3 tbsp. lemon juice
2 tbsp. minced garlic
1/4 C. chopped cilantro

Directions:
Mix the cans of diced tomatoes and green chilis together.
Add the chopped onions, salt, lemon juice, garlic and cilantro.
Stir until all ingredients are mixed together.
Place in the refrigerator for 1-2 hours before serving.
Serve with your favorite tortilla chips!!!

Submitter: 
Allyson
Description: 
This is a SUPER easy and great salsa recipe that was given to me from a coworker. I bring this to alot of gatherings and get great reviews from everyone. Enjoy!!!
