Title:
Beef Bourguignon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 tablespoon salt
1 3/4 teaspoons ground black pepper
1 pounds bacon rashers
7 pounds cubed stew beef
3/4 cup butter
3 onions chopped
3 1/2 cloves garlic, minced
7 cups red wine
3 bay leaves
2/3 cup chopped parsley
1 3/4 teaspoons dried thyme
2 cups coarsely chopped mushrooms
2 cups pearl onions

Directions:
Cut bacon into 1/2 inch slices and fry in a large pan. Fry until crisp. Drain and set aside, retain the fat.
In a plastic bag combine the flour, salt and ground black pepper. Coat the beef cubes with this mixture
Add the meat to the bacon fat and brown we'll  on all sides. Set browned  meat to one side.
Add butter to pan and add the onions, carrots and garlic. Saut√© for 5 to 10 minutes, or until onion is tender, add the wine, bay leaves, parsley, thyme.
Return the meat to the pan, add the crispy bacon.
Cook over a low heat for 1 hour. Add the mushrooms and pearl onions and continue to cook for a further two hours.
