Title:
Pan Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4c+2t Warm Water
1pkg Rapid Rise Yeast
1/4t Brown Sugar
1/2t Salt
1T+1/4t Olive Oil
1 3/4c Flour

Directions:
Dissolve yeast and brown sugar in water
Add salt and oil
Add flour kneading until pulls away from bowl if using a stand mixer
Let double in size punch down and repeat
(optional Butter and cornmeal) large cast iron skillet spread dough and add whatever toppings you want
Cook in 425 degree preheated oven for 15-20 minutes.
