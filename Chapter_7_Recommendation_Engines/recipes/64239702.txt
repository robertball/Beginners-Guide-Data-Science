Title:
Balsamic Brussels sprouts, feta, walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2202017.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb brussels sprouts
1 large red onion
6 garlic cloves
1/3 C balsamic vinegar
1/4 C Olive Oil
2 T maple syrup
2 oz feta
1/4 cup walnuts
1/4 cranberries

Directions:
Slice off ends of brussels sprouts. Slice in half. Chop onion. Coursely chopped garlic. Toss in olive oil, balsamic, maple syrup season with salt and pepper to taste. Roast at 450 for until sauce in thickens and  sprouts are tender. Put in bowl with remaining ingredients. Stir to combine.

Easy yummy way to enjoy brussels sprouts.
