Title:
Philly Dills Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635169.jpg
Ingredients:
Jar of Polish dills
Cream cheese, softened for spreading.
Pastromi meat (could use ham if you can't find pastromi)

Directions:
1) Wrap each pickle in paper towel over night.
2) Spread thin sliced meat with cream cheese. Wrap pick in meat (only the one layer to cover the pickle). Refrigerate.
3) Slice and serve.
