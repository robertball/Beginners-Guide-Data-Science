Title:
Mary Jane's Ratatouille Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can tomato sauce
2 cloves minced garlic
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 zucchini, trimmed and very thinly sliced
1 yellow squash, trimmed and very thinly sliced
1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves, minced  or to taste
1 teaspoon fresh oregano leaves, minced
or about 3/4 tsp of Italian Seasoning
1/4 cup american, cheddar or parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Spread 1/2 of  tomato sauce into the bottom of a baking dish.
Put half of squash/zucchini in pan
Sprinkle with fresh or dried italian herbs/seasoning.
Repeat Layer.
Sprinkle cheese on top.
Bake in the preheated oven until vegetables are roasted and tender, about 45 to 60  minutes. Let cool 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
A beautiful deconstructed ratatouille is composed of sliced colorful vegetables arranged over a garlic-infused tomato sauce and baked.Modified from: Ratatouille
