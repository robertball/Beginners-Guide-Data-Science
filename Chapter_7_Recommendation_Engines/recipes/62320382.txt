Title:
Chicken Chili Dry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500 grams Boneless Chicken
3 TBS Soy Sauce
2 TBS Vinegar
2 tsp Black Pepper
1 tsp White Pepper
1 tsp Salt
1 Egg White
1 Medium Onion chopped
4-5 Green Chilies slitted and halved
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Cut the boneless chicken into paper thin bit size strips.
Combine soy sauce, vinegar, beaten egg white, black pepper, white pepper and salt in a bowl. Add chicken to the marinate and set aside for at least 30 minutes.
Heat oil in a shallow pan and fry chicken until tender and juices run clear.
Add the onions and continue frying until onoins are caramalized and chicken is golden brown.
Add the green chilies and fry for another 1 minute.
Serve with egg fried rice.
