Title:
Sweet Hawaiian Crock Pot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1522402.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb. Chicken tenderloin chunks
1 cup pineapple juice (chunks optional)
Large pieces of Onion (optional)
Large chucks of green peppers(optional)
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup soy sauce

Directions:
Roll the chicken chunks in corn starch then brown in pan. Put in crock pot with other ingredients adding one can of pineapple chunks not just the juice, also large pieces of onion and green or red peppers.
Combine all together, cook on low in Crock-pot 6-8 hours...that's it! Done! Serve with brown rice and you have a complete, easy meal!!
