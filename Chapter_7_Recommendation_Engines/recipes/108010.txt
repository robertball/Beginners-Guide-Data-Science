Title:
Summer Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978267.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
1 teaspoon Archer Farms™ Italian seasoning
3 large Roma tomatoes, seeded and chopped
2 tablespoons refrigerated pesto sauce
1 tablespoon Market Pantry™ balsamic vinaigrette dressing
1/8 teaspoon Market Pantry salt

Directions:
Heat grill.
Sprinkle chicken with Italian seasoning.
When ready to grill, place chicken over medium heat and cover.  Grill for 6 to 8 minutes or until thoroughly cooked, turning once.
Meanwhile, In small bowl, stir together tomatoes, pesto, vinaigrette and salt. Serve tomato mixture over cooked chicken.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
Grilled chicken does not have to be ho-hum. With a little pesto, some Roma tomatoes and a smidge of balsamic dressing, simple grilled chicken becomes summer magic.
