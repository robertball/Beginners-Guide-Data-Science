Title:
KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX® Sweet Minglers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993059.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package semi-sweet chocolate morsels
1/4 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup peanuts (optional)
6 cups KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX® cereal
1 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
In large microwave safe bowl, melt chocolate at HIGH 1 minute. Stir and  heat 30 seconds longer at HIGH or until melted when stirred. Stir in peanut  butter and peanuts. Gently stir in KELLOGG'S(R) CRISPIX(R) cereal, until evenly  coated.
Place powdered sugar in 2-gallon zipper-type plastic bag. Add coated cereal to  sugar and close bag. Gently toss cereal mixture until evenly coated. Store in airtight container in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX®
Description: 
Semi-sweet chocolate, peanuts, and peanut butter mingle with KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX® cereal to create a sweet snack your family will love.
