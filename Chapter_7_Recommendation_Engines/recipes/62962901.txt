Title:
Cheesecake bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of cressent rolls
2 blocks of cream cheese
1 egg (seperated)
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup sugar (for topping)
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (for topping)

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees
in a 9 x 13 pan layer w/ 1 package of rolls
Mix: cheese, sugar, vanilla & yolk then put mixture onto of rolls.
layer 2nd package of rolls
brush top w/ egg whites then combine sugar & cinnamon and sprinkle ontop
bake for 30 minutes

Submitter: 
cookinmama
Description: 
An excellent twist on traditional cheesecake.
