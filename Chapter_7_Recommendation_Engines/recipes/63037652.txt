Title:
Mesquite Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 pound Owen's Sage pork Sausage
2 cups Stonewall Kitchen Mesquite Steak Sauce, divided
3 eggs, lightly beaten
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2-3 TBL fresh parsley, chopped fine
salt & pepper
2 cups breadcrumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl combine all ingredients, in the order listed, reserving one cup of steak sauce. Place mixture on a rimmed  baking sheet or large casserole, and form into a loaf shape. Cover with remaining mesquite sauce. Bake for 1 hr and 15 minutes, or until no longer pink and juices run clear.
