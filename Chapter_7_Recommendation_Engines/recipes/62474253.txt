Title:
Spaghetti with Sausage and Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. uncooked pasta
1 pound of mild Italian sausage, casings removed
1 medium onion, cut into strips
1 green bell pepper, cut into strips
1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
1 ye
llow bell pepper, cut into strips
2 garlic cloves minced
1 T. olive oil
1 (28 oz.) can diced tomatoes with basil, garlic and oregano
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1/2 c. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to directions
Cook sausage over medium heat until no longer pink. Drain on paper towels and set aside.
Saute onion, peppers and garlic in olive oil in same pan for 5 minutes.  Stir in tomatoes, salt and pepper.  Cook until thoroughly heated.  Stir in sausage pasta and cheese.  Transfer to a serving platter and serve immediately. Delicious and makes enough for 2 meals!
