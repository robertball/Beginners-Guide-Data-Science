Title:
Meat Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb beef tenderloin or 4 rump steaks, cut into 1-inch cubes
4 cup good veg oil
crusty bread
green salad

Directions:
1. prepare sauces and season meat
2. fill pot 1/2 full of oil, and heat until hot but not smoking.  when bread cube takes 30 sec to brown, oil is ready
3. should take 25-30 sec for rare, 30-35 sec for medium
