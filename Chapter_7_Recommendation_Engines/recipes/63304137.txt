Title:
Chicken Caruseau Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4537697.jpg
Ingredients:
2 TBSP butter or margarine
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 (14 oz) jar spaghetti sauce
2 cups celery, chopped
garlic powder
italian seasoning
6 servings rice (about 1 1/2 cups before cooked)

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet.  Cut chicken into 1 inch chunks.  Season and saute chicken until done.  Add spaghetti sauce and simmer uncovered 10 minutes.  Add celery to pan.  Cover and simmer 10 minutes more.
While sauce is simmering, cook rice according to directions.  Pour sauce over rice and serve.
