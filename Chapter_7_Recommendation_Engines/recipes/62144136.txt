Title:
Mom's Savory Soy Sauce Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack of Chicken Thighs (12 pack/8 pack)
Soy Sauce
Garlic Salt
Pepper

Directions:
pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
Pour soy sauce in the glass cassorole dish the chicken will be in (enough to coat the bottom NOT ALOT).
Place Thighs skin side up in dish, Drench soy sauce on all thighs..more or less to your liking
sprinkle garlic salt on all the chicken..again to your liking.
Cover with Foil
bake for 1 hour and 30 minutes..or 2 hours if frozen

Submitter: 
Heather
Description: 
A SUPER-SIMPLE blend of soy-sauce, garlic salt and chicken that has been a tradition for years!
