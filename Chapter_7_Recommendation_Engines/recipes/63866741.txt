Title:
Double Pumpkin Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Wet Ingredients:
1 medium egg
1 1/3 cup milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 cup pumpkin pur√©e (I made it my own by baking fresh pumpkin and scope out the flesh, keep in refrigerator and use when needed)
2 Tbsp white sugar
1 Tsp ground cinnamon
Dry Ingredients:
1/2 cup wholewheat flour
1/2 cup white flour
1 Tsp baking powder
1 Tsp salt
1/2 cup finely chop toasted pumpkin seeds

Directions:
Lowly heat a pan and toast the pumpkin seeds for 5 minutes, set a side
Mix all dry ingredients and whisk well to create air in the mixture
Mix all wet ingredients, lightly whisk then pour mixture into dry mixture
Light oil a non-stick pan on  medium heat
Scope batter and fry into mini pancake about 2 minutes per side
Serve witty maple syrup and butter (I like it plain)

Submitter: 
Michelle Foo
Description: 
I've been making pumpkin pancakes from various recipes but just feel something is missing. So I decided to add some texture to it. It turned out to be so delicious!
