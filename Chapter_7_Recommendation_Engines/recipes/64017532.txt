Title:
Cheeseburger Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked rotini
2 tsp oil
11/2 cups finely chopped onion
1 clove finely chopped garlic
1 lb lean ground beef
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
2 tbs tomato paste
28ox diced tomatoes
2 tbs Dijon mustard
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
1/4 cup chopped dill pickle

Directions:
1. Preheat oven 350 degrees. Oil 9x13 baking dish. In large pot of boiling water cook pasta according to package for al dente and drain well.
2. In large skillet, heat oil over med-low heat.  Add onions and cook until soft. Stir in garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Stir in beef and cook until browned; season with salt and pepper.  Stir in tomato paste and then tomatoes and mustard. Let mixture bubble gently until slightly thickened, about 2 min.
3. Toss meat mixture with pasta and spread into prepared dish.  Top with cheddar and bake until cheese melted, about 15 minutes.  Sprinkle chopped pickles over top and serve.
