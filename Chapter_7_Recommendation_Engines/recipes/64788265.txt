Title:
Denstaedt Meatloaf Potato soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Tablespoons butter unsalted or salted will work
1 medium yellow onion chopped (about 1.5 cup/200g)
6 large garlic cloves minced
2/3 cup all-purpose flour 42g
2 ½ lbs gold potatoes  diced into pieces no smallerer than 1”
8 cups chicken broth 945ml
1 cups milk 475ml
2- 12oz cans evaporated milk
1 ½ teaspoon* salt
1 teaspoon ground pepper
1 pound meatloaf cut into large, bite sized chunks
2 Cups mashed potatoes
8oz frozen corn
8oz frozen peas
2/3 cup sour cream 160g
Shredded cheddar cheese, chives, and additional sour cream and bacon for topping optional

Directions:
Melt butter in large pot and chopped onion, carrots and celery and cook over medium heat until onions are tender (3-5 minutes).
Add garlic and cook until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
Sprinkle the flour over the ingredients in the pot and stir until smooth (use whisk if needed).
Add diced potatoes to the pot along with chicken broth, milk, evaporated milt, salt, pepper and stir well.
Bring to a boil and cook until potatoes are tender when pierced with a fork (about 10 minutes).
Add meatloaf
Add mashed potatoes, frozen corn and peas.
Add sour cream stir well.
Allow soup to simmer for 15 minutes bfore serving.
Top with additional sour cream, bacon, cheddar cheese, or chives. Enjoy!
