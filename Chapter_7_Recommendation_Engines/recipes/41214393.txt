Title:
Lemon Cream Cheese Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 sticks unsalted butter, softened
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1 Tbl Crisco
3 cups sugar
1 1/2 Tbl vanilla
1 Tbl fresh lemon juice
Grated rind of one large lemon
6 large eggs, room temperature
3 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
For optional icing:
2 cups powdered sugar
2 Tbl lemon juice
1 tsp vanilla
3-4 Tbl milk
Stirred together to desired consistency

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Grease and flour Bundt pan
On medium speed beat butter until soft.  Add cream cheese and Crisco and mix until light and fluffy, about 5 minutes.
Add sugar, vanilla, lemon juice and rind.  Beat well. Scrape sides of bowl.
Add eggs, one at a time. Beat until fluffy again.
Add flour and salt. Mix until flour is incorporated.
Bake for 1 1/4 hour.  Cool completely before removing from pan.
When cool drizzle with lemon icing.
