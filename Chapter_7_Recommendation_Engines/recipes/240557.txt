Title:
Warm Hug Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1812498.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup prepared hot chocolate
1 fluid ounce Irish cream liqueur
1 fluid ounce creme de cacao

Directions:
Combine hot chocolate Irish cream liqueur, and creme de cacao in a coffee glass or mug.

Submitter: 
ellie
Description: 
A rich, tasty adult beverage that is as nice as a warm hug on a cold day. Whipped cream and chocolate shavings are optional but make this beverage a nice tasty after dinner drink or for anytime you feel like a warm hug.
