Title:
Columbian Salsa Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag HEB fresh Brussels Sprouts
1/2 med. Onion chopped
3-6 Tablespoon Rustico Olive Oil
1 Tbs Adams Reserve Grilled Vegetable Rub
1/2 Cup - Criolla Columbian Salsa

Directions:
Season Brussels Sprouts and onion with Gilled Vegetable Rub
Saute seasoned Brussels sprouts and onion in Olive oil 2-3 mins
Add 1/2 Cup Criolla Salsa; continue to saute 2-3 minutes longer
