Title:
Mom's Famous Party Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head of iceburg lettuce torn or cut into pieces
1 lb bacon
1 1/2 cup mayonaisse
3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 bag frozen brocolli/cauliflower mix

Directions:
Cook bacon until desired crispness then remove from pan to paper towels to drain excess grease; allow to cool then crumble into pieces.  Cook frozen  broccoli/cauliflower per package directions; drain and allow to cool.  Once bacon and veggies are cooled, layer the salad in a large bowl as follows: Torn lettuce pieces, cooked broccoli/cauliflower, mayonaisse (spread over top of veggies), grated parmesan cheese then crumbled bacon last.  Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve, but don't make more than a few hours ahead or lettuce may be soggy.  Before serving, mix all layers together.

Submitter: 
Irish916
Description: 
This is a recipe that my mom handed down to me that I'm always asked to bring to parties.  It's a mix of lettuce, veggies, bacon, mayo and parmesan cheese. It's definitely not low fat, but it sure does taste good!
