Title:
Crockpot Cocktail Weiners Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 packages of lil smokies (any variety)
1 cup ketchup
2 cups honey barbecue sauce
1/2 cup bourbon
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 tablespoon italian seasoning

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a crock pot and cook on low for 3-4 hours

Submitter: 
Staci Booth
Description: 
I don't like the cocktail wieners with jelly so I made this recipe up. Everytime I make these they get eaten quickly.
