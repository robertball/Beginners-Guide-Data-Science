Title:
Rosarita Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila
3 ounces Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix
1 ounce cranberry juice
1/2 cup crushed ice
Handful of cranberries (frozen or fresh)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in blender.  Pour entire contents into margarita glass.

Submitter: 
Cuervo®
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with the gold standard in tequila, Jose Cuervo Especial® tequila.
