Title:
Best Ever Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
1 tsp. salt
2/3 c. margarine
cold water

Directions:
Sift flour and salt into medium bowl.
Cut in shortening with fork or pastry cutter until crumbly.
Sprinkle mix with ice water and mix lightly with fork until mixture holds together.
Form dough into 2 balls.
Roll each out onto a floured surface.
Fill with desired toppings or bake alone.
To bake shell, bake 5 - 10 min at 350.
