Title:
pepper steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds sirloin steak, 1/2 inch thick
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 medium onions, chopped (about 1cup)
1 cup beef broth
3 tbsp soy sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
2 green peppers, cut into 1 inch pieces
2 tbsp. cornstarch
1/4 cup cold water
2 tomatoes, peeled and cut into eighths
3 - 4 cups hot cooked rice

Directions:
Trim fat and bone from meat; cut meat into 4 - 6 serving pieces.
Grease large skillet lightly with fat from meat.
Push meat to one section.
Add onion; cook and stir until tender.  Stir in broth, soy sauce and garlic.  Cover;
Simmer 10 min or until meat is tender.
Add green peppers.  cover; simmer 5 minutes
Blend  cornstarch and water; stir gradually into meat mixture.  Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils.  Boil and stir 1 minute.
Add tomatoes heat through.
Serve over rice.
