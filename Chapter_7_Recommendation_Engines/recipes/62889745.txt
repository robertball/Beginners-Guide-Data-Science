Title:
Cabernet Wine Jelly with Rosemary, Garlic and Pepper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Cabernet Sauvignon
3 1/2 cups sugar
3 cloves garlic, peeled and slightly crushed
1 Tbsp peppercorns
3 jalapenos, halved and seeded
1 sprig rosemary (about 1 Tbsp leaves)
1 2-oz. pouch liquid pectin

Directions:
Combine wine and sugar in saucepan and bring to a boil.
Add garlic, peppercorns, jalapenos and rosemary; boil about 2 minutes.
Remove from heat and strain.  Return liquid to saucepan and bring to rolling boil.
Add pouch of pectin, all at once, and boil for exactly 1 minute, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat.  Skim foam, if necessary, and ladle into hot sterilized jars leaving 1/4" headspace
Cover with two piece lids.  Process in hot water bath for 10 minutes.  Let stand 24 hours to set.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Wine jelly infused with rosemary, garlic and peppercorns.  I added jalapenos for a bit of a kick, but did not taste them in the end product at all.
