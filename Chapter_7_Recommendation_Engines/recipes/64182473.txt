Title:
Spicy Fall Pumpkin and Black Bean Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1505567.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
1 pound lean ground turkey
1 small tin diced tomatoes
1 tin of Ro Tel
1 tin of black beans (rinsed)
1/2 tsp rubbed cumin seeds
2 tbsp chili powder
1/2 cup pure pumpkin puree (not pie filling)
2 tbsp fresh chopped cilantro (optional)

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large pot. Add onions and stir until soft. Add rubbed cumin seeds and chili powder and stir. Add ground turkey. When ground turkey is cooked, add tomatoes, Ro Tel, black beans and pumpkin. If it's too thick at this point, i add a little water to one or all of the tins, swish around to get any leftovers and pour into pot. Simmer with the lid on for up to an hour. Stir in chopped cilantro just before serving.
