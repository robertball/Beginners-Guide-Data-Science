Title:
Spicy Shrimp Creole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp Butter or Margarine
1 med Onion (chopped)
1 can Stewed Tomatoes
1 can Diced Tomatoes
1 can Cream of Celery Soup
2 cups Chicken Broth
2 tsp Worchester Sauce
1 tbsp Crushed Red Peppers
1 tbsp Garlic (crushed)
2 tbsp Cajun Seasoning
1 lb Shrimp (peeled)

Directions:
1. Saute onions in butter until onions are translucent.
2. Mix celery soup and broth until soup isn't lumpy then add to cooked onions.
3. Add tomatoes and remaining seasonings.
4. Bring to a boil and then simmer for about 10 minutes.
5. Add shrimp and cook for additional 10 minutes.

Description: 
Super simple and fast spicy shrimp dish.
We love it served over rice.
