Title:
Parmesan Spinach Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed, blotted
1 1/2 cups Italian-style seasoned bread crumbs
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 small yellow onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 lb. bacon, cooled and crumbled
4 eggs, lightly beaten
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350&#0176;
In a small skillet, melt the butter and saute the onion and garlic.
In a medium bowl, mix the frozen spinach, Italian-style seasoned bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, eggs, bacon, salt and pepper.
Add the sauted vegetables.
Shape the mixture into 1 inch balls.
Arrange the balls in a single layer on a large baking sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes, until lightly browned.
Can be made ahead of time, frozen, thawed and baked right refore guests arrive.
