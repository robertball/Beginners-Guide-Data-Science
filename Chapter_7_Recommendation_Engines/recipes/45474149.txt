Title:
Chocolate Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (19.8 ounce) package brownie mix
1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
1/2 cup water
1/2 pint heavy cream
3 T sugar
1 (12 ounce) container frozen vanilla whipped topping, thawed
1 jar caramel sauce
1 pkg heath toffee bits
1 (1.5 ounce) bar chocolate candy

Directions:
Prepare brownie mix according to package directions and cool completely. Cut into 1 inch squares.
In a large bowl, combine pudding mix and water. Mix until smooth, then beat to stiff peaks cream and sugar. Fold in cream to puding until no streaks remain.
In a trifle bowl or glass serving dish, place half of the brownies, caramel, heath toffee bits, chocolate whipped cream, and french vanilla cool whip. Repeat layers.  Shave chocolate onto top layer for garnish. Refrigerate 8 hours before serving.
