Title:
PEACHES AND CREAM COOLER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C. APRICOT NECTAR, CHILLED
1 1/2 C. PINEAPPLE JUICE, CHILLED
1 1/2 C. HALF AND HALF CREAM, CHILLED
3 T. FRESH SQUEEZED LEMON JUICE
3 T. HONEY
3 TSP. VANILLA
6 C. FROZEN SLICED PEACHES
12 ICE CUBS
2 1/4 C. SPARKLING WATER, CHILLED

Directions:
IN A BLENDER, COMBINE THE FIRST 9 INGREDIENTS, COVER AND PROCESS UNTIL SMOOTH. YOU MAY HAVE TO DO THIS IN 3 BATCHES. ADD SPARKLING WATER, COVER AND PROCESS UNTIL BLENDED. POUR INTO PUNCH BOWL THAT SITS ON A BIGGER PUNCH BOWL FILLED WITH ICE, OR POUR INTO INDIVIDUAL GLASSES. SERVE IMMEDIATELY.
