Title:
Thyme for dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp dried thyme
2 Tbls. butter (cold)
1/2 cup milk or buttermilk

Directions:
In medium mixing bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, salt and thyme.
Cut in butter with pastry blender until mixture resembles course crumbs.  Stir in milk just until blended.  (It should be lumpy)
Drop by 6-8 spoonfuls onto bubbling beef or chicken stew.  Cover and cook for 10 minutes. (Don't peek!)  Check for doneness by inserting toothpick into dumpling.  If toothpick isn't clean, re-cover and cook additional 2-3 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Cook these with your favorite beef or chicken stew.
