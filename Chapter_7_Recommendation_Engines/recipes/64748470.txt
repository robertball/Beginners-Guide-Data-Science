Title:
Meatballs with Onions & Brown Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6678591.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 lb. hamburger meat
½ teaspoon garlic salt
½ teaspoon pepper
1 cup diced onion
1 cup thinly sliced onion
2 teaspoons minced garlic
2 Tablespoons Flour
1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
2.5 cups Beef broth

Directions:
1.	In a bowl combine ground beef, diced onion, garlic salt and pepper
2.	Roll with palms, and roll into 1 inch sized meatballs
3.	In large skillet, cook the meatballs over medium-low heat until no longer pink in the middle
4.	Remove Meatballs and add thinly sliced onions, cook until transparent.
5.	Add Garlic and cook 1 minute
6.	Stir in the Flour and cook until browned
7.	 While Stirring, slowly add the Worcestershire sauce and  2.5 cups Beef Broth
8.	Reduce liquids until thicken
9.	Add meatballs back into pan with gravy coat with gravy. And sever over mashed potatoes
