Title:
Southern garlic mash potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 med. potatoes
1 pkg. cream cheese
1 carton sour cream
4 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup chopped chives
salt and pepper to taste
paprika

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 350
2) Boil potatoes
3) Beat sour cream and cream cheese together
4) Add hot potatoes and beat until smooth
5) Add butter, chives and salt and pepper
6) Pour into well greased casserole dish
7) Dot with butter and sprinkle with paprika
Cook for 25 minutes

Submitter: 
mollymoo
Description: 
My mom made this for a Thanksgiving dinner and I think it got more attention than the turkey!
