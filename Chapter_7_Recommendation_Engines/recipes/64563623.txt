Title:
Easy Turkey-Tortilla Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBL cream cheese spread
4 slices turkey
1/4 cup shredded lettuce

Directions:
Spread tortilla with 1 TBL cream cheese spread; cover with 4 slices turkey. Place 1/4 cup shredded lettuce on bottom half of tortilla; roll up, cut into 4 slices.
