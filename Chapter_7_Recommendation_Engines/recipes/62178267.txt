Title:
Vegetable pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3C cooked rice
1 can each of cream of mushroom and celery soup
1C shredded cheddar cheese
1 jar (2oz) diced pimentos; drained
1pkg (10oz each) frozen chopped
spinich, broccoli, thawed and drained
1 1/2C french fried onions

Directions:
Combine rice,soups, cheese and pimentos; stir well and set aside. In medium bowl combine the vegetables and fried onions. Spread half of the rice mixture in a 8x12in greased baking dish. Top with vegetables mixture then with remaing rice mixture. Bake uncoverd at 350 for 40min. Top with remaining onions, bake uncovered 5 minutes or until golden brown.
