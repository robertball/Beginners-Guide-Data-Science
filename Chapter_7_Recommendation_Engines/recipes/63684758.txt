Title:
Garlic Feta Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1024838.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large Yukon Gold Potatoes, peeled and cut into large cubes
1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons butter
6 oz feta cheese, crumbled
2 garlic cloves, minced
Salt
Parsley (optional)

Directions:
Place potatoes in a saucepan, and cover with water.  Bring to a boil and cook until tender (about 20 minutes).
Remove from heat, and drain well.
Mash with potato masher until chunks are gone.
Add milk, butter, feta cheese, minced garlic, and salt (to taste).
Mix well.
Garnish with parsley or additional crumbled feta.

Submitter: 
Cookie734
Description: 
The ultimate in comfort food, with a tangy twist.  The feta and garlic give these creamy mashed potatoes a uniquely delicious flavor.
