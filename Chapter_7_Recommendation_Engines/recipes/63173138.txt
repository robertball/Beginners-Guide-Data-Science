Title:
Italian Bakery Style Sponge CAke Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cake flour
1 cup sugar, divided
3 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cream of tartar
Juice of one small orange
1/2 cup canola oil
8 egg whites
8 egg yolks

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 325F
In large bowl mix egg whites with 1/2 cup sugar and cream of tartar ,
In separate bowl, mix egg yolks and other 1/2 cup of sugar and orange juice
In another  bowl mix flour and baking powder on low speed.
Add egg yolk mixture to egg white mixture .
On low speed , add flour mixture and beat until throughly blended.
Pour into greased  8 inch round cake pans.
Bake 1 hour or until toothpick inserted in center of cake comes out clean.
