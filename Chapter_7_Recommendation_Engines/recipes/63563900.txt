Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds chuck roast
salt, pepper, garlic powder - to taste
1 packet dry onion soup mix
1 can beef broth
1 lb. baby carrots
1 white onion, chopped
1.5 lbs (approx) small red potatoes halved

Directions:
Season the roast with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Brown on all sides in a large skillet over high heat, about 4 minutes per side.
Place the roast in the slow cooker and add the soup mix and broth. Cover and cook on Low setting for 8 to 10 hours.(Add vegetables to crock pot after cooking meat 6 hours so they will not be too soft.)
