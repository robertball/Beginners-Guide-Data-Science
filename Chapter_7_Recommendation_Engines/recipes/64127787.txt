Title:
Chicken Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads of heirloom lettuce
1 pound of ground chicken
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 small white onion, chopped
1 tbsp of ground ginger
2 tbsp coconut oil
4 cloves of garlic, chopped
1/4 cup chopped almonds
1 tbsp white wine vinegar
1 tsp mustard
1 tbsp Sriracha
1 tbsp raw honey
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Prep lettuce as wraps and set aside.
Heat a large skillet over medium heat with sesame oil.
Cook chicken until almost done.
Add onions, ginger, and coconut oil and cook until onions are soft.
Add remaining ingredients and cook an additional 3-4 minutes.
Serve in lettuce wraps.
