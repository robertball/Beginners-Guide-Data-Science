Title:
Coney Dog Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
2-3 tbsp garlic king
half a sweet onion
1 tbsp  celery seed
1 tbsp bone dust
1 half jar of chili or salsa
2 tbsp mustard
2 tbsp ketchup
1\4 cup sugar
1/4 cup white vinegar
salt and pepper to taste
2 cups of hot spicy clamato

Directions:
heat a large fry pan, melt garlic king, and fry ground beef and pour off fat
add onions, caramelize
add balance of items, simmer for 45 minutes
cool, refrigerate, reheat and serve the next day
heat the dogs any which way
assemble in bun, with diced onion and mustard
apply generous Coney Sauce

Submitter: 
George. P. Cooper
Description: 
good tailgate food for beating the Lions. 
Make the day before and reheat when q'ing the dogs
