Title:
Rhubarb Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c Flour
1/2c Brown sugar
1c butter (softened)
5c Rhubarb (chopped)
6 Egg Yolks
1c Whipping Cream
2c Sugar
6 Tbs Flour
1 tsp Vanilla
6 Egg Whites
Pinch of Salt
12 Tbs Sugar
Dash of Cream of Tartar
Shredded Coconut (if desired)

Directions:
Take first (3) ingredients (Flour, Brown Sugar, and Butter) and mix together. Put in greased 9x13 pan. Press flat on the bottom and up sides to about 1". Bake at 325F for 10 minutes.
Meanwhile mix the egg yolks, cream, sugar, flour, and vanilla in medium bowl.
Put Rhubarb over baked crust and pour egg mixture over the rhubarb. Bake at 325F for 45 minutes.
Meanwhile beat the Egg Whites, Salt, Sugar, and Cream of Tartar) until it standsin peaks. Spread on top of filling. Sprinkle coconut over meringue, if desired. Bake at 325F for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
David S. Novak
Description: 
Along with the Sour Cream Rhubarb Pie my mother made, this was another recipe that the family would beg her to make. I submit this recipe to the memory of my mother Grace Mary Novak.
