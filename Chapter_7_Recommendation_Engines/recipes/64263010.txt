Title:
Lime Cottage Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 oz pkg lime jello
2/3 cup hot water
2 Tbsp sugar (optional)
1 cup cottage cheese
1 small can crushed pineapple, drained
1 cup walnuts, chopped
1 cup whipped topping

Directions:
In a medium bowl, dissolve jello and sugar in hot water.  Refrigerate until jello begins to thicken.
Add cottage cheese, pineapple, nuts and whipped cream and mix well.  Pour into mold or bowl and refrigerate until set.

Barbara Bartz
