Title:
Sweet Potato Streusel Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pie crust
Topping
3/4 cup flour
1/2 cup favorate nut chopped
1/2 cup brown sugar
6 tablespoons butter melted
1/4 teaspoon salt
For the Filling
2 cups sweet potato puree
1 cup evaporated milk
3/4 cup sugar
2 large eggs lightly beaten
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ginger
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350. Bake the pie.
2. Make the topping. Combine everything. Freeze until hard. about 30 minutes.
3. Make the filling. Whisk the sweet potato, milk, sugar, eggs, spices. Pour in crust. Bake 30 minutes. Scatter toppings on pie and continue baking until the center is almost set but jiggles slightly. About 30-45 more minutes. Let cool compleatly.
