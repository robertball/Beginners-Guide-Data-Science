Title:
Grilled Beef Tenderloin with Fresh Tomato-Pesto Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992609.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons refrigerated pesto
1 small tomato, chopped
2 Sutton and Dodge™ beef tenderloin steaks
1 tablespoon Archer Farms™ pure or extra virgin olive oil
1 teaspoon Archer Farms™ steak seasoning
1/4 cup Market Pantry™ grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
Preheat grill. In a small bowl, combine pesto and tomato.
Rub steaks with olive oil. Sprinkle with steak seasoning. Grill 6 to 8 minutes over high heat for medium rare or to desired  degree of doneness. Remove to serving plate and cover loosely with foil. Let rest 5 minutes.
Top steaks with tomato-pesto relish. Garnish with Parmesan cheese, optional.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
The fresh tomato-pesto relish gives this everyday meal a gourmet feel. Pesto adds a bold, satisfying flavor to beef. So simple - just mix it up, grill it up and you're done.
