Title:
Mexican Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cooked, boneless chicken breast halves, shredded
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans
1 cup whole kernel corn
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers
2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
1 tablespoon ground cumin

Directions:
Place cooked chicken, kidney beans, corn, tomatoes, onion, red and green bell peppers, chiles, broth and cumin in a large pot over medium heat.  Simmer 45 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Shredded chicken in a cumin-spiced soup with kidney beans, corn, tomatoes, red and green bell pepper, and green chiles.Modified from: Mexican Soup
