Title:
Crab Salad with Champagne Mango Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad Dressing:
1/4 C. olive oil or vegetable oil
1/3 C. champagne vinegar
1/3 C. champagne /sparkling white wine
2 Tbsp. honey
1 Tsp. chopped chives
1 Tsp. mint, finely choped
1/2 C. Mango puree
Juice of 1/2 lime.
For the Salad:
1/2 lb. mixed baby greens
1/2 lb. cooked crab or lobster,
1/2 lb. cooked shrimp, cooled & drained
1 can Mandarin oranges, drained
1 C. bean sprouts
1 Tbsp. sesame seeds
1/4 C. slivered almonds, toasted
2 plum tomatoes, sliced
1 avocado, peeled, cut strips

Directions:
Combine all the dressing ingredients in a bottle, shake well or mix in small bowl with whisk.
Mix seafood, oranges, bean sprouts, and sesame seeds in a bowl.
Arrange the greens on individual plates or place in a salad bowl. Place a dollop of seafood mixture and top with toasted almonds. Or put all seafood mixture on top of the greens and top with tomatoes and avocado slices.  Drizzle each plate with dressing or drizzle over salad in bowl. Option: Serve with dressing on the side

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Elegant dish to serve for ladies lunch or brunch.
Serve with a nice champagne,  prosecco or white wine.
Crosants or cloverleaf rolls.
