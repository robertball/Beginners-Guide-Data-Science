Title:
Olive Garden Salad Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag American Blend Dole Salad
4-5 slices red onions
4-6 black olives
2-4 banana pepper
1/2 cup croutons
1 small tomato, quartered
Freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Chill one salad bowl in the freezer for at least 30 minutes.  Place bag of salad in bowl.  Add the rest of the ingredients and toss.
