Title:
Shrimp and Pineapple Burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package (saffron)yellow rice mix
1 1/4 cups water
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1/2 a lime
1-1 1/2 lbs pre cooked, deveined shrimp.
1/2 of a pineapple, cubed
1 jalapeno, minced
1 clove garlic, minced
1/8 cup of red onion
1 teaspoon ground cumin
Plain 0% Greek Yogurt
Burrito sized Tortilla shells

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions. To a saute pan add: Olive oil, onion, and jalapeno..  Saute until tender, 2-3 mins.
Add the shrimp, pineapple, beans, corn, lime juice, and cumin to pan.  Stir in the cooked rice.
Serve folded in burritto shells, using greek yogurt as a substitue for sour cream.
