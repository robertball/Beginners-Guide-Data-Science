Title:
Personal Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Breakfast Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup quick oats
2 T whole wheat pastry flour(other flours would probably be fine too)
1/4 tsp baking powder
1 T Natural Peanut butter
1/4 t. vanilla extract
1 T water
1 t. sugar
3 T greek yogurt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in microwave safe bowl to form thick sticky ball.
Flatten slighly.
Microwave for 70 seconds.
Let cool briefly and enjoy while warm.

A indulgent, yet healthy, quick breakfast
