Title:
Quick n' Easy Chicken Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/668347.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breats
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup milk
1 package of Birdseye steamfresh mixed vegetables
1 box Pillsbury refrigerated pie crust
2 Tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces, and put them in a large fry-pan with the olive oil, cook on med/high and stir every few minute until golden.
Place Steamfresh mixed veggies in microwave, and follow directions on package.
In a med. bowl mix the soup, and the milk until well mixed, and add the veggies.
In a high round baking dish, unroll, and place 1 of the 2 pie crusts, making sure the bottom, and side of the baking dish is covered.
Add the soup, milk, veggie mixture, then add the chicken.
Cover the top with the second pie crust, and pinch all around the sides, make a few slits in the top for steam to escape.
Place in 375 degrees F preheated oven on middle rack, and bake for 30 to 40 minutes or until top is golden brown.
