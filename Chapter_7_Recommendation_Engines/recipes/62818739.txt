Title:
Homemade Spaghetti and meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
2 pounds extra lean ground beef
1/4 c. diced bell pepper
1/4 c. Diced yellow onion
1 tbsp garlic powder
2 eggs
1 c. bread crumbs or oats
Sauce:
1/4 c. butter or margarine
1/2 c. garlic powder
1/4 c. Italian seasoning
1/4 c. oregano
1/4 c. basil
1/2 bell pepper diced
1/2 yellow onion diced
1/2 c. jarred minced garlic
1 can mushrooms
1 can tomato juice
2 packages powdered spaghetti sauce mix
1/4 c. sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees:
in a bowl mix all ingredients for meatballs together. roll mixture into balls slightly smaller than golf balls. Place balls on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes
Sauce:
While meatballs are cooking: In dutch oven melt butter
Add peppers, onions, minced garlic, garlic powder, oregano, Italian seasoning, and basil. Saute on medium heat until onions are clear and peppers are softened, not mushy.
Add Tomato juice, spaghetti sauce mix, and sugar.
Simmer on medium heat until the meatballs are done.
Pull meatballs out of oven, and spoon onto a paper towel covered plate. let soak for 1 minute.
Add meatballs to sauce and simmer 5 minutes longer.
Serve over cooked spaghetti
