Title:
Classic Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 medium potatoes cooked and diced
1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
2 TAB Cider Vinegar
2 TAB Sugar
1 TAB Yellow Mustard
1 TSP Salt
1 TSP Garlic Powder
1/2 TSP Pepper
2 Celery ribs, sliced
1 cup onion, minced
5 hard-boiled eggs chopped

Directions:
Boil peeled potatoes in salted water until done.  Cool to room temperature.
Place diced potatoes in a large bowl.
Mix mayonnaise, cider vinegar, sugar, mustard, salt, garlic powder, and pepper in another bowl.
Add to potatoes
Add Celery & onion and mix well
Stir in Eggs
Sprinkle a little paprika on top
