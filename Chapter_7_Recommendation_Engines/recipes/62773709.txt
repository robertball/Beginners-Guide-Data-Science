Title:
Italian Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb of spicy italian sausage (if you cannot get spicy italian sausage, use regular italian sausage and add a 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes)
3/4 lb ground chuck
1/2 cup fresh bread crumbs
2 cloves garlic, peeled and grated
2/3 cup grated parmeasan, romano or asiago cheese
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon black ground pepper
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 pinch dried basil
1 egg
3 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
1 cup of your favorite marinara sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In glass bowl, combine all ingredients except the marinara.
Shape in to a loaf and place in a lightly greased loaf pan.
Pour the marina over the loaf (enough to coat).
Place in oven and bake for 45-55 minutes.

Submitter: 
Liz and Sylvan
Description: 
The key to this moist, delicious meatloaf is the addition of 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise.

If you can only get italian sausage in links, just remove from the casing.
