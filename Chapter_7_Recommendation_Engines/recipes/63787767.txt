Title:
delicious slow cooker beef burgundy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cubed beef- sirloin or stew beef
1 large can cream of mushroom soup
1 pkg fresh mushrooms
1/2 cup beef broth
1/2 cup red wine
1 pkg egg noodles

Directions:
mix soup,mushrooms,broth and wine in the crockpot- add beef and combine all.  cook on low for 6 hrs.  use additional beef broth to get desired consistency if needed.
pour beef mixture over noodles and serve!!   Very simple preparation for a delicious dinner!

Submitter: 
Ohio
Description: 
My family asks for this recipe every week!  It is so simple to put together in the morning.  The beef is so tender it will melt in your mouth!  Serve over noodles with a salad and crusty bread
