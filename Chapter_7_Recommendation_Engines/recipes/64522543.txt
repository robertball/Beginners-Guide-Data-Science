Title:
Tuscan Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1 jar (6.5 oz) Matiz Catalan ALL I Oli
1/2 c kalamata olives, chopped
14 oz can artichoke hearts, drain and chopped
1/2 c sundried tomatoes, julienne cut
1/2 c fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
Combine all ingredients together in a large bowl using a wooden spoon or spatula or fork.
Refrigerate  mixture at least 30 minutes.  (may be refrigerated overnight.
Serve chilled with your favorite crudites or crackers.
