Title:
Cheesy Apple Bacon Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbs butter
2 medium granny smith apples peeled and coarsely chopped (about 3 cups)
3 tbs packed brown sugar
4 cups cubed firm bread (7 slices)
1 pound bacon-crisply cooked and chopped
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese (8oz)
2 and 1/2 cups milk
1 teaspoon ground mustard
2 tsp worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
5 eggs

Directions:
Grease 2 quart casserole dish.  Melt butter in skillet over med heat.  Cook apples in butter 2-3 min stirring occasionally until tender-crisp.  Stir in brown sugar.  Reduce heat to low and cook 5-6 min stirring occasionally until apples are tender.
Layer half of each of the bread, bacon, apples and cheese in casserole. Repeat 2nd layer with other half.
Mix remaining ingredients and pour over top of casserole.  Cover tightly and refrigerate at least 2 hours.
Heat oven to 350.  Bake uncovered 40-45 minutes until knife inserted in center comes out clean.  Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
