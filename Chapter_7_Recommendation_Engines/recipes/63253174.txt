Title:
Strawberry Lemon Mojitos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 lemon wedges
8 oz gold or aged rum
2 oz simple syrup or agave nectar
3 oz fresh lemon juice
ice cubes, plus crushed ice
24 mint leaves, spigs for garnish (optional)
4 strawberries, plus two halved strawberries, for garnish

Directions:
In a cocktail shaker, muddle the lemon wedges with the whole strawberries and mint leaves.  Add ice & rum, lemon juice & syrup, shake well.  Strain into crushed ice filled glasses.  Garnish with berry halves and mint sprigs.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
A truly yummy, cold, fruity, minty and potent potable from Food & Wine magazine.  The color of this drink is so pretty.
