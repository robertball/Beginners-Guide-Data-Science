Title:
Never Better Brocolli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bunch Brocolli
1/4 cup butter
2 Onions chopped
2 cups chopped celery
1 clove garlic chopped
1/2 cup flour
4 cups milk
4 cups chicken broth
1/4 tsp Thyme
1/2 tsp Marjoram
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Trim brocolli and cut into 1/2 inch pieces.
Steam brocolli in salted water until tender.
In a large pot, melt butter.
Saute onions, celery, & garlic in the melted butter until light brown.
Gradually stir the flour into the onion mixture.
Add milk, broth, and herbs.
Stir over low head until the soup comes to a boil and thickens.
Add brocolli, salt, and pepper.
If soup is not thick enough, it can be thickened by adding cornstarch.

Submitter: 
Janet P.
Description: 
A very special recipe made by my Aunt. We serve this every year at Christmas Eve (and of course, throughout the year as well).
