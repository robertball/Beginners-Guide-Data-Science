Title:
Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Beef, browned and drained
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can whole kernel corn, drained
1 can green beans, drained
2 C. mashed potatoes
2 C. cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400°.
Combine beef, soup, corn, and beans in a medium bowl
Pour mixture into 9x13 glass dish
Cover with mashed potatoes and sprinkle with cheese.
Bake 15-20 min. at 400°.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Not very authentic (I use beef rather than lamb) but still tasty.
