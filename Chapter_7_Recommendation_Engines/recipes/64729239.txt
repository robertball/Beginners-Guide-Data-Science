Title:
Simple air fryer Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 pounds Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
2 tsps olive oil
1 tsp powdered or granulated garlic
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Preheat the air fryer to 390 degrees for 15 minutes.
Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
Add the mixture  to the basket and spread evenly.
Halfway through the cycle shake the basket.

Submitter: 
FrackFamily5 CA—>CT
Description: 
No bells and whistles with this one just delicious sprouts, crispy brown edges and soft insides.   I’ve got a 10 quart fryer so if the sprouts look crowded in your basket,  make in two batches. Just place in a 170 degree oven to keep warm.
