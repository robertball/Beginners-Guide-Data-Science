Title:
Avocado Ham Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987818.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package HORMEL速 NATURAL CHOICE速 Deli Ham
1/4 cup sour cream
1 small loaf French bread, sliced in half horizontally
6 slices Pepper Jack cheese, diagonally sliced in half
1 avocado, peeled, pitted and thinly sliced
Alfalfa sprouts, for garnish, if desired

Directions:
Spread cut sides of French bread with sour cream.  Top with ham and cheese. Place sandwich under broiler.  Toast until golden brown.  Top with sliced avocado and sprouts, if desired.  Cut each sandwich in half and serve open-faced.

Crisp French bread loaded with ham, cheese, and avocado is lightly toasted for a quick, fantastic open-faced sandwich.
