Title:
Dream Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F830590.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
3  bananas
2 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crusts
1 (21 ounce) can apple pie filling

Directions:
Slice bananas, and arrange on the bottom of each pie crust.
Mix confectioners' sugar, cream cheese, and nondairy whipped topping with an electric mixer for about 2 minutes.  Divide mixture into pie shells.  Top with fresh fruit or pie filling of you choice.
Chill in freezer for 2 hours, or leave in refrigerator over night.  Serve!!!

Submitter: 
Dee Dee Perez
Description: 
The best no-bake pie ever, and its easy too!!  You can also use flavored cream cheese!  Great for holidays!! Any pie filling can be used in this recipe.
