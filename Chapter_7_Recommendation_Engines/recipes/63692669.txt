Title:
Chicken-n-Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen boneless skinless chicken
1/2 C. Powder chicken broth base (by soups)
3 Tbsp. Minced garlic (jars can be found by fresh produce)
1 bag dried Amish noodles or frozen egg noodles
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Place whole bag of chicken in a Large stock pot.  Cover chicken with water and add chicken broth base and garlic.  Bring water to boil then turn down to simmer.  Put lid on stock pot and let simmer until chicken is tender enough to be pulled apart with fork (about 1 1/2 hrs....maybe more).
Shred chicken apart in the pot with fork or tongs to bite size pieces.
Add noodles and bring back to full boil, turn down heat and simmer until noodles are done to desired consistency (about 20 min).
Salt and pepper to taste
