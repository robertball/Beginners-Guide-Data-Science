Title:
Pureed Carrot & Ginger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons canola oil
1/2 medium onion, chopped (if tolerated)
3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh ginger root
3 cups carrots, chopped
1 medium potato, peeled and chopped
8 cups vegetable or chicken stock
salt, to taste
dash of nutmeg

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large pot and add the onion and ginger, sauteing until the onion is translucent.
Add carrots, potato and vegetable stock. Bring to a boil.
Cover, reduce heat, and simmer until vegetables are very tender, about 35-45 minutes.
Puree in batches in a blender or food processor or remove from heat and puree in the pot using an immersion blender.
Add salt and nutmeg. Serve alone or over white rice.
