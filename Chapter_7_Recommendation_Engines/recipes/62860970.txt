Title:
Cheesey Potatos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/685092.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs shredded hashbrowns
1 1/2 stick butter
8oz sour cream
1 can cream of celery
2 cups shredded cheddar
un-frosted corn flakes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Grease 9x13 pan. Melt 1 stick of butter in a pot. Add sour cream, cream of celery, salt, pepper, and cheese. Melt.
Place potatos in the pan. Add sour cream mix and distribute. Cover with corn flakes. Melt 1/2 stick of butter and drizzle over the corn flakes.
Cover pan with foil, bake for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
