Title:
Veggie Sauce a la Sharon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
Lots of finely minced or pressed garlic
Coarse salt to taste
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Brocolli flowerettes
Coliflower flowerettes
Zuchinni halves
Green onions
Bell peppers

Directions:
Puree 1st four ingredients in a food processor and refrigerate for 4 hours prior to use.
Remove from refer and bath veggie pieces with Veggie Sauce.
Place veggie pieces on a baking sheet and roast in 400* oven until done.
Serve hot or at room temperature.
For a special treat, spoon a small amount of pesto over roasted veggies prior to serving.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Use to marinate veggie pieces prior to bbq'ing or oven roasting.
