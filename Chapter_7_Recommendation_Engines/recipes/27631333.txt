Title:
Frozen Chocolate Bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe but firm bananas
6 oz dark chocolate, chopped or semisweet chocolate chips
2 TBS. veggie oil
1/2 cup granola, chopped pecans and walnuts or sprinkles

Directions:
Line a baking sheet with nonstick foil or parchment paper.
Cut the bananas in half and insert a popcicle stick into each half, as shown. Place them on the baking sheet and freeze for 15 minutes.
Meanwhile, melt the chocolate with oil in a Pyrex measuring cup in the microwave (check it every 30 seconds) or in a half-full pan of simmering water (about 2 minutes). Stir until smooth.
Roll each banana in the chocolate, then quickly sprinkle with your topping.
Freeze until the chocolate sets (30 minutes). Serve or freeze in an airtight container for a week.
