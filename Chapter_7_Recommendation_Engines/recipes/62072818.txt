Title:
Drop Doughnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Bluberry muffin mix
1/2 cup all purpose flower
3/4 cup milk
1 egg
Oil for shallow frying
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamin

Directions:
1. put 1 inch of oil in deep fryer, set fryer to 365 degrees
2. Wash bluberries and set aside to drain.
3. Combine dry muffin mix and flower.
4. Add milk and egg.
5. Stir only until all indgredients are     moistened
6. Fold in blueberries
7. Drop by tablespoon into fryer(not all batte at once!!  5 or 6 at a time)
8. Fry until brown
9. Set out to dry
10. roll in sugar and cinnamin(or put sugar and cinnamin in a bag, and shake)
11. Eat and Enjoy!!!!
