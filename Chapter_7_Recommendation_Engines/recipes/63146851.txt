Title:
Crisp Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/912280.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
2 1/2 cup diced cooked roast beef
2 1/2 cup diced cooked potatoes
1 medium onion, diced
1 tablespoon minced parsley
1/2 cup gravy
1 cup milk
1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a heavy skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. Cook onion.  In a bowl, combine the remaining ingredients; add to the skillet. Cover; cook until crisp on the bottom. Turn and brown other side.

Submitter: 
Kristin
Description: 
This is a family favorite that provides a quick and nourishing meal. It is also a good way to use leftover beef roast.Modified from: Crisp Hash for Two
