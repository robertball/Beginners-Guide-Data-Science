Title:
24 Hour Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of romaine
8 strips of fried side bacon
1 1/4 cup cauliflower pieces
1 red onion
2 tbs of white sugar
1/2 cup of parmesan cheese
1 cup of Miracle Whip or salad dressing

Directions:
This is a layered salad so use a large salad bowl and layer the items as follows:
1. Clean the romaine leaves and break in pieces
2. Slice the fried bacon in 1 inch pieces and spread
3. Break the cauliflower in small pieces and spread
4. Slice the onion and cut in halves for large half rings and spread
5. Sprinkle sugar evenly
6. Sprinkle parmesan cheese evenly
7. Spread salad dressing over to cover
8. Cover with wrap and refrigerate
When the salad is required, thoroughly mix and serve.

Submitter: 
George. P. Cooper
Description: 
This is my sister-in-law's recipe for when you need a salad for an occasion and will be too busy, so you make it up the day before. But it is also called 2-4 hour salad in the family.
