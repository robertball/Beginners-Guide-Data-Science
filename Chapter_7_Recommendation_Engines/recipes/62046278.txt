Title:
Totally Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Whole Cut Up Fryer Chicken -or- 1 Pkg. Skinless Boneless Chicken (to cut time)
1 Large Onion (diced)
8 Oz. Sour Cream
1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Can Cream of Mushroom Soup
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 Stick Butter (melted)
1 Roll of Ritz crackers (crumbled)

Directions:
Cook chicken, debone and cube. (This may even be done the day before and stored until needed.) Skinless, boneless chicken breasts may be substituted to save time. Lightly saut√© diced onion in a pat of butter until translucent. Mix chicken, diced onion, salt and pepper to taste, sour cream and both cans of soup in a casserole dish. Bake at 350* degrees for 20 mins. (If you would prefer to add vegetables to this casserole, please remember to precook them slightly before baking or adjust oven-cooking time as needed.)
Melt butter and mix with crumbled crackers. Sprinkle this mixture over the chicken. Return to oven for 2 to 3 mins until golden brown.
(This recipe may be made in advance and stored in an airtight freezer container for up to 3 months.)

Submitter: 
MIDNIGHT_ANGEL
Description: 
A creamy chicken casserole using sour cream. A must try for anyone who loves chicken casserole. The best part is it only takes about 30 mins. Vegetables may be added if desired. This recipe may even be stored in the freezer for last minute meal planning.
