Title:
Shrimp,  Avocado, & Arugula Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
pinch of sea salt
1/2 tsp chili powder
1 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1/2 red bell pepper, diced
4 scallions, sliced
3 tbs fresh cilantro, chopped
1 avocado, cubed
1 C grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
1 tbs lime juice
4 C arugula

Directions:
Rinse the shrimp and pat dry.
Sprinkle with salt and chili powder.
Heat 1/2 tbs of the oil in skillet on medium heat and carefully place the shrimp in the pan and sear on each side for about 2 minutes or until cooked through.
Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
Combine all vegetables except the arugula in a serving bowl and toss with the remaining olive oil and lime juice.
Gently fold in the shrimp.
Serve over the arugula sprinkled with a little extra olive oil.
