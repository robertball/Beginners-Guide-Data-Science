Title:
Summer salad garden delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
1 tsp pepper flakes
2 small chicken breast halves cut into chunks
4 large cloves of garlic chopped
1 shallot thinly sliced
2 cups cherry tomatoes halved
3 peaches cut into chunks
2 cups of torn romaine lettuce
Ceasar salad dressing.
4 tbsp of goat cheese
1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil

Directions:
In a large skillet or Wok, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
Add sesame seed and pepper flakes
Add chicken breast and stir and cook for about 3 to 4 minutes
Add garlic and shallot.
Stir and cook until chicken is done.
In a bowl mix tomatoes, peaches, romaine and dressing.
Transfer chicken to serving plate.
Cover with salad mixture
Top with goat cheese and basil
Enjoy

Submitter: 
Ron Lloyd
Description: 
This is a delicious salad. The ingredients may be in your garden or at least at your local farmers market.
