Title:
Rabibt in Red Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Rabbits cut into pecies (sectioned) seasoned well with salt & pepper
1 Bottle good dry red wine
1 Carrot chopped
1 onion chopped
3 ribs celery chopped
1 Bouquet (fresh parsely,thyme, and oregano)
3 cloves garlic
2 Tbs olive oil.

Directions:
In large caaserole over medium high heat, brown meat in the olive oil. Remove meat and saute the veggies and herbs until the onions a translucent. Then add rabbit back to pot.
Add wine, bring to a slow boil and stir. Cover and cook on low heat for 90min. Strain liduid into sauce pan and cook over high heat until it reduces slighty and becomes a little thicker. Serve over meat.
