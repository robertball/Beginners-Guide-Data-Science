Title:
Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag fresh cranberries
2 apples
1 c. sugar
1½ c. boiling water
1 can cherry pie filling
2 pkg. cherry Jell-O
1½ c. cold water
2 cans mandarin oranges
½ c. chopped walnuts

Directions:
Chop cranberries and apples together. Add 1 cup sugar and let sit. Meanwhile, dissolve Jell-O in the boiling water. Mix in the cold water. Stir Jell-O mixture into cranberry mixture. Stir in mandarin oranges and chopped nuts.
