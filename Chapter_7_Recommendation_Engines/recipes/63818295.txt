Title:
Potatoes Romanoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large potatoes
2 c dairy sour cream (1 pint)
1 bunch green onions or chives cut up
1 1/3 c shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided
1 1/3 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
Paprika

Directions:
Cook potatoes in jackets until fork tender. Peel.
Shred potatoes into large bowl.
Stir in sour cream, onions or chives, 1 c. Cheese, salt and pepper.
Turn into greased 2 quart casserole.
Top with remaining cheese.
Sprinkle with paprika.
Cover and refrigerate several hours or overnight.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees F for 32-40 min. Until heated through.

NJHoffman
Description: 
Hearty potato side dish easy to prepare ahead for a dinner party.
