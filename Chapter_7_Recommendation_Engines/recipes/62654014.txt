Title:
Red Lobster Cheddar Bay Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/588427.jpg
Ingredients:
# 2 cups Bisquick baking mix
# 1/2 cup cold water
# 3/4 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
# 1/4 cup butter
# 1 teaspoon dried parsley
# 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
# 1/2 teaspoon italian seasoning

Directions:
1. 1 Heat oven to 450.
2. 2 Combine baking mix, water and grated cheese in a bowl.
3. 3 Roll out on a lightly floured surface, until 1 inch thick.
4. 4 Cut biscuits, and place on an ungreased pan.
5. 5 Melt butter and spices together.
6. 6 Brush the biscuits with the butter and bake for 8 to 10 minutes.
