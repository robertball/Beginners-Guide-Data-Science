Title:
Karen's Hash Brown Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 lb) bag frozen hash browns
1 C. minced onion
2 C. sour cream
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
3 C. shredded cheddar cheese
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/4 C. butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375*.
Thaw hash browns for 30 minutes.
Mix all ingredients, saving some cheese to sprinkle over the top.
Bake in a greased 9x13 pan
Bake for 1 hour
