Title:
Winter Mash roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb minced beef
1 chopped onion
garlic
1/2 pint beef stock
4 large boiled potatoes
butter (to add to the boiled potato)
salt and pepper
oil to fry

Directions:
fry in a little oil the beef, onions and garlic.
mash the potatoes, add butter and place on a piece of aluminium foil paper, and flatten.
Place the minced beef onto the mashed potatoes, and using the foil paper roll into a long shape (like a swiss roll)
place in the oven and on a low heat cook until brown.
