Title:
Creamy Parmesan Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986309.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tablespoons butter
1 1/2 tablespoons Robin Hood® All Purpose Flour
1 (370 ml) can Carnation® Regular, 2% or Fat-Free Evaporated Milk
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup chicken stock
2 ounces bacon or pancetta, chopped

Directions:
Prepare Carnation(R) Simple White Sauce by melting butter in medium saucepan, add flour and cook over low heat stirring for 1 minute.
Whisk in milk and salt. Bring to a boil, lower heat and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally until sauce has thickened.
Add Parmesan cheese, stirring until melted. Stir in chicken stock.
Cook bacon or pancetta in a skillet, until crisp, about 2-3 minutes. Add to white sauce and stir until combined.

Submitter: 
Carnation®
Description: 
This creamy white sauce with Parmesan cheese and chopped bacon can be prepared ahead of time, then heated and served over Rosemary Garlic Chicken or your favourite vegetables.
