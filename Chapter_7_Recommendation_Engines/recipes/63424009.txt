Title:
Fennel potato leek soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large leek (white part only) end trimmed and thinly sliced
1/2 cup diced fresh fennel
2 large russet potatoes peeled and diced
2 tablespoons chopped Italian parsley
4 cups chicken broth
pinch of cayenne pepper
salt to taste
1 tablespoon unsalted butter
1 cup milk (optional)

Directions:
Place leek, fennel, potatoes, and parsley in slow cooker
Add broth. Cook on high for 4 to 6 hours.
Puree. Sprinkle cayenne pepper, add salt, and swirl in the butter. Stir milk (optional).
