Title:
Quinoa Stuffed Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup vegetable broth
1 cup quinoa, rinsed
2 Tbs. olive oil
1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
1 1/2 tsp dried sage
1/4 cup goat cheese
1/4 cup dried cranberries
4 medium baking apples
1/4 cup shredded chedder cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bring broth to a boil. Add quinoa, cover, reduce to a simmer for 17 minutes.
2. Heat oil in the skillet. Cook onion until soft. Add quinoa and sage. Cook until combined and heated through. Remove from heat. Stir in goat cheese and cranberries.
3. Halve apples through stem and scoop out core and seeds. Fill apples apples with the mixutre. Place in a baking dish and cover with tin foil. Bake for 45 minutes.
4. Top with chedder cheese. Bake for 15 more minutes.
