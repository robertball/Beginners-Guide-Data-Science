Title:
Grilled Portobello Mushroom Caps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 portobello mushroom caps, cleaned
1 large tomato
6 slices deli mozzarella, sliced thick
1/2 cup Kraft Roasted Red Pepper Parmesan dressing

Directions:
Place mushrooms on hot, oiled grill, tops up.
Brush tops with dressing.
Grill 4 minutes and flip.
Generously brush other side with dressing.
Dip each tomato slice in dressing and place on mushrooms on grill.
Top each tomato with 1 slice mozzarella. Grill until cheese is melted.
Remove from grill.
Serve as a side or on a toasted hamburger bun for vegetarians.

Submitter: 
Angie Duffy Hernandez
Description: 
Grilled Portobellos topped with tomatoes and mozzarella.
