Title:
Butterscotch Bliss Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package instant butterscotch pudding mix
3c. low-fat milk
1/2c. whipping cream (or more low-fat milk)
1/2c. caramel dip (the type used for dipping apple slices)
1/2c. dry-roasted peanuts

Directions:
Mix instant pudding powder with 2c. milk in a medium bowl-- whisk for 2 minutes (or per box directions). Add remaining milk and cream to mixture and stir until blended. Chill mixture in refrigerator for 5-10 minutes.  Process mixture in ice cream machine, per manufacturer's directions. Spread caramel dip into a thin layer at the bottom of the same medium bowl. Sprinkle peanuts on top of caramel. When ice cream has reached soft-serve consistency, remove from machine and mix in bowl with caramel and peanuts. Cover bowl tightly and place ice cream in freezer 2-3 hours to finish hardening.

Description: 
Gooey and luscious butterscotch ice cream with a caramel ribbon and a little crunch- no cooking required!
