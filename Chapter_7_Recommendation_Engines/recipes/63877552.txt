Title:
Pressed cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
3 sticks butter
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Stir together flour and baking powder.  Set aside.
Beat butter for 30 secs, add sugar and bear until fluffy.
Add egg and vanilla.
Gradually add flour mixture, beating until well combined.
Force dough through a cookie press onto an Ungreased cookie sheet.
Sprinkle with colored sugar, if desired.
Bake 7-8 minutes in a 400F oven or until barely golden on the edges.
Cool on a wire rack.
