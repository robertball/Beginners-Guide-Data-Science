Title:
Cheesy Cauliflower Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head of cauliflower
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp curry powder
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup bread crumbs
2 tbsp melted butter

Directions:
1. Cut cauliflower into large chunks and rinse.
2. Arrange cauliflower in a microwave-safe casserole dish with 2 tbsp water. Cover and microwave on high for 5 minutes.
3. In a bowl, stir cream of mushroom soup, mayonnaise, curry powder and cheddar cheese until well blended.
4. Remove cauliflower from microwave, stir in soup mixture.
5. Melt butter in small bowl in microwave. Add enough breadcrumbs while stirring until crumbly. Sprinkle on top of cauliflower mix.
6. Put casserole dish uncovered in 350 oven for 45 minutes or until brown and bubbly and cauliflower is fork tender.

Submitter: 
MONICA DEREGT
Description: 
A very quick and tasty way to prepare cauliflower. The hint of curry surprises and delights everyone who I have made it for, even people who don't usually enjoy cauliflower! It is a perfect accompaniment to turkey dinner.
