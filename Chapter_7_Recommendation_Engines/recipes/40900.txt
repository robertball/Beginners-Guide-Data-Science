Title:
Warm Spinach Garlic Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982119.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach
1/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon olive oil
3 teaspoons bottled roasted garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 dashes bottled hot pepper sauce
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese (cut up)
Keebler速 Club速 Crackers

Directions:
Cook spinach according to package directions.  Drain well; press out excess liquid.
In medium saucepan combine milk, oil, roasted garlic, salt and hot pepper sauce.  Add both packages of cream cheese;  cook and stir over low heat until cheese has melted and mixture is heated through.  Stir in spinach.  Serve chilled or warm with Club(R) Crackers.
