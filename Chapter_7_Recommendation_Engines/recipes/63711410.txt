Title:
Vegetable Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box Long Grain & Wild Rice Mix (Uncle Ben's)
1 Cup diced Roma, Cherry or Grape Tomatoes
1 Small bunch Parsley, finely chopped
3-4 Scallions, sliced
1 Jar Marinated Artichoke Hearts, drained
1 small can black olives, sliced
1 pkg. frozen petite peas, cooked crisp
Dressing
1/4 Cup Salad Oil, Canola
1/2 Cup Vinegar, White Balsamic, wine or herb
1/4 Cup Sugar

Directions:
Cook the Rice mix using 1 1/2 Cups of water for 20-22 minutes. See directions on box.
Place cooked rice in a large bowl to cool.
Chop all the vegetables and add to the rice mixture.
Cook the frozen peas for 2 minutes after water boils.
Drain and cool.
Blend the salad dressing ingredients in a blender or shake in a covered bottle until creamy.
Toss the dressing over the rice, vegetable mixture and chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
The Vegetable Rice Salad is one of my favorite dishes to bring for any occasion because it is not only delicious, but has a colorful presentation and is excellent for pot luck or buffet style parties.
This recipe is well received whenever I serve it. I am frequently asked for the recipe.
