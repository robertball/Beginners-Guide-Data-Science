Title:
Savory Backyard Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978252.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons McCormick® Grill Mates® Montreal Chicken® Seasoning
1 teaspoon McCormick® Ground Allspice
1 teaspoon McCormick® Chili Powder
1 teaspoon McCormick® Thyme Leaves
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Caraway Seed, crushed
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Ground Mustard
4 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
4 pounds chicken parts

Directions:
Preheat grill.
Combine first six ingredients in a small bowl. Stir in balsamic vinegar and oil. Rub mixture evenly on chicken.
Grill chicken over medium direct heat for 25-30 minutes, or until internal temperature of the thickest portion of breast reaches 170 degrees F and thigh reaches 180 degrees F. Turn every 5 minutes to prevent chicken from burning.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Grill Mates® Montreal Chicken Seasoning, allspice, chili powder and other herbs and spices combine to make a grilling paste that makes succulent, juicy chicken. It works well with the chicken skin on or off.
