Title:
Pink Honey Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/660533.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
3 fresh strawberries, sliced
1 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/4 cup SPLENDA® Brown Sugar Blend
1 teaspoon honey
7 cups water
1 3/4 cups fresh lemon juice
2 slices orange

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine 1 cup water, strawberries, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, SPLENDA(R) Brown Sugar Blend, and honey. Bring to a boil, and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Cool to room temperature, cover, and chill.
In a large pitcher, mix together water, lemon juice, and orange slices. Stir in cooled strawberry and sweetener mixture; chill. Serve in a tall glass over ice.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Freshly squeezed lemon juice and orange slices are combined with a sweetened strawberry mixture to create a long, cool drink for summer.
