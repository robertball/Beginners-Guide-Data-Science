Title:
Cheesy Ham and Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (32 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes
8 ounces cooked, diced ham
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of potato soup
1/2 diced onion
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cook onion until translucent. In a large bowl, mix hash browns, ham, onion, cream of potato soup, sour cream, and Cheddar cheese. Spread evenly into prepared dish. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Bake 1 hour 10 min. in the preheated oven, or until bubbly and lightly brown. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Tina Loop-Duckett

Breakfast casserole with potatoes and ham in a creamy sauce. I added onion. Be sure to only use cream of potato soupModified from: Cheesy Ham and Hash Brown Casserole
