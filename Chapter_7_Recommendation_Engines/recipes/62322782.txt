Title:
Honey Pork Oriental Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. pork steaks, cut into 1 inch cubes
2 Tbsp. oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 envelope brown gravy mix
1/2 cup honey
3 Tbsp. soy sauce
3 Tbsp. vinegar
4 carrots, sliced
1 medium onion, diced
Hot cooked rice

Directions:
Brown pork with garlic in oil over medium heat for 15 minutes or until no longer pink inside.
Combine remaining ingredients, except rice, as prepared.
Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
Serve over rice.
