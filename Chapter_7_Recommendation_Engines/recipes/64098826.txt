Title:
Guapa Juice cloned DETOX+  Tonic Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium carrots
1/2 beet
1 celery stalk
1/2 cucumber
Ice cubes (optional)

Directions:
Wash all the vegetables and peel them if covered in a wax coating.
Slowly add each to your juicer as per manufacturer's instructions.
Stir well and serve over ice if desired.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
I recently had this juice at a kiosk place called Guapa Juice in Liege, Belgium. It is cool and fresh and a quick vitamin kick to start your day! Use organic produce when possible to avoid contaminates.
