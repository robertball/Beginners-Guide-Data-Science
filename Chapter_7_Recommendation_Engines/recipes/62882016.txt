Title:
Chicken Meatballs w/ Sweet Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/694650.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground chicken
1/2 cup chopped cilantro
1/4 cup dried breadcrumbs
1/4 cup grated onion
2 cloves garlic, grated
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 TB vegetable oil
1/4 cup peanut butter
2 TB soy sauce
4 TB sesame oil
3 TB sugar
chopped peanuts, for garnish

Directions:
Mix chicken, cilantro, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, salt and pepper; roll into 1-TB balls.
In a medium skillet, heat oil over medium heat.  Add meatballs and cook through, 6 to 8 minutes.
Meanwhile, mix remaining ingredients (except peanuts) and warm in microwave.
Toss meatballs with half of sauce and sprinkle with peanuts.
Serve on toothpicks with remaining sauce for dipping.

Submitter: 
Kemberli Paes
Description: 
Serve as appetizer or alongside rice and stir fried veggies.
