Title:
PA Dutch Milk Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tb flour
4 Tb sugar
1 1/2 cups cream
1 Tb butter
1 9" pie shell
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Put flour and sugar into pie shell. Mix together and spread over pie shell w/fingers. Cover w/cream and dot w/ butter. Bake 400 degrees for 35 minutes.
