Title:
Creamy Ranch Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBS Vegetable oil
4 Boneless pork chops 3/4-inch thick
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
3/4 cup milk
1 pkg. dry ranch salad dressing mix

Directions:
1) Heat oil in 10 inch skillet over med-high heat. Add pork chops and cook until well browned on both sides.
2) Stir the soup, milk, and 1/2 pkg. salad dressing mix in the skillet and heat to a boil. Reduce the heat to low. Cover and cook for 10 minutes or until pork is cooked through. Sprinkle with paprika.
