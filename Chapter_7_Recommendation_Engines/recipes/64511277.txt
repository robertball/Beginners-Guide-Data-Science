Title:
Quick & Easy Nut Chews Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2743417.jpg
Ingredients:
graham crackers to cover bottom of jelly roll pan
1 cup nuts (your choice), chopped, shaved, etc.
1 cup butter or margarine
3/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Arrange graham crackers to cover bottom of a 10" x 15" jelly roll pan.
Sprinkle nuts evenly over crackers.
In small saucepan, boil butter and sugar for 3 minutes.
Drizzle over crackers and nuts.
Bake @ 350 x 8 minutes.
Cool.
Break apart to serve. A pizza cutter also works to separate them.
Freezes well.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Carmelly, crunchy, buttery goodness. These are waaay too easy to make, so share them or you may eat them all yourself!
