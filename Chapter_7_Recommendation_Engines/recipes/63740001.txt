Title:
Homestyle Garlic Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil, 2 Cups Real Mayo, 1 Cup Buttermilk, 5 Tablespoons Sour Cream, 2 Hardboiled Eggs Diced, 1 Small Onion Diced, 1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder, 2 Pinches Kosher Salt, 1 Tea Spoon Black Pepper, 2 Table Spoons Dry Parsley

Directions:
Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and whisk together until you see a nice smooth creamy dressing.  About 30 seconds.  Place contents into 1 qt jar and refrigerate for up to 1 week.

Submitter: 
gbrlbll
Description: 
Ranch dressing connoisseurs will love this recipe!   It is a staple in our household and we make a fresh batch every week.
