Title:
Dukan Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744540.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb lean ground pork or turkey or sirloin
1/2 small yellow onion, chopped
1 package dry taco seasoning (the less sodium one doesn’t have any sugars)
1/2 serrano chili, chopped
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
1 plum tomato, seeded and chopped (leave out if you are in Attack)
1 green onion, chopped
2 T red onion, chopped
4 oz fat free cream cheese
1/2 cup low fat or fat free sour cream
1/2 cup fat free cheddar, shredded

Directions:
Brown pork with the yellow onion for five minutes.  Remove from heat and drain, add all the rest of the ingredients and mix well.  Place mixture in a casserole dish sprayed with cooking spray and bake at 375 for 25-30 minutes.  Serve with celery sticks.

Submitter: 
tjmallick
Description: 
It’s football season and everyone wants to have fun on Sunday watching the games!  This dip is delicious and if you make it with the right ingredients, can be very Dukan-friendly!Perfect for Attack or Cruise, this dip can be made with any ground meat you want.
