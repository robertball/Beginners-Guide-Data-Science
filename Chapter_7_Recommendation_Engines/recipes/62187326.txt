Title:
Easy Chicken Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cooked Lasagna noodles
1 box of chicken stuffing (Like Stove Top)
1 stick butter or margarine, melted
2 cans of cream of chicken soup
2 cups shredded Mozzarella cheese
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup milk
3 to 4 cooked chicken breast, diced into chunks
1 tsp onion powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook Stuffing as directed.  To prepare sauce, combine both cans of soup, milk, butter, onion powder, salt and pepper.  In a lasagna pan, layer 4 cooked noodles, 1/2 the cubed chicken, all of the stuffing, 1/2 the sauce mixture, 1/2 of each cheeses. Next layer, add the next 4 noodles, rest of the chicken, rest of the sauce and the last of both cheeses.  Bake at 350 for 30 - 45 min or until cheese is melted and golden brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
A yummy recipe using stuffing within the layers of the lasagna.  A great way to use thanksgiving leftovers by using turkey and stuffing instead of chicken.
