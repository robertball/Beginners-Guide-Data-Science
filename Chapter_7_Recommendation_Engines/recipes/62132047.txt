Title:
Grilled/Marinated Mediterranean Zuchinni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 zuchinni
2 tbl McCormick Mediterranean Spiced Sea Salt
Olive Oil to coat
1/2 tsp crushed garlic if desired

Directions:
Cut ends off of zuchinni, then cut lenghwise into quarters.  Place in freezer bag.  Add spiced sea salt, garlic if desired, and enough olive oil to generously coat all zuchinni.  Close bag tightly and shake to evenly coat.
Let stand at least 30 minutes for flavors to infuse.
Grill 7-10 minutes.
