Title:
Strawberry Cream Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, separated
pinch salt
pinch cream of tartar
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup ground almonds
1 tsp almond extract
1 litre whipping cream
3 cups sliced fresh strawberries

Directions:
Beat egg whites with salt, cream of tartar until soft peaks form
Gradually add 1/2 cup sugar and beat until whites are firm and stiff
Beat yolks with 1/2 cup sugar and almond extract
Put 1 spoonful of egg white into yolk mixture and blend
Pour yolk mixture into whites and fold gently while slowly adding flour and ground almonds
Lightly spray cookie sheet with Pam
Pour batter onto cookie sheet
Bake at 400 degrees until just golden
Let cool
Spread alyer of whipped cream over cake and then top with strawberries
Roll into log
Top with more cream and strawberries
Garnish with slivered almonds and/or shaved chocolate
