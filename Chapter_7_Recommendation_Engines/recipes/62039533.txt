Title:
Spicy Sweet Potato Flan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
1 cup mashed sweet potatoes
2 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
2 cups milk
10 large eggs
1 tea ground cinnamon
1/2 tea allspice
1/4 tea ground cloves
1 tea vanilla
optional garnish- toasted shredded coconut

Directions:
Sprinkle sugar in a 10" round cake pan.  Cook over medium heat shaking constantly til sugar melts and turns golden.  Remove from heat (mixture will cool and crack).  Process 1/2 cup sweet potatoes, 1 can evaporated milk and 1/2 of each of the next 6 ingredients in a blender til smooth.  Pour into large bowl.  Process next half of ingredients and pour into bowl.  Pour all into cakepan.  Cover with foil.  Place pan in a larger shallow pan and add hot water to a depth of 1/2".  Bake at 325 for 1 hour and 15 min.  Cover and chill 4 hours.  Run knife around edge to loosen and invert.  Sprinkle with coconut.

Description: 
A twist on traditional flan, this spicy custard blended with yams makes for a pretty and tasty presentation during the holidays!
