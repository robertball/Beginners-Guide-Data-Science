Title:
Venison Stew Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4582079.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds Venison Stew Meat
1 medium onion chopped
4 garlic cloves mined
2 jalapeno sliced
12 ounce can mild enchilada sauce
1/2 teaspoon Cayene Pepper
1/2 teaspoon Cumin
12 ounces Medium Salsa
15.5 ounce can light red kidney beans
16 ounce can refried beans

Directions:
Add butter to a 10 inch cast iron skillet on high and when butter is melted add onion, garlic, and jalapeno pepper. Stir for 5 minutes and then add to slow cooker.
Coat lightly in flour the venison stew meat and brown in skillet then add to slow cooker.
Add enchilada sauce and seasonings to slow cooker and cook on high for 5 hours.
Stir in salsa and kidney beans to slow cooker and cook on low for another hour.
Stir in refried beans to thicken chili and serve in a bowl.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
Big chucks of venison stew meat in a enchilada sauce combined with fresh garlic, onions, and jalapeno. Slow cooked with flavor from spices and mild salsa added. Finally, kidney beans added and refried beans to thicken chili. Great over a bowl of rice.
