Title:
Mint Lime Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon chopped mint
1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
1 / 8 teaspoon cumin
1 clove garlic, minced
1 / 2 teaspoon honey
1 / 4 cup olive oil
Sea salt, to taste

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk dressing ingredients to combine.
