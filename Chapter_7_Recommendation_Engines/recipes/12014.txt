Title:
Summer Pasta Toss I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/667342.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces spaghetti, broken into pieces
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 cups chopped salami
1/2 cup Italian-style salad dressing
2 tomatoes, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped

Directions:
In a large pot with boiling salted water cook spaghetti until al dente. Rinse with cool water. Drain.
In a large bowl combine cooked and drained spaghetti, grated Parmesan cheese, salami, Italian dressing, tomatoes, and green bell peppers. Toss gently to mix together.
Chill in the refrigerator before serving.

Submitter: 
KRYM
Description: 
An easy, light meal. Great for those dog days of summer!
