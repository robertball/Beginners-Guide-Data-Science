Title:
Flourless Peanut Butter Cookies II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup white sugar
1 egg
or add 1/2 cup brown sugar and 1/4 cup white sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
Combine ingredients and drop by teaspoonfuls on cookie sheet. Flatten slightly with fork dipped in sugar. Bake for 8 minutes.  Let cool.   Recipe doesn't make very many, so you could double recipe as you desire.
