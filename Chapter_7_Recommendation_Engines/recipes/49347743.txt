Title:
CORN BREAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup plus 1 tbsp. vegetable oil
1cup 		yellow cornmeal
1 cup 		all purpose flour
2 tsp. 		baking powder
1 tsp. 		salt
1/4 tsp. 	cayenne
1 cup 		buttermilk
1 		large egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Put 1 tbsp. oil in a round baking pan and
put in oven to heat for at least 10 minutes.
Stir together cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt and cayenne in large
mixing bowl.  Add buttermilk, the remaining 1/4 cup oil and egg and stir
just to blend.
Pour batter into preheated pan and bake until lightly golden brown,
about 25 minutes.  Let cool 10 minutes before serving.
