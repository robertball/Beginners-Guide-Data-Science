Title:
moist chocolate cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
3/4 cup baking cocoa
2 cups sugar
1 cup vegetable oil
1 cup hot brewed coffee
1 cup milk
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
Icing:
1 cup milk
5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2cup butter, softened
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
sift together all dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl.  Add oil, coffee and milk;  mix at medium speed for 2 minutes.  Add edds and vanilla; beat 2 more minutes. Pour into two greased and floured 9-inch cake pans.  Bake at 325 for 25-30 min.  Meanwhile, for icing combine the milk and flour in a saucepan; cook until thick.  Cover and refrigerate.  In a medium mixing bowl, beat butter, shortening, sugar and vanilla until creamy.  Add chilled milk/flour mixture and beat for 10 minutes.  Frost cooled cake
