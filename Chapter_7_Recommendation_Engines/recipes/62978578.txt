Title:
Paula Deen's Sweet Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Mashed Sweet Potatoes
(can nuke 2 for 12 min for this)
1 cup sugar
1/3 cup butter, melted
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 cup heavy cream, half and half or whole milk
Topping
1 cup brown sugar
3 tbs butter, melted
1/3 cups flour
1 cup pecans or walnuts chopped
*Mix together w/ fork and sprinkle over casserole

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Mix all ingredients together except for cream.
Beat with an electric mixer until smooth
Add cream, mix well
Pour into greased casserol dish (1 1/2 quarts), add topping and Bake for 25-30 minutes
