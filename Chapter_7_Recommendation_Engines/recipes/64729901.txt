Title:
Chicken Salad (Vegetarian) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6275804.jpg
Ingredients:
4 C diced Fri-Chick
2 C thinly sliced celery
3/4 C mayonnaise
1/4 C whipping cream
3/4 C lemon juice
2 T curry powder
2 T dill weed
1/2 t salt
seedless red and green grapes* optional

Directions:
Combine Fri-Chick and celery.
Combine next 8 ingredients, stirring well; add to chicken mixture and toss well.
Chill.
Serve salad in croissants or lettuce leaves.
Garnish with grapes.
