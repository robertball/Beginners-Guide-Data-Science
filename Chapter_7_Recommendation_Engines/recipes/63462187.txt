Title:
Broccoli rice, cheese casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. chopped onions
2 Tbs. butter or 2 Tbs. margarine, divided
2 C's. cooked white rice
1 (10 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup (cream of chicken soup)
1 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped broccoli, cooked,well drained
1 C. Cheez Whiz (About 4 ounces from a block of Velveeta is equal 1 cup.)
1/2 C. fresh bread, cubes (crushed ritz crackers for the topping)

Directions:
Preheat oven 350F.
Stir onion in 1 Tbs. butter and cook in skillet until soft.
Add rice, soup, broccoli and cheez whiz; mix lightly.
Spoon into 1 1/2 quart casserole.
Toss together remaining 1 Tbs. butter, melted, and bread cubes; sprinkle over casserole.
Bake for 30-35 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
