Title:
S'More Krispie Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/654863.jpg
Ingredients:
Treat:
1/4 Cup Lite Butter
4 Cup Mini Marshmallows
3 Cups Rice Krispie Cereal (plain)
2 Cups Cocoa Rice Krispie Cereal
*************
Filling:
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
2 tablespoons butter, cubed
1 tablespoon water
1/4 cup and 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
**************
Top Coat:
1/2 Cup Mini Mallows
2 Tbsp Lite Butter
1 Tsp Cinnamon
2 Tbsp Cinnamon
Handful of mini chocolate chips/mini mallows

Directions:
Melt margarine in large sauce pan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until melted and well-blended. Cook 2 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
Add cereal. Stir until well coated.
Press Half the mixture down in a pie pan
Set aside rest.
To make the filling microwave butter and chocolate chips until soft add water, gradually add confectioners' sugar
Spread over the mixture in the pan
Apply the rest of the rice krispie mixture and pat down
Top coat melt butter and marshmallow in pan as previously done (smaller pan)
Add sugar and cinnamon to marshmallow mix
Spread over top of the krispie mix
Let sit in fridge for about one hour
Pull out, place mini chocolate chips and marshmallows on the top and press down
Replace in fridge until ready to serve!
