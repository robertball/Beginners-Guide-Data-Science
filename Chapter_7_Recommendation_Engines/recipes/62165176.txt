Title:
Homemade Beef Breakfast Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons dried sage
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon dried marjoram
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
2 teaspoons dried basil
2 pounds ground beef (as fatty as possible)
1 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
In a small, bowl, combine the sage, salt, ground black pepper, marjoram, brown sugar, onion powder, crushed red pepper and basil. Mix well.
Place the beef  in a large bowl and add the mixed spices to it. Mix well with your hands
Refrigerate 24 hours to let flavors blend.
Form into eight patties and fry being careful not to overcook
