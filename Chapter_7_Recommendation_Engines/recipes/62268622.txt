Title:
Corn and Chicken Soup in the Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Campbell's cream of potato soup
1 can chicken broth
2 cans creamed corn
2 chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)
3 celery stalks, sliced thin
1 large onion, chopped
3 whole green onions, sliced
3 medium carrots, peeled and sliced
2 cloves garlic, smashed
1/2 tsp thyme
salt & pepper to taste
******
1 cup skim milk
1 cup instant potato flakes
1/4 cup minced parsley

Directions:
Put soup, broth, corn, chicken, celery, onion, green onions, carrots, garlic, thyme, salt & pepper into the crockpot.
Cook on low for about 7 hours.
Break apart the chicken and add the milk, potato flakes and parsley.  Cook another 30 minutes.
Adjust seasonings and serve.

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
You can throw this altogether in the crockpot in the morning and come home to a great meal!
