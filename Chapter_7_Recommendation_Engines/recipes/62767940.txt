Title:
Actually Delicious Turkey Burgers without the bun Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pounds ground turkey
2 Tbls. seasoned bread crumbs
1 Tbls. cup finely diced onion
1 egg lightly beaten
1 Tbls.chopped fresh parsley
2 Tbls. diced sweet bell pepper
1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 tsp. soy sauce and
1/2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 slices pepper jack goat cheese
chopped watercress or greens

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix ground turkey, seasoned bread crumbs, onion, egg whites, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper. Form into 2 patties.
Grill the patties on oiled prepared grill over medium heat, turning once
Melt cheese on top or put it on after
lay the patties on greens on individual plates

Submitter: 
Lynn
Description: 
Turkey burgers are a delicious change of pace. Slap these moist patties on the grill for a special summer treat!Modified from: Actually Delicious Turkey Burgers
