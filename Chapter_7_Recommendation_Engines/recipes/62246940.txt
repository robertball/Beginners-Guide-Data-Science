Title:
Coffee Pecan Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/330368.jpg
Ingredients:
A:
5T Unsalted butter
3/4C Granulated Sugar
B:
1 Large egg
C (mixed together):
2C All purpose flour
1T Baking powder
1/2t Salt
D (mixed together):
1C Milk
1 1/2T Instant coffee granules
1t Vanilla extract
E:
About 1/2C pecan toasted and roughly chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 205C.Prepare muffin tins (greased and floured or lined) and set aside.
Cream together A until light and fluffy.
Add egg and mix well.
Add C and D alternately beginning and ending with C. Do not overmix.
Fold in pecans. Turn batter into prepared pan and place in oven.
Bake at 205C for 12 minutes then turn down to 170C and bake another 13-18 minutes more or till done (this step is really important, if baked at 205C for the total time the muffins will be dry).
Enjoy!
