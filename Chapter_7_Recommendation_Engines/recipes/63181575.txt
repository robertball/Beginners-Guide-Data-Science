Title:
Black Bean Chili Veganized Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 large onion, large chopped
2 stalk celery, large chopped
3 carrots, large chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1/2 tsp. black pepper
4 - 5 cups vegetable broth
3 (15 oz.) cans black beans, drained and rinsed or Thawed Frozen
1 (15 oz.) can whole kernel corn, drained and rinsed, or Thawed Frozen
1 (15 oz.) can kidney or white beans, drained and rinsed
4 lg. Roma Tomatoes, chopped and crushed

Directions:
Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Saute onion, celery, carrots and garlic for 5 minutes. Season with chili powder, cumin, and black pepper; cook for 1 minute. Stir in vegetable broth, 1 cans of black beans, 1 can of kidney or white beans, and corn. Bring to a boil and then turn down to medium.
Meanwhile, in a food processor or blender, process remaining 2 cans black beans and 1/2 the tomatoes until smooth. (If necessary add 1/2 cup of vegetable broth).  Stir into bean and veggie mixture and simmer for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
VH
Description: 
Vegan option that tastes like chili.  Black beans and fresh tomatoes pureed and combined with vegetable broth, chunky celery, carrots, onion and corn along with kidney or white beans.  Slow cooker friendly.Modified from: Vegan Black Bean Soup
