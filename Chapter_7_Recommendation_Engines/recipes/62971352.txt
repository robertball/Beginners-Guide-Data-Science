Title:
J's Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/768163.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Bag Frozen Birds Eye Classic Mixed vegetables
1 Bag Green Giant Steamers Broccoli, Carrots, Cauliflower & Cheese Sauce
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 1/2 pound Chicken Breast
1 tablespoon Parsley (dried)
1 teaspoon Thyme (dried)
1 teaspoon Mrs. Dash (original blend)
1 can Evaporated Milk
1 can Vegetable Broth
3 tablespoon Melted Butter
1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
1 cup Instant Mashed Potatos
2 cup Bisquick

Directions:
1. Cut chicken into small pieces, cook in frying pan with a little oil
2. combine Frozen Vegetables, Cream of chicken, Cooked chicken, Parsley, Thyme, Mrs. Dash, 1/2 can Evaporatived Milk
3. Put in 8x11 in. casserole bowl. pour in vegetable broth until you can start to see the broth through the mix
4. Combine Bisquick, Potato flakes, Parmesan cheese, rest of vegetable broth & evaporated milk. Mix to pancake batter considecy. add milk if needed
5. Top vegetable mix with batter
6. Bake 400 degrees 60 to 70 minutes

Submitter: 
Kelly Jo Telladira
Description: 
This is an easy and most delicious recipe.     You'll especially like this if you like to spice up your food and love the taste of Parmesan Cheese.
