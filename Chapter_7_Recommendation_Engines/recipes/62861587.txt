Title:
Spicy Sausage, Egg and  Cheese Tapas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/685337.jpg
Ingredients:
Ingredients
•5 tbsp olive oil
•8 oz gound chorizo sasage
•2 Spanish onions, halved and thinly sliced
•12 large (US extra large) eggs
•2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley, plus extra to garnish
•1 cup grated locatelli Pecorino Romano or other hard cheese
•Salt and ground black pepper

Directions:
Preparation
Heat 1 tbsp of the oil in a 12-inch non-stick frying pan and fry the sausage until golden brown and cooked through. Lift out with a slotted spoon and drain on kitchen paper.
Add a further 1 tbsp oil to the pan and fry the  onions for 2-3 minutes, turning frequently.
cook over a gentle heat for about 30 minutes turning occasionally, until softened and  golden.
In a large mixing bowl, beat together the eggs, parsley, cheese, sausage and seasoning.
stir in the onions until coated.
Heat the remaining 2 tbsp oil. Add the onion mixture and cook, over a very low heat, until the egg begins to set. Use a metal spatula to prevent the tortilla from sticking and allow the uncooked egg to run underneath.
Preheat the broiler to high. When the eggs have set have set around the edge, which should take about 5 minutes, protect the pan handle with foil and place under the broiler until the middle is set the whole thing is golden.
Cut into wedges and serve at room temp garnished with parsley.
