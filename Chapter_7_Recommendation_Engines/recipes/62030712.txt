Title:
Pasta e Fagoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Olive oil to sauté onions and garlic
1 small red onion (chopped fine)
2 cloves of garlic (chopped fine)
1-24 ounce can V8 juice
2-16 ounce cans Italian style stewed tomatoes w/basil and oregano
1 4-ounce can sliced mushrooms
1 27-ounce can kidney beans
1 packet “Shillings Italian Chicken Marinade”
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1 8-ounce packet dried cheese filled tortellini
1-pound ground turkey

Directions:
Brown turkey meat, red onion and garlic in bottom of large soup pan with Olive oil. Add ingredients. (Season to personal taste)
The last item you should add is the dried pasta, cooked according to package directions.
Sprinkle fresh Parmesan cheese on each bowl of soup.

Submitter: 
Clark Hamblen
Description: 
This is a hearty soup that will thrill your family. I love it with a crusty french or italian bread!
