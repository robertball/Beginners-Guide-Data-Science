Title:
Tracy Ann's Corn and Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/458974.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can "Hot" Diced Tomatoes & Habaneros
1 can Yellow & White Corn (drained)
1 can Black Beans (rinsed & drained)
1 cup "Medium" Picante
1/2 medium onion (diced)
1 tsp. Cumin
1 tsp. Cilantro

Directions:
Mix together.  Stir well and chill for 2 hours  Serve with taco chips.  Makes about 9 servings.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
You can make this salsa as mild or hot as you want by the degree of heat in the tomatoes & chiles and the picante you use.  Can be eaten as a light healthy lunch with taco chips, or as an appetizer.
