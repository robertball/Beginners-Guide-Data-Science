Title:
English Muffin Chicken Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the chicken:
1 10 oz canned chicken (drained)
Half red onion diced
One quarter of red bell pepper finely diced
1 tablespoon vegetable or canola oil
Half teaspoon crushed garlic
Half teaspoon curry powder
Black pepper powder (optional)
Salt for taste
Chopped cilantro
For the base:
1 box of pre-sliced English Muffins
Butter or margarine (optional)
Pasta sauce or ketchup
Mozzarella cheese or Swiss cheese

Directions:
To make the chicken:
Heat one table spoon of oil in a pan
Fry onions till golden brown
Reduce heat and add garlic, curry powder, and black pepper (if using black pepper)
Add the diced red bell pepper and saute till the peppers are soft
Add the chicken and simmer till all the spices are mixed in (about 5 minutes)
Add salt to taste
Add chopped cilantro
To make the pizzas, preheat oven to 300 degrees
While oven is preheating, lightly toast the English Muffins (you can put it in a regular toaster on a low setting)
Add margarine or butter if needed (you can omit this if you like)
Put some ketchup or pasta sauce on each slice
Add the prepared chicken on top of each slice
Add cheese (you can vary the amount depending on personal preference)
Bake on a cookie tray for about 10 minutes or till cheese has melted
Serve immediately

A quick and easy snack for kids and adults.  Great for parties or pot-lucks!
