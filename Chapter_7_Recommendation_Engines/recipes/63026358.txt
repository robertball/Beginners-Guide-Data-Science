Title:
Chocolate Choco/White Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
3/4 t baking soda
2/3 cup cocoa
1/4 t salt
2 eggs
1 cup butter, room temp
1 1/2 cup sugar
2 t vanilla
2 cups chips (white, chocolate, or mixture)

Directions:
Oven to 350
Sift flour, salt, baking soda, and cocoa together.
In seperate bowl, beat sugar, eggs, butter, and vanilla.
Gradually add dry to wet.
Bake @ 350 for 8 to 10 minutes.  9 is perfect for me.
Have cut recipe in half with no problems.
