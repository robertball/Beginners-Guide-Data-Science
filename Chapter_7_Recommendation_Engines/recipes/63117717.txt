Title:
30-Second Brownie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1 tablespoon flour
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon water
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
splash of vanilla
pinch of salt
1 teaspoon mini chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Stir all ingredients into a mug and microwave for 30 seconds.
