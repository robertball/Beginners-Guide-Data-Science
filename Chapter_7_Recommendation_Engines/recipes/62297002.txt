Title:
Katy's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground meat browned with garlic powder and 1 small chopped onion.
1 large can spaghetti sauce (Hunts or Del Monte, both are good and cheap!!)
1 bunch of fresh mushrooms or two cans of sliced
1-2 boxes of lasagna noodles
1 Container ricotta cheese
1 container cottage cheese
2 cup shredded cheese
2 eggs
1 tsp oregano
Grated parmesan

Directions:
Preheat oven 425
Add spaghetti sauce to meat and set aside.
Mix in separate bowl, ricotta, cottage, 1 c. shredded cheese, 1 tsp oregano and lightly beaten eggs.
In bottom of casserole dish, spray with non-stick spray.
Spread a thin layer on bottom of dish so noodles wonâ€™t get hard
Layer in order:
Noodles
Meat Sauce
Cheese mixture
Mushrooms
Layer until you end up with meat sauce on top. Sprinkle with remaining cup of shredded cheese and sprinkle with parmesan.
Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 mins or until cheese is bubbly golden brown.
Let stand 5 mins, then serve
Serve with salad and bread sticks
