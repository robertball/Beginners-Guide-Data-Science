Title:
Spicy Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs Simply Potatoes Southwestern Style Hash Browns
1 10 oz can cream of mushroom soup
8 oz sour cream
8 oz Cheeze Whiz
2 C shredded Colby-Jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease sides and bottom of 13x9 inch pan with cooking spray.
In large bowl mix together hash browns, soup, sour cream and Cheeze Whiz. Add shredded cheese and mix in to combine.
Place hash brown mixture into prepared pan.  Bake 45 minutes uncovered.

Submitter: 
cara1997
Description: 
This a recipe that I created myself when I thought other cheesy potato recipes were rather bland.  They have just the right amount of spice to them. I usually make these when making barbeque ribs. My family and friends just love them and are always asking for the recipe.
