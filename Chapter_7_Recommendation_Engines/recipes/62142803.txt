Title:
EASY yellow cake corn bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow cake mix
3 Jiffy corn muffin mixes

Directions:
Prepared the cake mix according to directions on package.  Mix in dry muffin mixes and blend well.  Spread into a cooking spray coated sheet cake pan and bake according to directions on the cake mix.
