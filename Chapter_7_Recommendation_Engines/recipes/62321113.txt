Title:
Beef Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup water
2 Tbsp. lemon juice
1 Tbsp. brown sugar
2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
1/4 tsp. hot pepper sauce
1 clove garlic, minced (or 1/2 Tbsp. garlic salt)

Directions:
Stir all ingredients together.  Makes 1 1/4 cup.
Pour over steaks in a shallow dish or resealable plastic bag.
Marinate in refrigerator overnight.
Discard marinade and cook steak as desired.
