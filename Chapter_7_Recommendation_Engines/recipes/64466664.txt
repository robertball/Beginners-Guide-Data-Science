Title:
DinoMites Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2448810.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup Nutella
2 eggs
1/2 cup + 2 TBS flour
Optional ingredients:
Crushed pretzels
Sea salt
Candy eyes are a great addition to liven them for a party!

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a bowl whip 1 cup of Nutella with 2 eggs.
Add flour and mix till combined.
Spoon into mini muffin tin.
Bake for 10 mins.
Cool and frost with remaining Nutella.
Optional: Sprinkle tops with crushed pretzels and sea salt

Submitter: 
Laura Conroy
Description: 
Tasty treat using the ever popular Nutella!
