Title:
Tomato Avocado Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3637962.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 avocado - peeled, pitted, and mashed
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 slice ciabatta bread
3 slices tomato
1 pinch ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix mashed avocado and garlic salt together in a bowl; spread onto ciabatta bread. Layer tomatoes over avocado and top with black pepper.
