Title:
Kelly's Walnut Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460092.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon butter
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups walnut halves, chopped coarsely or pulsed 3 seconds in food processor

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Beat butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla until blended.  Add all remaining ingredients EXCEPT walnuts, blend until even.  Finally, add walnuts until mixed.  Drop teaspoon sized amounts of batter on a greased baking sheet.  Bake 8-10 minutes, until edges and bottom of the cookies are golden brown.  Makes 2 dozen.

Submitter: 
Bellwether Nesingwary
Description: 
Warm, doughy cookies provide the perfect backdrop for freshly chopped walnuts.  The smell of buttery, brown sugary smell mixed with walnuts while baking will bring back memories.
