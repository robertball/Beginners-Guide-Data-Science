Title:
Saucy Shrimp Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp mayonnaise
1 1/2 tsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 tsp horseradish
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 1/2 tsp chopped fresh parsley
1 1/2 cups cooked black tiger shrimp, cut into chunks

Directions:
In a bowl, stir together mayonnaise, lemon juice, horseradish, garlic powder, parsley and pepper. Add pieces of shrimp and gently fold to coat.
Place mixture evenly in 4 to 6 martini glasses. Chill until ready to serve, ideally for about an  hour to let flavours blend. When serving, garnish each glass with one large, tail on shrimp and a wedge of lemon.
