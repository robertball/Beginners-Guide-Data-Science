Title:
Creamy Pear Lime Gelatin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (29 ounces)pears in juice
1 package (3ounce) lime gelatin
3 ounces cream cheese
1 cup whipped topping

Directions:
Drain pears, reserving juice.  Set pears aside.  Measure juice.  Add water to equal 1 1/2 cups.  Pour into a saucepan.  Bring to a boil.  Stir in gelatin until it is dissolved.  Gradually add cream cheese.  Whisk until smooth.  Refrigerate until cool.  Mash the pears and fold into gelatin mixture.  Fold in whipped topping.  Pour into a serving bowl.  Refrigerate until set.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
This light refreshing salad is packed with pears and full of flavor.
