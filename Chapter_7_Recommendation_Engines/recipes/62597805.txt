Title:
Strawberries and Cream Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz cool whip
1 12 oz can sweetened condensed milk
3 pints fresh strawberries
1 8oz package cream cheese
1 angel food cake

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, sweetened condensed milk, and cool whip (until smooth).
Slice strawberries.
Layer angel food cake, strawberries and cream mixture in trifle fashion.
Chill and serve.
