Title:
Lemon Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs chuck roast, or bottom round roast
1 cup dry red wine
1/2 cup lemon juice
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 cup	onion, chopped
1/2 tsp	salt
1 tsp. dried celery flakes
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1/4 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp ground ginger

Directions:
Brown meat well on all sides in about 1 Tbsp. of olive oil in a large Dutch oven.  Add 1 cup of wine (OR 1/2 cup water and 1/2 cup dry red wine) and simmer, covered, for 1 hour.
Meanwhile, blend remaining ingredients in clean jar with a tight lid by shaking all ingredients together.  Add to beef.  Simmer another 1-1/2 hours, basting occasionally and turning roast once or twice.
Remove meat to platter.  Mix together 2 heaping tablespoons flour with a little water in a jar.  Add to liquid in pot and simmer to make a gravy.  Carve roast into slices, and add 1 thinly sliced lemon to all as garnish.

Submitter: 
LINDA W.
Description: 
This is a family favorite, and reminds me of winter afternoons when I was young.  The scent of the beef and lemon wafting throughout the house is just wonderful!
