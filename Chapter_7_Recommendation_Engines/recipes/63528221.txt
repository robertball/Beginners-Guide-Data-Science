Title:
Lean and Green Green Bean Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups green beans or 202.5 grams, cooked from frozen or canned (3 Greens)
1/8 tsp garlic salt (1/2 condiment)
1/4 tsp basil (1/4 condiment)
A little over 3/4 cup or 3.35 oz 2% part skim mozzarella cheese, (5/6 lean)
1/4 cup 1% cottage cheese (1/6 lean)
2 tbsp Walden Farms Marinara Sauce (1 condiment)
2 tsp Parmesan cheese, grated (2/3 condiment)

Directions:
Prepare green beans and place in a baking dish. Mix the garlic salt and basil with the cottage cheese. Spread over green beans. Spread marinara sauce over the cottage cheese mixture. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes and then broil until cheese is lightly browned. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
