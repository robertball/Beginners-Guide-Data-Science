Title:
Light and Easy Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978264.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup water
1 pound chicken, cubed
2 teaspoons Morton® Nature's Seasons® Seasoning Blend
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 (16 ounce) package frozen Chinese-style vegetables
1 teaspoon soy sauce
1/8 teaspoon ground ginger
6 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Combine water, Morton(R) Nature's Seasons(R) Seasoning Blend, soy sauce and ginger. Add chicken and allow to marinate 15 minutes.
Heat oil in skillet. Add chicken and marinade to skillet; cook, stirring constantly, for 5 minutes. Add vegetables and continue cooking 5 to 8 minutes. Serve over cooked rice.

Submitter: 
Morton Salt
Description: 
Chicken cubes are marinated briefly before stir-frying with ready-cut Chinese style vegetables to make a quick and satisfying meal.
