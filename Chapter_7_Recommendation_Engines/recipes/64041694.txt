Title:
Jalapeño cheddar burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 pounds ground beef
Precooked bacon, to taste, optional
1/2 cup fresh jalapeno pepper, chopped
1 1/2 cups mild cheddar cheese, larger shredded variety
1/2 cup red onion
1 teaspoon seasoning salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Grill. Serve with guacamole on side.

Submitter: 
LINDA
Description: 
This is our favorite recipe for company now. They are fantastic. If we need to make it less spicy we leave out the seeds from the jalapeños. Serve with guacamole on the side, grilled onions along with expected burger sides. Everyone will love them.
