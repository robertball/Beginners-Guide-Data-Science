Title:
Cranberry Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 16 oz container cool whip or frozen whip topping- thawed so soft
2. 1 can 14 oz whole berry cranberry sauce
3. 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
4. 2 cups miniature marshmallows ( white or colored)

Directions:
in large bowl  add 1. 2. 3.  and stir together till just blended then add 4.  stir to cover marshmallows...cover bowl and chill in fridge
best if made the day before
might add some nutmeg or cinnamon or vanilla or orange zest
