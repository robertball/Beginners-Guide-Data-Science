Title:
Ambrosia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 to 1 cup orange juice
2 to 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
2 red apples
2 oranges
1/2 cup pecans
1 cup coconut

Directions:
Mix juice and sugar.
Core and slice apples, cover in sweet juice.
Peel and section oranges.
Add to apples.
Gently toss in coconut and pecans.
Marinate in glass dish overnight in fridge while remembering to stir occasionally to ensure all fruit remains moist.
