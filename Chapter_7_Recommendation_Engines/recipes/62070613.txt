Title:
Berry and Cream Cheese Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rasberries
1/2 cup blueberries
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup cream cheese
1/2 cup white sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 tbsp. chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
In blender add rasberrys, blueberries, and milk. Blend until berries are chopped. Then add cream cheese, white sugar, vanilla and chocolate chips. Blend until cream cheese is completely stirred in.
Serve with whipped cream.
