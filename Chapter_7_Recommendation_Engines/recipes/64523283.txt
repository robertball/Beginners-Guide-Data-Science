Title:
G. Flavor Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
- 6 Tbsp. Sweet Paprika
- 2.5 Tbsp. Finely Ground Sea Salt
- 2.5 Tbsp. Season Salt
- 2.5 Tbsp. Fresh Ground Black Pepper
- 4 Tbsp. Garlic Powder
- 4 Tbsp. Onion Powder
- 2 Tbsp. Cajun Spice
- 2 Tbsp. Sweet Basil
- 2 Tbsp. Oregano

Directions:
Mix dried spices in sealable container and shake it like a Polaroid picture.
