Title:
Pimento Cheese Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup pimento cheese (regular or spicy)
1 small egg white, beaten stiff
30 mini pastry shells

Directions:
preheat oven to 375
beat egg white until stiff. fold in cheese. spoon into shells. bake 10-12 min, until puffed & lightly browned
note: add 1/3 tp 1/2 cup imitation crab meat
