Title:
Dad's Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 can whole kernel corn
1 can cream style corn
1 1/2 cup milk (or half and half for an extra creamy sauce)
one bag of tater tots
Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Brown ground beef, drain thoroughly.
In large casserole dish, combine soup and milk, whisking until creamy.  Stir in corns and ground beef.
Spread tater tots in single layer over top of meat/corn mixture.
Bake for 30 minutes or until tater tots are golden brown.
Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Jen K
Description: 
This is the only meal my kitchen-challenged father could make when he kept us overnight.  I make it for my kids, and now my friends are making it for their families.  It's yummy comfort food in our home.
