Title:
Celery Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch celery
1 onion
5-7 garlic cloves
2 T butter
2 T olive oil
1/2 to 1 apple
2 c chicken stock
handful parsley
sprinkle herbs de provence
lemon/citrus
salt
pepper

Directions:
saute onion, celery, garlic, apple in butter and olive oil
add stock
cook until very soft
blend all and season to taste
