Title:
Chunky Gazpacho and Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium tomatoes(1 lb)
2 celery stalks
2 green onions
1 large cucumber
1 large yellow pepper
1 large green pepper
2 tbsp. red wine vinegar
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. hot pepper sauce
1 (15 1/4 to 19 oz.) can red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 (16 oz.) can great northern beans, rinsed and drained

Directions:
About 30 minutes before, or early in the day:
Cut tomatoes into wedges. slice celery diagonally into 1/2 inch think pieces. Thinly slice green onions. With vegetable peeler, peel some strips of skin from cucumber. Cut cucumber lengthwise into quarters. Cut quarters crosswise into 1/2 inch pieces. Cut peppers into 2 inch pieces.
In large bowl with wire whisk or fork, mix tomato juice, oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, and hot pepper sauce. Add tomatoes, celery, green onions, cucumbers, peppers, and beans. Toss gently. Cover and refrigerate if not serving right away.
