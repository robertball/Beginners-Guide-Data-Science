Title:
Banana Split Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2cups graham cracker crumbs
1 1/2 cup butter softened
2 cups 10x sugar
2 eggs
1 small can crushed pineapple drained well.
sliced bananas
strawberries
chocolate shavings
crushed peanuts for topping
any other seasonal fruits or nuts
12 oz. cool whip
marashino cherries for garnish

Directions:
CRUST:  crush graham crackers and mix with 1/2 cup melted butter.  Pat into the bottom of a 13x9x2 pan.
FILLING:  beat 1 cup butter softened with the 10x sugar and 2 eggs with electric mixer for at least 15 minutes.  Spread over crust.
Top filling with the drained pineapple, sliced bananas, strawberries.
Spread entire dessert with cool whip and decorate with cherries, nuts, and chocolate shavings.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.  This also looks very pretty in a trifle bowl.
