Title:
chicken enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5844132.jpg
Ingredients:
INGREDIENTS:
* 4-5 soft flour tortillas
* 1 1/4 cups Monterey Jack shredded cheese,
Poaching:
* 2 cups chicken broth
* 2 pieces boneless skinless chicken breast (each breast cut into 4 equal pieces)
* 1 bay leaf, fresh or dried
* 1 sprig fresh oregano
* 1/2 small onion, cut into 4 equal parts
Filling:
* 1/2 can (2 Tbsp) green chilies
* 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
* pinch salt
Sauce:
* 1 cup tomato sauce
* 1 T Taco seasoning
* 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon,
* 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
* 1 tablespoons tomato paste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F.
Poaching Chicken:
Bring 2 cups broth to a boil in a medium pot. Immediately add chicken, bay leaf, oregano and onion. Return to a boil, cover and reduce heat to simmer. Poach chicken in broth 10 minutes. Remove the bay leaf and Reserve ¼ c. of the poaching liquid for future use.
Shredding Chicken:
Remove chicken breasts to a blue cutting board and shred with 2 forks. Place in bowl and add 1/4 cup of cooking liquid, tomato paste, chilies, cumin and salt and work through the chicken until combined.
Sauce:
Combine 1 c. tomato sauce, taco seasoning, cinnamon and chili powder in a small saucepan and heat on low heat, keeping warm until needed.
Filling:
Spread ¼ c of sauce on bottom of 8x8 baking dish.
Pile chicken mixture into flour tortillas and roll. Place 4 or 5 tortillas (depending on the number in your group) flat on blue cutting board. Divide chicken mixture evenly among each tortilla and roll tightly. Line casserole or baking dish with enchiladas, seam side down.
Topping & Baking:
Pour remaining tomato sauce over the chicken enchiladas and top with cheese. Bake enchiladas in oven for 5 minutes to melt cheese and set enchiladas. Serve.
