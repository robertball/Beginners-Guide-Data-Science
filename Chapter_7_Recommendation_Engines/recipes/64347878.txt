Title:
Chocolate Streusel Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups unsifted flour
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
1 pkg cream cheese, softened (8 oz)
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 egg
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 cup cold butter
2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar and cocoa. Cut in butter until crumbly (mixture will be dry). Reserving 2 cups crumb mixture, press remainder firmly on bottom of 13x9 baking pan.
Bake 15 min.
In large mixer bowl, beat cheese until fluffy.
Gradually beat in sweetened condensed milk until smooth.
Add egg and vanilla; mix well.
Pour over prepared crust.
Combine nuts with reserved crumb miture; sprinkle over cheese mixture.
Bake 25 min or until bubbly.
Cool
Chill.
Cut into bars.
Store covered in refrigerator.

Description: 
VERY rich dessert. Delicious, but a little bit goes a long way because it is so rich!
