Title:
Easy Swedish Meatball Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c beef stock or 1 t beef bouillon dissolved in 1 c hot water
1 c heavy cream
3 T flour
1 T soy sauce
1 t black pepper
1/2 t rosemary

Directions:
In saucepan mix together all ingredients and whisk until smooth. Heat on low, stir occasionally.
Add defrosted meatballs to sauce, and continue heating for several minutes.
Serve over egg noodles or rice.

Submitter: 
Mary B
Description: 
A quick but very tasty sauce to add to already prepared frozen meatballs.

This recipe makes a generous amount of sauce for a 1/2 package (20 oz) of meatballs.
