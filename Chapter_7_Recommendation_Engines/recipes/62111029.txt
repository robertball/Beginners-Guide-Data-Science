Title:
Mexican Corn and Green Chilis Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can of fire roasted green chilis
1/2 pkg frozen white corn
1/2 C sour cream
salt and pepper
warm corn tortillas

Directions:
Slice chilis into thin strips
Simmer all ingredients in medium pan for 5 min.
Serve with warm corn tortillas

Submitter: 
jalexandropoulos
Description: 
This dish is served as a main course in Mexico.  Green chilis and white corn are simmered in a sour cream sauce and served with warm corn tortillas. So simple and delicious!
