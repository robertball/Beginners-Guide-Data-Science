Title:
Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cup sifted flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup shortening
3/4 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 1/4 cup quick oats
1/2 cup nuts
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven at 375
Sift flour, soda, salt
Cream shortening, sugars until smooth
Beat in eggs and vanilla
Stir in flour mixture and oats
Fold in nuts and raisins
Drop onto greased cookie sheets
Bake 10 minutes
