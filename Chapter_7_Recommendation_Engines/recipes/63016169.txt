Title:
Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (20 oz) pkgs. refrigerated chocolate chip
cookie dough, sliced 1/4 inch thick (freeze rolls of dough until firm to help with slicing)
2 (8 oz) pkgs. cream cheese, softened
1 egg
1/4 c. sugar
1/2 t. vanilla

Directions:
Cover bottom of 9 x 13 inch baking pan with 1 roll of sliced dough to form crust.
Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes; remove from oven.
Mix cream cheese, egg, sugar and vanilla
on low speed until well combined.
Spread over crust; top with remaining roll of sliced
dough.
Bake 25-30 minutes or until golden brown;
Cool and then refrigerate until time to serve.

Submitter: 
Daisy K
Description: 
This recipe came from a Reader's Digest magazine years ago... a pullout type ad section of Christmas recipes.  I took them to a Christmas work party one year and won 1st place in the dessert contest!  So good!
