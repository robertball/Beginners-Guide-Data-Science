Title:
Leftover Turkey Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. sweet pickles
3 eggs, hard boiled and peeled
2 c. leftover turkey meat, roughly chopped
2 T. Dijon mustard
3/4 c. Mayonnaise
1 T. sasoning salt
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine the pickles, eggs and turkey meat in a food processor and pulse until you get your desired consistency.  Pour mixture into a large bowl and add the rest of the ingredients.  Mix until well combined.  Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
This is really good to use up all of those leftovers.  There's only so much sliced turkey one can eat.  Try this variation, you won't be sorry!
