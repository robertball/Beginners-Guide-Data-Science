Title:
Italian Sausage and Zucchini Ribbons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp. cooking oil
1 lb. Hot Italian sausage
½ cup chopped onion
4 garlic cloves, minced
28 oz. can diced tomatoes (with juice)
1 cup diced red pepper
2 tsp. dried basil
2 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. dried thyme
1 Tbsp. cooking oil
2 zucchinis, peeled into 4-inch long ribbons with veg. peeler
½ cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook sausage in frying pan until brown. Add onion and garlic. Heat and cook for 2 minutes.
Add next 5 ingredients. Stir and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to med-low and simmer (uncovered) for 15 minutes until soft.
Cook zucchini until softened and add to sausage mixture.
Sprinkle with cheese.
