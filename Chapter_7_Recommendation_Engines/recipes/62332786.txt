Title:
Shoe Peg Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/481181.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans white shoe-peg corn
2 cans small English peas
1 bunch of green onions, finely chopped
2 stalks of celery, finely chopped
1/2 of a bell pepper, finely chopped
1 small jar of diced pimentos
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup white vinegar
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Drain corn, peas, and pimentos and pour into a large bowl.  Add green onions, bell pepper, and celery. Mix together.  In a small boiler bring sugar and vinegar to a boil and stir until sugar is melted.  Pour the mixture over the vegetables.  Pour the oil over the vegetables and stir.  Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least two hours.  Best if refrigerated overnight.

Description: 
A refreshingly sweet and tangy corn salad that goes great with a spicy main dish. A simple pour, pour, chop, and stir recipe that can be made the night before or two nights before you serve it.
