Title:
Ellie's Dijon Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Salmon filets (1 per person - easy to just add more if needed)
Dijon mustard
Mayonnaise (optional)
Salt & Pepper (to taste)
Lemon wedges or juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.  Spray rimmed baking sheet or glass dish with nonstick spray.
Place salmon on tray, skin side down.  Brush top and sides of filets with a thin layer of dijon mustard (and a little mayonnaise, if desired).  Sprinkle with a little salt and fresh black pepper
Bake for approximately 15 minutes, or until JUST cooked and flaky.  Don't overcook!
Serve with lemon wedges or lemon juice.  Goes great with a side of roasted broccoli.
