Title:
Slow Cooker Kielbasa and Beer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds kielbasa sausage, cut into 1 inch pieces
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
1 (20 ounce) can sauerkraut, drained
1 onion
1 sweet apple

Directions:
in a frying pan brown the sausage, diced onion and diced apple, til the sausage is lightly browned. Transfer to a slow cooker, add the beer and sauerkraut.  Cook on low for 5 to 6 hours, until the meat is tender and plump.
