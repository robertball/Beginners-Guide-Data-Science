Title:
Chicken loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large skinless, boneless chicken breasts
3 white buns
1 onion
1 garlic clove
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 eggs
1 tsp. worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 cup chili sauce
1 TB yellow mustard
2 TB brown sugar
1 tsp. worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Put chicken in a meat grinder along with onion and garlic
Add chicken broth, salt and pepper, worcestershire sauce and eggs and mix thoroughly
Place meat mixture in a 9x5 loaf pan
For sauce, mix chili sauce, mustard, brown sugar and worcestershire sauce in a small bowl
Spread on top of chicken
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Bake for approximately 60 minutes or until thermometer inserted reached 170 degrees

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This is a great way to use some boneless, skinless chicken breasts.  I use whatever buns or bread I have laying around. I used wheat potato buns.  We like this meatloaf out of chicken better than made out of beef.  Great served with mashed potatoes and corn.
