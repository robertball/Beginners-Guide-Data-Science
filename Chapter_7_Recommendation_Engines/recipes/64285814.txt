Title:
Borracho (Drunken) Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound dried pinto beans, washed
1 pound chopped bacon
1 onion, diced
6 cloves garlic, chopped
1 1/2 tablespoons dried oregano
1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
2 (14.5 ounce) cans fire roasted diced tomatoes
3 bay leaves
1/4 cups dried cilantro
1 packet dried Sazon
1 tsp baking soda
chicken stock

Directions:
Soak beans in a large pot of water overnight.
Fry bacon, drain and saute onions, garlic and oregano in a bit of the grease.
Drain beans, and placed all ingredients in crock pot, adding just enough chicken stock to cover beans.
Cook on low 8 hours, turn up on high for 2 more hours if beans are still hard. Stir occasionally.
