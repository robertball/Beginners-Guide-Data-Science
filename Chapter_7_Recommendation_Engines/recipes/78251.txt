Title:
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies Treats® Turkeys Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980983.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal or Kellogg's® Cocoa Krispies™ cereal
Prepared chocolate and orange frosting
Candy corn

Directions:
Trace your handprint onto a piece of sturdy paper or cardboard and cut out handprint pattern. Set aside.
In a large microwave safe bowl, heat margarine and marshmallows at HIGH for 3 minutes, stirring after 2 minutes. Stir until smooth.
Add Kellogg's(R) Rice Krispies(R) cereal. Stir until well coated.
Using spatula coated with cooking spray or waxed paper, press mixture into 15 1/2 x 10 1/2 x 1-inch pan coated with cooking spray. Cool slightly.
Using the handprint pattern, cut around the pattern with knife. Decorate with frosting and candy corn as desired. Best if served same day.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
Description: 
This is a fun project for the holidays with children. Make turkey shaped treats, and decorate them with frosting and little candy corn beaks.
