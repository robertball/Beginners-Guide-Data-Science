Title:
Fruit Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886749.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can orange juice, thawed & undiluted
6 medium bananas, mashed
2 cups sugar
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple, crushed
1 cup mandarin oranges, mashed
40 ounces of ginger ale or 7-Up
1 (20 ounce) package strawberries, mashed
25 (9 ounce) cups

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir orange juice and sugar together until sugar has dissolved.
Gentle stir in fruit until well coated.
Add in ginger ale/7-Up.
Fill cups about two-thirds full of mixture and freeze.
Remove from freezer about 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Great for a children's party or a treat on a hot summer day.
