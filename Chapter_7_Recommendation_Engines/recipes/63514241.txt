Title:
Mama D's Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons oil
3 tablespoons butter
3 large onions chopped
Bundle of celery chopped
5 lbs of potatoes peeled and chopped
1 tablespoon minced garlic
Salt and pepper to taste
12 cups  milk
4 cups heavy cream
Toppings:
Bacon
Green onion
Shredded cheese

Directions:
Heat oil and butter in a large pot on med/low. Add garlic, celery, onion, potatoes, salt and pepper and brown until potatoes are soft and onions are clear. Add 4 cups of milk and 4 cups of heavy cream. After it cooks down add the other 4 cups of milk. Cook for 1 hour stirring often. Add 4 more cups of milk and cook until heated on low. When serving top bowls with bacon, cheese, and green onions
