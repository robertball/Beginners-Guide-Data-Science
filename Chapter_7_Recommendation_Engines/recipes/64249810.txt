Title:
Bacon Wrapped Scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Large Scallops
1 package Bacon
1/4 cup  Lemon Juice, freshly squeezed
1/8 cup Olive Oil.
1 tsp Lemon Pepper

Directions:
Rinse and prepare scallops.  If large then cut in half with the grain.
Slowly whisk olive oil into lemon juice till slightly thickened,  whisk in lemon pepper.    Add shrimp and marinate 4 hrs.
Cut each bacon strip in half, diagonally.  Wrap each scallop (or scallop half) in bacon strip half.  Secure with tooth pick.
Broil in middle of oven till bacon begins to crisp.   Turning half way through.  10 min.

Submitter: 
Randy Warner
Description: 
Simple Lemon and Olive Oil Marinade make these delightful.
