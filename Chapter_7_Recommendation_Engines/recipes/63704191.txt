Title:
Lasagna Biscuit Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030779.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb Italian Sausage
1/2 jar spaghetti sauce
3/4 cup cottage cheese
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp basil
1 egg
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 can regular flaky biscuits

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Cook meat in skillet.
Drain and stir in spaghetti sauce.
In small bowl, mix cottage cheese, garlic, basil, and egg.
Separate dough into 10 biscuits.
Place each biscuit in a regular-sized muffin cup. Firmly press dough in bottom and side of each cup.
Layer 1 tbsp of meat sauce in bottom of each biscuit cup.
Top with 1 tablespoon of cottage cheese mixture.
Top with mozzarella.
Repeat layers.
Bake 17-20 minutes.
Serve with leftover meat sauce.
