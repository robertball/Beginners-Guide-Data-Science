Title:
Ma's Chicken Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/499648.jpg
Ingredients:
49 oz chicken broth
1 box Uncle Ben's long grain & country wild rice
1 jar (12 oz.) chicken gravy
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 stalk celery
1/2-1 cup shredded carrots (if desired)
1/2 medium sized red onion
1/2-1 box frozen chopped spinach
2-3 chicken breasts
1 tsp. poultry seasoning

Directions:
Either pan fry chicken and cut up into bite size pieces or boil the chicken and shred it.
Dice onion & celery
Saute onion & celery, (and carrots)
Pour broth, rice & seasoning packet, gravy, soup, onion/celery mixture, chicken, and spinach into crock pot.
Heat through, stirring occasionally
The soup is done when the rice is fully cooked
