Title:
Spicy Pumpkin Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/285449.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2-1 teaspoon red pepper flake
1 teaspoon garlic
1/2 large onion, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 (15 oz) can kidney beans, drained
1 (15 oz) can black beans, drained
1 (15 oz) can Great White Northern beans, drained
2 (4 oz) cans tomato sauce
1 (4 oz) can tomato sauce seasoned with garlic and onion
2 (15 oz) cans petite diced tomatoes
1 (15 oz) can fire roasted tomatoes
1 (15 oz) can pumpkin puree
2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon cumin
1-2 teaspoons salt

Directions:
Begin to brown the ground beef in a large pan over medium heat.
Add onion, red pepper flake and garlic to beef as it browns.
After beef is browned add diced green and red pepper.
Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
While ground beef is cooking, mix together all other ingredients in crock pot.
Add ground beef mixture to crock pot.
Cook on low until for 1-2 hours or until desired temperature is reached.
