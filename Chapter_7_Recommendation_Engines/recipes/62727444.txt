Title:
Spinach & Berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/622933.jpg
Ingredients:
8-10 blackberries
1/2 cup blueberries
1 cup baby spinach leaves, loosely packed
8 ice cubes
2 tablespoons light sour cream
2 tablespoons sugar, or more to taste
1 cup juice, I recommend a light berry kind (I used a acai berry juice)

Directions:
Add all ingredients to blender; puree until smooth.  Serve immediately or refridgerate for later.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
I came up with this smoothie when I was trying to incorporate more spinach into my diet because it so healthy for you.  With the berries you can hardly taste the spinach.  Feel free to sub other berries.
