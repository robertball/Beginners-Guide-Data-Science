Title:
Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/602013.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Potatoes,Cubed
1 Medium Yellow Onion,Wedged (think apple slices)
1/2 small bag Baby Carrots
2 cloves minced Garlic
3-4 lb. Beef Roast
1 Tbsp Olive Oil (Optional)
1 Can Beef Broth
1 Packet of Slow Cooker Pot Roast Seasoning
1 tsp Italian Seasoning
1 Tbsp Worcestershire (aprox 5 bottle shakes)

Directions:
Put Potatoes, Onion and Carrots in Bottom of Slow Cooker. Stir to mix all three together. Heat Olive Oil in Pan on High Heat. Sear Roast on all sides then Place Roast in Slow Cooker on top of Vegetables. Mix Beef Broth and Seasoning Packet and Pour over Roast in Slow Cooker. Sprinkle Minced Garlic and Italian Seasoning on top of that. Finish with a few shakes of Worcestershire. Close Lid and DONT OPEN for 8-10 hours.
