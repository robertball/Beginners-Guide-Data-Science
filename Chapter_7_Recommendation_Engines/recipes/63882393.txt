Title:
Brownies From Scratch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
2 1-oz cakes of unsweetened chocolate
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/3 cup sifted all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F (175C)
Put water in bottom of double boiler and heat.
Melt butter or margarine and chocolate in top of double boiler. Remove pan from hot water.
Add sifted dry ingredients, eggs and vanilla; blend together thoroughly and fold in walnuts.
Spread in a lightly greased 8" square pan. Bake about 25 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cut while warm.
