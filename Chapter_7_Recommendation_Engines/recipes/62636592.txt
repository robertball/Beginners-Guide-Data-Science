Title:
Air Fried Buffalo Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Skinless/Boneless Chicken Breast
Wash
1/2 Cup Fat Free Plain Greek Yogurt
1/4 Cup Egg Substitute (or egg white)
1 Tbs + 1 Tsp Franks Hot Sauce
Breading
1 Cup Panko
1 Tbs Sweet Paprika
1 Tbs Garlic Pepper
1 Tbs Cayenne

Directions:
1- Cut chicken into 1 inch wide by 1/2 in thick strips
2- Blend dry ingredients in large bowl and set aside
3- Blend yogurt, egg substitute and Franks in a large bowl
4- Coat strips thoroughly in wash and bread in panko mixture (ensuring to reblend the seasonings regularly as the spices tend to sink to the bottom)
5- set in single layers in the *Wave oven and "fry" for 16 minutes +/- turning over halfway through the cooking cycle.
Serve with fat free ranch or blue cheese and more Franks for dipping!

Submitter: 
Nekmor
Description: 
By elimating the butter and frying in a NuWave or SuperWave oven we get a recipe the family loves and is practically fat free!
