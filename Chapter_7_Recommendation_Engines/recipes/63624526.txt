Title:
Robert’s Killer Kraut – 30 MM, LC, LF, GF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1008145.jpg
Ingredients:
7 beef franks (1 package) 97% fat free (Hebrew National)
32 oz sauerkraut (1 bag), Krrrrisp Kraut (Silver Floss)
1 ½ c frozen diced onions & peppers (Quirch Foods)
1 c matchstick carrots
¾ c Splenda
½ c apple cider vinegar
1 tsp celery seed
1 tsp caraway seed
1 tsp crushed red peppers

Directions:
Boil franks for 7 minutes
In a large bowl, mix all other ingredients thoroughly
Add franks to kraut mix and stir in to cover
Let marinade for 20 minutes or more

Submitter: 
Robert
Description: 
Kraut with a killer kick. This kraut is great as a cold salad or as a heated meal.1 serving = 74 calories. 30 minute meal, low carb., low fat, gluten free.
