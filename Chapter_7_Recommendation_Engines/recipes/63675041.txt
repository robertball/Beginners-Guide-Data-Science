Title:
Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small box of Angel Hair Pasta
Celery Seasoning
Italian Seasoning
Garlic Powder
1 diced Onion
1 diced Green Pepper
1 lb of Hamburger
Prego Sauce
Tomato Sauce
Tomato Paste
Tomato Juice from a can of whole Tomato's
Salt
Pepper
1 tbsp. of Butter

Directions:
In a frying pan on medium heat put a tbs of butter and melt it, then combine onion, green Pepper, celery Seasoning, Italian seasoning, and garlic Powder and fry it until the onions and green pepper are soft and browned slightly. In a separate frying pan on medium heat put in hamburger and break it up into medium to small pieces and cover and cook until browned. Drain the grease and add to onion and green pepper mixture. On medium heat add tomato sauce and paste. Squeeze and add tomato juice from the can of whole tomato's, Prego sauce and a dash of salt and pepper. Cook pasta and leave separate from sauce until you eat!
