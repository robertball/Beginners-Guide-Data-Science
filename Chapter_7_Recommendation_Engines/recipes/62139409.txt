Title:
Party Parmesan Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Angel Hair Pasta
1/2 c. chopped red onion
1/2 c. butter (I use the NOTSO butter)
1 small can chopped black olives
4 tbsp chopped garlic
1 c. grated parmesan
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta by pkg directions. Drain.
Add butter and garlic to HOT pasta and mix very well.
Add parmesan, onions and black olives and mix well.
You can also add shredded chicken if you would like to add a meat.
Serve hot or cold.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
I was dabbling one day with what I had in the pantry and fridge. This lite pasta dish you can do hot or cold! Great for Potlucks or office parties!
