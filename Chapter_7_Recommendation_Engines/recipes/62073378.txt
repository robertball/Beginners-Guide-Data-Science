Title:
potato pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 x 9inch pie crust
4 medium ptoatoes, skinned and diced
2 medium onions, diced
1 cup cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. boil potatoes and onion in separate pans each until potatoes are soft and onion is transparent.
2. grate cheese.
3. layer 1 third of cheese in bottom of pie crust, followed by potatoes, another third of cheese then onion.
4. finally, top with remaining cheese and cook in a pre heated oven at 175oC for roughly half an hour or until pie starts to brown
