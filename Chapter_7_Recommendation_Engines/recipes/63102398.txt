Title:
Greek Chicken with Yogurt Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/786868.jpg
Ingredients:
Sauce:
1 Medium Cucumber, pelled, finely chopped
1/4 cup greek plain yogurt fat free
1/4 cup sour cup fat free
2 Teaspoons fresh chopped parsley
2 Teaspoons fresh lemon juice or lime (I like Lime)
1/4 Teaspoon Ground Cumin
Chicken:
4 Thin boneless skinless chicken breast
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons olive oil
2 tablespoons water
Fresh Parsley for garnish

Directions:
1. In medium bowl, mix all sauce ingredients.  Cover, refrigerrate until serving time.
2. Sprinkle and then rub both side of the chicken with the salt and cinnamon.  In a skillet heat olive oil over med-high heat.  Cook chicken in oil browning both sides for 10-12 minutes.  Reduce heat to low and add water.  Cover ook for another 2-3 minutes longer or until juices of the chicken run clear.
3. Serve chicken with yogurt sauce over top and garnish with parsley.  YUMMY!!!!

Submitter:

Description: 
Ok Before you say oh no I will not like this... I am telling you that it is wonderful!!!  I have not had a chicken dish this good in a long time.  You have to use the cinnamon and the yogurt sauce together or it will not work.  I have 2 of the pickiest eaters in my house and they both loved it.
