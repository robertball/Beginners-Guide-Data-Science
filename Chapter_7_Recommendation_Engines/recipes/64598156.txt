Title:
Twice Baked Potatoes for the Freezer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3898680.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lb. bag russet potatoes
1/4 cup butter
1/3 cup milk
1/4 teaspoon salt
Pinch black pepper
Fresh chives (to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Wash and scrub potatoes
Place potatoes on center rack and bake for approximately 1 hour or until center is soft.
Remove and cool to the touch.  (About 15 minutes)
Cut potatoes in half and remove potato from the skin leaving the skin intact.
Place potato in a large bowl and using an electric mixer, beat until fluffy.
Add butter, salt, pepper, and milk and beat until smooth.
Place potato mixture into a pastry bag fitted with a large opening tip, and pipe into potato skins.
Sprinkle with fresh chives
Place in freezer bags and freeze until ready to use.
To serve, simply thaw and bake at 350 until hot.  (Approx. 20 -30 min.) or thaw and microwave on high. (Approx. 3 minutes)

Submitter: 
SHORECOOK
Description: 
One would think you spent all day in the kitchen when you serve these potatoes as a side dish.  Feel free to season the potato filling to your liking.
