Title:
Fire Roasted Corn Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ears of Corn
I medium tomato chopped
1/2 c hot tomato sauce, like el pato
1/4 c prepared medium picante sauce, like,Pace
3 Tbs chopped cilantro
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Remove the husks from corn, heat grill and place over high heat. Turning occasionally until charred.
Cool.
Cut corn off of cob and place into a medium bowl. Add cilantro and mix well.
Add remaining ingredients. Mix well.
Transfer to serving bowl, chill before serving.
