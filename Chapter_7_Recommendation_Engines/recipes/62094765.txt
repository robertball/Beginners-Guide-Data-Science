Title:
Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup olive oil
1/3 cup Balsamic Vinegar
1/4 cup Low Sodium Soy Sauce
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a jar, fasten lid, and shake, shake, shake! Allow to sit for one hour before use, so that flavors can blend.

Submitter: 
Janna
Description: 
Ever run out of bottled Balsamic Vinaigrette just when you need it most? I did, so I whipped this up from ingredients I had on hand. I'll never go back to the commercial stuff!
