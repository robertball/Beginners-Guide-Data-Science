Title:
Fiber Fiber Whole-Wheat Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded carrot
1 cup shredded apple
1/2 cup diced prunes
1/2 cup raisins
1/4 cup walnuts
1/4 cup pecans
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup applesauce
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon cloves
2 cups whole-wheat flour
2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees and spray muffin tins.
Combine carrot, apple, prunes, raisins, nuts in a large bowl.
Beat in eggs and add vanilla and applesauce.
Mix in sugars.
In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and spices    with a whisk.
Add to carrot mixture until well incorporated.
Fill muffin tins to the top (to create muffin tops).
Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
