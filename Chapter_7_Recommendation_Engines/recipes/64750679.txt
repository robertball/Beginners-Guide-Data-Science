Title:
Instant Pot Pinto Beans (No Soak) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 strips uncooked bacon
4 c. chicken broth
1 1/2 c. water
Onion to taste
1 1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 1/4 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp paprika
1 tsp chili powder
1 lb. dry pinto beans rinsed
2 whole green chili peppers (heat of your choice)

Directions:
Turn Instant Pot on saute. Add bacon when hot and cook for about 4-6 minutes turning once.
Add all other ingredients.
Place lid on and set for high pressure.
Set timer for 45 minutes.
Allow natural pressure release.
Remove cover and remove bacon.
If you want a thicker consistency to the beans just use a potato masher until it's where you want it.
