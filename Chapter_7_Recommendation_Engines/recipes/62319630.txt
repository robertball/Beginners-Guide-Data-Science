Title:
Bananas Foster Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/377532.jpg
Ingredients:
2 heaping teaspoons brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 cup water
1 Banana, cut into 4 pieces
Approximately 1 cup crushed ice
Approximately 1/2 cup skim milk

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine brown sugar, water and cinnamon.
Heat until boiling and stir until brown sugar is completely dissolved then remove from heat.
In Magic Bullet cup, add banana
Fill rest of cup with crushed ice.
Pour sugar/cinnamon mixture over crushed ice.
Add skim milk until cup is about full.
Blend the mixture together until smooth
Enjoy

Submitter: 
Linda
Description: 
A yummy cinnamon-banana smoothie. I use a Magic Bullet blender, which is perfect for making single-serving smoothies.
