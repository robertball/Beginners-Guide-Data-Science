Title:
Blueberry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 cup sour milk (1 cup milk + 1 tsp. vinegar)
2 1/2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups blueberries
Topping:
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp. flour
1/4 cup shortening
1/4 cup oats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Cream together shortening and sugar. Add eggs and sour milk.  Sift in dry ingredients and mix well.  Add vanilla then fold in blueberries.  Pour into greased 13x9 pan.
Mix together topping ingredients with a fork until crumbly.  Sprinkle on top of batter and bake for 40 minutes or until golden brown and toothpick comes our clean.
