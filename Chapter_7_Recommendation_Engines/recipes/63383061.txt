Title:
cream cheese and blue cheese ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of 8oz cream cheese
1 3oz blue cheese
1 small onion
1 pound sharp chedder cheese

Directions:
leave cream cheese out at room temp , to soften up
add cream cheese to a large bowl add chedder cheese
grate onion on small side of grater and add it to the cheese add blue cheese mix well and shape in a ball or ring best if  made 24 hours in advance

Submitter: 
Roberta Pactricia Fischer
Description: 
this is so easy to make and feeds a large crowd and doesnt cost a whole lot of money to make
