Title:
minestrone cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-cups uncooked mini lasagna noodles (mafalda-4ounces)    3-Tablespoon olive oil    1-cup chopped onion    1-cup sliced carrots(2 medium)    2-medium stalks celery sliced(1-cup)    1-medium greenbell pepper,chopped    1-medium zucchini or summer squash,quartered lengthwise and sliced    2-cloves garlic    2 cans red kidney beans,drained and rinsed (15-ounce each)    1-can diced tomatoes with Italian Herbs undrained
(15-ounce (contidina))    1/2-cup finely grated parmesan cheese    1-teaspoon salt    1/4-teaspoon pepper    1/4-cup refrigerated basil pesto

Directions:
1)-Heat oven to 350-degrees.  Cook and drain pasta as directed on package using minimum cooking time.    2)-In 10-inch skillet,over medium high heat oil,add onion,carrots,celery and bell-pepper. Cover and cook 8to10,stirring occassionally until carrots are tender. Uncover,stir in zucchini and garlic. Cook and stir 1 minute longer.    3)-Place pasta in ungreased 2-1/2 quart casserole. Stir in carrot mixture,beans,tomatoes,1/4-cupof the cheese,salt,pepper and pesto. Cover casserole.

Submitter: 
Sue Rose-Zamora
Description: 
pillsbury casserole,very filling and doesn't have any meat.David really liked it, couldn't believe how satisfying it was without meat. Just kidney beans!
