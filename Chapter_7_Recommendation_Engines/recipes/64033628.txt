Title:
Mexican Beef Casserole (Grandma's) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. extra lean ground beef
1 diced onion
1 can (14/15 oz.) diced tomatoes drained
1 can corn drained
1 can black beans rinsed & drained
1 can of cannellini beans
1 pkg.  taco seasoning
12 corn tortillas
1 (8 oz.) container sour cream (might need more?)
Mexican blend shredded cheese
1 medium sized can of sliced black olives
Sliced jarred jalapeno peppers

Directions:
Spray 2 qt. baking dish 8 x12 with baking spray.
Place half tortillas in bottom. Half ground beef mixture on top. Top with enough sour cream to cover the ground beef mixture. Layer last of the tortillas then beef & sour cream. Bake at 350 for 25 min. Remove & add shredded cheese & jalapenos on top.
Cook another 5 minutes until cheese is melted.
Let cool 5 minutes before serving.
