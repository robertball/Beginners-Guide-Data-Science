Title:
Five Star Jerky Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. dark soy sauce
2 T. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. powdered ginger
1/4 tsp. ground Anise
3 lb. lean brisket

Directions:
Trim meat completely of fat and cut across grain into slices 1/8'" thick.
Blend all ingredients except meat in small bowl. Dip each piece of meat into marinade, coating well.
Place in shallow dish. Pour remaining marinade over top, cover and refrigerate overnight.
Place in food dehydrater and dehydrate 8 to 12 hours.
