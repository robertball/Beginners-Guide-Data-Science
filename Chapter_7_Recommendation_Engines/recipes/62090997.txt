Title:
7-Up Cake with Lemon Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb. (3 sticks) butter, softened
3 c. sugar
5 eggs
3 c. flour
2 Tbs. lemon extract
3/4 c. 7-Up, or other lemon-lime flavored soda
2/3 c. powdered sugar
1 Tbs. lemon juice
2 tsp. 7-Up

Directions:
Cream eggs and sugar together for 5 minutes.
Preheat oven to 325Â° F.
Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating after each addition.
Add the flour and lemon extract.
Fold in the 3/4 c. of soda.
Pour batter into well-greased 12-cup Bundt pan.
Bake for 60 to 80 minutes.
When the cake cools, remove the cake from the pan and make the lemon glaze. Mix together the powdered sugar, lemon juice, and 2 tsp. 7-Up. Drizzle the glaze over the cake.

Submitter: 
AdicaArethusa
Description: 
This recipe makes a delicious, sweet, and lemony cake.  The lemon-lime pop adds a nice flavor.
