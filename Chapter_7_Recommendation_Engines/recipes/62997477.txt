Title:
Cooked (Bechemel Type) Frosting - old fashioned Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C milk
3T flour
1 stick margarine
½ C shortening
1 C sugar
1t vanilla

Directions:
Bring flour and milk to boil in a small saucepan whisking constantly until thick and no longer tastes “floury". Cool completely.
Beat margarine, shortening, sugar and vanilla well.  Add cream sauce and beat 5 – 10 minutes until light and fluffy and sugar is no longer grainy.
You can add melted chocolate, drained crushed pineapple, chopped maraschino cherries, grated orange rind etc… as desired to flavor cake or use as filling for cake roll or Gobs.

Submitter: 
Scott Collins
Description: 
Creamy and fluffy, but not too sweet, perfect for filling cake rolls or gobs or to use as a frosting on a plain cake.
