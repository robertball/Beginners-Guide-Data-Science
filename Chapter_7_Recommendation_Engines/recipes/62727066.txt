Title:
Garlic-Parmesan Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 garlic cloves, peeled
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1/3 cup red wine vinegar, or more to taste
1/4 cup Parmesan, grated
pepper, freshly ground

Directions:
Use a garlic press and push the cloves into a small bowl (or finely mince the garlic.)  Sprinkle the salt over the pressed garlic.  Using the back of a wooden spoon press and mash the salt into the garlic.  Add the olive-oil to the garlic salt mixture.  Whisk in the vinegar, Parmesan cheese, and pepper.  Makes 1 1/2 cups.

Submitter: 
kirklandcook
Description: 
This dressing is a delicious and tangy.  The red wine vinegar is a must as it complements the garlic and cheese more than other types of vinegar.
