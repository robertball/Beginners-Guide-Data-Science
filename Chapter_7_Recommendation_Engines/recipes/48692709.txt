Title:
Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans buttermilk biscuits
1/2 cup sugar
2 tablespoons cinnamon
1 stick butter or margarine
Powdered sugar
Milk
additional sugar

Directions:
Spray Bundt pan with Pam
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
Cut biscuits in quarters
Put sugar and cinnamon in bag and mix them
Put some of the biscuit quarters in the bag at a time
Shake the bag.
Put the mixed quarters in the pan.
Repeat until all the quarters are in the pan.
Melt the butter
Add enough sugar to the left over sugar and cinnamon mixture to make 1/2 cup mixture.
Add sugar mixture to the melted butter.
Pour butter sugar mixture over the biscuits in the pan.
Bake in oven for 45 minutes or until golden brown.
Remove from oven and cool about 10 minutes and then remove Monkey Bread from pan and cool.
Mix approx. 1 cup powdered sugar with a few teaspoons milk until you get a desired consistancy.
Pour over Monkey Bread
