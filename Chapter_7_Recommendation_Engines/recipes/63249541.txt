Title:
Grill-Pickle Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium yellow onion cut in 1/2 inch thick slices
5 dill pickles(3 inches long) halved and patted dry with paper towels
olive oil
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup yellow mustard
1/8 tsp black pepper
2 tsp pickle juice
salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat grill to high. Oil grill grates well.Lightly brush olive oil on onion slices and pickle halves. Grill on high until onions are tender and pickles and onions have deep dark char marks. While vegetables are cooking, mix honey and mustard and pepper in a small bowl. During last few minutes of grilling brush vegetables with honey mustard. When vegetables are charred and glazed remove to cutting board. Allow to cool until easy to handle. Mince pickles and onions with a knife or pulse a few times in food proccessor. Stir remaining honey mustard into minced vegetables. Stir in pickle juice and salt to taste.

Submitter: 
hazelnut
Description: 
Grilled pickles and onions glazed with honey and mustard make a delicious relish to gourmet your hot dog. Great on brats and burgers, too.
