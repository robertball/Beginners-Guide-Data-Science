Title:
Steamed asparagus. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 to 10 stalks of asparagus per person
Boiling hot water

Directions:
Place steamer basket into large pot. Fill with water up to but not touching steamer basket. Place pot of water on high heat; Bring to boil. While waiting for water to heat, prepare fresh asparagus: snap off bottoms by bending sharply, remove hard outer cover on stem.  Place as[aragis into steamer basket using tongs. Steam for 3 to 8 minutes - test for your preference of doneness by piercing stem with fork.
