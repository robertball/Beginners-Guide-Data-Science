Title:
Salt free Seasoning Blend Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1025224.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp dried minced onion
1 Tbsp thyme
1 Tbsp Rosemary
1 Tbsp Onion Powder
2 Tbsp Cumin
2 Tbsp Sugar
2 Tbsp granulated garlic
2 Tbsp freshly gound pepper
1 crushed Bay Leaf

Directions:
Mix all ingedients together in glass bowl. Be sure to break up and chunks that appear. Cover tightly and keep by stove for handy all around seasoning blend. You can add salt separately if needed.  Shake or stir before each use.
