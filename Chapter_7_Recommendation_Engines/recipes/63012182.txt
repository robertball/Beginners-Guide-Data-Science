Title:
Pretzel Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 2 large bags of pretzels
2. 12 oz bottle orville redenbacker buttery flavored popcorn oil
3. 1 oz pkh hidden valley ranch dressing ( dry powder in pkg)
4. 1 tsp lemon pepper

Directions:
Combine 3. and 4.
in very large container combine 1. 2. and toss with 3.4. - DO HALF PKG OILS at a TIME toss and let absorb add more pretzels oil toss and stir every 15 min till look dry...store in airtight containers
