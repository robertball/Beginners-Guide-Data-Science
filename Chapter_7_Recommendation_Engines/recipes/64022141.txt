Title:
Roasted Garlic Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head broccoli, rinsed, dried, and chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
tbsp olive oil
Salt and pepper (a few shakes)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Put all ingredients in a ziploc bag.  Shake to coat.
Spread broccoli out on a foil-covered baking sheet.  Bake for 30 minutes on top rack.
