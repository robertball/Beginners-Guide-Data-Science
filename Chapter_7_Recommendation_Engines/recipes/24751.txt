Title:
Spicy Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1367763.jpg
Ingredients:
5 ounces Pepper Jack cheese, shredded
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a 2 quart casserole dish combine cheese, spinach and milk.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Jaymie Tara
Description: 
A cheesy, spicy spinach good for both dipping and as a side.  My family and friends beg for it!
