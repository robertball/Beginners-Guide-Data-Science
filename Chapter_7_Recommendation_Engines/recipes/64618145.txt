Title:
Tex-Mex Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 hard-cooked eggs, cut in half; lengthwise
1 tsp dijon mustard
2 Tbsp mayonnaise
1 tsp jalapeno brine, or more to taste
1 tsp chili powder, or more to taste
1/2 tsp smoked paprika
8 pickled jalapeno slices
Chili powder and cilantro for garnish

Directions:
Combine yolks, mustard, mayo, brine, chili powder, paprika and mix until creamy. Refil the eggs with the mixture. Top each egg with a jalapeno slice. Sprinkle chili powder and cilantro on top for garnish.
*add some chopped pickled jalapenos for more punch

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Put some yee-haw in your deviled eggs by adding chili powder, cumin, jalapeno brine, and smoked paprika. I adapted this from Allrecipes' Deviled Eggs and The Devil's Own Deviled Eggs.
