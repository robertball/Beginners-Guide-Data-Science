Title:
Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/697858.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cans biscuits
1 stick butter
3/4 cup sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Mix cinnamon and sugar togehter. Break biscuits into four balls. Roll biscuits in cinnamon and sugar mixture. Put balls in a greased bunt pan.
Bring to a boil,one stick butter, one cup sugar and two tsp. vanilla, pour over biscuits and cook in oven at 350 degrees for 30 min.
