Title:
Ube Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4570949.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 ube (purple yam), peeled
1/2 cup milk
1 scoop vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer. Bring water to a boil. Add ube, cover, and steam until tender, about 20 minutes. Cool until easily handled and cube. Measure 1/4 cup of the cooked ube.
Combine 1/4 cup ube, milk, and vanilla ice cream in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
yummers
Description: 
Make yourself a tasty purple milkshake with the addition of ube, aka purple yam. The cooked ube brings nice flavor to the shake!
