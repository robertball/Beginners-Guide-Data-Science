Title:
Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
1 cup light corn syrup
1/2 cup butter, melted
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/4 cup pecan have toasted
9" pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ยบ F. In a medium bowl beat eggs (2 minutes). Mixing well after each ingredient addition add brown sugar, then corn syrup, melted butter and vanilla.  Make sure all ingredients are combined well. I use a handheld mixer.
Place pecans on bottom of pie shell.  Pour mixture over pecans. Pecans should float to top.
Bake for 50 minutes or until pie is firm. Cool and serve.
