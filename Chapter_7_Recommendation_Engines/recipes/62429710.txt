Title:
Jim's Pineapple Infused Vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/450231.jpg
Ingredients:
14 Fresh pineapples
5- 1.75 liters of Smirnoff Orange Vodka

Directions:
Cut the pineapples into slices and arrange them in a glass jug anyways you like. I used a 5 gal glass jug. Pour over the orange flavored vodka and let it marinate!

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
Fresh pineapple bathed in Vodka! The taste is so good even after a day or 2! This just gets better with time! No refrigeration needed.
