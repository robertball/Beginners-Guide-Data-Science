Title:
Vegetarian Cutlets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5874718.jpg
Ingredients:
GLUTEN
1 C or Can Soy Beans or any canned bean
3 C Gluten Flour
1/2 C Minute Tapioca
1/2 C whole Wheat Flour
1/2 C Soy Flour
1 tsp Onion Powder
1 tsp Garlic Powder
3 Tbs Beef Seasoning
3 Tbs Yeast Flakes
Handful of Sesame Seeds
1/4 C Ground Flax or Chia Seeds
4 Packages Golden GW Seasoning
3 1/4 C Hot Water
1/4 C Soy Sauce
1 Tbs Paprika
BROTH
10 Cups Water
1 Tbs Savorex or Vegex
3 Tbs beef seasoning
4 Tbs yeast flakes
1/2 C Soy Sauce
Large Onion diced
1 2-3 Bay leaves
7 Black Peppercorns

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients and drained beans above for the gluten.
Add 3 1/4 Cups of hot water, 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce all at once stir into dough.
Lightly knead dough.
Form dough into a roll like a cinnamon roll.
Let it sit 15 minutes while making the broth.
Mix broth ingredients together and bring to a boil.
Slice gluten to a 1/3" pieces.
Drop into lightly simmering water, one at a time.
Keep broth lightly simmering.
After all gluten is in broth turn heat down and cover and simmer for one hour.
Simmer longer if pieces are bigger.
Place in casserole dish and serve.
Freezes well.
Can dip in bread crumb mix and fry.
*If canning for later use
Place uncooked patties into can fill with broth
Process in pressure cooker at 10 lbs of pressure for 1 hour and 25 minutes
