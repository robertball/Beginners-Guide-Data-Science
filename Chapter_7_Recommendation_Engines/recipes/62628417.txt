Title:
Ham & Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked ham, diced
1 5 lb. bag of baby red potatoes, sliced
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 pint of heavy whipping cream
1 small block of smoked cheddar cheese, shredded
1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped (optional)
Garlic powder, black pepper and seasoning salt to taste

Directions:
Butter a 9 x 13 inch glass baking dish.  Put in order: potatoes, onion, ham, seasonings and whipping cream.  Cover with tin foil and bake at 325 degrees for 45 minutes.  Take foil off and sprinkle with cheese and parsley and bake another 5 - 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
jkohnen
Description: 
This is a great recipe to use up left over ham.
