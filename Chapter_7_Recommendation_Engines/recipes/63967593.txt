Title:
Best Banana Bread by Penni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Stick of Unsalted Butter
1 Cup of Sugar
2 Eggs
3 Bananas (preferably aged appropriately black but not rotten)
2 Cups of Sifted Flour
1 Teaspoon Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Cream butter, add sugar slowly and cream well.
Add eggs one at a time and beat well.
Add bananas.
Mix together flour, salt and baking soda.
Add dry and wet mixtures together and thoroughly mix.
Lastly add vanilla and mix.
Spray loaf pan with oil and pour mixture in and bake for 1 hour 15 minutes (or until toothpick comes out clean)
Take out of the oven and let cool.  Once cool wrap in foil and keep refrigerated.
