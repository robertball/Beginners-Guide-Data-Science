Title:
Ranch Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Chicken breasts
2 c. Italian bread crumbs
1/2 c.  Parmesan Cheese
1 TBS Garlic Salt
1/2 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
1 TSB Italian Seasonings
1 24oz Bottle of Ranch Dressing
2 c. Italian Bread Crumbs

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. While that's being done, take two bowls in the first one put all of the dry ingredients and in the other  the Ranch dressing. Drege the chicken in the ranch dressing then into the bread crumb mixture, place on a cookie sheet.   Cook the chicken for 35 min or intil the juices run clear.
