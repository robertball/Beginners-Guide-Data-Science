Title:
Southern Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  (15 oz) cans while shoepeg corn,  drained and rinsed
2  (15 oz) cans black eyed peas, drained and rinsed
1 medium red onion, small diced
2 med. red bell pepper, small diced
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
1/3 cup canola oil
3-6 Tablespoons sugar.  (I used 6)

Directions:
Place vegetables in a bowl.
Combine in microwave bowl vinegar, oil and sugar.   Heat on high and stir until sugar is dissolved.
Pour liquid over vegetables and toss to combine
Chill 1 hours or overnight.  Overnight is best.
Stir several times to mix marinade with vegetables
Serve with corn chips.  Scoops tastes best.
