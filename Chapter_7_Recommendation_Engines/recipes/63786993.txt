Title:
Traditional Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075875.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups (15 0z.) canned garbanzo beans, drained
1/3 cup tahini
1 lemon, juiced
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cloves garlic, halved
4 tablespoon's olive oil
1 pinch paprika
1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley

Directions:
Place the garbanzo beans, tahini, lemon juice, salt and garlic and olive oil in a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth. Transfer mixture to a serving bowl.
Sprinkle with paprika and parsley.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
As a spread, dip or garlicky pita filling, classic hummus is both delicious and satisfying. Best of all, it's whipped up in seconds in a blender or food processor.Modified from: Hummus III
