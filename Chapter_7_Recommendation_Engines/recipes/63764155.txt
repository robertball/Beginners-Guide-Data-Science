Title:
Rhubarb Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1043695.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
1 1/2 lbs. ground turkey (or beef)
3 cups rhubarb, chopped into 1/2" cubes
1 medium onion, chopped
1 large red bell pepper, chopped
3 cloves of garlic, minced
2 cans diced tomatoes
2 cans black beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup frozen white corn
2 Tbsp. chili powder
2 Tbsp. cacao powder
1 Tbsp. organic sugar
1 1/2 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. sea salt
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. allspice
1/8 tsp. cayenne
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, saute onions, peppers and rhubarb with a pinch of salt until tender.
Add ground turkey and garlic to the pot and cook until almost browned.
Add spices and stir into mixture for 3 minutes.
Add diced tomatoes, black beans and corn and thoroughly combine. Bring to a boil then reduce heat.  Cover and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring once halfway through.
Check for salt and pepper and serve.

Description: 
This chili was a fun experiment that my kids gave 5 stars!  Rhubarb is very popular in desserts and should make an appearance in the main dish occasionally.  Serve with your favorite chili toppings, or eat on its own.
