Title:
Nisha's Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1070232.jpg
Ingredients:
Rib Seasoning:
1 cup salt
1/4 cup pepper
1/4 cup garlic powder
1/4 cup parsley flakes
4 tsp. paprika
Rib Sauce:
4 cups ketchup
2 cups mustard
1 cup apple cider vinegar
2 1/2 cups brown sugar
1 Tbsp. chili powder
2 Tbsp. Garlic powder
2 Tbsp. paprika
1 tsp. ground mustard
3 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 Tbsp. dry basil
olive oil
1 Tbsp. black pepper
Costco pack of ribs

Directions:
Open rib pack, rinse with water, and season ribs. Cover ribs with tin foil and bake in oven at 350 for 4 hours (less time if cooking less ribs).
Mix sauce ingredients (may need more oil to be a smooth consistency).
Cover ribs in sauce before serving (or let people cover their own...sauce kept in squirt bottle).
