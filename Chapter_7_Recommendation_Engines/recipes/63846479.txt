Title:
Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068374.jpg
Ingredients:
2 quarts half-and-half cream
1/2 pint heavy cream
1 1/2 cups sugar
4 teaspoons vanilla extract ( half if use Mexican vanilla)
1 pinch salt

Directions:
Combine half-and-half, cream, sugar, vanilla and salt in freezer container of ice cream maker.
Freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
No-cook, no eggs, made with half-and-half and cream. Great, simple, classic vanilla ice cream!Modified from: Vanilla Ice Cream V
