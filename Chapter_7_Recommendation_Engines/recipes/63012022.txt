Title:
Cream Cheese Jam Toppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 8oz cream cheese
2. 2 sticks oleo/ margerine
3. 2  cups flour
4. 1-2 cups jam ( your choice flavor strawberry works well)
5. 2-3 cups powered sugar

Directions:
soften 1 and 2 and blend together....add 3 blend and form a ball...wrap and chill overnight
roll out on a floured surface....cut small round shapes ( a glass drinking cup works well)...drop 1/2 tea of 4 in the center and fold circle in half ...moisten edges with water and seal with fork...bake ungreased  cookie sheet 400* 5-10 min...roll in 5 while still slightly warm cool on plate...you may decide to put two rounds together with the jam in the center

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
melt in your mouth..low in sugar
dough must be refrigerated so make dough day before baking
