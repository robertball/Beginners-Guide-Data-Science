Title:
Easy Pizza Sauce III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 tsp ground oregano
1 tsp Italian seasoning
2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
1 teaspoon ground paprika
2 pinches red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp onion powder
1 sprinkle of fresh parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a medium bowl, Mix together tomato sauce and tomato paste until smooth. Stir in oregano, Italian seasoning, garlic, paprika, red pepper flakes, onion powder and a bit of fresh parmesan.
Can be frozen, in cubes.
