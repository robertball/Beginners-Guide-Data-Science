Title:
Croquetas de zanahoria y cebolla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1123341.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 kg of carrots
2 onions size medium
50 g of Parmesan cheese
milk
self-raising flour
neutral (seed) oil
salt
pepper

Directions:
Peel the carrots and cut them, peel the onion and cut into pieces it.
Now pour the carrots and onions in milk, and add the self-raising flour.
Now season, add cheese and dough that forms do tiny balls and put it to fry in a pan with oil.

Submitter: 
María Renée Rada
Description: 
Good and hot croquettes of carrot, onion and Parmesan cheese, ideal for vegetarians and children eat vegetables.
