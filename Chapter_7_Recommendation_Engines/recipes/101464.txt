Title:
Lawry's Chili Con Queso Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993069.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2/3 cup water
1 (1 ounce) package Lawry's ® Hot Taco Spices & Seasonings*
8 ounces processed cheese
1/2 cup prepared salsa
Tortilla chips

Directions:
In 10-inch skillet, brown ground beef; drain, if desired. Stir in water and Lawry's(R) Hot Taco Spices & Seasonings. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, 3 minutes. Stir in cheese until melted, about 8 minutes. Stir in salsa. Serve with chips.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
This cheesy, beefy dip is packed with flavor and perfect for dipping your favorite tortilla chips.
