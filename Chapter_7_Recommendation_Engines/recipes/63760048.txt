Title:
Carrots and Green Beans in White Wine Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1042774.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon peanut oil
4 carrots sliced (or sliced diagonally for variety)
1 cup fresh green beans cut into 1 inch pieces
1/4 cup low sodium, low fat vegetable broth
1 tablespoon light/low salt soy sauce
1/4 cup white wine
1/4 teaspoon corn starch

Directions:
In small bowl, whisk white wine, soy sauce, and corn starch until fully blended.
Add peanut oil and broth mix to small wok or skillet and heat on medium-high, add the carrots and green beans.
Bring to boil, and then simmer for about 20 minutes or until carrots and beans are soft.
Serve.
