Title:
Spicy Tuna Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup light mayonnaise
1-2 Tbsp. finely chopped, seeded jalapeno pepper
2 tsp. lime juice
1 can (6oz.) water-packed chunk light tuna, drained
2 cups thinly sliced iceberg lettuce
1/2 cup shredded Mexican blend  cheese
1-2 plum tomatoes, seeded & diced
6 flour tortillas (7 in.)

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine mayonnaise, jalapeno pepper, and lime juice; mix well. Stir tuna into mayonnaise mixture.
Spread tuna mixture evenly over each tortilla to within 1/4 in. of edge. op with lettuce, cheese and tomato, pressing lightly.
Arrange optionaltoppings over tortilla, if desired.
Roll up each tortilla tightly or fold in half and serve immediately.
