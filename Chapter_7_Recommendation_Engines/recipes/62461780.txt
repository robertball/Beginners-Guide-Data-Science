Title:
No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup butter/margarine
2 cups sugar
½ cup milk
2 Tbsp. cocoa
½ cup creamy peanut butter
3 cups dry quick-cooking oats
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Add first 4 ingredients into a 4-quart saucepan.
Bring to a rolling boil and hold for 1 minute.
Remove from heat.
Stir in next 3 ingredients and drop by tablespoon onto wax paper.
Let cool until set.
