Title:
Tuna Nicoise Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad leaves
Cucumber slices
2 tomatoes
1 small red onion
2 eggs (hard boiled and peeled)
1 can tuna in oil or water
4 small potatoes (boiled and peeled)
Grated cheese to taste
French salad dressing, salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Arrange salad leaves, vegetables and onion rings on a plate. Top with egg quarters, spoonfuls of tuna, quartered potatoes.
Drizzle with some dressing, sprinkle with salt, pepper and cheese.
