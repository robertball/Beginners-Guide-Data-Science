Title:
Tamale Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can chili (no beans)
1 28 oz can or 2 15oz cans of beef tamales with sauce (example Hormel)
1 (10oz) can Rotel tomatoes
Â½ pound processed cheese (example Velveeta)

Directions:
Empty tamales on plate
Remove the paper tamale wrappers and discard.
Mash tamales with a fork, leaving some small chunks.
Combine mashed tamales, the remainder of the tamale sauce from can and all other ingredients in a pot (for stovetop) or microwave safe bowl (for microwave heating) and heat until cheese is melted and all ingredients a well incorporated.
Serve with corn chips or tortilla chips (we use Fritos Scoops, or our favorite tortilla chips)

Submitter: 
Dennis Morazan
Description: 
My mother-in-law gave this super easy dip recipe to me years ago.  It has been a hit at every party we have every served it at.
