Title:
Dark Chocolate Fruit and Nut Bar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/911030.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can Eaglebrand semisweet condensed milk
3 cups dark semisweet chocolate chips
1 teaspoon butter
¼ cup dried cranberries
¼ cup dried blueberries
¼ cup chopped pistachios
¼ cup chopped pecans
¼ cup sliced almonds

Directions:
In a microwave safe dish add chocolate, milk and butter stir.
Cook in microwave for 1.5 minutes, stir, and cook another 1.5 minutes.
Add dry ingredients, stir and pour on parchment paper.
Using another piece of parchment paper over chocolate mixture flatten chocolate.
Refrigerate until set, slice and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Danielle Girouard
Description: 
A fiend of mine had made 5 minute fudge for a fundraiser, I took her recipe and adapted it to make these chocolate bars, they are fast and so easy to make and always a big hit.
