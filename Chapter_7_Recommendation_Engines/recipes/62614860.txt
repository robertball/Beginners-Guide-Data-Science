Title:
Chocolate Monkeys Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup mashed, over-ripe banana
1 egg, beaten
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/8 tsp freshly ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup chocolate chunks

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (162.78 degrees C). Line cookie sheets with parchment paper.
In a large bowl, cream together butter, 1/4 cup brown sugar, and white sugar. Mix in banana, egg, and vanilla. Sift together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and cinnamon; mix into the creamed mixture. Stir in chocolate chunks. Drop dough by heaping spoonfuls onto the prepared baking sheets.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, until edges are a golden, brown.
