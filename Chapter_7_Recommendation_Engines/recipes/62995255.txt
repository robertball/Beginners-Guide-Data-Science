Title:
Taco Cheese Log Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) pkg Cream Cheese, room
4 cups shredded Colby Jack Cheese
3 heaping TBSP mayo
1 pkg Taco Seasoning
1 1/2 cupes chopped pecans

Directions:
In a medium size bowl, mix thoroughly the cream cheese, shredded cheese, mayo and taco seasoning. Mix until all incorperated and all cheese clumps are worked through. Divide into 3 balls and set aside.
On a cookie sheet or shallow pan. Pour in chopped pecans.
Take each cheese ball and slightly roll in the pecans, forming a log shape and cover log with pecans as much as possible. Turning the logs on to plastic wrap and wrap logs and store into refrigerator for at least 4 hours. Then serve with your favorita tortilla chip or buttery cracker.
