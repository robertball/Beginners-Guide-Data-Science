Title:
Broccoli Puff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 oz) packages frozen chopped broccoli
1 (10.75 oz) can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup mayo
1 beaten egg
1/4 cup milk
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cook and drain broccoli.
Mix soup, cheese, mayo, egg, and milk.
In 2quart casserole dish mix broccoli and soup mixture well.
Melt butter and mix with bread crumbs.  Sprinkle on top of broccoli.
Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly.
