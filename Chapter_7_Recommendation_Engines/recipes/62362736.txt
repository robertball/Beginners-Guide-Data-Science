Title:
Saucy Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. fresh green beans, trimmed
1 small sweet onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Tab. olive oil
8 oz. tomato sauce
1 Tab. sugar
salt/pepper
1 Tab. red wine vinegar

Directions:
cook beans in boiling water until done. Saute onion and garlic in the olive oil until onion is tender..add tom sauce and sugar..cook and stir about 5 min. add beans and salt/pepper, vinegar. cook about 5 more min.
