Title:
Crispy Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup corn syrup
1 cup semi-sweet (or dark) chocolate chips
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup icing sugar
2 cups Rice Krispies cereal

Directions:
Melt butter in microwave, till starting to bubble.
Stir in chocolate until smooth.
Stir in vanilla and icing sugar, mix until smooth.
Add cereal, mixing well.
Spread in greased 8" square pan.
Chill until set, about 30 minutes.
Cut into squares.
Store in fridge.
