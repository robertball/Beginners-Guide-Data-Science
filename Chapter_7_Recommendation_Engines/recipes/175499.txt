Title:
Ocean Spray® Cosmopolitan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984495.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces vodka
1 1/2 ounces Ocean Spray® Cranberry Juice Drink, any flavor, chilled
1/2 ounce fresh lime juice
Lime twist, garnish

Directions:
In a cocktail shaker filled halfway with ice, combine all ingredients and shake well. Strain mixture into a chilled stemmed cocktail glass. Garnish with lemon twist

Submitter: 
Ocean Spray®
Description: 
Relax in style with this refreshing adult beverage!
