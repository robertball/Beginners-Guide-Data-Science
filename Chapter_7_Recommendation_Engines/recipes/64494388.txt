Title:
Shrimp and Sautéed Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cleaned, deveined, and shelled large shrimp
1 1/2 tbsp evoo
1 1/2 tbsp Simply Asia  Sweet Ginger Garlic Seasoning
1/4 c dry white wine
3 c fresh spinach

Directions:
In a medium skillet, add 1 tbsp evoo and heat until a slight shimmer occurs on surface.
In a separate bowl, combine shrimp, 1 tbsp evoo, and seasoning, and toss lightly.
Add shrimp to skillet and sauté til opaque.
Pour in wine to quickly bubble and deglaze the skillet.
Remove shrimp to a small bowl and cover to keep warm.
In the same skillet, add final 1/2 tbsp evoo and toss in spinach, quickly stirring until leaves are just wilted.
Throw shrimp back in for a final toss and serve.
