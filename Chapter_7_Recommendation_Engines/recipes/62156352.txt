Title:
chicken spaghetti bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb thin reg. or wheat spaghetti
3 13 oz cans of chicken breast OR 5 boiled chicken breasts, cubed
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
4 cups shredded cheese

Directions:
preheat oven to 350.
boil spaghetti. drain.
in large bowl mix noodles, chicken, and soups.
pour into pan.
top with cheese
bake for 30 minutes.

a cheesy baked blend of chicken and spaghetti
