Title:
BUSH'S® Butter Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986046.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup finely chopped celery
1 cup finely chopped carrots
3/4 cup finely chopped onion
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
4 cups water
2 (16 ounce) cans BUSH'S® Butter Beans, drained
1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 teaspoon basil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In large pan, cook celery, carrots and onion in oil until soft. Add flour; cook and stir for 1 minute. Gradually add water, stirring until smooth.
Add remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer for 20 to 25 minutes or until slightly thickened.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
Warm up winter with this deliciously easy tomato and butter bean soup.
