Title:
Mixed Berries Energy Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991034.jpg
Ingredients:
1 frozen peeled banana, sliced
1/2 cup fresh or frozen blackberries, pureed and strained
1/2 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
2 packets Stevia Extract In The Raw®
1/2 cup light vanilla soymilk
1/2 cup lowfat plain yogurt
2 tablespoons toasted wheat germ, divided (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except wheat germ in blender and process until well blended. Pour and serve.

Submitter: 
Stevia Extract In The Raw®
Description: 
Tastes just as good as the higher calorie version - and you save calories and carbs by using Stevia.
