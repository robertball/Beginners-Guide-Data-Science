Title:
Avocado Crostini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rustic bread
3 cloves of garlic
2 Ripe avocados
Olive oil
Sea salt
Crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
Cut avocado into slices or chunks.
Slice bread and place on grill to toast.
When bread is just starting to char, remove and rub one side with a clove of garlic.
Place avocado on garlic rubbed side of toasted bread and drizzle with olive oil.
Sprinkle with sea salt and red pepper flakes and serve immediately.
