Title:
Peppermint Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/743923.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package or 8oz semisweet chocolate chips
2 teaspoons canola oil
1 teaspoon peppermint extract
1 package white chocolate chips (use canola oil)
2/3 cup chopped candy canes

Directions:
in bouble boiler over boiling water, melt white choclate chips. Remove from heat and stir in crushed candy and mint extract.
line a small sheet pan with wax paper. Pour in white choclate mixture and spread evenly. refridgerate until firm.
melt semi-sweet choclate and spread evenly over white choclate layer. refridgerate until firm. Break into pieces. store in refrigerator.

cookinmama
Description: 
This layered peppermint bark is a delectable blend of semisweet chocolate, white chocolate, and peppermint.Modified from: 5-Ingredient Peppermint Bark
