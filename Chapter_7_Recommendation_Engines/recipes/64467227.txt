Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2469608.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package frozen puff pastry (17.3 oz), thawed.
1/2 container frozen whipped topping, thawed.
4 oz. semi-sweet chocolate, chopped.
1/2 cup heavy cream
2 teaspoons chipotle powder.
2 cups strawberries, cut lengthwise.
2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar, or as needed for dusting.

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2.  Unfold both sheets of pastry puff and lay unto a lightly floured work surface.  Cut each sheet into 3 strips along the fold marks, and then cut each strip into four squares.  Place on a baking sheet.
3.  Bake in preheated oven for 10 - 15 minutes, until golden brown.
4.  Melt chocolate, cream, and chipotle in a sauce pan until creamy.
5.  Lightly pull apart puff pastry squares,  making a top and bottom half.
6.  Layer chocolate sauce (bottom), strawberries (middle), and whipped topping (top)in each square.
7. Dust top with powdered sugar.
8.  Take a picture!
