Title:
Bill's Penne with Spicy Vodka Tomato Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound uncooked penne pasta
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 onion, minced
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
1 (6 ounce) can of tomato sauce
3/4 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons vodka
1 tsp Italian herb seasoning
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
Optional: 2 (3.5 ounce) links sweet Italian sausages or 2 marinated grilled chicken breasts, cut into 1/2" pieces

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 10 to 12 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In large skillet, heat oil over moderate heat. If using sausage, remove casing from sausage and add to skillet. Cook, breaking up the meat, until brown.
Add garlic, onion, Italian seasoning and red pepper and cook, stirring until garlic is golden brown.
Add canned tomatoes, tomato sauce, basil and salt; bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes.
Add vodka and cream and bring to boil. If using chicken, add chicken pieces at this point to the sauce. Reduce heat to low and add pasta, toss for 1 minute. Stir in fresh parsley and serve!

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Hearty Italian pasta dish. Garlic, onion, Italian seasonings and red pepper flakes are simmered with tomatoes & basil and finished with vodka and cream to make a rich, spicy sauce. Toss with penne and fresh parsley to serve.Modified from: Penne with Spicy Vodka Tomato Cream Sauce
