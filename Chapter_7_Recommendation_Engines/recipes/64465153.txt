Title:
Stracciatella- Italian Egg Drop Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
48 oz can Chicken Broth (or 6 cups chicken broth)
4 eggs
1 tablespoon Flour
2 tablespoons of  Parmesan  plus more for serving
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
Salt and pepper to taste
Garnish with fresh chopped parsley

Directions:
Bring chicken broth to a boil.
In a bowl whisk the eggs, then little by little add flour and cheese.
Once broth comes to a boil reduce heat to medium-low. Slowly with a whisk add two ladles of broth to egg mixture.
Stir broth in a circular motion. Gradually drizzle egg mixture into moving broth, stirring gently with a fork to form thin strings of egg.
Season with salt and pepper if desired.
Top with grated Parmesan and parsley.

A family favorite that my grandmother use to always prepare for us when we were sick and for holiday dinners as our second course.
