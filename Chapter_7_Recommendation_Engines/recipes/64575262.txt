Title:
Garlic Parmesan Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3592847.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can chickpeas, drained
6 garlic gloves
1/4 cup tahini
3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup parmesan
1/2 tsp freshly ground pepper
1/4 tsp cumin
1/8 cup lemon juice
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Cut the top of the garlic head off and place the garlic bulb on a piece of aluminum foil large enough to enwrap the garlic bulb. Spray the garlic with a just a bit of olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Wrap the garlic, place on a baking sheet & baking 40 minutes in the oven.
Remove garlic from the oven and remove from the aluminum foil. Allow it to cool 20 minutes.
Then in a food processor or blender add all the ingredients for the hummus including the garlic and process until smooth.
Transfer the hummus to a bowl. Drizzle with a little extra olive oil, parmesan and pepper if you like before enjoying!
