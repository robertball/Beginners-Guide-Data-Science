Title:
Au Gratin Potatoes Ecuador Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ricotta cheese
11 ounce) stock, chicken
4 1/2 cups peeled and shredded potatoes
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (optional)
1/3 c parmesan or cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray one 8 inch square baking dish with vegetable cooking spray.
In a large bowl, stir together the ricotta and stock. Add grated potatoes stacked, cheese, and onions. Mix well, and pour into the prepared baking dish. Cover the dish with a lid or aluminum foil.
Bake for 45 minutes in the preheated oven. In a small bowl, melt the butter. Sprinkle over the top of the potatoes with another 1/4 cup of cheddar or parmesan cheese, and return to the oven.
Bake uncovered, for an additional 20 minutes or until bubbly and corn flakes are golden brown. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with chopped parsley.
