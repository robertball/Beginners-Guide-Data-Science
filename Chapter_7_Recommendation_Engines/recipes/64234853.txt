Title:
Turkey Herb Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1705783.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups chopped fresh parsley
4 tsp minced fresh thyme
2 tsp chopped fresh sage
1 1/2 tsp minced rosemary
2 medium cloves garlic, minced or pressed (abt 2 stp)
3/4 tsp grated lemon zest
1 tsp table salt
1 tsp black pepper
1/4 cup olive oil or butter

Directions:
Process parsley, thyme, sage, rosemary, shallot, garlic, lemon zest, salt, and pepper in food processor until consistency of coarse paste, ten 2-second pulses.
Add mustard and olive oil; continue to process until mixture forms smooth paste, ten to twelve 2-second pulses; scrape sides of processor bowl with rubber spatula after 5 pulses.
Transfer mixture to small bowl.
Using hands, carefully loosen skin from meat of breasts, thighs, and drumsticks.
Using spoon, slip 1 1/2 tablespoons paste under breast skin on each side of turkey.
Using fingers, distribute paste under skin over breast, thigh, and drumstick meat.
Rub 1 tablespoon paste inside the turkey cavity.
Rotate turkey breast side down; apply half remaining herb paste to turkey skin; flip turkey breast side up and apply remaining herb paste to skin, pressing and patting to make paste adhere; reapply herb paste that falls onto baking sheet.
