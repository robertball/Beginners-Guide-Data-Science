Title:
FLAPPER PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Graham Wafer Crust for 9” pie plate or 9”x 9” baking dish.  Reserve ½ cup for topping.
2 cups milk
1 Tbsp. Cornstarch
½ cup sugar
3 egg yolks (save whites for later)
½ tsp. Vanilla
6 Tbsp. Sugar

Directions:
Pour milk into top of double boiler or heavy saucepan.  Mix cornstarch and sugar and stir into milk.  Add slightly beaten egg yolks and vanilla.  Cook, stirring constantly until mixture comes to a boil and becomes thick.  Pour into piecrust.  Let cool.
Now beat 3 egg whites, gradually adding sugar until egg whites are firm and form peaks.  Pile gently onto cooled pie filling.  Spread for an attractive topping.  Sprinkle with remaining crumbs.  Bake at 350º F for 7-10 minutes or until lightly browned.  Let cool and serve.
