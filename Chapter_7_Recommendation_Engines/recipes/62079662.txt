Title:
Cheesey Garden Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb pepper bacon, chopped
1/2 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup red onion, diced
1/2 cup carrots, diced
1 1/2 cups water
1 cup frozen mixed vegetables (examples peas, green beans, lima beans)
2 cups potatoes, finely diced (about 1/2 inch square pieces)
1 can cream style corn
2 cups milk (you may substitute 1 cup of the milk with heavy cream for a richer soup)
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
-In a large stock pot combine bacon, celery, onion and carrots.
-Saute until bacon starts to crisp and onions are clear.
-Add the water and frozen veggies, stir and cook on Medium heat for 5 min.
-Add Potatoes, cook an additional 10 min., stiring ocassionally.
-Stir in milk(milk/cream), corn, and Salt & Pepper
-Continue cooking, stirring often until potatoes are tender
-Remove from heat, Stir in cheese until melted.
-Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Beth Maples
Description: 
This is a wonderfuly creamy soup, perfect for those cold winter nights. Everytime I make this everyone asks for the recipe. Enjoy!
