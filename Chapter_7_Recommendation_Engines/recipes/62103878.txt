Title:
Stuffed Bellpepper Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 onion, diced
1 green bellpepper, diced
1 can of spaghetti sauce of your choice
2 cups cooked white rice
2 cups preshredded cheddar cheese
1 tablespoon sugar
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a pan combine onions, peppers, and meat and cook over medium heat until meat is browned and vegetables are cooked.
Add spaghetti sauce, rice, and sugar. Mix well. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Transfer to a 9x13 baking dish. Top with cheese and bake for 15 minutes.
