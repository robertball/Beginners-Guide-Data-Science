Title:
Kentucky Blackberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Topping~
4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2  cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tablespoon salt
1 tablespoon baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 cup cold butter cubed
1 2/3  cups buttermilk, plus additional for brushing
Filling~
6 c. blackberries, fresh or frozen
1 cup sugar
2 tsp cornstartch
1/4 cup butter
2 tsp lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
For the filling combine sugar and corn starch in a bowl and mix together.
Add berries and lemon juice to sugar mixture and mix to cover berries.
Butter 13x9x2-inch glass baking dish.
Add berries to prepared dish. Dot with butter.
Topping~ In another bowl sift all dry ingredients together.Reserve 1/4 cup of sugar for top.
Using a pastry blender cut in butter.
Gently fold in buttermilk again reserving about 1/4 cup for brushing the top.
Spoon on dough on top of the berries using a large spoon. Theres no right or wrong way, you just want to have your dough even in thickness and covering most of the fruit.
Brush dough with buttermilk.
Sprinkle remaining sugar on top.
Bake cobbler until fruit is tender and biscuits are golden, about 25 minutes.
