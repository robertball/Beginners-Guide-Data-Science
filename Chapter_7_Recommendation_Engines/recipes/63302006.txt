Title:
Homemade Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
1 onion, diced
1 green pepper, diced
1 1/2 cloves garlic
1 cup cream cheese
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 tablespoon parsley
1 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
15 oz. tomato sauce
8 10" flour tortillas
2 cups enchilada sauce

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Heat oil in skillet over medium heat.
3. Add shredded chicken, onion, green pepper, garlic, cream cheese, and 1 cup Monterey Jack. Heat until cheese melts.
4. Add the chili powder, parsley, oregano, salt, pepper and tomato sauce. Stir.
5. Divide mixture evenly into tortillas and roll them up. Cover with enchilada sauce (homemade is best) and remaining Monterey Jack cheese.
6. Bake for 15 minutes.
