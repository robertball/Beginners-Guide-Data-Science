Title:
Best Beef Dip Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds beef chuck roast
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
3 bay leaves
1 cup soy sauce
3 cups water
3 cups low sodium beef broth

Directions:
Place roast in slow cooker. Season with garlic and rosemary and add bay leaves. Pour in soy sauce, broth and water. Cook on Low setting for 6 to 10 hours. Unlike most roasts, the longer it cooks, the better.

Submitter: 
Crystal S
Description: 
A favorite with all my family and friends. Throw all the ingredients into slow cooker, head to work, and dinner will be ready when you get home.Modified from: Best Beef Dip Ever
