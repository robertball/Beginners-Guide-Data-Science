Title:
Jambalaya With Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked white rice
4 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 onion, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
1 stalk celery, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup uncooked bacon, cut into strips
2 cups peeled and diced tomatoes
1 teaspoon dried oregano
3 cups hot water
3 teaspoons chicken bouillon
2 pounds shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Put rice on to cook according to package directions.
Dissolve bouillon in hot water and set aside.
In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour until smooth. Stir in onion, bell pepper, celery, garlic, and bacon; cook and stir until bacon is soft-cooked and vegetables are softened.  Stir in tomatoes, bouillon broth and oregano. Cover, and simmer until just a little liquid remains.
Add shrimp, and stir to evenly distribute. Cook for about 8 minutes until shrimp turns pink.
Serve over rice.
