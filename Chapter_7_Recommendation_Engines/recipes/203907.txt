Title:
Pineapple-Orange Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990817.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Cuervo Especial Gold
2 ounces lime margarita mix
1 ounce pineapple juice
1 ounce orange juice
1 tablespoon sugar
1 wedge orange

Directions:
Fill a glass halfway with ice.
Add Cuervo Especial Gold, lime margarita mix, pineapple juice, orange juice, and sugar. Stir well.
Garnish with orange wedge.
