Title:
The Best Meatballs You'll Ever Have Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695996.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pound lean ground beef
2 1/4 cup Italian Style Bread Crumbs
3/4 cup chopped sweet onion
3 egg, lightly beaten
6 cloves garlic, chopped
1/2 cup + 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
3 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
Non-stick cooking spray like "Pam"

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, croutons, sweet onion, egg, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, red pepper flakes, and Cajun seasoning. Form the mixture by hand into meatballs in a size of your choosing.
Place the meatballs on a cookie sheet, and bake at 350, turning occasionally, 20 - 25 minutes, or to desired doneness.

Submitter: 
Carol Hammond
Description: 
Very tasty meatballs full of spice and baked to perfection. Serve with spaghetti or use them for Hoagies.  After baking, place in freezer so that they can be dropped into a large ziplock bag for later.Modified from: The Best Meatballs You'll Ever Have
