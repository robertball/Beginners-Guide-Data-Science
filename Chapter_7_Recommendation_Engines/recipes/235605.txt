Title:
Slow-Cooked Sweet and Spicy Baby Back Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075337.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Pace® Picante Sauce
3/4 cup peach preserves
2 packets Swanson® Flavor Boost™ Concentrated Beef Broth
2 tablespoons peeled and grated fresh ginger root
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
4 pounds baby back pork ribs

Directions:
Stir the picante sauce, preserves, concentrated broth, ginger and red pepper in a 6-quart slow cooker. Add the ribs and turn to coat.
Cover and cook on LOW for 7 to 8 hours or until the ribs are fork-tender.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Baby back ribs are slow cooked in an amazing combination of peach preserves, concentrated beef broth, fresh ginger and crushed red pepper resulting in incredibly tender ribs that are bursting with flavor!
