Title:
Yellow Squash and Corn Saute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/830092.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium yellow squash
1/2 cup water
2 pieces of cooked corn on the cob
2-3 Tbsp butter
salt and pepper to taste
1Tbsp chopped parsley (optional)

Directions:
Cut up the yellow squash to small pieces and place in a medium saute pan with water.
Cut corn off the cob.
Cook squash on medium heat until soft.
Drain any significant water that is left.
Lightly stir in corn and butter, salt, pepper and parsley. And cook until heated through.

Submitter: 
AuntE
Description: 
A pretty side dish that is tasty. A great use for using up summer squash and leftover corn on the cob.
