Title:
Mixed Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/329034.jpg
Ingredients:
2 or 3 Bananas
3 Cups Frozen Fruit Mix
1 Cup Plain or Vanilla Yogurt
1/2 Cup Orange Juice
1/4 Cup Wheat Germ
2 to 3 Tablespoons Honey
1 Teaspoon Vanilla

Directions:
Place bananas, yogurt, frozen fruit, orange juice and wheat germ into blender and blend until almost smooth.  Add honey and vanilla and blend until the desired consistency is reached.

Submitter: 
BRANGIE
Description: 
Thick and fruity, this is a healthy and inexpensive snack or breakfast.
