Title:
Ms B's Husband's Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 clove garlic
1 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
pepper to taste
2 (14-oz) cans tomato sauce
5 scallions, chopped with some of greens included
1 (8-oz) package small egg noodles
1 (16-oz) container of sour cream
1 (8-oz) package cream cheese
1 cup Cheddar cheese, grated
Olive oil to coat baking dish lightly

Directions:
Brown ground beef. Drain grease.
Add garlic, salt, pepper, sugar and tomato sauce. Cover and simmer 15 minutes.
Cook egg noodles and drain. Start preheating oven to 350.
Add scallions to cream cheese and sour cream; mix well.
Grease baking dish lightly with olive oil. Layer dish with noodles, meat sauce, sour cream mixture and grated cheese, ending with grated cheese on top.
Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Claire Brandau
Description: 
The ultimate comfort food. Mom could have made this every night and Dad would have been perfectly happy with that arrangement! This meal is best if prepared the day before and left in the fridge to allow the flavors to come out.
