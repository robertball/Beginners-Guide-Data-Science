Title:
Marinated Vermicelli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. uncooked vermicelli
1 medium tomato, cubed
1 medium cucumber, coarsely chopped
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 (4oz) can sliced ripe olives, drained
1/4 cup chopped red onion
2 tablespoons Salad Supreme Seasoning
1/2 cup purchased zesty Italian salad dressing

Directions:
Cook vermicelli to desired doneness as directed on the package. Drain; rinse with cold water to cool.
Meanwhile, in large bowl, combine all remaining ingredients except salad dressing.
Add cooked vermicelli and salad dressing to salad; toss to mix. Sprinkle Salad Supreme Seasoning on top. Refrigerate  overnight to blend the flavors.

Submitter: 
Tonyell Dasia Clyburn
Description: 
A neighbor of mine growing up use to make this dish and I was in love with it. A few years ago, I finally figured out the ingredients and this is my version of it.
