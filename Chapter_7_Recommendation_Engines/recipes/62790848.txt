Title:
Creamy Tomato Bowtie Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
1 lb bulk sweet Italian sausage (or remove from casings)
1/2 t dried red pepper flakes
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 c diced onion
1 (29 Oz) can tomatoes (drained and chopped)
1 1/2 c whipping cream
1/2 t dried basil or oregano
1 pkg (1 lb) bowtie pasta
Fresh grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
Saute' sausage and pepper flakes until no longer pink.  Add onion and garlic, cook until tender.  Add tomatoes and seasonings.  Simmer 30 minutes.  Add cream and simmer until slightly thickened.  In the meantime, cook pasta as directed on package for al dente.  Toss pasta and sausage together.  Sprinkle cheese on top if desired.

Submitter: 
It's A New Day
Description: 
Any type of sausage could be used (sweet or hot) if you prefer and you can sub half-and-half or even fat free half-and-half for the whipping cream.  A versatile recipe!
