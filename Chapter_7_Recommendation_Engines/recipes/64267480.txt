Title:
Crab Rangoon Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Alaska King Crab legs, thawed then meat removed and roughly chopped (about 6oz)
8 oz 1/3 less fat cream cheese, softened to room temp.
1/2 cup non fat plain Greek yogurt or low fat sour cream
2 green onions, chopped
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp ground ginger
30 frozen phyllo shells (2-15 count pkgs)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Add cream cheese, yogurt, green onions, sugar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, ground ginger to a medium sized bowl.  Stir until smooth.
Fold in chopped crab.
Spoon 2 Tsps of the mixture into each mini phyllo shell.
Bake 10-12 minutes or until mini cups are golden brown around the edges.
Cool slightly and serve.
For extra kick serve with your favorite hot sauce as a side.
