Title:
Orange Cream Cycle Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/427618.jpg
Ingredients:
Orange Cupcake
6 Tbls Unsalted Butter
1/2 cup Sugar
2 Eggs
3 tsp Orange Extract
1 1/2cups Sifted Cake Flour
1/2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Baking Soda
Pinch of Kosher salt
1/2 cup Plain Yogurt
Orange Food Coloring
Cream Filling
1/2 Cup Heavy Cream
2 tsp Confectioners’ Sugar
1/2 tsp Vanilla
1/2 tsp plain gelatin
1 Tbls Water
Milk Icing
1 1/2 cups Confectioners’ Sugar
2 tps Orange Extract
1 Tbls Milk
Orange Food Coloring

Directions:
In a Large bowl Cream butter then slowly add sugar, mix until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time until blended. Add Orange Extract and Food Coloring
In a medium bowl Sift together Cake Flour, Baking powder and baking soda.
Alternate Flour and Yogurt until mixed. The batter will be light and airy.
Line cupcake pan, fill 3/4 of the way full. Place   in a 350 degree oven and bake for 18 minutes. Makes 12 cupcake.
In a medium bowl Whip heavy cream and confectioners’ sugar until soft peaks form. Place gelatin in warm water to dissolve. Cool mixture slightly before adding to cream. Continue to beat until stiff peaks form. Refrigerate until ready to use. This will be enough to fill each cupcake and have some left over for a drop piped on top.
In a small bowl combined confectioners’ sugar, orange extract, milk and food coloring. This is enough to spread over 12 cupcakes.

Food Fan
Description: 
I think this is the best cupcake I have ever had. Light and fluffy orange cake filled with vanilla whipped cream and topped with beautiful orange milk icing.
