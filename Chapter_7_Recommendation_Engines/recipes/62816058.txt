Title:
Country Lemon Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
butter pecan crust (recipe follows)
3 eggs, beaten
1 cup sugar
1 Tbl flour
3 Tbl lemon juice
1 1/2 tsp grated lemon peel
confectioners sugar

Directions:
preheat oven to 350. lightly grease 9" pie pan. while crust is baking, prepare filling
CRUST: combine 1 1/4 cup all purpose flour,1 egg yolk,1/3 cup chopped pecans, 3 Tbl confectioner sugar,1/2 cup marg softened, 1/4 tsp salt. blend with fork.form dough into ball. dust fingertips w/flour;press dough into bottom & sides of greased pie pan. bake 10min
Combine eggs, sugar, flour, lemon juice, & peel. stir until smooth. pour mixture into baked crust. bake 20-25min or morem until filling is set. cool on wire rack. dust w/confectioner sugar. refrigerate, covered
