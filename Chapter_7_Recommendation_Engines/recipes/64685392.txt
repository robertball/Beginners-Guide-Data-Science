Title:
Cacio e Pepe Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5013406.jpg
Ingredients:
12 lasagna noodles (broken into 2 inch pieces)
16 oz. fresh ricotta cheese
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup grated pecorino cheese (plus a little more for topping)
2 teaspoons ground black pepper
olive oil
chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley for garnish
paprika for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
In medium pot boil  lasagna noodles until al dente then drain.
In medium bowl, mix together well the drained noodles, ricotta, mozzarella and pecorino and pepper.
Divide noodle mixture into four olive oil greased 8 oz. ramekins.
Top with more pecorino; drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle a little more black pepper.
Bake at 450 degrees until golden brown in spots and heated thru approximately 10 minutes.
Garnish with parsley and paprika.

Submitter:

Description: 
Fast, fresh individually portioned lasagna bowls that please the stomach everytime.  I serve with a small garden side salad and baked asparagus, but easily could be meal by itself.
