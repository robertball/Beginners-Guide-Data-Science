Title:
curried zucchini soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, peeled, halved lengthwise and cut into thin half-moons
2 Tbs. extra-virgin olive oil
1 Tbs. curry powder, or to taste
Fine sea salt, to taste
4 small zucchini (12 oz. total), ends trimmed, cut lengthwise then crosswise into 1" pieces
1 qt. chicken stock

Directions:
In saucepan combine onion, oil, curry and salt.  Heat over medium heat, stirring until onions are soft, 4 minutes.  Add zucchini and cook until soft, 4 minutes.  Add stock and stir to blend.  Bring to boil, cover and simmer, 20 minutes.  Remove saucepan from heat.  Puree soup using hand blender until zucchini and onion are indistinguishable.
