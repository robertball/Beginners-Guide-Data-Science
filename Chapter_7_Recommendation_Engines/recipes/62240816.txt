Title:
Fig Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
1 egg
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1 cup chopped fresh figs or dried figs( if using dried , soak in a cup of hot water for one hour then cut up in small pieces)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
Cream sugars and butter and add beaten egg.
Sift dry ingredients and blend with creamed mixture.  Fold in figs .
Drop by spoonfuls on greased sheet.  Bake for 15 to 20 minutes.
