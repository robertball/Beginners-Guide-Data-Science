Title:
Vegan Eggplant Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1.5-lb eggplants, sliced paper-thin, longways
1lb (dry) lasagna noodles, cooked
33.5oz Hunt's Tomato Sauce box, divided into 2 parts
6 cloves garlic, dry-roasted
16oz can chickpeas, drained
16oz can white beans, drained
.25c + 1T coarsely chopped fresh parsley, packed
3.8oz can black olives, drained
salt
black pepper
garlic mill
dried savory
olive oil

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
Lightly paint the eggplant with olive oil.
Combine the eggplant and one portion of the tomato sauce in a lasagna pan.  Season to taste with salt, black pepper, garlic mill, and dried savory.
Bake until the eggplant is tender, about 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine 4 cloves garlic, the olives, and .25c parsley in a food processor.  Blend well.
Add the chickpeas and white beans.  Blend again, until smooth.
When the eggplant is done, remove it from the oven and from the pan.  Turn the oven down to 350 degrees F.
Place a layer of about .5c of sauce on the bottom of the pan.  Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top of that, then a layer of the bean spread, then a layer of eggplant strips.  Repeat thrice, using up the eggplant and bean spread.
Place a layer of lasagna noodles on the top, then a layer of sauce.  Sprinkle 1T parsley and 2 cloves chopped garlic on top.
Place in the oven for about 30 minutes.  Remove and let cool for 10 minutes.  Serve.
