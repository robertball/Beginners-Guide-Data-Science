Title:
Amy's Pecan Heath Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 Tablespoon cold water
1 Tablespoon white corn syrup
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup chopped pecans
handful of chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix butter, water, and syrup until melted.
Stir in sugars and heat to 275F.
Add 1/2 of the pecans.
Cook to 300F.
Pour into a buttered 13 x 9 pan.  Let cool.
Melt chocolate.
Spread onto candy and sprinkle with more pecans.
Let cool completely to set and break into chunks.

Submitter:

Description: 
A home-made version of the famous candy.
