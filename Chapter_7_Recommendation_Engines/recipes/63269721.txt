Title:
Breakfast Tortilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 T milk
1/2 t butter or buttery spread
1 6" corn tortilla
1 slice mozzarella-flavored soy "cheese" or white American cheese
To taste:
dill weed
celery salt
pepper

Directions:
Whisk together the egg, milk, butter, and seasonings in a bowl.
Microwave for 30 seconds or until mostly solidified.
Tear the cheese into little pieces.  Arrange most of the pieces on the tortilla.
Slide the mostly-cooked egg onto the tortilla.  Place the additional pieces of cheese on top of the egg.
Microwave for another 30 seconds or until the cheese is melted.
