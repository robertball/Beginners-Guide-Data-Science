Title:
Barbecue Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C tomato puree
1/4 C soy sauce or less
3/4 t. pepper
3/4 t. salt
few dashes hot sauce
1 T Worcestershire sauce
1/2 C cider vinegar
2 T steak sauce
2-3 T brown sugar (use greater amount if not using molasses)
4 T lemon juice
Zest of lemon
1 t. garlic powder
1 t. onion powder
1/2 C molasses

Directions:
Mix all together in saucepan and heat to bubbling. Cook until desired thickness
