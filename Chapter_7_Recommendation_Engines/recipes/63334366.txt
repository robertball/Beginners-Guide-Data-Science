Title:
American Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp olive oil
2 cups water
15 oz tomato sauce
15 oz can diced tomatoes
1 Tbsp soy sauce or Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp Italian herb blend
1/2 tsp salt
1 bay leaf
2 cups uncooked pasta

Directions:
Heat oil in large Dutch oven.  Cook ground beef and onions until the onions are softened and ground beef is no longer pink.  Add in garlic and cook another minute or two.  Add next 7 ingredients.  Bring to a boil, reduce hea, cover, simmer about -0-15 minutes.  Stir in pasta.  Cover and continue to simmer, stirring every few minutes, until pasta is cooked (10-15 minutes).
