Title:
Fish Fillets in Black Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup dried porcini mushrooms
1 Cup Water
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
2 Medium Onions
3 Cloves Garlic
1/2 Teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 cup ground walnuts or pecans
1 bay leaf
2 pounds snapper or striped bass filets
Flour for dredging
2 tablespoons minced parsley
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Soak porcini in warm water for 30 minutes
Drain mushrooms, reserving water, and chop porcini
Place an oven-proof pan on the stove and heat oil
Saute chopped onions and garlic
Add salt and pepper, nutmeg, and ground nuts
Cook over medium heat for a few minutes and then add mushrooms, mushroom liquid, and bay leaf.
Continue cooking and stirring for 5-10 more minutes until the sauce darkens.
Dredge the fish fillets in flour, add to the pan and cover with sauce.
Place pan in preheated oven at 350F degrees for 20 minutes or until done. You can test with a fork, the meat should flake easily.
Garnish with parsley and serve

Submitter: 
Barnaby
Description: 
This is a baked fish recipe from Argentina that combines both the Spanish and Italian influences in that culture.
