Title:
Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Shells or Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz box of jumbo shells or manicotti
1 16 oz pasta sauce
2 tbsp olive oil
8 oz mushrooms diced
4 cloves garlic minced
10 oz bag baby spinach leaves
2 eggs
15 oz container Ricotta cheese
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1/3 cup chopped fresh basil
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
parmesan cheese for sprinkling on top

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook shells according to package directions. Drain and set aside to cool.
In a skillet, heat oil. Saute garlic in oil. Stir in mushrooms, and spinach. Cook until spinach is wilted. Take off heat and set aside.
Beat eggs in a bowl. Stir in ricotta, basil, salt, pepper.
Stir in mushroom mixture and 1/3 cup of parmesan cheese.
Spread 1/2 of the jar of pasta sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish. Fill each shell with the cheese mixture and set in the dish.
Drizzle the rest of the sauce on the shells then cover with the mozzarella cheese and parmesan cheese. Garnish with dried parsley.
Cover with foil. Bake for 30 minutes uncover and bake for 5 minutes to melt and brown the cheese.
