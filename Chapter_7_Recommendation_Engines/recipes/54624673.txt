Title:
Rainbow Ambrosia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (32 ounce) container plain or vanilla yogurt
2 (16 ounce) bags mini marshmallows
1 (15 ounce) can pineapple tidbits, drained
1 pint blueberries
1 (16 ounce) package frozen strawberries, thawed and THOROUGHLY drained
1 (15 ounce) can Mandarin oranges, drained

Directions:
Stir together the yogurt and marshmallows in a large bowl.
Carefully fold in the pineapple and blueberries, and very gently fold in the strawberries and Mandarin oranges.
Refrigerate at least one hour before serving.

Submitter: 
evian257
Description: 
This is a simple, tasty, and colorful variation of a classic ambrosia salad. My son loves it; he keeps asking me when I'm going to make some more of that 'marshmallow casserole'!Modified from: Strawberry Ambrosia
