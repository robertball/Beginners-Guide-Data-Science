Title:
Spinach Cheese Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch spinach, cooked and chopped
1/4 cup onion, chopped
2 cups cheddar cheese
1-1/4 cups milk
4 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup flour
1-1/2 tsp mustard powder
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a greased pie plate, add spinach and onion.  Sprinkle with cheese.
Mix remaining ingredients and pour over the cheese.  Bake for 35 minutes or until set.
