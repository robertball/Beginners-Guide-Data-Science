Title:
Karyn's Sausage and Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 pieces buttered bread
1 1/2 lbs sausage
8 eggs
1 cup milk
1 lb Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Break bread and place in bottom of casserole, cook sausage and break over bread. Mix eggs and milk. Pour over sausage. Grate cheese and put over all ingredients. Sit in refrigerator overnight. Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees. Enjoy!!
