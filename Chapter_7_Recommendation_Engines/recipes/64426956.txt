Title:
Glutn Free Gingery Lemon Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2331828.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups rice flour
3/4 cup gluten free flour
1/4 cup corn meal
1/4 cup flax seed meal
2 tsp. baking powder
1 T finely chopped ginger
1 tsp. grated lemon peel
1 cup mashed bananas
1 cup soy milk
1/4 cup oil
1/4 cup honey
1 egg

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Spray muffin tin.
Mix rice flour, gluten free flour, corn meal, flax seed meal, ginger and lemon peel in medium bowl.
Mix mashed bananas, soy milk, oil, honey and egg in another bowl.  Combine with the ingredients in other bowl.
Scoop into 12 muffin wells, dividing dough evenly.
Bake 25 minutes, until starting to become golden.
