Title:
Bruce's Special Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups shortening
1 cup butter
8 cups confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream shortening and butter until fluffy.  Add sugar, and continue creaming until well blended.
Add salt, vanilla, and 6 ounces whipping cream. Blend on low speed until moistened. Add additional 2 ounces whipping cream if necessary. Beat at high speed until frosting is fluffy.

Submitter: 
Bruce Stone
Description: 
Great buttercream for frosting and borders.Modified from: Rick's Special Buttercream Frosting
