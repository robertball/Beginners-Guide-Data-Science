Title:
Guacamus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1009825.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chickpeas (or 1 can rinsed)
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup lime juice
2 tsp ground cumin
1 small avocado peeled, cored and diced
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients in the bowl of a food processor and pulse until almost smooth.  Transfer to a bowl, cover and refrigerate for an hour or longer before serving.
