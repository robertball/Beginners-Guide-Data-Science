Title:
Hot Butter Rum Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg. golden cake mix with butter
1 pkg French vanilla instant pudding mix (four servings size) pour from the package
4 eggs
1/2 Cup water
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup Rum
1/2 cup chopped pecans
Glaze
1 stick of butter or margarine
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup Rum (I like more Rum then this)
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Cake: Mix together all ingredients. Pour into a bunt cake pan prepared with cooking spray. Bake at 325 for approximately 50 minutes.
Glaze: Boil all ingredients for 2 minutes, pour over hot cake in bunt pan. Cool 30 minutes then remove from pan. Dust with powered sugar and serve with ice cream.

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
This is a favorite of my family for as long as I can remember... Grandmother then mother always made it for Thanksgiving...  Now my 83 year old mother requested that I find her recipe and make it once again this year for our Family Thanksgiving Pot Luck dinner.
