Title:
Cod in tomato with wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cod loins (Any hearty white fish)
8 - 10 King prawns (Uncooked, but peeled if possible)
6 Vine tomatoes
3/4 onion (finely chopped)
2-3 cloves garlic (chopped)
1-2 red chilli's (Deseeded & Chopped)
1 cup white wine (or more if adventurous)
Handful of basil, chopped
smaller handful of parsley, chopped
1 tablespoon cooking oil
Pinch salt and pepper

Directions:
Peel tomatoes (You can use tinned or if fresh - slice in a cross, add to boiling water (2 mins then cold 1 min to ease peeling).
Add oil to frying pan on low heat
Add onion and garlic and saute (low heat-2 mins)
Add chilli's and chopped tomatoes and reduce (mashing helps if you're impatient)
After 2 mins, pour in the wine and reduce for 3 mins (medium heat)
Add salt, pepper, parsley and basil (stir)
Add cod loins, cover with lid or foil
Turn after 5 mins and cook for a further 3 mins (covered)
Add prawns to sauce and re-cover
Cook for further 2 mins, add salt/pepper to taste
Serve with vermicelli noodles, new potatoes or salad for the health conscious
