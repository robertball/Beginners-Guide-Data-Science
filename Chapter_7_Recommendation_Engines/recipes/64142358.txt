Title:
Chocolate Hazelnut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1373904.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, room temperature
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup chocolate hazelnut spread, such as Nutella
1/4 cup cocoa powder, unsweetened
1 cup flour
1 cup milk chocolate chips
1/2 cup chopped or sliced hazelnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F, and line a baking sheet with aluminum foil or parchment paper.
Cream the butter and sugar using an electric mixer until white and fluffy. Add vanilla and egg, beat well. Add Nutella and mix completely. Slowly add cocoa powder and flour and mix well. Stir in chocolate chips and hazelnuts.
Drop onto cookie sheets and bake for 8 minutes.
After 8 minutes, turn the oven off and allow the cookies to sit for two minutes. Then, take the cookies out of the oven to serve immediately or to cool.

Submitter: 
Mai Kitchen
Description: 
These cookies are super moist and soft, with flavors of both chocolate and hazelnut clearly pronounced.
