Title:
My Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
3 tsp cinnamon
dash of salt
2 eggs
1/4 tsp vanilla
2 cans pumpkin puree
1 can evaporated milk
2- 9" pie crusts

Directions:
1. pre heat oven to 350*
2. Mix first four ingredients in a large bowl until thoroughly mixed. Then stir in eggs until well blended. Add the rest of ingredients and mix well.
3. Pour into pie crusts evenly
4. Cover edges of pie crusts with foil and bake 1 hour or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Remove foil for last 30 minutes of cooking.

Submitter: 
Jena LaBree
Description: 
A fabulous pumpkin pie with brown sugar, vanilla & cinnamon to add flavor and bring out the best in your pumpkin pie.
