Title:
Creamed Spinach, Boston Market Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz cream of celery soup
4 tablespoons butter or margarine
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1 small onion, diced
1 tablespoon flour
salt and pepper to taste
20 oz frozen chopped spinach, cooked and drained well
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a skillet over medium heat, saute the onions and garlic salt in butter. Whisk in soup, flour, salt and pepper and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add spinach and cheese and cook until cheese is melted and spinach is heated through.

Submitter: 
Anne Marie Sweden
Description: 
This is a homemade version of the delicious, cheesy creamed spinach you might eat at Boston Market. It's easy to make but tastes like gourmet.
