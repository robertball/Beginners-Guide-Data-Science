Title:
Big Ray's Lemony Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1132123.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup olive oil
2 large lemons, cut into rings
1/3 cup lemon juice
6 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp lemon pepper
2 large lemons, cut into rings
2 medium onions, cut into rings
8 chicken leg quarters (pounds)

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, mix the first five ingredients until blended; add onions and lemon slices. Seperate legs and thighs and add chicken to bag; seal bag and turn to coat. Refrigerate overnight.
Prepare grill for direct heat. Drain chicken, discarding marinade in bag. Place chicken on grill rack, skin side up, placing rins of onion and lemon on chicken pieces.  Grill, uncovered, over direct medium heat 5-7 minutes. Turn; grill 5-7 minutes  longer or until a thermometer reads 170°-175°.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Lemon juice, onions and garlic add tangy flavor to chicken when firing up the grill, plus with this amount of oil your chicken skin will look and taste almost like fried chicken.
