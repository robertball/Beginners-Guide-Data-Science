Title:
Kathy's Lemon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box lemon or white cake mix
1 can lemon pie filling
8 oz container cool whip
1 container lemon supreme frosting

Directions:
Mix cake according to box directions and pour into 9x13 cake pan.
Scoop dollops of pie filling on cake and bake according to box directions. Cool.
Fold together cool whip and frosting and frost cake.
Keep in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
KKraft
Description: 
Easy and versatile. Switch out lemon for chocolate cake mix and cherries or try a new version.
