Title:
Crabby Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887011.jpg
Ingredients:
12 hard-cooked eggs
1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat - drained, flaked and cartilage removed
1/4 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
1 tablespoon mustard
2 teaspoons Old Bay seafood seasoning
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Slice eggs in half lengthwise. Remove yolks and set the whites aside.
In a small bowl, mash yolks with a fork. Add crab, mayonnaise, relish, mustard, seafood seasoning and pepper; mix well.
Stuff or pipe into egg whites.
Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Not the traditional taste of deviled eggs. The surprise ingredient is crab, which makes for a delightful change.Modified from: Crabby Deviled Eggs
