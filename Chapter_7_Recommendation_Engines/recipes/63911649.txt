Title:
Puffed Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb butter
1 1/2 cups milk
8 eggs
1/2 cup orange juice
3/4 cup sugar
1 1/3 cup flour
3/4 tsp salt
1 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
{reheat oven to 375 degrees.  While preheating place 3 quart baking dish (lasagna pan) in oven with butter in it to melt.  Be careful not to burn the butter.
Blend milk, eggs, orange juice, sugar, flour and salt in a blender until smooth.  Pour into the baking dish and bake for 30 minutes.
Remove from oven and let rest a few minutes.  Cut in squares and serve with your favorite toppings.
