Title:
Tortellini Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter melted
1 clove garlic
2 1/2 ounce parm cheese grated (2/3 cup)
1/2 tsp pepper
1 can chicken broth
3 tablespoon flour
1 cup milk
16 ounces tortellini

Directions:
combine: butter & flour; whish until smooth, add pressed garlic and milk to butter mixture: whisk well, Microwave covered high 3-4 minutes or until thickened, stiring every 60 seconds. Add cheese whisk until smooth, mix well set aside
combine tortellini and broth in deep covered baker. microwave, covered on hight 12 -15 minutes or until tortellini are tender, stiring ever 4-5 minutes. Add cheese sauce to baker; mix well. Microwave covered on high 2-3 minutes or until heated through.
