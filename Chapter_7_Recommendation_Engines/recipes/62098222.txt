Title:
Veggie Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of refrigerated biscuits
1 bell pepper (any color)
1/2 large onion chopped
1 large tomato chopped
5 green onions chopped
1 lb of bacon cooked till crispy
1 cup of cheddar cheese
1 stick butter melted

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350.  in a large bowl cut or break canned biscuits into about 1" pieces.  Add chopped vegetables, cheese and crumbled bacon.  Add melted butter and mix with hands.  Grease or spray bunt pan or bread pan liberally and add mixture.  Cook till biscuits are brown and cooked through, about 45 mins to an hour.  I've made this recipe with sausage and spicy cheese and served with marinara sauce.  No matter what you add it will come out wonderful!

Submitter: 
Danielle Mark Worrell
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by my mother in law.  It is very versatile and easy!!  Any meat or cheese can be used based on preference!  ENJOY!!
