Title:
BBQ Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of cabbage
5 slices of bacon,diced
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 Tbsp butter
1/2 cup BBQ sauce
sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Core cabbage head.
Fry bacon and onion.
Mix with butter and BBQ sauce.
Put mixture in center of cabbage.
Set foil on BBQ.
Cook on medium heat for 1 hour.
Slice and serve with sour cream.

Submitter: 
CLField
Description: 
A unique and delicous way to have your veggies. Best done on the outdoor grill.
