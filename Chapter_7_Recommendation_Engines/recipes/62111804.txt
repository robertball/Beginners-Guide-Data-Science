Title:
Maple Nut Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. butter
1/3 C. brown sugar
2 tsp. maple extract
8 C honey roast corn, rice, wheat nuts
Optional 1 C. walnuts
pretzels
goldfish

Directions:
Preheat oven 250. Microwave butter, brown sugar and maple flavoring for 30-45 seconds. Put Chex, nuts in pan. Pour butter mixture over and bake 45 min. stirring every 15 min. Mix in pretzels, goldfish and blend again.
