Title:
Squash and Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp oil
2 Cups chopped onions
6 cloves garlic, finely chopped
3 tbsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1 can (28oz) diced tomatos
1 1/2 cups veggie or chicken broth
2 cups cubed peeled butternut squash
1 cup diced green bell pepper
1 cup diced red bell pepper
3 cans (19oz) mixed beans, rinsed and drained
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
1.  Heat oil in dutch oven over medium heat.
2.  Add onions and garlic; cook for 5 minutes.
3.  Stir in chili powder, cumin, salt and red pepper flakes.  Cook, stirring for 1-2 minutes.
4.  Add next 6 ingredients (tomotoes thru beans).  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until veggies are tender.
5.  Chili may be prepared to this point and then refridgerated up to 24 hours.
6.  Stir in cilantro before serving
