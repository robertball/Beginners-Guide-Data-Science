Title:
SWEET POTATO SOUFFLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups mashed sweet potatoes
1 cup white sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
2 eggs ( beaten)
3/4 stick melted butter
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
TOPPING
1 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup flour
1/3 cup melted butter
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Combine all ingredients above. Down to the topping and put in 9 inch baking dish. (spryed with Pam)
Mix topping ingredients together and put on top.
Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Marjorie Taylor
Description: 
I got the recipe from my neighbor after she brought over some and my husband loved it and wanted the recipe. I serve it as a side dish, but its like eating dessert.
