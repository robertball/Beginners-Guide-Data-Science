Title:
Tanimura & Antle Sweet Gem™ Seafood Cocktail Lettuce Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985725.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound small whole shrimp, cooked
1/2 pound imitation crabmeat
1/4 cup cocktail sauce
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
3 tablespoons red onion, diced
2 heads Tanimura and Antle Sweet Gem™ Gourmet Lettuce
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Remove outer leaves from Tanimura & Antle Sweet Gem(tm) lettuce, and set aside. Chop Tanimura & Antle Sweet Gem(tm) lettuce hearts.
Mix shrimp together with imitation crabmeat, cocktail sauce, lemon juice, celery seed, red onion and chopped Tanimura & Antle Sweet Gem(tm) lettuce hearts. Season with pepper to taste and chill for 1 hour.
Serve the seafood cocktail in Tanimura & Antle Sweet Gem(tm) lettuce leaves as an appetizer or luncheon salad.

Submitter: 
Tanimura & Antle®
Description: 
A mixture of lettuce hearts, small cooked shrimp, and imitation crabmeat flavored with lemon juice and cocktail sauce is served on a bed of lettuce leaves. Serve as an appetizer or a luncheon salad.
