Title:
Hot Spinach Red Pepper Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup diced red bell pepper
1/2 cup frozen chopped spinach, thawed
8 oz cream cheese
2 tbsp milk
1/2 cup grated parmesean cheese
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 tsp salt
pinch fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Boil the spinach and red pepper in a cup of water for 10 minutes.  Drain in collander pressing out as much liquid as possible.
Heat the cream cheese and milk in a small saucepan over medium heat on the stove until hot and softened.
Add the spinach and red peppers to the cream cheese and stir well.
Add the remaining ingredients to the cream cheese mixture and combine.
Serve hot with in a bread bowl or with crackers or toasted bread.

Submitter: 
Becky | The Two Bite Club
Description: 
A cheesy and creamy hot dip best served in a bread bowl or spread on toasted bread.
