Title:
Top Secret (CK) Caramel Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Base:
1 cup margarine
1 1/2 cups flour
1 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 tbsp. cocoa
Filling:
1 cup margarine
4 tbsp. flour
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
2 tbsp. corn syrup
1 can sweetened condensed milk (Eagle Brand)
Fudge Frosting:
1/2 cup margarine
3 2/3 cups icing sugar
dash salt
1/2 cup cocoa
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/3 cup water

Directions:
Base:
Cream ingredients together and spread in 10" x 15" greased cookie sheet.
Bake @ 350 x 7 - 10 minutes.
Filling:
Mix margarine, flour, brown sugar, milk and corn syrup. Cook over low heat until dissolved (don't boil).
Pour over base.
Bake @ 350 x 10 min. (Should be bubbling all over.)
Cool. Frost.
Frosting:
In micro., melt margarine.
Add cocoa, stirring until smooth and well blended.
Add icing sugar, vanilla & salt alternately with water; beat with mixer until creamy & smooth.
