Title:
Cookie Dough Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 blocks (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
1/4 c. butter, softened
1/4 c. powdered sugar
1/3 c. brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
Dash of salt
2/3 c mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Beat all with mixer.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Serve at room temp, but store in fridge.
Tastes best if make a day ahead.

Submitter: 
chefmom
Description: 
Great dip with pretzel sticks (thick ones), nilla wafers or ginger snaps.
