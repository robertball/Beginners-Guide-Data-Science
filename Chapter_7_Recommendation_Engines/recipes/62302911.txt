Title:
Pepsi Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless, skinless chicken breast cubed
1 tablespoon on extra virgin olive oil
salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste
12 oz of pepsi (1 can)
20 oz of ketchup

Directions:
In a large saute pan warm extra viring oive oil over a medium heat. Add cubed chicken and cook to golden brown seasoning with salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste.
Coat chicken throughly with ketchup then cover in pepsi. Let cook over low heat stiring occasionally for 1-1&1/2 hours or until sauce is thickened. Serve over steamed white rice.

Submitter: 
Kat
Description: 
Okay so yes this sounds gross, but there is something so yummy about the way pepsi and ketchup caramlize together for just the right hint of spice and sweetness
