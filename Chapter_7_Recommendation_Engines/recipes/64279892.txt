Title:
Ruby Red Slipper Brandied Cranberry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ( 12 ounce pkg.) fresh cranberries, washed
1 Cup or less sugar
2 Tbsp. good brandy or cognac  (St Remy VSOP)

Directions:
Preheat the oven 350 F
Place the cranberries in a 8x8 pyrex baking dish and sprinkle with the sugar.
Bake at 350 F. for 40-45 minutes then remove from the oven to cool.
After cooling for 1/2 hour, mix in the brandy or cognac
Pour into a glass dish and cover with a light sprinkling of sugar. optional
Chill or serve at room temperature

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
This is the perfect cranberry recipe to serve during the holidays or when cranberries are in season. 
Serve with turkey as a garnish, or as a salad or over cream cheese for appetizer/snack. Try it as a toping for vanilla ice cream.The possibilities are up to you.  Creates a beautiful ruby red  presentation and is  really delicious.
