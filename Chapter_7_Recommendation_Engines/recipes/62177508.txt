Title:
Chewy coconut cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C brown sugar
1/2c sugar
1/2C shortening
1 egg
1 1/4C flour
1/2tsp baking soda
1/4tsp salt
1 1/2C coconut (flacked)
1/2tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix sugars and shortening and cream together. Then add the egg and mix well. Then add in the flour, soda and salt. Once combined add the vanilla and coconut.  Drop by teaspoon full on cookie sheet and bake at 375 for 10min.
