Title:
Caesar-ish Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	Romaine lettuce
•	Croutons
•	Parmesan Cheese
•	Pepper
•	Fresh Garlic
•	Good Olive Oil
•	Lemon
•	Worcestershire Sauce

Directions:
Put a few tablespoons of olive oil in bowl
Press a couple hunks of garlic and ground into olive oil with fork - let marinade for 10 + minutes
Wash lettuce, drain water and cut
Add lettuce to bowl with juice of one half lemon, few shakes of worcestershire sauce, parmesean cheese, fresh pepper and top and serve with croutons or homemade croutons if you want to over achieve.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
BUSYBEE25
Description: 
Tasty, easy, fresh salad. Lighter than a regular caesar, but equally as tasty! Be sure to use fresh Parmesan.
