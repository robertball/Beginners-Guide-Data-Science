Title:
crispy wontons with sweet chilli sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz wontons
1 pt vegetable oil
for the sauce
6 oz large fresh red chillies, finely chopped
3 T garlic
1 T sugar
1 T rice vinegar
1 T fish sauce
1 T vegetable oil
5 oz water
For filling
4 oz prawns, chopped
12 oz minced pork
2 t salt
1 t pepper
2 T garlic
3 T chopped onion
2 T fish sauce
1 t sugar
3 T cilantro
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Bring all chilli sauce to boil, turn down to low, cover and simmer 15 minutes. Remove from heat and cool. Puree until smooth, add more salt and cool.
Mix all filling ingredients and chill 15 min. Fill wontons and fry
