Title:
Cona Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1 can of sliced pineapples, with juice
4 slices of provolone cheese
4 slices of thick cut ham
2 tbs brown sugar
4 tbs soy sauce

Directions:
Tenderize chicken.
Combine the brown sugar, soy sauce and half the juice from the canned pineapple into a large ziplock bag and add chicken.
Allow chicken to marinate for one hour.
In a large skillet cook chicken until done.
Line a large casserole dish with aluminum foil.
Place chicken in the casserole dish and layer with ham, cheese and lots of pineapple.
Bake in the oven at 350Â° until the cheese is melted.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Angie
Description: 
Pineapple, provolone cheese and ham make a glorious mount for this Hawiian style chicken dish. Serve with loaded, cheesy mashed potatoes for a winning combo! My husband said he knew he would marry me after I made this dish for him!
