Title:
Mama Mia!  It's A Zuppa de Lasagna! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound sweet italian sausage, (casings removed if using links)
1/2 pound hot italian sausage, casings removed if using links)
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 small green bell pepper, chopped
1 small red bell pepper, chopped
1 small sweet onion, chopped
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon dried basil
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 32 oz box low sodium beef broth
2 15 oz cans diced tomatoes with juices, italian style if available
1 15 oz can tomato sauce
salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 cups dry curly pasta
Parmesan cheese rind (optional)
Salt and pepper
Shredded  mozzarella or Parmesan cheese for topping (optional)

Directions:
1.  Place sausage in a large soup pot and cook over mediumhigh heat until the sausage is browned and crumbled.  Using a slotted spoon, remove sausage and add the onions, peppers, and garlic to the pan.  Saut√© on medium heat until soft and translucent, about  5 minutes.  Return the sausage to the pan  and combine.
2. Stir dried herbs, sugar, broth, tomatoes, and tomato sauce into the meat mixture.  Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.  Lower heat and simmer 20 minutes.
3. Add pasta and Parmesan rind to the soup and continue to simmer until pasta is soft, about 20 minutes more.
4. Taste, and salt and pepper accordingly. Remove Parmesan rind if used.
5. Ladle into soup bowls and top with cheese, if desired.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This is a soupy take on everyone's favorite italian dish, lasagna, but with much less work.  All of the warm, comforting flavors are still there, though.
