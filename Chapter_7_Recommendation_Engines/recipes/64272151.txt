Title:
Parmesan Crusted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1922452.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Mayo
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
4 chicken breast halves
4 teaspoons Italian seasoned bread crumbs

Directions:
Combine Mayo with cheese.
Arrange chicken on baking sheet and top with Mayo mixture.
Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
Bake 20 minutes at 425 F.
