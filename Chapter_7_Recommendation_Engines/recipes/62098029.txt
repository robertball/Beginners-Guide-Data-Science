Title:
Spicy Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups vegetable oil
2/3 cup all purpose flour
1 T salt
1 T course black pepper
1 T white pepper
1 tsp cayenne pepper
2 tsp paprika
3 eggs
1 frying chicken with skin, cut up

Directions:
Heat oil over medium heat in deep fryer or wide deep pan on stovetop
In re-sealable, gallon sized, plastic bag combine flour, salt, peppers & paprika
Break eggs into a separate shallow bowl & beat until blended
Check to make sure oil is hot by dropping flour into oil. If it bubble around the flour it's ready.
Dip each piece of chicken in egg mixture, then place 2 at a time into the flour mixture bag & shake bag to coat.
Drop each piece into the hot oil & fry for 15 to 25 minutes or until dark golden brown.
Remove chicken from oil and place on paper towels to drain oil.
