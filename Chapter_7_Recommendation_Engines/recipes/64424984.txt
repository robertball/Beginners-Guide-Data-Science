Title:
Paleo Trail Mix Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2312382.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup almond meal
1/3 cup raisins
1/3 cup "Enjoy Life" chocolate chips
1/4 shredded unsweetened coconut
2 Tbsps sunflower seeds
1/4 cup honey
3 Tbsps coconut oil, melted

Directions:
Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl. Mix. Add melted coconut oil and liquid honey. Combine well. Scoop out tablespoonfuls and put on ungreased cookie sheet, pressing the scooops down slightly. Bake in 350 degree oven for 8=9-10 minutes or until edges are golden. Let rest ?cool down before enjoying. Makes 15 cookies.

Submitter: 
LatinaCook
Description: 
This is my favorite Paleo cookie recipe. It is so easy and tasty that everyone likes it, even if not following the Paleo diet. No refined ingredients and a very satisfying flavor and texture!
