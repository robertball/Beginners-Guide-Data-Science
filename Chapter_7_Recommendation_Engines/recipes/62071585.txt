Title:
BLUEBERRY CRUNCH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups blueberries fresh or frozen
1 (20 oz.) can crushed pineapple with
juice
3/4 cup sugar (reserving 1/4 cup)
1 box yellow dry cake mix
1 cup chopped pecans
1 stick of melted butter

Directions:
Mix pineapple, blueberries, and 1/2 cup sugar.  Spread in 13x9 pan sprayed with Pam.  Sprinkle dry cake mix over top. Sprinkle chopped pecans and remaining 1/4 cup sugar over top and drizzle melted butter over top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.  Poke holes in top for air bubble to escape.  Return to oven for 10 minutes or until Golden brown.
