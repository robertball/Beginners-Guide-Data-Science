Title:
Baked Salmon with Dijon & Garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients for Baked Salmon
1.5 lbs salmon (this was wild sockeye salmon)
2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
2 large OR 3 small cloves of garlic, pressed
1.5 teaspoons Dijon mustard (grey poupon)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1/8 cup mild olive oil
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
Lemon slices (mostly for effect

Directions:
How to Make the Best Baked Salmon:
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F.
Line rimmed baking dish with foil
Combine all ingredients except salmon into bowl and mix well
Generously brush all sides of your salmon with the sauce and top with fresh lemon slices.
Bake at 450 degrees F for 12-15 minutes or until just cooked through. Donâ€™t over-cook or your fish will be dry.
