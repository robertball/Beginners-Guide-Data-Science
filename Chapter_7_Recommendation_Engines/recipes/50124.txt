Title:
Caramel Cappuccino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992863.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup fat free milk
1/2 cup vanilla fat-free frozen yogurt
1 packet Cappuccino NESTLE® CARNATION® INSTANT BREAKFAST®
1 tablespoon caramel ice cream topping or syrup

Directions:
PLACE milk, frozen yogurt, Carnation Instant Breakfast and caramel topping in blender; cover. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Carnation® Instant Breakfast™
Description: 
If you haven't tried the taste of caramel and cappuccino together yet, you don't know what you're missing. This Caramel Cappuccino blends together so quickly, you'll want to make this creamy drink as often as possible.
