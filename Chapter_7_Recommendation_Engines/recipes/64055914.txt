Title:
German Oven Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups milk
3/4 cup unsifted flour
1/3 cup plus 2 TBSP sugar
3 extra large eggs
1/4 tsp salt
3 cups fresh strawberries, halved
8 oz sour cream
1/4 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Put milk, flour, 1/3 cup sugar, eggs and salt in container of blender. Process until smooth. Have ready strawberries which have been tossed with remaining sugar. In a serving bowl, mix together sour cream and brown sugar until smooth.
Place butter in oven safe skillet and melt butter in the oven. Remove pan. Butter should be sizzling. Swish butter around plate to grease sides. Increase oven to 450 degrees. Immediately pour egg mixture all at once into sizzling butter. Bake 20 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees. Bake 8 to 10 minutes longer or until golden brown. Edges will puff up and pancake will form a well in the center. Spoon strawberries into center of pancake. To serve, cut in wedges. Serve with sour cream mixture to be used as a topping.
Following are filling variations that  may be used instead of strawberries.
Cinnamon-Apple filling
3 medium apples, peeled and thinly slices
1 1/2 tsp lemon juice
3 TBSP brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
3 TBSP butter
Toss together apples and lemon juice. Mis together brown sugar and cinnamon. Melt butter in a shallow pan. Add apples. Simmer mixture 15 to 20 minutes or until apples are barely tender. Lightly stir occasionally.
Orange-Nut Filling
1 cup orange marmalade
1/2 cup small pineapple chunks, drained
2 TBSP white wine
1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
1/2 cup toasted coconut
In medium pan, mix together marmalade, pineapple and wine. Simmer, covered about five minutes. Spread on pancake and sprinkle with nuts and coconut.
