Title:
Kilohana Hawaiian Chocolate chunk cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/497381.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c. flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. sugar
1 c. sofened butter
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 c. semi-sweet chocolate chopped into chunks
1 c. macadamia nuts
(you can also do, 1 1/2 c. chocolate chunks and 1/2 c. macadamia nuts.)

Directions:
preheat oven to 300.
mix butter and sugars, then the eggs and vanilla. Next add the baking soda salt and flour. Finally add the chocolate chunks and macadamia nuts. Scoop dough, in mounds, on a lightly greased cookie sheet (preferably non-stick spray).  Bake for 22-25 min or until golden brown.
