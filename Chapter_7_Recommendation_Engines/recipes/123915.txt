Title:
No-Bake Cornflake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cornflakes
1 1/2 cups flaked coconut
3/4 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1 1/2 cups sugar
Dash salt
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cornflakes, coconut and pecans; set aside.  Place remaining ingredients in a 1-qt. saucepan. Bring mixture to 240 degrees F (soft-ball stage), stirring constantly. Add syrup mixture to dry ingredients; stir well. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto waxed paper.

Submitter: 
Denise  Marnell
Description: 
I grew up on a farm where we hand-milked cows and had plenty of milk and cream to use for cooking. Sometimes we'd substitute light cream for the evaporated milk in this recipe. We'd rarely let these cookies cool before sampling them, and a batch never lasted a day!
