Title:
Applesauce Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1 3/4 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. nutmeg
1/8 tsp. ground cloves
1/2 tsp. salt
1 3/4 cups applesauce

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar together.
Add egg.
Mix dry ingredients together in a separate bowl or measuring cup.
Add dry ingredients to butter/sugar/egg mixture alternately with applesauce. Don't over-mix.
Bake in greased muffin tin @ 350 x 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
My kids love these moist muffins that have a bit of spice flavor.
