Title:
Plum-Good Sausage and Meatballs` Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10-12 oz jar plum jam or preserves
1 18 ounce bottle barbecue sauce
1 16 ounce link cooked jalepeno smoked sausage or smoked sausage, sliced into bite size pieces
1 16 ounce package of frozen cooked meatballs, thawed.

Directions:
Combine jam and barbecue sauce in a 4 quart slow cooker. Add the sausage and meatballs. Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 5-6 hours.
Serve meatballs with toothpicks.
