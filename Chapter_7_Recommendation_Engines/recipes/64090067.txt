Title:
Creamy Banana Strawberry Split Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1254769.jpg
Ingredients:
3 ice cubes
1 banana, frozen
3/4 cup strawberries, frozen or fresh
1 cup almond milk
1 scoop vanilla protein powder
1 teaspoon local honey
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
2 teaspoons ground flax/chia seeds mix

Directions:
Add all ingredients into your blender and press the button to create your smoothie then go outside in a nice comfy chair, sit back and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
My Creamy Banana Strawberry Split Smoothie is filled with protein and chia seeds for a great healthy drink!
