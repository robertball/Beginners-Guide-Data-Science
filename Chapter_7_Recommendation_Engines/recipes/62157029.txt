Title:
Luscious Lemon Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ cups flour
1 stick butter, melted
1/4 tsp. salt
1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 cup powdered sugar
1 (12 oz.) pkg. frozen whipped topping, thawed, divided
3 packages lemon instant pudding and pie filling
4 ½ cups cold milk
2 tsp. lemon zest

Directions:
First Layer:
For crust, combine flour, butter and salt; mix well. Pat into a 9 x 13 baking pan to form crust. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Cool completely.
Second Layer:
Combine cream cheese powdered sugar and 1/2 of whipped topping. Spread over cooled crust.
Third Layer:
Stir pudding into milk until well combined; refrigerate until set: about 5 minutes. Spread over cream cheese layer.
Fourth layer:
Spread remaining whipped topping. Srinkle lemon zest over all. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Gail Cobile
Description: 
A delightful dessert that adds lemony coolness to top off a summer meal.
