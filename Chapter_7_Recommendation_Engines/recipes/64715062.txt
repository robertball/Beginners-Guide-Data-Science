Title:
Tom Kha Gai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp vegetable oil
1 1/2 Tbs Thai red curry paste
2 cans chicken stock
2 chicken bullion cubes
2 cans unsweetened coconut milk
1 large lemongrass stalk trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
6 thin slices fresh galangal or ginger
2 Thai chilies seeded and cut into thin slices (one red and one green)
3 Tbs fresh lime juice
2 Tbs soy sauce
1 lb chicken breast cut into thin strips
1/4 cup fresh Thai basil cut into thin strips (reserve some for garnishing)

Directions:
Warm the oil in a soup pot over medium heat. Add the curry paste, stirring until fragrant (about 1 min).
Add the chicken stock, coconut milk, lemongrass, ginger, chilies, lime juice, and soy sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
Add the chicken strips and simmer for 15 minutes.
Add the 1/4 cup basil and simmer for another 5 minutes adding salt to taste
Remove the lemongrass and ginger and serve immediately, garnished with fresh basil leaves or cilantro.
