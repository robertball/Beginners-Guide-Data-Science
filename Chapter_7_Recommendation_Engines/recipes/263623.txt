Title:
Spicy Basil Mayo Summer Condiment Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5284966.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
4 teaspoons dried basil

Directions:
Combine mayonnaise, sriracha sauce, and basil in a bowl. Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
BMG
Description: 
Nice condiment for pork sandwiches, fries, burgers, or any summer foods!
