Title:
Best Foods Honey Mustard Chicken Fingers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
INGREDIENTS
6 Tbsp. Hellmann's® or Best Foods® Light Mayonnaise
1 Tbsp. Hellmann's® or Best Foods® Honey Mustard
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
1-1/2 cups finely crushed corn flakes
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat oven to 425°. Combine Hellmann's® or Best Foods® Light Mayonnaise, Creamy Dijon Mustard and honey in medium bowl; reserve 1/2 for dipping.
Add chicken to remaining mayonnaise mixture; stir to coat. Mix corn flakes with Parmesan Cheese, then roll chicken in crumbs.
Arrange on ungreased baking sheet. Bake 10 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked. Serve with reserved honey mustard sauce.
