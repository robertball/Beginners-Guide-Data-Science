Title:
Baked French Toast with Pecan Streusal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/381593.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz) loaf French Bread, cut into 1/2 inch slices
8 large eggs
3 cups half and half
3/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
2 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp salt
Pecan Streusal:
1 cup butter softened
3/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
2 Tbsp all purpose flour
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 cup chopped pecans
Combine first 4 ingredients in a small bowl; stir in pecans.  Makes 3 cups.

Directions:
Arrange bread slices in a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
Combine eggs and next 5 ingredients in a large bowl, whisking until well blended.  Pour egg mixture over bread.  Cover and chill 8 hours or overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Sprinkle Pecan Streusel over bread slices.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until slightly puffed and golden.  Serve with Maple Syrup.  Makes 8 to 10 servings.
