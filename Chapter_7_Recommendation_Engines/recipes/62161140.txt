Title:
Healthy Pasta-sauce Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 pounds ground turkey
1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
1 yellow or red bell pepper, finely chopped
1/2 tablespoon dried oregano
1/2 tablespoon dried basil
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1/4 cup tomato-based pasta sauce
Pinch of hot sauce
Salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with foil.
Combine all ingredients together, mix with a wooden spoon or hands until everything just combined.
On the baking sheet, shape the meet mixture into a loaf. Bake in preheated oven for 50-60 minutes.

Submitter: 
windband
Description: 
A Meatloaf made of ground turkey, bell pepper, onion, garlic, tomato-based pasta sauce, and herbs. No egg and no breadcrumb. Very flavorful and healthy!
