Title:
Valentine Kiss Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351049.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup margarine
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
1 (1lb) bag chocolate kisses- or can use 3 chocolate
chips per cookie instead

Directions:
1. Cream peanut butter, margarine, and sugar.
2. Beat in eggs.
3. Combine flour, baking powder, and cinnamon and add to mixture gradually.
4. Chill dough for 30 minutes.
5. Wrap about one tsp dough around each kiss or choc. chips.
6. Place on cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
My husband loves peanut butter, chocolate and cinnamon, so these are perfect!
