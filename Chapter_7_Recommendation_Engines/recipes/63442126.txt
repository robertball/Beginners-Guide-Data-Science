Title:
Broiled Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 Pork Chops
Salt
Pepper
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Set oven to broil, low.
Salt and pepper pork chops
Spray each pork chop with cooking spray, both sides
Broil until golden brown, flip and brown opposite side.
