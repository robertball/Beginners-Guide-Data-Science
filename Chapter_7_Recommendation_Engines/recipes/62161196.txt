Title:
Chicken Fried Steak and gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cube steaks
1 pkg crushed cracker crumbs
1/2 cup mashed potato flakes
1 cup flour
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste
oil
gravy:
1 can of evaporated milk
1/4 cup of reserved oil from cooked steaks
2 to 3 tbs of flour
pepper and salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in fryer
Tenderize steaks using a meat mallet or prick with a fork sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Place flour in a pie plate season with salt and pepper next whisk eggs and milk in another pie plate also seasoning with salt and pepper  forming a assembly line last Pour cracker crumbs and mash potatoes flakes in a another pie plate season with salt and pepper
Dredge steak in flour shake off excess than milk/egg mixture let excess drip back in plate  and last press into cracker/potato mix place on plate follow steps with the reaming steaks
gently place each steak in deep fryer cooking around seven minutes or until center is no longer pink and steak is crispy
For Gravy
Using a metal soup ladle scoop up oil from fryer about 1/4 a cup place in  sauce pan heat on med to med hi wisk in flour until paste forms giving a bit to let the flour taste cook out pour in slowly while whisking to prevent lumps from forming evaporated milk bring to a boil then reduce heat whisking the whole time and salt and pepper to taste.
Note:If it becomes to thick add additional milk or water if is not thick enough boil a little more while whisking or add a tablespoon of corn starch to two tablespoons of cold water wish and add to gravy while whisking turn up heat let boil reduce this will thicken it also.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Laymance
Description: 
My favorite recipe for chicken fried steak!! Make sure you tenderize steaks well.
