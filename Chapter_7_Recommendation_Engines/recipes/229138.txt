Title:
Eyeball Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F3301127.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons peanut butter
2 slices bread
8  green olives, sliced

Directions:
Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread; top with green olives. Place second piece of bread atop the filling to make a sandwich.

Submitter: 
maddie
Description: 
Great for Halloween or whenever, this peanut butter sandwich with a twist is fun and delicious. Sliced green olives look like eyeballs and taste like mmmmm...
