Title:
Chicken & Bok Choy Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp oil
1 yellow onion, diced
2 garlic cloves, minced
6 large bok choy stalks with leaves, finely chopped
4 carrots, sliced
2 celery stalks, sliced
6 small potatoes, diced
4 tsp Better Than Bouillon (or 4 cans low-sodium chicken broth)
2 chicken breast halves

Directions:
Heat oil in a large stockpot. Add onion & garlic, saute until translucent.
Add 6 cups water & bouillon (or chicken broth in place of water & bouillon), bok choy, carrots, celery, and potatoes. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat, simmer 10 minutes. Cut chicken into 1/2-inch pieces.
Add chicken, simmer 10 more minutes. Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
I bought Bok Choy with no idea how to use it. Threw it in a simple chicken & veggie soup, and the family loved it!
