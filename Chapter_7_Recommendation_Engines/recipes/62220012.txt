Title:
Chicken Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup hot water
1/4 cup Kraft Teriyaki Barbecue Sauce
1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
2 Tbsp. Grey Poupon Honey Dijon Mustard
1 lb. bonless skinless chicken breast halves, cut into 12 thin strips

Directions:
Mix water, barbeque sauce, peanut butter, soy sauce, cilantro and mustard in medium bowl with wire whisk.
Pour half of the mixture into large resealable plastic bag.  Add chicken;  seal bag.  Shake gently to coat chicken.  Refrigerate 1-2 hours to marinate.  Refrigerate remaining sauce mixture for dipping.
Preheat grill to medium-high heat.  Thread chicken on to 12 skewers in ribbon fashion;  discard any remaining marinated in bag.
Grill 5-7 min. or until chicken is cooked through.  Serve with the reserved dipping sauce.
