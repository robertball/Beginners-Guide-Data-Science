Title:
Lime Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups jasmine rice
4 cups chicken broth
3 T lime juice
2 T butter
alternative ingredients
3 T lemon juice substitue instead of lime
3 T greek seasoning

Directions:
cook in rice cooker according to directions
or
place in sauce pan
bring to boil
reduce to low
simmer 20 min
fluff with fork
