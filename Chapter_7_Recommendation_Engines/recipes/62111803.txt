Title:
Healthy Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup steal cut oats
1/4 c oat bran
2 1/2 tbls ground flaxseed
2 1/2 tbls wheat germ
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 lrg ripe banana, mashed
1/4 c applesauce
1/4 c brown sugar, packed
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
6 oz package of chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F.
In medium bowl, combine flour, oats and oat bran, flaxseed, wheat germ, baking soda and salt; set aside.
In large mixing bowl, beat banana, applesauce and brown sugar until creamy. Beat in egg and vanilla until just incorporated. Stir dry mixture into wet mixture until well blended; add chocolate chips.
Spoon onto greased cookie and bake 10-12 minutes. Cool on pan for 2 minutes before tranfering to wire rack; enjoy.
