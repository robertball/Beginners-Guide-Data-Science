Title:
Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Butter
1/4 Cup Powdered Sugar
1 Cup Flour
TOPPING
2 eggs
1 tbsp Lemon Juice
1 Cup Sugar
2 Tbsp Flour
1/4 Tsp Salt
1/2 Tsp Baking Powder

Directions:
Mix Butter, powdered sugar and flour for crust and pat into 8 inch pan
bake at 350 til brown.
Make Topping
Beat 2 eggs with 1 cup sugar till fluffy and add lemon juice
Fold in flour, salt and baking powder
Spread on to p f crust and bake at 350 till set (about 20-25 min)
Let cool and dust wit hpotdered sugar
Cut into bars

Lemony filling with a pastry crust
