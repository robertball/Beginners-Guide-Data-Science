Title:
Minty Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c melted butter
2 1/2 c sugar
2 beaten eggs
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp peppermint extract
3 drops red food coloring (can color 1/2 green too)
4 c flour
1/2 c sugar for dipping
DIP:
2 c choc chips
1 stick butter

Directions:
Melt butter, add sugar and stir.  Let cool then add eggs, soda, salt and extract, stirring.  Add food coloring to desired color.  Add flour one cup at a time.  Dough will be stiff.  Chill 2 hours or overnight.  Preheat oven to 350.  Place 1/2 c sugar in bowl.  Line sheet with parchment paper.  Roll dough into walnut sized balls, roll in sugar, place on cookie sheet and flatten slightly.  Bake 10-12 min.  Let cool 2 min then slide parchment to cooling rack.
DIP:
Melt chips and butter in microwave, stirring to melt chips.  Keep dip hot so it is thin.  Dip 1/3 to 1/4 of each cookie and place on parchment paper to cool and harden.  When dry, store btw wax paper in cool place.
