Title:
Wilma's Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123213.jpg
Ingredients:
2 beaten eggs
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup vinegar
miracle whip
sweet pickle juice
salted, boiled potatoes, cut in chunks(about 2 1/2 lbs)
boiled eggs, chopped
celery, chopped
chopped onions

Directions:
Mix eggs and sugar together well.
Add vinegar and mix.  Cook over medium heat until thickened.
Add miracle whip and sweet pickle juice until desired consistency and sweetness.
Mix well with potatoes, boiled egg, celery and onion.
