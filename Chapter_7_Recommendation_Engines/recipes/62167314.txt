Title:
Southwestern Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. sour cream
1 c. mayonnaise
2 (10 oz) cans mexi-corn
1 bunch green onions
2 c. shredded cheddar
1 can Rotel

Directions:
Drain corn and Rotel.
Thinly slice onions.
In a medium bowl mix mayo and sour cream.
Add the onions, corn, cheese and Rotel.
Stir well.
Chill 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
jlsramek821
Description: 
This dip is a favorite at potlucks, I always get recipe requests. I usually serve it with tortilla chips.
