Title:
Peanut Butter Logs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980269.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup JIF® Crunchy Peanut Butter
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, softened
1 1/4 cups sifted powdered sugar
3 cups crisp rice cereal
Chopped peanuts

Directions:
Blend JIF(R) and butter or margarine; stir in powdered sugar. Add cereal; mix well, crushing cereal slightly.
Shape into three 7 x 1 1/4-inch logs. Cover with peanuts. Wrap in waxed paper or clear plastic wrap; chill till firm.
To serve, carefully cut into 1/2-inch-thick slices.

Submitter: 
JIF®
Description: 
Let your kids help you cook up this simple candy dish, as much fun to make as it is to eat.
