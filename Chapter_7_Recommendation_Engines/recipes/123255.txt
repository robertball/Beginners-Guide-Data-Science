Title:
English Muffins with Bacon Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976546.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
4 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
4 English muffins, split

Directions:
In a bowl, combine butter and mustard; stir in bacon. Toast the English muffins; spread with bacon butter. Refrigerate any leftover butter.

Submitter: 
Edna  Hoffman
Description: 
For a change from the usual breakfast bread. Edna toasts up a batch of English Muffins with Bacon Butter. 'The hint of Dijon mustard in this hearty spread really dresses up the English muffins,' she comments.
