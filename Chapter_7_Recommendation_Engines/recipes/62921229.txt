Title:
Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound 80% lean ground beef
1.5 tsp minced garlic
*** Taco Seasoning ***
1 Tbsp chili powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/8 tsp cayenne chili powder
1/4 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp paprika
1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
**                  **
1 cup fresh salsa
1/4 cup water
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1/2 Tbsp chili powder
1/4 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 can chili beans, drained
1 can yellow corn, drained
3 cups crushed plain tortilla chips
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup lettuce
1/2 cup chopped tomato
1/4 cup sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
In a deep skillit, brown ground beef and garlic over medium heat (add onions if desired). Drain. Stir in taco seasoning ingredients and salsa. Simmer for 10 min over medium to low heat.
In a separate bowl, whisk together cream of chicken soup, sour cream, chili powder, paprika, cumin, and black pepper. Stir in beans and corn. Add to meat mixture in deep skillet and heat through over medium low heat (about 5 minutes).
Into a 13 x 9 in pan, spoon enough of the beef mixture to cover the bottom of the pan. Arrange 1/2 of the crushed tortilla chips on top of meat mixture and sprinkle a layer of 1 cup cheese. Repeat layer.
Bake at 375 degrees for 40 minutes. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
Garnish with lettuce, tomato, and sour cream.
