Title:
Buttermilk Sauce (caramel) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup buttermilk or sour cream
1/2 cup butter
2 tablespoons maple syrup
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large saucepan, combine sugar, buttermilk, butter, maple syrup, add baking soda at the end of boiling; boil for 7 minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat and sitr in vanilla.  Spoon over pancakes or bread puddings, or ice cream, etc.

Submitter: 
Mywemans
Description: 
This is a great breakfast item, now a tradition for my family. It tastes like French toast without the bread!Modified from: German Pancake with Buttermilk Sauce
