Title:
Hoagie Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 pound Ham
1/4 pound Turkey Breast
1/4 pound Genoa Salami
1/4 pound Provolone Cheese
1/2 cup Red Onion, finely diced
1/2 cup Plum Tomato, seeded and finely diced
1/2 cup sliced Hot Banana Peppers
1/2 head Iceberg Lettuce, diced
3/4 cup Mayonnaise
1 1/2 Tbsp Red Wine Vinaegar
1 1/2 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 1/2 tsp Dried Oregano
1/2 tsp Black Pepper
2 large loaves Italian Bread

Directions:
Add the Meats, Cheese, Onion, Tomato, Banana Peppers and Lettuce to a large bowl and mix.
In a medium bowl mix the Mayonnaise, Vinegar, Olive Oil, Oregano and Black Pepper until well combined.
Add the prepared dressing to the ingredients in the large bowl. Fold until evenly coated.
Carve out the center of 1 bread loaf to make a bowl. Fill with the dip.
Cut the carved out center and the second loaf into bite size pieces and serve with the dip.

Description: 
All the deliciousness of an Italian Hoagie mixed up into a fabulous dip! Perfect for any picnic, party or  tailgate. This is sure to be a hit!
