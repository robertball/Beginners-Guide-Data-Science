Title:
Burning Blackberry Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3852086.jpg
Ingredients:
1 oz. Sky blood orange vodka
1/2 oz. Cointreau
1/2 oz. Monin habanero lime
1/2 oz. lime juice
2 blackberries

Directions:
Crush the blackberries in the bottom of a martini glass. In a shaker combine other contents with crushed ice. Shake well and pour into glass. Enjoy.
