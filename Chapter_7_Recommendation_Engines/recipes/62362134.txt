Title:
Bread and Butter Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups granulated sugar
3/4 cup soft butter
16 slices white bread
2 cups powdered sugar
5 cups milk
4 eggs
1 tsp nutmeg
2 tsp cinnamon
Topping: cinnamon/sugar(optional)

Directions:
Cream together butter and granulated sugar.
spread on bread slices and place bread in a greased 9 x13 baking dish.
mix remaining ingredients and pour over bread. let mixture sit for 10 minutes untill all the bread has soaked up the egg mixture, mash the bread with a fork, bread and egg mixture will be "soupy".  sprinkle the top with  cinnamon/sugar mix .bake in the oven 350 degrees for 50-60 minutes top will be golden brown and crunchy while the middle is soft and gooey. serve warm with whipped cream and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Kristy Guillotte
Description: 
this is a cross between a custard and bread pudding. it is so sweet and yummy.
