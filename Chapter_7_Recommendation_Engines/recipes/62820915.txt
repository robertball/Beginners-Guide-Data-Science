Title:
Cilantro Lime Grilled Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/669030.jpg
Ingredients:
1 block firm or extra firm tofu, well-pressed
1 T. olive oil
1/4 cup lime juice
2 cloves garlic, minced
5 T. chopped cilantro
2 t. chili powder
1/4 t. cayenne pepper
salt & pepper to taste
bamboo skewers

Directions:
Slice tofu into 4 thick slabs or chop into cubes and place on bamboo skewers.
Whisk together remaining ingredients and brush over tofu, coating well.  Allow to marinate in the refrigerator for at least two hours, or overnight.
Grill until just blackened, brushing occasionally with lime/cilantro mixture.

Submitter: 
Smitty
Description: 
This is my absolute favorite way to eat tofu.  The marinade is delicious and extremely flavorful.  Serve with grilled vegetables or in a tortilla.
