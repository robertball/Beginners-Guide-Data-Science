Title:
Chipotle Orange Marinated Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup orange juice
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1 cup white wine
2 T chopped fresh cilantro
2 chopped garlic cloves
1 chopped chipotle
salt and freshly ground pepper
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Directions:
In small saucepan reduce citrus juice and white wine. Add garlic, chipotle and salt. Cool add cilantro. Pour marinade over chicken in a bowl, coat well. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours. Cooked in skillet inside about 8 minutes per side. Would be great on the grill.

Submitter: 
Cat Hill
Description: 
A spicy citrus marinade - orange juice, lemon juice and white wine , and chopped chipotle.Modified from: Chipotle-Citrus Marinated Chicken
