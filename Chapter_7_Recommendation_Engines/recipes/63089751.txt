Title:
Peanut Butter Buddies Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/781905.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Package Wafer Cakes (Donia or other imported)(180g/6.3 oz.)
1 1/3 cup melted butter
4 cups powdered sugar
2 cups peanut butter (creamy)
24 oz. chocolate almond bark (melted)

Directions:
Peanut Butter Spread:
In a mixing bowl, add melted butter, powdered sugar and peanut butter.
Blend on low speed, scraping down the sides until fully blended.
Beat on medium high for 10 minutes. Scrape bowl and ready for spreading!
Assembly: You will repeat steps of layering wafer cake and peanut butter spread.
Line your table with wax paper.
Lay first wafer cake down (diamond shaped up)
Spread Peanut Butter Spread to wafer cake generously edge to edge. (about 1/2 inch through out the cake)
Next, add layer of wafer cake again.
Repeat spread of peanut butter spread to layer.
Add the third layer of wafer cake.
Spread final layer of peanut butter spread.
Top with the final layer of wafer cake onto the peanut butter cake. (diamond shape up)
Scrape and smooth sides of cake if any peanut butter spread spilled from layering.
Transfer cake to refridgerator to chill for 30 minutes.
After chill, cut cake lengthwise and cut again widthwise 12 times. (gives you 24 bars total)
Melt chocolate bark in microwave per directions of package.
Spread two teaspoons of melted chocolate on each bar, covering it completely.
Let it set and turn upside down to spread more chocolate on bottoms of bars!
Chill in refridgerator till set and serve!
*chill time is cooking time
**Store in air-tight container refrigerated.
