Title:
French Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter
4-6 onions, peeled and thinly sliced
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer (Guinness)
2 Cans Beef Broth
2 Cans Beef Consomme'
l T. Sugar
l T. Flour
Splash of Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
12 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 bay leaf
6 slices French bread, toasted
3 cups shredded Gruyere cheese

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, melt butter.  Stir and cook onions in butter until golden brown.  Add l T. sugar to onions while browning.  Stir in lT. flour just before onion is done. 15 minutes.
Stir in the garlic and cook 1 minute.  Pour in the beer and beef broth and beef consomme. Season with mustard, thyme, black pepper and the bay leaf.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer 30 minutes.  Add a splash of Worcestershire.
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Remove bay leaf from soup and ladle soup into a large casserole or individual ovenproof bowls, leaving about 1 inch of room at the top of the dish or dishes.  Float slices of toasted bread on the soup and pile generously with Gruyere.
Bake in preheated oven 10 minutes, until cheese is lightly browned and bubbly.
