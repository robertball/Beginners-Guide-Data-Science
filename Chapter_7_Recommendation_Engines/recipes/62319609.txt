Title:
Chicken Florentine Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/4 cup butter
3 teaspoons minced garlic
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1/2 cup half-and-half
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 bag baby spinach
4 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
4 slices bacon
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Fry 4 pieces of bacon in a large skillet, crumble and set aside.
Add spinach and mushrooms to the pan and saute in the bacon grease.
Place in bottom of dish of a 9 x 9 inch pan.  Top with uncooked chicken.
Using same pan, mix in garlic, lemon juice, cream of mushroom soup, Italian seasoning, half-and-half, 1 cup mozzerella and Parmesan cheese.
Bake for 25 minutes at 350.
Remove from oven, top with 1 cup mozzarella and continue baking for 10 minutes at 350.
