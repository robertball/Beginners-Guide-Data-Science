Title:
Kahlua Hot Fudge sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 can (14 oz.) Eagle brand sweet-condensed milk
1/2 - 1/4 cup Amaretto or Kahlua liquer
1 jar (12 oz) caramel topping

Directions:
Stir constantly on low heat until everything is melted a mixed. Serve on favorite ice cream, but peppermint is recommended!

Submitter: 
Lisa Eagen
Description: 
One of my mom's holiday favorites. It is great served on peppermint ice cream!
