Title:
Tina's "To-Taste" Slow Cooker Wassail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
96 ozs. apple juice (no sugar added)
1-2 cups pineapple juice
1 cup lemon juice concentrate
8 whole cloves
3 cinnamon sticks
1/2-1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup light brown sugar
The juice of 3 small oranges, about 1/2 cup

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a slow cooker and cook on low 6-8 hours.  Adjust all ingredients to taste.
