Title:
Fudge Ice Cream Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. chocolate chips
2 cubes margarine
2 2/3 cup evaporated milk
4 cups powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Melt chocolate chips and butter in saucepan. Add milk and powdered sugar.
Boil 8 minutes, stirring constantly. Add vanilla.
