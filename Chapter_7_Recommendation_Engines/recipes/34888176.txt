Title:
Baked Fettucine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 small carton whipping cream
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 small can mushrooms, or fresh
1 pkg grated mozarella cheese
1/4 cup chopped onion
Cooked thin vermicelli spaghetti (small pkg)

Directions:
Melt stick of butter over low heat in saucepan.
Add garlic, onion and mushrooms.
Simmer for 10-15 minutes while cooking spaghetti (according to package instructions).
Add whipping cream to butter mixture.
To Bake:  Layer spaghetti, extra mushrooms, if desired, sauce, and cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes. (Don't bake longer than 20 minutes.)
Add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Peggy
Description: 
A wonderful creamy fettucini that's baked in the oven.  My family loves it.
