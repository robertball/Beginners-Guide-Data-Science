Title:
TESC Vegan Oat Fudgies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/542222.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups margarine
3 cups turbinado sugar
1 T vanilla
2 T egg substitute
1/2 cup water
3 cups oats
3 cups flour
4 cups non-dairy barley malt chocolate chips
2 T margarine
1 cup soy milk

Directions:
Cream the margarine and sugar. Add vanilla.
In a small bowl beat the egg substitute with water. Add to margarine mixture. And beat.  Divide the batter into two parts and set aside.
Combine chocolate chips, margarine and soymilk in saucepan over low heat.  Stir often until melted.
Take half of the dough and flatten evenly into an oiled sheet.  Spread chocolate over dough.  Place remaining dough in blobs over the chocolate layer.
Bake at 350 until golden brown.
