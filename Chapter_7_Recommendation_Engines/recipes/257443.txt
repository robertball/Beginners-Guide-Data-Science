Title:
Watermelon Fizz Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed seeded watermelon
10 ice cubes
6 fluid ounces club soda
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers melon-flavored vodka (such as SkyyÂ®)
2 tablespoons frozen limeade concentrate
1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 fresh strawberries

Directions:
Combine watermelon, ice cubes, club soda, melon-flavored vodka, limeade concentrate, mint, and sugar in a blender. Blend for 40 seconds; pour into glasses and garnish with strawberries.
