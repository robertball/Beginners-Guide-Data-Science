Title:
Chocolate Crinkles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
1/2 cup cocoa
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
3 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Mix margarine, cocoa and sugar.  Blend in 1 egg at a time until well blended.  Add vanilla, flour, baking powder and salt.
Roll into small balls and roll in powdered sugar.  Place on greased baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes at 375 degrees F.
