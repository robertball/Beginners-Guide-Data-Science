Title:
Carmel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags (snyder's orginal) puffed corn
2 sticks butter (not oleo)
1/2 cup light karo
2 Tbsp. dark molasses
2 cups brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Pour corn in foil turkey roaster that has been sprayed with pam.
Then mix all the ingredients together, down to the vanilla and soda, and boil for three minutes. Remove from heat and add vanilla and soda and stir well. It will foam up. Then pour over corn in roaster and mix well.
Put in oven at 250 degrees for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
Then pour out on cookie sheets to cool.
