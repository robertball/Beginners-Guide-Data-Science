Title:
Italian Crescent Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups shredded cooked chicken or cooked/drained hamburger
1 cup spaghetti sauce
1 cup shredded cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
8 oz can crescent rolls
1 T melted butter
1 T parmesan cheese
1 t italian seasoning
1 t garlic

Directions:
spray 13 x 9 pan with pam
layer ingredients in order listed
roll out crescent rolls and place on top
brush with melted butter
sprinkle with parm and seasonings
bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes
