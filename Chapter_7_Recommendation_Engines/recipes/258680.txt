Title:
Pork and Celery Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
9 ounces skinless pork belly, thinly sliced
1 small hot chile pepper, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced, or more to taste
1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
5 ounces Chinese celery, leaves discarded and celery chopped into 1-inch pieces
2 teaspoons rice wine
1 1/2 teaspoons soy sauce
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
1/4 teaspoon monosodium glutamate (MSG)

Directions:
Heat oil in a skillet over high heat; cook and stir pork slices until lightly browned, about 3 minutes. Add chile pepper, garlic, and ginger; cook and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
Stir celery, rice wine, soy sauce, salt, white pepper, and MSG into pork mixture; cook and stir until heated through, about 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
cindyfj
Description: 
This is a well-flavored Chinese stir fry, which is quick and easy to make. Pork is stir-fried with celery, chiles, garlic, and ginger. Enjoy alongside rice.
