Title:
Vanilla Bean Frozen Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/927198.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups evaporated cane sugar
1-1/2 cups 2% milk
27 ounces of vanilla greek yogurt
3/4 cup half & half
3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 vanilla bean strands
pinch of sea salt

Directions:
Pre-freeze ice-cream maker container for 24 hours before beginning process.
In a large bowl, whisk together sugar and milk until sugar is dissolved. Slit the vanilla beans and scrape seeds into sugar mixture. Add yogurt, half & half, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix until smooth and well combined.
Refrigerate mixture for at least one hour, until very chilled.
Remove ice-cream maker bowl from freezer, and transfer mixture to the bowl. Follow machine instructions.
Remove from bowl and serve immediately or transfer to an airtight container to be stored in the freezer.
Note:  frozen yogurt tends to get very hard in the freezer, so you should take it out about 15 minutes prior to serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
Is there anything better than vanilla bean frozen yogurt?  Start with this recipe and keep it simple, or add some frozen mixed berries & shaved chocolate five minutes before it's done.  The options are endless!
