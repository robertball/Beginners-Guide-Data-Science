Title:
Texas Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/949996.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon soda
1 cup water
2 sticks oleo
4 Tablespoons cocoa
2 eggs
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon vanilla
for frosting:
4 cups confectioners sugar
1 stick oleo
4 Tablespoons cocoa
1 teaspoon vanilla
6 Tablespoons milk
1 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Put flour, sugar, salt and soda in mixing bowl
Bring to boil water, oleo, and cocoa and add to flour mixture
Mix and add eggs, sour cream, and vanilla
Bake in large cookie pan at 350 degrees about 20 minutes
Ice while hot:
Put oleo, cocoa, vanilla, and milk in a pan and bring just to boiling point.
Add to sugar
Add nuts.
