Title:
Puerto Rican Cabbage, Avocado, and Carrot Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/963818.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of red or green cabbage
1/2 carrot shredded
4 slices of hass avocado
Dressing
1/2 tsp of olive oil
1/2 tsp of lime juice or vinegar
***add cilantro if you desire***

Directions:
Mix the cabbage and carrots and dressing together first then gently mix in the avocado slices.

Description: 
A great side salad for any Puerto Rican meal.
