Title:
Yum Yum Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c sugar
3 c flour
1/2 c cocoa powder
2 tsp vanilla
2 tsp baking soda
2 T vinegar
2/3 c vegetable oil
2 c water
1 pkg (8oz) cream cheese, softened
1 egg
1/2 c sugar
dash of salt
12 oz chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine the first 8 ingredients and mix thoroughly.
Cream the cream cheese, egg, sugar, and salt.  Add chocolate chips and blend well.
Fill cupcake papers half full with chocolate batter and top with 1 T of cream cheese mixture.
Bake 15-20 min at 350.

Submitter: 
ESTEPHAN
Description: 
This is a classic favorite in the PA Dutch Community.  Always a hit.  Moist chocolate cupcakes filled with cream cheese and chocolate chips.
