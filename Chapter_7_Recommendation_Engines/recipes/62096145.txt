Title:
Ham Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Cubed fully cooked ham
2 Cups cubed and peeled potatoes
1 large carrot, sliced
2 celery ribs, cleaned and chopped
3 cups of water
2 Tbsp. chopped greeen pepper
2 tsp. finely chopped onion
7 tbsp. butter, divided
3 tbsp. all-purpose flour
1-1 1/2 cups milk
1/8 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1/2 cup unseasoned bread crumbs or crushed Buttery crackers

Directions:
In a saucepan, add water, potaotes, carrot and celery and cook until tender. Drain.
In a skillet, saute ham, green pepper, and onion in 3 tbsp. of butter until tender
Add to potatoe mixture and transfer all to a 2 quart baking dish
In a saucepan melt remaining butter and stir in flour until smooth
Gradually stir in milk, salt and pepper
Bring to a boil, stirring constantly and cook for 2 minutes or until thickened.
Stir in cheese until melted, pour over the ham mixture
Sprinkle with bread crumbs
Bake uncovered at 350' for 25-30 minutes or until heated through and bubbly.
Let sit 5-10 minutes before serving.
