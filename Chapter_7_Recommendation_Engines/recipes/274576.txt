Title:
School Lunch Ham and Cheese Wrap  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
10 (9 inch) flour tortillas
1 (8 ounce) package whipped cream cheese
1 pound shaved deli ham
1 (16 ounce) bottle ranch dressing
1 (8 ounce) package shredded carrots
1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
2 cups shredded lettuce

Directions:
Spread each tortilla with a thin layer of cream cheese. Lay shaved ham thinly over cream cheese layer. Spread a thin layer of ranch dressing on ham. Sprinkle lightly with carrots, followed by Cheddar cheese. Top with a light layer of lettuce.
Fold side of tortilla shell towards the center. Roll up unfolded sides. Secure with a toothpick if necessary.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
Tasty and versatile (not to mention fun!) alternative to the boring sandwich for school lunches. Mix and match varieties of cheeses and meats to your heart's content! Teens love this sandwich!
