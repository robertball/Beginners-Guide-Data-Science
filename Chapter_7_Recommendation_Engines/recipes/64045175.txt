Title:
Gluten Free Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I did gluten free crushed chocolate wafers 1 and 1/4 cup and mixed in 1/4 cup coconut oil. Pressed it in the pan then baked it for about t
10 minutes. The filling was a can of coconut milk in the fridge overnight with a package of vegan vanilla pudding and 1/4 cup cocoa powder and 1/4 cup stevia. put it all together then in the fridge. The topping was another can of coconut milk overnight chilled with 3 tbsp stevia and 1 tsp of mint extract
