Title:
Poppy Seed Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/2 cup oil
1 pkg vanilla instant pudding mix
1 cup cold water
4 tbsp. Poppy seeds
1 white cake mix

Directions:
Beat eggs until frothy.  Add remaining ingredients & beat well.
Bake @325 degrees for 40 - 50 min in well greased & floured bundt pan.
