Title:
Bannock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups of warm water
1 tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. oil or melted margarine
3 1/2 cups flour
1/2 tbsp. yeast
1/3 cup canola oil

Directions:
Combine water, sugar, salt and margarine in a medium bowl. Combine flour and yeast in another bowl.Slowly add dry ingredients to liquid ingredients and lightly mix together.
Pour 1/3 cup of canola oil into frying pan and turn burner on to medium heat. Let oil start to bubble in frying pan (approximately 7 minutes) Do bread crumb test â€“if bubbles form around the piece of bread you are ready to cook!!
Form dough into small balls and flatten out on a greased counter with a greased rolling pin
Lightly drop flattened dough into frying pan and let one side brown before flipping over and browning the other side. ENJOY!
