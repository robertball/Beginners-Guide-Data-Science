Title:
Melt in Your Mouth Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BEAT TOGETHER
1 cup margarine
1 cup white sugar
1 cup powdered sugar
THEN ADD
2 beaten eggs
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup Wesson Oil
2 teaspoons vanilla
SIFT AND ADD
4 cups flour, plus
4 tablespoons flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cream of tartar

Directions:
Refrigerate the dough until chilled.
Then roll into balls and place on greased cookie sheet. Press with glass dipped in sugar.
Bake in 350 degree oven about 8 to 10 minutes.
Bet you can’t stop yourself from eating these!
