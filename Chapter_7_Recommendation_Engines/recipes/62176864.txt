Title:
Slow Cooker Vegetarian Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 can garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
1 can vegetarian baked beans
2 cans black beans, rinsed and drained
2 cans chopped tomatoes (with juice)
1 can tomato paste
1 can corn, drained
1 can diced jalapenos or green chilis
1 large onion, chopped
2 green bell peppers, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 TBP cayenne pepper
1 TBP cumin
1 1/2 TBP chili powder
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Dump everything in the slow cooker. Cook on high for 2 hours, then on low for 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Jenny Wadkins
Description: 
Everything goes into the cooker and emerges hours later as vegetarian chili.Modified from: Grandma's Slow Cooker Vegetarian Chili
