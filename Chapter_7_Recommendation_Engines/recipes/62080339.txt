Title:
Pumpkin Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 9" unbaked deep dish pie crust
Apple Portion:
2 granny smith apples, peeled, cored and cut into medium pieces
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Brown Sugar
1/4t Cinnamon
Pumpkin portion:
1/2 Sugar
1/4c Brown Sugar
1/2t Salt
1t Cinnamon
1/2t Ginger
1/4t Cloves
2 eggs
15 oz can Pumpkin
12oz can Evaporated Milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Toss together the apple portion ingredients in a small bowl until evenly covered.  Spread the apples into pie plate.
For the pumpkin, beat eggs together. In separte bowl, combine all dry ingredients, and then mix into eggs.  Add pumpkin.  Slowly beat in evaporated milk.
Very gently and slowly, pour the pumpkin mixture into the pie shell.  (Using a spoon held upside down over the pie helps to distribute it without mixing it with the apples
5. Bake at 425 for 15 minutes, then at 350 for 45-60 minutes. Cool completely, then refrigerate until served.

Submitter: 
Grace Gutberlet
Description: 
Since pumpkin and apple pies both have similar spices, the two work well together.  It might sound odd, but beleives me, it works.
