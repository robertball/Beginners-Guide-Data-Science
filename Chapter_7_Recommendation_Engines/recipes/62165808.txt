Title:
"PIZZA" PORK CHOPS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5- boneless pork chops
5- 1/2" slices of tomato
1/4 c chopped fresh basil
1 TB chopped fresh oregano
2 cloves of garlic - minced
5 slices of mozzarella
olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat grill
Place pork chops in a disposable pan
Top with slice of tomato, garlic , basil, oregano, salt and pepper.
Drizzle with olive oil
cover with aluminum foil
Grill over medium heat 25 mins
Top with mozzarella slices and recover with foil.
Remove from heat and let cheese melt 3-5 mins.

Submitter: 
CNM CATERING
Description: 
I threw these delicous pork chops together with some extra things from the garden. My 5yr old daughter named them pizza pork chops because she says they tasted like pizza.
