Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/3 c flour
3 c sugar
2 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg
1 can(15oz) solid pack pumpkin
1 c vegetable oil
4 eggs, lightly beaten
2/3 c water
Caramel Glaze:
1/4 c butter
1/4 c sugar
1/4 packed brown sugar
1/4 c heavy whipping cream
2/3 c powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. In a large bowl, combine first 6 ingredients. In another bowl, combine pumpkin, oil, eggs, & water;mix well. Stir into dry ingredients. Pour into 2 greased loaf pans.Bake 50-60 min or until inserted toothpick comes out clean. Let cool, remove from pans. Mix glaze in a saucepan on the stove over med heat until all sugar has disolved. Drizzle over the top of the loaves. Once it dries, you can wrap the breads.
