Title:
Chocolate Chip Dessert Cheeseball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/2 c. butter, softened
1/4 tsp. vanilla
3/4 c. powdered sugar
2 tb. brown sugar
3/4 c. miniature chocolate chips
1/2 c. finely chopped pecans
1 box graham cracker sticks

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese, butter and vanilla until fluffy.
Add the powdered sugar and the brown sugar a little at a time until incorporated into the cream cheese.
Stir in the chocolate chips.
Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least two hours.
Mold the mixture into a large ball shape and put back in the refrigerator for another hour.
Roll the cheeseball in the chopped pecans before serving.
Serve with the graham cracker sticks for dipping, smearing.
