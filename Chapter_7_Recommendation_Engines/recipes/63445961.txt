Title:
Autumn Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 C. mashed cooked pumkin( may be canned)
1 1/3 C. sweetened condensed milk (15 oz)
1 egg
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp ginger
1 C. hot water
Pastry for one crust pie

Directions:
1. Beat together all ingredients, less pastry, with rotary beater or could just mix by hand.
2. Pour into pastry lined pie pan
3. Bake at 375 for 50-55 min.
