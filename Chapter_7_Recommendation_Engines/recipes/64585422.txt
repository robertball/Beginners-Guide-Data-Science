Title:
Pear Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
22 oz filtered or sparkling water
8 pear slices
1 cinnamon stick
1/2 vanilla bean

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a 32-oz mason jar or pitcher.
Stir gently, cover, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (longer for more flavor).
Store in refrigerator up to 3 days.
