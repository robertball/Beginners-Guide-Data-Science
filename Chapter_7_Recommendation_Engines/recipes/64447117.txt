Title:
Potato Lentil Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBS olive oil
2 cloves garlic
2 russet potatoes
2 1/2 cups lentils
1/2 cup onion, diced
3 slices bacon
1/4 cup goat cheese
salt, pepper, rosemary, and thyme to taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 425
Finely chop the garlic
Dice the potatoes
Mix the potatoes, garlic, and olive oil, put on a cookie sheet and roast until the potatoes are golden (about 15-20 minutes).
Cook the lentils in boiling water.
While lentils are cooking dice the onion.
Fry the bacon.
Remove bacon from pan and saute the onions.
Drain lentils once done and add cooked lentils to the saute pan.
Crumble the cooked bacon on top of the lentils and mix in.
Add roasted potatoes into the mix.
Flavor with salt, pepper, and herbs.
Top with goat cheese and serve!

Description: 
A quick and easy dinner recipe that holds up great as leftovers!
