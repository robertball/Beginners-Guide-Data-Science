Title:
Bacon Wrapped Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breast
8 slices Bacon
8 pieces of Dried beef
1 pnt container of sour cream
1 can Cream of mushroom soup
1 can Cream of Chicken soup

Directions:
Wrap the chicken breast in bacon
moisten dried beef in boling water
layer bottom of caserole dish with dried beef
place chicken on top of beef
mix soup and sour cream
pour mixture evenly over chicken
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes
