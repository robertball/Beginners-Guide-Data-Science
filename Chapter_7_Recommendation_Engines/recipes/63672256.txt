Title:
Sausage Ravioli In Herbed Garlic Oil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 25 oz package frozen Italian Sausage Ravioli
3 cloves fresh garlic, minced
1 Tbsp dried parsley flakes
2 Tbsp grated Romano cheese
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp coarse garlic salt

Directions:
Cook the ravioli according to package, drain and set aside.
In the same sauce pan gently simmer the extra virgin olive oil, minced garlic, and red pepper flakes just until the garlic turns slightly golden.
Remove from heat and return the ravioli to the pan and toss with the parsley, garlic salt, and cheese.
Serve hot and enjoy!

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
My husband isn't a big fan of marinara sauce, but I love ravioli so we have this often as a compromise. It works well with cheese filled ravioli too, but I really love the flavor combo with the sausage. Serve with a crisp green salad and garlic bread for a hearty meal.
