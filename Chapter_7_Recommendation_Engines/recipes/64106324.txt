Title:
Elizabeth's Amazing Cranberry Ring Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounce bag of fresh cranberries
2 cups sugar
large can crushed pineapple
3 small packages lemon gelatin
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Wash and sort cranberries.
Process in a blender: one cup of cranberries to 1/2 cup water. Will be soupy, but not smooth.
Mix 2 cups sugar and 2 cups of blended cranberries.
Return this sugary mixture to rest of cranberries in the blender. Mix well.
Place blender in fridge and let stand overnight.
Next day:
Drain pineapple juice and add enough water to make one cup. Boil.
Dissolve gelatin into boiling water.
Pour over cranberries.
Add orange juice, drained pineapple and nuts.
Pour into mold and congeal overnight.

Submitter: 
aunt star
Description: 
This is the only cranberry sauce ever served at my mother's Thanksgiving or Christmas table. I buy extra bags of cranberries in season and freeze them so I can enjoy this cool side dish year round.
