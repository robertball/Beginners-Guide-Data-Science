Title:
edamame roasted garlic hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bulb of roasted garlic
2 c edamame hulled
1/3 c tahini
1 t salt
1/4 c lemon juice
1/2 c cilantro, roughly chopped
2 T olive oil-more as needed
1 t smoked paprika

Directions:
Roast the garlic in the oven-cut off top. drizzle in olive oil, wrap in foil, bake at 350 for an hour, let cool. Squeeze all garlic out, (separate the hard bits and paper and through out)-place in food processor.
Place remaining ingredients-except paprika- in processor and add more oil if it's not smooth enough.
Process until smooth as you like it, place in serving dish and sprinkle with paprika.
Serve with baked pita chips.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
The addition of the roasted garlic and cilantro really make this dish shine.
