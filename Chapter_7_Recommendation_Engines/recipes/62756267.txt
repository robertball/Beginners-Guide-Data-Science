Title:
Chilli Rellenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871076.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Lb Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1 Lb Longhorn Cheese
2 Large Cans Mild Diced Chili's
4 Eggs
3 T Flour
1 Can Evaporated MIlk

Directions:
In 13X9 baking pan, layer chili's then cheese, chili's then cheese.  Pour mixture of eggs, milk and flour over top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
