Title:
Clara's Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2997351.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. Crisco
1 c. butter
2 eggs
1/2 c. molasses
1 tsp. soda
1 tsp. ginger (scant)
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. cloves
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
pinch of salt
5 1/2 c. flour

Directions:
Mix all ingredients until smooth.
Separate in section and form into about 1 foot rolls. Wrap in parchment paper and refrigerate overnight.
When ready to bake, cut rolls into 1/2" to 1" pieces and place on cookie sheet.
Bake for 10 minutes or less at 375.
Let cool and frost with cream cheese frosting.

Submitter: 
Lisa Eagen
Description: 
Christmas favorite compliments of great aunt Clara Bertine Larson (1891-1980).
