Title:
Oatmeal Cinnamon Cream pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cookie:
1 cup butter
3/4 cup brown sugar (dark brown is best)
1/2 white sugar
1 tblsp molasses
1 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups AP flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 cup quick cooking oats
Cream Filling:
2 tsp hot water
1/4 tsp table salt
1- 7oz. jar marshmallow cream
1/2 cup shortening
1/2c. powdered sugar
3/4 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In medium bowl, combine AP flour, salt, baking soda, and cinnamon. Combine well and set aside
In lg bowl, cream the butter, both sugars, molasses, vanilla, and eggs.
Add the flour mixture to the creamed mixture and stir to combine. Stir in oats
With a 2tsp (#60) cookie scoop, drop onto parchment-lined baking sheets. Be aware that cookies will spread, so space accordingly.
Bake at 350 for 7-10 minutes, or until just starting to brown. Cookies will look moist so be sure not to overbake!
To prepare filling:
In a small cup, dissolve the salt in the hot water. Allow to cool.
In a small bowl, combine marshmallow cream, shortening, powdered sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla. Mix on high until fluffy.
Added cooled salt water to mixture and mix well.
Spread on the back of one cooled cookie. Add another to form sandwich.
Store in an airtight container between layers of waxed or parchment paper in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Kimberly
Description: 
A soft and chewy oatmeal cookie with a cinnamony filling. Sure to be a favorite in any lunchbox!
