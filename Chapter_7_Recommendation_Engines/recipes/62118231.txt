Title:
Genoa Salami and Manzanilla Olive Calzones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ricotta cheese
1/4 cup Italian cheese blend
1 tsp. oregano
1/4 lb. thinly sliced genoa salami, shredded
1/4 cup finely chopped manzanella olives
flour, for dusting
1 can refrigerated pizza dough
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
In a medium bowl, combine cheeses, olives, salami, and oregano.
On a floured surface, unroll pizza dough in 1 piece, and slice into 8 squares.
Place 2 tablespoons of filling on half of each square.
Lightly moisten the edges with water, fold dough over, and seal, pressing with fork tines.
Using the points of the fork, pierce vents into top of each calzone.
Place on a greased baking sheet.
Brush with egg.
Bake for 15 minutes or until golden.

Submitter: 
TINATED
Description: 
These mini calzones are a delicious appetizer for any occasion, from a shower to a Superbowl party. They can be served along side warmed tomato sauce.
