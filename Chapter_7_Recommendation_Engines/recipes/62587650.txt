Title:
Copycat Mr. Hero's Roman Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/552227.jpg
Ingredients:
1 6-inch italian hoagie sub (or nice crusty bread)
2 hamburger patties
2 hard salami slices
mayo
3 white onion slices
shredded lettuce
2 tomato slices
2 provolone slices
zesty italian dressing
butter

Directions:
Split hoagie sub, spread butter on one side of sub and place on grill for toasting.
After toasting remove.
Spread mayo on both sides of sub.
On bottom part of sub, layer lettuce, tomato and onion.
Drizzle italian dressing on both sides and set hoagie aside.
Grill burgers and add provolone slices on each burger before removal of grill and place on hoagie.
Place salami on grill and grill for about 30 seconds to 1 minute-flipping on both sides to cook evenly.
Place salami ontop of cheeseburgers.
Assemble sandwich together-press together to get the flavors together!!
Cut in half and serve!!
Add wafer fries with cheddar sauce (opt)

Description:

Salami and cheese are cooked on top of burger that gives a unique taste that no other burger can!! This recipe is actually how used to be made when I was growing up (no brainer)and they post different ingredients what is actually used for protecting the recipe.  YUM!!  Great for tailgaters, parties, anytime-can make larger or small!!  So easy to make!!
