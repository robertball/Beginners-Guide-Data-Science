Title:
Suprisingly Succulent  Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pita bread
1 tbsp black bean dip
1/3 avocado
dash lemon juice
1/4 tomato
5-8 leaves fresh basil, chopped
1 tsp fresh cilantro, chopped
salt, pepper, garlic powder to taste
1 green onion
4-5 slices orange pepper
1 tbsp banana pepper, chopped
4-5 thin slices smoked mozarella cheese

Directions:
Separate top and bottom of pita.
Spreak black bean dip on bottom half of pita.
Layer rest of ingredients on top of the bottom pita with the avocado and tomato toward the bottom, and the cheese as the last ingredient at the top.
Cap with the top of the pita.
Toast for appx 10 minutes at 350, or until cheese is melted.
Eat and enjoy!

Submitter: 
nurkdenflurn
Description: 
A vegetarian sandwich that satisfies the appetite without a heavy gut punch.  The fresh basil and cilantro are what really make the sandwich!
