Title:
Crab stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breast
3 clove garlics (minced)
1/4 cup light cream cheese
1 can crab, drained
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Mix cream cheese, garlic, and crab.
Slice open chicken breast and place crab mixture inside.
Place stuffed breast in a covered baking dish with a splash of water and bake for 20 min.
Revmove lid and broil for 5min.
Serve with covered in liquid from the baking dish.
