Title:
Pe'pe's Mushroom Chicken Piccata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1 egg
2 tablespoons milk
3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
4 tablespoons butter
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup white wine
2 tablespoons FRESH lemon juice
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, for garnish
FRESH MINCED GARLIC.

Directions:
In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together flour, salt and paprika. In a separate dish or bowl mix together egg and milk. Dredge chicken pieces in seasoned flour mixture, then egg mixture, then in bread crumbs.
In a large skillet, heat butter or margarine over medium high heat. Saute chicken pieces until golden brown. Add GARLIC, mushrooms and onion and saute for 3 to 5 minutes.
REMOVE from skillet and keep warm.
In a medium bowl, combine the broth, lemon juice and cornstarch. Mix together.  Deglaze the skillet with the wine, add the broth mixture to the mushrooms, garlic and onion.  Saute until reduced, then simmer on low.
Pour sauce over chicken, just prior to serving.  Reserve some gravy for starchy side-dish.

Submitter: 
Pe'pe
Description: 
Classic Piccata reaches a new peak with the marvelous addition of mushrooms! This is a favorite in our family. Serve with rice and a green salad if desired.Modified from: Mushroom Chicken Piccata
