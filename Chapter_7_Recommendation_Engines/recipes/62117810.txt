Title:
Grif's Old School Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz. package lasagna noodles
1 24oz. jar of prepared spaghetti sauce
3 cloves garlic (chopped)
1/2 tsp dried basil
1 tsp dried oregano
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
1 32oz. container ricotta cheese
1/2 cup red wine
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 egg
2 sprigs fresh thyme
1/4 grated parmesan cheese
2 TBL olive oil
6 fresh basil leaves (cut into ribbons)
24 oz mozzarella (divided)
1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan (divided)
1 tsp italian seasoning
1 lb. ground beef
salt
pepper

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees
drop the lasagna noodles in boiling water for 5 mins
drain, rinse and set aside
in a large skillet over medium heat brown ground beef
while browning add dried herbs, crushed red pepper,and      onion powder
after the meat has been browned drain the fat if needed
add the spaghetti sauce and wine reduce heat to low and let simmer
in a large mixing bowl add ricotta, nutmeg, egg, thyme leaves, grated parmesan, olive oil, fresh basil, 16 oz. mozzarella grated, & 1/4 cup fresh parm grated mix to incorporate
in a large baking dish cover the bottom with the meat sauce
cover that with a layer of the par-cooked pasta noodles
top the noodles with a layer of cheese mixture
cover with another layer of pasta
repeat the layer until you reach the top of the pan
on the top layer of pasta put all remaining meat sauce
grate remaining cheeses on that
sprinkle with italian seasoning
cover with foil
bake for 45 mins
remove foil raise oven temp to 375 degrees
bake 15 more mins
after cooking allow to rest for at least 10 mins
