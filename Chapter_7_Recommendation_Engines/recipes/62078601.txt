Title:
Sweet & Spicy Appetizer Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen meatballs (you could also use your favorite recipe for homemade meatballs)
30 oz. ketchup
10 oz. spicy ketchup (Heinz makes this)
2 cups packed light brown sugar
1-2 Tbsp worchestershire sauce

Directions:
Place ketchups, brown sugar and worchestershire sauce in pot over low to medium flame until melted.  Taste sauce.  Adjust to your taste (spicy or sweeter).
Place frozen meatballs into crockpot and pour sauce over.  Stir to coat.
Cook on low for 4-6 hours or high for 2-3.

Submitter: 
RAEVYAN
Description: 
This recipe grew out of too many potlucks at my work.  For those of you who are tired of the same old grape jelly and chili sauce meatballs - this recipe is for you!
