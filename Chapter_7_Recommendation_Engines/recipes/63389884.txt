Title:
Dena and Rich's Amazing Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. Ground hamburger
1 Diced Onion
1 Diced Green Pepper
1 Diced Red Peppter
2 Cans chili beans, undrained
2 Cans light kidney beans, drained
2 Can dark kidney beans, drained
3 16oz Cans of Tomato Sauce
1 Can Chili Rotel
1 1/2 Tbl. Chili Powder
2 Tea. Paprika
Sprinkle Onion Powder
2-3 Tea. Cayenne Pepper
For spicer chili, add curry, cumin or hot rotel.
Top with shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In large pot, brown ground beef with onions and peppers.  Drain grease.  Add all other ingredients to pot and bring to boil.  As soon as it boils, reduce heat to low.   Simmer for 1-2 hours.
