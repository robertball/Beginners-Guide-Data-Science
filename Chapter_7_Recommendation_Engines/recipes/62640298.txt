Title:
Lemon Breakfast Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¾	cup fat free milk
1	dash salt
1/3	cup couscous
½	cup lemon low fat yogurt
½	cup reduced  calorie sour cream
1	tablespoon honey
¼	teaspoon finely shredded lemon peel
3	cups assorted fruit, such as sliced strawberries, kiwifruit, nectarine, or star fruit, and or blueberries or raspberries
Chopped crystallized ginger (optional)
Fresh Mint (optional)

Directions:
1.	In medium saucepan bring the milk and salt to boiling; stir in the couscous, simmer, covered, for 1 minute.  Remove from heat; let stand for 5 minutes.  Stir with a fork until fluffy.  Cool.
2.	In small bowl combine the yogurt, sour cream, honey, and lemon peel; stir into the couscous.  In another bowl combine desired fruit.
3.	To serve, divide half of the fruit mixture among  6 parfait glasses.  Spoon couscous mixture over fruit; top with remaining fruit.  If desired, garnish with chopped crystallized ginger and mint.
