Title:
Grilled Cajun Ranch Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup ranch dressing
3 tbsp Worcestershire
2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
2 tsp salt
1 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp white vinegar
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1tbsp white sugar
4 chicken breasts
1 jar Alfredo sauce
Pasta of your choice

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together all the ingredients (minus the chicken and Alfredo!). Let stand for 5 minutes. Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 inch. Place chicken in large bag or bowl with lid, mix with marinade. Refridgerate for at least 2 hours. Grill chicken until done.
Cook pasta of your choice until aldente. Add jar of Alfredo and toss.
Slice the grilled chicken and serve over pasta
