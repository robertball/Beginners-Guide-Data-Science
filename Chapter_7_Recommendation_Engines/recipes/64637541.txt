Title:
Artichoke Fondu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14.5 oz jars artichoke hearts, drained, and chopped
2 large garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup shredded provolone cheese
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/2 cup sour cream
Parsley, for garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients well,
pour into a casserole (or other ovenproof dish)
bake for 20-25 minutes (until hot and bubbly).
Sprinkle with parsley.
Serve it with toasted bread, sliced baguette or chopped fresh veggies as dippers.

Submitter: 
N8TE
Description: 
You'll be fond of this artichoke fondue that's sure to prove popular with family and friends. Serve it with toasted bread, sliced baguette or chopped fresh veggies as dippers. It's a great way to start a meal or party.
