Title:
OLD FASHIONED RICE PUDDING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Just in time for Thanksgiving and Christmas! Ingredients: 2/3 c. Minute Rice 2 3/4 c. milk 1/3 c. sugar 1 tbsp. butter 1/2 tsp. salt 1/2 tsp. vanilla 1/4 tsp. nutmeg Cinnamon 1/2 c. raisins (opt.)

Directions:
Combine Minute Rice, milk and raisins, sugar, butter, salt, vanilla and nutmeg in a buttered 1 quart baking dish. Bake in 350 degree oven for 1 hour, stirring after 15 minutes and again when pudding is done. Sprinkle with cinnamon. Serve warm or chilled.
