Title:
Shadden's BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ cup oil
½ stick butter
2 small onions (chopped very fine)
3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
½ bottle A-1 Sauce
¾ bottle ketchup
1 tablespoon chili powder
2 tablespoons brown sugar
½ tablespoons Tabasco
¼ lemon (grated; including rind)
Salt and pepper to taste
Cayenne pepper to taste (for added heat)

Directions:
– Sauté onion in butter and oil until tender
– Add other ingredients, mix well and cook for 30-45 minutes

Submitter: 
Jay Lynne Stuart
Description: 
The original BBQ sauce from the famous Shadden's in Marvel, AR.
