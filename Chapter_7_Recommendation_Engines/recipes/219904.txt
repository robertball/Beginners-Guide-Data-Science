Title:
Bill's Famous Rum Squeeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/974581.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces light rum
3 fluid ounces sweet and sour mix
1/2 fluid ounce lime juice
1 tablespoon coconut flavored syrup
ice cubes, as needed

Directions:
Pour the rum, sweet and sour mix, lime juice, and coconut syrup into the pitcher of a blender; drop in the ice cubes and blend to a slushy consistency. Pour into a glass to serve.

Submitter: 
bill r
Description: 
Can't decide between sweet or sour? This rum squeeze might be the answer. It is a slurpy cocktail with a kick, a perfect complement to any sunset.
