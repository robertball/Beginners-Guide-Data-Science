Title:
Grilled Arctic Char on Bed of Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 arctic char filets 6 oz each
1 tsp kosher salt
freshly ground black pepper
extra virgin olive oil
1 ripe mango
6 ripe strawberries
fresh roasted pecans (substitute pine nuts or walnuts)
fresh lemon juice
Mixed greens (enough for 2 servings), washed and dried

Directions:
Heat grill pan to medium high with 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
Season both sides of the fish with salt and pepper.
Grill presentation side down for 3-4 minutes for a 1 inch thick filet.
Flip and cook skin side down for another 3-4 minutes.
Squeeze lemon juice over the fish while grilling.
Remove the grill pan from the heat.
Place a handful of greens on each of 2 serving plates.
Arrange sliced strawberries, sliced mangoes and roasted pecans on top of the greens.
Season both plates with salt and pepper.
Carefully place one filet skin side down on each of the plates.
Drizzle the pan juices over the salads.
Drizzle an additional 1 teaspoon of extra virgin olive oil on each salad to finish.
Serve while the fish is warm.

Submitter: 
AvelaineS
Description: 
Arctic char is a close cousin of the salmon, with a much milder and sophisticated flavor. It might be too pricey and hard to find when not in season depending where you live, so feel free to substitute salmon. This is a delicious, light and satisfying meal.
