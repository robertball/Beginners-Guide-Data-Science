Title:
Jamaican Jerk Seasoning Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP Onion Powder
2 TBSP Garlic Powder
2 TBSP Thyme (dried leaves)
2 TBSP Brown Sugar
1.5 TBSP Allspice (powder)
1 TBSP Cayenne Pepper (powder)
2 Tsp. Black Pepper (powder)
2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
1.5 Tsp. Parsley (dried leaves)
1.5 Tsp. Ground Cinnamon
1.5 Tsp. Ground Nutmeg

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Use as a "dry rub" or mix with a small amount of olive oil to form a paste that can be used to "rub into meat" or coat meat prior to cooking (BBQ preferred but you can also broil or roast).

Submitter: 
TheRock
Description: 
This is a seasoning mix for chicken or pork to create Jerk Chicken or Jerk Pork in the Jamaican tradition. This mix is spicy, sweet and savory all at the same time. Made from all dry ingredients (dried leaves, ground or powdered spices), this will store well, keep for a long time and be readily available for a quick meal preparation. Serve it with peas and rice (black eyed peas/beans and rice) and fried plantain for a traditional, delicious and nutritious Jamaican meal.
