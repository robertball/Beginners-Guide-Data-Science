Title:
Cauliflower with Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. grape seed oil
1 Tbsp. ginger, chopped
1/2 onion, chopped
1 Tbsp cumin seeds
1 Tbsp. turmeric
1 tsp salt
Pinch of freshly ground pepper
1 tomato, chopped
1 large sweet potato, peeled and cut into 1 inch cubes
1/2 cup water
1 small cauliflower, cut into bite sized florets

Directions:
Place oil in a large non=stick skillet and heat over medium heat.
Add the onion and ginger and cook for 4 minutes.  Stir in the spices and salt and pepper and toast for 15 seconds and then add tomatoes, sweet potatoes and water.
Bring to a boil and then turn down the heat and cook until they are tender, about 10 minutes.
Add the cauliflower and stir well.
Place a tight fitting lid on the pan and cook until the cauliflower is tender-crisp, about 5 - 8 min.
