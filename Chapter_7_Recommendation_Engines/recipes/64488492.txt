Title:
Amish Baked Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Warm Water
2/3 C sugar & 1/3 C honey
1 pkg yeast
1/4 C veg oil
6 Cups bread flour ( not reg flour but bread flour)....some swap flour out with red spring wheat flour or other flours but would add baking powder)

Directions:
dissolve  sugar water and yeast sit till foamy
Mix salt and oil into the yeast ....mix flour one cup at a time...
knead dough till smooth ball forms  about 8 min
place in well oiled bowl
cover with damp cloth...allow to rise till double about 1 hr
punch dough down and knead again for about 8 min
...shape into 2 loaves and put in well oiled loaf pans
allow to rise again till 1 inch over the edge of the pan about 45 min
gently place in the oven
bake at 350 * 30 min
removw from oven and allow to cool about 10 min then remove from pan and place on rack to cool
