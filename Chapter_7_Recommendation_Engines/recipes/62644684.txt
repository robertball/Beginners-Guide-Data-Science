Title:
Baby Super Food - Red Lentil Hors d' oeuvres Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup split red lentils
1 onion finely chopped
1 tablespoon of butter
Ground oatmeal or wheat germ

Directions:
Cook 1 cup of split red lentils (or other lentils) to yield 2 cups of cooked lentils. Fry 1 onion in butter on stove-top until soft. Mix onions to lentils and add 1/4 cup of wheat germ or oatmeal. Form into balls (will be very sticky) bake or fry,
