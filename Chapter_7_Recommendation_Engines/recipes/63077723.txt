Title:
STUFFED FRENCH TOAST Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 slices of bread (with crust removed if preferred)
¦12 eggs
¦2 C. of milk
¦16 oz. of cream cheese, softened
¦1 tsp. of vanilla (or flavor as desired)

Directions:
Cube bread and set aside. Mix eggs, milk, cream cheese and vanilla together in a bowl. Grease a 9X13" pan and then line it with the following: add 1 layer of bread cubes, spread evenly. Add 1/2 of the liquid mixture, pouring evenly. Add the rest of the bread cubes, spread evenly, and then add the rest of the liquid mixture evenly. Refrigerate overnight. The next morning take it out and let it cool to room temperature (very important). Bake in a 375 F oven for 45 min.
Serves 6 (approx.)

Submitter: 
Faye Watkins
Description: 
An easy, oven-baked version with vanilla and cream cheese.
