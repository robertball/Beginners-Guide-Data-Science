Title:
Oat Cakes (gluten free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3760682.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups gf oats (Bobs Red Mill)
1 cup gf flour (Bobs Red Mill)
1 tsp xanthan gum (Bobs Red Mill)
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
3/4 cup room temperature butter
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup boiling water

Directions:
Add all the dry ingredients together (first 7 on the list) and mix well.
Add the butter and use a pastry cutter to integrate it in.
Add the vanilla and water. Mix together.
Press into a 9X13 pan.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
Cut with a pizza cutter into squares.
