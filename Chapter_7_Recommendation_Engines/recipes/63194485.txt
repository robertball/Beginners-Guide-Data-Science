Title:
Mom's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb gr beef
1 c green pepper, chopped
1 c chopped onion
1 28oz can crushed tomatoes
1 8oz can tomato sauce
1 15oz can kidney beans, drained
1 1/2 tbsp chili powder
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp cayene pepper
1/8 tsp paprika

Directions:
Brown ground beef on medium heat with onions and green peppers in 3 qt saucepan until cooked through. Drain fat.
Add crushed tomato, tomato sauce, drained & rinsed kidney beans to the pot.
Add chili powder, salt, cayene pepper and paprika. Mix well.
Simmer, stirring often until the green peppers soften slightly.
Add chili powder to taste.

My Mom's traditional hearty beef chili!
