Title:
Eggs in a Basket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Slices of Bread (your favorite)
4 Eggs
Butter, softened
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Preheat skillet on medium high heat.
Butter both sides of each bread slice.
Using a cookie or biscuit cutter, cut a hole in each slice of bread.
Put slices in skillet.
Break 1 egg in the center (hole) of each bread slice.
Let cook until the bottom side is firm.
Season with salt, pepper (italian seasoning too, if desired)
Flip over the toast and let the egg cook on the other side and toast browns.

Submitter: 
Teeki
Description: 
I make these for my dauther and husband and they love them.  The kids can eat them on the way out the door.  Sometimes I variate and add cheese or different spices.  This is a nice quickie and you can't go wrong!
