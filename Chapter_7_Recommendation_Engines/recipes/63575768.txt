Title:
Cake Mix Extender from Keyingredient.com Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For White or Yellow Cake
Use:
1 box of white or yellow cake mix
1 c. cake flour or may use all-purpose flour
1/2 c. sugar
1 t. baking soda
1/2 c. water or milk
1/3 c. oil or softened butter
1 egg
1 t. pure vanilla extract
------------------------------------------------
For Chocolate Cake
Use:
1 box of chocolate cake mix
1 c. cake flour or may use all-purpose flour
1/2 c. sugar
1 t. baking soda
1/2 c. water or milk
1/3 c. oil or softened butter
1 egg
1 t. pure vanilla extract
1/4 c. cocoa
1/3 c. sugar (additional)

Directions:
Mix cake mix according to package directions.  Combine above ingredients and add to cake mix batter. Bake according to package directions. Cake may need to bake 5-10 additional minutes.

Submitter: 
Shearone
Description: 
Add extender to cake mixes for added volume or servings.
