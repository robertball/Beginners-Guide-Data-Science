Title:
Easy Crockpot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 chicken breasts, cubed
2 14 oz cans Italian diced tomatoes
1 envelope savory herb and garlic soup. It
Bow tie pasta
Shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mix soup mix and tomatoes in crock pot and stir, add chicken and stir. Cook on low for 6-8 hours.
Cook 6 servings pasta according to directions.
To serve, layer pasta, chicken and cheese on plates.
