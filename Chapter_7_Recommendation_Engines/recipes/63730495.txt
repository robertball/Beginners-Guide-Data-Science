Title:
Tomato Lime Garlic Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups rough chopped sun dried tomatoes
2 cups rough chopped tomatoes (sees removed)
3/4 cup fresh squeezed lemon juice
3/4 cup fresh squeezed lime juice
2 whole garlic clusters, minced
1 cup white wine
1/3 cup white sugar (or more to taste)
2 cups of heavy cream
4 Tsp salt
3 Tsp white pepper
4 tablespoons of butter

Directions:
Place sun dried tomatoes, tomatoes, lemon juice, lime juice, garlic, wine and sugar in the pan over medium heat.  Bring to a simmer and reduce the mixture by half
Add the heavy cream and reduce until thick
Add the butter and turn the heat to low until served over poultry/fish

Submitter: 
Craig Carlson
Description: 
This recipe is a knock off of the the tomato lime garlic sauce that used to be regularly served at the Bonefish Grill.
