Title:
Good Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 quarts water
3 cups sugar
1 tablespoon lemon extract
2 tablespoons citric acid
1/2 bag ice
3 slices lemons

Directions:
Mix top 4 ingredients together.  Add ice and lemons and serve.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
I got this from my sister-in-law and it has become a standard.  Everyone loves it!!!  We used it so much at church events that it became known as Monson Punch.  One of my most requested recipes!!  We made it for my niece's wedding and we couldn't make it fast enough!
