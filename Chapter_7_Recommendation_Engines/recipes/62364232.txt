Title:
Black bean salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can white beans (Goya)
1 can black beans (Goya)
1 cucumber
1 red pepper
1 onion or bunch of green onions
1/2 c mayo
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and chill for half hour before serving.
