Title:
Grandma's Opal's Chunky Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart fresh cranberries (or 4 cups)
1 cup sugar
2 cups water
1 small box lemon Jell-O
15 marshmallows (mini or big?...must test)
1 cup chopped pecans
1 cup diced apples

Directions:
Run cranberries through food chopper.  Add water and sugar and boil 5 minutes.  Remove from fire.
Dissolve gelatin and marshmallows in hot liquid.
When cool, add nuts and celery.  Chill until firm and serve.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
My grandma Opal made this cranberry salad every Christmas.  It's more like a chunky relish rather than a Jell-O mold syle and so much better.
