Title:
Texas Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans Black beans
2 cans Black Eyed Peas
1 can of carrots, sliced thin (or 1 1/2 cups fresh and par-boiled)
1 bunch fresh cilantro chopped finely
1 can chopped green chiles peppers or 1/2 cup chopped fresh green peppers anaheim, or serrano
1 can of campbells condensed tomato soup
1/4 cup of rice wine vinegar (seasoned or not)
1/8 cup of sugar
1 tbsp salt

Directions:
1.  In a large mixing bowl, mix beans, peas, carrots, chiles and cilantro
2.  In a smaller bowl, mix tomato soup, rice wine vinegar, sugar and salt.
3. Add liquid mixture to larger bowl, mix thoroughly.
1.  Chill for a few hours before eating for best taste.

Submitter: 
charlotte henry
Description: 
This recipe has been handed down from our mama in Texas!  This is nice on baked potatoes, with tortilla chips, on a burrito or even on salad. It is such a beautiful summer cool dish.
