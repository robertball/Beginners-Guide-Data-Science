Title:
Peppermint Pattie Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Devils food cake mix
1 stick of butter (softened)
1 Tbsp water
2 eggs
Confectioners sugar
Peppermint Patties

Directions:
Mix ingredients together well.  Roll into 1 inch balls then roll in confectioners sugar.
Bake 375 degrees for 8 minutes.
Immediately top with Peppermint Pattie.
Cool cookies.
