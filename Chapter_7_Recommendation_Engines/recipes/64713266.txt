Title:
Tequila Lime Chicken (Marinade) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c tequila (blanco)
1/3 c lime juice
1/3 c olive oil
1/2-1 tsp lime zest
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp chili powder
1 tsp sea salt
1-2 garlic cloves
1-2 inches hatch green chili mild (charred)
1 rotisserie chicken cut into pieces

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in blender
Pour over chicken in glass pyrex pan
Bake 400 degrees for 40 min
