Title:
Home on the Range Ranch Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds Idaho potatoes, peeled and chopped
3 tablespoons butter, softened
1/3 cup half and half
1 tablespoon canned chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
2 tablespoons Hidden Valley Ranch® Original Ranch® Dressing Mix
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place potatoes in large pan with water over high heat and boil until tender but still firm, about 15 - 20 minutes.
Heat butter, half and half and peppers in sauce pan over low heat until combined.
Drain potatoes and place in mixer with butter sauce.  Add dressing mix and process until smooth.  Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
This dish is hearty in nature and bold in flavor, not unlike many things in Texas. It would satisfy any hungry cowboy.
