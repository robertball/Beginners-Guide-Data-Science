Title:
KOOL-AID速 Lemonade-Iced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986472.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups boiling water
4 regular-size tea bags
1 cup SPLENDA速 No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 (0.13 ounce) package KOOL-AID速 Lemonade Flavor Unsweetened Soft Drink Mix
4 cups cold water
Optional garnish: lemon slices

Directions:
Pour boiling water over tea bags in large heatproof pitcher; let stand 10 min. Remove and discard tea bags.
Add SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener and soft drink mix; stir until drink mix is dissolved. Stir in cold water. Serve over ice cubes in eight tall glasses.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA速 Sweetener Products
Description: 
Cool off on a hot summer day with a tall refreshing glass of this lemony iced tea.
