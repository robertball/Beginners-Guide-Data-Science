Title:
Pork Diane Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/715998.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons of olive oil
1 10 oz Pork Loin
1/2 Onion
2 cloves of garlic
8 oz mushrooms
2 oz of brandy
2 oz of chicken stock
2 oz of cream or sour cream
2 Oz of mozzarella cheese (Optional)
Small loaf of ciabatta bread

Directions:
slice pork loin in half, Pound the pork loin thin between cling wrap, or in a large one gallon plastic bag.
In a fry pan, add some olive oil, add flattened pork loin, and cook thru. When Done You Can Cut Into Slices Or Leave whole. Set the pork loin a side.
In a fry pan add some olive oil, add mushrooms, And Onions and brown.
After they are almost browned add garlic.
when the garlic starts to brown add brandy and flambe.
After the flame goes out, add chicken stock, cream, mustard, and worcestershire sauce, and reduce.
add pork back in to warm as the sauce is reducing.
if you wish put some mozzarella cheese into the sauce prior to serving.

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
I adapted this recipe from steak Diane sauce, and used pork pounded very flat
