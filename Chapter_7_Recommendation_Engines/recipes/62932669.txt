Title:
Rueben Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of egg noodles
Thousand Island Dressing & Mayonnaise.
6 ounces shredded cheddar cheese
6 ounces shredded swiss
12 ounces sauerkraut
1 lb deli corned beef, sliced thick

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Cut corned beef into 1" squares.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain off most water, but not completely.
Add the dressings to the noodles little at a time to desired creaminess; mix well. Stir in cheese, sauerkraut and corned beef. Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
