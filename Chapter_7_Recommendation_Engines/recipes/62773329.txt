Title:
3 Bean Salad/Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tins of your favorite combination of beans or corn, drained and rinsed
1 clove garlic, peeled and grated
1/2 red onion, peeled and minced
1/2 cucumber, peeled, seeded and finely chopped
1/2 small red pepper, seeded and finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon, peeled and grated fresh ginger
1 fresh chili, seeded and finely chopped (choose a chili with a heat level to your taste)
1 15 oz. tin chopped tomatoes, drained or 2 fresh tomatoes, seeded and finely chopped
1 handful of fresh cilantro chopped
1/2 cup cidar vinegar
2 tablespoons prepared honey or dijon mustard
4 tablespoons canola oil
2 teaspoons sugar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl combine beans, onions, chili, cucumber, garlic, ginger, tomato and cilantro.
In a seperate small bowl whisk together vinegar, oil, mustard, sugar and salt and pepper to taste.
Pour over the vegetables and allow to sit for 1 hour before refrigerating.

Submitter: 
Liz and Sylvan
Description: 
This dish can be served as a three bean salad or a salsa.

I first had this dish at a church dinner on our beloved Cayman Brac.  It is a typical Caribbean recipe as it makes use of pantry staples.  I've added fresh ingredients where possible.
