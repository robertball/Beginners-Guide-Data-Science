Title:
Egg Paint Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/142039.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg yolk
4 drops red food coloring

Directions:
In small cup or bowl , stir the egg yolk with a small amount of food  coloring. Use a clean paintbrush to create designs on the cookies before you bake them.

Submitter: 
Christie B
Description: 
This shiny paint is a nice alternative to frosting your cookies.  Paint the cookies before you pop them in the oven!
