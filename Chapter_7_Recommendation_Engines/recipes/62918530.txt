Title:
Berry Cherry Cheesecake Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. fat free cream cheese
1/2 c sugar
2 tsp vanilla
2 egg whites or 1/4 c. egg substitute
1 can light cherry pie filling
2 c. fresh or frozen blueberries
1 pkg. yellow cake mix
1/2 c. lemon or vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Preheat 325 degrees. Spray a 13 x 9 glass pan.
Mix cream cheese, vanilla and sugar,  Add egg and blend well.  Pour into 13x9.  Next, combine blueberries and pie filling, then spread over top of cheesecake.  In same bowl, combine cakemix and yogurt until crumby.  Sprinkle evenly overtop of cheescake. Bake for about 25 min.  Cool, cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
