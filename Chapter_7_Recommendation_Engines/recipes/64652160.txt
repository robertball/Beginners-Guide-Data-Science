Title:
MAMA MIA! Mozzarella and Roasted Red Pepper Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise (I use Hellmanns®)
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup finely shredded mozzarella cheese
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1/4-1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/4 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
1/4 cup roasted red peppers, drained and chopped
3 green onions, chopped fine
Additional roasted red peppers, drained and chopped, for garnishing, optional

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except for additional roasted red peppers; mix well.
Spoon into serving bowl.
Top with additional roasted red peppers, optional.
Serve or refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
This dip is too simple to be this good! Serve with Triscuit's® or your favorite crackers. Also great with pretzels or as a veggie dip. Enjoy!
