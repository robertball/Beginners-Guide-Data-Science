Title:
Refreshing Turkey Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
1-2 tsp onion powder or 1/3 cup chopped fresh onion
1-2 tsp ground pepper (white or black)
1-2 tsp fresh or dried dill weed
3 tbsp lemon juice
1 cup garbanzo beans (less than 1 - 15oz can)
flavored tortilla wraps (large size)
neufchatal or fat free cream cheese (1 tablespoon per wrap)
1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
baby carrots sliced into "matchsticks"
sprouts (clover, alfalfa, bean)

Directions:
Brown 1 lb ground turkey.
Add onion, pepper, and dill to taste, along with lemon juice and equal amount water.Stir in garbanzo beans. Continue to heat over medium high until liquid has cooked down.
Spread spinich herb "wraps" or other tortillas with a thin but tacky layer of cream cheese. Place spoonfuls of filling down center of wrap. Top with parmesan, carrot sticks, then sprouts and wrap.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
LAMB2LOVE
Description: 
The tangy lemon and fragrant dill of this low fat turkey wrap loaded with crisp and tasty veggies will be sure to please on your summer table!
