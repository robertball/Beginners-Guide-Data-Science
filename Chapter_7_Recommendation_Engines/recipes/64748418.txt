Title:
Greek Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6664713.jpg
Ingredients:
Dressing
1/2 c olive oil
1/4 c lemon juice
splash red wine vinegar
1 tsp oregano
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp salt, to taste
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes, to taste
Salad
1/2 cucumber, peeled, seeds scooped slightly, chopped
1/2 c cherry tomatoes, halved
1-2 avocados, chopped
1/2 c olives
1/4 to 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced, chopped
4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
1/4 c basil, to taste, chopped
1/4 c parsley, to taste, chopped

Directions:
combine dressing ingredients
combine salad ingredients
toss all
add chicken, shrimp or other protein
