Title:
Restaurant-Style Zuppa Toscana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package italian sausage
1/4 C Butter
1/4 C Flour
2 potatoes, cut into 1/4-inch slices
3/4 cup chopped onion
1 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic
2 cups kale - washed, dried, and shredded
2 cans chicken stock
4 C Milk (approx)

Directions:
Melt butter in a large saucepan.  Place onions and sausage in saucepan and cook over medium heat until sausage is cooked. Add garlic and cook an additional minute.  Add flour, cook through, then make a roux with chicken stock.
Add milk to desired consistency, and potatoes, simmer 15 minutes.
Add kale. Simmer 4 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
KESSIANNE
Description: 
Crumbled bacon, Italian sausage, chopped kale and cream are combined in this chicken stock based soup with potatoes.Modified from: Restaurant-Style Zuppa Toscana
