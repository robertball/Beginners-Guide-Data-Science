Title:
Beef and Macaroni Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/799656.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups raw macaroni
2.5 pound of ground beef
2 teaspoon garlic powder
3 teaspoon chili powder
Pinch salt
Pinch pepper
3 cans of tomato soup
4 oz mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook macaroni about 7 minutes.  Brown meat in largest saucepan and drain off all fat.
Add garlic, chili powder, salt and pepper.  Mix well and cook on low about 5 minutes.
Add cooked macaroni and tomato soup.  Heat thoroughly.
Just before serving ad grated cheese.  Cover and heat until cheese melts.
