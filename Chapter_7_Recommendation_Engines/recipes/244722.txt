Title:
Burger with Bacon Avocado Ranch Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2497401.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Avocado Dressing
6 slices thick-cut bacon cooked crisp, crumbled
1 medium tomato, seeded and diced
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 medium red onion, sliced
4 seeded hamburger rolls

Directions:
In a small bowl, add the bacon, tomato, and Hidden Valley(R)Original Ranch(R)Avocado Dressing, and mix well to create a salsa.
Shape the beef into 4 burgers. Grill the burgers and onion slices for about 5 to 7 minutes on each side or until an internal temperature of 160 degrees F has been reached.
Toast the rolls. Place a burger in each roll. Top each burger with 2 tablespoons of the salsa and grilled onions to taste, and serve.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley®
Description: 
Grilled hamburgers topped with bacon avocado ranch salsa are a great way to add flavor to your summer barbeques.
