Title:
Crock Pot Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
4 T butter
10 oz block sharp cheddar cheese, shredded (do not used bagged)
1/2 c sour cream
1 (10.75 oz) can condensed cheddar cheese soup
1/2 t dry mustard
1/2 t black pepper
1 c whole milk

Directions:
1.  Boil the macaroni in water for 6 minutes (drain).
2.  In a sauce pan, melt butter then add cheese.  Stir until cheese melts.
3.  Add the sour cream, soup, mustard, and pepper stirring after each addition.
4.  Add the milk slowy and whisk until combined.
5.  Add the macaroni to the slow cooker and then the cheese mixture and stir.  Cook on low for 2.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
