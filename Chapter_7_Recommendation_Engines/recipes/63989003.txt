Title:
Meatloaf Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1106908.jpg
Ingredients:
1 slice of meatloaf (your favourite)
1 TBS margarine or butter
2 slices of bread
3 slices of cheddar cheese (or shredded cheese)
ketchup
mustard (optional)

Directions:
1. Reheat your meatloaf slice in microwave. 2. Melt butter or margarine in non-stick skillet over medium heat. 3. Place both slices of bread in skillet on top of melted margarine. 4. Build sandwich in skillet onto one slice of bread in the following order: squirt of ketchup, slice of cheese, and warmed meatloaf. Top with mustard if using. Flip second slice of bread on top.  5. When bottom of sandwich is lightly browned, use a spatula to flip sandwich over and place slice of cheddar cheese on top of bread.  Allow cheese to melt a bit on top, then flip it over onto the skillet, allowing the cheese to melt and crisp in the skillet.  Repeat with second slice of cheese on top. Cheese will form a crispy crust on the outside of sandwich.

Submitter: 
Kitchen Kitty
Description: 
This grilled cheese and meatloaf sandwich has crisped cheese on the outside and warm melted cheese on the inside. Perfect with sliced pickles and extra ketchup for dipping.
