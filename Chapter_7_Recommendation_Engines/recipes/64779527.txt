Title:
Carnivore White BBQ Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless, bone in, chicken leg quarters (or try with skin)
1.5 c mayo
1/4 c sour cream
1/2 tsp siracha
layer of chili powder (1/2 to 1 tsp?)
layer of garlic powder (1/2 to 1 tsp?)
1/4 to 1/2 tsp turmeric
maybe add smoked paprika
1/4 tsp liquid smoke plus more to baste
salt/pepper to taste
pinch red pepper

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
Combine mayo, sour cream, siracha, chili powder, garlic powder, turmeric, liquid smoke, salt/pepper; mix well
Baste chicken quarters with very small amount of liquid smoke
Put chicken in greased baking dish or foil lined pan
Cover with sauce
Sprinkle with crushed red pepper
Bake 60 min at 350 or until done
