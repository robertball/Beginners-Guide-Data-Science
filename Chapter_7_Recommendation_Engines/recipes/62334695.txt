Title:
Quick Vegetable pasta salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/387260.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb bag of frozen mixed veggies, broccoli, cauliflower and carrot mix
1 8 ounce bag frozen pepper strips
1 lb box of pasta, penne or rotini
1/4 chopped olives, green or Kalamata
3 ounces Feta cheese
1 cup champagne vinegar dressing, store bought

Directions:
Boil pasta for 10 minutes or until al dente.  WHen there is 4 minutes left to the pasta's cooking time, add both bags of frozen veggies.  Drain, well, toss pasta and veggies with oils, feta cheese and dressing. Serve hot or allow to cool over night fora cold pasta salad.
