Title:
Shrimp Etoufee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
3 table spoons vegetable oil
1/4 cup all purpose flour
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper (for color)
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 small chopped onion
1 pound deveined & peeled shrimp
1 10.75 ounce can chicken broth
4 cups cooked rice
1 tablespoon Zatarain's Creole Seasoning

Directions:
In a skillet, melt butter & vegetable oil until it bubbles. Be mindful not to let it burn.
Gradually add flour by whisking it into the butter/oil mixture. After blending flour, butter & oil, let it come to a bubble while whisking and avoiding the chance of browning.
After three minutes, add garlic & onion to roux.
When onions are clear, add celery, bell pepper, Zatarain's Creole Seasoning & shrimp.
Keep stiring with a wooden spoon until well blended and then add chicken broth. Blend smoothly and gently with your whisk. The mixture will be a smooth and yellow/saffron color.
Let simmer until it thickens.
Spoon into a bowl.
Put cooked rice in a dry measuring cup and place on top of etoufee.
Top with green onions,serve & enjoy.
