Title:
Melanie's Panchetta Penne in Vodka Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound penne pasta
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup butter
1.5 cups vodka
2 teaspoon dried red pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
16 ounces tomato sauce
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
2 cup heavy cream
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
12 oz Panchetta

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and reserve.
In a large saucepan cook panchetta until crispy, remove from pan and follow next step
Add oil & melt butter over medium low heat; add vodka, red pepper flakes and Worcestershire sauce. Stir well and simmer for 5 minutes.
Blend in tomato sauce, tomato paste and cream; increase heat to bring to a boil. Simmer for 5 minutes or until sauce has slightly thickened; season with salt and pepper to taste.
Reduce heat to very low and stir in cheese; add cooked pasta and more cheese, if desired. Serve.

Submitter: 
Melanie Greer
Description: 
Penne or rigatoni served in a vodka cream sauce. So easy and when you make it, everyone is so impressed! The sauce can made a day in advance, which really brings out all the flavors.Modified from: Penne in Vodka Cream
