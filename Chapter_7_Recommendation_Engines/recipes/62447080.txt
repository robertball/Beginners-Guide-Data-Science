Title:
The Bubbly Berry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ice
1-16 fl. Oz. Daily’s Strawberry Daiquiri/Margarita mix,well chilled
1-2 Ltr.-Sprite,well chilled
1-8 oz. pkg. Birdseye Frozen Strawberries,slight thawed
Fresh strawberries,well chilled,cut almost in half
Fruit Skewers-Pineapple,orange slice,marachino cherry,well chilled

Directions:
In a Blender combine:
4 cups of ice
16 fl. Oz. of Daily’s Strawberry Daiquiri/Margarita mix
4 fl. Oz. of Sprite
4 Oz. Birdeye Frozen Strawberries
Pour into your favorite glass
Garnish with a fresh strawberry and fruit skewer

Submitter: 
Kat Keen
Description: 
Non-alcoholic drink to enjoy on a lazy afternoon.
