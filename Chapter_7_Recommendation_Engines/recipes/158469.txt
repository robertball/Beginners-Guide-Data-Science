Title:
Golden Cherry Granola Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast
2 cups bread flour
1 1/4 cups whole wheat flour
3 tablespoons nonfat dry milk powder
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 1/4 cups water
2 tablespoons honey
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2/3 cup granola
1/2 cup dried tart cherries

Directions:
Place yeast in appropriate dispenser in bread machine. Add bread flour, whole wheat flour, dry milk powder and salt to bread machine in order suggested by manufacturer's manual. Add water, honey and vegetable oil to dry ingredients. Set machine on correct cycle.
Add granola and cherries at the end of the first kneading or when buzzer sounds.
Serve bread warm or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
The Cherry Marketing Institute
Description: 
Dried tart cherries add color and zest to this whole wheat and granola bread.
