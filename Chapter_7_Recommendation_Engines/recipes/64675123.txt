Title:
Jennie-Lou S&S; fish sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-5 sliced and seeded Jalapeno peppers
3 cups of sugar(caster)
5 peeled and sliced medium tomatoes
1/4 cup of rice vinegar
1/8 white grape juice or Apple juice
1/4 cup of water

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium -high heat
1.) stir sugar and peppers
2.) slowly add sliced tomatoes. Once sugar is evenly coated.
3.) Add 1/2 of the vinegar and Apple juice
4.) Bring to a boil and add remaining liquids ( water)
5.) Pour sauce through mesh strainer.
6.) Dice 5-10 pepper pieces and add back to sauce.
7.) Let set will get thicker as it cools
