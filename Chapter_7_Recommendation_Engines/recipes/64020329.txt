Title:
Spicy Feta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1124928.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup plain yoghurt (drain liquid)
1/2 cup olive oil
3 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tbsp paprika
1 tsp freshly ground pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper, or to taste
Pinch sugar
1 lb feta, cut into cubes (creamy Greek or Macedonian feta is best)

Directions:
Combine everything except the feta in food processor and pulse until blended. Add feta, a couple cupes at a time. Process until thick and smooth.
Refrigerate for at least an hour before serving.
Great on pitas, used as a dip or alongside souvlaki.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
This is best feta dip ever.  It is so tangy and salty and completely addictive. People often comment that they would like to lick the bowl.
