Title:
Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tomatoes, chopped
3 tomatillos, chopped
2 jalapeños, minced
Olive oil
Course kosher salt
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 large white onion, chopped
4 Serrano Chiles, minced
4 Mangoes, chopped
2 limes
1 large bunch cilantro, chopped or torn
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Drizzle the Jalapeños, tomatillos, & half the tomatoes with olive oil. Sprinkle generously with salt and broil on low for 10 mins. Allow to cool for 10 minutes following
In the meantime, pulse the the onion, garlic Chile’s, mango & cilantro at low speed until just chunky enough. Set aside.
Pulse broiled/cooled Ingredients until slightly chunky.
Combine both of raw and broiled ingredients well.
Add cumin and garlic powder.
Sprinkle with the juice of two limes and combine all ingredients well.
Refrigerate for At least 3 hours before serving

Submitter: 
Renee_poole
Description: 
Chunky mango salsa with roasted jalapeños and other fresh ingredients to bring out the flavor of this famous dipping sauce!
