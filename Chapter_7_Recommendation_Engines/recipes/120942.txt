Title:
Picante Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991950.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (4 ounce) boneless skinless chicken breast halves
1 (16 ounce) jar picante sauce
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
Hot cooked rice

Directions:
Place chicken in a greased shallow 2-qt. baking dish. In a small bowl, combine the picante sauce, brown sugar and mustard; pour over chicken. Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees F for 30-35 minutes or until chicken juices run clear. Serve over rice if desired.

Submitter: 
Karen  Stattelman
Description: 
My husband used to claim this entree as his specialty until I made it and discovered how quick and easy it is. Our two sons love the juicy chicken while my husband and I enjoy the twist that brown sugar and mustard give the picante sauce.
