Title:
Spicy herb-coated halibut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp butter, melted
1 tsp garlic pepper
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp dried thyme
1 lb halibut

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except halibut. Place in single layer, spoon mixture on top of fish. Bake @ 375 for 13-18min.

Submitter: 
Chocolate
Description: 
Easy and quick spicy herb coated halibut. (Can sub any white fish.)
