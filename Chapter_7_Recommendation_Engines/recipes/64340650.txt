Title:
Clam Chowder from Nancy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
1 lb of pork sausage (Bob Evans or other regular sausage)
8 red potatoes (unpeeled), chopped
1 large onion, chopped
3 16 oz cans of baby clams--drain and reserve liquid
1 qt half & half
2-3 cans evaporated milk
2-3 bay leaves
salt and pepper to taste
2-3 t thyme to taste
1-2 t marjoram to taste
1-2 t paprica to taste
Optional:
A couple of tablespoons of sugar to lessen "clammy" taste and enhance flavor
2-3 or more drops of Tabasco sauce

Directions:
in a large soup pot, brown sausage.
Add the butter, heating until it's melted.
Add onions and salute until until translucent.
Add herbs to onions.
Add potatoes, reserved clam juice, and enough water to cover potatoes.
Boil potatoes until tender, about 20 minutes.
Add Half & half and evaporated milk and heat, but do not boil.
Add clams and heat.
Thicken with paste made of flour and water.
Add sugar and tabasco sauce, if desired.
