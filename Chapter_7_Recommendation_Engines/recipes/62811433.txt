Title:
Fresh Summertime Bowtie Ham Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/661327.jpg
Ingredients:
16 0z package Bowtie pasta (or any other pasta)
16 Oz package ham chunks
20 grape tomatoes - sliced in half
1/2 Cup yellow pepper (diced)
1/2 Cup red pepper (diced)
1/2 Cup orange pepper (diced)
(or 1 1/2 cups variations of any peppers)
6 green onions (chopped)
2 Tbs minced garlic
1 1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
1/4 cup fresh cilantro (chopped)
1/2 cup Romano shredded cheese

Directions:
In a large pot, boil and cook past according to package.  Drain.
While pasta is cooking, open ham chunks and drain.
In a large bowl, add sliced tomatoes, chopped peppers, green onions, minced garlic, ham chunks, salt and pepper.  Mix together well.  Add pasta and mayonnaise and mix well.  Add fresh cilantro and Romano cheese, mix one last time.  Chill for at least one hour before service.

Submitter: 
AbbiCarp
Description: 
I made up this recipe while trying to figure out a summer pasta salad to go with our kabobs that we were grilling.  I threw in a little of this and a little of that and the family went nuts!  To make this healthier, use fat-free mayonnaise and wheat pasta.
