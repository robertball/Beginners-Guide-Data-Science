Title:
Spicy Slow-Cooker BBQ Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4947829.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 lbs pork baby back ribs (thaw if frozen)
2 cups ketchup
1 cup chili sauce
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
4 tablespoons vinegar
2 teaspoons dried oregano
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1 dash hot sauce
salt and pepper to taste
optional: if you want like more heat add 1 small Habanero chopped and seeded pepper.

Directions:
Preheat oven o 400 degrees F
Season ribs with salt and pepper and place rib rack on baking sheet.
Brown in oven 15 minutes per side then remove and drain fat.
In large mixing bowl, combine the ketchup, chili sauce, brown sugar, oregano, vinegar,  hot sauce and Worcestershire sauce.
Place ribs n slow cooker and pour mixed sauce over ribs to coat.
Cover and cook on low 7 hours or until tender.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
You never can go wrong with a simple slow cook rib dish. I like mine on the spicy side so I will also throw into the slow cooker a chopped seeded Habanero pepper.
