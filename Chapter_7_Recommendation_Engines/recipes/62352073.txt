Title:
Grilled Veggie! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4c. Extra virgin olive oil
4 tabl. Balsamic vinegar
3 minced garlic cloves
16 fresh asparagus spears
8 fresh mushroom caps
2 sm. zucchini halved, and slice 1/4-1/2"
1 med. red bell sliced
1/2 c. fetta cheese

Directions:
In a small bowl combine Olive oil, vinegar, garlic, asparagus, mushrooms, zucchini and red bell.
Heat grill, cover with foil, and spary with non-stick. Grill veggies 4 min, turn, and continue to grill another 4-6 min. Sprinkle with fetta and serve!

Submitter: 
Mommy bakes
Description: 
This is a flavorful way to get all members of your family to eat their veggies! Using Olive oil, and balsamic to add flavor, then grilled, and topped with fetta cheese-Yummy!
