Title:
Honeydew Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3649908.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 honeydew melon, peeled and seeded
1 sweet potato, peeled
1 Granny Smith apple
1/4 lemon, peeled and seeded
3 leaves kale
1 (2 inch) piece fresh ginger root

Directions:
Process honeydew melon, sweet potato, Granny Smith apple, lemon, kale leaves, and ginger root in a juicer. Stir juice thoroughly.
Pour juice into two 1-pint jars. Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Dalton Sexton
Description: 
This is for the juicing community. 
This is a light and sweet juice that has its share of protein as well.
 I gave it this name because the surface foam, before you stir it, looks just like traditional party punch with the green and orange swirl.
