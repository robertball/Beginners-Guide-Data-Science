Title:
Hummus Quesadilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cooked chicken breast, shredded
6 tablespoons red pepper hummus
4 tablespoons medium salsa
1 cup shredded reduced fat cheddar cheese
2 flour tortillas
garlic powder, to taste (optional)

Directions:
Evenly spread the hummus and salsa on each tortilla.
Next, layer with chicken; placing chicken on one half of the tortillas.
Sprinkle with garlic powder.
Sprinkle cheese evenly over chicken.
Carefully fold over tortillas and place in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cheese is melted.
Serve with extra salsa or guacamole.

Submitter: 
A Turtle in the Kitchen
Description: 
I made this for a quick lunch using leftover BBQ chicken. The hummus gives it a nice creaminess and is healthier than sour cream.
