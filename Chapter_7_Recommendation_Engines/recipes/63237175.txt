Title:
Oreo Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/843497.jpg
Ingredients:
Cookie:
2 chocolate cake mixes (only Betty Crocker) *don't follow box instructions
4 eggs
2/3 cup canola oil
Icing:
1 block cream cheese
4 cups icing sugar
vanilla to taste
2 TBsp butter

Directions:
Mix cookie ingredients together and form into small balls. Bake @ 350 for 7-8 min. Let cookies cool on cooling rack before icing.
Cream together icing ingredients and spread on cookies to make sandwiches.
