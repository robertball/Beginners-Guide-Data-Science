Title:
Chicken Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
1/4 Cup Tuscan Chicken Blend (Tones-Grinder)
1/4 Cup Paprika
1/4 Cup Fresh Ground Pepper
2 Tbs. Onion Powder
2 Tbs. Garlic Powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Pat the Chicken Dry & Rub Chicken down with Olive Oil. Rub in the "Rub" all over the chicken, evenly. This makes the chicken very brown upon baking or grilling with a crispy beautiful skin.

Submitter: 
NessTheMess
Description: 
This is one of my favorite rubs ever. It give a sweet & spicy flavor that can't be beat! This makes a beautiful roast chicken in the oven or on the Grill.
