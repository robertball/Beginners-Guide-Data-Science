Title:
Savory Grilled Flat Iron Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz Flat Iron steaks
2 TBLS Dijon Mustard
2 Large cloves of Garlic/Crushed
Worcestershire Sauce- to taste
Fresh cracked black pepper - to taste

Directions:
1. In a stoneware or glass dish spread 1 TBS. of Dijon mustard where each steak will lay.  Place steak on top.
2. Spread crushed garlic on top of steaks.
3. Sprinkle Worcestershire sauce generously on steaks and around steaks.
4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
5. Preheat grill on high heat.
6. Crack fresh ground pepper to taste on top side of steak.
7. Place mustard side of steak on grill- sear with top of grill open on high heat for 6 minutes.
8. Flip steaks and grill for additional 4 minutes or until desired doneness.
