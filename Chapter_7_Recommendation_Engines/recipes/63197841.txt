Title:
Famous Mexican Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages softened low fat cream cheese softened
1 can Hormel chili (beans only)
1 can Hormel chili (turkey meat)
1 (8 ounce) bag of finely shredded cheddar cheese
(Do not use low fat cheese as it does not melt the same.)
1 bag of tortilla chips

Directions:
In a pie dish, evenly spread cream cheese.  Then evenly spread the cans of chili as a second layer over the cream cheese. Then evenly layer the cheese on top of the chili.  Heat in the microwave until the cheese melts.  Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
slattekm
Description: 
A delicious bean dip - perfect for family parties.  Bring cool and heat up at when you arrive.
