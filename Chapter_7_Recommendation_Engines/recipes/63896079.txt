Title:
Dave's Wieners in Sweet Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10-oz jar currant or apple jelly
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 teaspoon celery seed
1/3 cup cider vinegar
2 pkg all-beef hotdogs

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients and bring to boil.
Cut wieners into bite size pieces.
Heat in sauce.
Place heated wieners in sauce in chafing dish.
