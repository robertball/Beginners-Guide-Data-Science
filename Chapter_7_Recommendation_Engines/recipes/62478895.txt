Title:
Asian Sweet Potato Pie I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/484310.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound) Asian Sweet Potato's
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/3 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1 egg/1 egg white
1/4 tsp cloves
1/4 tsp ginger
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp flour
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust

Directions:
Boil sweet potato whole in skin for 40 to 50 minutes, or until done.  Run cold water over the sweet potato, and remove the skin.
Break apart sweet potato in a bowl.  Add butter and mix well with mixer. Stir in sugars, flour, milk, eggs, nutmeg, cloves, ginger, lemon juice, cinnamon and vanilla. Beat on medium speed until mixture is smooth.  Pour filling into an unbaked pie crust.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 55 to 60 minutes, or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.  Pie will puff up like a souffle, and then will sink down as it cools.

Submitter: 
AlaskanDelicacy
Description: 
For this lovely pie, Asian sweet potatoes are boiled, peeled and mashed together with butter, sugar, milk and eggs, then seasoned with nutmeg, cinnamon and vanilla.Modified from: Sweet Potato Pie I
