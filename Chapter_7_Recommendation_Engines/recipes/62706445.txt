Title:
Steve's Champion Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground meat (we use either venison or beef)
1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
1/4 cup finely chopped celery
1/4 cup finely chopped carrot
1/4 cup diced peppers (green, red, yellow, orange)
1/2 pkg. Williams Chili Seasoning mix
1-16 oz. can Bush's medium chili beans
1-8 oz. can tomato sauce
1-6oz. can tomato paste
1/2 bottle of your favorite beer
1 Tbls. Chili Powder (or to taste)
1-14 oz. can diced tomatoes (optional)

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown ground meat until done then transfer to paper towel lined plate to drain. Reserve 2 tablespoons of pan drippings.
Saute next four ingredients in the pan drippings until veggies are crisp tender. Add drained meat and Chili seasoning, mix well and allow to simmer.
Meanwhile, in large saucepan combine chili beans, tomato sauce, tomato paste, diced tomatoes (if desired) and beer. Stir until mixture is smooth.
Add veggies and meat to chili in saucepan. Stir in chili powder. Bring to bubbling then reduce heat and let simmer 30 minutes to an hour.

Submitter: 
Lisa Garnier
Description: 
Over the years my husband and I have worked to create our favorite chili. This thick, chunky veggie-filled concoction is a great warm up on cold nights.
