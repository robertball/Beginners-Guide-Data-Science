Title:
NO better grilled shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh peeled shrimp tail on
1/4 c soy sauce
1/8 c sriracha
1/8 c asian mustard
1 t chinese five spice
1/4 c brown sugar

Directions:
whisk together ingredients, and marinate shrimp for 1 hr. grill shrimp until done.
