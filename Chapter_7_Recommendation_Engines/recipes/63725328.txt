Title:
Classic Italian Basil Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
120ml/8tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
60g/2oz fresh Basil Leaves - rinsed and dried
2 cloves Garlic - peeled
2tbsp Pine Nuts - lightly toasted
1tsp Salt
60g/4tbsp Parmigiano Reggiano Cheese - freshly grated
30g/2tbsp Pecorino Romano Cheese - freshly grated
1Â½oz Butter - softened to room temperature
1lb dried Pasta - Spaghettini/Linguine/Spaghetti/Fettucine

Directions:
Put pine nuts in a little frying pan, on high heat and, stirring continuously, lightly toast;
Put the garlic, basil, pine nuts, salt, and olive oil in a bowl (or food processor) and using a blender, blend until the mixture is smooth and creamy;
Stir, or if having used a processor, transfer the contents to a bowl, and mix in the 2 grated cheeses;
While the pasta you will serve with the pesto is cooking, add 2tbsp of the pasta water and the butter to the pesto and mix well;
When the pasta is done, toss it with the pesto and serve at once.
