Title:
Beer Brat Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Grilled Beer Brats (Chopped)
1 Head of Broccoli (Chopped)
1/2 c. Chopped Onion
1/2 c. Fresh Peppers
1/2 c. Mushroom
1/2 c. Mayo
1/2 15oz. can of Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 Egg
1/2 Breadcrumbs
1 c. Cheddar Cheese
1 TBSP. Garlic
Seasoning to taste

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350'
Saute vegetables until soft
Mix all ingredients (except breadcrumbs) in a large bowl until well incorporated
Pour into greased 9 x 9 Baking Dish and sprinkle breadcrumbs on top
Bake uncovered for 1 Hour
Let cool for 6-10 minutes.
Enjoy
