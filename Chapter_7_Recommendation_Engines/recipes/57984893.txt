Title:
Impossible Buttermilk Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup Bisquick
1/3 cup butter, (5 1/3 tbls) melted
1 teaspoon mexican vanilla
3 eggs

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a 9 inch pie plate.
Beat together white sugar, buttermilk, baking mix, melted butter or margarine, vanilla, and eggs until smooth.  Pour filling into pie plate.
Bake until knife inserted in center comes out clean, about 50 minutes. Cool 5 minutes.
Serve with fresh whipped cream and strawberrys,peaches,or any other fresh fruit.
