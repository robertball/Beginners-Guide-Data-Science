Title:
sticky buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
frozen bread dough for rolls about 15
3/4 cups white sugar
3/4 cups brown sugar
3/4 cups vanilla ice cream
3/4 cups butter

Directions:
prepare this the night before and let rise in oven
place the frozen rolls in a 13x9 pan ungreased
in a sauce pan melt together sugar, brown sugar, ice cream and butter over med heat until sugars are disolved pour warm mixture over rolls and place in oven uncovered and let rise over night bake at 350 degrees for 30 mins. serve warm

Submitter: 
dave
Description: 
this recipe always goes over great at our house the kids love to eat them warm right out of the oven
