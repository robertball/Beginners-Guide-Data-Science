Title:
Macadamia Nut Pesto Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30oz can garbanzo beans - drained, keep liquid (shake can before opening)
1 cup salted dry roasted macadamia nuts
5 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp onion powder
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt (add more or less depending on saltiness of nuts)
1 tsp pepper
150mL drained liquid from beans
2 - 3 tbsp pre-made pesto

Directions:
Add all ingredients (except pesto) to high powered blender or food processor.
Blend on high until desired consistency, adding more liquid if necessary.
Pulse / fold in pesto.
Serve with pita, veggies or chips.
