Title:
Avocado Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Avocados
3/4 cup of Sour Cream
1/3 cup of Milk
1 Tablespoon Lemon Juice
1/2 teaspoon Celery Salt
a few dashes of Hot Pepper Sauce

Directions:
Add the 2 Avocados, seeded, peeled and mashed. Combine the rest of the ingredients and mix.
If necessary stir in additional milk to achieve desired serving consistency.
Cover and refrigerate and use within a few hours to prevent discoloration.
