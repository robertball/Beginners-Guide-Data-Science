Title:
Warm Savvoy Cabbage and Fennel Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 half head of Savvoy cabbage shredded.
1 bulb fennell sliced into strips
1/2 tsp of red pepper flakes (optional if you want to add some zip)
1 tsp of fennel seeds partially crushed up.
3 tbsp olive oil
salt and papper to taste

Directions:
Add olive oil to your hot saute pan.
Add in the fennel and red pepper flakes.
Saute on medium heat until the fennel begins to carmelize just slightly - 5-7 minutes.  The edges should begin to brown lightly.
Add the shredded Savvoy cabbage.
Add the fennel seeds.
Season with salt and pepper.
Mix all the ingradients in the pan.  Stir frequently until the cabbage is wilted, but still has just enough crunch for your liking.
Serve hot.
Enjoy
