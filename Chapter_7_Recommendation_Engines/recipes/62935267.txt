Title:
Millennium Italian Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/719139.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 cup shells or elbow pasta
8 oz. cottage or ricotta cheese
12 oz grated mozzarella cheese
1 can of cubed tomatoes, in tomato sauce.
4 oz sliced pepperoni
1 green pepper, diced (Optional)
8 oz mushrooms quartered or sliced (Optional)
1/2 onion, diced (Optional)
Spices And herbs
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon of Oregano, dried
1/2 teaspoon of rosemary, dried
1/2 teaspoon of thyme, dried
1/2 teaspoon of Basel, dried
1 teaspoon of black pepper
salt to taste

Directions:
Cook shells as directed on package. Drain.
Saute ground beef, till browned
Saute onions, till slightly browned
mix in a bowl, all cottage or ricotta cheese, Some Mozzarella cheese, garlic, tomatoes, pasta, green pepper, mushrooms, spices and herbs, some peperoni, ground beef, and onions until well blended.
Note: if you add allot of peperoni you may want to decrease the salt or leave it out and let the diners to salt to there taste.
sprinkle some mozzarella cheese on top.
place pepperoni slices on top of mozzarella cheese.
Optional: Add More Green Peppers, onions, and add some sliced mushrooms to the top.
Bake at 375Â°F for 45 min. until hot and bubbly.
