Title:
Orange Cream Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3311180.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vanilla schnapps
1/4 cup orange juice
1 wedge orange, garnish

Directions:
Pour vanilla schnapps into a 4 ounce glass. Fill glass with orange juice and garnish with an orange wedge.

Submitter: 
ButterflyJen
Description: 
Tastes just like a frozen treat from the icebox...but with a kick! Orange juice mixed with vanilla schnapps.
