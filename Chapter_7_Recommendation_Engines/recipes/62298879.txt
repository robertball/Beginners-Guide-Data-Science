Title:
Pasta Con Broccoli with Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 to 1 bunch of chopped broccoli
8 ounces cooked pasta (shell or cavatelli)
1 cup chopped mushrooms
1 cup 1/2 & 1/2 (1 pint carton)
8 ounces grated Parmesan
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons spaghetti sauce
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Steam broccoli and set aside.  Cook pasta and set aside.
In sauce over medium heat, bring 1/2 & 1/2, butter and garlic seasoning to a low boil.  Once the milk has thickened add spaghetti sauce.  Fold in the mushrooms and stir for 2 minutes (this is important).
Add parmesan and blend until smooth.  Fold in pasta and broccoli.  Serve while hot.

Description: 
This is a pretty good interpretation of a famous Italian chain restaurant's dish.  Take a bite, close your eyes and you will feel like you are there.
