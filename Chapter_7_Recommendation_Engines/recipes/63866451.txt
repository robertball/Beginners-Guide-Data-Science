Title:
NYTimes granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 c. rolled oats
1 1/2 c. oat bran
1 1/2 c. coconut chips
1 1/2 c. pecans, chopped (or walnuts, almonds, etc.)
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 1/2 T. cinnamon
3/4 tsp. salt
1/2 c. oil + 1 T.
2/3 c. honey/maple syrup
1 1/2. T. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Line two baking sheets with parchment.
Toss together the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Combine oil, honey, and vanilla in a saucepan and warm till liquid is fluid. Stir this into the dry ingredients. Mix till evenly coated.
Spread granola evenly, leaving a donut hole in the center (for even baking) and bake on middle and lower racks, without stirring, for 30-40 minutes, rotating the the pans front to back and top to bottom halfway through, till golden.
Allow to cool, store in jars.
