Title:
Shrimp and Corn Maque Choux Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbsp olive oil
3/4 cup green pepper, small dice
3/4 cup red pepper, small dice
1 1/2 cups onion, small dice
2 (10 oz) packages frozen corn kernels
1 lb raw shrimp, any size peeled, deveined and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
2 cups heavy cream
3 plum tomatoes, seeded and small dice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat a pan over med heat and add the olive oil. Add peppers and onions to the pan and cook until tender (do not brown).
Add corn and shrimp to the pan, let cook for a few minutes and then add the cream and cook unitl cream thickens slightly.
Add tomatoes and season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
luci
Description: 
Maque choux (pronounced mock shoe is a traditional creamy corn and sweet pepper side dish that hails form southern Louisiana. This version includes bits of shrimp.
