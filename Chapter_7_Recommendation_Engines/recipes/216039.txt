Title:
Ultimate 7 Layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987691.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
1 tablespoon taco seasoning mix
1 cup KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1 cup your favorite salsa
1 cup shredded lettuce
1 cup KRAFT Mexican Style Finely Shredded Four Cheese
4 green onions, sliced
2 tablespoons sliced black olives
chips and crackers

Directions:
Mix beans and seasoning mix; spread onto bottom of pie plate.
Top with layers of all remaining ingredients except chips. Refrigerate several hours or until chilled.
Serve with chips and crackers.

Submitter: 
KNUDSEN
Description: 
A layered Mexican-inspired dip of refried beans, sour cream, salsa, lettuce, and cheese is garnished with green onions and black olives, then served with chips and crackers for a fresh-tasting appetizer.
