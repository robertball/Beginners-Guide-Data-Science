Title:
Cook'n Guard Dog-Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint  cream
1/3 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup chocolate syrup
1/2 oz Kahlua coffee liquer

Directions:
combine all ingredients and mix well, until sugar is dissolved.
pour into ice cream maker and freeze as directed.
(or if you are brave and don't have a machine,  mix it all. put into a freezable container once all the sugar had dissolved.
