Title:
Creamy Chicken and Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126899.jpg
Ingredients:
2	large carrots diced (about a cup)
2	medium stalks celery, diced (about a cup)
1	large onion, diced (about a cup)
4	cups chicken broth
2	cups water
2	cooked boneless skinless chicken breasts, diced
2	tablespoons chicken base
1	teaspoon dried parsley
1	teaspoon garlic powder, or roasted garlic rub seasoning (Napa style)
1/8	teaspoon turmeric powder
¾ 	teaspoon black pepper
¾	cup Rice blend (texmati, white, brown, wild and red quick cooking rice)
¼ 	cup flour
2	tablespoons butter or margarine
¾	cup half and half
¼	to ½ cup milk

Directions:
In a 5 quart Dutch oven or larger pot over medium heat, sauté onion and celery until softened almost softened; add carrot and garlic cook another 5 minutes, add broth, water and chicken. Bring just to boiling, then stir in rice; Cover and cook 15 to 20 minutes or until rice is just done.
Meanwhile, Combine salt, pepper and flour in a small bowl. in a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt butter. Whisk in flour mixture by tablespoons, to form a roux and stir cooking 1-2 minutes. Reduce heat to low stir in half and half a little at a time, until fully incorporated and smooth. Add milk if necessary, Cook until thickened, 5 minutes.
Stir cream mixture into soup. Cook over medium heat until heated through, 10 to 15 minutes.  Add extra milk to thin if needed.

Semigourmet
Description: 
Enjoy this hearty chicken and wild rice soup with a nice crusty bread!
