Title:
Little Dave's Big Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
93/7 lean Beef- (# of people divided by 4) +1 = lbs.
4-6 T /lb. of meat of bread crumbs (not too dry/not too wet)
Lawry's Steak and Chop marinade- 3-4 T./lb.
1-2 T/lb. Sweet Chili sauce
2-4 T McCormick's Montreal Steak seasoning
4-6 T Feta Cheese (optional)

Directions:
In lg. MB, combine all ingredients.
Chill (overnight if possible) in refrigerator.
Form into patties. Put on plate, separate w/ waxed paper. (To prevent plumping in the center while cooking, create an indention in the middle of the patty using your thumb.)
Grill to desired color.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
These are my brother's recipe for the best burgers out there. I love to add in the feta with the meat. It really creates a great flavor.
