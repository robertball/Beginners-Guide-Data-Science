Title:
Peanut Butter Graham Cracker Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of Creamy peanut Butter
1/2 cup of Pancake syrup
Graham Crackers

Directions:
Take the peanut butter and put in bowl and add as much pancake syrup as you like (I usually dont measure).
Mix real well, and put bowl in freezer for about 10 minutes til chilled and stiffened.
Then smear on graham crackers to make a sandwich, and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Amanda L.
Description: 
My kids love these. I only had a few ingredients one night for a dessert for the kids so I mixed up what I had on hand and we loved them, now these are a favorite!
