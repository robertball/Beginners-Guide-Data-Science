Title:
Steak Pizzaiola with Rigatoni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large cut of flat iron steak (about 3/4 pd)
salt & pepper
2 tsp balsamic vinegar
2 Tbs Olive oil
1 med onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 28 oz can stewed tomato
1 heaping Tbs Italian seasoning
pinch of crushed red pepper
1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup fresh basil, sliced or torn
fresh parsley, chopped for garnish
1 pd rigatoni or penne with lines

Directions:
rub steak with salt and pepper, drizzle with vinegar
cover and refrigerate for 30 min to 1 hr
bring pot of water to boil, add rigatoni
slice steak on angle
meanwhile, heat oil in pan
add onion and garlic, saute until translucent (about 2 min)
add steak to pan brown over med heat
add can of tomato, italian seasoning and red pepper
bring to a low boil
then turn heat to low
drain pasta
toss w/ steak and sauce
add parm and basil
arrange on plate
sprinkle with more parm and parsely
serve immediately

Submitter: 
KMOMMYZ
Description: 
A little bit of steak goes a long way in this dish, and it really satisfies!  Your kids will love the pizza taste of this simple pasta dish!
