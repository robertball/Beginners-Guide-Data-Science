Title:
Asian Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 teaspoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil (optional)
1/4 cup vegetable oil
3 tablespoons rice vinegar
1 (8 ounce) package pre- made fried rice noodles
Mixture of romaine, iceberg and spincach lettuce - rinsed, dried, and chopped
4 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and shredded
3 green onions, chopped
1 can mandarin oranges, drained
1 tablespoon sesame seeds, toasted

Directions:
Prepare the dressing 30 minutes ahead of time by combining the brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, salad oil, and rice vinegar in a salad dressing carafe.
add chicken, oranges and green onions.
In a large bowl prepare the lettuce and let chill for 20 minutes.
Just before serving, combine all ingredients.

Submitter: 
KKraft
Description: 
This is a crunchy, wonderfully satisfying salad that is fabulous with grilled chicken, leftover from yesterday 's barbecue. The dressing is sweet and sour, mingling brown sugar with soy sauce and rice wine vinegar. Fried rice noodles add the crunch. Serves six.Modified from: Asian Chicken Salad
