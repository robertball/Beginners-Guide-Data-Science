Title:
Italian Olive Oil Bread Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T crushed red pepper
1 T black pepper
1 T oregano, dried
1 T rosemary, dried
1 T basil, dried
1 T parsley, dried
1 T garlic powder
1 T garlic, dried minced
1 t salt

Directions:
Mix all together and store in sealed container.  To use, put 1 T of mix in small dipping bowl, pour olive oil over, and combine well.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Lovely blend of Italian spices to be mixed with olive oil and served with warm bread.
