Title:
Herb-and-Nut Cream Cheese Log Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987927.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
1/3 cup KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 cup chopped toasted PLANTERS Pecans, divided
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
RITZ Crackers

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, Parmesan and 1/4 cup nuts.
Shape into 8-inch log. Roll in combined remaining nuts and parsley; press gently into log to secure.
Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
RITZ
Description: 
Parmesan, cream cheese and toasted pecans are rolled in nuts and fresh parsley for a tasty, festive appetizer.
