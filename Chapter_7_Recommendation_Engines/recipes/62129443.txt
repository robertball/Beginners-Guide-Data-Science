Title:
Taco Salad Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Ranch-style beans, drained
1 can refried beans
1/2 pkg. taco seasoning mix
1 8 oz container sour cream
1 pkg. onion soup mix
1 jar salsa
1/4 head of iceberg lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces
1/2 cup crushed tortilla chips
1/4 cup shredded cheddar or taco blend cheese

Directions:
In a shallow casserole dish, mix together the Ranch-beans and the refried beans. Season with the taco seasoning, mix well. Spread on bottom on dish.
In a small bowl, mix the sour cream and the onion soup mix. Layered the sour cream mixture over the beans. Top with salsa. Refrigerate.
When ready to serve, layer the lettuce over the dip mixture. Sprinkle crushed chips over the lettuce, top with shredded cheese.

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
I made this up with ingredients I had when I needed something to take to a Cinco de Mayo luncheon. It has the ingredients of a layered taco dip, but the addition of lettuce makes it like a salad. The best of both worlds!
