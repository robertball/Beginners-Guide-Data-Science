Title:
Pita Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 garlic pita bread cut open
4 skinless, boneless chicken brest, cooked and cut into bit size pieces
3/4 cup tomato sauce
1 tbsp minced garlic
2 cups chicken broth
ground pepper to taste
4 tbsp butter
2 cups egg noodles, cooked
2 head lettece

Directions:
1.) On medium heat stir together chicken broth, garlic, pepper, tomato sauce, and chicken broth. Keep on heat until boiling and then add in egg noodles. While sauce in on the stove, take the lettece rip it into medium sized pieces and then clean.
2.) Stir in noodles and chicken for a few minutes and then put into pita bread without the sauce. Then pour the sauce onto the lettece. Then put the pita bread on top of the lettece and serve while still warm.
