Title:
Easiest, Best Ever Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 small onion, diced
1 tablespoon butter
1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can light red kidney beans
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1-2 teaspoons chili
1 can Rotel tomatoes with chiles
1 quart tomato juice

Directions:
Brown and drain meat.
Saute onions in butter.
Combine with meat and add remaining ingredients.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for an hour to meld flavors.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Quick and easy soup that will warm you up on a cold winter day.  Also easy to double or triple for a crowd.
