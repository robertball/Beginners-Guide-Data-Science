Title:
Spicy Egg Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Strips of bacon
7 Large eggs
1 tsp. dried oregano or 1 TBL fresh
2 TBL jarred minced jalapenos (more or less to taste)
1/2 Cup grated sharp cheddar cheese
Fresh ground black pepper to taste
1 TBL water

Directions:
Fry bacon over medium heat until crisp
While bacon is frying whisk together all remaining ingredients
Remove bacon from pan and crumble into egg mixture
Add egg mixture to pan and scramble until desired doneness
Remove from pan and serve additional cheese may be grated over the top as a garnish

Submitter: 
Matt Grifo
Description: 
Add a kick to your morning routine.  My 2 year old loves them jalapenos and all!
