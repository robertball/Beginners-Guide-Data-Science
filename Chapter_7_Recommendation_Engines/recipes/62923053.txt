Title:
** Easy Rich & Sweet Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Basic Muffin Recipe:
2 c. AP flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 c. softened butter
1/2 c. sugar
2 eggs
1/2 c. milk
Peach or Pear Muffins:
1 recipe basic muffins
1 c. diced fresh or canned peaches (or pears), patted dry
1/4 tsp. almond extract
Coconut Jumble Muffins:
1 recipe basic muffins
3/4 c. flaked coconut
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
2 Tbl. honey
2 tsp. grated orange peel
1 tsp. grated lemon peel
Frosted Pecan Muffins:
1 recipe basic muffins
3/4 c. chopped pecans
1/2 c. powdered sugar
2 tsp. water
1 drop vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees.
Grease 18 muffin-pan cups.
Basic Muffin Recipe:  Combine flour, baking powder & salt in med. bowl, set aside. Cream butter & sugar with hand mixer. Add eggs one at a time, beating a low speed after each addition.  Add dry ingredients & milk alternately, beginning and ending with dry ingredients. Mix until just well blended. Spoon into prepared cups, filling each about 3/4 full. Bake 20-25 min. until golden. Immediately remove from pan. Serve warm.
Peach or Pear Muffins:   Prepare muffin recipe as directed except reserve 2 Tbl. flour to toss with peaches or pears. Add almond extract to milk. Gently fold peaches into finished batter.
Coconut Jumble Muffins:   Prepare recipe as directed except add coconut & nutmeg to dry ingredients. Beat honey & citrus peels into butter/sugar mixture. Then proceed to as directed. Bake 15-20 min.
Frosted Pecan Muffins:   Add chopped pecans to basic batter. Bake as directed. Mix powdered sugar, water & vanilla to frost warm muffins.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Easy & moist. This was the muffin recipe I used when the kids were little; always had the basic ingredients on hand. Can be made plain or with coconut, peaches, pears, or frosted w/pecans.
