Title:
Almond Butter & Raspberry Smoothie Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large banana
1 1/2 cups frozen raspberries
1/2 cup almond milk
2 Tbsp. almond butter
1 Tbsp. honey, plus additional for drizzling
5 strawberries, sliced
1/4 cup granola

Directions:
Blend the banana, 1 cup of the raspberries, soy milk, ice, almond butter, and 1 tablespoon of the honey until smooth.
Transfer to a bowl and top with the strawberries, granola, remaining 1/2 cup raspberries, and a drizzle of honey.

Refreshing smoothie bowl featuring berries
