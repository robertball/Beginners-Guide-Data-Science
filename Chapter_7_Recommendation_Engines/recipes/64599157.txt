Title:
Big Ray's Spicy Apricot-Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3915027.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup apricot preserves
1/4 cup chili sauce
1 Tbsp hot mustard
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
8 boneless skinless chicken thighs

Directions:
Preheat broiler. In a small saucepan, combine the first five ingredients; cook and stir over medium heat until heated through.
Place chicken in a 13 x 9 baking pan coated with cooking spray. Broil 3-4 in. from heat 6-8 minutes on each side or until a thermometer reads 165Â°. Brush occasionally with preserves mixture during the last 5 minutes of cooking.
Cook's Note: Can also be done on the grill or even the crock pot, low for 6 hours.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Save yourself a trip to the store and check the fridge first. Chicken turns sweet and hot when you pull out the chili sauce, mustard and apricot preserves.
