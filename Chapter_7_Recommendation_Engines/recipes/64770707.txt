Title:
Texas Hill Country Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7475639.jpg
Ingredients:
-3lbs. 80/20 burger meat
-1 yellow onion diced
-1 8oz. can tomato sauce
-2 cloves garlic minced
-3 tblsp. flour
-12 tblsp. Fiesta brand quick chili mix seasoning
-1 container low sodium beef broth (32oz.)
-2 tblsp. cayenne pepper
-2 tblsp. season salt
-2 tblsp. black pepper
-1 -15 oz. can Hormel chili

Directions:
-Season meat with black pepper and season salt and brown together with diced onion.
-Drain
-Add flour, chili seasoning, and cayenne with about 1/3 of the beef broth.
-Stir with a fork til there are no lumps.
-Add the rest of the broth, tomato sauce, and canned chili.
-Add garlic and bring to a boil and then simmer for 30 minutes on low heat.
-Eat with some quality tamales and crackers. Donâ€™t forget to stir in Louisiana hot sauce once in your bowl.
