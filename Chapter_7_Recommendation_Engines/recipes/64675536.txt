Title:
Stuffed Mushrooms with Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 large mushrooms (about 1 1/2 Pounds)
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 5-ounce package fresh baby spinach
Kosher salt and black pepper
3/4 cup panko bread crumbs
1/2 cup Gruyere, grated (2 ounces)

Directions:
Heat over to 375
Remove stems from mushrooms, finely chop stems and reserve
Coat a baking sheet with oil
Bake the mushroom caps, stem-side down, until tender, 10-12 minutes
Heat oil in medium skillet over medium heat
Add garlic and chopped mushroom stems and cook until tender, 3-5 minutes
Add spinach, 1/4 teaspoon salt and 1/8 teaspoon pepper and cook until wilted.
In a bowl, combine the spinach mixture, bread crumbs and cheese
Spoon into mushroom caps and bake until golden, 12-15 minutes
The caps can be baked and stuffed (but not baked for the second time) up to 2 days in advance and refrigerated
