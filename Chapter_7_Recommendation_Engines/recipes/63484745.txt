Title:
Mom's Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1 egg
1/4 tsp paprika, oregano, pepper, salt
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
1 clove garlic minced

Directions:
mix all. shape into 24- 1 1/2" balls.  place on baking sheet.  bake uncovered at 400 degrees for 20 mins until browned.
