Title:
Baking Powder Biscuits-Calument Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 c all-purpose flour
1 T baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 c shortening
3/4 c milk

Directions:
Mix flour, baking powder and salt.  Cut in shortening.  Stir in milk with forck until soft dough is formed.  Turn out on floured board and knead 20 times.  Roll 1/2 inch thick, cut with floured 2-inch cutter.  Place on ungreased baking sheet.  Bake at 450F for 10 minutes or until golden brown.  Or drop dough by T, without kneading, on baking sheet.  Makes 16 biscuits.
