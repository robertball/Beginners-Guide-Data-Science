Title:
Crab Apple Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 gallon crab apples
water
1 cup sugar to 2 qt juice

Directions:
Pour boiling water over apples just to cover
Leave overnight
Drain off juice, measure
Put in large saucepan. Add 1 cup sugar to 2 qt juice.
Boil until sugar dissolved
Put into jars, strained through cloth

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
a great way to use up that bumper crop of crab apples
