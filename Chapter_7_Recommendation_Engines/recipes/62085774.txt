Title:
Tortilla Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package 10 in flour tortillas
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
2 small cans mushroom soup
2 cups milk
2 small can green chiles
1 can refried beans
cheese to taste

Directions:
In a pan cook ground beef and drain fat, add cream of mushroom soup, milk, green chiles and cook over med. heat for 10 mins.  Take each tortilla and spread with refried beans and place 2 in a 8x10 baking dish.  Spoon some of meat mixture on top and sprinkle with cheese, then put the next layer of tortillas and continue making sure you have enough meat mix to cover the top when finished. Bake at 350 for 30 to 40 mins.  Let cool for 10 mins and serve.

Submitter: 
Kim Casey Nugent Roberds
Description: 
Tortillas layered with beans, beef, green chiles, cheese and cream of mushroom soup
