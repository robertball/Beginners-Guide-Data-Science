Title:
Zucchini Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups shredded zucchini
1 sweet onion shredded
1 carrot shredded
2 tablespoons garlic
2 cooked chicken breasts cubed
1 can of mushroom soup
1 small sour cream
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 cup butter
1 garlic croutons

Directions:
mix soup, cream, pepper and garlic.
add veggies, chicken
melt butter and stir with croutons
in baking dish, put in half crouton mix
cover with veggie mix
apply balance of croutons
bake 40 minutes covered
bake 10 minutes uncovered
