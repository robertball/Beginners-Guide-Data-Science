Title:
Classic Orange Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
half of a peeled banana
6 fresh or frozen strawberries, washed without stems.
1 large scoop of frozen yogurt
12 oz. orange juice
a handful of ice cubes (to taste)

Directions:
Pour the orange juice into the blender. Add the remaining ingredients and blend thoroughly.  You can make your smoothie thicker or thinner by adding more or less ice.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a delicious fruit smoothie made without adding any extra sugar!
