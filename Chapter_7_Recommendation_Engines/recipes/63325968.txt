Title:
Decadent Hot Fudgy Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup water
6 level tablespoons cocoa
2 tablespoons canola oil
1/2 cup sugar
1 tablespoon dark corn syrup
pinch of salt
3 tablespoons butter
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In small saucepan, stir water into cocoa and oil.
Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until smooth.
Remove from heat.
Add sugar, corn syrup, and salt.  Return to medium heat and cook for 7-8 minutes or until slightly thickened, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and add butter and vanilla, stirring until blended.

Submitter: 
GINGERPET
Description: 
Unlike a lot of recipes, this calls for no evaporated milk.  The corn syrup and butter, make it a rich topping for ice cream.
