Title:
Parmesan Fried Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium unpeeled zucchini, cut into 1/8 inch rounds
1/2 cup Italian seasoned dry bread crumbs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp parsley
pepper to taste
2 eggs
Vegetable Oil â€“ enough to fill large skillet to depth of 1 inch
Parmesan cheese for garnish
Salt to sprinkle

Directions:
In a medium bowl mix bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, garlic powder, parsley, and pepper
In separate bowl, whisk eggs
Dip zucchini rounds into egg, then into bread crumb mixture to coat
Heat oil over medium heat
Fry zucchini until golden brown on both sides, about 3 minutes. Do not crowd skillet
Transfer zucchini to paper towels to drain, sprinkle with salt and parmesan and serve hot or at room temperature

Submitter: 
lilcupcake
Description: 
Turn your fresh zucchini into a great appetizer using breadcrumbs and parmesan. Excellent served hot, but is equally delicious prepared ahead of time and served at room temperature
