Title:
Grilled Chicken Pesto Pasta with Artichoke Hearts and Roasted Red Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken breast, cut into 3-4 strips
1 cup prepared zesty Italian salad dressing
1 (1.5-oz.) package Sun-dried tomato pesto sauce mix
1 cup water
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 8-oz package uncooked penne pasta
4-5 canned or jarred artichoke hearts, drained and patted dry
3-4 canned or jarred roasted red peppers, drained and patted dry
1 tablespoon freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Place the chicken strips and the Italian dressing in a zip-seal bag and shake to coat the chicken. Let marinate in the refridgerator for at least 1 hour.
2. Lightly oil a grill and grill the chicken strips over medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes per side or until done.
3. Cut the roasted red peppers into about 1/2-inch by 2-inch strips and set aside.
4. Heat water and cook pasta as directed on the package.  While the pasta is gooking, prepare the Pesto sauce as directed on the package.
5. When the pesto sauce is ready, lower heat, add chicken strips, peppers, and artichoke hearts.  Cover and cook over medium-low heat until the pasta is ready.
6. Transfer the sauce mixture to a bowl; toss in the pasta, sprinkle with parmesan, and serve.

Description: 
Strips of chicken breast are marinated in zesty Italian salad dressing, then grilled and tossed with penne, artichoke hearts and roasted red peppers in a Sun-dried tomato pesto sauce.
