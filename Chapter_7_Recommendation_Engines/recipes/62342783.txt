Title:
Jim's French Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound onions, sliced
4 tablespoons olive oil
2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 pinch ground black pepper
6 slices French bread, toasted
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
2/3 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1 cup Gruyere cheese

Directions:
Cook and stir onions in olive oil in a covered large saucepan over medium heat for 20 minutes. Stir in the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce and black pepper and sugar; heat to a boil. Remove from heat.
Place bread on a baking sheet; sprinkle bread with  Gruyere cheese then top with swiss cheese. Broil 4-to-5 inches from heat for about 1 minute or until cheese is melted and golden.
Ladle soup into bowls and top with bread.
