Title:
One Bowl Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate
1 cup butter
2 cups white sugar
3 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 bag of chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
Microwave chocolate and butter or margarine in large bowl at HIGH for 2 minutes or until butter or margarine is melted.
Stir until chocolate is melted.  Stir in sugar.  Mix in eggs and vanilla. Stir in flour. Once mixed together, add chocolate chips, stir well
Spread in greased 13  x  9 inch pan.  Bake  for 35 minutes (do not overbake).
