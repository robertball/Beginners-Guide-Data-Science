Title:
Cajun cabbage soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6732474.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head green cabbage( roughly chopped)
2 cups  chopped carrots
2 cups chopped celery
1 yellow onion (diced)
2-3 cloves minced garlic
1 can of no sugar added tomatoes
4 cups unsalted chicken or veggie stock
2  1/2 c water
2 tsp kitchen bouquet browning agent
1 Bay leaf
4-5 dashes Louisiana hot sauce
Tony Chachere’s creole seasoning (to taste)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Add small amount of olive oil to pot.
Add chopped onion,carrots,and celery.
Sauté until they just start to caramelize.
Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
Season with salt and pepper .
Add chicken stock and water along with kitchen bouquet.
Bring to a boil, then reduce to low simmer.
Add your bay leaf,cabbage, tomatoes, hot sauce, and tony’s.( spice will develop as soup cooks so be careful)
Simmer soup until cabbage is tender and the liquid has reduced by half.
Adjust seasoning as needed.
Enjoy!
