Title:
Peanut Butter Mousse Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 9" chocolate cookie pie crust
Fil
3/4 tsp Gelatin Powder
1 tbsp Water
1 1/2 c. Heavy Cream
1 c. Creamy Peanut Butter
1/2 .c Brown Sugar
1/4 c. Sugar
Ganache
1/2 c. Heavy Cream
1 c. Bitter-sweet Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Filling:
In a small bowl, sprinkle gelatin powder over 1 tbsp water and set aside.
Bring 1/2 cup of heavy cream and brown sugar to a simmer over medium-high heat.  In an heat-proof bowl, combine the peanut butter and the heavy cream mixture until completely incorporated. Add the gelatin mixture and set aside.
In a separate bowl, whip the remaining 1 c. heavy cream until frothy.  Add sugar and continue whipping to stiff peaks.
Mix together 1/3 of the whipped cream with the peanut butter mixture until fully incorporated.  Fold in the remaining whipped cream.  Add peanut butter mousse to the pie crust and refrigerate to thicken about 30 minutes.
Ganache:
Bring heavy cream to a simmer over medium-high heat.  Pour over chocolate chips in a heat resistant bowl and stir until chocolate and cream are completely incorporated with no lumps.
Pour ganache over peanut butter mousse and spread out with an off-set spatula.
Refrigerate until mousse is completely set, about 2 hours.

Description: 
A rich peanut butter mouse topped with a decadent chocolate gnache.
