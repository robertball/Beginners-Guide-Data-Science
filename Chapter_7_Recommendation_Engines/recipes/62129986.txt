Title:
Key Lime Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 c graham cracker crumbs
5 tbsp butter -melted
1 c sugar
1 tbsp sugar
3 8oz pkgs cream cheese - softened
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 c fresh lime juice
3 eggs
whipped topping if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine graham cracker crumbs, butter and 1 tbsp sugar in a bowl, mixing well. (should be crumbly)
Press the crumbs onto the bottom and half way up the sides of an 8" pan.
Bake crust for 5 minutes and set aside.
In a large bowl combine cheese, 1 c sugar, and vanilla mix until smooth.
Add lime juice and eggs and continue to mix until creamy.
Pour filling evenly into crust.
Bake for 60-65 minutes or until top is turning brown.
Remove from oven and let cool.
Refriderate until chilled, then serve with whipped topping.

Description: 
If you love key lime pie and cheesecake, then you will adore this mix of the two.
