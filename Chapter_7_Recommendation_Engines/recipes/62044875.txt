Title:
Lemon Pepper Garlic Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 chicken wings - split
1/2 cup lemon juice
2 T. Garlic powder
1 T. Black pepper
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. paprika
1/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
Deep fry wings for 15 minutes. While wings are frying, mix remaining ingredients in small bowl.  Line pan with foil and place cooked wings on pan in single layer.  Pour sauce over wings and bake 10 minutes at 375.  Serve

These wings have a wonderful flavor and even my picky 12 year old loves them!
