Title:
Darla's Mediterranean Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/870476.jpg
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons olive oil
2 cups white wine
2 large bone in chicken breast halves
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup diced onion
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 tablespoon red pepper flakes
1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
3 cups tomatoes, chopped
1/2 cup chopped crimini mushrooms
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat the oil and 4 tablespoons white wine in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken breasts and saute about 4  minutes each side. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside.
Saute garlic in pan drippings for 30 seconds, then add the onion and green bell pepper and saute for 3 minutes.  Add dried basil(if using), thyme, and red pepper flakes.  Lower heat, add the rest of the white wine and simmer for 10 minutes. Add fresh basil and simmer for 5 more minutes.
Add tomatoes and mushrooms.  Return chicken to skillet and cover. Cook over low heat until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink inside. Add parsley to the skillet and cook for 1 minute.  Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve.
