Title:
Grilled Venison Backstrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Venison Loin
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp Pepper
1 tsp Garlic powder

Directions:
Light your gas grill and allow to warm to at least 15 minutes, or until internal temperature reaches 400 degrees.
Trim any excess silver skin from the back strap. Season with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
Place back strap over direct high heat and grill for two minutes. Flip the steak and grill another two minutes. Remove from direct heat and grill over indirect heat until internal temperature of the back strap is 140 degrees. (Aprox. 4 to 5 minutes). Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
Jim Walter
Description: 
Venison backstrap on the grill, tender, juicy and healthy.
