Title:
Smartie Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup peanut butter
3/4 cup shortening
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/4 cups flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/4 cups rolled oats
1/2 cup chopped nuts or flaked coconut
1/2 cup candy-coated chocholate pieces

Directions:
Cream peanut butter, shortening and brown sugar together.
Beat in egg and vanilla.
Sift flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt, and stir into peanut butter mixture.
Stir in oats and nuts.
Roll into balls.
Flaten with a fork and center a chocolate candy on top.
Bake at 375F for 8 minutes.
