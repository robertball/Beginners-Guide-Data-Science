Title:
Apple Brown Sugar Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST:
1 1/2 cups butter cookie crumbs
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter, melted
FILLING:
1 1/2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, room temperature
3 eggs, beaten
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons buttermilk
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/4 apple cut in half
1/3 cup lightly toasted slivered almonds

Directions:
Combine crust ingredients and mix well.
Press firmly onto bottom and sides of deep 9-inch pie pan or springform pan.
Chill 30 minutes.
For filling, combine cream cheese, eggs, brown sugar, buttermilk and vanilla in food processor.
Process about 3 minutes or until smooth and free of lumps.
Place apple pieces standing up, facing each other in center of crust; pour filling around the apple pieces.
Bake at 350 degrees F for about 30 minutes until lightly browned.
Remove from oven and top with toasted almonds
Cool, then cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours.
Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Fishwrap
Description: 
The apple conducts the heat to the center of the pie, which is great for slow ovens such as mine.Modified from: Kathy's Brown Sugar Cheese Pie
