Title:
Won Ton Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535538.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups water
4 cubes chicken bouillon
1 piece fresh ginger, minced
2 carrots, peeled and sliced
1 cup sugar snap peas
1 cup sliced mushrooms
2 green onions, diced
2 cups chopped bok choy
Any prepackaged frozen pot stickers, cooked in the microwave according to package directions

Directions:
In a large pot, combine water, bouillon cubes and ginger. Bring to a boil.
Add carrots to the broth, and cook for 5 minutes.
Then add sugar peas, mushrooms, green onions, and bok choy. Boil for 5 minutes or until veggies are tender.
Stir in the pot stickers and simmer for 5 minutes.
