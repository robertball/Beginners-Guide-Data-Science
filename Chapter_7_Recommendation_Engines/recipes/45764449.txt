Title:
Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs. thinly sliced round steak or ground round
1 tbsp. vegetable oil
1 can cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1/2 cup water or beef broth
1 envelope onion soup mix
1/2 cup light sour cream
1 pkg. hot cooked egg noodles
Minced, fresh parsley (optional)

Directions:
In a large skillet, stir fry beef in oil until no longer pink.
Drain if using ground round.
Return to skillet and stir in soup, water or broth, and onion soup mix.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20 min.
Stir in sour cream; heat through (do not boil).  Serve over noodles; garnish with parsley if desired.
