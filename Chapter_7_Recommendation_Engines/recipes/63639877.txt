Title:
Steakhouse Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. small red potatoes cut into 1 inch cubes
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup mayo
1/4 ranch dressing
6 slices bacon, cooked, crumbled
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
4 green onions, thinly sliced

Directions:
Place potatoes in 2 qt. microwaveable dish.  add water; cover with lid.  Microwave on HIGH 12 to 15 minutes or just until potatoes are tender; drain.  Place in large bowl.
Refrigerate 1 hour or until completely cooled.
Mix dressings. Add to potatoes with remaining ingredients; mix lightly.
