Title:
Pepperoni Pizza Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Cream Cheese
1/2 cup Sour Cream
1/8 Tsp. Garlic Powder
1/8 Tsp. Oregano
1/8 Tsp. Dried Red Pepper Flakes
1/2 Cup Chopped Green Bell Pepper
1/2 Cup Chopped Green Scallion
1/2 Cup Chopped Pepperoni
1 Cup Pizza Sauce
1 Cup Shredded Mozzarella

Directions:
In a larg bowl mix cream cheese, sour cream, garlic powder, oregano and dried red pepper flakes.
Spread mixture into a 9" pie plate.
Spread pizza sauce.
Top with Scallion, green pepper, pepperoni
Bake for 15 minutes
Top with cheese and bake another 5 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
Serve with Nacho chips.
