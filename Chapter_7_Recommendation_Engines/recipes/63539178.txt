Title:
Tangy Fruit Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can [46 ounces] pineapple juice
1 can [12 ounces] frozen orange juice thawed
3/4 cup lemonade concentrate
1 cup water [divided]
1/2 cup sugar
2 large ripe bananas
1 package [20 ounces]frozen unsweetened whole strawberries
2 liters ginger ale, chilled

Directions:
1.In a punch bowl or large container combine pineapple juice,orange juice,lemonade concentrate,1/2 cup water and sugar.
2.place bananas,strawberries,and remaining water in a blender;cover and process until smooth.
3.Stir into the juice mixture. Cover and refrigerate.Just before serving,stir in ginger ale.
