Title:
Apricot Gelatin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 16oz cans apricot halves
dash of salt
2 - 3oz pkgs orange gelatin
1 - 6oz can orange juice concentrate
1 T. lemon juice
1 c. lemon-lime soda

Directions:
Drain apricots reserving 1 1/2 c. juice.
Set apricots aside.
In a small saucepan, bring apricot juice and salt to a boil over medium heat.
Remove from heat; add gelatin, stirring until dissolved.
In a blender, combine orange juice concentrate, lemon juice, and apricots until smooth.
Add to gelatin.
Add soda to gelatin.
Mix together.
Cover and refrigerate until firm.
