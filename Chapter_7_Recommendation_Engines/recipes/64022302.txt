Title:
Hungarian Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pork chops
1 clove of garlic - minced
1 bay leaf
1 cup sour cream - room temperature
1 medium onion - chopped
3 tbsp. butter
3/4 cup chicken bouillon
2 tsp. paprika

Directions:
Trim fat from pork chops then sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Melt butter in large saute pan then add onion and garlic and saute until soft.
Remove onion and garlic from pan and put pork chops in.
Brown chops and pour off excess fat when nicely browned.
Lower heat, add bouillon, bay leaf and onion/garlic mixture.
Cover and cook on low heat for 1 hour.
Remove chops from pan and keep warm.
Reduce juices by half, then turn off heat and add paprika and sour cream.
Be sure not to boil sour cream or it will curdle.
Pour over chops and serve.
