Title:
Orange Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz. small curd cottage cheese
16 oz. Cool Whip (thawed)
6 oz. Orange Jello
2 cans mandarin oranges

Directions:
In large mixing bowl, mix cottage cheese, Cool Whip, orange Jello (powder)and mandarin oranges.  Transfer to glass serving bowl and chill at least 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
CAROL46
Description: 
This is a sweet, fluffy, Jello salad. It adds some color to your meal.
