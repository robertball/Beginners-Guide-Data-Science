Title:
Amazingly tasty & crispy chicken schnitzel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1092612.jpg
Ingredients:
12 fine slices ??chicken breast
3 tablespoons honey
3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
2 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoons crushed garlic
2 large eggs
2 teaspoons salt and pepper
Bread crumbs (about 3 cups)
Vegetable oil (Depending on the size of the pan)
Lemon wedges

Directions:
1. Mix the eggs, honey, mustard, soy and garlic
2. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan.
3. Put all the chicken slices in the mixture and knead.
4. Coat each slice in the crumbs (to double crust and crisp, repeat 3 and 4)
5. Put the slices carefully in the oil for 3-4 minutes for each side.
6. Serve with lemon wedge and enjoy
