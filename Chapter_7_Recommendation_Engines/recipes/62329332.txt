Title:
Creamy Lemon Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups chicken broth
2 carrots, cut into small chunks
2 shallots chopped
2 stalks celery diced
4 cups cubed, cooked chicken
1 bag cooked brown rice
2 eggs
1/4 cup lemon juice
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown chicken, add shallots, carrots and celery, cook until veggies are starting to soften.  Add stock, bring to a boil and cook until veggies are tender, add bag of cooked rice. Whisk together eggs and lemon juice, temper eggs with hot broth, quickly stir into soup.

Description: 
Lemon juice gives this creamy soup its special taste. Add a green salad and crusty rolls, and you have a family-pleasing meal on the table in minutes.Modified from: Creamy Lemon Chicken Soup
