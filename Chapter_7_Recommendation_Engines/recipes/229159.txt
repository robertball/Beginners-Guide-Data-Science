Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package dried cranberries

Directions:
Flatten cranberries between fingers and spread them onto a sheet of waxed paper to dry until they start to curl slightly, about 1 hour.
Put cranberries into a bowl labeled 'Dried Bloody Scabs' and watch the kids scoop them up by the handful!

Submitter: 
Deborah McCarthy
Description: 
Quick, easy, disgusting, but tasty! This can also be done with dried cherries.
