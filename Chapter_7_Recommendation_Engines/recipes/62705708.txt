Title:
Crispy Pork Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 ounces (approximately 2 cups) Smoked Pork, recipe here
2 cups Mexican cheese
1/2 lime
1 can black beans, drained
2-3 tablespoons finely chopped, fresh cilantro
10 flour tortillas
Salsa, sour cream, and guacamole for garnish

Directions:
Cook pork according to crockpot recipe.
Combine beans, cilantro, and lime juice. Divide the bean mixture on each of the 10 tortillas, approximately 2 tablespoons in each. Sprinkle with a small amount of cheese. Divide pork evenly. Roll tortillas, securing with toothpicks. Spray lightly with canola oil.
Bake 350 for approximately 25 minutes, or until golden brown and slightly crispy.
