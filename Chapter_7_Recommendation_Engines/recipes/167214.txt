Title:
KRETSCHMAR® Sausage Cassoulet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986603.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups diced tomatoes
2 cups Great Northern Beans (seasonal)
5 bay leaves
1 pound KRETSCHMAR® Dinner Sausage

Directions:
Add diced tomatoes, Great Northern Beans and bay leaves in a large stock pot or cassoulet.  Add KRETSCHMAR(R) loop, dinner sausage.  Heat on top of stove over low heat for one hour (heat longer for a more savory flavor).  Remove sausage, cut and serve with beans and broth.

Submitter: 
Kretschmar®
Description: 
Try this tasty stew made with KRETSCHMAR® Dinner Sausage, great northern beans and diced tomatoes. Pair with a dinner salad and bread and you have the perfect dinner with very little prep time. It’s so easy to prepare and tastes great!
