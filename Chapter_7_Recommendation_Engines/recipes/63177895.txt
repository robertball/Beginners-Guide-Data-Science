Title:
Martha's Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. boneless cooked chicken
3 c. halved seedless grapes
2 c. sliced celery
1 1/2 c. slivered almonds
1/2 c. sliced green onions (optional)
1 c. sour cream
1 c. mayonnaise
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Use more sour cream and mayonnaise for a creamier salad. Mix thoroughly. Chill well.
