Title:
Chicken Parmigian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 quality frozen breaded chicken breasts
1 bottle spaghetti sauce w/o meat
1 cup shredded cheese mozarella preferred

Directions:
spray 13 x 9 pan with pam
place thin layer sauce in bottom of pan
layer chicken
cover with sauce
cover with shredded cheese
cover with foil
bake 350 degrees x 1 hours.

Submitter: 
Michele Weyland
Description: 
Just as good as restaurant style but cheaper and easier
