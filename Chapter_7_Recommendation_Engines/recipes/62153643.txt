Title:
One Dish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground turkey
1 red or green bell pepper, diced
1 pkg. Taco Seasoning mix
2 cups of instant white rice
2 cups water
1 cup of your choice of cheese
1 bag bite sized nacho chips
Salsa and Sour Cream

Directions:
Cook turkey and pepper on a large pan over medium heat until turkey is no longer pink.
Add rice, water ans seasoning. Stir and bring to a boil.
Turn off heat, cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
Stir, sprinkle with cheese, cover again until cheese is melted.
Spoon into casserole dish, scoop out with nacho chips. Enjoy!
