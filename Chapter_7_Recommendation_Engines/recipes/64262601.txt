Title:
Sausage, Craisin, Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1832286.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf of bread cubed
1 pound spicy ground turkey sausage
1 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped celery
2 tablespoons italian seasoning
3/4 cup dried cranberries
3/4 cup vegetable broth
4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Directions:
Soak dried cranberries in melted butter and broth for 30+ minutes.
Preheat oven to 350 degree F (175 degree C). Spread bread cubes in a single layer on a large baking sheet. Bake for 5 to 7 minutes in the preheated oven, or until evenly toasted. Transfer toasted bread cubes to a large bowl.
In a large skillet, cook the sausage and onions over medium heat, stirring and breaking up the lumps until evenly browned. Add the celery, and seasoning; cook, stirring, for 2 minutes to blend flavors.
Pour sausage mixture over bread in bowl. Add soaked cranberry mixture to bread bowl and mix lightly. Transfer to serving dish and warm in oven if needed.

Submitter: 
Amethyst Douglas
Description: 
Freshly toasted bread cubes, turkey sausage, dried cranberry combine to create a tasty fruited stuffing.Modified from: Awesome Sausage, Apple and Cranberry Stuffing
