Title:
Weeknight Lasagne Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991578.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 pound lean ground beef
2 green peppers, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 (700 ml) jar pasta sauce
1 2/3 cups water
1/4 cup KRAFT Zesty Italian Dressing
12 oven-ready lasagna noodles, broken into quarters
1 cup KRAFT 4 Cheese Italiano Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Brown meat in large saucepan; drain.
Add peppers, garlic, pasta sauce, water and dressing; bring to boil. Stir in noodles; cover.
Cook on medium-low heat 10 to 15 minutes or until noodles are tender, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat. Sprinkle with cheese; cover. Let stand 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese
Description: 
All the taste of long-cooking lasagne comes through in this quick stove top simmer.
