Title:
Strawberry Spinach Salad With Feta and Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/994039.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) bag baby spinach leaves
1 quart fresh strawberries
4 ounces crumbled feta cheese
1 small sweet onion (such as Vidalia®), sliced
1 (2 ounce) package bacon bits
1/2 (16 ounce) bottle reduced-fat raspberry vinaigrette salad dressing (such as Kraft® Raspberry Vinaigrette Lite)

Directions:
Place spinach into a large salad bowl and slice strawberries over spinach; sprinkle with feta cheese, onion, and bacon bits. Pour raspberry dressing over salad and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
MALLOYLK
Description: 
Strawberry spinach salad has feta cheese, sweet onion, and crumbled bacon all tossed with a raspberry vinaigrette dressing.
