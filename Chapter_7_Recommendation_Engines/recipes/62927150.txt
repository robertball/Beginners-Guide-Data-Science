Title:
Green Tomato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup seasoned, dry bread crumbs
1 t. sugar
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
4 green tomatoes cut into 1/4 in. slices
1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar
1 T. butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Grease 1 1/2 qt. casserole. Mix salt, pepper, and sugar in small bowl. Arrange 1/3 tomato slices in casserole dish. Sprinkle with 1/3 of sugar mixture, 1/3 of bread crumbs, and 1/3 of cheese. Repeat for 2nd layer. Repeat for 3rd layer, but omit cheese. Dot with butter. Cover with foil, and bake for 1 hr. Un-cover, sprinkle with remaining cheese, and bake until cheese is melted, about 5-7 mins. Remove from oven, and let sit 15 mins.
