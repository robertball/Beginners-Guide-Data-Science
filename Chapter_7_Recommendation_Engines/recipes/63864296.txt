Title:
Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Gingerbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084737.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
1 cup vegetable oil
4 eggs
1 pkg.(15 ounce) frozen (thawed & drained)pumpkin puree
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoons ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground allspice
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground cloves
2/3 cup milk
1 cup miniature chocolate chips
For Glaze:
1 cup powdered sugar
4 T. milk
1 t. vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease two 9x5 inch loaf pans.
In a large mixing bowl, combine sugar, oil and eggs; beat until smooth.
Mix in pureed pumpkin.
In medium bowl, sift together the flour, soda, salt, baking powder, ginger, allspice cinnamon, and cloves.
Alternately, add dry ingredients and milk to pumpkin mixture and blend just until all ingredients are mixed.
Fold in miniature chocolate chips.
Divide batter between prepared pans.
Bake in preheated oven until toothpick comes out clean, about 1 hour.
Allow loaves to cool for about 10 minutes before removing to cooling rack.
When cool, drizzle with glaze.
