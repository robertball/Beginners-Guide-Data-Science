Title:
Sunshine Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. baking soda
1 Tbsp. cinnamon
2 cups grated carrots
1/3 cup chopped dry apricots
1/3 cup sunflower seeds
1/3 cup chocolate chips
1/3 cup coconut
1 banana, mashed
3 eggs
1 cup oil
2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Mix first 4 dry ingredients, then stir in carrots, apricots, sunflower seeds, choc. chips, coconut and banana.
In a separate bowl, beat together eggs, oil and vanilla, and stir into flour mixture until just moistened. Spoon into greased muffin tins.
Bake at 375 for 15-20 minutes.
