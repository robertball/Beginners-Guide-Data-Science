Title:
Mint Chocolate Hazelnut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3272749.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup unsweetened applesauce
3/4 cup Truvia baking blend
1 egg white
3 very ripe bananas
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 ½ cups Gluten Free baking flour
1/2 tsp mint extract
1/2 cup mint chocolate chips
1/4 cup toasted hazelnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees F
Toast hazelnuts on medium heat in a small pan on the stovetop ~5-7 minutes or when you just smell the nuts
Remove nuts from heat, allow to cool then chop nuts finely
Meanwhile mash bananas, add sugar, egg, mint extract & applesauce in a large mixing bowl
Stir dry ingredients into wet ingredients then fold chocolate chips & hazelnuts into the batter
Place muffin liners in a muffin tin & fill liners ½ way full of batter
Bake 15-18 minutes at 375 degrees F

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Bursting with mint and hazelnut flavor, these low calorie Mint Chocolate Hazelnut Muffins are dairy and oil free. Made with applesauce, bananas and whole wheat flour this every day muffin is about to be your new favorite.
