Title:
Top Secret Blueberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 cup plain flour
1 cup pecans
8 ounce cream cheese
1 cup sugar
container whipped topping
1/2 can blueberry pie filling

Directions:
Blend butter, flour and pecans.
Press into pie plate.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
Cool completely.
Meanwhile, mix cream cheese and sugar.
Add whipped topping.
Put half of this mixture into pie crust.
Cover with blueberry pie filling.
Cover with the remaining whip mixture.
Keep refrigerated.

Submitter: 
aunt star
Description: 
My brother wants this instead of a birthday cake! Mom got the recipe out of the local newspaper, but apparently she was the only one who saw it because everyone begged for the recipe. She wouldn't share. She only gave it to us kids once we were grown.
