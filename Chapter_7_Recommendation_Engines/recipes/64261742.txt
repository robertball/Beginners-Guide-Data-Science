Title:
Magazine Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 ounce bottle clam broth
3 6.5 ounce cans chopped clams, broth reserved
2 cups cubed potatos (about 3)
1 cup each chopped onion, diced celery, diced carrots
1 T minced garlic
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup flour
1 pint half and half
2 T red wine vinegar
1 t salt and pepper to taste
4 strips bacon

Directions:
Cook the bacon in a large skillet until crispy.  Remove to drain on towels and set aside.  Add garlic, potatos, onion, celery and carrot to bacon fat and brown for 8-10 minutes. Pour clam broth and juice from canned clams into skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
In a soup pot, melt the butter over med-low heat. Add the flour, whisking constantly for about 4-5 minutes.  Stir in the veggie/clam broth mixture and the half and half.  Heat just to boiling, then reduce heat.  Add the clams, vinegar, bacon and salt and pepper.  Heat through and serve.

Submitter: 
SueHoo
Description: 
This is a variation of a great clam chowder recipe I found in the allrecipes magazine.  I love the creaminess and the bacon adds some extra yumminess!
