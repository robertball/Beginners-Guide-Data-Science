Title:
Best slow cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - 4 lb. roast
1/2 cup pasta sauce or 8 oz. tomato paste
1 can beef broth
1/2 cup water
3 beef bouillon cubes
2.5 lb. red potatoes, cut into fourths if  they are large
3 cups of baby carrots
3 Tbs cornstarch
2 Tbs cold water
1 tsp gravy master (optional)

Directions:
-Place roast in slow cooker.
-Pour in broth,1/2 cup of water,& pasta sauce.
-Crush bouillon cubes and add to liquid.
-Put the carrots & potatoes around the roast.
-During the last 1/2 hour mix the cornstarch and cold water to thicken gravy.
- Add the gravy master to darken and make the gravy richer.

Submitter: 
Lori McTavish Bizjak
Description: 
I needed a pot roast recipe one day and didn't have the ingredients for the recipe I usually use , so I tried it in the slow cooker. It is the greatest pot roast, so easy and delicious I often use it for pot lucks.
