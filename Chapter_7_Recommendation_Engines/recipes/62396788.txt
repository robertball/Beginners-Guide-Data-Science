Title:
Raspberry Iced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon water
3 gallon size tea bags
1 cup fresh raspberries
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup Country Time lemonade powdered drink mix

Directions:
Bring water to a boil.
Add tea bags and raspberries.
Add sugar.
Steep for 3 - 5 minutes.
Remove tea bags.
Add lemonade mix.
Pour into pitcher and add ice to cool.
