Title:
Roasted carrots and sweet potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon coconut oil
4 medium carrots, chopped
2 medium sweet potatoes, chopped
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon smoked paprika
¼ teaspoon cinnamon
¼ teaspoon chili powder
Sea salt to taste
Fresh herbs for garnish (optional)

Directions:
1.    Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2.   Toss carrots and sweet potatoes with the coconut oil and spices. Lay flat on a sheet pan.
3.    Roast for 15 - 20 minutes, or until vegetables are tender. Add salt to taste, garnish with fresh
herbs (if using) and serve warm.
