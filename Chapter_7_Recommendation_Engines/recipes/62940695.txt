Title:
Turkey Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/721797.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb of ground turkey
3 Kaiser rolls
1 tomato, sliced
1 onion, sliced
3 leaves of lettuce
6 tablespoons of mayonnaise
3 oz of gruyere cheese
salt to taste
pepper to taste

Directions:
Take turkey and make 3 patties
Put patties in a fry pan and cook on each side for 5 mins. flip patties one last time and add gruyere cheese to the top of the patties, cover to allow the cheese to melt.
Put patties, on a Kaiser roll with mayonnaise on each slice. top with lettuce, onions, tomatoes, add salt and pepper to taste.
Serve with tater tots, or french fries.

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
Turkey Burger Is an alternative to a beef burger, and tastes great.
