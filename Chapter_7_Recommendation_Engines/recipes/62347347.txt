Title:
Salmon with white wine and Parsley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Salmon Fillet, with skin still on
1 Lemon
10 sprigs Fresh Parsley
4 cloves Fresh Garlic
1/2 cup White Cooking Wine
Black Pepper
Sea Salt

Directions:
Crush then Mince Garlic, set aside
Slice Lemons into rings, set aside
Sprinkle Salmon with desired amounts of Salt and  Pepper
Heat 1/2 cup of White cooking wine, till just boiling, in cast iron pan
Place Parsley in Pan, sprinkle garlic on parsley, top with lemon slices
Place Salmon skin side up on top of all ingredients
Cover, reduce heat to Medium, and allow to cook for about 10 min, depending on thichness
