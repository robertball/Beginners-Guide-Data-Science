Title:
Tilapia with Lemon White Wine sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tilapia Filets
Juice from half a lemon
1 1/2 cups white wine
2 eggs
2 cups flour
salt and fresh pepper to taste
2TB butte
fresh chopped parsley

Directions:
Crack the eggs in a bowl and mix with salt and pepper.  In another bowl mix the flour and salt and pepper.  Rinse the tilapia and dip in the flour mixture then the egg, then again in the flour.  Repeat for all filets.   Pan fry in butter unil light golden brown.  At least 3 min on each side. Important: only flip fish 1 time.  Add more butter as needed to fry all fish.  Remove from pan and keep warm in oven while making sauce.   Melt 1 TB butter add lemon juice, wine and reduce.  Taste and make adjustments as needed.  Pour sauce over fish and spinkle with parsley.  Serve immedietly.
