Title:
Delicious Omelette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/841432.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs for 1 omelette
1 cup meat (ham, bacon, sausage, turkey etc.)
2 small potatoes
1 small onion
2 small tomatoes
3 green onion stalks
3 TBSP garlic
1/4 cup vegetable oil or 1/4 cup butter
1/3 cup cheese for 1 omelette
salt and pepper to your liking

Directions:
Saute meat (ham, bacon, sausage, turkey etc.),potatoes, onion, tomatoes, green onion stalks, garlic & vegetable/butter. Once all items are cooked remove from frying pan and place on a plate on the side. In the same frying pan add 3 beated eggs and salt and peppered, spread inside entire pan on LOW HEAT! once eggs arent "raw" looking add small portion of cooked mixture on one side of the eggs. Add half of the cheese and then flip the side without mixture on it on top of mixture to make it a sandwich. top with cheese.
