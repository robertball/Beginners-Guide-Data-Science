Title:
Pumpkin Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/481572.jpg
Ingredients:
1 29 oz. can plain pumpkin
1 12 oz. can evaporated milk
3 eggs
1 1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
1 package white or yellow cake mix
1 cup walnuts
4 Tbsp. melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Beat first 6 ingredients together until combined; pour into a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking pan.
Sprinkle top with dry cake mix and nuts.
Melt butter and drizzle over top.
Bake for 50-60 minutes.
