Title:
Mini Venison Cheeseburger Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1019285.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs Fresh pizza dough or 6 premade mini pizza shells
8 oz ground venison
1 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 1/2 cups pizza sauce
1 lb shredded mozzarella
8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups of chopped lettuce
2 medium tomatoes -sliced
1 large onion-sliced
1/2 lb Cooked Largely Chopped Bacon-optional

Directions:
Mix together ground venison, garlic salt and pepper and make into burgers. Cook burgers until done medium-well.
Set oven temperature - 400* degrees
Prepare pizza dough into mini pizza shapes.
Add 1/4 cup of pizza sauce to each pizza and spread around.
Add 2-3 TBSP of mozzarella cheese on top of sauce.
Slice venison burgers and add slices to top of mozzarella cheese, (can add bacon here),top with cheddar, lettuce, tomato and onion. Divide leftover Mozzarella cheese and add remaining to top of pizza.
Cook on stoneware or pizza pan for 10 min on bottom and then 5 min on low broil.
