Title:
Shrimp Avocado Cocktail with Lemon Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound tiny bay shrimp
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 avocadoes
1/2 cup sour cream
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon parsley, minced
1 tablespoon horseradish
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 head Boston lettuce

Directions:
1.  Peel and cube avocadoes.  Sprinkle with lemon juice.
2.  Mix together sour cream, lemon juice, parsley, horseradish, and salt.
3.  Line cocktail glasses with crisp lettuce.  Alternate shrimp and avocado.  Top with lemon cream dressing.

Submitter: 
kirklandcook
Description: 
This appetizer is tasty and looks beautiful served in a martini glass as an appetizer or first course.
