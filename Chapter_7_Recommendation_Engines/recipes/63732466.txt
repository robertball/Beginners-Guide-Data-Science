Title:
Garbage Plate Western NY Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1036426.jpg
Ingredients:
To make a garbage plate you will need my hot meat sauce. Make that 1st.
Use any ingredients!
Mac n cheese
Leftover potatoes
French fries
Sausage slices
A hamburger
Cheese

Directions:
For a classic NY style garbage plate make a cheese burger. Place it in the center.
Now add some mac n cheese to one side of the plate
Arrange some potatoes, french fries, baked potato, smashed potato, your call, on another side.
Add anything else in your fridge you think will go good.
Heat it all up separate in a microwave, or cook it all individually.
Now top with my hot meat sauce and serve!
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Chef Jeff
Description: 
This plate all depends on what you want to top my hot meat sauce with. A true NY garbage plate consists of a burger, mac n cheese and potatoes. You can make any plate with what you have left over from a fast food joint or in the fridge. Use your imagination! Then top it all with hot meat sauce! Now you got a true NY style garbage plate!
