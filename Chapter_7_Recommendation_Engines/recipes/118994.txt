Title:
Seasoned Oven Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986224.jpg
Ingredients:
6 medium baking potatoes
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 teaspoon seasoned salt

Directions:
Cut each potato lengthwise into thirds; cut each portion into thirds. In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the butter, oil and seasoned salt. Add potatoes; shake to coat. Place in a single layer on a greased baking sheet. Bake, uncovered, at 450 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until tender, turning once.

Submitter: 
Pat Fredericks
Description: 
For a swift side dish, Patricia bakes Seasoned Oven Fries 'These potato wedges are as tasty as the deep-fried versions but with less mess,' she reports.
