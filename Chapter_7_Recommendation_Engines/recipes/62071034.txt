Title:
Amy's Kidney Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
4-small cans of dark kidney beans rinced and drained
6-boiled eggs chopped
1-green pepper chopped
1-small red onion chopped
Dressing:
1-cup mayo
2-TBSP milk
2-TBSP vinigar
3-tsp sugar or sugar substitute
salt and fresh ground pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium to large serving bowl whisk together ingredients for dressing, when well combined add salad ingredients.  Can eat right away but the longer it sits in the refrig the better it tastes.  I like to make it the day ahead of when i am wanting to serve. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Amy Clum Nash
Description: 
This Salad is a summer time pleaser.  Everytime i take it to barbq's, or gathering, everyone wants the recipe.  Simple and quick to make with just a few ingredients.  Everyone will love it!
