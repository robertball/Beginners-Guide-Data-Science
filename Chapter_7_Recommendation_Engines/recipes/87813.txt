Title:
Laura's Beefy Hoppin' John Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound Laura's 92% Lean Ground Beef
1/2 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup chopped celery
2 (14.25 ounce) cans low-salt broth
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
3/4 cup uncooked long-grain rice
1 (15.5 ounce) can black-eyed peas, rinsed and drained

Directions:
Cook ground beef in a large skillet until browned; drain.
Add onion, garlic and celery and cook 5 minutes or until tender.
Add broth and crushed red pepper; bring to a boil.
Add rice; cover and simmer 20 minutes or until rice is tender.
Add black eyed peas and cook 10 more minutes or until heated and liquid is absorbed.

Submitter: 
Laura's Lean Beef
Description: 
Rice and beans are staples for so many cultures. Our version of Hoppin' John is lower in fat but high in flavor. And it's a one-skillet meal that's easy to make.
