Title:
Artichoke Dijon Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz penne or ziti
3 T. oil
3 T. red wine vinegar or balsamic
1 T. Dijon
1/4 c. fresh chopped parsley
2 large cloves garlic, minced
2 Tsp. dried oregano
1/2 tsp. black peppper
2 tomatoes, diced
4 whole scallions, chopped
1 can chickpeas, drained
1 can (14-oz) artichoke hears, rinsed and coarsely chopped

Directions:
Cook pasta.  Drain and cool under cold running water.
Combine salad dressing ingredients (oil through pepper).  Add remaining ingredients.  Add pasts.
