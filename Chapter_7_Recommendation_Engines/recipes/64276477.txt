Title:
Jerk marinade ll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow onion chopped
2 tsp fresh thyme leaves
1 tsp salt
2 tsp sugar
1 tsp Jamaican allspice
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
2-3 habenero peppers chopped
3 tbs soy sauce
1 tbs vegetable oil
2 Tbs cider vinegar or distilled white vinegar
3 tbs escallion finely chopped

Directions:
In a blender or food processor,combine all the ingredients and process until smooth. Store leftover in refrigerator in a tightly closed jar for about a month.

This is a simpler version of the jerk marinades used there are many different versions this one is mine.
