Title:
Rivels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Pour flour on a flat surface such as a cutting board.
Make a well in the center, then pour in the egg and salt.
Using your hands and a knife, work these 3 ingredients together into a noodle-type pastry.
Knead once or twice, then chop it into small pieces.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
Rivels are little homemade egg noodles.  They are good in beef and chicken stew, as well as in vegetable soups.
