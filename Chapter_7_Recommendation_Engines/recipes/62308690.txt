Title:
Vegan Black Bean and Corn Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz refried beans
20 oz black beans
15 oz whole kernel corn
1 cup quick oats
1 tbsp ground pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients until well mixed. Put in loaf pan. Bake @ 350 for 20-30 minutes. Top with salsa, BBQ Sauce, ketchup, tomato sauce, or leave dry.

Submitter: 
Cynthia
Description: 
A Vegan Style 'Meat' loaf. Easy and quick. I make the refried beans from scratch so that I get a good flavor. I recommend topping with salsa.
