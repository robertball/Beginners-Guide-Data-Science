Title:
Broccoli Casserole for the Soul Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads of fresh broccoli or 2 10 ounce packages of frozen chopped broccoli
1 can of Cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup of mayaonnaise
2 cups of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 egg
3/4 cup fried onions

Directions:
Steam broccoli to a bright green, not mushy, cut into bite size pieces (if using frozen broccoli, thaw); set aside. Combine soup, mayonnaise, cheese and egg, then add broccoli. Place in 9 x 11 glass baking dish. Sprinkle the fried onions over mixture. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Let stand for 3 - 5 minutes and serve.

Description: 
This is easy to assemble and full of flavor and comes from the south. It is a tasty addition to any meal.
