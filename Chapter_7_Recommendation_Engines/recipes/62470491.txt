Title:
Greek Style Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Burgers:
1/3 cup Italian seasoned dry bread crumbs
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 Tbsp. milk
1/2 0.7 oz envelope Italian salad dressing mix
1 lb. uncooked ground turkey
Greek Salsa:
2 Tbsp. white wine vinegar
2 tsp. olive oil
1/2 0.7 oz. envelope Italian salad dressing mix
1 cup finely chopped tomato
1/4 cup finely chopped cucumber
1/4 cup finely chopped kalamata olives
Topping:
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine bread crumbs, egg, milk, and salad dressing for burgers.  Add turkey and mix well.  Shape into 4 patties.
Grill turkey patties until cooked through.
While turkey patties are cooking, combine all of salsa ingredients in a small bowl.
Serve cooked turkey patties on whole wheat hamburger buns.  Serve with greek salsa and feta cheese for patty toppings.

Submitter: 
DarcieA
Description: 
This is a nice alternative to a basic hamburger.  Our friends gave us this recipe (I believe it came from Better Homes and Gardens), and it has quickly become a family favorite.
