Title:
Tomato, Cucumber and Red Onion Salad with Feta Cheese and Kalamata Olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cucumbers - halved lengthwise, seeded, then quartered lengthwise and chopped
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 teaspoon salt
3 large tomatoes, seeded and coarsely chopped
2/3 cup coarsely chopped red onion
1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted and halved
6 oz crumbled feta cheese
6 tablespoons olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss together the cucumbers, vinegar, sugar and salt.
Add tomatoes, onion, olives, feta cheese and oil to cucumbers and toss to blend.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Let stand for 1 hour in refrigerator or at room temperature to allow flavors to marry.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This light and delicious salad features fresh cucumbers, tomatoes and red onions with a sweet and sour dressing enlivened with feta cheese and kalamata olives.Modified from: Tomato, Cucumber and Red Onion Salad with Mint
