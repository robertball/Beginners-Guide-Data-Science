Title:
Cinnamon Pecan Cookie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. butter
1/4 C sugar
2 C flour
1 egg yolk
4 t. cinnamon
1 t. vanilla
1 egg white
1 C chopped pecans
powdered sugar

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar.  Gradually mix in flour.  Add egg yolk, cinnamon and vanilla.  Put in buttered cookie sheet and pat down until it is 1/4 inch thick.  Spread unbeaten egg white on top.  spread nuts and press into dough.  Bake 350 degrees for 30 minutes.  Dust powdered sugar on top.
Cut into bars and enjoy!
