Title:
Smoky Penne with Corn and Cherry Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. penne
1 chipotle chile, finely chopped
2 T. oil, divided
2 cloves garlic, minced
4-oz. goat cheese, crumbled
1/3 c. chopped cilantro
2 c. halved cherry tomatoes
2 c. corn kernels

Directions:
Cook pasta, drain, and return to pot.
Meanwhile heat 1 T. oil in a skillet and add chile and garlic.  Saute a minute till fragrant.  Stir in goat cheese and 1/3 c. water, and cook for 2 or 3 minutes until a smooth sauce forms, stirring constantly.  Simmer 2 minutes more, then remove from heat and add cilantro and remaining 1 T. oil.
Pour over pasta, and add tomatoes and corn.  Serve.
