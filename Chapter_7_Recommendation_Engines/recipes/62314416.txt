Title:
Baked Lentils with Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1) 2-1/4 cups water 2) 1-3/4 cups dried lentils  3)1cup chopped onion 4) 2 medium carrots (thinly sliced)5)1/2cup celery(thinly sliced) 6) 2 garlic cloves minced 7) 1-teaspoon salt 8) 1/4-teaspoon pepper 9) 1/8-teaspoon dried marjoram 10) 1/8-teaspoon rubbed sage 11) 1/8-teaspoon dried thyme 12) 1 whole bay leaf 13) 2-cups fresh tomatoes (chopped)14)  1/2-cup green pepper(finely chopped)  15)2-Tablespoons parsley 16) 2-1/2 cups shredded chedder cheese(10ounce)

Directions:
1)In a 13x9 baking dish, combine the first 12 ingredients. Cover and at 350 for 45mins.    2)Stir in the tomatoes and green pepper. Cover and bake 15mins. longer.  Sprinkle with parsley and cheese. Bake uncoverd for 5-10 mins. longer, or until cheese is melted.  Discard bay leaf.
