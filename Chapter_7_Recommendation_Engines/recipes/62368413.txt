Title:
Spicy Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup packed brown sugar (if not in beans already)
1/4 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons prepared mustard
2 tablespoon molasses
2 or more clove garlic, finely chopped
2 tablesspoons Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (or hot sauce)
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
Cooked Kielbasa, sausage or a dozen slices cooked crumbed bacon if you prefer
1 onion, chopped
1 large stalk celery, chopped
2 cans (28 oz.) pork and beans

Directions:
Cook meat and onions on stove stop until brown. Combine everything in heavy pot, cook at 325 for an hour until hot & bubbily or 3 hours on low in crock pot.

Great for picnic - Add as much hot ingredients as you and your guests prefer.
