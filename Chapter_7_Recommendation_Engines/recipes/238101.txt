Title:
Nutella® Popsicles® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4529580.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy whipping cream
1 cup milk
6 tablespoons chocolate-hazelnut spread (such as Nutella®)

Directions:
Stir cream, milk, and chocolate-hazelnut spread together in microwave-safe bowl. Microwave until chocolate hazelnut spread is melted, 1 to 2 minutes, stirring every 30 seconds. Pour the mixture into ice pop molds and freeze until set, 3 to 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Thetenthdoctorlover
Description: 
Great smooth taste for hot summer days! Add mint leaves after the mixture is melted to give it a fresher taste!
