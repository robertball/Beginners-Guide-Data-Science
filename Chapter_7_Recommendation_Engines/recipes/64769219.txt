Title:
Mom's 7 layer cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs (1 package)
1/2 cup butter (melted)
1 (14 oz) swetened condensed milk
1 cup butterscotch chips
1 cup chocolate chips
1 1/2 cup coconut
1 cup nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray a 9x13 pan with nonstick spray.
Mix graham cracker crumbs in a bowl with melted butter.
Pour mixture into the pan, layer evenly, and press firmly to create a crust.
Pour condensed milk over the graham cracker mixture in the pan.
Layer the rest of the ingredients evenly- butterscotch chips, chocolate chips, coconut, and nuts.
Bake for 25-30 minutes or until lightly browned.
