Title:
Stacy Earl's Maple Syurp Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 Tbs Olive Oil
1 tsp Maple Syrup
1 tsp stone ground mustard
1 tsp apple cider vinegar
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and serve over salad of roasted beets, walnuts, avocado, sliced red onions and greens, etc.
