Title:
Campbell's Kitchen Broccoli and Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985995.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular, 98% Fat Free or 25% Less Sodium)
1/2 cup milk
2 teaspoons yellow mustard
1 (16 ounce) package frozen broccoli flowerets, thawed
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/3 cup dry bread crumbs
2 teaspoons butter, melted

Directions:
Stir the soup, milk, mustard, broccoli and cheese in a 1 1/2-quart casserole.
Mix the bread crumbs with the butter in a small bowl and sprinkle over the broccoli mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes or until hot.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Serve this creamy broccoli and cheese casserole as a side dish or a vegetarian main course.
