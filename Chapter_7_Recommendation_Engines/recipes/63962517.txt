Title:
German Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup flour
6 tablespoons butter or margarine

Directions:
Put milk and eggs in blender and process well.  Tap in flour a little at a time.  Add salt.  Melt butter in 9 x 13 inch glass baking dish in 425 degree oven.  Pour batter into baking dish.  Bake 20-25 minutes.  Cut into pieces and serve with syrup or powdered sugar.
