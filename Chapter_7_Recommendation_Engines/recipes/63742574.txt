Title:
Healthy Make-ahead Breakfast Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups of egg beaters
6 canadian bacon slices
6 light, whole grain english muffins
6 extra-thin slices of cheddar (or your favorite cheese)
1/3 cup chopped sun dried tomatoes (rehydrated if using dried)
2 Tbs of minced, dried onions
3 large minced garlic cloves
black pepper, to taste
fresh herbs, to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Add egg beaters, sun-dried tomatoes, onions, garlic, pepper, and herbs (whatever you have) to a greased small casserole dish.
Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes (watch as it will depend on your oven and the exact size of your casserole dish).
Once cooled, cut the egg into 6 squares and assemble sandwiches.
Wrap individually in plastic wrap and place all in a freezer bag.
