Title:
Tykvenitsa millet breakfast cereal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup millet
3 cups pumpkin or butternut squash, shredded
2 cups rice milk
2 cups water
salt, butter or margarine to taste

Directions:
Place all the ingredients except salt and butter into the slow cooker. Mix well, cover and cook on Low 4 to 5 hours until creamy consistency is achieved.
Add butter and salt, mix well and serve.

Submitter: 
tantuc
Description: 
This used to be one of my favorite hot cereals when I was a child. It has a pleasing sweet flavor without added sugar and a sunny yellow color. I adapted this Russian recipe for a slow cooker.
