Title:
vegetable pizza III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of crescent rolls
1 sm. pk. finely grated cheddar
broccolli, cauliflower, grated carrots, green peppers, green onions, celery mushrooms.
pick any of your favorites and chop them up finely
16 oz of sour cream
8oz cream cheese(softened)
1 env. dry ranch dressing mix

Directions:
Spread crescent roll and flatten, pressing edges together on a 9x13 cookie sheet to make crust.
Bake according to package directions, and cool.
While crust bakes, mix sour cream, cream cheese and dressing.
Mix until smooth.
Spread dressing mix on cool crescent crust.
Top with your choice of veggies and shredded cheese.
