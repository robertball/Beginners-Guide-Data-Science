Title:
Virgin Strawberry Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/878559.jpg
Ingredients:
a handful of frozen strawberries, hulled
1/4 cup Splenda, or less
1 tablespoon lemon juice
3/4 cup chilled diet lemon-lime soda

Directions:
In the container of a blender, combine the strawberries, Splenda, lemon juice and diet lemon-lime soda. Blend until smooth. Pour into a fancy glass to serve.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
This drink is a low-calorie, slushy mocktail that my husband and I enjoy while waiting on dinner to finish cooking.Modified from: Virgin Strawberry Daiquiri
