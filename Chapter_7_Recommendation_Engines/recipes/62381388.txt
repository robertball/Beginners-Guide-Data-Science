Title:
Natalie's Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 bunch of cilantro (large stems removed)
2 cans of diced tomatoes
juice of 3 limes and their zest (zest is optional)
1 onion
1 8 oz can of tomato sauce

Directions:
Process everything in a blender or food processor until smooth or to desired consistency.  Keep in refrigerator; stronger flavor the second day.

Submitter: 
GNAT0328
Description: 
Light, fresh, very lime-y salsa.  Just the way we like it.
