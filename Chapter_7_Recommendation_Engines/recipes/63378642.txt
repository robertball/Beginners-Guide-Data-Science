Title:
Mexican Spoon Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup yellow corn meal
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
3/4 cup milk
1/3 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs (beaten)
1 17 oz can cream style corn
1 4 oz can chopped green chiles
1 1/2 cups grated monterey jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Mix corn meal, salt, and baking soda.
Stir in milk and oil; mix well.
Add eggs and corn; mix well.
Pour 1/2 mixture into greased 9x9 baking pan.
Sprinkle 1/2 the cheese and 1/2 of the chiles over mixture.
Repeat layers ending in cheese.
Bake for 45 minutes or until wooden pick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
MATTOON250
Description: 
This delicious Mexican spoon bread pairs well with tacos, burritos, or any other Mexican dish.  It may also be served as a substitute for potatoes with any kind of meat or poultry.
