Title:
eastern omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, beaten
1/2 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
1/4 t crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
salt and pepper to taste
4-5 slices deli style american cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees
spray a 9 inch glass pie plate with non stick spray.
mix the beaten eggs with cilantro and seasoning. pour in the pie plate.
Cook in the microwave for about 1 minute.  stir egg, top with sliced cheese, and put in the oven for 7-8 minutes. check often to make sure egg isnt burning.
enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
Maryam
Description: 
A wonderfully tasty omelet with fresh cilantro, red pepper flakes, and cheese. So good even my toddler loves it.
