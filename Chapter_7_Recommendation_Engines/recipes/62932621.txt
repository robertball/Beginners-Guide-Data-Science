Title:
Chocolate Melting Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz semi-sweet chocolate
8 oz sweet chocolate
2 cups (4 sticks) butter
12 eggs (room temperature)
1/2 cup sugar
3/4 cup flour

Directions:
Melt the chocolate and butter.
Mix eggs and sugar, and whisk for a few minutes, then add the flour.
Add the egg mix to the melted chocolate and mix.
Pour the mix into 12 greased ramekins.
Bake directly in the oven at 325 degrees for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
HongKongCV
Description: 
These small cakes, baked in individual ramekins, are a chocolate lover's dream.  On the outside, a moist chocolate cake.  On the inside, molten chocolate, tasty enough to satisfy anyone's sweet tooth.  This is just like what is served on Carnival cruise ships!
