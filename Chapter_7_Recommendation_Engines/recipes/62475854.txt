Title:
Taco Seasoning Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp hot chili powder
1 1/2 tsp paprika
1 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp + freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
optional ground cayenne pepper to taste
optional crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
Mix all measured spices in a small bowl.
TO USE:  Add to browning meat (when meat is partially browned). After browned, add 1/2 to 1 cup water, cook down to blend flavors.

Submitter: 
KATHYESG
Description: 
Much lower salt than pre-made.  Salt can be omitted.

Great for low fat option with 1/2 ground buffalo & 1/2 ground turkey breast or whatever you prefer.
