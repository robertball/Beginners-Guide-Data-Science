Title:
Pumpkin Pie (Fresh Pumpkin) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups pureed fresh pumpkin
1 can sweetened condensed milk
2 egg
2 tsp pumpkin pie spice (or 1 tsp cinnamon 1/2 tsp nutmeg, 1/4 tsp allspice for ginger free pie)
1/2 tsp salt
1 pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Whisk pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices and salt in medium bowl until smooth. Pour into crust.
Bake 15 minutes at 425, reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F and continue baking 35 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted 1 inch from crust comes out clean.
Cool. Garnish as desired.
Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.
