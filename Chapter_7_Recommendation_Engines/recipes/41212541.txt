Title:
Cheesy Wings (low carb!) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3943807.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds chicken wings
2 cups grated Parmesan cheese (not shredded)
4 Tbl dried parsley
2 Tbl dried oregano
4 tsp paprika
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1/2 cup butter

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
Line a shallow baking pan with foil or parchment paper (or use Pampered Chef 11x17 stoneware)
Cut the wings into three parts (toss the tips or save for soup)
Combine the Parmesan cheese, parsley, oregano, paprika, salt and pepper in a bowl
Melt the butter in a shallow bowl
Dip each drumstick in butter, roll in the cheese and seasoning mixture and arrange in the foil-lined pan
Bake for 1 hour, then kick yourself for not making more!

Submitter: 
Sharon Springer Andrews
Description: 
Good hot or cold and they are Low Carb! They taste even better the next day.
