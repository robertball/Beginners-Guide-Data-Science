Title:
Spicy Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp.oil
1/2 cup diced  onion
1/2 cup diced green pepper
2 cloves garlic, finely choppped
1 can diced green chilies, undrained
1 tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. oregano, crumbled
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. ground pepper
1 can (680 mL) tomato sauce
1 cup plain yogurt
1 tbsp. flour
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
4 cups shredded cooked chicken
8 flour tortillas
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  To prepare enchilada sauce, heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat.  Add onion, green pepper and garlic; saute for 2 minutes.  Add next 8 ingredients (green chilies through pepper); cook, stirring for 2 minutes.  Stir in tomato sauce, yogurt and flour; cook stirring for 1 minute than add cilantro.  Spread 1 cup of enchilada sauce in bottom of greased 9x13 baking dish.
To prepare filling, combine chicken with 1 cup sauce.  Spoon about 1/2 cup of filling down one side of each tortilla.  Sprinkle each with some cheese and then roll up tortillas jelly roll fashion and place seam side down in a single layer on top of enchilada sauce in baking dish.  Pour remaining sauce over top and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Bake uncovered for 30-35 minutes until bubbly and heated through.
