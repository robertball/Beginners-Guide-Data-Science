Title:
Crockpot Chicken Parisienne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. chicken parts
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup dry white wine(may use
chicken broth)
1(10 3/4-oz.)can condensed cream
of mushroom soup
1(4-oz.)can sliced mushrooms,
drained
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup flour

Directions:
Place chicken pieces in crockpot. Season with salt and pepper. Mix wine and soup; pour over chicken. Add mushrooms. Cover and cook on Low for 7-9 hours.
Remove cooked chicken; keep warm. Stir together sour cream and flour. Stir sour cream mixture into mushroom gravy. Heat for 30 minutes.
