Title:
Broccoli Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chopped fresh broccoli
1 1/2 cups cubed, cooked chicken meat
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/3 cup milk
1/2 c sour cream
1/2 onion, diced
1 garlic clove
1/2 c sliced mushrooms
1/4 c butter
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
4 tablespoons butter, melted
1 cup Panko bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Cook broccoli in boiling water for 3-4 minutes.  Drain.
Saute onion, garlic and mushrooms in 1/4 c butter for 5 minutes.  Let cool.
Stir together 1/2 c melted butter and bread crumbs.
In seperate bowl, combine soup, sour cream, milk, butter and onion mixture, chicken,  1/2 c cheese and broccoli.  Please in casserole dish.
Top with remaining cheese and then top with bread crumbs.  Bake 45 minutes.
