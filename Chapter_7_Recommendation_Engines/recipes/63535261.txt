Title:
Avocado Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/965845.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ripe avocado
1 clove garlic
1 tablespoon veganaise or mayonaise
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Spoon avocado into bowl
Crush garlic
Add to avocado
Add Veganaise (Mayonaise)
Salt & Pepper
Blend together until creamy.
Spread on your sandwich.

Submitter: 
ChrissyintheKitchen
Description: 
Avocado spread, delicious and nutritious, spread this on a bread for a healthy and quick meal.
