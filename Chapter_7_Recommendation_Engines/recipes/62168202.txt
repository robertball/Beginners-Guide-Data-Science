Title:
Kalamata Olive Aioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayo
1 Tablespoon chopped garlic
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/4 cup pureed pitted Kalamata olives

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together thoroughly.
Serve with anything!  Especially good as a dip with pita chips, raw veggies etc, or as a spread on a sandwich.

Submitter: 
Kathy W.
Description: 
This fabulous spread can be used with crackers, spread on a sandwich or used to dip French Fries!  It is sooo versatile and easy to make.  Enjoy
