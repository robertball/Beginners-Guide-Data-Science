Title:
G's Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsps bacon grease
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp chipotle powder
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp oregano
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1/4 cup salsa or picante sauce
1 cup water
1 teaspoon chicken granules
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
corn chips/sour cream for garnish

Directions:
Saute onion, garlic and red pepper in bacon grease over medium heat till tender. Add spices to the saute and stir to mix.
Add beans, water, chicken granules, and salsa. Simmer for 25 minutes.
Use an immersion blender to puree to desired thickness. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Ladle soup over corn chips and top with a garnish of sour cream.

Submitter:

Description: 
This quick and easy thick soup uses canned black beans and is served over corn chips to add texture and crunch.  The flavor of bacon and added spices make it tasty and satisfying. I think this is a bit like a warm dip and a soup all-in-one...Modified from: Black Bean Soup II
