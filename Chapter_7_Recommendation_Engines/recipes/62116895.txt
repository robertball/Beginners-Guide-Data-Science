Title:
Maple Muffins with Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BATTER:
1/4 c. Butter, melted
1 can Sweetened Condensed Milk (14 oz.)
2 Eggs
1t maple flavoring
1/2 t cinnamon
2 c. flour
2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
2t lemon juice
TOPPING:
1/4 c. brown sugar
1/4 c. flour
1/4 c. butter, NOT melted
1/4 c granola, crushed

Directions:
Mix all ingredients for the batter.
In separate bowl, mix topping ingredients, cutting the butter into the other ingredients, to create lumps.
Pour batter into greased muffin tins or muffin cup papers. Sprinkle the batter with the topping mixture.
Bake at 365 degrees for 15-20 min.
Let cool for 5 min. and serve.

Submitter: 
germanerin
Description: 
This recipe was made by combining ingredients left over from a pie I was making. I was looking for something to serve for the following morning and the muffins ended up being a hit! They are not too sweet, and not too bland due to the topping.
