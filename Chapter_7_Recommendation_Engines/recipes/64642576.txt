Title:
Anna's Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 28 oz Baked Beans14 oz Diced Tomatoes2 cups of white sugar4 TBSP apple cider vinegar1 pound bacon

Directions:
In large bowl, combine first 4 ingredients.  Top with Bacon stripsPour into small roaster (or 9 x 13 pan)Bake in pre-heated oven for 2 to 2  1/2 hours.  They are "done" when slightly thickened. You can cover if bacon getting too dark.  An alternative (if you prefer really crisp bacon) you can fry bacon on stove.  Add bacon drippings in the last half hour of cooking.
