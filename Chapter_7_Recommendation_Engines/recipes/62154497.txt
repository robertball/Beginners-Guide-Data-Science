Title:
San Antonio Sweet and Spicy Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One 46 ounce jar of whole dill pickles
One 16 ounce can of pickled jalapeno slices
4 garlic cloves
4 cups of sugar

Directions:
Drain jar of pickles and jalapeno slices.
Slice whole pickles in thick slices
Add pickle and japapeno slices into original pickle jar. Add garlic cloves.
Pour sugar into jar. You will not be able to pour full amount at once. You will need to pour as much sugar as you can, cover jar with lid, shake and let the sugar disolve over a period of time until you have added the full 4 cups.
Cover the jar and let sit in dark pantry or refrigerator for a period of at least 72 hours. During this time, shake and turn the jar at least twice a day.
Serve as a side or as a snack! Enjoy!
