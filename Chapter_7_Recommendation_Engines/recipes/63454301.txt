Title:
Baked Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/939393.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tbsp butter, melted
1 c brown sugar
4 eggs, beaten
1 tbsp baking powder
1 tbsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
2 c milk
6 c regular or quick cook oats
2 c fresh or frozen fruit (any kind)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and spoon into greased 9x13 baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes until set, but not too dry.
Leftovers heat up easily in microwave with milk.

Submitter: 
KKraft
Description: 
Baked oatmeal adds a unique texture to traditional oatmeal. Even hubby loves it.
