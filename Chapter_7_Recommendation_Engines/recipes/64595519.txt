Title:
Banana Nut Bread - Fanny Farmer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ripe bananas, well mashed
2 eggs well beaten
2 cups flour
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Grease a loaf pan
Mix the bananas & eggs together in large bowl
Stir in flour, sugar, salt & baking soda
Add walnuts
Pour into pan
Bake 1 hour

Submitter: 
Leslie Grossman
Description: 
This is from an old Fanny Farmer cookbook - a family favorite!
