Title:
Kay's Fruit Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup self rising flour
1 cup sugar
1 cup milk
1/2 stick butter
1 can pie filling

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Melt butter in 8 x 11 dish
Mix flour, sugar and enough milk to make consistency of pancake batter.
Pour cake mixture into dish with melted butter
Pour pie filling on top
Bake 30 - 40 minutes
