Title:
Susan's Quick and Easy Tartar Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 Tbsp. finely chopped onion
2 Tbsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. prepared horseradish (optional)
dill weed, freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and allow to chill for 1 hour to allow flavors to marry.
