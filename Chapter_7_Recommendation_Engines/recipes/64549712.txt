Title:
Cinnamon French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tubes of cinnamon rolls and the frosting that comes with them
6 eggs
2 Tsp cinnamon
2 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup of milk
1 cup of maple syrup

Directions:
Cut up cinnamon rolls into very small pieces
Put the frosting aside for now
Spray a 9x13 pan
Add the cinnamon rolls
Mix the eggs, cinnamon, vanilla and milk together
Pour over the cut up cinnamon rolls
Pour 1 cup of maple syrup over the rolls
Bake at 375 for 25 minutes
Remove from oven and top with the frosting that comes with the rolls

Submitter: 
Cindy Yonkman
Description: 
Delicious, easy make ahead breakfast treat!
