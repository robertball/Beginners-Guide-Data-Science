Title:
Krysie's Ham and Potatoes au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 20 ounce package of diced potatoes with onion
1 1/2 cup diced cooked ham
1/4 cup margarine
1/4 cup flour
2 1/2 cups skim milk
1 cup grated low fat cheddar cheese
1/4 teaspoon ground mustard
1/4 teaspoon lemon pepper

Directions:
Combine potatoes and ham in a greased 1.6 liter casserole.
Melt margarine over medium heat. Stir in flour. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens.
Add cheese, ground mustard and lemon pepper to the sauce. Stir until the cheese melts.
Pour cheese sauce over ham and potatoes, stirring to mix.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 50 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Ham and potatoes in a tasty, creamy cheese sauce. Packaged potatoes with onion significantly simplify preparation. Low fat dairy ingredients reduce fat, calories and cholsterol.
