Title:
Roasted Beet and Kale Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1009265.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Bunch of fresh kale, cleaned and cut to bite size pieces
3 large red beets, roasted, skin removed and cut to 1" squares
1/4 cup dried cherries
1/8 cup golden raisins
1/2 cup chopped cashews
1 Tbs dijon mustard
1/2 cup apple cider
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 lemon, juiced
Salt & pepper to taste
2 tsp apple cider vinegar
2-3 Tbs olive oil

Directions:
Salad:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Clean beets and cut off the greens and long tail (leave skin on)
Line a cookie sheet in reynolds wrap.
Drizzle beets in olive oil and rub until completely covered.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Bake in oven for 1 hour to hour and a half (until beet is cooked all the way through and tender)
Turn beets half way through baking.
Remove from oven and let completely cool (about 30-45 minutes)
Peel skin off of beat and cut to 1" bite size pieces.
In large bowl add beets, kale, cashews, golden raisins and dried cherries.
Salad Dressing:
In medium glass bowl add apple cider, apple cider vinegar, dijon mustard, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt and pepper and whisk well.
Slowly add 2-3 Tbs of olive oil while whisking constantly.
Pour over salad and toss.
Cover bowl and place in fridge to chill for at least an hour for flavors to marinate.

This is an incredibly tasty salad that is packed full of nutrients and vitamins! It is also one that will keep you feeling satisfied longer than most.
