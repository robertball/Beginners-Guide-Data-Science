Title:
Pampered Chef Curly Pasta & Chicken Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (8oz) uncooked fusilli pasta
2 T cornstarch
1/2 t salt
1 can (10.5oz) condensed chicken broth
1 lemon
2 large carrots
1 medium red pepper
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
2 t vegetable oil, divided
2 pressed garlic cloves
2 cups broccoli flowerets
2 T basil
1 oz parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Cook pasta according to package directions, drain and keep warm.
2. Meanwhile, combine cornstarch and salt in medium bowl. Whisk in chicken broth, set aside. Zest lemon, add to chicken broth mixture. Cut carrots diagonally and bell pepper into 1 inch pieces.
3. Cut chicken into 1 inch strips. Heat 1t of oil in skillet over medium-high heat. Press garlic into skillet. Add chicken, stir-fry 5 minutes. Remove from skillet. Heat remaining oil in skillet, add carrots and bell pepper; stir-fry 2-3 minutes until tender.
4. Add broth mixture and browned chicken. Bring to boil; reduce heat to medium and cook until mixture is slightly thickened, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat. Stir in pasta and basil. Serve immediately. Top with cheese.
