Title:
Jan's Non Stick Pan Coating Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup all purpose flour

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a mixer bowl and blend until smooth and creamy.
Place in an air tight container.  May be kept at room temperature.
Apply to baking pans with wax paper, paper towel or pastry brush.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
A great substitute for aerosol pan release.  Use when recipes call for greasing and flouring your pans. Simple, easy and no aerosol cans to dispose of or buy.
