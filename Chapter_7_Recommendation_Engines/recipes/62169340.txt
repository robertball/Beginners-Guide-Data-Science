Title:
Rachel's Chicken Cruton Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/337678.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup OR ½ cream of chicken for mushroom haters
1 can cream of celery soup
2 packages of seasoned croutons (the kind you prefer)
1 pound boneless chicken breasts or tenderloins, sliced fajita style or 1 inch chunks (again your preference)
1 cup of shredded  cheddar cheese
1 teaspoon Cumin
1-2 teaspoons oregano
Salt, pepper etc to your taste

Directions:
Set oven to 375.
Boil chicken in 3-4 cups of water on stove top until cooked.
In medium sized bowl, pour chicken stock (water from chicken or your own broth) over 1 package of croutons.
Place in a 13x9 baking dish and flatten to form a crust.
In separate bowl, mix all cans of soup, seasonings and chicken.
Pour mixture over “Crust” in baking dish.
Top dish off with crutons from other bag (not the whole bag!)
top with cheese! Put in oven for 15-20 minutes.
Serve and enjoy!
