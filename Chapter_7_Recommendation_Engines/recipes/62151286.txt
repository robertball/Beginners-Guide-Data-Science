Title:
Breton Cracker Cookie Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 packages of Original Breton Crackers
1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup chocolate chips
1/2 cup nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Line cookie sheet with foil
Place crackers on the cookie sheet in one layer. Make sure the crackers are over lapping each other.
In a saucepan, on low heat, melt the butter.
Then add the brown sugar and bring to a boil. Boil for 3 minutes.
Pour mixture over crackers evenly.
Bake in the oven for 15 minutes.
Remove from oven and then sprinkle the choclate chips over the crackers.
Let chocolate chips melt then spead evenly over crackers with a knife.
Let cookies cool then break brittle up and store in tupperware.
