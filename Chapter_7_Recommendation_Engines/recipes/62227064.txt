Title:
Garlic and Feta Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Green beans
feta
garlic salt
butter

Directions:
Steam the green beans al dente
Put 1 T butter in a serving dish; let it sit to room temp.
Toss the beans in the butter; season with garlic salt
sprinkle with feta, toss again
