Title:
Not your Mothers Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
l head iceberg lettuce, shredded
3 large tomatoes, chopped
4 green onions, diced (include tops)
16 oz can chili beans drained, not chili or con carne
16 oz can whole kernal corn, drained
8 oz. cheddar cheese, grated
1-16 oz. bottle Catalina dressing
1-14 oz. bag tortilla chips, crushed

Directions:
Combine lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and cheese.  Add drained beans and corn.  When ready to serve, combine with crushed chips and catalina dressing.

Submitter: 
Carol Keller
Description: 
Delicious salad that has a very different taste than your usual taco salad.  No meat by choice. Does not keep well but that's not usually a problem!
