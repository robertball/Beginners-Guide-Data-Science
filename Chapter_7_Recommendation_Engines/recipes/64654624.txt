Title:
Fudgy Nutella Cookies with Sea Salt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4547567.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Nutella
1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
2 eggs
1 cup + 2 tablespoons flour
Coarse Sea Salt for Sprinkling (to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine all ingredients, except salt, in a large mixing bowl.  Mix with electric mixer until well combined.  Place dough in freezer for 10 minutes.
Roll dough into approximately 1-inch balls and place on ungreased baking sheet, about 2 inches apart.  Bake for 10 minutes.
Remove from oven and sprinkle with sea salt.  Cool for a few minutes until cookies are set then, carefully, transfer to a wire rack.
