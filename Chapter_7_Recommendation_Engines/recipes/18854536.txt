Title:
Christmas Tree/Wreath Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/309596.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter shortening
1 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
2/3 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 1/2 cup sifted flour

Directions:
Combine shortening, salt and vanilla. Add sugar, eggs. Mix well. Add flour a litle at a time. If you are making the Christmas Trees. Add about 6 drops of food coloring.
