Title:
Broccoli, Cauliflower Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/301754.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 16 oz bag frozen broccoli
1- 16 oz bag frozen cauliflower
1 box Uncle Bens Wild Rice
2 cans of cream of mushroom soup
1 large jar Cheeze Whiz
1 pound of colby cheese [shredded]
sprayed 9x13 dish
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook broccoli and cauliflower,drain; set aside.
Cook rice according to package directions; set aside.
Mix cheeze whiz and cream of mushroom soup together in a bowl; set aside.
Layer broccoli and cauliflower in bottom of pan.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Layer rice on top of vegetables.
Dollop cream of mushroom soup mixture over rice.[ will spread out while cooking].
Sprinkle cheese over top.
Bake in a preheated 350*oven for 30 to 45 min. till golden brown and bubble.
You can make this ahead of time. Put plastic wrap over top. Take plastic wrap off before placing in the oven.
