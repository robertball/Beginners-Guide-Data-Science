Title:
Heart Healthy Chocolate Cake (Vegan) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030821.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons Chia seeds
3 tablespoons Flax seeds
1 cup hot water
2 ounces unsweetened baking chocolate
1/4 cup hot water
1/2 cup agave syrup
1/2 cup raw sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon olive oil
4 ounces firm tofu
1 1/2 cups Spelt flour
1/2 cup Oat flour
3/4 cup cocoa powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon sea salt
1 tablespoon lecithin
Frosting:
4 tablespoons Chia seeds
1 cup water
1/2 cup agave
6 ounces firm tofu
1 teaspoon vanilla
1-1 1/2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 F.
Put seeds (Chia and Flax) in a blender, add 1 cup of hot water, let sit for 10 minutes. Blend mixture at top speed.
Add baking chocolate and 1/4 cup hot water to chia/flax mixture in blender.
Add agave syrup and raw sugar to blender mixture.
Add olive oil. Blend again at top speed.
Add 4 ounces of tofu and vanilla to blender.
Blend again.
In a bowl, mix together, flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, salt.
Combine dry and wet ingredients.
Divide batter into two separate cake pans.
Bake for 25-30 minutes.
Let each cake cool thoroughly.
Frosting:
Add Chia seeds and hot 1/2 cup water to blender. Let sit for 10 minutes.
Blend at high speed.
Add agave, tofu, vanilla, chocolate chips.
Blend at top speed.
Place blended mix in refrigerator for about 2 hours or until chilled.
Spread on cakes and place cakes one on top of the other.
