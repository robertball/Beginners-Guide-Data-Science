Title:
Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-8oz pkg cream cheese
8 oz sour cream
4 T Miracle Whip
1/4 C milk
10 oz frozen, chopped spinach
1 C shredded mozzarella cheese
3/4 C shredded parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
2 T chopped onion
1 Can or jar (12 oz) marinated, quartered artichoke hearts

Directions:
Preheat over to 350.F
Put cream cheese, sour cream, miracle whip, milk, both cheeses, and garlic in a microwave safe dish; cover. Microwave at 1 - 2 minute increments, stirring each time until well blended. Add onion, spinach, and artichoke hearts; mix well.
Bake in greased 2-quart baking dish at 350 degrees F for 50 minutes. Uncover, bake another 10 minutes.
Serve with chips and salsa.
