Title:
Hearty Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup lemon-lime soda
1/2 cut soy sauce
1/4 cup honey
1 teaspoon dried thyme
3/4 teaspoon dired rosemary, crushed
1/4 teaspoon pepper
6 bone-in pork loin chops (7 ozs each)

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the first six ingredients; add the pork chops.  Seal the bag and turn to coat; refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight, turning the bag occasionally.
Drain and discard marinade.  Grill pork, covered, over medium heat 6-8 minutes on each side or until juices run clear.
