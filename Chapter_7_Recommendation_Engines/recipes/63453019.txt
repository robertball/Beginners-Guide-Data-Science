Title:
Chinese Chicken & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless chicke breast cut into julienne strips or tofu
2 cups of water
1 cup brown rice
1/2 tsp sea salt
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 cloves of garlic minced
1 1/2 cup broccoli flourets
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1/2 cup water chestnuts
1 cup penny-sliced carrots
1 cup green onions sliced
Marinade
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 tbsp minced ginger root
2 tsp low-sodium soy sauce or gluten free tamari
1 clove garlic minced
dash hot pepper sauce

Directions:
1. combine all marinade ingredients, place chicken in marinade and let sit for an hour in refrigerator, meanwhile oven 350F.  Prepare six-quart covered casserole dish with cooking spray.  Add rice, water & salt and bake for 40 minutes or until the liquid is absorbed.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet and gently saute garlic until just fragrant.  Add sliced chicken and saute until no-longer pink.  Add chopped vegetables and cook until soft.  Add to rice.
