Title:
Roasted Eggplant, Tahini and Yogurt Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium sized eggplants roasted, peeled and cooled
1 head of garlic, roasted (no oil)
Â¼ cup of tahini
1 cup  fat free plain yogurt
1/3 cup chopped parsley
1 oz lightly toasted pine nuts
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Scoop out flesh of roasted eggplant and add to a food processor. Add the rest of the ingredients (except the pine nuts)to the food processor and blend to smooth consistency. Pour into a bowl and add the pine nuts.
Enjoy!

Description: 
A super yummy spread that goes great on pita, in a sandwich, or just eaten plain.
