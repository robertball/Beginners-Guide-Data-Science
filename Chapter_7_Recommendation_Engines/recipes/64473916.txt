Title:
Chicken Salad Croissant Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole rotisserie chicken, shredded
1 c. finely diced celery
1/2 c. chopped red onion
1 medium red delicious apple, peeled & chopped
1 c. dried cranberries
1 c. mayonnaise
green leaf lettuce
croissant

Directions:
Mix the first 6 ingredients until well combined.  Chill until ready to serve.  Halve the croissants and top with green lettuce and two heaping tablespoons of the chicken mixture.  Refrigerate any leftover chicken salad.

Submitter: 
Kathy Friedrich Estes
Description: 
I am fairly picky about my chicken salad and I fell in love with this recipe.  My family loved it.
