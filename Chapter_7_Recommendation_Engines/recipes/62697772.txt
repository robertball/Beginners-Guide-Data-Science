Title:
Queso Fresco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 gallons whole pasteurized milk
1 pack direct set mesophilic starter or 4 oz. prepared
1/4 tsp. 'plus' rennet
2-3 TBS. salt

Directions:
Heat milk to 90 degrees, add starter, stirring, for 3 min.
Add a little over 1/4 tsp. rennet.
Let set for 30-45 min. until clean break
Cut the curds.
Raise temp. to 95 over 20 min, no more than 2 degrees in 5 min, stirring gently every few minutes to prevent forming into a mass.
After 20 min, allow to set undisturbed for 5 min, allowing it to drop to bottom and form a mass.
Drain the whey and mix in the salt.
Keep curds at 95 and allow to set for 30 min.
Line mold and press at 35 pounds for 6 hours.
Remove from mold and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Valorie
Description: 
An easy cheese which can be aged for as long as you want. Use instead of mozzarella.
