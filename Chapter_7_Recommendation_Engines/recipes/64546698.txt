Title:
Baked taco bowls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3238644.jpg
Ingredients:
10 inch flour tortilla wraps -- one for each salad you are making.
Tortilla bowl makers

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Fit tortillas in bowl makers or over small oven-proof bowls.
Prick tortillas well.
Bake for 10-15 minutes.  Check after 10 minutes. They brown quickly!

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
Use these for serving taco salad--they look great, and you can use the crispy fragments to scoop up every last bit of the salad!
