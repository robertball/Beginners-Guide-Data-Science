Title:
Pumpkin Spice Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1419066.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups water
dash salt
1/2 cup pure pumpkin puree
1-1/3 cups large flake oats
1 tbsp molasses
1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp vanilla
approx. 1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Bring salted water to a boil.
Add pumpkin puree and return to a boil.
Add oats & stir well, reducing temperature to simmer.
Stir in spices, molasses and vanilla.
Simmer while stirring for 10 minutes, adding milk to keep at desired consistency.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Debbie Wind Bailey
Description: 
I love pretty much anything pumpkin!  After having some amazing pumpkin pancakes yesterday, I decided I needed to use some of the leftover pumpkin puree for something a little healthier.  I came up with this Pumpkin Spice Oatmeal and both my 12 year old son and I loved it!
