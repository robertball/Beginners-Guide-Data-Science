Title:
Speedy Salmon Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992961.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14.75 ounce) can salmon, drained
1/3 cup finely chopped onion
1 egg
5 saltines, crushed
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 teaspoons butter or margarine

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first seven ingredients; mix well. Shape into six patties. In a skillet, fry patties in butter over medium heat for 3-4 minutes on each side or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home's Fast Family Favorites
Description: 
Seven easy ingredients to make nicely seasoned Speedy Salmon Patties. They're also tasty with chopped green peppers added to the mixture.
