Title:
Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 white onion chopped
2 garlic cloves chopped
3/4 cup Jasmine rice
2 teaspoons chicken bouillon
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
3 cups water
juice from 1/2 lemon
2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium pot
add onion, garlic, rice and spices and cook on high until rice has a little color (1-2 minutes)
Add 3 cups water and continue until boiled
Reduce and summer for about 20 minutes until its mostly evaporated while stirring occasionally
Add lemon and dill before serving
