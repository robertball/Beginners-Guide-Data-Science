Title:
Mandarin Orange & Almond Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. granulated sugar
1 c. sliced almonds
1 head romaine lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces
1 can (11oz.) mandarin oranges, drained
1/2 c. thinly sliced red onion
For DRESSING
2 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp. granulated sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 c. vegetable oil

Directions:
In small skillet melt sugar over medium-high heat stirring constantly until lightly browned. Add almonds; stir and coat with carmelized sugar. Remove from pan, spread on wax paper and allow to cool.
For dressing, combine lemon juice, mustard, sugar, and salt; whisk in oil.
Toss lettuce, oranges, onion, and sugared almonds together.
Add dressing.
