Title:
Sherry Chicken & Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
Salt & Pepper
Garlic Powder
1 small onion, chopped
1 8oz pkg fresh mushrooms, sliced
2 tbsp butter
1 tbsp olive oil
1/4 cup cream sherry
Chicken Broth(optional)
4 slices of provolone or swiss cheese

Directions:
Season chicken breast with salt,pepper and garlic powder. Heat butter and oil in large skillet. Add chicken and saute on medium heat until cooked thoroughly, about 3-4 minutes on each side depending on thickness. Remove from skillet and set aside. Add onions and mushrooms to skillet.Add a tbsp of oil if necessary. Saute until veggies are slightly wilted.  Add sherry to skillet to deglaze pan for about 2 minutes. Return chicken to skillet. Cover and let simmer on low for another 15 minutes turning chicken once. Spoon mushroom/onion mixture over each piece of chicken and place 1 slice of cheese over each piece of chicken. Turn burner off, replace cover until cheese melts.
