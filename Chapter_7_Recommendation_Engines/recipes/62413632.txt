Title:
Summer Veggies & Rice Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1&1/4 cup dry rice
2 cubes vegetable boullion
1 yellow or red pepper, chopped
1 onion, chopped
4-6 cloves garlic, minced
1T vegetable oil
2 small zucchini, cut into 1/4" rounds
2 tomatoes, slided
1 can of any white bean, rinsed
1/8 c oregano
1/3 c sour cream
1/3 c plain yogurt
1 c shredded cheese

Directions:
Cook the rice according to package directions, adding in the boullion cubes.  Set aside.
Saute the onion, garlic and peppers in the oil until
tender.  Add them to the sour cream, yogurt, beans & oregano.  Set aside.
Spray a 9x13" glass pan with cooking spray then layer the rice, the zucchini rings (stagger them into
2 layers) then the tomatoes.  Then top with the yogurt/sour cream mixture.
Microwave for 10-15 minutes then top with the shredded cheese and microwave another 5-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
SUSANK29
Description: 
This is a great way to use those summer vegetables in
a vegetarian main dish without having to turn on the oven.
