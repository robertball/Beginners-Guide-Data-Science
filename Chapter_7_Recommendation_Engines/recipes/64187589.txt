Title:
Fry Sauce ( Edies) dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1 Cup Mayonnaise
1/2 Cup Heinz® Ketchup
Garlic Salt
Seasoned Salt
Worcestershire Sauce
Buttermilk or Half and Half
Directions
Mix all ingredients well.
Use buttermilk or half & half to thin to individual preference. Use garlic salt, seasoning salt and Heinz® Worcestershire Sauce to taste
