Title:
Sheila's Italian Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound spicy Italian sausage
1 pound ground beef or 1 add'l lb of spicy or sweet italian sausage
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 med onion diced
1/2 small bell pepper
2 (32 ounce) containers of beef broth add water as needed
2 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style stewed tomatoes
1 cup cubed carrots
1 stalk celery sliced
2 (14.5 ounce) can great Northern beans, or cannellini beans, drained
1 9oz package of fresh spinach - packed, rinsed and torn
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt (optional)
1/2 tsp oregano
1   tsp basil
8 oz ditalini pasta

Directions:
In a stockpot or Dutch oven, brown sausage with garlic, and vegetables.  Stir in  broth, tomatoes, oregano, basil  and season with salt and pepper. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes.
Stir in beans and pasta. Cover, and simmer another 15 minutes or until pasta is tender.
Remove from heat, and add spinach.  Replace lid allowing the heat from the soup to cook the spinach leaves.  Soup is ready to serve after 5 minutes.
