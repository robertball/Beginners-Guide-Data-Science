Title:
Juanitas Picadillo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger meat
1 can rotel
1 small onion (diced)
3 potatoes ( cut in to 1 1/2 inch pieces)
1 tbs. ground cumin ( you can add more to your taste)
1 tbs. groung garlic powder
pinch of salt

Directions:
1.)Crumble the hamburger meat in a large sautee pan, add a little water only enough to cover meat 1/4 of the way.
2.)Cook the hamburger meat with the ground cumin and garlic powder.
3.)In anouther pan boil potatoes till tender but yet firm. Drain and add to hamburger meat.
4.)Add in your onion and rotel with juice from can. Let simmer for 10-15 min.
Serve with rice, beans and warmed tortillas.
