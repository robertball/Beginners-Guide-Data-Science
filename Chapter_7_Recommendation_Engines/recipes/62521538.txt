Title:
Barbacoa pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb trimmed pork roast (tenderloin)
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 can coke or dr. pepper (try any sugar soda)
15 oz can red enchilada sauce (any tomatoe sauce ok)

Directions:
Mix sauce in bottom of crockpot
place pork roast in sauce
cook until fork tender
low 6-8 hours or high 4-6 hours
shred
serve on tortillas or chips and salad with cheese and taco fixings
