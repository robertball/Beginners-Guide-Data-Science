Title:
floyd eudy's chicken and dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4lbs chicken
1 cup celery
1 cup chopped onions
1 cup carrots
2 bell peppers
parsley
1 clove garlic
salt & pepper to taste
15 flour tortillas
1 can cream of chicken soup

Directions:
skin chicken pieces, wash & cover with water.
add the salt, pepper, celery, onions, carrots, bell peppers & parsley.
bring to a boil.  reduce to low heat and cook 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
remove chicken and set aside to cool; break into bite size pieces.
strain broth & throw vegetables away.
return broth to cooking pan & add 1 can campbells cream of chicken soup.
bring to boil again.
add flour tortillas that have been cut into pieces- cut strips, then cut in half. add a few at a time.
add chicken back into pan.
cover and let simmer 10-12 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
easy chicken and tortilla dumplings. great comfort food for cold winter time.
