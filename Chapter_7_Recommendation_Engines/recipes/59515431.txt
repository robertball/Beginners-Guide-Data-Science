Title:
Toaster Oven Chicken Cordon Bleu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/268570.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 slices Swiss cheese
4 thin slices high quality cooked ham
1/2 cup fresh bread crumbs
2 tablespoons melted butter
1 tablesspoon parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat the toaster oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Coat a 7x11 inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness.
Sprinkle each piece of chicken on both sides with salt and pepper.  Place 1 cheese slice and 1 ham slice on top of each breast. Roll up each breast, and secure with a toothpick.  Place in baking dish, and bake for 30 minutes.
While the chicken bakes, toss the melted butter, crumbs and parmesan.  When the chicken has baked for 15 minutes, Sprinkle the crumb mixutre over the chicken.  Bake an aditional 15 minutes.
Allow the chicken to rest for 5 minutes.  Slice and serve immediately.

Description: 
This fun baked dish is ideal for the toaster oven.  Give it a try!  This dish is best served sliced in rounds and served with  a side of bernaise or holladaise.  This is how we enjoyed it in France.Modified from: Chicken Cordon Bleu I
