Title:
Alta's Lower-Fat Vanilla & Cinnamon Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/322277.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups fat free Half & Half cream
2 cups 2% milk
1/2 cup heavy cream
5 tsp pure vanilla extract
1/4 tsp imitation butter flavoring, adds richness (optional)
Pinch of salt
2/3 cup sugar
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/3 cup water

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients, mix well.
In a small saucepan, combine and cook last three ingredients until mixture is rapidly boiling and sugar is well dissolved.
Slowly add the sugar syrup into the milk and cream mixture stirring constantly until incorporated thoroughly.
Chill mixture for 15 minutes before placing into your ice cream maker.
Freeze in an ice cream machine according to the manufacturers instructions.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
This is a delicious and rich egg-less vanilla  ice cream made with fat free half and half, 2% milk and ground cinnamon. A very satisfying treat.
