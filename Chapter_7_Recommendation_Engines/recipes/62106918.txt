Title:
Hobo Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb raw hamburger meat
1/4 c. milk
4 medium potatoes, sliced thin
1 medium onion, sliced thin
6 slices American cheese
1 can cream of chicken soup
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pat hamburger into 2 quart glass dish.
Pour milk over meat and
add salt and pepper to taste.
Next, layer the potatoes and
add salt and pepper to taste again.
Add the layer of sliced onion
top with cheese.
Pour the chicken soup over the top and spread to cover the cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours or until potatoes are done.

Submitter: 
Donna L Whitaker
Description: 
This is a very easy recipe to make that my grandmother used to make for us. Very delicious!
