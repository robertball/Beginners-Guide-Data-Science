Title:
Cabbage & Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1/2 tsp garlic salt
3/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 16-oz can of Kidney beans (undrained)
1/2 medium head of cabbage chopped
1 28-oz can of diced tomatoes
1 tomato can of water
4 beef boullion cubes
1 Tbsp sugar

Directions:
In a dutch oven (I use a big pan pot) brown meat and drain.
Add all the remaining ingredients bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for 1 hr.
Fell free to double the recipe and/or to use light and dark kidney beans.
