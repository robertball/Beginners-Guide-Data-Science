Title:
Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
5 roma tomatoes, seeded and chopped
1 yellow pepper, chopped
1/2 cup chopped cilantro
1/2 cup chopped green onions
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 jalopeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped (optional)
3 T fresh lime juice
1 T red wine vinegar
1 T olive oil
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground cumin

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl. Refrigerate several hours or overnight so that flavors blend. Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Dana Kinney
Description: 
I make this salsa for all kinds of gatherings and am asked for the recipe each and every time. There are never any leftovers. It is delicious!
