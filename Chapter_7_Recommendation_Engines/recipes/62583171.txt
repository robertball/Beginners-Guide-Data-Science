Title:
Hearty, Healthy Black Bean soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/549709.jpg
Ingredients:
A drizzle of olive oil
1 Ham bone with some meat
1/2 onion, peeled and chopped
1/2 bag of baby carrot
3-4 stalks celery, chopped, including the leafy parts
2 Tbsp. chopped canned green chilies
4 cloves garlic, crushed
1 small red or yellow bell pepper, seeded and chopped (optional)
1/2 bag of fingerling potatoes
2 fresh tomatoes
2 tsp. ground cumin
1 19 oz. can black beans, drained
1 can sweet corn niblets (optional)
1 can of V8 juice
4 cups water
salt and pepper
Sour cream, chopped cilantro, and/or chopped green onions for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Place ham bone into a large slow cooker.
Heat the oil in a large saucepan and sauté the onion, garlic, and celery for 5 minutes until the onion becomes to translucent.  Pour over the ham bone.
Sauté the carrots, potatoes and other vegetables for about another minute to coat the oil on surface of the vegetables.  Add them to the slow cooker along with the beans, cumin, chilies, tomatoes, V8, water, salt and pepper.
Cover and cook on High until warm. Reduce heat to Low, and continue to cook for 5 to 6 hours.
Remove the bone only and add the meat comes from the bone back to the rest.  Put the soup in a refrigerator over night, and discard all thick fat on the top of the soup.  Heat again and serve with the garnish.

Description: 
The Best Black Bean soup!  If you are looking for a recipe for a hearty, healthy soup using a leftover ham bone, this is for you!  Key is discarding thick fat from the soup and must use green chilies.  Enjoy!!
