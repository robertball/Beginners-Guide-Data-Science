Title:
Sugar-free Chocolate Mocha Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2C fat-free milk
1 pkg. sugar-free choc. instant pudding mix
4 oz. fat-free whipped topping
1/2t. instant coffee crystals

Directions:
Pour milk into med. bowl. Dissolve coffee crystals in milk. Add pudding mix and stir until dissolved, 2 min. Stir in whipped topping. Chill until set. Can sprinkle w/shaved dark choc. or serve w/sliced bananas or strawberries

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Can make without coffee crystals.  Can use vanilla pudding mix and omit coffee and add vanilla extract
