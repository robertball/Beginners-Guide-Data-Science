Title:
Just-4-Kids Ranch Cheeseburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
1 pound ground beef
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
4 hamburger buns

Directions:
Combine Ranch mix, beef and cheese.  Shape into patties; cook thoroughly, until meat is no longer pink in the center.  Toast buns before serving, if desired.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Your kids will love these Ranch flavored cheeseburgers.
