Title:
Creamy dijon flounder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 flounder fillets
1 teaspoon greek seasoning (Cavenders)
1 c. heavy cream
1/2 c. dijon mustard
2 dashes crushed red pepper
1 tsp. orange marmalade

Directions:
Heat skillet on medium and spray with cooking spray.
Sprinkle fish with Cavenders seasoning and add to pan.  Cook 3-4 min on each side.
Mix remaining ingredients with whisk and add to pan.  Simmer 10-15 minutes until sauce is thickened and bubbly.

Submitter: 
paynes
Description: 
This is a great sauce to add to any white fish.  My mother in law shared this recipe with me.
