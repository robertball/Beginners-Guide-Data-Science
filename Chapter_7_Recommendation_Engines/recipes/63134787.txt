Title:
Scrambled Eggs with Chives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs
4 tablespoons of water
1 tablespoon of chopped chives
olive oil
paprika

Directions:
Beat eggs and water. Mix in chopped chives.
Coat the pan with a thin film of olive oil. Scramble egg mixture over medium heat.
Sprinkle with paprika and serve.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
A simple recipe that is a nice change from regular scrambled eggs.
