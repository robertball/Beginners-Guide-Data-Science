Title:
Bundt Cake With Poppy Seeds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (18.25 oz.) of white or yellow cake mix
1 box (3.5 oz.) of instant vanilla pudding
1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
4 eggs
1/2 cup of vegetable oil
1 cup of hot water
1/4 cup of poppy seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together all of the ingredients. Grease and lightly flour a 12 inch bundt pan.  Pour all of the mixed ingredients into your pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour.
serves 10 or 12

Submitter: 
Linda Grein
Description: 
This is a very good tasting and very moist cake to serve as desert, for birthdays, or when it's wanted.
