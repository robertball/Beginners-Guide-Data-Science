Title:
Homemade Vanilla Coffee Creamer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint Fat Free Half and Half
1 can Fat free sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp of vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine half and half and condensed milk in a small pot and warm until steaming.
Add cinnamon and vanilla cook five minutes longer, careful not to boil.
Remove from heat, let stand 5 minutes.
Store in container for up to 10 days.

Submitter: 
Erinn Danna
