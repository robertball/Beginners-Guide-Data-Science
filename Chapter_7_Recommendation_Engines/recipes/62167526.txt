Title:
Hearty Tex-Mex Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 red pepper, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 pkg. taco seasoning mix
1/2 cup water
4 cups frozen cubed hash browns
1 pkg. (10oz.) frozen corn
1/2 lb. (8oz.) Velveeta cheese, cut in to cubes

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 F.
Brown ground beef with peppers an onions.
Drain and return to skillet.
Stir in taco seasoning and water.
Mix in potatoes, corn, and Velveeta.
Spoon in 12x9-inch baking dish. Cover.
Bake for 20 minutes. Stir.
Uncover dish and bake for another 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
MochaMe
Description: 
All in one bake. Serve with a nice salad. A family favorite!
