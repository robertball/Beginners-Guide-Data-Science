Title:
Shrimp with Cilantro Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pesto:
2 TBS pine nuts
2 cloves of garlic chopped
2 cups fresh cilantro
2 tsp. fresh lemon juice
2/3 cup grated parmesan
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup olive oil
Pepper to taste
Shrimp:
1-1/2 TBS olive oil
2 cloves garlic minced
1-1/2 lbs. medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/4 cup lemon juice
Pepper to taste
1 TBS. chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Pesto:
In a food processor, puree pine nuts, garlic, cilantro, lemon juice, cheese and salt. Slowly add oil and blend until almost smooth.
Add pepper, and mix in to incorporate. Cover and refrigerate. It will keep at least a day or two.
Shrimp:
In a large skillet, heat oil over high heat. Add garlic and cook 10 seconds, stirring frequently.
Add shrimp in one layer and cook 1 minute. Gently flip shrimp over and cook another minute.
Add lemon juice and pepper; cook until shrimp are cooked through. Stir in cilantro. Remove shrimp to platter and serve pesto on the side.
