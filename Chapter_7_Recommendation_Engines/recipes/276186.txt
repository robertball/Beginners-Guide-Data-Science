Title:
Pomegranate Holiday Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7155730.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups ice, or as needed
2 fluid ounces vodka
2 fluid ounces pomegranate juice
1 lime, juiced
1/2 fluid ounce orange-flavored liqueur (such as CointreauÂ®)
2 1/2 fluid ounces club soda, or as needed
1 teaspoon pomegranate seeds, or to taste

Directions:
Fill 2 highball glasses and a cocktail shaker with ice.
Add vodka, pomegranate juice, lime juice, and orange-flavored liqueur to the cocktail shaker and secure the lid. Place one hand on the lid and one hand on the shaker and shake vigorously for a slow count of 10, or until the outside of the shaker frosts up. Divide cocktail between the prepared glasses. Top with soda water and pomegranate seeds and stir before serving.
