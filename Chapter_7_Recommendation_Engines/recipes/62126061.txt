Title:
Rice & Kilbasa Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 kilbasa, sliced into 1/2 inch rounds
1 onion, diced
1 red bell pepper, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 16 oz. can kidney beans, undrained
1/4 tsp crushed red peppers
2 shakes habenero sauce
salt & pepper to taste
3 c cooked rice

Directions:
Fry kilbasa, onion, bell pepper and garlic in a large skillet until onions are translucent.
Add crushed red peppers, habenero sauce, salt & pepper to sausage mixture.  Fry while constantly stirring for 2 minutes.
Add rice and mix well.  Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Erin Noel
Description: 
Savory kilbasa, rice & beans make for a hearty one-skillet meal.
