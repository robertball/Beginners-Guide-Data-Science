Title:
Cheap White Bread Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 slices white bread
Shredded cheese
Your choice of toppings:  pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, peppers, etc.
Melted butter (w/wo garlic powder)
Pizza sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450&#0176
Wash hands thoroughly.
Slice crust from bread.
Place 1 teaspoon of pizza sauce on bread.
Top with cheese.
Add one other favorite topping.
Fold corners in and then squeeze with 'clean' hands to form ball.
Dip into melted butter.
Bake on cookie sheet for 10 minutes or until golden brown, turning once.
Dip in warm sauce if desired.
