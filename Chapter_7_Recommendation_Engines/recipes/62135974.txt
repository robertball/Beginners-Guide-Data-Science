Title:
Rotisserie Chicken Shepherds Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/322286.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups boned and skinned rotisserie chicken meat, pulled.
2, 10.5 oz cans of Swanson's Chicken Ala King, found in the canned tuna isle of your store.
1/4 tsp dried crushed tarragon leaves
1, 6.5 oz can sliced mushrooms, drained
1, 16 oz pkg frozen petite peas, cooked in salted water and drained.
1, 14.5 oz can sliced carrots, drained
1, 4 oz pkg Idahoan loaded baked instant potatoes, prepared with 1 1/2 cups very hot water.
1/3 cup whipped cream cheese
1 egg yolk
1/2 tsp dried dill weed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In large bowl combine chicken meat, Chicken Ala King, tarragon and mushrooms.
Gently stir in peas and carrots.
Pour and spread chicken mixture evenly in prepared 3 quart baking dish sprayed with non-stick cooking spray.
For the topping, add the cream cheese, egg yolk and dill to the prepared mashed potatoes stirring until smooth.
Top chicken mixture with mashed potatoes.
Bake uncovered in preheated 350 degree F oven for 30-40 minutes until heated through and top is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
Tender rotisserie chicken meat is combined with tarragon and a few other simple ingredients for a fantasticly easy and light version of shepherds pie. Delish.
