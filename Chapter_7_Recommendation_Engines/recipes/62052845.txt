Title:
Chocolate Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C butter, softened
1/2 C shortening
1 1/2 C sugar
2 eggs
2 t vanilla
2 3/4 C flour
1/3 C cocoa powder
2 t cream of tartar
1 t baking soda
2T sugar
2t cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Cream together butter, shortening, 1 1/2 C sugar, eggs and vanilla. Slowly blend in the flour, cocoa powder, cream of tartar soda and salt.  Shape dough by rounded teaspoonfuls into balls.  Mix together 2 T sugar and 2 t Cinnamon.  Roll balls in mixture, place on baking sheets covered with parchement paper <or ungreased baking sheets> 2 inches apart.  Bake 8-10 minutes until set but not hard.  Remove immediately to cooling racks

Submitter: 
Jackie Wood
Description: 
A  little twist to those wonderful cinnamon-sugar cookies my mom used to make!  Alternate them on the plate with regular snickerdoodles.  Great for the holidays!
