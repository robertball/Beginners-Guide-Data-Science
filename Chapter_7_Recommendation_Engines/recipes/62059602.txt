Title:
awesome artichoke spinach dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup real mayo
1cup grated parmesan cheese
3/4 cup cream cheese
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon chopped garlic
3 tablespoons spinach (I use frozen leaf)
1 14 oz can (drained) artichoke hearts
1/2 of a small red onion coarsely chopped

Directions:
Combine all ingerdients in medium saucepan and simmer over low heat until cheese melts and mixture is bubbly. Stir frequently. Let cool and serve warm with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
AMYJOHNSON1
Description: 
This is a combination of some of my favorite artichoke dip recipes. I could never find one that had everything I was looking for so I created this one. It gets rave reviews at gatherings.
