Title:
Kendra's Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. salt
3 cups flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together, well.
Roll into small balls and press with the bottom of a glass to flatten, slightly.
Bake 12 to 15 minutes
