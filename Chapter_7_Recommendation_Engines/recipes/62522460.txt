Title:
Cinnamon Bubble Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954647.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pkg (18) frozen rolls
1 c. margarine
1 c. brown sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2.  Coat Bundt pan with non-stick spray
3.  Sprinkle half (1/2 c.) of the nuts in bottom of bundt pan.
4.  Layer frozen rolls in bundt pan.
5.  Combine margarine, brown sugar and cinnamon in microwave safe measuring cup and microwave in 30-second increments until melted and combined.
6.  Pour over rolls in bundt pan.
7.  Sprinkle remaining (1/2 c.) nuts on top.
8.  Let rise overnight.
8.  Bake lightly covered with foil for 20 min., uncovered for 15 min. (total 35 min.)
