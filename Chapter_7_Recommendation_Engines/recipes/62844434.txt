Title:
Baked Italian Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2# macaroni
2 1/2 cups milk (maybe more)
1 cup butter
2 cloves garlic
1# sausage, browned
12 oz. frozen spinach, thawed, rinsed
8 oz mushrooms, chopped, sauted
2 cans tomato soup
6 cups shredded cheese
2 tsp onion powder
2 Tbs Italian seasoning
1-2 tsp salt
1/2 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Boil macaroni according to package directions.
Combine all ingredients except bread crumbs, mix well.
Pour into extra large cake pan, top with bread crumbs.
Bake @350 for 45 minutes.
