Title:
Bev's Easy Broccoli Puff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
1 cup biscuit mix
1 cup milk
2 eggs
1 cup shredded cheddar
dash of cayenne (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.  Grease a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish.  Beat biscuit mix, eggs, and milk until smooth.  Stir in broccoli, cheese, and cayenne (if using).   Pour mixture into prepared casserole, and bake for 1 hour, allowing top to bake to a golden brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
A nutritious and delicious alternative to stuffing, and easy enough to serve on a weeknight!
