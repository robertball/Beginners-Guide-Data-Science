Title:
Frozen Lime Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/12160.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen limeade concentrate
12 fluid ounces rum
1 tray ice cubes

Directions:
In a blender, combine limeade concentrate, rum and ice cubes. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Bea Gassman
Description: 
You can make this a banana daiquiri simply by adding a banana!
