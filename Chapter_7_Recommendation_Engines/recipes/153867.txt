Title:
Maple-Glazed Ham Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987029.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup long grain rice or dried medium noodles
1 (15.25 ounce) can DEL MONTE® Pineapple Tidbits In Its Own Juice
1/4 cup maple-flavored syrup
1 teaspoon cornstarch
1 (15.25 ounce) can DEL MONTE® Green Lima Beans, drained
8 ounces ham or smoked turkey, cut into bite-size strips, cooked

Directions:
Cook rice or noodles according to package directions.
Drain pineapple, reserving 2 tablespoons juice. Stir together reserved pineapple juice, maple syrup and cornstarch in large skillet. Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly, cook and stir 2 minutes more.
Stir in beans, ham and pineapple. Heat through. Serve over rice or noodles.

Submitter: 
DEL MONTE®
Description: 
Serve this ham and pineapple dish over hot rice.
