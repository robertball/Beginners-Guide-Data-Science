Title:
Chocolate Pudding Cream Cheese Brownies - Marlene Cook Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brownie Mix - and ingredients to make brownies
8 oz. cream cheese - softened
1 c. powdered sugar
1 - small tub of cool whip
1 - family size chocolate pudding (instant)
3 c. milk

Directions:
Make brownies according to box directions (9x13 pan).  Let cool.
In the meantime, mix softened cream cheese & powdered sugar (add a little at a time).  The stir in cool whip just until mixed.  Do not over-mix.
Spread on cooled brownies.
Mix pudding with 3 cups of milk then gently spread on top of white mixture.
Refrigerate until set.
