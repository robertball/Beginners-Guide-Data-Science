Title:
Classic Stovetop Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1 Small Onion, Chopped
1 Green Pepper, Chopped
1/2 Tsp. Dried Basil
1/4 tsp. sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
3/4 tsp. garlic powder (Or two cloves of fresh minced Garlic)
2 Tbsp. Chili Powder
3/4 tsp. Oregano
1 (15 oz) Can Tomato Sauce
1 (14.5 oz) Can Stewed Tomatoes with juice
1 (14.5 oz) Can Diced Tomatoes with juice
1 (15.5 oz) can Chili beans (Choose mild or spicy depending on your preference)
1 (15.5 oz) Can Kidney beans, Rinsed and drained

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown beef and onions. Drain Juices
Transfer to a dutch oven. Add all remaining ingredients except both cans of beans
Mix well and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to a simmer, cover and simmer 45 minutes. Stirring occasionaly.
Add both cans of beans and simmer 15 minutes.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Jodster
Description: 
This is a Classic, unfooled around with recipe. It's a bit milder than some but it can be given a little kick if desired. You can alway's add more or less of something to taylor to your tastes. But It's still a really good basic recipe. It's even better the next day.
