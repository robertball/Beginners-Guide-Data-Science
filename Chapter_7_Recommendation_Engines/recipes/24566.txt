Title:
Easy Devonshire Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351671.jpg
Ingredients:
3 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 pinch salt
1 cup heavy cream

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream together cream cheese, sugar and salt.  Beat in cream until stiff peaks form.  Chill until serving.

Submitter: 
STARP
Description: 
A wonderful topping for scones, or use as a dip for fruit.
