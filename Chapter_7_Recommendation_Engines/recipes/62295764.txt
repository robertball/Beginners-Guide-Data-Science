Title:
Taco Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages/tubes of refrigerated crescent rolls
1 (8 oz) carton of sour cream
1-1/2 lbs. of ground beef
2 c. shredded sharp cheddar chees
1/2 c. chopped onion
1 package taco seasoning
crushed Doritos
2/3 c. water

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 degrees. Spread crescent rolls in 9x12 glass baking dish. Brown meat and onion. While meat is cooking, put baking dish in oven for just a couple minutes, or until crescent rolls get a little "puffy". Drain meat mixture, add taco seasoning and water. Spread meat mixture in the dish over the dough. Spread sour cream over meat. Sprinkle cheese, then add chips. Bake for 20 minutes.
