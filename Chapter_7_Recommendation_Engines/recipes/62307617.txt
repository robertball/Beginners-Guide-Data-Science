Title:
Grama Kid's Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 or 4 large cucumbers, peeled and sliced thin.
1 large or 2 medium Yellow onions, cut in quarters and sliced very thin.
---
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup (or a little less) white vinegar
1/2 cup (or a little less) granulated sugar

Directions:
In a medium - large bowl, slice cucumbers and onions and in layers, sprinkling each layer generously with salt and a little pepper. Add a single layer of yellow onion, sliced in rings on top of each 1 inch layer of cucumber, ending with the onion on top. Let set at room temperature for 2 to 3 hours.(If juice forms, use it in the salad).
In a small bowl mix together the mayo, white vinegar and sugar.
Pour over the cucumber and onion combination and mix together. Cover and let flavors blend in the refrigerator for a short while before serving.

Submitter: 
americanwumn
Description: 
This is a delicious way to eat all those crazy cucumbers that grow like crazy in the warm weather without just making them into pickles!
It's a refreshing little side salad to go with lunch or dinner.
