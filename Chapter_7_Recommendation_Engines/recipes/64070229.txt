Title:
Bowl of Brown Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs beef ribs and bones
2 lbs beef round cut into small pieces
2 chicken thighs
whole game hen
3 C dark beer
4 C beef broth
1 cup barley
2 apples, cored and chopped
1 cup pearl onions
2 carrots, cut into chunks
1 T salt
2 t poudre forte
Worcestershire sauce or molasses

Directions:
Brown all meat. Place inside big pot or crock pot. Cover with beer and beef broth. Cook on a gentle simmer about 6 hours for slow cooker, and 3 hours on the stove top. Add the barley, onions, carrots, apples and seasonings. Cook for about 2 hours more in the slow cooker, and 1 hour on the stove.
