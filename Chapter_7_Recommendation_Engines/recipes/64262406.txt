Title:
Butter-Pecan Roca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb real butter
2 cups sugar
2 tablespoons light corn syrup
6 tablespoons water
1 cup finely chopped Pecans
1 (11 1/2 ounce) bag milk chocolate chips

Directions:
1)Over Medium heat, melt butter in a large pot.
2)Add corn syrup, water and sugar to pot and cook until temp reaches 285 degrees stirring constantly with a wooden spoon (mixture will rise and bubble quite a bit).
3)Remove from heat and Stir in 1/2 of the Pecans.
4)Turn mixture onto a greased cookie sheet WITH EDGES!
5)While the mixture is cooling, melt the chocolate over low flame until all the chips are gone, and chocolate is smooth.
7)Pour the hot chocolate over the Pecan mixture and spread evenly.
8)Sprinkle with remaining Pecans and allow candy to cool completely and chocolate to harden before breaking apart and serving.
