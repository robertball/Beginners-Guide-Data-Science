Title:
Roasted Red Pepper & Almond Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (250 mL) sliced almonds, toasted
1/4 cup (50 mL) mayonnaise
1 cup (250 mL) jarred roasted red peppers, drained and patted dry
1 oz (30 g) Parmesan cheese, finely grated (or nutritional yeast)
1 tbsp (15 mL) balsamic vinegar
1 garlic clove
1/4 tsp (1 mL) salt
1/8 tsp (0.5 mL) coarsely ground black pepper
Additional sliced almonds and diced roasted red peppers (optional)
Grilled pita wedges, crackers, or crudites (optional)

Directions:
Place almonds and mayonnaise in food processor; cover and process until finely chopped. Add peppers, cheese, vinegar, garlic, salt, and pepper; process to desired consistency, scraping sides as necessary. Place into a bowl.
Top with additional almonds and peppers, if desired. Serve with grilled pita wedges, crackers or crudites, if desired.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This versatile dip adds zesty flavor to any food it's served with.
