Title:
Country-Style Pork Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/694561.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz of pork
2 oz of onion, diced
5 oz of carrots
5 ounces of brussel sprouts
4 oz chicken stock
1/2 teaspoon of dried dill weed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
5 ounces of cauliflower

Directions:
If using large brussel sprouts, cut in half.
Cut cauliflower into appropriate sizes (between 1-inch to 2-inch sizes)
Peal and Cut Carrots lengthwise and cut into 1-inch pieces
Rinse brussel sprouts, carrots and cauliflower separate under running cold water.
trim excess fat from pork shoulder, cut pork into 1/2 or 3/4 inch squares.
Chop Onion, Diced
Brown Onion, Pork and pork fat in a 10-inch skillet for about 5 mins. Remove pork fat after browned. If you do not have any pork fat fry the pork in a bit of oil.
Stir in chicken stock, dill, and pepper. Cover, heat to boiling.
Stir brussel sprouts, into pork mixture, Cover, Heat to Boiling; reduce heat. simmer 5 mins. If your using fresh brussel sprouts cook for 10 mins.
Stir carrots into pork mixture, Cover, Heat to Boiling; reduce heat. simmer 5 mins.
Stir cauliflower into pork mixture, Cover, Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer until cauliflower is tender, about 3-5 mins.
At this point there should be very little water\broth in the pan.
strain off into a strainer or scoop out with a slotted spoon.
Salt to taste

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
A nice and easy family dinner using one pan. This is something we have made for the past 30+ years from time to time. All the ingredients have been weighed to increase accuracy.
