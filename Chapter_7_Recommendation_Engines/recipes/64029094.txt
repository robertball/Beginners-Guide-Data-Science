Title:
Big Ray's Barbecue Ribs in the Crock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1119605.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds pork baby back ribs
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
2 Tbsp  soy sauce
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp black pepper
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 cloves minced garlic
1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
2 tsp liquid smoke
1 cup ketchup
1 cup favorite bbq sauce
1 cup chili sauce
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
4 tablespoons vinegar
2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 dash hot sauce
1/2 tsp liquid smoke
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Mix red wine vinegar, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, black pepper, olive oil, minced garlic, Dijon mustard and liquid. Pour into a resealable plastic bag with ribs and marinade overnight.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Take ribs from marinade, discard liquid.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Place in a shallow baking pan. Brown in oven 15 minutes. Turn over, and brown another 15 minutes; drain fat.
In a medium bowl, mix together the ketchup, bbq sauce, chili sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, oregano, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, liquid smoke, and salt and pepper. Place ribs in slow cooker. Pour sauce over ribs, and turn to coat.
Cover, and cook on Low 6 to 8 hours, or until ribs are tender. Carefully remove from slow cooker and broil a few minutes each side.
