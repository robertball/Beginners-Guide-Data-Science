Title:
Steak House Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
2tsp chicken boullion
1/2 cup hot water
1 tsp greek seasoning
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp seasoning salt
4-6 rib eye steaks depending on size.

Directions:
Melt butter in 9x13 pan. Disolve shicken boullion in hot water.  Mix seasonings with chicken boullion and pour into pan with butter.  Bake at 375 15-20 min.  Turn and bake another 15-20 minuters.  Do not cover.
