Title:
Cucumber Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz Sour Cream
8oz Cream Cheese (soften to room temperature)
1 Packet of Hidden Valley Ranch Dip Mix
1 Large Cucumber (diced)
1 Jar Chunky Salsa
1 Package Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Mix sour cream and ranch until blended and then add cream cheese. Spread the mixture in the bottom of a 8x8 round serving dish. Layer with diced cucumber, salsa, and top with your favorite shredded cheese.  Refrigerate and serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Valerie
Description: 
If you are looking for something refreshing, quick and easy, this is it!  Even better than 7 Layer Dip!
