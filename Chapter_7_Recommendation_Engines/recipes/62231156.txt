Title:
Citrus Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (8 serving size) Orange Jello
1 cup orange juice
1 (15 oz) can red grapefruit
1 (9 oz) can mandarin oranges
8 oz nondairy whipped topping
8 oz mayonnaise

Directions:
Dissolve Jello in 2 cups boiling water; stir until dissolved completely (3 minutes). Add 1 cup cold water and 1 cup orange juice, stir until blended. Pour into glass serving dish. Drain grapefruit and mandarin oranges; add to Jello. Refrigerate until set, about 4 hours.  Prepare topping by combining whipped topping and mayonnaise, beat together until well blended. Spoon topping on individual servings.
