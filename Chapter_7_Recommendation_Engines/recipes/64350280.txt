Title:
Black Bean and Chicken Enchilada Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (10 oz) enchilada sauce
12 corn tortillas (6 in)
2 cups coarsely shredded rotisserie chicken
1 small onion, chopped
1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
3 cans (4 oz) green chilies
3 cups crumbled queso fresco or shredded Mexican cheese blend
2 avocados
2 tbsp sour cream
2 tbsp lim juice
1/2 tsp salt
Chopped fresh tomoates and cilantro

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spread 1/2 c enchilada sauce into greased 13x9-inch baking dish; top with four tortillas,1 cup chicken, 1/4 cup onion, 1/4 cup beans, 1/3 cup green chilies, and 1 cup cheese. Repeat layers. Drizzle with 1/2 cup enchilada sauce and top with remaining tortillas, onion, beans, chilies, sauce and cheese.
Bake, uncovered for 25-30 minutes or until bubbly and cheese is melted. Let stand for 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, in food processor or blender, blend one avocado, sour cream, lime juice, and salt. Process until smooth, and set aside
Top lasagna with remaining avocado (cubed), tomatoes, and cilantro.
Serve with avocado sauce.
