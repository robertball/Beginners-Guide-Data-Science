Title:
Classic Hot Fudge Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup butter, cut into 8 pieces
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, bring cream and butter to a simmer, stirring occansionly.
Add sugars and stir until completley dissolved. Whisk in cocoa powder until smooth, making sure there are no clumps. Serve immediately over ice cream or keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week and rewarm in the microwave.
