Title:
Thai Gingered Carrot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup minced onion
1/2 cup grated fresh ginger
1 cup cooked brown rice
4 cloves garlic, minced
7 cups Chicken Stock (or 7 cups water, 7 teaspoons better than Bouillon)
8 cups sliced peeled carrots
1 can coconut milk
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

Directions:
Heat oil in heavy large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add onion, garlic and cup minced ginger and saute until onion is translucent, about 8 minutes. Ad chicken stock, cooked rice and sliced carrots. Cover and simmer until carrots are tender, about 30 minutes.
Working in batches, puree mixture in blender or processor. Return soup to saucepan over low heat. Mix coconut milk and cumin. Season soup to taste with salt and pepper.

Description: 
The addition of coconut milk gives this a Thai flavor and flair. The pureed rice adds a surprisingly creamy texture and thickness. Infused with lots of grated fresh ginger and a hint of garlic, this creamy carrot soup is the perfect start to an autumn lunch or dinner.Modified from: Gingered Carrot Soup
