Title:
Easy Baked Brie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (8 oz.) refrigerated crescent dinner rolls*
1 round (8 oz.) Brie cheese
3 tablespoons whole berry cranberry sauce
1 tablespoon chopped pecans*
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350`F. Spray cookie sheet with cooking spray.
2. Unroll dough;separate dough into 2 sections. Press dough to form 2 squares, firmly pressing perforations to seal. Cut corners off both dough squares to make round and reserve to use for cutouts.
3. Place 1 dough round on cookie sheet. Cut cheese crosswise into 2 equal layers. Place bottom half of cheese on center of dough circle on cookie sheet.
4. Spread cranberry sauce over cheese, sprinkle with chopped pecans, and top with remaining cheese half.
Bring dough up around side of cheese, pressing and pinching dough.
5. Place remaining dough on top of cheese round, and press evenly around side of cheese.
6. Brush dough with beaten egg.
7. Bake 20 to 30 minutes or until deep brown. Cool 15 minutes. and place warm pastry-wrapped cheese on platter.

Submitter: 
Tiffanie DeMayo
Description: 
This goes wonderfully with fresh fruit. 

I use phyllo dough in place of crescent rolls, but phyllo is harder to work with. Both pastries are good.
