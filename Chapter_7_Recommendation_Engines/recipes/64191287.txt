Title:
Deconstructed Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1536073.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Package Key Lime Mix
1/3 Cup cold water
2 cups boiling water
2 egg yolks
1 Tbsp Butter
Green food coloring (optional)
Sweet cookie ( gram crackers)
Whipped cream

Directions:
Add egg yolks and cold water in saucpan. Mix
Add the package of Key Lime Mix and mix.
Add the boiling water. If wanted, ad the colring here. Turn heat on Medium and consistently mix until it thickens into a "custard". Add butter and pull of heat. Let cool for 2 minutes, then mix. Let sit for 2 more
Minutes, then mix again. Let sit for 1 more minute.
Get a small glass bowl and add the crushed sweet cookie at the bottom. Add some Key Lime curd. Let sit for 3 hours and top with whipped cream.

Very easy and yummy. Great for people who want pies without the hassle. For a more detailed look, put the filling in small tart shells.
