Title:
Taco Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
LAYER 1 - 3 Avocadoes
1 Tablespoon lemon juice
2 Tablespoons mayo
LAYER 2 - 3/4 cup sour cream
1 package taco mix
4 tablespoons mayo
LAYER 3 - 2-oz. shredded cheddar cheese
2-oz. shredded monterey jack
1 small can chopped chilies,
drained
LAYER 4 - 1 bunch chopped green onions
1 chopped seeded tomato
1 small can chopped olives

Directions:
Coarsley mash the avocadoes with the lemon juice and mayo.  Place in the bottom of your serving dish and spread to the edges.
Mix the sour cream, taco mix and mayo together and spread over the avocado mixture.
Sprinkle the cheeses and chilies evenly over the sour cream layer.
Mound the chopped tomato in the center of your dish.  Surround the tomato with the chopped olives and then use the green onions to fill in the space from the olives to the edge of the dish.
Refrigerate to blend the flavors.
Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Kathy W.
Description: 
The presentation on this dish is fabulous, but the fact that it is gobbled up is what makes you really happy.  I use a corningware quiche dish to serve it in so that the white dish offsets the great colors.  Also in a pinch I use frozen guacamole instead of making it myself.
