Title:
Chicken pot pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. cooked diced chicken
3 T. butter
1 medium onion, chopped
1 C. slicked celery
1/3 c. flour
1 1/2 c. milk
1 c. chicken broth
2 cups of mixed frozen vegetables
1 tsp dried parsley
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1 9" pie crust
1 lightly beaten egg

Directions:
Saute onion and celery in butter. Stir in flour and cook 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Gradually add milk and broth, cooking and stirring until it boild and thickens. Add chicken, vegetables, and seasonings. Pour into 1 1/2 qt. round casserole dish.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Roll out crust to 1" large diameter than the casserole dish., cover dish, flute edges and cut vent holes. brush with beaten egg. Bake 30 minutes or until crust is golden and filling is bubbly and hot.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
A great way to use up leftover baked chicken. Everyone enjoys this comfort food!
