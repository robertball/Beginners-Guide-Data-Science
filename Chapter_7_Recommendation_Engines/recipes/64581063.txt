Title:
Super Easy Chicken Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp minced garlic
1/2 ground ginger
4 TBSP soy sauce
4 TBSP lime juice
4 Chicken Breasts

Directions:
Whisk first four ingredients in a large bowl. Add chicken and marinate up to four hours. Grill chicken and baste with leftover marinade, if desired.

Submitter: 
JENNG7
Description: 
This chicken marinade is so easy to make. Besides the chicken it only contains FOUR ingredients that you most likely have on hand.
