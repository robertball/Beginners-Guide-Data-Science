Title:
Jim's pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pork chops
2 cups Wishbone Italian dressing

Directions:
Marinate the pork chops in wishbone Italian dressing for 2 hours up to 24 hours.Take the pork chops and the marinade and put in a large frying pan. Cook on medium high turning the chops occasionally. The marinade with turn from a milky looking mixture to clear then to a blackened look as the meat cooks. The blackensd bits are the spices that are in the dressing. make sure you get the pork chops coated with this on both side. Delicious!!
