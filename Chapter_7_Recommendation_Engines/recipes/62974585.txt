Title:
Homemade Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/736643.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds potatoes (russets are what we use)
1/2 cup milk
3 tablespoons butter (or margerin)
salt and pepper (to taste)

Directions:
Wash potatoes and peel them.
Then cut them into big bite sized pieces.
Start your potatoes in cold water, turn the heat up to medium high and bring the potatoes to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer the potatoes until you can pierce them with a fork with almost no resistance. Carefull not to over cook, or they will be watery. Drain off all the water, and return to pot.
With a hand potatoe masher or electric mixer, start mashing potatoes.
salt and pepper (taste the potatoes!)
contenue mashing.
and add milk and butter to your mashed potatoes.
contenue mashing or whipping until mashed to a smooth creamy texture.
