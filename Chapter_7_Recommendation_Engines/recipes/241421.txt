Title:
Fruity Pebbles™ the Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2089059.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups orange juice
2 cups frozen strawberry daiquiri mixer
2 cups lemon-lime soda (such as Sprite®)
1 cup vodka

Directions:
Mix orange juice, daiquiri mixer, lemon-lime soda, and vodka together in a pitcher.

Submitter: 
Molly
Description: 
My friend, Jane, shared this recipe at our Christmas Bash 2014. It was enjoyed by all. It is very simple to make this recipe as an adult version or a kid-friendly version. Serve over ice. Cheers!
