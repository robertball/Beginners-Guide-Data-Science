Title:
ROASTED PORK BACKLOIN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pork Backloin
1 lb Bacon
1 Green Pepper
1 Onion
Canadian Seasioning

Directions:
Seasion Loin with Canadian Seasioning, Slice Green Pepper and Onion then spred over loin, wrap Loin in bacon. Wrap put in fridge for at least 3 hrs to over night.
Preheat oven to 350 deg bake covered for 30 min, take cover off last 45 min to help krisp bacon. Remove from oven let rest @ 10 min, slice and serve
