Title:
BEST bacon appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 piece(s) applewood bacon
1 1/2 tsp cream cheese
1 tsp brown sugar (light or dark)

Directions:
mix cream cheese and 1/2 of the brown sugar until fully mixed together
spread cream cheese mixture on a piece of uncooked bacon and roll it up
bake in the oven on 375
