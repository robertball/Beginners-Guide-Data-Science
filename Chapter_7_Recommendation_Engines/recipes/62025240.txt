Title:
Rocky Road Drops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
1 bag semi sweet chocolate chips
1 bag butterscotch chips
2 cups raisins
2 cups peanuts
2 cups mini marshmallows
4 cups chow mein noodles
Combine raisins, peanuts, marshmallows, and chow mein noodles in a large bowl.  Melt chips in a double boiler.  Add to other ingredients.  Quickly combine.  If there is not enough chocolate, add more melted chocolate.  Drop by tablespoon amounts onto wax paper.  Let harden and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Shannon LePage
Description: 
These are wonderful to give to friends as gifts.  We make them every year at Christmas.  You can change quanities and ingredients as needed.
