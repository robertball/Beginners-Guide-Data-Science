Title:
Noodles with Soy Sesame Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10-12 oz noodles
2-4 garlic cloves
Small chunk fresh ginger
¼ c sesame oil
¼ c tamari or soy sauce
¼ c rice wine vinegar
Little agave nectar
Little peanut butter or tamari
Little water
1-2 T sesame seeds, toasted
cilantro to serve
green onion to serve
red pepper flakes to serve

Directions:
make noodles and set aside (with little sesame oil if desired)
combine garlic, ginger, soy, vinegar, oil
add agave, peanut butter, and/or water to taste
add sesame seeds
add red pepper flakes if desired
heat sauce if desired (or add to hot noodles)
combine sauce with noodles
serve with cilantro, green onion, red pepper flakes

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
Easy Asian sesame noodles; sauce is good base for other (stir fry, etc)
