Title:
Ham and Cheddar Sandwich with Cranberry-Maple Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mayo:
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1-2 tablespoons maple syrup
1/2 medium apple, unpeeled, finely diced
1/3 cup Craisins, minced
1 teaspoon allspice
2-3 slices 1/4 inch thick cooked and sliced ham
2-4 slices cheddar cheese
4 slices whole wheat bread

Directions:
Mix together mayo ingredients.
Divide mayo mixture evenly and spread on two slices of bread.
Add ham and cheddar according to preference.

Submitter: 
A Turtle in the Kitchen
Description: 
My boyfriend raved about this sandwich when I made it for his lunch the other day. I have an egg allergy and couldn't try it, but since he requested it for lunch again the next day, I knew it must have been good.
