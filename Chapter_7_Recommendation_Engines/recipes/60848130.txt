Title:
Pomegranate Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup hot water
1-1/2 cups pomegranate juice
3/4 cup tequila
1/2 cup fresh lime juice (about 6 limes)
1/4 cup orange liqueur

Directions:
Stir together sugar and 1/4 cup hot water until sugar is dissolved. Stir in pomegranate juice and next three ingredients.
Pour desired amount of pomegranate juice mixture into a cocktail shaker filled with ice cubes. Cover with a lid, and shake 30 seconds or until thoroughly chilled. Remove lid, and strain into chilled cocktail glass. Repeat procedure with remaining pomegranate mixture. Serve immediately.
