Title:
White Cherry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Butter
2 1/2 Cups Sugar
12 Eggs (10 Separated 2 whole)
3 Cups Flour for cherries
2 Cups flour for cake
2 tsp almond extract
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 Lemon
2 lbs cherries

Directions:
1. Cream butter
2. Add 2 cups sugar gradually to butter
3. Beat in yolks (10 separated eggs) and 2 whole eggs into step 2
4. Add the juice of one lemon , almond and vanilla extract to step 3 and beat well
5. Add 2 Cupps flour gradually to step 4
6. Flour fruit with 3 cups flour then add to step 5
7. Beat egg whites (10) and add 1/2 cup sugar in separate bowl
8. Fold in whites to step 6
9. Grease 3 loaf pans
10. bake at 350 degrees F for 1 hour then turn down to 325 degrees F for 1/2 hour
