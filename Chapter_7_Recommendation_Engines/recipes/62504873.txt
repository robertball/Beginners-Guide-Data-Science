Title:
"Dads Good Stuff" Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Chunked chicken breast
1 pkg Chili-O seasoning
1 lrg can Hunts Tomato Sauce
1 can of corn
2 lrg cans Bushes Chili Beans

Directions:
Cut chicken into 1" chunks
Cook chicken until it starts to brown
Pour all other ingredients in
Cook on medium to med-high for 10 minutes or until hot.
Very simple and fast and makes a great cold weather meal!

Submitter: 
Greg Pfaff
Description: 
Hearty chili made with chicken that is not only great for cold weather but is better for you than normal chili.

Serves 4-6
