Title:
Cheesy Green Onion Corn Saute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBL oil
1 pkg (16 oz) frozen corn, thawed, drained
1/4 cup chopped green onion
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add corn and green onion, cook and stir until thoroughly heated. Sprinkle with cheese, cover until cheese melts.
