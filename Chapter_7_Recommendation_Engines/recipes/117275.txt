Title:
Sugar Spiced Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984296.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
2 cups unblanched whole almonds

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first six ingredients.  Add almonds; toss to coat.  Spread into a greased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan.  Bake at 250 degrees F for 30 minutes or until lightly browned, stirring occasionally.  Cool.  Store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Sherri  Jackson
Description: 
'These zippy almonds are spicy yet sweet, and once you start eating them, you can't stop,' says Sherri Jackson of Chillicothe, Ohio.
