Title:
Sweetened Condensed Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tab butter (unsalted) room temperature
1 cup dry milk
1/3 cup boiling water
1 1/2 to 2 cups confectioner's sugar

Directions:
Place butter in the blender, add the dry milk, boiling water & 1 1/2 cups confectioner's sugar and mix until smooth. May need to add up to extra 1/2 cup depending on thickness.  Don't want it too watery.

Submitter: 
Virginia
Description: 
Cheaper than buying, quick & easy to make especially if staples are on hand.  I learned this trick many years ago from a magazine and have used this recipe about 15 years.
