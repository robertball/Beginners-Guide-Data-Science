Title:
Not Exactly Chicken Parmesan - BBQ Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Oil for frying chicken
1 egg
1/4 cup Milk
2 cups Italian Bread Crumbs (Approx)
2 pounds of chicken breast fillets
Your favorite BBQ sauce - 1 1/2 cups (Approx)
2 cup Cheddar Cheese or Cheddar Mixture

Directions:
Mix egg and milk together.
Slice chicken breast in half (as if you were going to butterfly, but cut all the way through)
Dip chicken in the egg mixture and bread with bread crumbs
Heat oil and fry chicken for about 2 minutes per side.
Drain Chicken on paper towels for a couple of minutes.
Place all the chicken on a foil lined cookie sheet.
Top each breast with BBQ sauce (maybe a TBSP or 2 per breast depending on size) and cheese.
Bake in preheated 325 degree oven for about 10-15 minutes.
