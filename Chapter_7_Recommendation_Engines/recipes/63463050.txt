Title:
Lloyd's Eggrolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/942758.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Lb. Hamburger, browned and drained
1 Med. onion, chopped fine
1 Can Bean sprouts, drained
1 Can Bamboo shoots, drained
1 Can Water chestnuts, drained and chopped fine
1.5 Cups Celery, chopped
1 Can Crab meat
1 Bottle Brown spicy mustard
Soy sauce
Grated carrot, one cup optional
Mushrooms to taste

Directions:
Sautee onion in butter,add celery and mushrooms when celery is soft.
Combine all ingredients in large bowl
Add entire bottle of brown mustard,and soy sauce to mixture to taste
Spoon into eggrollwrap or wonton wrap,deep fry,cool eat,yum
