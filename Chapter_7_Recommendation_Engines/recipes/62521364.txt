Title:
Ho-Ho Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick oleo + 6 T., divided
1/2 c. oil
1 c. + 1 T. water, divided
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 c. buttermilk or sour milk
2 c. flour
4 c. sugar (divided)
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla, divided
1 c. butter flavored Crisco
1/2 c. + 6 T. milk, divided
1/2 tsp. salt
1 c. powdered sugar
1 package milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Melt 1 stick oleo.  Cream together with 2 c. sugar, oil, eggs, 1 tsp. vanilla.  Add cocoa, baking soda, 1 c. water, buttermilk and flour.  Beat until mixed well.  Bake in greased cookie sheet (11x15) at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes.  Let cool completely.
Beat 1 c. sugar, butter flavored Crisco, 1/2 c. milk and 1 T. water, 1/2 tsp. salt, 1 tsp vanilla.  Beat 5 minutes and add powdered sugar.  Spread on cool cake.
Combine 1 c. sugar, 6 T. oleo, 6 T. milk in sauce pan.  Boil for 1 minutes.  Remove from stove and add chocolate chips.  Stir until starting to thicken.  Spread over filling.
