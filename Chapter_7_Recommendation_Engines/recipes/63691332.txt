Title:
Mexican Coffee Cup Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. Shredded Hash Browns
1 Egg
1 Tbls. Water
1 Tbls. Cheese or Salsa

Directions:
Spray cup with cooking spray.
Scramble hash browns, egg and water in cup and microwave for one minute.
Add salsa and/or cheese for topping.
