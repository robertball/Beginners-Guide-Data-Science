Title:
Carmel Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 8 oz pkg cream cheese at room temp
1 cup brown sugar
1 t. vanilla
1/4 c. peanuts chopped fine

Directions:
Combine and stir til blended, chill.
Serve with apple slices.

Submitter: 
Livie's Mommy
Description: 
A sweet carmel dip we prefer to use for sliced apples, made with cream cheese and brown sugar
