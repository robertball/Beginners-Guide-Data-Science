Title:
Lemon Meringue Cake with Lemon Curd Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2198099.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 c cake flour
1/2 c all-purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/8 tsp salt
2/3 c granulated sugar
3/4 c unsalted butter, cubed
2 eggs, room temperature
1 tbs fresh lemon zest
1 tsp lemon extract
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 c whole milk
Lemon Curd Filling:
5 tbs fresh lemon juice
2 eggs
2 egg yolks
4 tbs unsalted butter (1/2 stick)
Meringues:
8 egg whites
2 c powdered sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract
Topping:
1/2 pint heavy cream
1/4 c granulated sugar
Zest of 1 lemon

Directions:
Cake:Preheat oven to 350.Spray bottom of cake pan with cooking spray & line cake pan with parchment paper. Spray pan again with cooking spray, ensuring to get the sides. Dust pan with flour.Sift first four ingredients together & set side. With mixer, cream sugar & butter until fluffy. Add eggs & beat until smooth. Add lemon zest, lemon extract, vanilla extract and milk. Mix until smooth. Slowly add dry ingredients; mix until well just well blended.
Pour batter into prepared cake pan & bake 25-30 minutes or until inserted toothpick comes out clean.
Cool cake completely. Once cool, run a butter knife around the edges to ensure the sides are free. Remove cake from pan. Set aside.
Lemon Curd Filling: Bring water to a steady simmer in a double-boiler pan over medium heat. In the top portion of the double boiler, off the heat, whisk together all ingredients except the butter. As you continue to whisk, place the top portion of the double boiler over the simmering water. Continue whisking until the mixture is thick enough to coat the back of a spoon. Add butter & whisk until it is melted into the mixture. Cover and chill for a minimum of 2 hours. Curd may be refrigerated for up to a week.
Meringues: Preheat oven to 425. Beat egg whites in clean, dry bowl with a mixer until stiff peaks form. Slowly add sugar & continue beating until the mixture is thick & creamy. Beat in vanilla. Line baking sheets w/parchment paper & mound 2 meringues onto paper, each one about 8"  wide. Reduce  oven to 245 degrees. Put meringues in oven & cook for 90 min. Next, turn off heat. Leave meringues in oven until cooled.
Topping: Whip cream in a clean, dry bowl with electric mixer. Add sugar & beat until incorporated. Top the top layer, with freshly whipped & fresh lemon zest.

Submitter: 
Amy Lawler
Description: 
A nice alternative to lemon meringue pie, this cake consists of two layers of lemon cake, lemon curd, moist layers of lemon meringue topped off with a light, homemade whipped cream! This recipe is a wonderful treat for a spring or summer event!
