Title:
Carrie's Pad Thai Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1107245.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 lime, juiced
1/8 cup soy sauce
1/8 cup fish sauce (omit if vegetarian)
1 teaspoon tamarind concentrate mixed with ¼ c water
1 (12 ounce) package dried rice noodles
1/4 cup peanut oil (or sesame oil mixed with canola)
1 clove garlic, minced
4 eggs
1 tablespoon paprika
¼ -½ tsp chili powder (optional)
chopped lettuce or baby spring mix
fresh flax oil
1/2 cup chopped peanuts
1/2 cup chopped green onion or chives
½ cup chopped cilantro
fresh bean sprouts (optional)
1/2 lime, cut into wedges

Directions:
Put hot water in a bowl and soak noodles (for approx. 10 minutes, until soft).
Combine sugar, juice of ½ lime, soy/fish sauce, and tamarind & water in a sauce pan and set to medium, stirring occasionally.
Heat peanut oil in a fry pan or wok on medium and add garlic, stirring until soft. Add eggs, stirring to scramble them.  Cook until eggs are almost done.
Strain noodles and add to egg mixture. Pour ½ contents of sauce pan over noodles and stir thoroughly. Continue to cook on medium until noodles are done, adding more of the sauce pan mixture as needed. Sprinkle paprika and chili powder (optional) over noodles.
Take lettuce and put on plate. Drizzle with fresh flax oil. Take generous portion of noodle mixture and place on top. Garnish with chopped cilantro, bean sprouts (optional), green onion, and chopped peanuts. Serve with lime wedge to squeeze over meal as desired.

Submitter: 
Carrie Medina
Description: 
This is my favorite Pad Thai creation. After I started making it at home, I could never go back. This is a full meal, suitable for vegetarians and meat-eaters alike. Please enjoy!
