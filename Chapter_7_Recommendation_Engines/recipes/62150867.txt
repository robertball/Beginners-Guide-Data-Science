Title:
The Original Finger Steak Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
About 2 pounds round or flank steak
1 cup flour
1 egg
1 cup milk
1 tsp salt
1 1/4 tsp sugar
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp garlic salt or powder

Directions:
Tenderized and sliced against the grain in finger-sized portions.
Mix all other ingredients and beat until smooth
Put prepared steaks into batter and allow to set at least 1 hour or longer
Deep fry till brown and steaks are done to suit your taste.
