Title:
Deep fried Cajun turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 pound thawed turkey
6 quarts of water
1 pound of brown sugar
1 pound of kosher salt
1/2 large babe of ice
1 cup/240 mL beer (try a dark ale)
1/2 cup/120 mL honey
1/2 cup/120 mL apple cider
2 tablespoons kosher salt
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 teaspoons white pepper
2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1 cup melted butter
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 teaspoons white pepper
2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1 cup melted butter

Directions:
Brine:
Bring 6 quarts of water to a boil stir in brown sugar and salt. Once fully dissolved let cool. Add ice. In a large container place turkey with brine. Refrigerate overnight 8-16 hrs.
Injection:
Melt butter slowly as not to burn.
Add rest of ingredients and mix well.
Inject into your turkey. Time permitting let turkey sit for 30-40 min.
Cook:
Pat turkey dry. Carefully lower bird into frier.
As a rule of thumb deep fry bird for 3 min per pound plus 5 min. Temp of meat should be 165 degrees F. Remove turkey and cover with aluminum foil for 15 min

Submitter: 
Kyle Mcalister
Description: 
Deep fried deliciousness. Deep frying this Cajun bird gives you a crispy exterior and juicy meat on the inside. When you carve this bird you will see the juices pour out.
