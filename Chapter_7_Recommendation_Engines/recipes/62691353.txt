Title:
Mexican Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6- Chicken breast halves
Marinade:
juice from 5 limes
1/3 cup olive oil
5 garlic cloves minced
1-Tbls paprika
1-tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
4 Tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
Crock pot ingredients:
Marinated chicken
2-Tbsp olive oil
1-sm chopped onion
1/2 green pepper
1-sm jalpeno pepper
3- cloves garlic minced
1-can mild chili pepper chopped (4oz can)
1-10 can mexican diced tomatoes (rotel)
1-tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix all marinade ingredients with chicken brest and marinade over night.
Remove chicken from marinade and salt and pepper. Discard the marinade.
Grill chicken a couple minutes per side.
Heat oil in skillet and saute onion, green pepper, garlic and jalapeno till soft.
Load crock pot with chicken, sauteed veggies, canned peppers, rotel tomatoes, oregano, cumin and salt. Cover and cook for 4-6 hours.
Shred roughly and serve with mexican condiments and soft shells.
