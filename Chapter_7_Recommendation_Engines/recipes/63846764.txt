Title:
Sweet Potato casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042778.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked sweet potatoes
(fresh or canned)
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs beaten
1/2 stick margarine melted
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
Topping
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/3cup plain flour
1 cup pecans chopped
1/3 stick margarine melted

Directions:
Mash sweet potatoes. Add sugar, eggs, salt, margarine, milk and vanilla. Mix well and put in shallow 1 1/2-quart baking dish. Mix topping and spread over sweet potatoes. Bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.
