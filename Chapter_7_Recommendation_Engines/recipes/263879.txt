Title:
Paleo and Keto Almond Butter Mocha for Two Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5322378.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups warm almond milk
1 tablespoon almond butter
2 tablespoons instant espresso powder
1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon stevia powder

Directions:
Combine almond milk, almond butter, espresso powder, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and stevia powder in a blender; mix until well combined and slightly thickened, about 2 minutes. Serve in 2 mugs; dust with cocoa powder.

Submitter: 
Fioa
Description: 
This low-carb rich and decadent almond butter mocha will keep you fueled all day! It is ideal for keto and Paleo diets.
