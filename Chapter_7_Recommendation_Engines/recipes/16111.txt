Title:
Carrot and Raisin Salad I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/188225.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded carrots
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 cup raisins
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the carrots, celery, raisins, mayonnaise and vinegar. Mix together and refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
Honda
Description: 
This is an excellent lunch box salad (my husbands favorite)!
