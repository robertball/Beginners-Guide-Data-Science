Title:
Lemon-Rosemary Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979119.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons Lawry's® Seasoned Salt
2 teaspoons whole dried rosemary, crumbled
1 teaspoon Lawry's® Lemon Pepper
1 (4 pound) roasting chicken

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
In small bowl, combine Lawry's(R) Seasoned Salt, rosemary and Lawry's(R) Lemon Pepper. Rub seasoning mixture under chicken skin, over outside and inside cavity of chicken.
In 13 x 9-inch roasting pan,  with or without rack, place chicken breast-side-up. Roast 60 minutes or until meat thermometer inserted in thickest part of the thigh reaches 180 degrees F. Let stand 10 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
A simple preparation is often the best. The herbs and seasonings in this recipe perfectly complement the flavor of roast chicken.
