Title:
Roasted Vegetable Succotash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups chopped Yukon gold potatoes
2 cups chopped sweet potatoes
2 cups prepaired, blended squash
2 TB oil
1 TB minced garlic
3 cups cooked Lima Beans
1 cup canned coconutmilk
1 TB each chopped fresh parsley, thyme, oregano

Directions:
Place potatoes and suash on a baking sheet.  Toss with oil and garlic; salt and pepper to taste.  Bake at 400 for 30 minutes.
Transfer potatotes to a 2-quart baking dish
Stir in remaining ingredients and return to the oven for 15 minutes, or until bubbly.
