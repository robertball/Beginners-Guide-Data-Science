Title:
Southwest Ranch Cheeseburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978353.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
8 KRAFT DELI DELUXE Process American Cheese Slices
4 Kaiser rolls, split, toasted
1/4 cup KRAFT Ranch Dressing
8 slices OSCAR MAYER Bacon, crisply cooked
1 (16 ounce) jar TACO BELL HOME ORIGINALS Thick 'N Chunky Salsa

Directions:
Shape meat into 4 patties.
Cook patties in skillet on medium heat 7 to 9 minutes on each side or until cooked through (160 degrees).
Top each patty with 2 process cheese slices; cover.  Continue cooking until process cheese begins to melt.  Fill rolls with cheeseburgers; top with dressing and bacon.  Serve with salsa.
