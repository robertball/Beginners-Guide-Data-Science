Title:
Snapper or Redfish on the Half Shell Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1/4 cup minced parsley
2 garlic cloves (minced)
Lemon wedges
Pepper
Salt
2 fillets

Directions:
Preheat oven to 475
Put foil in baking pan with high sides and lightly oil
Lay fillets in pan and brush with 2 tablespoons of olive oil
Sprinkle with crushed red pepper and season with salt and pepper
Place in oven for about 20 minutes or until fish flakes with fork
Meanwhile, in small bowl, mix parsley and garlic with remaining 1/4 cup of olive oil and season with salt and pepper.  Transfer fish to plates and drizzle with the parsley-garlic oil and serve with lemon wedges

Description: 
Delicious way to make fish on half shell without having to mess with a grill.
