Title:
Sticky Rice & Mango Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sweet rice
2 cups coconut milk
3/4 cup white sugar, or to taste
2 mangos, peeled and sliced

Directions:
Combine the rice and coconut milk in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce heat to low. Add Sugar. Simmer for 20 to 30 minutes, until mixture is very sticky. Portion the rice into 4 bowls. Top with mango slices, and pour some cream of coconut over the top of each bowl. Add about 1/4 cup of sugar to the coconut milk to pour over the dish.
