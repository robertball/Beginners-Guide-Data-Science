Title:
Green drink with aloe vera juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup aloe vera juice
1 cup of baby spinach
1 cup of baby kale
1 cup of baby chard
1/2 cucumber
1 banana
1/2 cup of old fashioned oats
1/2 cup of blueberries
1 tsp of cinnamon
1 pinch of cayenne pepper
(1 scoop of protein optional)

Directions:
mix oats and aloe vera juice together and let oats soak for 10 minutes. Then mix remaining ingredients all in mixer or blender cup.Blend or mix with electric device approximately 2 minutes or until smooth. Drink and enjoy.
