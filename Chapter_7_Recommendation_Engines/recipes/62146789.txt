Title:
5 Can Vegetarian Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. can yellow corn, drained and rinsed
1 15 oz. can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 15 oz. can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes with green chilies
1 15 oz. can of vegetable broth
1/3 cup Morningstar vegetarian Italian flavor recipe crumbles
1/2 tsp. cumin
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. black pepper or a pinch of cayenne pepper
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients except tortilla chips into a soup pot and heat to a low boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 45 minutes. Serve over broken up tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Heidi Habeck-Johnson
Description: 
This is a recipe I created with items I had in the cupboard and it tastes so good that I make it as a regular meal. The veggie Italian flavor crumbles give it a good flavor.
