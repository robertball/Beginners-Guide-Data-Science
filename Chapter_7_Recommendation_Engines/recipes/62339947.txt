Title:
Overnight Banana Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/390643.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
1 lb loaf of frozen bread dough, thawed
2 ripe bananas, coarsely chopped
2 tablespoons butter

Directions:
The night before:
Grease a loaf pan.
Cut loaf in half, then each half in half, half each piece and then lengthwise for 16 pieces. Shake the sugar and cinnamon together in a large plastic bag add the bread dough, toss in cinnamon-sugar, add bananas to the bag and shake to coat evenly. Pour the mixture into the prepared loaf pan.
Melt the butter in the microwave.  Pour the butter over the bread dough. Allow dough to rise overnight in the refrigerator.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 30 to 40 minutes. Remove from pan immediately. Serve warm.
