Title:
Six-Second Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup apple
2 Tbs. onion
1 cup cooked chicken (leftovers, even lunch meat)
2 Tbs. walnuts
1-2 Tbs. mayonnaise
cinnamon (optional)
nutmeg (optional)
curry powder (optional)
2 slices bread

Directions:
Add the ingredients, except bread, in order to the Short Cup and twist on Cross Blade.
Use the quick Pulse technique to chop and mix the ingredients until you've reached a good consistency.
Spread between two slices of bread and enjoy.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Feel free to add other things. Serve on toast, or add a scoop on top of mixed salad greens. Hollow out the inside of a tomato or pepper and fill it with the mixture. Or skip the walnuts and apple and add a dash of curry powder.
