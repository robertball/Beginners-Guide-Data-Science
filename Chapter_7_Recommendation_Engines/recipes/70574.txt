Title:
5 Minute - Double Layer Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985708.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) READY CRUST® Graham Cracker Pie Crust
1 1/4 cups cold milk
2 (3.9 ounce) packages chocolate flavor instant pudding & pie filling
1 (8 ounce) tub frozen non-dairy whipped topping, thawed, divided

Directions:
Beat milk, pudding and 1/2 of the whipped topping in medium bowl with wire whisk 1 minute. (Mixture will be thick.) Spread in crust.
Spread remaining whipped topping over pudding layer in crust.
Enjoy immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve. Garnish as desired.

Submitter: 
Keebler Ready Crust®
Description: 
This pie can be whipped up in minutes with things you may already have on hand. Perfect for unexpected company, or a quick after dinner treat. There is always room for this light dessert of chocolate and whipped topping.
