Title:
Strawberry Ham Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510749.jpg
Ingredients:
4 oz Cream Cheese
2 tablespoons Strawberry Jam
1/3 cup Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
12 rounds Deli thin sliced Smoked Ham

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Soften cream cheese and mix with strawberry jam.
Press each slice of ham into a mini muffin pan creating a well in the middle (the ham will stick up the sides above the top of muffin pan).
Fill each ham piece with cream cheese mixture and sprinkle top with cheddar cheese.
Bake for 15 minutes.
Let the ham bites rest in muffin pan for at least 5 minutes before removing.
Serve and enjoy!
