Title:
(ellenc) Don't Trifle with My Banana Pudding! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8oz) package cream cheese
1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
1 tablespoon vanilla
2 cups cold milk
2 (3.5 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
4 cups heavy cream, divided, whipped
6 large bananas, 1/4 inch slices
1 (12 ounce) package Keebler's Pecan Sandies, 1/2 coarsely crumbled, the remaining half crushed
1 (12-16 ounce) package prepared pound cake, cubed
4 ounces banana liqueur, in a spritz bottle

Directions:
In a large bowl, whip 2 cups heavy cream until stiff peaks are just about to form. Set aside.
In another large bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Beat in condensed milk, vanilla, cold milk, and pudding mix until smooth. Fold in the whipped cream.
Whip the remaining heavy cream until stiff peaks are just about to form. Set aside.
In a trifle bowl, layer the coarsely crumbled Pecan Sandies cookies. Top with one third of the pudding, one third of the bananas, and half the pound cake cubes; spritz cake with half the liqueur, and top with one third of the whipped cream.
For the next layer, sprinkle one third of the crushed cookies over the whipped cream. Top with half the remaining pudding, half the remaining bananas, and the rest of the pound cake cubes; spritz cake with the remaining liqueur. Top with half the remaining whipped cream.
For the final layer, top with half the remaining cookies, the remainder of the pudding, and the rest of the bananas; finish with the last of the whipped cream and sprinkle with the remaining cookie crumbs.
