Title:
Toffee Bars with Coconut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of yellow cake mix
1/2 cup of butter
2 eggs
1 (14 ounce) can of sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 package of miniature Heath bars
2 cups flaked coconut
1 cup sliced almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9x13 pan.
Combine cake mix, butter and 1 egg. The mixture will be crummy. Press into greased pan.
Chop up Heath bars in a food processor.
In another bowl, mix together condensed milk, vanilla and egg until well blended. Add the almonds, heath bars and 1/2 the coconut. Spread over the crust. Top with remaining coconut.
Bake for 30-35 minutes. Once cool, cut into small squares. The dessert is very rich.

Submitter: 
Windy Lawson
Description: 
Delicious combination of toffee, chocolate, coconut and almonds.
