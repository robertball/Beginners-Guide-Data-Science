Title:
Traditional Julienne Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 Hearts of Romaine Lettuce
2 Tomatoes, Sliced
1/2 of a Large English Cucumber, Sliced
1/2 of a Green Pepper, Seeded and Sliced
1/4 of a small red onion, Sliced
4 Thick Slices of Ham, cut into strips
4 Thick Slices of Turkey, cut into strips
2 Slices Each of Swiss and/or Havarti, cut into strips
1/4 cup grated carrots
2 Hard Boiled Eggs Cut into Quarters

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and toss together with your dressing of choice. Garnish with the quartered eggs.

Submitter: 
Trish McQuhae
Description: 
The traditional Julienne salad that you find at your local restaurant. A delicious and nutritious salad. Serve as a side or a meal.
