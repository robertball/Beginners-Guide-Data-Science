Title:
Blueberry Buckle with Crunchy Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1109536.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1/2 TBSP cinnamon
2 beaten eggs
1/2 cup milk
2 cups blueberries-if frozen thaw completley
Topping:optional
1 cup brown sugar
1 TBSP flour
3 TBSP soft butter
1/2 tsp cinnamon
optional 3/4 cup walnuts

Directions:
Sift together first 4 ingredients in a separate bowl. Then cream shortening with sugar, add cinnamon and eggs and beat until fluffy. Add milk and dry ingredients alternatively to batter. Once mixed completely gently fold in blueberries. Do not beat. Cream topping ingredients together until a crumbly appearance. Spread topping on top of batter.
Pour into a 9x13 greased pan and bake for 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
AuntE
Description: 
A handed down recipe that can be enjoyed at anytime. With coffee in the morning to afternoon dessert with whipped cream or dinner dessert warmed up with ice cream on the top.
