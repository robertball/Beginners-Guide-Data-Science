Title:
Peanut Butter Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T milk
1/4 c blueberries
1 T 1 t chunky peanut butter (can use smooth, if so then use 1 t less)
2/3 c rolled oats
1/8 t vanilla
2 t agave nectar
salt to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly in a microwave-safe bowl.
Microwave for one and a half minutes.
Stir until the cooked blueberries have colored the mixture.
