Title:
Coconut Cranberry Chews Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/311453.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 lb. butter @ room temperature
2 cups sugar
1 Tbl. orange peel
2 Tsp. vanilla
3 1/4 cups flour
1 Tsp. baking powder
1/4 Tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups dried cranberries
1 1/2 cups sweetened flaked dried coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In mixer on medium speed beat together butter, sugar, orange peel, and vanilla
In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt
Gradually add dry ingredients to butter mixture, stir to mix, then beat on low until dough comes together, about 5 minutes.  Dough should be smooth, not crumbly.  Mix in cranberries and coconut.
Shape dough into one inch balls and place two inches apart on buttered 12x15 inch baking sheet
Bake at 350 degrees for 8-11 minutes.  Cool five minutes before removing from pan
