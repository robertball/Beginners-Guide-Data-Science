Title:
Moms potato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 to 3 lb. red potatoes
1/2 lb. bacon
1/2 c. mayonnaise
1/4 c. milk
2 tbsp. Gulden's spicy brown mustard
Garlic powder to taste
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes whole and in their skins. Let them cool for about 2 hours. After they're cooled or chilled, dice them into 1 to 1 1/2 inch chunks. Cook 1.2 pound of bacon and crumble. Mix mayonnaise, milk, mustard, and all spices very well in a container that you can shake and pour from. Add bacon to potatoes in bowl. Pour mayonnaise mixture over the potatoes and mix gently. Chill and enjoy
