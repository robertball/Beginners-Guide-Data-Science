Title:
Broccoli Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 t. butter, melted
1/2 c. onion, chopped
1/4 c. melted butter
1/4 c. flour
2 c. half and half
2 c. chicken broth
1/2 lb. fresh broccoli, steamed
1 c. carrots, julienned & steamed
salt and pepper, to taste
1 t. chicken broth concentrate
1 c. water
1/4 t. nutmeg
8 oz. grated sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
Saute onion in 1 t. butter.  Set aside.  Cook melted butter and flour using a whisk over medium high heat for 3-5 minutes.  Stirring constantly, slowly add the half and half.  Add the chicken broth.  Simmer for 20 minutes.
Add the steamed broccoli, carrots, and onions.  Cook over low heat about 15 minutes.  Add salt, pepper, chicken stock base, and water.  The soup should be thickened by now.  Blend soup together using a stick blender.  Stir in the nutmeg and cheese.
