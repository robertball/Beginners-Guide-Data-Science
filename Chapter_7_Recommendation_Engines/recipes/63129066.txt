Title:
No Bake Chocolate Peanut Butter Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798126.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. Sugar
1/2 c. Milk
1/4 c. Margarine
3 tbsp. Cocoa
2 c. Oatmeal (1 minute instant)
1/2 c. Peanut Butter
1 tsp. Vanilla Extract
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Mix sugar, milk, margarine,and cocoa in saucepan and bring to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring often. Let boil for one minute, then remove from heat.
Stir in oatmeal, peanut butter, and vanilla.
Drop by spoon onto aluminum foil (sprayed with cooking spray) and let stand until firm.

Description: 
My sister and I used to make this with our grandmother when we were younger. They are so simple yet so tasty! You can take them anywhere and count on them disapearing quickly!
