Title:
Corn Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1058471.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. corn meal
1 c. flour
1/8 tsp ground red pepper
2 Tbsp. sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp.salt
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 c. milk
2 Tbsp. melted shortening
1 lb. wieners

Directions:
Mix cornmeal with flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
Add egg and milk; blend in shortening. Mix well. Dip hot dogs in batter.
Fry in deep fat.
