Title:
Easy Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10oz) pkgs. frozen cut corn
16 oz. sour cream
1 T. onion, chopped
2 sticks of butter
2 stacks of Ritz or Towne House crackers
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Thaw frozen corn and drain thoroughly.
Mix corn, sour cream, onion, salt, and pepper.
Melt butter.
Crush crackers.
Mix together butter and crackers.
Mix half of the butter and cracker mixture in with the corn mixture.
Spoon into a greased Pyrex dish.
Cover and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
Remove cover and sprinkle ramaining crumb mixture on top.  Return to oven until lightly brown.

Submitter: 
JennyD
Description: 
This is a wonderful recipe that does not include cornbread.  It's easy to make and always goes fast.
