Title:
Crockpot Roast Beef French Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb boneless roast beef (chuck or round roast)
1 cup vegetable broth
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1 tbs soy sauce
1 tbs Worcestershire sauce
1 tbs brown sugar
4 cloves garlic
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes

Directions:
Place beef in the bottom of the crockpot and add other ingredients.  Cook on low for 7-8 hours.  Shred beef, reduce gravy and spoon out extra fat.  Serve on thick sliced bread or buns.  Freezes well.
