Title:
Limoncello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 lemons
1 liter of vodka
3 cups white sugar
4 cups water

Directions:
Zest the lemons, and place zest into a large glass bottle or jar. Pour in vodka. Cover loosely and let infuse for one week at room temperature.
After one week, combine sugar and water in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil. Do not stir. Boil for 15 minutes. Allow syrup to cool to room temperature.
Stir in vodka mixture to syrup. Stainr into glass bottles and seal each bottle with a cork. Let mixture age for 2 weeks at room temperature.
Place bottled liquer into freezer. Serve chilled.
