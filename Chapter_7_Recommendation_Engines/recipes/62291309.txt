Title:
Slow Cooker Thai Pork with Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c chicken broth
6 T soy sauce
6 T creamy peanut butter
3 T honey
6 cloves garlic, minced or shredded
2 T ginger, minced
1 t crushed red pepper flakes
2 red bell peppers, thinly sliced and cut into bite-sized lengths
1 lb. boneless pork chops or other suitable pork

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a crock pot and cook on low for 5-6 hours, until pork reaches 160 degrees.
Remove pork from sauce, shred it and return it to the sauce until heated through.
Serve over brown or jasmine rice.

Submitter: 
fullerla
Description: 
Pork, peppers and spices are cooked in a Thai peanut sauce until aromatic and tender.  Served over brown or jasmine rice with steamed carrots on the side, this dish makes a satisfying, healthy meal.
