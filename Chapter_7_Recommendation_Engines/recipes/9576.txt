Title:
Chocolate Butter-Creme Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/666139.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 pinch salt
1 1/2 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate, melted
1 1/2 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In small bowl mix at medium speed, sugar, butter (softened),  and salt until light and fluffy.
Add melted chocolate, milk and vanilla extract, beating until well blended.
