Title:
Tonkatsu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless pork cutlets
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup flour
1 egg, beaten
3/4 cup panko (japanese coarse bread crumbs)
oil for frying
Tonkatsu sauce for serving

Directions:
1.  Tenderize meat with mallet; season with salt and pepper to taste.
2.  Heat oil in frying pan, about 1/2 inch deep.
3.  Place egg, flour, and bread crumbs in separate bowls.  Coat each cutlet in flour, then egg, and finally in bread crumbs.
4.  Fry until bread crumbs are golden brown, and pork is cooked through.
5.  Place on paper towels to absorb excess oil.
6.  Serve with tonkatsu sauce and rice.

Submitter: 
AMYSMEANS
Description: 
These breaded and fried pork cutlets are a traditional Japanese dish which is very simple and easy to make.  Serve with sticky rice and enjoy!  Also great if substituted with chicken breasts.
