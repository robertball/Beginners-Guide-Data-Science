Title:
Pork Tenderloin au poivre with goat cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One Pork Tenderloin
1/2 8 ounce log of goat cheese sliced
1/4 cup of peppercorns processed in a blender or food processor
Dijon Mustard

Directions:
Coat tenderloin with dijon mustard, then roll in the crushed peppercorns.  Place on medium high grill until internal temperature is 160 degrees.  Let the tenderloin sit for five minutes, slice in 1/2 inch pieces and top each piece with goat cheese.  Place under broiler for about 2 - 3 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kate Kane Naylor
Description: 
This is a simple, elegant pork tenderloin recipe that my family loves.
