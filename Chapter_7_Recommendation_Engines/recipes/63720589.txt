Title:
Mauigirl's Greek Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1033604.jpg
Ingredients:
3  teaspoons dried oregano
1  teaspoon dried thyme
2  teaspoons dried mint
1  teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
1/2 teaspoon dried minced onion
1/4 teaspoon dried minced garlic

Directions:
Combine oregano, thyme, mint, basil, marjoram, onion, salt and garlic in a small bowl. Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.
