Title:
Baked Rice with Smoked Gouda Cheese and Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow onion, sliced thinly
3 T EVOO
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 t sugar
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 C all-purpose flour
1/2 C milk
14 oz chicken or vegetable broth (1 3/4C)
2 C smoked Gouda, shredded
1 1/2 C partially thawed frozen spinach
3 C brown rice, cooked
Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish
Parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
Heat oil in large, heavy bottomed saucepan over med-low heat. Add onion, sugar and season with salt and pepper. Saute 15-20 min, adding more oil if it begins to stick to the pan. Add garlic and saute until fragrant. Remove from pan and set aside.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease a 9 x 13-in. baking pan.
In same pan the onions were sauteed in, melt butter and whisk in flour. Cook for 2-3 min., whisking. Slowly whisk in milk and chicken broth. Bring to a simmer, whisking often. Stir in Gouda. Add spinach and onion mixture. Turn off heat. Whisk well and break apart any clumps of spinach.
Stir in cooked rice. Pour into baking pan. Bake for 25-30 min until bubbly. Let set for 10 min before serving. Garnish w/parsley and serve w/Parmesan if desired.
