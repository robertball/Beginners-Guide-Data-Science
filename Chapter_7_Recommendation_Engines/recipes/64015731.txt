Title:
Sausage and Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Hillshire Farm smoked sausage
6 cups red new potatoes, cut into 1 inch chunks
1 green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch chunks
1 red bell pepper, cut into 1 inch chunks
1 large onion, sliced
1 tbsp. evoo
seasonings: garlic powder, pepper, sage parsley, rosemary and/or oregano

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Cut sausage into 1 inch slices, set aside.
place potatoes in saucepan; cover with water. Bring to boil, reduce heat, simmer 10 minutes or until crisp-tender. Drain.
Combine sausage, potatoes, peppers, onion, evoo and seasonings in a 12x8 inch baking dish; mix lightly.
Bake for 30 minutes or until potatoes are lightly browned and vegetables are tender.
