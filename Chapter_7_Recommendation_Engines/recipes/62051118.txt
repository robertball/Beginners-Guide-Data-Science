Title:
Grilled Zucchini Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium zucchinis
1/2 tbs onion powder
1/2 tbs seasoning salt
1/2 tbs garlic powder
1 tbs olive oil

Directions:
Thinly slice zucchinis lenthwise Try to get about 1/4 inch wide) Mix in oil and seasonings.  (Adjust seasonings to personal preference) Marinate for about 30 minutes. Grill on medium for about 10 minutes.  Turn over strips.  Grill for about 10 more minutes.  Remove strips when the edges are crisp and grill marks are present.

Submitter: 
Amy Lawler
Description: 
Grilled zucchini that can be used in place of the french fry.
