Title:
Lettuce wrap - easy and simple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/432134.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of rice
1 8oz can of vegetable broth
1 1/2 cups of water
1 chopped garlic
1/4 cup of chopped onion
1 splash of olive oil
Romaine Lettuce leaves - washed and dried
Lime juice or soy sauce - Optional

Directions:
1.   Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat and add rice. Cook, stirring constantly, until  golden. While rice is cooking, stir in onions and garlic, cook until tender.
2.  Stir in vegetable broth; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
3.  Let rice cook for a few minutes. Take a romaine lettuce leaf and scoop some rice on the leaf. Add a hint of lime or soy sauce and you then its ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Sylvia
Description: 
This is an easy and simple recipe that my 4 year old daughter loves. It great lunch for a hot summer day.
