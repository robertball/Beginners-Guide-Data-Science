Title:
Chocolate Power Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Chocolate Chips
1 Cup peanut butter or nut butter
1/2 Cup butter or Earth Balance
1 Cup Sunflower Seed
1 Cup Coconut
1 Cup Wheat Germ
1/2 Cup Sesame Seeds

Directions:
1. In a large bowl, mix the sunflower seeds, coconut, wheat germs and sesame seeds.
2. Melt together chocolate chips, peanut butter and butter.
3. Pour melted ingredients over dry mixture and stir
4. Place in greased cookie sheet approx. 10X16. Press well.
5. Refrigerate for 1 hour, cut into bars.
