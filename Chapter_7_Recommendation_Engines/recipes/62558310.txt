Title:
Really Good (and Easy!) Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon butter
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped celery
3-4 carrots, sliced
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
2 boxes chicken broth
1/2 box vegetable broth
1 Tb. chicken soup base
1/2 pound chopped cooked chicken breast
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 tsp. poultry seasoning
1/4 tsp. dried thyme
pepper to taste
1 1/2 cups egg noodles, cooked

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, melt butter and saute onions, celery and carrots with garlic powder until just tender, 5 minutes.  Pour in chicken and vegetable broths and stir in chicken base, oregano, poultry seasoning salt and pepper.  Bring slowly to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
Meanwhile, cook the noodles in boiling water until halfway done.  Drain the noodles and add to the simmering soup, along with the cut-up chicken breast.  Cook for 30 minutes before serving.
Serve with good bread.
