Title:
Potato Sausage (Karv) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 yards casings (soak in cold water overnight)
2 1/2 lbs ground beef
1/2 lb ground pork
6 lbs potatoes
2 onions
3-4 tbsp salt

Directions:
Grind potatoes and onions and mix well with meat.
Stuff casings, but don't overstuff.
Make each ring about 2 feet.
Poke stuffed casings with needle in multiple places.
Place rings in cold water (2 per large pot), bring to simmer and cook 30 minutes
