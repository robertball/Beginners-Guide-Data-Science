Title:
Red Lotus Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2829562.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger lychee liqueur
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger cranberry juice

Directions:
Fill a Collins glass with ice.  Pour in lychee liqueur, vodka, and cranberry juice.  Stir with a straw and enjoy.

Submitter: 
SIERRAMUM
Description: 
Picked up a bottle of lychee liqueur over the weekend and put together this easy drink recipe.  It's so yummy, even my beer drinking husband had a couple of these!  Try other cranberry juice combinations (i.e. cran-raspberry) for a nice variation.
