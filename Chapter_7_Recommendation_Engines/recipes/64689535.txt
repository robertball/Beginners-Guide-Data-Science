Title:
Basic Marinara for the Instant Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons olive oil
1 medium onion, finely diced. About 1 cup
2 large cloves garlic, microplaned or finely chopped (about 1 tablespoon)
1/4 cup dry red wine
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes
1 28 oz can whole San Marzano tomatoes
3/4 tsp sea salt
fresh ground pepper, to taste
A healthy pinch of red pepper flakes
1 Tablespoon dry basil
1 Tablespoon dry oregano
1 Tablespoon dry parsley
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Press the saute button.  When hot add the oil and onion, cook for 3-5 minutes or until onion is translucent.
Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
Add the wine and reduce by 1/2 the amount.
Add the tomatoes, stir, when bubbling add the herbs.
Add the bay leaf and press Keep Warm.
Place the lid on the pot, set the vent to sealing, press manual, and adjust the time to 10 minutes.
Manually release the pressure when the time has elapsed.
Remove the bay leaf and discard.
Use a stick blender to puree the sauce.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
Tastes like it's been simmering all day, but you'll have a savoury, rich dish on the table in just over an hour.
