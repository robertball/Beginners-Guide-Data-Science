Title:
Marinated Spicy Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. soy sauce
1/4 c. fresh lemon juice
1 Tbsp. brown sugar
1 Tbsp. chili sauce
1/4 tsp. garlic powder (or substitute w/ 1/2 c. minced garlic)
8 center cut pork chops

Directions:
1. In a lg. resealable bag, mix together the soy sauce,lemon juice,brown sugar,chili sauce, and garlic. Place the pork chops into the bag, carefully seal the bag, and marinate for 6-12 hrs. in the refridgerator. Turn the bag over halfway though.
2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
3. Arrange pork chops on a lightly oiled grate, and cook 5-7 min. on each side, until internal temperature reaches 160 degrees F

Submitter: 
Ralph Libby
Description: 
A delicious and easy meal sure to have them asking for more! Serve with some grilled vegatables such as zucchinini, bell pepers, ears of corn, potatoes, portabella mushrooms, etc.

Prepare the marinade in the AM, grill that afternoon!
Enjoy!
