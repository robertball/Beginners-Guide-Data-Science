Title:
Liquid Vampire Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1601747.jpg
Ingredients:
ice cubes, or as needed
6 fluid ounces Cabernet Sauvignon wine
1 splash cranberry juice
1 splash raspberry schnapps

Directions:
Fill a glass with ice cubes. Combine Cabernet Sauvignon wine, cranberry juice, and raspberry schnapps in a shaker. Shake cocktail well and pour over ice.

Submitter: 
Marie Gould StOnge
Description: 
A delicious halloween beverage for adults! Serve with a straw and plastic fangs over straw! I make this every year in a beverage server or punch bowl for my annual Halloween bash, and it always goes over good. Delicious and tastes somewhat like a sangria!
