Title:
Southwestern Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breast, cooked and chopped
3/4 c mayonnaise
1/4 c celery, chopped
cilantro, chopped (to taste)
2 T Southwestern Seasoning (Pampered Chef) OR
Taco Seasoning

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Chill for one hour so that seasoning flavors blend into mayonnaise.  Serve for sandwiches or on lettuce.

Submitter: 
Ms. Munchie
Description: 
A southwestern variation I came up with for a little more zest!
