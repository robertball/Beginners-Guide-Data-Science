Title:
Hamburger & Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds Ground Beef
1 med. onion, chopped
Velveeta Cheese
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 1/2 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste
1 bag Tater Tots

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Brown ground beef and onion, drain. Put into large casserole dish. Add slices of Velveeta cheese, enough to cover ground beef. In bowl mix milk and soup, pour over cheese. Cover with tater tots and bake 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Paula Bellingar Gonzalez
Description: 
This is a child pleaser. Easy to double, or even triple.
