Title:
Classy Wilted Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large bag of greens (any kind/misture you like)
2 tbsp olive oil
2 slices bacon or turkey bacon
1/4 tsp (each) sugar, salt, pepper
1 tbsp (each) cilantro & basil
1 tbsp vinegar

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet on med-high.  Brown bacon until crispy.  Crumble or cut bacon into small pieces.  Add all remaining ingredients to skillet (including bacon).  Sautee until greens have cooked down (approx. 5 mins).
