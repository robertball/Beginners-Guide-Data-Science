Title:
Korean Beef Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tbsp chili garlic sauce
1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
1 tsp brown sugar
1 lb beef tenderloin, sliced into 4 oz filets
salt and pepper to taste
4 oz rice noodles
2 tbsp peanut oil, divided
1 large red onion, thinly sliced
2 cups cabbage kimchee
8 oz sugar snap peas
2 carrots, sliced into ribbons
1 cup beef broth
1 1/2 tsp corn starch
toasted sesame seeds
sliced radishes

Directions:
Season filets to taste with salt and pepper.
Combine soy sauce, garlic sauce, sesame oil, and brown sugar in a medium bowl.  Add beef, toss to coat, and chill 30 minutes.
Prepare noodles according to package directions; drain.
Heat 1 tbsp peanut oil in a wok over high until it shimmers.  Add beef, reserving marinade.  Cook to desired doneness, 3-4 minutes per side for medium rare.  Transfer beef to a plate and let it rest.
Heat remaining peanut oil in wok over high until it shimmers.  Add onion and stir fry 1 minute.  Add kimchee, peas, and carrots; stir fry until heated through (1-2 minutes).
Combine broth, cornstarch, and marinade; add to wok and bring to a boil.  Cook until thickened, 2-3 minutes.  Return beef and juices to wok and heat through (1-2 minutes).
Serve over noodles and garnish with sesame seeds and radish.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
Kimchee gives this stir fry it's distinctive Korean taste.  Spicy beef is complemented by cool radishes and the mild rice noodles adsorb the flavor perfectly.
