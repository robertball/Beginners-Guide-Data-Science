Title:
Dirt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20oz. Oreo cookies-crumble in blender
2 (8oz.) pkgs. cream cheese, room temp
1 stick margarine
1 cup powdered sugar
3 cups milk
2 small pkgs. instant vanilla pudding
12oz. Cool Whip

Directions:
Mix cream cheese with butter and sugar. Mix milk and instant pudding mix, fold in Cool Whip. Mix pudding mixture and cream cheese mixture together. Layer in flower pot cookie crumbs and cream cheese-pudding batter. Make sure you start with crumbs and end with crumbs. Refigerate overnight. Decorate with flowers, worms or whatever. Serve with a trowel.

Submitter: 
Paula Bellingar Gonzalez
