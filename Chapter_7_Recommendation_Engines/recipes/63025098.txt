Title:
Heathy Chicken-Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium carrots, peeled and finely diced
2 stalks celery, diced
1 8oz can water chesnuts, drained and finely diced
3 scallions, thinly sliced
2 Tbsp. fresh ginger, grated or minced
1 large red bell peper, seed and diced
1 pound ground chicken
1/3 cup bottled chinese plum sauce
2 Tbsp. rice vinegar
1 tsp. chili paste
1/4 cup unsalted roasted cashews, chopped
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, minced
1 head Boston or Bibb lettuce

Directions:
Coat skillet w/veg. oil and heat pan to medium heat.Add carrots,celery,water chesnuts,scallions,ginger,garlic and bell pepper.Saute 5 mins.Add ground chicken and cook until no longer pink,using a wooden spoon break chicken into fine pieces.Season with salt and pepper,stir in plum sauce,soy sauce,vinegar and chili paste.Reduce heat to low and simmer until heated through.remove pan from heat and stir in cashews and cilantro.Let mix cool 5 minutes.Fill 12 lettuce leaves with 1/2 cup of mix each. serves 4

Description: 
This Healthy Chicken wrap is full of yummy things; Celery, Carrots, scallions, ginger and chesnuts. It dosen't matter if your on a diet or you just want a healthy way to satisfy your taste buds, you'll sure be pleased.
