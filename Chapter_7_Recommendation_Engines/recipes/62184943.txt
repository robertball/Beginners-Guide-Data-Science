Title:
Wild Rice and Turkey Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 ounce pkg. wild rice mix (like Uncle Ben's) 1 can cream of chicken soup, 3 cups cubed leftover turkey, 1 cup chopped celery, 1/4 cup chopped onion, 1 - 5 ounce can sliced water chestnuts drained, 3 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 cup water, 1 1/2 cups buttered soft bread crumbs.

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions. Stir in soup, add next six ingredients, mix well. Put int a 3 quart casserole, sprinkle bread crumbs on top. Bake 350 degrees for one hour.

Submitter: 
Nadine Wanek
Description: 
Great way to use up leftover turkey, really different casserole good enough for company. Fast and easy.
