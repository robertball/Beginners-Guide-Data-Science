Title:
Heaven in a Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (19.8 ounce) package fudge brownie mix
1 (17.5 ounce) pouch chocolate chip cookie mix
4 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Stir fudge brownie mix, chocolate chip cookie mix, eggs, and butter together in a slow cooker.
Cook on Low for 3 hours.

Submitter: 
cbwilliamson
Description: 
So good! Serve with vanilla ice cream.
