Title:
Slow Cooker Tapioca Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups milk
2/3 cup white sugar
1/2 cup small pearl tapioca
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 tsp vanilla Extract
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinamon

Directions:
Stir together the milk, sugar, tapioca, vanilla, salt  and eggs in a slow cooker.  Cover, and cook on Medium for 3 hours, or on Low for 6 hours, stirring occasionally. lightly sprinkle with cinamon Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Charles Millwood Jr.
Description: 
Classic tapioca pudding is made with very little hassle in a slow cooker. There is no need to presoak small tapioca pearls prior to cooking.Modified from: Slow Cooker Tapioca Pudding
