Title:
Pesto Pasta Caprese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/868239.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz pkg vermicelli
1 tsp chicken bouillon
3 tbsp prepared pesto (commercial or homemade)
3-4 med. tomatoes, cubed
1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped
1-1/2 cups fresh mozzarella, cubed
1 can ripe olives, med., drained
1 small jar marinated artichokes, quartered and drained
1/2 tbsp Italian seasoning
Sea salt and Fresh ground pepper to taste
4-5 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
1/2 cup bread crumbs
tsp minced garlic
Extra virgin olive oil
2 tbsp Parmigiano reggiano, grated (for garnish)

Directions:
1. Bring water to rolling boil and add salt, bouillon and pasta; boil 5 min until just undercooked.
2. In large pan, saute sliced garlic in 1/4 cup olive oil over medium heat until slices begin to turn translucent. Add cooked pasta to pan and toss with garlic oil; saute over low-medium heat for 3 minutes.
3. In a separate bowl, combine tomatoes, basil, mozzarella, olives, artichoke, Italian seasoning, salt and pepper and 1/4 cup olive oil; toss until well mixed.
4. Remove pasta from heat and toss with pesto until evenly covered; add tomato-mozzarella mixture and toss.
5. In small pan, saute 2 tbsp. olive oil and minced garlic; add breadcrumbs and stir until browned.
6. Plate pasta dish and garnish with breadcrumbs, Parmesan and fresh basil. Serve.

Submitter: 
Terri
Description: 
This may well be my new favorite meal! Great summer dish with fresh tomatoes and herbs. Based on a Michael Symon recipe from The Chew, but embellished a bit. Toasted bread crumbs add the perfect little crunch. Tasty, easy and quick.
