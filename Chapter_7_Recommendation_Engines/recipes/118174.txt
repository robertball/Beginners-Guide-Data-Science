Title:
Roasted Asparagus With Thyme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985659.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds fresh asparagus, trimmed
3 tablespoons olive oil
2 teaspoons minced fresh thyme
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Place asparagus in a roasting pan or baking pan lined with heavy-duty foil. Drizzle with oil and toss to coat. Sprinkle with the thyme, salt and pepper. Bake, uncovered, at 425 degrees F for 10-15 minutes or until crisp-tender.

Sharon  Leno
