Title:
Chicken Fiesta Soup ll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless chicken breasts (I use frozen)
14 1/2 oz can diced tomatoes w/green chili's
4 oz chopped green chili's
28 oz green enchilada sauce
14 1/2 oz can chicken broth
1 c. chopped onion (I prefer sweet)
2 tsp. cumin
1 tsp chili powder
2 tsp chopped garlic
salt/pepper to taste
1 can corn, drained

Directions:
Put all ingredients, except corn, in a crockpot, cook on low for 7 hours.  Remove breasts, shred  with 2 forks. Return to crockpot, add corn and continue cooking for 1 hour more.
To Serve: sprinkle with cheese, crumbled tortilla chips and a dollop of sour cream.

Submitter: 
Cathy Brown-Bergmann

Better than anything we've tried in a restaurant !  Awesome flavor and very satisfying.  To serve: Sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese, crumbled tortilla chips and a dollop of sour cream.  Enjoy !
