Title:
Achiote Citrus Marinated Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. freshly squeezed orange juice
1/4 c. achiote paste (found in Mexican markets or on the Mexican eisle of a well stocked grocery store)
2 to 3 jalapeno peppers, roasted
6 cloves garlic, roasted
1 Tbsp. black peppercorns
1 Tbsp. salt
1 bunch cilantro, stems and leaves
3 to 4 lbs. shrimp, peeled and deveined
wooden skewers, soaked in water for 1 hour (if desired)**

Directions:
Peel the roasted garlic and remove stems from roaster jalapenos.
Combine 1/2 c. of the orange juice, the achiote paste, garlic, jalapenos, peppercorns and salt in the jar of a blender or in bowl of a food processor. Puree until the peppercorns are completely crushed.
Add the cilantro and remaining orange juice and puree until smooth.
Pour this marinade over the shrimp in a glass pan or in a resealable plastic (Ziplock) bag. Put in the refrigerator and allow to marinate for 1 to 2 hours.
Remove shrimp from marinade. Using a prepared grill or a cast iron grill pan, cook shrimp for 3 to 4 minutes on one side; turn and continue to cook for another 3 to 4 minutes on the other side.
Coarsely chop the shrimp and use as taco filling.
