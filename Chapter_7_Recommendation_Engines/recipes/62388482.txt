Title:
Best Ever Texas Caviar Black Eyed Pea Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can field peas with snaps
1 can black-eyed peas with jalapenos
1 small can chopped black olives
2 avocados
2 cans Ro-Tel, 1 drained
chopped bell pepper (red and green; optional)
chopped onion (
1 small bottle Zesty Italian dressing
chopped jalapenos to taste

Directions:
Drain all cans except one Ro-Tel.  Mix all Together.  Chill 1 hour.  Chop avocados into bite size chunks, add just before serving.  Serve with Frito's Scoops.  Enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
Angela Naumann
Description: 
Always Requested and a family favorite, this zesty Texas dip has the ability to kick a party up a notch.  Serve with Frito's Scoops or other favorite corn chips.
