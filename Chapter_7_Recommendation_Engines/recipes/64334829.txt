Title:
Mom's Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs chicken skinless
1/3 cup butter or margarine
1/3 cup honey
1/4 cup dijon mustard
4 tsp curry
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1 onion
2 cup carrots
1 cup parsnips
2 cups frozen peas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
place chicken in roaster
mix together and heat the butter, honey, mustard, curry and cayenne.
Pour over chicken
Add onions, carrots and parsnips
cook for 45 minutes
