Title:
Natalie's Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound pork sausage
9 eggs
1 cup milk
3/4 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups Sharp cheddar cheese (shredded)
1 1/2 cups garlic and onion croutons

Directions:
Heat oven to 325 degrees.
Cook and stir sausage over medium heat until light brown; drain. Place sausage in greased baking dish (whatever size, depending on how thick you want it)
Beat eggs, milk and salt; stir in cheese.
Pour egg mixture on sausage; sprinkle with crutons.
Bake uncovered until knife inserted in center comes out clean, about 45-50 minutes.
Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
