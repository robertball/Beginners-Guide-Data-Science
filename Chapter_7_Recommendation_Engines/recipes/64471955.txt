Title:
Winter Vegetable Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chopped medium onion
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 T olive oil
1 14.5-oz can diced tomatoes (and juices)
1 small butternut squash (peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces)
3 chopped medium carrots
1 15-oz can navy beans (rinsed)
1 3-inch piece Parmesan rind (optional)
1/2 cup small pasta shells
1/4 cup chopped Savoy cabbage

Directions:
Cook onion, salt, and pepper in oil in large pot over medium heat, stirring frequently, until onion is soft, 8-10 minutes.
Add tomatoes and simmer until thickened, 6-8 minutes.
Add squash, carrots, beans, Parmesan rind (optional), and 6 cups water.
Simmer until vegetables are tender, 20-25 minutes.
Add pasta shells and cabbage.
Simmer until pasta is tender.
Serve topped with Parmesan and crusty bread on the side.
