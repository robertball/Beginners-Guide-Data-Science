Title:
Fat Free Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 angel food cakes
2 (small) boxes vanilla FF-SF instant pudding
3 cups skim milk
8 oz sour cream
1 large can crush pineapple
1 qt strawberries (16oz frozen, defrosted)
cool whip

Directions:
Break cake up & pour pineapple juice over
Mix pudding with milk, then add sour cream & fruit. Layer in bowl with cake.
Top with cool whip
