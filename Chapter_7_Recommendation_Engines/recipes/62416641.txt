Title:
Broccoli with Roasted Red Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups broccoli florets
1 clove garlic, minced
Couple of splashes of olive oil
1/4 cup diced roasted red pepper
1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
couple of squeezes of lime juice

Directions:
Place broccoli in a steamer basket. Place in a saucepan over 1 in. of water; bring to a boil. Cover and steam for 5-8 minutes or until crisp-tender.
Meanwhile, in a nonstick skillet, saute garlic in olive oil. stir in the red peppers, parsley and lime juice. Transfer broccoli to a large bowl; add red pepper mixture and toss to coat.
