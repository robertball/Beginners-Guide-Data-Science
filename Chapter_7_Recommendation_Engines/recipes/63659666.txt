Title:
Honey Mustard Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1472400.jpg
Ingredients:
8	chicken thighs
6	tablespoons Dijon mustard or whole grain mustard
3	tablespoons honey
3 	tablespoons of Agave nectar
1	teaspoon plus 1/8 teaspoon Sriracha hot sauce

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
Mix mustard, honey, Agave nectar and sriracha; reserving 4 to 5 tablespoons.
Lay chicken thighs in a single layer in greased 8x12‚Äù baking dish, sprayed with cooking spray.   Pour remaining mustard mixture over chicken.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until cooked through.  Serve with reserved sauce drizzled over chicken.
