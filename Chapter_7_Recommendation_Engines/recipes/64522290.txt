Title:
Perfect Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3036752.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vegan buttery spread
2 cups turbinado sugar
1/2 cup apple sauce
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 cups oatmeal
Chocolate chips to taste

Directions:
Mix the buttery spread, sugar, applesauce and vanilla together until smooth.
Then, add the flour, baking soda, baking powder and oatmeal and mix.
Add dark chocolate chips to taste.
Scoop dollops of dough onto a baking sheet, about 2 inches apart. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Cool before eating. Makes about 24 cookies.

Submitter: 
Heather
Description: 
These are my mom's chocolate chip cookies made even better. They are chewy, addictive and cruelty-free.
