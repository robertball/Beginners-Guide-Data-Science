Title:
Sweet Potato and Fennel Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped onion
2 Tbl olive oil
1 medium bulb fennel (about 1 1/2 cups)
1 large sweet potato (about 3 cups)
1 clove garlic, pressed
1/2 tsp powdered sage (original cumin- mix?)
3 cups stock
1-2 Tbls. cider vinegar (forgot to try)

Directions:
Saute onion and fennel in large stockpot until onion is translucent.
Add chopped sweet potato for a few minutes.
Add garlic through stock, and cook until potatoes are soft, about 30 minutes.
Puree and stir in cider vinegar.
