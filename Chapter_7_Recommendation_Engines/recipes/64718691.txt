Title:
Chipotle chicken by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5846092.jpg
Ingredients:
2  boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs ( or combination of the two)
2 tablespoons of Olive oil
1 tbls lemon juice
1 tbsp lime juice
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1 tbsp chili powder
1 tbsp cumin
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1 tsp minced garlic
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped peppers
1 tbsp jalapeño or Serrano peppers diced
Add during cooking:
2tbsp olive oil
1tsp smoked or plain paprika

Directions:
And chicken and vegetables  to ziploc bag.
Combine oil, juices and seasoning, mix well and pour over chicken and veggies to coat.
Refrigerate 2 hours, turning occasionally
Preheat cast iron skillet with oil, when hot add chicken mixture., cook on med high until done.  Stirring often. Add paprika during cooking.
Numerous serving options:
Serve over or with cilantro rice like a baked burrito bowl.
Add to soft or hard shells, top a taco.
Over tortilla chips with cheese, onions, olives etc.
I made fiesta corn (frozen corn with peppers, onions, garlic and cumin over high heat) and heated a can of Busch’s Black Bean Fiesta seasoned bean blend.  Added everything to a bowl of lettuce and topped with Taco Bell Salsa Verde.
