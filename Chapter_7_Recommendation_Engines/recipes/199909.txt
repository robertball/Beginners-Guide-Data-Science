Title:
Island Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3551443.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice
2 cups pineapple juice
1/2 cup ginger ale
3 tablespoons coconut milk
1 tablespoon white sugar

Directions:
Combine the ice, pineapple juice, ginger ale, coconut milk, and sugar in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Colleen
Description: 
This cool and refreshing smoothie will make you feel like you're strolling along the beaches in the Caribbean.
