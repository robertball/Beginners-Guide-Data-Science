Title:
Pina Colada Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg. can unsweetened pineapple juice
1 - 12oz can frozen pink lemonade
1 - 15oz can cream de coconut
1 fifth of cheap light rum
7-up OR Sprite OR Fresca

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except the soda pop.
Freeze.
To serve - filla  glass with half slush and half soda pop.

Description: 
An alcholic beverage sure to cool you down on a hot summer day.
