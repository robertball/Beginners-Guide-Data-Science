Title:
Nutty Monkey Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/684096.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups quick oats
2 cups sugar
1 3/4 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
3 to 4 very ripe bananas, mashed (about 2 cups)
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup melted butter
2 eggs
1 cup coarsely chopped peanuts
1 cup milk chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9 x 13 inch baking pan.
In a large bowl, combine oats, sugar, flour, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
In a separate large bowl, mash the bananas and stir in the peanut butter and melted butter, mixing well. Add the eggs and peanuts and stir.
Pour wet ingredients into dry, add chocolate chips, and mix until combined.
Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 40 to 50 minutes or until top springs back when gently pressed.
Allow to cool completely before cutting.

Submitter: 
Alberta Rose
Description: 
This is the peanut butter and banana version of my Chunky Monkey Brownies. Another great use for those ever present over ripe bananas.
