Title:
Apple Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup steel oats
4 cups water
1/2 cup milk
1 large apple chopped
1 T cinnamon
1/2 t nutmeg
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 t salt
1/2 t vanilla
1 T butter

Directions:
Layer ingredients in slow cooker. Stir. Cook on low for 8-10 hours.
