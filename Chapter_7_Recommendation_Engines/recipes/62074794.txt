Title:
Open Face Veggie Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices marbled rye bread or whole wheat bread
2 tablespoons olive oil
cream cheese
poupon mustard
2 red peppers cut into strips
1 thinly sliced red onion
2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
pepper jack cheese

Directions:
Place oil in wok or frying pan.  Saute vegetables until soft.  Place toasted bread on cookie sheet and spread with cream cheese and mustard. Pile equal amounts of cooked veggies on top of each piece of toast. Slice pepper jack cheese and place on top of veggies.  Place under broiler until cheese is melted.  Serve and Enjoy!

Submitter: 
CAROL46
Description: 
Open faced sandwich topped with Pepper Jack cheese.  Excellent served with a cup of soup.
