Title:
Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/810374.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package of Egg Noodles
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
2 cups shredded Sargento 4 Cheese Italian cheese
2 cups milk
2 (12 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked
1 cup mayonnaise*
1 (10 3/4 ounce) can of Sweet Peas
1 cup Bread Crumbs

Directions:
1) Cook noodles according to package directions.
2)Meanwhile, in a saucepan, combine the soup, cheese and milk. Cook and stir over med-low heat until cheese is melted.
3)Drain noodles and place in a large bowl.
4) Add the tuna, mayonnaise and peas to soup mixture.
5)Mix soup mixture with noodles.
6)Pour into a greased 3 qt baking dish.
7)Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 15 minutes.
8)Take out and sprinkle bread crumbs all over the top of the casserole.
9)Bake another 10 minutes.
10)Let cool for 5-10 minutes and ENJOY!
