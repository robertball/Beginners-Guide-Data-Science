Title:
Noodles with Thai Peanut Curry Sauce and Broccolini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1263641.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz pad Thai noodles
1 bunch broccolini, chopped on diagonal to desired size
1 can light coconut milk
1/2 c peanut butter (used cashew and peanut)
1/4 c red Thai curry paste
4-5 cloves garlic
Small chunk ginger
2-4 T soy sauce or Tamari
1 T fish sauce
2-4 T apple cider vinegar
lime juice from approx 1/2 lime
2-4 T chopped peanuts or cashews
handful Thai basil (or cilantro or basil)
To serve: Thai basil, cilantro, green onion, peanuts, lime

Directions:
prepare noodles
blanch broccolini for 3-4 min and submerge in ice water
combine all sauce ingredients except chopped peanuts, Thai basil, and lime juice in blender and blend until desired consistency (smooth)
put sauce in sauce pan over low heat and lightly simmer
add peanuts/cashews
add thai basil
add broccolini to sauce
add lime juice more soy or other to taste
add noodles to sauce
adjust seasonings
serve with Thai basil, cilantro, green onion, peanuts, additional soy
