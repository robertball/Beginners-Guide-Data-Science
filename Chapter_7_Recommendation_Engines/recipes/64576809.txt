Title:
Caramel Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
3 tbsp. cream or milk
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 1/2 cups icing sugar

Directions:
Heat brown sugar, cream and butter in a med. saucepan until it boils.
Boil x 2 minutes. Remove from heat. Cool.
Add icing sugar. Beat until smooth. Add more milk or icing sugar until of desired consistency.
Makes about 1 1/3 cup (enough for 8" or 9" square cake).

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Delicious butterscotch/caramel flavored cooked frosting - I'm sure you can find many applications for this!
