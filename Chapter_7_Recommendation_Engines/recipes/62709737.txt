Title:
Spicy Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 Onion - chopped
2 cloves Garlic - chopped
1 large Sweet Potato - peeled & cut into small cubes
1 tsp Cumin Seeds
1½ tsp Paprika
¼ to ½ tsp or to taste Crushed Chilles or Chilli Flakes
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a frying pan and fry the onion on low medium heat for about 5 minutes or so or until softened;
Add the garlic and fry for another minute or so;
Add the sweet potato, spices, salt and pepper and fry for 10-12 minutes, stirring frequently, or until the sweet potato is tender and golden-brown

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A piquant, spicy, packed with flavour side dish to accompany any main or use as an appetiser. Great with salads and fit for any occasion.
