Title:
Fresh Picante Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695863.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large tomatoes - quartered
1 medium jalapeno - seeded based on desired heat
1 medium tomatillo - husk removed
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
2 sprigs of fresh cilantro
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Fire roast tomatillo on grill/broiler using high heat until skin sears about 5 minutes.
Place all ingredients in food processor and blend until pulpy.

The essence of salsa blended into a versatile sauce used for topping off a meal or cooking.
