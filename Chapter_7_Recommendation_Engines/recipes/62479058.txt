Title:
cranberry turkey breast crock pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 lb boneless turkey either breast or rolled turkey white and dark meat
1 can cranberries, either jellied or whole
1 envelope dry onion soup mix
1/2 cup orange juice

Directions:
Mix the onion soup mix, the cranberries, and the orange juice together.
Spray the sides and bottom of the crock pot with oil or use a liner
Put the turkey in the crock pot (3 qt) and cover with the mixed sauce.

Submitter: 
Verna Schroter
Description: 
Got the recipe from my retired neighbor and the taste is wonderful  The 2nd day left-overs can be layered with mashed potatoes/whatever, turkey slices, any cooked veggie with some of the remaining sauce on top and re-heated.  Great casserole.
