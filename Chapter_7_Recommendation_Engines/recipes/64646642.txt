Title:
Crockpot Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4529258.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef, browned
1 pound Italian sausage, browned
1 bag of frozen cheese ravioli (25 oz)
1 28 oz can of petite diced tomatoes
1 10 oz can Rotel Tomato & Green Chilies
1 12 oz can tomato sauce
1 6 oz can tomato paste
1 egg
1 12 oz container ricotta cheese
1 tablespoon salt
1 tablespoon black pepper
1/2 tablespoon oregano
1/2 tablespoon thyme
1 tablespoon of Italian seasoning
1 tablespoon of garlic powder
1 tablespoon onion powder
4 cups of mozzarella cheese
1 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large stock pot, brown the beef  & Italian sausage completely and drain off any remaining fat.
Stir in tomatoes, Rotel, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and seasonings.
In a separate bowl, mix egg and ricotta cheese.
In a 6 quart crock pot, cover the bottom with some of the sauce.
Then place a layer of ravioli across the bottom.
Place layer of ricotta cheese mixture.
Then place a layer of the mozzarella cheese & parmesan cheese.
Continue layering until all the meat sauce and the ravioli is used.
Top with sauce and mozzarella cheese.
Cook on low for 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Kay Gooding
Description: 
Cooking lasagna in the crockpot is easy with not much prep time.
