Spanish Style Tuna Tapas Recipe

2 Tins Tuna in Olive Oil - mashed and retained in oil
Â½ tsp Garlic (or to taste) - finely chopped
1oz Onion (or to taste) - finely chopped
1oz Green Pepper (or to taste) - finely chopped
1 oz Red Pepper (or to taste) - finely chopped
Optional - touch finely chopped red/green chilli - to taste re heat
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
Crusty Bread

Directions:
Put the tuna and oil in a small bowl, add the garlic, onion, peppers and, if using, chilli, season with salt and pepper and combine thoroughly.
Serve with crusty bread.
