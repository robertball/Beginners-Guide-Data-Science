Title:
Grilled Pastrami Style Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. dark brown sugar
1 tsp. kosher salt
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. ground ginger
1 tsp. ground coriander
1 tsp. pepper
1/4 to 1/2 tsp. allspice
1 1 1/2 lb. center cut salmon fillet
1/2 tsp. olive oil

Directions:
Combine first 7 ingredients. Place salmon skin side down and brush evenly with olive oil.  Sprinkle spice mixture evenly over salmon and gently rub into fish.  Cover lightly with plastic wrap and chill for 15 minutes.
Grill for 10 minutes or until fish flakes evenly with a fork.
