Title:
Quiche-y Brunch Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups milk/cream (whatever you have)
4 eggs
3/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon each of baking powder and mustard powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
4-5 challots, caramelized adn finished with balsamic vinegar
6 stalks asparagus, cooked and chopped
8 ounces shredded Tex mex cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 10 inch quiche dish.
In a large bowl, beat together milk, eggs, flour baking soda, mustard powder, salt, butter and parmesan cheese. Batter will be lumpy. Stir in asparagus, shallots and Cheddar cheese. Pour into prepared quiche dish. finish with sliced tomato.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, until eggs are set and top is golden brown.
