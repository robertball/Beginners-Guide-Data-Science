Title:
Cold Brew Iced Coffee Concentrate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups ground coffee.
- Water

Directions:
Place 1 1/2 cups of ground coffee into a 64oz glass container (with lid)
Fill the container with water.  Make sure all of the grounds are wet and fill to 64oz mark.
Place lid tightly on the container and shake several times.
Place the container in a dry dark location and wait three days.
Place a funnel into an empty storage container that can hold at least 48oz.  Next place a coffee filter on top of the funnel.
Slowly pour the coffee concentrate through the filter, filling the empty storage container.
You will now have approximately 48oz of cold brew iced coffee concentrate that can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
Making a glass of iced coffee use 4oz of cold brew iced coffee concentrate.
Add 4oz of water.
Add cream, sugar, or syrups. (optional)
