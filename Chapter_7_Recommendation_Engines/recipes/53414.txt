Title:
Beef and Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979213.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
3/4 pound thin beef strips
2 cups fresh broccoli florets
1 (.87 ounce) package McCormick® Brown Gravy Mix
1 cup water
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Cracked Black Pepper

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add beef strips; saute 3-4 minutes; add in broccoli.
Combine Gravy Mix, water and black pepper. Pour over beef mixture. Stir and bring to a boil. Cover and simmer 5-8 minutes or until broccoli is tender.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Gourmet Collection®
Description: 
A quick dinner idea! Beef and Broccoli is delicious over rice or oriental noodles. Use 1 (16 ounce) bag frozen broccoli instead of fresh broccoli if you prefer.
