Title:
Apple Breakfast Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C packed brown sugar (I used Splenda)
1/4 t cinnamon
1/3 C water
2 med. apples, peeled, thinly sliced
2 T butter or margarine
1/2 C biscuit mix
2 eggs

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees. Generously grease 9-in glass pie plate w/cooking spray.
In medium bowl, mix sugar and cinnamon. Add apples; toss to coat. Set aside.
In 2Qt saucepan, heat water and butter to boiling. Reduce heat to low. Add biscuit mix; stir vigorously until mix forms a ball. Remove from heat. Beat in eggs, one at a time; continue beating until smooth.
Spread batter in bottom of pan. Arrange apples on top to within 1 inch of edge of pan.
Bake about 23 min. or until puffed and edges are golden brown. Serve immed. Can drizzle w/syrup if desired.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Made this w/apples and it was very good. Think it might be good w/berries, peaches, plums, any type of fruit
