Title:
Chipotle Salsa - Version 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/964053.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large white onion
1 red pepper
1 jalapenos – seed them to remove the “heat” (I like mine HOT)
1 bunch of cilantro (yes the whole bunch)
4 cloves of garlic
1 or 2 canned chipotle peppers
2 tsp liquid smoke
1-2 T white vinegar
2-3 T lime juice
salt & pepper to taste
2 small cans/1 lrg can diced tomatoes – drained

Directions:
1. Add first 5 ingredients to food processor and pulse to make large chunks
2. Add rest of ingredients and pulse until desired consistancy is reached.
