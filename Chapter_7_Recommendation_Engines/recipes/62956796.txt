Title:
Curried Lentils With Kale And Sticky Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/728717.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups sticky brown rice, prepared
16oz (2 1/2 cups) dried lentils, sorted, rinsed
8 cups water
2 medium carrots, cut into 1/2-inch slices (1 cup)
2 medium stalks celery, cut into 1/2-inch slices (1 cup)
1 medium onion, chopped (1/2 cup)
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 extra-large vegetarian vegetable bouillon cubes
3 teaspoons curry powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 bay leaves
1 (14.5-oz.) can diced tomatoes, undrained
2 cups coarsely chopped fresh kale

Directions:
In 3 1/2 or 4-quart slow cooker, combine all ingredients except tomatoes and kale; mix well.
Cover; cook on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.
About 5 minutes before serving, stir tomatoes into soup. Cover; cook an additional 5 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
Just before serving, remove and discard bay leaves. Place kale into individual bowls, followed by the lentils, and then top it off with rice.
