Title:
Coconut Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz can crushed pineapple
3/4 c milk
1/2 c sour cream
1 pkg coconut cream pudding mix
fresh fruit

Directions:
In a blender combine the 1st four ingredients, cover & blend for 1 min till smooth. Store in the fridge.
