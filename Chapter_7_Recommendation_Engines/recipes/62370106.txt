Title:
Roadside Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
3 c. half and half
1 t. salt
2 pkg. frozen hash browns (12 oz. each)
Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Heat butter, half and half, and salt in large sauce pan.  Add hash browns and stir until thawed and thick.  Transfer to a casserole, cover generously with parmesan cheese.  Bake in oven for about 1 hour at 325 degrees, until the top browned and bubbly.
