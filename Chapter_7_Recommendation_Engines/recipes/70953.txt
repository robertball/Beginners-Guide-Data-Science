Title:
Country Scallop Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (6.5 ounce) package RICE-A-RONI® Broccoli Au Gratin
2 tablespoons margarine or butter
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 cups water
1 (7 ounce) package fully cooked sausage links, cut into slices
1 cup frozen whole kernel corn
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
Oyster crackers (optional)

Directions:
In large skillet over medium heat, saute rice-pasta mix, margarine or butter and onion until pasta is light golden brown, stirring frequently.
Slowly stir in water and seasonings; bring to a boil.  Cover; reduce heat to low.  Simmer 10 minutes.
Stir in sausage, corn, and bell pepper; return to a simmer.  Cover; simmer 5 minutes or until rice is tender.  Stir; let stand 5 minutes.  Top with oyster crackers, if desired.

Submitter: 
RICE-A-RONI®
Description: 
Sausage corn and peppers accent this seasoned rice dish for a down home dinner that won't keep you in the kitchen all night.
