Title:
Jamaican Rice and Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15oz can large red Kidney Beans
2 cloves of garlic crushed
1 14 oz can of unsweetened coconut milk
1 cup water
1 cups uncooked rice
2 stalks scallion chopped
1 tsp dried Thyme
1 tsp adobo

Directions:
Add water to the coconut milk to make 2 1/4 cups. Place beans, garlic, scallion, coconut water, rice and seasonings in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat to a simmer and cook for 17 minutes then take off the heat and let it sit covered for 10 minutes until all of the water has been absorbed. Remove cover, fluff with a fork and enjoy.
