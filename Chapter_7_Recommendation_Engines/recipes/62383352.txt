Title:
Tina's Chicken Garlic Vinegrette Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken breast tenderloins
6 cups of green salad mix
1 lb.slicedStrawberries
1 chopped Mango
1/5 chopped Red Onion
1 sliced Avacado
1 peach chopped
1 red pepper chopped
croutons if desired
Dressing:
Seasoned Vinegar
Olive Oil
3 cloves of Minced garlic

Directions:
1.Season chicken with grill seasing and grill until 165 degrees
2. mix all fruits, veggies, and lettuce together
3.  Using a clear liquied measuring cup, fill cup with 1/4 cup vinegar, pour olive oil on top of vinegar until level reaches 1/3 cup, then add garlic and mix until blended well.
4.  Pour dressing over salad and toss until dressing coats salad well.  Add chicken and croutons if desired.

Submitter: 
Wendy Dixon
Description: 
This salad is so delicious with fresh ingredients and a wonderful homemade dressing.
