Title:
Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (30 ounce) package frozen Southern hash brown potatoes
2 cups shredded colby & monterey jack cheese
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 small onion, chopped
1 cup butter
3 cups crushed corn flakes
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
In a large bowl, combine the hashbrowns, cheese, sour cream, onion and soup.
Spread mixture in 9x13 casserole dish.
Mix cornflakes with butter, garlic powder and salt and evenly distribute over potato mixture.
Cover with foil.
Bake at 425 degrees F (220 degrees C) for 1 hour.
Take off foil and bake for another 20 mins or until cornflakes become golden brown.
