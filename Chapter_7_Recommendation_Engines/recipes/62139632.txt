Title:
Cinnamon Pecan Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. Butter
1/2 c. Shortening
3/4 c. Brown Sugar
3/4 c. White Sugar
2 Eggs
2 t. Vanilla
2 c. Flour
1 t. Baking Soda
1 t. Salt
2 1/2 c. Quick Oats
1 1/2 c. Cinnamon Chips
1 c. Chopped Pecans

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Cream together butter, shortening, sugars, eggs and vanilla.
3. Combine flour, soda, and salt, and add to the creamed ingredients. Mix well.
4. Mix in the oats.
5. Stir in the Cinnamon Chips and Pecans.
6. Drop by rounded spoonfuls about 2 inches apart on cookie sheet sprayed with nonstick spray or lined with parchment paper.
7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until cookies are set. They may not brown much, Do not overbake.
8. Cool on pan for 2-3 minutes before trying to remove them.

Submitter: 
Sarah Travis Stacey
Description: 
A soft oatmeal cookie with cinnamon chips and toasted pecans.
