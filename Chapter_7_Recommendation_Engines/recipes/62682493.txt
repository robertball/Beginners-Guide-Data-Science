Title:
Miso-Shure style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Make dashi
6 C water
2-3" square piece of kombu (seaweed)
3T bonito flakes
Make miso soup
dashi
2T soy bean paste
garlic
ginger
tofu (4oz)
diced green onion
sliced mushrooms

Directions:
Bring 6 C water to a boil.
Wash ~2-3" square piece of kombu under cold water tap.
Add kombu to water once it comes to a boil.
Stir and let boil 3 minutes.
Remove kombu from the water and add 3T bonito flakes.
Allow to come to boil again, then remove from heat immediately.
Let it settle for a few minutes.
Strain off the bonito (I pour through a fine mesh strainer linedwith cheesecloth) and set aside.
Make miso soup
Bring the dashi to a boil.
Mix a little hot liquid with 2T soy bean paste, stirring untilsmooth.
Pour mixture back into the saucepan, stir well.
Add tofu (4oz) and return to boil.
Simmer for a few seconds only.
Ladle into bowls and garnish with diced green onion and sliced mushrooms (I usually pan fry mushrooms first).
