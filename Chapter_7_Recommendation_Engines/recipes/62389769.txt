Title:
Apple fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C flour
1 t baking powder
3 T sugar
1/4 t salt
1 egg well beaten
1/2 C milk
4 tart apples (ex. granny smith)

Directions:
Sift flour, baking powder, sugar and salt into medium bowl.
Beat egg and milk, add to flour mixture.
Core, peel and cut up the 4 apples into small wedges. Dip one by one into batter.
Fry in deep oil at 375Â° for about 2 minutes per side.
Drain on paper towel before serving.

Description: 
A breakfast treat handed down from my grandmother.
