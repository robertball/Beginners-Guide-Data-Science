Title:
Mom's Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 potatoes
10 eggs
3/4 cup onion
sweet relish
salt and pepper
1 tsp. mustard
lots of mayo
can black olives

Directions:
Boil potatoes and eggs.  Cut potatoes into bite size pieces.  Chop eggs.  Chop onions.  Add eggs and onions to potatoes.  Add relish, salt and pepper, mustard, mayo and black olives.  Mix well and refrigerate for an hour or more.

Submitter: 
Kelly Norton
Description: 
This is our favorite potato salad recipe from Grandma Bingham!  It is not exact but oh, so good!
