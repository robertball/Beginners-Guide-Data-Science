Title:
Shrimp Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped
1/4 cup (60 ml) vegetable oil
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
2 tsp fresh ginger, finely grated
4 tomatoes, seeded and diced
1 can (14 oz/398 ml) coconut milk
1 1/2 tsp garam masala
1 green or red chili pepper, chopped (optional)
1 tsp salt
• 2.2 lb (1 kg) fresh shrimp, shelled and deveined
• Juice of 1 lime
• Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish

Directions:
1. In a skillet, brown the onion in the oil. Add the garlic and ginger. Cook for 1 minute. Add the tomatoes, coconut milk, garam masala, pepper and salt. Simmer over low heat for 10 minutes.
2. Add the shrimp. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat and simmer for 3 to 5 minutes. Add the lime juice and sprinkle with cilantro leaves. Adjust the seasoning and serve.
