Title:
Cook'n Wishbone-Homemade Ice Cream in a Coffee Can Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 cup whipping cream
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
ice  cream salt (rock salt) 1 large 3 lb and 1 small 1 lb  coffee can with plastic lid

Directions:
freeze both cans overnight.
mix the milk, cream, and sugar together  in the small coffee can. put lid on and place in larger coffee can.
pack ice into larger can around smaller can, sprinkle with salt so that it makes can really cold.
put lid on larger can and start rolling (tape shut if necessary). it takes a while, so be patient. replace ice when it starts to melt and add more salt too
roll until contents are thick, about 30 minutes
