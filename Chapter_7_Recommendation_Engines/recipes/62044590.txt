Title:
ABC Mac 'n Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-1/2 cups dry elbow macaroni
1 lg. can evaporated milk
1 pound sharp cheddar cheese, sliced
1 Tablespoon butter

Directions:
Cook the pasta according to package directions.  Drain and rinse under cold water.  In deep 2 qt. casserole dish, layer pasta and cheese, ending with cheese.  Pour milk over all, dot with butter.  Cover dish with foil, bake at 350 for 1 hour, or if you are in a rush, bake at 400 for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
AUNT MAMIE
Description: 
So easy to prepare; put your feet up while it bakes--your family will love it, as ours does.
