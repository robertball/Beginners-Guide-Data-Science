Title:
Shrimp and Pasta with Creamy Pesto Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. penne pasta
1 lb. cooked shimp (35-40 count)
1/2 C. pesto
1/4 C. whipping cream
1/4 C. Chicken broth
3 Tbl. chopped, drained, oil-packed sun dried tomatoes
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1.  In a 4-6 quart sauce pan over high heat bring 3 quarts water to boil.  Cook pasta until tender (7-12 minutes).  Drain and return to pan
2.  Add shrimp, pesto, cream, broth, and tomatoes to pasta - stir over medium head until well incorporated (2-3 minutes).  Add salt and pepper to taste

Submitter: 
Mark Winter
Description: 
This dish can be made lighter with half & half as a substitute for the whipping cream.   A big hit with everyone, especially if your kids like shrimp!
