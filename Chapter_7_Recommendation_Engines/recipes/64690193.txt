Title:
Hula's Cranberry Orange Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks soften butter
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 egg
2 1/2 tsp orange zest
2 TBSP OJ (John that = Orange Juice)
3 cups flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
2 cups cranberries
1/2 cup walnuts or more if you really like nuts

Directions:
Set oven to 350
Cream with mixer 2 sticks of butter + 1 cup sugar + 1/2 cup brown sugar
Beat in 1 egg
Add & mix slightly 2 1/2 tsp orange zest + 2 TBSP OJ
Mix 3 cups flour + 1/2 tsp baking soda + 1/2 salt and blend into mixture
Stir in 2 cups cranberries & 1/2 cup walnuts
Weight out 108gm (or 3.8 oz) cookie balls, then squish them a bit into cookie shape.
