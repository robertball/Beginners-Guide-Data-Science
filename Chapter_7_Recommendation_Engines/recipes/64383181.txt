Title:
Bloody Ceasar Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups ripe tomatoes, cut into wedges (use assortment of colours and sizes)
3 T fresh lime juice
1 clover garlic
1/4 minced onion
1 cup finely diced celery
1/2 cup diced cucumber
2 t celery salt
1T prepared horseradish
3 dash's worchestershire sauce
1 dash tabasco
salt and black pepper

Directions:
toss the tomato, lime juice and garlic - cover and let sit for 30 minutes
strain liquid from the tomatoes into a small pot and reduce to 2T.  Add this reduction back to the tomatoes and toss with the onion, celery, cucumber, celery salt, horseradish, worchestershire and tabasco.  Season to taste and chill until ready to serve

Submitter: 
M. Karen
Description: 
This salad is a step beyond the expected, all the flavours you love in the classic patio-sipping ceasar drink - works wonderfully here with summer tomatoes
