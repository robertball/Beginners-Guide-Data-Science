Title:
maple syrup crumplet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crumpets
Coconut oil
Cinnamon
Maple syrup

Directions:
Melt coconut oil in frying pan
Fry crumpets with sprinkled cinnamon on each side while frying
Once browned, remove to serving plate
Pour maple syrup and enjoy!!

Description:
