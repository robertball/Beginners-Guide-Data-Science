Title:
CHOCOLATE PEANUT BUTTER BARS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 CUPS POWDERED SUGAR
2 CUPS GRAHAM CRACKER CRUMBS
1 CUP SMOOTH PEANUT BUTTER
1/2 CUP PLUS 2 TBSP BUTTER, MELTED
2 CUPS MILK CHOCOLATE OR SEMI-SWEET CHOCOLATE CHIPS, MELTED

Directions:
MIX POWDERED SUGAR, GRAHAM CRACKER CRUMBS, PEANUT BUTTER AND BUTTER IN A MEDIUM SIZE BOWL UNTIL BLENDED.
SCRAPE INTO UNGREASED 9x13 BAKING PAN AND PRESS UNTIL EVEN.  SPREAD MELTED CHOCOLATE OVER TOP.  REFRIGERATE AT LEAST 2 HOURS OR UNTIL CHOCOLATE IS FIRM.  LET STAND UNTIL ROOM TEMP BEFORE CUTTING.

Submitter: 
Diane Blanchard Angell
Description: 
A MOUTH WATERING CHEWY BAR.  THEY DISAPPEARED IN MINUTES WHEN I TOOK THEM TO WORK.
