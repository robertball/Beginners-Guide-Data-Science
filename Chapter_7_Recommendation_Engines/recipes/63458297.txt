Title:
Slow Cooker Chocolate Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. dry roasted peanuts
4 oz.German's sweet chocolate
1 - 12 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 1/2 lbs. white almond bark

Directions:
Put in slow cooker in this order written.Put slow cooker on low for 3 hours do not stir.After 3 hours stir with wooden spoon until smooth.Drop in mini-cupcake pans.Let harden package and share with others.

Submitter: 
Beadlady2
Description: 
It's a good holiday recipe that the kids can help with and you can work on other things while it's cooking.
