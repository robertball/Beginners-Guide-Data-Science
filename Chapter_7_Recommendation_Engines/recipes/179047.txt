Title:
Creamy Chicken and Wild Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979912.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1 1/2 cups water
4 large carrots, thickly sliced
1 (6 ounce) package uncooked seasoned long-grain and wild rice mix
8 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Directions:
Stir the soup, water, carrots, rice and seasoning packet in a 3 1/2-quart slow cooker. Add the chicken and turn to coat.
Cover and cook on LOW for 7 to 8 hours* or until the chicken is cooked through.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Boneless chicken breasts, carrots and wild rice mix simmer to tenderness in a creamy chicken gravy.
