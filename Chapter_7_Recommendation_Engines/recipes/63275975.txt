Title:
St. Germain Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz St. Germain Liqueur
2 oz Champagne or Sparlking White Wine
2 oz Sparling Water or Club Soda

Directions:
Add ice in a cocktail shaker
Add ingredients in a 2/2/1 ratio
Give one or two quick shakes
Pour into a martini glass and garnish with a twist of Lemon
