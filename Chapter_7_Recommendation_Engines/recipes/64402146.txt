Title:
Crispy Baked French Fried Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 potatoes, cut in about same size
1 Tbsp tomato paste
2 Tbsp flour
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp thyme

Directions:
Boil cut potatoes 10 min, mix the dry ingredients in a small bowl, drain potatoes, then in same pan toss in 1 Tbsp tomato paste. Shake and sprinkle on dry ingredients, shake, sprinkle. Bake for 30mn !
