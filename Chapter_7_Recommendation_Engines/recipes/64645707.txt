Title:
Slow Cooker Hawaiian Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
3/4 cup soda cracker crumbs (or bread crumbs)
2 tbsp. milk or water
2 large eggs
1/4 cup finely chopped onion, optional
1 clove garlic, crushed, optional
1/4 tsp. ground ginger (or more)
1 1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 lb lean ground beef
Sauce:
398 ml can crushed pineapple, with juice
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup vinegar
3 tbsp. soy sauce
1 1/2 tbsp. cornstarch, or more
1 1/2 tbsp. water, or more (equal parts with starch)

Directions:
Mix meatball ingredients together well.
Shape into 1 - 1 1/2" balls. Place on baking sheet lined with foil. Brown x 15 min. in 350 oven.
Place meatballs in slow cooker.
Meanwhile, in med. saucepan, mix first 4 sauce ingredients. Stir. Bring to boil.
Mix cornstarch & water in small bowl or cup. Stir gradually into boiling mixture until slightly thickened.
Pour over meatballs.
Cover and cook on low x 4 - 5 hours, depending on size of meatballs.
Serve over rice or pasta.

Yummy pineapple sauce over meatballs slow cooked and served over rice or pasta.
