Title:
Shoe String Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 potatoes peeled and cut into fry shape
salt and pepper to taste
Mixture:
2 Tbs parsley
3/4 cup grated sharp cheddar
1/4- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp paprika or  Ancho chili powder
1-3 Tbs butter
1/2 cup cream

Directions:
Turn oven to 350 degrees F.  Spray baking dish with non stick cooking spray.   layer half the potatoes in the bottoms of the dish. layer half the mixture on top and spread out. place 2nd layer of potatoes on top and then the 2nd layer of mixture on top with a few tabs of butter. Pour cream over the top.  Bake for 1 hour uncovered.

Submitter: 
Christina S. Wall
Description: 
My grandmother use to make these and her daughter my aunt passed me the recipe a few years ago after she served it on Christmas Eve.
