Title:
Quick Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1238671.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup stemmed kale
1 cup skim milk
1 sliced frozen banana
1/2 green apple, cut into chunks
1/2 pear, cut into chunks
1/4 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
1 tablespoon peanut butter
1 tablespoon flax seed meal
1 tablespoon wheat germ
2 sprigs fresh parsley, or more to taste

Directions:
Blend kale, milk, banana, apple, pear, oats, peanut butter, flax seed meal, wheat germ, and parsley in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
shuether
Description: 
A quick, healthy breakfast for anyone on the go. Substitute any liquid for the milk.
