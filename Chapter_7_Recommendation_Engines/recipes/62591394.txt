Title:
our fav burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pound ground chuck
minced garlic
liquid meat marinade
1 stick butter
salt pepper

Directions:
freeze butter stick until firm cut into small cubes
make your portion of burger into a ball
push a hole into center
add 1 tbl spoon of marinade into center
1/2 teaspoon garlic into center blend well
make into patty salt and pepper to taste
take butter pieces and push them into patty about 6 to 8 pieces into each one try to cover top of butter into the meat
also be sure to check both sides so that no butter is showing
grill to perfection add your favorite topping and enjoy
