Title:
The greatest roasted red pepper dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 very large red onion, quartered
3-4 Ripe red peppers, halved and seeded
2 whole bulbs of garlic, intact
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 tub of garlic and herb cream cheese
2 tablespoons Mayonaise
1 teaspoon chilli flakes (optional)
Squeeze of lemon juice
salt and pepper

Directions:
preheat oven to 400 degrees.
slice the very tops off the garlic bulbs and place on baking sheet.
Add onion and peppers.
Drizzle with olive oil.
Bake for 45 mins - 1 hour or until skin on peppers is blistered and blakening.
Remove from oven and allow to cool.
Pop garlic cloves out by peeling back the skin and or squeezing them to pop them out, add to food processor with onion, red peppers, cream cheese, mayonaise, chilli flakes, lemon juice and salt and pepper to taste.
Blend for 1 minute or until dip looks smooth.
Chill and serve with warm pita bread.
This makes a good deal of dip so if you aren't entertaining it also freezes really well.

Submitter: 
Louise T
Description: 
Roasted peppers, onion and garlic are combined in this very simple to make dip which tastes just amazing. It's really popular at parties where I get rave reviews every time.
