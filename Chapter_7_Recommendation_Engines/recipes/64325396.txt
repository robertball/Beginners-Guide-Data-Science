Title:
Vegetarian Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Onion
1 Orange bell pepper
Handful of baby carrots
Can of pinto beans
can of black beans
can of diced rotelli tomatoes
rice
fajitas
fixings (sour cream, guacamole, onions, tomatoes, shredded cheese, black olives, hot sauce, etc)

Directions:
mash pinto beans, add to crock pot with rotelli tomatoes.
Saute carrots until soft, then saute onions & peppers. Add to crock pot with black beans, etc. Cook on low 2 or 3 hours.
