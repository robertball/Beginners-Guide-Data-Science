Title:
Jamaican Jerk Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon Ground allspice
1 tablespoon Dried thyme
1 1/2 teaspoons Cayenne pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons Ground black pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons Ground sage
3/4 teaspoon Ground nutmeg
3/4 teaspoon Ground cinnamon
2 tablespoons Garlic powder or fresh
1 tablespoon Sugar
1/4 cup Olive oil
1/4 cup Soy sauce
1/4 cup White Vinegar
1/4 cup Orange juice
1 Lime juice
1 Ground or Chopped Habanero Pepper
3 Green onions/ Scalloins chopped
1 cup Onion chopped
4 to 6 chicken breasts, or equal volume of any other meat.

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the allspice, thyme, cayenne pepper, black pepper, sage, nutmeg, cinnamon, salt, garlic powder, sugar, olive oil, soy sauce, vinegar, orange juice, lime juice, habanero, onion, and mix well. Add the chicken breasts, cover and marinate for at least 2 hours, longer if possible.
Preheat an outdoor grill. Grill 4-6 minutes on each side.
