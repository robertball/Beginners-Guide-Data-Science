Title:
Creamy Spinach and Pasta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sour cream
2 cups mayonnaise
2 cups sharp cheddar, shredded
2 tbsp. white wine
1 tbsp. dried basil
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
9 oz. bag fresh spinach, chopped
1 cup finely chopped red onion
1 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
1 cup shredded carrot
1 lb. penne or pasta of your choice

Directions:
Cook pasta to al dente and drain.
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine sour cream, mayonnaise, cheddar, wine, basil, garlic powder, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
Mix well.
Fold in spinach, onion, bell pepper, carrot and pasta, mixing thoroughly.
Pour into large greased baking dish.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes, covered.
Uncover and bake for 15 more minutes or until bubbling around edges and heated through.
