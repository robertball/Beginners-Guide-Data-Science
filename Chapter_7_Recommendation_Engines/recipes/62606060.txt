Title:
Meat Loaf Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1-3/4 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 tea. salt
1/2 tea. sage
1/4 tea. pepper
1-1/2 lbs ground beef
1 envelope butter and herb instant mashed potatoes
1 teas. prepared mustard
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the first seven ingredients.  Crumble beef over mixture and mix well.  Press onto the bottom and up the sides of an ungreased deep dish 9-inch pie pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes or until meat is no longer pink.
Meanwhile, prepare mashed potatoes according to package directions; stir in mustard.  Drain meat loaf; spread potatoes over top.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake 3-5 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.  Let stand for 5 minutes before cutting.
