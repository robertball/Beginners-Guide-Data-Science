Title:
Cinnamon Candied Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/905530.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup chopped pecans
1 Tbsp. butter
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 /2- 1 tsp. cinnamon
Dash of salt

Directions:
Melt butter in frying pan. Add all ingredients and stir constantly to cook (until sugar is melted). Watch carefully because they burn easily.
Spread cooked pecans onto cookie sheet to cool.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
Perfect for a salad with Craisins, strawberries, and feta.
