Title:
Fruit Cake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups mixed candied fruit
1 cup raisins
1/2 cup brandy
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
3/4 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 cups flour
1/3 cup melted butter

Directions:
Combine candied fruit, raisins, and rum and soak for at least 12 hours.
Preheat oven to 375
Mix brown sugar and soda and beat in eggs one at a time;add in vanilla, butter and cinnamon.
Gently fold in flour, then fruit.
Spoon onto ungreased cookie sheet with teaspoon and bake for 8 minutes.
Move to bakers rack to cool.
