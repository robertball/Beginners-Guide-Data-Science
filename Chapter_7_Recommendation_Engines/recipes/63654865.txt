Title:
Hugo cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1015712.jpg
Ingredients:
10-15 ml Elderflower Syrup
150 ml Sparkling wine
50 ml Soda Water
3-4 Mint leaves
1 Lime wedge
Ice

Directions:
In a huge wine glass add a little elderflower sirup, 3-4 icecubes, mint leaves and a squeezed slice of lime. Gently stir with a bar spoon.
Add lots of ice.  Add sparkling wine and top up with club soda.
Serve with a straw and a few mint leaves for garnish.

Submitter: 
George
Description: 
New from Europe, this is the latest spring/summer drink of choice for Germany, Austria and North Italy.  

Light and delicious, it is probably the easiest cocktail to make ever.  Ladies will love Hugo.
