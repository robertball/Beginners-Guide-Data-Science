Title:
Greek Yogurt Parmesan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 small/medium sized chicken breasts
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1 cup Greek yogurt -plain
1/4- 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 package of dry ranch dressing mix
1/2 tsp pepper
(optional) dab on yellow mustard

Directions:
Thin out Chicken slices using a meat tenderizer.
Spread mixture over chicken breasts, bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes.
Heat oven to a broil and cook chicken an additional 5- 7 min.. flipping over once in between to brown on both sides and toast panko crumbs.

Description: 
Quick, easy, and delicious meal for two! Mainly uses ingredients you have at hoe and is great for week nights with little time to prepare!
