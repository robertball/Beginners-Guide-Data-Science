Title:
Rocky Road Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter/margarine
1/3 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 cup flour
1/2 cup mini marshmallows
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F. In a medium bowl, beat margarine until softened. Add sugars and beat until fluffy. Add egg and vanilla and beat well. Gradually beat in flour.
Spread dough evenly in an ungreased, round or square 9-inch cake pan.
Bake 15 minutes until golden brown; remove from oven. Sprinkle marshmallows and chocolate chips on top of hot crust. Return cake pan to oven and bake 6-8 minutes more, or until marshmallows are golden.
