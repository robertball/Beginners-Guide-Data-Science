Title:
Cheesy Chicken Alfredo Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1044935.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large chicken breast cubed
2 tbls olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1 garlic clove finely chopped
1 tsp Italian seasoning
2 tbls flour
1 (12oz.) package frozen mixed vegetables (carrots, green beans, peas, corn)
1 (16 oz.) jar alfredo sauce
1/4 water
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 (8oz.) package refridgerated crescent roll dough

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees, lightly grease 13X9 baking pan with cooking spray.
Heat oil in a large skillet, add chicken breast, garlic, salt, and Italian seasoning to hot skillet over medium high heat. Saute for approximately 10 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
Drain fat from pan, add flour and toss chicken to distribute flour.
Heat frozen vegetables in the microwave for 4 1/2 minutes then add to the chicken along with Afredo sauce. Add water to empty sauce jar, cover with lid, shake well, poor into skillet, toss well. Bring mixture to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.
Transfer chicken mixture to prepared casserole dish, spread evenly. Lay crescent dough into a flat layer on top of chicken mixture. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese.
Cook for 30 minutes in oven until dough is cooked to a golden brown and cheese is melted.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is quick and easy to make. My picky 14 year old daughter lapped it up. Great for leftovers, reheats well.
