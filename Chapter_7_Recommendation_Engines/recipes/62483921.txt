Title:
Rum Frosted Pumpkin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488082.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ginger
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 egg
2 egg whites
1 plus 2 tablespoons cup canned pumpkin
1/4 cup canola oil
2 tablespoons water
4 ounces reduced fat cream cheese
1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon rum extract

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the first eight ingredients. Add egg, egg whites, pumpkin, oil and water; mix well. Transfer to an 11-in. x 7-in. x 2-in. baking pan coated with nonstick cooking spray. Bake at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack.
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese. Beat in confectioners' sugar, then vanilla and rum. Frost bars. Chill for 15 minutes, then cut. Refrigerate leftovers.
