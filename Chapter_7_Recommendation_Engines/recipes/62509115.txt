Title:
Prairie Dust Steak Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup paprika
1/4 cup kosher salt, finely ground
1/4 brown sugar
2 tablespoons mustard powder
1/4 cup chili powder
1/4 cup ground cumin
2 tablespoons ground black pepper
1/4 cup granulated garlic
2 tablespoons cayenne

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a container and apply as desired to steak as rub.

Submitter: 
Justin Will
Description: 
This was the recipe I found online to simulate Longhorn Steakhouse's Renegade steak, a favorite of mine. It turns out pretty close actually.
