Title:
Strawberry shortcake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. flour
1 C. sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1 stick real butter
1 C. milk

Directions:
preheat 350
mix all together, put in long loaf cake pan, 2 bread pans or muffin pan... greased.
Bake for 30 35 min. or until toothpick comes out clean.
