Title:
Pineapple Walnut Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (7 oz.) pkg. vanilla wafers
1 c. butter
1 c. superfine sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
2 c. crushed pineapple (2 cans)
1/2 - 3/4 c. chopped walnuts

Directions:
Crush wafers to crumbs.  Set aside 2 T. for topping.
Cream butter, adding sugar gradually.
Add eggs to creamed butter 1 at a time, beating well after each.
Add vanilla, drained pineapple, and nuts; blend well.
Line loaf pan with foil, leaving enough excess to fold over the top.
Press half of crumbs into the bottom of the pan.  Add 1/4 pineapple walnut mix and spread evenly.
Sprinkle an even layer of crumbs, then repeat layers, finishing with a layer of pineapple.
Sprinkle reserved 2 T crumbs on top and fold over foil to cover.
Refrigerate 24 hours.
Slice and serve.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
This is incredibly rich and decadent, so don't bother to count the calories or fat.  This is best eaten within a couple days, but it has never lasted that long in my house!
