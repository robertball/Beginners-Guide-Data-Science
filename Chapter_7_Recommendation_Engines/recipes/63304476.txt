Title:
Bill's Chicken or Turkey stock - No Salt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 quarts of water
3 lbs. uncooked chicken or turkey meat on the bone
1 large chopped onion
2 carrots chopped
2 stalks of celery chopped
Fresh herbs
1-2 sprigs of parsley
2 bay leaves
1 bunch celery tops
2 garlic gloves sliced
Dry herbs
1/4 Teaspoon Thyme
1/4 Teaspoon Sweet Basil
1/4 Teaspoon Taragon
1/4 Teaspoon Marjoram
2 Teaspoons Mrs. Dash No Salt table blend
1 Teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a large sauce pan (4-5 quart).  Bring the mixture to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for 5-6 hours.  Stir occasionally and remove the foam from the pan as it cooks.  Add water during cooking as needed.
Strain off grease, strain stock into a large bowl or storage container, let cool, remove remaining fat/grease from surface.
Use in soups or where ever stock is called for.  Store in the refrigirator upto two weeks, or, freexe for later.

Submitter: 
Bill Falk
Description: 
Takes a little time, but, the low sodium content is great for no salt diets, and, the flavor is superb!!
