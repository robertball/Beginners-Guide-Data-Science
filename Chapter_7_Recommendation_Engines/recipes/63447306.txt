Title:
Hot Chocolate Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c sugar
1/3 c milk
1/4 c butter
Dash salt
3 tbsp white syrup
3 tbsp cocoa
1 tbsp canola oil
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp almond

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except vanilla and almond in sauce pan, bring to boil, and boil 2 minutes.
Add vanilla and almond.
Serve warm over vanilla ice-cream.
