Title:
Grilled Chicken Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
lemon pepper for seasoning
peppered bacon
sliced cheese - your choice
sliced buns - your choice

Directions:
1. Wash chicken breasts and pat dry.  Season with lemon pepper and set aside.
2. Slice bacon in half.  Fry bacon until chewy and drain on paper towels.
3.  Drain all the drippings from the pan and add back in 1 tbs of bacon fat.
4.  Place chicken breasts in pan and fry until edges are opaque.  Flip to the other side and arrange bacon on top of chicken.  Add cheese slices, cover and allow cheese to melt.
5.  Serve on bun with your choice of condiments.
