Title:
Havreflarn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
3/4 c. sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
2 T. flour
1 t. baking powder
1 egg
1 c. oatmeal
empty paper towel tubes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Melt butter.
Stir in remaining ingredients
Mix until well blended
Butter cookie sheets and then dust with flour.
Drop batter from tsp. 3 inches apart.
Bake 6-8 minutes
Once removed from oven, let stand for 1-2 minutes before removal.
Drape over foil covered paper towl tubes
Careful - these cookies are very fragile. Make one pan at a time because once they come out of the oven, they harden quickly

Submitter: 
Linda T
Description: 
Thin , crispy and delicious Swedish oatmeal wafers.. made by my mother, who was first generation Swedish American, and now by me
