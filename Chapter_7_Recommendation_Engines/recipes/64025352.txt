Title:
Beans and Weenies Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One large can of Bush's Original Baked Beans
One package sliced turkey hot dogs
One package Jiffy Mix cornbread (1 egg and 1/3 cup milk)
One cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix baked beans and hot dogs in a casserole dish or 9x9 baking dish. Make Jiffy Mix according to package directions.  Place spoonfuls of Jiffy Mix on top of bean and weenie mixture.  Bake for 45 minutes or until center of cornbread is baked or edge of cornbread is turning brown.

Submitter: 
Lisa Dawes Strachota
Description: 
This would be great for a backyard barbecue or pitch-in. Kids love it!
