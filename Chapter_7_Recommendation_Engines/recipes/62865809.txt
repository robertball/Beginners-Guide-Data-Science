Title:
Smoked Sausage Penne by Laurie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz of Velveta Queso Blanco Cheese
1/3 cup milk
1 can Rotel tomatoes
1/2 lb Eckridge Smoked Sausage
8 oz penne rigate pasta
1 cup shredded italian cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Cut Smoked Sausage lenthwise then slice into small pieces. Fry sausage in a skilled until browned, set aside.  Cooked noodles according to directions.  Meanwhile, melt velveta milk, and rotel tomatoes.  Combine smoked sausage, cheese mixture, pasta, and shredded cheese. Add salt and pepper to taste.  Bake in 8x8 inch pan for 30 minutes.
