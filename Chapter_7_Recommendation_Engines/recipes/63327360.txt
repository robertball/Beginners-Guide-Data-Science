Title:
Beef Caldillo Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds beef stew meat
1 large red onion, diced
3 (10.5 ounce) can Rotel diced tomatoes
1.5 cup HerdezÂ® Salsa Verde
3 cups chicken broth
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 small cloves garlic
1.5 teaspoon ground cumin
1.5lbs potatoes, peeled and diced
1 lb mushrooms, quartered
1/2 lb sliced carrots

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute stew meat and onions. Add diced tomatoes, HERDEZ(R) Salsa Casera, HERDEZ(R) Salsa Verde, chicken broth,pepper, garlic and groundcumin. Cover and simmer over low heat until the meat is tender, about 1 1/2 hours.
During the last 30 minutes of cooking, add the diced potatoes. Let stew rest in hot pot ~ 30 minutes before serving.
