Title:
Hazelnut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, or butter substitute
2 1/4 cups flour
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup confectioner's sugar (plus more for rolling)
1/2 tsp nutmeg
3/4 cups chopped hazelnuts

Directions:
Mix butter and 1/2 cup sugar
add vanilla
gradually add in flour and nutmeg
roll into about 2 inch balls
bake on ungreased cookie sheets
at 375 for 12 min
cool completely and roll in confecioners sugar

Submitter: 
KMOMMYZ
Description: 
These tender cookies are a snap to make and taste so yummy!
just be sure to cool completely before rolling in powdered sugar!
