Title:
Halibut soft Taco's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium mango peeled and cubed
1/2C cubed avacado
1/4C chopped red onion
2Tomatoes
2 Jalapeno's chopped and seeded
2Tbsp minced fresh parsley
3tsp olive oil
1tsp lemon juice
1tsp honey
1 lb halibut steaks
1/2tsp salt
1/4tsp pepper
1/4tsp garlic salt
4 bibb lettuce leaves
4 flour torillas
4tsp taco sauce

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the mango, avacado,onion, jalapeno, parsley, tomatoes, 2tsp oil,lemon juice, and honey; set aside. Brush halibut with remaining oil; sprinkle with salt, pepper and garlic salt.
Coat grill rack with cooking spray before starting the grill. Grill balibut, covered, over high heat or broil 3-4 inches from the heat for 3-5 minutes on each side or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
Place lettuce leaves on tortillas top with fish and mango mixture. Drizzle with taco sauce.
