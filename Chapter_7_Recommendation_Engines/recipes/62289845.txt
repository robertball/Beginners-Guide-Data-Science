Title:
Meyer Lemon Olive Oil and Honey Chèvre Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. (8 oz.) Laura Chenel Chèvre (soft goat cheese)
2 Tbsp. Meyer Lemon Olive Oil
2 Tbsp. Honey

Directions:
Using a small pot and low heat, gently whisk together meyer lemon olive oil and honey until smooth & warm. Remove from heat and allow to cool. Using a mixer, mix goat cheese till creamy and add cooled honey mixture, mix well, Refrigerate for at least 2 hrs.

Submitter: 
Celeste
Description: 
This is fabulous on nut bread, crackers, flat bread, and fruit!
