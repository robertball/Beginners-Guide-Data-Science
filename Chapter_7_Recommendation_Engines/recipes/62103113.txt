Title:
Chicken, Tortilla, Corn & Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cooked chicken
1 can of corn (15oz)
1 red bell pepper chopped
1/2 cup diced green onion
2 15oz cans chicken broth
1 cup Pace Picante Sauce
1 tsp dried oregano or basil
1 tsp cumin
2 cups grated jack cheese
corn tortillas, cut up
sour cream

Directions:
Bring to boil in large pot; chicken broth, chicken, bell pepper, green onion, pace picante sauce, oregano or basil & cumin.  Simmer for 20 min.  Add cheese & tortillas and stir until melted.  Add a dollop of sour cream to each serving.  Serve with warm tortillas.
