Title:
Caribou Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed potatoes
1 can stewed tomatoes (or 1 cup of diced fresh tomatoes)
3 large crushed and chopped garlic cloves
1/2 diced onion
1 bay leaf
1 1/2 cups rice
salt and pepper to taste
1 can carrots (or 2 washed and chopped carrots)
1-2 pounds of thawed cubed caribou or game meat
1 or 2 bones from game of possible
1  tablespoon crushed red pepper (optional)

Directions:
In a large soup pot (recipe is for a 12 quart soup pot) add meat and bone to pot.
Fill with water till water is about 2 inches above meat.
Add all ingredients except for rice.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer.
Once boiling add rice.  Boil till rice is done.
Remove bone and bay leaf and serve.

Submitter: 
inupiaqcook
Description: 
A modern version of the staple meat of the Inupiaq Eskimo of the North Slope of Alaska, will also work with most game meats (such as moose).
