Title:
Beer & Cream Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tubs Whipped Cream Cheese
2 C Finely Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 pk Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing (dry)
1/3 c Beer

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and chill for at least 3hrs. Best if chilled over night.

Submitter: 
MissaLissa
Description: 
My future daughter in-law gave me this recipe. It's a quick and easy appetizer for a party and is best served with pretzel's. Trying different beer's is a fun way to change it up.
