Title:
Mexican Peach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 oz Patron Reposado
1 oz Peach Schnapps
1 oz Margarita Mix
1 oz Cranberry Juice
1 Lime Wedge
Ice

Directions:
Mix Patron, Peach Schnapps, Margarita Mix, and cranberry juice in shaker, pour over ice into a short glass, and add a lime wedge for garnish.
