Title:
Corn  & Carrot Chowda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups frozen corn
2 14.75 oz cans cream style corn
2 1/2 cups milk
1 can condensed cream of potato soup
1 15 oz can chicken broth
2 14.75 oz cans white potatoes quartered
1 cup chopped onion
2 cups of frozen crinkle cut carrots
2 tablespoons of butter or margerine
2 ham steaks cubed
2 tablespoons corn starch
1/4 cup warm water
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Add all ingredients excluding corn starch and cold water to a slow cooker.
combine corn starch and cold water in a seperate bowl until it is a smooth mixture. add to slow cooker
cook in slow cooker on low for 6-8 hours.

Submitter: 
Amy L Drouillard
Description: 
A flavorful corn and carrot chowder to warm you up. Great for tailgating.
