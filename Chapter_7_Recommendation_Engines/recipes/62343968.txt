Title:
Salt and Pepper chicken wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of chicken wings
1/4 cup of Kosher salt
2 tablespoons white pepper
1 tablespoon black pepper

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees. In a small bowl combine kosher salt and both black and white pepper. Mix together. Take a large baking tray and cover with tin foil (for easy clean up) and spray with Pam cooking spray. Spread chicken wings out on tray and sprinkle with salt/pepper mix. Turn wings over and sprinkle other side of wings. Place in pre-heated oven and cook for 25 min, remove from oven and turn wings and then place back in oven for another 30 minutes.
