Title:
Barley and Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound kielbasa cut in 1/2 lemgthwise then into 1/2 inch slices
5 cups chicken broth(or more*)
3 cups water
2 cups pearl barley
1 onion chopped
3/4 cup chopped celery
3/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1 10oz. package frozen chopped spinach thawed

Directions:
In a 4 to 5 quart pot over medium-high heat cook kielbasa about 5-7 minutes,until browned stir often.Remove with slotted spoon set aside. Add remaining ingriedents except spinach to pot. Bring to a boil reduce heat and simmer uncovered 40-45 minutes until barley is tender. Stir in kielbasa and spinach cook 3-4 minutes until heated through. *You may need to add more broth.
