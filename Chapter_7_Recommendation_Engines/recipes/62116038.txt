Title:
Bean Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Navy Beans (cooked)
4 Eggs
1 14 oz. can evaporated milk
1 stick butter
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 T. Flour
2 cups Sugar
2 T. Vanilla

Directions:
Cook beans until soft, then drain liquid.
Preheat oven. to 350 degrees.
In electric blender, blend beans, butter, milk, eggs, nutmeg,cinnamon  and flour about 2 minutes on medium speed.
Pour mixture into a large mixing bowl.
Add sugar and vanilla.
Mix well.
Pour into pie shells.
Bake about one hour or until golden brown.
Makes 2 or 3 Bean Pies.
Note: After five minutes cover with cling wrap.
