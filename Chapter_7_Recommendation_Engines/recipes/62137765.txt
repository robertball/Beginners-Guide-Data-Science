Title:
Consomme' Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked, white rice
1 can beef consomme' soup
1 can water
4 beef bouillion cubes
1 sm. onion, diced
1/2 pkg fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/2 cube butter, chopped
8 slices cooked bacon, chopped

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350
2. Add first seven ingredients to ungreased 9X13 baking dish.
3. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
4. In the meantime, cook up bacon and chop it. Set aside to drain on paper towel. Will be added after rice dish is finished baking.
4. Uncover rice dish and bake additional 10 minutes.
5. Sprinkle chopped bacon on top and serve.
*Note: can be prepared ahead of time and kept in refrigerator. Throw in oven when ready and add bacon at the end of baking.
