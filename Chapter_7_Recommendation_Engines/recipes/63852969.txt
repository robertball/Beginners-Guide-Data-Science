Title:
Tator Tot Hotdish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # hamburger
Tator Tots
Cream Mushroom Soup
Frozen Veggies - green beans

Directions:
Brown hamburger with salt and pepper; add diced onions.
Mix in soup and vegetables. Pour into casserole dish. Top with tator tots. Bake at 375 for 45 minutes
