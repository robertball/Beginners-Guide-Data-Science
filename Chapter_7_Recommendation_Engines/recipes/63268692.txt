Title:
5-In-1 Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. hot dogs, sliced
1 lb. can whole potatoes, drained and diced
1/2 small onion
2 T. butter or margarine
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 can of water
1 - 8oz can of green beans

Directions:
Brown hot dogs and potatoes.
Cook onion in butter until transparent.
Add soup, water, and beans.
Simmer 10 minutes, then serve.
