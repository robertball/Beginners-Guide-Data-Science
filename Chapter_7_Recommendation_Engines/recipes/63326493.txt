Title:
Caprese on Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf of Sourdough or other bread sliced
2 whole pealed garlic cloves
3 large ripe tomatoes, sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced 1/4 inch thick
1/3 cup fresh basil leaves
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Lightly toast bread slices and rub with fresh garlic cloves.
On each slice of bread place a slice of mozzarella cheese, 1-2 basil leaves and a slice of tomato.
Drizzle with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
hamalpn
Description: 
Traditional Italian Caprese salad on lightly toasted bread. It makes a quick and easy supper for 4 or appetizers for 14.
