Title:
Dawn's Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup Shortening
2/3cup Sugar
2/3cup Brown Sugar
2 eggs
1tsp. Vanilla
1tsp. Baking Soda
1tsp. Salt
2 1/4cup Flour
1cup Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Mix shortening, sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.  In a small bowl mix eggs, vanilla, baking soda, and salt.  Add to sugar mixture and beat well.  Add flour 1 cup at a time until well blended.  Stir in chocolate chips.  Refridgerate for 1 hour.  Spoon dought 2 inches apart on cookie sheet and bake for 8 minutes.  Remove from oven and keep on cookie sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a cooling rack.  ENJOY!
