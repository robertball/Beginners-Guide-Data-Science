Title:
Gluten Free Chocolate Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1832272.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Gluten Free F&E; Chocolate Cake Mix
3 Bananas
1 Egg
5 Tablespoons Butter
1/3 Cup Brown Sugar
1/4 Cup Semisweet Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease 10 muffin cups, or line with muffin papers.
In a large bowl, mix together baking mix, mashed bananas, egg and melted butter. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups. Top with chocolate chips.
Bake in preheated oven for 18 to 20 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of a muffin comes out clean.
