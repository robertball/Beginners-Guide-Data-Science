Title:
Chocolate Covered Strawberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/273557.jpg
Ingredients:
16 ounces milk chocolate chips
2 tablespoons shortening
1 pound fresh strawberries with leaves
1 cup chocolate candies
1/2 cup coconut, shredded
sugar sprinkles

Directions:
Insert toothpicks into the tops of the strawberries.
In a double boiler, melt the chocolate and shortening, stirring occasionally until smooth. Holding them by the toothpicks, dip the strawberries into the chocolate mixture.
Turn the strawberries upside down and insert the toothpick into styrofoam for the chocolate to cool. Sprinkle topping of choice on top.

Submitter: 
Cooks4Kids
Description: 
This recipe goes over great with me and I love that it is mostly fruit. I cover the strawberries in chocolate and then sprinkle coconut, chocolate candies, and sprinkles on top. The kids go crazy for these.Modified from: Chocolate Covered Strawberries
