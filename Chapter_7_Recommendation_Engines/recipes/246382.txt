Title:
Fudgy Chocolate Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups confectioners' sugar
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine confectioner's sugar, milk, vegetable oil, cocoa powder, corn syrup, and vanilla extract together in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until glaze is smooth and hot, 2 to 3 minutes. Do not boil.

Submitter: 
Niki11784
Description: 
This works well on any cake, pours nicely and looks beautiful. This recipe makes enough to glaze one 9x13-inch cake. 
Works on any cake.
