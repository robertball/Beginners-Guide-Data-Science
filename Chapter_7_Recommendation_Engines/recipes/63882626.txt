Title:
Steak Diane Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup thinly sliced fresh mushrooms
2 tbsp minced onion
1 clove garlic, crushed
1/8 tsp salt
1 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup butter or margarine
2 tbsp snipped parsley
2 tbsp butter or margarine
1 pound beef tenderloin cut into 8 slices

Directions:
Cook and sir mushrooms, onion, garlic, salt, lemon juice and Worcestershire sauce in 1/4 cup butter until mushrooms are tender. Stir in parsley and keep sauce warm.
Melt 2 tbsp butter in skillet. Cook tenderloin slices over medium-high heat to medium doneness, turning once, about 3-4 minutes on each side.
Serve topped with sauce.

Description: 
This beef tenderloin dish is simple to make but wonderfully complex in flavor.
