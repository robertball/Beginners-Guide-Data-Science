Title:
Steak and Potato Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/587182.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
2 red potatoes, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 red peppers, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
2 small grilled steaks, thin sliced
4 small mushrooms, sliced
1 small can V8
1 T soy sauce
1 T sweet and sour sauce
1 T red wine vinegar
1 t hoisin sauce
1 t sweet chili sauce
add pepper to taste
2 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Add potatoes, celery, peppers, and onion to hot pan with olive oil.
Cook 5 minutes on medium.
Add steak and mushrooms. Cook 2 minutes on medium.
Add remaining ingredients. Cook 5 minutes on medium.
Add V8 and turn down to medium-low. Cover and cook for 10 minutes stirring occasionally.
Serve over cooked rice.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
I was looking for an interesting way to serve left-over grilled steak and thought stir fry would be fun.
