Title:
chopped vegetable salad with  mozzerella and olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head romaine lettuce chopped
4 medium plum tomatoes seeded and chopped
1 cucumber peeled seeded and chopped
1 small red onion chopped
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
4 ounces cubed mozzerella cheese
1/2 cup pitted halved black olives
salt and pepper

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingridients. Drizzle on oil and vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Toss to coat. Stie in olives and cheese.

Nice starter salad for an Italian meal. strong vinegar taste.
