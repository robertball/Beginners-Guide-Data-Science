Title:
Fried Sphaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbl olive oil
1 med onion, thinly sliced
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 lb uncooked spaghetti or vermicelli
1 1/2 cups water
14 oz can plum tomatoes, chopped with juice
1/4-1/2 cup black olives, roughly chopped
3 Tbl drained capers
hot red pepper flakes to taste
salt & pepper
1/4 cup chopped basil 9or dried equiv)
grated parmesan

Directions:
heat 2Tbl oil on med-high heat in large skillet. saute onions & garlic until soft, about 5 min. remove w/slotted spoon & set aside,
add last Tbl oil to skillet & heat. break raw spaghetti in two. place sgaghetti in skillet, saute for 5minm shaking oan & stirring constantly, until strands start to brown.
add onions & garlic, water, tomatoes w/juice, olives, capers & red pepper flakes & dried basil. season w/s & p. cover & simmer iver med-low heat for 12 min for spagh/8-9 min for vermicelli, stirring occasionally & checking for doneness. pasta should be tender but firm. serve with parm
