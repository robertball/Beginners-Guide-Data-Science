Title:
Recycle your cereal muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup butter
1 egg
2 cups unbleached flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
2 cups prepared grain cereal (I like Sunny Boy Cereal)
1 cup buttermilk or sour milk or plain yogurt
1 cup berries I (like raspberries and blueberries)

Directions:
prepare muffin tin
preheat oven to 400
cream together butter, sugar, egg,
add cold, wet cereal and milk
mix well
combine in seperate bowl; flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda
add to wet mixture. Do not over mix
fold in berries
spoon into muffin tin
bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown and test for doneness.

Submitter: 
Jenny
Description: 
Have you cooked too much hot cereal and ready to dump it?   This is a great way to use leftover grain cereals the next day! Deliciously moist without adding tons of fat. Hearty and satisfying.
