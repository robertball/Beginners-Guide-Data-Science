Title:
Peanut butter pancakes with Blackberry syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/730972.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup p-nut butter
(we use all natural white choclate p-nut butter)
1 cup whole wheat flour (sifted)
2 tablespoons cane sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 egg
1 cup almond milk
2 tablespoons canola oil (do not use EVOO)
SYRUP
1 large or 2 small container(s) blackberries
you could also use: stawberries, blueberries ect.
1 cup water
1 cup sugar
1 tablespoon corn starch (optional)
1 lemon

Directions:
In a double boiler, melt 1/2 cup p-nut butter
Mix flour, sugar, salt, powder (set aside)
Whisk: 1 egg, almond milk & oil into p-nut butter
Add wet ingredients to dry (don't over mix)
Cook on hot griddle, check to flip when you see bubbles on the edges. Will not bubble all over like normal pancakes.
Syrup
Bring water, sugar & berries & lemon zest to a boil
Simmer about 5 minutes
Strain into bowl, press juice out of berries
Return to saucepan simmer w/ 1 tbls corn starch or until desired consistency
