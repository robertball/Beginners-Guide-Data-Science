Title:
Easy Black Bean Lasagna by cooks4forty Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/397716.jpg
Ingredients:
1	(15 oz.) can black beans, drained
1	(28 oz.) can crushed tomatoes, undrained
1	(15 oz.) can fat-free refried beans
3/4	cup chopped onions
1/2	cup chopped green bell pepper
3/4	cup chunky style salsa
1/2 cup water
1     (4 oz.) can diced green chiles
1	tsp. chili powder
1/2	tsp. cumin
1	(8 oz.) container cottage cheese
1/8	tsp. garlic powder (or use 1 tsp. fresh garlic)
2	eggs
10	uncooked lasagna noodles
2	cups shredded cheddar
2     cups mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray a 13" x 9" baking dish.
In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, black beans, refried beans, onions, peppers, salsa, water, green chiles, chili powder, and cumin.  Mix well.
In small bowl, combine cottage cheese, garlic powder, eggs and salt and pepper to taste.
Spread 1 cup of the tomato mixture in bottom of baking dish.  Top with half of the noodles, overlapping slightly.  Top with half of remaining tomato mixture.  Spoon cottage cheese mixture over top, top with half of the cheese, then the remaining noodles, tomato mixture and cheese.   Cover tightly with spray-coated foil (can be refrigerated up to one day at this point).
Bake covered at 350 for 45 minutes, uncover and cook for another 15 minutes.  Let stand 15 minutes before serving.  (If you have refrigerated it before-hand, add 10-15 minutes to the baking time.)

Submitter: 
cooks4forty
Description: 
A no boil lasagna that's requested everywhere I go.  It's meatless, but you won't miss it - it's a very hearty meal.
