Title:
Caramel Layer Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123286.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag (14 oz.) caramels
2/3 c. evaporated milk
1 box German Chocolate Cake Mix
2/3 c. softened margarine
1 bag walnut pieces
6 oz. chocolate chips

Directions:
Melt caramels with 1/3 c. evaporated milk.
Stir constantly in double boiler over medium heat.
Take off heat and set aside.
Mix cake mix with 1/3 c. evaporated milk.
Add margarine.
Take 2/3 c. cake batter and smooth into 9x13 greased pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 6 minutes.
Sprinkle chocolate chips and walnuts over top.
Pour melted caramel mix over that.
Drop remaining cake batter over caramel layer.
Return to oven for 15-18 minutes.
Cool for 30 minutes and cut into bars.
