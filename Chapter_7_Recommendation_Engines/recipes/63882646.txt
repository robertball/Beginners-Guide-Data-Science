Title:
Italian Lamb Shanks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/4 pound lamb shanks
1 8oz bottle of Italian salad dressing
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 tbsp parsley flakes
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp onion powder or onion salt
1/3 cup shortening

Directions:
Place meat in shallow glass dish, pour salad dressing over meat and cover tightly. Refrigerate for at least 5 hours, turning meat occasionally. Drain meat and preserve marinade.
Mix parmesan cheese, flour, parsley flakes, salt and onion salt.
Coat cheese mixture, reserving excess.
Melt shortening in Dutch oven or large skillet and brown shanks over medium heat, turning occasionally.
Reduce heat, sprinkle remaining cheese mixture over shanks. Add reserved marinade, cover tightly and simmer, turning occasionally, for 2 1/2 hours or until tender.
Serve additional Parmesan with shanks.
