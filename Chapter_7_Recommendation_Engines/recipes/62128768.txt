Title:
Ultimate Cheesy Scalloped Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. can whole kernel corn, UNdrained
1 15 oz. can creamed corn
1 c. uncooked elbow macaroni
1/3 cup minced fresh onion
1 8 oz. package Velveeta cheese, cubed
1 stick of butter (1/2 c.), melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together and put in a 2-quart casserole dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
SUE1956
Description: 
My husband and I were served this at a church dinner years ago as a side dish to baked ham (which it goes wonderfully with). It's a snap to make, and it's just the recipe to reach for when you're craving some real comfort food!
