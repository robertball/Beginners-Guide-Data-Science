Title:
Pumpkin Custard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/935731.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups pumpkin puree
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon ground gloves
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
In a saucepan, stir the pumpkin over medium-high heat for 10 minutes or until slightly dry and caramelized.
Remove from heat; add the sugar, salt, cinnamon and ginger. Mix well.
Add the eggs, cream and milk. Mix until smooth; pour into baking pan.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes. Allow to completely cool on rack before cutting.

Description: 
A hot pumpkin custard  made with eggs, cream and pumpkin puree is spiced with ginger and cinnamon and baked in a baking dish.Modified from: Pumpkin Custard Pie
