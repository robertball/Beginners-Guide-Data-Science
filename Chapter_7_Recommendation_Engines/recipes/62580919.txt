Title:
Satiny Chocolate Mint Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/548307.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips
3 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon light corn syrup
1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract

Directions:
Place all ingredients except extract into a glass measuring cup. Microwave on high for 45 seconds. Remove & stir. Microwave again for 30 seconds and stir. Stir until chips are melted and mixture is smooth, then add extract. If necessary, heat mixture in 15 second intervals until smooth.
Spread warm glaze over top of cake, letting it drizzle down the sides.

A glossy chocolate glaze to drizzle over a Bundt cake. It's also great on cookies and doughnuts.Modified from: Satiny Chocolate Glaze
