Title:
Bobbing for Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/735602.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
2 fluid ounces Southern Comfort liqueur
1/4 cup apple cider, or as needed

Directions:
Fill a highball glass with ice. Pour in Southern Comfort, then fill the rest of the glass with apple cider. Stir before serving.

Submitter: 
Lindsay
Description: 
Southern Comfort and apple cider make a tasty fall drink that calms the nerves.
