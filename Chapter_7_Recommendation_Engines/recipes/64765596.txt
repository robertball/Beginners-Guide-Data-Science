Title:
Amy's French Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7308168.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Stick of unsalted butter
1/2 Cup Olive oil
6 Large Vidalia onions, quartered and sliced
Red wine
2 - 48oz Boxes of Beef broth
Dried thyme (maybe about 1 Tablespoon)
Salt & pepper
Crusty bread
Gruyere cheese
Provolone cheese
Swiss cheese
Muenster cheese

Directions:
Combine butter and olive oil to large soup pot
Once butter has melted, add onions.
Sauté onions until translucent.
Salt and pepper onions.
Continue to sauté onions until caramelized.
Sprinkle in the thyme and let cook for 2- 3 minutes.
Add wine and stir to deglaze the onion love from the bottom of the pot.
Let cook for about 2-3 minutes.
Add beef broth.
Bring soup up to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium and let simmer for at least 20 minutes.
Ladel into oven safe bowls.
Add crusty bread (you can toast it but I don't)
Top with whatever cheese combo you like.
Broil until bubbly.
