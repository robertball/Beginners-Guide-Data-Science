Title:
Mom's Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked noodles;
2 cans tuna;
1 cup milk;
2 cups peas;
1/2 cup onion, cooked and chopped;
2 cans condensed cream of mushroom soup;
1/4 cup breadcrumbs & butter

Directions:
Prheat oven to 375. Mix everything except breadcrumbs in a casserole dish. Bake until bubbly, about 25 min. Top with buttery breadcrumbs and bake about 5 minutes more.
