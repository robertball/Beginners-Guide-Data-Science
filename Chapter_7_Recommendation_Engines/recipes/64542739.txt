Title:
Spicy Korean Green Onion Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch Thinly sliced green onion
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp raw sugar
1 Tbsp sesame oil
1 Tbsp sesame seeds
2 tsp Korean chili powder (Gochugaru)
2 tsp white vinegar
1 tsp minced garlic

Directions:
Rinse thoroughly the thinly sliced green onion in running cold water for 2 to 3 mins.
Drain/Remove the excess water on the green onion.
Mix the green onion with the dressing lightly but thoroughly. (You'll want to wear a food handling glove).
Serve immediately.
