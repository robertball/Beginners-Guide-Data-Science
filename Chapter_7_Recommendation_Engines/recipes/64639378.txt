Title:
Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4510131.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 T. coconut oil
1 lb. ground chicken
2 large onions
3 large stalks celery
1 large can petite diced tomatoes
1 can Navy beans, optional
1 qt. chicken stock
1 T. chili powder
1/4 t. chipotle chili powder
1 T. cumin powder
sea salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown chicken well in coconut oil, breaking up like ground beef.
Add onions and celery and continue sauteing.
Add spices, tomatoes and stock. Bring to a boil.
Simmer for 1 hour.
Taste for salt and pepper.
