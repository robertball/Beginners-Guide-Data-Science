Title:
Slow Cooker Cranberry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/773236.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (16 ounce) bottle Catalina salad dressing
1 (14.5 ounce) can whole berry cranberry sauce
1 envelope onion soup mix

Directions:
Place the chicken breasts in the bottom of a slow cooker. Pour the salad dressing, cranberry sauce, and onion soup mix over the chicken.
Cook on Low 4 to 6 hours.

Submitter: 
momof2boys
Description: 
This is a delicious and easy way to prepare chicken breasts.  I like to serve the cooked sauce over mashed potatoes or rice.
