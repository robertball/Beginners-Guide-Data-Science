Title:
Low Sodium Vegetable Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package pretty pasta shapes
6 ounces cheddar cheese, cubed
1/2 medium red onion, chopped
1 large cucumber, chopped
3/4 cup chopped yellow bell pepper
1 (2.5) can sliced, pitted black olives, drained
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
1 cup chopped broccoli
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
1/2 cup shredded carrots
1/2 bunch green onions, sliced
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon Mrs. Dash
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon ground mustard seed
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta, and cook according to package directions, until al dente. Drain, and rinse with cold water.
In a large bowl, mix the cooled pasta with all of the salad ingredients.
In a jar with a lid, mix the olive oil, vinegar, garlic, Mrs.Dash, oregano, ground mustard, salt, and pepper. Seal jar, and shake well.
Pour the dressing mixture over the pasta salad, and toss to coat. Cover, and chill 8 hours in the refrigerator.
