Title:
Fruit Punch Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1139146.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup fresh blueberries
1 cup fresh green grapes, some halved and some whole
1 peach, largely diced
1/2 Granny Smith apple, largely diced
8 strawberries, largely diced
Juice of 1/2 fresh squeezed orange

Directions:
Mix together first 5 ingredients in a medium bowl.
Squeeze orange over mixture, then stir.
Serve immediately, or store overnight.

Submitter: 
Synexx
Description: 
This fruit salad tastes EXACTLY like that fake, red fruit punch mix, only with really healthy, delicious ingredients!
