Title:
Dijon Crusted Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mayonnaise with Olive Oil
1 tablespoon prepared Dijon-style mustard
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup dry panko
1 teaspoon  grated Parmesan cheese or other
4 (4 ounce) fillets halibut
1/2 tablespoon margarine, melted
1/4 cup dry panko
1 teaspoon grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking sheet or use Reynolds Parchment Paper lining for quick cleanup.
In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, mustard, horseradish, and lemon juice. Stir in 1/4 cup panko and 1 teaspoon Parmesan cheese or other. Arrange fish fillets on the prepared baking sheet. Spread crumb mixture lightly and evenly over fish.
In a small bowl, mix together melted margarine, 1/4 cup panko, and 1 teaspoon Parmesan cheese or other. Sprinkle over the coated halibut.
Bake for 15 to 18 minutes, or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
