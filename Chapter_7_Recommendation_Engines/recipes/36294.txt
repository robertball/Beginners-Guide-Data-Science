Title:
The Works Steak Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981675.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 1/2 teaspoons McCormick速 Grill Mates速 Montreal Steak Seasoning
2 tablespoons ketchup
4 slices bacon, crisp-cooked and crumbled
1/2 teaspoon McCormick速 Dill Weed
1 medium onion, cut into 1/2 inch slices
4 slices American cheese
4 hamburger rolls
Lettuce
Tomato slices

Directions:
In a bowl, mix together ground beef, Montreal Steak Seasoning, ketchup, bacon and dill weed.  Shape into four patties.
Brush onion slices with oil.  Place patties and onion slices on grill over medium heat.  Grill 8-12 minutes or until burgers are well-done (160 degrees F internal temperature) and onion is tender, turning once.
Add cheese slices to each burger one minute before cooking is completed.  Place on hamburger rolls.  Top with lettuce and tomato.
