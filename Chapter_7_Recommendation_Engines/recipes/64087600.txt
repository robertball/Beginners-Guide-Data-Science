Title:
Smoked Bacon Wrapped Scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 dozen medium sea scallops
Olive oil
Bone Dust to die for
1 lb of thin sliced bacon
Skewers
Hickory wood chips

Directions:
In ziploc bag, apply olive oil and rub. Shake to cover
Let sit for 30 mins
Set bacon in strips on grill and broil gently to somewhat cook.
Roll scallops and pin with skewer.
Apply more rub.
Place on rack and install heat probe
Heat smoker using Hickory chips
Put racks in smoker when smoke starts
When temp reaches 145f remove
