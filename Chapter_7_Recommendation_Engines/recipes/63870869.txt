Title:
Spicy Goat Cheese Aioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz soft goat cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tsp minced garlic
2 tsp lemon juice
1 TBSP Dijon mustard, such as Grey Poupon
2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp chili powder
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise & dijon mustard together in a nonreactive bowl.
Soften the goat cheese in the microwave for about 15 seconds.  Immediately add it to the bowl, and mix until smooth.
Add remaining ingredients and stir until smooth.

Submitter: 
June Brown
Description: 
I couldn't find a recipe for a good spicy goat cheese aioli, so I came up with my own.  It went over really well.
