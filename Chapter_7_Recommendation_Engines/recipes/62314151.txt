Title:
Slow Cooker Turkey Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 pound) bone-in turkey breast
1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
1 teaspoon sage
4 red potatoes
1 Sm bag baby carrots
1 can chicken broth

Directions:
Rinse the turkey breast and pat dry. Cut off any excess skin, but leave the skin covering the breast. Rub onion soup mix and sage all over outside of the turkey and under the skin. Place potatoes, carrots and broth in the slow cooker. Place turkey breast on vegtables, cover and cook on High for 1 hour, then set to Low, and cook for 7 hours.

Submitter: 
Charles Millwood Jr.
Description: 
Quick and easy way to cook turkey in the slow cooker. With only two ingredients, the only hard part is waiting.Modified from: Slow Cooker Turkey Breast
