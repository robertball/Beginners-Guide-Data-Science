Title:
Ginger Lemon Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 oz of vodka (suggested sky)
1 oz ginger simple syrup (recipe below)
3/4 oz fresh squeezed lemon
sugar for rim
Ginger Simple syrup
6 inches of ginger peeled and diced into cubes
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 cups of water

Directions:
in saucepan bring ginger, sugar and water to a rolling boil.  Let simmer for 3 minutes, stir the whole time.  Let chill and strain.  Can be refrigerated for up to one month
Dip rim of martini glass in lemon juice and then in sugar to coat rim.
In martini shaker, mix vodka, ginger syrup and fresh lemon with ice.  Shake well.  Strain into martini glass.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Colleen Kain
Description: 
A refreshing summer cocktail, easy to whip up, this is sure to delight any guest.  A new twist on a classic cocktail!
