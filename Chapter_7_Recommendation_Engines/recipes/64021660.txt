Title:
CATALINA SALAD DRESSING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 cup onion, grated
1 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. Worchestershire sauce
1 cup vegetable oil
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In the bowl of a food processor, combine the ketchup, sugar, red wine vinegar, grated onion, paprika and Worchestershire sauce; pulse until well blended. With the processor running, slowly trickle in the oil until amalgamated. Taste for salt and pepper.
Cover and refrigerate 4 hours or overnight for the best flavor. Whisk before serving. Keep the dressing in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.

Submitter: 
lgmarge
Description: 
This is a recipe that was popular in the 1960-1970's that I hear is making a comeback. I love it drizzled on a cottage cheese and lettuce salad.
