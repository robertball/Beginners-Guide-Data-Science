Title:
Red Chile and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of hamburger
1 can of pinto beans-do not drain
2 can of red kidney beans-do not drain
1 can of white kidney beans-do not drain
1 cup of chopped sweet onion
1/2 cup of green chile (6 chiles) or 2 cans chopped green chile
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 tsp. onion powder
2-3 garlic cloves minced
1/2 tsp. cumin
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. red pepper
2 boxes(42 oz.) of low sodium chicken broth 16 cups
2 cans of diced tomatoes blended

Directions:
Chop onions and chile. Blend tomatoes in a blender. Set aside.
In a large pot, fry onions and ground beef together. Drain grease.
Add seasonings, chile, chicken broth and tomatoes.
Bring to a boil.
Turn down heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
Add beans, simmer for 10 more minutes

Submitter: 
Lela
Description: 
A Red Chile with pinto beans, red kidney beans and white kidney beans. Serve with cornbread or flour tortillas.
