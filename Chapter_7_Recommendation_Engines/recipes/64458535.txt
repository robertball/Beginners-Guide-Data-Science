Title:
Easy Baked Zucchini Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 zucchini, thinly sliced
1 tablespoon garlic-flavored olive oil
sea salt  and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 225 degrees.
Place sliced zucchini in a bowl. Drizzle oil over zucchini, sprinkle with salt and pepper and toss to coat.
Spread zucchini onto a baking sheet, and sprinkle with paprika
Bake for 2 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
Thinly sliced zucchini are tossed in olive oil and baked in this quick and easy zucchini chip recipe that adults and kids will love.Modified from: Easy Baked Zucchini Chips
