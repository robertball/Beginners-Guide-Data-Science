Title:
Fettucine with Green Peas&Ham in coconut sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups Fettucine pasta
2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
1/3 cup diced onion
1 clove garlic(chopped)
1 sprig thyme(chopped)
1Tbsp. chopped escallion
1 Tbsp. white wine
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/2 cup milk
11/2 cups coconut milk
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1/3 cup diced green pepper
1/4 cup diced tomatoes
1/3 cup diced red pepper
1 cup diced ham
1 cup green peas
1/2 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
1.Place pasta in boiling water and stir frequently with a pair of thongs until pasts is nearly tender.Rinse with cold water,drain and set aside.
2.Heat vegetable oil in a medium skillet and saute onion,garlic,thyme and escallion.
3.Add white wine,salt,black pepper,milk,coconut milk and parmesan cheese to the sauteed seasonings and allow to simmer.
4.Add diced peppers,tomatoes and green peas,ham,cooked pasta and stir until pasta gets hot.
5.Sprinkle on black pepper and serve hot.
