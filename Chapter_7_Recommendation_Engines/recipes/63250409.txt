Title:
Potato Crunchy Tenders for two Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Olive oil for frying
3/4 cups milk
1 egg
1 (7.6 ounce) package garlic flavored instant mashed potatoes  (Divided - half for Mashed potatoes and half for the chicken
1 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoons ground black pepper
3/4 pounds chicken tenders (6-8 tenders)
1 tablespoon flour

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
While the oil is heating, beat the milk and egg together in a bowl.
In another bowl, stir together the 1/2 of the instant mashed potatoes, salt, and pepper.  To use for Breading.
Mix up the other half according to directions (be sure to use on 1/2 the liquid.)
Stir the chicken tenders with the milk mixture to coat thoroughly, then shake off excess milk and dip each tender into the potato flakes. Place the breaded tenders into the hot oil, and fry until golden brown, 7 to 10 minutes. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
Mix 1 Tablespoon Flour  1/4 t salt and 1/4 t pepper to taste.   Blend into the milk mixture and fry it into the pan used to make gravy.
Now you have Chicken, mashed potatoes and gravy.  Add a vegetable and a salad and you have a great meal!!
Had 8 tenders and 4 left over with 3/4 pound of chicken.

Submitter: 
Donna Ruark
