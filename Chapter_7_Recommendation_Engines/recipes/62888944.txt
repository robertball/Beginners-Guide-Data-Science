Title:
Midwestern Cucumber Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/697587.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of regular sour creme
1 table spoon of minced garlic
1/2 large cucumber
1 tablespoon of Oregano
1 teaspoon of salt

Directions:
Take a large cucumber and cut it in half. Discard the other half. Take the remaining half , peel and dice the cucumber very finely. Take your salt and season the cucumber in a bowl. Let stand for about 10 minutes to extract the natural flavor of the cucumber. Once time has passed add 1 tablespoon of oregano, and 1 tablespoon of garlic. Stir the mixture for about 30-45 seconds until well blended. Finally add the cup of sour creme. Stir again for 30 seconds until all ingredients are combined. Refrigerate for 20 minutes and serve!

A light take on traditional cucumber sauce using basic household ingredients.
