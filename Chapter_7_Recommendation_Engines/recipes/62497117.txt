Title:
Cherry Chip Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 1/2 cups cake flour
2 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
12 Tbsp. (1 1/2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
8 Tbsp. (1/2 cup) vegetable shortening
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 large eggs, room temperature
3 large egg whites, room temperature
3/4 cup milk, room temperature
1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
1 tsp. pure almond extract
1 cup mini semi sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup (about 30) maraschino cherries, stemmed, drained and chopped
Chocolate Frosting:
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
1/4 cup Dutch-processed coco powder
3 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar

Directions:
For the Cake:
Pre heat oven to 350 degrees.
Butter a 9x13 inch cake pan.
Combine the flour, baking powder and salt in a bowl and set aside.
In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with a paddle, cream the butter and shortening on medium speed for 2 minutes. Add the sugar and beat for 1 minute or until light and creamy. Scrape the inside surface of the bowl.
Whisk together the eggs, egg whites, milk, vanilla and almond extracts in a separate bowl.
Reduce the mixture speed to low and pour 1/3 of the egg mixture into the bowl and mix to combine. Add 1/3 of the flour mixture and mix to combine. Repeat the process two more times ending with the flour mixture. Scrape the inside surface of the bowl and mix for 1 minute, fold in the chips and chopped cherries with as rubber spatula. Pour the cake batter into the prepared pan and spread the batter evenly. Bake on middle rack for 35 to 40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool in pan on wire rack, till completely cool.
For the Frosting:
In heavy saucepan, combine the butter, milk, vanilla and coco powder, heat over medium heat, stirring continuously, until the mixture is smooth. Remove the pan from the heat. Whisk in the confectioners' sugar 1/2 cup at a time, mix until smooth after each addition. Cool and frost the cake.

Celeste
Description: 
Maraschino cherries and mini chocolate chips make for a great cake! I got this recipe from a Calphalon cake pan insert.
