Title:
Tex-Mex  Restaurant Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes
1 jalapeno, seeded
1/4-1/3 C white onion, diced
1 Tsp granulated garlic
3 TBS Cilantro
1 Tsp Cumin powder
salt and pepper

Directions:
Pulse all ingredients in a food processor until well blended.  Check for salt flavor and adjust as needed.  Chill about 1 hour if you can wait that long.

Submitter: 
Swanee Speed
Description: 
A west Texas original.  Just like the Tex-Mex joints we love.  Not too chunky.  Canned tomatoes-no way  Yes way!  That's how the restaurants get that same dependable flavor year-round.
