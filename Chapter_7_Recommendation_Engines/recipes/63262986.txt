Title:
Classic Caramel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Brown Sugar
1 Cup Butter or Margarine
1/2 Cup Corn Syrup
1/2 Tsp Salt
1 Tsp Baking Soda
6 Quarts Popped Corn

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200F.
Mix the sugar, butter, corn syrup, and salt together in a large sauce pan. Boil for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the baking soda.
Pour the liquid over popcorn, then stir until evenly coated.
Spread the coated popcorn onto baking sheets and bake at 200F for one hour, stirring every 15 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
A classically sweet and crunchy caramel corn recipe passed along through my family. A favorite treat around Halloween, and any time you feel like something sweet.
