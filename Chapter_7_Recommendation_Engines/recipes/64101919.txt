Title:
Sweet Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Medium Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
1 tsp salt
2 tsp pepper
1 tbsp Dried Basil
1 tbsp Olive Oil
5 Garlic Cloves (Minced)
4 tbsp brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 400 Degrees F.
Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil paper and spray with non-stick spray.
Season chicken with salt, pepper and dried basil (Both sides).
In a medium sauce pan, heat olive oil till shimmering.
Add in minced garlic and cook for 2 minutes until soft and aromatic.
remove oil from heat and stir in brown sugar till smooth.
evenly spread brown sugar mixture over each chicken breast.
place on foil and bake till juices run clear (30-40 minutes).
