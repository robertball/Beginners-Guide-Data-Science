Title:
Tuna Patties My Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 5oz cans tuna in water drained
1 egg
15 crushed saltine crackers
1 TBSP Dijon mustard
1 TSP Old Bay seasoning
1/8 TSP black pepper
2 chopped green onions
1/2 chopped small red pepper
3/4 cups panko bread crumbs
1 TBSP butter
1 TBSP olive oil

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl except panko, butter and olive oil. Cover and place in refrigerator for at least 2 hours. Melt butter and olive oil in pan on medium heat. Put panko onto a plate. Shape tuna mixture into 2 patties and press both sides into panko bread crumbs. Fry one side until golden brown, flip, fry other side until golden.

Submitter: 
Party Chef
Description: 
You want to refrigerate these so the flavors have time to meld and the patties hold their shape when frying.
