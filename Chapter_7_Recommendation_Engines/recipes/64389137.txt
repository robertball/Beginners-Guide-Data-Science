Title:
Spicy Creamed Spinach Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2222475.jpg
Ingredients:
12 tablespoons egg white
1 large egg
1 package frozen creamed spinach
2 tablespoons of salsa
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp onion powder
1/4 cup sliced jalapenos

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Crack the large egg into a medium sized bowl, along with the egg whites.
Sprinkle in salt, pepper, and onion powder. Add the salsa.
Next, place thawed creamed spinach into mixture and stir well.
Pour the mixture into a lightly greased pan. Even out mixture with a spoon
Finally, place jalapeno slices on top.
