Title:
Easy Southwest Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979112.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) package Lawry's® Taco Spices & Seasonings
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 tablespoon Bertolli ® Classico™ Olive Oil
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves

Directions:
In small bowl, combine Taco Spices & Seasonings, lime juice and oil; mix thoroughly.  Brush mixture on both sides of chicken.  Grill or broil chicken 10 to 15 minutes, or until thoroughly cooked, turning halfway through cooking time.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
Amplify chicken with the flavors of the Southwest. All you need is a little taco seasoning and a dash of lime juice. Cook on the grill or in the broiler.
