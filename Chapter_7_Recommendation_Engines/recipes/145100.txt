Title:
Baseball Antipasto Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981246.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head Tanimura & Antle® Iceberg Lettuce
1/2 cup zesty Italian dressing
4 slices provolone cheese
1/2 cup chopped roasted red pepper
1/2 cup chopped salami

Directions:
Core lettuce; rinse and drain well. Cut crosswise into 4 even slices.
Drizzle lettuce slices with dressing, as desired. Top each with 1 cheese slice.  Combine red pepper and salami; arrange on top of cheese to resemble the stitching on a baseball.

Submitter: 
Tanimura & Antle®
Description: 
A mix of crisp lettuce, cheese, salami, and red pepper come together in an easy, baseball-inspired salad.
