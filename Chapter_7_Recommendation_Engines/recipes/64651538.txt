Title:
Chicken Sausage and Sweet Potato Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4540261.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 pounds sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into (1-inch) cubes (30 cubes)
3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
1 teaspoons sea salt
1/2 – 3/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
6 chicken or pork sausage (hot or sweet) cut in 1/2 inch pieces (30 pieces)
30 decorative wooded picks
Creamy Cilantro Dip

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450°.
Line a baking sheet with foil and brush with olive oil or melted coconut oil. Add in sweet potato cubes then drizzle with 2 tablespoons olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
Bake for 18-20 minutes, turning after 10 minutes.
In a large sauté pan over medium-high heat add 1 tablespoon olive oil. Add in sausage and cook for about 7 minutes. Until each side is browned. Place on a paper towel lined plate.
To assemble; Take you wooden pick and add a sweet potato square and a sausage slice. Repeat until finished.
Serve with Creamy Cilantro Dip.

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
The other night I made plantain chips with a creamy cilantro dip. I thought this chicken sausage and sweet potato squares recipe would also go great with the same dip! This chicken sausage and sweet potato squares is the perfect paleo recipe as an appetizer for your next party!
