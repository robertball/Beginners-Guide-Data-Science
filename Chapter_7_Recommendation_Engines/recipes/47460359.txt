Title:
Portuguese Sweet Bread I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk (slightly warm)
1 egg (room temp)
2 tablespoons softened butter
1/3 cup white sugar
3/4 teaspoon salt
3 cups bread flour
2 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Mix ingredients together. Place in glass mixing bowl with teaspoon of veg oil. Place in warm place until doubles in size approx 45 minutes. Punch down. Place in loaf pan and let rise again. for 20-30 minutes.
Bake at 350 degrees for approx 25-30 minutes.

Description: 
This recipe is for the bread machine. It is similar to homemade grandmother's sweet bread.Modified from: Portuguese Sweet Bread I
