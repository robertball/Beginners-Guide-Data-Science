Title:
Big Ray's Almond Joy Brownie Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1037341.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups sugar
1/2 cup cocoa powder
1 1/4 cup flaked coconut
1 tsp almond extract
3/4 cup coarsely chopped whole almonds
1 1/4 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix the coconut with the almond extract.
Layer ingredients in order given in a 1-quart wide-mouth canning jar, wiping down jar after the cocoa powder.  Press each layer firmly in place before adding next ingredient.
Attach this to the Jar: "Almond Joy Brownies: Makes 2 dozen brownies".  Empty jar of brownie mix into large mixing bowl. Use your hands to thoroughly blend mix. Add: 3/4 cup butter or margarine, melted; 4 eggs, slightly beaten. Mix until completely blended. Spread batter in a greased 9" x 13" baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Cool completely in pan. Cut into 2-inch squares.
