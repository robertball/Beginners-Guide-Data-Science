Title:
Pistachio-pineapple pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz can crushed pineapple
small box FF pistachio instant pudding
cool whip
graham cracker crust

Directions:
Beat together pineapple & pudding mix. Add 1 3/4cup cool whip.
Pour into crust. top with cool whip.
Referigerate for 2 hours before serving
