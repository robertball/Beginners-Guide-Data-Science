Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060086.jpg
Ingredients:
1 28oz can of petit Diced tomatos with juice
1 16 oz can of Tomato Sauce
1 16 oz can of chili beans
1 16 oz can of red kidney beans
1 16 oz can of red kidney beens pureed
1 lb Hot Sausage browned
1 lb ground Sirloin browned
6 cloves minced garlic
1 package of Mcormics Chilli seasoning
1 large onion chopped
1 cup of Coke a Cola (NOT DIET)
1 bay leaf
1 tbls Chili Powder
Tabasco to taste

Directions:
Brown the Sausage and Sirloin draining off all the fat. In a large pot or slow cooker combine all ingredients stir and bring to a slow simmer. Cook on low for at least 4-5 hours or longer. The longer the better
