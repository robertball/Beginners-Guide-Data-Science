Title:
Five Decker Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lb Meat Loaf Mix
1 egg
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 cup minced onion
1/2 cup Katsup
6 to 8 Carrots, sliced
4 Russet Potatoes, sliced
1 onion, sliced
1 lb bacon
salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Spray 9 X 13 dish with non-stick spray.
Layer bacon in bottom of dish.  Preheat oven to 350.
Cover with potatoes, onion, and carrot.
Mix together meatloaf mix, egg, bread crumbs, onion, and katsup.  Put on top of vegetables. (Spread with extra katsup, if desired).
Bake on bottom rack of oven for 45 minutes.
Let sit for 10, then serve.
