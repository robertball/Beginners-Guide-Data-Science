Title:
Perfectly dry roasted Chickpeas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3270242.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can Chickpeas, rinsed, and dried thoroughly
1 teaspoon of your favourite seasonings combined. (sea salt, pepper, garlic powder, chili powder, Chipotle, cayenne pepper, curry powder, lemon pepper.. Etc..)

Directions:
Rinse one can of chickpeas and let air dry on paper towel for about 45 minutes to an hour. Or pat dry, thoroughly
Place chickpeas on a parchment lined baking sheet
Place sheet in oven and heat oven to 400
Cook chick peas for 40-50 minutes, shaking pan every 10 minutes.
Remove from oven and toss hot chickpeas with your seasoning blend.
Turn oven off and return on to oven. Let the chickpeas sit in oven till completely cool for a couple hours or overnight.
Enjoy!
Can be stored at room temperature in a container.. Stays crispy for days.. If they last that long.

Submitter: 
Chef V
Description: 
Roasting these without oil or salts first let them crisp up perfectly. Letting them cool in the oven is crucial to the crunch factor. Season with your favourite flavours .
