Title:
Calabacitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Corn (Fresh or Frozen)
2 Cups Diced Onions (Red or Yellow)
2 Cups Diced Red Bell Pepper
2 Cups Diced Green Zucchini
1/4 Cup Butter
Salt to Taste
Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Heat pan to medium high and brown the corn. Add onions and cook till translucent. Reduce heat to medium and add the rest of the ingredients. Cook until bell pepper and zucchini are desired softness.
