Title:
Purple Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head small red cabbage, cored and thinly sliced into bite-sized shreds
1 large carrot, shredded
1/2 onion thinly sliced
1 red, yellow, or green bell pepper, thinly sliced
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon celery seed
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
1. Combine the vegetables in a large bowl.
2. Whisk the remaining ingredients together until smooth. Pour over the vegetables and toss to combine. Season with kosher salt and pepper.
3. Chill for at least an hour. Serve.

Submitter: 
Tim Wilkerson
Description: 
A colorful, summery accompaniment for lots of dishes. I generally serve this with burgers, like my Nothing-matters-any-more-and-what-if-it-did Burger.
