Title:
Cranberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/742170.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 Tbsp baking powder
1 cup sugar
2 eggs, slightly beaten
3 Tbsp vegetable oil
1 cup orange juice
grated peel of one orange
1 1/2 cups fresh cranberries, halved

Directions:
Grease and flour a large loaf pan.
Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
Add eggs, oil, orange juice, orange peel and cranberries.
Mix well.
Pour into loaf pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 60-70 minutes (use toothpick test).
Allow it to cool ten minutes before removing from pan.

A not-too-sweet version of traditional cranberry bread.
