Title:
Paula Deans English Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14Tbsp Butter
1C sugar
2Tbsp cold water
1/2C pecans
1tsp Vanilla
dash salt
16oz chocolate chips

Directions:
Generously butter a cookie sheet. Put butter,sugar and water in a heavy pan or skillet over medium high heat. Bring to a bubbling boil, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon, about 10min.
Remove spoon from pan and cook to a very brittle stage.(300-310)on candy thermomater.
Remove from heat and add nuts to mixture. Add vanilla and salt. Pour onto prepared cookie sheet and spread to 1/4 in thickness. Cool slightly, top with chocolate chips or chocolate bars and spread as it melts. Cool completley and break into peices. Store in a airtight container.
