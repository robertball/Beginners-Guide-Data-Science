Title:
Creamy Chipotle Lime Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1 med onion, diced OR 3/4 cup frozen diced onion
1 6 oz can tomato paste
1 15 oz can petite diced tomatoes
1 15 oz can black beans, rinsed
1 15 oz can dark red kidney beans, rinsed
2 Tbsp Chili powder
Southwest Chipotle seasoning (I use Mrs Dash)
2 Tbsp lime juice or equivalent
1 Tbsp Oregano
1 Tbsp sugar
1/2 cup sour cream
1 1/2 c water
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large dutch oven, brown the ground beef and diced onion over med heat
Mix in the tomato paste and water.
Add all remaining ingredients except the sour cream.
Cook over med heat until 160 degrees through, about 10 minutes, stirring well.
Remove from heat and mix in sour cream.
Serve with tortilla chips or cornbread.

Submitter: 
KY Piano Teacher
Description: 
A creamy, smoky, spicy chili with a kick of chipotle and lime.  Quick and easy to make, always a crowd pleaser!
