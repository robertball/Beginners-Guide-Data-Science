Title:
New England Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head cabbage, chopped
16 ounces kielbasa, diced
6 potatoes, chopped
1 cup beer
3 tbsp Dijon mustard
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together the cabbage, kielbasa, potatoes and water. Transfer to a 9x13 inch baking dish and  bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.
Cover with aluminum foil

Submitter: 
Adrienne
Description: 
A great staple casserole!  You can play with many different seasonings to make it your own.  I personally chose to add dijon mustard with just a little salt and pepper.Modified from: New England Dinner
