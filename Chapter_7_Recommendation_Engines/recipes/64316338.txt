Title:
BlackBerry Butter Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2001781.jpg
Ingredients:
12 tart Shells (pre-made or homemade)
1 cup Blackberries
3/4 Cup brown sugar
1/3 cup Honey
1 egg
2 Tablespoons of melted butter
1 lemon zested
1 Tablespoon Vanilla
Pinch of Salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 and place tart shells on to tray
In a small bowl add washed and dried Blackberries and the zest of 1 lemon and mash with fork, Set aside
In another bowl whisk together egg, brown sugar, honey, butter, vanilla and pinch of salt.
Divide the blackberries evenly in the bottoms of tart shells.
Spoon butter Tart filling over top the Blackberries.
Bake in oven for 22-27 mins, depending on how gooey you like your tarts.

Submitter: 
Chef V
Description: 
Its like a butter tart and fruit tart married up. The bitterness of the Blackberries compliments the sweetness of the butter Tart.
