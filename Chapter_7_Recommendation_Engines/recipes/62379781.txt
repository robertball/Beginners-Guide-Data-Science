Title:
Kuhlua Chocolate Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T butter, softened
1 1b powdered sugar (3 1/2 - 4 c)
3 T. unsweetened cocoa powder
6 T. Kahlua
1 t. instant espresso powder or 1 Starbucks VIA packet

Directions:
In a large bowl, crean together butter and powdered sugar.  Add cocoa powder. Kahlua and instant coffee powder. Beat until smooth.
Frost Chocolate Kahlua with frosting - be generous as there is plenty of frosting.
Decorate with chocolate shot or shaved chocolate
