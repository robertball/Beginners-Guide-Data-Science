Title:
Corn Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs
1 15.25 oz can corn kernels, drained
2 14.75 oz cans creamed corn
½ cup butter, melted
½ cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
½ cup sugar
¼ cup corn starch
1 tablespoon dried minced onion
1 ½ teaspoons seasoned salt
½ teaspoon nutmeg
½ teaspoon dried ground mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Beat eggs in 2.5 liter casserole.  Add corn, creamed corn, melted butter, milk, and vanilla.  Stir to combine. In a separate bowl, combine sugar, corn starch, dried onion, seasoned salt, nutmeg, and ground mustard.  Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients all at once, and stir to combine.  Bake in 400 degree oven for 1 hour, stirring after 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
ZChef
Description: 
This corn pudding has been a big hit with our family for several years.  We hope that you and your family enjoy it as well.  It is a great side dish with chicken, turkey, pork chops, or ham.
