Title:
Butter Koek Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. of Butter
1 cup of sugar
1 egg
1 tsp almond extract
2 cups of flour
Milk to brush the top with
Slivered almonds if you desire

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar with a spoon.
Add in the egg and almond extract.
Add 2 cups of flour
Place in a 9 inch greased pie dish.
Brush with milk.(You may sprinkle it with slice almonds)
Bake at 300 degrees for 45-50 minutes in a pie plate.
Cut into 16 pieces

Cindy Yonkman
