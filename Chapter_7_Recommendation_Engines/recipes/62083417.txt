Title:
Granny's Ice Cream II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 egg whites
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup vanilla yogurt

Directions:
1. Beat the egg whites until light and fluffy in a glass bowl.
2. Carefully add the sugar, until peaks form.
3. Add the yogurt, stirring carefully.
4. Put into freezer, stirring every 30 minutes until frozen.

Submitter: 
Blayde
Description: 
I revised the granny's ice cream, using vanilla yogurt instead of sour cream, making it more creamy and less sour. It is still very versatile and easy.
