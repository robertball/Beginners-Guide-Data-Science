Title:
Nando's Hamburger soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs gr beef
I med onion (chopped)
3 cloves garlic (chopped)
4 carrots (chopped)
3 sticks of celery (chopped)
2 c water
3 cans beef broth
1 can tomato soup
2 bay leafs
3 tblsp parsley
1/2 tsp thyme
8 tblsp pearl barley

Directions:
Brown meat, onion, and garlic. Drain fat. Mix remaining  ingredients, and simmer 1 1/2, or slow cooker.

Submitter: 
Fernando D'Agostino
Description: 
Quick and easy yummy soup, great for cold winter days.
