Title:
cheese grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042681.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups boiling water
1 cup quick cook grits
1 1/2 tsp salt
6 tbs butter or marg.
1 1/2 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 tsp hot sauce
1.25 cups of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 tbs sherry
1 egg beaten

Directions:
Combine the boiling h2o, grits and salt in a saucepan; mix well
Cook covered over low heat for 5 mins or until most of the water is absorbed.
Stir in the butter, worcestershire, hot sauce, 1 cup of cheese and sherry. Fold in the
egg until mixed.
Spoon into a greased 2 1/2 qt baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining cheese
Bake a 300 for 1 hr. or until bubbly.
Note- this may be prepared 1 day in advance and refrigerate. Bring to room
temperature and bake just before serving.
