Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 beaten egg
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup cooked white rice
1/2 cup crushed croutons
7 saltine crackers crushed
1/8 tsp. red pepper flakes
1/8 tsp. dill weed
1/8 tsp. thyme
1/8 tsp. minced garlic
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/8 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1/2 cup ketchup

Directions:
Place thawed ground beed in large bowl then add cooked rice,crushed croutons, crushed crackers, red pepper flakes,dill weed, thyme, minced garlic, and onion mix well. Add 1 beaten egg, water, and salt and pepper and blend really well. Place mixture into greased meatloaf pan. Bake at 350 for 50 minutes. Add a layer of ketchup for the last 10 minutes of baking. Serve hot.
