Title:
Oil Free Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 ounces chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1 whole lemon, juiced
1 whole garlic clove
2 tbsp vegan mayo (fat-free)
2 tsp water
½ tsp cumin
½ tsp coriander

Directions:
Blend until smooth and creamy.
Add additional water or mayo if necessary and salt to taste.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Low fat oil free hummus. Skips the tahini.
