Title:
Ya-Ya's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28 oz. Can Crushed tomatoes
2 8 oz Cans Tomato paste (roasted garlic flavor is best)
1 Sweet onion minced
3 large cloves garlic sliced thinly
2 bay leaves
1 TBS dried oregano
1&1/2 tsp. dried basil
1/8 - 1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes.
1/8 cup brown sugar.
1/4 tsp.salt

Directions:
Fry onion until translucent.
Add garlic to pan saute until fragrant.  Add crushed tomatoes.  Fill can with water and add water.  Add tomato paste and fill cans with water and add to pan.
Add all spices and sugar.   Simmer as long as you can.  2 hours uncovered and then covered.

Submitter: 
Singer6
Description: 
Vegetarian spaghetti sauce. I love the subtle kick from the pepper flakes.
