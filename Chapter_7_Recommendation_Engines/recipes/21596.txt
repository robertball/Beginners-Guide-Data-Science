Title:
Balalaika Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/820942.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce vodka
1/2 fluid ounce cointreau
1 fluid ounce lemon juice
1 slice lemon, for garnish

Directions:
Pour the vodka and Cointreau in a highball glass with ice. Add enough lemon juice to reach the halfway point of the glass, or to taste. Garnish with a slice of lemon on the outside of glass.

Submitter: 
Leslie A.
Description: 
Vodka and cointreau (a type of orange brandy), with a tart shot of lemon juice, round out this easy Nordic cocktail.
