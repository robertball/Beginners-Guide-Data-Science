Title:
Lemon Dill Crab Cake Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup buttermilk
2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill leaves
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley leaves (or dried)
1 tablespoon grated lemon zest
2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
1 garlic clove, minced

Directions:
1-Combine all of the ingredients in a bowl and stir well.
2-Refrigerate until chilled. The sauce will thicken as it chills.

Submitter: 
Nick Gibietis
Description: 
A nice sauce for any crab cake recipe. Not too over powering and just right.
