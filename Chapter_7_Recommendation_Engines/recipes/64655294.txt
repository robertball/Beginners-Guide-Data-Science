Title:
Bacon Pancetta Dippers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. Flour
1 ½ c. Whole Milk
3 tsp. Baking Powder
½ lb. Bacon or Pancetta
Pinch of Sea Salt
Pinch of Nutmeg
2 Tbsp. Butter, Melted
2 Eggs
Vito & Joe’s Italian Extra Virgin
Olive Oil

Directions:
Heat a cast-iron pan on a stove top.
Add a splash of Vito & Joe’s Italian
Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
Place the pancetta in the pan.
Flip with tongs to
cook evenly.
When cooked, place on a plate covered with a paper towel
and set aside.
Remove the pan from heat.
Mix the flour, salt, baking powder, and nutmeg in a large mixing bowl.
Stir in butter, milk, then eggs, being careful not to over mix.
Let the
mixture rest for 10-15 minutes.
Place the pan back on the stove on medium heat. Coat the bottom with
butter. Place the bacon in the batter and coat heavily. Place in the pan
using tongs. Cook as you would pancakes.
If desired, sprinkle with powdered sugar. Serve with a cup of maple
syrup for dipping.
