Title:
Sausage and Mostaccioli with Rich Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz packaged dried mostaccioli or corkscrew macaroni
12 oz bulk sweet Italian sausage or ground turkey sausage
2 cups sliced fresh shiitake mushrooms
1 small red sweet or green pepper, cut into bite sized pieces
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups whipping cream
2 tblsp snipped fresh basil or 1/2 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
fresh basil leaves (optional)

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions
In a medium skillet saute mushrooms and red/green pepper.
For sauce, in a large skillet brown sausage with onion and garlic.
Stir whipping cream, basil, and black pepper into sausage mixture.  Add mushrooms and red/green pepper.  Cook over medium-low heat for 5 to 8 minutes or till slightly thickened, stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat.  Stir the cheese into the sauce.
Spoon the sauce over pasta.  If desired, garnish with fresh basil leaves.
