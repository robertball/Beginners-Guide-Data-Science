Title:
Aloha Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/268707.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium head green cabbage, rinsed and very thinly sliced
1 large carrot, shredded
1 (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
1 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the cabbage, carrot, pineapple, mayonnaise and salt and let chill for at least an hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Denyse
Description: 
The very best slaw I ever had...sweet and crunchy!  You can also substitute pineapple tidbits or chunks of fresh pineapple, if you wish.
