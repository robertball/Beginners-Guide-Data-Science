Title:
Sugar-Free Chocolate Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz. pkgs. cream cheese, softened
2 1 oz. squares unsweetened chocolate, melted and cooled
Sugar substitute equivalent to 1 cup of sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, chocolate, sugar substitute and vanilla until smooth. Stir in pecans. Pour into an 8 inch square baking pan lined with foil. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Cut into 16 squares. Serve chilled. Yield: 16 servings.
