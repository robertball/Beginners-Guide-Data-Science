Title:
A Berry Purple Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple in juice, undrained
1 (16.5 ounce) can blueberries or blackberries in light syrup, drained
1 1/2 cups ice cubes
1 (6 ounce) container lemon or other fruit-flavored low-fat yogurt

Directions:
In blender container, combine all ingredients; process until smooth.  Serve immediately or cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Canned Food Alliance
Description: 
This smoothie is prepared in just 5 minutes with the use of canned fruit!
