Title:
Harvest Crunch Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/520529.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. heavy cream or half & half
1 c. brown sugar
1 1/4 c. pumpkin puree
1 T. flour
1 tsp. vanilla (or Maple flavoring)
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/2 tsp. allspice
1 tsp. cinnamon
2 eggs
1-9" unbaked pastry shell
Topping:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 cup butter, chilled
1 cup chopped Pecans or Walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F  Mix first 11 ingredients, beat well.  Pour in unbaked pie shell and bake 30 minutes. Mix topping ingredients, sprinkle on top of partially baked pie and return to oven another 15-20 minutes or till pie is set.  Serve with Whipped Cream or Ice Cream.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Root
Description: 
This is our favorite pumpkin pie. Rich, Creamy and Crunchy, it's sure to be a holiday favorite!
