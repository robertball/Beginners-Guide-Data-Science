Title:
(ellenc) White Chocolate Chip Orange Cranberry Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 (3.3 ounce) package white chocolate instant pudding mix
2 teaspoons vanilla, almond, or orange extract
2 eggs
2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup oats
1 cup white chocolate chips
1 cup slivered almonds
1 cup dried orange flavored cranberries (Craisins)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Mix the butter, brown sugar, and pudding mix. Beat until creamy. Add the vanilla and eggs; beat 2 minutes with hand-held mixer on high speed. Stir in flour, baking soda, salt, and oats. Blend in the white chocolate chips, slivered almonds, and Craisins. Dough will be soft.
Drop from teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheets about 1 inch apart; flatten slightly. Bake at 375 degrees F on upper oven rack for 8 minutes. Let cool for 5 to 10 minutes before removing from cookie sheet.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a very versatile recipe; the pudding mix, chips, nuts, and dried fruit can all be switched out for different flavors to put your own spin on the cookies.
