Title:
Simple-Sweet Mexican Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz bag of frozen corn
1-2 TBS of Honey
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp garlic salt
oregano to taste

Directions:
Place corn in a microwave safe dish.  Drizzle honey on top of corn.  Sprinkle corn with cumin, garlic salt and oregano.
Cook on normal frozn vegetable setting or on high power for 3 minutes.  Mix well.  Cook on high again for 3 minutes or until hot and cooked through.
Serve immediately.
