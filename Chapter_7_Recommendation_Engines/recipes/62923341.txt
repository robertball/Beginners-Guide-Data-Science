Title:
Chili & Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart canned tomatoes
2 lbs. lean ground beef
1 whole yellow onion, chopped
1 whole white onion, chopped
3/4 cup McCormick's Chili Seasoning
1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans drained and rinsed
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans drained and rinsed
1 (12 ounce) packages whole wheat angel hair pasta
8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef with chopped onion in skillet. In large pot add tomatoes (juice and all), beans, and chili seasoning. Stir in browned ground beef and onions. Bring to simmer and cook for 30 minutes on medium heat (you can also put this in a crock pot and cook all day long on low!). Cook spaghetti per package directions. Serve chili over spaghetti and top with cheese.

Submitter: 
Tina Pendergraph
Description: 
In Cincinnati, we usually put chili on top of spaghetti!
