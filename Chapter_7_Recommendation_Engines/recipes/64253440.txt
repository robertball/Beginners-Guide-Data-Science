Title:
Crawfish Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick Butter
1 bunch Green Onions, chopped
1/4 lb. fresh Mushrooms, sliced
1 8-oz. package Cream Cheese
2 cans Cream of Potato Soup
2 cans Niblets Corn and liquid from can, DO NOT DRAIN
1 lb. rawfish Tails
1 pint Half-and-Half
Salt, Pepper and Tony Cachere's Cajun Seasoning

Directions:
If crawfish tails are frozen, thaw overnight in refrigerator.
Melt butter in a large pot. Saute green onions and mushrooms in the butter.
Add cream cheese, stirring constantly until cream cheese is melted and incorporated in with the butter, green onion, and mushroom mixture.
Add potato soup, corn (including the reserved liquid) and crawfish.  Mix together well.
Add Half & Half; mix thoroughly. Add salt, pepper, and Tony Cachere's Cajun seasoning to taste.
Heat and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kevin G
Description: 
The rich, creamy chowder marries well with the crawfish.  If you cannot get crawfish tails, you may substitute 1 pound peeled and deveined small shrimp tails.
