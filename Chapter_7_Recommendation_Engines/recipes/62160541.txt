Title:
spinach and sundried tomato lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of lasagna noodles cooked to the package directions
2 cans of spaghetti sauce
1 onion
1 green pepper
1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
1 tablespoon of chopped garlic
3 tablespoons of chopped sundried tomatoes the kind in the oil
1 teaspoon of seasoning salt
1 teaspoon of pepper
4 cups of shredded mozzerella
1/2 cup of parm.cheese
1 egg
32 oz. ricotta cheese...
2 tablespoons of brown sugar
1 package of frozen spinach..thawed out and squeezed dry

Directions:
brown your ground beef with your onion and green pepper...and your garlic
drain
add sauce and brown sugar
simmer for about 15 minutes
meanwhile put your ricotta cheese.. your parm.cheese..your sundried tomatoes..your season salt..pepper..spinach..and egg in a big bowl  and mix well
get a 9x13 baking dish and add 1 cup of your sauce
add 1 layer of noodles
put a decent layer of ricotta mix on the noodles
sprinkle with about 1/2 cup of mozz.cheese
put 1/3 of a cup of more sauce and continue the steps til you run out of ingredients and end in the sauce with 2 cups of mozz. cheese on top...
bake at 350 for about 30 to 45 min or til hot n middle and bubbly...

Submitter: 
Karen Steinke Reilly
Description: 
this is your classic lasagna with a little twist you add sundried tomatoes and spinach to the ricotta cheese ,with a hearty meat sauce.
