Title:
Fish stir up side dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups used fish fry oil
4 cups cornmeal
6 eggs beaten
1/2 cup sliced green onions
salt, pepper, and granulated garlic to to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet. Add cornmeal and cook, stirring constantly, until golden brown.  Add eggs, a little at a time, stirring until all is incorporated.  Add green onions and season to taste.  Serve hot with ketchup.
