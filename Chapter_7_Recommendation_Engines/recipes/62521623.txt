Title:
Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups dry rice
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1/2 can water
1 cup peas (or vegie of choice)
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
spray 9 x 13 pan with pam
mix rice and wet ingredients together and pour in pan
place chicken on top
salt and pepper
cover with foil
cook 350 degrees 1 hour
