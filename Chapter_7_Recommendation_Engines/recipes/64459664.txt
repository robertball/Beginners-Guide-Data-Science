Title:
Crispy Beer Batter Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large onions
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp paprika
1 cup beer
1 tbs vegetable oil

Directions:
Cut the onions crosswise into 1/2 rings. Mix flour and paprika in a large bowl, season with salt & pepper. Whisk in beer until foam subsides. Lightly cook 2 baking dishes with cooking spray. Warm the oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add onion rings to batter. Using tongs, lift onions out of batter a few at a time allowing excess batter to drip off. Place onion , single layer in skillet and fry for 1 minute on each side until browned. Transfer to baking sheet and bake at 425 for 8 minutes, or until crisp.
