Title:
No Flour Pancakes (or Waffles) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
1 cup egg substitute (or 4 eggs)
1 cup non-fat cottage cheese
1/2 cup sugar substitute (or sugar)
1/2 Tbsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

Directions:
Put everything, except blueberries, in a food processor or blender and process until almost smooth.  Transfer to a bowl, and mix in blueberries.
Drop by 1/4 cups onto medium-hot skillet.  Flip when underside is golden brown, and bubbles are forming on top.
Minus the blueberries, makes a great waffle recipe!

Submitter: 
Bette
Description: 
A great alternative for all us dieters out there.  The blueberries add great flavor.
