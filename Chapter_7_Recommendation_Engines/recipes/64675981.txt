Title:
Barbeque Chicken/Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
1/4 cup Worchestershire sauce
1/2 cup catsup
2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon sugar
2 tablespoons vinegar
2 cloves garlic, pressed
1 medium onion, chopped
A few drops red hot sauce
Salt/pepper

Directions:
Combine sauce
Grill:  baste often for the last few minutes of cooking
Oven:  Pour over chicken that has been browned
Cover roaster and bake in moderate over until tender
