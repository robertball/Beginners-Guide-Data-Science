Title:
Romaine Salad with Mandarin and Sliced Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sliced almonds
1/4 cup sugar
8 cups torn romaine lettuce
1 can (11 oz) mandarin orange segments, drained
2 Tbsp thinly sliced scallions
Dressing
1/4 cup canola oil
2 Tbsp each honey and cider vinegar
1 Tbsp chopped parsley

Directions:
Put a square of foil on work surface. Stir  sugar in a small saucepan over medium heat 5 minutes until most sugar melts and starts to turn golden. Gently stir in nuts to coat. Pour on foil to cool.
Put 2 cups lettuce in each of 4 salad bowls. Top with oranges and scallions. Break clumps of almonds; add to salads.
Dressing: Put ingredients in a small jar and shake to blend; drizzle over salads.
