Title:
Cinnamon Creme Anglaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups low-fat milk
1/3 cup sugar
1 3" cinnamon stick
4 large egg yolks

Directions:
Combine milk, sugar and cinnamon in a small, heavy pot over medium-low heat.
Cook 5 min. or until sugar dissolves, stirring occasionally.
Place egg yolks in a small bowl and whisk until blended.
Gradually add about half of the hot milk mixture, stirring constantly. (Do not remove cinnamon stick)
Stirring constantly, add egg mixture to mixture in pot and cook over medium-low heat (do not boil)for about 6 minutes or until mixture coats the back of a spoon.
Strain sauce through a fine sieve and discard cinnamon.
Place bowl in a large, ice-filled bowl for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until sauce is room temperature.
Cover and chill.
