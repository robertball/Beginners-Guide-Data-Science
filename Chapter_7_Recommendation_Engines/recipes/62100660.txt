Title:
One-dish Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077662.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean ground beef
1 packet onion soup mix
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/4 c milk
1/2 t garlic powder
1 bag tater tots, thawed
2 c shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In the bottom of a 13x9 baking dish, press uncooked ground beef.  Sprinkle beef evenly with the dry onion soup mix and garlic powder.  Pour soup and milk over beef. Top with tater tots.
Bake covered at 350 for 45 mins.  Uncover and bake for an additional 15 mins.  If desired, top with shredded cheese for last 5 mins.

Submitter: 
Angie Mayer
Description: 
This comforting casserole is easy to prepare, packed with yummy flavors, and can be made in one dish!  How great is that?!
