Title:
Chocolate & Peanutbutter Acorns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
herseys mini chips
herseys kisses
nutter butters or ritz mini's with peanut butter
optional - peanut butter

Directions:
you can melt herseys mini's in microwave at 5 second intervals until melted or use smooth peanut butter
dip the flat end of the hersey's kiss into peanut butter or chocolate, your choice and top with a ritz mini
dip the flat end of the mini chip into melted chocolate or swipe with peanut butter and stick it to the top centre if ritz bits
do not refrigerate and keep out of reach from your husband until ready to serve

Submitter: 
M. Karen
Description: 
These are perfect for thanskgiving
