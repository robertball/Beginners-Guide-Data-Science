Title:
Alex's Cabbage Soup Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/904271.jpg
Ingredients:
Half a cabbage sliced thin and de-cored
2 onions chopped fine
cloves of garlic to your taste
1/2 cup celery sliced thin - you can use the leafy parts too.
1 cup bellpepper chopped
Vegetable oil of your choice
2 bags of frozen soup veggies
1 can of stewed tomatoes
1 can of tomato sauce, or V8
2 TBL mustard seeds
1/2 TBL celery seeds
tiny  pinch of cumin
1 tspn poultry seasoning or Savory if available
Onion powder to taste
Red peppers flakes
black pepper
and / or tobasco sauce to taste
"Better than Boulion" Beef stock

Directions:
Saute onions, celery, peppers in oil till soft, add chopped garlic, let simmer for about a minute
Add cabbage let it get nice and soft...brown a little for more flavor
Add frozen veggies and all other ingredients adding water if needed to cover.
Bring to a boil and then reduce heat
Simmer till done, about 2 hours.  Add salt and pepper but remember the beef stock already has a lot of salt
