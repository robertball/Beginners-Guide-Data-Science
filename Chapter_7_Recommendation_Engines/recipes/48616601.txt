Title:
Orzo with Chickpeas, Goat Cheese and Oregano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups orzo (about 9 ounces)
1/4 cup olive oil
3 Tbl. lemon juice
1 clove garlic, minced
15 oz. can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained and rinsed
2 Tbl. chopped fresh oregano
Salt and ground pepper, to taste
5 oz. log goat cheese, crumbled

Directions:
Bring a large saucepan of salted water to a boil.  Add orzo and cook, stirring occasionally, until just tender, about 8 minutes.  Drain the orzo and set aside.
In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice and garlic.  Add the chickpeas, oregano, and orzo.  Toss to combine.
Season with salt and papper.  Gently stir in the goat cheese.
