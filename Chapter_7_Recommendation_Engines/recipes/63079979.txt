Title:
Crock Pot Leftover Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/777964.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups of day old wheat bread cubed
1 & 1/2 cups left over cooked oatmeal
5 eggs
2 & 1/2 cups 1% milk
1/2 cup agave nectar
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
Brown Sugar Butter Sauce (optional)
1/2 cup water
2 cups packed brown sugar
1 tablespoon coffee liqueur, Irish cream liqueur, OR vanilla extract
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Grease crock pot with butter or non-stick cooking spray.
In large bowl whisk together oatmeal, eggs, milk, agave, vanilla, and cinnamon.
Add bread to bowl and mix until all bread is wet.
Pour into greased crock pot.
Cover with lid and cook on high for 4 hours or low for 8 hours.
For the Brown Sugar Butter Sauce; boil water and brown sugar for 2 minutes stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in flavoring, butter, and heavy cream. Serve warm over completed bread pudding.

Submitter: 
meyer
Description: 
This healthier version of bread pudding is perfect for breakfast as is, or topped with maple syrup, yogurt, and fresh berries. Also makes a great (not so healthy) dessert topped with whipped cream, vanilla ice cream, or the Brown Sugar Butter Sauce below. Enjoy!
