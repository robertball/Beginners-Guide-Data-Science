Title:
Chorizo Omelet w/Fried Red Pepper Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Omelet
3 eggs
Milk
Butter
1 smoked Goya chorizo
a few saffron threads (optional)
Sauce
1 large red tomato
1 large red bell pepper
½ small finely minced onion
1 clove of garlic minced
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon smoked Spanish paprika
1 tablespoon Spanish Sherry vinegar or other light vinegar (no balsamic)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mince or grind Chorizo in food processor.  Fry in pan with some the minced onion and place aside.  Grind saffron (if using), add to eggs in bowl and had enough milk to thin.  Place tomato and bell pepper in blender and purée.
In separate pan fry eggs in butter and cook, add chorizo and fold sides up.  In pan that was used to fry chorizo, add olive oil and fry garlic and onion till translucent add tomato-pepper puree and heat through.  Add paprika, salt and pepper and vinegar remove from heat.
To plate, place sauce on bottom and put omelet on top.

Description: 
This is a Spanish inspired omelet thats perfect for the weekend for breakfast, lunch or dinner.
