Title:
Chocolate Coconut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. graham cracker crumbs
1/2 c. melted butter
2 c. coconut
1 can sweetened condensed milk (14 oz.)
12 oz. chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix graham cracker crumbs and butter and press into 9Ã—13 pan.
Mix coconut and milk and spread over crust.
Bake for 15 minutes or until browned.
Melt chocolate chips and spread over baked stuff.
Stick in the fridge until completely cooled.
Cut into squares. Enjoy!
