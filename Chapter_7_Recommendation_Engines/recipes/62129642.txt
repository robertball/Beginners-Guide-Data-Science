Title:
Curried Shrimp and Crab Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 small onion, very finely chopped
1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
3 Tsp. fresh parsley for garnish, chopped
2 tsp. curry powder, or to taste
3/4 pound medium shrimp, shelled and deveined
1/2 pound fresh or frozen crabmeat, drained and squeezed dry
1 cup heavy or whipping cream
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. freshly ground pepper
2 Tsp. freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet.
Add garlic, onion, and parsley.
Cook over low to medium heat till onion is tender.
Stir in curry powder.
Add shrimp and crabmeat and stir .
Cook for about 4 minutes.
Add the rest of the ingredients and cook at a very gentle boil till sauce thickens.
Make sure the pasta is very thoroughly drained to remove excess water before adding sauce.
Toss the sauce with hot pasta and garnish with parsley before serving.

Submitter: 
IrvineHousewife
Description: 
My good friend Gabbi, who's from India, and I were trying to create a fushion dish with curry and pasta. This is what we came up with. Those of you who love curry will particularly enjoy this sublime sauce. I recommend using conchiglie, rotelle, or fusilli.
