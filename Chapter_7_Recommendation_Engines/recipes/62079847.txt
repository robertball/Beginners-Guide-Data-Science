Title:
Hot Cocoa Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups nonfat dry milk
1 cup sugar
½ cup unsweetened cocoa
½ cup non-dairy creamer
dash of salt

Directions:
Mix all together and store in tightly sealed container.
To make cocoa: mix 3-4 tbsp. with 6-8 oz. hot water.
For flavored cocoa, use flavored non-dairy creamer.

Submitter: 
Colleen Eckman
Description: 
This is a great gift thats quick and easy to make. But don't forget to make some for yourself!
