Title:
Spicy Sausage Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1x 16 oz. package turkey smoked sausage
1x cup uncooked long grain rice
1.5x cups water
1x 14 oz. can diced tomatoes
1/2x 8 oz. package frozen corn
2x small zucchini
2x green onions, chopped
1x tsp. Italian seasoning
1x tsp. crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
Slice sausage into 1/2" pieces and boil pieces in 1/2" water in skillet for 5 minutes.  Drain off excess water and fat.  Layer rice in bottom of 9"x13" baking dish and add water.  Mix tomatoes, corn, zucchini, turkey and seasonings in large bowl.  Place on top of rice and water.  Cover dish tightly with foil and bake in preheated oven for 40-45 minutes until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.
