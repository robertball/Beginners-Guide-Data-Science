Title:
Overnight Coconut Pecan Steel Cut Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/808026.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 c. steel cut oats
1 T. unsweetened shredded coconut
1 T. crushed pecans
1/2 t. brown sugar
1/8 t. cinnamon
1/3 c. milk
1 T. milk

Directions:
mix oats, coconut, pecans, sugar and spice in a small container. pour 1/3 c. milk over oat mix. cover and refridgerate overnight. before serving stir and top with an additional 1 T. milk.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
No cook method of preparing steel cut oats. Soaking overnight makes for a chewy and heart healthy wholesome breakfast or snack that is ready when you are to eat.
