Title:
Best Ever Beef Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs lean beef cubes cut in 1 inch pieces
Seasoned salt and black pepper, for seasoning
2-3 tablespoons cooking oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon tomato paste
2 teaspoons onion powder
2 bay leaves
48 ounces good quality canned beef gravy (1-#10 can or 3-14.5 ounce cans)
8 ounces low sodium beef broth
3 tablespoons cornstarch
3 tablespoons water

Directions:
Season the beef with seasoned salt and pepper.  In dutch oven, brown the beef, in batches, on all sides in the heated oil.  Once all meat is browned, combine in dutch oven.
Add garlic, tomato paste, onion powder, bay leaves, beef gravy and beef broth to beef.  Heat to boiling, then simmer over low heat for 1-2 hours.
During last 15 minutes of cooking, if gravy needs thickened, combine cornstarch and cold water into meat mixture.  Simmer for 15 more minutes until thickened.
Remove bay leaves, adjust seasonings to taste, and serve.
