Title:
Philly Cheesesteak Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/694636.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound of ground beef
1/2 of a chopped onion
1/2 of a chopped green pepper
1/2 of a chopped red pepper
thin strips of steak
1 can of cream of mushroom soup
1/4 to 1/2 a cup of mayonnaise
2 cans of refrigerated biscuits
1 cup of shredded white cheddar cheese
1 cup of shredded provolone cheese

Directions:
First, preheat oven to 350 degrees. After heated line 1 can of biscuits in the casserole dish and cook for about 6 min so they are about half way cooked.
Cook the shredded steak until well done. Then cook the ground beef, peppers and onions. After meat has browned, throw the strips of steak back in the pan. Then mix in mayonnaise. Afterwards, mix in can of cream of mushroom soup.
Put meat mix on top of biscuits. Top it off with cheese and add the other can of biscuits to top it off. Try to stretch the dough so it covers the mix.
cook for 20-30 min and then eat A LOT!
