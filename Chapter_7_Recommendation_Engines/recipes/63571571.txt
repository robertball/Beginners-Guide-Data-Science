Title:
Brownie Cup Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975392.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1/4 cup butter, cubed
1 egg
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup all purpose flour
1/4 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
n a microwave, melt chocolate chips and butter; stir until smooth. Cool slightly.
In a small bowl, beat egg and sugar. Stir in vanilla and chocolate mixture. Gradually add flour then fold in nuts.
Fill paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full. Bake at 325Â° for 20-25 minutes or until tops begin to crack.
Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan to a wire rack.
