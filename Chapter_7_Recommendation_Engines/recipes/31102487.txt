Title:
Velveeta Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup flour
2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups cubed velveeta
2 cups cooked macaroni, drained
1/4 bread crumbs
1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F. Melt butter in large saucepan on low heat. Blend in flour, cook 1 minute, stirring constantly. Gradually add milk, stirring with wire whisk until well blended; cook until thickened, stirring frequently. Add velveeta; cook and stir until melted.
Add macaroni; mix lightly. Spoon into 1 1/2 quart casserole dish. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and parmesan cheese.
Bake 20-25 minutes or until heated through.
Variations: Add 1/2 tsp dry mustard powder or 1 tsp Dijon mustard to the velveeta sauce before mixing with the macaroni. Or, add layers of one or more of the following ingredients between layers of half each of the macaroni mixture before baking; crumbled crispy cooked bacon, chopped ham, sauteed green and red pepper strips, sauteed chopped onions, chopped tomatoes.
