Title:
Carmelized Onion and Goat Cheese Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ready made flatbread (I like Presidents Choice)
1 large onion
2 tbsp olive oil
dash salt
dash sugar
pine nuts
chopped mixed olives (deli)
cherry tomatos
goat cheese
BEST ever pizza sauce

Directions:
1.  Heat oil in frying pan.
2.  Chop onions into 1/4's and then slice each quarter into strips
3.  Saute onions, stirring constantly.  Sprinkle with pinch of salt about 10 mins in.  Continue to stir onions until they start to turn caramel in color.
4.  Sprinkle with pinch of sugar, stir.  Once nice golden brown color, remove from heat.
5.  Spread BEST ever pizza sauce on flat bread.  Coat with carmelized onions.  Sprinkle on olives and cherry tomatoes and chunks of goat cheese.
6.  Cook at 400C for about 15 mins.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
