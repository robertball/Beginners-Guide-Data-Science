Title:
Buffalo Chicken Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/478823.jpg
Ingredients:
The measurements are for an 8 X 8 dish.
3 boneless chicken breast halve
1 bottle of buffalo wing sauce
1 jar of tomato sauce
15 oz container of ricotta cheese
1 bottle blue cheese dressing
3 stalks of celery – chopped
Italian seasoning to taste
2 cups of shredded pizza cheese (cheddar and mozz combined)
9 sheets of ready to bake lasagna

Directions:
Day before slow cook chicken breast with a nice coating of buffalo sauce – 4 hrs on high. It will break apart with the fork. Same result if chicken is boiled then shredded and tossed with sauce.
In a bowl mix together tomato sauce and buffalo sauce to desired heat.
In a separate bowl mix together ricotta, blue cheese to desired taste and dash of Italian seasoning.
In yet another bowl mix together shredded chicken and chopped celery.
Layers in an 8X8 pan:
Thin layer of sauce
3 noodles
Layer of sauce
Sprinkle of shredded cheese
Chicken
Ricotta
Thin layer of sauce
3 noodles repeat until all ingredients used
Remainder of shredded cheese on top
Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees covered in foil.
Remove foil bake 15 minutes or until cheese is bubbling.

Description: 
Shredded buffalo chicken and blue cheese infused ricotta layered between lasagna noodles topped with cheese...easily tailored to satisfy one's taste buds.
