Title:
Low Carb Very Moist and flavorful turkey meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground turkey (I use the frozen roll kind - much cheaper
1/4 cup milk (or beef broth if doing dairy free)
1 egg, beaten
1 cup quick oats
1/2 cup onion chopped fine
1/4 cup mushrooms, OPTIONAL
1/4 cup ketchup
2 squirts mustard
1 tbsp worchestire sauce or soy sauce
1 teaspoon or less salt
pepper to taste
1 teaspoon italian seasonings
1/2 to 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Put all ingredientsin large bowl.
mix well with your hands.
Shape into loaf and put into loaf pan.
Bake meatloaf uncovered 55 minutes to 1:10 or until the top is browned and the juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Danni Elena Wolfe Diol
Description: 
This is our family favorite. I always make a double batch and freeze one. Very moist and tender, loved by both kids and adults. This recipe modifies very easily, so add the stuff you like the best.
