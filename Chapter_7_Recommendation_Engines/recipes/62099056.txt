Title:
Tamale Chile Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans kidney beans (or red chili), drained
2 cans tamales, sliced in 1/2 inch pieces
2 cans tomato sauce (like the kidney bean size)
1/2-1 T chili powder

Directions:
In medium to large saucepan combine above ingredient, simmer on low for 5 minutes.
Serve with cornbread.

Submitter: 
PacNWCook
Description: 
A mild chile with a twist.  It is sweeter and has a great tamale flavor. I made this when I was about 14 years old.  My kids love it today.  Great with cornbread!
