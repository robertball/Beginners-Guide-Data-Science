Title:
Sweet Almond Orange Cream Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989623.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Thomas'® 100% Whole Wheat Bagel Thins™ Bagel
1/4 cup chopped toasted whole almonds
1/2 cup golden raisins
1/4 cup honey
8 ounces reduced fat cream cheese
1 teaspoon orange zest
1 pinch salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Cook almonds for 5-7 minutes on a cookie sheet until fragrant. Remove and let cool to touch.
Into a food processor add cream cheese, raisins, orange zest and honey. Pulse to combine. Scrape down sides if needed.
Add almonds and pulse to combine nuts into cream cheese mixture.
Refrigerate for an hour or more to allow flavors to combine.
Toast Thomas(R) Bagel Thins(TM) Bagel and spread 2 tablespoons of cream cheese mixture.

Submitter: 
Thomas' Bagel Thins
Description: 
Filled with golden raisins, toasted almonds, orange zest and honey, our cream cheese recipe is the perfect blend of sweet and crunchy.
