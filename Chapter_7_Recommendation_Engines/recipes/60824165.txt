Title:
Mexican Chicken Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 clove garlic, minced
3 tablespoons butter
4 cups chicken broth
3/4 teaspoon ground cumin
2 cups half-and-half cream
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 cup pepper jack cheese shredded
2 (14.75 ounce) cans cream-style corn
1 (4 ounce) can diced tomatoes green chiles
1 dash hot pepper sauce
fresh cilantro sprigs, for garnish (optional)

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, brown chicken, onion, and garlic in butter until chicken is no longer pink.
Dissolve the bouillon in hot water; Pour into Dutch oven, and season with cumin. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 5 minutes.
Stir in cream, chicken broth, cheeses, corn, tomatoes & chilies, and hot pepper sauce. Cook, stirring frequently, until the cheese is melted. Stir in chopped tomato. Garnish with cilantro.
