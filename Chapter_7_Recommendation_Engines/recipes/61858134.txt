Title:
Flatlander Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 cups water
1  onion chopped
5 clove of garlic crushed
1/2 cup chili powder
1 teaspoons ground cumin
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2  teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon paprika
1 can each of red and dark kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1/4 flour

Directions:
Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble, and set asiide.
Saute chopped onion for about 5 min  Add garlic to pan and saute everything for 5 more mins.
Add all ingredients to a large kettle. Bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
