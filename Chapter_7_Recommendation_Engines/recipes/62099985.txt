Title:
Easy Cheesy Chicken Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups broad egg noodles
1 lb chicken breast, cut into bite size pieces
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove of garlic, minced
2 cups fresh or frozen broccoli
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup cream cheese
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
1/4 cup chopped Italian parsley (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a medium skillet, add onion garlic and chicken; season with salt and pepper to taste.  Meanwhile cook egg noodles according to package directions and add the broccoli for the last 5 minutes of cook time.  Once the chicken breasts are cooked through, add the chicken broth, mayonnaise and cream cheese and heat until the cream cheese is melted and everything is combined.  Drain the noodles and broccoli and add to the skillet with the chicken; stir to combine.  Add the shredded cheddar cheese and stir until the cheese is melted.  Once all is combined, sprinkle with parmesan cheese and Italian parsley.  Serve and enjoy!

Description: 
This is such a quick and satisfying meal.  Yummy, cheesy chicken mixed with broccoli and egg noodles; served with a green salad and crusty bread, you can't go wrong for a quick weeknight meal. FYI - you can also use low-fat/non-fat cheese in this dish!
