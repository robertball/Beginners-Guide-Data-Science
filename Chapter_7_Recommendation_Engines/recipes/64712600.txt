Title:
Instant Pot Southwestern Marinated Chicken Drumsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5633226.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ounces orange juice
1 lime, juiced
1 lemon, juiced
1 tablespoon chili powder
2 cloves garlic crushed
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon ground chipotle powder
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 onion, sliced and rings separated
2 tablespoons cilantro chopped
1 1/2 pounds chicken drumsticks, about 6 drumsticks

Directions:
I a bowl combine orange juice, lime juice, lemon juice, chili powder, crushed garlic, cumin, oregano, chipotle powder and garlic salt until well combined. Pour into a sealable freezer bag, add the onion, cilantro and drumstick. Seal the bag and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight turning the bag frequently.
Place marinade in pressure cooker and the drumsticks on the inner rack. Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 15 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build. When cooking time is up allow for 15 minutes natural-pressure relief. Release remaining pressure, unlock lid and serve.
If crispy chicken is desired place drumsticks under a broiler a cook for 4 minutes turning after 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
A mixture of spices and fruit juices combine to make flavorful, tender and juicy drumsticks in a pressure cooker for a main meal or just to snack on.
