Title:
Slow Cooker Balsamic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken breasts
1 medium onion sliced
4 garlic cloves
1/2 C Olive Oil
1 tsp. each:  dried oregano, basil, rosemary
1/2 tsp thyme
Ground Black Pepper and Salt to taste

Directions:
1. Pour Olive Oil on the bottom of slow cooker
2. Add Chicken Breast. Salt and Pepper each breast.
3. Put sliced onion on top of chicken then put in all the dried herbs and garlic cloves.
4. Pour in vinegar
5. Cook on high 4 hours
