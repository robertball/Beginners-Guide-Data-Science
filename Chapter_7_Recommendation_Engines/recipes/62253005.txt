Title:
Ricotta Cheese Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 Large Pasta Shells
1 tsp Salt
15 oz. Ricotta Cheese
2 Eggs
1 Cup Parmesan Cheese, grated
2 tsp. Parsley
26 Oz. Pasta Sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Bring large pot of water to boil.
Add 1 tsp. salt and pasta shells to boiling water.
Boil pasta until al dente
Drain pasta and run under cold water to cool.
Mix ricotta, eggs, parmesan, mozzarella and parsley.
Spread 1 cup sauce in 9x12 casserole dish
Spoon 3 tbsp filling into each shell.
Place filled shells into casserole dish.
Pour remaining sauce on top of shells and bake uncovered until bubbly.  About 30 Minutes.
