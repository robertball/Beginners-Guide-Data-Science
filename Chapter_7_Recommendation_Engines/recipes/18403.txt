Title:
Apple and Sunflower Seed Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/628241.jpg
Ingredients:
2 green apples - washed, cored and cubed
1/2 cup sunflower seeds
1 head romaine lettuce - rinsed, dried, and chopped
2 dill pickles, diced
2 tomatoes, diced
1/2 cup ranch-style salad dressing

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together the apples, sunflower seeds, lettuce, pickles and tomato.  Pour dressing on top, toss and enjoy!

Submitter: 
SYLV
Description: 
This sour apple salad is both nutritious and delicious - it contains fruit, vegetables and seeds.  You can use any type of dressing and lettuce in this recipe.
