Title:
Vegan Lentil soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag lentils
1.5 c chopped carrots
1.5 c celery chopped
1 medium onion chopped
1 head garlic
2 cups spinach
8 cups veggie broth
2 cups water
1.5 t salt
.5 t pepper
1 t cumin
1 t spike
1 t olive oil

Directions:
Drizzle garlic with olive oil. Roast garlic at 450 for 15 mins. Bring all other ingredients to a boil. Reduce to a simmer. Simmer for 40 mins. Remove half soup, combine with garlic blend until smooth. Return to pot. Simmer 30 more mins.
