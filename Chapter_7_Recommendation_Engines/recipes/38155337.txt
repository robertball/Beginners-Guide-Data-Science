Title:
Crock Pot Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. macaroni, cooked (I use spirals)
1 quart or more spaghetti sauce
8 oz. sliced provolone cheese
1 lb. hamburger, cooked and drained
1 pt. sour cream
1 can mushrooms (optional)
1 cup grated Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
Combine sauce, cooked hamburger, cooked macaroni and mushrooms.
Layer 1/2 of mixture in crockpot.
Add sour cream
Add provolone cheese
Add the rest of the mixture
Top with grated mozzarella cheese
Cook 4-6 hours on low in crockpot.

Submitter: 
Jody Roberts
Description: 
This is a pretty versatile crock pot lasagna recipe.
