Title:
Peanutbutter Protein Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c ice
1/4 c peanut butter
1/2 c vanilla yogurt
1/8 c sugar
1 scoop chocolate whey protein
1 c milk

Directions:
add ingredients into blender in order they are listed.
puree mixture until well combined.
enjoy :-)

Submitter:

Description: 
A wonderful and easy smoothie that I made to taste just like the ones you can get at your local healthclub.
