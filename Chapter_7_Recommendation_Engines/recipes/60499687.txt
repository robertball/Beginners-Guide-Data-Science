Title:
Crunch & Munch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 c. popped microwave popcorn (naturl flavor)
1/4 c. unsalted peanuts
1/2 c. butter
1/2 c. white sugar
1/4 c. light KaroÂ®

Directions:
Spread popcorn and peanuts on a baking sheet and keep warm in oven set to 300&#0176;
Melt butter in saucepanand add sugar and corn syrup.
Simmer, stirring occasionally.
Insert candy thermometer and pour mixture over popcorn when temperature reaches 300 &#0176;
Stir well.
Put popcorn back in oven for 5 minutes, then stir again, repeating heat and stir until all corn and peanuts are evenly coated.
Pour onto waxed paper.
When cool, break into bite-size chunks and store in air-tight container.
