Title:
Chipotle Shrimp Tacos with Pineapple-Jicama Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shrimp Tacos:
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tablespoon tequila
1 juice of lime
2 chipotle peppers and tablespoon adobo sauce (freeze extra)
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2  teaspoon salt, or to taste
1/2 teaspoon white pepper
Salsa:
1-1/2 cups diced peeled pineapple
1 cup diced peeled jicama
1/2 cup diced red onion
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1-2 serrano chile,seeded, chopped (can sub jalapenos for less heat)
12-15 corn tortillas

Directions:
Prepare grill, medium-high heat.  For salsa mix 6 ingredients in medium bowl. Season salsa with salt and pepper. Refridgerate until ready to serve.
To prepare shrimp marinade in blender, combine olive oil, lime juice, tequila, chipotle peppers and adobo, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper; blend until smooth, 1-2 minutes. Pour marinade into bowl, add the shrimp and toss to coat. Marinate for 10 minutes.
Place 5-6 shimp per skewer and grill the shrimp until translucent, about 1 1/2 to 2 minutes on each side. Grill tortillas 30-60 seconds on each side or until done.  Remove tortillas from heat and place in towel to keep warm.
To assemble tacos place 4-5 peices of shrimp on each tace and top with salsa.  Enjoy!

Description: 
Just in time for grilling season!  These chipotle tacos are hot.  But the cool, sweet, crunchy taste of the slaw balances them perfectly.
