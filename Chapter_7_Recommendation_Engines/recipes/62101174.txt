Title:
Mars Bars Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Mars Bars (58g size)
1/2 cup margerine
5 cups Rice Krispies
1 pkg.(270g) Milk Chocolate Chips
1/4 cup margerine

Directions:
Chop Mars Bars into small chunks.
In a large saucepan, melt Mars Bars and 1/2 c. margerine over low heat, stirring until smooth.  Do not boil.
Add Rice Krispies and stir to coat.
Pour mixture into sprayed 9x9 inch pan, and spread evenly.
In a small saucepan, combine chocolate chips with 1/4c margerine.
Melt over low heat stirring until smooth.
Spread over Rice Krispie base.
Let cool for 15 min.
Cut, serve and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Bev Clarke
Description: 
We got this recipe from my children's babysitter many years ago.  It immediatly became a family favorite.  Everyone who has tasted these squares has asked for the recipe.
