Title:
SCALLOPED RHUBARB Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C SUGAR
2 C DICED RHUBARB
1 T CINNAMON
6 ROLLS, DICED
1/2 C SUGAR
1 STICK BUTTER, MELTED

Directions:
COMBINE 1 C SUGAR WITH RHUBARB AND CINNAMON IN SAUCEPAN AND COOK UNTIL MUSHY
ADD TO A BUTTERED, OVEN PROOF, CASSEROLE DISH, TOP WITH DICED BREAD, SPRINKLE WITH HALF CUP SUGAR, POUR MELTED BUTTER OVER TOP.
BAKE 350 FOR 35 MINUTES`
