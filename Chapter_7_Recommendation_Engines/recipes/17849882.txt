Title:
Koek (Dutch loaf cake) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1/2 cup peel or 1 cup raisins
1 big spoon of shortening
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp cloves
1/2 tsp allspice
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
3 cups flour
2 tsp baking soda

Directions:
Bring first six ingredients to a boil; shut off burner.
Add sugar, flour and soda.  Mix.
Bake in loaf pan at 350 F for 50 to 55 minutes.
