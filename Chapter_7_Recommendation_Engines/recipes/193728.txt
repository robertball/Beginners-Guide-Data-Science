Title:
Crispy Parmesan Chicken Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987613.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups FRENCH'S® Original or Cheddar French Fried Onions
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 pound boneless skinless chicken tenders

Directions:
Crush French Fried Onions in plastic bag with hands or rolling pin. Transfer to pie plate or waxed paper.
Combine mayonnaise and cheese. Brush mixture on chicken. Dip into onion crumbs, pressing firmly to adhere. Place chicken on baking sheet.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 15 minutes or until no longer pink in center.

Submitter: 
French's®
Description: 
For this finger-licking appetizer or main dish, chicken tenders are coated in a tasty Parmesan cheese mixture, rolled in crushed French fried onions, and baked until crispy.
