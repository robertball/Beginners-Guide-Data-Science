Title:
chicken casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast
1/2 lb. provel cheese or swiss cheese
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/4 cup of white wine
1 box of croutons, plain or seasoned
1/4 cup melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  In a 9x13 casserole dish put the chicken in the dish and put provel or swiss cheese on top of chicken, enough to cover the chicken.  Mix the cream of chicken soup with the white wine and pour over the cheese.  Put croutons on top and then the melted butter.  Bake for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Amy
Description: 
This is a one dish meal that my family loves.  It was given to me from one of my high school friends mother's and we added different cheese which makes it even creamier.
