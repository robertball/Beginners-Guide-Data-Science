Title:
Frozen Chocolate Bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507376.jpg
Ingredients:
6 bananas
1 - 12 oz package semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 T vegetable oil
12 popsicle sticks
candy sprinkles

Directions:
Peel the bananas and cut in half.  Push popsicle stick in the cut end of the banana and place on a wax papered baking sheet.  Repeat until all bananas are speared, freeze until hardened.
Over double boiler, melt chocolate chips.  Take off heat, add 2 Tablespoons oil and mix until well blended.
Dip the banana in chocolate chip mixture until coated, sprinkle candy sprinkles over immediately.  Set on baking sheet and repeat until all bananas are done.
Ready to eat immediately, or the bananas can be frozen for a treat later!

Description: 
This is the easiest, yummiest recipe for frozen chocolate bananas.  The best part is I usually have the ingredients on hand and it's easy.
