Title:
Cran Apple Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614283.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ½ cups all purpose flour
3 tbs granulated sugar
1 ¾ tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
1 large egg lightly beaten
1 tbs margarine melted
1 cup milk
1 granny smith appl peeled and finely chopped
(about 1 cup)
½ cup fresh or frozen cranberries

Directions:
In a bowl sift together flour, sugar, baking powder and cinnamon. Mix well set aside.
In another bowl combine egg, margarine and milk mix well. Then add in apples and cranberries.
Add fruit mixture to flour mixture until combined.
(Batter will be lumpy)
Heat a large non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
Pour batter into skillet making 3-inch rounds repeat with remaining batter.
Cook until tops are bubbly about 2 minutes. Using a flat spatula flip pancakes cook until golden 1 minute longer.
Place on plate and cover to keep warm.
