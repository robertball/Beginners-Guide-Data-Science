Title:
Parmesan Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1074768.jpg
Ingredients:
2	teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
¼	cup diced yellow onion
1 	medium clove garlic, sliced
¾	cup heavy cream
¼ 	cup fresh grated Parmesan Cheese
1/8	teaspoon ground white pepper

Directions:
In a deep saucepan over medium low heat, add oil and onions, and sweat until they are translucent.  Add cream, using a stick blender puree the onion and garlic.  Add pepper;  Slowly bring to a boil turn down to a simmer stirring occasionally let cook until reduced and thickened.  Add Parmesan cheese; stir and turn heat down to low and stir frequently until it reduces and is as thick as you want.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
Creamy thick, Parmesan flavored sauce, great in soups, over pastas or chicken.
