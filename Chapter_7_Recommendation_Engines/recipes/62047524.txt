Title:
My Mexican Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cans of Whole Green Chiles (Cut into small pieces or larger pieces)
6 Potatos (cut into small pieces about ½ inch or smaller)
1 or 2 lbs. Stew Meat (cut into small pieces about ½ inch or smaller)
1 Large or Medium Onion (dice into small pieces about ½ inch or smaller)
2 Minced Garlic Cloves
2 Tbs. Veggetable Oil
3 Cubes Beef  Boulion
2 ts Salt
Peppercorn or Pepper

Directions:
1.)	Salt diced meat and then brown in stew pot with 2 Tbs. Veggetable Oil
2.)	Add other ingredients
3.)	Top off with water and boil till potatos, etc. are cooked.
4.)	Serve and add pepper as desired
