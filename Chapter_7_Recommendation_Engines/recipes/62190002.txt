Title:
chicken burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion
1/4 c green bell pepper
6 Tbs butter
1/2 c flour
3 c chicken broth
1 can diced tomatoes w/chilies
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 can chili
1 8oz package cream cheese
8 c chopped chicken
24 tortillas
6 c shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In large skillet saute onion and green pepper in butter until tender.  Stir in flour until blended, gradually add broth.  Bring to boil and stir for 2 minutes.  reduce heat and add tomatoes and seasonings.  Cook for 5 minutes.  Stir in cream cheese and chili until the cheese is melted.  Stir in chicken.  Spoon 1/2 c into tortilla and sprinkle 1/4 c shredded cheese.  Fold ends and sides over filling.  Place in a 9x13 pan and bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes.  Freeze remaining burritos, to cook frozen burritos thaw overnight in refrigerator and bake at 350 for 50-55 minutes.
