Title:
Broomstick Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup smooth Peanut Butter
1 cup Nutella
1/2 cup of Quacker Oatmeal Square cereal or cereal of your choice
1 pound bag of small pretzel rods

Directions:
In a medium size bowl, combine peanut butter and Nutella.  Mix to combine, transfer to serving bowl.  Crunch cereal in a plastic resealable sandwich bag. Sprinkled crunched cereal over top Peanut Butter-Nutella mixture. Dip pretzels into the bowl, scoop up the dip and enjoy your broomstick!

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
An easy to put together dessert dip that is perfect for a Halloween party or fun snack. The pretzels look like broomsticks after they have dipped into this yummy, crunch dip.
