Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages philly cream cheese
2 eggs
1 cup sugar
2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
2 rolls of cookie dough (any kind you like)

Directions:
1. Mix philly cream cheese, eggs, sugar and vanilla extract in mixing bow together until smooth. It will still have little lumps from the cream cheese.
2.  Spread first roll of cookie dough on the bottom of  a 13x9 pan (will be bottom crust) (do not grease pan)
3. Spread mixture on top.
4. Pinch pieces off of the 2nd cookie dough roll and place on top (doesn't have to be neat) this will spread while baking.
Bake at 350 for 30-45 depending on oven.  Remove and let it cool.
