Title:
Spicy Lime and Dill Grilled Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. fresh fish (haddock, perch, cod, etc.)
6 Tbsp. butter, melted
1 lime, juiced (not strained)
1 tbsp. dried dill weed
1 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp red pepper flakes
1 medium onion, cut into 1/8" thin slices

Directions:
Lay out four pieces of aluminum foil (approximately 8" x 10" each) and spray with cooking spray.
Divide fish into thirds and lay on individual foil pieces.
Blend together: butter, lime juice, dill weed, salt and red pepper flakes.
Pour evenly over fish.
Lay onion slices on fish to cover.
Roll foil up lengthwise to seal.
Roll up ends of foil to seal.
With grill on medium heat, grill fish packets approximately 5-7 minutes per side.
Open packets and serve, or eat out of packets "hobo style!"

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
Super-fast entree that tastes like you spent time in the kitchen. Light, crisp flavors of lime and dill say summertime! even if you grill this in the winter!
