Title:
Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs potatoes, cut into 1" chunks
Water (to cover potatoes)
2 tbsp butter
1/3 cup half and half or milk
Salt and pepper
1/4 cup sour cream

Directions:
Place potatoes in instant pot and cover with water.
Cook on manual pressure for 7 min.
Quick pressure release.
Drain water.
Add butter, salt, pepper, half and half (or milk), and sour cream.
Mash to desired texture.
