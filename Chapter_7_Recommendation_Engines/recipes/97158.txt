Title:
Italian Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988767.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
2 tablespoons finely diced onion
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon plain bread crumbs
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1 egg white, lightly beaten
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Mix together turkey, onion, parsley, garlic, bread crumbs, Italian seasoning, egg white, salt and pepper. Form into 4 patties.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat, when wisps of smoke rise add the patties. Cook turkey patties until brown, about 4-6 minutes on each side or to an internal temperature of 180 degrees F (85 degrees C).

Submitter: 
Pam Anderson
Description: 
Try these tasty burgers served on top of salad greens with an aioli sauce.
