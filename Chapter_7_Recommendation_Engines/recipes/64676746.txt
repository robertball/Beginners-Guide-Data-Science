Title:
Coconut Macaroons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/3 cups (14 oz bag) sweetened shredded coconut
3/4 cup +2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 large egg whites
1/4 teaspoon salt
4 ounces semi sweet chocolate

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Line baking sheets with parchment paper
Mix together coconut and milk
Beat egg whites and salt until stiff peaks are formed
Folk whites into coconut with rubber spatula
Drop by heaping tablespoons mounds about 1" apart
Bake 25 minutes
Melt chocolate in microwave
Dip bottoms of macaroons  into chocolate and return them to the sheet
Chill to allow chocolate to set
Best kept in airtight container at room tem
