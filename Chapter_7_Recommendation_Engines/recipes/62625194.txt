Title:
Mushroom Cake with Avacado Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Red pepper coulis
2 red bell peppers
2 garlic
2 T milk
1 1/2 t honey
Mushroom Cake
2 T butter
2 T oil
16 oz mushrooms
2 portobello
8 oz shitake
2 garlic
2 eggs
2 T parmesan
2 T fresh basil and parsley
1 t salt
1/2 t pepper
1/2 c panko
Avacado Pesto
1/4 c pine nuts
1 c mashed avacado
1/4 c parmesan
2 T cilantro
1 T parsley
2 t lime juice
1/4 c oil
1 T butter

Directions:
Char and peel peppers, add rest, puree in a blender.
Saute mushrooms, add garlic, process altogether mix in panko form 8 patties. Fry in skillet.
For the avacado pesto, mix all except oil and make an emulsion.
