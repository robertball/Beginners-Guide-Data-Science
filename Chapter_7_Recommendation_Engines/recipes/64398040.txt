Title:
Ginny's Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz elbow macaroni
3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 2/3 cup milk
2 eggs, beaten
1 tablespoon prepared mustard (optional)
4 Tbps butter
4 oz neufchatel cream cheese (softened)
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 sleeve ritz crackers crushed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes; drain and put in large bowl.  Add the butter and cream cheese and stir till melted.
Stir Cheddar cheese, milk, eggs, mustard, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl.  Make sure cheese shreds are mixed with liquid well. Add to macaroni and mix well.  Pour into a greased baking dish.  Add ritz crackers to top and dot with butter.
Bake in the preheated oven until slightly brown and thickened, about 45 minutes.
