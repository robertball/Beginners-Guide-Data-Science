Title:
Dads Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Red Pepper
1/2 Yellow Pepper
1 clove Garlic
1/2 Onion
2 lbs. Italian Sausage
1 (28 oz) Tomato Sauce
Olive Oil

Directions:
Par- Boil Sausage for 15 min.
Saute the peppers, onion, garlic.
Add sauce to peppers, onion, garlic, olive oil mixture and sausage.
Simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
Season to taste.
