Title:
Sweet Red & Chili Pepper Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/398204.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8oz block of cream cheese
1/3 c Sweet Red and Ancho Chili Pepper Spread

Directions:
Place block of cream cheese on serving plate and allow to soften slightly.
In microwave safe dish, place 1/3 c of Spread in microwave for 15-25 seconds until slightly warm.
Pour the spread mixture over the cream cheese.
Best served with crackers.

Submitter: 
My4boys
Description: 
This spread has a nice presentation for any get together. The sweet red and ancho chili pepper spread is both sweet and spicy and goes beautifully with the cream cheese base. Best served on crackers.
