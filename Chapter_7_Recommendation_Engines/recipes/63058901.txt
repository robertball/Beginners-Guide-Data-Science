Title:
Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups dried split peas
2 quarts cold water
1 large smoked turkey wing or drumstick
2 onions, thinly sliced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 stalks celery, chopped
3 carrots, chopped
1 potato, diced

Directions:
In a large stock pot, cover peas with 2 quarts cold water and soak for l hour.
Once peas are soaked, add smoked turkey, onion, salt and pepper .  Cover, bring to boil and then simmer for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
Remove bone; cut off meat, dice and return meat to soup.  Add celery, carrots and potatoes.  Cook slowly, uncovered for 30 to 40 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
