Title:
New Orleans Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979603.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions
1 1/2 teaspoons Lawry's® Garlic Salt
1/4 teaspoon dried basil leaves, crushed
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme leaves, crushed
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
4 onion rolls, halved and lightly toasted

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients except rolls. Shape into 4 patties. Grill or broil to desired doneness. Serve on rolls and top, if desired, with grilled onions.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
A festival of bayou flavor abounds in these big, easy burgers.
