Title:
WW Raspberry Zinger Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18oz) boxed cake mix, white
10 oz diet sprite
2 egg whites
1 (3 oz) box raspberry SF jello
1 1/2 c boiling water
8 oz FF Cool Whip

Directions:
Mix dry cake mix with egg whites and Sprite using electric mixer.  Pour into ungreased 9 x 13 pan and bake as directed on box.  Let cool for 15 minutes.  Poke holes in cake with end of wooden spoon.  Add jello to boiling water and stir until dissolved.  Pour over cake.  Cover and refrigerate 2 hours or overnight.  Top with Cool Whip before serving.
