Title:
Mammama's Celery Seed Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup white sugar
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp celery seed
1 1/2 tbsp minced dry onion
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Just mix all the ingredients together in a jar well and chill before serving! Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Ben
Description: 
This is by far my favorite salad dressing. My grandmother used to make this for us all the time and evryone loved it! It is a little on the sweet side but pretty well balanced.
