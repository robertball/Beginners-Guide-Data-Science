Title:
Applesouce Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 cup Sugar
1 1/2 cup whole wheat flour(leveled)
2 4 oz baby applesouce or 1 cup applesouce
1/2 cup extra light olive oil
1 cup ground walnuts (or 3/4 cup walnuts 1/4 cup ground    almonds)
1 tsp bakeing powder
2 tbsps shredded coconut (optional)
2 oz bittersweet bakeing choclate

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Line a 9x13 inch baking pan and sprinkle bottom with coconut.
Beat eggs with sugar on high speed, for five minutes.  Gradually add remaining ingredients while mixing gently, until smooth.  Pour batter into pan and bake for 45-50 minutes.  Break choclate into small cubes put on warm cake wait till melted and spread around.
