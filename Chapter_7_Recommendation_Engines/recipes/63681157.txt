Title:
Caprese' Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1023905.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large Boneless, skinless chicken breasts.
8 slices of Fresh Mozzarella (2 slices for each piece of chicken)
1 tomato, sliced
Fresh basil
Olive Oil
Salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Prepare baking dish with cooking spray.
1. Pound chicken breasts until they are about 1/4" thick.  Cut the chicken half.
2. Place chicken in baking pan. drizzle with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
3. Cook for 25 - 30 minutes until cooked and juices run clear.
4. Place Mozzarella on top of chicken.  Place chicken back into oven for approximately 5 minutes until cheese starts to melt.
5. Remove chicken from oven, top with tomato slices, basil, salt and pepper and ENJOY.
