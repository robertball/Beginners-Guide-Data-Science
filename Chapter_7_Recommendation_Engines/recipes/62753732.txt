Title:
Light or Dark Brown Sugar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white granulated sugar
1 Tbsp. unsulfered molasses

Directions:
In a medium sized bowl, using a mixer on medium speed, mix together the sugar and molasses.  Keep mixing until molasses is completely incorporated and no blops of molasses remain.
Store in an air-tight container.  Add a slice of bread to keep your new creation moist.

Submitter: 
JillH
Description: 
Making your own brown sugar is so simple and easy and tastes exactly like store-bought!  You'll never run out of brown sugar if you keep two simple ingredients on hand:  white granulated sugar and molasses.  Recipe ingredients are for light brown sugar.  For dark brown sugar, simply use 2 Tbsp. molasses for every 1 cup of white sugar.
