Title:
Oatmeal Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ cup water
1 cup oatmeal
1t salt
1 stick butter
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 beaten eggs
1 t vanilla
1 ½ t baking soda
1 ½ c flour
½ C. Nuts (optional)
1t cinnamon.
Frosting:
½ Cup Borwn sugar
1 Cup Sugar
1 T shortning
2 T Butter
1 T white Syrup
1/3 C Milk
1t vanilla

Directions:
Cook the oatmeal and water and salt together until it is thick. Stir in all remaining ingredients while still hot. Bake your cake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
To make the frosting, combine all ingredients except the vanilla, and bring it to a rolling boil for one minute. Then stir in the vanilla, and frost over cake once it has completely cooled.
