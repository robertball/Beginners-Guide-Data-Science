Title:
Shredded Chicken Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976988.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds cooked, shredded chicken breast meat
1 pound tomatillos
1 (7 ounce) can ORTEGA® Diced Green Chiles
2 cloves garlic
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 cup chopped onion
2 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
COMBINE tomatillos, chiles, garlic and salt in food processor or blender; cover.  Process for 1 minute or until mixture is smooth.
HEAT vegetable oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.  Add onion; cook, stirring occasionally, for 1 to 2 minutes or until onion is tender.  Stir in tomatillo mixture and chicken.  Cook, stirring occasionally, for 4 to 5 minutes or until heated through.  Remove from heat; stir in cilantro.
USE filling in burritos, tacos, enchiladas or tamales.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
This versatile filling features the tangy flavor of tomatillos. Tomatillos are often called green tomatoes,  but they are,  in fact,  a different fruit altogether. If using canned tomatillos,  an 11 ounce can is equal to one pound fresh.
