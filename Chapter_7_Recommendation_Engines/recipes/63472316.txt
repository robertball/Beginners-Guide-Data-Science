Title:
Pepperoni Pizza Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. (8 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese
2 c. (8 oz) shredded cheddar cheese
1 c. mayonnaise
c. chopped pepperoni
1 can (4 oz) mushroom stems & pieces - drained & chopped
1/2 c. chopped onion
1/2 c. chopped green pepper
1 can (6 oz) ripe olives - drained & chopped
crackers, breadsticks, or french bread

Directions:
In a large bowl combine first nine ingredients.  Transfer to a 11 x 7 x 2" baking dish.  bake uncovered at 350 deg for 25-30 min or until edges are bubbly and lightly browned.  Serve with crackers, breadsticks, or french bread.
