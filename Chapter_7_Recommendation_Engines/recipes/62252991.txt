Title:
Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Medium Potatoes, Thinly sliced
3 tbsp. Butter or Margarine
2/3 cup Onion, minced
3 tbsp. Flour
1 1/2 tsp Salt
Dash Ground Black Pepper
1 1/2 Cup Milk
1/4 tsp Paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degress
On medium heat in a sauce pan, melt butter and saute oinions until tender.
Stir in flour, salt and pepper.
Slowly stir in milk until thickened.
In a 2 quart casserole dish, layer about half of the potatoes.
Pour half of sauce on top of potatoes.
Repeat with remaining potatoes and sauce.
Sprinkle with paprika.
Bake covered for 45 minutes or until tender.
