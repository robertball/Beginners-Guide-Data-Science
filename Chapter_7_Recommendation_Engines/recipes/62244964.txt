Title:
Momma's Best Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (16 oz) cans baked beans
10 oz crushed pineapples (with juice)
1/2 cup molasses
1/2 cup BBQ sauce
2 Tbsp yellow mustard
5 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
8 oz French's french fried onions, crushed
Salt and freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a large bowl, mix together beans, pineapple, molasses, BBQ sauce, mustard, bacon, and onions. Add some salt and pepper to taste. Pour beans in a 9x13 inch casserole dish. Cook in oven for 1 hour or until beans brown and bubble. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
gourmanda
Description: 
My Mom has made these baked beans since I was a little girl and I have never found any that I liked better. They are smokey, thick,and flavorful. Perfect for picnics in the summer and a hot dish to warm you up in the winter!
