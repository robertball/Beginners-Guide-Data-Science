Title:
Delicious Baked Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. white fish fillets
1 tbsp. fresh lime juice
1 tbsp. mayonnaise
1/8 tsp. onion powder
1/8 tsp. pepper
1/2 cup breadcrumbs
1 1/2 tbsp. butter - melted
2 tbsp. fresh parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.  Place fish in greased baking dish.  Combine lime juice, mayo, onion powder and pepper.  Spread mixture over fish and sprinkle with breadcrumbs.  Drizzle with butter and bake for 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily. sprinkle with parsley and serve.
