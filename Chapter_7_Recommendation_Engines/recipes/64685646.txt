Title:
Unstuffed Eggrolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp sesame oil
12 eggs or 1 lb ground pork
1 tbsp soy sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp turmeric
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 package coleslaw mix
2 carrots, shredded
4 oz rice noodles
3 green onions, diced

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package directions.  Drain and set aside.
Heat oil over medium heat in a large skillet.  Add eggs or pork and cook through completely.  Stir in soy sauce, garlic, and seasonings.  Add cabbage and carrots.  Cook 2-4 minutes, until vegetables are crisp tender, stirring frequently.
Remove from heat and add noodles and green onions.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
Everything that makes an eggroll great, without most of the work.  Serve with sweet and sour sauce to drizzle over it.
