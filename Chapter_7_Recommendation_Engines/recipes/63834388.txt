Title:
Creamy Tomato Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (16-20 ounce) cheese tortellini
2 cans condensed tomato soup
2 cups chicken broth
2 cups 2% milk
2 cups half & half
1 tsp. basil
1/4 tsp. oregano
salt & pepper to taste
1 pkg. (8 ounce) mushrooms
1/4 c. chopped white onion
1/4 tsp. fresh garlic
1 fresh tomato (sliced)
1/2 c. real shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook tortellini according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a Dutch oven, combine the soup, broth, milk, cream, and seasonings. Heat through, stirring frequently. Sautee mushrooms, onion, and garlic in a pan; carefully add to soup. Carefully add chopped tomato. Drain tortellini; carefully add to soup. Stir in cheese. Heat thoroughly. Sprinkle each serving with additional cheese if desired.
