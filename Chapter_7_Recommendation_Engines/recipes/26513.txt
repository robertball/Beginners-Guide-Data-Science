Title:
Greek Salad Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980100.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package Mediterranean-style salad greens
1 2/3 cups cucumber, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup Greek-style vinaigrette
1 package (12) ORTEGA® Taco Shells
Toppings: crumbled feta cheese, sliced ripe olives, chopped tomato

Directions:
TOSS salad greens, cucumber and vinaigrette in large bowl.
FILL taco shells with salad mixture.  Top with cheese, olives and tomato.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
These salad tacos are a fun and attractive way to serve your family its vegetables, and they taste as fabulous as they look. Kids will love adding on their own toppings.
