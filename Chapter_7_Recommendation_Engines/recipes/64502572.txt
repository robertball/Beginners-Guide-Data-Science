Title:
Chicken enchiladas verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken breast meat, cooked and shredded
Corn tortillas
Salsa verde
Cream cheese
Mild white cheese

Directions:
Heat together gently the cream cheese and salsa verde (stovetop or microwave) and mix together until smooth.  Add chicken and heat.  Put a generous spoonful of the chicken in each corn tortilla and fold.  Spread the remaining sauce, after using the chicken, onto the tops of each.  Sprinkle with shredded cheese and some of the salsa without the cream cheese in it.  Heat until cheese melts.
