Title:
Homemade Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb lean ground beef
2 onions
2 bell peppers
1T minced garlic
3 14oz cans chili beans
3 14oz cans diced tomatoes
2T chili powder
1t cumin
1t salt

Directions:
1. brown meat, onions, peppers & garlic
2. Add remaining ingredients, simmer until blended and thickened - about 45 min - 1hr.
