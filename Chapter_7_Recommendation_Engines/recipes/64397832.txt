Title:
Oriental Salmon with Stir Fry Veggies over Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2344297.jpg
Ingredients:
4 fillets salmon
4 cloves garlic
2-3 Tbsp Braggs Liquid Amino Acids
2 Tbsp water
2 tsp stevia
½ tsp ground ginger
2 tsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 cloves garlic
1 package fresh snow peas
1 cup carrots
½ cup sliced onion
1 cup mushrooms
Cooked Brown Rice

Directions:
Brown Rice: Cook per directions from package
Salmon: In a pan or zip lock bag, mix 2 minced garlic, ½ Braggs liquid amino acids, water, stevia and ginger. Add salmon fillets and coat well. Set aside for at least 30 minutes. Place salmon and marinade in baking dish and cook for 20 minutes or until salmon is done. You can also pan cook the salmon.
Stir Fry Vegetables: In a skillet, heat olive oil on medium. Add 2 minced garlic and cook for a minute. Add sliced mushrooms, onions and carrots, 1 package of peas and ½ of Braggs liquid amino acids. Stir to coat and cook until they change to a bright color and are still a bit crunchy.
Serve veggies and salmon over brown rice. Add more Braggs to personal taste.
