Title:
3 - 2 - 1 Peanut butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds powdered sugar
2 pounds peanut butter, creamy or crunchy
1 pound stick butter

Directions:
In lagre bowl mix powdered sugar with peanut butter until crumbly then set aside.Melt butter pour into peanut butter and mix well then pat onto a wax paper covered cookie sheet.Let cool.

Submitter: 
Marilyn McDonald Mahuron
Description: 
This is a good recipe to make and give as a gift or set out for a party.
