Title:
Sugar-Free Strawberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz can diet Sprite or 7-UP
(Note: the sugar free Minute Maid fruit sodas are great !)
1 (4 serving size) package of sugar-free strawberry jello
2 T cornstarch
1/4 C water
1 pint strawberries, sliced
1 graham cracker crust (save the plastic protective cover)

Directions:
Pour soda into a saucepan.
Stir in the gelatin until dissolved.
Mix cornstarch & water until dissolved.
Add the cornstarch mixture to the soda and stir.
Cook over medium heat until the mixture is thick & clear.
Cool, then mix with the sliced strawberries.
Pour fruit into the crust.
(Use the plastic crust protector as a cover for the pie.)
Refrigerate until serving time.
Serve topped with sugar free Cool Whip.

Submitter: 
NONA
Description: 
This is a favorite summer dessert for our family.  No one can believe that there's no added sugar !   At last a dessert that doesn't make anyone feel guilty.

Note: Other fruits also work; tried peaches or mixed berries and change the soda & jello flavors to compliment the fruit.
