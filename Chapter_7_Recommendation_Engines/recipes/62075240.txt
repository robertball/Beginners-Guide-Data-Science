Title:
Gourmet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 russet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-2 inch chunks
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
7 tbsp. butter
1 cup sour cream
1/3 cup chopped onion
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Boil potatoes till soft, about 15 minutes, drain.
While potatoes are cooking, melt cheese and 5 tblsp. butter in saucepan over low heat.  Add sour cream, onion, salt and pepper.  Mix well.
Place potatoes in 2 quart baking dish.
Pour cheese mixture over potaoes.
Mix a bit.  Dot potatoes with remaining butter.
Bake for 35-40 minutes till bubbly and browned.
