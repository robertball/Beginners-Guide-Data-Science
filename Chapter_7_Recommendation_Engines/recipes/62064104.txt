Title:
Easy Chicken Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz. chicken tenderloins
2 T vegetable oil
1 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 cup red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 T garlic powder
1/2 T chili powder
1/2 t paprika
1/2 t basil
tortillas and desired garnishes

Directions:
1. Mix spices together. Coat chicken with seasonings.
2. Saute chicken, onion, and pepper in oil over medium heat until chicken is cooked and vegetables are tender.
3. Place chicken and vegetables on tortillas, serve warm with desired garnishes (cheese, sour cream, salsa, lettuce, tomato, guacamole).
