Title:
Lesa's hamburger stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean gr beef
seasoning salt to taste
1T butter or margarine
1 onion (chopped)
1 lrg or 2 small cloves garlic (chopped fine)
1 can mushrooms (drained)
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 T worcestershire sauce
1 T prepared mustard
1/3 C white wine (I used white zinfandel)
8 Oz container of sour cream

Directions:
In a sauce pan, saute onions and garlic in butter until soft.  Add gr beef to pan and season with seasoning salt.  Brown the hamburger until done, but not browned.  Add mushrooms, cream of mushroom soup, worcestershire sauce, wine and mustard.  Add the sour cream, just before serving and warm through.  Serve over hot noodles or rice.
