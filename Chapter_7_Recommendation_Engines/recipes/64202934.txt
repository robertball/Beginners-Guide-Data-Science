Title:
Vinegar free(almost) bbq sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 small to med onion, minced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 C whiskey
1 T honey, or more to taste
1/3 C brown sugar, packed
1/2 C bottled lemon juice
6 oz tomato paste
1/4 tsp white pepper
1/2 Tbl kosher salt
optional:
**2 Tbl Worcestershire (omit for vinegar free)
water to thin if desired.

Directions:
Combine onion, garlic and whiskey in a large pot or skillet over med heat. Simmer for 10 min, or until onion is translucent.
Mix in remainder of ingredients, including any of the optional ones you'd like. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
Continue to simmer for about 20 min, stirring a few times.

Submitter: 
linda2d
Description: 
My homemade version of bourbon bbq sauce developed for vinegar allergy. **Worcestershire has vinegar, but in our case that small amount is allowable.
