Title:
Gluten Free Banana Nut Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1348386.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Blanched Almond Flour
1/2 Cup Coconut Flour
3 Bananas Ripened/Mashed
1/4 Cup Coconut Oil-Melted (or butter for substitute)
3 Large Eggs
1 Tsp Vanilla
1 Tsp Baking Powder
1 Tsp Baking Soda
1 Tsp Cinnamon
Pinch of Salt
2 Tablespoons(Raw)Honey
1/2 Cup Chocolate Chips
1/3 Cup Walnuts or Pecans

Directions:
1. Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees
2.In large bowl whisk together bananas, coconut oil (butter), vanilla, eggs, and honey until well mixed.
3. In separate bowl mix together Almond Flour, Coconut Flour, Baking Soda, Baking Powder, Salt, and Cinnamon
4. Slowly mix in dry ingredients to wet ingredients
5. Stir in Chocolate Chips and Walnuts/Pecans
6. Grease muffin tin or place in muffin cup
7. Fill about half way
8. Bake for 20-22 Min at 350 degrees

Submitter: 
JenRioux
Description: 
This is a recipe I created by searching through and trying out many recipes using both almond and coconut flour for a gluten free option for one of my favorite sweet tooth treat. Perfect for the mornings for breakfast or when you have a sweet tooth.
