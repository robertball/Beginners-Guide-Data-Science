Title:
Roasted Brussel Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T softened butter
1 vanilla bean, seeds scraped
1/4 c toasted chopped pecans
black pepper & salt
1 lb brussel sprouts, trimmed
3 T canola oil
3 T pomegranate molasses
seeds from 1 pomegranate
1 lime, finely zested
1 T finely grated orange zest

Directions:
1. Combine butter and vanilla beans in small bowl.  Fold in pecans and season with pepper. Cover & refrigerate for @ least 30 minutes.
2. Preheat oven to 375.  Place sprouts in med roasting pan, toss with oil and season with salt & pepper.  Roast until lightly golden brown, about 20 min.
3. Remove pan, add molasses and stir to combine.  Return to oven, and cook until tender, about 10 min.
4. Transfer sprouts to a large bowl and add seeds & zest.  Serve with Vanilla butter
