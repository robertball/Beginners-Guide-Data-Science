Title:
Chicken Corriander Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/702444.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chichen breast
1/2 onion finely chopped
3 Tbsp.marg.
2 Tbsp.oil
2 Tbsp.ground corriander
1 Tbsp.turmeric
1 Tbsp.chili powder
1/3 cup water

Directions:
Spread chiken on wax paper,top with chopped onion and let stand for 20 min.In large frying pan ,melt marg.add oil.Stir in spices and cook gently for 1 min.Add chichen and cook on both sides till lightly browned,add onions cook till soft.Add water.Transfer to casserole dish,cover and bake 40 min.at 325.When done remove from oven and sprinkle with lemon juice,if desired.
Serve with rice and spoon juice from chichen over rice.

Something a little different.Nice combination of turmeric,chili powder and corriander.
