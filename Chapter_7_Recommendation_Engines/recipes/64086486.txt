Title:
Cheesecake Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 graham cracker crust
1 package (8 oz) cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sugar
2 cups (or one 4 1/2 oz container) cool whip, thawed

Directions:
Beat cream cheese with 1/2 cup sugar until creamy. Blend in whipped topping. Pour into crust. Chill at least 3 hours.

Description: 
This dessert is SO simple and delicious. It's creamy and not TOO rich.
