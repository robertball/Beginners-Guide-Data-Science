Title:
Lemon Poppy Seed Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2267600.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Vanilla Cake mix
1 3.4 oz instant lemon pudding mix
1 cup sour cream (can use plain or vanilla greek yogart)
1 cup light EVOO (can use vegetable oil)
4 eggs
1/2 cup water
2 TBSP poppy seed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Whisk cake and pudding mix together
Add remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly
Pour into greased and floured bundt pan
Bake for 40 - 50 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
