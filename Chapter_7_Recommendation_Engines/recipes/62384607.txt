Title:
Mexi-waiin Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 10" Flour tortillas
1/2 cup chopped pineapple
1/2 lb diced ham
1/3 cup diced pickled jalapenos
1/4 cup crumbled bacon
1/2 cup cheddar cheese
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
Sour Cream

Directions:
Heat cormal or griddle to medium heat.
Lay tortillas flat on counter.  On half of the quesadilla divide pineapple, ham, jalapenos, bacon and cheese between each one evenly.  Fold them in half.
Place folded tortillas on cormal/griddle until golden brown; about 3-5 minutes each side. Flip and let other side get golden brown also.
Remove and serve with sour cream.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
Came up with these quesadillas when I was trying to use up leftover pineapple and thinking of what to make for lunch!  You can leave the jalapenos off for children.
