Title:
Holiday Cornbread Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (6 ounce) pkgs yellow cornbread mix
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1 1/3 cups milk
1 green bell pepper, chopped
4 medium sized tomatoes, chopped
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 lb bacon
1 (12 ounce) jar of mayonnaise, or to taste

Directions:
Prepare yellow cornbread according to the package instructions, using eggs and milk. Cool and crumble into a large bowl.
Cook bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until very crispy. Remove to a plate with a paper towel to drain grease. Cool, and crumble into cornbread.
Mix in bell pepper, tomatoes, green onions. Add mayonnaise to taste. Refrigerate or serve at room temperature.

Submitter: 
Sharalyn
Description: 
This recipe is a holiday favorite of my large family, but it can be enjoyed any time of year. The red and green colors make it a great seasonal dish, and the bacon brings home the flavor.
