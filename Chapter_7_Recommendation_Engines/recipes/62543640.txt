Title:
Turkey/Rice  Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 cups shredded or diced cooked turkey
2 celery stalks, chopped
1 carrot, peeled and sliced
1 onion, peeled and diced
2 bay leaves
1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
1/2 tsp. ground sage (optional)
6-8 cups chicken broth (add as needed)
garlic salt to taste
ground black pepper, to taste
1 cup (uncooked) regular long-grain white rice
1/2 package frozen green peas--about 8 oz.

Directions:
Place the turkey in a large, deep pot, and add the  celery, carrots, onion, bay leaves, poultry seasoning, sage, and chicken broth.  Bring to a boil over medium heat reduce heat to medium, and simmer (covered) for about 45 minutes.
Season to taste with garlic salt and pepper. Stir in the rice and return to a boil over medium-high heat. Lower heat to medium, and simmer 15 minutes. Stir in the peas, and continue to simmer until rice is tender, about 10 minutes more. Adjust seasonings to taste.

Submitter: 
Margaret A. Schwarz
Description: 
This classic turkey soup makes fine use of leftover turkey with added vegetables such as carrots, celery, onions, peas and white rice.Modified from: Day-After-Thanksgiving Turkey/Rice  Soup
