Title:
Pork and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds dried cannellini beans
4 pounds boneless pork shoulder
2 slices thick bacon, diced
8 garlic cloves, peeled
2 onions, chopped
2 carrots, diced
6 cups chicken stock
6 tomatoes, peeled, seeded and chopped
1 cup dry white wine
1 tablespoon salt

Directions:
In large bowl, soak beans in enough warm water to cover for 3 hours. Add more water when needed. Drain and rinse. Cut pork into cubes. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cook bacon in dutch oven over medium until crisp. remove with slotted spoon and reserve. Turn heat to high and brown pork in small batches. Remove pork and discard bacon fat. Put pork back in dutch oven with vegetables, bacon, beans and broth. Bring to a simmer on the stovetop. Cover and put in oven for 1 hour. (Check every 20 minutes that it is still simmering. If not, place back on stove top and bring to simmer; then return to oven.) Remove and stir in tomatoes, wine and salt. Cook 1 hour longer.

Submitter: 
PIPPYMOE
Description: 
This is a lovely spin of ham and beans, using cubed pork roast instead.
