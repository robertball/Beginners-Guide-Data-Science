Title:
Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 qt. home made chicken stock
Carrots
2 stalks Celery
4 sm. cloves garlic (minced)
2 large Potatoes cubed
Chopped Chicken
2 good bunches of fresh Thyme
2 Bay Leaves
spinkle of dried basil
2 sprinkles of dried tarragon
S & P to taste
Roix made with butter/flour/milk

Directions:
Slice carrots and Celery thinly then fry in vegetable oil till soft but not cooked and add garlic at end.
Add Potatoes and cover with water and cook till done.
Add Chicken Stock.
Add spices and herbs.
Make roix and drizzle in while wisking.
When it is the right consistancy season for finish.

Submitter: 
Dale Stevens
Description: 
A hearty Stew made with home made stock, rotisary chicken, celery, carrots, Potatoes and onions.
