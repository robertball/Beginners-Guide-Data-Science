Title:
CREAMED SPINACH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 oz) can cream of celery soup
1 tablespoon flour
4 tablespoons of butter or margarine
20 oz. cooked and drained spinach
1 small onion, finely diced
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a saucepan on medium heat, stir together with wire whisk, soup, flour, butter, garlic powder, salt and pepper until smooth and piping hot.  Combine with spinach and onion and stir well.  Great side dish for fish.

Submitter: 
Graden
Description: 
Even spinach haters will eat this.  This dish has the flavor of a spinach side dish offered by one of our national chain restaurants.  Enjoy!
