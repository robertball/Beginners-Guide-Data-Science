Title:
Chickpea Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TB evoo
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 red pepper flakes
3 cups cooked chickpeas
28 oz. crushed tomatoes
14.5 o chicken broth
1 sprig fresh basil
1 pkg fresh mozzarella
12 oz pasta

Directions:
Heat oil, add garlic and red pep, brown 30 sec.  Add chickpeas, warm 5 min, season w/ salt and pep if desired.
Add tomatoes and broth, bring to boil.  Reduce to simmer and cook until sauce has reduced slightly.
Cook pasta.
Stir basil in to sauce and serve immediately over pasta.
Individuals add mozzarella to own servings if desired.

Like traditional spaghetti, but vegetarian.....fresher and lighter too.
