Title:
Alysha's cabbage salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666749.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cabbage head finely chopped or shredded
1 red cabbage head finely chopped or shredded
1 medium red onion diced
1 each small-medium  red, orange or yellow bell
pepper diced
1 large tomato cored, seeded & diced
1 fresh jalapeno seeded & diced
a small bunch of cilantro( about a handful)
1 cup white vinegar
2 tbsp salt
3/4 black pepper

Directions:
place cabbage in a non metal mixing bowl, add onion, tomato & bell pepper. mix well
chop the cilantro , add to the cabbage mix than add the vinegar and mix again add salt & pepper. cover and place in refrigerator  for an hour. mix again before serving
eat with chips or as a garnish on tacos.

my own spin on the cabbage salsa served at many Mexican restaurant
