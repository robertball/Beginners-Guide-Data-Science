Title:
White Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon yeast (easiest just to use a small packet)
1 tablespoon sugar
3 cups warm water
1 tablespoon salt
1 tablespoon olive oil
6 cups all purpose flour

Directions:
Combine yeast, sugar and warm water in a large bowl. Let sit in a cold oven with the oven light on for 15 minutes.
Add salt, oil and flour. Turn out on a floured surface and knead until all the air is out.
Put back into bowl, cover and let dough rise for one hour in cold oven with the oven light on.
Knead again until all air is gone. Divide dough between two loaf pans and spray tops of bread with cooking spray.
Cover pans and return to the cold oven with the oven light on for 45 minutes.
After final rise, turn oven to 350 degrees and bake for 50 minutes.
