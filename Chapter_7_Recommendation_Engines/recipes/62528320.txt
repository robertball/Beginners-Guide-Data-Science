Title:
Make-Ahead Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/739812.jpg
Ingredients:
10 pounds Russet potatoes, cooked and mashed
1 1/2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
16 ounces sour cream
4-5 garlic cloves
1/2 cup milk
2 teaspoons onion salt
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine mashed potatoes, cream cheese, sour cream, milk, onion salt, and pepper to taste. Mix well and place in a crockpot.
Cover and heat for 2-2 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
I made these for our Church Thanksgiving Dinner so I could make them when I had time.
You can make these ahead several days and store in the fridge. If baking cold, let stand 30 minutes first. Originally submitted to ThanksgivingRecipe.com.
