Title:
Easy Cheesey Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz Fresh/Frozen Hashbrowns
2 cans Cream of Chicken Soup
8 oz Cream Cheese
3/4 cup Milk
2 cups of Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Mix Cream of Chicken Soup, Cream Cheese and Milk on stove in pot until smooth...Place Hashbrowns in casserole dish, pour mixer over potatoes and bake 1 1/4 hours at 350 degrees...Remove from oven and cover with Cheddar Cheese
