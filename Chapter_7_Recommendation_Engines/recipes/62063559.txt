Title:
Jalapeno Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups bottled Ranch dressing (I use Hidden Valley)
1 tbsp fresh cilantro (half if using dry), minced
1/4 cup pickled jalapenos, chopped
1 tomato, chopped
1 avocado, peeled and chopped (optional)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth. Best if chilled for 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Nicole
Description: 
A spicy twist on traditional Ranch dressing.  I use this on taco salads and as a dipping sauce for taquitos, mini-tacos, jalapeno poppers, etc. It's good with or without the avocado! Add more peppers to taste. I make a batch with habaneros for my father-in-law, too!
