Title:
Quick n Easy Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup softened butter or margarine
3 cups confectioners' sugar
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
2 tbsp milk
For Chocolate Buttercream Frosting:
Add 2-3 tbsp of cocoa, to taste.

Directions:
Blend butter and sugar. Stir in vanilla and 2 tbsp milk, and cocoa if desired, beat until frosting is smooth and of desired consistency, adding more milk 1/4 tsp at a time if too thick.

Submitter: 
CBerrier86
Description: 
This buttercream frosting is so simple and easy, as well as kid friendly! You can flavor it with whatever you like, but vanilla and chocolate are my favorite.
