Title:
MA's Kickin Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5472730.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can garbanzo beans - drained/rinsed (save 1/2 cup liquid from the can)
1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce (add more if  you want more spice)
6 stuffed green olives (with pimento)
1/2-3/4 teaspoon sesame seed oil
3 Tablespoons olive oil
1/2 liquid saved from the drained beans
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (or 2 cloves mashed garlic)

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a mini blender and blend on high speed until smooth.   Cover and chill for at least an hour before serving.
Suggestion:  Use as a spread for  veggie wraps!!!

Submitter: 
MA McBridges
Description: 
Full of protein and flavor I think this is my best yet!  Serve traditionally with naan, as a veggie dip, with pita or tortilla chips!    It's delicious with a lil spicy kick of chipotle pepper!
