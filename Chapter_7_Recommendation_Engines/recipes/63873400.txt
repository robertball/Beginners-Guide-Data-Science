Title:
Cherry Bits Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CUP SOFTENED  BUTTER
1 BEATEN EGG
1 1/2 CUP CONFECTION SUGAR
1 TEASPOON ALMOND EXTRACT
2 CUPS FLOUR
1 TEASPOON BAKING POWDER
1 - 11 OZ. JAR MARACHINO CHERRIES DRAINED & CHOPPED -RESERVE JUICE FOR GLAZE

Directions:
BEAT UNTIL SMOOTH:
1 CUP SOFTENED BUTTER
1 BEATEN EGG
1 1/2 CUP CONFECTION SUGAR
1 TEASPOON ALMOND EXTRACT
GRADUALLY ADD FLOUR & BAKING POWDER TO ABOVE MIXTURE.
FOLD IN MARACHINO CHERRIES.
COVER AND REFRIGERATE AT LEAST ONE HOUR
GLAZE: COMBINE RESERVED CHERRY JUICE AND CONFECTIONER SUGAR (ABOUT 1/2 C) YOU MAY ADD MORE CONFECTIONER SUGAR TO GET DESIRED CONSISTANCY. YOU DON'T WANT THE GLAZE TO BE TOO RUNNY. SET ASIDE.
PRE HEAT OVEN TO 375*
ROLL BALL ABOUT THE SIZE OF A SMALL WALNUT (ABOUT A  TEASPOON)
BAKE AT 375 DEGREES ON UNGREASED COOKIE SHEET FOR 6-8 MINUTES COOKIES SHOULD NOT BROWN.  COOL ON RACK AND GLAZE

Submitter: 
Krista
Description: 
My Mother always made these each Christmas! They are easy and delicious!
