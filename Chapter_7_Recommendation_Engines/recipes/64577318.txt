Title:
4 Ingredient Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 fresh squash sliced
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 sleeve Ritz crackers crushed
1 can/bottle spaghetti sauce

Directions:
Layer in covered baking dish the sliced squash, Parmesan and cracker crumbs - reserving 1/8 cup each crumbs and cheese.
Pour entire amount of prepared sauce on top of all layers.
Top with reserved cheese and crumbs.
Bake covered 45 mins at 350.
Remove lid and cook 15 more mins to brown top.
Let rest about 30 mins prior to serving for full cracker crumb absorption of excess liquid.
