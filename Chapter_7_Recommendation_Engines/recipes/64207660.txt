Title:
Healthy Ham and Egg Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large eggs
3/4c egg whites
7oz diced ham
1 1/2C frozen spinach
1c diced red pepper
1/4c feta
2 Tbsp water
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Prepare 12 muffin tins with spray oil or muffin liners and set aside.
Incorporate all ingredients in a large bowl and mix well.
Ladle egg mixture into muffin tins and bake for 20-25 minutes.
These freeze well and can be reheated at anytime.

Submitter: 
blue tuberosa
Description: 
This is a quick & healthy breakfast option that you can make in advance and freeze. Simply reheat on your way out the door in the morning.
