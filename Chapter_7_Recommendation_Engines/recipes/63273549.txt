Title:
White Bread (Bread Machine) Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup Milk
4 teaspoons
3 cups bread flour
4 teaspoons sugar
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon active dry yeast

Directions:
Layer all ingredients with flour then yeast last.
Cook on basic in bread machine.
