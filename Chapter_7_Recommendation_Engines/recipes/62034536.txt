Title:
Grandma's Breading and Gravy for  Chicken Fried Steak  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Bisquick
2 cups Flour
1 cup Bread crumbs (seasoned or unseasoned)
1 Dry Italian salad dressing Packet or -Fried Chicken Seasoning (optional)
2 cups Milk
3 Tablespoons of White Vinegar
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Bisquik Mixture: Mix Bisquik, bread crumbs, seasonings and 1/2 the flour in a bowl.
For Binder: In a seperate bowl mix vinegar and 1/2 the milk together, allow a couple minutes for this to thicken.
Take your cubed steak or chicken and coat with remaining milk, followed by dredging in remaining flour. For final coating dip meat into the Vinegar/Milk  mixture and then dredge in the bisquik mixture. Cook according to your own fried chicken or chicken fried steak recipe.
GRAVY:
3 Chicken Boullion cubes
1 1/2 cups Water
Bisquik to thicken
Gravy: Wisk chicken boulion, water and bisquik in saucepan on medium/high heat. Wisk until bubbly and thickened; serve over Entree.

Submitter: 
DIXYCHIK
Description: 
This recipe is designed for  frying. A Perfect Crunchy, Tasty, Golden Coating Everytime! The vinegar and milk thicken to a buttermilk like consitancy, and  Bisquick is the secret for a perfect thick crunchy breading that imparts richness and delivers restaurant style flavor.
