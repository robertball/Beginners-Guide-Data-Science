Title:
Swedish Rye Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/817168.jpg
Ingredients:
7 tsp yeast
5 C warm water
1 C sugar
4 C rye flour
2T salt
1 C molasses
1 C shortening, melted
1 or 2 drops anise oil
3T gluten or whole wheat flour
2T caraway seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Dissolve yeast in 1/4 cup warm water.
Let work for 15 to 20 min.
Pour 5 C warm water in large bowl.
Mix all ingredients together and add enough white flour to make dough knead well
Let rise until double in bulk, punch down and let rise again
Put into loaves makes 6 loaves
Bake for 20min then turn them around for 25 min.
