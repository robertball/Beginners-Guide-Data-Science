Title:
OATY CHEESE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1/2 cup quick cooking oatmeal
1/4 cup nutritional yeast
2 Tbsp cornstarch or tapioca starch (stretchy)
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1/2 Tbsp onion powder
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 (4 oz) jar diced pimento

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and process several minutes until very smooth and well blended. Pour into a saucepan, cook and stir until thickened.

A quick and easy cheese sauce.
