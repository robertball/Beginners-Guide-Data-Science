Title:
Rhonda's Chicken Stroganoff with vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken, cubed
1 can chicken broth
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 can cream of celery soup
8 ounces sour cream
1 pound baby carrots
1 pound red potatoes, peeled
1 can white corn, drained
1 bunch green onions
1 shallot
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 bell pepper, chopped
16 ounces egg noodles
2 TBSP olive oil
2tsp poultry seasoning
1 tsp sage
salt and pepper to taste
1 stick butter
1 pkg frozen broccoli

Directions:
In large add chicken, sage, poultry seasoning, salt and pepper and enough water to cover.  Bring to a boil.  Add 1/2 stick butter, carrots and potatoes.  Cook until carrots are tender.  In a medium saucepan add olive oil and heat til hot but not smoking.  Add green onions, shallot, garlic and bell pepper to oil and saute until almost tender.  Add drained corn and other 1/2 stick butter.  Saute til liquid is removed.  Add mixture to chicken and vegetables.  Stir in soups and broth, and cook 5 minutes. Add broccoli, and egg noodles and cook until noodles are done to desired tenderness. Simmer until liquid is almost completely gone.  Remove from heat and stir in sour cream til smooth.  Serve.  You can substitute low or no fat sour cream and soups, and even omit the butter in order to make this healthier,

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
I just made this up one day with things I had around the house and it was a huge hit, even for my picky eater.
