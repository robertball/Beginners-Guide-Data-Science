Title:
Oxtail Meat Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oxtails OR 1 tail and rest stew meat, or one rabbit.
4 T. butter
salt and pepper to taste
3 stalks celery, chopped
1 large onion, chopped
2 T. flour
1 c. dry white wine
1 28 oz. can Contadina italian style tomatoes, chopped w/ juice
1 c. beef stock
1 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
1/2 t. sugar
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/4 lb. sliced mushrooms

Directions:
THE DAY BEFORE SERVING:
Lightly flour the oxtails/meat and saute in foaming butter.
Add celery, carrots and onion and stir w/ wooden spoon.
Add flour and wine, simmer for another minute.
Add remaining ingredients.
Cover and place in the 375&#0176; for about 2 hours.  Refrigerate.
THE DAY OF SERVING:
Remove from fridge, skim fat and remove bones.
Place mixture in a 3 quart baking dish.
Cover with pastry, puncturing several holes in it and brush w/ beaten egg or milk.
Bake for 30-45 minutes until the crust is golden brown and pie is heated through.
Brush w/ egg after 15 minutes.
