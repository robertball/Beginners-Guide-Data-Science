Title:
Authentic Red Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 tbsp garlic, minced
2 cups white onions, minced
2 cups green pepper, diced
1 cup celery, diced
1/4 cup fresh parsley, minced
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
2 bay leaves
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp cumin
3 tsp Tabasco sauce
1 tbsp sugar
salt (to taste)
pepper (to taste)
4 tomatoes, diced
4 cans kidney beans, drained
1 lb polish sausage, cooked and cubed
1/2 lb Andouille sausage, cooked and cubed
8 cups cooked rice
1 cup green onions, minced (1/2 for cooking, 1/2 for garnish)

Directions:
Heat oil and saut garlic, white onion, green pepper, green onion and celery until
onions are translucent.  Add parsley, cayenne, bay leaf, thyme, cumin, tabasco, sugar, salt, pepper, tomatoes and kidney beans.
Mix well and let heat thoroughly.  Remove 1 cup to a food processor and puree.
Add back to the pot and stir in.  Add sausages and cook for 30 minutes.  Remove the bay leaves and mix with the rice.  Serve with green
onions for garnish and Tabasco for those that want it hotter.

Submitter:

Description: 
After visiting New Orleans several times I wanted to make that authentic taste in my kitchen.
After many attempts, this is the recipe that I think is the most authentic.  It is so delicious on a cold winter day!  Serve it with cornbread, of course!
