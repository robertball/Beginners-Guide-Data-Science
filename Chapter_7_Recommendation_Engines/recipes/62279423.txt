Title:
Sugar-Free Creamy Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon unsweetened dark cocoa powder (such as Hershey's Special Dark)
a dash of salt
1 cup 2% milk
3 packets sucralose sweetener, such as Splenda
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Place cocoa powder, salt, and sweetener into a mug.
Heat milk in the microwave on high 1 1/2 to 2 minutes or until hot.
Add milk to cocoa mixture gradually, stirring to combine. Stir in vanilla extract.
Top with light whipped cream or marshmallows if desired.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
Like hot chocolate but need a sugar-free version? This version is made with sugar substitute and is perfect for diabetics. Use skim milk if you prefer a fat free version.
