Title:
Hot Cross Buns (Bread machine) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C milk
2 eggs
3 TBSP butter
1/4 C Sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
3-3 1/4 C flour
1 1/2 tsp yeast
At end of kneading cycle 3/4 C Raisins
For brushing:
1 egg yolk and 2 tsp water
For Frosting:
1 C confectioners sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 TBSP milk

Directions:
Put ingredients in order into bread machine on dough cycle. About 5 minutes prior to end of kneading cycle add raisins. If you miss this point at the end of the cycle completion knead in raisins by hand. After kneading let dough rest for about 10 minutes. Cut dough into 12 pieces and shape into balls and place into a greased baking dish. Cover and let rise about 35 minutes or until doubled in size. Score a cross onto each bun. Brush with egg yolk and water mixture. Bake in a preheated oven of 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
Drizzle with frosting shaped into a cross(in the score)
