Title:
The Colbert Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One can of any flavor Pringles Super Stacks
One can whipped cream
Favorite beverage

Directions:
Get plate and set on counter, open Pringles can spread chips on plate.
Put on something to watch on the tube and take plate of chips and can of whipped cream to couch.
Take one chip and apply whipped cream then place another chip on top to make The Colbert Sandwich.
Enjoy with favorite beverage.

A delicious blend of salt and sweet. Very decadent.
