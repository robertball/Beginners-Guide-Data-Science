Title:
HOT Pepper Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C hot peppers - no seeds
1/2 C mild pepper - no seeds
2 C cider vinegar
1 1/2 C dried apricots - chopped
6 C sugar
3 oz liquid pectin

Directions:
Puree all fruit and peppers with some of the vinegar.
Pour into a pot and add the rest of the vinegar and sugar.
Boil 5 min, skim any foam that forms.
Cool 2 min.
Add pectin and food coloring if desired.
Pour into sterile jars and close lids.
Process in a hot water bath for 10 min.
