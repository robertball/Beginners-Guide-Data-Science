Title:
Wilted Leaf Lettuce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion (diced)
5 strips bacon (cut small into pieces)
2 tablespoons water
1/3 cup vinegar
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Slowly sautee onion and bacon in fry pan, until onion is cooked and bacon is crisp.  DON&#8217;T DRAIN.  Add water, vinegar, sugar and salt.  Heat and pour over leaf lettuce pieces, toss and serve. Also goes well over spinach.
