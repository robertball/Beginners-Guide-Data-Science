Title:
Bacon and Cheese Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/287123.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 Tbs baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
5 Tbs shortening
1 cup) buttermilk
4-5 slices bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled
1 cup grated Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Combine the dry ingredients in a bowl and cut in the shortening until it resembles coarse crumbs. Add the buttermilk and stir just enough to form a ball.  Fold in the bacon and cheese. Drop heaping tablespoonfuls on an ungreased baking sheet and bake in a preheated 450F oven until golden brown, 10 to 12 minutes. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
I Love Troy
Description: 
Easy drop biscuits.  Although cheddar taste the best in this recipe, you can sub any other cheese you have on hand.
