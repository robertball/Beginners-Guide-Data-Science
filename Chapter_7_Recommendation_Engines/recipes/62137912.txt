Title:
Peach Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
First Layer:
1 c. flour
1/2 c. margarine
1 T. sugar
1/2 c. chopped pecans
Second Layer:
1 c. sugar
1 (9oz) carton whipped topping
8 oz. cream cheese
3 c. sliced fresh peaches
Glaze:
1 c. sugar
1 (3oz) pkg. Peach Jell-O
4 T. clear jel
1 1/2 c. water

Directions:
First Layer:
Mix all ingredients and spread in a 9 x 13 baking dish.  Bake at 350 for 20 mintues.
Second Layer:
Cream sugar and cream cheese.  Fold in whipped topping.  Spread mixture over crust.  Top cream cheese mixture with peaches.
Glaze:
Combine sugar and clear jel. Stir in water.  Cook until thick.  Stir in peach gelatin until thoroughly blended.  Cool.  Spoon glaze over peaches. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Tyler
Description: 
This is like a 4-layer Delight, but with peaches.
