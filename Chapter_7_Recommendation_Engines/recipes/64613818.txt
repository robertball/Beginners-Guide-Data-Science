Title:
Blackberry Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2-lb frozen cobbler
1 white cake mix
1&1/4  c milk
2 eggs
1/4 c melted butter
1/2 c flour
1/3 c sugar
1/3 c packed brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 c cold butter

Directions:
Bake cobbler at 400 on baking sheet for 50-60 minutes as package directs.
Stir together cake mix, milk, eggs and melted butter.
Spoon into greased 9x13 pan.
Spread cobbler evenly over top (chop up as needed).
Combine rest of ingredients and cut in cold butter till crumbly.
Sprinkle over cobbler.
Bake at 350 for 45-50 minutes.
