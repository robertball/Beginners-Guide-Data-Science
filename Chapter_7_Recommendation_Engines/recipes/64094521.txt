Title:
Beef with Oyster Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb flank steak, sliced
4 Tbsp oil
1/2 lb asparagus, cut diagonally
4 Tbsp chicken stock
3 strips ginger, julienne
3 scallions, julienne
1 clove garlic, minced
3 Tbsp oyster sauce
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp sherry

Directions:
Stir fry beef in 2 Tbsp oil at 400 degrees F until color changes and remove.
Reheat 2 Tbsp oil to 400 degrees F and stir fry asparagus for 2 minutes.  Add 2 Tbsp stock and cook 1 minute, until crunchy.  Remove and set aside.
Reheat 1 Tbsp oil to 275 degrees and stir fry ginger, scallion and garlic for 1 minute.  Add oyster sauce, soy sauce, sugar, sherry and remaining 2 Tbsp stock.   Add asparagus and turn to 425 degrees F.  When sauce boils, add beef and stir fry quickly until heated through.  Serve with rice.
