Title:
Feta-Tomato Omelet on Naan Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 tbsp. feta
2 tbsp. water
1 1/2 tsp. oregano
1 tsp. pepper
1 diced tomato
1 slice naan bread
oil

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet on medium heat.
Beat eggs with feta, water, oregano and pepper.  Pour into skillet.
When eggs are almost set, add tomatoes.  Cook til eggs are set.
Serve on top of Naan.

Submitter: 
linda
Description: 
Feta, tomato and oregano lend a wonderful flavour to this omelet.  It can also be served on pita bread, but I prefer the naan since it's a bit thicker.
