Title:
Sweet and Savory Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/598764.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/4 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup chopped onions
1 clove garlic, minced
1 egg (beaten)
1 tbs cooking oil
2 cans mushroom soup
4 tbs white vinegar
4 tbs brown sugar
4 tbs soy sauce
Pinch of pepper
1 package broad egg noodles

Directions:
Mix together beef, bread crumbs, onion, garlic, and egg. Shape into 16 meatballs.
Add oil to a frying pan and brown meatballs.
In a bowl combine soup, vinegar, brown sugar, and soy sauce, stir until well combined and smooth.
Add sauce to meatballs, cover, and cook over low heat for 20-25 minutes, stir occasionally.
Serve over boiled egg noodles.
