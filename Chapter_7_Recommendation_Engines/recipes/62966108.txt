Title:
Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick AND 2 TBS butter
1 1/4 sugar
1 egg
3TBS vanilla
1 tsp baking powder
1tsp bakind soda dissolved in 1/2 cup buttermilk
1/4 tsp salt
31/2 to 5 cups + flour

Directions:
Mixx all together. Add flour last, gradually to stiffen.
Bake 10-12 minutes at 350 on ungreased sheet
Note- these cookies are thick and expand when baked, so tiny or detailed cookie cutters are not reccomended.

Submitter: 
Lisa Ann Critchfield
Description: 
These cookies were sold at a church in my hometown. They are not too sweet and are intended to eat without icing, but I usually add it =)
