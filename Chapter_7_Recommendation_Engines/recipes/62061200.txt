Title:
Korean Wontons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded cabbage
1 cup canned bean sprouts
1/2 cup shredded carrots
1 1/2 teas. plus 2 Tbls. veg. oil (divided)
1/3 lb. ground beef
1/3 cup sliced green onion
1 1/2 teas. sesame seeds, toasted
1/2 teas. ground ginger
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 teas. sesame oil
1/2 teas. salt
1/2 teas. pepper
1 package wonton wraps
1 egg lightly beaten
3 Tbls. water

Directions:
Stir fry cabbage, bean sprouts, & carrots in 1 1/2 teas. oil until tender. (Set aside) Cook beef until no longer pink. Drain. Add to veggie mixture, stir in the onions, seeds, ginger, garlic, sesame oil, salt & pepper. Put 1 Tbls. filling in each wonton, and fry at 375* F, for 1-2 minutes each side until light brown or brown.
