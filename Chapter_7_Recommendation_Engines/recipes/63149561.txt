Title:
Black Bean Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Quinoa
2 Cups Water (or broth)
2-3 Cloves Garlic, minced
1 Can Black Beans, drained and rinsed
1 Tbl. Butter
Parmesan Cheese to taste (optional)
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Rinse Quinoa and bring it to a boil with water or broth.  Reduce heat to low, add minced garlic, and cover.
When the water has been absorbed and the Quinoa is done, add Black Beans and Butter.  Stir occasionally for 5 minutes or until the Black Beans are nice and hot.
Add Salt & Pepper to your liking and top with a little parmesan cheese.

Description:
