Title:
Blackberry Mango Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/846787.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 9" refrigerated pie crust, 3 eggs, 1 C sugar, 1/4 C butter or margarine- melted, 1 C mango puree, 2 Tbsp lemon juice, 6 oz fresh blackberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 & spray pie pan with nonstick spray. Place pie crust in pan, trimming edges to fit & set aside.
In a double boiler (or mixing bowl set on top of simmering pot) combine eggs & sugar. Add melted butter, mango puree, & lemon juice. Cook over simmering water for 15 minutes, stirring frequently.
Remove from heat & pour into prepare pie crust, let sit 5 minutes.
Arrange blackberries evenly on mango mixture.
Bake 15-20 minutes until semi-firm set & starting to brown on top.
Chill completely before serving.

Submitter: 
Jaree
Description: 
I'm not sure if you can qualify this as a tart or pie, BUT fresh fruit & minimal amounts of sugar make for a delicious summer dessert that looks harder than it is.
