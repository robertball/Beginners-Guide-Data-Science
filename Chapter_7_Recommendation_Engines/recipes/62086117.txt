Title:
Citrus Herb Vinnaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
1/4 cup olive oil
2tbsp. orange juice
2 tbsp. dijon mustard
2 tsp. oregano
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. basil
dash salt and pepper

Directions:
Mix ingredients with whisk in a bowl or shake in a salad dressing jar and serve.

Submitter: 
rebeccalovestocook
Description: 
I made this up one night while trying to put an end to the same old salad dressing.  It has a nice flavor with a little tang and is quick and simple to make!
