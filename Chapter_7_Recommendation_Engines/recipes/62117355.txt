Title:
Cranberry Sauce with Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Apple Cider
3/4 Cup Sugar
4 whole Cinnamon Sticks
16 whole Cloves (adjust to taste)
1 Orange
2 1/2 Cups whole Cranberries

Directions:
With paring knife, peel away ZEST from Orange and set aside zest.
Juice Orange and set aside juice.
In medium saucepan, add first four ingredients.
Add reserved juice and zest from Orange.
Stirring occasionally, heat to boil.
Reduce heat and simmer 10-12 minutes.
Add Cranberries and increase heat to bring to a boil.
Cook on high while stirring constantly until Cranberries begin to pop.
Remove from heat and remove Orange zest and Cinnamon sticks.
Pour into your favorite serving dish to cool and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Jay Lynne Stuart
Description: 
Cranberries, oranges, apples, cinnamon and cloves; this one has it all and it's soooo easy!
