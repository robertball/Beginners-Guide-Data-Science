Title:
Chicken and Zucchini Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Zucchini - diced
1 onion - diced
1 1/2 cup uncooked rice (cook as directed)
2 Chicken Breasts cooked and diced
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp lemon pepper
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 tbsp Italian seasoning
1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
Saute onion and zucchini.
Combine onion and zucchini with salt, lemon pepper, garlic powder and Italian seasoning.
Let this cool for a bit and add Parmesan cheese.
Mix Chicken, Cheddar Cheese, and rice into onion's and zuccini.
Put in a baking dish
Bake uncovered at 350 F for 30-45 minutes.
