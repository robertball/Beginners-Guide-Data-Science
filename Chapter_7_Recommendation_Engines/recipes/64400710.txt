Title:
Potato Applesauce Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs red new potatoes, cooked, cooled, cut
3 stalks celery chopped fine
6 T unsweetened applesauce
1/4 c dijon mustard
1/2 c minced shallots
ground pepper
fresh chopped dill opt

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix everything except the potatoes. Add in the potatoes and mix.
