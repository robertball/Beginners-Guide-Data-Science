Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies - from Time-Warp Wife Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 cup butter (softened)
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1 small package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
12 oz semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Combine flour, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
In a large mixing bowl beat together butter, sugars, vanilla and pudding mix. Add eggs, mix well. Add flour mixture , mix well. Stir in chocolate chips.
Place in refrigerator for 1 hour.
Place heaping Tbsp of batter onto un-greased cookie sheet and bake until golden (10-12 mins). Take out of oven and let sit for 2 mins before transporting to a wire rack. Enjoy!
