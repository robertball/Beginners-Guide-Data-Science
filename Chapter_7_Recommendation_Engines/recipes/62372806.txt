Title:
Bread and Butter Pickles by whodunitrdr Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts cucumbers; sliced 1/8" thick
1 quart onions; sliced 1/8" thick
1/3 cup salt
3 cups vinegar
2 1/2 cups sugar
3 tablespoons mustard seed
3 teaspoons celery seed
1 1/2 teaspoons turmeric
1 1/2 teaspoons alum

Directions:
Sprinkle salt over cucumbers and onion in a large kettle, let stand for one hour and then drain off the liquid.
Add remaining ingredients except the alum powder, stir together and bring to a boil to scald the pickles.
Sprinkle alum powder over mixture, stir to combine, then ladle pickles into hot jars and seal.
Process in water bath for 5 minutes for pints, 10 minutes for quarts. (Start counting time after water returns to a boil.)
Makes about 5 pints.

Submitter: 
whodunitrdr
Description: 
This is the recipe that Mom always made and it remains a favorite. Store bought pickles just do not compare to these. Recipe can be doubled and tripled as long as you have a large enough kettle.
