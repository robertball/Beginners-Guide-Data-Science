Title:
Baily's Irish Creme Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1 c graham cracker crumbs
3 TBLS sugar
3 TBLS melted butter
Filling
3 8oz. packages cream cheese
1 c sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 c sour creme
1/3 c Baily's Irish Cream
4 eggs
Topping
1 c sour cream
1/4 c sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven 350
Crust
Mix graham cracker, sugar and melted butter.  Press in 10"   springform pan, bottom and sides.  Bake for 10 minutes.
Filling
Cream sugar and cream cheese, add eggs then vanilla extract and sour cream. Mix until smooth.  Pour into crust in springform pan.  Bake for 60 minutes.
Topping
Mix sour cream and sugar until smooth.  Spoon onto filling and smooth out.  Bake for 10 minutes longer.  let cool and refridgerate overnight preferably and ENJOY!
