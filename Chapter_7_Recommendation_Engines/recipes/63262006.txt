Title:
Grandma Ethel's apple butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12-14 apples
2c apple juice
sugar
cinnamon
allspice
cloves

Directions:
Wash, core and quarter apples. Combine apples with juice and cook until tender.
Press through a sieve to remove peel. Measure remaining fruit and for each pint add 1c sugar, 1t cinnamon, 1/2 t allspice. 1/2t cloves.
Stir together, cover and cook on high in a slow cooker for 6-8 hours, stirring every 2 hours.
Remove cover after 3 hours to allow it to cook down.
Spoon into hot sterilized jars and process in boiling water bath to seal.
