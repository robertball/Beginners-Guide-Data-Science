Title:
Crunchy Cranberry and White Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup oil
1 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 t vanilla
1 t baking soda
1 t salt
3 1/3 cups flour
2 cups corn flake crumbs
1 c dried cranberries
1 c white chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. thoroughly cream butter and sugar.  Add oil, egg, vanilla, baking soda, and salt.  incorporate flour, corn flake crumbs, dried cranberries, and white chocolate chips.  Place rounded tablespoons one inch apart on an ungreased cookie sheet and flatten slightly with fork.  bake for 14-15 minutes or until lightly golden brown.

Submitter: 
Anisa
Description: 
These cookies are rich and crunchy, perfect for holiday serving and sharing.
