Title:
Cold Coffee Break Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5557206.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp butter, melted
4 Individual 1.7 oz Donut Sticks
4 cups Coffee Ice Cream, softened
2 - 3.4 oz Instant Vanilla Pudding & Pie Filling
1/2 cup milk
2 Tbsp. Instant Coffee Granules
1 - 8 oz Tub Cool Whip, softened
6 Mini Chocolate Frosted Donuts

Directions:
1. In a food processor or blender process 4 donut sticks until finely ground. In a bowl combine the ground donut sticks together with melted butter and press into the bottom  of an 8 x 8 glass baking dish. Freeze 10 minutes.
2. Spread softened coffee ice cream over the frozen crust and place back into freezer for about 5 minutes.
3. Meanwhile combine milk, 2 boxes of vanilla pudding, and ground coffee granules until smooth. Fold in cool whip topping and spread over the coffee ice cream.
4. Cover with plastic wrap and place in freezer for 2 hours. Before serving place mini chocolate donuts over the top.

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
Donut Sticks on the bottom layered with cold coffee ice cream and flavored whipped topping. Chocolate donuts placed on the top and your sweet tooth will be filled with this rich dessert!
