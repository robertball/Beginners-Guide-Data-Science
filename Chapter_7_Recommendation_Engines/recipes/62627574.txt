Title:
Pistachio and Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 6oz cans Tuna, drained
1/4 cup pistachios, shelled
2 stalks green onions, chopped
3 tbsp Mango Chutney
2 tbsp Mayo, light is fine
1 tbsp Olive Oil
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl toss togerther the tuna, pistachios and green onions, set aside. In a seperate bowl whisk together the mango chutney,mayo and olive oil until smooth. Pure over the tuna mixture and toss to combine. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
jennifer macias
Description: 
This recipe is a variation of an amazing tuna salad served at a small coffee roastery.  It's great on its own or as a sandwich!
