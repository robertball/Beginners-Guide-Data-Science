Title:
Chicken Caesar Salad Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979919.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped cooked chicken breast
1/4 cup low-fat Caesar salad dressing
1/4 cup finely diced red onions
2 tablespoons shredded Parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
2 slices Roman Meal Bread
6 leaves baby romaine lettuce
6 cherry tomato halves (optional)

Directions:
Combine chicken, dressing, red onions, cheese and parsley in small bowl.
Place 3 lettuce leaves on toast. Top each with 1/2 cup chicken salad. Garnish with tomatoes if desired.
