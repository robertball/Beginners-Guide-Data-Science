Title:
Mom's Zucchini Bread - tweaked w/review ideas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
3 eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
3 teaspoons vanilla extract
3 cups grated zucchini
Crumb topping:
1/2 cup regular oats
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Grease and flour two 8 x 4 inch pans.  Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Sift flour, salt, baking powder, soda, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
Beat eggs, oil, vanilla, applesauce, and sugar (white and brown) together in a large bowl.  Add sifted ingredients to the creamed mixture, and beat well.  Stir in zucchini until well combined.  Pour batter into prepared pans.
Bake for 15 minutes, then remove from the oven and top with crumb mixture. Bake for an additional 35 to 45 minutes, or until tester inserted in the center comes out clean.  Cool in pan on rack for 20 minutes.  Remove bread from pan, and completely cool.
