Title:
Rabo Encendido (Cubano) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs. Ox Tails
1/4 cup Olive Oil
1 1/2 cup Vino Seco
1 tsp Salt
2 cups Diced Onions
1 cup Diced Carrots
2 Potatoes quartered
6 Garlic Cloves; Mashed with 1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 Packet Sazon Accente
2 tsp Sazon Completa
1/2 tsp Allspice
3 Bay Leaves
1/4 cup Green Olives; halved
2 cans Tomatoe Sauce
3 cups Chicken Broth

Directions:
Marinate meat in vino seco, olive oil and salt for 12 hours. Drain and preserve 1/2 cup of marinade.
Brown oxtails all sides; set aside.
Saute onions, carrots and potatoes until onions are clear; then add garlic/salt mash and stir for additional 30 seconds.
Add remaining ingredients, bring to a boil and simmer until meat easily falls off the bone (roughly 4 hour).
