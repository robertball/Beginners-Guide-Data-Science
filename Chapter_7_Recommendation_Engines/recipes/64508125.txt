Title:
Jackie's Cranberry Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart boiling water
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup cinnamon imperial red hots
12 whole cloves
1 quart cranberry juice
6 ounces orange juice concentrate
6 ounces frozen lemonade concentrate
4 quarts boiling water

Directions:
Bring 1 quart of water to a boil.   Add sugar and red hots and cook until dissolved.
Add cranberry juice, orange juice, lemonade and 4 additional quarts of boiling water.
Simmer.   May freeze - keeps well.
