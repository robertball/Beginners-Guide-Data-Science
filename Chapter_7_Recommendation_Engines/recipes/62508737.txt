Title:
Low suger hot pepper Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/505272.jpg
Ingredients:
6 green Thai peppers
2 hot red peppers
3 yellow habanero peppers
4 small red sweet peppers or one red bell pepper
1 green bell pepper
1 ½ cup white vinegar
6 cups Splenda
1 box SURE JELL fruit pectin for less or no sugar needed recipes
¼ cup real sugar
1 cup water

Directions:
Seed and finely chop the peppers.
Combine the ¼ cup real sugar and sure jell set aside.
Combine vinegar and peppers in a medium pot.  Bring to a boil and simmer for 5 to 10 min.
In a large pot; add water and sure jell/sugar mix stir well till no clumps are noticeable.
Add in peppers and vinegar. Bring to a rolling boil (one that cannot be stirred down) boil 1-3 min.
Stirring constantly lower heat and add in the Splenda slowly, stir till dissolved.
Ladle into sterile jars leaving ¼ inch headspace.
Process jars in a hot water bath for 8 to 10 min.
Refrigerate jelly after seal is broken.

Submitter: 
Robograndma Davis
Description: 
After much research and reading I came up with a recipe that is low in sugar and jells well. Big flavor and nice heat. We grow our own peppers but you should be able to find most of them at your market.
