Title:
Rolo Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120634.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cup flour
3/4 cup cocoa
1 tsp. baking soda
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup margarine
2 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
48 Rolos
1 tablespoon sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Combine sugars, margarine, vanilla, and eggs.
Then add flour, cocoa, and baking soda. Mix well and put in fridge for 30 minutes.
Roll dough into 1 inch balls with 1 Rolo in center.
Roll each ball in sugar.
Bake 7-10 minutes
