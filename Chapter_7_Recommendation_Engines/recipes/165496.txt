Title:
Caesar Wedge with a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982452.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head Tanimura & Antle® Iceberg Lettuce
3/4 cup creamy Caesar dressing
1/2 cup chipotle salsa
1/2 cup sliced ripe olives
6 tablespoons shredded Parmesan cheese
Garlic crostini

Directions:
Core lettuce; rinse and drain well.  Cut into 6 wedges.
Mix dressing and salsa; spoon over wedges, as desired.  Sprinkle with olives and cheese.  Serve with crostini.

Submitter: 
Tanimura & Antle®
Description: 
Start with a crisp wedge of iceberg lettuce, top with a creamy Caesar and salsa dressing, then sprinkle with ripe olives and Parmesan cheese.
