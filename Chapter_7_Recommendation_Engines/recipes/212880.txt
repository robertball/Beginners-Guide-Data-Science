Title:
Minty Mint Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/319347.jpg
Ingredients:
1 onion, quartered
2 fresh green chile peppers
1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Blend the onion, green chile peppers, mint, lemon juice, and salt together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
vburrito
Description: 
Gives anything a nice little kick. Particularly good with Chaat Masala.
