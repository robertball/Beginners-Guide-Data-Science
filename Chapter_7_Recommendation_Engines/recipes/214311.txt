Title:
JELL-O Chocolate-Peanut Butter Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990117.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3.9 ounce) package JELL-O Chocolate Instant Pudding
2 cups cold milk
3 tablespoons cold milk
3 tablespoons peanut butter
1 cup thawed Cool Whip whipped topping

Directions:
Beat pudding mix and 2 cups milk with whisk 2 min.; set aside. Gradually add remaining milk to peanut butter in medium bowl, stirring with whisk until well blended. Stir in COOL WHIP.
Spoon half the pudding evenly into 6 parfait glasses; top with layers of peanut butter mixture and remaining pudding.
Refrigerate 30 min.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
Chocolate pudding is layered with a simple peanut butter mousse and chilled for this easy, creamy dessert.
