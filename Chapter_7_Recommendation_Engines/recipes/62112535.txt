Title:
Pumpkin Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp Canola Oil
1 Onion chopped
1 Red pepper chopped
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1lb ground turkey
2 (15 oz) cans diced tomatoes (I prefer the ones w/ chilis)
1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce,
1 (15 oz) can kidney beans, rinsed & drained
1 (4 oz) can diced green chilis
1/2 cup frozen corn, thawed
1 Tbsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat.  Saute the onion, garlic & pepper until tender.  Add the turkey and cook until brown.  Drain excess juices.  Add all the remaining ingredients.  Bring to a boil then reduce the heat to low.  Simmer for 30 mins stirring occasionally
*This recipe is even better reheated!

Submitter: 
Kristin Turrell
Description: 
This is my favorite fall dish.  I like to make a cornbread with a few tablespoons of molasses mixed in as an accompanyment.
