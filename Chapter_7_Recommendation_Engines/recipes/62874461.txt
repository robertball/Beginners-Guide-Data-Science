Title:
Sesame Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
for 8 servings
1 lb whole wheat spaghetti
1/2 cup reduced sodium soy sauce
2 tablespoons sesame oil
2 tbs canola oil
2 tbs rice wine vinegar
1 1/2 tps crushed red pepper
1 bunch scallions, minced divided
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro divided (optional)
4 cups snow peas trimmed and sliced on bias
1 medium red bell pepper (thinly sliced)
1/2 cup toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
cook spaghetti, drain, rinse under cold water
mix all other ingredients together including 1/4 cup scallions and 2 tbs cilantro. Add noodles, snow peas and bell pepper; toss to coat
to serve: mix in sesame seeds and garnish with remaining scalions and cilantro
