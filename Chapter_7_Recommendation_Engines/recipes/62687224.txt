Title:
Quick Sour Dough Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/603903.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. all-purpose flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. sugar
1 egg
1 c. sourdough
1 c. milk
3 tbsp. vegetable oil

Directions:
In large bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and sugar; set aside.  In a medium bowl, beat egg.  Stir in sourdough starter, milk and oil.  Stir into flour mixture until dry ingredients are just moistened.  Preheat griddle according to manufacturer's directions.  Or place griddle over medium-high heat until a drop of water bounces across surface.  Grease griddle.  For each pancake, pour 1/4 to 1/2 cup batter onto preheated griddle.  Cook 1-2 minutes on each side or until golden brown.  Makes 4-6 servings.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Light and airy.  Family favorite I've served up for over 35 years. I obtained an excellent starter with a strain of yeast  I've kept going since 1983. (See basic starter recipe in my Recipe Box).  Basic pancake recipe was from HP Books SOURDOUGH COOKERY published 1977 (out of print).
