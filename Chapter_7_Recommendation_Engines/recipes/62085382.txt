Title:
Tex Mex Black Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp olive oil
2 cans black beans, drained
1 jar salsa
2 tsp cinnamon
2 tbsp honey

Directions:
Sautee garlic in olive oil in large skillet for 2 minutes.  Add black beans, salsa, cinnamon & honey and bring to a simmer.  Allow to simmer for 20 minutes.  Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Mary Beth
Description: 
A twist on a family favorite for a quick and healthy vegetarian entree.
