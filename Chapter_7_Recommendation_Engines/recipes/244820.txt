Title:
Delicious Green Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2912647.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut milk
1 banana
1 mango - peeled, seeded, and chopped
3/4 cup fresh spinach, or to taste

Directions:
Blend coconut milk, banana, mango, and spinach together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
lynnv123
Description: 
An easy, delicious green juice. You can substitute kale for spinach.
