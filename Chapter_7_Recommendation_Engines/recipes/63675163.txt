Title:
Mango Cilantro Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbl white wine vinegar
2 tbl canola oil
2 tbl honey
2 tbl mayonnaise
1/2 tsp celery seed (optional)
1/2 tsp poppy seed (optional)
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 pkg (10 oz) finely shredded cabbage
3 green onions chopped
1 firm-ripe mango, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1/3 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
In a medium bowl whisk together the first 8 ingredients.  Add remaining ingredients and toss until well combined.   Chill for an hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Randy Warner
Description: 
This is different twist on cabbage slaw.  Very easy and the mango and honey makes is sweet, no sugar in this recipe.  Great by itself or wrap a corn tortilla around grilled tilapia and top with this cilantro slaw for a great tasting fish taco!
