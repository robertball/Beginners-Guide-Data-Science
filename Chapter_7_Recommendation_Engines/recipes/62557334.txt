Title:
Tonja's Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1   Cup Buttermilk
1   Teaspoon baking soda
1   Teaspoon baking powder
1   Cup White Sugar
1/2 Cup Shortening
1   Egg
2   Teaspoons Cinnamon
1/2 Teaspoon Nutmeg or Allspice
1   Cup Flour
Icing
1/2  Cup Margarine
1    Cup Brown Sugar
1/4  Cup Milk
3    Cups Powdered Sugar

Directions:
Combine the buttermilk, baking soda and baking powder.
Stir in the sugar, shortening, egg, cinnamon, allspice and flour.
Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
Icing
In a small saucepan melt the butter.
Stir in the brown sugar and cook till boiling.
Remove from heat and add the milk.
Add in the powdered sugar and beat until smooth.
Pour on top of the cake and spread evenly.
Let cool.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
EMET
Description: 
A really tasty spice cake which has been in family for generations.  It is very easy to make!
