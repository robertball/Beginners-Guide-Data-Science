Title:
Rhubarb Pie 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6C young unpeeled rhubarb cut up
4Tbsp tapioca
3C sugar
4Tbsp butter
1 pre Baked pie crust

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients and pour into pie crust. Dot with butter and top with another pie crust. Bake at 425 for 50-60min.
