Title:
Mini Puffed Oven Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 to 3/4 cup flour
3/4 cup milk
2 eggs
1/8 tsp salt

Directions:
Place mini muffin tin in oven and heat to 400.
Combine all ingredients in blender or food processer and combine well.
Remove tin from oven and liberally coat with spray or melted butter (I didn't and they stuck like crazy)
Fill muffin tins 3/4th full and bake 15 to 18 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Delightful little airy pancake puffs - absolutely adorable, pillowy, and utterly consumable.
