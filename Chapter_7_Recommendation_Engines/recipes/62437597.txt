Title:
Not Just Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 avocados, peeled and chopped
1/4 cup minced cilantro
1/2 onion, chopped
2 tomatoes, chopped
1/3 cup chopped red pepper
1/4 cup chopped cucumber
1 cup plain yogurt
1/3 cup sour cream
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup chopped green onions
1/2 cumin
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Put onion, yogurt, sourcream, and avocado in a food proccesor. Mix in remaining ingredients. Refrigerate and allow flavours to blend.

Submitter: 
Felicity
Description: 
This is lower fat than regular guacamole has lots more than just avocados. Makes a decent size batch.
