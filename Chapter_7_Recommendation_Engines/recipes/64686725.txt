Title:
Creamy Chicken Soup in Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken breast
1 medium onion, sliced
5-10 garlic cloves, minced
4 c chicken stock
1-3 tsp herbs de provence
4-5 sprigs rosemary
salt to taste
pepper to taste
2 8oz packages cream cheese
2-3 large handfuls of basil
2-3 c parmesan, shredded
2-4 T good quality olive oil
1/2 lemon, juiced
splash of balsamic vinegar (optional)

Directions:
put onion, garlic, chicken in crockpot;
season chicken with herbs de provence, rosemary, salt, pepper if desired
pour in chicken stock
add cream cheese broken into pieces
cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours
during last hour, shred chicken if desired
add parmesan cheese and basil, let melt
add olive oil and lemon juice to taste
season as needed
serve with basil and drizzle of olive oil
