Title:
Cara's Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 large avocados
1/2 chopped onion
1 finely chopped serrano pepper
1/2 bunch of fresh cilantro (finely chopped leaves only)
2-3 fresh chopped tomatoes
1/2 lime squeezed
course salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
add all ingredients together in mixing bowl, mash with masher

Submitter: 
Cara Moreno
Description: 
Quick and easy.  A total crowd pleaser!
