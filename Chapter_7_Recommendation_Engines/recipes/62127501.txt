Title:
Grandma Rose's Fresh Apple Cake by whodunitrdr Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups apples ; peeled and grated
1/2 cup oil
2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups unsifted flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons cinnamon
1 cup walnuts ; chopped

Directions:
Mix together apples, oil, sugar and vanilla.
Sift dry ingredients together and add to apples.
Fold in nuts. Pour into greased 9 x 13 inch pan and bake at 350ยบ for 1 hour or until done.
Cool in pan on rack.

Submitter: 
whodunitrdr
Description: 
A good cake to make when you are out of eggs. Very moist, it does not need frosting. Excellent served warm with vanilla ice cream. It also keeps for several days.
