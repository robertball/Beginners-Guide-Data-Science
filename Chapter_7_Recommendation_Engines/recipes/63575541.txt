Title:
Spicy Apricot Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6039973.jpg
Ingredients:
1 5 1/2 lb brisket
2 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 (10)cans shredded sauerkraut, drained
2 (10oz)jars apricot preserves
2(12oz)bottles chili sauce

Directions:
Place brisket fat side up in a roasting pan. Sprinkle with garlic salt and pepper.Stir together sauerkraut, preserves, and chili sauce. Pour over beef. Cover and bake at 350 for 3 1/2 hours. Remove brisket from pan and reserve sauce mixture in pan. Let stand for 1 hr.. Cut brisket across grain into thin slices. Place slices in a 13x9 baking dish. pour sauce over brisket. Chill and reheat in oven.
