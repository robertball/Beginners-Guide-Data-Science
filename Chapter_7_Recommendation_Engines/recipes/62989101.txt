Title:
Momma's Sweet Potatoe Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Sweet Potatoes
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 stick margarine
1 can sweeten condensed milk
2 Tbsp SR flour ( if all purpose add 1 Tbsp baking powder).
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 Tbsp Vanilla
2 eggs
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg or pumpkin spice
pecans to top if desired

Directions:
Boil sweet potatoes until soft and peel.
In a bowl add sugar,flour,spices. Beat eggs add with melted margarine and vanilla to dry ingredients. Add back to sweet potatoes mix until smooth with mixer.
bake for app 45 min until set makes two pies.

Submitter: 
Stephanie Foster Middleton
Description: 
An old southern recipe from Joyce Savage. originated somewhere in southern Mississippi.
