Title:
Yummy Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup salsa
1/2 cup sour cream (low-fat can be used)
1/2 cup mayonnaise (low-fat can be used)
1 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 Tbsp. jarred jalapenos, minced (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and chill. Serve with tortilla chips or fresh veggies.

Submitter: 
GINAGINA
Description: 
I searched high and low for a simple sour cream and salsa dip, but when I couldn't find one I came up with this yummy recipe. You can make it as spicy or as mild as you want. This is best prepared a day ahead.
