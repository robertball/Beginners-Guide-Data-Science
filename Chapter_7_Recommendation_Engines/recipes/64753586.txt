Title:
Mexican beef for fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 rouladan strips, cut in strips
rub
broth
1/4 cup orange juice
1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
2 tablespoons lime juice
1/2 orange, zested
1/2 lime, zested

Directions:
stir beef with rub and broth and place in ziploc and refrigerate overnight
make marinade and add beef for 2 hours
Q the beef on grill sheets
