Title:
chili pilaf with steak and chimichurri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2Tbsp butter
1/4C orzo pasta
1 3/4C chicken stock
1C rice
1/4C pimientos, drained and finely chopped
1Tbsp chili powder
1 1/2tsp paprika
1/4C sherry vinegar
1 large shallot, chopped
1 clove garlic grated or minced
1C packed parsley
7 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves stripped
6 sprigs fresh majoram, leaves stripped
Grated peel of one lemon
1/2C Evoo
salt and pepper
4 8oz stip steaks at room temperature

Directions:
Preheat a grill pan or large cast iron skillet over medium high heat. Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the orzo and cook, stirring, until toasted and golden-brown, about 3 minutes. Stir in the chicken stock, rice, pimientos, chili powder and paprika and bring to a boil. Lower the heat, cover and simmer for 18min. Remove from the heat and fluff with a fork.
While the pilaf is working, using a food processor, pulse the vinegar, shallot, garlic,parsley, thyme, marjoram and lemon peel until finely chopped, scraping down the food processor occadionally. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and sti in the evoo; season with salt and pepper.
Season the steaks with salt and pepper. Cook on the grill pan, turning once for  8 min for rare. let the meat rest for a few minutes, then serve with the pilaf and chimichurri.
