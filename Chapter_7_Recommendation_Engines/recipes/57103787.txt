Title:
Bananas Flamed with Oranges and Pistachio Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp unsalted butter
2 1/2 Tbsp sugar
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1/2 cup + 2 Tbsp fresh orange juice
1 Tbsp julienned orange rind
6 bananas, peeled and sliced in half lengthwise and once crosswise
1/3 cup orange liqueur
1/2 cup pistachio nuts
6 servings ice cream
1 cup whipped cream

Directions:
In a skillet, melt the butter, add the sugar and cook over medium heat until the sugar has browned.
Stir in lemon juice, orange juice and orange rind.
Return to heat and stir until sugar has dissolved.
Continue cooking until slightly thickened.
Add bananas and simmer 2 to 3 minutes, spooning the sauce over while it cooks.
Add liqueur and flame.
Sprinkle with pistachios.
Serve with ice cream and whipped cream on top.
