Title:
State Fair Corn Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. yellow cornmeal (not stoneground)
1 c. flour
3/4 tsp. salt
4 tsp. baking powder
1/4 c. sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 - 1 c. milk
1/4 c. oil
Oil for deep frying
10 non-dyed craft sticks (popsicle sticks)
10 hot dogs, any type

Directions:
Push ice cream sticks into each hot dog, set aside.
Mix all ingredients together, including the 1/4 c. oil. Mix until there are no lumps.
Start with 1/2 c. milk, and add as much as necessary until the batter is slightly thicker than pancake batter.
Drop into 360-370 degree oil and fry until golden.
Remove with tongs and drain on paper toweling or a cooling rack.
Enjoy!
