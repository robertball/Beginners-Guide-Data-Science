Title:
Egg Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups instant rice
2 cups water
3 Tablespoon butter
3 eggs
handful onion (or dried onion)
3 Tablespoons soy sauce
1/3 cup water

Directions:
Boil rice in water; let sit 5 minutes.  In another pan, melt butter.  Add eggs and onion.  Sautee onion for 3 minutes while eggs are scrambling.  Add water, rice, and soy sauce.  Cook for 5 minutes until thoroughly heated through.

Submitter: 
Barb Andrews
Description: 
This recipe comes from my mother.  It is a nice variation to ordinary rice, and makes also delicious side dish to any meal, served along with a vegetable or salad.  Enjoy!
