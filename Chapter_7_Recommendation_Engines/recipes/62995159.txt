Title:
Sesame Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb fresh green beans
1/2 C water
1 T butter or margarine
1 T soy sauce
2 tsp sesame seeds, ideally toasted

Directions:
In a saucepan, bring beans and water to a boil. Reduce heat to medium. Cover and cook for 10-15 minutes or until the beans are crisp-tender. drain.
Add butter, soy sauce and sesame seeds. Toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
Very fast to fix and a great way to use your fresh produce.
