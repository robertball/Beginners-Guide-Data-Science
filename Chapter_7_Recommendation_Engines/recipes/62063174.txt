Title:
Italian Crock Pot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
(whole or cut into bite-sized pieces)
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 cube BUTTER (not marg.)
1 env. Dry Italian dressing mix
1 (8oz) pkg cream cheese, room temp.

Directions:
Melt butter; pour over chicken in crock pot. Sprinkle with dry Italian dressing mix. Cover,and cook for 4 hours. Blend soup and cream cheese,pour over chicken.
*Cook additional 1/2 hour.
Serve over angel hair pasta.
