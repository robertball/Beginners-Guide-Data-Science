Title:
Couscous Salad Italia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5.7 oz. box Parmesan Couscous
1 15-oz can Garbanzo beans, drained well
1 small can sliced Black olives, drained well
1 red pepper, finely chopped
1/2 red onion, peeled and finely chopped
1 English cucumber, peeled, seeded and finely chopped
1 small tomato, chopped
1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves, finely chopped
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
1/4 cup olive oil
1 to 3 fresh limes, juiced

Directions:
Prepare Couscous as directed on package.  Let cool.  In large bowl, add garbanzos, black olive slices, chopped vegetagbles and feta cheese.  Add in cooked couscous.  Sprinkle olive oil and fresh lime juice all over.  Toss well to combine.

Submitter: 
Sherbg
Description: 
A wonderful, light salad, especially perfect to serve along with a grilled entree at your next BBQ or picnic. Looks very pretty in a glass bowl.
