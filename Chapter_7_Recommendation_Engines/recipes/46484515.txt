Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour *
1/4 teaspoon baking soda *
1/4 tsp sea salt *
1/2 cup butter, softened *
1/4 cup white sugar *
1/2 cup brown sugar *
1 egg *
2 teaspoons vanilla extract *
1 cup mini chocolate chips *
1 box yellow cake mix *
1 1/3 cups water *
1/3 cup canola oil *
3 eggs

Directions:
Directions Cream the butter, white & brown sugars. Mix in the egg and vanilla extract. Sift together the dry ingredients, then gradually add to the wet mix. Form the dough into balls of dough (tablespoon sized). Place balls in freezer. Preheat oven to 350. Combine cake mix, water, oil, and eggs. Blend, then mix on medium for 2 minutes. Spoon into prepared cupcake liners (2/3 full). Place a frozen cookie dough ball on the top center of each cupcake. Bake 18-22 minutes, checking for doneness. Frost w/the frosting of your choice!
