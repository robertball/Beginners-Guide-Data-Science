Title:
The Best Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz Small elbow macaroni
1 can Cream of Celery soup
1 cup Sour cream
2 cups Grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup Medium chopped onion
1/2 to 1 cup Crushed plain potato chips (such as Lays)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350
2. Cook macaroni to instructions on box
3. Drain completely
4. In a 13x9" oven-safe pan, add macaroni, cream of celery soup, sour cream, cheddar cheese, and onion. Mix thoroughly.
5. Place pan uncovered in oven, cook for 20 minutes.
6. Remove carefully, sprinkle crushed chips over entire pan, cook for another 5-10 minutes.
