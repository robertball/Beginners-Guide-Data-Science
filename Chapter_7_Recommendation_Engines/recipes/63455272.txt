Title:
Special Tuna & Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/939525.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 can of sweet peas, drained
2 cans of tuna, drained
3 boiled eggs, peeled and chopped
1/4 cup of mayonnaise
1 tbsp. sweet relish
seasoning of choice however you like

Directions:
In a medium size bowl, mix all the ingredients well.
It`s best to serve after it`s chilled some in the refrigerator, but it can be served immediately on toasted bread or bagel.

Submitter: 
Lovely Mommy
Description: 
A family favorite tuna salad recipe in which peas and eggs were added to make a little more healthier filling.
