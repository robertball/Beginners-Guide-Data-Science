Title:
Cranberry Pineapple Creme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans whole berry cranberry sauce
1 can jellied cranberry sauce
1 can crushed pineapple with juice
Cool Whip to taste

Directions:
Combine the cranberry sauces and the pineapple.
Fold in the desired amount of Cool Whip.
Spoon/pour into a freezer-safe dish.  Cover with plastic wrap and freeze until firm, but not hard.  Serve immediately upon removing from the freezer.

Submitter: 
Keri
Description: 
A deliciously tart yet sweet way to dress up your Thanksgiving table!
