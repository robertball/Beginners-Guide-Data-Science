Title:
Coke Cola Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pound Chuck Roast
2 Tablespoons Butter
1/4  Teaspoon Salt
1/4  Teaspoon Pepper
1 Package dried onion soup mix
2 10oz Cans Cream of Mushroom Soup
12oz Can Coke Cola
7oz Can Mushroom Pieces

Directions:
Melt the butter in a skillet and brown the roast adding both the salt and pepper.
Combine the dried union soup, mushroom soup and Coke Cola in medium bowl.
Pour mixture over the roast into a roasting pan.
Bake covered at 325 degrees for two hours.
Add the mushrooms and bake covered for an additional 30 minutes.
Let cool for 10 minutes and enjoy!

Submitter: 
EMET
Description: 
We have made this tasty roast for many years.  The recipe always gets many complements and is incredibly easy to make.
