Title:
Lela's Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/720250.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can diced tomatoes 14.5 oz.(drained)
½ jalapeno chopped
1 T. diced sweet onion
1 heaping tsp. of New Mexico Extra Hot Crushed Chili
Juice ½ lemon
½  small can tomato juice (5.5oz can)
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. onion powder
2 T. dried cilantro ( or fresh finely chopped)
½ tsp. garlic salt
½ tsp. salt

Directions:
Drain a can of diced tomatoes.
Chop ½ jalapeno and dice 1 tablespoon of onion.
Add tomatoes, chopped jalapeno, diced onion, juice of ½ lemon, ½ small can of tomato juice, 1 tsp. of onion powder, and 1 tsp. garlic powder to a blender.
Chop until onion and jalapeno are very small.
Pour into a glass bowl.
Add 2 T. dried cilantro, 1 heaping tsp. of New Mexico Chili,
½ tsp.  garlic salt, and ½ tsp. salt
Mix all ingredients together.
Chill in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Lela
Description: 
In our family, we love salsa on tacos, tostadas, eggs and especially with tortilla chips. My salsa has New Mexico extra hot crushed chili, and 1/2 small can of tomato juice. It is very addictive. Add Tortilla chips and enjoy.
