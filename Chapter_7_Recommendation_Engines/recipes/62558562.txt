Title:
Peaches and Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/534812.jpg
Ingredients:
2+ lb. Boneless Pork Roast
Olive Oil
Old Bay Seasoning
6 Garlic Cloves
6 Fresh Peaches Sliced
1 C. Bourbon
1 T. Whole Black Peppercorns
1 Lemon- cut and juices (with pulp)
1 C. Lite Brown Sugar
1 T. Worchestershire Sauce

Directions:
The night before or at least 4-6 hours before starting the charcoal on the grill, In a lg. ZipLock bag, marinate the sliced peaches in the Bourbon, peppercorns, lemon juice, brown sugar and Worchestershire Sauce.
Prepare the Pork Roast by rubbing with Olive Oil and sprinkling all over with Old Bay Seasoning. Place in sturdy metal pan, or heavy duty aluminum pan large and deep enough for roast and marinade.
Add Marinade over and around Pork roast, Cover with foil and place on grill. Close lid and let it smoke. Test doneness with meat thermometer to 170 degrees. Grilling time is around 1 hour or until juices run clear with poked with a cooking fork.
Serve sliced with peaches in the "syrup" drizzled over pork or as gravy for mashed potatoes or side dish of choice.
