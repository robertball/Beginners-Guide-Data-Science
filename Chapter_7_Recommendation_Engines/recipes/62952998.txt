Title:
Mom's Version Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans no-salt french cut green beans, drained
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  Campbell's® Condensed 98% Fat Free OR low sodium Cream of Mushroom Soup
2 small cans French's® French Fried Onions
1 cup slivered almonds, toasted OR 1 small can sliced Water Chestnuts, drained
1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce

Directions:
Toast almonds and set aside if using.  Stir soup, soy sauce, almonds and beans and 1.5 cans of the onions onions in a bowl.  Pour into 3-qt. casserole.
Bake at 350 degrees F. for 25 min. or until hot. Stir.
Top with remaining onions. Bake for 5 min. more.

Submitter: 
VH
Description: 
This traditional casserole made with french cut green beans, cream of mushroom soup, and French fried onions, slivered almonds and soy sauce.Modified from: Campbell's® Green Bean Casserole
