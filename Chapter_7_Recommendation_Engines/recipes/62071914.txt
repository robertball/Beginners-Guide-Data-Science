Title:
Asparagus Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can water chestnuts, drained
1 8 oz can sliced mushrooms, drained
4 eggs, boiled and chopped
1 small jar pimentos
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 cans asparagus spears, drained
1 small can french fried onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease or spray an 8x8 baking dish.
Place asparagus in baking dish.
Mix water chestnuts, mushrooms, pimentos, eggs, and soup; pour over top of asparagus.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 minutes.
Top with french fried onions and bake 10 minutes more.

Submitter: 
LTWARD
Description: 
Asparagus, water chestnuts, eggs, and pimentos combine to create a colorful dish.
