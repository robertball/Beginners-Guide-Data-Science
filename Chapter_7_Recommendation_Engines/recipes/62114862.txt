Title:
A Plus Peanut Butter Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter
1 c light corn syrup
2 c peanut butter
1 c sugar
1 c crisp rice cereal
2 c crushed corn flakes
1/2 c butter
4 T instant vanilla pudding mix
4 c powdered sugar
4 T milk
1 c chocolate chips
1/4 c butter

Directions:
Melt 1/2 c butter, corn syrup, peanut butter and sugar in a saucepan; mixing well. Add crisp rice cereal and crushed corn flakes. Press into 9x13" pan. Let it set in the refrigerator. Combine 1/2 c butter, instant vanilla pudding mix, powdered sugar and milk; mix until smooth. Spread over peanut butter layer.  Melt together the chocoalte chips and 1/4 c butter; spread on top of the vanilla layer. Cool in the refrigerator until set.

Submitter: 
wilco
Description: 
A friend made these last year for a presentation and everyone was asking for second helpings. She handed the recipe out to everyone. They're the best bars I've ever tasted and aren't hard to make at all!
