Title:
Cavatini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6857269.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups pasta (sea shells)
1# hamburger
1/2 cup green pepper
1 cup onion, diced
1/2 cup mushrooms
1/2 cup pepperoni
1# can tomatoes
small can pizza sauce
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. chili powder
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta, drain and set aside.
Brown hamburger until nearly done.
Add green pepper, onions, mushrooms and pepperoni and cook until done.
Add tomatoes,pizza sauce, salt and chili powder and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer until thickened.
Combine meat mixture and pasta into 2 qt. baking dish.
Top with cheese and bake at 350 degrees until cheese is melted. (about 20 minutes)
