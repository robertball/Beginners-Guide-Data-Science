Title:
Chicken and Mushroom in Wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 chicken breasts
1 cup white wine
6 oz. fresh sliced mushrooms or 1 can
2-3 garlic cloves, chopped
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tbsp. parsley, chopped
1/4 cup goat cheese

Directions:
Throw all but the goat cheese in a crock pot and cook for 4-5 hours.
Put the cheese in the last 30 minutes.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Chardonnay Queen
Description: 
This recipe is what happened when I had leftover wine.  This rarely happens.  The wine enhances the chicken flavor and the garlic gives it zing.
