Title:
Jim's Shrimp Amelia with spicy horseradish mustard dipping sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/390090.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds uncooked shrimp
6 cloves minced garlic
1/4 cup lemon juice
4 pearl onions diced
1/4 teaspoon artificial butter extract
1/4 teaspoon sea salt or to taste
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
Spicey horesradish mustard dipping sauce
2-tablespoons dijon mustard
1 tablespoon hot horseradish
1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Mix the garlic,lemon juice,onions and butter extract and sea salt in a small bowl. Pour over the raw shrimp in a baking dish. let it marinade for 30-60 minutes in the refrigerator. Bake in the oven at 375 for approx 12 minutes until the shrimp are cooked.
For the sauce mix the dijon mustard, hot horseradish and black pepper in a small bowl and refrigerate until the shrimp are ready to dip.
