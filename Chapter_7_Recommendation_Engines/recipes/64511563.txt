Title:
"Sierra" Chocolate Chip Cookies (& Cookie Pizza!) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2824791.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine or butter, softened (I usually use margarine)
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
2 eggs
2 1/3 cups flour
1 cup chocolate chips (or sub in PB, butterscotch, dark chocolate chips)

Directions:
Mix margarine and sugars till well blended.
Stir in eggs and vanilla.
Add dry ingredients and mix well.
Drop by tablespoonfuls onto cookie sheet.
Bake @ 350 x 13 minutes.
* Cookie Pizza *
Use 3 cups of cookie dough.
Spread in ungreased pizza pan.
Bake @ 350 x 13 - 14 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A family favorite shared by a close high school friend. Always my go-to c.c. cookie recipe. These are also great with other flavors of chips!
