Title:
Easy Roasted Garlic Parmesan Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds broccoli, cut into florets
3-4 Tbsp olive oil
Juice from half a lemon, about 1 Tbsp
Kosher salt
2-3 garlic cloves, minced
Freshly ground black pepper
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 425Â°F.
In a large bowl toss the broccoli florets and minced garlic with olive oil, salt and lemon juice until lightly coated.
Arrange the broccoli florets in a single layer on a greased baking sheet.
Roast for 16-20 minutes.
Sprinkle pepper and parmesan cheese on broccoli after removing from oven.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
J Alex Alexander
Description: 
Easy and delicious, this turns broccoli into an addictive treat!
