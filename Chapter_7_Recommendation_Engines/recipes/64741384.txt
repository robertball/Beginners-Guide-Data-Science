Title:
Autumn Olive Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup organic cane sugar
1/2 cup light or dark brown sugar
2 eggs
1 Tablespoon vanilla extract
4 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoons baking powder
2 1/2 cups unbleached flour
4 cups autumn olive berries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°F.
Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Cream sugars with the butter.
Add the egg, vanilla extract and milk then mix well.
In a separate bowl combine all dry ingredients.
Slowly add the wet mixture into the dry mixture and continue to stir until completely mixed.
Once all ingredients are blended fold in the autumn olive berries.
Drop by spoonfuls onto the baking sheet leaving about 1” between cookies.
Bake 20-25 minutes until thoroughly baked (test with a toothpick to ensure the centre is dry).

Description: 
Autumn olives, beautiful, tasty and invasive!  So pick all the berries you can.  Parks allowing picking of these berries without fine or penalty.
