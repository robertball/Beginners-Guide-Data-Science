Title:
Classic Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef sirloin tip or bottom round roast
2 medium potatoes, cut into chunks or new potatoes
2 medium carrots, cut into 2-inch chunks
1 medium onion, cut into wedges
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian stewed tomatoes, undrained
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1/2 cup beef broth
garlic cloves
1/2 cup wine
1-3 bay leaves
1 can of mushrooms
1 stalk of celery

Directions:
Insert 6 garlic cloves into the roast and sear on both sides. Deglaze the Dutch oven with Marsala wine and beef broth. Place meat in an ovenproof skillet or Dutch oven. Add the potatoes, carrots and onion celery and a can of mushrooms. Sprinkle with pepper. Top with tomatoes. Spread soup over meat. Pour beef broth around vegetables and add 3 bay leaves to broth mixture along with juices from tomatoes and mushroom soup. Cover and bake at 325 degrees F for 2 + hours or until meat and vegetables are tender. Thicken cooking liquid if desired.
