Title:
Pearline’s Baked Tomato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
2  -  #2 ½ can whole tomatoes
½ small onion – chopped
5 to 6 slices of white bread – crumbled
¼ cup sugar
¼ teaspoon red pepper
¾ stick butter
¼ teaspoon salt

Directions:
Pour juice off tomatoes.  Mix all ingredients thoroughly except ¼ stick of butter, and place into greased casserole.  Dot rest of butter on top and bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes until set.
Serves 6 to 8.  May be prepared 2 or 3 days in advance and frozen.
Easy, good and colorful.
