Title:
Anna's Banana Cocoa-nut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089607.jpg
Ingredients:
3 or 4 ripe bananas, smashed
1/3 cup melted butter
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg, beaten
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon baking soda
pinch salt
3/4 c all-purpose flour
3/4 c whole wheat flour
1/2 c flaked coconut
1/4 c unsweetened cocoa nibs
1/4 c flax seeds
1/2 c chopped nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F. With a wooden spoon, mix butter into the mashed bananas in a large mixing bowl. Mix in the sugar, egg, and vanilla. Sprinkle the baking soda and salt over the mixture and mix in. Add the flour, mix lightly.  Add coconut, cocoa nibs, flax, and nuts, mix lightly. Pour mixture into a muffin tins. Bake for approximately 20 minutes.
