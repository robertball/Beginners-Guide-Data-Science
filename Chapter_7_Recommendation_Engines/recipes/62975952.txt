Title:
Date Nut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 cup sugar
1 cup chopped dates
½ cup chopped nuts
1 ½ cups flour
1 ½ cups oatmeal
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup brown sugar
½ tsp salt

Directions:
Combine water, sugar, and dates in a saucepan stirring constantly over medium heat until thick and smooth.
Mix remaining ingredients for the crust. Great 8-inch square pan. Using ¾ of the mixture, press crust mixture into pan. Add cooled date mixture, spreading evenly over crust. Spread nuts evenly over dates. Top with remaining crust mixture.
Bake at 350° for 45 minutes. Cut into 2”x2” squares while warm but not hot.
