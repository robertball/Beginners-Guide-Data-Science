Title:
Caramel Sticky Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 frozen bread loaves
1 large box cook-n-serve vanilla pudding
1 cup brown sugar
1 1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 stick of butter
2 Tbsp milk

Directions:
Let bread loaves thaw until you can just cut through them. Cut each piece into eight sections.
Butter a 9 x 13 baking dish and sprinkle with nuts or raisins if you want. Place bread slices on top of nuts.
Stir together the pudding, brown sugar and cinnamon in small bowl. Melt butter with milk.  Add to the dry mixture and stir until smooth.
Drizzle over bread slices until coated. Place in your cold oven overnight and let rise. In the morning, prick bread with fork to let gas escape. Put back in oven and set at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
When done flip dish upside down on foil or 9 x 13 cake saver. Your will have to scrape baking dish.
