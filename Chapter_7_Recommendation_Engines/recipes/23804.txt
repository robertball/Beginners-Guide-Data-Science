Title:
Maple Syrup Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2804953.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup maple syrup
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
1 cup chopped dates
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together the cream cheese and maple syrup until blended.  Stir in the pineapple, dates and walnuts.  Fold in the whipped topping.  Chill at least 1 to 2 hours.  This salad can be frozen.

Submitter: 
JENNILYNN
Description: 
This is a wonderful blend of ingredients.  Makes a great luncheon salad.
