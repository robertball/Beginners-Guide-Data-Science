Title:
Tostadas & Avocado Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp sour cream
2 Tbsp Chicken broth
2 Tbsp Lemon or Lime juice
2 tsp oil
1/4 tsp cayenne
1/8 tsp salt
1 avocado
6-8 flour or corn tortillas
2 garlic cloves
2 jalapeno peppers
1/2 onion
Taco seasoning (1/2 packet)
2 cups cooked chicken or shrimp
lettuce
diced tomatoes
queso fresco

Directions:
Blend together sour cream through avocado in mini- chopper.
Cook together peppers, onions, taco seasoning, & chicken or shrimp.
Brown tortillas in skillet.
Layer: Tortilla, Chicken / Shrimp mixture, lettuce, tomatoes, avocado sauce , & queso fresco
