Title:
Easy, Small Batch Blueberry Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1397951.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Cups fresh blueberries
1 Cup sugar
1 Tbl. fresh lemon juice
1 Pinch cinnamon (optional)

Directions:
Mix the blueberries, sugar, and lemon juice in a large saucepan.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thick; approximately 30 minutes.  Makes a smidgeon over 1 cup.

Submitter: 
Deb C
Description: 
Simple and easy, the delicious flavor of the blueberries takes center stage.
