Title:
Vegan Pumpkin Pie Smoothie  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7205406.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pumpkin puree
1/2 cup almond milk
1/2 cup cold water
1 tablespoon pumpkin syrup (such as Torani®)
1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
2 packets stevia-erythritol sweetener (such as Truvia®), or to taste
5 cubes ice

Directions:
Combine pumpkin puree, almond milk, water, pumpkin syrup, pumpkin pie spice, and sweetener in a blender. Blend until well combined, 5 to 10 seconds. Add ice cubes and blend until smooth.

Description: 
A delicious pumpkin smoothie with all the flavors of fall. Vegan, fat-free, and low in calories!
