Title:
Easy Quiche II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic
2 cups milk
4 eggs
3/4 cup biscuit baking mix
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1 (10 ounce) package chopped frozen broccoli or spinach, thawed and drained
1-1/2 cups cubed cooked ham
8 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 10 inch quiche dish.
Sautee onion and garlic until onion is browned.
In a large bowl, beat together milk, eggs, baking mix, butter, parmesan cheese, black pepper and dry mustard. Batter will be lumpy. Stir in sauteed onion & garlic, broccoli/spinach, ham and Cheddar cheese. Pour into prepared quiche dish.
Bake in preheated oven for 50 minutes, until eggs are set and top is golden brown.
