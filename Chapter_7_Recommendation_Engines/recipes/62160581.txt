Title:
lemon white chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter softened
3/4 cup white sugar
1 egg
2 teaspoon lemon extract
1 1/4 cup all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup white chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
2. Cream together the butter and sugar.  Stir in the egg and lemon extract. In a seperate bowl mix flour,  baking soda, and salt.  Add flour mixture to the butter mixture.  Stir in the white chocolate chips.
3. Spoon cookie dough onto ungreased cookie sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes or until lightly browned.  Let cool on wire rack.
