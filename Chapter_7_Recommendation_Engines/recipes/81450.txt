Title:
Sweet Cottage Cheese and Bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/846602.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cottage cheese
2 teaspoons honey
1 banana, sliced

Directions:
In a small serving bowl, stir together the cottage cheese, honey, and banana slices. Serve or eat immediately.

Submitter: 
HUGGEY5
Description: 
Cottage cheese drizzled with honey and mixed with banana slices is a delicious and healthy treat that you can enjoy anytime.
