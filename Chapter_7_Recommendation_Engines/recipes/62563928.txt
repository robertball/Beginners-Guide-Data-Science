Title:
EASIEST Crockpot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bone-in cut up chicken
Seasoning salt and pepper to taste
Baby cut carrots
Potatoes, peeled and cut into wedges
1 C chicken broth

Directions:
Place carrots and potatoes in the bottom of the slow cooker with chicken broth.
Season the chicken with the seasoning salt and pepper and place in the slow cooker on top of the veggies.
Set the slow cooker to Low for about 8 to 10 hours, or until the chicken is no longer pink and the juices run clear.

Description: 
Chicken with on-hand ingredients, slow-cooked for a tasty EASY dinner!
The leftovers are wonderful to use in other chicken dishes as the flavor and tenderness (and did I mention easy-to-prepare?) can't be beat!!Modified from: Baked Slow Cooker Chicken
