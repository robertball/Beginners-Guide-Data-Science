Title:
The Morning After Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups diced cooked ham
3 baking potatoes; peeled and cubed
1 medium onion; chopped
1 green bell pepper; chopped
1 red bell pepper; chopped
8 ozs. fresh mushrooms; sliced
10 eggs
1/4 cup milk
3 tablespoons butter or margarine; divided
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup shredded swiss or gruyere cheese
salt and pepper to taste
paprika to taste (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees (F). Spray 9" x 13" baking dish with cooking spray, or lightly grease.
Melt one tablespoon butter/margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add potatoes and cook for approximately 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned. Layer on bottom of baking dish.
Melt remaining 2 tablespoons butter/margarine in skillet and add onions, mushrooms and both peppers. Cook, stirring occasionally, about 5 to 6 minutes, or until onion is translucent. Spoon vegetables over potatoes in baking dish.
Layer ham on top of vegetables.
In a large bowl, beat eggs and milk until a light froth appears on top. Slowly stir in cheeses until well mixed. Add salt and pepper to taste. Pour mixture over ingredients in baking dish. Sprinkle with paprika (optional). Place in oven and bake for 40 to 45 minutes or until eggs are set and top is beginning to brown. Serve warm.

Submitter:

Description: 
A delightful and easy way to use up the leftover ham from your Christmas or Easter dinners and to continue the rave reviews from last night's guests!
