Title:
Great Pumpkin Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
3 eggs
1 cup white sugar
1 t. cinnamon
4 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow or spice cake mix
1 cup butter, melted
1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
In a large bowl, combine pumpkin, milk, eggs, sugar and spices. Mix well, and pour into a 9x13 inch pan.
Sprinkle dry cake mix over the top, then drizzle with melted butterand 1 t. cinnamon. Top with walnuts or pecans.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
This is a very easy cake made with canned pumpkin and yellow cake mix. Serve with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.Modified from: Great Pumpkin Dessert
