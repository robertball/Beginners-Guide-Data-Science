Title:
Afternoon Pick-Me-Up Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz chopped peeled beet (1 cup)
4 oz chopped pineapple (1/2 cup)
1/2 oz seeded jalapeno pepper

Directions:
Juice in electric juicer. Serve immediately for best quality, or within 24 hours.
Stir pulp into ground turkey/pork for meaty beet burger.
