Title:
Flourless Chocolate Torte (Vegan & Gluten Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Tablespoons Coconut Oil
8 Ounces Bittersweet Chocolate
6 Tablespoons Chia Seeds
1 1/4 cups very warm water
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup agave
1/4  cup raw sugar
For Garnish:
1 Tablespoon  cocoa powder
1-2 teaspoons raw sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275 F
Use some coconut oil to grease a 9-inch springform cake pan
In a high speed blender add Chia Seeds and warm water.
Let sit for 10 minutes.
In double boiler melt chocolate and coconut oil.
Add agave.
Blend the Chia Seeds at a high speed in the blender.
Add the Chocolate mixture and sugar to the Chia Seeds in the blender.
Blend on low at first then med-high until fully blended.
Careful this mixture is quite thick and hard to blend.
Pour mixture into cake pan.
Spread out and flatten.
Bake for 55-60 minutes
Sift Cocoa Powder over the cake as a garnish.
Sprinkle raw sugar

Description: 
A Flourless Chocolate Torte that is vegan. We use agave and raw sugar to sweeten this cake and Chia seeds for the eggs. This torte is good for you and delicious! :-)
