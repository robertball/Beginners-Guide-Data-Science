Title:
Creamy Banana Blast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1009120.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ripe bananas
1 cup evaporated milk
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 cups crushed ice
1 pinch ground nutmeg
4 pinches ground cinnamon, for garnish

Directions:
Blend the bananas, evaporated milk, milk, sugar, ice, and nutmeg in a blender until smooth; pour into glasses and garnish each with a pinch of cinnamon to serve.

Submitter: 
ALI GARCIA
Description: 
This is a different touch in a simple banana smoothie, but the result is a blast!!! Try it, you will never think again that the banana smoothie is simple and boring.
