Title:
Nectarine Refresher Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4532873.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup orange juice
1/2 cup cold water
3 nectarines, diced, or more to taste
7 ounces frozen strawberries
1 banana, sliced

Directions:
Pour orange juice and water into a blender. Add nectarines, strawberries, and banana; blend until smooth.
