Title:
Baked Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.0 kg salmon
1 medium tomato
113 g can mushrooms
2 spring onions, cut up
1 lemon, sliced crosswise
1 large onion, finely chopped
butter, optional
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 190 C. Rinse salmon. Pat dry with paper towel.
Line baking pan with heavy duty foil large enough to fold and seal over mixture.
Place mixture on the pan. Place salmon over the mixture. Slice lemon and arrange over salmon.
If desired, place 2 tbsp butter, halved, in between lemon slices. Fold and seal foil. Bake 1 hour.

Submitter: 
lola
Description: 
A very easy recipe to do. Good tasting too. Can be served with rice or baked potatoes or eaten by itself. If desired, potatoes can be baked during baking time.
