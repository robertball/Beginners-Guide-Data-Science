Title:
Kringla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 suger
1 egg
1 cup sour cream
3/4 buttermilk
1 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. baking powder
3 1/2-4 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Cream sugar and egg. Add sour cream, buttermilk and vanilla. Mix dry ingredients together and add. Mix well. Batter will be sticky. Chill. Take walnut size pieces and roll into pencil shapes.Form into figure eight shapes. Bake at 375 degrees on a lightly greased cookie sheet until bottom is light brown.  When serving spread with butter.

A Norwegian soft pastry.
