Title:
Cake Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/547882.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 packaged cakes
1 (16 ounce) container prepared frosting
flavoring of choice
16 oz. chocolate chips
1 T shortening

Directions:
Crumble the cake into a large mixing bowl and stir in the frosting until well blended.
Melt chocolate and shortening in a glass bowl over a pan of simmering water, stirring occasionally until smooth.
Use a melon baller or small scoop to form balls of the chocolate cake mixture. Dip the balls in chocolate using a toothpick or fork to hold them. Place on waxed paper to set.
Can be sprinkled with crushed peanuts, sprinkles, coconut, or colored chocolate.

Submitter: 
SugarDuJour
Description: 
Mix and match cake and icing flavors as well as the chocolate coatings.  The variations are unlimited, and they can be nicely packaged and given as gifts.Modified from: Cake Balls
