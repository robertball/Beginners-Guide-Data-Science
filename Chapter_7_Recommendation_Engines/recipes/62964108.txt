Title:
Chocolate Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup melted butter
1/3 cup cocoa
1 cup icing sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 TBSP boiling water

Directions:
Combine ingredients and whisk until smooth. Pour and spread on cooled cake; glaze will harden quickly.

Submitter: 
Jenny Deveau
Description: 
Sweet chocolate icing that hardens when cooled. Perfect for cakes.
