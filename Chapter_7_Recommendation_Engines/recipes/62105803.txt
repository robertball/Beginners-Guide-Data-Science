Title:
Lori's Banana Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lori's Banana Pumpkin Muffins
Ingredients
2 medium ripe bananas
15 oz can pumpkin
2 eggs (slightly beaten)
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup safflower oil
1/3 cup buttermilk
Dry Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup oat flour (found in health area of grocery store)
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
dash of salt
Topping:
2-3 TBS melted butter
2 TBS granulated sugar
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Directions
Preheat Oven to 350 degrees, line 2 muffin pans with foil liners or spray a nonstick pan.
In a large bowl, mash bananas, add pumpkin, 2 eggs, vanilla, pumpkin pie spice, sugar, safflower oil, and buttermilk, mix by hand until well incorporated.
In a separate bowl mix dry ingedients: flour, oat flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix gently until blended.
Fill muffin cups with a medium scoop, bake 15-20 minutes until they spring back to the touch.
Mix cinnamon and sugar.
Melt butter, baste each muffin with butter while still warm and then sprinkle with cinnamon-sugar mix.
Makes 23 muffins.

Submitter: 
Lori
Description: 
My daughter asked me to make pumpkin muffins one day and I had ripe bananas and came up with these.  They are outstanding!  Much better than any pumpkin muffin I've ever had.  Moist, wonderful, terrific!
