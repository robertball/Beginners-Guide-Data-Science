Title:
Slow Cooker Cuban Ropa Vieja Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 1/2 pounds beef flank steak
1 cup beef broth
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 small onion, sliced
1 red bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
4 teaspoons minced garlic
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon white vinegar
1/4 cup brown sugar or Splenda Brown Sugar Blend
2 packets Goya Sazon

Directions:
Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Brown the flank steak on each side, about 4 minutes per side.
Transfer beef to a slow cooker.
In a large bowl, combine the beef broth and tomato sauce, then add the onion, bell pepper, garlic, tomato paste, cumin, cilantro, olive oil and vinegar.
Stir until well blended; pour over beef in slow cooker.
Cover, and cook on High for 4 hours, or on Low for up to 10 hours.
Remove 1-2 cups of liquid the last hour of cook time.
Blend the liquid with the brown sugar and Goya Sazon.
Shred the beef and return to the slow cooker along with the blended liquid for the last hour of cooking.
Serve with cooked white rice and black beans.
