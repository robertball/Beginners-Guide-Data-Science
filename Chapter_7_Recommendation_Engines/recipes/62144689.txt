Title:
Bollitas de Nieve Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups confectioners sugar
4 cups butter
1 tbsp. vanilla
1 tbsp. ground mace
1 tsp. salt
5 cups ground, toasted nuts (pecans, walnuts, almonds, hazelnuts or any combination)
8 cups flour
additional confectioners sugar, 1 tbsp cinnamon per cup, if desired

Directions:
Combine flour, nuts and salt; set aside
Cream butter and sugar, scraping bowl at least once
Add vanilla and mace and blend well, scraping bowl again
Add flour mixture, about two cups at a time, blending to incorporate completely
Using a 1 tbsp ice cream scoop, place balls of cookie dough on a silpat lined sheet (may place close together)
Bake @ 350 degrees for 12-14 minutes, or until bottoms are lightly browned
When cool enough to handle, roll in powdered sugar
Prior to serving or boxing, roll cookies in powdered sugar again
