Title:
Mae's Quick and Easy 30 minute Mediteranean Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Lean ground beef
1 Envelope onion soup mix
1 15 oz. Can cut green beans(drained)
1 15 oz. Can diced tomatoes w/peppers and onions(undrained)
1 Tbs. Dehydrated onion flakes
4 Beef boullion cubes(crumbled)
1 Quart water
2 tsp. Bouquet sauce(for color)
Salt and pepper to taste
3 1/2 cups instant rice
3 1/2 cups water
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup water
2/3 cup flour

Directions:
In 2 qt. Pyrex bowl combine rice and equal parts water, 1/2 tsp. salt.  Microwave on high for 10 minutes then cover and let stand for 15 minutes.
Brown ground beef in deep skillet til no longer pink, add vegetables, soup mix, boullion, onion flakes and 1 qt. water.
Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
While simmering put 1 cup water and 2/3 cup flour in a pint jar and shake vigorously to mix. Add additional Tbs. water if too thick.
Slowly add thickener stirring constantly to prevent lumps.
Add Bouquet sauce, simmer an additional 5 minutes.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Ladle over rice for a hearty dinner.

Submitter: 
Mae Long
Description: 
Simple ingredients, simple instructions and one pan, and in 30 minutes or less, voila *DINNER*.
