Title:
Holiday Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon apple cider
1 (64 fluid ounce) bottle cranberry juice cocktail
1 (750 milliliter) bottle amaretto liqueur
4 cinnamon sticks, or more to taste

Directions:
Stir apple cider, cranberry juice, amaretto, and cinnamon together in a large pot over medium-low heat to heat through and let flavors meld, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
MATTOON250
Description: 
Great cocktail for Christmas family gatherings. Refrigerate if not serving immediately and reheat as needed.
