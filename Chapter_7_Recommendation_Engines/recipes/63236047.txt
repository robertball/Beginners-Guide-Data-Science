Title:
Pasta with chicken, walnuts and apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/843220.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Granny Smith apple, cored, peeled and diced
1/2 cup walnuts, chopped
4 TB butter, divided
Chicken breast, skinless, boneless, cubed
1-1/2 cups chicken broth
3.3 oz. container goat cheese, crumbled
1 tsp. dried thyme
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/4 cup water
1 TB cornstarch

Directions:
In a large skillet, melt 2 TB butter
Add apple and saute for 5 minutes
Add walnuts and saute for another 5 minutes until apples are a little crispy.
Remove and set aside
In same skillet, add chicken and saute until brown and crispy.
Add apple/walnut mixture back in
Add chicken broth, thyme, black pepper and goat cheese
Cook over medium heat until cheese is melted, stirring constantly.
In a small measuring cup, add water with cornstarch and stir until no lumps
Add to skillet and cook over medium high heat until sauce is slightly thickened.

Description: 
A delicious pasta dish made with chicken and cooked in a rich, creamy goat cheese sauce along with apples and walnut and lightly seasoned with thyme.  Served over pasta of your choice, a green salad, a nice loaf of fresh bread and a glass of Chardonnay and you have a fantastic meal!
