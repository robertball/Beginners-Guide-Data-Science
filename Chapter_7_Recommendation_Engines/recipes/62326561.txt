Title:
Easy Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef or shredded chicken
1 tsp cimin
1 tsp chili powder
salt & Pepper to taste
2 cloves minced garlic
1/2 cup finley chopped onion
1 lg. can rotel with lime and cilantro
1 (16 ounce) can black beans, drained
3 cups tortilla chips, crushed
2 cups sour cream
1 (2 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
1/2 cup chopped fresh tomato
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook ground beef or chicken, onion & seasonings until no longer pink. Stir in rotel, reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed. Stir in beans, and heat through.
Spray a 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray. Spread crushed tortilla chips in dish, and then spoon meat  mixture over chips. Spread sour cream over meat, and sprinkle olives, green onion, and tomato over the sour cream. Top with Cheddar cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until hot and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
Ground beef, salsa and chili beans are layered with tortilla chips and sour cream and topped with melted cheese in this tasty dish.Modified from: Easy Mexican Casserole
