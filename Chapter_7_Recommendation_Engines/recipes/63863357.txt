Title:
Candied Fried Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter
16 ounces frozen corn, thawed
3 tablespoons honey
1/3 cup heavy cream
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
In large skillet over medium high heat, melt butter. Add corn, stirring to coat.  Cook for 2 minutes. Add honey and cook for another 3 minutes.  Increase heat and add heavy cream, continue to stir.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Continue to cook until most of the cream has been absorbed, about 5 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Barb0607
Description: 
Sweet corn is a great side dish for fried chicken or fish and chips.
