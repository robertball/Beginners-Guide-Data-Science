Title:
Lemon Sugar Cookies 1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups butter, softened
4 cups confectioners' sugar
4 eggs
1/3 cup lemon juice
1 tsp lemon extract
3 tablespoons milk
2 teaspoons grated lemon peel
6 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
sprinkled powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  In a mixing bowl, cream butter, lemon juice, and confectioners' sugar.  Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in lemon extract, milk, and lemon peel.
Combine flour and baking soda; gradually add to the creamed mixture.  With a spoon, drop cookies 1 in. apart on ungreased baking sheets.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned on bottom. Let sit for 5 minutes then remove to wire racks to cool.  When cool, sprinkle with powedered sugar.
