Title:
Crock Pot Chicken quesadilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3265499.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package 8-10 inch size flour tortillas
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 can red enchilada sauce (10-11 oz each)
1/4 cup water
I package reduced sodium taco seasoning
1 package nacho and taco shredded cheese
Optional:
Diced onions
Black olives
Sour cream (for dipping)
Salsa ( for dipping)

Directions:
Combine enchilada sauce, water, and taco seasoning package in crockpot.
Cut chicken breasts in half lengthwise and add to crockpot
Cook on low for 4-5 hours
Remove chicken and shred, placing back in crockpot afterwards
Spread shredded chicken on a flour tortilla along with shredded cheese in a skillet.
Place a tortilla shell on top and cook on med heat for 2-4 minutes, flipping halfway through

Submitter: 
Proud-Meinke
Description: 
Very simple and quick! These can be made for a quick weeknight dinner and picky eaters!
