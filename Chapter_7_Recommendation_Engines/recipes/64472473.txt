Title:
Bulletproof Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2481098.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. Grass Fed unsalted butter
1 Tbsp. MCT oil
1 Tbsp. raw cacoa powder
1/8 tsp. raw vanilla powder
pinch of salt
liquid Stevia to taste
11 oz. hot water
*optional: I add 1 Tbsp. Hydrolyzed Collagen Protein Powder and my vitamins/supplements into the blender also.

Directions:
Place all the ingredients into the blender and mix until well blended.
Add the Collagen Protein Powder after you blend and mix by hand to get the best results from the Collagen. (Great for the skin.)

Carol Castellucci Miller

This is an alternative to Bulletproof Coffee for people following the Bulletproof Diet, Ketogenic Diet, or any high fat low carb diet.
