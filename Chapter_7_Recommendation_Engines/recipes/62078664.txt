Title:
Apple Raspberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (15 oz) refrigerated pie curust (2 crusts)
1-1 1/4 cups sugar
1/3 cup cornstarch
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
5 medium baking apples such as Braeburn, peeled, cored and sliced (about 5 cups sliced)
1 cup frozed sweetened raspberries (not in syrup)
1 egg
1 tablespoon water
Vanilla ice cream (optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400F. Let pie crust stand at room temperature 15 minutes. Gently unfold crust onto lightly floured Baker's Mat. Roll one crust into 12-inch circle. Place crust in pie plate, pressing dough into bottom and up sides.
2. In large bowl, combine sugar, cornstarch and cinnamon; mix well. Toss apple slices with sugar mixture until evenly coated; gently stir in raspberries. Spoon apple mixture into bottom crust.
3. Roll remaining pie crust into 12-inch circle; place over filling. Fold top crust under bottom crust; seal and flute edges. Whisk egg and water in small bowl; brush evenly over crust. Cut four slits in top crust to allow steam to escape. Gently place pie crust shield (or tin foil if you do not have a pie crust sheild) over edge of pie. Bake 45 minutes' remove pie crust sheild (or tin foil). Bake for additional 10-15 minutes or until crust is golden brown and apples are tender. Remove from oven; cool at least 2 hours. Serve with ice cream, if desired.
