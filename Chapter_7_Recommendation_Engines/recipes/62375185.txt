Title:
Funeral Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. Butter
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can evap. Milk
1/2 grated cheese
1 lg. Pkg. Hash brown potatoes (32 oz.)
1 cup sour cream
minced onion
potato chips, crushed (or corn flakes)
Topping (if using corn flakes:
1 1/2 C. corn flakes, crushed
6 T butter, melted
6 T parmesean

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°
Mix all potato ingredients in lg. Bowl
Pour into greased cassarole
Top with chips or corn flakes (mixed)
Bake 30 min. at 350°

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Another husband favorite. I love these with ham. (You can include it in the recipe or serve along side.)
