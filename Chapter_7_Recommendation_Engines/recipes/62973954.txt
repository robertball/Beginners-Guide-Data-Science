Title:
Ramen Noodle Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package oriental flavor ramen noodles
1 1/2 cups hot water
8 ounces boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 2-inch strips
2 teaspoons cooking oil, divided
1 cup of sliced onion wedges
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 cups broccoli florets
1 cup fresh bean sprouts
1/2 cup sliced water chestnuts
1/2 cup water
oriental ramen seasoning pkt
1 teaspoon soy sauce
1 teaspoon oyster sauce
Sriracha sauce to taste, adjust the amt to your heat level
1 Roma tomato, cut into wedges

Directions:
In a bowl, place noodles in boiling water for 2 minutes; drain and set aside.In a large nonstick skillet, stir-fry chicken in 1 teaspoon oil until no longer pink. Remove and keep warm. Stir-fry the broccoli, onion and garlic in remaining oil over high heat until crisp-tender. Add the ramen seasoning pkt, water,soy sauce, oyster sauce, bean sprouts, water chestnuts and noodles; toss gently. Add tomato; heat through.

Stir-fry combines tender strips of chicken with vegetables and popular ramen noodles. This version has more veggies and flavor.Modified from: Ramen Noodle Stir-Fry
