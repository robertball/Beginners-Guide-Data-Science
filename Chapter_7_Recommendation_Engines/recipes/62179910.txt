Title:
Austrian Jam Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter, softened
1/2 c sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 egg, seperated
1 1/4 flour
2/3 c chopped almonds
Raspberry Jam (or any other jam)

Directions:
Beat butter, sugar, vanilla and egg yolk until fluffy.
Stir in flour, until forms a ball; chill for several hrs.
Roll 1 tsp dough into balls. Dip into slightly beaten egg white; roll in almonds.
Place on parchment lined baking sheets 1" apart. Prss an indentation with finger in each ball; fill with jam
Bake in a 300 degree overn for 20 mins or until lightly golden. Cool on wire racks. Store in airtight containers.
