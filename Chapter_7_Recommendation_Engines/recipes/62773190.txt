Title:
Brazillian Chicken Salad (Salpicao) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/643986.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken breast, (roasted in oven or cooked in pressure cooker helps keep it moist), remove skin and bones, shred and cool.
1 cup shredded carrot
1 cup cooked peas (fresh or frozen work best, not canned)
1/2 cup raisins
1 whole apple, peeled and diced
2 cups shoestring potatos (found in potato chip isle in grocery store)
1 cup mayonnaise
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Place cooled shredded chicken breast in large bowl.  Add shredded carrot, cooked peas, raisins and diced apples. Stir in mayonnaise and salt & pepper to taste. Gently fold in one cup of the shoestring potatos.  Press mixture firmly into 9x9 glass bowl. Cover and chill.  Right before serving, top with one cup of shoestring potatoes.  Serve.

Submitter: 
cynnergyrl
Description: 
Acquired from a friend, this is great make ahead meal to bring to a pot luck or brunch.  We love it on a hot day - a cool and crisp chicken salad eaten by itself or on toasted whole wheat pita.  Yum!
