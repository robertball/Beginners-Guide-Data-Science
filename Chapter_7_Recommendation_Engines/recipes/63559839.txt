Title:
Ham and Cheese Cresent Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can Pillsbury Crescent Dinner Rolls
8 thin slices ham
4 thin slices cheddar cheese, each cut into 4 strips

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Separate dough into 8 triangles.  Place one piece of ham on each triangle; place 2 strips of cheese down center of ham. Fold in edges of ham to match shape of dough triangle.
Roll up each crescent, ending at tip of triangle.  Place with tips down on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 15 to 19 minutes or until golden brown.  Immediately remove from cookie sheet.  Serve warm.
