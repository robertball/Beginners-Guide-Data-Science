Title:
Homemade low sodium Taco Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2	tablespoons chili powder (gebhardt)
1	teaspoon garlic powder
1	teaspoon onion powder
Scant ¼  teaspoon cayenne
2	teaspoons Mrs. Dash Southwest Chipotle Seasoning
2       teaspoons Mrs. Dash Fiesta Lime seasoning
1 ½	teaspoons dried oregano
1	teaspoon paprika
3	teaspoons ground cumin
1	 to 2 teaspoons sea salt
1 ½	teaspoons black pepper

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together all ingredients. Store in airtight container.    Add 2 teaspoons to 1 tablespoon seasoning mix and 2/3 cup water  to 1 pound ground cooked meat.  Cook stirring occasionally until water has cooked off.
