Title:
Ham and Cheese Tostadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983638.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tostada shells
8 slices OSCAR MAYER Smoked Ham
4 KRAFT Singles
1/2 cup shredded lettuce
1/2 cup chopped tomato
1/4 cup sliced pitted ripe olives

Directions:
Top shells with ham and Singles.  Place on microwavable plates.
Microwave each on HIGH 15 seconds or until Singles begin to melt.
Top with lettuce and tomato; sprinkle with olives.  Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro, if desired.
