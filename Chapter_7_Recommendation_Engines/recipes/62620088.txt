Ingredients:
1 Tbs. Old Bay Seasoning
1 tsp. salt
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup vinegar
1 lb. shrimp

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine the first four ingredients.  Bring to a boil.  Add shrimp, stir gently.  Cover; steam until tender.  Drain, remove shell and vein on back.
