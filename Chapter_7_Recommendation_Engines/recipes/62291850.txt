Title:
Dad's Beef Bourguignon (fancy name for beef stew) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 tbls salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 lbs stew meat, or cut up chuck roast
4 tbls ea butter & Olive Oil
2 onions, chopped
bunch of carrots, chopped (how ever many you like)
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 bottle of red wine (cabernet works)
1/2 carton chicken stock
3 bay leaves
3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1 teaspoon dried thyme
2 pkgs sliced mushrooms
1 lb pkg of frozen peas with pearl onions

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt and ground black pepper. Coat the beef cubes with this mixture.
Melt the butter and olive oil in a dutch oven over medium high heat. Add the meat and brown well on all sides. Remove meat and set aside.
Add the carrots to the pot and saute for approx five minutes. Add the onion and garlic and saute another five minutes.
Add the bay leaf, parsley, and thyme. Stir for a couple of minutes then add the wine and chicken stock. Put the meat back into the pot.
Bake covered at 350 for 2 1/2 hours. Add the mushrooms, and the pkg of peas and pearl onions, and bake for 30 more minutes (still covered).

Submitter: 
Spock
Description: 
Stew meat is browned then braised in red wine with carrots, garlic, and onions. Mushrooms, peas and pearl onions are added for the last half hour of cooking. (I used a four pound beef chuck roast, and cut it into large chunks).Modified from: Beef Bourguignon II
