Title:
Peanut Butter Oatmeal Chocolate Chunk Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C flour
1 C old-fashioned or quick-cooking oats
1/2 t baking soda
1/2 t baking powder
1/4 t salt
1/2 C (1 stick) butter,softened
1/2 C sugar
1/2 C firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 C peanut butter
1 egg
1 1/2 t. vanilla
6 squares semisweet baking choc, coarsely chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Mix flour, oats, baking oda, baking powder,and salt. Set aside. Beat butter, sugars and peanut butter in lg. bowl with electic mixer on med. speed until light and fluffy. Add egg and vanilla; mix well. Gradually add flour mixture, mixing until well blended after each addition. Stir in chocolate.
Drop heaping tablespoonfuls of dough, 2 inches apart, onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake 10-12 min. or until lightly browned. Cool 1 min; remove from baking sheets to wire racks. Cool completely

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Can sustitute 1 C semisweet choc. chunks for the chopped choc. squares
