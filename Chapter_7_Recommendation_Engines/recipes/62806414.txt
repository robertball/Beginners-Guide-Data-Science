Title:
Tex-Mex Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can tuna in water
1 tbsp mayo (low fat will work)
2 tbsp verde salsa
1/2 cup corn (slice the kernels off a leftover corn on the cob, or frozen will work
1 jalapeno, diced (optional)
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp chili powder
salt and pepper to taste
juice of 1 lime
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Drain the tuna well and empty into a bowl. Add the next six ingredients and mix well. Taste for seasoning and add salt and pepper if needed. Refrigerate at least an hour. Add lime juice and stir right before serving. Use tortilla chips to scoop up the tuna and eat it like a dip

Submitter: 
MomZilla (Evin)
Description: 
A different and spicy way to enjoy high-protein tuna!
