Title:
Dianne's Roasted Potatoes and Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666125.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds red potatoes, cut lengthwise into wedges
2 cups carrots, peeled and cut into 1" pieces
2 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 tsp. kosher salt, or to taste
1/2 tsp. fresh ground black pepper, or to taste
1 tsp. dried rosemary, crushed
1/2 tsp. dried thyme

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Lightly spray a cookie sheet with cooking spray.
In a large bowl toss togeter the potatoes, carrots, olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, rosemary and thyme.
Spread mixture evenly on cookie sheet. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, stirring once or twice, until potatoes and carrots are tender and lightly browned.
