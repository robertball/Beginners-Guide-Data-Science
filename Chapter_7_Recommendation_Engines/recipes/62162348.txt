Title:
Mini Cheddar Meatloaf Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/336295.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
3/4 c. milk
1 c. shredded cheese
1/2 c. quick-cooking oats
1/2 c. chopped onion
1 tsp. salt
1 lb. lean ground beef or chicken
2/3 c. ketchup
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp. prepared mustard

Directions:
In a medium-sized bowl beat the egg and milk.
Stir in cheese, oats, onion and salt.
Add the beef/chicken and mix well.
Shape into palm-sized balls and lay in a greased 7x11 or 9x13 square baking dish.
Combine the ketchup, brown sugar and mustard and spoon over the loaves.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until meat is no longer pink.

Submitter: 
Melaknee
Description: 
Cheesy meatloaf in a whole new fashion! My favorite form ever. Perfect with mashed potatoes and green beans.
