Title:
British Hot Cross Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
2 1/2 tsp. dry active yeast
1/2 cups unbleached all purpose flour
1 2/3 cups unbleached all purpose flour
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cinammon
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground ginger
4 Tbsps unsalted butter (soft, sut in pieces)
1 lg. egg
2/3 cup currants
1/4 cup finely diced citron peel
2 TBSPs sugar
1 TBSP water
2/3 cup confectioner's sugar
2 tsps water

Directions:
for the sponge, heat milk to 110
pour milk into bowl and whisk in yeast.
stir in 1/2 cup flour, cover with plastic wrap.
let it ferment until bubbly (15 min. approx.)
When the sponge is ready, prepare dough as follows:
combine flour, sugar, salt and spices in bowl of mixer.
Place paddle on mixer.
Add butter, mix until it is finely worked in (2 mins approx)
Remove bowl from mixer, scrape in the sponge.
Add egg and return to mixer.
Mix on low speed (2 min)
Stop the mixer and allow to rest for 10 minutes.
Mix the dough on med. speed until smooth/elastic (2 min)
Reduce speed to lowest and add currants and peel.
Mix until evenly disributed.
Scrape the dough into a buttered bowl.
Turn the dough over so the top is buttered.
Cover with plastic wrap.
Allow to rise until doubled in size (1 hour approx)
Turn the risen dough out into a lightly floured work surface
Press it into a rough square
divide dough into 12 equal pieces
round each piece with your hands.
Arrange on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Press a cross into dough with back of knife.
Cover the buns with a damp tea towel.
Let rise until doubled (45 minutes)
Set oven for 375 , rack in middle of oven.
Bake buns until deep golden (15 to 20 minutes)
While buns are baking, bring sugar and water to boil.(glaze)
Transfer buns when they are done with the parchment paper onto rack.
Brush on glaze immediately.
For the icing combine confectioners sugar and water in a small saucepan and stir well to mix.
Place over heat and stir until icing is just warm to touch.
Scrape the icing into a paper cone/baggie, snip off end.
Pipe in a cross over COOLED buns.
Let icing dry for 30 minutes.
For advance preperation bake buns.  But do not apply glaze or icing. Freeze. When ready to serve, defrost, place on cookie sheet and reheat at 350 for 5 minutes. Put on glaze and icing .
