Title:
Scallop Corn w/Oysters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cream style corn
1 bag frozen shoepeg corn
2 eggs
1 c. crushed saltines
1 c. oysers
1/2 c. butter
1 small jar pimentos
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 medium sized onion
1/4-1/2 c. milk

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.
Place in prepared 9x13 pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for at least 1 hour, or until crispy.
