Title:
Vanderwolf's Family Secret Chocolate Cake Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/556839.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup baking cocoa
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking soda
pinch of salt
2 cups sugar
1 cup sour milk or cream
2 eggs
2 tsps Vanilla
1/2 cup oil
1 cup boiling water (do not use hot tap water, use boiling water)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Mix together first 5 ingredients. Make a hollow in centre of bowl and add remaining 5 ingredients into centre.
Mix all together until blended. Pour into 2 8" pans or 1 9 X 13 pan. Bake 45 mins or until toothpick inserted in centre come out clean.
