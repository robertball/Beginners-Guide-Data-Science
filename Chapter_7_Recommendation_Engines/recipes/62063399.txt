Title:
Squash Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium butternut squash
1 16oz package of goat cheese
1 head of garlic
Juice one small lemon
Walnut topping (optional)
Chips or french bread to dipping

Directions:
Cut squash in half and clean out the center.  Drizzle lightlly with olive oil and bake flesh-side down.  Bake in oven for approx. 30-40 minutes at 350 until tender.
Cut top of garlic head off and drizzle with a little olive oil.  Sprinkle with salt & pepper to taste and wrap in foil to bake along with the squash.
When the squash is cooked, scoop flesh out into bowl and add the roasted garlic (minus the skin).
Add the goat cheese, the lemon juice and mix until well blended.  Add walnut topping.
Chips or sliced french bread to dip. Enjoy!
