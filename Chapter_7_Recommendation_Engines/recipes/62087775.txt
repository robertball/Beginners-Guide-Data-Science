Title:
Layer Cake I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup shortning
1 3/4 cup sugar
3 eggs
1 1/2 cup milk
2 3/4 cups flour
2 1/2 tbsp. baking powder
1 1/2 tbsp. vanilla
16 oz. container of icing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Grease and flour two 8 inch cake pans.
Mix shortning and sugar in a large mixing bowl.
Once mixed, add eggs.
Then, add the other ingredients. Mix until smooth.
Pour into pans and put into preheated oven and bake for 30-40 minutes.
Let the cakes cool completely.Then,assemble one cake at a time, coating the top of each layer with icing.

Submitter: 
Chrisa
Description: 
This is a yummy cake that can be layered with any flavor icing you prefer- Or used for a cupcake batter. (If used for cupcakes, bake at 400 degrees for 20 min.) It is easy, and can be made with only one bowl!
