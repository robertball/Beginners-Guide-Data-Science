Title:
Carrot Zucchini Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1031298.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large zucchini
4 large carrots
2 eggs
3 Tbl mayonnaise
1 Tbl onion powder
1 Tbl garlic salt
1 Tbl red pepper flakes
1 cup garganzo bean flour

Directions:
mix ingredients together in bowl
cook by tablespoon full dollops in frying pan on medium heat with oil, like pancakes, until both sides are cooked.
