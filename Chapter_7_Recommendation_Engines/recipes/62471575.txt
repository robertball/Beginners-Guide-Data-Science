Title:
Sausage and Green Chili Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
2 cups milk
Pinch dry mustard
1 clove minced garlic
Salt and fresh ground pepper to taste
6 slices stale white bread
Butter
2 cups grated cheese
1 lb. cooked sausage
1 can chopped green chilies

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Blend together eggs, milk, dry mustard, garlic, salt and pepper.
Cube bread; brush with butter and place in a large souffle dish.
Top bread cubes with cheese, sausage and green chilies, then cover with egg custard.
Bake uncovered for 1 hour or until done.
