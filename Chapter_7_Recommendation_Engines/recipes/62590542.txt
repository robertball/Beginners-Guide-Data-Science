Title:
Chicken Nacho Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1/2c salsa
1/4c sour cream
1/2c crushed tortilla chips
1/2c Mexican cheese

Directions:
Slice chicken with a sharp knife and place on baking sheet.
Top with salsa, sour cream, and crushed chips.
Bake 20 minutes at 400.
Top with cheese and bake 10 minutes
