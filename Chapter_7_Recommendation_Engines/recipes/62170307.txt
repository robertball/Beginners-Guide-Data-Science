Title:
Cincinnati Chili with twist Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of hamburger meat or ground turkey
1 large onion
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
20 oz tomatoes, crushed
16 oz beef stock
3 cans kidney beans in chili sauce (already flavored)
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon honey
1/2 oz of dark chocolate (optional)
1 box Spaghetti noodles
2 oz grated sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown meat with onion and garlic; drain fat.
Stir in chili powder, paprika, pepper, cumin, cinnamon and nutmeg.
Add tomatoes, beef stock, chili beans, vinegar, honey and chocolate.
Reduce heat to med-low.  Simmer 30 mins, stirring occasionally until flavors are blended.
In large pot boil water for noodles.
Cook noodles as directed.
Serve Chili on noodles with cheddar cheese as topping.

Submitter: 
MeSprague
Description: 
This is a variation of the classic Cincinnati Five way Chili.  Some Kidney beans and extra spice make this chili a spicy twist to Cincinnati Chili.
