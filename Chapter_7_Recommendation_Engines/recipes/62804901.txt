Title:
Twice Baked Potatoes a-la-microwave Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes, cleaned and dried
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup cheddar (or more to taste)
1 tablespoon onion grated or finely chopped
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
3/4 cup milk
Paprika

Directions:
Prick and bake potatoes in microwave until done. (may also bake at 350 for 1 hour).  While hot slice lengthwise.  Scoop out insides saving shells.  Whip together potatoes, butter, cheese, onion, salt and pepper.  Gradually add milk.  Spoon potato mixture back into shells and sprinkle with Paprika.  Return to the microwave for 5 minutes.  (or return to 350 oven for 20 minutes.)

Description: 
Quick and easy - may be done in oven or if you're in a hurry great in the microwave.
