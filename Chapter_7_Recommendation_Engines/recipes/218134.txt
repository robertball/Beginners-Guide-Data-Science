Title:
Dana's Cream Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/763587.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup toasted pine nuts
1 cup fresh basil leaves
2 cloves garlic
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Place the pine nuts, basil, and garlic in a food processor. Pulse until coarsely chopped. Mix the cream cheese and balsamic vinegar together in a bowl until well blended, then stir in the pine nut mixture.

Description: 
Easy spread you can make ahead of time -- great flavor!
