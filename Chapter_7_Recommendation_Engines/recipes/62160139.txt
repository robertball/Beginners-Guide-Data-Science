Title:
Rich and Creamy Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 c. finely crushed graham crackers
4 T. butter, melted
1 T sugar
Filling:
4 8oz packges cream cheese..at room temperature
1 3/4 c sugar
5 eggs
24oz sour cream
1 t vanilla
a squirt of fresh lemon juice (optional)

Directions:
For the Crust:
Combine the graham crackers, butter and sugar. Press into the bottom of a 10" springform pan. Set aside.
Preheat the oven to 375
Filling:
In a very large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese just to start blending it. Add the sugar, eggs, and sour cream and beat until creamy, scraping down the sides. Add the vanilla and lemon juice (if using) by hand. Pour into the graham crust, filling no more than 3/4 of the way up the pan. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes..Uncover and continue baking another 5-10 minutes or until the outside turns just slightly darker. Turn the oven off and do not open for 1 hour. Remove and let cool. Cover with foil and refrigerate for 24 hours.

Submitter: 
Janpie
Description: 
A graham cracker crust holds a sinful combination of cream cheese and sour cream that will have you sneaking into the fridge for another bite. The longer it sits, the better it tastes!
