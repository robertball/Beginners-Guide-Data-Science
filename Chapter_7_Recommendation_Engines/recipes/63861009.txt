Title:
Pancakes like Perkins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3707470.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c milk
1 tbsp vinegar
1 tbsp oil
1 egg
1 1/4 c flour
1/4 c sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Combine milk and vinegar and let sit for a few minutes.
Combine all dry ingredients in another bowl.
Add oil and egg to milk mixture and stir.
Combine dry and wet mixture and stir slightly.
Pour amounts of  @ 1/4 c on pre-heated grill. Flip as air bubbles pop.
