Title:
Kellogg's速 Almond Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup light corn syrup
1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon almond flavoring
4 cups All-Bran速 Original

Directions:
In 3-quart saucepan, combine corn syrup and sugar. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until sugar is dissolved and mixture bubbles. Remove from heat. Stir in almond flavoring. Add Kellogg's(R) All-Bran(R) cereal. Stir until well  coated.
With back of spoon, press mixture evenly and firmly into 13x9x2-inch pan  coated with cooking spray. Let stand until cool. Cut into 3x1-1/2-inch bars.

Description: 
These almond-flavored cereal bars are a great after-school snack.
