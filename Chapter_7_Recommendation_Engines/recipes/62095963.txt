Title:
Ham and Pierogies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Boxes frozen potato Pierogies
2 large ham steaks, diced
3 Tbs butter or margarine
1 sm can sliced mushrooms
1 clove garlic, crushed
green onions, diced
1 can mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup milk
salt, pepper, garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350; line four-sided cookie sheet with foil, grease lightly with cooking spray. Place pierogies in single layer, spray again with cooking spray. Bake 18 - 20 minutes until lightly golden, turning over half way through.
Meanwhile, in large fry pan, melt 3 Tbs butter, add ham steak, mushrooms, green onion and garlic.  Saute until lightly browned. Add baked pierogies to ham and veggie mixture. Saute to blend flavors.
Mix mushroom soup, sour cream and milk until blended.  Add salt, pepper, addl garlic powder if desired. Pour over ham mixture, heat through and serve.
