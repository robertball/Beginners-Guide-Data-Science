Title:
Old McDonald's Oyster Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060004.jpg
Ingredients:
3 loaves dried bread
3 medium onions
3 stalks of celery
2 cans oysters (or 2 cups small, fresh oysters)
9 eggs
1 cube (1/2 cup) melted butter (real butter, not margarine)
3 teaspoons poultry seasoning
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Put bread in large pan, and add just enough water to crumble bread.
Chop onions and celery finely, add to bread mix.
Add a little salt and pepper.
Add oysters with juice, and eggs.
Use beater and add poultry seasoning, mix well.
Add melted butter; mix well.

Submitter: 
markfernandez
Description: 
Nothing says Thanksgiving like the smell of a turkey in the oven, especially if that turkey is stuffed with grandmother's Oyster Stuffing.  If I can make it half as good as she did, then Thanksgiving Dinner would be twice as good as it otherwise would.
