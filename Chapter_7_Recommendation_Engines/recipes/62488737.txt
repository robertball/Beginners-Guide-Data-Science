Title:
Lasagna Spirals Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. olive oil
10 lasagna noodles
1 pound ricotta cheese
1 10-ounce pkg. chopped frozen spinach, thawed (squeeze water out)
1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
6 basil leaves, chopped
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 tsp. each salt & pepper
4 cups tomato sauce
1 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
1) Preheat over to 350 degrees. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Add olive oil. Cook noodles 7-8 minutes. Drain.
2) Mix ricotta, spinach, mozzarella, basil, egg, garlic, salt and pepper in a bowl.
3) Spread 1 cup tomato sauce in the bottom of baking dish. Spread each noodle with a layer of ricotta mix, then roll up and place seam down in the dish. Top spirals with 1 1/2 cups tomato sauce and 1/2 cup parmesan cheese. Bake 20 minutes. Serve with extra sauce and cheese on the side.
