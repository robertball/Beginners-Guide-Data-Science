Title:
Gina's' Amazing No-cake Strawberry Short-cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 quart strawberries washed, sliced
2. teas cinnamon
3. 2 talb sugar ( may use sugar substitute)
4. 1 tea ginger
5. tea vanilla
6. 2 cups strawberry awake cereal ( may use any crunchy wheat flake cereal)-
7. 1/2 cup whole pretzels ( about 17 small ones)
8. 2 tabl butter ( may use I can't believe its not butter...need the butter flavor)
9. 8 oz light ( or fat free) cool whip
4-6 parfait or small truffle glasses ( Walmart sells them 1.97 each)

Directions:
remove stems wash and slice strawberries..place in small bowl and add 2. 3. 4. 5. ..stir and refrigerate for at least 2 hrs (can do night before)
in large baggie place 6. and 7.
melt 8. and pour over pretzel cereal mix...toss in the baggie till all covered evenly
assemble parfait
( save a sliced strawberry to garnish the top)
3 tabl pretzel mix on bottom
a layer of strawberries with small amount liquid from strawberries
2 tabl cool whip
2 tabl pretzel mix
layer of strawberries
top with cool whip
place strawberry slice on top...I had a heart shaped cookie for the top...could use a piece of chocolate as well
serve immediately  or chill till serve

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
point friendly- lo cal dessert
fresh strawberries, cool whip and cereal/pretzel crumb crust
delights the pallet..sweet salty is a surprise...my quest cleaned their parfait glass
