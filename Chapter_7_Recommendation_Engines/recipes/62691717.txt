Title:
Rice with Butter and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of rice
1/2 cup Parlsey chopped
3 tbs Butter
3 tbs Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Take one-half cup of rice.
Boil in salted water.
After twenty minutes of boiling take off the fire and drain.
Then put the rice back into a saucepan with three tablespoons of grated cheese (Parmesan) and three tablespoons of butter.
Add the chopped parsley
Mix well and serve as an entree, or around a plate of meat.

Submitter: 
Mare
Description: 
I love just plain white rice with only a few ingredients. My aunt used to make this for us all the time. Love it!
