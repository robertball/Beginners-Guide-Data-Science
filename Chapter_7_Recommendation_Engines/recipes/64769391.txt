Title:
Mom's Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 bone-in chicken breasts
2 cups of broth from chicken above
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 bag Cornbread stuffing
Stick of butter

Directions:
Boil chicken in enough water to cover until done.  Allow chicken to cool, then shred.  Mix 2 cups of the broth from the chicken with the cans of soup in a bowl and set aside.  Melt butter in a bowl and mix in cornbread stuffing crumbs.  Place the buttered crumbs in the bottom of a 9 x 13 pan saving some to sprinkle on top.  Place shredded chicken on crumb mixture, pour soup mixture over chicken and top with remaining crumbs. Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes

Kisha McDaniel Logsdon
Description: 
Stuffing style chicken casserole that Mom use to make.
