Title:
NOGTINI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4oz. eggnog
2oz. Kahlua (may substitute Amaretto or Southern Comfort)
2oz. vodka
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
ice

Directions:
Swirl a martini glass with chocolate syrup. Shake all ingredients in martini shaker and strain into martini glass. Garnish with dollop of whipped cream and dash of nutmeg.

Submitter:

Description: 
New traditional holiday drink. An eggnog martini.
