Title:
Sweet Treat Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (8 oz.) softened cream cheese
1T. grated onion
1t. minced garlic
1/4 cup cubed butter
1/4 cup packed dark brown sugar
1 t. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 t. prepared mustard
1 cup finely chopped, toasted pecans
Firm crackers

Directions:
Toast the pecans.
In a small bowl, mix cream cheese, onion and garlic. Shape into a round, square or whatever shape you choose (approximately 1" high) and set aside.
In a small saucepan, mix the butter, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce and mustard. Cook and stir over medium heat for 4-5 minutes or until sugar is disolved. Remove from heat and stir in pecans.
Cool lsightly, spoon over cheese mixture. Serve with firm crackers.
