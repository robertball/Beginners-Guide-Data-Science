Title:
Berry Fiesta Milk Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983705.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup nonfat milk
1 cup mixed berries, fresh or frozen (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
2 teaspoons sugar
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
4 ice cubes

Directions:
Place milk, berries, sugar and cinnamon in blender and puree for 1 minute.
Add ice cubes and process until smooth and frothy. Pour into tall glass and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
California Milk Advisory Board
Description: 
Juicy ripe berries are blended with milk and cinnamon for this refreshing and frothy milk drink. Enjoy as a snack, dessert, or drink with dinner.
