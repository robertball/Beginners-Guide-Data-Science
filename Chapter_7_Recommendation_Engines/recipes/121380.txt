Title:
White Sauce for Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/581891.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons stick margarine
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons butter-flavored granules*
3 teaspoons reduced-sodium chicken bouillon granules
1 1/2 cups boiling water
1 cup fat-free milk
pepper to taste

Directions:
In a saucepan, melt margarine. Stir in flour, butter-flavored granules and bouillon until blended. Gradually add water and milk.  Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.  Season with pepper.

Submitter: 
Ruth  Marie Lyons
Description: 
'My husband, Jimmy, and I created the recipe for this creamy, versatile sauce,' says field editor Ruth Marie Lyons of Boulder, Colorado. 'I like to serve it over pasta and cooked vegetables...or draped on turkey, chicken, shrimp or tuna.'
