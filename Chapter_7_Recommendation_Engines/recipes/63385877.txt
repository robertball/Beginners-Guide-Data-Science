Title:
Grandma Stuck's Kosher Dills Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups water
1 cup vinegar, distilled
2 or 3 cloves of garlic
2 or 3 stalks of dill
1/4 cup salt

Directions:
Pack cucumbers, dill and garlic into hot jars leaving 1/4 inch headspace ladle hot liquid over pickles. Remove air bubbles. Adjust two piece caps.
Process in boiling water for 15 minutes.
Let sit for at least one month.

Description: 
A delicious recipe for making homemade kosher dills.
