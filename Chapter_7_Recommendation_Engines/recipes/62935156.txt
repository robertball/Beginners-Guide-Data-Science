Title:
Pork and Herb Terrine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/719123.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb sliced bacon
1 lb ground lean pork
1 cup minced onion
2 cloves of garlic, minced or pressed
1 package 10oz of spinach, cooked and drained.
1 tablespoon of of dried tarragon
1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
1 teaspoon of dried Oregano
1 teaspoon of dried thyme
1 teaspoon of dried marjoram
2 teaspoons of salt
1/2 teaspoons of black pepper
1/2 cup of dry sherry, Optional
2 tablespoons (2 packages) Unflavored gelatin
1 cup of whipping cream
4 eggs, slightly beaten
1/2 lb of boiled ham, diced
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Line a 9x5 inch loaf pan with bacon 4 to 6 slices, overlapping slightly to cover pan. In A skillet fry remaining bacon (4 to 6 slices) until crisp. remove and reserve. pour out all but 2 tablespoons of fat.
put pork into a large mixing bowl, set aside.
add onion, and garlic to bacon fat. saute 3 mins., string. lower heat. add spinach and heat through.
add pork with herbs, seasonings, sherry, if used, gelatin, cream and eggs. blend well.
spread half the mixture into lined pan. top with crumbled bacon and ham. cover with remaining meat mixture, patting down well. fold bacon lining the pan over the top. center bay leaf on top.
cover pan with foil, sealing well. place in another pan. add 1-inch of boiling water.
bake at 375F for 1 hour and 45 mins. until juices are no longer pink. remove foil. weight down. cool and chill 4 to 24 hours. before slicing.

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
Elegant, yet economical, this tasty chilled meatloaf can be served in little squares on crisp croutons as an appetizer, in a thick slice on lettuce as a main dish, or with mustard for picnic fare.
