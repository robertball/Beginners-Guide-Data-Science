Title:
Mocha Ricotta Creme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1009583.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup part-skim ricotta cheese
1/2 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 packet sugar substitute
Dash espresso powder
5 mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix together the ricotta, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and sugar substitute in a dessert bowl. Serve chilled with a dusting of espresso powder and sprinkled with the mini chocolate chips.

Submitter: 
The South Beach Diet Online
Description: 
Sweetened ricotta and chocolate makes the perfect South Beach Diet Phase 1 dessert.
