Title:
Easy microwave poached eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup water
1 tablespoon white vineger
2 large eggs

Directions:
Put 1/4 cup cold water in microwave safe measuring cup. Add one tablespoon white vinegar and two cold large eggs. Microwave for 80 seconds, drain and serve immediately. Your cooking time may vary by a few seconds.

Submitter: 
Bob Duchesneau
Description: 
You are less than three minutes from perfect poached eggs.
