Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2441312.jpg
Ingredients:
12 Beet Pickled Eggs (recipe follows),  8oz Greek Honey yogurt and 1small bunch or bag of Baby Kale.

Directions:
Beet Pickled Eggs: Hard boil one dozen eggs (or more) in boiling water for ten minutes. Cool in ice water for 10 minutes and peel. Place in a covered container with 1/2 organic apple cider vinegar, 2-3 cloves crushed garlic, 1Tabledoon dry mustard, two fennel fronds chopped and 1 Tablespoon of Sea salt. Add two 13oz or larger cans of beet with the juice and one sliced onion. Add enough water to cover all ingrediants. Keep covered and refrigerated for 3-7 days to produce color and taste. Serve cold, sliced in half on a bed of Kale and a side order of Greek Honey yogurt to be used as fluffy toppers. May also be served as appetizers with 1teaspoon Greek honey yogurt and bits of Baby Kale on top as shown. As a Brunch item consider on plates with a bed of Baby Kale, sides of Greek honey yogurt, as well as some of the beets and onions. Perfect for making Art with your food! Any ingrediants left over May be used to start the next batch. Therefore this recipe, like good Gishships are unending!
