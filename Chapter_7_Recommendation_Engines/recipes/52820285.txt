Title:
Cheese Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dry white wine
1/2 pound shredded Swiss cheese
1/2 pound shredded Gruyere cheese
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, cut into 1 inch cubes

Directions:
Simmer wine in fondue pot. Add Swiss cheese, Gruyere cheese, 1/4 pound at a time. Stir after each addition of cheese until melted. Stir in flour. When all the cheese has melted, stir in salt. Serve with cut-up French bread.
