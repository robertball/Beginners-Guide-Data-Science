Title:
Pinapple Angle Food cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One angle food cake mix
One 14oz to 16oz can of crushed pineapple
One jar of marachino cherries
whipped cream for garnish

Directions:
mix angle food cake mix with entire can of pineapple including juice.  drain cherries and chop add to cake mixture.  Bake as directed on box.  Nothing else needed.
