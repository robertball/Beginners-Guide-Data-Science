Title:
English Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (17-oz) cans english peas,drained
2 hard-cooked eggs,chopped
1c.colby cheese,cooked
1med. onion,finely
1/4 tsp.Celery
dash of pepper
2 t. blue cheese dressing (optional)
mayonnaise

Directions:
combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Add mayonnaise as desired,using enough to mix well.
