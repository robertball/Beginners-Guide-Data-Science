Title:
Breakfast Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/833830.jpg
Ingredients:
8 flour tortillas, fajita size
2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar jack or mexican blend)
6-8 eggs, cooked scrambled
12 slices precooked bacon, torn into 1 inch pieces
Cooking Spray
Salsa

Directions:
Heat nonstick pan over medium-low heat. Spray lightly with cooking spray.
Place one tortilla in pan.  Sprinkle with 1/4 cup cheese, layer of eggs (about 3/4 cup), bacon, and another 1/4 cup cheese.  Top with tortilla.
When lightly browned CAREFULLY flip over with large spatula.  Brown other side lightly, remove and cut into 4 triangles. (Cooking time usually about 2 minutes each side, but will vary.)
Repeat process for other quesadillas and serve with salsa.
