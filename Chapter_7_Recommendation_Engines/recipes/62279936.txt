Title:
Hashbrown Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 regular size bag hashbrowns
1/4 cup milk
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 cups shredded cheddar
8 oz. sour cream
dash of onion powder
dash garli powder
1 lb bacon cut into bits and fried

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Leave out a handful of bacon bits. Spread mix into a 9x13 pan, sprinkle with bacon bits and a little cheddar cheese. Bake at 350 for 35-45 mintues
