Title:
Spicy Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2 tablespoons of butter
1 small onion or ½ of a large onion
2 cloves of garlic (or 1 teaspoon of garlic powder)
1 tablespoon of brown sugar
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon dry mustard
½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 cups ketchup
2-3 tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce (or to taste)
A splash of Tabasco or Hot Sauce (optional)
Buns

Directions:
Dice the onion and garlic cloves then set aside.
Brown ground beef, onion and garlic.
Salt and pepper to taste
Continue cooking until meat is browned.
Drain and rinse meat in hot water.  Drain carefully
Next add in one cup of water and rest of intredients. Stir until well combined and then simmer for 15 minutes stirring occasionally.
Serve on toasted bread or buns of your choice.
