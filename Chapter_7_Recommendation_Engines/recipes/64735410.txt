Title:
Skillet Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken thighs
Salt/pepper
1/2 Tablespoons olive oil
2 Tablespoons unsalted butter
6 cloves garlic, lightly smashed
3/4 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup white wine
1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 teaspoons salt
1/2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Directions:
Season chicken
Heat up skillet  with olive oil
Pan fry chicken on both sides, but not cooked through. Set aside
Add butter to skillet and saute the garlic
Put chicken back in skillet with broth, wine, lemon juice and salt
Lower heat and cover ; cook for 10 minutes, turning once
Top with parsley
