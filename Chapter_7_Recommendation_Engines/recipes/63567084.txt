Title:
Mediterranean Rice Casserole Recipe

Ingredients:
4 cups cooked brown rice?
2 lbs. ground organic turkey or beef?
1/3 cup organic extra-virgin olive oil?
1.2  sliced almonds / sunflower
1 cup raisins?
2 large onions, chopped?
8 cloves garlic, minced or finely grated?
1 cup chopped parsley?j
juice of  lemon (about 6 tbsp)?
4 tbsp. balsamic vinegar?
1 pack chopped organic kale
4 tbsp. dried dill weed?
4 tsp dried thyme?
1 tsp ground cinnamon?
1.5 tsp ground allspice?
1 tsp paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a large pot or dutch oven, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add onions and garlic and saut√© until onion is golden brown, around 10 minutes. Add remaining ingredients except for rice and stir well. Turn heat to minimum, cover, and let simmer for about 5 minutes to combine flavors and allow greens to wilt.
Add rice and mix well. Turn the mixture into a greased casserole and bake until heated through, about 20 minutes. Makes 16 side dish servings or 8 entree servings. May be frozen.
