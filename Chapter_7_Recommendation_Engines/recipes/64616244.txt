Title:
Hermits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups butter
3 cups brown sugar
4 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
4 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1 teaspoon ginger
1 teaspoon mace
2 teaspoons cinnamon
2 cups raisins
2 cups dates chopped
2 cups walnuts or pecans, chopped

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar. Add all dry ingredients, flour and fruit. Drop onto greased sheet. Do not overbake.  Bake at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
K. Lea
Description: 
A flavorful spice-filled moist cookie.  Use good baking soda (bought the dollar store version and it didn't rise).  Don't cook too long, the cookies stay soft and the flavor is great, even after a couple of weeks.  Baked cookies freeze well.  This makes a large batch.
