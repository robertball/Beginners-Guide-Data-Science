Title:
Simple Peach Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/671273.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of chopped peaches (fresh or frozen)
1 heaping tbs light brown sugar.
1/2 tbs butter

Directions:
Heat in a sauce pan on med/high, stirring frequently for about 10 minutes or until peaches are soft and sauce thickens.

Submitter: 
Kristi Francis
Description: 
Chunky and fresh, this buttery peach sauce is a perfect topper for anything from pancakes to ice cream.
