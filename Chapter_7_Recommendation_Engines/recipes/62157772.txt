Title:
Sweet and Spicy Chicken on the Grill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup honey
2 tablespoons butter
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
salt and pepper to taste
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked
1 red bell pepper
1 green bell pepper
1 Vidalia onion
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Preheat grill for medium heat.
Combine first five ingredients in a small saucepan and boil, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and set aside.
Cut the chicken and peppers into strips and the onion into wedges; combine in medium bowl.
Pour honey mixture onto chicken and vegetables; mix thoroughly.
Grease a small baking sheet with olive oil.
Spread Chicken and vegetables onto baking sheet; cover with aluminum foil.
Cook on grill at medium to low heat for 15-20 minutes or until vegetables are tender, stirring FREQUENTLY (the honey will quickly caramelize and burn).

Submitter: 
A Turtle in the Kitchen
Description: 
This is a great alternative to Chinese food, and super easy to make if you use leftover chicken!
