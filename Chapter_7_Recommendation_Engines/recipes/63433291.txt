Title:
Chicken, Sweet Potato, White Bean Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 chicken beasts, cut up
2 15 oz cans chicken broth
1 medium sweet potato
1 15 oz can cannelini beans
1-2 tsp chili powder
1/8 tsp cumin
Salt and pepper to taste
Avocado

Directions:
Brown chicken, onion and garlic in oil.
Add broth, sweet potato, and seasonings.
Bring to boil and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
Add beans and simmer another 10-15 minutes.
Serve with avocado slices.
