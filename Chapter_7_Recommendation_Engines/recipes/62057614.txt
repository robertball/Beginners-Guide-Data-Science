Title:
Easy Baked Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tilapia fillets
1 pat butter
Old Bay Seasoning
Lemon slices
garlic salt
1 pkg California blend frozen vegetables

Directions:
Coat a 13 x 9 baking dish.  Place fillets and butter in the dish, sprinkle Old Bay seasoning on each and top with lemon slice.  Sprinkle each with garlic salt.  Add frozen vegetables, salt and pepper to taste and bake covered for 25-30 minutes at 375 degrees.

Submitter: 
F_Gory
Description: 
This easy recipe only takes a few minutes to prepare, uses few ingredients, and is flavorful.
