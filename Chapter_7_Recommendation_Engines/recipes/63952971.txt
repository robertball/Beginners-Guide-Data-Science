Title:
Spinach Lasagna III - 6 serving size Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 lasagna noodles (pre cooked are fine)
1 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 cup chopped fresh mushrooms
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 1/2 tsp minced garlic
2 cups fresh spinach (or even more)
1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
1/3 cup grated Romano cheese
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 egg
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 1/2 cups tomato pasta sauce
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. In a skillet over medium-high heat, cook mushrooms, onions, and garlic,chopped spinach in olive oil until onions are tender. Drain excess liquid and cool.
Combine ricotta cheese, Romano cheese, salt, oregano, basil, pepper, and egg in a bowl. Add cooled mushroom mixture. Lay 2 lasagna sheets in bottom of dish.Spread one third of the cheese/spinach mixture over noodles. Sprinkle 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese on top. Spread 1/2 cup pasta sauce over cheese. Repeat layering 2 times.
Cover dish with foil, bake for 1 hour. Cool 15 minutes before serving.

Description: 
Fresh spinach and plenty of ricotta, Romano and mozzarella make this a cheesy and hearty dish. This lasagna can also be made without the spinach.Modified from: Spinach Lasagna III
