Title:
15-Minute Chili and Rice Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef or ground turkey
1 (15.5 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 cups water
1 (1.25 ounce) envelope chili seasoning mix
2 cups Minute® White Rice, uncooked

Directions:
Brown meat in large skillet on medium heat. Drain.
Add beans, tomatoes with their liquid, tomato sauce, water and seasoning mix;  stir. Bring to boil.
Stir in rice; cover. Reduce heat to low. Cook 5 minutes. Stir before serving.

Submitter: 
Minute® Rice
Description: 
This recipe combines ground beef with kidney beans, tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili seasoning and Minute® Rice for a hot and hearty pot of chili in a hurry.
