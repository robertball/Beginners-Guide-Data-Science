Title:
Peanut Butter Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
6 tbsp. margarine, softened
2/3 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray an 8x8 or 9x9 inch glass baking pan with cooking spray.
In a small bowl, combine the flour, baking powder and salt. In the bowl of a stand mixer, combine the peanut butter, margarine and sugars. Beat until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time.  Add vanilla, mix well. Add dry ingredients and blend to combine.
Turn batter into prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes, or until light golden brown and toothpick comes out clean.
Cool completely and frost with Peanut Butter Fudge frosting, before serving!
