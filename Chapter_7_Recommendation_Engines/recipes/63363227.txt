Title:
Chocolate Orange Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/905211.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package super moist chocolate fudge cake mix
1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
1/2 cup Vegetable Oil
4 eggs
1/2 cup hot water
1 cup sour cream
2.5 teaspoonâ€™s Orange Bakery Emulsion (Or use orange extract)
2 x 3 oz Bar of Orange milk chocolate, chopped (I use the all natural Blood Orange milk Chocolate from World market)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a Bundt pan.
Combine cake mix, pudding mix, oil, eggs, water, sour cream, and extract.
Beat until smooth. Stir in chocolate. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake for 1 hour. Allow to cool.
