Title:
Oven Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup Bisquick
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. butter
1 1/2 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. pepper
4 chicken breasts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Melt butter in baking pan.
Mix remaining ingredients and toss chicken to coat.
Bake 35 minutes, skin side down then turn and cook 20 more minutes or until juices run clear.
