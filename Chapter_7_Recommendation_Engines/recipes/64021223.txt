Title:
Cinnamon Nut Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Batter:
3 3/4 cups flour
1 1/4 cups sugar
5 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1/2 cup plus 2 tsp. butter or shortening
2 eggs plus 1 egg white
1 1/4 cups milk
Filling:
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp. cinnamon
2 tbsp. flour
2 tbsp. melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Combine all filling ingredients and set aside.
In a large bowl, combine dry ingredients.
Cut shortening into dry ingredients.
Add milk and eggs and mix until combined.
I greased loaf pans, layer batter and filling.
Bake for 20-25 minutes or until loaves test done.
