Title:
Bad Breath Banishers-dog treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c brown rice flour
1 TB activated charcoal (drugstores, not briquets)
3 TB canola oil
1 egg
1/2 c each chopped fresh mint and parsley
2/3 lowfat milk

Directions:
Preheat oven 400F. lightly oil cookie sheet. combine flour and charcoal. add all other ingredients. drop teaspoon-fulls on oiled sheet about 1" apart. bake and cool
