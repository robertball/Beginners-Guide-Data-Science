Title:
Mrs. Miller's Iced Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups strong brewed coffee
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 cup half & half
3 cups milk
Ice cubes

Directions:
In a glass or metal pitcher, mix coffee and brown sugar. Add half & half and milk, stir.
Place ice cubes into a tall glass, fill with coffee mixture, stir and serve.

Submitter: 
Billie Miller
Description: 
A perfect summer afternoon drink to share with your girlfriends. Use more or less sugar to taste.
