Title:
Dark 'n' Stormy Ginger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4464005.jpg
Ingredients:
ice cubes
3 fluid ounces ginger beer
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers dark spiced rum
1 slice lime

Directions:
Fill a highball glass with ice; add ginger beer. Slowly pour rum over ginger beer and garnish glass with lime slice.

Submitter: 
AZ
Description: 
Bring the taste of the tropics home to wherever you live with this boater's favorite drink. Be careful because they can creep up on you! You can use ginger ale if you need to but real ginger beer is what makes this drink special. You can stir it if you want to or enjoy the layers of ginger and rum.
