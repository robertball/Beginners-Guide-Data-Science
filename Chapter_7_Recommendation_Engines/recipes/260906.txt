Title:
Caramel Apple Salad with Cream Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/293474.jpg
Ingredients:
5 large Granny Smith apples, cut into bite-size chunks
4 chocolate-coated caramel-peanut nougat candy bars (such as Snickers®), cut into bite-size pieces
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow cream (such as Marshmallow Fluff®)

Directions:
Mix apples and candy bar pieces together in a large bowl.
Mix softened cream cheese and marshmallow cream together in a separate bowl. Fold into the apple-candy mixture. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
23DAISYS
Description: 
Granny Smith apples, cream cheese mixture, and candy combine to make a tasty salad great for picnics... or any time for that matter.
