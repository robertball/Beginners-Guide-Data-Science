Title:
That Sweet and Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130078.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white sugar
1/3 cup white vinegar
2/3 cup water
1/4 cup Teriyaki Sauce
1 tablespoon ketchup
2 tablespoons cornstarch
2 Garlic Cloves
1 Teaspoon Honey
1/4 Teaspoon Lemon Zest

Directions:
In a sauce pan, bring water, teriyaki sauce, and vinegar to a boil.
Add ketchup, corn starch, and sugar.
Mince garlic, red peppers, and lemon zest together.
Add garlic, peppers, lemon zest to the rest.
Stir and let sauce thicken.
Strain sauce into serving bowl or tup a ware.
Cool off in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Josh Charlton Cornelius
Description: 
I brought out this recipe from this website. It needed more of a punch to it. The soy sauce made this seem like a common made recipe. Now there's a very mild spiciness and flavor that brings out the taste and clarity.
