Title:
Chris's Easter Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of yeast (2 1/4 teaspoons)
1/4 cup warm water
1 (3 oz) package of instant lemon pudding mix
1/4 cup margarine
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup scalded milk
4-4 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
4 eggs
1 tablespoon water
multicolor sprinkles for decoration

Directions:
Dissolve the yeast in warm water.
Mix pudding, margarine and salt in a large bowl.  Pour milk over pudding mixture.
Mix until margarine is melted and pudding is dissolved.
Cool the pudding mix to to luke warm.
Add 1 1/2 cups of flour, mix well.
Beat in yeast mixture and 3 eggs with an electric mixer.
Add enough remaining flour, a little at a time, to make a soft dough.
Turn the dough onto a floured surface and allow to rest for 10 min.
Knead for 10 min and place in a greased bowl.
Allow to rise for 1 hour.
Divide the dough into 6 equal parts.
Shape each part into a rope (about 20-24 inches long if you can)
Make 2 braids, shape each braid in a circle and place in 2 greased 9inch round cake pans.
Allow to rise for 1 hour.
Beat remaining egg with 1 tablespoon of water.  Brush the egg mixture on each braid and shake sprinkles over each braid.
Bake in a preheated oven at 375 for about 25 min.

Submitter: 
Christine Douglas- Kaiser
Description: 
A braided sweet bread.  My family expects this every Easter.
