Title:
Garlic Orange Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp orange peel cut in 1" long thin strips
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/3 cup water
1/4 cup tomato paste
1/4 cup orange juice
2 tbsp molasses
1/8 tsp pepper
Egg noodles

Directions:
Cook beef until color just changes, stirring often. Transfer to bowl.
Reduce heat to medium. Cook onion, garlic and orange peel 3 minutes, stirring often.
Stir in soup, water, tomato paste, orange juice, molasses and pepper. Heat to boiling. Reduce heat to low: simmer 5 minutes. Add beef; cook 5 minutes more, stirring occasionally. Serve over noodles.
