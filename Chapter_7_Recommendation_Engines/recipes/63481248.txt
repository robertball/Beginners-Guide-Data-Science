Title:
Vegan Gluten Free Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/955784.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup raw sugar
1/2 cup agave
3 Tablespoons Ghee or Vegan Margarine
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon gluten-free vanilla extract
3/4 cup cocao powder
3 Chia Seed Eggs
3/4 cup brown rice flour
1/2 cup almond flour (almond meal)
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 cup or 1/2 cup raw cacao nibs (optional)
1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees.
Make Chia Eggs: 3 Tablespoons of Chia seeds + 3/4-1 cup of warm water.
let sit in blender for about 5-10 minutes .
Blend Chia seeds and cacao nibs together at high speed.
Add  raw sugar and agave into eggs and blend at high speed in blender.
Add vanilla & ghee to blender and blend.
In large bowl, mix flours, baking powder and salt together.
Add cocao powder and chocolate chips to flours.
Add wet  ingredients from blender.
Mix together.
Pour into an oiled baking pan.
Bake for 45-55 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
These gluten free egg free brownies are divine! If you love chocolate and want all the taste of a brownie but none of the problems of wheat and eggs, try these!
