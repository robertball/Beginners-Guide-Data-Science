Title:
Skillet Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/787061.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
4 cups shredded cabbage
1 medium green pepper, cut into thin strips
2 tablespoons water
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, cubed and softened

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet; add cabbage and green pepper and toss to coat. Stir in water, salt and pepper. Cover; simmer for 8-10 minutes or until cabbage is tender. Add cream cheese; stir until melted.

Submitter: 
Charmaine Fricke
Description: 
I use this dish often when the schedule gets tight and I need a 'hurry up' vegetable to cook. It adds plenty of substance to a simple meal.
