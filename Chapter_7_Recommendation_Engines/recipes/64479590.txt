Title:
Connie Lu's Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb ground beef
1 packet taco seasoning
1 bag shredded lettuce
1 pkg cherry  (or grape) tomatoes
1/2 btl Thousand Island dressing
3 dolups of sour cream
1 pkg Kraft Mexican Cheese
1/2 bag Dorito's

Directions:
Brown ground beef; drain; add taco seasoning as per seasoning directions; cool til warm, not HOT
While beef is cooling, cut tomatoes in half and toss with lettuce, thousand island dressing and cheese;
Once beef is cool, add sour cream. Toss into lettuce mixture.
Serve with Dorito's on side to keep the chips from getting soggy;
Add chips to salad when ready to eat;
ENJOY!!

Submitter: 
The Kitchen Witch
Description: 
This quick and easy salad great for summer as a main dish or side!
