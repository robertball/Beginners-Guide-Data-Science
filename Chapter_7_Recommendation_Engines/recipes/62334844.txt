Title:
Jim's Summer Spaghetti Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/387392.jpg
Ingredients:
3- 10.5 oz containers of cherry tomatoes
1- 12 oz can of black olives
1 - bunch fresh basil
1- tablespoon minced garlic
12- tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
5-tablespoons red wine vinegar
1-tablespoon capers
sea salt and cracked pepper to taste
1- 16 oz pkg angel hair spaghetti

Directions:
Cut all the cherry tomatoes in 1/2 and put in a large mixing bowl being careful not to lose the juice. Squeeze the tomoatoes while in the bowl gently so they don't look beat up but the juice comes out.
take the black olives and drain the juice. cut the olives in 1/2.
Add the olice oil, garlic , basil(chopped) and capers.Mix all the ingredients together well. Set aside the bowl and let it stand 20-30  mins
Boil the spaghetti until al dente.
Pour the tomato miture over the spaghetti and stir making sure to coat it all.

This is an awesome spaghetti salad that is the hit of every BBQ party. Very tasty and refreshing!
