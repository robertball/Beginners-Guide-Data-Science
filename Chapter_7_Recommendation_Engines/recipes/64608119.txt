Title:
Top Me Twice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 Eggs
1 cup Sugar
1 Tsp Vanilla
2 Cups Flour
1 Tsp Salt
1 Tsp Baking Soda
1 - 14 oz Can Crushed Pineapple, with Juice
1st Topping:
1/2 Cup Coconut
1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
2nd Topping:
1/2 Cup butter melted
1/2 Cup White Sugar
1/2 Cup Cream
1 Tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Cake;
Beat eggs until light, add sugar and vanilla.
Net add dry ingredients, followed by pineapple with juice,
Mix and pour int 9x13 inch greased pan
1st Topping:
Mix and sprinkle on unbaked cake.
Bake cake at 350 F for 35-40 minutes testing with a toothpick
2nd Topping:
Mix well.
Pour evenly over hot cake
Good served warm or cold.
Store left overs in refridgerator
