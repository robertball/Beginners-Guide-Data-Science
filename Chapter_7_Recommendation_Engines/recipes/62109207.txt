Title:
Twisted Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 10-oz cans condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup water
2 cups cut green beans (canned, frozen or fresh, doesn't matter)
2 tbsp minced garlic
1 tbsp dried onion flakes
1 tbsp basil
1 6-oz can french-fried onions
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Cube and cook chicken in skillet over med heat with a little EVOO and pepper.  Set aside.
In med bowl, mix soup, water, garlic, onion flakes, basil and pepper to taste.
Layer in an 8x8 casserole dish starting with 1/2 soup mixture, chicken, green beans, then remaining soup mixture.
Cover and cook in preheated oven for 25 minutes.  Uncover, sprinkle with french-fried onions and continue cooking for 5 minutes or until onions are crunchy.

Submitter: 
Kris Swiggum
Description: 
A twist on the traditional green bean casserole.  This makes it a meal-in-one! And SOOOO easy!
