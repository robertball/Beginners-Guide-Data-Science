Title:
Christine's Bacon and brussel sprout cranberry salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/708001.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb thick sliced bacon cut into 1in chunks
1 lb brussel sprouts cleaned and cut into 1/4 in slices
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup chopped unsalted roasted almonds
1/2 medium size gala apple diced
1/4 cup water
balsamic vinagrette dressing of your choice to taste

Directions:
cook bacon in wok untill 3/4 way done, drain off fat, add remaining ingredients except dressing and stir fry on med high apx 7-10 min until brussel sprouts are bright green and some what tender to the fork. then place in a serving dish and drizzle with balsamic vinagrette dressing of your choice.

Submitter: 
Christine Folkes
Description: 
wonderful quick and healthy warm salad. can be prepared in advance.
