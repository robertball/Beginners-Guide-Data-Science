Title:
Creole Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. cooked chicken, diced
1 pkg. summer sausage, sliced
2 c. pasta, cooked
1 c. chopped bell peppers
1 can Great Northern beans
1 jar alfredo sauce
3 tbs creole seasoning
2 c. shredded colby jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Grease a 9X13 baking dish and combine all ingredients except cheese. Stir to mix then top with cheese and bake for 30 minutes.

Description: 
Comfort food with a zest! Easy to throw together, economical, and makes fantastic leftovers.
