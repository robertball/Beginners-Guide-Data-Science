Title:
Warm Spiced Wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bottle merlot
2 cinnamon sticks
2 cloves
1 whole nutmeg
4 3-inch orange peel strips
4 3-inch lemon peel strips
3 tablespoons sugar

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine all ingredients.
Simmer for 10-15 min.
Serve in your favorite holiday mugs.

Submitter: 
Steph
Description: 
A great way to warm your Thanksgiving dinner guests.
