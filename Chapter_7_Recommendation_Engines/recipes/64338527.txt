Title:
Mushroom Lentil Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sliced mushrooms
2 cups cooked brown lentils (drained)
2 tbs (40g) tapioca flour
1/4 cup (70g) oats
1 tsp (5g) onion powder
1 tsp (5g) garlic powder
1 tsp (5g) whole fennel seeds
1 tbs (20g) mixed Italian herbs
1 tsp (5g) ground sage
1 tsp (5g) paprika
1 tbs (20g) tomato paste
salt and pepper to taste ( I used 1 tsp course celtic sea salt and 1/4tsp white pepper)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Dry "fry" mushrooms, they do not have to be done too well.
Add all ingredients to food processor and mix til it holds its shape.
Form into 6 patties, put on parchment lined sheet pan
Bake 15 min. turn over bake 10 min.
