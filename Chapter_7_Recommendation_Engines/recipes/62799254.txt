Title:
Cranberry Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup dried cranberries
2 cups broccoli florets
4 cups shredded cabbage
1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
1 cup raisins
1 small onion, finely chopped
8 slices of cooked bacon, crumbled
1 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup sugar
2 Tblsp. cider vinegar

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cranberries, broccoli, cabbage, walnuts, raisins, onion and bacon and mix.  Combine mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar in a small bowl and stir well.  Pour over first mixture and toss well to coat.  Cover and refrigerate.
