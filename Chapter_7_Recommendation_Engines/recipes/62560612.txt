Title:
Chocolate Oatmeal Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536139.jpg
Ingredients:
½ cup butter
½ cup powdered cocoa
2 cups sugar
½ cup milk
3 cups quick oats
½ cup shredded coconut
½ cup peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large saucepan, mix butter, cocoa, sugar, and milk.
Bring mixture to a rolling boil and boil for about 1 minute, stirring frequently
Remove from heat and add peanut butter, vanilla, coconut and oats.
Mix thoroughly.
Drop into round balls (about one tablespoon full) onto waxed paper and let cool in refrigerator for at least an hour.

Submitter: 
aimeejean
Description: 
No bake, extremely delicious cookies you can put in the freezer and pull out when you have company.  Great for the Holidays!
