Title:
OREO Stuffed Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695256.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks softened butter
3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla extract (or almond extract depending on your taste)
3 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
10 oz bag chocolate chips
1 package Oreos, regular or double-stuffed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Using one large bowl, cream butter and white and brown sugars until well combined. Add in eggs and vanilla (or almond) extract until all ingredients are combined.
Add the flour, salt and baking soda. Add chocolate chips until just combined (do not overmix).
Put a spoonful of cookie dough on top of the Oreo. Take another scoop of cookie dough and place it under the Oreo. Seal edges of dough together around the Oreo by pressing and cupping dough until Oreo is enclosed.
Place onto a parchment lined baking sheet and bake for 10-13 minutes (or until golden brown). Let cool for 5 minutes before transferring to cooling rack.
