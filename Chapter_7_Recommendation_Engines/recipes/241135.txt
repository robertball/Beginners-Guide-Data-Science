Title:
Polska Kielbasa with Cabbage and Egg Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2027466.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package wide egg noodles
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 (13 ounce) package Hillshire FarmÂ® Turkey Polska Kielbasa, sliced into bite-size pieces
1 medium onion, diced
1 medium head cabbage, cored and cut into 1-inch pieces
1/4 cup water
2 tablespoons butter (optional)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook egg noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 5 minutes. Drain.
Heat oil in large skillet with a cover over medium heat. Add Polska Kielbasa and onion; cook and stir until sausage is brown and onions are tender, about 10 minutes. Add chopped cabbage and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper. Add 1/4 cup water to the skillet and cover. Cook over medium heat until cabbage is tender, about 10 minutes.
Stir in cooked and drained noodles.  Add 2 tablespoons butter and stir over medium heat to combine. Season with freshly ground black pepper to taste.
