Title:
Whole Wheat Maple Walnut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/8 c. whole milk
2 c. whole wheat flour
1 c. bread flour
1 t. salt
3 T. maple syrup
1 T. butter
1 T. dry milk
1 1/2 t. yeast for bread machine
1/2 c. finely chopped walnuts

Directions:
Put ingredients in pan of bread machine in order listed.
Set bread machine on whole wheat setting (4 hours).
Press start.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Whole wheat bread made with the goodness of maple syrup and walnuts. This recipe makes a nice versatile loaf ideal for sandwiches, French toast, a dinner table bread basket ... anything.
