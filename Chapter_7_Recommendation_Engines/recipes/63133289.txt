Title:
Homemade Chicken Stock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken
2 bay leaves
6 springs of thyme
6 black peppercorns
1 head of garlic, split in half
1 large onion, peeled and quartered
3 carrots, coarsley chopped
Leafy tops from one bunch of celery
2 tablespoons salt

Directions:
Place the chicken and all ingredients in a large Dutch oven and cover with water.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer about 1 hour, until the chicken is cooked through. Remove the chicken.  Continue to simmer the liquid until flavor is concentrated to taste, about another 30 minutes. Strain the stock. Makes about 2 quarts.

Submitter: 
Barb0607
Description: 
Flavorful chicken stock is a great base for any recipe.  The cooked chicken is great to add to soups or to use for chicken salad.
