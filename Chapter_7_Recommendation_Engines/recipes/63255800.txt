Title:
Chef Jeff's Signature Kahlua Pulled Pork  (2012) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/851615.jpg
Ingredients:
5-7 lb pork shoulder
1 1/2 cups Kahlua Liquor
1 TBL sea salt
2 TBL black pepper
1 1/2 cups beef stock
4 fresh garlic cloves (pressed)
4 whole green onions
2 dried chipotle peppers

Directions:
Mix all ingredients listed and mix thourghly.  Place pork shoulder in a large thick food safe plastic bag.  Add marinade and marinade for 2 or 3 hours.  Overnight would be fine.  Place pork shoulder in a dutch oven and bake 350 for 4 hours.  Let stand in dutch oven covered for 30 minutes after removing from oven

Submitter: 
Jeffrey Morris
Description: 
This sinfully delicious style of slow cooking pork shoulder was inspired from one of my favorite Chef Instructors from Culinary School....This is an easy way to seriously enjoy some great Pulled Pork Tacos.
Enjoy..
