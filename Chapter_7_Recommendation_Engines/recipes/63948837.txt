Title:
Chicken Cutlets with Tarragon-Mushroom Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups unsalted/low sodium chicken broth
1/2 carrot, cut into one-inch pieces
1/2 celery stock, cut into one-inch pieces
1/4 onion, halved
1 bay leaf
1 garlic clove, crushed
.
4 (6 ounce) boneless-skinless chicken breasts
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
1/2 teaspoon black pepper, divided
3 tablespoons flour
.
2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
2 teaspoons butter, divided
1 cup quartered mushrooms
1/2 cup frozen pearl onions
.
1 tablespoon water
2 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
1 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon

Directions:
Place first 6 ingredients in a saucepan; bring to boil.  Cook 6 minutes or until reduced to 1 1/4 cups.
Remove solids with slotted spoon; discard.
Cut chicken breast halves in half horizontally to form 8 cutlets.  Sprinkle evenly with 1/4 teaspoon and 1/4 teaspoon pepper.  Place flour in a shallow dish; dredge chicken in flour.
Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Add 1 tablespoon oil and 1 teaspoon butter to pan; swirl until butter melts.  Add four of the cutlets to pan; cook 2 minutes on each side or until golden brown.  Remove from pan; keep warm.  Repeat procedure with remaining oil & butter,  and remaining cutlets.
Return skillet to medium-high heat, add mushrooms and pearl onions; cook 5 minutes until browned stirring occasionally.  Add stock mixture; bring to a boil scraping pan to loosen any browned bits.
Combine one tablespoon water with cornstarch in small bowl; stir into mushroom mixture, cook stirring 1 minute until sauce is thickened.  Add remaining salt,pepper and tarragon.
Spoon sauce over chicken to serve.
