Title:
Spanish Rice with Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon, diced
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
3 cups cooked rice
1 1-lb can tomatoes
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
Fry bacon in large skillet until crisp; remove and drain bacon. Pour off all but 2 tbsp of drippings.
Add onion and green pepper to drippings and cook, stirring until onion is tender. Stir in bacon and remaining ingredients and cook uncovered over low heat for 15 minutes

Submitter:

Description: 
This twist on Spanish rice is great for the bacon lover and a fabulous side dish.
