Title:
Masala Masoor Recipe Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Ingredients:
1 teacup masoor (whole) 3/4 cup
2 sliced onions
2 large chopped tomatoes
7 or 8 small whole onions
4 tblsp ghee
salt to taste
1 tblsp chopped coriander for decoration.
To Be Ground Into A Paste:
8 cloves garlic
8 kashmiri chillies
2 tsp coriander seeds
1 tsp cumin seeds
35 mm (1 1/2") piece ginger
I use a coffee grinder to ground the seeds only and then add finely grated ginger to make my paste.

Directions:
How to make Masala Masoor:
Wash the masoor dal. Wash in the stockpot your will be cooking the dal in.
Add enough water to cover the dal about 1 inch water.
Add whole onion to lentil, bring to a boil and simmer on low until lentils are cooked. about 40 min.
Heat the ghee in a fry pan and fry the sliced onions for 3 minutes
Add the paste and fry for 3 to 4 minutes
Add the cooked masoor and salt
After 5 minutes, add the tomatoes. Cook for 10 minutes.
Serve hot with basmati rice or roti's, sprinkle with chopped coriander.
