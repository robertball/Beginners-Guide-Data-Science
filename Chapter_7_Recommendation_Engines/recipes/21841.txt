Title:
Green Chile Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1055009.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7 ounce) package garlic cheese spread
2 (4 ounce) cans chopped green chile peppers
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium low heat, warm the garlic cheese spread, green chile peppers and cream of mushroom soup. Stir occasionally, until the mixture is well blended. Transfer to a medium bowl and serve.

Submitter: 
DEBOKC
Description: 
This is my version of chile con queso. It is easy and delicious. Cream of mushroom soup balances the spicy flavor of green chiles. Serve with tortilla chips.
