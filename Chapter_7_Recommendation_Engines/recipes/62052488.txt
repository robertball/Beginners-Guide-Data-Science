Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef or turkey
1 15 oz can stewed diced tomatoes
1 15 oz can tomato sauce
1 medium onion (chopped)
1 green bell pepper (chopped, seeds removed)
2 small cans of tomato paste
1 cup of Instant Rice (Minute)
Chili Powder (to taste)

Directions:
Brown and crumble the ground beef with the chopped onion and green pepper and chili powder.Drain. Drain the canned tomatoes, reserving juice. Add tomatoes and tomato sauce to the meat mixture. Measure juice drained from tomatoes, and add water as necessary to bring to 1 cup. Add water/juice to pan, along with 1 cup of instant rice, and more chili powder. Cover and simmer, stirring occassionaly for about 10 minutes. When rice is tender, and most of liquid is absorbed, add the two cans of tomato paste. Stir to blend in, and remove from heat. Add additional chili powder if necessary. Serve as is, top with cheese, or stuff pepers or cabbage leaves with this mixture.

Submitter: 
GENELLEB
Description: 
A great family recipe handed down for generations. Use this as a quick and healthy dinner as is, or fill green bell peppers and bake topped with cheese. My family loves this, and yours will too!
