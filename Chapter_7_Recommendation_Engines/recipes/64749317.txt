Title:
Ginger syrup (Gembersiroop) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6691616.jpg
Ingredients:
250 ml water
500 g cane sugar
150g ginger, peeled

Directions:
Grind the ginger to a pulp in a blender or food processor
Add water and sugar to a pan , and bring to a boil while stirring constantly
Once the sugar is dissolved, reduce heat to simmer, add ginger pulp, and continue stirring for 30 minutes.
Remove from heat, and allow pulp/syrup mixture to cool
Using a fine sieve, strain as much pulp from syrup as possible
store in refrigerator
