Title:
Saltine Crunch Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4261846.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 box saltines
1 c. butter (2 sticks) or coconut oil, etc.
2/3 c. packed brown sugar
12 oz. pkg chocolate chips
1/3 c. chopped, toasted pecans, walnuts,  almonds, peanuts, etc. (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Melt butter and brown sugar in a sauce pan. Boil for 3 minutes.
Line a jelly roll pan with saltines.
Pour the syrup over the saltines.
Bake for 7 minutes.
Sprinkle with chocolate chips and let stand for 10 minutes, then spread evenly.
Sprinkle with nuts if desired.
Loosen edges. Let cool.
Cut or break into bars or pieces.
