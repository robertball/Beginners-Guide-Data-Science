Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2463835.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
3 cups mini marshmallows
6 cups crispy rice cereal
8 oz. nondairy whipped topping, thawed
1 jar marshmallow fluff
24 gummy candies

Directions:
Melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until they melt.
Remove from heat and add cereal, stirring until it's coated.
Let mixture cool until you can handle it. Meanwhile, butter 2 mini muffin tins.
Butter your hands and press cereal-marshmallow mixture into muffin tins.
Mix whipped topping and marshmallow fluff. Dollop fluff mixture into muffin tins.
Place tins in freezer until fluff mixture is set (it will not freeze).
Top each Fluffy Bite with a gummy shark, T-rex, or other apex predator.
