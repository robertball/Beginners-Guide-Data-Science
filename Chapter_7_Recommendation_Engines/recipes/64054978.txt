Title:
Smothered Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Round steak cut into bites
flour
salt
pepper
smoked paprika
onion powder
garlic powder
1 can cream of mushroom
1 can water
1 packet onion soup mix
kitchen bouquet

Directions:
Mix flour, salt, pepper, paprika, onion powder, garlic powder. Dredge steak in flour. Brown.
Put steak, soup, water and onion soup in crockpot.
Cook on low 4-6 hours until tender.
Stir in kitchen bouquet.
