Title:
Just like take out Cashew Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon Sesame Oil
1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
1 teaspoon garlic powder
3 large boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into bite size pieces
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
3 cups fresh broccoli florets
1 cup thinly sliced carrots
2 teaspoons cornstarch
1/2 cup dark soy sauce
1/3 cup hoisin sauce
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 cup salted cashews
cooked rice

Directions:
In large bowl, combine first four ingredients; add chicken and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hrs. Remove chicken from marinade and reserve marinade. Heat oil in wok or large skillet over; stir-fry chicken until no longer pink, about 5 minutes. With slotted spoon remove chicken and set aside. Then in the same wok add broccoli and carrots, stir-fry for 4 minutes or until crisp. Combine Cornstarch, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, ginger and hte marinade; stir into the vegetable mixture. Cook until heathed through. Stir in chicken and cashews; heat through. Then serve over rice.
