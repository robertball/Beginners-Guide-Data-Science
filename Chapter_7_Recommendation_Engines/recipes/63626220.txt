Title:
Chocolate silky pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces of unsalted butter (softened)
8 ounces of cream cheese    (softened)
2 cups of powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
Pre-made chocolate graham cracker pie crust
Whipped topping of choice

Directions:
cream together butter and cream cheese.
Slowly add sugar and mix until smooth.
Add vanilla and mix thoroughly.
Mix in cocoa powder.
After thoroughly blending, switch to whisk attachment and whip until the mixture stops "taking air".
Spoon into pie crust.
Top with whipped cream.
Garnish with shaved Hershey bar (if desired)

The general comments on this frosting are that it is not too sweet, or too tart.
