Title:
Sauerkraut Rye Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sauerkraut - rinsed and well drained
3/4 cup warm water
1 1/2 tablespoons molasses
1 1/2 tablespoons butter
1 1/2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 heaping tablespoon caraway seeds
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 cup rye flour
2 cups bread flour
2 teaspoons active dry yeast
2 tablespoons cocoa powder

Directions:
Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select Basic Bread Cycle; press Start.

Submitter: 
Marlene Osborn
Description: 
This bread is very moist and tasty. Who doesn't love the mouth-watering aroma of sauerkraut?Modified from: Sauerkraut Rye Bread
