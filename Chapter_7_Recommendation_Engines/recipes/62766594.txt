Title:
Chicken and Vegetable Tortellini Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium carrots, sliced
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 3/4 inch pieces
1 cup sliced celery
1 can cannellini beans, drained, rinsed
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
14 oz. chicken broth
2 cups water
1 9-oz package refrigerated cheese-filled tortellini
1 cup firmly packed fresh baby spinach leaves
2 green onions, sliced
1 tsp. dried basil
2 Tbsp. shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a 4 quart slow cooker, layer carrots, garlic, chicken, celery and beans,   Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Pour roth and water over the top.  Stir to combine.  Cover; cook on low setting for 6 to 8 hours.
About 20 minutes before serving, stir tortellini, spinach, onions and basil into the chicken mixture.  Increase heat setting to high; cover and cook 15 to 20 minutes or until tortellini are tender.  Sprinkle individual servings with Parmesan cheese.
