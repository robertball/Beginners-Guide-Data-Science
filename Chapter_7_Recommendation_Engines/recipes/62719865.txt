Title:
Super Easy Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (10.5 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 can (10.5 oz) condensed cream of chicken soup
1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
1 1/2 pounds frozen meatballs
1 can (4 oz) of mushrooms NOT DRAINED

Directions:
Stir soups and evaporated milk together in slow cooker.
Stir in frozen meatballs and mushrooms with their liquid.
Cook on low for 5 hours.

Submitter: 
scarlett
Description: 
Easy Swedish Meatballs - just 5 ingredients in the slow cooker.  Serve over cooked rice or noodles.
