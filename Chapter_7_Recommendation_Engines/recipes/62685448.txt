Title:
Spicy, creamy vegetable soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil, divided
1/2 white onion, chopped
1/2 cup diced carrot
1/2 cup dice celery
4 large cloves garlic, diced fine
2 bay leaves
1 tbsp parsley
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp rosemerry
1 tsp dill weed
6 cups water
1 can hot rotel tomatoes
3 tblsp chicken base
1 cup potato peeled and chunked
1/2 cup fresh green bean, snapped and washed (opt)
1 container fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/2 bunch kale, coarsely chopped
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
In the bottom of large soup pot heat 1 tbsp olive oil, add onions, carrots and celery and cook until onions are translusent (3 min).  Add garlic, bay leaves, parsley, thyme, rosemerry, dill weed and cook until the garlic is fragrent (about 1-2 min).  Then add water, bring to boil and add chicken base. stir until base is dissloved.  Then add the can of tomatoes (undrained), potatoes and green beans bring back to a boil and simmer until veggies are tender.  While the pot simmers heat your other tbsp olive oil in a large skillet and brown sliced mushrooms, after they are golden brown add them to the pot as well. After everything is cooked through finish with cream and kale, let cook for an additional 5 min and then serve.
