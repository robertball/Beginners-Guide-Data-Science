Title:
Big Ray's Hot and Spicy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129657.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cayenne pepper sauce
1 teaspoon garlic salt
3 lbs chicken, cut up
2 tablespoons butter
1 cup baking mix
3/4 teaspoon onion salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
Blue cheese dressing

Directions:
Stir together hot sauce and garlic salt until blended. Pour over chicken; cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours but no longer than 24 hours.
Heat oven to 450ÂºF. Melt butter in 15 1/2 x 10 1/2 in pan, in oven. Stir together baking mix, onion salt and peppers. Remove chicken from sauce; discard sauce. Dust chicken with baking mixture. Place in single layer in pan.
Bake 25 minutes; turn and bake an additional 20 to 25 minutes or until chicken is golden brown and juice is no longer pink when centers of pieces are cut. Serve with dressing.
