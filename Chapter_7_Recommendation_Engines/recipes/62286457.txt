Title:
Lentils with port-glazed shallot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. ruby port
8 oz. small shallots plus 1 large shallot, peeled
12 oz. dried lentils, rinsed and drained
1 T. oil

Directions:
Combine port and small shallots in heavy saucepan.  Simmer over medium heat till shallots are tender and port glazes shallots, stirring occasionally, about 35 minutes.
Finely chop large shallot.  Combine with drained lentils and 3 c. water in heavy pan.  Bring to boil, cover and simmer till lentils are just tender, about 30-40 minutes.
Stir oil and port mixture into lentils.  season to taste with salt and pepper.
