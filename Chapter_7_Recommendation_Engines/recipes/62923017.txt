Title:
** Judi's Baked Apple Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 (8 oz.) pkg. Brown 'n Serve link sausage, cut into chunks
4 c. pancake mix
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. cinnamon
2 c. milk
4 eggs
2 c. chopped apples

Directions:
Brown sausages if not already fully cooked.
Mix pancake mix, nutmeg, cinnamon, milk, & eggs until smooth.
Stir sausage & apples into batter.
Pour into well-greased 9 x 13 pan. Bake 375 degrees for 35 min. until a toothpick comes out clean in center.
Serve hot w/maple syrup.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Recipe from sister Judi many, many years ago. Think she got it off a package of brown and serve sausage.
