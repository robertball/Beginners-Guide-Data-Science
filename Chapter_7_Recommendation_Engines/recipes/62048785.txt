Title:
nutty pretzle salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/764201.jpg
Ingredients:
<<<<>>>>>>>>
1 reg size cool whip
1 package 8 oz of cream cheese
1 cup of sugar
<<<<<<>>>
2 cups of crushed pretzles
1 1/4 c margarine (melted)
3 tbsp sugar
<<<<>>>>
1 large package of jello your favorite kind!!

Directions:
prepare jello per box direction; chill until slightly thickened
Prepare crust. combine pretzles, Melted margarine, and sugar and bake at 350 degrees for 10 min and cool......
Next Combine cream cheese sugar, and cool whip and mix it all together...
Spread on top of prettzle crust once the crust has cooled....
Once the jeelo has slightly thickened pour on top of everything eles! Let it sit in the frig until jello sets! Cut and serve!!
