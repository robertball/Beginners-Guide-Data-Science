Title:
Mediterranian Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/552385.jpg
Ingredients:
-500 grams patatos
-500 gram sardine fillets
-200 grams cherry tomatos
-2 cloves garlic
-1 tblspn dried basil
-2 tblspns bread crumbs
-3 tblspns extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
-put patatos without peeling in cold water and boil
-once cooked cool and peel
-cut in thin slices (e.g. like when making scalloped  patatos).
-pour once tablspn of olive oil in casserole (quiche casserole)
-line casserole with sliced patatos, in on even layer
-layer evenly with sardine fillets
-dice cherry tomatos over top the layer of sardine fillets
-sprinkle with herbs and bread crumbs and drizzle with 2 tblspns olive oil
-chop garlic and sprinkle over top
-put in preheated oven at 180 degress celcius for 20 minutes and serve hot, makes 4 servings.
-In Italy it is usually served after an entre' but can also be eaten as a main dish with a tossed salad.
