Title:
Meatball sub casserole for 4 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Italian bread, cubed
4 ounces softened cream cheese
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 cup water
1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 1/2 cups jarred marinara sauce
1 (12 oz pkg) turkey or beef meatballs, Italian style, ready to eat ( no need to thaw first)

Directions:
Spread bread cubes in a 9x9 baking dish treated with nonstick cooking spray
Combine cream cheese, mayonnaise, Italian seasoning, pepper & water; pour over bread
Cover with 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese
Place meatballs on top of cheese  layer (I don't use the entire package but others do)
Pour the marinara sauce over the meatballs
Top with remaining cheese
Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees......  or
Instead of baking,  you can cover with aluminum foil and freeze then thaw later & bake

Submitter: 
Jane
Description: 
This easy to make casserole for 4 can be baked immediately or frozen to bake later.
