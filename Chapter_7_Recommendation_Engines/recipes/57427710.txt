Title:
Scalloped Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped celery
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon honey
2 teaspoons prepared mustard
2 cloves garlic
2 tbsp minced herbs
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 slices day old whole wheat bread,  cubed
4 cups chopped fresh tomatoes

Directions:
In a nonstick skillet, cook onion and celery in butter until tender. Stir in the flour until blended; cook 1 minute longer. Stir in the honey, mustard, salt and pepper until blended. Stir in bread cubes and tomatoes.
Transfer to an 8-in. square baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 35-40 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
laughingmagpie
Description: 
Warm and comforting, this old-fashioned side dish is a great way to use up garden bounty. From West Salem, Wisconsin, Norma Pipe says, 'I lightened the original recipe by using less butter and flour and only a bit of honey.'Modified from: Scalloped Tomatoes
