Title:
Sour Cream and Garlic Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbs butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbs all purpose flour
14 oz chicken broth, low sodium
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground white pepper
1/4 cup sour cream
1/3 cup chopped chives

Directions:
Heat the butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until it softens and begins to turns a light golden color.
Add the flour and stir until until well incorporated and the flour also becomes a light golden color.
Gradually stir in the chicken broth blending the mixture into a smooth sauce.
Bring to a light simmer and continue to stir until the sauce coats the back of a spoon.
Add salt and pepper and adjust to taste. Whisk in the sour cream and transfer to the serving dish.
Sprinkle the chives over the sauce and serve.
