Title:
Minnesota Poodle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4537330.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ice cubes
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger coffee flavored liqueur
1/2 cup root beer
1 teaspoon heavy cream

Directions:
Place the ice cubes in a highball glass. Pour the vodka, coffee liqueur, root beer, and cream over the ice cubes in that order.
