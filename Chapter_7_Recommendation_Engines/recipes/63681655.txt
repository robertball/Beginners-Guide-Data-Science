Title:
Hennepin Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 pds pot-roast
5 pds salt potatoes
5-6 large carrots
1 pd bag of pearl onions
1 bunch celery
25.4 oz Hennepin
½ cup dark molasses
½ cup packed brown sugar
4 cups beef stock

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F
Chop all but potatoes into large chunks.
Sear beef in oil in a pan that you can put into the oven. Add beer.
Add beef stock, molasses and brown sugar. Add veggies and potatoes.
Cover and place into oven for about 3 hours.
