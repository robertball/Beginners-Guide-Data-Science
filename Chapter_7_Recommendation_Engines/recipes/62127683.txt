Title:
Onion Dip Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard-boiled eggs
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1-2 tsp. spicy brown mustard
1-2 Tbsps. onion soup mix (or to taste)

Directions:
Cool and peel 6 hard-boiled eggs.  Cut in half lengthwise, and place yolks in a separate small bowl; mash with fork.
Add mayonnaise, mustard and onion soup mix.
Scoop yolk mixture into egg halves.  Sprinkle with Paprika if desired.  Refridgerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Kimberly
Description: 
Another creative use for dry onion soup mix!
