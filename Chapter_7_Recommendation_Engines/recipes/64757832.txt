Title:
low carb stuffed pepper skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
12 oz steamed riced cauliflower
1 green bell pepper diced
1 red bell pepper diced
2 cloves minced garlic
1 c tomatoes and green chilis
1 tbl tomato paste
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp paprika
pinch cayenne
1 c beef broth
1 cup shredded mozzarella
1/4 cup shredded pepper jack

Directions:
cook beef peppers and garlic together
steam cauliflower
add all ingredients except cheese
cook 10 min
melt pepper jack and stir in
cover with mozzarella melt and serve
