Title:
Shepherds pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749810.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lean ground beef
1 cup chopped onions
2 cloves minced garlic
1/4 cup flour
1/4 tsp.each of salt,pepper, dried thyme and savory
1-10 oz.can beef both,plus 1/2 can water
2 tsp.worcestershire sauce
1 bay leaf
1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
1/2 cup corn
Garlic potatos:
2 lbs.potatoes[5-6 med.]
6 cloves garlic,lightly crushed
3/4 cup buttermilk or 2% milk
1 egg,lightly beaten

Directions:
In large skillet over med. heat,cook ground beef untill no longer pink breaking it up as it cooks.
Add onions and garlic,cook untill soft.
Stir in flour and spices.Add broth and water,bay leaf,carrots and worcestershire sauce.
cover and simmer,stirring occasionally for 20 mins.or untill thickened and carrots are tender.
Stir in corn,then add salt to taste.Remove bayleaf.
Spread mixture in a greased 11x7 inch baking dish.
Garlic potates:
Cook potates in boiling salted water with garlic.
Drain and mash with milk,pepper and salt.Add egg but reserve 1 Tbsp.
Spread potates over ground beef mixture,brush with reserved egg.
Bake at 400F oven for 20 mins.or untill bubbly.
