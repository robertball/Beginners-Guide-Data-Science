Title:
Tuscan Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 TBS extra virgin olive oil
1 onion chopped
2 carrots sliced
1 leek,sliced
2 cloves garlic chopped
14 oz can chopped tomatoes
1 Tbs tomatoe puree
1.5 pints chicken stock
Salt & pepper
16 oz canned cannellini beans drained,rinsed
9oz spring greens shredded
8 Slices ciabatta bread
Fresh grated parm cheese for garnish.

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large saucepan and fry the onion,carrots and leek over a low heat, stirring for 10 min. or until soft. Add garlic and cook for 1 min more. Add tomatoes with juice,tomato puree and stock. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Mash half of the beans with a fork and add to the pan. Bring to a boil,then lower the heat and simmer for about 30min.
When ready to serve, toast the bread until golden.Place 2 slices in each bowl, ladle in soup and sprinkle with a little parmesan.
