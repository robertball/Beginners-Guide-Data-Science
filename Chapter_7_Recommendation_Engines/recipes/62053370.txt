Title:
RaRa's Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup flour,
1 cup sugar,
1/3 cup shortening, or butter,
1/2 cup peanut butter,
1 egg.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  In a mixer bowl, beat sugar, shortening, peanut butter, and egg for about 30 seconds. By hand gradually stir in flour until well mixed.  Roll into one inch balls.  Place on greased cookie sheet, and bake 10-15 minutes til golden brown.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
I made these for my son when he was growing up, and he loved them.  I never had brown sugar, so I always just used granulated sugar and they were wonderful.
