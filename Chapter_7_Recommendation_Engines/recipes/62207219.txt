Title:
Saucy Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
1 cup roughly chopped fresh tomatoes
juice from 2 lemons
juice from 2 limes
2 tablespoons chopped garlic
1/2 cup white wine
5 tablespoons sugar
************
1 cup fat free 1/2 & 1/2
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon white pepper
*********
1 T butter
1 lb shrimp, peeled & deviened
1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
8 kalamata olives
4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
Chopped parsley for garnish

Directions:
Place in a saucepan the first 7 ingredients.  Stir and cook until reduced by half.
Add in the next 3 ingredients.  Stir and let simmer until the mixture thickens.
Meanwhile, in another saucepan melt the butter and add the shrimp, olives and sun-dried tomatoes, saute just until the shrimp are pink and done.  Add the ingredients of the other saucepan  to the shrimp and stir together.  Plate on 4 plates and sprinkle with the crumbled feta cheese and chopped parsley.

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
This is one delicious meal served with angel hair pasta.  I had this at Bonefish Grill and came up with something pretty similar that's very yummy.
