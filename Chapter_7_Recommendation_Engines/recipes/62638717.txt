Title:
Asian Style Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Beef bouillon cubes
3 sections of egg noodles
1 small head of broccoli
2 cups of bean sprouts
1 Tbls. Shiracha sauce
1 Tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Add 3 bouillon cubes to medium pot of boiling water. Add noodles to the mix once it has dissolved, followed by the broccoli & sprouts. Then add spices. You can also add cooked steak to add depth but this was our take on good ramen.

Submitter: 
Jessica Warden
Description: 
We were in the mood for something simple and warm. So with what we had in the pantry and the fridge, came up with this.
