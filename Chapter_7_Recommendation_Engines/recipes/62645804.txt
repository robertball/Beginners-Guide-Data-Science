Title:
Hearty Miso Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c Seafood stock (or dashi stock)
2 c water
1 shallot diced
1 c shitake mushrooms chopped
2 medium carrots chopped
1 habanero pepper chopped
3 tbs yellow miso mixed with 1 tbs water
1 tbs wakame flakes
1 tbs oyster sauce
1 14.0 oz package silken or soft tofu, drained and cubed

Directions:
In a large pot bring seafood stock, oyster sauce, and 2 c water to a boil. Add wakame flakes and boil for 2 minutes. Add shallot, mushrooms, and carrots. Boil for 5 minutes. Add tofu and reduce heat to simmer for 5 minutes.
Remove from heat. Add in the miso / water mixture and stir. Top with chopped scallions for added flavor.

Submitter: 
Jim McNamara
Description: 
A twist on traditional miso soup. Since ingredients for making dashi stock aren’t readily available in my area I use seafood stock from the local grocer. Vegetable stock also works to make it vegetarian. Leave the habanero pepper out if you don’t like it hot.
