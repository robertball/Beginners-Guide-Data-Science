Title:
Chicken and Noodle Cordon Bleu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp butter
4 Tbsp flour
2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups swiss cheese, shredded
3 cups cooked chicken, chopped
6 - 8 slices deli ham, chopped
4 cups cooked noodles
salt and pepper
1 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
In medium saucepan, melt butter.
Add flour and stir until combined.
Cook for approximately 1 minute.
Slowly whisk in the milk, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens.
Stir in the shredded cheese and whisk until smooth.
In a casserole dish, mix the chopped chicken, chopped deli ham, and noodles.
Add the cheese sauce and mix.
Add seasonings to your taste.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 25 - 30 minutes.
Mix the melted butter with the bread crumbs and spread over the casserole.
Bake uncovered until bread crumbs are browned, about 10 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Carol Potts
Description: 
I came up with this recipe when I suddenly had to cook lunch for 50 people and found cooked chicken, deli ham, and swiss cheese in the refrigerator.
