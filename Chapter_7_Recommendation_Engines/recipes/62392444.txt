Title:
Danish Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. oil (may substitute applesauce for some oil)
2 c. sugar
2 eggs
2 c. mashed ripe bananas
3 c. flour
1 tsp. salt
2 tsp. soda mixed with 2 tbsp. hot water
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 c. nuts, optional

Directions:
Mix in order given.  Bake at 350 deg in 2 loaf pans for 45 min.

Submitter: 
Melissa Burnett Mandrona
Description: 
Delicious simple banana bread that my kids love.  Add chocolate chips for a nice option.
