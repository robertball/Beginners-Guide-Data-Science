Title:
POLENTA, Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 and 1/4 cup water( or you could use chicken broth)
2cups yelow cornmeal
couple pinches salt

Directions:
Bring water or broth to a boil,add salt.When water is boiling add the cornmeal,stirring continuously with a long wooden spoon.Turn the heat to mediun. Cook the polenta stirring frequently,for about 15 minutes,until it comes away from the sides of your pot.Rinse a deep dish in cold water and pour the polenta into it,and leave it to cool.Cut in slices,spoon hot spaghetti sauce over the Polenta.

Submitter: 
manella
Description: 
This is a very easy dish to make. Makes a great side dish for any type of meat, but also good on it's own.
