Title:
Cajun Kale and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1436849.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups chicken broth
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon garlic pepper blend
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 cup shredded kale
1/2 cup uncooked rice

Directions:
Stir chicken broth, salt, oregano, paprika, garlic pepper blend, and cayenne pepper together in a pot over medium heat; bring to a boil
Stir kale and rice into the boiling broth mixture; return the broth to a boil, place a cover on the pot, reduce heat to low, and continue cooking at a simmer until the rice is cooked through and tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
