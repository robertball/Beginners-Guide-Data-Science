Title:
Apple Smiles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1119707.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium red apple, cored and sliced
3-4 tablespoons peanut butter
32 miniature marshmallows

Directions:
Wash, core and cut apple in half. Cut each half into 8 slices.
Spread peanut butter on one side of each apple slice.
Place 3 or 4 mini-marshmallows on top of peanut butter on one apple
slice. Top with another and press together to make one "smile."
Continue assembling remaining apple slices.
