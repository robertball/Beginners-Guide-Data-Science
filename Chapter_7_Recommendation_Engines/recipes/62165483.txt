Title:
Cucumber Crab Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Medium Cucumbers
1/2 lb jumbo lump crabmeat
1/2 cup mayo
1 tsp Oldbay seasoning
1 clove garlic - finely chopped
2 TB Horseradish
1 tsp finely chopped celery
2 TB  green pepper- finely chopped
salt and pepper to taste
**** For an extra kick replace green pepper with jalapeno

Directions:
1.Slice cucumbers (peel if you prefer) in 1/2" - 3/4" slices
2.In a medium bowl mix the rest of the ingredients together.
3.Top each cucmber slice with crab mixture.
4.Enjoy!

Submitter: 
CNM CATERING
Description: 
Easy appetizer! The crab and horseradish mixture gives the cucumber slices a nice flavor. Great with any summer time BBQ!
