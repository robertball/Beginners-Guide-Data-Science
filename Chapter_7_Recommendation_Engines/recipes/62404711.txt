Title:
Bonnie's Cajun Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1/2 medium onion
2/3 cup white vinegar
1 tsp. salt
2-3 cloves garlic
2 tsp. Dijon mustard
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup vegetable oil
2 Tbsp. poppy seeds
Salad:
4 chicken breasts, seasoned and grilled
2 Tbsp. Lusanne cajun seasoning
1 box (16 oz.) penne cooked
1 small can (11 oz.) mandarin oranges, drained
1 small can (8 oz.) cashews
2 -3 cups snow peas (uncooked)

Directions:
1. In blender combine all dressing ingredients except oil and poppy seeds.  Then slowly add oil with blender running until the dressing thickens.  Stir in poppy seeds.
Sprinkle chicken generously with the cajun seasoning and grill.  Cut into pieces about 3/4" x 3/4".  Cool in refrigerator.
Cook pasta al dente, rinse and drain.  Pour some poppy seed dressing over it and chill at least an hour.
At serving time,  toss all ingredients together with more dressing in a large bowl.

Submitter: 
food-dancer
Description: 
A refreshing mix of pasta,  cajun grilled chicken,  mandarin oranges, snow peas and cashews with a sweet-sour poppyseed dressing.  A perfect summer meal.
