Title:
Easy Herb Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985812.jpg
Ingredients:
8 eggs
2 tablespoons water
4 ounces OSCAR MAYER Shaved Smoked Ham, chopped
1 cup KRAFT Shredded Mozzarella Cheese, divided
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Beat eggs and water with wire whisk in medium bowl. Stir in ham, 1/2 cup of the cheese and 2 tablespoons of the cilantro.
Pour into greased 9-inch pie plate; sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Bake 30 minutes or until puffed and golden brown. Sprinkle with remaining cilantro.

Submitter: 
Kraft
Description: 
This frittata is a great way to use up leftover eggs, meat, and cheese in your refrigerator.
