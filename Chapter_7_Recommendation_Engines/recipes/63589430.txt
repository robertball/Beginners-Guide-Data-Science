Title:
Ginger Garlic Chicken & Rice Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, 3 cups Minute Rice (white or brown), 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 white onion, 1 can of chicken broth, 2 cups baby carrots, 3 tsp brown sugar, 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp ground ginger, pinch of salt, black pepper, pinch of cayenne pepper, 1/2 tsp savory, 1/2 tsp basil, 1 tbsp butter or magarine

Directions:
Chop the onion
Add olive oil to pan and heat over medium heat
Add chopped onion to pan and season with 1 tsp garlic. Season with salt, pepper and cayenne pepper to taste.
Reduce heat to medium low and cook approx. 10 minutes or until onions are tender, stirring occasionally to prevent burning.
Add chicken breasts to pan, seasoning each with salt and pepper to taste before adding ginger, savory, basil and remaining garlic powder to the entire mixture.
Cook mixture over medium heat to brown each side of chicken.
Once browned, add chicken broth and carrots to mixture and cook over medium high heat for 20-30 minutes or until carrots are tender.
Remove chicken from mixture and set aside.
Add Minute Rice to chicken broth & carrot mixture and cook as directed on box.

Description: 
Ginger meets garlic in this super easy, one pan chicken dinner. No need for sides, no messy kitchen!!
