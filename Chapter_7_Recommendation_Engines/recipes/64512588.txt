Title:
Herbed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole chicken breasts, halved, with skin removed
10 3/4 oz. can cream of mushroom or chicken soup
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup wine vinegar
3/4 cup water
1/2 tsp. minced garlic
1 tsp. ground ginger
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
1 tbsp. brown sugar

Directions:
Arrange chicken in slow cooker.
Combine the remaining ingredients in a bowl.  Pour over chicken.
Cover. Cook on low 6-8 hours, or until tender but not dry.
Uncover and cook 15 minutes more.
