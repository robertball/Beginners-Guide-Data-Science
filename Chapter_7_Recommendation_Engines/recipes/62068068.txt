Title:
slow cooked onion pot roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4lb beef chuck roast
salt
pepper
garlic salt
2tb veg oil
1 1/2tsp crushed garlic
2 dried bay leaves
1 lg yellow onion
1 can cream mushroom soup
1 package dried onion soup mix
flour and water to thicken

Directions:
Sprinkle roast on both sides with salt, pepper, garlic powder. Heat oil in pan and sear all sides of roast. Put roast in slow cooker and rub on crushed garlic and onion soup Add mushroom soup, bay leaves and onion chunks. Cover with water and cook on high for 6 hours.  Thicken gravy when meat is done and serve over mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
crazymama76
Description: 
Easy pot roast recipe.  I was tired of plain pot roast and mixed it up for a family dinner and it was a hit.
