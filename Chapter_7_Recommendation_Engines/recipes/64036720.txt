Title:
Apple Stuffed Porkloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ea Porkloin
9 cups Apples-Chopped
9 slices Bread diced
1/8 cup Poultry Seasoning
1/8 cup Season Mix
2 ea Eggs
1 cup Chicken Stock

Directions:
1. With Chef's Knife, cut porkloin 1/2 inch thick, start 1/2 inch from cutting board and roll loin out as cutting until its fully rolled out.
2. Cover loin with plastic wrap and pound out with meat hammer until all loin is 1/4 inch thick, with out splitting it.
3. Combine apples, bread, poultry seasoning, and season mix, eggs, and chicken stock.
4. Put stuffing on pork evenly and roll like a Jelly Roll with fat cap up.  Cut in half, put on rack and season outside of meat in a 4 inch pan.
Ready to Cook!!!!
