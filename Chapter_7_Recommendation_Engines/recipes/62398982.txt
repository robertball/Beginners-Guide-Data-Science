Title:
Double Baked Potato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lg baking potatoes
1 bunch chopped green onions using tops
1/2 lb lean bacon, fried well done and crumbled
1/2 lb grated cheddar cheese
1 stick margarine
1 16 oz sour cream
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bake potatoes at 400 for about an hour or until done. Whole still hot cut in half and remove pulp. In a large bowl combine all ingredients. Place heaping amount in each jalf potatoe skin. These can be made in teh morning ot even several days beforeand refrigerated Before serving heat about 1/2 hour ar 350 or until hot.
