Title:
Easy Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) bag frozen whole kernel corn
2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth (or 4 cups boxed broth)
1 (10 ounce) can chunk chicken (or 2 cups of shredded rotisserie chicken)
1 (15 ounce) can black beans
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (4 ounce) chopped green chile peppers
1 Tbs lemon or lime juice
1/2 cup chopped onions
2-4 garlic cloves chopped
2 tsps hot sauce
2-3 tsp taco seasoning mix

Directions:
Open the cans of chicken broth, chunk chicken, black beans, diced tomatoes, and green green chilies. Add corn, onions, garlic, lemon, hot suce and seasoning into a large saucepan or stock pot. Simmer over medium heat until chicken is heated through. Top with tortilla chips and shredded cheese.
