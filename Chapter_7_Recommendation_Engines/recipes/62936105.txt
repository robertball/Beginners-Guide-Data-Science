Title:
Southwestern Black bean and Tofu stir fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce
2/3 cup orange juice
1/3 cup ketchup
1 tbs brown sugar
1 chipotle in adobe sauce (a piece ~1 1/4" x 1 1/4")
1 tbs lime juice
1/2 tsp salt
Main Ingredients
1 large onion
1 large red pepper (or 2 small)
9 ounces package of Portobello mushrooms, cut into chunks
3 large cloves of garlic
14 oz extra firm tofu, drained and cubed
1 can of Kuhners jalapeno black beans with lime

Directions:
Sauce instructions
Add ingredients to a blender and run until pepper is fully chopped
Other Instructions
Roast and blacken the pepper either in the oven under the broiler or on a grill.  Place in a paper bag until cool.  Peel blacked skin off.  Cut pepper in half.  Discard seeds.  Coarsely chop and set aside.
Coarsely chop the onion.
Mince the 3 cloves of garlic.
Add onion to a pot with 3 tbs olive oil, and cook the onions until they start turning brown and caramelizing
Add the garlic and continue cooking for ~2 minutes.
Add the roasted red pepper and Portobello mushrooms and cook for ~2 to 3 minutes more.
Remove from heat and pour into a bowl
Return pot to the burner.  Add 1 to 2 tbs olive oil.  Brown the tofu.
After the tofu is browned, add the onion mixture, sauce, and the beans.  Bring to a simmer.
Serve over rice...
