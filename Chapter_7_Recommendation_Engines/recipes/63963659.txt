Title:
Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
crust
2 c almond flour
¼ c butter/coconut oil (melted)
¼ c honey
Lemon juice/zest
Salt
filling
2 eggs
¼ c honey/agave plus  more
1 T sugar
¼ c Greek yogurt
1 c whole lemon puree/juice

Directions:
preheat to 350
put all crust ingredients in bowl and mix well
bake 350 approx 15 min until golden
meanwhile, mix all filling ingredients
chop lemons and remove seeds
put in blender to create puree
add other filling ingredients
immediately put filling into crust and return to oven at 350 for approx 20 min

two layer whole lemon bars
