Title:
WW Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts
3 TBS Hellman's light mayonnaise
1 TBS mustard
1TBS dill pickle relish

Directions:
1.  Boil the chicken until cooked through.
2.  After the chicken is cooked, use a food processor to shred the chicken
3.  Add remaining ingredients
4.  Refrigerate the chicken salad for two hours then serve.

Submitter: 
wkngmomof2
Description: 
This is a very simple chicken salad with minimal ingredients and lots of taste!  This is 1 point on My WW Purple approach.
