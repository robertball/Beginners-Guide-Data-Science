Title:
Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c dry bread crumbs or crushed saltines
1 c milk
1.5 lbs ground beef
2 beaten eggs
1/4 c minced onion
1 t salt
1/8 t pepper
1/2 t sage
Sauce:
3 T brown sugar
1/4 c ketchup
1/4 t nutmeg
1 t dry mustard

Directions:
Pour milk over crumbs.
Add meat, eggs, onion, and seasonings. Mix well.
Place into loaf pan and shape.
Mix sauce ingredients together and spread over loaf.
Bake at 350F for 45-60 minutes
