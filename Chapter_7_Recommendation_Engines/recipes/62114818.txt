Title:
Tomato Rarebit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp flour
3/4 C. milk
3/4 C. canned tomatoes
1/8 tsp. baking soda
1 C. shredded Cheddar cheese
2 eggs, beaten slightly
1/4 tsp. yellow mustard or dry mustard
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
In a heavy saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter, add the flour and cook stirring constantly.  (like a roux or white sauce)
Add the milk  and cook until thick, stirring continuously.
Mix the baking soda with the canned tomatoes, then add to the sauce.
Add the mustard to the eggs and mix into the tomato sauce.
Place the saucepan over boiling water (double boiler)
Add the cheese, salt and pepper and continue to cook until the cheese melts.
Serve over toast points.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Great for a hot lunch during those cold winter days. Just add ham or sausage and or a salad to complete a evening dinner.
