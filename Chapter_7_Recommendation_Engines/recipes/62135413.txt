Title:
Grits and Ham Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3cups milk
3/4cups uncooked quick-cooking grits
1 1/2cups chopped cooked ham
1cup shredded cheddar cheese
3 eggs, lightly beaten
2 green onions, chopped
3tbl chopped fresh parsley
1/2tsp salt
1/2tsp garlic powder
1/4tsp pepper

Directions:
Bring milk to a boil over med/high heat, stir in grits. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer stirring often for 5 mins or until thickened.
Stir in ham and remaining ingredients.
Spoon into and lightly greased shallow 2qt baking dish.
Bake at 375 for 25 minutes or until set

Submitter: 
Cara
Description: 
Delicious for breakfast with fresh fruit and juice.
