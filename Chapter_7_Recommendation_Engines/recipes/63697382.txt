Title:
Cheesecake Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whipping cream
2 8 oz. packages cream cheese, softened
2/3 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla OR 1 teaspoon almond extract

Directions:
Whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
Combine cream cheese and sugar, beating well.
Add vanilla OR almond extract and beat thoroughly.
Fold in the whipped cream.
Chill until ready to eat.
Put a dollop on fruit cobbler, use as spread on fruit pizza crust (baked cookie dough) or as cheesecake in a spring form pan on your choice of crust.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
This is a great, no-bake, simple filling to use for cheesecake, fruit topping, fruit pizza spread or to eat by the spoonful!
