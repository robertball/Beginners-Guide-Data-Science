Title:
Sandy's Poppy Seed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
2 1/4 cups sugar
3 eggs
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 tsp. almond extract
1 1/2 tsp. butter flavoring
1 cup milk
1 1/4 cup oil
2 Tbsp. poppy seeds
Glaze:
1/2 tsp. butter flavoring
1/2 tsp. almond extract
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/4 cup  orange juice
3/4 cup  sugar
Bring above ingredients to a boil, and pour over hot bread as directed below.

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients together and make a well in the middle of them. Mix liquid ingredients and eggs together, then pour into the well.  Add poppy seeds and beat until blended. Pour into 2 greased bread pans.  Bake at 350 for 45-60 minutes, depending on your oven.  Top will be cracked and slightly moist. When done pour hot glaze syrup on top, and let bread cool in the pan.

Submitter: 
phonicsteacher
Description: 
An interesting twist on a quick, tasty bread.  The glaze makes all the difference!  Great for brunch or with a cup of coffee or tea.
