Title:
Deviled eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 peeled hard boiled eggs
2 TBS mayonnaise
2 tsp mustard
1/2 tsp smoked paprika

Directions:
cut each egg in half from tip to bottom
scoop egg yolks out and place in bowl set aside
place egg whites on a medium sized platter
crush egg yolks with a fork until fine
add mayonnaise and mustard
stir until even
place into a sandwich
cut a finger sized whole in the bottom
squeeze into egg whites
sprinkle paprika onto each

Submitter: 
eve
Description: 
Yummy these deviled are so good when your done eating them you will be begging for more. They make great appetizers for guests or for a party. Don't worry about time they don't even take much time out of your day
