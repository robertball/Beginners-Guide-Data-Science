Title:
Easiest Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 small onion, chopped
1 15 oz can tomato sauce
2 chicken bouillon cubes
1 small bay leaf
1/4 teaspoon dried marjoram
1/4 teaspoon sugar
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1/8 teaspoon dried thyme
6 chicken pieces, skin removed.

Directions:
In large skillet, saute chopped onion in butter until onion is clear.
Add tomato sauce, chicken cubes, and remaining seasonings.  Heat and stir until blended.  Add chicken pieces.  Cover and simmer 30-40 minutes stirring occasionally or until chicken is done.
Serve over spaghetti or pasta of choice.

Submitter: 
GINGERPET
Description: 
This recipe has been a favorite for over 30 years.  The particular blend of seasonings makes it anything but ordinary. Serve with a fresh garden salad and crusty bread.
