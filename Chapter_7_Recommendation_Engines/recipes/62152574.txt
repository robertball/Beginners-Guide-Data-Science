Title:
Tamale Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4   Cup Polenta
3     Cups water
1     Tsp Salt
1     tbsp oil
1     lb lean ground beef
2     small cloves chopped garlic
1/2   cup finely chopped onion
1/2   chopped ripe olives
2 1/2 cups crushed canned tomatoes
1 1/2 Chili Powder
1/2   Oregano
1 3/4 Salt
1/8   Tsp pepper
1/2   Tsp Ground Cumin
1/2   Cup seedless raisins
1/2   Cup grated cheddar or american cheese.

Directions:
Sift Polenta into boiling water in top of double boiler.
Cook over direct heat stirring constantly until thickend.
Place over top of boiling water and cook for 20 to 30 Min.Stirring frequently.
Allow Polenta to cool slightly.
Turn into greased 8 cup baking dish
Pat evenly around sides and bottom of dish.
Refrigerate until ready to use.
Heat oil in skillet and cook beef until gray.
Drain off any excess fat.
Add rest of ingredients except cheese.
Cooke about 15 min until liquid is partially evaporated.
Turn into prepared baking dish and sprinkle with cheese.
Bake @ 375 for 45 to 50 min.
