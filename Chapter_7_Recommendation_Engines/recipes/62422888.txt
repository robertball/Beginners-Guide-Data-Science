Title:
Easy Turkey Croquettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. leftover mashed potatoes
1/2 c. finely chopped celery
1/8 c. finely chopped onion
1-1/2 c. chopped cooked turkey
salt & pepper to taste
1 egg
3 T. breadcrumbs
breadcrumbs for coating
beaten egg for coating
oil for frying

Directions:
Combine mashed potatoes, celery, onion, turkey, 1 egg, salt and pepper and 3 T. breadcrumbs.
Make "logs" approximately 1 x 3 inches. Dip in beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
Chill in refrigerator for 1/2 hour. Fry in 1/4 inch oil until deep golden brown, turning occasionally.
Freeze the fried-up leftovers. Heat in toaster oven or oven for a quick dinner.

Submitter: 
Anne Norman Hastings
Description: 
What to do with that leftover turkey?  Make Croquettes!  Or fry-up and freeze for those nights you need a quick dinner.
