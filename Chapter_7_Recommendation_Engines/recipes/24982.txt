Title:
Party Cake Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 cup cold milk
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine pudding mix and milk. Beat until smooth. Blend in cream cheese. Fold in the whipped topping, then fold in the crushed pineapple.

Submitter: 
SEBRING SOCKS
Description: 
This is a topping for a Pineapple Party cake and I believe could be used for a filling also.
