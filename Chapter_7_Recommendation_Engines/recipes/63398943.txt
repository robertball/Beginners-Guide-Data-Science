Title:
Classic French Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/8 c water
1 1/4 t salt
1 T sugar
3 3/4 c flour
1 1/2 t yeast

Directions:
Mix.  Knead 6 minutes.  Let rise to double.  Punch down.  Divide in half.  Roll each half out then role up as a jellyroll.  Place on a greased baking sheet and let rise.  With a sharp knife, make 3-4 diagonal cuts about 1/4 inch deep across the loaf.  Bake 20 minutes at 350'.
