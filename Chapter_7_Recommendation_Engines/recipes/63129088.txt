Title:
Sunnyside Up Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798136.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Eggs
Butter or non-stick spray
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Heat griddle or frying pan on medium heat. Place pat of butter and let melt. Crack fresh egg onto griddle and let cook slowly until white is cooked thoroughly. Immediately remove from heat and serve.

Submitter: 
AuntE
Description: 
A fried egg that isn't flipped. A beautiful presentation and works well for dipping your toast in.
