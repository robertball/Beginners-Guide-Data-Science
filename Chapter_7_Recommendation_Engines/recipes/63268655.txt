Title:
Cheesy Vegetable Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 20oz bag mixed vegetables
2 T. margarine
2 T. flour
1 c. milk
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. dry mustard
1/8 t. paprika
1/4 t. pepper
1 - 8oz pkg colby jack shredded cheese

Directions:
Cook frozen vegetables; drain liquid. Turn into 1 1/2 qt. casserole dish.
In saucepan, melt margarine over medium heat; add flour to make a roux.
Gradually add milk, stirring constantly, until thickened (About 5 mins). Add spices and 1 c. of cheese; stir until cheese is melted.
Spoon sauce over vegetables and toss to coat.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes; sprinkle with remaining cheese and cooke another 5 minutes. Serve.
