Title:
Cooler Corn! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/765263.jpg
Ingredients:
1 dozen shucked ears of corn

Directions:
Wipe clean a Coleman cooler
Fill with the shucked ears.
Next, pour two kettles-full of boiling water over the corn and close the lid.
Wait at least 30 minutes to open the lid.  The corn will be perfectly cooked!
The corn will remain at the perfect level of doneness for a couple of hours.
