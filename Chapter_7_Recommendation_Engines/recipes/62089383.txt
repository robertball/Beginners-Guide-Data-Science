Title:
Toasted Pecan Clusters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBLS Real Butter
3 Cups Pecan Pieces
12 oz. Chocolate candy coating bark

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
Melt butter in a 15x10 jelly roll pan.
Spread pecans evenly in pan with butter.
Bake for 30 minutes stirring every 10 minutes.
Melt candy coating in a heavy saucepan over low heat.
Remove from heat and cool for 2 minutes.
Stir in pecans.
Drop by rounded teaspoonful onto wax paper.
Cool.
