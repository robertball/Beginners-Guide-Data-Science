Title:
Super Summer Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) cans sweet white corn
1 (14.5 ounce) can black beans
1/2 red onion, chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
1/4 cup sugar (add more to taste, up to an additional 1/4 cup)
1/4 cup rice wine vinegar (add more to taste, up to an additional 1/4 cup)
Lime or lemon juice, to taste
1 can (15 ozs.) petite diced tomatoes, drained, or one tomato, chopped finely
salt to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together corn, beans, onion, red pepper, and sugar. Stir in rice wine vinegar, and season with salt.

Submitter: 
biz_kid1
Description: 
This is a different, sweet salsa that is perfect for summer and receives rave reviews! It stars sweet white corn and black beans, with red onion, red pepper, sugar, and the crisp tang of rice wine vinegar. Serve with chips.Modified from: Super Summer Salsa
