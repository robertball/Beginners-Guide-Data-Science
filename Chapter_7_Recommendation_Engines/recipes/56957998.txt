Title:
Waldorf Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken 1 lb, seasoned with lemon pepper
apple 1
red grapes 1 cup
almonds 1/4 cup
Orange Crasins .25 cup
.25 cup miracle whip
A splash of lemon or cider vinagre
Garlic salt to taste

Directions:
Season the chicken with olive oil and lemon pepper; grill or broil it.
Chop the apples and slice the grapes
Mix miracle whip, acid and garlic salt
After the chicken cools, chop and blend with the remaining ingredients
