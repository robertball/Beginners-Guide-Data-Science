Title:
Ellen's Spicy Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can diced tomatoes with jalapenos
1 11oz can Mexicorn (drained)
1 4oz can diced jalapenos
1 15oz can black beans (drained)
1 tsp Cumin
Fresh lime juice (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Nurse Ellen so not a
Description: 
Super delicious spicy salsa you can make in about 5 minutes. Great for a quick snack or for topping your favorite Mexican dish.
