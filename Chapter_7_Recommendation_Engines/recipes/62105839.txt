Title:
Strawberry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box white cake mix
1/2 cup oil
4 eggs
1/2 cup frozen strawberries thawed
1/2 cup milk
1 box strawberry jello (small)
Iceing:
1 box powder sugar
1 stick butter
1/2 cup strawberries thawed

Directions:
Mix cake mix and jello
Add oil and milk
Add eggs one at a time
Add 1/2 cup thawed strawberries
Pour batter in a 9x13 pan that has been greased and floured
Bake according to package directions for a 9x13
When cool, mix box of powder sugar, stick of butter and 1/2 cup thawed strawberries
Spread over top of cake

Submitter: 
kehn
Description: 
This recipe has been in our family since 1957 and has got to be the world's best strawberry cake!
