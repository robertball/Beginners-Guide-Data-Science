Title:
Rhubarb Lover's Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups of rhubarb, trimmed and washed of hard strings and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1/3 to 1/2 cup white sugar (or sugar substitute)
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1 egg
1/4 cup melted butter
2/3 cup milk or half and half
1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
1 tsp sugar (or sugar substitute)
1 tbsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Place rhubarb in saucepan, add water until just covered, bring water to boil, reduce to medium heat, cover and stew until tender,10 minutes most likely.  Remove from heat and stir in 1/3 cup of white sugar.  Cool until room temperature is met or put in fridge overnight.
Sift dry ingredients together.
Beat egg in separate dish and blend in wet ingredients.
Slowly blend wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix thoroughly.  Add 1 cup of cooled rhubarb mixture.
Grease griddle, fry pan or cooking vessel of choice and add 1/3 cup of batter and flip pancakes when indicated.
Serve with remaining rhubarb sauce liberally poured over pancakes and whipped cream.
