Title:
Restaurant Style Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28oz can whole tomatoes with juice
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp cumin
1/4 bunch cilantro
1 lime
1/4 cup onion (optional)
Chopped jalapenos (optional add to taste)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a food processor and pulse until done.

Submitter:

Description: 
This salsa is just like what you would expect to find at your favorite Mexican restaurant.
