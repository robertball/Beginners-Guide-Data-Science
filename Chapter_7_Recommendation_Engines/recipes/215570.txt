Title:
Dan's W.P.B. Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F657443.jpg
Ingredients:
¼ teaspoon white sugar
¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 tablespoons peanut butter
2 slices white bread
1 slice watermelon

Directions:
Stir the sugar and cinnamon together in a small bowl; set aside.
Spread 1 tablespoon peanut butter onto each slice of bread. Sprinkle the peanut butter with the sugar mixture. Sandwich the watermelon slice between the bread.

Submitter: 
epiCinAcan
Description: 
I wanted something new for lunch, and we didn't have much fruit, so this is what I came up with. A mix of watermelon, peanut butter, and a couple other ingredients. Please trust me, it's not gross.
