Title:
Bracciole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs Flank Steak..or thinly sliced cuts-pound meat with a meat tenderizer until thin
1 C chopped fresh parsley
4 cloves garlic minced
1/2 C parmesean cheese
1 chopped hard boiled egg
1/2 med onion chopped
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
4 T Olive oil- divided
pinch sugar
chopped tomato
1 8 oz can tomato sauce
need toothpicks!

Directions:
Saute chopped onion until tender in 2T olive oil  season with salt and pepper .
Filling: Combine chopped parsly,garlic, cheese, crumbled egg and dash salt and pepper drizzle with oil until it reaches a sticky consistency.
spread filling onto steak in a thin layer. Roll meat "like a jelly roll" fasten ends with toothpicks to contain filling.
Saute rolls in oil with the onion keep turning until browned on all sided.  Add tomato sauce and  pinch of sugar, chopped toamto-simmer on very low heat for 1 & 1/2 hours. Serve with Egg Noodles..or as addition to spaghetti or ravioli.
