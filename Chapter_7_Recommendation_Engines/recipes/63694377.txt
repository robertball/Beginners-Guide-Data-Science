Title:
Watermelon Juice without Watermelon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1027037.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Oranges
1 Zucchini
1 Apple
1 Pear
1 Cucumber

Directions:
Peel oranges, wash zucchini, apple, pear and cucumber then juice with juicer. This blend came out tasting like watermelon, we were amazed. Makes around 24 to 32 ounces of juice. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Tanya Martin
Description: 
Juice that taste like watermelon but you do not use any watermelon. Make juicing fun by playing with your food and making faces.
