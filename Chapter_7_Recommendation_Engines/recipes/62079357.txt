Title:
Moroccan spicy carrot salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Ib young carrots
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
1 pint water
1 tbsp wine venegar
half tsp sweet paprika
large pinch ground dried chili
half tsp powdered cumin
salt freshly ground black peper
fresh cilantro leaves

Directions:
Peel the carrots and cut into thin rounds.
Place them in shallow pan together with the water, garlic,oil,paprika, chilli and a little salt and freshly ground black black pepper.
Bring to the boil and cook over a high to medium heat uncovered for a about 20 minutes or until the carrots are tender and all the water evaporated.
Stir in the cumin and the vinegar.
Remove from heat and allow to cool to room temperature. Granish with the fresh cilantro leaves and a little more paprika for color.
