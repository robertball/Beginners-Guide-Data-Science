Title:
Red Lobster Cheddar Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. biscuit mix such as Bisquick
1/2 c shredded mild cheddar cheese
2/3 c milk
4 T butter
1/4 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 450
2.  Stir together biscuit mix, cheddar and milk until a soft dough forms.  Do not over mix
3.  Spoon onto a greased cookie sheet.  Smooth down tops so you won't have hard baked points on biscuit.
4.  Bake for 8 to 10 min.
5.  While baking, melt butter then add garlic powder
6. Once the biscuits are done, brush the butter on top.
Serve Warm.
