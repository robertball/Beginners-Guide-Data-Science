Title:
Lentil Soup With Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2257351.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups chicken or Vegetable Broth (previously made or boxed)
3 carrots, chopped
3 celery stalks, chopped
1 onion, diced
3 cloves garlic,minced
1 can of Lentils, rinsed
1 head cauliflower, cut into small florets
1 Tablespoon Marjoram
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Heat olive oil in pan, and cook onion, carrots and celery till soft , about 5 mins
Add Cauliflower, Garlic, Marjoram, Salt, Pepper .continue to cook another 5 mins.
Add Lentils, Broth, and Bay leaf.
Cover and Cook on a low simmer for 20 mins. Or till Cauliflower is soft, and flavors are blended.
Enjoy!

A simple soup with TONS of flavor, ready in under an hour. A go to on a rainy day.
