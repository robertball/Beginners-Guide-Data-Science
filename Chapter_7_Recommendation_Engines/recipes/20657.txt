Title:
Carla's Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/673507.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the frozen whipped topping, marshmallow creme, and cream cheese. Mix until smooth. Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Clara Simons
Description: 
A thick, creamy fruit dip.
