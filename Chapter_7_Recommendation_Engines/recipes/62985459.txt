Title:
Mini Meatball Minestra Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp. olive oil
1/4 lb. pancetta - diced
1 medium-large onion - diced
3 cloves garlic - minced
2 head escarole - chopped
1 15 oz. can cannellini beans - drained
2 quarts chicken stock
3 cups water
1 lb. ground beef, pork and veal mix
1 large egg
2 handfuls bread crumbs
Splash of milk
Handful of grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
1 tsp. fennel seeds
Handful of fresh parsley - chopped
1 cup uncooked ditalini pasta
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Put olive oil in Dutch oven and add pancetta.  Cook until crisp then add onions and 2 cloves of garlic and saute 4-5 minutes then add escarole and wilt.
Season with salt and pepper.
Stir in beans, then add stock and water and bring to a boil.
Combine meat, egg, bread crumbs, cheese, fennel seed, parsley and salt and pepper.
Mix and roll in to 1 inch meatballs and add to soup.
Cook 2 minutes then add pasta and cook 6 minutes more.
Serve garnished with Parmigiano-Reggiano.
