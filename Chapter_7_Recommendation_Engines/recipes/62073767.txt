Title:
Awesome Beef Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet Asian style dry salad dressing mix
1/4 cup oil
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 Tbsp. honey

Directions:
Whisk all marinade ingredients together.
Place meat into a ziplock bag and pour in the marinade.  Squeeze out excess air and seal.
Marinate in refrigerator for at least 4 hours, turning bag occasionally.
Grill meat to desired doneness and discard the marinade.

Submitter: 
Valerie Kasper
Description: 
This is absolutely the best and simplest beef marinade I have ever used.  Everyone raves over it!
