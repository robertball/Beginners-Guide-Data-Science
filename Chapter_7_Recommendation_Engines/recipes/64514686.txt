Title:
Creamy Cajun Chicken Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2795194.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Boxes Kraft Macaroni and Cheese
4 Tyson's Grilled Chicken Breasts
1 Green Pepper
1 Shallot - Medium
1 stick of Butter
1 tbsp. Garlic - Minced.
1 cup Heavy Cream
1 can Rotel Diced Tomatoes with Green Chilies
2 tsp Cajun Seasoning
1/2 tsp Black Pepper

Directions:
Prepare Pasta per directions and set aside.
Combine both packets of Kraft Cheese powder with Heavy Cream and Cajun Seasoning.  Mix well.  Set aside.
Dice Green Pepper - Set aside
Mince Shallot - Set aside
Drain Tomatoes - Set aside
Defrost and Dice chicken breasts
Melt the stick of butter in a large skillet.
Add Shallots, Garlic and Chicken, Black Pepper. Let simmer for a few minutes.
Add tomatoes and combine
Add Pasta and Cheese - Combine
Serve with Garlic Bread

Submitter: 
Dave Niezabitowski Sr.
Description: 
A creamy chicken and pasta dish in a cajun cheese sauce.  Created out of necessity, I have fine tuned this over time.
