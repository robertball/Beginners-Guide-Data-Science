Title:
Big Ray's Unstuffed Cabbage Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/995834.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 medium onion
1 1/2 pounds green cabbage (about 1/2 medium sized head)
1 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
1 Tbl brown sugar
1 Tbl distilled white vineger
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Chop onion and cook with beef in a 4-to-5 qt. Dutch oven over medium heat, breaking up chunks, until beef is lighlty brown. Drain off fat.  Core cabbage and cut into 1 inch strips.  Add cabbage to beef mixture and cover, cooking for 5 to 7 minuted, stirring occasionally, until cabbage wilts.  Stir in remaining ingrediatns and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and cover, simmering for 8 to 10 minutes until cabbage is tender.  Serve with rice.
