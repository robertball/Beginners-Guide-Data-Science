Title:
Figs and Tosted Almonds Brie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One  wheel of brie
5-6 fresh figs(or canned in syrup)
½ cup brown sugar (reduce to ¼ if using canned figs)
2 tablespoon water (if using canned figs use some of the syrup)
½ cup tosted almonds
½ teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
1. In a small saucepan, heat the sugar and water until it disolves.
2. Cut the figs into quarters and add the to the sugar sauce.
3. Add the tosted almonds and the vanilla.
4. Pour mix over brie and bake at 325, for 10-15 min or until is soft.
5. Serve with water crakers.

Submitter: 
Casandra Yaz
Description: 
This has a been a hit everytime I have served it.  Is a great appetizer for the holidays or when figs are in season.
