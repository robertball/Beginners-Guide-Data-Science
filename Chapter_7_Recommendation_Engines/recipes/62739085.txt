Title:
Confetti Salad From BJ's Journal/Summer 2011 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup red onion, chopped
1 cup asparagus, 1" pieces
1 cup cooked fresh corn kernels
1 cup zucchini, 1/2" dice
1/2 cup chayote squash, 1/2" dice
1 cup green beans, 1" pieces
1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
1/2 cup red wine vinaigrette
1 to 2 jalapenos, finely diced (optional)

Directions:
Set up large bowl of ice and water.  On stove or side burner of gas grill, bring large pan of salted water to a boil.
Using cooking basket, boil green beans and asparagus for 1 minute, then plunge into ice water.
Repeat process for squashes, cooking zucchini for 3 minutes and chayote for 5 minutes.
Place all ingredients in large salad bowl and gently toss.
Serve family style or dish up single servings in hollowed out tomato halved.

A timeless picnic favorite, this crunchy-cool salad brings all the colors of the rainbow to your table - and a day's supply of antioxidants to boot
