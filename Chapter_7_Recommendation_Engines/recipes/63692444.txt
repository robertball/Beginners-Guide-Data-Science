Title:
Spinach Dip (TGIF Knock Off) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, grated
1 -2 teaspoon of minced garlic
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
10 ounces frozen spinach (thawed and drained)
6 ounces artichoke hearts (chopped)
chopped tomato

Directions:
1
Lightly spray a shallow casserole dish or boat with cooking spray.
2
Combine all ingredients (chopping artichokes if necessary).
3
Place ingredients in casserole and bake at 325 for 20 minutes.
4
Garnish with chopped tomatoes.
5
Pretty served in a bread bowl.
