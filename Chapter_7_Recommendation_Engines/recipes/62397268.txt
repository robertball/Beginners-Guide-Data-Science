Title:
Patsy's Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head each broccoli and cauliflower
1 red pepper
1/2 to 1 sweet onion
Dressing
2/3 c vinegar , olive oil and sugar or Splenda
1/4 t salt , garlic powder and pepper

Directions:
Chop veggies in small pieces mix together.  Mix together ingredients for dressing and pour over veggies. Let flavors mix for 1 hour and enjoy.
