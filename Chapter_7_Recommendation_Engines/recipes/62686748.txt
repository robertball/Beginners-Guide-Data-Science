Title:
Not-So-Spicy Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can chick peas
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1/2 cup black olives (chopped)
1/2 onion (chopped)
1/2 green bell pepper (chopped)
1/2 red bell pepper (chopped)
3 medium tomatoes (chopped)
1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette salad dressing
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine peas, beans, corn, onion, green and red bell pepper, and tomatoes. Season with balsamic vinaigrette salad dressing and garlic; mix well. Cover, and refrigerate overnight to blend flavors.
