Title:
Salmon with Balsamic-Honey Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. balsamic vinegar
2 T. white wine or chicken broth
2 T. Dijon mustard
2 T. honey
5 garlic cloves, minced
1 T. olive oil
8 salmon fillets (6  oz. each)
1/2 t. each salt and pepper
1 T. minced fresh oregano

Directions:
Combine the first six ingredients in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 4-5 minutes or until thickened
Place salmon skin side down on a greased 15x10x1-in.baking pan.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Spoon glaze over salmon; top with oregano..
Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees for 12-15 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
