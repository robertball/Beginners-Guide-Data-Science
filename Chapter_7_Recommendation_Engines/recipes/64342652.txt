Title:
Autumn Sunshine Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2097248.jpg
Ingredients:
Juice of  1/2 lemon
Juice of 1 clementine
1 fl oz maple syrup
1 fl oz Grand Marnier
2 fl oz bourbon
1 piece of clementine peel, 1/4" by 1" for garnish
6 ice cubes

Directions:
Chill a 7 ounce cocktail (martini) glass in the freezer, as well as your cocktail shaker.
Put six ice cubes in the shaker, and then add all of the other ingredients to the shaker except the clementine peel. Shake vigorously for 15 seconds.
Strain cocktail into chilled glass and flame the peel over the cocktail, rub peel on the rim and then drop into cocktail.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Doc Simonson
Description: 
I named this cocktail Autumn Sunshine because its flavor is bright, like the sun, but the maple syrup, for some reason, reminds me of burning leaves in the Autumn. It is definitely the color of Autumn!
