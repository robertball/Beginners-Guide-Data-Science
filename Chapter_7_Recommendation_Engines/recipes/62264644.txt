Title:
BLT Dip Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup maoynnaise
1 cup sour cream
1/4 tsp garlic powder
Pinch of ground pepper
2 Tbls finely chopped green onion or chives
1 can (14.5 oz)diced tomatoes- drained well
1 pound bacon, crispy-cooked & crumbled

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except the tomatoe and bacon.
Chill until its time to serve at least 1-2 hours
Just before serving, stir in bacon and tomato
Serve withe crakers, pita triangles

Submitter: 
Denise Maggio Medina
Description: 
This Recipes is a big hit for any Party and very easy to make!!!! You can make a great presentation and hollow-out a head of Iceburg lettuce and fill with dip and serve with crakers ect......
