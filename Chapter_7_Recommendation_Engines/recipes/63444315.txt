Title:
Spicy Chicken breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic minced
1 small onion, finely chopped
4 TBS. finely chopped cilantro
3 TBS. lime juice
2 TBS. oil
1 TBS. chili powder
pepper
salt
8 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a ziploc bag and marinate 2  to 3 hours. Take chicken out and bake at 350 until juices run clear

Submitter: 
iceemama
Description: 
This is an American Heart Association recipe. I increased the amount of cilantro and I added the salt.
