Title:
hot hummus dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I -8 oz plain ( or flavored )hummus
1 cup of feta cheese- divided
1 cup ok kalamata olives- chopped
one medium tomato- diced

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees
mix hummus with 1/2 cup of feta, and place in a small dish- a pie tin or bread loaf pan will do.
top with the remaining feta, diced tomatos and olives.
bake at 350 degrees for 25-35 minutes.
Serve with pita chips or crackers.

Easy to make, and different from the average dip..
