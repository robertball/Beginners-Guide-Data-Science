Title:
Savory Pumpkin Bread Budding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb mild sausage
1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
1 medium onion
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
8 cups cubed day old bread
6 eggs
2 12 once cans evaporated milk
1 15 ounce can pure pumpkin
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
2 teaspoons Italian seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease a 13 X 9 inch baking pan. In a large skillet, brown sausage, peppers, onion and garlic for 8-10 minutes. In a large bowl, toss bread cubes, cheese and sausage mixture. In another bowl whisk eggs, milk, pumpkin and cheese. Add salt, pepper and seasoning. Fold egg mixture into bread mixture and transfer to prepared baking pan. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until puffed, golden and set in center. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
J. Glenn Kunzler
Description: 
A delicious main dish (I typically serve it for breakfast) made from an unusual but delicious combination of ingredients.  Made with mild sausage, red bell pepper, onion, pumpkin and cheddar cheese--a great make-ahead meal.
