Title:
Cherry Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cup red and yellow cherry tomatoes, some halfed
salt and pepper to taste
2/3 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup sour cream
3 Tbsp fresh basil leaves
1 shallot minced
2 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
Add cherry tomatoes to a lafge serving bowl.  Season with salt and pepper, to taste and toss.
In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, sour cream, basil, shallots and garlic.  Season with Pepper to taste.  Drizzel dressing over tomatoes and toss.
