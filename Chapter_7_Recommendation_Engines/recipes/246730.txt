Title:
Duncan Hines速 German Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3433959.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 package Duncan Hines速 Moist Deluxe速 Swiss Chocolate Cake Mix
3 large eggs
1 1/4 cups water
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup chopped pecans
Frosting:
2/3 cup granulated sugar
2/3 cup evaporated milk
2 large egg yolks
1/3 cup butter or margarine
1 1/3 cups flaked coconut
1 cup chopped pecans
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour 13x9-inch pan.
Prepare cake mix according to package directions. Stir in 1/2 cup pecans. Pour into pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 35 to 40 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely.
For frosting, combine sugar, evaporated milk, egg yolks and butter in medium saucepan. Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture comes to a boil. Remove from heat. Add coconut, 1 cup pecans and vanilla extract. Stir until thick. Spread on top of cake.
