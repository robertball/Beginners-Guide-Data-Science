Title:
BROILED TILAPIA WITH MUSTARD-CHIVE SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FISH:
COOKING SPRAY
1 BAD TILAPIA
EXTRA VIRGIN OLIVE OIL, FOR DRIZZLING
KOSHER SALT & PEPPER
SAUCE: (THIS IS DOUBLED)
1/2 CUP GREEK YOGURT
4 TSP HONEY
2 TSP DIJON MUSTARD
JUICE FROM 1 LARGE LEMON
4 TBLS CHOPPED CHIVES

Directions:
FOR THE FISH: PREHEAT A BROILER. SPRAY BAKING SHEET WITH PAM, SET ASIDE.
DRIZZLE THE TILAPIA WITH OLIVE OIL, SALT & PEPPER ON BOTH SIDES. ARRANGE IN A SINGLE LAYER ON BAKING SHEET AND BROIL 8 - 10 MINUTES.
FOR THE SAUCE: IN A SMALL BOWL, MIX YOGURT, HONEY, MUSTARD UNTIL SMOOTH. WHISK IN LEMON JUICE AND CHIVES.
TRANSFER FISH TO PLATE AND DRIZZLE WITH SAUCE (HEAT SAUCE IN MICRO FOR 45 SECONDS)
