Title:
Chaat Masala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/299561.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups  Garbanzo beans (washed & strained)
1 medium Potato (chopped)
1 medium Onion (mildly chopped)
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp chopped cilantro
1 Tbsp grated coconut
½ cup crunchy sev (optional)
1 bag pani puri (available in indian grocery stores)

Directions:
Wrap the chopped potato in a damp paper towel & microwave for 4 minutes. Let it cool.
Mix all remaining ingredients in a mixing bowl. Add potato and mix well. Add crunchy sev if desired.
Scoop into Pani puri & top with mint or tamarind-date chutney (or both!)

Submitter: 
vburrito
Description: 
Fun, crunchy, tangy snack found at roadside carts all over India. Serve with Mint or Tamarind-Date chutney. My mom makes them as a summer treat. These are a little messy but worth it!
