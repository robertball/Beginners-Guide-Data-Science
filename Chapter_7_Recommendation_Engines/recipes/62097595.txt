Title:
Squirrel and rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 squirrel
3 cups water
1 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. onion salt
2 tsp. garlic powder
1 1/2 c. rice

Directions:
Add squirrel to 3 cups of water. Boil til tender. ( 1 hour )
Remove squirrel from broth and tear into pieces.
Add 1 tsp. of butter to wok or other frying pan. Melt and add pieces of squirrel. Sprinkle on garlic powder and onion salt. Stir till heated thoroughly.
Add 3 cups of stock and pepper. Bring to a boil and add rice.
Lower heat and simmer for 15 minutes or until rice is desired tenderness.
Serve with white acre peas and cornbread.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Sure fire recipe for tender and flavorful squirrel. Quick and easy too.
