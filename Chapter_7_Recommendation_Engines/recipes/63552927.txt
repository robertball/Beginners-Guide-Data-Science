Title:
Garlic Lime Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb pork chops
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon chili powder
1/8 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon italian seasoning
2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 teaspoons garlic powder
3 tablespoons lime juice

Directions:
1. In a small bowl, mix together salt, black pepper, chili powder, paprika, 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder, onion powder, and Italian Seasoning. Sprinkle spice mixture generously on both sides of pork chop.
2. Heat butter and olive oil in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Saute pork until golden brown, on each side and cooked through.  Remove from pan.
3. In skillet where pork was cooked mix 2 teaspoons garlic powder and lime juice with leftover juices. Stir until mixed and pour over the pork chops.
Serve as is or slice to make delicious pork tacos.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Thompson
Description: 
Deliciously seasoned pork chops that can be eaten as is or turned into pork tacos.
