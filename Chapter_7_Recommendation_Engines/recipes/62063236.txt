Title:
Broccoli & Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10 oz. packages of frozen broccoli, defrosted and drained
1 10 oz. package of frozen peas, defrosted and drained
15 oz. jar of cheese whiz
10.75 oz. condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 challah bread unsliced
1/2 c. butter, melted

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
2.  In a medium bowl combine the cheese whiz and cream of mushroom soup.  Add the broccoli and peas and mix well. Pour the mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
3.  Slice off the top of the challah and pull off pieces of the inside and place the pieces on the casserole until the whole casserole is covered.  Using a pastry brush, brush the butter generously over the bread.
4.  Bake for 45 to 60 minutes in the preheated over or until browned.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
This cheesy casserole is a cinch to whip up.  It is always requested for family gatherings.
