Title:
Raw Nut Milk Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups homemade almond milk
1 cup raw cashews
2 tsp golden flax seed
2 cups water
3/4 cup light olive oil
1/4 cup honey
1/2 cup evaporated cane juice crystals (organic sugar)
1/4 tsp sea salt
1-2 tsp flavoring extract for desired flavor (see notes)
mix-ins for desired flavor (if desired)

Directions:
Measure almond milk into a large glass jar or other container with a lid.
In the blender, combine cashews, flax seed, water, oil, honey, crystals, salt and flavorings as directed. Blend on high until very smooth.
Add to milk. Chill completely, about 2 hours, in refrigerator.
Shake or stir well, and freeze in a conventional ice cream freezer according to the manufacturer's instructions.

Submitter: 
Bestfoodist
Description: 
If you know how to make nut milks in the blender, and want real ice cream made from them, this is the best recipe!
