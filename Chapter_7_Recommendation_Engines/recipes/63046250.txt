Title:
Pimento (Red Pepper) Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 C Mayo
1/4 t salt
1/4 t black pepper
1/4 t cayenne pepper
1/4 lb extra sharp white cheddar cheese grated (abt 4 oz)
1/4 lb extra sharp yellow cheddar cheese grated (abt 4oz)

Directions:
In large bowl , mix ingredients with fork until combined. Cover and refrigerate for 30 min before serving.

Submitter: 
Tiffany Donnelly
Description: 
This is great mix with hot pasta. Yum!
