Title:
FRIED MOZZERELLA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 OZ MOZERELLA
1/2 CUP FLOUR
2 EGGS, LIGHTLY BEATEN
SALT & PEPPER TO TASTE
1 CUP BREAD CRUMBS
1 CUP OLIVE OIL

Directions:
CUT MOZZERELLA INTO SMALL SQUARES, ROLL IN FLOUR, DIP IN EGG, SEASON W/SALT & PEPPER. ROLL IN BREAD CRUMBS, DIP IN EGG MIXTURE AGAIN. ROLL IN BREAD CRUMBS AGAIN. FRY IN HOT OLIVE OIL JUST LONG ENOUGH FOR BREAD CRUMBS TO TURN GOLDEN BROWN.
