Title:
Air Fryer Keto Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F7018073.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds chicken wings
1 tablespoon taco seasoning mix
2 teaspoons olive oil

Directions:
Combine chicken wings, taco seasoning, and oil in a resealable plastic bag. Shake to coat.
Preheat the air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 2 minutes.
Place wings in the air fryer and cook for 12 minutes, turning after 6 minutes. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
thedailygourmet
Description: 
These air-fried chicken wings are finished in under 15 minutes and a great dish if you are on the keto diet. If desired, separate wings into drumettes.
