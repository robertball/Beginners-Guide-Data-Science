Title:
Tortilla Ham / Turkey Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
HAM
1 1/2 lbs. (3 8oz pkgs) cream cheese, softened
1 envelope Italian salad dressing mix
10 (6 inch) flour tortillas
1 1/2 lbs sliced fully cooked ham
TURKEY
1 1/2 lbs. (3 8oz pkgs) cream cheese, softened
1 small envelope ranch dressing mix
10 (6 inch) flour tortillas
1 1/2 lbs sliced fully cooked turkey

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese and salad dressing mix. Spread over one side of each tortilla. Top with ham or turkey; roll up. Wrap tightly in press and seal and refrigerate overnight. Cut into 1-in. slices.
