Title:
Big Daddy's Pineapple Smoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups unsweetened pineapple juice
1 cup skim milk
2 cups ice cubes
2 (8 ounce) cans unsweetened crushed pineapple
2 scoops of Mila

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender, cover and process until smooth. Pour into glasses; serve immediately.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
This cool quick beverage is perfect for the beach. It's a not-too-sweet smoothie that's sure to refresh any time of day.Modified from: Pineapple Smoothies
