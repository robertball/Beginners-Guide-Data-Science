Title:
Turkey Chorizo, Spinach, and Cheddar Frittata Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 large eggs
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 cup wilted spinach (can use frozen)
1 cup turkey chorizo
1/4 cup reduced fat cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
In medium bowl, whisk eggs, milk, salt and pepper together.
Grease a standard muffin pan with olive oil or cooking spray.
Place one rounded tablespoon of the turkey chorizo in each well of a standard muffin pan.
Place one rounded teaspoon of the wilted spinach in each well of a standard muffin pan.
Evenly divide the egg mixture between the muffin wells, almost to the top.
Sprinkle 1 teaspoon of cheese on top of each muffin well.
Bake for 14-18 minutes or until center of frittatas is just set.
Remove from pan immediately.
Can be refrigerated or frozen and reheated in the microwave.

Submitter: 
Andrew Rocco
Description: 
Delicious and healthy frittata muffins packed with flavor. A high protein way to start the day.
