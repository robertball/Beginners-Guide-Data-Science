Title:
Vitamix: Four fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium orange, whole peeled but with pith intact
1 inch thick slice pineapple with core, peeled
1/2 banana peeled
3 whole strawberries with green tops
2 tablespoons agave nectar
1 cup ice cubes
.5 cup water

Directions:
Place all ingredients into the Vitamix in the order listed
Select Variable 1
Turn machine on and slowly increase speed to Variable 10, then to High
Blend for 30 seconds or until desired consistency
