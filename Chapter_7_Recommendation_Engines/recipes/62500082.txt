Title:
Trish's Yummy Puffed Wheat Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter/margarine
1 cup corn syrup
1 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
6 heaping Tbsp. cocoa
1 tsp vanilla
10 cups puffed wheat

Directions:
Bring first 5 ingredients to a boil for 30 sec.
Add vanilla.
Mix sauce into puffed wheat and put in a greased 9 x 13 pan.
