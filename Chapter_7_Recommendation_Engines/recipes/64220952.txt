Title:
Outaouais garlic-butter rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1651114.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of Basmati rice
1 and 1/3 cups of Chicken stock
5 cloves of garlic finely chopped
4 tablespoons of butter cut into chunks

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400'F
In a casserole dish rinse the rice with room temperature water until the water runs clear. This usually takes about 5 rinses.
Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes
Add the chicken stock, garlic, and butter. Stir the rice to get an even mixture
Cover, and bake for 40 minutes without opening the lid.

Submitter: 
Andrew Moore
Description: 
This is a classic French Canadian dish, and has been a staple in my family for years. The entire house smells amazing as this dish is baking! This dish shines when you mix it with some sauteed vegetables! Enjoy!!
