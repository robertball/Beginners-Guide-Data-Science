Title:
The Anderson's Rhubarb Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mix the following three ingredients together and put in greased 9"x13" pan:
4 1/2 cups cut up rhubarb
1  2/3 c sugar
6 tbsp flour
In separate bowl, combine the following:
1 c brown sugar
1 c oatmeal
1 1/2 c flour
3/4 c butter

Directions:
Sprinkle second mixture over rhubarb mixture that you placed in 9x13" pan.
Bake at 375 degrees F for 35-40 minutes.
Serve with ice cream or whip cream.
