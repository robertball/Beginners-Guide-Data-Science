Title:
Chicken tortilla Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1100828.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb BLSL chicken breasts
I tbsp cooking oil
1 large white onion
1 green pepper
1/8 plus 1/2 tsp sea salt divided
1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper
1Tbsp cumin
2 tsp chili powder
1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 jalapeño peppers, seeds and chopped
2 cups diced tomatoes
1 cup frozen or fresh corn kernels
Cooking spread
8 6" corn tortilla, quartered.
3 oz shredded cheese. Jack and cheddar cheese mixed
3 green onions

Directions:
Poach chicken breasts. Fill a large sauce pan 2/3 full of water and bring to a boil. Add chicken so water covers each breast. Cover and reduce heat to medium low. Cook on low simmer for about 22-25 minutes
Preheat oven to 350. In a large skillet on medium high heat heat oil. Add onion, bell pepper, 1/8 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp peer per and cook for 12 to 15 min. Stirring occasionally until soft and lightly browned
Add cumin,chili powder and oregano then stir to combine.
Add garlic, jalapeño, and cook until until garlic is soft, ~1 minute.
Add tomatoes and bring to a simmer. Stir in corn and cook until headed~1 minute. Reduce heat to medium low.
Stir in chicken and cook until heated through ~1 minute. Season with remaining salt and pepper and remove from heat.
Mist a 9x13-in glass baking dish with cooking spray. Arrange 1/2 of quartered tortillas over bottom of dish. (Gaps are acceptable) Top with 1/2 of chicken mixture, then sprinkle with cheese. Add a second layer of tortilla, chicken and cheese. Cover with foil and bake for 35-45 minutes until bubbly and cheese is melted. Rest for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with green onions.
