Title:
Broccoli Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 tablespoon butter
1 onion, chopped
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
broccoli
2 cups milk
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
8 eggs, beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Cook  the chopped broccoli in the microwave  until tender but firm.
In a bowl, mix the Cheddar cheese, flour,  salt, and mustard. Mix in 1/2 cup butter until evenly moist. Press into the bottom of a 9 x 13 dish.
Melt the 1 tablespoon butter in microwave, and cook the onion  until tender. Spread steamed broccoli in dish and sprinkle over it the onion. Mix together the milk, beaten eggs, nutmeg and salt and pour over the brocolli and onions.
Bake 50 - 60 mins in the preheated oven.
