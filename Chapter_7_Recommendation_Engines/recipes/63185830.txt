Title:
HERBED CHICKEN IN PASTRY Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 BONELESS CHICKEN BREASTS
1 TBSP BUTTER
1/2 PKG (17.3 OZ SIZE) PUFF PASTRY SHEETS (1 SHEET)
1 EGG
1 TBSP WATER
1 CONTAINER (4 OZ) ALOUETTE GARLIC & HERBS SPREADABLE CHEESE
1/4 CUP CHOPPED PARSLEY

Directions:
SEASON CHICKEN W/SALT & PEPPER. MELT BUTTER IN SKILLET. ADD CHICKEN AND COOK UNTIL BROWNED. REMOVE CHICKEN. COVER & REFRIGERATE 15 MINUTES OR UP TO 24 HRS
THAW PASRY SHEET AT ROOM TEMPATURE 30 MINUTES. MIX EGG & WATER. PREHEAT OVER TO 400.
UNFOLD PASTRY ON LIGHTLY FLOURED SURFACE. ROLL INTO 14" SQUARES AND CUT INTO 4 (7") SQUARES. SPREAD ABOUT 2 TBSP OF THE CHEESE SPREAD IN CENTER OF EACH SQUARE. SPRINKLE W/1 TBSP PARSLEY & TOP WITH COOLED CHICKEN. BRUSH EDGES OF SQUARES WITH EGG MIXTURE. FOLD EACH CORNER TO CENTER ON TOP OF CHICKEN AND SEAL EDGES. PLACE SEAM SIDE DOWN ON BAKING SHEET AND BRUSH W/EGG MIXTURE
BAKE FOR 25 MINUTES OR UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN
