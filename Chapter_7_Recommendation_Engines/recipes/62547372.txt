Title:
Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. cooking oil
1 c. sugar
2 eggs, beaten
3 ripe bananas, mashed
2 c. flour
1 t. baking soda
1/2 t. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
3 T. milk
1/2 t. vanilla
1/2 c. chopped nuts

Directions:
Beat oil and sugar together.
Add eggs and banana pulp and beat well.
Add dry ingredients, milk and vanilla, mixing well.
Stir in nuts.
Pour into greased and floured loaf pan (9x5).
Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for about 1 hour.
Cool well and store overnight before cutting.
