Title:
Easy Vegetable Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of potato soup
1/2 bag frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
1/2 bag frozen broccoli and carrots, thawed
1 can mushrooms, drained
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp. fresh thyme (or 1/3 tsp. dried thyme)
1/2 tsp. fresh rosemary (or 1/8 tsp. dried)
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1, 9" pie crust
1 egg, lightly beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
In a medium bowl, combine potato soup, mixed vegetables, milk, thyme, and black pepper.
Spoon filling into pie plate. Cover with top crust, and crimp edges to seal.  Slit top crust, and brush with beaten egg if desired.
Bake for 40 minutes.  Remove from oven, and cool for 10 minutes before serving.
