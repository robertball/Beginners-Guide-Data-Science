Title:
Golden Graham Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. light corn syrup
3 tbsp. butter
1 pkg. (11.5 oz.) milk chocolate morsels
3 c. miniature marshmallows
1 tsp. vanilla
1 pkg. (12 oz.) Golden Graham cereal (9 c.)

Directions:
Grease rectangular pan, 13x9x2 inches.
Heat syrup, butter and morsels to boiling, stirring constantly. Remove from heat, stir in vanilla.
Pour over cereal; toss until coated. Fold in marshmallows, 1 cup at a time. Press in pan with buttered back of spoon. Let stand 1 hour.
Cut into 2 inch squares. Store loosely covered at room temperature up to 2 days. Makes 24 squares.
