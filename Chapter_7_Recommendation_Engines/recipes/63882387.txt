Title:
Hushpuppies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077006.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c enriched cornmeal
1 t flour
1 tsp sugar
1 egg, beaten
1/4 tsp soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 c buttermilk

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. add eggs, buttermilk & water.
Drop by the spoonful into hot fat.
Fry to a golden brown.
