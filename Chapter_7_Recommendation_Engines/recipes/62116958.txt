Title:
Onion Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. container sour cream
8 oz. package cream cheese, softened
1 oz. pkg. dry onion soup mix
1 teaspoon Worcheshire sauce

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Chill for several hours for the flavors to blend.  Serve with potato chips.

Submitter: 
FoodFan
Description: 
A different twist on a popular recipe.  Very addicting, especially when served with kettle potato chips.
