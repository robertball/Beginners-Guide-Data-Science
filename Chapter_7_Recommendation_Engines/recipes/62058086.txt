Title:
Caramel Brickle Ice Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 crushed Oreo cookies
2 Tbsp. butter, melted
1 pint butter pecan ice cream, softened
1 pint chocolate chip ice cream, softened
1/2 cup caramel ice cream topping
1/4 cup brickle chips
Garnish:
1/2 cup whipping cream, whipped
1/3 cup caramel ice cream topping
1/4 cup brickle chips

Directions:
Mix Oreos and butter in a 9 pie pan; press in bottom and up sides of pan. Freeze 30 minutes or until set.
Spoon butter pecan ice cream over crust; smooth top. Spread 1/2 cup caramel topping over ice cream; sprinkle with 1/4 cup brickle chips.
Carefully spoon chocolate chip ice cream in small mounds around outside edge of pie, working toward center to cover pie; smooth top. Freeze 4 hours or overnight.
Let stand at room temperature 15 minutes before serving. Garnish with whipped cream, caramel topping and chips.

Submitter: 
Sue S.
Description: 
Butter pecan ice cream and chocolate chip ice cream in an oreo cookie crust, with whipped cream, caramel sauce and brickle chips ... yummmm!! This also works well in muffin cups for little individual ice cream treats
