Title:
Fire & Ice Chicken Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5322544.jpg
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts thinly sliced
1 green onion diced
3T-1/4 c fresh cilantro chopped
1 1/2 t. diced garlic from a jar
1 t salt
1/4 t cheyenne  red pepper
1/4 t ginger powder
couple dashes curry powder (more if you like the curry taste)
1 1/4 t paprika
juice of 2/3 of a lemon (you'll use the other 1/3 for the sauce)
1/2 onion thinly sliced
3 T olive oil
Ingredients for assembly:
1-2 roma tomatoes diced
1 c shredded romaine lettuce
1/2 cucumber peeled, seeded and diced
6-8 pieces naan (you can use flatbread, ciabatta roll or tortilla)
Cucumber Sauce:
1/3 c  plain yogurt
1 green onion thinly sliced and chopped
3 T fresh cilantro finely chopped
1/2 t salt
1/8 t black pepper
dash cumin
juice of 1/3 lemon (You'll use the 2/3 for the chicken marinade)
1/8 t dill weed
1/2 cucumber peeled, seeded, grated and pressed to remove liquid (reserve liquid for marinade, and other half of cucumber for assembling the wrap)

Directions:
1. Make cucumber sauce (at bottom of ingredient list) in bowl by mixing all ingredients together,  cover and chill.
2. In separate bowl add green onion, cilantro, garlic, salt, cheyenne pepper, ginger powder, curry spice, paprika, lemon juice, thinly slice onion, and reserved liquid from cucumber. Mix well and set aside.
3. Thinly slice chicken breast and cut into bite size pieces. Add to above mixture in bowl, combine well, and let marinade for 30 min-1 hour in fridge.
4. Heat skillet on high with 3 T of olive oil. Place chicken mixture in pan, and stir fry until meat is thoroughly cooked.
Too assemble:
Place 1 t of cucumber sauce, and spread on warmed naan. Add 1/4-1/3 c cooked chicken meat. Top with 2-3 t more cucumber sauce, diced tomatoes, diced cucumbers and a little lettuce. Fold in half, and enjoy!
