Title:
Seafood Restaurant's Famous Cheese Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954866.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups biscuit baking mix
1/2 cup shredded cheddar
2/3 cup milk
1 egg
1/2 cup melted butter
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon dried parsley

Directions:
Blend first four ingredients until dough is firm.
Using a large (muffin) scoop, place the dough on a greased baking pan.
Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
While biscuits are baking, combine remaining ingredients.
Brush baked biscuits with the garlic topping.
