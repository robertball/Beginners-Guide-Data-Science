Title:
Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon allspice
1/2 cup molasses
1 1/4 cups sugar
2/3 cup Crisco
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
2/3 cup milk
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
3 eggs
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Mix together the first set of ingredients.
Stir in the baking powder and baking soda.
Add eggs and milk.
Mix thoroughly.
Pour into 2 greased layer pans and bake at 375 degrees for 35 minutes.
