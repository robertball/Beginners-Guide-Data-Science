Title:
Judy's Triple Orange Jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 6 oz pkg orange gelatin
2 cups boiling water
1- 6 oz can orange juice concentrate (frozen)
1- 20 oz can crushed pineapple
1- 11 oz can mandarin oranges
1 cup whipping cream
1 small box INSTANT lemon pudding
1 cup milk

Directions:
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water
Stir in orange juice concentrate until thawed
Stir in undrained pineapple
Chill until partially set
Fold in mandarin oranges
Pour into 13 x 9 x 2 pan
Chill until almost firm
Beat whipping cream
Beat pudding mix & milk with rotary beater about 1-2 minutes or until smooth
Fold into whip cream
Spread over gelatin
Chill 5-6 hours until firm
Cut into squares
