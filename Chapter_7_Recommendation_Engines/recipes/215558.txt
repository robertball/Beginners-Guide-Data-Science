Title:
Gobble-It-Up Turkey with Homemade Ketchup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993516.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices whole wheat bread, toasted
1 tablespoon Hellmann's® 1/2 the Fat mayonnaise-type dressing
2 slices cooked skinless turkey breast
2 slices tomato
1 leaf green leaf lettuce
1 slice mozzarella
1 tablespoon homemade ketchup*
1 cherry tomato, for garnish (optional)
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Spread Hellmann's 1/2 the Fat mayonnaise-type dressing on one slice of bread and 1 tablespoon of ketchup on the other. Layer turkey, lettuce, tomato and cheese between slices of bread and season with pepper to taste. Garnish with a cherry tomato.

Submitter: 
Hellmann's®
Description: 
Slices of tomato and mozzarella and a dab of homemade ketchup make a family-favorite turkey sandwich even better.
