Title:
Sweet Onion Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large sweet onion, chopped
1 pound lean ground beef
Garlic powder
Worcestershire sauce
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat grill for high heat.
Peel and grate the sweet onion in the food processor.  Place onion mixture in sieve/strainer and press juices out until mixture is dry.
Add to ground beef along with salt, pepper, a little garlic powder and a dash of Worcestershire sauce.
Form into patties.  Grill burgers to desired degreeof doneness.  Remove from grill, and serve with your favorite condiments.
