Title:
Moist and Savory Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups Swanson® Chicken Broth (regular, Natural Goodness™ or Certified Organic)
Generous dash ground black pepper
1 1/2 stalk celery, coarsely chopped
1 medium onion, coarsely chopped
2 Tbs butter
1 1/2 tsp thyme
1 tsp sage
1 tsp  rosemary
2 tsp minced parsley
2 Tbs chopped walnuts
2 Tbs finely chopped dried cranberries
1/2 medium apple, chopped
4 cups Pepperidge Farm® Herb Seasoned Stuffing

Directions:
Heat broth with butter, black pepper, celery and onion, along with other spices in 2-quart saucepan over high heat to a boil. Reduce heat to low, add nuts and dried cranberries. Cover and cook for 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
Add stuffing and stir lightly to coat.

Submitter: 
Big Dan
Description: 
Perfectly-seasoned Pepperidge Farm® Herb Seasoned Stuffing, celery and onion are bathed in Swanson® Chicken Broth for a classic, moist stuffing dish.Modified from: Moist and Savory Stuffing
