Title:
Pam's Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags Hormel frozen meatballs (Red Bag)
28 oz canned diced tomatoes
2 jars sweet & sour sauce
1 large can crushed pineapple
1/2 cup worchestershire sauce
1/2 cup brown sugar
Red pepper flakes

Directions:
Put all ingredients into large slow cooker.  Cook 6-8 hours on low.
Serve over rice for a main dish or serve with toothpicks for a great appetizer.

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
I got this recipe from a co-worker, hence Pam's Meatballs!
