Title:
Honey Dijon Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/483517.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon honey
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Whisk balsamic vinegar, olive oil, mustard, honey, salt, and pepper in a bowl.

Submitter: 
GumboGirl
Description: 
Easy and delicious. Perfect for dipping bread in or great for dressing a salad.
