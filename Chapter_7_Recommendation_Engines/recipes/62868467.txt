Title:
Summer Lemon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Head Iceberg Lettuce
1/2 White or Spanish Onion
Juice of 2 Lemons
1/8 Cup of Lite Oil
1 teaspoon Salt
1 teaspoon Pepper

Directions:
Shred lettuce, thinly dice onion and combine.
In a small cup, stir together lemon juice, oil, salt and pepper.
Pour dressing on salad and toss gently.

Submitter:

Description: 
A simple, tangy and refreshing addition to a Summer meal.
