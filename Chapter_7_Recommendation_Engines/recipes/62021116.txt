Title:
Southwest Corn Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/3 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cumin seed
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 egg
1/4 cup bacon grease or cooking oil
1 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â° F.  Grease a muffin tin. Mix cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, chili powder, and cumin seed in a large bowl.  Add egg, oil, and milk, stir to make a batter.
Pour or spoon batter into the prepared muffin tin.
Bake for 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into a muffin comes out clean.
