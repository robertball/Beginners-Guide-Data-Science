Title:
Pasta With Eggplant & Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C. olive oil
1 large garlic clove
1 large onion, thinly sliced
2 green pappers, thinly sliced
1 eggplant (about 1 lb) peeled & diced
8 large black olives, sliced
1 large can Italian plum tomatoes
2 tsp capers
1 can anchovies, drained and chopped
1/4 tsp. basil or 5 or 6 fresh basil leaves, torn
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
1 lb. spaghetti or green noodles
1/4 C grated Romano cheese

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat.
Add garlic and brown.
Saute onion, pepper, eggplant and olives until vegetables are tender for about 10-15 minutes.
Add tomatoes and cook 20 minutes longer
Add capers, anchovies, basil & red pepper flakes.
Reduce heat to low and simmer 30 minutes with lid.
Stir occasionally.
Add salt and peper to taste.
Cook spaghetti or green noodles in  boiling water according to package directions. Drain.
Top spaghetti with sauce and grated cheese.
