Title:
Low Carb Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  lb yellow squash (2-3 med)
1/2  onion, chopped
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1  cup shredded  cheese
1   egg
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Steam squash and onion.
Mix Mayo, cheese and egg.
Add squash, And salt and pepper to taste.
Pour into a greased quart sized casserole dish.
Top with additional shredded cheese.
Bake at 350 for 40 minute.

Submitter: 
Kisha McDaniel Logsdon
Description: 
This recipe is really versatile. The ingredients can be increased/decreased to your liking. I have even made it with a combination of squash and zucchini and it was delicious!
