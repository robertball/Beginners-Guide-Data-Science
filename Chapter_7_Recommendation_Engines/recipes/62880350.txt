Title:
Simple Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons canola oil
½ pound thinly sliced chicken breast
1 bag (16 oz) Birds Eye® Broccoli Stir-Fry
¾ cup stir-fry sauce
1 bag (10 oz) Birds Eye® Steamfresh® Long Grain White Rice with Mixed Vegetables, cooked according to package directions

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large nonstick skillet over MEDIUM-HIGH heat. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink, about 8 minutes: remove from skillet and set aside.
Add remaining 1 tablespoon oil and broccoli stir-fry to skillet. Cook, stirring frequently, until vegetables are tender.
Stir in stir-fry sauce and reserved chicken and continue cooking for an additional 2 minutes or until heated through.
Serve with prepared rice.
