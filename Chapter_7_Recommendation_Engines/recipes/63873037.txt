Title:
Chicken and Smoked Sausage Gumbo with white Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup olive oil
1 pound smoked Kielbasa cut crosswise 1/2 inch thick pieces
4 lbs boneless skinless chicken
1 tablespoon creole seasoning
1 cup all purpose flour
2 cups chopped onions
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped bell peppers
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne
3 bay leaves
9 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup chopped green onions
2 tablespoons chopped parsley leaves
1 tablespoon file powder
white rice
hot sauce

Directions:
In large pot heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium high heat.
Add sausage and cook until well browned about 8 minutes
Remove sausage with slotted spoon drain and set aside
Season chicken with creole seasoning and add to the oil remaining in the pan
Cook chicken over medium high heat until well browned about 5-6 minutes
Remove Chicken from the pan and let cool
Combine 1/2 cup oil and flour in the pan still at a medium high heat
Stir slowly and constantly for 20-25 minutes to make a dark brown roux about the color of chocolate
Add in onions, celery, and bell peppers and cook until wilted 4-5 minutes
Add the sausage, salt, cayenne, and bay leaves and cook 2 minutes
Stirring slowly add in the chicken broth and bring mixture to a boil
Reduce heat to medium low and cook uncover and stirring occasionally for 1 hour
Add chicken to the pot and simmer an additional 1 1/2 hours stirring occasionally and skimming off any fat that rises to the surface
Remove the pot from the heat
With a slotted spoon remove the chicken and discard the bay leaves
Shred the chicken and return back to the pot
Stir in green onions, parsley, and file powder
Spoon rice into the bottom of deep bowls and ladle the gumbo over the top
Serve with hot sauce on the side
