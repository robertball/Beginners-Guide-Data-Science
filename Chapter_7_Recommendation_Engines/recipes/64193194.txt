Title:
Aunt Jessie's Chocolate Squares Recipe

Ingredients:
1/2 c butter
1/2 cup white sugar
add 2 eggs beaten
1/2 cup flour
pinch of salt
1-1/2 tablespoons cocoa
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 cup chopped dates

Directions:
all I have is oven 350
I would presume, mix all ingredients together, bake in 8 x 8 greased baking dish (325 if glass) for 40-50 minutes
