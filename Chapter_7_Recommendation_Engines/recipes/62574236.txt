Title:
Bean Custard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) pie shell
4 eggs
1 can evaporated milk
2 cups cooked navy beans, drained
1 stick (8-9 Tablespoons) butter
2 Tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2 cups packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C)
In an electric blender, blend eggs, milk, beans, butter, flour and nutmeg for 2 minutes on medium or high
Pour blend into a large bowl, mix in sugar and vanilla. Mix well. Pour mixture into pastry shell.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake until a knife inserted into center of pie comes out clean (about an additional 45 minutes).

Submitter: 
Joseph S.
Description: 
A sweet custard pie that replicates the taste and texture of those great Bean Pies sold by street vendors in cities around the U.S.Modified from: Navy Bean Pie
