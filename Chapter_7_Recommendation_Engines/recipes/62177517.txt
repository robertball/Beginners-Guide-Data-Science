Title:
Chocolate chip and coconut bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Graham crackers
1 stick melted butter
1C chopped nuts
1C coconut
1 12oz bag of chocolate chips
1 14oz can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Crush the graham crackers and combine with the butter and press into a 13x9in pan. Layer all ingredients start with nuts, coconut, chocolate and then the milk. Then bake for 25-30min at 350.
