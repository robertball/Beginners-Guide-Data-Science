Title:
Berry Smoothie from Carnation Breakfast Essentials® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4461296.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup strawberry kiwi juice
1 packet Strawberry Sensation Flavor CARNATION BREAKFAST ESSENTIALS® Complete Nutritional Drink
1/2 cup frozen berries (strawberries, raspberries and/or blueberries)
1/2 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Place juice, Carnation Breakfast Essentials Drink, berries and ice in blender; cover. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Carnation Breakfast Essentials
Description: 
This is a berry, berry yummy drink for smoothie lovers. Take a look and try the Berry Smoothie recipe from Carnation Breakfast Essentials today!
