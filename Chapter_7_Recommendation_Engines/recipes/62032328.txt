Title:
Oriental Chicken Salad II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3oz pkgs chicken ramen noodle soup mix
(first take a mallet and break noodles in pkg, then remove the flavor pkg, reserve for dressing)
16oz fresh cole slaw mix
1 bunch green onions-chopped
1/2 cup slivered almonds
1 1/2 cups cooked chicken-diced or cubed
DRESSING
1/2 cup vegetable oil
3 Tablespoons rice vinegar (unseasoned)
2 Tablespoons sugar
2 Tablespoons soy sauce
Flavor packet from soup
1 Tablespoon sesame seeds
1 Tablespoon sesame oil

Directions:
Toss salad ingredients together (ramen noodles are not precooked).  Mix dressing ingredients, pour over salad and mix well.
Refridgerate a least 3-4 hours or overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
CANELSON
Description: 
I've tried several variations of this recipe and like this one the best.  I grill extra breasts to set aside for this a couple of days later.  Great for a picnic lunch with a wonderful bread and a fresh melon salad
