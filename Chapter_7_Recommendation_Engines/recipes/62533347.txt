Title:
Individual Hot Sub Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Rolls
Soft Butter
8 Slices salami or boiled ham
8 Slices mozzarella cheese
8 Slices cooked turkey or chicken

Directions:
Heat oven to 425.  Cut rolls horizontally.  Spread cut surfaces with butter.  Place ham slice on bottom section of each roll and top with cheese slice.  Top with turkey slice.  Wrap each sandwich with foil; heat 15 to 20 minutes.
Garnish with lettuce & tomato if desired.

Submitter: 
Amy Thomas Link
Description: 
A good do-ahead.  Make them in the morning, wrap in foil and refrigerate until time to heat them.
