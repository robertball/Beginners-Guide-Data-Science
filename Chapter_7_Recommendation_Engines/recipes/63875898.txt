Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) package of cream cheese, softened
1 Cup of Brown Sugar
2 teaspoons of vanilla
2 1/4 Cup of Flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 (10 oz) package chocolate chips
1 pound confectioners chocolate

Directions:
If cream cheese isn't room temperature place in microwave for 30 sec. Blend together cream cheese, brown sugar and vanilla.
Add flour and mix until blended.
Sprinkle salt over the dough and pour on chocolate chips. Mix until blended.
Make balls of dough (your desired size) and place in freezer for 30 min.
Melt confectioners chocolate and dip the cookie balls in the melted chocolate. Place on foil or wax paper until hardened.
Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Amanda Nye
Description: 
This is an easy recipe for deliciously rich cookie truffles.
