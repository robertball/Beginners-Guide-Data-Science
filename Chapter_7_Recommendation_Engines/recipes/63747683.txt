Title:
Grandma Wintersteen's Gingersnaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c shortening
2 c sugar
1 egg
1/4 c molasses
2 c flour
2 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
1 T ginger
1 t cinnamon

Directions:
Beat together shortening and 1 cup sugar.  Add egg and molasses and mix well.
In a separate bowl, stir together remaining ingredients.  Add to sugar mixture and mix.
Roll dough into 1 inch balls, and roll each ball in the remaining sugar.  Place 2 inches apart on baking sheets and bake 10-12 minutes until tops are cracked.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
A recipe from a coworkers grandmother.  Just the right amount of soft and crisp.
