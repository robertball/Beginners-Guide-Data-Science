Title:
Chicken Crescents Italiano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut in to small bite sized pieces
4 T butter or margarine*
1 pkg.(0.7oz) dry italian dressing mix-start with half a package and adjust to your taste.
1 c shredded mozzarella cheese
2 pkgs.refrigerated crescent rolls

Directions:
In a skillet, melt the butter over med-low heat. Add the chicken and saute until the chicken turns white and is just cooked through. Remove from heat and add the dressing mix. Combine well. Lightly oil a cookie sheet.
Unroll the crescent rolls leaving 2 triangles intact to form a rectangle. Seal the seam. Spoon some of the chicken mixture on one half of the crescent roll. (Don't overfill). Top with shredded mozzarella. Fold over the crescent roll and seal the sides. Repeat with remaining crescent rolls. Bake according to crescent roll package..375 for 11-13 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Chicken, butter, italian dressing mix and mozzarella cheese are encased in crescent rolls and baked till golden brown.
