Title:
Lemon-Basil Orzo With Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup(s) orzo (rosamarina), uncooked
2 teaspoon oil, olive
2 teaspoon lemon peel, grated
2 clove(s) garlic, minced
1/4 cup(s) basil, fresh, chopped
1/2 ounce(s) cheese, Parmigiano-Reggiano, 2 tablespoons grated
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper, black ground
sprig(s) basil, fresh, (optional)

Directions:
Cook the orzo according to package directions, omitting the salt and fat. Drain.
