Title:
Indian Butter Chicken Sous Vide or Regular Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb chicken thighs, boneless skinless
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/4  tsp red pepper
1 tsp curry powder
1/2 tsp Tumeric
Sauce
3 tbsp butter
1 onion, minced (or pulse until very fine)
1 tbsp garlic, minced (3-4 cloves)
1 3/4 cup tomato sauce (15 oz) (or marinara sauce)
1 3/4 cup coconut milk (15 oz) OR
1 c half and half cream
OPTIONAL: 2-3 c fresh baby spinach roughly chopped
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper to taste
1 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
1 tbsp tandoori Masala (or curry powder, or curry paste)

Directions:
Make chicken: mix together olive oil, salt, pepper, and curry powder.
Put in chicken things and toss to coat.
Place coated chicken in a bag and vacuum seal or expel all air.
SOUS VIDE: heat water in sous vide machine to 145.
Place bag in water and cook for 2-3 hours
MAKE SAUCE
While chicken is cooking, melt butter in large saucepan on Medium.
Add onions and garlic and cook 5-7 minutes until onions are soft,
Add tomato sauce, cream, and seasonings for sauce.
Mix to combine.
Lower heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add chopped up cooked chicken thighs.
FINISH DISH: Taste and adjust seasonings.
Serve over hot basmati rice with naan bread for dipping.

Submitter: 
Rachael Barney Smith
Description: 
A rich, creamy, Indian-Spiced sauce with tomatoes, garlic, onion, cream and butter.
