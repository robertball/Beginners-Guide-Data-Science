Title:
Ham & Cheese Tortilla Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 Flour Tortillas
20 slices Turkey Ham (retangular)(Or any other deli meat)
6 8oz pkg cream cheese
1 8oz sour cream
1 pkg dry ranch mix
1 5oz can diced black olives
18 green onions diced
2 cups shredded cheddar
2-3 diced jalapenos, remove veins and seeds
1 tbsp garlic powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, except tortillas and ham.
Take a tortilla, place a slice of ham closer to the edge you will start rolling.
Add cheese mixture and spread on top of ham until it is completely covered leaving the far end of the tortilla clear of mixture.
Roll. The mixture should spread across the tortilla as you roll.
Set aside and complete rolling.
Let stand in fridge for 1-2 hours then slice into 1-1.5" rounds.
Arrange on serving tray or airtight container. Serve immediately or next day.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
A lady at my work used to make these. Huge hit with all the state employees! This recipe makes the PARTY amount.
