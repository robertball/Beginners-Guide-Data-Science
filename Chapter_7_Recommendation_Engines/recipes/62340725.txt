Title:
Chicken Ginger Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/391081.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons Hoisin sauce
1/4 cup chicken broth
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 teaspoons sesame oil (plus a little extra for drizzle)
1 tablespoon powdered (or fresh minced) ginger
1 teaspoon cornstarch
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
3 cups of stir fry vegetables (such as carrots, snap peas, broccoli, water chesnuts, bamboo shoots, or whatever you prefer!)
1 can cut chinese baby corn, drained
1 -1 1/2 lbs skinless boneless chicken grease cut into bite size strips.

Directions:
1. In a small bowl, mix the hoisin sauce, chicken broth, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger and corstarch, whisk together and set aside.
2. Heat 1 tbs vegetable oil, and small drizzle of sesame oil in pan add chicken and stir fry until cooked through, about 3-4 minutes depending on thickness, remove from pan and set aside on plate.
3. Heat remaining tbs of oil on medium-high heat.  Add the vegetables and stir fry, (starting with vegetables that take longest to cook), adding vegetables as you stir.
4. Once vegetables are cooked, add chicken and sauce, stir over medium heat until heated through, about 3 minutes.
