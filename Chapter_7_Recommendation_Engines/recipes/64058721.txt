Title:
Healthier Cheesy Ham Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package hash brown potatoes
2 cups cooked ham, diced
2 cups shredded low-fat sharp Cheddar cheese
1 onion, diced
1 (10.75 ounce) can  low-fat condensed cream of chicken soup
1 1/2 cups low-fat sour cream
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a large (2.8 litre) casserole dish.
Stir hash browns, ham, Cheddar cheese, onion, cream of chicken soup and sour cream in a large bowl; season with black pepper.
Transfer mixture to prepared baking dish.
Bake until bubbly and top is lightly browned, or about 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Ham, cheese and hash brown potatoes are baked in a creamy sauce for a delicious casserole that can be served for brunch or dinner. Use of low fat ingredients decreases the amount of fat, cholesterol and calories in an otherwise decadent dish!Modified from: Cheesy Ham Potato Bake
