Title:
Mexican Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 red onion, chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
1/2 c. water
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. chili powder (or to taste)
1 sm. can chopped green chilis
1 pkg. spaghetti (cooked according to directions)
1 pkg. Mexican Velveeta Cheese, cut into 12 slices

Directions:
Brown ground beef, add onion and pepper.  Stir and simmer for 5 minutes.  Add tomato sauce, water, garlic, oregano, chili powder and chilis.  Simmer on low heat for 20 minutes.  Mix in cooked spaghetti.  Turn into 9"x13" baking dish and top with slices of Mexican process cheese.  Cover tightly with aluminum foil.  Bake at 400 degrees for 10-15 minutes.  Take out of oven, remove foil, let rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
MTWOMAN
Description: 
I always have the ingredients for this recipe on hand.  When we have guests coming for dinner I will put this mildly zippy baked spaghetti dish together, pop it in the oven to bake alongside some garlic bread, then make a tossed salad.  I have time to relax with my guests.
