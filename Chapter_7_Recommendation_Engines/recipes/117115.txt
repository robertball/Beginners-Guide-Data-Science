Title:
Luscious Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992805.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, softened
2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
FILLING:
1 egg
1/2 cup sugar
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1/8 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon confectioners' sugar

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, cream butter and confectioners' sugar; gradually beat in flour. Press onto the bottom of an ungreased 8-in. x 4-in. x 2-in. loaf pan. Bake at 325 degrees F for 14-16 minutes or until set and the edges are lightly browned.
For filling, in a mixing bowl, beat the egg, sugar, lemon juice, flour and baking powder until frothy. Pour over warm crust. Bake for 18-22 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool on a wire rack. Dust with confectioners' sugar. Cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Marina Jines
Description: 
'These bars are wonderful as a snack or dessert,' says Marina Jines of Enid, Oklahoma. 'Beware-they're habit-forming! If you don't want to eat the extras, why not share them with a neighbor or a shut-in?'
