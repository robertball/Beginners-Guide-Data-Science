Title:
Mom's meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
meatball mixture:
1 lb. meat
1/2-3/4 c. bread crumbs
1/4 onion chopped
salt and pepper to taste
1 egg
Sauce:
1 can stewed tomatoes
1 small can tomato sauce
Italian seasoning

Directions:
Mix ingredients together, form into balls. Fry until brown. add sauce ingredients. cook for a few hours.
