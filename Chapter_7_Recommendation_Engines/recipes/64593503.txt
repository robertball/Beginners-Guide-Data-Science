Title:
Instant Pot Salted Chicken Quarters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lbs chicken leg and thigh quarters
2 tsp salt
2 T sugar
2 T wine
2 tsp soy sauce
2 T orange juice
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Combine 2 tsp salt with the 2 T sugar, rub all over chicken quarters.
Put another tsp salt in bottom of Instant Pot.
Add seasonings, orange juice, soy sauce , and wine.
Put lid on the pot, set to seal.
Press poultry button. Once Instant Pot preheats, it will take 15 minutes to cook.
Quick release steam from pressure cooker.
Preheat your oven on broiler setting.
Put chicken on a foil lined pan. Place 3-4 inches from broiler in oven to brown the skin, if desired.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
Melt in your mouth and super easy, this has a slightly teriyaki flavor.
