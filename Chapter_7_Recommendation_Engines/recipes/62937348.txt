Title:
Cheese and Spinach Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/727094.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. Unsalted butter
1/2 Onion finely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 10-oz package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry.
1 1/2 cups grated Gruyere
1 9" unbaked pie shell
3 large eggs, lightly beaten
1 1/2 cups milk
salt and pepper
pinch of ground nutmeg

Directions:
preheat oven to 375 F.  Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.  Saute onion, until tender, 5 minutes.  Add garlic, saute for 1 minute.  Transfer to a bowl and let cool.
Sprinkle onion mixture, spinach and gruyere over bottom of pie shell.  Beat eggs and milk together, season with salt, pepper and nutmeg.  Gently pour into crust.
Bake quiche for 40 to 45 minutes, until set and nicely browned.  Remove from oven and let set for 10 minutes.
