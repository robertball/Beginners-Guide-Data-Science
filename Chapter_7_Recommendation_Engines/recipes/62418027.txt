Title:
Grilled Turkey Drumsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 turkey legs
1/4 c butter/margarine, melted
1/3 c canola oil
1/3 c dry sherry wine
1/3 c soy sauce
1 garlic clove, minced
1/4 chopped fresh parsley
1/4 tsp salt and salt

Directions:
Place turkey legs in a large shallow dish or heavy-duty ziplock bag; combine butter and next 8 ingredients, stirring well. Pour over legs, cover and seal, chill for 8 hrs turning occassionally. Drain legs, reserveing marinade in heavy-duty aluminum foil. Grill, covered with grill lid, over medium heat (300-350 degrees) about 1 hour, turning turkey after 30mins. Remove drumsticks to grill, and continue cooking 20-30mins, basting frequently with reserved marinade.
