Title:
Cornish Hens with Grapes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cornish Hens cleaned and dressed
Salt and Pepper to taste
2 dozen green seedless grapes, split in half
1 sm onion chopped
1 toe garlic pressed
1/2 Chinese brown sauce (bead molasses)
Butter
2 tblsp Sweet Vermouth
1 tsp chopped chives

Directions:
Salt and pepper hens inside and out
Dust outside of hens with flour and brown in butter in an iron pot (dutch oven) on the top of stove. Add chopped onions and saute until golden.
Add vermouth and grapes and garlic.
Cover tightly and simmer until hens are tender
Check occasionally and add a little water as needed to keep from drying out.
Remove birds to hot platter and add Chines brown gravy sauce to juices and stir well. Pour sauce over birds. This may be ignited for a festive occasion. Use warm brandy or 151 proof rum and light. Serve with wild rice.

Submitter:

Description: 
My mother used to make this. A nice alternative to turkey for a small Thanksgiving dinner.
