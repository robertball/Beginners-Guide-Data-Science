Title:
Chocolate Coffee Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4301257.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup rolled oats
1 tablespoon chia seeds
3/4 cup freshly brewed coffee
2 tablespoons milk
2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 tablespoon maple syrup, or more to taste

Directions:
Combine oats and chia seeds in a microwave-safe bowl. Stir in coffee. Let stand for 2 minutes.
Cook oats in the microwave until tender, about 2 minutes. Stir in milk, cocoa powder, and maple syrup.

Description:

Well, we think you should take your oatmeal to the next level by adding Koffee Kult Coffee to your oatmeal instead of water. This oatmeal is so easy to make and it's full of caffeine.
