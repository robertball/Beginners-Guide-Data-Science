Title:
Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med. onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbl olive oil
4 C beef broth
4 C chicken broth
14.5-oz can chopped tomatoes
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
4-oz can green chili
2 C cooked chopped chicken
Frozen corn
Can of kidney beans, drained
Carrots, sliced and cooked

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic in oil.  Cook all ingredients in crock pot all day or just simmer in pot till heated through.  Top with shredded cheese and crumbled tortilla chips.
