Title:
Beef Tenderloin with Black Olive Roasted Red Pepper Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978359.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons Market Pantry Butter™, softened
2 tablespoons Tassos™ Black Olive Bruschetta Topping
4 tablespoons diced roasted red peppers
4 (6 ounce) pieces Sutton & Dodge™ Filet Mignon
Archer Farms™ Steak Seasoning
black pepper

Directions:
Preheat broiler or grill.
Combine butter, black olive bruschetta topping and peppers in a small bowl and mash together with a fork to combine well. Season to taste with black pepper.
Brush steaks with a little olive oil and season liberally on both sides with Archer Farms steak seasoning.
Grill or broil steaks to desired doneness.
While steaks are still hot, top each steak with a dollop of prepared butter and serve as it melts over the steak.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
Gourmet-style compound butter - fresh butter flavored with black olives and roasted red peppers - melts into the top of fork-tender grilled filet mignon. Extra butter? It's also delicious with broiled fish, roasted chicken or even baked potatoes.
