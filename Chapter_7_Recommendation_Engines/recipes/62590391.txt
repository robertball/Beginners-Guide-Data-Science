Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough for Ice Cream (Eggless) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
5 tablespoons brown sugar
6 tablespoons white sugar
4 tablespoons butter, softened
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 tablespoons whole milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together the brown sugar, white sugar, and butter until smooth. Stir in vanilla and milk. Mix in the flour and salt until well blended. Stire in chocolate chips. Shape into a loaf or log, and freeze for 1 to 2 hours.
Cut into small chunks, and mix into softened ice cream. Freeze for 15 minutes or until firm before serving.
