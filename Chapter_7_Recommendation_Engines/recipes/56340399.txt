Title:
Slow Cooker Sweet 'n' Sour Wings/Drumsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. chicken wings or drumsticks
1 cup brown sugar, packed
1/4 cup flour
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup white vinegar
1 1/2 tbsp. ketchup
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1 tbsp. onion flakes
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. prepared mustard

Directions:
Mix brown sugar and flour in saucepan. Add water, vinegar and ketchup and stir
Add remaining 5 ingredients. Heat and stir until boiling and thickened
Pour over wings and drumsticks
Cover and cook on low in slow cooker for 8-9 hours or on high for 4 hours
