Title:
Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium tomatoes
2 jalapenos (or more)
1/2 cilantro bushel
1/4 onion, or to taste
2 teaspoons salt
dried oregano

Directions:
Boil water in 1 medium pot, and 1 small pot.
Add tomatoes to medium pot, and jalapenos to smaller pot.  Boil until skin begins to tear from tomatoes.  Set aside and let cool.
Meanwhile, chop onion and cilantro.
Rip skin off tomatoes and pulse them briefly in a blender until chunky.  Pour into a bowl, and add the jalapenos, onions, and cilantro a little at a time until your desired hotness level.
Add salt and 2 teaspoons oregano.
If salsa lacks flavor, add more salt and oregano.
