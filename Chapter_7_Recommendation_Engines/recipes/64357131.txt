Title:
Pumpkin Whoopie Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups packed brown sugar
1 cup vegetable oil
2 cups solid pack pumpkin puree (16 oz can)
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Sift together:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp ground cloves
White frosting:
2 egg white
2 tablespoons milk
sprinkle of salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups confectioners' sugar
3/4 cup shortening
Variance: add marshmallow Fluff as desired reducing some confectioners sugar.
Cream Cheese Frosting:
2 3oz pkt cream cheese softened
1/2 cup butter
2 tsp vanilla extract
Beat 'till fluffy
5 cups Powdered sugar (start with 4 and add as needed to achieve a spreadable consistency.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease baking sheets.
Combine the oil and brown sugar. Mix in the pumpkin and eggs, ( all upper ingredients) beating well.
Add the flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, ginger and cloves (lower ingredients). Mix well.
Drop dough by heaping teaspoons onto the prepared baking sheets.
Bake at 375 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 8 to 10 minutes.
Let cookies cool then make sandwiches from two cookies filled with Whoopie Pie Filling.
To Make Whoopie Pie Filling:
Beat egg white and mix with the milk, 1 teaspoon vanilla and 1 cup of the confectioners' sugar. Mix well then beat in the shortening and the remaining cup of confectioners' sugar. Beat until light and fluffy.
See notes above for variations.
