Title:
Carrot & Turnip Puree Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs carrots, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
2 lbs turnips, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
1/2 cup 0% plain Greek yogurt
1/4 teaspoon ginger
1 tablespoon softened butter, optional
salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Cook carrots and turnips in boiling salted water for 30 to 40 minutes or until tender.
Drain and transfer to a food processor or blender. Add yogurt, ground ginger, and softened butter. Process until smooth.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
