Title:
Cindy's Best Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/309419.jpg
Ingredients:
36 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips (3 12 oz bags)
1 pint marshmallow cream
2 sticks butter (no subs)
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups walnuts (or your choice of nuts)
4 1/2 c. white sugar
1 12oz can evaporated milk

Directions:
Place the first 5 ingredients in a very large heat-safe bowl, set aside.
Bring the sugar & evaporated milk to a rolling boil in a very large pot, then turn down to medium and continue to boil for exactly 9 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Pour hot mixture over ingredients in bowl and mix well, making sure all the butter and marshmallow is well blended.
Pour into 10x15 buttered (or parchment paper lined) jelly roll pan and refrigerate.  Cut into 1 inch squares and serve.  Freezes well!

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
This fudge is very creamy and rich.  One little piece will put you in heaven!!
