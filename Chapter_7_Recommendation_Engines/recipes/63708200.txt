Title:
Quinoa Vanilla Bean Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Quinoa, rinsed very well and drained
3 Cups Fat Free Milk
1/2 Cup Heavy Cream
1 T Vanilla Bean Paste
1 cup Splenda
1/4 t salt
2 eggs
1 T Light Butter

Directions:
In pot over medium heat add milk and whipping cream.  Heat to boiling.  Add Splenda, Salt and Quinoa.  Reduce heat to simmer for 45 minutes.  Whisk eggs together and add some of the hot mixture into the eggs little by little to temper the eggs.  Then add eggs mixture to pot.  Cook an additonal 5 minutes.  Remove from heat and add vanilla bean paste and butter.  Mix well.  Serve warm or chill.
