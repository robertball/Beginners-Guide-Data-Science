Title:
Quick chicken pot pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2-2 cups of cooked chicken-cubed
1 can of cream of chicken soup
1 cup chicken stock
1 cup cooked cubed potato
1 cup canned/frozen veggie of your choice
1 cup flour
1 cup milk
1 stick butter
Spices to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Combine soup and stock in a bowl.
In a casserole dish mix chicken, potatoes, veggies.  Pour in soup/stock mixture over top.
Season to taste with salt, pepper and sage.
In a bowl make a runny dough combining the flour, milk and butter.
Pour this over top of mixture and bake in oven for 45-50 mins-until crust is golden.
*you can substitute cream of mushroom for cream of chicken soup.
*any veggies work well, corn, peas, carrots, etc
*homemade stock works the best...if you use cubes/packaged be careful adding salt.

Submitter: 
CraZee A
Description: 
Quick and easy comfort food, and a good (tasty!)use for leftover chicken.  Almost any ingredient can be substituted, so you can make it with what you have on hand.  Thanks go to my friend Smantha for sharing this :-)
