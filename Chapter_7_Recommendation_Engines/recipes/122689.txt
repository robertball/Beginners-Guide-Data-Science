Title:
Zucchini Carrot Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976493.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.5 ounce) package carrot cake mix
1 egg
1/2 cup applesauce
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups shredded zucchini
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the cake mix, egg, applesauce and oil; mix well.  stir in the zucchini, raisins and pecans.  Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups three-fourths full.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until muffins test done.

Submitter: 
Anita  Sterrett
Description: 
In Anchorage, Alaska, Anita Sterrett uses carrot cake mix to stir up these moist muffins chock-full of zucchini, nuts and raisins. 'They make great snacks and are wonderful for dessert when spread with cream cheese frosting,' she notes.
