Title:
Chuy's Jalapeno Ranch Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2969226.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups mayonnaise
1/3 cup buttermilk
1/3 cup chopped jalapeno
1/3 cup green chili
1/3 cup cilantro leaf
1 (1 ounce) packet dry ranch dressing mix

Directions:
Mix ingredients in blender or food processor, until smooth.
Serve with tortilla chips, or as a salad dressing.
