Title:
30-Minute Pepperoni Pizza Soup in Bread Bowls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1111350.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes with roasted garlic
4 cups water
2 Tbsp low sodium chicken base
3 Tbsp tomato paste
2 Tbsp diced sun-dried tomatoes (packed in oil)
2 Tbsp dried Italian seasoning
2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 (5 oz) package mini pepperoni
4 oz fresh shredded Parmesan cheese
(optional garnish) shredded mozzarella
(optional) store bought or homemade bread bowls

Directions:
Combine the first 8 ingredients in an 8 qt pot over medium high heat. Stir to combine. Bring to a boil.
Add pepperoni (reserve a few for garnish if desired). Stir. Reduce to medium heat and boil for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in Parmesan cheese. Remove from heat.
Meanwhile: Cut a 3‚Äù circle in the top of bread bowls. Remove most of bread inside.
Pour soup into bread bowl.
Garnish with a pinch of mozzarella cheese.
Serve and enjoy.

Description: 
Creating this Pepperoni Pizza Soup was so easy and it was so good it was devoured in no time flat.  A perfectly seasoned tomato soup base with mini pepperoni and loads of Parmesan cheese make this our perfect pizza soup.  Stir in your favorite toppings to make it your favorite pizza soup, but around here Munchkin is a pepperoni pizza fan.
