Title:
Meatball Madness Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Olive oil
1-2 Large onions, diced
Salt
4 Cloves Garlic
1 pound ground beef
1 pound ground veal
1 pound ground pork
4 large eggs
2 cups fresh parmigiano
1/2 cup freshly chopped Italian parsley leaves
2 cups Italian breadcrumbs
1 cup water

Directions:
1. Add olive oil to pan and saute onions on medium high heat.  Season onions generously with salt and cook 5-7 minutes until soft with no color.  Add garlic and saute for another 1 to 2 minutes.  Remove from heat and let cool.
2.  In large bowl combine meats, eggs, Parmigiano, parsley and bread crumbs.  Squish the mixture with you hands.  Add the onion mixture and season generously with salt and squish more.  Add water and squish until incorporated.  The mix should be quite wet.
3. Preheat oven to 350
4.  Shape meatballs to golf ball size or larger.  Coat sauce pan with olive oil and bring to high heat.  Brown meatballs on all sides, but do not cook completely.
5.  Place seared meatballs on cookie sheet with foil.  Bake them for approximately 15 minutes, or until cooked completely.
6.  Serve with your favorite marinara sauce over pasta or zoodles.
