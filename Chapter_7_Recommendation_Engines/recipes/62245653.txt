Title:
Zuppa Toscana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground Italian sausage
OR
1/2 lb ground Italian sausage and 1/2 ground hot Italian sausage (based on personal preference)
1 medium white or yellow onion
6 to 8 slices bacon crumbled
3 tsp garlic puree
10 cups low sodium chicken stock
1 cup heavy cream
1 1/4 lb sliced potatoes with skin left on (approximately 4 large potatoes)
1/4 bunch kale
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute Italian sausage in a large pot.  Drain excess fat and set sausage aside.
In the same pan, saute bacon, onions and garlic until bacon is crispy and onion is translucent.
Add the chicken stock to the onions, bacon and garlic and cook until boiling.
Add potatoes and cook until soft - approximately 30 minutes.
Add heavy cream and cook until thorougly heated (do not let it come to a boil).
Stir in the sausage.
Chop kale into large pieces and add to soup.  Cook kale until slightly wilted.
