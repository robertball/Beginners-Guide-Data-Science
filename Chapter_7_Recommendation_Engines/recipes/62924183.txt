Title:
Bison Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs - Ground Bison
(1) medium Onion (Diced)
(3) 15.25oz cans - kidney Beans
(2) 15oz cans - Crushed Tomatoes
(1) 10.75oz can - Condensed Tomato Soup
1/4 cup - White Vinegar
3-1/2 Tbsp - Chili Powder
2 tsp - Chocolate Sea Salt
3 tsp - Cinnamon (Vietnam Extra Fancy)

Directions:
In large stove pot, brown meat. Add diced onion and cook until onion softens. Stir in all canned ingredients (do not drain canned ingredients, add them all as they are). Add Chili Powder, Chocolate Sea Salt, and Cinnamon, missing as you each of the different spices.
Turn temperature to low and simmer for 60 minutes, stiring often.

Submitter: 
David S. Novak
Description: 
The chocolate sea salt and cinnamon really bring out the flavor of this recipe.
