Title:
Crisp Oven Baked Fries or Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large potatoes, cut as fries or wedges
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon of olive oil

Directions:
Soak potatoes in ice water for 30 minutes
Preheat oven to 450 degrees
Remove potatoes and dry off with paper towel (important)
In a large plastic bag, add all dry ingredients.  Place potatoes in bag, seal, and mix thoroughly.
Open bag and add olive oil. Seal bag and mix again coating all potatoes
Arrange potatoes on foil lined baking sheets sprayed with cooking spray
Bake for 35 minutes or until golden brown

Submitter: 
Ron Perry
Description: 
This is a quick and healthy way to have crispy fries (or wedges) without all the cooking grease.
