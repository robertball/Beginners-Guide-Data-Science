Title:
No-Bake Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 premade 9 inch graham cracker crust
8 oz. package cream cheese
1/2 c. sour cream
1/3 c. sugar
1/2 t. vanilla
4 oz. cool whip

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except pie crust,pour into prepared graham crust, chill 4 hours. Top with Cherry pie filling and whipped cream, if desired.

Submitter: 
Cindy Carnes
Description: 
This is a simple, fast dessert for when you are in a hurry and need something to fix.
