Title:
Caramel No-Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/630305.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
30 caramel candy squares, remove wrappers
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup milk
3 cups quick oats
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup coconut (optional)

Directions:
In a large sauce pan combine sugars, caramels, butter and milk.
Bring to a boil stirring constantly for three minutes.  Remove from heat and add the oats, peanut butter and the optional coconut while stirring.
Mix thoroughly then drop onto waxpaper lined cookie sheets by teaspoons.
Allow a few hours for cookies to harden.

Submitter: 
Jackie C
Description: 
Similar to the traditional chocolate no-bake cookie but with a caramel twist instead of chocolate.  These are requested of me often.  I make these all the time and freeze them pulling out a few at a time.  They freeze very well.
