Title:
Healthified Sweet Potato Napoleons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large sweet potatoes (washed and sliced in 1/2 inch slices)
Butter flavored Pam cooking spray
2 large blocks softened fat-free cream cheese
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 - 3 tsp Splenda
Pumpkin pie spice
Splenda brown sugar blend

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Place sweet potato slices on a prepared cookie sheet
Bake flat until just tender; about 10 - 15 minutes
Meanwhile, combine the cream cheese, vanilla, and Splenda in a small bowl until well blended.
Spread half of the potato slices with the cream cheese mixture.
Sprinkle some pumpkin pie spice on the top of the cheese mixture - to taste.
Then top that with the remaining naked potato slices of similar size.
Spray the tops with butter flavored Pam and sprinkle with brown sugar Splenda.
Bake until cheese melts.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
This is a fantastic healthier option for sweet potato or yam casserole. I created it by necessity due to becoming a new diabetic. It's almost fat free and sugar free! I substitute cream cheese, vanilla, and Splenda for the marshmallows you usually find in yams during the hollidays.
