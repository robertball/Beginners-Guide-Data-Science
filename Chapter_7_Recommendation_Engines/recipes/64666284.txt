Title:
Protein Powder Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. Oats
1 c. Flour
2/3 c. Vanilla Protein Powder
1/2 c. Pumpkin or Apple Sauce
1 Egg
1/4 C. Peanut Butter
1 tsp. Baking Sod
1/2 T. Vanilla
2 T. Mini Chocolate Chips (optional)

Directions:
1. Mix all the ingredients together, adding chocolate chips in last.
2. Bake at 350 degrees for about 10-12 minutes on a sprayed cookie sheet. Press each cookie down with a fork to make crisscross lines

Tiffany Donnelly
Description: 
High protein, low sugar, great snack cookie.
