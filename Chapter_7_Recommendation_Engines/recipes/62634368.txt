Title:
Imitation Crab Dip and Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/578992.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz Cream Cheese (softened)
4 oz light Havarti cheese, shredded
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tbsp chives
2 tsp horseradish
8-10 oz imitation crab meat, flaked
1 cup packed baby spinach, shredded
Paprika, to taste
Crackers or bread sticks for dipping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
mix cream cheese, sour cream, mayo, horseradish and chives
gently fold in crab meat and spinach
Sprinkle with paprika, if desired
bake in quart sized baking dish for about 25 minutes

Submitter: 
Pam Morin
Description: 
I make a batch of this for a party (or double if it's a big party!) and reserve about 1/2 a cup per lb of haddock to make baked stuffed rolled haddock the next day.  Don't bake the portion you reserve for the haddock.
