Title:
Big Ray's Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1090477.jpg
Ingredients:
7 cups thinly sliced cucumbers
3 Tbl sugar
1 large onion, thinly sliced
1 medium green pepper, thinly sliced
1 cup apple cider vinegar
1 tsp salt
1 Tbl celery seed

Directions:
In a large serving bowl, combine all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Serve with a slotted spoon.

Submitter:

Description: 
What do you do when the garden gives an abundance of produce? Here is a great salad to use up those cucumbers.
