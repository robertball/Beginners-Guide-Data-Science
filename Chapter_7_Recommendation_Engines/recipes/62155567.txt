Title:
Party Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg.(20 oz.) frozen cauliflower florets
1 pkg.(20 oz.) frozen broccoli florets
4 Tblsp. butter
3 Tblsp. flour
3 cups milk
6oz.(1 1/2 cups) shredded cheddar cheese
4 oz. (1 cup) grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 tsp. salt
3 cups ham - chopped
3 cups fresh bread crumbs, toosed with 4 tblsp. melted butter

Directions:
Cook cauliflower and broccoli in boiling salted water until slightly underdone. Drain and set aside. Melt butter in 1 qt. saucepan,stir in flour and blend well. Gradually add milk,stirring constatntly until thickened. Add Cheddar,Parmesan,and salt, and stir over low heat until cheese melts. Place vegetables in an ungreased 4 qt. casserole dish and sprinkle with chopped ham. Pour cheese sauce over all. Make a border of buttered crumbs around edge of casserole. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 20-30 minutes or until crumbs are lightly browned.
