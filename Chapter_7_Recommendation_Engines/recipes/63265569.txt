Title:
Creamy Garlic Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz soft cream cheese
1 generous tsp roasted chopped garlic
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/3 c shredded parmesan
1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Devour.

Submitter: 
ChefMom
Description: 
The best, most versatile, garlic cream cheese spread, ever. Excellent on sourdough, in stuffed mushrooms, or stuffing chicken. I have never found a person who didn't rave about this recipe.
