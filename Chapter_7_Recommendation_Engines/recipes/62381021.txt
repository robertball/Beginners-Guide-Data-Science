Title:
Simple and Succulent Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large fillet of salmon
2-3 tsp lemon pepper
2-3 tsp butter (softened)
the juice of 1/2 a lemon
1 1/2 tspsalt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large baking pan, line with foil and place salmon in the foil. Use enough foil to create a tent over the salmon.
Season salmon on both sides with lemon pepper and salt. Spread butter in a thin layer over both sides of the salmon.
Fold foil to create a tent over the salmon, and bake for approximately 25 minutes.
Open the foil tent and and bake for an additional 5 minutes.
Enjoy.
