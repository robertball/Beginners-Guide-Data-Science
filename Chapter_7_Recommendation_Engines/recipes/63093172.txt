Title:
Gingerbread party mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3c Cinnamon Chex
3c Rice chex
1 1/2c cinnamon grahams
6 Tbs butter
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4tsp ground cloves
1/4tsp ground nutmag
2tsp vanilla
2 Tbs crystallized ginger chopped
1/4 c while vanilla chips

Directions:
1. large microwave bowl add cereals and grahams snack, set aside.  In small microwave bowl, microwave butter on high 40 sec. Stir in cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg and vanilla until blended.  Pour over cereal and stir until coated.
2. Microwave on high 5 min, stirring every 2 minutes, stir in ginger until blended.  Spread mixture on paper towels.
3. Microwave white vanilla chips 1 min. stiring every 30 seconds until melted./  Drizzle over snack mix, let stand until set.
