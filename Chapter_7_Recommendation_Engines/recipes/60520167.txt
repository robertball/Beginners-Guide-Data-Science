Title:
Pumpkin Bread IV Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups canned pumpkin puree
3/4 cup vegetable oil
3/4 applsauce
2 cups white sugar
2 sups brown sugar
6 eggs
4 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 1/2 teaspoons ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp. ginger
3/4 teaspoons ground cloves

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour three 9x5 inch loaf pans.
In a large bowl, mix together the pumpkin, oil, applaesauce, sugar, and eggs. Combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves; stir into the pumpkin mixture until well blended. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour. The top of the loaf should spring back when lightly pressed.
