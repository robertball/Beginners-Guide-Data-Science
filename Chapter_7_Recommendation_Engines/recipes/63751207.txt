Title:
Cindi's "White Castle" Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1059008.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs. ground beef
1 lb. Velveeta cheese (cubed)
2 small onions, finely chopped (or 1 cup frozen diced onions)
1 package dry onion soup mix
24-36 dill pickle slices
24-36 small buns

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit.
In a large pan on medium high heat, brown ground beef and drain off fat.
Stir in soup mix, cheese, and onion until cheese melts.
Cut buns in half.
Spread beef mixture on buns.  Top with a pickle slice.  Wrap each slider individually in foil and place on metal tray.
Bake sliders for at 350 degrees Fareneheit for 15-20 minutes.  Serve immediately.
