Title:
Hot Rice Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground chuck
1 med onion- peeled and chopped
2/3 C finely chopped celery
3/4 C white rice
1 can mushrooms- chopped up
1 can cr. of mushroom soup
1 can cr of chicken soup
1 can water
4 T soy sauce
2 t garlic powder
1/2 t ground black pepper
1 can sliced water chestnuts- rinsed and chopped in quarters.

Directions:
Brown Ground beef,half way through the browning stage, add chopped onions and celery.  Drain and put in slowcooker.  Add the rest of the ingredients mix well, cover and cook on low for approx. 6 1/2 hours.

Description: 
This is one of our favorites that Grandma makes!  Never any leftovers.
