Title:
Whole Wheat Date Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/361376.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
1/3 cup butter
1 egg
8 oz. lowfat plain yogurt
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a mixer or food processor, cream the butter, sugar and egg together until a creamy consistancy.
In another bowl, combine whole wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt together.
Alternating with the flour mixture and the yogurt, add to the creamed mixture
Fold in dates
Spray muffin pan with non-stick cooking spray or line with muffin cups and fill each 2/3 full
Bake for approximately 20 minutes

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
These muffins made out of whole wheat are very moist and light.  The dates give them a sweet flavor.
