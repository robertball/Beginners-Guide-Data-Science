Title:
Zucchini Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Small straight Zucchini
8 slices Thick Bacon
4 slices Smoked Fontina Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Cut off ends of Zucchini and slice lengthwise into strips slightly less than 1/4 inch (discarding the outsides).
Place Zucchini directly on oven rack near the bottom.
Cut cheese in half lengthwise.
Cook Bacon on paper towels in the microwave (3 min. for 8 slices) and pat dry.
Remove softened Zucchini from oven(don't turn off oven).
Layer: Zucchini, Bacon, Cheese.
Roll tightly and secure with toothpicks or skewers.
Place Roll-Ups on an oven safe tray, and cook for 10 min.
