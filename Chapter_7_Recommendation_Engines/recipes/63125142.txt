Title:
Spicy Cajun Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh or frozen large shrimp in shells
1/2 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
2 tbs tomato paste
1 tbs lemon juice
1/2 tsp bottled minced garlic (1 clove)
2 tbs butter or margarine, melted
4 tsp cajun seasoning

Directions:
Thaw shrimp, if frozen.  Peel and devein shrimp, leaving tals intact.  Rinse shrimp; pat dry with paper towels.  In a small bowl stir together mayonnaise, tomato paste, lemon juice, and garlic.  Cover and chill until ready to serve.
Brush both sides of each shrimp with melted butter and sprinkle both sides with cajun seasoning.  Place on the unheated rack of a broiler pan.
Broil 4-5 inches from the heat for 2 minutes.  Turn shrimp; broil for 1-2 minutes more or until shrimp are opaque.  Serve shrimp with mayonnaise mixture.
