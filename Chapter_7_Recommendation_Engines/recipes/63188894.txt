Title:
Girl's Chicken Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Small Ciabatta Rolls
4 Small Chicken Breasts, Pounded to uniform thickness
Thinly Sliced Red Onion
2 Cups Baby Spinach
1 Pink Lady or Fuji Apple, cored and thinly sliced
1 lb Brie
Apricot Jam
Dijon Mustard

Directions:
Slice rolls, season chicken with salt and pepper.
Grill Chicken until just done; remove to dish, cover with brie, and keep warm.  Toast rolls on grill.
To assemble: Spread mustard on bottom and Jam on top of roll.  Layer on bottom - Spinach, Chicken/Cheese, Apple, Onion, top of roll.
Serve immediately.
