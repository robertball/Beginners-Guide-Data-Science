Title:
Sharyn's Apricot-Mustard seared Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound) pork tenderloin or less - we did 8 oz for 2 people
3 tablespoons reduced sugar apricot preserves
1/4 cup mustard country mustard with mustard seed
White wine to thin & extend sauce
Cilantro garnish

Directions:
Season tenderloin with salt and pepper. Stir together the preserves and mustard in a small bowl. Sear pork - all sides in small amount of olive oil. Cover with sauce and place in oven at 400 degrees for 10 minutes. Remove pork from oven - remove pork from skillet. Add white & more sauce and cook until thickened. Return pork to warm & then slice and return to pan to serve.
pork should be pink in center - 145 degrees.
