Title:
Healthy Blue Cheese Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup light mayonaisse
6 oz container no fat Greek yogurt
1/2 tsp oregano
1 1/2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
1 teaspoon canola oil
salt and pepper to taste
4 tablespoons crumbled reduced fat blue cheese

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients but blue cheese together.  Add blue cheese and stir to mix.  Refrigerate for 3 hours before using.

Description: 
Reduced fat recipe for blue cheese dressing.  Has approximately 45 calories per tablespoon of dressing.
