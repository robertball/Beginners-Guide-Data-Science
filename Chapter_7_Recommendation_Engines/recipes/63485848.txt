Title:
No fuss lemon pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large eggs
1/2 bottle of minute maid premium lemon juice (3 3/4 oz)
1 1/4 cups of sugar
1/4 cup butter melted
1 unbacked 9inch pastry shell

Directions:
Combine first 3 ingredients in blender; blend 3 minutes or until smooth. Add butter; blend 30 seconds. Pour into pastry shell. Back at 350 degree for 30-35 mins
9 in pie can sereve with icecream or frozen yogurt
