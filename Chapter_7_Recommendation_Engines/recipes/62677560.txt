Title:
Fancy Pear Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/599632.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup milk
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese
1 pear, thinly sliced
2 Tbls Butter
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Add milk, flour, and salt to eggs.
Spray 6 custard cups or muffin tins with cooking spray
Place a small pat of  butter in each.  Sprinkle with brown sugar, nutmeg and cheese.  Top with pear.
Evenly distribute batter into cups.
Bake 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.
