Title:
Vicki's Stuffed Cabbage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground round
1 sm head cabbage, diced
1 onion, chopped
1 clove garlic
1/2 c uncooked instant rice
1/2 ketchup
1 can tomato soup
1 can water
1/3 C. crushed ginger snaps
2 c cheese
salt
pepper

Directions:
Brown ground beef, onion, and garlic. Season with salt and pepper.
Add tomato soup, water, rice and ginger snaps.
Layer beef mixture with cabbage and cheese. (Cabbage, sauce, cheese, repeat)
Bake, covered at 350 for 1 hour.
Remove cover and bake 15 minutes.
Let sit for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ruth Rae
Description: 
This is a quick, inexpensive, and filling casserole for the cold winter months.
