Title:
Cliff`s Hot Dog Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2399062.jpg
Ingredients:
1- # ground beef
1- 14.5 ounce can of diced tomatoes
1- 6 once can of tomato paste
1/2 cup water
2 tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons chili powder
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons black pepper
2 teaspoons onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon mustard powder
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
Dash hot sauce
Dash of Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
In large stock pot brown ground beef
Add rest of ingredients
Bring to boil
Turn heat down to simmer
Cover and simmer for two hours stirring occasionally

Submitter: 
Cliff G
Description: 
Serve on Hot Dogs with onion, mustard, relish or your favorite topping.
