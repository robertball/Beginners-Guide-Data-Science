Title:
Teriyaki Thai Chicken Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/5577979.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Kikkoman Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce
2 tablespoons garlic, crushed
2 teaspoons ground black pepper
2 teaspoons curry powder
3 pounds chicken pieces (legs, thighs, breast halves)*

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, mixing well. Marinate chicken* for 4 hours or overnight. Grill following your favorite method.

Submitter: 
Kikkoman
Description: 
Curry powder and ground black pepper kick up this prepared teriyaki marinade for grilled chicken.
