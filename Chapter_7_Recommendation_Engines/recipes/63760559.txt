Title:
Sweet Habanero Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Vidalia Onion - sliced into rings or crescents
1 Habanero Peppers - de-seeded & diced
4 TBS Butter or Margarine
2 tsp Brown Sugar
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Slice the onions into rings or crescents
De-seed & dice the habaneros carefully - do not touch eyes
Add to medium saute pan with brown sugar and 2TBS of butter
Salt & Pepper
Saute until onions begin to turn translucent - about 5-8 minutes
Add additional butter and saute until onion caramelizes in color
Remove from pan & serve as a condiment
Add rest of butter

Submitter: 
Doug Matthews
Description: 
Sweet Heat for your burgers, hot dogs or anything else you would normally put some sauteed or grilled onions on.
