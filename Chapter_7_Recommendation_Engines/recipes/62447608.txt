Title:
Perhaps the Best Salsa Ever?? Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. can petite diced tomatoes
1/2 green bell pepper diced
1/2 fresh onion diced
1 tsp. garlic
1 tsp. white vinegar
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. finely diced jalepenos
1 tsp. cilantro
pinch of sugar

Directions:
Drain about halfway the can of tomatoes, and pour in a medium size glass bowl.  Dice bell pepper and onion, and pour in bowl.  Measure vinegar and cilantro, and put in bowl.  Add a pinch of sugar, and stir everything around.  Optional: Put in fridge for 20 min. for it to chill completely

Submitter: 
NancyH
Description: 
Even my daughter, who doesn't like anything with whole tomatoes in it, loves this recipe for salsa.  Everywhere that I have made it for people, they are ALWAYS asking for this recipe! I have enjoyed it, and hope you do too!
