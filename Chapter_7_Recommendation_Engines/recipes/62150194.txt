Title:
simple cucumber salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch radishes sliced
4-5 pickling cucumbers sliced
1 red onion sliced
1 lemon
4 tbsp apple cider vinegar or wine vinegar
2 tsp salt
pepper
1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
combine all vegetables and mix the lemon juice,vinegar,salt,pepper,olive oil in seperate bowl and pour over top.
