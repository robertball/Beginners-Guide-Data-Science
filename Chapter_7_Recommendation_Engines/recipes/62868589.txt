Title:
Seafood Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 big flour tortillas, cut into strips
Vegetable oil
1/4 c. margarine
1/2 c. diced onion
1/4 c. flour
2 c. milk
1 lb. crab chunks
15 oz. shrimp
16 oz. mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Fry tortilla's in oil until golden brown. Set aside.
Saute the onion in margarine until tender; add flour to make a roux.
Whisking, add the milk. Continue to whisk as you bring the mixture to a boil and boil HARD for one minute.
Remove from heat. Add seafood and stir.
Layer ingredients in a greased 9"x13" pan as so; tortilla, creamy filling, cheese, tortilla, filling, cheese.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 45-60 minutes. Uncover and bake another 15 minutes for a golden brown cheese top.
