Title:
LOW SUGAR OR NOT, ICING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 envelope of Dream Whip Topping Mix
1 cup soda (diet or not)

Directions:
Mix dry Dream Whip and soda.
Whip until it forma peaks.
Frost cake when cake is cool.
Keep in fridge.

Submitter: 
Julie Jane Anderson
Description: 
If it is severed right after frosting the cake, it will be fluffy. If is is refrigerated for a while it will be flatter, but it is a great low sugar frosting.
