Title:
Pasta e Fagioli soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 small onion diced, 1 cup
1 large carrot Juliannes, 1 cup
3 stalks celery chopped, 1 cup
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 14.5-ounce cans Italian diced tomatoes
1 15-ounce can red kidney beans, with liquid
1 15-ounce can great northern beans, with liquid
1 15-ounce can tomato sauce
24 ounces V-8 juice
1 tsp white vinegar
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp basil
1 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 lb ditali pasta, 1/2 box

Directions:
Brown ground beef and drain off grease.
Add onions, carrots, celery, and garlic and saut√© for 10 minutes.
Add the rest of the ingredients except pasta and simmer for 1 hour.
Cook pasta al dente and add to the pot 10 minutes before serving.
Garnish with shredded parmasean and enjoy!
