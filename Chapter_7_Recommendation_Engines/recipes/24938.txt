Title:
Raspberry Sauce  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979080.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen raspberries - thawed and drained, juice reserved
1/4 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons cornstarch
2 tablespoons raspberry flavored liqueur

Directions:
Add enough water to reserved juice to measure 1 cup. Stir together sugar and cornstarch in 1-quart saucepan. Stir in juice and raspberries.
Heat to boiling over medium heat. Boil and stir 1 minute; strain. Stir in liqueur.  Cool slightly before serving.  Refrigerate any remaining sauce.
