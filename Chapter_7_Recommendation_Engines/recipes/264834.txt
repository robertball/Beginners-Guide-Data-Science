Title:
Apricot Moscow Mule Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5418661.jpg
Ingredients:
Ginger Simple Syrup:
1/8 cup chopped fresh ginger
1/8 cup white sugar
1/8 cup boiling water
Moscow Mule:
crushed ice
3 fluid ounces vodka
1 1/2 fluid ounces freshly squeezed lime juice
1/2 apricot, thinly sliced
4 fluid ounces club soda
1 lime wedge

Directions:
Put ginger, sugar, and boiling water in a blender; let stand 5 minutes. Blend into a smooth syrup.
Fill a copper mug with crushed ice. Add ginger simple syrup, vodka, lime juice, and apricot. Top with club soda. Squeeze lime wedge over top and drop into mug. Stir well.

Submitter: 
Juliana Hale
Description: 
The sour formula is the root of many cocktails, including margaritas and mules, perhaps because it's so easy to remember and so simple to adapt. All you need is: 1 part sweet, 1 part sour, and 2 parts alcohol.
