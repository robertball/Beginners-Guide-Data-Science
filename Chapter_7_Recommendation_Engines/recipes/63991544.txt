Title:
Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
2/3 cup diced onion
1 1/2 cups uncooked white rice (calrose)
2 teaspoon ground cumin
2 teaspoon chili powder
1 can diced tomatoes (undrained)
3 clove garlic, minced
1/8 teaspoon powdered saffron or turmeric
2 pkts Goya with saffron
16 oz chicken broth

Directions:
In a large saucepan, heat vegetable oil over a medium-low heat. Place the onions in the pan, and saute until golden.
Add rice to pan, and stir to coat grains with oil. Mix in cumin, chili powder, tomatoes, garlic, saffronor turmeric, Goya pkts and chicken broth. Cover, bring to a boil, and then reduce heat to simmer.  Cook for 27 minutes, or until rice is tender.  Stir occasionally.

Submitter: 
Jackie
Description: 
Saffron gives this special rice a wonderful flavor and color, along with cumin and chili powder. This is a local favorite in San Antonio.Modified from: Mexican Rice
