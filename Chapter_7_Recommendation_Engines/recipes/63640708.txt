Title:
Never Fail Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1011965.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz packaged Egg Noodles (any variety)
1 whole small fryer chicken
10 oz fresh chopped spinach
1 lb baby carrots
6 stalks celery
3 sprigs fresh Rosemary
1 T sea salt
1  minced clove fresh garlic
1 whole  large onion
3 quarts tap water

Directions:
Place water, chicken, rosemary, and whole onion in a large stock pot. Bring to a rolling boil.
Boil until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
Allow contents of stock pot to cool.
Remove chicken, and onion.
Debone chicken, and remove any undesirable skin/meat/bone material.
Onion may be chopped and placed back in the pot with the stock, or discarded.
Meat may be chopped, or shredded, depending on preference.
Once meat is prepared, place it back in the pot.
Add minced garlic, sea salt, carrots, and celery.
Bring pot to a boil again, and allow to boil until carrots and celery are tender.
Turn burner to med/low, and add spinach. Cook until spinach is wilted, as in the picture.

Submitter: 
Melissa Brennan
Description: 
I created this recipe for my 5 children. They loved canned soup, but the sodium content is far too high. They all love this soup. It's hearty enough for a satisfying meal, yet light enough to be part of an overall healthy eating plan.
