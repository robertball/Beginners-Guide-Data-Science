Title:
Red Enchilada sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
half cup onion (or half onion) chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
half cup green bell pepper chopped
1 tablespoon butter or olive oil
1 15 oz. can tomato sauce
1 15 oz. can diced tomatoes
1/2 cup of chicken broth
1 tablespoon ancho chili powder
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon dried marjoram
1 teaspoon smoked paprika
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried coriander powder

Directions:
Melt butter in medium sauce pan over medium high heat, add onions, bell peppers and garlic and cook until soft and tender, about 3-4 minutes.
Stir in tomato sauce and diced tomatoes, and add spices.
Bring to low boil stirring and reduce to low and simmer for 20 minutes.
I use my emulsion blender right in the pot to smooth the sauce and gets the chunks out.

Submitter: 
*Sherri*
Description: 
I made my own enchilada sauce, this is  a thicker hearty sauce with rich Mexican flavor, the ancho chili powder makes the sauce.
