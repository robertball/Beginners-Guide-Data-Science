Title:
Pepperoni Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package Tri-Colored pasta
2 Tblspoons butter
1 Package pepporonis torn into pieces
1 bottle italian dressing
1 1/2 cup Parmesean cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions about 6 minutes or al dente
Drain, add butter to pasta till melted
Add 3/4 bottle Italian dressing and mix add pepporoni pieces and cheese and mix well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Starr
Description: 
My husband and 2 of my kids love this pasta cold but I do have one kid that will only eat it warm. My 4 yr old steals the pepporoni as I cut it up everytime. I just like how it tastes when its been sitting for a couple of hours and before serving I add extra paremesean cheese. Enjoy
