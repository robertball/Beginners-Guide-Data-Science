Title:
Heinz Cowboy Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
1 onion, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
1 green pepper, diced
1 can sliced mushrooms
1 jar Heinz Chili Sauce
1 can Nally's Lima Beans & Ham
1 can Pork-n-Beans
1 can red kidney beans, drained
1 can garbonzo beans, drained
1 can black beans
salt and pepper to taste
chili pepper to taste, optional

Directions:
Brown hamburger, drain. Add onion, celery, mushrooms and green pepoer, saute until heated through.
Add hamburger mixture and the remaining ingredients to crock pot. Cook on low 4 - 6 hours. Serve over cornbread.
(May also be heated in large sauce pan on stove, med. low heat, until heated through).
Leftovers can be frozen.

Submitter: 
anmlnrs
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by a co-worker at our office pot luck. The secret is in the Heinz!  Put ingredients in a crock pot and cook on low all day for best flavor.  Serve over warm cornbread.
