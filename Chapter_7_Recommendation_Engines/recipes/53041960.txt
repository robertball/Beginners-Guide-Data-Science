Title:
Cream of Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 onion, chopped
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 quart tomato juice
l can stewed tomatoes with celery and green peppers
salt to taste
2 cups milk
l T. sugar

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, over medium heat, saute onions in butter until translucent. Remove from the heat. Stir in the flour so that no lumps remain, then slowly whisk in the tomato juice. and can of stewed tomatoes.  Return to the heat and add sugar and  salt to taste. Cook until just boiling but turn off the heat before it boils. Let cool 10 minutes then slowly stir in milk. Serve immediately.
