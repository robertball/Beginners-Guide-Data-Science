Title:
Coos Bay Crab Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound crab meat
2 eggs (beaten to blend)
2 T mayonnaise
1 t mustard
1 t horseradish
1/4 t salt
1/8 t black pepper
1 t Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c cracker crumbs
1/2 c cracker crumbs

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl (except the last portion of cracker crumbs).
Portion into 2oz balls and flatten into discs.
Coat cakes in the remaining cracker crumbs.
Fry in hot oil 1/4" deep until golden.
Remove to draining tray.
