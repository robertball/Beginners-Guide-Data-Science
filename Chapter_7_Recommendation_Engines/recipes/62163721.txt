Title:
Killer Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. ground beef
4 cloves minced garlic
1 pkg. bacon
4 stocks celery (chopped)
2 med. onions (chopped)
3 green peppers (chopped)
5 19oz. cans total kidney beans (red, white, romano, and black) variations to preference.
3 28oz. cans diced tomatoes
2 14oz. cans tomato sauce
2 cups red wine
12 dashes tabasco sauce
2 tbsp. italian seasoning
4 tbsp. brown sugar
6 tsp. red pepper flakes
4 tsp. oragano
3 tbsp. paprika
6 tbsp. chili powder
4 tbsp. cumin
2 tbsp. vinegar
2 tbsp. basil
2 tbsp. minced onion flakes
3 tbsp. parsley flakes

Directions:
Slice bacon into 1/4" stips and begin cooking in small pot. Brown the hamburger in a stock pot. once bacon and hambuger is cooked, combine together in stock pot and add the minced garlic. set pot to simmer.
Saute celery, onions, and green peppers. Add to stock pot.
Add all tomatoes to stock pot. Bring to almost boiling. Add all the beans and the rest of the spices.
Once all the ingedients have been mixed, simmer for about 1 1/2 hours stirring often.
Serve with dinner buns for dipping.

This chili is just right for the bold and the timid. It has some heat but not too much for everyone to enjoy.
