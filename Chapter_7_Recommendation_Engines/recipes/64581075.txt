Title:
Grilled Portabello Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large portabello mushrooms
Olive oil
Basalmic vinegar
Salt and pepper
Basil
Onions
Chopped tomatoes
Low fat mozzarella diced

Directions:
Clean and dry mushrooms.
Mix oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, basil and onions.
Pour mixture over mushrooms and let still 30 minutes.
Grill over medium for 10 minutes on each side.
