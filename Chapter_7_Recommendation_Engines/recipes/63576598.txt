Title:
Memphis Dust Rib Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4476388.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup + 2 Tbsp. packed brown sugar
1/4 cup + 2 Tbsp. white sugar
1/4 cup paprika
2 Tbsp. Morton's kosher salt
2 Tbsp. garlic powder
1 Tbsp. black pepper
1 Tbsp. ground ginger powder
1 Tbsp. onion powder
1 tsp. rosemary powder (optional)

Directions:
Combine spices and rub on ribs!
