Title:
Christmas Breakfast Brioche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup warm water
1 pkg quick rise yeast
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs + 1 yolk
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup flour
2 1/4 cups flour
1/4 cup red candied cherries, sliced
1/4 cup green candied cherries, sliced
1 egg white
Frosting:
1 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix warm water, yeast, sugar, butter, 2 eggs, 1 yolk, 1/2 tsp salt and one cup flour in a mixer.
Beat on high speed for 10 minutes.
Gradually add 2 1/4 cups flour to dough
Fold in cherries by hand being careful not to manipulate dough too much.
Cover with a towel in the bowl and let sit for one hour to rise.
Punch to deflate dough and plaace in fridge overnight to rise again.
Take handfuls of dough and manupulate on floured surface to form a "snake" shape.
Twist snake shaped dough into a braid and place one inch apart on cookie sheet.
Repeat until all dough is used.
**be careful not to manipulate dough too much as this can result in tough brioche!**
Let sit one more hour to rise.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Brush braids with remaining egg white.
Bake for 15 - 20 minutes
Check often braids should not brown!
Allow Brioche braids to cool
Spread with frosting
FROSTING:
cream the chream cheese and butter in a mixer.
Add vanilla
gradually add powder sugar until creamy.

Description: 
Wonderful frosted Brioche recipe that I inherited from my Auntie Jean. We only prepare this once a year on christmas because it is a complicated recipe, but WORTH the effort!
