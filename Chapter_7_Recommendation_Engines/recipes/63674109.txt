Title:
Refrigerator Sweet Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1022852.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups sliced cucumbers
2 cloves garlic, halved
1 3/4 c. water
1 t. mustard seed
1 t. celery seed
1 t. turmeric
2 c. sliced onions
1 c. julienne carrots
2 c. apple cider vinegar
9 t. stevia or 18 packets

Directions:
Place sliced cucumbers and garlic in a glass bowl. Set aside.
In a saucepan, stir together turmeric, mustard seed, celery seed, stevia, and water. Bring to a boil.
Stir in onions and carrots. Boil for 2 minutes.
Stir in vinegar and cook 1 more minute.
Pour over cucumbers and garlic.
Cool, cover and refrigerate at least 24 hours before serving.
