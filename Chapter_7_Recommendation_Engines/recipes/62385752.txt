Title:
Spicy Tofu and Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/421398.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon dark sesame oil
1 tablespoon water
1 teaspoon cornstarch
1 teaspoon sugar
1 tablespoon organic canola oil
1 pound asparagus, cut diagonally into 2-inch pieces
8 ounces extra-firm tofu, drained and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
2 scallions, minced
1/2 teaspoon hot red pepper flakes

Directions:
In a small bowl, stir together the soy sauce, sesame oil, water, cornstarch, and sugar. Set aside.
In a large skillet or wok heat the canola oil over medium-high heat. Add the asparagus and stir-fry for 1 to 2 minutes. Add the tofu, garlic, ginger, scallions, and red pepper flakes, and stir-fry until the tofu is golden brown. Add the soy sauce mixture and stir-fry to coat the tofu and asparagus.
