Title:
Chicken and Cheddar Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (6.9 ounce) package RICE-A-RONI® Chicken Flavor
2 cups water
1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
1 pound chicken tenders
1 cup chopped tomato
1/4 cup sliced green onions
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In large skillet over medium heat, saute rice-vermicelli mix as package directs. Slowly stir in 2 cups water, seasonings and basil.
Place chicken over rice; bring to a boil.  Cover; reduce heat to low.  Simmer 15 minutes; stir.  Top evenly with tomato and green onions.  Return cover and continue to cook 3 to 5 minutes or until heated through.  Sprinkle with cheese.

Submitter: 
RICE-A-RONI®
Description: 
A great stovetop chicken and rice dish with the added flavors of tomato and Cheddar cheese.
