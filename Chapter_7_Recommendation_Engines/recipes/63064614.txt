Ingredients:
CLASSIC
2tbsp Dijon Mustard
60ml/4tbsp White Wine Vinegar
200ml/13tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil - or less to taste re piquancy, thickness and sharpness of the dressing
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
L'ANCIENNE
1heaped tbsp Wholegrain Mustard
60ml/4tbsp White Wine Vinegar
200ml/13tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil - or less to taste re piquancy, thickness and sharpness of the dressing

Directions:
DIRECTIONS ARE IDENTICAL FOR BOTH DRESSINGS
Add the mustard and vinegar with salt and pepper to taste to a small bowl and whisk together;
Gradually add the oil, pouring in slowly, whisking continuously to avoid oil/vinegar separation
