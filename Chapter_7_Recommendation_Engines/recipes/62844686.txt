Title:
Risotto Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6oz Arborio rice
1 veggie stock cube
2oz Gruyere cheese. Grated
2 Tbs pesto
2oz dried bread crumbs or panko
Oil for frying
Basil leaves for garnish

Directions:
Place the rice and stock cube in a saucepan and add 1 1/2 pints water. Cook over high heat , brining to a boil. cover and simmer for 15mins or until rice is tender.
Drain the rice thoroughly,then stir in the cheese and pesto,set aside to cool.
Usig dampened hands, divide the rice mixture and roll into walnut sized balls. Roll in breadcrumbs coating them well.
Heat 1/2 inch oil in a frying pan, fry balls until they are crisp and golden, drain on paper towls to remove excess oil.
Serve with Basil leaves.
