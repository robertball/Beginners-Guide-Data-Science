Title:
Sweet and Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993880.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
3 TBSP cornstarch
1 cup ketchup
1/4 cup Trader Joe's Chili Sauce (or plain sweet and sour sauce, a sprinkle of red pepper flakes)
1/8 cup Kikkoman's Teriyaki Sauce "Miso Spicy"
2 TBSP soy sauce
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/2 onion, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 small can of pineapple chunks
1 package of frozen meatballs (Costco and Trader Joe's carries some good ones)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine the first seven ingredients and mix well.
Place the last four ingredients into a slow cooker and then pour the prepared sauce over top.
Cook on LOW for 5-6 hours or on HIGH for 3-4 hours.

Submitter: 
Rachael Welch
Description: 
Seriously, I made this up using 4-5 different recipes and then added stuff I had in my fridge. It's a trial and error recipe, I got lucky! The measurements are not firm, just to your liking =) Enjoy!
