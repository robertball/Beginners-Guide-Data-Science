Title:
Caprese Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2815469.jpg
Ingredients:
1 container of miniature mozzarella balls (or a large ball or log cut into bite-sized pieces)
1 pint of cherry or grape tomatoes, washed and dried
Fresh Basil leaves, washed and dried
Balsamic Crème
Toasted Herb Bread Cubes (optional)

Directions:
Thread mozzarella ball, basil leaf & cherry tomato onto a small skewer or long toothpick.
Place around a plate and drizzle with Balsamic crème.
Can be made ahead and kept in the refrigerator covered in plastic until ready to serve.  Drizzle balsamic just before serving.
ALTERNATIVE:
Thread a large toasted herb bread cube at the bottom of each skewer for a more substantial offering.

Submitter: 
Kipp
Description: 
A portable take on the classic Insalata Caprese.
