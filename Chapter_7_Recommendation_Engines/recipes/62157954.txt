Title:
East Meets West Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283632.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. fresh chicken tenders, cut into 1" pieces
1 large green pepper, chopped
1 large onion, chopped
1 med. carrot sliced diagonally
4 Tbs. extra virgin olive oil, divided
2 stalks celery, sliced
10 button mushrooms, sliced
1 (14 oz) can chicken stock/broth
2 pkgs. Ramen Noodles Oriental flavor
2 Tbs. cornstarch
2 Tbs. dary soy sauce
1 Tbs. Goya Adobo seasoning
1 Tbs. rice vinegar
1 tsp. sesame oil
1 tsp. fish sauce
1/2 tsp. hot chili oil, or to taste
1/2 tsp. ground giinger

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and cornstarch. Stir, turning chicken to coat well. Set aside.
In large skillet, heat 2 Tbs. olive oil. Add green pepper, onion, carrot, celery, and mushroom slices. Saute 2-3 minutes or until onion is just translucent. Break noodles into small pieces and add to vegetables along with seasoning packets.  Add sesame oil, hot chili oil, fish sauce and ground ginger; stir to combine. Add chicken stock/broth, cover, remove from heat until noodles have absorbed liquid.
Meanwhile, in medium skillet heat remaining 2 Tbs. olive oil.  Add chicken pieces to hot oil, separating each piece.  Cook chicken stirring until all sides are golden and chicken is cooked through, about 6 minutes.
Place noodle and vegetable mixture on a serving platter and top with chicken pieces.  Serve with hot cooked rice.

Submitter: 
Gail Cobile
Description: 
Well seasoned Ramen Nodles, lots of veggies, fried chicken, and hot cooked rice. When 2 cultures collide good food is the desired result!
