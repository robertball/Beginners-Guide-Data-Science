Title:
Mema's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb ground beef
1 cup dry oatmeal
2 eggs beaten
1/2 cup chopped onions
2 tablespoons green peppers, chopped
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
4 ounces tomato sauce
1/2 cup ketchup
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon mustard

Directions:
Mix ground beef, oatmeal, eggs, onion, green pepper, salt, tomato sauce, and 2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce together in order.
Shape into a loaf.
To make sauce:
Mix Ketchup, brown sugar, 1 1/2 tablespoon worcestershire sauce, and mustard together in mixing bowl.
Set aside.
Bake in a 1 1/2 quart loaf pan for 1 hour 20 mins at 350 degree's.
Remove from oven.
Pour sauce overtop.
Return to over for an additional 15 minutes.
Enjoy
