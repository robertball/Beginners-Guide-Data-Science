Title:
Gingerbread Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7471323.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Irish cream liqueur (such as Bailey's®)
1 ounce milk
1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger coffee-flavored liqueur (such as Kahlua®)
1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger gingerbread-flavored syrup (such as Monin®)
1/4 cup ice, or as needed

Directions:
Combine Irish cream liqueur, milk, coffee-flavored liqueur, vodka, and gingerbread flavored syrup in a cocktail mixer. Fill with ice, cover, and shake vigorously until combined. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
