Title:
Smoothie Breakfast Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126838.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1/2 C. Quick oats
1 C. Water
1/4 tsp. Salt
1 C. Frozen packaged cranberries, thawed
1 C. Frozen packaged blueberries, thawed
3 Tbsp. Granulated sugar
1 C. Lemonade
1 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs, beaten

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
2. Grease a 12 cup muffin pan. Set aside
3. Cook oats in 1 cup of boiling water with 1/4 tsp of salt added to water until thickened. Set aside
4.  In a blender combine blueberries, cranberries, lemonade and sugar. Blend until mixture is well blended together
5. Pour one cup of blender contents into a large bowl and add the one cup of sugar and cooked oatmeal. Blend all ingredients together until just combined
Blend flour, b. powder and b. soda together in a small bowl.  Add dry ingredients to wet mixture and stir until all ingredients are mixed and batter is nice and smooth
6. Pour batter into greased muffin cups and fill 2/3 full
7. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes until lightly browned
8. Remove from oven and allow to cool before placing on a serving plate

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very light and delicious muffin with the essence of blueberry and cranberry throughout the inside of the muffin.
