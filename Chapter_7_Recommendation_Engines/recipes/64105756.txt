Title:
Salsa Quacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1254670.jpg
Ingredients:
2 avocados, mashed
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
Juice of 1 lime
1/4 cup red onion, minced
1 teaspoon garlic cloves, minced
3 tablespoons prepared salsa
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
Cut the avocados in half. Remove seed. Scoop out avocado from the peel, put in a mixing bowl.
Using a fork, roughly mash the avocado. Add in salt and lime juice and mix into the avocado using a fork.
Add in remaining ingredients and mix again using the fork.
Cover with plastic wrap  pressing it down to touch the guacamole.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
