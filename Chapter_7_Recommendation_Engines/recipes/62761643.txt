Title:
Gourmet Layered Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/638768.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tub (475 g) Tre Stelle® Ricotta, placed in a sieve to
drain in refrigerator overnight
1 tbsp (15 mL) fresh lemon zest, grated
Salt and pepper to taste
3/4 cup (175 mL) Rosenborg® Noble Blue Cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup (125 mL) pesto
1/4 cup (50 mL) sun dried tomatoes in oil,
drained and roughly chopped
1 tbsp (15 mL) toasted pine nuts (optional)

Directions:
Add lemon zest, salt and pepper to Ricotta.
To assemble: on a 10” serving plate spread Ricotta evenly to edges of plate. Thinly spread pesto onto centre. Rim pesto edge with crumbled Blue cheese and rim outer edge of Ricotta with sun dried tomatoes. Sprinkle with toasted pine nuts.
