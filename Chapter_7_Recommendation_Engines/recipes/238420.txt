Title:
Fiery Red Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1233097.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large garlic cloves
1 (14.5 ounce) can whole tomatoes, drained
1 medium white onion, quartered
1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
1 jalapeno, stemmed and quartered (or more to taste)
1/2 teaspoon natural cane sugar, or to taste
Kosher salt, to taste
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 (14.5 ounce) can whole tomatoes, drained

Directions:
Place first eight ingredients in Oster(R) Versa(R) Performance Blender in order listed. Add remaining can of tomatoes.
Select programmed DIP/SALSA setting.

Submitter: 
Oster® Versa®
Description: 
Customize this tomato salsa with as much jalapeno pepper as you like. Using a blender makes it fast and easy.
