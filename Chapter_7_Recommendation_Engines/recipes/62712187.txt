Title:
Mashed "Potatoes" includes Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/616117.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz cauliflower, florets
3 small uncooked Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled (or unpeeled if you like), cut into 1-inch chunks
2 cloves garlic, peeled
1 tsp salt, divided
1/4 c low-fat milk (or substitute Greek Yogurt or fat-free sour cream)
2 tsp butter
1 tbsp chives, fresh, snipped (optional)

Directions:
Place cauliflower, potatoes, garlic and 1/2 tsp of salt in a medium saucepan.  Add enough wter to cover ingredients and bring to a boil.  Boil until vegetables are tender, about 10-15 minutes; drain and return to saucepan. Let set to dry out but keep warm.
Stir in (heated) milk, butter, remaining 1/2 tsp of salt.
Mash with potato masher or mixer till smooth.
Garnish with, or stir in chopped up fresh chives (optional).

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Weight Watchers Recipe; Surprise, surprise:  cauliflower replaces nearly all the usual butter in these calorie-friendly mashed spuds. And they are wonderful.
