Title:
WOW, that's a good Tuna Melt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of water packed tuna drained
1 tsp yellow mustard
1 tbs mayonnaise
5 green olives chopped
1 tsp chopped pickled jalapenos
salt and pepper to taste
2 slices of italian or other crusty bread sliced 1.5 inches thick- toasted
2 slices of american cheese

Directions:
In a small bowl mix first 5 ingredients along with salt and pepper. (you can set tuna mixture in the refrigerator to meld flavors for about an hour if you desire)
Place toasted bread slices on a baking pan, top with tuna mixture and cheese.
Broil for about 3 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
