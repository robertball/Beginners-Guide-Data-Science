Title:
Pesto Stuffed Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken breast per person
Pesto Stuffing (pre-made pesto, 1/2 mayo, 1/2 miracle whip, parmesan cheese, garlic powder, ricotta cheese)
Roma tomato, sliced thin for topping
Mozarella cheese
Bread crumbs
Egg wash
Butter
Capers

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Wash and dry chicken, dip in egg and breadcrumbs
Butterfly the chicken and let it sit while you mix the filling
Make pesto mix and stuff into chicken
Put chicken into baking dish, top with sliced tomato, mozzarella, breadcrumbs and butter
Bake for 35-40 minutes
