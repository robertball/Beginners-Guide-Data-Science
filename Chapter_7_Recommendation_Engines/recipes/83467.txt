Title:
Marzetti® Hawaiian Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978932.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups Cabbage, shredded
1 (8 ounce) can Pineapple, drained and crushed
1/4 cup Raisins
1/4 cup Nuts, chopped
1/4 cup Coconut, flaked
3/4 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing

Directions:
In mixing bowl, combine ingredients.  Mix well.  Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
This tropical delight features pineapples, raisins, and flaked coconut. Close your eyes and you can hear the wind rustling through the palms.
