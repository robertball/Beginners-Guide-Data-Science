Title:
Spinach & Mushroom Breakfast Burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tb olive oil
2-3 mushrooms, sliced
1 1/2 C fresh spinach, chopped
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 C cheddar/jack cheese blend
1 10-inch flour tortilla

Directions:
Heat oil in pan and saute mushrooms for 2-3 minutes.  Add spinach and cook until soft, about 2 more minutes.  Add beaten eggs to mixture and stir until egg is firm and cooked.  In a large skillet, heat tortilla on high heat until brown spots start to appear.  Remove from heat and place hot filling onto tortilla.  Top with shredded cheese.  Wrap and serve with salsa.

Submitter: 
Trina
Description: 
A quick and tasty breakfast without all the grease.  Also great for low-carb dieters if you subtract the tortilla.
