Title:
Fruit Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1025391.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg (18 oz) refrigerated sugar cookie dough
1 pkg (8 oz) cream cheese
1/4 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
assorted fruit:  strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, kiwi

Directions:
Pat cookie dough into a pizzalike circle about 1/4 inch thick.
Bake according to package instructions.  Let cool.
Mix cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla together and spread evenly on cooled cookie crust.
Slice fruit and arrange on top of cookie.
Serve immediately or refrigerate.
