Title:
Big Ray's Sweet Cranberry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1131480.jpg
Ingredients:
4 lb. bone-in chicken pieces (breasts and/or thighs)
1 can (16 oz.) whole berry cranberry sauce
2 Tbs ketchup
2 Tbs sugar
3 Tbp red wine vinegar
1 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp worcestershire sauce
3 tbp canola oil
salt and pepper, to taste
1 medium onion. chopped

Directions:
Heat oven to 350°F.
Place chicken in 2 (13x9-inch) baking dishes.
Mix remaining ingredients; pour over chicken.
Bake 50 min. or until chicken is done (165°F).

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Toss it in the oven or in a slowcooker, this sweet cranberry chicken meal will satisfy the most picky eater. If you don't want to mix up the dressing from scratch, use a 8 oz bottle of Catalina or Russian dressing.
