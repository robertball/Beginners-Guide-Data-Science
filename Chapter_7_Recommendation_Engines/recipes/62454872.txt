Title:
Kevin's Sea Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Jumbo Macaroni Shells ( 18 shells plus extra for the tops)
1/2 cup sour cream
4 table spoons real butter
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1/4 cup sifted flour
1/4 cup white wine
1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
1 1/2 lbs Wild caught Shrimp 25/30 count
3/4 cup grated swiss cheese
1/2 cup chopped green onions
1 teaspoon salt
2 cup half & half
1 cup fresh parmesan cheese.

Directions:
Shell and devein the shrimp, chop in half or thirds and sauté with chopped onions and garlic until opec , set aside
Cook pasta according to package let cool
mix sour cream, shrimp, onions & garlic and grated swiss cheese in a mixing bowl
Stuff the Shrimp mixture in each shell, after stuffing place another shell over the top of the stuffed shell, place them in a casserole dish.
In a skillet sauté butter,onions, mushrooms for three minutes,stir in flour salt pepper and half and half and white wine, simmer until thick and pour over shells. Sprinkle with Parmesan Cheese and Bake at 375 for 25 minutes. Serve with Grilled veggies, a Toasted French White Loaf and a Cob Salad.

Submitter: 
Kevin McRae
Description: 
A wonderful Rich blend of Shrimp and Pasta. 
Kevin's Sea Shells are great for a typical meal or a Special meal. 
Kevin's Seashells look and taste expensive but are really an easy low cost way to impress everyone. Remember to use only fresh wild caught shrimp from the Gulf Coast. 

Serve with a Cobb Salad and a Buttered French White Loaf and Sweet Tea or a good Blush wine.
