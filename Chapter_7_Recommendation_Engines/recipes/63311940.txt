Title:
Southwest Fajita Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. plain yogurt
1/4 c. sour cream
2 T taco sauce
2 T minced onion
1 T lime juice
1/2 T dried cilantro
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients together.
Cover and chill if desired.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
Delicious for taco or fajita salads.
