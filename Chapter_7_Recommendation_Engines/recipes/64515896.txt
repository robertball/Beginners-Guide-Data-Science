Title:
Spinach-Orzo Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the salad:
5 cups Spinach leaves, washed, patted dry, chopped fine
2 cups orzo pasta, boiled and drained
1/2 cup red, yellow & orange peppers, diced (any combination)
1/3 cup red onion, diced
1/2 cucumber, seeded and diced
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
1/2 cup pitted olives, black or Kalamata, halved or sliced
1 carrot, grated large
1 cup Feta cheese, cubed  (Apetina Feta in oil is very good for this recipe and the seasoned oil can be used as part of the dressing)
Dressing:
1 cup vegetable oil (or the oil from the Apetina Feta + vegetable oil to make 1 cup)
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar (or more according to taste)
2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon dried oregano
Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Mix together the chopped spinach, orzo, peppers, onion, cucumber and cherry tomatoes.
Whisk together, or shake to combine in a sealed container, the dressing ingredients.
Toss salad with the dressing to coat.  Do not toss until ready to serve.
Spread salad out over a platter or a large bowl.
Top with olives and Feta.

Submitter: 
Kipp
Description: 
The Mediterranean flavor in this salad make it a wonderful side salad or the perfect base to be topped with grilled chicken, fish or lamb kofta.  Enjoy!
