Title:
Honey Wheat Oat Flour Bread Machine Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4051364.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup water (80 degrees F/27 degrees C)
3/4 cup milk (80 degrees F/27 degrees C)
1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil
6 tablespoons honey
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
3/4 cup oats
1 cup oat flour
1 1/4 cups wheat flour
1 1/4 cups bread flour
1 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Place warm water, milk, oil, honey, salt, oats, oat flour, wheat flour, baking flour, and yeast in the pan of a bread machine in the order listed.
Set cycle for light crust according to manufacturer's instructions.

Submitter: 
WillowTree
Description: 
After getting our bread machine, we were searching for our perfect daily loaf of bread. After some experimenting we found it! 
This blend of flours gave us a light, sweet bread perfect for soft sandwiches, toast, or just eating straight out of the pan.
