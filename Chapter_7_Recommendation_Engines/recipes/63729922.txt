Title:
Sushi Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/4 C. Water
3 1/2 C. Short Grain/Sushi Rice
Pinch of Salt
Pinch of Pepper
6 Tbsp. Rice Vinegar
6 Tbsp. Sugar
3 tsp. Salt

Directions:
Bring water to a boil.
Add Rice.
Cover.
Lower heat to low.
Leave until water is absorbed (about 15 minutes).
Over Medium/High heat stir dressing until ingredients dissolve.
Turn off heat.
Cool before putting 1/2 on the rice.
Stir and then put the other half on the rice.
