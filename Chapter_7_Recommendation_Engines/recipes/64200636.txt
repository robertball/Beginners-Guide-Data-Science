Title:
Jamaican Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sweet potatoes, skinned and cut into 2 inch pieces
1 20 oz can pineapple tidbits, drained and juice reserved
1 8 oz can water chestnuts
1 cup golden raisins
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
4 tsp Caribbean jerk seasoning
1/4 cup dried onion
3 tbsp grated fresh ginger
2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tbsp lime zest
1 tsp whole cumin seeds, lightly crushed

Directions:
Place sweet potatoes in a slow cooker.  Add pineapple, water chestnuts, and raisins and mix well.  Sprinkle chicken with seasoning and place on top of potato mixture.
Combine reserved pineapple juice with remaining ingredients in a small bowl.  Pour over chicken.
Cover and cook on low 7 to 9 hours or on high 3 to 4 hours.  Serve over rice, if desired.
