Title:
Tuesday Night Quick Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/807203.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (5 ounce) package mixed salad greens
1 pint cherry tomatoes, cut in half
1/4 cup crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
1 teaspoon lemon juice
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix baby greens and tomatoes in a large bowl. Sprinkle with gorgonzola. In a small bowl, mix oil, vinegar, and lemon juice. Sprinkle in salt and pepper, and whisk until well blended. Pour dressing over the salad mixture, and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
MOTTSBELA
Description: 
Need a quick salad for a weeknight after getting home late from work? Try this! It's easy to vary. I served with a baked potato that I cooked in the microwave (and finished in the oven).
