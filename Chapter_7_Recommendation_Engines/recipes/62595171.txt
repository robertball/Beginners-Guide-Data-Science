Title:
Taco Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef
1t chili powder
3oz cream cheese
18 pasta shells
2T butter
Taco sauce (1c)
Shredded cheddar
Tortilla chips, crushed

Directions:
Boil and drain pasta shells.
Toss shells with 2T butter.
Brown and drain beef.
Add chili powder and cream cheese to beef.
Stuff shells and place in baking dish.
Top with taco sauce.
Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
Top with cheese and chips and bake 15 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
Easy to make ahead of time!
