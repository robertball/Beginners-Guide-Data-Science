Title:
Sweet Potato Flan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs
40 oz can of sweet potatoes
1 can of sweetened condensed milk
1 can of evaporated milk
1/2 cup of sugar
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Prepare flan mold as usual.
In a blender, add eggs, milks and sweet potatoes and process until smooth.
Add vanilla, sugar and salt and process again.
Pour in mold and place in baño de María at 350 until done.

Submitter: 
Milly Suazo-Martinez
Description:
