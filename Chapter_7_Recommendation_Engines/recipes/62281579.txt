Title:
Jerry's Carrot Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large carrots, peeled and grated (approx. 2cups)
1/2 cup raisins
1/3 cup mayonnaise
2 Tbl. or less lemon juice
1/4 tsp. salt
2 Tbl. sugar

Directions:
Squeeze excess water from carrots.  Combine with raisins in a medium bowl.  Stir in mayo, lemon juice and salt until thoroughly mixed.  Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving.
