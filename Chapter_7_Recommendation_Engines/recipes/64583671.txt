Title:
Oriental Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package shredded cabbage
1 c chopped green pepper
1/3 c chopped green onion
1 small bag sliced almonds, toasted
1 package (chicken) ramen noodles, crushed
----
2 tbsp sugar
1/2 c canola oil
Chicken seasoning from Ramen noodles
1/2 tsp pepper
2 tbsp vinegar

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine vegetables.
In a separate bowl, prepare dressing by mixing sugar, oil, chicken seasoning, pepper and vinegar.
Right before serving mix in nuts and noodles to vegetables, then mix in dressing.

Submitter:

Description: 
Crunchy cabbage and a sweet/savory dressing.
