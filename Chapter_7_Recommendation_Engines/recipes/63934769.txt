Title:
Garlic and Soy Grilled Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole pork, boneless center loin chop
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 tbs minced garlic
oregano, basil, thyme, sage to taste - fresh if possible
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Sprinkle the pork chops all over with soy sauce, garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cover and refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours
Spray broiling pan with cooking spray
Preheat broiler to 500F
Broil the pork chops, turning once halfway through cooking time, for 10 to 15 minutes, or until a thermometer inserted in the center registers 145F
