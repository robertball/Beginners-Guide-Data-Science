Title:
Sweet and Tangy Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1092487.jpg
Ingredients:
1 14 ounce package of coleslaw mix
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup white vinegar
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon poppy seeds
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a small saucepan combine sugar, vinegar, olive oil, salt, mustard, pepper and poppy seeds. Bring to a boil. Pour dressing over the coleslaw mixture and mix well.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
A quick, tasty and healthy alternative to creamy coleslaw.
