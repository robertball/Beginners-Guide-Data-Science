Title:
Tonja Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 Pounds Hamburger
1/2 of a small onion
3 10.5 oz cans Condensed Tomato Soup
2 Cans Joan of Arc Light Kidney Beans Drained
1/2 Can water for each can of beans used
Salt
Pepper
Chilli Powder to taste

Directions:
Cook hamburger and onion on stove until done.  Drain grease and then combine all the other ingredients in pan.  Boil, then bring to simmer for 2 hours
Rinse cans before adding water
Water is added by using 1/2 can for each can of beans added.
Enjoy!
