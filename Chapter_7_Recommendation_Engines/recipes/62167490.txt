Title:
Patty's  Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Roma tomatoes-chopped
3 Tbls.basil-chiffonade
1 med.onion-chopped
3 garlic cloves-chopped
3-4 tbs balsamic vinegar
1 tsp.each salt and pepper
1 tsp.sugar
1 tbs.olive oil

Directions:
Saute the onions, garlic in olive oil till translucent.
Add the tomatoes,basil,balsamic vinegar,salt,pepper.Cook down till thickened.

Submitter: 
Patty Cakes
Description: 
I was making grilled pizza for dinner and was looking at a basket of Roma tomatoes and started adding things to it. I had a can of spaghetti sauce but didn't want to use it if I didn't have to.
