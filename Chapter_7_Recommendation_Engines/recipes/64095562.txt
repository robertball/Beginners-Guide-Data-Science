Title:
Pam's Baked Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup vegetable oil
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
3 cups quick cooking oats
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Beat together oil and sugar. Mix in eggs, milk, salt, baking powder, oatmeal. Pour into a lightly greased square baking dish. Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Bake at 350 degrees until firm, about 35 minutes. Serve hot.
