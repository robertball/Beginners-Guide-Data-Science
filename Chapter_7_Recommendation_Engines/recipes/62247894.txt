Title:
Egg Nog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 doz eggs
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup rum
1 cup brandy or apple jack
2 cups bourbon (good quality)
2 quarts milk
1 cup heavy cream
1 pint light cream

Directions:
Separate egg whites and yolks. Beat egg yolks with sugar until smooth. VERY SLOWLY add rum while stirring constantly. Carefully add brandy,then add bourbon. Be careful not to curdle eggs. Stir in milk and light cream. Whip egg whites to soft peaks and fold into milk/liquor mixture. Whip heavy cream to medium peaks and fold in also.

Submitter: 
Mad Butcher
Description: 
This recipe is very time consumiming but very good. It has been passed down through the generations.
