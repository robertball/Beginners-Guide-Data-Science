Title:
Salad Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pizza Dough:
1 1/3 cup lukewarm water
1 package yeast (1 teas)
1 Tbls sugar
3 1/4 cup flour
2 Tbls olive oil
1 teas salt
Cheese layer:
1 Tbls olive oil
1 Tbls fine chopped shallots
4 cups coarsely chopped mushrooms(Portabellas or any)
16 oz fresh mozzarella sliced 1/4 in thick
salt and pepper
Salad layer:
8 cups chopped romaine
1 1/2 cups chopped tomatoes
1/2 cup sliced red onions
1/4 cup lemon- balsamic vinaigrette
Lemon-Balsamic Vinaigrette:
3 Tbls fresh squeeze lemon
2 teas balsamic vinegar
1 clove crushed garlic
1/2 teas salt
1/4 teas fresh ground pepper
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Dough:
Put water, oil, sugar, salt, flour and yeast in that order in your bread machine and set on dough cycle.
Once it is done place dough in bowl coated with olive oil, roll over to coat then cover with clean cloth and place in a non-drafty area and allow dough to double...about an hour.
Punch down dough, turn out on lightly floured surface. Divide dough in half and allow to rest for twenty min.
Shape each dough ball into rough rectangle about 8x14 with a rolling pin.
Cheese layer:
Saute shallots and mushrooms in olive oil until vegetables are soft... approx. 4 mins. Season with salt and pepper.
Place dough on pan and put slices of mozzarella  across the dough.
Sprinkle half the mushroom mixture on top cheese and bake in 450 deg oven till crust is brown and crisp...15-20 min. repeat for the other dough.
Vinaigrette:
Combine all the ingredients and mix well.
Salad:
Toss romaine, tomatoes and onions with 1/4 cup vinaigrette.
Place salad on finished pizza crust and sprinkle with parmesan cheese. The crust can be made ahead of time and assemble prior to eating.
