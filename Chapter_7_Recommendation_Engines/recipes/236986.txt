Title:
Dubliner® Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1118301.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups shredded Irish Cheddar cheese (such as Dubliner®)
1 cup plain yogurt
1/2 cup finely chopped green onions
1/4 cup mayonnaise
2 teaspoons prepared horseradish
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Stir Cheddar cheese, yogurt, green onions, mayonnaise, horseradish, and black pepper together in a bowl; refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Phyllis Cook Sliger
Description: 
I made this for a St. Patrick's Day party and it went over great! Serve with crackers, chips, or veggies.
