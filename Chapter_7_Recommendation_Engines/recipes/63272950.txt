Title:
Baked Caramel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 qt. popped popcorn
1/2 c. butter or margarine
1 c. brown sugar, firmly packed
1/4 c. corn syrup
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. baking soda
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees; place popcorn in a large, 4" deep, buttered baking pan. Put in oven.
In a large saucepan, melt butter over low heat. Stir in brown sugar, corn syrup, and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
Boil, without stirring, for 5 minutes. Remove and quickly stir in vanilla and baking soda.
Remove popcorn from the oven and slowly pour mixture over the popcorn, mixing well. Turn into the buttered pan. Bake for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
Remove from oven and cool completely.
VARIATION: Add 1.5 c. of pecans/almonds/cashews before baking.
