Title:
Amy's Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayo
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 8oz container blue cheese
1tsp red wine vinegar
1/2 tsp worsteshire sauce
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp white pepper

Directions:
Combine the sour cream, mayo, and buttermilk until smooth.  Add vinegar, worsteshire sauce and dry spices, combine until smooth.  Add blue cheese to incorporate.  Refridgerate for an hour then serve.

Submitter: 
ALD0014
Description: 
Creamy, best blue cheese dressing out there.  I put this on a simple salad with tomatoes ad cucumber.  Best when its all fresh from the garden.
