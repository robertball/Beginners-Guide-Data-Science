Title:
Driftwood Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large tomatoes
1 large onion
2 jalapenos
3 tbsp chopped cilantro
1 8oz can green chiles
1 tbsp cumin
1 8oz can tomato sauce
1 tsp oregano
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Add all ingredients into food processor.
Blend til consistancy you prefer
Pour into large sauce pan.
Simmer for 30 mins.
Can in pint sized canning jars or chill for 2 hours and serve.
Will keep on shelf for 2 mons

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
My best friends brother made something like this when we were camping out at Driftwood, Texas. I have revised his recipe, but its still just as great!
