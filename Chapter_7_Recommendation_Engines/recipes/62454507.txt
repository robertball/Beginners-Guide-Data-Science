Title:
Peachy Pork Picante Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
4 pork chops, chopped
1/2 cup bell peppers, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 teaspoon cilantro
4 Roma tomatoes, chopped
3 peaches, peeled, pitted, chopped
chopped ginger

Directions:
Saute pork chunks and onions with olive oil  5-10 minutes.
Add other ingredients and cook 15-20 minutes.
Serve over rice.
