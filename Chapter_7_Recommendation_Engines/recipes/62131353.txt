Title:
Easy Fish Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium-large potatoes
1 1/2 lb white fish cut into chunks (de-boned & skinless)
1/2 lb frozen or fresh, raw, peeled prawns
1 medium spanish (white) onion - chopped
6oz medium chedder cheese - grated
2 tsps dijon/french mustard
300ml(approx)single cream
1 tsp lemon juice
salt & pepper
4oz butter

Directions:
Put a small slit in each potatoe and bake in the oven for approximately 1hr 10min (or until cooked) at 200 dgrs C.
Place raw fish & prawns in a large oven proof dish(approx 30cm in diameter).
On the stove, melt 2oz butter and gently fry the onion until soft(approx 5 mins).
Add the mustard, cream, cheese, lemon juice and seasoning and mix to combine, remove from the heat.
Add this mixture to the fish and mix well.
Scoop out the flesh from the potates into a separate bowl, add 2oz of butter, season well and mash.
Spread the potato on top of the fish, cover all the mixture with the potato using the back of a fork.
place in a pre-heated oven (180 dgrs C)for approx 55mins. Serve with a green salad or green steamed vegetables like broccoli.

Submitter: 
CharlieB
Description: 
Living by the sea, we try to eat fish often and this hearty meal is an easy way to enjoy a healthy dish with a little bit of what you fancy!  Good enough for a dinner party and the kids love it too.
