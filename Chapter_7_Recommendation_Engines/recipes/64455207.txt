Title:
Vegan No Oil Avocado Hamburger Buns to Convert to  GF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Eggless egg wash:
1oz sugar
1oz water OR use ground flax & water
Dough:
2 tbsp sugar (use any kind you prefer)
¾ tsp salt
½ cup almond milk
¼ cup + 2 tbsp water
14oz all-purpose flour, or plain flour
¾ tbsp instant dry yeast (0.3oz)
3.5oz mashed avocado

Directions:
Place first 4 ingredients in a container and mix well. Make the wash (can be flax seed water. Put 4 T flour into a small bowl, add in the yeast and mix. Blend avocado and put in large bowl. Pour the wet ingredients in and whisk until smooth. Add in half the yeast/flour (2T)and make sure to whisk for 30 seconds to dissolve the yeast. Add the other half of the yeast/flour and whisk another 30 seconds. Add in the rest of the flour in four separate times using a spatula to mix. Use hands when it gets too thick. Place into a bowl and cover and let rise 2 hrs at room temperature. Knead lightly and then cut into 3.5 oz sections. Line a pan with foil rings leaving at least 1/2" between rings. Form into buns, place in rings, brush with wash and let rise one hour.  In 1/2 hour, preheat oven to 355 degrees. When finished rising, very carefully brush tops again and sprinkle topping of choice. Put in oven. Rotate the pan after 7 minutes and complete baking for another 7 minutes.
