Title:
Salmon Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup nabisco Italian herbed crisp flatbread (8 crackers)
1 lb skinless salmon fillets cut into 2 inch pieces.
1 egg
3 tbsp dijon or honey mustard
ciabatta buns
avocado

Directions:
1. In food processor, place crackers and blend until coarsely ground.  Add half salmon, egg and a tbsp of the mustard.  Cover and blend until thoroughly combined. Add remaining salmon and pulse until coarsely chopped.  With damp hands shape into 4 patties.
2. Brush lightly with olive oil .  Grill directly on a greased grill rack over medium heat for 3 mins per side or until thoroughly cooked.  Grill buns during final one minute
