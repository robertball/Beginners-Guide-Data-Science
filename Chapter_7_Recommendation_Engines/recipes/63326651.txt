Title:
Veggie Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion, chopped
2 bay leaves
1 teaspoon ground cumin
2 tablespoons dried oregano
1 tablespoon salt
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 green bell peppers, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
2 small or 1 medium zucchini, chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
8 oz. canned green chiles, drained, chopped
12 oz.  vegetarian burger crumbles
3 28 oz. cans crushed tomatoes in tomato puree
1/4 cup chili powder
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can black beans
1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans
2 cups frozen corn kernals, preferrably roasted

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Stir in the onion, bell peppers, carrots, and celery.  Cook until softened.  Add garlic, chiles, bay leaves, cumin, oregano, chili powder and salt.  Stir in the vegetarian burger crumbles and cook until thawed.  Add tomatoes and beans.   Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 45 minutes. Stir in zucchini and continue simmering another 20-30 minutes.  Stir in the corn, and continue cooking 5 minutes before serving.
