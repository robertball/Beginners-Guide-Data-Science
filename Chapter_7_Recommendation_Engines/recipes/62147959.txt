Title:
Fudgy Chocolate Brownie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
250g caster sugar
185g butter
240g Hershey's semi sweet chocolate chips
1tbsp vanilla extract
3 eggs
110g all-purpose flour
150g chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat the oven at 160 degrees celsius
Grease your pan and sprinkle some cocoa powder(instead of flour) so that you can remove you brownies easily
Melt chocolate and butter using a double boiler and leave it to cool
In a mixing bowl add the sugar, eggs , vanilla and whisk
Add the melted chocolate and mix
Add the flour and mix again
Fold in the walnuts
Bake for about twenty mins.

Submitter: 
Nisha
Description: 
This is for those looking for a rich fudgy chocolatey brownie..One of the first desserts I did have ever tried and it was a winner with my family and bf.. Enjoy!
