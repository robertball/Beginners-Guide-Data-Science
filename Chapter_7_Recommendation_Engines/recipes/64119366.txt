Title:
Blueberry Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 egg whites
1/2 cup oats
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 dash salt
1/4 cup blueberries
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 dash cinnamon

Directions:
Put egg whites, oats, baking powder, almond milk, and salt in the blender.
Blend for 30 seconds on medium-high speed.
Spray a pan with nonstick cooking spray, pour batter on pan, and add half the blueberries.
Cook like a regular pancake.
For topping, add applesauce and cinnamon.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Applesauce is an inventive substitute for syrup atop these blueberry beauties.
