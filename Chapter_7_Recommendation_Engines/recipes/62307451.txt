Title:
Linguine with Peppers and Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package linguini pasta
1 pound sweet Italian sausage, casings removed
2 red bell peppers, chopped
2 onions, chopped
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
2 plum tomatoes, chopped
1 cup white wine
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
While the pasta is cooking, prepare the sauce. Saute sausages in a heavy skillet over medium high heat until light brown, breaking up clumps with back of spoon.  Add peppers, onion, and garlic; saute until tender. Then add tomatoes.  Cook two minutes.  Add wine and simmer until liquid is slightly reduced, about 6 minutes.
Drain pasta, and add to the skillet.  Toss to combine.  Add cheese, and toss to blend.  Serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick saute of tasty Italian sausage with sliced bell peppers, onion, garlic and wine makes a terrific meal when tossed with hot linguine and a grating of Parmesan cheese.Modified from: Linguine with Peppers and Sausage
