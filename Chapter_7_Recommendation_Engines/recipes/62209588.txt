Title:
Afif's Awesome Creamed Rosemary Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy cream
2 sprigs fresh rosemary
1-2 cloves garlic
7-8 med red potatoes

Directions:
Cook potatoes till soft, skins on
Boil cream with garlic and rosemary in it until you can smell the herbs, simmering maybe 20 min or so
Strain herbs and garlic out of cream and use to mash potatoes with
tah dah!!!
