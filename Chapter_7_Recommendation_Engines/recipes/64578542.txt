Title:
Low-Carb Grain-Free Banana Protein Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3636596.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups rolled oats
1/4 cup whole ground flaxseed meal
1/4 cup almond flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 ripe bananas, mashed
2 eggs
1/2 cup natural creamy peanut butter
1/4 cup nonfat plain greek yogurt
1/4 cup agave nectar syrup
1 tbsp powdered stevia baking blend
2 tbsp vanilla extract
3 tbsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp imitation maple flavor (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mash bananas or pulse in a food processor.
Mix all ingredients in a mixer or food processor.
Grease muffin tins or line with foil liners.
Fill each tin 3/4 of the way full.
Bake at 350 for 25 minutes.
Allow to cool in tins for 5 minutes before removing and placing on a wire cooling rack to finish cooling.

Submitter: 
jamjar
Description: 
These delicious muffins are packed full of protein and fiber, and they make a great grab-and-go breakfast or snack. Each muffin contains only 146 calories, no flour, no grains, and no refined sugars! Great for paleo, keto, and low carb diets! You can jazz them up by adding chocolate chips, nuts, chia seeds, etc!
