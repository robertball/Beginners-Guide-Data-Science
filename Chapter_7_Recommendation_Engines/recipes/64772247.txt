Title:
Princeess Toast  /  Prinzessen Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 oz of sliced roasted or fried Chicken Breast
2 slices of Buttered Toast
3 oz of Jarred,canned or fresh cooked White Asparagus
5 oz sour cream or creme fraiche
2 tbsp Butter
2 tbsp flour
1/2 Milk
white Pepper, Salt, Nutmeg
Parsley, Dill

Directions:
Toast and Butter the Bread;  arrange on a plate.  thick slices of Chicken Breast, arrange atop the Bread.
Heat the whole spears of Asparagus and arrange on the Chicken.   mix the flour with 2 tbsp. of milk. heat the Butter in the pan and add the slurry of flour/milk.   heat and add the sour-cream. stir and add Salt, Pepper and Nutmeg. adjust to a nice consistency with enough milk.   Dress the Asparagus with the Sauce; sprinkle with Parsley and Dill.  serve a rest of sauce;  apart.
*  (one may replace the Toast with a Bed of Rice).
