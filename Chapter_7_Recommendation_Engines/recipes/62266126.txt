Title:
Tangy Buttermilk Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/8 C. Buttermilk (or 4 TBSP dry buttermilk powder and 1 1/2 C. water)
3 C. bread flour
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/1/2 TBSP sugar
3/4 C. grated extra sharp white cheddar
1 1/2 tsp. active dry yeast

Directions:
Place all ingredients in the bread pan in the order given. Select a light crust setting. Bake according to your breadmakers instructions.

Submitter: 
Avon- status quo PRO
Description: 
A delicious tangy cheese bread made even better using extra sharp cheese. I prefer white cheddar in this bread.
