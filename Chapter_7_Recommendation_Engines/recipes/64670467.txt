Title:
Las Mareas Salsa Shrimp Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 -2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/2 pineapple, peeled and cubed (or 1 large can)
2 kiwi, peeled and diced (firm ones work best)
2 tomatoes, diced
1 navel orange, supremed
1 avocado, peeled and cubed
1 -4 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely sliced
1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
2-3 teaspoons fresh dill
1 lime, juice of
salt and pepper
water, for boiling

Directions:
Cook shrimp in boiling salted water with 1 tsp dill.
Once they are just pink, remove from water and hold in ice or cold water.
In large bowl, toss remaining ingredients together.
Top with shrimp and serve.

Submitter: 
Beth
Description: 
Shrimp, pineapple, fruit...a salad made at our B&B that our guests rave about!  Hope you enjoy it.
