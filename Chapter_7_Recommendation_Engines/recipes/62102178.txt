Title:
Grandma Schmidts Pancake syrup Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
250 ml  White sugar
250 ml  Cream
2.5 ml  Maple flavoring

Directions:
Bring everything to a boil over medium heat and simmer several minutes.
Serve with pancakes or waffles.
