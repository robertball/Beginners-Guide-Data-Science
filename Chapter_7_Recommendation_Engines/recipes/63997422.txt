Title:
Best Ever Buttercream Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Powdered Sugar
1 Cup Salted Butter - softened
1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
Dash of heavy whipping cream (or milk, or half and half)

Directions:
Whip butter in mixer with the whisk attachment on low for about 30 seconds.  Slowly incorporate the powdered sugar, 1 cup at a time.  Once the sugar is incorporated, turn mixer on high and whip mixture about 3 minutes.  It should be light and fluffy.  Scrape down the sides and bottom of the bowl and add vanilla.  Whip for one minute more.  Add a small dash of heavy cream and whip one more minute longer.

Submitter: 
battgirl6
Description: 
Lightest, fluffiest buttercream.  Stands up well to piping and does not melt when out of the fridge for a long period of time.
