Title:
Alessandro's Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1489874.jpg
Ingredients:
400 g chicken breast, cubed
juice of 2 limes
2 g curry or more to taste
small sprig of rosemary

Directions:
1. Take the chicken breast, previously cubed, and put it in a bowl with the juice, curry and rosemary. Mix everything in the bowl; leave it there for about an hour.
2. When an hour has passed, put the chicken and juice into a nonstick pan or a normal pan with a little bit of oil and start cooking the chicken on a low height setting.
3. Wait about ten minutes for the chicken to be cooked, then serve it.
