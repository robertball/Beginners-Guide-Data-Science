Title:
Beer Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
3 cups self-rising flour
1/3 cup white sugar
2 T.melted butter
1 tsp.salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together the sugar, salt and flour. Add beer and continue to mix, first using a wooden spoon, then your hands.  Batter will be sticky.  Pour into a 9 x 5 inch greased loaf pan.  Let sit for 30 - 60 minutes and add the melted butter on top and then
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees ) for 50 for 60 minutes.  The top will be crunchy, and the insides will be soft.  Serve topped with butter or cheese spread.

Submitter: 
Linda622
Description: 
Self-rising flour, beer and a little sugar make an easy, tasty quick bread that serves as a fine accompaniment to hearty entrees.Modified from: Beer Bread I
