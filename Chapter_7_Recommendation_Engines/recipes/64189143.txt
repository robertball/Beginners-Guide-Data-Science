Title:
Sweet Thai Style Chicken Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1527332.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 cups Jasmine rice
3 cups water
1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
3 chicken breasts
1/2 cup soy sauce
approx 2 cups clover sprouts
1 bunch fresh cilantro finely chopped
4 green onions chopped
1 mango diced
2 tbsp. curry powder
1 tsp. salt
1 cup cashews finely chopped
1 cup white sugar

Directions:
1. Put the rice, water and salt in a medium sized pot, bring to a boil then reduce heat to low and cover. Let it cook for 15 minutes, then shut off the burner and let sit for 5 more minutes.
2. Place chicken breasts in a pan set to medium high heat, add soy sauce and a splash of water, cover and let cook for 20 minutes, turning occasionally and adding water as necessary.
3. In a medium saucepan, combine coconut milk, sugar, and curry powder and stir until well mixed. Bring to a simmer and add mango. Cook for 5 minutes then heat only to keep warm.
4. To serve, put rice in the base of a bowl, cover with a bed of clover sprouts, add cilantro, cashews, green onions and cubed chicken. Add mango sauce on top as desired.

Description: 
This is a fresh and sweet Thai style dish, with great flavor and lots of room for creative tweaking. Enjoy!
