Title:
Angel Lush with Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4 oz. pkg. Jell-O Vanilla Flavor Instant Pudding & Pie Filling
1 20 oz. can Dole Crushed Pineapple in juice, undrained
1 cup thawed Cool Whip Whipped Topping
1 10 oz. pkg. Prepared round angel food cake
10 small strawberries

Directions:
MIX dry pudding mix and pineapple with juice in a medium bowl. Gently stir in whipped topping.
CUT cake horizontally into 3 layers.
PLACE bottom cake layer, cut side up, on serving plate. Spread 1 1/3 cups of the pudding mixture onto cake layer; cover with middle cake layer. Spread 1 cup of the pudding mixture onto the middle cake layer; top with remaining cake layer. Spread with remaining pudding mixture. Refrigerate at least 1 hour or until ready to serve. Makes 10 servings.
