Title:
Loosemeat Sandwiches III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 1/2 cups chicken or beef broth
garlic powder
1 onion, chopped
24 slices dill pickle slices
4 ounces prepared mustard
8 hamburger buns

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef and onion until brown.  Drain.  Return to pan with salt, pepper, and broth to cover. Add some garlic powder, worchertershire sauce and paprika. Reduce heat to low and simmer, uncovered, until liquid is gone, 15 to 30 minutes.
Serve meat on buns topped with chopped onion, dill pickle slices and mustard.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
These are my wife's version of the famous loose meat sandwiches.  They are pretty tasty and very easy to make.Modified from: Loosemeat Sandwiches III
