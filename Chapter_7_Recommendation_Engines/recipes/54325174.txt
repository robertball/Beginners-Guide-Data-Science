Title:
Tender 'n' Tangy Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup vinegar
1/2 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons sugar
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 garlic clove, minced
1 teaspoon ground mustard
1 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
3.58 pounds pork spareribs
1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions:
Combine the first nine ingredients in a slow cooker.
Cut ribs into serving-size pieces; brown in a skillet in oil. Transfer to slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours or until tender

Submitter: 
Eve
Description: 
These ribs are so simple to prepare. Just brown them, then combine with the sauce ingredients in your slow cooker.Modified from: Tender 'n' Tangy Ribs
