Title:
Vanishing Whole Wheat Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/365178.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter,softened
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
3 bananas mashed
2 cups Whole Wheat flour (preferably Bob's red Mill)
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda

Directions:
Heat Oven to 350 degrees F. Then grease a 9x5 inch pan. Cream butter and sugar together until smooth. Add the eggs and mashed bananas to the mixture then add the Flour and Baking soda and mix until just moist. Pout into prepared pan and bake. Cook for one hour or until fork comes out clean! Remove from pan and let cool!

Submitter: 
*Cozycook*
Description: 
This was a experimental recipe that I was trying when I was out of white all purpose flour! i decided to do a little make-shift bread, I was surprised at how yummy the effects turned out.... It was DELICIOUS and it's also extremely easy! so for those of you who rather prefer whole to white flour you'll really enjoy this recipe! :D *cozycook*
