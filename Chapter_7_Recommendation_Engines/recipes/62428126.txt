Title:
Verna's Glazed Porc Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 porc chops
Sauce :
2 tsp sage
2/3 cup brown sugar
2 tsp salt
2 tsp paprika
2 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp black pepper
2 tbsp water
1 small onion

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and pour evenly over chops. Slice onion and arrange on top of chops also.
Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes or until porc chops are cooked.
