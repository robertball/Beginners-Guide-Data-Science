Title:
Sweet and Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lbs packaged Italian style meatballs
1 12 ounce jar Chili Sauce
1 18 ounce jar grape jelly
1/3 cup of water

Directions:
Mix meatballs with entire jar of Chili Sauce and add grape jelly two tablespoons at a time.. testing for desired sweetness.  Add the water little at a time.  You may need to adjust to more or less water to your taste. Cover and simmer on stovetop or place in crockpot to keep warm.

Submitter: 
RHONDA35
Description: 
This is a fast and yummy way to prepare cocktail meatballs for any party or gathering.  Don't let the Chili Sauce and Grape jelly mixture scare you.  It's delicious!!
