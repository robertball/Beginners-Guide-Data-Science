Title:
Specht tortellini vegetable soup Recipe

1.5lb Cheese and Roasted Garlic Tortellini
Bagged fresh spinach
Bagged coleslaw
Steamfresh broccoli and cauliflower
Ground black pepper
Salt
ground Cayenne pepper
Crushed tomato
Reduced sodium chicken broth

Directions:
Cook tortellini as instructed
Dump most of the water when it's mostly done
Dump the remaining ingredients in a large pot
Season to your liking.
