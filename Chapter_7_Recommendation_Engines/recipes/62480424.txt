Title:
GARBANZOS CON CHORIZO Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can garbanzo beans, undrained or 1/3 lb dry garbanzos cooked and 1/2 cup cooking liquid
2 links of dry chorizo, diced
2 tbs olive oil
1 large onion chopped
3-4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 red pepper, chopped
1 Roma tomato chopped
1 tsp oregano
3 cups cooked rice
15 rings of pickled jalapeno (dice 7 rings, leave 8 whole)

Directions:
In a large pan saute the onion and garlic in the olive oil until the onion is just translucent.  Add the chorizo and garbanzos with the liquid, the diced peppers, oregano and tomato.  Cover and reduce heat to low, cook about 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve over hot cooked rice, garnish each plate with the jalapeno rings.
