Title:
Pistachio Cream Dream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- One box Jello Instant Pistachio Pudding Mix
- One 20 oz can crushed pineapples (drained)
- One cup sweetened shredded coconut
- One 500mL container Nutri Whip Cream
- One 500mL container cottage cheese

Directions:
1. Combines all ingredients and mix on low to medium speed with a mixer.  Continue mixing until the Nutri Whip is whipped (about 1 minute)
2. Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
RABZ
Description: 
This is easy to make and wonderful to taste.  A blend of pictachios, pineapples, cottage cheese and cream...
