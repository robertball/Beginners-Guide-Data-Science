Title:
El Torito Corn Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup Masa Harina
10 oz corn
2 T cream
3 T cornmeal
2 t shortening
3 T water, cold
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 t baking powder
1/4 t salt

Directions:
1) Whip butter and shortening until creamy
2) Slowly add masa harina while mixing.  Once fully incorporated, add cold water Tblsp at time. Set aside
3) Place corn in food processor and coarsly grind.  Stir into masa mixture and set aside.
4) In a large bowl, quickly mix cornmeal, sugar, cream, baking powder and salt.  Add to masa mixture.
5) Pour into greased 8x8" pan and cover with foil.
6) Place 8x8 pan in large pan.  Pour boiling water in larger pan 1/2 way up to smaller pan.
Bake at 350 for 40-48"  - Serve a small scoop of corn cake along with enchillads and refried beans.  YUM !
