Title:
Clone of Alamo Cafe Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. Roma tomatoes, peeled and chopped
3/4 lb. onion, chopped
1 clove of garlic, minced
3/4 c. water
1/4 c. fresh cilantro
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. black pepper
1 Tbs. chili powder
8 chicken bouillon cubes
4 cooked chicken breasts, shredded
4 c. chicken broth
2-3 corn tortillas
cooking oil

Directions:
In a food processor or blender, puree the tomatoes, onion, garlic, water and cilantro for 2 minutes.  Pour pureed mixture into a large pot, and add the salt and pepper, chili powder, bouillon cubes and broth.  Simmer for 1 hour.  Add shredded chicken and simmer another 30 minutes.  When ready to serve, cut up tortillas into 1/4" strips and fry in hot oil until they are crispy and slightly browned.  Serve soup in individual bowls and garnish with fried tortilla strips.  Additional garnishes may include any combination of shredded Monterrey Jack cheese, avocado slices and sour cream

Submitter: 
Bibi
Description: 
The Alamo Cafe is a well-known Tex-Mex restaurant in San Antonio.  This recipe is a clone of their Tortilla Soup.  Of course, the garnishes are up to you, but besides the tortilla strips, you may garnish with avocado slices, shredded Monterrey Jack cheese or sour cream, any combination or all the above!
