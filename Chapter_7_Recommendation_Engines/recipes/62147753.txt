Title:
Brown Beef and Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
¾ onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 c water
1 can kidney beans, drained
1 can diced tomatoes
½ c chili powder
2 tsp garlic powder
1 bay leaf
2 tsp oregano
½ tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground clove
1 tsp worst
Dash salt

Directions:
Brown ground beef. Drain.
Add onion and garlic and cook 6-8 minutes, until soft.
Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Kristin C
Description: 
A dark brown chili with just a hint of heat. The clove flavor always brings compliments.
