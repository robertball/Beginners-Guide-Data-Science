Title:
Herbed Skillet Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975923.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 (14 ounce) can Swanson® Natural Goodness™ Chicken Broth
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme leaves, crushed
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
12 small new potatoes, cut into quarters
2 medium carrots, cut into 1 inch pieces
2 stalks celery, cut into 2 inch pieces

Directions:
Mix cornstarch and 1/4 cup broth.
Mix remaining broth, thyme, pepper, potatoes, carrots and celery in skillet. Heat to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 20 minutes or until tender.
Add cornstarch mixture. Cook and stir until mixture boils and thickens.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Taste the difference Swanson® Chicken Broth can make when potatoes, carrots and celery are cooked in broth instead of water!
