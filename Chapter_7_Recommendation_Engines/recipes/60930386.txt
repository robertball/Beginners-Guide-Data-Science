Title:
Autumn Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup applesauce unsweetened
1 TBS jelly-all fruit
1/2 oz pecans dry roasted
1 cup of canned pumpkin
2 egg whites
1/2 TBS orange extract
1-1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp baking soda
1-1/2 cups flour
3/4 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 cup raisins
1-1/2 cups of equal

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Beat applesauce and equal until blended. beat in egg whites, orange extract and pumpkin. Mix in combined flour, baking powder, baking soda and spices; mix in raisins and chopped pecans.
Spread batter evenly in greased loaf pan.
Bake bread until browned - aprox 50-60 minutes. Cool bread and then remove from pan and cool on wire rack.
Heat spreadable fruit until melted in a small saucepan; brush on bread and garnish with pecans.
