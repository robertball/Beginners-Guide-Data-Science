Title:
Bacon Wrapped Almond Stuffed Dates Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 Pitted Dates
40 Whole Almonds
20 slices of Center Cut Bacon
40 toothpicks (soaked in water)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Slit dateds length wise with knife and push one almond per date into the slits.
Cut each bacon strip into 2 pieces and wrap each half around each almond stuffed date, secure with toothpick.
Place bacon wrapped dates on a foil lined cookie sheet.
Bake for 20-25 mins until bacon is crispy.
Allow to cool serve warm or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
KIKI810
Description: 
My mother used to served these little bundles at family gatherings where wine and beer were being served.  The salty-smoky flavors keep your guests satisfied before dinner.  Enjoy!!
