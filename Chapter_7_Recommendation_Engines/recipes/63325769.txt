Title:
Crockpot Pinto beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1lb. bag of pinto beans
1-small package of smoked ham hocks
2-bay leaves
1/4 cup of sugar
1-tablespoon of salt
1-tsp of pepper
1-tsp of garlic powder
2-tsp of seasoning salt
water

Directions:
Rinse pinto beans throughly to remove all the dirt and grit. You do not need to soak them because of the slow cooker process.
In a large crockpot add pinto beans, smoked ham hocks, bay leaves, sugar, salt, pepper, garlic powder, seasoning salt, and enough water to fill the crockpot half full.
Cook on high for 8 hours.
