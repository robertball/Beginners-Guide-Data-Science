Title:
Carrot Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion
12 large carrots
1/2 lb Velveeta cheese, cut into pieces
1/2 cup margerine
1/4 cup flour
1 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
2 cups milk
bread crumbs

Directions:
Cut carrots (or use precut frozen carrots) into thin rounds and boil in salted water until tender.
Sauce: Combine margerine, flour, onion, salt, dry mustard, celery salt, pepper and milk in saucepan. Cook until thick.  Place 1/2 carrots in buttered casserole, cover with cheese and add balance of carrots, covering with sauce mixture.  Top with buttered bread crumbs.  Bake at 350 until bubbly.
