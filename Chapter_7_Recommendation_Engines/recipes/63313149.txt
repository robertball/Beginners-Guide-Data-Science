Title:
Mushroom Gravy Round Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. round steak
1 1/2 cups flour
2 Tbs. butter
1 medium onion, sliced
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 1/3 cups water
2 beef bouillon cubes
Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)

Directions:
Cut round steak into serving sizes. Dip in flour and brown in butter. Place pieces in a crock pot. Add onion.
Mix mushroom soup and water. Pour over steaks.
Cook on low for 8 hours.
Remove steaks from crock pot.
Add salt, pepper, and bouillon cubes to the remaining liquid in order to make the gravy.

Submitter: 
Kaley
Description: 
Served with mashed potatoes and your vegetable of choice, this recipe is the utmost in comfort food. Even my mother (a stringent mushroom hater) enjoyed this meal!
