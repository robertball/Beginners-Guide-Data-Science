Title:
Meat Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 to 3/4 c. Vegetable or Olive Oil
1/4 c. Soy Sauce
1/4 c. Worchestershire Sauce
3 T. Prepared Yellow Mustard
1/8 c. Lemon Juice or Vinegar
Salt & Pepper
Garlic & Onion Powder

Directions:
Mix everything in a pan that will hold all of your meat in a single layer.  Add meat, turn to cover.  Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours.  Grill meat and baste during cooking!

Submitter: 
Torrerizor
Description: 
This meat marinade has got to be the easiest, best tasting thing I've ever done to inexpensive cuts of beef, pork, or venison.  As a newlywed, we cut all corners, but thanks to this recipe, not on taste!
