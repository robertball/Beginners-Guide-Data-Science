Title:
Beef Chili With Kidney Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T veg. oil or corn oil
2 med. onions, chopped fine (about 2C)
1 red bell pepper, cut into 1/2 in. cubes
6 med cloves garlic, minced (about 2T)
1/4C chili powder
2t ground coliander
1T ground cumin
1t red pepper flakes
1t dried oregano
1/2t cayenne pepper
2# 85% lean ground beef
2 (15oz) cans red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 (28oz) can diced tomatoes, with juice
1 (28oz) can tomato puree
salt
2 limes, cut into wedges

Directions:
Heat oil in large Dutch oven over med. heat until simmering, 3-4 min. Add onions, bell pepper, garlic, chili powder, cumin, coriander, pepper flakes, oregano, and cayenne; cook, stirring occ. until veg. are softened and beginning to brown, about 10 min. Increase heat to med-high and add half the beef; cook breaking up pieces with wooden spoon, until no longer pink, 3-4 min. Add remaining beef and cook, breaking up with spoon, until no longer pink, 3-4 min.
Add beans, tomatoes, puree, 1/2t. salt; bring to boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer covered, stirring occ. for 1 hr. Remove cover and continue to simmer 1 hr. longer, stirring occ. (if chili begins to stick to bottom of pot, stir in 1/2C water and continue to simmer), until beef is tender and chili is dark, rich and slightly thickened. Adjust seasoning with additional salt. Serve with lime wedges and condiments if desired.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
If you wish, serve with these condiments: diced fresh tomatoes, diced avacado, sliced scallions, chopped red onion, chopped cilantro leaves, sour cream, and shredded Monterey Jack or cheddar cheese
