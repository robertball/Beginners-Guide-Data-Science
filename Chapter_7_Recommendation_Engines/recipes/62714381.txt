Title:
Raspberry-Cranberry Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large box raspberry jello
1 can gelled cranberry sauce
2 packages/envelopes Dream Whip
1 cup & 2 tablespoons milk
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, room temperature

Directions:
Disolve jello in 2 cups of boiling water.
Place cranberry sauce in a seperate small bowl; stir and mash.
Combine the jello, cranberry sauce and 1 cup of cold water.  Whisk until fairly smooth, pour into a glass serving pan. Let set until fairly firm.
Whip Dream Whip and milk until peaked.  Add cream cheese and combine well.
Top the jello with the Dream Whip & cream cheese mixture.
Sprinkle with chopped walnuts.
