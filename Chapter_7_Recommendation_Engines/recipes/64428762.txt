Title:
Justice's Slow Cooked Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-3 lbs baby back ribs
-1/2 onion chopped
-1 elephant clove garlic minced
1/2 tbs olive oil
1/2 cup pepsi
-1 18 ounce bottle of BBQ sauce
-1/2 tsp salt
-1/2 tsp peper
-1/4 tsp garlic powder
-1/4 tsp onion powder
-1/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
-Sautee onions in 1/2 tbs olive oil till just browned.  Add minced garlic and sauté for another 60 seconds
-Season ribs with brown sugar, salt, pepper, garlic powder and onion powder
-Place seasoned ribs in slow cooker, pour 1/2 cup pepsi in, top with sautéed onions and garlic
-Cook on high for four hours or low for eight hours
-Preheat oven to 375 degrees, placed cooked ribs on a baking sheet and top generously with BBQ sauce.  Cook for 15 minutes.
-Enjoy!!!!
