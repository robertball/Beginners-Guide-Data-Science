Title:
Brunch Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
1 1/2 cups chopped zucchini
1 cups cubed fully cooked bacon
3/4 cup chopped onions
3/4 cup chopped green pepper
1 garlic clove, minced
1/6 cup vegetable oil
8 oz package cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup half-and-half cream
6 eggs
2 cups cubed day-old bread
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute the mushrooms, zucchini, onions, green peppers and garlic in oil until vegetables are tender. Drain and pat dry; set aside.
In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and cream until smooth. Beat in eggs. Stir in the bread, cheese, salt, pepper, vegetable mixture and bacon.
Pour into greased 11-in. x 7-in. x 2-in. baking dishe. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 50 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.

Description: 
Bacon, zucchini, mushrooms and cheese flavor this rich, hearty egg dish. It adds appeal to a breakfast or lunch buffet and cuts easily, too. Make sure you bring the recipe--everyone will want it! --Arlene Butler, Ogden, UtahModified from: Brunch Strata
