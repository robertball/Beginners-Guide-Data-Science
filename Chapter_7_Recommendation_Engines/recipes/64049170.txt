Title:
Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 potatoes, peeled and quartered
1 pound lean ground beef
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
sliced mushrooms
1 (15 ounce) frozen mixed vegetables
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of celery soup
salt and pepper to taste
5 tablespoons butter, divided
milk for mashed potatoes
seasoning salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat a 9x13 deep baking dish with cooking spray.
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook potatoes in boiling water until tender, about 15 minutes. Drain,  Mash potatoes with milk, salt, pepper, and 2 tbsp butter.
In a large skillet, add oil and heat.
Add garlic, onion, and mushroom and saute until almost done. Add ground beef and cook until brown over medium-high heat. Drain fat from pan. Stir in mixed vegetables, mushroom soup, celery soup, salt, and pepper; heat through.
Pour into prepared baking dish, cover with mashed potatoes, and dot with butter.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until potatoes are golden and beef and vegetable mixture is hot and bubbly.
