Title:
Gluten Free Mocha Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/811659.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cooked dried beans, drained*
2 T oil
2 T chia seeds
1 T instant coffee
1/4 cup maple syrup
1/3 cup carob powder
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Mash beans.  Combine beans, oil, chia seeds, instant coffee, maple syrup, and carob powder until well mixed.  Add raisins.
Drop cookies onto parchment papered cookie sheet. These will retain their shape, so many can be placed on a cookie sheet.
Bake for 12 1/2 to 14 minutes.  Let cool on pan for 2 minutes, then remove to a cooling rack.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
These cookies have no flour, eggs or leavening agents at all, with beans being the basis.
