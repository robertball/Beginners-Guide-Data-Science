Title:
Paleo Breakfast Burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2324040.jpg
Ingredients:
1 slice of deli meat
6 tablespoon of egg white

Directions:
Cook the egg white in a greased pan.
Once it looks fluffy and scrambled, place the egg white on a slice of deli meat.
Carefully wrap the meat into a burrito like shape.

Submitter: 
Chef Lizzie
Description: 
A simple 2-ingredient paleo recipe that's perfect for busy mornings!
