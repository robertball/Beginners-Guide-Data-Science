Title:
Chicken, Spinach & Mushroom Alfredo with Penne Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb penne (whole wheat or regular)
4 slices lean bacon
1 shallot, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, crushed
8oz fresh baby spinach leaves, coarsely chopped
1/2 red pepper, chopped
2 T dry white wine
1 jar light Alfredo sauce
1/2 tsp salt, 1 tsp fresh ground pepper, 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
4 chicken breasts, or 12-14 tenderloins)
1 tsp Fox Point seasoning and 1 tsp. California Seasoned Pepper (Penzey's spices)or yr seasoning of choice
4 lg mushrooms, sliced
1/4 cup grated parmesan

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 deg.
Place the uncooked chicken in a 9x12 baking pan and sprinkle liberally with seasoned pepper and Fox Point seasoning. Cover with sliced mushrooms.
Fry bacon in a large skillet until done. Remove from pan and coarsely chop.  Set aside. Keep 1 T bacon grease and gently fry chopped shallot and minced garlic for 3 mins until slightly browned and softened. Add red pepper, chopped spinach and cook down for 3-4 minutes. Add the wine and cook for a another minute or so. Add chopped cooked bacon. Add jar Alfredo sauce, cayenne pepper and salt and pepper.Stir well and heat gently for 2-3 minutes.  Pour sauce over seasoned chicken and sprinkle with 1/2 of the Parmesan.
Cover and bake for 50 minutes. Uncover and add remaining parmesan. Cook an additional 10 minutes until sauce is slightly thickened and cheese is melted.
Cook pasta al dente and serve with Alfredo. Enjoy!
