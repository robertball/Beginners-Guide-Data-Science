Title:
Crockpot Teriyaki Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 2 1/2 pounds round steak
1 tsp. ground ginger
1 Tbsp. sugar
2 Tbsp. canola oil
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 clove garlic, finely minced

Directions:
Cut steak into thin strips. Combine remaining ingredients in small bowl. Place meat in crockpot; pour sauce over. Cover and cook on Low for 6-8 hours.
