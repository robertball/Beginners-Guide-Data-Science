Title:
Nicole's Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/487433.jpg
Ingredients:
1 mango, peeled and chopped
1/2 red pepper, finely chopped
1-2 jalapeños, seeded, and finely chopped
1 lime
1/4 cup green onions, chopped
1 tbsp vinegar
1tbsp olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop mango, red pepper, jalapeños, and green onions. Put all ingredients in a non corrosive bowl.  Zest the lime, and then squeeze the juice into the mixture. Add vinegar, olive oil and salt and pepper.  Let sit for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors mix well.  Enjoy with tortilla chips, on a salad, over chicken, shrimp or fish - it is a great addition to many main courses!
