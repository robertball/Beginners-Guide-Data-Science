Title:
White Pizza with Broccoli and Red Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of pizza dough
1 15 oz container ricotta cheese
1 16 oz pkg mozzarella cheese, shredded
2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
1 pkg frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
1 small jar roasted red peppers, chopped
1 large clove garlic, minced
2 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Lightly grease a 11 x 15 inch jelly roll pan or baking sheet
In a medium skillet, saute minced garlic in 2 tbsp olive oil
Add thawed and drained chopped broccoli
Saute for 5 - 7 minutes on medium low heat
In a small bowl, mix half of the mozzerella with the whole container of ricotta cheese, reserve other half of shredded mozzarella cheese.
Stretch and press pizza dough into prepared pan.
Spread ricotta and mozzarella mixture over pizza dough
Top with prepared broccoli
Sprinkle chopped roasted red peppers on top of pizza
Sprinkle 2 tbsp of parmesean cheese on top of pizza
Bake at 400 degrees for 15 to 18 minutes until golden brown and bubbly.
Cool slightly, cut into 8 pieces and serve.
This recipe can be cut into smaller pieces for a nice party appetizer.
