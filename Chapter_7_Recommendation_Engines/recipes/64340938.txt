Title:
Mary's Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion,
1 clove minced garlic,
1/4 cup salad oil,
1 medium chopped tomato,
1 small bay leaf,
1/2 tsp cinnamon,
3 whole cloves,
5 cups cubed raw chicken,
1 1/2 tsp salt,
1 tablespoon curry powder,
1/2 tsp cumin,
1/2 tsp coriander,
1/8 tsp pepper,
1/8 tsp powdered saffron,
1 1/2 cups water,
1/4 cup fresh coconut milk

Directions:
1. In large skillet, cook garlic and onion in oil until tender but not brown. Add tomato, bay leaf, cinnamon, and cloves. Cover and cook 5 minutes
2. Add chicken cubes and simmer uncovered slowly until the the juice of the chicken has steamed off (about 30 minutes).
3. Stir in salt, curry powder, cumin, coriander, pepper, saffron and water. Simmer 35 to 40 minutes, or until chicken is tender. Blend in coconut milk.

Submitter: 
Brandon Baker
Description: 
This is my mother-in-law's chicken curry recipe. Nothing but love comes out of her kitchen, so it's at least worth a try! 

Control the zesty level based on the type of curry powder you use. Yellow tends to be more mild, while red is more zesty.
