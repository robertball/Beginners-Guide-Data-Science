Title:
Mommy's Grape Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
8 oz. sour cream
1 T. vanilla
4 lbs. red seedless grapes, washed and dried
light brown sugar
chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix softened cream cheese and sour cream, add vanilla and grapes.  Mix lightly.  Sprinkle with brown sugar and chopped pecans.  Mix before serving.
