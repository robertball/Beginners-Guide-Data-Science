Title:
Beer beef stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs stew beef
1/2. C flour
2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2. Tbsn butter
2.  Tbsn oil or bacon fat
2. Large onions chopped
2. Cloves of garlic minced
3. Tbsn tomato paste
24. Oz beer or ale
1. Tbsn brown sugar
1/4. tsp time
2. Tbsn minced parsley

Directions:
Cut beef into 1 1/2 inch cubes.  Combine flour, salt and pepper and coat the beef.  Heat butter and oil together until sizzling and brown the beef on all sides.  Remove the beef, add the onions and cook until softened and starting to brown.  Ad more butter or oil if needed.  Place the meat and onions in a casserole.  Deglaze the pan with some of the beer to loosen any browned bits.  Add to the casserole along with the rest of the ingredients.  Cover and bake at 350 for about 2 1/2 hours.
