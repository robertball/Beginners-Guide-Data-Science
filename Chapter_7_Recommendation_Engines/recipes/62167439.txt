Title:
Cinnamon Buttermilk Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/3 cup sugar
2-1/2 t. baking powder
¾ t. salt
½ t. baking soda
¾ c. cold butter
1 c. buttermilk
1 - 12 oz. package cinnamon chips
Cinnamon Sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl combine the flour, sugar baking powder, salt and baking soda.  Cut in butter.  Stir in the buttermilk just until combined.  Turn out onto a floured counter surface and fold in Cinnamon Chips.  Divide dough in half.  Shape each portion into a ball and pat into a 6” circle.
Cut each circle into six wedges.  Place on a lightly greased baking sheet.  Brush with milk and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.
Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 min. or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
CATHYRAE
Description: 
I took and old Scone recipe and made some changes.  These scone are sure to be a favorite and they freeze well.
