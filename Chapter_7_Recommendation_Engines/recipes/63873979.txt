Title:
Baked Beans By Chef James Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 15.5oz baked beans
1 medium onion minced
2 large cloves of garlic minced
2 tbsp butter or margarine
6 thin slices of hickory smoked bacon
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 tbsp dry mustard
1 tsp onion powder
1.5 tbsp worcestershire sauce
1 tsp liquid smoke
1 tsp tabasco sauce
1 cup ketchup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In large saucepan sauté minced onions and garlic in butter until translucient. Set aside.
In a large mixing bowl add baked beans,brown sugar,ketchup, onion powder, dry mustard, worcestershire sauce, liquid smoke, tabasco and sautéed onions and garlic.
Mix thoroughly until all ingredients combine.
Pour mixture into large baking (casserole) dish.
Placed thin sliced bacon side by side on top of mixture.
Bake uncovered for 25 minutes until beans become bubbly.
Remove beans from oven and place cooked bacon in food processor and process into puree.
Add pureed bacon to beans and mix thoroughly.
Serve immediately or chill and serve cold.

Description: 
A great recipe to compliment a family barbecue. Definitely comfort food on a cold winter night. Add your favorite brand hotdogs to create an awsome beans and frank casserole.
