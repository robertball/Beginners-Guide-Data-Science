Title:
Essanaye's crock pot Navy Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/572958.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package Navy Beans
1 46 ounce can of Chicken Broth
1 medium sweet onion, diced
3 stalks celery, diced
1 cup diced carrots
2-3 bay leaves
3-4 cloves garlic, diced
2 ham hocks
1 cup diced ham

Directions:
Rinse, sort and soak the beans in cold water overnight.  Place all ingredients in crock pot on high for 2 hours, then set to low and continue cooking 6 hours.  About an hour before done, remove the ham hocks, strip the meat off, (discard the bone/skin), return the meat to the pot and continue cooking.
Makes 6 servings.  Serve with Essanaye's corn bread.
