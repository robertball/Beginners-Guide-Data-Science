Title:
Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 tbsp minced garlic
1  package McCormick's pot roast mix
1  cup water or beef broth
5 1/2 pounds pot roast (or tri tip roast)
2 tbsp corn starch

Directions:
In a slow cooker, mix cream of mushroom soup, pot roast mix, minced garlic and water. Place pot roast in slow cooker and coat with soup mixture.
Cook on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.
1/2 hour before removing add corn starch.
Serve over wide egg noodles

Submitter: 
David R Peacock
Description: 
This simple pot roast makes its own gravy in the slow cooker.Modified from: Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast
