Title:
Clean-Eating Pumpkin Spice Coffee Creamer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2683169.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups almond milk
3 tablespoons honey
3 tablespoons pumpkin puree
3 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Blend almond milk, honey, pumpkin puree, vanilla extract, and pumpkin pie spice together in a blender; store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
