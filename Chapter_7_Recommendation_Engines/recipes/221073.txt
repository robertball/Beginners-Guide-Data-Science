Title:
Simple Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/873237.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large cucumber
1 small onion, chopped
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1 teaspoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon distilled white vinegar
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Toss cucumber, onion, sour cream, sugar, vinegar, and black pepper together in a bowl.
Chill completely in refrigerator to serve.

Submitter: 
penny
Description: 
Cucumber and onion in a sour cream and vinegar dressing makes a simple and quick cucumber salad.
