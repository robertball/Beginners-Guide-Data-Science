Title:
AZ's Step-Mom's Hot Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pd Crab meat, remove any bits of shell
2-8oz blocks of cream cheese, softened
1 cup sour cream
4 big tablespoons of mayo
2 teaspoons of Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon of yellow mustard
3 shakes of garlic salt
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Mix everything together except the cheddar cheese.
Put into a 8x8 pan and sprinkle with cheese.
Bake at 350 F for 30 minutes.
Let sit to cool.
Serve with raw veggies, crackers, pita bread, naan, you get the picture!

Submitter: 
Dog Fish Head aka Fabius
Description: 
This recipe comes from AZ's dearly loved step-mom who according to AZ made a killer crab dip!
