Title:
Beef Burgundy For A Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs Round Steak cut up and browned in large pan
4 Onions chopped and saute'd with meat
1 1/2 t Kitchen Bouquet
1/2 c tomato paste (1 small can)
1 1/2 c boiling water
4 beef bouillion cubes
1 bottle of Burgundy Wine

Directions:
Stir in ingredients in with browned meat and onions. Add 1 1/2 cup wine. Simmer 2 hours. Shortly before serving add 1/2 c more of wine. You may want to thinken slightly with cornstarch.

Submitter: 
Julia
Description: 
This recipe makes a lot, so when I make it for my family I freeze half for later. I serve it with plain boiled potatoes and a salad. You could make it in the early and let it simmer in a crockpot till your company comes for dinner. It seems kinda fancy, but really it's very simple.
