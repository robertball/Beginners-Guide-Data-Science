Title:
Pastichio (Baked macaroni and meat in cream sauce) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large onions, chopped
1/4 lb butter
2 lb ground beef
1/2 cup chopped parsley
1 lb macaroni
1 tbs salt
1 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 tbs white wine
Cream sauce
7 eggs
3 tbs flour
1/4 lb melted butter
1 1/2 quarts warm milk
2 cups grated Romano cheese

Directions:
In large skillet, saut√© onion with butter; add beef and brown. Add remaining ingredients except macaroni and simmer about 30 minutes. While sauce is simmering, boil 1 lb of macaroni in salted water until done and drain well.
Beat eggs and add flour. Add warm milk and continue beating.  Pour half of this mixture into macaroni, add 1 cup grated cheese and mix together.  Pour half of macaroni mixture into buttered pan, 15x10in, and sprinkle with half of melted butter and 1/2 cup of cheese. Cover with meat sauce and spread evenly. Cover with remaining macaroni mixture, sprinkle with remaining melted butter and rest of cheese.  Pour other half of milk mixture over all.  Bake at 400 defgees for 45-50 minutes. Cool slightly, cut into squares
