Title:
Instant Pot Vanilla Bean Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. cream cheese - room temperature
2 eggs - room temperature
1 egg yolk - room temperature
3/4 cup sugar
Pinch of salt
2 Tbs plain Greek yogurt or sour cream
1 Tbs vanilla extract
1 vanilla bean
Crust:
1-1/2 cups crushed vanilla cookies, or graham crackers
2 Tbs sugar (optional)
4 Tbs butter, melted

Directions:
Crust: Mix together cookie crumbs, sugar and butter.
Press into 7" springform pan
Beat the cream cheese in mixer until softened.
Add yogurt or sour cream, eggs, egg yolk, salt, and sugar. Beat until well blended.
Add vanilla extract and vanilla bean caviar and beat until mixed.
Pour mixture over prepared crust
Place trivet I to Instant Pot. Add 2 cups water.
Using a foil sling, lower pan onto trivet.
Place lid on pot and close vent.
Set to manual for 23 minutes.
Natural Pressure Release
There may be some condensation on top of the cheesecake. Gently pat with a paper towel to remove.

Submitter: 
Crikkitt
Description: 
A creamy delicious cheesecake using cream cheese, eggs, yogurt and vanilla beans made in the Instant Pot.
