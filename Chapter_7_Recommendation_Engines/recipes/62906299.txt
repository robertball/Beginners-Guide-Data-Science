Title:
Pizza Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/723092.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Bisquick
1/2 cup water
1 ound ground Sausage
Container Pizza Sauce - divided
1/2 cup motzerella Cheese
Sliced olives

Directions:
Brown suasage in skillet.
Mix Bisquick and water into dough.
Spread dough into large rectangle about 1/2 inch thick.
Cover dough with pizza sauce.
Spread cooked sausage, cheese and olives evenly over dough surface.
Roll up dough into a loaf.
Place on cookie sheet
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
Finished when golden brown.
Let stand a couple of minutes.
Slicce the loaf into discs and server topped with pizza sauce.
