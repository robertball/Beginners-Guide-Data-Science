Title:
Hot Lasagna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981425.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (40 ounce) box Stouffer's® frozen Family Style Favorites Lasagna with Meat & Sauce
1 (10 ounce) package mixed salad greens
1/2 cup creamy Parmesan salad dressing
1/2 cup sliced ripe olives, drained
1 large tomato, cut into wedges
1 cup salad croutons
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Toss salad greens and salad dressing in large bowl; place on large serving platter. Spoon hot lasagna onto center of salad greens; arrange olives and tomato on remaining greens. Sprinkle croutons and cheese over lasagna.

Submitter: 
Stouffer's®
Description: 
Top fresh greens with hot lasagna for a delicious result.
