Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2443329.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag Marshmallows
1 stick of butter
1 bag of Oreos
1 bunch of kale

Directions:
1. Melt the butter and marshmallows over low heat. Be careful not to burn.
2. Chop your kale into managable pieces.
3. Throw everything in your food processor and pulse till everything is one.Sprinkle with abnowesomeness
4. Spread into greased pan or one lined with wax paper and allow it to cool.
5. Cut into squares and share with a friend!
