Title:
Mock Beef Seasoning Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C + 1 T Brewer's Yeast Flakes
3 T Sweet Pepper Flakes
3 T Parsley flakes, powdered
3 T Salt
4 1/2 t Dill Seed, powdered
4 1/2 t Celery Seed, powdered
4 1/2 t Onion Powder
1 T Basil, powdered
1 t Rosemary, powdered
1 t Thyme, powdered

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and use a beef seasoning.
Store in a jar.
