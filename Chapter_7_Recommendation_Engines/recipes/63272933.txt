Title:
Beef Barbecue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 - 2 lbs. chuck roast
1 med. onion, chopped fine
2 t. dry mustard
1/4 c. brown sugar
1/4 t. ginger
1 t. celery salt
2 t. paprika
2 T. vingear
1 c. ketchup
1 c. meat stock (from chuck roast)

Directions:
Simmer meat in salted water until it shreds, about 2 hour.
Combine meat and 1 c. stock with remaining ingredients.
Simmer about 1.5 hours.
Serve on buns.
