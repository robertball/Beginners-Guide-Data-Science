Title:
Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 c. sour cream
1/2 c. butter
1 Tbsp sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1 can cream style corn
1 can whole kernel corn
1 box Jiffy cornbread mix

Directions:
Mix ingredients.  Pour into a greased square baking dish.  Bake at 350 for 45-60 minutes.
