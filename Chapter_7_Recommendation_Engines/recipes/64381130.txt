Title:
Crock Pot Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042766.jpg
Ingredients:
1 rack baby back ribs
4 tbs rib or pork seasoning(rub)
21/2 tbs red wine vinegar
1 bottle BBQ sauce (molasses based)
dash of liquid smoke
salt and pepper
additional BBQ sauce for serving

Directions:
Cut rack of ribs in half. Rub each half with 2 tbs of rib seasoning.
Place ribs in crock pot on top of each other. Add red wine vinegar and liquid smoke. Pour 3/4 BBQ sauce over ribs.
Cook on low for 7-8 hrs.
Flip the ribs about halfway through cooking time(place bottom ribs on top and top ribs on bottom) Add remaining BBQ sauce.Continue cooking until done.
