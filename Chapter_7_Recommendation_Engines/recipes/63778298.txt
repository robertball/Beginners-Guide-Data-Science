Title:
Refreshing Watermelon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1047068.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small seedless watermelon, diced to bite size pieces
3 limes, juiced
1 bunch of fresh mint, stems removed and chopped

Directions:
In a large bowl mix together watermelon, lime juice and mint.
Stir to incorporate lime juice and mint throughout salad.
Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours for the flavors to marinate before serving.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
Sweet watermelon mixed with the bold flavors of mint and lime make this an incredibly refreshing dish! This is a great dish to bring to BBQ's, Potlucks and enjoy during warm weather.
