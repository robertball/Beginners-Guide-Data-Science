Title:
G's BBQ Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C. paprika
1 T. dark brown sugar, packed
1 T. white sugar
2 t/ salt
1 t. celery salt
1 t. black pepper
1/2 t. cayenne pepper, or to taste
1 t. dry mustard
1 t. garlic powder
1 t. onion powder
2 t. oragano
2 t. thyme
3 T chili powder
2 t cumin
1 t. meat tenderizer

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Seal and store in a cool dark place until ready to use.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
This is the BOMB! Sweet and spicy! Fantastic on all BBQ.
