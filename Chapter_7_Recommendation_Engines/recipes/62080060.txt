Title:
Tuscan Sausage and Chard Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb mild Italian sausage (no casing)
1/2 onion, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
6 cups water
2 chicken boullion cubes (Knorr)
1 bunch swiss chard cleaned and chopped
1 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp Italian herb blend
Grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brown the sausage.  Add onion and red bell pepper and saute 3 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients, bring to a boil, turn down and simmer 20 minutes.  Garnish with grated parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Peach822
Description: 
A wonderful and quick soup.  Serve with a good crusty bread and some vino!
