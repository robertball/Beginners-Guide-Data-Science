Title:
Chicken Tacos with Rice & Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 6oz.chicken breasts, grilled then diced
5 Tbsp. pico de gallo
1 Tbsp. low-fat sour cream
4 small tortillas (rice-glutenfree)
1/2 avocado, peeled and sliced
5 sprigs fresh cilantro
2c. black beans, cooked
2 1/2c. of brown rice, cooked

Directions:
Assemble chicken taco ingredients on tortillas. Top with avocado slices.
Serve with 1/2c. of black beans & 1/3c. of rice seasoned with salt, pepper, & cilantro.
