Title:
Striker's Potatoes O'Brien Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Large Russett Potatoes
1 Large Green Bell Pepper
1 Large Red Bell Pepper
1 Large Yellow Onion
4 Tbs Vegetable Oil

Directions:
Boil the potatoes for about 10 minutes, then set aside to cool. Do not peel. Dice peppers and onion into 1/2" pieces. After Potatoes cool, dice them into 1/2" pieces. Put the vegetables into a large bowl and toss with vegetable oil. Do not mix with a spoon because you will mash the potatoes. Put the mix into a freezer bag and freeze immediatly. Cook on med/hi heat on a non-stick surface until potatoes are crispy and brown.

Submitter: 
STRIKER
Description: 
I'm tired of buying this at the store, so I decided to make my own. In my opinion, this is the best way to prepare potatoes for a typical breakfast. This recipe lasts a long time in the freezer.
