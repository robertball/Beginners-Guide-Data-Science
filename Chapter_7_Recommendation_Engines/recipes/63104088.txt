Title:
Mushroom Stuffed Chicken Breast (no breadcrumbs) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 whole chicken breasts pounds out and sliced inhalf to make 4 total pieces (cutlets)
1 onion finely chopped
3 cloves of garlic finely chopped
1 8oz container of mushrooms finely chopped
1 zucchini finely chopped
Ground Almonds 2-3 Tbsp
Olive oil for sauteeing
2 Tbsp red wine (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees.
In a saucepan:
Sautee onion, garlic, zuchini in olive oil.
When soft add mushroom.
Add a little red wine if desired.
Wait til liquid evaporates.
Stir in some ground almond to bind the ingredients.
Place stuffing on 1 chicken breast and roll up.
Secure with toothpicks.
Optional: spray chicken with olive oil spray and coat with ground almonds
place toothpick side down in baking dish.
Bake about 30 minutes til ready.
