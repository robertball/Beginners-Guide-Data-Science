Title:
Summer Philly Steak Sandwiches Chicago Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs thin cut steak (fairly lean)
medium green pepper
medium sweet onion
olive oil
water
italian seasoning blend
32 oz beef broth
4 oz butter
flour as needed
8 oz shredded monterrey jack cheese
milk as needed
italian sandwich rolls

Directions:
in a large container or plastic bag, combine liberal amounts of oil, seasonings, sliced peppers, and onions with a splash of water, shake to blend, and add thin cut steaks.  Seal, shake and refrigerate for 1 hour.
heat outdoor grill to high
remove steak from marinade and quickly sear and remove from heat
add 32 oz beef broth with peppers and onions from marinade to foil tray and return to grill set on low.  Allow time for peppers to soften and steak to become tender, approx 30 min
in a small pot, melt 4 oz butter and add a few tablespoons of flour to create a roux, splash milk to thin slightly.
add shredded cheese to roux and melt on low, stirring frequently.  Add milk as needed to thin, but maintain desired consistency.  Add additional seasonings if desired.
slice rolls and add steak, peppers, onions, and cheese sauce.
enjoy!

Submitter: 
dacubz
Description: 
A tasty cross between Philly Steak and Italian beef, done outdoors.  An on the fly recipe created that was gone in no time flat!  Serve with fries or your favorite sides.
