Title:
Canned Salmon Lunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1966799.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14.75 ounce) can salmon, drained and flaked
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup chopped mild pickled pepper rings
1 tablespoon pickled pepper juice

Directions:
Mix salmon, mayonnaise, peppers, and pepper juice in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Caramia
Description: 
All five of my kids eat this, and my picky eater requests it for lunch often. Serve on crackers.
