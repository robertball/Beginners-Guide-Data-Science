Title:
Crustless Broccoli-Cheddar Quiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Butter (for ramekins)
Coarse salt
1 pkg. (10 oz.) frozen broccoli florets
6 lg. eggs
1/2 C half-and-half
Ground pepper
1/8 t. ground nutmeg
3/4 C (3oz) shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Butter 4 (8oz) ramekins or a 9-in pie dish; set aside. Bring a med. pot of salted water to a boil. Add broccoli; cook 1 min. Drain well; transfer to a cutting board, and blot dry with paper towels. Chop coarsely.
In lg. bowl, whisk together eggs, half-and-half, 1/2 t. salt, 1/4 t. pepper, and nutmeg. Stir in broccoli and cheese.
Place ramekins on a rimmed baking sheet. Ladle broccoi mixture into ramekins, dividing evenly. Bake until golden brown, 35-40 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
In these individual quiches, broccoli gives the creamy cheddar and egg a nice crunch; eliminating the crust cuts down on baking time. Made in 4-in. ramekins.
