Title:
Chicken and Lentil Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large onions, sliced
2 tbs minced garlic
2 tbs curry powder
s/p
2 tbs olive oil
4 tbs flour
1 cup (8 oz) lentils
2 cups shredded chicken
4 cups chicken broth

Directions:
In a medium pot simmer lentils in chicken broth 15-20mins.  In a large pot saute onions in olive oil then add garlic, curry powder, and flour.  Stir in lentils and broth and bring to boil.  Season with salt and pepper
