Title:
Spaghetti Marco Polo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. spaghetti
1 Tbsp. salt (for the water)
1 Tbsp. EVOO
1 Tbsp. lemon juice
8 oz. swiss cheese, cut in 1 cm cubes
1 large tomato, diced
1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
3 green onions, chopped
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (pecans or almonds are a suitable substitution)
1 can black olives, drained and chopped
2 5 oz. cans tuna, drained and tossed
freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
While the pasta is cooking, prepare the other ingredients.
Remove spaghetti from heat and drain.
Return pasta to pot on low heat and add EVOO, lemon juice, and swiss cheese.  Toss gently until the swiss cheese is evenly distributed and beginning to melt.  Remove from heat.
Add tomato, parsley, green onions, nuts, black olives, and tuna. Toss.  Serve immediately, with freshly ground black pepper to taste.

Description:
