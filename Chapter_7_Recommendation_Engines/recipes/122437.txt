Title:
Ruby-Red Pretzel Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983910.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) can jellied cranberry sauce
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup vinegar
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground mustard
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon cold water
red food coloring
pretzels

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine the first seven ingredients; whisk over medium heat until smooth.  Combine flour and cold water until smooth; add to cranberry mixture.  Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes.  Transfer to a bowl; stir in food coloring if desired.  Cover and chill overnight.  Serve with pretzels.

Submitter: 
Grace  Yaskovic
Description: 
Plain pretzels get a pretty coating and tangy taste from this thick, festive blend. Grace Yaskovic of Branchville, New Jersey shares the recipe.
