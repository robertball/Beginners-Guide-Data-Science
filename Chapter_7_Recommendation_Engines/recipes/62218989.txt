Title:
Stuffed Whitefish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (4lb) whole fish  (whitefish, salmon or pickerel)
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 c finely chopped celery
2 c dry bread crumbs
1/3 tsp ground sage
1/4 tsp poultry seasoning
1/4 tsp salt
fresh ground pepper
Garnish
lemon wedges
fresg parsley

Directions:
scale and clean fish. Prepare stuffing: In a large fry pan, heat oil and saute onion and celery until transparent, about 4 to 5 minutes. Stir in bread crumbs, sage, poulty seasoning, salt, pepper to taste and 1/4 of water. Stuff fish, place on sprayed foil and seal. Bake at 400F allowing 10 minutes per inch of thickness. (measure at the thickest point) Serve on a hot platter, garnish with lemon wedges and parsley.

Submitter: 
C.Lynn
Description: 
I use fresh whitefish from our lake. Wonderful for any whole fish.
