Title:
Mendelssohn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/940886.jpg
Ingredients:
ice cubes
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Irish cream liqueur
1/2 cup club soda

Directions:
Fill a highball glass with ice cubes. Pour in the Irish cream liqueur then top off with a few splashes of club soda.

A delicious and refreshing cocktail made with Irish cream liqueur!
