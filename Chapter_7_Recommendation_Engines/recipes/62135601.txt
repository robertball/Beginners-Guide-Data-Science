Title:
Wine Harvest Risotto with Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb mild italian sausage, sliced
1 med onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, mashed
1 1/4 c.Uncle Bens converted Rice  1 can chicken broth (13 3/4oz)
1 c. white wine
1 lbs large prawns, shelled & deveined
1/2 c. parmesan cheese
chopped parsley

Directions:
Brown sausage slices in large frying pan, spoon off drippings. Add onion, garlic, and rice, and cook over med heat stirring occassionally until onions begin to brown. Add chicken broth and 1 c. of wine. Cover & simmer for 20min. Stir in cheese, arrange raw shrimp over rice mixture, cover again, and cook 12-15min longer, until shrimps are tender and pink, and rice has absorbed most of the moisture. Taste and add salt if needed.
Sprinkle with chopped parsley. At table may drizzle the rice with 1/4c. wine, stirring it, that is optional per taste.
May use frozen shrimp already deveined, that will save time in cooking and cleaning it. Prep time will be about 15min.
