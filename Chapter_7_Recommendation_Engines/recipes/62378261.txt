Title:
Heavenly Potatoes and Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/416411.jpg
Ingredients:
5 pounds russet potatoes, sliced
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1/2 cup butter
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
2 cups shredded Italian cheese mix
1/4 cup chopped green onion
2 cups cooked, chopped ham
salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 cups Parmesan cheese flavored bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Slice potatoes in a large pot of water, and bring to a boil. Boil until slightly tender, about 5 minutes. Drain, and transfer to a large bowl.
Mix sour cream, butter, cream of chicken soup, Italian cheese, green onions, ham, salt and pepper with the potatoes. Spread mixture in the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
Bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven.

Description:
