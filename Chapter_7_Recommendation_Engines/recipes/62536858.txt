Title:
No Bake Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521117.jpg
Ingredients:
1-chocolate graham cracker pie crust
¾ cup creamy peanut butter
8 or 9 oz whipped topping
4 oz cream cheese-softened
1 cup confectioner’s sugar
chocolate bar (for garnish curls)

Directions:
In medium mixing bowl with hand mixer, mix cream cheese, confectioner's sugar, peanut butter until well blended.  Continue to blend in whipped topping until well mixed.  Spoon into chocolate pie crust.  With vegetable peeler over chocolate bar, garnish with chocolate curls. Chill for 1/2 hr or more.

Submitter: 
Mendy
Description: 
I got this super easy recipe from my Aunt that serves it at her annual Christmas Eve cocktail party.  It always gets rave reviews.
