Title:
Chicken Fajitas in a Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4023765.jpg
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts
sliced red onion
sliced sweet peppers
sliced tomatoes
rice
black beans
hominy corn or sweet corn kernels
shredded cheese
salsa
cilantro
taco seasoning
salt
pepper
olive oil

Directions:
Slice red peppers, tomatoes, and onions. Lay out on foil wrapped cookie sheet. Lay chicken breasts in the middle.
Drizzle with olive oil. . Sprinkle veggies with sea salt and pepper. Toss
Sprinkle 1 T taco seasoning on chicken breasts.
Put salsa on top of chicken breasts.
Put in 400 degree oven for 35 minutes.
Remove from oven and slice chicken.
Serve on a bed of rice. Add black beans, corn, cilantro and guacamole if desired.

Submitter: 
Sherillynn
Description: 
Great to make and put in the fridge for lunch all week long. Super easy and flavorful.
