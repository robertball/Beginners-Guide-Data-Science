Title:
Lemon Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/4 C chicken broth
1/4 C plus 2 T lemon juice
1 1/2 grated lemon rind
1 1/2 T vegetable oil
2 C Long grain brown rice
1 zucchini, diced small
1/4 plus 2 T fresh dill
1/4 C plus 2 T margarine

Directions:
In heavy saucepan combine stock, lemon juicem lemon rind, and oil. Bring to a boil. Stir in rice. Cook, covered over low heat 40 minutes or until liquid is almost absorbed. Stir in zucchin, dill, salt, and pepper to taste and simmer covered about 5 more minutes. Stri in margarine, fluff with a fork and serve very hot.
