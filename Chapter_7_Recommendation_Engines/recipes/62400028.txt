Title:
Longevity Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
3 Tbsp rice vinegar
2 Tbsp soy or Tamari sauce
2 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 tsp sesame oil
1 tsp chili paste with garlic (available in the Asian grocery section)
dash of crushed red pepper if desired
Salad ingredients:
1 4oz pkg lo mein noodles or spaghetti noodles,
cooked
1/2  red, yellow or green pepper, julienned
4 sliced green onions
1/4 chopped fresh cilantro or as desired
shredded cooked chicken (optional)

Directions:
Mix dressing ingredients until the sugar is dissolved.  Pour over salad ingredients, toss  and chill 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Teresa Haider
Description: 
Asian inspired, cold noodle salad.  Add the optional crushed dried red pepper if you like a spicier version.
