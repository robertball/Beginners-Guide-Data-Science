Title:
Mediterranean Tomato Soup with Feta Topped Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 Tablespoon butter or margarine
4 cups chicken broth
2 cans diced tomatoes (14 oz cans)
1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme leaves
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup sour cream
3/4 cup crumbled garlic-and-herb feta cheese
3 oz cream cheese
1/8 teaspoon coarsely ground pepper
24 Club crackers

Directions:
For soup, in large saucepan or Dutch oven cook onion in butter until tender.  Stir in broth, undrained tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, thyme and the 1/4 teaspoon pepper.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat.  Simmer, covered, for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Meanwhile, for cracker topping, in small bowl stir together feta cheese, cream cheese, and 1/8 teaspoon pepper. Cover and refrigerate until needed.
To serve, ladle soup into bowls.  Spoon dollops of sour cream on top of soup.  Spread feta mixture on Club crackers, and serve with soup.
