Title:
Hoecakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup self rising cormeal
1 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder
2 eggs
3/4 cup buttermilk
1 Tbl. sugar
1/3 cup plus 1 Tbl. water
oil or butter for frying

Directions:
With a whisk, mix ingredients together. Heat butter or oil in a skillet. Place about two tablespoons of batter per hoecake into the skillet and fry on both sides until golden brown. Serve with syrup of your choice.

Submitter: 
LINDA MCLEAN
Description: 
A cross between a pancake and corn bread, this southern treat was said to have been cooked on the back of a spade over an open fire; hence the name Hoecakes!
