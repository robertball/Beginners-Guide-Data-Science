Title:
Simmered Kabocha Pumpkin and Fried Tofu with sweet soy sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/497095.jpg
Ingredients:
the half of Kabocha pumpikn
1 package of Fried Tofu
1/4 of  medium Onion
Dashi soup  2 cup
Soy sauce    3 TBS
Sugar  3 TBS
Mirin   3 TBS
Sake    3 TBS
a pinch of Salt

Directions:
Cut the pumpkin like 2 inch cube and cut off those angles and a partly of skin.
and dip in water at least 15 min. ( for prevent it from breaking up in simmering )
Cut the Fried Tofu 1 for 16 peaces.
Cut the Onion thinly.
Measure the Soy sauce, Mirin, Sake, Sugar and Dashi.
Add all of the seasoning and all ingredients into a pan.
Cover with foil.
Bring to boil and simmer 15 min.
Wait for cool down. In the meantime the taste settles down.

Submitter: 
kyoko
Description: 
This is a kind of traditional Japanese Nimono. It's really good for lunch box too. You can get Japanese Kabocha pumpkin at Japanese market or chinese market or some asian market.
