Title:
Stacey's Homemade Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large idaho baking potatoes, peeled and cut into large chunks
5 ounces evaporated milk
1/4 cup butter
11/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Boil potatoes until fork tender about 25 minutes.   Remove from heat and drain.  Cut butter into slices and place in bowl.  Pour hot potatoes over butter and allow to melt for a couple minutes.  Using hand mixer beaters mash potatoes without turning on mixer.  Sprinkle salt and pepper over potatoes.   Turn on mixer and begin beating adding just enough milk to make your desired consistency.

staceywatts
Description: 
One of my favorite things growing up this dish accompanies just about any main dish extremely well.
