Title:
Mashed rutabagas with bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 rutabagas
3 strips chopped bacon, cooked to crisp, grease reserved
2 TB butter
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring large pot of water to a boil.
Peel & dice the rutabagas, then add to boiling water.
Cook until pieces break apart with a fork.
Drain rutabagas and mash (electric mixer is easiest) with butter, salt & pepper, and 1-2 TB of the reserved bacon grease.
When you reach your desired consistency, stir in chopped bacon.

Submitter: 
Laura Fields
Description: 
Rutabagas are addictive on their own.  Add bacon and they are heaven in your mouth!
