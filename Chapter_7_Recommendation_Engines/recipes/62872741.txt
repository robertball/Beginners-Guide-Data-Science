Title:
Banana Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup butter, cubed
1/4 cup half and half (or cream)
2 T pure maple syrup
2 bananas, sliced

Directions:
Add all ingredients together except bananas in a small saucepan.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer over low heat 5 minutes.
Carefully stir in banana slices and simmer for another 2 or 3 minutes.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Ye gods but this stuff is wonderful!  Modified from a Taste of Home recipe - they recommend putting over ice-cream.  Haven't tried on ice cream yet, but drizzled over pancakes or banana nut muffins (or cake) is wonderful.
