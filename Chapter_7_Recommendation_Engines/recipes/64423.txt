Title:
Classic Mandarin Orange Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982628.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound spinach or mixed greens
1 (15 ounce) can DOLEÂ® Mandarin Oranges, drained
1/2 cup sliced ripe olives
1/2 cup sliced red onion
1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Combine spinach, mandarin oranges, olives and onion in large serving bowl.
Pour vinaigrette over salad; toss to evenly coat.  Top with feta cheese.

Submitter: 
Dole Food Company
Description: 
Give this easy salad an extra crunch by adding slivered almonds or pecan pieces, if desired.
