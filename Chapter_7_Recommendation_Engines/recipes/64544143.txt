Title:
Chicken Provencal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 whole shallots
3 c. canned chopped Roma tomatoes with juices
12 garlic cloves, peeled
1/4 c. lemon juice
2 T.chopped fresh rosemary or 1 T. dried
2 T. thyme fresh leaves or 1 T. dried
3 whole boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut in half
1 can (14-oz) artichokes hearts, drained and quartered
1/2 c. Parmesan or shredded mozzarella

Directions:
In a 9 by 13 baking dish, combine all but the last 3 ingredients.  Bake, uncovered, in a 350 oven 45 minutes or until shallots soften.
Place chicken breasts on top and distribute artichokes over all.  Spoon some of the tomato mixture from the pan over top. Cover and bake 20 minutes. Sprinkle on the cheese and bake uncovered another 10 minutes.
