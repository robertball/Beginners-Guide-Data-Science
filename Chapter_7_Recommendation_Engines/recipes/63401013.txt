Title:
Chewy Chocolate-White Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11 1/2 ounces white chocolate chips
1 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar, packed
2 large eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 1/4 cups unsifted flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions:
Heat oven to 350ÂºF.
Stir flour with baking soda, cocoa powder and salt; set aside. In large mixing bowl, beat butter with sugar and brown sugar at medium speed until creamy and lightened in color. Add eggs and vanilla, one at a time. Mix on low speed until incorporated. Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture. Stir in white chocolate chips. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until edges are slightly crisp.
