Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese, softened*
1 tablespoon apple juice
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon or apple pie spice
1 medium red apple, chopped
1/4 cup chopped pecans
Keebler速 Town House速 Original Crackers

Directions:
In small mixing bowl beat cream cheese and juice on medium speed of electric mixer until fluffy. Beat in brown sugar and cinnamon or spice.
Stir in apple and pecans. Garnish as desired. Serve with crackers.

Submitter:

Description: 
This creamy spread with cinnamon, chopped apples, and pecans makes a great after-school or tea-time snack.
