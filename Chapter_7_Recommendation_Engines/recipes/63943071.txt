Title:
Martie's Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 to 2 heads of broccoli
1 lb of bacon
1 red onion chopped
1 cup golden or regular raisins
3/4 cups mayonnaise or miracle whip
1/4 cup sugar
1 tbsp vinegar
8 oz sharp cheddar cheese diced (or shredded

Directions:
Cook up the bacon until crisp. Crumble put into a small bowl.
Cut the broccoli into florets. Dice up stems put into a large bowl.
Dice up the onion and place with the broccoli.
Add the cup of raisins and cheddar cheese. Then add crumbled bacon.
In a small bowl combine mayo, sugar and vinegar. Pour over broccoli in large bowl. Mix until all is completely covered.
Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Kevin Gorton
Description: 
This recipe is from my mother-in-law Martie and is always a crowd-pleaser at family events and church picnics. The combination of bacon cheese and raisins give it a sweet and salty taste.
