Title:
Potato Skins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large baking potatoes
2 Tablespoons of cooking oil
4 teaspoons of chili powder
Several drops of hot sauce
1 pound cooked, chopped bacon
2/3 Cup chopped tomato
2/3 Cup chopped green onion
1/2 Cup sour cream

Directions:
Scrub potatoes and prick with a fork.
Bake in a 425 degree oven for 50 minutes or until tender.
Set potatoes aside to cool.
Cut each potato lengthwise into 4 wedges.
Scoop out most of white portion of each potato wedge with a spoon.
In a small bowl combine oil, chili powder, and hot sauce.
Using a pastry brush, brush the insides of the potato wedges with the oil mixture.
Place the potato wedges in a single layer on a large baking sheet.
Sprinkle wedges with bacon, tomato, and green onions.
Top with cheese.
Bake about 10 minutes more or until cheese melts and potato wedges are heated through.
Serve with sour cream.
