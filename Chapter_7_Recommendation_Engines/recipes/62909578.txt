Title:
My Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/707555.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs Of Beef, Chuck Steak, Cut Into 1 inch Squares
2 Tablespoons Of Olive Oil
6 cups of beef stock
3 Tablespoons of Worcestershire sauce
3 Cloves of garlic
1 onion, chopped
2 bay leaves
1 tablespoon of salt
1 teaspoon of paprika
1 teaspoon of thyme
1 teaspoon of rosemary
1 teaspoon of pepper
6 carrots, sliced
6 potatoes, quartered
3 tablespoons of butter
6 tablespoons of flour

Directions:
Brown beef in a fry pan using olive oil
have 6 cups of beef broth already heated to boiling, add to crock pot
once beef is browned add it to the crock pot
Add Worcestershire sauce, Garlic, Onion, Bay leaves, Salt, Pepper, pepper, Thyme, Rosemary, potatoes, and carrots.
melt butter in a cup, add flour, mix. about 30 Min's before serving, add butter, and flour mixture to the stew.
