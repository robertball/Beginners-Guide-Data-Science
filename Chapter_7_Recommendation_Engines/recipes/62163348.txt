Title:
The Perfect Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 t. baking powder
1/2 t. baking soda
1 t. salt
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 cup mashed bananas

Directions:
Sift: flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
Set aside.
Cream shortening and sugar.
Add bananas.
Blend in flour mix.
Pour in pan.
Let sit for 20 min.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Trixi Jinsin
Description: 
Amazing banana bread that I usually make in small loaves.
