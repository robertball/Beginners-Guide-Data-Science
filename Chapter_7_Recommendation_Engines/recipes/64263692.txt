Title:
Toll House Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Eggs
1/2 Cup flour
1/2 Cup Sugar
1/2 Cup brown sugar
1/2 Cup melted butter
6 oz chocolate chips
1 Cup walnuts (optional)
1 9" pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Beat eggs until foamy. Add flour, sugars, butter and chips (walnuts). Pour into unbaked pie shell. Bake for about 1 hour. Let cool before slicing.

Submitter: 
Christine Denstaedt
Description:
