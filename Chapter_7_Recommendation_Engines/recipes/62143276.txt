Title:
Sour Cream Strawberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/937433.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large box Cherry jello
1 cup boiling water
1 cup mashed banana
1 large can crushed pineapple, drained and strained
1 or 2 boxes frozen strawberries, thawed
1/4 cup chopped pecans
1 pint sour cream

Directions:
spray 9" x 11" casserole dish with cooking spray
Mix jello and boiling water. Then add banana, pineapple, strawberries and pecans, mixing well.
Pour half of mixture into casserole dish. Place in refridgerator for 1 hour (or in freezer for 30 minutes)
Mixture should be set. Then, using spatula, spread sour cream over congealed mixture. Last, slowly spoon remaining jello mixture on top of sour cream. Refridgerate for at least 2 hours. Cut into squares before serving.

Submitter: 
Ginger Hackley
Description: 
This is so good, and is a MUST have at Thanksgiving and Christmas.  It is a side salad, but is good enough for dessert. The dish with red and white layers is very pretty on the table.
