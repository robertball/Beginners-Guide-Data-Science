Title:
Nutter Butter Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Nutter Butter cookies, crushed
1 stick butter or margarine, melted
1 (8oz.) package cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 cup peanut butter, creamy or chunky
1 cup milk
1 (16 oz.) container Cool Whip, thawed
20 miniautre Reese's Peanut Butter cups, cut into 1/4's

Directions:
Crush cookies in large resealable plastic bag and mix with melted butter or margarine.  Pat down into a 13x9 pan.  Refrigerate crust while mixing the filling.  Beat together the cream cheese and powdered sugar.  Mix in peanut butter and milk.  Beat until smooth and then fold in Cool Whip.  Spoon into crust and top with quartered miniature Reese's cups;  cover and freeze until firm.
