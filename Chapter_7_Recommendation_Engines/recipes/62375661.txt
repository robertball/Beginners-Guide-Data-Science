Title:
European Style French Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mild herb vinegar (e.g. Kressi Essig)
1/4 cup salad oil
1/4 cup half & half or milk
1 teaspoon mild mustard (e.g. Thomy Senf Mild)
1 egg yolk or 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
1 teaspoon salad herb mix
seasoned salt, to taste (e.g. Herbamare)
pepper, to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well. If the dressing is too thick, add milk. If it's too thin, add mayonnaise.

Submitter: 
Angela
Description: 
This is the same salad dressing served in every restaurant in Switzerland. I have never been able to find the same one in the US...until now!
