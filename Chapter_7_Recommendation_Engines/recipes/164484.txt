Title:
Fireman's Sour Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/859819.jpg
Ingredients:
4 fluid ounces rum
3 fluid ounces lime juice
1 fluid ounce grenadine syrup
1 fluid ounce simple syrup

Directions:
Pour the rum, lime juice, grenadine, and simple syrup into a cocktail shaker over ice. Cover, and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted. Strain into clean test tubes or shot glasses to serve.
