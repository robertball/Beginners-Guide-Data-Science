Title:
Breakfast In A Glass Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/837476.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
1/4 cup strawberries
1/3 cup nonfat milk
1 (8 ounce) container nonfat plain yogurt

Directions:
In a blender, combine banana, strawberries, milk and yogurt. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
SHIPPY
Description: 
A banana is blended with strawberries, milk and yogurt. A delicious way to start the day!
