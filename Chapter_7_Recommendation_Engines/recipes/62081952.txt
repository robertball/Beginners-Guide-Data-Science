Title:
ROAST BEEF TO DIE FOR Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 LBS RUMP ROAST (OR ANY CUT YOU LIKE)
1 ENVELOPE OF BROWN GRAVY MIX
1 ENVELOPE OF ITALIAN DRESSING MIX
1 ENVELOPE OF RANCH DRESSING MIX

Directions:
PUT ROAST IN A 4-5 QT CROCK POT.  SPRINKLE EACH ENVELOPE OVER ROAST.  PUT ON LOW FOR 10-12 HOURS.  THE LONGER THE BETTER.
