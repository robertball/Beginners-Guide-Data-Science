Title:
Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Sticks of Stick Butter (melted)
1 1/2 Cups Sugar
2 Tbsp. Cinnimon
2 Loaves Frozen Bread Dough (Thawed)

Directions:
Combine sugar and cinnamon in a sauce pan and heat until caramelized.
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Roll dough into small balls, roll in sauce.
place the coated dough balls in a bundt pan.
Pour the remainder of sauce over top of the dough in the bundt pan.
Bake for 30 min. covered and 30 min. uncovered.
Invert immediately after removed from the oven, onto a platter.
